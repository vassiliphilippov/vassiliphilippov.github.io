renationalize

verb
to return to state control
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈnæʃənəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˌriːˈnæʃnəlaɪz /; / ˌriːˈnæʃənəlaɪz / (BrE)
to bring an industry or asset back under government ownership or control after it was privatized
