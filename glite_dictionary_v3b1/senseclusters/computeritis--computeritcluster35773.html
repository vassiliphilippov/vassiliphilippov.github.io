computeritis

noun
discomfort from excessive computer use
Pronunciation: / kəmˌpjuːtəˈraɪtɪs / (AmE), / kəmˌpjuːtəˈraɪtɪs / (BrE)
Physical discomfort or health problems resulting from excessive use of a computer, such as eyestrain or repetitive strain injury.

Examples

Computeritis symptoms
Many people are experiencing computeritis due to their increased screen time while working from home.
She started to develop computeritis after spending hours working at her desk every day.
The company hosted a workshop on how to prevent computeritis among employees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eyestrain - fatigue or pain in the eyes from close work or screens
  • synonym: repetitive strain injury - injury caused by repetitive movement, often relating to computer use
