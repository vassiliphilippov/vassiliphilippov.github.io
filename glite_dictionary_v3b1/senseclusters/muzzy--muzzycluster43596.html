muzzy

adjective
confused or unclear in thinking
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌzi / (AmE), / ˈmʌzi / (BrE)
unable to think clearly; confused or vague, especially in mind or thought.

Examples

Muzzy mind
After taking the medicine, she felt a bit muzzy and couldn't concentrate on her work.
His muzzy thoughts made it difficult to follow the conversation at the meeting.
I always wake up muzzy after a late night out.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear-headed - thinking clearly and rationally
  • synonym: woolly-headed - having confused or vague ideas; not clear in thought
