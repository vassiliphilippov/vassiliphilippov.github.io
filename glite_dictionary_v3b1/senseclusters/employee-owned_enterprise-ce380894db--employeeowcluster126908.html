employee-owned enterprise

noun
business owned by its employees
2.4 3.7
Pronunciation: /əm.ˈplɔɪ.iː oʊnd ˈen.tər.praɪz/ /; / /ɪm.ˈplɔɪ.iː oʊnd ˈen.tər.praɪz/ (AmE), /ɪm.ˈplɔɪ.iː əʊnd ˈen.tə.praɪz/ /; / /əm.ˈplɔɪ.iː əʊnd ˈen.tə.praɪz/ (BrE)
A business or organization in which the employees own a significant share or all of the ownership, often through shares or cooperative arrangements.

Examples

An employee-owned enterprise business
After the buyout, the company transitioned into an employee-owned enterprise to give staff a direct stake in its success.
An employee-owned enterprise often fosters higher morale and productivity among its workers.
The law firm is proud to be an employee-owned enterprise, where every employee is a shareholder.
