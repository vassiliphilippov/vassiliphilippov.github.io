upending

noun
the act of turning upside down
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌpˈɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of turning something upside down or setting it on end

Examples

The upending of beliefs
His theory led to the upending of long-held scientific beliefs.
The upending of the box revealed a hidden compartment underneath.
There was a sudden upending of the canoe in the middle of the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inversion - the act of turning something the opposite way up or inside out
  • antonym: uprighting - the act of setting something upright
