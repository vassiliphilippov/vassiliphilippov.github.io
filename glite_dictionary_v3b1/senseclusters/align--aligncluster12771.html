align

verb
arrange parts correctly
1.0 2.9
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈlaɪn/ (AmE), /ə.ˈlaɪn/ /; / /æ.ˈlaɪn/ (BrE)
to arrange or position things so they are in a straight line or in the correct relationship to each other
