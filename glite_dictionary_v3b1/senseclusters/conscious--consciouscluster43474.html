conscious

adjective
aware; awake; having perception
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.ʃəs / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.ʃəs / (BrE)
aware of and able to perceive or experience one's surroundings, sensations, or thoughts; awake and responsive to the environment.

Examples

Conscious and awake
After fainting briefly, I became conscious again to the sound of voices around me.
He was always conscious of the impression he made on others.
She remained conscious throughout the entire operation, astonishing the medical staff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aware - having knowledge or perception of a situation or fact
  • antonym: unconscious - not awake and not aware of and responding to one's environment
