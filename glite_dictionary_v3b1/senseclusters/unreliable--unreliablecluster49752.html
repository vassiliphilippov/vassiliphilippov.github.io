unreliable

adjective
dangerously unstable or unpredictable
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈlaɪ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈlaɪ.ə.bəl / (BrE)
not safe or dependable; dangerously unstable or unpredictable, especially likely to fail or collapse

Examples

Unreliable bridge
Engineers declared the support beams unreliable after thorough inspection.
Heavy rain made the old bridge unreliable for crossing safely.
You should avoid hiking that trail in winter because it's often unreliable and slippery.
