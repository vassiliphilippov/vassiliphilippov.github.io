long row to hoe

noun
difficult or challenging task
9.9
Pronunciation: / lɔŋ ɹoʊ tə hoʊ / (AmE), / lɒŋ rəʊ tə həʊ / (BrE)
a difficult or challenging task or undertaking, often one that requires persistent effort over time

Examples

A long row to hoe

Building a successful business from scratch is often a long row to hoe.
Convincing the board to approve her proposal was a long row to hoe.
