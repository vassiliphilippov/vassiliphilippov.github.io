docilely

adverb
obediently
5.5 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd…ëÀês…ôli / (AmE), / Ààd…ô äsa…™li / (BrE)
in a quiet, obedient, or easily managed manner

Examples

To docilely obey
The horse followed its trainer docilely around the ring without hesitation.
She docilely accepted the instructions given by her supervisor.
Children sat docilely in their seats, waiting for the teacher to begin the lesson.

Synonyms

    2.7 1.75/m

  • submissively

  • meekly
    4.3 1.15/m

  • compliantly
