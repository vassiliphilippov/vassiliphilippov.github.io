paprikash

noun
Hungarian meat stew
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːprɪˌkɑːʃ / (AmE), / ˈpæprɪˌkæʃ / (BrE)

Examples

Her grandmother taught her the traditional way to make paprikash from scratch.
