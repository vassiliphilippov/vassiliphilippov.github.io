licked

adjective
defeated; overcome
8.7
Pronunciation: / lɪkt / (AmE), / lɪkt / (BrE)
Defeated or overcome, especially in a contest or struggle.

Examples

The team licked badly
Even after losing three games, the team refused to consider themselves licked.
His expression was that of a licked man, worn down by adversity.
She left the meeting feeling completely licked by her opponent's arguments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: victorious - having won a victory
