longline

verb
to fish with longline
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔŋˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋˌlaɪn / (BrE)
to fish by setting or operating a long fishing line with many baited hooks

Examples

To longline for fish
Many commercial fishermen longline to catch tuna in deep ocean waters.
The crew plans to longline overnight to maximize their catch.
Some environmental groups oppose the practice of longlining due to its impact on other marine life.
