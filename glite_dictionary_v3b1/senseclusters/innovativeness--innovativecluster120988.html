innovativeness

noun
originality; introducing new ideas
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnəˈveɪtɪvnəs / (AmE), / ˌɪnəˈveɪtɪvnəs / (BrE)

Examples

Her innovativeness in technology

The company's success is driven by its commitment to innovativeness and forward-thinking solutions.
Universities encourage innovativeness among students through research and creative projects.
