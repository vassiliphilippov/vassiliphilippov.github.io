candlepins

noun
a bowling game with slender pins
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkændlˌpɪnz / (AmE), / ˈkændlˌpɪnz / (BrE)
A bowling game played with slender, straight pins and smaller balls, commonly found in New England, USA.
