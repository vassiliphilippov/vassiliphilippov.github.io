determinable

adjective
able to be determined or fixed
Pronunciation: / dɪˈtɜːrmɪnəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈtɜːmɪnəbl / (BrE)
capable of being determined, limited, or precisely identified

Examples

A determinable limit
Legal disputes often involve issues that are determinable by a court.
The exact date of the event is not determinable from the records.
The speed at which the reaction occurs is determinable under laboratory conditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: indeterminable - not able to be determined or fixed
