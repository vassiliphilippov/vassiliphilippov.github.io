nagami

noun
shrub with oval kumquats; the fruit
7.6
Pronunciation: / nəˈɡɑːmi / (AmE), / nəˈɡɑːmi / (BrE)
A shrub of the kumquat family bearing small, oval, edible orange fruits; also, the fruit of this shrub.

Examples

The nagami fruit
In the orchard, nagami are prized for their sweet, edible skins and tart pulp.
She sliced a few nagami kumquats to add a tangy flavor to the salad.
The nagami shrub produced a generous harvest of bright orange fruits this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kumquat - a small, edible citrus fruit with a sweet rind and tart flesh
