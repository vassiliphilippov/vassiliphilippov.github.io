flagger

noun
person placing markers
Pronunciation: / ˈflæɡər / (AmE), / ˈflæɡə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to place flags or markers to indicate boundaries, survey points, or work locations

Examples

Site flagger
The flagger carefully marked the construction site before the crew arrived.
Each flagger must ensure the markers are visible and accurate for the survey team.
Before any digging started, a flagger placed flags to show the property line.

Related Terms

  • synonym: surveyor - a person who measures land and marks boundaries
  • synonym: worker - a person who does a particular job, especially manual work
