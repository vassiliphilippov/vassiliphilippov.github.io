sire

verb
to father offspring
Pronunciation: / saɪɚ / (AmE), / saɪə / (BrE)
(of a male animal or man) to be the father of (an offspring); to father or cause to be born.

Examples

To sire offspring
He hopes his champion bull will sire next year's calves.
The male lion sired several cubs with different females in the pride.
The prize-winning stallion sired over fifty foals during his lifetime.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beget - to father or produce (offspring); to cause to be born
  • synonym: father - to be the biological male parent of a child or offspring
  • antonym: mother - to give birth to or be the female parent of offspring
