panna cotta

noun
Italian creamy dessert
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑnə ˈkɑtə / (AmE), / ˌpænə ˈkɒtə / (BrE)
an Italian dessert made from sweetened cream thickened with gelatin and often served with fruit or sauce

Examples

Italian panna cotta
For her birthday, Maria made a delicious panna cotta topped with raspberry sauce.
The restaurant's signature dessert is a silky panna cotta with a hint of vanilla.
He learned to prepare panna cotta during his cooking class in Italy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: custard - a sweet dessert made with milk, eggs, and sugar
  • synonym: mousse - a light, creamy dessert made with whipped cream and eggs
