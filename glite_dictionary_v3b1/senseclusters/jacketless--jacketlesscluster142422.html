jacketless

adjective
without a jacket
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒækɪtləs / (AmE), / ˈdʒækɪtləs / (BrE)
not wearing or lacking a jacket

Examples

Jacketless at work
He arrived at the meeting jacketless even though everyone else wore suits.
Despite the chilly wind, she walked home jacketless and seemed unfazed.
The students stood jacketless in the playground during the unexpected warm spell.

Related Terms

  • antonym: jacketed - wearing a jacket
