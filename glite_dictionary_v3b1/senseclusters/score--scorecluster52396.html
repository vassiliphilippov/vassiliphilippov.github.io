score

noun
written music for a composition
Pronunciation: / skɔr / (AmE), / skɔː / (BrE)
the written version of a musical composition, showing parts for different instruments or voices on separate staves

Examples

Orchestra score
A copy of the opera's score was displayed in the museum.
She preferred to play from the full score rather than the simplified piano version.
The conductor studied the score carefully before the rehearsal began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sheet music - written or printed music; musical notation
