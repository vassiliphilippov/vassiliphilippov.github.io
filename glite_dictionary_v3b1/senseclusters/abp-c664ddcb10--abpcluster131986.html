ABP

sym
arterial blood pressure
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ biː ˈpiː / (AmE), / ˌeɪ biː ˈpiː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'arterial blood pressure', a measurement of the pressure in the arteries.

Examples

ABP reading
The doctor checked the patient's ABP every hour during surgery.
A sudden drop in ABP can be a sign of shock or blood loss.
Nurses are trained to monitor ABP using specialized equipment in the ICU.
