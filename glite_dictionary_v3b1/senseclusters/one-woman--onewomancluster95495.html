one-woman

adjective
performed or involving only one woman
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌn ˌwʊm.ən / (AmE), / ˈwʌn ˌwʊm.ən / (BrE)
(of a play, performance, or event) performed by only one woman, or (of something) involving only one woman.

Examples

A one-woman show
Her one-woman campaign for clean water inspired the whole community.
She starred in a gripping one-woman show that captivated the entire audience.
The gallery featured a one-woman exhibition of photographs by the artist.
