tabor

noun
small, single-headed drum
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪ.bɚ /; / ˈteɪ.bər /; / ˈtæm.bɔr / (AmE), / ˈteɪ.bə /; / ˈtæmbʊə / (BrE)
a small drum with one head, traditionally played with a stick, often used in folk or medieval music
