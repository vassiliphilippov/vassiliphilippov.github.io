montagnard

adjective
of mountain people
Pronunciation: / ˌmɒntəˈnjɑrd / (AmE), / ˌmɒntəˈnjɑːd / (BrE)
relating to mountain people or to mountainous regions and their culture

Examples

Montagnard culture
The montagnard communities have preserved unique traditions in the remote highlands.
Researchers documented montagnard languages spoken only in isolated mountain villages.
The festival celebrated montagnard music and crafts from the surrounding peaks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lowland - relating to low-lying land or its people
  • synonym: mountainous - having many mountains or relating to mountains
