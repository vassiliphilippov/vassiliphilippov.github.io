backsaw

noun
hand saw with reinforced spine
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌsɔ / (AmE), / ˈbakˌsɔː / (BrE)
A hand saw with a reinforced metal spine along the upper edge to keep the blade rigid, used for precise woodworking.

Examples

A backsaw tool
A backsaw is ideal for making fine, accurate cuts in wood.
She bought a new backsaw for her carpentry class.
The woodworker carefully used a backsaw to cut the dovetail joints.
