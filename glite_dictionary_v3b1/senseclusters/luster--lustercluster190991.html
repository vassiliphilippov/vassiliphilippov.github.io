luster

noun
shiny ceramic/porcelain coating
4.6 3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈlʌ.stər/ (AmE), /ˈlʌ.stə/ (BrE)
a shiny, glossy, or softly glowing appearance, especially on a surface like metal, ceramics, or porcelain

Examples

Ceramic luster
Artisans perfected the technique to apply a metallic luster to fine ceramics.
Her collection of porcelain figures all featured a delicate pearly luster.
The vase was finished with a golden luster that shimmered in the light.
After polishing the silverware, its original luster was restored.
The diamond's luster made it particularly attractive to collectors.
The pearl's soft luster set it apart from the common stones.
Many antique plates feature a delicate lustre that shimmers in the light.
The artist applied a gold lustre to the vase to give it a brilliant sheen.
This beautiful cup was finished with a pearly lustre to enhance its elegance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gloss - a shiny or lustrous surface
  • antonym: matte - a surface without shine or gloss
  • synonym: sheen - a soft, smooth shine on a surface
