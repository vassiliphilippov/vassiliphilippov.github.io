sensitive scalp

noun
scalp that reacts easily or is easily irritated
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛn.sə.tɪv skælp / (AmE), / ˈsɛn.sɪ.tɪv skælp / (BrE)
A scalp that reacts easily to stimuli such as touch, hair products, or environmental factors, often resulting in discomfort or irritation.

Examples

Sensitive scalp irritation
Because of her sensitive scalp, she avoids using harsh dyes and hair treatments.
If you have a sensitive scalp, it's important to choose your hair products carefully.
Many people with a sensitive scalp prefer mild shampoos to avoid irritation.
