slovenly

adjective
untidy and dirty; careless in appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈslʌvənli / (AmE), / ˈslʌvənli / (BrE)
(of a person or their appearance) untidy and dirty; careless in habits or dress.

Examples

Slovenly appearance
His slovenly appearance made a bad impression at the job interview.
She was criticized for her slovenly handwriting in the exam.
The room looked slovenly after the children played in it all afternoon.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tidy - neat and orderly in appearance or habits
  • synonym: unkempt - having an untidy or disheveled appearance
