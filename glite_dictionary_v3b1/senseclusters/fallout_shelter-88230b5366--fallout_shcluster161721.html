fallout shelter

noun
shelter from radioactive fallout
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔlˌaʊt ˌʃɛltɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːlaʊt ˌʃɛltə / (BrE)
a specially constructed shelter designed to protect people from radioactive fallout following a nuclear explosion

Examples

A fallout shelter basement
During the Cold War, many schools had a fallout shelter in the basement.
The family stocked their fallout shelter with canned food and water in case of emergency.
The old fallout shelter is now used as a storage room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bunker - a reinforced underground shelter, often used during wartime
