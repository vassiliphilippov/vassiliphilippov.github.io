gum acacia

noun
natural thickener from acacia trees
Pronunciation: / ɡʌm əˈkeɪʃə / (AmE), / ɡʌm əˈkeɪsiə / (BrE)
A natural gum obtained from acacia trees, used as a thickener in foods and pharmaceuticals.

Examples

Gum acacia thickener
Many candy manufacturers use gum acacia to give their products the right texture.
Pharmaceutical syrups often contain gum acacia as a stabilizer.
The artist mixed her paints with gum acacia to improve their consistency.
