slumpy

adjective
droopy or dejected
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈslʌmpi / (AmE), / ˈslʌmpi / (BrE)
looking or feeling as if in a slump; droopy, tired, or dejected

Examples

Feeling slumpy after work
After a long day at work, she felt slumpy and just wanted to rest.
The students looked slumpy during the early morning class.
His slumpy posture showed that he was feeling down.

Related Terms
