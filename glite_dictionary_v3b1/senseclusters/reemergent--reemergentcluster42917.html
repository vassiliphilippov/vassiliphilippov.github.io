reemergent

adjective
appearing again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪˈmɜrdʒənt / (AmE), / ˌriːɪˈmɜːdʒənt / (BrE)
appearing or becoming visible again after being hidden, inactive, or absent

Examples

The reemergent threat
The reemergent threat of the virus prompted new safety measures in the city.
Scientists studied the reemergent species that had not been seen for decades.
Interest in traditional crafts has become reemergent among younger generations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disappearing - ceasing to be visible or present
  • synonym: recurring - happening or appearing again repeatedly
