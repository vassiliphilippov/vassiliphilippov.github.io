Tephrosia purpurea

noun
a tropical Indian shrub with purplish flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / tɛˈfroʊsiə pɚˈpjʊriə / (AmE), / tɛˈfrəʊsiə pɜːˈpjʊəriə / (BrE)
A tropical shrub of the pea family native to India, with purplish flowers, used in traditional medicine and as green manure.
