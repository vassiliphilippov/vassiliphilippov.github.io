severity

noun
extreme plainness or lack of decoration
5.2
Pronunciation: / səˈvɛrəti / (AmE), / sɪˈvɛrɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely plain, lacking ornament or decoration

Examples

Severity of style
Her dress had a severity that contrasted with the elaborate styles others wore.
The severity of the building's design made it stand out among its more ornate neighbors.
The poem's severity enhanced its emotional impact by focusing on simplicity and truth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: austerity - plain and simple quality, lack of decoration
