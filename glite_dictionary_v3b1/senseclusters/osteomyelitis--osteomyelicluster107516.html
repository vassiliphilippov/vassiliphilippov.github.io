osteomyelitis

noun
inflammation of bone and bone marrow
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːstiˌoʊˌmaɪəˈlaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌɒstiˌəʊmaɪəˈlaɪtɪs / (BrE)
An inflammation of bone and bone marrow, usually due to bacterial infection.

Examples

Chronic osteomyelitis infection
Chronic osteomyelitis can lead to long-term complications if not addressed promptly.
The patient was diagnosed with osteomyelitis after experiencing severe pain in his leg.
Treatment of osteomyelitis often requires both antibiotics and surgery to remove infected tissue.
