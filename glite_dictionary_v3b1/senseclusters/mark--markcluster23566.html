mark

noun
notable impression or achievement
Pronunciation: / mɑrk / (AmE), / mɑːk / (BrE)
an impression or achievement that makes a person or event remembered or noticed, especially for doing something important or extraordinary

Examples

Make a mark in history
He made his mark in the tech industry by developing an innovative application.
Many athletes aspire to create a mark in the history of their sport.
The artist hoped to leave a lasting mark on the world with her paintings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impression - a lasting effect or influence left by someone or something
  • synonym: legacy - something handed down or left behind, especially a lasting impact or influence
