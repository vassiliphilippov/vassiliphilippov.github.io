yellowtail snapper

noun
a colorful tropical food fish with a yellow stripe
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊˌteɪl ˈsnæpər / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊteɪl ˈsnæpə / (BrE)
a brightly colored fish of the tropical Atlantic and Caribbean, notable for its broad yellow stripe along the side and tail, valued as a food fish
