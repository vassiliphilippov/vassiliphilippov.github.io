post-treatment

adjective
after medical care
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstˈtriːtmənt / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈtriːtmənt / (BrE)
occurring or provided after a treatment, especially referring to care, monitoring, or processes following medical or other treatment.

Examples

Post-treatment care
The doctor scheduled a post-treatment checkup to monitor the patient's recovery.
Careful post-treatment instructions were given to ensure proper healing after the procedure.
Researchers analyzed the post-treatment results to determine the effectiveness of the new drug.

Related Terms

  • synonym: postoperative - relating to the period after a surgical operation
  • antonym: pre-treatment - occurring or provided before a treatment
