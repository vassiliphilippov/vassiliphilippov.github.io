pirouette

noun
rapid spin in ballet
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪr.uˈɛt / (AmE), / ˌpɪə.ruˈɛt / (BrE)
A rapid spinning movement performed on one foot, especially in ballet.

Examples

A perfect pirouette
Mastering the pirouette is a major milestone for aspiring ballet students.
She lost her balance during the second pirouette and had to steady herself.
The dancer executed a flawless pirouette in the center of the stage.
