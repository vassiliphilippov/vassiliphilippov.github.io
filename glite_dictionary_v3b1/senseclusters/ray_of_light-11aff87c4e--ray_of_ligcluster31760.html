ray of light

noun
source of hope or encouragement
Pronunciation: / reɪ əv laɪt / (AmE), / reɪ əv laɪt / (BrE)
a person or thing that brings hope or encouragement, especially in a difficult situation

Examples

A ray of light in trouble
After months of gloom, the team's unexpected win was a ray of light for the fans.
In the midst of tragedy, her generous actions were a ray of light for the community.
The letter from her old friend was a ray of light during a lonely week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beacon of hope - someone or something that gives people hope for the future
  • synonym: silver lining - a hopeful or comforting prospect in the midst of difficulty
