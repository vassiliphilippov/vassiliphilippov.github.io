vinifera grape

noun
common European grape for wine and table use
Pronunciation: / vɪˈnɪfərə ɡreɪp /; / vəˈnɪfərə ɡreɪp / (AmE), / vɪˈnɪfərə ɡreɪp / (BrE)
a common European grape species (Vitis vinifera) grown in many varieties for making wine and for eating

Examples

The vinifera grape thrives in temperate climates with dry summers and mild winters.
