Synonyms

  • inconsiderate
    4.1 1.07/m
    inconsiderate ignores others' needs and rights in action, while thoughtless often accidentally fails to consider consequences for people.
  • unthinking
    4.9 0.43/m
    unthinking acts or speaks without any thought about results, while thoughtless also lacks concern for how actions affect others.
  • tactless
    4.9 0.27/m
    tactless often says or does things that hurt in delicate situations, while thoughtless is a broader lack of concern or forethought.
  • uncaring
    4.2 1.03/m
    uncaring shows little or no sympathy and seems cold, while thoughtless often hurts others because of careless actions, not deliberate harm.
