thoughtless

adjective
not caring about others; inconsiderate
4.4 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈθɔːt.ləs/ /; / /ˈθɔːt.lɪs/ (AmE), /ˈθɔːt.ləs/ /; / /ˈθɔːt.lɪs/ (BrE)
not considering how your actions or words may affect other people; lacking concern for others or for the consequences
