retest

noun
market level test
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈtɛst / (AmE), / ˌriːˈtɛst / (BrE)
an instance when a price or market level returns to a previously broken support or resistance to test if it holds

Examples

Market retest level
After the initial drop, the stock made a retest of the previous support level.
Many traders look for a retest before confirming a new trend direction.
A failed retest of resistance can signal a strong move in the opposite direction.

Related Terms

  • antonym: breakout - a movement of price beyond a support or resistance level
  • synonym: pullback - a temporary reversal in the price of a financial asset
