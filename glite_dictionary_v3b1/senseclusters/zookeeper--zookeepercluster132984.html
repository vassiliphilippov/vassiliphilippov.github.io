zookeeper

noun
person caring for zoo animals
Pronunciation: / ˈzuːˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈzuːˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to care for and manage animals in a zoo

Examples

A zookeeper feeds animals
The zookeeper carefully cleaned the lion's enclosure every morning.
Children watched as the zookeeper explained how to feed the penguins.
Becoming a zookeeper requires knowledge of animal behavior and safety procedures.
The zookeeper carefully cleaned the lion's enclosure every morning before visitors arrived.
Children watched as the zookeeper explained how to feed the penguins safely.
During the storm, the zookeeper made sure all the animals were secure and comfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: animal keeper - a person responsible for the care of animals
  • synonym: caretaker - a person who takes care of a place or animals
