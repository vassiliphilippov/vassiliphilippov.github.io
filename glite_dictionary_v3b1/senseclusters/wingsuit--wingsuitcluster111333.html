wingsuit

noun
gliding jumpsuit
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪŋ.suːt/ /; / /ˈwɪŋ.sjuːt/ (AmE), /ˈwɪŋ.sjuːt/ /; / /ˈwɪŋ.suːt/ (BrE)
a special jumpsuit with fabric between the arms and legs that enables a person to glide through the air during freefall

Examples

A wingsuit flight
The athlete wore a wingsuit to glide over the mountain range during the extreme sports event.
She trained for months before making her first wingsuit jump from the helicopter.
Videos of people flying in a wingsuit have become popular on social media.
