Mandingo

proper noun
West African ethnic group
9.3
Pronunciation: / mænˈdɪŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / mænˈdɪŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
a member of a West African ethnic group or the language spoken by this group

Examples

The Mandingo people
The Mandingo are known for their traditional music and storytelling in West Africa.
She studied the Mandingo language as part of her research on African cultures.
Many Mandingo communities can be found in countries like Mali, Senegal, and The Gambia.
