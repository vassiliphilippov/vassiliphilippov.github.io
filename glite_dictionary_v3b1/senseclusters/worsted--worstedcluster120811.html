worsted

noun
smooth, hard woolen fabric
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːstɪd / (AmE), / ˈwʊstɪd / (BrE)
A smooth woolen fabric with a hard textured surface and no nap, woven from long-staple worsted yarns.

Examples

Worsted suit
He wore a finely tailored worsted suit to the meeting.
The fabric store carries a wide selection of worsted materials in various colors.
Traditional men's suits are often made of worsted for their durability and smooth texture.
