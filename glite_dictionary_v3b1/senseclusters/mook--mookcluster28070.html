mook

noun
magazine-book hybrid
Pronunciation: / muːk / (AmE), / muːk / (BrE)
a publication that combines features of a magazine and a book, often with in-depth articles and high-quality design

Examples

Art mook
The new art mook features interviews with famous painters and stunning photography.
She collects every travel mook she can find for inspiration on her next trip.
This science mook offers detailed articles that go far beyond what you find in regular magazines.
