tamanu

noun
Pacific tree or oil
Pronunciation: / təˈmɑːnuː / (AmE), / təˈmɑːnuː / (BrE)
a tropical tree native to the Pacific islands, or its oil used in cosmetics and traditional medicine

Examples

Tamanu oil benefits
Many natural skincare products contain tamanu for its moisturizing properties.
The oil extracted from tamanu seeds is prized in traditional medicine.
Farmers in the Pacific islands harvest tamanu for both wood and oil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: argan oil - oil from the argan tree, used in cosmetics
  • synonym: calophyllum - a genus of tropical trees including the tamanu tree
