roadmap

noun
detailed plan or guide
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈroʊd.mæp/ (AmE), /ˈrəʊd.mæp/ (BrE)
a detailed plan or guide that shows the steps needed to achieve a goal

Examples

Project roadmap
The manager shared a roadmap to help the team reach their sales targets this year.
Before starting the project, they created a roadmap outlining each important milestone.
A clear roadmap can make it easier to track progress and stay motivated.
The company presented a clear roadmap for expanding into international markets over the next five years.
Before starting the project, the manager shared a roadmap outlining each phase and its objectives.
A well-designed roadmap can help teams stay focused and measure their progress toward their goals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plan - a set of actions arranged to achieve something
  • synonym: strategy - a careful plan or method for achieving a goal
