Synonyms

  • permit
    2.1 17/m
    a permit is an official document for a specific activity, while a license is the general official permission or its document.
  • permission
    1.0 43/m
    permission is the general idea of being allowed to act, while a license is the formal permission or paper that shows it.
  • authorization
    4.4 5.01/m
    authorization is a formal approval used in official systems and documents, while a license is the public permission or its document.
  • authority
    3.1 17/m
    authority is the power or right to give approval, while a license is the official permission or document given to someone.
