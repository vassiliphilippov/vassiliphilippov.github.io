buildable

adjective
suitable for construction
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪldəbəl / (AmE), / ˈbɪldəbəl / (BrE)
suitable or able to be built on or for construction

Examples

Buildable land
The architect confirmed that the plot was buildable according to local regulations.
They searched for buildable land to construct their new home.
Due to environmental restrictions, not all areas of the property are buildable.
