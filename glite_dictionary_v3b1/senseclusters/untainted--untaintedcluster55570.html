untainted

adjective
not spoiled or damaged; pure
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈteɪntɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈteɪntɪd / (BrE)
not spoiled or damaged; not affected by anything bad, especially in regard to reputation or character.

Examples

Untainted reputation
Despite the rumors, her reputation remained untainted by scandal.
He prided himself on an untainted record after years of service.
Their support was crucial because their names were untainted by controversy.
