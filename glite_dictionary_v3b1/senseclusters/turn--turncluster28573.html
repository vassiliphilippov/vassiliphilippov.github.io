turn

verb
shape by rotating on a lathe or wheel
7.5 8.19/m
Pronunciation: / t…ùn / (AmE), / t…úÀên / (BrE)
to shape or form (an object, especially of wood or clay) by rotating it on a lathe or wheel and cutting or forming it as it moves

Examples

To turn wood
Apprentices learn how to turn chair legs into elegant shapes.
She turned a beautiful vase from a lump of clay.
The craftsman skillfully turned the wooden bowl on the lathe.

Synonyms

  • lathe
    7.8 0.00/m
    lathe means shaping material by rotating it on a machine and cutting it, while turn means shaping wood or clay on a wheel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carve - to cut and shape hard material
  • synonym: lathe - machine tool for shaping wood or other material by turning
