buffet

noun
self-service meal
2.8
Pronunciation: / bəˈfeɪ / (AmE), / ˈbʌfeɪ / (BrE)
a meal or restaurant where people serve themselves from a variety of dishes laid out on a table or counter

Examples

Hotel buffet

The hotel offers a large buffet breakfast every morning.
We enjoyed the wedding buffet with its wide selection of dishes.

The hotel offers a breakfast buffet with a wide selection of dishes.
We decided to have lunch at the new buffet around the corner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cafeteria - a restaurant where customers serve themselves or are served at a counter
  • synonym: restaurant - a place where people pay to sit and eat meals
  • synonym: smorgasbord - a meal with a variety of hot and cold dishes served buffet-style
