half-crazed

adjective
partially insane; nearly mad
2.3
Pronunciation: / hæf ˈkreɪzd / (AmE), / hɑːf ˈkreɪzd / (BrE)
Partially insane or mentally disturbed; driven almost to madness.

Examples

Half-crazed with fear
After searching for hours in the storm, she stumbled into the cabin, half-crazed with fear.
His half-crazed ranting worried everyone in the quiet village.
The prisoner escaped, half-crazed from days without sleep or food.
