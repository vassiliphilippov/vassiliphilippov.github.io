tighten

verb
make something tighter
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪtən /; / ˈtaɪtn̩ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪtən /; / ˈtaɪtn̩ / (BrE)
to make something tight or tighter, or to become tight or tighter (physically)

Examples

To tighten a screw
She used a wrench to tighten the bolt.

You should tighten your seatbelt before the ride starts.
As the weather grew colder, the skin on her hands began to tighten.
He pulled on the string and felt it tighten around the package.
The rules will tighten if too many people break them.
