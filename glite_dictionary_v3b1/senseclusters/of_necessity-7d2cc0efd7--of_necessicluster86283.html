of necessity

adverb
because it cannot be avoided
5.4
Pronunciation: / əv nəˈsɛsəti / (AmE), / əv nɪˈsɛsɪti / (BrE)
used to indicate that something happens because it cannot be avoided or is inevitable

Examples

Of necessity happens
His absence will, of necessity, affect the schedule.
Such changes are, of necessity, slow and gradual.
We are of necessity limited by the available resources.

Related Terms

  • synonym: necessarily - as a necessary result; inevitably
  • antonym: needlessly - without necessity; unnecessarily
