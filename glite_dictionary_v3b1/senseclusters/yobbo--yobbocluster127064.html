yobbo

noun
rowdy or aggressive young man
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈjɑːboʊ / (AmE), / ˈjɒbəʊ / (BrE)
a young man who behaves in a rowdy, loud, and often aggressive way; a lout

Examples

A yobbo causing trouble
A group of yobbos were shouting and throwing bottles outside the pub.
He was behaving like a real yobbo at the football match.
Nobody wants to live next door to a yobbo who parties all night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gentleman - a man of good manners and behavior
  • synonym: lout - an uncouth or aggressive man or boy
