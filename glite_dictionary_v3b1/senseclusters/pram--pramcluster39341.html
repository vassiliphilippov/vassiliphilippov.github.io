pram

noun
baby carriage (especially British)
4.5
Pronunciation: / præm / (AmE), / pram / (BrE)
a small vehicle with four wheels, used for carrying a baby while pushing it along

Examples

She pushed the sleeping baby in a pram through the park.
The old pram was passed down through three generations of the family.
