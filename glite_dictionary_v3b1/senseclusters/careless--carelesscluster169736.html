careless

adjective
effortless; unstudied in manner or style
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛrləs / (AmE), / ˈkeələs / (BrE)
Showing a natural ease or grace that appears effortless and unstudied.

Examples

Careless style
He struck a careless pose, exuding effortless sophistication.
Her careless laughter brought a relaxed charm to the dinner party.
The painting captures the careless elegance of a dancer lost in motion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: effortless - achieved with little or no effort
  • antonym: studied - deliberate or planned rather than spontaneous
