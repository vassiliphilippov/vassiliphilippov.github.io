pre-teaching

noun
introducing concepts early
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈtiːtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈtiːtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of introducing key concepts or vocabulary to students before a lesson begins

Examples

Pre-teaching of vocabulary
Effective pre-teaching of vocabulary can help students feel more confident during the lesson.
The teacher used pre-teaching strategies to introduce difficult science terms before the experiment.
Pre-teaching grammar points allows learners to focus on communication during class activities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: preview - an advance introduction or overview of material
  • antonym: review - to go over material again after it has been taught
