matchlock

noun
early firearm with a burning fuse ignition
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmætʃˌlɑk / (AmE), / ˈmætʃˌlɒk / (BrE)
an early type of firearm in which the gunpowder was ignited by a slow-burning match or fuse lowered into a touchhole
