grille

noun
small screened opening in a door or window
6.0
Pronunciation: / ɡrɪl / (AmE), / ɡrɪl / (BrE)
a framework of metal bars or mesh used as a protective barrier or screen in doors, windows, or as a small opening for communication

Examples

Window grille
A metal grille in the office door allows staff to communicate with visitors securely.
She slipped the paperwork through the narrow grille built into the reception window.
The teller spoke to me through the grille at the bank counter.
He looked at her through the window's protective grille.
The car's front grille was damaged in the accident.
The ornate grille on the mansion’s front door was made of wrought iron.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grate - framework of metal bars covering an opening
  • synonym: screen - a mesh or grating that covers an opening
