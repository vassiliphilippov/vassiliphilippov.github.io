unbuckle

verb
undo a buckle or fastening
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbʌk.əl / (AmE), / ʌnˈbʌk.əl / (BrE)
To undo or release a buckle, especially on clothing, a belt, or a strap.

Examples

To unbuckle a belt
Before stepping out of the car, please unbuckle your seat belt.
He struggled to unbuckle his backpack during the emergency.
She quickly unbuckled her shoes and kicked them off by the door.
