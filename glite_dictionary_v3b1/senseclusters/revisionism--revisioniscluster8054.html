revisionism

noun
moderate or evolutionary form of Marxism
3.5 7.2
Pronunciation: /riːv.ˈɪʒ.ən.ɪ.zəm/ /; / /rɪ.ˈvɪʒ.ən.ɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈvɪʒ.ən.ɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
