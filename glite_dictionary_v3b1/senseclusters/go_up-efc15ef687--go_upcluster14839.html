go up

verb
be built or constructed
4.0
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ ˈʌp / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ˈʌp / (BrE)
(of a building or structure) to be erected, built, or constructed.

Examples

A new building goes up
A fence went up around the construction site last week.
A new shopping mall is going up on the edge of town.
Several skyscrapers have gone up in downtown recently.
