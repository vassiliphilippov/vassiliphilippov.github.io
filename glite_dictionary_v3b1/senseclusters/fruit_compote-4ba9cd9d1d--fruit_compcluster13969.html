fruit compote

noun
dessert of stewed or baked fruit
7.5 3.3
Pronunciation: /fruːt ˈkɑːm.poʊt/ /; / /fruːt kəm.ˈpoʊt/ (AmE), /fruːt ˈkɒm.pəʊt/ /; / /fruːt kɒm.ˈpɒt/ (BrE)
A dessert made by stewing or baking pieces of fruit in sugar syrup, sometimes with added spices or liqueur.
