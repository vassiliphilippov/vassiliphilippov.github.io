nonsaturated

adjective
not fully saturated
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈsætʃəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈsætʃəreɪtɪd / (BrE)
not fully soaked, filled, or saturated with a substance

Examples

Nonsaturated soil
The nonsaturated soil allowed water to drain quickly after the rain.
A nonsaturated solution can still dissolve more salt if needed.
The air in the desert is typically nonsaturated, making it feel very dry.

Related Terms

  • antonym: saturated - completely soaked or filled with a substance
  • synonym: unsaturated - not holding as much of a substance as possible
