testicular vein

noun
vein that drains blood from the testicles
Pronunciation: / tɛˈstɪkjələr veɪn / (AmE), / tɛˈstɪkjʊlə veɪn / (BrE)
A vein that carries blood away from the testicles toward the heart.

Examples

A blockage in the testicular vein can lead to swelling and discomfort.
