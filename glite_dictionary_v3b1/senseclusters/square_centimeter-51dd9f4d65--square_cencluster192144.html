square centimeter

noun
unit of area in the metric system
1.3 1.9
Pronunciation: /skwer ˈsen.tɪ.miː.tər/ /; / /skwer ˈsen.tə.miː.tər/ (AmE), /skweə ˈsen.tɪ.miː.tə/ /; / /skweə ˈsen.tə.miː.tə/ (BrE)
a unit of area equal to a square that measures one centimeter on each side, used in the metric system
