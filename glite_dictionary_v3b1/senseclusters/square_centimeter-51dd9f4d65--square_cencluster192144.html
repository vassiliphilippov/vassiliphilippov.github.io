square centimeter

noun
unit of area in the metric system
Pronunciation: / skwɛr ˈsɛntɪˌmiːtər /; / skwɛr ˈsɛn.təˌmitɚ / (AmE), / skwɛː ˈsɛntɪmiːtə / (BrE)
a unit of area equal to a square that measures one centimeter on each side, used in the metric system

Examples

One square centimeter
Each tile is precisely one square centimeter in size, making calculation easy.
Her drawing covered only about four square centimeters of the notebook page.
The label on the package indicated an area of 20 square centimeters.
Bacteria can multiply rapidly on just one square centimetre of skin.
Each sticker measures only two square centimetres in size, making it quite small.
The room's surface area was calculated to be 24,000 square centimetres.
