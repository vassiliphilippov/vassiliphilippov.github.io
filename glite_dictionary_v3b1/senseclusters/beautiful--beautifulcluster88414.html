beautiful

adjective
of weather: highly enjoyable
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbjuːtəfəl / (AmE), / ˈbjuːtəfəl /; / ˈbjuːtəf(ə)l / (BrE)
very pleasing to the senses or mind; giving great pleasure, especially in appearance, sound, or thought

Examples

A beautiful day
It was a beautiful day for a picnic, with clear skies and warm sunshine.
The forecast promises beautiful conditions for the outdoor concert this evening.
We woke up to beautiful weather, perfect for a long walk in the park.
Many people consider the mountain landscape to be one of the most beautiful sights in the world.
She gave a beautiful performance that captivated the entire audience.
The garden looked especially beautiful after the rain, with every flower glistening in the light.
