succumb

verb
fail to resist, often fatally

to die or fail to resist an overwhelming force, pressure, illness, or temptation

Examples

To succumb to illness
After a long battle with cancer, he finally succumbed to the disease.
Despite her determination, she succumbed to exhaustion and had to rest.
Many plants succumb to drought when water is scarce for extended periods.
