histiocytic

adjective
relating to histiocytes
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪstiəˈsɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌhɪstiəˈsɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving histiocytes, which are a type of immune cell in the body

Examples

Histiocytic immune cells
The patient was diagnosed with a histiocytic disorder affecting the immune system.
Researchers studied the histiocytic response in tissue samples from the experiment.
A histiocytic infiltration was observed under the microscope during the examination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: macrophagic - relating to macrophages, another type of immune cell
