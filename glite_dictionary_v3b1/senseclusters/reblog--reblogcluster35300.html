reblog

verb
to share a post
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈblɔg / (AmE), / ˌriːˈblɒg / (BrE)
to share someone else's blog post or content on your own blog or social media account, usually with credit to the original source

Examples

To reblog a photo
If you like this recipe, feel free to reblog it so your followers can try it too.
Many users reblog interesting articles to keep their blogs active and engaging.
She was happy when her artwork was reblogged by a popular influencer.
She reblogged a funny cat video to her followers.
Many users reblog interesting articles to spread information quickly.
He reblogged the announcement so more people would see it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: original - the first or initial version of something
  • antonym: original post - the first version of a blog entry or social media post
  • synonym: retweet - to share someone else's tweet on your own Twitter account
  • synonym: share - to post something so others can see it
