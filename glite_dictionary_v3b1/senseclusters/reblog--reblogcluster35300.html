reblog

verb
to share a post
3.9
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈblɑːg/ /; / /rɪ.ˈblɑːg/ /; / /rə.ˈblɑːg/ (AmE), /riː.ˈblɒg/ /; / /rɪ.ˈblɒg/ /; / /rə.ˈblɒg/ (BrE)
to share someone else's blog post or content on your own blog or social media account, usually with credit to the original source

Examples
