remount

verb
provide with fresh horses
2.1
Pronunciation: / riˈmaʊnt / (AmE), / rɪˈmaʊnt / (BrE)
to provide with fresh horses, especially to replace those that are tired or unfit for service

Examples

To remount the cavalry
After the battle, they worked quickly to remount the entire unit with strong, fresh horses.
Supplies arrived to remount the exhausted regiment before the next advance.
The cavalry officer needed to remount his troops after a long day's march.
