influential

adjective
having great influence or power
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnfluˈɛnʃəl / (AmE), / ˌɪnflʊˈɛnʃəl / (BrE)
having great effect on someone or something; able to change or affect decisions, behavior, or events

Examples

An influential article
His influential article changed the way experts think about this subject.
She became an influential leader in her community after years of dedication.
The committee included several influential members who shaped the final decision.

Related Terms

  • antonym: insignificant - having little or no importance or effect
  • synonym: powerful - having great power or strength
