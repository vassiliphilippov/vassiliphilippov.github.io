George Bryan Brummell

proper noun
English fashion icon and dandy, Regency era
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔrdʒ ˈbraɪ.ən ˈbrʌm.əl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːdʒ ˈbraɪ.ən ˈbrʌm.əl / (BrE)
(1778–1840) An English dandy and fashion leader during the Regency era, noted for his influence on men's dress and social etiquette.

Examples

George Bryan Brummell fashion leader
George Bryan Brummell was known for his impeccable sense of style and wit in London society.
Many attribute the rise of the modern suit to the influence of George Bryan Brummell.
The legacy of George Bryan Brummell endures in men's fashion to this day.
