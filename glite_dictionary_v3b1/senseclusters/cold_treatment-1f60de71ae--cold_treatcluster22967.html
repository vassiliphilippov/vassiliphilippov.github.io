cold treatment

noun
the use of cold to treat pain or swelling
2.2
Pronunciation: / koʊld ˈtritmənt / (AmE), / kəʊld ˈtriːtmənt / (BrE)
a therapeutic method involving the application of cold to the body, typically to reduce pain or swelling

Examples

Cold treatment with ice
A simple ice pack can serve as an effective cold treatment for inflammation.
After the marathon, he applied a cold treatment to his injured knee.
Doctors recommend cold treatment as a first step for minor sprains or strains.
