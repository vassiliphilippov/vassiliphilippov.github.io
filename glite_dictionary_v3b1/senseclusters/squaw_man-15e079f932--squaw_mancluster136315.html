squaw man

noun
offensive: white man married to a Native American woman
9.9
Pronunciation: / skwɑː mæn /; / skwɑ mæn / (AmE), / skwɔː mæn / (BrE)
(offensive, dated) a white man married to a Native American woman (sometimes also used for men who support women's or Native American rights)
