Synonyms

  • downward-sloping
    4.1 0.00/m
    downward-sloping describes a surface that leans noticeably downward, while downhill names a slope, often steep and used in walking or skiing.
  • descending
    3.2 0.00/m
    descending describes something moving or falling toward a lower level, while downhill describes a sloping surface rather than motion.
  • downward
    1.0 2/m
    downward means toward a lower place or level in general, whereas downhill specifically refers to a sloping surface or direction.
