silkworm moth

noun
moth species producing silk
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlkˌwɜrm mɔθ /; / ˈsɪlkˌwɝm mɔθ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlkˌwɜːm mɒθ / (BrE)
