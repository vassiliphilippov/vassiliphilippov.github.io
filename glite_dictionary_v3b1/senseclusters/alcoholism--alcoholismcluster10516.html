alcoholism

noun
addiction to alcohol
2.3 3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈæl.kə.hɔː.lɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈæl.kə.hɒ.lɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
a chronic addiction to alcohol, where a person cannot control their drinking and it causes health or social problems
