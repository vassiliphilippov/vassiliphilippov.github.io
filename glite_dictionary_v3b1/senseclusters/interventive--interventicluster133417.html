interventive

adjective
involving intervention
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈvɛntɪv / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈvɛntɪv / (BrE)
describing an action, study, or strategy that deliberately introduces a treatment or measure to cause and assess change, especially in general, medical, or therapeutic situations
