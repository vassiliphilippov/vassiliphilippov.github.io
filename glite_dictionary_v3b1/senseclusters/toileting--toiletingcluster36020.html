toileting

noun
toilet facility management
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔɪlɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɔɪlɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the provision, management, or use of toilets and sanitation facilities, especially in public health or building contexts

Examples
