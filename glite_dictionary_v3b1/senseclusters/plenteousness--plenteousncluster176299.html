plenteousness

noun
state of being plentiful or abundant
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈplɛn.ti.əs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈplɛn.ti.əs.nəs / (BrE)

Examples

The plenteousness of the harvest ensured that no one went hungry that year.
We were grateful for the plenteousness of resources in the new land.
