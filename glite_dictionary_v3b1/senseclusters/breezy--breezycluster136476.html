breezy

adjective
cheerful and lively in manner
2.8 1/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàbriÀêzi / (AmE), / ÀàbriÀêzi / (BrE)
cheerful, lively, and refreshing in manner or style; having an easy and animated disposition.

Examples

Breezy attitude
He delivered the presentation in a breezy and confident style.
Her breezy manner made everyone feel at ease during the interview.
The author's breezy writing makes even complex topics seem accessible.
