scrubbed

adjective
cleaned by scrubbing
Pronunciation: / skrʌbd / (AmE), / skrʌbd / (BrE)
made clean by scrubbing, especially by rubbing hard with a brush and water or other cleaning agent.

Examples

A scrubbed floor
After an hour of hard work, the floor was finally scrubbed and gleaming.
She arrived with a scrubbed face, ready for the interview.
The scrubbed kitchen surfaces sparkled under the bright lights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cleaned - made clean, made free from dirt or mess
  • antonym: dirty - not clean, soiled
