scurvy grass

noun
a vitamin C-rich Arctic cress plant
Pronunciation: / ˈskɝːvi ɡræs / (AmE), / ˈskɜːvi ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a small, herbaceous plant of the cress family, found in northern regions, formerly valued for its high vitamin C content in preventing or treating scurvy
