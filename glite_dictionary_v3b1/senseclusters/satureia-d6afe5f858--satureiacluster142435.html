Satureia

noun
genus of aromatic herbs (savory)
Pronunciation: / sæˈtʊəriə / (AmE), / sæˈtjʊəriə / (BrE)
A genus of aromatic herbs of the mint family, including the savory plants used in cooking.

Examples

Satureia aromatic herbs
Botanists often refer to both summer and winter savory by the genus name Satureia.
Some herbal remedies use extracts from the Satureia species.
The chef added Satureia to the stew for a robust, peppery flavor.
