bo tree

noun
sacred fig tree of India (Ficus religiosa)
9.9 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / bo ä ÀåtriÀê / (AmE), / b…ô ä ÀåtriÀê / (BrE)
A sacred fig tree of India (Ficus religiosa), known for its great size and longevity, especially revered in Buddhism.

Examples

A bo tree in temple
A bo tree can live for centuries, often becoming the centerpiece of temple courtyards.
Many pilgrims travel to India to meditate beneath the ancient bo tree where the Buddha is believed to have reached enlightenment.
The leaves of the bo tree are heart-shaped and rustle gently in the breeze.
