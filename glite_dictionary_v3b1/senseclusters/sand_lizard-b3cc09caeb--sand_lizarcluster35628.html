sand lizard

noun
a lizard from arid western U.S. regions
Pronunciation: / sænd ˈlɪz.ɚd /; / sænd ˈlɪzərd / (AmE), / sænd ˈlɪz.əd /; / sand ˈlɪzəd / (BrE)
a type of lizard that lives in sandy or dry areas, known for its sandy color and adaptation to such habitats
