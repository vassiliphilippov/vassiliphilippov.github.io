atheist

noun
person who does not believe in a god
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪθiɪst / (AmE), / ˈeɪθiɪst / (BrE)
A person who does not believe in the existence of a god or gods.

Examples

An atheist thinker
As an atheist, she often participates in discussions about religion and philosophy.
Despite being raised in a religious family, he became an atheist in his twenties.
Many atheists advocate for the separation of church and state.

Related Terms

  • antonym: agnostic - person who believes the existence of God is unknown or unknowable
  • antonym: believer - person who believes in the existence of a god or gods
