wall mural

noun
decorative artwork applied to a wall
3.2 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / w…îl Ààmj är…ôl / (AmE), / w…îÀêl Ààmj ä…ôr…ôl / (BrE)
A large decorative artwork, often painted or printed, applied directly to a wall to enhance its appearance.

Examples

A wall mural painting
A colorful wall mural can make a small space feel more vibrant and dynamic.
The living room was transformed by a striking wall mural depicting a forest scene.
They hired an artist to paint a custom wall mural in the nursery.

Synonyms

  • mural
    3.5 7.28/m
    mural is a general term for artwork on walls or ceilings, while wall mural specifies artwork placed only on a wall.
  • wall painting
    2.7 0.26/m
    wall painting is a plain term for a painting done on a wall, while wall mural suggests a large decorative artwork covering part of a wall.
