refugee

noun
person fleeing from danger or persecution
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛfjəˈdʒi / (AmE), / ˌrɛfjʊˈdʒiː / (BrE)
a person who has been forced to leave their country in order to escape war, persecution, or disaster

Examples

A refugee from war
As a child, he arrived as a refugee with little more than hope for a better life.
The country provided shelter to refugees fleeing war and violence.
Thousands of refugees crossed the border in search of safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: asylum seeker - a person who has left their home country and is seeking protection as a refugee
