underskin

noun
subcutaneous bump
8.6
a small lump, bruise, or lesion located beneath the surface of the skin and felt as a bump

Examples

A small underskin bump
After the injection she noticed a tender underskin that remained for several days.
The doctor examined the underskin to check for signs of infection.
He felt a small underskin on his arm after bumping into the table.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bruise - an injury causing discoloration under the skin
  • synonym: nodule - a small swelling or lump under the skin
