timidly

adverb
in a shy or fearful manner

In a shy or lacking-in-confidence manner; showing nervousness or fear.

Examples

To timidly knock

She spoke timidly at the meeting, barely raising her voice above a whisper.
The kitten approached the new toy timidly, unsure if it was safe to play with.
