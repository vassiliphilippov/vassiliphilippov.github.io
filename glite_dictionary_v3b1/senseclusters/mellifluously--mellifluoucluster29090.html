mellifluously

adverb
smoothly and sweetly
Pronunciation: / məˈlɪfluəsli / (AmE), / məˈlɪfluəsli /; / məˈlɪflʊəsli / (BrE)
in a smooth, sweet, and pleasant-sounding way

Examples

To speak mellifluously
The narrator read the story mellifluously, captivating everyone in the room.
Birds sang mellifluously outside her window as she woke up.
He spoke mellifluously, making even difficult topics sound beautiful.
The singer's voice flowed mellifluously through the concert hall, enchanting the audience.
He spoke so mellifluously that everyone listened in rapt attention.
The poem was read mellifluously, making the words sound even more beautiful.

Related Terms

  • antonym: harshly - in a rough or unpleasant-sounding way
  • synonym: sweetly - in a pleasant or melodious way
