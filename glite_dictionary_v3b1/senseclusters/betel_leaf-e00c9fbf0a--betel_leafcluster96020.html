betel leaf

noun
leaf of the betel pepper vine
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtəl liːf / (AmE), / ˈbiːtəl liːf / (BrE)
The leaf of the betel pepper vine, commonly used in Asia for chewing with areca nut and sometimes tobacco.

Examples

A betel leaf with areca nut
In many Indian households, guests are offered a betel leaf with areca nut after a meal.
She bought fresh betel leaves from the market to prepare a festive snack.
The betel leaf is often used in traditional ceremonies throughout Southeast Asia.

Related Terms

  • synonym: betel - a plant whose leaves are chewed with areca nut
