battle royal

noun
noisy, disorderly fight; free-for-all
Pronunciation: / ˌbætəl ˈrɔɪəl / (AmE), / ˌbætl ˈrɔɪəl / (BrE)
A noisy, disorderly fight involving several people or groups.

Examples

A loud battle royal
The debate turned into a battle royal when everyone started shouting at once.
The meeting erupted into a battle royal over the new policy proposal.
There was a battle royal in the bar after the game ended in a draw.
