pseudomilitary

adjective
military-like
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːdoʊˈmɪlɪˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˌsjuːdəʊˈmɪlɪtəri / (BrE)
resembling or imitating the military in appearance or style but not actually military

Examples

Pseudomilitary uniforms
The organization used pseudomilitary uniforms to create a sense of discipline among its members.
Their pseudomilitary drills impressed some people but worried the local authorities.
The club's pseudomilitary structure included ranks and strict rules, though it was not an actual army.
