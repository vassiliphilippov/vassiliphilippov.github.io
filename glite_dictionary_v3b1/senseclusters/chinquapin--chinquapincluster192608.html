chinquapin

noun
chestnut tree with edible nuts
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪŋ.kəˌpɪn /; / ˈtʃɪŋkəˌpɪn / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪŋ.kwəˌpɪn /; / ˈtʃɪŋkəˌpɪn / (BrE)
a small tree or shrub of the chestnut or beech family, native to the southeastern United States, that produces edible nuts
