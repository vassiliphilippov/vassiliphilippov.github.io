MOC

noun
custom LEGO model
9.3
Pronunciation: / ɛm oʊ si / (AmE), / ɛm əʊ si / (BrE)
a custom-built model, especially a LEGO creation, made by an individual hobbyist rather than from an official set

Examples

LEGO MOC build
He spent weeks designing his latest MOC for the online LEGO competition.
The community praised her detailed MOC of a medieval village.
At the convention, dozens of unique MOCs were displayed by talented builders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: model - a small copy or replica of something
  • antonym: set - an official collection of LEGO pieces sold together
