terror-stricken

adjective
overcome with terror or intense fear
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛrər ˌstrɪkən / (AmE), / ˈtɛr.ər ˌstrɪk.ən / (BrE)
suddenly experiencing intense fear or panic; filled with terror

Examples

terror-stricken face
His terror-stricken face showed how much the ordeal had shaken him.
She was terror-stricken when she heard the screams coming from upstairs.
The terror-stricken villagers ran from their homes seeking safety.

  • synonym: terrified - extremely frightened
