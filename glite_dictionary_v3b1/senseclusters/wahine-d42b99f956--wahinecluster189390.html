Wahine

proper noun
New Zealand ferry disaster
Pronunciation: / wɑːˈhiːni / (AmE), / wɑːˈhiːni / (BrE)
the name of a New Zealand ferry that sank in Wellington Harbour in 1968 in a major maritime disaster

Examples
