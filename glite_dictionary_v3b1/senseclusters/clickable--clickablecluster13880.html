clickable

adjective
able to be clicked
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪkəbəl / (AmE), / ˈklɪkəbəl / (BrE)
able to be selected or activated by clicking, especially using a mouse or similar device

Examples

Clickable link
All clickable links on the website are highlighted in blue.
The button is not clickable until you fill out the form.
Icons that are clickable usually change color when you hover over them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interactive - allowing user interaction or response
  • synonym: selectable - able to be chosen or selected
  • antonym: static - not changing or responding to user actions
