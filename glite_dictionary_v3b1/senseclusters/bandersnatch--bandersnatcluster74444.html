bandersnatch

noun
imaginary creature
Pronunciation: / ˈbændərˌsnætʃ / (AmE), / ˈbændəsnætʃ / (BrE)
a fictional creature invented by Lewis Carroll, often used to refer to something strange or imaginary

Examples

A bandersnatch creature
In the fantasy novel, a bandersnatch guarded the entrance to the hidden cave.
The children imagined a bandersnatch living under their bed at night.
He described his wild idea as chasing a bandersnatch through a dream.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chimera - an imaginary monster or a thing that is hoped for but impossible
  • synonym: jabberwock - a fictional monster from Lewis Carroll's 'Jabberwocky'
