cockeyed

adjective
absurd or wildly unreasonable
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑkˌaɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɒkaɪd / (BrE)
(of an idea, plan, or suggestion) foolish or wildly unreasonable; absurd.

Examples

Cockeyed idea
He came up with some cockeyed explanation that nobody believed.
It’s a cockeyed idea to build a swimming pool on the roof.
That’s a cockeyed scheme; it would never work in practice.
