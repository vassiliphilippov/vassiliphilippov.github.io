blue

adjective
sad or causing sadness; gloomy
Pronunciation: / bluː / (AmE), / bluː / (BrE)
feeling sad, depressed, or causing feelings of sadness; gloomy or melancholic

Examples

Feeling blue
I've been feeling blue ever since my friend moved away.
She listened to blue tunes whenever she was feeling down.
The rainy, gray afternoon put me in a blue mood.
After hearing the news, she felt blue for the rest of the day.
He always gets a little blue during the long winter months.
Whenever I'm blue, listening to music helps lift my mood.
