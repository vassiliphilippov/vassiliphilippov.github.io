gittern

noun
historical stringed instrument like a guitar
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪtərn / (AmE), / ˈɡɪtən / (BrE)
a stringed musical instrument of the Middle Ages and Renaissance, resembling a guitar and typically having a pear-shaped body and wire strings

Examples

A gittern instrument
She studied medieval music and learned to play the gittern as part of her research.
The museum displayed a rare gittern dating from the Renaissance period.
Unlike the modern guitar, the gittern often had wire strings and a rounded back.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guitar - a modern stringed musical instrument with a long neck and flat body
