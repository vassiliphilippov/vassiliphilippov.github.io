Christmas flower

noun
poinsettia; plant with red leaves used at Christmas
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪs·məs ˌflaʊ·ər / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs·məs ˌflaʊ·ə / (BrE)
A tropical American plant, especially the poinsettia, with bright red petal-like leaves and small yellow flowers, commonly associated with Christmas decorations.

Examples

Red Christmas flower
Every December, stores fill their windows with vibrant red Christmas flowers.
She received a potted Christmas flower as a gift from her neighbor.
We placed a beautiful Christmas flower beside the fireplace for the holiday season.
