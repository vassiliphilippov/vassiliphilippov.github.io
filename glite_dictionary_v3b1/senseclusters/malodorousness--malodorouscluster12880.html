malodorousness

noun
quality of having a bad smell
Pronunciation: / ˌmæˈloʊdərəsnəs / (AmE), / ˌmæˈləʊdərəsnɪs / (BrE)
the characteristic or state of having a strong, unpleasant smell

Examples

Strong malodorousness
