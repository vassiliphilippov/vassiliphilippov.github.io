disidentify

verb
to make anonymous
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsaɪˈdɛntɪfaɪ / (AmE), / ˌdɪsaɪˈdɛntɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to remove identifying information or features from something, especially to make data or records anonymous

Examples

To disidentify data
Researchers must disidentify all personal data before sharing it with other institutions.
The hospital policy requires staff to disidentify patient records for privacy reasons.
To protect user privacy, the company disidentifies sensitive information in its reports.

Related Terms

  • antonym: identify - to recognize or establish the identity of something
