lobsterwoman

noun
female lobster catcher
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːbstərˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈlɒbstəˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman whose occupation is catching lobsters

Examples

A lobsterwoman at work
As a lobsterwoman, she faces many challenges at sea, especially during stormy weather.
She has worked as a lobsterwoman along the rugged coastline for over twenty years.
The local lobsterwoman offers fresh catch to restaurants every morning.
