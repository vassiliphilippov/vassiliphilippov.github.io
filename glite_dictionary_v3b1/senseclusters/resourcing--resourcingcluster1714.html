resourcing

verb
to provide resources
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsɔrsɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈzɔːsɪŋ / (BrE)
to provide someone or something with the necessary resources, or to obtain and allocate resources for a task or project.

Examples

To resource a team
The company is resourcing the new project by bringing in additional staff and equipment.
Effective resourcing is essential to ensure the success of large-scale construction projects.
They spent weeks resourcing the event to make sure everything would run smoothly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: funding - to provide money for a project or activity
  • synonym: supplying - to provide what is needed
  • antonym: withholding - to refuse to give something that is needed
