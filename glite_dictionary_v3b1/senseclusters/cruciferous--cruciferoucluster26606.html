cruciferous

adjective
of the cabbage family of plants
9.3
Pronunciation: / kruˈsɪfərəs / (AmE), / kruˈsɪfərəs / (BrE)
relating to or belonging to the plant family Cruciferae (Brassicaceae), which includes vegetables such as cabbage, broccoli, and cauliflower

Examples

Cruciferous vegetables
Broccoli, a cruciferous plant, is rich in vitamins and dietary fiber.
Kale is a highly nutritious cruciferous vegetable popular in health-conscious diets.
Many studies link cruciferous vegetables to a reduced risk of certain cancers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brassicaceous - relating to the plant family Brassicaceae (same as Cruciferae)
