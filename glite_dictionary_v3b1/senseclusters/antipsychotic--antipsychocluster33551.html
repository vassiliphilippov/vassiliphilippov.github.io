A drug used to treat psychotic disorders by reducing symptoms such as delusions and hallucinations; often has a calming effect.

Examples

An antipsychotic drug
Many antipsychotic medications can cause side effects such as drowsiness or weight gain.
She responded well to the new antipsychotic after her symptoms became more severe.
The psychiatrist prescribed an antipsychotic to help manage his hallucinations.

Synonyms

  • antipsychotic agent
    5.6 0.00/m
    antipsychotic agent is a formal medical term used in papers, while antipsychotic is the common, everyday name.
  • neuroleptic
    8.7 0.01/m
    neuroleptic is a medical term that often emphasizes neurological side effects, while antipsychotic is the broader treatment name.
  • major tranquilizer
    6.6 0.00/m
    major tranquilizer is an older phrase that stresses strong calming and sedation, while antipsychotic is the standard clinical term.
