nark

noun
police informer or spy
8.4
Pronunciation: / nɑrk / (AmE), / nɑːk / (BrE)
a person who informs the police about criminal activities; an informer or spy for the police

Examples

Police nark
Sam turned out to be a nark who told the police about the gang's plans.
The criminals were caught after a nark provided a tip-off to the police.
They suspected there was a nark in the group leaking information to the authorities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grass - a British informal term for a police informer
  • synonym: mole - a spy who infiltrates an organization, often passing information to the police
  • synonym: snitch - a person who informs authorities, especially police, about others
