handywoman

noun
woman skilled in repairs or odd jobs
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhændiˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈhændiˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who is skilled at a variety of repair, maintenance, or odd jobs, especially around the home

Examples

A handywoman repairs things
As a skilled handywoman, she could repair almost anything around the house.
The community center called their trusted handywoman for help with the broken shelves.
We hired a local handywoman to fix the leaky faucet and paint the hallway.
