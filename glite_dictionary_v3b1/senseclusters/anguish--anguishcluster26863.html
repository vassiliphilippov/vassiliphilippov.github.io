Examples

To anguish over loss
It would anguish me to let my friends down in such a critical moment.
The thought of losing her family deeply anguished her for months.
The unfair accusations anguished him to the point of sleepless nights.
He continued to anguish over the decision for weeks, unable to find peace.
The loss of her friend caused her to anguish night after night.
Watching the disaster unfold, he could only anguish helplessly from afar.
