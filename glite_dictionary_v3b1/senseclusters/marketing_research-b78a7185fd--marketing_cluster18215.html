marketing research

noun
gathering and analyzing market information
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrkətɪŋ rɪˈsɝtʃ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪtɪŋ rɪˈsɜːtʃ / (BrE)
the process of gathering, analyzing, and interpreting information about a market, including information about the target market, consumers, competitors, and the effectiveness of marketing strategies

Examples

Accurate marketing research
Accurate marketing research can help businesses identify new opportunities and reduce risks.
She works in marketing research, analyzing data to advise companies on advertising strategies.
The company invested in marketing research to better understand customer needs before launching the new product.
