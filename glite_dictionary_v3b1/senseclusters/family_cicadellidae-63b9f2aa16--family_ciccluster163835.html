family Cicadellidae

noun
family of leafhoppers (insects)
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli sɪˌkædəˈlɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæməli sɪˌkadɪˈlɪdiː / (BrE)
A large family of small, jumping insects known as leafhoppers, many of which are important agricultural pests.

The family Cicadellidae insects
Entomologists study the family Cicadellidae to better understand agricultural pest management.

There are over 20,000 species in the family Cicadellidae found around the world.
