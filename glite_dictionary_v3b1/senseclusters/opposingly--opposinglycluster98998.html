opposingly

adverb
in an opposing way
6.2
Pronunciation: / əˈpɑzɪŋli / (AmE), / əˈpɒzɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows opposition or acts against something

Examples

Argue opposingly
The committee members argued opposingly about the proposed changes to the policy.
She looked opposingly at the new rules, clearly disagreeing with them.
The two candidates stood opposingly on the main issues during the debate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adversely - in a way that is harmful or opposed
  • antonym: supportively - in a way that gives support or approval
