gasworks

noun
place where coal gas is manufactured
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæsˌwɝks / (AmE), / ˈɡasˌwɜːks / (BrE)
a place where coal gas is produced, especially for lighting and heating in towns and cities; typically an industrial facility from the 19th or early 20th century

Examples

Old gasworks plant
Many cities once relied on gasworks to provide lighting for streets and homes.
The old gasworks have been converted into a public park.
You can still see the brick chimneys from the former gasworks in the industrial district.
