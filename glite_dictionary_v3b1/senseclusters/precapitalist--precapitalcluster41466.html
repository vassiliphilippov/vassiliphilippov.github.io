precapitalist

adjective
before capitalism
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈkæpɪtəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈkæpɪtəlɪst / (BrE)
existing or occurring before the development or rise of capitalism

Examples

Precapitalist society
Anthropologists study precapitalist societies to understand how economies functioned before modern markets.
The novel is set in a precapitalist village where trade is based on barter and tradition.
Many precapitalist cultures had communal land ownership rather than private property.

Related Terms

  • antonym: capitalist - relating to or characteristic of capitalism
  • synonym: feudal - relating to the social system before capitalism, based on land ownership and service
