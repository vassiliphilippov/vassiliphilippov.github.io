rebid

noun
second offer
5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈriː.bɪd/ /; / /riː.ˈbɪd/ (AmE), /ˈriː.bɪd/ /; / /riː.ˈbɪd/ (BrE)
a second or later offer made after an initial bid, especially in auctions or card games

Examples

A higher rebid
After losing the first round, she made a higher rebid to win the painting.
In bridge, a rebid can show additional strength in a player's hand.
The auctioneer called for a rebid when the initial offers were too low.
