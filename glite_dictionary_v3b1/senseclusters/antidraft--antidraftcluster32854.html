antidraft

noun
draught excluder
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈæntiˌdræft / (AmE), / ˈæntiˌdrɑːft / (BrE)
a device or material used to block unwanted air currents from entering a room, especially under doors or windows

Examples

Door antidraft
She placed an antidraft at the bottom of the door to keep the room warm.
During winter, many people use an antidraft to stop cold air from coming in.
The old house felt much cozier after we installed an antidraft along the window sill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: draught excluder - a strip or device used to prevent cold air from entering a room
