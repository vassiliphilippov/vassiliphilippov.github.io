pyramidal

adjective
having the shape of a pyramid
4.4
Pronunciation: / pɪˈræmɪdəl / (AmE), / pɪˈræmɪdəl / (BrE)
having the shape or form of a pyramid; resembling a pyramid in structure or appearance.

Examples

A pyramidal shape
A pyramidal monument stood in the center of the ancient plaza.
He arranged the oranges in a neat pyramidal pile on the table.
The crystal has a distinct pyramidal shape visible under the microscope.
