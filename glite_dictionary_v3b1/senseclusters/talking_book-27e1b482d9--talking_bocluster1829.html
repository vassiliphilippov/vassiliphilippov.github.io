talking book

noun
audio recording of a book being read aloud
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔkɪŋ ˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈtɔːkɪŋ ˌbʊk / (BrE)
a recording of a book being read aloud, typically intended for people who are visually impaired or prefer listening to books
