Sanka

proper noun
decaf coffee brand
Pronunciation: / ˈsæŋkə / (AmE), / ˈsaŋkə / (BrE)
a brand of instant decaffeinated coffee, sometimes used to refer to any decaf coffee

Examples

A cup of Sanka
She ordered a cup of Sanka with her breakfast at the diner.
After dinner, he offered his guests Sanka instead of regular coffee.
Many people choose Sanka when they want coffee without the caffeine.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: coffee - a drink made from roasted coffee beans
  • synonym: decaf - coffee with most of the caffeine removed
