ready-made

adjective
(of phrase/idea) unoriginal, often repeated
6.3 3.8
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛdiˈmeɪd / (AmE), / ˌrɛdiˈmeɪd / (BrE)
(of a phrase, remark, idea, etc.) repeated so often as to lack originality or thought.

Examples

The review criticized the novel for its use of ready-made characters and predictable storyline.
