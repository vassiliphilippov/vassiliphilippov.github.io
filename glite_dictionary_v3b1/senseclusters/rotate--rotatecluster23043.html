Synonyms

  • turn
    1.0 238/m
    turn is a common, general action to change direction or face, while rotate describes steady circular spinning around an axis.
  • revolve
    3.4 7.27/m
    revolve is regular movement along a path around another object, while rotate means spinning around its own central axis.
  • swivel
    4.7 3.51/m
    swivel is a quick or free turn on a joint like a chair, while rotate means smooth continuous spinning around an axis.
  • pivot
    4.2 1.28/m
    pivot is turning mainly at a fixed point to change direction, while rotate is circular spinning around an axis.
  • go around
    2.0 2.50/m
    go around is an informal phrase for moving in circles or visiting places, while rotate means to spin around an axis.
