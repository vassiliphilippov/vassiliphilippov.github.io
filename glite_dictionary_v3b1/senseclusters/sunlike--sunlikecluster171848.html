sunlike

adjective
warm and radiant
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having a warm, radiant, or cheerful quality, especially resembling the sun in brightness or positivity

Examples

Sunlike smile
Her sunlike smile warmed everyone and made the long meeting feel much brighter.
The teacher's sunlike personality made students feel welcome every morning.
He brought a sunlike energy to the team, lifting everyone's spirits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheerful - noticeably happy and optimistic
  • antonym: gloomy - dark or lacking in cheerfulness
  • synonym: radiant - shining brightly or full of happiness
