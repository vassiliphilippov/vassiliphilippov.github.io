dead

adverb
completely; as an informal intensifier
Pronunciation: / dɛd / (AmE), / dɛd / (BrE)
(informal, chiefly British) Completely; as an intensifier meaning absolutely or thoroughly.

Examples

Dead right
I'm dead tired after working all day.
She was dead sure she had left her keys on the table.
You were dead right about the weather forecast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absolutely - completely; used for emphasis
  • antonym: partly - not completely; only in part
  • synonym: utterly - completely; with great intensity
