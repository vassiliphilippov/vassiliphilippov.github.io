poison pea

noun
Australian poisonous shrub of genus Gastrolobium

any of several Australian evergreen shrubs of the genus Gastrolobium, noted for their whorled compound leaves that are poisonous to livestock and their showy yellow to deep reddish-orange flowers, followed by two-seeded pods.

Examples

Poison pea plants
