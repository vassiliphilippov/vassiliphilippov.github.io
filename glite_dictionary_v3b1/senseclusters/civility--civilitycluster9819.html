civility

noun
formal politeness or courtesy
5.5
Pronunciation: / səˈvɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / sɪˈvɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
polite or respectful behavior or speech

Examples

Show civility to others
Despite their disagreement, both candidates treated each other with civility during the debate.
Good civility is important in maintaining a positive atmosphere at work.
She greeted him with the bare minimum of civility necessary to be polite.
A little civility goes a long way in customer service.
Despite their differences, the debate was marked by civility and mutual respect.
She always treats her colleagues with civility, even in stressful situations.

  • antonym: rudeness - lack of manners or politeness; impolite behavior
