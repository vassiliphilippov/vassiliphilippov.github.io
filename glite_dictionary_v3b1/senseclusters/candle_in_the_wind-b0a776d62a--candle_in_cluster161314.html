candle in the wind

noun
someone fragile in tough circumstances
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkændl ɪn ðə wɪnd / (AmE), / ˈkændl ɪn ðə wɪnd / (BrE)
a person or thing that is fragile and easily affected by difficult or challenging circumstances

Examples

A candle in the wind
After the loss of her job, she was like a candle in the wind, uncertain and fragile.
During her illness, his mother appeared as a candle in the wind, struggling to stay strong.
The young prince seemed a candle in the wind amid the pressures of the court.
