infantilizer approach

noun
method of treating someone as a child
8.3
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfæntəˌlaɪzər əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / ɪnˈfæntəlʌɪzə əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a method or practice of treating someone as if they are a child, often inappropriately or unnecessarily
