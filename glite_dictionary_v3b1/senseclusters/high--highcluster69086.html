high

adjective
(of meat) smelling spoiled or tainted
Pronunciation: / haɪ / (AmE), / haɪ / (BrE)
(of meat) having a strong, unpleasant smell indicating spoilage or taint.

Examples

High meat smell
I could tell by the high odor in the kitchen that the meat had spoiled.
Some people used to prefer game meat just slightly high for its stronger flavor.
The venison was left too long and soon became high and unfit to eat.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fresh - (of meat) not spoiled, recently slaughtered or prepared
