nondelinquent

noun
not overdue or delinquent
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.dɪˈlɪŋkwənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.dɪˈlɪŋkwənt / (BrE)
a person, account, or case that is not overdue or involved in delinquent behavior, especially regarding payments or obligations

Examples

A nondelinquent account holder
The bank rewarded each nondelinquent with a lower interest rate on their loan.
As a nondelinquent, he maintained a perfect record of on-time payments for years.
Only nondelinquent accounts are eligible for the new customer loyalty program.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compliant - following rules or meeting required standards
  • antonym: delinquent - a person or account that is overdue or involved in wrongdoing
