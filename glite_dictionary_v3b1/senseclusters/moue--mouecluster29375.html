moue

noun
disdainful or pouting expression
9.9 0.08/m
Pronunciation: / muÀê / (AmE), / muÀê / (BrE)
A pouting or disdainful expression made with the lips; a grimace showing displeasure or annoyance.

Examples

A moue of displeasure
His attempt at an apology was met with a dismissive moue.
She made a small moue of disapproval when she heard the news.
With a perfectly practiced moue, the actress turned away from the camera.
