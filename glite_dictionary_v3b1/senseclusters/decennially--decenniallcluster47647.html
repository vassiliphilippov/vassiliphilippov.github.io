decennially

adverb
every ten years
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɛniəli / (AmE), / dɪˈsɛniəli / (BrE)
every ten years or once in a period of ten years

Examples

Reviewed decennially
The government reviews its long-term policies decennially to ensure they remain effective.
Major infrastructure projects are planned decennially to accommodate population growth.
The organization holds a special conference decennially to celebrate its achievements.
