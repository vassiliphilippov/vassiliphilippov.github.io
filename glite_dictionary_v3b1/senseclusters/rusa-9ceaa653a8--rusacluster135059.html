Rusa

proper noun
Urartian king's name
Pronunciation: / ˈruːsə / (AmE), / ˈruːsə / (BrE)
a personal name of several kings who ruled the ancient kingdom of Urartu in the Armenian Highlands during the 9th to 7th centuries BCE

Examples

Rusa I king
The ancient city walls were built during the reign of Rusa I.
Historians believe that Rusa II expanded Urartu's territory significantly.
A statue of King Rusa was discovered near Lake Van by archaeologists.
