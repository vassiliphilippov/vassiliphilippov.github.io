quarrelsome

adjective
prone to arguing or fighting
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔrəlˌsəm / (AmE), / ˈkwɒr.əl.səm / (BrE)
Tending to argue or fight with others frequently.

Examples

Quarrelsome person
Despite his quarrelsome nature, people valued his honesty.
His quarrelsome behavior made it difficult for him to keep friends.
She can be quite quarrelsome when she is tired or stressed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: argumentative - prone to arguing or expressing disagreement
  • antonym: peaceful - not inclined to argue or quarrel; calm or tranquil
