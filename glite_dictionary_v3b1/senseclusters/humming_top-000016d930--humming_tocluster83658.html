humming top

noun
spinning toy that hums
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌmɪŋ tɑp / (AmE), / ˈhʌmɪŋ tɒp / (BrE)
a spinning toy that produces a humming sound as it spins, usually operated by twisting or pushing a plunger
