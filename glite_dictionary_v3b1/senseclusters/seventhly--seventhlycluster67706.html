seventhly

adverb
in the seventh place (enumeration)
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛvənθli / (AmE), / ˈsɛvənθli / (BrE)
Used to introduce the seventh point or item in a list or argument.

Examples

Seventhly, in order
Seventhly, we must consider the environmental impact of the proposal.
Seventhly, you have no right to cancel the lease in mid-year.
He listed his arguments in detail: firstly, secondly, and finally seventhly.
