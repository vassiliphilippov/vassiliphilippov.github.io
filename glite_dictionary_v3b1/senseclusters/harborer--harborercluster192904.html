harborer

noun
one who keeps feelings
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈhɑːr.bər.ər/ /; / /ˈhɑːr.bər/ (AmE), /ˈhɑː.bə.rə/ (BrE)
a person who secretly holds or keeps an emotion, thought, or intention inside themselves
