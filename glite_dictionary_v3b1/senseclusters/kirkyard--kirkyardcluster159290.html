kirkyard

noun
Scottish churchyard
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜrkjɑrd / (AmE), / ˈkɜːkjɑːd / (BrE)
a churchyard, especially one found in Scotland or Northern England, often used as a burial ground

Examples

Old kirkyard
The old kirkyard was filled with weathered gravestones and ancient trees.
Many local legends are connected to the kirkyard beside the village church.
Tourists often visit the historic kirkyard to see the famous poet's grave.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cemetery - a place where dead people are buried
  • synonym: churchyard - the land around a church, often used as a cemetery
