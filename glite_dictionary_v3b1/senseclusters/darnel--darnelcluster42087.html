darnel

noun
a poisonous grass found in grainfields
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑr.nəl / (AmE), / ˈdɑː.nəl / (BrE)
a weedy annual grass (Lolium temulentum) often found in grainfields, whose seeds are sometimes considered poisonous

Examples

Wild darnel grass
Historically, darnel has been mistaken for wheat until the harvest.
Removing darnel from crops is important, as its seeds can be toxic if consumed.
The farmer was concerned to find darnel growing among his wheat crop.
