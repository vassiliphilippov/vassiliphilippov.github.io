cheongsam

noun
Chinese women's dress
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɔŋˌsɑm / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒŋˌsæm / (BrE)
a close-fitting Chinese dress for women, with a high neck and a slit skirt

Examples

A cheongsam dress
She wore a beautiful red cheongsam to the wedding ceremony.
The museum displayed an elegant silk cheongsam from the 1920s.
Many performers chose to wear a cheongsam during the cultural festival.

Related Terms

  • antonym: kimono - a traditional Japanese robe
  • synonym: qipao - a traditional Chinese dress similar to a cheongsam
