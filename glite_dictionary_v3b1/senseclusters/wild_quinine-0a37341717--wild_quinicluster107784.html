wild quinine

noun
a North American medicinal herb
Pronunciation: / waɪld kwaɪˈniːn / (AmE), / waɪld kwɪˈniːn / (BrE)
a stout perennial herb (Parthenium integrifolium) native to the eastern United States, with whitish flowers and leaves traditionally used in herbal remedies

Examples
