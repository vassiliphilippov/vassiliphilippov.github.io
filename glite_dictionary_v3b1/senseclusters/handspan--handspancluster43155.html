handspan

noun
width of a hand
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌspæn / (AmE), / ˈhændˌspæn / (BrE)
the distance or length measured by the width of a human hand with fingers fully extended (from the tip of the thumb to the tip of the little finger)

Examples

A handspan length
The ancient ruler was measured as exactly one handspan wide.
She estimated the length of the cloth using her handspan as a guide.
The gap between the shelves was just a handspan apart.
A pianist with a large handspan can easily reach wide chords on the keyboard.
He measured the width of the book using his handspan as a rough guide.
Her handspan is impressive, allowing her to play difficult pieces on the guitar.
