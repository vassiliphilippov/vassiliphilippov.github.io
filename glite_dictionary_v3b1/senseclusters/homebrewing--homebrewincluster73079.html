homebrewing

noun
making alcohol at home
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌbruːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌbruːɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of making beer or other alcoholic drinks at home, rather than buying them from a store

Examples

Homebrewing beer
Many people have taken up homebrewing to create unique flavors of beer.
He learned the basics of homebrewing from online tutorials and books.
Some countries have strict laws about homebrewing alcoholic beverages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brewing - the process of making beer or other alcoholic drinks
  • synonym: distilling - the process of making spirits or strong alcoholic drinks
  • synonym: winemaking - the activity of making wine
