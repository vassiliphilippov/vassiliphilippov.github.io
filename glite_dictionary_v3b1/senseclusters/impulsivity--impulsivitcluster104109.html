impulsivity

noun
acting without thinking

Pronunciation: /ɪm.pʌl.ˈsɪ.vɪ.tɪ/ /; / /ɪm.pəl.ˈsɪ.vɪ.tɪ/ (AmE), /ɪm.pʌl.ˈsɪ.vɪ.tɪ/ /; / /ɪm.pəl.ˈsɪ.vɪ.tɪ/ (BrE)
