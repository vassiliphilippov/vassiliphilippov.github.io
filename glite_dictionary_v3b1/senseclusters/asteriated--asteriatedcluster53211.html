asteriated

adjective
exhibiting a star-shaped pattern (in crystals/gemstones)
6.3
Pronunciation: / əˈstɪriˌeɪtɪd / (AmE), / əˈstɪəriˌeɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of some crystals, especially gemstones) exhibiting a star-shaped pattern known as asterism when light is reflected.

Examples

Asteriated gemstone
Only certain gemstones, like star rubies, are naturally asteriated.
The jeweler marveled at the asteriated sapphire, which displayed a perfect six-rayed star.
Under bright light, the crystal appeared beautifully asteriated due to tiny needle-like inclusions.
