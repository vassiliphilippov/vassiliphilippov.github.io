inert

adjective
slow, inactive, or lacking energy
6.3 4.4
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈnɜːrt/ /; / /ə.ˈnɜːrt/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈnɜːt/ /; / /ə.ˈnɜːt/ (BrE)
not moving or not having the energy or will to act; motionless or inactive

Examples
