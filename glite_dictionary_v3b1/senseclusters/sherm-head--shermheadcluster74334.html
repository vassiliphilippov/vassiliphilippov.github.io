sherm-head

noun
slang for a reckless drug user
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɜrm hɛd / (AmE), / ˈʃɜːm hɛd / (BrE)
A slang term for a person who recklessly uses certain drugs, especially those involving PCP or similar substances.

Examples

A sherm-head user
At the party, a couple of sherm-heads were causing a commotion in the backyard.
He was once considered a sherm-head, but now he has changed his lifestyle.
She warned her brother not to hang out with sherm-head types from the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: junkie - a person with a strong addiction to drugs
  • antonym: teetotaler - a person who never drinks alcohol or takes drugs
