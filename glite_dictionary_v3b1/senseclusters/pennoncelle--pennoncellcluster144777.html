pennoncelle

noun
small pennant on a lance
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛnənˈsɛl / (AmE), / ˌpɛnɒnˈsɛl / (BrE)
A small, narrow pennant, especially one carried on a lance in battle or for heraldic display.

Examples

A pennoncelle on lance
During the tournament, every competitor’s pennoncelle bore their family colors.
Medieval manuscripts often illustrate warriors carrying a pennoncelle at the tip of their lance.
The knight’s lance was adorned with a bright pennoncelle fluttering in the wind.
