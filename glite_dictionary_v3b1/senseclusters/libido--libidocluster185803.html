libido

noun
sexual drive or desire
Pronunciation: / lɪˈbiːdoʊ / (AmE), / lɪˈbiːdəʊ / (BrE)
a person's overall sexual drive or desire; in psychoanalysis, the instinctive energy or force associated with sexual desire

Examples

Strong libido
According to Freud, the libido is a central force in human behavior.
After recovery, her libido returned to normal levels.
Some medications can decrease your libido as a side effect.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apathy - lack of interest, enthusiasm, or concern; sometimes contrasted with sexual desire
  • synonym: sex drive - sexual desire or urge; synonym for libido
