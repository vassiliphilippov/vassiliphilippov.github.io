Synonyms

    3.8 1.14/m
    sexual desire is a strong wish for sexual closeness with another person, while libido also names the psychoanalytic energy behind that wish.
  • sex drive
    4.2 1.54/m
    sex drive is the common, everyday term for a person's urge for sex, while libido can mean that urge or its psychic energy.
  • libidinal energy
    8.6 0.01/m

  • lust
