cysticercosis

noun
tapeworm larva infection
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪstɪsərˈkoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌsɪstɪsəˈkəʊsɪs / (BrE)
a disease caused by infection with the larval stage of a tapeworm, often affecting muscles or the brain

Examples

Cysticercosis infection
The doctor explained that cysticercosis can cause serious health problems if the brain is affected.
After eating undercooked pork, the patient developed symptoms of cysticercosis.
Imaging tests revealed multiple cysts in the muscles due to cysticercosis.
