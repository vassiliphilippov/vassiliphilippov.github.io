grader

noun
automated grading system
4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈɡreɪ.dər/ (AmE), /ˈɡreɪ.də/ (BrE)
a computer program or automated system that evaluates student work and assigns marks, commonly used for programming exercises and online assessments.

Examples

Automatic grader
The online course uses a grader to automatically evaluate code submissions for correctness.
During the contest they relied on the grader to score thousands of student programs quickly.
When the department expanded, engineers upgraded the grader to reduce false positives in results.
