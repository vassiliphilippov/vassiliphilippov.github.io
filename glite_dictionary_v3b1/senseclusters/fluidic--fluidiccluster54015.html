fluidic

adjective
relating to fluids
7.8
Pronunciation: / fluˈɪdɪk / (AmE), / fluˈɪdɪk / (BrE)
relating to the use, movement, or control of fluids, especially in technological or engineering contexts

Examples

Fluidic systems
The laboratory uses fluidic systems to precisely control the flow of chemicals.
Recent advances in fluidic technology have improved the efficiency of microchips.
Engineers are developing new fluidic devices for medical diagnostics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hydraulic - operated by the movement or pressure of liquid
  • synonym: pneumatic - operated by air or gas under pressure
