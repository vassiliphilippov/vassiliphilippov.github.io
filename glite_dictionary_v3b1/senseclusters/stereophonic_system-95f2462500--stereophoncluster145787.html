stereophonic system

noun
sound system with 3D effect
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌstɛriəˈfɑnɪk ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˌstɪəriəˈfɒnɪk ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
A system of sound reproduction that uses two or more channels and speakers to create a three-dimensional auditory effect.

Examples

A stereophonic system setup
His old radio was replaced by a modern stereophonic system that produced excellent sound quality.
I prefer listening to music on a stereophonic system because the sound feels more immersive.
The theater installed a new stereophonic system to enhance the audience experience.
