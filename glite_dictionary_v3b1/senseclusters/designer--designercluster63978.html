designer

noun
person who creates visual concepts
1.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzaɪnər / (AmE), / dɪˈzʌɪnə / (BrE)
a person who specializes in creating visual concepts, often for print or digital media, as in graphic design

Examples

Graphic designer
As a freelance designer, he created logos and brochures for small businesses.
She worked as a designer for a major advertising agency.
The company is looking to hire a talented graphic designer to refresh their brand image.

Related Terms

  • synonym: graphic artist - a person who creates visual art, especially for commercial purposes
  • synonym: illustrator - a person who draws or creates pictures for books, magazines, or advertising
