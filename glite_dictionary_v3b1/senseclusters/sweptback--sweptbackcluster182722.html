sweptback

adjective
hair styled flowing backward
Pronunciation: / ˈswɛptˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈswɛptˌbæk / (BrE)
(of hair) combed or styled so that it flows backward away from the face.

Examples

Sweptback hair
He always kept his hair sweptback for a neat and professional appearance.
The actor's sweptback hairstyle became his signature look in the 1950s.
With her sweptback hair, she looked both elegant and confident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slicked-back - hair smoothed backward, often with product
