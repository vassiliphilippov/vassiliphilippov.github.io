pyro

noun
fire effects device
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪroʊ / (AmE), / ˈpaɪrəʊ / (BrE)
a pyrotechnic device or charge used to create flames, sparks, or special fire effects, especially in entertainment

Examples

Stage pyro effects
The concert featured impressive pyro that lit up the entire stage during the final song.
Safety checks are essential before using any pyro at a public event.
The special effects team set up the pyro to synchronize with the music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: explosive - a substance or device that can cause an explosion
  • synonym: firework - an explosive device used for visual or sound effects
