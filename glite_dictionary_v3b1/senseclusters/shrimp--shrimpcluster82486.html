shrimp

noun
small edible shellfish
Pronunciation: / ʃrɪmp / (AmE), / ʃrɪmp / (BrE)
a small shellfish with a long body and ten legs, often eaten as food

Examples

Grilled shrimp dish
Fishermen catch shrimp along the coast and sell them at the market.
He is allergic to shrimp and avoids eating seafood.
She ordered a plate of grilled shrimp for dinner.
Some people are allergic to shrimp and other shellfish.
The chef added fresh shrimp to the pasta dish.
We saw dozens of shrimp swimming near the coral reef.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crab - edible marine crustacean with a broad, flat body and pincers
  • degree higher: lobster - large edible marine crustacean with large claws
  • synonym: prawn - another type of small edible crustacean similar to shrimp
