edition

noun
slightly different version of something
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˈdɪʃən / (BrE)
a person or thing that is a slightly different form or version of another of the same kind

Examples

A new edition of him
He is a younger edition of his father, sharing the same mannerisms and expressions.
The restaurant released a vegan edition of their popular burger last month.
This model is a limited edition with a new color scheme and extra features.

Related Terms

  • synonym: variant - something differing in some respect from others of the same kind
  • synonym: version - a particular form of something
