tribalist

noun
member of a tribe
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪbəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈtraɪbəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who belongs to or strongly identifies with a tribe, especially an indigenous or traditional community

Examples

An indigenous tribalist
The tribalist shared stories about his community's customs and traditions.
As a proud tribalist, she participated in the annual cultural festival.
Many tribalists work to preserve their native languages and heritage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: individualist - a person who values independence over group identity
  • synonym: tribesperson - a member of a tribe
