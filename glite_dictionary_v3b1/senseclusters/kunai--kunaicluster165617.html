kunai

noun
Japanese hand tool
Pronunciation: / kuːˈnaɪ / (AmE), / kuːˈnaɪ / (BrE)
a small Japanese hand tool with a pointed blade, used for gardening, masonry, or as a weapon in martial arts and by ninjas
