frizette

noun
hair ornament curl
Pronunciation: / frɪˈzɛt / (AmE), / frɪˈzɛt / (BrE)
a small curl or artificial lock of hair, often worn as a decorative ornament in the hair

Examples

A frizette curl
She pinned a delicate frizette above her ear to complete her evening look.
In historical portraits, you can often spot a frizette among the elaborate hairstyles.
The actress wore a golden frizette to add flair to her costume.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hairpiece - an artificial addition of hair worn for decoration or to cover baldness
  • synonym: ringlet - a small, tight curl of hair
