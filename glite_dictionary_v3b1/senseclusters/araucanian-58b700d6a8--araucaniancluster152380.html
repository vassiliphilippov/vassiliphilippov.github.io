Araucanian

noun
person from Araucanía
Pronunciation: / ˌærɔːˈkeɪniən / (AmE), / ˌærɔːˈkeɪniən / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from the Araucanía region of Chile, not necessarily of indigenous descent

Examples

An Araucanian resident
The Araucanian community celebrates traditional festivals unique to their region.
As an Araucanian, she often shared stories about her hometown in southern Chile.
Many Araucanians have moved to the capital but maintain strong cultural ties.
