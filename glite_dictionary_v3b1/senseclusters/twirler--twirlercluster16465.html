twirler

noun
person who twirls a baton
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɜrlər / (AmE), / ˈtwɜːlə / (BrE)
a person, typically a performer, who twirls a baton as part of a routine, especially in marching bands or parades

Examples

Parade twirler performs
Each twirler wore a sparkling costume for the parade performance.
My cousin practiced every day to become a skilled twirler in the high school band.
The lead twirler dazzled the crowd with her baton tricks during the halftime show.

Related Terms

  • synonym: majorette - a female baton twirler in a marching band
