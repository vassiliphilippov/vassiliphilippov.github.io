supersafe

noun
high-security strongbox
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərseɪf / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəseɪf / (BrE)
a very secure lockable container or strongbox designed to protect money, documents, or valuables

Examples

A supersafe vault
The company installed a supersafe in the office to store sensitive documents and cash.
Only authorized personnel can access the supersafe using a fingerprint scanner.
They kept their most valuable jewelry locked away in a supersafe at home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: safe - a strong container for protecting valuable items
  • synonym: vault - a secure room or compartment for storing valuables
