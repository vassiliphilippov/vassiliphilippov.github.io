ingredient

noun
component of a mixture or compound
1.0 1.5
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡriːdiənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡriːdiənt / (BrE)
a substance or item that is part of a mixture, especially in food, recipes, or chemical compounds

Examples
