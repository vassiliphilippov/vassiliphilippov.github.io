unhunted

adjective
not hunted
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhʌntɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhʌntɪd / (BrE)
not pursued, chased, or killed by hunting.

Examples

The unhunted deer
The forest is a safe haven for unhunted deer and other wildlife.
In this protected area, many species remain unhunted and thrive in their natural habitat.
The documentary showed the difference between unhunted animals and those living in threatened regions.
