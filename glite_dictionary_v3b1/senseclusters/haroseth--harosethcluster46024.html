haroseth

noun
Passover fruit paste
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɑːˈroʊsɛθ / (AmE), / həˈrɒsɛθ / (BrE)
a sweet, dark paste made from fruits and nuts, traditionally eaten at Passover during the Seder meal
