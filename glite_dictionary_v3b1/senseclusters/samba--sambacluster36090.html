samba

verb
to dance the samba
Pronunciation: / ˈsæmbə / (AmE), / ˈsæmbə / (BrE)
to dance the samba, a lively Brazilian dance to syncopated music

Examples

To samba at parties
He spends hours each week sambaing at the local dance studio.
She learned to samba while studying abroad in Brazil.
The couple sambaed across the dance floor with energetic steps.
