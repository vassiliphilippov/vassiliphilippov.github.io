edematous

Swollen with an excessive accumulation of fluid, especially in body tissues.

Examples

Edematous tissue
Edematous tissue can be a sign of underlying health problems such as heart or kidney disease.
The doctor noticed edematous swelling around the surgical incision.
The patient's leg became edematous after his injury, indicating fluid buildup.
