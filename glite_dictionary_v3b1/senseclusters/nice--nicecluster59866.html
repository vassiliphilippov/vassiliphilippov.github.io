nice

adjective
pleasant or attractive
Pronunciation: / naɪs / (AmE), / naɪs / (BrE)
pleasant, enjoyable, or attractive in nature or appearance; giving pleasure or satisfaction.

Examples

A nice day

The weather is so nice today, let's go for a walk.
We had a nice time at the beach last weekend.
