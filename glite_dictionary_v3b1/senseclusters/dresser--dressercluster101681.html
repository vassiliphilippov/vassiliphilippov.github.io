dresser

noun
a chest of drawers for clothes
2.1 1.3
Pronunciation: /ˈdres.ər/ (AmE), /ˈdres.ə/ (BrE)
a piece of furniture with drawers, sometimes with a mirror, used for storing clothes and sometimes for dressing or applying makeup

Examples

Bedroom dresser
He bought a new dresser for his apartment to organize his clothes.
She keeps all her t-shirts folded neatly in the top drawer of her dresser.
The antique dresser in the bedroom was handed down from her grandmother.
Every morning, he sat at the dresser to fix his hair before work.
She kept her jewelry neatly arranged on the dresser in her bedroom.
The old oak dresser had a large mirror and several drawers for cosmetics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chest of drawers - a piece of furniture with drawers stacked one above another
  • synonym: vanity - a piece of furniture with a mirror, used for dressing or applying makeup
  • antonym: wardrobe - a tall piece of furniture for hanging clothes
