transit

noun
transportation system or facility
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtræn.sɪt / (AmE), / ˈtræn.zɪt / (BrE)
a system or facility providing the means and equipment for transporting passengers or goods from one place to another

Examples

Public transit system
Many commuters rely on public transit to get to work every day.
The city is working to improve its transit to reduce traffic congestion.
The new airport transit hub will connect trains and buses for easier travel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: transportation - the action of transporting someone or something
