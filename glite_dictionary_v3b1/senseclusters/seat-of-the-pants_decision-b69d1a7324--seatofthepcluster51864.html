seat-of-the-pants decision

noun
improvised or instinctive decision
Pronunciation: / ˌsit əv ðə ˈpænts dɪˈsɪʒn̩ / (AmE), / ˌsiːt əv ðə ˈpɑːnts dɪˈsɪʒn / (BrE)
an improvised or instinctive decision made without careful planning or analysis

Examples

A seat-of-the-pants decision
Sometimes a seat-of-the-pants decision leads to unexpected success.
The manager made a seat-of-the-pants decision when the data was unavailable.
Under pressure, she relied on a seat-of-the-pants decision to solve the problem.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calculated decision - a decision made after careful analysis
  • synonym: snap decision - a decision made quickly and without careful thought
