overtaxation

noun
excessive burden
7.4
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.tæk.ˈseɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.tæk.ˈseɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the state of being burdened or strained by excessive demands, work, or pressure

Examples

Overtaxation of workers
The overtaxation of staff during the busy season led to increased absenteeism.
Continuous overtaxation can result in burnout and decreased productivity.
Parents worried about the overtaxation of their children with too many extracurricular activities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: relief - the removal or reduction of a burden or stress
  • synonym: strain - a state of being stretched beyond normal limits
