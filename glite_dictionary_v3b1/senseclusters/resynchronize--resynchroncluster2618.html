resynchronize

verb
to restore synchronization
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈsɪŋkrəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˌriːˈsɪŋkrənaɪz / (BrE)
to make things operate together again after they have become out of sync

Examples

To resynchronize devices
After the software update, we had to resynchronize all the devices on the network.
The technician will resynchronize the audio and video tracks to fix the delay.
If your watch stops, you should resynchronize it with the official time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: desynchronize - to cause things to stop being in sync
  • synonym: synchronize - to cause things to happen at the same time or rate
