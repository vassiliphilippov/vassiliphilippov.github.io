Argentinidae

noun
family of small marine silvery fishes
Pronunciation: / ɑrˌdʒɛn.tɪˈniː.di /; / ɑrˌdʒɛntɪˈniːdi / (AmE), / ɑːˌdʒɛn.tɪˈniː.diː /; / ɑːˌdʒɛntɪˈniːdiː / (BrE)
a family of small marine fishes with long, silvery bodies, related to salmons and trouts
