autofill

verb
to complete automatically
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːtəʊfɪl / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊfɪl / (BrE)
to automatically complete a form or field with previously saved information

Examples

To autofill a form
Many browsers can autofill your name and address when you shop online.
If you enable the feature, your phone will autofill passwords for saved websites.
The app failed to autofill the payment details, so I had to enter them manually.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - to remove all data from a form or field
  • synonym: populate - to fill in data or information automatically
