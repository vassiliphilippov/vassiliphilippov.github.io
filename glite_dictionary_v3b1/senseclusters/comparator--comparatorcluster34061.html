comparator

noun
tool for comparing
6.7
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpæɹəˌtɔɹ / (AmE), / kəmˈpærətə / (BrE)
a word, device, or tool used to compare differences or similarities between things

Examples

Grammar comparator
In grammar, 'than' is a common comparator used to show differences between two things.
The scientist used a digital comparator to measure the length of the samples accurately.
A good comparator helps students understand how two ideas are similar or different.
