tastemaker

noun
trend influencer
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪstˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈteɪstˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who influences or decides what is popular or fashionable in areas like culture, fashion, art, or food

Examples

A tastemaker in fashion
The magazine named her a leading tastemaker in the world of interior design.
Many young artists hope to catch the attention of a tastemaker to boost their careers.
As a respected tastemaker, he often introduces new styles to the fashion industry.
The famous designer became a tastemaker by introducing bold new trends each season.
Many young musicians hope to catch the attention of a tastemaker in the industry.
Social media has given rise to a new generation of tastemakers who shape popular culture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: follower - a person who adopts trends or ideas started by others
  • synonym: influencer - a person who affects what other people do or buy, especially on social media
  • synonym: trendsetter - someone who starts or popularizes new styles or ideas
