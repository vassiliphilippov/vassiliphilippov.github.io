huller

noun
fruit stem remover
7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈhʌl.ər/ (AmE), /ˈhʌl.ə/ (BrE)
a small handheld tool used to remove the stems, cores, or hulls from soft fruit such as strawberries

Examples

Strawberry huller
She used a huller to quickly remove the stems from a bowl of strawberries.
A huller makes preparing fruit for desserts much easier and faster.
Before making the pie, he cleaned each berry with a small huller.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corer - a tool used to remove the core from fruit
  • synonym: peeler - a tool used to remove the outer skin of fruits or vegetables
