actionable

adjective
practical; can be acted on
5.2
Pronunciation: /ˈæk.ʃə.nə.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈæk.ʃə.nə.bəl/ (BrE)
Capable of being acted on; (of information, advice, or insights) practical and specific enough to be implemented as concrete steps.

Examples

Actionable advice
The consultant delivered actionable recommendations that reduced costs within weeks.
We need actionable data from the survey to decide which product features to build next.
The threat intelligence report lacked actionable details such as IP addresses and timestamps.

Related Terms

  • synonym: practical - concerned with what is useful or effective rather than with theory or ideas
