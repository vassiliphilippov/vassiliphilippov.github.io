triphasic

adjective
three phases
8.1
Pronunciation: / traɪˈfeɪzɪk / (AmE), / traɪˈfeɪzɪk / (BrE)
having or involving three distinct phases or stages

Examples

A triphasic medication
The doctor recommended a triphasic medication to better match the patient's hormonal cycle.
Researchers observed a triphasic response in the experiment, with three clear stages of activity.
She switched to a triphasic birth control pill for more stable hormone levels.

Related Terms

  • antonym: biphasic - having two distinct phases or stages
  • antonym: monophasic - having a single phase or stage
