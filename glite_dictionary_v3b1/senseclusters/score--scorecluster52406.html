score

noun
particular point or aspect; respect
1.2
Pronunciation: / skɔr / (AmE), / skɔː / (BrE)
a particular aspect or matter regarded as a reason or basis for something, especially used in the phrase 'on that score' meaning 'in that respect' or 'as regards that point'

Examples

On that score
On the financial score, the company is doing well this year.
There were no complaints on the technical score, but the presentation needed improvement.
You don't have to worry on that score; I've already taken care of everything.

Related Terms

  • synonym: account - regard or consideration; reason or grounds for something
  • synonym: respect - a particular aspect, point, or detail
