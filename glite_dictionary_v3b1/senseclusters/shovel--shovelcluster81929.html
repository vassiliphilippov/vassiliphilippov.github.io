shovel

noun
fireplace tool for scooping ashes
3.7 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌvəl / (AmE), / ˈʃʌvəl / (BrE)
a tool with a broad blade and handle, used for digging, lifting, or moving loose material such as soil, sand, snow, or ashes
