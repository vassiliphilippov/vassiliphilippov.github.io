Synonyms

  • United States Constitution

    United States Constitution is the formal full title used in legal texts and history books, while US Constitution is the shorter common name.
  • U.S. Constitution
    3.3 2.43/m
    U.S. Constitution uses periods in its abbreviation and appears in formal writing, while US Constitution is the punctuation-free modern form.
  • Constitution of the United States
