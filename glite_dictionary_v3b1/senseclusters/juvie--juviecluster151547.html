juvie

noun
juvenile offender
9.9 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd íuÀêvi / (AmE), / Ààd íuÀêvi / (BrE)
an informal term for a young person who has committed a crime or been in trouble with the law

Examples

A juvie offender
After the fight at school, he was sent to juvie for a month.
Many kids in juvie hope to turn their lives around after release.
She volunteers at a center that helps former juvie offenders find jobs.

Synonyms

  • juvenile delinquent
    5.3 0.66/m
    juvenile delinquent is a formal legal label for a young offender, while juvie is an informal, casual word used in conversation.
  • delinquent
    6.1 1.09/m
    delinquent is a general term for someone who breaks rules, while juvie is slang for a young offender in trouble with law.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adult - a person who is fully grown or developed
  • synonym: juvenile delinquent - a young person who has committed a crime
