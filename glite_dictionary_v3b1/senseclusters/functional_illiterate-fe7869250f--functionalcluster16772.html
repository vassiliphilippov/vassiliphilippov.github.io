functional illiterate

noun
person with insufficient practical reading and writing skills
Pronunciation: / ˌfʌŋkʃənl ɪˈlɪt̬ɚɪt / (AmE), / ˌfʌŋkʃənl ɪˈlɪtərət / (BrE)
a person who can read and write only to a limited extent, not enough to manage daily practical tasks effectively

Examples

A functional illiterate person
Despite completing primary school, John remained a functional illiterate and struggled to fill out basic forms.
Many functional illiterates find it difficult to understand medical instructions or public transportation timetables.
Programs aimed at adult education seek to help the functional illiterate develop essential literacy skills.
