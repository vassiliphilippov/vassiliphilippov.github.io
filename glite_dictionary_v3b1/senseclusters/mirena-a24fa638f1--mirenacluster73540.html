Mirena

proper noun
contraceptive device brand
Pronunciation: / mɪˈreɪnə / (AmE), / mɪˈreɪnə / (BrE)
a brand name for a hormonal intrauterine device used for long-term birth control

Examples

Mirena birth control
After consulting her doctor, she decided to get a Mirena for long-term contraception.
The Mirena is often recommended for women seeking a low-maintenance birth control option.
Some women experience lighter periods after having a Mirena inserted.

Related Terms

  • synonym: IUD - a device inserted into the uterus to prevent pregnancy
  • synonym: contraceptive - a method or device used to prevent pregnancy
