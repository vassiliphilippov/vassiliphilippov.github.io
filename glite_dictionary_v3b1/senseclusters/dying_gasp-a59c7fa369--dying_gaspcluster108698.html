dying gasp

noun
final breath before death
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪɪŋ ɡæsp / (AmE), / ˈdaɪɪŋ ɡɑːsp / (BrE)
the last moment of life or the last breath before death

Examples

The dying gasp moment
Many stories describe the dying gasp of ancient civilizations facing collapse.
The soldier uttered a name in his dying gasp before falling silent.
With a dying gasp, the candle's flame flickered and went out.

Related Terms

  • synonym: death rattle - a rattling sound made during the dying process
  • synonym: last breath - the final breath taken before death
