six-lined racerunner

noun
species of fast North American lizard
9.1
Pronunciation: / sɪks laɪnd ˈreɪsərˌrʌnər / (AmE), / sɪks laɪnd ˈreɪsərˌrʌnə / (BrE)
a very swift lizard native to eastern and central United States, characterized by six light stripes running the length of its body
