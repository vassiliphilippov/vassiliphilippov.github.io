presolar

adjective
before solar system
Pronunciation: / priːˈsoʊlər / (AmE), / priːˈsəʊlə / (BrE)
existing or occurring before the formation of a solar system

Examples

Presolar grains
Researchers analyze presolar grains found in meteorites to understand the origins of the universe.
The presolar nebula contained material from earlier generations of stars.
Some isotopes in meteorites are believed to be of presolar origin.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postsolar - occurring after the formation of a solar system
  • synonym: primordial - existing from the beginning of time
