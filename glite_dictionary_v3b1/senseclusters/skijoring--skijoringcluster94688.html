skijoring

noun
skiing while pulled
9.8
Pronunciation: / skiˈjɔrɪŋ / (AmE), / skiˈjɒrɪŋ / (BrE)
a sport in which a person on skis is pulled over snow by a horse, dog, or vehicle

Examples

Skijoring race event
He tried skijoring for the first time with his energetic dog.
In some countries, skijoring competitions are held on frozen lakes each winter.
The thrill of skijoring comes from the speed and teamwork with the animal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cross-country skiing - skiing over long distances on flat or rolling terrain
  • synonym: dog sledding - a sport where a sled is pulled by dogs over snow
