call waiting

noun
telephone feature for alerting to a second call
6.5
Pronunciation: / kɔl ˈweɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / kɔːl ˈweɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a telephone service feature that alerts you to an incoming call while you are already using the line

Examples

Call waiting service
I missed your call because I don't have call waiting on my phone line.
She heard the beep and realized her call waiting was alerting her to another caller.
With call waiting, you can answer a second call without ending the first.
