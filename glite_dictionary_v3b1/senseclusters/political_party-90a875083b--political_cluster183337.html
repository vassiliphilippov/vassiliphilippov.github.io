political party

noun
group seeking political power
1.0
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪdɪkəl ˈpɑrti / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
An organized group of people with similar political aims and opinions, seeking to influence public policy by getting its candidates elected to public office.

Examples

Join a political party
After years of being independent, she finally decided to join a political party.
Joining a political party can be an important way to participate in civic life.
The country's main political party announced a new platform ahead of the elections.
