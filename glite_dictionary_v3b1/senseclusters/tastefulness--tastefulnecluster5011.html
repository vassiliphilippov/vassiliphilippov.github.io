tastefulness

noun
elegance from good taste
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪstəf(ə)lnəs / (AmE), / ˈteɪstəf(ə)lnəs / (BrE)
elegance or beauty shown by good taste, especially in style, design, or manners

Examples

The tastefulness of style
She admired the tastefulness of his clothing, which was stylish without being flashy.
The tastefulness of the decor impressed every guest who entered the room.
The website is praised for its tastefulness in design and subtle color palette.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elegance - graceful and stylish appearance or manner
  • antonym: tastelessness - lack of taste or refinement; vulgarity
