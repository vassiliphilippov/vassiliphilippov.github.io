inopportuneness

noun
occurring at an inconvenient time
Pronunciation: / ɪˌnɑːpɚˈtuːn.nəs / (AmE), / ɪˌnɒp.əˈtjuːn.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of happening at a time that is not suitable or convenient

Examples

The inopportuneness of timing
Her suggestion was rejected due to its inopportuneness during the crisis.
The inopportuneness of his arrival disrupted the carefully planned schedule.
We laughed later about the inopportuneness of the power outage in the middle of the presentation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inopportune - occurring at an inconvenient or unsuitable time
  • antonym: opportuneness - the quality of happening at a suitable or advantageous time
