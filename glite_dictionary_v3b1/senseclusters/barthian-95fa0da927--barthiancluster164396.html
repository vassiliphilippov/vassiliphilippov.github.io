Barthian

adjective
of Karl Barth's ideas
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrθiən / (AmE), / ˈbɑːθiən / (BrE)
relating to the ideas, works, or theological approach of Karl Barth

Examples

Barthian theology
The professor gave a detailed analysis of Barthian theology in her lecture.
Many modern theologians have been influenced by Barthian perspectives on scripture.
His approach to ethics is distinctly Barthian in its emphasis on divine revelation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Lutheran - relating to the teachings of Martin Luther
