bupleurum

noun
herbal medicinal plant
Pronunciation: / bjuːˈplɪrəm / (AmE), / bjuːˈplɪərəm / (BrE)
a plant of the genus Bupleurum in the parsley family, sometimes used in herbal medicine

Examples

The bupleurum plant
