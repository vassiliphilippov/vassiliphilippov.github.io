civilization

noun
a society at a specific time and place
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪvələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɪvɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌsɪvəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
an advanced society or culture, or a particular group of people with a well-organized way of life
