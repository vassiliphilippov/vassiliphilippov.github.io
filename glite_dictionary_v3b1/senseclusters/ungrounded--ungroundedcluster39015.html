ungrounded

adjective
without basis
6.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡraʊndɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡraʊndɪd / (BrE)
not based on facts, evidence, or a solid foundation

Examples

Ungrounded rumors
The rumors about the school closing were completely ungrounded and caused unnecessary panic.
She dismissed his ungrounded fears about flying as irrational.
Critics argued that the claims made in the article were ungrounded and misleading.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baseless - without foundation in fact
  • antonym: justified - having a good reason or basis
  • synonym: unfounded - not supported by evidence or facts
