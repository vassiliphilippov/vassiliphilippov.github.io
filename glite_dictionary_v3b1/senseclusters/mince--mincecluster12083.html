mince

verb
cut into very small pieces
Pronunciation: / mɪns / (AmE), / mɪns / (BrE)
to cut food, especially meat or vegetables, into very small pieces, often using a special tool or machine

Examples

To mince onions
After mincing the garlic, mix it into the dressing.
She carefully minced the fresh herbs for the sauce.
You need to mince the onions before adding them to the pan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chop - to cut into pieces, usually with repeated sharp blows
  • synonym: dice - to cut food into small cubes
