arrowlike

adjective
resembling an arrow
Pronunciation: / ˈæroʊˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈærəʊˌlaɪk / (BrE)
moving or going in a straight, fast, and direct way, like an arrow

Examples

Arrowlike speed
The athlete's arrowlike sprint left his competitors far behind.
She admired the arrowlike precision of the falcon's dive.
The river cut an arrowlike path through the valley.
The bird flew in an arrowlike path across the sky, never wavering from its course.
Her arrowlike movements helped her win the race by reaching the finish line first.
The missile traveled with arrowlike precision toward its target.

Related Terms

  • antonym: curved - having a shape that is bent or rounded
  • synonym: direct - moving in a straight line toward a place or object
  • synonym: straight - extending in one direction without curves or bends
  • antonym: zigzag - moving in sharp turns or angles rather than straight
