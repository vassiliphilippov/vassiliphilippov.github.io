nondependent

adjective
without dependents
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.dɪˈpɛndənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.dɪˈpɛndənt / (BrE)
not having dependents or persons who rely on one for financial or legal support, especially in tax or legal contexts

Examples

Nondependent taxpayer
She filed her taxes as a nondependent because she supports herself financially.
Applicants who are classified as nondependent may be eligible for different benefits.
The form asks whether you are a nondependent or if you have children to support.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dependent - relying on someone for financial or legal support
  • synonym: independent - not relying on others for support
