acquired

adjective
developed or obtained after birth
7.7
Pronunciation: / əˈkwaɪɚd / (AmE), / əˈkwaɪəd / (BrE)
(of a quality or condition) obtained or developed after birth through environmental influences rather than inherited genetically.

Examples

An acquired skill
Language is an acquired skill, developed through exposure and practice.
Some diseases are acquired rather than present at birth.
The athlete showed an acquired ability to remain calm under pressure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inherited - (of a quality or trait) passed genetically from parent to offspring
