hirsute

adjective
covered with hair; hairy
9.4 0.19/m
Pronunciation: / hÉœrËŒsut / (AmE), / hÉœËËˆsjuËt / (BrE)
covered with hair; having a lot of hair on the body or face.

Examples

A hirsute caterpillar

The hirsute man stood out in the group because of his thick beard and chest hair.

Synonyms

  • hairy
    1.0 4.95/m

  • thick-haired
    2.3 0.00/m
    thick-haired describes hair that is dense or coarse on the head or body, while hirsute simply means covered with hair.
