camporee

noun
scout camping event
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌkæmpəˈriː / (AmE), / ˌkæmpəˈriː / (BrE)
a large organized camping event for scouts or similar youth groups, often involving activities and competitions

Examples

A camporee event
The regional camporee brought together hundreds of scouts from different towns.
At the camporee, teams competed in outdoor skills and games throughout the weekend.
She earned several badges during her first camporee with the scout troop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: camp - an outdoor place where people stay in tents or shelters
  • synonym: jamboree - a large celebration or gathering, especially for scouts
