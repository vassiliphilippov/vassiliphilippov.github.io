toy terrier

noun
a small, active terrier breed
Pronunciation: / tɔɪ ˈtɛriər / (AmE), / tɔɪ ˈtɛriə / (BrE)
a very small, lively terrier breed often kept as a pet or companion dog

Examples

A toy terrier dog
Her toy terrier loves chasing after tennis balls in the yard.
The toy terrier barked excitedly when the guests arrived.
They decided to adopt a toy terrier because they wanted a small, energetic pet.

Related Terms
