unzoned

adjective
not divided into zones
6.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈzoʊnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈzəʊnd / (BrE)
not divided into separate areas for control, especially for heating, cooling, or other environmental systems

Examples

Unzoned building
The building was unzoned, so one thermostat controlled the temperature for all rooms.
Older homes are often unzoned, making it difficult to adjust heating in individual areas.
Because the office was unzoned, some employees found their rooms too hot while others were cold.
