mismark

noun
incorrect label or mark
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪsˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈmɪsˌmɑːk / (BrE)
an incorrect or wrong label, tag, or mark placed on an object that may cause confusion or error

Examples

A mismark on a box
The shipment was delayed because of a mismark on the package.
A simple mismark can lead to products being sent to the wrong location.
After discovering the mismark, the team quickly relabeled all the boxes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: correction - the act of fixing an error or mistake
  • degree lower: mark - a visible impression or label on an object
  • synonym: mislabel - an incorrect or wrong label attached to something
