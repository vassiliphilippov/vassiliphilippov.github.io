Pachomian

noun
follower of Pachomius
Pronunciation: / pəˈkoʊmiən / (AmE), / pəˈkəʊmiən / (BrE)
a monk or nun who is a member of a religious community following the monastic rule of Saint Pachomius

Examples

A Pachomian monk
The Pachomian tradition emphasized communal living and shared work among monks.
A Pachomian would follow strict rules established by Saint Pachomius in the Egyptian desert.
Historians have found letters written by a Pachomian to other monastic communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Benedictine - a monk or nun following the rule of Saint Benedict
  • antonym: hermit - a person living in solitude, often for religious reasons
