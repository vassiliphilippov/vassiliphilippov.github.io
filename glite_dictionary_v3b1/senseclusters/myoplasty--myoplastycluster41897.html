myoplasty

noun
muscle repair surgery
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪəˌplæsti / (AmE), / ˈmaɪəˌplæsti / (BrE)
a surgical operation to repair or reconstruct muscle tissue

Examples

Myoplasty surgery
After the accident, the surgeon recommended myoplasty to restore movement in the patient's leg.
The doctor explained that myoplasty could help improve muscle function after severe injury.
Advances in myoplasty have made muscle reconstruction more successful and less invasive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: muscle graft - a surgical procedure where muscle tissue is transplanted
  • antonym: myotomy - a surgical incision into a muscle
