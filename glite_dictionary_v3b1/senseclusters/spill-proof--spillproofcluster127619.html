spill-proof

adjective
prevents spills
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪlˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈspɪlˌpruːf / (BrE)
designed to prevent liquids from escaping or spilling out

Examples

Spill-proof cup
The new travel mug is completely spill-proof, so you can carry it in your bag without worry.
Parents often buy spill-proof cups for their young children to avoid messes.
She was relieved that her laptop case was spill-proof after accidentally knocking over her coffee.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leakproof - designed to prevent any liquid from leaking out
  • synonym: watertight - not allowing water to pass through
