sheath knife

noun
fixed-blade knife carried in a sheath
4.0
Pronunciation: / ʃiθ naɪf / (AmE), / ʃiːθ nʌɪf / (BrE)
A knife with a fixed blade that is carried in a sheath for protection and easy access.

Examples

A sheath knife
A sheath knife is safer to carry because the blade is protected.
He always carries a sheath knife when hiking in the wilderness.
The hunter pulled his sheath knife from his belt to cut the rope.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pocketknife - a small folding knife that fits in a pocket
