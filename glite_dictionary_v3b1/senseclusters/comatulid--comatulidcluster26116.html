comatulid

noun
free-swimming stalkless crinoid
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈmætʃʊlɪd / (AmE), / kəˈmatjʊlɪd / (BrE)
A free-swimming, stalkless crinoid with ten feathery arms, typically found on muddy sea bottoms.

Examples

A comatulid sea animal

Unlike other crinoids, a comatulid does not have a stalk and can swim freely.
