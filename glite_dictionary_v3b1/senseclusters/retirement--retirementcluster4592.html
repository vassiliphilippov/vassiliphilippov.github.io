retirement

noun
decommissioning a product or system
3.2
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtaɪərmənt / (AmE), / rɪˈtaɪəmənt / (BrE)
The process of taking a product, system, asset, or service out of active use; decommissioning or phasing out.

Examples

Retirement of equipment
The company announced the retirement of its legacy software next month due to security risks.
During the audit, the IT team scheduled the retirement of several outdated servers this quarter.
Product managers often plan a phased retirement to move customers from old systems to new ones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decommission - to take equipment or a facility out of active service
  • antonym: deployment - the process of putting a system or product into active use
  • synonym: phase out - to discontinue something gradually over a period of time
