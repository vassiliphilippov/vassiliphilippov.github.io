assemble

verb
to put parts or people together
1.7 2.5
Pronunciation: / əˈsɛm.bəl / (AmE), / əˈsɛm.bəl / (BrE)
to put together the parts of something to form a whole; to gather components or people into a group or structure

Examples

To assemble a machine
Engineers will assemble the parts of the machine in the workshop.
The students assembled a robot as part of their project.
Volunteers assemble every Saturday to build houses for families in need.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disassemble - to take apart something that was assembled
  • synonym: gather - to collect things or people together
