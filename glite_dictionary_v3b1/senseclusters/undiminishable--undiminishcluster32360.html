undiminishable

adjective
cannot be reduced
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈmɪnɪʃəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈmɪnɪʃəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to reduce or lessen in amount, strength, or intensity

Examples

An undiminishable hope
His undiminishable optimism inspired everyone around him, even during difficult times.
The mountain's undiminishable beauty attracts visitors year after year.
She faced the challenge with undiminishable courage and determination.

Related Terms

  • antonym: diminishable - able to be reduced or lessened
  • synonym: irreducible - impossible to make smaller or simpler
