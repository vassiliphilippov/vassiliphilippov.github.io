willing

adjective
ready or prepared to do something
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
ready or prepared to do something, or showing willingness to help or participate.

Examples

She is always a willing helper, ready to assist whenever needed.
The students were willing to participate in the project despite their busy schedules.
