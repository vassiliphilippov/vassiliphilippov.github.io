sunburned

adjective
affected by excessive sun exposure
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌbɝnd /; / ˈsʌnˌbɝnt / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌbɜːnd /; / ˈsʌnˌbɜːnt / (BrE)
Having skin that is reddened and sometimes painful due to excessive exposure to the sun's ultraviolet rays.

Examples

Sunburned skin
After a long day at the beach, he was completely sunburned and sore.
Her shoulders got badly sunburned during the hiking trip.
Make sure to wear sunscreen if you want to avoid getting sunburned.
After a long day at the beach, she was completely sunburnt and had to apply aloe vera.
His sunburnt arms were a painful reminder to use sunscreen next time.
You could tell they'd been hiking by their sunburnt faces and dusty shoes.

  • synonym: sunburnt - having skin affected by sunburn (alternate spelling, chiefly British)
