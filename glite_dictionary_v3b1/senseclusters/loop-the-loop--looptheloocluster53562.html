loop-the-loop

noun
aerobatic maneuver; vertical circular flight
6.9
Pronunciation: / luːp ðə luːp / (AmE), / luːp ðə luːp / (BrE)
an aerobatic maneuver in which an aircraft makes a complete vertical circle.

Examples

A loop-the-loop maneuver
He felt weightless at the top of the loop-the-loop during the daring flight.
Many amusement park rides simulate the sensation of a loop-the-loop.
The stunt pilot performed a perfect loop-the-loop at the airshow.
