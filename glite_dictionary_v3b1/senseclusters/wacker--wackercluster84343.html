wacker

noun
slang for odd person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwækər / (AmE), / ˈwækə / (BrE)
a slang term for a foolish, strange, or eccentric person

Examples

A real wacker
Everyone thought he was a bit of a wacker because of his wild stories.
She laughed and called her brother a wacker for wearing mismatched shoes.
At the party, one wacker danced alone in the middle of the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely or imprudently
  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intellectual ability
  • synonym: weirdo - a person who behaves in a strange or unusual way
