frostbite

verb
to suffer frostbite
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɔstˌbaɪt / (AmE), / ˈfrɒstˌbaɪt / (BrE)
to become affected by frostbite, especially as a result of exposure to extreme cold.

Examples

To frostbite toes
If you stay outside too long in the snow, your toes might frostbite quickly.
The climber realized his fingers were starting to frostbite as the temperature dropped.
Doctors warned that exposed skin could frostbite within minutes in such extreme cold.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freeze - to become hardened or damaged by cold
  • antonym: thaw - to become unfrozen or warm after being frozen
