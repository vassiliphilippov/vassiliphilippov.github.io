tugboat

noun
small boat for towing ships
5.4 2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌɡˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈtʌɡ.bəʊt / (BrE)
a small, strong boat that moves larger ships by pulling or pushing them in harbours, rivers, or canals

Examples

A tugboat pulls ships
A fleet of tugboats was needed to move the stranded oil tanker.
The tugboat carefully guided the large container ship out of the harbor.
We watched as a red tugboat pushed the barge along the busy river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: towboat - a boat used for towing, especially on rivers
