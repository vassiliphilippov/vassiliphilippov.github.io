crab

verb
direct an aircraft into a crosswind
9.7 7.1
Pronunciation: / kræb / (AmE), / kræb / (BrE)
(of an aircraft or pilot) to direct the aircraft at an angle into a crosswind to compensate for drift.

Examples

To crab a plane
Pilots often crab the aircraft during landing in strong crosswinds.

The instructor explained how to crab properly when approaching the runway.
