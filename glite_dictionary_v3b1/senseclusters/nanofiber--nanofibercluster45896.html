nanofiber

adjective
containing nanofibers
Pronunciation: / ˈnænoʊˌfaɪbər / (AmE), / ˈnænəʊˌfaɪbə / (BrE)
made of or containing extremely thin fibers with diameters measured in nanometers.

Examples

Nanofiber material
The laboratory developed a nanofiber material for use in advanced air filters.
Researchers are studying nanofiber membranes for their potential in water purification.
The nanofiber coating improved the strength and flexibility of the medical device.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: microfiber - made of or containing very fine fibers, larger than nanofibers
  • synonym: nanostructured - having a structure on the nanometer scale
