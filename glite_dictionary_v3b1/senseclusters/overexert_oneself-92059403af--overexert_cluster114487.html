overexert oneself

verb
strain oneself beyond healthy limits
5.1 5.7
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ɪg.ˈzɜːrt wʌn.ˈself/ /; / /oʊ.vər.ɪg.ˈzɜːrt wə.ˈself/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.ɪg.ˈzɜːt wʌn.ˈself/ /; / /əʊ.və.ɪg.ˈzɜːt wə.ˈself/ (BrE)
