overexert oneself

verb
strain oneself beyond healthy limits
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzɜrt wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪɡˈzɜːt wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to make such a great physical or mental effort that it is more than is healthy or advisable

Examples

To overexert oneself physically
Athletes are warned not to overexert oneself during intense training sessions.
It's easy to overexert oneself when trying to meet a tight deadline.
You shouldn't overexert oneself during recovery from an illness.

Related Terms
