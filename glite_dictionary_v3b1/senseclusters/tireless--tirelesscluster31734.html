tireless

adjective
not becoming tired; persistent
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪɚləs /; / ˈtaɪərləs / (AmE), / ˈtaɪələs / (BrE)
showing great energy and persistent effort without becoming tired

Examples

She was a tireless advocate for human rights throughout her life.
The volunteers worked tireless hours to help rebuild the town.
