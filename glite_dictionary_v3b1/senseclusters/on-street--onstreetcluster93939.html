on-street

adjective
located at the sides of streets
3.9 0.29/m
Pronunciation: / …ën Ààstrit / (AmE), / …ín ÀàstriÀêt / (BrE)
Located or occurring at the sides of streets, especially in relation to parking or traffic.

Examples

On-street parking
On-street parking is limited to two hours in the city center.
New regulations have reduced on-street advertising to improve the area's appearance.
The city council plans to increase on-street bicycle racks to promote cycling.

Synonyms

  • streetside
    3.2 0.00/m
    streetside is beside the pavement and used for shops or cafes, while on-street refers to parking or traffic at street sides.
  • curbside
    2.7 1.25/m
    curbside is the edge next to a curb used for short stops or pickups, while on-street describes parking or traffic along street sides.

Related Terms

  • antonym: off-street - not located on the sides of streets, e.g. in private parking lots
