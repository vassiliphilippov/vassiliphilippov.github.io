ferial

adjective
relating to a feria (church weekday)
7.8 9.8
Pronunciation: /ˈfe.rɪ.əl/ (AmE), /ˈfe.rɪ.əl/ (BrE)
Relating to a feria; pertaining to a weekday on which no special feast is celebrated, particularly in the Roman Catholic Church calendar.

Examples

A ferial day
During ferial seasons, no special vestments are required by the clergy.
Most of January consisted of ferial days, without solemn feasts in the calendar.
The church's ferial mass on Wednesday followed the standard weekday liturgy.
