lion's foot

noun
a perennial herb with lion-paw-shaped leaves
1.0 8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈlaɪ.ənz fʊt/ (AmE), /ˈlaɪ.ənz fʊt/ (BrE)
a perennial herb native to the southern and eastern United States, known for its drooping clusters of pink or purple flowers and thick basal leaves resembling a lion's foot; sometimes classified in the genus Prenanthes
