Synonyms

    7.2 2/m
    resuscitate means a medical act to bring someone back to life or consciousness, while revive is more general and everyday.
  • bring around
    5.3 0.09/m
    bring around means to restore a fainted person to consciousness in casual speech, while revive covers both medical and general waking.
  • bring back to life
    4.8 0.4/m
    bring back to life means return someone to living or full function in extreme cases, while revive often means simple waking.
  • reanimate
    7.2 0.8/m
    reanimate means restore life or strong energy, often in a dramatic way, while revive is more commonly used for real people.
  • revivify
    8.5 0.06/m
    revivify means give new energy or freshness to something, often metaphorical, while revive usually means bringing back consciousness.
