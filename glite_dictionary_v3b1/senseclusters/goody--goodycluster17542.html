goody

noun
overly virtuous person
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʊdi / (AmE), / ˈɡʊdi / (BrE)
a person, especially a child, who is considered overly virtuous or well-behaved, often in a way that annoys others

Examples

A goody child
The other kids teased her for being such a goody in class.
He was called a goody because he never broke any rules at school.
Sometimes being a goody can make it hard to fit in with your peers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: do-gooder - a person who tries to help others in a way that is annoying or interfering
  • antonym: rebel - a person who resists authority or control
  • synonym: teacher's pet - a student who is liked by the teacher and often seen as trying too hard to please
