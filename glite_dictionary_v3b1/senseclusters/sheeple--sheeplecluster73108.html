sheeple

noun
unquestioning followers
Pronunciation: / ˈʃiːpəl / (AmE), / ˈʃiːpəl / (BrE)
people who follow others or authority blindly without independent thought or critical thinking

Examples

Blind sheeple followers
Some critics argue that the media turns people into sheeple who never question what they are told.
He refused to be one of the sheeple and always did his own research.
The politician accused his opponents' supporters of acting like sheeple during the debate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: follower - a person who accepts the leadership of another
  • antonym: individualist - a person who acts independently and thinks for themselves
