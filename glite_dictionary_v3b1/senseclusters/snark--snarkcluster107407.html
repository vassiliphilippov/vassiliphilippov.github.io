snark

verb
to make sarcastic remarks
8.4
Pronunciation: /snɑːrk/ (AmE), /snɑːk/ (BrE)

Examples

Instead of offering helpful feedback, he just snarked at every suggestion.
People online often snark about celebrities and their latest scandals.
