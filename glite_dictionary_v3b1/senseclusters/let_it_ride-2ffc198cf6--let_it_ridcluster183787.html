let it ride

verb
allow a situation to continue without interference
Pronunciation: / lɛt ɪt raɪd /; / ˌlɛt ɪt ˈraɪd / (AmE), / lɛt ɪt raɪd /; / ˌlɛt ɪt ˈraɪd / (BrE)
to allow a situation to continue without interfering or making changes
