metaplastic

adjective
abnormally changed tissue
9.2
Pronunciation: /mə.tə.ˈplæ.stɪk/ /; / /me.tə.ˈplæ.stɪk/ (AmE), /mə.tə.ˈplæ.stɪk/ /; / /me.tə.ˈplæ.stɪk/ (BrE)
describing tissue that has changed from one type to another, especially in an abnormal or disease-related way

Examples
