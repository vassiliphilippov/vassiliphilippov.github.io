rebuilding

noun
the act of building again
Pronunciation: / riˈbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / riːˈbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of building something again after it has been damaged or destroyed

Examples

Community rebuilding
Efforts at rebuilding the community brought everyone together.
The rebuilding of the city after the earthquake took several years.
They began the rebuilding of the old library last month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reconstruction - the process of reconstructing, especially after damage
