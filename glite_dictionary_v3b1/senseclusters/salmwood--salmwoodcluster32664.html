salmwood

noun
tropical American tree of the genus Cordia
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsælmˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈsɑːlmˌwʊd / (BrE)
A large tropical American tree of the genus Cordia, valued for its abundant creamy white flowers and valuable wood.

Examples

A salmwood tree
A salmwood tree can be recognized by its abundant creamy white flowers in the spring.
Conservationists are concerned about the declining numbers of salmwood in the wild.
The furniture was made from rare salmwood harvested in Central America.
