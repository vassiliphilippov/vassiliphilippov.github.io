sincere

adjective
genuine; not pretending or deceitful
1.0 2.5
Pronunciation: / sɪnˈsɪr / (AmE), / sɪnˈsɪə / (BrE)
genuine and honest; showing real feelings or beliefs, not pretending or deceitful

Examples

A sincere apology
Her apology sounded sincere, and I believed she truly regretted her mistake.
The author's sincere passion for the subject is evident throughout the book.
You can always count on him for sincere advice, even if it's hard to hear.
He offered a sincere apology for his mistake, and everyone could tell he meant it.
Her sincere smile made a lasting impression on everyone she met.
It is important to give sincere feedback so people know you truly care.
