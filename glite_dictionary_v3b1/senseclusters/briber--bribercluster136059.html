briber

noun
person who gives a bribe
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbraɪbər / (AmE), / ˈbraɪbə / (BrE)
a person who offers or gives a bribe to influence someone illegally or dishonestly

Examples

The briber offers money
Authorities arrested the briber at the scene with evidence of cash in his possession.
The briber offered the official a large sum of money to overturn the decision.
The court found both the briber and the bribee guilty of corruption.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bribe - money or favor given to influence someone dishonestly
  • antonym: bribee - the person who receives a bribe
