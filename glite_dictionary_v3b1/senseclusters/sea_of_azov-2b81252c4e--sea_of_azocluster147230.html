Sea of Azov

proper noun
inland sea between Russia and Ukraine
Pronunciation: / si əv ˈæzˌɑv /; / si əv əˈzɑv / (AmE), / siː əv ˈæzɒv /; / si əv əˈzɒv / (BrE)
an inland sea (or shallow bay) in Eastern Europe between Russia and Ukraine, connected to the Black Sea
