responsive

adjective
adapts layout to device screens
3.5
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈspɑːn.sɪv/ /; / /rə.ˈspɑːn.sɪv/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈspɒn.sɪv/ /; / /rə.ˈspɒn.sɪv/ (BrE)
(of a website, app, or design) able to adjust its layout and content automatically to fit different screen sizes and devices.

Examples

Responsive website design
The company's responsive website adapts automatically to mobile and desktop displays.
Designers built a responsive layout that rearranges images and text on small screens.
If a site isn't responsive, users on phones often zoom or scroll horizontally to read.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adaptive - design that changes to suit different conditions or user needs
  • synonym: mobile-friendly - suitable for use on mobile devices, with readable text and usable navigation
