metabolic

adjective
relating to the body's chemical processes
1.8 3.7
Pronunciation: /me.tə.ˈbɑː.lɪk/ /; / /mə.tə.ˈbɑː.lɪk/ (AmE), /me.tə.ˈbɒl.ɪk/ /; / /mə.tə.ˈbɒl.ɪk/ (BrE)
Relating to or resulting from the chemical processes that occur within a living organism in order to maintain life.
