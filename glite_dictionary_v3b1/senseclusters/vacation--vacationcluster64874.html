vacation

verb
spend time away for rest or recreation
5.0
Pronunciation: / veɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / vəˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
to spend a period of time away from home or work, especially for rest or recreation

Examples

To vacation abroad
Last year, she vacationed in Europe for two weeks.
They are vacationing on an island in the Caribbean this month.
We vacation in the mountains every summer to escape the city heat.
