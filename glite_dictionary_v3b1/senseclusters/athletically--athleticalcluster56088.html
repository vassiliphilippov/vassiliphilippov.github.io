athletically

adverb
in a sporty way
3.7
Pronunciation: / æθˈlɛtɪkli / (AmE), / æθˈlɛtɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that shows physical strength, fitness, or skill like an athlete

Examples

To athletically jump high
He jumped athletically over the fence during the obstacle course.
The dancer moved athletically across the stage, impressing the judges.
She climbed the rock wall athletically, reaching the top in record time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clumsily - in a way that lacks skill or coordination
  • synonym: gracefully - in a smooth and elegant way
