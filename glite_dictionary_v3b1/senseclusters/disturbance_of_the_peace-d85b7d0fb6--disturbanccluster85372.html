disturbance of the peace

noun
interruption of public order or tranquility
5.1 5.1
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstɝbəns əv ðə pis / (AmE), / dɪˈstɜːbəns əv ðə piːs / (BrE)
any act that interrupts public order or tranquility, often considered a minor legal offense
