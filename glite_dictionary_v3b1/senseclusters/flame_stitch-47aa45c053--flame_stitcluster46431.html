flame stitch

noun
zigzag embroidery stitch pattern
Pronunciation: / fleɪm stɪtʃ / (AmE), / fleɪm stɪtʃ / (BrE)
a type of needlepoint embroidery stitch that creates a pattern of zigzag lines resembling flames

Examples

Flame stitch embroidery
Many antique cushions feature the vibrant patterns of flame stitch work.
She learned the flame stitch technique in her advanced embroidery class.
The chair was upholstered in a beautiful flame stitch fabric that added color to the room.
