lay aside

verb
to save money for future use
Pronunciation: / leɪ əˈsaɪd / (AmE), / leɪ əˈsaɪd / (BrE)
to save or accumulate money or resources for future use

Examples

To lay aside money
He managed to lay aside enough money to buy a new car by the end of the year.
It's wise to lay aside some funds for unexpected expenses.
She tries to lay aside a portion of her income each month for emergencies.
