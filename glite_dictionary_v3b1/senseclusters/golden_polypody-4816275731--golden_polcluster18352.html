golden polypody

noun
a type of mat-forming fern
Pronunciation: / ˈgoʊl.dən pəˈlɪ.pə.di /; / ˈɡoʊl.dən pəˈlɪp.ə.di / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊl.dən pəˈlɪ.pə.di /; / ˈɡəʊl.dən pəˈlɪp.ə.di / (BrE)
a type of fern with large, deeply divided or lobed fronds, found in various regions and sometimes grown indoors
