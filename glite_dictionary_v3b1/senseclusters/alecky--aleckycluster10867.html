alecky

noun
cheeky person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈælɪki / (AmE), / ˈælɪki / (BrE)
an informal, often disapproving term for a person who acts in a smart-aleck or cheeky manner

Examples

A real alecky
Nobody appreciated the alecky who kept interrupting the meeting with jokes.
Her brother can be such an alecky when he tries to show off in front of guests.
The teacher quickly lost patience with the alecky in the back row.
