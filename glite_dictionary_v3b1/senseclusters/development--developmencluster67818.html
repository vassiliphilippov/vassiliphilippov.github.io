development

noun
an area constructed for a specific use
1.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvɛləpmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈvɛləpmənt / (BrE)
a group of buildings or an area that has been constructed for a particular purpose, such as housing or commerce

Examples

A housing development
New families have moved into the development on the east side of town.
The city approved a commercial development near the river.
We bought our house in a quiet residential development.

Related Terms

  • synonym: subdivision - a tract of land divided into lots for sale or development
