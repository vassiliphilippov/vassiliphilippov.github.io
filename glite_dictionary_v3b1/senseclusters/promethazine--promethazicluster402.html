promethazine

noun
antihistamine and antiemetic drug
Pronunciation: / proʊˈmɛθəˌzin / (AmE), / prəˈmiːθəziːn / (BrE)
an antihistamine medication used to treat allergies, motion sickness, and nausea, and sometimes as a sedative

Examples

Promethazine medicine
Promethazine is sometimes used as a sedative for children undergoing minor procedures.
He took promethazine before his flight to prevent motion sickness.
The doctor prescribed promethazine to relieve her allergy symptoms.
