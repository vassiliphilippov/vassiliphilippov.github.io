order Sphaeriales

noun
order of ascomycetous fungi
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər sfɪəˈriəˌliz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə sfɪəˈrɪəliːz / (BrE)
a large order of ascomycetous fungi, typically having dark, hard perithecia with definite ostioles; in modern classifications, its species are often reassigned to several different orders.

Examples

The order Sphaeriales fungi
Identification of members of the order Sphaeriales is based on the characteristics of their perithecia.
Recent studies have split the order Sphaeriales into several distinct groups.
The order Sphaeriales includes fungi with tough, dark fruiting bodies.
