sheep bell

noun
bell tied around a sheep's neck
Pronunciation: / ʃip bɛl / (AmE), / ʃiːp bɛl / (BrE)
A bell attached around the neck of a sheep to enable its location by sound.

Examples

A sheep bell ring
A lost sheep bell was found at the edge of the field after the storm.
She bought a new sheep bell to help keep track of her lamb in the large pasture.
The farmer heard the sheep bell tinkling in the distance and knew the flock was close by.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cowbell - a bell hung around the neck of a cow
