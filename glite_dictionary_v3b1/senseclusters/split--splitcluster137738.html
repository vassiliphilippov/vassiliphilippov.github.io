split

noun
bowling: separated pins left after first ball
Pronunciation: / splɪt / (AmE), / splɪt / (BrE)
(in tenpin bowling) a situation in which the remaining pins are separated by a gap after the first ball, making it difficult to knock them all down in the next shot.

Examples

Bowling split
He was disappointed when he left a split in the final frame.
Making a spare after a tough split is a real challenge for bowlers.
She managed to pick up the split and save her score.
