fore-yard

noun
spar for foresail on a ship
3.7
Pronunciation: / fɔr-jɑrd / (AmE), / fɔː-jɑːd / (BrE)
the horizontal spar mounted on the lower foremast of a square-rigged ship, used to support the foresail

Examples

The fore-yard spar
During the restoration, the fore-yard was replaced with a stronger wooden spar.
From the deck, you could see crewmen working high above on the fore-yard.
The sailors climbed up to the fore-yard to adjust the foresail before the storm hit.
