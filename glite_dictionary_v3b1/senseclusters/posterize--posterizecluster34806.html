posterize

verb
to make into a poster
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstəˌraɪz / (BrE)
to print or reproduce a photograph or image in a style or size suitable for a poster, often with bold colors or simplified details

Examples

To posterize an image
They decided to posterize the concert photo for sale at the merchandise table.
The artist used software to posterize the image, giving it a bold, graphic look.
You can posterize your favorite vacation picture to decorate your room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enlarge - to make something bigger in size
  • antonym: miniaturize - to make something much smaller
