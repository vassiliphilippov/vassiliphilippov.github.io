Alfred Nobel

proper noun
Swedish inventor, founder of Nobel Prizes
Pronunciation: / ˈælfrɛd noʊˈbɛl / (AmE), / ˈælfrɪd nəʊˈbɛl / (BrE)
(1833–1896) A Swedish chemist and inventor best known for inventing dynamite and establishing the Nobel Prizes through his will.

Examples

Alfred Nobel inventor
Alfred Nobel is remembered both for inventing dynamite and for founding the prestigious Nobel Prizes.
Many scientists dream of receiving a Nobel Prize, established by Alfred Nobel.
The legacy of Alfred Nobel continues to inspire innovation and peace around the world.
