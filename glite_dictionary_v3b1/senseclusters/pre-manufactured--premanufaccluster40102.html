pre-manufactured

adjective
made in advance
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˌmænjuˈfæktʃɚd / (AmE), / ˌpriːˌmænjuˈfæktʃəd / (BrE)
made in advance, usually in a factory, before being assembled or used at the final location

Examples

Pre-manufactured homes
The company offers pre-manufactured homes that can be assembled quickly on site.
Using pre-manufactured components helped reduce the construction time significantly.
Many modern buildings use pre-manufactured panels for efficiency and cost savings.
