neurolinguistics

noun
study of language and the nervous system
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪks / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪks / (BrE)
the scientific study of the relationship between language and the structure and function of the nervous system, especially the brain

Examples

Study of neurolinguistics
Recent advances in neurolinguistics have improved our understanding of language disorders.
She specializes in neurolinguistics and researches how brain injuries affect language abilities.
The seminar on neurolinguistics attracted students from both linguistics and neuroscience departments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: psycholinguistics - the study of the psychological processes related to language
