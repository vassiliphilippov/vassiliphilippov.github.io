wave of criticism

noun
sudden large amount of criticism
Pronunciation: / weɪv əv krɪˈtɪsɪzəm / (AmE), / weɪv əv krɪˈtɪsɪzəm / (BrE)
a sudden large amount of criticism directed at someone or something

Examples

A wave of criticism
A wave of criticism followed the announcement of the new policy.
The company was hit by a wave of criticism when its product failed to meet expectations.
The politician faced a wave of criticism after making controversial remarks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flood of criticism - an overwhelming quantity of criticism
  • synonym: storm of criticism - a very large and intense outpouring of criticism
