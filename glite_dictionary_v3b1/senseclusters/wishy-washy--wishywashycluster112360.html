wishy-washy

adjective
weak or indecisive; lacking courage
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪʃi ˌwɑʃi / (AmE), / ˈwɪʃi ˌwɒʃi / (BrE)
lacking strength, firmness, or courage; weak and indecisive

Examples

A wishy-washy answer
He was too wishy-washy to stand up for what he believed in.
Her wishy-washy response convinced no one at the meeting.
Voters criticized the candidate for being wishy-washy on important issues.

Related Terms

  • antonym: courageous - showing courage; brave
  • synonym: spineless - lacking courage or determination
