perspectively

adverb
from a viewpoint
Pronunciation: / pərˈspɛktɪvli / (AmE), / pəˈspɛktɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or considers a particular point of view or perspective

Examples

Perspectively analyze issues
The problem must be analyzed perspectively to understand all possible outcomes.
She approached the debate perspectively, considering each side's arguments carefully.
When viewed perspectively, the artist's choices reveal a deeper meaning in the painting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: objectively - in a way not influenced by personal feelings or opinions
  • synonym: subjectively - in a way based on personal feelings or opinions
