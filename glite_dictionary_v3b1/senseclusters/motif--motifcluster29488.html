motif

noun
recurrent idea in art or literature
Pronunciation: / moʊˈtif / (AmE), / məʊˈtiːf / (BrE)
a distinctive or recurring theme or idea in a literary, artistic, or musical work

Examples

Literary motif
Classical music often uses a short melodic motif to unify a composition.
One recurring motif in her paintings is the image of a lone tree.
The story’s central motif is the struggle between good and evil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: theme - the central subject or topic in a literary or artistic work
