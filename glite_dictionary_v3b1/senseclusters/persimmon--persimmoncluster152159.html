persimmon

noun
type of sweet orange fruit
Pronunciation: / pərˈsɪmən / (AmE), / pəˈsɪmən / (BrE)
A sweet, orange fruit resembling a plum, edible when fully ripe, produced by trees of the genus Diospyros.

Examples

A ripe persimmon
The persimmon must be fully ripe before eating, or it will taste very astringent.
The dessert was topped with slices of fresh persimmon and a sprinkle of cinnamon.
We picked a basket of persimmons from the tree in my grandfather's garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plum - a sweet, fleshy fruit with a central stone
