positionless

adjective
not limited by role
Pronunciation: / pəˈzɪʃənləs / (AmE), / pəˈzɪʃənləs / (BrE)
not restricted to a specific position or role, especially in sports where players can perform multiple functions

Examples

Positionless basketball
The coach prefers a positionless approach, allowing players to switch roles during the game.
Modern basketball often features positionless athletes who can defend and attack from anywhere on the court.
Her positionless skills make her valuable in any lineup, adapting to whatever the team needs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: specialized - restricted to a specific role or function
  • synonym: versatile - able to adapt or be used for many different functions
