jade

noun
light green color
Pronunciation: / dʒeɪd / (AmE), / dʒeɪd / (BrE)
a light green color that varies from bluish green to yellowish green, similar to the color of the gemstone jade

Examples

Jade green color
She wore a beautiful dress in a rich jade shade.
The artist mixed just enough yellow and blue to achieve the perfect jade for his landscape painting.
The walls were painted a soothing jade to create a calming atmosphere.
