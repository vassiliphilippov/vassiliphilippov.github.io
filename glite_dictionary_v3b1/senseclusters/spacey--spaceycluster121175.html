spacey

adjective
ethereal; suggestive of outer space
6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈspeɪ.siː/ /; / /ˈspeɪ.sɪ/ (AmE), /ˈspeɪ.siː/ /; / /ˈspeɪ.sɪ/ (BrE)
having an ethereal, atmospheric, or futuristic quality that suggests outer space; used of music, art, visuals, or atmosphere.

Examples

Spacey music
The ambient soundtrack offered a spacey atmosphere that felt out of this world.
Her painting used soft blues and drifting shapes to create a quietly spacey effect.
Critics praised the producer's spacey synth textures for giving the album a futuristic edge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cosmic - relating to the universe or having a vast, space-like quality
  • synonym: ethereal - delicate and light in a way that seems not of this world
  • antonym: grounded - realistic, down-to-earth; opposite of an otherworldly or spacey quality
