ossification

noun
formation of bone tissue
4.6 8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːsəfəˈkeɪʃən /; / ˌɑːsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɒsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
