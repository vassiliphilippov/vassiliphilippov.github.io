polyfoam

noun
lightweight foam made from polyurethane
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑliˌfoʊm / (AmE), / ˈpɒliˌfəʊm / (BrE)
a type of lightweight, flexible foam made by combining water with polyurethane plastics, commonly used in insulation, packaging, and upholstery

Examples

Lightweight polyfoam material
For fragile shipments, polyfoam is commonly used as a protective packaging material.
Many modern sofas use polyfoam cushions instead of traditional stuffing.
The new mattress is made from polyfoam to provide extra comfort and support.

  • synonym: polyurethane - a polymer used in foam and other materials
