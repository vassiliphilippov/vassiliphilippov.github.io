Synonyms

  • chip in
    3.2 1/m
    chip in is informal and means people give money or effort together, while pitch in focuses on joining others to help.
  • contribute
    2.7 40/m
    contribute is a neutral formal word for giving money, work, or ideas, while pitch in emphasizes actively joining others to help.
  • join in
    1.1 7/m
    join in means taking part in an activity, often already happening, while pitch in stresses helping others by offering work.
  • kick in
    4.1 0.8/m
    kick in often means giving money or starting to pay, while pitch in means joining others to help with work or resources.
