Synonyms

  • crouch
    1.8 3/m
    crouch keeps your body low by bending knees and leaning forward, while get down simply means lower your body by bending or kneeling.
  • kneel
    1.4 7/m
    kneel means rest on your knees, often for respect or worship, while get down is a general way to lower your body.
  • duck down
    4.2 0.1/m
    duck down means lower your body quickly to avoid being seen or hit, while get down is more neutral lowering.
  • squat
    3.4 1/m
    squat is a deliberate exercise or movement where you bend and rise again, while get down just means lower your body.
