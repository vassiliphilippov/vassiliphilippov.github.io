spy satellite

noun
satellite used for surveillance or intelligence
6.4
Pronunciation: / spaɪ ˈsæt̬əlˌaɪt / (AmE), / spaɪ ˈsætəlʌɪt / (BrE)
a satellite equipped with sensors to observe and gather information, especially for military or intelligence purposes

Examples

A spy satellite system
A malfunction in the spy satellite delayed the transmission of crucial data.
Intelligence agencies rely heavily on spy satellites for real-time information.
The country launched a new spy satellite to monitor potential threats from space.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reconnaissance satellite - a satellite for photographing or observing the Earth, especially for military purposes
