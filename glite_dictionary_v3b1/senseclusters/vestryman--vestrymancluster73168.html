vestryman

noun
male member of a church vestry
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛstriˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈvɛstriˌmən / (BrE)
a man who is a member of a church vestry, responsible for the management of church affairs

Examples

A vestryman in church
After years of service, the retired vestryman was honored by the congregation.
As a vestryman, he was deeply involved in repairing the old chapel.
The vestryman spoke at the weekly church council meeting about the community outreach program.

Related Terms

  • antonym: vestrywoman - a woman who is a member of a church vestry
