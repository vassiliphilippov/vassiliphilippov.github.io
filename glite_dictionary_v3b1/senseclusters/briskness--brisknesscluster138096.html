briskness

noun
liveliness; energetic quality
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪsk.nəs / (AmE), / ˈbrɪsk.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being lively, energetic, and quick in movement or action

Examples

With briskness and energy
She completed her morning walk with admirable briskness, finishing well ahead of the others.
The briskness in his voice made everyone pay attention to his instructions.
With the briskness of a seasoned waitress, she served all the guests in record time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alacrity - eager and cheerful readiness
  • antonym: sluggishness - quality of being slow, inactive, or lacking energy
