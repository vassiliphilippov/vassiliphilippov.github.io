providence

noun
God's protective care; divine foresight
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑː.vɪ.dəns /; / ˈprɑːvɪdəns / (AmE), / ˈprɒv.ɪ.dəns /; / ˈprɒvɪdəns / (BrE)
the protective care and guidance of God or a spiritual power

Examples

Divine providence
Many people saw their survival during the famine as an act of providence.

Many people believe that providence guides the events of their lives.
She trusted in providence to see her through in difficult times.
The survivors attributed their escape to providence rather than luck.
