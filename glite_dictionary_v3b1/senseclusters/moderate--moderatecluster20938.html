moderate

noun
person with centrist political views
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːdərət / (AmE), / ˈmɒdərət / (BrE)
a person whose political opinions are not extreme, favoring neither the far right nor far left

Examples

A political moderate
As a moderate, she often supported bipartisan policies.
The party nominated a moderate to appeal to a broader electorate.
Voters seeking compromise frequently identify as moderate rather than partisan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: centrist - a person with moderate political views
  • antonym: radical - a person with extreme political views
