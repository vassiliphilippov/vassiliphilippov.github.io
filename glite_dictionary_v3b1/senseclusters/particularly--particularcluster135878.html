particularly

adverb
uniquely or characteristically
3.7
Pronunciation: / pərˈtɪkjələrli / (AmE), / pəˈtɪkjʊləli / (BrE)
In a way that is characteristic of a person, place, or thing; in a manner that is unique or especially typical.

Examples

Particularly French style
The painting was particularly French in its style and composition.
The recipe is particularly associated with the southern region of the country.
This novel is particularly notable for its use of regional dialects.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generally - in most cases; usually
  • synonym: uniquely - in a way that is one of a kind or distinctive
