short-lived

adjective
lasting a short time
3.0
Pronunciation: / ʃɔrt laɪvd / (AmE), / ʃɔːt lɪvd / (BrE)
lasting for only a short period; not enduring for long

Examples

A short-lived trend
Many trends in fashion are notoriously short-lived.
The beauty of the sunset was short-lived, fading quickly into darkness.
Their excitement proved to be short-lived when the event was cancelled.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ephemeral - lasting for a very short time
  • antonym: permanent - lasting or intended to last indefinitely
  • synonym: transient - lasting for only a short period of time
