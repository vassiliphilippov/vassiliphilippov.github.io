osteoplastic

adjective
bone repair or formation
Pronunciation: / ˌɑstiəˈplæstɪk / (AmE), / ˌɒstiəˈplæstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving the repair or formation of bone tissue

Examples

Osteoplastic surgery
The surgeon performed an osteoplastic procedure to reconstruct the patient's jaw.
Advances in osteoplastic techniques have improved outcomes for bone injuries.
An osteoplastic operation was necessary to reconstruct the damaged skull.

Related Terms

  • synonym: osteogenic - relating to the production of bone
  • antonym: osteolytic - relating to the destruction or dissolution of bone
