fop

noun
dandy; vain man concerned with fashion
Pronunciation: / fɑp / (AmE), / fɒp / (BrE)
a man who is excessively concerned with his clothes and appearance, often in an affected or vain manner

Examples

A fop man
Despite his reputation as a fop, he was surprisingly sharp-witted in debate.
He was dismissed as a fop by his more serious colleagues, who scoffed at his obsession with clothes.
The novel's villain is a vain fop who spends hours perfecting his attire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beau - a man who gives exaggerated attention to dress and appearance
  • synonym: dandy - a man unduly devoted to style, neatness, and fashion in dress and appearance
  • antonym: plain - a person not concerned with clothing or appearance
