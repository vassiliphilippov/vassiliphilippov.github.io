assessment

noun
amount determined as payable, e.g. as a fee or tax
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈsɛsmənt / (AmE), / əˈsɛsmənt / (BrE)
an amount of money that is officially determined as due or payable, often as a charge, tax, or fee for public or group expenses.

Examples

Special assessment fee
Each homeowner received a special assessment to fund the new community playground.
The annual assessment for trash collection must be paid by July 1st.
The club members were upset by the unexpected assessment for roof repairs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: assessment - the act of evaluating or appraising a person or situation
  • synonym: levy - an amount of money collected officially, especially for government purposes
  • synonym: tax - a compulsory contribution to state revenue
