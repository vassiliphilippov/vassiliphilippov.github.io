Merlin

proper noun
legendary magician and advisor to King Arthur
Pronunciation: / ˈmɜrlɪn / (AmE), / ˈmɜːlɪn / (BrE)
(in Arthurian legend) a legendary magician and prophet, famous as King Arthur's advisor and mentor.

Examples

Merlin the wizard
Many stories describe Merlin conjuring powerful spells to protect King Arthur's realm.
Some believe that Merlin foresaw the downfall of Camelot.
The wise counsel of Merlin guided Arthur through many challenges.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wizard - a man with magical powers, especially in legends and fairy tales
