vitelline

noun
egg yolk or sac
9.9
Pronunciation: / vɪˈtɛlaɪn / (AmE), / vɪˈtɛliːn / (BrE)
the yolk of an egg, or the yolk sac or yolk material in an embryo

Examples

The vitelline sac
The scientist carefully observed the vitelline under the microscope during the experiment.
Damage to the vitelline can affect the development of the embryo.
In some species, the vitelline provides essential nutrients for early growth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: yolk - the yellow part of an egg that nourishes the embryo
