Joe

proper noun
male given name
Pronunciation: / dʒoʊ / (AmE), / dʒəʊ / (BrE)
a common male given name, often used as a short form of Joseph or as a generic name for an average man

Examples

Joe Smith
My uncle's name is Joe, and he visits every summer to help in the garden.
At the meeting, Joe introduced himself as the new project manager.
Everyone was happy when Joe brought homemade cookies to the office.
The movie tells the story of an ordinary Joe who becomes a hero.
Anyone can relate to Joe in this situation because he represents the common man.
The advertisement featured a typical Joe enjoying a cup of coffee before work.
