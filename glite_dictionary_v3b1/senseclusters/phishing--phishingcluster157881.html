phishing

adjective
fraudulent online attempt
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃɪŋ / (BrE)
describing a message, website, or attempt intended to trick people into revealing personal or financial information online

Examples

Phishing email alert
He received a phishing email that looked like it was from his bank.
Always check for phishing links before entering your password online.
The company warned employees about phishing websites that mimic official pages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fraudulent - intended to deceive or cheat
  • antonym: legitimate - genuine and not intended to deceive
