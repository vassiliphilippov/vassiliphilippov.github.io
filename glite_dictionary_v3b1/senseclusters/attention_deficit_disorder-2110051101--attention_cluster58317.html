Synonyms

  • attention deficit hyperactivity disorder
    3.8 0.5/m
    attention deficit hyperactivity disorder is the formal medical name used by doctors and textbooks, while attention deficit disorder sounds older and less precise.
  • ADHD
    4.0 10/m
    ADHD is the common abbreviation used in speech and clinics, while attention deficit disorder is the older full name or narrower label.
  • ADD
    4.0 4/m
    ADD is an older, informal label often used for quiet inattentive cases, while attention deficit disorder is the clinical term describing the condition.
