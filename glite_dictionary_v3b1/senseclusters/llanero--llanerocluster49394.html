llanero

noun
folk music performer
9.9
Pronunciation: / jaˈneɾoʊ / (AmE), / jaˈneərəʊ / (BrE)
a performer or musician who plays traditional llanero folk music, especially styles like joropo from the plains of Venezuela and Colombia

Examples

Llanero musician
The llanero captivated the audience with his energetic harp playing and singing.
Many young musicians dream of becoming a famous llanero in their region.
She learned the traditional songs from a local llanero during her visit to Venezuela.

Related Terms

  • synonym: joropo - a lively traditional music and dance from Venezuela and Colombia
