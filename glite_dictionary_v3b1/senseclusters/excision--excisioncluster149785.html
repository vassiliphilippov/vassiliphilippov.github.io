Synonyms

  • cutting out
    6.9 0.72/m
    cutting out is the plain, everyday phrase for surgically removing a part, while excision is the technical surgical term.
  • exeresis
    9.9 0.00/m
    exeresis is a rare medical word for surgical removal, while excision is the more common surgical term used in general.
  • extirpation
    9.9 0.01/m
    extirpation means complete surgical removal of tissue or growth, while excision can mean cutting out a smaller part or lesion.
  • resection
    7.8 0.45/m
    resection is the removal of part of an organ in surgery, while excision is a general term for cutting out tissue.
  • ablation
    8.3 0.29/m
    ablation is removing tissue by cutting, heat, or drugs to treat disease, while excision usually means cutting out by surgery.
  • abscission
    9.9 0.01/m
    abscission is the act of cutting off that can happen naturally or surgically, while excision usually refers to surgical cutting out.
