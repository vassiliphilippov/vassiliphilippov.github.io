protectant

noun
protective substance
Pronunciation: / prəˈtɛktənt / (AmE), / prəˈtɛktənt / (BrE)
a substance that is applied to protect something from damage, harm, or deterioration

Examples

A protectant spray
It is important to apply a protectant to your car's paint to prevent fading.
Gardeners often use a protectant to shield plants from pests and harsh weather.
Before hiking, she sprayed her boots with a water-resistant protectant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coating - a layer of a substance spread over a surface for protection
  • synonym: sealant - a substance used to block the passage of fluids through surfaces
