fictively

adverb
in an imaginary way
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪktɪvli / (AmE), / ˈfɪktɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that is imagined, invented, or not real; as if in fiction

Examples

Set fictively in future
The novel is fictively set in a distant future where humans live on Mars.
He fictively described himself as a famous explorer to entertain the children.
The artist fictively reimagined the city skyline in her latest painting.

  • synonym: imaginarily - in a way that exists only in the imagination
