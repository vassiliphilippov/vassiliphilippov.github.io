Branta leucopsis

noun
species of European goose (barnacle goose)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbræntə luːˈkɑpsɪs / (AmE), / ˈbræntə ljuːˈkɒpsɪs / (BrE)
A European species of goose, smaller than the brant, which breeds in the far north; commonly known as the barnacle goose.

Examples

Branta leucopsis goose
Researchers observed a flock of Branta leucopsis nesting near the Arctic shoreline.
The Branta leucopsis migrates south to spend the winter in coastal Europe.
Unlike the brant, the Branta leucopsis has a distinct white face and smaller stature.
