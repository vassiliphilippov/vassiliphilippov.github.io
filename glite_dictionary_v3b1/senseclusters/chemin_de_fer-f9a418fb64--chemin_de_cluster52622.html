chemin de fer

noun
casino card game, variant of baccarat
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʃəˌmæn də ˈfɛr / (AmE), / ʃəˌmæn də ˈfeə / (BrE)
a card game, especially a form of baccarat, played in casinos where players bet against the banker to reach a total closest to nine

Examples

Chemin de fer card game
Baccarat and chemin de fer are popular among high rollers.
He spent the evening at the casino playing chemin de fer with his friends.
She learned how to play chemin de fer during her trip to Monte Carlo.
