unstable

adjective
suffering from severe mental illness
1.0 3.6
Pronunciation: /ə n.ˈsteɪ.bəl/ /; / /ʌn.ˈsteɪ.bəl/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈsteɪ.bəl/ /; / /ə n.ˈsteɪ.bəl/ (BrE)
(of a person) emotionally or mentally unpredictable or likely to have sudden changes in mood or behavior

Examples

Unstable person
After the tragedy, he became increasingly unstable and withdrawn from his friends.
Her unstable mental state worried her family and colleagues.
The hospital specializes in treating unstable patients who need constant supervision.
After the traumatic event, she became noticeably more unstable in her behavior and decisions.
His unstable personality made it difficult for him to keep long-term relationships.
The child's unstable emotions led to frequent mood swings throughout the day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inconstant - not constant; changing often, especially in loyalty or mood
  • synonym: mentally ill - having a mental illness
  • antonym: stable - not suffering from mental illness; emotionally steady
  • synonym: unsteady - not firm, fixed, or perfectly balanced; can also describe a person’s state of mind
