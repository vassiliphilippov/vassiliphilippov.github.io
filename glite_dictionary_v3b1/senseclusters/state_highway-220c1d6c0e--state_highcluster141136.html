state highway

noun
major road maintained by a state
2.3
Pronunciation: / steɪt ˈhaɪˌweɪ / (AmE), / steɪt ˈhaɪweɪ / (BrE)
A major public road maintained by a state government, designed for high-speed traffic.

Examples

A state highway road
Many small towns are connected by a network of state highways.
The state highway is closed for repairs after the recent flood.
We took the state highway to avoid the congestion in the city center.

Related Terms

  • synonym: motorway - a wide road for fast traffic, especially in the UK
