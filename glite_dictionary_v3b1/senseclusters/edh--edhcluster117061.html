Pronunciation: / ɛð / (AmE), / ɛð / (BrE)
a letter (ð) used in Old English and phonetic alphabets to represent the voiced th sound, as in 'this'

Examples

The letter edh
The letter edh was used in Old English to write words like 'þeod'.
In the International Phonetic Alphabet, edh represents the sound in 'this' and 'breathe'.
Linguists often refer to edh when discussing historical English spelling.
