G-man

noun
male FBI agent
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːˌman / (BrE)
a male special agent of the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI), especially one involved in law enforcement or criminal investigations

Examples

G-man in trench coat
Old Hollywood movies often depict a G-man in a trench coat tracking criminals.
Serving as a G-man, he worked on several high-profile cases in the 1940s.
The notorious gangster was finally caught by a G-man after years on the run.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: detective - a person, especially a police officer, whose job is to investigate crimes
  • synonym: federal agent - an agent employed by a federal law enforcement agency in the US
  • degree higher: secret agent - someone employed by a government to obtain secret information or perform covert operations
