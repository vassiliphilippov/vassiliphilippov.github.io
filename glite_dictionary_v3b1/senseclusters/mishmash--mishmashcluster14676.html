mishmash

noun
confused mixture; jumble
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪʃˌmæʃ / (AmE), / ˈmɪʃˌmæʃ / (BrE)
A confused mixture or jumble of different things.

Examples

A mishmash of styles
Her living room decor is a mishmash of old and new furniture.
The festival was a mishmash of different cultures, foods, and traditions.
The report ended up as a confusing mishmash of unrelated ideas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jumble - an untidy or confused mixture
  • synonym: medley - a varied mixture of people or things
