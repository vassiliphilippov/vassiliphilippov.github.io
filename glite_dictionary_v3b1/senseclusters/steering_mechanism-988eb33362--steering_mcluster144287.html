steering mechanism

noun
system for controlling direction of movement
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪrɪŋ ˈmɛkəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈstɪərɪŋ ˈmɛkənɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
a system of components that controls the direction in which a vehicle or object moves, especially in motor vehicles

Examples

Vehicle steering mechanism
After the accident, the steering mechanism had to be repaired before the truck could be driven again.
Modern bicycles often feature a simple yet effective steering mechanism connected to the handlebars.
The steering mechanism in this car is extremely responsive, allowing for precise turns.
