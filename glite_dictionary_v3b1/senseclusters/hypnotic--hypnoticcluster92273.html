hypnotic

adjective
relating to hypnosis
2.6
Pronunciation: / hɪpˈnɑtɪk / (AmE), / hɪpˈnɒtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or producing hypnosis; having the quality of inducing hypnosis or a trance-like state

Examples

Hypnotic trance
Her voice was so hypnotic that everyone in the room listened in silence.
Researchers studied the hypnotic effects of certain repetitive sounds.
The therapist used a hypnotic technique to help the patient recall memories.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mesmeric - of or relating to mesmerism or inducing a trance-like state
