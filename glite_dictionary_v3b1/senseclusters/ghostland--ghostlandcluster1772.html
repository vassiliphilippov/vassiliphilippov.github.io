ghostland

noun
deserted area
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈgoʊst.lænd/ /; / /ˈgoʊst.lənd/ (AmE), /ˈgəʊst.lænd/ /; / /ˈgəʊst.lənd/ (BrE)
a deserted or abandoned place where there are very few people or little activity, often used figuratively

Examples

Deserted ghostland
After the factory closed, the whole district became a ghostland of empty stores.
The abandoned amusement park felt like a ghostland at night, silent and eerie.
Once a lively market, the square is now a ghostland with only a few visitors each week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ghost town - a town that has been abandoned and is no longer inhabited
  • antonym: metropolis - a large, busy city with many people and activities
  • synonym: wasteland - an area that is empty, neglected, or unused
