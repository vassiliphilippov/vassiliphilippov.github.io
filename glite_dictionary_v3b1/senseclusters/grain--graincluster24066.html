grain

noun
the smallest possible unit; a tiny amount
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪn / (AmE), / ɡreɪn / (BrE)
a very small amount of a quality or substance; the smallest possible unit of something, often used figuratively.

Examples

A grain of truth
Even a grain of kindness can make someone's day better.
She didn't show a grain of fear during the storm.
There is not a grain of evidence to support his claims.

Related Terms

  • synonym: particle - an extremely small piece or amount of something
  • synonym: speck - a tiny spot or small amount
  • synonym: trace - a very small amount of something
