haha

noun
sound of laughter
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːˌhɑː / (AmE), / ˈhɑːˌhɑː / (BrE)
a short laugh or the sound of laughter, often written to show amusement in text or speech

Examples

A loud haha
She let out a loud haha when she saw the funny video.
His message ended with a haha to show he was joking.
The children burst into a cheerful haha during the game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chuckle - a quiet or suppressed laugh
  • synonym: giggle - a light, silly laugh
  • antonym: sob - a sound of crying
