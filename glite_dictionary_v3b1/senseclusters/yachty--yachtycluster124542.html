yachty

noun
yacht enthusiast
Pronunciation: / ˈjɑti / (AmE), / ˈjɒti / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who owns, sails, or frequently spends time on a yacht

Examples

A yachty at sea
As a seasoned yachty, he knew how to handle rough seas and sudden squalls.
The marina was filled with yachties preparing for the annual regatta.
She became a true yachty after spending every summer sailing along the coast.

  • synonym: yachtsman - a person who sails or owns a yacht
