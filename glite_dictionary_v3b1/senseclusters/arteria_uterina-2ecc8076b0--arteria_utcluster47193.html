arteria uterina

noun
artery supplying uterus and vagina
8.1
Pronunciation: /ɑːr.ˈtɪr.iː.ə juː.ˈtɪr.ɪ.nə/ /; / /ɑːr.ˈtɪr.iː.ə juː.tə.ˈriː.nə/ (AmE), /ɑː.ˈtɪə.ri.nə juː.tə.ˈriː.nə/ /; / /ɑː.ˈtɪə.ri.nə juː.ˈtɪə.rɪ.nə/ (BrE)
a branch of the internal iliac artery that supplies blood to the uterus and the upper part of the vagina
