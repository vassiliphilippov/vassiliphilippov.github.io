State of Israel

proper noun
official name for the country of Israel
Pronunciation: / steɪt əv ˈɪzrəɪl / (AmE), / steɪt əv ˈɪzreɪl / (BrE)
The official name for the country of Israel, a Jewish republic in southwestern Asia at the eastern end of the Mediterranean Sea, established in 1948.
