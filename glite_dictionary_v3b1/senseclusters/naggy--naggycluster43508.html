naggy

adjective
complaining persistently
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnæɡi / (AmE), / ˈnæɡi / (BrE)
often complaining or criticizing in an annoying or persistent way

Examples

A naggy voice
His naggy attitude made it hard for his friends to relax around him.
She became naggy about the unfinished homework, reminding her brother every hour.
Nobody likes a naggy coworker who constantly points out small mistakes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: complaining - expressing dissatisfaction or annoyance
  • synonym: critical - tending to find fault or judge harshly
  • antonym: supportive - giving encouragement or help
