unhealthily

adverb
in a harmful way
3.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɛlθɪli / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛlθɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is harmful or not good for health (physical, mental, or social)

Examples

Eat unhealthily
He ate unhealthily for several years before changing his diet.
Working late every night can lead you to live unhealthily without enough rest.
Some people cope with stress by unhealthily avoiding meals or eating too much junk food.
He was unhealthily focused on his appearance, ignoring other important aspects of life.
The children were unhealthily attached to their electronic devices, rarely playing outside.
She worried that her son was eating unhealthily after moving away to college.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dangerously - in a way that is likely to cause harm or injury
  • synonym: harmfully - in a way that causes harm or damage
  • antonym: healthily - in a way that is good for health
