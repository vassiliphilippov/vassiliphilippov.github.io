land

verb
deliver a blow or hit
Pronunciation: / lænd / (AmE), / lænd / (BrE)
to deliver (a blow, hit, or punch) with force and accuracy

Examples

To land a punch

She was quick enough to land several blows before the referee intervened.
The boxer managed to land a punch to his opponent's jaw in the final round.
