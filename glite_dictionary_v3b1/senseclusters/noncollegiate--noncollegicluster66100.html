noncollegiate

adjective
not college-affiliated
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkəˈlidʒiət / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkəˈliːdʒiət / (BrE)
not related to, connected with, or affiliated with a college or university

Examples

Noncollegiate sports team
After graduation, she joined a noncollegiate basketball league in her city.
The organization offers noncollegiate courses for adults seeking new skills.
He preferred a noncollegiate environment for his professional training.
