decrepitly

adverb
in a decayed manner
Pronunciation: / dɪˈkrɛpɪtli / (AmE), / dɪˈkrɛpɪtli / (BrE)
in a way that shows weakness, decay, or poor condition due to age or neglect

Examples

To stand decrepitly
The abandoned factory stood decrepitly at the edge of town, its windows broken and walls crumbling.
He walked decrepitly across the room, his movements slow and unsteady from years of hard labor.
The old bridge decrepitly spanned the river, barely holding together after decades without repair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feebly - in a weak or frail manner
  • antonym: robustly - in a strong or healthy manner
