brocatelle

noun
decorative upholstery fabric
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌbroʊkəˈtɛl / (AmE), / ˌbrɒkəˈtɛl / (BrE)
a heavy, richly patterned fabric with raised designs, often used for upholstery, drapery, or ceremonial garments
