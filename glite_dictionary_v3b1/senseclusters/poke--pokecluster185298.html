poke

noun
archaic term for a bag
Pronunciation: / poʊk / (AmE), / pəʊk / (BrE)
(archaic, dialectal) a bag, especially one made of paper or plastic, used for carrying purchases or goods.

Examples

A paper poke
In Scotland, fish and chips are sometimes served in a newspaper poke.
She brought her groceries home in a brown poke.
The shopkeeper handed the sweets to the child in a small paper poke.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bag - a container made of flexible material, used for carrying things
