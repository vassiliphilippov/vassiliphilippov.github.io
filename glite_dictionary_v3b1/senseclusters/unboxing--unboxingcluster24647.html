unboxing

verb
to open a package
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɑksɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɒksɪŋ / (BrE)
opening a package or product, especially to reveal its contents for the first time.

Examples

To unbox a phone
She filmed herself unboxing the latest smartphone for her YouTube channel.
The children were excited while unboxing their birthday presents together.
Many people enjoy watching unboxing videos to see what is inside new products.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pack - to put things into a container or box
  • synonym: unwrap - to remove the wrapping from something
