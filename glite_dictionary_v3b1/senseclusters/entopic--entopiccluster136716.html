entopic

adjective
in normal position
8.9
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈtɑpɪk / (AmE), / ɛnˈtɒpɪk / (BrE)
located in the normal or usual place (especially in the body)

Examples

An entopic pregnancy occurs when the embryo implants in the uterus, its normal location.
