electric burn

noun
burn caused by electric current
4.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪk bɝːn / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk bɜːn / (BrE)
A burn caused by heat generated from an electric current passing through the body or tissue.

Examples

Electric burns injury
Electric burns can cause deep tissue damage and require immediate medical attention.
She was hospitalized because of a severe electric burn on her arm.
The patient suffered an electric burn to his hand after touching a live wire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chemical burn - a burn caused by contact with chemicals
  • synonym: thermal burn - a burn caused by external heat such as fire or hot objects
