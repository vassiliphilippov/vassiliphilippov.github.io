leftover pizza

noun
uneaten pizza saved for later
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛftˌoʊvɚ ˈpit͡sə / (AmE), / ˌlɛftˌəʊvə ˈpiːtsə / (BrE)
pizza that remains after a meal and is kept to be eaten later

Examples

A box of leftover pizza
After the party, there was a box of leftover pizza in the fridge.
He grabbed a slice of leftover pizza as a midnight snack.
She heated up the leftover pizza for her lunch the next day.
