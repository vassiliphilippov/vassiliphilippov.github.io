starstruck

adjective
admiring celebrities
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrˌstrʌk / (AmE), / ˈstɑːˌstrʌk / (BrE)
feeling very impressed, excited, or in awe when meeting or seeing a famous person

Examples

To be starstruck by celebrities
The young fans were completely starstruck when they met the famous singer after the concert.
He became starstruck at the movie premiere, unable to speak to any of the actors.
Despite working in the industry for years, she still gets starstruck around celebrities.
He became starstruck when he saw the famous singer at the airport.
Many fans feel starstruck during their first visit to a movie premiere.
She was so starstruck that she could barely speak to the celebrity chef.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awed - filled with admiration or wonder
  • antonym: unimpressed - not feeling admiration or respect
