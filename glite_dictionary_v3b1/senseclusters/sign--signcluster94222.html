sign

noun
structure displaying advertisements or information
Pronunciation: / saɪn / (AmE), / saɪn / (BrE)
a board, structure, or public display that gives information, instructions, or advertisements

Examples

A sign on the road
Drivers slowed down to read the sign advertising the upcoming music festival.
The sign in front of the store was old and faded, so they decided to replace it.
The new mall put up a brightly colored sign near the main road to attract customers.
Protesters carried signs demanding action from the government.
She hung a sign on the door to let customers know the store was closed.
The park entrance has a large sign with the opening hours.

Related Terms

  • synonym: billboard - a large outdoor board for displaying advertisements
  • degree lower: label - a small tag or sticker attached to something giving information
  • synonym: notice - a written or printed announcement displayed publicly
  • degree lower: poster - a large printed picture, notice, or advertisement displayed in a public place
