mindlessness

noun
lack of meaning or intelligent thought
3.3 5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪnd.ləs.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪnd.ləs.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality or state of lacking thought, meaning, or care

Examples

The mindlessness of actions
Critics complained about the mindlessness of the movie's plot.
She was bothered by the mindlessness of the endless television advertisements.
The mindlessness of his daily routine left him feeling empty and disconnected.
Acts of mindlessness can often cause unintended harm to others.
She was frustrated by the mindlessness of the repetitive tasks at work.
The mindlessness of his actions led to serious consequences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emptiness - the quality of lacking meaning or substance
  • synonym: recklessness - the quality of being careless about danger or the consequences of actions
  • antonym: thoughtfulness - the quality of thinking carefully or showing good sense
