naturist

noun
person who practices nudity for health or recreation
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃərɪst / (AmE), / ˈnatʃərɪst / (BrE)
a person who practices or advocates living in the nude, especially for reasons of health, recreation, or religion

Examples

A naturist lifestyle
As a lifelong naturist, he believes nudity promotes a healthier body image.
Many European beaches welcome naturists during the summer season.
The campground is popular among naturists who enjoy outdoor living in the nude.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nudist - a person who engages in nudity, especially as a social practice
