mosquito craft

noun
small fast torpedo boat
Pronunciation: / məˈskitoʊ kræft / (AmE), / məˈskiːtəʊ krɑːft / (BrE)
a small, fast, lightly armed and unarmored torpedo boat, used especially for patrol or attack

Examples

A mosquito craft boat
Because mosquito craft were lightly armored, their crews needed to depend on speed and maneuverability.
During the war, several mosquito craft patrolled the coastline to intercept enemy ships.
The navy relied on mosquito craft for quick strikes and reconnaissance missions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: PT boat - a fast attack craft equipped with torpedoes, used by the US Navy especially in World War II
