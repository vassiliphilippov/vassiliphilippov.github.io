deistic

adjective
relating to deism
Pronunciation: / diˈɪstɪk / (AmE), / diːˈɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of deism or deists.

Examples

Deistic beliefs
His views were considered deistic rather than strictly orthodox.
Some philosophers promote a deistic understanding of the universe.
The founding fathers of the United States often held deistic beliefs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: theistic - relating to belief in the existence of a god or gods
