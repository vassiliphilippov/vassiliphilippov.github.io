subscribe

verb
apply to buy shares or stocks
3.6
Pronunciation: / səbˈskraɪb / (AmE), / səbˈskraɪb / (BrE)
to apply to buy or agree to purchase shares or stocks, typically during a new issue

Examples

To subscribe for shares
Many investors chose to subscribe for shares in the company's initial public offering.
She advised her client to subscribe before the deadline to secure the shares.
The firm decided to subscribe 10,000 units of the new stock issue.
