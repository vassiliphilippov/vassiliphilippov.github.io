Ostyak language

noun
Ugric language of the Ostyak (Khanty) people
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑstiˌæk ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɒstɪˌak ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A Ugric language of western Siberia, related to Hungarian, spoken by the Ostyak people (Khanty).

Examples

Ostyak language spoken
Few people outside Russia speak the Ostyak language today.
Linguists have studied the Ostyak language for its relation to Hungarian and other Uralic tongues.
The grammar of the Ostyak language features vowel harmony and agglutination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Hungarian - the Ugric language of Hungary
  • synonym: Khanty - the modern name for both the Ostyak people and their language
