predominate

verb
to be greater in number or influence
1.0
Pronunciation: / prɪˈdoʊmɪˌneɪt / (AmE), / prɪˈdɒmɪneɪt / (BrE)
to have greater numbers, strength, influence, or importance than others in a particular group or situation

Examples

Predominate in the group
In this region, pine trees predominate over other types of vegetation.
New technologies predominate the discussions at the annual industry conference.
Women predominate in the nursing profession across many countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dominate - to have a commanding influence on
  • synonym: prevail - to prove more powerful or superior
