Synonyms

  • preponderate
    9.4 0.01/m
    preponderate is used in formal texts to mean being greater in influence or number, while predominate is more general and common.
  • dominate
    3.8 20/m
    dominate means to control or be the most noticeable part of something, while predominate focuses on being larger or more influential.
  • prevail
    5.1 19/m
    prevail often means to win or become widespread over time, while predominate stresses greater numbers or influence now.
  • reign
    5.2 4/m
    reign suggests a strong or lasting influence in a place or time, while predominate simply means to be greater.
