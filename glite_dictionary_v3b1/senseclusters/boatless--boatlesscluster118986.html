boatless

adjective
without a boat
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊtləs / (AmE), / ˈbəʊtləs / (BrE)
not having or lacking a boat

Examples

Completely boatless
After selling his canoe, he was completely boatless for the summer.
The boatless tourists watched enviously as others sailed across the lake.
She felt boatless and left out when her friends went fishing on the river.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boated - having a boat
  • synonym: landlocked - having no access to the sea
