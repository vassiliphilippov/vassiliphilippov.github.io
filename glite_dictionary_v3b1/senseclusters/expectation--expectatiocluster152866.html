expectation

noun
mean value in statistics
3.2 6.3
Pronunciation: /ek.spek.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ɪk.spek.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ek.spek.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
In statistics and probability, the mean or average value of a random variable, calculated as the sum of all possible values divided by their number.

Examples

Statistical expectation
Statisticians often refer to the expectation as the mean or average in probability distributions.
The expectation of a fair die roll is 3.5.
You can calculate the expectation of a random variable by summing the products of possible values and their probabilities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mean - the average value in a set of numbers
