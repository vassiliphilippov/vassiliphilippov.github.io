puka

noun
South American shrub or small tree
Pronunciation: / ˈpuːkə / (AmE), / ˈpuːkə / (BrE)
A South American shrub or small tree with long, shiny evergreen leaves and clusters of green or yellow flowers.

Examples

Bolivian puka tree
Researchers discovered a rare puka specimen thriving in the rainforests of Bolivia.
The puka is valued for its attractive evergreen foliage in local gardens.
The botanical guide described the puka as a species with clusters of yellow flowers.
