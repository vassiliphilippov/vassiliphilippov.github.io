Pompeian

noun
resident of Pompeii
Pronunciation: / pɑmˈpeɪən / (AmE), / pɒmˈpeɪən / (BrE)
a person who lived in or came from the ancient Roman city of Pompeii

Examples

An ancient Pompeian
The ancient Pompeians left behind detailed frescoes on their villa walls.
A Pompeian merchant might have sold goods in the bustling marketplace.
Archaeologists study the lives of Pompeians through the ruins preserved by volcanic ash.
