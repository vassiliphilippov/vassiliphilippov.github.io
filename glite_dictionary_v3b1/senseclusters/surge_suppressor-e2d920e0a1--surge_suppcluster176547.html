Synonyms

    surge protector is the common everyday name for devices that stop sudden voltage spikes, while surge suppressor is a technical term for the same device.
  • spike suppressor
    6.3 0.00/m
    spike suppressor protects against very short current or voltage bursts at outlets, while surge suppressor covers both short spikes and longer surges.
  • spike arrester
    7.2 0.00/m
    spike arrester sits on power lines and diverts sudden high-voltage spikes to ground, while surge suppressor protects equipment at outlets.
  • arrestor
    7.5 0.00/m
