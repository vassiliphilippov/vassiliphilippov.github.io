winding

noun
curve or twist
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪndɪŋ / (BrE)
a curve, twist, or spiral, especially in a path, wire, or river

Examples

A winding in wire
The winding of the mountain road made driving slow and difficult.
She admired the winding of the river as it snaked through the valley.
The electrician checked the copper winding inside the motor for damage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bend - a curve or angle in something that is usually straight
  • antonym: straight - without bends or curves
