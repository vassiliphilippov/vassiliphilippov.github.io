chesty

adjective
arrogant or prideful
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛsti / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛsti / (BrE)
(of a person) having or showing an exaggerated sense of self-importance or pride; arrogant.

Examples

Chesty attitude
Don't be so chesty; everyone contributed to the project.
His chesty behavior annoyed the rest of the team.
Jason became quite chesty after receiving the award at work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrogant - having or revealing an exaggerated sense of one's own importance or abilities
  • antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low estimate of one's importance
