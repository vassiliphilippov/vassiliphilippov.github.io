nonparty

adjective
not affiliated politically
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈpɑːrti / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈpɑːti / (BrE)
not belonging to or associated with any political party

Examples

Nonparty voter
She prefers to remain nonparty and does not support any political group.
The debate included both party and nonparty candidates for the city council.
His nonparty stance appealed to voters tired of traditional politics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: independent - not belonging to any political party
  • antonym: partisan - strongly supporting a particular political party
