complex number

noun
number with a real and imaginary part
1.0 5.1
Pronunciation: /kəm.ˈpleks ˈnʌm.bər/ /; / /ˈkɑːm.pleks ˈnʌm.bər/ (AmE), /kəm.ˈpleks ˈnʌm.bə/ /; / /ˈkɒm.pleks ˈnʌm.bə/ (BrE)
