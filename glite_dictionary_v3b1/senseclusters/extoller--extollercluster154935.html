extoller

noun
person who praises highly
9.2
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstoʊlər / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɒlə / (BrE)
a person who praises something or someone enthusiastically and publicly; one who extols.

Examples

An extoller of values
As a dedicated extoller of traditional values, he often gave impassioned speeches at community events.
She became an avid extoller of his artistic talent, speaking about it at every opportunity.
The literary critic was no mere observer but a vocal extoller of emerging poets.

Related Terms

  • antonym: detractor - someone who criticizes or belittles
  • synonym: praiser - someone who gives praise
