prosthodontics

noun
branch of dentistry replacing teeth with artificial devices
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˌprɑːsθəˈdɑnɪks / (AmE), / ˌprɒsθəˈdɒntɪks / (BrE)
the branch of dentistry concerned with the design, creation, and placement of artificial replacements for teeth and other parts of the mouth or jaw

Examples

Prosthodontics treatment
Advances in prosthodontics have greatly improved the appearance and function of dental implants.
After losing several teeth in an accident, he was referred to a specialist in prosthodontics.
Students planning to focus on prosthodontics must complete extra years of training beyond dental school.
