fulcrum

noun
central or pivotal point
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊlkrəm / (AmE), / ˈfʌlkrəm / (BrE)
the central or most important point in an activity, event, or situation; a pivotal or crucial element in a relationship or discussion

Examples

Fulcrum of the story
In the novel, the secret meeting acts as a fulcrum for the entire plot.
She became the fulcrum of the team's efforts to win the championship.
Trust is often the fulcrum on which strong relationships are balanced.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pivot - the central point, pin, or shaft on which a mechanism turns or oscillates; a key person or thing
