despotically

adverb
in a tyrannical way
8.8
Pronunciation: / dɛsˈpɑːtɪkli / (AmE), / dɛsˈpɒtɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is tyrannical, oppressive, or like a despot

Examples

To rule despotically
The king ruled despotically, ignoring the needs and rights of his people.
Workers complained that the manager acted despotically when making decisions.
Throughout history, some leaders have governed despotically to maintain absolute power.

Related Terms

  • antonym: democratically - in a way that involves equal participation and freedom
  • synonym: oppressively - in a harsh or unjustly controlling way
  • synonym: tyrannically - in a cruel and oppressive way
