any port in a storm

x
accepting any help in a crisis
Pronunciation: / ˌɛni ˈpɔrt ɪn ə ˈstɔrm / (AmE), / ˌɛni ˈpɔːt ɪn ə ˈstɔːm / (BrE)
used to say that in a difficult or dangerous situation, any solution or refuge, however unideal, is acceptable

Examples

Any port in a storm
In desperate times, people take any port in a storm to get back on their feet.
She didn’t love the job, but it was any port in a storm after being unemployed for months.
When my flight was cancelled, staying at a cheap motel was any port in a storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: last resort - the only remaining possible option
  • synonym: refuge - a safe place or shelter from trouble or danger
