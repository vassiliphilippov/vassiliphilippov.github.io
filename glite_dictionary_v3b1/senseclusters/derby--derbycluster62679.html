derby

noun
hard felt hat with a narrow brim
Pronunciation: / ˈdɜrbi / (AmE), / ˈdɑːbi / (BrE)
a hard, round felt hat with a narrow brim, typically worn by men

Examples

A black derby hat
He tipped his derby politely as he entered the room.
In old movies, businessmen often wore a black derby to work.
The actor's costume included a classic derby and a cane.
