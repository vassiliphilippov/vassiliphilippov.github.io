give a snook

verb
show contempt or disrespect (rude gesture)
8.3 8.7
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv ə snʊk / (AmE), / ɡɪv ə snuːk / (BrE)
to show contempt or disrespect, especially by making a rude gesture such as putting one's thumb to one's nose and wiggling the fingers

Examples

To give a snook at fans
After losing the match, he gave a snook at the opposing team's fans.
Some children would give a snook to authority figures when they felt rebellious.
The student gave a snook to the teacher when told to sit down.
