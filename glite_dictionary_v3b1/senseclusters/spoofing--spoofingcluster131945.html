spoofing

noun
online deception
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈspuː.fɪŋ/ /; / /ˈspʊ.fɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈspuː.fɪŋ/ /; / /ˈspʊ.fɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of pretending to be someone or something else, especially online, in order to deceive

Examples

Email spoofing scam
Cybercriminals often use spoofing to make emails look like they come from trusted sources.
The company warned customers about phone spoofing scams targeting their personal information.
Website spoofing can trick users into entering passwords on fake login pages.

Related Terms

  • antonym: authentication - the process of verifying identity
  • synonym: impersonation - the act of pretending to be another person
  • synonym: phishing - the act of tricking people into giving personal information online
