thunderousness

noun
the state of being very loud, like thunder
Pronunciation: / ˈθʌndɚəsnɪs / (AmE), / ˈθʌndərəsnɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being extremely loud and deep, like thunder.

Examples

The thunderousness of drums
She was startled by the thunderousness of the drums during the parade.
The thunderousness of the applause filled the entire concert hall.
The thunderousness of the waterfall drowned out their voices.

Related Terms

  • antonym: quietness - the quality of being quiet
