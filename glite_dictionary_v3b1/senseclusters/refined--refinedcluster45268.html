refined

adjective
elegant, tasteful, sophisticated
3.7
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfaɪnd / (AmE), / rɪˈfaɪnd / (BrE)
(of a person, behavior, or style) showing elegance, good taste, or cultured manners

Examples

Refined manners
His refined manners impressed everyone at the formal dinner.
She has a refined sense of style and never looks out of place at elegant events.
The restaurant features a refined atmosphere, with soft music and tasteful decor.
Despite growing up in a small town, she developed refined tastes in art and cuisine.
He always dresses and acts in a refined way, no matter the occasion.
Her manners were so refined that everyone felt at ease around her.
Her refined manners made her a favorite guest at formal dinners.
The diplomat spoke in a refined tone that impressed everyone in the room.
Years of travel gave him a refined understanding of international customs.

  • synonym: elegant - pleasingly graceful and stylish in appearance or manner
