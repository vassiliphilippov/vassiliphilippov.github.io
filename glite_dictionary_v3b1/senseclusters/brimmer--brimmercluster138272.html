brimmer

noun
wide-brimmed hat
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪmər / (AmE), / ˈbrɪmə / (BrE)
a hat with a brim, especially one with a wide or projecting brim

Examples

A wide brimmer
She shaded her eyes with a wide brimmer as she walked through the sunlit market.
He wore a stylish brimmer to keep cool during the summer festival.
The old photograph showed a man in a large brimmer standing by the river.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cap - a soft, close-fitting hat, often without a brim
  • synonym: sunhat - a hat with a wide brim to protect from the sun
