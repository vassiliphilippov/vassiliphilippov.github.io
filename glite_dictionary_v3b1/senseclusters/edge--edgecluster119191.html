edge

verb
add a border or edging
4.6 3.4
Pronunciation: /edʒ/ (AmE), /edʒ/ (BrE)
to provide with a border or edge, or to sharpen the edge of something

Examples

To edge the fabric
She decided to edge the curtains with delicate lace.
The scarf was edged in blue thread for a decorative touch.
They edged the lawn with stone to create a tidy appearance.
Before cutting, the chef carefully edged the knife for maximum sharpness.
She edged the garden shears so they would cut cleanly through the thick branches.
The blacksmith must edge the sword before it is ready for battle.
