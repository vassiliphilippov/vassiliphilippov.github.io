cartoon

verb
draw cartoons
4.5 2.7
Pronunciation: /kɑːr.ˈtuːn/ (AmE), /kɑː.ˈtuːn/ (BrE)
to draw a humorous or satirical picture or series of pictures (a cartoon), often for publication in a newspaper or magazine

Examples

To cartoon a friend
He spent hours cartooning his classmates in the back of his notebook.
She loves to cartoon everyday situations with a witty twist.
The artist was asked to cartoon the leading politicians for the Sunday edition.

Related Terms
