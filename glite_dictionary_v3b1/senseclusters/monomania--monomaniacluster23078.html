monomania

noun
obsessive focus on one idea or thing
9.9 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / Àåm…ëÀên…ôÀàme…™ni…ô / (AmE), / Àåm…ín…ôÀàme…™ni…ô / (BrE)
An obsessive focus on or enthusiasm for a single idea, subject, or thing, to the exclusion of others.

Examples

Near monomania focus
He approached his latest project with a near monomania, unable to talk about anything else.
His monomania with chess made him neglect everything else in his life.
The artist's monomania drove her to paint the same subject repeatedly for years.
