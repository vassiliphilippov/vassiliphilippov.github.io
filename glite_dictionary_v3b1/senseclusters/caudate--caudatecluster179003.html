caudate

adjective
having a tail or tail-like appendage
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔːˌdeɪt /; / ˈkɔː.deɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɔː.deɪt / (BrE)
having a tail or a tail-like extension (especially in biology, such as leaves or other structures)
