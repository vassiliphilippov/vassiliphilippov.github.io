clearcut

noun
area with all trees removed
6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈklɪr.kʌt/ (AmE), /ˈklɪə.kʌt/ (BrE)
an area of forest where all the trees have been cut down at once

Examples

A forest clearcut
The satellite images showed a large clearcut where the forest once stood.
After the clearcut, the land was left bare and exposed to erosion.
Many animals lost their habitat due to the recent clearcut in the region.

Related Terms
