floorboard

noun
floor of a vehicle interior
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔrbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈflɔːbɔːd / (BrE)
the flat part of the floor in a vehicle, especially where passengers place their feet

Examples

Car floorboard
He found a coin wedged between the seat and the floorboard in the back of the van.
Mud and leaves collected on the floorboard after our camping trip.
She accidentally dropped her phone onto the floorboard while getting out of the car.

Related Terms

  • synonym: floor - the lower surface of a room or vehicle on which one may walk
