crested swift

noun
Asian tree-nesting swift with crest
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛstɪd swɪft / (AmE), / ˈkrɛstɪd swɪft / (BrE)
a bird of Southeast Asia and the East Indies, related to swifts, distinguished by its upright crest and tree-nesting habits

Examples

The crested swift bird

Unlike most swifts, the crested swift builds its nest high in tree branches.
