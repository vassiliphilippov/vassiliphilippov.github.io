uplifter

noun
person who inspires
6.0
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈlɪftər / (AmE), / ʌpˈlɪftə / (BrE)
a person who works to improve the social, economic, or moral condition of a community or group

Examples

Uplifter for the community
She was recognized as an uplifter for her efforts to improve education in the city.
The community needed an uplifter to inspire hope and positive change.
Many people saw him as an uplifter because of his dedication to helping others succeed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: benefactor - a person who gives help or support to others
  • synonym: mentor - an experienced person who guides and supports others
  • antonym: oppressor - a person who keeps others in hardship or limits their freedom
