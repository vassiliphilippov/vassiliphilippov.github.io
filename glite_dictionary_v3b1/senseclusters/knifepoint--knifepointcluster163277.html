knifepoint

noun
threat with a knife
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪfpɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈnaɪfpɔɪnt / (BrE)
the situation of being threatened with a knife, especially during a robbery or attack

Examples

At knifepoint
The victim was forced to hand over his wallet at knifepoint late last night.
She was held at knifepoint during the robbery but was not physically harmed.
The store owner bravely confronted the thief who threatened him at knifepoint.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gunpoint - the situation of being threatened with a gun
