die off

verb
become extinct; decrease in number until none remain
6.1
Pronunciation: / daɪ ɑf / (AmE), / daɪ ɒf / (BrE)
(of a group of living things) to become extinct or gradually decrease in number until none remain.

Examples

Animals die off
If the disease spreads unchecked, the animals could die off within a few years.
Many species in the rainforest are beginning to die off due to habitat loss.
The flowers will die off once the first frost arrives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: die out - to cease to exist; to become extinct
  • antonym: survive - to continue to live or exist
