Sergei Pavlovich Diaghilev

proper noun
Russian ballet impresario and founder of Ballets Russes
9.3
Pronunciation: /sər.ˈgeɪ ˈpæv.lə.vɪtʃ diː.ə.ˈgiː.lev/ (AmE), /sə.ˈgeɪ ˈpæv.lə.vɪtʃ diː.ə.ˈgiː.lev/ (BrE)
