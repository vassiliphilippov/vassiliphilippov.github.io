no joy

noun
no success; unsuccessful attempt
5.8
Pronunciation: / noʊ ˈdʒɔɪ / (AmE), / nəʊ ˈdʒɔɪ / (BrE)
used to indicate that an attempt has failed or produced no positive result

Examples

No joy calling
I tried calling him this morning and again in the afternoon—still no joy.
She rebooted her computer several times, but there was no joy in fixing the issue.
They searched the area for hours, but unfortunately found no joy.
