canyoneering

noun
canyon exploration sport
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌkænjəˈnɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌkænjəˈnɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport of exploring canyons by hiking, climbing, and sometimes swimming or rappelling

Examples

Canyoneering in canyons
They planned a weekend of canyoneering in the remote slot canyons of Utah.
Proper equipment is essential for safe canyoneering in challenging environments.
Many adventure seekers enjoy canyoneering because it combines hiking, climbing, and swimming.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hiking - the activity of walking long distances in the countryside
  • synonym: mountaineering - the sport of climbing mountains
