inpouring

adjective
pouring or flowing inward
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌpɔrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌpɔːrɪŋ / (BrE)
(of people, things, or substances) pouring or flowing inward; entering in large quantities or continuously.

Examples

Inpouring of tourists

The government struggled to manage the inpouring funds from international donors.
The sudden inpouring of rain flooded the narrow streets.
