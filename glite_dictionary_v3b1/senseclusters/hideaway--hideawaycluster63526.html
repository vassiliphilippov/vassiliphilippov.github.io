hideaway

noun
a secluded place for privacy or escape
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪdəˌweɪ /; / ˈhaɪ.dəˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdəˌweɪ /; / ˈhaɪ.dəˌweɪ / (BrE)
