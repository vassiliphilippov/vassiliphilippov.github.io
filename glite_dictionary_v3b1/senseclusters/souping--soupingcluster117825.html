souping

noun
enhancing or modifying
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsuːpɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of enhancing or modifying something, especially to improve its performance or appearance

Examples

To soup up a car
He spent the afternoon souping up his old car to make it faster.
The team is focused on souping their computers for better gaming performance.
After souping the engine, the motorcycle could reach much higher speeds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: modification - the act of changing something to improve it
  • synonym: upgrading - the process of improving or making something better
