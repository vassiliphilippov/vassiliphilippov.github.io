delicioso

interjection
expressing great taste
9.5
Pronunciation: / deɪliˈsoʊsoʊ / (AmE), / deɪliˈsəʊsəʊ / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express that something tastes very good or is delicious

Examples

Say 'delicioso!'
After tasting the homemade cake, she exclaimed, 'delicioso!' with a big smile.
He took a bite of the fresh bread and said, 'delicioso,' impressing his Spanish friends.
Whenever the chef serves his signature dish, someone always shouts 'delicioso!' in the restaurant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delicious - tasting very good
  • synonym: yummy - tasting very good (informal)
