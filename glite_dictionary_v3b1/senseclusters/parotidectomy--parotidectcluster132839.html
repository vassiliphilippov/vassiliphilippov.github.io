parotidectomy

noun
removal of parotid gland
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpæɹəˌtɪˈdɛktəmi / (AmE), / ˌpæɹəʊˌtɪˈdɛktəmi / (BrE)
a surgical operation to remove all or part of the parotid gland near the jaw

Examples

A parotidectomy operation
The surgeon performed a parotidectomy to remove the tumor from the patient's jaw area.
After her parotidectomy, she experienced some temporary numbness in her face.
A parotidectomy may be necessary if a patient has a benign or malignant growth in the parotid gland.
