beach wagon

noun
car with extended rear cargo space
8.5
Pronunciation: / bitʃ ˈwæɡən / (AmE), / biːtʃ ˈwæɡən / (BrE)
a car with a long body and a rear door, providing extra space behind the rear seats, typically known as a station wagon

Examples

An old beach wagon
He remembers riding in the old beach wagon as a child, watching the scenery roll by.
The family bought a used beach wagon for their summer road trips.
We packed all our gear into the beach wagon and drove to the shore.
