underwrite

verb
to guarantee insurance coverage
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈraɪt / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈraɪt / (BrE)
to accept financial responsibility for and guarantee compensation for loss or damage, typically by providing insurance

Examples

To underwrite insurance
Many insurance companies refuse to underwrite properties in areas prone to flooding.
Our firm will underwrite your risk, ensuring full compensation in case of an accident.
The bank agreed to underwrite the new health insurance policy for local businesses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guarantee - to provide a formal assurance or promise
  • synonym: insure - to cover with an insurance policy
