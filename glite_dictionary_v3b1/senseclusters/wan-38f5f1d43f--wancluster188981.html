WAN

noun
large-scale computer network
Pronunciation: / wæn / (AmE), / wæn / (BrE)
a computer network that extends over a large geographical area, often comprising several local area networks (LANs) linked together

Examples

A WAN network
Our company uses a WAN to connect branch offices across different cities.
The university invested in a new WAN to support faster data transfer between campuses.
Troubleshooting a WAN often requires coordination with multiple service providers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: LAN - a local area network, covering a small geographic area
