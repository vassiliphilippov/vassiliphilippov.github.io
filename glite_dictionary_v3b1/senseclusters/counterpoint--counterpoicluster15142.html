Examples

A counterpoint idea

Synonyms

  • counterposition
    8.2 0.01/m
    counterposition states a viewpoint that directly opposes another, while counterpoint highlights a contrasting or balancing idea between elements.
  • counterargument
    5.1 0.99/m
    counterargument is a set of reasons used to oppose a claim, while counterpoint can be any contrasting or balancing idea or element.
  • counterview
    6.6 0.02/m
    counterview names an opposing opinion or perspective, while counterpoint covers any contrasting or balancing idea beyond just views.
  • antithesis
    7.8 0.20/m
    antithesis places two opposite ideas side by side for strong effect, while counterpoint can simply contrast or balance parts.
  • counterweight
    5.7 0.03/m
    counterweight balances or offsets a physical or practical force, while counterpoint is a contrast or balance of ideas or elements.
