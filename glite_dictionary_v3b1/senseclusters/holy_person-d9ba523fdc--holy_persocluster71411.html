holy person

noun
a person of exceptional holiness
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊli ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ˈhəʊli ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who is exceptionally holy, often regarded as spiritually pure or virtuous

Examples

A holy person of faith
She was revered as a holy person because of her selfless service to others.
The villagers believe that the holy person can heal sickness with prayer.
Throughout history, many have sought the guidance of a holy person in times of crisis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: saint - a person acknowledged as holy or virtuous and typically regarded as being in heaven after death
