puddle

verb
form or make a small pool of liquid
3.6 2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌdəl /; / ˈpʌd.əl / (AmE), / ˈpʌdəl /; / ˈpʌd.əl / (BrE)
to form or cause to form a small pool of liquid, especially water, on a surface

Examples

Water puddles on floor
After the dog jumped in, the floor began to puddle with muddy water.

The children loved to puddle in the rain after the storm.

The heavy rain puddled the playground, making it unusable for the day.

The gardener accidentally puddled the flower beds by overwatering them.
