teiid lizard

noun
tropical New World lizard with distinct belly scales
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtiː.ɪd ˈlɪz.ərd / (AmE), / ˈtiː.ɪd ˈlɪz.əd / (BrE)
A tropical lizard native to the New World, characterized by a long tail and large rectangular scales on its belly.
