slit lamp

noun
instrument for detailed eye examination
Pronunciation: / slɪt læmp / (AmE), / slɪt læmp / (BrE)
a specialized lamp that projects a narrow, intense beam of light, used with a microscope to examine the eye's interior, especially the retina and optic nerve

Examples

A slit lamp microscope
A routine eye checkup may include an inspection with a slit lamp.
The slit lamp revealed signs of early glaucoma during the examination.
The ophthalmologist used a slit lamp to carefully examine the patient's retina.
