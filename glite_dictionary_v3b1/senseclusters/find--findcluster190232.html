find

verb
form an impression; judge
Pronunciation: / faɪnd / (AmE), / faɪnd / (BrE)
to form an opinion or impression about someone or something, especially as a result of experience or intuition

Examples

I find it easy
He found their attitude to be quite rude during the meeting.
I find her arguments convincing and well thought out.
Some people find horror movies entertaining, while others dislike them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feel - to have an emotional impression or reaction
  • synonym: judge - to form an opinion or conclusion about
