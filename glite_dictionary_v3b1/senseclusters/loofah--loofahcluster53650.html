loofah

noun
dried plant sponge used for washing
Pronunciation: / ˈluːfə / (AmE), / ˈluːfə / (BrE)
the dried fibrous part of the fruit of a tropical plant, used as a sponge for washing or cleaning

Examples

A loofah sponge
Natural loofahs are popular alternatives to synthetic sponges.
She scrubbed her back with a loofah to exfoliate her skin.
The loofah hung drying beside the soap in the shower.

Related Terms
