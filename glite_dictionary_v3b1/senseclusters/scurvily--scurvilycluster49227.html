scurvily

adverb
in a despicable or mean way
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈskɝːvɪli / (AmE), / ˈskɜːvɪli / (BrE)
In a despicable, mean, or contemptible manner.

Examples

To act scurvily
He treated his former friends scurvily once he gained power.
She considered it scurvily done to leave the workers unpaid.
The merchant scurvily cheated his customers out of their rightful goods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meanly - in a spiteful or unkind manner
  • antonym: nobly - in a noble or honorable manner
