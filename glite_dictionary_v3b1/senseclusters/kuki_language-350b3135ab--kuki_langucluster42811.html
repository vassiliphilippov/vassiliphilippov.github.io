Kuki language

noun
language group of India, Myanmar, and Bangladesh
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːki ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkuːki ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A group of Kamarupan languages spoken in western Myanmar (Burma), Bangladesh, and easternmost India.

Examples
