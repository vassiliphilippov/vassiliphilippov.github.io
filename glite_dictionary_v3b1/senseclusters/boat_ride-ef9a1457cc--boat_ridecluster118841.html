boat ride

noun
journey on a boat
Pronunciation: / boʊt raɪd / (AmE), / bəʊt raɪd / (BrE)
a trip or short journey taken on a boat, especially for pleasure or sightseeing

Examples

A boat ride on canals
She suggested a boat ride as the perfect way to see the city's canals.
The children were excited for their first boat ride at the amusement park.
We took a relaxing boat ride along the river at sunset.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cruise - a voyage for pleasure, especially on a ship
