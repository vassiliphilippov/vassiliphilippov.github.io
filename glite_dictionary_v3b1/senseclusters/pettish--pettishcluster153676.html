pettish

adjective
easily irritated or annoyed
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛtɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɛtɪʃ / (BrE)
easily irritated or annoyed, especially about trivial matters; inclined to be peevish or sulky.

Examples

A pettish mood
He became pettish whenever his plans were changed without warning.
His pettish attitude made teamwork difficult during stressful moments.
She gave a pettish response, clearly irritated at the interruption.
