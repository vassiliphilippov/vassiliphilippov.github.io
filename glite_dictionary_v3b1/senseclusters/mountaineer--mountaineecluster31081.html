mountaineer

noun
someone who climbs mountains
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˌmaʊn.təˈnɪr / (AmE), / ˌmaʊn.tɪˈnɪə / (BrE)

Examples

The experienced mountaineer reached the summit just as the sun was rising.
