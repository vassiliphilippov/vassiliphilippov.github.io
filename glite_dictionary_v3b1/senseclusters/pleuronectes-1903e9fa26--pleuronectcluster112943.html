Pleuronectes

noun
genus of flatfish
Pronunciation: / plʊˈrɒn.ɛk.tiːz / (AmE), / plʊəˈrɒn.ɛk.tiːz / (BrE)
A genus of flatfish in the family Pleuronectidae, including species such as the European plaice.
