box one's ears

verb
to hit someone on the ears
Pronunciation: / bɑks wʌnz ɪrz / (AmE), / bɒks wʌnz ɪəz / (BrE)
to hit someone on the side of the head or ears, usually as a punishment or reprimand

Examples

To box one's ears hard
If you don't behave, I'll box your ears like your grandmother used to.
She jokingly warned she would box his ears if he forgot again.
The teacher threatened to box his ears for talking back in class.
