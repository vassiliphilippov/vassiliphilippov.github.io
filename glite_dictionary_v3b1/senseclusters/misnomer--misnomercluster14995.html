misnomer

noun
incorrect or inappropriate name
Pronunciation: / mɪsˈnoʊmər / (AmE), / mɪsˈnəʊmə / (BrE)
a name or term that is incorrect or inappropriate for what it refers to

Examples

A misnomer name
Calling the koala a bear is a misnomer, since it is actually a marsupial.
Describing the chilly room as 'tropical' was a clear misnomer.
The restaurant’s name, 'Vegetarian Delight,' is a misnomer because they serve meat dishes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: misname - to give a wrong or inappropriate name to something
  • antonym: proper name - a correct or appropriate name for someone or something
