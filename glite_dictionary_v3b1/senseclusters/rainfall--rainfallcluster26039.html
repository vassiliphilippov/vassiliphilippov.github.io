rainfall

noun
amount of rain that falls
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪnˌfɔl / (AmE), / ˈreɪnfɔːl / (BrE)
the amount of rain that falls in a particular area during a particular period of time

Examples

Average rainfall amount
Average annual rainfall in this region is about 800 millimeters.
Due to a lack of rainfall, the crops failed to grow properly.
The heavy rainfall last night caused flooding in several neighborhoods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: precipitation - all forms of water, such as rain, snow, sleet, or hail, that fall from clouds
