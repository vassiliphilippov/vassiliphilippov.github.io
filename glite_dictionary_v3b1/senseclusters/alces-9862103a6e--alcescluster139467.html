Alces

noun
genus of moose and elk
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈælˌsiːz / (AmE), / ˈæl.siːz / (BrE)
A genus of large mammals comprising the moose and the Eurasian elk.

Examples

The moose, classified as Alces alces, is native to both North America and Eurasia.
