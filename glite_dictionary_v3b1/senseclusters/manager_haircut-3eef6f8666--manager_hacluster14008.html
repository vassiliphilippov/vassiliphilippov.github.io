manager haircut

noun
stereotypical demanding customer's haircut
Pronunciation: / ˈmænɪdʒɚ ˈhɛrˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈmænɪdʒə ˈheəkʌt / (BrE)
(informal) A stereotypical short, often angular women's haircut, popularly associated with demanding or authoritative customers, especially those who insist on speaking to the manager.
