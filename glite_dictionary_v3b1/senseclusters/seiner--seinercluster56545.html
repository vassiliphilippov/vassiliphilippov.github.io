seiner

noun
fishing with seine net
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪnər /; / ˈseɪnər / (AmE), / ˈsaɪnə /; / ˈseɪnə / (BrE)
a person or boat that catches fish using a seine net

Examples

A seiner boat
The experienced seiner skillfully guided his boat along the coast at dawn.
After hours at sea, the seiner hauled in a net full of sardines.
Many coastal communities depend on the work of a seiner for their livelihood.
The seiner returned to port with a large catch of fish.
A modern seiner can haul in thousands of pounds of fish in a single trip.
The crew of the seiner worked together to pull in the heavy net.
