focal point

noun
center of attention or interest
5.3 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈfoʊ.kəl pɔɪnt/ (AmE), /ˈfəʊ.kəl pɔɪnt/ (BrE)
the main thing, person, or issue that receives the most attention or is most important in a situation or discussion

Examples

The focal point of discussion
Economic development is the focal point of the government's new policy.
Her artwork served as the focal point of the entire exhibition.
The new library quickly became the focal point of the community.
Climate change became the focal point of the conference discussions this year.
Her resignation was the focal point of debate during the board meeting.
The painting acts as the focal point of the room, drawing everyone's attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: centerpiece - an important item or issue that is the focus of attention
  • synonym: focus - the center of interest or activity
  • antonym: sideline - an issue or activity unimportant compared to the main one
