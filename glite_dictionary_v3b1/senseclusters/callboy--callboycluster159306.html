callboy

noun
male escort or prostitute
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔlˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈkɔːlˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
a man who is hired to provide sexual services or companionship, especially as a male escort or prostitute

Examples

Hire a callboy
The police arrested a callboy during the undercover operation in the city center.
He worked as a callboy to support himself while studying at university.
The magazine published an interview with a former callboy who shared his experiences.

Related Terms

  • antonym: call girl - a woman who is hired to provide sexual services
  • synonym: gigolo - a man paid to be a companion or lover, especially for women
  • synonym: prostitute - a person who has sex for payment
