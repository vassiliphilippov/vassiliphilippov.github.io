surplusage

noun
excess amount; surplus
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝː.plə.sɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.pləs.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
an amount or quantity that is more than what is needed; an excess

Examples

The surplusage amount
During the meeting, he pointed out the surplusage in the company's inventory.
Efforts were made to eliminate surplusage in the budget to save costs.
The contract contained unnecessary surplusage that made it confusing to read.

Related Terms

  • synonym: surplus - an amount that is more than needed
