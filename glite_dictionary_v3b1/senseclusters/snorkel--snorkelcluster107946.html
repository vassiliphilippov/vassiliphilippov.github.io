snorkel

noun
tube for breathing while swimming facedown
7.9 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsnɔːr.kəl/ (AmE), /ˈsnɔː.kəl/ (BrE)
a tube that allows a swimmer to breathe while keeping their face in the water, with one end in the swimmer's mouth and the other above the water's surface

Examples

A snorkel mask
He bought a new snorkel and fins for his vacation in Hawaii.
The instructor handed each participant a snorkel and explained how to use it.
While using a snorkel, she could observe the colorful fish below without lifting her head.
