ruffly

adjective, adverb
with ruffles
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌfli / (AmE), / ˈrʌfli / (BrE)
decorated with or in the style of ruffles or frills; having many gathered or pleated fabric details

Examples

The curtains hung ruffly around the window, adding a soft touch to the room.
She tied a ruffly ribbon around the gift box for decoration.
The blouse was designed ruffly at the sleeves, making it look elegant and playful.
