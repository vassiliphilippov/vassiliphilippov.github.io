arduous

adjective
difficult and requiring much effort
6.0 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈɑːr.dʒuː.əs/ /; / /ˈɑːr.djuː.əs/ (AmE), /ˈɑː.djuː.əs/ /; / /ˈɑː.dʒuː.əs/ (BrE)
