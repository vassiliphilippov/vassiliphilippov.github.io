accommodatingly

adverb
in a helpful or obliging manner
Pronunciation: / əˌkɑməˈdeɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / əˌkɒməˈdeɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is helpful and eager to please or adapt to others' needs or wishes.

Examples

Answered accommodatingly
He accommodatingly answered all of our questions without hesitation.
She accommodatingly changed her plans to suit the group's needs.
The concierge accommodatingly arranged a taxi for the late-night guests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obligingly - in a helpful and willing manner
