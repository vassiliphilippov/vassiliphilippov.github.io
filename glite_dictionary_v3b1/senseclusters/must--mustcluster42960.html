must

noun
something essential or necessary
1.0
Pronunciation: / mʌst / (AmE), / mʌst / (BrE)
something that is essential or absolutely necessary

Examples

A must for travelers
A passport is a must if you plan to travel abroad.
For a successful interview, preparation is a must.
Good walking shoes are a must for this hike.

Related Terms

  • synonym: necessity - something that is necessary or indispensable
  • antonym: option - something that can be chosen but is not required
