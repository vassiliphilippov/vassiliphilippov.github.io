Sears Tower

proper noun
formerly tallest skyscraper in Chicago
Pronunciation: / sɪrz ˈtaʊər / (AmE), / sɪəz ˈtaʊə / (BrE)
a skyscraper in Chicago, built in 1974, formerly the tallest building in the world; now officially known as the Willis Tower

Examples

The Sears Tower building
Many locals still call it the Sears Tower despite its official name change.
The Sears Tower was once the tallest building in the world and remains a Chicago landmark.
Tourists flock to the Sears Tower to visit its famous observation deck.
