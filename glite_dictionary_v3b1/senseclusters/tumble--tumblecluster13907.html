tumble

verb
mix or throw together in disorder
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌmbəl / (AmE), / ˈtʌmbəl / (BrE)
to throw or mix things together in a confused or disorderly mass

Examples

To tumble toys together
She tumbled all the items into the box without sorting them.
The books were tumbled together in a heap after the move.
The coach decided to tumble the player groups to make things more interesting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jumble - to mix things together in a confused or untidy way
  • antonym: sort - to arrange things in a particular order
