jolty

adjective
marked by sudden jolts or movements
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒoʊlti / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒlti / (BrE)
characterized by or causing sudden, irregular jolts or movements

Examples

A jolty ride
After the earthquake, the elevator ride felt unusually jolty and unsettling.
Her voice grew jolty with excitement as she described the roller coaster ride.
The jolty carriage made reading impossible on the journey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jerky - characterized by abrupt stops and starts
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface or motion
