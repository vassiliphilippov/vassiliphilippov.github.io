Sandinista

adjective
of the Sandinistas
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌsændəˈnistə / (AmE), / ˌsændɪˈniːstə / (BrE)
relating to the Sandinistas, their ideology, policies, or government in Nicaragua.

Examples

Sandinista government policies
The Sandinista government introduced major land reforms in the 1980s.
Many artists were inspired by Sandinista ideals during that period.
International aid increased during the Sandinista administration in Nicaragua.

Related Terms

  • antonym: counterrevolutionary - opposing a revolution or revolutionary government
  • synonym: revolutionary - relating to or supporting political revolution
