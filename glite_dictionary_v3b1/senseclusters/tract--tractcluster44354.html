tract

noun
large area of land
8.7
Pronunciation: / trækt / (AmE), / trækt / (BrE)
a large area of land, especially one that is not divided into smaller parcels

Examples

A tract of land
The developer purchased a large tract of land outside the city limits.
They hiked across a remote tract of wilderness.
Vast tracts of forest were cleared for agriculture.

  • synonym: parcel - a piece or section of land
