driveler

noun
person who drools
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɪvələr / (AmE), / ˈdrɪvələ / (BrE)
a person who drools, especially letting saliva fall from the mouth unintentionally

Examples

Baby driveler
The baby became a little driveler when he started teething.
After his surgery, he was embarrassed to be called a driveler by the nurse.
The old dog was a constant driveler when he slept on the couch.

Related Terms
