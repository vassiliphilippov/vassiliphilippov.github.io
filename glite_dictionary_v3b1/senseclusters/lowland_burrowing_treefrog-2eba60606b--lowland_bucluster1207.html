lowland burrowing treefrog

noun
burrowing frog of the US southwest
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊˌlænd ˈbɜroʊɪŋ ˈtriˌfrɔg / (AmE), / ˈləʊˌlænd ˈbɜːrɪŋ ˈtriːˌfrɒg / (BrE)
A terrestrial, burrowing, nocturnal frog found in grassy areas and scrub forests of the southwestern United States, known for its very hard upper head surface.
