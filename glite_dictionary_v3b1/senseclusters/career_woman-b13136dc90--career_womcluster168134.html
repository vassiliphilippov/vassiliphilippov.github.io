career woman

noun
woman focused on her career
2.6
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɪr ˌwʊmən / (AmE), / kəˈrɪə ˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman whose main priority in life is her profession or career, often placing work above other interests or traditional roles.

Examples

A career woman works
As a career woman, she often worked late nights to achieve her goals.
Being a career woman sometimes meant making sacrifices in her personal life.
The conference featured several prominent career women sharing their experiences in the workplace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: careerist - a person whose main concern is progress in their career
