pigging

verb
to eat greedily
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈpɪ.ɡɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈpɪ.ɡɪŋ/ (BrE)
to eat a lot or very greedily, especially in a way that resembles a pig.

Examples

Stop pigging all the cookies and leave some for your sister.
