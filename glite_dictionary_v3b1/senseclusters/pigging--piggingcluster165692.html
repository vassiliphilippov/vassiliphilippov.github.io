pigging

verb
to eat greedily
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡɪŋ / (BrE)
to eat a lot or very greedily, especially in a way that resembles a pig.

Examples

Pigging out on food
They were pigging on snacks during the movie and barely left any for later.
After the long hike, we ended up pigging out at the nearest burger place.
Stop pigging all the cookies and leave some for your sister.

Related Terms

  • synonym: devour - to eat something quickly and eagerly
  • synonym: gorge - to eat a large amount greedily
  • antonym: nibble - to eat in small bites
