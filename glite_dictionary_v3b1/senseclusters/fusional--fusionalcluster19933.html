fusional

adjective
relating to fusion
7.6
Pronunciation: /ˈfjuː.ʒə.nəl/ (AmE), /ˈfjuː.ʒə.nəl/ (BrE)
relating to or produced by fusion; formed by joining, melting, or combining two or more parts into one

Examples

In linguistics, a fusional language combines several grammatical features into a single word ending.
The chef created a fusional dish by blending flavors from different cuisines into one meal.
