dacha

noun
Russian country house
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑː.tʃə / (AmE), / ˈdætʃ.ə / (BrE)
A Russian country house, typically used as a seasonal second home, often located outside a city.

Examples

A dacha house
During the summer, her family would spend weekends at their dacha near the lake.
He invited us to his dacha on the outskirts of Moscow for a traditional meal.
The writer found inspiration for his novel while staying at a quiet dacha in the woods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cottage - a small house, usually in the countryside
