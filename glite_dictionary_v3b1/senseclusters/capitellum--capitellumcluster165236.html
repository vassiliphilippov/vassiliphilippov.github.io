capitellum

noun
small bone knob
Pronunciation: / ˌkæpɪˈtɛləm / (AmE), / ˌkæpɪˈtɛləm / (BrE)
a small rounded part or knob, especially at the end of a bone such as the humerus

Examples

Elbow capitellum
The X-ray showed a fracture near the capitellum of the elbow.
In anatomy class, we learned that the capitellum helps the arm bend smoothly.
Damage to the capitellum can affect the movement of the forearm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: condyle - a rounded prominence at the end of a bone
