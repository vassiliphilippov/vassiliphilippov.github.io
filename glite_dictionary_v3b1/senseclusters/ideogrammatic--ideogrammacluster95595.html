ideogrammatic

adjective
using idea symbols

Pronunciation: /aɪ.dɪ.oʊ.græm.ˈmæt.ɪk/ /; / /aɪ.də.græm.ˈmæt.ɪk/ (AmE), /aɪ.dɪ.əʊ.græm.ˈmæt.ɪk/ /; / /aɪ.də.græm.ˈmæt.ɪk/ (BrE)
relating to symbols or writing systems where characters represent ideas or concepts rather than sounds

Examples

Ideogrammatic writing system
Chinese characters are often described as ideogrammatic because they represent ideas rather than sounds.
