amelogenesis

noun
formation of tooth enamel
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˌmiːloʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (AmE), / əˌmiːləʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (BrE)
the process by which tooth enamel is formed during the development of teeth

Examples

Amelogenesis process
Amelogenesis occurs during the earliest stages of tooth development in children.
Genetic mutations affecting amelogenesis can result in enamel defects.
Researchers are studying the mechanisms of amelogenesis to prevent dental diseases.
