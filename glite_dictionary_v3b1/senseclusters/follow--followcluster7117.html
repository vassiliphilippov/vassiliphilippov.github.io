Synonyms

  • comply
    3.8 30/m
    comply means act in line with a rule or request, while follow covers obeying rules, advice, or beliefs in many situations.
  • adhere
    4.7 9/m
    adhere means stick closely to a belief or rule over time, while follow can mean accept or act on rules or advice.
  • obey
    1.0 18/m
    obey means do what an authority tells you to do, while follow can also mean accept rules, advice, or beliefs more broadly.
  • conform to
    3.7 7/m
    conform to means change behavior to meet rules or standards, while follow means accept or act according to rules or advice.
  • observe
    3.4 8/m
    observe means respect or keep a law, rule, or custom, often formally, while follow is a more general, everyday word.
  • go by
    1.8 3/m
    go by means act according to a rule or standard in a practical way, while follow covers accepting or obeying rules or advice.
  • stick to
    3.0 16/m
    stick to means keep doing or obeying a rule or plan without changing it, while follow can mean accept or act on advice.
