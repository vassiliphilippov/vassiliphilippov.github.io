unwritten

adjective
not formally written; customary
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɪtn̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɪtən / (BrE)
(of a rule, law, or custom) not formally recorded in writing but accepted and followed in practice.

Examples

Unwritten rule
Many office customs remain unwritten but are strongly observed by all employees.
Their agreement was unwritten, relying solely on trust and mutual respect.
There is an unwritten rule about taking off your shoes before entering the house.

Related Terms

  • antonym: written - recorded in writing or print
