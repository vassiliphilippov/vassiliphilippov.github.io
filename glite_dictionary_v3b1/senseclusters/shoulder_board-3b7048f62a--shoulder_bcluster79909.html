shoulder board

noun
epaulet showing rank on uniform
6.8 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / Àà Éo äld…ö b…îrd / (AmE), / Àà É…ô äld…ô b…îÀêd / (BrE)
a flat piece of stiff material worn on the shoulder of a uniform, often to indicate rank or position, especially in the military or police

Examples

Shoulder board on uniform
Cadets were instructed to sew the new shoulder boards onto their uniforms before inspection.
He recognized the general by the stars on his shoulder board.
The captain's shoulder board displayed three gold stripes.

Synonyms

  • shoulder mark
    6.7 0.00/m
    shoulder mark is a narrow cloth tag sewn on uniform shoulders to show rank, while shoulder board is a stiff, flat badge used to display rank.
  • epaulet
    9.3 0.26/m
    epaulet is a soft decorative shoulder pad often with fringe or braid worn for show, while shoulder board is a stiff flat piece for rank.
