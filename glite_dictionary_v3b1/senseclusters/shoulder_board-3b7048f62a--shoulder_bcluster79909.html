shoulder board

noun
epaulet showing rank on uniform
4.1 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈʃoʊl.dər bɔːrd/ (AmE), /ˈʃəʊl.də bɔːd/ (BrE)
a flat piece of stiff material worn on the shoulder of a uniform, often to indicate rank or position, especially in the military or police

Examples

Shoulder board on uniform
Cadets were instructed to sew the new shoulder boards onto their uniforms before inspection.
He recognized the general by the stars on his shoulder board.
The captain's shoulder board displayed three gold stripes.
