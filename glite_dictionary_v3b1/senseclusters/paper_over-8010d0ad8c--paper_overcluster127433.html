paper over

verb
conceal or hide faults or problems
7.5 6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪpɚ ˌoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə ˌəʊvə / (BrE)
to try to hide or disguise faults, problems, or differences instead of dealing with them openly

Examples

To paper over cracks
He hoped no one would notice if he papered over the cracks in his argument.
Management tried to paper over the drop in sales by focusing on minor successes.
They cannot continue papering over their disagreements indefinitely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cover up - to hide the truth about something, especially a mistake or wrongdoing
