stayover

noun
overnight guest
3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈsteɪ.oʊ.vər/ (AmE), /ˈsteɪ.əʊ.və/ (BrE)
an overnight stay at someone else's home or another place, or a person who stays overnight

Examples
