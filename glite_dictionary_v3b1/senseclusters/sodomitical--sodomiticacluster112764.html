sodomitical

adjective
of Sodom or its sins
Pronunciation: / ˌsɑːdəˈmɪtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌsɒdəˈmɪtɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the biblical city of Sodom, its people, or the sins traditionally associated with it, especially sexual immorality.

Examples

Sodomitical city
The medieval text condemned the city for its sodomitical practices and moral decline.
He was accused of holding sodomitical beliefs that went against the teachings of the church.
Many old laws were created to punish what was considered sodomitical behavior.
