factory-made

adjective
made in a factory, not by hand
1.4 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈfæk.tər.i.meɪd/ /; / /ˈfæk.tri.meɪd/ (AmE), /ˈfæk.tri.meɪd/ /; / /ˈfæk.tə.ri.meɪd/ (BrE)
Produced in quantity in a factory rather than being handmade or crafted individually.

Examples

Factory-made products
Many factory-made products are cheaper than their handcrafted counterparts.
She preferred handmade jewelry to factory-made accessories.
The shelves were filled with factory-made furniture that all looked the same.
