bloco

noun
carnival music group
Pronunciation: / ˈbloʊkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈblɒkəʊ / (BrE)
a group of musicians and dancers who perform together in the street during Carnival, especially in Brazil

Examples

Carnival bloco parade
During Carnival, the largest bloco attracts thousands of people to the streets.
She joined a local bloco to experience the excitement of Brazilian music and dance.
Each bloco has its own unique costumes and musical style for the parade.
Every year, thousands join the bloco to celebrate Carnival in the streets of Rio.
The bloco played lively music as crowds danced and sang along.
Tourists were excited to follow the colorful bloco through the city center.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carnival - a festive event with music, dancing, and costumes
  • synonym: parade - a public procession, often with music and dancing
  • synonym: samba band - a group that plays samba music, often in parades
