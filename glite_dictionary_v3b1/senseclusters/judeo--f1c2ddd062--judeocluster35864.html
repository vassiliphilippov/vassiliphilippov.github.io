Jewish-related prefix
8.1
Pronunciation: / dʒuˈdeɪ.oʊ / (AmE), / dʒuˈdeɪ.əʊ / (BrE)
a prefix meaning Jewish or relating to Jews, used in compound words

Examples

Judeo-Christian traditions
The term Judeo-Christian is often used to describe shared religious traditions.
Scholars study Judeo-Arabic texts to understand medieval Jewish communities.
The phrase Judeo-Persian refers to the language spoken by Jews in Persia.
