grinder

noun
large sandwich with meats and cheese (U.S.)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraɪndər / (AmE), / ˈɡraɪndə / (BrE)
a large sandwich made from a long crusty roll filled with meats, cheese, and various toppings; known by different names in different regions of the United States

Examples

A grinder sandwich
He prefers his grinder loaded with extra onions and peppers.
I ordered a chicken parm grinder from the local deli for lunch.
In Connecticut, a grinder usually comes toasted and packed with Italian meats.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hero - yet another regional word for this kind of sandwich, especially in New York
  • synonym: hoagie - another regional term for a large sandwich on a long roll
  • synonym: submarine - a similar type of large sandwich, especially in the northeastern U.S.
