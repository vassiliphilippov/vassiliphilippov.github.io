angered

adjective
filled with strong anger
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡərd / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡəd / (BrE)
Feeling or showing strong anger; very angry or enraged.

Examples

An angered person
He tried to remain calm, but the unjust accusation angered him deeply.
She was clearly angered by his rude remarks during the meeting.
The new policy angered many employees who felt it was unfair.
