East Chadic

noun
a group of Chadic languages in Chad
9.9
Pronunciation: / ist ˈtʃædɪk / (AmE), / iːst ˈtʃadɪk / (BrE)
a group of Chadic languages spoken mainly in Chad and neighboring regions, forming part of the larger Afroasiatic language family
