preserve

noun
protected area for wildlife
Pronunciation: / prɪˈzɝv /; / prɪˈzɜrv / (AmE), / prɪˈzɜːv / (BrE)
an area of land where animals or plants are protected, or an activity or field reserved for a particular group

Examples

A wildlife preserve
Hunting is strictly forbidden in the animal preserve established by the government.
Rangers patrol the preserve to ensure the animals are kept safe from poachers.
The national preserve attracts tourists who want to see rare wildlife in their natural habitat.
Chess was once seen as the exclusive preserve of intellectuals.
For decades, engineering was considered a preserve of men.
The legal profession is no longer just the preserve of the upper classes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: domain - a particular sphere of activity or knowledge
  • synonym: reserve - a protected area of land for animals or plants
  • synonym: sanctuary - a safe place, especially one for animals or birds
  • synonym: sphere - a particular area of activity, interest, or expertise
