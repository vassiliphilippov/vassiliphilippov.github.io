shatterproof glass

noun
glass designed not to shatter easily
Pronunciation: / ˈʃætɚˌpruf ɡlæs / (AmE), / ˈʃætəˌpruːf ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
Glass that is made with layers of plastic, resin, or similar materials between sheets of glass to prevent it from breaking into sharp pieces when shattered.

Examples

Shatterproof glass panels
After the incident, the school replaced all the doors with shatterproof glass panels.
Many smartphones now come with shatterproof glass screens to prevent accidental damage.
The car windows are made of shatterproof glass to ensure passenger safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: safety glass - glass made to minimize injury when broken
