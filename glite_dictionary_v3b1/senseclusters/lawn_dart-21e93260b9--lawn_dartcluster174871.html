lawn dart

noun
weighted outdoor throwing dart for a lawn game
Pronunciation: / lɔn dɑrt / (AmE), / lɔːn dɑːt / (BrE)
A heavy, pointed missile, typically with plastic fins, used in an outdoor game where players throw it toward a target on the ground.

Examples

A lawn dart game
A lawn dart landed just inches away from the winning mark.
Before regulations changed, families often played with lawn darts at summer barbecues.
The children took turns tossing the lawn dart toward the target circle.
