Great Rift Valley

proper noun
large geological depression in Asia and Africa
5.3
Pronunciation: /greɪt rɪft ˈvæl.iː/ /; / /greɪt rɪft ˈvæl.ə/ (AmE), /greɪt rɪft ˈvæl.iː/ /; / /greɪt rɪft ˈvæl.ə/ (BrE)
