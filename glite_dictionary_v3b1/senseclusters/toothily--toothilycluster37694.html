toothily

adverb
with tooth-like edges
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈtuː.θɪ.li/ /; / /ˈtuː.θə.li/ (AmE), /ˈtuː.θɪ.li/ /; / /ˈtuː.θə.li/ (BrE)
in a way that has or uses tooth-like projections, or with a serrated or jagged action or edge

Examples

Saw moves toothily

She smiled toothily, showing all her teeth in a wide grin.
The knife cut toothily into the bread, creating uneven slices.

  • antonym: smoothly - in a way that is even and without roughness or jaggedness
