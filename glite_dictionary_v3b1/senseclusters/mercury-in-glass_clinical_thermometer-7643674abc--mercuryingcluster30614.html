mercury-in-glass clinical thermometer

noun
clinical thermometer using mercury in glass
4.8 4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈmɜːr.kjə.riː.ɪn.glæs ˈklɪn.ɪ.kəl θər.ˈmɑː.mɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈmɜː.kjʊ.riː.ɪn.glɑːs ˈklɪn.ɪ.kəl θə.ˈmɒm.ɪ.tə/ (BrE)
