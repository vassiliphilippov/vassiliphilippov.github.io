rouleau

noun
a roll of ribbon or fabric
9.1
Pronunciation: / ruːˈloʊ / (AmE), / ruːˈləʊ / (BrE)
a roll or coil of ribbon, fabric, or similar material, especially used in sewing or decoration

Examples

A rouleau of ribbon
Each invitation was tied with a delicate rouleau of silk ribbon.
She stored her collection of vintage rouleaux in a decorative box.
The seamstress reached for a rouleau of satin to finish the dress.
