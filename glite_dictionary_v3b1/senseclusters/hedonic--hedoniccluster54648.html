hedonic

adjective
relating to pleasure
Pronunciation: / hɪˈdɑnɪk / (AmE), / hɪˈdɒnɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or concerned with pleasure or the pursuit of pleasure.

Examples

A hedonic lifestyle
She criticized the hedonic lifestyle promoted by certain celebrities.
Some psychologists study hedonic responses to food and music.
The resort offered a hedonic experience for guests seeking ultimate relaxation and enjoyment.
