indistinguishability

noun
impossibility of being distinguished
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˌstɪŋɡwɪʃəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪsˌtɪŋɡwɪʃəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being impossible to differentiate or tell apart; exact sameness

Examples

Quantum particles exhibit indistinguishability, which is crucial in understanding their behavior.
