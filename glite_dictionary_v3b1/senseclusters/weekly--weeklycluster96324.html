weekly

adverb
once a week; every week (adverb)
1.0 2.62/m
Pronunciation: / Ààwik.li / (AmE), / ÀàwiÀêk.li / (BrE)
happening once a week; occurring or done every week

Examples

She visits weekly

Synonyms

    1.0 7.08/m
