screech

verb
make a loud, harsh cry
4.0 5/m
Pronunciation: / skrit É / (AmE), / skriÀêt É / (BrE)
to make a loud, high-pitched, harsh or piercing sound or cry

Examples

To screech loudly
She screeched when she saw the spider on her pillow.
The car screeched to a halt as it approached the intersection.
The owl began to screech loudly in the middle of the night.
Children screeching with excitement could be heard from the playground.
Owls sometimes screech at night, startling campers in the woods.
The brakes screeched as the driver slammed them suddenly.

  • squeal
