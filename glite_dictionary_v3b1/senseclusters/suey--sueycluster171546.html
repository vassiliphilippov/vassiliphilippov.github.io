suey

noun
pig-calling cry
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːi / (AmE), / ˈsuːi / (BrE)
an exclamation or call used to attract pigs or hogs, especially on farms

Examples

A loud suey for hogs
The farmer called out suey to gather the pigs for feeding time.
With a loud suey, she signaled the hogs to come closer to the pen.
Children laughed as they tried shouting suey to see if the pigs would respond.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sooey - a shout used to call pigs
