drawing ink

noun
black ink for drawing or writing
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɔɪɪŋ ɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈdrɔːɪŋ ɪŋk / (BrE)
a black liquid ink specially prepared for use in drawing, printing, or writing
