grandiflora

adjective
large-flowered (botany)
Pronunciation: / ˌɡrændɪˈflɔrə / (AmE), / ˌɡrændɪˈflɔːrə / (BrE)
having large flowers; used especially in scientific names of plants to indicate large-flowered varieties.

Examples

Grandiflora rose variety
The rose variety labeled grandiflora produces exceptionally large and showy blooms in the garden.
Botanists often use the term grandiflora when describing species with noticeably bigger flowers.
The catalog lists several grandiflora hybrids prized for their impressive floral displays.
