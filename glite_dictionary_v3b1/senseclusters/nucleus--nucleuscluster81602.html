nucleus

noun
core or essential group
6.2 5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈnuː.kli.əs / (AmE), / ˈnjuː.kli.əs / (BrE)
A small central group of indispensable people or things forming the core of a larger organization or activity.

Examples

Nucleus of a team
At its start, the club had only five members, but they were the nucleus of all future activities.
The research team formed the nucleus of the new department.
These early volunteers became the nucleus for the organization’s nationwide efforts.
