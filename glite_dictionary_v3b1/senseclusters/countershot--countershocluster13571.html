countershot

noun
a return or retaliatory shot
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntərˌʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntəʃɒt / (BrE)
A return shot made in response to an opponent's shot, or a retaliatory shot.

Examples

A countershot return
After receiving a powerful serve, she executed a flawless countershot.
In the film, the director used a countershot to show the other character's reaction.
The player's quick countershot caught his opponent off guard.
