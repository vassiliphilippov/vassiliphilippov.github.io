Synonyms

  • link
    1.1 40/m
    link means to join or connect two or more things directly, while link up is the phrasal verb for making that connection.
  • connect
    1.1 20/m
    connect means to join parts so they work as one, while link up is often used for people or systems joining together.
  • join
    1.0 59/m
    join means to come or be brought together into one group, while link up usually suggests making a connection between things.
  • interconnect
    4.7 0.54/m
    interconnect means to connect many parts so they work together, while link up is more about forming a single direct link.
  • hook up
    3.7 5.45/m
    hook up means to connect devices or systems using cables or setup, while link up is a more general joining action.
  • couple
    3.1 13/m
    couple means to join two things or people together, often in a technical way, while link up is more general.
