link up

verb
join or become connected
3.2
Pronunciation: / lɪŋk ʌp / (AmE), / lɪŋk ʌp / (BrE)
to join or connect with someone or something, or to connect things together to form a single unit

Examples

Link up with friends
After several years apart, the cousins decided to link up for a family reunion.
Researchers around the world link up to share their findings at this conference.
The river and the canal link up just before entering the city.
Engineers managed to link up several computers to create a network.
She linked up the wires to complete the circuit.
The two trains will link up at the next station.

Related Terms

  • synonym: connect - to join or be joined with something else
  • antonym: detach - to disconnect or separate something
  • synonym: join - to bring or put together
  • antonym: separate - to move apart or become disconnected
