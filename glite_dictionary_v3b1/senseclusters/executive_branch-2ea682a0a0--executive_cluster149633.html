executive branch

noun
branch of government enforcing laws
2.9 3.2
Pronunciation: /ɪg.ˈzek.jə.tɪv bræntʃ/ (AmE), /ɪg.ˈzek.jʊ.tɪv brɑːntʃ/ /; / /eɡ.ˈzek.jʊ.tɪv brɑːntʃ/ (BrE)
