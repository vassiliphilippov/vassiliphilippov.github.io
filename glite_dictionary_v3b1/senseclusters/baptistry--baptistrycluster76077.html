baptistry

noun
bowl for baptismal water
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbæp.tɪ.stri / (AmE), / ˈbæp.tɪ.stri / (BrE)
A bowl or vessel used to hold water for baptism in a church.

Examples

The baptistry bowl
