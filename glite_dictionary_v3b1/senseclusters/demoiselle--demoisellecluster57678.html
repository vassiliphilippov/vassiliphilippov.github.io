demoiselle

noun
small colorful tropical reef fish
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛmwəˈzɛl / (AmE), / ˌdɛm.wɑːˈzɛl / (BrE)
A small, brightly colored tropical marine fish found on coral reefs, especially of the damselfish family.

Examples

Colorful demoiselle fish
Many species of demoiselle are popular in home aquariums for their vivid colors.
The reef was teeming with demoiselles and other small fish.
We spotted a brilliant blue demoiselle darting among the coral branches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: damselfish - small, brightly colored tropical marine fish commonly found near coral reefs
