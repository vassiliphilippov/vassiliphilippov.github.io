postmortem

noun
discussion after an event to analyze it
6.5 5.7
Pronunciation: /poʊst.ˈmɔːr.təm/ /; / /ˈpoʊst.mɔːr.təm/ (AmE), /pəʊst.ˈmɔː.təm/ /; / /ˈpəʊst.mɔː.təm/ (BrE)
a discussion or analysis of an event after it has happened, especially to examine what went right or wrong

Examples

A postmortem meeting
After the project ended, the team held a postmortem to identify what worked and what didn't.
The coach led a detailed postmortem to analyze the reasons behind the team's defeat.
We always conduct a postmortem after major incidents to improve our response in the future.
