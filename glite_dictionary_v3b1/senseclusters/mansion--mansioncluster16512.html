mansion

noun
zodiac division in astrology
Pronunciation: / ˈmænʃən / (AmE), / ˈmænʃən / (BrE)
(Astrology) One of the twelve equal divisions of the zodiac.

Examples

Zodiac mansion
Each mansion of the zodiac is said to influence a different aspect of one's personality.
In traditional astrology, each zodiac sign corresponds to a mansion of the heavens.
The astrologer divided the chart into twelve mansions before beginning the reading.
