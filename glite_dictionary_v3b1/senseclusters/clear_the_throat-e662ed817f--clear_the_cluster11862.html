clear the throat

verb
to cough to remove mucus from the throat
1.0 2.3
Pronunciation: /klɪr ðə θroʊt/ (AmE), /klɪə ðə θrəʊt/ (BrE)
to cough slightly to remove mucus or food from one's throat, often to make the voice clear or get attention

Examples

To clear the throat softly

The lecturer discreetly cleared the throat to regain the audience's attention.
Whenever she's nervous, she tends to clear the throat repeatedly.
