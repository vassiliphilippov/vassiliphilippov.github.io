Allahu

proper noun
Arabic word for God
Pronunciation: / ɑːˈlɑːhuː / (AmE), / ɑːˈlɑːhuː / (BrE)
the Arabic word for 'God', especially used in Islamic prayers and phrases

Examples

Allahu akbar prayer
Muslims often say Allahu akbar during daily prayers.
The call to prayer begins with the words Allahu akbar, meaning 'God is greatest'.
He whispered Allahu in a moment of gratitude and reflection.
In the phrase Allahu akbar, the word means 'God' as the subject.
The imam recited Allahu during the prayer, emphasizing the nominative form.
Many Arabic expressions begin with Allahu to refer to God as the subject.

  • synonym: God - the supreme being in monotheistic religions
