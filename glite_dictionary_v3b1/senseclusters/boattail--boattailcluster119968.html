boattail

adjective
tapered rear design
7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈboʊt.teɪl/ (AmE), /ˈbəʊt.teɪl/ (BrE)
having a rear end that tapers smoothly like the stern of a boat, designed to reduce aerodynamic drag.

Examples

Boattail shape
