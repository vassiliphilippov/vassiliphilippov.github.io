pat-down

noun
body search by patting
4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpæt.daʊn/ (AmE), /ˈpæt.daʊn/ (BrE)
a quick search of a person's body by patting the outside of their clothes to check for concealed items

Examples

Airport pat-down
Before boarding the plane, each passenger received a pat-down from security staff.
The police officer performed a pat-down to ensure the suspect was not carrying any weapons.
At the concert entrance, a quick pat-down was required for everyone entering the venue.
