drive-thru

noun
restaurant where food is served to customers in their cars
4.6 2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdraɪv θru / (AmE), / ˈdraɪv θruː / (BrE)
a place or service (such as a restaurant, bank, or pharmacy) where you can be served or buy things without leaving your car

Examples

A drive-thru restaurant
Many coffee shops now offer a drive-thru for added convenience.
Rain or shine, the drive-thru is always busy during lunch hours.
We grabbed burgers at the drive-thru because we were in a hurry.
Rain or shine, the pharmacy drive-thru is always convenient for quick pickups.
The bank's drive-thru allows you to deposit checks without leaving your car.
We picked up burgers at the drive-thru on our way home.
