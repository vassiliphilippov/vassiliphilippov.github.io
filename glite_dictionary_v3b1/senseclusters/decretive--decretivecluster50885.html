decretive

noun
official order
Pronunciation: / dɪˈkriːtɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈkriːtɪv / (BrE)
an official order, decree, or authoritative decision (rare or archaic)

Examples

A royal decretive
The king's decretive was read aloud to the assembled crowd in the square.
After much debate, the council finally issued a decretive to resolve the dispute.
Historians found a decretive from the 17th century among the old manuscripts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decree - an official order issued by a legal authority
  • synonym: edict - an official proclamation or command
