vindictiveness

noun
desire for revenge
Pronunciation: / vɪnˈdɪktɪvnəs / (AmE), / vɪnˈdɪktɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of having or showing a strong or unreasoning desire for revenge

Examples

His vindictiveness shows
His vindictiveness made it difficult for him to let go of old grudges.
She was shocked by the vindictiveness in his response to the criticism.
The vindictiveness of the campaign left lasting divisions among the staff.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forgiveness - the action or process of forgiving or being forgiven
  • synonym: revenge - the action of inflicting hurt or harm on someone for a wrong suffered
