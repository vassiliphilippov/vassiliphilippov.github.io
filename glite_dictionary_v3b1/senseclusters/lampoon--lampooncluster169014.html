lampoon

noun
satirical imitation; mocking composition
7.7 6.4
Pronunciation: / læmˈpun / (AmE), / lamˈpuːn / (BrE)
A written work or speech that satirically imitates and ridicules a person, group, or style, often in a humorous way.

Examples

A lampoon article
His clever lampoon of celebrity culture became a viral sensation online.
She wrote a lampoon targeting the clichés often found in romantic comedies.
The magazine published a lampoon of the politician's latest speech, drawing laughter and controversy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parody - an imitation of the style of a writer or artist with deliberate exaggeration for comic effect
  • synonym: satire - the use of humor, irony, or ridicule to criticize
