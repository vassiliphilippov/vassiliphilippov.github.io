look-at-me behavior

noun
attention-seeking behaviour
4.5
Pronunciation: / lʊk æt miː bəˈheɪvjɚ / (AmE), / lʊk æt miː bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
behaviour intended to draw attention to oneself in a self-centered or conspicuous way

Examples

Look-at-me behavior annoys classmates
Her constant look-at-me behavior annoyed her classmates during the presentation.
Many celebrities are criticized for their look-at-me behavior on social media.
The team leader warned against look-at-me behavior that distracted from group goals.
