anteroposterior

adjective
front-to-back direction
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌæntəroʊpɑːˈstɪriər / (AmE), / ˌæntərəʊpɒˈstɪəriə / (BrE)
relating to, extending, or directed from the front to the back, especially of the body

Examples

Anteroposterior body view
The radiologist examined the anteroposterior view of the patient's chest X-ray.

Doctors measured the anteroposterior diameter of the patient's skull for the examination.
