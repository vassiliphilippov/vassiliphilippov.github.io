anteroposterior

adjective
front-to-back direction
Pronunciation: / ˌæntəroʊpɑːˈstɪriər / (AmE), / ˌæntərəʊpɒˈstɪəriə / (BrE)
relating to, extending, or directed from the front to the back, especially of the body

Examples

Anteroposterior body view
The radiologist examined the anteroposterior view of the patient's chest X-ray.
An anteroposterior measurement is often used to assess the depth of the body.
The technician positioned the patient for an anteroposterior scan of the spine.
The X-ray was taken in the anteroposterior position to provide a clear view of the chest.
Doctors measured the anteroposterior diameter of the patient's skull for the examination.
The injury extended in an anteroposterior direction from the forehead to the back of the head.
