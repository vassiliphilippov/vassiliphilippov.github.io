reconcentrate

verb
to gather people again
9.8
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈkɑːn.sə.treɪt/ /; / /riː.ˈkɑːn.sə.treɪt/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈkɒn.sən.treɪt/ /; / /riː.ˈkɒn.sən.treɪt/ (BrE)
to move or force people into a central or confined location again, often for control or security purposes

Examples

To reconcentrate the population
The authorities decided to reconcentrate the population in designated areas for easier management.
During the conflict, officials attempted to reconcentrate families into guarded camps.
After the evacuation, the government planned to reconcentrate residents in a safer zone.
