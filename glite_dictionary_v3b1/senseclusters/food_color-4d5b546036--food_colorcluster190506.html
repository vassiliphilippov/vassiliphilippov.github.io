food color

noun
substance for coloring food
2.3 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / fuÀêd Ààk ål…ôr / (AmE), / fuÀêd Ààk ål…ô / (BrE)
a substance added to food or drink to give or restore color, typically approved for consumption

Examples

Many bakers use food color to create vibrant cakes and pastries.
