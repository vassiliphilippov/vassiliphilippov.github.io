monastery

noun
residence of a religious community
6.0 8.58/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…ëÀê.n…ôÀåster.i / (AmE), / Ààm…ín.…ô.st…ôr.i / (BrE)
a building or complex where a community of monks or other religious devotees live, work, and worship together

Examples

A monastery building
Monks in the monastery follow a strict daily routine of prayer and work.
The monastery was known for its peaceful gardens and beautiful architecture.
The old monastery perched on the hill is now a museum.

Synonyms

  • abbey
    6.5 4.41/m
    an abbey is a large religious complex often led by an abbot, while a monastery is any place where monks live and worship.
  • friary
    9.3 0.03/m
    a friary is where friars live and work among people, while a monastery houses monks who live a more secluded life.
  • cloister
    9.1 0.43/m
    a cloister is a secluded part or life within a religious house, while a monastery is the whole building where a community lives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: convent - residence for a community of nuns or religious women
