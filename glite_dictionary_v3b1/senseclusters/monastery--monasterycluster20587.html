monastery

noun
residence of a religious community
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑː.nəˌster.i / (AmE), / ˈmɒn.ə.stər.i / (BrE)
a building or complex where a community of monks or other religious devotees live, work, and worship together

Examples

A monastery building
Monks in the monastery follow a strict daily routine of prayer and work.
The monastery was known for its peaceful gardens and beautiful architecture.
The old monastery perched on the hill is now a museum.

  • synonym: convent - residence for a community of nuns or religious women
