botanically

adverb
relating to botany
Pronunciation: / bəˈtænɪkli / (AmE), / bəˈtænɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to plants or the science of botany

Examples

Considered botanically correct
The avocado is botanically considered a berry, even though it is used as a vegetable in cooking.
Some foods we call vegetables are botanically classified as fruits.
He explained the differences between herbs and shrubs botanically during the lecture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scientifically - in a way that relates to science
  • antonym: zoologically - in a way that relates to animals or animal science
