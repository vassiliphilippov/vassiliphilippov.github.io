fierce warrior

noun
brave and aggressive fighter
Pronunciation: / fɪrs ˈwɔːriər / (AmE), / fɪəs ˈwɒriə / (BrE)
a person who is brave, aggressive, and determined in battle or conflict

Examples

A fierce warrior
As a fierce warrior, she never backed down from a challenge on the battlefield.
He became legendary as the most fierce warrior in the king's army.
The tales often spoke of a fierce warrior who defended the village from invaders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: warrior - a person who fights in battles or is known for courage and skill in war
