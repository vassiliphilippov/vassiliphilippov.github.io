skimmer

noun
kitchen utensil for removing surface fat or solids
1.0 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈskɪ.mər/ (AmE), /ˈskɪ.mə/ (BrE)
a kitchen utensil with a flat or perforated surface used for removing fat, foam, or other substances from the surface of liquids

Examples

Soup skimmer
A skimmer is essential for making clear broth by taking off the fat and impurities.
After frying the potatoes, he lifted them out of the oil with a skimmer.
She used a skimmer to remove the foam from the boiling soup.
