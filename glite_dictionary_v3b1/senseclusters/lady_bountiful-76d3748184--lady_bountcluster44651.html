Lady Bountiful

noun
charitable or generous woman
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪdi ˈbaʊntɪfəl / (AmE), / ˈleɪdi ˈbaʊntɪfəl / (BrE)
a woman who is generous or charitable, particularly one who gives aid or money to others, sometimes in a condescending manner

Examples

A Lady Bountiful woman
Everyone in town regarded her as a true Lady Bountiful for her endless acts of charity.
She didn't want to appear as a Lady Bountiful but genuinely cared for those she helped.
The local newspaper praised the Lady Bountiful for her selfless donations to the children's hospital.

Related Terms

  • synonym: philanthropist - person who seeks to promote the welfare of others through generous donations
