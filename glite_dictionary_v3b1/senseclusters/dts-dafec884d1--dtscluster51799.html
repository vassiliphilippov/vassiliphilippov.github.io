DTs

noun
alcohol withdrawal delirium
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdiː.tiːz/ (AmE), /ˈdiː.tiːz/ (BrE)
A severe form of delirium involving sudden and severe mental or nervous system changes, usually caused by withdrawal from alcohol.

Examples

The DTs after drinking
After quitting drinking abruptly, he developed hallucinations and the DTs.
Doctors quickly recognized the signs of DTs in the disoriented man.
The patient was admitted to the hospital suffering from the DTs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delirium tremens - acute episode of delirium caused by alcohol withdrawal
