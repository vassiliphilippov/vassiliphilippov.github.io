butt weld

noun
joint formed by welding metal edges together
Pronunciation: / bʌt wɛld / (AmE), / bʌt wɛld / (BrE)
A joint made by welding two pieces of metal end to end along their edges.

Examples

A butt weld joint
A properly executed butt weld can withstand high pressure and stress.
Inspectors checked the quality of the butt weld along the steel beams.
The pipes were joined with a butt weld to ensure a strong connection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: butt joint - a joint formed by placing the ends of two parts together without overlap
