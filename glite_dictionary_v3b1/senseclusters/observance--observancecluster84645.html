observance

noun
formal ceremony or event for a special occasion
Pronunciation: / əbˈzɝː.vəns / (AmE), / əbˈzɜː.vəns / (BrE)
a formal ceremony or event that takes place to mark or honor a special occasion

Examples

A holiday observance
A moment of silence was included in the observance of Remembrance Day.
Many people attend religious observances during major holidays.
The city held an observance each year to honor the war veterans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ceremony - a formal religious or public occasion, typically one celebrating a particular event
  • synonym: ritual - a religious or solemn ceremony consisting of a series of actions performed according to a prescribed order
