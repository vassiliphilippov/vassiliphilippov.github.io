whirl

noun
spinning or spiraling shape
Pronunciation: / wɝl / (AmE), / wɜːl / (BrE)
a rapid spinning or circular movement

Examples

A whirl of leaves
A sudden whirl of autumn leaves blew across the empty street.
In the photograph, her hair appeared to be caught in a whirl of motion.
The dancer's skirts spun out in a colorful whirl on stage.
He gave the top a quick whirl to start it spinning.
The leaves were caught up in a whirl of wind.
With a sudden whirl, the dancer spun across the stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eddy - a circular movement of water, air, or smoke
  • synonym: spin - the act of turning around quickly
  • antonym: stillness - absence of movement or rotation
  • synonym: swirl - a twisting or spiraling shape or movement
  • synonym: twist - the action of turning something quickly
