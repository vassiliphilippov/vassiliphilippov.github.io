Synonyms

  • spin
    1.1 11/m
    spin is a general turning motion used for objects or people, while whirl is a rapid, circular movement.
  • twirl
    2.7 0.54/m
    twirl is a quick, playful turning often done by people or objects, while whirl is a faster, more forceful circular movement.
  • swirl
    2.7 3.77/m
    swirl is a twisting, spiral motion often seen in liquids or smoke, while whirl is a rapid circular movement of objects.
  • gyration
    7.8 0.25/m
    gyration is a repeated circular or spiral movement often described in formal writing, while whirl is a single rapid spin.
