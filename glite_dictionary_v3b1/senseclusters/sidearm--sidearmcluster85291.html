sidearm

noun
small personal firearm
4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈsaɪd.ɑːrm/ (AmE), /ˈsaɪd.ɑːm/ (BrE)
a small firearm, such as a pistol or revolver, carried at a person's side for personal defense

Examples

A police sidearm
The detective reached for his sidearm when he heard a noise in the alley.
Soldiers are often issued a sidearm in addition to their main weapon.
She practiced drawing her sidearm quickly in case of emergencies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pistol - a small handheld firearm
  • synonym: revolver - a type of handgun with a revolving cylinder
  • antonym: rifle - a long-barreled firearm designed to be fired from the shoulder
