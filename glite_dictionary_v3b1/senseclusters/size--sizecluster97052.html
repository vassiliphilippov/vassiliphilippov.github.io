size

noun
standard measurement (esp. in clothing, shoes)
Pronunciation: / saɪz / (AmE), / saɪz / (BrE)
the physical magnitude, dimensions, or extent of something, or a standard measurement for clothes, shoes, etc.

Examples

Clothing size
Before ordering online, check the size chart to make sure the clothes will fit.
He wears a size 13 shoe, so it's hard for him to find sneakers in stores.
The shirt comes in every size, from small to extra-large.
Even the size of the salad was enough to serve two people.
He couldn't believe the size of the mansion when he saw it in person.
The size of the stadium amazed visiting fans from smaller towns.
Please select the correct shoe size before ordering online.
The size of the crowd surprised the event organizers.
The box varied in size, but each held the same amount.

Related Terms

  • synonym: magnitude - great size or extent
  • synonym: measurement - the act or process of measuring something
  • antonym: shape - the external form or appearance of something
  • antonym: smallness - the state of being small in size
