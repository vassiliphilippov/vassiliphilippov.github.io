underinsurance

noun
insufficient insurance coverage
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərɪnˈʃʊrəns / (AmE), / ˌʌndərɪnˈʃʊərəns / (BrE)
the state of having insurance that does not fully cover the costs, value, or losses you might face
