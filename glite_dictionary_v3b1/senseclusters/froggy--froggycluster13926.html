froggy

adjective
hoarse or croaky voice
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɑːɡi / (AmE), / ˈfrɒɡi / (BrE)
having a hoarse, rough, or croaky voice, especially for a short time

Examples

Froggy voice
After cheering at the game all night, his voice sounded froggy the next morning.
She woke up with a froggy voice because of her cold.
My throat feels froggy whenever I talk too much at parties.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - having a smooth and easy-to-hear voice
  • synonym: hoarse - having a rough or harsh-sounding voice
