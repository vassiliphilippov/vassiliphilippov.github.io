in-basket

noun
tray for incoming documents
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌbæskɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌbɑːskɪt / (BrE)
a tray or container placed on a desk for holding incoming documents, mail, or work to be dealt with

Examples

An in-basket tray
Her in-basket was overflowing with reports she needed to review.
Please leave any completed forms in my in-basket before you leave.
The secretary quickly sorted the mail into the manager’s in-basket.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inbox - an electronic or physical location for incoming messages or documents
  • antonym: out-basket - a tray for outgoing documents
