flexography

noun
printing with flexible plates
Pronunciation: / flɛkˈsɑːɡrəfi / (AmE), / flɛkˈsɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
a printing process that uses flexible relief plates to print on various materials, especially for packaging

Examples

Flexography printing process
Many food packages are printed using flexography for its efficiency.
The company invested in new flexography equipment to improve their packaging quality.
Labels and plastic bags are often produced with flexography because it works well on flexible materials.
