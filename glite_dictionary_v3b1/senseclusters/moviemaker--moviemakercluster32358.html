moviemaker

noun
film creator
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːviˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈmuːviˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who creates, directs, or produces movies

Examples

A moviemaker directs films
The moviemaker spent years developing the script before filming began.
Many aspiring moviemakers dream of winning an award at a major film festival.
As a talented moviemaker, she brings unique stories to the big screen.
The talented moviemaker received international praise for her documentary.
Aspiring to be a moviemaker, he studied film at university.
Many young moviemakers start by creating short films with friends.

Related Terms

  • antonym: actor - a person who performs in movies or plays
  • synonym: director - a person who directs the making of a film
  • synonym: filmmaker - a person who makes films or movies
