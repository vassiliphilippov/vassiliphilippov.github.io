stroke

noun
maximum movement range of a mechanical part
5.6
Pronunciation: / stroʊk / (AmE), / strəʊk / (BrE)
the maximum movement distance available to a pivoted or reciprocating part, such as in a machine, often controlled by a cam

Examples

Machine stroke length
Engineers adjusted the stroke of the arm to optimize the machine's efficiency.
The length of the cam determines the stroke of the lever.
The piston completed its full stroke before reversing direction.
