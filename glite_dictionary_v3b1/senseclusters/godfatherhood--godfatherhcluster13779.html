godfatherhood

noun
role of protector
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑdˌfɑðɚˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈɡɒdˌfɑːðəhʊd / (BrE)
the role, influence, or authority of a godfather, especially as a protector or powerful sponsor, often used metaphorically

Examples

Business godfatherhood
His godfatherhood in the business world opened many doors for young entrepreneurs.
The politician's godfatherhood was evident in the way he protected his allies.
Many admired her godfatherhood as she guided and supported new artists in the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mentorship - guidance or advice provided by a mentor
  • synonym: patronage - support or sponsorship given by a powerful person
  • antonym: subordination - the state of being under the authority of another
