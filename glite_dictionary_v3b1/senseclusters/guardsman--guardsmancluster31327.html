guardsman

noun
member of guard or guards unit
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrdzmən / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdzmən / (BrE)
a male soldier who is a member of a military unit called 'the guard' or 'guards', often involved in ceremonial duties or elite operations.

Examples

A guardsman in uniform
As a guardsman, he wore a distinctive uniform and participated in major state ceremonies.
Several guardsmen were honored for their bravery during the military parade.
The guardsman stood at attention outside the palace gates, unmoving and vigilant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guard - a person who keeps watch, especially a soldier assigned to protect
