Tadzhik

noun
an Iranian language of Tajikistan and Iran
9.0
Pronunciation: / tɑːdˈʒiːk / (AmE), / tædˈʒiːk / (BrE)
the Iranian language spoken by the Tajik people, closely related to Farsi, used primarily in Tajikistan and parts of Iran
