convolution of Broca

noun
motor speech center in the brain
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːnvəˈluːʃən əv ˈbroʊkə / (AmE), / ˌkɒnvəˈluːʃən əv ˈbrəʊkə / (BrE)
the area of the brain in the left hemisphere, usually in the frontal lobe, that functions as the motor speech center in most people
