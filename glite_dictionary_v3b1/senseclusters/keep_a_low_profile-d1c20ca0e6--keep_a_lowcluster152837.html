keep a low profile

verb
avoid attracting attention
3.4 3.2
Pronunciation: / kip ə loʊ ˈproʊfaɪl / (AmE), / kiːp ə ləʊ ˈprəʊfaɪl / (BrE)
to deliberately avoid attracting attention to yourself; to act in a way that does not make you noticeable

Examples

To keep a low profile

When traveling in unfamiliar cities, it's often safer to keep a low profile.
