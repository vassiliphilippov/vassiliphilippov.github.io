imprint

noun
mark made by pressure or printing
3.5 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈɪm.prɪnt/ /; / /ɪm.ˈprɪnt/ (AmE), /ˈɪm.prɪnt/ /; / /ɪm.ˈprɪnt/ (BrE)
