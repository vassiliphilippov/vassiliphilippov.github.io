tacket

noun
small nail or tack
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtækɪt / (AmE), / ˈtækɪt / (BrE)
a small nail or tack, especially one used in shoemaking or carpentry

Examples

A tacket for shoes
The cobbler hammered a tacket into the heel of the shoe to secure it.
She found a rusty tacket among the old tools in her grandfather's workshop.
A single tacket was enough to hold the thin piece of wood in place.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tack - a small, sharp nail with a flat head
