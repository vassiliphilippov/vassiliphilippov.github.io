grittily

adverb
with courage or toughness
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɪtɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪtɪli / (BrE)
in a way that shows courage, toughness, or determination, especially in difficult situations

Examples

Grittily facing problems
Despite the harsh criticism, she responded grittily and continued her work.
The team played grittily through the final minutes, refusing to give up.
He faced the long recovery grittily, determined to walk again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: resolutely - in a determined and unwavering way
  • antonym: timidly - in a shy or lacking courage way
