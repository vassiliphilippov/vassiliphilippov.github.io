zugzwang

noun
chess disadvantage
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtsuːɡˌtsvɑːŋ / (AmE), / ˈtsuːɡˌtsvaŋ / (BrE)
a situation (originally in chess) where any move you make will make things worse, often used for similar dilemmas in life
