tawa

noun
Indian cooking pan
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːwə / (AmE), / ˈtɑːwə / (BrE)
a flat, round metal pan used in Indian cooking for making bread such as chapati or frying foods

Examples

Chapati tawa
She cooked the chapati on a hot tawa.
A tawa is essential in many Indian kitchens for making flatbreads like roti and paratha.
He heated oil in the tawa before frying the vegetables for dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: griddle - a flat pan or surface for cooking food with dry heat
  • synonym: skillet - a frying pan with a long handle
