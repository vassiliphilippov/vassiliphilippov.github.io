brogressive movement

noun
men's movement for progressive views
Pronunciation: / broʊˈɡrɛsɪv ˈmuːvmənt / (AmE), / brəʊˈɡrɛsɪv ˈmuːvmənt / (BrE)
A movement that promotes progressive social and political views specifically among men, often focusing on issues such as gender equality and masculinity.

Examples

The brogressive movement group
He joined a brogressive movement group to discuss gender equality from a male perspective.
The brogressive movement has gained traction in urban communities concerned about toxic masculinity.
The brogressive movement seeks to redefine traditional expectations of men in society.
