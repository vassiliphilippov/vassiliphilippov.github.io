opportunist

noun
person who exploits opportunities for gain
1.3 4.8
Pronunciation: /ɑː.pər.ˈtuː.nɪst/ /; / /ə.pər.ˈtuː.nɪst/ (AmE), /ɒ.pə.ˈtjuː.nɪst/ /; / /ɒ.pə.ˈtuː.nɪst/ (BrE)
a person who seeks to gain personal advantage by taking immediate opportunities, often without regard for principles or ethics

Examples

A clever opportunist
As an opportunist, she changed her stance whenever it suited her advancement.
Some consider him an opportunist rather than a true leader.
The opportunist took credit for the team's work whenever he could.
