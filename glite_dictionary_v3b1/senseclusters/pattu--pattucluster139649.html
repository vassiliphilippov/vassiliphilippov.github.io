pattu

noun
South Indian fabric
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːtu / (AmE), / ˈpʌtu / (BrE)
a fine silk or cotton fabric from South India, commonly used to make saris and traditional garments

Examples

Pattu sari
She wore a beautiful pattu sari at the wedding.
The shop displayed colorful pattu fabrics imported from South India.
Traditional ceremonies often feature garments made from luxurious pattu.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cotton - a soft, white fiber used to make fabric
  • synonym: silk - a soft, smooth fabric made from silkworm threads
