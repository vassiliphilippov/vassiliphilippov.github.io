service station

noun
place for fuel and car maintenance
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːvɪs ˌsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs ˌsteɪʃən / (BrE)
A place where petrol or gasoline and oil are sold, and where cars can be repaired or maintained.

Examples

A service station shop
Many service stations are open 24 hours to serve long-distance travelers.
The nearest service station offers both fuel and minor repairs for vehicles.
We stopped at a service station to fill up the car and grab some snacks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: garage - Place where cars are repaired and maintained
  • synonym: gas station - American term for a place selling fuel for cars
