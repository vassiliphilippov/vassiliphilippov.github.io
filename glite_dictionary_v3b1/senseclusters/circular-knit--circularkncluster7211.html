circular-knit

adjective
knitted in tubular form without seams
8.8 5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈsɜːr.kjə.lər.nɪt/ /; / /ˈsɜːr.kjə.lər nɪt/ (AmE), /ˈsɜː.kjə.lə.nɪt/ /; / /ˈsɜː.kjə.lə nɪt/ (BrE)

Examples
