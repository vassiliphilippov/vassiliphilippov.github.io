circular-knit

adjective
knitted in tubular form without seams
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːkjələr nɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkjʊlə nɪt / (BrE)
knitted in a tubular form, typically without seams, using a circular or tubular knitting machine.

Examples

Circular-knit fabric
Many winter hats are made from circular-knit wool for added stretch and warmth.
She prefers circular-knit sweaters because they have no uncomfortable side seams.
The company uses circular-knit fabric for its athletic wear to provide a smooth finish.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flat-knit - knitted as a flat piece rather than tubular
