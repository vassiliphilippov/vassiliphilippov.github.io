Tagamet

proper noun
trade name for a stomach acid-reducing drug
Pronunciation: / ˈtæɡəˌmɛt / (AmE), / ˈtæɡəmɛt / (BrE)
A proprietary name for cimetidine, a drug used to treat peptic ulcers and conditions involving excessive stomach acid by reducing acid secretion.

Examples

Tagamet medicine
After a week on Tagamet, his heartburn had greatly improved.
Some patients experience side effects when taking Tagamet for acid reflux.
The doctor prescribed Tagamet to help manage her peptic ulcer symptoms.
