gyve

verb
to shackle with chains
Pronunciation: / ɡaɪv / (AmE), / ɡaɪv / (BrE)
to shackle or restrain someone with chains, especially around the ankles

Examples

To gyve a prisoner
The guards gyved the prisoners before leading them to the courtyard.
In old tales, villains would gyve their captives to prevent escape.
He was gyved at the ankles, unable to move freely.

Related Terms

  • antonym: free - to release from confinement or restraint
  • synonym: shackle - to restrain with metal fastenings
