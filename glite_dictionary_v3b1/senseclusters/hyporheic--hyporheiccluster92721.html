hyporheic

adjective
under stream bed
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpoʊˈriːɪk / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəˈriːɪk / (BrE)
relating to the region beneath and alongside a stream bed where groundwater and surface water mix

Examples

Hyporheic zone study
Researchers measured nutrient levels in the hyporheic zone to assess stream health.
The hyporheic area plays a crucial role in filtering water beneath riverbeds.
Many aquatic insects depend on the hyporheic environment for part of their life cycle.

  • antonym: surface - relating to the top layer or outside part
