accordion door

noun
type of interior folding door
7.5
Pronunciation: / əˈkɔɹdiən dɔɹ / (AmE), / əˈkɔːdiən dɔː / (BrE)
an interior door that consists of multiple vertical panels which fold together in sections rather than swinging open on hinges

Examples

An accordion door panel
Instead of a traditional hinged door, they chose an accordion door for the pantry.
The accordion door folded neatly to one side, revealing the storage closet.
We installed an accordion door to save space in the laundry room.
