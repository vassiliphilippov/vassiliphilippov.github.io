forend

noun
front part of gun stock
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔːrˌɛnd / (AmE), / ˈfɔːrˌɛnd / (BrE)
the front part of the stock of a firearm, usually held by the non-trigger hand for support

Examples

The forend of rifle
He placed his left hand on the forend to steady the rifle before firing.
The wooden forend of the shotgun was polished and smooth to the touch.
Hunters often customize the forend of their firearms for better grip and comfort.

Related Terms

  • antonym: buttstock - the rear part of a firearm's stock, held against the shoulder
  • synonym: forearm - the part of a firearm's stock in front of the trigger guard
