re-release

noun
new issue of old work
Pronunciation: / ˌriː.rɪˈliːs / (AmE), / ˌriː.rɪˈliːs / (BrE)
a new release of something, such as a film or album, that was previously made available to the public

Examples

A re-release album
The band announced a re-release of their most popular album with bonus tracks.
Fans were excited about the re-release of the classic video game after many years.
The re-release of the movie attracted a new generation of viewers to theaters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: debut - the first public appearance or release of something
  • synonym: reissue - a new publication or release of something previously issued
