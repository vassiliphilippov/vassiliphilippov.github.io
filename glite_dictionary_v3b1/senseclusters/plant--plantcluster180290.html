Synonyms

  • industrial plant
    3.5 0.32/m
    industrial plant refers to large buildings for industrial production and heavy equipment, while plant is a general term for such sites.
  • manufacturing plant
    3.2 0.80/m
    manufacturing plant describes a large facility where goods are made on machines, while plant can mean any industrial production site.
  • factory
    1.0 51/m
    factory is a building where goods are made, often with assembly lines, while plant covers any industrial buildings or complex.
  • works
    3.9 2.80/m
    works often means an extensive industrial site or specific production area in formal or British use, while plant is the general industrial site term.
