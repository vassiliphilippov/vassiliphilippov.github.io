pop

adjective
new and broadly appealing, esp. to youth
1.0
Pronunciation: / pɑp / (AmE), / pɒp / (BrE)
(of music, art, etc.) new and intended to appeal to the general public, especially young people.

Examples

Pop style art
Her latest single has a pop sound that's topping the charts.
That movie uses pop culture references to connect with younger viewers.
The gallery is showcasing pop art from the 1960s.
