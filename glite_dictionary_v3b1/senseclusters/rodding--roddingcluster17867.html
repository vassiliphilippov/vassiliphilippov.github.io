rodding

noun
reinforcing with rods
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈrɑː.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈrɒ.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process of inserting or fixing metal rods to support or reinforce a structure, material, or concrete

Examples

Rodding concrete beams
The engineer recommended rodding the concrete beams to increase their load-bearing capacity.
Proper rodding is essential to prevent cracks in large concrete slabs.
During construction, rodding was used to reinforce the walls of the new building.
