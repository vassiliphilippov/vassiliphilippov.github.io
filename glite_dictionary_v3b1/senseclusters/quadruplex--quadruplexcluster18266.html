quadruplex

noun

Pronunciation: /kwə.ˈdruː.pleks/ /; / /kwɑː.ˈdruː.pleks/ (AmE), /kwə.ˈdruː.pleks/ /; / /kwɒ.ˈdruː.pleks/ (BrE)
(biology) a four-stranded nucleic-acid structure formed by guanine-rich sequences in DNA or RNA, often called a G-quadruplex.
