Gaeltacht

proper noun
Irish-speaking region
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪltɑːxt / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪltɑːxt / (BrE)
a region in Ireland where Irish (Gaelic) is the primary spoken language and culture is preserved

Examples

The Gaeltacht region
Many students visit the Gaeltacht to improve their Irish language skills.
The government provides support to preserve the culture of the Gaeltacht regions.
She spent her summer in a Gaeltacht learning traditional Irish songs and dances.
