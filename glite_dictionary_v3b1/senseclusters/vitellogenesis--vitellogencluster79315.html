vitellogenesis

noun
yolk formation in eggs
9.9
Pronunciation: / vɪˌtɛloʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (AmE), / vɪˌtɛləˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (BrE)
the process by which yolk is produced and deposited in the developing eggs of animals

Examples

Vitellogenesis in fish
Scientists observed vitellogenesis in fish to learn how nutrients are stored in eggs.
The disruption of vitellogenesis can affect the reproductive success of many insects.
Hormones play a crucial role in regulating vitellogenesis during egg development.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oogenesis - the formation and development of an ovum (egg cell)
