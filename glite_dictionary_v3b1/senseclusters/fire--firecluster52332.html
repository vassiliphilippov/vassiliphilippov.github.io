fire

verb
bake in a kiln (pottery, bricks)
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə / (BrE)
to bake (pottery, bricks, etc.) in a kiln to harden them

Examples

To fire pottery
After shaping the clay, you need to fire it to make it durable.
The artist fires each ceramic piece twice to achieve the desired finish.
The pottery was carefully fired in the kiln for several hours.
