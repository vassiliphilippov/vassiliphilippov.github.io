lens system

noun
arrangement of lenses forming images
2.1 3.4
Pronunciation: /lenz ˈsɪs.təm/ (AmE), /lenz ˈsɪs.təm/ (BrE)
an arrangement of two or more lenses used together to focus or direct light and form images, especially in optical devices such as cameras, microscopes, or telescopes

Examples

A lens system in cameras
A microscope's lens system magnifies tiny objects so they can be studied in detail.
Engineers redesigned the lens system to reduce optical distortions in the new telescope.
The camera's lens system enables it to capture sharp and detailed photographs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lens - a piece of glass or other transparent material shaped to focus light
  • synonym: optical system - a set of optical components arranged to perform a specific function with light
