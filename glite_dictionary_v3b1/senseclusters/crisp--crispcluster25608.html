crisp

verb
make food brown and firm by heating
Pronunciation: / krɪsp / (AmE), / krɪsp / (BrE)
to make (food) brown, firm, and brittle by heating

Examples

To crisp the bacon
She likes to crisp the bacon in a hot oven until it is perfectly golden.
The chef will crisp the duck skin before serving the dish.
You can crisp the potatoes in a skillet for extra texture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: toast - to make (something, especially bread) brown and crisp by heating in a toaster or oven
