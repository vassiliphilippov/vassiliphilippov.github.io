sixer

noun
pack of six items
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪksər / (AmE), / ˈsɪksə / (BrE)
a package or container holding six similar items, especially bottles or cans of beer

Examples

A sixer of beer
He picked up a sixer of soda for the picnic.
They shared a cold sixer while watching the game on TV.
Can you grab a sixer of beer on your way home?

Related Terms

  • synonym: six-pack - a package of six bottles or cans, especially of beer
