labor coach

noun
person who supports a woman during labor
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪ.bɚ koʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.bə kəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a person, often the partner or a trained assistant, who supports a woman during childbirth by helping her use techniques learned in childbirth classes

Examples

Labor coach support
Her husband acted as her labor coach, helping her with breathing exercises.
Many hospitals now encourage the presence of a labor coach in the delivery room.
The labor coach stayed by her side during the entire delivery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doula - a trained professional who provides continuous support during childbirth
