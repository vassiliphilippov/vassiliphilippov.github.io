grow

verb
become more mature; develop emotionally
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡroʊ / (AmE), / ɡrəʊ / (BrE)
(of a person or character) to become more mature, emotionally or psychologically; to develop in attitude, understanding, or behavior.

Examples

Grow in confidence
After studying abroad, she began to grow in confidence and independence.
He needed some time away from home to grow emotionally.
Young people often grow a lot during their first year of university.

  • synonym: mature - become fully developed in personality or attitude
