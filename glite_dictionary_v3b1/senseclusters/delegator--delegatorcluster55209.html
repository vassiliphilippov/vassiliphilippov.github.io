delegator

noun
one who assigns tasks
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛlɪˌɡeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈdɛlɪˌɡeɪtə /; / ˈdɛlɪɡeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who gives tasks or responsibilities to other people

Examples

The delegator assigns tasks
In a well-organized team, the delegator ensures that everyone knows their responsibilities.
The software delegator forwards incoming requests to the appropriate processing module.
As a manager, being an effective delegator can improve productivity and reduce stress.
As a skilled delegator, she ensured every team member had a clear role in the project.
The delegator trusted his assistant to manage the client meeting independently.
Good leaders are often effective delegators who empower others to succeed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assigner - a person who gives tasks or duties to others
  • antonym: delegatee - a person or component who receives tasks or responsibilities
  • synonym: supervisor - a person who oversees and directs others' work
