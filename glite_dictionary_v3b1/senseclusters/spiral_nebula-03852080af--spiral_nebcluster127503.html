spiral nebula

noun
galaxy with spiral arms
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪrəl ˈnɛbjələ / (AmE), / ˈspaɪərəl ˈnɛbjʊlə / (BrE)
a galaxy with a spiral structure, featuring arms of younger stars winding out from a central core of older stars

Examples

A spiral nebula galaxy
Astronomers once believed a spiral nebula was a type of cloud within our own galaxy.
Photographs reveal the stunning beauty of each spiral nebula in the night sky.
The discovery that the Andromeda spiral nebula is actually a separate galaxy revolutionized astronomy.
