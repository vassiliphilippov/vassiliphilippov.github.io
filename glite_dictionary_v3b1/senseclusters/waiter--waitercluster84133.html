waiter

noun
restaurant server
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪtər / (AmE), / ˈweɪtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to serve food and drinks to customers at their tables in a restaurant or café

Examples

A waiter serves food
After working as a waiter for years, he knows how to handle difficult customers.
The waiter brought us the menu and took our order.
We left a generous tip for our waiter because the service was excellent.

Related Terms

  • antonym: customer - a person being served by a waiter in a restaurant
  • synonym: server - a person who serves food and drinks to people in a restaurant
  • synonym: waitress - a female person who serves food and drinks in a restaurant
