shigellosis

noun
intestinal infection by Shigella bacteria
4.9
Pronunciation: / ʃɪɡəˈloʊsɪs / (AmE), / ʃɪɡɛˈləʊsɪs / (BrE)
an acute infectious disease of the intestines caused by Shigella bacteria, resulting in diarrhea, fever, and abdominal pain

Examples

Shigellosis infection
After experiencing severe diarrhea, she was diagnosed with shigellosis by her doctor.
Proper hygiene and sanitation are important in preventing shigellosis outbreaks in communities.
