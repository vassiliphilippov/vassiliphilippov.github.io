Hottentot bread

noun
edible root of elephant's-foot plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑtənˌtɑt brɛd / (AmE), / ˈhɒtəntɒt brɛd / (BrE)
the thick edible rootstock of the elephant's-foot plant (Dioscorea elephantipes), eaten as food in some regions of southern Africa

Examples

Botanists have studied how Hottentot bread survives in arid environments.
