award

verb
to give as an honor or reward
1.5 21/m
Pronunciation: / …ôÀàw…îrd / (AmE), / …ôÀàw…îÀêd / (BrE)
to officially give something, such as a prize, money, or honor, to someone for achievement or as a result of a decision

Examples
