accented

adjective
bearing the main stress in pronunciation
9.5 4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈæk.sɛn.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈæk.sɛntɪd /; / ˈæk.sɛn.tɪd / (BrE)
(of a syllable or word) pronounced with greater stress or emphasis than others

Examples

Accented syllable
