dumb Dora

noun
foolish or naive person (dated slang)
Pronunciation: / dʌm ˈdɔɹə / (AmE), / dʌm ˈdɔːrə / (BrE)
An unintelligent or naive woman, especially one who is easily fooled or acts silly; a foolish person.

Examples

A dumb Dora woman
Don't be such a dumb Dora—think before you speak!
In old movies, the comic relief was often played by a dumb Dora character.
She called me a dumb Dora for missing the deadline.
