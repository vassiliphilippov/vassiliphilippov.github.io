pathfinding

noun
finding a route
Pronunciation: / ˈpæθˌfaɪndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːθˌfaɪndɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of determining a route or path from one point to another, especially in computing or games

Examples

Advanced pathfinding algorithms
Modern video games use advanced pathfinding to help characters move around obstacles.
The robot's pathfinding system allows it to reach its destination without bumping into walls.
Good pathfinding is essential for efficient delivery routes in logistics software.

Related Terms

  • synonym: navigation - the act of planning and following a route
  • synonym: routing - the process of selecting a path for traffic in a network or system
