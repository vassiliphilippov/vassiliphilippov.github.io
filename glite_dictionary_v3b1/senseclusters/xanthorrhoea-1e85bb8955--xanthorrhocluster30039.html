Xanthorrhoea

noun
genus of Australian grass trees
9.9
Pronunciation: /zæn.θə.ˈriː.ə/ (AmE), /zæn.θə.ˈriː.ə/ (BrE)
A genus of Australian perennial plants known as grass trees, characterized by thick, woody stems and long, narrow leaves, sometimes classified in the family Xanthorrhoeaceae.
