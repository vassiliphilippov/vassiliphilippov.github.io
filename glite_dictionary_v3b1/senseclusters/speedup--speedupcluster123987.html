speedup

noun
an increase in speed; acceleration
3.6 0.35/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàspiÀêdÀå åp / (AmE), / ÀàspiÀêd åp / (BrE)
an increase in speed or the rate at which something happens; the act of accelerating.

Examples

A speedup in traffic
A sudden speedup in traffic flow occurred after the road construction ended.
The company implemented several measures to achieve a speedup in production.
We noticed a significant speedup in the computer's performance after the upgrade.

Synonyms

  • acceleration
    3.7 4.52/m
    acceleration is the technical process of increasing speed in physics or machines, while speedup is the general increase in rate.
  • quickening
    6.7 0.16/m
    quickening means a gradual or lively increase in speed in a process, while speedup is a simple rise in rate or pace.

Related Terms

  • antonym: slowdown - a reduction in speed or rate
