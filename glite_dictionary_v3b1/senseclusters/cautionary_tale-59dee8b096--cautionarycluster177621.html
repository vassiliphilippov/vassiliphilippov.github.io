cautionary tale

noun
story with a warning or lesson
9.9 3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkɔː.ʃə.nər.ɪ teɪl/ (AmE), /ˈkɔː.ʃən.rɪ teɪl/ /; / /ˈkɔː.ʃə.nər.ɪ teɪl/ (BrE)
A story told to warn others about the possible dangers or consequences of certain actions or behaviors.

Examples

A cautionary tale
Her experience with online scams served as a cautionary tale for her friends.
Many urban legends function as cautionary tales meant to scare children into good behavior.
The story of Icarus is often considered a classic cautionary tale about hubris.
