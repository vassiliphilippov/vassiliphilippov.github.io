processability

noun
ease of processing
Pronunciation: / ˌprɑːsəˈsæbɪlɪti /; / ˌprɑːsəˌsəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌprəʊsəˈsæbɪlɪti /; / ˌprɒsəˌsəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the extent or quality of being able to be processed or handled easily by people or systems (such as language, materials, or substances)
