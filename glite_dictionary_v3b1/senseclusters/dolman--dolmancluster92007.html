dolman

noun
a hussar's decorated short jacket
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊlmən / (AmE), / ˈdɒlmən / (BrE)
A close-fitting, short jacket with decorative braiding, traditionally worn over the shoulders by hussars.

Examples

Military dolman jacket
Antique military uniforms often include the distinctive dolman worn by cavalrymen.
He draped his dolman over his shoulders before mounting his horse.
The officer's dolman was richly decorated with gold braid and frogging.
