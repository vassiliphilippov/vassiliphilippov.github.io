prefabrication

noun
factory-made building sections for assembly
7.4 4.9
Pronunciation: /priː.fæ.brɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /priː.fə.brɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /priː.fə.brɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /priː.fæ.brɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
