old maid

noun
loser in the card game Old Maid
5.9
Pronunciation: / oʊld meɪd / (AmE), / əʊld meɪd / (BrE)
a card game where players try to avoid being left with the unmatched card, or the player who loses by holding that card

The rules of old maid are simple enough for young kids to learn quickly.
