chisel

noun
tool with a flat cutting blade
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪzəl / (BrE)
A tool with a flat steel blade and a sharp cutting edge, used for shaping wood, stone, or metal.

Examples

A wood chisel
Every sculptor has a favorite chisel for detailed work.
She carefully tapped the chisel with a mallet to create precise lines in the stone.
The carpenter picked up his chisel to shape the edges of the table.

Related Terms
