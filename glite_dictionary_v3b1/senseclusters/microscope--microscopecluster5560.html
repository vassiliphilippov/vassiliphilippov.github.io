microscope

noun
optical instrument for magnifying small objects
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪ.krəˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˈmʌɪ.krə.skəʊp / (BrE)
an optical instrument that makes small objects appear larger, allowing them to be observed in detail

Examples

A microscope lens
She purchased a digital microscope for her biology experiments.
The scientist used a microscope to examine the bacteria.
With a powerful microscope, even tiny cells become clearly visible.
