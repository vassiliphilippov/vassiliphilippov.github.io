from

preposition
indicating exclusion
Pronunciation: / frʌm / (AmE), / frɒm / (BrE)
used to show that something is excluded, prevented, or kept away from another thing or place

Examples

Safe from danger
The fence protects the garden from wild animals at night.
She kept her phone from getting wet by using a waterproof case.
Please keep children from touching the hot stove.

Related Terms

  • synonym: away - at a distance from a place or person
  • antonym: to - indicating movement toward a place or person
