cryptorchid

adjective
undescended testicles
9.9
Pronunciation: / krɪpˈtɔrkɪd / (AmE), / krɪpˈtɔːkɪd / (BrE)
having one or both testicles undescended into the scrotum; affected by cryptorchidism

Examples

Cryptorchid puppy
The veterinarian diagnosed the puppy as cryptorchid after a routine examination.
A cryptorchid condition can require surgical intervention to prevent future health problems.
Farmers often check young animals to see if any are cryptorchid before breeding.

Related Terms

  • antonym: normal - having both testicles properly descended
