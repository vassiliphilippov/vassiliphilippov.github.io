insurgence

noun
organized rebellion against government
6.3 6.9
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈsɜːr.dʒəns/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈsɜː.dʒəns/ (BrE)
an organized rebellion against an established government, often involving armed conflict and subversion

Examples

An insurgence against government
Reports of insurgence have led the government to declare a state of emergency in the affected areas.
The country's unstable political situation made it vulnerable to insurgence from various factions.
The military quickly suppressed the insurgence before it could spread to neighboring regions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insurgency - an active revolt or uprising, especially a prolonged one
  • synonym: rebellion - open resistance to authority, especially government
  • degree higher: revolution - a forcible overthrow of a government or social order
