insurgence

noun
organized rebellion against government
8.7 0.07/m
Pronunciation: / …™nÀàs…ùÀê.d í…ôns / (AmE), / …™nÀàs…úÀê.d í…ôns / (BrE)
an organized rebellion against an established government, often involving armed conflict and subversion

Examples

An insurgence against government
Reports of insurgence have led the government to declare a state of emergency in the affected areas.
The country's unstable political situation made it vulnerable to insurgence from various factions.
The military quickly suppressed the insurgence before it could spread to neighboring regions.

Synonyms

  • insurgency
    8.4 6.36/m
    insurgency is an organized armed campaign that uses subversion and long-term fighting, while insurgence is an organized rebellion against a government.
  • insurrection
    9.1 2.28/m
    insurrection is a planned attempt to overthrow the government, often violent and short, while insurgence is an organized rebellion.
  • rebellion
    4.1 7.73/m
    rebellion is organized armed resistance against authority aiming to overthrow power, while insurgence tends to involve subversion and armed conflict.
  • uprising
    5.1 11/m
    uprising is a sudden act of resistance by people, often short and local, while insurgence names an organized armed rebellion.
