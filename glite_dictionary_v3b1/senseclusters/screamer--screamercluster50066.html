screamer

noun
a very hard hit ball (in sports)
Pronunciation: / ˈskriːmɚ / (AmE), / ˈskriːmə / (BrE)
a ball that is struck very hard, especially in sports such as baseball or cricket

Examples

A fast screamer
He dove to stop the screamer, but the ball was just too fast.
The batter hit a screamer down the left-field line for a double.
The crowd gasped as the striker fired a screamer from outside the box.
