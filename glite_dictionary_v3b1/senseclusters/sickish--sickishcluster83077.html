sickish

adjective
feeling mildly sick or nauseated
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪkɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɪkɪʃ / (BrE)
Feeling somewhat sick, especially experiencing mild nausea or the urge to vomit.

Examples

Feeling sickish after eating
Eating that undercooked fish made him feel a bit sickish all afternoon.
I woke up this morning with a sickish feeling in my stomach.
She started to feel sickish after the long car ride.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nauseous - feeling or causing a feeling of sickness in the stomach
  • synonym: queasy - feeling sick, especially with a slight nausea
