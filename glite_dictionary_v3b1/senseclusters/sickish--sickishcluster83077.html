Feeling somewhat sick, especially experiencing mild nausea or the urge to vomit.

Examples

Feeling sickish after eating
Eating that undercooked fish made him feel a bit sickish all afternoon.
I woke up this morning with a sickish feeling in my stomach.
She started to feel sickish after the long car ride.

Synonyms

  • queasy
    4.1 1.44/m
    queasy is a light, uneasy stomach feeling often linked to motion or food, while sickish means mildly ill and nauseous.
  • nauseated
    4.5 0.83/m
    nauseated describes feeling as if you will vomit, while sickish means feeling only a mild sickness or slight urge to vomit.
  • nauseous
    4.4 1.72/m
    nauseous often means causing nausea for others, while sickish means feeling a mild physical sickness and wanting to vomit.
  • qualmish
    9.9 0.00/m
    qualmish feels uneasy and easily disgusted by sights or ideas, whereas sickish refers to a mild physical nausea or queasiness.
