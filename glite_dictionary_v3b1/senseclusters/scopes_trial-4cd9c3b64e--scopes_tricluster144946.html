Scopes trial

noun
1925 US trial over teaching evolution in schools
8.4
Pronunciation: / skoʊps traɪəl / (AmE), / skəʊps traɪəl / (BrE)
the highly publicized 1925 American court case in which John T. Scopes was tried for teaching evolution in violation of Tennessee law
