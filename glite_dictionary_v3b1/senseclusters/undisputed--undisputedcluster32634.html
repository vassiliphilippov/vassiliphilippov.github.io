undisputed

adjective
not questioned or doubted
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈspjutɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈspjuːtɪd / (BrE)
accepted by everyone; not questioned or doubted.

Examples
