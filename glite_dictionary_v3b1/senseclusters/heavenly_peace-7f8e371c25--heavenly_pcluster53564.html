heavenly peace

noun
perfect tranquility or bliss
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛvənli pis / (AmE), / ˈhɛvənli piːs / (BrE)
A state of perfect tranquility, calmness, or bliss, as if in heaven.

Examples

Heavenly peace and calm
After a long day, Sarah found heavenly peace while sitting by the tranquil lake.
Many seek heavenly peace through meditation and quiet reflection.
The gentle music brought a sense of heavenly peace to the entire room.
