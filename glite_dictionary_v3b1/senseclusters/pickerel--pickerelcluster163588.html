pickerel

noun
a small North American pike
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkərəl / (AmE), / ˈpɪkərəl / (BrE)
any of several small, slender pike native to North America, especially of the genera Esox or related genera

Examples

A pickerel swims
A young pickerel darted through the weeds as we paddled by.
Anglers often use live bait to catch pickerel in shallow waters.
The lake is known for its large population of pickerel and bass.
