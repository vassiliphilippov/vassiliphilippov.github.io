mail pouch

noun
bag used to carry mail
Pronunciation: / meɪl paʊtʃ / (AmE), / meɪl paʊtʃ / (BrE)
a large, strong bag used to carry and transport mail, especially in bulk or for delivery services

Examples

A mail pouch
Each mail pouch was sealed before being loaded onto the train.
Security around the mail pouch was strict to prevent theft of important documents.
The postman collected the letters and placed them carefully into the mail pouch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mailbag - a bag used for carrying letters and parcels
