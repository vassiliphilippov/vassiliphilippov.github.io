judger

noun
contest evaluator
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌdʒər / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌdʒə / (BrE)
a person who evaluates and decides the outcome or gives scores in a contest or competition

Examples

Talent show judger
Each judger gave detailed feedback to the contestants after their performances.
The competition required three judgers to ensure fairness in scoring.
As a judger, she had to remain impartial and focus on the criteria.

Related Terms

  • synonym: judge - a person who decides the results in a competition or court
  • antonym: participant - a person who takes part in a competition
  • synonym: referee - an official who supervises a game or contest
