colorfast

adjective
does not fade or run in wash
6.1 3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlərˌfæst / (AmE), / ˈkʌləˌfɑːst / (BrE)
(of fabric or dye) having color that does not fade or run when washed or exposed to light.

Examples

Colorfast fabric
Always check that curtains are colorfast before cleaning them with water.
The colorfast fabric kept its vibrant look even after many summers in the sun.
These jeans are made from colorfast denim that won't lose its shade in the wash.
