ornithosis

noun
infectious disease from birds; atypical pneumonia
9.3 9.9
Pronunciation: /ɔːr.nɪ.ˈθoʊ.sɪs/ /; / /ɔːr.nə.ˈθoʊ.sɪs/ (AmE), /ɔː.nɪ.ˈθəʊ.sɪs/ /; / /ɔː.nə.ˈθəʊ.sɪs/ (BrE)
an infectious disease, similar to atypical pneumonia, caused by certain bacteria and transmitted to humans from infected birds

Examples

Ornithosis infection
After handling sick pigeons, he developed a severe case of ornithosis.
Proper protective equipment is essential to prevent ornithosis in people working with birds.
Veterinarians warned about an outbreak of ornithosis among local poultry workers.
