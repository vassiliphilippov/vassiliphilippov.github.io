sweater

noun
knitted garment for the upper body
Pronunciation: / ˈswɛt̬ər / (AmE), / ˈswɛt.ə / (BrE)
A warm knitted or crocheted garment worn on the upper part of the body, usually with long sleeves and no opening at the front.

Examples

A wool sweater
He tied his sweater around his waist when it got too warm outside.
My grandmother knitted me a blue sweater for my birthday.
She pulled on her favorite sweater before heading out into the chilly morning.
