shape

noun
visual appearance or outline
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪp / (AmE), / ʃeɪp / (BrE)
the visible form, outline, or appearance of something or someone

Examples

You could just make out the shape of a boat in the fog.

The shape of the cloud resembled a horse galloping across the sky.
