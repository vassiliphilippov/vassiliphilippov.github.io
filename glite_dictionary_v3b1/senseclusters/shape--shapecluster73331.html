Synonyms

    1.3 90/m
    form is the visible structure or arrangement of something, while shape refers to the object's outer look or general outline.
  • contour
    4.5 6.65/m
    contour is the curved outline and arrangement of features, while shape means the general visible form or appearance of something.
  • outline
    2.7 7.29/m
    outline is the outer line that marks an object, while shape is the whole visible form or overall appearance of the object.
