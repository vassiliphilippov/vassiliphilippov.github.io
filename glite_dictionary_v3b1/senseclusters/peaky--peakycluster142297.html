peaky

adjective
pale and unwell
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːki / (AmE), / ˈpiːki / (BrE)
looking pale and unwell, as if sick or tired

The nurse noticed the child appeared peaky and suggested a check-up.
You seem a bit peaky today; are you feeling alright?
