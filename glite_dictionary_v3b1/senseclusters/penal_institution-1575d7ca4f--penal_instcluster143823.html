penal institution

noun
facility for confining offenders
6.3 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàpiÀên…ôl Àå…™nst…ôÀàtuÀê É…ôn / (AmE), / ÀàpiÀên…ôl Àå…™nst…™ÀàtjuÀê É…ôn / (BrE)
A place where people are held as punishment for crimes and to protect society, such as a prison or jail.

Examples

A penal institution
After his conviction, he was transferred to a high-security penal institution for fifteen years.
Research shows that rehabilitation programs in a penal institution can reduce rates of reoffending.
The government announced reforms to improve conditions in the penal institution system.

Synonyms

  • penal facility
    6.0 0.00/m
    a penal facility is a specific building where offenders are confined for punishment and public safety, while a penal institution is a broader term.
  • correctional institution
    4.9 0.11/m
    a correctional institution is a government-run place holding people for crimes or trial, while a penal institution focuses on punishment and public protection.
  • correctional facility
    4.2 0.46/m
    a correctional facility is a set of buildings focused on rehabilitating offenders, while a penal institution highlights confinement and punishment.
