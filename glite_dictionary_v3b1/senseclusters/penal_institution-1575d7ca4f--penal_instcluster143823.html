penal institution

noun
facility for confining offenders
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːnəl ˌɪnstəˈtuːʃən / (AmE), / ˈpiːnəl ˌɪnstɪˈtjuːʃən / (BrE)
A place where people are held as punishment for crimes and to protect society, such as a prison or jail.

Examples

A penal institution
After his conviction, he was transferred to a high-security penal institution for fifteen years.
Research shows that rehabilitation programs in a penal institution can reduce rates of reoffending.
The government announced reforms to improve conditions in the penal institution system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jail - a place for the confinement of people accused or convicted of a crime
  • synonym: prison - a building where people are kept as punishment for a crime or while waiting for trial
