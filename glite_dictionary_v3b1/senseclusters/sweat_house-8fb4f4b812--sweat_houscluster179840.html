sweat house

noun
steam bath hut
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈswɛt haʊs / (AmE), / ˈswɛt haʊs / (BrE)
a small building or hut used for steam baths or sweating rituals, often for health or spiritual purification

Examples

A sweat house ritual
The group entered the sweat house to begin the traditional purification ceremony.
After hours in the sweat house, they felt refreshed and spiritually cleansed.
The ancient community built a sweat house near the river for healing rituals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sauna - a room or building used for dry or wet heat sessions
  • synonym: steam bath - a bath in steam for cleansing and relaxation
