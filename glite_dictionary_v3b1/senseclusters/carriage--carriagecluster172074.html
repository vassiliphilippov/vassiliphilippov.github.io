carriage

noun
horse-drawn passenger vehicle
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛrɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkærɪdʒ / (BrE)
A vehicle with wheels, usually pulled by one or more horses, used for carrying people.

Examples

Horse-drawn carriage
Children gathered on the sidewalk to watch the old-fashioned carriage pass by.
The queen rode through the streets in a splendid carriage pulled by four white horses.
They hired a horse-drawn carriage for their wedding day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coach - a large, closed, four-wheeled horse-drawn carriage
  • synonym: wagon - a vehicle with four wheels, used for transporting goods or passengers
