ski

noun
long runner for gliding on snow
Pronunciation: / ski / (AmE), / ski / (BrE)
each of a pair of long, narrow pieces of wood, metal, or plastic worn on the feet for gliding over snow.

Examples

A pair of skis
All of her ski equipment fit into the back of the truck.
He strapped on his skis and headed for the slopes.
The ski is designed to glide smoothly over powdery snow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: snowboard - a flat board used for gliding on snow, ridden sideways
