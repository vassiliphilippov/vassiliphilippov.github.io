endangerment

noun
state of being at risk
5.4
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdeɪndʒərmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈdeɪndʒəmənt / (BrE)
the state of being at risk of harm, loss, or extinction

Examples

Animal endangerment
The rapid deforestation has led to the endangerment of several animal species in the region.
Laws are in place to prevent the endangerment of children in unsafe environments.
Pollution is a major cause of the endangerment of marine life worldwide.

Related Terms

  • antonym: safety - the condition of being protected from harm
  • synonym: threat - a situation that could cause harm or danger
