penman

noun
male journalist or writer (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛn.mən /; / ˈpɛnˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈpɛn.mən / (BrE)
a person who writes, especially a professional writer, journalist, or someone skilled in handwriting or calligraphy
