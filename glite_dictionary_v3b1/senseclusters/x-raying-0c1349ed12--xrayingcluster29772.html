X-raying

noun
obtaining images using X-rays
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɛks ˌreɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛks ˌreɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of obtaining images of objects or body parts by using X-rays

Examples

Dental X-raying
Advances in X-raying have improved the detection of dental problems.
Airport security relies on X-raying luggage to find prohibited items.
The doctor recommended X-raying my chest to check for pneumonia.

Related Terms

  • antonym: MRI - magnetic resonance imaging, a different imaging technique
  • synonym: radiography - the process or technique of making X-ray photographs
