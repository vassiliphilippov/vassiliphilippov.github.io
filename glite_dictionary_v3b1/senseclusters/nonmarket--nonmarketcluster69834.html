nonmarket

adjective
not involving commerce
6.1
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈmɑːr.kɪt/ /; / /ˈnɑːn.mɑːr.kɪt/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈmɑː.kɪt/ /; / /ˈnɒn.mɑː.kɪt/ (BrE)

Examples

Nonmarket activities
Volunteering is considered a nonmarket activity because it does not involve payment.
Many household tasks, such as cooking for family, are classified as nonmarket work.
Government-provided services like public education are examples of nonmarket goods.
