nonmarket

adjective
not involving commerce
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈmɑrkɪt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
not related to buying or selling goods or services in a market

Examples

Nonmarket activities
Volunteering is considered a nonmarket activity because it does not involve payment.
Many household tasks, such as cooking for family, are classified as nonmarket work.
Government-provided services like public education are examples of nonmarket goods.

Related Terms

  • antonym: commercial - related to buying and selling in a market
  • antonym: economic - relating to the production, distribution, and use of income, wealth, and goods
