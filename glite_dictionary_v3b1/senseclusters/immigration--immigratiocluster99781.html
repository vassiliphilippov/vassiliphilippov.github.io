immigration

noun
body or process of people coming to live in a foreign country
2.7 2.6
Pronunciation: /ɪ.mə.ˈgreɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ɪ.mɪ.ˈgreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɪ.mɪ.ˈgreɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ɪ.mə.ˈgreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of coming to live permanently in a country that is not your own, or the people who do this

Examples
