traveler

noun
a person who travels
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtrævələr / (AmE), / ˈtrævələ / (BrE)
a person who is traveling or who often travels from place to place

Examples

A traveler on a journey
A lone traveler walked quietly along the empty road at dawn.
As a frequent traveler, she has visited over thirty countries.
The traveler packed his bags and set off for South America.
As a frequent traveller, she keeps a packed suitcase ready at all times.
Experienced travellers always research their destinations before leaving home.
The traveller arrived at the station just in time to catch the last train.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commuter - a person who travels regularly between home and work
  • antonym: local - a person native or resident to a particular place, not a traveler
  • synonym: tourist - a person who is traveling or visiting a place for pleasure
