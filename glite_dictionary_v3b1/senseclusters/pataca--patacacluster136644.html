pataca

noun
currency unit of Macao
Pronunciation: / pəˈtɑkə / (AmE), / pəˈtækə / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Macao, equal to 100 avos.

Examples

Macao pataca notes
She paid the taxi fare in pataca notes.
The souvenir costs 50 patacas, which is about six US dollars.
Travelers can exchange their currency for patacas at the airport upon arrival in Macau.
