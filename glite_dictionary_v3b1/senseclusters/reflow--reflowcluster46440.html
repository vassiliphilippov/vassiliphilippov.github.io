reflow

verb
to melt solder again
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈriː.floʊ/ /; / /rə.ˈfloʊ/ (AmE), /ˈriː.fləʊ/ /; / /rə.ˈfləʊ/ (BrE)
to melt solder again by heating, especially to form or repair joints on electronic components

Examples

To reflow solder
The technician will reflow the solder to fix the broken connection on the motherboard.
If the screen flickers, you might need to reflow the graphics chip to restore proper contact.
They used a heat gun to reflow the joints on the old circuit board.
