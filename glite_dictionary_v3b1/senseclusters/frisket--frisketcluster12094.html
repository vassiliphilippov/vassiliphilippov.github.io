frisket

noun
protective masking sheet
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfrɪs.kət/ /; / /ˈfrɪs.kɪt/ (AmE), /ˈfrɪs.kət/ /; / /ˈfrɪs.kɪt/ (BrE)
a thin sheet or mask used to cover and protect parts of a surface during painting or printing

Examples

A frisket sheet
The artist placed a frisket over the paper to keep certain areas white while painting.
After printing, she removed the frisket to reveal the untouched sections of the design.
Using a frisket helps achieve clean edges in watercolor artwork.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stencil - a sheet with cut-out designs used to apply a pattern
