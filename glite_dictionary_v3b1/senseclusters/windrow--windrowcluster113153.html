windrow

noun
row of drying crops
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪnˌdroʊ / (AmE), / ˈwɪndrəʊ / (BrE)
a long line or pile of cut crops, grass, or other material (such as hay, snow, or leaves) left in a field to dry or for later collection
