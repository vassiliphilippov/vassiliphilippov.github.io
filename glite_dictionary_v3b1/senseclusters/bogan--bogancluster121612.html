bogan

noun
unsophisticated person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbəʊɡən / (BrE)
an unsophisticated or uncultured person, especially in Australia, often associated with working-class style or behavior

Examples

A bogan in a flannel shirt
Some people unfairly label others as bogans based on their clothing or accent.
He laughed and said he was proud to be called a bogan by his city friends.
The movie features a group of bogans who get into all sorts of trouble.

Related Terms

  • antonym: snob - a person who believes they are superior to others, especially in matters of taste or culture
  • synonym: yobbo - a rude or uncultured person, especially in Australia or Britain
