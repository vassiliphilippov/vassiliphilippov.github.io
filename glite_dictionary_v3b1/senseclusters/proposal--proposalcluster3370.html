proposal

noun
an offer of marriage
Pronunciation: / prəˈpoʊzəl / (AmE), / prəˈpəʊzəl / (BrE)
an act of asking someone to marry you

Examples

Marriage proposal
After weeks of nervous anticipation, his proposal was met with tears of joy.
He surprised her with a romantic proposal at the top of the mountain.
She turned down his proposal, choosing to focus on her career instead.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: engagement - the period or agreement following a marriage proposal
  • antonym: rejection - the act of refusing a marriage proposal
