audiometrically

adverb
by measuring hearing
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːdiəˈmɛtrɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɔːdiəˈmɛtrɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to the measurement of hearing ability using sound tests

Examples

Test audiometrically
The doctor assessed the child's hearing audiometrically to determine the extent of the loss.
Hearing aids are often adjusted audiometrically for each patient to ensure the best fit.
The research study measured participants' responses audiometrically before and after treatment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: audiometric - relating to the measurement of hearing ability
