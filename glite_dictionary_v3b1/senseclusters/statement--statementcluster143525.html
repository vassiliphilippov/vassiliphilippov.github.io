statement

noun
the act of expressing something
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪtmənt / (AmE), / ˈsteɪtmənt / (BrE)
a clear or definite expression of something in speech or writing, especially a formal or factual account

Examples

Police statement
Every witness is required to give a statement to the police.
His resignation letter included a clear statement of his reasons for leaving.
She made a powerful statement about the need for change in her country.
Her statement was clear and left no room for misunderstanding.
The company issued a statement denying all the allegations.
The police asked him to make a formal statement about what he saw.
Each statement in his report was carefully verified for accuracy.
She presented a convincing statement to support her case.
The lawyer demanded that every statement made in court be backed by evidence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assertion - a statement declaring something is true
  • synonym: claim - an assertion that something is true, often without providing evidence or proof
  • synonym: declaration - a formal or explicit statement or announcement
  • antonym: denial - a statement refuting or rejecting a claim
  • antonym: question - a sentence worded to elicit information
