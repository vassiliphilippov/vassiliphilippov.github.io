plain

noun
basic knitting stitch
1.0 7.4
Pronunciation: /pleɪn/ (AmE), /pleɪn/ (BrE)
a basic knitting stitch made by inserting the needle through the front of the stitch from the left-hand side

Examples

Plain stitch
She prefers to use plain when making scarves because it's quick and easy.
The teacher demonstrated the plain stitch for the beginners' knitting class.
To start, knit four rows in plain before switching to a pattern stitch.
