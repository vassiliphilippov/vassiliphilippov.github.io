moko

noun
Māori facial tattoo
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈməʊkəʊ / (BrE)
traditional Māori tattooing, especially the intricate facial tattoos that signify identity and status

Examples

Traditional moko tattoo
The chief's moko was a symbol of his status and heritage.
She studied the meaning behind each pattern in the traditional moko designs.
Receiving a moko is considered a significant cultural milestone in Māori society.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tattoo - a permanent design made on the skin with ink
