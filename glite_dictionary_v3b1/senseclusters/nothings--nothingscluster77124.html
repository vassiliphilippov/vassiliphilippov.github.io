nothings

noun
trivial remarks; idle talk
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌθɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈnʌθɪŋz / (BrE)
trivial or inconsequential things said in conversation, especially as expressions of affection or to fill a silence

Examples

Sweet nothings
She whispered sweet nothings into his ear as they danced.
Their daily routine included sharing small nothings over morning coffee.
They exchanged a few nothings before returning to their own thoughts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chitchat - informal light conversation
  • synonym: small talk - polite conversation about unimportant matters
