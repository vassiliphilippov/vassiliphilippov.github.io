chippy

noun
informal carpenter
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪpi / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪpi / (BrE)
an informal term for a carpenter or woodworker, especially one who builds or repairs structures

Examples

A skilled chippy

Everyone in the village knows the local chippy for his excellent craftsmanship.
We hired a skilled chippy to build custom shelves for the living room.
