adiposis

noun
excessive accumulation of body fat
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌædɪˈpoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌadɪˈpəʊsɪs / (BrE)
A medical condition characterized by excessive fatness or the accumulation of fat in the body.

Examples

Excessive adiposis
