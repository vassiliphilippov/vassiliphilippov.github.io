life and soul of the party

noun
most lively and entertaining person at events
2.2
Pronunciation: / laɪf ənd soʊl əv ðə ˈpɑːrti / (AmE), / laɪf ənd səʊl əv ðə ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
a person who is very lively and entertaining at social gatherings, making events enjoyable for others

Examples

Life and soul of the party at celebrations
At every celebration, Jane is the life and soul of the party, always keeping everyone entertained.
He loves to dance and joke around, which makes him the life and soul of the party.
Without her, the gathering just wouldn't have the life and soul of the party it usually does.
