sublimate

adjective
made pure or refined
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌblɪˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈsʌblɪˌmeɪt / (BrE)
(adjective) Purified or refined, especially by a process that removes impurities.

Examples

Sublimate motives
Her motives for charity were of the most sublimate kind, free of ego or personal gain.
The scientist admired the sublimate form of the chemical after several distillations.
True art, in his opinion, is the sublimate product of creative passion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: impure - containing unwanted substances; not pure
  • synonym: pure - not mixed or tainted with any other substance or material
  • synonym: refined - made pure by removing unwanted substances
