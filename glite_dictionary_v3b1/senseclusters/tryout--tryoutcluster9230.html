tryout

noun
a test to assess suitability or skill
4.5 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈtraɪ.aʊt/ (AmE), /ˈtraɪ.aʊt/ (BrE)
a test or audition to assess the suitability, skill, or ability of a performer or participant

Examples

A tryout for team
After a successful tryout, he was offered a part in the band.
She was nervous about her tryout for the lead role in the school play.
The coach watched each player's tryout before selecting the final team.
