ethnobiological

adjective
cultural study of living things
Pronunciation: / ˌɛθnoʊˌbaɪəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɛθnəʊˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study of how different cultures understand, classify, and use living things such as plants and animals

Examples

Ethnobiological study
The museum features an ethnobiological exhibit showing how indigenous groups use local animals.
Her ethnobiological research focuses on traditional knowledge of medicinal plants.
Students learned about ethnobiological practices in different cultures during the seminar.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: biological - relating to biology or living organisms
  • synonym: ethnobotanical - relating to the study of how people use and understand plants
