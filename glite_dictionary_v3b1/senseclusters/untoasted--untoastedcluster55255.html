untoasted

adjective
not browned by heat (bread/food)
2.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtoʊstɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtəʊstɪd / (BrE)
(of bread or similar food) not having been browned by exposure to heat.

Examples

Untoasted bread slice
She prefers her sandwich with untoasted bread for a softer texture.
The untoasted slices were left on the counter for those who dislike crisp bread.
Would you like your bagel toasted or untoasted?
