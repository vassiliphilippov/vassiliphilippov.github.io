annexationist

noun
supporter of annexation
Pronunciation: / əˌnɛkˈseɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / əˌnɛkˈseɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or advocates the addition of territory to a country or state

Examples

An annexationist leader
The annexationist movement gained momentum after the discovery of valuable resources in the neighboring region.
As an outspoken annexationist, she campaigned for the country's borders to be extended.
Debates between the annexationist and his opponents dominated the political scene for months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expansionist - a person who favors expanding a country's territory or influence
  • antonym: separatist - a person who supports separation from a larger political entity
