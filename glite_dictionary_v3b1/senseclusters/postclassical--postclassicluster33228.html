postclassical

adjective
after classical era
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstˈklæsɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈklæsɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the period after the classical era, especially in history, art, or literature

Examples

Postclassical period
The university offers a course on postclassical philosophy and its influence on modern thought.
Many postclassical artworks show a blend of classical techniques and new medieval themes.
Historians debate when the postclassical period truly began in Europe.

Related Terms

  • antonym: classical - relating to the ancient Greek or Roman world
  • synonym: medieval - relating to the Middle Ages, the period after the classical era
