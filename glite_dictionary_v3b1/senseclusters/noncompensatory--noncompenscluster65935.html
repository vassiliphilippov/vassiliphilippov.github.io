noncompensatory

adjective
unpaid; not reimbursed
8.7
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.kəm.ˈpen.sə.tɔːr.i/ /; / /nən.kəm.ˈpen.sə.tɔːr.i/ (AmE), /nɒn.kəm.ˈpen.sə.tə.ri/ /; / /nɒn.kəm.ˈpen.sə.tɔː.ri/ (BrE)
not providing compensation, payment, or reimbursement; unpaid or not remunerated

Examples

Noncompensatory internship
The internship was strictly noncompensatory, so students received experience but no salary or reimbursement.
Many volunteer positions are noncompensatory and offer no financial reward.
The award is noncompensatory, recognizing achievement without any monetary prize.
