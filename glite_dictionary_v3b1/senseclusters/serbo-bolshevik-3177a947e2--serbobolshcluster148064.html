Serbo-Bolshevik

adjective
of Serbian and Bolshevik political connection
Pronunciation: / ˌsɜrboʊ boʊlˈʃɛvɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɜːbəʊ bɒlˈʃɛvɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of connections between Serbian and Bolshevik history, political ideologies, or movements.

Examples

Serbo-Bolshevik ideology
His article explores the rise of Serbo-Bolshevik ideology after World War I.
Many political opponents accused him of holding Serbo-Bolshevik sympathies.
The Serbo-Bolshevik alliance was pivotal during the revolutionary period.
