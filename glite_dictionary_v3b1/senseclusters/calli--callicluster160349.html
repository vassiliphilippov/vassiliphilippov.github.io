calli

noun
plant cell masses
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkælaɪ / (AmE), / ˈkælaɪ / (BrE)
plural of callus: masses of undifferentiated plant cells formed in tissue culture or at a wound site.

Examples

Plant tissue calli
Researchers observed that the calli developed rapidly on the nutrient medium.

Healthy calli are essential for successful plant regeneration in tissue culture.
