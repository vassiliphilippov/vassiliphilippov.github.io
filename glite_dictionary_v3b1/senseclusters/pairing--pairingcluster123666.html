pairing

noun
establishing a wireless device connection

Examples

Bluetooth pairing process
The pairing of my phone with the car's Bluetooth system completed quickly.
Troubleshooting required restarting both devices before the pairing process would begin again.
Many modern printers support NFC to simplify the initial pairing with a smartphone.
