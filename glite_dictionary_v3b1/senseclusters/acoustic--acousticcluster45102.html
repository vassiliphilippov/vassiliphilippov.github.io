acoustic

adjective
relating to sound or acoustics
Pronunciation: / əˈkuːstɪk / (AmE), / əˈkuːstɪk / (BrE)
relating to sound or the science of sound; concerned with acoustics.

Examples

Acoustic properties
Engineers measured the acoustic properties of the new concert hall.
The design of the auditorium focused on improving acoustic performance for the audience.
They used special materials to control the acoustic environment in the recording studio.
