pedestal

noun
position of great esteem or admiration
6.0 5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈped.ə.stəl/ (AmE), /ˈped.ə.stəl/ (BrE)
A position in which someone is greatly admired or regarded as superior, often excessively or unrealistically.

Examples
