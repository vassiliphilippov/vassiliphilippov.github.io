homer

noun
a pigeon trained to return home
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmə / (BrE)
a pigeon that has been trained to return to its home or original location after being released; a homing pigeon

Examples

A racing homer
Each homer wears a tag to identify its owner.
The club breeds homers for long-distance races across the country.
They released the homer miles from its loft, and it returned swiftly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: homing pigeon - a pigeon trained to fly home from a distance
