rodless

adjective
without rods
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːdləs / (AmE), / ˈrɒdləs / (BrE)
lacking a rod or rods; not containing or using a rod as part of its structure or mechanism

Examples

A rodless cylinder
The new rodless cylinder allows for a more compact machine design.
Engineers developed a rodless actuator to fit into tight spaces.
A rodless system reduces the risk of mechanical failure due to fewer moving parts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rod - a straight, thin stick or bar, often used as a support or part of a mechanism
