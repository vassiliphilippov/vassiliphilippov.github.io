jurisdictional

adjective
relating to official legal authority
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɝː.ɪs.dɪkˈʃə.nəl / (AmE), / ˌdʒʊə.rɪs.dɪkˈʃə.nəl / (BrE)
relating to the official power or authority to make legal decisions and judgments within a specific area

Examples

Jurisdictional boundaries
A dispute arose over the jurisdictional boundaries between the two counties.
Changes in law led to new jurisdictional responsibilities for the agency.
The court decided it did not have jurisdictional authority over the case.
