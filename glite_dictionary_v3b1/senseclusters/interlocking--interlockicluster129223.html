interlocking

adjective
closely joined or linked together
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈlɑkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈlɒkɪŋ / (BrE)
(of things) linked or fitted closely together, so that they are difficult to separate; joined as by dovetailing.

Examples

Interlocking pieces
The interlocking parts of the gear prevented it from being taken apart easily.
The puzzle pieces are designed with interlocking shapes to hold them firmly together.
They built a walkway out of interlocking bricks for added stability.

Related Terms

  • synonym: connected - joined or linked together
  • antonym: separate - not joined, being apart
