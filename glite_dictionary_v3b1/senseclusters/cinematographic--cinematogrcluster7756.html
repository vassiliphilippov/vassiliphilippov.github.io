cinematographic

adjective
film-like qualities
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪnəmətoʊˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɪnəməˌtəˈɡræfɪk /; / ˌsɪnəməˈtəʊɡrəfɪk / (BrE)
relating to movies or having qualities typical of movies, such as being visually dramatic or film-like
