Synonyms

  • cinematic
    4.9 4.47/m
    cinematic is the common word for things that look like movies, while cinematographic sounds more formal and technical in style.
  • filmic
    6.6 0.36/m
    filmic describes a style that uses film-like visual drama, while cinematographic focuses on qualities specifically related to movies.
  • filmlike
    4.1 0.01/m
    filmlike suggests a simple resemblance to a movie's look or mood, while cinematographic implies intentional cinematic technique.
  • cinematical
    6.7 0.00/m
    cinematical is an uncommon or older form used for movie-related things, while cinematographic is the standard modern adjective.
