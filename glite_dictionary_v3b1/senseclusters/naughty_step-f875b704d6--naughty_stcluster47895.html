naughty step

noun
place for child discipline (punishment method)
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔti stɛp / (AmE), / ˈnɔːti stɛp / (BrE)
a designated place where a child is sent as a punishment for misbehaving, used as a disciplinary technique

Examples

Sit on the naughty step
After arguing with his sister, Max was put on the naughty step to calm down.
Many parents use the naughty step as a way to teach children about consequences.
The teacher introduced a naughty step in the classroom to manage disruptive behavior.
