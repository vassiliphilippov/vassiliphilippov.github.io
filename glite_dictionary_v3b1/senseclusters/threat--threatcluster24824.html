threat

noun
source of danger or harm
1.0
Pronunciation: / θrɛt / (AmE), / θrɛt / (BrE)
a person or thing that is likely to cause harm, danger, or fear

Examples

A threat to safety
Climate change is now considered the biggest threat to global biodiversity.
The new virus represents a serious threat to public health.
Wildfires are a constant threat in dry, forested areas.
Many saw her as a threat because of her ruthless reputation.
Once he moved away, the neighborhood felt safer without such a threat around.
The new gang leader quickly became a threat to everyone in the area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: danger - the possibility of suffering harm or injury
  • synonym: menace - a person or thing that is likely to cause harm
  • antonym: protection - the action of keeping someone or something safe from harm
  • antonym: protector - a person who protects others from harm or danger
