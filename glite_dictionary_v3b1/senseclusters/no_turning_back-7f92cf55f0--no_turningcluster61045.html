no turning back

noun
irreversible decision or point of no return
Pronunciation: / ˌnoʊ ˌtɝːnɪŋ ˈbæk / (AmE), / ˌnəʊ ˌtɜːnɪŋ ˈbæk / (BrE)
used to indicate that a decision or action cannot be reversed or undone; the point beyond which one must continue on the current course

Examples

There is no turning back
After pressing the launch button, she knew there was no turning back now.
Once they signed the contract, there was no turning back for the company.
We crossed the border, realizing that there was no turning back from our decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: point of no return - the stage at which reversal is no longer possible
