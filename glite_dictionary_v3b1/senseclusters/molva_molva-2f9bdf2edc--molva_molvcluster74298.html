Molva molva

proper noun
a large northern European marine fish (common ling)
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊl.və ˈmoʊl.və / (AmE), / ˈmɒl.və ˈmɒl.və / (BrE)
A species of large, elongated marine fish found in Greenland and northern European waters, often salted and dried as food; also known as the common ling.
