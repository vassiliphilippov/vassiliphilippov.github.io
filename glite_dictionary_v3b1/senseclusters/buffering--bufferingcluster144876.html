buffering

noun
streaming delay
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌfərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbʌfərɪŋ / (BrE)
a temporary pause or delay in streaming or loading as data is stored before playback continues

Examples

Video buffering
The video kept buffering every few minutes, making it hard to watch.
Slow internet speeds often cause buffering when streaming movies online.
She waited patiently while the music was buffering on her phone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lag - a noticeable delay between an action and its effect
  • synonym: loading - the process of data being transferred or prepared for use
