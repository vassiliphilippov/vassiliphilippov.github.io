cuvette

noun
small basin or bowl
9.9
Pronunciation: / kuːˈvɛt / (AmE), / kjuːˈvɛt / (BrE)
a small basin or shallow bowl, especially used for holding or washing liquids; now rare or archaic

Examples

A silver cuvette
The museum displayed a silver cuvette once used for ceremonial washing.
In the old kitchen, a porcelain cuvette sat next to the main sink.
He carefully poured the solution into the cuvette for the experiment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: basin - a wide, open container for holding liquids
  • synonym: bowl - a round, deep dish used for holding food or liquid
