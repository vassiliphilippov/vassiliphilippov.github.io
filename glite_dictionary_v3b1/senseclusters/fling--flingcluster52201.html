fling

verb
to indulge oneself for pleasure
Pronunciation: / flɪŋ / (AmE), / flɪŋ / (BrE)
to indulge oneself, especially in an activity that is unusual or out of character; to do something for enjoyment or pleasure, often impulsively

Examples

To fling oneself into fun
After months of hard work, she decided to fling herself into a weekend of shopping and relaxation.
He rarely spends money on himself but occasionally flings out on fancy dinners.
Sometimes you just need to fling caution to the wind and do something spontaneous.

Related Terms

  • synonym: splurge - to spend money freely or indulge extravagantly
