spoopy costume

noun
playful or silly spooky costume
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈspuːpi ˈkɑːstuːm / (AmE), / ˈspuːpi ˈkɒstjuːm / (BrE)
A humorous or cute costume intended to be mildly spooky, often in a playful or silly way.

Examples

A spoopy costume with eyes
He wore a spoopy costume with googly eyes and fake cobwebs to make everyone laugh.
Her spoopy costume was a hit at the party.
Social media is full of photos of pets in spoopy costumes every Halloween.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: spooky costume - a costume that is frightening or intended to scare
