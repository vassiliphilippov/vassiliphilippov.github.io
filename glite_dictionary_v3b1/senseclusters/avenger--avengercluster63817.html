avenger

noun
person who takes vengeance
Pronunciation: / əˈvɛndʒər / (AmE), / əˈvɛndʒə / (BrE)
a person who seeks to inflict punishment or harm in return for an injury or wrongdoing; one who takes vengeance

Examples

The avenger of honor
He saw himself as an avenger of his family's honor after the betrayal.
History often romanticizes the lone avenger fighting for justice.
In many legends, an avenger rises to punish those who committed great wrongs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forgiver - a person who pardons an offense or offender
  • synonym: revenge - the action of inflicting hurt or harm on someone for an injury or wrong
