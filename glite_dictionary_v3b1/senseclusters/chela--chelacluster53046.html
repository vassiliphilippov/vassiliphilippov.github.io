chela

noun
Hindu disciple or pupil
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪlə / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪlə / (BrE)
A disciple or pupil, especially of a Hindu religious teacher (swami).

Examples

A chela learns
As a chela, she spent years studying the scriptures under her guru's guidance.
Each chela must follow a strict discipline to attain spiritual growth.
The swami was accompanied by his devoted chela throughout his pilgrimage.
