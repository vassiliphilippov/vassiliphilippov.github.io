March 17

noun
date of St. Patrick's Day
1.0
Pronunciation: / mɑrtʃ sɪvənˈtiːn / (AmE), / mɑːtʃ ˈsɛv.ənˌtiːn / (BrE)
The date on which St. Patrick's Day, the feast of the patron saint of Ireland, is celebrated, especially by Irish communities worldwide.

Examples

March 17 holiday
Every year on March 17, people all around the world wear green to celebrate Irish culture.
Many families prepare traditional Irish dishes for dinner on March 17.
The parade for St. Patrick's Day takes place on March 17 in many cities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: St. Patrick's Day - feast day of the patron saint of Ireland, celebrated on March 17
