red eye

noun
beer and tomato cocktail
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛd aɪ / (AmE), / ˈrɛd aɪ / (BrE)
a mixed drink made with beer and tomato juice, sometimes including seasonings or a raw egg

Examples

Order a red eye drink
He ordered a red eye at the bar to try something different from his usual beer.
Some people add a raw egg to their red eye for extra richness.
The bartender mixed a red eye using tomato juice, beer, and a dash of hot sauce.
