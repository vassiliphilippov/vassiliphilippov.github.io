Plumeria

noun
tropical flowering plant genus
9.1
Pronunciation: / pluːˈmɪriə / (AmE), / pluːˈmɪəriə / (BrE)
A genus of tropical American deciduous shrubs and small trees with candelabra-like branches and fragrant white or pink flowers.

Examples

Plumeria fragrant flowers
