rep

noun
exercise repetition
Pronunciation: / rɛp / (AmE), / rɛp / (BrE)
a single complete movement of an exercise, counted as one unit during a workout

Examples

Ten reps
Try to complete twelve reps of each exercise for the best results.
He increased his reps each week to build more strength.
The trainer watched as she finished her last rep with perfect form.
He managed to do fifteen reps of push-ups before taking a break.
Try to increase the number of reps each week to build strength.
The trainer counted each rep aloud to keep everyone motivated.

Related Terms

  • synonym: repetition - the act of doing something again, especially in exercise
  • degree higher: set - a group of repetitions performed without stopping
