bird

noun
badminton projectile made of feathers and cork/rubber
6.5
Pronunciation: / bɝd / (AmE), / bɜːd / (BrE)
A badminton projectile consisting of a small ball of cork or rubber with a crown of feathers, used in play; also called a shuttlecock.

Examples

Hit the bird (badminton)
A feather from the bird broke off during the match, requiring a quick replacement.
He hit the bird so hard it sailed over the net and out of bounds.
Make sure the bird is dry before starting a new game of badminton.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shuttlecock - a projectile used in badminton, made of feathers and cork or rubber
