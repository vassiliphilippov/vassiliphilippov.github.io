unconvincing

adjective
not persuasive; not credible
2.6 4.1
Pronunciation: /ʌn.kən.ˈvɪn.sɪŋ/ /; / /ən.kən.ˈvɪn.sɪŋ/ (AmE), /ʌn.kən.ˈvɪn.sɪŋ/ /; / /ən.kən.ˈvɪn.sɪŋ/ (BrE)
