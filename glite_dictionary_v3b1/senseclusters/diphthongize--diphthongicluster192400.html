diphthongize

verb
change a vowel to a diphthong
9.9 8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈdɪf.θɑːŋ.aɪz/ /; / /ˈdɪf.θɔːŋ.aɪz/ (AmE), /ˈdɪf.θɒŋ.aɪz/ (BrE)
(of a vowel) to change from a single, simple sound (monophthong) to a diphthong; to pronounce as a diphthong.

Examples
