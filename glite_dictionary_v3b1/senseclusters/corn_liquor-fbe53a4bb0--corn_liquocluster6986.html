corn liquor

noun
illicit corn-based whiskey
5.5
Pronunciation: / kɔrn ˈlɪkər / (AmE), / kɔːn ˈlɪkə / (BrE)
illegal whiskey distilled from a mash primarily made of corn, often known as moonshine.

Examples

Barrels of corn liquor
Authorities discovered several barrels of corn liquor hidden in the woods.
Many Southern communities have a long history with corn liquor production.
The old barn was used to distill corn liquor during Prohibition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moonshine - illegally distilled alcohol, especially in rural areas
