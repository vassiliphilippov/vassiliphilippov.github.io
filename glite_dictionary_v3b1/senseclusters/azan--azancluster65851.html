azan

noun
Islamic call to prayer
Pronunciation: / əˈzɑːn / (AmE), / əˈzɑːn / (BrE)
the Islamic call to prayer, announced from a mosque to invite Muslims to perform their prayers

Examples

The azan call
The azan echoed through the city at dawn, signaling the start of the first prayer.
He paused his work when he heard the azan from the nearby mosque.
Tourists were fascinated by the sound of the azan during their visit to Istanbul.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adhan - the Islamic call to prayer, also spelled azan
  • synonym: iqama - the second call to prayer in Islam, given just before the prayer begins
