skimmed milk

noun
milk with the cream removed
5.4 1.3
Pronunciation: /skɪmd mɪlk/ (AmE), /skɪmd mɪlk/ (BrE)
Milk from which the cream has been removed, resulting in a lower fat content.

Examples

Skimmed milk carton
Many people find the taste of skimmed milk to be less creamy than regular milk.
She always buys skimmed milk to reduce her daily fat intake.
The recipe calls for skimmed milk instead of whole milk.
