sealift

verb
to ship by sea
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːlɪft / (AmE), / ˈsiːlɪft / (BrE)
to transport goods, vehicles, or people by ship, especially in large quantities or for military purposes

Examples

To sealift supplies
The military plans to sealift supplies to the remote base before winter arrives.
After the earthquake, emergency aid was sealifted to the affected region.
They decided to sealift construction materials to the island to speed up the project.
