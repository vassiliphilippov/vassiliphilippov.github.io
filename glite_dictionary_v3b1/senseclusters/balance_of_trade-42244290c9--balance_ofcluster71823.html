balance of trade

noun
difference between imports and exports
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbæləns əv treɪd / (AmE), / ˈbæl.əns əv treɪd / (BrE)
the difference in value between a country's imports and exports of goods over a period of time

Examples

The balance of trade deficit
A negative balance of trade can impact the strength of the national currency.
Economists analyze the balance of trade to assess the overall health of an economy.
Rising exports improved the country's balance of trade last year.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: trade deficit - when imports exceed exports
  • degree higher: trade surplus - when exports exceed imports
