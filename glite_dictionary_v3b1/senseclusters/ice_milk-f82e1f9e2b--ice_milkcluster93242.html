ice milk

noun
frozen dessert made from milk
9.9
Pronunciation: / aɪs mɪlk / (AmE), / aɪs mɪlk / (BrE)
a frozen dessert similar to ice cream but made mainly from milk instead of cream

Examples

A bowl of ice milk
On hot summer days, my grandmother would always serve homemade ice milk to cool us down.
Some people prefer ice milk over ice cream because it contains less fat.
The café offers both ice milk and traditional ice cream as frozen treats.
