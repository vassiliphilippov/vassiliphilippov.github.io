carline

noun
thistle-like plant
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɑːˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a thistle-like plant of the genus Carlina, with spiky leaves and a large, often decorative flowerhead

Examples

Carline thistle
The carline grows wild in dry, rocky areas across southern Europe.
Gardeners often use the dried carline flowerhead as a natural decoration.
She identified the plant as a carline by its spiky leaves and large flower.
