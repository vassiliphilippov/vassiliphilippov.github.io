defile

noun
narrow mountain pass or gorge
8.2
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfaɪl / (AmE), / dɪˈfaɪl / (BrE)
a narrow passage or gorge, especially between mountains

Examples

Mountain defile
A river ran through the defile, carving the passage deeper into the mountains over centuries.
Enemy troops had concealed themselves above the narrow defile waiting to ambush the caravan.
The hikers carefully made their way through the steep defile before reaching the valley beyond.
