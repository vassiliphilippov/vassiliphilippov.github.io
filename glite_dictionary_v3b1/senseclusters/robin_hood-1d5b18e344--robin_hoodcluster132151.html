Robin Hood

proper noun
legendary English outlaw and folk hero
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˌrɑːbɪn ˈhʊd / (AmE), / ˌrɒbɪn ˈhʊd / (BrE)
a legendary English outlaw of the 12th century, famous for robbing the rich to help the poor

Examples

Robin Hood legend

The tales of Robin Hood have inspired numerous books, films, and television shows.
