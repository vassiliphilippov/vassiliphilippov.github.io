shitless

adjective, adverb
to the utmost degree; extremely
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪtləs / (AmE), / ˈʃɪtləs / (BrE)
used after verbs to mean extremely or completely; to the utmost degree (informal, vulgar)

Examples

Scared shitless
He was scared shitless before his first skydiving jump.
She laughed shitless at his ridiculous joke.
The children were bored shitless during the long car ride.
The horror movie scared me shitless and I couldn't sleep all night.
He was bored shitless during the long, dull lecture.
When the dog barked suddenly, she jumped and looked shitless with fear.
