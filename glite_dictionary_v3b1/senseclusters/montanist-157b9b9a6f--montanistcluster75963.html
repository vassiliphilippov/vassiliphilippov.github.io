Montanist

adjective
of Montanism
Pronunciation: / ˈmɒntənɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɒntənɪst / (BrE)
relating to Montanism or its followers, especially their prophetic and ecstatic religious practices in early Christianity

Examples

Montanist beliefs
The church's rituals were considered Montanist by mainstream Christian leaders of the time.
Some scholars debate whether the ecstatic visions were truly Montanist in origin.
Her beliefs were influenced by Montanist teachings about prophecy and spiritual gifts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: orthodox - conforming to established or traditional beliefs, especially in religion
  • synonym: prophetic - relating to prophecy or inspired revelation
