Garamond

proper noun
French type designer
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡærəmɒnd / (AmE), / ˈɡærəmɒnd / (BrE)
Claude Garamond (c.1510–1561), a French type designer known for creating influential serif typefaces.

Examples

Garamond the type designer
Many books in the sixteenth century were printed using typefaces designed by Garamond.
Modern graphic designers often study the work of Garamond for inspiration.
The legacy of Garamond continues in the popular serif fonts that bear his name.
