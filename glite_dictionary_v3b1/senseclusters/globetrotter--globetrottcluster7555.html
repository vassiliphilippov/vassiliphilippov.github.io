globetrotter

noun
person who travels widely
Pronunciation: / ˈgloʊbˌtrɑːtər / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊbˌtrɒtə / (BrE)
a person who travels widely and frequently to many different countries or places

Examples

A globetrotter travels
As a globetrotter, he felt most at home in airports and train stations around the world.
She has built a reputation as a globetrotter, visiting over fifty countries in ten years.
The magazine featured the adventures of a famous globetrotter who documents his travels online.

Related Terms

  • synonym: traveler - a person who travels from place to place
