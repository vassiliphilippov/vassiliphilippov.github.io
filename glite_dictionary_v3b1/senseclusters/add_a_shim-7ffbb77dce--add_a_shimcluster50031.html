add a shim

verb
to insert a shim for adjustment
9.1
Pronunciation: / æd ə ʃɪm / (AmE), / æd ə ʃɪm / (BrE)
to insert a thin piece of material into a space in order to make adjustment or fill a gap, especially for leveling or correcting alignment

Examples

To add a shim for leveling
To keep the shelf level, I had to add a shim under one end.
When installing the window, the carpenter added a shim for a perfect fit.
You may need to add a shim if the floor is uneven.
