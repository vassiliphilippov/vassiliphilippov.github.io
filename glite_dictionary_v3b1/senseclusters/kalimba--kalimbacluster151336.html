kalimba

noun
African thumb piano
8.8
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɪmbə / (AmE), / kəˈlɪmbə / (BrE)
an African musical instrument with metal tines that are plucked to produce melodic sounds

Examples

To play the kalimba
She played a gentle melody on the kalimba during the performance.
The musician introduced the audience to the unique sound of the kalimba.
Children enjoyed learning simple songs on the kalimba in music class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mbira - a traditional African instrument with metal keys played by plucking
  • antonym: xylophone - a percussion instrument with wooden bars struck by mallets
