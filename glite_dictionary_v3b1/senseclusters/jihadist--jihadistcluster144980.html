jihadist

noun
Muslim involved in jihad, often militant
Pronunciation: / dʒɪˈhɑdɪst / (AmE), / dʒɪˈhɑːdɪst / (BrE)
A Muslim who participates in or supports jihad, especially in a militant or armed struggle for a religious cause.

Examples

An jihadist fighter
After his arrest, he was found to have connections with an international jihadist network.
The documentary explores the motivations of a former jihadist who has now left extremism behind.
The government took measures to prevent suspected jihadist groups from gaining ground in the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jihad - a holy war waged by Muslims; a spiritual struggle within oneself against sin
