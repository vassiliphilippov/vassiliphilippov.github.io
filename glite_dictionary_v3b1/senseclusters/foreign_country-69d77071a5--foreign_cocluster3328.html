foreign country

noun
a country other than one's own
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔɹən ˈkʌntri / (AmE), / ˈfɒrən ˈkʌntri / (BrE)
A country other than one's own; any nation where a person is not a citizen.

Examples

A foreign country
After moving to a foreign country, she struggled to adapt to the new culture.
He dreams of traveling to every foreign country in the world.
Studying in a foreign country can broaden your perspective.

Related Terms

  • antonym: home country - the country where one is a citizen or permanent resident
