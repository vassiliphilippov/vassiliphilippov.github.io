Doctor of Theology

noun
advanced degree in theology
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑktɚ əv θiˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˈdɒktə əv θɪˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
A doctoral degree awarded for advanced study and research in theology.

Examples

The Doctor of Theology degree
After years of dedicated study, she was finally awarded the Doctor of Theology degree.
He pursued a Doctor of Theology to deepen his understanding of religious texts.
Many seminarians aspire to earn a Doctor of Theology at a prestigious university.
