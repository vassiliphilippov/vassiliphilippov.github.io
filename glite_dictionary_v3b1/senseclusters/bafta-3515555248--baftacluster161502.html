BAFTA

proper noun
UK film and TV academy
Pronunciation: / ˈbæftə / (AmE), / ˈbæftə / (BrE)
the British Academy of Film and Television Arts, a UK organization that promotes and rewards excellence in film, television, and games

Examples

The BAFTA organization
She was thrilled to win a BAFTA for her outstanding performance in the film.
The BAFTA ceremony attracts many famous actors and directors each year.
Receiving a BAFTA is considered a great honor in the British entertainment industry.
