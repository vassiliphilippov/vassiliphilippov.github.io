roach

noun
the stub of a marijuana cigarette
Pronunciation: / roʊtʃ / (AmE), / rəʊtʃ / (BrE)
the small remaining end of a marijuana cigarette after most of it has been smoked

Examples

He saved the roach from his joint to smoke later when he ran out.

There was nothing left but a tiny roach in the ashtray.
