biennium

noun
two-year period
Pronunciation: / baɪˈɛniəm / (AmE), / baɪˈɛniəm / (BrE)
a period of two years

Examples

The biennium period
The government set its budget for the next biennium.
During the last biennium, the company expanded into two new markets.
The committee reviews its goals at the end of each biennium.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biennial - occurring every two years
  • degree higher: triennium - a period of three years
  • degree lower: year - a period of twelve months
