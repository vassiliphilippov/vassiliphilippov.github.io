rube

noun
unsophisticated or gullible person
Pronunciation: / ruːb / (AmE), / ruːb / (BrE)
a person who is unsophisticated or naïve, especially someone from a rural area

Examples

A rube from town
Don't take me for a rube; I know what you're trying to pull.
He felt like a complete rube at the elegant dinner party.
The city folk laughed at the rube who had never seen an escalator before.
Only a rube would be impressed by those cheap tricks.
She felt like a rube in the fancy restaurant, unsure of which fork to use.
The city lawyer thought he could take advantage of a rube like me.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bumpkin - an unsophisticated or socially awkward person from the countryside
  • antonym: sophisticate - a person with much worldly experience and knowledge of fashion and culture
