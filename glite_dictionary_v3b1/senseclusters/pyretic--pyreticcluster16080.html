pyretic

adjective
causing or relating to fever
Pronunciation: / paɪˈrɛtɪk / (AmE), / paɪˈrɛtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or causing fever.

Examples

A pyretic agent
A pyretic agent can trigger an elevated body temperature.
Some infectious diseases have a strongly pyretic effect on the patient.
The doctor noted a pyretic reaction following the vaccination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: febrile - having or showing the symptoms of a fever
