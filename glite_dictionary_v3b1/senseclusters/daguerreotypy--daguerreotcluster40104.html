daguerreotypy

noun
early photographic image
Pronunciation: / dəˈɡɛrəˌtaɪpi / (AmE), / dəˈɡɛərəˌtaɪpi / (BrE)
a photograph or image produced by the daguerreotype process, an early method of photography using a silvered copper plate

Examples

A daguerreotypy portrait
The museum displayed a rare daguerreotypy of the city's main square from 1842.
Experts carefully restored the faded daguerreotypy to reveal the faces of the people in the portrait.
Collectors value each daguerreotypy for its historical significance and unique appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: daguerreotype - an early photographic process using a silvered copper plate
  • synonym: photograph - an image made using a camera
