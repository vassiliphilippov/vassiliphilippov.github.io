jehad

noun
Islamic holy struggle or striving
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈhɑd / (AmE), / dʒɪˈhɑːd / (BrE)
A holy struggle or effort in Islam, often referring to striving for a moral, spiritual, or political objective.

Examples

A jehad for justice
He interpreted his duty as a jehad for justice and equality in his country.
The concept of jehad encompasses both personal spiritual development and defense of the Islamic community.
The leaders called upon their followers to engage in a peaceful jehad against corruption.

  • synonym: jihad - Islamic holy struggle or striving; variant spelling
