Examples

Watermarking digital images
Many photographers use watermarking to protect their images from being copied online.
The company implemented watermarking to ensure their documents could not be easily forged.
Digital watermarking can be either visible or hidden within the file data.
The company uses watermarking to protect its official documents from being copied illegally.
Modern digital images often include watermarking to show ownership and discourage unauthorized use.
Banknotes are made with watermarking to help prevent counterfeiting and ensure authenticity.
