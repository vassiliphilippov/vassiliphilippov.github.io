rotten egg gas

noun
foul-smelling gas (hydrogen sulfide)
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑtən ɛɡ ɡæs / (AmE), / ˈrɒtən ɛɡ ɡas / (BrE)
a toxic gas with a foul odor, especially hydrogen sulfide, commonly associated with the smell of decomposing eggs

Examples

Smell of rotten egg gas
A strong smell of rotten egg gas indicated the presence of decomposing organic material.
The plumber warned that rotten egg gas could leak from faulty pipes.
When the scientist opened the container, a wave of rotten egg gas was released.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hydrogen sulfide - a poisonous gas with a characteristic smell of rotten eggs
