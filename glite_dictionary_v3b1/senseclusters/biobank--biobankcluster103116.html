biobank

noun
biological sample repository
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪoʊˌbæŋk / (AmE), / ˈbaɪəʊˌbæŋk / (BrE)
a place or organized collection where biological samples (such as blood or tissue) are stored for scientific or medical research

Examples

A biobank of samples
The hospital's biobank contains thousands of tissue samples for future research.
Scientists accessed the biobank to study genetic factors in rare diseases.
A new biobank was established to support cancer research across the country.
The hospital stores thousands of blood samples in its biobank for future research.
Scientists used data from the national biobank to study rare genetic diseases.
Access to the biobank requires strict ethical approval and patient consent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: database - an organized collection of data, often digital
  • synonym: laboratory - a place equipped for scientific experiments and research
  • synonym: repository - a place where things are stored and managed
