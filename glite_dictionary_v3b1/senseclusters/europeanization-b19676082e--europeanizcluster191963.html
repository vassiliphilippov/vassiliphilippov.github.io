Europeanization

noun
assimilation into European culture
7.7
Pronunciation: / jʊˌroʊpiənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / jʊəˌrəʊpɪənaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / jʊəˌrəʊpiənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of becoming more European in character, culture, or standards, often by adopting European customs or values

Examples

The Europeanization process
Debates over Europeanization have shaped the policies of several Eastern European countries.
Some critics argue that Europeanization can threaten indigenous cultures and languages.
The 19th century saw the Europeanization of many aspects of life in Turkey.
Debates about the Europeanisation of education have increased in recent years.
Some critics argue that Europeanisation led to the loss of traditional values.
The country's rapid Europeanisation affected its laws and social customs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Europeanization - the US spelling of the same concept
  • synonym: westernization - adoption of Western cultural features
