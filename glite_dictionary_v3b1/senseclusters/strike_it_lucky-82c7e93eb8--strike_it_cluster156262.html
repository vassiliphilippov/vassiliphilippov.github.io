strike it lucky

verb
experience unexpected good luck
Pronunciation: / ˈstraɪk ɪt ˈlʌki / (AmE), / ˈstraɪk ɪt ˈlʌki / (BrE)
to have an unexpected piece of good luck; to experience sudden good fortune without planning or foreseeing it.

Examples

To strike it lucky suddenly
He struck it lucky with a parking space right outside the theater.
She hopes to strike it lucky at the casino this weekend.
We were searching for hours but finally struck it lucky and found the perfect picnic spot.
