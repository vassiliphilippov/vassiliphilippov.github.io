unethically

adverb
in a morally wrong way
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɛθɪkli / (AmE), / ʌnˈɛθɪkəli / (BrE)
In a way that is not morally correct or acceptable.

Examples

To act unethically
He admitted that he had unethically used confidential information for personal gain.
Journalists are expected not to behave unethically in their reporting.
The company acted unethically by misleading its customers about the product's safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unethical - not morally correct or acceptable; lacking moral principles
