Islay

proper noun
Scottish island in the Inner Hebrides
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ.lə / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.lə / (BrE)
an island off the west coast of Scotland, at the southern end of the Inner Hebrides, known for its whisky distilleries and rugged scenery.
