refire

verb
to fire again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈfaɪər /; / ˌriːˈfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈfaɪə / (BrE)
to fire something again (such as a kiln, engine, or object), often to improve, correct, or restart it
