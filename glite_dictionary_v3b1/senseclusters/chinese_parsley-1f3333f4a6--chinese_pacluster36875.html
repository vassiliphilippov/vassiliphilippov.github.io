Chinese parsley

noun
coriander; aromatic herb used in cooking
4.9 2.4
Pronunciation: /tʃaɪ.ˈniːz ˈpɑːr.sliː/ (AmE), /tʃaɪ.ˈniːz ˈpɑːs.liː/ (BrE)
