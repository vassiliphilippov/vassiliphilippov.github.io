cautiously

with care and avoidance of risk

Examples

To cautiously speak
He spoke cautiously about the ongoing investigation, choosing his words carefully.
Investors acted cautiously during the economic downturn, preferring low-risk options.
She cautiously crossed the icy street to avoid slipping.
