order Pedipalpi

noun
former arachnid order; whip scorpions
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər ˌpɛdɪˈpælpaɪ / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ˌpiːdɪˈpalpʌɪ / (BrE)
A former order of arachnids that includes whip scorpions, now generally regarded as part of the order Thelyphonida.

Examples

The order Pedipalpi
Fossils of the order Pedipalpi have helped scientists understand the evolution of arachnids.
Many biologists now classify animals from the order Pedipalpi under the order Thelyphonida.
The order Pedipalpi is better known to most people as whip scorpions or vinegaroons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: order Thelyphonida - Current recognized order containing whip scorpions
  • synonym: whip scorpion - Arachnid with a whip-like tail; member of this group
