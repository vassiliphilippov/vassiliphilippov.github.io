electrocutioner

noun
person who executes using electricity
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktɹəˈkjuːʃənɚ / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktɹəˈkjuːʃənə / (BrE)
a person who executes condemned prisoners using electrical means, especially the electric chair

Examples

The electrocutioner operates chair
As an electrocutioner, his duties were shrouded in secrecy and controversy.
Few people have ever met an electrocutioner in their lifetimes.
The electrocutioner prepared the electric chair for the impending execution.

Related Terms

  • synonym: executioner - a person who carries out a death sentence
