indigo finch

noun
small North American songbird with blue plumage
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndɪˌɡoʊ fɪntʃ / (AmE), / ˈɪndɪɡəʊ fɪntʃ / (BrE)
a small North American songbird with deep blue plumage, also known as the indigo bunting

Examples

The indigo finch sings
During our walk, we spotted an indigo finch singing from the treetop.
Many birdwatchers hope to catch a glimpse of the elusive indigo finch each spring.
The vibrant blue feathers of the indigo finch make it easy to identify in the wild.
