nae

adverb
Scottish 'no' or 'not'
9.9
Pronunciation: / neɪ / (AmE), / neɪ / (BrE)
used in Scottish English to mean 'no' or 'not'.

Examples

nae chance
There's nae chance of rain today, so we can go hiking.
He said he had nae money left after paying the bills.
She has nae idea what happened at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aye - used in Scottish English to mean 'yes'
  • synonym: no - used to give a negative response or indicate absence
  • synonym: not - used to make a word or group of words negative
