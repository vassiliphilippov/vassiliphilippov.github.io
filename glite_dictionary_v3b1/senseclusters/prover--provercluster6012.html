prover

noun
truth demonstrator
7.5 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàpruÀêv…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàpruÀêv…ô / (BrE)
a person or tool that demonstrates or establishes the truth of something, especially in mathematics or logic

Examples

Math prover
The mathematician used a computer prover to check the theorem.
A skilled prover can construct logical arguments to support their claims.
Modern software often includes an automated prover to verify complex proofs.

Synonyms
