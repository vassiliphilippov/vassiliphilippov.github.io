Jasper Johns

proper noun
American pop artist (born 1930)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒæspər ˈdʒɑnz / (AmE), / ˈdʒæspə ˈdʒɒnz / (BrE)
(born 1930) an American artist known for his significant contributions to pop art and for his innovative use of everyday imagery in painting and printmaking.
