pepa

noun
hard fruit seed
the hard seed or pip found inside certain fruits.

Examples

A fruit pepa
She carefully removed the pepa from the peach before eating it.
The orange juice tasted better after he took out every pepa.
Be careful not to bite into a pepa when eating grapes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flesh - the soft edible part of a fruit
  • synonym: pip - a small hard seed in some fruits
  • synonym: stone - the large hard seed inside some fruits
