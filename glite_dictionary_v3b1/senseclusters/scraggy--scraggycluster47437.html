scraggy

adjective
very thin and bony
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈskræɡi / (AmE), / ˈskræɡi / (BrE)
Very thin and bony in appearance; lean and gaunt.

Examples

Scraggy arms
She wore a loose dress to conceal her scraggy frame.
The old tree's scraggy branches reached out against the night sky.
The stray dog looked scraggy after weeks without proper food.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plump - having a pleasantly soft, rounded body
  • synonym: scrawny - unattractively thin and bony
  • synonym: skinny - very thin, especially in a way that is unattractive
