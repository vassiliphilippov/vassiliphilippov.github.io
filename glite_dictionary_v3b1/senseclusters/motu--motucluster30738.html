motu

noun
small tropical islet
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtu / (AmE), / ˈməʊtuː / (BrE)
a small, low island or islet, especially one found in a tropical lagoon

Examples

A tropical motu
The boat anchored near a tiny motu surrounded by clear blue water.
They picnicked on a secluded motu far from the main island.
Palm trees lined the sandy shore of the motu where we swam all afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atoll - a ring-shaped coral reef or island
  • synonym: islet - a very small island
  • antonym: mainland - the principal landmass of a region, as opposed to islands
