sabino

adjective
white-patched coat
Pronunciation: / səˈbiːnoʊ / (AmE), / səˈbiːnəʊ / (BrE)
having or showing a coat pattern with irregular white patches and roaning, especially in horses.

Examples

Sabino horse pattern
The breeder was excited to see a sabino pattern appear in the new foal's coat.
Many horse enthusiasts appreciate the unique look of a sabino horse.
Her favorite mare is a striking sabino with white legs and a speckled body.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pinto - having large patches of white and another color on a horse's coat
  • antonym: solid - having a coat of a single, uniform color
