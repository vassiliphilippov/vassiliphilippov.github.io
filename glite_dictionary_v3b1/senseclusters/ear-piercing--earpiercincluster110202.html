ear-piercing

adjective
extremely loud or shrill sound
1.2
Pronunciation: / ɪr ˈpɪr.sɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪə ˈpɪə.sɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a sound) extremely loud, shrill, or piercing to the ears; jarring or uncomfortable to hear.

Examples

Ear-piercing sound
As the fire alarm blared its ear-piercing warning, we quickly evacuated the building.
Her ear-piercing laughter could be heard from across the street.
The ear-piercing scream echoed through the empty hallway, making everyone wince.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deafening - extremely loud; so loud as to make hearing difficult
  • synonym: shrill - high-pitched and piercing in sound
  • antonym: soft - quiet; gentle in sound
