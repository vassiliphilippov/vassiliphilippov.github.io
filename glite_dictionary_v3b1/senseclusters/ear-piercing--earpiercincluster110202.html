ear-piercing

adjective
extremely loud or shrill sound
1.2 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈɪr.pɪr.sɪŋ/ /; / /ˈiːr.pɪr.sɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈɪə.pɪə.sɪŋ/ /; / /ˈiːə.pɪə.sɪŋ/ (BrE)
