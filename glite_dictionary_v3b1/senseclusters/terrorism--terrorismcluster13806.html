terrorism

noun
unlawful use of violence for political aims
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛr.əˌrɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈter.ə.rɪz.əm / (BrE)
the unlawful use of violence and intimidation, especially against civilians, in the pursuit of political, religious, or ideological aims

Examples

Acts of terrorism
Many innocent lives have been lost to acts of terrorism around the world.
The government pledged to combat terrorism with increased security measures.
The report outlined the global impact of terrorism in the past decade.

Related Terms

  • antonym: counterterrorism - activities to prevent or respond to terrorism
  • synonym: terrorist - a person who uses terrorism
