paperbark

noun
thin papery bark
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪpərˌbɑrk / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpəbɑːk / (BrE)
the thin, papery outer bark of certain trees, especially used as a material for making containers, artwork, or mulch

Examples

Paperbark sheets
The artist used paperbark to create a beautiful and unique painting.
Traditional containers were often made from paperbark because of its flexibility and strength.
After the storm, strips of paperbark littered the ground beneath the trees.
