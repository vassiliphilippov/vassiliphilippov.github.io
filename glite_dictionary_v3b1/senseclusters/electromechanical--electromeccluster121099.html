electromechanical

adjective
relating to electrical-mechanical systems
4.8
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊməˈkænɪkəl / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊməˈkænɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to a device or system in which electrical and mechanical processes are combined or interact

Examples

An electromechanical relay
An electromechanical relay is often used to switch circuits on and off.
Modern washing machines use electromechanical timers to control cycles.
The engineer repaired the electromechanical components of the robot.
