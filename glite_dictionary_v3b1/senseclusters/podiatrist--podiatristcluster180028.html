podiatrist

noun
specialist in foot care
9.9
Pronunciation: / poʊˈdaɪətrɪst / (AmE), / pəˈdaɪətrɪst / (BrE)
a medical specialist who diagnoses and treats conditions of the feet

Examples

Podiatrist treats feet
After injuring his ankle, Michael made an appointment with a podiatrist for treatment.
Many athletes regularly visit a podiatrist to prevent foot-related injuries.
The podiatrist recommended custom orthotics to relieve her foot pain.
