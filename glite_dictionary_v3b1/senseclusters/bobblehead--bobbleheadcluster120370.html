bobblehead

noun
person who agrees blindly
8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑː.bəl.hed/ (AmE), /ˈbɒb.əl.hed/ (BrE)
an informal term for a person who nods repeatedly or agrees with others without independent thought

Examples

A bobblehead at work
He was called a bobblehead because he agreed with everything his boss said.
During the meeting, she acted like a bobblehead, nodding at every suggestion without question.
No one wants to be seen as a bobblehead who never shares their own ideas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: follower - someone who accepts the leadership or ideas of another
  • antonym: independent thinker - a person who forms their own opinions
  • synonym: yes-man - a person who always agrees with someone in authority
