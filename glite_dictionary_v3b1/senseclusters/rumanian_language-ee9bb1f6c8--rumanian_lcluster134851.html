Rumanian language

noun
the language spoken in Romania
Pronunciation: / ruˈmeɪniən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ruːˈmeɪniən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Romance language spoken in Romania and Moldova, an eastern Romance language that is the official language of Romania
