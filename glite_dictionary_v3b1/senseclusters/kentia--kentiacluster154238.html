kentia

noun
decorative palm tree
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛntiə / (AmE), / ˈkɛntiə / (BrE)
a type of palm tree, often grown indoors as a decorative houseplant

Examples

The kentia palm
The kentia is popular for its elegant, arching fronds and ability to grow indoors.
She placed a large kentia in the corner to brighten up her living room.
With proper care, a kentia can thrive for many years as a houseplant.
