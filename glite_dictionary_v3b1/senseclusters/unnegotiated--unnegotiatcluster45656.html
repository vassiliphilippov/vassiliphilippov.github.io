unnegotiated

adjective
not processed or settled
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnnɪˈɡoʊʃieɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnnɪˈɡəʊʃieɪtɪd / (BrE)
not transferred, settled, or processed as a negotiable financial instrument such as a cheque or bill.

Examples

Unnegotiated cheque
The unnegotiated cheque was returned to the sender after thirty days.
Funds from an unnegotiated bill cannot be accessed until it is processed.
The bank keeps records of all unnegotiated financial instruments for security purposes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: negotiated - transferred, settled, or processed as a negotiable instrument
