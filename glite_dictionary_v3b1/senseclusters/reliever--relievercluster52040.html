reliever

noun
person who calms or lessens anxiety
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈliː.vər/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈliː.və/ (BrE)
a person or thing that reduces pain, stress, discomfort, or anxiety

Examples
