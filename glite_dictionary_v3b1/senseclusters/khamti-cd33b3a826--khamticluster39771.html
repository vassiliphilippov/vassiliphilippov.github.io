Khamti

noun
a Tai language spoken in parts of Myanmar
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɑmˈti / (AmE), / kæmˈtiː / (BrE)
A branch of the Tai languages spoken primarily in Kachin State, Putao district, Myitkyina area, and north Sagaing Region of Myanmar.

Examples

Khamti language
Bilingual signage in both Burmese and Khamti can be found in certain villages.
Linguists have studied Khamti to trace the migration of Tai-speaking peoples.
The Khamti language is spoken by a small community in northern Myanmar.
