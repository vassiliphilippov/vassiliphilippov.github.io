oneself

pronoun
reflexive pronoun
Pronunciation: / wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
the reflexive form of 'one', used to refer back to the person speaking or people in general.

Examples

To trust oneself
It is important to believe in oneself when facing challenges.
One should always take care of oneself during stressful times.
Learning to trust oneself is a valuable skill in life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: herself - the reflexive form of 'she'
  • synonym: himself - the reflexive form of 'he'
  • synonym: yourself - the reflexive form of 'you'
