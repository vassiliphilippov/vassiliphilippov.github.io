limbless

adjective
having no limbs
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪmləs / (AmE), / ˈlɪmləs / (BrE)
having no limbs; lacking arms, legs, or similar appendages

Examples

Limbless person
After the accident, he was tragically left limbless and needed constant care.
Certain species of lizards are limbless and resemble snakes.
The illustrated guide showed both limbed and limbless amphibians.
