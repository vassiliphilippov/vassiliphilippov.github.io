dust coat

noun
long protective overcoat
4.6
Pronunciation: / dʌst koʊt / (AmE), / dʌst kəʊt / (BrE)
a loose, long coat, usually reaching to the ankles, worn to protect clothing from dust

Examples

A dust coat over clothes
He wore a dust coat over his suit when driving through the countryside.
In the early 20th century, travelers often donned a dust coat to protect their clothes.
The archaeologist removed his dust coat before entering the museum.

Related Terms
