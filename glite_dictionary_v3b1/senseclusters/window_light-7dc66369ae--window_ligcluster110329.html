window light

noun
pane of glass
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndoʊ laɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɪndəʊ laɪt / (BrE)
a single pane of glass in a window, especially in traditional or older windows

Examples

A broken window light
The old cottage had a broken window light that needed to be replaced.
Each window light was carefully cleaned to let in more sunshine.
The craftsman measured the window light before cutting a new piece of glass.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mullion - a vertical bar between the panes of glass in a window
  • synonym: pane - a sheet of glass in a window or door
