ribby

adjective
having raised ridges
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪbi / (AmE), / ˈrɪbi / (BrE)
having raised ribs or ridges, especially in texture or design

Examples

Ribby fabric texture
The ribby fabric of the scarf made it extra warm for winter days.
He preferred ribby socks because they stayed up better on his legs.
The potter created a ribby vase with decorative ridges around its surface.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ribbed - having a surface marked with raised lines or ridges
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and flat surface without raised parts
