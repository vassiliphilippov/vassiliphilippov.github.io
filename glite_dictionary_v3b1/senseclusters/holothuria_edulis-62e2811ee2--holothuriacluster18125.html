Holothuria edulis

noun
an edible sea cucumber species
9.1
Pronunciation: / hoʊ.ləˈθjʊr.i.ə ˈɛd.jʊ.lɪs / (AmE), / hɒl.əˈθjʊə.ri.ə ˈɛd.jʊ.lɪs / (BrE)
a species of edible sea cucumber found in warm coastal waters from Australia to Asia, often used as food, especially in Chinese cuisine
