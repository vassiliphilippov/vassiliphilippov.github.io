in limbo

adverb
in an uncertain or undecided state
8.4 5.8
Pronunciation: /ɪn ˈlɪm.boʊ/ /; / /ən ˈlɪm.boʊ/ (AmE), /ɪn ˈlɪm.bəʊ/ /; / /ən ˈlɪm.bəʊ/ (BrE)
in an uncertain or undecided state, often awaiting a decision or outcome

Examples

To be in limbo

His visa status is in limbo until the government processes his application.
The negotiations have stalled, leaving the agreement in limbo.
