cut

adjective
made tidy by trimming
5.7
Pronunciation: / kʌt / (AmE), / kʌt / (BrE)
(of hair, grass, etc.) made neat and tidy by being trimmed or shortened with scissors or another sharp tool.

Examples

Cut hair
His cut hair looked neat and professional.
She preferred a cut appearance over a messy one.
The lawn was freshly cut before the guests arrived.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shaggy - (of hair or fur) long and untidy
  • synonym: trimmed - (of hair, grass, etc.) made neat by cutting small amounts off
