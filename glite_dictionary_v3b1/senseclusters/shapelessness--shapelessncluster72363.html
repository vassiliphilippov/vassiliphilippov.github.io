shapelessness

noun
the state of being without a definite shape
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪpləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪplɪsnəs /; / ˈʃeɪplɪsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of not having a clear, definite, or attractive shape; being formless or amorphous
