meadowed

adjective
having meadows
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdoʊd / (AmE), / ˈmɛdəd / (BrE)
covered with meadows or having the appearance of a meadow

Examples

Meadowed hills
The meadowed hills were bright with wildflowers in spring.
They built their cottage on a meadowed slope overlooking the river.
We enjoyed a picnic in the meadowed valley surrounded by tall grass and wildflowers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: barren - lacking vegetation or plant life
  • synonym: grassy - covered with or full of grass
