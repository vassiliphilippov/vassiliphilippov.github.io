troubling

adjective
causing distress or concern
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌblɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtrʌblɪŋ / (BrE)
causing distress, worry, or anxiety; unsettling

Examples

A troubling problem
She found his behavior deeply troubling and decided to speak with him.
The doctor shared some troubling news about his condition.
There has been a troubling increase in violent incidents recently.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comforting - making someone feel less worried or anxious
  • synonym: disturbing - causing anxiety or worry
