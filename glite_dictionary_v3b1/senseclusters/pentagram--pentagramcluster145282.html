pentagram

noun
five-pointed star figure
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛn.təˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈpɛn.tə.ɡræm / (BrE)
A five-pointed star drawn with five straight lines connecting the vertices of a pentagon, often enclosing another pentagon; used as a symbol in art, geometry, and religion.

Examples

A pentagram symbol
Many flags and emblems feature a pentagram as a central symbol.
She recognized the geometric structure as a pentagram inside a circle.
The magician drew a pentagram on the floor for the ritual.

Related Terms

  • synonym: star polygon - a polygon in a star shape, e.g., pentagram, hexagram
