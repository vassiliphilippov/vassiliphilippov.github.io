Gogol

proper noun
Russian writer; pioneer of realism
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊɡəl / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊɡɒl / (BrE)
(1809–1852) A Russian writer renowned for introducing realism to Russian literature, noted for works such as 'Dead Souls' and 'The Overcoat'.
