medium

adjective
meat cooked with a little pink inside
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːdiəm / (AmE), / ˈmiːdiəm / (BrE)
(of meat or steak) cooked so that it is still slightly pink inside, but not rare or well done.

Examples

Medium steak
I’d like my steak cooked medium, please.
She prefers her burgers medium rather than rare.
The lamb was perfectly medium—tender and juicy with a hint of pink.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: rare - (of meat) cooked very lightly, mostly red inside
  • degree higher: well done - (of meat) cooked thoroughly with no pink inside
