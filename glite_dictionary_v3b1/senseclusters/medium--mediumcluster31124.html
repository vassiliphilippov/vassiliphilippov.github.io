medium

adjective
meat cooked with a little pink inside
4.2 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈmiː.dɪ.əm/ /; / /ˈmiː.di.əm/ (AmE), /ˈmiː.dɪ.əm/ /; / /ˈmiː.di.əm/ (BrE)
(of meat or steak) cooked so that it is still slightly pink inside, but not rare or well done.
