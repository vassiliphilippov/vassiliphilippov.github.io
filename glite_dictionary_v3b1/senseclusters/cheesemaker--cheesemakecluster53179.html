cheesemaker

noun
cheese-making machine
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːzˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːzˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a machine or appliance used to make cheese, especially in homes or on a small scale

Examples

Home cheesemaker
She bought a cheesemaker to experiment with making different types of cheese at home.
The new cheesemaker makes it easy to prepare fresh mozzarella in just a few hours.
With a compact cheesemaker, even beginners can try making soft cheeses in their own kitchen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheese press - a device used to press curds when making cheese
  • synonym: yogurt maker - a machine used to make yogurt at home
