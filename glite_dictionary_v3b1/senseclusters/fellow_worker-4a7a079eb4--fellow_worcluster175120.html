fellow worker

noun
a colleague or coworker
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛloʊ ˈwɜrkər / (AmE), / ˈfeləʊ ˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person who works with you in the same organization or job; a colleague

Examples

Fellow workers support
Having supportive fellow workers can make any job more enjoyable.
She collaborated closely with her fellow worker on the new project.
The company provides an open space for every fellow worker to share ideas.
