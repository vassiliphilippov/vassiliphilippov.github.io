triumphantly

adverb
in a victorious or proud manner
Pronunciation: / traɪˈʌmfəntli / (AmE), / traɪˈʌmfəntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows great happiness or pride, especially as a result of success.

Examples

He triumphantly announced
He triumphantly announced that the company had exceeded its targets.
She raised the trophy triumphantly above her head as the crowd cheered.
The team returned triumphantly after winning the national championship.
