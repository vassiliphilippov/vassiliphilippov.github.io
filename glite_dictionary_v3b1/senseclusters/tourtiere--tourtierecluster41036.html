tourtiere

noun
traditional Quebec meat pie
Pronunciation: / tʊrˈtjɛr / (AmE), / tʊəˈtjɛə / (BrE)
A traditional Canadian meat pie, typically made with minced pork or other meats and eaten especially at Christmas in Quebec.

Examples

A tourtiere pie
Many families in Quebec have their own secret recipe for homemade tourtiere.
My grandmother prepares a delicious tourtiere every Christmas Eve for our family dinner.
The bakery down the street sells a hearty tourtiere filled with spiced pork and potatoes.
