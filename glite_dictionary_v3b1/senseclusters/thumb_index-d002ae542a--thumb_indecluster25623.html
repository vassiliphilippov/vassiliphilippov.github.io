thumb index

noun
notch in book edge for section access
Pronunciation: / θʌm ˈɪndɛks / (AmE), / θʌm ˈɪndɛks / (BrE)
one of a series of rounded notches cut into the edge of a book, allowing quick access to sections or chapters

Examples

A thumb index in books
Adding a thumb index made the manual much more user-friendly.
She prefers dictionaries with a thumb index for easier navigation.
The reference book has a thumb index so you can quickly find each chapter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tab - a small projection used to mark or divide sections in books or files
