unvigilant

adjective
not alert or watchful
7.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈvɪdʒələnt / (AmE), / ʌnˈvɪdʒɪlənt / (BrE)
not alert or watchful; failing to be vigilant or attentive to potential danger or problems.

Examples

An unvigilant attitude
Her unvigilant attitude allowed several mistakes to go unnoticed.
It is dangerous for drivers to be unvigilant when approaching a busy intersection.
The security guard was dismissed for being unvigilant during his shift.

Related Terms

  • antonym: vigilant - keeping careful watch for possible danger or difficulties
