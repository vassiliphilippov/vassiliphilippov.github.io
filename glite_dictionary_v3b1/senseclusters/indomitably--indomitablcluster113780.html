indomitably

adverb
with unyielding spirit
8.7
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɑːmɪtəbli / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɒmɪtəbli / (BrE)
in a manner that cannot be subdued, defeated, or overcome

Examples

To indomitably continue
Despite many setbacks, she continued indomitably toward her goal.
The team fought indomitably until the final whistle, refusing to surrender.
He faced the harsh criticism indomitably, never letting it shake his confidence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bravely - in a courageous or fearless manner
  • synonym: resolutely - in a determined and unwavering way
  • antonym: timidly - in a shy or lacking confidence manner
