downpipe

noun
car exhaust section
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊnˌpaɪp / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnˌpaɪp / (BrE)
a section of a vehicle's exhaust system that directs exhaust gases from the exhaust manifold or turbocharger toward the catalytic converter or the rest of the exhaust system

Examples

Car exhaust downpipe
The mechanic installed a new downpipe to help the engine run more efficiently.
A damaged downpipe can cause exhaust leaks and reduce vehicle performance.
Upgrading the downpipe is a common modification for car enthusiasts seeking more power.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exhaust pipe - a pipe that carries exhaust gases from an engine out of a vehicle
  • degree higher: tailpipe - the end section of a vehicle's exhaust system where gases exit
