lesser butterfly orchid

noun
a south European orchid species
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛsər ˈbʌtərflaɪ ˈɔrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɛsə ˈbʌtəflaɪ ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
