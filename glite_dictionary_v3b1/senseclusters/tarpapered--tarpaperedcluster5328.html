tarpapered

adjective
covered with tar paper
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːrˌpeɪpərd / (AmE), / ˈtɑːˌpeɪpəd / (BrE)
covered or roofed with tar paper, a waterproof material used in building construction

Examples

Tarpapered roof
The small cabin was tarpapered to protect it from heavy rain and snow.
They lived in a tarpapered shack at the edge of the field.
After the storm, the workers quickly tarpapered the damaged roof to prevent leaks.
