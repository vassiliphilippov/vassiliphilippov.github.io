noncognizance

noun
lack of awareness
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈkɑɡnɪzəns / (AmE), / nɒnˈkɒɡnɪzəns / (BrE)
the state of not being aware of or not recognizing something

Examples

Noncognizance of rules
His noncognizance of the new policy caused confusion at the meeting.
The manager's noncognizance of the team's efforts led to frustration.
Due to her noncognizance of the warning signs, the problem grew worse.

Related Terms

  • antonym: awareness - knowledge or perception of a situation or fact
  • synonym: ignorance - lack of knowledge or awareness
