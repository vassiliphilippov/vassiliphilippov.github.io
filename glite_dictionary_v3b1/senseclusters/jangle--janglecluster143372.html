jangle

verb
make a harsh metallic ringing sound
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒæŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒæŋɡl̩ / (BrE)
to make a harsh, discordant, ringing sound, typically of metal objects striking each other

Examples

To jangle coins
Coins jangle in his pocket with every step he takes.
Her bracelets jangle whenever she moves her arm.
The wind caused the chimes to jangle noisily on the porch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jingle - to make a light, ringing sound like small bells
