infraction

noun
minor violation of a law or rule
4.4 5.7
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈfræk.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈfræk.ʃən/ (BrE)
a violation or breaking of a rule or law, especially a minor offense less serious than a felony

Examples

A infraction of rules
Any infraction of the school's dress code will be reported to the principal.
Parking in a no-parking zone is usually treated as a minor infraction.
The officer issued a ticket for the traffic infraction.
