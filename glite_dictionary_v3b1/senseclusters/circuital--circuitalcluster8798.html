circuital

adjective
circular movement
Pronunciation: / sɚˈkjuːɪtəl / (AmE), / səˈkjuːɪtəl / (BrE)
moving in or forming a circuit, or taking a circular or circuitous route or motion

Examples

Circuital path
The river takes a circuital path through the valley before reaching the sea.
Their conversation became circuital, returning to the same topics again and again.
The marathon route is circuital, looping around the city center twice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: circular - having the form of a circle or moving in a circle
  • antonym: direct - moving in a straight line without deviation
