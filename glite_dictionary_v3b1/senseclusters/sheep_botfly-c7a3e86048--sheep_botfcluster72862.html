sheep botfly

noun
parasitic fly affecting sheep
8.3
Pronunciation: / ʃip ˈbɑtˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ʃiːp ˈbɒt.flaɪ / (BrE)
A large fly whose larvae are internal parasites of sheep, causing health problems in the host.

Examples

The sheep botfly larvae
Farmers are concerned about outbreaks of sheep botfly because the larvae can cause serious discomfort in flocks.
The sheep botfly lays its eggs near the nostrils of sheep, where the larvae quickly develop.
Veterinarians recommend routine checks to detect infestations of sheep botfly in sheep herds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: botfly - fly whose larvae are parasites of mammals
