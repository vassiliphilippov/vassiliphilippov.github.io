electropneumatic

adjective
using electricity and air
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊnjuˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊnjuːˈmætɪk / (BrE)
relating to systems or devices that use both electricity and compressed air to operate

Examples

An electropneumatic system
The new train uses an electropneumatic braking system for improved safety and efficiency.
Technicians repaired the electropneumatic valve that controls the flow of compressed air.
Many modern factories rely on electropneumatic equipment to automate their production lines.
