woodchopper

noun
person who chops wood
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊdˌtʃɑpər / (AmE), / ˈwʊdˌtʃɒpə / (BrE)
a person who chops wood, either as a job or as a hobby

Examples

The woodchopper chops logs

As a hobby, she became a skilled woodchopper during the cold winter months.

The woodchopper stacked logs neatly by the cabin.
Every morning, the woodchopper sharpened his axe before heading into the forest.
Villagers relied on the woodchopper to provide firewood during the cold winter months.
