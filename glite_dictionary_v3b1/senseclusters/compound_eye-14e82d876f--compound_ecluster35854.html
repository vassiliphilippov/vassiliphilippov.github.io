compound eye

noun
biomimetic multi-lens camera
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmˌpaʊnd aɪ / (AmE), / ˈkɒmpaʊnd aɪ / (BrE)
(in engineering and imaging) a camera or sensor system that imitates an insect compound eye, consisting of an array of microlenses or photoreceptors to provide a wide field of view, improved motion detection, or redundancy.

Examples

Compound eye camera
Engineers fitted the surveillance drone with a compound eye sensor to widen its field of view.
Researchers built a miniature compound eye camera using an array of microlenses and tiny photodiodes.
The conference featured several talks on how compound eyes improve motion detection in robotic vision systems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biomimetic sensor - a sensor designed to imitate biological systems
  • synonym: camera - a device for recording visual images
