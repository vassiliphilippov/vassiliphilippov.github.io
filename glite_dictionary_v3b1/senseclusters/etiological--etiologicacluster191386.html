etiological

adjective
relating to the study of causation
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌiːtiəˈlɑdʒɪkəl /; / ˌiːtiəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌiːtɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
