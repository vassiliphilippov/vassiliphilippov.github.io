etiological

adjective
relating to the study of causation
9.8 0.43/m

relating to the cause or origin of something, including the philosophical study of causation, and often used in contexts such as medicine or science.

Examples

An etiological approach helps identify the root causes behind observed phenomena.
Her research focused on the etiological factors that contribute to childhood anxiety disorders.
The philosopher published an etiological analysis of the origins of moral responsibility.
An aetiological investigation revealed that contaminated water was responsible for the outbreak.

The study provided an aetiological account of the disease's spread.
