spinal column

noun
series of bones in the backbone
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪnəl ˈkɑləm /; / ˈspaɪ.nəl ˈkɑː.ləm / (AmE), / ˈspaɪnəl ˈkɒləm /; / ˈspaɪ.nəl ˈkɒl.əm / (BrE)
the series of bones (vertebrae) running from the skull to the lower back, forming the backbone and enclosing the spinal cord
