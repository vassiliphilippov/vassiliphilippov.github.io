imagist

noun
Imagist movement member
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˈɪmədʒɪst / (BrE)
a poet, writer, or artist who is part of or associated with the Imagist movement, which emphasizes clear and vivid imagery
