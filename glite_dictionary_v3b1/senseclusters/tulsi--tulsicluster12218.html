tulsi

noun
herbal tea from holy basil
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtʊlsi / (AmE), / ˈtʊlsi / (BrE)
a herbal tea or infusion made from the leaves of the tulsi (holy basil) plant, often used for its medicinal properties

Examples

Tulsi tea
Many people drink tulsi to help relieve stress and boost their immune system.
She offered her guests a cup of warm tulsi after dinner.
In traditional medicine, tulsi is valued for its soothing and healing properties.
