clave

noun
Latin percussion sticks
Pronunciation: / ˈklɑːveɪ / (AmE), / ˈklɑːveɪ / (BrE)
a percussion instrument made of two short wooden sticks that are struck together to produce a sharp sound, especially in Latin music
