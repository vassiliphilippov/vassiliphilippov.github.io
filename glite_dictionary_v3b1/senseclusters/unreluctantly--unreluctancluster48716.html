unreluctantly

adverb
without hesitation
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈlʌktəntli / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈlʌktəntli / (BrE)
in a way that shows no hesitation or unwillingness

Examples

To unreluctantly offer help
He unreluctantly offered his seat to the elderly woman on the crowded bus.
They unreluctantly accepted the invitation to join the team for dinner.
The children unreluctantly shared their toys with the new student.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reluctantly - in a way that shows hesitation or unwillingness
  • synonym: willingly - in a way that shows readiness or eagerness
