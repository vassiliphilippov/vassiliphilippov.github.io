flow

noun
steady movement or progression

Examples

Flow of information
She tried to keep up with the flow of the conversation during the lively debate.
The flow of the river quickened after the storm.
Traffic flow was disrupted due to road construction.
She admired the smooth flow of the dancer's movements.
The flow of water from the broken pipe flooded the basement.

Synonyms

  • stream
    1.0 16/m
    a stream is a continuous movement along a path, often narrow or directed, while flow is a broader steady movement or supply.
  • current
    3.2 13/m
    a current is a strong directional movement in water, air, or ideas, while flow is a general steady movement or supply.

Related Terms

  • synonym: current - a flow, especially in figurative senses like current of thought
  • antonym: ebb - the movement of the tide out to sea; decline or decrease
  • synonym: stream - a continuous flow of liquid, air, or gas
