cuckoo clock

noun
clock that chimes like a cuckoo
4.1 2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkʊ.kuː klɑːk/ /; / /ˈkuː.kuː klɑːk/ /; / /ˈkʊ.kuː klɔːk/ (AmE), /ˈkʊ.kuː klɒk/ /; / /ˈkuː.kuː klɒk/ (BrE)
