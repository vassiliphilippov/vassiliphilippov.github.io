circumflex humeral artery

noun
artery supplying the shoulder
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜrkəmˌflɛks hjuˈmɛrəl ˈɑrt̬əri / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkəmˌflɛks hjuːˈmɛrəl ˈɑːtəri / (BrE)
an artery in the shoulder region that supplies blood to the shoulder joint and surrounding muscles

Examples

Circumflex humeral artery blood supply
Damage to the circumflex humeral artery can lead to poor blood supply to the shoulder joint.
The circumflex humeral artery branches from the axillary artery and wraps around the humerus.
The surgeon carefully isolated the circumflex humeral artery before proceeding with the shoulder operation.
