excoriation

noun
skin-picking disorder
Pronunciation: / 5bkccsk54ric8e6a8359n / (AmE), / 6akccsk54d0r6ac8e6a8359n / (BrE)
a psychiatric disorder (also called skin-picking disorder) in which a person repeatedly picks at their skin, causing lesions, distress, or impairment in daily life.

Examples

Excoriation disorder diagnosis
After years of picking, she received a diagnosis of excoriation disorder from the psychiatrist.
His compulsive skin-picking led to visible sores and disrupted sleep, so he sought help for excoriation.
Treatment for excoriation often includes therapy, habit-reversal training, and sometimes medication to reduce urges.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dermatillomania - a clinical name for compulsive skin picking; essentially synonymous with excoriation (skin-picking) disorder
