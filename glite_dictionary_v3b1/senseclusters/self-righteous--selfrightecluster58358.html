self-righteous

adjective
overly moralistic or sanctimonious
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlfˈraɪtʃəs / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˈraɪtʃəs / (BrE)
Having or showing an unfounded or exaggerated sense of moral superiority; arrogantly moralistic or pious.

Examples

A self-righteous attitude
His self-righteous attitude alienated many of his colleagues at work.
She gave a self-righteous lecture on environmental responsibility, ignoring her own frequent flights.
They found his self-righteous comments both annoying and hypocritical.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sanctimonious - making a show of being morally superior to others
