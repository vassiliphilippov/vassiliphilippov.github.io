cremation chamber

noun
furnace for burning bodies to ashes
1.4
Pronunciation: / krɪˈmeɪʃən ˈtʃeɪmbɚ / (AmE), / krɪˈmeɪʃən ˈtʃeɪmbə / (BrE)
a furnace or room where a dead body is burned and reduced to ashes during cremation.

Examples

Modern cremation chambers
Modern cremation chambers are designed with advanced filtration systems to minimize emissions.
The body was placed in the cremation chamber, where it would be reduced to ashes.
Witnesses observed the respectful procedures as the coffin entered the cremation chamber.
