pew

noun
long bench in a church for seating
6.6
Pronunciation: / pju / (AmE), / pjuː / (BrE)
A long bench with a back, placed in rows in a church, for the congregation to sit on.

Examples

Church pew
She knelt quietly in the front pew during the service.
The old wooden pews creaked as the congregation stood for the hymn.
The wedding guests filled every pew in the church.

  • synonym: bench - a long seat for several people
