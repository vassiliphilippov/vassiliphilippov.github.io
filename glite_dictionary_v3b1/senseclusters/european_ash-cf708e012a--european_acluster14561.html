European ash

noun
species of ash tree native to Europe
8.9
Pronunciation: / jʊˈroʊpiən æʃ / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən æʃ / (BrE)
a tall ash tree native to Europe and western Asia, with dark green leaves that are pale and downy beneath

Examples

The European ash tree
Many birds nest in the branches of the European ash.
The European ash is commonly found in woodlands and parks across England.
The leaves of the European ash are easily recognized by their shiny upper surface.
