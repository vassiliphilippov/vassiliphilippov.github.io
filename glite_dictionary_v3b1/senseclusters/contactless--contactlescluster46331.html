contactless

adjective
without physical touch
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːnˌtæktləs / (AmE), / ˈkɒntæktləs / (BrE)
not requiring physical touch, especially for payment, access, or operation

Examples

Contactless payment method
You can use your phone for contactless payments at most supermarkets now.
The hotel room uses a contactless key card system for entry.
Many public transport systems have adopted contactless ticketing to improve hygiene.

Related Terms

  • antonym: manual - requiring physical touch or operation by hand
  • synonym: touchless - not needing to be touched to operate
