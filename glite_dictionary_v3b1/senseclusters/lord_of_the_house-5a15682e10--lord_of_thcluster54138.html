lord of the house

noun
person in charge of a household
2.4
Pronunciation: / lɔrd əv ðə haʊs / (AmE), / lɔːd əv ðə haʊs / (BrE)
the person who has authority over or is considered to be in charge of a household.

Examples

The lord of the house
After his promotion, Tom joked that he was finally the lord of the house.
Growing up, her grandfather was respected as the lord of the house.
Some traditions expect the eldest male to be the lord of the house.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lady of the house - the woman in charge of a household
  • synonym: master of the house - the person with chief authority over a household
