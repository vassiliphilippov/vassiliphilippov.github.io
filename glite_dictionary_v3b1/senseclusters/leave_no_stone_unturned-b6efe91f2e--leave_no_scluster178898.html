leave no stone unturned

verb
search thoroughly and exhaustively
Pronunciation: / liv noʊ stoʊn ʌnˈtɜrnd /; / liv noʊ stoʊn ʌnˈtɝnd / (AmE), / liːv nəʊ stəʊn ʌnˈtɜːnd / (BrE)
to search or investigate everywhere and make every possible effort to achieve something
