frenemy

adjective
friendly but rivalrous
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛnəmi / (AmE), / ˈfrɛnəmi / (BrE)
describing someone who appears friendly but is actually a rival or harbors hidden hostility

Examples

Frenemy behavior
Her frenemy attitude made it hard to know if she was truly supportive.
He received advice from a frenemy who secretly wanted him to fail.
The frenemy relationship between the two coworkers created constant tension in the office.

Related Terms

  • antonym: friendly - kind and pleasant toward others
  • antonym: hostile - unfriendly or antagonistic
