detachedly

adverb
in an impartial way
Pronunciation: / dɪˈtætʃɪdli / (AmE), / dɪˈtætʃɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that is calm, objective, and not emotionally involved

Examples

To listen detachedly
She listened detachedly to the heated debate, not letting her emotions influence her judgment.
The scientist observed the experiment detachedly, recording the results without personal involvement.
He described the events detachedly, as if he had not been affected by them at all.
He listened detachedly to the news, as if it did not concern him.
The judge spoke detachedly, keeping personal feelings out of the decision.
She described the accident detachedly, without showing any sign of distress.

Related Terms

  • antonym: emotionally - in a way that shows strong feelings
  • synonym: impartially - in a fair and unbiased manner
  • synonym: objectively - in a way that is not influenced by personal feelings
