limb

verb
to remove tree branches
7.9
Pronunciation: / lɪm / (AmE), / lɪm / (BrE)
to cut the branches off a tree, especially after it has been felled

Examples

To limb a tree
After the storm, workers began to limb the damaged trees along the road.
It is safer to limb a tree after it has been cut down.
The crew used chainsaws to limb the fallen pine before removing the trunk.
