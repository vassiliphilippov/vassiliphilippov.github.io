infauna

noun
sediment-dwelling animals
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈfɑː.nə/ /; / /ɪn.ˈfɔː.nə/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈfɔː.nə/ (BrE)
animals or small organisms that live within the sediments at the bottom of a sea, river, lake, or other body of water

Examples
