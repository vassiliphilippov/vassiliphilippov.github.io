tip

verb
to incline or bend from upright

to tilt, incline, or overturn something by moving it from its upright position

Examples

To tip the chair
He tipped his head to the side in curiosity.
She carefully tipped the vase to pour out the last few drops of water.
The chair tipped backward when he leaned too far.
Please tip the fan downward if the air feels too strong.
She accidentally tipped the cup and spilled the water.
When you tip the chair forward, it can fall over easily.

Please don't tip that vase; it's very fragile.
The strong wind threatened to tip the tree over.
