bloodedness

noun
purity of lineage
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌdɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈblʌdɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality of belonging to a particular family, race, or lineage, often referring to purity of descent

Examples

Royal bloodedness
The royal family's bloodedness was carefully documented in official records.
Some breeders value the bloodedness of their animals above all else.
He took great pride in the bloodedness of his noble ancestors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ancestry - one's family or ethnic descent
  • antonym: commonness - lack of distinguished family background
  • synonym: lineage - direct descent from an ancestor
