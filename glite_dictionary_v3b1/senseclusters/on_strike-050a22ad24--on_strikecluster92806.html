refusing to work in protest

Examples

Synonyms

  • go on strike
    3.2 0.98/m
    go on strike is a verb phrase meaning workers stop work to protest, while on strike describes the workers' state during that protest.
  • strike
    3.2 7.23/m
    strike is the action of stopping work to press demands, while on strike names the state of workers during that action.
  • strike work
    5.7 0.00/m
    strike work is to stop working to demand better conditions or pay, while on strike describes workers in that state.
  • down tools
    8.1 0.02/m
    down tools means workers stop work quickly as protest, while on strike is the general phrase for being in that state.
  • walk out
    4.1 0.41/m
    walk out is when employees leave the workplace to protest, while on strike usually means they stop working together for demands.
