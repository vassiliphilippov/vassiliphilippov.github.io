misprision

noun
error or misunderstanding
Pronunciation: / mɪsˈprɪʒən / (AmE), / mɪsˈprɪʒən / (BrE)
an error, misunderstanding, or mistaken interpretation, especially in reading or reporting something

Examples

Misprision of facts
The historian's argument was weakened by a misprision of the original document's meaning.
A simple misprision led to the widespread rumor about the event.
He apologized for his misprision after realizing he had misunderstood the instructions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: misinterpretation - an incorrect understanding or explanation
  • synonym: mistake - an action or judgment that is wrong
  • antonym: understanding - the correct interpretation or comprehension
