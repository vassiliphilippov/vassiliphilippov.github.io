opiate

verb
to dull awareness
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈoʊ.pi.eɪt/ /; / /ˈoʊ.piː.ət/ (AmE), /ˈəʊ.pi.eɪt/ /; / /ˈəʊ.piː.ət/ (BrE)
to make someone dull, apathetic, or less aware, as if under the influence of opiates; to stupefy.

Examples

To opiate the mind
The constant background music opiated the customers, making them less aware of their surroundings.
Years of routine work had opiated his enthusiasm for new challenges.
She feared that too much television would opiate the minds of young viewers.
