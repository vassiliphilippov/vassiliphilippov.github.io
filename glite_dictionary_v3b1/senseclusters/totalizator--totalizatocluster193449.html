totalizator

noun
betting machine that calculates and shares winnings
7.2 8.5
Pronunciation: /ˈtoʊ.təl.ɪ.zeɪ.tər/ /; / /ˈtoʊ.tə.laɪ.zə.tər/ (AmE), /ˈtəʊ.təl.ɪ.zeɪ.tə/ /; / /ˈtəʊ.tə.laɪ.zə.tə/ (BrE)
a machine or electronic system that records bets and calculates the total amount wagered, dividing winnings among successful bettors
