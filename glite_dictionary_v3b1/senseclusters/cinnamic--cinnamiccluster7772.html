cinnamic

adjective
like cinnamon
Pronunciation: / sɪˈnæmɪk / (AmE), / sɪˈnæmɪk / (BrE)
resembling or relating to cinnamon, especially in smell or taste.

Examples

Cinnamic aroma scent
The candle released a pleasant cinnamic aroma that filled the entire room.
She preferred teas with a cinnamic flavor, especially during the winter months.
The dessert had a subtle cinnamic taste that reminded him of his grandmother's baking.
