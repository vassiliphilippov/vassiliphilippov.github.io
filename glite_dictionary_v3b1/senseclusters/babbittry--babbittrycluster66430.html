babbittry

noun
middle-class materialism
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbæbɪtri / (AmE), / ˈbæbɪtri / (BrE)
narrow-minded, materialistic attitudes and behavior typical of the conventional middle class

Examples

The babbittry attitude
The novel satirizes the babbittry of small-town businessmen.
Critics accused the politician of promoting babbittry instead of progressive ideas.
Her art challenged the babbittry that dominated the local community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: philistinism - a lack of appreciation for art or culture; materialistic values
