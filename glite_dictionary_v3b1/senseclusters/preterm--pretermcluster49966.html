preterm

noun
premature infant
5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈpriː.tɜːrm/ (AmE), /ˈpriː.tɜːm/ (BrE)
a baby born before the usual or expected time, especially before 37 weeks of pregnancy

Examples

A preterm baby
The hospital has a special unit to care for each preterm born in the region.
Doctors monitor the health of a preterm closely to prevent complications.
Parents of a preterm often receive extra support during the first weeks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: full-term baby - a baby born at or after 37 weeks of pregnancy
  • synonym: premature baby - a baby born before the full term of pregnancy
