nutsy

adjective
silly or crazy
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌtsi / (AmE), / ˈnʌtsi / (BrE)

Examples

Nutsy jokes
Her nutsy sense of humor always cheers up the whole group.
That nutsy plan might just be crazy enough to work.
He wore a nutsy hat covered in colorful feathers to the party.
