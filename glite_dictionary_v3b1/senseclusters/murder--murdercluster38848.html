murder

noun
unlawful intentional killing of a person
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɜrdər / (AmE), / ˈmɜːdə / (BrE)
the unlawful and intentional killing of one human being by another

Examples

A murder case
Investigators are still searching for clues in the murder case.
She wrote a novel about a murder that shocked the small town.
The jury found him guilty of murder after a lengthy trial.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: assassination - the murder of an important or prominent person
  • synonym: homicide - the act of killing another person
  • degree lower: manslaughter - unlawful killing without premeditation
  • antonym: suicide - the act of intentionally killing oneself
