Acoraceae

noun
plant family including Acorus
Pronunciation: / ˌækoʊˈreɪsiː /; / əˌkɔːrəˈsiː.i / (AmE), / ˌakəˈreɪsiː /; / əˌkɔːrəˈsiː.i / (BrE)
a small family or subfamily of flowering plants that includes the genus Acorus, sometimes considered separate from the Araceae family
