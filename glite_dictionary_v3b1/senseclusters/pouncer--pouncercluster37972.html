pouncer

noun
tool for transferring designs
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpaʊn.sər/ (AmE), /ˈpaʊn.sə/ (BrE)
a small tool or pad used to apply pounce powder or to transfer a design through pricked holes onto another surface

Examples

An embroidery pouncer

The artist dabbed the pouncer gently to apply the powder through the tiny holes.
A pouncer is essential for accurately copying intricate designs onto canvas.
