hornbill

noun
large tropical bird with a prominent bill
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrnˌbɪl / (AmE), / ˈhɔːnbɪl / (BrE)
a tropical bird of Africa and Asia with a very large curved bill topped by a bony casque, known for its distinctive appearance

Examples

A hornbill bird
Many cultures revere the hornbill as a symbol of strength and nobility.
The hornbill flew across the forest canopy, its enormous bill catching the sunlight.
We saw a colorful hornbill during our safari in Southeast Asia.
