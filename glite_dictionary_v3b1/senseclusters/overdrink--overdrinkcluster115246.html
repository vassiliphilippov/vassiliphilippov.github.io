overdrink

verb
to drink excessively
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈdrɪŋk / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈdrɪŋk / (BrE)
to drink more than is safe or sensible, especially alcohol or another beverage

Examples

To overdrink at parties
It is easy to overdrink at holiday parties if you are not careful.
He regretted that he had overdrunk the night before and felt unwell all day.
Doctors warn that people who overdrink regularly risk serious health problems.
He tends to overdrink when he is stressed, which worries his friends.
After she overdrank at the celebration, she felt unwell the next day.
It is important not to overdrink at social events to stay healthy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: abstain - to deliberately avoid drinking alcohol
  • synonym: binge - to drink or eat excessively in a short period
