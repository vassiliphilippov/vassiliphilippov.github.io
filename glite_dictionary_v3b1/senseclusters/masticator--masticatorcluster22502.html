masticator

noun
chewer or grinder
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmæstɪˌkeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈmæstɪkeɪtə / (BrE)
a person, animal, or machine that chews or grinds food or other material

Examples

A masticator machine

As a skilled masticator, the cow chewed its cud for hours each day.
Dentists study the muscles of the masticator to understand how we chew our food.
