communalization

noun
transfer to community
9.1
Pronunciation: /kə.mjuː.nə.laɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /kə.mjuː.nə.laɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of transferring private property or services to community ownership or control

Examples

Communalization of land
The communalization of water resources helped ensure fair access for all residents.
After years of debate, the city approved the communalization of local healthcare services.
Many rural areas have benefited from the communalization of agricultural land.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nationalization - the process of bringing private assets under government ownership
  • antonym: privatization - the process of transferring ownership from public or community to private hands
