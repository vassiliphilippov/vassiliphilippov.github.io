Pronunciation: / fər ə ˈreɪni deɪ / (AmE), / fə ə ˈreɪni deɪ / (BrE)
kept or saved for use at a future time when it might be needed, especially in case of difficulty or emergency

Examples

Money saved for a rainy day
He kept his emergency fund untouched for a rainy day.
It's wise to save your bonus for a rainy day.
My grandmother always told me to put some money aside for a rainy day.
