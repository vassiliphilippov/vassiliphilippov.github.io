aboriginality

noun
state of being aboriginal
Pronunciation: / ˌæbəˌrɪdʒəˈnæləti / (AmE), / ˌæbəˌrɪdʒɪˈnælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being aboriginal, especially in relation to indigenous peoples or their culture

Examples

Aboriginality of a people
The museum's new exhibit celebrates the aboriginality of the region's first inhabitants.
Policies that respect aboriginality help preserve traditional languages and customs.
She spoke about the importance of recognizing aboriginality in national identity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indigeneity - the state of being native to a particular place
