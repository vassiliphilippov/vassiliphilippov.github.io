A lawyer who represents and pleads the case of a client in a court of law.

Examples

Courtroom advocate
As an advocate, he has represented several high-profile clients.
She hired an experienced advocate to defend her case in court.
The advocate presented convincing arguments to the jury.

Synonyms

  • pleader
    7.8 0.17/m
    a pleader argues cases in court for clients, while an advocate is the lawyer who represents and pleads a client's case.
  • trial lawyer
    3.6 2.13/m
    a trial lawyer focuses on arguing cases at trials, while an advocate represents and pleads a client's case in court.
  • litigator
    6.8 1.32/m
    a litigator handles lawsuits and court disputes, while an advocate especially pleads the client's case directly in court.
  • counsel
    4.3 25/m
    counsel advises and sometimes represents clients in legal matters, while an advocate mainly pleads a client's case before the court.
  • attorney
    4.6 88/m
    an attorney represents clients in many legal matters, while an advocate focuses on pleading a client's case in court.
