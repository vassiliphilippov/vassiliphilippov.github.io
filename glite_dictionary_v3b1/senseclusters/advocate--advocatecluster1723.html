advocate

noun
lawyer who pleads cases in court
1.1 4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈæd.və.kət/ /; / /ˈæd.və.keɪt/ (AmE), /ˈæd.və.kət/ /; / /ˈæd.və.keɪt/ (BrE)
A lawyer who represents and pleads the case of a client in a court of law.

Examples

Courtroom advocate
As an advocate, he has represented several high-profile clients.
She hired an experienced advocate to defend her case in court.
The advocate presented convincing arguments to the jury.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barrister - a lawyer entitled to represent clients in the higher courts
  • synonym: solicitor - a lawyer advising clients and preparing cases, but not always representing them in court (UK)
