heftily

adverb
substantially; considerably
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛftɪli / (AmE), / ˈhɛftɪli / (BrE)
to a large or considerable degree; substantially or significantly.

Examples

Heftily increased price
The price of the new model was heftily increased compared to last year.
He was heftily fined for breaking the company's safety regulations.
Profits dropped heftily after the unexpected market downturn.

Related Terms

  • antonym: slightly - by a small amount
  • synonym: substantially - to a large or significant extent
