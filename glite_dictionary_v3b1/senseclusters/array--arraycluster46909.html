array

verb
arrange or display in order
Pronunciation: / əˈreɪ / (AmE), / əˈreɪ / (BrE)
to arrange or display things in an orderly, often attractive, manner

Examples

To array the items
She carefully arrayed the flowers in a vase on the table.
The lawyer arrayed the evidence in a logical sequence for the jury.
The storefront windows were beautifully arrayed with holiday decorations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrange - to put things in a neat, attractive, or required order
  • antonym: scatter - to throw or spread things over a wide area in a disorganized way
