toman

noun
Iranian currency unit
Pronunciation: / toʊˈmɑːn / (AmE), / təʊˈmæn / (BrE)
a former unit of currency in Iran, now used informally to refer to ten rials

Examples

One toman note
The vendor asked for one thousand toman for a cup of tea in the bazaar.
Although the rial is the official currency, prices are often quoted in toman in Iran.
He exchanged his dollars for toman before traveling to Tehran.

  • synonym: rial - the official currency of Iran
