user

noun
person who exploits others
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːzər / (AmE), / ˈjuːzə / (BrE)
a person who manipulates or takes advantage of others selfishly or unethically for their own benefit

Examples

A user of people
It's easy to be hurt by a user who pretends to care but only exploits your kindness.
James realized too late that Laura was just a user who wanted him for his connections.
She ended the relationship when she saw he was acting like a user, always taking and never giving.

Related Terms

  • antonym: benefactor - a person who gives help to others
  • synonym: exploiter - a person who uses others for their own benefit
