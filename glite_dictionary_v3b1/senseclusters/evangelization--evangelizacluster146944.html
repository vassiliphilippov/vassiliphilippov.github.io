evangelization

noun
enthusiastic advocacy
7.9
Pronunciation: /ɪ.væn.dʒə.lə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ə.væn.dʒə.lə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɪ.væn.dʒə.lə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ə.væn.dʒə.lə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)

Successful evangelization of the product relied on energetic presentations and community events.
