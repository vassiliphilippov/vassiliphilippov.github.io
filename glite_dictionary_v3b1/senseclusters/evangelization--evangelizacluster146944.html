evangelization

noun
enthusiastic advocacy
Pronunciation: / ɪˌvændʒələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌvændʒəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the enthusiastic promotion or organized advocacy of an idea, product, or cause to gain supporters or users

Examples

Product evangelization
The company's evangelization of its new software helped it quickly gain a large user base.
Her passionate evangelization of environmental causes inspired many people to take action.
Successful evangelization of the product relied on energetic presentations and community events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advocacy - public support for or recommendation of a particular cause or policy
  • antonym: opposition - active resistance or disagreement with an idea or cause
  • synonym: promotion - the act of publicizing or supporting something to increase its popularity
