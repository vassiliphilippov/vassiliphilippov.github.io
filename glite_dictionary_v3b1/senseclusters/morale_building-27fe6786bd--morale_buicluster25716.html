morale building

noun
improving confidence and enthusiasm
Pronunciation: / məˈrɑl ˌbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / məˈrɑːl ˌbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
anything that serves to improve or strengthen the confidence, enthusiasm, or discipline of a person or group

Examples

Morale building activities
Organizing social events can be a key part of morale building in any company.
Recognition from the manager served as effective morale building for the workers.
The weekly team lunch was a great morale building activity for the entire staff.
