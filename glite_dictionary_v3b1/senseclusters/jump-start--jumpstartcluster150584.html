jump-start

verb
start a car with a dead battery using another car
5.8 3.1
Pronunciation: /dʒʌmp.ˈstɑːrt/ (AmE), /dʒʌmp.ˈstɑːt/ (BrE)
to start a car engine with a dead battery by connecting it to another car's battery using jumper cables

Examples

To jump-start a car
He had to jump-start his car after leaving the headlights on all night.
If your battery is dead, I can help you jump-start your vehicle with my cables.
She jump-started her neighbor's car early in the morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boost - to provide extra power to start a car engine, especially when the battery is low
