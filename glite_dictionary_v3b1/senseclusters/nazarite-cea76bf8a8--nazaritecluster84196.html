Nazarite

adjective
strictly abstinent
Pronunciation: / ˈnæzəˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈnæzəraɪt / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a Nazirite or the Nazirite vow, especially marked by strict abstinence or religious dedication

Examples

Nazarite rules
He followed a Nazarite lifestyle, avoiding wine and cutting his hair as a sign of devotion.
The community respected her Nazarite commitment to spiritual purity and self-denial.
His Nazarite practices set him apart from others who did not observe such strict rules.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ascetic - practicing severe self-discipline and abstention
  • antonym: indulgent - allowing oneself to enjoy pleasures freely
