Protestant work ethic

noun
belief in hard work and discipline for moral good
5.1 4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈprɑː.tɪ.stənt wɜːrk ˈeθ.ɪk/ /; / /ˈprɑː.tə.stənt wɜːrk ˈeθ.ɪk/ (AmE), /ˈprɒ.tɪ.stənt wɜːk ˈeθ.ɪk/ /; / /ˈprɒ.tə.stənt wɜːk ˈeθ.ɪk/ (BrE)
