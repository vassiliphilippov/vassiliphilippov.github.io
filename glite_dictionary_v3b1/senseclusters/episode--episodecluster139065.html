episode

noun
distinct event in a series
Pronunciation: / ˈɛpɪˌsoʊd / (AmE), / ˈɛpɪsəʊd / (BrE)
an incident or event that is distinct within a series of related happenings

Examples

A dramatic episode
During her career, each episode of success was followed by a new challenge.
The book describes a tragic episode in the nation's history.
The most exciting episode of the trip was our unexpected hike through the mountains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: event - something that happens, especially something important
  • synonym: incident - an individual occurrence or event
