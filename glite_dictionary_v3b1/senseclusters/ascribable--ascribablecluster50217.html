ascribable

adjective
able to be attributed to something
Pronunciation: / əˈskraɪ.bə.bəl / (AmE), / əˈskraɪ.bə.bəl / (BrE)
able to be assigned or attributed to a particular cause, source, or person.

Examples

Illness ascribable to habits
His illness was ascribable to years of unhealthy habits.
Many of the project's successes are ascribable to effective leadership.
Some errors in the report are ascribable to rushed editing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attributable - able to be regarded as caused by something
