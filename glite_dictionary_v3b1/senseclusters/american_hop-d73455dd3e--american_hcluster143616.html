American hop

noun
North American climbing plant related to hops
7.1
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈmer.ɪ.kən hɑːp/ (AmE), /ə.ˈmer.ɪ.kən hɒp/ (BrE)
A climbing native North American plant of the hemp family, sometimes mistaken for the European hop and used similarly in brewing.

Examples

The American hop plant
Botanists studied the American hop to compare its chemical properties to those of related species.
The American hop is often used by craft brewers for its unique flavor profile.
Unlike the European variety, the American hop thrives in the wilds of the Rocky Mountains.
