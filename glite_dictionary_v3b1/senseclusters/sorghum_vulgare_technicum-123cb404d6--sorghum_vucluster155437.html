Sorghum vulgare technicum

noun
a grass used for making brooms
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔrɡəm vʌlˈɡɛrɛ tɛkˈnɪkəm / (AmE), / ˈsɔːɡəm vʌlˈɡɛːrə tɛkˈnɪkəm / (BrE)
A tall grass cultivated for its long, stiff-branched flower clusters, which are used in making brooms and brushes.
