doodler

noun
casual sketcher
Pronunciation: / ˈduːdlər / (AmE), / ˈduːdlə / (BrE)
a person who draws simple or random pictures, usually while not paying attention or thinking about something else

Examples

The doodler draws pictures
During the lecture, the doodler filled the margins of his notebook with little drawings.
She is known as a talented doodler who creates fun sketches while talking on the phone.
Many meetings have at least one doodler quietly drawing in their notebook.
During long meetings, the doodler filled his notebook with little drawings.
She was known as a doodler because her papers were always covered in sketches.
Every classroom seems to have at least one doodler who draws in the margins.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artist - a person who creates art, especially drawings or paintings
  • synonym: sketcher - a person who makes quick or casual drawings
