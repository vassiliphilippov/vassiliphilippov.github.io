decon

noun
removal of contaminants
7.7 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàdiÀêk…ën / (AmE), / ÀàdiÀêk…ín / (BrE)
the process of removing dangerous substances such as chemicals, biological agents, or radioactive materials from people, equipment, or areas

Examples
