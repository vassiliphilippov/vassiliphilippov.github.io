dilithium

noun
fictional power mineral
Pronunciation: / daɪˈlɪθiəm / (AmE), / daɪˈlɪθiəm / (BrE)
a fictional mineral used as a power source in science fiction, especially for starship engines

Examples

Dilithium crystals
The starship could not travel at warp speed without enough dilithium crystals.
Engineers searched distant planets for rare dilithium to power their advanced technology.
Without dilithium, the crew feared they would be stranded in deep space.
