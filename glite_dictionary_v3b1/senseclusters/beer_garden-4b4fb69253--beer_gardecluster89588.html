beer garden

noun
outdoor area for drinking beer
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪr ˌɡɑrdn̩ / (AmE), / ˈbɪə ˌɡɑːdn̩ / (BrE)
an outdoor area where beer and other drinks are served, typically attached to a pub or tavern

Examples

A sunny beer garden
During summer, the beer garden is always packed with people enjoying the sunshine.
Let's meet at the beer garden after work for some drinks.
They opened a new beer garden in the center of town last month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pub - a place where people go to drink alcoholic beverages, especially beer and ale
