Ural

proper noun
Russian region
Pronunciation: / ˈjʊrəl / (AmE), / ˈjʊərɑːl / (BrE)
the region of Russia surrounding the Ural Mountains, known for its industrial areas and administrative divisions such as the Ural Federal District

Examples

The Ural region
After graduation she accepted a job in the Ural and moved there to work in metallurgy.
The Ural is famous for its mineral resources and heavy industry.
Many rivers in the Ural region flow from the mountains into the surrounding plains.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Moscow - the capital city of Russia, west of the Ural region
  • synonym: Siberia - a vast region in Russia east of the Ural Mountains
