unsanitized

adjective
not cleaned or filtered
6.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsænɪtaɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsænɪtaɪzd / (BrE)
not cleaned, processed, or altered to remove sensitive, private, or harmful information.

Examples

Unsanitized data
The report included unsanitized data that revealed confidential client details.
Sharing unsanitized information online can put people's privacy at risk.
Always check that files are not unsanitized before sending them to external partners.
