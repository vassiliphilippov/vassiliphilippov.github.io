hermaphroditic

adjective
having both male and female reproductive organs
4.4 7.6
Pronunciation: /hər.mæ.frə.ˈdɪ.tɪk/ (AmE), /hə.mæ.frə.ˈdɪ.tɪk/ /; / /hɜː.mæ.frə.ˈdɪ.tɪk/ (BrE)

Examples
