tell

verb
inform confidently or with certainty
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / tɛl / (AmE), / tɛl / (BrE)
to say, inform, or state something to someone; to make something known

Examples

To tell the truth
Can you tell me where the nearest bank is?
He told everyone the news with great confidence.
I tell you, there is no way she made that mistake.
Did you tell anyone that we were leaving early?
Please tell your teacher if you need help with the assignment.
She forgot to tell me about the change in schedule.
Can you tell us your name and where you are from?
He refused to tell anyone about his plans for the future.
Please tell me the truth, even if it is difficult to hear.
