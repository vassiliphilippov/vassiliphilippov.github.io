Examples

To tell the truth
Can you tell me where the nearest bank is?
He told everyone the news with great confidence.
I tell you, there is no way she made that mistake.

Synonyms

  • say
    1.0 4682/m
    say expresses words someone speaks aloud, while tell means giving information to someone with a clear listener and purpose.
  • inform
    1.0 62/m
    inform gives facts formally or officially, while tell is more general and everyday when you make something known to someone.
  • state
    1.6 122/m
    state expresses something clearly or formally in speech or writing, while tell is more informal and used in direct speech to someone.
  • share
    1.0 43/m
    share lets you give thoughts, feelings, or news to others, while tell focuses on making a specific fact known to someone.
  • communicate
    2.3 52/m
    communicate covers sending or exchanging information by speech, writing, or signals, while tell means directly saying something to someone.
