dicamba

noun
synthetic herbicide
Pronunciation: / daɪˈkæmbə / (AmE), / daɪˈkæmbə / (BrE)
a synthetic herbicide used to control broadleaf weeds in agriculture and landscaping

Examples

Dicamba herbicide spray
The farmer sprayed dicamba on his soybean fields to eliminate broadleaf weeds.
Concerns have been raised about the environmental impact of using dicamba near residential areas.
Some crops are genetically modified to tolerate dicamba applications during the growing season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glyphosate - a widely used herbicide for killing weeds
  • synonym: herbicide - a chemical used to destroy unwanted plants
