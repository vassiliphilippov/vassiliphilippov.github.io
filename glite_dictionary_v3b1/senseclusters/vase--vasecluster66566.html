vase

noun
container for flowers or decoration
Pronunciation: / veɪs / (AmE), / vɑːz / (BrE)
A decorative container, often made of glass or porcelain, used for holding cut flowers or as an ornament.

Examples

A vase of flowers
He accidentally knocked over the vase, shattering it into pieces.
She arranged the lilies in a beautiful vase on the dinner table.
The antique vase was handed down through generations in her family.
