tumorigenesis

noun
formation of tumors
Pronunciation: / ˌtuːmərəˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (AmE), / ˌtjuːmərəˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (BrE)
the process by which tumors develop or form in the body

Examples

Tumorigenesis process
Scientists are studying the genetic mutations involved in tumorigenesis.
Certain chemicals have been shown to accelerate tumorigenesis in laboratory animals.
Understanding tumorigenesis is crucial for developing new cancer treatments.
