Examples

An epidural injection during childbirth
After receiving the epidural injection, she felt numbness in her lower body.
Many women choose an epidural injection for pain relief during labor and childbirth.
The doctor recommended an epidural injection to help manage the patient's back pain.

Synonyms

  • epidural anesthesia
    5.0 0.01/m
    epidural anesthesia is the formal medical term used in hospitals and reports, while epidural injection refers to the act of giving the anesthetic.
  • epidural
    5.5 0.61/m
    epidural is the common, short word people use for the procedure and pain relief, while epidural injection names the specific injection.
