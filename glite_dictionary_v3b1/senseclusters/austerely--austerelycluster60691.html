austerely

adverb
in a plain or severe way
Pronunciation: / ɔˈstɪrli / (AmE), / ɔːˈstɪəli / (BrE)
In a way that is plain, simple, or severe; without decoration or luxury.

Examples

To austerely dress
She austerely dressed in simple clothing, avoiding any bright colors or patterns.
The ceremony was austerely conducted, focusing on tradition rather than celebration.
The old monastery was austerely decorated, with only wooden benches and stone walls.

Related Terms

  • synonym: austere - plain and without decoration or luxury
  • antonym: lavishly - in a rich, elaborate, or luxurious way
