supernally

adverb
in a heavenly way
Pronunciation: / suːˈpɜrnəli / (AmE), / suːˈpɜːnəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the heavens or something divine; in a heavenly or sublime manner

Examples

Supernally beautiful voice
The choir sang so supernally that the audience was moved to tears.
Her kindness seemed almost supernally inspired, as if from another world.
The landscape was supernally lit by the rising sun, creating a breathtaking view.

Related Terms

  • synonym: celestially - in a manner relating to the sky or heavens
  • synonym: divinely - in a way that is like or from a god
  • antonym: earthly - in a way relating to the earth or ordinary life
