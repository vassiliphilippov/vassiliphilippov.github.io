cyclocross

noun
bicycle racing on varied terrain
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪkləˌkrɔs / (AmE), / ˈsaɪkləˌkrɒs / (BrE)
a type of bicycle racing held on mixed terrain with obstacles, requiring riders to dismount and carry their bikes at times

Examples

Cyclocross race event

The muddy course made the cyclocross race especially challenging for beginners.
Many riders enjoy cyclocross because it combines speed, skill, and endurance.
