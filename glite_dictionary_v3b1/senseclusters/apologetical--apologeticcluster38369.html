apologetical

adjective
defensive or excusing
Pronunciation: / əˌpɑlədʒɛˈtɪkəl / (AmE), / əˌpɒlədʒɪˈtɪkəl / (BrE)
offering a defense, justification, or excuse to avoid blame or criticism

Examples

Apologetical explanation
His apologetical tone made it clear he was trying to avoid blame for the mistake.
The report was written in an apologetical style, explaining every decision in detail.
She offered an apologetical explanation for her late arrival at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accusatory - suggesting someone has done something wrong
  • synonym: defensive - trying to justify or protect oneself from criticism
  • synonym: justificatory - intended to justify or defend
