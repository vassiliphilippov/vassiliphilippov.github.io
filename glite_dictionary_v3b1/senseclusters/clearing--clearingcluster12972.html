clearing

noun
open area in a forest
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklɪə.rɪŋ / (BrE)
an open area of land with few or no trees, especially in the middle of a forest

Examples

Forest clearing
A cabin stood alone in the center of a small clearing.
The deer emerged cautiously from the trees into the clearing.
We pitched our tent in a sunny clearing surrounded by tall pines.
