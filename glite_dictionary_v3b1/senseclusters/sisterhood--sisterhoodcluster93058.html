sisterhood

noun
religious community of women; nuns' order
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪstɚˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈsɪstəˌhʊd / (BrE)
a society of women, especially nuns, who live together as a religious community

Examples

Catholic sisterhood
Joining the sisterhood meant committing to a life of service and faith.
She devoted her life to the sisterhood and its charitable works.
The sisterhood gathered every morning for prayers and meditation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sorority - a society of women, especially in colleges
