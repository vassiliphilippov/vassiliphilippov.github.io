Anglo-Indian

adjective
relating to British India or its English population
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˌæŋɡloʊˈɪndiən / (AmE), / ˌæŋɡləʊˈɪndiən / (BrE)
relating to British India or to English people living in India, especially during the period of British rule.

Examples

Anglo-Indian culture
He specializes in Anglo-Indian architecture of the nineteenth century.
Her research focuses on Anglo-Indian literature from colonial times.
The Anglo-Indian community played a unique role during the British Raj.
