headend

noun
signal processing facility
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌɛnd / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌɛnd / (BrE)
a facility that receives, processes, and distributes signals in a cable or broadcast system

Examples

Cable headend facility
The technicians upgraded the headend to improve the quality of cable TV services.
All incoming satellite signals are processed at the headend before being sent to subscribers.
A power outage at the headend disrupted television service for the entire city.
