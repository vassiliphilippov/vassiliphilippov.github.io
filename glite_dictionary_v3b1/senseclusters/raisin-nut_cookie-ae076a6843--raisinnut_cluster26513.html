raisin-nut cookie

noun
cookie with raisin and nut filling
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪ.zən nʌt ˈkʊk.i / (AmE), / ˈreɪ.zɪn nʌt ˈkʊk.i / (BrE)
a cookie made with a filling or mixture of raisins and nuts, often forming a paste inside the dough
