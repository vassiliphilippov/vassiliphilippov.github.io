nurser

noun
milk-feeding infant
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈnɜrsər / (AmE), / ˈnɜːsə / (BrE)
an infant or young animal that feeds on its mother's milk by suckling

Examples

Baby nurser

As a nurser, the calf depended entirely on its mother for nutrition.
