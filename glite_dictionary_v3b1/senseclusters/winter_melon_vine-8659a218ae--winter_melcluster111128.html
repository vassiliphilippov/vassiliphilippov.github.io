winter melon vine

noun
climbing plant with smooth white or greenish fruit
6.9 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪn.tər ˈmel.ən vaɪn/ (AmE), /ˈwɪn.tə ˈmel.ən vaɪn/ (BrE)
A climbing plant cultivated for its large, smooth-skinned, white or green-fleshed fruit, lacking a musky aroma, used especially in Asian cuisine.
