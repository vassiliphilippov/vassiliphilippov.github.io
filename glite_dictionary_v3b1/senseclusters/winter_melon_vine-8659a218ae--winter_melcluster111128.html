winter melon vine

noun
climbing plant with smooth white or greenish fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪn.tɚ ˌmɛl.ən vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈwɪn.tə ˌmɛl.ən vaɪn / (BrE)
A climbing plant cultivated for its large, smooth-skinned, white or green-fleshed fruit, lacking a musky aroma, used especially in Asian cuisine.

Examples

The winter melon vine plant
Farmers recommend planting winter melon vine in well-drained soil for optimal growth.
She learned to cook soup using fresh winter melon vine harvested from her backyard.
The winter melon vine sprawled across the garden fence, bearing several large fruits by midsummer.
