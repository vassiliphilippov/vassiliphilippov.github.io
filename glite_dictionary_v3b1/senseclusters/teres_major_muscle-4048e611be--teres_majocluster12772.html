teres major muscle

noun
muscle of the upper back
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛriz ˈmeɪdʒər ˈmʌsəl / (AmE), / ˈtɪəriːz ˈmeɪdʒə ˈmʌsl / (BrE)
a muscle of the upper back that moves the arm and rotates it medially

Examples

Teres major muscle pain
A tear in the teres major muscle can cause pain in the back of the shoulder.
Physical therapists often target the teres major muscle when treating rotator cuff injuries.
The teres major muscle aids in moving the arm toward the body during exercise.
