uprise

verb
rise to one's feet
7.7 6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈʌp.raɪz/ /; / /ə.ˈpraɪz/ (AmE), /ə.ˈpraɪz/ /; / /ˈʌp.raɪz/ (BrE)
to get up or rise to one's feet, especially from sitting or lying down

Examples

People uprise from seats

Each morning in the monastery, the monks would uprise before dawn to begin their prayers.
She uprose quietly, careful not to wake the children still sleeping.
