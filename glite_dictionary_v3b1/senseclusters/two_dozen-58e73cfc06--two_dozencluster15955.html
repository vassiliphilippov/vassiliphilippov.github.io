two dozen

noun
a quantity of twenty-four
Pronunciation: / tuː ˈdʌz.ən / (AmE), / tuː ˈdʌz.ən / (BrE)
A quantity equal to twenty-four; two times twelve, typically used for counting items in groups.

Examples

Two dozen eggs
Her recipe calls for two dozen eggs to make enough cakes for the party.
The florist arranged two dozen roses in a large vase.
We ordered two dozen bagels for the office breakfast.
