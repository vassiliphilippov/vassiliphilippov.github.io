the outside world

noun
the world beyond one’s immediate surroundings
2.7
Pronunciation: / ði ˈaʊtsaɪd wɜrld / (AmE), / ði ˈaʊtsaɪd wɜːld / (BrE)
the world outside one's immediate environment, especially beyond a place of seclusion or isolation

Examples
