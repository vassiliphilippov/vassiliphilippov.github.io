brailler

noun
braille text producer
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪlər / (AmE), / ˈbreɪlə / (BrE)
a person who writes or produces text in braille, especially for visually impaired individuals

Examples

A skilled brailler
The school hired a brailler to create accessible textbooks for blind students.
As a volunteer brailler, she helps make library materials available in braille.
Each brailler must be skilled in both reading and writing braille efficiently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scribe - a person who copies or writes documents by hand
  • synonym: transcriber - a person who converts spoken or written material into another form
