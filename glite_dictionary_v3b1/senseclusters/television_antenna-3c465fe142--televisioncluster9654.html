television antenna

noun
device for receiving TV signals
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛləˈvɪʒən ænˈtɛnə / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˈvɪʒən ænˈtɛnə / (BrE)
An aerial device designed to receive television broadcast signals.

Examples

Adjust the television antenna
He adjusted the television antenna to get a clearer picture on the screen.
Many people replaced their television antenna with cable or satellite services.
The old house still had a television antenna mounted on the roof.
