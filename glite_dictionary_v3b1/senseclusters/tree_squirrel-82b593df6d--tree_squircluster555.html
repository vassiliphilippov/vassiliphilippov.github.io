tree squirrel

noun
squirrel that lives in trees
Pronunciation: / tri ˈskwɝːrəl / (AmE), / tri ˈskwɪrəl / (BrE)
A type of squirrel that lives mainly in trees, as distinguished from ground squirrels and flying squirrels.

Examples

A tree squirrel climbs
A tree squirrel quickly darted up the trunk to escape the approaching dog.
Unlike the ground squirrel, the tree squirrel rarely leaves its arboreal habitat.
We spotted a gray tree squirrel gathering acorns in the park.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flying squirrel - a squirrel adapted for gliding leaps between trees
  • antonym: ground squirrel - a squirrel that lives primarily on the ground
