sour on

verb
become disillusioned with
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊər ɑn / (AmE), / ˈsaʊə ɒn / (BrE)
to become disillusioned with or unfavorable towards someone or something

Examples

To sour on a person
After years of working there, she gradually soured on the company culture.
Investors quickly soured on the project when the early results were poor.
Many fans have soured on the team after a series of disappointing losses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disillusioned - disappointed after realizing something is not as good as it seemed
  • antonym: enthusiastic - showing intense interest or approval
