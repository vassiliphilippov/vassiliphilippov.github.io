pinch of sugar

noun
a small amount of sugar
1.8
Pronunciation: / pɪntʃ əv ˈʃʊɡər / (AmE), / pɪntʃ əv ˈʃʊɡə / (BrE)
a small amount of sugar, typically as much as can be held between the thumb and forefinger

Examples

Sometimes a pinch of sugar can make tomatoes taste less acidic in salads.
The chef sprinkled a pinch of sugar into the sauce to balance the flavors.
