fungo

noun
baseball practice ball
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
a ball that is tossed and hit by a coach or player for fielding practice in baseball

Examples

Fungo practice ball
The coach hit several fungoes to the outfielders during practice.
Each player took turns catching fungo balls to improve their fielding skills.
Before the game, the assistant used a fungo to warm up the infielders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: batting practice - practice in which players hit balls to improve their batting
  • synonym: fielding drill - a practice exercise to improve fielding skills
