discriminately

adverb
with selective judgment
Pronunciation: / dɪˈskrɪmɪnətli / (AmE), / dɪˈskrɪmɪnətli / (BrE)
in a way that shows careful distinction or selective judgment

Examples

Discriminately chosen winners
The scholarship was awarded discriminately to students who demonstrated both need and academic excellence.
Resources were distributed discriminately, ensuring that only the most urgent cases received assistance.
She chose her friends discriminately, valuing honesty and loyalty above all else.

Related Terms

  • antonym: indiscriminately - in a way that does not show careful choice or distinction
  • synonym: selectively - in a way that involves choosing carefully
