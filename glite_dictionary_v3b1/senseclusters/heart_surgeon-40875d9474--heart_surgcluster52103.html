heart surgeon

noun
doctor who performs heart surgery
6.7
Pronunciation: / hɑrt ˈsɝːdʒən / (AmE), / hɑːt ˈsɜːdʒən / (BrE)
A doctor who performs surgical operations on the heart and its associated blood vessels.

Examples

Heart surgeon performs surgery
After his heart attack, he was referred to a renowned heart surgeon for a bypass operation.
Becoming a heart surgeon requires many years of training and a steady hand.
The heart surgeon explained the risks and benefits of open-heart surgery to the family.
