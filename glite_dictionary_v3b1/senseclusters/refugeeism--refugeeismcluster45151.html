refugeeism

noun
state of being a refugee
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛfjuˈdʒiːɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌrɛfjʊˈdʒiːɪzəm / (BrE)
the state or condition of being a refugee, or the movement and experiences of refugees

Examples

The rise in refugeeism
The rise in refugeeism has challenged many countries to rethink their immigration policies.
Her research focuses on the social impacts of refugeeism in Europe over the past decade.
The documentary explores the causes and effects of refugeeism in modern society.

Related Terms

  • synonym: asylum - protection given by a country to someone fleeing danger in their own country
  • synonym: displacement - the forced movement of people from their home or country
