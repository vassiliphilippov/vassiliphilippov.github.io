decider

noun
final deciding match
4.6
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsaɪdər / (AmE), / dɪˈsaɪdə / (BrE)
a game, event, or factor that determines the winner or outcome in a competition or situation

Examples

A final decider match
The championship came down to a tense decider after both teams won two games each.
Fans eagerly awaited the decider to see which side would claim the trophy.
The series was tied, making the next match the crucial decider.
The final game of the series was the decider for the championship title.
Rain turned out to be the unexpected decider in the outdoor concert's cancellation.
As the decider, her vote carried extra weight in the committee's decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: final - the last game or match in a competition
  • synonym: judge - a person who decides the result of a competition or dispute
  • synonym: playoff - an extra game to decide a winner when scores are tied
  • synonym: tiebreaker - an extra round or factor used to decide a winner when scores are equal
