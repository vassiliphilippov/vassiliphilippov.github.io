glissade

verb
to slide down slope
Pronunciation: / ɡlɪˈseɪd / (AmE), / ɡlɪˈsɑːd / (BrE)
to slide smoothly down a slope, especially on snow or ice, in a controlled manner

Examples

To glissade downhill
The children glissaded down the icy hill, laughing all the way to the bottom.
After reaching the summit, the hikers glissaded back to camp on the snow-covered slope.
She expertly glissaded past the trees, enjoying the thrill of the descent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slide - to move smoothly along a surface while maintaining contact
  • antonym: stumble - to trip or lose balance while moving
