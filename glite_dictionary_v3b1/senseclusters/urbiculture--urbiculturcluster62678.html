urbiculture

noun
urban plant cultivation
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrbɪˌkʌltʃər / (AmE), / ˈɜːbɪˌkʌltʃə / (BrE)

Examples

The rise of urbiculture has led to more community gardens in urban areas.
