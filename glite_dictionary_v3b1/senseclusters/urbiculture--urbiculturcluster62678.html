urbiculture

noun
urban plant cultivation
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrbɪˌkʌltʃər / (AmE), / ˈɜːbɪˌkʌltʃə / (BrE)
the practice or study of cultivating plants and green spaces within cities or urban environments

Examples

Urbiculture gardens
Many cities encourage urbiculture to make neighborhoods greener and more sustainable.
Workshops on urbiculture teach residents how to grow vegetables on rooftops and balconies.
The rise of urbiculture has led to more community gardens in urban areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: horticulture - the art or practice of garden cultivation and management
  • synonym: urban gardening - growing plants in city environments
