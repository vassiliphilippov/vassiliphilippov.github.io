taro

noun
tropical starchy root vegetable
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːroʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɑːrəʊ / (BrE)
a tropical plant with large leaves, grown in warm regions for its starchy, edible root, which is used as a vegetable or staple food
