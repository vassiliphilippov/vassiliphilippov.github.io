fishhook

noun
barbed hook for catching fish
3.6 1.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪʃ.hʊk/ (AmE), /ˈfɪʃ.hʊk/ (BrE)
A curved, sharp-pointed hook, often with a barb, used for catching fish.

Examples

A fishhook with barb
Always handle a fishhook carefully to avoid getting pricked by the barb.
He attached a worm to the fishhook before casting his line into the river.
The fishhook snagged on a rock, making it difficult to reel in.
