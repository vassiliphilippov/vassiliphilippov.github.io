MiG

noun
metal inert gas welding
7.0
Pronunciation: / mɪɡ / (AmE), / mɪɡ / (BrE)
a welding process that uses a continuously fed wire electrode and an inert gas to join metals

Examples

MiG welding process
He learned MiG welding at college and now repairs car body panels for a living.
Many beginners start with MiG because it is easier to learn than other welding methods.
The workshop invested in a new MiG machine to improve production efficiency.
