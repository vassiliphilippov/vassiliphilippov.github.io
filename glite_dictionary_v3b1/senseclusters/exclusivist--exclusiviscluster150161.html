exclusivist

noun
person who excludes others
8.8
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈskluːsɪvɪst / (AmE), / ɪkˈskluːsɪvɪst / (BrE)
a person or group that excludes others or believes only their group, doctrine, or system is valid

Examples

An exclusivist group
The private school was criticized for being an exclusivist institution that admitted only wealthy students.
As an exclusivist, he believed only a select group should have access to the club's resources.
The organization moved away from its exclusivist policies to welcome a more diverse membership.
The exclusivist refused to consider any viewpoint outside his own religious tradition.
Critics argue that an exclusivist approach can lead to intolerance and division.
As an exclusivist, she believed her ideology was the only correct one.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elitist - a person who believes that a society or system should be led by an elite
  • antonym: inclusionist - a person who supports including all people or groups
  • antonym: inclusivist - a person who accepts or includes different groups or beliefs
  • synonym: sectarian - a person strongly supporting a particular group or sect
