guayacan

noun
hardwood tropical tree
Pronunciation: / ˌgwaɪəˈkɑːn / (AmE), / ˌgwaɪəˈkæn / (BrE)
a tropical American tree with extremely hard wood, also known as lignum vitae

Examples

Guayacan tree wood
The craftsman carved the statue from guayacan wood.
Many old ship parts were made from the durable guayacan tree.
The dense wood of guayacan is prized for making tools and ornaments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lignum vitae - a tropical hardwood tree known for its very hard wood
