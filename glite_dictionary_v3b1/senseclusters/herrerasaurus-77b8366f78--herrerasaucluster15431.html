Herrerasaurus

noun
primitive Triassic theropod dinosaur genus
Pronunciation: / hɛˌrɛrəˈsɔrəs / (AmE), / hɛˌrɛərəˈsɔːrəs / (BrE)
a primitive genus of theropod dinosaur found in Argentina from the early Triassic period, named after the rancher who discovered it.

Examples

The Herrerasaurus dinosaur
A mounted skeleton of Herrerasaurus is displayed in the national museum.
Scientists believe that Herrerasaurus was one of the earliest meat-eating dinosaurs.
The fossilized bones of Herrerasaurus were unearthed in northwestern Argentina.
