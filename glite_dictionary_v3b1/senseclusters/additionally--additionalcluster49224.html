additionally

adverb
in addition; furthermore
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈdɪʃənəli / (AmE), / əˈdɪʃnəli / (BrE)
used to introduce an extra fact or idea; moreover; in addition.

Examples

Used additionally to add
Additionally, all students must complete a science course before graduating.
He works as a consultant and additionally manages several community projects.
The new heating system is more efficient and additionally saves on electricity costs.
