tolbooth

noun
toll collection booth or gatehouse
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊlˌbuθ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊlˌbuːθ / (BrE)
A booth at a tollgate where a collector receives payment for passage along a road, bridge, or other route.
