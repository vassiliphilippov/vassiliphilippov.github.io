air-freight

verb
transport cargo by aircraft
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌfreɪt / (AmE), / ˈeəˌfreɪt / (BrE)

To air-freight cargo

The company decided to air-freight the medical supplies to ensure their quick arrival.
