Samanid

proper noun
Persian dynasty member
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsæmənɪd / (AmE), / ˈsæmənɪd / (BrE)
a member of the Samanid dynasty or the dynasty itself that ruled parts of Central Asia and Iran in the 9th and 10th centuries
