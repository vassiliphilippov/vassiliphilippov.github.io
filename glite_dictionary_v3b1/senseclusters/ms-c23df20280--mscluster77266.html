Ms

noun
female title, marital status neutral
1.1
Pronunciation: / mɪz / (AmE), / mɪz / (BrE)
a title used before the surname or full name of a woman, regardless of her marital status

Examples

Title Ms Carter
Ms Carter will be leading the meeting this afternoon.
Please address your letter to Ms Thompson in the human resources department.
The teacher prefers to be called Ms instead of Miss or Mrs.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: Miss - title for an unmarried woman
  • degree higher: Mrs - title for a married woman
