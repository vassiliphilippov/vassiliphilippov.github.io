TRT

noun
total running time
Pronunciation: / ti ɑr ti / (AmE), / ti ɑː ti / (BrE)
the total running time or duration of a film, recording, or broadcast

Examples

TRT of a movie
The TRT of the documentary is just under two hours.
Please check the TRT before scheduling the broadcast slot.
The album's TRT includes a hidden bonus track at the end.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duration - the length of time something lasts
  • synonym: length - the measurement of how long something is in time
