gem clip

noun
wire or plastic device for fastening papers
8.8
Pronunciation: / dʒɛm klɪp / (AmE), / dʒɛm klɪp / (BrE)
A device, typically made of wire or plastic, used for holding sheets of paper together; a type of paper clip.

Examples

A gem clip fastens papers
A single gem clip can fasten up to twenty pieces of paper.
Please hand me a gem clip to organize these documents.
She used a gem clip to keep all the receipts together in her folder.
