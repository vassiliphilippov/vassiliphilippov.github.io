growth

noun
process of increasing or developing
2.0 1.4
Pronunciation: /groʊθ/ (AmE), /grəʊθ/ (BrE)
the process of increasing in size, amount, importance, or development; or developing from a simpler to a more complex form

Examples

Economic growth
The city's rapid growth has led to a rise in housing demand.
There has been substantial growth in the number of tech startups recently.
We need to support the healthy growth of small businesses in the community.
Cultural growth often results from the exchange of ideas between societies.
His research focuses on the growth of complex organisms from single cells.
The growth of technology has transformed how we communicate and work.

  • synonym: increase - an instance of becoming larger or more in quantity
