combustibly

adverb
with sudden emotion
Pronunciation: / kəmˈbʌstəbli / (AmE), / kəmˈbʌstəbli / (BrE)
in a way that shows sudden strong emotion or is easily provoked, as if ready to burst out.

Examples

React combustibly
She reacted combustibly to the unexpected news, raising her voice in surprise.
During the debate, he answered combustibly, unable to hide his frustration.
The team responded combustibly to criticism, defending their project with strong emotion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmly - in a peaceful and relaxed way
  • synonym: passionately - in a way that shows strong feelings or emotions
