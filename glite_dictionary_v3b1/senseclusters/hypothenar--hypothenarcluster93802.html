hypothenar

adjective
of palm below little finger
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpoʊˈθiːnɑr / (AmE), / ˌhɪpəʊˈθiːnɑː / (BrE)
relating to the fleshy area of the palm located beneath the little finger.

Examples

Hypothenar muscles
The doctor checked the hypothenar muscles for signs of weakness after the injury.
Pain in the hypothenar region can indicate nerve compression in the hand.
Physical therapists often massage the hypothenar area to relieve tension.
