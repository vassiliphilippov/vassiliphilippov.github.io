noisette

adjective
light brown color
Pronunciation: / nwɑɪˈzɛt / (AmE), / nwaˈzɛt / (BrE)
having a light brown or hazel color, especially used to describe hair, leather, or fabric.

Examples

Noisette hair color
She dyed her hair a beautiful noisette shade for the summer.
The living room was decorated with noisette curtains and matching cushions.
He chose a noisette suit for the wedding to complement the autumn theme.
