fanny pack

noun
small pouch worn around the waist
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfæni pæk / (AmE), / ˈfɒni pæk / (BrE)
A small pouch worn with a strap around the waist, typically with the pouch resting on the lower back or hip, used for carrying personal items.

Examples

A fanny pack strap
His old photos show him sporting a neon fanny pack in the '90s.
She kept her keys and phone in her fanny pack during the festival.
Tourists often wear a fanny pack for convenience and safety while traveling.
