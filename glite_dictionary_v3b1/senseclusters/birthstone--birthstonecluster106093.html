birthstone

noun
gem for birth month
3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈbɜːrθ.stoʊ/ (AmE), /ˈbɜːθ.stəʊ/ (BrE)
a gemstone that is traditionally associated with the month of a person's birth and often worn as jewelry
