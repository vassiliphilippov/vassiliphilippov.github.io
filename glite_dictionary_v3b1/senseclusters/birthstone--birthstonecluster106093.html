birthstone

noun
gem for birth month
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːθstoʊn /; / ˈbɜrθˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθstəʊn /; / ˈbɜːθˌstəʊn / (BrE)
a gemstone that is traditionally associated with the month of a person's birth and often worn as jewelry

Examples
