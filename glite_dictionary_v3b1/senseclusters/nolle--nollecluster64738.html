nolle

noun
withdrawal of charge
9.9 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / Ààn…ëÀêli / (AmE), / Ààn…íli / (BrE)
a formal notice by a prosecutor indicating that a criminal charge will not be pursued further

Examples

A nolle filed
The prosecutor filed a nolle after deciding not to proceed with the case.
When new evidence appeared, the court accepted a nolle from the district attorney.
The defense attorney requested a nolle due to lack of sufficient proof.

Synonyms

  • nolle prosequi
    9.9 0.01/m
    nolle prosequi is a formal legal order recorded in court, while nolle is a shorter prosecutor's notice to stop a criminal charge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dismissal - the act of ending a legal case without a conviction
  • antonym: prosecution - the act of bringing legal action against someone
