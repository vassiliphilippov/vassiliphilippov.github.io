Señorita

noun
Spanish title for an unmarried woman
3.7
Pronunciation: / seɪˌnjɔˈritə / (AmE), / ˌseɪnjəˈriːtə / (BrE)
a Spanish title or form of address for an unmarried girl or woman, equivalent to 'Miss' in English

Examples

Senorita called politely
At the restaurant, the waiter politely called out, "Señorita, your table is ready."
She prefers to be called Señorita instead of Señora.
The teacher addressed her as Señorita Martinez throughout the class.
