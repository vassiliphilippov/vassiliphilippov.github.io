stylopod

adjective
upper limb segment
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪləˌpɑd / (AmE), / ˈstaɪləˌpɒd / (BrE)
relating to or located in the stylopod, the upper segment of a vertebrate limb such as the humerus or femur.

Examples

Stylopod region injury
The stylopod region appeared swollen after the fracture, requiring immediate orthopedic evaluation.
In tetrapods, the humerus is the bone found in the stylopod segment of the forelimb.
Researchers studied the development of the stylopod to understand limb evolution in vertebrates.

Related Terms

  • antonym: autopod - relating to the distal segment of a limb
  • antonym: zeugopod - relating to the middle segment of a limb
