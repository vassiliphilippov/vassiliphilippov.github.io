stylopod

adjective
upper limb segment
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈstaɪ.lə.pɑːd/ (AmE), /ˈstaɪ.lə.pɒd/ (BrE)
relating to or located in the stylopod, the upper segment of a vertebrate limb such as the humerus or femur.
