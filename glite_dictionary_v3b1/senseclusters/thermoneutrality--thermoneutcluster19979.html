thermoneutrality

noun
neutral temperature state
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌθɜrmoʊnuːˈtræləti / (AmE), / ˌθɜːməʊnjuːˈtrælɪti / (BrE)
the state in which an organism does not need to use extra energy to maintain its body temperature

Examples

Maintain thermoneutrality state
The laboratory maintained thermoneutrality to ensure the animals did not waste energy on temperature regulation.
At thermoneutrality, the mouse's metabolic rate was at its lowest and most stable.
Researchers studied how quickly the birds returned to thermoneutrality after being exposed to cold.

Related Terms

  • synonym: homeostasis - the maintenance of stable internal conditions in an organism
  • antonym: hyperthermia - abnormally high body temperature
  • antonym: hypothermia - dangerously low body temperature
