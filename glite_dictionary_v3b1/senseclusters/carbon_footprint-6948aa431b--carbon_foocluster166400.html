carbon footprint

noun
greenhouse gases emitted by activities
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrbən ˈfʊtˌprɪnt / (AmE), / ˈkɑːbən ˈfʊtprɪnt / (BrE)
the amount of greenhouse gases emitted into the atmosphere as a result of the activities of an individual, organization, or product

Examples

Calculate carbon footprint
Calculating your carbon footprint can help you find ways to live more sustainably.
Many companies are working to reduce their carbon footprint by using renewable energy.
Traveling by train instead of by plane significantly lowers your carbon footprint.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ecological footprint - measure of the impact of activities in terms of natural resource consumption and waste production
