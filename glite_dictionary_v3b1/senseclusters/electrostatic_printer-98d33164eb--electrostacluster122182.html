electrostatic printer

noun
printer using electric charge for imaging
1.6 4.2
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˈstætɪk ˈprɪntər / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈstætɪk ˈprɪntə / (BrE)
