isolator

noun
separating device or person
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / ˈaɪsəleɪtə / (BrE)
a device or person that separates or keeps things apart to prevent contact, interference, or transmission, especially for safety

Examples

Electrical isolator
The engineer installed an isolator to reduce electrical noise in the circuit.
A medical isolator is used to keep patients with contagious diseases separate from others.
Earthquake-resistant buildings often use a special isolator to absorb ground movement.
The electrical isolator ensures safety during maintenance work.
A laboratory isolator is used to prevent contamination of sensitive experiments.
The technician installed a new isolator to separate the faulty circuit from the rest.

Related Terms

  • antonym: connector - a device that joins or links things together
  • synonym: separator - a device or person that divides or keeps things apart
