blue-eyed

adjective
regarded as a favorite
Pronunciation: / bluː aɪd / (AmE), / bluː aɪd / (BrE)
Regarded with special favor or as a favorite, often by someone in authority or power.

Examples

Blue-eyed favorite
As the blue-eyed girl of her department, she often received the best assignments.
He became the blue-eyed candidate for the promotion after impressing the CEO.
The manager clearly sees Tom as his blue-eyed employee who can do no wrong.

Related Terms
