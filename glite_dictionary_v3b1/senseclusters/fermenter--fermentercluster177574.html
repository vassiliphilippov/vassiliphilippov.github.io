fermenter

noun
instigator of change
7.3
Pronunciation: / fərˈmɛntər / (AmE), / fəˈmɛntə / (BrE)
a person or thing that causes agitation, unrest, or rapid change, especially by stirring up feelings or actions

Examples

A social fermenter
The activist became a fermenter of social change in her community.
His controversial ideas made him a fermenter among his colleagues.
Some saw her as a troublemaker, but others admired her role as a fermenter of progress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agitator - a person who urges others to protest or rebel
  • synonym: instigator - a person who initiates something, especially trouble or change
  • antonym: peacemaker - a person who helps others resolve conflict and restore calm
