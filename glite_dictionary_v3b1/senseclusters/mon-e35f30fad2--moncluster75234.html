Mon

noun
member of a Southeast Asian ethnic group
Pronunciation: / mɑn / (AmE), / mɒn / (BrE)
A member of a Buddhist ethnic group native to Myanmar and adjacent parts of Thailand.

Examples

Mon people
A community of Mon settled along the river in Thailand.
She is proud of her Mon heritage and often visits her ancestral village.
The Mon have preserved their cultural traditions for centuries despite influences from neighboring groups.
