word it differently

verb
to express in other words
Pronunciation: / wɝd ɪt ˈdɪfɚəntli / (AmE), / wɜːd ɪt ˈdɪfərəntli / (BrE)
to express the same idea using different words or phrasing

Examples

To word it differently
If you don't understand the instruction, I can word it differently.
She tried to word it differently so the children could grasp the idea.
Sometimes, it's helpful to word it differently for your audience.
