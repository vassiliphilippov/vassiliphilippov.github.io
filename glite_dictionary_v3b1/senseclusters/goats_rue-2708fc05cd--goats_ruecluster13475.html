goat's rue

noun
a European perennial plant with blue flowers
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊts ruː / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊts ruː / (BrE)
A tall bushy European perennial plant (Galega officinalis) with pinnate leaves and slender spikes of blue flowers, sometimes used medicinally.

Examples

European goat's rue
Although not commonly cultivated, goat's rue is occasionally found in botanical gardens.
Clusters of blue flowers covered the goat's rue growing at the edge of the meadow.
The herbalist recommended goat's rue as a traditional remedy for boosting milk production.
