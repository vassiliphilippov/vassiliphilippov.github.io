picaroon

noun
pirate or rogue
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪkəˈruːn / (AmE), / ˌpɪkəˈruːn / (BrE)
an old term for a pirate or a dishonest adventurer, especially one who roams in search of plunder

Legends tell of a clever picaroon who outwitted the navy at every turn.
He dressed as a picaroon for the costume party, complete with a fake sword and eyepatch.
