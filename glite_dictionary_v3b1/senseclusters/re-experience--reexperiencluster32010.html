re-experience

noun
experiencing again
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˌriː ɪkˈspɪriəns / (AmE), / ˌriː ɪkˈspɪəriəns / (BrE)
an instance of experiencing something again, especially a feeling, event, or memory

Examples

A re-experience of joy
The movie brought a powerful re-experience of her childhood emotions.
For many veterans, certain sounds can trigger a sudden re-experience of past events.
Reading his old letters was a bittersweet re-experience of their friendship.

Related Terms

  • synonym: memory - the ability to remember past experiences
  • synonym: reliving - the act of experiencing something again in one's mind
