Vermeer

proper noun
Dutch painter (1632–1675), noted for use of light
Pronunciation: / vərˈmɪr / (AmE), / vɛəˈmɪə / (BrE)
(1632–1675) A Dutch painter celebrated for his exquisite use of light and color in domestic interior scenes.

Examples

Vermeer paintings
Art historians often study Vermeer to understand 17th-century Dutch painting techniques.
The Girl with a Pearl Earring is perhaps Vermeer's most famous work.
The painting by Vermeer captivated everyone with its subtle lighting and serene atmosphere.
