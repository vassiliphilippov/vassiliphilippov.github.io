initial public offering

noun
company's first public stock sale
6.7
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɪʃl ˈpʌblɪk ˈɔfərɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˈnɪʃl ˈpʌblɪk ˈɒfərɪŋ / (BrE)
the first time a company offers its shares for sale to the public on the stock market

Examples

The initial public offering
Facebook's initial public offering was one of the most anticipated events in financial history.
Many investors are eager to buy shares during an initial public offering in hopes of large profits.
The company raised $100 million during its initial public offering last year.
