devil

noun
used in exclamations of surprise or confusion
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈdɛvəl / (BrE)
(in exclamations) used to express surprise, confusion, or annoyance, often for emphasis or to question something unexpected.

Examples

Devil! What happened
Devil! Where did you come from at this hour?
The devil take it, I completely forgot about the meeting.
What the devil is going on in here?
