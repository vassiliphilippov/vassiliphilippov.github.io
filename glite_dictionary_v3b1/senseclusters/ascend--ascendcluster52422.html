Examples

To ascend a mountain
The road ascends steeply toward the mountain pass.

Synonyms

  • go up
    1.0 12/m
    go up is the everyday phrase for moving higher in space or level, while ascend sounds more formal and literary.
  • rise
    1.0 68/m
    rise is used for things like the sun or bread moving upward naturally, while ascend sounds more formal and deliberate.
  • move up
    2.0 1.65/m
    move up is common for changing position or rank and can be physical or figurative, while ascend implies upward movement.
  • mount
    3.6 7.25/m
    mount often means to climb or increase gradually with effort, while ascend simply means to go upward or higher.

Related Terms

  • synonym: climb - to go upward, often by using the hands and feet
  • antonym: descend - to move or slope downwards
  • synonym: rise - to move upward or appear above the horizon (as the sun, moon, etc.)
  • antonym: set - to go below the horizon (of a celestial body)
