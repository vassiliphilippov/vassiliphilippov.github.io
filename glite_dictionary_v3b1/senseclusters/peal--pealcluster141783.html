peal

noun
deep, prolonged sound of bells or thunder
5.8 0.62/m
Pronunciation: / pil / (AmE), / piÀêl / (BrE)
a deep, long, and loud sound, especially made by bells or thunder

Examples

A peal of thunder
A loud peal of thunder shook the windows during the storm.
Suddenly, a peal of laughter erupted from the children in the playground.
The church bells rang out in a joyful peal on Christmas morning.
