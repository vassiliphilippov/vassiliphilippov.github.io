bend

verb
move a joint so it is not straight
3.8
Pronunciation: / bɛnd / (AmE), / bɛnd / (BrE)
to make or become curved or angled instead of straight (for objects, body parts, roads, rivers, etc.)

Examples

To bend a stick
He tried to bend his finger, but it was too sore.
She bent her elbow to take a sip from the cup.
You should bend your knees when you lift heavy objects.
As the river bends to the east, it goes through a narrow valley.
She had to bend the wire into a circle for her art project.
The road bends sharply after the bridge, so drive carefully.
As the river bends, the scenery becomes even more beautiful.
Be careful as the highway bends sharply just after the tunnel.
The narrow path bends to the left after the old bridge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curve - to have or form a curve; to bend
  • antonym: straighten - to make something straight or return it to its original position
