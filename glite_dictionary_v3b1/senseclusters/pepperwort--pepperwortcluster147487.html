pepperwort

noun
water fern or small plant with four-part leaves
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…õp…ôrÀåw…úrt / (AmE), / Ààp…õp…ôw…úÀêt / (BrE)
any of several small plants, especially of the genus Marsilea, characterized by compound leaves with four leaflets and typically found in wet habitats; also sometimes refers to certain plants of the genus Lepidium.

Examples

Wild pepperwort plant
Botanists studied the pepperwort to better understand its unique leaf structure.
Some species of pepperwort can be found in marshy fields throughout Europe.
The shallow edges of the pond were thick with pepperwort thriving among the reeds.
