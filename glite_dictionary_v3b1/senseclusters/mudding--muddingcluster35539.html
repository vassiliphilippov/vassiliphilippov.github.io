mudding

verb
to apply mud or compound
4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌd.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈmʌd.ɪn/ (AmE), /ˈmʌd.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈmʌd.ɪn/ (BrE)
to cover or smear something with mud or a mud-like material, especially to apply joint compound to drywall during finishing

Examples

To mud a wall
The workers spent the afternoon mudding the new drywall in the living room.
Before painting, you should finish mudding all the seams and corners.
He learned the basics of mudding from his father, who was a contractor.
