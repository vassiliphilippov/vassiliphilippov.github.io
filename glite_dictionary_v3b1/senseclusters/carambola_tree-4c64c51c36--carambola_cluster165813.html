carambola tree

noun
tree producing star fruit
Pronunciation: / kəˈræmbələ triː / (AmE), / kəˈræmbələ triː / (BrE)
A tropical tree native to Southeast Asia, known for bearing star-shaped, yellow-brown fruit called carambola or star fruit.

Examples

The carambola tree fruit
Botanists studied the growth patterns of the carambola tree in different climates.
Many tropical gardens feature a carambola tree for its unique-looking fruit and attractive foliage.
The carambola tree in our yard produces sweet, juicy star fruit every summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: star fruit - the fruit produced by the carambola tree, star-shaped in cross-section
