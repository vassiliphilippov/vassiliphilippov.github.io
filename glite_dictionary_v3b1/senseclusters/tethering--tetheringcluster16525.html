tethering

noun
sharing internet connection
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈte.ðər.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈte.ðə.rɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of sharing a device's internet connection with other devices, usually via Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or USB

Examples

Phone tethering
Many travelers use tethering to get their laptops online when there is no Wi-Fi available.
Some mobile plans charge extra for tethering because it uses more data.
He enabled tethering on his phone so his tablet could access the internet.
