variola major

noun
severe form of smallpox virus
9.2
Pronunciation: / vəˌraɪələ ˈmeɪdʒər /; / vɛrˈaɪ.ələ ˈmeɪ.dʒɚ / (AmE), / vəˌraɪələ ˈmeɪdʒə /; / vəˈrʌɪ.ələ ˈmeɪ.dʒə / (BrE)
the more severe and common form of smallpox, a highly contagious viral disease with a high fatality rate and symptoms like fever and skin eruptions
