picture-perfect

adjective
flawless or ideal in appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkt͡ʃɚ ˌpɚ.fɪkt / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃə ˌpɜː.fɪkt / (BrE)
Extremely attractive or flawless in appearance, as if suitable for a photograph; ideal or immaculate.

Examples

A picture-perfect view
After the renovation, their kitchen was truly picture-perfect.
The bride and groom looked picture-perfect on their wedding day.
The mountain town was picture-perfect under a blanket of fresh snow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perfect - entirely without flaws or defects
