knifepoint

noun
tip of a knife
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪfpɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈnaɪfpɔɪnt / (BrE)
the sharp tip or end of a knife, often used to threaten or harm someone.

Examples

A sharp knifepoint
He accidentally cut his finger with the knifepoint while preparing dinner.
The robber threatened the cashier at knifepoint and demanded all the money.
She used the knifepoint to carefully remove the splinter from his hand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blade - the flat cutting part of a knife
  • antonym: hilt - the handle of a knife or sword
