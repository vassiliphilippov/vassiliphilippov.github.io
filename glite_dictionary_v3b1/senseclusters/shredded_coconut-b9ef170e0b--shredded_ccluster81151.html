shredded coconut

noun
finely grated coconut meat
Pronunciation: / ˈʃrɛdɪd koʊˈkʌnət / (AmE), / ˈʃrɛdɪd kəʊˈkʌnət / (BrE)
Coconut meat that has been finely grated into thin strips or flakes, commonly used in cooking and baking.

Examples

Shredded coconut flakes
She sprinkled shredded coconut over the top of the cake for added texture.
The recipe calls for one cup of shredded coconut to be mixed into the batter.
You can toast shredded coconut in the oven for a nuttier flavor.
