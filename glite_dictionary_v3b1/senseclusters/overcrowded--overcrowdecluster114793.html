Synonyms

  • crowded
    1.0 14/m
    crowded is filled with many people in one space, while overcrowded has too many people and becomes uncomfortable or ineffective.
  • packed
    1.3 1.82/m
    packed is extremely full with people pressed close together, while overcrowded describes a space with too many people and poor comfort.
  • congested
    4.4 0.93/m
    congested makes movement or traffic slow and blocked, while overcrowded focuses on too many people making a place uncomfortable.
  • wall-to-wall
    4.2 0.52/m
    wall-to-wall means a space is completely filled across the whole area, while overcrowded stresses lack of room and discomfort.
  • chockablock
    7.9 0.00/m
    chockablock is a very informal way to say something is packed full, while overcrowded is a neutral term for uncomfortable crowding.
