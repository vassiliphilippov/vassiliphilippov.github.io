before Christ

adverb
of years before the birth of Christ
1.0 3.9
Pronunciation: /bɪ.ˈfɔːr kraɪst/ /; / /bə.ˈfɔːr kraɪst/ (AmE), /bɪ.ˈfɔː kraɪst/ /; / /bə.ˈfɔː kraɪst/ (BrE)
