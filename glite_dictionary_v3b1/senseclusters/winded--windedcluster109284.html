winded

adjective
out of breath; breathing heavily
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndɪd / (AmE), / ˈwɪndɪd / (BrE)
out of breath; breathing heavily and with difficulty, usually after physical exertion

Examples

Completely winded after running
After running up the stairs, he was completely winded and had to stop for a moment.
She became winded halfway through the soccer match but quickly recovered.
The hike left everyone winded by the time they reached the summit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breathless - gasping for breath, out of breath
  • synonym: panting - breathing with short, quick breaths
