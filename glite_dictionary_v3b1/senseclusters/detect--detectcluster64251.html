detect

verb
discover the presence of something
1.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈtɛkt / (AmE), / dɪˈtɛkt / (BrE)
to discover or determine the existence or presence of something, especially something difficult to notice

Examples

To detect early signs
Scientists are working to detect early signs of the disease.
She used a special sensor to detect movement in the building.
The smoke alarm failed to detect the presence of smoke in the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discover - find or learn something for the first time
  • antonym: miss - to fail to notice or detect something
  • synonym: perceive - become aware of something through senses
