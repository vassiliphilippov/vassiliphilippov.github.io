finish

noun
surface texture or coating; finishing substance
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪʃ / (BrE)
a decorative or protective surface, texture, or coating, or a high level of refinement or perfection in something

Examples

Glossy finish
He applied another layer of finish to protect the surface from water damage.
The car's metallic finish made it stand out under the sunlight.
The dining table has a smooth, glossy finish that highlights the grain of the wood.
Although the sculpture was beautiful, the finish made it truly exceptional.
Her writing style has an impressive finish that few authors can match.
The cabinetmaker worked for years to achieve that level of finish in his furniture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coarseness - lack of refinement or rough quality
  • synonym: coating - a layer of a substance spread over a surface for protection or decoration
  • synonym: polish - refinement or smoothness of quality
  • synonym: varnish - a liquid applied to surfaces to provide a hard, glossy finish
