fireboy

noun
boy tending fire
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəbɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy employed to tend or maintain a fire, especially in historical settings

Examples

The fireboy tends fire
The fireboy rose early each morning to light the kitchen hearth.
In the grand manor, a fireboy was responsible for keeping every fireplace burning.
During the harsh winter, the diligent fireboy ensured the family stayed warm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: servant - a person employed to perform domestic duties
  • synonym: stoker - a person who tends and feeds a fire, especially on a steamship or train
