family court

noun
court for family-related legal cases
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfæmli kɔrt / (AmE), / ˈfæmli kɔːt / (BrE)
A court that deals with cases involving family matters such as divorce, child custody, and adoption.

Examples

Family court cases
After the divorce, all matters regarding the children were handled by family court.
She works as a judge in the local family court, hearing cases about adoption and guardianship.
The couple appeared in family court to resolve their custody dispute.
