isotype

noun
duplicate holotype specimen
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsəˌtaɪp / (AmE), / ˈaɪsəʊˌtaɪp / (BrE)
a duplicate specimen of the holotype, collected at the same time and place, kept as a taxonomic reference in a herbarium

Examples

Plant isotype specimen
The herbarium preserved an isotype to ensure the species could be studied by future botanists.
Researchers examined the isotype to confirm the identity of the rare plant.
Each isotype is stored alongside the holotype for reference in taxonomic studies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: holotype - the single specimen designated as the type of a species
  • synonym: paratype - a specimen other than the holotype used in the original description of a species
