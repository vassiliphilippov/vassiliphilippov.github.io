wisher

noun
person giving wishes
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪʃər / (AmE), / ˈwɪʃə / (BrE)
a person who expresses good wishes or congratulations to someone, especially on a special occasion

Examples

Birthday wisher
Many wishers sent cards and messages to congratulate her on her graduation.
The birthday wisher handed over a colorful card with a big smile.
All the wishers gathered to celebrate the couple's anniversary with warm words.

Related Terms

  • synonym: well-wisher - a person who wishes someone well or expresses support
