Weimar Republic

proper noun
German republic (1919–1933)
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪˌmɑr rəˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / ˈvaɪmɑː rəˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
the German republic established in 1919 following World War I and ending with the rise of the Nazi regime in 1933

Examples

The Weimar Republic era
Artists and intellectuals found new freedom of expression during the years of the Weimar Republic.

The collapse of the Weimar Republic led to the rise of Adolf Hitler.
