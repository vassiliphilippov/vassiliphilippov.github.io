naughty

adjective
mildly indecent; sexually suggestive
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔti / (AmE), / ˈnɔːti / (BrE)
(of words, jokes, or actions) suggestive of sexual impropriety; mildly indecent or risqué, especially in a playful or humorous way.

Examples

Naughty joke
He gave me a naughty wink across the table.
She laughed at his naughty jokes, pretending to be shocked.
The comedian told a naughty story that made everyone blush.
