centime

noun
a hundredth of a franc or similar unit
8.2
Pronunciation: / sɑ̃ˈtim /; / sɑnˈtim / (AmE), / sɒnˈtiːm /; / sɒ̃ˈtiːm / (BrE)
a unit of money equal to one hundredth of the main currency in France, Switzerland, and some other French-speaking countries
