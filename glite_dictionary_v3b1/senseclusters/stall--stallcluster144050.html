stall

verb
cause an engine to stop running
3.9 1.82/m
Pronunciation: / st…îl / (AmE), / st…îÀêl / (BrE)
(of a vehicle or engine) to stop running, usually unintentionally or because of a fault

Examples

Car stalls suddenly
New drivers sometimes stall their cars when learning to use a manual transmission.
She accidentally stalled the engine at a busy intersection.
The old truck would often stall when I tried to accelerate quickly.
If you don't press the clutch soon enough, the engine might stall.
The old truck suddenly stalled at the intersection, blocking traffic for several minutes.
Their boat stalled in the middle of the lake when the motor stopped working.

Synonyms
