sawdust stove

noun
stove burning sawdust as fuel
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔːˌdʌst stoʊv / (AmE), / ˈsɔːˌdʌst stəʊv / (BrE)
a simple stove designed to burn sawdust as fuel, typically inexpensive and easy to construct

Examples

A sawdust stove
He built a sawdust stove using scrap metal and old bricks for his workshop.
Many rural families rely on a sawdust stove to keep their homes warm during the winter.
Using a sawdust stove is an economical way to reduce heating costs.
