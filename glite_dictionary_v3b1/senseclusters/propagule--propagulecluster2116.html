propagule

noun
plant reproductive part
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːpəˌɡjuːl /; / ˈprɑpəˌɡjul / (AmE), / ˈprɒpəˌɡjuːl / (BrE)
a part of a plant or organism, such as a seed, spore, cutting, or fragment, that can grow into a new individual or colony
