nonstudent

noun
person not enrolled
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈstuːdənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈstjuːdənt / (BrE)
a person who is not enrolled as a student at a school, college, or university

Examples

A nonstudent visitor
The library offers special programs for nonstudents who live in the community.
Only nonstudents are required to pay an entrance fee for the event.
The university gym is not open to nonstudents during exam week.

Related Terms

  • antonym: student - a person who is enrolled in a school, college, or university
