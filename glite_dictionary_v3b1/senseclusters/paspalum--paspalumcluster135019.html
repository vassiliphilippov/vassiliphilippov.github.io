paspalum

noun
tropical American grass used as pasture
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpæspələm / (AmE), / ˈpaspələm / (BrE)
A tall, tufted perennial grass native to tropical America, widely grown as pasture and forage grass in the southern United States.

Examples

Tall paspalum grass
