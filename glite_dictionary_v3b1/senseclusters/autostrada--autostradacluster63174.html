autostrada

noun
Italian expressway or motorway
9.1 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / Àå…ëÀêto äÀàstr…ëÀêd…ô / (AmE), / Àåa ät…ô äÀàstr…ëÀêd…ô / (BrE)
A high-speed expressway, especially one in Italy or an Italian-speaking country, often with tolls and limited access.

Examples

The autostrada toll
The Italian autostrada is known for its fast-moving traffic and toll booths.
To reach Florence, take the autostrada south from Bologna.
While driving in Italy, we stopped at a service area along the autostrada for lunch.

Synonyms

  • motorway
    1.8 0.57/m
    a motorway is a wide, high-speed road with full controlled access and no crossings, while an autostrada is the Italian equivalent, often tolled.
  • expressway
    2.8 1.73/m
    an expressway is a fast divided road usually without at-grade intersections, while an autostrada is Italy's controlled-access, often tolled highway.
