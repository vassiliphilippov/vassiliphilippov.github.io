bicornuous

adjective
having two horns or horn-shaped parts
9.9
Pronunciation: / baɪˈkɔr.nu.əs / (AmE), / baɪˈkɔː.njuː.əs / (BrE)
having two horns or horn-shaped parts, especially used to describe anatomical structures.

Examples

Bicornuous uterus
An abnormality leading to a bicornuous shape can affect reproductive health.
Certain species of animals, like deer, naturally have a bicornuous skull structure.
The veterinarian identified the rare condition of a bicornuous uterus in the cow.
