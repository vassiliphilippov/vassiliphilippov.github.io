organometallic

adjective
containing metal and carbon
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːrɡənoʊməˈtælɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːɡənəʊməˈtælɪk / (BrE)
describing a compound that contains a metal atom bonded directly to a carbon atom in an organic group

Examples

Organometallic compound
Researchers developed a new organometallic catalyst to speed up the chemical reaction.
The unique properties of organometallic compounds make them useful in industrial processes.
She wrote her thesis on the structure of organometallic molecules in pharmaceuticals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inorganic - not containing carbon-hydrogen bonds; not organic
  • synonym: metallic - relating to or containing metal
