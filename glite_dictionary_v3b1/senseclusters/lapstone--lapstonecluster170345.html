lapstone

noun
shoemaker's stone tool
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlæpˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈlæpˌstəʊn / (BrE)
a flat stone used by shoemakers to shape and smooth leather during the making of shoes

Examples

A lapstone for shoemaking
The shoemaker rested the leather on his lapstone before shaping it with a hammer.
Old workshops often display a lapstone among other traditional cobbler tools.
He inherited his grandfather's lapstone, which had been used for decades in the family business.
