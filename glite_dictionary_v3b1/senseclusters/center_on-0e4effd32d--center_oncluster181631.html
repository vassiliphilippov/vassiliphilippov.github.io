Synonyms

  • center
    2.8 10/m
    center directs attention or places something in the middle, while center on means to have something as the main subject.
  • focus on
    2.0 70/m
    focus on directs most attention to one subject, while center on describes what is the main subject of a discussion or story.
  • revolve around
    3.5 4/m
    revolve around uses a circular image to show something is central, while center on simply says what is the main subject.
  • concentrate on
    3.1 13/m
    concentrate on means giving all attention or effort to an activity, while center on says what is the main subject.
