representationist

noun
believer in realism
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛprɪˈzɛnteɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌrɛprɪˈzɛnteɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes that perception or art should represent or reflect reality as it is

Examples

Representationist view of art
The representationist insisted that art should closely imitate the natural world.
As a representationist, she preferred paintings that depicted real-life scenes.
Many philosophers debate the views of a representationist regarding perception and reality.

Related Terms

  • antonym: abstractionist - a person who creates or supports abstract art, not directly representing reality
  • synonym: realist - a person who believes in representing things as they really are
