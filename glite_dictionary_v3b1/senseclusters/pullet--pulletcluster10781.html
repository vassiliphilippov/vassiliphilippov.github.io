pullet

noun
meat of a young chicken for frying
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊlɪt / (AmE), / ˈpʊlɪt / (BrE)
The meat of a medium-sized young chicken, considered especially suitable for frying.

Examples

Fried pullet meat
Freshly cooked pullet is often more tender than older chicken meats.
The chef recommended using a pullet for the best fried chicken.
