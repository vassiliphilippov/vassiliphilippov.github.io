exasperatingly

adverb
in an exasperating way
5.1 7.5
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzæspəˌreɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɑːspəˌreɪtɪŋli / (BrE)

Examples

To exasperatingly annoy
He exasperatingly pointed out every small mistake I made.
She exasperatingly refused to answer any of my questions.
The meeting dragged on exasperatingly slowly, testing everyone's patience.
