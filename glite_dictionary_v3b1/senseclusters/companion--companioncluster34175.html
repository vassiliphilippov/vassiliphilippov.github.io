companion

noun
robot or virtual assistant that provides company
4.8
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpænjən / (AmE), / kəmˈpæn.jən / (BrE)
a non-human device or program (for example, a robot or app) that accompanies, assists, or keeps a person company, often providing care or social interaction.

Examples

Companion robot
The nursing home introduced a small companion robot to cheer residents during morning activities.
Many smartphone apps act as virtual companions, reminding users to exercise and take medicine.
Engineers designed the companion to learn residents' routines and respond when help is needed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assistant - a person or device that helps or supports someone in tasks or daily life
  • synonym: robot - a machine capable of carrying out complex actions automatically, often used for tasks or social interaction
