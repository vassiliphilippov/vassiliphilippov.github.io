bucko

noun
informal male address
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbʌkəʊ / (BrE)
an informal, friendly way to address a man, often used to mean friend or buddy

Examples

Hey, bucko
Hey bucko, are you coming to the game tonight or not?
Listen here, bucko, you need to finish your work before you leave.
Don't worry, bucko, I've got your back if anything goes wrong.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buddy - a close friend or companion
  • synonym: pal - an informal term for a friend
