machine

noun
extremely efficient or relentless person
Pronunciation: / məˈʃin / (AmE), / məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
A person who is extremely efficient or relentless, especially in performing a particular task.

Examples

Work like a machine
Everyone on the team respects her because she works like a machine under pressure.
He trained so hard that he became a machine on the football field.
Once she starts working, she's a machine and doesn't stop until the job is done.

Related Terms

  • synonym: robot - a person who acts in a mechanical, unemotional way
