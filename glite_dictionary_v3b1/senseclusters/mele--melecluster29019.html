mele

verb
to sing Hawaiian chant
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛleɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɛleɪ / (BrE)
to sing or chant a traditional Hawaiian song or chant, often as part of a ceremony or celebration

Examples

To mele at celebrations
The group gathered to mele in honor of their ancestors at the ceremony.
She learned how to mele from her grandmother, who taught her traditional Hawaiian songs.
At the festival, children were invited to mele along with the elders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chant - to sing or say something repeatedly, often in a rhythmic way
  • synonym: sing - to produce musical tones with the voice
