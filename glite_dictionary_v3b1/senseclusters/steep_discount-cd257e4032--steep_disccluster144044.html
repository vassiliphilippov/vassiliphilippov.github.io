steep discount

noun
large price reduction
2.9
Pronunciation: / stip ˈdɪsˌkaʊnt / (AmE), / stiːp ˈdɪskaʊnt / (BrE)
a very large or substantial reduction in the price of something

Examples

A steep discount
Because of the damaged packaging, the laptop was sold at a steep discount.
Customers rushed to the store when they heard about the steep discount on designer clothes.
The new phone was available at a steep discount during the holiday promotion.
