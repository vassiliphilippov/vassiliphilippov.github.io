linearly

adverb
in a linear manner
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪniɚli / (AmE), / ˈlɪniəli / (BrE)
In a linear manner; in a way that is straight or changes proportionally in one direction.

Examples

Change linearly over time
If you plot these values, you will see that they change linearly over time.
The cost does not rise linearly with the number of users, due to discounts.
The system's output increases linearly with the input voltage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nonlinearly - in a nonlinear manner
