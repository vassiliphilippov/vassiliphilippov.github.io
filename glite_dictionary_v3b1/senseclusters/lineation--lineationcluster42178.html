lineation

noun
marking or outlining with lines
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪniˈeɪʃən /; / ˌlaɪniˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌlɪniˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of marking with lines or the lines/outlines themselves, especially in art, writing, or geology
