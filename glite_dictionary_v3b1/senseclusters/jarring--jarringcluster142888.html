Synonyms

  • grating
    6.2 0.02/m
    grating is an unpleasant, harsh sound that irritates the ear, while jarring describes a sound that also startles or shocks.
  • cacophonous
    8.4 0.1/m
    cacophonous describes a mix of loud, clashing sounds, while jarring means a single harsh sound that feels disturbing or shocking.
  • jangling
    5.9 0.00/m
    jangling means a sharp, metallic ringing that makes you wince, while jarring is any harsh sound that startles or disturbs.
  • rasping
    6.9 0.01/m
    rasping is a rough, scraping sound like a harsh voice or tool, while jarring is a more sudden, startling harsh sound.
  • raucous
    7.1 1/m
