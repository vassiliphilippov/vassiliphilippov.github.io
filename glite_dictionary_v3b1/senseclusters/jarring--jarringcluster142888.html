jarring

adjective
making a harsh, unpleasant sound
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɑːrɪŋ / (BrE)
causing a harsh, unpleasant, or discordant sound that is disturbing or startling

Examples

A jarring sound
A jarring clang rang out when the metal doors slammed shut.
Her voice took on a jarring tone during the argument.
The jarring sound of the alarm jolted everyone awake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grating - sounding harsh and unpleasant
  • antonym: soothing - having a calming or gentle effect in sound
