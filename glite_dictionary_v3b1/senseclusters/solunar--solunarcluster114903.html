solunar

noun
sun and moon influence
Pronunciation: / soʊˈluːnər / (AmE), / səʊˈluːnə / (BrE)
the theory that the positions and phases of the sun and moon affect the activity patterns of animals, especially fish and game.

Examples

Solunar theory for fishing
Many anglers use solunar tables to determine the best times for fishing.
The solunar theory suggests that animal activity peaks during certain lunar phases.
Hunters often plan their trips based on solunar predictions for increased success.

  • synonym: solar - relating to the sun
