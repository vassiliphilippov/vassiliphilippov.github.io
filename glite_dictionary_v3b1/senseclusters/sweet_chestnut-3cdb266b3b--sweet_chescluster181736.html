sweet chestnut

adjective
reddish‑brown colour
Pronunciation: / swit \u02c8t\u0283\u025bs.n\u028ct / (AmE), / swi\u02d0t \u02c8t\u0283\u025bs.n\u028ct / (BrE)
A medium to dark reddish‑brown colour resembling the nut or timber of the sweet chestnut tree, used to describe hair, fabrics, leather, and finishes.
