act like a rockstar

verb
behave flamboyantly or extravagantly
1.0 6.4
Pronunciation: /ækt laɪk ə ˈrɑːk.stɑːr/ (AmE), /ækt laɪk ə ˈrɒk.stɑː/ (BrE)
