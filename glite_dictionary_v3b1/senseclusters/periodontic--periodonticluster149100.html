periodontic

adjective
relating to periodontics (dental specialty)
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪr.i.oʊˈdɑn.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌpɛr.i.əˈdɒn.tɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving periodontics, the branch of dentistry concerned with the structures supporting and surrounding the teeth.

Examples

Periodontic dental care

The practice offers comprehensive periodontic care, including gum surgery and maintenance.
