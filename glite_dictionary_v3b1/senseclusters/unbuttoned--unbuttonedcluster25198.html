unbuttoned

adjective
not fastened with buttons
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbʌtnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbʌtənd / (BrE)
(of clothing) not fastened with buttons; with buttons left undone.

Examples

Unbuttoned shirt
He walked in with his unbuttoned shirt flapping in the breeze.
Leaving the jacket unbuttoned gave his outfit a relaxed look.
She noticed his coat was unbuttoned despite the cold weather.

Related Terms

  • antonym: buttoned - with buttons fastened
