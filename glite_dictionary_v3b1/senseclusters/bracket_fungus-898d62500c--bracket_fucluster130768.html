bracket fungus

noun
woody fungus forming shelf-like growths
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbrækət ˌfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / ˈbrækɪt ˌfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
a woody fungus that grows in shelf-like projections on tree trunks and wooden structures

Examples

A bracket fungus on tree
A bracket fungus was growing in layers on the old oak tree.
Many species of bracket fungus play a crucial role in decomposing wood in forests.
The decaying log was covered in large bracket fungi of various colors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: polypore - a type of fungus with pores or tubes on the underside
