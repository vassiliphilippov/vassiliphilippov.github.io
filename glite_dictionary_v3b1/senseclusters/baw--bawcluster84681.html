baw

verb
to cry loudly
9.9
to cry or weep loudly, especially in distress or frustration

Examples

To baw in tears
The toddler began to baw when his toy was taken away.
She could not help but baw after hearing the bad news.
He started to baw so loudly that everyone in the room turned to look.

Related Terms

  • antonym: laugh - to make sounds and movements of the face and body that show amusement
  • synonym: sob - to cry noisily while taking short, sudden breaths
  • synonym: wail - to make a long, loud cry of sadness or pain
