condemning

adjective
expressing disapproval or censure
Pronunciation: / kənˈdɛmɪŋ / (AmE), / kənˈdɛmɪŋ / (BrE)
expressing strong disapproval or censure; declaring something to be wrong or unacceptable

Examples

A condemning tone
Her condemning tone made it clear she did not support their actions.
Many critics wrote condemning reviews of the controversial film.
The senator made a condemning statement about the proposed policy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: approving - showing approval or support
  • synonym: condemnatory - expressing strong disapproval; censorious
