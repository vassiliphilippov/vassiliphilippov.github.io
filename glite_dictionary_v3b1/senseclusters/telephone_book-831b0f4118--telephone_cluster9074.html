telephone book

noun
directory of names and phone numbers
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛləˌfoʊn bʊk / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪfəʊn bʊk / (BrE)
a book or directory listing the names, addresses, and telephone numbers of individuals or businesses in a particular area, usually organized alphabetically

Examples

A telephone book listing
Every household used to receive a new telephone book every year.
I looked up her number in the telephone book, but she wasn't listed.
Many people no longer rely on a telephone book since information is available online.
