duel

noun
contest between two opponents
5.5 1.76/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàduÀê…ôl / (AmE), / ÀàdjuÀê…ôl / (BrE)
a contest or struggle between two skillful opponents, whether individuals or groups

Examples

A duel of rivals
The election campaign turned into a bitter duel between the leading candidates.
The teams' duel for the championship drew nationwide attention.
The two chess grandmasters engaged in a fierce duel that lasted several hours.

Synonyms

  • match
    1.4 23/m
    a match is a formal contest between teams or players, while a duel is a direct contest of skill between two opponents.
  • contest
    2.4 43/m
    a contest is a competition to win something among many participants, while a duel is a direct one-to-one test of skill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: battle - a sustained fight between opposing forces
  • synonym: competition - a contest between rivals
  • antonym: truce - an agreement to stop fighting
