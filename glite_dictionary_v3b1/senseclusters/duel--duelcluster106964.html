duel

noun
contest between two opponents
5.5 2/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàduÀê…ôl / (AmE), / ÀàdjuÀê…ôl / (BrE)

Examples

A duel of rivals
The election campaign turned into a bitter duel between the leading candidates.
The teams' duel for the championship drew nationwide attention.
The two chess grandmasters engaged in a fierce duel that lasted several hours.
