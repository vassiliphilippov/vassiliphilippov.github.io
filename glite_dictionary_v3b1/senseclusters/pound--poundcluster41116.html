pound

noun
public enclosure for stray dogs or animals
6.5
Pronunciation: / paʊnd / (AmE), / paʊnd / (BrE)
an enclosed public place where stray or unlicensed animals, especially dogs, are kept temporarily

Examples

Animal pound
After her dog went missing, she checked the pound every day.
Stray dogs are often held at the pound until someone claims them.
You should make sure your dog is licensed or it might end up in the pound.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shelter - a place that provides temporary accommodation, especially for animals or people
