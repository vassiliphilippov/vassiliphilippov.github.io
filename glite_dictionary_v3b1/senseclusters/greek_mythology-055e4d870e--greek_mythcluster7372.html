Greek mythology

noun
myths and legends of ancient Greece
2.4
Pronunciation: / ɡrik mɪˈθɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ɡriːk mɪˈθɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the collection of traditional stories and legends from ancient Greece, especially about their gods, heroes, and mythical creatures
