breakdown

noun
failure or collapse of a system or order
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪkˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈbreɪk.daʊn / (BrE)
the failure or collapse of a system, relationship, or order, so that it can no longer function successfully

Examples

Breakdown in talks
A breakdown in communication between management and staff caused widespread confusion.
Negotiations ended in a breakdown after neither side could reach a compromise.
The financial crisis led to the breakdown of several major banks across the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collapse - sudden failure or breakdown of a system or institution
  • synonym: failure - lack of success or breakdown in function or structure
