experiment

verb
carry out a scientific test or investigation
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspɛrəmənt / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɛrɪmənt / (BrE)
to carry out a scientific test or investigation to discover something unknown or to test a principle or hypothesis

Examples

To experiment in the lab
As a chef, he loves to experiment with new flavors and ingredients in his recipes.
Scientists often experiment with different variables to observe chemical reactions.
The teacher encouraged her students to experiment and try different approaches to solving the problem.

Related Terms

  • synonym: investigate - to carry out a systematic inquiry or examination to discover facts or information
  • synonym: test - to carry out a procedure to determine the quality, performance, or reliability of something
