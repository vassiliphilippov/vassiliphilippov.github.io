dam

noun
female parent of an animal
Pronunciation: / dæm / (AmE), / dæm / (BrE)
the female parent of an animal, especially when referring to domestic livestock

Examples

A calf's dam
Records showed that each calf's dam was healthy and well cared for.
The dam of the foal was a prize-winning mare.
The breeder tracked the lineage by noting both the sire and the dam.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sire - the male parent of an animal, especially livestock
