wrist

noun
joint connecting hand and forearm
1.0 27/m
Pronunciation: / rÉªst / (AmE), / rÉªst / (BrE)
The joint connecting the hand to the forearm, located between the distal end of the radius and the carpal bones.

Examples

A wrist watch
He wore a watch on his left wrist every day.
She accidentally twisted her wrist while playing tennis yesterday.
The doctor examined my wrist to check for any fractures.

Synonyms

  • wrist joint
    3.8 0.01/m
    wrist joint refers to the joint that lets the hand move, while wrist names the whole area where hand meets forearm.
  • radiocarpal joint
    6.1 0.00/m
    radiocarpal joint forms the main connection between the radius and carpal bones, while wrist is the general joint or area.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ankle - joint connecting foot and leg
