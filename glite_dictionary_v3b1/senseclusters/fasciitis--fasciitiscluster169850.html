fasciitis

noun
inflammation of fascia
9.0
Pronunciation: /fæ.ʃiː.ˈaɪ.tɪs/ /; / /fæ.ʃɪ.ˈaɪ.tɪs/ (AmE), /fæ.ʃiː.ˈaɪ.tɪs/ /; / /fæ.ʃɪ.ˈaɪ.tɪs/ (BrE)
inflammation or swelling of the fascia, a connective tissue layer in the body

Examples

Fasciitis foot pain
The doctor diagnosed her with fasciitis after she complained of persistent foot pain.
Athletes are sometimes affected by fasciitis due to overuse of certain muscles.
Treatment for fasciitis often includes rest, ice, and physical therapy.
