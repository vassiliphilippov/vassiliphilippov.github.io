airboat

verb
to travel by airboat
5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈer.boʊt/ (AmE), /ˈeə.bəʊt/ (BrE)
to travel in or transport people or goods using an airboat, especially over water or marshland

Examples

To airboat across marshes
We will airboat across the marsh tomorrow to reach the research site.
The guides airboated tourists through the winding waterways of the Everglades.
Scientists often airboat to remote locations that are inaccessible by road.
