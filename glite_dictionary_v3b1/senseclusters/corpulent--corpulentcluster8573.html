corpulent

adjective
excessively fat; bulky
7.3 0.47/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…îÀêrpj…ôl…ônt / (AmE), / Ààk…îÀêpj äl…ônt / (BrE)
(of a person) having a large, bulky body; excessively fat.

Examples

A corpulent man
Despite his corpulent appearance, he was surprisingly agile on the dance floor.
Doctors advised the corpulent patient to adopt a healthier lifestyle.
The corpulent gentleman struggled to squeeze into the theater seat.

Synonyms

  • obese
    3.8 8.73/m
    obese describes medical-level excess body fat and health risk, while corpulent is a formal word for a large, bulky body.
  • fat
    1.0 50/m
    fat is a plain, everyday word for excess flesh on a person, while corpulent sounds more formal and old-fashioned.
  • rotund
    5.8 0.50/m
    rotund describes a round, somewhat plump shape and can sound polite, while corpulent normally stresses excessive bulk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obese - very overweight; excessively fat
  • antonym: slender - gracefully thin; slim
