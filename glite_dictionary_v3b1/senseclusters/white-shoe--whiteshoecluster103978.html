white-shoe

adjective
conservative, elite (esp. US law/business)
9.1 7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈwaɪt.ʃuː/ /; / /ˈhwaɪt.ʃuː/ (AmE), /ˈwaɪt.ʃuː/ (BrE)
