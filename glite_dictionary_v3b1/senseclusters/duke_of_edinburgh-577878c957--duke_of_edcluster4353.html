Duke of Edinburgh

proper noun
British royal title, esp. for Prince Philip
6.1
Pronunciation: /duːk əv ˈɛ.dɪn.bɜːr.oʊ/ /; / /djuːk əv ˈɛ.dɪn.bɜːr.oʊ/ (AmE), /djuːk əv ˈɛ.dɪn.bərə/ /; / /dʒuːk əv ˈɛ.dɪn.brə/ /; / /duːk əv ˈɛ.dɪn.bərə/ (BrE)
the title held by the British prince who was the husband of Queen Elizabeth II, especially referring to Prince Philip (1921–2021)

Examples
