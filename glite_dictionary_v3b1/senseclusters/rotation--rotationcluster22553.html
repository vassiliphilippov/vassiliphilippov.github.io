rotation

noun
planned recurring sequence (of crops, personnel, etc.)
Pronunciation: / roʊˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / rəʊˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a planned recurring sequence or schedule, especially of crops, personnel, or duties, so that each appears or occurs regularly in turn

Examples

Crop rotation
Farmers practice crop rotation to preserve soil nutrients and prevent disease.
The company implements a staff rotation to ensure fairness in weekend shifts.
The team's starting pitcher rotation changed after the star player's injury.

Related Terms

  • synonym: schedule - a plan of activities or events and when they will happen
