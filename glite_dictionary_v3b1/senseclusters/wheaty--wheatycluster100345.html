wheaty

adjective
pale yellow-brown
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːti / (AmE), / ˈwiːti / (BrE)
having a pale yellowish-brown color similar to that of ripe wheat

Examples

Wheaty color
The curtains in the living room were a soft wheaty shade that matched the furniture.
She wore a wheaty dress that complemented her sun-kissed skin.
The fields turned a beautiful wheaty color as harvest season approached.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beige - a pale sandy yellow-brown color
  • degree higher: dark brown - a deep brown color
  • degree lower: ivory - a creamy white color
  • synonym: tan - a light brown color
