noun
sheet of glass in a window or door

A single sheet of glass in a window or door.

Examples

A glass pane
She carefully cleaned each pane until it sparkled in the sunlight.
The new door was fitted with a frosted glass pane for privacy.
The storm shattered a pane of glass in the living room window.

Synonyms

  • pane of glass
    1.6 0.61/m
    pane of glass is a flat sheet shaped to fit a window or door frame, while pane is the shorter general name.
  • windowpane
    2.4 1.04/m
    windowpane is a single sheet set in a window frame, while pane is the simple, general word for that glass.
  • window light
    4.2 0.00/m
    window light is an older term for a single window sheet used in traditional windows, while pane is the modern, general word.
