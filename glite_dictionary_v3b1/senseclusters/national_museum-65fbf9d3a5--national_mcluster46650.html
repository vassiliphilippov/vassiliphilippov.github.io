national museum

noun
principal museum representing a nation
Pronunciation: / ˈnæʃənl məˈzuːəm / (AmE), / ˈnæʃənəl mjuˈziːəm / (BrE)
A museum established to represent and preserve the cultural, historical, or artistic heritage of a nation, often housing significant national artifacts and exhibits.
