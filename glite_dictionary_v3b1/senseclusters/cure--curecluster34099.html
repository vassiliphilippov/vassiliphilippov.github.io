cure

verb
harden or stabilize a material
9.9
Pronunciation: / kjʊr / (AmE), / kjʊə / (BrE)
to cause a material, such as resin, cement, or soap, to harden and become stable, often by drying, heating, or chemical reaction

Examples

To cure resin
Exposure to sunlight will cure the resin, resulting in a solid and durable finish.
If you don't properly cure the concrete, it may crack over time.
The adhesive needs to cure for 24 hours before you can use the product.
