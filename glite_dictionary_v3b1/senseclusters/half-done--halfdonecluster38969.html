half-done

adjective
partially completed or cooked
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhæf dʌn / (AmE), / ˈhɑːf dʌn / (BrE)
Partially completed or partially cooked; not fully finished or prepared.

Examples

A half-done task
His homework was half-done and needed to be finished before school.
She served the chicken half-done, which worried some of the guests.
The report was only half-done when the manager asked for an update.

Related Terms

  • antonym: done - finished or fully cooked
  • synonym: incomplete - not finished; lacking some part
  • synonym: unfinished - not completed or concluded
