Synonyms

  • unfinished
    2.7 6/m
    unfinished means not completed and suits formal projects or tasks, while half-done feels more casual and can mean partly cooked.
  • semifinished
    4.4 0.00/m
    semifinished describes a product partly made for later work in factories, while half-done is more general for tasks or cooking.
  • semi-processed
    4.9 0.00/m
    semi-processed means a material has had some factory steps done, while half-done is broader for any partly finished item or food.
  • underdone
    3.8 0.10/m
    underdone means not cooked long enough and is usually about food, while half-done can mean partly finished in many situations.
  • half-baked
    4.1 0.01/m
    half-baked usually means not cooked through, often used for baking or a poor idea, while half-done is more neutral.
