peroxidation

noun
oxidation of fats
8.8
Pronunciation: /pə.rɑːk.sɪ.ˈdeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /pə.rɒk.sɪ.ˈdeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a chemical process in which fats or oils react with oxygen, leading to their deterioration and possible damage to cells
