anomalistic

adjective
relating to orbital anomaly
9.9
Pronunciation: /ə.nə.ˈmɑː.lɪs.tɪk/ (AmE), /ə.nə.ˈmɒ.lɪs.tɪk/ (BrE)
relating to the anomaly or the point of closest approach in an object's orbit, especially in astronomy

Examples

Anomalistic year
The anomalistic year is slightly longer than the sidereal year due to the Earth's shifting orbit.
Scientists measured the planet's anomalistic period to determine how often it comes closest to its star.
The anomalistic month refers to the time between the Moon's closest points to Earth.
