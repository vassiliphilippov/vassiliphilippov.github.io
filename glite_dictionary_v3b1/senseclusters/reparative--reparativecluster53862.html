reparative

adjective
intended to repair
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpærətɪv / (AmE), / rɪˈpærətɪv / (BrE)
intended to repair, restore, or improve something that is damaged or broken

Examples

Reparative surgery
The surgeon performed a reparative operation to mend the patient's heart valve.
After the storm, the city launched several reparative projects to fix damaged roads.
Therapy can have a reparative effect on emotional wounds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: restorative - having the ability to restore health or strength
