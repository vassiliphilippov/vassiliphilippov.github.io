ruff

noun
trump card play in card games
7.1
Pronunciation: / rʌf / (AmE), / rʌf / (BrE)
In card games, the act of playing a trump card to win a trick when unable to follow the suit led.

Examples

A clever ruff in cards
A clever ruff can sometimes change the outcome of a close match.
Her last two ruffs surprised the players and turned the game in her favor.
Unable to follow suit, he decided to ruff with a heart and took the trick.
