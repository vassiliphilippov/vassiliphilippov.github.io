streaked

adjective
marked with stripes or lines
2.7 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / striËkt / (AmE), / striËkt / (BrE)
marked with lines, stripes, or long narrow stains or discolorations

Examples

Streaked hair color
Her blond hair was streaked with pink dye for a bold new look.
The glass was streaked with fingerprints and needed to be cleaned.
The painter left the wall looking streaked after the rain washed over it.
