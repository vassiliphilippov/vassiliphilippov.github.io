cheesebox

noun
cheese container; armored structure
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːzˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːzˌbɒks / (BrE)
a box or container used for storing or transporting cheese, or a historical term for a type of armored structure

Examples

A cheesebox container
The farmer placed the fresh cheese in a wooden cheesebox for storage.
Historians studied the remains of the ironclad ship's cheesebox on the deck.
She found an old cheesebox in the attic filled with antique kitchen tools.
