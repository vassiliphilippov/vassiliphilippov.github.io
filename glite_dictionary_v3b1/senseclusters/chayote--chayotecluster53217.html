chayote

noun
edible climbing plant
Pronunciation: / tʃaɪˈoʊteɪ / (AmE), / tʃaɪˈəʊti / (BrE)
a tropical climbing plant (Sechium edule) that produces an edible green pear-shaped fruit, commonly grown for food.

Examples

Chayote plant vines
The farmer planted chayote along the fence to take advantage of its climbing vines.
In many Latin American dishes, chayote is cooked and served as a vegetable.
The market had fresh chayote available throughout the summer season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: squash - an edible gourd, related to chayote
  • synonym: vegetable pear - another name for chayote, an edible green fruit
