tombolo

noun
land bridge to island
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːmboʊˌloʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɒmbələʊ / (BrE)
a narrow strip of sand or land that connects an island to the mainland or to another island

Examples

A tombolo strip
A sandy tombolo links the small island to the coast.
During low tide, visitors can walk across the tombolo to reach the island.
The village was built on a tombolo that connects two larger land masses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: isthmus - a narrow strip of land connecting two larger land areas
  • synonym: peninsula - a piece of land almost surrounded by water but still connected to the mainland
