underdeck

noun
area below main deck
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌdɛk / (AmE), / ˈʌndədɛk / (BrE)
the space or area located below the main deck of a ship or similar structure

Examples

The underdeck space
The crew stored the extra supplies in the underdeck to keep them dry.
Passengers were not allowed to enter the underdeck during the voyage.
Heavy machinery was transported in the underdeck to balance the ship's weight.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deck - the main floor or platform of a ship
  • synonym: hold - the main storage area of a ship below the deck
