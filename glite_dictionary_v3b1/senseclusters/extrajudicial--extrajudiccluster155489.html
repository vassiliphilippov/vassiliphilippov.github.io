extrajudicial

adjective
outside the legal process
1.9 7.0
Pronunciation: /ek.strə.dʒuː.ˈdɪ.ʃəl/ /; / /ek.strə.dʒʊ.ˈdɪ.ʃəl/ (AmE), /ek.strə.dʒuː.ˈdɪ.ʃəl/ /; / /ek.strə.dʒʊ.ˈdɪ.ʃəl/ (BrE)
(of an action or decision) taking place outside the normal course or authority of a legal proceeding; not sanctioned by law or court.

Examples

Extrajudicial arrest
Human rights organizations document cases of extrajudicial arrests in the region.
The government faced international criticism after an extrajudicial killing was reported by the media.
The law strictly prohibits extrajudicial punishment in any form.
