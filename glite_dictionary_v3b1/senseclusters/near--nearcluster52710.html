near

adjective
on the left side (horse, animal, vehicle)
5.6 9.8
Pronunciation: / nɪr / (AmE), / nɪə / (BrE)
(of the side of an animal, especially a horse) situated on the left side when the animal is facing forward, used chiefly in British and equestrian contexts.

Examples

Near side horse
Always hitch the harness on the near horse first.
The near wheel of the carriage was slightly damaged in the accident.
The rider mounted from the near side, as is customary.

Related Terms

  • antonym: off - (of an animal) on the right side when facing forward; the opposite of 'near' in equestrian terminology
