cat thyme

noun
Mediterranean plant attractive to cats
Pronunciation: / kæt θaɪm / (AmE), / kæt θaɪm / (BrE)
A Mediterranean plant (Teucrium marum) with small hairy leaves and reddish-purple flowers, known for attracting cats.
