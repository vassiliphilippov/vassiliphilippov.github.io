psychometrist

noun
test administrator
9.6
Pronunciation: / saɪˈkɑːmətrɪst / (AmE), / saɪˈkɒmətrɪst / (BrE)
a person who is trained to give and score psychological tests

Examples

The psychometrist scores tests
The psychometrist carefully explained the instructions before starting the intelligence test.
After the session, the psychometrist scored the results and prepared a report for the psychologist.
A skilled psychometrist can make patients feel comfortable during assessments.
The psychometrist carefully explained the instructions before starting the intelligence test.
After the assessment, the psychometrist scored the results and prepared a detailed report.
Hospitals often employ a psychometrist to assist psychologists with patient evaluations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: examiner - a person who administers tests or assessments
  • synonym: psychologist - a professional who studies and treats mental processes and behavior
  • synonym: psychometrician - an expert in the theory and technique of psychological measurement
