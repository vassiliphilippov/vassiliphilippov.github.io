housesit

verb
to care for a home
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊsˌsɪt / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌsɪt / (BrE)
to stay in someone's home and take care of it while they are away

Examples

To housesit for friends
We asked our neighbor to housesit while we traveled abroad for two weeks.
Would you be able to housesit for us and water the plants?
She agreed to housesit and take care of the mail during their vacation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: babysit - to look after a child while the parents are away
  • synonym: petsit - to look after someone's pets while they are away
