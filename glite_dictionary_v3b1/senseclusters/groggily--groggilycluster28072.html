groggily

adverb
in a tired manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɑːɡɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡrɒɡɪli / (BrE)
in a way that shows you feel weak, tired, or confused, especially after waking up

Examples

To speak groggily
He answered the phone groggily, still half asleep from his nap.
After staying up all night, she moved groggily through her morning routine.
The child rubbed his eyes and walked groggily into the living room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drowsily - in a sleepy or half-awake way
  • antonym: energetically - in a lively or active way
