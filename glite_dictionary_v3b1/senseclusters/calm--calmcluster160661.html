calm

noun
mental composure or steadiness
1.0 4.00/m
Pronunciation: / k…ëm / (AmE), / k…ëÀêm / (BrE)
a state or period of peacefulness, quiet, or lack of disturbance (in a person or environment)

Examples

A sense of calm
During the emergency, his calm was reassuring to everyone around him.
Even in the face of criticism, she maintained her calm and addressed the issue professionally.
The coach's calm under pressure helped inspire confidence in the team.
After the storm passed, a profound calm settled over the sea.
In the early morning calm, not a ripple disturbed the surface of the lake.
The sailors welcomed the calm, taking the opportunity to repair their rigging.
