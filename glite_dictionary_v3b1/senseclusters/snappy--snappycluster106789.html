snappy

adjective
irritable or curt in speech
Pronunciation: / ˈsnæpi / (AmE), / ˈsnæpi / (BrE)
tending to speak in a quick, irritable, or curt manner; easily annoyed or impatient in speech.

Examples

Snappy reply
His snappy comments made the meeting tense and uncomfortable.
Please don't get snappy with me; I was only trying to help.
She gave a snappy reply when asked about the delays in the project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness, anger, or other strong emotions
  • synonym: irritable - easily annoyed or made angry
