tradesperson

noun
skilled manual worker
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪdzˌpɜrsən / (AmE), / ˈtreɪdzˌpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who works in a skilled manual trade, especially in construction, repairs, or similar fields

Examples

A tradesperson fixes pipes
We called a tradesperson to repair the broken tiles in our bathroom.
Becoming a qualified tradesperson requires years of training and experience.
The building project needed every type of tradesperson, from electricians to carpenters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artisan - a worker skilled in a traditional craft
  • synonym: craftsperson - a person skilled in a particular craft or manual trade
  • antonym: laborer - a person doing unskilled physical work
