English rose

noun
traditional English beauty (woman)
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ roʊz / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ rəʊz / (BrE)
a woman regarded as a classic example of English beauty, typically having a fair complexion and delicate features

She was the very image of an English rose, with her fair skin and gentle manner.
