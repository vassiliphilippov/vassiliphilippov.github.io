raise

noun
increase in salary or wages
1.0
Pronunciation: / reɪz / (AmE), / reɪz / (BrE)
an increase in the amount of money you earn at your job

Examples

A 5% raise
After working hard all year, she finally received a 5% raise.
Employees were delighted when the company announced a raise across all departments.
He plans to use his raise to save for a new apartment.
