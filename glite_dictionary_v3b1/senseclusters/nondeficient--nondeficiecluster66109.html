nondeficient

adjective
not lacking
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑndɪˈfɪʃənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒndɪˈfɪʃənt / (BrE)
having enough of a particular quality or element; not lacking or deficient.

Examples

Nondeficient in vitamin
The patient was found to be nondeficient in vitamin D after the blood test.
A nondeficient supply of nutrients is essential for healthy plant growth.
Her diet was carefully planned to remain nondeficient in all essential minerals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deficient - lacking in a necessary quality or element
  • synonym: sufficient - having enough of something; adequate
