Synonyms

  • urban planning
    3.9 0.73/m
    urban planning is the practical work of designing streets and buildings and public spaces, while urbanism studies how cities form and function.
  • city planning
    3.3 0.32/m
    city planning focuses on managing a city's physical layout and projects, while urbanism studies the wider growth, form, and social life of cities.
  • town planning
    3.6 0.11/m
    town planning arranges local development and services in smaller towns, while urbanism looks at how whole cities grow and work.
