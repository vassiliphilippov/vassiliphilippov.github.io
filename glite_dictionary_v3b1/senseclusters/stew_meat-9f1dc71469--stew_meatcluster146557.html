stew meat

noun
meat for slow-cooked stews
2.4 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / stuÀê Àåmit / (AmE), / stjuÀê ÀåmiÀêt / (BrE)
Meat cut into small pieces, typically from tougher cuts, intended for cooking slowly in a stew to make it tender.

Examples

Pre-cut stew meat
Many supermarkets sell pre-cut stew meat specially labeled for slow cooking.
She bought a pound of stew meat to make beef stew for dinner.
The recipe calls for stew meat to be browned before adding vegetables and broth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stew - a dish of meat and vegetables cooked slowly in liquid
