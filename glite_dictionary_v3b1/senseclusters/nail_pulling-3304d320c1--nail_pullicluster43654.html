nail pulling

noun
torture by removal of nails
9.9
Pronunciation: / neɪl ˈpʊlɪŋ / (AmE), / neɪl ˈpʊlɪŋ / (BrE)
A form of torture in which a person's fingernails or toenails are forcibly removed.

Examples

Nail pulling torture
Reports detailed acts of torture such as nail pulling and electrocution.
Survivors recounted the horrors of nail pulling during interrogations.
The regime was notorious for using nail pulling as a method of extracting confessions from prisoners.
