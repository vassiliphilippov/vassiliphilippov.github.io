notch

noun
display cutout for camera/sensors
5.6 0.96/m
Pronunciation: / nt83 / (AmE), / n52t83 / (BrE)
(in smartphones and other mobile devices) a cutout at the top of a screen that houses the front-facing camera and sensors.

Examples

Phone notch
The new phone's notch houses the selfie camera and several proximity sensors along the top edge.
Many users prefer phones without a notch, choosing full-screen displays or tiny punch-hole cameras instead.
App developers must design around different notches to ensure important UI elements are not obscured.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cutout - an opening or hollowed area in a surface
  • synonym: screen cutout - a portion removed from a display to make room for cameras or sensors
