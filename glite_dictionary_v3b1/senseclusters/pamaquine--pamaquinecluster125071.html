pamaquine

noun
malaria treatment drug
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpæməˌkwiːn / (AmE), / ˈpæməˌkwiːn / (BrE)
a synthetic drug used to treat malaria, especially relapsing types caused by certain parasites

Examples

pamaquine drug
The doctor prescribed pamaquine to help eliminate the malaria infection.
Researchers studied the effectiveness of pamaquine against relapsing malaria.
Some patients may experience side effects when taking pamaquine for treatment.
