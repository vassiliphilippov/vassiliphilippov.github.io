star chart

noun
map of star positions in the sky
5.2
Pronunciation: / stɑr tʃɑrt / (AmE), / stɑː tʃɑːt / (BrE)
a map showing the positions of stars and other celestial objects in a particular region of the sky

Examples

A star chart map
Before telescopes, sailors relied on a star chart for celestial navigation.
She hung a large star chart on her wall to learn more about the constellations.
The amateur astronomer used a star chart to find the Milky Way in the night sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: constellation chart - chart showing the groupings of stars into constellations
  • synonym: sky map - chart showing the appearance of the night sky from a specific location at a specific time
