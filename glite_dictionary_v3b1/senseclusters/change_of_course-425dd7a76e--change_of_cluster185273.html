change of course

noun
a shift in direction
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪndʒ əv kɔrs / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪndʒ əv kɔːs / (BrE)
an act of changing the direction in which you are moving, either physically or in a figurative sense

Examples

A sudden change of course
A sudden change of course caused confusion among the passengers.
After much debate, the company made a change of course in its marketing strategy.
The captain ordered a change of course to avoid the incoming storm.
