chromoly

adjective
containing chromium-molybdenum steel
8.4
Pronunciation: / kroʊˈmɑːli / (AmE), / krəʊˈmɒli / (BrE)
made from or containing chromium-molybdenum steel, a strong and heat-treatable alloy used in manufacturing.

Examples

Chromoly steel frame
The bicycle frame is made from chromoly for extra strength and durability.
Many high-performance car parts use chromoly because of its resistance to stress and heat.
He prefers chromoly tubing over aluminum for building custom motorcycles.
