consideringly

adverb
in a kind manner
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɪdərɪŋli / (AmE), / kənˈsɪdərɪŋli / (BrE)
in a thoughtful or considerate manner; showing care or careful reflection

Examples

Listen consideringly
He listened consideringly to his friend's concerns before offering advice.
The teacher spoke consideringly to the student who was struggling with the assignment.
She always acts consideringly toward her colleagues, making the workplace more pleasant.
She paused consideringly before answering the difficult question.
The manager listened consideringly to each team member's suggestion.
He gazed consideringly at the painting, trying to understand its meaning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carelessly - without giving enough attention or thought
  • synonym: kindly - in a kind or gentle manner
  • antonym: selfishly - in a way that only considers one's own needs or desires
  • synonym: thoughtfully - in a way that shows careful consideration or concern for others
