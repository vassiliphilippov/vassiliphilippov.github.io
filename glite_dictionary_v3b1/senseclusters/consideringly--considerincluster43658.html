consideringly

adverb
in a kind manner
5.8
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɪdərɪŋli / (AmE), / kənˈsɪdərɪŋli / (BrE)

Examples

She paused consideringly before answering the difficult question.
The manager listened consideringly to each team member's suggestion.
He gazed consideringly at the painting, trying to understand its meaning.
