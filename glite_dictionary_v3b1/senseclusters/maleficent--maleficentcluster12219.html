maleficent

adjective
having a harmful or evil intent
Pronunciation: / məˈlɛfəsənt / (AmE), / məˈlɛfɪsənt / (BrE)
having or showing a harmful or evil intent or effect

Examples

Maleficent stare
He regarded her with a maleficent stare that sent chills down her spine.
Legends spoke of a maleficent spirit haunting the dark forest.
The villain's maleficent schemes threatened the entire kingdom.

Related Terms

  • antonym: benevolent - well meaning and kindly
  • synonym: malevolent - having or showing a wish to do evil to others
