telekinetically

adverb
by telekinesis
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛləkɪˈnɛtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪkɪˈnɛtɪkli / (BrE)

Examples

To move telekinetically

In the movie, the villain telekinetically lifted objects to block the hero's path.
She claimed she could telekinetically bend spoons with just her mind.
