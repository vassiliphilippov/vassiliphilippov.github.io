agriculturist

noun
person skilled in agriculture
Pronunciation: / ˌæɡ.rɪˈkʌl.tʃɚ.ɪst / (AmE), / ˌæɡ.rɪˈkʌl.tʃə.rɪst / (BrE)
A person who is skilled in or engaged in the science, art, or practice of agriculture, especially in the cultivation of the soil.

Examples

A agriculturist studies soil
As a trained agriculturist, she developed new methods of sustainable farming.
Many agriculturists are researching ways to adapt to climate change.
The agriculturist advised local farmers on crop rotation to improve their yields.

Related Terms

  • synonym: farmer - a person who owns or manages a farm
