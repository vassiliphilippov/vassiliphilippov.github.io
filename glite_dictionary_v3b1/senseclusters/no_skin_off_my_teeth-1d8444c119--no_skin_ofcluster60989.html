no skin off my teeth

x
not a problem for me; doesn't affect me
Pronunciation: / noʊ skɪn ɑf maɪ tiθ / (AmE), / nəʊ skɪn ɒf maɪ tiːθ / (BrE)
used to say that something does not affect or trouble one at all; not a problem or inconvenience

Examples

It's no skin off my teeth
If they don't listen to my advice, it's no skin off my teeth.
It's no skin off my teeth if they decide to go somewhere else.
You can change the date if you want; it's no skin off my teeth.
