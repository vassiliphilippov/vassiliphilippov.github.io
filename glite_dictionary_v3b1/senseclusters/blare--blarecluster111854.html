blare

noun
loud harsh noise
4.0
Pronunciation: / blɛr / (AmE), / bleə / (BrE)
a loud, harsh, and often unpleasant noise

Examples

The blare of horns
She woke to the blare of her alarm clock early in the morning.
The blare from the loudspeakers made it hard to concentrate.
The blare of horns filled the busy street during rush hour.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blast - a sudden loud sound or noise
  • synonym: din - a loud, unpleasant, and prolonged noise
  • antonym: murmur - a soft, low, and continuous sound
