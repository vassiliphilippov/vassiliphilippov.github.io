abbess

noun
female head of a convent
Pronunciation: / ˈæb.əs / (AmE), / ˈæb.ɪs / (BrE)
the woman who is the head of a convent of nuns

Examples

The abbess leads
After years of dedicated service, she was elected abbess by her fellow nuns.
The abbess ensured the daily routines ran smoothly within the abbey.
The abbess welcomed the visitors to the convent with warmth and grace.

  • antonym: abbot - the man who is the head of a monastery
