saddle blanket

noun
blanket placed under a horse's saddle
Pronunciation: / ˈsædəl ˌblæŋkət / (AmE), / ˈsædəl ˌblaŋkɪt / (BrE)
A thick cloth or pad placed under a saddle to protect a horse's back and absorb sweat.

Examples

A saddle blanket pad
Many riders choose a colorful saddle blanket to match their tack.
She placed a thick saddle blanket on the horse before putting on the saddle.
The saddle blanket soaked up much of the horse's sweat during the long ride.

Related Terms

  • synonym: saddle pad - a pad placed under a saddle for similar purpose
