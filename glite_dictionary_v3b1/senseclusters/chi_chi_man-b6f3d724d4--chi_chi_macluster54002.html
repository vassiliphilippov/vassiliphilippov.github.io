chi chi man

noun
flamboyant or effeminate man (often derogatory)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiː ˌtʃiː mæn / (AmE), / ˈtʃiː ˌtʃiː man / (BrE)
(slang, often derogatory) A flamboyant or effeminate man, especially one perceived as affected; sometimes used as an insult.
