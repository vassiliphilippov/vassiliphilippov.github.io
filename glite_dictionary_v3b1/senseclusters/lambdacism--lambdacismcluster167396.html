lambdacism

noun
speech disorder with /l/ sounds
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlæm.dəˌsɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈlam.də.sɪz.əm / (BrE)
a speech defect involving excessive or atypical pronunciation of the sound /l/

Examples

Lambdacism speech defect
Children affected by lambdacism may substitute other sounds for /l/ or exaggerate its use.
Her mild lambdacism made it challenging to pronounce certain words clearly.
The speech therapist diagnosed his difficulty with the /l/ sound as lambdacism.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rhotacism - speech defect involving the excessive or unusual pronunciation of the /r/ sound
