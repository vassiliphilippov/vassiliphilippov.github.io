patent trolling

noun
abusive enforcement of patents for profit
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpætnt ˌtroʊlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpætnt ˌtrəʊlɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of obtaining or enforcing patents primarily to demand licensing fees or settlements, often without intending to produce or market the patented invention
