swilling

noun
rapid, greedy drinking of liquid
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈswɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of drinking large amounts of liquid, especially alcohol, quickly and greedily.

Examples

Swilling whiskey
After hours of swilling whiskey, they finally left the bar.
His constant swilling of water worried the doctor.
The swilling of beer at the festival lasted well into the night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gulping - drinking or swallowing something quickly and eagerly
