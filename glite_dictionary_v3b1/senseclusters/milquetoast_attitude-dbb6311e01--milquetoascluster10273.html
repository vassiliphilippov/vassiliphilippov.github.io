milquetoast attitude

noun
timid, unassertive attitude
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlkˌtoʊst ˈætɪˌtud / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkˌtəʊst ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
A way of thinking or behaving that is extremely timid, unassertive, or lacking in confidence.

Examples

Milquetoast attitude at work
Because of his milquetoast attitude, he rarely took risks at work.
Her milquetoast attitude made it difficult for her to advocate for herself during meetings.
Management was frustrated by the team’s milquetoast attitude toward new challenges.

  • synonym: timid - showing a lack of courage or self-confidence
