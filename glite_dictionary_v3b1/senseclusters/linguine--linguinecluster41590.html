linguine

noun
type of flat, narrow pasta
5.3
Pronunciation: / lɪŋˈɡwiːni / (AmE), / lɪŋˈɡwiːni / (BrE)
Pasta in long, slender, flat strips, typically used in Italian cuisine.

Examples

A plate of linguine
I boiled some linguine and tossed it with pesto for a quick dinner.
She ordered the linguine with clam sauce at her favorite Italian restaurant.
The recipe calls for fresh linguine instead of spaghetti for a richer texture.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: fettuccine - pasta in long, flat and wider strips
  • synonym: spaghetti - pasta in long, thin, round strands
