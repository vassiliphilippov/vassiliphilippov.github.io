big spender

noun
person who spends money extravagantly
Pronunciation: / bɪɡ ˈspɛndɚ / (AmE), / bɪɡ ˈspɛndə /; / bɪɡ ˈspendə / (BrE)
a person who spends a lot of money, often freely or in a showy or extravagant way

Examples

Being a big spender can impress some people, but it may also lead to financial trouble.
He's always been a big spender when it comes to holidays and entertainment.
The restaurant attracts big spenders looking for a luxurious dining experience.
