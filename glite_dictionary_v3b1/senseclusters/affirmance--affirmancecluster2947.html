affirmance

noun
confirmation of decision
9.7 0.15/m
Pronunciation: / …ôÀàf…úrm…ôns / (AmE), / …ôÀàf…úÀêm…ôns / (BrE)
the act of confirming or upholding a decision, especially by a higher court

Examples

Court affirmance
The judge's affirmance of the lower court's ruling meant the sentence would stand.
After reviewing the evidence, the appellate court issued an affirmance of the original decision.
The lawyer was pleased with the affirmance because it validated her arguments.

Synonyms
