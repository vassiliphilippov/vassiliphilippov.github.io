carelessly

adverb
in a rakish or jaunty manner
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛr.ləs.li / (AmE), / ˈkɛə.ləs.li / (BrE)
in a stylishly unconventional or jaunty manner; with an air of easy confidence and disregard for convention

Examples

Carelessly wear a scarf
He tossed his scarf carelessly over his shoulder with a confident smile.
She wore her hat carelessly at a slight angle, drawing everyone's attention.
The artist carelessly arranged his brushes on the table, as if inspired by the chaos.
