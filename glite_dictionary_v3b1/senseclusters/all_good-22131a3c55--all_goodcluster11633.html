all good

adjective
everything is fine; no problem
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɔl ɡʊd / (AmE), / ɔːl ɡʊd / (BrE)
used to say that everything is fine or acceptable; there are no problems or worries

Examples

It's all good
After a quick check, the mechanic said it's all good.
Don't worry about the misunderstanding; it's all good now.
Even though we were late, the teacher told us it was all good.

Related Terms

  • synonym: no problem - used to indicate that something is not a problem or cause for concern
