weedy

adjective
very thin and weak-looking
5.4 0.10/m
Pronunciation: / Ààwidi / (AmE), / ÀàwiÀêdi / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) very thin and physically weak in appearance; lacking flesh or muscle.

Examples

A weedy boy
As a teenager he was rather weedy and lacked confidence in sports.
She described herself as a weedy child who preferred books to outdoor games.
The weedy boy struggled to lift the heavy backpack.

Synonyms

  • scrawny
    4.2 1.79/m
    scrawny looks very thin and unhealthy with little muscle, while weedy describes a thin person who seems weak and frail.
  • scraggy
    7.9 0.02/m
    scraggy is thin and rough in appearance, often with ragged hair or clothes, while weedy means thin and weak.
  • bony
    2.5 3.15/m
    bony shows clear, sharp bones and little flesh, while weedy means thin and lacking muscle rather than just visible bones.
  • skinny
    1.3 13/m
    skinny is simply very thin and often used in casual talk, while weedy focuses on weakness and lacking muscle.
  • underweight
    3.1 0.75/m
    underweight describes a body weighing below a healthy standard, while weedy describes looking thin and physically weak.

Related Terms

  • antonym: robust - strong and healthy
  • synonym: scrawny - very thin and bony
  • synonym: skinny - very thin, often unattractively so
