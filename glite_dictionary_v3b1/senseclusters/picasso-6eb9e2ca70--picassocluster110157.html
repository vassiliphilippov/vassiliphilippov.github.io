Picasso

noun
exceptionally creative person
Pronunciation: / pɪˈkɑːsoʊ / (AmE), / pɪˈkæsəʊ / (BrE)
a person who is exceptionally original or creative in art or design, especially used figuratively to compare someone to the famous artist Pablo Picasso

Examples

A real Picasso
Everyone in the class called her a Picasso because of her unique painting style.
He became the Picasso of digital design with his groundbreaking graphics.
After seeing his sculptures, the critics hailed him as a modern Picasso.

Related Terms

  • antonym: amateur - a person who engages in art or design as a hobby, not professionally or at a high level
  • synonym: genius - a person with exceptional creative or intellectual ability
  • synonym: master - an expert or highly skilled artist
