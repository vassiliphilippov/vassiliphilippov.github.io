Picasso

noun
exceptionally creative person
5.4
Pronunciation: /pɪ.ˈkɑː.soʊ/ /; / /pɪ.ˈkæs.oʊ/ (AmE), /pɪ.ˈkɑː.səʊ/ /; / /pɪ.ˈkæs.əʊ/ (BrE)
