assaulter

noun
person who attacks
Pronunciation: / əˈsɔltər / (AmE), / əˈsɔːltə / (BrE)
A person who attacks someone physically or violently.

Examples

The assaulter attacked
The assaulter was quickly apprehended after the incident in the park.
The police were able to identify the assaulter from security camera footage.
Victims of the attack described the assaulter as tall and wearing a black jacket.
