testaceous

adjective
having a hard shell or outer covering
Pronunciation: / tɛˈsteɪʃəs / (AmE), / tɛˈsteɪsɪəs / (BrE)
relating to or possessing a hard shell or outer covering, especially in reference to animals or seeds.

Examples

Testaceous shell structure
Some mollusks possess a testaceous structure that separates them from other soft-bodied animals.
The biologist described several testaceous seeds she found in the tropical rainforest.
Turtles are testaceous creatures, protected by their distinctive shells.
