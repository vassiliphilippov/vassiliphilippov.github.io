luxuriousness

noun
quality of great comfort and luxury
2.3
Pronunciation: / lʌgˈʒʊriəsnəs / (AmE), / lʌgˈzjʊəriəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely comfortable, elegant, or rich in a way that involves great expense; wealth as shown by sumptuous living

Examples

The luxuriousness of fabrics
He admired the luxuriousness of the fabrics and furnishings in the designer's home.
Some people prefer the luxuriousness of fine dining, while others enjoy a more rustic experience.
The luxuriousness of the hotel made her feel like royalty during her holiday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: luxury - great comfort, especially as provided by expensive and beautiful things
  • synonym: opulence - great wealth or luxuriousness
  • antonym: simplicity - the quality of being plain or uncomplicated
