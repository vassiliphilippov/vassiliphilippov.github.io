Swedenborgian

adjective
of Swedenborg's teachings
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌswiːdənˈbɔːrɡiən / (AmE), / ˌswiːdənˈbɔːɡiən / (BrE)
relating to Emanuel Swedenborg, his writings, or the religious movement based on his teachings

Examples

Swedenborgian beliefs
The church is known for its Swedenborgian architecture and spiritual philosophy.
She became interested in Swedenborgian beliefs after reading about Emanuel Swedenborg's visions.
A Swedenborgian perspective often emphasizes the symbolic meaning of biblical texts.
