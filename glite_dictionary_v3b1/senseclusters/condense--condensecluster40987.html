condense

verb
make concise; shorten
3.7
Pronunciation: / kənˈdɛns / (AmE), / kənˈdɛns / (BrE)
to make something shorter or more concise by removing unimportant details

Examples

To condense a text
Can you condense your report so it fits on one page?
The author managed to condense a complex theory into just a few paragraphs.
The editor asked me to condense the introduction by removing redundant information.

Related Terms

  • antonym: expand - make something longer or more detailed
  • synonym: summarize - state the main points briefly
