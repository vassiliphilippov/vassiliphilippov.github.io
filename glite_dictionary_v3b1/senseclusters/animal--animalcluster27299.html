animal

noun
a living organism capable of movement
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈænəməl / (AmE), / ˈænɪməl / (BrE)
A living organism that feeds on organic matter, typically has specialized sense organs and nervous system, and is capable of voluntary movement.

Examples

Wild animal
Children are often fascinated by every kind of animal at the zoo.
The biologist studied how each animal in the ecosystem interacted with its environment.
The dog is an animal that has been domesticated for thousands of years.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plant - a living organism lacking the power of voluntary movement
