Qing

proper noun
the last imperial dynasty of China (1644–1912)
Pronunciation: / tʃɪŋ / (AmE), / tʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the last imperial dynasty of China, ruling from 1644 to 1912, established by the Manchu people and overthrown by revolutionaries

Examples

The Qing dynasty
After the fall of the Qing, China transitioned to a republic.
Many historical sites in Beijing date back to the Qing era.
The Qing Dynasty was marked by territorial expansion and cultural development.
