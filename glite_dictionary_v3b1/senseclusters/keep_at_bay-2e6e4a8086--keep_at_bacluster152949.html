Synonyms

  • ward off
    4.4 2/m
    ward off stops something unpleasant from coming close quickly, while keep at bay means holding it at a safe distance over time.
  • fend off
    3.9 2/m
    fend off is actively defending against an attacker or problem, while keep at bay means preventing it from getting too close.
  • repel
    4.2 1.0/m
    repel forces someone or something back, often by strength or chemicals, while keep at bay means keeping the threat away or under control.
  • keep away
    1.6 9/m
    keep away tells someone to stay away or prevents a thing from coming near, while keep at bay implies ongoing control.
