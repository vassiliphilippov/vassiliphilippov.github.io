intervertebral

adjective
between the vertebrae
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈvɜrtəbrəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈvɜːtɪbrəl / (BrE)
Situated or occurring between the vertebrae of the spine.

Examples

Intervertebral discs
Herniated intervertebral discs can cause significant back pain and nerve issues.
Regular exercise may help maintain healthy intervertebral spaces in the spine.
The doctor explained that the intervertebral discs help cushion the bones of the spine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vertebral - relating to the vertebrae or the spinal column
