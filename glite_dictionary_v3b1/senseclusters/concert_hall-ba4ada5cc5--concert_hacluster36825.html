concert hall

noun
large venue for musical performances
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑnsɚt hɔl / (AmE), / ˈkɒnsət hɔːl / (BrE)
A large building or room designed for the performance of concerts, especially of classical music.

Examples

A concert hall building
Acoustics in this concert hall are renowned among musicians and audiences alike.
Every seat in the concert hall was filled for the pianist’s debut.
The orchestra will be performing at the new concert hall downtown next weekend.
