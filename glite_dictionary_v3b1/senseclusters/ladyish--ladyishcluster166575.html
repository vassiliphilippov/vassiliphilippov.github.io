ladyish

adjective
like a refined woman
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪdiɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈleɪdiɪʃ / (BrE)
having qualities, appearance, or manners considered typical of a refined or proper woman

Examples

Ladyish behavior
Her ladyish behavior impressed everyone at the afternoon tea.
He commented on her ladyish handwriting, which was neat and elegant.
The actress wore a ladyish hat that completed her vintage look.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ladylike - having the polite or refined qualities expected of a woman
  • antonym: unladylike - not having the qualities or manners expected of a refined woman
