spike heath

noun
small evergreen shrub of southern Europe and Asia Minor
9.9
Pronunciation: / spaɪk hiːθ / (AmE), / spaɪk hiːθ / (BrE)
a small, evergreen, mat-forming shrub of southern Europe and Asia Minor, with stiff stems and terminal clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers

Examples
