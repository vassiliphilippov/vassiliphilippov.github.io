gentile

adjective
relating to non-Jewish people
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛn.taɪl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛn.taɪl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of people who are not Jewish.

Examples

Gentile culture
Her research focuses on gentile attitudes toward Judaism in medieval Europe.
Some ancient laws distinguished between gentile and Jewish customs.
The gentile communities in the region celebrated different festivals from their Jewish neighbors.
