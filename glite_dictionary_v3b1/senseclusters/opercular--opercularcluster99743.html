opercular

adjective
of the brain's operculum
Pronunciation: / oʊˈpɜːrkjələr / (AmE), / əʊˈpɜːkjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to the operculum, especially the cortical regions of the brain that cover the insula.

Examples

Opercular cortex
The surgeon carefully avoided the opercular cortex during the procedure.
Damage to the opercular area can affect language and motor functions.
Researchers studied the opercular regions to understand their role in sensory processing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insular - relating to the insula region of the brain
