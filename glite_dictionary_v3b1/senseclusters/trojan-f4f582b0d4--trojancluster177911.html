Trojan

noun
malicious software disguised as legitimate
6.8 0.17/m
Pronunciation: / c8tro8ad9259n / (AmE), / c8tr598ad9259n / (BrE)
a type of malicious software that disguises itself as legitimate or useful software to trick a user into installing it, allowing attackers hidden access, control, or data theft.

Examples

A Trojan virus
The security scan detected a Trojan disguised as a software updater on his laptop.
After installing the free program, her bank account was emptied by a hidden Trojan.
IT staff isolated the infected workstation before the Trojans could spread to other systems.
