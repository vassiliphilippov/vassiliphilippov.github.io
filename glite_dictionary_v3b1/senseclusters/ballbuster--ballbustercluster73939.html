ballbuster

noun
tough, demanding person
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔlˌbʌstər / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlˌbʌstə / (BrE)
a person, especially a woman, who is extremely tough, demanding, or difficult to deal with

Examples

A tough ballbuster boss
Everyone in the department knows that their new manager is a real ballbuster.
He has a reputation as a ballbuster who never accepts excuses from his team.
Some people admire her for being a ballbuster, while others find her style too harsh.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pushover - a person who is easy to influence or defeat
  • synonym: taskmaster - a person who makes others work very hard
