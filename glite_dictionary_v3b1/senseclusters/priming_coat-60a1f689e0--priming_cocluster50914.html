priming coat

noun
first layer of paint or preparation
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪmɪŋ koʊt / (AmE), / ˈpraɪmɪŋ kəʊt / (BrE)
the first layer of paint or size applied to a surface to prepare it for subsequent coats

Examples

A priming coat layer
Before applying the final color, make sure a priming coat is spread evenly over the entire surface.
She waited for the priming coat to dry before starting on the two finishing layers.
The purpose of a priming coat is to seal the material and ensure better adhesion of the topcoat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: primer - a preparatory coating put on materials before painting
