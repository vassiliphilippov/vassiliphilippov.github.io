mouche

noun
small blemish
9.9
Pronunciation: / muːʃ / (AmE), / muːʃ / (BrE)
a small spot, blot, or blemish on a surface, especially as an imperfection (archaic)

Examples

He noticed a tiny mouche on the otherwise perfect surface of the antique table.
