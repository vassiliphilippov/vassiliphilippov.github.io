biopharmaceutical

noun
drug from living cells
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪoʊˌfɑrməˈsuːtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌbaɪəʊˌfɑːməˈsjuːtɪkəl / (BrE)
a drug or medical product produced using living cells or biological processes

Examples

A new biopharmaceutical
The new biopharmaceutical offers hope for patients with rare genetic disorders.
Researchers are testing a biopharmaceutical that could improve immune system response.
Many modern treatments for cancer are based on biopharmaceutical innovations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biologic - a drug made from living organisms or their products
  • antonym: chemical drug - a drug produced by chemical synthesis rather than biological processes
  • synonym: pharmaceutical - a drug or medicine used to treat disease
