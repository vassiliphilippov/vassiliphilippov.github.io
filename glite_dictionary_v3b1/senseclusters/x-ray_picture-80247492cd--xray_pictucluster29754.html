X-ray picture

noun
photographic image made using X rays
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈeks.reɪ ˈpɪk.tʃər/ (AmE), /ˈeks.reɪ ˈpɪk.tʃə/ (BrE)
a photographic image produced by X rays and used especially in medical diagnosis

Examples

A X-ray picture
Before the surgery, they reviewed several X-ray pictures to plan the procedure.
Her chest X-ray picture revealed signs of pneumonia.
The doctor examined the X-ray picture to look for any broken bones.
