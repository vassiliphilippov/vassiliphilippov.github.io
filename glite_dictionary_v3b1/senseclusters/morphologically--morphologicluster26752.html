morphologically

adverb
in terms of form or structure
Pronunciation: / ˌmɔrfəˈlɑdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˌmɔːfəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to the form or structure of something, especially in biology or linguistics.

Examples

Classified morphologically
The fossils were classified morphologically, based on their physical characteristics.
The two species are morphologically similar but belong to different genera.
Words in this language are morphologically complex, often formed by adding several affixes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: functionally - in a functional manner; with regard to function
  • synonym: structurally - in a structural manner; with regard to structure
