magnifying glass

noun
search icon in digital interfaces
Pronunciation: / ˈmæɡ.nɪˌfaɪ.ɪŋ ɡlæs / (AmE), / ˈmæɡ.nɪˌfaɪ.ɪŋ ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
a small graphical icon, usually shaped like a magnifying glass, used in websites and apps to open or start a search function.

Examples

Magnifying glass icon
Click the magnifying glass icon in the top-right corner to search the site.
She tapped the magnifying glass on her phone to find the contact quickly.
Developers used a small magnifying glass as the button for the app's search feature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: search button - a clickable control that activates a search, often represented by a magnifying-glass icon
  • synonym: search icon - a pictogram used in user interfaces to represent the search function
