noncorporate

adjective
not a corporation
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈkɔːrpərət / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈkɔːpərət / (BrE)
not having the legal status of a corporation or not connected to a corporation

Examples

Many freelancers prefer to remain noncorporate to keep their independence.
The event was open to both noncorporate individuals and company representatives.
