Treasury Secretary

noun
government official in charge of the Treasury
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛʒəri səˌkrɛtɛri / (AmE), / ˈtrɛʒəri səˈkrɛtəri /; / ˈtrɛʒəri səˌkrɛtəri / (BrE)
the government official in charge of the Treasury Department, especially in the United States, responsible for managing government finances and economic policy
