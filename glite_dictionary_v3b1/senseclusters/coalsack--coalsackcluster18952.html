coalsack

noun
dark sky patch; coal bag
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊlˌsæk / (AmE), / ˈkəʊlˌsæk / (BrE)
a dark patch in the night sky caused by interstellar dust blocking the light of stars, or a sack used for carrying coal

Examples

The coalsack nebula
The Southern Cross is easily seen near the coalsack in the night sky.
Astronomers studied the coalsack to learn more about interstellar dust clouds.
He carried the heavy coalsack from the shed to the fireplace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coal bag - a bag used for carrying coal
  • synonym: nebula - a cloud of gas and dust in space
