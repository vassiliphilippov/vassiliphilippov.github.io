yak's milk

noun
milk from a yak
2.9
Pronunciation: / jæks mɪlk / (AmE), / jæks mɪlk / (BrE)
the milk obtained from a yak, often used as a beverage or to make cheese and butter, especially in regions of Central Asia and the Himalayas

Examples

Yak's milk drink
Locals in Tibet often drink yak's milk for its rich nutrients and creamy flavor.
She tried yak's milk for the first time during her trip to Nepal.
The cheese made from yak's milk has a unique taste unlike any other dairy product.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cow's milk - milk obtained from cows, the most common type of milk for human consumption
