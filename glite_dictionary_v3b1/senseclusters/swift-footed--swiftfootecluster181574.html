swift-footed

adjective
moving quickly on foot
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪft ˌfʊtɪd / (AmE), / ˈswɪft ˌfʊtɪd / (BrE)
Able to move very quickly on foot; having fast-moving feet.

Examples

A swift-footed runner
Despite his age, the swift-footed dog chased the ball with remarkable energy.
Legends often speak of swift-footed messengers who could deliver news across vast distances.
The swift-footed athlete outran all her competitors in the final stretch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fleet-footed - able to run very fast
