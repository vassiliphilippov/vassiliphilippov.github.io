Examples

Synonyms

  • fast-footed
    4.8 0.01/m
    fast-footed describes someone whose feet move very quickly in running or sport, while swift-footed means moving quickly on foot more generally.
  • nimble
    4.4 1.56/m
    nimble shows quick, light movement and a fast response in action, while swift-footed focuses especially on fast-moving feet.
  • agile
    3.7 1.62/m
    agile highlights quick, controlled changes of direction and balance in movement, while swift-footed emphasizes pure foot speed.
  • light-footed
    4.2 0.05/m
    light-footed means moving lightly and gracefully with quick steps, while swift-footed stresses high speed of the feet.
  • light on one's feet
    4.8 0.11/m
    light on one's feet describes a person who moves easily and with grace, while swift-footed stresses fast movement with the feet.
  • quick
    1.0 10/m
    quick is a general word for moving or happening fast in many ways, while swift-footed refers specifically to fast movement on the feet.
