criminalize

verb
declare illegal; treat as criminal
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪm.ə.nəl.aɪz /; / ˈkrɪmɪnəˌlaɪz /; / ˈkrɪm.ə.nəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈkrɪm.ɪ.nə.laɪz /; / ˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪz / (BrE)
