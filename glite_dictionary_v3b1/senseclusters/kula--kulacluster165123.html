kula

noun
South Asian family group
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːlə / (AmE), / ˈkuːlə / (BrE)
a family, clan, or social group, especially in South Asian or Hindu contexts

Examples

A large kula
Each kula in the village had its own unique traditions and customs.
The festival was celebrated by every kula to honor their ancestors.
Membership in a kula often determined one's social status in the community.

Related Terms
