autokinetic

adjective
self-generated motion
Pronunciation: / ˌɔtoʊkɪˈnɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəʊkɪˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
describing movement that is generated by an organism or object itself, not by an external force

Examples

Autokinetic movement
The autokinetic behavior of the bacteria allowed them to move toward nutrients without outside influence.
Scientists studied the autokinetic properties of the new material, which could change shape on its own.
The drone's autokinetic system enabled it to navigate obstacles without remote control.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automatic - working by itself with little or no direct human control
  • antonym: externally driven - moved or controlled by an outside force
  • synonym: self-propelled - able to move by its own power
