Erythrina variegata

noun
ornamental Asian and Australian tree with scarlet flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˌrɪθrɪnə ˌværiˈɡeɪtə / (AmE), / ɪˌrɪθrɪnə ˌværɪˈɡɑːtə / (BrE)
a small to medium-sized thorny tree native to tropical Asia and northern Australia, known for its dense clusters of scarlet or crimson flowers and black seeds
