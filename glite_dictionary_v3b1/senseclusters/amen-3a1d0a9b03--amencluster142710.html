Amen

proper noun
Egyptian god of air and breath
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑː.mɛn / (AmE), / ˈɑː.mɛn / (BrE)
An ancient Egyptian deity personifying air and breath, chiefly revered in Thebes.

Examples

Amen the god
Legends describe Amen as the creator god, embodying the vital force of air and breath.
Many temples in Thebes were dedicated to Amen, reflecting his importance in ancient Egyptian religion.
The priests of Amen played a significant role in the politics of the New Kingdom.
