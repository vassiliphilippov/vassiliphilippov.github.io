laddering

noun
stepwise arrangement
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈlædərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlædərɪŋ / (BrE)
a technique of arranging, analyzing, or exploring items or ideas in a stepwise or hierarchical order

Examples

Laddering technique in psychology
The psychologist used laddering to understand the underlying values behind the participants' choices.
In marketing research, laddering helps reveal the reasons why customers prefer certain products.
The teacher applied laddering to organize the lesson topics from basic to advanced.

  • synonym: hierarchy - a system in which items are ranked one above another
