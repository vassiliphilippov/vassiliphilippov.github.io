billable

adjective
can be charged
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪləbəl / (AmE), / ˈbɪləbəl / (BrE)
able to be charged to a client as work or hours for payment

Examples

Billable hours
Only billable hours are counted toward your monthly target.
The lawyer kept a detailed record of all billable activities for the client.
Some tasks are not considered billable and cannot be included in the invoice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chargeable - able to be charged or invoiced
  • antonym: nonbillable - not able to be charged to a client
