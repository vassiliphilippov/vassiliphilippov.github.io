CPA

noun
cost-per-action (marketing metric)
5.8 0.19/m
Pronunciation: / si pi e6a / (AmE), / sid0 pid0 e6a / (BrE)
In digital marketing, cost-per-action (CPA) is a pricing or performance metric where an advertiser pays for a specific user action (for example, a sign-up, download, or purchase).

Examples

CPA rate online
The marketer tracked conversions and calculated the CPA to optimize the budget.
They negotiated a lower CPA for new customer sign-ups during the holiday campaign.
When testing ad creatives, she focused on reducing the CPA without hurting conversion quality.
