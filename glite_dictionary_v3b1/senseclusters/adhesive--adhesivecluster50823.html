adhesive

noun
substance that sticks things together
1.2 3.0
Pronunciation: /əd.ˈhiː.sɪv/ /; / /æd.ˈhiː.sɪv/ (AmE), /əd.ˈhiː.sɪv/ /; / /æd.ˈhiː.sɪv/ (BrE)

Examples

Strong adhesive
