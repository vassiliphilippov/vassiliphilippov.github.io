adhesive

noun
substance that sticks things together
1.2
Pronunciation: / ədˈhisɪv / (AmE), / ədˈhiːsɪv / (BrE)
a substance that bonds surfaces together by sticking them to each other

Examples

Strong adhesive
She applied a thin layer of adhesive to the back of the wallpaper.
Spray adhesive is often used for arts and crafts projects.
Strong adhesive is needed to fix the broken vase.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cement - a binding substance used to join materials together
  • synonym: glue - a sticky substance used for joining things together
  • antonym: solvent - a substance that dissolves other substances (has opposite function to adhesive)
