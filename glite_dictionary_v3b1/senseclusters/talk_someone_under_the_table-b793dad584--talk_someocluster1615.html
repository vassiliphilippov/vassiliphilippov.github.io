talk someone under the table

verb
outtalk or outsmart someone in conversation
Pronunciation: / tɑk ˈsʌmwʌn ˌʌndɚ ðə ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / tɔːk ˈsʌmwʌn ˌʌndə ðə ˈteɪbl̩ / (BrE)
