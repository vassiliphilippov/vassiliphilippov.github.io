gynostegium

noun
crown of fused stamens in some plants
Pronunciation: / ˌgaɪnəˈstiːdʒiəm / (AmE), / ˌgʌɪnəˈstiːdʒɪəm / (BrE)
a crown-like structure formed by the fused stamens in plants of the genus Asclepias and related milkweeds

Examples

The gynostegium structure
Botanists study the gynostegium to understand pollination mechanisms in Asclepias.
Each gynostegium houses structures essential for pollen transfer.
The gynostegium is a key identifying feature in milkweed flowers.
