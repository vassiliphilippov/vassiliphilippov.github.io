glyphosate

noun
herbicide chemical
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlaɪfəˌseɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡlɪfəseɪt / (BrE)
a chemical compound widely used as a herbicide to kill unwanted plants and weeds

Examples

Glyphosate herbicide use
Many farmers use glyphosate to control weeds in their fields.
The use of glyphosate has increased in agriculture over the past decade.
Some people are concerned about the environmental impact of glyphosate.
