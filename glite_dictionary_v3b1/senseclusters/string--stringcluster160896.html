string

noun
linear sequence of things (esp. characters or words)
Pronunciation: / strɪŋ / (AmE), / strɪŋ / (BrE)
a sequence or series of things, such as numbers, events, or ideas, arranged in a particular order

Examples

A string of numbers
She couldn't remember the long string of numbers needed to access her account.
The DNA string consists of a unique combination of nucleotides.
To print your name, type the entire string into the program.
After a string of victories, the team finally lost a game.
She had a string of bad luck before things started to improve.
The company opened a string of new stores across the country last year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chain - a connected series of things
  • synonym: sequence - an ordered set of things or events
  • synonym: succession - a number of things following one after another
