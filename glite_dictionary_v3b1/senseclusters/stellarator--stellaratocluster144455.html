stellarator

noun
fusion plasma device
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈstel.ə.reɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈstel.ə.reɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a device that uses magnetic fields to confine hot plasma for nuclear fusion experiments

Examples

A stellarator device
The laboratory installed a new stellarator to advance their nuclear fusion research.
Unlike a tokamak, a stellarator can operate continuously without large electric currents.
Engineers are working to improve the efficiency of the stellarator for future energy production.
