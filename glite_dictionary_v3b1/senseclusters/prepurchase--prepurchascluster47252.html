prepurchase

noun
advance purchase
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈpɜːrtʃəs / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈpɜːtʃəs / (BrE)
a purchase made in advance of the usual time or before an event occurs

Examples

A prepurchase offer
The company offered a discount for prepurchase of tickets to the concert.
Customers who made a prepurchase received early access to the new product.
A prepurchase can help secure items that may sell out quickly during peak season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advance purchase - a purchase made ahead of time
  • antonym: postpurchase - an action or event occurring after a purchase
