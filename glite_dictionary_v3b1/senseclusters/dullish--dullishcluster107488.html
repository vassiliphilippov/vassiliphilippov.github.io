dullish

adjective
somewhat dull
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌlɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈdʌlɪʃ / (BrE)
somewhat dull or lacking in brightness, interest, or excitement

Examples

Dullish gray sky
The sky was a dullish gray, making the afternoon feel gloomy.
Her response was dullish, lacking the enthusiasm we expected.
The paint color looked dullish compared to the vibrant shade on the sample card.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boring - not interesting or exciting
  • degree higher: dull - not interesting, lively, or bright
  • antonym: lively - full of energy and excitement
