transplant

noun
medical operation moving organ/tissue
Pronunciation: / ˈtrænsˌplænt / (AmE), / ˈtræns.plɑːnt / (BrE)
a medical operation in which an organ or tissue is moved from one person or animal (the donor) to another (the recipient)

Examples

Heart transplant surgery
After the transplant, the patient's body gradually accepted the new kidney.
The hospital specializes in heart transplant surgery for children and adults.
Thousands of people are on the waiting list for an organ transplant each year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: graft - a piece of tissue or organ transplanted surgically
  • synonym: transplantation - the act or process of transplanting an organ or tissue
