chukar

noun
Eurasian partridge
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌkɑr /; / ˈtʃʌkər / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌkɑː / (BrE)
a type of partridge native to Eurasia, often found in rocky hills and dry grasslands
