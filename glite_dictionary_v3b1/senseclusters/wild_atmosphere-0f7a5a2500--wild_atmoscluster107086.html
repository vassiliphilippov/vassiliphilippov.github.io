wild atmosphere

noun
lively, uncontrolled environment
1.0
Pronunciation: / waɪld ˈætməsˌfɪr / (AmE), / waɪld ˈætməsfɪə / (BrE)
an environment that is lively, exciting, and lacking in restraint or control

Examples

A wild atmosphere at stadium
A wild atmosphere greeted the team as they walked into the stadium.
She wasn't used to the wild atmosphere of downtown clubs.
The concert had a wild atmosphere that kept everyone on their feet all night.

Related Terms
