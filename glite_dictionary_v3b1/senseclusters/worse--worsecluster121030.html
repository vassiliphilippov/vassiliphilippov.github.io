worse

noun
something that is worse; something inferior or more serious
Pronunciation: / wɝs / (AmE), / wɜːs / (BrE)
A thing that is inferior in quality, condition, or effect compared to another; something more serious, bad, or undesirable.

Examples

For better or worse
For better or for worse, she decided to move to a new city.
He was accused of lying and cheating, and possibly worse.
Sometimes we fear the worse even when there's little reason to do so.

Related Terms

  • antonym: better - something superior in quality or condition
