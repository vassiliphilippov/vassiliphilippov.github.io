Ponzi scheme

noun
fraudulent investment scam
4.3 0.69/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ënzi skiÀêm / (AmE), / Ààp…ínzi skiÀêm / (BrE)
a fraudulent investment scheme where returns to earlier investors are paid using the money from new investors, not from real profits

Examples

A Ponzi scheme collapse

Authorities warned investors to be cautious of any opportunity that might be a Ponzi scheme.
The company was exposed as a Ponzi scheme after it failed to pay returns to new clients.

  • synonym: investment fraud - illegal or deceptive practices in financial investments
  • antonym: legitimate investment - an investment based on real assets or profits, not deception
  • synonym: pyramid scheme - an illegal investment scam based on a hierarchical setup of recruiting new investors
