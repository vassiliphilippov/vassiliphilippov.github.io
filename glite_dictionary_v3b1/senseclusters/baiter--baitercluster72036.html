baiter

noun
person who sets bait
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈbeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who prepares or sets bait to catch animals or fish, especially for trapping or angling

Examples

Fishing baiter
The baiter skillfully attached worms to each hook before the fishing tournament began.
As a professional baiter, she knew exactly how to attract the largest fish in the lake.
The team relied on their experienced baiter to prepare the traps for the early morning hunt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angler - a person who fishes with a rod and line
  • synonym: trapper - a person who catches animals in traps
