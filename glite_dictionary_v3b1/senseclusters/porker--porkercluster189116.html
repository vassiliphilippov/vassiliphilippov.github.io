porker

noun
a pig fattened for meat
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔrkər / (AmE), / ˈpɔːkə / (BrE)
A pig that has been fattened for the purpose of providing meat.

Examples

A fat porker

The farmer sold three porkers to the butcher before the festival.
