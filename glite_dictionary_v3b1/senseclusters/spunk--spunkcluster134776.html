spunk

noun
courage; determination; spirit
9.0
Pronunciation: / spʌŋk / (AmE), / spʌŋk / (BrE)
courage, determination, or spirit, especially in the face of difficulty or danger.

It takes a lot of spunk to stand up for what is right, even when others disagree.
