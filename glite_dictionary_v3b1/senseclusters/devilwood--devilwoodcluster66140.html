devilwood

noun
small tree of the southern U.S. with white flowers and purple fruits
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛvəlˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈdɛvəlˌwʊd / (BrE)
a small tree of the southern United States, with clusters of dull white flowers and dark purple fruits, commonly found in woodlands and swamps

Examples

The devilwood tree
He planted devilwood along the walkway for its attractive purple fruits.
The garden featured a beautiful devilwood in full bloom near the pond.
Wild devilwood trees are a common sight in the wetlands of Georgia.
