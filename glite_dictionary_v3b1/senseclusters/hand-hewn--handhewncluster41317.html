hand-hewn

adjective
shaped by hand with heavy tools
Pronunciation: / hænd hjuːn / (AmE), / hænd hjuːn / (BrE)
cut or shaped by hand using heavy tools such as an axe or chisel, often resulting in a rough appearance

Examples

Hand-hewn wooden beams
She admired the hand-hewn beams supporting the old farmhouse's roof.
The cabin was constructed from hand-hewn logs, giving it a rustic charm.
The path wound between hand-hewn stones covered in moss.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hewn - cut or shaped with hard blows, esp. using tools
