fresh pair of eyes

noun
a new perspective from someone else
Pronunciation: / frɛʃ pɛr əv aɪz / (AmE), / frɛʃ peə əv aɪz / (BrE)
someone who looks at a situation, problem, or work with a new perspective or impartial viewpoint, often uncovering issues or insights previously missed.

Examples

A fresh pair of eyes
She asked her colleague for a fresh pair of eyes to review the final draft.
Sometimes all it takes is a fresh pair of eyes to find a simple solution.
We're too close to the project; we need a fresh pair of eyes to spot any problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: new perspective - a different or original way of viewing something
  • synonym: objective - not influenced by personal feelings; impartial
