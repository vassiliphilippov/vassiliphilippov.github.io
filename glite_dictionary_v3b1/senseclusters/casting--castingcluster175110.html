casting

noun
wireless media streaming to another device
3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈkæ.stɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈkɑː.stɪŋ/ (BrE)
(in technology) the process of sending audio or video from one device to another so it plays on the other device, typically over a local network or the internet.

Examples

Casting video to TV
The app supports casting video from your phone to the living-room TV.
During the meeting, she used casting to show her laptop screen on the conference monitor.
Some smart TVs have built-in casting so you can play music directly from your phone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: screen mirroring - duplicating a device's display on another screen, often over a wireless connection
  • synonym: stream - to transmit or receive audio or video data over the internet or a local network
