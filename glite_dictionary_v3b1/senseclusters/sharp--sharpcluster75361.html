sharp

adverb
precisely on the stated hour
Pronunciation: / ʃɑɹp / (AmE), / ʃɑːp / (BrE)
used after a time to indicate precisely on the stated hour or time, with no delay

Examples

At 8 sharp
Lunch will be served at 12 sharp in the main dining hall.
Please arrive by 12 sharp for the meeting.
The concert is scheduled to begin at 12 sharp, so don't be late.

Related Terms

  • antonym: about - approximately, not exactly (time)
  • synonym: exactly - precisely, without deviation
