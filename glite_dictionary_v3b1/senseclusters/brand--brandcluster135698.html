brand

noun
mark symbolizing disgrace or infamy
Pronunciation: / brænd / (AmE), / brænd / (BrE)
a mark, typically a physical one, that symbolizes disgrace or infamy attached to a person

Examples

The brand of shame
After his betrayal, he carried the brand of a traitor for the rest of his life.
In ancient societies, criminals sometimes bore a physical brand as a sign of their offense.
The scandal left a permanent brand on the family name.

Related Terms

  • antonym: honor - public esteem or respect, often in contrast with disgrace
  • synonym: stigma - a mark of disgrace associated with a particular circumstance, quality, or person
