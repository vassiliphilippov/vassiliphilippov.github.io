innocent

adjective
completely lacking in something
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnəsənt / (AmE), / ˈɪnəsənt / (BrE)
completely lacking or wanting in a particular quality or thing; devoid of.

Examples

Innocent of humor
His speech was innocent of any trace of humor or irony.
The landscape appeared innocent of human habitation for miles around.
The novel was utterly innocent of suspense, making it a dull read for most people.
