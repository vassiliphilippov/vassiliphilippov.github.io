sabino

noun
horse coat pattern
Pronunciation: / səˈbiːnoʊ / (AmE), / səˈbiːnəʊ / (BrE)
a horse with a distinctive white spotting pattern on its coat, often with irregular edges and white legs or face

Examples

A sabino mare
The breeder was proud of his champion sabino mare with her striking white markings.
Many horse enthusiasts admire the unique appearance of a sabino in the show ring.
The foal was identified as a sabino because of its white legs and speckled face.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pinto - a horse with large patches of white and another color
  • antonym: solid - a horse with a coat of a single color and no white markings
