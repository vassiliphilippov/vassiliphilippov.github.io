tripod

noun
three-legged stand or support
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪpɑd / (AmE), / ˈtraɪpɒd / (BrE)
a stand or support with three legs, used to hold something steady, such as a camera or a cooking pot

Examples

A tripod stand
A sturdy tripod helps keep the telescope steady during observations.
The photographer set up her tripod before taking the group picture.
We cooked soup over an open fire, hanging the pot from a tripod.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stand - a piece of furniture or equipment for holding something
