invitee

noun
person who is invited as a guest
4.6
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌvaɪˈti / (AmE), / ɪnˌvaɪˈtiː / (BrE)
a person who is invited, especially as a guest or visitor to a place or event

Examples

The invitee to a party
As an invitee to the wedding, she felt honored by the invitation.
Each invitee received a formal email with the details of the event.
The invitee signed the guest book upon arrival at the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guest - a person who is invited to visit or stay in someone's home or attend an event
  • antonym: host - a person who receives or entertains guests
