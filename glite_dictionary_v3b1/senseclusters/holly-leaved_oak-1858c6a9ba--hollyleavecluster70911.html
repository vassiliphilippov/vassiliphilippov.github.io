holly-leaved oak

noun
evergreen oak tree with holly-like leaves
7.1 7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈhɑː.lɪ liːvd oʊk/ (AmE), /ˈhɒ.lɪ liːvd əʊk/ (BrE)

Examples
