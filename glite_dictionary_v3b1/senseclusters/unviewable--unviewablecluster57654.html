unviewable

adjective
impossible to watch
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈvjuːəbl̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈvjuːəbl̩ / (BrE)
so unpleasant, offensive, or of such poor quality that people cannot or will not watch it

Examples

Unviewable movie quality
The documentary was so poorly filmed that it became unviewable after just ten minutes.
Many critics called the horror movie unviewable due to its graphic violence and disturbing scenes.
The video quality was so low that the entire recording was basically unviewable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unwatchable - so bad or unpleasant that it cannot be watched
  • antonym: watchable - pleasant or good enough to be watched
