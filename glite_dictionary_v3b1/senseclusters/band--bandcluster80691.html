band

noun
driving belt in machinery
2.6 1.0
Pronunciation: / bænd / (AmE), / bænd / (BrE)
a thin, flat strip or loop of material that encircles, binds, or decorates something

Examples

Rubber band
He noticed that the main drive band was loose and quickly tightened it.
Over time, the rubber band in the machine may wear out and need replacing.
The engine uses a band to transmit power from the motor to the wheels.
A gold band encircled the base of the vase.
She tied her hair back with a red band.
The package was held together by a rubber band.
He received a leather band to wear on his ankle as a token of friendship.
She wore a golden band around her wrist as a simple accessory.
The traditional costume included a colorful band tied around the upper arm.

  • synonym: bracelet - a piece of jewelry worn around the wrist
