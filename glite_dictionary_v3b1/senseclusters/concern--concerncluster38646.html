concern

noun
source of worry or anxiety
1.7
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɝːn /; / kənˈsɝn / (AmE), / kənˈsɜːn / (BrE)
something or someone that is important or causes worry, anxiety, or interest

Examples

A major concern
Noise from the construction site became a daily concern for residents.
Rising fuel prices are a major concern for many families.
The safety of the children was their primary concern.
Environmental issues are a growing concern for many people today.
Financial security is a major concern for most families.
The safety of the children was her main concern during the trip.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disregard - lack of consideration or attention to something important
  • synonym: interest - a subject or activity that attracts your attention
  • antonym: relief - a feeling of reassurance and relaxation following anxiety or distress
  • synonym: worry - a feeling or cause of anxiety or unease
