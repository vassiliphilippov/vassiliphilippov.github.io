roofless

adjective
without a roof
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈruːf.ləs /; / ˈruːfləs / (AmE), / ˈruːf.ləs /; / ˈruːfləs / (BrE)
without a roof or shelter; unprotected or exposed

Examples

Roofless building
After the storm, many buildings were left roofless and exposed to the rain.
Several families lived in roofless homes after the earthquake destroyed their village.
Vandals broke into the abandoned church, leaving it roofless and in ruins.
After the storm destroyed their house, the family was left roofless and vulnerable to the elements.
In her poem, she describes feeling emotionally roofless after the loss of her parents.
The charity supports roofless people by providing food and temporary shelter.
