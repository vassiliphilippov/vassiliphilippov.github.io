overenthusiasm

noun
excessive excitement
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪnˈθuziˌæzəm / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪnˈθjuːziˌæzəm / (BrE)
a state of being excessively enthusiastic or excited about something

Examples

Her overenthusiasm at meeting
Her overenthusiasm at the meeting made some people feel uncomfortable.
Sometimes overenthusiasm can lead to unrealistic expectations about a project.
The coach warned the team that overenthusiasm could cause them to lose focus during the game.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apathy - lack of interest or enthusiasm
  • degree lower: enthusiasm - strong excitement or interest in something
