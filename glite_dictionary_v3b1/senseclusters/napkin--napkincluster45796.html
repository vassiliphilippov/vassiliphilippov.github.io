napkin

noun
piece of table linen for wiping mouth or hands
Pronunciation: / ˈnæp.kɪn / (AmE), / ˈnæp.kɪn / (BrE)
A small piece of cloth or paper used at meals to wipe the mouth and hands or to protect clothing.

Examples

A dinner napkin
At the restaurant, the waiter handed each guest a clean napkin.
Please place your napkin on your lap before you start eating.
She wiped her mouth delicately with a white napkin.

  • synonym: serviette - British term for napkin
