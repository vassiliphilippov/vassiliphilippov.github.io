grazing land

noun
land for livestock to graze
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪzɪŋ lænd / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪzɪŋ lænd / (BrE)
an area of land covered with grass or other vegetation suitable for animals to feed on

Examples

Grazing land area
Much of the region's grazing land was damaged by drought last year.
The government has set aside new grazing land to support local sheep farmers.
The rancher moved his cattle to the grazing land beyond the river for the summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meadow - a field of grass, especially one used for hay
  • synonym: pasture - land covered with grass for grazing animals
