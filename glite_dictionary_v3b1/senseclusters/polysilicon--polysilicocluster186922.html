polysilicon

noun
raw silicon material
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑliˈsɪlɪkən / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˈsɪlɪkən / (BrE)
a form of silicon consisting of small crystals, used as a raw material in electronics and solar panels

Examples

Polysilicon for solar panels
The price of polysilicon has a major impact on the cost of solar panels worldwide.
Engineers improved the efficiency of solar cells by refining the quality of polysilicon used.
Factories in several countries produce polysilicon for the electronics and renewable energy industries.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monocrystalline silicon - a single-crystal form of silicon used in high-efficiency electronics
  • synonym: silicon - a chemical element used in electronics and solar panels
