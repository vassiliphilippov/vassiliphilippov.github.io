pendens

noun
pending legal matter
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛndɛnz / (AmE), / ˈpɛndɛnz / (BrE)
a legal term referring to something that is pending or awaiting a decision, especially in court proceedings

Examples

Case pendens
The judge noted that the case was still pendens and no verdict had been reached.
A property with a pendens status may be difficult to sell until the dispute is resolved.
Lawyers discussed the implications of the matter being pendens during the hearing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pending - awaiting a decision or settlement
  • antonym: resolved - settled or decided
