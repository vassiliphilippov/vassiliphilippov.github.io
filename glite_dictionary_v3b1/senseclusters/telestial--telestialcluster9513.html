telestial

adjective
lowest Mormon glory
Pronunciation: / təˈlɛstiəl / (AmE), / təˈlɛstiəl / (BrE)
relating to the lowest of the three degrees of glory in Mormon theology, considered below terrestrial and celestial

Examples

The telestial kingdom
According to their beliefs, only certain people will enter the telestial kingdom after death.
The concept of the telestial world is unique to Mormon doctrine.
He studied the differences between the celestial, terrestrial, and telestial glories.
