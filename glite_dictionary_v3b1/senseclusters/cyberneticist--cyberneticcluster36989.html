cyberneticist

noun
cybernetics specialist
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪbərˈnɛtɪsɪst / (AmE), / ˌsaɪbəˈnɛtɪsɪst /; / ˌsaɪbənɛˈtɪsɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies or works in the field of cybernetics, focusing on systems, control, and communication in machines and living things

Examples

A cyberneticist studies systems
The cyberneticist presented her research on feedback loops in biological systems at the conference.
As a leading cyberneticist, he contributed to the development of intelligent robotics.
Many cyberneticists collaborate with computer scientists to advance artificial intelligence.
The cyberneticist developed a new model for understanding feedback in biological systems.
As a leading cyberneticist, she contributed to advances in artificial intelligence.
Many cyberneticists collaborate with engineers and biologists to solve complex problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: engineer - a person who designs, builds, or maintains engines, machines, or structures
  • synonym: systems theorist - a person who studies complex systems in nature, society, or science
