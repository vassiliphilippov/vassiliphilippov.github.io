formulaically

adverb
in a set pattern
Pronunciation: / fɔːrˈmjuleɪɪkli / (AmE), / fɔːˈmjʊːleɪɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that follows a fixed pattern or formula, often lacking originality or creativity

Examples

Formulaically written story
The story unfolded formulaically, with every twist easy to predict.
Many pop songs are written formulaically to appeal to a wide audience.
Critics argued that the sequel was made formulaically rather than with genuine inspiration.

Related Terms

  • antonym: creatively - in a way that shows originality and imagination
  • synonym: mechanically - in a way that is automatic and without thought or creativity
