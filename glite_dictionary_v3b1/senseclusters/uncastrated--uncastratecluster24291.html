uncastrated

adjective
not castrated (esp. of animals)
4.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkæstreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkæstreɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of an animal, especially a male) not having undergone castration; retaining the reproductive organs.

Examples

An uncastrated ram
He bought an uncastrated ram for the purpose of increasing his flock.
Only uncastrated animals were eligible for breeding programs at the ranch.
The farmer separated the uncastrated bulls from the rest of the herd.

Related Terms

  • antonym: castrated - having had the testicles or ovaries removed
