main

noun
main course of meal
2.5
Pronunciation: / meɪn / (AmE), / meɪn / (BrE)
the main course of a meal, usually the largest or most important dish served after the starter

Examples

Main course
For my birthday dinner, I chose the grilled salmon as my main.
The waiter brought out the main after we finished our soup.
She skipped the starter and went straight to the main because she was very hungry.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dessert - the sweet course served at the end of a meal
  • synonym: entrée - the main dish of a meal, especially in American English
  • antonym: starter - a small dish served before the main course
