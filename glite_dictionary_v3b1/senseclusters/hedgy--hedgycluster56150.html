hedgy

adjective
evasive; noncommittal
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdʒi / (AmE), / ˈhɛdʒi / (BrE)
tending to avoid giving clear or direct answers; evasive or noncommittal

Examples

Hedgy reply
When asked about the project deadline, she gave a hedgy answer that left everyone confused.
His hedgy comments made it hard to know what he really thought about the proposal.
The politician was criticized for being too hedgy during the interview.

Related Terms

  • antonym: direct - saying something in a clear and straightforward way
  • synonym: evasive - not giving a clear or direct answer
