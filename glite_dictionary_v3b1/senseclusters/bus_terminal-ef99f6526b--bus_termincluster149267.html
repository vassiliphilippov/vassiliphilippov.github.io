bus terminal

noun
station for bus passengers
Pronunciation: / bʌs ˈtɜrmɪnəl / (AmE), / bʌs ˈtɜːmɪnəl / (BrE)
A station where passengers get on and off buses, typically the starting or ending point of bus routes.

Examples

The bus terminal building
After arriving at the bus terminal, she looked for a taxi to get home.
All intercity buses depart from the main bus terminal at regular intervals.
The city built a new bus terminal downtown to serve commuters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bus station - a place where buses regularly stop and pick up or drop off passengers
  • antonym: train station - a place where trains regularly stop for passengers to board or depart
