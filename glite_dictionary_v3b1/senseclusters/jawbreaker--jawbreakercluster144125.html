jawbreaker

noun
a large, hard candy
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔˌbreɪkər / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːˌbreɪkə / (BrE)
a large, round, very hard candy that dissolves slowly in the mouth

Examples

A big jawbreaker candy
He gave his nephew a huge jawbreaker as a treat after dinner.
It took her nearly an hour to finish the entire jawbreaker.
The children traded marbles and jawbreakers at recess.
