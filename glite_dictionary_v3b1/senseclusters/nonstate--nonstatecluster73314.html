nonstate

noun
entity not government
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑnˌsteɪt / (AmE), / ˈnɒnˌsteɪt / (BrE)
a person, group, or organization that is not part of any government, especially in international or political contexts

Examples

A nonstate group
The agreement included both government and nonstate representatives in the negotiations.
Many nonstate actors play a crucial role in delivering humanitarian aid during crises.
The conference focused on the influence of nonstate organizations in global politics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: non-governmental organization - an organization independent from government, often working in humanitarian or social fields
  • antonym: state - a government or country as an organized political community
