tulip

noun
a brightly colored spring-flowering plant
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈtuː.lɪp/ /; / /ˈtjuː.lɪp/ (AmE), /ˈtjuː.lɪp/ /; / /ˈtʃuː.lɪp/ (BrE)
