count one's blessings

verb
be grateful for what one has
1.4
Pronunciation: / kaʊnt wʌnz ˈblɛsɪŋz / (AmE), / kaʊnt wʌnz ˈblɛsɪŋz / (BrE)
to be grateful for the good things in one's life, especially when facing difficulties

Examples

To count their blessings
Even though their house was small, they would count their blessings every day.
She realized she needed to count her blessings after hearing about her friend's difficulties.
Whenever I feel discouraged, I try to count my blessings instead of focusing on problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appreciate - to recognize the value of something or someone
  • antonym: take for granted - to fail to appreciate the value of something
