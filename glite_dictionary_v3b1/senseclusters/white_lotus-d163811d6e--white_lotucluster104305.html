white lotus

noun
person feigning innocence or virtue
9.9
Pronunciation: /waɪt ˈloʊ.təs/ /; / /hwaɪt ˈloʊ.təs/ (AmE), /waɪt ˈləʊ.təs/ /; / /hwaɪt ˈləʊ.təs/ (BrE)
Informal slang for a person (often privileged) who pretends to be innocent, pure, or morally superior in order to manipulate others or avoid responsibility.

Examples

She is a white lotus
Many social media users called her a white lotus after she pretended not to know the rules.
The article argued that wealthy influencers often behave like a white lotus, playing innocent to gain sympathy.
At the meeting he was exposed as a white lotus, using fake concern to win support for his project.

Related Terms
