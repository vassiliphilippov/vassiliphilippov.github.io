cholic

adjective
having colic symptoms
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑlɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɒlɪk / (BrE)
suffering from or showing symptoms of colic, especially severe abdominal pain or cramping.

Examples

Cholic pain

Parents often worry when their baby becomes cholic and cannot be comforted.
A cholic episode can cause severe discomfort and sleepless nights for both child and parents.
