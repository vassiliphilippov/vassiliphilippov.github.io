noncommercially

adverb
not for profit
5.7
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.kə.ˈmɜːr.ʃə.liː/ (AmE), /nɒn.kə.ˈmɜː.ʃəl.iː/ (BrE)
in a way that does not involve commerce, business, or making a profit

Examples

Distribute noncommercially food
The organization distributes food noncommercially to people in need.
You may use this software noncommercially for educational purposes.
The film was shown noncommercially at a local community center.
