bounce-back

noun
physical rebound
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊnsˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈbaʊnsˌbæk / (BrE)
the act of rebounding or returning to a previous position, especially when an object like a ball hits a surface and comes back.

Examples

Ball bounce-back
The quick bounce-back of the ball surprised the goalkeeper.
After hitting the wall, the tennis ball's bounce-back was faster than expected.
He caught the bounce-back and immediately passed it to his teammate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deflection - the act of changing direction after hitting something
  • synonym: rebound - a bounce back after hitting a surface
