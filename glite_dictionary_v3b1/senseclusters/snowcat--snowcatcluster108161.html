snowcat

verb
to groom snowy surface
7.7
Pronunciation: /snoʊ.ˈkæt/ (AmE), /snəʊ.ˈkæt/ (BrE)
to groom or smooth a snowy surface, especially a ski slope, using a tracked snow vehicle called a snowcat

Examples

To snowcat a slope
The crew will snowcat the main trail before the ski resort opens in the morning.
After the heavy snowfall, workers snowcatted the slopes to make them safe for skiers.
It takes several hours to snowcat all the runs on the mountain each night.
