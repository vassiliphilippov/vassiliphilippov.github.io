requisition

noun
official request form
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛkwəˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛkwɪˈzɪʃən / (BrE)
an official form used to request something, especially within an organization or government office

Examples

Requisition form
Before hiring new equipment, you must complete a requisition and submit it to the office.
He forgot to sign the requisition, so the order was delayed.
Please attach the approved requisition to any purchase request for supplies.
