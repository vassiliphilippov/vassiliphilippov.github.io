ingenuously

adverb
in an honest and innocent way
5.6
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʒɛnjuəsli / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒɛnjuəsli / (BrE)
in an open, honest, and innocent manner; without deceit or pretense

Examples

To speak ingenuously
His questions were asked ingenuously, without any hidden motive or malice.
She ingenuously admitted that she had never heard of the famous author.
They ingenuously believed everyone would tell the truth in the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artlessly - in a natural and simple manner, without artificiality
  • synonym: candidly - in an open and honest way
  • antonym: deviously - in a manner showing a skillful use of underhand tactics
