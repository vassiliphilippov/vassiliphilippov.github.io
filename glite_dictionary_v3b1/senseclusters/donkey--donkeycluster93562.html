donkey

noun
domestic animal used as a beast of burden
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑŋki / (AmE), / ˈdɒŋki / (BrE)
A domestic animal of the horse family, typically smaller than a horse, with long ears and a braying call, often used for carrying loads; known for being patient but sometimes stubborn.

Examples

A donkey carries loads
Children laughed as they watched the donkey bray loudly in the field.
Stubborn as a donkey, he refused to change his mind.
The farmer loaded supplies onto his donkey before heading to the market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ass - another word for donkey (especially in older or formal contexts)
  • synonym: mule - the offspring of a male donkey and female horse
