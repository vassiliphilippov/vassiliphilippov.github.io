urn-shaped

adjective
shaped like an urn
Pronunciation: / ˈɝːn ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈɜːn ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a concave shape resembling that of an urn, typically rounded with a narrow base and a wider opening at the top.

Examples

An urn-shaped vase
She bought a striking urn-shaped vase for her living room.
Some mushrooms have a distinctive urn-shaped cap that sets them apart from other species.
The plant has urn-shaped flowers that attract hummingbirds.
