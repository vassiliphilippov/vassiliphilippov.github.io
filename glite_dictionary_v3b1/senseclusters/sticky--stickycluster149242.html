sticky

adjective
hot and humid (of weather)
4.5 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈstɪ.kiː/ (AmE), /ˈstɪ.kiː/ (BrE)
hot and humid, making you feel sweaty and uncomfortable

Examples

Sticky weather
Everyone was uncomfortable in the sticky heat of the crowded subway.
It’s hard to sleep through these sticky summer nights with no breeze at all.
The air was so sticky that you could feel moisture clinging to your skin.
After running in the humid heat, her shirt felt sticky against her back.
He wiped his sticky forehead with the back of his hand after the workout.
The sticky sensation made her want to change out of her damp clothes immediately.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clammy - unpleasantly damp and sticky or slimy to touch
  • synonym: muggy - (of weather) uncomfortably warm and humid
  • synonym: steamy - (of air or weather) hot and humid or full of steam
