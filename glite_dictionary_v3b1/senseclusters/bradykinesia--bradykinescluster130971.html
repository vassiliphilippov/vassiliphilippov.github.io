bradykinesia

noun
slowness of movement
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌbreɪdiˌkɪˈniːziə / (AmE), / ˌbrædɪkɪˈniːziə / (BrE)
abnormally slow movement, especially as a symptom of certain medical conditions such as Parkinson's disease

Examples

Slow bradykinesia movement
The neurologist noted bradykinesia as one of the patient's main symptoms.
People with Parkinson's disease often struggle with bradykinesia in their daily activities.
Physical therapy can help manage bradykinesia and improve mobility.
