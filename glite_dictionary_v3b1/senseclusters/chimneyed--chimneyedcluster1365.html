chimneyed

adjective
with vertical shafts
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪmniːd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪmniːd / (BrE)
having or formed with a vertical, chimney-like shaft or cavity, especially in rocks, mines, or geological formations

Examples

Chimneyed rock
The old castle had a chimneyed tower rising above the surrounding forest.
Geologists studied the chimneyed cliffs to understand how the vertical cavities formed.
Miners avoided the most chimneyed sections of the cave due to safety concerns.
