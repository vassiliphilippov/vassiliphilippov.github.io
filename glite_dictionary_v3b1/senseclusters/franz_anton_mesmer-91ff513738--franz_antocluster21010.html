Franz Anton Mesmer

proper noun
Austrian physician; developed mesmerism
9.9
Pronunciation: / frænz ˈæn.tɑn ˈmɛz.mɚ / (AmE), / frants ˈan.tɒn ˈmɛz.mə / (BrE)
(1734–1815) An Austrian physician known for developing mesmerism, an early form of hypnotism used to treat diseases.

Examples

Franz Anton Mesmer theory
Franz Anton Mesmer believed that a magnetic fluid flowed through the human body.
Many credit Franz Anton Mesmer with laying the foundations for modern hypnotism.
The theories of Franz Anton Mesmer were both controversial and influential in medical history.
