impactor

noun
influencing factor
7.0
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpæktər / (AmE), / ɪmˈpæktə / (BrE)
something that has a strong effect or influence on something else, especially a factor causing change

Examples

A major impactor
Pollution is a major impactor on public health in many cities.
Climate change is considered a significant impactor on global weather patterns.
The new regulations could become an important impactor in the industry’s future.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cause - a person or thing that gives rise to an action, phenomenon, or condition
  • antonym: effect - a change that is a result or consequence of an action or other cause
  • synonym: influencer - someone or something that affects or changes how people or things behave
