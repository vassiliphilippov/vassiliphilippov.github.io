impactor

noun
influencing factor
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈɪm.pæk.tər/ (AmE), /ˈɪm.pæk.tə/ (BrE)
something that has a strong effect or influence on something else, especially a factor causing change

Examples

A major impactor
Pollution is a major impactor on public health in many cities.
Climate change is considered a significant impactor on global weather patterns.
The new regulations could become an important impactor in the industry’s future.
