crescendo

verb
grow louder (music)
9.1 5.1
Pronunciation: /krə.ˈʃen.doʊ/ /; / /krɪ.ˈʃen.doʊ/ (AmE), /krə.ˈʃen.dəʊ/ /; / /krɪ.ˈʃen.dəʊ/ (BrE)
(of sound, especially music) to gradually increase in loudness or intensity.

Examples

To crescendo loudly
As the argument continued, the voices in the room crescendoed to a fever pitch.
The orchestra began to crescendo as the piece reached its climax.
The thunder crescendoed before finally fading away in the distance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fade - to gradually become quieter or disappear (in sound/music)
