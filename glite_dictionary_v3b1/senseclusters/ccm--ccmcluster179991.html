ccm

noun
unit of volume
Pronunciation: / si si ɛm / (AmE), / siː siː ɛm / (BrE)
a unit of volume equal to one cubic centimeter, commonly used in science and medicine

Examples

5 ccm of blood
The doctor measured 5 ccm of blood for the test.
This engine has a capacity of 1500 ccm.
Please inject 2 ccm of the solution into the vial.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: liter - a metric unit of volume equal to 1,000 cubic centimeters
  • synonym: ml - a unit of volume equal to one thousandth of a liter
