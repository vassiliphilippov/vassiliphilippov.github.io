plexor

noun
medical hammer for examining reflexes
Pronunciation: / ˈplɛk.sɚ / (AmE), / ˈplɛk.sɔː / (BrE)
A small hammer with a rubber head used by doctors to tap parts of the body during examinations, especially to test reflexes or examine the chest.
