nganga

noun
African spiritual healer
9.9
Pronunciation: / ŋɑːŋɡɑː / (AmE), / ŋaŋɡa / (BrE)
a traditional healer or spiritual doctor in certain Central and Southern African cultures

Examples

Village nganga healer
The community respected the nganga for their knowledge of herbal medicine and spiritual guidance.
During important ceremonies, the nganga would lead rituals to protect the village.
People from nearby towns traveled to consult the nganga about their health and fortunes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shaman - a person regarded as having access to the spirit world, especially in indigenous cultures
  • synonym: witch doctor - a traditional healer who uses magic or folk remedies
