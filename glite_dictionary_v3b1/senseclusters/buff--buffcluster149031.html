buff

verb
give a temporary in-game benefit
7.7
Pronunciation: /bʌf/ (AmE), /bʌf/ (BrE)
(in gaming) to give a character, player, or group a temporary beneficial effect, improvement, or stat increase.

Examples

To buff a character
The mage used a spell to buff the party's defenses for battle.
He spent the opening five minutes buffing his teammates before the difficult boss encounter.
She buffed the support player, increasing their attack speed and survivability.
