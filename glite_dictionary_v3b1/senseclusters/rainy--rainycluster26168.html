rainy

adjective
characterized by rain; wet weather
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪni / (AmE), / ˈreɪni / (BrE)
(of weather) marked by or characterized by rain; having or bringing much rain.

Examples

The rainy season
It's best to carry an umbrella during the rainy season in this region.
The kids played board games inside on the rainy afternoon.
We had a very rainy weekend and couldn't go hiking as planned.

Related Terms
