sabzi

noun
South Asian vegetable dish
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑbzi / (AmE), / ˈsæbzi / (BrE)
a dish or mixture of cooked or raw vegetables, especially common in South Asian cuisine

Examples

Spicy sabzi dish
She prepared a spicy sabzi for dinner with rice and bread.
At the restaurant, I ordered a mixed vegetable sabzi with naan.
His favorite lunch is homemade sabzi served with lentils and rice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curry - a dish of meat or vegetables cooked in a spiced sauce
  • synonym: salad - a cold dish of mixed vegetables, sometimes with dressing
