global organization

noun
international alliance or body of many countries
Pronunciation: / ˈɡloʊ.bəl ˌɔr.ɡən.aɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊ.bəl ˌɔː.ɡə.naɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
an international alliance or body composed of members from many different countries, usually established to address worldwide issues or promote cooperation

Examples

A global organization meeting
Membership in a global organization offers countries a platform to collaborate on climate change.
She was appointed as a director of a leading global organization focused on public health.
The United Nations is a well-known global organization that promotes peace and security.
