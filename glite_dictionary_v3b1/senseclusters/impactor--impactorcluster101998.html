impactor

noun
object that strikes
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpæktər / (AmE), / ɪmˈpæktə / (BrE)
an object or device that strikes another object with force, especially in scientific or engineering contexts

Examples

A impactor device
The spacecraft was designed to act as an impactor on the comet's surface.
Scientists studied the crater left by the impactor after the experiment.
A high-speed impactor can simulate meteorite collisions in laboratory tests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: projectile - an object that is thrown or propelled through the air
  • antonym: target - an object that is struck or aimed at
