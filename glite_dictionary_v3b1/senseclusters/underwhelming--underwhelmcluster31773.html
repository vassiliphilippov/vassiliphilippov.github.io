underwhelming

adjective
disappointing; unimpressive
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈwɛlmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈwɛlmɪŋ / (BrE)
not as impressive, exciting, or effective as expected; disappointing or unimpressive

Examples

An underwhelming movie
The movie was underwhelming and failed to live up to the critics' praise.
Despite the expensive advertising campaign, the product launch was rather underwhelming.
Her performance in the play was underwhelming compared to her previous roles.
