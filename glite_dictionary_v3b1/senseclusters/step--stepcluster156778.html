step

noun
part of a stairway for the foot
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /step/ (AmE), /step/ (BrE)
a flat horizontal surface on a stairway where you place your foot when moving up or down

Examples

A stair step

The old wooden step creaked loudly as he descended into the basement.
