step

noun
part of a stairway for the foot
1.0
Pronunciation: / stɛp / (AmE), / stɛp / (BrE)
a flat horizontal surface on a stairway where you place your foot when moving up or down

Examples

A stair step
Please watch your step when entering the building; the stairs are narrow.
She placed her foot on the first step and began to climb the stairs.
The old wooden step creaked loudly as he descended into the basement.
