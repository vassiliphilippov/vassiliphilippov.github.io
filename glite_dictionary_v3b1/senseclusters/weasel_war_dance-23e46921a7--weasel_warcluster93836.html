weasel war dance

noun
frenzied celebratory or playful dance
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːzəl wɔr ˌdæns / (AmE), / ˈwiːzəl wɔː ˌdɑːns / (BrE)
a playful, frenzied dance-like movement performed by a weasel when excited or happy; metaphorically, any exuberant or wild celebration

Examples

A weasel war dance
After their successful prank, the friends performed a mock weasel war dance in the hallway.
The puppies greeted their owner with leaps and spins reminiscent of a weasel war dance.
When the ferret got a new toy, it did a weasel war dance around the room.
