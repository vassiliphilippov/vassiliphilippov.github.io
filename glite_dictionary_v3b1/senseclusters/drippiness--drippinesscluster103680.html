drippiness

noun
flashy, fashionable style
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɪpinəs / (AmE), / ˈdrɪpinəs / (BrE)
informal slang: the quality of having a fashionable, flashy, or ostentatious style of dress or appearance.

Examples

Fashion drippiness
Everyone noticed his expensive watch and overall drippiness as he entered the club.
The designer's new collection celebrates streetwear drippiness with bold logos and gold chains.
Her Instagram bio is full of selfies showing her drippiness and carefully curated outfits.

Related Terms
