soft on

adjective
excessively or foolishly fond of
8.0 1.03/m
Pronunciation: / sÉ”ft É‘n / (AmE), / sÉ’ft É’n / (BrE)
foolishly or excessively fond of someone or something, often to the point of lacking judgment

Examples

Be soft on someone
Don't get soft on him just because he gave you flowers.
Ever since he met her, he's been soft on her despite her obvious flaws.
It's clear the teacher is soft on that student and often overlooks his mistakes.
