ecophysiological

adjective
organism-environment physiology
Pronunciation: / ˌiːkoʊˌfɪziəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌiːkəʊˌfɪziəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the physiological processes of organisms as they interact with their environment

Examples

Ecophysiological responses
Researchers study ecophysiological responses of animals to changes in temperature and humidity.
The ecophysiological traits of desert plants help them conserve water in extreme conditions.
Understanding ecophysiological factors is important for predicting how species will adapt to climate change.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ecological - relating to the relationships between living things and their environment
  • synonym: physiological - relating to the normal functions of living organisms
