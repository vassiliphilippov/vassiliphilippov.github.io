dopey

adjective
sleepy or sluggish
4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈdoʊ.piː/ /; / /ˈdoʊ.pɪ/ (AmE), /ˈdəʊ.piː/ /; / /ˈdəʊ.pɪ/ (BrE)
feeling or looking sleepy, sluggish, or not very alert, often as if under the influence of tiredness, drugs, or illness

Examples

Dopey feeling
After staying up all night, she felt dopey during her morning class.
The medicine made him so dopey that he could barely keep his eyes open.
He looked dopey as he wandered around the house in his pajamas.
After taking the medicine, he felt dopey and could barely keep his eyes open.
She acted a bit dopey during the meeting because she had not slept well.
The puppy looked dopey as it yawned and stumbled around the living room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alert - quick to notice and respond; attentive
  • synonym: drowsy - feeling sleepy and lethargic
  • synonym: groggy - weak and unable to think clearly, often after sleep
