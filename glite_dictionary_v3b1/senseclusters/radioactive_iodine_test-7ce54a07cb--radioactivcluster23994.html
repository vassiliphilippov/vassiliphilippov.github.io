radioactive iodine test

noun
thyroid function test using radioactive iodine
1.5 4.0
Pronunciation: /reɪ.di.oʊ.ˈæk.tɪv ˈaɪ.ə.daɪn tɛst/ /; / /reɪ.di.oʊ.ˈæk.tɪv ˈaɪ.oʊ.daɪn tɛst/ (AmE), /reɪ.di.əʊ.ˈæk.tɪv ˈaɪ.ə.daɪn tɛst/ (BrE)
a test of thyroid function in which a patient is given an oral dose of radioactive iodine-131, and its uptake by the thyroid gland is measured

Examples

A radioactive iodine test result
A radioactive iodine test is often used to help diagnose thyroid disorders.
Before her surgery, she underwent a radioactive iodine test to assess her thyroid function.
The doctor ordered a radioactive iodine test to check for an overactive thyroid.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thyroid function test - any test used to assess how well the thyroid gland is working
