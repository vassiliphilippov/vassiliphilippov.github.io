application

noun
formal request for something (job, help, etc.)
Pronunciation: / ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
a formal request, usually in writing, for something such as a job, assistance, or admission to an organization or institution

Examples

Job application
She submitted her application for the scholarship before the deadline.
The company receives hundreds of applications for each open position.
You must complete an application form to enroll at the university.

Related Terms

  • synonym: petition - a formal written request, especially to an authority
  • synonym: request - an act of asking for something to be given or done
