hairdo

noun
arrangement or style of hair
2.9 1.86/m
Pronunciation: / Ààh…õrÀådu / (AmE), / Ààhe…ô.duÀê / (BrE)
A way in which a person's hair is cut and arranged, especially as a particular style.

Examples

A stylish hairdo
He spent an hour at the salon getting his hairdo just right for the wedding.
She arrived at the party with a stunning hairdo that caught everyone's attention.
The actress changed her hairdo for every scene in the film.

Synonyms

  • hairstyle
    1.4 2.50/m
    hairstyle names the usual way hair is cut and arranged for daily wear, while hairdo suggests a styled, finished look for occasions.
  • hair style
    1.9 0.01/m
    hair style appears as a two-word descriptive phrase in writing, while hairdo names a single finished or fashionable hair arrangement.
  • haircut
    1.0 3.90/m
    haircut focuses on the way hair is cut or the length at a salon, while hairdo means the arranged, finished look people wear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: haircut - the act of cutting the hair or the style in which it is cut
  • synonym: hairstyle - a particular way of cutting and arranging the hair
