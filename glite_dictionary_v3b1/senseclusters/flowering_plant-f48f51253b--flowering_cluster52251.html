flowering plant

noun
plant that produces flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊ.ɚ.ɪŋ ˌplænt / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ər.ɪŋ ˌplɑːnt / (BrE)
A plant that produces flowers and has seeds enclosed within an ovary.

Examples

A flowering plant species
Many types of fruits come from flowering plants that require pollination.
Orchids are among the most diverse group of flowering plants on Earth.
The rose is a well-known flowering plant grown in gardens worldwide.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angiosperm - plant whose seeds develop inside an ovary; scientific term for flowering plant
  • antonym: gymnosperm - plant whose seeds are not enclosed in an ovary
