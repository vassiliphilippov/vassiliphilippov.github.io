kopeck

noun
one hundredth of a ruble (currency)
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊˌpɛk / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌpɛk / (BrE)
A monetary unit of Russia and some other countries, equal to one hundredth of a ruble.

Examples

A kopeck coin
He still keeps a jar of old kopeck coins as a souvenir from Russia.
Prices in the market were listed in rubles and kopecks.
When I visited Moscow, I found a kopeck coin on the street.
