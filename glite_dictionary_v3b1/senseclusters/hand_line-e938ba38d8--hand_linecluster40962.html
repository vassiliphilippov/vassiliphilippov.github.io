hand line

noun
a fishing line operated by hand
4.6 4.5
Pronunciation: /hænd laɪn/ (AmE), /hænd laɪn/ (BrE)
A fishing line that is operated mainly by hand, rather than being attached to a rod or reel.

Examples

A hand line for fishing
A hand line is useful when fishing in small boats without much equipment.
Fishermen in the village still use the traditional hand line method.
He caught several trout using only a hand line from the shore.
