Examples

A emanation of light

They studied the emanation of radioactive particles from the substance.

Synonyms

  • emission
    3.5 9.26/m
    emission is the act of sending out gas, light, or heat from a source, while emanation names that outflow or process.
  • emittance
    8.2 0.01/m
    emittance is the act or measure of how well something gives off energy, while emanation is the thing or process that comes from a source.
  • release
    3.6 12/m
    release is the act of letting out a substance or energy, often intentionally, while emanation is any thing or process that flows from a source.
  • extramission
    9.9 0.00/m
    extramission is a rare technical word for sending out light or particles, while emanation is the more common word for the thing or process.
