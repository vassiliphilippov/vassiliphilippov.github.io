sideline interest

noun
secondary job or hobby
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪd.laɪn ˈɪn.trəst / (AmE), / ˈsaɪd.laɪn ˈɪn.trəst / (BrE)
A hobby, activity, or secondary job pursued in addition to one's main occupation.

Examples

A sideline interest hobby
Many teachers have a sideline interest to supplement their incomes.
She spends her evenings painting as a sideline interest apart from her accounting career.
Writing fiction became his sideline interest after years of working as a journalist.
