pastorally

adverb
in a rural manner
Pronunciation: / ˈpæstərəli / (AmE), / ˈpɑːstərəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the countryside, rural life, or pastoral scenes

Examples

Pastorally beautiful landscape
The fields were described pastorally in the travel journal, evoking a sense of peace.
She painted the village pastorally, highlighting its simple and natural beauty.
The author writes pastorally about life on the farm, focusing on its quiet rhythms.
