movement

noun
organized campaign for a principle or goal
3.6 86/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàmuÀêvm…ônt / (AmE), / ÀàmuÀêvm…ônt / (BrE)
a group of people working together to achieve a shared goal, especially in politics or society

Examples

Civil rights movement
