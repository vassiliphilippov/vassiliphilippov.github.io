tear-gassing

noun
use of tear gas
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪrˌɡæsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɪəˌɡæsɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of using tear gas to disperse or control crowds, especially during protests or riots

Examples

Police tear-gassing protesters
The police resorted to tear-gassing the demonstrators to clear the street.
International observers condemned the tear-gassing of peaceful protesters during the rally.
Repeated tear-gassing led to health concerns among residents living near the protest site.

Related Terms

  • synonym: containment - the act of keeping something under control
  • synonym: dispersal - the act of scattering or driving people away
