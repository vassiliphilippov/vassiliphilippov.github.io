trustless

adjective
not needing trust
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌstləs / (AmE), / ˈtrʌstləs / (BrE)
not requiring trust in a central authority or intermediary to operate or function

Examples

Trustless blockchain system
Blockchain technology is often described as a trustless system because it does not rely on intermediaries.
A trustless network allows users to interact directly without needing to trust each other.
Many cryptocurrencies are built on trustless protocols to ensure security and transparency.

Related Terms

  • antonym: centralized - controlled by a single central authority
  • synonym: decentralized - not controlled by a single central authority
