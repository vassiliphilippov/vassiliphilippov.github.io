distributionist

noun
distribution coordinator
7.2
Pronunciation: / dɪˌstrɪbjuˈʃənɪst / (AmE), / dɪˌstrɪbjuːˈʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person whose job is to organize, manage, or coordinate the distribution of goods, products, or media

Examples

A distributionist manager
The distributionist ensured that all shipments arrived at their destinations on time.
As a distributionist, she coordinated deliveries between multiple warehouses and retailers.
Every distributionist in the company attended the annual logistics conference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distributor - a person or company that supplies goods to stores or businesses
  • synonym: logistician - a person who manages the flow of goods and services
