furphy

noun
Australian water cart
Pronunciation: / ˈfɜːrfi / (AmE), / ˈfɜːfi / (BrE)
a water cart or tank used to carry and supply water, especially one historically used in Australia for troops or livestock

Examples

Old furphy cart
The museum displayed a restored furphy that was once used to supply water to soldiers.
During the drought, the farmer relied on an old furphy to bring water to his cattle.
A rusty furphy stood in the paddock, reminding locals of the area's history.
