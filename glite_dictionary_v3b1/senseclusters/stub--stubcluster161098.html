verb
to extinguish by pressing
4.0 0.23/m
Pronunciation: / stÊŒb / (AmE), / stÊŒb / (BrE)
to put out a small flame or cigarette by pressing it against a hard surface.

Examples

To stub a cigarette
She was stubbing her cigarette on the pavement when someone shouted.
He quickly stubbed out the match before it could burn his fingers.
After smoking, please remember to stub your cigarette in the ashtray.

Synonyms

  • stub out
    5.3 0.50/m
    stub out is the common phrasal form used for putting out cigarettes, while stub is the shorter verb form used less often.
  • press out
    3.4 0.00/m
    press out means crushing a burning cigarette to put it out, while stub means pressing a cigarette's tip on a hard surface.
