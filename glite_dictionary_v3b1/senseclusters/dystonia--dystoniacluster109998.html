dystonia

noun
muscle contraction disorder
8.1
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈstoʊ.niː.ə/ /; / /dɪ.ˈstoʊ.njə/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈstəʊ.niː.ə/ /; / /dɪ.ˈstəʊ.njə/ (BrE)
a medical condition where involuntary muscle contractions cause twisting movements or abnormal postures
