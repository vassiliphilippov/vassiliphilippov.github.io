first round

noun
initial stage of a competition
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝst ˌraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ˌraʊnd / (BrE)
the initial stage or set of matches in a competition or tournament, typically involving all participants before any are eliminated

Examples

The first round of play
Many teams are eliminated after the first round of play.
She was nervous before the first round but played exceptionally well.
The underdog surprised everyone by advancing past the first round of the tournament.

Related Terms

  • antonym: final round - the last stage of a competition
