mutton chop

noun
style of facial hair growing down the face
7.3 6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌt.ən tʃɑːp/ (AmE), /ˈmʌt.ən tʃɒp/ (BrE)
A style of facial hair in which whiskers grow down the sides of a man's face in front of the ears, with the rest of the beard usually shaved.

Examples
