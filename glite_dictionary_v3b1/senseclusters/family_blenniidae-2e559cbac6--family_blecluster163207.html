family Blenniidae

noun
taxonomic family of combtooth blennies
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli bləˈnɪɪˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli blɛnˈɪɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of small marine and freshwater fish known as combtooth blennies, distinguished by elongated bodies and comb-like teeth.
