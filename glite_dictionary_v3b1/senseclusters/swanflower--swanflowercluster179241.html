swanflower

noun
an orchid with flowers like a swan's neck
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑːnˌflaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈswɒnˌflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
any of several orchids of the genus Cycnoches, noted for their slender, arching columns of flowers that resemble a swan's neck

Examples

The swanflower orchid
Botanists are fascinated by the swanflower's unique floral column, which curves like a swan's neck.
She hoped to add a swanflower to her collection of exotic orchids this year.
The greenhouse had several rare swanflowers in full bloom, admired for their elegant shape.
