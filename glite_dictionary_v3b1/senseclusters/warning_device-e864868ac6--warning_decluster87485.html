warning device

noun
device that alerts to danger
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrnɪŋ dɪˌvaɪs / (AmE), / ˈwɔːnɪŋ dɪˌvaɪs / (BrE)
a device designed to alert people to the presence of danger or an undesirable event

Examples

A loud warning device
A loud siren served as the main warning device during emergencies.
Every car must have a warning device to alert drivers to mechanical failures.
The building is equipped with a warning device that activates in case of fire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alarm - a device that sounds a warning or signal
  • synonym: alert - a warning signal or message
