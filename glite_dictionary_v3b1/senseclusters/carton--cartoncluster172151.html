carton

noun
the amount held by a carton
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːr.tən / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.tən / (BrE)
the quantity of something that a single carton can hold; a container's standard amount

Examples

A carton of milk
He drank an entire carton of chocolate milk after the game.
She bought a carton of orange juice from the store.
The refrigerator was full of cartons of milk and eggs.
