needlestick

noun
accidental needle injury
5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈniː.dəl.stɪk/ (AmE), /ˈniː.dəl.stɪk/ (BrE)
an accidental puncture of the skin by a needle, especially in medical or laboratory settings

Examples

A needlestick injury
The doctor took immediate action after experiencing a needlestick during the procedure.
Hospitals have strict protocols to prevent needlestick injuries among staff.
A needlestick can expose healthcare workers to bloodborne diseases if not handled properly.
