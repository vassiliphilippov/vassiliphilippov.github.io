nanotech

adjective
nanoscale technology
Pronunciation: / ˈnænoʊˌtɛk / (AmE), / ˈnænəʊˌtɛk / (BrE)
relating to the science, engineering, or application of materials and devices at the nanoscale, typically less than 100 nanometers.

Examples

Nanotech materials

Researchers are exploring nanotech solutions for more efficient solar panels.
