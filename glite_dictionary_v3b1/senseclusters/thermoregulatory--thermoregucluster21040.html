thermoregulatory

adjective
regulating body temperature
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌθɜrmoʊˈrɛɡjələˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˌθɜːməʊˈrɛɡjʊlətəri / (BrE)
relating to the process of maintaining a stable internal body temperature

Examples

Thermoregulatory response
Sweating is a thermoregulatory response that helps cool the body during exercise.
Certain animals have unique thermoregulatory adaptations to survive in extreme climates.
The hypothalamus plays a key role in the body's thermoregulatory processes.
