otolaryngol

noun
ear, nose, throat specialty
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊtoʊˈlærɪŋɡɒl / (AmE), / ˌəʊtəʊˈlærɪŋɡɒl / (BrE)
the medical specialty concerned with the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the ear, nose, and throat

Examples

Otolaryngol specialty
She decided to specialize in otolaryngol after her medical school rotations.
Advances in otolaryngol have improved treatments for hearing loss and sinus problems.
The hospital's otolaryngol department is known for its expertise in complex throat surgeries.
