merit system

noun
employment system based on ability
5.8 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈmer.ɪt ˈsɪs.təm/ /; / /ˈmer.ət ˈsɪs.təm/ (AmE), /ˈme.rɪt ˈsɪs.təm/ /; / /ˈme.rət ˈsɪs.təm/ (BrE)
a system for hiring and promoting employees, especially in government, based on ability and qualifications rather than on political connections or other non-merit factors

Examples

A merit system for hiring
Many countries replaced the old patronage approach with a formal merit system to prevent corruption.
Promotions in the company are determined strictly through a merit system, ensuring fairness.
The new law requires all government agencies to adopt a merit system for hiring employees.
