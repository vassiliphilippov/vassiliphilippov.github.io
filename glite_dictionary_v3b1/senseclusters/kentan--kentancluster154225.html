kentan

noun
East Asian perennial plant; tiger lily
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛn.tæn / (AmE), / ˈkɛn.tæn / (BrE)
An East Asian perennial plant with large, reddish-orange, black-spotted flowers and strongly reflexed petals, commonly known as the tiger lily.
