hover

verb
remain suspended in the air
4.1 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈhʌ.vər/ (AmE), /ˈhʌ.və/ /; / /ˈhɒ.və/ (BrE)
to stay in one place in the air, without moving in any direction

Examples

A helicopter hovers above
A helicopter began to hover over the accident scene, searching for a place to land.

Suspended in midair, the drone continued to hover silently above the crowd.
