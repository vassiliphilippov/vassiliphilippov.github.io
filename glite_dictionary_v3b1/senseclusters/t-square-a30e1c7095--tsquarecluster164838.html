T-square

noun
drafting tool for drawing straight lines
Pronunciation: / ˈtiˌskwɛr / (AmE), / ˈtiː skweə / (BrE)
a tool with a straightedge and a perpendicular crosspiece used by draftsmen to draw parallel or straight lines

Examples

A T-square tool
Every student in the technical drawing class must have a T-square and a set of pencils.
Her T-square helped her draw perfectly parallel guidelines for her design project.
The architect reached for his T-square to draft the building's elevations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: set square - a triangular tool used for drawing and measuring angles
