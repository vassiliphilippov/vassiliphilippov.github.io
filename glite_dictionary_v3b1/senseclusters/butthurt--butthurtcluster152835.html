butthurt

adjective
easily offended
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtˌhɜrt / (AmE), / ˈbʌtˌhɜːt / (BrE)
easily upset, offended, or annoyed by something minor or trivial, often in a petty or exaggerated way

Examples

A butthurt reaction
He became butthurt when his friends teased him about his new haircut.
Don't get butthurt over a harmless comment; it wasn't meant to offend you.
She was so butthurt after losing the game that she refused to talk to anyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oversensitive - easily hurt or offended by criticism or minor issues
  • antonym: unbothered - not upset or affected by something
