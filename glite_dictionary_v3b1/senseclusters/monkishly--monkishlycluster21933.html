monkishly

adverb
in a monk-like way
9.3
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌŋ.kɪʃ.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈmʌŋ.kɪʃ.lɪ/ (BrE)
in a way that resembles or is characteristic of a monk, especially by being simple or avoiding luxuries

Examples

To live monkishly
He chose to live monkishly, spending his days in quiet study and reflection.
After losing his job, he began to eat and dress monkishly to save money.
She admired how her friend lived monkishly, never seeking attention or material things.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ascetically - in a way that avoids physical pleasures or luxuries
  • antonym: luxuriously - in a way that involves great comfort or wealth
