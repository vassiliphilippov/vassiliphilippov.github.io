translationally

adverb
in a research-to-application way
8.4
Pronunciation: / trænzˈleɪʃənəli / (AmE), / trænzˈleɪʃənəli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to converting basic scientific research into practical or clinical applications

Examples

The new therapy was developed translationally to ensure it could move quickly from the lab to the clinic.
Researchers are working translationally to bridge the gap between scientific discovery and patient care.
The project was designed translationally to turn basic research findings into real-world treatments.
