firebird

noun
mythical glowing bird
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərˌbɝːd /; / ˈfaɪərˌbɜrd / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəbɜːd / (BrE)
a magical or mythical bird from stories and legends, often glowing or associated with fire and rebirth

Examples

The magical firebird
In the old tale, the prince searched the forest for the magical firebird.
Legends say the firebird can light up the night with its glowing feathers.
The ballet tells the story of a hero and the mysterious firebird he must find.
The ancient tale describes a firebird whose feathers glow with the colors of flames.
In the story, the hero must capture the magical firebird to save his kingdom.
Artists often depict the firebird as a symbol of hope and transformation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: phoenix - a mythical bird that rises from its own ashes
