accent

noun
feature that emphasizes by contrast
Pronunciation: / ˈæk.sɛnt / (AmE), / ˈæk.sənt / (BrE)
a feature or detail that gives special importance or emphasis to something, often by contrast or distinction

Examples

A red accent
He placed the vase as a colorful accent on the otherwise plain table.
Her dress features a silver accent that catches the light beautifully.
The designer added a blue accent to the room to make it stand out.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emphasis - special importance or prominence given to something
