crutcher

noun
person using crutches
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌtʃər / (AmE), / ˈkrʌtʃə / (BrE)
a person who uses crutches or assists others in using them, especially after injury or illness

Examples

A skilled crutcher
After his accident, he became a skilled crutcher and helped others regain their mobility.
The hospital volunteer acted as a crutcher for patients learning to walk again.
As a crutcher, she offered support and encouragement to those recovering from leg injuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caregiver - a person who assists others with daily activities or medical needs
  • synonym: patient - a person receiving medical care or treatment
