tiptoe

verb
walk on tiptoes
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪpˌtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɪptəʊ / (BrE)
to walk quietly and carefully on the toes, usually to avoid making noise or to move stealthily

Examples

To tiptoe quietly
He tiptoed into the kitchen for a midnight snack.
She tried to tiptoe past the sleeping baby without making a sound.
The children were tiptoeing around the classroom during the surprise party preparations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: creep - to move slowly and quietly, especially in order to avoid being noticed
  • synonym: sneak - to move in a secretive or stealthy manner
