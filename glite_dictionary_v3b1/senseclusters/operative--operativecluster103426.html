operative

adjective
relating to surgical procedures
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːpəˌreɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˈɒpərətɪv / (BrE)
relating to or involving surgical procedures or treatment by surgery, as opposed to non-surgical medicine.

Examples

Operative surgery
Advances in operative techniques have reduced recovery time for patients.
Her condition required operative intervention, as medication alone was insufficient.
The surgeon discussed the operative risks associated with the procedure.
