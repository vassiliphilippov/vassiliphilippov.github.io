talk about

verb
discuss or mention
Pronunciation: / tɑk əˈbaʊt /; / tɔk əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / tɔːk əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to discuss or mention a subject in speech or conversation

Examples

To talk about a project
At dinner, they talked about the upcoming project and shared their ideas.
She never likes to talk about her past with anyone.
We need to talk about our travel plans before we book anything.
During the meeting, we will talk about the company's future plans.
She spent an hour talking about her experiences traveling abroad.
The book talks about the impact of social media on teenagers.
Are you free later to talk about your new project?
She refused to talk about what happened at the meeting.
We need to talk about the plan before making any decisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mention - to refer to something briefly without going into detail
