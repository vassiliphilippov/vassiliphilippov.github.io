Synonyms

  • discuss
    1.6 180/m
    discuss is used for talking about a subject in detail, while talk about is a general way to mention or cover topics.
  • bring up
    2.6 30/m
    bring up means to introduce a subject into a conversation, while talk about covers discussing or mentioning that subject.
  • chat about
    1.6 2/m
    chat about means to talk in a relaxed, friendly way about a topic, while talk about is neutral and fits many situations.
  • talk of
    2.5 7/m
    talk of means people are mentioning a topic as news or interest, while talk about simply means to discuss or mention it.
  • speak of
    1.9 30/m
    speak of is slightly more formal and often appears in writing, while talk about is more common in everyday speech.
  • mention
    1.0 170/m
    mention means to refer to something briefly or in passing, while talk about implies a fuller discussion or longer conversation.

Related Terms
