shoulder flash

noun
military shoulder insignia
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊldər flæʃ / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊldə flæʃ / (BrE)
a cloth emblem worn on the shoulder of a military uniform, indicating the wearer's division or unit

Each division in the army is marked by a unique shoulder flash on their uniforms.

The soldier proudly displayed his shoulder flash, which identified his regiment.
