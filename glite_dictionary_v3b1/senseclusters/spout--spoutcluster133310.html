Synonyms

  • gush
    3.2 2/m
    gush pours out in large, sudden amounts, while spout names a sudden forceful flow often from a pipe or opening.
  • spurt
    3.1 0.7/m
    spurt sends out bursts of liquid or gas suddenly, while spout describes a steady or sudden forceful stream or jet.
  • jet
    3.0 0.3/m
    jet shoots a narrow, strong stream of liquid or gas, while spout means flow that can be sudden or continuous.
  • squirt
    2.3 2/m
    squirt makes a thin, quick stream of liquid, while spout refers to a larger sudden or steady flow from an opening.
  • spew
    5.1 7/m
    spew forces out large amounts often in a rough, uncontrolled way, while spout focuses on a directed sudden flow.
