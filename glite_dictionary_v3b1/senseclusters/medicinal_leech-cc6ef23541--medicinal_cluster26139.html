medicinal leech

noun
leech used in bloodletting
7.5
Pronunciation: / məˈdɪs.ɪ.nəl liːtʃ / (AmE), / mɪˈdɪs.ɪ.nəl liːtʃ / (BrE)
a large European freshwater leech (Hirudo medicinalis) formerly used in medicine for bloodletting
