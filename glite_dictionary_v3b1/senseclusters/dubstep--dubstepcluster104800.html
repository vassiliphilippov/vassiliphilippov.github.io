dubstep

noun
genre of electronic dance music
7.4 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈdʌb.step/ (AmE), /ˈdʌb.step/ (BrE)
A genre of electronic dance music featuring dark, syncopated rhythms, heavy basslines, and a tempo around 140 beats per minute.
