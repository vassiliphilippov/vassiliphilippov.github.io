afternoon treat

noun
a snack or meal had in the afternoon
Pronunciation: / ˌæftɚˌnun ˈtrit / (AmE), / ˌɑːftəˌnuːn ˈtriːt / (BrE)
A snack or small meal typically eaten in the afternoon, often as a light refreshment.

Examples

An afternoon treat of cookies
After a long day at school, the kids enjoyed an afternoon treat of cookies and milk.
I always look forward to my afternoon treat with a cup of tea.
It's common in some cultures to have a sweet pastry as an afternoon treat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: snack - a small amount of food eaten between meals
