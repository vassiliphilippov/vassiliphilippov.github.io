docent

noun
museum guide
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊsənt / (AmE), / ˈdəʊsənt / (BrE)
a person who guides and educates visitors in a museum, gallery, or similar institution

Examples

Museum docent
The docent explained the history behind each painting in the gallery.
During our visit, a friendly docent answered all of our questions about the exhibits.
Each school group was assigned a docent to guide them through the museum.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curator - a person in charge of a museum or its collections
  • synonym: guide - a person who shows others the way or explains things
