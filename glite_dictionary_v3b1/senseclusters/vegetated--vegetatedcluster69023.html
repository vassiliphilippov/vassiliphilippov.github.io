vegetated

adjective
grown by cuttings
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛdʒɪˌteɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒɪteɪtɪd / (BrE)
formed or produced by vegetative growth or propagation, such as from cuttings or runners rather than from seeds

Examples

Vegetated mats
The hillside was quickly stabilized with vegetated mats to prevent soil erosion.
Farmers often use vegetated strips along fields to improve water quality.
The nursery specializes in vegetated plugs for rapid landscape restoration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: propagated - grown or reproduced by any means
  • antonym: seeded - grown from seeds
