registry

noun
official record or list
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdʒɪstri / (AmE), / ˈrɛdʒɪstri / (BrE)
An official record or list of names, items, or events, often maintained by an authority.

Examples

Wedding registry
Before the ceremony, they signed their names in the wedding registry.
The company was removed from the official registry after it ceased operations.
The hospital maintains a registry of all its patients for medical records.
