dust off

verb
make ready again after neglect
Pronunciation: / dʌst ˈɔf / (AmE), / dʌst ˈɒf / (BrE)
to prepare something for use or action again after it has not been used for a period of time

Examples

To dust off old tools
It's time to dust off my old camera and start taking photos again.
She decided to dust off her Spanish skills before her trip to Mexico.
The company plans to dust off its classic logo for the anniversary celebrations.
