awakening

noun
the act of waking
Pronunciation: / əˈweɪkənɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈweɪkənɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of waking from sleep.

Examples

Morning awakening
After a long awakening, he finally opened his eyes to the morning light.
Her sudden awakening in the middle of the night startled her parents.
The early awakening left him feeling groggy throughout the day.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sleep - the state of rest in which consciousness is suspended
  • synonym: wake - to stop sleeping; to become awake
