Nephelium longana

noun
tree yielding the edible longan fruit
Pronunciation: / nɛˈfiliəm lɒnˈɡɑnə / (AmE), / nɛˈfɛlɪəm ˈlɒŋɡənə / (BrE)
A tropical tree native to southeastern Asia and Australia, grown for its sweet, edible fruit known as longan, which resembles the lychee.

Examples

The Nephelium longana tree
Farmers have started cultivating Nephelium longana commercially in new regions due to high demand.
The botanical garden displays several tropical species, including Nephelium longana, for educational purposes.
The fruit of Nephelium longana is popular in Asian desserts for its translucent flesh and sweet flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: longan - the edible fruit produced by Nephelium longana
  • degree higher: lychee - similar tropical fruit, from Litchi chinensis
