nebulousness

noun
cloud-like quality
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛbjələsnəs / (AmE), / ˈnɛbjʊləsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being like a nebula, with diffuse or cloud-like appearance, especially in space.

Examples

Space nebulousness
Astronomers observed the nebulousness surrounding the distant star cluster through their telescopes.
The nebulousness of the gas clouds made it difficult to determine their exact shape.
Scientists discussed the nebulousness present in the region where new stars are forming.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clarity - the quality of being clear or easy to see
  • synonym: haze - a slight obscuration of the lower atmosphere, typically caused by fine suspended particles
