longnose

noun
insult for big nose
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔŋˌnoʊz / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋˌnəʊz / (BrE)
an informal and often insulting term for a person with a long or prominent nose

Examples

Called longnose at school
The children laughed and called him longnose on the playground, which made him feel embarrassed.
She hated being called longnose by her classmates because of her appearance.
Using the word longnose to describe someone is rude and can hurt their feelings.
