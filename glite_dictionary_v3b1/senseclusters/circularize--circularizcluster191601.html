circularize

verb
distribute circulars or notices
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜːrkjəˌlaɪz /; / ˈsɜrkjələˌraɪz /; / ˈsɝː.kjə.ləˌraɪz /; / ˈsɜːrkjələˌraɪz /; / ˈsɜːr.kjə.ləˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkjʊləˌraɪz /; / ˈsɜːkjʊləraɪz /; / ˈsɜː.kjʊ.ləˌraɪz /; / ˈsɜː.kjʊ.lə.raɪz / (BrE)
to send information, notices, letters, or requests to many people, often to inform, canvass, or gather opinions
