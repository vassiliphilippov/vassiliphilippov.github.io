symbiote

noun
mutually dependent partner
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmbiˌoʊt / (AmE), / ˈsɪmbiəʊt / (BrE)
an organism (or sometimes a person) that lives closely with another, usually so both benefit

Related Terms

  • antonym: parasite - an organism that lives off another and benefits at its expense
  • synonym: symbiont - an organism living in symbiosis with another
