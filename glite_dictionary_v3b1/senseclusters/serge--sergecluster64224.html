serge

noun
strong twilled woollen fabric
Pronunciation: / sɜrdʒ / (AmE), / sɜːdʒ / (BrE)
A strong woollen fabric with a twilled weave, often used for making suits and uniforms.

Examples

A serge fabric
His new suit was made of fine serge imported from Italy.
She wore a navy blue serge skirt to the interview.
The tailor recommended serge for its durability and classic appearance.
