dimple

verb
form dimples when smiling
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪmpəl / (AmE), / ˈdɪmpəl / (BrE)
to form dimples in the skin, especially on the cheeks, typically as a result of smiling

Examples

Her cheeks dimple
As she smiled shyly, her face dimpled with delight.
Her cheeks dimple when she laughs at a funny joke.
The baby dimpled up at his mother during their playful game.
