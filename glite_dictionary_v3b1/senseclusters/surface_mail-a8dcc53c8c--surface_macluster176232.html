surface mail

noun
mail sent by land or sea
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːfɪs meɪl / (AmE), / ˈsɜːfɪs meɪl / (BrE)
mail that is transported by land or sea, rather than by air

Examples

Surface mail delivery
Delivery via surface mail can take several weeks, especially for international destinations.
He chose surface mail because he was not in a hurry to receive the documents.
The package was sent by surface mail to save on postage costs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: airmail - mail that is sent by air
