DM

noun
direct/private message on social media
3.7 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / diÀê …õm / (AmE), / diÀê …õm / (BrE)
A private message sent directly to a user on a social media platform or messaging app, not visible to the public.

Examples

Send a DM
She sent a DM to confirm the meeting details with her colleague.
If you have time, please check your DMs for the event link I posted.
He preferred to handle customer complaints in a DM rather than publicly on the page.

Synonyms

  • pm
    5.5 0.00/m
    pm is a common label for private messages on forums and chat, while DM is the usual term on social media.

Related Terms

  • synonym: direct message - a private message sent directly to a single user on a social platform
  • synonym: private message - a message sent privately between users rather than publicly
  • antonym: public post - a message or update visible to all followers or the public, as opposed to a private DM
