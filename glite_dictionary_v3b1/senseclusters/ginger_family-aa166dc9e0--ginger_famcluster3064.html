ginger family

noun
family of tropical plants including ginger
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪndʒər ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪndʒə ˈfæməli / (BrE)
A family of tropical plants (Zingiberaceae), including ginger and related species, known for aromatic roots and rhizomes.

Many spices, such as turmeric and cardamom, belong to the ginger family.
