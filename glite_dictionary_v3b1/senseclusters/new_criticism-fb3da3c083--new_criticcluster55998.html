new criticism

noun
literary criticism focusing on close reading of text
2.2 7.6
Pronunciation: /nuː ˈkrɪ.tɪ.sɪ.zəm/ /; / /njuː ˈkrɪ.tɪ.sɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /njuː ˈkrɪ.tɪ.sɪ.zəm/ /; / /nuː ˈkrɪ.tɪ.sɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
