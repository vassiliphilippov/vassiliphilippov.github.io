touch-up

adjective
for minor repairs
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌtʃˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈtʌtʃˌʌp / (BrE)
intended for or relating to small repairs or improvements to something that is already finished or in use

Examples

Touch-up paint
He bought a touch-up marker to cover the scratches on his desk.
The painter used a touch-up brush to fix small spots on the wall.
She keeps a touch-up kit in her car for quick paint fixes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: repair - to fix or restore something that is damaged
  • synonym: restore - to bring something back to its original condition
