storage tank

noun
large vessel for holding liquids or gases
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔrɪdʒ tæŋk / (AmE), / ˈstɔːrɪdʒ tæŋk / (BrE)
a large container, typically made of metal, used for holding gases or liquids, often in industrial or commercial settings

Examples

A large storage tank
Rainwater is collected in a large storage tank behind the building.
The chemical plant installed a storage tank to hold the new solvent safely.
The farm uses a storage tank to store liquid fertilizer before applying it to the fields.
