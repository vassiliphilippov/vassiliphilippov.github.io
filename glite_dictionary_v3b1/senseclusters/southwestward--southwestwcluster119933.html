southwestward

noun
direction between south and west (225°)
Pronunciation: / ˌsaʊθˈwɛstwərd / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˈwɛstwəd / (BrE)
the direction or point on the compass that is midway between south and west, corresponding to 225 degrees

Examples

The southwestward point
A steady southwestward breeze cooled the city all afternoon.
The hikers continued in a southwestward direction to reach the campsite before nightfall.
The storm was moving southwestward across the region, bringing heavy rain.
