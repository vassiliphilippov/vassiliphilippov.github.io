bipolarization

noun
onset of bipolar disorder
9.5
Pronunciation: / baɪˌpoʊlərəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / baɪˌpəʊləraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the development or onset of bipolar affective disorder, marked by alternating episodes of mania and depression

Examples

Early bipolarization of mood
The early bipolarization of his mood made diagnosis challenging for the doctors.
Researchers are studying the factors that contribute to the bipolarization of mental health conditions.
Family members noticed signs of bipolarization before the official diagnosis was made.

Related Terms

  • antonym: depression - a mental health disorder characterized by persistent sadness
