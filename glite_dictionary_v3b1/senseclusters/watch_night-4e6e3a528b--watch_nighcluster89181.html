watch night

noun
devotional service on New Year's Eve
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑtʃ ˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɒtʃ ˌnaɪt / (BrE)
A devotional service, especially one held on New Year's Eve to mark the transition into the new year, often involving prayer and reflection.

Examples

A watch night service
Many congregations gather for a watch night service to welcome the new year in prayer.
People attended the watch night meeting to reflect and set intentions for the coming year.
The tradition of watch night has its roots in the Methodist Church's New Year's Eve vigils.
