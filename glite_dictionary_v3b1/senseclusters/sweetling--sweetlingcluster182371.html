sweetling

noun
affectionate term
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈswiːtlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈswiːtlɪŋ / (BrE)
a term of affection for someone who is sweet, dear, or beloved

Examples

My little sweetling
She smiled and hugged her little sweetling before bedtime.
He whispered, 'Goodnight, sweetling,' as he turned off the light.
The grandmother always called her grandchildren her precious sweetlings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sweetheart - a person loved by someone else
