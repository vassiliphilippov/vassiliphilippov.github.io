composite

noun
thing made of different parts
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpɑzət / (AmE), / kəmˈpɒzɪt / (BrE)
a thing made up of different or related parts forming a whole

Examples

A modern composite
The architect designed a modern composite that blends residential, commercial, and recreational areas.
The city center is a composite of old and new buildings, reflecting its long history.
The conference was a composite of experts from various scientific fields.

Related Terms

  • synonym: complex - a whole made up of interrelated parts; complicated structure
  • degree lower: component - a part or element of a larger whole
