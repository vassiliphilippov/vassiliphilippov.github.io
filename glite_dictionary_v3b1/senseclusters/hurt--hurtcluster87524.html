hurt

noun
emotional pain; psychological suffering
Pronunciation: / hɝt / (AmE), / hɜːt /; / həːt / (BrE)
pain, injury, harm, or emotional suffering

Examples

A deep hurt
After the argument, there was a look of hurt in his eyes.
It takes time to recover from the hurt caused by betrayal.
She still feels a deep hurt from her childhood experiences.
She never intended any hurt by her actions.
The criticism did a great hurt to his confidence.
Years of war brought much hurt to the country.
He couldn't hide the deep hurt he felt after their argument.
Over time, her emotional hurt began to heal.
The child's hurt was evident when she started to cry.

  • synonym: harm - physical or psychological damage or injury
  • synonym: injury - physical damage to the body
  • synonym: pain - physical or emotional suffering or discomfort
  • synonym: wound - an injury to the body, especially one involving a cut or break in the skin
