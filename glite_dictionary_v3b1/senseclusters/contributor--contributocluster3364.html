contributor

noun
writer whose work is published in a publication
1.8 3.0
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈtrɪ.bjə.tər/ /; / /kən.ˈtrɪ.bjuː.tər/ (AmE), /kən.ˈtrɪ.bjə.tə/ /; / /kən.ˈtrɪ.bjuː.tə/ (BrE)
