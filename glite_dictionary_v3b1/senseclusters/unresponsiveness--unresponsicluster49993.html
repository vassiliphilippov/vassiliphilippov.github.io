unresponsiveness

noun
lack of response or reaction
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈspɑns.ɪv.nəs / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈspɒn.sɪv.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of not reacting or responding, especially quickly or with emotion, to people or events

Examples

Emotional unresponsiveness
Despite repeated questions, his unresponsiveness frustrated the interviewers.
Her unresponsiveness to my emails made me worry that something was wrong.
Medical staff monitored the patient's unresponsiveness closely after the surgery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: responsiveness - the quality of reacting quickly and positively
