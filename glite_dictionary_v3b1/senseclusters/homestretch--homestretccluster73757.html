homestretch

noun
final stage before completion
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌstrɛtʃ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌstrɛtʃ / (BrE)
the final part of an activity, project, or process; the last stage before completion

Examples

Project homestretch
After months of hard work, we're finally in the homestretch of the project.
She kept her focus during the homestretch and finished the marathon strong.
The team pulled together in the homestretch to meet the tight deadline.

  • synonym: finish line - the end point of a race, especially a marked line
