nonabsorbable

adjective
not able to be absorbed
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnəbˈzɔrbəbəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnəbˈzɔːbəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be absorbed, especially by the body or by a material

Examples

Nonabsorbable stitches
The doctor chose nonabsorbable stitches to ensure the wound stayed closed for a longer period.
Some surgical meshes are made from nonabsorbable materials to provide permanent support.
Because the thread was nonabsorbable, it needed to be removed after the incision healed.
