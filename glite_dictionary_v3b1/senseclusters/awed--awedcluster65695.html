Synonyms

  • awestricken
    8.6 0.00/m
    awestricken is an older form often found in writing, while awed is the common modern word for feeling wonder.
  • wonderstruck
    6.0 0.00/m
    wonderstruck is full of surprise and admiration at beauty, while awed covers a wider mix of reverence or fear.
  • transfixed
    6.7 0.00/m
    transfixed is held motionless by shock or amazement, while awed is the general feeling of wonder, respect, or fear.
  • overawed
    7.2 0.00/m
