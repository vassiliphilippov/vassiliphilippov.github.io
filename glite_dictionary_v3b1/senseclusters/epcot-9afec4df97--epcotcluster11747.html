Epcot

proper noun
Disney model community
Pronunciation: / ˈɛp.kɑt / (AmE), / ˈɛp.kɒt / (BrE)
the planned community concept by Walt Disney called the 'Experimental Prototype Community of Tomorrow', intended as a model town for the future

Examples

Walt Disney's Epcot city
Walt Disney envisioned Epcot as a futuristic city that would solve urban problems.
The original plans for Epcot included advanced transportation and innovative housing designs.
Many urban planners studied Epcot for inspiration in designing modern communities.
