aligner

noun
device for alignment
Pronunciation: / əˈlaɪnər / (AmE), / əˈlaɪnə / (BrE)
a person or device that moves or adjusts parts or objects so they are in the correct position or alignment

Examples

Machine aligner
The orthodontist recommended a clear aligner to straighten her teeth over several months.
A skilled aligner is needed to ensure the machine parts fit together perfectly.
The mechanic used an advanced aligner to check the car's wheel alignment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adjuster - a device or person that makes small changes to improve position or function
  • antonym: misaligner - a device or person that causes things to be out of alignment
