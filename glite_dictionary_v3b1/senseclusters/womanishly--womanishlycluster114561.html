womanishly

adverb
in a feminine way
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊmənɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈwʊmənɪʃli / (BrE)
in a way that is considered characteristic of women, often suggesting delicacy or gentleness

Examples

To act womanishly
He answered womanishly, his voice soft and careful in the tense meeting.
The artist painted womanishly, using delicate strokes and gentle colors.
She moved womanishly through the crowd, graceful and unhurried.

Related Terms

  • synonym: femininely - in a manner typical of women or girls
  • antonym: manfully - in a way considered typical of men, often implying strength or courage
