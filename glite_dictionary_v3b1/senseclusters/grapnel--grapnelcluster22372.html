grapnel

noun
a small anchor with several flukes
8.4 7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈgræp.nəl/ (AmE), /ˈgræp.nəl/ (BrE)
a small anchor or hook with several prongs, used for anchoring, grasping, or holding things (often attached to a rope)
