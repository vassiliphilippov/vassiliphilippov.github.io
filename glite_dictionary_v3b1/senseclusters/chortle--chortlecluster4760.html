chortle

noun
a soft, partly suppressed laugh
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɔr.təl / (AmE), / ˈtʃɔː.təl / (BrE)
a soft, partly suppressed laugh; a joyful, gleeful chuckle

Examples

A happy chortle

She let out a little chortle at his silly joke.
The child suppressed a chortle as he tried not to get caught.
