Synonyms

  • suck in
    3.7 1.10/m
    suck in pulls air or liquid into a space by a partial vacuum, while suck is the general action of drawing things in.
  • suck up
    4.3 0.92/m
    suck up absorbs liquids and sometimes influence in informal speech, while suck is the neutral verb for drawing things in.
  • draw in
    4.0 1.04/m
    draw in pulls something inward by suction or by taking in air, while suck is the common verb for making air or liquid move in.
  • draw
    4.1 4.67/m
    draw takes air or smoke into the mouth or lungs, often used for breathing, while suck focuses on creating a vacuum to pull things in.
