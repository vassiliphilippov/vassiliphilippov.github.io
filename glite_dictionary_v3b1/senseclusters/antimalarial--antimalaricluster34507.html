antimalarial

adjective
against malaria
Pronunciation: / ˌænti məˈlɛriəl / (AmE), / ˌænti məˈlɛːriəl / (BrE)
designed to prevent or treat malaria, a disease transmitted by mosquitoes

Examples

Antimalarial medicine
Doctors recommend antimalarial medications for travelers visiting regions where malaria is common.
The new antimalarial treatment has shown promising results in clinical trials.
Researchers are developing more effective antimalarial drugs to combat resistant strains of the disease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antimalarial drug - a medicine used to prevent or treat malaria
