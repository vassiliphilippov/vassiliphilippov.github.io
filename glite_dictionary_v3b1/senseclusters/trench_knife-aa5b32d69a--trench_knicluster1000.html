trench knife

noun
double-edged combat knife
7.5
Pronunciation: / trɛntʃ naɪf / (AmE), / trɛntʃ naɪf / (BrE)
A knife with a double-edged blade, originally designed for close-quarters combat, especially in trench warfare.

Examples

A trench knife blade
