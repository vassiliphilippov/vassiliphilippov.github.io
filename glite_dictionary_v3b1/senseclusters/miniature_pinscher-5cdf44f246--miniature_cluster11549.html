miniature pinscher

noun
a small German dog breed
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪniəˌtʃɚ ˈpɪnʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪətʃə ˈpɪnʃə / (BrE)
A small, German breed of dog resembling a Doberman pinscher, known for its lively temperament and sleek appearance.

Examples

A miniature pinscher dog
At the dog show, several miniature pinschers competed in the agility trials.
Her family adopted a miniature pinscher from the animal shelter last year.
The miniature pinscher is often mistaken for a small Doberman but is actually a different breed.
