eejit

noun
slang for fool
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈiːdʒɪt / (AmE), / ˈiːdʒɪt / (BrE)
a slang term for a foolish or silly person, especially in Irish or Scottish English

Examples

You silly eejit
He forgot to lock the door again, the eejit.
Only an eejit would try to fix the computer without turning it off first.
She called him an eejit for leaving his umbrella at home on a rainy day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fool - a person lacking good sense or judgment
  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence or ability
  • synonym: idiot - a person who acts in a very stupid or foolish way
