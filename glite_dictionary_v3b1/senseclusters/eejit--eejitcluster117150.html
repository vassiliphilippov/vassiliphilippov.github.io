eejit

noun
slang for fool
Pronunciation: / ˈiːdʒɪt / (AmE), / ˈiːdʒɪt / (BrE)
a slang term for a foolish or silly person, especially in Irish or Scottish English

Examples

She called him an eejit for leaving his umbrella at home on a rainy day.
