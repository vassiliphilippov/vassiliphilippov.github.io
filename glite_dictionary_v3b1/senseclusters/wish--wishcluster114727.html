wish

noun
a desire or longing for something
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /wɪʃ/ (AmE), /wɪʃ/ (BrE)
a desire, hope, or request for something to happen or be true

Examples

Make a wish
He made a wish when he blew out the birthday candles.
Her wish was to travel the world before she turned thirty.
It was my wish to see everyone happy during the holidays.
It was his dying wish to see his family together one last time.
She made a wish before blowing out the candles on her birthday cake.
The company tried to honor the wishes of its customers whenever possible.
He made a silent wish as he blew out the birthday candles.
It is my wish that you both find happiness in your new life together.
It was her deepest wish to see the ocean before she died.
