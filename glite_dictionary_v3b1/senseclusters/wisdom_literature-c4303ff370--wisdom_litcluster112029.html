wisdom literature

noun
biblical books of wisdom and teachings
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪzdəm ˌlɪt̬ərətʃər / (AmE), / ˈwɪzdəm ˌlɪtərətʃə / (BrE)
the biblical books, such as Proverbs, Ecclesiastes, and others, that are considered to contain profound wisdom or teachings

Examples

Wisdom literature books
Biblical scholars often analyze wisdom literature to understand ancient views on morality and life.
