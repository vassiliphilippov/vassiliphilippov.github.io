mids

noun
midterm exams
Pronunciation: / mɪdz / (AmE), / mɪdz / (BrE)
informal plural term for midterm examinations taken in the middle of an academic term or semester

Examples

College mids
I have three mids next week, so I need to start studying now.
After finishing her mids, she felt relieved and went out with friends.
Many students find mids more stressful than final exams.

Related Terms

  • antonym: finals - examinations held at the end of an academic term
  • synonym: midterms - examinations held in the middle of an academic term
