light plane

noun
small private airplane
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪt pleɪn / (AmE), / ˈlaɪt pleɪn / (BrE)
a small, lightweight airplane designed for private or recreational flying

Examples

A light plane flies
He bought a light plane to travel between small towns for business.
Learning to fly a light plane was her dream since childhood.
The light plane landed smoothly on the grassy airstrip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aircraft - a vehicle capable of flight, such as an airplane or helicopter
  • antonym: jet - a fast airplane powered by jet engines
