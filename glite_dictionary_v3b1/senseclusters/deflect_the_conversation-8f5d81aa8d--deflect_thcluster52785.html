deflect the conversation

verb
change the subject to avoid a topic
Pronunciation: / dɪˈflɛkt ðə ˌkɑnvɚˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈflɛkt ðə ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃən / (BrE)
to intentionally change the topic of a discussion to avoid addressing a particular issue

Examples

To deflect the conversation away
She tried to deflect the conversation away from her recent breakup.
They quickly deflected the conversation when financial matters were mentioned.
Whenever politics came up at dinner, he would deflect the conversation to sports.
