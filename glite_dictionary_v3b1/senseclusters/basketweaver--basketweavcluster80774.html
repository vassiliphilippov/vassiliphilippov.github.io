basketweaver

noun
someone who weaves baskets
Pronunciation: / ˈbæskɪtˌwiːvər / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪtˌwiːvə / (BrE)
a person who is skilled in making baskets by weaving materials such as reeds, canes, or willow

Examples

A basketweaver crafts baskets
A local basketweaver supplies beautifully crafted baskets to the village market every Saturday.
My grandfather was a skilled basketweaver who passed his techniques down to the younger generation.
The basketweaver demonstrated her craft at the country fair, attracting a crowd of curious onlookers.
