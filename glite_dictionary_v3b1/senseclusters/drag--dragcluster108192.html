drag

noun
clothing of the opposite sex (esp. for performance)
Pronunciation: / dræɡ / (AmE), / dræɡ / (BrE)
clothes typically worn by the opposite sex, especially used to refer to women's clothing worn by a man, often for performance or entertainment.

Examples

In drag on stage
He dressed in drag for the charity event and stole the show.
Many performers in the cabaret wore drag to entertain the audience.
The film features a scene where the main character appears in drag to disguise himself.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cross-dressing - the act of wearing clothing typically associated with the opposite sex
