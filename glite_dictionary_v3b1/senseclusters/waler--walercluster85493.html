waler

noun
Australian horse breed
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪlər / (AmE), / ˈweɪlə / (BrE)
a breed of horse from Australia, famous for its strength and endurance, especially used by cavalry

Examples

Australian waler horse
The cavalry relied on the waler for long journeys across the Australian outback.
A strong waler was chosen to carry supplies during the expedition.
Many soldiers praised the waler for its stamina and calm temperament in battle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thoroughbred - a breed of horse known for racing and speed
