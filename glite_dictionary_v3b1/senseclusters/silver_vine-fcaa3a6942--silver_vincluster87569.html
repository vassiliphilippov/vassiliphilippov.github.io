silver vine

noun
ornamental Asian vine with silvery leaves and edible fruit
6.3 7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪl.vər vaɪn/ (AmE), /ˈsɪl.və vaɪn/ (BrE)
an ornamental vine of eastern Asia with yellow edible fruit and leaves marked with silver-white patterns, known for its attractiveness and occasional use as a cat stimulant

Examples

The silver vine plant
The silver vine thrives in humid climates and is popular in ornamental gardens across Japan.
The large yellow fruits of the silver vine are both decorative and edible.
When dried, silver vine can have an effect on cats similar to catnip.
