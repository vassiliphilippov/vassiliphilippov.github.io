herbage

noun
collective vegetation of pastureland
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɝːbɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈhɜːbɪdʒ / (BrE)
succulent herbaceous plants collectively, especially those found in pastureland and eaten by grazing animals.

Examples

Fresh herbage in pasture
After the rain, the pasture was thick with herbage for the cattle to graze.
Spring brings fresh herbage that attracts wildlife to the meadow.
The farmer checked the quality of the herbage before letting the sheep out to feed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vegetation - plants collectively, especially in a particular area or habitat
