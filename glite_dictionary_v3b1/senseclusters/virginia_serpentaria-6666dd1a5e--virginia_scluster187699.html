Virginia serpentaria

noun
a North American woodland birthwort
Pronunciation: / vərˈdʒɪn.jə ˌsɜr.pənˈtɛr.i.ə / (AmE), / vəˈdʒɪn.i.ə ˌsɜː.pənˈteə.ri.ə / (BrE)
A species of birthwort (Aristolochia serpentaria) native to the woodlands of the eastern United States, known for its slender, curved roots.
