exfoliant

adjective
removing dead skin
5.6
Pronunciation: /ɪks.ˈfoʊ.lɪ.ənt/ /; / /eks.ˈfoʊ.lɪ.ənt/ (AmE), /ɪks.ˈfəʊ.lɪ.ənt/ /; / /eks.ˈfəʊ.lɪ.ənt/ (BrE)
