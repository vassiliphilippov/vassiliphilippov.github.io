cade

noun
hand-reared animal
Pronunciation: / keɪd / (AmE), / keɪd / (BrE)
an abandoned or hand-reared animal, especially a young lamb or calf raised by humans after being rejected by its mother

Examples

Cade lamb
The farmer cared for the cade lamb after its mother died.
Raising a cade calf requires extra attention and bottle feeding.
She adopted a cade goat that had been left alone in the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fosterling - a young animal or child raised by someone not its parent
  • synonym: orphan - a young animal without its mother
