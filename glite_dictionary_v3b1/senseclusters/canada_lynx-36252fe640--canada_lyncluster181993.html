Canada lynx

noun
wild cat species of northern North America
Pronunciation: / ˈkænədə lɪŋks / (AmE), / ˈkænədə lɪŋks / (BrE)
a medium-sized wild cat with tufted ears and a short tail, native to forests of northern North America

Examples

The Canada lynx animal
Scientists are tracking the population of the Canada lynx to study its conservation status.
The Canada lynx is specially adapted to cold, snowy environments.
Thick fur and large paws help the Canada lynx survive in harsh northern forests.
