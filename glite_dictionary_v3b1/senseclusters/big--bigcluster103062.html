big

adjective
generous, tolerant; magnanimous
Pronunciation: / bɪɡ / (AmE), / bɪɡ / (BrE)
generous, understanding, or giving freely; showing magnanimity or forgivingness

Examples

A big heart
He made a big gesture by helping his competitor after losing the race.
Her big heart showed when she welcomed everyone, even those who had wronged her.
It was very big of you to forgive her so quickly.
He was known as a big tipper at every restaurant he visited.
Her big donations helped many local charities thrive.
They received a big welcome from the community upon their arrival.
