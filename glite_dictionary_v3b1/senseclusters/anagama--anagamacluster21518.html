anagama

noun
Japanese pottery kiln
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌænəˈɡɑːmə / (AmE), / ˌænəˈɡɑːmə / (BrE)
a traditional Japanese wood-fired kiln used for firing pottery, often producing unique glaze effects

Examples

A anagama kiln
The potter built an anagama in the forest to fire his handmade ceramics.
Firing pottery in an anagama can create beautiful natural ash glazes.
Artists from around the world visit Japan to learn about the anagama tradition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: noborigama - a multi-chambered climbing kiln used in Japanese pottery
