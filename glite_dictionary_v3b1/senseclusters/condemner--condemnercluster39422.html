condemner

noun
person declaring unfit
Pronunciation: / kənˈdɛm.nɚ / (AmE), / kənˈdɛm.nə / (BrE)
a person or official who declares a building or property unfit for use and orders its closure, demolition, or seizure

Examples

Building condemner
The condemner visited the site to assess the safety of the old warehouse.
After the inspection, the condemner posted a notice on the building's door.
Residents waited anxiously for the condemner to announce the fate of their homes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: approver - a person who officially accepts or authorizes something
  • synonym: inspector - an official who examines buildings for safety or compliance
