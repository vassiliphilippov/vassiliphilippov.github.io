tenfold

adjective
consisting of ten parts; ten times as much
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛnˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈtɛn.fəʊld /; / ˈtɛnˌfəʊld / (BrE)
consisting of ten parts or being ten times as much or as many

Examples

Tenfold increase
He received a tenfold return on his investment in technology.
Membership in the club grew tenfold after the new program launched.
The company's profits increased tenfold over the past decade.
Profits rose to tenfold their original amount after the new product launch.
She experienced tenfold improvement in her performance through consistent practice.
The population of the city increased tenfold over the last century.
