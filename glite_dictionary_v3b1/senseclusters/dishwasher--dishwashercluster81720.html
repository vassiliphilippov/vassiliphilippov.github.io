dishwasher

noun
machine for washing dishes automatically
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪʃˌwɑːʃɚ /; / ˈdɪʃˌwɑʃər / (AmE), / ˈdɪʃˌwɒʃə / (BrE)
a machine that washes dishes, cookware, and utensils automatically
