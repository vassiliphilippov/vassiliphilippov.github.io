unmetaphysical

adjective
not abstract or philosophical
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnmɛtəˈfɪzɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnmɛtəˈfɪzɪkəl / (BrE)
not related to metaphysics or abstract philosophical ideas; concerned only with concrete or observable matters

Examples

An unmetaphysical approach
Her approach to the problem was entirely unmetaphysical, focusing on practical solutions.
The scientist gave an unmetaphysical account of the phenomenon, avoiding any philosophical speculation.
In his writing, he preferred an unmetaphysical style that dealt only with observable facts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concrete - based on facts, things, or actions, not on ideas or feelings
  • antonym: metaphysical - relating to abstract philosophical ideas beyond the physical world
  • synonym: practical - concerned with real situations rather than ideas or theories
