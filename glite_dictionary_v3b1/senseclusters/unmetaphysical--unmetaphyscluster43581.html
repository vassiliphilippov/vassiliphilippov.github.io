unmetaphysical

adjective
not abstract or philosophical
8.2
Pronunciation: /ʌn.met.ə.ˈfɪz.ɪ.kəl/ /; / /ən.met.ə.ˈfɪz.ɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /ən.met.ə.ˈfɪz.ɪ.kəl/ /; / /ʌn.met.ə.ˈfɪz.ɪ.kəl/ (BrE)

Examples

An unmetaphysical approach
