cataractous

adjective
relating to cataract
Pronunciation: / ˌkætəˈræktəs / (AmE), / ˌkætəˈræktəs / (BrE)
affected by or relating to a cataract in the eye

Examples

cataractous eye lens
The surgeon examined the patient's cataractous lens before recommending treatment.
Vision can become blurry in a cataractous eye due to the clouded lens.
After surgery, the formerly cataractous eye showed significant improvement in clarity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cataract - a clouding of the lens of the eye
  • antonym: clear - not affected by cloudiness or cataract
