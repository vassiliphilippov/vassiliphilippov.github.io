postmodernist

noun
advocate of postmodernism
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstˈmɑːdərnɪst / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈmɒdənɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or practices postmodernism, especially in art, literature, or culture, often questioning traditional ideas and conventions

Examples

A postmodernist thinker
The postmodernist challenged the traditional boundaries between different art forms in his latest exhibition.
Many postmodernists believe that there is no single truth in literature or culture.
As a postmodernist, she often questioned the established rules of architecture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: avant-gardist - a person who introduces new and experimental ideas in art or culture
  • antonym: modernist - a person who supports modernism in art or culture
