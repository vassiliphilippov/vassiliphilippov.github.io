enterocolitis

noun
intestinal inflammation
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɛntəroʊkəˈlaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌɛntərəʊkəˈlaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of both the small intestine and the colon

Examples

Enterocolitis inflammation
The patient was diagnosed with enterocolitis after experiencing severe abdominal pain and diarrhea.
Treatment for enterocolitis often includes antibiotics and supportive care in the hospital.
Children with weakened immune systems are at higher risk of developing enterocolitis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gastroenteritis - inflammation of the stomach and intestines
