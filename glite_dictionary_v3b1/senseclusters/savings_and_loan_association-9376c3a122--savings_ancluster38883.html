savings and loan association

noun
type of financial institution specializing in home mortgages
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪvɪŋz ənd loʊn əˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈseɪvɪŋz ənd ləʊn əˌsəʊsiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
a financial institution that accepts savings deposits and is primarily engaged in making mortgage loans, especially for residential property

Examples
