nonowner

noun
person without vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈoʊnər / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈəʊnə / (BrE)
a person who does not own a motor vehicle but may drive one, often for insurance purposes

Examples

Nonowner car insurance
As a nonowner, he needed special insurance to drive his friend's car occasionally.
The policy is designed for nonowners who frequently rent or borrow vehicles.
Even though she is a nonowner, she drives her parents' car on weekends.
