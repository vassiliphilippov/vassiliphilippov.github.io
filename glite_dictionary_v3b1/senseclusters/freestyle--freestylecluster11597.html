freestyle

noun
unrestricted style
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːstaɪl / (AmE), / ˈfriːstaɪl / (BrE)
a style of performance, competition, or activity without fixed rules, allowing for individual creativity or improvisation

Examples

Freestyle dance round
The dance competition included a freestyle round where participants could show off their own moves.
In swimming, the freestyle event allows athletes to use any stroke they prefer.
He impressed the crowd with his quick thinking during the freestyle rap battle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: improvisation - the act of creating or performing spontaneously
  • antonym: routine - a fixed or set way of performing something
