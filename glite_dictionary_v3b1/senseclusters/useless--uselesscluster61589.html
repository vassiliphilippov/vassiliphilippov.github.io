useless

adjective
having no use or value
1.0 23/m
Pronunciation: / Ààjusl…ôs / (AmE), / ÀàjuÀêsl…ôs / (BrE)
Having no practical use or unable to perform its intended function; not helpful or effective.

Examples

Useless effort
After hours of searching, their efforts proved useless.
He felt useless sitting there with nothing to do.
Many people find that tool completely useless for fixing the problem.

Synonyms

  • ineffective
    3.4 9.06/m
    ineffective fails to produce the desired result in practice, while useless cannot perform its intended function or be helpful.
  • unusable
    2.3 1.12/m
    unusable cannot be used because it is not fit or safe, while useless may be usable but fails to help or work.
  • futile
    6.0 6.54/m
    futile describes an effort that cannot succeed, while useless describes something that has no practical use or effect.
  • pointless
    3.5 8.19/m
    pointless has no purpose or reason and feels wasteful, while useless fails to do its intended job or be helpful.
  • purposeless
    5.3 0.42/m
    purposeless lacks aim or direction and feels empty, while useless fails to serve a practical function or help people.
  • worthless
    3.0 11/m
    worthless has no value or importance, often in money or esteem, while useless means it cannot do its job or help.
