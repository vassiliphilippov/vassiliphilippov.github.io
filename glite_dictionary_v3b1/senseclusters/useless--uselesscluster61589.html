useless

adjective
having no use or value
Pronunciation: / ˈjusləs / (AmE), / ˈjuːsləs / (BrE)
Having no practical use or unable to perform its intended function; not helpful or effective.

Examples

Useless effort
After hours of searching, their efforts proved useless.
He felt useless sitting there with nothing to do.
Many people find that tool completely useless for fixing the problem.
