lobster thermidor

noun
lobster dish with cheese sauce, gratinéed
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑb.stɚ θɜr.məˌdɔr / (AmE), / ˈlɒb.stə ˌθɜː.mɪˈdɔː / (BrE)
a dish of diced lobster meat mixed with a rich cheese sauce (Mornay sauce), returned to its shell, sprinkled with cheese, and browned under a grill or broiler.
