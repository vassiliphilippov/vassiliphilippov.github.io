originality

noun
quality of being new or creative
1.0 3.7
Pronunciation: /ə.rɪ.dʒə.ˈnæ.lɪ.tiː/ /; / /ə.rɪ.dʒɪ.ˈnæ.lɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /ə.rɪ.dʒɪ.ˈnæ.lɪ.tɪ/ /; / /ə.rɪ.dʒə.ˈnæ.lɪ.tɪ/ (BrE)
the quality of being new, creative, or not copied from something else; creativity and independent thinking

Examples

Creative originality
Critics praised the film for its originality and fresh perspective.

His originality allows him to view problems from entirely new perspectives.
The book stands out for its remarkable originality and depth of insight.
