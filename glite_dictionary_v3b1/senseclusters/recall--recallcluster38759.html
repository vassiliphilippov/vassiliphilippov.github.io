recall

noun
official request to return
2.3
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɔl / (AmE), / rɪˈkɔːl / (BrE)
an official request or order for someone to return, especially to their original place or position

Examples

Official recall order
After the faulty product was identified, a nationwide recall was announced.
The government issued a recall of its ambassador after the incident.
The sudden recall of the diplomat surprised many in the embassy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: summons - an official call to appear or return, often in legal or formal contexts
