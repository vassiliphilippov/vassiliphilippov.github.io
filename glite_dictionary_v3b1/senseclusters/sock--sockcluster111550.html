sock

noun
a fabric device for showing wind direction
8.2 1.30/m
Pronunciation: / sÉ‘k / (AmE), / sÉ’k / (BrE)
a tubular fabric device, often cone-shaped and mounted on a pole, used for indicating wind direction, especially at airports

Examples

Airport wind sock
During the storm, the sock flapped violently atop the control tower.
Pilots often check the sock before takeoff to judge the wind conditions.
The airport's sock pointed steadily to the north, indicating strong winds.

Synonyms

  • windsock
    5.1 0.13/m
    windsock is the common airport cone that shows wind direction and strength, while sock is a less common name for the same device.
  • wind cone
    5.5 0.00/m
    wind cone is a two-word term for the mast-mounted fabric cone seen at airfields, while sock is the shorter, less specific name.
  • drogue
    9.9 0.00/m
    drogue is a technical or older aviation term for a wind cone on a mast, while sock is the everyday airport name.
