bonder

noun
person who connects others
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑndər / (AmE), / ˈbɒndə / (BrE)
a person who helps others form social or emotional connections and brings people together

Examples

A natural bonder
As a natural bonder, he quickly made everyone feel comfortable at the party.
The team relied on their bonder to help new members feel included.
Her role as a bonder was essential for building trust within the group.

Related Terms

  • antonym: divider - a person who causes separation or conflict between people
  • synonym: facilitator - a person who makes it easier for a group to work together
  • synonym: mediator - a person who helps people resolve differences and reach agreement
