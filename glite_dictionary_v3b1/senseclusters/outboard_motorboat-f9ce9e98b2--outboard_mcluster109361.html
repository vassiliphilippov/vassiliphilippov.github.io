outboard motorboat

noun
motorboat with outboard motor
5.8 4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈaʊt.bɔːrd ˈmoʊ.tər.boʊt/ (AmE), /ˈaʊt.bɔːd ˈməʊ.tə.bəʊt/ (BrE)
