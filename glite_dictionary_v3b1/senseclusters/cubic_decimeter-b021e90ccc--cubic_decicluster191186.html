cubic decimeter

noun
metric unit of volume (1,000 cm³)
7.6 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkjuː.bɪk ˈde.sɪ.miː.tər/ (AmE), /ˈkjuː.bɪk ˈde.sɪ.miː.tə/ (BrE)
A metric unit of volume equal to one liter, or 1,000 cubic centimeters.

Examples

One cubic decimeter
He filled the aquarium with exactly ten cubic decimeters of water.

The tank has a capacity of 75 cubic decimeters, which is equivalent to 75 liters.
A cubic decimetre of water is exactly equal to one litre.
Scientifically, a cubic decimetre is defined as 1,000 cubic centimetres.
The engine's displacement is measured in cubic decimetres or litres.
