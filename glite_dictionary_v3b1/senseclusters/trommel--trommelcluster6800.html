trommel

noun
rotating screening device
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtrɑː.məl/ /; / /ˈtroʊ.məl/ (AmE), /ˈtrɒ.məl/ (BrE)
a rotating cylindrical screen used to separate materials by size, especially in mining or recycling

Examples

Mining trommel
The mining company used a large trommel to sort rocks and soil by size.
A trommel is often used in recycling plants to separate different types of waste.
Workers cleaned the trommel regularly to keep the screening process efficient.
