Philippian

proper noun
person from Philippi
Pronunciation: / fɪˈlɪpiən / (AmE), / fɪˈlɪpiən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Philippi, or a member of the early Christian church in Philippi

Examples

Philippian church member
The Philippian church played an important role in the spread of Christianity.
A Philippian merchant welcomed Paul and Silas into her home.
Many Philippians are mentioned in the letters of the New Testament.
