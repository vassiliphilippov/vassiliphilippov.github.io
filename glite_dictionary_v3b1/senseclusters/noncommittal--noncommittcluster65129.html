noncommittal

adjective
not revealing commitment or opinion
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkəˈmɪtəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkəˈmɪtəl / (BrE)
not expressing or revealing commitment to a definite opinion, course of action, or decision

Examples

A noncommittal answer
His tone was noncommittal; I couldn't tell whether he agreed or disagreed with the plan.
The doctor's noncommittal answer left us uncertain about the possible outcomes.
When asked about the merger, the CEO gave a noncommittal response and avoided specifics.

Related Terms

  • antonym: committed - willing to give time and energy to something; devoted
  • synonym: indecisive - not able to make decisions quickly or firmly
