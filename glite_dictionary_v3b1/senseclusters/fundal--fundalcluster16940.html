fundal

adjective
of an organ's base
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌndəl / (AmE), / ˈfʌndəl / (BrE)
relating to the fundus, which is the base or bottom part of an organ in the body

Examples

Fundal placenta
The ultrasound showed a fundal placenta in the upper part of the uterus.
A fundal examination is important during pregnancy check-ups.
The doctor measured the fundal height to assess fetal growth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: basal - relating to the base of something
