English cocker spaniel

noun
a breed of small dog with wavy hair
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈkɑkər spæn.jəl / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈkɒkə ˈspæn.jəl / (BrE)
A breed of small dog with wavy, silky hair, known for its lively temperament and developed originally in England.

Examples

An English cocker spaniel dog
Her family has bred English cocker spaniels for generations.
The English cocker spaniel is known for its affectionate nature and beautiful coat.
They adopted an English cocker spaniel puppy from the animal shelter last month.
