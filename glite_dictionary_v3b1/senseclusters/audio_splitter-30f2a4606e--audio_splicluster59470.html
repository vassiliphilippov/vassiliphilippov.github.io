audio splitter

noun
device for dividing audio output
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːdioʊ ˈsplɪtər / (AmE), / ˈɔːdiəʊ ˈsplɪtə / (BrE)
a device that divides an audio signal into two or more outputs, allowing multiple headphones or speakers to be connected to a single audio source

Examples

An audio splitter device
He connected two pairs of headphones to his phone using an audio splitter.
If you want to record a podcast with a guest, an audio splitter is very useful.
The audio splitter made it easy for everyone to listen to the same song during the car ride.

Related Terms

  • synonym: splitter - a device that divides something into two or more parts
