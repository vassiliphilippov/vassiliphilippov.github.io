available

adjective
able to be used or obtained
Pronunciation: / əˈveɪləbəl / (AmE), / əˈveɪləbəl /; / əˈveɪləbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be used, obtained, or accessed; not currently occupied or in use

Examples

Available for use
All conference rooms are available for booking after 5 p.m.
I'm sorry, but Dr. Lee is not available to take your call right now.
This model is available in several different colors and sizes.
Is this seat available or is someone sitting here?
The latest update will be available to download tonight.
We have several rooms available for rent starting next month.

  • synonym: obtainable - able to be obtained or gotten
  • antonym: unavailable - not able to be used or obtained
