polish

noun
smoothness and glossiness
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑː.lɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɒl.ɪʃ / (BrE)
the quality of being smooth and glossy in appearance or texture

Examples

A car's polish
A good wax will restore the lost polish to your old car.
The marble floors had a beautiful polish that reflected the light.
Years of handling gave the wood a natural polish and warmth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dullness - the lack of shine or gloss
  • synonym: lustre - a gentle shine or soft glow, especially of a surface
  • synonym: sheen - a soft, smooth shine on a surface
