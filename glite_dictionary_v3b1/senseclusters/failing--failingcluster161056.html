failing

noun
instance of failing to meet a standard
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfeɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
an instance of not achieving the minimum required standard or performance, especially in an examination or test

Examples

A failing on a test
Despite a single failing, she managed to maintain her scholarship.
His failing in mathematics meant he had to repeat the course.
The school's policy is strict when it comes to repeated failings by students.
