non-executive

noun
board member without management role
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn ɪɡˈzɛkjətɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒn ɪɡˈzɛkjʊtɪv / (BrE)
a person, especially on a company's board, who does not have executive or day-to-day management responsibilities

Examples

A non-executive on the board
The board appointed a new non-executive to ensure independent oversight of company policies.
As a non-executive, she provides advice but does not manage daily operations.
Several non-executives attended the annual meeting to review the company's performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: director - a member of a company's board responsible for overseeing activities
  • antonym: executive - a person with management or decision-making responsibilities in a company
