terrarium

noun
enclosed container for growing plants
5.4
Pronunciation: / təˈrɛriəm / (AmE), / təˈrɛːrɪəm / (BrE)
a glass container, usually enclosed, for keeping and observing living plants or small land animals indoors

Examples

A glass terrarium
After watering the plants, she carefully closed the lid of her glass terrarium to maintain humidity.
He created a beautiful terrarium filled with ferns and moss for his living room.
The children observed the tiny ecosystem inside the terrarium during their science class.
She created a beautiful terrarium filled with moss, ferns, and tiny figurines.
The biology class observed insects living in a terrarium for their science project.
A terrarium can be a great way to bring a bit of nature indoors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aquarium - a glass tank for keeping and displaying aquatic animals or plants
  • synonym: vivarium - an enclosure for keeping and raising living animals or plants for observation or research
