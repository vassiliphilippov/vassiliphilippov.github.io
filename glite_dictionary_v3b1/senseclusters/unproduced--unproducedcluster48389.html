unproduced

adjective
not provided as required
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnprəˈduːst / (AmE), / ˌʌnprəˈdjuːst / (BrE)
not presented, supplied, or delivered when required, especially in legal or official contexts.

Examples

Unproduced evidence
The lawyer explained that the unproduced evidence could be crucial to the case.
Several unproduced documents were requested again by the court.
The investigation was delayed due to unproduced records from the company.

Related Terms

  • antonym: submitted - presented or delivered as required
  • synonym: withheld - deliberately not given or released
