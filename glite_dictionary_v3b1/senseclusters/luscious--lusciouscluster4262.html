luscious

adjective
richly pleasing to the taste or smell
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌʃəs / (AmE), / ˈlʌʃəs / (BrE)
having a rich, sweet, and pleasant taste or smell that is very pleasing to the senses, especially taste

Examples

Luscious chocolate cake
Her chocolate cake was famous for its luscious flavor and creamy texture.
The strawberries were so luscious that I couldn't stop eating them.
We enjoyed a luscious meal made with fresh local ingredients.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bland - lacking strong flavor or taste
  • synonym: delicious - very pleasant to taste or smell
