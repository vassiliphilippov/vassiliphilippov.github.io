rum cherry

noun
species of North American wild cherry
Pronunciation: / rʌm ˈtʃɛri / (AmE), / rʌm ˈtʃɛri / (BrE)
A large North American wild cherry tree (Prunus serotina) with round, black, sour edible fruit.

Examples

Wild rum cherry tree
Birds are especially fond of the tart fruit produced by the rum cherry.
He used rum cherry wood to craft a durable piece of furniture.
The rum cherry thrives in forests throughout the eastern United States.
