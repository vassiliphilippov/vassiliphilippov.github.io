smoke bush

noun
ornamental shrub with smoke-like flower clusters
8.3
Pronunciation: / smoʊk bʊʃ / (AmE), / sməʊk bʊʃ / (BrE)
a shrub or small tree with clusters of airy, smoke-like flowers, known as 'smoke bush'
