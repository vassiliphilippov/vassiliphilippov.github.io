posttraumatic

adjective
after trauma
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊsttrəˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌpəʊsttrəˈmatɪk / (BrE)
relating to or occurring after a traumatic event or injury

Examples

Posttraumatic stress symptoms
The patient developed posttraumatic stress symptoms after the accident.
Doctors monitored her for any posttraumatic complications following the surgery.
He attended therapy to address his posttraumatic anxiety.
