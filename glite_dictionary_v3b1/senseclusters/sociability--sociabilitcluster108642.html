sociability

noun
quality of being sociable
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊʃiəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌsəʊsiəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality or tendency of being sociable; the enjoyment of or disposition toward friendly social interactions

Examples

Friendly sociability
Despite his sociability, he enjoyed spending quiet evenings at home.
Her natural sociability made her popular at every gathering.
The club encourages sociability among its members through various events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sociableness - the quality of being sociable
  • antonym: unsociability - the tendency to avoid social interaction
