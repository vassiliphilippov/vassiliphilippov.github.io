spearlike

adjective
resembling a spear
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪrˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈspɪəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or shaped like a spear, especially in being long and pointed

Examples

Spearlike spines
The cactus has spearlike spines that protect it from animals.
She admired the spearlike shape of the tall, narrow leaves in the garden.
The sculpture featured a spearlike design that reached toward the sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lanceolate - shaped like a lance or spearhead, especially in reference to leaves
  • antonym: rounded - having a curved or circular shape, not pointed
