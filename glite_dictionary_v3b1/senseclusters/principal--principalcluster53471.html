principal

adjective
most important; main
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪnsəpəl / (AmE), / ˈprɪnsəpəl / (BrE)
most important; main; chief in rank, authority, or importance

Examples

Principal reason
His principal concern is the safety of the children.
The principal reason for her resignation was a disagreement with the board.
The principal streets of the town were closed for the festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: main - chief or most important
  • synonym: primary - of chief importance; principal
  • antonym: secondary - less important; coming after the main
