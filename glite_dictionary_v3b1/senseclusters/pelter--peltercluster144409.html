pelter

noun
a heavy or violent rain
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛltər / (AmE), / ˈpɛltə / (BrE)
a very heavy or violent rainstorm; a downpour

Examples

A heavy pelter
Rain poured down in a pelter, drenching everyone within minutes.
They waited under the awning for the pelter to let up before heading outside.
We got caught in a real pelter on our way home from the market.
