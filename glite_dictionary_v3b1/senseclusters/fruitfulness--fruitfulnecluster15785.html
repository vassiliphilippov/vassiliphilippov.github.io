fruitfulness

noun
the quality of producing growth or results
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfruːtfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈfruːtfəlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of producing healthy growth, results, or benefits; the state of being fruitful

Examples

Fruitfulness of the land
Rainfall plays a vital role in the fruitfulness of the region's soil.
She admired the fruitfulness of his imagination and creativity.
The fruitfulness of the land ensured a good harvest every year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: barrenness - the quality of being unproductive or infertile
  • synonym: fertility - the ability to produce offspring or growth
