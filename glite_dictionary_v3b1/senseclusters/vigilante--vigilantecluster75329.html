vigilante

noun
unofficial law enforcer
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌvɪdʒəˈlænti / (AmE), / ˌvɪdʒɪˈlænti / (BrE)
a person who tries to enforce the law or punish wrongdoers without legal authority, often as part of a group

Examples

A vigilante group
Authorities warned that taking vigilante action could lead to more violence.
Some residents formed a vigilante group to patrol the neighborhood at night.
The story follows a vigilante who seeks justice outside the legal system.

Related Terms

  • antonym: outlaw - a person who has broken the law, especially one who remains at large or is a fugitive
  • synonym: self-appointed - acting without official authority or appointment; self-assumed
