subtenant

noun
person renting from tenant
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌtɛnənt / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌtɛnənt / (BrE)
a person who rents property from a tenant rather than directly from the property owner

Examples

A subtenant rents
The subtenant agreed to follow all the rules set by the main tenant.
If the subtenant damages the apartment, the main tenant may be held responsible.
After moving in, the subtenant paid rent directly to the tenant each month.

Related Terms

  • antonym: landlord - a person who owns and rents out property
  • synonym: tenant - a person who rents property from the owner
