fallopian

adjective
of the uterine tubes
Pronunciation: / fəˈloʊpiən / (AmE), / fəˈləʊpiən / (BrE)
relating to the tubes that connect the ovaries to the uterus in female mammals

Examples

Fallopian tubes
The surgeon carefully examined the fallopian tubes for any signs of damage.
A blockage in the fallopian tubes can affect a woman's fertility.
Doctors use special tests to check the health of the fallopian tubes.
