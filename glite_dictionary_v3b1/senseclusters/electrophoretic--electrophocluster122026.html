electrophoretic

adjective
relating to electrophoresis
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊfəˈrɛtɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəfəˈrɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or caused by electrophoresis, the movement of particles through a fluid under the influence of an electric field

Examples

Electrophoretic mobility
Changes in buffer composition affected the electrophoretic behavior of the molecules.
Researchers analyzed the samples using electrophoretic techniques to separate the DNA fragments.
The electrophoretic mobility of the protein was measured in the laboratory.
