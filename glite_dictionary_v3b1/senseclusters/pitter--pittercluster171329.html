pitter

noun
fruit pit remover
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪtər / (AmE), / ˈpɪtə / (BrE)
a tool, machine, or person that removes the pit or stone from fruit such as cherries or olives

Examples

Cherry pitter
She used a pitter to quickly remove the stones from a bowl of cherries.
The bakery invested in an automatic pitter to speed up their pie production.
Before making the jam, he carefully ran each olive through the pitter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corer - a tool for removing the core from fruit
  • synonym: de-stoner - a machine or tool for removing stones from fruit
