gun barrel

noun
the tube of a gun from which a bullet is fired
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌn ˌbærəl / (AmE), / ˈɡʌn ˌbærəl / (BrE)
the long metal tube of a gun through which the bullet or shot travels when the gun is fired
