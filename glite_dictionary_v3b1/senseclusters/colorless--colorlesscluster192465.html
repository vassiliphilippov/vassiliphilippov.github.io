colorless

adjective
dull or uninteresting; lacking variety
4.8 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌ.lər.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈkʌ.lə.ləs/ (BrE)
Lacking in variety, interest, or distinctive qualities; dull or uninteresting.

Examples

A colorless story
Critics called the film colorless, saying it lacked originality and excitement.
His memoir was surprisingly colorless, despite his adventurous life.

He gave a colourless report that failed to capture anyone's attention.
The professor's lectures were so colourless that many students struggled to stay awake.
