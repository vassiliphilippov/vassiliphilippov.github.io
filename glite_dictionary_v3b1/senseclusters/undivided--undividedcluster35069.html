undivided

adjective
given wholly to one thing; not shared
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.dɪˈvaɪdɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈvaɪdɪd / (BrE)
(of attention, affection, etc.) given completely to one person or thing; not shared or distributed among several things.

Examples

Undivided attention
His undivided loyalty made him a trusted friend.
I want your undivided attention while I explain the rules.
She gave her undivided support to the new project, ignoring all distractions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exclusive - not shared; restricted to one person or thing
  • antonym: shared - used or enjoyed by more than one person or group
