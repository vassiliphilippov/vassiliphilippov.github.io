retributor

noun
one who repays
9.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtrɪbjutər / (AmE), / rɪˈtrɪbjʊtə / (BrE)
a person who repays or returns something, especially as payment or compensation; now rare or archaic

Examples

Debt retributor
The retributor ensured that every debt was settled without delay.
In ancient tales, a retributor would restore justice by returning what was taken.
She prided herself on being a fair retributor in all her dealings.

Related Terms

  • antonym: debtor - a person who owes money
  • synonym: repayer - a person who pays back money or a favor
