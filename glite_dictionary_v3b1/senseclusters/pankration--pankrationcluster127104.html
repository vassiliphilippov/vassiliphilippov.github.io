pankration

noun
ancient combat sport
9.9
Pronunciation: / pænˈkreɪʃiən / (AmE), / panˈkreɪʃiən / (BrE)
an ancient Greek sport that combined wrestling and boxing, allowing almost all fighting techniques

Examples

Ancient pankration event
The ancient Olympics featured pankration as a popular event.
Competitors in pankration used both striking and grappling techniques to defeat their opponents.
Historians consider pankration one of the earliest forms of mixed martial arts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wrestling - a sport involving grappling and holds
