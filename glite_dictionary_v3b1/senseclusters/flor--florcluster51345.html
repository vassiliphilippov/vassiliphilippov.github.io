flor

noun
yeast film on wine
9.9
Pronunciation: /flɔːr/ (AmE), /flɔː/ (BrE)
a layer of yeast that forms on the surface of certain wines, especially sherry, during fermentation and aging

Examples

The flor layer
The unique taste of this sherry comes from the flor that develops during aging.
Winemakers carefully monitor the flor to ensure the wine matures correctly.
Without a healthy flor, the wine would lose its distinctive flavor and aroma.

Related Terms

  • synonym: yeast - a microorganism used in fermentation
