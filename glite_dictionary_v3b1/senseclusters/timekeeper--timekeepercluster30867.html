timekeeper

noun
person who tracks time in sports
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪmˌkipər / (AmE), / ˈtaɪmˌkiːpə / (BrE)
an official in sports responsible for keeping track of the time elapsed during a game or event

Examples

Sports timekeeper
As the official timekeeper, she monitored each period carefully to ensure fairness.
The timekeeper signaled the end of the match with a loud buzzer.
The coach argued with the timekeeper about the remaining seconds in the game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: referee - an official who enforces the rules in sports
