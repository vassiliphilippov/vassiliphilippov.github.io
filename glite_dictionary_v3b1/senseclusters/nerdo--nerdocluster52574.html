nerdo

noun
socially awkward but tech-savvy person (slang)
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈnɝːdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈnɜːdəʊ / (BrE)
An informal term for a person who is socially awkward but highly knowledgeable about technology.

Examples

A nerdo in glasses
At the party, the nerdo in glasses fixed the Wi-Fi in seconds while everyone else watched.
Despite being labeled a nerdo, she was always the first to solve any tech problem at work.
In high school, Mark was considered a nerdo because he spent his weekends rebuilding old computers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geek - an enthusiast or expert especially in technical fields, sometimes socially awkward
  • synonym: nerd - a person who is very interested in technical subjects and is often socially awkward
