naturalistically

adverb
in a naturalistic way
Pronunciation: / ˌnætʃərəˈlɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌnatʃərəˈlɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that explains or interprets events using natural causes, laws, or methods rather than supernatural explanations

Examples

Naturalistically explain events
The documentary explained animal behavior naturalistically, focusing on environmental and biological factors.
She described the origins of the festival naturalistically, without mentioning any myths or legends.
The author wrote naturalistically about human emotions, avoiding magical or supernatural elements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scientifically - in a way based on scientific methods or principles
  • antonym: supernaturally - in a way that involves supernatural or spiritual explanations
