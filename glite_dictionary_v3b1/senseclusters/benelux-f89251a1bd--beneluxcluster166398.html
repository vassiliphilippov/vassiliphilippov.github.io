Benelux

proper noun
economic union of Belgium, Netherlands, and Luxembourg
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛnəˌlʌks / (AmE), / ˈbɛnɪˌlʌks / (BrE)
An economic union comprising Belgium, the Netherlands, and Luxembourg, established to promote cooperation and reduce trade barriers among the three countries.

Examples

The Benelux union
Many European policies were first tested within the Benelux before being adopted more widely.
The Benelux countries signed an agreement to facilitate cross-border travel.
Trade among the Benelux nations has flourished since the creation of the customs union.
