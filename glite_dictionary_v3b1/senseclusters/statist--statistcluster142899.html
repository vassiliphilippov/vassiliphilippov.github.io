statist

noun
supporter of state control
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪtɪst / (AmE), / ˈsteɪtɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports strong government control over economic and social policy

Examples

A statist thinker
The statist believed that government should play a central role in regulating the economy.
Critics of the policy accused its supporters of having a statist mindset.
Debates between a statist and a libertarian often focus on the limits of government power.
The new policy was welcomed by statists who believe in a strong central government.
Critics accused the candidate of being a statist because of his support for government regulation.
Debates between statists and libertarians often focus on the role of government in society.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authoritarian - a person who favors strict obedience to authority
  • synonym: collectivist - a person who favors collective control, especially by the state
  • antonym: libertarian - a person who advocates minimal government intervention
