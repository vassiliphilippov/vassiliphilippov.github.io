Shakspere

proper noun
alternate spelling of Shakespeare
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪkspɪr / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪkspɪə / (BrE)
an alternative spelling of Shakespeare, referring to William Shakespeare, the renowned English poet and playwright (1564–1616)

Examples

Some scholars debate why Shakspere himself used multiple spellings of his name.
