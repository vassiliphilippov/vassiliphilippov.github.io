casement

noun
side-hinged window sash
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪsmənt / (AmE), / ˈkeɪsmənt / (BrE)
a window sash that is hinged at the side and opens outward or inward like a door

Examples

A casement window
Rain pattered softly against the old wooden casement.
She opened the casement to let in some fresh air.
The house featured elegant casements overlooking the garden.
