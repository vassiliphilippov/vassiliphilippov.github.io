born loser

noun
habitually unsuccessful person
Pronunciation: / bɔrn ˈluzər / (AmE), / bɔːn ˈluːzə / (BrE)
a person who seems destined to fail or is habitually unsuccessful

Examples

A born loser in games
After losing every game in the season, his teammates started calling him a born loser.
No matter how hard she tried, she felt like a born loser at work.
The movie's main character is a born loser who finally gets a lucky break.

Related Terms

  • synonym: failure - lack of success or the person who fails
  • antonym: winner - person who succeeds or prevails
