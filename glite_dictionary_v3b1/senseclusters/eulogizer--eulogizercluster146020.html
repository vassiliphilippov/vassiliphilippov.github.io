eulogizer

noun
person who praises
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːlədʒaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈjuːlədʒaɪzə / (BrE)
a person who praises someone, especially in an exaggerated or formal way, often to gain favor

Examples

A eulogizer of leaders
The eulogizer spoke warmly about the achievements of the retiring principal.
He became known as the office eulogizer because he always complimented his supervisors.
At the ceremony, the eulogizer moved the audience with heartfelt stories and praise.

Related Terms

  • antonym: critic - a person who expresses disapproval or finds fault
  • synonym: flatterer - a person who gives excessive or insincere praise
