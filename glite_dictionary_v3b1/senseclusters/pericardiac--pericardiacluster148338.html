pericardiac

adjective
around the heart or relating to the pericardium
8.4
Pronunciation: /pə.rɪ.ˈkɑːr.dɪ.æk/ /; / /e.rɪ.ˈkɑːr.dɪ.æk/ (AmE), /pə.rɪ.ˈkɑː.dɪ.æk/ /; / /e.rɪ.ˈkɑː.dɪ.æk/ (BrE)
