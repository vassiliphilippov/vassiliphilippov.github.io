bursty

adjective
in short bursts
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜːrsti / (AmE), / ˈbɜːsti / (BrE)
happening in short, intense periods rather than continuously or steadily over time

Examples

A bursty internet connection
The internet connection was bursty, making it difficult to stream videos smoothly.
Some people have a bursty work style, focusing intensely for short periods.
The rainfall was bursty, with heavy showers followed by long dry spells.

Related Terms

  • antonym: steady - happening in a continuous and regular way
