lancelike

adjective
shaped like a lance; narrow and pointed
5.8 7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈlæn.slaɪk/ /; / /ˈlæns.laɪk/ (AmE), /ˈlɑːn.slaɪk/ /; / /ˈlɑːns.laɪk/ (BrE)
(of a leaf or other object) narrow and tapering to a pointed apex, resembling the shape of a lance head.

Examples

A lancelike leaf
He admired the lancelike petals of the rare flower in the garden.
The plant's lancelike foliage helps it blend into its grassland habitat.
The tree's leaves are lancelike, tapering gradually to a fine point.

Related Terms
