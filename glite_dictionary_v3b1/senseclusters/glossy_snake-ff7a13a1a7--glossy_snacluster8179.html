glossy snake

noun
nocturnal snake with shiny tan scales
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɑːsi sneɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡlɒsi sneɪk / (BrE)
a nocturnal burrowing snake of the western United States with shiny tan scales, commonly found in arid and semi-arid regions

Examples

The glossy snake
The glossy snake is well adapted to burrowing in sandy soils of the desert.
Unlike rattlesnakes, the glossy snake is non-venomous and quite shy.
We spotted a glossy snake basking in the early morning sun near the canyon trail.
