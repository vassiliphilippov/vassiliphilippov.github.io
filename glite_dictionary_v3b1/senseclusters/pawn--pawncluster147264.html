pawn

noun
person used by others for their own ends
7.2 2.87/m
Pronunciation: / p…în / (AmE), / p…îÀên / (BrE)
a person who is used by others to achieve their own goals, often without regard for that person's interests or wellbeing

Examples

A pawn in a plan
In the end, he realized he had been just a pawn in their scheme.
She refused to be anyone's pawn and made her own decisions.
The politician was merely a pawn in a much larger game.
