ace

noun
unstoppable serve in tennis or similar sports
3.6
Pronunciation: / eɪs / (AmE), / eɪs / (BrE)
(in tennis and similar sports) a serve that is so well hit that the receiver cannot touch or return it.

Examples

A tennis ace
After serving several aces in a row, she took control of the match.
His ace in the final set stunned the crowd and his opponent.
She won the game with a powerful ace down the center line.

Related Terms

  • antonym: double fault - an error in serving twice, giving a point to the opponent
