aftergrowth

noun
secondary development
Pronunciation: / ˈæftərˌɡroʊθ / (AmE), / ˈɑːftəˌɡrəʊθ / (BrE)
a secondary development or result that appears later, often as an unintended consequence.

Examples

An aftergrowth of problems
The new policy resulted in an unexpected aftergrowth of administrative work.
There was a noticeable aftergrowth of small businesses following the economic reforms.
The artist's fame brought an aftergrowth of imitators trying to copy his style.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aftermath - the consequences or results following an event
  • antonym: cause - something that brings about a result
  • synonym: consequence - a result or effect of an action or event
