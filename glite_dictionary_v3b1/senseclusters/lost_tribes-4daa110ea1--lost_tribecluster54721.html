Lost Tribes

noun
ten exiled tribes of Israel
Pronunciation: / lɔst traɪbz / (AmE), / lɒst traɪbz / (BrE)
the ten tribes of Israel that were exiled by the Assyrians around 720 BC and whose fate remains a historical and religious mystery
