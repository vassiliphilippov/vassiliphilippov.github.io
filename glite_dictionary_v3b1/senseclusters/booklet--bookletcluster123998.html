booklet

noun
small book or pamphlet
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊklət / (AmE), / ˈbʊklɪt / (BrE)
a small book, often with a paper cover, that contains information or instructions on a particular subject

Examples

A booklet of instructions
Each participant received a colorful booklet outlining the conference schedule and speakers.
She picked up the museum booklet to read about the upcoming exhibitions.
The instructions were printed in a handy booklet included with the product.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pamphlet - a small booklet or brochure containing information or arguments about a single subject
