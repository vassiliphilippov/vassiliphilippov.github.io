nonrationalist

adjective
rejecting pure reason
9.0
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈræʃənəlɪst / (AmE), / nɒnˈræʃənəlɪst / (BrE)
not based on or relating to rationalism; rejecting reason as the sole source of knowledge or truth

Examples

Nonrationalist thinking style
Her nonrationalist approach valued intuition and emotion over strict logical analysis.
The philosopher was known for his nonrationalist views, challenging the dominance of reason in Western thought.
Some spiritual traditions take a nonrationalist stance, emphasizing faith and experience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irrational - not based on reason or logic
  • antonym: rationalist - believing that reason is the main source of knowledge
