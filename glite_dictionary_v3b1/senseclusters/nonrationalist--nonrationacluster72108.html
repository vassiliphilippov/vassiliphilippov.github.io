nonrationalist

adjective
rejecting pure reason
9.0
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈræ.ʃə.nəl.ɪst/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈræ.ʃə.nəl.ɪst/ (BrE)
not based on or relating to rationalism; rejecting reason as the sole source of knowledge or truth

Examples

Some spiritual traditions take a nonrationalist stance, emphasizing faith and experience.
