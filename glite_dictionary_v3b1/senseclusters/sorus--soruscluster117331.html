sorus

noun
cluster of sporangia on a fern frond
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔr.əs / (AmE), / ˈsɔː.rəs / (BrE)
A cluster of sporangia, usually found on the underside of a fern frond.

Examples

Fern sorus cluster
A sorus can appear as a small brown spot on the back of a mature fern leaf.
Botanists used a magnifying glass to study the structure of each sorus on the frond.
The underside of the fern was covered in rows of sori, each packed with tiny spores.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sporangium - a structure in which spores are produced in fungi, algae, and plants
