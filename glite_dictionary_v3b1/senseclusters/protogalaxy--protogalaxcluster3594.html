protogalaxy

noun
forming galaxy
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊtoʊˈɡæləksi / (AmE), / ˌprəʊtəʊˈɡæləksi / (BrE)
a large cloud of gas and dust in space that is in the process of forming into a galaxy

Studying a protogalaxy helps astronomers understand how galaxies are born and evolve.
