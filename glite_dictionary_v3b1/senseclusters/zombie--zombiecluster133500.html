zombie

noun
person acting mechanically or apathetically
4.9 4.09/m
Pronunciation: / Ààz…ëÀêm.bi / (AmE), / Ààz…ím.bi / (BrE)
A person who acts or responds in a mechanical, apathetic, or emotionless way, as if lacking consciousness.

Examples

Zombie at work
After working late, I felt like a zombie during the morning meeting.
He moved through his chores as if he were a zombie, showing no sign of interest.
She stared at the screen like a zombie, barely reacting to anything around her.

Synonyms

  • zombi
    8.4 0.00/m
    zombi is an alternate spelling for the same idea and appears less often in writing, while zombie is the common modern form.
  • automaton
    8.4 0.63/m
    automaton is a formal word for someone who acts like a machine, while zombie suggests a lifeless, human-like figure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automaton - a person who acts in a robotic or mechanical fashion
