oared

adjective
with oars
4.6
Pronunciation: / ɔrd / (AmE), / ɔːd / (BrE)
having or equipped with oars, especially for the purpose of rowing

Examples

Oared boat
The explorers crossed the river in an oared canoe.
They rented an oared boat for a peaceful afternoon on the lake.
Ancient ships were often oared rather than powered by sails.

Related Terms

  • antonym: motorized - equipped with a motor
  • synonym: rowboat - a small boat propelled by oars
