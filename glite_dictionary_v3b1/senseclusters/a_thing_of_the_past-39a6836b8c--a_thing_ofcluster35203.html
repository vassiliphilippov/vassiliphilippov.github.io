a thing of the past

noun
no longer existing or relevant
Pronunciation: / ə ˈθɪŋ əv ðə pæst / (AmE), / ə ˈθɪŋ əv ðə pɑːst / (BrE)
something that no longer exists, is no longer used, or is no longer relevant

Examples

A thing of the past now
For many people, commuting to the office every day is now a thing of the past.
He worries that personal customer service will soon be a thing of the past.
With the advent of smartphones, payphones have become a thing of the past.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obsolete - no longer produced or used; out of date
  • synonym: outdated - no longer useful or current; old-fashioned
