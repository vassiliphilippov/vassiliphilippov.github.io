halfpipe

noun
U-shaped sports ramp
Pronunciation: / ˈhæfˌpaɪp / (AmE), / ˈhɑːfˌpaɪp / (BrE)
a U-shaped ramp or structure used in sports like skateboarding or snowboarding for performing tricks

Examples

A halfpipe ramp
The snowboarder launched into the air from the halfpipe and landed a perfect trick.
Many skate parks feature a halfpipe for advanced riders to practice their skills.
During the competition, she impressed the judges with her moves on the halfpipe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: quarterpipe - a ramp with a quarter-circle shape used in similar sports
  • synonym: ramp - a sloped surface used for moving up or down
