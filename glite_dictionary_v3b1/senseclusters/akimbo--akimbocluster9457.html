akimbo

adjective, adverb
with limbs bent outward at the joint
Pronunciation: / əˈkɪmboʊ / (AmE), / əˈkɪmbəʊ / (BrE)
with the limbs (especially arms) bent outward, usually with hands on hips and elbows turned outward
