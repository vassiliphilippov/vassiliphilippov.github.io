stylomandibular

adjective
of styloid and mandible
Pronunciation: / ˌstaɪloʊˌmændɪˈbjulər / (AmE), / ˌstaɪləʊˌmændɪˈbjuːlə / (BrE)
relating to both the styloid process and the mandible, especially in anatomical structures

Examples

The stylomandibular ligament
The stylomandibular ligament helps support the lower jaw during movement.
Injury to the stylomandibular area can affect jaw function and cause pain.
Surgeons must be careful not to damage the stylomandibular structures during certain dental procedures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mandibular - relating to the mandible or lower jawbone
