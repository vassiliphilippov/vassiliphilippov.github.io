meanspiritedly

adverb
in an unkind or spiteful way
Pronunciation: / ˌmiːnˈspɪrɪtɪdli / (AmE), / ˌmiːnˈspɪrɪtɪdli / (BrE)
In a manner that is unkind, spiteful, or petty.

Examples

Meanspiritedly refused help
He meanspiritedly refused to help, even though it would have cost him nothing.
She meanspiritedly mocked her colleague's mistakes in front of the entire team.
The critics meanspiritedly dismissed his efforts without considering the challenges he faced.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cruelly - in a way that causes pain or suffering
  • antonym: kindly - in a friendly or generous manner
