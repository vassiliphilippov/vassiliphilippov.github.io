Piper longum

noun
species of tropical climbing plant
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪ.pər ˈlɒŋ.əm / (AmE), / ˈpaɪ.pə ˈlɒŋ.əm / (BrE)
A slender tropical climbing plant of the eastern Himalayas, also known as Indian long pepper, cultivated for its fruit used as a spice.

Examples

Piper longum plant
Piper longum is valued in Ayurvedic medicine for its therapeutic properties.
Botanists discovered several specimens of Piper longum growing wild in the foothills.
The dried fruit of Piper longum is used as a spice known as long pepper.
