family Glareolidae

noun
family of birds: pratincoles and coursers
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli ɡlɛəriˈɒlɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ˌɡleərɪəˈlɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of birds that includes the pratincoles and coursers, found mainly in the Old World tropics and subtropics.
