Adlerian

noun
follower of Adler
9.0
Pronunciation: / ædˈlɪriən / (AmE), / ædˈlɪəriən / (BrE)
a person who follows or practices the psychological theories of Alfred Adler

Examples

An Adlerian psychologist
As an Adlerian, he emphasized the importance of social interest in therapy.
The conference brought together many Adlerians from around the world to discuss new research.
She became an Adlerian after studying the works of Alfred Adler in graduate school.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Freudian - a person who follows the theories of Sigmund Freud
  • synonym: Jungian - a person who follows the theories of Carl Jung
