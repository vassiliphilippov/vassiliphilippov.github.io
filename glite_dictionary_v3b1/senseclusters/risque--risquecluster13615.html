risque

adjective
slightly indecent or suggestive
Pronunciation: / rɪˈskeɪ / (AmE), / rɪˈskeɪ / (BrE)
slightly indecent or suggestive of sexual impropriety, especially in a humorous or daring way

Examples

A risque joke
Her novel contains several risque scenes that some readers found daring.
That film was considered too risque for children to watch.
The comedian told a risque joke that made the audience laugh and blush.

Related Terms

  • synonym: naughty - suggestive of sexual impropriety, especially in a playful or mild way
  • synonym: racy - lively, entertaining, and slightly shocking, especially in a sexual way
