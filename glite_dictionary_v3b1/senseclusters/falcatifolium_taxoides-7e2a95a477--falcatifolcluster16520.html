Falcatifolium taxoides

noun
rainforest tree or shrub of New Caledonia
9.9
Pronunciation: / fælˌkætɪˈfoʊliəm tæksˈɔɪdiːz / (AmE), / fælˌkatɪˈfəʊliəm taksˈɔɪdiːz / (BrE)
a species of tree or shrub native to the rainforests of New Caledonia, recognized by its conic crown and pale green, sickle-shaped leaves; serves as a host for the rare parasite yew

Examples

Falcatifolium taxoides tree species
