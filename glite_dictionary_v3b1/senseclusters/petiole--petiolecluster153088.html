petiole

noun
leaf stalk attaching blade to stem
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːtiˌoʊl / (AmE), / ˈpiːtɪəʊl / (BrE)
the slender stalk that attaches the blade of a leaf to the stem

Examples

The petiole of leaf
Damage to the petiole can cause a leaf to wilt or fall prematurely.
Some plants have a very short petiole, making the leaves appear almost sessile.
The petiole of each leaf supports the blade above the stem for maximum sunlight exposure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stem - the main structural axis of a plant
