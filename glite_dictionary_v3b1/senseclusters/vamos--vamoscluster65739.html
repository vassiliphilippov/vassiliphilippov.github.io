vamos

verb
Spanish for 'let's go'
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑːmoʊs / (AmE), / ˈvæmɒs / (BrE)
the first-person plural present tense of the Spanish verb 'ir', meaning 'we go' or 'let's go', often used to encourage action

Examples

We vamos now
The coach shouted vamos to motivate the team before the match.
When the concert started, the crowd cheered and yelled vamos together.
She smiled and said vamos as they set off on their hike.
