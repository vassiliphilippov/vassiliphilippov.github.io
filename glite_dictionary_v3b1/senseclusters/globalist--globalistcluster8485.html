globalist

adjective
favoring international integration
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɡloʊbəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊbəlɪst / (BrE)
supporting or relating to the idea of increasing international integration and cooperation across countries.

Examples

Globalist policies
The new government adopted a globalist approach to economic policy, encouraging free trade agreements.
Critics argued that the globalist agenda overlooked local industries and traditions.
Her globalist perspective led her to support international cooperation on climate change.

Related Terms

  • synonym: internationalist - supporting cooperation among nations
  • antonym: nationalist - favoring the interests of one's own nation over others
