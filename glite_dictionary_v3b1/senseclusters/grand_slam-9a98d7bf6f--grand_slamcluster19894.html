grand slam

noun
winning all tricks in bridge
Pronunciation: / ɡrænd slæm / (AmE), / ɡrænd slæm / (BrE)
a winning achievement in which a player or side takes all the tricks in a single hand of bridge

Examples

A grand slam win
His expert play led to a grand slam, taking every trick in the game.
Making a grand slam in bridge is a rare achievement celebrated by players.
The team erupted in cheers after they completed a grand slam during the final hand.
