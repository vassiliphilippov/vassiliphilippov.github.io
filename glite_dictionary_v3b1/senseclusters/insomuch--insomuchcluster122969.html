insomuch

adverb
to such an extent; so much
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnsoʊˈmʌtʃ / (AmE), / ˌɪnsəʊˈmʌtʃ / (BrE)
To such an extent; so much; to the degree that.

Examples

Tired insomuch
He was tired insomuch that he could barely keep his eyes open.
She contributed insomuch to the project that it would not have succeeded without her.
The evidence was lacking, insomuch as no witnesses could be found.

  • synonym: insofar - to the extent that; as far as
