shank

verb
hit a golf ball incorrectly with the heel of a club
7.2
Pronunciation: / ʃæŋk / (AmE), / ʃæŋk / (BrE)
(in golf) to hit a ball with the heel of the club, causing it to go off in an unintended direction.

Examples

To shank a ball
Golfers often get frustrated when they shank an easy shot near the green.
He accidentally shanked his drive, sending the ball into the trees.
She shanked the ball badly and missed the fairway completely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slice - (in golf) to hit a ball so that it curves sharply to one side
