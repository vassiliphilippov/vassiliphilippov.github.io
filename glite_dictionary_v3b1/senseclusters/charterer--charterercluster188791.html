charterer

noun
one who grants a charter
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑːrtərər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːtərə / (BrE)
a person or organization that grants a charter, formally creating or authorizing a company, institution, or body

Examples

Company charterer
The city council acted as the charterer when it established the new public library.
As the charterer, the organization had the authority to set the institution's rules and structure.
The charterer must ensure all legal requirements are met before granting official status.

  • antonym: grantee - a person or organization that receives a grant or charter
