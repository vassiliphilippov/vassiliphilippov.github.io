freshness

noun
rudeness; impudence
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛʃ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈfrɛʃ.nɪs / (BrE)
rude or impertinent behavior; the quality of being cheeky or taking liberties, especially in speech or manner.

Examples

Student freshness
I won't tolerate that kind of freshness in my classroom.
She answered with remarkable freshness, surprising everyone in the meeting.
The child was scolded for his freshness toward the teacher.

Related Terms

  • antonym: politeness - respectful and considerate behavior
