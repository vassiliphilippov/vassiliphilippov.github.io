throbber

noun
loading icon
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈθrɑbər / (AmE), / ˈθrɒbə / (BrE)
a graphic or icon that indicates a computer or device is loading or processing information

Examples

Loading throbber
The throbber appeared in the corner of the screen while the file was uploading.
If the throbber keeps spinning, try refreshing the webpage.
A colorful throbber let users know the app was still working.

Related Terms

  • synonym: progress bar - a visual indicator showing the progress of a task
  • synonym: spinner - a rotating icon showing that something is loading
