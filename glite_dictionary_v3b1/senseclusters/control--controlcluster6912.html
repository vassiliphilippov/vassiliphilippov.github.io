control

noun
official economic restriction, esp. on prices or wages
1.5
Pronunciation: / kənˈtroʊl / (AmE), / kənˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
an official restriction on prices, wages, or other economic factors, imposed by a government to regulate the economy

Examples

Price controls
After the war ended, most price controls were gradually lifted.
Many economists debated the effects of wage controls during the crisis.
The government imposed strict controls on rent to keep housing affordable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: regulation - the action of controlling or managing something, especially by rules
