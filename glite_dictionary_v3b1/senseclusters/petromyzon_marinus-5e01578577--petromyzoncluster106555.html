Petromyzon marinus

noun
species of large sea lamprey
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛtroʊˈmaɪzɒn məˈriːnəs / (AmE), / ˌpɛtrəˈmaɪzən məˈriːnəs / (BrE)
a large anadromous lamprey native to the North Atlantic, sometimes used as food and known for its destructive impact on native fish in the Great Lakes
