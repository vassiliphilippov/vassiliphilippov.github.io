Zamiaceae

noun
family of cycads including zamias
Pronunciation: / zæmiˈeɪsiiː /; / ˌzæmiˈeɪsiˌi / (AmE), / zamiːˈeɪsɪiː /; / ˌzæmɪˈeɪsɪiː / (BrE)
a family of tropical and subtropical cycad plants (zamias), often considered distinct from the related Cycadaceae
