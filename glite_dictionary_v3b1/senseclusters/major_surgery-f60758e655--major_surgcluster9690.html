major surgery

noun
serious surgery involving anesthesia or risk
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪdʒər ˈsɝːdʒəri / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdʒə ˈsɜːdʒəri / (BrE)
a surgical procedure that involves significant risk, typically requiring anesthesia or respiratory assistance

Examples

The hospital specializes in major surgery such as heart transplants and joint replacements.
