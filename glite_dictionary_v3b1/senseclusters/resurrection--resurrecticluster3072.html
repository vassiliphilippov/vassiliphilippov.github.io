resurrection

noun
revival or return to use
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛzəˈrɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛzəˈrɛkʃən / (BrE)
the act of bringing something back into use, practice, or attention after a period of inactivity or neglect

Examples

Resurrection of a tradition
A resurrection of public interest saved the historic theater from demolition.
The new government announced the resurrection of the old economic policies.
There was a resurrection of classic rock music in the 1990s.

Related Terms

  • synonym: revival - an improvement in the condition or strength of something
