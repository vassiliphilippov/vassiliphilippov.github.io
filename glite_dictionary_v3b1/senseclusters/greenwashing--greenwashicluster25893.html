greenwashing

noun
false eco-friendly claims
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːnwɑːʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnwɒʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of giving a false impression of environmental responsibility, especially by companies or products

Examples

Greenwashing by companies
The company faced criticism for its greenwashing after exaggerating its environmental efforts in advertisements.
Many consumers are learning to spot greenwashing and demand real sustainability from brands.
Accusations of greenwashing can damage a business's reputation among environmentally conscious customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deception - the act of making someone believe something that is not true
  • antonym: transparency - the quality of being open and honest
