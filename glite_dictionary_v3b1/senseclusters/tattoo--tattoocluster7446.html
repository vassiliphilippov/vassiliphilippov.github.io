tattoo

verb
to imprint deeply
6.0
Pronunciation: /tæ.ˈtuː/ /; / /tə.ˈtuː/ (AmE), /tæ.ˈtuː/ /; / /tə.ˈtuː/ (BrE)
to cause an image, idea, or memory to be fixed firmly and permanently in someone's mind, as if marked like a tattoo.

Examples

To tattoo a memory
The tragic scene was tattooed in her memory for years after the event.
His father's words were tattooed on his mind, guiding him through difficult times.
The image of the city at dawn became tattooed in his thoughts after his first visit.
