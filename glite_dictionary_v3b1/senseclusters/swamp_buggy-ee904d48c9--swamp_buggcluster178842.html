swamp buggy

noun
amphibious vehicle for swamps
7.5 6.4
Pronunciation: / swɑmp ˈbʌɡi / (AmE), / swɒmp ˈbʌɡi / (BrE)
a large, amphibious motor vehicle with four-wheel drive and a raised chassis, designed for use in swamps or marshy land

Examples

A swamp buggy vehicle
Driving a swamp buggy requires skill, especially over uneven and muddy ground.
The researchers used a swamp buggy to travel through the flooded marshlands.
Tourists enjoyed an adventurous ride in a swamp buggy during their visit to the Everglades.
