smooth-faced

adjective
having no facial hair
5.1 3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈsmuːð.feɪst/ (AmE), /ˈsmuːð.feɪst/ (BrE)
(of a person) having no hair on the face; clean-shaven, especially used to describe young boys or men.

Examples

A smooth-faced young man
A smooth-faced youth greeted us at the entrance with a bright smile.
He remained smooth-faced throughout his twenties, never growing a beard or mustache.
The actor appeared smooth-faced in his latest role, making him look much younger.
