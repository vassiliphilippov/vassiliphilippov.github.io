nonsegregated

adjective
not separated by group; integrated
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈsɛɡrəˌɡeɪtɪd / (AmE), / nɒnˈsɛɡrɪɡeɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a place or organization) not separated or divided by race, religion, or another distinguishing feature; having had segregation ended.

Examples

A nonsegregated school
Decades ago, some neighborhoods became nonsegregated following civil rights reforms.
Employees now work together in a nonsegregated environment that values diversity.
The school was declared nonsegregated after the court ruling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: integrated - with different groups brought together; not separated
