smoke-filled

adjective
full of smoke
3.0
Pronunciation: / smoʊk fɪld / (AmE), / sməʊk fɪld / (BrE)
(of a room or place) full of smoke, especially from cigarettes or burning substances.

Examples

A smoke-filled room
After the fire started, they ran out of the smoke-filled kitchen coughing.
Negotiations often took place in smoke-filled back rooms late into the night.
The smoke-filled bar made it hard to see across the room.
