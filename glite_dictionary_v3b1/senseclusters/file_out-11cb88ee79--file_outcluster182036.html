file out

verb
to leave in a line, one after another
Pronunciation: / faɪl aʊt / (AmE), / faɪl aʊt / (BrE)
to leave a place in an orderly line, one after another

Examples

To file out quietly
After the meeting ended, everyone filed out of the conference room in silence.
The students filed out of the classroom quietly after the bell rang.
We watched as visitors file out of the theatre in an orderly fashion.
