virulent strain

noun
highly infectious or harmful microorganism type
7.1 5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈvɪr.ə.lənt streɪn/ /; / /ˈvɪr.jə.lənt streɪn/ (AmE), /ˈvɪr.ə.lənt streɪn/ /; / /ˈvɪr.jʊ.lənt streɪn/ (BrE)
a type or variant of a microorganism, especially a virus or bacterium, that is highly infectious, destructive, or severe in its effects

Examples

A virulent strain of virus
A virulent strain of influenza caused severe symptoms in many communities this year.
