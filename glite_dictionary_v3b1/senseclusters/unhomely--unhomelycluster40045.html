unhomely

adjective
strange and unsettling
8.7
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈhoʊm.liː/ /; / /ə.ˈhoʊm.liː/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈhəʊm.liː/ /; / /ə.ˈhəʊm.liː/ (BrE)
strange, uncomfortable, or lacking the warmth and comfort of home; making you feel uneasy or not at home

Examples

An unhomely place
The abandoned house had an unhomely atmosphere that made visitors uneasy.
There was something unhomely about the empty streets at night.
His new apartment felt unhomely until he decorated it with personal items.
The hotel room was clean but felt strangely unhomely and cold.
Despite the expensive furniture, the apartment remained unhomely to its new tenants.
She tried to decorate the space, but it still looked unhomely and bare.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comfortable - giving a sense of ease or relaxation
  • antonym: cozy - comfortable and warm
  • synonym: uncanny - strange or mysterious, especially in an unsettling way
  • synonym: uninviting - not welcoming or attractive
