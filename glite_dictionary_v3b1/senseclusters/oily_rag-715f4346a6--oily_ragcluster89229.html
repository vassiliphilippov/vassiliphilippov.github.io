oily rag

noun
rag soaked with oil
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɔɪli ræɡ / (AmE), / ˈɔɪli ræɡ / (BrE)
a piece of cloth that has been soaked with oil, often used for cleaning or polishing

Examples

An oily rag
Be careful; a discarded oily rag can easily catch fire if exposed to heat.
He used an oily rag to wipe down the greasy engine.
The garage smelled strongly of oily rags left in the corner.

Related Terms
