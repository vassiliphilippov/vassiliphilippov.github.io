investigational

adjective
related to investigation
7.1
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌvɛstəˈɡeɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ɪnˌvɛstɪˈɡeɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or carried out as part of an official inquiry or investigation, especially by authorities such as police or government.

Examples

Investigational report
The new drug is still in the investigational stage and not yet approved for public use.
Police launched an investigational process to determine the cause of the accident.
The committee requested all investigational reports before making a final decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: investigative - relating to or designed to examine and discover facts
  • antonym: routine - done as a usual or regular procedure, not for investigation
