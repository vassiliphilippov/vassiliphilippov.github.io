message in a bottle

noun
message sent adrift in a bottle, often found unexpectedly
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛsɪdʒ ɪn ə ˈbɑtəl / (AmE), / ˈmɛsɪdʒ ɪn ə ˈbɒtəl / (BrE)
a message placed inside a bottle and set adrift, especially one intended to be found by someone unknown or at a distant time or place; also, any information or plea sent out in hope of a response, often unexpectedly discovered.

Examples

A message in a bottle found
For them, the old diary was like a message in a bottle from the past.
The sailors discovered a message in a bottle that had floated for years before reaching the shore.
Writing her dream on paper, she tossed the message in a bottle into the ocean.
