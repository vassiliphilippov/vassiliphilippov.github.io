encircling

adjective
surrounding; enclosing all around
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɜrklɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɜːklɪŋ / (BrE)
surrounding or forming a circle around something; enclosing on all sides.

Examples

An encircling fog
Dense fog formed an encircling blanket around the mountains.
He wrapped his encircling arms gently around the child.
The castle was protected by an encircling moat.
