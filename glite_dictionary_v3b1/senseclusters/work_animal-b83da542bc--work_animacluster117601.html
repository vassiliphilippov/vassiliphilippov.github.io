work animal

noun
animal used for heavy labor
Pronunciation: / wɝk ˈænɪml̩ / (AmE), / wɜːk ˈænɪml̩ / (BrE)
an animal that has been trained and is used for heavy labor, such as pulling loads or plowing fields

Examples

A work animal pulls loads
As machinery became common, the need for work animals gradually declined.
Donkeys have been used as work animals in many parts of the world for centuries.
The farmer relied on his work animal to plow the fields each spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beast of burden - an animal, such as an ox or donkey, used for carrying loads
