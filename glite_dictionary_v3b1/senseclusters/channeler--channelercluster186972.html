channeler

noun
one who directs flow
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃænələr / (AmE), / ˈtʃænələ / (BrE)
a person or thing that directs or transmits something into a particular channel or pathway

Examples

Water channeler
The irrigation system included a channeler to guide water to the crops efficiently.
As a channeler of information, she made sure the right messages reached the right people.
Engineers designed a channeler to divert excess rainwater away from the building.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conduit - a channel for conveying water or other fluid
  • synonym: transmitter - a person or thing that transmits something
