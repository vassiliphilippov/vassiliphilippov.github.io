contradictorily

adverb
in a contradictory manner
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːn.trəˈdɪk.tɚ.ə.li / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.trəˈdɪk.tər.ɪ.li / (BrE)
In a way that is self-contradictory or inconsistent with itself.

Examples

Act contradictorily often
His actions were often contradictorily opposed to his stated beliefs.
She spoke contradictorily, first denying and then admitting the same facts.
The witness answered the questions contradictorily, confusing the jury.
