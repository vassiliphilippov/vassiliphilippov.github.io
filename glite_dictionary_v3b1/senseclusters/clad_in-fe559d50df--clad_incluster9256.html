clad in

adjective
dressed in or covered with something
Pronunciation: / klæd ɪn / (AmE), / klæd ɪn / (BrE)
dressed in or covered with a particular type of clothing or material.

Examples

Clad in fine clothes
Guests arrived clad in their finest evening wear for the celebration.
The hills were clad in a thick blanket of snow during winter.
The knight was clad in shining armor as he rode into battle.

Related Terms
