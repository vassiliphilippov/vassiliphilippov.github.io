Illuminati

noun
elite influential group
Pronunciation: / ɪˌluːməˈnɑːti / (AmE), / ɪˌluːmɪˈnɑːti / (BrE)
a group of people regarded as especially enlightened, knowledgeable, or influential in a particular field, often seen as an elite

Examples

The Illuminati elite
Some believe the Illuminati secretly control major world events from behind the scenes.
In the tech industry, the Illuminati are said to decide which startups will succeed.
The novelist joked about being invited to a meeting of the literary Illuminati.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elite - a group of people with the most power or influence
  • synonym: establishment - the most powerful people in a society or field
  • antonym: outsider - someone not part of a particular group or elite
