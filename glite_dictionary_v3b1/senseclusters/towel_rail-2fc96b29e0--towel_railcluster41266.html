towel rail

noun
horizontal bar for hanging towels
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtaʊəl reɪl / (AmE), / ˈtaʊəl reɪl / (BrE)
a long horizontal bar fixed to a wall, used for hanging towels to dry or store

Examples

The towel rail hangs
Could you please put the clean towels on the towel rail next to the sink?
She hung her damp bathrobe over the towel rail to dry after her shower.
The newly renovated bathroom includes a heated towel rail for extra comfort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: towel rack - a frame or stand for holding towels, often used in American English
