tack onto

verb
attach or fasten to a surface
Pronunciation: / tæk ˈɑntu / (AmE), / tæk ˈɒntu / (BrE)
to attach or fasten something onto a surface using a tack or similar object

Examples

To tack onto the wall
He quickly tacked onto the wall a note reminding himself of the meeting.
She decided to tack onto her report an extra page of references.
The children tacked onto the bulletin board their drawings from art class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fasten - to attach firmly or securely in place
