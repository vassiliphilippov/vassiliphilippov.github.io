Jacquard loom

noun
automatic loom for weaving patterned fabrics
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʒəˈkɑrd lum / (AmE), / ʒæˈkɑːd luːm / (BrE)
a loom fitted with a special mechanism for automatically controlling the pattern of the woven fabric, allowing complex designs to be created
