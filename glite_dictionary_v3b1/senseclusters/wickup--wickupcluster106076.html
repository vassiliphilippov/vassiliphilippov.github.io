wickup

noun
North American perennial plant, also called fireweed
6.9 9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪk.ʌp/ /; / /ˈwɪk.əp/ (AmE), /ˈwɪk.ʌp/ /; / /ˈwɪk.əp/ (BrE)
A tall North American perennial plant (Chamerion angustifolium), with creeping rootstocks, narrow leaves, and spikes of pinkish-purple flowers, commonly found in burned-over areas or recent clearings; also known as fireweed.
