soup du jour

noun
soup featured on a given day
6.5
Pronunciation: / suːp du ˈʒʊr / (AmE), / suːp djʊ ˈʒʊə / (BrE)
the soup that is offered as a special on a particular day, especially in a restaurant

Examples

The soup du jour
I asked the server what the soup du jour was before ordering lunch.
The restaurant changes its soup du jour daily to highlight seasonal ingredients.
Today's soup du jour is roasted tomato and basil, served with fresh bread.

Related Terms

  • synonym: soup of the day - the particular soup offered as a special each day
