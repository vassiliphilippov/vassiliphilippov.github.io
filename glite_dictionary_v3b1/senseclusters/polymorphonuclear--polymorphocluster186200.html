polymorphonuclear

adjective
multi-lobed nucleus
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑlimɔːrfəˈnuːkliɚ / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˌmɔːfənjuːˈkliːə / (BrE)
having a nucleus with several lobes, especially referring to certain types of white blood cells

Examples

Polymorphonuclear cells
A polymorphonuclear cell plays a key role in fighting infections in the body.
Doctors often examine polymorphonuclear leukocytes to diagnose certain diseases.
The presence of polymorphonuclear cells in a blood sample can indicate an immune response.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neutrophilic - relating to neutrophils, a type of white blood cell with a multi-lobed nucleus
