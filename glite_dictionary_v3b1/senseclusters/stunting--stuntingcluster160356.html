stunting

noun
impaired growth
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstʌntɪŋ / (BrE)
a condition in which growth, especially in children, is slowed or stopped due to poor nutrition or health

Examples

Childhood stunting
Chronic illness and poor diet can lead to stunting in young children.
International organizations are working to reduce stunting rates in developing countries.
Early intervention is crucial to prevent stunting and support healthy child development.

Related Terms

  • synonym: malnutrition - a state of poor nutrition caused by insufficient or unbalanced diet
  • synonym: wasting - a condition where the body loses weight and muscle due to illness or lack of food
