clientele

noun
group of customers or patrons
Pronunciation: / ˌklaɪənˈtɛl / (AmE), / ˌkliːɒnˈtɛl / (BrE)
the customers or patrons collectively of a business, organization, or professional person

Examples

Exclusive clientele
The boutique caters to an exclusive clientele seeking unique fashions.
The restaurant prides itself on serving a loyal clientele who return week after week.
With its location near the university, the café attracts a young clientele.

Related Terms

  • synonym: customers - people who buy goods or services from a business
