transhuman

noun
enhanced human
9.1
Pronunciation: /træns.ˈhjuː.mən/ /; / /trænz.ˈhjuː.mən/ (AmE), /træns.ˈhjuː.mən/ /; / /trænz.ˈhjuː.mən/ (BrE)
a person whose physical or mental abilities have been greatly enhanced beyond normal human limits, usually through advanced technology

Examples

A transhuman mind
In the novel, a transhuman can access the internet directly with their mind.
Debates about ethics often arise when discussing the rights of a transhuman in society.
The scientist dreamed of becoming a transhuman by merging with artificial intelligence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cyborg - a person whose body is enhanced with mechanical or electronic devices
  • degree lower: human - a person with typical human abilities and limitations
  • degree higher: posthuman - a being whose capabilities far surpass those of current humans
