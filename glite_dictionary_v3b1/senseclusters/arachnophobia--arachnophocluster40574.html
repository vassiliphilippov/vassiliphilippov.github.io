arachnophobia

noun
extreme fear of spiders
Pronunciation: / əˌræk.nəˈfoʊ.bi.ə / (AmE), / əˌræk.nəˈfəʊ.bi.ə / (BrE)

Examples

Fear of arachnophobia
He suffers from arachnophobia and panics at the sight of even small spiders.
Her arachnophobia made camping in the woods a terrifying experience.
Many people with arachnophobia are unable to enter rooms where they believe spiders might be present.
