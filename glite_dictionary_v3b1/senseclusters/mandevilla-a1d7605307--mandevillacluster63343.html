Mandevilla

noun
genus of tropical flowering vines
Pronunciation: / ˌmændəˈvɪlə / (AmE), / ˌmændɪˈvɪlə /; / ˌmandɪˈvɪlə / (BrE)
a genus of tropical South American woody vines with large, showy flowers, often grown as ornamental plants
