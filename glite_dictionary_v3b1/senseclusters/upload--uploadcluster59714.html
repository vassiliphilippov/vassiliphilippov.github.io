upload

verb
transfer data to a central computer or server
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌloʊd / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌləʊd / (BrE)
to transfer a file or data from a smaller computer or local device to a central computer, server, or the internet

Examples

To upload a file
He uploaded his vacation photos to a shared online album.
I am uploading the new software update to the server right now.
Please upload the completed form to our website before the deadline.

Related Terms

  • antonym: download - transfer data from a central computer to a smaller or local device
  • synonym: transfer - move data from one computer to another
