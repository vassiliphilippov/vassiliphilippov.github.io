mat board

noun
art mounting board
3.9
Pronunciation: /mæt bɔːrd/ (AmE), /mæt bɔːd/ (BrE)
a stiff, flat board used to mount, support, or frame artwork or photographs within a picture frame

Examples

A black mat board can make the colors in a photograph stand out more.
He bought a large sheet of mat board to use for his framing project.
