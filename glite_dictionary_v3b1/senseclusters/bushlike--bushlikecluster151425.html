bushlike

adjective
resembling the bush
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊʃˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈbʊʃˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling a bush or the bush (wild, rural, or uncultivated areas)

Examples

bushlike plants
The landscape became increasingly bushlike as we traveled farther from the city.
They built a small house in a bushlike setting, surrounded by wild plants and trees.
Her stories often describe bushlike communities where people live close to nature.
The new hedge is very bushlike and provides good cover for small animals.
Her hair grew out in a bushlike mass after the humidity increased.
Some desert plants have a bushlike shape to conserve water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rural - relating to the countryside rather than the city
  • synonym: shrubby - having the form or characteristics of a shrub
  • antonym: tree-like - resembling a tree
  • antonym: urban - relating to a city or town
