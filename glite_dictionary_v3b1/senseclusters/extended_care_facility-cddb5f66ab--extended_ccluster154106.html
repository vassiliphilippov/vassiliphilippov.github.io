extended care facility

noun
medical institution for prolonged care
3.3
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɛndɪd kɛr fəˈsɪlɪti / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛndɪd keə fəˈsɪlɪti / (BrE)
