reregister

verb
to register again
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈrɛdʒɪstər / (AmE), / ˌriːˈrɛdʒɪstə / (BrE)
to register something again, especially after a previous registration has expired or changed

Examples

To reregister a vehicle
You must reregister your vehicle every year to keep it legal on the road.
After moving to a new city, she had to reregister to vote in local elections.
If your membership has lapsed, please reregister online to regain access.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deregister - to remove from an official register
  • synonym: register - to officially record or enroll something
