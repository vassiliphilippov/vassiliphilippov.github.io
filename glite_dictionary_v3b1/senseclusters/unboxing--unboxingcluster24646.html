unboxing

noun
opening new products
3.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɑksɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɒksɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of opening a new product and showing its contents, often recorded on video for others to watch

Examples

A product unboxing
The popular YouTuber posted an unboxing of the new gaming console on her channel.
He enjoys watching unboxing videos before deciding which gadgets to buy.
During the live stream, she did an unboxing of the limited edition sneakers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boxing - the act of putting something into a box
  • synonym: review - an evaluation or assessment of a product or service
