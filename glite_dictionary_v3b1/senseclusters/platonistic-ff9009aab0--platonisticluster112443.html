Platonistic

adjective
relating to Platonism or its doctrines
Pronunciation: / pleɪtəˈnɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌplætəˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of Platonism or its doctrines.

Examples

Platonistic philosophy

Many modern philosophers borrow Platonistic concepts to discuss issues of universals and particulars.
