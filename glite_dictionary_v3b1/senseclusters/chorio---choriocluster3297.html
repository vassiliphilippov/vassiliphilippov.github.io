chorio-

x
relating to chorion
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔri.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɔːri.əʊ / (BrE)
a prefix relating to the chorion, the outer membrane surrounding the embryo in mammals, reptiles, and birds

Examples

Chorio- prefix
The prefix chorio- is used in medical terms related to the chorion membrane.
In biology, chorio- appears in words describing conditions or structures involving the chorion.
Doctors may refer to a chorio- infection when discussing diseases affecting the chorion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amni- - prefix relating to the amnion, another membrane around the embryo
