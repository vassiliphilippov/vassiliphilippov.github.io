dodgeball

noun
ball-throwing game
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑdʒˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈdɒdʒˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a game in which players throw balls at each other while trying to avoid being hit themselves

Examples

Play dodgeball in gym
We played dodgeball in gym class and everyone tried to avoid getting hit.
During recess, the children organized a quick game of dodgeball on the playground.
She was the last player left in dodgeball and managed to win the game for her team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capture the flag - a team game where players try to capture the other team's flag
  • synonym: tag - a children's game where players chase and touch others
