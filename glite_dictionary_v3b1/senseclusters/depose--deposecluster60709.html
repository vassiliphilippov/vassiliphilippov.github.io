depose

verb
give sworn testimony
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpoʊz / (AmE), / dɪˈpəʊz / (BrE)
to give evidence or make a formal statement under oath, especially in a legal context

Examples

To depose in court
After deposing in the case, he was advised not to discuss his testimony.
Several experts deposed under oath about the safety standards of the company.
The witness was required to depose before the grand jury regarding the events she had witnessed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: swear - declare or affirm solemnly, especially under oath
  • synonym: testify - give evidence as a witness in a law court
