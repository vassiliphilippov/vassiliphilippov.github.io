vegetative

adjective
consisting of vegetation or plants
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛdʒətətɪv / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒɪtətɪv / (BrE)
(of a layer, cover, or region) consisting of or relating to vegetation or plants.

Examples

Vegetative cover
Our study focuses on vegetative growth in alpine regions.
The vegetative cover helps prevent soil erosion on the hillside.
The forest floor was covered by a thick vegetative layer that nourished the soil.
