aeroplane

noun
powered fixed-wing aircraft
1.1 1.2
Pronunciation: /ˈeɪ.rə.pleɪn/ /; / /ˈeə.rə.pleɪn/ (AmE), /ˈeə.rə.pleɪn/ /; / /ˈeɪ.rə.pleɪn/ (BrE)
A powered flying vehicle with fixed wings and a weight greater than that of the air it displaces, propelled by propellers or jets.

Examples

A model aeroplane
He built a model aeroplane for the school science fair.
The aeroplane soared gracefully above the clouds, carrying passengers across the continent.
The noise from the aeroplane startled the birds in the nearby field.

  • synonym: airplane - an aircraft with fixed wings powered by propellers or jets (American English)
