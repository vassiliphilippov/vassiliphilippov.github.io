homologate

verb
to identify as similar
Pronunciation: / həˈmɑːləˌɡeɪt / (AmE), / həˈmɒləˌɡeɪt / (BrE)
to identify or treat as having the same origin, structure, or function, especially in biology or comparative anatomy

Examples

To homologate body parts
Scientists homologate anatomical features to understand evolutionary relationships between species.
The study aimed to homologate the functions of these proteins across different organisms.
Researchers homologated the gene sequences across several species to identify conserved regions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: differentiate - to recognize or show the difference between things
  • synonym: equate - to consider as equal or equivalent
