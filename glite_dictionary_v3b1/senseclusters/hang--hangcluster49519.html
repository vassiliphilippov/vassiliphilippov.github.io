hang

verb
become unresponsive (computer)
5.6
Pronunciation: /hæŋ/ (AmE), /hæŋ/ (BrE)
(of a computer program, device, or system) to stop responding to input or appear frozen, often temporarily, while still running.

Examples

The app hangs often
The app tends to hang when processing large data files for export.
My laptop hung during the presentation, leaving me unable to continue the demo.
If the browser hangs, try clearing the cache and restarting it right away.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crash - to stop working because of a failure, often causing a program to close or the system to reboot
  • synonym: freeze - to stop responding or updating, as a program or computer interface
