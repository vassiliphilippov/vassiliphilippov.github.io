formless

adjective
without a definite shape or structure
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrmləs / (AmE), / ˈfɔːmləs / (BrE)
lacking a clear or definite shape or structure

Examples

A formless shape
Ideas in his mind were still formless, waiting to be shaped into a story.
The fog drifted in, its formless mass obscuring the road ahead.
The sculpture captured the chaos of nature in a formless swirl of metal.
Fear took a formless shape, creeping into his mind at night.
The artist tried to capture the formless nature of clouds in her painting.
The fantasy novel described a formless creature that could not be seen, only felt.
