Synonyms

  • shapeless
    3.5 1.45/m
    shapeless looks like a blob or mass without a clear outline, while formless covers lack of shape or organized structure.
  • amorphous
    7.3 1.09/m
    amorphous describes things with no regular form or clear internal order, while formless is more general and used in many contexts.
  • structureless
    6.5 0.02/m
    structureless refers to a lack of organized parts or system, while formless usually points to missing shape or loose form.
  • unstructured
    4.4 0.88/m
    unstructured describes things not arranged or planned and often temporary, while formless focuses on missing clear shape or definite form.
