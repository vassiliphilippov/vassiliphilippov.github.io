Krakatoa

proper noun
Indonesian volcanic island; site of 1883 eruption
Pronunciation: / ˌkrækəˈtoʊə /; / ˌkrɑːkəˈtoʊə / (AmE), / ˌkrækəˈtəʊə / (BrE)
a volcanic island in Indonesia between Java and Sumatra, famous for its catastrophic eruption in 1883
