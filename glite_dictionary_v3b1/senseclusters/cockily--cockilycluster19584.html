cockily

adverb
with arrogance
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːkɪli / (AmE), / ˈkɒkɪli / (BrE)
in a way that shows too much confidence or arrogance

Examples

To cockily say
He cockily declared that he would win the competition without any trouble.
She walked cockily into the meeting, certain that her idea would be chosen.
The young player cockily celebrated his goal, drawing mixed reactions from the crowd.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrogantly - in a proud or self-important way
  • antonym: modestly - in a humble or unassuming way
