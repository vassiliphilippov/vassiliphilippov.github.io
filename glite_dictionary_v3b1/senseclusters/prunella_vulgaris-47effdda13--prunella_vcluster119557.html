Prunella vulgaris

noun
a blue-flowered medicinal perennial plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / pruˈnɛlə vʌlˈgɛrɪs / (AmE), / pruˈnɛlə vʌlˈɡɑːrɪs / (BrE)
A decumbent blue-flowered perennial plant of the mint family, native to Europe and naturalized in North America, traditionally believed to have medicinal properties.
