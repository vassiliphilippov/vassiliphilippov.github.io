telephonically

adverb
by telephone
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛləˈfɑnɪkli / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˈfɒnɪkli / (BrE)
by means of a telephone or using telephone communication

Examples

To speak telephonically
The doctor consulted with her patient telephonically to discuss the test results.
All customer support inquiries were handled telephonically during the office closure.
He prefers to resolve issues telephonically rather than by email.

Related Terms

  • synonym: by phone - using a telephone to communicate
  • antonym: in person - face-to-face, not using a telephone or electronic means
