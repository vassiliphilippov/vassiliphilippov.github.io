perioperative

adjective
around surgery period
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛriˌɑːpəˈreɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˌpɛriˌɒpəˈreɪtɪv / (BrE)
relating to the period immediately before, during, and after a surgical operation

Examples

The perioperative period
The doctor explained the perioperative risks to the patient before the procedure.
Careful perioperative planning can help reduce complications after surgery.
Nurses monitored the patient's vital signs during the perioperative period.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: postoperative - relating to the period after a surgical operation
  • degree lower: preoperative - relating to the period before a surgical operation
