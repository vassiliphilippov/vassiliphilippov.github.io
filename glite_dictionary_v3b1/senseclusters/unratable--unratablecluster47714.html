unratable

adjective
not subject to local property taxes
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈreɪ.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈreɪ.tə.bəl / (BrE)
(of property) not liable or not subject to local property taxes or rates.

Examples

Unratable property
Because the land is used for public utility purposes, it is classified as unratable.
Certain nonprofit institutions occupy unratable buildings according to city tax regulations.
The organization owns several unratable properties that are exempt from municipal taxes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ratable - subject to local property taxes or rates
