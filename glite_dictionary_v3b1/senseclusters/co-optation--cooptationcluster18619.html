co-optation

noun
absorbing into a group
7.9
Pronunciation: /koʊ.ɑːp.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /kə.ɑːp.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /kəʊ.ɒp.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
