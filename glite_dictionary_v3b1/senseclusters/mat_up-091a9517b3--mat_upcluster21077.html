mat up

verb
to become tangled or matted
8.9
Pronunciation: / mæt ʌp / (AmE), / mæt ʌp / (BrE)
(of hair, fur, or fibers) to become tangled and pressed together into a thick mass; to form a matted texture.

Examples

To mat up fur
After days without grooming, the dog's fur began to mat up and was difficult to brush.
Her long hair would often mat up overnight if she forgot to braid it before bed.
If you leave wet fabric in a corner, it might mat up and feel rough to the touch.

Related Terms

  • antonym: detangle - to remove tangles or mats from hair or fibers
  • synonym: tangle - to become twisted together in a confused mass
