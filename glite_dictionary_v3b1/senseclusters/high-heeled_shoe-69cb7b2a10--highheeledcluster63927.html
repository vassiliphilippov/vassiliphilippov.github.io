high-heeled shoe

noun
shoe with a tall or raised heel
5.1
Pronunciation: / haɪˌhild ˈʃu / (AmE), / haɪˌhiːld ˈʃuː / (BrE)
A shoe with a tall or raised heel, causing the heel of the wearer's foot to be significantly higher than the toes.

Examples

A pair of high-heeled shoes
Many fashion designers include high-heeled shoes in their collections.
She wore a pair of elegant high-heeled shoes to the party.
Walking in high-heeled shoes for long periods can be uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stiletto - a type of high-heeled shoe with a very thin, high heel
