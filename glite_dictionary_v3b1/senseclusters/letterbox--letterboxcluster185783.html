letterbox

noun
mail collection box
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛtərˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈlɛtəbɒks / (BrE)
a box or slot in a house or building where mail is delivered

Examples

A letterbox for mail
She found a postcard waiting in the letterbox.
The postman slipped the letters through the slot of the letterbox every morning.
Please check the letterbox to see if the package has arrived.
She heard the morning newspaper drop through the letterbox as she made her coffee.
The dog barked every time someone posted a letter through the letterbox.
Rain sometimes gets in if the letterbox is not closed properly.

Related Terms
