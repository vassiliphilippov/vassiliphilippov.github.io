impaction

noun
the state of being tightly packed or fixed
8.2
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpækʃən / (AmE), / ɪmˈpækʃən / (BrE)
the condition of being closely pressed together and firmly fixed, especially so as to be difficult to move or dislodge
