joiner

noun
skilled woodworker who joins pieces of wood
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔɪnər / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪnə / (BrE)
a skilled worker who constructs and assembles wooden items by joining pieces of wood, especially doors, window frames, and furniture

Examples

Skilled joiner crafts furniture
As a joiner, she specialized in crafting elegant staircases from oak.
The joiner carefully assembled the window frames by hand.
They hired a skilled joiner to build custom bookshelves for their office.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carpenter - a person who makes and repairs wooden structures and frameworks
