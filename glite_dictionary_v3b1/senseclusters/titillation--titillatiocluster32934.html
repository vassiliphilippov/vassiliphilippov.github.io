titillation

noun
the act of tickling
Pronunciation: / tɪt̬əˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / tɪtəˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of lightly touching someone so as to cause them to laugh or squirm; tickling

Examples

A titillation of the feet
Children often giggle uncontrollably at even the slightest titillation.
Her playful titillation quickly turned the mood lighthearted.
The titillation of his feet made him burst into laughter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tickling - the act of touching someone lightly to cause laughter
