fulminate

verb
develop suddenly and intensely
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊl.mɪˌneɪt, ˈfʌl- / (AmE), / ˈfʌl.mɪ.neɪt / (BrE)
(of a disease or symptom) to develop suddenly and intensely.

Examples

Symptoms fulminate rapidly
Symptoms of hepatitis sometimes fulminate and require immediate medical attention.
The infection can fulminate within hours if left untreated.
The patient's condition began to fulminate after a period of stability.
