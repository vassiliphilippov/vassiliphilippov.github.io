completeness

noun

(in data management) the degree to which a dataset or information source contains all required records and fields, with few or no missing values.

Examples

Data completeness
The data team evaluated the completeness of the dataset to ensure no missing values.
Our data-quality dashboard shows each table's completeness as a percentage for comparison.
Model performance dropped after discovering low completeness in several critical training features.
