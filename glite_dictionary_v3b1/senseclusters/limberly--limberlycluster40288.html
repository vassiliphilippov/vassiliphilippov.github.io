limberly

adverb
flexibly or supplely
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪmbərli / (AmE), / ˈlɪmbəli / (BrE)
in a flexible, supple, or agile way; moving with ease

Examples

To move limberly
The dancer moved limberly across the stage, impressing the audience with her grace.
He climbed the tree limberly, reaching the top in just a few seconds.
Yoga practitioners often stretch limberly to improve their flexibility.
The gymnast moved limberly across the balance beam, impressing the judges with her grace.
He stretched limberly before the race, preparing his muscles for the sprint ahead.
The dancer spun limberly on stage, captivating the audience with her fluid movements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agilely - in a quick and nimble manner
  • antonym: clumsily - in an awkward or uncoordinated manner
  • synonym: flexibly - in a way that bends or moves easily
  • antonym: stiffly - in a way that is not flexible or supple
