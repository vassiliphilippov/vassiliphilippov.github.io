reacquisition

noun
buying back assets
7.2
Pronunciation: /riː.æk.wɪ.ˈzɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rɪ.æk.wɪ.ˈzɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /riː.æk.wɪ.ˈzɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rɪ.æk.wɪ.ˈzɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act of getting something back that was previously lost, sold, or given up

Examples

Reacquisition of property
