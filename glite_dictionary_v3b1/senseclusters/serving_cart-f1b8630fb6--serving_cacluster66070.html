serving cart

noun
mobile cart for serving food/drinks
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːvɪŋ kɑɹt / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪŋ kɑːt / (BrE)
a small, mobile table or trolley used to serve food and drinks, typically with wheels for easy movement

Examples

A serving cart with wheels
She bought a vintage serving cart for their new apartment.
The hotel uses a serving cart to bring breakfast to guests' rooms.
The waiter rolled the serving cart into the dining room, filled with desserts.
