buffalo

verb
to intimidate or overawe
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌfəˌloʊ / (AmE), / ˈbʌfələʊ / (BrE)
to intimidate or overawe someone, especially by a display of force or aggressive behavior

Examples

To buffalo people

The manager tried to buffalo his employees into working overtime.
