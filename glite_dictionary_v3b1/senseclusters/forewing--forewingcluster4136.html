forewing

noun
front wing of a four-winged insect
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌwɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌwɪŋ / (BrE)
either of the front pair of wings on an insect that has four wings

Examples

The forewing of insect
Damage to the forewing can affect an insect's ability to fly properly.
Scientists examined the structure of the beetle's forewing under a microscope.
The butterfly's forewing is marked with striking yellow bands.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hindwing - either of the rear pair of wings on four-winged insects
