quotative

adjective
relating to quoting
Pronunciation: / ˈkwoʊtətɪv / (AmE), / ˈkwəʊtətɪv / (BrE)
describing or relating to the act of quoting or reporting speech or text

Examples

Quotative marker
In some languages, a quotative particle is used to introduce reported speech.
The teacher explained how 'like' can serve as a quotative marker in casual conversation.
Linguists study the use of quotative expressions in different dialects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reported - relating to speech or information that is repeated by someone else
