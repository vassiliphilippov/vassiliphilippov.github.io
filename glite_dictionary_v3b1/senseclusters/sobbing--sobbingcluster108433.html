sobbing

noun
crying with convulsive gasps
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɒbɪŋ / (BrE)
A series of convulsive gasps or cries made while weeping.

Examples

Loud sobbing sounds
Her shoulders shook with sobbing as she tried to catch her breath.
The child's uncontrollable sobbing could be heard throughout the house.
The movie ended in silence, broken only by the quiet sobbing of the audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: laughing - the act of making sounds of amusement or happiness
  • synonym: weeping - shedding tears; the act of crying
