all-metal

adjective
made entirely of metal
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɔl ˌmɛt̬əl / (AmE), / ˈɔːl ˌmɛtəl / (BrE)
Made entirely of metal, without any parts of another material.

Examples

An all-metal airplane
Early aircraft designs were mostly wood, but the all-metal airplane revolutionized aviation.
The museum displayed an all-metal sculpture that shimmered in the sunlight.
We installed an all-metal roof to withstand hail and snow.
