per

preposition
for each unit
1.0
Pronunciation: / pɚ / (AmE), / pɜː / (BrE)
for each or for every unit of something, used to express rates or ratios

Examples

Miles per hour
The speed limit is 60 miles per hour on this highway.
You should take one tablet per day with water.
The hotel charges $100 per night for a standard room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: each - every individual item or person in a group
  • synonym: per capita - for each person
