oopsie

noun
minor mistake
Pronunciation: / ˈuːpsi / (AmE), / ˈuːpsi / (BrE)
a small or minor mistake, often said in a playful or childlike way

Examples

A little oopsie
He dropped his ice cream and laughed, saying it was just an oopsie.
Whenever a child makes an oopsie, parents often respond with a smile.
During the game, she made a little oopsie but quickly recovered.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blunder - a careless or serious mistake
  • synonym: error - a mistake or inaccuracy
