boastingly

adverb
with excessive pride
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊstɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈbəʊstɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows excessive pride or self-satisfaction about one's achievements or possessions

Examples

Boastingly show pride
He spoke boastingly about his recent achievements at the meeting.
She boastingly described her expensive new car to everyone at the party.
The athlete boastingly recounted his record-breaking performance to the reporters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modestly - in a way that does not show off or boast
  • synonym: proudly - in a way that shows pride
