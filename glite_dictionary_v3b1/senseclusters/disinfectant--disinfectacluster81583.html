disinfectant

adjective
preventing infection; having disinfecting properties
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛktənt / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛktənt / (BrE)
used to prevent infection by inhibiting the growth or action of microorganisms; having disinfecting properties.

Examples

Disinfectant wipes
Doctors recommend washing your hands with disinfectant soap during flu season.
The nurse wiped the wound with a disinfectant solution before applying the bandage.
These wipes have disinfectant properties and are effective against most bacteria.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antiseptic - preventing the growth of disease-causing microorganisms (adj.)
