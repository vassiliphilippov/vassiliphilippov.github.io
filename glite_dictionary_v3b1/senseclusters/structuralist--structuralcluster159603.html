structuralist

adjective
relating to structuralism
Pronunciation: / ˈstrʌktʃərəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈstrʌktʃərəlɪst / (BrE)
relating to or based on the theory or method of structuralism, which analyzes systems by examining relationships and patterns within them.

Examples

Structuralist theory
The structuralist approach to language studies focuses on the relationships between words and their functions.
Many critics use a structuralist perspective to analyze recurring themes in literature.
Her structuralist analysis revealed hidden patterns in the organization of the text.

Related Terms

  • antonym: poststructuralist - relating to theories that critique or move beyond structuralism
  • synonym: structuralism - a theory focusing on the relationships and patterns within a system
