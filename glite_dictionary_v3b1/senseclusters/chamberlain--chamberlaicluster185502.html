chamberlain

noun
officer managing a royal or noble household
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪmbərlɪn / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪmbəlɪn / (BrE)
an officer who manages the household of a king, nobleman, or other important person, often with ceremonial duties

Examples

Royal chamberlain
As chamberlain, he organized receptions and managed the royal servants.
The chamberlain prepared the state rooms for the visiting dignitaries.
The king appointed a trusted chamberlain to oversee all the palace affairs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: steward - person who manages another's property or affairs
