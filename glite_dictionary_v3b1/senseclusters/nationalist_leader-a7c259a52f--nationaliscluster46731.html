nationalist leader

noun
leader of a nationalist movement
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈnæʃənəˌlɪst ˈliːdər / (AmE), / ˈnæʃ(ə)nəlɪst ˈliːdə / (BrE)
the leader of a nationalist movement, especially one advocating for national independence or autonomy.

Examples

A nationalist leader emerges
During the revolution, she emerged as a prominent nationalist leader in her country.
Several former nationalist leaders helped shape the new government after gaining independence.
The nationalist leader called for independence in his speech to the assembled crowd.
