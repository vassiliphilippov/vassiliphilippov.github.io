archaeologically

adverb
in an archaeological way
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrkiəˈlɑːdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɑːkiəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to archaeology, archaeological methods, or evidence

Examples

Artifacts found at the site were archaeologically dated to over two thousand years ago.

The site was archaeologically excavated to reveal artifacts from the Bronze Age.
The building's age was determined archaeologically through the analysis of pottery fragments.
