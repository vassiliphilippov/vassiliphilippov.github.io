cityscape

noun
view or depiction of a city
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪtiˌskeɪp / (AmE), / ˈsɪtiˌskeɪp /; / ˈsɪtɪskeɪp / (BrE)
a view or artistic representation (such as a painting or photograph) of a city or urban area

Examples

A cityscape painting
Artists often find inspiration in the vibrant colors and patterns of the urban cityscape.
The new development dramatically altered the historic cityscape.

She is best known for her vibrant cityscape watercolors, which capture the energy of downtown life.
The gallery is hosting an exhibition of cityscape paintings by local artists.
This cityscape shows the skyline at dusk, with all the buildings glowing in the evening light.
