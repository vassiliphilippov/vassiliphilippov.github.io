food source

noun
origin or supplier of food
2.1
Pronunciation: / fuːd sɔːrs / (AmE), / fuːd sɔːs / (BrE)
a place, organism, or substance from which food is obtained

Examples

A food source for animals
Farmers are always looking for a reliable food source for their livestock.
Pollution can threaten the main food source for fish in the river.
Wild berries served as a food source for early settlers during the harsh winter.
