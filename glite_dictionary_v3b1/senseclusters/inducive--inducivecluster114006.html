inducive

adjective
tending to induce or influence
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈduːsɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈdjuːsɪv / (BrE)
tending to induce or influence; leading or contributing to a particular result or action.

Examples

An inducive effect
His persuasive words were inducive to her accepting the offer.
Such an environment is hardly inducive to creative thinking.
The humid climate is inducive to the rapid growth of mold.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inductive - tending to induce, but also reasoning from specific cases to general principles
