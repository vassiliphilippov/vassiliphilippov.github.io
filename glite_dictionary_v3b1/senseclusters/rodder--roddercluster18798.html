rodder

noun
pipe cleaning tool
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈrɑːd.ər/ (AmE), /ˈrɒd.ə/ (BrE)
a machine or tool used to clear blockages in pipes or sewers by pushing or rotating a long rod

Examples

Pipe rodder
The plumber used a rodder to remove the stubborn blockage from the kitchen drain.
A mechanical rodder can reach deep into pipes that are otherwise hard to access.
After several attempts, the crew finally cleared the sewer using a powerful rodder.

  • synonym: plunger - a tool used to clear blockages in drains using suction
