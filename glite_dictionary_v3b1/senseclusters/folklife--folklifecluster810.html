folklife

noun
traditional customs and arts
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊkˌlaɪf / (AmE), / ˈfəʊkˌlaɪf / (BrE)
the traditional customs, arts, beliefs, crafts, and everyday practices of a community or group of people

A new book explores the folklife of fishing communities along the coast.
