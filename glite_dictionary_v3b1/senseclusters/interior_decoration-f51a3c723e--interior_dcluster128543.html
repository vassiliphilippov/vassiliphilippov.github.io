interior decoration

noun
decoration of building interiors
1.3
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɪriər ˌdɛkəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɪəriə ˌdɛkəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the art or process of decorating and designing the inside of a building or room, especially with regard to color, furnishings, and materials
