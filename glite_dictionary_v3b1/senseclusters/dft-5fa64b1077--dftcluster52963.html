DFT

noun
UK transport department
Pronunciation: / diː ɛf tiː / (AmE), / diː ɛf tiː / (BrE)
the Department for Transport, a UK government department responsible for transport policy, infrastructure, and regulation

Examples

UK DFT announcement
The DFT announced new funding for rural bus services across the region today.
According to the DFT, road safety has improved significantly in the last decade.
The DFT is working on new regulations for electric vehicles in the UK.
