dodgy

adjective
risky or unreliable
8.2 0.55/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆdÉ‘dÊ’i / (AmE), / ËˆdÉ’dÊ’i / (BrE)
involving risk or uncertainty; likely to be dangerous, unreliable, or suspicious

Examples

A dodgy area
It's a dodgy area at night, so be careful walking alone.
The investment scheme seemed a bit dodgy, so I decided not to risk my money.
Their story sounded dodgy, and I wasn't sure I could trust them.
