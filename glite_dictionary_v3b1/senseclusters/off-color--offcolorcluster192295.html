off-color

adjective
slightly indecent; in poor taste

Pronunciation: / ˌɔfˈkʌlər /; / ˈɒf ˌkʌlər /; / ˈɔf ˌkʌlər / (AmE), / ˌɒfˈkʌlə /; / ˈɒf ˌkɒlə /; / ˈɒf ˌkʌlə / (BrE)
(of jokes, remarks, or humor) slightly indecent, rude, or in poor taste, often meant to be funny
