curvaceously

adverb
in a curvaceous manner
Pronunciation: / kɝːˈveɪʃəsli / (AmE), / kɜːˈveɪʃəsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows or suggests curves, especially of a woman's body; in a curvaceous manner.

Examples

Walk curvaceously down runway
She walked curvaceously down the runway, drawing all eyes to her.
The actress danced curvaceously in the vibrant stage performance.
The sculpture was curvaceously designed, emphasizing the flowing lines of the body.
