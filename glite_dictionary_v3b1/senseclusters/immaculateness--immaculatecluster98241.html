immaculateness

noun
state of perfect cleanliness or purity
5.4
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmækjələtnəs / (AmE), / ɪˈmækjʊlətənɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being perfectly clean or free from any stain or blemish.

Examples

The immaculateness of workspace
He prided himself on the immaculateness of his workspace, where every surface gleamed.
Her white dress was a symbol of immaculateness throughout the ceremony.
The immaculateness of the hotel room impressed even the most demanding guests.
