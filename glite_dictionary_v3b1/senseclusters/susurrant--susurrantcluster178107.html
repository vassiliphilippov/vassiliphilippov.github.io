susurrant

adjective
making a low continuous sound
8.3
Pronunciation: / suˈsɜr.ənt / (AmE), / suˈsʌr.ənt / (BrE)
making a low, continuous, and indistinct sound similar to a whisper or murmur.

Examples

A susurrant murmur
A susurrant murmur filled the hall as the audience waited.
She found the susurrant sound of the river deeply calming.
The susurrant breeze rustled through the leaves all night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: murmuring - making a low continuous sound
  • synonym: whispering - making a soft, quiet sound like a whisper
