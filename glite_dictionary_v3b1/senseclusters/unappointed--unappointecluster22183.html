unappointed

adjective
not scheduled
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈpɔɪntɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈpɔɪntɪd / (BrE)
not arranged, scheduled, or fixed in advance, especially referring to a time, meeting, or visit

Examples

Unappointed visit
She arrived at an unappointed time, surprising everyone in the office.
Visitors are not allowed during unappointed hours for security reasons.
He made an unappointed visit to the doctor, hoping to be seen quickly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: appointed - arranged or set for a particular time
  • synonym: unscheduled - not planned or arranged for a specific time
