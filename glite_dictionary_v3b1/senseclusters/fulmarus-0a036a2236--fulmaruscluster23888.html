Fulmarus

noun
a genus of seabirds (fulmars)
Pronunciation: / fʊlˈmɛərəs / (AmE), / fʊlˈmɑːrəs / (BrE)
a genus of seabirds called fulmars, found in cold oceans and related to petrels
