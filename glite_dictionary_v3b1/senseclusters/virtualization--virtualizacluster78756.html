virtualization

noun
making virtual
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˌvɝːtʃuələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌvɜːtʃuəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of converting real-world activities, services, or resources into virtual forms, often using digital technology

Examples

Virtualization of classrooms
The virtualization of classrooms allowed students to attend lessons from home during the lockdown.
Many companies have invested in virtualization to improve flexibility and reduce costs.
The rapid virtualization of services has changed how people interact with businesses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automation - the use of technology to perform tasks without human intervention
  • synonym: digitization - the process of converting information into a digital format
