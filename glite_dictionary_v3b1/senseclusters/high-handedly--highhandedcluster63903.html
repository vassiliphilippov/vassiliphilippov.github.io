In a way that is domineering or shows a disregard for others' opinions or rights; in an overbearing or autocratic manner.

Examples

To high-handedly dismiss concerns
He high-handedly dismissed their concerns as unimportant.
She imposed her decisions high-handedly, without considering her team's input.
The manager acted high-handedly, refusing to listen to any objections.

Synonyms

  • domineeringly
    8.1 0.01/m
    domineeringly shows an overbearing control of others, while high-handedly ignores their opinions and enforces decisions without consent.
  • overbearingly
    7.9 0.01/m
    overbearingly pushes others around and controls them sharply, while high-handedly ignores others' opinions and acts without regard for rights.
  • dictatorially
    9.1 0.02/m
    dictatorially rules as if one were a dictator in power, while high-handedly treats people without listening or seeking consent.
  • imperiously
    8.6 0.29/m
    imperiously gives orders with a proud, superior tone, while high-handedly also ignores others but is harsher and more arbitrary.
