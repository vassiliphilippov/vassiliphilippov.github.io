weekender

noun
small suitcase for a weekend trip
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːkˌɛndər / (AmE), / ˈwiːkˌɛndə / (BrE)
a small suitcase or bag suitable for clothing and accessories needed for a weekend trip

Examples

A weekender bag
For short getaways, I prefer to travel light with just a weekender.
He bought a new leather weekender to carry on the plane for business trips.
She packed her essentials into a stylish weekender before leaving for the train station.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overnighter - a small bag for an overnight trip
  • degree higher: trunk - a large suitcase or lockable box for luggage
