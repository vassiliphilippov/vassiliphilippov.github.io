chronologically

adverb
in time order; by chronology
Pronunciation: / krəˈnɑlədʒɪkli / (AmE), / krəˈnɒlədʒɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to the order of events or time; according to chronology.

Examples

Explain chronologically
He explained the story chronologically, starting from the earliest memories.
The events are listed chronologically in the document for clarity.
We need to organize these photographs chronologically to create a timeline of her life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sequentially - in a sequence or particular order
