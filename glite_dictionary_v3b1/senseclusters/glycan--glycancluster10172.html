glycan

noun
sugar molecule chain
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlaɪkæn / (AmE), / ˈɡlaɪkæn /; / ˈɡlaɪkan / (BrE)
a molecule or chain made up of sugar units (a carbohydrate), found in living organisms, often as part of glycoproteins or glycolipids
