goldfinch

noun
small yellow-plumaged American finch
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldˌfɪntʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldˌfɪntʃ / (BrE)
a small songbird with bright yellow and black feathers, found in America and Europe; the American goldfinch has a bright yellow body, and the European goldfinch has a red face and yellow-and-black wings
