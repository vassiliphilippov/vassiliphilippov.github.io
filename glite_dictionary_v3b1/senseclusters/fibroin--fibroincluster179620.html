fibroin

noun
silk protein
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪbroʊɪn / (AmE), / ˈfɪbrəʊɪn / (BrE)
a protein that forms the main structural component of silk fibers, especially those produced by silkworms and some other insects
