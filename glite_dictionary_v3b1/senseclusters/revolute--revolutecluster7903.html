revolute

adjective
edges rolled backward
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛvəˌlut / (AmE), / ˈrɛvəljuːt / (BrE)
having edges that are rolled backward or inward, especially referring to thin materials or surfaces.

Examples

Revolute paper edges
The old photograph had revolute corners from years of being handled.
She noticed the revolute edges of the parchment as she unrolled the ancient scroll.
The map's revolute border made it difficult to lay completely flat on the table.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curled - having edges that are bent or rolled
  • antonym: flat - having a smooth, even surface without rolled edges
