frugivorous

adjective
fruit-eating (animal)
9.6 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / fruÀêÀàd í…™v…ôr…ôs / (AmE), / fruÀêÀàd í…™v…ôr…ôs / (BrE)
eating mainly fruit, especially used to describe animals whose primary diet consists of fruit

Examples

Frugivorous animals
The toucan is a frugivorous bird that feeds mostly on tropical fruits.
Many rainforest mammals are frugivorous, relying on fruit as their main food source.
Some bats are highly frugivorous and help disperse seeds by eating fruit.
