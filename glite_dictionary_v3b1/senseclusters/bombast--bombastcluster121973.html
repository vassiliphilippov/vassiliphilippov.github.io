bombast

noun
pompous or pretentious language
9.9 7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑːm.bæst/ (AmE), /ˈbɒm.bæst/ (BrE)
Language that is pompous, inflated, or pretentious, and often lacking in real meaning.

Examples

Excessive bombast
Critics dismissed the novel for its excessive bombast and lack of depth.
His habit of using bombast made him seem arrogant to his colleagues.
The politician's speech was full of bombast but lacked any real substance.
