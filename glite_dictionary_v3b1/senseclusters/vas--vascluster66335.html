vas

noun
tube for body fluid (medicine)
Pronunciation: / væs / (AmE), / vas / (BrE)
a tube in the body through which a fluid such as blood or semen circulates, especially in anatomy

Examples

The vas deferens
Damage to a vas can disrupt normal fluid transport in the body.
Doctors used imaging technology to trace the vas during surgery.
In the human male, the vas deferens carries sperm from the testis to the urethra.

  • synonym: duct - a tube or channel that carries liquid or gas in the body or an organ
