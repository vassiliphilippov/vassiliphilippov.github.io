recapture

verb
take back by force
1.0 4.6
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈkæp.tʃər/ /; / /rɪ.ˈkæp.tʃər/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈkæp.tʃə/ /; / /rə.ˈkæp.tʃə/ (BrE)
