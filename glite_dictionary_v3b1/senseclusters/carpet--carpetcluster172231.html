carpet

verb
cover with a carpet
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑr.pət / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.pɪt / (BrE)
to cover (a floor or surface) with a carpet or carpets

Examples

To carpet a room
The hotel planned to carpet all the guest rooms with new, plush flooring.
They decided to carpet the entire living room to make it warmer.
We carpeted the stairs last winter for extra comfort and safety.

Related Terms
