invitation

noun
enticing quality; allurement
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.vɪˈteɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.vɪˈteɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a tempting or enticing quality that attracts someone to do something, especially something potentially unwise

Examples

Invitation to trouble
Her reckless attitude was an invitation for disaster.
His easygoing smile was an invitation to approach and talk.
Leaving the door unlocked is an invitation to thieves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: allurement - the power to entice or attract through personal charm
  • synonym: temptation - the desire to do something, especially something wrong or unwise
