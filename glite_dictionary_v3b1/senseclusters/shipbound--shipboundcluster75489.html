shipbound

adjective
confined to a ship
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪpˌbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpˌbaʊnd / (BrE)
confined to or required to stay on a ship, especially for an extended period

Examples

The shipbound crew
After weeks at sea, the shipbound crew longed for land.
The scientists remained shipbound during the entire research expedition.
Being shipbound for months can be challenging for sailors missing their families.

Related Terms

  • antonym: landlocked - unable to leave land or surrounded by land
  • synonym: seaborne - carried or transported by sea
