encrusted

adjective
covered with a hard crust
6.8
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkrʌstɪd / (AmE), / ɪnˈkrʌstɪd / (BrE)
covered with or having a hard outer layer or crust.

Examples

Encrusted with mud
Her boots were encrusted with mud after the long hike.
The ancient chalice was encrusted with jewels and gold.
The rock was encrusted with barnacles beneath the tide.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bare - without a cover or crust
  • synonym: coated - covered with a layer of something
