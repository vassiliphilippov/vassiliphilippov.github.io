mule

noun
hybrid offspring of donkey and horse
3.7 7/m
Pronunciation: / mjuÀêl / (AmE), / mjuÀêl / (BrE)
A hybrid animal that is the offspring of a male donkey and a female horse, usually sterile.

Examples

A mule carries loads
Farmers often prefer using a mule for carrying heavy loads up mountainous terrain.
The mule is prized for its strength and endurance in agricultural work.
Unlike horses, a mule cannot usually reproduce.
