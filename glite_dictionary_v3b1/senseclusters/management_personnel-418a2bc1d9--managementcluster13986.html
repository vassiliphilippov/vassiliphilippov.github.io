management personnel

noun
people responsible for planning and direction
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmænɪdʒmənt ˌpɜrsəˈnɛl / (AmE), / ˈmænɪdʒmənt ˌpɜːsəˈnel / (BrE)
The group of employees in an organization who are responsible for overall planning, decision-making, and direction.

Examples

The management personnel team
A restructuring plan was announced by the management personnel to improve efficiency and teamwork.
All management personnel must approve the final version of the annual report before its release.
The company's management personnel attended a two-day leadership retreat to set new goals.
