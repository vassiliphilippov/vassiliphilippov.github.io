jingling

adjective
making high-pitched ringing sounds
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪŋ.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪŋ.lɪŋ / (BrE)
making a series of high-pitched ringing sounds like many small bells

Examples

Jingling bells
Children laughed as they listened to the jingling bells on the festive horse-drawn carriage.
I was annoyed by the constant jingling of her bracelets as she typed.
The jingling coins in his pocket gave away his presence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clinking - making a light, sharp, ringing sound
  • synonym: tinkling - producing a light, clear ringing sound
