cooperative

noun
jointly owned business organization
Pronunciation: / koʊˈɑːpərətɪv / (AmE), / kəʊˈɒpərətɪv / (BrE)
an organization or business owned and run by a group of people for their mutual benefit

Examples

A food cooperative
By joining the housing cooperative, residents have a say in how the property is managed.
She shops at a local food cooperative that is owned and run by its members.
The farmers formed a cooperative to sell their crops at better prices.
All the local farmers joined a cooperative to process and sell their crops collectively.
She is a member of a food cooperative that supplies organic produce to the community.
The housing cooperative allows members to make decisions about their living environment together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: co-op - short form of cooperative; a jointly owned store or enterprise
  • antonym: corporation - A company or group of people authorized to act as a single entity
