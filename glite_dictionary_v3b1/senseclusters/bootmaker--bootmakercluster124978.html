bootmaker

noun
person who makes boots
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːtˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈbuːtˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
A person whose occupation is making boots.

Examples

A bootmaker crafts boots
He apprenticed with a skilled bootmaker to learn how to craft leather boots by hand.
Many famous bootmakers have shops in that historic district.
The bootmaker carefully measured my feet before starting his work.
