Silurian

adjective
geological time period
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɪˈlʊriən / (AmE), / sɪˈljʊəriən / (BrE)
relating to the geological period between the Ordovician and Devonian, about 443 to 419 million years ago

Examples

Silurian rocks
The Silurian rocks in this region contain many well-preserved fossils.
Scientists study Silurian strata to learn about early marine ecosystems.
Coral reefs first appeared during the Silurian period.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: Devonian - relating to the geological period after the Silurian
  • degree lower: Ordovician - relating to the geological period before the Silurian
