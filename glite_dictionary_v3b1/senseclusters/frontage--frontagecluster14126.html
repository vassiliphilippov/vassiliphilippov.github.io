frontage

noun
the direction a building faces
Pronunciation: / ˈfrʌn.tɪdʒ /; / ˈfrʌntɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈfrʌn.tɪdʒ /; / ˈfrʌntɪdʒ / (BrE)
