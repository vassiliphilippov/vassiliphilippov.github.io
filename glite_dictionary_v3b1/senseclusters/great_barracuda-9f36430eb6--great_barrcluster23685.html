great barracuda

noun
large tropical predatory fish
8.1 6.4
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪt bəˈrɑːk(j)uːdə / (AmE), / ɡreɪt bəˈræk.juː.də / (BrE)
a large, predatory tropical fish (Sphyraena barracuda) noted for its size, speed, and sharp teeth, sometimes considered dangerous to swimmers and sought after as a food and game fish
