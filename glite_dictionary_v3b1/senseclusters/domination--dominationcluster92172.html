Synonyms

  • dominance
    5.1 4/m
    dominance describes a steady state of power within a group, while domination highlights active control over others.
  • ascendancy
    8.2 0.4/m
    ascendancy means growing influence or power over others, while domination is the condition of having control and authority.
  • hegemony
    9.5 0.8/m
    hegemony is long-term political leadership of one group or nation, while domination is general power or control over others.
  • mastery
    5.6 0.3/m
    mastery is strong control gained by skill or knowledge over something, while domination focuses on power over other people.
  • supremacy
    7.1 2/m
