scantily

adverb
in a skimpy or barely sufficient manner
5.1 5.2
Pronunciation: /ˈskæn.tɪ.liː/ (AmE), /ˈskæn.tɪ.liː/ (BrE)
In a way that is barely sufficient or is revealing, especially of clothing; in a skimpy manner.

Examples

To be scantily dressed
At the pool party, many guests were scantily attired due to the heat.
He criticized the actors for being scantily clad in the scene.
The performers were scantily dressed for the dance number.
