interministerial

adjective
between ministries
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˌmɪnɪˈstɪriəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˌmɪnɪˈstɪəriəl / (BrE)
involving or relating to two or more government ministries

Examples

An interministerial committee
The government created an interministerial committee to coordinate the response to the disaster.
An interministerial agreement was necessary to implement the new education policy.
Regular interministerial meetings help ensure cooperation between different branches of government.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interdepartmental - involving or relating to different departments
