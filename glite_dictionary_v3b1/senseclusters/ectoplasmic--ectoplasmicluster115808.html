ectoplasmic

adjective
ghostly or misty
Pronunciation: / ˌɛktoʊˈplæzmɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛktəʊˈplæzmɪk / (BrE)
having the appearance or qualities of ectoplasm, especially resembling a ghostly or misty substance

Examples

Ectoplasmic ghost shape
A faint, ectoplasmic glow hovered above the old piano in the abandoned house.
The movie used special effects to create an ectoplasmic mist swirling around the haunted room.
She described seeing an ectoplasmic figure drifting through the walls during the séance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ethereal - extremely delicate and light in a way that seems not of this world
  • synonym: ghostly - having the characteristics of a ghost
  • antonym: solid - firm and stable in shape, not liquid or gaseous
