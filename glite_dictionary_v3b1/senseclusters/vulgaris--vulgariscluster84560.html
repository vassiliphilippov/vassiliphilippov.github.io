vulgaris

x
Latin for common
Pronunciation: / vʌlˈɡɛərɪs / (AmE), / vʌlˈɡɑːrɪs / (BrE)
a Latin term meaning 'common', used in scientific names to indicate a species is widespread or typical

Examples

Bufo vulgaris
The scientific name for the common toad is Bufo vulgaris.
In medical terminology, acne vulgaris refers to the most typical form of acne.
The plant species Salvia vulgaris is widely distributed across Europe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: common - found frequently or in large numbers
