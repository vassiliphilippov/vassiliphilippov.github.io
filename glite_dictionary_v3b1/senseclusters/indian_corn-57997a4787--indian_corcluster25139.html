Indian corn

noun
maize with multicolored kernels
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən kɔrn / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən kɔːn / (BrE)
a type of maize with multicolored kernels, often grown for decoration or as food, also known as flint corn

Examples

Indian corn kernels
Indian corn has brightly colored kernels that make it popular around Thanksgiving.
During autumn, families often use Indian corn to decorate their homes.
Native Americans cultivated Indian corn long before European settlers arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corn - the chief cereal crop in many countries, called maize in American English
  • synonym: maize - a cereal plant that yields large grains (corn) set in rows on a cob
