Ben Hogan

proper noun
famous American golfer (1912–1997)
Pronunciation: / bɛn ˈhoʊɡən / (AmE), / bɛn ˈhəʊɡən / (BrE)
(1912–1997) An American professional golfer, widely regarded as one of the greatest players in the history of the sport, known for his major tournament victories and influence on golf technique.

Examples

Ben Hogan golf legend
Ben Hogan is often cited as one of the most influential figures in the history of golf.
After studying Ben Hogan's swing, many golfers have improved their own techniques.
The legacy of Ben Hogan lives on through numerous instructional books and tournaments named after him.
