sulfa

noun
synthetic antibacterial drug (sulfonamide)
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌl.fə /; / ˈsʌlfə / (AmE), / ˈsʌl.fə /; / ˈsʌlfə / (BrE)
any of a group of synthetic antibacterial drugs containing sulfonamides that inhibit the growth of bacteria by interfering with the use of PABA (para-aminobenzoic acid)
