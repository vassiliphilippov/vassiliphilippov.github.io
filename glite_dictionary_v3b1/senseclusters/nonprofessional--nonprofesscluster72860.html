nonprofessional

adjective
not engaged in a profession
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnprəˈfɛʃənl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnprəˈfɛʃənl / (BrE)
Not engaged in or connected with a profession; lacking professional status, training, or qualifications.

Examples

Nonprofessional advice
Her nonprofessional opinion was appreciated, even though she wasn't an expert.
Many nonprofessional volunteers helped organize the community event.
The documentary featured nonprofessional actors who had never been in front of a camera before.

  • antonym: professional - engaged in or connected with a profession
