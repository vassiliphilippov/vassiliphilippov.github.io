pad

noun
block of joined paper sheets
Pronunciation: / pæd / (AmE), / pæd / (BrE)
a number of sheets of paper fastened together along one edge, typically used for writing or drawing

Examples

A pad of paper
Please write your contact information on this pad before you leave.
She always keeps a pad of paper on her desk for jotting down ideas.
The artist pulled a fresh sheet from his sketch pad.
