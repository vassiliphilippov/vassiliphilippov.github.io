Fernao Magalhaes

proper noun
Portuguese explorer, led first world circumnavigation
9.4
Pronunciation: / fɛrˈnaʊ məɡəlˈjɛs / (AmE), / fɜːˈnaʊ məˈɡaljɛs / (BrE)
