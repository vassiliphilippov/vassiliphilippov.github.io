septoplasty

noun
nasal surgery
7.7
Pronunciation: /sept.ə.ˈplæs.tiː/ (AmE), /sept.ə.ˈplæs.tiː/ (BrE)
a surgical procedure to correct or straighten the nasal septum

Examples

Nasal septoplasty surgery
After years of breathing difficulties, he underwent a septoplasty to improve airflow through his nose.
The doctor recommended septoplasty to correct her deviated septum and reduce chronic congestion.
Recovery from septoplasty usually takes about a week, with most patients returning to normal activities soon after.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: deviated septum - a condition where the nasal septum is displaced
  • synonym: rhinoplasty - surgical reshaping of the nose
