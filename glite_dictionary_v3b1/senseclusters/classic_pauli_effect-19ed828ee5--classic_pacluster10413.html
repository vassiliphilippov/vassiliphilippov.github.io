classic Pauli effect

noun
humorous jinxing of lab equipment by presence
9.9 8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈklæs.ɪk ˈpɔː.liː ɪ.ˈfekt/ /; / /ˈklæs.ɪk ˈpaʊ.liː ə.ˈfekt/ (AmE), /ˈklæs.ɪk ˈpaʊ.liː ɪ.ˈfekt/ /; / /ˈklæs.ɪk ˈpɔː.liː ə.ˈfekt/ (BrE)
