packhouse

noun
produce packing building
Pronunciation: / ˈpækˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈpækˌhaʊs / (BrE)
a building where fruit or vegetables are sorted and packed for shipping or sale

Examples

A packhouse building
The apples were taken to the packhouse to be sorted and packed for delivery.
After harvest, workers spent long hours in the packhouse preparing produce for shipment.
Each packhouse must meet strict hygiene standards to ensure food safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: packing shed - a building where goods are packed for transport
  • synonym: warehouse - a large building for storing goods
