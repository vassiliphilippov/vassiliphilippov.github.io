oil-water interface

noun
boundary surface between oil and water
3.7 6.1
Pronunciation: / ɔɪl ˈwɔtɚ ˈɪntɚˌfeɪs / (AmE), / ɔɪl ˈwɔːtə ˈɪntəfeɪs / (BrE)
the surface or boundary where oil and water, which do not mix, come into contact with each other
