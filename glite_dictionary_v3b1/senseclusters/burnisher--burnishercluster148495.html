burnisher

noun
tool for polishing gold leaf in gilding
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝː.nɪ.ʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɜː.nɪ.ʃə / (BrE)
a hand tool with a smooth, hard tip, typically made of agate or another gemstone, used for polishing gold leaf or other surfaces in gilding and similar crafts.

Examples

A burnisher tool
After applying the gold, she used her burnisher to smooth and shine the surface.
Many artists prefer an agate-tipped burnisher for delicate gilding work.
The craftsman gently rubbed the gold leaf with a burnisher to achieve a mirror-like finish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: polisher - tool for making something smooth or shiny by rubbing
