loopful

adjective
full of loops
Pronunciation: / ˈluːpfəl / (AmE), / ˈluːpfʊl / (BrE)
containing or covered with many loops, especially in fabric or decorative surfaces.

Examples

Loopful fabric
The loopful towel was soft and absorbent, perfect for drying off after a shower.
She admired the loopful embroidery on the edge of the tablecloth.
A loopful carpet feels plush and comfortable under your feet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loopy - having many loops or shaped like loops
  • antonym: smooth - having an even, flat surface without loops or roughness
