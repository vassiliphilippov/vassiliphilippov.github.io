bath asparagus

noun
a European plant with edible shoots
Pronunciation: / bæθ əˈspærəɡəs / (AmE), / bɑːθ əˈspærəɡəs / (BrE)
a plant of the star of Bethlehem family (Ornithogalum pyrenaicum), native to Europe, with edible young shoots resembling asparagus

Examples

Wild bath asparagus
He foraged for wild bath asparagus along the woodland edges in spring.
In this region, chefs prepare bath asparagus as a delicate seasonal vegetable.
The young shoots of bath asparagus are harvested before they flower.
