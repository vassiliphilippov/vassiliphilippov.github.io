superteam

noun
team of stars
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərtiːm / (AmE), / ˈsuːpətiːm / (BrE)
a team composed of several highly talented or famous members, often formed to achieve outstanding success

Examples

Basketball superteam
The new superteam dominated the league with their impressive lineup of star players.
Fans were excited to see how the superteam would perform in the championship game.
Critics argued that forming a superteam made the competition less balanced.

Related Terms

  • synonym: all-star team - a team made up of the best players from different teams
  • antonym: underdog - a team or person expected to lose
