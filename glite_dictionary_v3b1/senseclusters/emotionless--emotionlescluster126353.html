emotionless

adjective
showing no emotion
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmoʊʃənləs / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊʃənləs / (BrE)

Examples

An emotionless face

She gave an emotionless reply, betraying nothing of her true thoughts.
