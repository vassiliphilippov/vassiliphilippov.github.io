chondrocyte

noun
cartilage cell
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑndroʊˌsaɪt /; / ˈkɑndrəˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒndrəsʌɪt /; / ˈkɒndrəsiːt / (BrE)
a cell found in cartilage that produces and maintains the cartilaginous matrix

Examples

A chondrocyte cell
A chondrocyte is essential for the growth and repair of cartilage in joints.

Healthy chondrocytes help keep your joints flexible and pain-free.

Damage to chondrocytes can lead to joint problems and cartilage breakdown.
