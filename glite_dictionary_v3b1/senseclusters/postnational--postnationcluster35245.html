postnational

noun
supporter of supranationalism
9.9
Pronunciation: /poʊst.ˈnæʃ.ən.əl/ (AmE), /pəʊst.ˈnæʃ.ən.əl/ (BrE)
a person who supports or believes in political, social, or legal systems that go beyond the traditional nation-state

Examples

A postnational thinker
As a proud postnational, she advocated for international laws that protect human rights everywhere.
The conference attracted many postnationals who believe in cooperation beyond national borders.
He was called a postnational because of his support for global environmental agreements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: globalist - a person who advocates for global cooperation and integration
  • antonym: nationalist - a person who strongly supports their own nation and its interests
