three-finger salute

noun
hand gesture of defiance or solidarity
Pronunciation: / θri ˈfɪŋɡɚ səˈlut / (AmE), / θri ˈfɪŋɡə səˈluːt / (BrE)
a gesture made by raising three fingers, used as a sign of defiance, solidarity, or greeting, especially in political contexts

Examples

Three-finger salute gesture
During the protest, many people raised a three-finger salute to show their support for the movement.
She greeted the crowd by giving a three-finger salute from the stage.
The three-finger salute has become a powerful symbol of resistance in several countries.
