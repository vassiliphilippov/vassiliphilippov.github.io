growth

noun
biological development in organisms
Pronunciation: / ɡroʊθ / (AmE), / ɡrəʊθ / (BrE)
The gradual process by which a living organism increases in size and complexity.

Examples

Child growth
A healthy diet is essential for normal growth in children.
Rapid growth occurs during the early years of life.
Scientists study the growth of bacteria under different conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: development - the process of developing or being developed, especially in biology
