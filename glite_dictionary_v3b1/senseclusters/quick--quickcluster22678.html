quick

adjective
moving swiftly and lightly
Pronunciation: / kwɪk / (AmE), / kwɪk / (BrE)
able to move swiftly and lightly; nimble or agile in movement.

Examples

Quick on his feet
Despite his age, the old man remained surprisingly quick on his feet.
Her quick reflexes allowed her to catch the falling cup.
The squirrel was so quick it darted across the lawn before I even noticed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agile - able to move quickly and easily
  • synonym: nimble - quick and light in movement or action
  • antonym: sluggish - moving slowly or lacking energy
