jellylike

adjective
thick and semi-solid like jelly
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛliˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛliˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having a thick, semi-solid consistency similar to that of jelly or gelatin.

Examples

A jellylike texture
After standing for several hours, the mixture turned jellylike and thickened.
She described the pudding as slightly jellylike in texture.
The substance became jellylike when cooled in the refrigerator.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gelatinous - having a jelly-like consistency
