petiteness

noun
smallness in size; being petite
6.0
Pronunciation: / pəˈtitnəs / (AmE), / pəˈtiːtnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being petite or very small in size

Examples

The petiteness of size
Despite the petiteness of the room, it felt warm and inviting.
Her petiteness was seen as an advantage in gymnastics competitions.
The petiteness of the rare flower made it difficult to spot in the wild.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bigness - the quality of being large in size
  • synonym: smallness - the quality of being small in size
