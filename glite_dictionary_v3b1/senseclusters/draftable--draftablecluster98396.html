draftable

adjective
able to be released from mold
Pronunciation: / ˈdræftəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈdrɑːftəbl̩ / (BrE)
having a shape or design that allows it to be easily released from a mold due to a suitable taper or draft angle.

Examples

Draftable part
The engineer checked if the new component was draftable before finalizing the mold design.
A part must be draftable to ensure it can be removed from the mold without damage.
Designers often add a slight angle to make products draftable during the manufacturing process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moldable - able to be shaped or formed in a mold
  • antonym: undraftable - not able to be released from a mold due to lack of taper
