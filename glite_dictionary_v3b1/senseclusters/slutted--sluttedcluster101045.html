slutted

verb
to shame as promiscuous
Pronunciation: / ˈslʌtɪd / (AmE), / ˈslʌtɪd / (BrE)

Examples

After the party, he was slutted by some classmates for his choice of outfit.
No one deserves to be slutted for how they express themselves.
