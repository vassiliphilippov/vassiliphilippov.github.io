radiant

adjective
shining brightly; emitting light
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪdiənt / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiənt / (BrE)
shining or glowing brightly; sending out light or seeming to send out light.

Examples

Radiant smile
Her face was radiant with joy as she entered the room.
The radiant stars lit up the night sky.
The radiant sunrise filled the sky with golden colors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dull - not bright; lacking shine or light
  • synonym: luminous - emitting or reflecting light; shining
