horse racing

noun
the sport of racing horses
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrs ˌreɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːs ˌreɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
The sport in which horses are raced against each other, usually ridden by jockeys for competition or betting.

Examples

Horse racing betting
Betting on horse racing is a popular pastime in several countries.
He has been fascinated by horse racing since he was a child.
Many people flock to the track to watch horse racing events.
