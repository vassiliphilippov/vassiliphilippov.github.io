grotty

adjective
dirty or unpleasant (informal)
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɑti / (AmE), / ˈɡrɒti / (BrE)
Unpleasant, dirty, or of poor quality; shabby or squalid, especially in an informal or British context.

Examples

A grotty place

She rented a grotty flat above the shop, but at least it was cheap.
The restaurant looked rather grotty, so we decided to eat elsewhere.
