West Indies

proper noun
Caribbean island group between North and South America
Pronunciation: / wɛst ˈɪndiz / (AmE), / wɛst ˈɪndiːz / (BrE)
a group of islands in the Caribbean, stretching from the southeastern United States to the northern coast of South America, known for their tropical climate and tourist resorts

Examples

West Indies islands
Cricket is a very popular sport throughout the West Indies region.
Many tourists visit the West Indies every year to enjoy its beautiful beaches and warm weather.
The hurricane caused significant damage to several islands in the West Indies.
