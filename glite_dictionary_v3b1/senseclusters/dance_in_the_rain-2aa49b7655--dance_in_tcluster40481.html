dance in the rain

verb
enjoy life without worries
4.3
Pronunciation: / dæns ɪn ðə reɪn / (AmE), / dɑːns ɪn ðə reɪn / (BrE)

Examples

Sometimes you just have to dance in the rain and forget about your troubles for a while.
