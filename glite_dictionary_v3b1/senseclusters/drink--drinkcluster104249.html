drink

noun
large deep body of water (informal)
Pronunciation: / drɪŋk / (AmE), / drɪŋk / (BrE)
(informal) A large and deep body of water, such as a sea, ocean, or lake.

Examples

Fell into the drink
He lost his balance and fell straight into the drink.
Local fishermen warn newcomers not to underestimate the power of the drink.
The ship disappeared beneath the waves and was swallowed by the drink.
