sagger

noun
person with sagging pants
Pronunciation: / ˈsæɡər / (AmE), / ˈsæɡə / (BrE)
an informal, often disapproving term for a person who wears trousers or other clothing low on the hips so they sag below the waist

Examples

A teenage sagger
The principal warned the sagger that he needed to pull up his pants at school.
Some people see being a sagger as a form of self-expression, while others disapprove.
The new dress code was introduced to discourage students from becoming a sagger.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fashionista - a person who is very interested in fashion and wears stylish clothes
  • synonym: slacker - a person who avoids work or effort, often used informally and disapprovingly
