containable

adjective
able to be controlled
4.0
Pronunciation: / kənˈteɪnəbəl / (AmE), / kənˈteɪnəbl / (BrE)
able to be kept under control or prevented from spreading.

Examples

Containable oil spill
Thanks to quick action, the oil spill remained containable and did not reach the coastline.
The disease was highly infectious but still containable with proper quarantine measures.
Firefighters declared the blaze containable after several hours of hard work.

  • antonym: uncontrollable - impossible to control or restrain
