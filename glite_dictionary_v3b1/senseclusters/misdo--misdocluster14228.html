misdo

verb
do wrongly or improperly
6.3
Pronunciation: / mɪsˈdu / (AmE), / mɪsˈduː / (BrE)
to do something wrongly or in an improper way

Examples

To misdo a task
If you misdo your assignment, you might have to complete it again.
She often worries that she will misdo important tasks at work.
They realized too late that they had misdone the calculations.

  • antonym: do - to perform an action or activity
