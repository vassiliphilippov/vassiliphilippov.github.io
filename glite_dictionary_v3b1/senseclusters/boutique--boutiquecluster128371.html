boutique

noun
small shop for fashionable items
Pronunciation: / buˈtik / (AmE), / buːˈtiːk / (BrE)
a small shop that sells fashionable clothes, accessories, or other specialized goods, often for women

Examples

A boutique shop
Many fashion designers display their clothes in exclusive boutiques around the city.
She opened a stylish boutique that specializes in handmade jewelry.
We spent the afternoon browsing in a trendy boutique downtown.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shop - a place where goods are sold to customers
