supercow

noun
genetically enhanced cow
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərkaʊ / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəkau / (BrE)
a cow that has been genetically modified or specially bred to have superior traits such as higher milk production or disease resistance

Examples

A supercow gives more milk
The supercow produces much more milk than an ordinary cow thanks to selective breeding.
Researchers hope the supercow will help farmers meet the growing demand for dairy products.
A supercow can resist certain diseases that affect regular cattle herds.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: dairy cow - a cow bred for milk production
  • synonym: genetically modified organism - an organism whose genetic material has been altered for specific traits
