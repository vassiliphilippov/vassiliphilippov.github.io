gas company

noun
company that supplies gas
Pronunciation: / ɡæs ˈkʌm.pə.ni / (AmE), / ɡas ˈkʌm.pə.ni / (BrE)
A company or public utility that supplies gas for heating, cooking, or energy to homes and businesses.
