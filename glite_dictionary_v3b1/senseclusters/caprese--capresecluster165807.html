caprese

noun
Italian mozzarella salad
Pronunciation: / kəˈpreɪzi / (AmE), / kaˈpreːze / (BrE)
an Italian salad made with fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil, usually drizzled with olive oil

Examples

A caprese salad
For lunch, I made a simple caprese with ripe tomatoes and fresh basil.
The restaurant served a delicious caprese as an appetizer before the main course.
She loves to prepare caprese for summer picnics because it is light and refreshing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Greek salad - a salad with tomatoes, cucumbers, olives, and feta cheese
  • synonym: insalata - an Italian word for salad
