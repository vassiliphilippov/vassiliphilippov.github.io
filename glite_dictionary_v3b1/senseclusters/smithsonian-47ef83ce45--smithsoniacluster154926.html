Smithsonian

proper noun
magazine on history and science
Pronunciation: / smɪθˈsoʊniən / (AmE), / smɪθˈsəʊniən / (BrE)
the monthly magazine published by the Smithsonian Institution, featuring articles on history, science, art, and culture
