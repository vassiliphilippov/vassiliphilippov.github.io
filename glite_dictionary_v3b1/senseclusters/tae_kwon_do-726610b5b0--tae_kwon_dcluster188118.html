tae kwon do

noun
Korean martial art with kicks and strikes
Pronunciation: / taɪ ˈkwɑn doʊ / (AmE), / taɪ ˌkwɒn ˈdəʊ / (BrE)
A Korean martial art characterized by high, fast kicks and striking techniques, similar to karate.

Examples

Tae kwon do classes
Many children take tae kwon do classes to improve their discipline and physical fitness.
My brother has been practicing tae kwon do for three years and has earned a blue belt.
She competed in the tae kwon do tournament and won first place.
