wonderful

adjective
extremely good; delightful or admirable
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌndɚfəl / (AmE), / ˈwʌndəfəl / (BrE)
Extremely good; causing delight, admiration, or pleasure.

Examples

A wonderful time
I had a wonderful time at the party last night.
She received some wonderful news about her new job.
The view from the mountain top was wonderful, especially at sunset.
