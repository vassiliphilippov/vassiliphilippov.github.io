taser

verb
to shock with stun gun
5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈteɪ.zər/ (AmE), /ˈteɪ.zə/ (BrE)
to shock someone with a Taser, a device that delivers an electric current to temporarily incapacitate them

Examples

To taser a suspect
The officer had to taser the man when he refused to drop his weapon.
She was accidentally tasered during the police demonstration at the safety seminar.
Authorities decided not to taser the suspect because he was already cooperating.
