fled the scene

verb
left a place quickly, often after an incident
4.2 3.6
Pronunciation: /fled ðə siːn/ /; / /fled ðiː siːn/ (AmE), /fled ðə siːn/ /; / /fled ðiː siːn/ (BrE)

Examples
