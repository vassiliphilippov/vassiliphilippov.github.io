contentless

adjective
lacking lexical meaning

having little or no lexical meaning and serving mainly a grammatical or placeholder function.

Examples

Contentless words
In some languages, certain words are considered contentless because they only serve a grammatical purpose.
The pronoun 'it' in 'It is raining' is often described as contentless in linguistic studies.
Function words like 'the' or 'of' are sometimes labeled as contentless by linguists.
