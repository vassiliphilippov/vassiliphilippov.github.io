darshan

noun
sacred viewing
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːrʃən / (AmE), / ˈdɑːʃən / (BrE)
a sacred viewing or audience with a holy person or deity, especially in Indian religions

Examples

To receive darshan
Thousands of devotees gathered at the temple to receive darshan from the revered guru.
During the festival, visitors waited in line for hours to have darshan of the deity.
She felt blessed after her darshan with the spiritual leader.

Related Terms

  • synonym: audience - a formal meeting with an important person
  • synonym: blessing - a prayer asking for divine favor and protection
