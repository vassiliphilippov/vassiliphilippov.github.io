dietary

adjective
relating to diet or eating habits
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪ.əˌtɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈdaɪ.ə.təri / (BrE)
relating to the food and drink that a person regularly eats and drinks, especially as it affects health

Examples

Dietary habits
He follows a strict dietary regimen to manage his health.
Her new dietary habits helped her feel more energetic throughout the day.
The hospital staff are careful to accommodate patients' dietary needs.
