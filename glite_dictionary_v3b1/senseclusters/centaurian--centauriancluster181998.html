centaurian

adjective
of centaurs or Alpha Centauri
Pronunciation: / sɛnˈtɔːriən / (AmE), / sɛnˈtɔːriən / (BrE)
relating to a centaur or to the Alpha Centauri star system

Examples

Centaurian planet
The science fiction story featured a centaurian species with advanced technology.
Astronomers speculated about the possibility of centaurian planets supporting life.
The artist painted a centaurian landscape inspired by ancient myths and distant stars.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Alpha Centauri - the closest star system to the Solar System
  • synonym: centaur - a mythical creature with the upper body of a human and the lower body of a horse
