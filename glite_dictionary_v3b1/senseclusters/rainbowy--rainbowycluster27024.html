rainbowy

adjective
multicolored appearance
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪnˌboʊ.i / (AmE), / ˈreɪnˌbəʊ.i /; / ˈreɪn.bəʊ.i / (BrE)
having bright, rainbow-like colors, sometimes used to show support for LGBTQ+ pride or culture

Examples

A rainbowy sky
The artist painted a rainbowy sky that brightened the entire room.
Her dress was so rainbowy that it caught everyone's attention at the party.
The oil on the water created a rainbowy sheen in the sunlight.
The parade was filled with rainbowy flags and banners celebrating diversity.
She wore a rainbowy scarf to show her support for LGBTQ+ rights.
The classroom was decorated in a rainbowy style for Pride Month.
