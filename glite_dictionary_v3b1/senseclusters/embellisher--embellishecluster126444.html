embellisher

noun
musical ornamenter
7.0
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbɛlɪʃər / (AmE), / ɪmˈbɛlɪʃə / (BrE)
a musician or performer who adds decorative notes or passages to a melody to make it more elaborate or expressive

Examples

A musical embellisher
The talented embellisher transformed the simple melody into a lively and expressive performance.
As an embellisher, he often added flourishes that delighted the audience.
The teacher praised her student for being a creative embellisher during the recital.

Related Terms

  • synonym: improviser - a musician who creates or adds music spontaneously
  • synonym: ornamenter - a person who decorates or adds embellishments
