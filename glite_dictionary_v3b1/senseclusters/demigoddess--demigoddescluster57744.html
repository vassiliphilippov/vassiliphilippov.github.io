demigoddess

noun
admired woman
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛmiˌɡɑdəs / (AmE), / ˈdɛmiˌɡɒdɪs / (BrE)
an admired woman who is regarded as having extraordinary beauty, talent, or status, sometimes likened to a goddess.

Examples

Famous demigoddess
The actress was considered a demigoddess by her fans for her talent and beauty.
In the world of fashion, she quickly rose to the status of a demigoddess.
Many young women looked up to her as a demigoddess and tried to follow her example.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goddess - a female deity or a woman greatly admired
  • synonym: icon - a person who is greatly admired or idolized
