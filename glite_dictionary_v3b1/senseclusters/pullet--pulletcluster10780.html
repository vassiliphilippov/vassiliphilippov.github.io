pullet

noun
young hen (less than a year old)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊlɪt / (AmE), / ˈpʊlɪt / (BrE)
a young hen, especially one less than a year old and not yet fully mature

Examples

A young pullet
At six months old, a pullet starts to lay her first eggs.
Raising pullets requires careful attention to their diet and environment.
The farmer separated the pullets from the older hens to monitor their growth.
