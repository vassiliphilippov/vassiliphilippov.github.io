broke

adjective
having no money
Pronunciation: / broʊk / (AmE), / brəʊk / (BrE)
having no money; lacking funds

Examples

Completely broke
After losing his job, he was completely broke and struggled to pay rent.
I'm broke until payday, so I can't go out this weekend.
She felt broke after paying all her bills at once.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flush - having plenty of money
  • synonym: penniless - having no money at all
  • synonym: skint - having very little or no money (British slang)
