latke

noun
potato pancake (Jewish cuisine)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑt.kə / (AmE), / ˈlat.kə / (BrE)
A pancake made of grated potato, egg, and a little flour, traditionally fried in oil and served as part of Jewish cuisine.

Examples

Crispy latkes
Crispy latkes are often served with sour cream or smoked salmon.
Every Hanukkah, my grandmother makes the best latkes with applesauce on the side.
We tried a delicious latke at the Jewish deli downtown.

Related Terms
