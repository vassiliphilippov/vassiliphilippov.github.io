fibroblastic

adjective
like fibroblasts
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪbroʊˈblæstɪk / (AmE), / ˌfɪbrəʊˈblæstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or resembling fibroblasts, the cells that produce connective tissue fibers

Examples

fibroblastic tissue cells
The pathologist noted a fibroblastic response in the tissue sample.
Some tumors have a distinctly fibroblastic appearance under the microscope.
Healing wounds often show increased fibroblastic activity as new tissue forms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fibrous - containing or resembling fibers
