spiderish

adjective
like a spider
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪdərɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈspaɪdərɪʃ / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a spider in appearance, movement, or manner

Spiderish movements

He wore a spiderish costume with long, black threads hanging from the sleeves.
