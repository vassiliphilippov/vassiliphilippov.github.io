gift shop

noun
shop selling items for gifts
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /gɪft ʃɑːp/ (AmE), /gɪft ʃɒp/ (BrE)
a shop that sells a variety of items suitable for giving as gifts, often found at tourist attractions or museums

Examples

The gift shop sells souvenirs
She bought a snow globe from the gift shop as a memento of her trip.
The gift shop near the hotel was filled with handmade jewelry and local crafts.
We stopped at the gift shop to buy postcards and souvenirs before leaving the museum.
