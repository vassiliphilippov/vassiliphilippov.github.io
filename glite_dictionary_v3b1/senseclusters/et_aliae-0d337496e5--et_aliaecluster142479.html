et aliae

adverb
Latin feminine plural for 'and others'
6.6
Pronunciation: / ɛt ˈæli.aɪ / (AmE), / ɛt ˈali.aɪ / (BrE)
(Latin) and others; used as a feminine plural to refer to additional people, particularly in citation lists.

Examples

Carver et aliae
In her research, Dr. Smith referenced Carver et aliae for supporting evidence.
The article by Johnson et aliae presents new findings on climate change.
The book's preface gives credit to Moretti et aliae for their assistance in compiling the data.
