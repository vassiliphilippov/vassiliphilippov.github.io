lipreading

noun
interpreting speech by observing lip movements
7.3 5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈlɪp.riː.dɪŋ/ /; / /lɪp.ˈriː.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈlɪp.riː.dɪŋ/ /; / /lɪp.ˈriː.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
The ability or act of understanding spoken words by visually interpreting the movements of the lips, without relying on sound.

Examples

Lipreading skills
Many people who are deaf rely on lipreading for daily communication.
She honed her lipreading skills after losing her hearing in childhood.
The teacher used clear articulation to help students practicing lipreading.
