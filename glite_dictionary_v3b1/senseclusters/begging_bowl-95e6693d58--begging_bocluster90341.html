begging bowl

noun
bowl for collecting donations
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛɡɪŋ boʊl / (AmE), / ˈbɛɡɪŋ bəʊl / (BrE)
a bowl used to collect donations, especially by beggars or in fundraising contexts

Examples

A begging bowl for money
He placed his begging bowl in front of the busy marketplace.
She felt uncomfortable holding out a begging bowl during the event.
The monastery relies on a begging bowl to receive alms from the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: donation box - a container for collecting donations, typically found in public places
