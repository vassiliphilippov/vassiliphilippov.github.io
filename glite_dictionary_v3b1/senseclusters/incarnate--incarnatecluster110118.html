incarnate

verb
to become flesh
7.3 0.18/m
Pronunciation: / …™nÀàk…ëÀêrne…™t / (AmE), / …™nÀàk…ëÀêne…™t / (BrE)
to take on a physical form, especially as a living being or in human flesh

Examples

Gods incarnate as humans
Many myths tell of gods who incarnate as animals or humans to interact with the world.
Some believe that the spirit can incarnate in different bodies over many lifetimes.
The artist tried to incarnate his vision in a sculpture made of marble.
