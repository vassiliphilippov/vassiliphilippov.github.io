duddle

verb
to wrap up warmly
to wrap someone or something up warmly or snugly, especially in a blanket or covering

Examples

To duddle a child
On cold nights, she would duddle her children in thick quilts before they went to sleep.
He likes to duddle himself in a cozy blanket while reading on the sofa.
After the bath, the puppy was duddled in a soft towel to keep warm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: swaddle - to wrap a baby tightly in cloths or blankets
  • antonym: unwrap - to remove a covering or wrapping from something
