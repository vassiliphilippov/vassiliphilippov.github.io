hit a snag

verb
encounter an unexpected difficulty
Pronunciation: / hɪt ə snæɡ / (AmE), / hɪt ə snæɡ / (BrE)

Examples

To hit a snag suddenly
Negotiations hit a snag over the issue of funding.
Our flight hit a snag when bad weather delayed our departure.
The production process hit a snag when a key machine broke down.
