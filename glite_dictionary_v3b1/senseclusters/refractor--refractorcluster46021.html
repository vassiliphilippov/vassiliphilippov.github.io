refractor

noun
vision testing device
7.3
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfræktər / (AmE), / rɪˈfræktə / (BrE)
an instrument used by eye-care professionals to measure vision and determine eyeglass prescriptions

Examples

Eye test refractor
The optometrist adjusted the refractor to find the correct prescription for my new glasses.
During the eye exam, I looked through the refractor as different lenses were switched in front of my eyes.
A modern refractor allows specialists to quickly assess a patient's vision needs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: phoropter - an instrument for testing vision and determining eyeglass prescriptions
  • synonym: retinoscope - an instrument used to examine the retina and measure refractive error
