multiconductor

noun
cable with multiple wires
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltikoʊnˈdʌktər / (AmE), / ˌmʌltikənˈdʌktə / (BrE)
a cable or assembly that contains two or more separate electrical conductors for carrying power or signals

Examples

A multiconductor bundle
The technician installed a multiconductor to connect the new lighting system throughout the building.
A multiconductor is often used in control panels to organize multiple signal paths in one cable.
They labeled each wire in the multiconductor to avoid confusion during maintenance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cable - a thick wire or group of wires covered in plastic, used for carrying electricity or signals
  • degree lower: wire - a thin, flexible thread of metal used to carry electricity
