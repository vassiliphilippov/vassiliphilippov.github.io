uncorrupted

adjective
not corrupted or debased
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.kəˈrʌp.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kəˈrʌp.tɪd / (BrE)
Not influenced by corruption; not debased, pure or morally upright.

Examples

Uncorrupted leader
Despite constant temptation, she remained uncorrupted throughout her long career.
The original manuscript has survived in an uncorrupted state for centuries.
They were praised for their uncorrupted sense of justice and integrity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: corrupt - dishonest or morally debased
  • synonym: pure - not mixed or adulterated, morally clean
