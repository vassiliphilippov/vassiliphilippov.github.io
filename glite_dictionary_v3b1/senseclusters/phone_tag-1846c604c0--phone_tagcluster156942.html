phone tag

noun
repeatedly missing each other's phone calls
9.7
Pronunciation: / foʊn tæg / (AmE), / fəʊn tæg / (BrE)
a situation in which two or more people repeatedly telephone each other but fail to make contact, often leaving messages or missing calls

Examples

Phone tag game
After a week of phone tag, we finally talked yesterday afternoon.
He was frustrated by the endless phone tag with customer service.
We've been playing phone tag all morning and still haven't spoken.
