mouth-to-mouth resuscitation

noun
emergency first aid breathing technique
5.0 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈmaʊθ.tə.maʊθ rɪ.sʌs.ɪ.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ˈmaʊð.tə.maʊð rɪ.sʌs.ɪ.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /maʊθ tə maʊθ rɪ.sʌs.ɪ.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ˈmaʊθ.tə.maʊθ rɪ.sʌs.ɪ.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ˈmaʊð.tə.maʊð rɪ.sʌs.ɪ.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ˈmaʊθ.tə.maʊθ rɪ.sə.sɪ.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a first aid procedure where a person breathes air into the lungs of someone who has stopped breathing, using direct mouth-to-mouth contact
