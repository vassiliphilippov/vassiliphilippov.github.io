heliport

noun
airport for helicopters
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛliˌpɔrt / (AmE), / ˈhɛliˌpɔːt / (BrE)
An airport or landing area specifically designed for the arrival, departure, and maintenance of helicopters.

Examples

A heliport area
Access to the remote island was possible only by boat or heliport.
Several helicopters were parked at the city's main heliport this morning.
The hospital built a heliport on its roof to receive emergency patients more quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airport - a place where aircraft land and take off
