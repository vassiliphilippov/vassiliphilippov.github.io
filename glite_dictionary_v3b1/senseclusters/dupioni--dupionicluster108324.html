dupioni

noun
rough silk fabric
Pronunciation: / duːpiˈoʊni /; / duːˈpiːoʊni / (AmE), / duːpiˈəʊni /; / djuːˈpiːəni / (BrE)
a type of silk fabric with a rough, uneven texture, often used for clothing and decorative items
