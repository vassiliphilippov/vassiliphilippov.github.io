Sur

proper noun
port city in southern Lebanon
8.4
Pronunciation: / sʊr / (AmE), / sʊə / (BrE)
A port city in southern Lebanon on the Mediterranean Sea, formerly a major Phoenician seaport renowned for its silk production.
