aplastic

adjective
lacking plasticity
Pronunciation: / eɪˈplæstɪk / (AmE), / eɪˈplæstɪk / (BrE)
not capable of being molded, shaped, or formed; lacking plasticity or pliability.

Examples

Aplastic clay
The clay from that site was too aplastic to form a smooth pot on the wheel.
Because the material was aplastic, it could not be used for sculpting detailed figures.
Engineers avoided the aplastic compound since it would crack under pressure instead of bending.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plastic - capable of being shaped or molded
  • synonym: rigid - not flexible or easily changed in shape
