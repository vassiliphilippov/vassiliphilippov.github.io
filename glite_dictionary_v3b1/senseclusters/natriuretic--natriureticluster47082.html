natriuretic

adjective
relating to sodium excretion in urine
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˌneɪ.tɹi.jʊˈɹɛ.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌneɪ.tri.juːˈrɛ.tɪk / (BrE)
relating to or causing natriuresis, the excretion of sodium in urine

Examples

Natriuretic hormone effect
Certain hormones in the body produce a natriuretic effect, increasing sodium output in the urine.
Researchers are studying new natriuretic drugs to treat heart failure.
The doctor prescribed a natriuretic agent to help reduce the patient's high blood pressure.
