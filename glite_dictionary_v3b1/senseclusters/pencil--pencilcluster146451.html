pencil

noun
cosmetic stick for face application
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnsl̩ / (AmE), / ˈpɛnsl̩ / (BrE)
a cosmetic in the form of a long, thin stick, used for applying color or definition to a specific part of the face, such as the eyebrows or eyelids

Examples

Eyebrow pencil
She bought a new eyebrow pencil to match her hair color.
The makeup artist sharpened the pencil before applying the eyeliner.
You can use a lip pencil to outline your lips for a fuller look.
