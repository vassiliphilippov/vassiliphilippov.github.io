trade

noun
skilled practical occupation
Pronunciation: / treɪd / (AmE), / treɪd / (BrE)
a skilled occupation, especially one requiring manual or practical training, such as carpentry or plumbing

Examples

A skilled trade
After finishing school, he decided to learn a trade like electrical work or plumbing.
Many young people enter the trade through apprenticeship programs.
Skill and dedication are needed to master any trade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: craft - an activity involving skill in making things by hand
  • antonym: profession - a paid occupation, especially one requiring advanced education or training
