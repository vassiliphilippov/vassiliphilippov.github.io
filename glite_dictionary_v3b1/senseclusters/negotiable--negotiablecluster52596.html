negotiable

adjective
able to be legally transferred to another

(of a document or instrument) able to be legally transferred by endorsement or delivery to another party, so that the rights of ownership pass to the transferee.

Examples

Negotiable instrument
A check is considered a negotiable instrument and can be endorsed to another party.
The company invested in negotiable bonds to maintain liquidity and flexibility.
These shares are negotiable and may be sold or transferred to another holder.

Synonyms

  • assignable
    6.9 0.03/m
    assignable is a right or property that can be legally passed to another person, while negotiable is a specific document transferred by endorsement or delivery.
  • conveyable
    7.4 0.00/m
    conveyable means property or goods can be legally moved to a new owner, while negotiable refers to a transferable legal document with rights passed.
  • transferable
    3.9 1.26/m
    transferable is anything that can be moved or given to another person in many contexts, while negotiable refers to a legal instrument passed by endorsement.
