pathognomonic

adjective
indicating a disease
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpæθəɡnəˈmɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌpæθəɡnəˈmɒnɪk / (BrE)
clearly and uniquely indicating a particular disease or condition

Examples

Pathognomonic sign

A rash on the palms and soles is pathognomonic for this rare infection.
The scientist noted a feature that was pathognomonic of the chemical reaction.
Her constant lateness became almost pathognomonic of her attitude toward the job.
