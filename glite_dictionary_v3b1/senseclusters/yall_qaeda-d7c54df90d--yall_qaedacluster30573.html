Y'all Qaeda

noun
satirical term for extremist group (US)
9.9
Pronunciation: / jɑl keɪˈɑdə / (AmE), / jɑːl keɪˈɑːdə / (BrE)
A satirical and derogatory term for groups, particularly in the United States, perceived as radical, extremist, or vigilante, often with a nod to rural or Southern origins.

Examples

Y'all Qaeda group
Opponents criticized Y'all Qaeda for promoting extremist views under the guise of patriotism.
Some commentators sarcastically referred to the armed occupiers as Y'all Qaeda.
The meme calling the group Y'all Qaeda quickly spread across social media.
