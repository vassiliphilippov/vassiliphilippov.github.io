ketembilla

noun
spiny shrub with edible purple fruit
7.1
Pronunciation: / kɛˌtɛmˈbɪlə / (AmE), / kɛˌtɛmˈbɪlə / (BrE)
A small, spiny shrub native to Sri Lanka and India, cultivated for its maroon-purple fruit with sweet, gooseberry-like pulp.

Examples

Ketembilla shrub
Children enjoy the sweet, tangy taste of fresh ketembilla picked straight from the shrub.

The market was selling homemade jams made from ripe ketembillas.
