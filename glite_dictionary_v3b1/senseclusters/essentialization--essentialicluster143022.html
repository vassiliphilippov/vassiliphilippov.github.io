essentialization

noun
making essential
Pronunciation: / ɪˌsɛnʃəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌsɛnʃəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of treating something or someone as if it has a fixed, essential, or core nature, often ignoring differences or complexity
