bleep

verb
to censor with beep
4.8
Pronunciation: / bliːp / (AmE), / bliːp / (BrE)
to replace or cover a word or sound with a beep, especially to censor offensive language on television or radio

Examples

To bleep bad words
The network bleeped several words during the live broadcast to avoid offending viewers.
Whenever someone swore on the show, their words were bleeped out.
They had to bleep the comedian's joke before airing it on television.

Related Terms

  • synonym: censor - to remove or block content considered offensive or inappropriate
