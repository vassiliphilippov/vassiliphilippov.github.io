turboshaft

adjective
powered by turboshaft engine
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜːrboʊˌʃæft / (AmE), / ˈtɜːbəʊˌʃɑːft / (BrE)
relating to, powered by, or designed to use a turboshaft engine, especially in aircraft or helicopters

Examples

Turboshaft helicopter
The new turboshaft helicopter can carry more passengers and uses less fuel.
Engineers are testing a turboshaft design for the next generation of military aircraft.
A turboshaft powerplant is ideal for helicopters due to its high efficiency and reliability.

Related Terms

  • synonym: turboprop - relating to or powered by a turboprop engine
