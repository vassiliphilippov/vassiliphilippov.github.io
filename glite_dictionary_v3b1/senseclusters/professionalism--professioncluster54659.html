professionalism

noun
qualities or conduct of a professional
1.0
Pronunciation: / prəˈfɛʃənəlɪzəm / (AmE), / prəˈfɛʃ(ə)nəlɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
the competence, skill, and proper conduct expected of a professional person

Examples

High professionalism
Her professionalism was evident in the way she handled the challenging situation.
The manager praised his team's professionalism during the busy season.
We appreciate the professionalism you've shown throughout this project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: amateurism - lack of professional skill or expertise
  • synonym: competence - the ability to do something successfully or efficiently
