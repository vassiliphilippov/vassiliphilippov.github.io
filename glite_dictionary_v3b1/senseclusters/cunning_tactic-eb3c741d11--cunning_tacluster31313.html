cunning tactic

noun
deceitful and clever strategy
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌnɪŋ ˈtæk.tɪk / (AmE), / ˈkʌnɪŋ ˈtæk.tɪk / (BrE)
a clever and deceitful strategy used to achieve a goal

Examples

A cunning tactic in chess
She won the chess match using a cunning tactic that her rival never saw coming.
The politician relied on a cunning tactic to discredit his opponent before the debate.
Through a cunning tactic, the company managed to outmaneuver its competitors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: honest tactic - a straightforward or transparent strategy
  • synonym: ruse - a clever trick or strategy to deceive someone
  • synonym: stratagem - a plan or scheme, especially one used to outwit an opponent
