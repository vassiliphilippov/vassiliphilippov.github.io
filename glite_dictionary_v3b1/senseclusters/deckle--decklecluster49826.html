deckle

noun
papermaking frame for forming sheets
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛkəl / (AmE), / ˈdɛkəl / (BrE)
A frame used in papermaking to shape pulp into sheets of paper.

Examples

Papermaker's deckle
Handmade paper often has rough edges created by the deckle.
