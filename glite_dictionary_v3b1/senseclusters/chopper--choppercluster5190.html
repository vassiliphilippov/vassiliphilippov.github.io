chopper

noun
helicopter (informal)
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑpər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒpə / (BrE)
an aircraft that obtains its lift from the rotation of overhead blades; a helicopter

Examples

A chopper flight
He took a ride in a military chopper during his tour of duty.
The rescue chopper landed on the rooftop to airlift the injured climber.
We could hear the sound of a chopper approaching from the distance.
