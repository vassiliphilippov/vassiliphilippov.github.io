butterweed

noun
a yellow-flowered North American plant
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌt̬ɚˌwid / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəˌwiːd / (BrE)
a yellow-flowered plant of the daisy family, commonly found in North America and often considered a weed
