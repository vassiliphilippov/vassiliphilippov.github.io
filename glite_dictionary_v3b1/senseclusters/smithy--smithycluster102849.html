smithy

noun
blacksmith's workshop
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɪθi / (AmE), / ˈsmɪθi / (BrE)
A workshop where a blacksmith works, especially one equipped for forging and shaping metal by heating and hammering.

Examples

A smithy workshop
He could hear the rhythmic clang of hammers coming from the smithy.
New horseshoes were made every week at the local smithy.
The old village smithy was famous for its decorative ironwork.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forge - a workplace where metal is shaped by heating
