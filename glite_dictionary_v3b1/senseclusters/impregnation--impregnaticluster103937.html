impregnation

noun
process of fertilization; union of gametes

the process by which male and female gametes unite to initiate the development of a new organism, such as fertilization of an egg in animals or of an ovule in plants

Examples

Animal impregnation

Synonyms

  • fertilization
    4.0 2.57/m
    fertilization is the joining of egg and sperm to start development, while impregnation names the biological process of that union.
  • fecundation
    9.9 0.01/m
    fecundation is the physical union of gametes in animals or plants, while impregnation is the general term for that fertilizing process.
  • syngamy
    9.9 0.01/m
    syngamy is the technical fusion of two reproductive cells at the genetic level, while impregnation refers to the whole fertilization event.
  • conception
    4.0 6.90/m
    conception is the start of pregnancy in a woman and may include implantation, while impregnation focuses on gamete union to begin development.
