corn-fed

adjective
strong and healthy but unsophisticated
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnˌfɛd / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnˌfɛd / (BrE)
(of a person, especially from rural America) strong and healthy in appearance but lacking sophistication or refinement.

Examples

Corn-fed farm boy
His corn-fed charm and honest manner impressed everyone at the city meeting.
She grew up in Iowa and has that typical corn-fed look—tall, broad-shouldered, and healthy.
The team was full of corn-fed boys from the Midwest, strong and eager but a bit naive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unsophisticated - lacking worldly experience and refinement
