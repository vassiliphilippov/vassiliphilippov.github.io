sectoral

adjective
relating to a sector
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛktərəl / (AmE), / ˈsɛktərəl / (BrE)
relating to a specific sector or division within a larger system, organization, or economy

Examples

Sectoral policies
The government introduced sectoral policies to address challenges in the manufacturing industry.
Analysts studied sectoral growth to understand which parts of the economy were expanding fastest.
There are significant sectoral differences in how companies responded to the new regulations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: departmental - relating to a department within an organization
  • antonym: overall - relating to the whole rather than a part
