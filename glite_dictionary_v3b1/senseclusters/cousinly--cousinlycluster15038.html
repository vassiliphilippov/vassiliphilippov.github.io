cousinly

adjective
like or fitting for a cousin
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌzənli / (AmE), / ˈkʌz(ə)nli / (BrE)
Characteristic of or appropriate to a cousin; showing the affection or behavior typical of cousins.

Examples

Cousinly advice
He offered his cousinly advice when she moved to the city.
She gave him a cousinly hug before saying goodbye.
There was a warm, cousinly bond between the two families.

Related Terms

  • synonym: friendly - kind and pleasant; showing friendliness
