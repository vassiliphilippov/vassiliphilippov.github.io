soba

noun
buckwheat grain or seed
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊbə / (AmE), / ˈsəʊbə / (BrE)
a type of buckwheat or its seeds, often used as a grain or ground into flour, especially in Japanese cuisine

Examples

Buckwheat soba
She bought some whole soba to grind into flour for homemade pancakes.
Traditional Japanese noodles are often made from soba instead of wheat.
Farmers harvested the soba crop before the first frost arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buckwheat - a plant with grain-like seeds used as food
  • antonym: wheat - a common cereal grain used for flour
