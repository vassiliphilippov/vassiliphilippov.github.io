sensitivity

noun
emotional awareness or responsiveness
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: /sen.sɪ.ˈtɪ.vɪ.tiː/ /; / /sen.sə.ˈtɪ.və.tiː/ (AmE), /sen.sɪ.ˈtɪ.vɪ.tiː/ /; / /sen.sə.ˈtɪ.və.tiː/ (BrE)
the ability to understand and respond to the feelings and emotions of yourself and others

Examples

Emotional sensitivity
Good managers often demonstrate sensitivity when handling employee concerns.

Her sensitivity to other people's emotions makes her a great listener.

Her sensitivity makes her an excellent listener and a supportive friend.
The manager's sensitivity to team members' emotions helped resolve conflicts quickly.
