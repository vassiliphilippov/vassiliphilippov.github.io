telegnosis

noun
knowledge of distant events without senses
Pronunciation: / tɛlɪɡˈnoʊsɪs / (AmE), / tɛlɪɡˈnəʊsɪs / (BrE)
the supposed ability to gain knowledge of distant or unseen events without the use of the normal senses; clairvoyance.

Examples

The telegnosis ability
Her alleged telegnosis made her a popular figure among believers in the supernatural.
Many paranormal investigators claim that telegnosis allows them to perceive events occurring far away.
Skeptics argue there is no scientific evidence to support the existence of telegnosis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clairvoyance - the supposed ability to perceive events in the future or beyond normal sensory contact
