Tridentine

adjective
of Council of Trent
Pronunciation: / traɪˈdɛntaɪn / (AmE), / trɪˈdɛntaɪn / (BrE)
relating to the Roman Catholic practices or liturgy established by the Council of Trent, especially the traditional Latin Mass

Examples

Tridentine Mass
The Tridentine Mass is celebrated in Latin and follows the liturgy set by the Council of Trent.
Many traditionalists prefer the Tridentine rites over modern forms of worship.
The Tridentine reforms had a lasting impact on Catholic doctrine and ceremonies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: post-Tridentine - relating to the period or practices after the Council of Trent
