pasty

adjective
having a sticky or glue-like texture
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪsti / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsti / (BrE)
Having a sticky, adhesive, or glue-like texture.

Examples

Pasty texture
After sitting in the rain, the paper turned pasty and hard to separate.
His hands were left pasty after gluing together the model.
The dough became pasty when too much water was added.
