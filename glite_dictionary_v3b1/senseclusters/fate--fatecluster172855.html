fate

noun
supernatural power controlling events
2.2 2.5
Pronunciation: / feɪt / (AmE), / feɪt / (BrE)
a power or force that is believed to control what happens in the future; destiny

Examples

Fate controls life
Many ancient cultures believed that fate controlled a person's life from beginning to end.
She wondered if meeting him was simply a coincidence or the work of fate.
The story explores whether our lives are shaped by fate or our own choices.
Many believe it was fate that brought them together after so many years apart.
Some people try to challenge their fate instead of accepting what appears inevitable.
The hero bravely accepted his fate, no matter how uncertain it seemed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chance - the occurrence of events in the absence of apparent intention or cause
  • synonym: destiny - the hidden power believed to control future events
  • degree lower: doom - a terrible fate; an unavoidable ill destiny
