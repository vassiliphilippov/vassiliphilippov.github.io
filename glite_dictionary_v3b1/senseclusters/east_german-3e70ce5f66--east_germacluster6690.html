East German

adjective
relating to East Germany or its people
Pronunciation: / iːst ˈdʒɜːrmən / (AmE), / iːst ˈdʒɜːmən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of East Germany or its people, especially during the period when it was a separate state (1949–1990)

Examples

East German government
He grew up under the strict education system of the East German regime.
Many East German athletes competed in the 1988 Seoul Olympics.
The East German border guards were known for their strictness.
