paddling

noun
punishment with paddle
Pronunciation: / ˈpædlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpædlɪŋ / (BrE)
a light beating or spanking with a paddle, often given as a form of punishment

Examples

A paddling punishment
The principal gave the student a paddling for breaking the school rules.
Some parents believe a paddling is an effective way to discipline children.
He remembered the paddling he received as a child for misbehaving in class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beating - an act of hitting someone repeatedly as a punishment
  • synonym: spanking - a punishment involving hitting someone, usually a child, on the bottom
