mall

noun
public pedestrian promenade
5.2
Pronunciation: / mɑl / (AmE), / mɔːl / (BrE)
a public area, often a wide walk or promenade, set aside for pedestrians

Examples

Pedestrian mall
Street performers and artists regularly gather along the waterfront mall.
The city redesigned the downtown area to include a central mall for pedestrians.
We enjoyed a pleasant evening stroll through the tree-lined mall in the park.
