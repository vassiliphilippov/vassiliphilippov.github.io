public concern

noun
issue important to the community
3.2 1.08/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp åbl…™k k…ônÀàs…úÀêrn / (AmE), / Ààp åbl…™k k…ônÀàs…úÀên / (BrE)

Examples

Water safety is always a public concern after heavy floods.
