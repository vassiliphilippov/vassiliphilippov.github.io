psychotropic

adjective
affecting the mind or mood
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkəˈtroʊpɪk / (AmE), / ˌsʌɪkəˈtrɒpɪk / (BrE)
(of a drug or substance) affecting the mind, emotions, or behavior.

Examples

Psychotropic drugs
Care must be taken when combining different psychotropic substances due to potential side effects.
Many psychotropic drugs are used to treat mental health conditions like depression or schizophrenia.
The doctor prescribed a psychotropic medication to help manage her anxiety.
