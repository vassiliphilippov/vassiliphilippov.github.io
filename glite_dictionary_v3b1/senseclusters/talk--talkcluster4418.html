talk

noun
conversation or discussion
1.0
Pronunciation: / tɔk /; / tɑk / (AmE), / tɔːk / (BrE)
an act of speaking or discussion, especially when people exchange ideas, information, or feelings

Examples

Casual talk
His latest novel sparked considerable talk among critics and readers alike.
The community gathered for a talk on environmental issues.
There was a lot of talk about the new regulations at the meeting.
After the meeting, the manager called him aside for a private talk.
She enjoyed a long talk with her friend over coffee.
The professor gave an inspiring talk about climate change to the class.
