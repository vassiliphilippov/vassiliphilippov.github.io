bath

noun
container of liquid for immersing objects
Pronunciation: / bæθ / (AmE), / bɑːθ / (BrE)
a vessel or container filled with liquid in which something is immersed for a specific purpose, such as cleaning, processing, or maintaining temperature

Examples

Ice-water bath
She immersed the sample in an ice-water bath to stop the reaction.
The metal parts were cleaned in a bath of solvent before assembly.
The photographic print was placed in a chemical bath for development.
