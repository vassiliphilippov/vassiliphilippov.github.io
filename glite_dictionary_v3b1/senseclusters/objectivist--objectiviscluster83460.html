objectivist

noun
believer in objectivity
8.2
Pronunciation: / əbˈdʒɛktɪvɪst / (AmE), / əbˈdʒɛktɪvɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes that opinions and actions should be based on objective facts rather than personal feelings or biases

Examples

An objectivist thinker
As an objectivist, he insists on using data to support every argument.
The debate included a passionate objectivist who challenged emotional reasoning.
Her objectivist approach means she rarely lets personal feelings influence her decisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: realist - a person who accepts things as they are and deals with them practically
  • antonym: subjectivist - a person who bases opinions and actions on personal feelings or perspectives
