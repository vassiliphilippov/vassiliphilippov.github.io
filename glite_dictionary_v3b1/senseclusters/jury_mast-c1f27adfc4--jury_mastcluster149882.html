jury mast

noun
temporary replacement mast on a ship
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʊɹi mæst / (AmE), / ˈdʒʊəri mɑːst / (BrE)
a temporary mast fitted to a sailing vessel to replace one that has been lost or broken

Examples

A jury mast replacement
