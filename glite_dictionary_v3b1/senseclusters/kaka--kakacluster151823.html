kaka

noun
New Zealand parrot
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːkɑː / (AmE), / ˈkɑːkɑː / (BrE)
a medium-sized forest parrot native to New Zealand, known for its brownish-green plumage and strong beak

Examples

A New Zealand kaka
The kaka is often seen feeding on nectar and fruit in native New Zealand forests.
Conservationists are working to protect the endangered kaka from predators.
We spotted a young kaka perched quietly among the tree branches during our hike.
