dinosaur exhibit

noun
exhibition featuring dinosaurs
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪnəˌsɔr ɪgˈzɪbɪt / (AmE), / ˈdaɪnəˌsɔː ɪgˈzɪbɪt / (BrE)
an exhibition or display featuring models, fossils, or reconstructions of dinosaurs, usually in a museum setting

Examples

The dinosaur exhibit features interactive activities and educational displays for all ages.
