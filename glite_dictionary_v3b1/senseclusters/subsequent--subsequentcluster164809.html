subsequent

adjective
coming after in time or order
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌb.sɪ.kwənt / (AmE), / ˈsʌb.sɪ.kwənt / (BrE)
Coming after something else in time or order; following.

Examples

Any subsequent changes
Any subsequent changes to the schedule will be communicated by email.
Damage from the initial storm was worsened by subsequent flooding.
The meeting was adjourned due to subsequent developments in the project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: following - coming after or as a result of something
  • antonym: previous - existing or occurring before in time or order
