military vehicle

noun
vehicle used by the military
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪl.əˌtɛri ˈvi.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˈmɪl.ɪ.təri ˈviː.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
a vehicle designed or used for military purposes, typically by the armed forces

Examples

Historic military vehicles
During the parade, several historic military vehicles were displayed to the public.
New technology has improved the safety of military vehicles in combat zones.
The convoy consisted mainly of military vehicles traveling through the desert.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: armored vehicle - a vehicle with protective armor, often used in combat
  • antonym: civilian vehicle - a vehicle used by the general public, not the military
