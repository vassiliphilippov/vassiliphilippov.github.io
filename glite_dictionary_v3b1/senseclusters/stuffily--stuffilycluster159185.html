stuffily

adverb
in a stuffy or overly formal manner
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌfəli / (AmE), / ˈstʌfɪli / (BrE)
In a manner that is formal, pompous, or lacking in fresh air.

Examples

To speak stuffily
Even at home, he stuffily corrected everyone's table manners.
She stuffily insisted on following the exact protocol at every meeting.
The manager greeted the guests stuffily, barely smiling.

Related Terms

  • antonym: informally - in a relaxed, unofficial, or casual manner
  • synonym: stiffly - in a manner that is formal or lacking ease
