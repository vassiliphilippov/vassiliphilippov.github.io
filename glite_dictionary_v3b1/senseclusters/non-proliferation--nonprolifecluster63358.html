non-proliferation

noun
prevention of spread, esp. of nuclear weapons
Pronunciation: / nɑn prəˌlɪfəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / nɒn prəˌlɪfəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
The prevention of an increase or spread of something; especially the prevention of the spread of nuclear weapons and related technology.

Examples

Nuclear non-proliferation treaty
Debates about non-proliferation continue to shape global security policies.
International treaties play a crucial role in non-proliferation efforts.
The organization supports non-proliferation to reduce the risks of nuclear conflict worldwide.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disarmament - The reduction or withdrawal of military forces and weapons
  • antonym: proliferation - Rapid increase in the number or amount of something
