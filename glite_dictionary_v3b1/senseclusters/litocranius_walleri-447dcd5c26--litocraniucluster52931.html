Litocranius walleri

noun
East African antelope (gerenuk)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌlaɪtoʊˈkreɪniəs ˈwɒləri / (AmE), / ˌlɪtəʊˈkrɑːnɪəs ˈwɒləri / (BrE)
A slender antelope of East Africa, known for its long neck and backward-curving horns, commonly called the gerenuk.
