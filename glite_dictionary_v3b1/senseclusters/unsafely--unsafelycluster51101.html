unsafely

adverb
in a dangerous way
2.8
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈseɪf.liː/ /; / /ən.ˈseɪf.liː/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈseɪf.liː/ /; / /ən.ˈseɪf.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that is not safe and could cause harm or danger

Examples

Cross unsafely on street
The children crossed the busy street unsafely without looking both ways.
He stacked the boxes unsafely, and they soon fell over.
If you use electrical equipment unsafely, you could get hurt.
The engineer operated the machinery unsafely, ignoring the warning signs.
If you handle chemicals unsafely, you could cause a serious accident.
The program crashed because the code accessed memory unsafely.
