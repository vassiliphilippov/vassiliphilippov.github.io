rock harlequin

noun
North American herb with pink, yellow-tipped flowers
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑk ˌhɑr.lɪˈkwɪn / (AmE), / ˈrɒk ˌhɑː.lɪˈkwɪn / (BrE)
A glaucous herb native to northeastern United States and Canada, with loose racemes of yellow-tipped pink flowers; sometimes classified under genus Fumaria.
