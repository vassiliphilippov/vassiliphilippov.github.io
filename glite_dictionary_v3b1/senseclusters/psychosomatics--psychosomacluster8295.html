psychosomatics

noun
mind-body medicine
7.2
Pronunciation: /saɪ.koʊ.sə.ˈmæt.ɪks/ (AmE), /saɪ.kəʊ.sə.ˈmæt.ɪks/ (BrE)
the branch of medicine that studies how mental and emotional factors can affect physical health and cause physical symptoms
