marasca

noun
type of bitter cherry used for liqueur
Pronunciation: / məˈræskə / (AmE), / məˈræskə / (BrE)
a small, bitter cherry of a southern European variety, used especially to make maraschino liqueur

Examples

A marasca cherry
Maraschino is made using both the fruit and the pits of the marasca.
She tasted a marasca for the first time and found it surprisingly tart.
The famous maraschino liqueur is traditionally produced from the juice of the marasca cherry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maraschino - a sweet liqueur made from marasca cherries
