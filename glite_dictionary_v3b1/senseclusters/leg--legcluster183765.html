leg

noun
animal limb used for movement
Pronunciation: / lɛɡ / (AmE), / lɛɡ / (BrE)
a limb used for walking or standing, in people or animals

Examples

Stretching your legs after a long flight helps improve circulation.
The cat curled up and tucked its legs beneath its body.
