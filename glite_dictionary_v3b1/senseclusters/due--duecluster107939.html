due

noun
something deserved or owed
Pronunciation: / duː / (AmE), / djuː / (BrE)
a thing that is deserved or owed to someone, especially as a right or just reward

Examples

Give due credit
Give credit where due—she did a fantastic job on the project.
He believes that everyone should receive their due for hard work.
The player was finally given his due after years of dedication.

Related Terms

  • synonym: debt - something, typically money, that is owed or due
  • synonym: desert - something deserved, especially a punishment or reward
