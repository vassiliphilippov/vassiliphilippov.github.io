biowaste

noun
hazardous biological waste
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪoʊˌweɪst / (AmE), / ˈbaɪəʊˌweɪst / (BrE)
biological waste, especially from healthcare or laboratories, that may be hazardous and requires special handling and disposal

Examples

Hospital biowaste
Hospitals must dispose of biowaste in special containers to prevent contamination.
Strict regulations govern the transport and treatment of biowaste from research laboratories.
Improper handling of biowaste can pose serious health risks to workers and the public.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hazardous waste - waste that poses substantial or potential threats to public health or the environment
  • synonym: medical waste - waste produced by healthcare activities that may be hazardous
  • antonym: recyclable waste - waste that can be processed and used again
