hitting

noun
the act of striking or contacting
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of striking or making forceful contact with something

Examples

Loud hitting sounds
After several minutes of hitting, he finally cracked the piñata open.
The hitting during the match was so aggressive that both players ended up with bruises.
The repeated hitting of the ball against the wall made a loud noise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: striking - the act of hitting something with force
