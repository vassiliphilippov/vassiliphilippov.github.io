medic

noun
a plant of the Medicago genus
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪk / (BrE)
any of several plants of the genus Medicago, native to the Old World, with small flowers and trifoliate leaves.

Examples

Medic plant
Farmers often plant medic to improve soil fertility.
Several species of medic are commonly grown as forage crops.
