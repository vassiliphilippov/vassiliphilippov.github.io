huffingly

adverb
with annoyance
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌfɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈhʌfɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows annoyance, irritation, or indignation

Examples

Speak huffingly
She answered huffingly when asked about the mistake she made.
After being criticized, he walked away huffingly from the meeting.
The child huffingly refused to apologize for his behavior.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmly - in a peaceful or relaxed manner
  • synonym: irritably - in an annoyed or easily angered manner
