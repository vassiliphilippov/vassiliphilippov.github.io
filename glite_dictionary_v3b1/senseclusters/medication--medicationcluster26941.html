A substance or drug used to treat, prevent, or alleviate the symptoms of disease.

Examples

Take medication daily

The doctor prescribed a new medication for her allergies.

Synonyms

  • medicine
    1.0 21/m
    medicine is the common word for treatments people take, while medication is the slightly more formal term used by doctors.
  • medicinal drug
    3.3 0.01/m
    medicinal drug sounds technical and is used in clinical contexts, while medication is the general everyday word for treatments.
  • medicament
    7.7 0.06/m
    medicament is a formal or rare medical term found in older texts, while medication is the common modern word patients hear.
  • drug
    1.9 275/m
    drug can mean either a medicine or an illegal narcotic, while medication always means a substance taken to treat or prevent illness.
