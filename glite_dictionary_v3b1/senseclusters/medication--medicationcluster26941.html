medication

noun
drug or substance for treating illness
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛdɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
A substance or drug used to treat, prevent, or alleviate the symptoms of disease.

Examples

Take medication daily
He takes his medication every morning to manage his blood pressure.
It's important not to stop your medication without consulting a healthcare professional.
The doctor prescribed a new medication for her allergies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: medicine - a compound or preparation used for the treatment or prevention of disease
  • synonym: remedy - a treatment that relieves or cures a disease or disorder
