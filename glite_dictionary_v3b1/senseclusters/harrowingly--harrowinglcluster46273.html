harrowingly

adverb
distressingly
Pronunciation: / ˈhær.oʊ.ɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈhær.əʊ.ɪŋ.li / (BrE)
in a way that causes great distress, fear, or pain

Examples

To harrowingly describe pain
The documentary harrowingly depicted the struggles faced by refugees during the war.
She described her experience harrowingly, leaving the audience in stunned silence.
The film's ending was harrowingly realistic and left a lasting impression on viewers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comfortingly - in a way that provides comfort or reassurance
  • synonym: distressingly - in a way that causes anxiety or pain
