firefighting

noun
dealing with crises
4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈfaɪr.faɪ.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈfaɪ.ə.faɪ.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of dealing with urgent problems or crises as they arise, often at the expense of long-term planning

Examples

Office firefighting
The manager spent most of her day firefighting instead of working on long-term projects.
When unexpected issues arise, the team often resorts to firefighting to keep things running.
Too much firefighting can prevent a company from making real progress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crisis management - handling urgent or emergency situations
  • antonym: planning - the process of making plans for the future
