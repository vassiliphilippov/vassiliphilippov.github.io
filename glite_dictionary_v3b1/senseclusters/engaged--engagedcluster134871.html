Examples

Synonyms

  • engrossed
    4.5 0.07/m
    engrossed means so involved you give all your attention and block out surroundings, while engaged can be less deeply focused.
  • absorbed
    3.6 0.00/m
    absorbed means losing track of time because of intense interest, while engaged may mean being busy but not completely lost in it.
  • occupied
    1.8 0.00/m
    occupied means being busy or tied up and not free to help, while engaged emphasizes attention or active involvement.
  • otherwise engaged
    4.6 0.07/m
    otherwise engaged means busy with a different duty and thus unavailable, while engaged simply means involved or focused on an activity.
