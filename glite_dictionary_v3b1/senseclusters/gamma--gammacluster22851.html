gamma

noun
SARS-CoV-2 variant (P.1, WHO 'Gamma')
7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈgæ.mə/ (AmE), /ˈgæ.mə/ (BrE)
A named lineage of the SARS‑CoV‑2 coronavirus (WHO designation 'Gamma', P.1), used to refer to that specific viral variant.

Examples

Gamma variant
Health officials reported that the gamma variant was linked to higher transmission in several cities.
Laboratories used genome sequencing to confirm samples infected with gamma during the outbreak.
The travel advisory warned visitors about regions experiencing rising cases of gamma.

Related Terms

  • synonym: variant of concern - a virus variant identified as having increased transmissibility, severity, or immune escape; Gamma was designated as one
