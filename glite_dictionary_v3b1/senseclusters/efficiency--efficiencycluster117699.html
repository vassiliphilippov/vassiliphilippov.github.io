efficiency

noun
ratio of output to input in a system
4.2 2.9
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈfɪ.ʃən.siː/ /; / /ə.ˈfɪ.ʃən.siː/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈfɪ.ʃən.siː/ /; / /ə.ˈfɪ.ʃən.siː/ (BrE)
the ability to achieve results with as little waste of time, effort, or resources as possible (in machines, systems, or people)

Examples

The new software greatly improved the team's efficiency at completing tasks.
