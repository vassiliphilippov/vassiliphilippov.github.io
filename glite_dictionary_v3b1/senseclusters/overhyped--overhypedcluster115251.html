overhyped

adjective
excessively promoted
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈhaɪpt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈhaɪpt / (BrE)
described or promoted as better or more important than it really is

Examples

An overhyped product
Many critics felt the new smartphone was overhyped and did not meet expectations.
The restaurant was so overhyped that the actual food was a letdown.
Some fans thought the concert was overhyped and not worth the high ticket price.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exaggerated - described as greater or more important than it is
  • antonym: underrated - not given as much praise or attention as deserved
