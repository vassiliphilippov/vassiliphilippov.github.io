scaggy carpet

noun
worn-out or shabby carpet
7.0 6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈskæ.gɪ ˈkɑːr.pət/ /; / /ˈskæ.gɪ ˈkɑːr.pɪt/ (AmE), /ˈskæ.gɪ ˈkɑː.pɪt/ /; / /ˈskæ.gɪ ˈkɑː.pət/ (BrE)
A carpet that is worn-out, threadbare, or in poor condition.

Examples

A scaggy carpet
After years of heavy use, the scaggy carpet in the hallway was full of stains and holes.
They decided to replace the scaggy carpet before putting their house on the market.
Visitors often commented on the old-fashioned furniture and the scaggy carpet in the living room.
