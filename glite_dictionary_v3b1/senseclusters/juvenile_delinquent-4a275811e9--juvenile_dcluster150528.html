juvenile delinquent

noun
a young offender
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuː.və.nəl dɪˈlɪŋ.kwənt / (AmE), / ˈdʒuː.vɪ.nəl dɪˈlɪŋ.kwənt / (BrE)
a young person, usually under the age of 18, who has committed a criminal offense or engaged in antisocial behavior

Examples

Juvenile delinquents commit crimes
Authorities are concerned about the increase in crimes committed by juvenile delinquents in urban areas.
He was labeled a juvenile delinquent after being caught vandalizing public property.
The new program aims to help juvenile delinquents reintegrate into society after serving their sentences.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adult offender - an adult who commits a crime
  • synonym: offender - a person who commits an illegal act
