marriage license

noun
official permit to marry
5.0 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈmæ.rɪdʒ ˈlaɪ.səns/ (AmE), /ˈmæ.rɪdʒ ˈlaɪ.səns/ (BrE)
an official document issued by a government authority allowing two people to marry

Examples

Obtain a marriage license
Most states require you to show identification when obtaining a marriage license.
They went to the courthouse to apply for their marriage license before the wedding.
Without a valid marriage license, the ceremony could not proceed legally.
They applied for their marriage licence at the city hall last week.
Without a valid marriage licence, the marriage is not legally recognized.
You must obtain a marriage licence before you can have a wedding ceremony.
