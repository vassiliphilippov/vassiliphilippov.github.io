rash

noun
red spots or eruption on the skin
1.2
Pronunciation: / ræʃ / (AmE), / ræʃ / (BrE)
a group of red spots on the skin, often caused by illness, allergy, or irritation

Examples

Skin rash
If you notice a rash spreading on your body, consult a doctor.
Some medications may cause a rash as a side effect.
The child developed a rash after eating strawberries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eruption - a sudden appearance of spots or lesions on the skin
