scarlet wisteria tree

noun
tropical tree with red or pink flower clusters
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈskɑːrlət wɪˈstɪriə triː / (AmE), / ˈskɑːlɪt wɪˈstɪəriə triː / (BrE)
a softwood tree with loose clusters of usually red or pink flowers, native to tropical Australia and Asia, and naturalized in southern Florida and the West Indies
