protagonist

noun
main character in a story
1.6
Pronunciation: / proʊˈtæɡənɪst / (AmE), / prəˈtæɡənɪst / (BrE)
The main character in a novel, play, film, or other work of fiction, around whom the plot centers.

Examples

The protagonist character
Critics praised the complexity of the protagonist and her moral dilemmas.
In the film, the protagonist must overcome great odds to save his family.
The novel's protagonist struggles with her own identity throughout the story.

Related Terms

  • antonym: antagonist - the character who opposes the protagonist in a work of fiction
  • synonym: hero - the admired or idealized main character in a story
