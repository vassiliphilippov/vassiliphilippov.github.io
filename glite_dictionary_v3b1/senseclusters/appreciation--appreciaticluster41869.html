appreciation

noun
recognition or understanding of value or quality
Pronunciation: / əˌpriʃiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌpriːsiˈeɪʃən /; / əˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the ability to understand or recognize the quality, value, or importance of something

Examples

Appreciation of music
A true appreciation of the problem requires understanding its historical background.
He has a deep appreciation for the efforts his team made on the project.
Her appreciation of classical music grew after she attended the concert.
He demonstrated great appreciation for the chef's subtle use of spices in the dish.
Her appreciation of classical music was evident in the way she described the symphony.
Only those with true appreciation for fine art could fully enjoy the exhibition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comprehension - the ability to understand
  • antonym: ignorance - lack of knowledge or awareness
  • synonym: taste - the ability to recognize what is beautiful, good, or proper
