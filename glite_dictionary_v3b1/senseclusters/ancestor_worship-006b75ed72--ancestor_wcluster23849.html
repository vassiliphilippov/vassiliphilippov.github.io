ancestor worship

noun
religious practice of honoring ancestors
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.sɛs.tɚ ˌwɜːr.ʃɪp /; / ˈæn.sɛs.tɚ ˈwɜr.ʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈæn.ses.tə ˌwɜː.ʃɪp /; / ˈæn.ses.tə ˈwɜː.ʃɪp / (BrE)
the religious or ritual practice of honoring and showing respect to deceased ancestors, often with the belief that their spirits can influence the living
