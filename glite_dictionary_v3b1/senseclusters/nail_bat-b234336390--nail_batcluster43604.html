nail bat

noun
bat with nails embedded for impact or intimidation
Pronunciation: / neɪl bæt / (AmE), / neɪl bat / (BrE)
A bat with sharp nails embedded in it, typically used as a makeshift weapon for intimidating or causing greater impact.
