console table

noun
narrow table for placing against a wall
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑnˌsoʊl ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈkɒnsəʊl ˈteɪbəl / (BrE)
A narrow table designed to be fixed to a wall or stand against a wall, often used for decorative purposes in hallways or entryways.

Examples

A console table by wall
A console table can add elegance to an entryway while providing extra surface space.
She placed a vase of flowers on the console table in the hallway.
The antique console table was beautifully carved and stood against the living room wall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hall table - a table placed in an entryway or hall
  • synonym: sideboard - a piece of furniture with drawers and cupboards for storing tableware
