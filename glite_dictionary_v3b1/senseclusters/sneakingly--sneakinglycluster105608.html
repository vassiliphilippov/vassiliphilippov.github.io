sneakingly

adverb
in a sneaky or furtive way
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsniːkɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈsniːkɪŋli / (BrE)
In a sneaky, furtive, or surreptitious manner.

Examples

To sneakingly look
He sneakingly glanced at his phone during the meeting.
She sneakingly took an extra piece of cake when no one was looking.
Tom sneakingly left the party early without saying goodbye.

Related Terms

  • synonym: furtively - in a way that attempts to avoid notice or attention; secretly
  • antonym: openly - in a way that is not secret or hidden
