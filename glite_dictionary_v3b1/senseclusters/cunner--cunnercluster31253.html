cunner

noun
a small North Atlantic fish
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌnər / (AmE), / ˈkʌnə / (BrE)
a small North Atlantic fish (Tautogolabrus adspersus), common along the northeastern coast of the United States, with a thick-set body and usually brownish color

A cunner fish
Anglers often catch cunner while fishing for larger species in the Atlantic.
