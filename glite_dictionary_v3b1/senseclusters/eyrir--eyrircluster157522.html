eyrir

noun
Icelandic monetary subunit
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪrɪr / (AmE), / ˈeɪrɪə / (BrE)
A monetary unit of Iceland, equal to one hundredth of a krona.

Examples

One eyrir coin
After inflation, the value of the eyrir became insignificant in daily transactions.
One eyrir is equivalent to one hundredth of an Icelandic krona.
The coins included denominations of five, ten, and twenty-five aurar and one eyrir.
