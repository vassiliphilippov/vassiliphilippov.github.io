floorcloth

noun
cloth for cleaning floors
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔrˌklɔθ / (AmE), / ˈflɔːˌklɒθ / (BrE)
a piece of cloth used for covering or cleaning a floor

Examples

A wet floorcloth
She wiped up the spill with a floorcloth.
After mopping, he rinsed the floorcloth and hung it to dry.
The cleaner brought a fresh floorcloth to scrub the kitchen tiles.
