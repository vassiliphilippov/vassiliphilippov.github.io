tea break

noun
short break from work for tea
1.0
Pronunciation: / tiː breɪk / (AmE), / tiː breɪk / (BrE)
a short, informal break from work, often taken to drink tea or another beverage

Examples

A tea break at work
During our tea break, we chatted about the upcoming holiday.
Let's take a tea break before starting the next meeting.
The office usually has a tea break at 3 p.m. every day.
