transferee

noun
person who is transferred
Pronunciation: / ˌtrænsfəˈri / (AmE), / ˌtrɑːnsfəˈriː / (BrE)
a person who transfers or is transferred from one place, position, or institution to another

Examples

New transferee joins
As a recent transferee from another department, she quickly adapted to her new role.
The company hosted a welcome event for every transferee joining the headquarters this year.
The new policy provides extensive support to each transferee during their transition period.

Related Terms

  • antonym: transferor - a person who makes a transfer to someone else
