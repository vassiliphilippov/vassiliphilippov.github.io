cipher

verb
(archaic) perform arithmetic calculations
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪfər / (AmE), / ˈsʌɪfə / (BrE)
(archaic) To perform arithmetic calculations or computations.

Examples

To cipher sums
He spent all afternoon ciphering the totals for the year's sales.
In earlier centuries, merchants would cipher their accounts by hand.
She ciphered quickly, double-checking the sums before reporting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calculate - to determine mathematically; to compute
  • synonym: reckon - (archaic) to compute or estimate
