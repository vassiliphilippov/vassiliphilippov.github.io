nauseously

adverb
with nausea or disgust
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔːʒəsli / (AmE), / ˈnɔːzɪəsli / (BrE)
in a way that causes, shows, or is like nausea or strong disgust; to a sickening or excessive degree

Examples

Turn away nauseously
He turned away nauseously after smelling the rotten eggs on the counter.
The medicine tasted so bitter that she grimaced nauseously after swallowing it.
They watched nauseously as the graphic scene played out in the movie.
The dessert was nauseously sweet, making it hard for anyone to finish their portion.
He smiled nauseously at the boss, hoping to get a promotion.
The perfume was nauseously strong and filled the entire room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disgustingly - in a way that causes disgust or revulsion
  • antonym: pleasantly - in a way that is enjoyable or pleasing
