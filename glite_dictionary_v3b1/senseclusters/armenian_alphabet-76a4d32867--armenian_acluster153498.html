Armenian alphabet

noun
script for the Armenian language
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈminiən ˈæl.fə.bɛt / (AmE), / ɑːˈmiːniən ˈæl.fə.bɛt / (BrE)
the script of 38 letters used to write the Armenian language
