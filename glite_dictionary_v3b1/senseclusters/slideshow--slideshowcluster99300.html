slideshow

noun
image presentation
Pronunciation: / ˈslaɪdˌʃoʊ / (AmE), / ˈslaɪdˌʃəʊ / (BrE)
a presentation or display in which a series of images or slides is shown one after another, usually on a screen

Examples

A slideshow presentation
The teacher prepared a slideshow to explain the topic to the class.
We watched a slideshow of vacation photos on the living room TV.
During the meeting, she presented a slideshow with charts and graphs to support her ideas.
The teacher prepared a slideshow to help explain the lesson to the class.
We enjoyed watching a slideshow of family photos at the reunion.
He created a digital slideshow to showcase his artwork online.

Related Terms

  • synonym: presentation - a talk or display to show information to an audience
