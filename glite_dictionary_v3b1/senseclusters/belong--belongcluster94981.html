belong

verb
be a part of; fit in
1.6
Pronunciation: / bəˈlɔŋ / (AmE), / bɪˈlɒŋ / (BrE)
to be owned by someone; to be the property of someone

Examples

Belong to you
Do these keys belong to you?
I'm not sure this painting belongs in a modern art gallery.
She felt like she didn't belong in her new school at first.
Please return any items that do not belong to you.
The bicycle belongs to Tom, but he lets his friends borrow it.
This house belongs to my grandparents, and has been in our family for generations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exclude - to prevent someone or something from being part of a group or place
  • synonym: fit - to be suitable or right in a particular situation
  • antonym: own - to have something as one's possession
  • synonym: possess - to have as property; to own
