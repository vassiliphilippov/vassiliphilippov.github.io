mysticete

noun
baleen whale
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪstɪˌsiːt / (AmE), / ˈmɪstɪˌsiːt / (BrE)
a large whale that has baleen plates instead of teeth for filtering food from water

Examples

A large mysticete whale
The blue whale is the largest living mysticete and can weigh over 150 tons.
Unlike toothed whales, a mysticete uses baleen plates to filter tiny organisms from seawater.
Scientists study the migration patterns of each mysticete species to understand their behavior.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baleen whale - a whale with baleen plates for filtering food
  • antonym: toothed whale - a whale with teeth instead of baleen plates
