theater

noun
military region where active operations occur
Pronunciation: / ˈθiːətər /; / ˈθiət̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈθɪətə / (BrE)
a large area where important military actions are occurring during a war; a region in which active military operations are taking place

Examples

The theater of war
He was stationed in the European theater during World War II.
Several countries contributed troops to the Gulf War theater.
The pacific theater was a major area of conflict in the Second World War.
He spent two years in the European theatre during World War II.
Medical staff were urgently needed in the conflict theatre to treat the wounded.
New strategies were deployed across the Pacific theatre.

Related Terms

  • synonym: battlefield - the site or area of a battle
