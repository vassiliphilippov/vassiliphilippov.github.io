reward

noun
numeric feedback signal in reinforcement learning
5.3
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈwɔːrd/ /; / /riː.ˈwɔːrd/ /; / /rə.ˈwɔːrd/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈwɔːd/ /; / /rə.ˈwɔːd/ (BrE)
(in machine learning, especially reinforcement learning) a numeric feedback signal given to an agent that indicates the desirability of a particular action or state; used to guide learning and decision-making.

Examples

AI reward signal
The agent's objective is to maximize the cumulative reward over all episodes during training.
Designing the right reward function can prevent an AI from learning unwanted behaviors.
Researchers gave a small negative reward when the robot bumped into obstacles during trials.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feedback - information about the result of an action used to adjust future actions; in RL, rewards are a form of feedback
  • synonym: reward function - a function that assigns numeric rewards to states or actions in reinforcement learning
