upgrader

noun
oil processing facility
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈʌp.greɪ.dər/ (AmE), /ˈʌp.greɪ.də/ (BrE)
an industrial facility that processes heavy crude oil or bitumen into lighter, more valuable fuels or feedstocks

Examples

Oil upgrader
The company built a new upgrader to convert heavy crude oil into synthetic fuels.
After the upgrader was completed, production of lighter oil products increased significantly.
Environmental regulations require the upgrader to reduce its emissions during processing.
