tackle

noun
fishing gear or equipment
Pronunciation: / ˈtækəl / (AmE), / ˈtækəl / (BrE)
equipment used for fishing, such as rods, lines, and hooks.

Examples

Fishing tackle

Most anglers keep their tackle organized in a special box.
She bought some new tackle for her fishing trip this weekend.
