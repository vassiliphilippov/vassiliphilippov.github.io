ingrowing

adjective
growing abnormally into the flesh
5.6 5.3
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈgroʊ.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈgrəʊ.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
(of a nail or hair) growing abnormally into the surrounding flesh, often causing pain or inflammation.

Examples

Ingrowing toenail
He suffered from an ingrowing toenail that made walking painful.
If left untreated, an ingrowing hair can become infected.
The doctor explained how to prevent ingrowing nails with proper nail care.

Related Terms
