senior high school

noun
public secondary school (grades 9–12)
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːniɚ haɪ skuːl / (AmE), / ˈsiːniə haɪ skuːl / (BrE)
A public secondary school, typically including grades 9 through 12, attended after junior high or middle school.

Examples
