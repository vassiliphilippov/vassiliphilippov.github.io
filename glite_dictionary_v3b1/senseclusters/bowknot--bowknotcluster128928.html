bowknot

noun
knot with two loops and loose ends
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊˌnɑt / (AmE), / ˈbəʊˌnɒt / (BrE)
a knot consisting of two loops and loose ends, commonly used to tie shoelaces or ribbons

Examples

A bowknot on shoes
He showed his younger brother how to make a bowknot when tying his shoes.
She tied her scarf with a perfect bowknot to keep it secure.
The little girl's dress was finished with a pink bowknot at the back.
