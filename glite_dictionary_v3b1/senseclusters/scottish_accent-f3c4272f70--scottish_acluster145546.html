Scottish accent

noun
pronunciation of English from Scotland
Pronunciation: / ˈskɑːtɪʃ ˈæksənt / (AmE), / ˈskɒtɪʃ ˈæksənt / (BrE)
the distinctive way of pronouncing English spoken by people from Scotland

Examples

He lost most of his Scottish accent after living abroad for many years.
Her Scottish accent made her instantly recognizable in the London office.
