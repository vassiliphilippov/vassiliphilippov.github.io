Palmae

noun
the palm family of tropical plants
Pronunciation: / ˈpæl.mi / (AmE), / ˈpæl.miː / (BrE)
A large family of tropical trees, shrubs, and vines with a tall trunk and a crown of large leaves; also known as the palm family.

Examples

The Palmae family
Coconut and date palms both belong to the Palmae family.
Many species in the Palmae are vital for human food and shelter in tropical regions.
The Palmae are characterized by their large, fan-shaped leaves and tall trunks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: palms - plants of the family Palmae or Arecaceae
