nyctophobia

noun
fear of the dark/night
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɪktəˈfoʊbiə / (AmE), / ˌnɪktəˈfəʊbiə / (BrE)
An extreme or irrational fear of the night or darkness.

Examples
