Eusebian

noun
follower of Eusebius
Pronunciation: / juːˈsiːbiən / (AmE), / juːˈsiːbiən / (BrE)
a follower or supporter of Eusebius, especially one who agrees with his theological or historical views

Examples

A Eusebian follower
The council was divided between the Eusebians and those who opposed Eusebius's teachings.
As a devoted Eusebian, he often quoted Eusebius in his sermons.
Historians debate the influence of Eusebians on early church doctrine.
