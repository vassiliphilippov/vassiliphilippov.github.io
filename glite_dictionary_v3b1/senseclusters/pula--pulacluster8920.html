pula

noun
currency unit of Botswana
Pronunciation: / ˈpuːlə / (AmE), / ˈpuːlə / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Botswana, equal to 100 thebe.

Examples

The pula currency
He exchanged his dollars for pula at the airport in Gaborone.
The price of the tour is 1,500 pula per person.
The store only accepts payments in pula.
