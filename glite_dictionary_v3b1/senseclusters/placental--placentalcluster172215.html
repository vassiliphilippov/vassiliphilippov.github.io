placental

adjective
of or relating to a placenta
Pronunciation: / pləˈsɛntəl / (AmE), / pləˈsɛntəl / (BrE)
relating to or having a placenta, especially in reference to mammals that develop a placenta during gestation.

Examples

Placental development
Research on placental development has improved our understanding of pregnancy complications.
Some mammals, such as humans and whales, are classified as placental animals.
Unlike marsupials, placental mammals nourish their young through an organ attached to the uterus.

  • antonym: nonplacental - not having a placenta
