unrevealing

adjective
not disclosing information
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈviːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈviːlɪŋ / (BrE)
not giving away information, details, or emotions; not making something clear or obvious

Examples

Unrevealing answer
His answer was unrevealing, leaving everyone unsure about his true intentions.
The report remained unrevealing about the causes of the incident.
She kept her feelings unrevealing during the entire conversation.

Related Terms
