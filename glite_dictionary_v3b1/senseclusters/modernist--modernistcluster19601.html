modernist

adjective
of modern styles
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑdərnɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɒdənɪst / (BrE)
relating to modern ideas, styles, or movements, especially in art, literature, or architecture

Examples

Modernist architecture
The museum is known for its impressive collection of modernist sculptures.
Her writing style is distinctly modernist, breaking away from traditional narrative forms.
Many modernist buildings feature clean lines and minimal decoration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contemporary - belonging to the present time; modern
  • antonym: traditional - following long-established customs or styles
