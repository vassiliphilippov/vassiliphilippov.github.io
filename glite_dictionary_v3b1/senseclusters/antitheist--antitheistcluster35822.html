antitheist

adjective
opposing belief in gods
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈθiːɪst / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈθiːɪst / (BrE)
actively opposing theism or the belief in the existence of gods

Examples

Antitheist arguments
Her antitheist views were clear in her debates about religion.
The organization adopted an antitheist stance in its public statements.
He wrote an antitheist essay challenging traditional religious beliefs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atheist - not believing in the existence of gods
  • antonym: theist - believing in the existence of one or more gods
