hexameric

adjective
having six parts
Pronunciation: / hɛkˈsæmərɪk / (AmE), / hɛkˈsæmərɪk / (BrE)
consisting of or involving six parts, units, or subunits

Examples

Hexameric structure
The enzyme has a hexameric structure, meaning it is made up of six identical subunits.
Scientists discovered that the virus forms a hexameric ring during replication.
A hexameric protein complex plays a crucial role in this biological process.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: pentameric - consisting of five parts or subunits
