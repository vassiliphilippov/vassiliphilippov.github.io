political detainee

noun
person detained for political reasons
1.7 6.1
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈlɪt.ɪ.kəl diː.teɪn.ˈiː/ /; / /pə.ˈlɪt.ə.kəl diː.tə.ˈniː/ (AmE), /pə.ˈlɪt.ɪ.kəl dɪ.teɪn.ˈiː/ /; / /pə.ˈlɪt.ə.kəl dɪ.tə.ˈniː/ (BrE)
a person who is held in custody by authorities, typically because of their political beliefs or activities rather than for a criminal offense

Examples

Political detainees held abroad
International organizations are calling for the immediate release of all political detainees.
She became a political detainee after participating in a protest against the regime.
The government released several political detainees ahead of the peace negotiations.
