fatigable

adjective
easily tired
7.8
Pronunciation: / fəˈtiːɡəbəl / (AmE), / fəˈtiːɡəbəl / (BrE)
able to become tired or fatigued, especially after activity or effort

Examples

Fatigable person

Some medical conditions can make muscles more fatigable than usual.
Unlike machines, humans are fatigable and require regular breaks to recover.
After several hours of hiking, even the most athletic person can become fatigable.
The medication made her less fatigable during her daily activities.
Children are surprisingly energetic but still fatigable after a long day of play.
