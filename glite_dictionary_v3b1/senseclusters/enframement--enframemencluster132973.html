enframement

noun
arranging within a frame
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfreɪmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈfreɪmənt / (BrE)
the act or process of composing or arranging a scene or image within a frame, especially in photography or art

Examples

Artistic enframement
The director's use of enframement gave the film a unique visual style.
Careful enframement can turn a simple photograph into a work of art.
Artists often experiment with enframement to guide the viewer's attention.
