birdie

noun
golf: one under par on a hole
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːdi / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdi / (BrE)
(Golf) A score of one stroke under par on a hole.

Examples

Score a birdie
After sinking a long putt, he celebrated his first birdie of the round.
Scoring a birdie can give a player a real confidence boost.
She made a birdie on the 15th hole, moving up the leaderboard.
