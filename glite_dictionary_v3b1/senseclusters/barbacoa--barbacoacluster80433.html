barbacoa

noun
Mexican slow-cooked meat
Pronunciation: / ˌbɑːrbəˈkoʊə / (AmE), / ˌbɑːbəˈkəʊə / (BrE)
a traditional Mexican dish of meat that is slow-cooked over an open fire or in a pit, often served shredded

Examples

A plate of barbacoa
We enjoyed delicious barbacoa at the family gathering last weekend.
The restaurant is famous for its tender barbacoa served with fresh tortillas.
She learned how to prepare authentic barbacoa from her grandmother.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barbecue - meat cooked over an open fire or grill
