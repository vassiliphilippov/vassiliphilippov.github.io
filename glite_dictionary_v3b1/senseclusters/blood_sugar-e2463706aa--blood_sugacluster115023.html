blood sugar

noun
glucose level in the blood
Pronunciation: / blʌd ˈʃʊɡɚ /; / blʌd ˈʃʌɡər / (AmE), / blʌd ˈʃʊɡə / (BrE)
the amount of glucose (sugar) present in the blood, used as a measure of health

Examples
