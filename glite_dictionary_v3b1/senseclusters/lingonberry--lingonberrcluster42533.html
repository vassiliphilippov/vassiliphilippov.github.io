lingonberry

noun
small red berry from a northern shrub
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪŋ.ənˌbɛr.i /; / ˈlɪŋ.ɡənˌbɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋ.ənˌbɛr.i / (BrE)
a small, tart red berry from a low evergreen shrub (Vaccinium vitis-idaea), native to northern regions and often used in jams and sauces
