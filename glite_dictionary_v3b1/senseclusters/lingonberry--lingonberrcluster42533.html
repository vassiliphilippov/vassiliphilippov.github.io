lingonberry

noun
small red berry from a northern shrub
6.8 6.6
Pronunciation: /ˈlɪŋ.gən.ber.iː/ /; / /ˈlɪŋ.gən.bə.riː/ (AmE), /ˈlɪŋ.gən.be.riː/ /; / /ˈlɪŋ.gən.bə.riː/ (BrE)
