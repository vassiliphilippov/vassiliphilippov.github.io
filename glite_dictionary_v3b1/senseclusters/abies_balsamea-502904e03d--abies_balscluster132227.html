Abies balsamea

noun
a North American fir tree with fragrant leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.bi.iːz bælˈseɪ.mi.ə / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.bi.iːz bælˈseɪ.mɪ.ə / (BrE)
A medium-sized fir tree native to northeastern North America, known for its fragrant leaves and use as pulpwood and Christmas trees.
