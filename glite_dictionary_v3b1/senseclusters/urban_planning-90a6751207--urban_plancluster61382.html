Synonyms

  • city planning
    3.3 0.32/m
    city planning is the process of designing and managing physical urban layouts and services, while urban planning covers both towns and cities.
  • town planning
    3.6 0.11/m
    town planning focuses on designing and arranging smaller towns and local areas, while urban planning covers larger city and town systems.
  • urbanism
    5.9 0.26/m
    urbanism studies how cities form, grow, and work in society, while urban planning is the practical design and organization of cities and towns.

  • synonym: urban design - the design of cities and towns, focusing on the physical form, structure, and appearance
