druggie

adjective
suggestive of drug users
Pronunciation: / ˈdrʌɡi / (AmE), / ˈdrʌɡi / (BrE)
typical of, associated with, or suggestive of people who use illegal drugs, especially in appearance or behavior

Examples

Druggie style clothes
He wore a druggie jacket that made him stand out in the crowd.
The movie portrayed a druggie lifestyle with dark clubs and reckless behavior.
Her parents worried about her new friends and their druggie attitudes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drug-related - connected to or involving drugs
