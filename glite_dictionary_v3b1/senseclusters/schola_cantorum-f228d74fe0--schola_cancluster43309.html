schola cantorum

noun
choir school in a cathedral or monastery
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌskoʊlə kænˈtɔrəm / (AmE), / ˌskəʊlə kænˈtɔːrəm / (BrE)
A choir school, especially one attached to a cathedral or monastery, where boys with singing ability receive a general education.

Examples

A schola cantorum school

Membership in the schola cantorum was highly sought after by musically talented boys.
