rotundness

noun
state of being round or rounded
Pronunciation: / roʊˈtʌndnəs / (AmE), / rəˈtʌndnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being round or rounded, especially in three dimensions

Examples

The rotundness of fruit
He admired the rotundness of the ripe, red apples in the basket.
The rotundness of the sculpture gave it a pleasing, soft appearance.
The architect was drawn to the rotundness of domed buildings for his new design.

Related Terms

  • antonym: angularity - the quality of having angles or sharp corners
  • synonym: roundness - the quality of having a circular or spherical shape
