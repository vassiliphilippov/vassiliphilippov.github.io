hot-button issue

noun
emotionally charged topic
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑt ˌbʌt̬.ən ˈɪʃ.u / (AmE), / ˈhɒt ˌbʌt.ən ˈɪʃ.uː / (BrE)
an issue or topic that provokes strong emotional reactions, often resulting in heated debate or controversy

Examples

A hot-button issue topic
Climate change has become a hot-button issue in recent political debates.
Education funding is often a hot-button issue for parents and teachers alike.
The candidate avoided discussing the hot-button issue of immigration reform during the interview.

Related Terms

  • synonym: controversial issue - an issue that causes disagreement or public debate
