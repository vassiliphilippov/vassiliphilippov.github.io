bulkily

adverb
in an awkward way
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌkɪli / (AmE), / ˈbʌkɪli / (BrE)
in a manner that is large, awkward, and difficult to move or handle

Examples

To move bulkily
The old sofa was carried bulkily up the narrow staircase by two movers.
She packed her winter clothes bulkily into the suitcase, making it hard to close.
The robot moved bulkily across the uneven ground, struggling to keep its balance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awkwardly - in a way that is difficult or uncomfortable
  • synonym: clumsily - in a way that lacks grace or is difficult to manage
  • antonym: neatly - in a tidy and orderly way
