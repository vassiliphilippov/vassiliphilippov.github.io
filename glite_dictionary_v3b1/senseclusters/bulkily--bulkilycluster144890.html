bulkily

adverb
in an awkward way
5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbʌl.kɪ.liː/ /; / /ˈbʌl.kə.liː/ (AmE), /ˈbʌl.kɪ.liː/ /; / /ˈbʌl.kə.liː/ (BrE)

Examples

To move bulkily
The old sofa was carried bulkily up the narrow staircase by two movers.
She packed her winter clothes bulkily into the suitcase, making it hard to close.
The robot moved bulkily across the uneven ground, struggling to keep its balance.
