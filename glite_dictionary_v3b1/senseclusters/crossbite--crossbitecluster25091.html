crossbite

noun
misaligned teeth
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔsˌbaɪt / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌbaɪt / (BrE)
a dental condition in which the upper and lower teeth do not align properly when the mouth is closed

Examples

A crossbite problem
The orthodontist recommended braces to correct her crossbite and improve her smile.
A severe crossbite can cause difficulty in chewing and jaw pain if left untreated.
Children with a crossbite may need early dental intervention to prevent future problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: malocclusion - improper alignment of the teeth when the jaws are closed
  • degree lower: overbite - a dental condition where the upper front teeth overlap the lower front teeth excessively
