prohibited

adjective
forbidden by law or authority
Pronunciation: / prəˈhɪbɪtɪd / (AmE), / prəˈhɪbɪtɪd / (BrE)
not allowed by law or by an authority; officially forbidden

Examples

Prohibited by law
Smoking is strictly prohibited in all public buildings.
Some items are prohibited from being brought into the country.
The sale of alcohol is prohibited to anyone under the age of 18.
Certain items are prohibited from being carried onto aircraft for security reasons.
Smoking is strictly prohibited in all public areas of the building.
The sale of alcohol is prohibited to anyone under the age of 21.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illegal - against the law
  • antonym: permitted - allowed by law or by an authority
