mountable

adjective
suitable for riding
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntəbl̩ / (BrE)
suitable for being ridden or used as a mount by a person or rider

Examples

Mountable pony
The pony was gentle and mountable even for young children learning to ride.
Only horses that are calm and mountable are chosen for beginner lessons.
The trainer checked if the new horse was mountable before allowing anyone to ride it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rideable - able to be ridden
  • antonym: unrideable - not able to be ridden
