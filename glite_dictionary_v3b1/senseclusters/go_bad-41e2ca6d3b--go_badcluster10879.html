go bad

verb
become unfit for use or consumption
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ bæd / (AmE), / ɡəʊ bæd / (BrE)
to become unfit for consumption or use, especially as a result of spoilage or decay

Examples

Milk goes bad
Despite the cold weather, some of the meat went bad during the long journey.
If you leave milk out of the fridge, it will go bad quickly.
The fruit had gone bad before we had a chance to eat it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rot - to decay or decompose, becoming unfit for consumption
  • synonym: spoil - to become or make unfit for use or consumption, especially through decay
