toeless

adjective
without toes or a toe covering
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊləs / (AmE), / ˈtəʊləs / (BrE)
(of footwear or garments) lacking a covering for the toes, or having an opening at the toe end.

Examples

A toeless shoe
A toeless design allows your feet to breathe in hot weather.
She wore her favorite toeless sandals to the party.
The athlete preferred toeless socks for extra comfort during training.

Related Terms

  • synonym: open-toe - (of shoes) having an opening at the toes
