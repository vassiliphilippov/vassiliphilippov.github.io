motivated

adjective
having incentive or desire to act
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊt̬ɪˌveɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈməʊtɪˌveɪtɪd / (BrE)
having a strong reason or incentive to do something; showing enthusiasm or determination to achieve a goal

Examples

A motivated team
A motivated team can overcome almost any obstacle together.
She is a very motivated student and always works hard to achieve her goals.
The company wants to hire highly motivated individuals who are passionate about their work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: driven - very motivated or determined
  • antonym: unmotivated - lacking motivation, incentive or enthusiasm
