Synaptomys borealis

proper noun
northern bog lemming species
Pronunciation: / sɪˌnæptəˌmaɪz bɔːˈreɪəlɪs / (AmE), / sɪˌnaptəˌmɪz bɔːˈriːəlɪs / (BrE)
