sea lily

noun
marine animal with stalked body and feathery arms
5.0
Pronunciation: / si ˈlɪli / (AmE), / si ˈlɪli / (BrE)
a marine animal with a stalked body and feathery radiating arms, belonging to the class Crinoidea, and typically attached to the sea floor

Examples

The sea lily animal
Despite its name, the sea lily is an animal, not a plant, related to starfish and sea urchins.
Fossils of the sea lily reveal intricate feathery arms once anchored to the ancient seabed.
The sea lily waves its arms gently to capture plankton from the surrounding water.
