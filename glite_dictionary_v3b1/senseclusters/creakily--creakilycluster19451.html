creakily

adverb
in a creaky manner
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːkəli / (AmE), / ˈkriːkɪli / (BrE)
In a creaky manner; making or characterized by creaking sounds or movements.

Examples

To move creakily
He rose creakily from his chair after sitting for hours.
The old floorboards groaned creakily as she tiptoed across the room.
The rusty gate swung open creakily in the wind late at night.
