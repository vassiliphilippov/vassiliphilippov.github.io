mindedness

noun
attitude or outlook
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪndɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈmaɪndɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having a particular attitude, way of thinking, or openness to ideas

Examples

Open-mindedness
Her open-mindedness helps her understand different viewpoints.
The team's mindedness toward innovation led to many creative solutions.
A lack of mindedness can make it difficult to adapt to change.

Related Terms

  • antonym: narrow-mindedness - unwillingness to accept different ideas or opinions
  • synonym: openness - willingness to consider new ideas
