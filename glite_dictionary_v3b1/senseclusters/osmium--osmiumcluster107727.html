osmium

noun
a dense blue-grey metallic element (Os)
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɑzmiəm /; / ˈɑːzmiəm / (AmE), / ˈɒzmiəm / (BrE)
a hard, brittle, blue-grey or blue-black metallic element that is the densest naturally occurring element, used in scientific equipment, alloys, and electrical contacts
