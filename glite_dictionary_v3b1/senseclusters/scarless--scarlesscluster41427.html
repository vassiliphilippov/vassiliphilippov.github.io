scarless

adjective
without scars
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈskɑrləs / (AmE), / ˈskɑːləs / (BrE)
not having any scars or not leaving a scar after healing or treatment

Examples

Scarless recovery
The new medical technique promises a scarless recovery after surgery.
Her skin remained scarless even after the accident.
Many people seek scarless cosmetic procedures for a more natural look.
