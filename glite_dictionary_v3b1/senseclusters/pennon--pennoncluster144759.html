pennon

noun
tapered flag or streamer
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnən / (AmE), / ˈpɛnən / (BrE)
a long, narrow flag that tapers to a point or several points, often used on ships, in military contexts, or for decoration

Examples

A pennon flag
A brightly colored pennon fluttered from the mast of the ship.
Knights carried a pennon on their lances during the tournament.
The festival grounds were decorated with a pennon at each entrance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: banner - a long strip of cloth bearing a slogan or design, used as a flag or standard
  • synonym: pennant - a long, narrow, tapering flag
