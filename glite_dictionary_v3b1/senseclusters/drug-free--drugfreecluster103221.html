drug-free

adjective
without illegal drug use
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdrʌɡ friː / (AmE), / ˈdrʌɡ friː / (BrE)
Not involving or using illegal drugs; characteristic of a person, place, or period where no illegal drugs are used.

Examples

Drug-free life
After years of struggle, he is finally living a drug-free life.
The company maintains a drug-free workplace policy to ensure employee safety.
The school is proud to be completely drug-free and actively educates students about the dangers of drug abuse.
