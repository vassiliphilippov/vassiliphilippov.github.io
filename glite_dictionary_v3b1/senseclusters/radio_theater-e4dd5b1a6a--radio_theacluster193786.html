radio theater

noun
audio drama broadcast on radio
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪdioʊ ˈθiətər / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiəʊ ˈθɪətə / (BrE)
a dramatized performance presented as audio only and broadcast on radio or released on audio media, featuring actors, music, and sound effects
