wheel chart

noun
circular chart with rotating parts
Pronunciation: / wil tʃɑrt / (AmE), / wiːl tʃɑːt / (BrE)
a circular chart with one or more rotating parts, used to display or align information by turning sections

Examples

A wheel chart display
A wheel chart makes it easy to compare different conversion rates by simply rotating the disk.
He designed a wheel chart to display color relationships for his art class.
The teacher used a wheel chart to help students learn about the phases of the moon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slide chart - a chart consisting of sliding strips to align information
