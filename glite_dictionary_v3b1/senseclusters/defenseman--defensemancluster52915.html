defenseman

noun
ice hockey position
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɛnsmən /; / dɪˈfɛnsˌmæn / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛnsmən /; / dɪˈfɛnsˌmæn / (BrE)
a player in ice hockey whose main job is to prevent the opposing team from scoring

Examples

The defenseman blocks shots
The defenseman blocked the shot and cleared the puck from the goal area.
Our team's best defenseman was injured during the second period.
A skilled defenseman can quickly turn defense into offense with a strong pass.
The team's best defenseman blocked several shots during the final period.
As a rookie defenseman, he quickly learned how to read the opposing forwards.
She was named the most valuable defenseman in the league this season.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forward - a player in ice hockey whose main job is to score goals
  • synonym: goalie - the player who guards the goal in ice hockey
