hubcap

noun
cover for the central part of a car wheel
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌbˌkæp / (AmE), / ˈhʌbˌkap / (BrE)
a cover that fits over the central part of a car wheel, often used for decorative or protective purposes

Examples

A hubcap on wheel
A shiny hubcap rolled across the street after the accident.
He replaced the missing hubcap on his car’s front tire.
Many people choose custom hubcaps to improve their car's appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wheel cover - a removable covering for a wheel, usually decorative
