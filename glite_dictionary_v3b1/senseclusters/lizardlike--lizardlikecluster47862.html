lizardlike

adjective
resembling a lizard
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪzərdlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈlɪzədlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling a lizard in appearance, movement, or qualities (such as being cold-blooded or unemotional)

Examples

Lizardlike appearance
The villain's lizardlike stare made everyone in the room uncomfortable.
Her lizardlike patience allowed her to wait silently for hours.
He moved with a lizardlike grace, quick and silent across the rocks.
The dragon's lizardlike scales shimmered in the sunlight as it moved.
She described the mysterious animal as having a lizardlike body and quick, darting movements.
The statue had a lizardlike tail curling around its base.
