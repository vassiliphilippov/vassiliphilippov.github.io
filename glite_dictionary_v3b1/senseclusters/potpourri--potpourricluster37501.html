potpourri

noun
mixture of assorted items
7.2 0.41/m
Pronunciation: / Àåpo ä.p äÀàri / (AmE), / Àåp…í.p äÀàriÀê / (BrE)
A mixture or collection of various different items, often diverse or unrelated.

Examples

Potpourri of ideas
Her playlist offers a potpourri of music genres, from classical to hip-hop.
The festival featured a potpourri of activities, including games, food stalls, and performances.
The museum's new exhibit is a potpourri of art from various cultures and periods.
