brochette

noun
small skewer for grilling
8.8
Pronunciation: / broʊˈʃɛt / (AmE), / brəʊˈʃɛt / (BrE)
A small skewer or spit, especially one used for grilling pieces of meat or vegetables.

Examples

Metal brochette
For the appetizer, she served shrimp and pineapple on a brochette.
The chef slid pieces of marinated chicken onto a brochette to grill over the open flame.
Vegetables cooked on a brochette can add a colorful touch to any barbecue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skewer - a long, thin metal or wooden stick used to hold pieces of food together during cooking
