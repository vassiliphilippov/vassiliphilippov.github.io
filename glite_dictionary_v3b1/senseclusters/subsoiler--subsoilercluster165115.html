subsoiler

noun
deep soil tiller
8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈsʌb.sɔɪ.lər/ (AmE), /ˈsʌb.sɔɪ.lə/ (BrE)
a farm implement used to break up and loosen soil deep below the surface without turning it over

Examples

A subsoiler tool
The farmer attached the subsoiler to his tractor before working the field.
Using a subsoiler helps improve water drainage and root growth in compacted soils.
After years of heavy use, the old subsoiler needed new parts to function properly.
