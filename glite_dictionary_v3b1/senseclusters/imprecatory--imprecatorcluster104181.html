imprecatory

adjective
wishing harm
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɪm.ˈprek.ə.tɑːr.iː/ /; / /ɪm.ˈprek.ə.tɔːr.iː/ (AmE), /ɪm.ˈprek.ə.tə.riː/ /; / /ɪm.ˈprek.ə.tɔː.riː/ (BrE)
expressing or relating to a wish or prayer for harm, a curse, or divine punishment, especially in religious texts

Examples
