staffer

noun
employee who is part of a staff
Pronunciation: / ˈstæfər / (AmE), / ˈstɑːfə / (BrE)
an employee who is a member of a staff, especially in a government, political, or journalistic organization

Examples

A staffer at newspaper
As a staffer at the newspaper, he often covered late-breaking stories.
The staffer handed the report to her manager before the meeting started.
White House staffers worked overnight to prepare the speech.

Related Terms

  • synonym: staff member - a person who is a member of staff
