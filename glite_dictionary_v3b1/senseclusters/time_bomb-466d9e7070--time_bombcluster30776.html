time bomb

noun
bomb set to detonate at a certain time
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪm bɑm / (AmE), / ˈtaɪm bɒm / (BrE)
a bomb equipped with a device that allows it to explode at a set time

Examples

A hidden time bomb
Police found a time bomb hidden in the warehouse.
Setting a time bomb requires careful planning to ensure it detonates at the right moment.
The discovery of a time bomb forced the evacuation of the building.
