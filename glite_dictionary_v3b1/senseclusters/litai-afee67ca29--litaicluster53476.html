Litai

noun
former Lithuanian currency
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈliːtaɪ / (AmE), / ˈliːtaɪ / (BrE)
the plural form of litas, which was the official currency of Lithuania before the euro.

Examples

Pay with Litai
Before the euro was introduced, people in Lithuania paid for goods with Litai.
He found some old Litai in a drawer while cleaning his house.
Tourists often exchanged their euros for Litai when visiting Lithuania before 2015.

Related Terms

  • synonym: litas - the former basic monetary unit of Lithuania
