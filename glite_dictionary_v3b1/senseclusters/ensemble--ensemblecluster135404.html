ensemble

noun
combination of predictive models
6.7
Pronunciation: /ən.ˈsɑːm.bəl/ /; / /ɑn.ˈsɑːm.bəl/ (AmE), /ɒn.ˈsɒm.bəl/ /; / /ə.ˈsɒm.bəl/ (BrE)
(in machine learning and statistics) a method or set of predictive models combined so their outputs are aggregated to improve accuracy, robustness, or generalization.

Examples

Ensemble method
The data scientist used an ensemble of models to boost accuracy on the test set.
Many competitions are won by ensembles that combine neural networks and decision trees.
To reduce overfitting, they trained different algorithms and formed an ensemble for predictions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: model averaging - combining predictions from several models to produce a single prediction.
