flush-seamed

adjective
laid edge to edge, not overlapping
9.7
Pronunciation: / flʌʃ simd / (AmE), / flʌʃ siːmd / (BrE)
(of materials) laid edge to edge so that the surfaces are even and do not overlap.

Examples

Flush-seamed metal roofing
For a sleek appearance, choose flush-seamed metal roofing.
The countertop is flush-seamed with the adjoining surfaces to prevent dirt buildup.
The panels were flush-seamed to create a smooth and uniform wall.
