coffee tree

noun
tree or shrub yielding coffee beans
3.9 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑː.fiː triː/ /; / /ˈkɔː.fiː triː/ (AmE), /ˈkɒf.ɪ triː/ /; / /ˈkɔːf.ɪ triː/ (BrE)
