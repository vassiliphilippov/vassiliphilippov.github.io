Silybum marianum

noun
species of thistle (milk thistle)
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪlɪbəm məˈriːənəm / (AmE), / ˈsɪlɪbəm məˈrɪənəm / (BrE)
a tall biennial thistle native to the Mediterranean, with large white-blotched leaves and purple flower heads, commonly known as milk thistle
