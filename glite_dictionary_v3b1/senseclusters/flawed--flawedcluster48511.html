flawed

adjective
having a defect or imperfection
1.0
Pronunciation: / flɔd / (AmE), / flɔːd / (BrE)
Having a defect or imperfection; not without fault.

Examples

A flawed gem
Even a flawed gem can still be valuable.
She realized her plan was flawed and needed significant changes.
The flawed design led to the product's early failure.
