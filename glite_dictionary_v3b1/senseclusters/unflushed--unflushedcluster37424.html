unflushed

adjective
pale or not rosy
6.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈflʌʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈflʌʃt / (BrE)
not having a reddish or rosy color, especially referring to a face that appears pale or lacking warmth.

Examples

Unflushed cheeks
After the long illness she returned home looking tired and unflushed in the cold light.
His cheeks remained unflushed even after running up the stairs.
The child's face was unflushed, showing no sign of excitement or embarrassment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flushed - having a reddish or rosy color in the face
  • synonym: pale - having little color in the face
