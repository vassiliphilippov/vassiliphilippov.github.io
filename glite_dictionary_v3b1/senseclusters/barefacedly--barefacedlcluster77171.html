barefacedly

adverb
without shame or concealment; openly
4.4 7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbeər.feɪs.liː/ (AmE), /ˈbeə.feɪs.liː/ (BrE)
In a way that shows no shame, embarrassment, or concealment; openly and boldly.

Examples

To barefacedly lie
He barefacedly took credit for work he had not done.
She barefacedly lied to the entire board without blinking an eye.
The politician barefacedly denied all accusations despite overwhelming evidence.
