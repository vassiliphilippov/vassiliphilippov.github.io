nail

verb
strike or hit with force
Pronunciation: / neɪl / (AmE), / neɪl / (BrE)
to hit or strike with great force, especially in sports or physical activity

Examples

To nail a shot
He nailed the baseball out of the park on his first try.
She nailed the landing after her difficult gymnastics routine.
The boxer nailed his opponent with a powerful right hook.

Related Terms

  • synonym: smash - to hit or break something with great force
  • synonym: whack - to strike something or someone with a sharp blow
