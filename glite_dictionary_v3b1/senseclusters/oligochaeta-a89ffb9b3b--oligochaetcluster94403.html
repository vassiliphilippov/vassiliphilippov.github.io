Oligochaeta

noun
class of annelid worms, including earthworms
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊlɪɡoʊˈkiːtə /; / ˌɑːlɪɡoʊˈkiːtə / (AmE), / ˌɒlɪɡəʊˈkiːtə / (BrE)
a class of segmented worms (including earthworms and their relatives) that have few bristles (chaetae) on each segment
