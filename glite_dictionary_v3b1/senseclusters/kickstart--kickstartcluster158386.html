kickstart

verb
to start by foot lever
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪkˌstɑrt / (AmE), / ˈkɪkˌstɑːt / (BrE)
to start an engine or vehicle by using a foot-powered lever, especially on a motorcycle.

Examples

To kickstart a motorcycle
He had to kickstart the old motorcycle after the battery died on the trip.
It took several tries to kickstart the engine on that chilly morning.
She learned how to kickstart her scooter when the electric starter failed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stall - to stop running, especially of an engine
  • synonym: start - to begin the operation of a machine or engine
