string quintet

noun
ensemble of five stringed-instrument players
8.4
Pronunciation: / strɪŋ kwɪnˈtɛt / (AmE), / strɪŋ kwɪnˈtɛt / (BrE)
A group of five musicians who play stringed instruments, especially as a performing ensemble.

Examples

A string quintet group
Several composers have written famous works specifically for string quintet.
She joined a string quintet that rehearses every weekend for local events.
The string quintet performed a beautiful arrangement at the concert hall last night.
