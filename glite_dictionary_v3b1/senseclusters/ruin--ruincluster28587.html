ruin

verb
fall into decay or destruction
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈruː.ən / (AmE), / ˈruː.ɪn / (BrE)
to fall into a state of decay or destruction; to become ruined or dilapidated

Examples

Old buildings ruin
After decades of neglect, the castle began to ruin amidst the surrounding forest.
Many old mansions ruin when there is no one left to care for them.
The factory will ruin quickly if repairs are not made soon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collapse - to fall down or give way suddenly
  • synonym: decay - to rot or decompose over time
