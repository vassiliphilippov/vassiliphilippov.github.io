pianissimo assai

adjective
very, very soft (music)
9.9
Pronunciation: / pi.əˈnɪs.ɪ.moʊ əˈsaɪ / (AmE), / piˌanɪˈsɪməʊ əˈsaɪ / (BrE)
(in music) to be played or sung very softly; very, very soft.

Examples

Play pianissimo assai
In the second movement, the strings enter pianissimo assai, barely audible to the audience.
The composer marked the choir part as pianissimo assai for a delicate effect.
The opening passage should be played pianissimo assai to create a sense of mystery.
