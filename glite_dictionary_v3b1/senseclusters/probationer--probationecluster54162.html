probationer

noun
person on probation or parole
Pronunciation: / proʊˈbeɪʃənər / (AmE), / prəˈbeɪʃənə / (BrE)
a person who has been released from custody on probation or parole and is under supervision instead of serving time in prison

Examples

Probationer under supervision
If a probationer violates the terms, they may be sent back to prison.
Several probationers attended a rehabilitation program as part of their release conditions.
The probationer must report weekly to their probation officer.
