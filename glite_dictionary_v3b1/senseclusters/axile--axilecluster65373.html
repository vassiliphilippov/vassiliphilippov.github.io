axile

adjective
relating to or attached to an axis
Pronunciation: / ˈæk.saɪl / (AmE), / ˈæk.saɪl / (BrE)
relating to or situated on or around an axis; attached to or occurring along the axis.

Examples

Axile rotation
Certain mechanical parts are designed to allow axile rotation.
The axile arrangement of seeds is common in tomato plants.
The ovules are axile in position within the ovary.

Related Terms

  • synonym: axial - relating to or forming an axis
