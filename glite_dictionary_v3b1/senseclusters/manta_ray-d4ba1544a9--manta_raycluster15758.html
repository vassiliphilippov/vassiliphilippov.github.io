manta ray

noun
large tropical sea ray
5.8 3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmæntə ˌreɪ / (AmE), / ˈmantə ˌreɪ / (BrE)
a very large tropical sea ray with triangular pectoral fins, feeding mainly on plankton, generally harmless to humans
