elastance unit

noun
unit measuring elastance (reciprocal of capacitance)
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlæstəns ˈjuːnɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈlæst(ə)ns ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
a unit of measurement for elastance, defined as the reciprocal of capacitance

Examples

An elastance unit measure
He used an elastance unit to describe the sensitivity of the electrical circuit.
Measurement of electrical systems sometimes requires expressing results in elastance units rather than capacitance.
The reciprocal of one farad is the elastance unit, commonly called the daraf.
