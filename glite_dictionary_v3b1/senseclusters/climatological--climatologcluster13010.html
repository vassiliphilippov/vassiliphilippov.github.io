climatological

adjective
related to climate study
Pronunciation: / ˌklaɪmətəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌklaɪmətəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the scientific study of climate and long-term weather patterns

Examples

A climatological report
The climatological report showed a steady increase in average temperatures over the past century.
Scientists use climatological models to predict future changes in global weather patterns.
Many universities offer courses in climatological research and data analysis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meteorological - relating to the science of weather and atmosphere
