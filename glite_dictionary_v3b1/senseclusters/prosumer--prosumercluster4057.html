prosumer

adjective
advanced, semi-professional
Pronunciation: / proʊˈsuːmər / (AmE), / prəˈsjuːmə / (BrE)
designed for advanced users, with features between those of consumer and professional products

Examples

Prosumer camera features
The new prosumer laptop offers more power than basic models but is easier to use than professional ones.
Many photographers choose prosumer cameras for their balance of features and price.
This prosumer software includes advanced editing tools not found in standard versions.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: consumer - intended for general users or the public
  • degree higher: professional - intended for experts or specialists
