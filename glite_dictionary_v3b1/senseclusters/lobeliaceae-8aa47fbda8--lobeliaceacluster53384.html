Lobeliaceae

noun
family of flowering plants
Pronunciation: / loʊˌbiːliˈeɪsiˌiː / (AmE), / ləʊˌbiːlɪˈeɪsɪiː / (BrE)
A family of flowering plants, sometimes included in the family Campanulaceae in certain classification systems.

Examples

Lobeliaceae plant family
Botanists sometimes debate whether to place Lobeliaceae within the Campanulaceae family.
Many species in the Lobeliaceae family are known for their brightly colored flowers.
Some garden plants belong to the Lobeliaceae, attracting hummingbirds and other pollinators.
