irreligiosity

noun
lack of religious belief
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrɪˌlɪdʒiˈɑːsəti / (AmE), / ˌɪrɪˌlɪdʒiˈɒsəti / (BrE)
the state or quality of not having religious beliefs, feelings, or practices, or being indifferent or opposed to religion

Examples

Open irreligiosity
His outspoken irreligiosity often sparked debates with his devout family members.
The rise of irreligiosity in the country has led to changes in public policy.

His irreligiosity was evident in his secular approach to life.
The rise of irreligiosity among young people has been noted in recent surveys.
She spoke openly about her irreligiosity and preference for scientific explanations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atheism - the belief that there is no god or gods
  • antonym: religiosity - strong religious feeling or belief
  • synonym: secularism - the principle of separating religion from civic affairs
