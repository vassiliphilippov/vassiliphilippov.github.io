put our differences behind us

verb
to reconcile and move on from disagreements
1.0
Pronunciation: / pʊt aʊɚ ˈdɪfɚənsɪz bɪˈhaɪnd ʌs / (AmE), / pʊt ˈaʊə ˈdɪfərənsɪz bɪˈhaɪnd ʌs / (BrE)
to stop arguing or disagreeing and reconcile, choosing to move forward without dwelling on past conflicts

Examples

To put our differences behind us
After years of rivalry, the two leaders agreed to put our differences behind us for the sake of peace.
Let’s put our differences behind us and work together on this project.
They decided it was best to put our differences behind us and focus on the future.
