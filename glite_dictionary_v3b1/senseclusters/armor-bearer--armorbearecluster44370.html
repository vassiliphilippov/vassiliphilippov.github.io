armor-bearer

noun
person carrying armor for a knight
5.8 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrmərˌbɛrər / (AmE), / ˈɑːməˌbɛərə / (BrE)
a person, especially a squire, who carried the armor or weapons for a knight or warrior

Examples

An armor-bearer carries weapons
As an armor-bearer, he was responsible for maintaining the weapons before every duel.
In medieval stories, the loyal armor-bearer often risked his life for his master.
The knight’s armor-bearer followed him closely onto the battlefield.
