junior

adjective
for young people or youths
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuːniər / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːniə / (BrE)
Designed for or suitable for young people, especially children or teenagers.

Examples

Junior clothing section
Many clothing stores carry a junior section for teenagers.
She joined a junior orchestra when she was ten years old.
The camp offers a special junior program for children aged 8 to 12.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adult - designed for grown persons
