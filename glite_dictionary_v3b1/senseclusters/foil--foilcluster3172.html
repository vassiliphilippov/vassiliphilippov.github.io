foil

verb
prevent or frustrate plans or efforts
Pronunciation: / fɔɪl / (AmE), / fɔɪl / (BrE)
to prevent the success of (someone's plans or efforts); to frustrate or thwart

Examples

To foil a plan
A sudden rainstorm foiled our plans for a picnic in the park.
Her attempts to cheat on the exam were foiled by the vigilant teacher.
The police managed to foil the robbery at the last minute.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frustrate - to prevent from achieving a goal or desire
  • synonym: thwart - to prevent someone from accomplishing something
