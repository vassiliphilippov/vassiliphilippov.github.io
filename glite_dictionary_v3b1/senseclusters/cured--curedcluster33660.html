cured

adjective
preserved by salting, drying, or brining
Pronunciation: / kjʊrd / (AmE), / kjʊəd / (BrE)
(of food, especially meat or fish) preserved by salting, drying, smoking, or brining.

Examples

Cured meat slices
Many traditional sausages are cured with a special blend of spices and salt.
She prefers cured salmon on her bagel rather than fresh fish.
The deli offers a wide selection of cured meats, including ham and salami.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fresh - not preserved by salting, drying, smoking, or brining
