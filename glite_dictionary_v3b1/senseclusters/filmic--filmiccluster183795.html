filmic

adjective
like a movie
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪlmɪk / (AmE), / ˈfɪlmɪk / (BrE)
having qualities, style, or appearance similar to a film or cinema

Examples

Filmic style
The documentary had a highly filmic look, making it feel more like a feature film.
Her use of color and composition gave the photographs a distinctly filmic quality.
The novel's vivid descriptions created a filmic experience in the reader's mind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cinematic - having qualities characteristic of movies or filmmaking
