Quercus montana

noun
oak tree species of eastern USA
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɜrkəs mɒnˈteɪnə / (AmE), / ˈkwɜːkəs mɒnˈteɪnə / (BrE)
A species of oak tree native to the eastern United States, valued for its durable wood used in timber, baskets, and chair seats.
