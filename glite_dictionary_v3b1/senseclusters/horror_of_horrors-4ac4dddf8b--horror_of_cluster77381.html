horror of horrors

noun
worst possible event; utmost distress
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrər əv ˈhɔrərz / (AmE), / ˈhɒr.ər əv ˈhɒr.əz / (BrE)
used to express that something is the worst possible thing or the most distressing event imaginable

Examples

Horror of horrors moment
She discovered, horror of horrors, that she had missed her final exam.
They arrived at the airport only to find, horror of horrors, their flight had been cancelled.
When his computer crashed before the deadline, it was truly the horror of horrors.
