edgeways

adverb
with the edge facing forward or toward the edge
Pronunciation: / ˈɛdʒˌweɪz / (AmE), / ˈɛdʒˌweɪz / (BrE)
with the edge facing forward, or on, by, or toward the edge.

Examples

Slide it edgeways
She managed to slide the book edgeways into the narrow gap on the shelf.
The coin fell edgeways onto the floor and rolled under the table.
When packing the glass panels, ensure they are placed edgeways rather than flat.
