chafer

noun
garden beetle
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪfər / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪfə / (BrE)
a type of beetle whose larvae feed on plant roots, often found in gardens and lawns

Examples

Garden chafer
The gardener discovered a chafer while digging up the flower bed.
Many lawns are damaged each year by chafer larvae eating the grass roots.
A chafer beetle landed on the window during the warm summer evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grub - the larval stage of a beetle
