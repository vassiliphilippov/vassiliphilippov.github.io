roundtop

noun
rounded-top structure
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊndˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈraʊndˌtɒp / (BrE)
a structure or object with a rounded top, especially a building, roof, or lid with a curved upper surface

Examples

Roundtop barn roof
The old barn was easily recognized by its large roundtop roof.
They stored the grain in a roundtop silo at the edge of the field.
The antique chest had a roundtop lid that opened smoothly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arched roof - a roof with a curved or arched shape
  • antonym: flat roof - a roof with a flat upper surface
