family Pelobatidae

noun
family of spadefoot toads (amphibians)
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli ˌpɛloʊˈbætɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ˌpɛləʊˈbatɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of amphibians commonly known as spadefoot toads, characterized by their burrowing habits and distinctive spade-like projections on their hind feet
