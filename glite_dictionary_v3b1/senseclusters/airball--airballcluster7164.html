airball

noun
missed basketball shot
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈeəˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a basketball shot that completely misses both the hoop and the backboard

Examples

An airball shot
The player shot an airball in the final seconds, disappointing his team.
Whenever someone throws an airball, the crowd often teases them loudly.
He was embarrassed after his three-point attempt turned into a clear airball.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brick - a badly missed basketball shot, often hitting the rim or backboard hard
  • antonym: swish - a basketball shot that goes through the hoop without touching the rim or backboard
