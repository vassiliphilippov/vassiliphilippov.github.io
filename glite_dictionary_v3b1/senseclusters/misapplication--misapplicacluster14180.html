misapplication

noun
incorrect or inappropriate use
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of using something in an incorrect or inappropriate way

Examples

Misapplication of a rule
Her theory failed due to the misapplication of a key principle.
The misapplication of the drug can lead to serious health risks.
There was a misapplication of funds in the project, which caused an audit.
