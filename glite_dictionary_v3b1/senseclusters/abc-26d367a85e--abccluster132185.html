ABC

noun
the alphabet
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪˌbiːˈsiː / (AmE), / ˌeɪˌbiːˈsiː / (BrE)
the alphabet, especially the basic sequence of letters in a language, often used to refer to the fundamentals of reading or writing
