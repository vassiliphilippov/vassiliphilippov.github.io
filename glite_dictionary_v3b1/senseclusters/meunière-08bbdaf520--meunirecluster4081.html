meunière

adjective
floured and fried in butter
8.9
Pronunciation: / mɜːnˈjɛr / (AmE), / mɜːnˈjɛə / (BrE)
prepared by dredging in flour and frying in butter, especially referring to fish dishes

Examples

Meuniere fish fillet
The chef recommended the trout meunière for its delicate flavor and crispy texture.
She learned to prepare fish meunière during her cooking class in Paris.
At the restaurant, the sole meunière was served with a lemon butter sauce.
