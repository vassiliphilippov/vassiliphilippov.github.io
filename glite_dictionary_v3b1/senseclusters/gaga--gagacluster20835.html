gaga

adjective
extremely enthusiastic or infatuated
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑː.ɡə / (AmE), / ˈɡɑː.ɡə / (BrE)
extremely enthusiastic, excited, or infatuated with someone or something; preoccupied or obsessed.

Examples

Gaga over a star
Ever since she met him, she's been gaga over him.
He's absolutely gaga about vintage cars.
The crowd went gaga when the band took the stage.
