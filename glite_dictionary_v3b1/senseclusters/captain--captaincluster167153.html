captain

noun
leader of a group or team
Pronunciation: / ˈkæp.tɪn / (AmE), / ˈkæp.tɪn / (BrE)
A person who is the leader or most important member of a group, organization, or team.

Examples

Captain of the team
As captain of the team, she inspired everyone to do their best.
During the meeting, Tom naturally assumed the role of captain and guided the discussion.
He was considered a captain of industry for his innovative leadership.

Related Terms

  • antonym: follower - a person who supports or is guided by another person
  • synonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group, organization, or country
