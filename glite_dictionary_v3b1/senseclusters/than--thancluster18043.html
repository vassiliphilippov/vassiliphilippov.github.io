than

preposition
comparison preposition
1.0
Pronunciation: / ðæn / (AmE), / ðæn / (BrE)
used to introduce the second element in a comparison, following adjectives, adverbs, or verbs

Examples

Taller than me
She is taller than her brother by several inches.
It costs less than I expected to repair the car.
He would rather walk than take the crowded bus.

Related Terms

  • antonym: as - used in comparisons to show equality
