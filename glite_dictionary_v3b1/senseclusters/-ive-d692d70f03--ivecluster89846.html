-ive

x
adjective-forming suffix
Pronunciation: / ɪv / (AmE), / ɪv / (BrE)
a suffix used to form adjectives from verbs, meaning 'tending to', 'having the quality of', or 'causing' a particular effect

Examples

-ive in 'active'
The word 'active' uses the suffix -ive to show someone who does things.
In 'creative', the suffix -ive means having the ability to create.
Adding -ive to 'attract' forms 'attractive', meaning able to attract.
