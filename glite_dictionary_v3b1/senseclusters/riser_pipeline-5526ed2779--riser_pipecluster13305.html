riser pipeline

noun
vertical pipe in a building
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪzɚ ˈpaɪplaɪn / (AmE), / ˈraɪzə ˈpaɪplaɪn / (BrE)
a vertical pipe in a building that carries fluids, such as water or gas, to different floors

Examples

The riser pipeline leak
A leak in the riser pipeline can affect water pressure throughout the entire building.
During construction, the crew installed a new riser pipeline for the building's heating system.
The fire suppression system relies on a riser pipeline to deliver water to each floor.
