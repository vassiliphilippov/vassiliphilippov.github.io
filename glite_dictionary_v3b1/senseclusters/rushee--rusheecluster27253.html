rushee

noun
fraternity candidate
Pronunciation: / rʌˈʃi / (AmE), / rʌˈʃi / (BrE)
a person who is being considered for membership in a fraternity or sorority, especially during recruitment events

Examples

A rushee at recruitment
Each rushee attended several events during Greek Week.
The fraternity interviewed every rushee before making their final selections.
As a rushee, she was excited to meet members of different sororities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: member - a person who belongs to a fraternity or sorority
  • synonym: pledge - a person who has accepted an offer to join a fraternity or sorority but is not yet a full member
