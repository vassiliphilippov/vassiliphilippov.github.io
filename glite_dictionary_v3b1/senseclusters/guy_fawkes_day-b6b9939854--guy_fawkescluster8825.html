Guy Fawkes Day

noun
British festival on 5 November
4.9 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / …°a…™ f…îks de…™ / (AmE), / …°a…™ Ààf…îÀêks de…™ / (BrE)
A British festival held on 5 November to commemorate the foiling of the Gunpowder Plot of 1605, traditionally marked by fireworks and the burning of effigies of Guy Fawkes.

Examples

Guy Fawkes Day fireworks
Every year, children look forward to Guy Fawkes Day with fireworks and bonfires.
On Guy Fawkes Day, people across Britain remember the foiled attempt to blow up Parliament.
Our town hosts a huge celebration for Guy Fawkes Day every November.

Synonyms

  • Guy Fawkes Night
    6.5 0.02/m
    Guy Fawkes Night is the usual name for the November 5 celebrations, while Guy Fawkes Day is the formal festival name.
  • Bonfire Night
    6.7 0.01/m
    Bonfire Night focuses on bonfires and fireworks in local celebrations, while Guy Fawkes Day names the historic festival remembering the plot.
  • November 5
    4.4 0.03/m
    November 5 refers simply to the date of the celebrations, while Guy Fawkes Day is the festival's traditional name.
