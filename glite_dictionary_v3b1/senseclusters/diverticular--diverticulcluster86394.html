diverticular

adjective
of a diverticulum
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪvərˈtɪkjələr / (AmE), / ˌdɪvəˈtɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or affected by a diverticulum, which is a pouch in the wall of a hollow organ

Examples

Diverticular disease
The doctor explained that a diverticular condition can cause abdominal pain and discomfort.
A high-fiber diet is often recommended for patients with diverticular disease.
The scan revealed several diverticular pouches in the colon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diverticulitis - inflammation of a diverticulum
