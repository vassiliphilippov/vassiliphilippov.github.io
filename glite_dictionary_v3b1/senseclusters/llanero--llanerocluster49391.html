llanero

adjective
of the Llanos region
Pronunciation: / jɑˈnɛɾoʊ / (AmE), / jɑˈnɛərəʊ / (BrE)
relating to the Llanos region of South America or its people, culture, or traditions

Examples

Llanero culture
The llanero music at the festival featured traditional instruments from the plains.
She wore a colorful dress inspired by llanero culture during the celebration.
Many llanero customs are still practiced in rural areas of Venezuela and Colombia.

  • synonym: plains - large areas of flat land
