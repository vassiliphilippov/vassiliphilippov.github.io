antisolar

noun
point opposite sun
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈsoʊlɚ / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈsəʊlə / (BrE)
the point on the celestial sphere that is directly opposite the sun from the observer's perspective

Examples

Antisolar point
The rainbow appeared exactly at the antisolar point in the sky after the rain stopped.
Astronomers use the antisolar direction to study certain atmospheric optical phenomena.
When facing away from the sun, your shadow points toward the antisolar position.

Related Terms

  • antonym: subsolar - the point on the celestial sphere directly beneath the sun
