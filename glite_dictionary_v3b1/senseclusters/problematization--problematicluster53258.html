problematization

noun
making an issue a problem
Pronunciation: / ˌprɑːbləmətaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌprɒbləmətaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of identifying or presenting something as a problem for analysis or discussion

Examples

The problematization of issues
The problematization of climate change has led to new research and policy discussions.
Her essay focused on the problematization of traditional gender roles in society.
Academic debates often involve the problematization of everyday concepts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: analysis - detailed examination of something
  • antonym: solution - the act of solving a problem
