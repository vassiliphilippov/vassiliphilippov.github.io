(in computing) to cause a computer system, network, website, or online service to stop working or become inaccessible, often by overloading, attacking, or exploiting it.

Examples

To bring down a website
A distributed attack managed to bring down the company's online store during the holiday sale.
Planned maintenance will bring down the servers for about two hours tonight.
A flaw in the update brought down the email service for thousands of customers yesterday.
