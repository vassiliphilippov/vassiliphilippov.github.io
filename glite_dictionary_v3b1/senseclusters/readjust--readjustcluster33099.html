readjust

verb
adjust again; make new adjustments
Pronunciation: / ˌriː.əˈdʒʌst /; / ˌriˈəˌdʒʌst /; / ˌriːəˈdʒʌst / (AmE), / ˌriː.əˈdʒʌst /; / ˌriːˈəˌdʒʌst /; / ˌriːəˈdʒʌst / (BrE)
