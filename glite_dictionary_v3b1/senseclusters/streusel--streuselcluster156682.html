streusel

noun
crumbly pastry topping
6.2 6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈstruːzəl / (AmE), / ˈʃtruːzəl /; / ˈstruːzəl / (BrE)
a crumbly mixture of flour, butter, and sugar used as a topping or filling for cakes, muffins, and pastries
