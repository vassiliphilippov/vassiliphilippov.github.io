outswing

noun
cricket ball curving away
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌswɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌswɪŋ / (BrE)
a ball in cricket that curves away from the batter after being bowled

Examples

Cricket outswing
The bowler's outswing made it difficult for the batter to reach the ball.
He practiced delivering outswing to improve his performance in the match.
A well-executed outswing can surprise even experienced batters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inswing - a ball in cricket that curves toward the batter after being bowled
