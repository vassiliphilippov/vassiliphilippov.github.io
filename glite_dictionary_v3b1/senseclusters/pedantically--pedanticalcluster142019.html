pedantically

adverb
in an excessively academic or precise way
Pronunciation: / pəˈdæn.tɪ.kli / (AmE), / pɪˈdæn.tɪ.kli / (BrE)
In a way that shows an excessive concern with minor details or rules, especially in learning or academic matters.

Examples

To speak pedantically
He corrected my grammar pedantically during the entire meeting.
She explained the procedure so pedantically that everyone lost interest.
The instructions are written pedantically, making them hard to understand.
