master race

noun
race deemed superior to others
2.7 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmæstɚ reɪs / (AmE), / ˈmɑːstə reɪs / (BrE)
a race that regards itself as superior to all others and destined to rule over them, especially as promoted in racist ideologies

Examples

The ideology of the master race led to widespread discrimination and violence.
The propaganda promoted the notion of a master race ruling over other peoples.
