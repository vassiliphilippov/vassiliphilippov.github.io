Customs Service

proper noun
government agency handling import tariffs
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌstəmz ˈsɝːvɪs / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəmz ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
the government agency responsible for enforcing import tariffs and regulations on goods entering a country

Examples

Customs Service officer
After years of working for the Customs Service, she became an expert in international regulations.
Many employees of the Customs Service are trained to detect illegal imports.
The Customs Service intercepted a large shipment of counterfeit goods at the port last week.
