fish and chips

noun
fried fish and French-fried potatoes dish
Pronunciation: / fɪʃ ənd tʃɪps / (AmE), / fɪʃ ən tʃɪps / (BrE)
a hot dish of fried fish and fried potatoes (fries), usually served as a takeaway meal, especially in Britain

Examples

Hot fish and chips

She ordered fish and chips with extra vinegar and tartar sauce.
We decided to have fish and chips for dinner at the local seaside shop.
