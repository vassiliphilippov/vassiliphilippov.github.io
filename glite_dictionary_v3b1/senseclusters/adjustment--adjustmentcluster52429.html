adjustment

noun
change to an amount based on circumstances
4.4
Pronunciation: / əˈdʒʌstmənt / (AmE), / əˈdʒʌstmənt / (BrE)
an amount added or deducted from a total to account for qualifying circumstances or factors

Examples

Adjustment for cost
Insurance claims may include an adjustment for depreciation.
The company made an adjustment to the invoice to reflect the returned products.
Your monthly statement will show an adjustment if you qualify for the discount.

Related Terms

  • synonym: allowance - an amount of money or other sum deducted or added for a particular purpose
