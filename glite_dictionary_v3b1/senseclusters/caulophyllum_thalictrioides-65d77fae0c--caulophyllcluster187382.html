Caulophyllum thalictrioides

noun
a herb with blue berries, used medicinally
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɔl.əˈfɪl.əm ˌθæl.ɪkˌtraɪ.oʊˈɪd.iːz / (AmE), / kɔː.lɒˈfɪl.əm ˌθæl.ɪkˌtrɪ.ɔɪˈdiːz / (BrE)
A tall herb native to eastern North America and Asia, with blue, berrylike fruit and a thick knotty rootstock, formerly used medicinally.
