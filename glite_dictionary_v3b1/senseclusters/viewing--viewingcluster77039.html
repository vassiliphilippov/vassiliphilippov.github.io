viewing

noun
funeral vigil to see the deceased

A gathering or vigil held for mourners to see and pay respects to a deceased person's body before the funeral or burial.

Examples

Funeral viewing
Many people attended the viewing to pay their final respects to the deceased.
The viewing was held at the funeral home and lasted for several hours.
The family held a viewing the night before the funeral to allow friends to say goodbye.

Synonyms
