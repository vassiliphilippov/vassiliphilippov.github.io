confed

verb
to join as allies
Pronunciation: / kənˈfɛd / (AmE), / kənˈfɛd / (BrE)
to join together as confederates or to form a confederation, especially in an informal or temporary way

Examples

To confed for strength
The smaller groups decided to confed in order to have a stronger voice in negotiations.
During the meeting, several organizations chose to confed for mutual benefit.
If we confed with our neighbors, we can achieve more than working alone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ally - to join with others for a common purpose
  • antonym: separate - to move apart or stop being joined
  • synonym: unite - to come together to form a single group
