Jawaharlal Nehru

proper noun
first Prime Minister of India
7.6
Pronunciation: / dʒɑːwɑːhɑːrˈlɑːl ˈneɪruː / (AmE), / dʒɑːwəˌhɑːlˈlɑːl ˈneɪruː / (BrE)
(1889–1964) Indian statesman and the first Prime Minister of India, serving from 1947 to 1964; a central figure in India's struggle for independence and in shaping the modern Indian nation.

Examples

Jawaharlal Nehru first Prime Minister
