excipient

noun
inactive drug ingredient
8.4
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsɪpiənt / (AmE), / ɪkˈsɪpiənt / (BrE)
a substance added to a medicine or product to give it the right consistency, stability, or to help deliver the active ingredient
