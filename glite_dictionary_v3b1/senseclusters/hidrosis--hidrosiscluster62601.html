hidrosis

noun
sweat secretion process
9.9 8.9
Pronunciation: /haɪ.ˈdroʊ.sɪs/ /; / /hɪ.ˈdroʊ.sɪs/ (AmE), /haɪ.ˈdrəʊ.sɪs/ /; / /hɪ.ˈdrəʊ.sɪs/ (BrE)
The process by which the sweat glands produce and secrete sweat to the surface of the skin.

Examples

Excessive hidrosis can be a sign of an underlying medical condition.
