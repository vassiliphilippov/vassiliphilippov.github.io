hidrosis

noun
sweat secretion process
Pronunciation: / haɪˈdroʊsɪs / (AmE), / hɪˈdrəʊsɪs / (BrE)
The process by which the sweat glands produce and secrete sweat to the surface of the skin.

Examples

Hidrosis symptoms
Doctors may prescribe medication to control severe hidrosis.
Excessive hidrosis can be a sign of an underlying medical condition.
Some individuals suffer from night hidrosis, leading to disturbed sleep.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perspiration - the process by which sweat is discharged through the pores of the skin
