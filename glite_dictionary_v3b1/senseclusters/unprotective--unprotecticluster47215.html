unprotective

adjective
not providing protection
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.prəˈtɛk.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.prəˈtɛk.tɪv / (BrE)
not providing protection; failing to guard or shield from harm or danger.

Examples

Completely unprotective sunglasses
Many sunglasses are stylish but completely unprotective against UV rays.
She criticized her boss for being unprotective in the face of harassment.
The fabric proved to be unprotective against both cold and rain.
