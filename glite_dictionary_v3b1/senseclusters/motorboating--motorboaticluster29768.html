motorboating

noun
using a motorboat
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtərˌboʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈməʊtəbəʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of traveling in or using a motorboat for transport or recreation

Examples

Motorboating on the lake
They spent the afternoon motorboating along the lake, stopping to swim at quiet coves.
Many tourists enjoy motorboating on the river during the summer months.
He learned the basics of motorboating before renting a boat for the weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boating - the activity of traveling by boat for pleasure
  • antonym: sailing - the activity of traveling in a boat using sails
