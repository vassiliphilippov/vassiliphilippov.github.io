Great Bear

proper noun
a northern constellation, Ursa Major
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɡreɪt ˈbɛr / (AmE), / ˌɡreɪt ˈbɛə / (BrE)
a large constellation in the northern sky, also known as Ursa Major, which contains the star formation called the Big Dipper.
