bonsoir

x
French evening greeting
Pronunciation: / bɔ̃swaʁ / (AmE), / bɔ̃swaʁ / (BrE)
a French greeting used in the evening to say 'good evening'

Examples

A warm bonsoir
He greeted the guests with a cheerful bonsoir at the party.
As she entered the restaurant, she smiled and said bonsoir to the staff.
The host welcomed everyone with a warm bonsoir before the dinner began.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bonjour - a French greeting meaning 'good day'
  • synonym: good evening - a polite greeting used in the evening
