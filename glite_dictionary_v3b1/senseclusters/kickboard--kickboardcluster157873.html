kickboard

noun
swimming training board
3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkɪk.bɔːrd/ /; / /ˈkɪk.boʊrd/ (AmE), /ˈkɪk.bɔːd/ (BrE)

Examples

Swimming kickboard

During warm-up, each swimmer grabbed a kickboard and started kicking laps across the pool.
