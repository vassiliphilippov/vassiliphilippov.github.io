hobby

noun
a rocking horse toy for children
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːbi / (AmE), / ˈhɒbi / (BrE)
a child's toy horse mounted on rockers, which a child straddles and pretends to ride

Examples

A child's hobby horse

The old wooden hobby was passed down from generation to generation in their family.
