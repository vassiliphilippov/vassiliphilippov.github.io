Filipino

noun
a person from the Philippines
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪləˈpinoʊ / (AmE), / ˌfɪlɪˈpiːnəʊ / (BrE)
a person who is a native or national of the Philippines

Examples

A Filipino person
My colleague is a proud Filipino who was born and raised in Manila.
She met several Filipinos during her vacation in Cebu.
The city held a festival celebrating Filipino culture last summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Philippine - relating to the Philippines or its people
  • antonym: foreigner - a person from another country
