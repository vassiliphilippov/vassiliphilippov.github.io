Neoplatonic

adjective
of Neoplatonism
Pronunciation: / ˌniːoʊpləˈtɑːnɪk / (AmE), / ˌniːəʊpləˈtɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to Neoplatonism, a philosophical system based on Plato's ideas and later spiritual interpretations

Examples

Neoplatonic philosophy
The philosopher's writings are deeply Neoplatonic in their emphasis on spiritual reality.
Many Renaissance artists were inspired by Neoplatonic ideas about beauty and the soul.
Her interpretation of the poem highlights its Neoplatonic themes of unity and transcendence.
