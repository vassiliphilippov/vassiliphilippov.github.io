Spanish gorse

noun
shrub with golden yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈspænɪʃ ɡɔrs / (AmE), / ˈspanɪʃ ɡɔːs / (BrE)
An erect shrub native to southwestern Europe, known for its racemes of golden yellow flowers.

Examples

Spanish gorse shrub
Gardeners often plant Spanish gorse as an ornamental shrub for its colorful blossoms.
The bright flowers of Spanish gorse brighten the hillsides each spring.
Unlike common gorse, Spanish gorse prefers slightly warmer climates.
