rim

verb
to roll around the edge of a hoop
7.3
Pronunciation: / rɪm / (AmE), / rɪm / (BrE)
(of a ball) to roll or bounce around the edge of a hoop, especially in basketball, often without going in.

Examples

The ball rims out
He sighed in disappointment as the shot rimmed out at the last second.
The ball rimmed the basket dramatically before finally dropping through.
The basketball rimmed the hoop but didn't fall in.
