disperser

noun
agent that spreads
6.4
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspɜrsər / (AmE), / dɪˈspɜːsə / (BrE)
a person, animal, thing, or device that spreads or scatters something over a wide area

Examples

Seed disperser
The wind acts as a natural disperser of pollen between flowers.
Birds are important dispersers of seeds in many ecosystems.
A fan can be used as a disperser to spread cool air throughout the room.
Wind is a natural disperser of seeds for many plant species.
Some animals act as dispersers by carrying seeds to new locations.
The machine functions as a disperser to evenly spread fertilizer across the field.

Related Terms

  • antonym: collector - a person or thing that gathers or brings things together
  • synonym: distributor - a person or thing that gives out or delivers something
