radio antenna

noun
device for receiving radio signals
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪdioʊ ænˈtɛnə / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiəʊ ænˈtenə / (BrE)
An aerial or device that receives radio waves and allows a radio receiver to pick up signals.

Examples

A broken radio antenna
She climbed onto the roof to repair the broken radio antenna after the storm.
The technician adjusted the radio antenna to improve the reception of the broadcast.
Without a proper radio antenna, the radio could not pick up any stations clearly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aerial - an electrical device for transmitting or receiving radio waves
