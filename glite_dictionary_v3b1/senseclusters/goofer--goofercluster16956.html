goofer

noun
folk magic practitioner
Pronunciation: / ˈɡuːfər / (AmE), / ˈɡuːfə / (BrE)
a person who practices folk magic or witchcraft, especially in the hoodoo tradition, often casting spells or curses

Examples

Hoodoo goofer
The villagers suspected a goofer was responsible for the mysterious illness spreading through the town.
Stories about a powerful goofer have been passed down for generations in the region.
She visited the goofer to seek protection from bad luck and evil spirits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conjurer - a person who performs magic or casts spells
  • synonym: witch - a person believed to have magical powers, especially in folklore
