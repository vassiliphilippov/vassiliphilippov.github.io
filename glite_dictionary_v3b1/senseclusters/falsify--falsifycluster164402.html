falsify

verb
to make false by altering or adding
9.9 7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔl.sɪ.faɪ /; / ˈfælsɪˌfaɪ /; / ˈfɔl.səˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːl.sɪ.faɪ /; / ˈfɔːlsɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to change or make up information, documents, or evidence in order to deceive people
