go

noun
an attempt or try
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ / (AmE), / ɡəʊ / (BrE)
an attempt or try at something; a period or turn during which someone is engaged in an activity

Examples

Have a go
Everyone should get a go on the trampoline.
Let me have a go at solving this puzzle.
She gave it another go before asking for help.
After an hour's go at the cash register, she was ready for a break.
Everyone gets a go at playing the game before the winner is decided.
It's your go now, so take over the controls.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attempt - an act of trying to do something
  • synonym: spell - a short period devoted to a particular activity
  • synonym: try - an attempt to do something
  • synonym: turn - an opportunity or period during which someone does something before passing control to another
