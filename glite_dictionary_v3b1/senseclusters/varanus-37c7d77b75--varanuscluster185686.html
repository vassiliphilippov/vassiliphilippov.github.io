Varanus

noun
genus of monitor lizards
Pronunciation: / vəˈrænəs /; / vəˈreɪnəs / (AmE), / vəˈrɑːnəs / (BrE)
a genus of large lizards known as monitor lizards, native to Africa, Asia, and Oceania, and the only living genus in the family Varanidae
