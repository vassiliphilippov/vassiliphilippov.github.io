interframe

noun
structural spacer
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntərˌfreɪm / (AmE), / ˈɪntəˌfreɪm / (BrE)
a structural member or spacer located between the frames of a machine, vehicle, or building to provide support or separation

Examples

Interframe support
The engineer inspected the interframe to ensure it was properly aligned with the main structure.
A damaged interframe can compromise the stability of the entire vehicle.
They installed a new interframe to separate the two main support beams.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: frame - a rigid structure that surrounds or supports something
  • synonym: spacer - a device used to keep parts apart or provide space
