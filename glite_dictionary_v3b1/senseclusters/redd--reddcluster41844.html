redd

noun
fish egg nest
9.9
Pronunciation: / rɛd / (AmE), / rɛd / (BrE)
a nest made in gravel by a female fish, especially a salmon or trout, to lay eggs

Examples

Salmon redd
The biologist carefully observed the salmon building a redd in the shallow stream.
Trout often return to the same river each year to create a redd for spawning.
Gravel beds are important habitats because many fish lay their eggs in a redd.
