must

noun
stale or mouldy smell or taste
9.1 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / mÊŒst / (AmE), / mÊŒst / (BrE)
An unpleasant smell or taste that is stale, old, or mouldy.

Examples

Musty smell
After years in the attic, the books gave off a strong must when opened.
He wrinkled his nose at the must of old bread left in the back of the cupboard.
The basement had an unmistakable must that lingered no matter how much she cleaned.

Synonyms

  • mustiness
    7.2 0.08/m
    mustiness is the damp, stale smell found in old rooms and clothes, while must is the word for that mouldy, stale smell.
  • fustiness
    8.6 0.00/m
    fustiness is a stale, slightly dusty smell often in old houses or clothes, while must is the common name for a mouldy smell.
  • moldiness
    6.8 0.01/m
    moldiness means the smell or taste of actual mold on food or walls, while must refers to a general stale, mouldy smell.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mustiness - the state of being musty; the quality of smelling or tasting old or stale
