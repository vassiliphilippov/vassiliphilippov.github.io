lithograph

noun
a print made by lithography
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪθəˌɡræf /; / ˈlɪθəˌgræf / (AmE), / ˈlɪθəɡrɑːf /; / ˈlɪθəgrɑːf / (BrE)
a print made by the lithography process (drawing on a flat surface and printing from it)
