quell the insurrection

verb
suppress a rebellion or uprising
8.5
Pronunciation: / kwɛl ði ˌɪnsəˈrɛkʃən / (AmE), / kwɛl ði ˌɪnsəˈrɛkʃən / (BrE)
to suppress or put an end to a rebellion or uprising, usually by force

Examples

To quell the insurrection quickly
He was praised for his efforts to quell the insurrection before it grew out of control.
Leaders feared that swift action was necessary to quell the insurrection and restore order.
The government sent troops to quell the insurrection in the northern provinces.

Related Terms

  • antonym: incite the insurrection - to provoke or start an uprising
  • synonym: suppress the rebellion - to forcibly stop a rebellion
