uuh

interjection
hesitation sound
Pronunciation: / ʊː / (AmE), / ʊː / (BrE)
used to express hesitation or to indicate that the speaker is thinking before responding

Examples

Say uuh when hesitating
He paused and said, "uuh, I need a moment to think about that answer."
"Uuh, can you repeat the question?" she asked, looking confused.
During the interview, he often used uuh when he was unsure how to respond.

Related Terms

  • synonym: uh - a hesitation sound used when pausing or thinking
  • synonym: um - a sound used to show hesitation or uncertainty
