unmortified

adjective
not decayed tissue
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmɔrtɪˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɔːtɪfaɪd / (BrE)
not affected by tissue death or decay; not gangrenous or necrotic, especially in a medical context

Examples

Unmortified tissue
The doctor confirmed that the wound was unmortified and showed signs of healing.
After the operation, the tissue remained unmortified, which was a positive sign for recovery.
It is important to treat infections early to keep the affected area unmortified.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gangrenous - affected by gangrene; decayed or dead tissue
  • antonym: necrotic - affected by the death of body tissue
