space

noun
the limitless expanse containing everything
Pronunciation: / speɪs / (AmE), / speɪs / (BrE)
the universe beyond the Earth's atmosphere; the region where stars and planets exist

Examples

Outer space
Astronauts trained for months before traveling into space for their mission.
Light travels through the vastness of space to reach Earth.
Scientists study the origins of the universe by observing phenomena in deep space.
Children often dream about traveling through space as astronauts.
Many scientists believe that life exists somewhere in space beyond our solar system.
The satellite was launched into space to collect weather data.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outer space - the physical universe beyond the Earth's atmosphere; another term for space in this sense
  • synonym: universe - all of space and everything in it, including stars, planets, galaxies, etc.
