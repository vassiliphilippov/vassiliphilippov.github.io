spread

noun
increase in body girth, esp. in middle age
Pronunciation: / sprɛd / (AmE), / sprɛd / (BrE)
the act, process, or result of distributing, extending, or increasing over an area, range, or period of time

Examples

There has been a noticeable spread of urban areas into the surrounding countryside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distribution - the act of sharing something out among a number of recipients
  • synonym: expansion - the process of becoming larger or more extensive
