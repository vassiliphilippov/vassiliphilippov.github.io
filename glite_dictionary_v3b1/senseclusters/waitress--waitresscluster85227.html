waitress

noun
female server in a restaurant
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪtrəs / (AmE), / ˈweɪtrəs / (BrE)
A woman whose job is to serve food and drinks at tables in a restaurant or cafe.

Examples

A waitress takes orders
As a college student, she worked as a waitress in a busy diner.
The waitress brought our drinks quickly and with a smile.
We asked the waitress for a recommendation from the menu.

Related Terms

  • synonym: waiter - a man who serves food and drinks at a restaurant or cafe
