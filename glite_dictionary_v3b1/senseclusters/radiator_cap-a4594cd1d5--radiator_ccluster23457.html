radiator cap

noun
cap covering radiator opening in vehicles
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪdiˌeɪɾɚ kæp / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiˌeɪtə kæp / (BrE)
a cap that covers the opening of a radiator, allowing coolant liquid to be added and helping maintain system pressure

Examples

A radiator cap cover
A faulty radiator cap can cause the engine to overheat.
After topping off the coolant, he securely tightened the radiator cap.
The mechanic warned me never to remove the radiator cap when the engine is hot.
