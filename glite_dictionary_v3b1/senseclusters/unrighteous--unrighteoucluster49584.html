unrighteous

adjective
not morally right; wicked
1.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈraɪtʃəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈraɪtʃəs / (BrE)
Not acting in a morally right or just way; wicked or sinful.

Examples

An unrighteous act
Despite their wealth, the company's leaders were known for their unrighteous practices.
He condemned the judge's unrighteous decision as a blatant miscarriage of justice.
Many people protested against the unrighteous law that discriminated against minorities.
