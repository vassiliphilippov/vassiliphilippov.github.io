steal away

verb
to leave secretly and quietly
6.3 0.31/m
Pronunciation: / stiÀêl …ôÀàwe…™ / (AmE), / stiÀêl …ôÀàwe…™ / (BrE)
to leave a place quietly and secretly, so that no one notices

Examples

To steal away quietly
As dawn approached, the thieves stole away into the shadows.
He stole away during the meeting, hoping no one would see him leave.
She tried to steal away from the party without anyone noticing.
