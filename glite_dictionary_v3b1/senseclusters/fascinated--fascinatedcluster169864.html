fascinated

adjective
intensely interested or attracted
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfæs.əˌneɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈfæs.ɪ.neɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
intensely attracted or interested by something so that all attention is focused on it

Examples

To be fascinated by stories
Everyone in the audience was fascinated throughout the performance.
She listened fascinated as the explorer described his journeys.
The children were fascinated by the magician's amazing tricks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bored - feeling weary because one is unoccupied or lacks interest
  • synonym: interested - showing curiosity or concern about something
