mucoviscidosis

noun
hereditary disease (cystic fibrosis) with thick mucus
7.6 9.1
Pronunciation: /mjuː.koʊ.vɪs.ɪˈdoʊ.sɪs/ (AmE), /mjuː.kəʊ.vɪs.ɪˈdəʊ.sɪs/ (BrE)
