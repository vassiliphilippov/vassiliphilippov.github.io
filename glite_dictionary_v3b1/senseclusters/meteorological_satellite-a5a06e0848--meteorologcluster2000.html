meteorological satellite

noun
satellite for weather observation
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˌmiːtiərəˈlɑdʒɪkəl ˈsætəˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˌmiːtiərəˈlɒdʒɪkəl ˈsætəlaɪt / (BrE)
An artificial satellite designed to monitor and transmit information about the Earth's weather and atmosphere.

Examples

A meteorological satellite orbit
Each meteorological satellite orbits the Earth and collects atmospheric information.
Scientists rely on data from a meteorological satellite to predict severe weather events.
The meteorological satellite sent back detailed images of the developing hurricane.
