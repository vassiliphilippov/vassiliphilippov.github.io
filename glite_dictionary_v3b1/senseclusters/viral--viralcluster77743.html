viral

adjective
caused by a virus
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪrəl / (AmE), / ˈvaɪərəl / (BrE)
relating to or caused by a virus

Examples

Viral infection
Many common colds are viral rather than bacterial in origin.
The doctor diagnosed her with a viral infection after noticing her symptoms.
The outbreak was traced to a viral pathogen spreading through the community.
