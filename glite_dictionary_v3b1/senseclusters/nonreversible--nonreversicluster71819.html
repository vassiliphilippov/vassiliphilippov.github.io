nonreversible

adjective
not capable of being reversed
4.1 5.6
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.rɪ.ˈvɜːr.sə.bəl/ /; / /nən.rɪ.ˈvɜːr.sə.bəl/ (AmE), /nɒn.rɪ.ˈvɜːs.ə.bəl/ /; / /nən.rɪ.ˈvɜːs.ə.bəl/ (BrE)
not able to be reversed, changed back, or used with either side facing outwards

Examples

A nonreversible belt
He bought a nonreversible belt by mistake, but he actually liked the color.
Some chemical reactions are nonreversible and cannot return to their original state.
The jacket is nonreversible, so you can only wear it one way.
