franc-tireur

noun
irregular sharpshooter or guerrilla fighter
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌfrɑ̃n tiˈrɜr / (AmE), / ˌfrɒ̃n tiˈrɜː / (BrE)
A member of an irregular military unit, typically acting as a sharpshooter or guerrilla fighter, especially in historical French contexts.

Examples

A franc-tireur soldier
After being captured, the franc-tireur was treated as an enemy combatant.
During the Franco-Prussian War, many franc-tireurs harassed enemy supply lines.
The army had difficulty distinguishing between villagers and franc-tireurs operating in the area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guerrilla - member of an irregular group engaging in warfare
  • antonym: regular soldier - member of a standard, official military unit
