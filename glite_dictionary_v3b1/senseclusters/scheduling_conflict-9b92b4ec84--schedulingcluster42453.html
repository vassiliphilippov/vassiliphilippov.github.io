scheduling conflict

noun

A situation in which two or more planned appointments or events overlap, making it impossible to attend or participate in all of them.

Examples

A scheduling conflict arises

Her scheduling conflict prevented her from joining the conference call.
I missed the seminar because of a scheduling conflict with another meeting.
