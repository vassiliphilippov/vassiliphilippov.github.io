donnybrook

noun
rowdy fair or festival
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑniˌbrʊk / (AmE), / ˈdɒnibrʊk / (BrE)
a fair or festival, especially one known for lively or rowdy behavior, originally held in Donnybrook, Dublin.

Examples

Annual donnybrook festival
Every summer, the town hosts a lively donnybrook with music, food, and games for all ages.
The annual donnybrook attracted visitors from neighboring villages eager to join the festivities.
Stories of the old donnybrook in Dublin describe days filled with excitement and celebration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carnival - a public event with music, dancing, and entertainment
  • synonym: festival - a day or period of celebration, often with events and entertainment
