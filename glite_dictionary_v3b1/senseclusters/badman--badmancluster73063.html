badman

noun
tough or dangerous man
Pronunciation: / ˈbædmæn / (AmE), / ˈbædmæn / (BrE)
a man who is considered tough, dangerous, or rebellious, often admired or feared in certain groups

Examples

A tough badman
In the story, the badman controls the neighborhood with fear and intimidation.
He tried to act like a badman, but everyone knew he was just pretending.
The legend of the badman spread quickly through the city streets.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hero - a person admired for courage or good actions
  • synonym: tough guy - a man who acts strong or intimidating
  • synonym: villain - a person who does evil or criminal things
