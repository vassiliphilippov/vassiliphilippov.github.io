quartz crystal

noun
specially cut quartz used in electronics
5.7 4.2
Pronunciation: /kwɔːrts ˈkrɪs.təl/ (AmE), /kwɔːts ˈkrɪs.təl/ (BrE)
a piece of quartz precisely cut and shaped to generate a stable electric signal at a specific frequency, used in electronic devices such as oscillators

Examples

A quartz crystal oscillator
A quartz crystal is essential for stabilizing the frequency in radios and clocks.
Technicians inspected the quartz crystal to ensure its cut was accurate for precise frequency control.
The watch keeps perfect time thanks to its quartz crystal oscillator.
