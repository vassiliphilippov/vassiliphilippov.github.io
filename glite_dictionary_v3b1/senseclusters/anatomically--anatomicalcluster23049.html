anatomically

adverb
in relation to anatomy
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.əˈtɑː.mɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˌæn.əˈtɒm.ɪ.kli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to the structure of organisms or their parts; with regard to anatomy.

Examples

Studied anatomically correct
Doctors studied the injury to understand what had gone wrong anatomically.
The sculpture was praised for being anatomically accurate.
These two animal species are anatomically similar despite their different habitats.

Related Terms

  • synonym: physiologically - in a way relating to the functions of living organisms
