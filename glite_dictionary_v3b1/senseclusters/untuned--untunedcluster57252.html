untuned

adjective
not optimized
6.1 0.00/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàtuÀênd / (AmE), /  ånÀàtjuÀênd / (BrE)
not optimized, configured, or adjusted for best performance or accuracy, especially in software, engines, or machines

Examples

Untuned engine
The untuned engine made a strange noise and used more fuel than usual.
If the software remains untuned, it may run slowly and produce inaccurate results.
An untuned guitar can sound unpleasant even if you play the right notes.

Synonyms

  • unoptimized
    6.2 0.08/m

  • synonym: unoptimized - not made as effective or efficient as possible
