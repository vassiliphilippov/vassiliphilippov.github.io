ecclesiasticism

noun
excessive emphasis on church forms or practices
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˌkliːziˈæstɪˌsɪzəm / (AmE), / ɪˌkliːzɪˈæstɪˌsɪzəm /; / ɪˌkliːzɪˈastɪˌsɪzəm / (BrE)
excessive or strong focus on church rituals, practices, or hierarchy

Examples

Strong ecclesiasticism focus
His focus on hierarchy and tradition revealed a tendency toward ecclesiasticism.

The church was criticized for its ecclesiasticism rather than its spiritual leadership.
