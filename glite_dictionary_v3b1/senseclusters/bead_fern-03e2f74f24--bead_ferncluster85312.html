bead fern

noun
a fern with beadlike lobes on its fronds
5.4
Pronunciation: / bid fɜrn / (AmE), / biːd fɜːn / (BrE)
a spreading fern found in eastern North America and eastern Asia, with fronds divided into beadlike lobes and sori forming rounded clusters
