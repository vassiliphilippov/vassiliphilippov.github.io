topgallant yard

noun
yard from which topgallant sail is set
Pronunciation: / tɑpˌɡæl.ənt jɑrd / (AmE), / tɒpˌɡæl.ənt jɑːd / (BrE)
the horizontal spar on a sailing ship from which the topgallant sail is set, located above the topsail yard.

Examples

The topgallant yard spar
During the refit, the topgallant yard was replaced with a stronger one.
From the deck, he could see a sailor perched on the topgallant yard adjusting the lines.
The crew worked quickly to secure the topgallant yard before the storm hit.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: main yard - horizontal spar carrying the mainsail
  • degree higher: royal yard - horizontal spar above the topgallant yard, carrying the royal sail
