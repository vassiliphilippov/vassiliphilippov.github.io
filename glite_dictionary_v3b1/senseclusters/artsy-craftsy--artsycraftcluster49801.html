artsy-craftsy

adjective
pretentiously artistic or overly crafty (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrtsi ˈkræftsi /; / ˈɑːrt.si ˈkræft.si / (AmE), / ˌɑːtsi ˈkrɑːfti /; / ˈɑːtsi ˈkrɑːftsi / (BrE)
trying to be artistic or creative in a way that seems amateurish, overly decorative, or affected; related to arts and crafts, often with a dismissive or playful nuance

Examples

Artsy-craftsy style
His artsy-craftsy style is not really appreciated in more traditional galleries.
She brought some artsy-craftsy homemade gifts to the party.
The decor in their living room is a little too artsy-craftsy for my taste.
I'm not really into artsy-craftsy gifts, but I appreciated the effort she made.
She decorated her kitchen with artsy-craftsy touches like handmade ceramic tiles.
The summer camp offers all kinds of artsy-craftsy activities for kids.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artsy - showing an interest or pretense in art
  • synonym: crafty - relating to or skilled in crafts (but also means clever/sly)
