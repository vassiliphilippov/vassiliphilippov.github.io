supertrain

noun
high-speed train
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərtreɪn / (AmE), / ˈsuːpətreɪn / (BrE)
a very fast or technologically advanced train, especially designed for rapid passenger transport

Examples

A supertrain speeds
The supertrain reduced travel time between the two cities to just ninety minutes.
Many commuters prefer the supertrain because of its speed and comfort.
A new supertrain line is being built to connect the capital with the coastal region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bullet train - a high-speed passenger train, especially in Japan
  • synonym: express train - a train that travels faster and stops at fewer stations than ordinary trains
  • antonym: local train - a train that stops at all or most stations along its route
