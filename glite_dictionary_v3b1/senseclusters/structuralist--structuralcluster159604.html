structuralist

noun
one who analyzes structures
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈstrʌk.tʃə.rəl.ɪst/ (AmE), /ˈstrʌk.tʃə.rəl.ɪst/ (BrE)
a person who studies, supports, or practices structuralism, especially in fields like philosophy, linguistics, or social sciences

Examples
