look

noun
expression on a person's face
Pronunciation: / lʊk / (AmE), / lʊk / (BrE)
an expression or feeling shown on someone's face

Examples

A disapproving look
His mother shot him a disapproving look when he arrived late.
She gave him a worried look before stepping on stage.
The child's face broke into a look of pure joy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expression - the look on someone's face that shows what they are thinking or feeling
  • degree lower: glance - a quick look or expression
