monostotic fibrous dysplasia

noun
fibrous dysplasia limited to one bone
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːnoʊˈstɑːtɪk ˈfaɪbrəs dɪsˈpleɪʒə / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəˈstɒtɪk ˈfaɪbrəs dɪsˈpleɪʒə / (BrE)
A form of fibrous dysplasia in which a single bone is affected by abnormal growth of fibrous tissue.

Examples

Monostotic fibrous dysplasia single bone
Because her monostotic fibrous dysplasia was limited to one bone, recovery was faster.
The X-ray revealed monostotic fibrous dysplasia in the patient's right femur.
Treatment for monostotic fibrous dysplasia may include medication or surgery depending on the severity.
