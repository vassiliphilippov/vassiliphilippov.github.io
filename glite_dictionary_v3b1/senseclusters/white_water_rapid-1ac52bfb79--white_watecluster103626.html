white water rapid

noun
fast, turbulent river section
7.5 3.5
Pronunciation: /waɪt ˈwɔː.tər ˈræp.ɪd/ /; / /waɪt ˈwɑː.tər ˈræp.ɪd/ /; / /waɪt ˈwɔː.tər ˈræp.əd/ (AmE), /waɪt ˈwɔː.tə ˈræp.ɪd/ /; / /waɪt ˈwɔː.tə ˈræp.əd/ (BrE)
a section of a river where the water flows quickly and turbulently, typically creating white, frothy waves
