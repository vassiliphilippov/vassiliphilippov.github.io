jump

verb
start a car by connecting to another battery
Pronunciation: / dʒʌmp / (AmE), / dʒʌmp / (BrE)
to start a vehicle with a dead battery by connecting it to another vehicle's battery

Examples

To jump a car battery
He offered to jump my battery after seeing me stranded in the parking lot.
If your lights are dim, you might need to jump the car to get it running.
My car wouldn't start this morning, so I had to jump it with my neighbor's help.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jump-start - to start a vehicle with a discharged battery by using jumper cables and another battery
