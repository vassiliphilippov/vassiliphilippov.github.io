Examples

An unpreventable disaster

Synonyms

  • unavoidable
    3.8 4.16/m
    unavoidable describes something people cannot avoid or prevent in practice, while unpreventable stresses it truly cannot be stopped.
  • inevitable
    5.3 0.01/m
    inevitable means certain to happen no matter what people try, while unpreventable highlights that nothing can stop it.
  • inescapable
    5.4 3.39/m
    inescapable often shows you cannot get away from or avoid something, while unpreventable focuses on not being stopped.
  • ineluctable
    9.9 0.31/m
    ineluctable is a formal, literary word for something destined to occur, while unpreventable is plain and used in general speech.
  • unstoppable
    4.0 2.90/m
    unstoppable describes a force or person that cannot be stopped, while unpreventable means an event cannot be prevented from happening.
