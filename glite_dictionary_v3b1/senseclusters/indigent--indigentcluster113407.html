indigent

adjective
extremely poor; needy
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈɪndɪdʒənt / (BrE)
Lacking the necessities of life; extremely poor and needing help from others.

Examples

An indigent family
After losing his job, he became virtually indigent and relied on friends for support.
Legal aid is available for indigent defendants who cannot afford an attorney.
The organization provided free meals to the indigent residents of the city.
