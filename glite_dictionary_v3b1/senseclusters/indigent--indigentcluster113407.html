  • needy
    1.5 3.19/m

  • necessitous
    9.9 0.03/m
    necessitous is a formal word for lacking life's necessities, while indigent is the standard term for extreme poverty needing help.
  • penurious
    9.9 0.03/m
    penurious describes extreme lack of money and means, while indigent stresses being very poor and in need of outside help.
  • impoverished
    6.2 5.23/m
    impoverished means made poor or lacking resources, while indigent highlights personal need for help and basic necessities.
  • impecunious
    9.9 0.08/m
    impecunious means having little or no money, while indigent implies a deeper lack of life's necessities and need for aid.
  • penniless
    4.3 1.02/m
