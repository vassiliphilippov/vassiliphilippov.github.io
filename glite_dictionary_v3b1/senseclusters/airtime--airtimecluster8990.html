airtime

noun
broadcast duration
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈeəˌtaɪm / (BrE)
the amount of time something is broadcast on radio or television

Examples

Airtime on TV
The politician was pleased with the amount of airtime her speech received on national television.
Advertisers compete for airtime during major sporting events because of the large audiences.
The documentary was given prime airtime on Sunday evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coverage - the reporting or broadcasting of an event
  • synonym: screen time - the amount of time something appears on a screen
