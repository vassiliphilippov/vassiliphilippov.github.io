allow

verb
make provision for; concede the possibility
Pronunciation: / əˈlaʊ / (AmE), / əˈlaʊ / (BrE)
to make provision for the possibility of something; to admit or acknowledge as true or valid

Examples

To allow for mistakes
He allowed that her argument might have some merit.
The design allows for up to 20% expansion due to heat.
We must allow for unexpected expenses in our budget plan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: admit - to accept or acknowledge the truth of something
  • synonym: concede - to admit that something is true or valid after first denying or resisting it
  • antonym: deny - to declare (something) to be untrue
