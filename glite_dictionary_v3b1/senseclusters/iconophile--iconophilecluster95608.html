iconophile

noun
admirer of icons
9.9
Pronunciation: / aɪˈkɑːnəˌfaɪl / (AmE), / aɪˈkəʊnəˌfaɪl / (BrE)
a person who supports or admires the use of religious images or icons, especially in worship

Examples

Religious iconophile
As an iconophile, she collected many religious paintings and artifacts.
The debate between the iconophiles and iconoclasts shaped the history of the church.
He was known as a passionate iconophile who defended the use of sacred images in worship.

Related Terms

  • antonym: iconoclast - a person who opposes or destroys religious images or icons
