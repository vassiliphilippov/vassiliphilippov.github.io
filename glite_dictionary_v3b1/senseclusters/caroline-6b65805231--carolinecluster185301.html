Caroline

adjective
of the period of Charles I or II of England
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkæroʊˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈkærəˌlaɪn / (BrE)
Relating to the period of the reigns of Kings Charles I and Charles II of England or their era.

Examples

Caroline era
Historians often study the Caroline court to understand 17th-century politics.
The Caroline era is known for its distinctive art and architecture.
The play was written during the Caroline period in England.
