conflictive

adjective
causing inner conflict
7.4
Pronunciation: / kənˈflɪktɪv / (AmE), / kənˈflɪktɪv / (BrE)
causing or involving internal mental or emotional conflict, often leading to contradictory feelings within a person

Examples

Conflictive feelings
She experienced conflictive feelings about moving to a new city for her job.
His conflictive thoughts made it hard for him to make a decision.
The situation was emotionally conflictive for everyone involved.
