wormwood oil

noun
oil from wormwood leaves used in absinthe
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrm.wʊd ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈwɜːm.wʊd ɔɪl / (BrE)
a dark, bitter essential oil obtained from wormwood leaves, used especially to flavor absinthe liqueurs

Examples

Bitter wormwood oil
Perfume makers sometimes explore the bitter aroma of wormwood oil in complex scent blends.
The use of wormwood oil is highly regulated due to its toxicity in large quantities.
Traditional absinthe gets its distinctive taste from the addition of wormwood oil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absinthe - a green alcoholic drink flavored with anise and wormwood
