slip by

verb
(of time) pass quickly or unnoticed
3.8 3.2
Pronunciation: / slɪp baɪ / (AmE), / slɪp baɪ / (BrE)
to pass quickly or without being noticed, especially about time or opportunities

Examples

Hours slip by

The summer seemed to slip by before we knew it.

The summer seemed to slip by before we even noticed.
