crowberry

noun
a low evergreen shrub with black berries
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkroʊˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈkrəʊˌbɛri / (BrE)
a low evergreen shrub of the genus Empetrum, bearing small purple flowers and black berrylike fruit, found in cool northern regions

Examples

The crowberry plant
Bears are known to eat crowberry fruit in late summer.
The tundra was covered in thick mats of crowberry and moss.
You can find crowberry bushes growing along northern coastal cliffs.
