nonmeasurable

adjective
cannot be measured
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈmɛʒərəbəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈmɛʒərəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be measured or quantified in amount or degree

Examples

Nonmeasurable effect
The impact of her kindness was nonmeasurable but deeply felt by everyone.
Some aspects of creativity are nonmeasurable and cannot be captured by tests.
The value of true friendship is often nonmeasurable in material terms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immeasurable - too large or extreme to be measured
  • antonym: measurable - able to be measured or quantified
