iodinate

verb
cause to combine with iodine
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ.ə.dɪˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.ə.dɪˌneɪt / (BrE)
to cause (a compound) to combine with iodine, especially in a chemical reaction

Examples

To iodinate a compound
Researchers discovered a new enzyme that can iodinate tyrosine residues in peptides.
Some pharmaceutical compounds are often iodinated during their synthesis process.
The lab technician will iodinate the protein to enable radio-label detection.
