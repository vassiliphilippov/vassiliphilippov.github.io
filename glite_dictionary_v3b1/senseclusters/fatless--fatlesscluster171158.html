fatless

adjective
without fat
Pronunciation: / ˈfæt.ləs / (AmE), / ˈfæt.ləs / (BrE)
Containing no fat or fat solids.

Examples

Fatless milk
Many shoppers are looking for fatless alternatives to traditional dairy products.
She prefers to cook with fatless cuts of meat for health reasons.
The recipe calls for fatless yogurt as an ingredient.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fat-free - not containing any fat
  • antonym: fatty - containing a lot of fat
  • synonym: lean - containing little or no fat (esp. of meat)
