solution

noun
homogeneous mixture, usually liquid
1.0 2.5
Pronunciation: /sə.ˈluː.ʃən/ /; / /sə.ˈljuː.ʃən/ (AmE), /sə.ˈljuː.ʃən/ /; / /sə.ˈluː.ʃən/ (BrE)
A liquid or other homogeneous mixture in which one or more substances are dissolved in another.

Examples

Solution of salt water
She cleaned the stain with a solution of vinegar and baking soda.
Sugar dissolves quickly to form a clear solution in hot tea.
The chemist prepared a solution of salt in water for the experiment.
