run

noun
American football play: advancing ball by carrying
Pronunciation: / rʌn / (AmE), / rʌn / (BrE)
(in American football) a play in which a player tries to advance the ball by carrying it, rather than passing it.

Examples

A run play
After several unsuccessful passes, the quarterback called for a run to the left side.
Coaches often debate whether to focus on the run or the passing game in their offensive strategy.
The team's defense is especially strong against the run, stopping most gains on the ground.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pass - (in American football) a play in which the ball is thrown to a teammate
