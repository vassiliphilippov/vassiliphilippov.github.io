cohost

noun
joint event presenter
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊˌhoʊst / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌhəʊst / (BrE)
a person who leads or presents an event, program, or show together with one or more others

Examples

TV cohost
Each cohost brought a unique perspective to the discussion on the radio show.
The awards ceremony was lively thanks to the chemistry between the two cohosts.
As a new cohost, she quickly became popular with the audience.
Each cohost brought a unique perspective to the discussion on the podcast.
The awards ceremony was lively thanks to the chemistry between the two cohosts.
As a cohost, he helps plan the topics and interview the guests every week.

  • synonym: host - a person who leads or presents an event or show
