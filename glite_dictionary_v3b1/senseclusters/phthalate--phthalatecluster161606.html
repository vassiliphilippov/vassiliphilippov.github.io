phthalate

adjective
containing phthalates
Pronunciation: / ˈθæleɪt / (AmE), / ˈθæleɪt / (BrE)
relating to, derived from, or containing phthalates, which are esters or salts of phthalic acid

Examples

Phthalate compound
The laboratory tested several phthalate compounds for their effects on human health.
Manufacturers are seeking phthalate alternatives for use in children's toys.
The new regulations limit the use of phthalate materials in food packaging.
