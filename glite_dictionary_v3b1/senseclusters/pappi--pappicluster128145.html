pappi

noun
seed bristles
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpæp.aɪ/ /; / /ˈpæp.iː/ (AmE), /ˈpæp.aɪ/ /; / /ˈpæp.iː/ (BrE)

Examples

Many plants in the daisy family have pappi that look like tiny parachutes.
