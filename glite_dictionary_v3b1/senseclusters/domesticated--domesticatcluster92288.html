domesticated

adjective
accustomed to home life
7.2
Pronunciation: / dəˈmɛstɪˌkeɪtɪd / (AmE), / dəˈmɛstɪˌkeɪtɪd / (BrE)
accustomed to home life; comfortable and adept in household routines.

Examples

Domesticated at home

He was never very domesticated until he started raising children with his partner.
She's so domesticated that she finds joy in everyday chores and home improvement projects.
