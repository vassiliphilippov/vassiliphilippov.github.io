commensuration

noun
measuring by standard
Pronunciation: / kəˌmɛnsjʊˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / kəˌmɛnʃʊˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of measuring or comparing things by a common standard

Examples

Commensuration of data
Accurate commensuration of data is crucial for comparing results from different experiments.
The engineer explained the importance of commensuration when working with international measurement systems.
Without proper commensuration, it is difficult to evaluate the effectiveness of various methods.
