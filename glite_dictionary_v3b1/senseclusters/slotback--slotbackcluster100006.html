slotback

noun
football offensive position
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈslɑtbæk / (AmE), / ˈslɒtbæk / (BrE)
a player in American or Canadian football who lines up in the slot position, typically between the last offensive lineman and a wide receiver

He trained as a slotback to take advantage of his speed and agility.
