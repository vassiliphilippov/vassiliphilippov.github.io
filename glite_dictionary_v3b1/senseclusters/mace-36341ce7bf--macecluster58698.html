Mace

noun
chemical self-defense spray
Pronunciation: / meɪs / (AmE), / meɪs / (BrE)
(Trademark) a liquid preparation, usually in aerosol form, that temporarily incapacitates a person by causing irritation to the eyes and dizziness; used as a self-defense spray.

Examples

Mace spray
After being sprayed with Mace, the attacker was quickly subdued by the police.
She carries a can of Mace in her purse for personal protection.
The security guard reached for his Mace when the intruder refused to back down.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pepper spray - a similar substance that incapacitates by irritating the eyes and respiratory system
