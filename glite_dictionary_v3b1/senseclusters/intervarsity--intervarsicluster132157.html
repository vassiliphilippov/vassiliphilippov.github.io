intervarsity

adjective
between universities
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈvɑːrsəti / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈvɑːsəti / (BrE)
relating to or taking place between different universities, especially in competitions or events

Examples

Intervarsity sports event
The annual intervarsity sports competition attracts teams from across the country.
She was proud to represent her school at the intervarsity quiz championship.
Many students look forward to the excitement of intervarsity events each year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intercollegiate - between colleges or universities
  • antonym: intrauniversity - within a single university
