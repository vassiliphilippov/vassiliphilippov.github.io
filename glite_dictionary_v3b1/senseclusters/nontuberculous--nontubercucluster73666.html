nontuberculous

adjective
not tuberculosis-related
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑntuˈbɜrkjʊləs / (AmE), / ˌnɒntjuːˈbɜːkjʊləs / (BrE)
not caused by or related to tuberculosis, especially referring to bacteria or infections

Examples

Nontuberculous lung infection
The patient was diagnosed with a nontuberculous lung infection after several tests.
Researchers are studying nontuberculous bacteria that can cause disease in humans.
Treatment for nontuberculous infections may differ from that for tuberculosis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tuberculous - caused by or related to tuberculosis
