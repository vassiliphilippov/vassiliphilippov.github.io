Tai Dam

noun
language spoken by the Tai Dam people of Vietnam
Pronunciation: / taɪ ˈdɑm / (AmE), / taɪ ˈdɑːm / (BrE)
a branch of the Tai language family spoken by the Tai Dam people, primarily in northern and parts of southern Vietnam.
