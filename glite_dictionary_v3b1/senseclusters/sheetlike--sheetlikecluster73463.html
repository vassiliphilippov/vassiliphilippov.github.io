sheetlike

adjective
resembling a thin, flat sheet
Pronunciation: / ˈʃitˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈʃiːtˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the form of a thin, flat sheet.

Examples

A sheetlike layer
A thin, sheetlike layer of clouds covered the city skyline.
The material forms a sheetlike membrane over the surface of the water.
The pathologist observed sheetlike deposits under the microscope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sheety - thin, flat, sheet-like in form
