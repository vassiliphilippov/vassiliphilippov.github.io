unstintingly

adverb
generously; without holding back
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstɪntɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈstɪntɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows great generosity or is done without holding back; without restraint.

Examples

Unstintingly sharing knowledge
He praised her for unstintingly sharing her knowledge with younger colleagues.
She gave unstintingly of her time to help the community.
The volunteers unstintingly supported the relief efforts after the disaster.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generously - in a generous manner, giving freely
