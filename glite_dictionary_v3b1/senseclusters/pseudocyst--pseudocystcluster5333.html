pseudocyst

noun
fluid-filled sac
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːdoʊˌsɪst / (AmE), / ˈsjuːdəʊˌsɪst / (BrE)
an abnormal fluid-filled sac in the body that does not have a true membrane lining

Examples

A pseudocyst in pancreas
The surgeon explained that the pseudocyst needed to be drained to relieve the patient's pain.
After the accident, a pseudocyst developed near his pancreas and required monitoring.
Doctors discovered a large pseudocyst during the abdominal scan.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cyst - a closed sac in the body with a distinct membrane
