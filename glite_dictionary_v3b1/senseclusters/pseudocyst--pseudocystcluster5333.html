pseudocyst

noun
fluid-filled sac
9.0
Pronunciation: /ˈsuː.doʊ.sɪst/ /; / /ˈsjuː.doʊ.sɪst/ (AmE), /ˈsuː.dəʊ.sɪst/ /; / /ˈsjuː.dəʊ.sɪst/ (BrE)
