pivot shot

noun
basketball shot made while pivoting
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪvət ʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈpɪvət ʃɒt / (BrE)
a one-handed basketball shot made while turning on the pivot foot

Examples

A pivot shot in basketball
He practiced his pivot shot for hours to perfect the move under pressure.
The player spun quickly and released a pivot shot that soared through the net.
With defenders closing in, she made a smooth pivot shot to score two points.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jump shot - a basketball shot made by jumping and releasing the ball at the peak of the jump
