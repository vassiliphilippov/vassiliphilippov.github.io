muscle system

noun
system of muscles in an organism
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌsəl ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈmʌsl̩ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
The system of muscles and associated tissues that enables movement in a living organism.
