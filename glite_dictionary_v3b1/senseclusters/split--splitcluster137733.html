split

noun
division into opposing factions
3.6 3.4
Pronunciation: /splɪt/ (AmE), /splɪt/ (BrE)
a division of a group or organization into opposing factions or parties, often involving conflict or disagreement

Examples

A team split

After years of disagreement, there was finally a split between management and workers.
The debate led to a split in the political party, with members forming rival groups.
