waterweed

noun
submerged aquatic plant (Elodea)
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtərˌwid / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌwiːd / (BrE)
a submerged aquatic plant of the genus Elodea, commonly found in fresh water and often considered invasive due to its rapid growth

Examples

Fresh waterweed plant
After a week of neglect, waterweed choked the once-clear water in the tank.
Gardeners often struggle to control waterweed in freshwater aquariums.
The pond became overgrown with waterweed, making it difficult for fish to swim.
