flexural

adjective
about bending
Pronunciation: / ˈflɛkʃərəl / (AmE), / ˈflɛkʃərəl / (BrE)
relating to the bending or flexing of a material, structure, or object

Examples

The flexural strength
The flexural properties of the metal were tested in the laboratory.
A bridge must have high flexural strength to support heavy loads.
The engineer analyzed the flexural behavior of the new composite material.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bending - the action of causing something to curve or flex
  • antonym: rigid - not able to bend or be flexed
