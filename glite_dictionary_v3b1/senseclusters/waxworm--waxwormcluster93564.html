waxworm

noun
caterpillar pet food
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwækswɜrm /; / ˈwækswɜːrm / (AmE), / ˈwækswɜːm / (BrE)
the larva (caterpillar) of a wax moth, often used as food for pets like reptiles and birds, or in scientific research
