follow-up

noun
subsequent medical check after treatment
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑː.loʊ.ʌp /; / ˈfɑloʊ ʌp / (AmE), / ˈfɒl.əʊ.ʌp /; / ˈfɒləʊ ʌp / (BrE)
a medical appointment or examination that happens after initial treatment to check progress or recovery

Examples

Follow-up visit
After surgery, regular follow-up visits are necessary.
His follow-up showed significant improvement compared to the initial appointment.
The doctor scheduled a follow-up to ensure her ankle was healing correctly.
During the follow-up, the physician checked for any side effects of the medication.
It's important not to miss your follow-up appointments for proper treatment monitoring.
The doctor scheduled a follow-up to review the patient's recovery after surgery.
