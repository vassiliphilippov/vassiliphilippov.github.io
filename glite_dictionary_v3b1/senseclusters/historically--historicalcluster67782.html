historically

adverb
in relation to history
Pronunciation: / hɪˈstɔrɪkli / (AmE), / hɪˈstɒrɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to history, historical facts, or the past

Examples

Historically important events
Historically, this region has been a center of trade and culture.
Historically, women had fewer opportunities in higher education.
The building is historically significant due to its unique architecture.
Historically, gold has been used as a store of value in many cultures.
Historically, these two countries have maintained close diplomatic ties.
The building is historically significant due to events that took place there centuries ago.
