chestful

noun
amount filling a chest
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛstfʊl / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛstfʊl / (BrE)
an amount that fills a chest or the capacity of a chest

Examples

A chestful of gold
They discovered a chestful of gold coins hidden beneath the floorboards.
A chestful of winter clothes was brought down from the attic each year.
The pirates loaded a chestful of jewels onto their ship before sailing away.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boxful - an amount that fills a box
  • degree lower: handful - an amount that fills a hand
