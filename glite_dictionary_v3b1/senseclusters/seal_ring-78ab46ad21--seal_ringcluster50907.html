seal ring

noun
ring with an engraved signet for sealing
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːl rɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsiːl rɪŋ / (BrE)
A ring set with a signet, typically engraved with a family crest or initials, used to stamp a seal in wax.

Examples

A seal ring with crest
He wore his father’s seal ring on his little finger.
She pressed her seal ring into the warm wax to mark the letter.
The ancient seal ring was discovered in the ruins of the old temple.

Related Terms

  • synonym: signet ring - a ring with an engraved seal, especially used for stamping a personal signature
