dittany

noun
a Eurasian perennial herb with aromatic flowers
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪtəni / (AmE), / ˈdɪtəni / (BrE)
A Eurasian perennial herb (Dictamnus albus) having white or pink flowers that give off a flammable vapor in hot weather.

Examples

The dittany plant
Gardeners enjoy growing dittany for its showy flowers and aromatic leaves.
In ancient times, dittany was valued for its medicinal properties and unique flammable vapor.
The dittany plant releases a strong fragrance when brushed against on a warm day.
