noncitizen

noun
someone who is not a citizen of a country
9.9
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈsɪtɪzən / (AmE), / nɒnˈsɪtɪzən / (BrE)
a person who is not a citizen of a particular country, especially one living or present there

Examples

A noncitizen worker
As a noncitizen, she required a special work permit to take the job.
Many noncitizens live and work in the country legally but cannot vote in elections.
The new policy provides healthcare coverage to every noncitizen living in the city.
