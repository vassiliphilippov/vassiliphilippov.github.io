pugil

noun
archaic boxer
Pronunciation: / ˈpjuːdʒɪl / (AmE), / ˈpjuːdʒɪl / (BrE)
an archaic or literary term for a boxer or fighter, especially one who competes with fists

Examples

A famous pugil
The old tale described a legendary pugil who never lost a match in the ancient arena.
In the poem, the pugil stands proud, his fists raised in defiance before the crowd.
Few could match the skill and courage of the renowned pugil from the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boxer - a person who fights in boxing matches
  • synonym: fighter - a person who fights, especially as a profession
