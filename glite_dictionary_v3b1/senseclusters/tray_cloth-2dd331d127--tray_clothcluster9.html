tray cloth

noun
small cloth for covering a tray
Pronunciation: / treɪ klɔθ / (AmE), / treɪ klɒθ / (BrE)
A small piece of table linen used to cover a tray, especially for serving food or drinks.

Examples

A tray cloth cover
He spilled coffee on the tray cloth, so it needed to be washed.
She carefully placed a clean tray cloth on the tray before serving tea.
The hotel room service always includes a neatly folded tray cloth with the breakfast tray.
