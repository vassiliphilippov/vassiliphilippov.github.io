ringtail

noun
North American raccoon
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪŋˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋteɪl / (BrE)
a small, raccoon-like mammal of North America and Mexico, known for its long bushy tail with black and white rings
