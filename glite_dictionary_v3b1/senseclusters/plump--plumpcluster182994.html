plump

adjective
slightly fat in a pleasing, rounded way
2.7 4.34/m
Pronunciation: / plÊŒmp / (AmE), / plÊŒmp / (BrE)
(especially of a person or part of the body) pleasantly full and rounded in shape, slightly fat in an attractive or healthy way.

Examples

A plump cat
After a summer of good eating, the cat looked quite plump and satisfied.
She chose the plump tomatoes from the basket for the salad.
The baby had plump cheeks that everyone loved to pinch.

    7.0 0.01/m
