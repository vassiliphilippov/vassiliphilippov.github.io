whip

noun
flexible instrument for whipping
5.4
Pronunciation: / wɪp / (AmE), / wɪp / (BrE)
A long, thin instrument with a handle and a flexible lash, used for striking or whipping.

Examples

A leather whip
An old leather whip hung on the wall as a decoration.
She cracked the whip loudly in the circus ring.
The cowboy used his whip to control the herd of cattle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lash - a flexible cord forming the striking part of a whip
