organoleptic

noun
sensory evaluation test
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːrɡənoʊˈlɛptɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːɡənəˈlɛptɪk / (BrE)
a test or evaluation of a product's qualities as perceived by the senses, such as taste, smell, texture, or appearance

Examples

Organoleptic test
The organoleptic revealed that the cheese had a strong aroma and creamy texture.
Food scientists use an organoleptic to assess the freshness of fruits and vegetables.
An organoleptic is often performed before a new beverage is released to the market.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chemical analysis - the examination of substances using chemical methods
  • synonym: sensory analysis - the scientific evaluation of food or products using the senses
