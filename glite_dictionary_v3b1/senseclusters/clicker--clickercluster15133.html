clicker

noun
response device
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪkər / (AmE), / ˈklɪkə / (BrE)
a small electronic device used to register responses or choices, often in classrooms or meetings

Examples

Classroom clicker
The teacher handed each student a clicker to answer questions during the lecture.
Audience members used a clicker to vote for their favorite presentation.
With a simple clicker, participants could respond instantly to the poll.

Related Terms

  • synonym: keypad - a small keyboard or set of buttons for entering data
  • synonym: remote - a handheld device used to control something from a distance
