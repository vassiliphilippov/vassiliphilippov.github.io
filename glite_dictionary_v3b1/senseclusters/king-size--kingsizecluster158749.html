king-size

adjective
extra large in size
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪŋˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋ.saɪz / (BrE)
Larger than the standard size; extra large, especially in relation to beds or other products.

Examples

A king-size bed
He always orders a king-size burger when he goes out to eat.
The hotel room came with a king-size bathtub that fit two people comfortably.
We bought a king-size bed for the guest room.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: queen-size - a bed or other item larger than standard but smaller than king-size
