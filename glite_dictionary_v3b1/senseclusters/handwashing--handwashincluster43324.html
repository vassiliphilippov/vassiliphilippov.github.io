handwashing

noun
cleaning hands
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌwɑʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhændˌwɒʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of cleaning your hands with soap and water to remove dirt or germs

Examples

Regular handwashing with soap
Proper handwashing is one of the best ways to prevent the spread of disease.
Children are taught the importance of handwashing at school.
Doctors recommend frequent handwashing during flu season to stay healthy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disinfection - the process of cleaning to destroy bacteria and viruses
  • synonym: sanitizing - making something clean and free from germs
