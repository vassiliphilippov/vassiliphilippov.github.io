spina

noun
thorn-like projection
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪnə / (AmE), / ˈspaɪnə / (BrE)
a spine or thorn-like projection, especially in anatomy or botany

Examples

Sharp spina on plants
The cactus is covered in sharp spina to protect itself from animals.
In anatomy, a spina can refer to a bony projection on a vertebra.
Some plants develop a spina as a defense against herbivores.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spine - a sharp, pointed projection on a plant or animal
  • synonym: thorn - a sharp, pointed growth on a plant
