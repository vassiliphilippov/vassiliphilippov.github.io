guestroom

noun
bedroom for guests
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɛstˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈɡɛst.rʊm / (BrE)
A bedroom in a house, hotel, or other accommodation reserved for the use of guests.

Examples

A guestroom in a hotel
Please leave fresh towels in the guestroom before our guests arrive.
The hotel’s guestroom featured a large bed and a private bathroom.
We converted the old study into a guestroom for visiting friends and family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spare room - a room in a house for guests or other temporary use
