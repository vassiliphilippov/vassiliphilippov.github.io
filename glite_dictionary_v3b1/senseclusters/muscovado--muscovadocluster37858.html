muscovado

noun
unrefined brown sugar
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌskəˈvɑːdoʊ / (AmE), / ˌmʌskəˈvɑːdəʊ / (BrE)
a type of unrefined brown sugar with a strong molasses flavor and moist texture

Examples

Dark muscovado sugar
The recipe calls for two tablespoons of muscovado to give the cookies a deep, rich flavor.
She prefers muscovado over regular sugar when making homemade caramel sauce.
Adding muscovado to your coffee can create a unique, slightly smoky sweetness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demerara - a type of light brown, partially refined sugar with large crystals
  • antonym: white sugar - fully refined sugar with no molasses content
