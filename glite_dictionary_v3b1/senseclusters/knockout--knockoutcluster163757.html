knockout

noun
organism with inactivated gene
7.5 0.28/m
Pronunciation: / \u02c8n\u0251\u02d0k\u02cca\u028at / (AmE), / \u02c8n\u0252k\u02cca\u028at / (BrE)
(in genetics and biomedical research) a living organism in which a specific gene has been deliberately inactivated or 'knocked out' to study that gene's function.

Examples

Knockout mouse
The researchers created a knockout mouse to study the gene's role in metabolism.
After breeding, they observed that the knockout showed no normal immune response to infection.
Using CRISPR, she generated a knockout line to confirm the protein's function in liver cells.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gene knockout - the deliberate inactivation or removal of a specific gene in an organism for study
  • antonym: wild type - an organism that has the typical form of a gene or phenotype found in nature; opposite of a genetic knockout
