Synonyms

  • traffic circle
    2.8 0.10/m
    traffic circle is a circular junction with many entry and exit points, while roundabout is a junction where traffic moves around a central island.
  • rotary
    4.3 0.10/m
    rotary is a one-way circular junction term often used as an alternative, while roundabout is the common general name.
  • circle
    3.5 3/m
    circle is a short name for a junction where traffic moves one way around an island, while roundabout is the usual full term.
  • gyratory
    7.5 0.00/m
    gyratory is a large, often complex system of one-way roads in cities, while roundabout is a single circular junction.
