interpretational

adjective
about performance style
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌtɜːrprəˈteɪʃənl̩ / (AmE), / ɪnˌtɜːprɪˈteɪʃənl̩ / (BrE)
relating to the way something is performed, presented, or understood, especially in art, music, or drama

Examples

Interpretational style
The actor's interpretational choices gave the character a unique personality.
Different conductors bring their own interpretational styles to the same piece of music.
Her interpretational approach to the poem made it feel modern and relevant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interpretive - relating to explaining or presenting something in a particular way
  • antonym: objective - not influenced by personal feelings or interpretation
