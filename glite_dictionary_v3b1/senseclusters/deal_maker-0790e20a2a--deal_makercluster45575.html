deal maker

noun
skilled negotiator
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːl ˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈdiːl ˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who is skilled at arranging agreements or business deals, especially in business or politics

Examples

A skilled deal maker
The company brought in a skilled deal maker to handle the complex negotiations.
As a natural deal maker, she quickly secured several important contracts for her firm.
In politics, being a successful deal maker can help pass important legislation.
