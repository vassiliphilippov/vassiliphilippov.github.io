Lakeland

proper noun
tourist region in northwestern England
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪklənd / (AmE), / ˈleɪklənd / (BrE)
a region in northwestern England known for its lakes, mountains, and scenic beauty, popular with tourists; sometimes refers specifically to the Lake District.
