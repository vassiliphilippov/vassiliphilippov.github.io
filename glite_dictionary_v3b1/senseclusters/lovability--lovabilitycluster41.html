lovability

noun
quality of being lovable
Pronunciation: / ˌlʌvəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌlʌvəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being easy to love or deserving of affection

Examples

Natural lovability
His natural lovability made him the favorite among his classmates.
The puppy's lovability was obvious from the moment she wagged her tail.
Many admired her lovability and warm personality at the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: charm - a quality that makes someone attractive or pleasant
  • antonym: unattractiveness - the quality of not being attractive or appealing
