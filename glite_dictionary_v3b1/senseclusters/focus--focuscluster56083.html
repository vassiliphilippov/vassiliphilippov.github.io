focus

noun
concentration of attention or effort
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊ.kəs / (AmE), / ˈfəʊ.kəs / (BrE)
concentration of attention or special emphasis on a particular subject or activity

Examples

Mental focus
Meditation can help improve your mental focus and reduce stress.
She lost her focus during the long meeting and missed important details.
The main focus of the conference is climate change.
In order to succeed, we need to shift our focus from quantity to quality.
Recent discussions have brought a new focus on climate change policies.
The manager placed focus on improving customer satisfaction this quarter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: distraction - something that takes attention away from focus
  • synonym: emphasis - special importance, value, or prominence given to something
