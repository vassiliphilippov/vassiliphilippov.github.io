rankly

adverb
blatantly or strongly
Pronunciation: / ˈræŋkli / (AmE), / ˈræŋkli / (BrE)
in a strong, obvious, or extreme manner, often in a way that is unpleasant or offensive

Examples

Smells rankly bad
The garbage rankly filled the air with its foul odor.
He was rankly dishonest about his intentions from the start.
The flowers grew rankly in the abandoned garden, taking over every corner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blatantly - in an obvious and open way
  • antonym: subtly - in a delicate or not obvious way
