fruitarian

noun
person eating only fruit
Pronunciation: / fruːˈtɛriən / (AmE), / fruːˈtɛːrɪən / (BrE)
a person whose diet consists mainly or exclusively of fruit, nuts, and seeds

Examples

A strict fruitarian
As a fruitarian, she avoids all animal products and most vegetables.
The fruitarian diet requires careful planning to ensure proper nutrition.
He became a fruitarian after learning about the environmental impact of other diets.

Related Terms

  • antonym: omnivore - a person or animal that eats both plants and animals
  • synonym: vegan - a person who does not eat or use animal products
