fuze

noun
igniter for propellants or munitions
5.7
Pronunciation: / fjuz / (AmE), / fjuːz / (BrE)
an igniter used to initiate the burning of a propellant, typically in munitions or explosives

Examples

A fuze in a bomb
Every rocket includes a special fuze to ignite its propellant safely.
The artillery shell was equipped with a timed fuze to ensure it detonated at the right moment.
Without a functioning fuze, the projectile would not have exploded upon impact.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fuse - a device for igniting explosives or propellants
