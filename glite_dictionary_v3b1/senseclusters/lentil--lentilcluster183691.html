lentil

noun
edible seed or plant (Lens culinaris)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛn.təl / (AmE), / ˈlɛn.təl /; / ˈlɛn.tɪl / (BrE)
a small, round, edible seed of a plant (or the plant itself), commonly used as food
