tee

verb
to place a ball on a tee
9.3
Pronunciation: / tiː / (AmE), / tiː / (BrE)
to place (a ball) on a tee in preparation for striking it, especially in golf or tee-ball

Examples

To tee the ball
Coaches taught the kids how to tee the ball properly in tee-ball.
Golfers must always tee their ball up at the start of each hole.
She carefully teed the ball before taking her first swing.
