product

noun
result of an action or circumstances
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːdəkt /; / ˈprɑdʌkt / (AmE), / ˈprɒdʌkt / (BrE)
something that results from a process, action, or chemical reaction

Examples

Product of hard work
Her confidence is the product of years of experience in the field.
The argument was the product of a misunderstanding between the two friends.
This feeling of satisfaction is often the product of hard work and perseverance.
The product formed in this experiment is a white crystalline solid.
The teacher asked the students to identify the product of mixing hydrochloric acid with sodium hydroxide.
Water is a common product of many combustion reactions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reactant - a substance that takes part in and undergoes change during a reaction
  • synonym: result - something that happens because of something else
