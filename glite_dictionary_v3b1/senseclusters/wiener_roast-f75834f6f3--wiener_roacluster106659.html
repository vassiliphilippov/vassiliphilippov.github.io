wiener roast

noun
outdoor cookout with roasted frankfurters
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːnər roʊst / (AmE), / ˈwiːnə rəʊst / (BrE)
an informal outdoor party where frankfurters (hot dogs) are cooked over an open fire and eaten as the main dish

Examples

A wiener roast party
Every summer, the park hosts a community wiener roast for local families.
The children gathered sticks for the wiener roast as the sun began to set.
We decided to have a wiener roast at the lake with our friends last Saturday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barbecue - outdoor meal where food is cooked over an open fire or grill
  • synonym: picnic - outdoor meal, usually informal and with prepared food
