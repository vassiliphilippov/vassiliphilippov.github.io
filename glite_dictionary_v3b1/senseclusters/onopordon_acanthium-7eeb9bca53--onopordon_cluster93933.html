Onopordon acanthium

noun
a Eurasian thistle; cotton thistle
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɑnəˈpɔrdən əˈkænθiəm / (AmE), / ˌɒnəˈpɔːdən əˈkanθɪəm / (BrE)
A biennial Eurasian thistle with white hairy leaves and pale purple flowers, also known as cotton thistle, and naturalized in North America.
