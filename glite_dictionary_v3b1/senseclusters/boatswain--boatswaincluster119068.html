A ship's officer in charge of equipment and the crew who maintain it, especially on a merchant vessel.

Examples

The boatswain on ship
After years at sea, he was promoted to boatswain on a large cargo vessel.
During the storm, the boatswain ensured all the ropes were securely fastened.
The boatswain supervised the crew as they prepared the ship for departure.
