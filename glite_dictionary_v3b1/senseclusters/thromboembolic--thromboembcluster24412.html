thromboembolic

adjective
blood clot blockage
Pronunciation: / ˌθrɑːmboʊɛmˈbɑːlɪk / (AmE), / ˌθrɒmbəʊɛmˈbɒlɪk / (BrE)
relating to or caused by a blood clot that blocks a blood vessel

Examples

Thromboembolic event
The doctor explained that a thromboembolic event can be life-threatening if not treated quickly.
Patients with certain heart conditions are at higher risk for thromboembolic complications.
Preventing thromboembolic disease is important after major surgery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embolic - relating to an embolus or blockage in a blood vessel
  • synonym: thrombotic - relating to the formation of blood clots
