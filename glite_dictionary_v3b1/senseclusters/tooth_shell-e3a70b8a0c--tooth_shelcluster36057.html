tooth shell

noun
marine mollusk with tapering tubular shell
3.5 8.0
Pronunciation: /tuːθ ʃel/ /; / /tʊθ ʃel/ (AmE), /tuːθ ʃel/ /; / /tʊθ ʃel/ (BrE)
