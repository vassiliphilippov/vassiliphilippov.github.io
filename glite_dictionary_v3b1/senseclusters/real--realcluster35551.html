Synonyms

  • actual

    actual describes facts or events that exist in fact and are not imagined, while real is broader and more general.
  • genuine
    2.0 15/m
    genuine describes something honest and not fake, often showing true feeling, while real is broader and can mean actually existing.
  • authentic
    3.8 20/m
    authentic is used for objects or stories proved original and trustworthy, while real is a more general word for not fake.
