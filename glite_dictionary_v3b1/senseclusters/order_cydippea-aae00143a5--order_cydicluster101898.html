order Cydippea

noun
order of ctenophores with long pinnate tentacles
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər sɪˈdɪpiə / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə sɪˈdɪpiə / (BrE)
a taxonomic order of ctenophores characterized by having two long, feather-like (pinnate) tentacles.

Examples

Members of the order Cydippea are distinguished by their two prominent tentacles.
