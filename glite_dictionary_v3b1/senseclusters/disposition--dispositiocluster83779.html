Synonyms

  • temperament
    4.3 5.62/m
    temperament is a person's usual emotional nature, while disposition also includes mood and a wider natural tendency to act or think.
  • propensity
    7.3 4.66/m
    propensity is a natural, often strong tendency to behave in certain ways, while disposition is a broader usual mood or tendency.
  • predisposition
    7.1 1.27/m
    predisposition is a tendency often based on prior factors to react in certain ways, while disposition is a person's usual mood.
  • inclination
    4.2 6.09/m
    inclination is a gentle want or preference that guides choices, while disposition is a broader, usual mood or way of reacting.
  • tendency
    2.8 31/m
    tendency is a general likelihood to act or happen over time, while disposition refers to a person's usual mood or nature.
