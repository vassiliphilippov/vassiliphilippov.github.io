absorb

verb
take on or pay for a cost or debt
Pronunciation: / əbˈzɔrb / (AmE), / əbˈzɔːb / (BrE)
(of a person or organization) to take on or pay for a cost, debt, or payment, especially one initially incurred by someone else.

Examples

To absorb costs
Our department had to absorb the budget cuts imposed by head office.
The company will absorb the medical expenses for all its employees.
The government decided to absorb the additional costs resulting from the new regulations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bear - to accept or take responsibility for (e.g., a cost); to carry
