bro

interjection
informal exclamation
Pronunciation: / broʊ / (AmE), / brəʊ / (BrE)
used as a casual exclamation or form of address to a friend, often to express surprise, emphasis, or sympathy

Examples

Bro, listen
Hey bro, did you see the game last night?
Bro, I can't believe you actually did that!
When he heard the bad news, he just said, 'bro, that's rough.'

Related Terms

  • synonym: dude - informal term for a man or friend
  • synonym: mate - informal term for a friend, especially in British English
