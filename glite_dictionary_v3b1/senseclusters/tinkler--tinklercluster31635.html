tinkler

noun
casual musician
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtɪŋ.klər/ /; / /ˈtɪŋ.kə.lər/ (AmE), /ˈtɪŋ.kə/ (BrE)
a person who plays a musical instrument, especially the piano or small bells, in a light and casual manner

Examples

A piano tinkler
As a child, she was a tinkler on the piano, playing simple tunes for her family.
The party was lively with a tinkler entertaining guests on the bells.
He never took lessons but enjoyed being a tinkler at local gatherings.
