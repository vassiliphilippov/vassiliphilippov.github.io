blue

noun
small butterfly of the family Lycaenidae
Pronunciation: / bluː / (AmE), / bluː / (BrE)
Any of various small butterflies of the family Lycaenidae, often having iridescent blue coloring on their wings.

Examples

A blue butterfly
A common blue landed gently on the child's outstretched hand.
Gardeners often spot blues among the wildflowers each summer.
The meadow was dotted with blues fluttering from flower to flower.
