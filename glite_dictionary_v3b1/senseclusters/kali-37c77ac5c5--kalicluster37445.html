Kali

proper noun
Hindu goddess of destruction and rebirth
8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑː.liː/ /; / /ˈkæ.liː/ /; / /ˈkeɪ.liː/ (AmE), /ˈkɑː.liː/ /; / /ˈkæ.liː/ /; / /ˈkeɪ.liː/ (BrE)
In Hinduism, a goddess associated with destruction and rebirth, often depicted as a fierce and powerful form of Devi and the consort of Shiva.

Examples

Kali goddess
Kali represents the power of time and change in Hindu mythology.
During the festival of Navaratri, many devotees worship Kali with elaborate rituals.
The image of Kali is often depicted with a garland of skulls and a fierce expression.
