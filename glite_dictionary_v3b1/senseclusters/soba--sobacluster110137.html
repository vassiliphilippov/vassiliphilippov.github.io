soba

noun
Japanese noodle shop
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊbə / (AmE), / ˈsəʊbə / (BrE)
a Japanese restaurant or shop that specializes in serving soba noodles, which are thin noodles made from buckwheat flour

Examples

Soba restaurant
We decided to have lunch at a small soba near the train station.
The soba was famous for its handmade buckwheat noodles and light broth.
After sightseeing, they stopped by a traditional soba for a quick meal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: noodle bar - a casual restaurant serving various noodle dishes
  • synonym: ramen shop - a restaurant specializing in ramen noodles
