ungroomed

adjective
not neat or tidy in appearance
5.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡruːmd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡruːmd / (BrE)
not neat or tidy in appearance; not having been made neat, tidy, or stylish.

Examples

An ungroomed appearance
His ungroomed appearance made him seem older and more tired than he was.
She felt embarrassed by her ungroomed hair at the formal event.
The children returned home from camp looking ungroomed and happily exhausted.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neat - tidy and in order, especially in appearance
  • synonym: unkempt - not tidy or cared for; messy in appearance
