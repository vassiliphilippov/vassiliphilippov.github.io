forested

adjective
covered with forests
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔr.ə.stɪd / (AmE), / ˈfɒr.ɪ.stɪd / (BrE)
(of land or an area) covered with forests or dense woodland.

Examples

Forested land
Deforestation has dramatically reduced the once forested areas across the continent.
Many animals thrive in forested habitats where they find shelter and food.
The mountain region is densely forested and difficult to access.
