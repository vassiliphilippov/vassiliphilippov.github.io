hypoallergenic

adjective
reduces allergies
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpoʊˌælərˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəʊˌaləˈdʒenɪk / (BrE)
designed to cause fewer allergic reactions than normal or to minimize the risk of allergies

Examples

Hypoallergenic soap
Many people with sensitive skin prefer hypoallergenic soaps to avoid irritation.
The hotel offers hypoallergenic bedding for guests with allergies.
She chose a hypoallergenic cat breed because her son is allergic to most animals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: allergenic - likely to cause allergic reactions
  • synonym: nonallergenic - not causing allergic reactions
