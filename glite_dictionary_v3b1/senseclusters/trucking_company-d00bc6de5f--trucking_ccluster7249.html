trucking company

noun
business transporting goods by truck
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌkɪŋ ˌkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˈtrʌkɪŋ ˌkʌmpəni / (BrE)
a business that transports goods or possessions by truck, typically for clients over various distances

Examples

A trucking company business
Several small businesses rely on a local trucking company to handle their shipments.
She started working for a trucking company after getting her commercial driver's license.
The trucking company delivered the furniture to our new office ahead of schedule.

Related Terms

  • synonym: haulage company - a company that transports goods by road or rail, especially in British English
