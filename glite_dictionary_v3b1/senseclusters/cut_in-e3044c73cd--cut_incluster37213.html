cut in

verb
mix ingredients using a cutting motion
6.1
Pronunciation: / kʌt ɪn / (AmE), / kʌt ɪn / (BrE)
to mix a solid fat into flour or other dry ingredients using a cutting motion, often with a knife or pastry blender, until the mixture has a crumbly texture

Examples

To cut in butter
Add the butter to the flour and cut in until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
She cut in the shortening with a pastry blender to make the dough light and flaky.
Using two knives, you can cut in the lard quickly, just as the recipe instructs.
