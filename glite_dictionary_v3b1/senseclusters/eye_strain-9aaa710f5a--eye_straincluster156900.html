eye strain

noun
discomfort in the eyes from overuse
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ streɪn / (AmE), / ˈaɪ streɪn / (BrE)
Discomfort or fatigue of the eyes caused by prolonged use or intensive focus, especially on screens or small details.

Examples

Eye strain after reading
Many people experience eye strain after reading on their phones for too long.
Staring at the computer for hours gave her severe eye strain.
Wearing proper lighting can help prevent eye strain when working late.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fatigue - physical or mental weariness resulting from effort or overuse
