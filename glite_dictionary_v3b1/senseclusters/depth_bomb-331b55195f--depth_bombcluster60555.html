depth bomb

noun
underwater bomb for attacking submarines
Pronunciation: / dɛpθ bɑm / (AmE), / dɛpθ bɒm / (BrE)
a bomb designed to explode at a specific depth underwater, used to attack submarines

Examples

A depth bomb attack
Engineers are testing a new type of depth bomb for increased accuracy.
Old war documentaries often show ships deploying depth bombs during maritime battles.
The navy dropped a depth bomb to target the submarine hidden beneath the waves.

Related Terms
