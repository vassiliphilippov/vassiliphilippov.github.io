maladapted

adjective
poorly adjusted
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌmæl.əˈdæp.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌmæl.əˈdæp.tɪd / (BrE)
not well-suited or adjusted to a particular environment, situation, or set of conditions

Examples

Maladapted species
Some species become maladapted when their habitats change rapidly due to climate shifts.
He felt maladapted to the fast-paced city life after years in the countryside.
A business can become maladapted if it fails to respond to new market trends.
