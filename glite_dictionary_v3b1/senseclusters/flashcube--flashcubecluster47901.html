flashcube

noun
camera flash device
Pronunciation: / ˈflæʃˌkjuːb / (AmE), / ˈflæʃˌkjuːb / (BrE)
a small cube-shaped device with several flashbulbs, used on older cameras to provide light for photographs

Examples

A flashcube device
Before digital cameras, photographers often used a flashcube to take pictures indoors.
He searched his drawer for a spare flashcube before the family gathering.
The old camera still had a flashcube attached to the top.
The old camera required a flashcube to take pictures indoors.
He found a vintage flashcube in his grandfather's photography collection.
After each photo, the flashcube rotated to use a new bulb.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flashbulb - a single-use bulb that produces a flash of light for photography
  • synonym: strobe - a device that produces regular flashes of light, often used in photography
