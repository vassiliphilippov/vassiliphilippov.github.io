unpouched

adjective
without a pouch
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpaʊtʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈpaʊtʃt / (BrE)
not having a pouch, pocket, or compartment (used for animals, clothing, bags, etc.)

Examples

An unpouched animal
Some marsupials are born unpouched and develop outside the mother's pouch.
The unpouched species relies on nests rather than carrying its young.
Unlike kangaroos, certain animals remain unpouched throughout their lives.
The designer released an unpouched handbag for those who prefer a minimalist look.
He wore an unpouched jacket that offered a sleek, uninterrupted appearance.
Some athletes choose unpouched shorts for greater comfort during competitions.
