military engineer

noun
armed forces engineer specializing in military works
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri ˌɛn(d)ʒəˈnɪr / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪt(ə)ri ˌɛn(d)ʒɪˈnɪə / (BrE)
a member of the armed forces who is trained in engineering, especially in construction, demolition, and maintenance of military works and facilities

Examples

Military engineer clears mines
As a military engineer, she specialized in clearing minefields and designing defensive structures.
Many military engineers are skilled in both civil and combat engineering tasks.
The military engineer supervised the construction of bridges and fortifications during the campaign.
