súgán

noun
Irish straw chair
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːɡɑːn / (AmE), / ˈsuːɡɑːn / (BrE)
a simple chair or stool with a seat made from woven straw rope, traditional in Ireland

Examples

A súgán chair
The old cottage still had a súgán by the fireplace, just as it did a century ago.
He repaired the seat of the súgán using fresh straw rope from the farm.
Visitors admired the handmade súgán for its rustic charm and traditional craftsmanship.

Related Terms

  • antonym: armchair - a comfortable chair with side supports for a person's arms
  • synonym: stool - a simple seat without a back or arms
