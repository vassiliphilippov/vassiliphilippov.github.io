vaingloriously

adverb
with excessive pride
9.3
Pronunciation: / veɪnˈɡlɔːriəsli / (AmE), / veɪnˈɡlɔːriəsli / (BrE)
in a way that shows too much pride in one's achievements or appearance

Examples

To speak vaingloriously
He spoke vaingloriously about his role in the project, taking credit for everything.
The athlete vaingloriously celebrated his victory, ignoring his teammates' contributions.
She vaingloriously displayed her awards, hoping to impress everyone at the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boastfully - in a proud or bragging manner
  • antonym: modestly - in a humble or unassuming way
