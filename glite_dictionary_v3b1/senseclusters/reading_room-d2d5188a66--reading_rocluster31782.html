reading room

noun
a room designated for reading
Pronunciation: / ˈriːdɪŋ ruːm / (AmE), / ˈriːdɪŋ ruːm / (BrE)
a room, typically in a library, reserved for the purpose of reading or quiet study

Examples

A reading room in library
Many museums offer a reading room for visitors who want to examine archival materials.
Please remain silent while you are in the reading room.
The university's new reading room is filled with natural light and comfortable chairs.
