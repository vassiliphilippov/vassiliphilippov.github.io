Ch'ing dynasty

proper noun
last imperial dynasty of China (1644–1912)
8.3
Pronunciation: /tʃɪŋ ˈdaɪ.nə.stiː/ (AmE), /tʃɪŋ ˈdɪn.ə.stiː/ (BrE)
