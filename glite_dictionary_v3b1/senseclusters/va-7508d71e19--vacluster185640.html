VA

noun
U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs (abbreviation)
Pronunciation: / ˌviː ˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌviː ˈeɪ / (BrE)
abbreviation for the United States Department of Veterans Affairs, the federal department responsible for the interests of military veterans.

Examples

VA hospital
After his service, he applied for assistance from the VA.
My grandfather receives medical benefits through the VA.
The VA hospital in our city provides excellent care for veterans.
