goji

noun
Asian berry shrub
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈɡoʊ.dʒiː/ (AmE), /ˈɡəʊ.dʒiː/ (BrE)
a spiny shrub native to Asia, especially Lycium barbarum, cultivated for its small red fruit called goji berries

Examples

Goji shrub

A mature goji can produce hundreds of bright red berries each season.
