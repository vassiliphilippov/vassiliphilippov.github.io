gunsel

noun
criminal's assistant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌnsəl / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnsəl / (BrE)
a slang term for a young man who acts as a criminal's assistant or gunman

Examples

The gunsel waited
The gunsel waited nervously outside the boss's office.
Police arrested the notorious gunsel after the robbery downtown.
In old gangster movies, a gunsel often follows the main villain everywhere.
The detective suspected the gunsel was carrying a hidden weapon.
Two gunsels waited outside the club, keeping an eye on the entrance.
He started as a gunsel before rising through the ranks of the gang.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boss - the leader of a criminal group
  • synonym: henchman - a loyal assistant to a criminal or powerful person
