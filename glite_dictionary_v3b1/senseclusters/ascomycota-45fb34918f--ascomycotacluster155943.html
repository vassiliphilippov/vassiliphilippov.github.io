Ascomycota

noun
major division of sac fungi
Pronunciation: / ˌæskəˈmaɪkətə / (AmE), / ˌæskəʊˈmaɪkətə / (BrE)
a large group of fungi (sac fungi) that produce spores in sac-like structures called asci; includes molds, yeasts, and morels
