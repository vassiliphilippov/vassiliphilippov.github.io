Dalcroze

proper noun
music teaching method
Pronunciation: / dælˈkroʊz / (AmE), / dælˈkrəʊz / (BrE)
a method of teaching music that uses movement and physical activity to develop musical understanding

Examples

The Dalcroze method
Many schools incorporate Dalcroze activities to help children experience rhythm through movement.
The Dalcroze method encourages students to use their bodies to understand musical concepts.
Teachers trained in Dalcroze often lead classes where students clap, walk, and dance to music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Kodály - a music education method focusing on singing and folk music
  • synonym: Orff - a music teaching approach using instruments and movement
