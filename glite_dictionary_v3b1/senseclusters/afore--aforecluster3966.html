afore

conjunction
archaic before
9.9 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / …ôÀàf…îr / (AmE), / …ôÀàf…îÀê / (BrE)
before (in time); used to introduce a clause referring to an earlier event, now archaic or dialectal.

Examples

Afore I arrived
She left the house afore I had time to say goodbye to her.
Finish your chores afore you go out to play.
The sun had not risen afore the travelers began their journey.
