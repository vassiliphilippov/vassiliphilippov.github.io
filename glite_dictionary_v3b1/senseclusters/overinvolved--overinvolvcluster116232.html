overinvolved

adjective
excessively complicated
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɑlvd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪnˈvɒlvd / (BrE)
containing too many elements, details, or parts, making something unnecessarily complicated or difficult to follow

Examples

Critics said the film's overinvolved narrative distracted from its main message.
