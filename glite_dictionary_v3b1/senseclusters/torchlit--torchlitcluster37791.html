torchlit

adjective
lit by torches
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔrtʃˌlɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɔːtʃˌlɪt / (BrE)
illuminated or lighted by the light of torches

Examples

Torchlit castle walls
The ancient castle was torchlit during the evening festival, creating a magical atmosphere.
They walked down the torchlit corridor, shadows flickering on the stone walls.
A torchlit parade made its way through the village streets on the night of the celebration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: candlelit - illuminated by the light of candles
  • antonym: dark - having little or no light
