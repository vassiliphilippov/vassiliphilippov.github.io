swallow-tailed kite

noun
North American raptor with forked tail
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑːloʊ teɪld kaɪt / (AmE), / ˈswɒləʊ teɪld kaɪt / (BrE)
a graceful North American bird of prey with a deeply forked tail and black-and-white plumage

Examples

A swallow-tailed kite bird
A swallow-tailed kite hunts for insects by gliding gracefully through the treetops.
The distinctive forked tail makes the swallow-tailed kite easy to identify in flight.
We spotted a swallow-tailed kite soaring above the river, its tail streaming behind it.
