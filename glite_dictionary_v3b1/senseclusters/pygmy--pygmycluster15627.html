pygmy

noun
an unusually small person or thing
5.6 6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪɡmi / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡmi / (BrE)

Examples

The species has a pygmy variant that rarely grows over 40 centimeters tall.
