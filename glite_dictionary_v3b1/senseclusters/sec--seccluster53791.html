sec

noun
one second (unit of time)
1.0
Pronunciation: / sɛk / (AmE), / sɛk / (BrE)
A second; the basic unit of time equal to 1/60 of a minute, recognized under the International System of Units (SI).

Examples

A few sec
He finished the race in just five sec.
Please wait a few sec before opening the file.
The timer was set for thirty sec to complete the task.
