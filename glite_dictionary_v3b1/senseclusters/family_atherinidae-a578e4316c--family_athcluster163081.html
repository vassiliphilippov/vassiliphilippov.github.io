family Atherinidae

noun
family of small spiny-finned fishes
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli æθəˈrɪnɪˌdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli æθəˈrɪnɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of small spiny-finned fishes found in both salt and fresh water, commonly known as silversides.
