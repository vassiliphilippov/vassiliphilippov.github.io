sideways

adverb
from or toward the side; obliquely
3.4 2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdˌweɪz / (AmE), / ˈsaɪd.weɪz / (BrE)
in or toward the side; with a side facing forward instead of straight ahead
