Guangzhou

proper noun
Chinese port city and Guangdong capital
Pronunciation: / ɡwɑŋˈdʒoʊ / (AmE), / ɡwæŋˈdʒəʊ / (BrE)
a city in southern China, the capital of Guangdong province, located on the Zhu Jiang (Pearl River) delta and known as a major port and commercial center
