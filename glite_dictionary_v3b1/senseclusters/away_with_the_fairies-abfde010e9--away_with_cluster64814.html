away with the fairies

adjective
lost in daydreams; distracted
9.0
Pronunciation: / əˈweɪ wɪð ðə ˈfɛriz / (AmE), / əˈweɪ wɪð ðə ˈfeəriːz / (BrE)
in a state of being distracted or lost in daydreams or fantasies, appearing inattentive to the present situation
