amity

noun
friendly or peaceful relationship
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈæm.ə.ti /; / ˈæmət̬i / (AmE), / ˈæm.ɪ.ti /; / ˈæmɪti / (BrE)
a friendly and peaceful relationship; mutual harmony between people, groups, or nations

Examples

Lasting amity between nations
The peace treaty was intended to ensure lasting amity among the neighboring nations.
The two countries have lived in amity for many years, setting an example for the region.
Their amity was apparent in the way they supported each other at work.
A spirit of amity prevailed among the members of the international team.
The two countries lived in amity after signing the peace treaty.
Their decades-long amity was admired by all their friends.

  • synonym: friendship - the emotions or conduct of friends; the state of being friends
