queasy

adjective
feeling sick or nauseous
4.1 1.44/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàkwiÀêzi / (AmE), / ÀàkwiÀêzi / (BrE)
feeling sick or likely to vomit, or causing this feeling

Examples

Queasy stomach
After the rollercoaster ride, she looked pale and a bit queasy.
He grew queasy just thinking about getting a shot.
The smell of the spoiled food made me feel queasy instantly.
A queasy look crossed his face as he watched the gruesome scene on television.
She felt a queasy sensation in her stomach after smelling the spoiled milk.
The queasy odor from the garbage made everyone leave the room.
