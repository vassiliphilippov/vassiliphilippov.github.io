Freesia

noun
genus of South African flowering plants
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːʒə / (AmE), / ˈfriːzɪə / (BrE)
a genus of cormous perennial herbs native to South Africa, known for their fragrant, funnel-shaped flowers

Examples

Freesia flowers
Freesia varieties are popular as cut flowers due to their long-lasting scent.
Many gardeners plant Freesia bulbs in the autumn for spring blooms.
The Freesia is prized for its delicate fragrance and bright colors.
