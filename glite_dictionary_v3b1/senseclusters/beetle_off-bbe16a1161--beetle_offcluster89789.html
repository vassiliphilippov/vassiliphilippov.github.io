beetle off

verb
leave quickly or suddenly
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtəl ɒf / (AmE), / ˈbiːtl ɒf / (BrE)
to leave quickly and suddenly, usually without attracting attention

Examples

To beetle off quickly
As soon as the meeting ended, he beetled off to catch his train.
She beetled off without saying goodbye, leaving everyone puzzled.
The children beetled off the moment they heard their mother calling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dash off - to leave somewhere in a hurry
  • synonym: run off - to leave a place or person quickly and unexpectedly
