melanocyte

noun
skin cell producing melanin
9.8
Pronunciation: / məˈlænəˌsaɪt / (AmE), / mɪˈlanəˌsaɪt / (BrE)
a cell in the skin's basal layer that produces the pigment melanin, primarily responsible for skin color

Examples

Skin melanocyte cell
A deficiency of melanocyte cells can lead to conditions such as vitiligo.
Exposure to sunlight stimulates melanocyte activity, resulting in a darker skin tone.
The dermatologist explained how melanocyte function is crucial for skin pigmentation.

  • synonym: melanin - the pigment produced by melanocytes
