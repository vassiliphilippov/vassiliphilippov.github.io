powdery-blue

adjective
moderate to pale blue color
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊ.dɚ.i bluː / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.dəri bluː / (BrE)
of a moderate to pale blue or purplish blue; resembling the color of powdered blue pigments

Examples

A powdery-blue butterfly
A powdery-blue butterfly landed gently on the leaf.
She painted her bedroom walls a calming powdery-blue to create a peaceful atmosphere.
The bridesmaids all wore powdery-blue dresses that complemented the spring flowers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sky blue - a light shade of blue, like a clear sky
