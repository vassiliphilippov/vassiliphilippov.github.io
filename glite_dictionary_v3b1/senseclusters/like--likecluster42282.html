like

noun
preferences or favorites
Pronunciation: / laɪk / (AmE), / laɪk / (BrE)
things that a person enjoys, prefers, or finds pleasing

Examples

His likes and dislikes
He listed his likes and dislikes on his social media profile.
Traveling and photography are among her many likes.
When choosing a gift, consider the person's likes and interests.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dislike - something that a person does not enjoy or prefers to avoid
  • synonym: preference - something that is liked or chosen over others
