particularist

adjective
focused on specifics
Pronunciation: / pərˈtɪkjələrɪst / (AmE), / pəˈtɪkjʊlərɪst / (BrE)
giving importance to specific cases or individual circumstances rather than general principles or rules

Examples

Particularist approach
Her particularist view led her to consider each student's situation before making a decision.
The committee adopted a particularist approach, focusing on the unique needs of each community.
A particularist perspective can help address exceptions that general rules might overlook.

Related Terms

  • synonym: individualistic - emphasizing individual cases or needs
  • antonym: universalist - favoring general principles over specific cases
