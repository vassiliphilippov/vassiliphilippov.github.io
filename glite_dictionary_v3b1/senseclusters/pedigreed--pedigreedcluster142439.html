pedigreed

adjective
having documented purebred ancestry
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛdɪˌɡrid / (AmE), / ˈpɛdɪɡriːd / (BrE)
(of an animal) having a recorded and recognized ancestry, showing that it is purebred.

Examples

Pedigreed dogs
Only pedigreed dogs are allowed to participate in this competition.
She takes pride in breeding pedigreed cats with outstanding lineages.
The pedigreed horse was entered in the national show for thoroughbreds.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mongrel - an animal, especially a dog, of no definable type or breed
  • synonym: purebred - of an animal, bred from parents of the same breed or variety
