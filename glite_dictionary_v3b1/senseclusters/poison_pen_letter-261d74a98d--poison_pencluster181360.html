poison pen letter

noun
malicious anonymous letter
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔɪzən pɛn ˈlɛtər / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪzən pɛn ˈlɛtə / (BrE)
an anonymous or unsigned letter written with the intention of harming, insulting, or defaming the recipient

Examples

A poison pen letter
He was shocked to find a poison pen letter tucked into his mailbox.
Sending a poison pen letter is a cowardly way to express anger.
The celebrity was deeply upset after receiving a poison pen letter criticizing her family.
