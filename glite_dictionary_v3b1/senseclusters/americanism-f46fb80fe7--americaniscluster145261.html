Americanism

noun
word or feature unique to American English
9.9 5.9
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈmer.ɪ.kə.nɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ə.ˈme.rɪ.kə.nɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
A word, phrase, or feature that is characteristic of American English, especially as distinguished from other varieties of English.

Examples

A common Americanism

Many Americanisms have found their way into international English.
The word 'sidewalk' is an Americanism for what the British call a 'pavement'.
