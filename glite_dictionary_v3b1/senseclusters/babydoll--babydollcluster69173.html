babydoll

noun
term of endearment
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪbiˌdɑl / (AmE), / ˈbeɪbiˌdɒl / (BrE)
an informal term of endearment used to address an attractive young woman or romantic partner

Examples

Hey, babydoll
He smiled and said, "Good morning, babydoll, did you sleep well?"
Whenever she walked into the room, he would greet her with a cheerful "babydoll!"
Don't worry, babydoll, everything is going to be just fine.

  • synonym: sweetheart - a person loved by someone; a term of affection
