crochet

noun
needlework using a hooked needle
3.1
Pronunciation: / kroʊˈʃeɪ / (AmE), / krəʊˈʃeɪ / (BrE)
Needlework made by forming interlocking loops of yarn, thread, or other material with a hooked needle.

Examples

Handmade crochet blanket
