three-way switch

noun
electrical switch for controlling a circuit from two places
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈθriː weɪ ˌswɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈθriː weɪ ˌswɪtʃ / (BrE)
an electrical switch with three terminals, used to control a circuit from two different locations

Examples

A three-way switch circuit
A three-way switch helps control lights from multiple locations in a room.
Electricians often use a three-way switch in stairwells for convenience.
We installed a three-way switch so the hallway light could be turned on from either end.
