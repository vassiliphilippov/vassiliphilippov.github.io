hayride

noun
easy experience
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪˌraɪd / (AmE), / ˈheɪˌraɪd / (BrE)
an easy, pleasant, or carefree experience, often used figuratively to describe something simple or enjoyable

Examples

An easy hayride
Finishing the project was no hayride, but the team managed to succeed.
He thought the exam would be a hayride, but it turned out to be quite challenging.
Don't expect your first year at the new job to be a hayride.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cakewalk - something very easy to do
  • antonym: ordeal - a difficult or unpleasant experience
