unreimbursed

adjective
not paid back
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnriːɪmˈbɜrst / (AmE), / ˌʌnriːɪmˈbɜːst / (BrE)
not having been paid back for money spent or costs incurred

Examples

Unreimbursed travel expenses
The company refused to cover his unreimbursed travel expenses from last month.
She kept all receipts for unreimbursed medical costs to claim them later.
Many employees are frustrated by the amount of unreimbursed work-related expenses they have each year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reimbursed - having received repayment for expenses
  • synonym: unpaid - not yet paid or settled
