three-martini lunch

noun
indulgent lunch with several martinis
5.7
Pronunciation: / θri mɑrˈtini lʌntʃ / (AmE), / θriː mɑːˈtiːni lʌntʃ / (BrE)
a leisurely lunch, typically in a business context, during which several martinis are consumed, often implying indulgence and relaxation

Examples

A three-martini lunch meeting
Her first day on the job included a memorable three-martini lunch with her new colleagues.
Stories of the three-martini lunch evoke images of the business culture of the 1960s and 70s.
The executives negotiated deals over a three-martini lunch at an upscale bistro.

Related Terms

  • synonym: liquid lunch - a lunch during which alcohol is consumed instead of or as well as food
