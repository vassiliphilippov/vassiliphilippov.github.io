loose lip

noun
talkative or indiscreet person
Pronunciation: / luːs lɪp / (AmE), / luːs lɪp / (BrE)
a person who is talkative or indiscreet, often revealing information that should be kept secret

Examples

A loose lip talks
A single loose lip can jeopardize an entire team's efforts if sensitive information is leaked.
As a manager, he struggled to deal with Mark's loose lip during confidential discussions.
Her loose lip nearly ruined the surprise party by mentioning it to the wrong person.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blabbermouth - a person who talks too much or reveals secrets
  • antonym: tight-lipped - not willing to speak or reveal information
