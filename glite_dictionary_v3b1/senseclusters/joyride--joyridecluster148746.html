joyride

noun
pleasure ride, often unauthorized or reckless
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔɪˌraɪd / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪraɪd / (BrE)
a ride taken in a vehicle purely for pleasure, often without the owner's permission and sometimes illegally or recklessly

Examples

A stolen car joyride
After reporting his car stolen, he discovered it had been taken on a joyride.
The police caught the suspects after a high-speed joyride through the city.
The teenagers went for a joyride in their parents' new car.
