nonpotable

adjective
not drinkable
6.1
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈpoʊtəbl̩ / (AmE), / nɒnˈpəʊtəbl̩ / (BrE)
not safe or suitable for drinking

Examples

Nonpotable water
The campsite provided only nonpotable water, so visitors had to bring their own drinking water.
A warning label indicated that the liquid in the container was nonpotable and should not be consumed.
Due to contamination, the tap water in the area was declared nonpotable until further notice.

Related Terms

  • antonym: potable - safe to drink
  • synonym: undrinkable - not fit to be drunk
