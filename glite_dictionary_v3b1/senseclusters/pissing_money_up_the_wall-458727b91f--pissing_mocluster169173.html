pissing money up the wall

verb
waste money carelessly
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪsɪŋ ˈmʌni ʌp ðə wɔl / (AmE), / ˈpɪsɪŋ ˈmʌni ʌp ðə wɔːl / (BrE)
to waste money in a careless or foolish way, spending without any benefit or sensible outcome

Examples

To piss money up the wall
If you buy that expensive gadget, you're just pissing money up the wall.
She realized she'd been pissing money up the wall on new clothes every month.
They accused the government of pissing money up the wall with inefficient programs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: save money - to keep money instead of spending it
  • synonym: waste money - to spend money carelessly or needlessly
