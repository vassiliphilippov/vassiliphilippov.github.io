umbilical

adjective
Of or relating to the navel or umbilical cord
Pronunciation: / ʌmˈbɪlɪkəl / (AmE), / ʌmˈbɪlɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling the umbilicus (navel) or the umbilical cord.

Examples

Umbilical cord care
Newborns are often monitored for umbilical infections after the cord falls off.
The fetus receives nutrients through the umbilical cord during pregnancy.
The surgeon made an umbilical incision to access the abdominal cavity.
