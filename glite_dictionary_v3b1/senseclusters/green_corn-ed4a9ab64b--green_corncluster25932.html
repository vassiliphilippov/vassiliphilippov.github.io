green corn

noun
corn eaten while young and soft
6.6
Pronunciation: / ɡrin kɔrn / (AmE), / ɡriːn kɔːn / (BrE)
corn that is picked and eaten while it is still young, soft, and not fully ripe (used as a vegetable)
