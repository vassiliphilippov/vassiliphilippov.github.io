Spencerian

noun
expert in Spencerian script
9.9
Pronunciation: / spɛnˈsɪəriən / (AmE), / spɛnˈsɪəriən / (BrE)
a person who writes in or is skilled at Spencerian script, a 19th-century cursive handwriting style

Examples

A skilled Spencerian
The museum invited a Spencerian to demonstrate the elegant handwriting style to visitors.
As a skilled Spencerian, she restored old documents using authentic techniques.
Many aspiring calligraphers study under a Spencerian to master the classic script.
