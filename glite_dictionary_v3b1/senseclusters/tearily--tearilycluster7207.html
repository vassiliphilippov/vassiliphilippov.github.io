tearily

adverb
with tears
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪrəli / (AmE), / ˈtɪərəli / (BrE)
in a way that shows tears or suggests that someone is crying or about to cry

Examples

She waved tearily
She waved tearily at the train as it left the station.
He smiled tearily after hearing the good news.
The child looked up tearily after falling and scraping his knee.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfully - in a happy and positive way
  • synonym: tearfully - in a way that is full of tears or sadness
