there you are

interjection
expression used when finding someone or something
Pronunciation: / ðɛr ju ɑr / (AmE), / ðeə juː ɑː / (BrE)
Used when you have found someone or something you were looking for, or when something has been resolved or explained.

Examples

There you are at last
'I've been searching the whole house for you.' 'Well, there you are.'
Ah, there you are! I was beginning to get worried.
I finally found my keys. There you are!

Related Terms

  • synonym: here you are - expression used when handing something to someone or locating them
