whipsaw

noun
long two-person saw
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪpˌsɔ / (AmE), / ˈwɪpˌsɔː / (BrE)
a long saw with handles at both ends, designed to be operated by two people

Examples

A whipsaw tool
A whipsaw requires strength and coordination from both operators to work efficiently.
During the demonstration, the carpenter showed how to use a whipsaw for cutting timber.
The two lumberjacks worked together with a whipsaw to fell the large tree.
