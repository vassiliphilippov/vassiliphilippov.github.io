gurgle

noun
bubbling sound of flowing liquid
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɝːɡəl / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːɡl̩ / (BrE)
a bubbling sound like water flowing from a bottle with a narrow neck or similar noises

Examples

A soft gurgle
A faint gurgle could be heard from the pipes behind the wall.
She listened to the gurgle of water pouring from the old bottle.
The gurgle of the stream was soothing as we walked along the path.

Related Terms

  • synonym: babble - a continuous murmuring sound, as of water flowing over stones
  • synonym: murmur - a low, continuous sound, especially of water or voices
