icebox cake

noun
chilled layered dessert, sometimes ice cream cake
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪs.bɑks keɪk / (AmE), / ˈaɪs.bɒks keɪk / (BrE)
A dessert made by layering ingredients such as cookies and whipped cream, then chilling until set; sometimes refers to ice cream molded to resemble a cake.

Examples

An icebox cake dessert
After assembling the icebox cake, you need to refrigerate it overnight for the best texture.
She made her famous icebox cake for the summer barbecue.
The restaurant offers a classic icebox cake with layers of chocolate wafers and whipped cream.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ice cream cake - cake made with layers of ice cream and cake or cookies
