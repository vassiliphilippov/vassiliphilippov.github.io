fluff bunny

noun
an overly sweet or cute-loving person
3.9
Pronunciation: / flʌf ˈbʌni / (AmE), / flʌf ˈbʌni / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is overly sweet, positive, or extremely fond of cute things

Examples

A fluff bunny person
His room is decorated with plushies and bright colors—he’s a total fluff bunny.
If you prefer serious discussions, the fluff bunny crowd might not be your style.
She’s such a fluff bunny, always spreading positivity wherever she goes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cynic - a person who believes people are motivated purely by self-interest
  • synonym: sweetheart - a likable, gentle, or kind person
