khalif

noun
Muslim civil and religious leader (caliph)
7.5
Pronunciation: / kəˈliːf / (AmE), / kəˈliːf / (BrE)
The civil and religious leader of a Muslim state, regarded as the representative of Allah on earth; also known as a caliph.

Examples

The khalif leader
Many Muslims around the world viewed the khalif as a unifying figure of their faith.
Some historians debate the legitimacy of successive khalifs after the first four.
The fall of the last khalif marked the end of a significant era in Islamic history.
