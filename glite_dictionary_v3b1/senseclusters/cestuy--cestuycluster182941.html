cestuy

noun
legal beneficiary
Pronunciation: / sɛsˈtwiː / (AmE), / sɛsˈtwiː / (BrE)
an old legal term for a person who benefits from a trust or estate

Examples

The cestuy beneficiary
In the old documents, the cestuy was named as the person entitled to the trust's income.
The lawyer explained that the cestuy would inherit the property after the trustee's duties ended.
Historically, a cestuy had rights to the benefits of an estate managed by another.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beneficiary - a person who receives benefits from a trust or will
