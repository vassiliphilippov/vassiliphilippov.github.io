perceive

verb
become aware through the senses
Pronunciation: / pərˈsiv / (AmE), / pəˈsiːv / (BrE)
to become aware of something through the senses, especially sight or hearing

Examples

To perceive a sound
From the deck, you could perceive the outline of mountains in the distance.
She immediately perceived the faint scent of perfume as she entered the room.
Some animals can perceive sounds at frequencies that are inaudible to humans.
