bemusedly

adverb
with mild confusion
Pronunciation: / bɪˈmjuːzɪdli / (AmE), / bɪˈmjuːzɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that shows confusion, mild amusement, or being lost in thought

Examples

To watch bemusedly
He watched the children play bemusedly, unsure of what game they were inventing.
She listened bemusedly as her friend tried to explain the complicated rules.
The cat stared bemusedly at the moving red dot on the wall.
She listened bemusedly as her friends debated the strange new rules.
The teacher watched bemusedly as the students tried to solve the tricky puzzle.
He smiled bemusedly at the unexpected question, unsure how to respond.
