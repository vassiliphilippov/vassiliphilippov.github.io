rainforest

noun
dense forest with heavy rainfall
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪnˌfɔːrɪst / (AmE), / ˈreɪnˌfɒrɪst / (BrE)
A dense forest in a region with high annual rainfall, typically characterized by a great diversity of plant and animal life.

Examples

A rainforest area
Scientists are concerned about the rapid rate of rainforest destruction.
The Amazon rainforest is home to thousands of unique species.
We learned about the different layers of the rainforest in biology class.

  • synonym: jungle - dense, tangled tropical forest
