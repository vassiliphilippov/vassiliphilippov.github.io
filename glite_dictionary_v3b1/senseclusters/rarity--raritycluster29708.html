rarity

noun
state of being rare; something uncommon
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛrəti / (AmE), / ˈreərəti / (BrE)
the quality of being rare, or something that is very uncommon or unusual

Examples

A rarity event
A solar eclipse is a rarity that many people are eager to experience at least once.
In that region, rain is a rarity, so every shower is celebrated.
Vintage cars in excellent condition are a true rarity these days.
Seeing a wild lynx in this area is considered a rarity.
Such kindness is a rarity in today's competitive world.
The coin is a true rarity and highly sought after by collectors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: abundance - a very large quantity of something
  • synonym: collectible - an object worth collecting because it is rare or interesting
  • antonym: commonplace - something ordinary or frequently encountered
  • synonym: scarcity - the state of being in short supply; shortage
