pyrethroid

noun
synthetic insecticide
8.3
Pronunciation: / paɪˈriːθrɔɪd / (AmE), / paɪˈriːθrɔɪd / (BrE)
a synthetic chemical compound used as an insecticide, similar to natural pyrethrins and commonly found in household and agricultural sprays
