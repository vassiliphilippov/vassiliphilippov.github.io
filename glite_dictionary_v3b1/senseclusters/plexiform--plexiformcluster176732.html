plexiform

adjective
network-like structure
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈplɛksɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈplɛksɪfɔːm / (BrE)
having a structure or form resembling a network or plexus, especially in tissues or organs

Examples

plexiform tissue pattern
The pathologist noted a plexiform pattern in the tissue sample.
Certain nerve tumors can develop a plexiform structure, making them difficult to remove.
Under the microscope, the blood vessels appeared plexiform and highly branched.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reticular - having a net-like or mesh structure
