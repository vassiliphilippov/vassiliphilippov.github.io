adventurism

noun
reckless political or foreign policy behavior
2.3
Pronunciation: / ədˈvɛn.tʃəˌrɪz.əm /; / ədˈvɛntʃərɪzəm / (AmE), / ədˈvɛn.tʃə.rɪ.zəm /; / ədˈvɛntʃərɪzəm / (BrE)
reckless or bold behavior, especially in politics or important decisions, involving risky or unplanned actions

Examples
