commend

verb
formally entrust to another's care
2.3
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɛnd / (AmE), / kəˈmɛnd / (BrE)
to formally entrust someone or something to the care or protection of another

Examples

To commend a child to care
Before leaving for her trip, she commended her pets to her neighbor's care.
He commends the students to the new principal's supervision each year.
In his letter, the general commended the soldiers to God's protection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: entrust - to assign the responsibility for doing something to someone
