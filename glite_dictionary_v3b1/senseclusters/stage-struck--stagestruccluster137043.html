stage-struck

adjective
obsessed with the theater
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪdʒˌstrʌk / (AmE), / ˈsteɪdʒˌstrʌk / (BrE)
Fascinated or obsessed with the theater, especially having a strong desire to act on stage.

Examples

Stage-struck young actor
As a stage-struck teenager, she dreamed of one day acting on Broadway.
His stage-struck enthusiasm led him to join every play at school.
Many stage-struck children beg their parents to take them to acting classes.
