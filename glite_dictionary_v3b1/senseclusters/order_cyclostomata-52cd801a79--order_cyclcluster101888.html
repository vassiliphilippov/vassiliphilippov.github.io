order Cyclostomata

noun
order of jawless aquatic vertebrates
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər saɪˌklɒˈstɑːmətə / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə saɪˌklɒsˈtəʊmətə / (BrE)
A taxonomic order of primitive jawless aquatic vertebrates that includes lampreys and hagfishes.

Examples

Order Cyclostomata animals
Lampreys and hagfishes both belong to the order Cyclostomata in zoological classification.
Scientists study the order Cyclostomata to understand evolutionary history among vertebrates.
The order Cyclostomata is characterized by a lack of jaws and paired fins.
