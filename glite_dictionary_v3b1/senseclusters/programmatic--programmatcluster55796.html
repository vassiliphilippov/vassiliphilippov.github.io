programmatic

adjective
automated by software
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊɡrəˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌprəʊɡrəˈmætɪk / (BrE)
relating to the use of computers or software to automate tasks or processes, especially in business or advertising

Examples

Programmatic advertising
The company adopted a programmatic approach to buying online advertisements.
With programmatic solutions, repetitive tasks can be completed much faster and with fewer errors.
Many marketing teams now rely on programmatic tools to optimize their campaigns automatically.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automated - operated by machines or computers rather than people
  • antonym: manual - done by hand or by people rather than by machines
