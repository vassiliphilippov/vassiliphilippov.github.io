lotos

noun
poetic lotus plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊtəs / (AmE), / ˈləʊtəs / (BrE)
an old or poetic spelling of the lotus plant, a water plant with large, showy flowers

Examples

A lotos flower
The ancient poem spoke of the lotos blooming beside the tranquil stream.
Travelers rested in the shade of the lotos during the hot afternoon.
He admired the delicate petals of the lotos floating on the pond.
