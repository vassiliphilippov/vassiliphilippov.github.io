unseeable

adjective
impossible or nearly impossible to see
8.4 0.09/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàsiÀê…ôb…ôl / (AmE), /  ånÀàsiÀê…ôbl / (BrE)
Impossible or almost impossible to see; not able to be perceived by the eye.

Examples

An unseeable detail
Some details in the painting are so fine they appear unseeable without a magnifying glass.
The tiny cracks in the glass were almost unseeable to the naked eye.
Ultraviolet light is unseeable, but it can still affect your skin.
