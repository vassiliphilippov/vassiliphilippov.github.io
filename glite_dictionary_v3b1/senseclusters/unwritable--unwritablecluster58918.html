unwritable

adjective
cannot be written
8.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈraɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈraɪtəbəl / (BrE)
impossible or extremely difficult to express adequately in writing

Examples

Unwritable feelings
Some emotions are so deep that they feel unwritable even to the most talented authors.
The complexity of the ancient language made certain ideas almost unwritable in modern terms.
He found the experience so overwhelming that it seemed completely unwritable in his journal.

Related Terms

  • antonym: expressible - able to be put into words or writing
  • synonym: indescribable - impossible to describe in words
  • synonym: ineffable - too great or extreme to be expressed in words
