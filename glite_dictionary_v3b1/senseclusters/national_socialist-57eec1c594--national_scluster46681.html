national socialist

adjective
relating to Nazi political ideology
Pronunciation: / ˌnæʃənl ˈsoʊʃəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌnæʃənəl ˈsəʊʃəlɪst / (BrE)
relating to the political ideology and movement associated with the Nazi Party in Germany, which combined nationalism with elements of socialism.

Examples

National socialist ideology
He studied the principles of national socialist ideology during his university course.
The national socialist regime under Hitler changed the course of European history.
The museum has an exhibition on national socialist propaganda materials from the 1930s.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Nazi - member of the National Socialist German Workers' Party or relating to its ideology
