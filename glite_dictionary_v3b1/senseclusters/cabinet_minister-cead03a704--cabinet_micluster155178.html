cabinet minister

noun
senior government official in a cabinet
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkæbɪnət ˈmɪnɪstɚ / (AmE), / ˈkæbɪnət ˈmɪnɪstə /; / ˈkæbɪnɪt ˈmɪnɪstə / (BrE)
a senior government official who is a member of the cabinet and leads a specific government department

Examples

Cabinet minister leads department
As a cabinet minister, she is responsible for national education policy.
Several cabinet ministers resigned over the controversial decision.
The Prime Minister consulted the cabinet minister on the proposed policy changes.
Each cabinet minister is responsible for a different government department.
She became the youngest cabinet minister in the country's history in 2015.
The cabinet minister announced several new policy changes during the press conference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: minister - a government official in charge of a department
