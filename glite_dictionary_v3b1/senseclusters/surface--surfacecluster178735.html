surface

verb
rise or come to the top; become visible
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝː.fɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.fɪs / (BrE)
to rise or come to the top layer; to become visible or apparent after being hidden

Examples

To surface after diving
After several minutes, the divers finally surfaced near the boat.
It took years for the truth to surface in the investigation.
The whale disappeared for a while and then suddenly surfaced again.
Rumors about the company's bankruptcy began to surface in the media last week.
She tends to surface at family gatherings after months of no contact.
The missing documents did not surface until years after the investigation had closed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emerge - to come out into view or become apparent
