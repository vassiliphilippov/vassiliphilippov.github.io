punctuate

verb
insert punctuation marks into text
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌŋktʃuˌeɪt / (AmE), / ˈpʌŋktjʊeɪt / (BrE)
to insert punctuation marks into written text in order to clarify meaning or separate sentences and elements

Examples

To punctuate a sentence
He often forgets to punctuate his sentences, making them hard to read.
Please punctuate this paragraph correctly before submitting your assignment.
The teacher showed the class how to punctuate direct speech properly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: edit - to prepare written material for publication by correcting and revising
