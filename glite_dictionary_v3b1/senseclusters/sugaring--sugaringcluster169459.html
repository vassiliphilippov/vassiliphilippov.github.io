sugaring

noun
hair removal method
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌɡərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌɡərɪŋ / (BrE)
a method of removing body hair by applying and pulling off a sticky sugar-based paste

Examples

Sugaring hair removal
Many people prefer sugaring because it uses natural ingredients and is gentle on the skin.
After trying sugaring, she noticed her skin felt smoother than with other hair removal methods.
Some salons offer sugaring as an alternative to traditional waxing for sensitive clients.
