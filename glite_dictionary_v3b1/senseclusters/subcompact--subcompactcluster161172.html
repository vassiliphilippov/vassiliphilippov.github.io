subcompact

noun
a very small car type
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈkɑmˌpækt / (AmE), / sʌbˈkɒm.pækt / (BrE)
a car that is smaller than a compact car, typically designed for efficiency and city driving

Examples

A subcompact car
My first car was a subcompact that got excellent gas mileage.
She traded her SUV for a subcompact to save money on fuel.
The city is ideal for driving a subcompact because parking spaces are limited.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: compact - a car larger than a subcompact but smaller than a mid-size
