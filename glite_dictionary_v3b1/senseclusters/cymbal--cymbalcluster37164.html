cymbal

noun
a percussion instrument; metal disk struck for sound
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmbəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪmbəl / (BrE)
a percussion instrument made of a thin, round metal plate that produces a loud, ringing sound when struck, either alone or in pairs

Examples

A cymbal crash
A single strike on the cymbal echoed across the auditorium.
She bought a new set of cymbals for her percussion ensemble.
The drummer crashed the cymbal at the climax of the song.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drum - a percussion instrument consisting of a hollow cylinder with a membrane
  • synonym: gong - a large metal disk that makes a loud, resonant sound when struck
