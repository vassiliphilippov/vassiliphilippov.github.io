peacemaker

noun
person who seeks to bring about peace
3.7 1.09/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàpiÀêsÀåme…™k…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàpiÀêsÀåme…™k…ô / (BrE)
a person who tries to create or maintain peace, especially by settling conflicts or disputes

Examples

A peacemaker negotiates
As a peacemaker, she negotiated a truce between the two neighboring communities.
He was widely respected for his role as a peacemaker during the labor dispute.
The school counselor often acted as a peacemaker among students after arguments.

Synonyms

  • mediator
    4.0 4.83/m
    mediator helps both sides talk and reach a fair agreement, while peacemaker aims to stop fights and keep peace between people.
  • conciliator
    7.0 0.13/m
    conciliator tries to calm feelings and reduce anger to help people agree, while peacemaker works to settle disputes and keep peace.
  • reconciler
    6.3 0.03/m
    reconciler seeks to restore friendly relations after a falling out, while peacemaker often works to prevent or settle fights between people.

Related Terms
