peacemaker

noun
person who seeks to bring about peace
1.1 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈpiːs.meɪ.kər/ (AmE), /ˈpiːs.meɪ.kə/ (BrE)
a person who tries to create or maintain peace, especially by settling conflicts or disputes

Examples

A peacemaker negotiates

He was widely respected for his role as a peacemaker during the labor dispute.
The school counselor often acted as a peacemaker among students after arguments.
