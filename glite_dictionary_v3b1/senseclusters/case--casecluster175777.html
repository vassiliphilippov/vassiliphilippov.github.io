case

noun
an instance or occurrence
Pronunciation: / keɪs / (AmE), / keɪs / (BrE)
a particular situation, occurrence, or fact being considered or discussed

Examples

It's a rare case that someone recovers so quickly from an illness like that.
There was another case of theft reported in the neighborhood last night.
Every case is different, so we need to consider all the factors before deciding.
If it rains, in that case we will need to postpone the event.
In the case of fire, please use the stairs and not the elevator.
He thought she would be at work, but that wasn't the case.
If that's the case, we need to reconsider our plan.
In many situations, the case turned out to be different from what was first assumed.
