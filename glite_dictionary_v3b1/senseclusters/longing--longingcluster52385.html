longing

noun
prolonged unfulfilled desire
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋɪŋ / (BrE)
a strong feeling of wanting something or someone that you cannot have or are unlikely to get

Examples

Deep longing for home
Despite his longing for adventure, he stayed where he was.
He felt a deep longing for the home he left behind.
There was a longing in her eyes that was impossible to ignore.

Related Terms

  • synonym: craving - a powerful desire for something
  • antonym: satisfaction - fulfillment of one's wishes, expectations, or needs
  • synonym: yearning - an intense feeling of longing for something
