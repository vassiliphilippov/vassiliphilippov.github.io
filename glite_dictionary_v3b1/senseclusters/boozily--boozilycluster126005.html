boozily

adverb
drunkenly
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːzɪli / (AmE), / ˈbuːzɪli / (BrE)
in a way that shows the effects of drinking alcohol or being drunk

Examples

Walks boozily home
He stumbled boozily out of the bar, singing loudly in the street.
She spoke boozily, her words slurring after several drinks at the party.
The man waved boozily at his friends before sitting down again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drunkenly - in a way that shows someone is drunk
  • antonym: soberly - in a way that is not affected by alcohol
