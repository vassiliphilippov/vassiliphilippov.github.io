AWOL

noun
person absent without leave
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪˌwɑl / (AmE), / ˈeɪˌwɒl / (BrE)
a person who is absent from their post or duty without official permission

Examples

An AWOL soldier
After three days, the soldier was declared AWOL by his commanding officer.
If a team member goes AWOL, the project can easily fall behind schedule.
When the manager returned, she found one employee had gone AWOL during the busiest hour.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deserter - a person who abandons their duty or post without permission
