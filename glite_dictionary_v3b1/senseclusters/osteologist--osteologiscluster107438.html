osteologist

noun
person who studies bones
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːstiˈɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌɒstiˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
A scientist or specialist who studies the structure and function of bones.

Examples

An osteologist studies bones
An experienced osteologist can often determine a person's age from their bones.
The osteologist carefully examined the ancient skeleton for signs of disease.
The museum hired an osteologist to help identify the animal remains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: osteology - the scientific study of bones
