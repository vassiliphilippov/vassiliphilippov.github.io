objectlessness

noun
lack of purpose
Pronunciation: / ˈɑbdʒɪktləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈɒbdʒɪktləsnəs / (BrE)
the state of having no object, purpose, or specific aim

Examples

A feeling of objectlessness
He struggled with a sense of objectlessness after losing his job.
The objectlessness of the meeting left everyone feeling confused and unmotivated.
Artists sometimes explore objectlessness in their work to challenge traditional ideas of meaning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aimlessness - the state of lacking a goal or direction
  • antonym: purposefulness - the quality of having a clear aim or purpose
