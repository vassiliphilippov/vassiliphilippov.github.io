unmaimed

adjective
not injured
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmeɪmd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmeɪmd / (BrE)
not injured, damaged, or deprived of a body part; whole and unharmed

Examples

To be unmaimed and safe
After the dangerous climb, he was grateful to be unmaimed and safe.
The animal escaped the trap unmaimed, running back into the forest.
Despite the explosion, several workers emerged unmaimed from the factory.

Related Terms

  • antonym: maimed - injured so that a part of the body is permanently damaged
  • synonym: unharmed - not hurt or damaged
