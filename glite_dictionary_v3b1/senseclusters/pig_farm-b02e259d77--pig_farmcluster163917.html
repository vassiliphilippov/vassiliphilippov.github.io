pig farm

noun
farm for raising pigs
Pronunciation: / pɪɡ fɑrm / (AmE), / pɪɡ fɑːm / (BrE)
A farm where pigs are raised or kept for breeding or meat production.

Examples

A pig farm
Environmental regulations for a pig farm are often strict to control pollution.
He grew up on a pig farm in rural Iowa.
The old barn was converted into a pig farm several years ago.
