growth

noun
abnormal tissue mass (tumor)
2.9
Pronunciation: / ɡroʊθ / (AmE), / ɡrəʊθ / (BrE)
an abnormal mass of tissue, such as a tumor, resulting from uncontrolled cell multiplication

Examples

A growth removed
After a biopsy, the growth was found to be benign and not cancerous.
He was worried about the small growth that appeared on his skin.
The doctor discovered a suspicious growth on her lung during the examination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tumor - an abnormal swelling of tissue, often a synonym in medical contexts
