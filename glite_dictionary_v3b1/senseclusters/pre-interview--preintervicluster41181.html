pre-interview

adjective
before an interview
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈɪntərvjuː / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈɪntəvjuː / (BrE)
happening or prepared before an interview, especially to describe meetings, questions, or materials used in advance

Examples

Pre-interview questions
The candidate filled out a pre-interview form to provide background information.
We scheduled a pre-interview call to discuss the job requirements in detail.
All applicants must complete the pre-interview assessment before moving to the next stage.
