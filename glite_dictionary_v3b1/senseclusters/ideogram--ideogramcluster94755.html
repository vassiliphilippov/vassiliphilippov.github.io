ideogram

noun
symbol representing an idea or object
1.8 6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪdiəˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈɪdɪəɡræm / (BrE)
A written symbol that represents an idea or object directly, rather than the sound of a word.

Examples

A ideogram symbol
In Chinese writing, one ideogram can represent an entire word or concept.
Many ancient scripts, such as Egyptian hieroglyphs, make use of ideograms.
The museum display explained how an ideogram conveys meaning without specifying its pronunciation.
