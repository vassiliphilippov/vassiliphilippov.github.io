Kernig's sign

noun
inability to extend knee due to hamstring stiffness; sign of meningitis
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkɜːr.nɪgz saɪn/ (AmE), /ˈkɜː.nɪgz saɪn/ (BrE)
a clinical sign in which the patient, with the thigh flexed, cannot fully extend the knee due to hamstring stiffness, indicating possible meningitis

Examples

Positive Kernig's sign
A positive Kernig's sign can be an important clue in diagnosing meningitis.
