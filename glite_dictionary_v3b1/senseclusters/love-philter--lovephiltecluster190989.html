love-philter

noun
magic potion causing love
9.7 9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌv ˌfɪltər / (AmE), / ˈlʌv ˌfɪltə / (BrE)
A drink supposed or believed to have magical properties or powers that causes the person who drinks or consumes it to fall in love, often specifically with the giver.
