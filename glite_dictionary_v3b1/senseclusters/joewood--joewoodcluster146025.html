joewood

noun
West Indian shrub or tree with hard wood
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒoʊˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈdʒəʊˌwʊd / (BrE)
A West Indian shrub or small tree with leathery, soap-like leaves and very hard wood.

Examples

The joewood tree
Botanists studied the saponaceous leaves of the joewood growing along the Caribbean coast.
Local artisans use joewood to make tools due to its extremely hard wood.
The furniture was crafted from joewood, known for its remarkable durability.
