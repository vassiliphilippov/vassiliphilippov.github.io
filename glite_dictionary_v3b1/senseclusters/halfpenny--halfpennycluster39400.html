halfpenny

noun
former British coin worth half a penny
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪpni, ˈhæfˌpɛni / (AmE), / ˈheɪpni, ˈhæfˌpɛni / (BrE)
a former British coin worth half of a penny, used in the United Kingdom until 1984

Examples

A halfpenny coin

He paid with a halfpenny he found on the street.
She collected old halfpennies as a hobby.
