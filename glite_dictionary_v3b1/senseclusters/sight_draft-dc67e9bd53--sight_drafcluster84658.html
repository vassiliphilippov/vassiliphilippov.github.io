sight draft

noun
a bill payable on presentation
7.9
Pronunciation: / saɪt dræft / (AmE), / saɪt drɑːft / (BrE)
a bill of exchange that is payable immediately upon presentation to the drawee

Examples

A sight draft payment
The exporter requested payment through a sight draft to ensure quick receipt of funds.
Upon presenting the sight draft, the bank immediately transferred the agreed sum to the beneficiary.
Using a sight draft can expedite international transactions by guaranteeing prompt payment.
