octant

noun
one of eight parts
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑktənt / (AmE), / ˈɒktənt / (BrE)
one of eight equal parts into which something, especially a three-dimensional space, is divided

Examples

A space octant
In three-dimensional geometry, each octant is defined by the signs of the coordinates.
The sphere was divided into eight octants for the purpose of the experiment.
The teacher asked the students to identify which octant the point belonged to.
