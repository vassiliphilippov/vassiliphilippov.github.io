surprise birthday event

noun
secretly organized birthday celebration
Pronunciation: / səˈpraɪz ˈbɝθ.deɪ ɪˈvɛnt / (AmE), / səˈprʌɪz ˈbɜːθ.deɪ ɪˈvɛnt / (BrE)
a birthday celebration secretly organized to surprise the person whose birthday it is

Examples

A surprise birthday event
He walked in and was genuinely shocked by the surprise birthday event his friends had arranged.
Planning a surprise birthday event requires careful coordination with all the guests.
We threw a surprise birthday event for our manager at the office last Friday.
