jet-lagged

adjective
tired from travel
3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒet.lægd/ (AmE), /ˈdʒet.lægd/ (BrE)
feeling tired and disoriented after a long flight across different time zones

Examples

Feeling jet-lagged after flight
After flying from New York to Tokyo, he felt extremely jet-lagged for several days.
She was so jet-lagged that she accidentally slept through her morning meeting.
Many travelers become jet-lagged when crossing multiple time zones in a short period.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fatigued - very tired or exhausted
  • antonym: refreshed - feeling rested and energetic
