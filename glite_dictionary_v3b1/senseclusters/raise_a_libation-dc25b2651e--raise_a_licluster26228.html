raise a libation

verb
offer a ritual drink in honor
9.9
Pronunciation: / reɪz ə laɪˈbeɪʃən / (AmE), / reɪz ə laɪˈbeɪʃən / (BrE)
to offer a drink as a ritual, often to honor a deity or person

Examples

To raise a libation to honor
After the speech, the guests raised a libation to celebrate the hero's achievements.
He solemnly raised a libation in memory of his ancestors during the ceremony.
In some cultures, people raise a libation at weddings to bring good fortune.

Related Terms
