Joneses

noun
social peers
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒoʊnzɪz / (AmE), / ˈdʒəʊnzɪz / (BrE)
a generic term for one's neighbors or social peers, especially as a standard for comparison in matters of status or possessions

Examples

Keep up with the Joneses
They bought a new car just to keep up with the Joneses in their neighborhood.
Some people feel pressured to match the lifestyle of the Joneses around them.
Trying to outdo the Joneses can lead to unnecessary stress and spending.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neighbors - people living near you or in your community
  • synonym: peers - people of the same social group or status
