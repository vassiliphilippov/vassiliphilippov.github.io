teemingness

noun
abundance; profusion
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːmɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ˈtiːmɪŋnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being full to overflowing; abundance or profusion

Examples

The teemingness of life
During the festival, the city's streets pulsed with the teemingness of visitors and performers.
Historians wrote about the teemingness of ideas during the Renaissance period.
The teemingness of the tropical rainforest makes it home to countless plant and animal species.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abundance - a very large quantity of something
  • synonym: plentifulness - the state of being plentiful
  • antonym: scarcity - the state of being scarce or in short supply
