epidemic myalgia

noun
acute disease causing sudden chest pain
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈdɛmɪk maɪˈældʒiə / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈdɛmɪk maɪˈældʒɪə / (BrE)
an acute infectious disease, often occurring in outbreaks, characterized by sudden attacks of pain, usually in the chest

Examples

Epidemic myalgia outbreak
Doctors warned that epidemic myalgia can spread quickly in crowded areas.
Several cases of epidemic myalgia were reported in the community after the outbreak.
The patient was diagnosed with epidemic myalgia following severe chest pain episodes.
