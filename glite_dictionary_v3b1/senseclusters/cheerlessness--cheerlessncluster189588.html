cheerlessness

noun
gloominess; absence of cheer
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪr.ləs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪə.ləs.nɪs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being gloomy, lacking happiness or hope

Examples

A cheerlessness feeling
Despite the sunny weather, a sense of cheerlessness lingered over the small town.
His voice was heavy with cheerlessness as he described the past few months.
There was a distinct cheerlessness in the office after the company's announcement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfulness - the quality or state of being noticeably happy and optimistic
  • degree higher: despair - the complete loss or absence of hope
  • synonym: gloom - a state of partial or total darkness; a feeling of sadness or depression
  • synonym: sadness - the condition or quality of being sad
