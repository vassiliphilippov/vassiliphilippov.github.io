broad-brush comment

noun
general remark lacking detail
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌbrɔd ˈbrʌʃ ˈkɑːmɛnt / (AmE), / ˌbrɔːd ˈbrʌʃ ˈkɒmɛnt / (BrE)
A remark or statement that is general and lacks specific details or nuance.

Examples

A broad-brush comment general
Critics accused the report of containing too many broad-brush comments and not enough specifics.
