unprovided for

adjective
not prepared or supplied
1.4 8.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnprəˈvaɪdəd fɔr /; / ˌʌn.prəˈvaɪ.dɪd fɔr / (AmE), / ʌnprəˈvaɪdɪd fɔː /; / ˌʌn.prəˈvaɪ.dɪd fɔː / (BrE)
