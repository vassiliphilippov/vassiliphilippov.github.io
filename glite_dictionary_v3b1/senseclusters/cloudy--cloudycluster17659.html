cloudy

adjective
covered with clouds
Pronunciation: / ˈklaʊdi / (AmE), / ˈklaʊdi / (BrE)
(of the sky or weather) full of or covered with clouds; not clear or sunny.

Examples

Cloudy sky
Even though it was cloudy all day, we decided to go for a hike.
The forecast predicts a cloudy afternoon with a chance of rain.
We woke up to a cloudy sky and cooler temperatures.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - (of the sky) free from clouds
