cloudy

adjective
covered with clouds
1.0 3.19/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆklaÊŠdi / (AmE), / ËˆklaÊŠdi / (BrE)
(of the sky or weather) full of or covered with clouds; not clear or sunny.

Examples

Cloudy sky
Even though it was cloudy all day, we decided to go for a hike.
The forecast predicts a cloudy afternoon with a chance of rain.
We woke up to a cloudy sky and cooler temperatures.

Synonyms

  • clouded
    2.1 0.01/m
    clouded is filled with clouds and often describes weather conditions, while cloudy means the sky is not clear or sunny.
  • cloud-covered
    3.9 0.06/m
    cloud-covered means a surface or sky is covered or obscured by clouds, while cloudy usually describes overcast weather in general.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - (of the sky) free from clouds
