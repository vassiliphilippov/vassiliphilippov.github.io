Plant tissue composed of parenchyma cells containing chloroplasts, responsible for photosynthesis.

Examples

Chlorenchyma plant tissue
Botanists observed that the chlorenchyma cells were especially abundant near the surface of the leaf.
In aquatic plants, chlorenchyma helps to keep the stems buoyant while providing cells for photosynthesis.
