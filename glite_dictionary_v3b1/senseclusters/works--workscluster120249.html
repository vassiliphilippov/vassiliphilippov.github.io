works

noun
moral or religious acts; good deeds
2.0 6.3
Pronunciation: /wɜːrks/ (AmE), /wɜːks/ (BrE)
actions performed as part of one's moral or religious duty, especially good deeds regarded as contributing to salvation or spiritual merit.

Examples

Religious works
Debates continue on the importance of faith versus works in religious doctrine.
She devoted her life to charitable works in her community.
The church teaches that works alone cannot bring salvation without faith.
