traveling salesman

noun
a salesperson who travels to see customers
Pronunciation: / ˈtrævəlɪŋ ˈseɪlzmən /; / ˈtræv(ə)lɪŋ ˈseɪlzmən / (AmE), / ˈtrævəlɪŋ ˈseɪlzmən /; / ˈtræv(ə)lɪŋ ˈseɪlzmən / (BrE)
a salesperson whose job involves visiting customers in different locations, typically over a wide area, to sell goods or services

Examples

My grandfather worked as a travelling salesman, selling farm equipment throughout the Midwest.
The life of a travelling salesman can be lonely, with long days away from family.
