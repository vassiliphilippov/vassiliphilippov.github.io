detainer

noun
custody holder
8.1
Pronunciation: / dɪˈteɪnər / (AmE), / dɪˈteɪnə / (BrE)
a person or authority who holds someone in custody, especially by legal right or order

Examples

Detainer of a prisoner
The detainer was responsible for ensuring the suspect remained in custody until trial.
A court order named the police as the official detainer in the case.
The lawyer questioned whether the detainer had the legal authority to hold her client.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jailer - a person in charge of a jail or prisoners
  • antonym: prisoner - a person who is kept in prison
  • synonym: warden - an official responsible for the supervision of a prison
