cleantech

adjective
environmentally friendly technology
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkliːnˌtɛk / (AmE), / ˈkliːnˌtɛk / (BrE)
relating to technologies or products that aim to reduce pollution, waste, or greenhouse-gas emissions.

Examples

Cleantech innovation
The government is investing in cleantech solutions to address climate change.
Many companies are shifting to cleantech manufacturing processes to reduce their carbon footprint.
A cleantech approach can make energy production more sustainable and less harmful to the environment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: green - concerned with or supporting environmental protection
  • antonym: polluting - causing harm to the environment by introducing contaminants
  • synonym: sustainable - able to be maintained without harming the environment
