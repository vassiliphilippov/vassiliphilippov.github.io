Synonyms

  • live
    1.0 331/m
    live is the everyday word for having a home in a place, while dwell sounds more formal or old-fashioned.
  • reside
    3.0 11/m
    reside is a more formal word used in official writing about where someone lives, while dwell is slightly more literary.
  • inhabit
    3.5 7.86/m
    inhabit often describes animals or long-term living in places like islands, while dwell is used more for people and homes.
  • occupy
    3.0 8.26/m
    occupy can mean to take up space or use a building temporarily, while dwell focuses on living there as a home.

  • synonym: reside - to live in a particular place
