fiscally

adverb
in relation to finance or government money
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪskəli / (AmE), / ˈfɪskəli / (BrE)
In a manner related to finances, government revenue, or monetary matters.

Examples

To be fiscally cautious
She is fiscally cautious and carefully monitors all household expenses.
The company needs to operate fiscally responsibly to ensure long-term success.
The government was praised for being fiscally conservative in its latest budget.
