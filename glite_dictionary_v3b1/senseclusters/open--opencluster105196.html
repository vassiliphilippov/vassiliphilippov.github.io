Synonyms

  • wide open
    2.3 4.93/m
    wide open is completely free and unrestricted for passage or view, while open simply means not closed or only partly unblocked.
  • clear
    1.0 43/m
    clear is free from obstructions or cloudiness and often shows a clear view, while open means not closed and allows passage.
  • exposed
    3.4 0.01/m
    exposed is left without cover or protection and may be in danger, while open can mean simply not closed or reachable.
