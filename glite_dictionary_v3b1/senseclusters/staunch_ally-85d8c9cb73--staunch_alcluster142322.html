staunch ally

noun
strong and loyal supporter
7.4
Pronunciation: / stɔntʃ ˈælaɪ / (AmE), / stɔːntʃ ˈælaɪ / (BrE)
a person, group, or country that gives strong and loyal support to another

Examples

A staunch ally
During the crisis, our neighbor proved to be a staunch ally when we needed help.
France has historically acted as a staunch ally of the United States in many conflicts.
She has always been a staunch ally of human rights organizations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: opponent - someone who opposes or competes against another
  • synonym: supporter - a person who supports a particular person, group, or cause
