wing

noun
eyeliner extension (makeup)
3.7
Pronunciation: /wɪŋ/ (AmE), /wɪŋ/ (BrE)
a small line or flick of eyeliner drawn outward from the outer corner of the eye to create an elongated, wing-like effect.

Examples

A makeup wing
She finished her makeup with a bold wing at the outer corner of her eye.
Makeup tutorials often show how to draw a precise wing using tape or a stencil.
He complimented her dramatic wing that made her eyes look larger in photographs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: winged eyeliner - a style of eyeliner in which the line is extended outward and often upward at the outer corner of the eye
