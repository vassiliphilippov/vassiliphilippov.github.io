garniture

noun
matching set of armor
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrnɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːnɪtjʊə / (BrE)
a coordinated set of armor, weapons, or decorative equipment forming a matching outfit, especially for ceremonial or display purposes

Examples

Ceremonial garniture set
The knight's garniture was carefully crafted to impress both allies and enemies on the battlefield.
A rare Renaissance garniture was the highlight of the museum's new exhibition.
Collectors value a complete garniture because matching pieces are often lost over time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: armor - protective metal clothing worn in battle
  • synonym: ensemble - a group of items intended to be used or worn together
