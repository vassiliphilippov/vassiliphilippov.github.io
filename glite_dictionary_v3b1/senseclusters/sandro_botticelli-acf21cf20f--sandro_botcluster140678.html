Sandro Botticelli

proper noun
Italian Renaissance painter

Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːn.droʊ ˌbɒ.tɪˈtʃɛl.i / (AmE), / ˈsan.drəʊ ˌbɒ.tɪˈtʃel.i / (BrE)
