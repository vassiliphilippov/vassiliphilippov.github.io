orthotic insole

noun
shoe insert for foot support
5.0 3.5
Pronunciation: /ɔːr.ˈθɑː.tɪk ˈɪn.soʊl/ (AmE), /ɔː.ˈθɒ.tɪk ˈɪn.səʊl/ (BrE)
A removable insert placed inside a shoe to support, align, or improve the function of the foot.

Examples

An orthotic insole insert
After injuring his foot, the doctor recommended an orthotic insole to provide extra support.
Many people with flat feet benefit from wearing an orthotic insole in their everyday shoes.
She slipped an orthotic insole into her sneaker to reduce heel pain during her morning walks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insole - the inner part of the sole of a shoe
  • synonym: orthotic - a device used to support or correct musculoskeletal function
