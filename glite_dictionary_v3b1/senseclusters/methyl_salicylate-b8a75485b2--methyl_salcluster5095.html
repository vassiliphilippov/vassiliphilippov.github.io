methyl salicylate

noun
wintergreen flavoring or fragrance agent
5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈmeθ.əl ˈsæl.ɪ.sɪ.leɪt/ (AmE), /ˈmeθ.əl ˈsæl.ɪ.sɪ.leɪt/ /; / /ˈmiː.θaɪl ˈsæl.ɪ.sɪ.leɪt/ (BrE)
a chemical compound used as a flavoring or fragrance agent to give a wintergreen taste or aroma in foods, oral-care products, cosmetics, and perfumes.

Perfume designers sometimes add methyl salicylate to cosmetic blends to create a sweet, minty note.
