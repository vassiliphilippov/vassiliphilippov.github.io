apery

noun
imitative or mimicking behaviour
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪpəri / (AmE), / ˈeɪpəri / (BrE)
the act of imitating or mimicking someone or something, often in a mocking or unoriginal way

Examples

Apery of speech
Children often engage in apery as they learn language and social behaviors.
His constant apery of his boss's gestures amused his colleagues.
The comedian's act relied heavily on apery of celebrity personalities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imitation - the action of using someone or something as a model
  • synonym: mockery - teasing and contemptuous language or behavior directed at a particular person or thing
