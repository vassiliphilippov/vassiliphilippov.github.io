she

pronoun
personified female object
Pronunciation: / ʃi / (AmE), / ʃi / (BrE)
used to refer to a ship, vehicle, country, or other inanimate thing that is personified as female

Examples

She sails well
The captain believed she would survive the rough seas and bring everyone home safely.
Many people say that she is the pride of the navy when referring to the ship.
After years of service, she was finally retired and displayed in the museum.

Related Terms

  • antonym: it - pronoun for inanimate things or animals of unknown gender
