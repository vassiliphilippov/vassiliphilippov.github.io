stalking

noun
hunting by stealthy pursuit or ambush
1.3 6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɔːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or method of following or approaching a person, animal, or prey stealthily, especially for hunting or observing
