Synonyms

  • stalk
    7.9 0.26/m
    a stalk is a hunt in which a hunter moves very quietly toward prey, while stalking is the general act of secretly following or watching.
  • tailing
    7.1 0.62/m
    tailing is secretly following a person to learn their movements in town, while stalking covers both hunting animals and observing people stealthily.
  • shadowing
    6.8 0.42/m
