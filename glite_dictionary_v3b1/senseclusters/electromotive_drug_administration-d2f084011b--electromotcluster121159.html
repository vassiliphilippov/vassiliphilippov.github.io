electromotive drug administration

noun
drug delivery by electric current
5.8
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˈmoʊtɪv drʌɡ ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈməʊtɪv drʌɡ ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
a medical technique that uses a local electric current to introduce the ions of a drug into body tissues

Examples

Electromotive drug administration technique
Physicians may recommend electromotive drug administration when conventional injections are unsuitable.
Recent studies show that electromotive drug administration increases the effectiveness of certain medications.
The clinic adopted electromotive drug administration to treat patients with chronic inflammation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: iontophoresis - drug delivery method using electric current to enhance absorption through the skin
