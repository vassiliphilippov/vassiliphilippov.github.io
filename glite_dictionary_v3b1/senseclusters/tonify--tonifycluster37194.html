tonify

verb
to adjust sound quality
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊnɪfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊnɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to give or adjust the tone or quality of a sound, voice, or musical instrument

Examples

To tonify a voice
The technician used special software to tonify the recording for a richer sound.
Producers often tonify vocals to match the mood of a song.
She asked the engineer to tonify her guitar so it would blend better with the band.

Related Terms

  • antonym: distort - to change the original sound quality in an unpleasant way
  • synonym: equalize - to adjust the balance of frequencies in sound
