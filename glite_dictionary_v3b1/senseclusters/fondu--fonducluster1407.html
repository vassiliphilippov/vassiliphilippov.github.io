fondu

noun
meat/seafood cooked in oil and dipped in sauces
Pronunciation: / fɑnˈduː /; / fɒnˈduː / (AmE), / fɒnˈdjuː /; / fɒnˈduː / (BrE)
a communal dish where pieces of food are dipped into a hot pot of melted cheese, chocolate, or hot oil

Examples

Cheese fondu
Each guest cooked their own meat in the fondu and tried different dipping sauces.
For her birthday party, she served a fondu with chicken, beef, and shrimp.
We enjoyed a seafood fondu at the fancy restaurant last night.
At the party, everyone enjoyed dipping bread into the bubbling pot of fondu.
Cheese fondu is a classic Swiss dish perfect for sharing on a cold night.
We ordered chocolate fondu for dessert and dipped strawberries and bananas into it.
