fondu

noun
meat/seafood cooked in oil and dipped in sauces
9.9 6.2
Pronunciation: /fɑːn.ˈduː/ /; / /fɑːn.ˈdjuː/ (AmE), /fɒn.ˈdjuː/ /; / /fɒn.ˈduː/ (BrE)
