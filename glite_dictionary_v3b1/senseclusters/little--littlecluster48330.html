little

adjective
not tall or low in stature
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬l̩ /; / ˈlɪt.əl /; / ˈlɪt̬.əl / (AmE), / ˈlɪtəl /; / ˈlɪt.əl / (BrE)
small in size or amount; not large or significant (sometimes used with affection or emotion)

Examples
