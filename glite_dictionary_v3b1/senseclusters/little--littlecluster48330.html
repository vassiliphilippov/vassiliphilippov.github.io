little

adjective
not tall or low in stature
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬l̩ /; / ˈlɪt.əl /; / ˈlɪt̬.əl / (AmE), / ˈlɪtəl /; / ˈlɪt.əl / (BrE)
small in size or amount; not large or significant (sometimes used with affection or emotion)

Examples

A little house

They live in a little house that feels very cozy and warm.
She wore a little dress that barely reached her knees.
There was only a little hope left after the storm.
They bought a little house in the country for the summer.
