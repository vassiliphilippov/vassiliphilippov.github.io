a suffix used to form nouns that refer to an action, process, or result

Examples

The suffix ment
Adding the suffix ment to 'encourage' creates the noun 'encouragement'.
The word 'agreement' is formed by attaching ment to 'agree'.
Many English nouns, like 'movement', use the suffix ment to show an action or result.
