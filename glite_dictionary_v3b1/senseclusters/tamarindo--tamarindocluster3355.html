tamarindo

noun
tropical tree with edible acidic fruit
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌtɑːməˈriːndoʊ / (AmE), / ˌtaməˈrɪndəʊ / (BrE)
A tropical evergreen tree with feathery foliage and fragrant flowers, producing long pods with edible, acidic pulp often used in cooking.

Examples

Tamarindo tree shade
Local markets often sell candies flavored with tamarindo pulp.
She bought a bag of dried tamarindo to use in her recipes.
The refreshing drink was made from tamarindo and served over ice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tamarind - the fruit or tree of Tamarindus indica
