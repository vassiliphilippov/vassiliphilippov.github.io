flower

noun
plant cultivated for its blooms
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊɚ /; / ˈflaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈflaʊə /; / ˈflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
the part of a plant that is often colorful and produces seeds; a bloom or blossom, or a plant grown for its flowers

Examples

A flower garden
Every spring, the flowers in the park attract crowds of visitors.
Roses are a popular type of flower grown for their beautiful blooms.
She planted a flower in the front yard to add some color to her garden.
Each flower on the tree has delicate white petals.
She picked a flower and placed it in her hair for the party.
The flower of the rose bush opened early in the morning.
