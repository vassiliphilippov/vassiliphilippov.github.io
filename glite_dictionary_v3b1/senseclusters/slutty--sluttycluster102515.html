slutty

adjective
sexually provocative
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈslʌti / (AmE), / ˈslʌti / (BrE)
describing clothing, appearance, or behavior that is sexually provocative or intended to attract sexual attention, often considered inappropriate or disrespectful

Examples

Slutty outfit
Some people thought her slutty outfit was inappropriate for the office party.
He criticized the movie for portraying women in a slutty manner.
The magazine featured a guide to avoid looking slutty while dressing stylishly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modest - not intended to attract sexual attention; reserved
  • synonym: provocative - intended to arouse sexual interest or attention
