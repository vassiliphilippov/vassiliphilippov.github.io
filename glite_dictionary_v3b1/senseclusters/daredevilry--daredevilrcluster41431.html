daredevilry

noun
reckless daring or risk-taking behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛrˌdɛvəlri / (AmE), / ˈdeəˌdevlri / (BrE)
reckless boldness or daring behavior, especially actions involving risk or danger

Examples

Bold daredevilry acts
Critics accused the pilot of unnecessary daredevilry during the dangerous air show.
His feats of daredevilry on the motorcycle left the audience gasping in awe.
The climbers' daredevilry worried their friends, who feared for their safety.

Related Terms

  • antonym: caution - careful avoidance of danger or risks
  • synonym: recklessness - lack of concern for danger or consequences
