ecological terrorism

noun
violence for environmentalist aims
Pronunciation: / ˌikoʊˈlɑdʒɪkəl ˈtɛrəˌrɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌiːkəˈlɒdʒɪkəl ˈtɛrərɪzəm / (BrE)
The use or threat of violence to achieve the political or social aims of environmental groups.

Examples

Ecological terrorism actions
Authorities are taking new measures to combat ecological terrorism targeting logging operations.
Some activists reject the label of ecological terrorism for their direct actions against polluters.
The group was accused of ecological terrorism after setting fire to construction equipment in protest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eco-terrorism - violence or sabotage committed in support of environmental causes
  • degree higher: terrorism - the unlawful use of violence and intimidation, especially for political aims
