eye mask

noun
mask covering area around the eyes
Pronunciation: / aɪ mæsk / (AmE), / aɪ mɑːsk / (BrE)
a mask that covers the area around the eyes, often with holes for the eyes, used for sleeping or as part of a costume

Examples

A soft eye mask
I use a soft eye mask every night to help me sleep.
She wore an elegant eye mask to the masquerade ball.
The actor removed his eye mask after the performance.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: blindfold - a piece of cloth used to cover someone's eyes
  • synonym: sleep mask - a mask worn over the eyes to block out light while sleeping
