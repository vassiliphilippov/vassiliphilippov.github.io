hackleback

noun
North American sturgeon
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhækəlˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈhækəlˌbæk / (BrE)
a species of North American sturgeon valued for its roe, often used to produce caviar

Examples

Hackleback sturgeon
The chef prepared caviar from hackleback sturgeon for the special dinner.
Many restaurants serve hackleback caviar as a more affordable alternative to traditional varieties.
Fishermen in the Midwest often catch hackleback in large rivers.
