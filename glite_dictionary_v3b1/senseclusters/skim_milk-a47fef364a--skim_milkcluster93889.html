skim milk

noun
milk with the cream removed; low-fat milk
1.0
Pronunciation: / skɪm mɪlk / (AmE), / skɪm mɪlk / (BrE)
milk from which the cream has been removed, leaving a lower fat content

Examples

A glass of skim milk
At breakfast, he poured a glass of skim milk instead of whole milk.
Many people use skim milk in recipes to make them healthier.
She prefers skim milk in her coffee to reduce the fat content.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: low-fat milk - milk with some fat removed, but not as much as skim milk
  • antonym: whole milk - milk with all of its cream and fat content
