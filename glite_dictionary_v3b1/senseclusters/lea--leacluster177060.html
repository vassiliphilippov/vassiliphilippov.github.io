lea

noun
grassy field for grazing
8.1 0.11/m
Pronunciation: / liÀê / (AmE), / liÀê / (BrE)
an open field of grassland, especially one used for grazing livestock

Examples

Green lea
Morning dew sparkled on the wide lea as the sun rose.
The cottage was surrounded by a beautiful lea stretching toward the river.
The sheep grazed peacefully on the green lea beneath the hills.

Synonyms

  • meadowland
    3.1 0.06/m
    meadowland is an open grass area with wildflowers and soft grass for grazing, while a lea is a general grazing field.
  • pasture
    1.4 9.41/m
    pasture is land managed for animals to graze, while a lea is a more natural open field often left unmown.
  • ley
    9.9 0.32/m
    ley is a field sown with grass for grazing or mowing, while a lea is any open grass field used for grazing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meadow - a field of grass, often cut for hay
