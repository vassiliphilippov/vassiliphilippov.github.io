soundstage

noun
indoor filming area
5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsaʊnd.steɪdʒ/ (AmE), /ˈsaʊnd.steɪdʒ/ (BrE)
a large room or studio specially equipped for filming movies, television shows, or music productions indoors

Examples

Movie soundstage
The director walked onto the soundstage to check the lighting before filming began.
Many famous movies have been shot entirely on a soundstage to control the environment.
The crew built an elaborate set inside the soundstage for the new science fiction series.
The director inspected the soundstage before the crew began setting up the lights.
Many famous movies have been filmed on this historic soundstage in Hollywood.
The musicians recorded their new album on a state-of-the-art soundstage downtown.

Related Terms

  • antonym: location - a place outside a studio where filming takes place
  • synonym: set - the scenery and props arranged for filming or performing
  • synonym: studio - a room or building where films, music, or television shows are produced
