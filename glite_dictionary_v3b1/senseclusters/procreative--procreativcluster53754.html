procreative

adjective
relating to producing offspring
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊkriˌeɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˈprəʊkriˌeɪtɪv / (BrE)
relating to or capable of producing offspring or new life.

Examples

Procreative abilities
Certain species have impressive procreative abilities, enabling them to multiply rapidly in ideal habitats.
She spoke about the procreative power of nature and its role in ecosystems.
Some medical treatments can unfortunately diminish a person's procreative potential.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reproductive - relating to reproduction or the ability to reproduce
  • antonym: sterile - not able to produce offspring
