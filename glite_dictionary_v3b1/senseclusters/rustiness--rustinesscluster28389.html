rustiness

noun
lack of skill from disuse or inexperience
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌsti.nəs / (AmE), / ˈrʌsti.nɪs / (BrE)
lack of skill or ability, especially due to not practicing or having not done something for a long time

Examples

Rustiness in skills
After years away from the piano, his rustiness was obvious during the recital.
Her rustiness in speaking French disappeared after a few weeks in Paris.
Some rustiness is to be expected when returning to a sport after a long break.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awkwardness - lack of grace or ease; clumsiness
  • synonym: ineptitude - lack of skill or ability
  • antonym: proficiency - a high degree of skill; competence
