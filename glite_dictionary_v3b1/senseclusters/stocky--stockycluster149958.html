stocky

adjective
short and solid in build
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑki / (AmE), / ˈstɒki / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) having a broad, solid, and often short build.

Examples

A stocky build
His stocky frame made him perfect for playing rugby.
She bred stocky horses known for their strength.
The stocky man moved quickly despite his build.

Related Terms

  • antonym: skinny - very thin, sometimes unhealthily so
  • antonym: slender - gracefully thin in build
  • synonym: thickset - having a broad and solid build
