hopeful

noun
an aspiring person, candidate, or competitor
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊpfəl / (AmE), / ˈhəʊpfəl / (BrE)
a person who aspires to achieve something, especially in a competition or for a position

Examples

A young hopeful
Many young hopefuls move to Hollywood dreaming of becoming stars.
The party’s presidential hopeful gave a rousing speech to the crowd.
There were more than fifty hopefuls auditioning for the lead role in the musical.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aspirant - a person who has ambitions to achieve something
  • synonym: candidate - a person applying for a job or competing for election
