olde

adjective
archaic spelling
Pronunciation: / oʊld / (AmE), / əʊld / (BrE)
an archaic or stylized spelling of 'old', often used to evoke a sense of the past or charm

Examples

The olde bookshop
The sign above the door read 'Ye olde Bookshop', welcoming visitors inside.
Tourists enjoyed the atmosphere of the olde village with its cobblestone streets.
They decorated the restaurant in an olde English style to attract more customers.
