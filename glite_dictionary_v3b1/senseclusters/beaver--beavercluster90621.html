beaver

noun
a full beard
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːvər / (AmE), / ˈbiːvə / (BrE)
A full beard that covers the cheeks and chin, especially as worn in former times.

Examples

A thick beaver beard
He prided himself on growing a fine beaver for the winter festivities.
His thick beaver gave him a distinguished and old-fashioned appearance.
In portraits of early explorers, a beaver was often seen as a sign of maturity.
