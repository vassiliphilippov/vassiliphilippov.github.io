underboss

noun
deputy leader in a crime syndicate
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚˌbɔs / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌbɒs / (BrE)
the second-in-command or assistant to the chief in an organized crime syndicate

Examples

The underboss of a gang
After the boss was arrested, the underboss took over operations temporarily.
He rose quickly through the ranks to become the underboss of the family.
The police targeted the underboss in an effort to weaken the entire organization.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: boss - chief leader in an organization or group
  • degree lower: capo - mid-level leader in a crime syndicate, subordinate to an underboss
