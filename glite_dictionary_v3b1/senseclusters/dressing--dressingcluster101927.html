dressing

noun
putting on clothes
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of putting on clothes

Examples

Morning dressing
After her shower, she quickly finished her dressing before breakfast.
Getting the children ready in the morning involves a lot of dressing and organizing.
He needed help with his dressing after his arm injury.
