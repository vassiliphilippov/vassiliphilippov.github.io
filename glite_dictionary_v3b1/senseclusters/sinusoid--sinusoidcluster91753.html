sinusoid

noun
wave-shaped curve of y = sin x
7.3 7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪnəˌsɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈsʌɪ.njuˌsɔɪd / (BrE)
A curve described by the equation y = sin x; a wave-shaped curve representing the sine function.
