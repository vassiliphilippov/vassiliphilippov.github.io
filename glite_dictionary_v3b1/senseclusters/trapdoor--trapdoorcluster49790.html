trapdoor

noun
hidden floor or ceiling door
Pronunciation: / ˈtræpˌdɔr / (AmE), / ˈtræpˌdɔː / (BrE)
a hinged or removable door in a floor or ceiling, often used to provide hidden or special access to another space

Examples

Hidden trapdoor
The magician disappeared through a hidden trapdoor on the stage.
They found a dusty trapdoor in the attic that led to a secret room.
A trapdoor in the floor allowed quick access to the basement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: door - a movable barrier used to open and close an entrance
  • synonym: hatch - a small door or opening, especially in a floor, wall, or roof
