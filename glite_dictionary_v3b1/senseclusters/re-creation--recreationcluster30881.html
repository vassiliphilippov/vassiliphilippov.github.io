re-creation

noun
the act of creating something again
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˌriː.krɪˈeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌriː.krɪˈeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of creating something again, especially after it has been destroyed or lost

Examples

A re-creation of artwork
Following the fire, there was a faithful re-creation of the lost artwork using old photographs.
The re-creation of the ancient temple took years of careful work by historians.
The film is a re-creation of a famous historical event, designed to be as accurate as possible.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: creation - the act of bringing something into existence for the first time
  • synonym: restoration - the act of returning something to a former condition or position
