blueline

noun
blue line on map
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a blue line drawn on a technical drawing or map to indicate a specific feature or route

Examples

Blueline on a map
The blueline on the map shows where the new highway will be built.
Engineers added a blueline to indicate the proposed water pipeline route.
Please follow the blueline to trace the river's path through the valley.

  • synonym: contour line - a line on a map joining points of equal elevation
