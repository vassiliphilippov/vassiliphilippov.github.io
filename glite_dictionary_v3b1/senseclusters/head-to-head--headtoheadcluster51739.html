head-to-head

noun
one-on-one contest or comparison
3.9 0.72/m
Pronunciation: / \u02cch\u025bd t\u0259 \u02c8h\u025bd / (AmE), / \u02cch\u025bd t\u0259 \u02c8h\u025bd / (BrE)
a direct one-on-one contest, comparison, or meeting between two people, teams, products, or ideas.

Examples

A head-to-head contest
The upcoming head-to-head between the two smartphones will decide which has longer battery life.
At the conference they filmed a live head-to-head debate between the industry leaders on AI safety.
They scheduled a head-to-head to compare the two candidates before the promotion.

Synonyms

    3.2 2.54/m

  • matchup
    3.7 3.75/m

  • duel
