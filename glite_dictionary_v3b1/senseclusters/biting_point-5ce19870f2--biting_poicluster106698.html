biting point

noun
critical or intense moment
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪtɪŋ pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈbaɪtɪŋ pɔɪnt / (BrE)

Examples

The negotiations reached a biting point when both sides refused to compromise.
