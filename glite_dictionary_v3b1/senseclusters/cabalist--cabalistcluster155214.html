cabalist

noun
expert in esoteric matters
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkæbəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈkabəlɪst / (BrE)
An expert who is highly skilled in obscure, complex, or esoteric matters; one who has secret or specialized knowledge, especially of mystical or occult subjects.

Examples

Mystical cabalist
Only a true cabalist could decipher the hidden meanings in those cryptic writings.
She was known among scholars as a cabalist in the art of mathematical puzzles.
The cabalist amazed his audience with his deep understanding of mystical texts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expert - a person with a high level of knowledge or skill
  • antonym: novice - a person who is new to something; beginner
