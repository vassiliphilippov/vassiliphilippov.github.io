anon

adverb
soon; in a short while (archaic)
6.0
Pronunciation: / əˈnɑn / (AmE), / əˈnɒn / (BrE)
(archaic or literary) soon; in a short while

Examples

Help comes anon
Fear not, for assistance shall arrive anon.
She said she would return anon to finish the story.
The king will come forth anon, as the herald announced.
Ever and anon, the old clock would chime in the silent hall.
The messenger promised he would return anon with news from the front.
We shall meet anon, after the feast has ended.
