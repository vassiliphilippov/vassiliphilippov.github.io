superculture

noun
dominant overarching culture
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌkʌltʃər / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəkʌltʃə / (BrE)
a dominant culture that includes and influences several smaller subcultures within a society or region

Examples

A global superculture
The spread of technology has helped create a global superculture that influences local traditions.
Many young people feel pressure to conform to the values of the dominant superculture in their country.
As a superculture develops, it often absorbs elements from various subcultures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mainstream - the ideas, attitudes, or activities regarded as normal or conventional
  • antonym: subculture - a cultural group within a larger culture, often with different beliefs or interests
