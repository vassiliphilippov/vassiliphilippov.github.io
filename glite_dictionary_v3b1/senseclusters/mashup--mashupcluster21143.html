Synonyms

  • smashup
    5.3 0.03/m
    smashup is a casual term for mixing styles and tracks loudly together, while mashup names a creative blend from different sources.
  • fusion
    4.1 2/m
    fusion is joining styles into one new form often in music or food, while mashup keeps parts recognizably separate and playful.
  • medley
    4.6 1/m
    medley is a sequence of songs tied together in performance, while mashup combines parts simultaneously to make a new track.
  • blend
    2.3 5/m
    blend is a smooth mix of different things, often without clear parts, while mashup mixes distinct sources in a creative way.
  • amalgam
    6.8 0.4/m
    amalgam is a formal word for a mixed whole made of many elements, while mashup feels informal and media-focused.
  • mixture
    1.2 3/m
    mixture is a neutral, general joining of things without creative intent, while mashup implies artful blending of media elements.
