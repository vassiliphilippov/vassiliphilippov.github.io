unoppressed

adjective
not oppressed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈprɛst / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈprɛst / (BrE)
not subject to oppression, injustice, or harsh treatment

Examples

An unoppressed society
They dreamed of living in an unoppressed society where everyone had equal rights.
The novel describes a future world where people are finally unoppressed and free to express themselves.
Activists work tirelessly to ensure that all communities remain unoppressed and treated fairly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: free - not under the control or power of another
  • antonym: oppressed - subject to harsh and unjust treatment
