phytotherapy

noun
treatment of illness with medicinal plants
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪtoʊˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / ˌfʌɪtəʊˈθɛrəpi / (BrE)
the use of plants or plant extracts to treat diseases or promote health, especially using plants not commonly part of the diet

Examples

Phytotherapy with plants
A growing body of research supports the effectiveness of phytotherapy for certain conditions.
Many people turn to phytotherapy as an alternative to conventional medicine.
The conference on phytotherapy attracted experts in herbal medicine from around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: herbalism - the practice of using herbs for medicinal purposes
