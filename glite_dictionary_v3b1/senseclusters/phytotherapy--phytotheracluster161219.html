Synonyms

  • botanical medicine
    4.7 0.02/m
    botanical medicine uses plant extracts as remedies in clinical or research settings, while phytotherapy is the general medical use of plants.
  • herbal therapy
    4.2 0.01/m
    herbal therapy uses plant extracts to treat people in everyday healthcare, while phytotherapy names the formal medical use of plants.
  • herbal medicine
    3.8 0.01/m
    herbal medicine refers to plant-based products or preparations for health, while phytotherapy is the medical practice of using those plants.
  • herbalism
    5.1 0.09/m
    herbalism is the traditional study and practice of using plants, while phytotherapy is the clinical medical use of plant extracts.
