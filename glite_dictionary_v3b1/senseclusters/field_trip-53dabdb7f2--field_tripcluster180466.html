field trip

noun
organized group educational visit
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːld ˌtrɪp / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ˌtrɪp / (BrE)
an organized visit by a group, especially students, to a place of interest for educational purposes

Examples

A field trip day
I learned more during that field trip than I did in weeks of lessons.
Our biology teacher organized a field trip to study plants in the local park.
The class went on a field trip to the science museum last Friday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excursion - a short journey or trip, especially for leisure or education
