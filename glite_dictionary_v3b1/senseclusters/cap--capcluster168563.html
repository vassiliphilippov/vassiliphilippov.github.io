cap

noun
small explosive device for initiating blasts
Pronunciation: / kæp / (AmE), / kap / (BrE)
a small mechanical or electrical device containing explosive material, used to initiate a larger explosion or reaction, as in blasting operations or toy guns.

Examples

Toy gun cap
Children love the sound of toy guns when the cap goes off with a pop.
The miner inserted a cap into the drill hole to trigger the explosives.
The technician checked each cap carefully before the demolition began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blasting cap - a small detonator used to trigger explosives
