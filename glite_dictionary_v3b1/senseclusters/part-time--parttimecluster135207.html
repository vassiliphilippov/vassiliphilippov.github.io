part-time

adjective
involving less than standard hours
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrt ˌtaɪm /; / ˈpɑrtˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈpɑːt ˌtaɪm /; / ˈpɑːt.taɪm / (BrE)
working or involving less than the usual or full number of hours (not full-time), especially for jobs or study

Examples

After his retirement, he took on a part-time consultancy role.
Many students find part-time jobs to support themselves during their studies.
She decided to work part-time while finishing her degree.
