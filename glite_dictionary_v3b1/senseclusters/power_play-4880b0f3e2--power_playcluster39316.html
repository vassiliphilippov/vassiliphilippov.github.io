power play

noun
sports tactic involving player concentration
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊər pleɪ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊə pleɪ / (BrE)
a tactic in a team sport in which several players concentrate in one area of the field to gain an advantage

Examples

Team power play
Our team often practices the power play to maximize our chances of scoring.
The coach called for a power play near the goal line to break through the defense.
They executed a brilliant power play during the final minutes of the game.
