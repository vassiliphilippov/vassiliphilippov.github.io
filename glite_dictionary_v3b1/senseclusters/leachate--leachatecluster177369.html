leachate

noun
liquid from waste
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈliːtʃeɪt / (AmE), / ˈliːtʃeɪt / (BrE)
liquid that has passed through waste or other material and contains dissolved or suspended substances from it

Examples

Landfill leachate

If leachate escapes into groundwater, it can cause serious environmental problems.

Engineers designed a system to collect and process leachate from the waste site.
If not managed properly, leachate can contaminate nearby groundwater sources.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contaminant - a substance that makes something impure or unsafe
  • synonym: effluent - liquid waste or sewage discharged into the environment
  • synonym: runoff - water that flows over the ground surface and may carry substances with it
