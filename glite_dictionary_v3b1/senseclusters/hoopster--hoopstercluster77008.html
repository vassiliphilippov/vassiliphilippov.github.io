hoopster

noun
hula hoop performer
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhuːpstər / (AmE), / ˈhuːpstə / (BrE)
a person who performs tricks or dances skillfully with a hula hoop

Examples

Skilled hoopster performance
The talented hoopster spun three hula hoops at once during her performance.
Children gathered around to watch the hoopster show off amazing tricks at the park.
At the festival, a colorful hoopster amazed the crowd with fast hula-hoop tricks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acrobat - a person who performs gymnastic or physical feats
  • synonym: hoop dancer - a performer who dances with one or more hoops
