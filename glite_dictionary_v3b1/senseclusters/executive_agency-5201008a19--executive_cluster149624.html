executive agency

noun
government agency within the executive branch
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɛkjətɪv ˈeɪdʒənsi / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɛkjʊtɪv ˈeɪdʒənsi / (BrE)
A government agency that is part of the executive branch and is responsible for specific administrative functions.

Examples

A executive agency office

Each executive agency has its own set of responsibilities and areas of oversight.
The Environmental Protection Agency is an important executive agency in the United States.
