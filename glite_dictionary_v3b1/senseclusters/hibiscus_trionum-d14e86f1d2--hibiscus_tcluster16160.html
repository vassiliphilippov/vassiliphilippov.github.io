Hibiscus trionum

noun
a weedy herb with yellow, purple-eyed flowers
Pronunciation: / haɪˈbɪskəs traɪˈoʊnəm / (AmE), / hɪˈbɪskəs traɪˈɒnəm / (BrE)
an annual weedy herb with short-lived yellow flowers with purple centers, native to the Old World tropics and naturalized in North America
