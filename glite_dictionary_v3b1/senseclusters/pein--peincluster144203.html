pein

noun
hammer end opposite face
9.9
Pronunciation: / piːn / (AmE), / piːn / (BrE)
the end part of a hammer head, opposite the striking face, used for shaping or bending materials

Examples

Hammer pein
The blacksmith used the pein of the hammer to curve the hot metal rod.
Make sure to use the pein when you need to shape or bend nails.
He explained that the pein is essential for certain metalworking tasks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peen - the end of a hammer head opposite the striking face
  • antonym: striking face - the flat part of a hammer used for hitting
