The mass of an atom of a chemical element, typically expressed in atomic mass units (amu).

Examples

Atomic mass unit
Knowing the atomic mass helps chemists calculate the proportions of elements in a compound.
Scientists use the atomic mass to identify elements in the periodic table.
The atomic mass of carbon is approximately 12 atomic mass units.

Synonyms

  • relative atomic mass
    5.3 0.00/m
    relative atomic mass compares an atom's mass to one-twelfth of carbon-12, while atomic mass refers to the mass of a specific atom.
  • atomic weight
    3.9 0.26/m
    atomic weight gives the average mass of all natural isotopes of an element, while atomic mass names the mass of a single atom.
