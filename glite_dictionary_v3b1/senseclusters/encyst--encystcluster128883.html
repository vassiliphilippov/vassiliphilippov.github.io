encyst

verb
to form a cyst
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɪst / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɪst / (BrE)
to enclose or become enclosed in a cyst or protective sac, especially as part of a biological process

Examples

To encyst in tissues
Certain parasites can encyst in the tissues of their host to survive harsh conditions.
The organism will encyst when the environment becomes unfavorable for growth.
Doctors observed that the bacteria had begun to encyst within the patient's lungs.
