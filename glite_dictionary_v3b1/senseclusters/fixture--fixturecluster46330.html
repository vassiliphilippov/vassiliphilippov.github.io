fixture

noun
permanently attached object/fitting
4.7 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪks.tʃər/ (AmE), /ˈfɪks.tʃə/ (BrE)
an object or piece of equipment that is permanently attached to a house or building and is not intended to be removed

Examples

Bathroom fixture
Light fixtures should be replaced if they no longer work properly.
The bathroom fixtures were all modern and stylish.
When you move out, please do not remove any fixtures from the apartment.
