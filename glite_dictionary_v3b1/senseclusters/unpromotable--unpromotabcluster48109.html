unpromotable

adjective
not eligible for promotion
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnprəˈmoʊtəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌʌnprəˈməʊtəbl̩ / (BrE)
not able to be promoted to a higher position or level

Examples

Unpromotable employee
Despite his hard work, the company considered him unpromotable because he lacked key skills.
She worried that her current role would make her unpromotable in the future.
Some employees feel unpromotable due to limited opportunities in their department.

Related Terms

  • antonym: promotable - able to be promoted to a higher position
