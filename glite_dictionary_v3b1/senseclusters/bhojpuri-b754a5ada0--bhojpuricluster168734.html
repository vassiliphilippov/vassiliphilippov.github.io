Bhojpuri

proper noun
language of northern India
Pronunciation: / boʊdʒˈpʊri / (AmE), / bəʊdʒˈpʊəri / (BrE)
an Indo-Aryan language spoken mainly in northern India and Nepal

Examples

Bhojpuri language
She learned to speak Bhojpuri from her grandparents in the village.
Many popular songs in northern India are sung in Bhojpuri.
The film was dubbed into Bhojpuri for local audiences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Hindi - an Indo-Aryan language widely spoken in India
  • synonym: Nepali - the official language of Nepal
