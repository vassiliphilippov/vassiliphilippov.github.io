whirlwind

noun
period of intense activity or rapid change
8.9 3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈwɜːrl.wɪnd/ /; / /ˈhwɜːrl.wɪnd/ (AmE), /ˈwɜːl.wɪnd/ /; / /ˈhwɜːl.wɪnd/ (BrE)
A situation or period of intense activity, rapid change, or confusion.

Examples

A whirlwind of activity
After the announcement, her life became a whirlwind of interviews and events.
The company's growth has been a whirlwind over the past year.
Their romance was a whirlwind, moving from first date to marriage in a few months.

Related Terms
