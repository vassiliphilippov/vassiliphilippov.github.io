lollygag

verb
spend time aimlessly; dawdle
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑliˌɡæɡ /; / ˈlæliˌɡæɡ / (AmE), / ˈlɒliˌɡæɡ /; / ˈlalɪˌɡaɡ / (BrE)
to spend time aimlessly without any apparent purpose; to dawdle or loiter

  • synonym: loiter - to stand or wait around without apparent purpose
