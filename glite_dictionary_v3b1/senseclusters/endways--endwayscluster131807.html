Synonyms

  • endwise
    7.1 0.01/m
    endwise shows the ends facing a direction or standing upright, while endways means lengthwise with the end forward toward the viewer.
  • lengthwise
    3.0 1.07/m
    lengthwise runs along the long side or full length of something, while endways implies the end points toward the observer.
  • lengthways
    3.4 0.00/m
    lengthways means along the length or longest side of an object, while endways stresses the end pointing forward.
  • longways

  • longwise
