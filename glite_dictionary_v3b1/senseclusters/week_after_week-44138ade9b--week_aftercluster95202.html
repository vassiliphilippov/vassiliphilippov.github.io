week after week

adverb
repeatedly over many weeks
Pronunciation: / wiːk ˈæftər wiːk / (AmE), / wiːk ˈɑːftə wiːk / (BrE)
for an indefinite number of successive weeks; repeatedly, one week after another

Examples

Week after week practice
He practiced the piano week after week until he finally improved.
She called her mother week after week, never missing a Sunday.
The team lost week after week, but their fans never gave up hope.
