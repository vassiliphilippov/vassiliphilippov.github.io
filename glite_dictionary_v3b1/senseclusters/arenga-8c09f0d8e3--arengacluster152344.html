Arenga

noun
genus of tropical Asian palm trees
Pronunciation: / əˈrɛŋɡə / (AmE), / əˈrɛŋɡə / (BrE)
A genus of tropical Asian and Malaysian palm trees, some of which are cultivated for their fiber or sap.

Examples

Arenga palm tree
Researchers studied the growth patterns of several Arenga species in Malaysia.
Some traditional sweeteners are made from the sap of the Arenga palm.
The Arenga palm is valued for its edible sap and strong fiber.
