snowcat

noun
tracked snow vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊˌkæt / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌkæt / (BrE)
a vehicle with tracks that is specially designed to travel over snow-covered terrain

Examples

A snowcat vehicle
The rescue team arrived in a snowcat after the heavy blizzard blocked the roads.
Tourists enjoyed a ride in the snowcat up to the mountain lodge.
A snowcat cleared the ski slopes early in the morning before the resort opened.

Related Terms

  • synonym: snowmobile - a small motor vehicle for traveling over snow, usually with skis at the front
