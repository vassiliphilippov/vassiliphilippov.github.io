rub shoulders

verb
mingle socially, especially with important people
3.0
Pronunciation: / rʌb ˈʃoʊldɚz / (AmE), / rʌb ˈʃəʊldəz / (BrE)
to associate or mix socially with other people, especially those who are important or famous

Examples

To rub shoulders with stars
At the exclusive party, she hoped to rub shoulders with movie stars and musicians.
During his internship, he had the chance to rub shoulders with top executives.
Young entrepreneurs often attend these conferences to rub shoulders with industry leaders.
