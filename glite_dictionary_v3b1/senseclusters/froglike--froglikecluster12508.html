froglike

adjective
resembling a frog
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɔgˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈfrɒgˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or similar to a frog in appearance, movement, or characteristics

Examples

Froglike legs
The creature's froglike legs allowed it to leap great distances across the pond.
Her costume included a green, froglike mask with big eyes and a wide mouth.
The statue had a froglike appearance, with squat limbs and a rounded body.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amphibian - a cold-blooded animal such as a frog, toad, or salamander
