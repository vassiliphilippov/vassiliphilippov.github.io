miscopy

noun
copying error
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪs.kɑː.piː/ (AmE), /ˈmɪs.kɒp.iː/ (BrE)
a copy of a text or document that contains mistakes, or an error made during copying

Examples

A miscopy in a book
The historian found a miscopy in the ancient manuscript that changed the meaning of the sentence.
After reviewing the report, she noticed a miscopy that needed to be fixed before submission.
A single miscopy in the legal document caused confusion during the trial.
