dong

noun
bell sound
Pronunciation: / dɑŋ / (AmE), / dɒŋ / (BrE)
a deep, resonant sound produced by a large bell or clock when struck

Examples

Loud dong sound
The dong of the clock signaled that it was midnight.
We heard a loud dong as the church bell rang across the town.
Each dong echoed through the empty hall, marking the passing hours.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chime - a clear, ringing sound made by a bell
  • synonym: clang - a loud, resonant metallic sound
