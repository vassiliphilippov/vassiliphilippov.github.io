nightshade vegetable

noun
edible plant from the nightshade family
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtˌʃeɪd ˈvɛdʒtəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtʃeɪd ˈvɛdʒtəbl̩ / (BrE)
Any edible plant belonging to the nightshade family, such as tomatoes, potatoes, peppers, and eggplants, some of which may cause allergic reactions in certain people.

Examples

Nightshade vegetables like tomatoes
Nightshade vegetables should be avoided by individuals with certain food sensitivities.
Some people develop rashes after eating nightshade vegetables like tomatoes and eggplants.
Tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers are all classified as nightshade vegetables.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nightshade - a plant of the Solanaceae family, especially one with poisonous properties
