electrographic

adjective
recording electrical activity
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to the recording of electrical activity, especially in the body or in medical tests

Examples

Electrographic brain test
The doctor ordered an electrographic test to check the patient's brain activity.
Modern hospitals rely on electrographic equipment to monitor vital signs during surgery.
Researchers analyzed the electrographic data to study patterns in muscle movement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electrical - relating to electricity
  • synonym: electrocardiographic - relating to the recording of the heart's electrical activity
