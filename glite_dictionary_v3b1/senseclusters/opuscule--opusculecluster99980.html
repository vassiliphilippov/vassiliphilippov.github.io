opuscule

noun
small literary work
Pronunciation: / oʊˈpʌskjuːl / (AmE), / əʊˈpʌskjuːl / (BrE)
a small or minor literary or musical work, often of little importance

Examples

A small opuscule
The author released an opuscule containing several short stories.
Her latest opuscule was praised for its concise and elegant style.
He composed a brief opuscule for piano that delighted the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: essay - a short piece of writing on a particular subject
  • antonym: tome - a large and heavy book
