Thamnophis proximus

noun
a nonvenomous striped North American snake species
Pronunciation: / θæmˈnɑːfɪs ˈproʊksɪməs / (AmE), / θæmˈnɒfɪs ˈprɒksɪməs / (BrE)
A species of nonvenomous snake, commonly known as the western ribbon snake, characterized by yellow or reddish stripes and found in North American woodlands, grasslands, and tropical regions.
