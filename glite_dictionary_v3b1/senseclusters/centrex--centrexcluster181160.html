centrex

noun
business telephone exchange service
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛn.tɹɛks / (AmE), / ˈsɛnt.rɛks / (BrE)
a telephone exchange service in which the telephone company provides central office switching for a business or organization, simulating a private branch exchange (PBX) system.

Examples

A centrex service
A centrex service allows employees to call each other using internal extensions.
Many businesses prefer centrex solutions over traditional PBX to reduce maintenance costs.
The company upgraded to a centrex system to handle its growing volume of calls.

Related Terms

  • synonym: PBX - private branch exchange, a private telephone network used within an organization
