Old World coral snake

noun
venomous elapid snake of Asia, Africa, Australia
2.9
Pronunciation: / oʊld wɝːld ˈkɔr.əl sneɪk / (AmE), / əʊld wɜːld ˈkɒr.əl sneɪk / (BrE)
