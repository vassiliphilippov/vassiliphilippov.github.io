prolifically

adverb
in great quantity
6.3
Pronunciation: / prəˈlɪfɪkli / (AmE), / prəˈlɪfɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that produces a large amount or many results, especially in a short time

Examples

To prolifically produce art
The artist painted prolifically throughout her career, creating hundreds of works.
He published prolifically in scientific journals, making significant contributions to his field.
Tom writes prolifically, often finishing several short stories in a single month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abundantly - in large quantities; plentifully
  • antonym: sparsely - in small amounts or numbers; thinly
