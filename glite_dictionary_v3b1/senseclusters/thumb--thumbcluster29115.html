thumb

verb
to travel by hitchhiking
Pronunciation: / θʌm / (AmE), / θʌm / (BrE)
to travel by getting free rides from passing vehicles, especially by signaling with an outstretched thumb

Examples

To thumb a ride
He thumbed a ride to the nearest town after his car broke down.
She spent the afternoon thumbing along the highway, hoping someone would pick her up.
They decided to thumb their way across Europe during their summer break.
