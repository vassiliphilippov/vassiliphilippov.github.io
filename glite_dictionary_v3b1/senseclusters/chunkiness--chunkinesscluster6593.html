chunkiness

noun
lack of smoothness
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌŋkinəs / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌŋkinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being uneven, broken into noticeable parts, or lacking smoothness, especially in movement, images, or sounds

Examples

Video chunkiness
The chunkiness of the video made it difficult to follow the action during the game.
She complained about the chunkiness in the audio, which caused the music to sound broken.
Upgrading the computer reduced the chunkiness in the animation, making everything look smoother.

Related Terms

  • synonym: choppiness - the quality of being rough or interrupted, especially in movement or sound
  • antonym: smoothness - the quality of being even and without interruptions
