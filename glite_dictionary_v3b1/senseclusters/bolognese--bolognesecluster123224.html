bolognese

noun
small Italian dog breed
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌboʊləˈneɪz / (AmE), / ˌbɒləˈneɪz / (BrE)
a small, white, long-haired toy dog breed originally from Bologna, Italy, known for its affectionate nature

Examples

Bolognese dog breed
The bolognese is a gentle companion that enjoys spending time with its family.
At the dog show, her bolognese won first prize for its beautiful coat.
Many people choose a bolognese because of its friendly and playful personality.
