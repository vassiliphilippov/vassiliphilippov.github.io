haunt

verb
recur constantly or trouble persistently
1.0 4.0
Pronunciation: /hɔːnt/ /; / /hɑːnt/ (AmE), /hɔːnt/ (BrE)
to repeatedly appear in someone's mind or to persistently trouble or follow someone, especially in a way that causes anxiety or unease

Examples

She felt as if her mistakes would forever haunt her.
The memory of that tragic night still haunts him years later.
