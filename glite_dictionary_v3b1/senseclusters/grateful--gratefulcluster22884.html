grateful

adjective
giving comfort or pleasure
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪtfəl / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtf(ə)l / (BrE)
(of a thing) giving comfort or pleasure; agreeable or welcome.

Examples

Grateful warmth
After hours in the cold, the grateful heat of the bath was all he wanted.
The aroma of baking bread filled the room with a grateful sense of home.
They welcomed the grateful shade provided by the trees at noon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comforting - providing physical or emotional comfort
  • synonym: pleasant - giving a sense of happy satisfaction or enjoyment
  • antonym: unpleasant - causing discomfort, displeasure, or unhappiness
