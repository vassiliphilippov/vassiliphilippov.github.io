millisievert

noun
radiation dose unit
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪliˈsiːvərt / (AmE), / ˌmɪliˈsiːvət / (BrE)
a unit for measuring the dose of ionizing radiation, equal to one thousandth of a sievert
