lick one's wounds

verb
recover after setback or hurt
Pronunciation: / lɪk wʌnz wundz / (AmE), / lɪk wʌnz wuːndz / (BrE)
to take time to recover after emotional pain, defeat, or disappointment

Examples

To lick his wounds
After failing the exam, he spent a quiet weekend at home to lick his wounds.
She needed some time alone to lick her wounds before returning to work.
The team retreated to the locker room to lick their wounds after the tough defeat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recover - regain strength or confidence after difficulty
