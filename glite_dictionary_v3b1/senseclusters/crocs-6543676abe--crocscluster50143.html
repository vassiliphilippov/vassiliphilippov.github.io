Crocs

noun
foam slip-on shoes
Pronunciation: / krɑks / (AmE), / krɒks / (BrE)
a brand of lightweight, slip-on clogs made of foam, often worn casually and known for their comfort

Examples

A pair of Crocs
He slipped on his Crocs before heading out to the garden.
Many nurses wear Crocs at work because they are comfortable for long shifts.
She packed her Crocs for the beach trip since they dry quickly and are easy to clean.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clogs - shoes with a thick, often wooden sole and open back
  • antonym: dress shoes - formal shoes worn for special occasions
  • synonym: sandals - open shoes with straps, worn in warm weather
