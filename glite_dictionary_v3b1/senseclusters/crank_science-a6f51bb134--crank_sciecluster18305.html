crank science

noun
pseudo-scientific, unproven theories
6.6
Pronunciation: / kræŋk ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / kræŋk ˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
Ideas or theories that are presented as scientific but are actually false, unproven, or based on flawed reasoning.

Examples

Crank science ideas
He dismissed climate change as just another example of crank science.
Many conspiracy theorists rely on crank science to justify their arguments.
The magazine was criticized for publishing articles that promoted crank science.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pseudoscience - claims or practices mistakenly believed to be scientific
