playfully

adverb
in a playful manner
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪfəlli / (AmE), / ˈpleɪfəli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows a sense of fun or lightheartedness; in a playful way.

Examples

To playfully tease
He nudged her playfully as they walked along the beach.
She teased her friend playfully about his new haircut.
The kitten batted at the yarn playfully, chasing it around the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jokingly - in a joking manner
  • antonym: seriously - in a serious manner
