elucidation

noun
clear explanation or clarification
8.1 6.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˌluːsɪˈdeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌluːsɪˈdeɪʃən / (BrE)
an explanation or the act of making something clear and easy to understand

Examples

A clear elucidation
Her detailed elucidation of the process clarified several points for the team.

The elucidation provided in the appendix resolved all of my remaining questions.
The teacher's elucidation of the complex theory made it much easier to grasp.
