team

verb
join others to form a team
2.5 9/m
Pronunciation: / tim / (AmE), / tiÀêm / (BrE)
to come together or join with others to form a team, especially for a particular purpose or activity

Examples

To team up
Many companies team with local artists to promote cultural events.
Researchers often team up to tackle complex scientific problems.
We decided to team together for the charity run.
