paiche

noun
large Amazonian fish
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪtʃeɪ / (AmE), / ˈpaɪtʃeɪ / (BrE)
a very large freshwater fish native to the Amazon River basin, known for its size and importance in local fisheries

Examples

Amazon paiche fish
Fishermen in the Amazon often catch paiche because of its impressive size and value.
The paiche can grow to over two meters long, making it a remarkable sight in the river.
Local markets sell paiche as a delicacy prized for its firm, tasty meat.
