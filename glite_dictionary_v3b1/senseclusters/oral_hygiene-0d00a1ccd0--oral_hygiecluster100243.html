oral hygiene

noun
keeping mouth, teeth, and gums clean
1.0 2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈɔːr.əl ˈhaɪ.dʒiːn/ /; / /ˈoʊr.əl ˈhaɪ.dʒiːn/ (AmE), /ˈɔː.rəl ˈhaɪ.dʒiːn/ /; / /ˈəʊ.rəl ˈhaɪ.dʒiːn/ (BrE)
the practice of keeping the mouth, teeth, and gums clean and healthy to prevent dental problems

Examples

Oral hygiene care
Children should be taught the basics of oral hygiene from an early age.
Practicing good oral hygiene reduces the risk of cavities and gum disease.
Your dentist can give you advice on oral hygiene products and techniques.
