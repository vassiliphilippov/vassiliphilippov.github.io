Synonyms

  • dealmaking
    4.1 0.07/m
    dealmaking focuses on closing business deals and the skill of arranging terms, while negotiation is the broader process of reaching an agreement.
  • bargaining
    2.9 0.1/m
    bargaining is haggling over price or terms to get a better deal, while negotiation covers wider formal talks to reach agreement.
  • talks
    2.3 0.10/m
    talks are formal meetings between groups to discuss an agreement, while negotiation is the full process of discussing and reaching terms.
