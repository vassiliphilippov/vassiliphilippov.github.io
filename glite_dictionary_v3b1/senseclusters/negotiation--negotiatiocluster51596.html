negotiation

noun
process of reaching an agreement by discussion
1.0 3.5
Pronunciation: / nəˌgoʊʃiˈeɪʃən /; / nɪˌɡoʊʃiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / nɪˌɡəʊʃiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of discussing something in order to reach an agreement, especially in business or politics

Examples

Business negotiation
After several hours of negotiation, the two companies finally reached an agreement.
Successful negotiation often requires patience and good communication skills.
Union representatives began a new round of negotiation with management on Tuesday.
After weeks of negotiation, the companies finally agreed on the merger terms.
Salary negotiations with the new employer took longer than expected.
The peace negotiation between the two countries ended successfully.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bargaining - discussion aimed at reaching an agreement, often about price or conditions
  • synonym: mediation - the process by which someone tries to end a disagreement by helping the two sides to talk about and agree on a solution
