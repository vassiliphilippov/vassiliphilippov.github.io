muted

adjective
soft or subdued
4.2 1.43/m
Pronunciation: / Ààmjut…™d / (AmE), / ÀàmjuÀêt…™d / (BrE)
not loud in sound or not bright in color; subdued or softened

Examples

Muted colors
The living room was decorated in muted shades of blue and gray for a peaceful feel.
He spoke in a muted voice so as not to disturb the others in the library.
The artist preferred muted colors to create a calm and gentle atmosphere in her paintings.
