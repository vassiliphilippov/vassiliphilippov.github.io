seasonal migration pattern

noun
regular movement based on seasons
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːzənəl maɪˈɡreɪʃən ˈpætɚn / (AmE), / ˈsiːzənəl maɪˈɡreɪʃən ˈpætn̩ / (BrE)
a regular movement of groups, such as animals or people, in response to changes in the seasons

Examples

The seasonal migration pattern
Climate change can alter the seasonal migration pattern of marine animals.
Many bird species exhibit a seasonal migration pattern, traveling south for the winter.
Researchers documented the seasonal migration pattern of wildebeest across the plains.
