assault

noun
attempted or threatened physical attack
Pronunciation: / əˈsɔlt / (AmE), / əˈsɔːlt / (BrE)
an act of threatening or attempting to cause physical harm to someone; an attack or attempted attack

Examples

A physical assault
She was the victim of an assault but managed to escape unharmed.
The suspect was charged with assault after threatening a passerby with a knife.
Verbal arguments are one thing, but an attempted assault is a criminal offense.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attack - an aggressive physical or verbal act against someone or something
  • antonym: defense - the act of protecting oneself from an attack
