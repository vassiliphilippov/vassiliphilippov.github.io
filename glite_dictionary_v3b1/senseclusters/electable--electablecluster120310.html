electable

adjective
eligible for election
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktəbəl / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktəbəl / (BrE)
legally allowed or eligible to be chosen for a public office by election

Examples

Electable for office
Only citizens over the age of 18 are electable for this position.
Her experience and reputation made her highly electable in the mayoral race.
The new law changed who is electable for public office in the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eligible - having the right to do or obtain something
  • antonym: ineligible - not having the right to do or obtain something
