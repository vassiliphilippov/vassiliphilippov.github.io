trophy wife

noun
attractive young wife of a wealthy older man
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtroʊ.fi waɪf / (AmE), / ˈtrəʊ.fi waɪf / (BrE)
an attractive younger wife, usually of a wealthy older man, who is seen mainly as a status symbol rather than as a traditional partner

Examples

A wealthy man's trophy wife
Although some call her a trophy wife, she manages several charitable foundations herself.
At the gala, many whispered about the CEO and his trophy wife attending together.
He faced criticism for marrying a trophy wife half his age after his divorce.
He was criticized for divorcing his first wife and marrying a trophy wife half his age.
Some people assumed she was just a trophy wife, but she runs her own business.
The gossip magazines often speculate about which celebrities have a trophy wife.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gold digger - someone who forms relationships to extract money or gifts
