coffee mill

noun
device for grinding coffee beans
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑfi mɪl / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi mɪl / (BrE)
a machine or device used for grinding roasted coffee beans into coffee grounds

Examples

A manual coffee mill
Many coffee enthusiasts prefer to use a manual coffee mill to control the grind size.
She carefully filled the coffee mill with fresh beans every morning before making breakfast.
The antique coffee mill was a prized possession inherited from her grandmother.
