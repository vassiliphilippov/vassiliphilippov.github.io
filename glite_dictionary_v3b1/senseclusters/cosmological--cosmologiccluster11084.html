cosmological

adjective
relating to the universe or its origins
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːzməˈlɑːdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌkɒzməˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study of the origin, structure, and laws of the universe
