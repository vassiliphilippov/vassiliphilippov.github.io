Synonyms

  • bore
    4.9 0.29/m
    bore makes a smooth, often deep round hole by turning a bit, while drill means making a hole using a power or hand tool.
  • pierce
    3.5 7.11/m
    pierce means to push a sharp point through something, while drill implies cutting away material with a rotating tool to make a hole.
  • perforate
    6.1 0.59/m
    perforate means to make one or many holes through material, often in formal contexts, while drill focuses on making a single hole with a tool.
