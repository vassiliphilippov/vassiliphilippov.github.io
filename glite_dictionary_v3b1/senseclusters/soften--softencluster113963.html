soften

verb
become or make soft or softer
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑf.ən / (AmE), / ˈsɒf.ən / (BrE)
to become soft or softer, or to make something become soft or softer

Examples

To soften bread
If left out in the rain, the cardboard will soften and lose its shape.
Over time, harsh criticism may soften as people understand your intentions.
Stir the butter until it begins to soften before adding the sugar.
