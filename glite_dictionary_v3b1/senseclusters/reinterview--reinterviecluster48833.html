reinterview

noun
second interview
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈɪntərˌvjuː / (AmE), / ˌriːˈɪntəvjuː / (BrE)
a second or additional interview with a person, usually for clarification or further evaluation

Examples

A second reinterview
After the first round, the candidate was called for a reinterview to discuss her qualifications in more detail.
The company scheduled a reinterview to clarify some points from the initial meeting.
He prepared thoroughly for his reinterview, hoping to make a better impression this time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interview - a formal meeting to ask questions and gather information
  • degree lower: screening - an initial evaluation or assessment, often before an interview
