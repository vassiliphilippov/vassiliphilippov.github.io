blower

noun
electric-powered air-moving device
Pronunciation: / ˈbloʊ.ər /; / ˈbloʊər / (AmE), / ˈbləʊ.ə /; / ˈbləʊə / (BrE)
a machine or device that produces a strong current of air, used for ventilation, cooling, heating, cleaning, or drying

Examples

Electric blower
He turned on the blower to ventilate the workshop after painting.
Modern HVAC systems usually include a powerful blower to circulate air throughout the building.
The industrial blower kept the warehouse cool even during the hottest days.
A leaf blower can make yard work much easier and faster.
The mechanic used a blower to clear dust from the engine compartment.
The ventilation blower in the laboratory must be kept running at all times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fan - a device with rotating blades that creates a current of air
