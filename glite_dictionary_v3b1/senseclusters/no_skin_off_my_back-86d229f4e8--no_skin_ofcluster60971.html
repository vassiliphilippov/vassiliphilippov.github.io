no skin off my back

x
expression of indifference or lack of concern
Pronunciation: / noʊ skɪn ɔf maɪ bæk / (AmE), / nəʊ skɪn ɒf maɪ bæk / (BrE)
used to show that you do not care about or are not affected by something that has happened or may happen to someone else

Examples

It's no skin off my back
If you don't want to come to the party, it's no skin off my back.
It's no skin off my back if they disagree with the decision.
Whether he stays or quits is no skin off my back.
