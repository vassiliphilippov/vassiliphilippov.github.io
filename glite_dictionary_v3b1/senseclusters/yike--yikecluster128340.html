yike

verb
to exclaim in surprise
Pronunciation: / jaɪk / (AmE), / jaɪk / (BrE)
to make a short exclamation or react suddenly with surprise, embarrassment, or mild disgust.

Examples

To yike at news
He couldn't help but yike when he saw the bill was much higher than expected.
She let out a quick yike as the cold water splashed on her feet.
Whenever he hears a sudden loud noise, he tends to yike in surprise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gasp - to take a quick breath in surprise or shock
  • synonym: shriek - to make a loud, high-pitched cry in fear or surprise
