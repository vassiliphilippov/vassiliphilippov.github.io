Synonyms

  • height
    1.0 8.90/m
    height is a general measure of how high something is, while elevation often names a land's raised level or summit.
  • altitude
    4.0 15/m
    altitude is the measured height above sea level often used for aircraft or maps, while elevation refers to land's raised level or summit.
