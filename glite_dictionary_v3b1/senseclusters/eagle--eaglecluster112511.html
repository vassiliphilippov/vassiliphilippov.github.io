eagle

verb
to swoop swiftly
Pronunciation: / ˈiːɡəl / (AmE), / ˈiːɡl̩ / (BrE)
to move or swoop down swiftly in the manner of an eagle.

Examples

To eagle across
The paper airplane eagled across the room toward the window.
The hawk eagled down from the sky to catch its prey.
With a sudden burst of speed, the drone eagled toward the ground.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dive - to move quickly downward through the air
  • antonym: soar - to fly or rise high in the air
