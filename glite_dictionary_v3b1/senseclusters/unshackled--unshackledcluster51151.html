unshackled

adjective
free from shackles or restraints
8.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃækəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃækəld / (BrE)
Not bound by shackles or chains; free from physical or figurative restraints.

Examples

Unshackled and free
After years of strict rules, the artist felt unshackled and painted freely.
He walked out of prison, finally unshackled and free to rebuild his life.
The reforms left the market unshackled from heavy regulation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: liberated - set free; released from confinement or oppression
  • antonym: shackled - bound by shackles or chains; restricted
