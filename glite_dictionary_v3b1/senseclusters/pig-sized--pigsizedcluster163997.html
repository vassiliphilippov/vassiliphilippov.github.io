pig-sized

adjective
about the size of a pig
Pronunciation: / pɪɡ saɪzd / (AmE), / pɪɡ saɪzd / (BrE)
having approximately the same size as a pig

He built a model spaceship that was nearly pig-sized, much larger than anyone expected.
The mysterious creature spotted in the woods was described as pig-sized by several witnesses.
