loosely

adverb
without strict attention to details
3.7 3.24/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàluÀêsli / (AmE), / ÀàluÀêsli / (BrE)
In a way that is not strict, exact, or limited; without regard to precise details or exceptions.

Examples

Loosely defined term
She used the term loosely because it did not exactly fit the situation.
The rules were interpreted loosely to allow for individual circumstances.
We define 'success' loosely here, to include many different kinds of achievements.
