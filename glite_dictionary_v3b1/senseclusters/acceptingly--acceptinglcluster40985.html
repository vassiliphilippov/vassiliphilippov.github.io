acceptingly

adverb
with tolerance
Pronunciation: / əkˈsɛptɪŋli / (AmE), / əkˈsɛptɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows willingness to accept or tolerate something

Examples

Nod acceptingly
He nodded acceptingly when he heard the final decision.
The team responded acceptingly to the changes in the schedule.
She listened acceptingly to their different opinions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: grudgingly - in a way that shows reluctance or unwillingness
  • synonym: tolerantly - in a way that shows willingness to allow or accept something
