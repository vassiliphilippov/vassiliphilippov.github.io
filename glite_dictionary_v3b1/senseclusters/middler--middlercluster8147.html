middler

noun
middle member
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdlər / (AmE), / ˈmɪdlə / (BrE)
a person or thing that is in the middle position within a group, sequence, or range

Examples

Family middler
As the middler in a family of five, she often played the role of peacemaker.
The middler in the relay team handed the baton smoothly to the anchor runner.
In a row of seven houses, the middler had the best view of the park.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extremity - the furthest point or limit of something
  • synonym: middleman - a person who is in an intermediate position between others
