middler

noun
middle member
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdlər / (AmE), / ˈmɪdlə / (BrE)
a person or thing that is in the middle position within a group, sequence, or range

Examples

Family middler
As the middler in a family of five, she often played the role of peacemaker.

In a row of seven houses, the middler had the best view of the park.
