natty

adjective
smart and fashionable in appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈnæti / (AmE), / ˈnæti / (BrE)
(of a person or their clothing) smart and fashionable in appearance or style.

Examples

A natty dresser
He always looks natty in his tailored suits and polished shoes.
She wore a natty little jacket to the party, drawing many compliments.
They admired the natty uniforms worn by the hotel staff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dapper - neat, trim, and stylish in appearance
  • antonym: sloppy - careless and untidy in appearance or dress
