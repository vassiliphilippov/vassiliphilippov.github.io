Wabash

proper noun
U.S. river, tributary of the Ohio
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːˌbæʃ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːˌbæʃ / (BrE)
a river in the United States, rising in western Ohio and flowing southwest across Indiana to join the Ohio River
