footboard

noun
narrow board for standing or foot support
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊt.bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈfʊt.bɔːd / (BrE)
a narrow platform or board on which to stand or place one's feet, often found on vehicles such as trains or beds for support or protection

Examples

Train footboard
He rested his feet on the footboard while sitting on the hospital bed.
The carpenter replaced the broken footboard on the vintage sleigh.
The old steam train had a metal footboard running along its side for passengers to step on.
