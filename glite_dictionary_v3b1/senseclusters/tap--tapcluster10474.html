tap

noun
light touch or gentle stroke
6.5 1.0
Pronunciation: /tæp/ (AmE), /tæp/ (BrE)
a light touch, blow, or sound made by gently hitting something

Examples

A gentle tap
A quick tap on the screen will wake up your phone.
I felt a tap on my shoulder and turned around.
She gave the door a gentle tap before entering.
He responded to her question with a gentle tap on the table.
I heard a tap at the window late last night.
The only sound in the room was the tap of her fingers on the desk.
A gentle tap of the hammer was all that was needed to fit the nail in.
He heard a soft tap at the window in the middle of the night.
With a quick tap on the shoulder, she got his attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knock - a short, sharp sound made by striking something
  • synonym: pat - a light touch with the hand or a flat object
  • antonym: push - an act of pressing against something to move it
  • synonym: rap - a quick, sharp blow or knock
