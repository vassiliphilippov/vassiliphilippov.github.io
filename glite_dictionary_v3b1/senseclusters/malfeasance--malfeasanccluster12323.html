malfeasance

noun
wrongful conduct by a public official
Pronunciation: / mælˈfiːzəns / (AmE), / mælˈfiːzəns / (BrE)
wrongful or illegal conduct, especially by a public official

Examples

Public malfeasance
An internal investigation uncovered malfeasance within the police department.
Laws are in place to prevent malfeasance by public officials.
The mayor was accused of malfeasance after the funds were found to be missing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corruption - dishonest or fraudulent conduct by those in power
  • synonym: misconduct - unacceptable or improper behavior, especially by an employee or professional
