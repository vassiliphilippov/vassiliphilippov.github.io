clocker

noun
employee time recorder
Pronunciation: / ˈklɑːkər / (AmE), / ˈklɒkə / (BrE)
a machine that records the times when employees start and finish work, also known as a time clock

Examples

A clocker machine
The company installed a new clocker to track when employees arrive and leave.
If the clocker is not working, staff must record their hours manually.
Every morning, workers line up to use the clocker before starting their shifts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: punch clock - a machine for recording the time employees arrive and leave
  • synonym: time clock - a device used to record employees' working hours
