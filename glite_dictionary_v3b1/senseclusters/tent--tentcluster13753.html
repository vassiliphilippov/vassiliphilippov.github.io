tent

noun
portable shelter for camping
3.8
Pronunciation: / tɛnt / (AmE), / tɛnt / (BrE)
a portable shelter made of cloth, supported by poles and ropes, and fastened to the ground, used especially for camping outdoors.

Examples

A camping tent
After the storm, their tent was blown over by the wind.
The children slept in a tent during their summer camping trip.
We set up our tent under the trees near the river.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cabin - a small, simple house or shelter, often in the woods
  • synonym: canopy - a covering fixed over a seat or shelter
