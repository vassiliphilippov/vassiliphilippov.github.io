perpetuate

verb
cause to continue; make lasting
5.1 5.3
Pronunciation: /pər.ˈpetʃ.ə.weɪt/ /; / /pər.ˈpetʃ.ʊ.eɪt/ (AmE), /pə.ˈpetʃ.ʊ.eɪt/ /; / /pə.ˈpetʃ.ə.weɪt/ (BrE)

Examples

Rumors can easily perpetuate harmful stereotypes in a community.
