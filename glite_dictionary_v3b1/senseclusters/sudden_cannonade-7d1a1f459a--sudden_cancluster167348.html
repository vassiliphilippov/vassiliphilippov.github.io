sudden cannonade

noun
a loud noise like cannon fire
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌd(ə)n ˌkænəˈneɪd / (AmE), / ˈsʌd(ə)n ˌkænəˈneɪd / (BrE)
A sudden and loud noise resembling the firing of cannons.

Examples

A sudden cannonade of thunder
A sudden cannonade of thunder shook the windows during the storm.
She was awakened by the sudden cannonade of fireworks outside her window.
The speech was interrupted by a sudden cannonade of applause from the audience.
