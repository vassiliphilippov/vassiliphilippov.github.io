whistle stop

noun
small railway station (stops on signal)
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪsəl stɑp / (AmE), / ˈwɪsl stɒp / (BrE)
A small railway station, especially one where trains stop only on request or for a signal, rather than as a main stop.

Examples

A whistle stop station
Many old movies show couples meeting at a lonely whistle stop in the middle of nowhere.
The train slowed down at every whistle stop along the rural route.
We waited at the tiny whistle stop for the evening passenger train to arrive.
