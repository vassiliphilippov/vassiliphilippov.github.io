stumble

verb
trip or nearly fall while moving
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌmbəl / (AmE), / ˈstʌmbəl / (BrE)
to trip, lose balance, or walk unsteadily, often nearly falling

The performer managed to stumble off stage without falling completely.
