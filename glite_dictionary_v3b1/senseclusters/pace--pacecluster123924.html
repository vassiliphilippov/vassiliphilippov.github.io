pace

verb
regulate speed or progress
4.0
Pronunciation: / peɪs / (AmE), / peɪs / (BrE)
to regulate or control the speed or progress of something

Examples

To pace yourself
It is important to pace yourself when running a marathon.
She tried to pace her speech to keep the audience engaged.
The coach asked the players to pace their energy throughout the game.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hasten - to make something happen sooner or increase speed
  • synonym: regulate - to control or maintain the rate or speed of a process
