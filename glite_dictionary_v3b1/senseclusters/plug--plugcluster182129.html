plug

noun
old, worn-out horse
Pronunciation: / plʌɡ / (AmE), / plʌɡ / (BrE)
an old or worn-out horse, especially one used for ordinary work; a horse past its prime

Examples

An old plug horse
After years of loyal service, the faithful plug deserved a peaceful retirement.
She bought a plug at auction, hoping it would be good enough for light work.
The farmer still rides his old plug around the fields each morning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: steed - a horse, especially a spirited one (contrasting in quality)
