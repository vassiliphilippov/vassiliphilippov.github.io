drilling

noun
the act or process of making holes
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdrɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of making holes in a material, especially using a drill (including for oil or gas extraction)

Examples

Oil drilling
Continuous drilling could be heard from the construction site next door.
Oil companies invest heavily in offshore drilling operations.
The drilling of the new well began early in the morning.
Deep drilling operations can reach oil reserves thousands of meters underground.
Environmental groups closely monitor drilling activities in sensitive areas.
The company invested millions in drilling for oil off the coast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boring - the act of making a hole in something using a tool
