stylistically

adverb
in terms of style or manner of expression
4.9 5.1
Pronunciation: /staɪ.ˈlɪs.tɪ.kliː/ /; / /staɪ.ˈlɪs.tɪ.kəl.iː/ (AmE), /staɪ.ˈlɪs.tɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /staɪ.ˈlɪs.tɪ.kliː/ (BrE)
In a way that relates to style or the distinctive manner in which something is expressed or created.

Examples

To stylistically differ
Her latest designs are stylistically different from her earlier work.
The film was stylistically influenced by the French New Wave.
The novel is stylistically unique, blending poetry with prose.
