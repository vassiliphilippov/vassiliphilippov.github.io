3D

adjective
three-dimensional form or appearance
Pronunciation: / ˌθriː ˈdiː / (AmE), / ˌθriː ˈdiː / (BrE)
having three dimensions; giving the illusion of depth, as in real life, movies, or graphics.

Examples

3D graphics
Many video games offer both 2D and 3D gameplay modes now.
She used advanced software to create a realistic 3D model of the building.
The movie was really impressive in 3D, with effects that jumped off the screen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: three-dimensional - having or appearing to have height, width, and depth
