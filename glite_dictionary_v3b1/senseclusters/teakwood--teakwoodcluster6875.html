teakwood

noun
hard, durable wood from the teak tree
5.3 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàtikÀåw äd / (AmE), / ÀàtiÀêkw äd / (BrE)
the hard, strong, durable yellowish-brown wood of the teak tree, valued for its resistance to insects and warping, and used for furniture and shipbuilding

Examples

Strong teakwood furniture
Because teakwood does not warp easily, it is ideal for use in outdoor furniture.
The boat was made of teakwood, chosen for its durability and resistance to water damage.
They ordered a custom table crafted from high-quality teakwood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: teak - a large tropical Asian tree whose wood is valuable
