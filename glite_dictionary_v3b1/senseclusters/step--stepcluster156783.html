step

musical interval of two semitones (whole tone)

In music, a melodic interval equivalent to two semitones, also known as a whole tone.

Examples

A musical step
In Western music, moving from C to D is a step because it covers two semitones.
The difference between a half step and a step is crucial when tuning an instrument.
The melody ascends in steps rather than leaps, giving it a smooth quality.
