Synonyms

  • settling
    4.3 1.14/m
    settling is a slow, natural sinking of ground or structures, while subsidence can be sudden or happen over longer periods.
  • sinkage
    7.4 0.02/m
    sinkage is the measured amount of downward movement in soil or ships, while subsidence names general land or structure sinking.
  • sinking
    4.4 0.21/m
    sinking is a broad decline or falling in many contexts, while subsidence specifically means land or structure sinking.
