overseasoned

adjective
excessively embellished
6.4
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ˈsiː.zənd/ (AmE), /əʊ.vər.ˈsiː.zənd/ (BrE)
excessively decorated, embellished, or filled with unnecessary elements, especially in language, writing, or performance

Examples

Overseasoned writing

Some readers found the novel overseasoned and preferred a simpler narrative style.
