evangelically

adverb
with strong zeal
Pronunciation: / ɪˌvæn.dʒəˈlɪk.li / (AmE), / ɪˌvæn.dʒɪˈlɪk.əl.i / (BrE)
in a way that shows strong enthusiasm or zeal, especially about beliefs or causes

Examples

Evangelically support change
She argued evangelically for environmental protection at the community meeting.
The teacher spoke evangelically about the value of lifelong learning.
He promoted the new project evangelically, inspiring many colleagues to join.

Related Terms

  • antonym: indifferently - in a way that shows little or no interest or enthusiasm
  • synonym: passionately - in a way that shows strong feelings or enthusiasm
  • synonym: zealously - in a way that shows great energy or enthusiasm
