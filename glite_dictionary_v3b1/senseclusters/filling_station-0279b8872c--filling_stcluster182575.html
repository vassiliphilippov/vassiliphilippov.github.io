Synonyms

  • petrol station
    1.0 0.21/m
    petrol station sells petrol and fuels to cars in daily life, while filling station sounds more formal or old-fashioned.
  • service station
    1.8 0.73/m

  • gas station
    1.0 6.93/m

  • gasoline station
