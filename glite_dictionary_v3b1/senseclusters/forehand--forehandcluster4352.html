forehand

adjective
stroke with palm facing forward
9.9 2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈfɔːr.hænd/ (AmE), /ˈfɔː.hænd/ (BrE)
(of a racket stroke) performed with the palm of the hand facing in the direction of the stroke.
