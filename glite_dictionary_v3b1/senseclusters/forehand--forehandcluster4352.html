forehand

adjective
stroke with palm facing forward
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌhænd / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌhænd / (BrE)
(of a racket stroke) performed with the palm of the hand facing in the direction of the stroke.

Examples

Forehand stroke
He hit a powerful forehand shot that landed just inside the baseline.
Her forehand return was faster and more accurate than her backhand.
The coach suggested he improve his forehand technique for better control.

Related Terms

  • antonym: backhand - (of a racket stroke) made with the back of the hand facing the direction of the stroke
