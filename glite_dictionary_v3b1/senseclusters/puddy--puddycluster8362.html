puddy

noun
child's word for cat
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌdi / (AmE), / ˈpʌdi / (BrE)
a nonstandard or childish word for a cat, often used by or to children

Examples

The puddy cat
The toddler giggled as the puddy chased a ball of yarn across the floor.
She named her new kitten puddy because she thought it sounded cute.
Whenever he saw a cat, he would point and say, 'Look, a puddy!'
