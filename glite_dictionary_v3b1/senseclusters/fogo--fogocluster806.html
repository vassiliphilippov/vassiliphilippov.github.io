fogo

noun
tropical skin disease
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈfəʊɡəʊ / (BrE)
a tropical disease found mainly in Brazil that causes skin ulcers and lesions

Examples

Fogo disease outbreak
The doctor diagnosed the patient with fogo after examining the skin lesions.
In some rural areas, outbreaks of fogo are still a serious health concern.
Treatment for fogo often requires both medication and proper wound care.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leprosy - a chronic infectious disease causing skin lesions
  • synonym: ulcer - an open sore on the skin or inside the body
