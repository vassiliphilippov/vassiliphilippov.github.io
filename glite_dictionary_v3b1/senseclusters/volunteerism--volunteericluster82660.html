volunteerism

noun
unpaid community service
Pronunciation: / ˌvɑːlənˈtɪrɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌvɒlənˈtɪərɪzəm / (BrE)
the practice or belief of giving your time and services freely, without pay, to help others or support a cause, often through organized activities

Related Terms

  • synonym: altruism - selfless concern for the well-being of others
  • antonym: compulsory service - work or service required by law or authority
  • antonym: employment - paid work or occupation
  • synonym: philanthropy - the desire to promote the welfare of others, often through donations
  • synonym: volunteering - the act of working without pay to help others
