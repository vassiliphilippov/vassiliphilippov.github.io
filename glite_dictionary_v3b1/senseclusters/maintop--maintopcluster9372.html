maintop

noun
platform on mainmast
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪnˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈmeɪnˌtɒp / (BrE)
a platform near the top of a ship's mainmast, used as a lookout or working area

Examples

The maintop platform
The sailor climbed up to the maintop to keep watch over the horizon.
During the storm, two crew members secured the rigging from the maintop.
From the maintop, you could see approaching ships long before they reached the harbor.
