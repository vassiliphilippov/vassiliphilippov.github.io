aerobic

adjective
requiring oxygen; related to exercise or respiration
4.4
Pronunciation: / ɛˈroʊbɪk / (AmE), / eəˈrəʊbɪk / (BrE)
relating to or requiring oxygen, especially referring to exercise that improves the efficiency of the cardiovascular and respiratory systems.

Examples

Aerobic exercise
She attends an aerobic class at the gym every morning.
Swimming is considered an excellent aerobic activity for all ages.
The doctor recommended more aerobic exercise to improve heart health.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anaerobic - not requiring oxygen; referring to exercise or processes occurring without air
