fuse

verb
combine or blend together
3.9 3.48/m
Pronunciation: / fjuz / (AmE), / fjuÀêz / (BrE)
to combine or blend two or more things together to form a single entity

Examples

To fuse ideas
During the project, individual tasks fused into a cohesive effort.
The artist decided to fuse jazz and classical music in her new composition.
The committee will fuse their ideas into a single proposal.
