querent

noun
person seeking guidance
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪrənt / (AmE), / ˈkwɪərənt / (BrE)
a person who asks a question, especially one seeking guidance from an astrologer or fortune-teller

Examples

Querent asks a fortune-teller
The querent listened carefully as the astrologer explained the meaning of the stars.
During the tarot reading, the querent asked about their future career prospects.
Each querent brings their own hopes and questions to the fortune-teller's table.
