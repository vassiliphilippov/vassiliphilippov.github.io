Hapsburg

proper noun
historical European royal family
Pronunciation: / ˈhæps.bɜrɡ / (AmE), / ˈhæps.bɜːɡ / (BrE)
a royal German family that ruled several European states and the Holy Roman Empire from 1440 to 1806

Examples

Hapsburg royal family

The Hapsburg dynasty played a central role in shaping European history for centuries.
The decline of the Hapsburg empire marked the end of a significant era in European politics.
