system

noun
organized set of related parts
1.0 2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
a set of connected parts or devices that work together as a whole, especially in machines, computers, or factories

Examples

A computer system
A computer system typically includes both hardware and software components.
The healthcare system is designed to provide medical services to everyone in the country.
Their new transportation system greatly improved efficiency across the city.
Each system in the factory is tested for efficiency before full operation.
He installed a surround sound system in his living room for a better movie experience.
The security system in our office includes cameras, alarms, and sensors.
