upbuilding

noun
moral improvement
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of strengthening or improving something, especially in a moral or spiritual sense

Examples

Upbuilding of character
The teacher's encouragement played a key role in the upbuilding of her students' confidence.
Community service projects contribute to the upbuilding of society as a whole.
His words were meant for the upbuilding of everyone present at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: corruption - the process of becoming morally worse
  • synonym: edification - the instruction or improvement of a person morally or intellectually
  • synonym: uplift - the act of raising someone morally or spiritually
