guitar pick

noun
tool for plucking guitar strings
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈtɑr pɪk / (AmE), / ɡɪˈtɑː pɪk / (BrE)
a small, flat tool used to pluck or strum the strings of a guitar

Examples

A guitar pick tool
He strummed the guitar with a bright red guitar pick.
Many beginners start with a soft guitar pick to make strumming easier.
She prefers using her fingers instead of a guitar pick when playing acoustic songs.
