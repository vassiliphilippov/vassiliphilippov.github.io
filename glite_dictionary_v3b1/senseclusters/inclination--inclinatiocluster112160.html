inclination

noun
the act of bending forward
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnklɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪnklɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of inclining or bending forward, especially as a gesture

Examples

Inclination of the head
He gave a polite inclination of his head before leaving the room.
Her inclination was barely perceptible, but it showed her respect.
With a slight inclination of his body, he greeted the guests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bow - the act of bending the head or upper body as a gesture
