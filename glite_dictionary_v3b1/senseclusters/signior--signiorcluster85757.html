signior

noun
Italian courtesy title for a man
Pronunciation: / siˈnjɔr / (AmE), / siˈnjɔː / (BrE)
(dated) an Italian courtesy title for a man, equivalent to 'mister'; used before a man's name or as a form of address.

Examples

Signior Lorenzo
Signior Lorenzo, will you join us for dinner this evening?
Many greeted him respectfully as Signior Bellini walked through the market.
The play depicted a noble signior wooing a Venetian lady.
