discernible

adjective
able to be noticed or recognized
2.8 4.4
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɜrnəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈsɜːnəbəl /; / dɪˈsɜːnəbl / (BrE)
able to be seen, noticed, or recognized; perceptible
