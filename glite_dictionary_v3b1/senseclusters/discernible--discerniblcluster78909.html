Examples

Synonyms

  • perceivable
    4.9 0.17/m
    perceivable is able to be noticed or felt by the senses, while discernible suggests small or not obvious differences that you must detect.
  • observable
    4.3 4.44/m
    observable can be clearly seen or watched, while discernible often refers to faint signs or small differences you need to look for.
  • distinguishable
    4.6 1.31/m
    distinguishable is able to be told apart from others, while discernible focuses on noticing a thing's presence or subtle qualities.
  • noticeable
    3.2 7.42/m
    noticeable is easy to see and often draws attention quickly, while discernible may mean a faint or hard-to-see sign.
  • detectable
    3.6 2.03/m

  • recognizable
    2.3 6.57/m
    recognizable is known or identified from memory or previous experience, while discernible means it can be noticed or perceived now.
