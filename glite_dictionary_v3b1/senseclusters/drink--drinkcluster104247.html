drink

noun
single serving of a beverage
Pronunciation: / drɪŋk / (AmE), / drɪŋk / (BrE)
a beverage or a serving of a beverage (alcoholic or not)

Examples

A cold drink
He poured himself a stiff drink after a long day.
She ordered a cold drink to cool off after her walk.
Would you like a drink with your meal?
He finished his drink in one long swallow.
May I offer you a drink of water before we begin?
She paused to take a drink before continuing her speech.
Could I have a cold drink with my meal, please?
The waiter offered us a selection of drinks before dinner.
Water is the healthiest drink you can choose.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beverage - a drink, especially one other than water
  • degree higher: gulp - a large and hurried swallow of liquid
  • degree lower: sip - a small mouthful of liquid
