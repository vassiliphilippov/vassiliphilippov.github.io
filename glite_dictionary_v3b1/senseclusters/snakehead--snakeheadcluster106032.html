snakehead

noun
organizer of illegal human smuggling
Pronunciation: / c8sne6akcch5bd / (AmE), / c8sne6akh5bd / (BrE)
(informal) a person who arranges or organizes the illegal entry or transport of people into a country, typically for profit.

Examples

A snakehead smuggler
Police arrested a snakehead who had been organizing secret crossings across the border.
Families paid a snakehead thousands of dollars to arrange the dangerous sea voyage to safety.
Investigators uncovered evidence showing that the snakehead coordinated travel, forged documents, and hidden routes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: people smuggler - a person or group who illegally transports people across borders, usually for payment
