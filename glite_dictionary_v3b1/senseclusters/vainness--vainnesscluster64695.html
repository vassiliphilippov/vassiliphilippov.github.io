vainness

noun
futility or ineffectiveness
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪnnəs / (AmE), / ˈveɪnnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being futile, pointless, or without real value; ineffectiveness or lack of result

Examples

The vainness of hope
He realized the vainness of his efforts after nothing changed.
The vainness of their search became clear as the day ended without results.
She spoke about the vainness of trying to please everyone all the time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: futility - the quality of being pointless or useless
  • antonym: usefulness - the quality of being useful or effective
