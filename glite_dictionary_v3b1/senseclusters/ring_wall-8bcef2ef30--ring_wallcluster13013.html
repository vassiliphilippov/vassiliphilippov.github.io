ring wall

noun
circular support wall
6.9
Pronunciation: /rɪŋ wɔːl/ (AmE), /rɪŋ wɔːl/ (BrE)
a circular foundation or load-bearing wall built to support a heavy structure such as a silo, tank, or tower

Examples

Ring wall foundation
The engineers designed a ring wall to support the base of the new storage tank.
A concrete ring wall was constructed to provide stability for the water tower.
Before building the silo, workers poured a strong ring wall as its foundation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foundation - the lowest load-bearing part of a building, typically below ground level
  • synonym: retaining wall - a wall built to hold back earth or water
