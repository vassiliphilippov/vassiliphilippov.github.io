OMB

noun
US agency overseeing federal budget
5.8
Pronunciation: / oʊ ɛm ˈbi / (AmE), / əʊ ɛm ˈbiː / (BrE)
the Office of Management and Budget, the executive agency that advises the President of the United States on the federal budget and oversees its administration

Examples

The OMB office
According to the OMB, the deficit is expected to increase next year.
Budget proposals must first be reviewed by the OMB before being sent to Congress.
The OMB released a new report on federal spending for the coming year.
