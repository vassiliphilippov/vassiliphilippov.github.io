planetary

adjective
of or relating to planets
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈplæn.ə.ˌtɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈplæn.ɪ.tər.i / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a planet or the planets, especially in terms of their physical nature or motion

Examples

Planetary motion
Jupiter's massive storms are unique among planetary weather systems in our solar system.
Scientists study planetary motion to understand how planets orbit the sun.
The mission's goal is to analyze the planetary atmosphere for signs of life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: planetary motion - the movement of planets in space
  • synonym: planetary system - a system of planets orbiting a star
