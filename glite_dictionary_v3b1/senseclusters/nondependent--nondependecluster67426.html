nondependent

adjective
not addicted or reliant
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.dɪˈpɛndənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.dɪˈpɛndənt / (BrE)
not relying on or addicted to something, especially a substance or person

Examples

Nondependent user
The clinic offers programs for both nondependent and dependent users of the medication.
He remained nondependent on painkillers after his surgery, which pleased his doctor.
Researchers compared the habits of nondependent drinkers with those who showed signs of addiction.
