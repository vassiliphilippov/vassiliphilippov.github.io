jaundice

verb
to affect with jaundice or prejudice
9.9 8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒɔːn.dɪs/ /; / /ˈdʒɑːn.dɪs/ (AmE), /ˈdʒɔːn.dɪs/ (BrE)
