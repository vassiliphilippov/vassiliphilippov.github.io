moon-ray

noun
a ray of light from the moon
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmunˌreɪ / (AmE), / ˈmuːnˌreɪ / (BrE)
A narrow beam or ray of light coming from the moon.

Examples

A moon-ray of light
A single moon-ray penetrated the clouds, casting a silvery glow on the lake.
She watched the moon-ray move slowly across her bedroom floor as the night passed.
The garden was bathed in moon-rays, making the flowers shimmer softly.
