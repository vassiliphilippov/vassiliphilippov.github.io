high

adjective
slightly intoxicated or euphoric
3.3
Pronunciation: / haɪ / (AmE), / haɪ / (BrE)
slightly and pleasantly intoxicated from alcohol or a drug (especially marijuana)

Examples

Look high after drugs
He looked high after leaving the party.
She admitted to feeling high for the very first time.
Some people become talkative and giggly when they're high.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drunk - affected by alcohol to the point of losing control of one's faculties
  • antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol or drugs; not intoxicated
