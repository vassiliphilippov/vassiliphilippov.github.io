nut

noun
slang term for testicle
7.3
Pronunciation: / nʌt / (AmE), / nʌt / (BrE)
(slang) a testicle; one of the two male reproductive glands that produce sperm and secrete androgens.

Examples

A kick to the nuts
A sudden kick to the nuts can be extremely painful.
He doubled over in pain after being hit in the nut.
They wore protective gear to avoid injuries to their nuts during the game.
