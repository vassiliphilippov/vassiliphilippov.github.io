processor

noun
business that processes agricultural products
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑː.ses.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ.ses.ə /; / ˈprɒs.ɛs.ə / (BrE)
a person or business that processes things, such as food, photographs, or applications

Examples

The photograph processor developed my film in less than an hour.
