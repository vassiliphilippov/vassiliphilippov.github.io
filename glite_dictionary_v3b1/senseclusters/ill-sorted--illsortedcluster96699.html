ill-sorted

adjective
not well matched; jumbled
4.9
Pronunciation: / ɪlˈsɔrtɪd / (AmE), / ɪlˈsɔːtɪd / (BrE)
(of things) not well matched or not easy to combine harmoniously; mismatched or jumbled.

Examples

An ill-sorted collection
Her ill-sorted collection of furniture made the living room look chaotic.
The novel tells the story of an ill-sorted marriage marked by frequent disagreements.
The team was an ill-sorted group of individuals with little in common.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mismatched - not well matched; unsuitably paired
  • antonym: well-matched - things that go together harmoniously or fit well
