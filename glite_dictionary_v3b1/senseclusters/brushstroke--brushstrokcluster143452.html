brushstroke

noun
mark made by brush
Pronunciation: / ˈbrʌʃˌstroʊk / (AmE), / ˈbrʌʃ.strəʊk / (BrE)
a mark, line, or movement made by a paintbrush in painting, often showing an artist's style
