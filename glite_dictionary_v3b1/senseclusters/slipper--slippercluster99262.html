slipper

noun
indoor slip-on shoe
3.6 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈslɪp.ər/ (AmE), /ˈslɪp.ə/ (BrE)
A light, low shoe that is easy to put on and take off, usually worn indoors for comfort.

Examples

She bought a new pair of fluffy slippers to wear around the house.
