recruitment

noun
the act of enlisting people
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkrut.mənt / (AmE), / rɪˈkruːt.mənt / (BrE)
the process of finding and enlisting people to join an organization, the armed forces, or take part in an activity.

Examples

Effective recruitment strategies
Effective recruitment strategies are essential for building a strong workforce.
The recruitment of skilled engineers is becoming increasingly competitive in the tech industry.
The army launched a major recruitment drive to encourage young people to join.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enlistment - the act of enrolling or being enrolled, especially in the armed forces
  • synonym: hiring - the act of taking someone on as an employee
