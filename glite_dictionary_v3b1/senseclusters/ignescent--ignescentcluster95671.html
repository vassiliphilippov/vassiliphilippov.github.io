ignescent

adjective
capable of emitting sparks or catching fire
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈnɛsənt / (AmE), / ɪɡˈnɛsənt / (BrE)
capable of emitting sparks or bursting into flame, especially when struck or rubbed

Examples

Ignescent minerals
Some minerals are known for their ignescent qualities in geological demonstrations.
The scientist warned that the powder was highly ignescent and should be handled with care.
When struck with steel, the ignescent stone produced a shower of sparks.
