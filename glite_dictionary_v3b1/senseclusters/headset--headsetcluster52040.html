headset

noun
head-mounted display for VR/AR
3.7 0.31/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆhÉ›dËŒsÉ›t / (AmE), / ËˆhÉ›dËŒsÉ›t / (BrE)
A head-mounted display device that presents immersive virtual-reality or augmented-reality images to the wearer, often including integrated audio, cameras, and motion-tracking sensors.

Examples

VR headset
He strapped on the headset to enter the immersive VR game.
The museum loaned us a lightweight headset that overlays historical information onto exhibits.
Developers tested several headsets to compare field of view and motion-tracking accuracy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: head-mounted display - A display device worn on the head that places images close to the eyes; often abbreviated HMD.
  • synonym: headphones - Audio devices worn on or around the head; related but primarily for listening rather than displaying images.
