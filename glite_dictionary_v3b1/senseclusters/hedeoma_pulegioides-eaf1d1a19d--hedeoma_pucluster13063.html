Hedeoma pulegioides

noun
a North American herb used for its oil as insect repellent
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛdiˈoʊmə pjuːˌliːdʒiˈoʊdiːz / (AmE), / ˌhiːdiˈəʊmə pjuːˌliːdʒiˈəʊdiːz / (BrE)
