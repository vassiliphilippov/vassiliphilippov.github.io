sinister

adjective
heraldic term: on the wearer's left
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪnəstər / (AmE), / ˈsɪnɪstə / (BrE)
(Heraldry) on or pertaining to the left-hand side of a shield from the wearer's perspective; the side opposite 'dexter'.

Examples

Sinister side
A bend sinister runs from the top right to the bottom left from the observer's view.
In heraldry, a bar sinister indicates illegitimacy.
The lion rampant faces the sinister side of the shield in the family crest.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dexter - heraldic right side (wearer's perspective)
