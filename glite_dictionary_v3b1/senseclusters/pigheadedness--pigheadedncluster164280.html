pigheadedness

noun
stubborn refusal to change
5.6 6.3
Pronunciation: / pɪɡˈhɛdɪdnəs / (AmE), / pɪɡˈhɛdɪdnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being stubborn or refusing to change one's mind, even when it is unreasonable.

Examples

Pigheadedness and stubbornness
Despite all the evidence, his pigheadedness kept him from admitting he was wrong.
Her pigheadedness prevented the team from trying a new approach to the problem.
We lost precious time because of the manager's pigheadedness during the meeting.
