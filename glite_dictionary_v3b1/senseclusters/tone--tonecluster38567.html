tone

verb
vary the pitch of one's speech
1.5 4.3
Pronunciation: /toʊn/ (AmE), /təʊn/ (BrE)
to vary the pitch or quality of one's voice or speech

Examples

To tone your voice
He tones his speech carefully during public presentations to keep the audience engaged.
It's important to tone your words to avoid sounding harsh during a difficult conversation.
She toned her voice to sound friendlier when speaking to the children.

Related Terms
