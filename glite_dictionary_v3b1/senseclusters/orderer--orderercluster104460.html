orderer

noun
person who places an order
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdərər / (AmE), / ˈɔːdərə / (BrE)
a person or organization that places an order to buy goods or services

Examples

Product orderer
All correspondence will be sent directly to the orderer of the product.
As the orderers changed their specifications, the supplier adjusted the shipment accordingly.
The orderer must provide valid payment details before the request is processed.
