subsistent

adjective
existing independently
Pronunciation: / səbˈsɪstənt / (AmE), / səbˈsɪstənt / (BrE)
existing independently or having its own existence, especially in a philosophical context

Examples

Subsistent being
In metaphysics, a subsistent entity is thought to exist on its own.
The theory claims that numbers are subsistent objects, not just mental constructs.
Some philosophers debate whether the soul is truly subsistent after death.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dependent - relying on something else for existence or support
  • synonym: independent - not relying on others; self-sufficient
