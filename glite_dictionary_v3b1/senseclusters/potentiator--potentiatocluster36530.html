potentiator

noun
something that enhances
8.5
Pronunciation: / pəˈtɛnʃiˌeɪtər / (AmE), / pəˈtɛnʃieɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that increases the power, effectiveness, or likelihood of something

Examples

Potentiator of success
The new training program acted as a potentiator for employee productivity.
Vitamin C can serve as a potentiator for certain medications, making them more effective.
Her encouragement was a powerful potentiator of his confidence before the exam.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enhancer - something that increases the quality or effect of something
  • antonym: inhibitor - something that reduces or prevents the effect of something
