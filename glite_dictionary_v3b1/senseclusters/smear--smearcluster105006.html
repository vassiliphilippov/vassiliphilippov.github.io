smear

noun
thin tissue/fluid sample for microscopic examination
1.0
Pronunciation: / smɪr / (AmE), / smɪə / (BrE)
a sample of tissue or fluid that has been spread thinly on a microscope slide for examination, especially for diagnosis

Examples

A medical smear
A Pap smear is an important test for detecting cervical cancer in women.
After preparing the tissue smear, the technician applied special stains for analysis.
The doctor examined the blood smear under the microscope for signs of infection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slide - a thin flat piece of glass used for holding objects for examination under a microscope
