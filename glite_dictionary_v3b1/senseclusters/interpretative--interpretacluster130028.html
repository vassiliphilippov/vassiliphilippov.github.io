interpretative

adjective
providing interpretation or explanation
5.7
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɝːprətɪtɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɜːprətətɪv / (BrE)
providing or relating to an explanation or interpretation of something, especially a work of art, literature, or data.

Examples

An interpretative approach
Her interpretative approach helps students understand challenging concepts in art history.
The museum provided an interpretative guide to enhance visitors' appreciation of the exhibition.
The speaker offered an interpretative analysis of the poem's symbolism.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interpretive - providing interpretation; variant form commonly used in American English
