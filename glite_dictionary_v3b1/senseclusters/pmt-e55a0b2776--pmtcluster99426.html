PMT

noun
premenstrual symptoms
6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈpiː.ɛm.tiː/ (AmE), /ˈpiː.ɛm.tiː/ (BrE)
abbreviation for premenstrual tension, a condition involving physical and emotional symptoms before menstruation

Examples

PMT symptoms
Many women report experiencing PMT symptoms such as irritability and bloating before their period.
Her PMT made it difficult for her to concentrate at work during that week.
Doctors often discuss ways to manage PMT with their patients who have severe symptoms.
