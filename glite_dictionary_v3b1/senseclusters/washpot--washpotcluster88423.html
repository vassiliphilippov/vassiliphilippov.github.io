washpot

noun
pot used for washing
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑʃˌpɑt / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃˌpɒt / (BrE)
a pot or vessel used for washing things, especially laundry or household items

Examples

A washpot for laundry
She filled the washpot with soapy water and began scrubbing the clothes by hand.
The old farmhouse still had an iron washpot behind the shed.
They heated water in a large washpot over the fire for cleaning and bathing.
