noncompetitiveness

noun
lack of competitiveness
7.9
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.kəm.ˈpet.ɪ.tɪv.nəs/ (AmE), /nɒn.kəm.ˈpet.ɪ.tɪv.nəs/ (BrE)

Examples

Team noncompetitiveness
The team's noncompetitiveness was evident in their repeated losses throughout the season.
Many experts blame the company's noncompetitiveness for its failure to attract new customers.
Government policies can sometimes contribute to the noncompetitiveness of certain industries.
