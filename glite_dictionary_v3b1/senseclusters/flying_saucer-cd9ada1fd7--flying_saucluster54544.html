flying saucer

noun
unidentified disc-shaped flying object
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪɪŋ ˌsɔsɚ / (AmE), / ˈflaɪɪŋ ˌsɔːsə / (BrE)
a disc-shaped object reported as appearing in the sky, typically regarded as a spacecraft from another planet

Examples

A flying saucer sighting
Many people claimed to have seen a flying saucer hovering over the city last night.
Reports of flying saucers were most common during the 1950s.
The movie featured aliens arriving on Earth in a giant flying saucer.
