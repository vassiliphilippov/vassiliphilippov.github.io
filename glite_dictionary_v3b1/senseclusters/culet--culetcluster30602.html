culet

noun
armor for lower back
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːlɪt / (AmE), / ˈkjuːlɪt / (BrE)
a small plate of armor that protects the lower back or buttocks, forming part of a suit of plate armor

Examples

Armor culet
The knight adjusted the polished culet at the base of his backplate before mounting his horse.
A well-crafted culet was essential for protecting a warrior during battle in medieval times.
Museum visitors admired the ornate culet displayed alongside other pieces of historical armor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: backplate - a piece of armor covering the back
  • synonym: tasset - a piece of armor for the upper thighs
