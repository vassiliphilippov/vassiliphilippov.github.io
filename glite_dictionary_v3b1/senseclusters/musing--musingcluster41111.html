musing

noun
deep or thoughtful reflection
Pronunciation: / ˈmju.zɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmjuː.zɪŋ / (BrE)
a period of deep or thoughtful reflection; a calm, prolonged consideration or contemplation

Examples

A quiet musing
His musing on the meaning of life led him to write poetry.
She sat by the window, lost in her musing about the future.
The professor's musing was interrupted by a knock at the door.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: daydream - a series of pleasant thoughts that distract from the present
  • degree higher: meditation - the action or practice of meditating; deep focus or contemplation
  • synonym: reflection - serious thought or consideration
