brood

verb
sit on eggs to incubate them
4.0
Pronunciation: / brud / (AmE), / bruːd / (BrE)
(of a bird) to sit on eggs to keep them warm until they hatch.

Examples

To brood eggs
If the bird does not brood the eggs, they will not hatch.
Some species of penguins brood their eggs for weeks in harsh conditions.
The hen began to brood as soon as she laid her eggs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incubate - to sit on eggs and keep them warm so they will hatch
