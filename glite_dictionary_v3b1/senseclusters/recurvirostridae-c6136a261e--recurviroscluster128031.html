Recurvirostridae

noun
family of wading birds (stilts and avocets)
Pronunciation: / riˌkɜːrvəˈrɑstrɪˌdeɪ /; / rɪˌkɜrvəˈrɒstrɪdiː / (AmE), / rɪˌkɜːvɪˈrɒstrɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of long-legged wading birds, including stilts and avocets, with slender, often upward-curving bills, found in marshes and shorelines
