legal proceeding

noun
series of actions in a court case
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈliːɡəl prəˈsiːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈliːɡəl prəˈsiːdɪŋ / (BrE)
A formal series of actions taken in a court of law to settle a dispute or determine a legal matter.

Examples

A legal proceeding in court
After many delays, the legal proceeding finally concluded with the judge's verdict.
Entering a legal proceeding can be a lengthy and costly process for both parties involved.
The company initiated a legal proceeding against its former employee for breach of contract.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lawsuit - a claim or dispute brought to a court of law for adjudication
  • synonym: trial - a formal judicial examination of evidence and determination of legal claims
