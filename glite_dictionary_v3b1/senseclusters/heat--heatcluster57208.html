heat

noun
period of sexual receptivity in female mammals
4.1 6.3
Pronunciation: /hiːt/ (AmE), /hiːt/ (BrE)
a period of sexual receptivity and fertility in some female mammals, during which they are capable of mating

Examples

A cow in heat
Farmers monitor their cows closely to detect when they are in heat for breeding purposes.
The cat goes into heat several times a year, attracting male cats from the neighborhood.
When a female dog is in heat, she may display restless behavior and attract male dogs.
