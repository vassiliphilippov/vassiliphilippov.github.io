women's work

noun
tasks traditionally done by women
1.5 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪm.ɪnz wɜːrk/ /; / /ˈwɪm.ənz wɜːrk/ (AmE), /ˈwɪm.ɪnz wɜːk/ /; / /ˈwɪm.ənz wɜːk/ (BrE)
