blackcoat

noun
person in black coat
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈblækˌkoʊt / (AmE), / ˈblækˌkəʊt / (BrE)
a person who is identified by wearing a black coat, often used to describe someone by their clothing

Examples

A blackcoat at the door
A blackcoat stood by the door, his glossy coat buttoned against the chill.
The detective was easy to spot as the only blackcoat in the crowd.
She watched the blackcoat walk briskly down the street, disappearing into the fog.
