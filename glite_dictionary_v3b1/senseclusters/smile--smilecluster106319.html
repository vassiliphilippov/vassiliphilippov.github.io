smile

noun
expression with upturned corners of the mouth
1.6
Pronunciation: / smaɪl / (AmE), / smaɪl / (BrE)
A facial expression in which the corners of the mouth are turned up, typically indicating pleasure, friendliness, or amusement.

Examples

A big smile
A smile spread across his face when he heard the good news.
Her smile was so infectious that everyone in the group started laughing.
She gave me a warm smile as I entered the room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: frown - a facial expression showing displeasure or concern
  • synonym: grin - a broad smile often showing the teeth
