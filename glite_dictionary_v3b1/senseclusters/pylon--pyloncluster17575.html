pylon

noun
tower guiding pilots or marking a turning point
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪ.lɑn /; / ˈpaɪlɑn / (AmE), / ˈpaɪ.lɒn /; / ˈpaɪlɒn / (BrE)
a tall structure used for marking, guiding, or supporting things, such as in aviation, races, roads, or sports fields

Examples

Airport pylon
A red pylon stood at the end of the runway to guide incoming planes.
During the air show, pilots looped their planes around each marked pylon with precision.
The race cars swerved tightly around the pylon marking the first turn.
The power lines were held up by a massive pylon at the edge of the field.
During the football game, the ball landed just inside the orange pylon marking the end zone.
Construction workers installed a new pylon to support the overhead traffic signs on the highway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: marker - an object used to indicate a place, position, or route
  • synonym: pole - a long, slender, vertical support
  • synonym: tower - a tall structure, sometimes used for guidance or observation
