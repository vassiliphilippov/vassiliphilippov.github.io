working day

noun
hours required to work in a day
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːkɪŋ deɪ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkɪŋ deɪ / (BrE)
the number of hours an employee is required to work in a single day for a standard wage

Examples

A working day of 8 hours
His working day usually starts at 9 AM and ends at 5 PM.
Many employees prefer a flexible working day to accommodate their personal schedules.
The new contract reduced the working day from ten hours to eight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: workday - a day on which work is done, especially the usual hours of work
