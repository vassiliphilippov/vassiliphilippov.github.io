unbanked

adjective
without banking services
7.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbæŋkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈbæŋkt / (BrE)
not having access to or not using formal banking services such as bank accounts or credit cards

Examples

Unbanked people worldwide
Millions of people around the world are still unbanked and cannot easily save or transfer money.
The government launched a program to help unbanked citizens open their first bank accounts.
Mobile payment systems are becoming popular among unbanked communities in remote regions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: banked - having access to and using formal banking services
