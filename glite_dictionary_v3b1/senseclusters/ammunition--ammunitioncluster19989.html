ammunition

noun
bullets and projectiles for firing from guns
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌæmjəˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌamjʊˈnɪʃən /; / ˌæmjʊˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
objects or materials (such as bullets, shells, missiles, or even nuclear, chemical, or biological substances) that are used as weapons

Examples

Live ammunition
He collected spent ammunition casings from the firing range.
Hunting rifles require a different type of ammunition than shotguns.
The soldiers ran low on ammunition during the battle.
Inspectors searched the facility for any signs of biological ammunition.
International treaties aim to prevent the spread of ammunition such as chemical and nuclear weapons.
The country was suspected of stockpiling ammunition capable of causing mass destruction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: munition - military weapons, equipment, and supplies, particularly ammunition
  • synonym: weapon - an object or device used to inflict harm or damage
