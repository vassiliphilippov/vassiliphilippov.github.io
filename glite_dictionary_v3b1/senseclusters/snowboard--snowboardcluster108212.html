snowboard

noun
board used for sliding down snow slopes
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a flat board resembling a large ski, ridden in a standing position to slide down snow-covered slopes, especially as a sport

Examples

A snowboard rental
After renting a snowboard, he spent the afternoon practicing on the beginner slope.
He designed his own custom snowboard with a unique graphic.
She strapped her boots onto the snowboard before heading down the mountain.
