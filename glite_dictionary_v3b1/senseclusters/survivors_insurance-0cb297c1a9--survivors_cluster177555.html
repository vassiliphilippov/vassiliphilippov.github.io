survivors insurance

noun
insurance for surviving dependents after death
Pronunciation: / sərˈvaɪvərz ɪnˌʃʊrəns / (AmE), / səˈvaɪvəz ɪnˌʃʊərəns / (BrE)
Insurance that provides financial support to the surviving spouse or dependents of a deceased person.

Examples

Survivors insurance benefits
His family received survivors insurance after his sudden passing.
Many countries offer survivors insurance as part of their social security system.
Without survivors insurance, widows and dependents might face financial hardship.
