sideload

verb
to install from unofficial source
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈsaɪd.loʊd/ (AmE), /ˈsaɪd.ləʊd/ (BrE)
to transfer or install data or software onto a device from a source other than the official app store

Examples

To sideload an app
Many users choose to sideload apps that are not available in the official store.
You should be careful when you sideload software from unknown sources.
He learned how to sideload music files onto his tablet using his computer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: download - to transfer data from the internet to a device
  • synonym: install - to put software onto a device so it can be used
  • antonym: uninstall - to remove software from a device
