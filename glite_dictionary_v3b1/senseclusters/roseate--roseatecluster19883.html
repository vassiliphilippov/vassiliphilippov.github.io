roseate

adjective
dusty purplish pink color
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊziˌeɪt / (AmE), / ˈrəʊziˌeɪt / (BrE)
having a dusty purplish pink color; resembling the color of a rose.

Examples

Roseate silk scarf
She wore a scarf of roseate silk that complemented her complexion.
The artist chose a roseate palette to give the portrait a soft, warm atmosphere.
The sky was bathed in a roseate hue as the sun set.
