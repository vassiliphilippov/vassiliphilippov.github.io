terminalis

adjective
at the end
Pronunciation: / tərˈmɪnəlɪs / (AmE), / təˈmɪnəlɪs / (BrE)
located at or relating to the end or tip of a structure, especially in scientific naming.

Examples

Terminalis flower position
The species name terminalis indicates that the plant's flowers grow at the tips of its branches.
In scientific descriptions, leaves described as terminalis are found at the very end of a stem.
Researchers noted that the fruit was terminalis, appearing only at the branch extremities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: central - located in the center
  • synonym: terminal - situated at the end or extremity
