sale

noun
event with reduced prices
Pronunciation: / seɪl / (AmE), / seɪl / (BrE)
an event during which goods are sold at reduced prices for a limited period

Examples

Big sale event
She waits for the annual sale to buy designer clothes at lower prices.
The department store is having a big sale this weekend with discounts on all items.
We bought a new television during the Black Friday sale.

Related Terms

  • antonym: auction - a public sale where goods are sold to the highest bidder
  • synonym: clearance - an event to sell remaining stock at reduced prices
