checkout counter

noun
counter where you pay for purchases
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛk.aʊt ˌkaʊn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛk.aʊt ˌkaʊn.tə / (BrE)
a counter in a supermarket or other store where you pay for your purchases

Examples

Self-service checkout counter
Most supermarkets now have a self-service checkout counter for customers in a hurry.
She realized she had forgotten her wallet while standing at the checkout counter.
The line at the checkout counter was especially long on Saturday afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cash register - a machine for recording sales and handling cash at a checkout counter
