mooning

verb
to act dreamily
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈmuː.nɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈmuː.nɪŋ/ (BrE)
to behave in a dreamy or distracted way, often because of longing or romantic feelings for someone

Examples

To mooning over someone
He spent the morning mooning over his crush instead of studying for the exam.
Instead of joining the conversation, she was mooning out the window, lost in thought.
You cannot keep mooning around the house all day if you want to get things done.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brooding - to think deeply about something that makes one unhappy
  • synonym: daydreaming - to be lost in pleasant thoughts and not paying attention
  • antonym: focusing - to concentrate attention or effort
