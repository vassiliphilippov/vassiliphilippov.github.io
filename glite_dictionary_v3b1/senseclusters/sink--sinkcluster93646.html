Synonyms

  • go under
    4.4 0.65/m
    go under is used for falling below the water and often becoming fully covered, while sink means to become submerged.
  • submerge
    3.9 4.19/m

  • go down
    3.8 2.49/m
    go down is a casual phrase for falling or sinking in water, while sink is the normal verb for becoming submerged.
