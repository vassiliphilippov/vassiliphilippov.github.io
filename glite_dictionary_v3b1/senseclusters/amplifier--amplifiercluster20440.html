amplifier

noun
device that increases signal strength
Pronunciation: / ˈæmpləˌfaɪər / (AmE), / ˈæmplɪfaɪə / (BrE)
a device that increases the strength of electrical signals, especially for sound or radio transmission

Examples

A amplifier device
Many home theater systems come with a built-in amplifier for enhanced audio performance.
She adjusted the amplifier to get the best sound quality for the concert.
The guitar sounded much louder when plugged into the amplifier.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amplify - to make something larger, louder, or stronger, especially an electrical signal
  • antonym: attenuator - a device that reduces the strength of electrical signals
