antiauthoritarianism

noun
opposition to authority
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiəˌθɔːrɪˈtɛriəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌæntiɔːˌθɒrɪˈteəriəˌnɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief in or practice of opposing or rejecting authority, control, or strict rules
