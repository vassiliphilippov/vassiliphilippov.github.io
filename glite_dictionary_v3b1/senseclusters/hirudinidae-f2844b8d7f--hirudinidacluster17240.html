Hirudinidae

noun
family of medicinal leeches
Pronunciation: / hɪˌruːdəˈnaɪdiː / (AmE), / hɪˌruːdɪˈniːdiː / (BrE)
A family of leeches in the class Hirudinea, including many common medicinal leeches.

Examples

The Hirudinidae family
Many species in the Hirudinidae family have been used in traditional medicine for bloodletting.
Researchers discovered a new member of the Hirudinidae in the wetlands region.
The Hirudinidae are distinguished by their sucker-like mouths and segmented bodies.
