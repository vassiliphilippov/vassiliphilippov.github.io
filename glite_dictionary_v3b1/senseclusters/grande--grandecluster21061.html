grande

noun
medium coffee size
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɑːndeɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡrændeɪ / (BrE)
a medium or large-sized drink, especially coffee, as served in some cafés such as Starbucks

Examples

A grande latte
She ordered a grande latte to take with her on the way to work.
Would you like your coffee in a grande or a venti size?
The café's grande cappuccino is perfect for a long afternoon study session.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: tall - a small-sized drink in some cafés
  • degree higher: venti - a large-sized drink in some cafés
