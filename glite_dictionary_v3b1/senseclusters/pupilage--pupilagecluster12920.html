pupilage

noun
period of tutelage
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpjuːpɪlɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpjuːpɪlɪdʒ / (BrE)
the period or condition of being a pupil or trainee under the instruction, training, or supervision of a teacher or mentor (especially in a profession such as law)

Examples
