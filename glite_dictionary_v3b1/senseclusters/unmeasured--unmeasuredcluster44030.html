unmeasured

adjective
without limits; boundless
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmɛʒɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɛʒəd / (BrE)
not limited or determined in extent, size, or amount; limitless or boundless.

Examples

Unmeasured kindness
Her generosity seemed unmeasured and unwavering.
The poet describes the unmeasured depths of the human soul.
We gazed at the unmeasured beauty of the night sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boundless - having no boundaries; unlimited
  • synonym: limitless - without end, limit, or boundary
  • antonym: measured - deliberate and restrained; determined by measurement
