comatose

adjective
in a deep state of unconsciousness
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊməˌtoʊs / (AmE), / ˈkəʊmətəʊs / (BrE)
in a state of deep unconsciousness and unresponsive, usually because of a coma

Examples

A comatose patient
After the accident, the driver remained comatose for several weeks.
She spoke softly to her comatose brother, hoping he might hear her.
The patient was comatose and required continuous monitoring in the intensive care unit.
Doctors are monitoring his comatose condition closely.
She remained comatose for several days before regaining consciousness.
The patient was found in a comatose state after the accident.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alert - quick to notice and respond to things
  • synonym: unconscious - not awake and aware of and responding to one's environment
