channel

noun
path for transmitting electrical signals
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæn.əl / (AmE), / ˈtʃæn.əl / (BrE)
a means or path through which electrical signals are transmitted

Examples

Audio channel
Each telephone conversation uses its own dedicated channel in the network.
Engineers tested whether the channel could handle high-speed data transmissions.
The radio station broadcasts on a different channel to avoid interference.
