rebounder

noun
person in quick new relationship
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈriːˌbaʊndər / (AmE), / ˈriːˌbaʊndə / (BrE)
a person who starts a new romantic relationship soon after a breakup, often before fully recovering emotionally

Examples

A rebounder after breakup
After her long-term relationship ended, she became a rebounder by dating someone new within a week.
Some people warn against being a rebounder because it can complicate emotional healing.
He did not want to be seen as a rebounder after his recent breakup.
