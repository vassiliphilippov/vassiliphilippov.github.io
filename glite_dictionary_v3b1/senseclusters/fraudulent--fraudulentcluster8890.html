fraudulent

adjective
intended to deceive or cheat
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɔdʒələnt / (AmE), / ˈfrɔːdjʊlənt / (BrE)
intended to deceive or cheat; involving fraud or deception.

Examples

Fraudulent online scheme
Customers lost thousands of dollars to a fraudulent online scheme.
He was arrested for his role in a fraudulent insurance claim.
The company was found guilty of fraudulent accounting practices that misled investors.
