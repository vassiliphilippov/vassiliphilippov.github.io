belittling

noun
the act of belittling
Pronunciation: / bəˈlɪtəlɪŋ /; / bɪˈlɪt.lɪŋ / (AmE), / bɪˈlɪtlɪŋ /; / bɪˈlɪt.lɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making someone or something seem unimportant or of little value

Examples

Constant belittling
He could not stand the belittling he received from his peers.
Her constant belittling of her colleagues created a negative atmosphere in the office.
The review's belittling of the artist's work was harsh and unfair.
His constant belittling of her work undermined her confidence.
Teachers should avoid any form of belittling when giving feedback to students.
The meeting quickly turned sour due to his belittling comments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demeaning - the act of causing someone to lose their dignity or importance
  • synonym: disparagement - the act of speaking about someone in a negative or belittling way
  • antonym: praise - the expression of approval or admiration for someone or something
