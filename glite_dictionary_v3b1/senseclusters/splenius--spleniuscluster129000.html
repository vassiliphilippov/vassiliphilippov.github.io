splenius

noun
muscle group in the neck
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈspliːniəs / (AmE), / ˈspliːnɪəs / (BrE)
either of two flat muscles in the neck that extend from the upper vertebrae to the base of the skull, involved in rotating, flexing, or extending the head and neck

Examples

The splenius muscle
Injuries to the splenius can cause difficulty in moving the neck.
Physical therapists often focus on strengthening the splenius after whiplash injuries.
The splenius muscle helps you turn your head from side to side.
