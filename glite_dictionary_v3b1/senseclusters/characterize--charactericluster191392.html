Examples

To characterize a person
A strong sense of community characterizes life in the small town.
Openness and honesty characterize her approach to teaching.
The 19th century characterized itself by major technological innovations.
Historians often characterize the period as one of rapid social change.
It is unfair to characterize her as lazy based on one incident.
