uncoffined

adjective
without a coffin
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkɑːfɪnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɒfɪnd / (BrE)
not placed in a coffin before burial or left without a coffin.

Examples

Uncoffined body found
The body was discovered uncoffined in a remote area of the forest.
During the war, many soldiers were buried uncoffined due to lack of resources.
The ancient remains were found uncoffined, wrapped only in cloth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coffined - placed in a coffin before burial
