groined

adjective
with intersecting arches
Pronunciation: / ɡrɔɪnd / (AmE), / ɡrɔɪnd / (BrE)
having or formed with groins, especially referring to the intersection of two vaults or arches in architecture

Examples

Groined ceiling
The medieval hall was topped with a groined ceiling that added elegance to the space.
Architects admired the groined arches that supported the ancient structure.
Restoration work revealed the original groined vaults hidden beneath the plaster.
