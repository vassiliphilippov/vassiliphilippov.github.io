nome

noun
Egyptian district
9.9
Pronunciation: /noʊm/ (AmE), /nəʊm/ (BrE)
a territorial division or district in ancient Egypt, each governed by its own local official

Examples

An ancient nome
Each nome in ancient Egypt was governed by a local official called a nomarch.
The boundaries of a nome often followed natural features like rivers or deserts.
Archaeologists discovered artifacts that once belonged to the capital of a powerful nome.
