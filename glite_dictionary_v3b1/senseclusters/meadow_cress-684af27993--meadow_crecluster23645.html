meadow cress

noun
a type of bitter cress plant
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdoʊ krɛs / (AmE), / ˈmɛdəʊ krɛs / (BrE)
a small plant of the cress family, with bitter-tasting leaves, found in meadows in Europe and North America

Examples

Meadow cress plant
Botanists recorded a new population of meadow cress thriving in the nature reserve.
The meadow cress adds a peppery flavor to spring salads.
We found wild meadow cress growing along the stream in the countryside.
