Nuprin

noun
trade name for ibuprofen
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːprɪn / (AmE), / ˈnjuːprɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for ibuprofen, a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used to relieve pain, inflammation, and fever.

Nuprin pain relief
After taking Nuprin, she felt relief from her headache in less than an hour.
Many people use Nuprin as an over-the-counter remedy for minor aches and pains.
My doctor recommended Nuprin to help manage my back pain.
