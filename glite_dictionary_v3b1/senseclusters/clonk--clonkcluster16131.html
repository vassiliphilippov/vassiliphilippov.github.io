clonk

noun
dull heavy sound
7.9
Pronunciation: /klɑːŋk/ /; / /klɔːŋk/ (AmE), /klɒŋk/ (BrE)
a dull, heavy sound made when a solid object strikes another object

Examples

A loud clonk
The wrench fell to the floor with a loud clonk that echoed through the garage.
She heard a clonk from the kitchen and went to see what had fallen.
The ball hit the metal post with a sharp clonk before bouncing away.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: bang - a sudden loud noise, often from something hitting another object
  • synonym: clunk - a low, dull sound made by a heavy object hitting something
  • degree lower: tap - a light, gentle sound made by a slight touch or hit
