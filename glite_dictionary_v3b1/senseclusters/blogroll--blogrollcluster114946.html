blogroll

noun
list of recommended links
Pronunciation: / ˈblɑːɡˌroʊl / (AmE), / ˈblɒɡˌrəʊl / (BrE)
a list of recommended or favorite blogs or websites displayed on a blog, usually as links

Examples

A blogroll of links
She updated her blogroll to include several new travel blogs she discovered last month.
Many bloggers use a blogroll to share their favorite sites with readers.
His blogroll features links to both personal and professional websites he admires.
She added several new sites to her blogroll after discovering interesting travel blogs.
Many bloggers display a blogroll to share their favorite online reads with visitors.
His blogroll includes both personal friends and well-known technology writers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blog - a regularly updated website or web page, typically run by an individual or small group
  • synonym: directory - a list of websites or resources organized for easy access
  • synonym: webring - a collection of websites linked together in a circular structure
