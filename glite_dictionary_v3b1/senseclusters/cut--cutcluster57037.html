cut

adjective
trimmed vegetation; mown
5.1
Pronunciation: / kʌt / (AmE), / kʌt / (BrE)
(of grass or vegetation) trimmed or mown by a hand implement or machine.

Examples

Cut grass
Children ran across the still-damp cut lawn after the gardeners finished.
The barn was filled with stacks of cut hay ready for the winter.
The field smelled fresh after the cut grass was gathered.
