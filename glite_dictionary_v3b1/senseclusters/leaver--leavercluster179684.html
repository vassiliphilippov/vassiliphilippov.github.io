leaver

noun
supporter of leaving (political)
Pronunciation: / \u02c8li\u02d0v\u0259r / (AmE), / \u02c8li\u02d0v\u0259 / (BrE)
a person who supports or votes for leaving a political union, organization, or agreement (especially used of supporters of the UK leaving the European Union).

Examples

EU leaver
A vocal leaver campaigned in the town square, insisting sovereignty must be restored.
Many older voters identified as leavers during the referendum, citing immigration and control.
The economist met a leaver who argued trade deals would improve after withdrawal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Brexiteer - a person who supports Brexit (the UK leaving the EU); often used specifically in UK politics
  • antonym: remainer - a person who supports remaining in a political union or agreement (opposite of a leaver)
