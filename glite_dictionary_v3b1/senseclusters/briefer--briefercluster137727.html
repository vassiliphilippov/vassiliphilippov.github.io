briefer

noun
person giving summary
4.1 0.17/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàbriÀêf…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàbriÀêf…ô / (BrE)
a person who gives a short summary or report of important information

Examples

A briefer explains

Before the operation, the briefer made sure everyone understood their roles.
Each morning, a briefer updates the team on the latest developments.
