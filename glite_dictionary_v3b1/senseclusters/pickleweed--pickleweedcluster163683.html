pickleweed

noun
salt marsh plant
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkəlˌwiːd / (AmE), / ˈpɪkəlwiːd / (BrE)
a salt-tolerant, fleshy plant that grows in salty soils near coasts or marshes, sometimes eaten pickled or fresh
