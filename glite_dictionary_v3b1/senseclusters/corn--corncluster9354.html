corn

noun
(British) cereal plant such as wheat or oats
3.7 0.33/m
Pronunciation: / k…îrn / (AmE), / k…îÀên / (BrE)
(British) any of several cereal plants, especially the main grain crop of a region such as wheat in England or oats in Scotland and Ireland.

Examples

British corn fields
In Scotland, corn often refers specifically to oats rather than wheat.
In medieval England, farmers relied on corn as their principal source of food and income.
The fields were full of ripening corn ready to be harvested in late summer.

Synonyms

  • cereal
    1.0 1.62/m
    cereal is a grass grown for its starchy seeds used as food, while corn means the main regional grain crop.
  • grain
    1.0 21/m
    grain is the edible seed or crop sold as food, while corn refers to the region's main cereal crop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grain - the seeds of cereal grasses used as food
  • degree higher: wheat - a cereal plant, the main ingredient for flour and bread
