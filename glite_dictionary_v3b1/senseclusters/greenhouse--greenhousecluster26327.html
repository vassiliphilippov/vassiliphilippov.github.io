greenhouse

adjective
relating to the greenhouse effect
5.9 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈgriːn.haʊs/ (AmE), /ˈgriːn.haʊs/ (BrE)
(of gases or related phenomena) relating to or resulting from the greenhouse effect, especially in causing global warming.

Examples

Greenhouse gases
Greenhouse warming is a serious concern for the future of the planet.
Carbon dioxide is a major greenhouse gas contributing to climate change.
Scientists are studying the rise in greenhouse emissions from vehicles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: greenhouse effect - the trapping of the sun's warmth in a planet's lower atmosphere
