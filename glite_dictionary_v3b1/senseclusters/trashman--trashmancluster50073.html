trashman

noun
rubbish scavenger
3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈtræʃ.mæn/ /; / /ˈtræʃ.mən/ (AmE), /ˈtræʃ.mæn/ /; / /ˈtræʃ.mən/ (BrE)
a person who collects or searches through trash or garbage, either as a job or to find things to reuse or sell

Examples

City trashman
The trashman found an old lamp that he cleaned and sold at the market.
Every morning, the trashman checks the bins behind the shops for anything valuable.
Some people look down on the trashman, but he helps reduce waste in the city.
The trashman comes every Tuesday to pick up our bins.
When the trashman arrived, the street was lined with bags of garbage.
Children waved at the trashman as he emptied the cans into his truck.
