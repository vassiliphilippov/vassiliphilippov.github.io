word processing

noun
creating and editing text on a computer
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝd ˌproʊsɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːd ˌprəʊsɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
the use of computers or software to create, edit, format, and print text documents efficiently

Examples

Word processing software
Modern offices rely heavily on word processing to manage correspondence and documentation.
She learned word processing skills to improve her job prospects.
The introduction of word processing revolutionized the way people write and edit documents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: desktop publishing - preparing documents with text and graphics for print
  • degree lower: typewriting - the act of producing documents on a typewriter
