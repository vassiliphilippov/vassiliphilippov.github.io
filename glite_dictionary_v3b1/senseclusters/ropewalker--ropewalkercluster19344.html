ropewalker

noun
an acrobat performing on a suspended rope
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊpˌwɔkər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊpˌwɔːkə / (BrE)
an acrobat who performs feats while balancing or moving along a rope suspended high above the ground

Examples

A ropewalker performs
As a child, he dreamed of becoming a ropewalker at the big top.
Many circus acts still feature a skilled ropewalker as the main attraction.
The ropewalker dazzled the audience with her daring flips above the arena.

Related Terms
