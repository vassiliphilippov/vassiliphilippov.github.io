unoffendable

adjective
not easily offended
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈfɛndəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈfɛndəbəl / (BrE)
impossible or very difficult to offend or upset by what others say or do

Examples

An unoffendable person
No matter what jokes people make, he remains completely unoffendable and just laughs along.
Her unoffendable nature makes her easy to get along with in any group.
It is rare to find someone as unoffendable as Mark, who never takes things personally.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sensitive - easily offended or upset by what others say or do
  • synonym: thick-skinned - not easily upset or offended by criticism or insults
