evangelist

noun
enthusiastic promoter, often in tech
5.7
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvæn.dʒə.lɪst / (AmE), / ɪˈvan.dʒə.lɪst / (BrE)
a person who enthusiastically promotes or advocates for a cause, product, technology, or idea, especially in business or technology

Examples

Tech evangelist
The company hired an evangelist to promote its new cloud platform.
As an open-source evangelist, she spoke at conferences and convinced developers to contribute regularly.
He quickly became the project's chief evangelist, winning support from partners and investors.
The company's evangelist demonstrated the open-source framework to developers at the conference.
She was hired as a developer evangelist to grow adoption of the new API among partners.
At startup meetups, product evangelists excitedly explain how tools solve developers' real problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advocate - a person who publicly supports or recommends a particular cause or policy
  • synonym: promoter - a person who encourages or helps to publicize or advance something
