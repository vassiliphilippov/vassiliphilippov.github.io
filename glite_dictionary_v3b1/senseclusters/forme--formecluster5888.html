forme

noun
printing frame
9.9
Pronunciation: / fɔrm / (AmE), / fɔːm / (BrE)
a frame or arrangement of type and spacing material used in printing to hold the layout for a page

Examples

Printing forme
The printer carefully locked the forme before starting the press.
Each page required a separate forme to be assembled by hand.
After proofing, the forme was adjusted to correct the alignment of the text.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chase - a metal frame used to hold type in printing
  • synonym: galley - a tray for holding composed type in printing
