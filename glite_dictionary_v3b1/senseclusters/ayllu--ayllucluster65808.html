ayllu

noun
Andean kinship group
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪjuː / (AmE), / ˈaɪluː / (BrE)
a traditional Andean community or kinship group, especially found in Peru and Bolivia

Examples

The ayllu community

Each ayllu had its own land and shared resources among its members.
Historians study the ayllu to understand social organization in the Andes.
