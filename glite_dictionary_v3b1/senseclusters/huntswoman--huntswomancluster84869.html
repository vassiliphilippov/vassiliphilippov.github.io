huntswoman

noun
female hunter
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌntsˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈhʌntsˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who hunts game animals, especially as a sport or profession

Examples

A huntswoman in forest
As a skilled huntswoman, she led the group on their morning expedition.
The huntswoman rode confidently through the forest, searching for signs of deer.
The book profiles a pioneering huntswoman who broke barriers in the sport of fox hunting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: huntress - a female who hunts animals; traditionally poetic or formal
  • antonym: huntsman - a man who hunts game animals, especially as a profession
