Aepyorniformes

noun
order of extinct flightless elephant birds
Pronunciation: / iːpɪˌɔrnəˈfɔrmiːz /; / ˌiːpiˌɔːrnɪˈfɔːrmiːz / (AmE), / iːpɪˌɔːnɪˈfɔːmiːz /; / ˌiːpɪˌɔːnɪˈfɔːmiːz / (BrE)
an extinct order of huge, flightless birds from Madagascar, commonly known as elephant birds
