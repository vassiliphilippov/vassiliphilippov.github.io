uncivilized

adjective
lacking cultural or social development
2.0 4.8
Pronunciation: /ən.ˈsɪ.və.laɪzd/ /; / /ʌn.ˈsɪ.və.laɪzd/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈsɪ.və.laɪzd/ /; / /ən.ˈsɪ.və.laɪzd/ (BrE)
lacking the social, cultural, or moral development considered characteristic of a civilized society; not socially or culturally advanced.

Examples

Uncivilized behavior
His uncivilized behavior shocked everyone at the formal dinner.
Some ancient practices are now viewed as uncivilized by contemporary standards.
The explorers described the remote region as uncivilized and untouched by modern society.
It was seen as uncivilised to eat with your hands at the banquet.
Many considered their violent behavior to be uncivilised and shocking.
The explorer wrote about the uncivilised customs he observed during his travels.
