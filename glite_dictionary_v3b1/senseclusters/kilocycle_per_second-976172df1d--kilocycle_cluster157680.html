kilocycle per second

noun
one thousand cycles per second (kilohertz)
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪloʊˌsaɪkəl pər ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˈkɪləsʌɪkəl pə ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
a unit of frequency equal to one thousand cycles per second, now called a kilohertz

Examples

One kilocycle per second
Instructions from the 1950s often specify kilocycles per second instead of the now common term kilohertz.
Old radio stations were often listed in kilocycles per second rather than in hertz.
The signal was measured at a frequency of 15 kilocycles per second.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kilohertz - a unit of frequency equal to one thousand hertz
