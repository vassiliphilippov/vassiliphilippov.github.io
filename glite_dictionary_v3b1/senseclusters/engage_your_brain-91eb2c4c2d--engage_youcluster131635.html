engage your brain

verb
start thinking actively; use your mind
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ jʊr breɪn / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ jɔː breɪn / (BrE)
to begin to think carefully or use your mind actively before speaking or acting

Examples

To engage your brain carefully
Before making comments in class, try to engage your brain.
She had to remind herself to engage her brain during the complex discussion.
Sometimes it helps to pause and engage your brain before reacting emotionally.

Related Terms

  • synonym: think - to use your mind to consider something
