unshrink

verb
to restore original size
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃrɪŋk / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃrɪŋk / (BrE)
to cause something that has shrunk to return to its original size

Examples

To unshrink a sweater
She tried to unshrink her wool sweater by soaking it in water.
Is it possible to unshrink a cotton shirt after it has been washed in hot water?
There are special products that claim to unshrink delicate fabrics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: restore - to bring something back to its original condition
  • antonym: shrink - to become smaller in size
