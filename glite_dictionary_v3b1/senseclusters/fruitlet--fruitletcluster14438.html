fruitlet

noun
small or diminutive fruit
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfruːt.lət / (AmE), / ˈfruːt.lət / (BrE)
a small or diminutive fruit, especially one that is a part of a cluster or a multiple fruit

Examples

A small fruitlet
After the flower is pollinated, the ovary swells and develops into a fruitlet.
Each raspberry is made up of dozens of tiny fruitlets clustered together.
The strawberry’s surface is dotted with many small fruitlets, each containing a seed.
