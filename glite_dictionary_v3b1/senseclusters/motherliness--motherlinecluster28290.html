motherliness

noun
tender, caring quality like a mother
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌðɚlinəs / (AmE), / ˈmʌðəlɪnəs / (BrE)
the quality of having or showing the tenderness, warmth, and affection typically associated with a mother

Examples

Her motherliness showed
Her motherliness was evident in the gentle way she comforted the crying child.
His aunt's motherliness made the whole family feel cared for and supported.
Many people were drawn to her because of her natural motherliness and warm personality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: motherly - having the qualities of a mother; kind and caring in a maternal way
