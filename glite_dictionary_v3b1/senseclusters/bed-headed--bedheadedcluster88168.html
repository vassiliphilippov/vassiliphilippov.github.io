bed-headed

adjective
having messy hair from sleep
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɛdˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
(of a person) having hair that is messy or disheveled from sleeping or just waking up.

Examples

Bed-headed hair
I didn't have time to brush my bed-headed locks before leaving the house.
She showed up to breakfast still looking bed-headed after a late night.
The little boy's bed-headed hair made everyone in the family smile.
