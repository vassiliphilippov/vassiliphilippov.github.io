babacu oil

noun
oil from babassu nuts, similar to coconut oil
7.0
Pronunciation: / bəˈbɑːsu ˌɔɪl / (AmE), / bəˈbɑːsu ˌɔɪl / (BrE)
a fatty oil extracted from the kernels of babassu nuts, closely resembling coconut oil in composition and uses

Examples

Babacu oil production
Industrial manufacturers often use babacu oil in cosmetics and soap production.
Some natural skincare products feature babacu oil for its moisturizing qualities.
The chef substituted babacu oil for coconut oil in the recipe due to its similar properties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coconut oil - fatty oil obtained from the dried meat of coconuts
