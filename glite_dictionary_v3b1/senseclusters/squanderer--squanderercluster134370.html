squanderer

noun
person who wastes resources
8.2 6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɑndərər / (AmE), / ˈskwɒndərə / (BrE)
A person who spends money, time, or other resources wastefully or extravagantly.

Examples

A squanderer of money
As a notorious squanderer, he quickly spent his entire inheritance on luxury cars and exotic vacations.
Her father warned her not to become a squanderer and to save for the future instead.
The business failed because its manager was a squanderer who ignored budgets and financial advice.

Related Terms

  • antonym: miser - a person who hoards wealth and spends as little money as possible
  • synonym: spendthrift - a person who spends money irresponsibly or extravagantly
  • synonym: waster - a person who wastes something, especially money
