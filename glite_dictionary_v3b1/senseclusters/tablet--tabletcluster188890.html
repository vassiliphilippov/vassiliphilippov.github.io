tablet

noun
portable touchscreen computer
2.6 2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtæb.lət / (AmE), / ˈtæb.lɪt / (BrE)

Examples

A tablet computer

She uses her tablet to read books and watch movies while traveling.
The new tablet features a larger display and improved battery life.
