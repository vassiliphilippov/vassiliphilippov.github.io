with

preposition
using a tool or means
Pronunciation: / wɪð / (AmE), / wɪð / (BrE)
used to show the instrument, tool, or means by which something is done

Examples

He fixed the chair with some glue and a clamp.
The artist painted the mural with bright, bold colors.
