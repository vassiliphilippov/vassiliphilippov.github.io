black weevil

noun
grain-infesting weevil
6.8
Pronunciation: / blæk ˈwiːvəl / (AmE), / blæk ˈwiːvəl / (BrE)
a brown weevil that infests stored grain, especially rice

Examples

The black weevil insect
Farmers noticed the presence of black weevil in their stored rice sacks.
Inspecting the warehouse, they found a large infestation of black weevil among the rice bags.
The black weevil can cause significant losses to grain products if not controlled.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rice weevil - a weevil (Sitophilus oryzae) that is a major pest of stored grain and rice
  • degree higher: weevil - a small beetle from the Curculionidae family, many species of which are pests
