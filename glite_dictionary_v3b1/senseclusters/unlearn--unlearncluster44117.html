unlearn

verb
remove learned information from a model
8.1 0.00/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàl…úrn / (AmE), /  ånÀàl…úÀên / (BrE)
(in computing, especially machine learning) to remove or reverse information, patterns, or the influence of specific training data from a model so it no longer retains or reproduces that learned content.

Examples

To unlearn data patterns
To comply with the request, engineers plan to unlearn the user's name from the model.
After the breach, the team unlearned all training examples containing personal data to protect privacy.
Researchers are experimenting with algorithms that can quickly unlearn outdated patterns without full retraining.

Synonyms

  • unteach
    9.5 0.00/m
    unteach means deliberately erasing specific knowledge from a trained model through targeted procedures, while unlearn means reversing learned patterns in a model.

Related Terms

  • synonym: erase - to remove or delete data or information
  • synonym: forget - to lose or remove memory or information; used metaphorically for removing learned patterns
