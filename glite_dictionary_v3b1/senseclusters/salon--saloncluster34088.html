salon

noun
art gallery or exhibition
Pronunciation: / səˈlɑn / (AmE), / ˈsælɒn / (BrE)
a gallery or exhibition, especially for the display of works of art

Examples

Art salon
He visited a salon last weekend to see new works by emerging artists.
Her sculpture was chosen for display in the prestigious Paris salon.
The annual art salon attracted painters and critics from all over Europe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gallery - a room or building for the display or sale of works of art
