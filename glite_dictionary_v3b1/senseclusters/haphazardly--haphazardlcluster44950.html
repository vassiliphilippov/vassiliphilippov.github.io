haphazardly

adverb
in a random or careless way
Pronunciation: / hæpˈhæzɚdli / (AmE), / hæpˈhæzədli / (BrE)
in a way that is not planned, organized, or orderly; at random

Examples

To haphazardly place items
Paint was splattered haphazardly across the canvas, giving the artwork a wild appearance.
She threw her clothes haphazardly into the suitcase as she rushed to leave.
The books were stacked haphazardly on the shelves, with no regard for order.
Books were stacked haphazardly on the shelves, making it hard to find anything.
The artist splashed paint haphazardly across the canvas to create an abstract effect.
Traffic cones were placed haphazardly around the construction site, confusing the drivers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carefully - in a way that shows attention and caution
  • antonym: methodically - in an orderly or systematic manner
  • synonym: randomly - without definite aim, reason, or pattern
