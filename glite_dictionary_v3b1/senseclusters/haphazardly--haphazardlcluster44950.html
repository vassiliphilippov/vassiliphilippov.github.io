haphazardly

adverb
in a random or careless way
9.5 5.0
Pronunciation: /hæp.ˈhæz.ərd.liː/ /; / /hæf.ˈhæz.ərd.liː/ (AmE), /hæp.ˈhæz.əd.liː/ /; / /hæf.ˈhæz.əd.liː/ (BrE)
