juvie

noun
youth detention center
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuːvi / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːvi / (BrE)
a slang or informal term for a juvenile detention center or the juvenile justice system where young offenders are held or tried

Examples

Many teenagers hope to avoid ending up in juvie for minor offenses.

After the fight at school, he was sent to juvie for a month.
Her brother spent time in juvie when he was younger.
Many kids in juvie hope to turn their lives around after release.
