juvie

noun
youth detention center
9.9 0.58/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd íuÀêvi / (AmE), / Ààd íuÀêvi / (BrE)
a slang or informal term for a juvenile detention center or the juvenile justice system where young offenders are held or tried

Examples

Her brother spent time in juvie when he was younger.
