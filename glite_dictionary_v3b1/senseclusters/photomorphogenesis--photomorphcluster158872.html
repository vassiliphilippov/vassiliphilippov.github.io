photomorphogenesis

noun
plant growth by light
9.9
Pronunciation: /foʊ.tə.mɑːr.fə.ˈdʒen.ə.sɪs/ /; / /foʊ.tə.mɔːr.fə.ˈdʒen.ə.sɪs/ (AmE), /fəʊ.tə.mɔː.fə.ˈdʒen.ə.sɪs/ (BrE)

Examples
