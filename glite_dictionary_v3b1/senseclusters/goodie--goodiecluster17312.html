goodie

noun
heroic character
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʊdi / (AmE), / ˈɡʊdi / (BrE)
a person who is considered morally good, especially the hero or good character in a story, film, or play

Examples

The goodie wins
In the old western, the goodie always wore a white hat and saved the town.
Children often cheer for the goodie when watching superhero movies.
The story was simple, with a clear goodie and a villain fighting for control.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hero - the main good character in a story
  • antonym: villain - the main bad character in a story
