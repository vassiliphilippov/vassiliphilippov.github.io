douar

noun
North African district
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈduː.ɑːr/ (AmE), /ˈduː.ɑː/ (BrE)
an administrative district or local government unit in parts of North Africa, often consisting of several villages or camps

Examples

Douar administration
The census recorded each douar separately to organize local administration.
Many families in the region belong to the same douar and share traditions.
During colonial times, a douar often included several small villages or camps.
