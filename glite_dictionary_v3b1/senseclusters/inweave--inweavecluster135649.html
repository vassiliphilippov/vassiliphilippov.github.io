inweave

verb
weave into fabric or design
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈwiv / (AmE), / ɪnˈwiːv / (BrE)
to weave something into a fabric or design, especially by interlacing threads or elements together

Examples

To inweave threads
Legends of the town are inwoven in its rich cultural traditions.
She skillfully inwove bright colors into the otherwise plain blanket.
The artist chose to inweave delicate silver threads into the tapestry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: weave - to form fabric by interlacing threads
