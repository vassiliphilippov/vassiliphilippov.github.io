church bell

noun
bell in a church tower
1.0
Pronunciation: / tʃɝtʃ bɛl / (AmE), / tʃɜːtʃ bɛl / (BrE)
a bell hung in a church tower, rung especially to summon people to services or to mark the time

Examples

The church bell rings
At noon, the church bell rang to announce the start of the wedding ceremony.
Residents gathered as the church bell tolled in memory of the town’s founder.
The sound of the church bell echoed across the quiet village every Sunday morning.
