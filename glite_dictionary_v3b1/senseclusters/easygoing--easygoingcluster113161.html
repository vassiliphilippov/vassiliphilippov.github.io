easygoing

adjective
relaxed and unhurried
2.3 2.2
Pronunciation: /ˈiː.zi.goʊ.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈiː.zi.ɡəʊ.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
relaxed, calm, and not easily upset or worried; not demanding or stressful

Examples

An easygoing person
The class had an easygoing start, allowing students to settle in.
Their journey proceeded at an easygoing pace, with frequent stops to admire the scenery.
We enjoyed an easygoing stroll through the park before dinner.
He enjoyed his easygoing job that allowed him plenty of time to relax.
She preferred the easygoing routine of freelancing over the stresses of full-time work.
Their lifestyle was so easygoing that every day felt like a vacation.
Despite the stressful environment, she remained easygoing and cheerful throughout the project.
His easygoing nature made him popular among his colleagues and friends.
They appreciated having an easygoing boss who understood their personal needs.
