buffalo clover

noun
a clover native to western U.S.
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌfəloʊ ˈkloʊvər /; / ˈbʌfəloʊ ˈkloʊvɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʌfələʊ ˈkləʊvə / (BrE)
a type of clover plant native to the United States, sometimes found in prairies or grasslands, and in some regions known to be toxic to livestock
