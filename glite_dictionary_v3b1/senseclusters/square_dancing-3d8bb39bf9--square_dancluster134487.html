square dancing

noun
American country dance where couples form squares
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɛr ˌdænsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskwɛː ˌdɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
A traditional American country dance in which four couples form a square and follow a caller's instructions.

Examples

Traditional square dancing
Have you ever tried square dancing with a live caller and a band?
Hundreds of people gathered in the barn for an evening of square dancing.
She teaches square dancing at the local community center every Friday night.

Related Terms
