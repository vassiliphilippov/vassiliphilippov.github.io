quackish

adjective
fraudulent or fake
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkwækɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkwækɪʃ / (BrE)
pretending to have professional skills or knowledge, especially in medicine, but lacking them

Examples

Quackish medical practice
The clinic was shut down after its quackish practices were exposed by investigators.
She warned her friend about the quackish remedies being sold online.
Many people fell for the quackish claims of the self-proclaimed health expert.
