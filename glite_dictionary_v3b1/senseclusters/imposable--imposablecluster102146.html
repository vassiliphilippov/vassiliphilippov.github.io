imposable

adjective
able to be enforced
6.7
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpoʊzəbəl / (AmE), / ɪmˈpəʊzəbəl / (BrE)
able to be imposed, applied, or enforced on someone or something

Examples

Rules imposable on businesses
The new regulations are only imposable on businesses with more than fifty employees.
A fine is imposable if the rules are broken repeatedly.
Certain restrictions are not imposable under current international law.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enforceable - able to be enforced by law or authority
  • antonym: unimposable - not able to be imposed or enforced
