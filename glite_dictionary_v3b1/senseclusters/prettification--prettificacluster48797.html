prettification

noun
making something more attractive
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌprɛt̬ɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌprɛtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making something more attractive or appealing, often in a superficial way

Examples

The prettification of buildings
Some critics argue that prettification of historic buildings can undermine their original character.
The prettification of the report made it easier to present to stakeholders.
The city spent millions on the prettification of its parks for the summer festival.
