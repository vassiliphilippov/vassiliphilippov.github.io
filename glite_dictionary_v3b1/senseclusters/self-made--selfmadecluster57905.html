self-made

adjective
having achieved success through own efforts
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlfˈmeɪd / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˈmeɪd / (BrE)
having achieved success or recognition by one's own efforts, rather than by inheritance or external help

Examples

Self-made entrepreneur
He became a self-made entrepreneur after starting his own company from scratch.
She is proud to be a self-made woman, having built her business without any family connections.
The self-made billionaire shared his story at the conference.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inherited - having gained (especially status or wealth) from ancestors rather than one's own efforts
  • synonym: self-sufficient - able to provide for oneself without help from others
