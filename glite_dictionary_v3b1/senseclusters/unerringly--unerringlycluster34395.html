unerringly

adverb
without making mistakes
4.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɛrɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈɛːrɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that never makes mistakes or errors; always accurate or correct

Examples

To unerringly find truth
He unerringly identifies the flaws in every argument presented.
She unerringly predicted the outcome of the election months before it happened.
The dog unerringly found its way back home through the dense forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accurately - in a correct or exact way
  • antonym: erroneously - in a mistaken or incorrect way
