pedder

verb
to sell door-to-door
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛdər / (AmE), / ˈpɛdə / (BrE)
to sell goods by traveling from place to place, especially in small amounts or directly to customers

Examples

To pedder goods
During the festival, she would pedder her handmade jewelry to visitors in the nearby towns.
He used to pedder fresh fruit from his farm to different neighborhoods every weekend.
Many people in the past would pedder goods to earn a living before shops became common.
