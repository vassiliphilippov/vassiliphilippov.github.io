nonfisherman

noun
person who does not fish
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈfɪʃərmən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈfɪʃəmən / (BrE)
a person who does not fish, especially as a hobby or occupation

Examples

A nonfisherman visitor
The lakeside park is enjoyed by both nonfishermen and those who love to fish.
As a nonfisherman, he preferred to hike along the river rather than cast a line.
Many nonfishermen visit the area for its beautiful scenery and peaceful atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fisherman - a person who catches fish for a living or as a hobby
