doubleness

noun
deceitful insincerity
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌbəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəlnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being deceitful or insincere by presenting two different faces or attitudes

Examples

Hidden doubleness
His apparent kindness masked a hidden doubleness that made people wary of trusting him.
The novel explores the doubleness of the main character, who leads a secret life.
She was admired for her charm, but her doubleness eventually led to her downfall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duplicity - deceitfulness or double-dealing
  • antonym: honesty - the quality of being truthful and sincere
