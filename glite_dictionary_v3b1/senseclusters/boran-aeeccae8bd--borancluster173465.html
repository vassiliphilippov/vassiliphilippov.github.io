Boran

proper noun
Sasanian queen of Persia
Pronunciation: / boʊˈrɑːn / (AmE), / bɔːˈrɑːn / (BrE)
the 7th-century Sasanian queen of Persia who ruled briefly after a period of dynastic turmoil

Examples

Queen Boran of Persia
Boran was one of the few women to rule the Sasanian Empire in ancient Persia.
Historians often discuss the challenges Boran faced during her short reign.
After her father's death, Boran became queen and tried to restore stability to the empire.
