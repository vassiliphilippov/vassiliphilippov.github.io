Podunk

proper noun
Native American people
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊdʌŋk / (AmE), / ˈpəʊdʌŋk / (BrE)
a member of the Podunk, an Algonquian-speaking Native American people historically living in the Connecticut River valley
