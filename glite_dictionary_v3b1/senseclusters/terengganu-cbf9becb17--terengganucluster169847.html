Terengganu

proper noun
state of Malaysia on the eastern coast
9.0
Pronunciation: / təˌrɛŋɡəˈnu /; / təˈrɛŋɡəˌnu / (AmE), / təˌrɛŋɡəˈnuː /; / təˈrɛŋɡəˌnu / (BrE)
a state (and former sultanate) on the east coast of Peninsular Malaysia, known for its beaches, islands, and traditional Malay culture
