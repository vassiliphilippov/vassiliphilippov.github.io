carless

adjective
without a car
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrləs / (AmE), / ˈkɑːləs / (BrE)
not owning, using, or having access to a car

Examples

A carless life
Living a carless life in the city can save money and reduce stress.
The new apartment complex is designed for carless residents who prefer walking or cycling.
After selling his vehicle, he became completely carless and relied on public transport.
