bobbin

noun
spool for winding thread or film
7.2 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑː.bɪn/ /; / /ˈbɑː.bən/ (AmE), /ˈbɒb.ɪn/ /; / /ˈbɒb.ən/ (BrE)

Examples
