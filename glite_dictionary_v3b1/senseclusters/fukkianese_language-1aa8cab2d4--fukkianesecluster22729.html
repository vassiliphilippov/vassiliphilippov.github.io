Fukkianese language

noun
a Chinese language variety from Fujian province
9.9
Pronunciation: /fuː.kiː.əˈniːz ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ/ /; / /fʌk.ɪ.əˈniːz ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ/ /; / /fʊk.ɪ.əˈniːz ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ/ (AmE), /fuː.kiː.əˈniːz ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ/ /; / /fʌk.ɪ.əˈniːz ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ/ /; / /fʊk.ɪ.əˈniːz ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ/ (BrE)
any of the various forms of Chinese spoken in Fujian (Fukien) province and neighboring regions, also known as Hokkien or Min Nan.
