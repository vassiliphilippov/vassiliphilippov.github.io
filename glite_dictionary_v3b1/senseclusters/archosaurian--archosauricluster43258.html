archosaurian

adjective
relating to archosaur reptiles
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːr.kəˈsɔːr.i.ən / (AmE), / ˌɑː.kəˈsɔː.ri.ən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of reptiles belonging to the subclass Archosauria, which includes crocodiles, dinosaurs, and pterosaurs.

Examples

Archosaurian features
Many paleontologists specialize in the study of archosaurian fossils to understand prehistoric life.
Modern crocodiles are considered to be archosaurian reptiles, closely related to ancient species.
The archosaurian lineage includes both flying pterosaurs and giant dinosaurs.
