expresso

noun
variant of espresso
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsprɛsoʊ / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛsəʊ / (BrE)
a common misspelling or variant spelling of 'espresso', a strong coffee made by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee beans

Examples

A strong expresso coffee
He ordered an expresso at the café, though the menu spelled it 'espresso'.
Some people mistakenly write expresso instead of the correct term 'espresso'.
The barista smiled politely when the customer asked for an expresso.
