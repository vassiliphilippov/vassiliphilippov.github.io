minginess

noun
extreme stinginess; meanness
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪni.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmɪni.nɪs / (BrE)
extreme unwillingness to spend money or share; excessive stinginess or meanness

Examples

Extreme minginess
His minginess was legendary; he would never offer to buy even a cup of coffee for friends.
She disliked the minginess that made family gatherings feel awkward and uncomfortable.
The company's minginess was evident in their refusal to provide basic amenities for employees.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generosity - willingness to give or share; unselfishness
  • synonym: stinginess - unwillingness to share or spend; meanness
