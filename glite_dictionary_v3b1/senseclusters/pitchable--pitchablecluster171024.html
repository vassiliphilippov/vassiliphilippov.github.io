pitchable

adjective
able to be tarred
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪtʃəbəl / (AmE), / ˈpɪtʃəbəl / (BrE)
capable of being coated, sealed, or treated with pitch, especially to make something waterproof.

Examples

Pitchable boat hull
The old boat was still pitchable and could be made watertight with a fresh coat of tar.
Before launching, the builders checked if the hull was pitchable to ensure it would not leak.
Only certain types of wood are pitchable and suitable for traditional shipbuilding methods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: waterproof - resistant to water; not allowing water to pass through
