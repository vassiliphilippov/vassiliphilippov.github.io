langley

noun
unit of solar radiation
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlæŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈlæŋ.li / (BrE)
a unit of solar radiation equal to one calorie per square centimeter, used to measure the amount of solar energy received at the Earth's surface
