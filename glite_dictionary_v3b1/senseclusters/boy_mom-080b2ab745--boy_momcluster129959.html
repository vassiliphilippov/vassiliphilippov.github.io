boy mom

noun
a mother of only sons
6.2
Pronunciation: / bɔɪ mɑm / (AmE), / bɔɪ mɒm / (BrE)
a mother who has only sons, especially one who actively embraces the lifestyle and activities associated with raising boys

Examples

A boy mom lifestyle
As a proud boy mom, she always has snacks and sports gear on hand.
Being a boy mom means you may become an expert on dinosaurs and mud puddles.
She loves sharing her experiences as a boy mom on social media.
