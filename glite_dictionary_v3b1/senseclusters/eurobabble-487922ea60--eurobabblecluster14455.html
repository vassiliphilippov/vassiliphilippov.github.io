Eurobabble

noun
jargon of EU documents and regulations
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈjʊroʊˌbæbəl / (AmE), / ˈjʊərəʊˌbabəl / (BrE)
complex and often obscure jargon used in European Union documents and regulations, especially regarded as excessively bureaucratic or difficult to understand
