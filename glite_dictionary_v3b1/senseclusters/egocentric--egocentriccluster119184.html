egocentric

noun
self-centered person
4.6 5.5
Pronunciation: /iː.ɡoʊ.ˈsen.trɪk/ /; / /iː.ɡəʊ.ˈsen.trɪk/ (AmE), /iː.ɡəʊ.ˈsen.trɪk/ /; / /iː.ɡoʊ.ˈsen.trɪk/ (BrE)
a person who is excessively self-centered and shows little concern for the feelings or needs of others

Examples

An egocentric person
An egocentric often dominates conversations and dismisses the opinions of others.
She soon realized that Mark was an egocentric who cared little for other people's feelings.
The psychologist described the patient as an extreme egocentric lacking empathy for those around him.
