videoconference

noun
video meeting
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪdioʊˌkɑːnfərəns / (AmE), / ˈvɪdiəʊˌkɒnfərəns / (BrE)
a meeting or discussion held between people in different places using video and internet technology

Examples

A videoconference meeting
We scheduled a videoconference with our overseas partners to discuss the new project.
The manager prefers a videoconference instead of traveling for every meeting.
Technical issues delayed the start of the videoconference by fifteen minutes.
The team held a videoconference to coordinate their work from different countries.
Due to travel restrictions, the annual meeting was replaced by a videoconference.
She joined the videoconference from her home office early in the morning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: in-person meeting - a meeting where all participants are physically present in the same location
  • synonym: meeting - an event where people come together to discuss something
  • synonym: teleconference - a meeting held by people in different places using telecommunication devices
  • synonym: webinar - an online seminar or presentation held over the internet
