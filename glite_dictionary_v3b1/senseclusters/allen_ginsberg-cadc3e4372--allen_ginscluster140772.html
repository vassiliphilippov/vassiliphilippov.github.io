Allen Ginsberg

proper noun
American poet and Beat Generation icon
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈælən ˈɡɪnzbɜːrɡ / (AmE), / ˈælən ˈɡɪnzbɜːɡ / (BrE)
(1926–1997) An American poet and leading figure of the Beat Generation, known for works such as 'Howl' and his influence on 20th-century literature and counterculture.
