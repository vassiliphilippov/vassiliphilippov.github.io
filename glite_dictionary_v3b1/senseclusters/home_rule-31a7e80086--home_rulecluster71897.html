home rule

noun
local self-government by a city or county
6.2 0.23/m
Pronunciation: / ho äm ruÀêl / (AmE), / h…ô äm ruÀêl / (BrE)
the right or power of a city or county to govern its own local affairs rather than being controlled by a central government

Examples

Home rule movement
Movements for home rule have historically sought more independent governance from central powers.
Thanks to home rule, the county can set its own tax policies.
The city council advocated for home rule to better address local issues.

Synonyms

  • self-rule
    5.8 0.54/m
    self-rule is a region governing itself, often after colonial rule, while home rule is local city or county self-government.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autonomy - self-government or independence in general
  • antonym: centralization - the concentration of control under a central government
