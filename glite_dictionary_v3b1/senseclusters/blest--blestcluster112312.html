blest

adjective
highly favored; blessed (literary)
4.2
Pronunciation: / blɛst / (AmE), / blɛst / (BrE)
(Archaic or literary) Highly favored or fortunate, especially due to divine grace; blessed.

Examples

A blest life
In times of trouble, she felt blest by the support of her friends.
The poem spoke of a blest land flowing with abundance.
They considered themselves blest to live in such a peaceful village.
