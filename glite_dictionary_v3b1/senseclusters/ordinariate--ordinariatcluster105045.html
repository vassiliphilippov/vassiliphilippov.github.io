ordinariate

noun
church district
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɔːr.dɪ.ˈneɪ.ət/ (AmE), /ɔː.dɪ.ˈneə.rɪ.ət/ (BrE)
a district, organization, or office under the authority of a church official (especially in the Catholic Church)

The ordinariate has its own liturgical traditions within the wider church.
