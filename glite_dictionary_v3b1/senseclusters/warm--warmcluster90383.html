warm

adjective
strongly enthusiastic or affectionate
1.0
Pronunciation: / wɔrm / (AmE), / wɔːm / (BrE)
friendly, affectionate, or showing enthusiasm and kindness; expressing strong or heartfelt feeling

Examples

Warm welcome
His warm support made all the difference during a difficult time.
She expressed warm thanks for everyone's hard work.
The speaker received a warm welcome from the enthusiastic crowd.
Her warm smile made everyone feel instantly at ease.
His warm personality helps put even nervous clients at ease.
We received a warm welcome from our new neighbors when we arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ardent - showing very strong feelings or enthusiasm
  • antonym: cold - unfriendly or lacking in affection (of people or behaviour)
  • synonym: cordial - warm and friendly; sincere
  • antonym: lukewarm - showing little enthusiasm or interest
