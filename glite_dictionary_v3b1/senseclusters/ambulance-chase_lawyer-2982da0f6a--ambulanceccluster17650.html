ambulance-chase lawyer

noun
lawyer who solicits accident victims
Pronunciation: / ˈæm.bjə.ləns tʃeɪs ˈlɔɪ.ər / (AmE), / ˈæm.bjʊ.ləns tʃeɪs ˈlɔɪ.ə / (BrE)
a lawyer who aggressively seeks clients by pursuing people involved in accidents or emergencies, especially for personal injury cases

Examples

After the car crash, an ambulance-chase lawyer handed me his business card at the hospital.
She refused to hire an ambulance-chase lawyer, preferring to find a reputable attorney through recommendations.
The city's new regulations target the unethical practices of ambulance-chase lawyers.
