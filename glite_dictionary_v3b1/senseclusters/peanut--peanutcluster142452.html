peanut

noun
small young child (informal, affectionate)
Pronunciation: / ˈpinʌt / (AmE), / ˈpiːnʌt / (BrE)
(informal) A young child who is small for their age, often used affectionately.

Examples

A little peanut
Although he's just a peanut, he's got a big personality.
Come here, peanut, and let me tie your shoes.
She calls her little sister peanut because she's the smallest in her class.
