guardian angel

noun
an angel assigned to protect a person
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːrdiən ˈeɪndʒəl /; / ˈɡɑːrdiən ˈeɪn.dʒəl / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdiən ˈeɪndʒəl /; / ˈɡɑːdiən ˈeɪn.dʒəl / (BrE)
a spirit, angel, or person who is believed to protect, help, or guide someone

Examples

A guardian angel protects
In times of trouble, he felt as if a guardian angel was watching over him.
She believes that her guardian angel has protected her from harm many times.
The nurse was like a guardian angel to the frightened children.
After the accident, he believed a guardian angel had saved his life.
During the storm, a stranger appeared like a guardian angel, helping them reach safety.
She always felt that her grandmother was her guardian angel, watching over her in difficult times.
