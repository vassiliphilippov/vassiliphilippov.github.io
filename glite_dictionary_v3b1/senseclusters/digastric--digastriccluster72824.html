digastric

adjective
two-bellied muscle
9.3
Pronunciation: / daɪˈɡæstrɪk / (AmE), / daɪˈɡæstrɪk / (BrE)
relating to a muscle that has two separate muscle bellies connected by a tendon

Examples

Digastric muscle structure
The digastric muscle plays an important role in opening the mouth.
During surgery, the doctor carefully avoided damaging the digastric structure.
An injury to the digastric area can make swallowing difficult.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biceps - a muscle with two heads or points of origin
