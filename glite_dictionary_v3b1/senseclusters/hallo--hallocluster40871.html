hallo

noun
attention-seeking shout
Pronunciation: / həˈloʊ / (AmE), / həˈləʊ / (BrE)
a loud call or shout used to attract someone's attention, especially from a distance

Examples

A loud hallo
He gave a loud hallo to get the hikers' attention across the valley.
A sudden hallo echoed through the forest, startling the birds.
With a cheerful hallo, she greeted her friends from across the street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hello - a greeting or call to attract attention
  • synonym: hey - an exclamation to attract attention
