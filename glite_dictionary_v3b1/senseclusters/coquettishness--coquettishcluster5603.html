coquettishness

noun
flirtatious behavior
9.1
Pronunciation: / koʊˈkɛtɪʃnəs / (AmE), / kəˈkɛtɪʃnəs / (BrE)
the quality of behaving in a playful, flirtatious, or teasing manner to attract attention

Examples

Coquettishness in behavior
Her coquettishness was obvious as she laughed and glanced over her shoulder.
The actor played the role with just the right amount of coquettishness to charm the audience.
He was drawn to her coquettishness, finding her playful teasing irresistible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flirtatiousness - the quality of being inclined to flirt
  • antonym: shyness - the quality of being reserved or timid
