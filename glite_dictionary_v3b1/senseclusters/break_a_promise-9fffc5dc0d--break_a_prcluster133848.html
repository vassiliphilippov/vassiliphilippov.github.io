break a promise

verb
fail to keep a promise
Pronunciation: / breɪk ə ˈprɑːmɪs / (AmE), / breɪk ə ˈprɒmɪs / (BrE)

Examples

To break a promise made

If you break a promise, it can be hard to regain someone's trust.
Politicians often face criticism when they break a promise made during their campaigns.
