Milesian thinker

noun
early Greek philosopher from Miletus
9.9
Pronunciation: / maɪˈliʒən ˈθɪŋkər / (AmE), / maɪˈliːziən ˈθɪŋkə / (BrE)
one of a group of early Greek philosophers from Miletus, regarded as pioneers of natural philosophy

Examples

Ancient Milesian thinker
Many historians consider Anaximander to be an important Milesian thinker.
She wrote her dissertation on the influence of Milesian thinkers in early Greek science.
The work of the Milesian thinker Thales laid the foundations for Western philosophy.
