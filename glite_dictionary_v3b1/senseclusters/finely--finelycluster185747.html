finely

adverb
in very small or thin pieces
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪnli / (AmE), / ˈfaɪnli / (BrE)
into or in very small or thin pieces or particles

Examples

Finely chopped onions
Chop the onions finely for the best texture in the soup.
Grate the cheese finely before adding it to the dish.
The sugar was ground finely to make a smooth icing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coarsely - in large pieces or particles
