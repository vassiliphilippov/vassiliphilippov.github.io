hobnob

verb
socialize with influential people
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑb.nɑb / (AmE), / ˈhɒb.nɒb / (BrE)
to mix socially, especially with people of higher status or influence

Examples

To hobnob with leaders
During the conference, she hobnobbed with industry leaders and innovators.
He loves to hobnob with celebrities at exclusive parties.
Journalists often hobnob with politicians to gain inside information.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mingle - to move among and engage with others at a social event
  • synonym: socialize - to interact with others in a friendly way
