floorless

adjective
without a floor
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔrləs / (AmE), / ˈflɔːləs / (BrE)
lacking a floor or having no floor inside a structure or space

Examples

Floorless cabin
The abandoned cabin was completely floorless, exposing only the dirt beneath.
They camped in a floorless tent to feel closer to nature.
The shed was old and floorless, making it difficult to store anything inside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bare - not covered or furnished; empty
