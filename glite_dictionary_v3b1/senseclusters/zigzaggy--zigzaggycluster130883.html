zigzaggy

adjective
erratic or unpredictable
Pronunciation: / ˈzɪɡˌzæɡi / (AmE), / ˈzɪɡˌzæɡi / (BrE)
changing direction, opinion, or behavior frequently in an erratic or unpredictable way

Examples

Zigzaggy behavior

Her zigzaggy career took her from teaching to engineering and then to art.
The team's zigzaggy performance this season has surprised many fans.
