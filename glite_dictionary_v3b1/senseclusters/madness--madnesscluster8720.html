madness

noun
intense anger or fury
Pronunciation: / ˈmædnəs / (AmE), / ˈmædnɪs / (BrE)
a feeling of intense anger or fury

Examples

A fit of madness
He erupted in a fit of madness when he heard the news.
His madness frightened everyone in the room.
You could see the madness in her eyes as she argued fiercely.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - a state of being free from agitation or strong emotion
  • synonym: fury - wild or violent anger
  • synonym: rage - a strong feeling of uncontrollable anger
