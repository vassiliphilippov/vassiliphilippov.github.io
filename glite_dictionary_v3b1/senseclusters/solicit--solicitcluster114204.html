solicit

verb
urge to commit an illegal act
1.5
Pronunciation: / səˈlɪsɪt / (AmE), / səˈlɪsɪt / (BrE)
to incite, persuade, or urge someone to commit an unlawful or disobedient act

Examples

To solicit a crime
He was arrested for attempting to solicit others to break the law.
It is illegal to solicit someone to commit fraud.
The official was accused of soliciting his staff to cover up the crime.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incite - to encourage or stir up unlawful behavior
  • synonym: induce - to lead or move by persuasion or influence
