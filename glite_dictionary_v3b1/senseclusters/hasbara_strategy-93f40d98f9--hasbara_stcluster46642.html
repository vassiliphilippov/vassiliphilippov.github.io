hasbara strategy

noun
Israeli public relations strategy
9.1
Pronunciation: / hɑzˈbɑrə ˈstrætədʒi / (AmE), / hɑzˈbɑːrə ˈstrætədʒi / (BrE)
a public relations strategy used to explain or justify Israel's policies and actions, particularly to foreign audiences

Examples

Hasbara strategy development
He specializes in developing hasbara strategies for countering criticism in the media.
Several diplomats updated their hasbara strategy in response to recent events.
The government organized a series of hasbara strategies to improve its international image.

Related Terms

  • synonym: public relations - the professional maintenance of a favorable public image by an organization or individual
