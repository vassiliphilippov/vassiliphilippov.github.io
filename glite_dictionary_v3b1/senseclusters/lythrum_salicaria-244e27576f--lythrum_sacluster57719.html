Lythrum salicaria

noun
plant with purple flower spikes
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪθrəm sælɪˈkæriə / (AmE), / ˈlɪθrəm sælɪˈkeəriə / (BrE)
A marsh plant with tall spikes of purple flowers, native to Europe but widespread in eastern North America.
