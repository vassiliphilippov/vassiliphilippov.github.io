anti-tax

noun
tax opponent
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈæntiˌtæks / (AmE), / ˈæntiˌtæks / (BrE)
a person, group, or movement that opposes taxes or efforts to increase them

Examples

Anti-tax group
The anti-tax group organized a rally to protest the proposed increase in property taxes.
Many politicians gained support by aligning themselves with the anti-tax movement.
Some voters identify as anti-tax because they believe government spending is too high.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tax protester - a person who refuses to pay taxes as a form of protest
  • antonym: taxpayer - a person who pays taxes
