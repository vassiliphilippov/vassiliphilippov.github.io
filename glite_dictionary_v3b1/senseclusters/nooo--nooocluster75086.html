nooo

interjection
exaggerated protest
Pronunciation: / noʊː / (AmE), / nəʊː / (BrE)
an exaggerated way of saying 'no', used to express strong emotion such as surprise, disappointment, or protest

Examples

To shout nooo loudly
When she saw her favorite team lose in the final seconds, she shouted nooo in frustration.
He dropped his ice cream on the ground and cried out, 'Nooo! Not again!'
As the movie character made a terrible decision, the audience groaned and some whispered nooo under their breath.

Related Terms

  • synonym: no - a word used to express refusal or denial
  • antonym: yes - a word used to express agreement or affirmation
