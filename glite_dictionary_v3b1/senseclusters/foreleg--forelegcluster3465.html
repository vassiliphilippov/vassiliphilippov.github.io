foreleg

noun
front limb of a quadruped
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌlɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːlɛɡ / (BrE)
the front limb of a four-legged animal

Examples

The foreleg of an animal
A frog's foreleg is generally shorter than its hindleg.
The horse injured its foreleg during the race and had to be withdrawn.
The veterinarian examined the dog's swollen foreleg carefully.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hindleg - the back limb of a four-legged animal
