counterproliferation

noun
prevention of weapon spread
7.6
Pronunciation: /kaʊn.tər.proʊ.lɪ.fə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /kaʊn.tə.prəʊ.lɪ.fə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
actions or policies aimed at preventing the spread of weapons, especially weapons of mass destruction.

Examples

Counterproliferation efforts
The government increased funding for counterproliferation programs to address new security threats.
International cooperation is essential for effective counterproliferation of chemical and biological weapons.
Experts discussed counterproliferation strategies at the global security conference last month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonproliferation - the prevention of the increase or spread of nuclear weapons
  • antonym: proliferation - the rapid increase or spread of something, especially weapons
