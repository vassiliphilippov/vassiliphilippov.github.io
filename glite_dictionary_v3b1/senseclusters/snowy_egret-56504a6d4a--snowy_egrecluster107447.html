snowy egret

noun
small white heron native to the Americas
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊ.i ˈiː.ɡrət / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊ.i ˈiː.ɡrət / (BrE)
a small white heron (Egretta thula) of the Americas, with slender black legs and bright yellow feet, noted for its delicate plumes
