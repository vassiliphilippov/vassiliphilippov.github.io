crown saw

noun
hollow cylindrical saw for cutting holes
Pronunciation: / kraʊn sɑ / (AmE), / kraʊn sɔː / (BrE)
a saw consisting of a hollow cylinder with teeth around its edge, used for cutting holes or circles in materials

Examples

A crown saw tool
A crown saw is ideal for creating large openings for pipes and wiring.
Make sure the crown saw stays steady to avoid damaging the surrounding material.
The carpenter used a crown saw to cut a perfect circle in the wood panel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hole saw - a saw used for cutting holes, similar in form to a crown saw
