maybe

noun
uncertain answer
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪbi / (AmE), / ˈmeɪbi / (BrE)
an uncertain or undecided answer, response, or possibility

Examples

A maybe answer
We received several maybes for the invitation, so we are not sure how many will attend.
The survey results included a few maybes along with the yes and no responses.
When asked about joining the team, she gave one of her usual maybes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: certainty - something that is sure or definite
  • synonym: possibility - something that might happen or be true
