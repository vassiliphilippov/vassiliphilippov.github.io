limnology

noun
study of inland fresh waters
Pronunciation: / lɪmˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / lɪmˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
The scientific study of inland bodies of fresh water, including their biological, physical, and geological characteristics.

Examples

Limnology of lakes
Research in limnology has improved our understanding of lake pollution and its effects on wildlife.
She chose to specialize in limnology because of her fascination with freshwater ecosystems.
The university offers an advanced course in limnology for environmental science students.
