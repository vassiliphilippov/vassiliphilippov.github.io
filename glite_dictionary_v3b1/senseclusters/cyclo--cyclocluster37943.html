cyclo

ring-shaped prefix
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪkloʊ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪkləʊ / (BrE)
a combining form used in chemistry to indicate a ring-shaped (cyclic) arrangement of atoms in a molecule

Examples

Cyclo prefix in chemistry
The prefix cyclo is used in cyclohexane to show the molecule has a ring structure.
In organic chemistry, cyclo appears in names of compounds with atoms arranged in a circle.
Adding cyclo to a hydrocarbon name indicates the presence of a closed ring of carbon atoms.
