topolatry

noun
worship of places or locations
Pronunciation: / təˈpɑlətri / (AmE), / təˈpɒlətri / (BrE)
the worship or excessive reverence of particular places or locations

Examples

Topolatry of places
Modern tourism can sometimes border on topolatry when people revere famous cities or landmarks.
Some ancient cultures practiced topolatry, believing that certain mountains were sacred.
The novel explores the theme of topolatry, depicting characters obsessed with the spiritual power of a particular place.

Related Terms

  • synonym: idolatry - the worship of idols; excessive devotion to something
