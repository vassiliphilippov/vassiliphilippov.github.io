splay

noun
outward bevel around a door or window
7.9
Pronunciation: / spleɪ / (AmE), / spleɪ / (BrE)
an outward bevel or slanted surface around a door or window that gives the appearance of increased size

Examples

A window splay
Historic buildings often feature a wide splay to enhance the entrance’s visual impact.
She admired the deep splay framing the old oak door.
The stone splay around the window let in more light to the room.
