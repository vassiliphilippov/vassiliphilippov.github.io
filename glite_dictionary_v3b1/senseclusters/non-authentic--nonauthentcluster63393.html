non-authentic

adjective
not verified genuine
Pronunciation: / nɑn ɔˈθɛntɪk / (AmE), / nɒn ɔˈθɛntɪk / (BrE)
not verified as genuine or lacking official proof of identity or authorization, especially in computing or security contexts

Examples

Non-authentic login
The system rejected the non-authentic access attempt due to missing credentials.
Only authorized users can enter; all non-authentic logins are blocked automatically.
Security software detected a non-authentic certificate and warned the user.

Related Terms

  • antonym: authentic - verified as genuine or real
  • synonym: unauthenticated - not confirmed as genuine or authorized
