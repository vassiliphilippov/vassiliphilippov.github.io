burgee

noun
yacht club flag
Pronunciation: / bɜrˈdʒi / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdʒi / (BrE)
a small, triangular flag used by yachts or sailing clubs as a distinguishing symbol

Examples

A burgee flag
The yacht proudly displayed its burgee during the annual regatta.
Each sailing club designs a unique burgee to represent its members.
He collected a burgee from every club he visited on his voyage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ensign - a flag flown on a ship to indicate nationality
  • synonym: pennant - a long, narrow flag, often triangular, used for signaling or identification
