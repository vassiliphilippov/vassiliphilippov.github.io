prima donnaish

adjective
self-important and temperamental
9.9 7.6
Pronunciation: /ˈpriː.mə ˈdɑː.nə.ɪʃ/ (AmE), /ˈpriː.mə ˈdɒn.ə.ɪʃ/ (BrE)
Behaving in a self-important and temperamental way, like a prima donna; demanding special treatment or attention.

Examples

A prima donnaish attitude
His prima donnaish attitude made working with him very difficult for the team.
She became especially prima donnaish when the director criticized her performance.
The coach warned the players against acting prima donnaish during the championship.

Related Terms

  • synonym: self-important - having an exaggerated sense of one's own value or importance
  • synonym: temperamental - liable to unreasonable changes of mood; emotionally unpredictable
