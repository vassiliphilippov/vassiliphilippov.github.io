jaw clenched

adjective
jaw tightly closed from emotion
2.5
Pronunciation: / dʒɑː klɛntʃt / (AmE), / dʒɔː klɛntʃt / (BrE)
having the jaw muscles tightly closed, typically as a result of anger or stress

Examples

Her jaw clenched tightly
Her jaw clenched in frustration when the computer suddenly shut down.
She noticed his jaw clenched as he struggled to stay calm.
With jaw clenched, he forced himself through the difficult conversation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clench - to close (teeth or a fist) tightly, especially with anger or stress
