undereye

noun
cosmetic for eye area
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌaɪ / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌaɪ / (BrE)
a cosmetic product used to treat or conceal the area beneath the eye, such as a cream or concealer

Examples

Undereye cream
She applied an undereye cream every morning to reduce puffiness.
The makeup artist recommended a new undereye to help conceal dark circles.
He noticed the undereye concealer made him look more awake for the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concealer - a cosmetic used to cover blemishes or dark circles
  • synonym: eye cream - a cream applied to the skin around the eyes
