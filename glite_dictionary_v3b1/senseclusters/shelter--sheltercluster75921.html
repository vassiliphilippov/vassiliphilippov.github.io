shelter

verb
avoid paying tax on income or assets
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛltɚ / (AmE), / ˈʃɛltə / (BrE)
to invest money or arrange financial matters so as to avoid taxation on the income or assets

Examples

To shelter money
By sheltering her assets in a trust, she legally reduced her tax bill.
The corporation managed to shelter a large amount of its profits offshore.
They look for ways to shelter their earnings from high taxes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shield - to protect or defend from harm or danger; to shield incomes from taxation
