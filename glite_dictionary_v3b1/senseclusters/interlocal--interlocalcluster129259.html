interlocal

adjective
between local governments
8.1
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.ˈloʊ.kəl/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.ˈləʊ.kəl/ (BrE)
relating to or occurring between two or more local governments or communities

Examples

Interlocal cooperation
The two counties entered into an interlocal agreement to manage the shared park.
An interlocal task force was created to address regional transportation issues.
Funding for the new library came from an interlocal partnership among several towns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intermunicipal - between or involving two or more municipalities
  • antonym: intralocal - within a single local government or community
