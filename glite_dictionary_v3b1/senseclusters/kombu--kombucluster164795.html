kombu

noun
edible brown seaweed
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːm.buː/ (AmE), /ˈkɒm.buː/ (BrE)
a type of edible seaweed, especially from the kelp family, commonly used in East Asian or Japanese cooking to add flavor to dishes
