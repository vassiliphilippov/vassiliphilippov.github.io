nonveteran

adjective
not a veteran
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈvɛtərən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈvɛtərən / (BrE)
not having served in the armed forces; used to describe someone who is not a veteran.

Examples

Nonveteran students
The program is open to both nonveteran and veteran students at the university.
Special discounts are available for nonveteran family members attending the event.
The survey compared the experiences of nonveteran employees with those who had served in the military.

Related Terms

  • synonym: civilian - a person who is not a member of the armed forces
  • antonym: veteran - someone who has served in the armed forces
