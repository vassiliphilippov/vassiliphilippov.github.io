waster

noun
person who destroys or ruins
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪstər / (AmE), / ˈwɑːstə / (BrE)
a person who destroys, ruins, or lays waste to something

Examples

A waster of dreams
Jealousy can become a waster of happiness if left unchecked.
The invading army was known as a waster of towns and villages, leaving destruction in its path.
The legends called him the waster of kingdoms for his ruthless campaigns.

Related Terms

  • antonym: preserver - a person who preserves or protects
