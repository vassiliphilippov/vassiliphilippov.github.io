mismatching

adjective
not matching
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈmætʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈmætʃɪŋ / (BrE)
not matching or not fitting well together in color, style, or type

Examples

Mismatching socks
He noticed his mismatching shoes only after he left the house.
The living room was decorated with mismatching furniture from different eras.
Her mismatching earrings made her outfit look unique and playful.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clashing - not looking good together because of differences
  • antonym: matching - corresponding in color, style, or type
