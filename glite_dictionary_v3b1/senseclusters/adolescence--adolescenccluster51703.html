adolescence

noun
period between childhood and adulthood
2.8 3.2
Pronunciation: /æ.də.ˈles.əns/ /; / /æ.dəʊl.ˈes.əns/ (AmE), /æ.də.ˈles.əns/ /; / /æ.dəʊl.ˈes.əns/ (BrE)
the period in a person's life between childhood and adulthood, marked by puberty and significant physical, emotional, and social changes

Examples
