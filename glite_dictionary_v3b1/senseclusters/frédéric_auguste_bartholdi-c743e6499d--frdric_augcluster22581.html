Frédéric Auguste Bartholdi

proper noun
French sculptor, creator of the Statue of Liberty
Pronunciation: / freɪˈdɪrɪk oʊˈɡʊst bɑrˈtoʊldi / (AmE), / freɪˈdɛrɪk əʊˈɡyːst bɑːˈtɒldi / (BrE)
(1834–1904) A French sculptor renowned for designing the Statue of Liberty, now located in New York Harbor.
