stainer

noun
person who applies stain to materials
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪnər / (AmE), / ˈsteɪnə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to apply a stain or color to wood, fabric, or other materials

Examples

The stainer applies color

Our company is hiring an experienced stainer to work in the furniture workshop.
The stainer carefully applied a mahogany finish to the new table.
