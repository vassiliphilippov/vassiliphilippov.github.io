scantily clad

adjective
wearing very little clothing
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈskæn.tɪ.li klæd / (AmE), / ˈskæn.tɪ.li klæd / (BrE)
Wearing very little clothing; dressed in a way that barely covers the body.

Examples

Scantily clad people
At the party, some guests were scantily clad, drawing surprised looks from others.
Several protesters showed up scantily clad to make a statement about body positivity.
The advertisement featured a scantily clad model promoting the swimwear brand.
