multiple myeloma

noun
cancer affecting multiple sites in bone marrow
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltɪpl ˌmaɪəˈloʊmə / (AmE), / ˈmʌltɪpl ˌmaɪəˈləʊmə / (BrE)
A type of cancer that forms in plasma cells and typically develops in several places within the bone marrow at the same time.

Examples

Multiple myeloma cancer
Researchers are working to develop new drugs to improve outcomes for people with multiple myeloma.
The doctor diagnosed her with multiple myeloma after tests revealed cancer in several areas of her bone marrow.
Treatment options for multiple myeloma include chemotherapy, stem cell transplant, and targeted therapies.
