approximate

adjective
almost correct or nearly resembling
2.8
Pronunciation: / əˈprɑːksəmət /; / əˈprɑksɪmət / (AmE), / əˈprɒksɪmət / (BrE)
almost correct or exact, but not completely so; close to the actual value, amount, or description
