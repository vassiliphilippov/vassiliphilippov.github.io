hollow

adjective
having a space inside; not solid
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːloʊ / (AmE), / ˈhɒləʊ / (BrE)
having a space inside; not solid throughout

Examples

Hollow wall
He knocked on the wall and realized it was hollow inside, not solid as he had thought.
Her cheeks became noticeably hollow after several weeks of illness.
The sound echoed within the hollow tree trunk, making it seem even larger.
