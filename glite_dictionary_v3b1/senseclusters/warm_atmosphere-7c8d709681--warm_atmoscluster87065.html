warm atmosphere

noun
friendly, inviting environment
Pronunciation: / wɔrm ˈætməsˌfɪr / (AmE), / wɔːm ˈætməsfɪə / (BrE)
a friendly and welcoming environment, creating a feeling of comfort and approachability

Examples

A warm atmosphere
A warm atmosphere at work helps team members collaborate more effectively.
She decorated her living room to create a warm atmosphere for guests.
The restaurant's warm atmosphere made us feel instantly comfortable and relaxed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cold atmosphere - an environment that feels unfriendly or uninviting
  • synonym: welcoming atmosphere - an environment that is friendly and makes people feel welcome
