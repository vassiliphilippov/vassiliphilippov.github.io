evangelically

adverb
in an evangelical way
7.2
Pronunciation: /ɪ.væn.ˈdʒel.ɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /iː.væn.ˈdʒel.ɪ.kəl.iː/ (AmE), /iː.væn.ˈdʒel.ɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /ɪ.væn.ˈdʒel.ɪ.kəl.iː/ (BrE)
in a manner relating to or promoting evangelical Christianity, especially with enthusiasm or zeal

Examples

He argued evangelically for the importance of biblical teachings in daily life.
