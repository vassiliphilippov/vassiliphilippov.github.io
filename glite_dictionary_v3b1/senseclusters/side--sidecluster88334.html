side

noun
family line of descent (e.g., mother's side)
Pronunciation: / saɪd / (AmE), / saɪd / (BrE)
a particular family line of descent, especially as related to one parent or relative (e.g., mother's side, father's side)

Examples

Mother's side of the family
He takes after his grandfather on his father's side.
My blue eyes come from my mother's side of the family.
We have Italian heritage on my grandmother's side.
