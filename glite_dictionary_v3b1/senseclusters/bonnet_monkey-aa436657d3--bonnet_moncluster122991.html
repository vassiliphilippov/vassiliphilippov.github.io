bonnet monkey

noun
Indian macaque with bonnet-like tuft
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑː.nɪt ˌmʌŋ.ki / (AmE), / ˈbɒn.ɪt ˌmʌŋ.ki / (BrE)
an Indian macaque (Macaca radiata) with a distinctive bonnet-like tuft of hair on its head, native to southern India

Examples

Bonnet monkeys in India
Researchers observed a troop of bonnet monkeys displaying social grooming behavior.
The bonnet monkey is often seen foraging in groups near temples in southern India.
Unlike the rhesus macaque, the bonnet monkey has a noticeable whorl of hair on its head.
