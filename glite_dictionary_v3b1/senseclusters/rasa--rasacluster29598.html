rasa

noun
Ayurvedic body fluid
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːsə / (AmE), / ˈrɑːsə / (BrE)
in traditional Indian medicine, a primary bodily fluid or nutritive plasma believed to nourish tissues and sustain life

Examples

Ayurvedic rasa balance
In Ayurveda, a healthy balance of rasa is considered essential for overall well-being.
Practitioners assess the quality of rasa to determine a patient's nutritional status.
An imbalance in rasa is believed to affect energy and immunity in traditional Indian medicine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plasma - the liquid component of blood that carries cells and nutrients
