publication

noun
issuing printed materials as a business
Pronunciation: / ˌpʌbləˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpʌblɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the business or industry of producing and distributing printed materials, such as books, newspapers, or magazines, for sale or circulation
