bonemeal

noun
fertilizer made from ground bones
4.9 5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊnˌmiːl / (AmE), / ˈbəʊnˌmiːl / (BrE)
a fertilizer made by grinding animal bones into a powder, used to enrich soil with nutrients, especially phosphorus and calcium
