dimpling

noun
formation of indentations
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪmplɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɪmplɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or result of forming small, round indentations on a surface

Examples

Golf ball dimpling
The dimpling on the golf ball helps it fly farther.
She noticed a slight dimpling on the surface of the cake after it cooled.
Engineers studied the dimpling effect to improve the aerodynamics of the car.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pitting - the formation of small holes or depressions on a surface
  • antonym: smoothing - the process of making a surface even and without indentations
