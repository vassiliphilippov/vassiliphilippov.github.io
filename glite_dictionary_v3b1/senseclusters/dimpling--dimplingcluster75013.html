dimpling

noun
formation of indentations
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪmplɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɪmplɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or result of forming small, round indentations on a surface

Examples

Golf ball dimpling
The dimpling on the golf ball helps it fly farther.

Engineers studied the dimpling effect to improve the aerodynamics of the car.
