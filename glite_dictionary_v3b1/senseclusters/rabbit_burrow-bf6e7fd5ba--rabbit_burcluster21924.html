rabbit burrow

noun
hole or tunnel made by wild rabbits
Pronunciation: / ˈræbɪt ˌbɝːroʊ / (AmE), / ˈræbɪt ˌbʌrəʊ / (BrE)
A hole or tunnel in the ground used by wild rabbits as a nest or home.
