unfoldment

noun
gradual development
7.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfoʊldmənt / (AmE), / ʌnˈfəʊldmənt / (BrE)
the gradual process of something being revealed, developed, or coming into view

Examples

The unfoldment of story
The unfoldment of the story surprised everyone with its unexpected twists.
We watched the unfoldment of the project from its early stages to completion.
The unfoldment of her talent became clear as she gained more experience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: concealment - the act of hiding or keeping something secret
  • synonym: development - the process of growing or becoming more advanced
  • synonym: revelation - the act of making something known or visible
