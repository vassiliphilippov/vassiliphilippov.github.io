Synonyms

  • spending money
    2.0 1.14/m

  • cash in hand
    3.4 0.08/m
    cash in hand is physical cash you have right now, while money in someone's pocket can mean any disposable funds, not only cash.
  • ready cash
    2.7 0.18/m
    ready cash is cash kept for immediate use in payments, while money in someone's pocket may include bank funds and other spendable resources.
  • ready money
    3.7 0.04/m
