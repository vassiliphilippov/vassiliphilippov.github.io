perhaps

adverb
maybe; possibly
4.2
Pronunciation: / pərˈhæps / (AmE), / pəˈhaps / (BrE)
Used to express uncertainty or possibility; maybe or possibly.

Examples

Perhaps she will come
Perhaps she will join us for dinner later this evening.
He thought he had locked the door, but perhaps he had forgotten.
There will perhaps be an announcement by the end of the week.

Related Terms

  • antonym: certainly - definitely; without doubt
  • synonym: maybe - possibly; expressing uncertainty
  • synonym: possibly - by chance; maybe
