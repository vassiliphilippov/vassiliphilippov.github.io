poodle

noun
breed of dog with curly coat
Pronunciation: / ˈpuː.dl̩ /; / ˈpuːdəl / (AmE), / ˈpuː.dl̩ /; / ˈpuːdl̩ / (BrE)
a breed of dog known for its intelligence and dense, curly coat, often kept as a pet or show dog
