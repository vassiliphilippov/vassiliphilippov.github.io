Holy Eucharist

noun
Christian sacrament with bread and wine
8.1 0.12/m
Pronunciation: / Ààho äli ÀàjuÀêk…ôr…™st / (AmE), / Ààh…ô äli ÀàjuÀêk…ôr…™st / (BrE)
the Christian sacrament in which bread and wine are consecrated and consumed in remembrance of the Last Supper

Examples

The Holy Eucharist celebration
At the heart of the Mass is the celebration of the Holy Eucharist.
Children prepare for their First Holy Eucharist as an important milestone in their faith.
Many Christians receive the Holy Eucharist every Sunday during the church service.

Synonyms

  • Holy Communion
    6.3 0.62/m

  • communion
    4.9 0.55/m

  • Holy Sacrament
    9.8 0.08/m
    Holy Sacrament speaks in a reverent, general way about a sacred rite, while Holy Eucharist names the specific bread and wine ceremony.
  • Eucharistic liturgy
    9.9 0.09/m
    Eucharistic liturgy highlights the whole worship service and prayers around the rite, while Holy Eucharist names the consecrated bread and wine.
  • Lord's Supper

  • Mass

  • liturgy
