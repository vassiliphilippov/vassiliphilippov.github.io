call oneself

verb
describe or refer to oneself
Pronunciation: / kɔl wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / kɔːl wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to describe or refer to oneself in a particular way, often by using a specific name or title

Examples

To call oneself a writer
After moving to the city, she began to call herself a writer.
Despite his wealth, he calls himself an ordinary person.
He jokingly calls himself the office coffee expert.

Related Terms

  • synonym: refer to oneself as - to use a particular name or description for oneself
  • synonym: style oneself - to give oneself a particular title or name
