apply

verb
refer a word or name to someone or something
3.3
Pronunciation: / əˈplaɪ / (AmE), / əˈplaɪ / (BrE)
to use a word, term, or name in reference to a particular person or thing

Examples

To apply a name

The term 'pioneer' is often applied to early tech entrepreneurs.
