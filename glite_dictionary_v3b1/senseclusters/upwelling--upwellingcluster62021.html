upwelling

noun
sudden surge of emotion
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌwɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌwɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
a sudden and strong rise or surge of feeling or emotion within a person

Examples

Upwelling of emotion
She felt an upwelling of gratitude as her friends surprised her with a party.
An upwelling of anger made it hard for him to speak calmly.
There was an upwelling of hope in the community after the announcement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flood - an overwhelming amount or rush of something, especially emotion
  • synonym: surge - a sudden powerful forward or upward movement
