Artemisia vulgaris

noun
European aromatic herb, known as mugwort
Pronunciation: / ɑːr.təˈmɪʒ.i.ə vʌlˈɡɛr.ɪs / (AmE), / ˌɑː.tɪˈmɪz.i.ə vʌlˈɡɑː.rɪs / (BrE)
A European perennial herb with hairy red or purple stems, dark green leaves white underneath, and red-brown florets; commonly known as mugwort.
