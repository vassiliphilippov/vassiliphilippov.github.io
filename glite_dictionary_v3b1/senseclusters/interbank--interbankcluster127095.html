interbank

adjective
between banks
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈbæŋk / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈbæŋk / (BrE)
relating to transactions or activities that take place between banks

Examples

Interbank loans
The interbank market is crucial for maintaining liquidity in the financial system.
Changes in interbank interest rates can influence consumer loan rates.
Banks often rely on interbank transfers to settle large transactions quickly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intrabank - occurring within a single bank
