weatherwise

adjective
skilled at predicting weather
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛðərˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈwɛðəˌwaɪz / (BrE)
having knowledge about the weather or able to predict weather conditions accurately

Examples

A weatherwise farmer
After years of farming, she became quite weatherwise and could sense when rain was coming.
The hikers relied on their weatherwise guide to avoid storms in the mountains.
Being weatherwise helped the fisherman decide the best days to go out to sea.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ignorant - lacking knowledge or awareness
  • synonym: weather-savvy - knowledgeable about weather conditions
