Communist

noun
member of a Communist party
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmjənɪst / (AmE), / ˈkɒmjʊnɪst / (BrE)

Examples

Communist party member
During the 1950s, being labeled a Communist could damage your reputation in America.
Several prominent Communists attended the international congress.
She was a lifelong Communist who campaigned actively for workers' rights.
