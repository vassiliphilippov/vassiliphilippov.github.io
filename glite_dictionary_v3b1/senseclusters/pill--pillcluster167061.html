pill

verb
to make into tablets
Pronunciation: / pɪl / (AmE), / pɪl / (BrE)
to form a substance into pills or tablets for use as medication

Examples

To pill medicine
The pharmacist pilled the powdered medicine before giving it to the patient.
In the past, apothecaries pilled herbs to make them easier to swallow.
She learned how to pill the mixture into small, round tablets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capsule - a small container with medicine inside, taken by mouth
  • synonym: tablet - a small, solid dose of medicine to be swallowed
