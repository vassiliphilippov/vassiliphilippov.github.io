denarcotize

verb
to remove narcotic dependence
9.9
Pronunciation: / diːˈnɑːrkəˌtaɪz /; / diˈnɑrkəˌtaɪz / (AmE), / diːˈnɑːkətaɪz /; / diˈnɑːkətaɪz / (BrE)
to free a person from the effects or dependence on narcotic drugs

Examples

To denarcotize patients
The clinic developed a program to denarcotize patients struggling with opioid addiction.
Doctors worked together to denarcotize individuals who had become dependent on painkillers.
It can take several weeks to fully denarcotize someone after long-term narcotic use.
Doctors worked quickly to denarcotize the patient after the overdose.
The new medication can help denarcotize individuals who have been exposed to strong painkillers.
Emergency staff are trained to denarcotize patients showing signs of narcotic poisoning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: narcotize - to administer a narcotic drug to someone
  • synonym: rehabilitate - to help someone return to a normal, healthy life after addiction
