unnervingly

adverb
in a disturbing way
7.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈnɜrvɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈnɜːvɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that makes someone feel nervous, uneasy, or uncomfortable

Examples

An unnervingly silent manner
He stared at me in an unnervingly silent manner, making me feel uneasy.
The house was unnervingly empty when she returned late at night.
Her unnervingly calm reaction surprised everyone in the tense meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comfortingly - in a way that makes someone feel less worried or more at ease
  • synonym: disturbingly - in a way that causes worry or discomfort
