backhandedly

adverb
with a backhand stroke
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌhændɪdli / (AmE), / ˈbækˌhændɪdli / (BrE)
in a manner using a backhand stroke or the back of the hand, especially when hitting or striking.

Examples

He swatted the fly backhandedly without even looking up from his book.
