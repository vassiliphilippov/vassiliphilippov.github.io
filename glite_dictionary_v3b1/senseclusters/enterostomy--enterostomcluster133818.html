enterostomy

noun
surgical opening into the intestine
Pronunciation: / ˌɛn.təˈrɑ.stə.mi / (AmE), / ˌɛn.təˈrɒs.tə.mi / (BrE)
a surgical procedure in which a permanent opening is made through the abdominal wall into the intestine

Examples

An enterostomy surgery
After being diagnosed with a severe bowel disorder, the patient underwent an enterostomy.
Some patients may need an enterostomy to allow for long-term intestinal drainage.
The recovery process following an enterostomy involves careful monitoring and wound management.

Related Terms

  • synonym: colostomy - surgical opening from the colon to the abdominal wall
  • synonym: ileostomy - surgical opening from the ileum to the abdominal wall
