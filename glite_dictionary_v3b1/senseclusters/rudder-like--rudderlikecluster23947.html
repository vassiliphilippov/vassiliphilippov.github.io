rudder-like

adjective
resembling a rudder
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌdərˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈrʌdəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having the shape or function of a rudder; resembling a rudder in appearance or purpose.

Examples

Rudder-like fins

Its rudder-like appendage makes it uniquely adapted to its aquatic environment.
The fish has a rudder-like tail that helps it steer in the water.
