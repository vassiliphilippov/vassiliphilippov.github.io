triumphalist

adjective
excessively proud of victory
Pronunciation: / traɪˈʌmfəlɪst / (AmE), / traɪˈʌmfəlɪst / (BrE)
showing excessive pride or celebration in one's success or victory, often in a way that may offend others

Examples

Triumphalist speech
His triumphalist attitude after the match upset the losing team.
The article criticized the government's triumphalist tone following the election results.
She tried to avoid sounding triumphalist when discussing her recent achievements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boastful - showing excessive pride and self-satisfaction
  • antonym: modest - not showing excessive pride or self-importance
