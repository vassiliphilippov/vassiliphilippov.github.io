paraglider

noun
lightweight glider aircraft
4.9
Pronunciation: /pær.ə.ˈglaɪ.dər/ (AmE), /pær.ə.ˈglaɪ.də/ (BrE)
a lightweight, free-flying aircraft with no rigid structure, used for recreational flying by gliding through the air

Examples

A paraglider flight
She soared over the mountains in her paraglider.
The paraglider caught a strong updraft and climbed higher into the sky.
Many adventure seekers travel to the coast to experience flying a paraglider for the first time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glider - an aircraft that flies without an engine
  • synonym: hang glider - a non-motorized glider with a rigid frame, used for flying
