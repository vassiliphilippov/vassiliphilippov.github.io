physiographic

adjective
relating to landforms
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪziəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌfɪziəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to the physical features, landforms, and natural characteristics of the Earth's surface

Examples

Physiographic regions
The physiographic regions of the country include mountains, plains, and coastal areas.
Scientists use physiographic data to study how rivers shape the landscape over time.
A physiographic survey helps identify areas prone to flooding or erosion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geographical - relating to the natural features of an area
  • synonym: topographical - relating to the arrangement of the physical features of an area
