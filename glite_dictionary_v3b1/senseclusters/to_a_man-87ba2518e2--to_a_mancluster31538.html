to a man

adverb
everyone without exception
8.1
Pronunciation: / tə ə mæn / (AmE), / tə ə mæn / (BrE)
used to emphasize that every single person in a group is included without exception

Examples

To a man they agreed
The committee members, to a man, opposed the new policy.
The soldiers, to a man, refused to abandon their posts.
They to a man volunteered to help with the search effort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: every last one - including every individual; without exception
  • synonym: without exception - not excluding any; applying to all
