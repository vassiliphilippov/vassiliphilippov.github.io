unmirrored

adjective
not mirrored
8.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmɪrərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɪrəd / (BrE)
not duplicated or copied by mirroring, especially referring to data, disks, or systems lacking redundancy

Examples

Unmirrored disk
The server's data was unmirrored, making it vulnerable to loss in case of hardware failure.
They realized too late that the important files were stored on an unmirrored drive.
For better protection, avoid keeping critical information on unmirrored systems.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mirrored - duplicated or copied for redundancy, especially in computing
