dedicator

noun
person who dedicates
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛdɪˌkeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈdɛdɪkeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who formally dedicates something, such as a book, monument, or work of art

Examples

Dedicator of a book
The dedicator included a touching note at the beginning of the novel.
A plaque listed the name of the dedicator who funded the new library wing.
During the ceremony, the dedicator explained the inspiration behind the monument.
