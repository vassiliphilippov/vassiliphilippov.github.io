send to the showers

verb
be dismissed from a game
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɛnd tə ðə ˈʃaʊɚz / (AmE), / sɛnd tə ðə ˈʃaʊəz / (BrE)
(of a player) to dismiss someone from a game, usually because of a violation or mistake, so they must leave the field or court and cannot continue playing.

Examples

To send to the showers early
After a heated argument with the referee, he was sent to the showers.
One more foul and you’ll be sent to the showers like the others.
The coach decided to send to the showers the player who broke the rules.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disqualify - to officially prevent someone from continuing in a competition or game
  • synonym: eject - to force a player to leave a game, usually for breaking rules
