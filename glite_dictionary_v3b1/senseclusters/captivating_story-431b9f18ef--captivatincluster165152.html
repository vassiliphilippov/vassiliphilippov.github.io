captivating story

noun
an engaging, attention-grabbing narrative
Pronunciation: / ˈkæp.tɪˌveɪ.tɪŋ ˈstɔːr.i / (AmE), / ˈkæp.tɪ.veɪ.tɪŋ ˈstɔː.ri / (BrE)
a narrative that is so interesting or engaging that it holds the attention completely

Examples

A captivating story
A captivating story can transport readers to entirely new worlds.
Everyone sat in silence, listening intently to her captivating story.
The author is known for writing one captivating story after another.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dull story - a story that is uninteresting
  • synonym: gripping story - a story that holds the attention strongly
