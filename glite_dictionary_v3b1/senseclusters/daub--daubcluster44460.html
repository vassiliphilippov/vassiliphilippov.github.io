daub

noun
a dirty spot or smear
Pronunciation: / dɔb / (AmE), / dɔːb / (BrE)
a spot or smear, especially of something dirty or sticky; a blemish made by dirt

Examples

A daub of mud
She noticed a small daub of mud on the carpet after the dog came in.
The child wiped the daub of jam from his shirt sleeve.
There was a visible daub on the corner of the painting's frame.

Related Terms

  • synonym: smudge - a dirty mark or smear
  • synonym: stain - a mark left by a substance; a dirty mark
