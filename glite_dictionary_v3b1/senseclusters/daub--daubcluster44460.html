daub

noun
a dirty spot or smear
7.7 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / d…îb / (AmE), / d…îÀêb / (BrE)
a spot or smear, especially of something dirty or sticky; a blemish made by dirt

Examples

A daub of mud

The child wiped the daub of jam from his shirt sleeve.
There was a visible daub on the corner of the painting's frame.
