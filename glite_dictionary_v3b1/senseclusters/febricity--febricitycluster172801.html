febricity

noun
elevated body temperature; fever
9.0 9.9
Pronunciation: / fəˈbrɪsəti / (AmE), / fɛˈbrɪsɪti / (BrE)
A rise in the body's temperature, often as a symptom of infection; fever.

Examples

High febricity level
