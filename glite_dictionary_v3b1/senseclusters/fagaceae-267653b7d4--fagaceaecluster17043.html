Fagaceae

noun
the beech family of trees and shrubs
Pronunciation: / fəˈɡeɪsiaˌi /; / fæˈɡeɪsiˌiː / (AmE), / faˈɡeɪsɪiː /; / fæˈɡeɪsiː / (BrE)
a family of trees and shrubs that includes beeches, chestnuts, and oaks
