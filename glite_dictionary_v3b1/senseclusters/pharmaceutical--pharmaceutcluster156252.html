pharmaceutical

noun
drug or medicine for medical treatment
Pronunciation: / ˌfɑːrməˈsuːtɪkl̩ / (AmE), / ˌfɑːməˈsuːtɪkəl / (BrE)
a drug or medicine prepared or dispensed in pharmacies and used in medical treatment

Examples

A pharmaceutical drug
New pharmaceuticals are rigorously tested before being approved for public use.
Patients rely on a wide range of pharmaceuticals to manage their health conditions.
The company specializes in developing pharmaceuticals for chronic illnesses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drug - a chemical substance used to treat, cure, or prevent disease
  • synonym: medication - a drug or remedy used for medical treatment
