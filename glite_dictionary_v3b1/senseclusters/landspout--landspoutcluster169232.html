landspout

noun
weak tornado over land
9.3
Pronunciation: /ˈlænd.sp.aʊt/ (AmE), /ˈlænd.sp.aʊt/ (BrE)
a weak, narrow tornado that forms over land and resembles a waterspout in appearance and development

Examples

A landspout tornado
A landspout briefly touched down in the open field, causing little damage.
Meteorologists warned that a landspout could develop during the afternoon storm.
Unlike a strong tornado, a landspout usually lasts only a few minutes.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: tornado - a violent rotating column of air extending from a thunderstorm to the ground
  • synonym: waterspout - a rotating column of air over water, similar to a tornado
