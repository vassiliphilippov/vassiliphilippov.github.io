Okapia

noun
genus of African mammals including the okapi
Pronunciation: / oʊˈkɑːpiə / (AmE), / əʊˈkɑːpiə / (BrE)
a genus of mammals that includes the okapi, an African ungulate closely related to the giraffe

Examples

Fossil records suggest that the range of Okapia was once much wider in Africa.
