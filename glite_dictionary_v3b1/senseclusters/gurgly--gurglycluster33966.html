gurgly

adjective
bubbling sound
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɜrɡli / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːɡli / (BrE)
making or characterized by a bubbling or gurgling sound, like water flowing or liquid moving.

Examples

A gurgly stream
The gurgly stream wound its way through the forest, filling the air with a soothing sound.
He woke up to the gurgly noise of the coffee maker brewing in the kitchen.
The baby's laugh was soft and gurgly, making everyone in the room smile.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bubbly - full of bubbles or making bubbling sounds
  • degree higher: noisy - making a lot of noise
  • antonym: quiet - making little or no noise
