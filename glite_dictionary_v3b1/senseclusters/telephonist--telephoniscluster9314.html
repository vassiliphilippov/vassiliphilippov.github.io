telephonist

noun
person connecting telephone calls
1.0 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈtel.ə.fə.nɪst/ /; / /ˈtel.ɪ.fə.nɪst/ (AmE), /ˈtel.ə.fə.nɪst/ /; / /ˈtel.ɪ.fə.nɪst/ (BrE)
