abominable

adjective
extremely detestable or unpleasant
Pronunciation: / əˈbɑm.ə.nə.bəl /; / əˈbɑmɪnəbəl / (AmE), / əˈbɒm.ɪ.nə.bəl /; / əˈbɒmɪnəbl̩ / (BrE)
extremely bad, unpleasant, or morally offensive; deserving strong dislike or condemnation
