right

noun
baseball outfield area to catcher's right
3.4
Pronunciation: / raɪt / (AmE), / raɪt / (BrE)
In baseball, the area of the outfield to the catcher's right, known as right field.

Examples

Right field
She played an excellent game in right, catching several difficult fly balls.
The batter hit a towering fly ball to right in the seventh inning.
The manager switched him from left field to right during the season.
