Anschluss

noun
union of Austria and Germany
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːnʃlʊs / (AmE), / ˈanʃlʊs / (BrE)
the political union of Austria with Germany in 1938, especially referring to its annexation by Nazi Germany

Examples

The 1938 Anschluss
The Anschluss was a turning point in the events leading up to World War II.
Many historians debate the international response to the Anschluss in 1938.
After the Anschluss, Austria became part of Nazi Germany until the end of the war.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annexation - the act of adding a territory to another country
  • antonym: separation - the act of becoming independent or apart
