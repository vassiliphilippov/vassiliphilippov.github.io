button

noun
small, rounded plant part (bud, etc.)
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtən / (AmE), / ˈbʌt(ə)n /; / ˈbʌt.ən / (BrE)
a part of a plant or any object that looks like a button in shape or appearance

Examples

A mushroom button
Before blooming, each mushroom forms a tight button at the soil's surface.
She gently touched the unopened button of the rose, waiting for it to flower.
The daisy's bright yellow button stood out against the green leaves.
The child pressed the plastic button on the toy to make it light up.
The flowers had small, round buttons at the center of each bloom.
The mushroom had a white button shape at the top.
