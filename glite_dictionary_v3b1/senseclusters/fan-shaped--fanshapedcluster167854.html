fan-shaped

adjective
shaped like a fan
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfæn ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈfæn ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having the shape of a fan, typically spreading out from a central point in a curved form.

Examples

A fan-shaped pattern
A fan-shaped arrangement of rocks marked the start of the trail.
The architect designed a fan-shaped auditorium to improve visibility for the audience.
The leaves of the ginkgo tree are fan-shaped and turn yellow in autumn.
