20/20

noun
normal vision measurement
Pronunciation: / twɛnti ˈtwɛnti / (AmE), / ˌtwɛnti ˈtwɛnti / (BrE)
Having normal visual acuity, as determined by the ability to read at 20 feet what should normally be seen at that distance.

Examples

20/20 vision
After the eye exam, the doctor confirmed that I have 20/20 vision.
Many people believe 20/20 means perfect eyesight, but it actually means normal sharpness of vision.
She was relieved to hear that her son had 20/20 on both eyes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perfect vision - the state of having no sight defects
  • antonym: poor vision - having less than normal ability to see clearly
