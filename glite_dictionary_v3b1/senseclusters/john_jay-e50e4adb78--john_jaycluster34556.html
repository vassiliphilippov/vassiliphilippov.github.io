John Jay

proper noun
first Chief Justice of the U.S. Supreme Court
6.0
Pronunciation: /dʒɑːn dʒeɪ/ (AmE), /dʒɒn dʒeɪ/ (BrE)
(1745–1829) American diplomat, jurist, and statesman who helped negotiate peace with Britain and served as the first Chief Justice of the United States Supreme Court.
