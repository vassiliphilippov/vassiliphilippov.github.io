smart

adjective
able to act independently or intelligently (technology)
4.4 17/m
Pronunciation: / sm…ërt / (AmE), / sm…ëÀêt / (BrE)
(of a device or system) capable of independent and apparently intelligent action, typically using computers or other technology to perform tasks automatically or efficiently.

Examples

Smart phone
Many households now have smart thermostats that can learn your preferences and adjust the temperature automatically.
She bought a smart washing machine that detects the fabric type and adjusts the cycle accordingly.
The military is developing smart missiles that can track moving targets without human intervention.
