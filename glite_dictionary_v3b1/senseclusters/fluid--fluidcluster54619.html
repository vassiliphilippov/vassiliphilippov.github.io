fluid

adjective
smooth and graceful in movement
Pronunciation: / ˈfluː.ɪd / (AmE), / ˈfluː.ɪd / (BrE)
moving smoothly and continuously, without being stiff or awkward; graceful in movement or style.

Examples

Fluid movement
He admired how fluid the horse's gait appeared on the track.
Her fluid dance moves captivated everyone in the audience.
The pianist's hands were fluid as they glided across the keys.

Related Terms

  • antonym: awkward - lacking grace or smoothness in movement
  • synonym: graceful - having or showing grace or elegance in movement
