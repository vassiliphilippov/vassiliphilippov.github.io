burnside

noun
strip of facial hair by the ears
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜrn.saɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɜːn.saɪd / (BrE)
A strip of facial hair grown down the side of a man's face in front of the ears, especially when the chin and rest of the beard are shaved.

Examples

Burnsides facial hair
He decided to grow distinguished burnsides for the upcoming play.
In Victorian times, burnsides were a popular fashion among men.
The old photograph showed a gentleman with impressive burnsides and a clean-shaven chin.
