burnside

noun
strip of facial hair by the ears
9.9 0.41/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…úrn.sa…™d / (AmE), / Ààb…úÀên.sa…™d / (BrE)
A strip of facial hair grown down the side of a man's face in front of the ears, especially when the chin and rest of the beard are shaved.

Examples

Burnsides facial hair
He decided to grow distinguished burnsides for the upcoming play.
In Victorian times, burnsides were a popular fashion among men.
The old photograph showed a gentleman with impressive burnsides and a clean-shaven chin.

Synonyms

  • sideburn
    4.3 1.09/m
    sideburn is the common, neutral word for the strip of hair at the face sides, while burnside is a less common variant name.
  • side-whiskers
    7.9 0.02/m
    side-whiskers is a descriptive hyphenated term for facial hair down the face sides, while burnside is the variant name in this entry.
  • chops
    3.7 0.04/m
    chops is an informal word for hair on the cheeks, often meaning fuller styles, while burnside names a narrow strip beside the ears.
