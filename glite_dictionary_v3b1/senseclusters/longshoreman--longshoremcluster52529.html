longshoreman

noun
port laborer loading/unloading ships
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔŋˌʃɔrmən / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋˌʃɔːmən / (BrE)
a person employed at a port to load and unload cargo from ships

Examples

A longshoreman loads cargo
After working as a longshoreman for twenty years, he knew every dock by heart.
Her grandfather was a longshoreman at the busy harbor in New York City.
The union representing the longshoremen called for a strike over working conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stevedore - person who loads and unloads ships, especially in British English
