ragtop

adjective
convertible roofed
Pronunciation: / ˈræɡˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈræɡˌtɒp / (BrE)
having a soft, foldable roof, especially referring to a convertible car with such a roof

Examples

Ragtop convertible
The classic car show featured a beautiful ragtop Mustang from the 1960s.
He drove his ragtop down the coast with the roof folded back to enjoy the sun.
Many people prefer a ragtop for summer drives because of the open-air experience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: convertible - a car with a roof that can be folded down or removed
  • antonym: hardtop - a car with a fixed, rigid roof
