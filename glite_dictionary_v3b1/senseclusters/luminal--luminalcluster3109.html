luminal

adjective
of a tube's interior
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈluːmɪnəl / (AmE), / ˈluːmɪnəl / (BrE)
relating to the lumen, the inner space of a tubular structure in the body (such as a blood vessel or intestine)

Examples

Luminal surface
The surgeon carefully inspected the luminal surface of the colon for any abnormalities.
A blockage in the luminal area of the artery can restrict blood flow.
Researchers measured the luminal diameter to assess the effectiveness of the treatment.
The luminal surface of the intestine is lined with specialized cells.
Doctors examined the luminal narrowing in the patient's artery using advanced imaging techniques.
Certain medications are designed to act directly on the luminal side of the stomach.

Related Terms

  • antonym: external - relating to the outside or outer surface
  • synonym: lumen - the inside space of a tubular structure
