urbanologist

noun
city development expert
Pronunciation: / ˌɜrbəˈnɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌɜːbəˈnɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies cities and urban development

Examples

An urbanologist studies cities
The urbanologist gave a lecture on the challenges of rapid urbanization.
As an urbanologist, she analyzes how cities grow and change over time.
Many urbanologists work with governments to improve city infrastructure.
The urbanologist presented new ideas for improving city transportation.
As an urbanologist, she researches how cities can become more sustainable and livable.
A team of urbanologists analyzed the effects of rapid population growth on urban infrastructure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sociologist - a person who studies society and social behavior
  • synonym: urban planner - a person who designs and plans city layouts
