upvalley

adjective
in upper valley
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌvæli / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌvæli / (BrE)
located in or relating to the upper part of a valley, closer to its source than its mouth

Examples

Upvalley cabin
They bought an upvalley cabin to enjoy the quiet mountain scenery.
The upvalley vineyards produce some of the region's best wines.
Heavy snow often blocks the upvalley roads in winter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: downvalley - located in or relating to the lower part of a valley, nearer its mouth
