ramp

verb
spread/grow by creeping or climbing (plants)
Pronunciation: / ræmp / (AmE), / ræmp / (BrE)
(of a plant) to grow or spread over or along something by creeping or climbing.

Examples

Vines ramp over fences
Bougainvillea often ramps over fences and trellises in tropical regions.
The ivy began to ramp across the old stone wall by summer.
Wisteria was ramping through the abandoned garden, its vines wrapping everywhere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: climb - (of plants) to grow upward by gripping or twining
  • synonym: trail - (of plants) to grow along the ground or over a surface
