wittiness

noun
clever and humorous quality
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪt̬i.nəs /; / ˈwɪti.nəs / (AmE), / ˈwɪt.i.nəs /; / ˈwɪtɪnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being clever and funny, especially in a quick and inventive way with words
