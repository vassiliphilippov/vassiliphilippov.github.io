provenance

noun
origin or source of something
7.9 2.20/m
Pronunciation: / Ààpr…ëÀê.v…ô.n…ôns / (AmE), / Ààpr…ív.…ô.n…ôns / (BrE)
the place of origin or earliest known history of something; the source from which something comes or is derived

Examples

The provenance of art
Knowing the provenance of a painting can greatly increase its value at auction.
The archaeologist carefully traced the provenance of the ancient artifact to its original excavation site.
The museum displayed documents confirming the provenance of each piece in its collection.
