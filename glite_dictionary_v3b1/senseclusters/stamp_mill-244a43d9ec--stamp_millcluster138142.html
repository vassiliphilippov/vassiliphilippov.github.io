stamp mill

noun
machine for crushing ore with heavy stamps
7.4
Pronunciation: / stæmp mɪl / (AmE), / stæmp mɪl / (BrE)
a machine in which ore is crushed by heavy iron stamps for further processing or extraction of metals

Examples

A stamp mill machine
Early prospectors relied on the stamp mill to extract gold from ore.
Restoring a historic stamp mill can be an expensive but rewarding project.
The old gold mine still has a functioning stamp mill used for demonstrations.
