crape myrtle

noun
ornamental flowering shrub or tree
8.9 7.0
Pronunciation: /kreɪp ˈmɜːr.təl/ (AmE), /kreɪp ˈmɜː.təl/ (BrE)
An ornamental shrub or small tree (genus Lagerstroemia), native to Asia (including eastern India) and widely grown in warm climates such as the southern United States, known for its showy clusters of colorful summer flowers and attractive bark.
