first baseman

noun
baseball player at first base
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˌfɝst ˈbeɪsmən / (AmE), / ˌfɜːst ˈbeɪsmən / (BrE)
(Baseball) a player who occupies the position of first base and is responsible for fielding balls hit near first base.

Examples

The first baseman catches
As a talented first baseman, she had quick reflexes and a strong arm.
During the game, the first baseman made several impressive plays near the base.
The first baseman caught the throw and got the runner out.
