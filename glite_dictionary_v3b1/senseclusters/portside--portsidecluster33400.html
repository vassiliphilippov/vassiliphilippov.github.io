portside

noun
left side of vessel
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔrtˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɔːtsaɪd / (BrE)
the left side of a ship or aircraft when facing forward

Examples

The portside of the ship
The crew gathered on the portside to watch the sunset over the ocean.
Please move the cargo to the portside to balance the ship.
From the portside windows, passengers could see the approaching coastline.

Related Terms

  • antonym: starboard - the right side of a ship or aircraft when facing forward
