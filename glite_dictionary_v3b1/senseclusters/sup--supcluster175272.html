sup

noun
stand-up paddleboard (abbreviation)
Pronunciation: / sʌp / (AmE), / sʌp / (BrE)
informal abbreviation for a stand-up paddleboard: a wide, buoyant board used for paddling while standing on the water.

Examples

A sup board
We rented a sup for a calm afternoon on the lake.
She carried her sup across the sand and launched it into the small bay.
During summer, many beginners try inflatable sups while taking lessons at the pier.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paddleboard - a board used for paddling while standing or kneeling on the water
