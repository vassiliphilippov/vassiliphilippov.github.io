Hesiod

proper noun
ancient Greek poet (8th century BC)
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhiː.sɪ.əd/ /; / /ˈhe.sɪ.əd/ (AmE), /ˈhiː.sɪ.əd/ /; / /ˈhe.sɪ.əd/ (BrE)
(c. 8th century BC) An ancient Greek poet whose surviving works, including 'Theogony' and 'Works and Days,' are key sources for Greek mythology and early Greek rural life.
