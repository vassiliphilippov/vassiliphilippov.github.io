mala

noun
Australian marsupial
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːlə / (AmE), / ˈmɑːlə / (BrE)
a small Australian marsupial, also known as the rufous hare-wallaby, native to arid regions of Australia

Examples

A mala marsupial
The mala is endangered due to habitat loss and introduced predators in Australia.
Researchers are working to reintroduce the mala to its former range in the wild.
Children at the wildlife sanctuary learned about the unique features of the mala.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kangaroo - a large Australian marsupial with powerful hind legs for jumping
  • synonym: wallaby - a small or medium-sized Australian marsupial similar to a kangaroo
