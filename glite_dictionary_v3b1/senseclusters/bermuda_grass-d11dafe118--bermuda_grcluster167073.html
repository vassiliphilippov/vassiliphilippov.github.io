Bermuda grass

noun
creeping lawn and pasture grass
9.9 3.2
Pronunciation: /bər.ˈmjuː.də græs/ /; / /bər.ˈmjuː.də grɑːs/ (AmE), /bə.ˈmjuː.də grɑːs/ /; / /bɜː.ˈmjuː.də grɑːs/ (BrE)
a creeping grass native to southern Europe and widely grown in warm regions for lawns and pastures, especially in the southern United States and India

Examples
