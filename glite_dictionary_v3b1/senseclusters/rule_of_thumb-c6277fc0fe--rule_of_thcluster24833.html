rule of thumb

noun
general, practical guideline
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈruːl əv θʌm / (AmE), / ˈruːl əv θʌm / (BrE)
a general principle or guideline based on practical experience rather than theory or scientific accuracy

Examples

A rule of thumb guideline
A good rule of thumb is to add one cup of water for every cup of rice.
As a rule of thumb, you should save at least 10% of your income every month.
When choosing hiking boots, follow the rule of thumb that they should fit snugly but not tightly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guideline - a general rule or piece of advice
  • synonym: principle - a fundamental truth or rule
