breastpin

noun
ornamental pin for women's clothing
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛstˌpɪn / (AmE), / ˈbrɛstˌpɪn / (BrE)
A decorative pin, often ornamental, that is fastened to the front of a woman's clothing.

Examples

A breastpin on dress
He admired the intricate design of the gold breastpin in the museum display.
She wore a sparkling breastpin on her dress at the gala.
The antique breastpin belonged to her grandmother and was passed down through generations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brooch - an ornament fastened to clothing with a hinged pin and catch
