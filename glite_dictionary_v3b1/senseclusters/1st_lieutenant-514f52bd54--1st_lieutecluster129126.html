1st lieutenant

noun
military rank above 2nd lieutenant, below captain
5.8
Pronunciation: /fɜːrst luː.ˈten.ənt/ (AmE), /fɜːst lef.ˈten.ənt/ (BrE)
a commissioned officer in the Army, Air Force, or Marines who ranks above a second lieutenant and below a captain

Examples

1st lieutenant rank

He served as a 1st lieutenant during his deployment overseas.
The 1st lieutenant coordinated the training exercises for the new recruits.
