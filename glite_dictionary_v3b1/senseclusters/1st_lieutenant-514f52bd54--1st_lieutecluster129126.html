1st lieutenant

noun
military rank above 2nd lieutenant, below captain
Pronunciation: / fɜrst luːˈtɛnənt / (AmE), / fɜːst lɛfˈtɛnənt / (BrE)
a commissioned officer in the Army, Air Force, or Marines who ranks above a second lieutenant and below a captain

Examples

1st lieutenant rank
After years of dedicated service, Sarah was promoted to 1st lieutenant in the Air Force.
He served as a 1st lieutenant during his deployment overseas.
The 1st lieutenant coordinated the training exercises for the new recruits.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: captain - an officer rank above 1st lieutenant
  • degree lower: second lieutenant - a junior commissioned officer rank below 1st lieutenant
