fitty

noun
minor argument
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪti / (AmE), / ˈfɪti / (BrE)
a minor argument or outburst, especially about something unimportant

Examples

A small fitty
They had a little fitty over who would sit in the front seat.
After a brief fitty, the friends quickly made up and laughed about it.
She threw a fitty when her favorite mug was missing from the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: fight - a violent or serious argument or struggle
  • synonym: row - a noisy argument, especially between people who know each other well
  • synonym: tiff - a small or petty quarrel
