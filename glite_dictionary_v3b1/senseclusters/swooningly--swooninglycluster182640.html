swooningly

adverb
with overwhelming emotion
Pronunciation: / ˈswuːnɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈswuːnɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows faintness or overwhelming emotion, often from admiration or love

Examples

Look swooningly at someone
She looked swooningly at the actor as he walked onto the stage.
The fans cheered swooningly when the band started playing their favorite song.
He spoke swooningly about his first trip to Paris, recalling every detail with emotion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adoringly - in a way that shows deep love or admiration
  • antonym: indifferently - in a way that shows no interest or emotion
