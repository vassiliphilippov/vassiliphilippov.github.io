Senora

noun
Spanish title for a married woman
7.7
Pronunciation: / seɪˈnɔːrə / (AmE), / seˈnɔːrə / (BrE)
a Spanish title or form of address for a married woman; equivalent to 'Mrs.' or 'madam' in English, used when speaking to or about Spanish-speaking women

Examples

Senora Lopez
Please give this package to Senora Lopez at the front desk.
She remembered meeting a kind Senora while traveling in Mexico.
The waiter greeted the Senora as she entered the restaurant.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Señorita - Spanish title for an unmarried woman
