loblolly

noun
southern pine tree
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːbˌlɑːli / (AmE), / ˈlɒbˌlɒli / (BrE)
a type of pine tree native to the southeastern United States, known for its tall, straight trunk and long needles

Examples

Loblolly pine tree
The loblolly is one of the most common pines in the southeastern United States.
We walked through a dense forest of loblolly trees on our hike.
Many birds build their nests high in the branches of the loblolly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: oak - a large tree with lobed leaves and acorns
  • synonym: pine - an evergreen tree with needle-shaped leaves
