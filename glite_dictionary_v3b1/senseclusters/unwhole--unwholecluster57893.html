unwhole

adjective
sick or unhealthy
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhoʊl / (AmE), / ʌnˈhəʊl / (BrE)
sick, unhealthy, or not in a sound physical condition; not whole (archaic or literary)

Examples

Unwhole body
After the long illness, he still felt unwhole and weak.
The poet described his spirit as unwhole after the loss of his friend.
In the old tale, the hero returned from battle unwhole in both body and mind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ill - not in good health; sick
  • synonym: unwell - not feeling healthy; slightly sick
  • antonym: whole - in good health; not broken or damaged
