hard drug

noun
potent, highly addictive illegal drug
Pronunciation: / hɑrd drʌɡ / (AmE), / hɑːd drʌɡ / (BrE)
a powerful and highly addictive illegal drug, such as heroin or cocaine

Examples

Addiction to hard drugs
Many rehabilitation centers specialize in treating addictions to hard drugs.
She warned her son about the dangers of experimenting with hard drugs.
The government has strict laws against possession of hard drugs like heroin and cocaine.

Related Terms

  • antonym: soft drug - a drug that is less likely to cause addiction or harm, such as cannabis
