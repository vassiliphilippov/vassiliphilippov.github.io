schnecken

noun
sweet rolled pastry with nuts and sugar
Pronunciation: / ˈʃnɛkən / (AmE), / ˈʃnɛkən / (BrE)
A type of sweet baked good made from rolled dough spread with sugar and nuts, then sliced and baked, often with honey or sugar and butter at the bottom.

Examples

A schnecken pastry
My grandmother baked a tray of delicious schnecken for the holiday breakfast.
The recipe called for chopped walnuts and cinnamon inside the schnecken.
You can find traditional schnecken in many Jewish bakeries in New York City.
