chon

noun
former monetary subunit of the South Korean won
9.9
Pronunciation: / tʃʌn / (AmE), / tʃɒn / (BrE)
A former monetary subunit of the South Korean won, equal to one hundredth of a won.

Examples

Old South Korean chon
Coins denominated in chon became rare after South Korea stopped minting them.

The vendor priced goods in both won and chon during the 20th century.
