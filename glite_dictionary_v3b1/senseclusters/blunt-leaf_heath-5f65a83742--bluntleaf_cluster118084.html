blunt-leaf heath

noun
Australian shrub with fragrant ivory flowers
Pronunciation: / blʌnt liːf hiːθ / (AmE), / blʌnt liːf hiːθ / (BrE)
a small upright shrub native to Australia and Tasmania, noted for its fragrant ivory-colored flowers
