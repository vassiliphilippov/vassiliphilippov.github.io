westerner

noun
person from the West
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛstərnər / (AmE), / ˈwɛstənə / (BrE)
a person from the western part of the world, especially Europe or North America

Examples

A European westerner
As a westerner, she found the traditional ceremonies both unfamiliar and fascinating.
The guide explained the differences between local customs and those of a typical westerner.
Many westerners travel to Asia to experience new cultures and perspectives.

Related Terms

  • antonym: easterner - a person from the eastern part of the world
