horsy

noun
toy rocking horse
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrsi / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsi / (BrE)
a child's toy horse, especially one that rocks back and forth like a rocking horse.

Examples

Rocking horsy
The little boy spent hours riding his favorite horsy in the playroom.
She asked for a new horsy for her birthday because her old one was broken.
In the corner of the nursery stood a wooden horsy with a painted red saddle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rocking horse - a toy horse mounted on rockers for children to ride
  • synonym: stuffed animal - a soft toy animal filled with stuffing
