uncircumcised

adjective
lacking refinement
9.9
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈsɜːr.kəm.saɪzd/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈsɜː.kəm.saɪzd/ (BrE)
(archaic or figurative) lacking refinement, culture, or moral or spiritual sensitivity; regarded as rude or uncivilized.

Examples

Uncircumcised people
In older literature, the term uncircumcised was sometimes used to describe those seen as lacking culture.
The speaker referred to the invaders as uncircumcised to emphasize their perceived lack of refinement.
Some critics found the artist's style too uncircumcised for the sophisticated audience.
