unofficially

adverb
without official approval
1.0 3.6
Pronunciation: /ə.nə.ˈfɪʃ.ə.liː/ /; / /ʌ.nə.ˈfɪʃ.ə.liː/ (AmE), /ʌ.nə.ˈfɪʃ.ə.liː/ /; / /ə.nə.ˈfɪʃ.ə.liː/ (BrE)

Examples

To act unofficially
Unofficially, she runs the office despite her junior title.
