civically

adverb
in a citizen-related way
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪvɪkli / (AmE), / ˈsɪvɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to the duties, rights, or activities of citizens or a community

Examples

To be civically engaged
He is civically engaged and volunteers for local community projects.
The students were encouraged to participate civically by voting in the school election.
Being civically responsible means staying informed about local issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: politically - in a way that relates to government or public affairs
  • synonym: socially - in a way that relates to society or its organization
