impossibly

adverb
to an impossible degree
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɑsəbli / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɒsəbli / (BrE)
To an extent or degree that cannot be achieved or believed; in an impossible way.

Examples

Look impossibly elegant
She looked impossibly elegant in her simple black dress.
The mountain peak seemed impossibly high from the valley below.
They had to move impossibly fast to finish the project on time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: possibly - in a way that can happen or be done
