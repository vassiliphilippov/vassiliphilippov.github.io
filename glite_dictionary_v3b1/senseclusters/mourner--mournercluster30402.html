mourner

noun
person grieving, especially over a death
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrnər / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnə / (BrE)
a person who expresses or feels grief, especially over someone's death

Examples

Grieving mourners at funeral
Dozens of mourners gathered to pay their last respects to the beloved teacher.
Each mourner left a flower at the memorial site.
The mourner stood silently at the grave, lost in thought.

Related Terms

  • antonym: celebrant - a person taking part in a celebration, especially religious; sometimes contrasted with mourner at funerals
  • synonym: griever - a person who grieves or mourns
