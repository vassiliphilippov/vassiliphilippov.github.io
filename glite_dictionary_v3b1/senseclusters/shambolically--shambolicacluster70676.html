shambolically

adverb
in a disorganized or chaotic way
Pronunciation: / ʃæmˈbɑlɪkli / (AmE), / ʃamˈbɒlɪkli / (BrE)
In a disorganized, chaotic, or disorderly manner.

Examples

Packed shambolically messy
She packed her luggage shambolically, tossing clothes in at random.
The event began shambolically, with people unsure where to go or what to do.
The meeting was run so shambolically that no decisions were reached.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chaotically - in a very disordered or confused way
  • antonym: orderly - in a neat, organized, or systematic way
