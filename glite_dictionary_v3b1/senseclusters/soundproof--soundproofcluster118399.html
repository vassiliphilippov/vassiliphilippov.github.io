soundproof

adjective
impervious to sound
1.0 3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊndˌpruːf / (AmE), / ˈsaʊnd.pruːf / (BrE)
(of a room, building, or material) not allowing sound to pass through or out; impervious to sound.

Examples

Soundproof walls
The hotel rooms are soundproof, so guests can sleep peacefully even in the city center.
They installed soundproof windows to reduce the noise from traffic outside.
We recorded the podcast in a soundproof studio to eliminate background noise.
