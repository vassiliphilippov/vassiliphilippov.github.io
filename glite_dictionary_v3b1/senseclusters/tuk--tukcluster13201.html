tuk

interjection
knocking sound
Pronunciation: / tʌk / (AmE), / tʌk / (BrE)
an exclamation used to imitate or represent a short, sharp knocking or tapping sound

Examples

Tuk! in the hallway
Suddenly, a loud tuk echoed through the empty hallway, making everyone stop and listen.
He knocked on the door with a quick tuk before entering the room.
The stone hit the window with a sharp tuk, startling the people inside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tap - a light, quick sound made by a gentle touch
