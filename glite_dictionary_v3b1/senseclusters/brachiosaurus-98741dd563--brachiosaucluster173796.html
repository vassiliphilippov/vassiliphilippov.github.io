Brachiosaurus

proper noun
Jurassic dinosaur
Pronunciation: / ˌbrækiəˈsɔrəs / (AmE), / ˌbrækiəˈsɔːrəs / (BrE)
a large, long-necked herbivorous dinosaur that lived during the Jurassic period

Examples

A towering Brachiosaurus
The towering Brachiosaurus could reach leaves high up in the trees with its long neck.
Scientists believe that Brachiosaurus lived in what is now North America and Africa.
Children at the museum were amazed by the life-sized model of a Brachiosaurus.
