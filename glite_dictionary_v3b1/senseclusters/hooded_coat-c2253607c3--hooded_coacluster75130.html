hooded coat

noun
long overcoat with a hood
5.5 1.4
Pronunciation: /ˈhʊd.ɪd koʊt/ /; / /ˈhʊd.əd koʊt/ (AmE), /ˈhʊd.ɪd kəʊt/ /; / /ˈhʊd.əd kəʊt/ (BrE)
a long overcoat with an attached hood that can be pulled over the head for protection from weather

Examples

A hooded coat in winter
During the snowstorm, she wore her thick hooded coat to stay warm.
He hung his wet hooded coat by the door to dry after walking home in the rain.
Many hikers prefer a hooded coat for added protection against wind and cold.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duffel coat - a thick woolen coat with a hood, often fastened with toggle buttons
  • synonym: parka - a warm jacket with a hood, often lined with fur or faux fur
