Indian mongoose

noun
small mammal native to southern Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən ˈmɑŋˌgus / (AmE), / ˈɪndɪən ˈmɒŋˌɡuːs / (BrE)
a small, keen-sighted mammal native to southern Asia, about the size of a ferret, often domesticated to control pests

Examples

The Indian mongoose animal
In some islands, the introduction of the Indian mongoose led to ecological problems.
The Indian mongoose is often kept on farms to control rats and snakes.
The alert eyes of the Indian mongoose spotted movement in the grass.
