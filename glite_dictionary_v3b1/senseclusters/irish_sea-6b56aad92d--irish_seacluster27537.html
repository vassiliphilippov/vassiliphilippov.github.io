Irish Sea

proper noun
sea between Great Britain and Ireland
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ.rɪʃ si / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.rɪʃ siː / (BrE)
the sea that lies between the islands of Great Britain and Ireland, forming part of the North Atlantic Ocean

Examples

The Irish Sea
Ferry services on the Irish Sea are often affected by rough conditions in winter.
The Irish Sea is known for its unpredictable weather and strong currents.
We sailed across the Irish Sea from Liverpool to Dublin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: North Sea - a sea to the east of Great Britain, between Great Britain, Scandinavia, Germany, the Netherlands, Belgium, and France
