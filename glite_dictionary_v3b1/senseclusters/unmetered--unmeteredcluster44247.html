unmetered

adjective
not measured or limited
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmiːtərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmiːtəd / (BrE)
not measured, controlled, or limited by a meter or set amount

Examples

Unmetered internet access
The hotel offers unmetered water usage to all its guests.
With unmetered electricity, they never worried about turning on extra lights.
Many internet providers now advertise unmetered data plans for streaming and downloads.

Related Terms

  • antonym: metered - measured or controlled by a meter
  • synonym: unlimited - not restricted in amount or number
