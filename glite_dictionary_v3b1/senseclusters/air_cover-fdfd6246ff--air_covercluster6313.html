air cover

noun
protection by military aircraft during operations
1.9
Pronunciation: / ɛr ˈkʌvɚ / (AmE), / ɛə ˈkʌvə / (BrE)
the use of military aircraft to protect ground or naval forces from enemy attack during operations

Examples

Air cover protection
The ground troops advanced under heavy air cover to avoid enemy attacks.
The navy requested air cover as they entered hostile waters.
Without adequate air cover, the operation could have ended in disaster.

Related Terms

  • synonym: air support - military support provided by aircraft
