floozy

noun
sexually promiscuous woman; sometimes prostitute
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfluːzi / (AmE), / ˈfluːzi / (BrE)
a woman regarded as sexually promiscuous, especially a prostitute or someone with a flamboyant or provocative manner

Examples

A floozy woman
He used to sneer at anyone he considered a floozy, showing his narrow-minded attitude.
Some people in the town unfairly called her a floozy because of her bold fashion choices.
The old detective novel described a shady bar filled with gamblers and floosies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lady - a polite or respectful way to refer to a woman
  • synonym: prostitute - a person, typically a woman, who engages in sexual activity for payment
