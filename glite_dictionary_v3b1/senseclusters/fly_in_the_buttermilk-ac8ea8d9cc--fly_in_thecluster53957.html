fly in the buttermilk

noun
minor flaw that spoils a good situation
8.9
Pronunciation: / flaɪ ɪn ðə ˈbʌt̬ɚˌmɪlk / (AmE), / flaɪ ɪn ðə ˈbʌtəˌmɪlk / (BrE)
