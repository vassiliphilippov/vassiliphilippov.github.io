reverse

noun
back side of a coin or medal
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɝːs / (AmE), / rɪˈvɜːs / (BrE)
the side of a coin or medal that does not bear the principal design, often the back side

Examples

Reverse of a coin
A national emblem appeared on the reverse of the medal.
Collectors carefully examined the reverse for rare mint marks.
The date was inscribed on the reverse of the coin.

Related Terms

  • antonym: obverse - the main or principal side of a coin or medal
