tabbouleh

noun
Middle Eastern salad with bulgur and herbs
7.9 6.1
Pronunciation: /tə.ˈbuː.leɪ/ /; / /tə.ˈbuː.liː/ (AmE), /tə.ˈbuː.leɪ/ /; / /tə.ˈbuː.liː/ (BrE)
A salad made of finely chopped parsley, tomatoes, mint, scallions, and bulgur wheat, typically seasoned with olive oil and lemon juice and common in Middle Eastern cuisine.

Examples

A bowl of tabbouleh
At the picnic, he brought a bowl of homemade tabbouleh to share.
Her tabbouleh recipe uses extra parsley and a splash of lemon juice.
We ordered tabbouleh and hummus as appetizers at the Lebanese restaurant.
I learned how to prepare tabooli from my grandmother.
She served a bowl of tabooli alongside grilled chicken at the picnic.
The restaurant's tabooli was fresh and full of flavor.
