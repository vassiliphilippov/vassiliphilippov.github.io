unsuppressed

adjective
not restrained or held back
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.səˈprɛst / (AmE), / ˌʌn.səˈprɛst / (BrE)
(of a feeling, action, or response) not held back, restrained, or kept in check; expressed openly.

Examples

Unsuppressed laughter
His unsuppressed laughter filled the quiet room, startling everyone present.
She felt a surge of unsuppressed joy when she saw her old friend again.
The speech was fueled by unsuppressed anger over the recent decision.
