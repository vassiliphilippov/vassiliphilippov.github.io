immortal

noun
a god or supernatural being
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɔrtl / (AmE), / ɪˈmɔːtl / (BrE)
a supernatural being, such as a god or goddess, who is believed to live forever and often has power over part of the world or nature

Examples

A mythic immortal
Each immortal in the myth had a unique power linked to nature.
In ancient Greece, the immortals often meddled in the affairs of mortals.
Legends say the immortal watched over the mountain for centuries.
