poinsettia

noun
decorative tropical plant, often red-leaved
Pronunciation: / pɔɪnˈsɛtiə / (AmE), / pɔɪnˈsɛtɪə / (BrE)
A tropical American plant with showy, usually red petal-like leaves surrounding small yellow flowers, commonly used as a decorative plant during the Christmas season.

Examples

A poinsettia plant
Many families place a poinsettia on their table during Christmas celebrations.
She decorated her living room with a large poinsettia for the holidays.
The bright red leaves of the poinsettia are often mistaken for flower petals.
