green leafy vegetable

noun
vegetable with edible green leaves
Pronunciation: / ɡrin ˈlifi ˈvɛdʒtəbl̩ / (AmE), / ɡriːn ˈliːfi ˈvɛdʒtəbl / (BrE)
a vegetable with edible green leaves, such as spinach, kale, or lettuce, often valued for its nutritional content

Examples

Spinach is a popular green leafy vegetable often used in salads and smoothies.
