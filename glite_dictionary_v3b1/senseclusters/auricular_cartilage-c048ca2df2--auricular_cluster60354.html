auricular cartilage

noun
cartilage in the outer ear
7.2
Pronunciation: / ɔːrˈɪkjələr ˈkɑːrtɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɔːˈrɪkjələ ˈkɑːtɪlɪdʒ / (BrE)

Examples
