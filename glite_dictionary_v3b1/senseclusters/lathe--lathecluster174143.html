lathe

verb
to shape with a lathe
7.8
Pronunciation: / leɪð / (AmE), / leɪð / (BrE)
to shape or cut material by rotating it on a lathe machine

Examples

To lathe wood
The artisan lathed a set of wooden candlesticks for the market.
Each metal rod was carefully lathed to achieve a precise diameter.
She lathes bowls from blocks of wood in her workshop.
The craftsman will lathe the block of wood into a decorative table leg.
She learned how to lathe metal parts precisely in her engineering class.
After hours of careful work, he had lathed a perfect cylinder from the rough material.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carve - to shape material by cutting, usually by hand
  • synonym: turn - to shape material by rotating it on a machine
