gouvernante

noun
female private tutor
9.9
Pronunciation: / guːvɛrˈnɑːnt / (AmE), / guːvɛrˈnɑːnt / (BrE)
a woman employed in a private household to educate and care for children

Examples

Children's gouvernante
The family decided to hire a gouvernante to oversee their children's education.
As a gouvernante, she was responsible for both teaching and supervising the children.
The gouvernante lived with the family and became a trusted member of the household.

Related Terms

  • synonym: governess - a woman employed to teach and care for children in a private home
  • synonym: nanny - a person, usually a woman, employed to care for children in a household
