Elamite

noun
extinct ancient language of the Elamites
Pronunciation: / ˈɛləˌmaɪt / (AmE), / ˈiːləˌmaɪt / (BrE)
an extinct ancient language once spoken by the Elamites in what is now southwestern Iran; its linguistic affiliations are not well understood
