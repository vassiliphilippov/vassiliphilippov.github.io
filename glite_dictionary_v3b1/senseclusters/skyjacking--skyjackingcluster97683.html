skyjacking

noun
aircraft hijacking
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈskaɪ.dʒæk.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈskaɪ.dʒæk.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of illegally taking control of an aircraft in flight by force or threat
