zoom

noun
a rapid upward movement
5.6
Pronunciation: / zuːm / (AmE), / zuːm / (BrE)
A rapid rise or upward movement, especially into the air.

Examples

Jet zoom upward
During the airshow, the jet performed an impressive zoom maneuver.
The pilot executed a steep zoom shortly after takeoff.
With a sudden zoom, the rocket shot into the sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: climb - the act of moving upward, especially in aviation
  • antonym: dive - a steep downward movement in flight
