skeletonize

verb
to strip to essentials
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈskɛlətəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈskɛlɪtənaɪz / (BrE)
to remove all but the essential parts, leaving only a basic structure or skeleton

Examples

To skeletonize a leaf
Certain caterpillars can skeletonize leaves, leaving only the veins behind.
The artist chose to skeletonize the design, focusing on its most basic shapes.
Over time, the wind and rain will skeletonize the old barn, exposing its wooden frame.
