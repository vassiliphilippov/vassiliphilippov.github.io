clockwise rotation

noun
rotation in the direction of a clock's hands
Pronunciation: / ˈklɑːkˌwaɪz roʊˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈklɒkˌwaɪz rəʊˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a rotation or movement proceeding in the same direction as the hands of a clock, typically to the right when viewed from above

Examples

A clockwise rotation
Meteorologists observed a clockwise rotation in the movement of the storm system.
The dance involves a rapid clockwise rotation on one foot.
Twist the lid with a gentle clockwise rotation to tighten it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: counterclockwise rotation - rotation in the opposite direction to a clock's hands, to the left
  • degree higher: rotation - the action of rotating around an axis or center
