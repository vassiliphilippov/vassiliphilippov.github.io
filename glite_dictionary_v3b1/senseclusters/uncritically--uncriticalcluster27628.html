uncritically

adverb
without critical judgment
4.7 5.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkrɪtɪkli / (AmE), / ʌnˈkrɪtɪkəli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of critical judgment or analysis.

Examples

To read uncritically
If you read that article uncritically, you might miss the subtle biases in its argument.
Many people tend to follow trends uncritically because it feels easier than asking questions.
She accepted the newspaper's report uncritically, without checking other sources.
