Stegocephalia

noun
extinct group of prehistoric amphibians
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌstɛɡoʊsɪˈfeɪliə / (AmE), / ˌstiːɡəʊsɪˈfɛːlɪə / (BrE)
an extinct group of large amphibians, mainly from before the Jurassic period, formerly classified as a division including early salamandriform amphibians

Examples

Fossil Stegocephalia
Many fossils of Stegocephalia have been discovered in early Carboniferous rock formations.
Some paleontologists use the term Stegocephalia to group primitive amphibians with heavily armored skulls.
The classification of Stegocephalia has changed as paleontologists learn more about amphibian evolution.
