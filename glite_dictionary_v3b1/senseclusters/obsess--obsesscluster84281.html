Examples

To obsess over problems
He let worries about his career obsess him day and night.
If you obsess over small details, you'll never finish the project.
Memories of the incident continued to obsess her for years.

Synonyms

  • preoccupy
    6.3 6/m
    preoccupy absorbs someone's thoughts and keeps them distracted from other things, while obsess makes them unable to stop thinking and feel troubled.
  • fixate
    7.0 2/m
    fixate focuses attention very strongly, often on one detail or person, while obsess causes repeated, troubling thoughts that you cannot stop.
  • brood over
    6.7 0.3/m
    brood over means quietly thinking and worrying about a problem for a long time, while obsess means intrusive, overwhelming thoughts that feel alarming.
