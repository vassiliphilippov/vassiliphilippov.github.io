turbolift

noun
futuristic elevator
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜːrboʊlɪft / (AmE), / ˈtɜːbəlɪft / (BrE)
a high-speed elevator used in spaceships or futuristic buildings, especially in science fiction

Examples

The turbolift ride
The crew entered the turbolift to reach the command deck quickly.
In the futuristic city, every skyscraper had a turbolift for rapid vertical travel.
She pressed the button and the turbolift whisked her to the observation lounge in seconds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elevator - a platform or compartment for raising and lowering people or goods between floors
  • synonym: lift - a British term for an elevator
