abrazo

noun
dance partner embrace
9.2
Pronunciation: / əˈbrɑːzoʊ / (AmE), / əˈbrɑːzəʊ / (BrE)
the close embrace or hold between partners in dances such as the Argentine tango

Examples

Tango abrazo
The dancers entered the abrazo before beginning their passionate tango performance.
A comfortable abrazo is essential for good connection in Argentine tango.
She adjusted her abrazo to better follow her partner's lead on the dance floor.
