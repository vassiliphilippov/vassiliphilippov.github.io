bandi

noun
South Asian jacket
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːndi / (AmE), / ˈbændi / (BrE)
a traditional South Asian jacket or tunic, typically sleeveless and worn by men over a kurta

Examples

A bandi jacket
He wore a silk bandi over his kurta for the festival.
The groom chose a richly embroidered bandi for the wedding ceremony.
During the celebration, many men dressed in a colorful bandi and traditional attire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kurta - a loose collarless shirt worn in South Asia
  • synonym: waistcoat - a sleeveless upper-body garment worn over a shirt
