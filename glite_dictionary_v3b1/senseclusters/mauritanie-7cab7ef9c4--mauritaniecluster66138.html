Mauritanie

proper noun
French name for Mauritania (African country)
Pronunciation: / moʊˌriːtəˈniː / (AmE), / məʊˌriːtəˈniː / (BrE)
The French name for Mauritania, a country in northwestern Africa bordering the Atlantic Ocean, mostly covered by the Sahara Desert.

Examples

Mauritanie country in Africa
After gaining independence, Mauritanie established Arabic and French as its official languages.
French maps often refer to the country as Mauritanie instead of Mauritania.
The capital of Mauritanie is Nouakchott, located near the Atlantic coast.
