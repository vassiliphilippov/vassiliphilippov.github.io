popularity contest

noun
competition based on popular support
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑː.pjəˈlær.ə.t̬i ˈkɑn.tɛst /; / ˌpɑː.pjəˈlɛr.ə.ti ˌkɑn.tɛst / (AmE), / ˌpɒp.jʊˈlæ.rɪ.ti ˈkɒn.tɛst /; / ˌpɒ.pjʊˈlær.ə.ti ˌkɒn.tɛst / (BrE)
a competition or situation where success depends on being liked or popular, rather than on merit or qualifications
