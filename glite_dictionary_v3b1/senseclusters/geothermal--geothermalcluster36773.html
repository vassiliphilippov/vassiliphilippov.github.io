geothermal

adjective
relating to Earth's internal heat
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒioʊˈθɜrməl / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːəʊˈθɜːməl / (BrE)
Relating to or produced by the internal heat of the Earth.

Examples

Geothermal energy
Iceland benefits greatly from its abundant geothermal resources for heating and electricity.
Many geothermal plants are located near volcanic regions due to the high underground heat there.
The country is investing in geothermal energy as a sustainable power source.
