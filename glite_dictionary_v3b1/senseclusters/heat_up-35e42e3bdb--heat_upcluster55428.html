Synonyms

  • heat
    1.0 23/m
    heat means to raise the temperature of something in a neutral or formal way, while heat up is a phrasal, more conversational verb.
  • warm up
    1.6 2.76/m
    warm up means to make something a bit warmer, often gently for food or people, while heat up implies stronger heating.
  • warm
    1.0 25/m
    warm means to make or become slightly warmer and often suggests a gentle change, while heat up means make hot quickly.
