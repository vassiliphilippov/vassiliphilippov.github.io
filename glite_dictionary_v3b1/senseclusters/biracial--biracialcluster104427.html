biracial

adjective
involving two racial groups
Pronunciation: / baɪˈreɪʃəl / (AmE), / baɪˈreɪʃəl / (BrE)
Consisting of, representing, or involving members of two different racial groups.

Examples

Biracial family
As a biracial child, he learned to navigate multiple cultural traditions.
The company appointed a biracial leadership team to promote diversity.
The school launched a biracial program to encourage cultural understanding.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: monoracial - consisting of only one race or racial group
  • degree higher: multiracial - involving or consisting of people from several different races
