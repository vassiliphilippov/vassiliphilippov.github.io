mean

adjective
typical or average value (statistical)
5.1 21/m
Pronunciation: / miÀên / (AmE), / miÀên / (BrE)
having a value that is the mathematical average or typical amount for a set of numbers, especially in statistics.

Examples

Mean value
In statistics, the mean is often used to represent a typical value in a data set.
The mean annual temperature has increased over the last decade due to climate change.
The mean weight of the participants was calculated for the study.

Synonyms

  • arithmetic mean
    3.0 0.15/m
    arithmetic mean is the standard mathematical term for adding numbers and dividing by their count, while mean names that average.
  • first moment
    7.2 0.11/m
    first moment is a technical statistics term for the sum divided by sample size, while mean is the usual word for the average.
  • mean value
    3.4 0.78/m
    mean value is a formal math phrase for an average found by a specific formula, while mean is the common statistical term.
  • average
    1.3 68/m
    average is a general, everyday word for a typical number or level, while mean is the precise statistical average.
