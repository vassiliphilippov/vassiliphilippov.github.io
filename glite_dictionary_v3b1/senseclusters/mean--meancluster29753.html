mean

adjective
typical or average value (statistical)
5.1 18/m
Pronunciation: / miÀên / (AmE), / miÀên / (BrE)
having a value that is the mathematical average or typical amount for a set of numbers, especially in statistics.

Examples

Mean value
In statistics, the mean is often used to represent a typical value in a data set.
The mean annual temperature has increased over the last decade due to climate change.
The mean weight of the participants was calculated for the study.
