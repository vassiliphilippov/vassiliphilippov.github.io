bacon-fed

adjective
plump or content from good living
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪkən fɛd / (AmE), / ˈbeɪkən fɛd / (BrE)
Having a well-fed, hearty, or contented appearance, as if nourished with plenty of rich food; often suggesting prosperity or enjoyment of good living.

Examples

A bacon-fed look
He had a bacon-fed look, cheerful and rosy-cheeked, after months in the countryside.
In the old paintings, the nobles are often depicted as bacon-fed and prosperous.
The children appeared bacon-fed after a summer at their grandmother's farm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: well-fed - having plenty of good food; not hungry or thin
