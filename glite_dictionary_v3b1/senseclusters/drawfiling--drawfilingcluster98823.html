drawfiling

noun
metal smoothing method
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɔˌfaɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdrɔːˌfaɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
a method of smoothing metal by moving a file lengthwise along its surface to achieve a fine finish

Examples

Drawfiling metal surface
The machinist used drawfiling to create a smooth, even surface on the steel rod.
After rough filing, he finished the piece by carefully drawfiling it along its length.
Proper drawfiling can remove small imperfections and leave the metal with a polished look.

Related Terms

  • synonym: filing - the process of shaping or smoothing a material with a file
  • synonym: grinding - removing material from a surface using an abrasive tool
