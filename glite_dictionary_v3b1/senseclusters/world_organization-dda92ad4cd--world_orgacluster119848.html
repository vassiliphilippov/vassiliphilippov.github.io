world organization

noun
international alliance of many countries
Pronunciation: / wɜrld ˌɔrɡənəˈzeɪʃən /; / wɝːld ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / wɜːld ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
an international group or alliance of countries formed to promote cooperation or address global issues
