gynecoid

noun
female-type pelvis
Pronunciation: / ˈɡaɪnɪˌkɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪnɪˌkɔɪd / (BrE)
a human pelvis with a rounded shape typical of females, considered most favorable for vaginal birth

Examples

A gynecoid pelvis
The X-ray revealed that she had a gynecoid pelvis, which is ideal for childbirth.
Obstetricians often note the presence of a gynecoid pelvis during prenatal assessments.
A gynecoid pelvis is generally associated with fewer complications during natural delivery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: android - a human pelvis with a heart-shaped structure typical of males
