cosmos

noun
a genus of colorful annual flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑz.moʊs / (AmE), / ˈkɒz.mɒs / (BrE)
a genus of flowering plants native to Mexico and Central America, known for their brightly colored, daisy-like blooms and pinnate leaves, often grown as ornamental annuals

Examples

Cosmos flowers bloom
Many gardeners plant cosmos because they attract butterflies and require little maintenance.
The cosmos in her garden bloomed in vibrant pink and orange all autumn long.
We admired the tall stems and delicate leaves of the cosmos swaying gently in the breeze.

Related Terms

  • synonym: marigold - a plant with bright yellow or orange flowers, commonly cultivated as an annual ornamental
