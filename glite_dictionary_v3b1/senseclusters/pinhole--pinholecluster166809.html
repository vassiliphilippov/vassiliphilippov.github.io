pinhole

noun
a very small hole
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈpɪn.həʊl / (BrE)
a very small hole, especially one made by a pin or similar object

Examples

A pinhole camera
A camera obscura works by allowing light to pass through a pinhole onto a surface.
She noticed a pinhole leak in the garden hose, causing a fine spray of water.
The fabric lets in light through a small pinhole near the seam.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aperture - an opening, hole, or gap
