March 2

proper noun
Texas Independence Day (March 2)
1.0
Pronunciation: / mɑrtʃ ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / mɑːtʃ ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
In Texas, the date commemorating the anniversary of the state's declaration of independence from Mexico in 1836.

Examples

March 2 holiday
Every year, Texans hold parades and festivals on March 2 to mark their independence.
Schools across Texas often fly the Lone Star Flag on March 2 to honor this historic day.
The ceremony commemorating the Texas Declaration of Independence took place on March 2 in Washington-on-the-Brazos.
