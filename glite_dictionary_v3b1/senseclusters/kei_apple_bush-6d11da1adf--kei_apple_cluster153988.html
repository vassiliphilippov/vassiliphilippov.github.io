kei apple bush

noun
South African spiny shrub with edible yellow fruit
6.4
Pronunciation: / keɪ ˈæpəl bʊʃ / (AmE), / keɪ ˈæpəl bʊʃ / (BrE)
a vigorous spiny shrub native to South Africa, cultivated for its round yellow juicy edible fruits
