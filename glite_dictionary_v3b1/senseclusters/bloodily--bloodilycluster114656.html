bloodily

adverb
involving much bloodshed or violence
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌd.ɪ.li / (AmE), / ˈblʌd.ɪ.li / (BrE)

Examples

To fight bloodily
History records that many revolutions ended bloodily and left societies deeply scarred.
The rebellion was bloodily suppressed by government forces.
The two gangs fought bloodily over control of the territory.
