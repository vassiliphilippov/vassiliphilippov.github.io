ticketing

noun
issuing penalty tickets
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪkɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɪkɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of issuing tickets, especially as penalties for parking or traffic violations

Examples

Parking ticketing
The city increased ticketing in busy areas to reduce illegal parking.
Strict ticketing policies have led to fewer traffic violations downtown.
After complaints from residents, ticketing for double parking became more frequent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fining - the act of imposing a monetary penalty for an offense
  • antonym: warning - an official notice given instead of a penalty
