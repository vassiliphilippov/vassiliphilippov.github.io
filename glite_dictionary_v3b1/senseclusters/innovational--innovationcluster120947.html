innovational

adjective
characterized by innovation; new and original
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnəˈveɪʃənl̩ / (AmE), / ˌɪnəˈveɪʃənəl / (BrE)
Introducing or resulting from new ideas, methods, or inventions; characterized by innovation.

Examples

An innovational idea
Her innovational spirit was evident in every project she led.
The awards recognize innovational achievements in science and technology each year.
The company developed an innovational approach to renewable energy that changed the industry.
