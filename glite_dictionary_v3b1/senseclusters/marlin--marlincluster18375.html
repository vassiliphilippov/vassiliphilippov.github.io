marlin

noun
large oceanic sport fish
2.0 5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrlɪn / (AmE), / ˈmɑːlɪn / (BrE)
A large, fast oceanic fish with an elongated upper jaw, popular in sport fishing and related to sailfishes and spearfishes.

Examples

A marlin fish
Marlins are known for their impressive speed and acrobatic jumps when hooked.
She read a book about a legendary struggle between a man and a marlin in the Gulf Stream.
The fisherman proudly displayed the massive marlin he caught off the coast of Hawaii.
