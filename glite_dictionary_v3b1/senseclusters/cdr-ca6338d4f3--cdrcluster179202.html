CDR

noun
design review meeting
Pronunciation: / si di ɑr / (AmE), / siː diː ɑː / (BrE)
a formal engineering meeting where a proposed design is evaluated for readiness before production or implementation

Examples

CDR meeting review
The engineering team presented their findings at the CDR to demonstrate the project's readiness.
Passing the CDR is a major milestone before moving to the production phase.
The spacecraft design was thoroughly examined during the CDR to ensure all requirements were met.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: PDR - Preliminary Design Review, an earlier stage design evaluation meeting
