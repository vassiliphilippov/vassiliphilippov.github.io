Synonyms

    4.2 0.03/m
    raggy looks like torn cloth or has rag-like edges, while raggedy suggests a scruffy, uneven, or old look.
  • tattered
    4.1 0.38/m
    tattered means badly torn and worn from long use, while raggedy is more about uneven, messy or shabby appearance.
  • tatty
    6.0 0.15/m

  • ratty
