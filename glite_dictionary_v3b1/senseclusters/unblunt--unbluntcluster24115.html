unblunt

verb
to make sharp again
9.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈblʌnt / (AmE), / ʌnˈblʌnt / (BrE)
to make something sharp again or to remove its dullness

Examples

To unblunt a blade
The chef tried to unblunt his favorite knife by carefully grinding the edge.
After years of use, the gardener needed to unblunt her pruning shears for clean cuts.
Special tools are required to unblunt old scissors and restore their sharpness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blunt - to make something less sharp or dull
  • synonym: sharpen - to make something sharp or sharper
