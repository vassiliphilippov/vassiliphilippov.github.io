top

verb
strike upper part of a ball (sports)
7.8 7.6
Pronunciation: / tɑp / (AmE), / tɒp / (BrE)
to strike the upper part of a ball, especially in golf, baseball, or pool, causing it to spin forward

Examples

To top the ball
He accidentally topped the baseball, resulting in a weak grounder toward shortstop.
If you top the ball in golf, it will rarely travel far.
When you top a shot in pool, the cue ball rolls forward after hitting the object ball.
