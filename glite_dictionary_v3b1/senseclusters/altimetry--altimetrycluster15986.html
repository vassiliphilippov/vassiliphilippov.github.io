altimetry

noun
measuring altitude
Pronunciation: / ælˈtɪmətri / (AmE), / ælˈtɪmətri / (BrE)
the science or technique of measuring altitude or height above sea level

Examples

Altimetry science
Modern satellites use altimetry to monitor changes in ocean surface height.
Accurate altimetry is essential for mapping mountainous regions and valleys.
Advances in altimetry have improved our understanding of global sea level rise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barometry - the measurement of atmospheric pressure
  • synonym: topography - the study of the surface features of land
