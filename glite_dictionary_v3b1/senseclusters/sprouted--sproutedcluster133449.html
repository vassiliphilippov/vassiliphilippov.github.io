sprouted

adjective
just emerged from the ground (plants)
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈspraʊtɪd / (AmE), / ˈspraʊtɪd / (BrE)
(of a plant or seed) having just begun to grow and emerged above the ground.

Examples

Green sprouted plants
After a few days of rain, green grass sprouted across the field.
She bought a bag of sprouted wheat to use in her bread recipe.
The beans have sprouted and are ready to be planted outdoors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: germinated - (of a seed) begun to grow or develop, especially by sprouting
