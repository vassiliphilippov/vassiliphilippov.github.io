boogaloo

noun
Latin dance music; second event
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːɡəˌluː / (AmE), / ˈbuːɡəˌluː / (BrE)
a style of dance music combining Latin rhythms with soul and R&B, or informally, a term for a second event or uprising
