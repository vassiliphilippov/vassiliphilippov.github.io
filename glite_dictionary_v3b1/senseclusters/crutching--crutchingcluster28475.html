crutching

noun
overreliance on support
8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈkrʌtʃ.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈkrʌtʃ.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of relying on something or someone for support, often in a way that limits independence or growth

Examples

Crutching on others
His constant crutching on his friends made it hard for him to solve problems alone.
Therapists encourage patients to avoid crutching on unhealthy habits during stressful times.
The team's crutching on outdated methods slowed their progress on the project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dependence - the state of relying on someone or something for support
  • antonym: self-reliance - the ability to depend on oneself
