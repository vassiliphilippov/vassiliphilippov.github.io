inframarginal

adjective
below a margin
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnfrəˈmɑːrdʒɪnəl / (AmE), / ˌɪnfrəˈmɑːdʒɪnəl / (BrE)
located below a margin or threshold, especially in technical or economic contexts

Examples

An inframarginal unit
In economics, an inframarginal unit is produced at a cost lower than the marginal cost.
The study focused on inframarginal changes that occur before reaching the threshold value.
Some policies only affect inframarginal participants, not those at the margin.

Related Terms

  • antonym: marginal - relating to a margin or at the edge
