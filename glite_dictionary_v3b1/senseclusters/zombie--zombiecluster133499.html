zombie

noun
a voodoo deity or spirit
Pronunciation: / ˈzɑːm.bi / (AmE), / ˈzɒm.bi / (BrE)
A deity or spirit in voodoo cults of African origin, especially as worshipped in the West Indies.

Examples

Zombie spirit worship
Anthropologists have documented rituals dedicated to the zombie in remote parts of the West Indies.
In certain communities, a zombie is invoked during important ceremonies for spiritual guidance.
The myths describe a zombie as a powerful deity that protects the village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loa - a spirit or deity in Haitian voodoo
  • degree lower: zombie - a reanimated corpse in popular culture or folklore
