endovascular

adjective
inside blood vessels
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɛndoʊˈvæskjələr / (AmE), / ˌɛndəʊˈvæskjʊlə / (BrE)
located in or relating to procedures performed inside a blood vessel (especially in medicine)
