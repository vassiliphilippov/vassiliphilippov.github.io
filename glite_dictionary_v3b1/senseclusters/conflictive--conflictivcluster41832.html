conflictive

adjective
causing conflict
Pronunciation: / kənˈflɪktɪv / (AmE), / kənˈflɪktɪv / (BrE)
causing or likely to cause disagreement, conflict, or tension between people or groups

Examples

Conflictive attitudes
The negotiations broke down due to the conflictive attitudes of both parties.
A conflictive environment at work can lower productivity and morale.
His conflictive comments made the meeting much more tense than necessary.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contentious - causing or likely to cause an argument; controversial
  • antonym: peaceful - free from conflict or disturbance
