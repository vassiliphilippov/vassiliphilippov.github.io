apneic

adjective
relating to apnea (breathing stop)
Pronunciation: / ˈæpniɪk /; / æpˈniːɪk / (AmE), / ˈapniːɪk /; / apˈniːɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or affected by apnea, a temporary cessation of breathing, especially during sleep.

Examples

Apneic episode
Infants born prematurely are at higher risk of apneic episodes.
Prompt intervention is required when a person becomes apneic after surgery.
The nurse closely monitored the apneic patient during the night.
An apnoeic patient may require emergency intervention if breathing does not resume rapidly.
Doctors monitored her apnoeic episodes during sleep using special equipment.
The child became apnoeic for several seconds after the seizure.
