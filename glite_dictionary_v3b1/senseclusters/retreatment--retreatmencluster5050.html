retreatment

noun
repeat medical treatment
5.9
Pronunciation: / riˈtritmənt / (AmE), / riˈtriːtmənt / (BrE)
the act of giving a treatment again, especially after the first attempt was unsuccessful

Examples

Medical retreatment
After the infection returned, the doctor recommended retreatment with a different antibiotic.
The dentist scheduled a retreatment of the root canal to address the persistent pain.
Some patients may need retreatment if the initial therapy does not fully resolve the problem.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: treatment - medical care given to a patient for an illness or injury
