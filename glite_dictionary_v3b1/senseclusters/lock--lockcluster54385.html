lock

noun
wrestling hold applying pressure or twisting
3.4
Pronunciation: / lɑk / (AmE), / lɒk / (BrE)
a wrestling hold in which a part of the opponent's body is twisted or pressured, often to restrain movement or force submission

Examples

Arm lock

She expertly transitioned into a head lock during the match.
The wrestler applied a tight lock on his opponent's arm, making escape impossible.
