shoot 'em up

noun
action genre focused on shooting
9.6
Pronunciation: / ʃuːt ɛm ʌp / (AmE), / ʃuːt ɛm ʌp / (BrE)
a genre of action film or video game involving a lot of shooting, typically featuring continual combat against enemies

Examples

A shoot 'em up game
He spent the evening playing his favorite shoot 'em up on the old arcade machine.
Many classic video games from the 1980s are considered shoot 'em ups.
The movie is a nonstop shoot 'em up packed with explosions and gunfights.
