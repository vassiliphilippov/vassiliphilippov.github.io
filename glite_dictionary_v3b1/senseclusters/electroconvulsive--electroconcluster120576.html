electroconvulsive

adjective
using electric convulsions
7.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊkənˈvʌlsɪv / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊkənˈvʌlsɪv / (BrE)
relating to the use of electric currents to cause convulsions, especially as a medical treatment for certain mental illnesses

Examples

Electroconvulsive therapy
The hospital offers electroconvulsive therapy for patients with severe depression.
Doctors sometimes recommend electroconvulsive treatment when other therapies have failed.
She learned about the history of electroconvulsive procedures in her psychology class.
