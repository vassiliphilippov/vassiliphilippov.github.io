screw augur

noun
a North American Spiranthes orchid with spiraling white flower clusters
9.9
Pronunciation: / skru ˈɔːɡər / (AmE), / skruː ˈɔːɡə / (BrE)
An orchid of the genus Spiranthes, characterized by tall, erect stems with densely flowered spiraling clusters of creamy white, vanilla-scented flowers, commonly found in damp areas of eastern and central North America.
