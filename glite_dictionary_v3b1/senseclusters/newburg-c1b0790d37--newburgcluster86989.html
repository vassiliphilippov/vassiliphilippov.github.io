Newburg

noun
rich cream sauce
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːbɜːrɡ / (AmE), / ˈnjuːbɜːɡ / (BrE)
a rich cream sauce, often flavored with sherry, typically served with seafood or chicken dishes

Examples

Newburg sauce on shrimp
The chef poured a generous amount of Newburg over the shrimp for a luxurious appetizer.
For the holiday meal, she made chicken Newburg with a touch of sherry in the sauce.
Many seafood restaurants offer lobster Newburg as a classic menu option.
