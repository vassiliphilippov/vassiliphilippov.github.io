D region

noun
lowest ionospheric region reflecting low-frequency waves
9.5
Pronunciation: /diː ˈriː.dʒən/ (AmE), /diː ˈriː.dʒən/ (BrE)
the lowest region of the ionosphere, extending from about 35 to 50 miles above Earth, which reflects low-frequency radio waves

Examples

The D region ionosphere

Within the ionosphere, the D region is especially important for long-distance communications.
