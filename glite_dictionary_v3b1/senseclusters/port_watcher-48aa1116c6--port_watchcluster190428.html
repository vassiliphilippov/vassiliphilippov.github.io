port watcher

noun
person who watches over a port or wharf
7.7
Pronunciation: / pɔrt ˈwɑtʃər / (AmE), / pɔːt ˈwɒtʃə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to guard or monitor activity on a wharf or in a port area

Examples

A port watcher on duty
As a port watcher, her main responsibility was to report any suspicious activity on the wharf.
Every large harbor employs at least one port watcher to maintain security.
The port watcher checked the shipments arriving at the dock throughout the night.
