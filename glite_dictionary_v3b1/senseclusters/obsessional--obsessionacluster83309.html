obsessional

adjective
relating to obsession
Pronunciation: / əbˈsɛʃənəl / (AmE), / əbˈsɛʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by an obsession; involving persistent, intrusive thoughts or urges.

Examples

Obsessional thoughts
His obsessional attention to detail often frustrated his coworkers.
She developed an obsessional fear of germs after reading several health articles.
The artist's obsessional focus on perfection delayed the completion of the project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obsessive - having or showing an unhealthy preoccupation with something
