picklock

noun
lock-opening tool or person
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkˌlɑk / (AmE), / ˈpɪkˌlɒk / (BrE)
a tool used to open locks without a key, or a person who uses such a tool to open locks illegally

Police found a picklock hidden in the suspect's backpack during the search.
In the novel, the clever detective disguised himself as a picklock to catch the criminals.
