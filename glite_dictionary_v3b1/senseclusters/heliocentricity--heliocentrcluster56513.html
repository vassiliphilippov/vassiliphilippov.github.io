heliocentricity

noun
sun-centered model
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːliəʊsɛnˈtrɪsəti /; / ˌhiːli.oʊsɛnˈtrɪsəti / (AmE), / ˌhiːliəʊsɛnˈtrɪsəti /; / ˌhiːli.əʊsɛnˈtrɪsəti / (BrE)
the idea or theory in astronomy that the Sun is at the center of the solar system, with planets orbiting around it
