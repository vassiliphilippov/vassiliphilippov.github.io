babby

noun
term of endearment
Pronunciation: / ˈbæbi / (AmE), / ˈbæbi / (BrE)
an informal term of endearment used to address a romantic partner or very close friend

Examples

Hey, babby
She smiled and called him babby when he brought home flowers after work.
Hey babby, do you want to watch a movie together tonight?
He texted his babby to say he would be late for dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baby - an affectionate term for a loved one
  • synonym: darling - a person who is very dear or loved
