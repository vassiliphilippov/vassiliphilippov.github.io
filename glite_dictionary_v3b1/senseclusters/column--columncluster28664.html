column

noun
vertical division of data or numbers
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑləm / (AmE), / ˈkɒləm / (BrE)
a vertical section or division of text or data on a page, chart, or table

Examples

A column of numbers
Each column in the spreadsheet represents a different month.
Please arrange the data into a column for easier comparison.
The accountant checked the last column for the total amounts.
Newspaper articles are often formatted in narrow columns for easier reading.
She scanned the columns of numbers and quickly found the error.
The financial report was laid out in three neat columns on each page.

Related Terms

  • antonym: row - a horizontal line of cells or data in a table
