tai chi chuan

noun
Chinese system of meditative exercise
Pronunciation: / taɪ tʃi tʃwɑn / (AmE), / taɪ tʃi tʃwæn / (BrE)
A Chinese system of slow, meditative physical exercise designed to promote relaxation, balance, and health.

Examples

Tai chi chuan class
He enrolled in a tai chi chuan class to help reduce his stress and increase flexibility.
Many people practice tai chi chuan in the park each morning to improve their wellbeing.
The slow, graceful movements of tai chi chuan promote both physical and mental balance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tai chi - A simplified term for tai chi chuan, especially in English usage
