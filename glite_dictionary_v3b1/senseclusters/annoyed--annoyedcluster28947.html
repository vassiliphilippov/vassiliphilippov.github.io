Synonyms

  • peeved
    6.7 0.49/m
    peeved is small, petty irritation often over little things, while annoyed covers a wider range of slight anger.
  • miffed
    7.1 0.40/m
    miffed is a quiet, offended feeling when someone is rude or unfair, while annoyed covers general irritation.
  • bothered
    1.6 0.00/m
    bothered shows worry, discomfort, or mild irritation about a situation, while annoyed focuses mainly on slight anger.
  • nettled
    7.4 0.02/m
    nettled feels more sharply irritated or made angry by an insult, while annoyed is a milder, common feeling.
  • vexed
    6.5 0.12/m
    vexed expresses irritation often from repeated problems or worries in a formal tone, while annoyed is everyday mild anger.
  • riled
    7.1 0.03/m
    riled means more strongly upset or provoked and often shows anger, while annoyed is a lighter, less angry feeling.
