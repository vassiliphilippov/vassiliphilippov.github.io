annoyed

adjective
feeling irritated or slightly angry
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈnɔɪd/ (AmE), /ə.ˈnɔɪd/ (BrE)
feeling slightly angry, irritated, or bothered, usually because of something unpleasant or repetitive

Examples

Feeling annoyed often
He looked annoyed when I interrupted his meeting.
It's easy to get annoyed if you have to wait in a long queue.
She was annoyed by the persistent noise coming from the construction site.
After waiting for an hour, he was clearly annoyed with the delay.
I get annoyed by people who talk loudly on their phones.
She sounded annoyed when I asked her the same question again.
