Mount Rushmore

proper noun
metaphorical four-person lineup of icons
Pronunciation: / ma8ant c8r8c83.m54r / (AmE), / ma8ant c8r8c83.m54d0 / (BrE)
a (figurative) group of four people widely regarded as the most important, influential, or iconic in a particular field or category.

Examples

Mount Rushmore of music

Fans debated which singers belonged on the Mount Rushmore of soul music during the podcast episode.
Business magazines often name a tech industry's Mount Rushmore to highlight four transformative founders.
