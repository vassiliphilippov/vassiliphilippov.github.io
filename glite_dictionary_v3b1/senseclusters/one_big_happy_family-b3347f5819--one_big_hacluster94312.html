one big happy family

noun
close, harmonious group
Pronunciation: / wʌn bɪɡ ˈhæpi ˈfæməli / (AmE), / wʌn bɪɡ ˈhæpi ˈfæməli / (BrE)
a group of people who have close, friendly relationships with each other, similar to those in a loving family

Examples

A one big happy family

The company's annual picnic made everyone feel part of one big happy family.
