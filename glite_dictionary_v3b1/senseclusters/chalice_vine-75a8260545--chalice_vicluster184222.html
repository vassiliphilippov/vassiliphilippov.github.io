chalice vine

noun
Mexican climbing plant with large yellow flowers
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃælɪs vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈtʃælɪs vaɪn / (BrE)
A Mexican evergreen climbing plant with large, solitary, funnel-shaped fragrant yellow flowers, often having purple-brown ridges in the throat.

Examples

Gardeners often train the chalice vine to grow along fences and trellises for a dramatic effect.
