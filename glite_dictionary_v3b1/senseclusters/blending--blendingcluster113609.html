blending

noun
ensemble method in machine learning

(in machine learning) an ensemble method for combining predictions from multiple models to produce a single, improved forecast or decision, often by weighting outputs or training a simple model on held-out predictions.

Examples

Model blending
The data scientist used blending to combine predictions from several models for an accurate forecast.
After tuning, their competition entry relied on blending rather than a single model to improve results.
In production, engineers monitor the performance of blending to avoid degrading predictions over time.
