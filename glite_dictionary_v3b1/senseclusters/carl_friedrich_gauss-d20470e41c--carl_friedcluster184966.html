Carl Friedrich Gauss

proper noun
German mathematician, pioneer in number theory
8.0
Pronunciation: /kɑːrl ˈfriː.drɪk gaʊs/ (AmE), /kɑːl ˈfriː.drɪk gaʊs/ (BrE)
