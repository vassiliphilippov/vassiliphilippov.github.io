theater critic

noun
person reviewing theatrical performances
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈθiːətər ˈkrɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˈθɪətə ˈkrɪtɪk / (BrE)
a person who reviews and evaluates theatrical performances for publications or media

Examples

Theater critic reviews plays
As a seasoned theater critic, he has seen hundreds of plays over the years.
The theater critic praised the actor's performance in her latest review.
The director was nervous about what the theater critic would write about the premiere.
