Synonyms

  • appear
    1.0 86/m

  • make an appearance
    3.5 1.58/m
    make an appearance is used for briefly attending events in a formal way, while show up is more general for arriving.
  • come out
    2.3 20/m
    come out is used for something becoming visible or being released, while show up usually means someone arrives at a place.
  • emerge
    3.5 31/m
    emerge often means come out from a hidden or covered place, while show up can mean simply arriving in plain sight.
