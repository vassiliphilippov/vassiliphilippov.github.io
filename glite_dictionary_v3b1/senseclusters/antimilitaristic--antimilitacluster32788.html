antimilitaristic

adjective
opposed to militarism
7.2
Pronunciation: /æn.tɪ.mɪl.ɪ.tə.ˈrɪs.tɪk/ (AmE), /æn.tɪ.mɪl.ɪ.tə.ˈrɪs.tɪk/ (BrE)

The organization promotes antimilitaristic values and encourages peaceful conflict resolution.
