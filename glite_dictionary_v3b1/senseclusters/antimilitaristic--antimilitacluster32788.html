antimilitaristic

adjective
opposed to militarism
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˌmɪlɪtəˈrɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌæntiˌmɪlɪtəˈrɪstɪk / (BrE)
opposed to militarism or to the influence of the military in society or government

Examples

Antimilitaristic views
The party's antimilitaristic stance attracted many young voters who wanted less military spending.
Her antimilitaristic views were shaped by her family's experiences during the war.
The organization promotes antimilitaristic values and encourages peaceful conflict resolution.

Related Terms

  • antonym: militaristic - supporting the use or influence of the military
  • synonym: pacifist - opposed to war or violence as a means of settling disputes
