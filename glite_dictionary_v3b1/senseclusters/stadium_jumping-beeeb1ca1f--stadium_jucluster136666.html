stadium jumping

noun
equestrian sport involving horse jumping over obstacles
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪdiəm ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsteɪdiəm ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport of riding horses in competitions over set courses designed to demonstrate skill in jumping over obstacles

Stadium jumping competition
