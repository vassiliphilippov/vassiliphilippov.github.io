sparkless

adjective
not producing sparks
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑːrkləs / (AmE), / ˈspɑːkləs / (BrE)
designed or made so that it does not produce sparks, especially to prevent fire or explosions

Examples

Sparkless tools
Workers must use sparkless tools when repairing equipment in the gas storage area.
The laboratory is equipped with sparkless lighting to ensure maximum safety.
All vehicles entering the mine are required to have sparkless engines to prevent accidents.
