unpathed

adjective
without a path
9.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpæθt / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɑːθt / (BrE)
not having a path or not traveled by paths; unexplored or untrodden.

Examples

The unpathed wilderness
The explorers ventured into the unpathed wilderness, hoping to discover new lands.
Legends spoke of treasures hidden deep within unpathed valleys and forests.
She dreamed of traveling to unpathed places far from civilization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trackless - having no paths or tracks
  • synonym: untrodden - not walked on; not traveled
