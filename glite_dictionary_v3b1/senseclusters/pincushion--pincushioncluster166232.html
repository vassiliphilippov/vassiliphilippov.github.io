pincushion

noun
small cushion for holding pins and needles
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnˌkʊʃən / (AmE), / ˈpɪnˌkʊʃ.ən / (BrE)
a small, firm cushion used for holding pins and needles for convenient access during sewing

Examples

A pincushion for pins
After finishing her project, she carefully placed each pin back into the pincushion.
She always keeps her pincushion next to the sewing machine for easy access.
The colorful pincushion on the table was filled with a variety of pins and needles.
