undy

noun
heraldic wavy line
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndi / (AmE), / ˈʌndi / (BrE)
a heraldic term for a wavy line or division used as a decorative element on a shield

Examples

A silver undy line
The knight's shield was decorated with a silver undy running across its center.
In heraldry, an undy often represents water or waves on a coat of arms.
The artist included a blue undy at the base of the family crest to symbolize the nearby river.
