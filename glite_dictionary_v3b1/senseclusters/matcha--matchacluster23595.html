matcha

adjective
pale green color
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːtʃə / (AmE), / ˈmætʃə / (BrE)
having the pale green color of powdered matcha tea, used to describe objects or materials

Examples

Matcha scarf
She wore a beautiful matcha scarf that complemented her eyes.
The bakery's new cake features a matcha frosting that looks as good as it tastes.
They painted the kitchen walls a soft matcha shade to create a calming atmosphere.
