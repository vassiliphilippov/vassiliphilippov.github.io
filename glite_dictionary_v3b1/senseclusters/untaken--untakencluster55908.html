untaken

adjective
not impressed
7.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈteɪkən / (AmE), / ʌnˈteɪkən / (BrE)
not pleased with, attracted to, or impressed by something or someone; disapproving or unimpressed.

Examples

Untaken with the idea
She seemed completely untaken with the idea, offering no words of encouragement.
Despite his efforts, the audience remained untaken by his performance.
He was untaken with the proposal and quickly dismissed it.
