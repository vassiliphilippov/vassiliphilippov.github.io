automatic drive

noun
a vehicle transmission that shifts gears automatically
Pronunciation: / ˌɔtəˈmætɪk draɪv / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈmætɪk draɪv / (BrE)
a system in a vehicle that automatically changes gears according to speed and driving conditions, without the need for manual gear shifting

Examples

Car with automatic drive
He upgraded his car from a stick shift to an automatic drive for easier commuting.
Many drivers prefer automatic drive vehicles for their convenience, especially in heavy city traffic.
The used car dealership offered both manual and automatic drive options.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automatic transmission - a system in vehicles that changes gears automatically
  • antonym: manual transmission - a system requiring manual gear changes by the driver
