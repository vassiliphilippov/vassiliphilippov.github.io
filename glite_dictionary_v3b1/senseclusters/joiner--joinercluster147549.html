joiner

noun
person who eagerly joins groups
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔɪnər / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪnə / (BrE)
a person who readily joins groups, clubs, or organizations, often seeking social involvement

Examples

Active joiner of clubs
As a natural joiner, she became a member of every club in her school.
He is such a joiner that he can't resist signing up for new organizations.
Not everyone is a joiner; some people prefer to keep to themselves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: socialite - a person who is well known in fashionable society and social circles
