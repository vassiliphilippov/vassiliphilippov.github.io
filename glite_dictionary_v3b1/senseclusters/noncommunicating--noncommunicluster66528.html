noncommunicating

adjective
not connected
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkəˈmjunɪˌkeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
not allowing communication, connection, or passage between parts or areas

Examples

Noncommunicating cyst
A noncommunicating cyst does not connect with other body cavities.
The doctor explained that the patient had a noncommunicating hydrocephalus, meaning the fluid could not flow between brain chambers.
In some cases, a noncommunicating compartment in the body can cause pressure to build up.

Related Terms

  • antonym: communicating - allowing connection or passage between parts
  • synonym: isolated - separated from others; not connected
