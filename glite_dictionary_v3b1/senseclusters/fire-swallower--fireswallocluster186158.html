fire-swallower

noun
performer who pretends to swallow fire
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪər ˌswɑːlɚ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə ˌswɒlə / (BrE)
a performer who creates the illusion of swallowing fire as part of an entertainment act

A fire-swallower performer

The circus hired a famous fire-swallower to amaze the audience with dangerous tricks.
