embattlement

noun
castle defensive wall
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbætəlmənt / (AmE), / ɪmˈbætəlmənt / (BrE)
a parapet or wall with regular gaps for shooting or defense, typically found on castles or fortifications

Examples

Castle embattlements
The ancient fortress was topped with stone embattlements that overlooked the valley.
Tourists admired the impressive embattlements along the castle walls during their visit.
Archers once stood behind the embattlements to defend the stronghold from attackers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: battlement - a low wall with gaps for defense on top of a fort or castle
  • synonym: parapet - a protective wall along the edge of a roof or balcony
