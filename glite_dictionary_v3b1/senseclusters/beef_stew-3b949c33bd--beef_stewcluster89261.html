beef stew

noun
a stew made with beef and vegetables
Pronunciation: / bif stu / (AmE), / biːf stjuː / (BrE)
A dish consisting of beef cooked slowly in liquid, usually with vegetables.

Examples

A beef stew dish
After simmering for hours, the beef stew was rich and flavorful.
Many traditional Irish pubs offer beef stew as a classic menu item.
She served a hearty beef stew for dinner on the cold winter night.
