goat herder

noun
person who tends goats
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊt ˌhɜrdər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊt ˌhɜːdə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to take care of and manage a flock of goats

Examples

A goat herder cares
As a child, she dreamed of becoming a goat herder in the countryside.
The goat herder led his animals across the rocky hillside each morning.
The goat herder repaired the fencing after a storm to keep the flock safe.
