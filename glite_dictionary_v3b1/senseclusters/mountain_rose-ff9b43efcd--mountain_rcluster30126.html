mountain rose

noun
European alpine rose with crimson flowers
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntən roʊz / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntən rəʊz / (BrE)
A European alpine shrub with crimson flowers, commonly found in mountainous regions.

Examples

A mountain rose flower
A cluster of mountain roses flourished near the chilly mountain stream.
Hikers often pause to admire the mountain rose during their ascent in the Alps.
The vibrant blossoms of the mountain rose stood out against the rugged terrain.
