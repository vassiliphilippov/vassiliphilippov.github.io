mint sauce

noun
vinegar-based sauce with chopped mint
Pronunciation: / mɪnt sɔs / (AmE), / mɪnt sɔːs / (BrE)
a sauce made from vinegar, sugar, and chopped mint leaves, traditionally served with lamb and other meats

Examples

Mint sauce with lamb
I always serve mint sauce with roast lamb on Sundays.
She made her own mint sauce using fresh mint from the garden.
The distinctive flavor of mint sauce complements many meat dishes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sauce - liquid or semi-liquid condiment served with food
