minimalist

adjective
relating to artistic minimalism
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnəməlɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪməlɪst / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by minimalism, especially in art, design, or music, involving simplicity and spareness of form or concept

Examples

Minimalist design
His minimalist approach to music strips compositions to their bare essentials.
Many modern homes now feature minimalist interiors with neutral colors and little furniture.
The minimalist style in her paintings is marked by clean lines and simple shapes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: minimal - very small in amount; least possible
