awakening

noun
sudden realization
Pronunciation: / əˈweɪkənɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈweɪkənɪŋ / (BrE)
a moment or process of becoming aware of something or realizing something important

Examples

A personal awakening
The book inspired a personal awakening that led him to change his career.
After years of routine, she experienced an awakening that made her see life differently.
The documentary sparked a national awakening about environmental issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: epiphany - a sudden and important realization
  • antonym: ignorance - lack of knowledge or awareness
  • synonym: realization - the act of becoming fully aware of something
