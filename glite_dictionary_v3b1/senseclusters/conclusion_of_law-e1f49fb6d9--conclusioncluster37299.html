conclusion of law

noun
judicial finding about application of law
Pronunciation: / kənˈkluʒən əv lɑ / (AmE), / kənˈkluːʒən əv lɔː / (BrE)
a determination by a judge or court regarding the application of the law to the facts of a case

Examples

Court conclusion of law
The attorney challenged the court's conclusion of law as being incorrect.
The distinction between a conclusion of law and a finding of fact is critical in legal proceedings.
The judge's conclusion of law determined the final judgment in the case.

Related Terms
