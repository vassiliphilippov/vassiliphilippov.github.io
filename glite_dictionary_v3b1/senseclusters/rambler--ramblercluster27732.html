rambler

noun
person who takes country walks
Pronunciation: / ˈræmblɚ / (AmE), / ˈræmblə / (BrE)
a person who enjoys taking long walks in the countryside, often as a leisure activity

Examples

A countryside rambler
As an enthusiastic rambler, she spends most weekends exploring the hills around her village.
He describes himself as a keen rambler who loves discovering new paths.
The club welcomes any ramblers interested in guided country walks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hiker - person who walks long distances, often over trails
