inflatable

adjective
designed to be filled with air or gas
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfleɪtəbl̩ / (AmE), / ɪnˈfleɪtəbəl / (BrE)
(of an object) designed to be filled with air or gas so that it becomes larger or firm.

Examples

Inflatable mattress
The children jumped on the large inflatable castle at the party.
This inflatable mattress is very convenient for camping trips.
We bought an inflatable kayak to use on the lake this summer.
