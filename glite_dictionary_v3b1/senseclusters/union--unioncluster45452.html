union

noun
emblem on a flag representing unity of states or countries
9.3 4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈjun.jən / (AmE), / ˈjuː.njən / (BrE)
a device or emblem on a national flag symbolizing the union of two or more sovereignties, often found in the upper inner corner

Examples

Union on a flag
Many flags display a union to signify the coming together of different states or regions.
On the British flag, the union represents the historic merging of England and Scotland.
The blue field in the upper corner of the United States flag is called the union.
