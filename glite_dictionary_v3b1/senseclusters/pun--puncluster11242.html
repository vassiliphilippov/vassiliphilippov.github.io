pun

noun
humorous play on words
4.5
Pronunciation: / pʌn / (AmE), / pʌn / (BrE)
a joke exploiting the different possible meanings of a word or the fact that there are words that sound alike but have different meanings

Examples

A funny pun
He ended his speech with a clever pun that made everyone laugh.
She enjoys telling jokes that have an unexpected pun in the punchline.
The headline relied on a pun for its humor and impact.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wordplay - the witty exploitation of the meanings and ambiguities of words
