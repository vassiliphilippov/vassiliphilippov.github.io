moonflower

noun
climbing plant with nocturnal white flowers
7.3 0.13/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàmuÀênÀåfla ä.…ö / (AmE), / ÀàmuÀênÀåfla ä.…ô / (BrE)
A tropical or subtropical climbing plant that produces large, fragrant, white flowers which bloom at night.

Examples

The moonflower blooms
Many pollinators are attracted to the moonflower because of its fragrance and nighttime blooming.
She planted a moonflower vine to enjoy its beautiful white blossoms every evening.
The moonflower blooms at dusk, filling the garden with a sweet scent through the night.

Synonyms

  • Ipomoea alba
    9.9 0.00/m
    Ipomoea alba is the scientific Latin name used by botanists and in books, while moonflower is the common English name people use.
  • belle de nuit
    9.9 0.00/m
    belle de nuit is a common name used in many regions and gardens for this night-blooming vine, while moonflower is the English common name.
