scaly-tailed

adjective
having a tail covered with scales
Pronunciation: / ˈskeɪli teɪld / (AmE), / ˈskeɪli teɪld / (BrE)
(of an animal) having a tail covered with scales.

Examples

A scaly-tailed animal
Researchers observed a rare, scaly-tailed mammal in the rainforest canopy.
Some rodents in Africa are known as scaly-tailed squirrels due to their distinct tails.
The scaly-tailed lizard basked in the sun on a flat rock.
