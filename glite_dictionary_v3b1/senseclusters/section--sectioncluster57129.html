section

noun
thin slice for microscope study
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈsek.ʃən / (BrE)
a very thin slice of tissue, mineral, or other substance, prepared for examination under a microscope

Examples

A thin section for study
A thin section of quartz revealed its crystalline structure clearly when viewed in polarized light.
Several sections of the tumor were examined to determine the extent of disease.
The pathologist studied the stained section under high magnification to identify abnormalities.
