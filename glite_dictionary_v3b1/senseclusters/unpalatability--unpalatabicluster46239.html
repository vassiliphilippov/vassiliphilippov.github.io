unpalatability

noun
quality of being difficult to accept
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌpælətəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌpælətəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being difficult or unpleasant to accept or agree with, especially in ideas or decisions

Examples

Unpalatability of truth
Despite its unpalatability, the decision was necessary for the company's survival.
She struggled with the unpalatability of the truth he had told her.
The unpalatability of the proposed budget made it difficult to gain public support.

Related Terms

  • antonym: palatability - the quality of being pleasant or acceptable, especially to the mind or taste
  • synonym: unacceptability - the state of being unacceptable
