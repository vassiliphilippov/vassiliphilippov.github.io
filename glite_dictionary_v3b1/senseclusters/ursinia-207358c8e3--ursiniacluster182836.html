Ursinia

noun
genus of South African ornamental plants
Pronunciation: / ɝːˈsɪniə / (AmE), / ɜːˈsɪniə / (BrE)
A genus of South African herbs and shrubs in the daisy family, grown as ornamentals for their bright yellow or orange flowers.

Examples

Bright Ursinia flowers
Many annual Ursinia varieties are easy to grow from seed in well-drained soil.
Several species of Ursinia are popular in gardens for their bright, daisy-like flowers.
The botanist studied various Ursinia plants native to South Africa.
