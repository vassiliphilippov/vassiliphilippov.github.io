street person

noun
a homeless person living on the streets
5.6 0.36/m
Pronunciation: / striÀêt Ààp…úrs…ôn / (AmE), / striÀêt Ààp…úÀêsn / (BrE)
a person who is homeless, often sleeping in public places or outdoors

Examples

A street person sleeps outside
He spent several years as a street person before finding stable housing.
Many cities struggle to provide support for the growing number of street persons.
The shelter was founded to help street people get back on their feet.

Synonyms

  • homeless person
    2.3 3.05/m
    homeless person is someone without a home who may live in shelters or on streets, while street person often sleeps outdoors.
  • rough sleeper
    7.1 0.00/m
    rough sleeper sleeps outdoors or in places not meant for living, while street person is any homeless person often on public streets.
  • unhoused
    6.7 0.00/m
    unhoused refers to people without a permanent home, often used as a neutral term, while street person implies sleeping outdoors.
  • houseless
    4.4 0.00/m
    houseless describes people who lack a home and is a less common alternative term, while street person often stresses sleeping outside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: homeless - without a home, especially living on the streets
