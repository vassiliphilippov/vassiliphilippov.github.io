chippie

adjective
easily offended
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪpi / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪpi / (BrE)
easily offended or showing a defensive, irritable attitude, especially in response to criticism or teasing

Examples

Chippie attitude
He became chippie whenever someone mentioned his recent mistake at work.
Her chippie attitude made it hard for others to joke around her.
If you are too chippie, you might misunderstand harmless comments as insults.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - not easily upset or offended
  • synonym: sensitive - quick to take offense or be affected emotionally
  • synonym: touchy - easily upset or offended
