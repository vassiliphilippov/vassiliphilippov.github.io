gasper

noun
person who gasps
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæspər / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːspə / (BrE)
a person who gasps, especially in response to surprise, shock, or pain

Examples

A surprised gasper
The loud noise startled a gasper in the front row, making everyone turn to look.
During the scary movie, one gasper kept drawing attention with sudden breaths.
When the results were announced, a gasper could be heard across the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gasp - to breathe in suddenly, often from surprise or pain
