lek

noun
monetary unit of Albania
Pronunciation: / lɛk / (AmE), / lɛk / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Albania, equal to 100 qindarka.

Examples

Albanian lek coins
After the currency reform, the new lek coins were introduced nationwide.
The price of coffee in Albania was listed as 150 lek per cup.
Tourists exchanged their euros for lek at the bank upon arriving in Tirana.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: qindarka - a subunit of the Albanian lek (1/100 of a lek)
