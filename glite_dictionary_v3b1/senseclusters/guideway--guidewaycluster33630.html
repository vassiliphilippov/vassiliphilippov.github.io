guideway

noun
vehicle guiding track
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈɡaɪd.weɪ/ (AmE), /ˈɡaɪd.weɪ/ (BrE)
a track or structure that guides the movement of vehicles (like trains or buses) or machine parts

Examples

A guideway track
The city built a new guideway for the light rail system to improve public transportation.
Engineers inspected the guideway to ensure it was safe for the automated buses.
A dedicated guideway helps trams avoid traffic and stay on schedule.
The new monorail glides along its elevated guideway above the city streets.
Engineers inspected the guideway to ensure it was free of debris before starting the machine.
A well-designed guideway helps reduce friction and wear in moving parts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: channel - a groove or passage for directing movement
  • synonym: roadway - the part of a road used by vehicles
  • synonym: track - a set of rails or a path for vehicles to travel on
