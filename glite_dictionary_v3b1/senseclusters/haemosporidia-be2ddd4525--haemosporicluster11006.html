Haemosporidia

noun
subclass of blood parasites (Apicomplexa)
6.0
Pronunciation: / hiːmoʊspɔːˈrɪdiə /; / hiːməˌspɔːˈrɪdiə / (AmE), / hiːməʊspɔːˈrɪdɪə /; / hiːməʊˌspɒˈrɪdɪə / (BrE)
