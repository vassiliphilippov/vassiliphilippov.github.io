theanthropism

noun
attribution of human traits to a god or object
Pronunciation: / θiˈænθrəˌpɪzəm / (AmE), / θiˈænθrəˌpɪzəm / (BrE)
the attribution of human characteristics or form to a deity or non-human entity

Examples

Theanthropism in gods
Debates about theanthropism are common in comparative religion studies.
Many mythologies display theanthropism by depicting gods with human emotions and bodies.
The artist's painting of the sun with a face is a classic example of theanthropism.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anthropomorphism - the attribution of human attributes to non-human entities
