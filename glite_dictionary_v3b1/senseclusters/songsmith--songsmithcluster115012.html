songsmith

noun
songwriter
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔŋˌsmɪθ / (AmE), / ˈsɒŋˌsmɪθ / (BrE)
a person who writes songs, especially as a profession

Examples

Famous songsmith
The famous songsmith was known for creating catchy melodies and heartfelt lyrics.
As a young songsmith, she dreamed of hearing her music on the radio.
Many artists rely on a skilled songsmith to help them craft their biggest hits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: composer - a person who writes music
  • antonym: performer - a person who presents songs or music to an audience
  • synonym: songwriter - a person who composes songs
