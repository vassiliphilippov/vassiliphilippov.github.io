smoke screen

noun
cloud of smoke concealing movement
5.5 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / smo äk skriÀên / (AmE), / sm…ô äk skriÀên / (BrE)
a cloud of smoke created to conceal military movements or activities from the enemy's view

Examples

Military smoke screen
A well-timed smoke screen allowed the troops to retreat without being seen.
During the exercise, the ship set up a smoke screen to obscure its position from observers.
The army released a smoke screen to hide their tanks as they advanced across the field.
