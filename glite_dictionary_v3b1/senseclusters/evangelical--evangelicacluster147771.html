evangelical

adjective
relating to the Christian gospel
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌiːvænˈdʒɛlɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌiːvænˈdʒelɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to the Christian gospel, especially as expressed in the first four books of the New Testament.

Examples

Evangelical message
His evangelical beliefs shaped his approach to community service.
Many early Protestant movements were deeply evangelical in character.
The evangelical message emphasizes personal faith and the authority of scripture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gospel - the teachings of Christianity, especially those found in the first four books of the New Testament
