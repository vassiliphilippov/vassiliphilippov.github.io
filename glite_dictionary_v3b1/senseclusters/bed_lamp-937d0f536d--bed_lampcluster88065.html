bed lamp

noun
lamp by a bed
Pronunciation: / bɛd læmp / (AmE), / bɛd læmp / (BrE)
a small lamp placed near a bed, used for reading or providing light at night

Examples

A bed lamp light
He reached over to switch off the bed lamp before falling asleep.
The soft glow from her bed lamp made reading at night comfortable.
She bought a new bed lamp with a dimmer for her bedroom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: table lamp - a lamp designed to stand on a table
