atone

verb
make amends; seek forgiveness
5.8
Pronunciation: / əˈtoʊn / (AmE), / əˈtəʊn / (BrE)
to do something to show you are sorry for doing something wrong; to make amends or seek forgiveness

Related Terms

  • antonym: condone - to accept or allow morally wrong behavior (antonym in sense of not making amends)
  • synonym: expiate - to make amends or reparation for (guilt or wrongdoing)
  • synonym: repent - feel or express sincere regret or remorse about one's wrongdoing or sin
  • antonym: sin - an immoral act considered to be a transgression against divine law
