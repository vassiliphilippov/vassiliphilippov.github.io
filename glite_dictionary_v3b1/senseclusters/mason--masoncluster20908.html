mason

verb
to build with stone
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪsən / (AmE), / ˈmeɪsən / (BrE)
to build or construct something using stone or brick and mortar

Examples

To mason a wall
The workers masoned the new library walls with carefully chosen bricks.
A team of experts masoned the stone archway over several weeks.
He learned how to mason a fireplace during his apprenticeship.

Related Terms

  • synonym: construct - to build something by putting parts together
  • antonym: demolish - to destroy or tear down a structure
