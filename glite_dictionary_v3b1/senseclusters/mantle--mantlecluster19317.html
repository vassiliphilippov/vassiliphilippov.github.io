mantle

verb
cover or spread over like a mantle
Pronunciation: / ˈmæn.tl̩ /; / ˈmæntəl / (AmE), / ˈmæn.tl̩ /; / ˈmæntəl / (BrE)
to cover or envelop something, especially as if with a cloak or layer
