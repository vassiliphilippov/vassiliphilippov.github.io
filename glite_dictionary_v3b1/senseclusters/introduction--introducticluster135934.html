introduction

noun
the act of putting something into another
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.trəˈdʌk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.trəˈdʌk.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of inserting one thing into another, or the process of placing something inside something else

Examples

Introduction of a probe
Careful introduction of the probe ensured accurate results during the experiment.
The introduction of the medication into the vein was performed by a nurse.
The technology allows for the introduction of new genetic material into plant cells.
