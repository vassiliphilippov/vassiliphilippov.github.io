fathometer

noun
ultrasonic depth-measuring instrument
5.2
Pronunciation: / fəˈθɑːmətər / (AmE), / fəˈθɒmɪtə / (BrE)
An instrument that measures the depth of water or submerged objects using ultrasonic waves.

Examples

The fathometer measures depth
After calibrating the fathometer, the crew monitored the depth readings throughout the voyage.
Modern fishing boats are often equipped with a fathometer to locate schools of fish and avoid shallow areas.
The research team used a fathometer to chart the underwater terrain before beginning their dive.
