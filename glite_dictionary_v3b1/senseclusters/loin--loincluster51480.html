loin

noun
lower back or flank area
5.8
Pronunciation: / lɔɪn / (AmE), / lɔɪn / (BrE)
either side of the lower back between the ribs and the hipbones, in humans and animals

Examples

Lower loin pain
He felt a sharp pain in his loin after lifting the heavy box.
She placed a heating pad across her lower loin to relieve the tension.
The butcher trimmed the fat from the loin of the lamb.
