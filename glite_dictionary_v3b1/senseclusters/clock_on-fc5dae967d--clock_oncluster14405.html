clock on

verb
register arrival at work
3.7
Pronunciation: / klɑk ɑn / (AmE), / klɒk ɒn / (BrE)
to record the time one arrives at work, especially using a time clock or similar system

Examples

To clock on work
All employees must clock on when they arrive in the morning.
She forgot to clock on and had to speak to her manager about her hours.
The new system allows workers to clock on using their ID badges.
