clock on

verb
register arrival at work
4.8 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / klÉ‘k É‘n / (AmE), / klÉ’k É’n / (BrE)
to record the time one arrives at work, especially using a time clock or similar system

Examples

To clock on work
All employees must clock on when they arrive in the morning.
She forgot to clock on and had to speak to her manager about her hours.
The new system allows workers to clock on using their ID badges.

Synonyms

  • clock in
    3.2 0.25/m
    clock in means recording arrival time with a machine or electronic system, while clock on refers to doing that using a time clock at work.
  • punch in
    3.5 0.31/m
    punch in means recording arrival time by using a time clock or electronic system, while clock on is a more neutral work phrase.
