unworthy

adjective
not deserving; lacking merit
1.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɝːði / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɜːði / (BrE)
not deserving respect, help, or good treatment

Examples

Unworthy of respect
His actions were considered unworthy of someone in his position.
She felt unworthy of the praise she received after the project succeeded.
They believed he was unworthy of their trust after breaking his promise.
He considered himself unworthy of such generosity from his friends.
She felt unworthy of the praise she received for her hard work.
The kingdom saw him as unworthy to inherit the throne.
