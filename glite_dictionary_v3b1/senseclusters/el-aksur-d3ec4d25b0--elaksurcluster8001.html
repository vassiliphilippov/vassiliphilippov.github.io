El-Aksur

proper noun
city in Egypt near the ruins of Thebes
Pronunciation: / ɛl ˈæksʊr / (AmE), / ɛl ˈæksʊə / (BrE)
a city in central Egypt on the east bank of the Nile, known as a center for visitors to the ancient ruins of Thebes and its surroundings

Examples

El-Aksur city
From El-Aksur, travelers can easily access the Valley of the Kings.
Many tourists visit El-Aksur to explore the ancient temples and tombs nearby.
The local economy of El-Aksur depends heavily on tourism.
