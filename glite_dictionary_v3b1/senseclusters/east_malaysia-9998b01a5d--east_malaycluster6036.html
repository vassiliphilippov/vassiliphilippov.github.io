East Malaysia

proper noun
the Malaysian part of Borneo
Pronunciation: / ˌist məˈleɪʒə / (AmE), / ˌiːst məˈleɪziə / (BrE)
the part of Malaysia located on the island of Borneo, comprising the states of Sabah and Sarawak and the federal territory of Labuan

Examples

East Malaysia region
Many tourists visit East Malaysia for its rainforests and rich biodiversity.
Oil and gas production play a crucial role in the economy of East Malaysia.
The states of Sabah and Sarawak are both located in East Malaysia.
