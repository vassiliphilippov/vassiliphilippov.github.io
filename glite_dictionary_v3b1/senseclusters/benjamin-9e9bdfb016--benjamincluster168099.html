Benjamin

noun
slang for $100 bill
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛndʒəmɪn / (AmE), / ˈbɛndʒəmɪn / (BrE)
a slang term for a one-hundred-dollar bill in the United States, referring to the portrait of Benjamin Franklin on the note

Examples

A Benjamin bill
He pulled out a few Benjamins to pay for the new laptop.
If you want those shoes, it'll cost you a Benjamin at least.
She saved up her Benjamins for a special vacation.
