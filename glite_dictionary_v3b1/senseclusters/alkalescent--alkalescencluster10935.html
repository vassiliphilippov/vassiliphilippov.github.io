alkalescent

adjective
slightly alkaline; tending to become alkaline
Pronunciation: / ˌæl.kəˈlɛs.ənt / (AmE), / ˌalkəˈlɛsənt / (BrE)
Slightly alkaline or tending to become alkaline.

Examples

Slightly alkalescent water
Certain cleaning agents are deliberately made alkalescent for gentle, but effective, use on delicate surfaces.
The mineral water’s alkalescent character makes it beneficial for neutralizing slight acidity.
The solution was only alkalescent, not fully alkaline, showing a faint blue on the litmus paper.

Related Terms

  • antonym: acidic - having the properties of an acid; sour, or sharply tasting
  • degree higher: alkaline - having the properties of an alkali; strongly basic
