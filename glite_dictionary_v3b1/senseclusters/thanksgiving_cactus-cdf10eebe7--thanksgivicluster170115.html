Thanksgiving cactus

noun
houseplant cactus with red flowers
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌθæŋksˈɡɪvɪŋ ˈkæktəs / (AmE), / ˌθaŋksˈɡɪvɪŋ ˈkæktəs / (BrE)
A South American cactus with jointed stems and usually red flowers, often grown as a houseplant and typically blooming around Thanksgiving.

Examples

The Thanksgiving cactus blooms
Many people confuse the Thanksgiving cactus with the Christmas cactus, but their flowering times are different.
My Thanksgiving cactus bloomed early this year, filling the windowsill with bright red flowers.
She inherited her grandmother's Thanksgiving cactus, which is now over thirty years old.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Christmas cactus - A similar cactus species that flowers around Christmas, also from the genus Schlumbergera
