hot spring

noun
natural spring of hot water
1.0
Pronunciation: / hɑt sprɪŋ / (AmE), / hɒt sprɪŋ / (BrE)
a natural spring of water that is heated geothermally, typically to a temperature of 70°F (21°C) or above

Examples

A hot spring pool
A natural hot spring bubbled up in the middle of the forest, surprising the hikers.
The volcano is surrounded by several hot springs that attract health enthusiasts.
Tourists flock to the area every year to bathe in the hot spring and enjoy its therapeutic waters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geyser - a hot spring that intermittently sends up jets of water and steam
