store-bought

adjective
purchased from a shop, not homemade
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔr ˌbɔt / (AmE), / ˈstɔː ˌbɒt / (BrE)
Bought from a shop or store rather than being homemade.

Examples

Store-bought food
He wore a store-bought suit to the wedding.
She prefers to bake her own bread instead of eating store-bought loaves.
The cake tasted surprisingly good for a store-bought dessert.
