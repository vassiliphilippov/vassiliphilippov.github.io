genus Ceratitis

noun
genus of fruit flies (including Mediterranean fruit fly)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs sɛrəˈtaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs sɛrəˈtaɪtɪs / (BrE)
