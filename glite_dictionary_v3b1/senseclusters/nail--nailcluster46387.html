nail

noun
hard plate on a finger or toe
2.9
Pronunciation: / neɪl / (AmE), / neɪl / (BrE)
the hard, flat, horny plate covering and protecting the upper surface of the tip of a finger or toe in humans and other primates

Examples

Paint a nail
He accidentally broke a nail while opening the box.
It is important to keep each nail clean and neatly trimmed.
She painted her nail with a bright red polish for the party.

  • synonym: toenail - nail on a toe
