Cassius Clay

proper noun
birth name of Muhammad Ali, the boxer
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkæsiəs kleɪ / (AmE), / ˈkæsiəs kleɪ / (BrE)
The birth name of Muhammad Ali (1942–2016), American heavyweight boxing champion and civil rights activist, known for winning the world heavyweight championship three times.

Examples

Cassius Clay world champion
Cassius Clay became world champion at just 22 years old.
Before changing his name, Cassius Clay had already gained national fame as a boxer.
Many people still remember Muhammad Ali by his original name, Cassius Clay.
