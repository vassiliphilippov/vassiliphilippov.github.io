nothin

interjection
informal reply to thanks
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌθɪn / (AmE), / ˈnʌθɪn / (BrE)
an informal spoken response meaning 'it's nothing' or 'don't mention it', used to reply to thanks or downplay help

Examples

'Thanks.' - 'Nothin.'
She thanked him for his help, and he just smiled and said, 'nothin'.
After carrying her bags, he replied 'nothin' when she expressed her gratitude.
'Nothin,' he shrugged, brushing off the compliment for his hard work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: no problem - an informal way to say that something was not a difficulty
  • synonym: you're welcome - a polite response to thanks
