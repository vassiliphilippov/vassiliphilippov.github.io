Gnetopsida

noun
a class of gymnospermous plants
9.9
Pronunciation: / niːˌtɑːpˈsaɪdə / (AmE), / nɛˌtɒpˈsɪdə / (BrE)
A class of gymnospermous flowering plants, considered to be an evolutionary link between conifers and angiosperms; classified as a class or subdivision in different botanical systems.
