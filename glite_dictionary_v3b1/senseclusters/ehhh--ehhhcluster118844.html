ehhh

interjection
uncertainty or indifference
Pronunciation: / ɛ / (AmE), / ɛ / (BrE)
used to express uncertainty, indifference, or lack of enthusiasm about something

Examples

Ehhh not interested
When asked if he wanted to go out, he just replied ehhh and shrugged.
She gave an ehhh when offered a second helping of dessert.
His reaction to the movie was simply ehhh, showing he was not impressed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meh - used to show indifference or lack of interest
  • antonym: sure - used to express agreement or acceptance
