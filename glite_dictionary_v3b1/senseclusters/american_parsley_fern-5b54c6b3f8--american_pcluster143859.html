American parsley fern

noun
fern with parsley-like fronds from North America
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈpɑrsli fɜrn / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈpɑːsli fɜːn / (BrE)
A rock-inhabiting fern native to northern North America, forming thick tufts and having fronds that resemble parsley.
