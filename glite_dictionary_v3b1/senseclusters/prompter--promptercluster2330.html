prompter

noun
device displaying words for reading aloud
4.6 0.20/m
Pronunciation: / Ààpr…ëÀêmp.t…ôr / (AmE), / Ààpr…ímp.t…ô / (BrE)
a device that displays words for people to read, especially during a performance or broadcast

Examples

TV prompter
During the play, the actors occasionally glanced at the prompter when they forgot their lines.
Television presenters often use a prompter to help them read lengthy scripts.
The news anchor relied on the prompter to deliver her lines smoothly.

Synonyms

  • teleprompter
    5.9 1.76/m
    teleprompter displays a prepared script for TV performers to read, while prompter is a broader name for any device that shows words during performances.
  • autocue
    6.7 0.02/m
    autocue is the TV name common in British broadcasting for a script display, while prompter is the general term for such devices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autocue - a British term for a teleprompter
