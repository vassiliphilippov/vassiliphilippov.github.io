noncontact

adjective
without physical touch
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈkɑntækt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈkɒntækt / (BrE)
not involving physical touch or direct contact with a person or object

Examples

Noncontact thermometer
The hospital uses noncontact thermometers to reduce the risk of spreading germs.
A noncontact sport is one where players do not physically touch each other during play.
Many public doors now have noncontact sensors to open automatically for visitors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contact - involving physical touch or direct interaction
  • synonym: touchless - not requiring physical touch
