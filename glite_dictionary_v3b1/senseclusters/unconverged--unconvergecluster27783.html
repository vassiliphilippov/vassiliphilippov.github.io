unconverged

adjective
not united
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈvɜrdʒd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈvɜːdʒd / (BrE)
not having come together, met, or become united; remaining separate or apart.

Examples

Unconverged ideas
The two theories remained unconverged despite years of research and debate.
After hours of searching, the rescue teams were still unconverged in different parts of the forest.
The lines on the graph stayed unconverged, indicating no intersection point.

Related Terms

  • antonym: converged - having come together or united at a point
  • synonym: divergent - moving or extending in different directions from a common point
