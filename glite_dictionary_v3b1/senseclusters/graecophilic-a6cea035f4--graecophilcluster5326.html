Graecophilic

adjective
admiring Greece or Greek culture
Pronunciation: / ɡriːkəˈfɪlɪk / (AmE), / ɡriːkəˈfɪlɪk / (BrE)
Having or showing a strong admiration for Greece or Greek culture, people, or things.

Examples

Graecophilic admiration
Her Graecophilic tastes were evident in the classical statues decorating her home.
Many Graecophilic scholars have extensively researched Hellenistic history.
The museum's Graecophilic director ensured the collection emphasized ancient Greek art.
