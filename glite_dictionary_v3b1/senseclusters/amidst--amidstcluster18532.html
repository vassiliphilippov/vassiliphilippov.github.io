amidst

preposition
in the middle of
Pronunciation: / əˈmɪdst / (AmE), / əˈmɪdst / (BrE)
in the middle of or surrounded by something

Examples

Peace amidst chaos
She found peace amidst the chaos of the city.
The house stood amidst tall trees and wildflowers.
He remained calm amidst all the confusion and noise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amid - in the middle of or surrounded by
  • synonym: among - in or through the middle of a group of people or things
