community event

noun
gathering/activity for local people
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjunɪti ɪˈvɛnt / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪti ɪˈvɛnt / (BrE)
an activity or gathering organized for people in a particular local area

Examples

Local community event
Attending a community event is a great way to meet your neighbors.
The local council is hosting a community event to celebrate the neighborhood's diversity.
Volunteers are needed to help set up for the annual community event at the park.
