orthologous

adjective
from common ancestor
Pronunciation: / ɔrˈθɑl.ə.ɡəs / (AmE), / ɔːˈθɒl.ə.ɡəs / (BrE)
describing genes or features in different species that come from the same feature in a common ancestor

Examples
