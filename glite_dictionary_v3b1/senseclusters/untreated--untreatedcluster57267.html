untreated

adjective
not chemically or physically processed
2.9 3.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtritɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtriːtɪd / (BrE)
not having been subjected to any chemical or physical process to alter its original state.

Examples

Untreated wood
