silver frost

noun
shiny, sparkling frost
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvɚ frɔst / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə frɒst / (BrE)
frost that appears shiny, silvery, or sparkling, as if covered with a layer of silver

Examples

Silver frost on branches
In the early morning, the branches were covered in silver frost that glittered in the sunlight.
Photographers love to capture landscapes touched by silver frost for their beauty and sparkle.
The children woke to a world transformed by a delicate layer of silver frost on the grass.
