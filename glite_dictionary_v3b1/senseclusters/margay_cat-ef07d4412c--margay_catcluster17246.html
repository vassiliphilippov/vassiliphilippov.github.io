margay cat

noun
a small spotted wildcat of the Americas
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrˌɡeɪ ˌkæt / (AmE), / ˈmɑːɡeɪ ˌkat / (BrE)
a small spotted wildcat native to Central and South America, found from southern Texas to Brazil

Examples

A margay cat spotted
Researchers have spotted a margay cat in the dense forests of the Amazon.
The margay cat is nocturnal and spends most of its life in the trees.
Unlike other wild cats, the margay cat can rotate its ankles to climb down trees headfirst.
