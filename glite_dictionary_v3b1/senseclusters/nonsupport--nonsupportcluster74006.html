nonsupport

noun
failure to provide support
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
the failure to provide financial or other required support, especially as legally obligated to a dependent

Examples

Nonsupport of children
He was arrested for nonsupport after failing to pay child support for several months.
The court found her guilty of nonsupport because she did not provide for her elderly parent.
Laws regarding nonsupport are designed to protect children and other dependents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neglect - failure to care for or support someone properly
  • antonym: support - providing financial or other necessary help
