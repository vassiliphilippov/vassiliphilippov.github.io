groom

verb
prepare someone for sexual exploitation (often online)
9.8
Pronunciation: /gruːm/ (AmE), /gruːm/ (BrE)
to establish a relationship of trust with someone, especially a child, in order to manipulate and sexually exploit them (often by online contact); to prepare a person for abuse or exploitation.

Examples

To groom a child online
An online predator will groom a young user to gain trust before asking for explicit photos.
The police arrested a man who had groomed minors by pretending to be a teenage friend online.
Campaigners urged social networks to do more to stop grooming and protect children from exploitation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grooming - the act of forming a relationship with a child or vulnerable person to lower their inhibitions for sexual abuse or exploitation.
  • synonym: sexual abuse - any form of sexual activity or exploitation inflicted on a person without their consent, especially a minor.
