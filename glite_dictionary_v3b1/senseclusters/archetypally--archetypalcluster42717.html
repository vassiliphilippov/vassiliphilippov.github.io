archetypally

adverb
in a typical way
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrkɪˈtaɪpəli / (AmE), / ˌɑːkɪˈtɪpəli / (BrE)
in a way that is typical of an original model or classic example

Examples

An archetypally romantic story
The film is archetypally romantic, following the classic story of two lovers overcoming obstacles.
His actions were archetypally heroic, showing courage in the face of danger.
The building is archetypally modernist, with clean lines and minimal decoration.
The hero in the story behaves archetypally, following the classic journey of transformation.
Her actions were archetypally maternal, showing care and protection for the children.
The villain is archetypally evil, representing the universal idea of darkness in literature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: typically - in a way that is usual or expected
  • antonym: uniquely - in a way that is unlike anything else
