ownself

pronoun
emphatic reflexive pronoun
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊnˌsɛlf / (AmE), / ˈəʊnˌsɛlf / (BrE)
a nonstandard or dialectal form of 'oneself', used for emphasis to refer to the person mentioned.

Examples

Do it ownself
If you want something done right, you have to do it ownself.
She fixed the problem all by her ownself without asking for help.
You should learn to rely on your ownself in difficult situations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oneself - the reflexive form referring to the person previously mentioned
