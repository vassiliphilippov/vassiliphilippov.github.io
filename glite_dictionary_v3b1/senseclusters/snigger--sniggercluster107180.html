snigger

verb
laugh quietly, often slyly
3.5 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈsnɪ.gər/ (AmE), /ˈsnɪ.gə/ (BrE)
to laugh quietly, especially in a sly or disrespectful way

Examples

To snigger at mistakes
I could hear someone sniggering at the back of the classroom.
She tried not to snigger during the serious meeting.
The boys sniggered as their teacher mispronounced the word.
