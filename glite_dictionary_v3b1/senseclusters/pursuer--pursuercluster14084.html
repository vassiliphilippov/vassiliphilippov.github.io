pursuer

noun
person who pursues or chases
9.3
Pronunciation: / pərˈsuːər / (AmE), / pəˈsjʊə / (BrE)
a person who is chasing, following, or trying hard to get someone or something

Examples

The pursuer chases
Despite the speed of his pursuer, the runner managed to escape into the crowd.
Every good mystery needs a clever detective as the pursuer of truth.
The thief glanced nervously over his shoulder at his pursuer in the alleyway.
As a pursuer of justice, she devoted her life to fighting corruption.
Many consider him a relentless pursuer of knowledge and innovation.
The pursuer never gave up on catching the thief, even after hours of running.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chaser - a person or thing that chases someone or something
  • synonym: follower - a person who follows others or a pursuit
  • antonym: fugitive - a person who is fleeing, especially from pursuit
  • antonym: pursued - person or thing being followed or chased
