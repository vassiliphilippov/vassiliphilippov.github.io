guzzler

noun
heavy consumer of resources
5.2 0.33/m
Pronunciation: / c8618czl59r / (AmE), / c8618czl59 / (BrE)
A thing or person that consumes a resource greedily or in large amounts (for example, fuel, data, battery power, or time).

Examples

A fuel guzzler
That streaming app is a real guzzler of mobile data and quickly used up her allowance.
My old SUV proved a serious guzzler, costing us much more in petrol than we expected.
Large, unoptimized images are web-page guzzlers that slow loading times and frustrate users.

Related Terms

  • antonym: efficient - using resources in a way that achieves maximum productivity with minimum waste
  • synonym: gas guzzler - a vehicle that uses a large amount of petrol or diesel
  • synonym: resource hog - a person or thing that uses more than its fair share of resources
