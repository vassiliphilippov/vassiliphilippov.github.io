alphanumerics

noun
letters and digits (sometimes punctuation) as a set
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌæl.fə.nuˈmɛr.ɪks / (AmE), / ˌæl.fə.njuːˈmɛr.ɪks / (BrE)
A set of characters consisting of letters and digits, sometimes including punctuation, used especially in computer systems and coding.

Examples
