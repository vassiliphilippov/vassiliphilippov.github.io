panhead

noun
rounded screw or engine
Pronunciation: / ˈpænˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈpænˌhɛd / (BrE)
a screw with a rounded, flat-topped head, or a Harley-Davidson motorcycle engine produced from 1948 to 1965

Examples

A panhead screw

Collectors value the classic panhead engines for their unique design and history.
Make sure to use a panhead when attaching the bracket to avoid damaging the surface.
