frontiersman

noun
man living on the frontier
7.6
Pronunciation: / frʌnˈtɪrzmən / (AmE), / frʌnˈtɪəz.mən / (BrE)
a man who lives on or explores the frontier, especially during a period of expansion into new territory

Examples

A frontiersman lives
As a frontiersman, he built his own cabin and lived off the land.
Many stories have been told about the bravery of the American frontiersman.
The legendary frontiersman helped map the unexplored wilderness in the 1800s.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pioneer - a person who is one of the first to settle a new area
