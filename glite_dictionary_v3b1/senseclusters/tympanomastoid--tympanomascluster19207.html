tympanomastoid

noun
ear junction region
9.9
Pronunciation: / tɪmˌpænəˈmæstɔɪd / (AmE), / tɪmˌpænəˈmæstɔɪd / (BrE)
the anatomical region of the ear where the tympanic (middle ear) and mastoid (bone behind the ear) areas meet

Examples

Tympanomastoid region
The doctor examined the tympanomastoid area for signs of infection.
Inflammation in the tympanomastoid region can cause pain and hearing problems.
Surgery was required to treat the abscess in the tympanomastoid structure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mastoid - the bone behind the ear involved in hearing
  • synonym: tympanum - the middle ear or eardrum
