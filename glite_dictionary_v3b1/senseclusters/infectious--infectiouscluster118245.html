Synonyms

  • communicable
    4.2 0.5/m
    communicable is the formal medical term used in reports and studies, while infectious is common in speech and writing.
  • transmissible
    5.3 0.2/m
    transmissible describes a disease that can move between people or animals, while infectious focuses on causing or carrying infection.
  • transmittable
    3.9 0.1/m
    transmittable means able to be passed from person to person in many ways, while infectious describes diseases that spread by infection.
  • contagious
    3.1 4/m
    contagious spreads quickly by close contact and can describe feelings or ideas, while infectious focuses on disease spread by infection.
  • infective
    7.4 0.2/m
    infective means able to cause infection at a biological level and is more technical, while infectious is common for diseases people catch.
  • contractible
    6.5 0.00/m
    contractible describes something a person can catch or acquire, while infectious describes the disease or agent that spreads between people.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contagious - able to be transmitted from one person to another, especially through direct contact
  • antonym: noninfectious - (of a disease or agent) not capable of causing infection
