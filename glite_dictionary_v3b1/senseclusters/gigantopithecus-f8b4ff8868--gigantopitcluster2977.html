Gigantopithecus

noun
very large person
Pronunciation: / dʒaɪˌɡæntoʊpɪˈθikəs / (AmE), / dʒɪˌɡæntəʊpɪˈθiːkəs / (BrE)
a humorous or informal term for a very large person or thing, especially one with a primitive or imposing appearance
