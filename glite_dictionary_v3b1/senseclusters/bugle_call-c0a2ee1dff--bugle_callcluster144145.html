bugle call

noun
signal tune played on a bugle
4.9 4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbjuɡəl kɔl / (AmE), / ˈbjuːɡl kɔːl / (BrE)
a signal or tune played on a bugle, especially to mark a specific time or event, especially in the military

Examples

A bugle call sounds

Each morning, troops assembled promptly in response to the familiar bugle call.
The sound of the bugle call echoed through the camp at sunrise.
