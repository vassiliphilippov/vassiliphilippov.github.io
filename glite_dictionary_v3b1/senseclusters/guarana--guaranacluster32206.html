guarana

noun
caffeinated plant extract
Pronunciation: / ˌɡwɑːrəˈnɑː / (AmE), / ˌɡwɑːrəˈnɑː / (BrE)
a powdered or concentrated extract from the seeds of a South American plant, used as a stimulant and source of caffeine

Examples

Guarana powder supplement
Many athletes add guarana to their drinks for a natural energy boost before workouts.
The herbal supplement contains guarana as its main source of caffeine.
Some energy drinks list guarana on their labels to attract health-conscious consumers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caffeine - a natural stimulant found in coffee, tea, and some plants
  • synonym: energy drink - a beverage containing stimulants like caffeine
