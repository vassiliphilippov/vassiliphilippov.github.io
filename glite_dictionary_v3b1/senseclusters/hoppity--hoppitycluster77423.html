hoppity

noun
small playful jump
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑpɪti / (AmE), / ˈhɒpɪti / (BrE)
a small, quick jump or bounce, often in a playful or light manner

Examples

A quick hoppity

With a cheerful hoppity, the rabbit crossed the garden path.
She added a playful hoppity to her dance routine, making everyone smile.
