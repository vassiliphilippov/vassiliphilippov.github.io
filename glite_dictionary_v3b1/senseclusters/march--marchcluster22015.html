march

verb
lie adjacent to or share a boundary with
6.9 6.9
Pronunciation: /mɑːrtʃ/ (AmE), /mɑːtʃ/ (BrE)
(of land, countries, or regions) to lie adjacent to another or to share a boundary with.

Examples

Counties march with

The duchy marches upon the lands of its ancient rivals.
The region of Lorraine marches with Germany to the east.
