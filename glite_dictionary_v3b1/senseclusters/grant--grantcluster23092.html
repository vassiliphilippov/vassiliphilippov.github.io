grant

noun
an officially granted right or privilege
Pronunciation: / ɡrænt / (AmE), / ɡrɑːnt / (BrE)
a right, privilege, or property officially given to someone, especially by law or authority

Examples

A land grant
He was entitled to land as a grant from the crown for his services.
The company received a grant giving it exclusive rights to operate in the region.
This grant allows her to practice law in multiple states.
The land passed to the new owner through a grant recorded at the county office.
The lawyer prepared the grant to officially transfer the property title.
Without a valid grant, the change of ownership may be challenged in court.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concession - a grant of rights, land or property by a government, local authority, corporation, individual, or other legal entity
