old chap

noun
affectionate term for an older man
8.0
Pronunciation: / oʊld tʃæp / (AmE), / əʊld tʃæp / (BrE)
an affectionate or friendly term for an older man, often used in British English

Examples

My old chap
Don't worry about the details, my old chap—everything will work out fine.
The old chap still enjoys his morning walks in the park despite his age.
When I visited London, the friendly old chap at the pub shared fascinating stories with me.

Related Terms
