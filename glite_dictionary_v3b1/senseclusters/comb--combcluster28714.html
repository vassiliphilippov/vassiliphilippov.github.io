comb

noun
red crest on a chicken's head
Pronunciation: / kəm / (AmE), / kɒm / (BrE)
the fleshy, red crest on the head of a domestic fowl or other gallinaceous birds

Examples

A chicken's comb
In the cold, a chicken's comb can be susceptible to frostbite.
Some breeds of chickens have a larger comb than others.
The rooster's comb was bright red and stood tall on his head.
