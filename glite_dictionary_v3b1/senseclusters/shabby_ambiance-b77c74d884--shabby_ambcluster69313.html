shabby ambiance

noun
worn or dilapidated atmosphere
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈʃæbi æmˈbiːəns / (AmE), / ˈʃæbi ˈæmbiəns / (BrE)
an atmosphere or environment that appears worn, dilapidated, or in poor condition

Examples

The shabby ambiance of a cafe
Despite the shabby ambiance of the café, locals kept coming back for the friendly service.
He was put off by the shabby ambiance, but the food was surprisingly delicious.
The old theater's shabby ambiance somehow added character to the show.
