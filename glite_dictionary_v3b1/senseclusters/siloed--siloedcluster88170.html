siloed

adjective
stored in a silo
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪloʊd / (AmE), / ˈsaɪləʊd / (BrE)
kept or stored in a silo, especially referring to grain or silage kept in a tall storage container

Examples

Siloed grain storage
The siloed corn was protected from rain and pests throughout the winter.
After harvest, the grain was siloed to keep it dry until processing.
Farmers ensure their crops are siloed properly to prevent spoilage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exposed - not protected or covered
  • synonym: stored - kept in a place for future use
