cacciatore

noun
Italian meat dish
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːtʃəˈtɔːreɪ /; / ˌkɑːtʃəˈtɔːri / (AmE), / ˌkætʃəˈtɔːri / (BrE)
an Italian dish made with meat (usually chicken or rabbit), tomatoes, onions, herbs, and sometimes wine
