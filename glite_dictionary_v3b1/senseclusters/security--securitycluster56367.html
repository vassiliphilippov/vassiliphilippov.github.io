security

noun
property pledged as assurance for debt
3.7 3.7
Pronunciation: /sɪ.ˈkjʊr.ə.tiː/ /; / /sə.ˈkjʊr.ə.tiː/ (AmE), /sɪ.ˈkjʊə.tiː/ /; / /sə.ˈkjʊə.tiː/ (BrE)
property or assets pledged as a guarantee for repaying a loan; collateral

Examples

Security for a loan
Creditors often require some form of security before extending large amounts of credit.
The house was offered as security for the bank loan.
Without adequate security, the lender refused to approve the mortgage.
He put up his house as security for the business debt.
The bank required her to provide security before granting the loan.
The creditor accepted stocks and bonds as security against possible default.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collateral - property pledged as security for repayment of a loan
  • synonym: guarantee - a formal assurance that obligations will be fulfilled
  • antonym: risk - the possibility of something bad happening, opposite of security in a financial context
