Examples

Security for a loan
Creditors often require some form of security before extending large amounts of credit.
The house was offered as security for the bank loan.
Without adequate security, the lender refused to approve the mortgage.
He put up his house as security for the business debt.
The bank required her to provide security before granting the loan.
The creditor accepted stocks and bonds as security against possible default.

Synonyms

  • collateral
    7.5 3/m
    collateral is property that a lender can take if you default, while security is the general name for pledged assets.
  • surety
    8.9 0.1/m
    surety is a guarantee or person who promises payment, while security is the pledged property or asset backing a loan.
  • pledge
    4.9 0.2/m
    pledge is an item or deposit given to hold as assurance for a debt, while security names the collateral on a loan.
