muzzleloader

adjective
involving muzzle-loaded guns
8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌz.əl.loʊ.dər/ (AmE), /ˈmʌz.əl.ləʊ.də/ (BrE)
relating to guns, hunting, or activities involving firearms that are loaded from the open end of the barrel (the muzzle)

Examples

Muzzleloader hunting season
The park holds a special muzzleloader hunting season each November.
He entered the muzzleloader shooting competition with his antique rifle.
Only muzzleloader firearms are allowed during this part of the hunting season.
