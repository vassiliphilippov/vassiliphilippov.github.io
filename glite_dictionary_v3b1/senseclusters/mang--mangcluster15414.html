mang

noun
Filipino male title
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɑŋ / (AmE), / mɑŋ / (BrE)
a respectful title used before a man's given name in the Philippines, similar to 'Mister' or 'uncle'

Examples

Mang Tony
Everyone in the neighborhood greets mang Tony with a smile every morning.
She asked mang Ramon for advice about fixing her bicycle.
At the family gathering, the children respectfully called him mang Ben.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Mister - a formal title used before a man's surname or full name
  • synonym: uncle - a respectful term for an older man, not necessarily a relative
