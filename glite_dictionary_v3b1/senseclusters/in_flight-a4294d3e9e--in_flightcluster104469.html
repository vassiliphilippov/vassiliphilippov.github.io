in flight

adverb
while flying through the air
Pronunciation: / ɪn ˈflaɪt / (AmE), / ɪn ˈflaɪt / (BrE)
While flying through the air; during the action of flight.

Examples

In flight birds
Photographers often try to capture birds in flight for dramatic effect.
The eagle looked majestic in flight above the mountains.
Thousands of tiny insects were visible in flight at dusk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: on the wing - while flying; in the air (of birds and insects)
