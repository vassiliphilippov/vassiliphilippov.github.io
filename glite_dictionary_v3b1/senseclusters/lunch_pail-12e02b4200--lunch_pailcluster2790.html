lunch pail

noun
container for carrying lunch
Pronunciation: / lʌntʃ peɪl / (AmE), / lʌntʃ peɪl / (BrE)
a container, typically with a handle, used for carrying lunch or meals to work, school, or elsewhere

Examples

A lunch pail handle
Every morning, he filled his lunch pail with sandwiches and apples for work.
She opened her lunch pail at noon and found a handwritten note from her mother.
The old metal lunch pail had dents from years of use on the construction site.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lunchbox - a box for carrying lunch or meals
