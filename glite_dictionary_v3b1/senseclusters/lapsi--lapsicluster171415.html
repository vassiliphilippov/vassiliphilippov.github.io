lapsi

noun
Nepalese sour fruit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːpsi / (AmE), / ˈlɑːpsi / (BrE)
the small, sour fruit of the Choerospondias axillaris tree, native to Nepal and used in cooking and preserves

Examples

Sour lapsi fruit
She prepared a spicy chutney using fresh lapsi her friend had brought from Kathmandu.
Dried lapsi is a popular snack among children in Nepal.
The market was filled with baskets of ripe lapsi ready for making preserves.
