glutinous

adjective
having a sticky texture
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡluːtənəs / (AmE), / ˈɡluːtɪnəs / (BrE)
having a sticky or glue-like texture; tending to stick or adhere

Examples

Glutinous sticky texture
A glutinous layer formed on the surface of the liquid as it cooled.
Her hands were coated in a glutinous substance after she spilled the syrup.
The glutinous rice stuck together, making it perfect for sushi rolls.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sticky - tending or designed to stick to things; adhesive
