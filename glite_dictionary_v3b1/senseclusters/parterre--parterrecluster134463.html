parterre

noun
ornamental patterned flower bed
8.3
Pronunciation: / pɑːrˈter / (AmE), / pɑːˈteə / (BrE)
an ornamental garden area in which flower beds and paths are arranged to form a decorative pattern, typically viewed from above

Examples

Formal parterre garden
Each spring, gardeners replant the parterre with thousands of colorful flowers.
The palace gardens featured an elaborate parterre designed in the shape of a star.
Visitors admired the manicured parterre from the balcony above, noting the symmetry of the design.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flowerbed - a section of ground in which flowers are grown
