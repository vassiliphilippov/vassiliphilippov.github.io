bongo drum

noun
pair of small hand drums
5.6 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑːŋ.goʊ drʌm/ /; / /ˈbɑːŋ.goʊ dʒrʌm/ (AmE), /ˈbɒŋ.gəʊ drʌm/ /; / /ˈbɒŋ.gəʊ dʒrʌm/ (BrE)
