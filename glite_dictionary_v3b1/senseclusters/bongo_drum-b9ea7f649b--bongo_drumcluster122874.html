bongo drum

noun
pair of small hand drums
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑŋgoʊ drʌm / (AmE), / ˈbɒŋgəʊ drʌm / (BrE)
Either of a pair of small, open-bottomed hand drums of different sizes, joined together and played with the fingers and palms.

Examples

A pair of bongo drums
Children sat in a circle, each taking turns tapping the bongo drum.
He played a lively beat on the bongo drum during the performance.
The musician packed his bongo drum before heading to the gig.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conga drum - a tall narrow drum played with the hands, from Cuba
