regard

noun
a long, fixed look
7.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˈɡɑrd / (AmE), / rɪˈɡɑːd / (BrE)
a long, attentive, or fixed look at someone or something

Examples

A steady regard
He held her in a thoughtful regard before finally speaking.
She met his regard with a steady expression, refusing to look away.
The teacher's stern regard silenced the noisy students.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gaze - a long steady look
  • antonym: glance - a quick or brief look
