unindustrious

adjective
not hardworking
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈdʌstriəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈdʌstriəs / (BrE)
not hardworking or lacking in diligence and effort

Examples

An unindustrious worker
The manager noticed that the new employee was rather unindustrious compared to his colleagues.
Her unindustrious habits made it difficult for her to keep up with her studies.
A team cannot succeed if its members are unindustrious and unmotivated.

Related Terms

  • antonym: industrious - hardworking and diligent
  • synonym: lazy - unwilling to work or use energy
