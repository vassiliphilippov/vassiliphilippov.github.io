noncoercive

adjective
without force or threats
6.8
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.koʊ.ˈɜːr.sɪv/ /; / /nən.koʊ.ˈɜːr.sɪv/ (AmE), /nɒn.kəʊ.ˈɜː.sɪv/ /; / /nən.kəʊ.ˈɜː.sɪv/ (BrE)
