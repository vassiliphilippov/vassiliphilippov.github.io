nihilistically

adverb
in a nihilistic way
Pronunciation: / ˌnaɪɪˈlɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌnaɪɪˈlɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows a belief that life is meaningless or rejects all values and beliefs

Examples

Nihilistically speak sadly
He spoke nihilistically about the future, believing nothing truly matters.
The novel's main character views the world nihilistically, seeing no purpose in any action.
Some critics argue that the film ends nihilistically, offering no hope or resolution.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cynically - in a way that shows distrust of others' motives or sincerity
  • antonym: optimistically - in a way that shows hope or confidence about the future
