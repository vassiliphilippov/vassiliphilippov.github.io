new-car smell

noun
figurative freshness or aura of novelty
Pronunciation: / \u02ccnu\u02d0 \u02c8k\u0251r sm\u025bl / (AmE), / \u02ccnju\u02d0 \u02c8k\u0251\u02d0 sm\u025bl / (BrE)
a figurative sense meaning an impression or aura of freshness, novelty, or premium newness around a product, design, or presentation (not an actual scent).

Examples

New-car smell of a product
Tech reviewers praised the new-car smell of the gadget's packaging, saying it felt premium and fresh.
The startup's launch had a new-car smell that excited investors despite the team's small size.
Despite being pre-owned, the boutique's display maintained a new-car smell of modern design and polish.

  • synonym: novelty - the quality of being new, different, or unusual
