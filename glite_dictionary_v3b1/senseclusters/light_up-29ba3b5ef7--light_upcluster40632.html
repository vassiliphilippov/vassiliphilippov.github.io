Synonyms

  • lighten
    2.3 1/m
    lighten is used for making color or a room brighter, while light up means to become bright or make something burn.
  • illuminate
    4.1 8/m
    illuminate is a formal word for lighting places to show things clearly, while light up is more general and can also mean burn.
  • light
    1.0 50/m
    light means to start a fire or make something burn or shine, while light up means become or make bright.
  • beam
    3.0 0.9/m
    beam means to send a strong, directed ray of light, while light up means to become or make something bright more widely.
  • shine
    1.0 6/m
    shine means to give off or reflect light across a surface, while light up means to make a place or object bright.
  • glow
    1.2 14/m
    glow means to emit a soft, steady light often from heat or chemicals, while light up means become or make very bright.
  • brighten
    2.7 3/m
    brighten means to make something more cheerful or colorful in look, while light up means physically become or make something bright.
