neonicotinoid

noun
nicotine-based insecticide
Pronunciation: / ˌniːoʊˈnɪkətɪnɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌniːəʊˈnɪkətɪnɔɪd / (BrE)
a type of insecticide chemically related to nicotine, used to control insect pests in agriculture

Examples

A neonicotinoid spray

Concerns have been raised about the impact of neonicotinoids on bee populations.
The use of neonicotinoids is regulated in several countries due to environmental risks.
