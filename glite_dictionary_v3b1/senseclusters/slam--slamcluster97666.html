slam

noun
loud impact noise (e.g. shut door)
2.0 1.9
Pronunciation: /slæm/ (AmE), /slæm/ (BrE)
a loud noise or forceful impact, especially when something is closed or struck suddenly

Examples

A loud slam
She winced at the sudden slam of the heavy book hitting the table.
The slam of the door echoed through the empty hallway.
The car door closed with a loud slam in the quiet night.
I heard a slam as the door was shut behind me.
The heavy slam startled everyone in the quiet corridor.
There was a loud slam when he dropped the box on the floor.

  • synonym: crash - a loud sound made by something breaking or hitting another object
