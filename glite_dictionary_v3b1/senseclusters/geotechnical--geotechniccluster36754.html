geotechnical

adjective
earth engineering
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiːoʊˈtɛknɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːəʊˈtɛknɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the engineering properties and behavior of earth materials such as soil and rock

Examples

A geotechnical survey
A geotechnical survey was required before building the bridge.
The engineers analyzed the geotechnical properties of the soil to ensure the building's stability.
She specializes in geotechnical engineering, focusing on landslide prevention and foundation design.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geological - relating to the science of the Earth's physical structure
  • synonym: structural - relating to the arrangement or construction of something
