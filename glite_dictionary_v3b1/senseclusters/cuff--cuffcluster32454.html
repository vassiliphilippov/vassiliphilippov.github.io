cuff

verb
to restrain with handcuffs
Pronunciation: / kʌf / (AmE), / kʌf / (BrE)
to confine or restrain someone with or as if with handcuffs

Examples

To cuff a suspect
Security guards threatened to cuff anyone caught trespassing on the property.
She was quickly cuffed and led to the police car after being apprehended.
The officers proceeded to cuff the suspect before reading him his rights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: handcuff - to restrain by fastening handcuffs to someone's wrists
