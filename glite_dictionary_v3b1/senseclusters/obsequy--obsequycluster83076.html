obsequy

noun
funeral ceremony or rite
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɑb.sɪ.kwi / (AmE), / ˈɒb.sɪ.kwi / (BrE)

Examples

The obsequy ceremony
The obsequy was attended by family, friends, and colleagues from around the world.

Traditional obsequies often include prayers and the singing of hymns.
