pharyngoscopy

noun
throat examination
8.8
Pronunciation: /fə.rɪŋ.ˈɡɑːs.kə.piː/ (AmE), /fə.rɪŋ.ˈɡɒs.kə.piː/ (BrE)
a medical examination of the pharynx using a special instrument to inspect the throat

Examples

A pharyngoscopy exam
The doctor recommended a pharyngoscopy to investigate the cause of her persistent sore throat.
During the pharyngoscopy, the specialist carefully examined the patient's pharynx for any signs of infection.
A pharyngoscopy can help detect tumors or other abnormalities in the throat area.
