corporatization

noun
adoption of corporate practices
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌkɔːrpərətaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɔːpərətaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of changing an organization, especially a public one, to be run like a business or corporation
