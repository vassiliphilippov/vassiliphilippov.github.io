olive-tree agaric

noun
red luminescent European mushroom
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːlɪv tri əˈɡærɪk / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪv tri əˈɡærɪk / (BrE)
A red, luminescent mushroom of Europe, often found growing on olive trees.

Examples

The olive-tree agaric mushroom
Mycologists studying luminescent fungi were excited to find several olive-tree agarics near a grove of old olives.
The olive-tree agaric emits a faint red glow in the dark forest.
The olive-tree agaric is rarely found outside southern Europe.
