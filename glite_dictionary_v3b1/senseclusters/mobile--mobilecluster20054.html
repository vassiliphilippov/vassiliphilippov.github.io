Examples

A mobile person
A child's mobile imagination can quickly shift from one idea to another.
Her mobile features expressed every emotion clearly.
The actor's mobile face was perfect for the role of a comedian.
Having a bicycle makes students much more mobile around campus.

Synonyms

  • movable
    2.5 1.80/m
    movable is easily moved or carried between places, while mobile suggests broader ability to move or travel freely.
  • transportable
    3.7 0.32/m
    transportable is designed to be moved for shipping or storage, while mobile focuses on ability to move or change freely.
  • portable
    2.9 13/m
    portable is light and easy to carry by one person, while mobile can mean moving on its own or changing location freely.
  • carryable
    3.1 0.01/m
    carryable is able to be carried by someone, but mobile also suggests moving by itself or changing position easily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agile - able to move quickly and easily
  • antonym: immobile - unable to move or change quickly
  • synonym: migratory - moving from place to place, especially periodically
  • synonym: nomadic - tending to travel from place to place rather than settle permanently
  • antonym: stationary - not moving; fixed in one place
