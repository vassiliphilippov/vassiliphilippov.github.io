thoughts racing

noun
state of having many competing thoughts
Pronunciation: / ˈθɔts ˈreɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈθɔːts ˈreɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
A state in which a person is having many competing or rapidly moving thoughts, often making it difficult to focus or relax.

Examples

Thoughts racing fast
After the shocking news, I found my thoughts racing and unable to settle.
His thoughts racing made it hard to focus on the task at hand.
Whenever she felt anxious, her thoughts racing kept her awake for hours.

Related Terms

  • synonym: busy mind - a mind filled with many thoughts or preoccupations
