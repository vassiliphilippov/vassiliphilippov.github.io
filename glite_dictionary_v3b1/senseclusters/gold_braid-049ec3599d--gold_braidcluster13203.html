gold braid

noun
decorative gold-colored trimming
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊld breɪd / (AmE), / ɡəʊld breɪd / (BrE)
a decorative cord or ribbon, usually made of gold-colored thread, used to trim clothing or furnishings

Examples

The gold braid trim
She admired the intricate pattern of gold braid on the ceremonial robe.
The general's uniform was decorated with gold braid on the sleeves and collar.
Thick gold braid edged the luxurious velvet curtains in the drawing room.
