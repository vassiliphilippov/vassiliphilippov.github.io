teaser

noun
worker who prepares wool fibers
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːzər /; / ˈtizər / (AmE), / ˈtiːzə / (BrE)
a person or tool used to separate and loosen wool fibers before spinning

Examples

Wool teaser tool
In the past, every spinning workshop relied on a skilled teaser to ensure the quality of the wool.
My grandfather once worked as a teaser in the Yorkshire wool mills.
The teaser carefully separated the tangled wool fibers before they were spun into yarn.
Older textile mills often had a mechanical teaser alongside the carding machines.
She used a metal teaser to separate the matted fleece.
The teaser was essential for preparing the raw wool before spinning.
