thionin

noun
biological purple dye
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈθaɪənɪn / (AmE), / ˈθaɪənɪn / (BrE)
a purple dye used in biological staining, especially for preparing specimens in microscopy

Examples

Thionin stain
The scientist used thionin to stain the tissue samples for better visibility under the microscope.
After applying thionin, the cell structures appeared more distinct in the prepared slides.
Laboratories often rely on thionin for highlighting specific features in biological specimens.
