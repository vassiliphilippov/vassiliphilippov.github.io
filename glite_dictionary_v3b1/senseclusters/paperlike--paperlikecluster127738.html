paperlike

adjective
resembling paper
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪpərˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the qualities of paper

Examples

paperlike material
Her drawing was done on a paperlike material made from pressed plant fibers.
The fake money looked surprisingly real, but felt paperlike compared to actual currency.
The leaves had a paperlike texture, dry and fragile to the touch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: papery - having a thin, paper-like texture
