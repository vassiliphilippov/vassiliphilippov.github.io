web

verb
form or construct a web by weaving
7.1
Pronunciation: / wɛb / (AmE), / wɛb / (BrE)
to construct or form a web, especially by weaving; to cover or connect with or as if with a web

Examples

To web a fence
Frost had webbed the old fence with intricate patterns.
The spider webbed the entire corner of the window overnight.
Vines began to web across the doorway, making it difficult to enter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: weave - to form (something) by interlacing long threads or strips
