radiologic

adjective
medical imaging
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌreɪdiəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌreɪdiəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to the use of X-rays or other medical imaging techniques for diagnosis or treatment

Examples

Radiologic imaging test
The doctor ordered a radiologic test to examine the patient's lungs.
Advances in radiologic technology have improved the accuracy of cancer detection.
A radiologic procedure can provide detailed images of internal organs.
