paneer

noun
Indian fresh cheese
Pronunciation: / pəˈnɪr / (AmE), / pəˈnɪə / (BrE)
a soft, fresh cheese made from curdled milk, commonly used in South Asian cuisine

Examples

Soft paneer cheese
The restaurant served a delicious paneer tikka with spicy chutney.
She added cubes of paneer to the creamy tomato sauce for extra protein.
Many vegetarian dishes in India use paneer as the main ingredient.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ricotta - a soft Italian cheese made from whey
  • synonym: tofu - a soft food made from soybeans, often used as a meat substitute
