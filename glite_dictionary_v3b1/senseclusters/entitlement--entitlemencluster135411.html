entitlement

noun
sense of deserving
6.1
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtaɪtəlmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈtaɪtəlmənt / (BrE)
the belief or feeling that one deserves special treatment, privileges, or benefits without earning them

Examples

Entitlement attitude
Her strong sense of entitlement often led to conflicts with her classmates.
Many people criticize the younger generation for their supposed entitlement.
A feeling of entitlement can prevent someone from appreciating what they have.

Related Terms

  • antonym: humility - the quality of having a modest view of one's importance
  • synonym: privilege - a special right or advantage available to a particular person or group
  • synonym: right - a moral or legal entitlement to have or do something
