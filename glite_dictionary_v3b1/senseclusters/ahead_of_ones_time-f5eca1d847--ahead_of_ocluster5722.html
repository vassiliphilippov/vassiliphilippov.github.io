ahead of one's time

adjective
more advanced than the era
2.5
Pronunciation: / əˈhɛd əv wʌnz taɪm / (AmE), / əˈhɛd əv wʌnz taɪm / (BrE)
having ideas, achievements, or qualities that are more advanced than those typical of the period in which one lives

Examples

Ahead of one's time ideas
Her approach to education was unconventional and ahead of her time.
Many consider Leonardo da Vinci to have been ahead of his time in both art and science.
The building's architecture was so modern that it was clearly ahead of its time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conservative - holding to traditional attitudes and values, resistant to change
  • synonym: progressive - favoring or implementing social reform or new, advanced ideas
