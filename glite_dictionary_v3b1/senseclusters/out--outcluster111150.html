out

adjective
no longer allowed to bat/run (sports)
1.0 5.4
Pronunciation: / aʊt / (AmE), / aʊt / (BrE)
(of a player in baseball, cricket, or similar sports) no longer allowed to continue batting or running, typically after being dismissed or put out by the opposing team.

Examples

Player is out
After missing the ball, she was declared out by the referee.
He tried to steal third base, but he was tagged out by the catcher.
The umpire called him out after the close play at first base.
