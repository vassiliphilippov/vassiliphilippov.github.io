lithographic

adjective
relating to lithography
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪθəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌlɪθəˈɡrafɪk / (BrE)
relating to, produced by, or involved in the process of lithography.

Examples

Lithographic printing process
A collection of rare lithographic posters was auctioned last week.
The artist displayed several lithographic prints at the gallery.
They use a lithographic process to reproduce fine art images.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lithography - the process of printing from a flat surface
