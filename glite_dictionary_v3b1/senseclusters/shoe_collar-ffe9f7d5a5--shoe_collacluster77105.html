shoe collar

noun
rim at the opening of a shoe or boot
Pronunciation: / ʃu ˈkɑlər / (AmE), / ʃu ˈkɒlə / (BrE)
The padded or stitched rim at the opening of a shoe or boot, often providing comfort and support around the ankle.
