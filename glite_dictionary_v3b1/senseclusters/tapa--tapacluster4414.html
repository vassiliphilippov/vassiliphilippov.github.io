tapa

noun
thin bark cloth from mulberry or similar trees
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːpə / (AmE), / ˈtɑːpə / (BrE)
a traditional cloth or material made from the bark of certain trees, especially in Polynesian or South Pacific cultures
