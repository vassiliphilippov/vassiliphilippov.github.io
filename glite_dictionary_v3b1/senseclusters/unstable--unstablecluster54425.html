Synonyms

  • unsteady
    3.3 2/m
    unsteady describes something that is not firm or balanced and may wobble, while unstable means not steady or likely to last.
  • precarious
    7.6 5/m
    precarious describes a dangerous, insecure position that may collapse, while unstable is a general word for not steady or secure.
  • shaky
    3.3 3/m
    shaky is informal and shows weakness or poor support, while unstable is a more neutral, wider term for not steady.
  • volatile
    7.0 6/m
    volatile means something changes quickly and unpredictably, while unstable covers being not steady, secure, or likely to last.
