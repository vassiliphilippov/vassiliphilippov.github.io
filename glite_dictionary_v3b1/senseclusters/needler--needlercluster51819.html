needler

noun
person or device using needles
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈniːdlər / (AmE), / ˈniːdlə / (BrE)
a person or device that uses or works with needles, especially for sewing or embroidery

Examples

Expert needler stitches
The skilled needler embroidered beautiful patterns on the fabric.
A modern needler can quickly stitch complex designs onto clothing.
She became a talented needler after years of practicing embroidery.
The tailor used a needler to finish the embroidery on the dress.
A modern needler can quickly insert hundreds of needles into fabric for quilting.
In science fiction stories, a needler is sometimes a weapon that fires tiny darts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embroiderer - a person who decorates fabric with needlework
  • synonym: needle - a thin, pointed tool used for sewing or injections
  • synonym: seamstress - a woman who sews, especially as a profession
  • synonym: sewing machine - a machine used to stitch fabric and other materials together with thread
