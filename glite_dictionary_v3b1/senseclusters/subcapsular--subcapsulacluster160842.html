subcapsular

adjective
beneath a capsule
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbˈkæpsjələr / (AmE), / ˌsʌbˈkæpsjʊlə / (BrE)
located or occurring beneath a capsule, especially in an organ or tissue

Examples

Subcapsular mass
The surgeon discovered a subcapsular mass during the operation.
A subcapsular hemorrhage can be difficult to detect without imaging tests.
The radiologist identified a subcapsular cyst on the patient's kidney scan.
