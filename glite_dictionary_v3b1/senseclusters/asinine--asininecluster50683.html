asinine

adjective
extremely silly or stupid
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈæsəˌnaɪn / (AmE), / ˈæsɪnʌɪn / (BrE)
extremely foolish, silly, or stupid; lacking good judgment.

Examples

An asinine mistake
It was an asinine decision to go hiking without any water.
She laughed at the asinine rumor that was spreading around the office.
Some people found his comments to be not just rude, but downright asinine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foolish - lacking good sense or judgment; unwise
  • antonym: sensible - showing good sense or judgment
