speculative

adjective
based on conjecture, not fact
4.4 5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈspek.jə.lə.tɪv/ /; / /ˈspek.jʊ.lə.tɪv/ (AmE), /ˈspek.jʊ.lə.tɪv/ /; / /ˈspek.jə.lə.tɪv/ (BrE)

Examples

Speculative theory
Her theory remains speculative because there is no concrete evidence to support it.
