slide projector

noun
device for projecting photographic slides
Pronunciation: / ˈslaɪd prəˌdʒɛktər / (AmE), / ˈslaɪd prəˌdʒɛktə / (BrE)
a device that projects an enlarged image of a photographic slide onto a screen

Examples

A slide projector device
Before digital presentations, conferences often relied on a slide projector for visual aids.
He found an old slide projector in the attic along with a box of family slides.
The teacher dimmed the lights and turned on the slide projector to display the photos.
