strummer

noun
person who plays strings
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈstrʌmər / (AmE), / ˈstrʌmə / (BrE)
a person who plays a stringed instrument by brushing or sweeping their hand or a pick across the strings

Examples

As a skilled strummer, she could play both fast and slow songs with ease.
Every evening, the young strummer practiced new chords on his old guitar.
The strummer entertained the crowd with lively folk songs at the festival.
As a skilled strummer, she could make even simple chords sound beautiful.
Many street performers are talented strummers who play for passersby.
