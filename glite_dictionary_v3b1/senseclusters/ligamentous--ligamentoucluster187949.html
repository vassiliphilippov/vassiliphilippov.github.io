ligamentous

adjective
like a ligament
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪɡəˈmɛntəs / (AmE), / ˌlɪɡəˈmɛntəs / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a ligament in the body

Examples

Ligamentous swelling
The doctor noted ligamentous swelling around the patient's ankle after the accident.
Physical therapy focused on improving ligamentous strength to support the injured joint.
An MRI revealed ligamentous damage that required several weeks of rest and recovery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tendinous - relating to or resembling a tendon
