intranuclear

adjective
within cell nucleus
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈnuːkliər / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈnjuːkliə / (BrE)
located or occurring within the nucleus of a cell

Examples

Intranuclear inclusions
The pathologist identified intranuclear inclusions in the tissue sample.
Certain viruses can cause intranuclear changes visible under a microscope.
Researchers studied the intranuclear transport of proteins in cancer cells.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extranuclear - located or occurring outside the nucleus of a cell
  • synonym: intracellular - located or occurring within a cell
