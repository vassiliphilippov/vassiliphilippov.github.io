muse

noun
source of artistic inspiration
Pronunciation: / mjuz / (AmE), / mjuːz / (BrE)
a person or personified force that is the source of inspiration for an artist, writer, or thinker

Examples

An artist's muse
Many poets have found their muse in nature and the changing seasons.
She became the muse for several of the artist’s most famous paintings.
Throughout his career, his wife was his greatest muse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inspiration - the process of being mentally stimulated to do or feel something, especially to do something creative
