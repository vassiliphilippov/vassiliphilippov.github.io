pyrenoid

noun
algal cell structure
9.9
Pronunciation: / paɪˈriːnɔɪd / (AmE), / paɪˈriːnɔɪd / (BrE)
a microscopic structure in algae and some plants that helps with carbon fixation during photosynthesis

Examples

A pyrenoid in algae
The scientist identified a single pyrenoid in the center of the algal chloroplast.
Researchers study the pyrenoid to understand how algae capture carbon dioxide.
Under the microscope, the pyrenoid appeared as a dense, round body within the cell.
