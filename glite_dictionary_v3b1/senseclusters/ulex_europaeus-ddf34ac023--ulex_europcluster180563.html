Ulex europaeus

noun
spiny evergreen shrub (common gorse)
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːlɛks ˌjʊˌroʊˈpeɪəs / (AmE), / ˈjuːlɛks ˌjʊərəˈpiːəs / (BrE)
a very spiny, dense evergreen shrub with fragrant golden-yellow flowers, common throughout western Europe, also known as common gorse

Examples

Ulex europaeus shrub
Beekeepers value Ulex europaeus for its abundance of early-season flowers.
Many walkers avoid paths thick with Ulex europaeus because of its sharp spines.
The hillsides were covered in Ulex europaeus, lending a brilliant yellow hue to the landscape.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gorse - a spiny shrub with yellow flowers, especially common in western Europe
