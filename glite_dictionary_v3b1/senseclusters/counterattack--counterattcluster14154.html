Examples

A sudden counterattack
After retreating briefly, the team mounted a fierce counterattack that surprised their opponents.
The army launched a swift counterattack after their defensive line was breached.
The general's strategy relied on a timely counterattack to regain control of the city.
A well-timed counterattack can quickly turn the tables in a chess match.
After losing his queen's pawn, he launched a counterattack on the kingside.
