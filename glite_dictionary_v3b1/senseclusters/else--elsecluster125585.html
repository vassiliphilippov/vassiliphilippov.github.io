else

adverb
in addition; different
Pronunciation: / ɛls / (AmE), / ɛls / (BrE)
used to refer to a different or additional person, thing, or situation apart from the one already mentioned

Examples

Something else
If you do not like this color, we can try something else.
Is there anyone else coming to the meeting today?
She wanted to go somewhere else for vacation this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: other - different or additional person or thing
  • antonym: same - not different; identical
