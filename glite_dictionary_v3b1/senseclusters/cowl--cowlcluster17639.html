cowl

noun
a loose hooded robe, esp. worn by monks
Pronunciation: / kaʊl / (AmE), / kaʊl / (BrE)
a loose hood or hooded robe, especially one worn by a monk

Examples

A monk's cowl
A heavy woolen cowl protected him from the mountain wind.
She admired the intricate embroidery on the cowl of the old religious robe.
The monk pulled his cowl over his head as he entered the dim chapel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hood - a covering for the head and neck with an opening for the face
