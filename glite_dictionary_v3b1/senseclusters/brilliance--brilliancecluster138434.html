brilliance

noun
magnificence or splendour
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪliəns / (AmE), / ˈbrɪljəns / (BrE)
the quality of being magnificent, splendid, or grand in appearance or effect

Examples

The brilliance of the show
Critics praised the brilliance of the production's set design and costumes.
The artist's use of color added a sense of brilliance to the gallery room.
The palace's brilliance impressed all who attended the royal ball.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grandeur - impressive beauty or scale
  • antonym: mediocrity - the quality of being average or unremarkable
  • synonym: splendor - magnificent and splendid appearance; grandeur
