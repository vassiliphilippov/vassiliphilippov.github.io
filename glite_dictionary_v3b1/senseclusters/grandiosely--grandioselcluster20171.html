grandiosely

adverb
in a grandiose or pretentious manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræn.di.oʊsli / (AmE), / ˈɡræn.di.əʊsli / (BrE)
In a manner that is grandiose; in an exaggeratedly impressive or pretentious way.

Examples

To grandiosely announce
He grandiosely announced his arrival as if expecting a fanfare.
The lobby was grandiosely decorated with gilded statues and towering columns.
The politician spoke grandiosely about his plans to change the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grandiosely - in an exaggeratedly impressive or grandiose manner
  • antonym: modestly - in a humble or unassuming manner
