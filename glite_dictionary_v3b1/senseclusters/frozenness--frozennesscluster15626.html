frozenness

noun
state of being frozen
Pronunciation: / ˈfroʊzənnəs / (AmE), / ˈfrəʊzənnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being frozen or extremely cold

Examples

Frozenness of the ground
The frozenness of the ground made it impossible to dig a hole for the fence post.
She was surprised by the frozenness of the air when she stepped outside in January.
The frozenness of his expression revealed no emotion during the tense meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: iciness - the state of being icy or very cold
  • antonym: warmth - the state of being warm or having heat
