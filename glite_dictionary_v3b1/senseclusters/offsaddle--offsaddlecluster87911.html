offsaddle

verb
remove a saddle from a horse
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɔfˈsædəl / (AmE), / ˌɒfˈsædəl / (BrE)
to remove the saddle from (a horse or other riding animal)

Examples

To offsaddle the horse
After a long ride, we stopped to offsaddle the horses and let them rest.
He always makes sure to offsaddle carefully to avoid startling his mare.
The children watched as the ranch hands offsaddled the tired ponies at sunset.
