myometrium

noun
uterine muscle layer
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪ.oʊˈmiː.tɹi.əm / (AmE), / ˌmaɪ.əʊˈmiː.tɹi.əm / (BrE)
the smooth muscle layer forming the wall of the uterus

Examples

The myometrium layer
Contractions of the myometrium are responsible for labor during childbirth.
Doctors examined the thickness of the patient's myometrium using ultrasound imaging.
During pregnancy, the myometrium undergoes significant changes to accommodate the growing fetus.
