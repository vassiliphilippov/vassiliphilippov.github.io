turn off the light

verb
switch a light off
Pronunciation: / tɝn ɔf ðə laɪt / (AmE), / tɜːn ɒf ðə laɪt / (BrE)
to switch a light off so that it stops producing light

Examples

To turn off the light switch
Before leaving the room, please turn off the light to save energy.
He turned off the light and immediately the room was plunged into darkness.
You should always turn off the light when you don't need it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: switch off the light - to turn a light off by means of a switch
  • antonym: turn on the light - to switch a light on so that it produces light
