housewife

noun
married woman managing household
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊsˌwaɪf / (AmE), / ˈhaʊs.wɪf / (BrE)
A married woman whose main occupation is managing the household and taking care of domestic affairs while her spouse earns the family's income.

Examples

A housewife at home
After leaving her job, she became a housewife and focused on raising her children.
Many housewives manage their family’s finances with great efficiency.
She described her daily routine as a housewife during the interview.

Related Terms

  • synonym: homemaker - a person who manages the household, not specifying gender
