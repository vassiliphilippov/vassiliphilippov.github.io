Dalmatian iris

noun
European iris with lilac-blue flowers
Pronunciation: / dælˈmeɪʃən ˈaɪrɪs / (AmE), / dælˈmeɪʃən ˈaɪrɪs / (BrE)
A European species of iris with soft lilac-blue flowers, native to the Dalmatia region.
