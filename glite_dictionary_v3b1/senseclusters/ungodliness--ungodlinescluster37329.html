ungodliness

noun
lack of religious piety or reverence
4.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡɑdlinəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡɒdlɪnɪs / (BrE)
lack of reverence or respect for God or religion; impiety or wickedness.

Examples

Signs of ungodliness
Many believed that the city's problems stemmed from moral decay and ungodliness.
She was troubled by what she saw as growing ungodliness in society.
The preacher warned the congregation against ungodliness and encouraged them to lead virtuous lives.

Related Terms

  • antonym: godliness - the quality of being devoutly religious or pious
  • synonym: impiety - lack of piety; irreverence toward God or religion
