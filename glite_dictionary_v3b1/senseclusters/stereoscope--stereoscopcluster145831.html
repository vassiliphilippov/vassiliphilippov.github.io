stereoscope

noun
optical device for viewing 3D images
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛriəˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˈstɪəriəˌskəʊp / (BrE)
an optical device used for viewing two photographs of the same object taken at different angles, creating a three-dimensional effect

Examples

A stereoscope device
A modern virtual reality headset is much more sophisticated than a traditional stereoscope.
She inherited her grandfather's collection of stereoscope cards and the device itself.
The antique stereoscope allowed people to see images in stunning three-dimensional detail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: viewfinder - device for framing and focusing an image in photography or optics
