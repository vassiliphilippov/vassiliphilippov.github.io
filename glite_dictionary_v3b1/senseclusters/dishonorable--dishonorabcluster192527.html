dishonorable

adjective
lacking honor or integrity
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈɑnərəbəl /; / dɪsˈɑːnərəbəl / (AmE), / dɪsˈɒnərəbəl / (BrE)
lacking honor, integrity, or moral principles; deserving shame or disgrace
