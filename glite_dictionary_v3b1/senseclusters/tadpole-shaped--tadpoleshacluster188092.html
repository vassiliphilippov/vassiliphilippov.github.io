tadpole-shaped

adjective
resembling the shape of a tadpole
Pronunciation: / ˈtædˌpoʊl ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈtadˌpəʊl ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape of a tadpole, typically with a rounded head and a tapering tail-like extension.

Examples

Tadpole-shaped bacteria
Some bacteria are tadpole-shaped, possessing a rounded front and an elongated rear.
The artist designed a sculpture that was distinctly tadpole-shaped, with a bulbous head and a narrow tail.
The designer's new lamp featured a tadpole-shaped base that caught everyone's attention.
