performance

noun
public presentation of entertainment
1.0
Pronunciation: / pərˈfɔrməns /; / pɚˈfɔrməns / (AmE), / pəˈfɔːməns / (BrE)
an act of presenting a play, concert, or other entertainment to an audience

Examples

The orchestra delivered a stunning performance that captivated the entire audience.
Tickets for tonight’s performance of the play have already sold out.
