chamber

verb
place or load in a chamber
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪmbɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪmbə / (BrE)
to place or load into a chamber, especially to load a cartridge into the chamber of a firearm

Examples

To chamber a round
Always make sure the weapon is not chambered before cleaning it.
He carefully chambered a round before aiming the rifle.
She quickly chambered another shell during the shooting competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: load - to put a charge, cartridge, or round into a firearm or device
