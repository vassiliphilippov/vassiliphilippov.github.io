indexable

adjective
can be linked to index
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndɛksəbəl / (AmE), / ˈɪndɛksəbəl / (BrE)
capable of being adjusted or linked to a financial or price index, such as inflation

Examples

Indexable bond
The company offers indexable bonds that increase in value with inflation.
Many retirement plans now include indexable benefits to protect against rising prices.
Some contracts have indexable payments that change according to the consumer price index.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adjustable - able to be changed or modified
  • antonym: fixed - not able to be changed or adjusted
