LQG controller

noun
optimal control system combining estimator and regulator
Pronunciation: / ɛl kju dʒi kənˈtroʊlɚ / (AmE), / ɛl kjuː dʒi kənˈtrəʊlə / (BrE)
a controller that combines a linear-quadratic estimator (Kalman filter) with a linear-quadratic regulator to optimally control dynamic systems under uncertainty

Examples

An LQG controller design
Designing an LQG controller requires precise modeling of the system dynamics and uncertainties.
The engineer implemented an LQG controller to stabilize the robot's movement.
With an LQG controller, the system can handle noise and disturbances effectively.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Kalman filter - an estimator used in LQG controller design
  • synonym: LQR - linear-quadratic regulator; a key component of LQG control
