earthmover

noun
heavy construction machine
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrθˌmuːvər / (AmE), / ˈɜːθˌmuːvə / (BrE)
a large machine or person that moves large amounts of soil, rock, or other material, especially in construction or landscaping

Examples

The construction site was busy with a large earthmover clearing the ground for the new building.
An experienced earthmover can shape the landscape quickly and efficiently.
They rented an earthmover to dig the foundation for their new house.
