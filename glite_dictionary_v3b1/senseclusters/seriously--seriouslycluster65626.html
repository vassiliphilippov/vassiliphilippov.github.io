seriously

adverb
in a serious manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪriəsli / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriəsli / (BrE)
In a serious or earnest manner; with seriousness and not joking.

Examples

Seriously mean it
Are you seriously telling me you’ve never been abroad?
He looked at me seriously before answering the question.
She spoke seriously about her plans for the future.

Related Terms

  • synonym: earnestly - in an earnest or sincere manner
  • antonym: jokingly - in a joking or playful manner
