compañero

noun
roommate or housemate
Pronunciation: / kəmˌpɑːnˈjeɪroʊ / (AmE), / kəmˌpænˈjeərəʊ / (BrE)
a person who shares a home, apartment, or living space with another; a roommate or housemate

Examples

Apartment compañero
My compañero from the apartment building brought extra coffee this morning.
After moving to Madrid, I quickly became friends with my new compañero.
Each compañero is responsible for cleaning the kitchen once a week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: housemate - a person you share a house with
  • synonym: roommate - a person you share a room or apartment with
