rattlebrained

adjective
lacking good sense; scatterbrained
Pronunciation: / ˈrætlˌbreɪnd / (AmE), / ˈræt.əl.breɪnd / (BrE)
Lacking good sense or discretion; scatterbrained or foolish.

Examples

A rattlebrained person
He admitted his rattlebrained behavior was due to lack of sleep.
Her rattlebrained ideas often got her into trouble at work.
It's hard to make progress in a meeting with so many rattlebrained suggestions.

  • antonym: sensible - showing good sense
