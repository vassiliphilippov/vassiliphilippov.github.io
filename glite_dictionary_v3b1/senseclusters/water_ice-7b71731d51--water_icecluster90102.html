water ice

noun
frozen dessert made from fruit juice, no milk
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚ aɪs / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə aɪs / (BrE)
A frozen dessert containing no milk, typically made from fruit juice and sugar, with a soft, mushy consistency.

Examples

A water ice dessert

Philadelphia is famous for its delicious water ice stands.
The kids enjoyed a lemon water ice after playing in the park.
