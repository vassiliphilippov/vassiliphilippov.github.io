birdcall

noun
the sound or song made by a bird
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝdˌkɔl / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdˌkɔːl / (BrE)
the characteristic sound or song made by a bird, typically used to communicate with others of its species

Examples

A birdcall song
Each species has its own distinct birdcall for attracting mates or warning of danger.
The forest was alive with the birdcall of robins and sparrows in the early morning.
We woke up to the beautiful birdcall outside our window.

Related Terms

  • synonym: birdsongs - the musical sounds produced by birds, often more structured than calls
  • synonym: chirp - a short, sharp sound made by small birds
