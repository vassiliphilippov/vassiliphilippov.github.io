concertedly

adverb
in a coordinated way
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɝːtɪdli / (AmE), / kənˈsɜːtɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that is done together with others or in a coordinated manner

Examples

To act concertedly together
The volunteers acted concertedly to organize the relief effort after the storm.
Both companies worked concertedly to develop a new product for the market.
The protesters concertedly voiced their concerns at the city council meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: independently - without the involvement or help of others
  • synonym: jointly - in cooperation with others
