multimodality

noun
use of multiple modes
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˌmoʊˈdæləti / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˌməʊˈdælɪti / (BrE)
the use or presence of multiple modes, methods, or forms, especially in communication or analysis

Examples

Multimodality in learning
Modern education often relies on multimodality, combining text, images, and audio to enhance learning.
The artist's exhibition was praised for its multimodality, blending sculpture, video, and sound.
Researchers studied multimodality in online communication, analyzing how people use emojis, text, and images together.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monomodality - the use of a single mode or method
  • synonym: multimedia - the use of different types of media together
