plughole

noun
hole in a sink or bath for draining water
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈplʌɡˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈplʌɡ.həʊl / (BrE)
the hole in a sink, bath, or similar container, into which a plug fits and through which water drains away

Examples

The plughole in sink
He dropped his ring, and it almost slipped into the plughole.
Please check if the plughole is blocked, as the sink isn't emptying properly.
The water swirled around the plughole before disappearing down the drain.
