cis

adjective
gender identity matches birth sex
7.0
Pronunciation: / sɪs / (AmE), / sɪs / (BrE)
describing a person whose gender identity matches the sex they were assigned at birth

Examples

cis gender person
He is cis and has always identified with the gender he was assigned at birth.
Some people are cis, while others are transgender or non-binary.
The survey asked whether participants identified as cis or trans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cisgender - having a gender identity that matches one's sex assigned at birth
  • antonym: trans - describing a person whose gender identity does not match their sex assigned at birth
