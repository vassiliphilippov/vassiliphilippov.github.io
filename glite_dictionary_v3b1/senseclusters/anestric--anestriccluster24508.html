anestric

adjective
relating to anestrus (sexual inactivity)
Pronunciation: / əˈnɛstrɪk / (AmE), / əˈnɛstrɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of anestrus, the period of sexual inactivity in female mammals.

Examples

Anestric period
Anestric females typically do not display any mating behavior until their cycle resumes.
Diet changes can trigger hormonal shifts in anestric animals, leading to earlier ovulation.
The veterinarian noted that the cow was in an anestric phase and not ready to breed.
