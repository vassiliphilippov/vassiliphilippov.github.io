intradural

adjective
within dura mater
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈdʊrəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈdjʊərəl / (BrE)
located or occurring within the dura mater of the brain or spinal cord

Examples

Intradural lesion
The MRI scan revealed an intradural lesion compressing the spinal cord.
Surgical removal of intradural tumors requires careful planning to avoid nerve damage.
Doctors discussed the risks associated with operating on intradural abnormalities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extradural - located outside the dura mater
  • antonym: subdural - located beneath the dura mater
