para

noun
soldier in the paratroops
Pronunciation: / ˈpærə / (AmE), / ˈpærə / (BrE)
a soldier who is trained to parachute into an operation, especially as a member of a paratroop regiment

Many paras take part in peacekeeping missions around the world.
