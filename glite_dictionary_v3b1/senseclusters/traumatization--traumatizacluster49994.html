traumatization

noun
causing trauma
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌtrɔːmətəˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌtrɔmətəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌtrɔːmətʌɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or condition of experiencing psychological or emotional trauma, often resulting in lasting mental or emotional harm
