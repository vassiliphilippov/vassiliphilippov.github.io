rock-bottom price

noun
lowest possible price
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌrɑk ˈbɑtəm ˈpraɪs / (AmE), / ˌrɒk ˈbɒtəm ˈpraɪs / (BrE)
the lowest possible price; a price as low as it can be

Examples

A rock-bottom price offer

Online retailers often attract customers by offering a rock-bottom price on electronics.
The store is advertising a rock-bottom price on last year's model.
