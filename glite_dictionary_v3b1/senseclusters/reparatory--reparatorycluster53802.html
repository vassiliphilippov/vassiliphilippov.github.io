reparatory

adjective
making amends
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpærəˌtɔri / (AmE), / rɪˈpærətəri / (BrE)
intended to make amends or provide compensation for a wrong or harm done

Examples

Reparatory justice
The government introduced reparatory policies to address past injustices against the community.
A reparatory payment was made to the victims as part of the settlement.
The organization launched a reparatory program to help those affected by the disaster.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compensatory - serving to make up for a loss or injury
  • antonym: punitive - intended as punishment
