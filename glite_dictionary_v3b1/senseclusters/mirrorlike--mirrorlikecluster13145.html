mirrorlike

adjective
resembling a mirror in reflectiveness
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪrərˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɪrəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Reflecting light in the way a mirror does; having a smooth, shiny surface that produces clear reflections.

Examples

Mirrorlike surface
The new skyscraper's windows had a mirrorlike appearance, reflecting the entire city skyline.
The pond was so still that its mirrorlike surface reflected the sky perfectly.
They admired the mirrorlike shine of the polished floor.

  • synonym: specular - reflecting light, mirror-like
