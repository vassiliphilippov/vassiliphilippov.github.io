cornflower

noun
plant with yellow flowers (U.S.)
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnˌflaʊə / (BrE)
a plant with colorful flowers, often grown in gardens; especially the blue-flowered European species or similar plants
