homo species

noun
species in the genus Homo
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmoʊ ˈspiːʃiz / (AmE), / ˈhəʊməʊ ˈspiːʃiːz / (BrE)
any of the species that belong to the genus Homo, including modern humans and their extinct relatives

Examples

Extinct homo species
Neanderthals and Denisovans are extinct homo species closely related to modern humans.
Researchers compared DNA samples from different homo species to trace migration patterns.
The study of ancient homo species reveals much about human evolution.
