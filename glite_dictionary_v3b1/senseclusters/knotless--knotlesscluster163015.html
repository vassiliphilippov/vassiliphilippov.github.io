knotless

adjective
without knots
4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈnɑːt.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈnɒt.ləs/ (BrE)
designed to function or hold securely without the need for tying knots, especially in medical or surgical contexts

Examples

Knotless suture technique
The doctor chose a knotless suture to reduce irritation in the patient's wound.
Modern surgical techniques often use knotless anchors for faster and more secure repairs.
A knotless design can help prevent complications caused by bulky knots in delicate tissues.
