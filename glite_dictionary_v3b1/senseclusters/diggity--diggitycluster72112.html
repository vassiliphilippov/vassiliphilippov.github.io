diggity

noun
slang for emphasis
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdɪ.gɪ.tiː/ /; / /ˈdɪ.gə.tiː/ (AmE), /ˈdɪ.gɪ.tiː/ /; / /ˈdɪ.gə.tiː/ (BrE)
