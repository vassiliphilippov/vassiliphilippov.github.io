diggity

noun
slang for emphasis
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪɡɪti / (AmE), / ˈdɪɡɪti / (BrE)
a playful slang word used for emphasis, especially in the phrase 'no diggity' to mean 'no doubt' or 'certainly'.

Examples

No diggity phrase
That movie was amazing, no diggity.
He finished the race first, diggity and all.
If you want the best pizza in town, this is it, no diggity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: definitely - without any doubt
  • synonym: no doubt - certainly; without question
