resonance

noun
deep, full, reverberating sound quality
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛzənəns / (AmE), / ˈrɛzənəns / (BrE)
the quality in a sound of being deep, full, and reverberating

Examples

Deep resonance
She was captivated by the deep resonance of the church bells ringing in the morning.
The resonance of the singer's voice filled the entire concert hall.
Wooden instruments are prized for the rich resonance they bring to the music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: echo - a reflected sound that is heard again
  • synonym: reverberation - the persistence of sound after the source is removed
