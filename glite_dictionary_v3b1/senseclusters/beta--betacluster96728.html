beta

adjective
second in rank or importance
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪ.tə / (AmE), / ˈbiː.tə / (BrE)
Second in rank, importance, or quality; not the most dominant or primary.

Examples

Beta position
As a beta candidate, she knew the odds of winning were slim compared to the favorite.
He played a beta role in the project, supporting rather than directing the team.
In the corporate hierarchy, she was often viewed as a beta leader rather than the top decision-maker.
