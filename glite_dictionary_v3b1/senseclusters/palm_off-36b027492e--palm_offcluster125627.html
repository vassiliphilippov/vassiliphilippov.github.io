palm off

verb
deceive into accepting something false
8.1 6.3
Pronunciation: /pɑːm ɔːf/ /; / /pɑːlm ɔːf/ (AmE), /pɑːm ɒf/ /; / /pɑːm ɔːf/ (BrE)
