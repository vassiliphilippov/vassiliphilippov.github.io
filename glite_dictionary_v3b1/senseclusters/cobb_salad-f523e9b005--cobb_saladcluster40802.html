Cobb salad

noun
American chopped salad
2.5
Pronunciation: / kɑb ˈsæləd / (AmE), / kɒb ˈsæləd / (BrE)
a salad made with chopped lettuce, chicken, bacon, hard-boiled egg, tomato, avocado, and blue cheese

Examples

A Cobb salad with chicken
I ordered a Cobb salad for lunch because I wanted something filling and fresh.
The restaurant's signature dish is their famous Cobb salad with homemade blue cheese dressing.
She made a large Cobb salad for the family picnic, adding extra avocado and bacon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Caesar salad - a salad with romaine lettuce, croutons, and Caesar dressing
  • synonym: chef's salad - a mixed salad with meats, cheese, and vegetables
