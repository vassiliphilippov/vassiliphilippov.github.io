starving

adjective
suffering from extreme hunger
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɑːvɪŋ / (BrE)
suffering severely or dying from lack of food; extremely hungry

Examples

A starving person
After three days lost in the woods, the hikers were starving and desperate for food.
I’m absolutely starving—let’s get something to eat right away.
The stray dog looked starving and in need of help.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: famished - extremely hungry, often after a long period without food
  • degree lower: hungry - feeling or showing the need for food
