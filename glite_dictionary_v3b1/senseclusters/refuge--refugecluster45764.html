refuge

noun
a source of help or protection
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛf.judʒ / (AmE), / ˈrɛf.juːdʒ / (BrE)
a person, place, thing, or situation that provides safety, protection, or relief from danger or trouble

Examples

A refuge from danger
In times of crisis, close friends became his refuge from the outside world.
Many see music as a refuge during stressful periods.
She found refuge in a quiet café when the storm began.
Her garden was a peaceful refuge from the stress of everyday life.
Many people sought refuge from the war in neighboring countries.
The hikers found refuge in a cave during the storm.
During the storm, many families found refuge in the local community center.
The cave provided refuge for hikers caught in the sudden downpour.
Wild animals sought refuge among the thick bushes to escape predators.

Related Terms

  • antonym: danger - the possibility of harm or injury; the opposite of safety or protection

  • synonym: haven - a place of safety or refuge
  • antonym: peril - serious and immediate danger
  • synonym: sanctuary - a place of refuge or safety
  • synonym: shelter - a place giving protection from bad weather or danger
