Synonyms

  • sanctuary
    4.1 4.03/m
    sanctuary is often a protected or holy place for people or animals in danger, while refuge is any safe shelter.
  • asylum
    5.7 7.42/m
    asylum is a place that gives legal or official protection to people in danger, while refuge is any shelter.
  • safe harbor
    3.9 0.08/m
    safe harbor is a legal or figurative place of protection used in law and business, while refuge means simple shelter.
  • harbor of refuge
