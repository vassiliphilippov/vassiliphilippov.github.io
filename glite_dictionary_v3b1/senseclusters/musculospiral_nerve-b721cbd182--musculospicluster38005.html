musculospiral nerve

noun
major nerve of the arm (radial nerve)
9.9
Pronunciation: /mʌs.kjʊ.loʊ.ˈspaɪr.əl nɜːrv/ /; / /mʌs.kə.loʊ.ˈspaɪr.əl nɜːrv/ (AmE), /mʌs.kjʊ.lə.ˈspaɪ.rəl nɜːv/ /; / /mʌs.kə.lə.ˈspaɪ.rəl nɜːv/ (BrE)
