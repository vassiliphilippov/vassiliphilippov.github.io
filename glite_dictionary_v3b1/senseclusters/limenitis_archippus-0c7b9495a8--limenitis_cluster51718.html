Limenitis archippus

noun
a North American butterfly resembling the monarch
Pronunciation: / lɪˈmɛnɪtɪs ɑːrˈkɪpəs / (AmE), / lɪˈmɛnɪtɪs ɑːˈkɪpəs / (BrE)
a North American butterfly, commonly called the viceroy, that resembles the monarch butterfly but is smaller and has a distinct black line across its hind wings
