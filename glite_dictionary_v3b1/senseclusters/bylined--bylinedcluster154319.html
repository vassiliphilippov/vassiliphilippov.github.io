bylined

adjective
credited as author
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪlaɪnd / (AmE), / ˈbaɪlaɪnd / (BrE)
credited with authorship in a publication, especially by having one's name printed with an article or story

Examples

Bylined article
Her first bylined story appeared in the Sunday magazine.
The journalist was thrilled to see his work bylined in a national newspaper.
Many writers hope to have a bylined article published early in their careers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anonymous - not named or identified as author
