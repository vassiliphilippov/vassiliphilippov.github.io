William Penn

proper noun
English Quaker; founder of Pennsylvania
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪljəm pɛn / (AmE), / ˈwɪljəm pɛn / (BrE)
(1644–1718) An English Quaker and founder of the American colony of Pennsylvania.
