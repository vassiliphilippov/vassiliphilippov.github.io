chocolate soufflé

noun
light, fluffy dessert made with chocolate
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑk.lət suˈfleɪ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒk.lət suːˈfleɪ / (BrE)
A light, fluffy baked dessert made with chocolate, typically consisting of egg yolks and beaten egg whites.

Examples

A chocolate soufflé dessert
Many chefs consider the perfect chocolate soufflé a test of their baking skills.
She ordered a chocolate soufflé for dessert at the restaurant.
The chocolate soufflé rose beautifully in the oven, just as the recipe promised.
