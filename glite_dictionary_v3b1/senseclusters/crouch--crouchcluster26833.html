crouch

noun
the act of bending low with limbs close to the body
3.6
Pronunciation: / kraʊtʃ / (AmE), / kraʊtʃ / (BrE)
a position in which the body is bent low with the limbs close to the torso, typically in preparation for movement or to avoid being seen

Examples

A low crouch
His sudden crouch behind the sofa saved him from being seen.
She stayed in a crouch to avoid attracting attention.
The runner waited in a crouch before the starting gun sounded.

Related Terms

  • synonym: squat - a posture where the knees are bent and the body is lowered close to the ground
