antimicrobial

adjective
stopping or killing microorganisms
Pronunciation: / ˌæntɪmaɪˈkroʊbiəl / (AmE), / ˌæntɪmaɪˈkrəʊbiəl / (BrE)
Capable of destroying or inhibiting the growth of disease-causing microorganisms.

Examples

Antimicrobial soap
Many soaps advertise their antimicrobial properties to attract health-conscious consumers.
Proper use of antimicrobial agents is crucial to prevent drug resistance.
The doctor prescribed an antimicrobial ointment to prevent infection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antibiotic - a substance that kills or inhibits the growth of bacteria
  • synonym: antiviral - a drug that suppresses the growth of viruses
