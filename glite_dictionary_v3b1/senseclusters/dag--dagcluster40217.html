dag

noun
matted wool; awkward person
Pronunciation: / dæɡ / (AmE), / dæɡ / (BrE)
a piece of matted or dirty wool on a sheep, or in Australian and New Zealand slang, a socially awkward or unfashionable person

Examples

Sheep dag removal
The farmer carefully removed the dag from the sheep's fleece before shearing.
In Australia, calling someone a dag can be a lighthearted way to say they are quirky.
He wore mismatched socks and told silly jokes, making him a real dag among his friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clod - a lump of earth or a dull, awkward person
  • synonym: nerd - a socially awkward or unfashionable person
