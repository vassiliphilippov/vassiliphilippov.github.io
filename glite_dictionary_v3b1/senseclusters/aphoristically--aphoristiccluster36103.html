aphoristically

adverb
in a concise, clever way
Pronunciation: / ˌæfərˈɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌæfərˈɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that expresses ideas concisely and cleverly, like an aphorism

Examples

Explain aphoristically
The professor explained the concept aphoristically, making it easy for everyone to remember.
He often writes aphoristically, packing deep meaning into just a few words.
Her speech was delivered aphoristically, with each sentence sounding like a wise saying.
