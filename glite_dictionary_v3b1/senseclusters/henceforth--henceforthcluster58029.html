From this time forward; starting now or from now on.

Examples

From henceforth forward
Henceforth, all employees must wear identification badges while in the building.
He declared that henceforth he would live a more honest life.
The two countries announced that they would henceforth cooperate on a range of environmental issues.

Synonyms

  • henceforward
    7.5 0.08/m
    henceforward is a formal written way to say from now on, while henceforth is the more common formal adverb.
  • hereafter
    5.3 1.46/m
    hereafter is often used in legal documents and formal texts, while henceforth is used in general statements about the future.
  • onwards
    2.2 1.48/m
    onwards is a more casual adverb that needs a start point like 'from now', while henceforth stands alone to mean from this time.
  • hence
    4.4 1.12/m
    hence often means 'for that reason' or marks time in set phrases, while henceforth clearly means from now on.
