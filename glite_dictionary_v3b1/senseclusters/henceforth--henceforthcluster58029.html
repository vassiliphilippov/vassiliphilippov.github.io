henceforth

adverb
from this time forward
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛnsˌfɔrθ / (AmE), / ˈhɛnsˌfɔːθ / (BrE)
From this time forward; starting now or from now on.

Examples

From henceforth forward
Henceforth, all employees must wear identification badges while in the building.
He declared that henceforth he would live a more honest life.
The two countries announced that they would henceforth cooperate on a range of environmental issues.
