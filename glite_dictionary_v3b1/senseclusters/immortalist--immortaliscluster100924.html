immortalist

noun
person claiming immortality
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɔːrtəlɪst / (AmE), / ɪˈmɔːtəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who is, or claims to be, immortal, especially in fiction or legend

Examples

A legendary immortalist
The mysterious immortalist claimed to have witnessed the rise and fall of ancient empires.
In the novel, an immortalist struggles with the loneliness of endless life.
Legends spoke of an immortalist who could not be harmed by time or disease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immortal - a being who lives forever and cannot die
  • antonym: mortal - a being who is subject to death
