watering

noun
the act of supplying water
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of wetting or supplying with water, especially to plants or land

Examples

Plant watering
Regular watering is essential for keeping these houseplants healthy.
The schedule called for watering the garden twice a week during summer.
Without proper watering, the crops quickly wilted in the heat.
