Chinese puzzle

noun
complex, ingenious puzzle, often with hidden compartments
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪˌniz ˈpʌzəl / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪniːz ˈpʌzl̩ / (BrE)
