sacking

noun
dismissal from employment
Pronunciation: / ˈsækɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsækɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of dismissing someone from their job; being fired or removed from employment

Examples

A sudden sacking
After the scandal, many employees feared a sudden sacking from their positions.
His repeated lateness eventually led to his sacking from the firm.
The company announced the sacking of several senior managers last week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dismissal - official removal from employment or position
  • synonym: firing - removal from a job or position, especially suddenly
  • antonym: hiring - the act of giving someone a job
