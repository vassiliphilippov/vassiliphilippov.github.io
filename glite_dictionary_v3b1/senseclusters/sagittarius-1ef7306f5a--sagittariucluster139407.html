Sagittarius

noun
person born under Sagittarius sign
Pronunciation: / ˌsædʒ.əˈter.i.əs / (AmE), / ˌsædʒ.ɪˈteə.ri.əs / (BrE)
(Astrology) A person born when the sun is in the zodiac sign Sagittarius, typically between November 22 and December 21.

Examples

A Sagittarius person
As a Sagittarius, he is known for being adventurous and optimistic.
Her best friend is a Sagittarius who loves to travel and try new things.
She always joked that being a Sagittarius made her open-minded and honest.
