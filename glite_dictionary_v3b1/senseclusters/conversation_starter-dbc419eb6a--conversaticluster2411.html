conversation starter

noun
something that initiates conversation
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːnvərˈseɪʃən ˌstɑːrtər / (AmE), / ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃən ˌstɑːtə / (BrE)
something that initiates or encourages people to start a conversation, such as a topic, remark, or object.

Examples

A conversation starter topic
He always brings up travel stories as a conversation starter when meeting new people.
That painting on the wall is a great conversation starter at dinner parties.
Wearing an unusual hat can be an effective conversation starter at social events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: icebreaker - an activity or remark intended to relieve tension and encourage conversation
