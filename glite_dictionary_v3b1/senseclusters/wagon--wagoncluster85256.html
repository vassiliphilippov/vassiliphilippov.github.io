wagon

noun
wheeled vehicle for carrying goods or people
Pronunciation: / ˈwæɡən / (AmE), / ˈwæɡən / (BrE)
a vehicle with four wheels, usually pulled by animals or a tractor, used for transporting goods or people

Examples

A farm wagon
The farmer loaded hay onto the wagon and drove it back to the barn.
The old wagon creaked as it traveled down the dirt road.
Tourists rode through the park in a horse-drawn wagon during the festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cart - a small open vehicle with two or four wheels, typically pulled by a horse or a person
