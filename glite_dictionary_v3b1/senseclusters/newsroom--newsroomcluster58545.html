newsroom

noun
office for journalistic news processing
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːzˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌruːm / (BrE)
an office or area in a newspaper, news agency, or broadcasting station where news is gathered, written, and edited before publication or broadcast

Examples

Busy newsroom
She started her career as an intern in the newsroom of a major newspaper.
Technological advances have dramatically changed how a newsroom operates in the digital age.
There was a sense of urgency in the newsroom when the breaking story came in.

Related Terms

  • synonym: editorial office - office where editing and editorial decisions are made
  • antonym: studio - room where broadcasting or recording occurs
