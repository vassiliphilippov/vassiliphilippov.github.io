squarehead

noun
unimaginative person
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɛrˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈskwɛəˌhɛd / (BrE)
an unimaginative, conventional, or old-fashioned person, often used as a mild informal insult

Examples

A boring squarehead
My uncle is a real squarehead who never wants to try anything different.
They called him a squarehead because he always follows the rules and avoids risks.
Don't be such a squarehead; come with us to the new restaurant tonight!

Related Terms

  • synonym: fuddy-duddy - an old-fashioned or conservative person
  • antonym: innovator - a person who introduces new ideas or methods
  • synonym: stick-in-the-mud - a person who is unwilling to try new things or change
