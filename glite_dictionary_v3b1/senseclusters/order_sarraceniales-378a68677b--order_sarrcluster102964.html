order Sarraceniales

noun
taxonomic order of insect-trapping plants
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər səˌræsəˈniːəˌliːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə səˌræsɪˈniːəliːz / (BrE)
a taxonomic order of insectivorous plants, characterized by specialized structures for trapping insects; includes families such as Sarraceniaceae, Nepenthaceae, and Droseraceae
