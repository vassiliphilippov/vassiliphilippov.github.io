light brown

noun
a pale shade of brown
Pronunciation: / laɪt braʊn / (AmE), / laɪt braʊn / (BrE)
a pale shade of brown; a brown color that is lighter in tone and less intense

Examples

A light brown color
His hair was a beautiful shade of light brown that caught the sunlight.
She painted her bedroom walls a soft light brown to create a cozy atmosphere.
The puppy’s fur was mostly light brown with darker patches on her ears.
