freezingly

adverb
extremely cold manner
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːzɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈfriːzɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely cold or causes a sensation of freezing

Examples

Freezingly cold air
The mountain air was freezingly cold even in the middle of summer.
She shivered as the freezingly wet rain soaked through her coat.
We waited freezingly at the bus stop as the wind howled around us.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bitterly - in a way that is extremely cold or harsh
  • synonym: chillingly - in a way that causes a feeling of cold or fear
  • antonym: warmly - in a way that is pleasantly or comfortably warm
