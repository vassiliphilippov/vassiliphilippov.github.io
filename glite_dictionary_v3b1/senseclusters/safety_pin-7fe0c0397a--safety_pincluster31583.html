safety pin

noun
small wearable symbol of solidarity
2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈseɪf.tɪ pɪn/ (AmE), /ˈseɪf.tɪ pɪn/ (BrE)
A safety pin worn or displayed as a small public sign of solidarity, protection, or support for a person, group, or cause.

Examples

Wear a safety pin for support
After the attack, many commuters wore a safety pin on their jackets to show support.
She left a safety pin on the table to indicate she felt safe in that space.
Volunteers handed out stickers with a tiny safety pin symbol at the rally.
