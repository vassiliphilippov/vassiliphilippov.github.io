plot

verb
map or lay out land for development
3.6 1.14/m
Pronunciation: / plÉ‘t / (AmE), / plÉ’t / (BrE)
to make a plan or map showing the layout of land, especially for development or construction

Examples

To plot land boundaries
She was hired to plot the boundaries of the farmland accurately.
The engineers will plot the new neighborhood before construction begins.
They need to plot the land in order to sell individual lots.

Synonyms

  • plat
    9.9 0.07/m
    plat is used to make a legal map that divides land into lots, while plot shows land layout for building or development.
  • map
    2.9 3.31/m
    map means to create a record of an area and its features, while plot focuses on planning land layout for construction.
  • map out
    3.7 1.88/m
    map out means to plan carefully in detail before action, while plot means to make a land map for development.
