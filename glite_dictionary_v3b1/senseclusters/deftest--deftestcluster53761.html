deftest

adjective
most skillful
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛftɪst / (AmE), / ˈdɛftɪst / (BrE)
most skillful, quick, and precise in movement or handling

Examples

The deftest dancer
Of all the dancers, she was the deftest on stage, moving with incredible grace.
The deftest surgeon was chosen to perform the complex operation.
He became the deftest player on the team after years of practice.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clumsiest - most lacking in skill or grace
  • degree lower: deft - skillful and quick in movement or handling
  • synonym: most skillful - having the highest level of skill
