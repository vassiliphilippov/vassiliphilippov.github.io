hippiedom

noun
hippie culture
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪpidəm / (AmE), / ˈhɪpidəm / (BrE)
the culture, lifestyle, or community associated with hippies, especially in the 1960s and 1970s

Examples

The hippiedom culture
Many young people were drawn to hippiedom during the late 1960s.
The festival was a celebration of hippiedom with music, art, and peace signs everywhere.
He wrote a book about his experiences living in hippiedom in San Francisco.

Related Terms

  • synonym: counterculture - a way of life and set of attitudes opposed to the mainstream
  • antonym: mainstream - the ideas, attitudes, or activities regarded as normal or conventional
