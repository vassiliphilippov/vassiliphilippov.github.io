civilization

noun
refined culture and manners
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪvələˈzeɪʃ(ə)n / (AmE), / ˌsɪvɪlaɪˈzeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the quality of being polite, cultured, and refined in thought, manners, and taste

Examples

A mark of civilization
For many, literature represents the highest level of civilization.
She admired his civilization in both conversation and conduct.
The atmosphere of the club was one of civilization and grace.

Related Terms

  • antonym: barbarism - lack of culture and refinement
  • synonym: refinement - the process of improving something to make it more polite or cultured
