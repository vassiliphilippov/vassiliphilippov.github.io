diagonalization

noun
matrix transformation to diagonal form
9.5 8.4
Pronunciation: /daɪ.ə.gə.nə.lə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /daɪ.ə.gə.nə.laɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /daɪ.ə.gə.nə.lə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /daɪ.ə.gə.nə.laɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of transforming a square matrix into a diagonal matrix, typically by finding a basis in which the matrix representation has nonzero elements only on the main diagonal

Numerical methods exist for efficient diagonalisation of large matrices.
