toluic acid

noun
any of three isomeric acids from toluene
Pronunciation: / toʊˈluːɪk ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / təˈluːɪk ˈæsɪd / (BrE)
Any of three isomeric crystalline acids derived from toluene, used in dyes and pharmaceuticals.

Examples

Toluic acid crystals
Many dyes are produced using toluic acid as a raw material.
Para-toluic acid is an important intermediate in the manufacture of terephthalic acid.
The laboratory synthesized all three toluic acid isomers for the experiment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: toluene - a colorless liquid hydrocarbon used as a solvent and starting material for chemical synthesis
