transmundane

adjective
beyond the physical world
7.4
Pronunciation: / trænzˈmʌn.deɪn / (AmE), / tranzˈmʌn.deɪn / (BrE)
existing or extending beyond the physical or material world; supernatural or transcending normal experience

Examples

A transmundane vision

Some poets seek inspiration in transmundane sources beyond the physical senses.
The philosopher explored transmundane realms that defy ordinary understanding.
