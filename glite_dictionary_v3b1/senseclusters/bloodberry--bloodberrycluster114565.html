bloodberry

noun
tropical plant with bright red berries
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌdˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈblʌdˌbɛri / (BrE)
A tropical American bushy plant with white to pale pink flowers, followed by clusters of bright red, berry-like fruits, often grown as a houseplant.

Examples

The bloodberry plant
Gardeners appreciate the bloodberry for its hardy nature and attractive flowers.
She placed a pot of bloodberry by the window to brighten up the living room.
The bloodberry adds a splash of color to the garden with its vibrant scarlet fruit clusters.
