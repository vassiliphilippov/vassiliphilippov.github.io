wrong order

noun
an incorrect food or drink delivery
Pronunciation: / rɔŋ ˈɔrdɚ / (AmE), / rɒŋ ˈɔːdə / (BrE)
a food or drink item delivered by mistake, different from what was actually requested

Examples

A wrong order of food
If you receive the wrong order, let the staff know immediately so they can fix it.
She was disappointed when the chef prepared a wrong order instead of her usual meal.
The waiter apologized after bringing us a wrong order for our table.

Related Terms

  • antonym: correct order - the correct food or drink requested
  • synonym: mistake - an action or judgement that is wrong; an error
