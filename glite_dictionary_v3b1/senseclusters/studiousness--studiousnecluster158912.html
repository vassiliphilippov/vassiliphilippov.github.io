studiousness

noun
quality of being dedicated to study
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈstuːdiəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈstjuːdiəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of spending a lot of time studying or reading; dedication to academic work

Examples

Great studiousness at school
Despite his natural intelligence, a lack of studiousness kept him from excelling in school.
Her studiousness impressed all of her teachers and classmates.
The university seeks students with exceptional studiousness and a passion for learning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diligence - persistent and careful work or effort
  • antonym: laziness - the quality of being unwilling to work or use energy
