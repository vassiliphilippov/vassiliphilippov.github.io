nonchemical

adjective
without chemicals
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈkɛmɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈkɛmɪkəl / (BrE)
not involving, using, or relating to chemicals or chemical processes

Examples

A nonchemical method
The farmers adopted a nonchemical approach to pest control to protect the environment.
Many people prefer nonchemical cleaning products for their homes.
The spa offers nonchemical treatments for skin care and relaxation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chemical - relating to or using chemicals
  • synonym: organic - produced without synthetic chemicals
