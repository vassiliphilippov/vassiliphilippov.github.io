sacralization

noun
making sacred
9.3
Pronunciation: /seɪ.krə.laɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /seɪ.krə.laɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)

Examples

Sacralization of a place
The sacralization of ancient sites often involves special rituals and ceremonies.
Some leaders promote the sacralization of national symbols to unite their people.
Anthropologists study the sacralization of objects in different religious traditions.

  • antonym: desecration - the act of treating something sacred with disrespect
