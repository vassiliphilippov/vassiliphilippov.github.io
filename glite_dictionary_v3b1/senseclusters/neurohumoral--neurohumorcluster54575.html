neurohumoral

adjective
nerves and hormones
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊhjuːˈmɔrəl / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊhjuːˈmɔːrəl / (BrE)
relating to the interaction between the nervous system and hormones in regulating bodily functions

Examples

The neurohumoral system
The neurohumoral system plays a key role in the body's response to stress.
Doctors study neurohumoral mechanisms to understand how the brain controls hormone release.
Certain medications affect neurohumoral pathways to treat heart failure.
