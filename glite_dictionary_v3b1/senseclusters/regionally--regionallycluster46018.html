regionally

adverb
in a way relating to a region
Pronunciation: / ˈriːdʒənəli / (AmE), / ˈriːdʒənəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or affects a particular region or regions, rather than the whole country or area

Examples

Vary regionally
Educational policies sometimes vary regionally across the country.
The company operates regionally to better meet the needs of different communities.
The food festival will be held regionally in each major city this year.
