ascitic

adjective
relating to abdominal fluid accumulation (ascites)
Pronunciation: / əˈsɪtɪk / (AmE), / əˈsɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or resulting from ascites, an abnormal accumulation of fluid in the peritoneal cavity

Examples

Ascitic fluid accumulation
An ultrasound confirmed the presence of ascitic accumulation in the abdomen.
The patient showed ascitic swelling due to liver disease.
They drained several liters of ascitic fluid during the procedure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ascites - an abnormal accumulation of fluid in the peritoneal cavity
