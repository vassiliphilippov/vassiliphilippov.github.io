sheefish

noun
large Arctic fish
Pronunciation: / ˈʃiːˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈʃiːˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a large freshwater fish native to Arctic and subarctic rivers of North America, valued for its size and taste

Examples

A large sheefish
Anglers travel to Alaska hoping to catch a giant sheefish in the remote rivers.
The sheefish is known for its silvery body and impressive size.
Local communities rely on the sheefish as an important source of food.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inconnu - another name for the sheefish, a large northern freshwater fish
  • synonym: salmon - a popular large freshwater and saltwater fish
