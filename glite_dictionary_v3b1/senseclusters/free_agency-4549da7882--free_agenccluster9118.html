free agency

noun
state of athlete free to sign with any team
2.6 3.1
Pronunciation: /friː ˈeɪ.dʒən.siː/ /; / /friː ˈeɪ.dʒən.sɪ/ (AmE), /friː ˈeɪ.dʒən.siː/ /; / /friː ˈeɪ.dʒən.sɪ/ (BrE)
(in sports) the state of being a professional athlete who is free to negotiate a contract to play for any team, not being bound by a contract to any one team.

Examples

Free agency in sports
Free agency has changed the way professional teams build their rosters.
After five years with the team, he entered free agency and received offers from several clubs.
The star player is expected to test free agency this summer.
