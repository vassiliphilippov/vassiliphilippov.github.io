so kvlt

adjective
very cool or subculturally authentic
9.9
Pronunciation: / soʊ kʌlt / (AmE), / səʊ kʌlt / (BrE)
Extremely cool or authentic, especially in reference to subcultural style or behavior associated with underground music scenes.

Examples

So kvlt style
Her collection of vinyl records is so kvlt that every metalhead is jealous.
That underground show last night was so kvlt, with only die-hard fans attending.
Wearing corpse paint to school would be considered so kvlt in black metal circles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cool - fashionable or impressive, especially in youth culture
  • antonym: poser - someone who pretends to be part of a subculture; not authentic
  • synonym: trve - authentic or genuine (especially in metal subcultures)
