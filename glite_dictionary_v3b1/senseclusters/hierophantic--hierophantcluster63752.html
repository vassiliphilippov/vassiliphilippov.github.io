hierophantic

adjective
revealing sacred mysteries
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪərəˈfæntɪk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪərəˈfæntɪk / (BrE)
relating to priests, sacred rituals, or the interpretation of holy mysteries

Examples

Hierophantic sacred rituals
The priest spoke in a hierophantic manner, unveiling the deeper meanings of the ritual.
Her writing has a hierophantic quality, as if she is interpreting ancient wisdom for modern readers.
The ceremony was filled with hierophantic symbols and mysterious chants.
The ancient temple was decorated in a hierophantic style that reflected its sacred purpose.
Her speech took on a hierophantic tone as she described the rituals.
The festival included several hierophantic ceremonies led by the high priest.
