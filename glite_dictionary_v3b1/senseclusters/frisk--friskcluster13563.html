frisk

noun
search of a person for concealed items
9.6 6.0
Pronunciation: / frɪsk / (AmE), / frɪsk / (BrE)
a quick search of a person's outer clothing for concealed weapons or illegal drugs, especially by the police

Examples

A police frisk
A routine frisk uncovered a concealed knife in his jacket pocket.
After stopping the suspect, the officer performed a quick frisk for weapons.
The airport security staff conducted a frisk before allowing him through the checkpoint.
