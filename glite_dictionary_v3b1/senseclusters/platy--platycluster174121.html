platy

noun
a small aquarium fish native to Mexico
Pronunciation: / ˈplæti / (AmE), / ˈplæti / (BrE)
A small, stocky freshwater fish native to Mexico and Central America, popular as an aquarium pet.

Examples

A platy fish
Many beginners start with platies because they are easy to care for and hardy.
She bought five red platies to brighten up her aquarium.
The platy is known for its vibrant colors and peaceful nature in community tanks.
