Vauxhall

proper noun
historic London garden
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɔksˌhɔl / (AmE), / ˈvɒksˌhɔːl / (BrE)
a famous public garden in London, popular in the 18th and 19th centuries for concerts, fireworks, and entertainments

Examples
