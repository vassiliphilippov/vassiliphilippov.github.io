Kekule formula

noun
structural formula of benzene ring
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛkjʊleɪ ˈfɔrmjələ / (AmE), / ˈkɛkjʊliː ˈfɔːmjʊlə / (BrE)
a structural formula representing benzene as a closed chain of six carbon atoms with alternating single and double bonds, each carbon atom bonded to one hydrogen atom
