majority

noun
the greater part; more than half
Pronunciation: / məˈdʒɔrəti / (AmE), / məˈdʒɒrəti / (BrE)
the greater part or number of a group; more than half of the people or things in a group

Examples

The majority of people
He spent the majority of his career working in finance.
The majority of students agreed with the proposal.
The new law passed with a clear majority in parliament.

Related Terms

  • antonym: minority - the smaller part or number
