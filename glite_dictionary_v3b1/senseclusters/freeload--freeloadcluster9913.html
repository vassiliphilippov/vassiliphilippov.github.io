freeload

verb
live at another's expense
7.5 0.3/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàfriÀålo äd / (AmE), / ÀàfriÀêÀål…ô äd / (BrE)
to obtain food, accommodation, or other benefits at another's expense without giving anything in return

Examples

To freeload off friends
He spent the entire summer freeloading off his generous friends instead of getting a job.
It's not fair for you to freeload when everyone else is contributing.
They realized he had been freeloading at their house for months without paying any rent.
