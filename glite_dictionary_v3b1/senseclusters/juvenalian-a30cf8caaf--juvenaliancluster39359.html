Juvenalian

noun
bitter satirist
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒuːvəˈneɪliən / (AmE), / ˌdʒuːvəˈneɪliən / (BrE)
a writer or satirist whose work is bitter, scornful, and critical, in the style of Juvenal

Examples

A Juvenalian writer
The critic was known as a Juvenalian for his harsh attacks on social injustice.
Her novels, written in a Juvenalian tone, exposed the hypocrisy of the elite.
Many readers found the Juvenalian style of the essay both powerful and unsettling.
