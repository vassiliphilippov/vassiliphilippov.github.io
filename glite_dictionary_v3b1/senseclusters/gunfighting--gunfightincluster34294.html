gunfighting

noun
gun battle
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌnˌfaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnˌfaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a fight or the skill of fighting with guns, especially in armed confrontations

Examples

Gunfighting skills
The old Western film is famous for its intense gunfighting scenes in the dusty streets.
Police tried to prevent gunfighting between rival gangs in the city last night.
Stories of legendary gunfighting in the Wild West have inspired many books and movies.
The old Western movies often feature dramatic gunfighting scenes between outlaws and sheriffs.
He took advanced courses in gunfighting to improve his tactical response skills.
Modern police training sometimes includes simulations of gunfighting situations to prepare officers for emergencies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duel - a formal fight between two people, often with weapons
  • antonym: hand-to-hand combat - fighting at close range without firearms
  • antonym: negotiation - a discussion aimed at reaching an agreement
  • synonym: shootout - a fight in which people shoot at each other with guns
