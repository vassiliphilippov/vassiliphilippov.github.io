gunfighting

noun
gun battle
5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈgʌn.faɪ.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈgʌn.faɪ.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
a fight or the skill of fighting with guns, especially in armed confrontations

Examples

Gunfighting skills
