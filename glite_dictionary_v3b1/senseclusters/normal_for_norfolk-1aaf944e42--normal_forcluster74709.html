normal for Norfolk

adjective
peculiar or odd, as is typical in Norfolk
7.6 8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈnɔːr.məl fər ˈnɔːr.fək/ /; / /ˈnɔːr.məl fɔːr ˈnɔːr.foʊlk/ (AmE), /ˈnɔː.məl fɔː ˈnɔː.fək/ /; / /ˈnɔː.məl fɔː ˈnɔː.fəʊlk/ (BrE)
used, often humorously or disparagingly, to describe something strange, eccentric, or peculiar as being typical in Norfolk, England; often implies that what is odd elsewhere is unsurprising there
