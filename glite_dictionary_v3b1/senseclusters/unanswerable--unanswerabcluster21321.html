unanswerable

adjective

impossible to be answered or refuted; not capable of being responded to with a satisfactory answer.

Examples

An unanswerable question
Her criticism was so unanswerable that no one bothered to reply.
Some philosophical questions are simply unanswerable by science alone.
The lawyer presented an unanswerable case in court.

Synonyms

  • unsolvable
    5.3 0.45/m
    unsolvable is used for problems that cannot be worked out, while unanswerable describes questions no one can reply to satisfactorily.
  • insoluble
    7.5 0.55/m
    insoluble describes problems or questions that cannot be solved, while unanswerable stresses that a satisfactory reply is not possible.
  • irresolvable
    7.9 0.10/m
    irresolvable means a dispute or problem cannot be settled, while unanswerable focuses on lack of a satisfactory reply.
  • inarguable
    6.7 0.40/m
    inarguable is something people cannot reasonably deny, while unanswerable often means a question cannot be answered well.
  • incontrovertible
    8.2 0.88/m
    incontrovertible names a fact that cannot be denied, while unanswerable often means a question has no good reply.
  • undisputable
    4.7 0.09/m
    undisputable says something is certainly true and not open to doubt, while unanswerable points to lack of a reply.
