pad

noun
informal living quarters
9.9
Pronunciation: / pæd / (AmE), / pæd / (BrE)
an informal term for temporary or modest living quarters, often an apartment or a place to stay

Examples

A friend's pad
He crashed at my pad for a couple of nights while looking for a new place.
Her downtown pad is small but cozy, perfect for one person.
They threw a party at their new pad over the weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apartment - a set of rooms for living in, usually on one floor of a building
  • synonym: home - the place where one lives permanently, especially as a member of a family or household
