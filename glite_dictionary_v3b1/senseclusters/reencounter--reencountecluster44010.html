reencounter

noun
meeting again
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˈkaʊntər / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈkaʊntə / (BrE)
an instance of meeting or experiencing someone or something again after a period of time apart

Examples

A reencounter with friends
Their reencounter at the airport brought back many memories from their childhood.
The reencounter between the artist and his lost painting was an emotional moment.
After years apart, the friends' reencounter was filled with laughter and stories.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: encounter - a meeting, especially one that is unexpected
  • synonym: reunion - a meeting of people who have been apart
