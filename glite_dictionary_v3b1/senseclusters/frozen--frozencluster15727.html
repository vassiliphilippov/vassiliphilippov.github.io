frozen

adjective
absolutely still from fear or shock
Pronunciation: / ˈfroʊzən / (AmE), / ˈfrəʊzən / (BrE)
unable to move because of extreme fear, surprise, or shock; absolutely still

Examples

Frozen with fear
He remained frozen in place, unable to respond to the sudden bad news.
She stood frozen with terror as the shadow loomed closer.
The children were frozen by the sound of breaking glass in the middle of the night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immobile - unable to move; motionless
  • synonym: paralyzed - unable to move or act, often from fear or shock
  • synonym: rooted - unable to move, usually from shock or surprise
