leading light

noun
influential or prominent person
Pronunciation: / ˈlidɪŋ ˌlaɪt /; / ˈliːdɪŋ laɪt / (AmE), / ˈliːdɪŋ ˌlaɪt /; / ˈliːdɪŋ laɪt / (BrE)
a person who is important, respected, and influential in a particular group or field

Examples

A leading light of art
As one of the leading lights of the movement, she inspired many young artists.
She has become a leading light in the world of environmental science.
The gallery was filled with leading lights of the contemporary art scene.
During the 1960s, she emerged as a leading light among civil rights activists.
He is considered a leading light in the world of modern architecture.
The new principal is a leading light in educational reform and innovation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: figurehead - a leader or representative without real power, sometimes used for prominent persons
  • synonym: luminary - a person who inspires or influences others, especially one prominent in a particular sphere
