fryer

noun
young chicken suitable for frying
Pronunciation: / ˈfraɪər / (AmE), / ˈfraɪə / (BrE)
the flesh of a young chicken, typically of medium size, suitable for frying

Examples

A whole fryer chicken
Many Southern dishes are made with a whole fryer cut into pieces.
The butcher recommended buying a fryer for the crispy chicken dinner.
The recipe calls for a fresh fryer to ensure tender meat.
