eyeline

noun
direction of gaze
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈaɪˌlaɪn / (BrE)
the direction or imaginary line along which a person is looking, especially in film or photography

Examples

Actor's eyeline
The director adjusted the actor's eyeline to match the camera angle.
In animation, keeping the character's eyeline consistent helps the audience follow the story.
She checked her eyeline in the mirror to ensure she was looking directly at the camera.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gaze - a steady look or stare
  • synonym: line of sight - the straight path that light travels from an object to the eye
