wild snapdragon

noun
showy European perennial plant; sometimes a weed
Pronunciation: / waɪld ˈsnæpˌdræɡən / (AmE), / waɪld ˈsnæpˌdræɡən / (BrE)
a European perennial plant with showy yellow and orange flowers, often considered a weed in North America

Examples

The wild snapdragon flower
Botanists observed that wild snapdragon has become naturalized in several parts of North America.
Gardeners often struggle to remove wild snapdragon when it spreads as a weed.
The field was covered in wild snapdragon blooming in vibrant yellow and orange.
