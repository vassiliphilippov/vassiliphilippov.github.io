family Bruchidae

noun
family of seed beetles in entomology
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli bruːˈkɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli bruːˈkɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of beetles known as seed beetles, whose larvae typically infest and consume seeds, especially those of legumes.
