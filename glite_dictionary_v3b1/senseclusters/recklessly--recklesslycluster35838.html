recklessly

adverb
without regard for danger; carelessly
1.0 3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈrek.ləs.liː/ /; / /ˈrek.lɪs.liː/ (AmE), /ˈrek.ləs.liː/ /; / /ˈrek.lɪs.liː/ (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of concern about danger or the consequences of one's actions.

Examples

Drive recklessly
He drove recklessly through the city streets, ignoring the traffic signals.
She recklessly invested all her savings in the unproven start-up.
The climber acted recklessly, taking unnecessary risks for a quick ascent.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cautiously - in a careful or wary manner
  • synonym: reckless - having no regard for danger or consequences
