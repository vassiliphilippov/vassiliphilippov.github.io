rah

noun
wealthy, showy youth
Pronunciation: / rɑː / (BrE)
an informal term for a young person from a wealthy or upper-class background who dresses and acts in a fashionable, showy way, especially in Britain

Examples

A rah at university
At the party, a group of rahs arrived in expensive cars and designer clothes.
He was labeled a rah because of his accent and flashy lifestyle at university.
Some students think being a rah is all about showing off wealth and connections.

Related Terms

  • synonym: snob - a person who believes they are superior because of their social class
  • synonym: toff - a wealthy or upper-class person, often seen as snobbish
