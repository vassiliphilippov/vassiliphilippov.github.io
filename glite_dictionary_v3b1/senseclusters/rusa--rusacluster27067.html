rusa

noun
Asian deer genus
Pronunciation: / ˈruːsə / (AmE), / ˈruːsə / (BrE)
a genus of deer native to South and Southeast Asia, known for their large size and antlers

Examples

The rusa deer
The rusa is commonly found in Indonesian forests.
Wildlife researchers observed a herd of rusa grazing near the riverbank.
The antlers of the male rusa are especially impressive during the mating season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deer - a hoofed grazing animal with antlers, found in many parts of the world
  • synonym: sambar - a large deer species native to South and Southeast Asia
