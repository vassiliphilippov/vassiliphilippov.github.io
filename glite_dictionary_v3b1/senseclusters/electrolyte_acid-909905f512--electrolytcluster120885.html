electrolyte acid

noun
sulfuric acid for storage batteries
5.2
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛk.trəˌlaɪt ˈæs.ɪd / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛk.trəˌlaɪt ˈæs.ɪd / (BrE)
Dilute sulfuric acid used as the electrolyte in storage batteries.

Examples

Dilute electrolyte acid
Be careful when handling electrolyte acid from a car battery, as it can cause burns.
Old batteries often lose power because their electrolyte acid diminishes over time.
The spilled electrolyte acid needed to be neutralized and cleaned up immediately.

Related Terms
