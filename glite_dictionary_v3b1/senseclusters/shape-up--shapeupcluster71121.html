shape-up

noun
hiring system for daily longshoremen work
9.9 7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈʃeɪp.ʌp/ (AmE), /ˈʃeɪp.ʌp/ (BrE)
a system of hiring longshoremen for daily work, where workers gather and are selected for jobs on the spot, traditionally by a union boss.

Examples

The shape-up meeting
Early each morning, longshoremen attended the shape-up hoping to be chosen for that day's work.
The old shape-up on the docks was criticized for its lack of fairness.
The union abolished the shape-up to implement a more organized hiring process.
