safe

adjective
not put out (in baseball); made a safe play
1.0
Pronunciation: / seɪf / (AmE), / seɪf / (BrE)
(in baseball or similar sports) having reached a base without being put out by the opposing team.

Examples

Runner is safe
He slid into home plate just in time to be called safe.
The crowd cheered when the runner was declared safe by the umpire.
The umpire signaled that the player was safe at second base.

Related Terms

  • antonym: out - (in baseball) having been put out while running the bases
