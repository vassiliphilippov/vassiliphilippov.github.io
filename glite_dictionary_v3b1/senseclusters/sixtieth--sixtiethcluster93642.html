sixtieth

noun
one part out of sixty equal parts
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪkstiəθ / (AmE), / ˈsɪkstiɪθ / (BrE)
Each of the sixty equal parts into which something can be divided.

Examples

A sixtieth of a minute
A minute is one sixtieth of an hour.
Each sixtieth of the pie was carefully measured for accuracy.
He divided the inheritance into sixtieths so that everyone could receive an equal share.
