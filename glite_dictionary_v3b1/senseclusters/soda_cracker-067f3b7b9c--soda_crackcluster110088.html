soda cracker

noun
a thin, crisp unsweetened cracker
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊdə ˌkrækər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊdə ˌkrækə / (BrE)
A thin, crisp, unsweetened cracker made with baking soda and cream of tartar as leavening agents.

Examples

A plain soda cracker
A plain soda cracker is often served with cheese or butter.
She munched on a soda cracker while waiting for her soup.
The recipe calls for crushed soda crackers as a topping.

Related Terms

  • synonym: saltine - a thin, salted cracker similar to a soda cracker
