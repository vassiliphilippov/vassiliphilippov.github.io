pebbling

noun
throwing small stones
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛblɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɛblɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of throwing small stones or pebbles at someone or something

Examples

Pebbling of windows
The council condemned the repeated pebbling of parked cars by local youths after dusk.
After the argument, the children started pebbling the old shed with handfuls of stones.
The teacher warned the students that pebbling the windows could result in detention.
