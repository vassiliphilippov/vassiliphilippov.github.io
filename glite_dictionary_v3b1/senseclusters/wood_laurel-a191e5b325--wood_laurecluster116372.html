wood laurel

noun
North American evergreen shrub
Pronunciation: / wʊd ˈlɔːrəl / (AmE), / wʊd ˈlɔːrəl / (BrE)
a North American evergreen shrub with glossy leaves and white or rose-colored flowers

Examples

Wood laurel with white flowers
I planted a wood laurel by the porch for its fragrant flowers.
The wood laurel blooms beautifully in late spring, adding color to the garden.
The leaves of the wood laurel are often used in decorative arrangements.
