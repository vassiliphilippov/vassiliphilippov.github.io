bogard

verb
to selfishly keep
9.9
Pronunciation: /bə.ˈgɑːrd/ (AmE), /bə.ˈgɑːd/ (BrE)
to selfishly take or keep something for oneself, especially when it should be shared with others

Examples

To bogard the snacks
Please don't bogard the remote—let someone else have a turn.
He tends to bogard the conversation and not let others speak.
At the party, she bogarded all the best snacks for herself.

Related Terms

  • antonym: share - to give a portion to others
