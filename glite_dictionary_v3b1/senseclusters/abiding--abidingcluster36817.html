abiding

adjective
lasting for a long time
Pronunciation: / əˈbaɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈbaɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a feeling or belief) lasting a long time; enduring.

Examples

An abiding interest
His abiding interest in history led him to pursue a career in research.
She had an abiding respect for her mentors that never faded with time.
Their friendship was marked by an abiding trust that survived many challenges.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fleeting - lasting for a very short time
  • synonym: lasting - continuing for a long time
