permaculture

adjective
sustainable agriculture
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrməkʌltʃər / (AmE), / ˈpɜːməkʌltʃə / (BrE)
relating to or using the principles of sustainable and self-sufficient agricultural design known as permaculture.

Examples

Permaculture methods
The farm uses permaculture methods to grow food while protecting the environment.
She attended a workshop on permaculture gardening to learn about sustainable practices.
Many communities are adopting permaculture designs to create more resilient landscapes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: organic - produced without synthetic chemicals or fertilizers
  • synonym: sustainable - able to be maintained without depleting resources
