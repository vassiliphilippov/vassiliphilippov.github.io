marble

noun
sculpture carved from marble
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːrbəl / (AmE), / ˈmɑːbəl / (BrE)
A sculpture made by carving marble, typically representing a human figure or other artistic subject.

Examples

A marble statue
An exquisite marble by Michelangelo draws crowds from around the world.
The museum features a famous marble of Athena at its entrance.
They restored the ancient marble after decades of neglect.

Related Terms

  • synonym: statue - a three-dimensional work of art, usually of a person or animal
