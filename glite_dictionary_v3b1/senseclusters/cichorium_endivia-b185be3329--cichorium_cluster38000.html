Cichorium endivia

noun
salad green with curly or flat leaves
6.8
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkɔriəm ɛnˈdaɪviə / (AmE), / sɪˈkɔːriəm ɪnˈdiːvɪə / (BrE)
A widely cultivated herbaceous plant, valued for its edible leaves used in salads; it has either curly serrated or broad flat leaves.

Examples

Cichorium endivia leaves
Farmers harvest Cichorium endivia both for local markets and restaurant supply.
Many gourmet salads feature Cichorium endivia for its crisp texture and slightly bitter flavor.
The chef prefers to use Cichorium endivia because its curly leaves hold dressing well.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chicory - a related plant sometimes used for salad greens, roots used as a coffee substitute
  • synonym: endive - an edible leafy plant used in salads, especially the cultivated varieties
