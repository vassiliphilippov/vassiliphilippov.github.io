Adderall

proper noun
ADHD medication
Pronunciation: / ˈædəˌrɔl / (AmE), / ˈædəˌrɔːl / (BrE)
a brand name prescription medication containing amphetamine salts, used to treat attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD) and narcolepsy

Examples

Adderall medication
The doctor prescribed Adderall to help him focus better at school.
Some students misuse Adderall to try to improve their concentration during exams.
She takes Adderall every morning as part of her treatment for ADHD.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Ritalin - a brand name medication used to treat ADHD
  • synonym: amphetamine - a stimulant drug used to treat ADHD and narcolepsy
