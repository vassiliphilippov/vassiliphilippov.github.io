sapucaia

noun
South American nut tree
Pronunciation: / ˌsæpʊˈkaɪə / (AmE), / ˌsapʊˈkaɪə / (BrE)
a tropical South American tree whose large fruits contain edible nuts used for food and oil

Examples

Sapucaia tree nuts
The sapucaia is valued for its nutritious nuts and the oil they produce.
Farmers in Brazil often harvest sapucaia nuts during the dry season.
The large, woody fruit of the sapucaia protects the seeds inside until they are ready to eat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Brazil nut - a large South American tree producing edible nuts
