Eleotridae

noun
family of fishes called sleepers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɛliˈɑtrɪdiː / (AmE), / ɛlɪˈɒtrɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of small, usually freshwater fishes known as sleepers, found in tropical and subtropical regions.
