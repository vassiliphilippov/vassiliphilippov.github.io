anthroposophical

adjective
of anthroposophy
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌænθrəpəˈsɑːfɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌænθrəpəˈsɒfɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to anthroposophy, a spiritual philosophy founded by Rudolf Steiner that emphasizes the development of human spiritual awareness

Examples

An anthroposophical school
The school follows an anthroposophical approach to education inspired by Rudolf Steiner.
Many anthroposophical communities emphasize organic farming and holistic health practices.
She read several anthroposophical texts to better understand the philosophy's view of human development.

Related Terms

  • antonym: materialistic - focused on physical or material things rather than spiritual
  • synonym: spiritual - relating to the human spirit or soul
