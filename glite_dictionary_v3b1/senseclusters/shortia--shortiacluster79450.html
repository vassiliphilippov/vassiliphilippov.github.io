shortia

noun
genus of evergreen perennial herbs
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɔrtiə / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːtiə / (BrE)
A genus of evergreen perennial herbs with smooth, leathery basal leaves and showy, solitary white flowers, found chiefly in eastern Asia and eastern North America.
