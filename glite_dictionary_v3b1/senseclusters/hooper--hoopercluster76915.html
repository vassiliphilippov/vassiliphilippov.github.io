hooper

noun
barrel hoop maker
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhuːpər / (AmE), / ˈhuːpə / (BrE)
a worker who makes, fits, or repairs hoops, especially the metal bands used to hold barrels together

Examples

Barrel hooper at work
The old winery still employs a skilled hooper to maintain its wooden barrels.
A hooper carefully measured and fitted the metal bands around the new cask.
Before machines, every brewery needed a hooper to keep the barrels in good condition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cooper - a person who makes or repairs barrels and casks
