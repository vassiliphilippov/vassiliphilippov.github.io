pasture land

noun
land for grazing livestock
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpæs.tʃɚ lænd / (AmE), / ˈpɑːs.tʃə lænd / (BrE)
land covered with grass or other plants suitable for grazing by livestock.

Examples

Green pasture land
Cattle grazed peacefully in the lush pasture land beyond the farmhouse.
Farmers rotated crops to keep their pasture land healthy and productive.
Much of the region's economy depends on sheep raised on pasture land.

Related Terms
