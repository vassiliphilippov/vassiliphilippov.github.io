reconverge

verb
to become stable again
8.5
Pronunciation: /riː.kən.ˈvɜːrdʒ/ /; / /rə.kən.ˈvɜːrdʒ/ (AmE), /riː.kən.ˈvɜːdʒ/ /; / /rə.kən.ˈvɜːdʒ/ (BrE)
to begin to approach a stable state or solution again after having diverged.

Examples

To reconverge on a solution

The two paths may separate briefly but eventually reconverge at the main road.
If the process is stable, it should reconverge even after unexpected changes.
