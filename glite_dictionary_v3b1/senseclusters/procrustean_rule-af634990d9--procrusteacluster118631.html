Procrustean rule

noun
strict rule ignoring individuality
6.6 9.1
Pronunciation: / proʊˈkrʌstiən ruːl / (AmE), / prəˈkrʌstiːən ruːl / (BrE)
A rule or standard applied strictly and uniformly, without regard for individual differences or circumstances.

Examples

A Procrustean rule applied
Applying a Procrustean rule to every case ignores important individual circumstances.
Many educators fear that a Procrustean rule stifles creativity in the classroom.
The company's Procrustean rule requires all employees to dress identically, regardless of job function.
