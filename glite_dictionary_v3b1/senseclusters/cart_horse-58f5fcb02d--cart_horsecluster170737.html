cart horse

noun
horse used for pulling carts
4.5
Pronunciation: / kɑrt hɔrs / (AmE), / kɑːt hɔːs / (BrE)
A horse that is specially bred and used for pulling carts or heavy loads.

Examples

A strong cart horse
His grandfather used to train a strong cart horse for farm work.
In Victorian England, the cart horse was a familiar sight in city streets.
The farmer harnessed the cart horse to carry the produce to market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: draft horse - a large, strong horse bred for work such as pulling heavy loads
