(in digital contexts) a professional who buys, sells, brokers, or manages virtual land and property in online platforms (for example metaverse environments), handling listings, transfers, and often tokenization or sales of digital plots.

Examples

Virtual land agent

The startup hired several experienced land agents to manage sales of its blockchain-based city parcels.
During the conference, the panel discussed how land agents set prices for virtual neighbourhoods and parcels.
