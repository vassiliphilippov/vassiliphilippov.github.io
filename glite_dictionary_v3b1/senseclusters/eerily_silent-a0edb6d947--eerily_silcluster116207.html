eerily silent

adjective
unnervingly quiet or still
9.9 5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɪrəli ˈsaɪlənt / (AmE), / ˈɪərɪli ˈsaɪlənt / (BrE)
Strangely or unsettlingly quiet; so silent as to cause unease or fear.

Examples

Eerily silent town
After the storm passed, the town was eerily silent without the usual sounds of birds or traffic.
The abandoned house was eerily silent as we walked through the halls.
The forest became eerily silent just before the sun set, making us feel uneasy.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: silent - making no sound; completely quiet
