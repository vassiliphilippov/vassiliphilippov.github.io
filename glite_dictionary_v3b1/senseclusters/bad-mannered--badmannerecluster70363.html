bad-mannered

adjective
not polite; lacking good manners
2.9
Pronunciation: / bæd ˈmænɚd / (AmE), / bæd ˈmænəd / (BrE)
not polite or showing poor social behavior; lacking good manners.

Examples

A bad-mannered person
His bad-mannered comment offended everyone at the table.
It is bad-mannered to interrupt someone while they are speaking.
The child’s bad-mannered behavior embarrassed his parents in public.

Related Terms

  • antonym: polite - showing good manners; respectful
  • synonym: rude - impolite; lacking respect in manner or speech
