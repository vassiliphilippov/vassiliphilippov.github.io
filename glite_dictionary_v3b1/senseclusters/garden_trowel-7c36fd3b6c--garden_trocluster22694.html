garden trowel

noun
small hand tool for gardening
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrdn ˈtraʊəl / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn ˈtraʊəl / (BrE)
A small hand tool with a pointed, scoop-shaped metal blade, used by gardeners for digging, planting, and other tasks in soil.

Examples

A garden trowel tool
After gardening, she cleaned the dirt off her garden trowel before putting it away.
He used a garden trowel to transplant the seedlings into larger pots.
The garden trowel is essential for making small holes in the soil for planting bulbs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trowel - a small hand tool with a flat, pointed blade used for spreading or digging
