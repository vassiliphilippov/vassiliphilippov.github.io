feelingly

adverb
with strong emotion
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfilɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈfiːlɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows strong emotion or feeling.

Examples

To speak feelingly
He described the loss feelingly, bringing tears to the eyes of his listeners.
She spoke feelingly about her experiences as a volunteer.
The author writes feelingly about the hardship his family endured.
