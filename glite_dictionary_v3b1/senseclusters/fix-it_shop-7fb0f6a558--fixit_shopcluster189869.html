fix-it shop

noun
repair and maintenance shop
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪks ɪt ʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈfɪks ɪt ʃɒp / (BrE)
A shop that specializes in repairing and maintaining household items or equipment.

Examples

A fix-it shop
He learned how to mend clocks while working at his uncle's fix-it shop.
Many communities have a small fix-it shop that can handle a variety of household repairs.
My blender stopped working, so I took it to the local fix-it shop for repair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: repair shop - a shop where repairs are made to equipment, machinery, etc.
