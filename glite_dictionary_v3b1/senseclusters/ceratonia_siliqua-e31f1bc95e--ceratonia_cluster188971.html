Ceratonia siliqua

noun
Mediterranean tree with edible pods; carob tree
9.9
Pronunciation: /se.rə.ˈtoʊ.niː.ə sɪ.ˈlɪ.kwə/ (AmE), /se.rə.ˈtəʊ.niː.ə sɪ.ˈlɪ.kwə/ (BrE)
