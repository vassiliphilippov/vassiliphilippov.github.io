Synonyms

  • unfortunately
    2.9 83/m
    unfortunately states that something unlucky or bad happened in a neutral way, while sadly shows the speaker's personal sorrow.
  • regrettably
    4.6 1.79/m
    regrettably is a slightly formal comment that something is unfortunate, while sadly sounds more personal and emotional.
  • regretfully
    3.8 0.83/m
    regretfully often means with personal regret when someone says sorry, while sadly expresses general disappointment or pity.
