halfheartedness

noun
lack of enthusiasm or commitment
Pronunciation: / ˌhæfˈhɑrtɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌhɑːfˈhɑːtɪdnəs / (BrE)
lack of enthusiasm, energy, or commitment; the quality of being half-hearted or not fully sincere.

Examples

Her halfheartedness showed
Her halfheartedness was obvious in the way she approached the project, barely putting in any effort.
The team's halfheartedness during practice led to a disappointing performance in the match.
There was a sense of halfheartedness in his apology, making it hard to believe he was truly sorry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apathy - lack of interest, enthusiasm, or concern
  • antonym: commitment - the state or quality of being dedicated to a cause or activity
  • antonym: enthusiasm - eager and intense interest or enjoyment
