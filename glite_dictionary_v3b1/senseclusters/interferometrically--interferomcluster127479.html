interferometrically

adverb
using interferometry
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˌfɛrəˈmɛtrɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˌfɛrəˈmɛtrɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to or uses interferometry, especially for making precise measurements using the interference of waves

Examples

Measured interferometrically precisely
The scientists measured the star's position interferometrically to achieve the highest possible accuracy.
Distances between the two mirrors were determined interferometrically during the experiment.
The device can detect tiny changes in length interferometrically that would be impossible to see otherwise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: precisely - in an exact or accurate manner
