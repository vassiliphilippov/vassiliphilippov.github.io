convocant

adjective
calling people together
9.9
Pronunciation: / kənˈvoʊkənt / (AmE), / kənˈvəʊkənt / (BrE)
serving to call people together for a meeting or gathering; convening or summoning.

Examples

Convocant bell

A convocant notice was sent to all members before the emergency meeting.
The teacher's convocant call brought the students together quickly.
