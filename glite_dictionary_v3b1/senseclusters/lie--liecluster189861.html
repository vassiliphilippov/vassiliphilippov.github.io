lie

noun
position or arrangement; how something lies
6.1
Pronunciation: / laɪ / (AmE), / laɪ / (BrE)
the position or arrangement of something, especially as it lies on the ground or in relation to other things

Examples

The lie of the ball
Golfers often check the lie of the ball before deciding on their next shot.
The lie of the fallen tree blocked the path completely.
The lie of the land made it difficult to build a road through the valley.

Related Terms

  • synonym: placement - the act of putting something in a particular position
  • synonym: position - the location or arrangement of something
