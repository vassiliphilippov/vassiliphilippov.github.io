selection

noun
an assortment from which to choose
Pronunciation: / səˈlɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / sɪˈlɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
a group or range of things from which a choice can be made

Examples

A wide selection
Customers can browse through our selection of handcrafted jewelry.
The shop offers a wide selection of fresh produce every morning.
There was a small selection of books available at the cafe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: choice - an act or possibility of choosing
  • synonym: collection - a group of things gathered together
  • synonym: variety - a number of different types of the same thing
