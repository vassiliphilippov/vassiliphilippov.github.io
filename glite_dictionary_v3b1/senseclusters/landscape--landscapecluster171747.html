landscape

noun
overall situation or conceptual outlook
Pronunciation: / ˈlændˌskeɪp / (AmE), / ˈlan(d)skeɪp / (BrE)
The general situation, environment, or outlook in a particular sphere; the overall mental or conceptual perspective.

Examples

The political landscape
Recent regulations are reshaping the financial landscape across Europe.
Social media has dramatically altered the communications landscape in just a few years.
The company must adapt to the constantly changing landscape of the technology industry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: climate - the prevailing situation or mood in a particular sphere
