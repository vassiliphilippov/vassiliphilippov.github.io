coyote

noun
forest firefighter for severe, remote fires
8.5
Pronunciation: / kaɪˈoʊti / (AmE), / kaɪˈəʊti / (BrE)
A forest firefighter who is sent to battle remote and severe forest fires, often for days at a time.

Examples

Forest coyote firefighter
As a coyote, she spent long days and nights far from civilization.
Only the toughest coyotes volunteered for fighting fires on the most inaccessible slopes.
The team sent a seasoned coyote to tackle the wildfire deep in the mountains.
