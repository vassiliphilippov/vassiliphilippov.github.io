condoler

noun
person offering sympathy
Pronunciation: / kənˈdoʊlər / (AmE), / kənˈdəʊlə / (BrE)
a person who expresses sympathy to someone who is grieving or suffering a loss

Examples

A kind condoler
The condoler offered kind words to the family after their loss.
Each condoler brought flowers and shared memories of the deceased.
As a thoughtful condoler, she wrote a heartfelt letter to her grieving friend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comforter - a person who provides comfort or consolation
  • antonym: mourner - a person who is grieving for someone who has died
