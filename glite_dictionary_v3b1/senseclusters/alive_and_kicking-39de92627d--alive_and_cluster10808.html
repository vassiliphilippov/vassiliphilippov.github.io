alive and kicking

adjective
still active or healthy
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈlaɪv ənd ˈkɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈlaɪv ənd ˈkɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
still active, healthy, or continuing to exist, especially after difficulties or despite expectations of decline

Examples

Alive and kicking today
Despite rumors about its decline, physical book sales are alive and kicking.
Even in his seventies, my grandfather is still alive and kicking and full of stories.
The old jazz club is alive and kicking after its grand reopening last year.
