tenosynovitis

noun
inflammation of tendon sheath
8.0 9.5
Pronunciation: /ten.oʊ.sɪ.nə.ˈvaɪ.tɪs/ (AmE), /ten.əʊ.sɪ.nə.ˈvaɪ.tɪs/ (BrE)
Inflammation of a tendon and its surrounding sheath, typically causing pain, swelling, and difficulty in movement.

Examples

Tenosynovitis pain
Athletes are sometimes at risk of developing tenosynovitis due to repetitive motion injuries.

Treatment for tenosynovitis may include rest, anti-inflammatory medication, and physical therapy.
