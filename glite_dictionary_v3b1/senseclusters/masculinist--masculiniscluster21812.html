masculinist

noun
supporter of men's rights
Pronunciation: / ˈmæskjəlɪnɪst / (AmE), / ˈmæskjʊlɪnɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or promotes the rights, roles, or interests of men in society

Examples

Masculinist movement
The masculinist group organized a conference to discuss men's issues in modern society.
Some critics argue that a masculinist perspective can overlook the challenges faced by women.
He identified as a masculinist and advocated for equal parental leave for fathers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: feminist - a person who supports or promotes the rights and equality of women
