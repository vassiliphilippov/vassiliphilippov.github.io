easer

noun
something that relieves
Pronunciation: / ˈiːzər / (AmE), / ˈiːzə / (BrE)
a person or thing that makes something less difficult, painful, or unpleasant

Examples

A easer of pain
A warm cup of tea can be a real easer after a stressful day.
He found that talking to friends was the best easer for his worries.
Laughter is often described as a natural easer of tension.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comforter - someone or something that provides comfort
  • antonym: intensifier - something that makes a feeling or situation stronger
  • synonym: reliever - something that reduces pain or discomfort
