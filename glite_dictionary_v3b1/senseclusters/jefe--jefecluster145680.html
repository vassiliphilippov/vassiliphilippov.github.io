jefe

noun
crime boss
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪfeɪ / (AmE), / ˈheɪfeɪ / (BrE)
the leader or boss of a criminal organization, especially in Latin American contexts

Examples

Drug jefe
The notorious jefe controlled the entire region's drug trade for years.
After the jefe was captured, several rivals fought for control of the organization.
The film tells the story of a young man rising to become the jefe of a powerful cartel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boss - the person in charge of an organization or group
  • synonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group
  • antonym: underling - a person lower in rank or status
