trencherman

noun
hearty eater, especially a man
7.8 9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈtren.tʃər.mən/ (AmE), /ˈtren.tʃə.mən/ (BrE)
A person, especially a man, who enjoys eating and drinking in large quantities; a hearty eater.

Examples

A trencherman at banquets
Despite his slim build, the famed poet was quite the trencherman at every banquet.
He was known as a true trencherman, never missing a lavish feast.
Their guest, a seasoned trencherman, sampled every dish on the table with gusto.
