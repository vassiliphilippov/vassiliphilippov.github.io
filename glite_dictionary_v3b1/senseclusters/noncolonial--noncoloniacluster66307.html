noncolonial

adjective
not forming colonies
6.7
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.kə.ˈloʊ.niː.əl/ /; / /nən.kə.ˈloʊ.niː.əl/ (AmE), /nɒn.kə.ˈləʊ.niː.əl/ /; / /nən.kə.ˈləʊ.niː.əl/ (BrE)
not forming or living in colonies; existing as separate, individual organisms rather than in connected or social groups.

Examples

Noncolonial jellyfish
Some jellyfish species are noncolonial and spend their entire lives as single individuals.
The noncolonial nature of these bacteria makes them harder to detect in group-based studies.
Unlike ants, which are colonial, most spiders are noncolonial and live alone.

  • synonym: solitary - living or existing alone
