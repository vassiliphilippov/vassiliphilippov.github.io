jive

verb
dance to jive music
Pronunciation: / dʒaɪv / (AmE), / dʒaɪv / (BrE)
to dance or act in a lively, energetic, or exaggerated way, especially to jazz or swing music

Examples

They jived to the upbeat music at the party.
At the dance competition, couples jived across the floor with impressive energy.
He jived with his friends, laughing and moving to the rhythm of the band.
