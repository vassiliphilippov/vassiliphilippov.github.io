surtax

noun
additional tax levied on taxed income
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜːrˌtæks / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.tæks / (BrE)
An additional tax imposed on certain types of income or transactions that have already been taxed.

Examples

A surtax on income
Critics argue that the new surtax will disproportionately affect middle-income families.
Residents earning above a certain threshold must pay an additional surtax each year.
The government decided to impose a surtax on luxury goods to increase revenue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tax - a compulsory financial charge imposed by a government
