stretch reflex

noun
muscle reflex responding to stretch
9.1 5.3
Pronunciation: /stretʃ ˈriː.fl.eks/ (AmE), /stretʃ ˈre.fl.eks/ (BrE)
a reflex contraction of a muscle in response to stretching within the muscle, important in maintaining posture and balance

Examples

The stretch reflex response
Damage to nerves can weaken or eliminate the stretch reflex in certain limbs.
Doctors test the stretch reflex by tapping the knee with a small hammer.
The stretch reflex helps athletes maintain balance during rapid movements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: myotatic reflex - another term for the stretch reflex
  • synonym: tendon reflex - a reflex action produced by the stimulation of tendons
