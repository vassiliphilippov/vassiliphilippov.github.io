hypoconid

noun
molar tooth cusp
Pronunciation: / haɪˈpɑːkənɪd / (AmE), / haɪˈpɒkənɪd / (BrE)
the outer back cusp of a lower molar tooth in mammals

Examples

The hypoconid cusp
The hypoconid is often used by paleontologists to distinguish between different mammal species.
Damage to the hypoconid can affect how efficiently a mammal chews its food.
Researchers measured the size of the hypoconid in several fossilized jawbones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hypocone - the outer back cusp of an upper molar tooth in mammals
  • antonym: protoconid - the main front cusp of a lower molar tooth in mammals
