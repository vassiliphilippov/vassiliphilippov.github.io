Diphyllobothrium

proper noun
tapeworm genus
9.9
Pronunciation: / daɪˌfɪloʊˈbɒθriəm / (AmE), / daɪˌfɪləˈbɒθriəm / (BrE)
a genus of tapeworms that can infect humans and other animals, often through consumption of raw or undercooked fish

Examples

Diphyllobothrium species

Properly cooking fish is important to prevent Diphyllobothrium from infecting humans.
