abstemiously

adverb
in a restrained or moderate way
9.1 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / …ôbÀàstiÀêmi…ôsli / (AmE), / …ôbÀàstiÀêmi…ôsli / (BrE)
In a moderate or restrained manner, especially with regard to eating and drinking; without overindulgence.

Examples

To abstemiously drink
Despite celebrating, he drank abstemiously all evening, avoiding overindulgence.
Living abstemiously helped her maintain her health and clear mind.
She dined abstemiously, choosing only a salad and water for her meal.

Synonyms

    6.5 0.05/m

  • sparingly
    3.5 1.45/m
    sparingly means using or giving little of something by choice, while abstemiously stresses avoiding overindulgence in eating or drinking.
  • lightly
    1.0 7.93/m
    lightly means doing something with a small amount or gentle touch, while abstemiously means avoiding excess in eating or drinking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moderately - in a way that is not excessive
