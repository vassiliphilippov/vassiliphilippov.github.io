tho

adverb
informal 'though'
8.6
Pronunciation: / ðoʊ / (AmE), / ðəʊ / (BrE)
informal spelling of 'though', used especially in online communication or texting to mean 'however' or 'nevertheless'

Examples

tho used online
I really wanted to stay home, tho my friends convinced me to go out.
The movie was long, tho it was definitely worth watching.
He failed the test, tho he had studied all night.
