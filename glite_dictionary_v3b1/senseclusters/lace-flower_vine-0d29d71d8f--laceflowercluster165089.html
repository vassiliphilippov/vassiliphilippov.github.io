lace-flower vine

noun
creeping Central American plant with fringed white flowers
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪs ˌflaʊər vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈleɪs ˌflaʊə vaɪn / (BrE)
a low-growing creeping perennial plant of Central America with deeply fringed white flowers, sometimes placed in the genus Episcia
