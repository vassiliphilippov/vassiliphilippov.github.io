autocross

noun
car handling competition
Pronunciation: / ˈɔtoʊˌkrɔs / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊˌkrɒs / (BrE)
a timed motorsport event where drivers navigate a course marked by cones to test their speed and handling skills

Examples

Autocross event
He entered his first autocross event to test his driving skills against the clock.
Many car enthusiasts enjoy autocross because it is accessible and emphasizes driver ability.
The parking lot was transformed into an autocross course for the weekend competition.
He spent weeks preparing his car for the upcoming autocross event.
Many beginners try autocross to improve their driving skills in a safe environment.
The club organizes a monthly autocross where drivers compete for the best time.

  • synonym: motorsport - competitive sporting events involving motor vehicles
  • synonym: rallycross - a form of car racing on mixed surfaces with multiple cars competing together
