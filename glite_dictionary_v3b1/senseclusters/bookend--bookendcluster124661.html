bookend

verb
to frame at both ends
5.4
Pronunciation: /bʊk.ˈend/ /; / /bʊk.ˈənd/ (AmE), /bʊk.ˈend/ /; / /bʊk.ˈənd/ (BrE)
to be placed or to place something at the beginning and end of something else, framing it.

Examples

To bookend a show
The conference was bookended by keynote speeches from two renowned scientists.
Her career was bookended by major awards at the start and end.
The day was bookended with a sunrise hike and a sunset dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enclose - to surround or close off on all sides
  • synonym: frame - to enclose or surround something at the edges
