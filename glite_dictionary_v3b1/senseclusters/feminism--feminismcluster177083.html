feminism

noun
belief in equal rights for women
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛm.əˌnɪz.əm /; / ˈfɛmɪˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈfɛm.ɪ.nɪz.əm /; / ˈfɛmɪnɪz(ə)m /; / ˈfɛmɪnɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief in and advocacy for equal rights and opportunities for women (often also supporting equality for all genders)

Related Terms

  • synonym: gender equality - the state of equal access to resources and opportunities regardless of gender
  • antonym: misogyny - dislike of, contempt for, or ingrained prejudice against women
  • antonym: sexism - prejudice or discrimination based on a person's sex or gender
