Valeriana officinalis

noun
species of valerian plant used medicinally
Pronunciation: / vəˌlɪriːˈænə ˌɒfɪsɪˈnɑːlɪs / (AmE), / vəˌlɪəriːˈɑːnɪə ˌɒfɪsɪˈneɪlɪs / (BrE)
A tall perennial plant of the valerian family, known for its fragrant flowers and rhizomes used in herbal medicine.
