butt

verb
be adjacent or share a boundary
9.9
Pronunciation: / bʌt / (AmE), / bʌt / (BrE)
(of land or property) to be adjacent to or share a boundary with something else.

Examples

The land butts up
Our garden butts up against the old churchyard wall.
The property butted the main road on its eastern side.
The two fields butt each other without any fence between them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adjoin - to be next to or joined with something
  • synonym: border - to form an edge along or beside something
