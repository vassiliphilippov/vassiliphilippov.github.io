Synonyms

  • emit
    4.4 8/m
    emit is a neutral technical word for sending out light, heat, or gas, while radiate suggests spreading from a center.
  • give off
    2.6 4/m
    give off produces smells, heat, or light in everyday speech, while radiate implies energy spreading from a central source.
  • emanate
    6.5 6/m
    emanate often describes smells, sounds, or feelings that come out from somewhere, while radiate refers to energy sent from a center.
  • give forth
    5.5 0.08/m
    give forth sounds old or formal and means to send out a sound or smell, while radiate is more common for energy from a center.
  • ray
    5.8 0.1/m
    ray sends out a beam or line of light, while radiate describes energy spreading outward from a central source.
