Hispaniola

proper noun
Caribbean island shared by Haiti and the Dominican Republic
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪspænˈjoʊlə / (AmE), / ˌhɪspanˈjəʊlə / (BrE)
an island in the Caribbean, divided between Haiti in the west and the Dominican Republic in the east

Examples

Hispaniola island
Christopher Columbus landed on Hispaniola during his first voyage to the Americas.
Deforestation has become a significant issue on Hispaniola in recent decades.
The island of Hispaniola is home to both Haiti and the Dominican Republic.
