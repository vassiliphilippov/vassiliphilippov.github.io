homo politicus

noun
person deeply involved in politics
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmoʊ pəˈlɪtɪkəs / (AmE), / ˈhəʊməʊ pɒˈlɪtɪkəs / (BrE)
A person who is actively and deeply involved in politics, often advocating for social or public issues.
