forced

adjective
compelled; not voluntary

Pronunciation: / f…îrst / (AmE), / f…îÀêst / (BrE)
done because of compulsion, necessity, or external circumstances; not done willingly or naturally

Examples

Forced smile

Synonyms

  • enforced
