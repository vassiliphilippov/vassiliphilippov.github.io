devoutness

noun
religious devotion or commitment
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvaʊtnəs / (AmE), / dɪˈvaʊtnəs / (BrE)
the quality of having deep religious feeling or commitment

Examples

Deep devoutness in faith
Despite his public devoutness, he was generous to people of all faiths.
Her devoutness was evident in the way she attended services every week.
The devoutness of the monks impressed all who visited the monastery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: impiety - lack of piety or reverence
  • synonym: piety - the quality of being religious or reverent
