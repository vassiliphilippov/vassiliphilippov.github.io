muskiness

noun
distinctive musky odor quality
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌs.ki.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmʌs.ki.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of having a smell reminiscent of musk

Examples

The muskiness of wine
Some wines are prized for their subtle muskiness that adds complexity to the aroma.
The muskiness of the perfume lingered in the air long after she left the room.
The old library had a faint muskiness mixed with the scent of ancient books.
