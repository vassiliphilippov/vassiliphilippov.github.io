perfectionistic

adjective
demanding flawlessness
Pronunciation: / pərˌfɛkʃəˈnɪstɪk / (AmE), / pəˌfɛkʃəˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
having a tendency to want everything to be perfect and free from mistakes

Examples

Perfectionistic approach
Her perfectionistic approach to work means she checks every detail several times.
A perfectionistic mindset can make it hard to finish creative projects.
He became less perfectionistic after realizing that mistakes are part of learning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: careless - not giving enough attention or thought to avoiding harm or errors
  • synonym: meticulous - showing great attention to detail
  • synonym: perfectionist - a person who refuses to accept any standard short of perfection
