substitutional

adjective
involving replacement
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbstɪˈtuːʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌsʌbstɪˈtjuːʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving the act of replacing one thing with another

Examples

Substitutional change in compounds
The chemist described the compound as having a substitutional structure.
In some alloys, atoms can occupy substitutional positions within the crystal lattice.
The substitutional method allows one element to replace another in the reaction.

Related Terms

  • antonym: original - existing from the beginning; not replaced
  • synonym: replacement - the act of substituting or replacing
