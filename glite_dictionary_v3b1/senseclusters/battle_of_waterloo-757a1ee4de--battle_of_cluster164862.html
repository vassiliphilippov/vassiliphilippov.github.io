Battle of Waterloo

noun
battle in 1815 where Napoleon was defeated
5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbæt.əl əv wɔː.tər.ˈluː/ /; / /ˈbæt.əl əv wɑː.tər.ˈluː/ (AmE), /ˈbæt.əl əv wɔː.tə.ˈluː/ (BrE)
the decisive battle fought on 18 June 1815 near Waterloo, in which the allied forces under the Duke of Wellington and General Blücher defeated Napoleon's French army, ending his rule as Emperor of the French

Examples

The Battle of Waterloo 1815
Many historians regard the Battle of Waterloo as a turning point in European history.
She wrote her thesis on the strategies used during the Battle of Waterloo.
The Battle of Waterloo marked the final defeat of Napoleon Bonaparte.
