pivotal point

noun
crucial turning moment or event
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪvətl pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈpɪvətl pɔɪnt / (BrE)
a critical moment or event when significant change takes place or a decisive outcome is determined

Examples

The pivotal point moment
Discovering the new evidence became the pivotal point of the trial.
Her decision to study abroad was a pivotal point in her career.
The debate marked a pivotal point in the election campaign.

Related Terms

  • synonym: turning point - a time at which a decisive change occurs
