ELN

noun
electronic lab notebook
6.8 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / i 5bl 5bn / (AmE), / id0 5bl 5bn / (BrE)
A digital system used to record, organize, and store laboratory notes, experimental procedures, and research data (electronic lab notebook).

Examples

Lab ELN system
Researchers store experimental logs in the ELN to ensure reproducibility and track protocol changes.
The lab switched from paper to an ELN that timestamps entries and makes records searchable.
Before publishing, she exported the raw data from the ELN for archival and sharing with collaborators.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electronic lab notebook - a digital notebook used to record laboratory work and data; same concept as ELN
  • antonym: paper lab notebook - a physical paper notebook used to record experiments and observations; traditional alternative to an ELN
