shareholder

noun
person or entity owning company shares
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛrˌhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈʃeəˌhəʊldə / (BrE)
a person or organization that owns shares in a company or corporation

Examples

Major shareholder
As a major shareholder, she has significant influence on the board's decisions.
Legal rights and responsibilities vary depending on whether a shareholder owns common or preferred shares.
The company announced a dividend for every shareholder at the annual meeting.
