antivirus

adjective
protecting against viruses
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈvaɪrəs / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈvaɪərəs / (BrE)
designed to prevent, detect, or remove computer viruses, especially in software or systems

Examples

Antivirus software
It is important to install antivirus software on your computer to protect your data.
The company updated its antivirus systems to defend against new threats.
Many email services now include antivirus filters to block harmful attachments.
