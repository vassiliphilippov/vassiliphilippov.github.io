commentariat

noun
media commentators
8.4
Pronunciation: /kɑː.mənt.ˈæ.r.iː.ət/ (AmE), /kɒ.mən.ˈteə.ri.ət/ (BrE)
the group of people, especially in the media, who regularly comment on or analyze current events and public affairs

Examples

The commentariat debates politics
The political commentariat debated the implications of the election results on television all night.
Members of the commentariat often shape public opinion through their columns and broadcasts.
Social media has given rise to a new kind of commentariat with influencers and bloggers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: journalist - a person who writes for newspapers or reports news
  • synonym: pundit - an expert who gives opinions or commentary in the media
