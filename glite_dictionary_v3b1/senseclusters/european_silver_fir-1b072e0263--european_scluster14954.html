European silver fir

noun
a European evergreen timber tree
8.2
Pronunciation: / jʊˌroʊpiən ˈsɪlvər fɜr / (AmE), / jʊəˌrəʊpiən ˈsɪlvə fɜː / (BrE)
a tall, evergreen tree of central and southern Europe, with a regular crown and smooth grey bark, grown for timber

Examples

Tall European silver fir
Dense forests of European silver fir can be found throughout the Alps.
The European silver fir is commonly planted in parks for its attractive shape and foliage.
The timber from the European silver fir is highly valued for construction and furniture making.
