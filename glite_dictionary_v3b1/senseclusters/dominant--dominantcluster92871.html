dominant

adjective
most frequent or common; prevailing
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːmɪnənt / (AmE), / ˈdɒmɪnənt / (BrE)
more frequent or common than others; prevailing or most widespread in a particular context

Examples

Dominant language
English has become the dominant language in international business.
In the region, oak trees are the dominant species in the forest canopy.
The dominant trend in fashion this year features bold, bright colors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prevalent - widespread or commonly occurring
  • antonym: rare - not common or occurring infrequently
