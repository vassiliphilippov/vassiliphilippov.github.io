exempt

verb
officially free from a duty or obligation
3.9 3.4
Pronunciation: /ɪg.ˈzempt/ /; / /eg.ˈzempt/ (AmE), /ɪg.ˈzempt/ /; / /eg.ˈzempt/ (BrE)
