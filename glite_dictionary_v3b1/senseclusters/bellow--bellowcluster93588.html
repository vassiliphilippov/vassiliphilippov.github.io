bellow

noun
a loud, deep roar or shout
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛloʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɛləʊ / (BrE)
a very loud and deep shout or roar, similar to the sound made by a large animal

Examples

A loud bellow
His bellow echoed off the walls, startling everyone in the room.
The lion's bellow could be heard throughout the entire savanna.
With a furious bellow, the coach called the players back to the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: roar - a loud, deep, prolonged sound
  • antonym: whisper - a very quiet or soft spoken utterance
