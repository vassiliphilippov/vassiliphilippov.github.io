chanson de geste

noun
Old French epic poem
Pronunciation: / ʃɑ̃ˌsɔ̃ də ˈʒɛst / (AmE), / ʃɒ̃ˈsɒ̃ də ˈʒɛst / (BrE)
an Old French epic poem, typically recounting the heroic deeds of legendary figures, especially of the period from the 11th to 14th centuries

Examples

A chanson de geste poem
Roland is the hero of a well-known chanson de geste called 'La Chanson de Roland'.
Scholars study the chanson de geste to understand the values of medieval society.
The chanson de geste was a popular genre in medieval French literature.
