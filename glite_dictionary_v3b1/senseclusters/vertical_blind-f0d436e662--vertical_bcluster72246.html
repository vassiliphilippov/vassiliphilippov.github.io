vertical blind

noun
window blind with vertical slats
Pronunciation: / ˈvɝtɪkəl blaɪnd / (AmE), / ˈvɜːtɪkəl blaɪnd / (BrE)
A type of window blind consisting of long, vertical slats that can be rotated to control light and privacy.

Examples

A vertical blind slat
She prefers vertical blinds over curtains because they're easier to clean.
The living room has modern vertical blinds that allow you to adjust the light easily.
They replaced the old shades with white vertical blinds for a sleek look.
