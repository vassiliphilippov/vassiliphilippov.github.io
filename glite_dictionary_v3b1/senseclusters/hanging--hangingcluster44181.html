hanging

noun
the act of suspending something
Pronunciation: / ˈhæŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhæŋɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of suspending something from above so that it moves freely or is displayed

Examples

Hanging of paintings
At the museum, there will be a hanging of contemporary art pieces next week.
The hanging of the new chandelier transformed the dining room's appearance.
They attended the hanging of the tapestry in the main hall.
