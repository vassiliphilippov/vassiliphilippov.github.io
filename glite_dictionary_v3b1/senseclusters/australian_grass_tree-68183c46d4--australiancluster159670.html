Australian grass tree

noun
stout Australian shrub with clustered flowers
Pronunciation: / əˌstreɪliən ɡræs triː /; / əˈstreɪliən ˈɡræs ˌtri / (AmE), / ɒˌstreɪliən ɡrɑːs triː /; / ɒˈstreɪliən ˈɡrɑːs ˌtriː / (BrE)
a stout Australian shrub or perennial plant with a thick woody stem and grasslike leaves, belonging to the genus Xanthorrhoea
