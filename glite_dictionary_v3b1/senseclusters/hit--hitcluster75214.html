hit

noun
a conspicuous success
Pronunciation: / hɪt / (AmE), / hɪt / (BrE)
a person or thing that is very successful, especially in entertainment or a particular event

Examples

A hit song
Her debut album became an instant hit and stayed at the top of the charts for weeks.
The comedy show was a huge hit with audiences of all ages.
Their new product turned out to be a surprise hit during the holiday season.

Related Terms

  • antonym: failure - lack of success; the opposite of a hit in this context
  • synonym: success - the accomplishment of an aim or purpose
