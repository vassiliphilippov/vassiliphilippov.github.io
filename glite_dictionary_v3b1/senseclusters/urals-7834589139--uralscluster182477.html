Urals

proper noun
mountain range dividing Europe and Asia in Russia
6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈjʊr.əlz/ (AmE), /ˈjʊə.rəlz/ (BrE)
a mountain range in western Russia that extends from the Arctic Ocean to the Caspian Sea, traditionally considered the dividing line between Europe and Asia

Examples

The Urals mountains
The explorers crossed the Urals during their expedition from Europe to Asia.
The natural resources found in the Urals have long supported Russian industry.
Winter in the Urals is known for its harsh and frigid conditions.
