Holy Trinity

noun
the three persons of the Christian Godhead
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈhoʊ.li ˈtrɪ.nɪ.tiː/ /; / /ˈhoʊ.li ˈtrɪ.nə.tiː/ (AmE), /ˈhəʊ.li ˈtrɪ.nɪ.tiː/ /; / /ˈhəʊ.li ˈtrɪ.nə.tiː/ (BrE)
