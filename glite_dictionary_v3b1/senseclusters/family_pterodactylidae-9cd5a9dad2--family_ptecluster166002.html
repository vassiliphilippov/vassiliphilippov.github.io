family Pterodactylidae

noun
extinct reptile family in Pterosauria
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli tɛroʊˌdæktɪˌlaɪdi / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli tɛrəˌdæktɪˌliːdiː / (BrE)
A family of extinct reptiles in the order Pterosauria, known for their winged forms and presence in the Jurassic and Cretaceous periods.
