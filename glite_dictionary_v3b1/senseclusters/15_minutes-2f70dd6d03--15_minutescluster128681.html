15 minutes

noun
a quarter of an hour
Pronunciation: / fɪfˈtiːn ˈmɪnɪts / (AmE), / fɪfˈtiːn ˈmɪnɪts / (BrE)
A period of time equal to one quarter of an hour.

Examples

A 15 minutes period
