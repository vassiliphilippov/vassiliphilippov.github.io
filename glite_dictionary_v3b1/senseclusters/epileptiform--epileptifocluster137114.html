epileptiform

adjective
like an epileptic seizure
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪfɔrm / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪfɔːm / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of an epileptic seizure or its activity

Examples

Epileptiform discharges
The doctor noted epileptiform discharges on the patient's brain scan.
Certain medications can reduce epileptiform activity in people with epilepsy.
Researchers observed epileptiform patterns during the neurological examination.
