cooper

noun
barrel or tub maker
9.9 0.10/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàkuÀêp…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàkuÀêp…ô / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make or repair wooden barrels, casks, or tubs

Examples

A skilled cooper
A skilled cooper can make a watertight cask without using glue.
He apprenticed as a cooper before opening his own workshop.
The old village still has a cooper who crafts barrels by hand.
