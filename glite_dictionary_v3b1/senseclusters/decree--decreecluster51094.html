decree

verb
order or decide officially
4.1 4.3
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈkriː/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈkriː/ (BrE)
to officially order, decide, or declare something by authority

Examples

To decree a holiday
The King decreed a national day of celebration.
The court may decree the dissolution of the partnership if necessary.
The government decreed that all businesses must close by 8 p.m.
After a lengthy debate, the committee decreed that the new policy would take effect immediately.
The government decreed new regulations to protect the environment.
The king decreed that a festival would be held every spring.
