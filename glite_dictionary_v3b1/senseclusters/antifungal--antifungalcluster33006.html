antifungal

adjective
destroying or inhibiting fungi
1.0 3.6
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.tiˈfʌŋ.ɡəl / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈfʌŋ.ɡəl / (BrE)
capable of destroying or inhibiting the growth of fungi, especially used of substances or treatments

Examples

Antifungal treatment
Doctors prescribed an antifungal medication for the patient's infection.
Regular use of antifungal shampoo can help prevent dandruff caused by fungi.
This cream contains an antifungal agent to treat athlete’s foot.
