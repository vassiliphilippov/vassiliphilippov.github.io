watchless

adjective
without a watch
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑtʃləs / (AmE), / ˈwɒtʃləs / (BrE)
not wearing or having a watch on one's person

Examples

Watchless wrist
After losing his old watch, he spent several days feeling watchless and disoriented.
She went watchless on vacation, relying on her phone to check the time.
Many people are now watchless because they use their smartphones instead.

Related Terms
