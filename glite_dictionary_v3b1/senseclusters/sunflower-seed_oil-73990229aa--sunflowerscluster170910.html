sunflower-seed oil

noun
oil extracted from sunflower seeds
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌflaʊɚ sid ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌflaʊə siːd ɔɪl / (BrE)
a pale yellow oil extracted from sunflower seeds, commonly used in cooking and as a salad oil
