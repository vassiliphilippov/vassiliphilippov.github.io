draw up

verb
prepare or compose (a plan or document)
2.6 3.1
Pronunciation: /drɔː ʌp/ /; / /drɔː əp/ /; / /drɑː ʌp/ (AmE), /drɔː ʌp/ /; / /drɔː əp/ (BrE)
to prepare and write out plans, documents, or detailed instructions; to compose or draft something official or formal.

Examples

To draw up a contract
Lawyers often draw up wills and other legal documents for their clients.
She was asked to draw up the contract before the meeting began.
The committee will draw up a proposal for the new project by next week.
