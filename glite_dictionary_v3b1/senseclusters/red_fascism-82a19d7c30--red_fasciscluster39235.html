red fascism

noun
communist-fascist authoritarian ideology
3.5
Pronunciation: / rɛd ˈfæʃɪzəm / (AmE), / rɛd ˈfaʃɪzəm / (BrE)
A political ideology or system that blends elements of communism and fascism, often associated with authoritarian leftist regimes.

Examples

Red fascism ideology
Debates about red fascism often focus on the overlap between authoritarian leftist and rightist politics.
Some critics accused the regime of adopting policies resembling red fascism.
The term red fascism is used in political discourse to describe left-wing authoritarian movements.

  • synonym: fascism - an authoritarian and nationalistic right-wing system of government and social organization
