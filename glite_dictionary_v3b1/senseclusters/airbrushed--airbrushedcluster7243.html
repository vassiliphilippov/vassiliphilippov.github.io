airbrushed

adjective
digitally perfected
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌbrʌʃt / (AmE), / ˈeəˌbrʌʃt / (BrE)
made to look smoother or more perfect, especially by digitally altering photographs or images

Examples

Airbrushed photo image
The advertisement featured an airbrushed model with flawless skin.
Many celebrities appear airbrushed on magazine covers, hiding their real imperfections.
He prefers natural photos over airbrushed images that look unrealistic.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unedited - not changed or improved; in original form
