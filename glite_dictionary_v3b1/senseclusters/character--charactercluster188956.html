character

noun
person with distinctive or eccentric traits
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkærɪktɚ / (AmE), / ˈkærɪktə / (BrE)
a person, especially one with distinctive or unusual traits, often considered eccentric or unique

Examples

Funny character in town
Everyone in the village says Mr. Jones is quite a character, always telling strange stories.
She's a real character—you never know what she'll do next.
We met an interesting character at the café who travels the world alone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eccentric - a person who is considered odd or unconventional
  • synonym: personality - the combination of characteristics or qualities that form an individual's distinctive character
