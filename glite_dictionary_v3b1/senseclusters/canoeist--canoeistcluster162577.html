canoeist

noun
person who paddles a canoe
Pronunciation: / kəˈnuːɪst / (AmE), / kəˈnjuːɪst / (BrE)
a person who paddles or races a canoe, either recreationally or competitively

Examples

A canoeist paddling fast
As a competitive canoeist, she has won several national championships.
Every summer, the lake attracts many canoeists from around the country.
The experienced canoeist guided us safely down the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kayaker - person who paddles a kayak
