mug

noun
large cup with a handle
Pronunciation: / mʌɡ / (AmE), / mʌɡ / (BrE)
a large cup with a handle, used for drinking hot drinks, or the amount it holds

Examples

A coffee mug
I bought a souvenir mug from the museum gift shop.
She drank her morning coffee from her favorite mug.
The cupboard was full of colorful mugs of different shapes and sizes.
Could I have another mug of tea, please?
He drank a mug of hot chocolate before bed.
She poured herself a mug filled to the brim with coffee.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cup - a small, bowl-shaped drinking vessel without a handle or with a small one
  • antonym: glass - a drinking vessel made of glass, typically without a handle
