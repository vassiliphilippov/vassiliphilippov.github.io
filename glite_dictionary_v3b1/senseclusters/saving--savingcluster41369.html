saving

adjective
characterized by thriftiness
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈseɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person) showing or characterized by thriftiness or careful use of resources.

Examples

Saving habits
Despite his wealth, Robert remained a saving man who avoided unnecessary expenses.
Her saving habits helped the family get through difficult times.
They admired her saving ways, especially in a culture that valued extravagance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: spendthrift - a person who spends money in an extravagant, irresponsible way
  • synonym: thrifty - using money and resources carefully and not wastefully
