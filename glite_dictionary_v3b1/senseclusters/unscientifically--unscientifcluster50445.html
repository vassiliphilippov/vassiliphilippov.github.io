unscientifically

adverb
not in a scientific manner
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.saɪənˈtɪf.ɪk.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.saɪənˈtɪf.ɪk.li / (BrE)
In a way that is not scientific or does not follow the principles or methods of science.

Examples

Argue unscientifically
He was criticized for arguing unscientifically during the debate.
If you approach the problem unscientifically, the results may be invalid.
The data was collected unscientifically, leading to unreliable conclusions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: scientifically - in a scientific way; according to the principles of science
