noooo

interjection
emphatic protest
Pronunciation: / noʊːːː / (AmE), / nəʊːːː / (BrE)
an extended form of 'no' used to express strong emotion, protest, or distress

Examples

Shout noooo loudly
When he realized he had missed the last train, he shouted noooo in frustration.
She dropped her ice cream and cried noooo as it hit the ground.
Watching the hero fall in the movie, the audience gasped and some whispered noooo.

Related Terms

  • synonym: no - a word used to express refusal or disagreement
  • antonym: yes - a word used to express agreement or affirmation
