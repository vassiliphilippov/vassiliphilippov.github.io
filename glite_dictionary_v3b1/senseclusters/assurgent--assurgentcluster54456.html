assurgent

adjective
growing upward (botanical term)
Pronunciation: / əˈsɜrdʒənt / (AmE), / əˈsɜːdʒənt / (BrE)
(of a stem or leaf) growing or extending upward, especially at an angle rather than strictly vertical.

Examples

Assurgent stem
In the field guide, an assurgent branch was noted for its upward angle.
Some species have leaves that are distinctly assurgent, aiding them in climbing.
The plant's assurgent stems allow it to receive more sunlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ascending - moving or growing upward
