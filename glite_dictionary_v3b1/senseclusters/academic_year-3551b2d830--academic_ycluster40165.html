academic year

noun
school or university teaching period
Pronunciation: / əˌkædəmɪk ˈjɪr / (AmE), / əˌkædəmɪk ˈjɪə / (BrE)
The period of the year during which students attend school or university and classes are held.

Examples

The academic year
During the academic year, students are expected to maintain regular attendance.
She plans to spend the next academic year studying abroad in France.
The university’s academic year begins in September and ends in June.

Related Terms

  • synonym: school year - the period of time when a school holds classes
