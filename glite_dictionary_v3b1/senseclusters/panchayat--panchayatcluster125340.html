panchayat

noun
village council in India or southern Pakistan
9.9 8.3
Pronunciation: / pʌnˈtʃɑː.jɑt / (AmE), / pʌnˈtʃɑː.jət / (BrE)
A local village council, especially in India or southern Pakistan, responsible for making decisions and settling disputes within a community.

Examples

Village panchayat meeting
Many rural areas in India hold regular panchayat meetings to make local decisions.
She was recently elected to the panchayat, becoming the first woman to serve on the council.
The panchayat plays a crucial role in resolving disputes within the village.
