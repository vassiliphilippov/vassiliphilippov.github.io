well disposed

adjective
inclined to be friendly or helpful
5.9
Pronunciation: / wɛl dɪˈspoʊzd / (AmE), / wɛl dɪˈspəʊzd / (BrE)
inclined to be friendly, helpful, or supportive toward someone or something

Examples

Be well disposed to ideas
He is generally well disposed to new ideas that benefit the community.
She was well disposed towards her new neighbors, always eager to lend a helping hand.
The staff at the hotel were well disposed to answer any questions we had.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hostile - unfriendly; antagonistic
