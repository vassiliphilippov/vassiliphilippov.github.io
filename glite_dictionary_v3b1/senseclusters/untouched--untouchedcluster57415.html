untouched

adjective
not affected or changed; not in contact
3.0 1.47/m
Pronunciation: / ÊŒnËˆtÊŒtÊƒt / (AmE), / ÊŒnËˆtÊŒtÊƒt / (BrE)
Not affected, changed, or used; not having come in contact with something.

Examples

Untouched by disaster
After the storm, the garden appeared strangely untouched by the wind and rain.
Please leave the evidence untouched until the police arrive.
The top of the mountain remained untouched by climbers for decades.
