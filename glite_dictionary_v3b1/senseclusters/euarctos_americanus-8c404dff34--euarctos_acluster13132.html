Euarctos americanus

noun
American black bear species
Pronunciation: / juˈɑrktəs əˌmɛrɪˈkeɪnəs / (AmE), / juːˈɑːktɒs əˌmɛrɪˈkeɪnəs / (BrE)
A species of bear native to North America, usually brown or black in color, smaller and less aggressive than the brown bear; also known as the American black bear.
