mezzo-rilievo

noun
medium-depth sculptural relief
9.9 9.4
Pronunciation: /ˈmet.soʊ rɪ.ˈliː.voʊ/ /; / /ˈmed.zoʊ rɪ.ˈliː.voʊ/ (AmE), /ˈmet.səʊ rɪ.ˈliː.vəʊ/ /; / /ˈmed.zəʊ rɪ.ˈliː.vəʊ/ (BrE)
A sculptural relief in which figures are raised more than in low relief but less than in high relief; a medium-depth relief carving.
