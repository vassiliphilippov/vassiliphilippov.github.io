safety school

noun
college or university with likely admission
1.6 3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪfti skul / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti skuːl / (BrE)
a college or university with a high acceptance rate, to which a student applies because they are confident of being admitted

Examples

A safety school choice
After getting accepted by all her safety schools, she finally relaxed.
It's smart to include at least one safety school when making a college application list.
She applied to a safety school to ensure she had a guaranteed college option.
