sponge

noun
person who freeloads from others
7.2 0.19/m
Pronunciation: / spÊŒndÊ’ / (AmE), / spÊŒndÊ’ / (BrE)
a person who habitually takes advantage of others' generosity or hospitality without offering anything in return

Examples

A sponge at dinner
He didn't realize his friend was a sponge until he was constantly paying for every meal.
It's frustrating when a sponge shows up to parties expecting to eat and drink for free every time.
Tired of being treated like a sponge, she finally started contributing to the household expenses.
