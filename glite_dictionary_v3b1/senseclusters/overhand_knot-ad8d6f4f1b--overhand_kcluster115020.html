overhand knot

noun
simple basic knot
4.1 3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈoʊ.vər.hænd nɑːt/ (AmE), /ˈəʊ.və.hænd nɒt/ (BrE)
A simple knot made by passing the end of a piece of rope or cord over itself and drawing it through the resulting loop.

Examples

An overhand knot tied
He tied the ends of the string together with an overhand knot to keep it from unraveling.
If you're learning to sail, mastering the overhand knot is essential.
The simplest way to secure a tag on a bag is with an overhand knot.
