Argiope aurantia

noun
a North American garden spider
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈdʒaɪ.əpi ɔːrˈæn.ʃə / (AmE), / ɑːˈdʒaɪ.əpi ɔːˈræntiə / (BrE)
A large, brightly colored North American garden spider known for its distinctive yellow and black markings and orb-shaped webs.
