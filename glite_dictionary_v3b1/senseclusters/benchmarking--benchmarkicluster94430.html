benchmarking

noun
performance comparison process
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛntʃˌmɑrkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɛntʃˌmɑːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of comparing a company's performance, products, or processes to industry standards or best practices

Examples

Benchmarking business performance
Many organizations use benchmarking to identify areas where they can improve their efficiency.
The manager suggested benchmarking their customer service against leading companies in the industry.
Through benchmarking, the team discovered new strategies to enhance their product quality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assessment - the process of measuring or judging the quality of something
  • synonym: evaluation - the act of assessing or judging something
