Red Planet

noun
Mars, the fourth planet from the sun
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛd ˈplænət / (AmE), / ˌrɛd ˈplænɪt / (BrE)
Mars, the fourth planet from the sun in the solar system, known for its reddish appearance due to iron-rich minerals on its surface.

Examples

The Red Planet Mars
A new spacecraft will soon launch to study the surface of the Red Planet more closely.
In ancient times, people gazed at the Red Planet and wondered if it was inhabited.
Scientists have discovered evidence of liquid water on the Red Planet.
