dittany of Crete

noun
aromatic shrub from Crete
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪtəni əv ˈkriːt / (AmE), / ˈdɪtəni əv ˈkriːt / (BrE)
a dwarf aromatic shrub (Origanum dictamnus) native to Crete, known for its soft, hairy leaves and pink-purple flowers, often used in herbal remedies
