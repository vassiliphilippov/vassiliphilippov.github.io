develop

verb
expand or elaborate a musical idea
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvɛləp / (AmE), / dɪˈvɛləp / (BrE)
(in music) to elaborate or expand a musical idea by working out its rhythmic and harmonic changes.

Examples

To develop a theme
A skilled jazz musician can develop a simple tune by improvising new harmonies and rhythms.
During the performance, the theme was developed with unexpected chord progressions.
The composer decided to develop the original melody into a more complex movement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elaborate - to add details, as in expanding a musical idea
