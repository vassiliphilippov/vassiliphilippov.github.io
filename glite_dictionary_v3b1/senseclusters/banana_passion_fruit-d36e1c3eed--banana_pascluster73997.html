banana passion fruit

noun
an elongated tropical fruit of several Passiflora species
7.2
Pronunciation: / bəˈnænə ˈpæʃən ˌfrut / (AmE), / bəˈnænə ˈpæʃən ˌfruːt / (BrE)
a tropical fruit with an elongated shape and soft, sweet pulp, produced by several species of Passiflora, cultivated for food and juice.
