rule-governed

adjective
regulated by rules or laws
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈruːl ˌɡʌvərnd / (AmE), / ˈruːl ˌɡʌvənd / (BrE)
controlled or determined by rules, customs, or natural laws; following a set of established guidelines or principles.

Examples

Rule-governed activity
Chess is a highly rule-governed activity, requiring players to adhere strictly to established procedures.
His rule-governed approach to science ensured that every experiment was conducted carefully and consistently.
Language is often described as a rule-governed system that enables effective communication.

Related Terms

  • antonym: arbitrary - based on random choice or personal whim, rather than rules
  • synonym: regulated - controlled by rules or laws
