womanish

adjective
characteristic of women (often disapproving)
7.2 7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈwʊ.mə.nɪʃ/ (AmE), /ˈwʊ.mən.ɪʃ/ (BrE)
showing qualities or behavior thought to be typical of women, often used disapprovingly for men

Examples
