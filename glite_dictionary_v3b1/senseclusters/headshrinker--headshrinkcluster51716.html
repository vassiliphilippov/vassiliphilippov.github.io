headshrinker

noun
maker of shrunken heads
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌʃrɪŋkər / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌʃrɪŋkə / (BrE)
a person who makes or prepares shrunken heads, especially as part of traditional or ritual practices

Examples

Tribal headshrinker
The tribe's headshrinker was highly respected for his knowledge of ancient rituals.
A skilled headshrinker could preserve the features of the head during the process.
Historians studied the tools used by a headshrinker in the remote Amazon region.
