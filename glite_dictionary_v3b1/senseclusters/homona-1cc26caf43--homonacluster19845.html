Homona

noun
genus of moths (tea tortrix)
9.9
Pronunciation: / hoʊˈmoʊnə / (AmE), / həʊˈməʊnə / (BrE)
a genus of moths in the family Tortricidae, commonly known as tea tortrix, some species of which are agricultural pests
