function word

noun
a word serving a grammatical purpose
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌŋkʃən wɜrd / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋkʃən wɜːd / (BrE)
A word that primarily serves to express grammatical relationships rather than convey lexical meaning, such as conjunctions, prepositions, articles, or pronouns.

Examples

A function word type
Because function words are usually unstressed, they can be challenging for language learners to hear clearly.
When learning a new language, it's important to recognize the role of function words.
Words like 'and', 'but', and 'the' are examples of function words in English.
