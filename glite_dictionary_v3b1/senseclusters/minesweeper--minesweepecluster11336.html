minesweeper

noun
ship that clears marine mines
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪnˌswiːpər / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnswiːpə / (BrE)
a ship equipped to detect and remove or destroy marine mines

Examples

A minesweeper ship
During the war, a minesweeper played a vital role in keeping the supply routes safe.
The crew of the minesweeper worked in dangerous waters to protect other vessels.
The navy deployed a minesweeper to clear the harbor before the cargo ships arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: minehunter - ship that detects and destroys individual mines
