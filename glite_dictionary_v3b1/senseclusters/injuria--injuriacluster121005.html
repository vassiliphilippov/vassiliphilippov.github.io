injuria

noun
legal injury
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʒʊəriə / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒʊəriə / (BrE)
a legal injury or wrongful act, especially as defined in Roman law

Examples

Roman law injuria
In Roman law, an injuria could result in compensation for the victim.
The scholar explained how the concept of injuria influenced later legal systems.
A deliberate insult was sometimes considered an injuria in ancient courts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tort - a wrongful act leading to legal liability
