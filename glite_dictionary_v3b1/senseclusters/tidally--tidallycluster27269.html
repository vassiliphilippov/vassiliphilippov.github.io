tidally

adverb
in relation to tides
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪdəli / (AmE), / ˈtaɪdəli / (BrE)
in a way that is related to or caused by tides

Examples

Tidally rising water
The river is tidally influenced, with water levels rising and falling each day.
Some coastal wetlands are tidally flooded during high tide.
The bridge was designed to withstand tidally driven currents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tidal - relating to or affected by tides
