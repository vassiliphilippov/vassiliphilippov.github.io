drown

verb
to cover or submerge in liquid
5.8
Pronunciation: / draʊn / (AmE), / draʊn / (BrE)
to cover or submerge completely in a liquid

Examples

Drown pancakes in syrup
Her pancakes drown in syrup every morning because she loves it so sweet.
The flowers will drown if the pot has no drainage holes.
The vegetables were completely drowned in butter and sauce.
