capital stock

noun
nominal value of a corporation's issued shares
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkæpɪtəl stɑk /; / ˈkæpɪtl stɑk / (AmE), / ˈkæpɪtəl stɒk /; / ˈkæpɪtl stɒk / (BrE)
the total number or value of shares that a corporation is authorized to issue, representing ownership in the company
