suckitude

noun
state of being extremely bad (slang)
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌkɪˌtuːd / (AmE), / ˈsʌkɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
(slang) the state or condition of being extremely bad or unpleasant; especially used humorously or informally to describe something of poor quality.

Examples

The suckitude of weather
I can't believe the suckitude of today's weather; it's relentless rain all week.
The suckitude of the weather this spring has made outdoor events a challenge.
We canceled our plans because of the weather's suckitude.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awfulness - the state of being extremely bad or unpleasant
  • antonym: greatness - the state of being extremely good or impressive
