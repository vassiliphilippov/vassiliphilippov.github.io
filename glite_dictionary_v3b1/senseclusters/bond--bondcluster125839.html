bond

verb
raise funds by issuing bonds
Pronunciation: / bɑnd / (AmE), / bɒnd / (BrE)
(of a government or company) to raise money by issuing bonds to investors.

Examples

To bond for funding
Many municipalities bond to pay for infrastructure improvements when immediate funds are lacking.
The city plans to bond next year to finance the new stadium construction.
The company decided to bond in order to cover its expansion costs.
