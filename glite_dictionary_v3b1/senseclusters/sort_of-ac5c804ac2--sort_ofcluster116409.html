sort of

adverb
to some degree; somewhat
Pronunciation: / sɔrt əv / (AmE), / sɔːt əv / (BrE)
to some degree; to a certain extent, but not completely or exactly

Examples

He sort of agrees
He sort of understands the rules, but he still makes some mistakes.
I was sort of surprised by her reaction, but not completely shocked.
The weather is sort of chilly today, so you might want a sweater.
