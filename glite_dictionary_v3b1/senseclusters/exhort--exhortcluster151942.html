Synonyms

  • urge

    urge is to strongly encourage, persuade, or advise someone to do something, while exhort is more formal and public in tone.
  • press
    3.9 13/m
    press is to insistently urge or demand action, often repeatedly, while exhort is more about strong moral or public encouragement.
  • encourage
    2.3 50/m
    encourage gives support and confidence to help someone try or continue, while exhort is stronger and more forceful urging.
  • spur
    4.1 4/m
