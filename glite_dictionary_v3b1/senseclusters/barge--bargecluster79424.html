barge

noun
large flat-bottomed boat for heavy loads
6.4
Pronunciation: / bɑrdʒ / (AmE), / bɑːdʒ / (BrE)
a large, flat-bottomed boat used for carrying heavy goods, especially on rivers or canals

Examples

A cargo barge
A tourist could take a leisurely cruise on a traditional barge through the countryside.
The barge slowly made its way down the canal, loaded with coal.
Workers used a barge to transport building materials along the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boat - a small vessel for traveling over water
  • degree lower: canoe - a narrow, light boat pointed at both ends and moved with a paddle
  • degree higher: ship - a large vessel for transporting people or goods by sea
