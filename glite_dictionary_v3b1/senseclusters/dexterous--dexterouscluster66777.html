dexterous

adjective
skillful, especially with the hands
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛk.stɚ.əs / (AmE), / ˈdɛk.stər.əs / (BrE)
showing skill, especially with the hands; skillful in performing tasks

Examples

A dexterous hand
His dexterous handling of the situation saved the project from failure.
She quickly repaired the watch with her dexterous fingers.
The surgeon’s dexterous hands made the complex procedure look effortless.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clumsy - awkward in movement or handling things
  • synonym: deft - skillful and quick in movement
