nylon

noun
synthetic fabric or material
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪˌlɑn /; / ˈnaɪ.lɑn / (AmE), / ˈnaɪlɒn /; / ˈnaɪ.lɒn / (BrE)
a strong, lightweight synthetic material used especially for making fibers, textiles, and plastics

Examples

Strong nylon fabric
Manufacturers often choose nylon for its durability and strength.
She wore a pair of nylon stockings to the party.
The jacket is made from waterproof nylon ideal for rainy weather.
Production of nylon revolutionized the textile industry in the twentieth century.
She wore a jacket made from nylon to keep dry during the rain.
The tent is constructed from lightweight nylon fabric.

Related Terms

  • synonym: polyester - a category of synthetic polymers used as a fabric or plastic
  • antonym: wool - natural fiber from sheep, often used for clothing
