miss

verb
fail to attend an event
Pronunciation: / mɪs / (AmE), / mɪs / (BrE)
to fail to be present at or take part in something (such as an event, class, or activity)

Examples

Miss a meeting
I'm disappointed I missed your birthday party last weekend.
She missed a crucial meeting because of a last-minute emergency.
Students who miss too many classes may fall behind in their work.
Several students missed class because of the flu outbreak.
The child had been missing for a week before the police found her.
Your name misses from the attendance list today.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absent - not present in a place, at an occasion, or as part of something
  • antonym: attend - to be present at an event or activity
  • synonym: skip - to deliberately not attend something
