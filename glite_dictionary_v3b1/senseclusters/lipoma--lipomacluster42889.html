lipoma

noun
benign fatty tissue tumor
8.4 6.3
Pronunciation: / lɪˈpoʊmə / (AmE), / lɪˈpəʊmə / (BrE)
A benign tumor made up of fatty tissue, typically soft and movable under the skin.

Examples

A lipoma tumor
Many people live with a lipoma for years without experiencing any symptoms.
She noticed a small, painless lipoma on her upper arm during a routine checkup.
The doctor diagnosed the lump as a harmless lipoma that did not require immediate removal.
