hyphal

adjective
of fungal filaments
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪfəl / (AmE), / ˈhaɪfəl / (BrE)
relating to or consisting of hyphae, the threadlike filaments that make up the body of fungi

Examples

Hyphal structure
The hyphal structure of the mold allowed it to absorb nutrients efficiently.
Scientists observed the hyphal growth under the microscope during the experiment.
A dense hyphal mat formed on the surface of the decomposing leaves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fungal - relating to or caused by fungi
