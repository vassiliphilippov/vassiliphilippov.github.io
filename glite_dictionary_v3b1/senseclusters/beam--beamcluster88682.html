beam

noun
long supporting piece in construction
6.0
Pronunciation: / biːm / (AmE), / biːm / (BrE)
a long, sturdy piece of wood, metal, or concrete, used to support parts of a building or other structure.

Examples

A wooden beam
A wooden beam ran across the ceiling of the old barn.
Due to damage, the main beam had to be replaced before construction could continue.
The workers installed a steel beam to support the roof.

Related Terms

  • synonym: girder - a large iron or steel beam used for building bridges and large buildings
