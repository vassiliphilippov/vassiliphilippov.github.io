ortolan

noun
small songbird eaten as a delicacy
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrtələn / (AmE), / ˈɔːtələn / (BrE)
a small brownish Old World bunting (Emberiza hortulana) often eaten as a delicacy, especially in France
