towardness

noun
friendliness or goodwill
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔrdnəs / (AmE), / ˈtəʊədnəs / (BrE)
a quality of being friendly, helpful, or cooperative toward others

Examples

Warm towardness
Her towardness made new students feel welcome during their first week.
The manager's towardness encouraged the team to work together more effectively.
He was known for his towardness and willingness to help anyone in need.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amiability - a friendly and pleasant manner
  • synonym: goodwill - a friendly or helpful attitude
  • antonym: hostility - unfriendliness or opposition
