coin

noun
digital currency unit (cryptocurrency)
6.0
Pronunciation: /kɔɪn/ (AmE), /kɔɪn/ (BrE)
a unit of digital currency native to a blockchain; a non-physical token representing value or a medium of exchange in a cryptocurrency system.
