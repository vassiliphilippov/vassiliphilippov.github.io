forest

verb
to plant trees on new land
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔr.əst / (AmE), / ˈfɒr.ɪst / (BrE)
to plant trees or establish a forest on land that previously did not have one

Examples

To forest empty land
Many countries are working to forest land that was previously used for agriculture.
The government plans to forest the area to combat soil erosion.
Volunteers helped forest the barren hillside last spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: afforest - to convert land into a forest, especially by planting trees
  • antonym: deforest - to clear trees or forests from land
