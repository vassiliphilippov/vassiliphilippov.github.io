declutch

verb
disengage a vehicle's clutch
Pronunciation: / diˈklʌtʃ / (AmE), / diˈklʌtʃ / (BrE)
To disengage the clutch of a vehicle, typically to separate the engine from the drivetrain.

Examples

To declutch a car
He declutched as the traffic light turned red.
Press the pedal to declutch before changing gears.
Skilled drivers can declutch quickly to avoid stalling the engine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clutch - mechanical device for engaging and disengaging power transmission
