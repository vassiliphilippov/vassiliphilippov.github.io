extracerebral

adjective
outside brain tissue
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstrəsəˈriːbrəl / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəsəˈriːbrəl / (BrE)
located or occurring outside the brain tissue

Examples

Extracerebral bleeding
The doctor explained that the bleeding was extracerebral and did not affect the brain directly.
An extracerebral tumor was found pressing against the outer surface of the brain.
The patient recovered quickly because the injury was extracerebral rather than inside the brain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intracerebral - located or occurring within the brain tissue
