Psittacosaurus

noun
a genus of primitive horned dinosaur
Pronunciation: / sɪˌtækəˈsɔrəs / (AmE), / sɪˌtækəˈsɔːrəs / (BrE)
a genus of small, herbivorous, beaked dinosaurs that lived during the Early Cretaceous period and are considered the most primitive horned dinosaurs.

Examples

A Psittacosaurus dinosaur
Children at the museum marveled at the life-sized model of Psittacosaurus.
Scientists believe Psittacosaurus used its beak to feed on tough vegetation.
The fossil remains of Psittacosaurus have been discovered across Asia.
