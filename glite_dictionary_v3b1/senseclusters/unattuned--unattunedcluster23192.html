unattuned

adjective
not in tune
7.5 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Àå ån.…ôÀàtjuÀênd / (AmE), / Àå ån.…ôÀàtjuÀênd / (BrE)
not tuned to the correct musical pitch or not in harmony with something.

Examples

Unattuned instrument
The violin sounded unattuned after months of not being played.
Her voice was unattuned to the choir's harmonies, making the performance uneven.
The old radio produced an unattuned sound that was difficult to listen to.

Synonyms

  • out of key
    5.8 0.04/m

  • untuned
    5.3 0.02/m

  • false
    6.1 0.19/m
