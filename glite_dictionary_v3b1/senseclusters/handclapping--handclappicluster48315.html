handclapping

noun
clapping game
2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈhænd.klæp.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈhænd.klæp.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of striking your hands together to make a sound, often as a game, to show approval, or as part of music or dance

During the ceremony, the audience showed their appreciation through enthusiastic handclapping.
