pheniramine

noun
an antihistamine drug
6.0
Pronunciation: / fəˈnɪrəˌmiːn / (AmE), / fɛˈnɪrəmiːn / (BrE)
an antihistamine drug used to relieve symptoms of allergies, such as rhinitis, skin rashes, and itching

Examples

Pheniramine allergy medicine
He developed drowsiness as a side effect of pheniramine.
Some over-the-counter cold medicines contain pheniramine as an active ingredient.
The doctor prescribed pheniramine to help control her allergy symptoms.

Related Terms
