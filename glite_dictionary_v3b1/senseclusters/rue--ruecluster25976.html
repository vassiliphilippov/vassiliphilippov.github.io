rue

noun
regret or sorrow for a wrong or loss
7.9 0.16/m
Pronunciation: / ruÀê / (AmE), / ruÀê / (BrE)
a feeling of regret or sorrow, especially for something wrong done or a disappointment

Examples

Deep rue
He was filled with rue after realizing the consequences of his actions.
The bitter rue in his voice was evident as he recounted the story.
To her lasting rue, she missed her only chance to see the play.

Synonyms

  • regret
    1.0 14/m
    regret is a common, clear feeling of sadness about a mistake or loss, while rue sounds more formal or literary.
  • ruefulness
    8.4 0.02/m
    ruefulness is a quiet, visible sorrow often shown in face or voice, while rue names the feeling itself.

Related Terms

  • antonym: joy - a feeling of great pleasure and happiness
  • synonym: regret - a feeling of sadness or disappointment over something that has happened or been done
  • synonym: remorse - deep regret or guilt for a wrong committed
