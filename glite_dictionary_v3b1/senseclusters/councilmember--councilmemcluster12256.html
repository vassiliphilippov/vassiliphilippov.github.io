councilmember

noun
member of a council
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊnsəlˌmɛmbər / (AmE), / ˈkaʊnsəlˌmɛmbə / (BrE)
a person who is a member of a council, especially in local government

Examples

A councilmember votes
The councilmember proposed a new initiative to improve public transportation in the city.
Several councilmembers voted against the budget increase during last night's meeting.
As a councilmember, she regularly meets with residents to hear their concerns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: councillor - an elected member of a council
  • antonym: mayor - the elected head of a city, town, or municipality
