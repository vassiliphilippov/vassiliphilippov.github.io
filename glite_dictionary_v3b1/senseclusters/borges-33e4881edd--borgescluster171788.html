Borges

proper noun
Argentinian writer (1899–1986)
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔr heɪz / (AmE), / ˈbɔː hɛz / (BrE)
(1899–1986) Argentinian writer known for his influential short stories, essays, and poems, especially for exploring philosophical and fantastical themes.
