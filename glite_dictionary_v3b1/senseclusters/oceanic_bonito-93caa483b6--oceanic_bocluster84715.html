oceanic bonito

noun
a fish used in Japanese cookery; may be skipjack tuna
5.8 6.7
Pronunciation: /oʊ.ʃi.ˈæn.ɪk bə.ˈniː.toʊ/ /; / /oʊ.si.ˈæn.ɪk bə.ˈniː.toʊ/ (AmE), /əʊ.ʃi.ˈæn.ɪk bə.ˈniː.təʊ/ /; / /əʊ.si.ˈæn.ɪk bə.ˈniː.təʊ/ (BrE)
a fish of the mackerel family whose flesh is dried and flaked for Japanese cookery, sometimes identified with the skipjack tuna

Examples

The oceanic bonito is prized in Japanese cuisine for its strong flavor.
Traditionally, oceanic bonito flakes are used to make dashi, a popular soup stock.
