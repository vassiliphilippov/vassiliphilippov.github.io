bleak

adjective
offering little hope; not encouraging
Pronunciation: / blik / (AmE), / bliːk / (BrE)
not hopeful or encouraging; offering little or no prospect of positive outcome

Examples

Bleak future
Her bleak outlook on life made it hard for her to stay motivated.
The news about the company's future was particularly bleak after the recent downturn.
They faced a bleak winter with no electricity and little food.
