overeducated

noun
person with excess education
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈɛdʒəˌkeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərˈɛdʒʊkeɪtɪd / (BrE)
a person who has more education than is needed or suitable for their job or situation

Examples

An overeducated person
Many overeducated are forced to accept jobs that do not use their degrees.
The company worried that hiring the overeducated might lead to dissatisfaction and quick turnover.
Some overeducated choose to retrain for careers that better match their interests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overqualified - having more skills or education than a job requires
  • antonym: undereducated - lacking sufficient education for a job or situation
