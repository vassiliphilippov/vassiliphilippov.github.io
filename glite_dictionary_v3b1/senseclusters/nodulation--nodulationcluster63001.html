nodulation

noun
formation of nodules
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑːdʒəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒdjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of forming small swellings or nodules, especially on plant roots, often as part of nitrogen fixation

Examples

Root nodulation
The nodulation of legume roots allows the plants to absorb more nitrogen from the soil.
Scientists study nodulation to improve crop yields and soil fertility.
Without proper nodulation, some plants struggle to grow in nitrogen-poor environments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nodule - a small swelling or lump, especially on a plant root
