nonnie

noun
grandmother term
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑni / (AmE), / ˈnɒni / (BrE)
an affectionate term for a grandmother or elderly woman, especially used by family members

Examples

My nonnie bakes cookies
Every Sunday the children visited nonnie and listened to her stories about the old country.
My nonnie makes the best homemade cookies during the holidays.
We called our grandmother nonnie because it made her smile every time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grandma - an informal term for grandmother
  • synonym: nana - an affectionate name for grandmother
