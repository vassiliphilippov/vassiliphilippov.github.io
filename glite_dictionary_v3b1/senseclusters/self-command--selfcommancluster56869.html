self-command

noun
resolute self-control
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf.kəˈmænd / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf.kəˈmɑːnd / (BrE)
the ability to control one's own actions, feelings, or impulses, especially with determination and resolve

Examples

Strong self-command
Despite the provocation, she showed admirable self-command and did not respond in anger.
Developing self-command is essential for achieving long-term goals.
His remarkable self-command allowed him to remain calm during the crisis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: self-control - the ability to control oneself, particularly one's emotions and desires
