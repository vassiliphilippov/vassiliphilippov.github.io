green monkey

noun
West African monkey with green-grey back
Pronunciation: / ɡrin ˈmʌŋki / (AmE), / ɡriːn ˈmʌŋki / (BrE)
a West African monkey with a greenish-grey back and yellow tail, commonly found in savannahs

Examples

The green monkey in savannah
The green monkey is often found in the savannah regions of West Africa.
Unlike other primates, the green monkey has a distinctive yellow tail and greenish-grey back.
Zookeepers observed the playful behavior of the young green monkey in its enclosure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vervet - a species of African monkey, closely related and similar in appearance
