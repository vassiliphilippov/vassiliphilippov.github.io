soapiness

noun
quality of being soapy
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊpinəs / (AmE), / ˈsəʊpinəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being soapy, or having soap on or in it

Examples

Skin soapiness
He disliked the lingering soapiness on his skin after a long shower.
The soapiness of the mixture suggested it still needed rinsing.
The soapiness of the water made it perfect for washing delicate fabrics.
