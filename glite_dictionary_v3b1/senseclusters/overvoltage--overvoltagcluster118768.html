overvoltage

noun
sudden voltage surge
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈvoʊltɪdʒ /; / ˈoʊvərˌvoʊltɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈvəʊltɪdʒ /; / ˈəʊvəˌvəʊltɪdʒ / (BrE)
a voltage in an electrical system that is higher than the normal or safe value, which can cause damage

Examples

Surge protectors are used to guard devices against overvoltage in power lines.
An overvoltage can damage sensitive electronic components if not properly managed.

  • synonym: surge - a sudden increase in electrical voltage
  • antonym: undervoltage - a drop in voltage below the normal level in an electrical system
