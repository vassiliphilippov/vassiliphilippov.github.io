bedbug

noun
small bloodsucking insect often found in beds
3.0 1.5
Pronunciation: /ˈbed.bʌg/ (AmE), /ˈbed.bʌg/ (BrE)
A small, bloodsucking insect that infests beds and bedding, typically feeding on humans at night.
