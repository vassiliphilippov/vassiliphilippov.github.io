bedbug

noun
small bloodsucking insect often found in beds
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdˌbʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈbɛdˌbʌɡ / (BrE)
A small, bloodsucking insect that infests beds and bedding, typically feeding on humans at night.

Examples

A bedbug insect
After traveling, she found several bedbugs in her suitcase.
Hotels sometimes get complaints about rooms with bedbugs biting guests at night.
The exterminator searched the apartment thoroughly for any signs of bedbugs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bug - a general term for an insect
  • synonym: flea - small, wingless, bloodsucking insect
