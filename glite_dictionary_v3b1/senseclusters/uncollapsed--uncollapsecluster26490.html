uncollapsed

adjective
still standing
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəˈlæpst / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəˈlæpst / (BrE)
not having fallen down, caved in, or given way; still standing or intact

Examples

Uncollapsed building
Despite the strong winds, the old barn remained uncollapsed throughout the storm.
Rescue teams searched for survivors in the uncollapsed sections of the building.
The bridge was one of the few structures left uncollapsed after the flood.
