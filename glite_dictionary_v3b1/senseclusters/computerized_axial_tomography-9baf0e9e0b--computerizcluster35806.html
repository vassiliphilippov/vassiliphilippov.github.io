computerized axial tomography

noun
medical X-ray imaging method
7.4
Pronunciation: / kəmˌpjuːtɚˌaɪzd ˌæksiəl təˈmɑːɡrəfi / (AmE), / kəmˌpjuːtəˌaɪzd ˌæksiəl təˈmɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
a medical imaging technique that uses X rays and computer processing to produce cross-sectional images of body organs along a single axis

Examples

Computerized axial tomography scan
Advancements in computerized axial tomography have significantly improved the accuracy of cancer diagnoses.
She underwent computerized axial tomography to help the medical team plan her treatment.
The doctor recommended a computerized axial tomography scan to determine the extent of the injury.

Related Terms

  • synonym: CAT scan - an abbreviation of computerized axial tomography, a medical imaging technique
  • synonym: magnetic resonance imaging - a medical imaging technique using magnetic fields, often compared with computerized axial tomography
