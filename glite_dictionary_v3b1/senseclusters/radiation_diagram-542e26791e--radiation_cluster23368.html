radiation diagram

noun
graph of antenna radiation pattern
Pronunciation: / ˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən daɪˌæɡræm / (AmE), / ˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən dʌɪˌaɡɹam / (BrE)
a graphical representation, in polar or Cartesian coordinates, showing the spatial distribution of radiation from an antenna as a function of angle

Examples

Antenna radiation diagram
A clear radiation diagram helps in understanding how an antenna distributes energy in space.
Engineers studied the radiation diagram to optimize the placement of the transmitter.
The radiation diagram of the antenna revealed its strongest signal direction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: radiation pattern - the variation of the strength of radio waves sent from or received by an antenna in different directions
