prickly lettuce

noun
a type of wild lettuce with prickly stems
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪkli ˈlɛtɪs / (AmE), / ˈprɪkli ˈlɛtɪs / (BrE)
a European annual wild lettuce (Lactuca serriola) with prickly stems, considered a troublesome weed in some parts of the United States

Examples

Prickly lettuce plant
Prickly lettuce often invades gardens and fields, making it difficult to control other plants.
Farmers are concerned about the rapid spread of prickly lettuce in their crops.
The stems of prickly lettuce can scratch the skin if handled carelessly.
