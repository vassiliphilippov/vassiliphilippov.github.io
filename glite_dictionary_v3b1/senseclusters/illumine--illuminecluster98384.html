illumine

verb
to light up; to brighten
Pronunciation: / ɪˈluːmɪn / (AmE), / ɪˈluːmɪn / (BrE)
to light up or make something clearer, either by adding light or by making it easier to understand

Examples

To illumine a path
A full moon illumined the path through the quiet forest last night.
As dawn broke, the sun's rays began to illumine the mountain peaks.
The candles illumine the old cathedral, casting a soft golden glow on the walls.
The rising sun began to illumine the dark valley below.
Her explanation helped to illumine the complex topic for everyone in the class.
A single candle was enough to illumine the small room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clarify - to make something easier to understand
  • synonym: illuminate - to light up, make brighter or clearer
  • antonym: obscure - to make something less clear or harder to understand
