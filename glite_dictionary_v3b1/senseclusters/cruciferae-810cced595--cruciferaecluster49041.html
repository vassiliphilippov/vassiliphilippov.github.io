Cruciferae

noun
plant family including mustards and cabbages
6.8
Pronunciation: / kruːˈsɪfɚˌi /; / kruːˈsɪfəˌri / (AmE), / kruːˈsɪfəˌriː /; / kruːˈsɪfəriː / (BrE)
a large family of flowering plants, including mustards, cabbages, broccoli, turnips, and cresses, known for their four-petaled flowers
