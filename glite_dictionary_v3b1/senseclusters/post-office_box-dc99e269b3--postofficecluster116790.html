Post-Office box

noun
numbered mail compartment at a post office
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊst ˌɑːfɪs bɑks / (AmE), / ˈpəʊst ˌɒfɪs bɒks / (BrE)
a numbered compartment in a post office assigned to an individual or organization for receiving mail, to be collected on the premises
