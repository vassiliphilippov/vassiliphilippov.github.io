menfolk

noun
male family members
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛnˌfoʊk / (AmE), / ˈmenfəʊk / (BrE)
the men of a family, group, or community, considered as a collective group

Examples

The menfolk gather
The menfolk gathered in the living room to watch the football game together.
During the festival, the menfolk prepared the barbecue while the children played outside.
In some traditions, the menfolk are responsible for certain ceremonies and rituals.
The menfolk gathered outside to discuss the upcoming festival.
During the holidays, the menfolk often help prepare the decorations.
In some traditions, the menfolk and womenfolk have separate roles during ceremonies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: men - adult male human beings
  • antonym: womenfolk - the women of a family or group, considered collectively
