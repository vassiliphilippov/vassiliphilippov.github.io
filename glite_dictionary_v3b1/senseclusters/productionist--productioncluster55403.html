productionist

noun
production-focused person
Pronunciation: / prəˈdʌkʃənɪst / (AmE), / prəˈdʌkʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who emphasizes or specializes in production, especially in industry or economics

Examples

Industrial productionist
The productionist believed that increasing factory output would benefit the entire economy.
As a dedicated productionist, she always looked for ways to improve efficiency on the assembly line.
Critics argued that the productionist approach ignored environmental concerns in favor of higher output.

Related Terms

  • antonym: consumerist - a person who advocates or emphasizes the interests of consumers
  • synonym: industrialist - a person involved in the ownership or management of industry
