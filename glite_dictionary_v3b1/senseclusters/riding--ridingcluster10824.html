riding

noun
county division
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈraɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
an administrative division of certain counties, especially in England or Canada, used for political or electoral purposes

Examples

A political riding
He was elected to represent the northern riding in parliament.
The county is divided into three ridings for administrative purposes.
Each riding selects its own representative for the national assembly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: district - an area of a country or city, especially one regarded as a distinct unit
