epicondylitis

noun
inflammation of tissues around an epicondyle
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˌkɑndəˈlaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˌkɒndɪˈlaɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation and pain of the muscles and soft tissues around an epicondyle, often caused by repetitive stress or overuse.

Examples

Epicondylitis pain
Golfers are also at risk of developing epicondylitis due to repetitive arm movements.
The tennis player was diagnosed with epicondylitis after weeks of persistent elbow pain.
Treatment for epicondylitis often involves rest, physical therapy, and anti-inflammatory medication.
