intergovernmental

adjective
between governments
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˌɡʌvərnˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˌɡʌvənˈmɛntl̩ / (BrE)
involving or occurring between two or more governments

Examples

An intergovernmental organization
The United Nations is an intergovernmental organization that promotes international cooperation.
An intergovernmental agreement was signed to improve cross-border transportation.
They attended an intergovernmental summit to address global health challenges.

Related Terms

  • antonym: domestic - relating to a single country or its internal affairs
  • synonym: international - involving two or more countries
