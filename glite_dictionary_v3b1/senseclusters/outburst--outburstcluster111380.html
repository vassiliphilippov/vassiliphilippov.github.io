Synonyms

  • burst
    3.5 6/m
    burst is a sudden, brief period of intense activity or feeling, while outburst is a broader, often more noticeable expression.
  • explosion
    3.0 3/m
    explosion is a sudden, violent release of force or feeling, while outburst can mean a sudden but less violent expression.
  • flare
    5.3 0.4/m
    flare is a quick, sharp rise of emotion or light, while outburst refers to a stronger, often longer expression.
  • blowup
    6.1 0.4/m
    blowup is a sudden, uncontrolled eruption of anger or strong feeling, while outburst is a more general sudden expression.
  • flare-up
    6.0 0.9/m
    flare-up is a sudden, often repeated rise of trouble or illness, while outburst names a sudden strong expression of feeling.
