subminiature

noun
very small camera
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbˈmɪniətʃər / (AmE), / ˌsʌbˈmɪnɪətjʊə / (BrE)
a very small camera or the small film format it uses, typically smaller than standard 35 mm cameras

Examples

Subminiature camera
Collectors value the subminiature for its compact size and unique photographic style.
He carried a subminiature on his travels because it was easy to hide and lightweight.
Some spy cameras from the 20th century were actually subminiature models designed for covert use.

Related Terms

  • antonym: large format camera - a camera that uses large film or digital sensors
  • synonym: miniature camera - a small-sized camera, often portable
