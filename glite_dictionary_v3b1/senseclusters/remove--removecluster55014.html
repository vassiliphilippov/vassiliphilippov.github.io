Synonyms

  • take out
    1.9 40/m
    take out is used for physically or briefly moving something from a container or place, while remove is more general and formal.
  • take away
    2.3 40/m
    take away often means removing something from a person or situation or reducing it, while remove is a broader, neutral term.
  • withdraw
    3.5 7/m
    withdraw means to pull back support or objects from use, while remove covers general taking away or dismissal in many contexts.
  • eliminate
    3.4 20/m
    eliminate means getting rid of something completely or permanently, often forcefully, while remove is more general and may be less final.
