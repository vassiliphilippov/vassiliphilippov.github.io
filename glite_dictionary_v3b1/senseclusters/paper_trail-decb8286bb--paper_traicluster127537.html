paper trail

noun
documentary record of activities or transactions
6.4 3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪ.pɚ treɪl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪ.pə treɪl / (BrE)
a series of written or printed documents that provide evidence of a person's activities, transactions, or decisions

Examples

A paper trail of receipts
Emails and receipts left a clear paper trail of her purchases.
The investigators followed the paper trail to uncover the fraud scheme.
When dealing with large transactions, it is important to create a paper trail for accountability.
