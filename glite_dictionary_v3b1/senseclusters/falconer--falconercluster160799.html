falconer

noun
person who trains falcons for hunting
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfælkənɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːlkənə / (BrE)
a person who keeps, breeds, and trains falcons or other birds of prey for hunting or sport

Examples

A falconer trains hawks
As a professional falconer, she cares for several hawks and trains them daily.
During the festival, the falconer demonstrated how his eagle responded to his commands.
The skilled falconer released his bird into the open field to hunt for prey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: falconry - the sport of hunting with falcons or other birds of prey
