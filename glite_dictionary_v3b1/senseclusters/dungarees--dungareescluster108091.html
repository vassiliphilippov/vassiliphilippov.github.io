dungarees

noun
denim trousers
Pronunciation: / ˌdʌŋɡəˈriz / (AmE), / ˌdʌŋɡəˈriːz / (BrE)
trousers or overalls made from denim or other strong fabric, often with a bib and shoulder straps, worn as work or casual clothing
