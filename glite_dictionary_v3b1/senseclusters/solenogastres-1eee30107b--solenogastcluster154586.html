Solenogastres

noun
order of small, shell-less marine mollusks
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊlɪnoʊˈɡæstriːz / (AmE), / ˌsəʊlɪnəʊˈɡæstriːz / (BrE)
an order of small, worm-like marine mollusks lacking shells, belonging to the class Aplacophora.
