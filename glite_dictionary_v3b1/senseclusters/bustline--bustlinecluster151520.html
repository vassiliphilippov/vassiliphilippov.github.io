bustline

noun
chest area of clothing
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌstˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈbʌstˌlaɪn / (BrE)
the part of a garment that is shaped or fitted around the chest, especially at the level of the bust

Examples

Dress bustline
The dress has a fitted bustline that highlights the shape of the upper body.
She chose a blouse with a decorative bustline to add style to her outfit.
The tailor adjusted the bustline to ensure the gown fit perfectly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neckline - the edge of a garment around the neck
  • synonym: waistline - the part of a garment that fits around the waist
