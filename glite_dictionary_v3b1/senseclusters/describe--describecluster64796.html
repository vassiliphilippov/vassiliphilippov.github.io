describe

verb
formally identify and record a species
1.1
Pronunciation: / dɪˈskraɪb / (AmE), / dɪˈskraɪb / (BrE)
(in science) To identify, name, and formally record a new species in a field such as botany or biology.

Examples

To describe a species
Hundreds of new insects are described each year by entomologists.
She spent her career in the Amazon, describing plants that were previously unknown to science.
The botanist was the first to describe this rare orchid as a new species.
