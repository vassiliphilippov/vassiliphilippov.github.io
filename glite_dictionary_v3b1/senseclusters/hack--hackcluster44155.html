hack

verb
to access computers illegally
Pronunciation: / hæk / (AmE), / hæk / (BrE)
to gain unauthorized access to a computer, network, or digital system

Examples

To hack a computer
The teenager learned how to hack into his school's computer network.
She used her skills to hack a quick solution to the software problem.
Authorities arrested a group that tried to hack several government websites.
The company discovered that someone had hacked their email system and stolen sensitive information.
After his account was hacked, he changed all his passwords to stronger ones.
Authorities warned that criminals may try to hack into government databases for personal data.

Related Terms

  • synonym: break into - to enter a place or system illegally or by force
  • synonym: crack - to break into a computer system, often illegally
  • antonym: protect - to keep something safe from harm or unauthorized access
  • antonym: secure - to protect a computer system from unauthorized access
