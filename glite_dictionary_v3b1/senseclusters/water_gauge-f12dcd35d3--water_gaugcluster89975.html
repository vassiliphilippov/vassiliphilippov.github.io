water gauge

noun
instrument showing water level
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔt̬ɚ ɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
an instrument used to indicate the level of water in a tank, boiler, or reservoir

Examples

A water gauge instrument
A damaged water gauge can lead to inaccurate level readings in the reservoir.
Please monitor the water gauge so the tank does not overflow.
The engineer checked the water gauge to ensure the boiler was safe to operate.
