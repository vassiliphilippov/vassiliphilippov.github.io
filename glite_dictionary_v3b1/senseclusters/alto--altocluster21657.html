alto

noun
second highest instrument in a group
Pronunciation: / ˈæl.toʊ / (AmE), / ˈæl.təʊ / (BrE)
(of a musical instrument) the second highest instrument in a family of similar instruments, such as the alto saxophone or alto flute.

Examples

Alto saxophone
In the quartet, the alto clarinet has a distinct, mellow sound.
She plays the alto flute in the school orchestra.
The alto saxophone is a popular choice for jazz musicians.
