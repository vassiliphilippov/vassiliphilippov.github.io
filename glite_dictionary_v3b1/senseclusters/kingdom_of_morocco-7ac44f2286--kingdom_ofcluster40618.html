Kingdom of Morocco

proper noun
country in northwestern Africa
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪŋdəm əv məˈroʊkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋdəm əv məˈrəʊkəʊ / (BrE)
A country in northwestern Africa, officially a constitutional monarchy, with a predominantly Muslim population; gained independence from France in 1956.

Examples

After years of colonial rule, the Kingdom of Morocco achieved independence in 1956.
