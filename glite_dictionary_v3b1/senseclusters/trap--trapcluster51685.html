trap

noun
sand bunker on a golf course
Pronunciation: / træp / (AmE), / træp / (BrE)
In golf, a sand-filled hollow or bunker that acts as a hazard on a course.

Examples

Golf trap
His ball landed in the trap near the green, making his next shot difficult.
Many players dread hitting into a trap because it often adds strokes to their score.
She practiced getting out of the trap to improve her sand play.
