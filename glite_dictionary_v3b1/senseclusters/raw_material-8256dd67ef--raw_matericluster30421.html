raw material

noun
basic substance used in manufacturing
2.8 5.89/m
Pronunciation: / r…îÀê m…ôÀàt…™ri…ôl / (AmE), / r…îÀê m…ôÀàt…™…ôri…ôl / (BrE)
A basic substance in its natural state, used to produce goods, finished products, or energy through manufacturing or processing.

Examples

A raw material source
Cotton is an important raw material in the textile industry.
Steel production relies heavily on the availability of quality raw materials.
The company imports raw materials to manufacture its goods locally.

Synonyms

  • feedstock
    7.4 1.56/m
    feedstock is an industrial input sent into a process to be converted, while raw material is a broad natural substance used to make goods.
  • raw
    2.4 0.56/m
    raw is unprocessed material in its natural state, while raw material is the common noun for substances used in manufacturing or processing.

Related Terms
