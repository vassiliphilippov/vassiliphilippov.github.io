heaven-sent

adjective
extremely fortunate or timely
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛvənˌsɛnt / (AmE), / ˈhɛv.ənˌsɛnt / (BrE)
(of an event or thing) coming at exactly the right time, as if by divine intervention; extremely fortunate or timely.

Examples

A heaven-sent gift
Her arrival with the needed documents was truly heaven-sent.
That heaven-sent breeze brought relief to everyone in the crowded room.
The sudden investment was a heaven-sent opportunity for the struggling company.

Related Terms

  • synonym: providential - occurring at a favorable time; as if by divine guidance
