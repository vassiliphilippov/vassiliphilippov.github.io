peculator

noun
person who embezzles entrusted funds
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛkjəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / ˈpɛkjʊleɪtə / (BrE)
a person who embezzles or misappropriates money or property entrusted to their care

Examples

A peculator steals money
As a notorious peculator, he was charged with several counts of fraud and theft.
Even trusted officials can sometimes turn out to be peculators, betraying the organizations they serve.
The company's accountant was revealed to be a peculator who had diverted thousands of dollars for personal use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embezzler - a person who steals money entrusted to them
