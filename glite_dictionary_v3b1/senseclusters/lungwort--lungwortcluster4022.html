lungwort

noun
spotted garden plant
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌŋˌwɝt / (AmE), / ˈlʌŋˌwɜːt / (BrE)
a garden plant with spotted leaves and blue or pink flowers, often grown for its early spring blooms

Examples

Flowering lungwort plant
The lungwort in her garden bloomed early, attracting bees with its colorful flowers.
Gardeners appreciate lungwort for its ability to thrive in shady areas.
He planted lungwort along the path to add color in the spring.
