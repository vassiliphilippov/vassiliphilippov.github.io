astringence

noun
sharp, puckering taste or sensation
Pronunciation: / əˈstrɪn.dʒəns / (AmE), / əˈstrɪn.dʒəns / (BrE)
The quality of being astringent, especially the sharp, puckering taste or sensation caused by certain substances.

Examples

Mild astringence taste
Green tea often leaves a mild astringence on the palate after drinking.
Some red wines are prized for their subtle astringence.
The astringence of the unripe persimmon made my mouth pucker.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astringency - the quality of being astringent; puckering mouth sensation
