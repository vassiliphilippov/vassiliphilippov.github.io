cuvette

noun
lab sample container
8.3
Pronunciation: / kuˈvɛt / (AmE), / kjuːˈvɛt / (BrE)
a small, transparent container used in laboratories to hold liquid samples for analysis, especially in spectrophotometry

Examples

A lab cuvette
The scientist carefully filled the cuvette with the prepared solution before starting the experiment.
Each cuvette must be clean and free of scratches to ensure accurate spectrophotometer readings.
After use, the technician rinsed the cuvette thoroughly to avoid contamination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beaker - a wide cylindrical glass container used in laboratories for mixing or heating substances
  • synonym: test tube - a glass tube used to hold, mix, or heat small quantities of liquid in laboratories
