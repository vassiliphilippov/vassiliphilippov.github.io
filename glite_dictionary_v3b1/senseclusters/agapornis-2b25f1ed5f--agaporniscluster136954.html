Agapornis

noun
genus of parrots (lovebirds)
Pronunciation: / ˌæɡəˈpɔrnɪs / (AmE), / ˌæɡəˈpɔːnɪs / (BrE)
A genus of small, short-tailed parrots known as lovebirds, native to Africa and Madagascar.

A Agapornis parrot
An Agapornis requires a spacious cage and plenty of social interaction.
