breed

verb
to cause or bring about
3.9 5.40/m
Pronunciation: / brid / (AmE), / briÀêd / (BrE)
to cause something to happen, especially a situation, feeling, or condition; to bring about

Examples

To breed trouble
His harsh words bred anger throughout the team.
Neglect often breeds further problems rather than solving them.
Poverty can breed resentment and social unrest.
