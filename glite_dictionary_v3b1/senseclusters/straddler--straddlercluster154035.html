straddler

noun
person between groups
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈstrædlər / (AmE), / ˈstrædlə / (BrE)
a person who supports, belongs to, or identifies with two different groups, sides, or cultures

Examples

Cultural straddler
As a straddler between two cultures, she often felt conflicted.
Many bilingual children become natural straddlers in their communities.
He was a political straddler, supporting ideas from both major parties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mediator - a person who helps resolve differences between groups
  • antonym: outsider - a person who does not belong to a particular group
