inexplicit

adjective
unclear or vague
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnɪkˈsplɪsɪt / (AmE), / ˌɪnɪkˈsplɪsɪt / (BrE)
not stated clearly or in detail; vague or ambiguous

Examples

An inexplicit contract
The contract was inexplicit, so both parties misunderstood their responsibilities.
Her answer was inexplicit, leaving everyone unsure about her true opinion.
Because the instructions were inexplicit, the students struggled to complete the assignment correctly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ambiguous - having more than one possible meaning; unclear
  • antonym: explicit - stated clearly and in detail
  • synonym: vague - not clearly expressed or defined
