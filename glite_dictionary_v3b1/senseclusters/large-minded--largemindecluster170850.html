large-minded

adjective
having broad, tolerant views
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑrdʒ ˌmaɪndɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɑːdʒ ˌmaɪndɪd / (BrE)
having or showing a broad and tolerant view; open-minded and generous in spirit

Examples

Large-minded generosity
He admired her large-minded generosity and willingness to understand others.
Her large-minded approach to debate allowed everyone to express their opinions freely.
The university sought leaders with large-minded visions for the future.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broad-minded - tolerant or liberal in one's thinking; open to different opinions
  • antonym: narrow-minded - having a limited or intolerant outlook; unwilling to accept different ideas
