supersecure

adjective
extremely safe
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərsɪˈkjʊr / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəsɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
extremely safe or protected, especially against threats, attacks, or unauthorized access

Examples

Supersecure computer system
The company uses a supersecure system to protect customer data.
They installed a supersecure lock on the front door after the break-in.
Our files are stored in a supersecure online vault with multiple layers of encryption.
