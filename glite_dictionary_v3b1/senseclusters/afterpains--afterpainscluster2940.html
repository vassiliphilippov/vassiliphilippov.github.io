afterpains

noun
pains occurring after childbirth
1.6 7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈæf.tər.peɪnz/ (AmE), /ˈɑːf.tə.peɪnz/ (BrE)
Pains experienced by a woman after childbirth, caused by contractions of the uterus as it returns to its normal size.

Examples

Strong afterpains
Many women experience afterpains as their uterus returns to its pre-pregnancy size.
Nurses advised her that afterpains are a normal part of the postpartum recovery process.
The afterpains were mild compared to the labor pains, but still noticeable.
