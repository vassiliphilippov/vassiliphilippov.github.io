pour one's heart out

verb
express deep feelings openly
2.0
Pronunciation: / pɔr wʌnz hɑrt aʊt / (AmE), / pɔː wʌnz hɑːt aʊt / (BrE)
to talk about your deepest feelings or emotions openly and honestly, especially when you are upset or vulnerable
