lander

noun
space vehicle for landing on celestial bodies
Pronunciation: / ˈlændər / (AmE), / ˈlændə / (BrE)
a spacecraft designed to land on the surface of the moon or another planet

Examples

A lander spacecraft
NASA announced plans to build a new lander for future planetary missions.
Scientists monitored the progress of the lunar lander as it descended onto the moon.
The Mars lander successfully touched down on the planet's surface.

Related Terms

  • antonym: orbiter - spacecraft designed to orbit a celestial body
  • synonym: rover - space vehicle that moves across the surface of a planet or moon
