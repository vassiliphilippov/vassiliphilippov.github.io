splashily

adverb
with splashing
Pronunciation: / ˈsplæʃɪli / (AmE), / ˈsplæʃɪli / (BrE)
in a way that causes liquid to splash or fly about, often making a mess or drawing attention

Examples

Children run splashily
The children ran splashily through the puddles after the rainstorm.
She painted splashily, leaving drops of color all over the canvas and floor.
The dog shook itself splashily after jumping out of the lake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: messily - in a way that creates a mess
  • antonym: neatly - in a tidy or orderly way
