faintly

adverb
in a weak or barely noticeable way
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪntli / (AmE), / ˈfeɪntli / (BrE)
In a way that is only just noticeable, weak, or slight.

Examples

To smile faintly
He smiled faintly when he heard the news, unsure how to react.
The perfume could be faintly detected in the crowded room.
The sound of music was faintly audible through the closed window.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dimly - in a way that is not bright or clear
  • antonym: strongly - in a strong or intense way
