organ

noun
official publication of a group or organization
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɔr.ɡən / (AmE), / ˈɔː.ɡən / (BrE)
an official publication, such as a newsletter or magazine, produced by a particular organization to represent its views or interests

Examples

Union organ
Many labor unions have their own organs to keep members informed about issues and events.
She writes editorials for the organ of the environmental advocacy group.
The party distributed its views through its official organ, which was widely read by members.

Related Terms

  • synonym: publication - a book, journal, or other work that is published
