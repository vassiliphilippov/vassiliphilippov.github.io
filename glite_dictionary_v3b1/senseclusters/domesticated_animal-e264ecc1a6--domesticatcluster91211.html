domesticated animal

noun
animal tamed and adapted to human life
3.4 1.1
Pronunciation: /də.ˈmes.tɪ.keɪ.tɪd ˈæn.ə.məl/ /; / /də.ˈmes.tə.keɪ.təd ˈæn.ɪ.məl/ (AmE), /də.ˈmes.tɪ.keɪ.tɪd ˈæn.ɪ.məl/ /; / /də.ˈmes.tə.keɪ.təd ˈæn.ə.məl/ (BrE)
an animal of a species that has been tamed and bred to live in a human environment, typically for work, companionship, or food

Examples

Dogs were among the first domesticated animals in human history.
The cow is a domesticated animal that provides milk for many people.
Unlike tigers, a domesticated animal such as the sheep is gentle and easy to handle.
