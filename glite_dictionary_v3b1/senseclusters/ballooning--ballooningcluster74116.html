ballooning

noun
flying in a balloon
Pronunciation: / bəˈluːnɪŋ / (AmE), / bəˈluːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or activity of flying in a hot-air balloon or gas balloon

Examples

Ballooning adventure
Ballooning offers a unique perspective on the landscape below.
He has always dreamed of trying ballooning over the countryside.
They spent their vacation ballooning in the south of France.
