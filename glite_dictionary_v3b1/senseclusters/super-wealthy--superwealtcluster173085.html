super-wealthy

adjective
extremely rich
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈwɛlθi / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈwɛlθi / (BrE)
having extremely large amounts of money or wealth, far more than most people

Examples

Super-wealthy families
Only the super-wealthy can afford homes in that neighborhood.
The super-wealthy often invest in rare art and luxury yachts.
Charities sometimes rely on donations from the super-wealthy to fund large projects.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: wealthy - having a lot of money or valuable possessions
