pippin

noun
type of eating apple
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪpɪn / (AmE), / ˈpɪpɪn / (BrE)
A type of apple of superior eating quality, typically with yellow or greenish-yellow skin flushed with red.

Examples

A pippin apple
She brought home a basket of pippins from the farmer's market.
The orchard grew several varieties, but my favorite was the pippin for its crisp, tart flavor.
The recipe called for peeled pippin apples, which hold their shape well when baked.
