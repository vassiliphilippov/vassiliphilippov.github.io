reinflate

verb
to fill with air again
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˈfleɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈfleɪt / (BrE)
to fill something (like a tire or ball) with air or gas again after it has become flat

Examples

To reinflate a tire
After fixing the puncture, she used a pump to reinflate the car tire.
The coach had to reinflate the soccer ball before the game could start.
If your air mattress goes flat, you can easily reinflate it with a portable pump.
After patching the hole, he used a pump to reinflate the soccer ball.
The mechanic had to reinflate the car tire before they could continue driving.
If your air mattress goes flat overnight, you may need to reinflate it in the morning.
