unconsoled

adjective
not comforted
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈsoʊld / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈsəʊld / (BrE)
not comforted or soothed after experiencing sadness or disappointment

Examples

Feeling unconsoled after loss
After the loss of her pet, she felt unconsoled for days.
He sat unconsoled in the empty room, staring at the wall.
Despite their efforts, the child remained unconsoled after the argument.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comforted - having received comfort or consolation
  • synonym: inconsolable - unable to be comforted or soothed
