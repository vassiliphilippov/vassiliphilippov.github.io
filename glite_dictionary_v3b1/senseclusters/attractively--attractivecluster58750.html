attractively

adverb
in a pleasing or beautiful way
Pronunciation: / əˈtræktɪvli / (AmE), / əˈtræktɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that is pleasing or appealing to the senses; in a beautiful or charming manner.

Examples

Dressed attractively
He dressed attractively for the interview, making a strong first impression.
The house was painted attractively in soft pastel colors.
The room was decorated attractively for the party, with flowers and candles everywhere.
