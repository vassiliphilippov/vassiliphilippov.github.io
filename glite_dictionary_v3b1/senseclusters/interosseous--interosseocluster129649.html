interosseous

adjective
between bones
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈɑsiəs / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈɒsɪəs / (BrE)
located or occurring between bones

Examples

Interosseous membrane
The surgeon carefully avoided the interosseous membrane during the procedure.
Physical therapy can help strengthen the interosseous muscles in the hand.
An injury to the interosseous ligament can affect wrist movement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intramuscular - situated or occurring within a muscle
