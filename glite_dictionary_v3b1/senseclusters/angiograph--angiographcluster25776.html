angiograph

noun
blood vessel X-ray image
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈændʒiəˌɡræf / (AmE), / ˈandʒiəˌɡrɑːf / (BrE)
an X-ray image or photographic record of blood vessels produced during angiography

Examples

A clear angiograph image
The doctor reviewed the angiograph to identify any blockages in the patient's arteries.
After the procedure, the technician printed the angiograph for the cardiologist to examine.
A clear angiograph can help detect problems in the blood vessels early.
