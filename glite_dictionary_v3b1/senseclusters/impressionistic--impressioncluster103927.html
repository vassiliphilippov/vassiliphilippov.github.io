impressionistic

adjective
of or relating to Impressionism
6.3 6.7
Pronunciation: /ɪm.pre.ʃən.ˈɪs.tɪk/ (AmE), /ɪm.pre.ʃən.ˈɪs.tɪk/ (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of Impressionism, especially in art, music, or literature, emphasizing a subjective and sensory impression rather than objective reality or detail.

Examples

Impressionistic painting style
Debussy is known for his impressionistic approach to music.
Her writing style is impressionistic, focusing more on feelings than on events.
The gallery displayed an impressionistic painting by Monet.
