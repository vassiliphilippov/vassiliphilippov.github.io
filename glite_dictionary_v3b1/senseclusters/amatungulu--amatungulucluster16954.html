amatungulu

noun
South African shrubby plant
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːmətʌnˈɡuːluː / (AmE), / ˌamətʊŋˈɡuːluː / (BrE)
A very large, closely branched South African shrub with forked bright green spines and shiny leaves.

Examples

The amatungulu shrub
Local gardeners prize the amatungulu for its tough, thorny branches and glossy leaves.
The amatungulu grows wild along many South African coasts.
The fruit of the amatungulu is sometimes used to make traditional preserves.
