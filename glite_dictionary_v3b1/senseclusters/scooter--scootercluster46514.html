scooter

noun
light motor vehicle with small wheels
2.5 1.41/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàskuÀêt…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàskuÀêt…ô / (BrE)
a light two-wheeled motor vehicle with small wheels and a low-powered gasoline engine, typically with a step-through frame and footrest platform

Examples

A motor scooter
He bought a vintage scooter that reminded him of the Italian models from the 1960s.
She rode her scooter to work every morning to avoid traffic jams.
The city is encouraging people to use scooters as an environmentally friendly mode of transportation.

Synonyms

  • motor scooter
    3.5 0.16/m
    motor scooter is the full, technical name often used in writing and manuals, while scooter is the common everyday word people use.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: bicycle - a vehicle with two wheels powered by pedaling, no engine
  • synonym: moped - a light, small motorcycle, usually with bicycle pedals
  • degree higher: motorcycle - a two-wheeled motor vehicle, generally more powerful than a scooter
