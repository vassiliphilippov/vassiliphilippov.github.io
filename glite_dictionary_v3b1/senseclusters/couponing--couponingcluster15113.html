couponing

noun
using coupons to save
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːpɑːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkuːpɒnɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of collecting, using, or distributing coupons to save money or promote sales when shopping

Examples

To coupon for savings
She started couponing to help her family save money on groceries every week.
Many people enjoy couponing as a way to reduce their monthly expenses.
Online forums offer tips and tricks for successful couponing at different stores.
Many families save money by couponing when shopping for groceries each week.
Extreme couponing has become a popular hobby for people looking to cut expenses.
The store's new couponing policy allows customers to combine multiple discounts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bargain hunting - searching for the best deals or lowest prices when shopping
  • synonym: discounting - the act of reducing prices to attract customers
  • synonym: promotion - an activity to increase sales or public awareness
  • antonym: spending - using money to buy goods or services
