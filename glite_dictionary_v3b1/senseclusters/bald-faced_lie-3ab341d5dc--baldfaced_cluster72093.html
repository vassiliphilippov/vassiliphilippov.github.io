bald-faced lie

noun
an obvious and shameless lie
7.1
Pronunciation: / bɔld feɪst laɪ / (AmE), / bɔːld feɪst laɪ / (BrE)
a lie that is obvious and shameless; a blatant untruth

Examples

A bald-faced lie
Calling in sick when you are actually on vacation is a bald-faced lie.
Her explanation was such a bald-faced lie that nobody believed her.
The politician told a bald-faced lie to cover up the scandal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barefaced lie - a lie that is obviously untrue; synonym for bald-faced lie
  • antonym: white lie - a harmless or trivial lie, often told to avoid hurting someone's feelings
