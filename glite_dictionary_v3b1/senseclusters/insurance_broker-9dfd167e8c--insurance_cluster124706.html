insurance broker

noun
person/company selling insurance policies for clients
6.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈʃʊr.əns ˈbroʊ.kər/ /; / /ɪn.ˈʃɜːr.əns ˈbroʊ.kər/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈʃʊə.rəns ˈbrəʊ.kə/ /; / /ɪn.ˈʃɔː.rəns ˈbrəʊ.kə/ (BrE)
