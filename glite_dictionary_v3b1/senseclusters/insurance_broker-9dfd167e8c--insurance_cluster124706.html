insurance broker

noun
person/company selling insurance policies for clients
6.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈʃʊrəns ˈbroʊkər / (AmE), / ɪnˈʃɔːrəns ˈbrəʊkə / (BrE)
a person or company who arranges and sells insurance policies on behalf of clients, offering advice and finding suitable cover from various providers

Examples

Insurance broker advice
After the flood, she contacted her insurance broker to file a claim and ask for advice.
Many small businesses rely on an insurance broker to find the best coverage for their needs.
The insurance broker helped us compare policies from different companies before we made our decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insurance agent - person who sells insurance, usually for one company
