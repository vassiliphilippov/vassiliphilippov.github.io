serow

noun
Asian goat-antelope species
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛroʊ / (AmE), / ˈsɛrəʊ / (BrE)
A short-horned, dark-coated goat-antelope found in mountainous regions of southern and southeastern Asia.

Examples

The serow animal
Conservationists are working to protect the endangered serow in the eastern Himalayas.
The serow is known for its agility on steep and rocky slopes.
We spotted a wild serow during our trek through the northern forests of Thailand.
