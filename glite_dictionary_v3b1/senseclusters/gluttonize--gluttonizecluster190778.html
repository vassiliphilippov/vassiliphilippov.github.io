gluttonize

verb
eat excessively or greedily
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlʌtəˌnaɪz /; / ˈɡlʌt.ən.aɪz / (AmE), / ˈɡlʌtənaɪz /; / ˈɡlʌt.ən.aɪz / (BrE)
to eat in an excessive or greedy manner, without restraint

Examples

To gluttonize food
Every Thanksgiving, he tends to gluttonize at the dinner table.
She regretted gluttonizing on sweets after the party.
Tourists often gluttonize at all-you-can-eat buffets.
During the feast, he couldn't help but gluttonise on the endless selection of food.
She regretted gluttonising after feeling uncomfortably full all evening.
Whenever the holidays come around, some people tend to gluttonise far more than they intend.
