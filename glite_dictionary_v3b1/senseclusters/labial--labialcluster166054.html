labial

adjective
relating to the lips or their use
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪ.bi.əl / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.bi.əl / (BrE)
relating to or situated at the lips of the mouth, especially in reference to sounds produced using the lips

Examples

Labial sound
A labial wound made it hard for him to pronounce certain syllables.
Many African languages feature voiced labial sounds not found in English.
The word 'pie' begins with a labial consonant.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: bilabial - (of a speech sound) made with both lips
  • antonym: dental - (of a speech sound) made with the tongue against the teeth
