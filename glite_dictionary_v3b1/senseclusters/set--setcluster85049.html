set

verb
(celestial body) go below the horizon
6.2 2.8
Pronunciation: /set/ (AmE), /set/ (BrE)
(of the sun, moon, or another celestial body) to disappear below the horizon as the earth rotates, ending visible daylight or moonlight for that day.

Examples

The sun sets
In the tropics, darkness falls quickly after the sun sets.
The sun will set at 7:10 p.m. tonight.
They watched as the full moon set behind the mountains.
