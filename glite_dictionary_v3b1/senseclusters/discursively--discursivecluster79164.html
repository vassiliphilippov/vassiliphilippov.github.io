discursively

adverb
in a rambling or digressive way
8.6
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈkɝːsɪvli / (AmE), / dɪsˈkɜːsɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that moves from topic to topic, often without a clear order or plan; in a rambling or digressive manner.

Examples

To write discursively
He tended to write discursively, making it hard for readers to follow his main argument.
She spoke discursively about her travels, never staying on one subject for long.
The professor answered the question discursively, providing many tangential anecdotes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: concisely - in a brief and clear manner
  • synonym: ramblingly - in a rambling manner
