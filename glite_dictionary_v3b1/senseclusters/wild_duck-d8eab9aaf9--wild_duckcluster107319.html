wild duck

noun
an undomesticated duck; often a mallard
5.2 2.0
Pronunciation: / waɪld dʌk / (AmE), / waɪld dʌk / (BrE)
a duck that lives in the wild and has not been domesticated, especially a mallard

Examples

A wild duck
A wild duck will usually fly away when approached by people.
Unlike its domesticated cousins, the wild duck forages for food in lakes and rivers.
We spotted a wild duck swimming across the pond early this morning.
