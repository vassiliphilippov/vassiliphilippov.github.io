leave

verb
forget to take something when departing
Pronunciation: / liv / (AmE), / liːv / (BrE)
to abandon or leave someone or something behind, either intentionally or by forgetting

Examples

To leave a bag behind
Don't leave your phone in the taxi by mistake.
He often leaves his glasses on the kitchen table and can't find them later.

After twenty years together, he finally left her, and she was heartbroken.
Don't forget to feed the fish if you leave town for a few days.
She was upset when her friends left her at the party without telling her.

  • antonym: bring - to take or carry someone or something to a place or person
  • synonym: forget - to fail to remember to bring something
