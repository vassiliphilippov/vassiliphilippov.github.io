explosion

noun
a sudden great increase
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsploʊʒən / (AmE), / ɪkˈspləʊʒən / (BrE)
a sudden and very rapid increase in something

Examples

Explosion of growth
Over the last decade, there has been an explosion of interest in renewable energy sources.
Social media platforms contributed to an explosion of information sharing worldwide.
The city faced an explosion in housing prices after the tech sector arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boom - a period of rapid economic growth or expansion
  • antonym: decline - a gradual and continuous loss, decrease, or deterioration
