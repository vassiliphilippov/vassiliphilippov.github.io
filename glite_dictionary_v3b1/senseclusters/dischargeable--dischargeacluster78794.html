dischargeable

adjective
able to release charge
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈtʃɑrdʒəbl̩ / (AmE), / dɪsˈtʃɑːdʒəbl̩ / (BrE)

Examples

Some modern devices use non-dischargeable batteries for safety reasons.
