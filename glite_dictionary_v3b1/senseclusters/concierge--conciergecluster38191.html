concierge

noun
digital/virtual assistant service
Pronunciation: / k\u0251n.si\u02c8\u025brd\u0292 / (AmE), / k\u0252n.si\u02c8\u025b\u0259\u0292 / (BrE)
a digital or virtual service (often software or an AI) that gives personalized recommendations, makes bookings, and provides access help, typically via an app or chat interface.

Examples

Virtual concierge service
The hotel app now offers a concierge that suggests activities and books tickets.
Our apartment building added a concierge chatbot to accept maintenance requests and deliveries.
Instead of calling reception, she asked the concierge feature in the app to reserve a table.
