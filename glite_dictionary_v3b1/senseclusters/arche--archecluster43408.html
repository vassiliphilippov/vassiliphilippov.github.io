arche

noun
rule or authority
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrki / (AmE), / ˈɑːki / (BrE)
the rule, authority, or office of a ruler or governing body, especially in ancient contexts.

Examples

The arche of a ruler
The ancient tribe accepted the elder's arche as final in all disputes and ceremonies.
Historians studied the arche of early city-states to understand their systems of governance.
Under the king's arche, new laws were introduced to maintain order in the kingdom.

  • synonym: dominion - the power or right to govern and control
