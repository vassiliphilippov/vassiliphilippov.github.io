chilla

noun
forty-day retreat
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪlə / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪlə / (BrE)
a period of forty days of spiritual retreat or meditation, especially in Sufi tradition

Examples

A forty-day chilla
He completed a forty-day chilla in the mountains to seek spiritual growth.
Many Sufi practitioners observe a chilla as part of their spiritual journey.
During her chilla, she spent her days in meditation and prayer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meditation - the practice of focusing the mind for spiritual or relaxation purposes
  • synonym: retreat - a period of withdrawal for meditation or reflection
