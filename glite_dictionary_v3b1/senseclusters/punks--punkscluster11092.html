punks

noun
members of punk rock youth subculture
5.6 5.8
Pronunciation: / pʌŋks / (AmE), / pʌŋks / (BrE)
Members of a youth subculture associated with punk rock music, characterized by distinctive, often shocking fashion and attitudes that emerged in the late 1970s.

Examples

Punks wear leather
In the late 1970s, punks often gathered in abandoned warehouses to listen to new bands.
The punks at the concert wore spiked hair and safety pins in their clothes.
The city saw a rise in punks embracing rebellious fashion and loud music.
