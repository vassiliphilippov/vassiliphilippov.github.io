yabber

noun
chatterer
Pronunciation: / ˈjæbər / (AmE), / ˈjæbə / (BrE)
a person who talks a lot in an informal or trivial way; a chatterer

Examples

A yabber at parties
Everyone knows Mark is a yabber who can keep a conversation going for hours.
At family gatherings, my aunt becomes the main yabber at the table.
Sometimes being a yabber helps break the ice in new situations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blabbermouth - a person who talks too much or reveals secrets
  • synonym: chatterbox - a person who talks a lot, especially about unimportant things
  • antonym: listener - a person who listens rather than talks
