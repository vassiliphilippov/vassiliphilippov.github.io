mutualization

noun
conversion to mutual ownership
8.2
Pronunciation: /mjuː.tʃu.ə.lə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /mjuː.tʃə.lə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /mjuː.tʃu.ə.lə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /mjuː.tʃə.lə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of converting a company or organization so that it is owned by its members or policyholders

Examples

After mutualization, members gained the right to vote on important company decisions.
The process of mutualization can increase customer loyalty by giving them a stake in the business.
