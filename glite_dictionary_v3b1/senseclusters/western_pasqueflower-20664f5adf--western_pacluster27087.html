Western pasqueflower

noun
plant species of western North America
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛstərn ˈpæskˌflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən ˈpæskˌflaʊə / (BrE)
A perennial herbaceous plant (Anemone occidentalis, also known as Pulsatilla occidentalis) native to western North America, bearing large, hairy flowers, typically with pale or white petals.
