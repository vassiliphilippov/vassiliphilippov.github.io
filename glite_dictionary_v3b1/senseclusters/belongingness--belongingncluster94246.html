belongingness

noun
state of membership
Pronunciation: / bɪˈlɔŋɪŋnəs / (AmE), / bɪˈlɒŋɪŋnəs / (BrE)
the state or fact of being a member, part, or component of a group or thing, without implying emotional connection

Examples

Belongingness to a group
The belongingness of each artifact to the collection was carefully documented by the curator.
Questions about the belongingness of certain documents arose during the investigation.
The committee discussed the belongingness of new members before making a decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: association - a connection or relationship with a group or organization
  • antonym: exclusion - the state of being left out or not included
  • synonym: membership - the state of being a member of a group or organization
