Sudan

proper noun
country in northeastern Africa
Pronunciation: / suˈdæn / (AmE), / suˈdɑːn / (BrE)
A country in northeastern Africa, bordering the Red Sea, which became independent from Egypt and the United Kingdom in 1956.

Examples

Sudan borders Egypt
After years of conflict, Sudan signed a peace agreement with neighboring countries.
Archaeologists have uncovered ancient pyramids in Sudan dating back thousands of years.
The Nile flows through the heart of Sudan, supporting agriculture and communities along its banks.
