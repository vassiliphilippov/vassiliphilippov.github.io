parental unit

noun
informal term for a parent
Pronunciation: / pəˈrɛntəl ˈjunɪt / (AmE), / pəˈrɛntəl ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
A humorous or informal term for a parent or guardian.

Examples

My parental unit
I have to check with my parental unit before making any weekend plans.
My teenage years were spent trying to outwit my parental unit.
Whenever my parental unit visits, they insist on reorganizing my kitchen.
