game time

noun
time elapsed or remaining in a game
2.8
Pronunciation: / ɡeɪm taɪm / (AmE), / ɡeɪm taɪm / (BrE)
the amount of time that has elapsed or remains in a game; the game's internal clock used to measure play time or match duration.

Examples

Check the game time
He paused the stream to check the game time and plan his next move.
With only thirty seconds of game time left, the coach called a timeout for a play.
The replay showed the game time clock stopped when the ball went out of play.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clock - the device or display that shows the time remaining or elapsed in a game
  • synonym: time remaining - the amount of playing time left before a game or period ends
