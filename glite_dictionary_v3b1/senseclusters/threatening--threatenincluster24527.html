threatening

adjective
indicating danger or harm
Pronunciation: / ˈθrɛtənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈθrɛtənɪŋ / (BrE)
Suggesting the presence of danger or harm; indicating that something bad may happen.

Examples

Threatening look
She gave him a threatening glare that silenced the room.
The dog's threatening bark kept strangers away from the gate.
Thunderclouds gathered overhead, casting a threatening shadow over the fields.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ominous - suggesting something bad is going to happen
  • antonym: promising - showing signs of good things to come
  • synonym: sinister - giving the impression that something harmful will happen
