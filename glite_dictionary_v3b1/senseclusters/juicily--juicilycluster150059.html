juicily

adverb
sexually suggestive manner
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuːsɪli / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːsɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is sexually attractive, suggestive, or shows sexual interest

Examples

She smiles juicily
She winked juicily at him from across the crowded room.
The actor smiled juicily in the romantic scene, making the audience blush.
He leaned in juicily and whispered something that made her laugh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: provocatively - in a way that provokes sexual interest
