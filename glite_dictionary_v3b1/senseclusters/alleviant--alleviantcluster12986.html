alleviant

noun
remedy that eases symptoms without curing

Examples

An alleviant for pain
Many over-the-counter drugs are used as alleviants for minor aches and pains.
The doctor prescribed an alleviant to manage her arthritis pain.
This ointment acts as an alleviant but will not cure the underlying infection.
