fletcher

noun
arrow maker
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈflɛtʃər / (AmE), / ˈflɛtʃə / (BrE)
a person who makes or sells arrows, especially in medieval times

Examples

The fletcher makes arrows
The fletcher carefully crafted each arrow to ensure it flew straight and true.
In the village, the fletcher was respected for his skill and attention to detail.
During the festival, the fletcher displayed his finest arrows for the archers to buy.
