shopaholic

noun
person addicted to shopping
Pronunciation: / ˌʃɑːpəˈhɑːlɪk / (AmE), / ˌʃɒpəˈhɒlɪk / (BrE)
A person who is addicted to shopping and finds it difficult to control the urge to buy things.

Examples

A shopaholic person
He joked about being a shopaholic after maxing out his credit card again.
Many shopaholics struggle to stick to a budget during sales season.
My sister is a true shopaholic—she can't go a week without buying new clothes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compulsive shopper - someone unable to resist the urge to shop
  • synonym: spendthrift - a person who spends money carelessly
