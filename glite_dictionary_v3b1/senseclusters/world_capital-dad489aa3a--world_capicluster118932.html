world capital

noun
city leading globally in a field
Pronunciation: / wɝːld ˈkæpɪtəl / (AmE), / wɜːld ˈkæpɪtəl / (BrE)
A city that is considered a global leader or center in a particular field, such as finance, fashion, art, or culture.

Examples

The world capital of fashion
For centuries, Rome was the world capital of the Catholic Church.
Paris is often described as the world capital of fashion and style.
Silicon Valley is considered by many to be the world capital of technology and innovation.
