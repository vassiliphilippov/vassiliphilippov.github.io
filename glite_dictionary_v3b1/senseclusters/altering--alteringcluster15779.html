altering

noun
sterilization of an animal
Pronunciation: / ˈɔltɚɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɔːltərɪŋ / (BrE)
the surgical sterilization of an animal to prevent it from reproducing, usually by spaying or neutering

Examples

Altering a pet
Many pet owners choose altering to prevent unwanted litters and certain health problems.
Responsible breeders advocate for altering pets that are not part of breeding programs.
The shelter requires all adoptions to include altering to help control the stray population.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spaying - surgical removal of a female animal's reproductive organs
