my poison

noun
someone's preferred indulgence or vice
6.4
Pronunciation: / maɪ ˈpɔɪzən / (AmE), / maɪ ˈpɔɪzən / (BrE)
used to refer to one's preferred indulgence, especially an alcoholic drink, food, or habitual vice

Examples

My poison drink
After a long week, a cold beer is always my poison.
For some, sweets are harmless, but chocolate is definitely my poison.
When the waiter asked for my drink order, I replied, 'Whiskey's my poison.'
