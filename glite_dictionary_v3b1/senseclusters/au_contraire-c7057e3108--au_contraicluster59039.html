au contraire

adverb
on the contrary
8.1 7.1
Pronunciation: /oʊ kɑːn.trə.ˈreər/ (AmE), /əʊ kɒn.trə.ˈreə/ (BrE)
used to express disagreement, meaning 'on the contrary' or 'quite the opposite'

Examples

Au contraire, I think
'That was not boring at all—au contraire, it was fascinating.'
Au contraire, the results of the experiment were very promising.
He claimed the plan was risky, but au contraire, it proved very effective.

Related Terms

  • synonym: on the contrary - used to introduce a statement that means the opposite of what has just been said
