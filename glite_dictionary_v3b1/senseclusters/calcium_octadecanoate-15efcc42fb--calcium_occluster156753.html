calcium octadecanoate

noun
insoluble calcium salt of stearic acid
9.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkæl.sɪ.əm ɑːk.tə.dek.ə.ˈneɪt/ (AmE), /ˈkæl.sɪ.əm ɒk.tə.dek.ə.ˈneɪt/ (BrE)
an insoluble calcium salt of stearic acid, often formed as scum when soap is mixed with hard water containing calcium ions
