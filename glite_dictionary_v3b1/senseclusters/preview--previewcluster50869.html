preview

noun
advance screening for select audience
2.9 8.98/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàpriÀåvju /; / ÀàpriÀêÀåvjuÀê / (AmE), / ÀàpriÀêvjuÀê / (BrE)
an advance showing or advertisement for a film, play, or other performance before it is officially released

Examples

A movie preview
The preview attracted critics and invited guests from all over the city.
Tickets to the preview sold out within minutes.
We attended a preview of the new film before its official release.
The preview generated a lot of excitement among fans waiting for the movie's release.
The cinema showed a preview of the upcoming superhero movie before the main feature.
We watched the preview and decided we wanted to see the film next weekend.
