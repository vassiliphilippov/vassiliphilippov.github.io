Interstate Commerce Commission

proper noun
former US federal agency regulating interstate transport
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈɪn.tər.steɪt ˈkɑː.mərs kə.ˈmɪʃ.ən/ /; / /ˈɪn.tər.steɪt ˈkɑː.mɜːrs kə.ˈmɪʃ.ən/ (AmE), /ˈɪn.tə.steɪt ˈkɒm.əs kə.ˈmɪʃ.ən/ (BrE)
