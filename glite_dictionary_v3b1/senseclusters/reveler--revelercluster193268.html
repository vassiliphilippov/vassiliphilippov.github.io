reveler

noun
person who parties or celebrates noisily
9.0 3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈrev.ə.lər/ (AmE), /ˈrev.ə.lə/ (BrE)
a person who enjoys themselves noisily, especially at parties or festivities

Examples

Noisy revelers at parties
At midnight, the revelers cheered and released balloons into the sky.
Security had to manage the crowd as more revelers entered the festival grounds.
The streets were filled with revelers celebrating the New Year.
As midnight approached, the revellers filled the streets, cheering and dancing in celebration.
Police kept a watchful eye as hundreds of revellers gathered for the festival downtown.
The club was packed with revellers enjoying the music and flashing lights.
