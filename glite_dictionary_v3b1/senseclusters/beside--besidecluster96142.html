beside

preposition
next to
Pronunciation: / bɪˈsaɪd / (AmE), / bɪˈsaɪd / (BrE)
at the side of or next to someone or something

Examples

Beside the table
She placed her bag beside the chair before sitting down.
A small table stood beside the window, holding a vase of flowers.
He walked beside his friend as they talked about their day.
