highlighter

noun
cosmetic for accentuating facial features
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪˌlaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈhaɪˌlaɪtə / (BrE)
A cosmetic product applied to the face, especially the eyes or cheekbones, to accentuate features by reflecting light.

Examples

Face highlighter
A touch of highlighter under the brow bone can make your eyes appear larger.
She applied highlighter to the tops of her cheekbones for a radiant look.
The makeup artist recommended using a liquid highlighter for a subtle glow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bronzer - a cosmetic used to give the skin a tanned appearance
