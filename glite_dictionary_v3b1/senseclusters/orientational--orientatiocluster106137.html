orientational

adjective
introductory or familiarizing
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːriənˈteɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌɔːriənˈteɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to providing guidance or information that helps someone find their way or become familiar with a place, situation, or context

Examples

An orientational session
The university offers an orientational session for all incoming students before classes begin.
New employees must complete an orientational program to learn about company policies.
The orientational materials helped visitors understand the layout of the museum.
The museum provided orientational signs to help visitors navigate the exhibits.
Her orientational skills made her an excellent guide in unfamiliar cities.
The app uses orientational data to show your current position on the map.
