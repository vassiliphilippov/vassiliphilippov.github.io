rose-cheeked

adjective
having rosy cheeks
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊz ˌtʃikt / (AmE), / ˈrəʊz ˌtʃiːkt / (BrE)
having cheeks with a pinkish or rosy color, often indicating health or youth

Examples

A rose-cheeked child
In the old portrait, a rose-cheeked woman gazed serenely into the distance.
She smiled, a rose-cheeked youth full of vitality and charm.
The rose-cheeked children played outside all afternoon, their laughter echoing through the yard.
