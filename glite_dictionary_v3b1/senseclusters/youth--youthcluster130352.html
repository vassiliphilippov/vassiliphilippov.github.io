youth

noun
freshness and vigor typical of young people
3.8
Pronunciation: / juθ / (AmE), / juːθ / (BrE)
the quality of being young, especially with freshness, vigor, and vitality; the state or appearance of being youthful

Examples

Youth and energy
Despite his youth, he showed remarkable maturity in handling the situation.
Her youth and enthusiasm made her an inspiring leader.
The painting captures the youth and hopefulness of its subject.

Related Terms

  • antonym: old age - the later part of life; seniority, often marked by declining vigor
  • synonym: youthfulness - the state of appearing or feeling young
