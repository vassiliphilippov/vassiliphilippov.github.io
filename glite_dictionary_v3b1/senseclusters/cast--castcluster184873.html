cast

noun
appearance or shape of someone's features
5.9
Pronunciation: / kæst / (AmE), / kɑːst / (BrE)
the shape, form, or appearance of something, especially a person's face or features

Examples

The ancient vase has a peculiar cast that sets it apart from others.
