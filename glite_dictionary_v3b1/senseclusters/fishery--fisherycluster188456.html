fishery

noun
workplace for catching and processing fish
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃəri / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃəri / (BrE)
A place or establishment where fish are caught, processed, and prepared for sale.

Examples

A fishery business
After college, he returned home to work at the family fishery.
Stringent new regulations were introduced to improve safety conditions in the local fishery.
The coastal town's economy relies heavily on its fishery for employment and trade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fish market - a marketplace where fish are sold, often raw or freshly caught
