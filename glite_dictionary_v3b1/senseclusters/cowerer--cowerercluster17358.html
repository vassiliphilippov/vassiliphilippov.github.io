cowerer

noun
person who crouches in fear
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊərər / (AmE), / ˈkaʊərə / (BrE)
a person or animal who crouches, shrinks away, or hides because they are afraid or lack courage

Examples

A cowerer in fear
The sudden explosion made the kitten a trembling cowerer in the corner.
During the storm, he became a cowerer, hiding under his blanket until it passed.
Unlike his bold friends, he was a cowerer when faced with danger.
The cowerer hid behind the door when the loud noise startled everyone.
No one wanted to be seen as a cowerer during the difficult situation.
Instead of standing up for himself, he acted like a cowerer in front of the group.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brave - showing courage and not afraid
  • synonym: coward - a person who lacks courage
