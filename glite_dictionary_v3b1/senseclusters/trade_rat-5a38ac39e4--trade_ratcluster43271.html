trade rat

noun
North American rodent that hoards objects
7.0
Pronunciation: / treɪd ræt / (AmE), / treɪd ræt / (BrE)
a common name for any of several bushy-tailed rodents of the genus Neotoma in western North America, known for collecting and hoarding various objects

Examples

The trade rat collects
At dusk, the trade rat emerged from its den to search for new treasures to collect.
Hikers sometimes find their gear missing, only to discover a trade rat nest full of odds and ends.
The old cabin was infested by a trade rat that stole shiny buttons and coins.
