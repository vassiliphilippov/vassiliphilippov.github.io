perforating vein

noun
vein connecting superficial and deep veins
1.4 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈpɜːr.fə.reɪ.tɪŋ veɪn/ (AmE), /ˈpɜː.fə.reɪ.tɪŋ veɪn/ (BrE)
a vein that passes through deep fascia to connect superficial veins with deep veins, especially in the leg

Examples

Perforating veins in leg
Perforating veins play a crucial role in venous blood flow from the leg muscles.
A blockage in a perforating vein can contribute to deep vein thrombosis.
During surgery, the doctor carefully identified the perforating vein in the patient's thigh.
