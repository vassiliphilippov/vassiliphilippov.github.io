nonstaining

adjective
does not leave marks
3.9
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈsteɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / nɒnˈsteɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
not causing or leaving a mark, spot, or discoloration on a surface

Examples

Nonstaining cleaner
This nonstaining cleaner is perfect for delicate fabrics and light-colored carpets.
The doctor recommended a nonstaining ointment for use on clothing and bedding.
Parents prefer nonstaining markers so children can draw without ruining their clothes.
