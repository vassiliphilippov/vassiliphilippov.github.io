uni

noun
sea urchin delicacy
Pronunciation: / ˈuːni / (AmE), / ˈuːni / (BrE)
the edible yellow or orange gonads of a sea urchin, often served as a delicacy in sushi and other cuisines

Examples

Fresh uni sushi
She ordered fresh uni at the sushi restaurant to try something new.
The chef recommended pairing the creamy uni with a light sake.
Many people describe the taste of uni as rich and slightly sweet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caviar - salted roe of sturgeon or other large fish, eaten as a delicacy
  • synonym: roe - the eggs of fish or certain marine animals used as food
