tiring

adjective
making you feel tired
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪɹɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
making you feel tired or exhausted; causing fatigue.

Examples

Tiring day
After a tiring day at work, he just wanted to relax at home.
Children can find long journeys especially tiring.
The hike was quite tiring, even for experienced walkers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exhausting - very tiring; making you extremely tired
  • antonym: refreshing - making you feel more energetic or less tired
