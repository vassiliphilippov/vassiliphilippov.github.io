little moppet

noun
endearing small child (affectionate)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬l ˈmɑː.pɪt / (AmE), / ˈlɪt.əl ˈmɒp.ɪt / (BrE)
An affectionate term for a small, cute, or endearing child.

Examples

The little moppet smiled
Everyone at the park adored the little moppet with curly hair and rosy cheeks.
Her grandmother pinched the cheek of the little moppet and gave her a candy.
Years later, the famous actor fondly recalled being a little moppet in her hometown.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moppet - a small child, especially one who is endearing
  • synonym: urchin - a mischievous child, sometimes endearing
