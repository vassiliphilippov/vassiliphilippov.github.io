saucer

noun
object with a round, flat shape
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔːsər / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsə / (BrE)
an object or shape that is round and flat like a small, shallow plate

Examples

A green saucer
The lily pad floated on the pond, almost perfect as a green saucer.
The moon appeared as a glowing saucer in the night sky.
The spaceship looked like a huge silver saucer hovering above the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disk - a flat, round object or shape
