skunkhead

noun
person sprayed by skunk
a person who smells strongly of skunk spray, especially after being sprayed by a skunk

Examples

Smelly skunkhead
After the camping trip, he was called a skunkhead because of the strong smell on his jacket.
No one wanted to sit next to the skunkhead on the bus after the incident in the woods.
She became a skunkhead when a skunk sprayed her while she was taking out the trash.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skunked - having been sprayed by a skunk
