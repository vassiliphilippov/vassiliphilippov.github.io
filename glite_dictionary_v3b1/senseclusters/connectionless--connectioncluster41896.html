connectionless

adjective
without dedicated connection
7.0
Pronunciation: / kəˈnɛkʃənləs / (AmE), / kəˈnɛkʃənləs / (BrE)
describing a network communication method that does not require a dedicated connection between sender and receiver

Examples

Connectionless network protocol
A connectionless protocol sends data without first establishing a direct link between devices.
Streaming services often use connectionless communication for faster delivery of content.
Unlike TCP, UDP is a connectionless protocol that does not guarantee delivery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: connection-oriented - requiring a dedicated connection for communication
