peritonsillar abscess

noun
pus-filled swelling near the tonsils
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛriˌtɑnsələr æbˈsɛs / (AmE), / ˌpɛrɪˌtɒnsɪlə ˈabsɛs / (BrE)
a painful collection of pus near the tonsils, usually resulting from a complication of tonsillitis

Examples

A peritonsillar abscess infection
A peritonsillar abscess can cause severe throat pain and difficulty swallowing.
Prompt treatment of a peritonsillar abscess reduces the risk of further complications.
The patient was diagnosed with a peritonsillar abscess and required immediate drainage.
