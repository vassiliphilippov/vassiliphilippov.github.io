unscored

adjective
not marked or scratched
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈskɔrd / (AmE), / ʌnˈskɔːd / (BrE)
not cut, scratched, or marked with lines, grooves, or notches; having a smooth or unmarked surface

Examples

Unscored wood surface
The craftsman preferred to leave the wood unscored for a cleaner finish.
Please keep the surface unscored so the paint will adhere evenly.
The archaeologist was surprised to find pottery with an unscored rim among the decorated pieces.

Related Terms

  • antonym: scored - marked with lines, cuts, or grooves
  • synonym: smooth - having an even and unmarked surface
