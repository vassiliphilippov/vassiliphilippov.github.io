guajira

proper noun
South American peninsula
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡwaˈxiɾa / (AmE), / ɡwaˈhiːrə / (BrE)
a peninsula in northern South America, divided between Colombia and Venezuela, and also a department in northern Colombia

Examples

The Guajira Peninsula
