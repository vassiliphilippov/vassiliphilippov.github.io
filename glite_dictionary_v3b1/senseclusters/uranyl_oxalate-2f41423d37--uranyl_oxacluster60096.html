uranyl oxalate

noun
a uranium-containing salt used in chemistry
9.9
Pronunciation: /jʊ.ˈræn.əl ˈɑːks.ə.leɪt/ /; / /jʊ.ˈreɪ.nəl ˈɑːks.ə.leɪt/ (AmE), /jʊ.ˈræn.əl ˈɒks.ə.leɪt/ /; / /jʊ.ˈreɪ.nəl ˈɒks.ə.leɪt/ (BrE)
a yellow crystalline salt formed by the reaction of uranyl compounds with oxalic acid, typically used in chemical analysis

Examples

Sample of uranyl oxalate
