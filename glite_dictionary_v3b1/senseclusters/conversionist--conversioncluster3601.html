conversionist

noun
advocate for conversion
8.8
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɜːrʒənɪst / (AmE), / kənˈvɜːʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who advocates for or works to convert others to a different belief, system, or way of thinking

Examples

Religious conversionist
The conversionist traveled to remote villages to share his new ideas.
As a passionate conversionist, she organized events to encourage people to adopt sustainable practices.
The debate featured a conversionist arguing for a shift to alternative energy sources.

Related Terms

  • synonym: proselytizer - a person who tries to persuade others to change their religion or beliefs
