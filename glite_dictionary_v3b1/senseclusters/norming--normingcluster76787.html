norming

noun
data normalization process
7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈnɔːr.mɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈnɔː.mɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process of adjusting data or test results to a common standard or scale, especially in mathematics, data processing, or testing

Examples

Norming test scores
The norming of test scores allows for fair comparison between students from different schools.
Before training the model, the data scientist performed norming on all input features.
Proper norming of vectors is essential in many machine learning algorithms.
The norming of the new intelligence test took several years to complete.
Researchers emphasized the importance of proper norming to ensure fair test results.
Without thorough norming, the test scores would be difficult to interpret accurately.

  • synonym: normalization - the process of making data comparable by adjusting values to a common scale
  • synonym: standardization - the process of bringing data to a standard form or scale
