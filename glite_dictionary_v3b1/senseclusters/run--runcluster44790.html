run

verb
travel a fixed route regularly
5.0
Pronunciation: / rʌn / (AmE), / rʌn / (BrE)
(of a vehicle, service, or vessel) to travel a particular route regularly, often according to a fixed schedule.

Examples

The train runs daily
A train runs from London to Manchester every morning.
Several buses run this route throughout the day.
The ferry runs between the two islands every hour.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ply - to travel a route regularly (especially of vehicles, boats, etc.)
