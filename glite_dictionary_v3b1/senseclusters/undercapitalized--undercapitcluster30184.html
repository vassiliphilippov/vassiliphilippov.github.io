undercapitalized

adjective
lacking capital letters
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈkæpɪtəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌndəkæpɪtəˌlaɪzd / (BrE)
written or printed with too few capital letters, especially where standard rules require them

Examples

Undercapitalized title
The title on the cover was undercapitalized, which made it look unprofessional.
Teachers often mark points off for undercapitalized sentences in student essays.
Her email was hard to read because many names were undercapitalized throughout the message.

Related Terms

  • antonym: capitalized - written with capital letters where required
  • synonym: uncapitalized - not written with capital letters where required
