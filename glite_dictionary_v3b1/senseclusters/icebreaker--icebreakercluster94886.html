icebreaker

noun
ship that breaks ice for navigation
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsˌbreɪkər / (AmE), / ˈaɪsˌbreɪkə / (BrE)
a ship designed with a reinforced bow to break up ice and keep shipping channels open in ice-covered waters

Examples

Arctic icebreaker
An icebreaker plowed through the thick Arctic ice to lead the way for cargo ships.
New icebreakers are being built to withstand even harsher frozen sea conditions.
The research team relied on an icebreaker to access the remote polar station during winter.
