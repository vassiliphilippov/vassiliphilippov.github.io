Utopian

noun
person who believes in a perfect society
2.4
Pronunciation: / juːˈtoʊpiən / (AmE), / juːˈtəʊpiən / (BrE)
a person who advocates or believes in the creation of a perfect or ideal society, often impractical or unrealistic

Examples

A Utopian thinker
As a young man, he was dismissed as a Utopian by more cynical politicians.
Many Utopians dream of a society free from poverty and injustice.
The debate featured a Utopian who argued that radical change was not only possible but necessary.

Related Terms

  • synonym: idealist - someone guided more by ideals than practical considerations
  • antonym: realist - a person who accepts a situation as it is and is prepared to deal with it accordingly
