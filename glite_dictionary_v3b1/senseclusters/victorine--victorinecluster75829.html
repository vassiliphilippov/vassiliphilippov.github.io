victorine

noun
member of Saint Victor Abbey
Pronunciation: / vɪkˈtɔːriːn / (AmE), / vɪkˈtɔːriːn / (BrE)
a member of the medieval Abbey of Saint Victor in Paris or its associated school of theology and philosophy

Examples

A medieval victorine
The victorine contributed significantly to medieval theological debates in Paris.
Many manuscripts written by a victorine are preserved in the abbey's library.
Scholars often reference the works of a prominent victorine from the twelfth century.
