freshly baked

adjective
recently cooked in an oven
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛʃli beɪkt / (AmE), / ˈfrɛʃli beɪkt / (BrE)
recently cooked in an oven and still warm, often with an appealing smell or taste

Examples

Freshly baked cookies

The kitchen was filled with the aroma of freshly baked bread.
