vinculum

noun

a horizontal line placed over numbers or expressions in mathematics to group them or indicate repetition

Examples

Math vinculum
In the decimal 0.3̅, the vinculum shows that the 3 repeats forever.
A vinculum is often used to group terms in a mathematical expression.
To indicate a repeating sequence, place a vinculum over the digits that repeat.
