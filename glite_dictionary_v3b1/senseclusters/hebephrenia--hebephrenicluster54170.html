hebephrenia

noun
disorganized type of schizophrenia
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːbəˈfriːniə / (AmE), / ˌhiːbɪˈfriːnɪə / (BrE)
A form of schizophrenia marked by disorganized behavior, disjointed thought patterns, and inappropriate, childlike actions, typically arising in adolescence.

Examples

Hebephrenia symptoms
Doctors diagnosed the teenager with hebephrenia after observing her disorganized speech and inappropriate laughter.
His erratic gestures and childish mannerisms were classic symptoms of hebephrenia.
Unlike other forms of schizophrenia, hebephrenia often emerges during adolescence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: schizophrenia - A severe mental disorder involving a breakdown in the relation between thought, emotion, and behavior
