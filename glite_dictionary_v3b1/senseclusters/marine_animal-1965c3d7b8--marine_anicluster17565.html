marine animal

noun
animal living in the sea
Pronunciation: / məˈrin ˈænɪməl / (AmE), / məˈriːn ˈænɪməl / (BrE)
An animal that lives in the sea, such as a fish, mollusc, or marine mammal.

Examples

Marine animal in ocean
Many marine animals have developed special adaptations to survive in their ocean environments.
Plastic pollution poses a serious threat to marine animals worldwide.
Scientists study marine animals to better understand the health of our oceans.
