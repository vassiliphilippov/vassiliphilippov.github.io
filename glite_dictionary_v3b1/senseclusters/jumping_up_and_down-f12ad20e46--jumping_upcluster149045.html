jumping up and down

noun
repeatedly jumping in place
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ ʌp ənd daʊn / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ ʌp ənd daʊn / (BrE)
The action of repeatedly jumping in one place, especially as an expression of excitement or agitation.

Examples

Jumping up and down excitedly
He expressed his joy by jumping up and down in the living room.
The children were jumping up and down when they heard the good news.
The fans started jumping up and down as their team scored the winning goal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hop - to make a short jump
  • synonym: leap - to jump high or a long way
