pit of despair

noun
a deep state of sadness or hopelessness
Pronunciation: / pɪt əv dɪˈspɛr / (AmE), / pɪt əv dɪˈspɛə / (BrE)
a state or feeling of extreme sadness, hopelessness, or depression

Examples

A pit of despair
After losing his job, Mark fell into a pit of despair and struggled to recover.
Spending the holidays alone left him in a pit of despair.
The character in the novel could not see a way out of her pit of despair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hopelessness - a feeling or state of having no hope
