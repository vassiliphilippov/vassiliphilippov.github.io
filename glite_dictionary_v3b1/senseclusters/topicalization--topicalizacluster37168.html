topicalization

noun
linguistic process of fronting topic
8.4 8.6
Pronunciation: /tɑː.pɪ.kə.lə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /tɒp.ɪ.kəl.ə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
(Linguistics) The construction of a sentence so that the topic or focus is placed at the beginning for emphasis, often by moving it from its typical position.

Examples

Topicalization in linguistics
Topicalization is common in informal speech when someone wants to emphasize a point.
