nuclear submarine

noun
submarine powered by nuclear energy
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːkliɚ ˌsʌbməˈriːn / (AmE), / ˈnjuːklɪə ˌsʌbməˈriːn / (BrE)
A submarine powered by a nuclear reactor, enabling it to remain submerged for extended periods.

Examples

A nuclear submarine
Because it uses nuclear power, a nuclear submarine can remain underwater for months at a time.
The navy commissioned a new nuclear submarine to strengthen its fleet.
The presence of a nuclear submarine in the region raised international tensions.
