authorized shares

noun
maximum shares allowed by a corporation's charter
3.7 5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈɔː.θə.raɪzd ʃeərz/ (AmE), /ˈɔː.θə.raɪzd ʃeəz/ (BrE)
The maximum number of shares a corporation is legally permitted to issue, as specified in its articles of incorporation.

Examples
