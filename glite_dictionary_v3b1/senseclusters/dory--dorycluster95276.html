dory

noun
marine fish with a deep, flat body
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔri / (AmE), / ˈdɔːri / (BrE)
a marine fish of several species with deep, laterally compressed bodies, found in mid-waters and deep slope waters; especially known for the John Dory.

Examples

John dory fish
A dory can often be identified by its large, round body and distinctive markings.
Fishermen caught several dory in the deep waters off the coast.
The dory is prized by chefs for its delicious white flesh.
