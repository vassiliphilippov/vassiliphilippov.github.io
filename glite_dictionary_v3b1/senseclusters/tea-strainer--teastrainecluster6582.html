tea-strainer

noun
device for straining tea leaves
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtiˌstreɪnɚ / (AmE), / ˈtiˌstreɪnə / (BrE)
a small device placed over a cup or teapot to catch loose tea leaves when pouring tea

Examples

A tea-strainer device
Don't forget to use the tea-strainer when serving loose leaf tea.
She poured the hot tea through a tea-strainer to remove the leaves.
The antique silver tea-strainer was her grandmother's prized possession.
