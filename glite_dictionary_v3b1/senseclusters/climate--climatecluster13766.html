climate

The general feeling, mood, or attitudes that exist in a particular place or situation at a given time.

Examples

Workplace climate
A positive climate at work encourages collaboration and creativity among employees.
The political climate has made it harder to reach agreement on important issues.
There has been a noticeable shift in the climate of public opinion regarding environmental matters.

Synonyms

    2.1 29/m
    mood is the short-term emotional state of a group or person, while climate describes longer, shared attitudes in a place.
  • feeling
    1.4 15/m
    feeling is an individual, immediate sense about a situation, while climate means the wider, shared mood or attitudes there.
