melaleuca

noun
tea tree oil
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛləˈluːkə / (AmE), / ˌmɛləˈluːkə / (BrE)
an essential oil from certain Melaleuca species, especially tea trees, used for antiseptic and medicinal purposes

Examples

Melaleuca oil remedy
Many people use melaleuca as a natural remedy for minor skin irritations.
The antiseptic properties of melaleuca make it popular in herbal medicine.
She added a few drops of melaleuca to her homemade cleaning solution.
