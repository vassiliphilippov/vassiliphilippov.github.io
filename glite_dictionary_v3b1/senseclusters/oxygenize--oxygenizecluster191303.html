oxygenize

verb
to supply or treat with oxygen
Pronunciation: / ˈɑksɪdʒəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɒksɪdʒəˌnaɪz /; / ˈɒksɪdʒənaɪz / (BrE)
to add or combine oxygen with something; to supply or enrich with oxygen

Examples
