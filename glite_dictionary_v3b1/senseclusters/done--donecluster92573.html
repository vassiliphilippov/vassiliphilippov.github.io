Synonyms

  • finished
    1.0 0.04/m
    finished means an activity is fully complete with no work left, while done is a common word for the same state.
  • complete
    1.0 42/m
    complete often describes something whole or fully finished, while done is everyday speech to say a task has ended.
  • through
    2.3 0.00/m
    through is used informally to say you have finished an activity, while done is more general and common in many contexts.
  • ended
    1.0 0.12/m
    ended describes something that has come to a close, often an event, while done focuses on a completed task.
  • concluded
    2.5 0.00/m
    concluded is a more formal word for bringing something to an end, while done is casual and used in everyday speech.
  • accomplished
    3.9 0.00/m
    accomplished implies success or achievement of a goal, while done simply says a task or activity is finished.
