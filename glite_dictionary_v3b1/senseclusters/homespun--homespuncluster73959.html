homespun

adjective
simple, rural; characteristic of country life
7.8 5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈhoʊm.spʌn/ /; / /ˈhoʊm.spən/ (AmE), /ˈhəʊm.spʌn/ /; / /ˈhəʊm.spən/ (BrE)

Examples
