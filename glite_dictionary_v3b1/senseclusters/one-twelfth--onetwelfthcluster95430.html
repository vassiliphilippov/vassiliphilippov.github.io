one-twelfth

noun
one of twelve equal parts; 1/12
3.1
Pronunciation: / wʌn twɛlfθ / (AmE), / wʌn twɛlfθ / (BrE)
Each of twelve equal parts into which something is divided; 1/12.

Examples

A one-twelfth share
Each partner owns one-twelfth of the company shares.
Only one-twelfth of the cake remained after the party.
The interest rate increased by one-twelfth each month.
