a dog's life

noun
life of comfort or hardship
Pronunciation: / ə dɔɡz laɪf / (AmE), / ə dɒɡz laɪf / (BrE)
a life that is either very comfortable and easy, or (historically) marked by hardship and misfortune

Examples

A dog's life of ease
After retiring to the countryside, he's been living a dog's life with no worries at all.
Sarah jokes that her husband leads a dog's life now that he works from home.
Some people complain about a dog's life, but it can mean great comfort for others.
