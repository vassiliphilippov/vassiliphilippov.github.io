knot

noun
a species of migratory sandpiper
7.6
Pronunciation: / nɑt / (AmE), / nɒt / (BrE)
a migratory sandpiper (Calidris canutus) that breeds in the Arctic and spends winters in the Southern Hemisphere, noted for its reddish-brown plumage during breeding season.

Examples

A knot bird
Conservationists monitor knot populations to assess the health of wetland habitats.
Flocks of knot gather on coastal mudflats each year to feed during migration.
The knot is easily recognized by its stout build and short bill.
