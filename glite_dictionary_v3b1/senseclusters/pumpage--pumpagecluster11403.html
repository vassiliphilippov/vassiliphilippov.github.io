pumpage

noun
pumping service fee
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌm.pɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpʌm.pɪdʒ / (BrE)
a charge or fee for pumping services, especially for moving liquids such as fuel or water

Examples

Pumpage fee
The invoice included a pumpage for transferring the oil from the tanker.
Customers questioned the new pumpage added to their monthly water bills.
A small pumpage is charged for each delivery to cover equipment costs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fee - a payment made for a service
  • synonym: surcharge - an additional charge or payment
