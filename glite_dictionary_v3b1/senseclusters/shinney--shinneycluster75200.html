shinney

noun
children's informal hockey game
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪni / (AmE), / ˈʃɪni / (BrE)
A simple form of hockey, typically played by children on streets, ice, or fields, using a ball or can as the puck.

Examples

To play shinney game
He fondly recalled the days when neighborhood kids would organize impromptu shinney matches after school.
On winter afternoons, children gathered on the frozen pond to play shinney until the sun set.
We used an old tin can as a puck during our game of shinney in the street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hockey - a team sport played on ice (ice hockey) or field, using sticks and a puck or ball
  • synonym: shinny - informal or improvised game of hockey, especially on ice
