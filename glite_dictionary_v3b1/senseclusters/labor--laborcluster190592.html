labor

verb
work hard; exert effort
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪbər /; / ˈleɪ.bɚ /; / ˈleɪbɚ / (AmE), / ˈleɪbə /; / ˈleɪ.bə / (BrE)
to work hard or make a strong effort, especially in difficult conditions

Examples

  • antonym: relax - to rest or do something enjoyable after work or effort
  • synonym: strive - make great efforts to achieve or obtain something
  • synonym: toil - to work extremely hard or incessantly
