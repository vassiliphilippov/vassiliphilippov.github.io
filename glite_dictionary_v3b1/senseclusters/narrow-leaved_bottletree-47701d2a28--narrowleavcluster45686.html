narrow-leaved bottletree

noun
Australian tree with cream and red flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnæroʊ liːvd ˈbɑtəl triː / (AmE), / ˈnærəʊ liːvd ˈbɒtl triː / (BrE)
