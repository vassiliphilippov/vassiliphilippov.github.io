endearment

noun
expression of affection
3.7
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɪrmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɪəmənt / (BrE)

Examples

A loving endearment
He called her by a sweet endearment that made her smile.
Parents often use endearments when speaking to their young children.
The letter was filled with loving endearments and kind words.
