metalepsis

noun
figure of speech using one figurative sense for another
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəˈlɛpsɪs / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəˈlɛpsɪs / (BrE)
A figure of speech in which a word or phrase from one figurative expression is used in a new context, substituting one metonymy for another.
