neurolysis

noun
nerve tissue removal
8.1
Pronunciation: / nʊˈrɑːlɪsɪs / (AmE), / njʊəˈrɒlɪsɪs / (BrE)
a medical procedure in which nerve tissue is destroyed or removed to relieve pain or restore function

Examples

Chemical neurolysis treatment
The doctor recommended neurolysis to help manage the patient's severe nerve pain.
After undergoing neurolysis, the patient reported significant relief from chronic discomfort.
In some cases, neurolysis can restore movement by removing damaged nerve tissue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neurectomy - surgical removal of part of a nerve
  • synonym: neurotomy - surgical cutting of a nerve
