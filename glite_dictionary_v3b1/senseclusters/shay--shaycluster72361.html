shay

noun
two-wheeled light carriage
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪ / (AmE), / ʃeɪ / (BrE)
a light carriage with two wheels and a folding calash top, usually drawn by one horse

Examples

A shay carriage
A battered shay stood at the gate, its faded calash top folded back.
He remembered fondly the family outings in their horse-drawn shay.
The old shay rattled down the country lane, its wheels clattering on the stones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chaise - a light open carriage, often with two wheels, for one or two people
