fell

verb
cause to fall by cutting or striking
9.9
Pronunciation: / fɛl / (AmE), / fɛl / (BrE)
to cause something to fall, especially by cutting it down or delivering a blow

Examples

To fell a tree
A strong gust of wind felled several power lines last night.
The disease felled many people during the harsh winter.
The workers felled the ancient oak tree with a single powerful cut.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cut down - to make something fall by cutting at its base, especially a tree
  • synonym: topple - to make something fall over
