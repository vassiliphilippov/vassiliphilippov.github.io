gaspy

adjective
breathing with difficulty
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæspi / (AmE), / ˈɡaspi / (BrE)
breathing with difficulty or making gasping sounds, often due to exertion, shock, or lack of air

Examples

Gaspy breathing
After running up the hill, she felt gaspy and needed to rest for a moment.
The gaspy sound in his voice worried the doctor during the examination.
He became gaspy after swimming several laps without stopping.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breathless - unable to breathe easily, often from exertion
  • synonym: wheezing - making a whistling sound while breathing
