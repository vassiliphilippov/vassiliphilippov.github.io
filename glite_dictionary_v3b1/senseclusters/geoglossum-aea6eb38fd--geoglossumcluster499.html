Geoglossum

noun
genus of fungi (earthtongues)
9.9
Pronunciation: /dʒiː.ə.ˈglɑː.səm/ /; / /dʒiː.oʊ.ˈglɑː.səm/ (AmE), /dʒiː.ə.ˈglɒs.əm/ (BrE)
A genus of fungi in the family Geoglossaceae, commonly known as earthtongues, characterized by club-shaped, dark fruiting bodies found on soil or decaying plant material.

Examples

The dark, slender fruiting bodies of Geoglossum are often found in wet meadows and woodlands.
