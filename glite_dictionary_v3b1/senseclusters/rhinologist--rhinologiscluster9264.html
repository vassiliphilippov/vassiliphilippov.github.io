rhinologist

noun
nose specialist
Pronunciation: / raɪˈnɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / raɪˈnɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist or researcher who studies the nose, its functions, and related disorders

Examples

Rhinologist research
The rhinologist presented new findings on nasal allergies at the international conference.
A team led by a rhinologist is studying how air pollution affects our sense of smell.
After years of research, the rhinologist published a book about chronic sinusitis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neurologist - a doctor who studies and treats disorders of the nervous system
  • synonym: otolaryngologist - a medical doctor specializing in the ear, nose, and throat
