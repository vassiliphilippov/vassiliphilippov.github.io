objectless

adjective
without a goal
Pronunciation: / ˈɑbdʒɪktləs / (AmE), / ˈɒbdʒɪktləs / (BrE)
having no object, goal, or specific target

Examples

Objectless wandering
He spent an objectless afternoon wandering through the park with no particular destination.
Her objectless search for meaning left her feeling lost and confused.
The conversation became objectless as they drifted from topic to topic without focus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aimless - without purpose or direction
  • antonym: purposeful - having a clear aim or goal
