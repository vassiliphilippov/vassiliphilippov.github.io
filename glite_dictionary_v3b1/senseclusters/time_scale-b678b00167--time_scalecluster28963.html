time scale

noun
system for measuring time or events
1.4
Pronunciation: / taɪm skeɪl / (AmE), / taɪm skeɪl / (BrE)
A system or arrangement for measuring or representing the duration or sequence of events over a period of time.

Examples

A long time scale
Climate change must be studied over a long time scale to understand its patterns.
Historians often use a different time scale to describe ancient civilizations.
On the geological time scale, the age of dinosaurs is relatively recent.
