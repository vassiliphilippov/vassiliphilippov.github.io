countenance

noun
a person's face or facial expression
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntənəns / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntənəns / (BrE)
a person's face or facial expression, especially as an indication of mood or character

Examples

A serious countenance
His angry countenance discouraged further questions from the staff.
She entered the room with a calm countenance, hiding her true feelings.
There was a solemn look on her countenance during the ceremony.
He wore a stern countenance during the entire meeting.
Her calm countenance hid the anxiety she felt inside.
The artist captured the sorrow in the sitter's countenance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: visage - a person's face, with reference to the form or proportions of the features
