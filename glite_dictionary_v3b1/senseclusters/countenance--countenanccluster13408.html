Synonyms

  • facial expression

    facial expression is a movement of the face that shows feeling, while countenance refers to the whole face or its expression.
  • expression
    2.5 39/m
    expression is a look or sign that shows feeling or thought, while countenance often means the face and its mood.
  • face
    1.0 86/m
    face is the physical front part of the head that we see, while countenance focuses on the face as an expression.
  • look
    1.0 59/m
    look is a quick visible signal people read on the face, while countenance more often names the whole face or its expression.
  • visage
    7.9 1.27/m

  • mien
    9.1 0.35/m
    mien is the way a person shows character by bearing and manner, while countenance names the face or facial expression.
