colliery

noun
coal mine and its buildings
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːl.jɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈkɒl.jə.ri / (BrE)
A coal mine and all the buildings and equipment associated with it.

Examples

A colliery mine
Many families in the region depended on the colliery for their livelihood.
The old colliery was closed in the 1980s and is now a museum.
The strike at the colliery lasted for several weeks and drew national attention.
