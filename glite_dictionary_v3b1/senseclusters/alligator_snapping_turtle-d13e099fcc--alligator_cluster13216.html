alligator snapping turtle

noun
large ridged freshwater turtle of the southeastern U.S.
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈæləˌɡeɪtɚ ˈsnæpɪŋ ˈtɝtəl / (AmE), / ˈalɪɡeɪtə ˈsnapɪŋ ˈtɜːtl̩ / (BrE)
a large freshwater turtle of the southeastern United States, distinguished by three prominent ridges on its shell and a powerful jaw

Examples
