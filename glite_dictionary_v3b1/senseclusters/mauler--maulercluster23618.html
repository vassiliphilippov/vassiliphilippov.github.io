mauler

noun
aggressive fighter or boxer
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔlɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɔːlə / (BrE)
a fighter, especially in boxing, known for aggressively battering or attacking an opponent

Examples

A fierce mauler
Fans admired her reputation as a relentless mauler in the ring.
Jack Dempsey, a legendary mauler, was feared for his powerful and aggressive fighting style.
The undefeated mauler quickly overwhelmed his opponent in the first round.
