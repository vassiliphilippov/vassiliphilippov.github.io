Boocercus eurycerus

noun
species of large African forest antelope
Pronunciation: / boʊˈsɜːrkəs ˌjʊərɪˈsɪrəs / (AmE), / bəʊˈsɜːkəs ˌjʊərɪˈsɪərəs / (BrE)
A large forest antelope of central Africa with a reddish-brown coat marked by white stripes and spiral horns; also known as the bongo.
