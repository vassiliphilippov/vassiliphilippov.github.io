empowerment

noun
gaining power, autonomy, or capacity
3.9
Pronunciation: /ɪm.ˈpaʊ.ər.mənt/ (AmE), /ɪm.ˈpaʊ.ə.mənt/ (BrE)
the process or state of becoming stronger, more confident, autonomous, or capable, especially in social, political, or personal contexts.

Examples

Community empowerment
Educational programs foster community empowerment by increasing access to resources and leadership training.
Her sense of empowerment grew after she completed the leadership course and started mentoring.
The policy aims to promote women's economic empowerment through microloans and skills development programs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agency - the capacity of individuals to act independently and make their own free choices
  • synonym: autonomy - the ability to make your own decisions without outside control
  • antonym: disempowerment - the process of making someone less powerful or depriving them of influence
