chape

verb
to add metal fitting
9.9
Pronunciation: / tʃeɪp / (AmE), / tʃeɪp / (BrE)
to cover or edge the end of a sheath, strap, or similar item with a protective or decorative metal fitting

Examples

To chape a sheath
The craftsman skillfully chaped the sword's sheath with a silver tip for extra durability.
It was common in medieval times to chape belts and straps to prevent fraying.
They decided to chape the leather sheath with a bronze tip to prevent wear.
