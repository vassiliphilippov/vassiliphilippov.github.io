ridley

noun
a type of marine turtle
6.1 7.8
Pronunciation: /ˈrɪd.liː/ (AmE), /ˈrɪd.liː/ (BrE)
A marine turtle of the genus Lepidochelys, found chiefly in warm coastal waters, especially the Kemp's ridley and olive ridley turtles.

Examples

Ridley turtle
Conservation efforts have helped increase the ridley turtle population in recent years.
Scientists studied the nesting habits of the Kemp's ridley along the Gulf Coast.
The ridley is one of the smallest and most endangered species of sea turtle.

  • synonym: olive ridley - a species of ridley sea turtle, Lepidochelys olivacea
