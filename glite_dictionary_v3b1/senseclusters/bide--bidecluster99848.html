bide

verb
to stay or wait
7.4
Pronunciation: /baɪd/ (AmE), /baɪd/ (BrE)
to remain or stay in a place for a period of time, often waiting for something to happen

Examples

Bide in a place
He decided to bide in the quiet town until the trouble passed.
They must bide their time and wait for the right opportunity.
After the meeting, she chose to bide in the lobby for a while.

Related Terms

  • synonym: linger - to stay somewhere longer than necessary
  • synonym: wait - to stay in one place until something happens
