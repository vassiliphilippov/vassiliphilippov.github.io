Norwegian elkhound

noun
breed of medium-sized hunting dog from Norway
5.8
Pronunciation: / nɔːrˈwiːdʒən ˈɛlˌkaʊnd / (AmE), / nɔːˈwiːdʒ(ə)n ˈɛlkaʊnd / (BrE)
a medium-sized dog breed with a heavy grey coat, developed in Norway for hunting elk and other large game

Examples

The Norwegian elkhound breed
At the dog show, her Norwegian elkhound won first place in the working group.
Historically, hunters in Norway used the Norwegian elkhound to track and corner large game like elk.
The Norwegian elkhound is known for its friendly temperament and loyalty to its owners.
