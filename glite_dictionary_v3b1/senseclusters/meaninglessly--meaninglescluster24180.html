meaninglessly

adverb
without purpose
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːnɪŋlɪsli / (AmE), / ˈmiːnɪŋlɪsli / (BrE)
in a way that lacks purpose, sense, or significance

Examples

To meaninglessly stare
He stared meaninglessly out the window, lost in thought.
The numbers were repeated meaninglessly without any explanation or context.
She laughed meaninglessly, not really finding the joke funny.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pointlessly - in a way that serves no useful purpose
  • antonym: purposefully - in a way that shows determination or intention
  • synonym: purposelessly - without a clear aim or reason
