Shekinah

noun
divine presence
Pronunciation: / ʃəˈkaɪnə / (AmE), / ʃɪˈkiːnə / (BrE)
the visible or tangible presence of God, especially as described in Jewish theology

Examples

The Shekinah appears
The rabbi spoke about the Shekinah as a symbol of divine presence.
Many believe the Shekinah filled the temple with light during ancient ceremonies.
In her prayers, she felt comforted by the sense of the Shekinah surrounding her.

Related Terms

  • synonym: divinity - the state or quality of being divine or godlike
  • synonym: manifestation - an event, action, or object that clearly shows or embodies something
