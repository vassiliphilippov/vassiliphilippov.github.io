Synonyms

  • interject
    6.5 2.68/m
    interject is to say something quickly between speakers, while interpose implies stepping between people or events to interrupt or mediate.
  • insert
    3.5 20/m
    insert means putting something into an object, text, or conversation, while interpose suggests placing yourself between others to intervene.
