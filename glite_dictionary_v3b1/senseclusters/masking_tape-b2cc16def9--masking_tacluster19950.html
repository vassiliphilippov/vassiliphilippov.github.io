masking tape

noun
adhesive tape for masking surfaces
Pronunciation: / ˈmæs.kɪŋ teɪp / (AmE), / ˈmɑː.skɪŋ teɪp / (BrE)
a type of adhesive tape used to protect areas of a surface from paint or to temporarily hold things in place

Examples

Masking tape for painting
He used masking tape to cover the edges of the window before painting the frame.
Make sure to remove the masking tape while the paint is still wet for a clean line.
She labeled the boxes with strips of masking tape and a marker.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tape - strip of material coated with adhesive used to stick things together
