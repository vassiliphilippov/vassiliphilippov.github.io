Zirbanit

proper noun
Babylonian goddess, consort of Marduk
Pronunciation: / zɪrˈbænɪt / (AmE), / zɪəˈbænɪt / (BrE)
In Babylonian and Assyrian mythology, the goddess regarded as the consort of Marduk.

Examples

Zirbanit goddess
In ancient texts, Zirbanit is frequently mentioned alongside Marduk as his consort.
Priests in Babylon dedicated offerings to Zirbanit during religious ceremonies.
Some scholars study the role of Zirbanit in the pantheon of Mesopotamian deities.
