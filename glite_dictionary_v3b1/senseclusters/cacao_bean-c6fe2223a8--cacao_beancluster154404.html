cacao bean

noun
seed of the cacao tree used for chocolate
1.0
Pronunciation: / kəˈkaʊ biːn / (AmE), / kəˈkaʊ biːn / (BrE)
the dried seed of the cacao tree, from which cocoa and chocolate are made

Examples

Roasted cacao beans
Roasting the cacao beans is an essential step in making rich cocoa powder.
She visited a plantation where cacao beans were harvested and fermented.
The factory imports raw cacao beans to produce high-quality chocolate bars.
