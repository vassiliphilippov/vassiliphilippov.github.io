cheeky chops

noun
playful term for a boldly cheeky person
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiki tʃɑps / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːki tʃɒps / (BrE)
a playful term for a person, especially a child, who behaves in a boldly impudent or cheeky way

She called her son cheeky chops after he tried to trick her for a cookie.
