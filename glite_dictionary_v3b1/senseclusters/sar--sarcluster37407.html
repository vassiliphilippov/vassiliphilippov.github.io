sar

noun
polite address term
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɑr / (AmE), / sɑː / (BrE)
a polite form of address similar to 'sir', used in some varieties of English, especially in South Asia and the Caribbean

Examples

Thank you, sar
The student respectfully said, "Thank you, sar," after receiving help from his teacher.
In some schools, children address their male teachers as sar instead of 'sir'.
"Good morning, sar," the shopkeeper greeted the customer with a smile.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sir - a respectful form of address for a man
