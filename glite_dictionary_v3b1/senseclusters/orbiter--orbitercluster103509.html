orbiter

noun
person in someone's social orbit
7.7 0.01/m
Pronunciation: /  / (AmE), /  / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who closely follows, supports, or stays near a celebrity or central figure, often as part of their social circle or entourage.

Examples

Celebrity orbiter
The actor's orbiter attended every premiere, quietly orbiting him like a loyal shadow.
Her social media orbiters repeatedly liked every photo but never directly messaged her.
Reporters noted several orbiters who kept appearing around the politician at events.

Synonyms

  • satellite
    7.4 1.61/m

Related Terms

  • synonym: groupie - a fan who follows a celebrity or musician closely; often someone in their social circle
  • synonym: hanger-on - a person who stays close to someone important, often for personal advantage
