hydrogen sulfate

noun
a chemical salt or ester of HSO4−
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪdrədʒən ˈsʌlˌfeɪt / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdrɪdʒən ˈsʌl.feɪt / (BrE)
a salt or ester of sulfuric acid containing the ion HSO4−

Examples

Hydrogen sulfate ion
Hydrogen sulfate ions can act as acids in aqueous solutions.
Potassium hydrogen sulfate is commonly used in laboratories as a reagent.
The presence of hydrogen sulfate in water can affect its acidity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bisulfate - an alternate name for hydrogen sulfate (HSO4−)
  • degree higher: sulphate - a salt or ester of sulfuric acid containing SO4^2− ion
