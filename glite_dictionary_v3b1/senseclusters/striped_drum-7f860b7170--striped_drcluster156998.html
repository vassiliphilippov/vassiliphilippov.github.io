striped drum

noun
a type of drumfish with stripes
Pronunciation: / ˈstraɪpt drʌm / (AmE), / ˈstraɪpt drʌm / (BrE)
a species of drumfish distinguished by horizontal stripes along its body, found in coastal waters.
