infinitesimally

adverb
to an extremely small degree
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnfɪnɪˈtɛsɪməli / (AmE), / ˌɪnfɪnɪˈtɛsɪməli / (BrE)
to an extremely small degree; by an amount so small as to be almost zero or unnoticeable
