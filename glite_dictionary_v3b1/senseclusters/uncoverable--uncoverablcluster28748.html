uncoverable

adjective
impossible to cover
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkʌvərəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈkʌvərəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be covered or not able to be discovered

Examples

Uncoverable costs
Some costs in the contract are uncoverable by insurance.
The evidence was so well hidden that it remained uncoverable for years.
Certain damages are uncoverable under the terms of this policy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coverable - able to be covered
  • synonym: inaccessible - not able to be reached or obtained
