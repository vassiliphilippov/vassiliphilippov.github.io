jefe

noun
informal respectful address
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪfeɪ / (AmE), / ˈheɪfeɪ / (BrE)
an informal term used to address a man in a friendly or respectful way, similar to 'chief' or 'boss'

Examples

Hey jefe
He greeted his friend with a smile and said, 'Hey, jefe, how are you today?'
The regulars at the café always call the owner jefe as a sign of respect.
When the team won, everyone cheered and shouted, 'Great job, jefe!'

Related Terms

  • synonym: boss - an informal term for a person in charge or as a friendly address
  • synonym: chief - an informal term of respect for a man
