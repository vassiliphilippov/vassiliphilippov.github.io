news conference

noun
event where reporters question officials/celebrities
3.2 8.50/m
Pronunciation: / nuz Ààk…ëÀên.f…ö.…ôns / (AmE), / njuÀêz Ààk…ín.f…ôr.…ôns / (BrE)
an event at which reporters are invited to ask questions of a politician, celebrity, or organization and receive official statements or responses

Examples

A news conference event
After the match, the coach answered questions at a news conference.
The company will schedule a news conference to announce the new product line.
The prime minister held a news conference to address the recent scandal.

Synonyms
