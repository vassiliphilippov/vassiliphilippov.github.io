keratinization

noun
process of cells becoming keratinized
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌkɛrətɪnəˈzeɪʃən /; / kəˌrætəˌnaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɛrətɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / kɪəˌrætɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process by which keratin is deposited in cells, causing them to become hard and horny, as in the formation of nails, hair, or the outer layer of skin
