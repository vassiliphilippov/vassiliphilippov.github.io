public debate

noun
a formal event with arguments for and against a proposition
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪk dɪˈbeɪt / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk dɪˈbeɪt / (BrE)
a formal event where a proposition is presented and opposed, typically followed by a vote to decide the issue

Examples

The candidates engaged in a public debate on national television before the election.
