camelback

noun
curved-back furniture
7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈkæm.əl.bæk/ (AmE), /ˈkæm.əl.bæk/ (BrE)
a style of sofa or chair with a back that curves into one or more rounded humps, resembling a camel's back, often seen in antique furniture

Examples

Camelback sofa
The antique shop displayed a beautiful camelback chair with elegant wooden legs.
They chose a camelback sofa to add a classic touch to their living room.
The curved design of the camelback makes it a popular choice for traditional interiors.

  • synonym: sofa - a long, comfortable seat for several people
