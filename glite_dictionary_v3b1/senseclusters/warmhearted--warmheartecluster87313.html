warmhearted

adjective
kind and sympathetic
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrmˌhɑrtɪd / (AmE), / ˈwɔːmhɑːtɪd / (BrE)
(of a person or their actions) kind, sympathetic, and friendly; showing affection and generosity towards others.

Examples

A warmhearted person
He is known for his warmhearted generosity and willingness to help others.
Her warmhearted response made the new student feel instantly at home.
The villagers gave the visitors a warmhearted welcome despite never having met them before.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coldhearted - lacking in compassion or kindness
  • synonym: kindhearted - having a kind and sympathetic nature
