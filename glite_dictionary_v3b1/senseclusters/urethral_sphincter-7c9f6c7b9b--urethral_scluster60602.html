urethral sphincter

noun
muscle that controls urine flow
4.7
Pronunciation: / jʊˈriːθrəl ˈsfɪŋktɚ / (AmE), / jʊˈriːθrəl ˈsfɪŋktə / (BrE)
a muscle surrounding the urethra that contracts to control the release of urine from the bladder

Examples

The urethral sphincter muscle
Doctors may assess the strength of the urethral sphincter during urological examinations.
Injury to the urethral sphincter can cause problems with bladder control.
The urethral sphincter plays a crucial role in maintaining urinary continence.
