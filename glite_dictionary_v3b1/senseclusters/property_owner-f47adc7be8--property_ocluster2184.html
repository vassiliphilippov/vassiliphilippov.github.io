property owner

noun
person who owns property or land
3.5 3.42/m
Pronunciation: / Ààpr…ëÀêp…öti Àåo än…ö / (AmE), / Ààpr…íp…ôti Àå…ô än…ô / (BrE)
a person who legally owns land or real estate, typically responsible for its use and management

Examples

A property owner pays taxes
As a property owner, she pays annual taxes on her land.
The property owner is responsible for repairs to the building.
The city contacted each property owner about the new zoning regulations.

Synonyms

  • landowner
    2.5 5.43/m
    landowner owns rural or urban land and often manages estates, while property owner refers to anyone who legally owns real estate.
  • landholder
    4.1 0.41/m
    landholder holds or controls plots of land, often for farming or rent, while property owner is any legal owner of real estate.
  • titleholder
    4.1 0.04/m
    titleholder holds the legal title to an asset, often as an organization, while property owner usually means a private real estate owner.
  • owner
    1.0 103/m
    owner has legal possession of any item or business, while property owner specifically owns land or real estate and manages it.
