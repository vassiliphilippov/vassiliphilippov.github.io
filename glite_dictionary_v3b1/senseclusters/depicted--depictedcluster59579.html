depicted

adjective
shown or illustrated in art
1.8
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɪktɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈpɪktɪd / (BrE)
represented visually, especially in a drawing, painting, or other artistic form.

Examples

Depicted in a painting
In the painting, the countryside is depicted in warm, glowing colors.
The ancient gods are often depicted with elaborate crowns and flowing robes.
The story is depicted through a series of vivid illustrations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: portrayed - described or depicted, especially in art or literature
