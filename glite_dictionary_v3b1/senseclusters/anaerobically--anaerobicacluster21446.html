anaerobically

adverb

7.6
Pronunciation: /æn.ə.ˈroʊ.bɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /ə.nə.ˈroʊ.bɪ.kəl.iː/ (AmE), /æn.ə.ˈrəʊ.bɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /ə.nə.ˈrəʊ.bɪ.kəl.iː/ (BrE)

Examples

Grow anaerobically

Yeast ferments sugar anaerobically to produce alcohol in brewing processes.
