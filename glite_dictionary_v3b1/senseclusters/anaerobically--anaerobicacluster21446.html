anaerobically

adverb
without oxygen
Pronunciation: / ˌænəˈroʊbɪkli / (AmE), / ˌænəˈrəʊbɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that does not require oxygen

Examples

Grow anaerobically
Certain bacteria can grow anaerobically in deep soil where oxygen is absent.
Yeast ferments sugar anaerobically to produce alcohol in brewing processes.
Some muscle cells function anaerobically during intense exercise when oxygen is limited.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aerobically - in a way that requires oxygen
