good ol' boy

noun
white southern US man with conservative views
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʊd oʊl bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡʊd əʊl bɔɪ / (BrE)
(in the southern US) a white man who is friendly and informal, often holding conservative or intolerant views and showing strong loyalty to his male peers.

Examples

A good ol' boy network
As a good ol' boy, he grew up hunting and fishing in rural Alabama.
He prides himself on being a good ol' boy with strong community ties.
The political club was full of good ol' boys who looked out for each other.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: old boy - a man who is a former pupil of a particular school
  • synonym: redneck - an uneducated white rural Southerner, typically with conservative views
