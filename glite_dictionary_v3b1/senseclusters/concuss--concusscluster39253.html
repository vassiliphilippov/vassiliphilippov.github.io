concuss

verb
to shake violently due to impact
Pronunciation: / kənˈkʌs / (AmE), / kənˈkʌs / (BrE)
to shake or jolt something or someone violently, especially as a result of a blow or explosion

Examples

The blast was so powerful it concussed the windows of nearby buildings.
The crash concussed the car's occupants, leaving them dazed and disoriented.

He was concussed after being struck on the head during the game.
The violent impact concussed everyone in the room, leaving them stunned.
