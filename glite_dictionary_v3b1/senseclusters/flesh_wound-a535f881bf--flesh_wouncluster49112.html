flesh wound

noun
minor wound affecting only soft tissue
2.4
Pronunciation: / flɛʃ wund / (AmE), / flɛʃ wuːnd / (BrE)
a wound that affects only the skin and soft tissue, without damaging vital organs or bones

Examples

A flesh wound
Although the knife cut looked serious, it turned out to be a superficial flesh wound with no lasting damage.
He suffered only a flesh wound in the accident, despite the severity of the crash.
The doctor assured her that it was just a flesh wound and would heal quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: injury - any physical damage to body tissue
  • synonym: laceration - a deep cut or tear in flesh or skin
  • degree lower: scratch - a slight injury that breaks the surface of the skin
