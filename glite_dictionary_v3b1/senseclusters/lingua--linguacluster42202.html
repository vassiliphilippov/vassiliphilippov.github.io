lingua

noun
the tongue (anatomy, zoology)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪŋɡwə / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋɡwə / (BrE)
the tongue, especially as an organ of speech or as found in animals; a mobile mass of muscular tissue in the mouth
