storybook

noun
book of stories, often for children
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔriˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈstɔːriˌbʊk / (BrE)
a book containing a collection of stories, typically for children

Examples

A storybook for children
He cherished the old storybook that once belonged to his grandmother.
She read her daughter a favorite storybook every night before bed.
The classroom has a shelf full of colorful storybooks for the children to enjoy.
