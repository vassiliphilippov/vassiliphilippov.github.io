mucositis

noun
inflammation of mucous membranes
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmjuːkoʊˈsaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌmjuːkəʊˈsaɪtɪs / (BrE)
painful inflammation and ulceration of the mucous membranes lining the digestive tract, often occurring in the mouth

Examples

Painful mucositis in mouth
Patients undergoing chemotherapy often develop mucositis as a side effect of their treatment.
The doctor prescribed a special mouthwash to help relieve the pain caused by mucositis.
Severe mucositis can make it difficult for patients to eat or drink comfortably.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stomatitis - inflammation of the mouth and lips
  • synonym: ulcer - an open sore on an external or internal surface of the body
