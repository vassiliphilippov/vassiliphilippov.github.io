ziggurat

noun
rectangular stepped temple tower (ancient Mesopotamia)
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈzɪɡəˌræt / (AmE), / ˈzɪɡərat /; / ˈzɪɡəræt / (BrE)
a rectangular, stepped or tiered temple structure built in ancient Mesopotamia by the Assyrians and Babylonians
