milliner

noun
person who makes or sells hats
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlɪnər / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪnə / (BrE)
a person who designs, makes, or sells women's hats

Examples

A milliner designs hats
As a renowned milliner, she created custom headpieces for many celebrities.
She visited the local milliner to order a hat for the wedding.
The elegant display in the milliner's window caught everyone’s attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hatter - a person who makes, repairs, or sells hats (more generally, not specifically women's hats)
