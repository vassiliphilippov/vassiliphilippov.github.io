slip

noun
sleeveless undergarment for women
Pronunciation: / slɪp / (AmE), / slɪp / (BrE)
a woman's loose, usually sleeveless undergarment worn beneath a dress or skirt.

Examples

A blue slip
Her blue slip peeked out from beneath the hem of her skirt.
She wore a white slip under her dress for extra comfort.
The shop sells silk and cotton slips in various sizes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: petticoat - a woman's undergarment hanging from the waist, worn under a skirt or dress
