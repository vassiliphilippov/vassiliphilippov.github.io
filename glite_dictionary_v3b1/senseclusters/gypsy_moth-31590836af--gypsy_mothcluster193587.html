gypsy moth

noun
destructive moth pest (Lymantria dispar)
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪpsi mɑθ /; / ˈdʒɪpsi mɔθ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪpsi mɒθ / (BrE)
A large European moth (Lymantria dispar) introduced into North America; its larvae are serious pests of shade trees and forests and the species can cause significant defoliation.
