Bachelor of Arts

noun
undergraduate degree in arts or sciences
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbætʃələr əv ɑrts / (AmE), / ˈbatʃələ əv ɑːts / (BrE)
a first university degree in subjects such as languages, history, or social sciences

Examples

A Bachelor of Arts degree
After completing his Bachelor of Arts, he decided to pursue a master's degree in psychology.
Many employers require at least a Bachelor of Arts degree for entry-level positions.
She received her Bachelor of Arts in English literature from a prestigious university.
