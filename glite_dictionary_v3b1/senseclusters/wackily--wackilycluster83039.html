wackily

adverb
in an eccentric or crazy way
Pronunciation: / ˈwækiˌli / (AmE), / ˈwækɪli / (BrE)
In a humorous, eccentric, or mildly crazy manner.

Examples

To wackily dance
He wackily danced through the grocery store aisles, making everyone laugh.
She decorated her room wackily, with polka-dot wallpaper and upside-down furniture.
The artist dressed wackily for the opening night, wearing mismatched shoes and a bright orange hat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eccentrically - in an unconventional or odd way
  • antonym: normally - in a typical or expected way
