Stevens' power law

noun
principle on perceived stimulus intensity
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàstiÀêv…ônz Ààpa ä.…ö l…î / (AmE), / ÀàstiÀêv…ônz Ààpa ä.…ô l…îÀê / (BrE)
