creamless

adjective
without cream
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːmləs / (AmE), / ˈkriːmləs / (BrE)
not containing or made with cream

Examples

Creamless coffee
She prefers her coffee creamless to avoid extra calories.
The restaurant offers a delicious creamless tomato soup for those with dairy allergies.
He ordered a creamless dessert to stick to his vegan diet.

Related Terms
