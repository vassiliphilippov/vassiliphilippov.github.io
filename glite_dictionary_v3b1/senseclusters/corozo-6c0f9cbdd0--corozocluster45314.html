Corozo

noun
genus of tropical American palm trees
Pronunciation: / kɔˈɾoʊ.zoʊ / (AmE), / kɒˈɾəʊ.zəʊ / (BrE)
A genus of tropical American palm trees belonging to the monocotyledonous family Arecaceae, known for their hard seeds and fibrous fruits.

Corozo palm tree
