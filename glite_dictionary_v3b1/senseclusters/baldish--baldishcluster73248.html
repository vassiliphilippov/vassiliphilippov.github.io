baldish

adjective
somewhat bare
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈbɔːl.dɪʃ/ /; / /ˈbɑːl.dɪʃ/ (AmE), /ˈbɔːl.dɪʃ/ (BrE)
somewhat bare or lacking covering, especially referring to land, surfaces, or areas with little vegetation or hair.

Examples
