pluff

noun
soft muffled sound
9.9
Pronunciation: / plʌf / (AmE), / plʌf / (BrE)
a soft, muffled sound, especially made when something light lands on a padded or soft surface

Examples

A gentle pluff sound
There was a gentle pluff as the kitten jumped onto the pile of blankets.
He tossed the book onto the bed, and it landed with a soft pluff.
The snowball hit the ground with a quiet pluff, barely making a sound.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plop - a soft sound made by something dropping into liquid or onto a soft surface
  • synonym: thud - a dull, heavy sound made by an object falling or hitting something
