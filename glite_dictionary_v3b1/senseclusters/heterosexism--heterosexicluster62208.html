heterosexism

noun
discrimination favoring heterosexuals
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛt̬əroʊˈsɛkˌsɪzəm /; / ˌhɛt̬əroʊˈsɛksɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌhɛtərəʊˈsɛksɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief that heterosexuality is the only normal or acceptable sexual orientation, often resulting in prejudice or discrimination against non-heterosexual people
