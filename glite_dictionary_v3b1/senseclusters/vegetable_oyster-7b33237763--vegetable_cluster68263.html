vegetable oyster

noun
herb with edible root tasting like oyster
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛdʒtəbl ˈɔɪstər /; / ˈvɛdʒtəbl ˈɔɪstɚ / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒtəbəl ˈɔɪstə / (BrE)
a plant (salsify) with an edible root that is said to taste like oysters
