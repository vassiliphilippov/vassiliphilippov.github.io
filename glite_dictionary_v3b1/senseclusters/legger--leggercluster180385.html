legger

noun
canal boat mover
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛɡər / (AmE), / ˈlɛɡə / (BrE)
a person who moves a canal boat through a tunnel by walking along the inside walls or roof of the tunnel

Examples

The legger guides boat
The legger guided the canal boat through the long, dark tunnel with steady steps.
In the past, a legger was essential for moving boats where horses could not go.
Tourists watched as the experienced legger pushed the narrowboat through the tunnel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boatman - a person who works on or operates a boat
