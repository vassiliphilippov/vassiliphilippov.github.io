areolar

adjective
relating to small spaces (areolae)
Pronunciation: / əˈriːələr / (AmE), / əˈrɪələr / (BrE)
relating to or resembling an areola; especially designating tissue composed of small spaces or divided into small areas.

Examples

Areolar tissue
In biology class, we studied the structure and function of areolar tissue.
Microscopes reveal the delicate, areolar pattern within certain connective tissues.
The doctor explained that areolar tissue plays an important role in supporting organs.
