erroneous belief

noun
belief based on incorrect information
8.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈroʊniəs bəˈliːf / (AmE), / ɪˈrəʊnɪəs bɪˈliːf / (BrE)
a belief based on incorrect or mistaken information

Examples

An erroneous belief persists
An erroneous belief can persist for generations if it is not challenged.
Despite her confidence, her argument was based on an erroneous belief about the data.
Many people hold the erroneous belief that vaccines cause the diseases they prevent.
