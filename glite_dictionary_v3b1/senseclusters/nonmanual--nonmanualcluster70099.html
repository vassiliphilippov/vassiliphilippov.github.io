nonmanual

adjective
not involving physical labor
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈmænjuəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈmænjuəl / (BrE)
not involving the use of hands or physical labor, especially in reference to work or tasks

Examples

Nonmanual office job
She was looking for a nonmanual position in the finance sector.
Most nonmanual jobs require computer literacy and communication skills.
The company offers both manual and nonmanual roles to its employees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clerical - relating to office work or desk jobs
  • antonym: manual - involving physical work or the use of hands
