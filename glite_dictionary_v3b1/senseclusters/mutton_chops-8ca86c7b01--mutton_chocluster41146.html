mutton chops

noun
thick sideburns
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌtən tʃɑps / (AmE), / ˈmʌtən tʃɒps / (BrE)
sideburns that are grown thick and long down the cheeks, resembling the shape of a cut of mutton

Examples

Thick mutton chops
The actor grew thick mutton chops to play a character from the 1800s.
His distinctive mutton chops made him stand out in the crowd.
In old photographs, many men wore mutton chops as a fashionable style.
