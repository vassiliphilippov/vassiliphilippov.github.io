conflicted

adjective
uncertain or torn
Pronunciation: / kənˈflɪktɪd / (AmE), / kənˈflɪktɪd / (BrE)
feeling uncertain or having opposing emotions about something

Examples

Feeling conflicted inside
She felt conflicted about accepting the new job offer far from home.
Many voters are conflicted when choosing between two strong candidates.
He was conflicted because he wanted to help but also needed to rest.
He felt conflicted about moving to a new city for work.
Many students are conflicted when choosing between two good universities.
She was conflicted about telling her friend the difficult truth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: certain - sure about something; having no doubt
  • antonym: resolved - having a clear decision or solution
  • synonym: torn - unable to choose between two options
  • synonym: undecided - not having made a decision
