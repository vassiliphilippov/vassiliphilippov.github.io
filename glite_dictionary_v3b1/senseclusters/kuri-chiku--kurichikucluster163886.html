kuri-chiku

noun
small bamboo with green culms turning black
Pronunciation: / kuːriːˈtʃiːkuː / (AmE), / kuːriːˈtʃiːkuː / (BrE)
A type of small bamboo with thin green stems that turn shiny black as they mature, commonly cultivated for its ornamental value.

Examples
