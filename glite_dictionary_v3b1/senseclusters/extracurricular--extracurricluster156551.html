extracurricular

noun
school activity outside class
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstrəkəˈrɪkjələr / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəkəˈrɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
an activity that takes place outside the regular school curriculum, such as sports, clubs, or volunteering

Examples

Extracurricular activities
Many students join extracurricular activities like drama club or soccer to explore their interests.
Colleges often look for applicants who participate in extracurricular programs outside of academics.
She balanced her studies with several extracurricular commitments, including volunteering and music lessons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: after-school activity - an organized activity students do after regular school hours
  • antonym: curricular - related to the regular school curriculum
