forebodingly

adverb
ominously
8.4
Pronunciation: / fɔrˈboʊdɪŋli / (AmE), / fɔːˈbəʊdɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that suggests something bad or unpleasant will happen

Examples

To speak forebodingly
The wind howled forebodingly as the storm approached the small village.
He glanced forebodingly at the darkening sky before heading inside.
The old house creaked forebodingly in the silence of the night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfully - in a happy or optimistic way
  • synonym: ominously - in a way that suggests something bad will happen
