bouton

noun
decorative button
9.9
Pronunciation: / buːˈtɒn / (AmE), / buːˈtɒn / (BrE)
a small button, especially one used as a fastener or decoration on clothing, from French

Examples

A shirt bouton
The tailor carefully sewed a gold bouton onto the front of the blazer.
She admired the delicate bouton that added elegance to her dress.
One bouton was missing from the cuff, so the shirt looked incomplete.

Related Terms

  • synonym: button - a small fastener used to join two pieces of fabric
