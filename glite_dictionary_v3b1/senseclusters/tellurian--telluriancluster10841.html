tellurian

noun
an inhabitant of the earth
Pronunciation: / təˈlʊriən / (AmE), / tɛˈljʊəriən / (BrE)
an inhabitant of the earth; a human being

Examples

A tellurian explorer
In old adventure novels, explorers might be described as bold tellurians braving the unknown.
Science fiction often refers to humans as tellurians when depicting life on other planets.
The alien ambassador greeted the assembled group of tellurians with guarded curiosity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: earthling - an inhabitant of the earth, especially as contrasted with a being from another planet
  • antonym: extraterrestrial - a being from outside the earth or its atmosphere
