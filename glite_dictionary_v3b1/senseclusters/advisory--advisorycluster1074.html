Synonyms

    7.1 1.61/m
    consultative describes a group that offers suggestions in meetings and lacks final say, while advisory means giving recommendations without decision power.
  • consultatory
    8.8 0.00/m
    consultatory describes services or processes meant to give guidance, while advisory describes people or bodies who provide recommendations.
  • consultive
    7.1 0.00/m
