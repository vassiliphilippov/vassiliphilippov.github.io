advisory

adjective
giving advice; providing recommendations
4.1 3.4
Pronunciation: /æd.ˈvaɪ.zə.rɪ/ /; / /əd.ˈvaɪ.zə.rɪ/ (AmE), /əd.ˈvaɪ.zə.rɪ/ /; / /æd.ˈvaɪ.zə.rɪ/ (BrE)
having the power or duty to give advice but not to make decisions; providing recommendations or suggestions.

Examples

Advisory board
An advisory committee was formed to review the proposed policy.
The panel serves in an advisory capacity to the board of directors.
We received an advisory report offering several courses of action.
