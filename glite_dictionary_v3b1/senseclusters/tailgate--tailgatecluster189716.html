tailgate

verb
drive too closely behind another vehicle
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪlˌɡeɪt / (AmE), / ˈteɪlɡeɪt / (BrE)
to drive too closely behind another vehicle, especially in a way that is dangerous

Examples

To tailgate a car
I always slow down when someone starts to tailgate me.
If you tailgate on the highway, you increase the risk of an accident.
The police issued a fine to the driver for tailgating another car.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rear-end - to crash into the back of another vehicle
