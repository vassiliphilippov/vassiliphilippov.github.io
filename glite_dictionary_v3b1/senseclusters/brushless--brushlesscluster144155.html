brushless

adjective
without brushes
Pronunciation: / ˈbrʌʃləs / (AmE), / ˈbrʌʃləs / (BrE)
operating without brushes or bristles, especially in reference to electric motors or devices

Examples

Brushless motor
The new fan uses a brushless motor, making it quieter and more energy-efficient.
Many modern power tools are now brushless for improved performance and durability.
A brushless design reduces maintenance because there are fewer parts that wear out.
