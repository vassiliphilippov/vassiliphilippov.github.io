psychoneurotic

adjective
affected by an emotional disorder
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkoʊnʊˈrɑːtɪk / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəʊnjuˈrɒtɪk / (BrE)
Affected by or relating to a psychoneurosis; suffering from an emotional disorder.

Examples

Psychoneurotic disorder
Doctors developed new therapies specifically for psychoneurotic disorders in the early twentieth century.
Her psychoneurotic symptoms made daily routines increasingly challenging.
The patient was diagnosed as psychoneurotic after displaying persistent anxiety and irrational fears.
