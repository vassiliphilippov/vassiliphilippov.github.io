naturale

noun
still water (menu)
Pronunciation: / na.tʃuˈrɑː.leɪ / (AmE), / na.tjuˈrɑː.leɪ / (BrE)
still (non-carbonated) water, as indicated on menus, especially using the Italian term.

Examples

Naturale water
When the waiter asked, she requested naturale instead of sparkling water with her meal.
The menu offered both naturale and frizzante, so he chose the still option.
In many Italian restaurants, you can order naturale if you prefer non-carbonated water.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sparkling water - water that is carbonated with bubbles
  • synonym: still water - water that is not carbonated
