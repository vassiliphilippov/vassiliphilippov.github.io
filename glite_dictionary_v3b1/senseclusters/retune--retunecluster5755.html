retune

verb
to adjust settings

to adjust or change the tuning of a musical instrument to improve its sound or accuracy

Examples

To retune a guitar
Before the performance, she carefully retuned her violin to ensure perfect sound.
The mechanic retuned the engine to improve the car's efficiency.
After changing the strings, you should always retune your guitar.
After the temperature changed, she needed to retune her violin before the concert.
The technician will retune the radio to improve the sound quality.
He quickly retuned his guitar between songs during the performance.

Synonyms

  • tune
    1.8 6.90/m

  • tune up
    3.4 0.04/m
    tune up is a common phrasal verb for preparing or checking an instrument before playing, while retune focuses on changing the tuning itself.
