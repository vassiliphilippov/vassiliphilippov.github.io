furthest

adverb
most distant (in space or time)
8.0 1.1
Pronunciation: /ˈfɜːr.ðəst/ /; / /ˈfɜːr.ðɪst/ /; / /ˈfɜːr.θəst/ (AmE), /ˈfɜː.ðəst/ /; / /ˈfɜː.ðɪst/ (BrE)
to the greatest distance in space or time; most distant (used especially as the superlative of 'far', sometimes interchangeably with 'farthest').

Examples

Swim the furthest
I wonder who can swim the furthest without stopping.
Of all the runners, he threw the ball the furthest.
She traveled the furthest of anyone in her family, reaching South America.
