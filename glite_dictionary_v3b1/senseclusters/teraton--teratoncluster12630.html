teraton

noun
unit for explosive energy equal to one trillion tons of TNT
6.6 9.0
Pronunciation: /ˈter.ə.tən/ (AmE), /ˈter.ə.tən/ (BrE)
a unit for measuring the explosive energy of a bomb or explosion, equal to one trillion (1,000,000,000,000) tons of TNT

Examples

One teraton bomb
A teraton is far beyond the destructive capacity of any human-made weapon.
Scientists use the term teraton to describe extremely large-scale explosions or impacts.
The Chicxulub impact released energy estimated at over 100 teratons of TNT equivalent.
