mass hysteria

noun
collective uncontrollable emotional reaction
5.8 4.9
Pronunciation: /mæs hɪ.ˈstɪr.iː.ə/ (AmE), /mɑːs hɪ.ˈstɪə.riː.ə/ (BrE)
A situation in which a large group of people simultaneously exhibit similar exaggerated or uncontrollable emotional reactions, often irrational or panicked.

Examples

Mass hysteria outbreak
During the broadcast, mass hysteria swept through listeners, many believing the story was real.
History records several instances of mass hysteria leading to irrational behavior in large communities.
The rumors of a food shortage sparked mass hysteria in the city.
