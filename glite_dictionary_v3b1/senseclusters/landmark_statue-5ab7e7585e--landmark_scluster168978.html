landmark statue

noun
notable or prominent statue in a location
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlændˌmɑrk ˈstætʃu / (AmE), / ˈlan(d)ˌmɑːk ˈstatjuː / (BrE)
a statue that is notably significant or easily recognized in a particular location, often used as a reference point

Examples

The landmark statue
Photographers often use the landmark statue as a backdrop for their portraits.
The city’s most famous landmark statue attracts thousands of visitors each year.
We agreed to meet by the landmark statue in the central square.
