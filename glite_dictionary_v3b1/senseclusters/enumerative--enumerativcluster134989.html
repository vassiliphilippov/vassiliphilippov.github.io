enumerative

adjective
relating to listing
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnuːmərətɪv / (AmE), / ɪˈnjuːmərətɪv / (BrE)
relating to the act of listing or counting things one by one

Examples

The enumerative method
The enumerative method requires you to list every possible combination.
Her report took an enumerative approach, detailing each item separately.
In mathematics, an enumerative analysis counts all possible outcomes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: descriptive - giving details about something
  • synonym: list - to mention items one after another
