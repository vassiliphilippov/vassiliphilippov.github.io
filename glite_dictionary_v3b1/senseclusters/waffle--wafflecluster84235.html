waffle

noun
cake cooked in a waffle iron
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːfəl / (AmE), / ˈwɒfəl / (BrE)
A flat cake with a grid pattern, made from leavened batter or dough cooked in a waffle iron.

Examples

A waffle with syrup
I ordered a Belgian waffle drizzled with maple syrup at the café.
She served homemade waffles with fresh strawberries and whipped cream for breakfast.
The smell of freshly baked waffle filled the kitchen every Sunday morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pancake - flat cake cooked on a griddle or frying pan
