waxer

noun
applies wax
Pronunciation: / ˈwæksər / (AmE), / ˈwæksə / (BrE)
a person or device that applies wax to a surface, especially for cleaning or hair removal

Examples

Salon waxer
The waxer at the salon made sure my legs were smooth and hair-free.
He used an electric waxer to polish the wooden floor until it shone.
Many car owners prefer to use a professional waxer for a perfect finish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: polisher - a person or device that makes something smooth and shiny
