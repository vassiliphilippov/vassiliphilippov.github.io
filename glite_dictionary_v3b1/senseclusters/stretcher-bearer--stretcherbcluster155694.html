stretcher-bearer

noun
person who carries a stretcher
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɛtʃər ˌbɛrər / (AmE), / ˈstrɛtʃə ˌbeərə / (BrE)
a person whose duty is to carry a stretcher, especially to transport the sick or injured

Examples

She worked as a stretcher-bearer with the Red Cross during the war.
The stretcher-bearer carefully navigated through the crowded hospital hallway.
