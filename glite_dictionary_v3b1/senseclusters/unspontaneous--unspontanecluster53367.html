unspontaneous

adjective
not natural or planned
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnspɑnˈteɪniəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnspɒnˈteɪniəs / (BrE)
not done naturally or without planning; lacking spontaneity or naturalness

Examples

Unspontaneous laughter
Her laughter sounded unspontaneous, as if she was trying too hard to fit in.
The actor's performance was criticized for being unspontaneous and lacking emotion.
Their conversation felt unspontaneous, with long pauses and awkward silences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rehearsed - prepared or practiced in advance
  • antonym: spontaneous - done naturally, without planning or effort
