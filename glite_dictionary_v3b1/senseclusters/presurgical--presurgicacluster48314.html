presurgical

adjective
before surgery
4.7
Pronunciation: / priːˈsɜrdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / priːˈsɜːdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
happening or done before a surgical operation

Examples

Presurgical tests
The doctor ordered several presurgical tests to check the patient's health.
A presurgical consultation helps patients understand the procedure and ask questions.
Proper presurgical preparation can reduce the risk of complications during the operation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postsurgical - happening or done after a surgical operation
  • synonym: preoperative - relating to the period before a surgical operation
