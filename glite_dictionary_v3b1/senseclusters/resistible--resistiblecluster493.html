resistible

adjective
able to be resisted
7.2
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɪstəbəl / (AmE), / rɪˈzɪstɪbəl / (BrE)
Able to be resisted, withstood, or overcome.

Examples

A resistible charm
Her charms were entirely resistible to those focused on their work.
Many found the offer easily resistible and chose not to accept it.
The fortifications made the city’s defenses resistible to the invading army.

Related Terms

  • antonym: irresistible - impossible to resist; too attractive or appealing to be resisted
