ten-cent piece

noun
coin worth ten cents (U.S.)
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛn sɛnt pis / (AmE), / ˌtɛn sɛnt piːs / (BrE)
A coin worth ten cents, especially in the United States; a dime.

Examples

A shiny ten-cent piece
He pulled a ten-cent piece from his pocket to pay for the newspaper.
She found a shiny ten-cent piece on the sidewalk.
The vending machine accepted only ten-cent pieces and quarters.

Related Terms
