settleable

adjective
suitable for inhabiting
6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈset.əl.ə.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈset.əl.ə.bəl/ (BrE)
suitable or available for people to move to and live in; able to be colonized or inhabited

Examples

Settleable planet
The scientists searched for settleable planets that could support human life.
Many regions were not considered settleable due to harsh climates and lack of resources.
Early settlers prioritized finding settleable land with access to water and fertile soil.
