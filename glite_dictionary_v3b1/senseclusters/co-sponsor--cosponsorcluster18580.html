co-sponsor

noun
joint supporter
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊˌspɑːnsər / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌspɒnsə / (BrE)
a person or organization that jointly provides support, funding, or organization for an event, project, or activity with others

Examples

Event co-sponsor
The charity event had several co-sponsors who helped cover the costs.
As a co-sponsor, the company received recognition in all promotional materials.
Each co-sponsor contributed resources to make the conference a success.

Related Terms

  • synonym: partner - a person or group that works together with others for a common purpose
  • synonym: sponsor - a person or organization that provides support or funding for something
