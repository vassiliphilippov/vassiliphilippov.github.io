parallel

verb
to match or be similar to
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛrəˌlɛl /; / ˈpærəˌlɛl / (AmE), / ˈpærəˌlɛl /; / ˈparəlɛl /; / ˈpærəlɛl / (BrE)
to be similar to or correspond closely with something else; to match or be analogous to

Examples

Her career path paralleled her brother's in many surprising ways.
The two fences parallel each other across the entire field.
