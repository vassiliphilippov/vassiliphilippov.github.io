cheeseball

noun
round cheese snack
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːzˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːzbɔːl / (BrE)
a small round food made from cheese, either a molded cheese mixture served as an appetizer or a crunchy cheese-flavored snack

Examples

A cheeseball snack
She served a homemade cheeseball with crackers at the holiday party.
Kids love to snack on crunchy cheeseballs during movie night.
The cheeseball was coated in chopped nuts and herbs for extra flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheese puff - a light, crunchy snack made from cheese-flavored cornmeal
  • synonym: cheese spread - a soft, spreadable cheese mixture often served as an appetizer
