pollenizer

noun
agent of pollination
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑləˌnaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈpɒləˌnaɪzə / (BrE)
an animal, insect, person, or machine that transfers pollen from one plant to another to enable pollination

Examples

Bee pollenizer
A bumblebee can act as a pollenizer for many types of fruit trees.
Some orchards use machines as pollenizers to increase crop yields.
The presence of a good pollenizer is essential for successful apple production.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pollinator - an organism that carries pollen to fertilize plants
