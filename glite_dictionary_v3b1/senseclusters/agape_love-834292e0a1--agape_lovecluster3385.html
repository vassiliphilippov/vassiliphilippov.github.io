agape love

noun
spiritual or selfless love
6.2
Pronunciation: / əˈɡɑːpeɪ lʌv / (AmE), / əˈɡɑːpeɪ lʌv / (BrE)
selfless, unconditional love for others, especially of a spiritual or altruistic nature, without sexual implications

Examples
