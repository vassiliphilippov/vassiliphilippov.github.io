breast cancer

noun
cancer of the breast
Pronunciation: / brɛst ˈkænsɚ / (AmE), / brɛst ˈkɑːnsə / (BrE)
A malignant tumor that develops from cells in the breast, commonly affecting women.

Examples

Breast cancer treatment
Advancements in treatment have improved outcomes for breast cancer patients.
Early detection increases the chances of surviving breast cancer.
She decided to raise money for breast cancer research after her aunt was diagnosed.
