warmer

noun
device for heating or warming
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrmər / (AmE), / ˈwɔːmə / (BrE)
a device used to heat water or to keep a room or objects warm

Examples

A warmer for milk
She placed the bottle in the warmer so the baby could have warm milk.
The restaurant uses a plate warmer to keep dishes hot before serving.
We bought a new space warmer for the living room.
