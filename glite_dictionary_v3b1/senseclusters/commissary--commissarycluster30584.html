commissary

noun
military base store for personnel
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑː.məˌseri / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.ɪ.sə.ri / (BrE)
a store on a military base that sells food and other supplies to military personnel

Examples

Military commissary
He stopped by the commissary after work to pick up groceries for the week.
Military personnel often find better prices at the commissary than at stores off base.
The new commissary on base offers fresh produce and household items to military families.

Related Terms

  • synonym: PX - post exchange; a store on a military base
  • degree higher: supermarket - a large self-service grocery store
