surrey

noun
pedal-powered sightseeing vehicle
6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈsɜːr.iː/ /; / /ˈsʌr.ɪ/ (AmE), /ˈsʌr.ɪ/ /; / /ˈsɜːr.iː/ (BrE)
a modern four-wheeled pedal-powered recreational vehicle, often seating two or more people and modeled after the traditional surrey carriage, used for sightseeing or group leisure rides.

Examples

A surrey bike ride
We rented a surrey bike along the boardwalk for a sunset tour.
Children laughed as they pedaled the bright red surrey past the seaside carousel.
The rental company stores several four-seat surrey vehicles in a covered shed during winter.
