dural

adjective
relating to the dura mater (membrane of the brain/spine)
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdʊrəl, ˈdjʊrəl / (AmE), / ˈdjʊərəl / (BrE)
relating to the dura mater, the outermost membrane covering the brain and spinal cord

Examples

Dural hematoma
A dural hematoma can compress the brain and requires urgent treatment.
He suffered from dural irritation following the spinal tap.
The surgeon noticed a small dural tear during the procedure.
