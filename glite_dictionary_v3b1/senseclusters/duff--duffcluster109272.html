duff

noun
steamed or boiled fruit pudding
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʌf / (AmE), / dʌf / (BrE)
a steamed or boiled pudding made with stiff flour and usually containing dried fruit such as currants, raisins, or citron

Examples

Plum duff dessert
After a hearty meal, the guests enjoyed slices of plum duff with their tea.
She served a traditional duff with custard for dessert on Christmas Day.
The recipe for raisin duff has been handed down for generations in my family.
