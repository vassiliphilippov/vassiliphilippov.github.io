warmly

adverb
in a warm manner
1.0 4.07/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…îrmli / (AmE), / Ààw…îÀêmli / (BrE)
in a warm, friendly, or enthusiastic way

Examples

To warmly welcome
She welcomed us warmly into her home.
The children were warmly dressed for the snowy day.
The house was warmly lit and felt very inviting.
He was warmly welcomed by his old friends after years abroad.
She smiled warmly at everyone who entered the room.
The host thanked the guests warmly for attending the event.

Synonyms

  • cordially
    4.0 0.70/m
    cordially is used in formal greetings and polite letters to show respectful friendliness, while warmly is more casual and homey.
  • affably
    4.4 0.20/m
    affably is friendly and easy to talk with in casual chat or meetings, while warmly adds stronger feeling and closeness.
  • amiably
    3.7 0.69/m
    amiably is polite and pleasant in small talk or smiles, while warmly shows deeper personal warmth or enthusiasm.
  • genially
    3.7 0.20/m
    genially is warm and cheerful in friendly talk or welcome, while warmly can be gentler and less overtly joyful.
  • welcomingly
    3.6 0.01/m
    welcomingly is used to invite and make people feel accepted at a place or event, while warmly is more general.
  • heartily
    3.4 2.24/m
    heartily expresses strong energy or full agreement in action or praise, while warmly focuses on kind, friendly feeling.
