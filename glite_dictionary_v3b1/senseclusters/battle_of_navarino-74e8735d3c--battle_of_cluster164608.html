Battle of Navarino

proper noun
decisive 1827 naval battle in Greek War of Independence
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt̬.əl əv nəˈvɑː.rɪ.noʊ / (AmE), / ˈbæt.əl əv nəˈvɑː.rɪ.nəʊ / (BrE)
the decisive naval battle fought in 1827 during the War of Greek Independence, in which the Turkish and Egyptian fleet was destroyed by an allied British, French, and Russian fleet

Examples

The Battle of Navarino 1827
British, French, and Russian forces cooperated at the Battle of Navarino to defeat the Ottoman-Egyptian fleet.
The Battle of Navarino marked a turning point in the Greek War of Independence.
The destruction of the enemy navy at the Battle of Navarino shocked Europe.
