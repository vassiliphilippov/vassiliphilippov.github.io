Battle of Navarino

proper noun
decisive 1827 naval battle in Greek War of Independence
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt̬.əl əv nəˈvɑː.rɪ.noʊ / (AmE), / ˈbæt.əl əv nəˈvɑː.rɪ.nəʊ / (BrE)
the decisive naval battle fought in 1827 during the War of Greek Independence, in which the Turkish and Egyptian fleet was destroyed by an allied British, French, and Russian fleet
