magneto

noun
device producing spark in engines
7.6
Pronunciation: / mægˈniːtoʊ / (AmE), / mæɡˈniːtəʊ / (BrE)
a small generator that uses magnets to produce a high voltage for sparking in engines, especially in ignition systems of internal combustion engines

Examples

A magneto generator
A faulty magneto can cause an engine to misfire or not run at all.
Early airplanes often relied on a magneto for ignition.
The vintage car's magneto needed repairing before it would start.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dynamo - a machine that converts mechanical energy into electrical energy
