excessively

adverb
to an extreme or undue degree
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsɛsɪvli / (AmE), / ɪkˈsɛsɪvli / (BrE)
to a degree that is more than is necessary, reasonable, or acceptable; too much

Examples

To exercise excessively at gym
He exercises excessively, often spending hours at the gym each day.
She worried excessively about every minor detail.
The meat was excessively salty, making it hard to eat.

Related Terms

  • antonym: moderately - to a reasonable degree
  • synonym: overly - to an excessive degree; too much
