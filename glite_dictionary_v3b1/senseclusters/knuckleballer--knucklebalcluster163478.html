knuckleballer

noun
unpredictable person
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌkəlˌbɔlər / (AmE), / ˈnʌkəlˌbɔːlə / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is highly unpredictable or behaves in unexpected ways

Examples

Office knuckleballer
No one knew what the knuckleballer would do next at the meeting.
Her reputation as a knuckleballer made her both exciting and difficult to work with.
The team's new manager is a real knuckleballer, always surprising everyone with his decisions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: steady - a person who is reliable and predictable
  • synonym: wild card - a person or thing whose behavior is unpredictable
