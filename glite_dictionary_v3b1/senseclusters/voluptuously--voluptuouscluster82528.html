voluptuously

adverb
in a sensually pleasing or shapely manner
8.0
Pronunciation: / vəˈlʌptʃuəsli / (AmE), / vəˈlʌptjʊəsli / (BrE)
in a way that is full of sensual pleasure or luxury; in a sensually appealing or indulgent manner

Examples

To voluptuously enjoy luxury
He inhaled the fragrance voluptuously, savoring every note of the rich perfume.
She moved voluptuously across the stage, captivating the audience with her grace.
The woman was described as voluptuously curved in the magazine feature.
Chocolate melted voluptuously in his mouth, indulging his taste buds with rich sweetness.
He inhaled the fragrance voluptuously, savoring every note of the luxurious perfume.
She reclined voluptuously on the velvet sofa, enjoying the soft fabric against her skin.

Related Terms

  • antonym: austerely - in a plain or severe manner, without luxury or sensuality
  • synonym: sensually - in a way that is related to the senses or physical pleasure
  • synonym: sensuously - in a way that relates to or affects the senses rather than the intellect
