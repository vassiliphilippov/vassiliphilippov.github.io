voluptuously

adverb
in a sensually pleasing or shapely manner
8.0 6.6
Pronunciation: /və.ˈlʌp.tʃuː.əs.liː/ /; / /və.ˈlʌp.tʃʊ.əs.liː/ (AmE), /və.ˈlʌp.tʃʊ.əs.liː/ /; / /və.ˈlʌp.tʃuː.əs.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that is full of sensual pleasure or luxury; in a sensually appealing or indulgent manner
