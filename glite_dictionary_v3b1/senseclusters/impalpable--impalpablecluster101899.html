impalpable

adjective
unable to be felt by touch
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpælpəbəl /; / ɪmˈpæl.pə.bəl / (AmE), / ɪmˈpalpəbl /; / ɪmˈpælpəbəl / (BrE)
unable to be felt, touched, or easily perceived; intangible or not noticeable by the senses
