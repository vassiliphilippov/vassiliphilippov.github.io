bogan

adjective
unsophisticated style
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbəʊɡən / (BrE)
having the style, tastes, or behavior considered unsophisticated or uncultured, especially in a way associated with working-class Australians

Examples

Bogan style clothes
He was criticized for his bogan taste in music and clothes.
The movie features a group of bogan characters from the suburbs.
She decorated her room in a deliberately bogan style for the party.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sophisticated - having refined tastes and manners
  • synonym: uncultured - lacking refinement or good taste
