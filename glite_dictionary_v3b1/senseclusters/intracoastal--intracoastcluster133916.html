intracoastal

adjective
within coastal areas
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈkoʊstəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈkəʊstəl / (BrE)
located within or along the coast, especially describing waterways that run parallel to the shoreline

Examples

Intracoastal waterway
The boat traveled along the intracoastal waterway to avoid the rough ocean.
Many homes are built near intracoastal channels for easy access to boating.
Wildlife thrives in the intracoastal regions between the mainland and the barrier islands.
The intracoastal waterway provides a safe route for boats traveling along the eastern United States.
Many homes are built along the intracoastal canal to offer easy access to boating.
Wildlife thrives in the marshes near the intracoastal areas of the Gulf Coast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coastal - located on or near a coast
  • antonym: inland - situated away from the coast
