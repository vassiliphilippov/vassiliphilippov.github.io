exceptionally

adverb
to an unusual or remarkable degree
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsɛpʃən(ə)li / (AmE), / ɪkˈsɛpʃənəli / (BrE)
To an unusually high or remarkable degree; outstandingly.

Examples

Performed exceptionally well
She performed exceptionally well on the final exam, earning the top score in the class.
The service at the restaurant was exceptionally prompt and friendly.
This year's harvest has been exceptionally abundant due to the favorable weather.
