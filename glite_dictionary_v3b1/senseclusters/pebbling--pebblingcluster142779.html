pebbling

noun
covering with pebbles
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛblɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɛblɪŋ / (BrE)
the action or process of covering a surface or area with small stones or pebbles, either intentionally or by natural means

Examples

Pebbling a path
The pebbling of the driveway improved its appearance and made it easier to walk on.
After heavy rain, natural pebbling occurred along the riverbank.
They spent the afternoon pebbling the garden path to create a decorative walkway.
