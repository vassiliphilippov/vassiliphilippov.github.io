overpowering

adjective
extremely strong and irresistible
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈpaʊɚɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈpaʊərɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a feeling, force, or quality) extremely strong and difficult or impossible to resist or withstand.

Examples

An overpowering feeling
He felt an overpowering urge to confess the truth to his friend.
Her overpowering grief was evident to everyone at the funeral.
The smell of flowers in the room was so overpowering that it made her dizzy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overwhelming - very great in amount; difficult to resist or manage
