mooring anchor

noun
anchor for securing buoys or markers
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmʊrɪŋ ˌæŋkɚ / (AmE), / ˈmʊərɪŋ ˌæŋkə / (BrE)
An anchor specially designed to secure a mooring buoy or a channel marker in place in the water.

Examples

A mooring anchor buoy
Divers checked the condition of the old mooring anchor after the storm.
Each channel marker is attached to the riverbed by a dedicated mooring anchor.
The buoy was held steady by a heavy mooring anchor beneath the water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mooring - A place or device for securing a vessel
