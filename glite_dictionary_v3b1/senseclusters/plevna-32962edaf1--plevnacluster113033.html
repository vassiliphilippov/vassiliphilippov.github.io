Plevna

proper noun
town in Bulgaria, site of famous 1877 siege
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈplɛvnə / (AmE), / ˈplɛvnə / (BrE)
A town in northern Bulgaria, known for the 1877 siege during the Russo-Turkish War when it was taken from the Turks by Russian forces.
