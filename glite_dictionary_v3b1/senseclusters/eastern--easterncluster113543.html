eastern

adjective
coming from the east (esp. wind)
Pronunciation: / ˈiːstərn / (AmE), / ˈiːstən / (BrE)
coming from the east, especially describing winds or directions.

Examples

Eastern wind
A strong eastern wind blew across the coastline yesterday.
The city experiences frequent eastern breezes during the spring months.
We had to secure the sails because of the sudden eastern gusts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: easterly - (of wind) coming from the east
  • antonym: westerly - (of wind) coming from the west
