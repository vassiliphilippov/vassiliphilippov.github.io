hinge

verb
to depend on
4.3
Pronunciation: /hɪndʒ/ (AmE), /hɪndʒ/ (BrE)
to depend entirely on or be determined by something, often used with 'on' or 'upon'.

Examples

Plans hinge on approval
Their plans hinged on whether the council would approve the permit by June.
The success of the project hinges on everyone working together as a team.
Her decision hinged upon the results of the medical test.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depend - to be determined or controlled by something else
  • antonym: ignore - to pay no attention to or not be affected by something
  • synonym: rest - to be based on or supported by something
