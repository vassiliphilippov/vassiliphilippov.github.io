sappy

adjective
overly sentimental or emotional
Pronunciation: / ˈsæpi / (AmE), / ˈsæpi / (BrE)
excessively sentimental or emotional, especially in a way that seems insincere or silly

Examples

Sappy love story
Critics complained that the commercial's message was a bit too sappy for their taste.
She wrote a sappy letter filled with clichés about true love and destiny.
The movie was so sappy that I nearly rolled my eyes at every romantic scene.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maudlin - tearfully or weakly emotional; foolishly sentimental
  • antonym: realistic - representing things in a practical and lifelike way
  • synonym: sentimental - showing or causing exaggerated emotion or nostalgia
