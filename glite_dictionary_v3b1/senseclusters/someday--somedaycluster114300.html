Synonyms

  • one day
    1.1 61/m
    one day suggests a single future occasion that will happen at some time, while someday sounds more vague and general.
  • sometime
    2.2 25/m
    sometime means at an unspecified time that can be past or future, while someday usually refers to a future time.
  • down the line
    4.4 3.81/m
    down the line refers to something happening at a later point in time, while someday means an unspecified future time.
  • down the road
    3.7 9.49/m
    down the road is a casual way to say something will happen eventually, while someday is neutral and simply says it will occur later.
