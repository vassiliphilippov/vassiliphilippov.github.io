azimuthal

adjective
relating to azimuth or angular direction
Pronunciation: / ˌæz.əˈmʌθ.əl / (AmE), / ˌæz.ɪˈmʌθ.əl / (BrE)
relating to or measured in terms of azimuth, especially the angular measurement in a spherical coordinate system.

Examples

The azimuthal angle
Astronomers adjusted their telescope along the azimuthal axis to observe the comet.
Map projections often use azimuthal layouts to represent polar regions.
The satellite's position was determined using azimuthal coordinates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: azimuth - the angular measurement in a spherical coordinate system
