seasonal worker

noun
person employed only during certain seasons
3.2 0.15/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàsiÀêz…ôn…ôl Ààw…úrk…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàsiÀêz…ôn…ôl Ààw…úÀêk…ô / (BrE)
a person who is employed only during certain times of the year, typically when extra labor is needed for seasonal work such as agriculture or tourism

Examples

A seasonal worker hired
During the harvest, the farm hires extra seasonal workers to help pick apples.
Many seasonal workers travel between states to follow the agricultural seasons.
The resort relies on seasonal workers during the busy winter months.
