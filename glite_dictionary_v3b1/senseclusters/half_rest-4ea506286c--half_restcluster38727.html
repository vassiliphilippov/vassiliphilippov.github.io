half rest

noun
a two-beat musical rest
6.6
Pronunciation: / hæf rɛst / (AmE), / hɑːf rɛst / (BrE)
a musical rest sign indicating a silence lasting half the duration of a whole rest, typically equal to two beats in common time

Examples

A half rest sign
During the half rest, the entire brass section remains silent.
Please count two beats of silence for every half rest you see.
The composer indicated a half rest in the second measure.
