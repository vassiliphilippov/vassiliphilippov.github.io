pretexting

noun
deceptive information gathering
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈpriː.tekst.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈpriː.tekst.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of pretending to be someone else or inventing a false reason in order to get information or hide your true purpose
