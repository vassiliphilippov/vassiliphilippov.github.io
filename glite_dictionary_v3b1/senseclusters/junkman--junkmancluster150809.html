junkman

noun
dealer in scrap
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌŋkmæn / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋkmæn / (BrE)
a person whose job is to collect, buy, sell, or remove old or unwanted items, especially for recycling, resale, or disposal

Examples

Local junkman visits
The junkman stopped by every week to see if we had anything to sell.
After cleaning out the garage, we called a junkman to haul away the old appliances.
As a child, he was fascinated by the treasures the junkman found in discarded items.
The junkman arrived early to clear out the old furniture from the garage.
After the renovation, they hired a junkman to dispose of the construction debris.
If you have unwanted items, a junkman can remove them quickly for a reasonable price.

Related Terms

  • synonym: garbage collector - a person whose job is to collect household waste
  • synonym: rag-and-bone man - a person who collects unwanted household items for resale or recycling
  • synonym: scrap dealer - a person or business that buys and sells scrap metal and other materials
