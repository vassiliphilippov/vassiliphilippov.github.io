Pocahontas

proper noun
Disney animated film
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊkəˈhɑːntəs / (AmE), / ˌpɒkəˈhɒntəs / (BrE)
a fictionalized Native American woman based on the historical figure, especially as portrayed in Disney's animated films, or the 1995 Disney animated film itself
