uncentered

adjective
not emotionally balanced
7.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɛntərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛntəd / (BrE)
not mentally or emotionally balanced or grounded; feeling unsettled or disoriented

Examples

Feeling uncentered
After the argument she felt uncentered, unable to focus on work or relax at home.
He became uncentered during the stressful week and struggled to make decisions.
Meditation can help when you are feeling uncentered and anxious.

Related Terms

  • antonym: centered - mentally and emotionally stable and calm
  • antonym: grounded - emotionally stable and sensible
  • synonym: unbalanced - not emotionally or mentally stable
