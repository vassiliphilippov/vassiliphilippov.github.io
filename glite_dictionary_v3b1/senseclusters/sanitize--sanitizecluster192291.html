Examples

Synonyms

  • hygienize
    7.5 0.00/m
    hygienize means to make something sanitary, often in formal writing, while sanitize is common everyday and practical cleaning.
  • disinfect
    3.7 0.94/m
    disinfect cleans with chemicals to kill germs, while sanitize makes something generally clean and reduces harmful bacteria.
  • sterilize
    4.1 1.60/m
    sterilize uses heat or strong methods to remove all microorganisms, while sanitize lowers germs to a safer, not always sterile, level.
  • degerm
    7.4 0.01/m
    degerm removes microbes from skin or surfaces by cleaning, while sanitize aims to make items broadly clean and safer from bacteria.
  • antisepticize
    9.5 0.01/m
    antisepticize treats wounds or surfaces with antiseptic liquid to prevent infection, while sanitize covers general cleaning to reduce germs.
  • decontaminate
    7.0 0.37/m
    decontaminate removes dangerous chemicals, radioactivity, or pollutants to make places safe, while sanitize focuses mainly on killing microbes.
