fosterling

noun
child raised by foster parents
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːstərlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɒstəlɪŋ / (BrE)
A child who is brought up by foster parents rather than their biological parents.

Examples

A fosterling child
As a fosterling, she experienced a different way of life than with her birth family.
The family welcomed the fosterling into their home with open arms.
The medieval lord raised a fosterling as his own son.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foster child - a child raised by someone other than their natural or adoptive parents
