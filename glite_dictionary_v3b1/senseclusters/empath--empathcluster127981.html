empath

noun
person sensing emotions
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɛmpæθ / (AmE), / ˈɛmpæθ / (BrE)
a person who can strongly feel or sense the emotions of others

Examples

An empath feels emotions
The main character in the story is an empath who feels the pain of everyone around her.
Some people believe that being an empath makes it difficult to be in crowded places.
As an empath, he often knows how others feel without them saying a word.
As an empath, she often feels overwhelmed in crowded places.
Many empaths find it challenging to separate their own feelings from those of others.
He realized he was an empath when he could sense his friend's sadness without a word being spoken.

Related Terms

  • synonym: empathic - showing the ability to understand others' feelings
  • antonym: insensitive - not noticing or caring about others' feelings
  • synonym: psychic - a person claiming to have paranormal mental abilities
  • synonym: sensitive - easily affected by the feelings or emotions of others
  • synonym: sympathizer - someone who shares or understands the feelings of others
  • antonym: unfeeling - not showing emotion or sympathy for others
