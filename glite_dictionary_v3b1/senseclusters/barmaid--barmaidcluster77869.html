barmaid

noun
female bartender
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrˌmeɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɑːmeɪd / (BrE)

Examples

The barmaid serves drinks

The barmaid greeted customers with a friendly smile as they entered the pub.
