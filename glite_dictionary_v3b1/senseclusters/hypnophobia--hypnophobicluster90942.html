hypnophobia

noun
morbid fear of falling asleep
6.3
Pronunciation: / hɪp.nəˈfoʊ.bi.ə / (AmE), / hɪp.nəˈfəʊ.bi.ə / (BrE)
An abnormal or morbid fear of falling asleep.

Examples

Fear of hypnophobia
Her hypnophobia prevented her from getting a full night's rest for months.
Living with hypnophobia can be very challenging, as it disrupts both sleep and daily life.
The patient was diagnosed with hypnophobia after experiencing intense anxiety at bedtime.
