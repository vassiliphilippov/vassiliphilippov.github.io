Andalusian

adjective
of or relating to Andalusia (Spain)
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌændəˈluːʒən / (AmE), / ˌandəˈluːziən / (BrE)
Relating to Andalusia, a region in southern Spain, or its people, language, or culture.

Examples

Andalusian culture
Many Andalusian traditions, such as flamenco, are celebrated around the world.
She studied Andalusian history to better understand Spain's rich past.
The Andalusian landscape is famous for its beautiful olive groves and white villages.
