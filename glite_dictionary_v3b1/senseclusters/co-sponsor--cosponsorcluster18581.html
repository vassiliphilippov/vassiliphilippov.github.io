co-sponsor

verb
to jointly support
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊˈspɑːnsər / (AmE), / ˌkəʊˈspɒnsə / (BrE)

Examples

Local businesses often co-sponsor community events to increase their visibility and support good causes.
