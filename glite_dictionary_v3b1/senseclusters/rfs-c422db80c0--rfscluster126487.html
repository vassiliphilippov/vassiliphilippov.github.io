RFS

proper noun
Australian fire service
7.9
Pronunciation: / ɑr ɛf ɛs / (AmE), / ɑː ɛf ɛs / (BrE)
the Rural Fire Service, an Australian volunteer firefighting organization, especially in New South Wales, responsible for bushfire response and emergency support

Examples

RFS firefighters
The RFS issued a warning about dangerous fire conditions in the region.
Hundreds of RFS volunteers worked through the night to protect local communities.
After the bushfire, the RFS provided support and information to affected residents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: CFA - Country Fire Authority, a volunteer firefighting service in Victoria, Australia
  • synonym: fire brigade - an organized group of firefighters
