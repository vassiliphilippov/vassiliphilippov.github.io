synchronicity

noun
meaningful coincidence
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪŋkrəˈnɪsəti / (AmE), / ˌsɪŋkrəˈnɪsəti / (BrE)
the occurrence of meaningful coincidences that seem related but have no apparent causal connection

Examples

Strange synchronicity
Many people believe that synchronicity can guide them toward important life decisions.
The synchronicity of their thoughts made the conversation feel almost magical.
He noticed a strange synchronicity when he kept seeing the same number everywhere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coincidence - a remarkable occurrence of events at the same time by chance
