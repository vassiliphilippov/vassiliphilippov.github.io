spore

verb
to release spores
Pronunciation: / spɔr / (AmE), / spɔː / (BrE)
to produce or release spores, as certain plants, fungi, or bacteria do for reproduction or survival

Examples

Fungi spore after rain
After heavy rain, many mushrooms spore across the damp forest floor.
Some ferns spore in the spring, releasing tiny particles into the air.
Scientists observed how the mold would spore rapidly under warm, humid conditions.
