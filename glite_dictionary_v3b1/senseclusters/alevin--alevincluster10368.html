alevin

noun
newly hatched fish
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈæləvɪn / (AmE), / ˈæləvɪn / (BrE)
a very young fish, especially a newly hatched salmon or trout, that still has its yolk sac attached

Each alevin is vulnerable to predators until it grows larger and stronger.
