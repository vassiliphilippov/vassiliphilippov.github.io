qwerty syndrome

noun
habit of typing errors from QWERTY keyboard use
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɜrti ˈsɪnˌdroʊm / (AmE), / ˈkwɜːti ˈsɪndrəʊm / (BrE)
the tendency to make frequent typing errors or habitual mistakes due to familiarity with the QWERTY keyboard layout

Examples

Qwerty syndrome typing errors
His qwerty syndrome caused him to repeatedly misspell common words at work.
Many new typists suffer from qwerty syndrome until they become familiar with the layout.
She blamed her frequent typos on qwerty syndrome after switching keyboards.
