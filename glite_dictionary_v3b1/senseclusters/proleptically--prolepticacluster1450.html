proleptically

adverb
in anticipation
9.9
Pronunciation: /prə.ˈlep.tɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /proʊ.ˈlep.tɪ.kəl.iː/ (AmE), /prə.ˈlep.tɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /prəʊ.ˈlep.tɪ.kəl.iː/ (BrE)
in a way that anticipates or refers to something before it actually happens, especially as a rhetorical or literary device
