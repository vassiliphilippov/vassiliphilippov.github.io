proleptically

adverb
in anticipation
9.9
Pronunciation: / proʊˈlɛptɪkli / (AmE), / prəˈlɛptɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that anticipates or refers to something before it actually happens, especially as a rhetorical or literary device
