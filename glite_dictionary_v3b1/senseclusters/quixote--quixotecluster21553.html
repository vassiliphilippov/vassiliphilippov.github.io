quixote

noun
impractical idealist
Pronunciation: / kiˈhoʊti / (AmE), / kwɪkˈsəʊt / (BrE)
a person who is extremely idealistic and impractical, often pursuing unrealistic goals

Examples

A quixote dreamer
Despite the odds, he remained a quixote in his fight for social justice.
Some called her a quixote for believing she could end world hunger alone.
The young activist was seen as a modern quixote chasing impossible dreams.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dreamer - a person who has ideas or plans that are not practical
  • antonym: realist - a person who accepts and deals with things as they really are
