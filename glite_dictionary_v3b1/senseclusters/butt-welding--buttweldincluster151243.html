butt-welding

noun
welding metal ends together
Pronunciation: / bʌtˈwɛldɪŋ / (AmE), / bʌtˈwɛldɪŋ / (BrE)
The process of joining two pieces of metal end to end in a single plane by welding.

Examples

Butt-welding metal pieces
Advanced techniques in butt-welding have improved pipeline construction efficiency.
Proper alignment is crucial for high-quality butt-welding results in structural steelwork.
The pipes were joined using butt-welding to ensure a strong, leak-proof connection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: butt joint - a joint formed by joining two pieces end to end
