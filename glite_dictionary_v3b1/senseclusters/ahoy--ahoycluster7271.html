ahoy

interjection
nautical greeting
8.3
Pronunciation: / əˈhɔɪ / (AmE), / əˈhɔɪ / (BrE)
used as a call or greeting to attract attention, especially by sailors at sea

Examples

Ahoy there!
The captain called out ahoy to the passing fishing boat.
With a cheerful ahoy, the sailor greeted his friends on the dock.
As the fog lifted, a distant voice shouted ahoy from another vessel.

Related Terms

  • antonym: goodbye - a word used to express farewell
  • synonym: hello - a common greeting or way to attract attention
