uncolonized

adjective
not settled by colonizers
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈkɑːləˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈkɒləˌnaɪzd / (BrE)
not settled, controlled, or occupied by a foreign power or colonizers

Examples

Uncolonized land
The remote valley remained uncolonized well into the twentieth century.
Many indigenous cultures thrived in uncolonized regions before outside contact.
Scientists studied the unique wildlife found only on uncolonized islands.

Related Terms

  • antonym: colonized - settled or controlled by a foreign power
  • synonym: independent - not governed by another country or outside authority
