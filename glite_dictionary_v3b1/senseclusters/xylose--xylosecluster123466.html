xylose

noun
a sugar from wood or straw
Pronunciation: / ˈzaɪˌloʊs / (AmE), / ˈzaɪləʊs / (BrE)
a sugar obtained from wood or straw, used especially as a sweetener for diabetics and in chemical research

xylose sugar
