against

preposition
protection or resistance
Pronunciation: / əˈɡɛnst / (AmE), / əˈɡɛnst / (BrE)
used to show protection from, resistance to, or preparation for something harmful or unwanted

Examples

Protect against danger
We took extra blankets to guard the children against the cold during the night.
Vaccines help protect people against certain diseases.
He wore a helmet against possible head injuries while cycling.

Related Terms

  • antonym: for - in support of or in favor of
  • synonym: versus - in opposition to; against
