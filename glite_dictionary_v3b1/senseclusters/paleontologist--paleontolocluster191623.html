paleontologist

noun
scientist who studies fossils
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪliˌɑnˈtɑlədʒɪst /; / ˌpeɪ.li.ɑnˈtɑl.ə.dʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌpælɪɒnˈtɒlədʒɪst /; / ˌpæ.li.ɒnˈtɒl.ə.dʒɪst / (BrE)
A scientist who studies fossils and ancient life forms through the science of paleontology.

Examples

A paleontologist studies fossils
As a paleontologist, she has discovered several new dinosaur species.
Many children dream of becoming a paleontologist after visiting natural history museums.
The paleontologist carefully examined the fossilized skeleton for signs of disease.
As a palaeontologist, Margaret has traveled to fossil beds all over the world.
Many children dream of becoming a palaeontologist after learning about dinosaurs.
The palaeontologist carefully examined the dinosaur bones at the dig site.

Related Terms

  • synonym: archaeologist - a scientist who studies human history through artifacts and remains
  • synonym: paleontologist - scientist who studies fossils (American spelling)
  • synonym: paleontology - the scientific study of fossils and ancient life forms
