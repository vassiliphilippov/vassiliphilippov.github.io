profitably

adverb
in a way that yields profit or benefit
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːfɪtəbli / (AmE), / ˈprɒfɪtəbli / (BrE)
in a way that produces profit or advantage; so as to yield financial or other benefits

Examples

Run profitably
