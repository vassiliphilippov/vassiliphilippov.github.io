dateless

adjective
lacking a date (letter, document, etc.)
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪtləs / (AmE), / ˈdeɪtləs / (BrE)
(of a letter, document, etc.) not bearing a date; without a written or printed date.

Examples

Dateless letter
She kept a dateless note in her memory box, unsure when it was written.
The contract was rendered void because it was dateless and unsigned.
The museum displayed a dateless manuscript believed to be centuries old.
