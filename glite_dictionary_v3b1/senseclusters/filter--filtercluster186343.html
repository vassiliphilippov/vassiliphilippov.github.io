filter

noun
digital image or video effect
3.2
Pronunciation: / c8f6al.t5a / (AmE), / c8f6al.t59 / (BrE)
A digital image or video effect applied (usually in software or an app) to change the appearance, color balance, or mood of a photo or clip.

Examples

A photo filter
She applied a filter to her photo to give it a vintage vibe.
Before posting the clip, he tested each filter to find the best mood for the scene.
The documentary used a subtle filter to warm the colors and suggest older footage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: image effect - a visual change applied to a picture or video to alter its appearance
  • synonym: preset - a saved set of visual adjustments or settings used to quickly change the look of a photo or video
