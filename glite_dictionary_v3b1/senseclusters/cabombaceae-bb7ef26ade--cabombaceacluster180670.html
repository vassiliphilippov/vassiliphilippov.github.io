Cabombaceae

noun
family of aquatic flowering plants

Pronunciation: / kəˌbɒmˈbeɪsiː /; / kəˌbɑːmˈbeɪsiˌiː / (AmE), / kəˌbɒmˈbeɪsiː / (BrE)
