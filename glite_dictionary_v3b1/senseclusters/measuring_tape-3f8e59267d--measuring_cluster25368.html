measuring tape

noun
digital measurement app or tool
3.5 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / \u02c8m\u025b\u0292\u0259r\u026a\u014b te\u026ap / (AmE), / \u02c8m\u025b\u0292(\u0259)r\u026a\u014b te\u026ap / (BrE)
A digital or software tool (often a smartphone app) that estimates distances or dimensions using a camera, sensors, or augmented reality rather than a physical tape.

Examples

Measuring tape app
She used the measuring tape app to estimate the room's length before ordering a sofa.
Before buying the bookshelf, he opened the measuring tape on his phone to check the space.
I couldn't tell the height, so I used the measuring tape feature on my smartphone to measure it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: measurement app - a software application that uses device sensors or camera input to estimate size or distance
  • synonym: tape measure - a flexible ruler used to measure length or distance; often used interchangeably with 'measuring tape' for physical tools
