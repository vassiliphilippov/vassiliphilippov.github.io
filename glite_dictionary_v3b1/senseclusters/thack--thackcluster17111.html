thack

verb
to thatch a roof
Pronunciation: / θæk / (AmE), / θæk / (BrE)
to cover a roof with thatch or similar material for protection against the weather

Examples

To thack a roof
The villagers gathered reeds to thack the roofs of their cottages before the rainy season.
It took several days for the workers to thack the barn with fresh straw.
In some rural areas, people still thack houses using traditional methods.
