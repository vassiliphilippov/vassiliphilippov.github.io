isosyllabism

noun
use of lines with equal syllables in verse
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪsoʊˈsɪləbɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌaɪsəʊˈsɪləbɪzəm / (BrE)
The practice or quality of using verse lines with the same number of syllables.

Examples
