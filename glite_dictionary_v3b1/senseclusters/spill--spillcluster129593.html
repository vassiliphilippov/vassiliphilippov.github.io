Synonyms

    4.9 0.16/m
    slop is pouring or knocking food around in a messy way, while spill means accidentally causing a liquid to flow from a container.
  • run over
    3.2 0.34/m
    run over means liquid flows over a container's brim and pours out, while spill is any accidental loss of liquid from a container.
  • overspill
    4.9 0.01/m
    overspill is when liquid flows over the edge because there is too much, while spill can mean any accidental pouring out.
  • run out
    1.3 1.22/m
    run out means liquid flows out and the container becomes empty, while spill stresses an accidental or careless loss of liquid from a container.
