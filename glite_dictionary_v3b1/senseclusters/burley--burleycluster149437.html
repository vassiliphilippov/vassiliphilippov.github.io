burley

noun
fish-attracting bait
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜːrli / (AmE), / ˈbɜːli / (BrE)
bait made of chopped fish, fish parts, or other attractants, scattered in the water to attract fish

Examples

Fish burley bait
The fishermen tossed burley into the water to attract a school of snapper.
Before casting their lines, they prepared a bucket of burley to increase their chances of catching fish.
Using burley is a common technique in saltwater fishing to draw larger fish closer to the boat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chum - bait of fish parts or blood used to attract fish
  • synonym: lure - an object used to attract fish so they can be caught
