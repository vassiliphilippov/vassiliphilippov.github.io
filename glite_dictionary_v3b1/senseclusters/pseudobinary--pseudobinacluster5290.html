pseudobinary

adjective
like a binary mixture
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːdoʊˈbaɪnɛri / (AmE), / ˌsjuːdəʊˈbaɪnəri / (BrE)
describing a system that behaves like a binary mixture but actually contains more than two components

Examples

A pseudobinary system
The researchers treated the complex mixture as a pseudobinary system to simplify their calculations.
In materials science, a pseudobinary phase diagram can help analyze alloys with more than two elements.
Although the solution had three components, it was considered pseudobinary for the purpose of the experiment.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: ternary - involving or consisting of three parts or components
