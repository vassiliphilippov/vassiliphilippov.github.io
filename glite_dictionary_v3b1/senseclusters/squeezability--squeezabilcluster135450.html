squeezability

noun
quality of being easily compressed
5.1 5.8
Pronunciation: /skwiːz.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /skwiːz.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
