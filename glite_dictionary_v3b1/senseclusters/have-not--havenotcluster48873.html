have-not

noun
a poor person; someone lacking wealth or possessions
Pronunciation: / ˌhævˈnɑt / (AmE), / ˌhævˈnɒt / (BrE)
a person who is poor or lacks wealth, especially in contrast to those who are well-off

Examples

The have-nots in society
Many believe that more should be done to support the have-nots in our society.
She grew up among the have-nots of the city, struggling to make ends meet.
The economic divide between the have-nots and the wealthy has widened in recent years.

Related Terms

  • antonym: have - a person who possesses wealth or advantages
  • synonym: pauper - a very poor person
