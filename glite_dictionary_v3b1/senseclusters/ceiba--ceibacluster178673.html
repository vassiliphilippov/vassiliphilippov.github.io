ceiba

noun
tropical American tree with showy flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪbə / (AmE), / ˈsiːbə / (BrE)
A tropical American tree, typically with palmately compound leaves and large, showy, bell-shaped flowers, belonging to the genus Ceiba.

Examples

The ceiba tree
Many animals find shelter in the branches of the ceiba during the rainy season.
The massive ceiba tree in the village square is over two hundred years old.
The pink flowers of the ceiba attract a wide variety of butterflies and birds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kapok - a tropical tree producing silky fibers, related to ceiba
