Examples

Synonyms

  • lop off
    4.6 0.52/m

  • cut off
    4.1 6.13/m
    cut off means to remove something by cutting it away from its attachment, while chop off suggests a sudden heavy strike.
  • sever
    5.6 4.96/m
    sever is a formal word for cutting something completely apart, while chop off is more everyday and suggests a strong chopping blow.
