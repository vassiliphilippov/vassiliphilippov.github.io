bicycle clip

noun
clip for cyclists' ankles
8.0 2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪsɪkəl klɪp / (AmE), / ˈbaɪsɪkl̩ klɪp / (BrE)
a metal or plastic clip worn around a cyclist's ankle to prevent trousers from getting caught in the bicycle chain
