electrothermal

adjective
electric and heat processes
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˈθɜrməl / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈθɜːməl / (BrE)
relating to the interaction between electricity and heat, especially the production of heat by electricity
