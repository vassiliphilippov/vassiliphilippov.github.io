respirator

noun
protective mask for filtering harmful substances
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛspəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈrɛspɪreɪtə / (BrE)
a protective device worn over the nose and mouth or entire face to filter harmful substances or gases from inhaled air

Examples

Wear a respirator mask
During the wildfire, she put on a respirator to avoid inhaling smoke.
Proper fit is essential for a respirator to provide effective protection.
The workers wore respirators to protect themselves from toxic fumes in the factory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mask - a covering worn over the face for protection or disguise
