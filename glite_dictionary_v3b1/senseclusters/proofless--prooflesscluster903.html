proofless

adjective
without evidence
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpruːfləs / (AmE), / ˈpruːfləs / (BrE)

Examples

Proofless theory
The theory remained proofless despite years of research.

A proofless accusation can damage someone's reputation unfairly.
