demolishing

noun
destruction or tearing down of a structure
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmɑlɪʃɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈmɒlɪʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of completely destroying or tearing down a structure, especially a building

Examples

The demolishing of buildings
Experts oversaw the safe demolishing of the historic theater.
Noise complaints increased during the demolishing of the abandoned warehouse.
The demolishing of the old factory took several weeks to complete.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demolition - the act of demolishing, especially by explosives or heavy machinery
