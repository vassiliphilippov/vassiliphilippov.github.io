Synonyms

  • razing
    7.8 0.1/m
    razing is the complete destruction of a building down to the ground, while demolishing means tearing a structure down.
  • tearing down
    4.2 0.6/m
    tearing down is a common, hands-on way to remove a building piece by piece, while demolishing sounds more general.
  • wrecking
    4.4 1/m
    wrecking is rough destruction that often damages parts of a building, while demolishing is the full act of taking it down.
  • leveling
    5.6 0.06/m
    leveling is destroying a building so it lies flat on the ground, while demolishing does not always mean flattening.
  • demolition
    4.2 5/m
    demolition is the formal noun for the planned act of destroying a building, while demolishing is the verbal form describing that action.
