groupwise

adjective, adverb
by groups
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡruːpˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɡruːpˌwaɪz / (BrE)
relating to or done by groups rather than individually or as a whole

Examples

In a groupwise way
The data was analyzed in a groupwise manner to identify trends among different teams.
Teachers organized the students groupwise for the science project presentations.
The survey results were presented groupwise to compare responses from each department.
The survey results were presented groupwise to highlight differences between departments.
Students were assigned projects groupwise rather than working alone.
The analysis was conducted groupwise to compare the performance of each team.
