puddly

adjective
full of puddles
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌdli / (AmE), / ˈpʌdli / (BrE)
full of puddles or having the appearance or characteristics of a puddle

Examples

A puddly playground
After the rain, the playground became quite puddly.
The puddly street made it difficult for children to ride their bikes.
Her shoes were soaked from walking across the puddly field.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dry - not wet or containing no moisture
  • synonym: muddy - covered in or full of mud
