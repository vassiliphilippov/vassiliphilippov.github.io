overdrawn

adjective
exaggerated or excessive
5.7
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ˈdrɔːn/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.ˈdrɔːn/ (BrE)
describing speech, writing, or description that is exaggerated, overly emotional, or excessively detailed.

Examples

Overdrawn story
