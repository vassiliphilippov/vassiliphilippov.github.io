Battle of Naseby

proper noun
1645 Civil War battle in England
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt̬l əv ˈneɪz.bi / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv ˈneɪz.bi / (BrE)
a decisive battle in 1645 during the first English Civil War, in which the Parliamentarians defeated the Royalists

Examples

The Battle of Naseby 1645
After the Battle of Naseby, Royalist forces never fully recovered.
Many historians have studied the tactics used at the Battle of Naseby.
The Battle of Naseby marked a turning point in the English Civil War.
