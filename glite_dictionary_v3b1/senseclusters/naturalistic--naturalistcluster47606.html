naturalistic

adjective
realistic, lifelike; not idealized
Pronunciation: / ˌnætʃəˈrəlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌnatʃəˈrɪlɪstɪk / (BrE)
representing subjects as they appear in nature, without idealization or abstraction; realistic

Examples

A naturalistic style
Critics admired the naturalistic approach of the novel, which closely mirrored everyday life.
The artist was praised for her naturalistic depiction of the forest landscape.
The movie's naturalistic dialogue made the characters seem authentic and believable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: realistic - showing things as they really are; not artificial or idealized
