hammering

noun
repeated heavy blows
Pronunciation: / ˈhæmərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhamərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of delivering repeated heavy blows, especially with force or intensity

Examples

The hammering of rain
Fans could hear the hammering of rain against the stadium roof.
The boxer received a severe hammering in the final round.
The constant hammering from upstairs made it hard to concentrate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pounding - the act of striking repeatedly with force
