rose periwinkle

noun
tropical plant with pink or red flowers
7.3
Pronunciation: / roʊz ˈperɪˌwɪŋkəl / (AmE), / rəʊz ˈperɪˌwɪŋkl̩ / (BrE)
A tropical, evergreen plant with woody stems and large pink or red flowers, commonly grown as an ornamental.
