millwright

noun
worker who builds or maintains mills
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɪl.raɪt / (BrE)
a skilled worker who designs, builds, installs, or maintains mills and mill machinery

Examples

A millwright repairs machines
After years as a millwright, he became an expert at repairing complex machinery.
She apprenticed as a millwright before starting her own business.
The old mill was restored by a team led by a skilled millwright.

Related Terms

  • synonym: engineer - a person who designs, builds, or maintains engines, machines, or structures
