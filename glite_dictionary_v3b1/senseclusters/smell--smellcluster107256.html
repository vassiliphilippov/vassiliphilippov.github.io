smell

verb
emit an odor
1.0
Pronunciation: / smɛl / (AmE), / smɛl / (BrE)
to give off or have a particular odor (good or bad)

Examples

Fish smells bad
If fish smells bad, it is probably not safe to eat.
The freshly baked bread smells delicious every morning.
The garden smells wonderful after the rain.
After hours of exercise, his shirt began to smell terribly.
If you don't clean the fridge soon, the leftovers will smell even worse.
You need to take the trash out; it really smells in here.
