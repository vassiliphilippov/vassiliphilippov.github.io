Newfoundlander

proper noun
person from Newfoundland
Pronunciation: / ˌnuːfaʊnˈlændər / (AmE), / ˌnjuːfaʊnˈlændə / (BrE)
a person who is from or lives in Newfoundland, a province of Canada

Examples

A Newfoundlander friend
The Newfoundlander welcomed us warmly when we visited St. John's.
As a proud Newfoundlander, she often talks about the island's unique culture.
Many Newfoundlanders have a strong connection to the sea and fishing traditions.
