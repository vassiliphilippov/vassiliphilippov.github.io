scooch

verb
to slide a little
Pronunciation: / skuːtʃ / (AmE), / skuːtʃ / (BrE)
to move a short distance by sliding or shifting, especially while seated

Examples

To scooch over
Could you scooch over a bit so I can fit on the bench?
She had to scooch closer to the table to reach her drink.
The children scooched together to make room for their friend.

  • synonym: slide - to move smoothly along a surface
