factionalism

noun
loyalty to a faction
Pronunciation: / ˈfækʃənəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈfækʃənəlɪzəm / (BrE)
the situation where a group is divided into smaller groups that compete or disagree with each other, often causing conflict
