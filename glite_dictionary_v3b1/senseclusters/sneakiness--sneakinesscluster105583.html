sneakiness

noun
the quality of being sneaky or deceptive
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsniːkinəs / (AmE), / ˈsniːkinɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being sly, stealthy, or secretly deceptive

Examples

Secret sneakiness
He was often criticized for his sneakiness in avoiding his share of the group work.
Her sneakiness allowed her to overhear the entire conversation without anyone noticing.
The sneakiness of the plan surprised even the most suspicious members of the team.

  • synonym: slyness - the quality of being cunning or crafty
