hot pot

noun
stew of meat and potatoes
Pronunciation: / hɑt pɑt / (AmE), / hɒt pɒt / (BrE)
A stew consisting of meat and potatoes cooked slowly in a tightly covered pot.

Examples

A hot pot stew
After a long walk, nothing was better than coming home to a warm hot pot for dinner.
She learned to make a traditional hot pot from her grandmother's recipe.
The family enjoyed a hearty hot pot on the cold winter evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stew - a dish of meat and vegetables cooked slowly in liquid
