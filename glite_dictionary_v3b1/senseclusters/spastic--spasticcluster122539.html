spastic

adjective
affected by muscular spasms
Pronunciation: / ˈspæstɪk / (AmE), / ˈspastɪk /; / ˈspæstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by muscle spasms, stiffness, or involuntary movements, especially due to a medical or neurological condition

Examples

The doctor specializes in treating spastic children with cerebral palsy.
