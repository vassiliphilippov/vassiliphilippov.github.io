Cancer

noun
astrological sign of the zodiac (June 21–July 22)
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkæn.sɚ / (AmE), / ˈkæn.sə / (BrE)
the fourth astrological sign of the zodiac, covering those born between about June 21 and July 22; represented by the crab.

Examples

Cancer zodiac sign
According to the horoscope, Cancer will have a lucky month.
My astrological sign is Cancer because I was born in late June.
People born under Cancer are said to be emotional and intuitive.
