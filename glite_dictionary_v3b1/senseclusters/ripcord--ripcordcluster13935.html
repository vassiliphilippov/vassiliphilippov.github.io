ripcord

noun
cord for releasing a parachute
7.0 5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈrɪp.kɔːrd/ /; / /ˈrɪp.kɑːrd/ (AmE), /ˈrɪp.kɔːd/ (BrE)
a cord that you pull to release a parachute or to let gas out of a balloon, usually to help someone or something descend safely
