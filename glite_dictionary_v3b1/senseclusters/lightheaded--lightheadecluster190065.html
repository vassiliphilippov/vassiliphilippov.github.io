lightheaded

adjective
not serious; frivolous or silly
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
Not serious or sensible; inclined to act in a frivolous or silly way.

Examples

Lightheaded behavior at work
She can be a bit lightheaded when it comes to important decisions.
The movie is perfect for anyone in a lightheaded mood who just wants to laugh.
Their lightheaded behavior embarrassed their more mature friends.

Related Terms

  • antonym: serious - acting or speaking earnestly, without frivolity
  • synonym: silly - lacking in seriousness or good sense; frivolous or foolish
