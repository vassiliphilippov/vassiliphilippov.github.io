sempervirens

adjective
evergreen (botanical)
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmpərˈvaɪrɛnz / (AmE), / ˌsɛmpəˈvɪərɛnz / (BrE)
remaining green and retaining leaves throughout the year; evergreen, especially in botanical contexts

Examples

Sempervirens plant
The Latin name sempervirens is often used for plants that keep their leaves all year.
Boxwood, known as Buxus sempervirens, is a popular evergreen shrub in gardens.
Many species labeled sempervirens are valued for their year-round foliage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deciduous - shedding leaves annually
  • synonym: evergreen - remaining green and keeping leaves all year
