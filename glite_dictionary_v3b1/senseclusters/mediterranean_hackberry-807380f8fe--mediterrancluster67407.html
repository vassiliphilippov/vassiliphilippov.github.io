Mediterranean hackberry

noun
deciduous shade tree of southern Europe
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪniən ˈhækbɛri / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪniən ˈhakbəri / (BrE)
A bright green deciduous shade tree native to southern Europe, known for its dense foliage.
