register

noun
air vent for admitting/excluding heated air
6.4 5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈre.dʒɪ.stər/ /; / /ˈre.dʒə.stər/ (AmE), /ˈre.dʒɪ.stə/ /; / /ˈre.dʒə.stə/ (BrE)
an opening or device in a building for controlling the flow of air (such as for heating or cooling)

Examples

An air register
Please avoid blocking the register with furniture, as it restricts the air circulation.
She cleaned the dust from the register to ensure the heating system worked efficiently.
The contractor installed a new register in the living room to improve airflow.
If you hear rattling, the register may be loose and need to be tightened.
Please make sure the register in the living room is fully open for maximum heat.
The old house had ornate metal registers on every floor vent.
