at arm's length

adverb
at a distance; emotionally or socially distant
6.3 3.9
Pronunciation: /ət ɑːrmz leŋθ/ /; / /ət ɑːrmz leŋkθ/ /; / /æt ɑːrmz leŋθ/ (AmE), /ət ɑːmz leŋθ/ /; / /ət ɑːmz leŋkθ/ /; / /æt ɑːmz leŋθ/ (BrE)
at a distance from someone or something to avoid becoming too close or involved

Examples
