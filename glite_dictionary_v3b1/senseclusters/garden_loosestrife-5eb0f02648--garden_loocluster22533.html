a tall, yellow-flowered perennial plant (Lysimachia vulgaris) native to Europe and Asia, often considered a weed in gardens and wetlands

Examples

Tall garden loosestrife
Garden loosestrife quickly spreads and can take over wetland habitats if not managed.
Although garden loosestrife is attractive, it is often seen as invasive outside its native range.
Many gardeners mistake garden loosestrife for other types of loosestrife due to its similar yellow flowers.
