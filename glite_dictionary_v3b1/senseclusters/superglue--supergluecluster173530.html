superglue

verb
to glue with strong adhesive
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌɡluː / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌɡluː / (BrE)
to fasten or repair something by applying superglue, a very strong adhesive.

Examples

To superglue pieces
I had to superglue the broken vase to keep it together.
She decided to superglue the loose sole back onto her shoe.
Be careful not to superglue your fingers when fixing the model airplane.
