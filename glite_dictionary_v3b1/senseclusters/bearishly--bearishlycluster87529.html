bearishly

adverb
roughly or clumsily
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛrɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈbɛə.rɪʃ.li / (BrE)
in a rough, clumsy, or surly manner, resembling the behavior or movement of a bear

Examples

Walk bearishly
He pushed his way bearishly through the crowd, barely noticing the people around him.
The actor moved bearishly across the stage, adding humor to the scene.
She bearishly dropped her bags on the floor, startling everyone in the quiet room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awkwardly - in a manner that is not smooth or graceful
  • synonym: clumsily - in a way that lacks grace or skill
  • antonym: gracefully - in a smooth and elegant manner
