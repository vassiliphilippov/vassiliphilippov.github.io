fuzzing

noun
formation of fabric fibers
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfʌzɪŋ / (BrE)
the process by which small fibers or a soft, hairy surface form on fabric, especially after wear or washing

Examples

Fabric fuzzing after washing
After several washes, the sweater's surface showed noticeable fuzzing around the cuffs and neck.
The old blanket developed fuzzing that made it feel softer but look worn.
Frequent use of the towel led to fuzzing along its edges.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pilling - the formation of small balls of fiber on fabric
  • antonym: smoothness - the quality of having an even, untextured surface
