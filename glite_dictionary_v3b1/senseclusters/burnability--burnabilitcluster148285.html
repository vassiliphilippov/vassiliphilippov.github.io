burnability

noun
ability to ignite and burn
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌbɜrnəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌbɜːnəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)

Examples

The burnability of materials
Knowing the burnability of building materials is vital for fire prevention.
Researchers are comparing the burnability of different types of wood for efficient fuel use.
The burnability of the fabric is tested to ensure it meets safety standards.
