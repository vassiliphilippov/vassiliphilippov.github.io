articulator

noun
a person who pronounces words
4.2
Pronunciation: / ɑɹˈtɪkjəˌleɪtɚ / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪkjʊˌleɪtə / (BrE)
a person who pronounces words clearly and distinctly; someone who articulates speech sounds

Examples

A clear articulator
Good radio hosts must be clear articulators to reach a broad audience.
She is a skilled articulator who enunciates every syllable clearly.
The speech therapist praised his progress as an articulator of difficult sounds.
