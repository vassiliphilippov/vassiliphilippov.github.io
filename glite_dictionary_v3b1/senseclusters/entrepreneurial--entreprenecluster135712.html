entrepreneurial

adjective
having qualities of an entrepreneur
5.8 5.8
Pronunciation: /ɑːn.trə.prə.ˈnɜːr.iː.əl/ (AmE), /ɒn.trə.prə.ˈnɜː.rɪ.əl/ (BrE)
having the qualities, mindset, or characteristics of an entrepreneur, especially being willing to take risks and start new business ventures
