affirmative

adjective
expressing approval or agreement
Pronunciation: / əˈfɜrmətɪv /; / əˈfɝː.mə.tɪv / (AmE), / əˈfɜːmətɪv /; / əˈfɜː.mə.tɪv / (BrE)
expressing agreement, approval, or a positive response; indicating 'yes'

Examples

Affirmative answer
She gave an affirmative answer, smiling as she nodded her head.
The committee's report was highly affirmative of the new policy.
The teacher responded with an affirmative gesture to show her approval.
In the vote, all but two members cast affirmative ballots.
She gave an affirmative answer to the proposal.
The committee issued an affirmative decision on the application.

Related Terms

  • synonym: approving - showing approval
  • antonym: negative - expressing denial or refusal
