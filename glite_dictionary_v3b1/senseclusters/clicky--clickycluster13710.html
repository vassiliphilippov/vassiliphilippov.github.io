clicky

adjective
designed to attract clicks
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪki / (AmE), / ˈklɪki / (BrE)
informal; designed to attract online clicks or attention, often by being sensational or catchy

Examples

Clicky headlines
The website uses clicky headlines to draw more visitors to its articles.
She prefers writing informative content rather than making it overly clicky just for attention.
That advertisement is so clicky that it almost feels misleading.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clickbait - content created to attract attention and encourage clicks
  • antonym: dull - not interesting or exciting
  • synonym: engaging - attracting and holding interest or attention
