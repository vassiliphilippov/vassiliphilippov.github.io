sawali

noun
woven bamboo matting
Pronunciation: / sɑːˈwɑːli / (AmE), / sɑːˈwɑːli / (BrE)
a type of woven bamboo matting used for walls or partitions, especially in traditional Filipino houses

Examples

Sawali bamboo matting
The walls of the cottage were made from sawali to allow air to flow through.
Traditional Filipino homes often use sawali for their interior partitions.
They repaired the old hut by replacing the damaged sawali panels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bamboo - a fast-growing plant used for building and crafts
  • synonym: matting - material made by weaving plant fibers together
