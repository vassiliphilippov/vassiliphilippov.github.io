credits

noun
film's list of contributors
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛd.ɪts / (AmE), / ˈkrɛd.ɪts / (BrE)
a list of people who have contributed to the creation of a film, typically shown at the end of the film

Examples

Film credits list
Her name appeared in the credits as one of the screenwriters.
Many stayed after the movie to watch the credits and see who worked on the soundtrack.
The credits rolled as the audience applauded the film.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acknowledgements - recognitions or thanks given to contributors, especially in publications
