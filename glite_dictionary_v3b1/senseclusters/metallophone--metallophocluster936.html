metallophone

noun
metal-bar instrument
8.4
Pronunciation: / məˈtæləˌfoʊn / (AmE), / mɛˈtaləfəʊn / (BrE)
a percussion instrument consisting of metal bars of different pitches that are struck to produce musical notes

Examples

A metallophone instrument
The composer included a metallophone in the orchestra to add a bright, ringing sound.
Children enjoyed playing the metallophone during their music class at school.
A skilled musician can create beautiful melodies on a metallophone by striking the bars gently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: xylophone - a percussion instrument with wooden bars struck to produce sound
