infected

adjective
containing disease-causing organisms
2.2
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɛktɪd / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɛktɪd / (BrE)
containing, affected by, or resulting from disease-causing organisms such as bacteria or viruses.

Examples

Infected wound
Doctors treated the infected tissue to prevent the spread of disease.
If food is stored improperly, it can become infected with harmful bacteria.
The wound became infected after it was exposed to dirty water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: septic - containing or resulting from disease-causing organisms
  • antonym: sterile - free from living germs or microorganisms
