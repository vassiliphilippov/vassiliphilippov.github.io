unshored

adjective
not at shore
9.9
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈʃɔːrd/ /; / /ʌn.ˈʃoʊrd/ (AmE), /ən.ˈʃɔːd/ /; / /ʌn.ˈʃɔːd/ (BrE)
not brought to or lying at the shore; remaining afloat or away from land, especially at sea.

Examples
