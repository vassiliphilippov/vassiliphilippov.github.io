swelled head

noun
exaggerated self-importance
Pronunciation: / swɛld hɛd / (AmE), / swɛld hɛd / (BrE)
an excessively high opinion of one's own importance or abilities; conceit

Examples

A swelled head
Don't let success give you a swelled head; remember to stay humble.
Ever since he won the award, he's had a swelled head and acts superior to everyone.
Her swelled head made it difficult for her to accept constructive criticism.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ego - a person's sense of self-esteem or self-importance
  • antonym: humility - the quality of having a modest view of one's importance
