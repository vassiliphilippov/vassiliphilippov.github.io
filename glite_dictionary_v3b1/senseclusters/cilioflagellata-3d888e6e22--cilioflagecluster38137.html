Cilioflagellata

noun
group of protist microorganisms
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪlioʊflædʒəˈleɪtə / (AmE), / ˌsɪlɪəʊflædʒɪˈleɪtə / (BrE)
a group of single-celled aquatic organisms, sometimes classified as a phylum within the kingdom Protista, or included in Pyrrophyta in some classifications

Examples

Cilioflagellata aquatic group
Certain texts include Cilioflagellata in the plant-related phylum Pyrrophyta.
Members of Cilioflagellata are distinguished by the presence of both cilia and flagella.
Scientists have debated whether Cilioflagellata should be classified as a separate phylum within Protista.
