jet

adjective
intensely black in color
Pronunciation: / dʒɛt / (AmE), / dʒɛt / (BrE)
Of the deepest, blackest black; resembling jet or coal in color.

Examples

Jet black hair
Her hair was a jet black that caught the sunlight in a striking way.
The artist used a jet hue for the night sky, making the stars stand out dramatically.
The cat's fur was so jet it almost seemed to shimmer in the darkness.
