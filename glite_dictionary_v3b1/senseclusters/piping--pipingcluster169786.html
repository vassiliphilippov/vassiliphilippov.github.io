piping

noun
strip of fabric cord for edging seams
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpaɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
a narrow, corded strip of fabric sewn into the seams of clothing or upholstery as an edging or decoration

Examples

Decorative piping on clothes
Adding piping can help highlight the shape and structure of a garment.
She carefully stitched piping into the cushion's seams for a professional look.
The dress was finished with a delicate piping along the collar and sleeves.
