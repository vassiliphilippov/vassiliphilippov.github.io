accentless

adjective
without accent
Pronunciation: / ˈæksɛntləs / (AmE), / ˈæksɛntləs / (BrE)
having no noticeable accent in speech or lacking diacritical marks in writing

Examples

Accentless English
Her accentless English surprised everyone in the room.
The document was typed in an accentless font for easier reading.
He spoke in such an accentless way that no one could guess where he was from.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accented - having a noticeable accent in speech or marked with diacritical signs
  • synonym: neutral - not showing any particular accent or style
