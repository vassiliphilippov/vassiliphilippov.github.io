business environmental analysis

noun
assessment of business environmental factors
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪznəs ɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntl əˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / ˈbɪznəs ɪnˌvaɪərənˈmɛntl əˈnalɪsɪs / (BrE)
the systematic process of identifying and assessing environmental factors affecting a business and developing strategies to respond to them

Examples

Regular business environmental analysis
A regular business environmental analysis can help organizations adapt to changes in regulations and technology.
Before entering a new market, the company conducted a thorough business environmental analysis to assess potential risks and opportunities.
The CEO emphasized the importance of business environmental analysis during the strategic planning meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: SWOT analysis - analysis of strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats facing a business
  • synonym: environmental scanning - surveying and interpreting relevant internal and external factors affecting a business
