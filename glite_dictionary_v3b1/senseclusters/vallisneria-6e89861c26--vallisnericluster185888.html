Vallisneria

proper noun
aquatic plant genus
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌvælɪsˈnɪəriə / (AmE), / ˌvælɪsˈnɪəriə / (BrE)
a genus of freshwater aquatic plants with long, ribbon-like leaves, often used in aquariums and known as eelgrass or tape grass

Examples

Vallisneria genus of plants
Vallisneria is a popular choice for aquariums because it provides shelter for fish.
The long leaves of Vallisneria can grow quickly in nutrient-rich freshwater tanks.
Many aquatic gardeners use Vallisneria to create a natural look in their ponds.

Related Terms
