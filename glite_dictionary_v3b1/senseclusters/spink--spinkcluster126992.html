spink

noun
small songbird
Pronunciation: / spɪŋk / (AmE), / spɪŋk / (BrE)
an old word for a small finch or songbird, especially the chaffinch

Examples

A spink bird
In old poems, the spink is often praised for its cheerful song.
A spink perched on the garden fence, filling the morning with music.
Farmers once believed that seeing a spink brought good luck in spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chaffinch - a common small European finch with a cheerful song
  • synonym: finch - a small songbird with a short, thick beak
