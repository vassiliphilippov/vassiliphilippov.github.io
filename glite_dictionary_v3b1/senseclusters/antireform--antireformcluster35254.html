antireform

noun
opposition to reforms
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪrɪˈfɔrm / (AmE), / ˌæntɪrɪˈfɔːm / (BrE)
opposition to political or social reforms or proposed changes

Examples

Antireform protests

Widespread antireform attitudes slowed the progress of educational changes.
