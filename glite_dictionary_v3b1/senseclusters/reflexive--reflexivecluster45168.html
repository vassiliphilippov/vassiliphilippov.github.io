reflexive

adjective
automatic, unconscious response
3.6 5.5
Pronunciation: /rə.ˈflek.sɪv/ /; / /rɪ.ˈflek.sɪv/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈflek.sɪv/ /; / /rə.ˈflek.sɪv/ (BrE)
(of an action or response) occurring automatically without conscious thought or volition, especially as part of a physiological reaction.

Examples

Reflexive reaction
Her reflexive flinch saved her from the flying ball.
Pupils contract in a reflexive manner when exposed to bright light.
Sneezing is often a reflexive response to irritants in the nose.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automatic - (of an action) done spontaneously or unconsciously
  • antonym: voluntary - done by intention or conscious decision
