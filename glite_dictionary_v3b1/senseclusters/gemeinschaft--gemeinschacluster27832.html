gemeinschaft

noun
formal association
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈmaɪnʃɑːft / (AmE), / ɡəˈmaɪnʃɑːft / (BrE)
a formal community or association of states, organizations, or people formed for common political or administrative purposes

Examples

A political gemeinschaft
The European Union is an example of a gemeinschaft created for political and economic cooperation.
After years of negotiation, the organizations established a gemeinschaft to address shared challenges.
Membership in the gemeinschaft required each state to follow common administrative rules.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alliance - a union formed for mutual benefit, especially between countries or organizations
  • synonym: association - a group of people organized for a joint purpose
  • antonym: individualism - the habit or principle of being independent and self-reliant
