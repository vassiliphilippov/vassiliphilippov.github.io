flyball

noun
dog relay sport
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈflaɪˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a competitive dog sport in which teams of dogs race over hurdles to retrieve a ball and return it to their handler

Examples

Flyball dog race
Our local club is organizing a flyball tournament for dog owners next weekend.
Training for flyball helps dogs improve their speed and teamwork skills.
Many energetic breeds enjoy participating in flyball because it combines running and retrieving.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agility - a dog sport where dogs navigate an obstacle course
  • synonym: obedience - a dog sport focused on following commands
