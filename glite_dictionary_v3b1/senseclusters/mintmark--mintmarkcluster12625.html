mintmark

noun
mark identifying a coin's mint
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪntˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈmɪntˌmɑːk / (BrE)
a mark on a coin that identifies the mint where it was produced

Examples

A mintmark on a coin
Collectors often search for coins with a rare mintmark to complete their collections.
Each branch of the U.S. Mint uses a different mintmark on the coins they produce.
The 1922 silver dollar is notable because it is missing its usual mintmark.
