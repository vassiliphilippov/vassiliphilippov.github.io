sad sack

noun
inept or unsuccessful person
Pronunciation: / sæd sæk / (AmE), / sæd sæk / (BrE)
a person who is habitually inept, unsuccessful, or makes mistakes due to incompetence

Examples

A sad sack person
After his fourth failed attempt, he felt like a real sad sack.
No matter how hard he tries, people still see him as a sad sack at work.
She played the role of the sad sack who couldn't get anything right.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loser - a person who is generally unsuccessful or inept
