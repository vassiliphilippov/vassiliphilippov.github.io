Ephesian

noun
resident of ancient Ephesus
Pronunciation: / ɪˈfiʒən / (AmE), / ɪˈfiːzɪən / (BrE)
a resident or native of the ancient Greek city of Ephesus

Examples

An Ephesian thinker
An Ephesian philosopher contributed significantly to early Greek thought.
Many artifacts from the Ephesian civilization are displayed in museums today.
The ancient Ephesian merchants were famous for their bustling markets.
