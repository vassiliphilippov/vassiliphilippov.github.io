unplait

verb
to undo braids
7.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpleɪt / (AmE), / ʌnˈpleɪt / (BrE)
to undo or separate the strands of hair or material that have been plaited or braided

Examples

To unplait hair
She carefully unplaited her daughter's hair before bedtime.
After the festival, he unplaited the colorful ribbons from the maypole.
The stylist unplaits each section before washing the hair.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plait - to braid or interweave strands together
  • synonym: unbraid - to undo or separate a braid
