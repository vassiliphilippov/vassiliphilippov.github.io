for the love of

x
expressing strong emotion or devotion
1.0
Pronunciation: / fər ðə lʌv əv / (AmE), / fə ðə lʌv əv / (BrE)
used to express strong emotion, urgency, or devotion, often as an exclamation or to indicate that something is done out of passion for someone or something

Examples

For the love of music
For the love of all that is good, please turn the music down.
She teaches children for the love of teaching, not for the money.
They traveled across the country for the love of adventure and discovery.
