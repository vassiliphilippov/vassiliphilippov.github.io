pink-orange

adjective
orange tinged with pink
Pronunciation: / pɪŋk ˈɔːrɪndʒ / (AmE), / pɪŋk ˈɒrɪndʒ / (BrE)
of a shade of orange that is tinged or mixed with pink; having the appearance of both pink and orange.

Examples

A pink-orange shirt
His shirt was a bold pink-orange that caught everyone's eye at the party.
She painted her bedroom in a lovely pink-orange shade that glows at sunset.
The sky turned a beautiful pink-orange as the sun began to set.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peach - a pale pinkish-orange color, like the fruit
