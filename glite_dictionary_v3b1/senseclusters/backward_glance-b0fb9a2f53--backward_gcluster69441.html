backward glance

noun
a quick look back over the shoulder
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌwɚd ɡlæns / (AmE), / ˈbækwəd ɡlɑːns / (BrE)
a quick look over one's shoulder at something or someone behind; a brief look back.

Examples

A backward glance over shoulder
He couldn't resist one backward glance at the house where he grew up.
She gave a final backward glance before stepping onto the train.
With a backward glance, he left the memories behind him.
