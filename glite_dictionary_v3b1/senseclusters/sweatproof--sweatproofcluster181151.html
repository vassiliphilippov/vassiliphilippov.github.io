sweatproof

adjective
resistant to sweat
Pronunciation: / ˈswɛtˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈswɛtˌpruːf / (BrE)
designed not to be affected or damaged by sweat, especially by preventing sweat from passing through or resisting moisture

Examples

Sweatproof headband
She wore a sweatproof headband to keep her forehead dry during her workout.
The new makeup claims to be sweatproof even on hot summer days.
He prefers sweatproof shirts when playing tennis outdoors.
These sweatproof headphones are perfect for running and workouts.
She bought a sweatproof sunscreen to protect her skin during exercise.
The new fitness tracker is completely sweatproof, so you can wear it while training hard.
