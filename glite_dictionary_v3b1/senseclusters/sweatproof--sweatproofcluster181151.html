sweatproof

adjective
resistant to sweat
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈswɛtˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈswɛtˌpruːf / (BrE)
designed not to be affected or damaged by sweat, especially by preventing sweat from passing through or resisting moisture
