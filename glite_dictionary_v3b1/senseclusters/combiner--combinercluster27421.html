combiner

noun
device that merges signals
6.2
Pronunciation: / kəmˈbaɪnər / (AmE), / kəmˈbaɪnə / (BrE)
a device or component that merges two or more signals, streams, or things into one output

Examples

Signal combiner
The engineer used a combiner to merge the audio and video signals for the broadcast.
A combiner is essential in systems where multiple antennas are used together.
They installed a new combiner to simplify the wiring in the control room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: merger - something that combines two or more things into one
  • antonym: splitter - a device that divides one signal or stream into several outputs
