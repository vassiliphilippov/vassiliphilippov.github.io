landlordism

noun
exploitative landlord practices
Pronunciation: / ˈlændˌlɔrdɪzəm /; / ˈlændˌlɔːrdɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈlændˌlɔːdɪzəm / (BrE)
the system or practice where landlords own and control property or land and rent it out to tenants, often having power over them

  • antonym: tenant - a person who rents land or property from a landlord
  • antonym: tenant farming - a system where people farm land owned by a landlord
