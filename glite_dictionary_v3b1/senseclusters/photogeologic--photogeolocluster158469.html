photogeologic

adjective
geology by photographs
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊdʒiəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊdʒiːəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to the study of geological features using photographs, especially from aerial or satellite images

Examples

Photogeologic survey
A photogeologic survey can reveal hidden faults and rock formations in remote areas.
Researchers used photogeologic techniques to map the surface of Mars from satellite images.
The photogeologic analysis provided new insights into the region's volcanic history.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geological - relating to the science of the Earth's physical structure
  • synonym: photographic - relating to photographs or photography
