spraddle

noun
wide spread posture
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsprædəl / (AmE), / ˈsprædəl / (BrE)
a wide, awkward position in which the legs or arms are spread apart.

Examples

A spraddle on the floor
The toddler sat in a spraddle on the floor, playing with his toys.
After the jump, she landed in a spraddle and laughed at her awkward pose.
He found himself in a spraddle after slipping on the wet pavement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: crouch - a position where the knees are bent and the body is lowered
  • synonym: straddle - a position with the legs wide apart on either side
