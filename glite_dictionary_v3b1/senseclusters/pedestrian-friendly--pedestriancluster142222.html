pedestrian-friendly

adjective
designed to benefit pedestrians
3.3 3.1
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈdes.triː.ən ˈfrend.lɪ/ /; / /pə.ˈdes.trɪ.ən ˈfrend.lɪ/ (AmE), /pə.ˈdes.trɪ.ən ˈfrend.lɪ/ /; / /pə.ˈdes.tri.ən ˈfrend.lɪ/ (BrE)
Designed or constructed to be safe, accessible, and convenient for people walking, often by minimizing hazards and providing amenities for pedestrians.

Examples

Pedestrian-friendly streets
Many cities are investing in pedestrian-friendly infrastructure such as wider sidewalks and safer crossings.
The neighborhood has become much more pedestrian-friendly since they added bike lanes and reduced traffic.
We chose a pedestrian-friendly location for our new office to encourage walking to work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: car-centric - designed primarily for automobile use
  • synonym: walkable - easy to walk in or through; convenient for pedestrians
