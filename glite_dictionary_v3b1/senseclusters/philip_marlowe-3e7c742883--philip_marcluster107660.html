Philip Marlowe

proper noun
fictional private detective created by Raymond Chandler
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪlɪp ˈmɑːrloʊ / (AmE), / ˈfɪlɪp ˈmɑːləʊ / (BrE)
a tough, cynical private detective, created by Raymond Chandler, who is a central character in several American detective novels
