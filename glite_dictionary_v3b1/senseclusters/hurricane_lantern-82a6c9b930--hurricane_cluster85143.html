hurricane lantern

noun
oil lamp with wind-protecting chimney
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhɝːrɪˌkeɪn ˈlæntɚn / (AmE), / ˈhʌrɪkən ˈlæntən / (BrE)
A portable oil lamp with a glass chimney and a perforated metal lid, designed to protect the flame from wind.

Examples

A hurricane lantern lamp
A hurricane lantern hung on the porch, glowing softly in the night.
He lit the hurricane lantern to keep the cabin illuminated during the storm.
They brought a hurricane lantern with them on the camping trip for emergency light.
