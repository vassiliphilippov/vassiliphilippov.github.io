physicalist

noun
believer in physicalism
8.5
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪz.ɪ.kəl.ɪst/ /; / /ˈfɪz.ə.kəl.ɪst/ (AmE), /ˈfɪz.ɪ.kəl.ɪst/ /; / /ˈfɪz.ə.kəl.ɪst/ (BrE)
a person who believes that only physical things exist and that everything can be explained by physical processes

Examples

A physicalist thinker
The physicalist maintained that all mental states are the result of physical processes in the brain.
As a physicalist, she rejected the idea of a non-physical soul.
Many scientists today are considered physicalists because they seek physical explanations for all phenomena.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dualist - a person who believes that mind and body are separate substances
  • synonym: materialist - a person who believes that only material things exist
