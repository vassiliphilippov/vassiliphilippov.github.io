arrestingly

adverb
in a striking way
Pronunciation: / əˈrɛstɪŋli / (AmE), / əˈrɛstɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that strongly attracts attention or interest

Examples

Appears arrestingly elegant
The actress appeared arrestingly elegant on the red carpet, turning heads as she walked by.
His speech was arrestingly honest, leaving the audience silent and thoughtful.
The mountain landscape was arrestingly beautiful at sunrise, with colors filling the sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: strikingly - in a way that is very noticeable or impressive
  • antonym: unremarkably - in a way that does not attract attention
