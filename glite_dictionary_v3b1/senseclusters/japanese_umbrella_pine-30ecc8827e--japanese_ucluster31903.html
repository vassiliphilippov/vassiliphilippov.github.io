Japanese umbrella pine

noun
evergreen Japanese tree with umbrella-shaped needles
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈpæniz ʌmˈbrɛlə paɪn / (AmE), / dʒəˈpæniːz ʌmˈbrɛlə paɪn / (BrE)
a tall evergreen tree native to Japan, with a symmetrical, spreading crown and needle-like leaves arranged in umbrella-shaped whorls at the tips of twigs

Examples

The Japanese umbrella pine tree
The Japanese umbrella pine is prized for its unique beauty in many botanical gardens.
The glossy needles of the Japanese umbrella pine give it an exotic appearance year-round.
We planted a Japanese umbrella pine near the pond to add structure to the landscape.
