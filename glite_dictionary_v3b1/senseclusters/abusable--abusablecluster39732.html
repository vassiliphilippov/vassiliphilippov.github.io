abusable

adjective
able to be misused
Pronunciation: / əˈbjuːzəbəl / (AmE), / əˈbjuːzəbəl / (BrE)
able to be misused or treated in a harmful or improper way

Examples

An abusable drug
Some prescription drugs are highly abusable if not taken as directed.
The new technology is powerful but also potentially abusable in the wrong hands.
Certain substances are considered abusable because they can lead to addiction or harm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: misusable - able to be used in the wrong way
  • antonym: safe - not likely to cause harm or be misused
