yus

noun
nasal vowel letter in Slavonic scripts
9.9
Pronunciation: /juːz/ (AmE), /juːs/ (BrE)
a Common Slavonic nasal vowel character used in the early Cyrillic and Glagolitic alphabets

Examples

The yus letter
In Old Church Slavonic texts, the letter yus is represented in several different forms.
Scholars often debate how the sound of yus should be reconstructed in modern pronunciation.
The Cyrillic script originally included both little and big yus for different nasal vowels.
