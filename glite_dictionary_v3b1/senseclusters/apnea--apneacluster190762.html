apnea

noun
breath-hold
Pronunciation: / æpˈniːə / (AmE), / æpˈnɪə / (BrE)
the deliberate holding of one's breath, especially during activities like free-diving

Examples

Training for longer apnoea is essential for free-divers to improve their performance.
