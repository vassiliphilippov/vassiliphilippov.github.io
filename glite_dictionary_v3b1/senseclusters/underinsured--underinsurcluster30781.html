underinsured

noun
person lacking coverage
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərɪnˈʃʊrd / (AmE), / ˌʌndərɪnˈʃʊəd / (BrE)
a person or group that does not have enough insurance coverage to protect against significant financial loss

Examples

An underinsured driver
After the accident, the underinsured struggled to pay for medical expenses not covered by their policy.
Many underinsured face unexpected costs when disaster strikes their homes.
Programs aim to help the underinsured access better health coverage options.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: insured - a person or group with adequate insurance coverage
  • degree lower: uninsured - a person or group with no insurance coverage
