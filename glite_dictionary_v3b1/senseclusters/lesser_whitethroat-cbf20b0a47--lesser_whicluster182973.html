lesser whitethroat

noun
a small Old World warbler (Sylvia curruca)
9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈles.ər ˈwaɪt.θroʊt/ (AmE), /ˈles.ə ˈwaɪt.θrəʊt/ (BrE)
A small Old World warbler (Sylvia curruca) similar to the greater whitethroat but smaller, found in Europe and western Asia.

Examples

The lesser whitethroat bird
The lesser whitethroat can often be heard singing in dense shrubs during spring.
Unlike its relative, the greater whitethroat, the lesser whitethroat is more difficult to observe.
We spotted a lesser whitethroat while birdwatching in the countryside.
