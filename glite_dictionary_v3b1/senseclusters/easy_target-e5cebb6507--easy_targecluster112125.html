easy target

noun
person or thing easily attacked or criticized
Pronunciation: / ˈiːzi ˈtɑrɡɪt / (AmE), / ˈiːzi ˈtɑːɡɪt / (BrE)
a person or thing that is likely to be attacked, criticized, or blamed because of their vulnerability or lack of defense

Examples

An easy target for scammers
Because he was new to the city, he became an easy target for scammers.
Cyberbullies often look for an easy target online.
The abandoned house was an easy target for vandalism.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scapegoat - a person who is blamed for the wrongdoings of others
