epidemic disease

noun
rapidly spreading infectious disease
2.4 3.0
Pronunciation: /e.pɪ.ˈdem.ɪk dɪ.ˈziːz/ /; / /e.pə.ˈdem.ɪk dɪ.ˈziːz/ (AmE), /e.pɪ.ˈdem.ɪk dɪ.ˈziːz/ (BrE)
