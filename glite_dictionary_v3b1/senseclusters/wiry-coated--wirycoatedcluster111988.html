wiry-coated

adjective
having stiff, wiry hair or fur
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪri ˌkoʊtɪd / (AmE), / ˈwaɪəri ˌkəʊtɪd / (BrE)
(especially of animals) having stiff, rough, wire-like hair or fur.

Examples

Wiry-coated dogs
Many hunters prefer wiry-coated dogs because their fur offers protection in rough terrain.
Our new terrier is a wiry-coated breed that hardly sheds at all.
The wiry-coated dog needed a special brush to keep its fur tidy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wire-haired - having hair resembling wire (especially of dogs)
