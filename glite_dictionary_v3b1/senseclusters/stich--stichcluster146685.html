stich

noun
line of verse
9.9
Pronunciation: / stɪk / (AmE), / stɪk / (BrE)

Examples

A stich of verse
Each stich in the ancient poem was carefully chosen for its rhythm and meaning.
The scholar analyzed every stich to understand the poet's technique.
In classical literature, a stich often carries a complete thought or image.
