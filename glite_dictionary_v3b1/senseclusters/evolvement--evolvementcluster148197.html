evolvement

noun
gradual development
6.4
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvɑlvmənt / (AmE), / ɪˈvɒlvmənt / (BrE)
the process or result of developing or gradually changing into a more advanced or complex form

Examples

The evolvement of language
The evolvement of language reflects changes in society over centuries.
Her career showed a steady evolvement from assistant to manager.
The evolvement of the city's skyline is visible in its modern architecture.
The evolvement of technology has changed the way we communicate and work.
Her career showed a clear evolvement from entry-level positions to leadership roles.
This policy is the evolvement of many years of research and public discussion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: development - the process of growing or becoming more advanced
  • synonym: evolution - the gradual development of something over time
  • antonym: regression - a return to a less developed state
