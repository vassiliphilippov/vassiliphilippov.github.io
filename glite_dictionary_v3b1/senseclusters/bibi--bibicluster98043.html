bibi

noun
South Asian honorific
9.1 0.42/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàbiÀêbi / (AmE), / ÀàbiÀêbi / (BrE)
a polite title or form of address for a woman, especially in South Asian cultures, similar to 'Mrs.' or 'Madam'

Examples

Bibi title
The villagers respectfully greeted bibi as she entered the community meeting hall.
Everyone addressed her as bibi to show their respect in the traditional ceremony.
In some South Asian families, the eldest woman is called bibi as a sign of honor.
