lefthander

noun
baseball pitcher using left hand
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛftˌhændər / (AmE), / ˈlɛftˌhændə / (BrE)
A baseball pitcher who throws the ball with the left hand.

Examples

A lefthander pitcher
Batters often struggle more against a skilled lefthander.
She was the only lefthander on the pitching staff this season.
