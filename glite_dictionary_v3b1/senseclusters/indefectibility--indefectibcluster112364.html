indefectibility

noun
incapacity to fail
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˌfɛktəˈbɪləti /; / ˌɪndɪˌfɛktəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˌfɛktɪˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
