chad

noun
slang for stereotypical confident young man
9.9
Pronunciation: /tʃæd/ (AmE), /tʃæd/ (BrE)
Informal slang for a young man regarded as conventionally attractive, confident, and socially dominant; often used jokingly or pejoratively for the 'alpha male' stereotype.

Examples

A chad at parties
A couple of tall, confident chads at the party kept joking and attracting everyone's attention.
He resented the campus chad who always interrupted class and bragged about his dates.
Online, the term chad is often used jokingly to describe stereotypical alpha males.
