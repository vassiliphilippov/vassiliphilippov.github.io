ding

verb
to dent or scratch
3.8
Pronunciation: / dɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪŋ / (BrE)
to make a small dent or scratch in something, especially a hard surface

Examples

To ding the door
He accidentally dinged the car door in the parking lot.
Be careful not to ding your phone when you drop it.
The moving company dinged the edge of the table during transport.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dent - to make a small hollow mark in a surface by hitting it
  • antonym: repair - to fix something that is damaged
  • synonym: scratch - to damage a surface by making thin, shallow marks on it
