forcibly

adverb
using force; with physical strength or violence
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔːrsəbli / (AmE), / ˈfɔːsəbli / (BrE)
Using physical force or strength; in a way that involves force or violence.

Examples

To be forcibly removed
She was forcibly separated from her family during the conflict.
The landlord forcibly evicted the tenants who refused to pay rent.
The protesters were forcibly removed from the building by the police.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forcefully - in a strong and assertive way
