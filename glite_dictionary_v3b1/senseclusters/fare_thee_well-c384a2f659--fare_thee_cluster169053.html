fare thee well

interjection
archaic farewell expression
6.0
Pronunciation: / fɛr ði wɛl / (AmE), / feə ðiː wɛl / (BrE)
used as an expression of good wishes when someone is leaving; a way to say farewell and wish someone well for the future

Examples

Fare thee well friend
He turned to her at the door and softly whispered, 'fare thee well.'
In old letters, people would often write 'fare thee well' before signing their names.
The knight waved and cried 'fare thee well' as he set out on his quest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: farewell - expression of goodbye
  • synonym: goodbye - expression used when parting
