peppy

adjective
full of energy or liveliness
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛpi / (AmE), / ˈpɛpi / (BrE)
full of energy, enthusiasm, or liveliness; lively and spirited.

Examples

A peppy song
His peppy style of presentation kept the audience engaged throughout the lecture.
She always brings a peppy attitude to the meetings, making them more enjoyable for everyone.
The band played a peppy song that got everyone up on their feet.
