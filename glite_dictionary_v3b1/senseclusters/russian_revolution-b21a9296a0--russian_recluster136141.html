Russian Revolution

proper noun
1917 Russian revolution against the czar
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌʃən ˌrɛvəˈluːʃən / (AmE), / ˈrʌʃ(ə)n ˌrɛvəˈluːʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the revolution in Russia in March 1917 that overthrew the czarist government and led to the establishment of a provisional government

Examples

The first Russian Revolution
After the Russian Revolution, the monarchy was replaced by a provisional government.
Many historians debate the social impacts of the Russian Revolution on European politics.
The Russian Revolution changed the course of world history in the early twentieth century.
