chola

noun
Latina subculture slang
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃoʊlə / (AmE), / ˈtʃəʊlə / (BrE)
a slang term for a Latina woman, often associated with a distinctive urban subculture and style in the United States

Examples

Urban chola style
The documentary explored the style and identity of chola culture in Los Angeles.
She dressed as a chola for the themed party, complete with hoop earrings and bold makeup.
Many young women proudly identify as cholas to express their cultural roots and individuality.
