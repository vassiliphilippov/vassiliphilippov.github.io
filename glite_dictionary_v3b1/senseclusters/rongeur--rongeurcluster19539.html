rongeur

noun
surgical cutting tool
Pronunciation: / rɑnˈʒɜr / (AmE), / rɒ̃ˈʒɜː / (BrE)
a strong surgical instrument used for cutting or removing bone or tough tissue

Examples

Surgical rongeur tool
The surgeon used a rongeur to carefully remove fragments of bone during the operation.
A rongeur is essential for procedures that require cutting through tough tissue or bone.
After making the initial incision, the doctor reached for the rongeur to continue the procedure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forceps - a surgical instrument for grasping or holding objects
  • synonym: scalpel - a small, sharp knife used in surgery
