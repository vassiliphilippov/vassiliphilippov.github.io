handloading

noun
manual loading
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌloʊdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhændˌləʊdɪŋ / (BrE)
the action of loading goods, materials, or equipment by hand instead of using machines

Examples

Handloading heavy boxes
Due to the lack of equipment, the workers relied on handloading to move the boxes onto the truck.
Safety guidelines must be followed during handloading to prevent injuries.
The fragile items required careful handloading instead of using the conveyor belt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manual handling - the process of moving or supporting loads by hand or bodily force
  • antonym: mechanized loading - the process of loading goods using machines or equipment
