hypospray

noun
needleless injector
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪpoʊˌspreɪ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪpəʊˌspreɪ / (BrE)
a handheld device that injects medicine through the skin without using a needle

Examples

Medical hypospray device
The nurse used a hypospray to give the patient his medication quickly and painlessly.
Scientists are developing a new hypospray for delivering vaccines without needles.
In the futuristic movie, doctors treated injuries with a portable hypospray device.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jet injector - a device that delivers medication through the skin using a high-pressure jet
  • antonym: syringe - a device used to inject fluids into the body, usually with a needle
