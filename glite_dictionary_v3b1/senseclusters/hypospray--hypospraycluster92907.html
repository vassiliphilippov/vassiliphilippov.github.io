hypospray

noun
needleless injector
9.7
Pronunciation: /ˈhaɪ.poʊ.spreɪ/ (AmE), /ˈhaɪ.pəʊ.spreɪ/ (BrE)

In the futuristic movie, doctors treated injuries with a portable hypospray device.
