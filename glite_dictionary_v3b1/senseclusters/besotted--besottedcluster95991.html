besotted

adjective
deeply infatuated
7.2
Pronunciation: / bɪˈsɑtɪd / (AmE), / bɪˈsɒtɪd / (BrE)
very strongly in love with or infatuated by someone or something, often to an excessive degree

Examples

Besotted with love
He was completely besotted with his new girlfriend and talked about her constantly.
The puppy was besotted with its owner, following her everywhere she went.
She became besotted by the charming city during her first visit.

Related Terms

  • antonym: indifferent - having no particular interest or sympathy
  • synonym: infatuated - having an intense but short-lived passion for someone or something
  • synonym: smitten - suddenly and strongly affected by love or attraction
