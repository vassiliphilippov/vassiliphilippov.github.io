collar

noun
ring or band securing a machine part
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːlər / (AmE), / ˈkɒlə / (BrE)
a short ring or band fastened around a rod or shaft to limit, guide, or secure a machine part

Examples

Metal collar
A metal collar prevents the wheel from sliding along the axle during operation.
Each collar is designed to fit snugly and guide the movement of the rod.
The engineer tightened the collar on the shaft to ensure the gear stayed in place.
