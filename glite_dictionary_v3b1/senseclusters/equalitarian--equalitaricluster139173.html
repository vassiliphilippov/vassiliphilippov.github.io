equalitarian

adjective
supporting equality
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌiːkwəˌlɛˈtɛriən / (AmE), / ˌiːkwəˌlɪˈteəriən / (BrE)
believing in or promoting equal rights and opportunities for all people

Examples

Equalitarian values
The new policy reflects an equalitarian approach to education for all students.
Her equalitarian beliefs influenced her work in social justice organizations.
Many modern societies strive to create more equalitarian systems of government.

Related Terms

  • synonym: egalitarian - believing in the principle that all people are equal
  • antonym: inegalitarian - not supporting or characterized by social equality
