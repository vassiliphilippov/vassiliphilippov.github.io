smallmouth bass

noun
freshwater North American game fish
9.4 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈsmɑːl.maʊθ bæs/ /; / /ˈsmɔːl.maʊθ bæs/ (AmE), /ˈsmɔːl.maʊθ bæs/ (BrE)
