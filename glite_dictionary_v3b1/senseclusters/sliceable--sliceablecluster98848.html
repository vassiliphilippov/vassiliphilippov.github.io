sliceable

adjective
can be cut into slices
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈslaɪsəbəl / (AmE), / ˈslaɪsəbəl / (BrE)
able to be easily cut into slices without crumbling or breaking apart

Examples

Sliceable bread
This bread is so soft that it is barely sliceable without falling apart.
The cake should be chilled to make it more sliceable for serving at the party.
Choose a firm, sliceable cheese for the perfect sandwich.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cuttable - able to be cut
  • antonym: unsliceable - not able to be cut into slices
