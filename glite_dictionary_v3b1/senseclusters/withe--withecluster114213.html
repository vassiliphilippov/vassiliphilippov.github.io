withe

noun
band or rope of twisted twigs or stems
Pronunciation: / wɪð /; / wɪθ / (AmE), / wɪð / (BrE)
a strong, flexible twig or a band made by twisting such twigs together, used for binding, tying, or making things like baskets or furniture

Examples

The basket was woven from willow withes by local craftsmen.
