unedifying

adjective
not enlightening or uplifting
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈɛdəˌfaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈɛdɪfaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
Not providing moral or intellectual improvement; not enlightening or uplifting.

Examples

An unedifying story
His behavior at the meeting was distinctly unedifying and disappointed many of his colleagues.
The film was criticized for its unedifying content and lack of positive messages.
Witnessing the argument was an unedifying experience for everyone involved.

Related Terms

  • antonym: edifying - providing moral or intellectual improvement
