mineral resources

noun
naturally occurring minerals in the earth
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnərəl rɪˌsɔrsɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɪnərəl rɪˌsɔːsɪz / (BrE)
natural substances found in the earth, such as metals, coal, and oil, that can be extracted and used for economic gain

Examples

Mineral resources extraction
Countries with abundant mineral resources often experience rapid industrial growth.
Many economies depend on the export of their mineral resources to generate income.
The government is investing in sustainable methods of extracting mineral resources.
