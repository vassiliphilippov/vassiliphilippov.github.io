bestest

adjective
very informal superlative
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛstɪst / (AmE), / ˈbɛstɪst / (BrE)
very informal or playful way to say 'the very best', often used by children or for emphasis

Examples

My bestest friend
You are my bestest friend in the whole world.
That was the bestest birthday party I have ever had.
She gave me the bestest present for my graduation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: best - of the highest quality or excellence
  • antonym: worst - of the lowest quality or most undesirable
