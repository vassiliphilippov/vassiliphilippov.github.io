glide

noun
smooth continuous movement on a surface
4.7
Pronunciation: / ɡlaɪd / (AmE), / ɡlaɪd / (BrE)
a smooth, continuous movement, or the activity of flying a glider without engine power

Examples

A dancer's glide
The dancer's final glide ended in a soft and perfect stop.
The skater's glide across the ice was graceful and effortless.
With a gentle glide, the canoe moved silently across the lake.
After taking lessons, she was finally able to enjoy her first glide over the countryside.
Many enthusiasts travel to the mountains for the perfect glide during summer.
The club offers weekend courses in glide for beginners and advanced pilots.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slide - a smooth movement along a surface while in contact with it
  • synonym: soaring - the activity of flying a glider, typically using updrafts to gain altitude
