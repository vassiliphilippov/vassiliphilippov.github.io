spread

adjective
fully extended or stretched out
1.8
Pronunciation: / sprɛd / (AmE), / sprɛd / (BrE)
fully extended, stretched out, or distributed over an area; arranged with food

Examples

Arms spread wide
She greeted her friend with arms spread wide in joy.
The eagle soared with its spread wings catching the wind.
The map lay spread out on the table for everyone to see.
Her relatives are spread all over the world, making family gatherings rare.
The population is spread thinly throughout the region, with few dense cities.
The rumors were spread across several countries, causing widespread confusion.
The hostess greeted us warmly at the door, with the kitchen counter already spread for breakfast.
The long dining table was spread with an array of delicious dishes.
We arrived to find the picnic blanket spread with snacks and drinks.

  • synonym: laid - prepared or set out, especially for a meal
