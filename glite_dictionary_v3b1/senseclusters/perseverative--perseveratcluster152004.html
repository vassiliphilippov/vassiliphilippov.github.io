perseverative

adjective

in phonology, describing a sound change where a preceding sound influences a following sound, known as carryover assimilation

Examples

Perseverative assimilation
A perseverative assimilation occurs when the first sound in a word changes the next sound.
The teacher explained how perseverative effects can alter the pronunciation of certain words.
In some languages, perseverative changes are common in rapid speech.
