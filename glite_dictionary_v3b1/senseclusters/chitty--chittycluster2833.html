chitty

noun
rotating savings scheme
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪti / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪti / (BrE)
a rotating savings and credit scheme, especially in South Asia, where members contribute money regularly and take turns receiving the pooled amount

Examples

Chitty savings group
She joined a chitty to help save money for her daughter's education.
Each member of the chitty contributes a fixed amount every month.
Winning the chitty draw allowed him to pay for urgent home repairs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: committee - a similar rotating savings and credit association
