embroidery hoop

noun
frame of hoops for embroidery
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbroʊdəri huːp / (AmE), / ɪmˈbrəʊdəri huːp / (BrE)
a pair of fitted rings or hoops used to hold fabric taut while embroidering

Examples

An embroidery hoop frame
He bought a new embroidery hoop for his cross-stitch project.
She adjusted the fabric in the embroidery hoop before starting her stitches.
Without an embroidery hoop, it's difficult to keep the fabric smooth and even.
