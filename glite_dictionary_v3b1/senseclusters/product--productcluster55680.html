product

noun
created or manufactured item
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑː.dʌkt /; / ˈprɑːdəkt / (AmE), / ˈprɒd.ʌkt /; / ˈprɒdʌkt / (BrE)
an item or substance that is made or manufactured, especially something offered for sale

Examples

A new product launch
Our company launches several innovative products every year.
Quality control ensures that each product meets strict safety standards.
The new product quickly became popular with consumers worldwide.
All products in the store are on sale this weekend.
Customers can choose from a wide range of products in the supermarket.
The company launched a new product last month to attract more customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goods - items produced for sale or use
  • synonym: merchandise - goods that are bought and sold
  • antonym: service - an action performed for someone; the opposite of a material product
