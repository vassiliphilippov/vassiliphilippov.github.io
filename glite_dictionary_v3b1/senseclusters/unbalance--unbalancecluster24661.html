unbalance

noun
mental disturbance or instability
4.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbæl.əns /; / ʌnˈbæləns / (AmE), / ʌnˈbæl.əns / (BrE)
a state of mental or emotional instability or disturbance

Examples

Emotional unbalance
After months of stress, he felt a sense of unbalance that he couldn't shake.
Periods of extreme unbalance can interfere with daily life and relationships.
The trauma caused a temporary unbalance in her mental health.
After the incident, she experienced emotional unbalance for several weeks.
Mechanical unbalance in the engine caused excessive vibration.
The constant noise led to a sense of unbalance and anxiety.
