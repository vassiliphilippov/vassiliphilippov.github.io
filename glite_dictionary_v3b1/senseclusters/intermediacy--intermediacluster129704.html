intermediacy

noun
state of being intermediate
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈmiːdiəsi / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈmiːdiəsi / (BrE)
the state or quality of being in an intermediate or middle position, or being in a temporary or transitional stage

Examples

Because of its intermediacy, the city serves as a convenient meeting point for travelers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intermediateness - the quality of being in an intermediate position
  • synonym: middle - the central point or position
  • synonym: transition - the process or period of changing from one state to another
