capsize

verb
overturn in water (boat/ship)
Pronunciation: / kæpˈsaɪz / (AmE), / kæpˈsaɪz / (BrE)
(of a boat or ship) to overturn in the water, usually by accident.

Examples

To capsize a boat
If the waves get too high, the boat could easily capsize.
Several kayaks capsized during the sudden storm on the lake.
The crew struggled to right the vessel after it capsized in rough seas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overturn - to turn over or cause to overturn
