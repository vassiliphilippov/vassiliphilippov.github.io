ligneous

adjective
woody; containing lignin or xylem
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪɡniəs / (AmE), / ˈlɪɡnɪəs / (BrE)
consisting of or resembling wood; containing or made up of lignin or xylem tissue.

Examples

Ligneous material
Certain fungi can break down ligneous material within dead wood.
The stem of the tree has a ligneous structure that provides strength and support.
This plant develops ligneous tissue as it matures, making its branches hard and durable.
