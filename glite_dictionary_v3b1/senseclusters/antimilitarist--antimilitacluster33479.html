antimilitarist

adjective
opposed to militarism
7.5
Pronunciation: /æn.tɪ.ˈmɪl.ɪ.trɪst/ /; / /æn.tɪ.ˈmɪl.ɪ.tər.ɪst/ (AmE), /æn.tɪ.ˈmɪl.ɪ.trɪst/ /; / /æn.tɪ.ˈmɪl.ɪ.tə.rɪst/ (BrE)

Examples
