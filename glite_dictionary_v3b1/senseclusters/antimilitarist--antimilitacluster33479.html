antimilitarist

adjective
opposed to militarism
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈmɪlɪtərɪst / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈmɪlɪtərɪst / (BrE)
opposed to militarism, military policies, or the influence of armed forces in society or government

Examples

Antimilitarist views
The candidate's antimilitarist stance appealed to voters who favored peace and diplomacy.
Many antimilitarist activists protested the government's decision to increase military spending.
Her antimilitarist beliefs were shaped by her family's experiences during the war.

Related Terms

  • antonym: militarist - supporting the use or influence of armed forces
  • synonym: pacifist - opposed to war or violence as a means of settling disputes
