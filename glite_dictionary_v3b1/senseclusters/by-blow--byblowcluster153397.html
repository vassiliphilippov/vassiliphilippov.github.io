by-blow

noun
illegitimate child
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪ ˌbloʊ / (AmE), / ˈbaɪ ˌbləʊ / (BrE)
an illegitimate child; a child born to parents not married to each other

Examples

The by-blow of nobles
He was whispered to be the by-blow of a nobleman and a servant girl.
In centuries past, a by-blow had few rights and faced much prejudice.
The old novel told the tragic story of a by-blow cast out by society.
