line

noun
a row of people or things, one behind another
1.0 50/m
Pronunciation: / laÉªn / (AmE), / laÉªn / (BrE)
a row or formation of people or things arranged in a straight or organized way

Examples

A line of people

Please form a line so everyone can get their tickets easily.

  • column
