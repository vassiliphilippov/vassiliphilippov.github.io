antheral

adjective
able to fertilize female organs (botany)
Pronunciation: / ˈænθərəl / (AmE), / ˈænθərəl / (BrE)
(of pollen or a reproductive unit) capable of fertilizing female organs, especially in plants.

Examples

Antheral pollen
Only antheral pollen can successfully fertilize the ovules during pollination.
Researchers study antheral cells to improve crop fertilization rates.
The antheral grains were observed under the microscope to determine their viability.
