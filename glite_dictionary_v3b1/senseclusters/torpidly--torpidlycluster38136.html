torpidly

adverb
in a sluggish or lethargic manner
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔːrpɪdli / (AmE), / ˈtɔːpɪdli / (BrE)
in a sluggish, inactive, or lethargic manner

Examples

To torpidly move
After the long flight, she answered the questions torpidly, struggling to stay awake.
He moved torpidly across the room, barely lifting his feet as he walked.
The snake lay torpidly in the sun, not reacting to the noises around it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lethargically - in a manner showing lack of energy or enthusiasm
