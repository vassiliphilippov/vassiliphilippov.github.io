unhesitantly

adverb
without hesitation
5.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɛzɪtəntli / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛzɪtəntli / (BrE)
in a way that shows no hesitation or doubt

Examples

To unhesitantly accept
She unhesitantly accepted the job offer, eager to start her new career.
He unhesitantly spoke up during the meeting to share his ideas.
The child unhesitantly jumped into the pool, showing no fear at all.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confidently - in a way that shows confidence
  • antonym: hesitantly - in a way that shows hesitation or uncertainty
