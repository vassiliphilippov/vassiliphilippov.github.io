ethnohistorical

adjective
cultural-historical study
8.8
Pronunciation: /eθ.noʊ.hɪ.ˈstɔːr.ɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /eθ.nəʊ.hɪ.ˈstɒr.ɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
relating to the study of cultures and histories using both historical and ethnographic methods

Examples

Ethnohistorical study
The museum's ethnohistorical exhibit explores the traditions of indigenous peoples through artifacts and documents.
Researchers used ethnohistorical sources to reconstruct the migration patterns of the community.
An ethnohistorical analysis can reveal how cultural practices have changed over centuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ethnographic - relating to the scientific description of peoples and cultures
  • synonym: historical - relating to history or past events
