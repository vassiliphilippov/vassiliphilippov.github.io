Peter Pan

noun
immature man; never grows up
Pronunciation: / ˌpiːtər ˈpæn / (AmE), / ˌpiːtə ˈpæn / (BrE)
a man who remains boyish or immature and refuses to grow up, after the character in J.M. Barrie's play

Examples

A Peter Pan man
Her brother is a real Peter Pan, always playing video games instead of working.
Some critics describe him as a Peter Pan who refuses responsibility.
The club attracts several Peter Pans who avoid adult commitments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adult - a person who is fully grown and mature
  • synonym: man-child - an adult man who behaves in a childish way
