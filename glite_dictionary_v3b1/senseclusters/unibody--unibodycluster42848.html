unibody

noun
single-piece structure
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːnibɑːdi /; / ˈjuːnɪˌbɑːdi / (AmE), / ˈjuːnɪbɒdi /; / ˈjuːnɪˌbɒdi / (BrE)
a structure made from a single piece, especially in vehicles or electronics, where the body and frame are integrated
