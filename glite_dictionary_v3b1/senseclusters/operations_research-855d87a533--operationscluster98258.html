operations research

noun
field of applying math to optimize decisions
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɑpəˈreɪʃənz rɪˈsɜrtʃ / (AmE), / ˌɒpəˈreɪʃənz rɪˈsɜːtʃ / (BrE)
the study of mathematical methods used to analyze complex systems and to determine the most efficient way to achieve a desired outcome or solve organizational problems
