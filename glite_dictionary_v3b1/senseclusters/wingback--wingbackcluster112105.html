wingback

noun
high-backed chair
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪŋˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋˌbæk / (BrE)
a chair with a high back and side panels that extend forward to support the head or block drafts

Examples

A wingback chair
She curled up in the cozy wingback by the fireplace to read her novel.
The antique wingback added a touch of elegance to the living room.
He chose a blue velvet wingback for his study because of its comfort and style.

Related Terms

  • synonym: armchair - a comfortable chair with side supports for a person's arms
  • synonym: recliner - a chair that can be tilted back for comfort
