castor

noun
shaker for powdered sugar
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkæstər / (AmE), / ˈkɑːstə / (BrE)
A small container with a perforated top, used for sprinkling powdered sugar or other fine substances.

Examples

Sugar castor
Please pass the castor so I can dust my pancakes with sugar.
She filled the castor with powdered sugar before setting the table.
The ornate silver castor added elegance to the dessert course.
