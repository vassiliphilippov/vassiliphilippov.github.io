spambot

noun
automated spam sender
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈspæmˌbɑt / (AmE), / ˈspæmˌbɒt / (BrE)
an automated computer program that sends large amounts of unwanted or spam messages online (such as emails, forum posts, or social media messages)

Examples

A spambot sends emails
A spambot infected the company's network and sent thousands of unwanted emails overnight.
Many email providers use filters to block messages sent by a spambot.
If your computer is part of a spambot network, it may send spam without your knowledge.
The forum was flooded with advertisements posted by a spambot.
Many email services use filters to block messages sent by a spambot.
A single spambot can send thousands of unwanted messages in just a few minutes.
The forum was overrun by a spambot posting links to suspicious websites.
After installing new security software, the number of spambots decreased significantly.
Many social media platforms use filters to block spambot activity and protect users.

Related Terms

  • antonym: antivirus - software designed to detect and remove malicious programs
  • synonym: bot - a computer program that performs automated tasks
  • synonym: botnet - a network of computers infected with malware and controlled as a group
  • synonym: malware - software designed to harm or exploit computers
  • synonym: spam - unwanted or unsolicited messages, especially online
  • antonym: troll - a person who posts inflammatory or off-topic messages online
