half-light

noun
soft, dim, partial light
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhæfˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈhɑːfˌlaɪt / (BrE)
a soft, dim light, as at dawn, dusk, or in faintly lit interiors, where things are only partially illuminated

Examples

The half-light of dusk
In the half-light of dusk, it was hard to see his face clearly.
She waited in the half-light of early morning before the sun had fully risen.
The room was filled with a gentle half-light, making the furniture look mysterious.

Related Terms

  • synonym: twilight - the soft glowing light from the sky when the sun is below the horizon
