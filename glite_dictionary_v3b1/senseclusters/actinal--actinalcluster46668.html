actinal

adjective
relating to the side with the mouth (zoology)
Pronunciation: / ˈæk.tɪ.nəl / (AmE), / ˈæk.tɪ.nəl / (BrE)
(of radiate organisms) situated on the surface or side that bears the mouth.

Examples

The actinal surface
In starfish, the actinal surface is the lower side where the mouth is located.
Researchers observed tube feet emerging from the actinal side of the specimen.
Species with obvious actinal and aboral surfaces are easy to identify.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aboral - on or relating to the side opposite the mouth (zoology)
