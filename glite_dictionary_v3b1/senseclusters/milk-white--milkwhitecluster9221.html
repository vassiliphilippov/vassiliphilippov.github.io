milk-white

adjective
pure white, like fresh milk
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlk waɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɪlk waɪt / (BrE)
having a white color like that of fresh milk; pure or creamy white.

Examples

Milk-white color
She chose a milk-white dress for the wedding, elegant and simple.
The clouds overhead were milk-white, promising a clear and sunny afternoon.
The swan glided across the lake, its feathers appearing milk-white in the dawn light.
