insensible

adjective
too slight to be noticed; barely perceptible
5.6 5.9
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈsen.sə.bəl/ /; / /ɪn.ˈsen.sɪ.bəl/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈsen.sə.bəl/ /; / /ɪn.ˈsen.sɪ.bəl/ (BrE)
too small or gradual to be noticed or clearly perceived; barely perceptible.

Examples

Insensible change
The difference in weight was so insensible that most people could not detect it.
The temperature dropped by an insensible amount overnight.
