bomb

noun
complete failure (informal)
6.3
Pronunciation: / bɑm / (AmE), / bɒm / (BrE)
a complete failure, especially an event, product, or performance that is unsuccessful or ineffective

Examples

The show was a bomb
His big idea for the party was a bomb; nobody enjoyed it.
The new play was a total bomb and closed after just one night.
Their marketing campaign turned out to be a bomb, attracting almost no customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flop - a complete failure, especially a theatrical production or other event
  • antonym: success - the achievement of an aim or purpose; opposite of failure
