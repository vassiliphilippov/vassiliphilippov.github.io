teetotaler

noun
person who abstains from alcohol
Pronunciation: / tiˈtoʊtələr / (AmE), / tiːˈtəʊtlə /; / tiːˈtəʊtələ / (BrE)
a person who never drinks alcoholic beverages

Examples

As a proud teetotaller, he always volunteers to drive his friends home.
