teetotaler

noun
person who abstains from alcohol

Examples

A teetotaler person
As a teetotaler, she often volunteers to drive her friends home after gatherings.
Despite the party atmosphere, he remained a proud teetotaler all evening.

Synonyms

  • teetotalist
    7.6 0.00/m
    teetotalist is a person who fully avoids alcohol and appears in formal or historical texts, while teetotaler is the common everyday term.
  • abstainer
    5.4 0.21/m
    abstainer is someone who chooses not to drink, often for a time or reason, while teetotaler implies never drinking permanently.
  • nondrinker
    4.8 0.14/m
    nondrinker is a neutral label for a person who does not drink, while teetotaler implies a strong lifelong decision against alcohol.
  • abstinent
    8.0 0.02/m
    abstinent describes someone who keeps away from alcohol or other pleasures, while teetotaler means a person who never drinks alcohol.
