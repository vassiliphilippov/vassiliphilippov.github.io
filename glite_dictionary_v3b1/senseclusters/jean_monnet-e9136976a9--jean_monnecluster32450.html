Jean Monnet

proper noun
French economist and European integration pioneer
8.3
Pronunciation: /ʒɑːn mɑːn.ˈneɪ/ /; / /dʒiːn mɑːn.ˈneɪ/ (AmE), /ʒɑːn mɒn.ˈneɪ/ /; / /dʒiːn mɒn.ˈneɪ/ (BrE)
