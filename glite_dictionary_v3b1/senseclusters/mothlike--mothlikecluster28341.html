mothlike

adjective
resembling a moth
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔθˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɒθˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or similar to a moth in appearance, movement, or behavior

Examples

Mothlike movement
The dancer moved across the stage in a mothlike manner, graceful and silent.
Its mothlike wings shimmered softly in the moonlight as it hovered near the lamp.
She wore a delicate, mothlike costume for the masquerade ball.
