iodization

noun
adding iodine
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪədaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌaɪədaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of adding iodine to a substance, especially to salt, to prevent iodine deficiency

Examples

Iodization of salt
Iodization of table salt is a common public health measure in many countries.
The government launched a campaign to promote the iodization of salt in rural areas.
Proper iodization helps reduce the risk of thyroid problems caused by iodine deficiency.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fortification - the process of adding nutrients to food
