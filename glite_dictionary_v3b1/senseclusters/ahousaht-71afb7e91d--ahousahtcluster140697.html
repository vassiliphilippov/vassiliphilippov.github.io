Ahousaht

proper noun
Indigenous group, Canada
an Indigenous people and community living on the west coast of Vancouver Island, Canada

Examples

The Ahousaht people
The Ahousaht have a long history of living along the coast of Vancouver Island.
Many members of the Ahousaht community are involved in preserving their language and traditions.
Visitors to Vancouver Island can learn about the culture of the Ahousaht people.
