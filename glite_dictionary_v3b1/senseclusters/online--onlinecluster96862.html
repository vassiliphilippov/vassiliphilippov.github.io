online

adjective
connected to a computer network
Pronunciation: / ˈɑnˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɒnˌlaɪn / (BrE)

Examples

Online banking service
An online banking system allows customers to manage their accounts remotely.
She prefers to do her shopping online rather than visiting stores in person.
The company offers a wide range of online courses for adult learners.
Her photos are available to view online through her website.
Many students now take online courses instead of attending traditional classes.
The store offers both online and in-person shopping options.
