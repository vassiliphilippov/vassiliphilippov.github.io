seizer

noun
person who captures
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːzər / (AmE), / ˈsiːzə / (BrE)
a person or thing that takes hold of, captures, or seizes something

Examples

The seizer of goods

As the seizer of the property, she was responsible for its safety.
The animal's quick reflexes made it an effective seizer of prey.
