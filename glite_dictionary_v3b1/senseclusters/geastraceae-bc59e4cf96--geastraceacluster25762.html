Geastraceae

noun
family of earthstar fungi
Pronunciation: / dʒiˌæstəˈsiːi / (AmE), / dʒiːˌæstəˈsiːiː / (BrE)
A family of fungi known as earthstars, characterized by their star-shaped fruiting bodies and belonging to the order Lycoperdales.

Examples

Geastraceae fungi family
Many species within the Geastraceae family produce star-shaped fruiting bodies.
Researchers recently discovered a new member of the Geastraceae in a North American forest.
The unique structure of Geastraceae fungi helps disperse their spores effectively.

Related Terms

  • synonym: earthstar - a type of fungus with a star-shaped spore case, member of Geastraceae
