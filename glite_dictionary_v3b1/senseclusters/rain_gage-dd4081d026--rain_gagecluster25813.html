rain gage

noun
instrument for measuring rainfall
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪn ˌɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈreɪn ˌɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
an instrument used for measuring the amount of rainfall over a given period

Examples
