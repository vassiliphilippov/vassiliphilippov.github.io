cast

verb
remove or discard something
Pronunciation: / kæst / (AmE), / kɑːst / (BrE)
to remove or get rid of something, especially by taking it off or discarding it

Examples

To cast off clothes
After a long day, he liked to cast off his work clothes and relax.
She struggled to cast her doubts aside before the meeting.
The snake will cast its old skin as it grows.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discard - to get rid of something no longer wanted
  • synonym: shed - to cast off or remove, as with clothing or an image
