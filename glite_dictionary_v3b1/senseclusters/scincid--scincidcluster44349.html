scincid

noun
a lizard of the family Scincidae (skink)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪn.sɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɪn.sɪd / (BrE)
A lizard of the family Scincidae, typically agile with reduced limbs, an elongated body, and shiny scales, often found in moist tropical regions.
