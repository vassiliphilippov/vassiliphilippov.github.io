impact

verb
press together tightly
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpækt / (AmE), / ɪmˈpækt / (BrE)
to press or pack together firmly; to wedge tightly

Examples

To impact the soil
Heavy machinery is used to impact the gravel before laying asphalt.
Over time, the garbage impacted tightly at the bottom of the bin.
The snow had impacted in the gutter, causing a blockage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compress - to press or squeeze something so that it takes up less space
  • antonym: loosen - to make something less tight or compact
