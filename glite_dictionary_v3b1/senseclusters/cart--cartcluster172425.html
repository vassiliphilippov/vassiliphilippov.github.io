cart

noun
wheeled vehicle for carrying loads
3.3
Pronunciation: / kɑrt / (AmE), / kɑːt / (BrE)
a vehicle with wheels, pushed or pulled by a person or animal, used for carrying goods or loads

Examples

A horse cart
She loaded all her groceries into the cart before heading to the checkout.
The gardener wheeled the cart full of tools across the yard.
Vendors sold snacks from a brightly painted cart near the park entrance.
Donkeys were used to pull the heavy cart up the hill.
The farmer loaded hay onto his cart before heading to the market.
We saw an old wooden cart parked beside the barn.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: wagon - a heavier vehicle usually pulled by animals
