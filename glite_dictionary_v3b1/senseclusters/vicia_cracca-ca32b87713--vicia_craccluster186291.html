Vicia cracca

noun
a perennial climbing plant with dense blue-purple flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪʃiə ˈkrækə / (AmE), / ˈvɪsɪə ˈkraka / (BrE)
A common perennial climbing plant of temperate regions in Eurasia and North America, known for its dense, elongated clusters of bluish-purple flowers; also called tufted vetch.
