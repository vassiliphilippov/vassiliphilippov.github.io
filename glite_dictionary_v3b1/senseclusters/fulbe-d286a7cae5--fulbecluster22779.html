Fulbe

noun
a nomadic people of West Africa
Pronunciation: / ˈfuːlbeɪ / (AmE), / ˈfʊlbeɪ / (BrE)
a member of a pastoral and nomadic people of West Africa, traditionally Muslim cattle herders

Examples

The Fulbe people
Many Fulbe communities maintain their traditional nomadic lifestyle despite modern pressures.
The Fulbe have a long history as skilled cattle herders across the Sahel region.
The language spoken by the Fulbe is known as Fulfulde or Pulaar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Fula - another name for the Fulbe people
  • synonym: Peul - French term for the Fulbe people
