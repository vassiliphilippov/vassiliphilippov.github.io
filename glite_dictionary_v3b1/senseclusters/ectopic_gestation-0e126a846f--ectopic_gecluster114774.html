ectopic gestation

noun
pregnancy outside the uterus
Pronunciation: / ɛkˈtɑpɪk dʒɛˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ɛkˈtɒpɪk dʒɛˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
A pregnancy in which the embryo implants and develops outside the uterus, most commonly in a fallopian tube.

Examples

ectopic gestation in fallopian tube
Advances in surgery have greatly improved outcomes for women experiencing ectopic gestation.
If untreated, an ectopic gestation can lead to serious health complications for the mother.
The doctor diagnosed her with an ectopic gestation after reviewing the ultrasound results.
