sixer

noun
Cub Scout group leader
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪksər / (AmE), / ˈsɪksə / (BrE)
the leader of a group of six children in Cub Scouts or a similar youth organization

Examples

Cub Scout sixer
As the sixer, she was responsible for organizing her group's activities during the camp.
The sixer made sure everyone in his team wore their uniforms correctly.
Each sixer reported to the Cub Scout leader at the end of the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: member - an individual belonging to a group but not leading it
  • synonym: patrol leader - the leader of a patrol in scouting organizations
