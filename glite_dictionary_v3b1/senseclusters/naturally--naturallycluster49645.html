naturally

adverb
as expected; unsurprisingly
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃɚəli / (AmE), / ˈnatʃ(ə)rəli / (BrE)
As might be expected; in a way that is unsurprising or only logical.

Examples

Naturally enough
Naturally, everyone was upset when the event was cancelled.
Naturally, the restaurant was crowded on a Friday night.
She naturally assumed that he was coming to the party as well.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obviously - in a way that is easily perceived or understood
  • antonym: surprisingly - in a way that causes surprise; contrary to what might be expected
