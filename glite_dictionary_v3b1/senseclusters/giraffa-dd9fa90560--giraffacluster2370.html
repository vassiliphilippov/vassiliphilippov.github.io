Giraffa

noun
genus of giraffes and related species
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈræfə / (AmE), / dʒɪˈræfə / (BrE)
The genus of large African mammals comprising the giraffe and, in some classifications, the okapi.

Examples

The Giraffa genus
Fossil evidence suggests that Giraffa had several extinct species in ancient times.
Scientists debate whether the okapi should also be included in the genus Giraffa.
The genus Giraffa includes the well-known giraffe found across the African savannah.

Related Terms

  • synonym: giraffe - a large African mammal with a very long neck and legs
