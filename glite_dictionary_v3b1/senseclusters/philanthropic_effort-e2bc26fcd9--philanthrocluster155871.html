philanthropic effort

noun
charitable or humanitarian activity
5.3
Pronunciation: / fɪˌlænθrəˈpɪk ˈɛfərt / (AmE), / fɪˌlænθrəˈpɪk ˈefət / (BrE)
an action or series of actions intended to promote the welfare of others, especially through charitable or humanitarian activities

Examples

Corporate philanthropic efforts
Corporate philanthropic efforts can have a profound impact on local communities.
Many organizations have made a significant philanthropic effort to support education in developing countries.
The billionaire's latest philanthropic effort focuses on providing clean water to remote villages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: altruism - selfless concern for the well-being of others
  • synonym: charity - the voluntary giving of help to those in need
