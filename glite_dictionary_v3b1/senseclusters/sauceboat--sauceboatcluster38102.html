sauceboat

noun
dish for serving gravy or sauce
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔsˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsˌbəʊt / (BrE)
A dish with a spout, often shaped like a boat, used for serving gravy or sauce at the table.

Examples

A sauceboat with gravy
After dinner, I washed the plates and the sauceboat by hand.
She poured the hot gravy from the sauceboat onto her mashed potatoes.
The elegant silver sauceboat was placed at the center of the dining table.
