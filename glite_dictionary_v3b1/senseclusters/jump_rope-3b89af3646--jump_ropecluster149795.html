jump rope

noun
game or exercise with jumping over rope
1.3
Pronunciation: / dʒʌmp roʊp / (AmE), / dʒʌmp rəʊp / (BrE)
A game or exercise in which a rope is swung over and under the body while one or more people jump over it as it passes under their feet.

Examples

Jump rope exercise
Many athletes use jump rope as part of their regular workout routine.
She learned new tricks with her jump rope and performed them at the talent show.
The children played jump rope at recess until the bell rang.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skipping rope - a British term for the rope or the game of jumping over it
