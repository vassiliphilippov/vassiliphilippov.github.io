faki

noun
Greek lentil soup
Pronunciation: / fɑːˈki / (AmE), / fæˈkiː / (BrE)
a Greek soup or stew made primarily with lentils, often served with olive oil and vinegar

Examples

A bowl of faki
On cold winter nights, my grandmother always made faki to warm us up.
Many Greek families enjoy faki as a healthy and traditional meal.
The restaurant's faki was rich in flavor and served with fresh bread.
