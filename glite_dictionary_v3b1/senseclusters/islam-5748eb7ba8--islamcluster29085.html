Islam

noun
the Muslim religion and civilization
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɪzlɑm /; / ɪˈslɑm / (AmE), / ˈɪzlɑːm / (BrE)
the religion founded by the Prophet Muhammad, based on the Qur'an, and the civilization and culture of its followers
