freedom fighter

noun
person in armed struggle for a cause
3.7 3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːdəm ˌfaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈfriːdəm ˌfaɪtə / (BrE)
a person who takes part in an armed struggle against an established authority, usually regarded as fighting for a cause such as independence or justice

Examples

A freedom fighter leader
He was celebrated as a freedom fighter after leading the revolution against the dictatorship.
Many young freedom fighters sacrificed their lives during the struggle for independence.
The government labeled them as terrorists, but locals saw them as freedom fighters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insurgent - a person fighting against an established government or authority
  • degree lower: patriot - a person who vigorously supports their country and is prepared to defend it
  • synonym: rebel - a person who rises in opposition or armed resistance against an established authority
