curbing

noun
edge between sidewalk and street
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝːbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɜːbɪŋ / (BrE)
an edge or border made of stones or concrete, forming the boundary between a sidewalk and a roadway
