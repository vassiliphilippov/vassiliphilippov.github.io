shakingly

adverb
with trembling
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪkɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪkɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that involves or shows shaking or trembling

Examples

To speak shakingly
He reached for the microphone shakingly, his nerves clearly visible to the audience.
The witness answered the lawyer's questions shakingly, still affected by the accident.
She handed over the letter shakingly, unable to hide her fear.

Related Terms

  • antonym: steadily - in a firm, stable, or unwavering manner
  • synonym: tremblingly - in a way that shows trembling or quivering
