nondisposable

adjective
not available for use or spending
Pronunciation: / nɑn.dɪˈspoʊ.zə.bəl / (AmE), / nɒn.dɪˈspəʊ.zə.bəl / (BrE)
(of assets or resources) not available to be used, spent, or disposed of.

Examples

Nondisposable funds
Some funds remain nondisposable due to legal restrictions placed upon them.
The project required the use of nondisposable assets, which could not be sold or liquidated.
You should separate nondisposable income from the money you can actually spend.
