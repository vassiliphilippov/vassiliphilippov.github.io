stainless steel

noun
rust-resistant steel alloy
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪnləs stil / (AmE), / ˈsteɪnlɪs stiːl / (BrE)
a type of steel alloy containing chromium that is highly resistant to rust and staining

Examples

Stainless steel appliances
Many kitchens have stainless steel appliances for their durability and sleek appearance.

The cutlery is made of stainless steel to prevent rusting.
