mist

noun
cosmetic facial spray
3.0
Pronunciation: / mɪst / (AmE), / mɪst / (BrE)
A light cosmetic spray, usually sold in a small bottle, used to hydrate, refresh, or set the skin, especially the face.

Examples

Facial mist
She used a hydrating facial mist before applying her makeup.
On hot days he kept a cooling mist in his bag to freshen his skin.
The brand released a new vitamin C mist that brightened her complexion after one week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: facial spray - a spray product applied to the face to hydrate or refresh the skin
  • synonym: hydrating spray - a product that delivers moisture to the skin in a fine spray
