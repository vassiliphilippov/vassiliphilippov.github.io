profiler

noun
shaping machine or tool
6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈproʊ.faɪ.lər/ (AmE), /ˈprəʊ.faɪ.lə/ (BrE)
a machine or tool used to cut or shape material into a specific profile, especially in woodworking or metalworking

Examples

Woodworking profiler

A modern profiler can quickly shape metal parts to precise specifications.
Before painting, they ran each board through the profiler to achieve the desired contour.
