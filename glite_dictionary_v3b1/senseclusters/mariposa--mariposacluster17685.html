mariposa

noun
a plant of the genus Calochortus
Pronunciation: / ˌmærɪˈpoʊzə / (AmE), / ˌmærɪˈpəʊzə / (BrE)
any of several plants of the genus Calochortus, native to the southwestern United States and Mexico, with tulip-shaped flowers having three sepals and three petals

Examples

A mariposa flower
Gardeners often plant mariposa for their unique tulip-shaped blooms.
The vibrant mariposa flowers brightened the desert landscape in early spring.
We saw a field dotted with blooming mariposa on our hike in New Mexico.
