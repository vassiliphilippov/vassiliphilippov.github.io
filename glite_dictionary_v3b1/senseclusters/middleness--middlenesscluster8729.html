middleness

noun
state of being average
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdlnəs / (AmE), / ˈmɪdlnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being average, ordinary, or neither very good nor very bad

Examples

Middleness of quality
The novel's middleness made it forgettable among so many more exciting stories.
He was frustrated by the middleness of his performance, wanting to stand out more.
Some people are content with middleness, while others always strive for greatness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: excellence - the quality of being outstanding or extremely good
  • antonym: inferiority - the state of being lower in quality or value
  • synonym: mediocrity - the quality of being average or not very good
