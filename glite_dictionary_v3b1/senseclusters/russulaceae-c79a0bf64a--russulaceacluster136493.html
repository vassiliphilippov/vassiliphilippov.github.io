Russulaceae

noun
family of gilled fungi (Russula, Lactarius)
Pronunciation: / ˌrʌs.jʊˈleɪ.si.i /; / ˌrʌsjuˈleɪsiː / (AmE), / ˌrʌs.jʊˈleɪ.sɪ.iː /; / ˌrʌsjuːˈleɪsɪiː / (BrE)
a family of fungi that includes the genera Russula and Lactarius, known for their gilled mushrooms, often with brittle gills and brightly colored caps
