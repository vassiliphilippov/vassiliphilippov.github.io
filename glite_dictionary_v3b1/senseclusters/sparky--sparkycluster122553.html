sparky

noun
lively person
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑːrki / (AmE), / ˈspɑːki / (BrE)
a lively, entertaining, or witty person who brings energy to social situations

Examples

She is a sparky
Everyone liked Maya because she was a real sparky who kept conversations fun.
At every party, there is always one sparky who gets everyone laughing.
The team needed a sparky to lift their spirits after the long meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dullard - a person who is not lively or interesting
  • synonym: live wire - a very energetic and lively person
