business district

noun
central commercial area of a city
1.0 2.1
Pronunciation: /ˈbɪz.nəs ˈdɪs.trɪkt/ /; / /ˈbɪz.nɪs ˈdɪs.trɪkt/ (AmE), /ˈbɪz.nəs ˈdɪs.trɪkt/ /; / /ˈbɪz.nɪs ˈdɪs.trɪkt/ (BrE)
The central area in a town or city where most business and commercial activities take place.
