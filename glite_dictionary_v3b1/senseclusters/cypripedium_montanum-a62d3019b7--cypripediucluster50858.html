Cypripedium montanum

noun
a North American lady's slipper orchid species
Pronunciation: / saɪprəˈpiːdiəm mɒnˈtænəm / (AmE), / sɪprɪˈpiːdiəm mɒnˈtænəm / (BrE)
A species of lady's slipper orchid native to North America, with white and dull purple flowers, found from Alaska to northern California and Wyoming.

Examples

The Cypripedium montanum flower
A photograph of the delicate Cypripedium montanum was published in the flora journal.
Botanists were delighted to discover a rare Cypripedium montanum blooming along the mountain trail.
The Cypripedium montanum is protected in several national parks due to its limited range.
