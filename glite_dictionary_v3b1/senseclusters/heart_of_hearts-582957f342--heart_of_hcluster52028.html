heart of hearts

noun
one's innermost feelings or beliefs
Pronunciation: / hɑrt əv hɑrts / (AmE), / hɑːt əv hɑːts / (BrE)
a person's true and deepest feelings, beliefs, or desires, especially those not openly acknowledged

Examples

In the heart of hearts feeling
He tried to act confident, but in his heart of hearts he was still afraid.
In my heart of hearts, I knew leaving was the right decision.
She believed in her heart of hearts that he would return one day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conscience - a person's moral sense of right and wrong
  • synonym: inmost - most deeply felt; innermost
