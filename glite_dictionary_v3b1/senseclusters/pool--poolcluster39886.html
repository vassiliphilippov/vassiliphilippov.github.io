pool

noun
swimming structure
Pronunciation: / puːl / (AmE), / puːl / (BrE)
a structure filled with water, designed for swimming or recreation

Examples

A swimming pool
The hotel has a large outdoor pool that guests can use all year round.
After school, the children went straight to the pool to cool off.
She learned to swim in the community pool when she was five years old.

Related Terms

  • synonym: swimming pool - a large artificial pool for swimming
