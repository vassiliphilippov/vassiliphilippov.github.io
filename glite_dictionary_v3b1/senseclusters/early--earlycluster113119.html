early

adjective
very young; at an initial stage of life
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrli /; / ˈɝːli / (AmE), / ˈɜːli / (BrE)
happening or existing at or near the beginning of a period, process, or development; relating to the first stages (used as an adjective)

Examples
