self-awareness

noun
system monitoring its own state

Pronunciation: / ccs5blf 59c8w5brn59s / (AmE), / ccs5blf 59c8we59n59s / (BrE)
(in computing and robotics) the ability of a system or agent to monitor and represent its own internal state, processes, or actions so it can adapt or control its behaviour autonomously.

Examples

Robot self-awareness
Researchers debate whether a robot's self-awareness requires subjective experience or only state-monitoring.
Modern AI systems can show limited self-awareness by tracking internal errors and adjusting strategies.
Engineers test autonomous cars for self-awareness so they respond safely to unexpected sensor failures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: metacognition - awareness and understanding of one's (or a system's) own thought or processing activities
  • synonym: self-monitoring - the process of observing and reporting a system's own internal state and performance
