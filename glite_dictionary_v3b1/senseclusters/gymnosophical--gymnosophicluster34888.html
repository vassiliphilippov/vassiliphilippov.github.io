gymnosophical

adjective
relating to gymnosophy or gymnosophists
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /dʒɪm.nə.ˈsɑːf.ɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /dʒɪm.nə.ˈsɒf.ɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of gymnosophy, especially the doctrines or practices of ancient Hindu or Buddhist philosophers who engaged in asceticism and meditation.

Examples

Gymnosophical teachings
His study delved into the gymnosophical traditions of Eastern philosophy.
She admired the gymnosophical wisdom espoused by ancient sages.
The gymnosophical practices of the ascetics fascinated early European travelers to India.
