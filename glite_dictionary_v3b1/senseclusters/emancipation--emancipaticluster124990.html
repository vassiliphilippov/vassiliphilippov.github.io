Synonyms

  • liberation
    3.9 13/m
    liberation is freeing people or groups from political or social oppression, while emancipation often refers to legal, formal freeing.
  • manumission
    9.9 0.11/m
    manumission is the formal act of freeing a slave or servant, while emancipation covers legal or social freeing more broadly.
  • release
    1.8 12/m
    release means setting someone or something free from physical confinement, while emancipation usually means legal or social freedom.
