effeminization

noun
making words feminine
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˌfɛmɪnəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌfɛmɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of assigning or forming feminine grammatical gender or feminine word forms in a language

Examples

Effeminization of nouns
The effeminization of certain nouns in the language followed the introduction of new grammatical rules.
Linguists study the effeminization of borrowed words as part of language evolution.
The effeminization of terms can affect how speakers perceive gender roles in language.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feminization - the process of making something feminine in form or character
  • antonym: masculinization - the process of making something masculine in form or character
