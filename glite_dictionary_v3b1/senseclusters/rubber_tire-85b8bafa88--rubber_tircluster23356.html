rubber tire

noun
tire made of rubber for a wheel
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌbər taɪr / (AmE), / ˈrʌbə taɪə / (BrE)
a tire made of a rubber ring fitted around the rim of a wheel, typically used on automobiles

Examples

A rubber tire on car
During the heat wave, the rubber tires on parked cars became hot to the touch.
The bicycle's rubber tire was punctured by a sharp piece of glass on the road.
The mechanic replaced the worn rubber tire with a new one to improve safety.
