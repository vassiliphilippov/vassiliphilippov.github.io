fan dance

noun
solo dance using large fans to reveal/conceal nudity
4.0 6.9
Pronunciation: /fæn dæns/ (AmE), /fæn dɑːns/ /; / /fæn dæns/ (BrE)
A solo dance in which large feathered or decorative fans are manipulated to conceal and reveal the dancer's body, often suggestively or to simulate nudity.

Examples

Burlesque fan dance
A fan dance often uses feathered fans to add drama and allure to the routine.
Many classic burlesque shows feature a fan dance as their centerpiece.
The performer captivated the audience with her graceful fan dance.
