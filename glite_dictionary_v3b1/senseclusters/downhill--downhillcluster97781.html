downhill

adverb
towards a worse state
Pronunciation: / ˌdaʊnˈhɪl / (AmE), / ˌdaʊnˈhɪl / (BrE)
(of a situation or condition) towards a worse or less successful state.

Examples

Things go downhill
After his injury, his health started to go downhill.
Ever since the funding was cut, the project has gone downhill rapidly.
The team's performance went downhill after the coach left.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deteriorate - to become progressively worse
  • synonym: worsen - to become worse
