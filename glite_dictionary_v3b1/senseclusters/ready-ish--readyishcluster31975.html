ready-ish

adjective
somewhat ready or prepared
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdi ɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈrɛdi ɪʃ / (BrE)
Somewhat ready; almost but not fully prepared.

Examples

Somewhat ready-ish
Are you ready-ish for your presentation, or do you need more time?
I'm feeling ready-ish, but I could use a few more minutes to finish up.
We left for the airport when we were only ready-ish, so we forgot a few things.
