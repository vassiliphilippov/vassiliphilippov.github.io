security force

noun
group hired to provide security
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkjʊrəti fɔrs / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊərɪti fɔːs / (BrE)
a group of people privately employed to protect the security of a business, industry, or organization

Examples

A private security force
A private security force was used to protect the celebrity during the event.
Companies often rely on a security force to respond quickly to emergencies and prevent theft.
The factory hired a security force to patrol the premises at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: security guard - a person employed to protect property, assets, or people
