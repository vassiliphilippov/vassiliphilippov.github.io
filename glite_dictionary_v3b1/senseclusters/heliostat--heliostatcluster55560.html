heliostat

noun
sun-tracking mirror device
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːliəˌstæt / (AmE), / ˈhiːliəʊˌstæt / (BrE)
a device with a mirror that automatically turns to reflect sunlight in a constant direction

Examples

A heliostat mirror
The heliostat kept the sunlight focused on the solar panel all day.
Engineers installed a heliostat to direct sunlight into the laboratory for experiments.
A large heliostat can provide natural light to buildings throughout the day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mirror - a surface that reflects light to form an image
  • synonym: solar tracker - a device that moves to follow the sun's position
