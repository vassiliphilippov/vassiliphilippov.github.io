arteria arcuata

noun
curved artery in the foot
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɑrˌtɪriə ɑrˈkjuːətə / (AmE), / ɑːˌtɪəriə ɑːˈkjuːətə / (BrE)
A curved artery located in the foot that supplies blood to some of the toes.

Examples

The arteria arcuata in foot
Damage to the arteria arcuata may reduce blood flow to the toes.
Surgical procedures on the foot must carefully avoid the arteria arcuata.
The podiatrist examined the function of the patient's arteria arcuata.
