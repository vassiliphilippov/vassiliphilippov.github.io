inferno

noun
an intense, uncontrollable blaze
6.2
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɜrnoʊ / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɜːnəʊ / (BrE)
a large and uncontrollable fire that burns fiercely

Examples

Raging inferno
As the oil rig exploded, workers scrambled to escape the growing inferno.
Firefighters struggled for hours to contain the inferno that engulfed the warehouse.
The wildfire turned the forest into a raging inferno visible for miles around.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blaze - a very large or fiercely burning fire
  • synonym: conflagration - a large destructive fire
