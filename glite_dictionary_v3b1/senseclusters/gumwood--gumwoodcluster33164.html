gumwood

noun
wood from gum trees, esp. sweet gum
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌmˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈɡʌmwʊd / (BrE)
the wood or lumber obtained from any of various gum trees, especially the sweet gum tree

Examples

Gumwood lumber
Builders often use gumwood as a cost-effective alternative to hardwoods.
The gumwood paneling gave the room a warm, natural appearance.
The furniture was crafted from gumwood for its attractive grain and durability.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sweet gum - a tree known for its star-shaped leaves and spiky fruit, source of gumwood
