Colombo

proper noun
capital city of Sri Lanka
4.7
Pronunciation: / kəˈlʌmˌboʊ / (AmE), / kəˈlʌmbəʊ / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Sri Lanka, located on the western coast of the island; known for its major harbor and economic significance.

Examples

Colombo city center
