Boraginaceae

noun
family of flowering plants (borage family)
Pronunciation: / bɔːˌrædʒəˈneɪsiˌiː /; / bɔːˌrædʒɪˈneɪsiˌi / (AmE), / bɒˌrædʒɪˈniːsiː /; / bɒˌrædʒɪˈneɪsiː / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants with spiral-shaped flowers and nutlike fruits, including species such as borage and forget-me-not
