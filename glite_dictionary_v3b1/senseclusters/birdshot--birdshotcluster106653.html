birdshot

adjective
covered in small holes
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜrdˌʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdˌʃɒt / (BrE)
marked by or containing many small holes or pellets, as if struck by birdshot

Examples

Birdshot metal sign
The metal sign was birdshot after years of being used for target practice.
He wore a jacket with a birdshot pattern, full of tiny holes for ventilation.
The old barn door looked birdshot from all the pellets that had hit it over time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peppered - covered with many small marks or holes
