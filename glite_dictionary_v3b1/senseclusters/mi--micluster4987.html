mi

noun
former British unit of length (nautical mile)

an abbreviation for nautical mile, a unit of length used in navigation (about 1,852 meters)

Synonyms

  • nautical mile
    4.4 0.88/m
    nautical mile is the full, standard name for the unit used at sea and in air navigation, while mi is its abbreviation.
  • international nautical mile
    4.4 0.00/m
    international nautical mile is the precise formal name based on latitude minutes, while mi is the short abbreviation used in writing.
  • naut mi
    6.8 0.00/m
    naut mi is an alternative short form seen on charts and tables, while mi is the more common abbreviation of the same unit.
  • air mile
    3.2 0.08/m
    air mile is a term used in aviation for the same 1,852-metre unit, while mi is the nautical mile abbreviation used more broadly.
  • mile
    1.0 3.18/m
    mile is a general or historical length term that can mean different miles in context, while mi specifically abbreviates nautical mile.
