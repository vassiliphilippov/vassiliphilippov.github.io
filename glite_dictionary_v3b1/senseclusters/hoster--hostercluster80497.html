hoster

noun
person entertaining guests
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊstər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊstə / (BrE)
a person who entertains or receives guests, such as at a party or as an innkeeper

Examples

Party hoster
The hoster greeted everyone at the door and offered them drinks.
As the hoster of the party, she made sure all her guests felt welcome.
Each year, a different hoster organizes the neighborhood barbecue.
The weary travelers were welcomed by the friendly hoster at the village inn.
In centuries past, a hoster would provide food and shelter to passing guests.
The old tale describes a generous hoster who helped many strangers during harsh winters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: guest - a person who is invited to visit or stay
  • synonym: host - a person who receives or entertains guests
  • synonym: innkeeper - a person who owns or manages an inn
  • synonym: landlord - a person who rents out rooms or property
