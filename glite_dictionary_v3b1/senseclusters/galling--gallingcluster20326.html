galling

adjective
annoying or irritating
4.6 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈgɔː.lɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈgɔː.lɪŋ/ (BrE)
making you feel annoyed or irritated, especially because something seems unfair or hard to accept

Examples

A galling defeat
It was particularly galling to lose after leading for most of the match.
She found it galling that her hard work was never acknowledged by her boss.
The galling delays at the airport tested everyone's patience.
