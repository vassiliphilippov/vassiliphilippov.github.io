chairwoman

noun
female presiding officer
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛrˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈtʃeəˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who leads or presides over a meeting, committee, or organization

Examples

The chairwoman leads meeting
As chairwoman of the board, she played a crucial role in major decisions.
The chairwoman called the meeting to order promptly at nine o'clock.
The chairwoman thanked everyone for their hard work and commitment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chairman - the male officer who presides at meetings
  • synonym: chairperson - officer who presides at meetings, gender neutral
