robotization

noun
becoming mechanical
Pronunciation: / ˌroʊbətaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrəʊbətaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making people, actions, or routines mechanical, emotionless, or lacking in human qualities

Examples

Robotization of people
The robotization of customer service has led to fewer personal interactions with clients.
Many fear that increased robotization in factories will result in job losses for workers.
Some critics argue that robotization can make workplaces less creative and more monotonous.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automation - the use of machines to perform tasks without human involvement
  • antonym: humanization - the process of making something more human or humane
  • synonym: mechanization - the process of introducing machines or mechanical methods
