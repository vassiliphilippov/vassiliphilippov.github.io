sunless weather

noun
lack of sunshine in the weather
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnləs ˈwɛðər / (AmE), / ˈsʌnləs ˈwɛðə / (BrE)
a period or condition in which there is little or no sunshine

Examples

Prolonged sunless weather
Prolonged sunless weather can have a noticeable effect on people's mood.
The city experienced several days of sunless weather in early November.
We canceled the picnic due to the forecast of more sunless weather this weekend.
