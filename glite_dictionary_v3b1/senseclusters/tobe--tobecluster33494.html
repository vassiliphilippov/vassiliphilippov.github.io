tobe

noun
West African garment
9.9 0.02/m
a long rectangular cloth or loose garment worn by women in parts of West Africa, wrapped around the body for everyday or ceremonial use

Examples

A colorful tobe

The market was filled with women choosing patterns for their new tobe.
During the festival, every woman wore her finest tobe to celebrate the occasion.
