feel lousy

verb
to feel unwell or miserable
1.6 5.5
Pronunciation: / fil ˈlaʊzi / (AmE), / fiːl ˈlaʊzi / (BrE)
to feel unwell, ill, or miserable; to be in a poor physical or emotional state.

Examples

To feel lousy all day
I've been feeling lousy all week and just want to stay in bed.
She started feeling lousy after eating the questionable seafood.
Whenever the weather changes suddenly, I tend to feel lousy for a day or two.
