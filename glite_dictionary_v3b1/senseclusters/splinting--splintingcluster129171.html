splinting

noun
immobilizing with a splint
Pronunciation: / ˈsplɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsplɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of immobilizing a body part with a splint to support healing or prevent movement

Examples

Splinting an arm
After the accident, splinting was used to keep his injured arm stable until surgery.
Proper splinting can reduce pain and prevent further damage to a broken finger.
The nurse demonstrated splinting techniques for first aid emergencies during the training session.

Related Terms

  • synonym: casting - the process of applying a cast to immobilize a body part
  • synonym: immobilization - the act of preventing movement to aid healing
