drab

noun
a dull brownish colour
6.8
Pronunciation: / dræb / (AmE), / dræb / (BrE)
a dull, greyish to yellowish or light olive brown colour

Examples

A drab shade
A carpet of drab covered the floor, giving the room an understated elegance.
Old army uniforms were often made in shades of drab to provide camouflage.
The walls were painted in a pale drab that made the room look even colder.

Related Terms

  • synonym: khaki - a light brownish-yellow colour
  • degree higher: olive - a yellowish-green colour
