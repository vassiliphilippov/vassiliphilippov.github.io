oka

noun
Turkish unit of weight (~2.75 lbs)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊkə / (AmE), / ˈəʊkə / (BrE)
a Turkish unit of weight, equal to approximately 2.75 pounds (1.24 kilograms)

Examples

A oka of flour
He bought three okas of flour at the market before the metric system was adopted.
Merchants used to weigh spices in okas at the bazaar.
The old recipe calls for one oka of sugar, which is roughly 2.75 pounds.
