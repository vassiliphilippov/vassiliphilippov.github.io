tourbillon

noun
whirlwind or vortex
Pronunciation: / ˈtʊrbɪˌjɒn / (AmE), / ˈtʊə.bɪ.jɒn / (BrE)
a rapidly rotating column of air, such as a whirlwind or vortex.

Examples

Tourbillon of dust
A sudden tourbillon of dust made it difficult to see the road ahead.
The dancers spun in a tourbillon of color and movement on stage.
Leaves rose in a tourbillon as the wind swept through the park.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vortex - a mass of whirling fluid or air
  • synonym: whirlwind - a column of air moving rapidly in a circular motion
