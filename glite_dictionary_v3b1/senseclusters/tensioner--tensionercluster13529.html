tensioner

noun
device for adjusting tension
4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈten.ʃən.ər/ (AmE), /ˈten.ʃən.ə/ (BrE)
a device that keeps a string, cable, belt, or chain tight and working properly, especially in machines or instruments
