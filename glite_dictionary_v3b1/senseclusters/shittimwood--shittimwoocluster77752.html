shittimwood

noun
wood used in the Biblical Ark of the Covenant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪtɪmˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈʃɪtɪmˌwʊd / (BrE)
The wood of the shittah tree, mentioned in the Bible as used for making the Ark of the Covenant and other sacred objects.

Examples

Sacred shittimwood
Ancient craftsmen sought out shittimwood for its strength and durability in sacred objects.
Biblical references often mention shittimwood as the material for holy artifacts.
The Ark of the Covenant was constructed from shittimwood, covered with pure gold.

Related Terms
