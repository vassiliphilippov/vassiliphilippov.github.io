transaction cost

noun
fee to process a digital transaction
3.9
Pronunciation: /træn.ˈzæk.ʃən kɑːst/ /; / /træn.ˈzæk.ʃən kɔːst/ (AmE), /træn.ˈzæk.ʃən kɒst/ (BrE)
a fee paid to process or include a transaction on a digital network (for example a blockchain); the amount often varies with network congestion and transaction complexity.

Examples

Blockchain transaction cost
When sending cryptocurrency, the transaction cost depends on network congestion and fee settings.
High transaction costs slowed adoption of the payment app during periods of heavy network activity.
Miners receive a transaction cost as compensation for validating each block of transfers on the ledger.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gas fee - a network-specific fee (commonly on Ethereum) charged per unit of computation for executing transactions or smart contracts
  • synonym: transaction fee - a payment made to network participants to process or prioritize a digital transaction
