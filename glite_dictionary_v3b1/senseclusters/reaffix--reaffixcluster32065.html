reaffix

verb
to attach again
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˈfɪks / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈfɪks / (BrE)
to attach or fasten something again after it has come loose or been removed

Examples

To reaffix a stamp
After checking the address, she reaffixed the stamp to the envelope.
The technician had to reaffix the warning label that had peeled off the machine.
He carefully reaffixed the poster to the wall using new tape.
