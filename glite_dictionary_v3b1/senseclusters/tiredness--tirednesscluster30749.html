Synonyms

  • fatigue
    4.2 9.15/m

  • weariness
    3.1 2.41/m
    weariness is a deep feeling of being very tired after effort, while tiredness covers any lack of energy.
  • lassitude
    9.3 0.33/m

  • lethargy
    6.3 0.98/m
