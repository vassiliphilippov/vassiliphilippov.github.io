pharmacologically

adverb
in a manner relating to pharmacology
3.8 6.1
Pronunciation: /fɑːr.mə.kə.ˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kəl.iː/ (AmE), /fɑː.mə.kə.ˈlɒdʒ.ɪ.kəl.iː/ (BrE)
In a way that relates to the science or study of drugs and their effects on the body.

Examples

Pharmacologically, the new compound shows promise in treating chronic pain.
The herb is being studied pharmacologically for its potential anti-inflammatory properties.
This substance is pharmacologically active and requires careful handling.
