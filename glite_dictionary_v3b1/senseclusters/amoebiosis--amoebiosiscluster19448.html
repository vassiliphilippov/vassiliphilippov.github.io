amoebiosis

noun
infection by disease-causing amoebae
Pronunciation: / əˌmiːbiˈoʊsɪs / (AmE), / əˌmiːbiˈəʊsɪs / (BrE)
Infection by disease-causing amoebae, often affecting the intestines.

Examples

Amoebiosis infection
Proper hygiene is essential to prevent amoebiosis in areas with unsafe drinking water.
The doctor diagnosed him with amoebiosis after the lab tests confirmed the infection.
Untreated amoebiosis can lead to severe complications in some patients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amoebiasis - another term for infection caused by amoebae
