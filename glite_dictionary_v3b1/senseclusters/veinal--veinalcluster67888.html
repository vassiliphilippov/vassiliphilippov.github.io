veinal

adjective
relating to plant veins
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈviːnəl / (AmE), / ˈviːnəl / (BrE)
relating to the veins of plants, especially in reference to patterns, tissues, or diseases involving these veins.

Examples

Veinal patterns
Symptoms of the virus included yellowing and veinal necrosis on the plant's foliage.
The botanist noted that the veinal patterns of the leaf were unusually complex.
This particular mosaic disease causes veinal discoloration and stunted growth in infected crops.
