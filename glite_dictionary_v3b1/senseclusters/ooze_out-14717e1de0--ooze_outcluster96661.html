ooze out

verb
slowly leak or flow out
3.4 0.53/m
Pronunciation: / uz a ät / (AmE), / uÀêz a ät / (BrE)
(of a liquid) to flow or leak out slowly in small amounts, often through small openings.

Examples

To ooze out slowly
A thick syrup began to ooze out of the broken jar.
Sweat can ooze out from your pores during intense exercise.
Water would ooze out between the rocks after heavy rain.

Synonyms
