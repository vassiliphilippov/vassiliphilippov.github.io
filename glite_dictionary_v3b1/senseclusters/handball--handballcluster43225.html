handball

noun
team ball sport
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈhændˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a team sport in which players use their hands to pass and throw a ball to score goals against the opposing team

Examples

Handball team sport
She joined the school handball team and quickly became one of the best players.
The fast pace of handball makes it exciting to watch and play.
During the tournament, our local club won the handball championship for the first time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: basketball - a team sport where players shoot a ball through a hoop
  • synonym: soccer - a team sport where players kick a ball to score goals
