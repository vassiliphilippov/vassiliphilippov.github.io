trend-setter

noun
algorithm or platform that creates/promotes trends
Pronunciation: / tr5bndcc5bs5bt59r / (AmE), / tr5bndcc5bs5bt59 / (BrE)
(in technology and media) a non-human agent, such as an algorithm, recommendation engine, or platform, that identifies, amplifies, or promotes trends online.

Examples

Algorithm trend-setter
The platform's recommendation engine became a trend-setter for short dance videos across the app.
Many marketers now study the streaming service's algorithms because the trend-setter shapes buying habits.
When the app's AI promoted the new slang, it acted as an unexpected trend-setter among teens.
