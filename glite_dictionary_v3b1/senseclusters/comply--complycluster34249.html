Synonyms

  • obey
    1.0 18/m
    obey is doing what a person in authority tells you to do, while comply is acting according to a request, rule, or command.
  • adhere to
    3.9 9/m
    adhere to means sticking closely to a rule or plan over time, while comply means acting to meet a request or order.
  • abide by
    3.5 5/m
    abide by means accepting and acting according to a rule or decision, while comply focuses on following a command or request.
  • follow
    1.0 50/m
    follow often means acting according to advice or a leader's example, while comply implies obeying a rule or order.
  • observe
    3.4 8/m
    observe can mean respecting a custom or law in a formal way, while comply is simply acting to meet a request or rule.
  • conform
    3.2 11/m
    conform is behaving like others or fitting social norms, while comply is acting to obey a rule or specific instruction.
