verb
to measure with rod
8.6 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / rÉ‘d / (AmE), / rÉ’d / (BrE)
to measure, mark, or probe something using a rod, especially in surveying or testing ground conditions

Examples

To rod the soil
The engineers rodded the soil to check for underground pipes before construction began.
During the survey, the team rods each section of the field to mark boundaries.
They rodded the marsh to determine the depth of the water table.
