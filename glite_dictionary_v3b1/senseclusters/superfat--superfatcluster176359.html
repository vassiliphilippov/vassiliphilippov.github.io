superfat

adjective
extra-moisturizing
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌfæt / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌfæt / (BrE)
containing extra oil or fat intentionally left unreacted to make a soap or cosmetic more moisturizing and gentle

Examples

Superfat soap bar
This handmade soap is superfat, making it ideal for people with sensitive or dry skin.
Many natural skincare brands offer superfat products to provide extra nourishment and softness.
A superfat formula can help prevent your skin from feeling tight after washing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moisturizing - helping to add or retain moisture in the skin
