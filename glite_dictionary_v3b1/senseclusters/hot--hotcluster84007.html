hot

adjective
having a high temperature
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /hɑːt/ (AmE), /hɒt/ (BrE)
Having a high or higher than desirable temperature; giving off heat or causing a sensation of heat or burning.

Examples

Hot stove
Be careful, the hot stove can burn your hand if you touch it.
I need a glass of water after being outside in the hot sun all day.
The tea was too hot to drink right away, so she waited for it to cool.
