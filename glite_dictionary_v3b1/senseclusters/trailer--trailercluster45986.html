Synonyms

  • prevue
    9.4 0.00/m
    prevue is an older, less used name for a short film advertisement made of scenes, while trailer is the modern common term.
  • preview
    2.9 9/m
    preview is an advance showing or short ad shown before release, while trailer specifically uses selected film scenes to promote the release.
  • coming attraction
    3.2 0.4/m
    coming attraction is a cinema notice for films to be shown soon, while trailer is a short promotional film clip for release.
  • sneak peek
    3.4 1/m
    sneak peek is an early, often exclusive look at part of a show, while trailer is a wider promotional clip for release.
  • teaser
    4.1 0.8/m
    teaser is a very short, curiosity-building ad that hides story details, while trailer shows selected scenes to promote release.
