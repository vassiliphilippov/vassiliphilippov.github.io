soufflé

noun
light baked dish
5.6
Pronunciation: /suː.ˈfleɪ/ (AmE), /suː.ˈfleɪ/ (BrE)
a light, fluffy baked dish made with egg yolks and beaten egg whites, often flavored with cheese, chocolate, or fruit

Examples

A chocolate soufflé
The chef served a delicious cheese soufflé as the appetizer for the evening.
She carefully folded the egg whites into the chocolate mixture to make the perfect soufflé.
A well-made soufflé should rise high and have a delicate texture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mousse - a light, airy dessert made with whipped cream and egg whites
  • synonym: pudding - a sweet or savory soft dessert or dish
