chutzpa

noun
shameless audacity or boldness
Pronunciation: / ˈxʊtspə, ˈhʊtspə / (AmE), / ˈxʊtspə, ˈhʊtspə / (BrE)
shameless audacity; impudence or bold insolence

Examples

Bold chutzpa
Her chutzpa impressed some people but offended others.
It took a lot of chutzpa for him to demand a raise after only a week on the job.
You need a bit of chutzpa to stand up to the boss like that.

Related Terms

  • synonym: audacity - boldness, especially to take risks or challenge authority
  • synonym: gall - bold and impudent behavior
  • antonym: modesty - lack of boldness or showing humility
