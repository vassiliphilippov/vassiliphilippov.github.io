nonsuspect

noun
person not suspected
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈsʌspɛkt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈsʌspɛkt / (BrE)
a person who is not suspected of being involved in a crime or wrongdoing

Examples

A nonsuspect witness
The police questioned every nonsuspect who was present at the scene.
As a nonsuspect, she was free to leave the station after giving her statement.
Investigators interviewed both suspects and nonsuspects to gather more information.

Related Terms

  • antonym: suspect - a person thought to be involved in a crime
