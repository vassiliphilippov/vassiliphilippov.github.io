org

noun
non-profit web domain
3.5
Pronunciation: / ɔːrɡ / (AmE), / ɔːɡ / (BrE)
the top-level internet domain .org, commonly used by non-profit organizations and charities for their websites

Examples

Org website address
The charity launched its new website at www.helpchildren.org to reach more donors.
Many educational resources are available on sites ending with org.
If you see a web address ending in org, it is likely a non-profit organization.

Related Terms

  • antonym: com - the top-level domain for commercial websites
  • antonym: net - the top-level domain originally for network providers
