workflow

noun
sequence or progress of work tasks
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːkˌfloʊ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːk.fləʊ / (BrE)
The sequence or rate of progress by which work is organized and completed in an organization or project.

Examples

The workflow process

The software helps manage the workflow in large organizations.
