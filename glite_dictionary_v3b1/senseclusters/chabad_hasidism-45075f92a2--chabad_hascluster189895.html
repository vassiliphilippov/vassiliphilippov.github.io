Chabad Hasidism

noun
a branch of Hasidic Judaism
9.1
Pronunciation: / xəˈbɑd həˈsɪdɪzəm / (AmE), / xəˈbad həˈsɪdɪzəm / (BrE)
A branch of Hasidic Judaism originating in Eastern Europe, known for its intellectual approach and associated with the Lubavitch movement.
