clone

noun
person almost identical to another
1.2 3.8
Pronunciation: / kloʊn / (AmE), / kləʊn / (BrE)
a person who closely resembles another, especially in appearance or behavior

Examples

Her clone at school
She was surprised to meet her clone at the party; they looked almost exactly alike.
Some critics say the movie's hero is a clone of action stars from the '80s.
The new band member was dismissed as just another clone of the lead singer.
