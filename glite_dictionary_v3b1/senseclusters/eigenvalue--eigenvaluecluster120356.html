eigenvalue

noun
value associated with a matrix in linear algebra
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪɡənˌvælju /; / ˈaɪɡənˌvæl.ju / (AmE), / ˈaɪɡənˌvæljuː /; / ˈaɪɡənˌval.juː /; / ˈaɪɡənˌvaljuː / (BrE)
(mathematics) a number (scalar) associated with a square matrix, defined by the property that there is a nonzero vector v such that Av = λv, or equivalently, subtracting λ times the identity matrix from the matrix results in a matrix with zero determinant
