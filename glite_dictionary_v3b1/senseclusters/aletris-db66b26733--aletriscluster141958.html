Aletris

noun
genus of bitter-rooted herbs
Pronunciation: / əˈliːtrɪs / (AmE), / əˈliːtrɪs / (BrE)
A small genus of bitter-rooted perennial herbs found in eastern North America and Asia, sometimes classified in the family Melanthiaceae.

Examples

Aletris herb species
Botanists recently reclassified Aletris under the Melanthiaceae family.
Several species of Aletris can be found in moist woodlands across eastern North America.
The roots of Aletris are sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
