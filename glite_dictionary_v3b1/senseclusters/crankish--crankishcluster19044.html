crankish

adjective
irritable or eccentric
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkræŋkɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkræŋkɪʃ / (BrE)
easily annoyed, bad-tempered, or showing strange and irritable behavior.

Examples

Crankish mood
His crankish attitude made it difficult for others to work with him.
After a long, stressful day, she became noticeably crankish and snapped at small things.
The professor's crankish remarks were both amusing and confusing to the students.

Related Terms

  • antonym: even-tempered - calm and not easily annoyed
  • synonym: grumpy - bad-tempered and easily annoyed
  • synonym: irritable - easily annoyed or angered
