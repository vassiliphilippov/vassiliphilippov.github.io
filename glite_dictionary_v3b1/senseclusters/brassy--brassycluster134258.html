brassy

adjective
like the sound of a brass instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈbræsi / (AmE), / ˈbrɑːsi / (BrE)
(of a sound) resembling that produced by a brass musical instrument; loud, bright, and metallic.

Examples

Brassy trumpet sound
His trumpet solo had a distinctively brassy tone that filled the hall.
She disliked the brassy quality of the recording, finding it too sharp and harsh.
The alarm rang out in a brassy blast, making everyone jump.

Related Terms

  • synonym: metallic - having a sharp, ringing sound like metal
