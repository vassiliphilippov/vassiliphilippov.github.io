Douglas Fairbanks

proper noun
American film actor noted for swashbuckling roles
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌɡləs ˈfɛrˌbæŋks / (AmE), / ˈdʌɡləs ˈfeəˌbæŋks / (BrE)
(1883–1939) An American film actor known for his swashbuckling roles in silent films and for being a major figure in early Hollywood.

Examples

Douglas Fairbanks silent films
Douglas Fairbanks starred in many classic silent films, becoming a symbol of adventure and heroism on screen.
As a founding member of United Artists, Douglas Fairbanks helped shape the film industry.
Many remember Douglas Fairbanks for his athletic performances in movies like 'The Mark of Zorro.'
