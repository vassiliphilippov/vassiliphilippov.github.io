druggo lifestyle

noun
lifestyle involving regular drug use
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdrʌɡoʊ ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (AmE), / ˈdrʌɡəʊ ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (BrE)
A way of living characterized by regular drug use and the behaviors, attitudes, and patterns commonly associated with drug users, especially in an informal or subcultural context.

Examples

The druggo lifestyle choice
After leaving school, he sadly adopted the druggo lifestyle for several years.
She managed to escape the druggo lifestyle and start fresh.
The documentary explored the realities of living a druggo lifestyle in big cities.

Related Terms
