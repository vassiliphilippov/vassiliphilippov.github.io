appeal

noun
request for money for a cause
1.6
Pronunciation: / əˈpil / (AmE), / əˈpiːl / (BrE)
an earnest or formal request for money, often for charity or a specific cause

Examples

Charity appeal
The annual appeal for winter clothing was advertised in the local paper.
The disaster relief organization launched an appeal to collect emergency funds from the public.
Their appeal managed to raise more than $50,000 for the new library.

Related Terms

  • synonym: solicitation - the act of asking for or trying to obtain something from someone
