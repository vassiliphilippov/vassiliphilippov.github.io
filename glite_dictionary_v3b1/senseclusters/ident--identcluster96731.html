ident

noun
aircraft identification signal
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪdɛnt / (AmE), / ˈaɪdɛnt / (BrE)
a code or signal transmitted by an aircraft to identify itself on radar to air traffic controllers

Examples

Aircraft ident signal

When instructed, the student pilot activated the ident feature on the transponder.
