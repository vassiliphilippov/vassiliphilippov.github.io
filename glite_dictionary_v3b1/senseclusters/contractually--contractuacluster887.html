contractually

adverb
as required by a contract
5.6
Pronunciation: / kənˈtræktʃuəli / (AmE), / kənˈtraktʃʊəli / (BrE)
in a way that is specified or required by a contract; by virtue of a contract

Examples

To be contractually bound
According to the agreement, workers are contractually entitled to paid holidays.
Partners in the firm are contractually obligated to disclose any conflicts of interest.
The company was contractually bound to complete the project within six months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: legally - in a way that is allowed or required by law
