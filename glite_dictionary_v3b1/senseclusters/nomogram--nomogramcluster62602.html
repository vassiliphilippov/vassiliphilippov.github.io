nomogram

noun
diagram for finding numeric values
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊ.məˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈnɒm.ə.ɡræm / (BrE)
A diagram representing the relationships between numerical variables, enabling values to be found by graphical means.

Examples

A nomogram diagram
An engineer can quickly estimate results by consulting a nomogram rather than performing complex calculations.
Researchers developed a new nomogram to predict the risk of disease recurrence.
The doctor used a nomogram to determine the correct dosage based on the patient's weight.
