big lug

noun
a clumsy but lovable person
Pronunciation: / bɪɡ lʌɡ / (AmE), / bɪɡ lʌɡ / (BrE)
a large, clumsy, but endearing person

Examples

A big lug person
Don't be so hard on the big lug; he means well, even if he messes up.
Everyone loves Jake; he's just a big lug who always makes us laugh.
She playfully called her brother a big lug as he stumbled carrying groceries.
