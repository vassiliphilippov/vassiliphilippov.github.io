chewable

adjective
able to be chewed
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃuː.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈtʃuː.ə.bəl / (BrE)
able to be chewed with the teeth; capable of being chewed.

Examples

Chewable vitamins
Parents often choose chewable vitamins because they are easier for kids to take.
The doctor prescribed a chewable tablet for children who can't swallow pills.
This steak is so tough that it's barely chewable.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inedible - not fit or suitable to be eaten or chewed
