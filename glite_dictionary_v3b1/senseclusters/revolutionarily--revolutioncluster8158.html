revolutionarily

adverb
in a radical way
7.7
Pronunciation: /rev.ə.ˈluː.ʃə.nə.rɪ.liː/ (AmE), /rev.ə.ˈluː.ʃə.nə.rɪ.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that supports, promotes, or participates in a political or social revolution or radical change

Examples

Revolutionarily speak for change
The activists spoke revolutionarily about transforming the entire political system.
He approached the problem revolutionarily, suggesting changes no one had considered before.
The new policy was implemented revolutionarily, breaking with all previous traditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conservatively - in a way that is cautious and avoids change
  • synonym: radically - in a way that relates to or affects the fundamental nature of something
