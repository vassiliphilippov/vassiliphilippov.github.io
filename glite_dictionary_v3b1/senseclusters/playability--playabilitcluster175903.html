playability

noun
ease and enjoyment
Pronunciation: / ˌpleɪəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌpleɪəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of a game or activity that makes it enjoyable and easy to play

Examples

Playability of a game
The playability of this board game makes it popular with both children and adults.
Developers focus on playability to ensure players have a smooth and enjoyable experience.
High playability keeps players engaged for hours.

Related Terms

  • antonym: difficulty - the quality of being hard to do or understand
  • synonym: usability - the ease with which something can be used
