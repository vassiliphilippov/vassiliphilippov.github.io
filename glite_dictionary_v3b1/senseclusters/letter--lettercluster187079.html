letter

noun
award for school sports achievement
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɛtə / (BrE)
an award, often in the form of a cloth initial, given to a student for achievement in school sports, typically in high school or college in the United States

Examples

A football letter
After a season of hard work, he finally earned his first letter in football.
She proudly wore her jacket decorated with three varsity letters.
Winning a letter was a big honor for student-athletes at their high school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: varsity letter - an award earned for excellence in school sports, typically a cloth initial representing the school
