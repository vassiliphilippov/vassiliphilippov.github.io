applaud

verb
show approval by clapping
Pronunciation: / əˈplɔd / (AmE), / əˈplɔːd / (BrE)
to show approval by clapping one's hands, especially after a performance, speech, or event

Examples

To applaud loudly
Everyone stood up to applaud the performers for their remarkable act.
She applauded her friend's courage during the ceremony.
The audience applauded loudly at the end of the show.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boo - to shout to show disapproval
  • synonym: cheer - to shout in encouragement or approval
