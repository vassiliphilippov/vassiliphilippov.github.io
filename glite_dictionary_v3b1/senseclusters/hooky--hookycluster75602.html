hooky

noun
truancy; unauthorized absence from duty
Pronunciation: / ˈhʊki / (AmE), / ˈhʊki / (BrE)
the practice of staying away from school or work without permission; truancy

Examples

Play hooky from school
He played hooky from school to go to the baseball game.
She often reminisced about the times she would skip class and play hooky with her friends.
Students who are caught playing hooky may face detention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: truancy - the action of staying away from school without permission
