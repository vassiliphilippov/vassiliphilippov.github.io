pearl-like

adjective
resembling a pearl
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈpɜːlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the qualities of a pearl, especially in appearance or luster

Examples

Pearl-like surface
The vase had a pearl-like finish that shimmered in the sunlight.
She admired the pearl-like glow of the moon on the calm lake.
The artist used paint with a pearl-like sheen to create a luminous effect.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dull - lacking brightness or shine
  • synonym: lustrous - having a shiny or glowing surface
