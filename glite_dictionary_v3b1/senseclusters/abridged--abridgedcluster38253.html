abridged

adjective
shortened (especially of texts)
Pronunciation: / əˈbrɪdʒd / (AmE), / əˈbrɪdʒd / (BrE)
(of a text) shortened by condensing or rewriting while retaining essential elements.

Examples

An abridged version
Many audio books are available in both abridged and unabridged formats.
The publisher released an abridged edition of the novel for younger readers.
We studied an abridged version of the play in our literature class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: condensed - shortened in form but retaining essential content
  • antonym: unabridged - not shortened; complete (of a text)
