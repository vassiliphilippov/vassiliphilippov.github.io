comparability

noun
quality of being able to be compared
4.4 4.7
Pronunciation: /kɑːm.pə.rə.ˈbɪl.iː/ /; / /kəm.pær.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /kəm.pə.rə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.ti/ /; / /kɒm.pə.rə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.ti/ (BrE)
