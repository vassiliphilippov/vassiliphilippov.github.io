rangy

adjective
tall and thin, with long limbs
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪndʒi / (AmE), / ˈreɪndʒi / (BrE)
(of a person) tall and thin, with long, slender limbs.

Examples

Rangy basketball player
A group of rangy teenagers were shooting hoops at the park.
He was a rangy basketball player with an impressive wingspan.
The rangy model walked elegantly down the runway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gangly - awkwardly tall and thin, loosely built
  • synonym: lanky - tall and thin, often with long limbs
  • antonym: stocky - broad and sturdily built, the opposite of tall and thin
