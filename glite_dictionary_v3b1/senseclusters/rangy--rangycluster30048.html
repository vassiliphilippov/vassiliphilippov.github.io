rangy

(of a person) tall and thin, with long, slender limbs.

Examples

Rangy basketball player
A group of rangy teenagers were shooting hoops at the park.

The rangy model walked elegantly down the runway.

Synonyms

  • lanky
    4.0 1.83/m
    lanky is tall and thin with long, loose limbs and often looks awkward, while rangy suggests long, slim, athletic limbs.
  • gangly
    6.1 0.69/m
    gangly is tall and thin with awkward, loose limbs that move ungainly, while rangy describes long, slender limbs more athletic.
  • willowy
    6.4 0.48/m
    willowy is attractively slim and graceful in movement, while rangy focuses on long, slender limbs without the graceful tone.
  • stalky
    5.9 0.00/m
    stalky is tall and thin with a stiff, plant-like look and awkward limbs, while rangy implies long, slim limbs.
  • leggy
    4.6 0.29/m
    leggy has notably long legs in proportion to the body, while rangy means generally tall with long, slender limbs.
