coll.

noun
abbreviation for collector
Pronunciation: / kɑl / (AmE), / kɒl / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'collector' or 'collected by', used on specimen labels or field notes to indicate who collected the item

Examples

coll. J. Smith
The label on the butterfly specimen included 'coll. J. Smith 1992' to show who collected it.
In the herbarium, many plant samples are marked with 'coll.' followed by the collector's name.
Researchers often use 'coll.' on field notes to indicate the person responsible for gathering the sample.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collector - a person who gathers or collects items, especially for study
