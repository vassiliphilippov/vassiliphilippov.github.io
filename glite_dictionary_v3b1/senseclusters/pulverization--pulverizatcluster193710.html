pulverization

noun
powder; substance in fine particles
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpʌlvɚɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpʌlvəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
A solid substance broken down into fine, loose particles through the process of pulverizing.

Examples

Pulverization of ore
The pulverisation of the ore made it easier to extract valuable minerals.
The baker used the pulverisation of sugar to create a smooth icing.
Years of weathering resulted in the pulverisation of the rocks into fine dust.

Related Terms

  • synonym: powder - Fine dry particles produced by crushing or grinding a solid substance
