imprudently

adverb
in an imprudent or careless manner
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpruːdəntli / (AmE), / ɪmˈpruːdəntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of care for the consequences of an action; without prudence or caution.

Examples

To speak imprudently
He spoke imprudently, not considering the impact of his words.
She imprudently invested all her savings in a risky scheme.
They imprudently ignored the weather warnings and went hiking anyway.

Related Terms

  • antonym: prudently - in a way that shows care and thought for the future
