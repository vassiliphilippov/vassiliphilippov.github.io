popcorn

noun
a corn variety that bursts when heated
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑpˌkɔrn / (AmE), / ˈpɒpˌkɔːn / (BrE)
a type of corn with small kernels that puff up and become light and fluffy when heated, often eaten as a snack

Examples

A bowl of popcorn
Farmers grow special varieties of popcorn that are different from regular sweet corn.
She bought a bag of popcorn to snack on during the show.
The smell of freshly made popcorn filled the movie theater.
She prefers her popcorn with just a little salt and butter.
The smell of fresh popcorn filled the entire theater lobby.
We made a big bowl of popcorn to eat during the movie.
