wood garlic

noun
wild plant with garlicky smell
9.0
Pronunciation: / wʊd ˈɡɑrlɪk / (AmE), / wʊd ˈɡɑːlɪk / (BrE)
A wild plant of the onion family with broad leaves and a strong garlic smell, found in damp woodlands in Europe and Asia.

Examples

Fresh wood garlic leaves
He recognized the strong scent of wood garlic during his walk in the woods.
In spring, wood garlic carpets the forest floor with its white flowers.
The leaves of wood garlic can be used in salads and pestos.
