untalented

adjective
lacking talent or natural ability
1.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtæləntɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtæləntɪd / (BrE)
not having talent or natural ability; lacking skill or aptitude in a particular area.

Examples

An untalented player
Despite hours of practice, he remained untalented at playing the piano.
She felt untalented compared to her artistic friends.
The untalented actor struggled to win the audience's attention.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gifted - having great natural ability
  • antonym: talented - having a natural ability or skill
  • synonym: unskilled - not having skill or training
