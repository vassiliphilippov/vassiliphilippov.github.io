nonambulatory

adjective
unable to walk
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈæmbjəˌlɛtɔri / (AmE), / nɒnˈæmbjʊlətəri / (BrE)
unable to walk or move about without assistance

Examples

Nonambulatory patients
The hospital provides special care for nonambulatory patients.
A nonambulatory resident may require a wheelchair for daily activities.
Special transportation is arranged for nonambulatory students attending the school.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ambulatory - able to walk or move about
  • synonym: immobile - unable to move
