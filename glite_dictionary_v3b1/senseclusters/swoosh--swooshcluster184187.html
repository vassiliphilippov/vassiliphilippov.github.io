swoosh

verb
move quickly with a whistling sound
Pronunciation: / swuʃ / (AmE), / swuːʃ / (BrE)
to move quickly with or cause to move with a rushing or whistling sound

Examples

To swoosh past
She swooshed the mop across the floor, leaving a trail of suds behind.
The train doors swooshed open as passengers waited on the platform.
The wind swooshed through the trees as the storm began to intensify.
