Synonyms

  • florescence
    8.4 0.05/m
    florescence is the full period when a plant is covered in blooms, while efflorescence means the same flowering process.
  • flowering
    2.0 3.24/m
    flowering is the active stage when a plant produces blossoms, while efflorescence is a more formal name for that blooming period.
  • blossoming
    2.5 0.54/m
    blossoming is the gentle opening and growth of flowers on a plant, while efflorescence refers to the whole flowering period.
  • bloom
    1.0 6.64/m
    bloom can mean the flower itself or its flowering time, while efflorescence names specifically the period when flowers bloom.
  • anthesis
    9.9 0.02/m
    anthesis is the technical moment when a bud opens and the flower shows its parts, while efflorescence spans the whole blooming period.
  • inflorescence
    8.7 0.01/m
    inflorescence usually names a group or arrangement of flowers on a plant, while efflorescence means the general flowering period.
