big eater

noun
person who eats a lot
Pronunciation: / bɪɡ ˈitər / (AmE), / bɪɡ ˈiːtə / (BrE)
a person who eats large quantities of food

Examples

A big eater at buffet
At the buffet, being a big eater was definitely an advantage.
Even though she's a big eater, she always finds time to exercise.
My brother is such a big eater that he often has seconds and thirds at dinner.
