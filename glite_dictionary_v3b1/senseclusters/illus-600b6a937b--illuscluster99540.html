illus.

adjective
abbreviation for illustrated
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlʌs / (AmE), / ɪˈlʌs / (BrE)
abbreviation for 'illustrated', used to show that a book or publication contains pictures or drawings

Examples

Illus. edition
The library has an illus. version of the classic novel with beautiful color plates.
Check the catalog for books marked as illus. if you want ones with pictures.
This illus. edition features detailed sketches by the original artist.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unillustrated - not containing pictures or drawings
