swipe

verb
strike with a sweeping motion
3.6
Pronunciation: / swaɪp / (AmE), / swaɪp / (BrE)
to hit or strike something with a sweeping or swinging motion

Examples

To swipe the ball
He took a wild swipe at the ball but missed completely.
She swiped at the mosquito buzzing near her ear.
The cat tried to swipe the toy off the table.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hit - to strike someone or something with force
  • degree higher: slam - to hit or strike with great force
  • degree lower: tap - to hit lightly, especially with a slight movement
