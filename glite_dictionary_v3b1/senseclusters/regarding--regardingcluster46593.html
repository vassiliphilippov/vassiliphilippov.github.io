regarding

verb
looking at carefully
Pronunciation: / rɪˈɡɑrdɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈɡɑːdɪŋ / (BrE)
present participle of 'regard'; looking at or considering someone or something carefully.

Examples

Regarding a painting
He stood in the gallery quietly regarding the sculpture for several minutes.
She spent the afternoon regarding the details of the contract before signing.
The teacher was regarding the students as they worked on their assignments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ignoring - not paying attention to something
  • synonym: observing - watching or noticing something carefully
