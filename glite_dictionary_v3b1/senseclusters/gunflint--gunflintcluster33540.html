gunflint

noun
piece of flint for sparking in flintlocks
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌn.flɪnt / (AmE), / ˈɡʌn.flɪnt / (BrE)
a piece of flint used in a flintlock weapon to produce the spark that ignites the gunpowder

Examples

A gunflint piece
Collectors of antique firearms often seek authentic gunflint as part of their restoration projects.
The blacksmith carefully shaped each gunflint for use in the colonial-era muskets.
Without a proper gunflint, the flintlock rifle would fail to fire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flintlock - a firearm with a lock that uses flint to ignite the charge
