swastika

noun
Nazi emblem; cross with bent arms
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑː.stɪ.kə / (AmE), / ˈswɒs.tɪ.kə / (BrE)
A symbol consisting of a cross with arms bent at right angles, used as the official emblem of the Nazi Party and the Third Reich.

Examples

Nazi swastika flag
A large red flag with a black swastika hung behind the speaker's podium.
Historians often discuss the origins and enduring infamy of the Nazi swastika.
The Nazi regime adopted the swastika as its official emblem in 1920.
