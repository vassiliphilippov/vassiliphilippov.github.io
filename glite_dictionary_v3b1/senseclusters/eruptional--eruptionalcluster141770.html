eruptional

adjective
caused by eruption
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrʌpʃənəl / (AmE), / ɪˈrʌpʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or caused by a sudden and violent outburst or eruption

Examples

Eruptional volcano activity
The eruptional activity of the volcano was visible from miles away.
Scientists studied the eruptional patterns to predict future volcanic events.
The eruptional force of the geyser surprised the tourists nearby.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - not showing sudden or violent activity
  • synonym: explosive - sudden and violent, like an explosion
