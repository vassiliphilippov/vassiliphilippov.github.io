harshness

noun
quality of being rough or severe
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrʃ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈhɑːʃ.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being unpleasantly rough, severe, or grating to the senses

Examples

Harshness of a voice
His voice lost some of its harshness after he calmed down.
Some critics complained about the harshness of the lighting onstage.
The harshness of the wind made it difficult to walk outside.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gentleness - the quality of being gentle or mild
  • synonym: severity - the quality of being very harsh or extreme
