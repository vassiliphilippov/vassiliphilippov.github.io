reinscribe

verb
to revise significance
9.5
Pronunciation: /riː.ɪn.ˈskraɪb/ /; / /riːn.ˈskraɪb/ (AmE), /riː.ɪn.ˈskraɪb/ /; / /riːn.ˈskraɪb/ (BrE)
to give a person, group, text, or practice a new or revised place or meaning within cultural or historical narratives

Examples
