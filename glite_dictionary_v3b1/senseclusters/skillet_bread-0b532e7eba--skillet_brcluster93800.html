skillet bread

noun
bread cooked in a skillet
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪlɪt brɛd / (AmE), / ˈskɪlɪt brɛd / (BrE)
Bread cooked in a skillet, often over an open fire; especially refers to cornbread or soda bread sometimes with ham or other additions.

Examples

Fresh skillet bread
Nothing beats the warm smell of fresh skillet bread after a long day of hiking.
She made a delicious skillet bread with bits of ham for breakfast.
The campfire dinner included beans and hot skillet bread cooked over the flames.
