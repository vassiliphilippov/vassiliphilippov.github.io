phenomenal

adjective
extraordinarily good or impressive
Pronunciation: / fəˈnɑːmɪnəl / (AmE), / fəˈnɒmɪnəl / (BrE)
extraordinarily good or impressive; remarkable

Examples

Phenomenal success
Her performance in the final was simply phenomenal and left the audience in awe.
Our team achieved phenomenal success in just a few short months.
The new restaurant received phenomenal reviews from critics and customers alike.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extraordinary - very unusual or remarkable
  • synonym: remarkable - worthy of attention; striking
  • antonym: unremarkable - not special or exceptional
