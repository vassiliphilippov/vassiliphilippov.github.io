tropical zone

noun
region between the tropics, hot climate
3.5 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / Ààtr…ëÀêp…™k…ôl zo än / (AmE), / Ààtr…íp…™k…ôl z…ô än / (BrE)
the region of the Earth's surface lying between the Tropic of Cancer and the Tropic of Capricorn, characterized by a hot climate throughout the year

Examples

The tropical zone region
Many exotic fruits originate in the tropical zone where the climate is warm and humid.
Rainforests are typically found within the tropical zone of the planet.
Scientists study weather patterns unique to the tropical zone to understand global climate change.
