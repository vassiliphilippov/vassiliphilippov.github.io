overfill

verb
fill beyond capacity
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈoʊ.və.r.fɪl/ (AmE), /ˈəʊ.və.fɪl/ (BrE)
to fill something beyond its capacity or limits, especially so that it cannot contain any more

Examples

To overfill the tank
Be careful not to overfill the tank, or the fuel may spill out.
If you overfill the glass, the water will overflow onto the table.
She tends to overfill her suitcase when she travels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overload - to put too much in or on something
  • antonym: underfill - to fill with less than enough
