electric storm

noun
storm with thunder and lightning
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪk stɔrm / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk stɔːm / (BrE)
A storm marked by thunder, lightning, and often heavy rain or hail, caused by strong rising air currents.

Examples

An electric storm with thunder
An electric storm brought heavy rain and loud thunder to the city yesterday.
The sudden electric storm knocked out power across the entire region.
We watched the sky light up during last night's electric storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thunderstorm - a storm with thunder and lightning, often with rain
