trim

noun
decorative edging on clothing
2.5
Pronunciation: / trɪm / (AmE), / trɪm / (BrE)
a decoration or edging added to clothing, especially along the borders, hems, or seams

Examples

Gold trim on a dress
She chose a jacket with gold trim to make it stand out.
The designer added velvet trim to the hem for a luxurious finish.
The wedding dress had delicate lace trim around the sleeves and neckline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trimming - decorative material added to clothing or other items
