rotator

noun
turning device
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊˌteɪtər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊˌteɪtə / (BrE)
a device or part that turns or spins around a central point

Examples

Machine rotator
The rotator in the washing machine helps to clean clothes evenly.
Engineers replaced the broken rotator to restore the machine's function.
A small rotator at the base allows the fan to turn in different directions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: motor - a machine that supplies power to make something move
  • synonym: spinner - a device that spins or rotates
  • antonym: stabilizer - a device that keeps something steady
