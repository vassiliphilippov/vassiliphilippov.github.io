musical comedy

noun
comic play or film with music and dance
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuzɪkəl ˈkɑmədi / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪkəl ˈkɒmədi / (BrE)
a play or film that combines songs, spoken dialogue, and dance with a light, humorous story

Examples

A musical comedy show
He prefers watching a musical comedy to a dramatic film.
She starred in a musical comedy set in 1920s Paris.
The theater's latest musical comedy drew large crowds every night.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: comedy - a play, film, or show that is intended to make people laugh
  • synonym: musical - a play or film with music and songs as a central feature
  • antonym: tragedy - a play, film, or show with a serious and sad theme
