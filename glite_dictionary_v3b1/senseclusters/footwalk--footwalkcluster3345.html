footwalk

verb
to walk a route
7.7 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààf ätw…îk / (AmE), / Ààf ätw…îÀêk / (BrE)
to travel or move along a route by walking, especially to inspect or check it on foot

Examples

To footwalk the route
Before the parade, organizers will footwalk the entire route to check for obstacles.
The security team needs to footwalk the premises every morning before opening.
Volunteers footwalked the park trails to ensure they were clear for the event.
