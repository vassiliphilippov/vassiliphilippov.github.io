unappetizingness

noun
quality of being unpleasant to eat
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈæpəˌtaɪzɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈapɪtʌɪzɪŋnəs /; / ˌʌnˈæpɪtʌɪzɪŋnəs / (BrE)
