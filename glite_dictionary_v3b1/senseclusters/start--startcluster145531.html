start

noun
advantage from beginning early
2.5 11/m
Pronunciation: / stÉ‘rt / (AmE), / stÉ‘Ët / (BrE)
an advantage gained by beginning something earlier than others, especially in a race or competition

Examples

An early start
Getting an early start can make all the difference in a tight contest.
She had a start on her competitors, giving her a clear lead in the race.
With a ten-minute start, the hikers were already up the trail before the others arrived.
