Qing dynasty

proper noun
the last imperial dynasty of China (1644–1912)
7.2
Pronunciation: /tʃɪŋ ˈdaɪ.nə.stiː/ /; / /kɪŋ ˈdaɪ.nə.stiː/ (AmE), /tʃɪŋ ˈdɪn.ə.stiː/ /; / /tʃɪŋ ˈdaɪ.nə.stiː/ (BrE)
