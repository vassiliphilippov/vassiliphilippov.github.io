midi

noun
mid-length skirt
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdi / (AmE), / ˈmɪdi / (BrE)
a skirt or dress with a hemline that falls midway between the knee and ankle

Examples

A floral midi
She wore a floral midi to the summer wedding.
The boutique displayed several stylish midis in the window.
A midi is perfect for both casual and formal occasions.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: maxi - a long skirt or dress that reaches the ankles
  • degree lower: mini - a very short skirt or dress, usually above the knee
