firetail

noun
bird with red tail
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəteɪl / (BrE)
a small bird native to Australia or Asia, known for its bright red or orange tail

Birdwatchers often hope to see a firetail when visiting the Australian bush.
