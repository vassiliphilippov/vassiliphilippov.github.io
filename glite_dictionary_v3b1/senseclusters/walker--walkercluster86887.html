walker

noun
device that helps babies walk
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːkər /; / ˈwɔkɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːkə / (BrE)
a frame or device with wheels or supports that helps a person (such as a baby or someone with difficulty walking) move more easily

Examples

Baby walker frame
Always keep an eye on your child while they are in a walker.
My nephew loves pushing his new walker across the living room floor.
The walker helps give babies confidence as they take their first steps.
My grandmother uses a walker to move safely around the house.
Some walkers come with a basket for carrying personal items.
The hospital provided a walker after his hip surgery.

  • synonym: rollator - rolling walking aid with a seat and wheels
  • synonym: zimmer frame - British term for a walking aid similar to a walker
