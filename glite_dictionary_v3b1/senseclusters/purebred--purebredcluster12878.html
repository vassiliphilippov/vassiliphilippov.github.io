purebred

noun
animal of unmixed breed
Pronunciation: / ˈpjʊrˌbrɛd / (AmE), / ˈpjʊəˌbrɛd / (BrE)
an animal whose parents are both of the same recognized breed, especially one with a recorded pedigree

Examples

A purebred animal
Raising a purebred horse requires careful attention to its lineage.
She prefers to adopt rescue animals rather than buy purebred pets from breeders.
The kennel club held a show exclusively for purebred dogs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: crossbreed - an animal resulting from the crossing of different breeds
  • synonym: pedigree - the record of descent of an animal, showing it to be purebred
