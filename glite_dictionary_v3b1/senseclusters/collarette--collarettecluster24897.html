collarette

noun
small decorative collar
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑləˈrɛt / (AmE), / ˌkɒləˈrɛt / (BrE)
a small collar or a collar-like band, often decorative, worn around the neck or forming a neck-like structure

Examples

Lace collarette
The vintage dress featured a lace collarette that added elegance to the neckline.
She wore a delicate pearl collarette to complement her evening gown.
The costume designer added a velvet collarette to complete the period look.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collar - a band or fold around the neck of a garment
  • synonym: ruff - a large, stiff, pleated collar worn in the 16th and 17th centuries
