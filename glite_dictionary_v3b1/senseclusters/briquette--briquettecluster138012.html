briquette

noun
compressed block of fuel
Pronunciation: / brɪˈkɛt / (AmE), / brɪˈkɛt / (BrE)
A small block of compressed charcoal, coal dust, or other combustible material used as fuel.

Examples

A briquette block
It's important to light every briquette evenly to ensure proper cooking temperatures.
The factory manufactures briquettes from sawdust and wood chips for heating homes.
We used a briquette barbecue to grill our dinner in the backyard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coal - black mineral used as fuel
