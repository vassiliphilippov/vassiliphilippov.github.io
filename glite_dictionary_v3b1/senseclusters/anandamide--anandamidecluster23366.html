anandamide

noun
endocannabinoid neurotransmitter
Pronunciation: / əˈnændəˌmaɪd / (AmE), / əˈnændəˌmaɪd /; / əˈnændəmaɪd / (BrE)
a naturally occurring chemical in the body that acts as a neurotransmitter and influences mood, happiness, and other brain functions
