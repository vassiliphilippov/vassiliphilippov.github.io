Christ's Resurrection

noun
Jesus rising from the dead after crucifixion
6.5
Pronunciation: /kraɪsts re.zə.ˈrek.ʃən/ (AmE), /kraɪsts re.zə.ˈrek.ʃən/ (BrE)
the event, according to Christian belief, in which Jesus Christ rose from the dead on the third day after his crucifixion.
