lucidly

adverb
in a clear and understandable manner
4.0 0.33/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàluÀês…™dli / (AmE), / ÀàluÀês…™dli / (BrE)
in a way that is clearly expressed and easy to understand

Examples

To speak lucidly
He spoke so lucidly that even beginners could follow the instructions.
Her arguments were presented lucidly throughout the article.
The professor explained the complex topic lucidly, making it easy for everyone to understand.
