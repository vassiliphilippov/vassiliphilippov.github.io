Parthenocissus tricuspidata

noun
species of ornamental Asiatic vine
Pronunciation: / pɑːrˌθɛnoʊˈsɪsəs traɪˌkʌspɪˈdeɪtə / (AmE), / pɑːˌθɛnəʊˈsɪsəs traɪˌkʌspɪˈdeɪtə / (BrE)
An Asiatic climbing vine with three-lobed leaves and small purple berries, often used as an ornamental plant on buildings.

Examples
