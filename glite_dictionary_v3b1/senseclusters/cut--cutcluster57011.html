Synonyms

  • omission
    5.4 2/m
    omission means leaving something out, often by mistake, while cut refers to a deliberate deletion or reduction.
  • excision
    7.9 0.01/m
    excision removes a specific part of a text as an editorial choice, while cut is a general omission or reduction.
  • cutdown
    5.5 0.00/m
    cutdown is a shortened, edited version of a film or ad, while cut is any omission, deletion, or reduction.
  • cutback
