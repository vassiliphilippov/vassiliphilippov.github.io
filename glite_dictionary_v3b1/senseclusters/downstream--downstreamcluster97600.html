downstream

adverb
in the direction with the current of a stream or river
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˌdaʊnˈstriːm / (AmE), / ˌdaʊnˈstriːm / (BrE)
in the direction away from the source of a stream or river, or with the current

Examples

Flow downstream
After the dam was built, water quality improved downstream.
Several villages are located downstream from the power station.
The boat drifted downstream, carried by the swift current.
