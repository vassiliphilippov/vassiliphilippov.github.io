locution

noun
specific expression or form of speech
8.8
Pronunciation: / loʊˈkjuːʃən / (AmE), / ləˈkjuːʃən / (BrE)
a word or phrase that is used by particular people in particular situations; an expression or form of speech

Examples

A locution in speech
Each region has its own peculiar locution for common phrases.
He often uses an old-fashioned locution that confuses younger speakers.
The legal locution was unfamiliar to most people in the courtroom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expression - a word or phrase, especially one that is particular to a group or context
  • synonym: phrase - a small group of words standing together as a conceptual unit
