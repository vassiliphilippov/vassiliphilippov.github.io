drepanocytic anemia

noun
a congenital anemia with crescent-shaped red cells
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˌdrɛpənoʊˈsɪtɪk əˈniːmiə /; / ˌdrɛpəˈnɑsɪtɪk əˈniːmiə / (AmE), / ˌdrɛpənəʊˈsɪtɪk əˈniːmiə /; / ˌdrɛpənəˈsɪtɪk əˈniːmiə / (BrE)
A congenital form of anemia in which red blood cells become abnormally crescent-shaped, most commonly affecting people of African descent; also known as sickle cell anemia.
