common

adjective
of low or inferior quality
2.5 4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑː.mən/ /; / /ˈkɑː.mɪn/ (AmE), /ˈkɒ.mən/ (BrE)
of low or inferior quality or value; lacking refinement, good manners, or taste; typical of ordinary people and sometimes considered crude or vulgar

Examples

Some critics saw the film as too common for an art festival audience.
He considered loud laughter to be common and unbecoming in polite society.

The poet wrote about the struggles of common people in everyday life.
