Synonyms

  • joyous

    joyous describes a calm, steady feeling shown in happy events, while joyful means strong, visible happiness or delight.
  • gleeful
    1.2 1.06/m
    gleeful shows a playful, bubbling kind of joy at small pleasures, while joyful covers broad, deep happiness felt or shown.
  • delighted
    1.0 9.32/m
    delighted is a pleased, thankful reaction to good news or gifts, while joyful is lasting, strong happiness or delight.
  • jubilant
    5.0 1.07/m
    jubilant shows loud celebration after success or victory, while joyful can be quiet or public happiness not tied to winning.
  • overjoyed
    2.1 0.33/m
    overjoyed feels so full of happiness it overwhelms a person, while joyful is great happiness that is strong but controlled.
  • elated
    4.0 0.81/m
    elated is high, excited happiness often from good news, while joyful is a warm, general feeling of great happiness or delight.
  • happy
    1.0 212/m
    happy is a common, everyday feeling of pleasure or contentment, while joyful means deeper, more visible happiness or delight.
  • glad
