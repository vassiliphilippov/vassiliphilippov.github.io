psychoeducational

adjective
psychology in education
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkoʊˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəʊˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to psychological support, assessment, or teaching in educational settings, especially to help with learning, behavior, or mental health

Examples

Psychoeducational support
The school provided psychoeducational support to help students with learning difficulties.
A psychoeducational evaluation can identify a child's strengths and areas for improvement.
Teachers attended a psychoeducational workshop on managing classroom behavior.
The school offers psychoeducational programs to help students understand anxiety and stress.
A psychoeducational assessment can identify learning difficulties in children.
The workshop included psychoeducational materials about coping with depression.

Related Terms

  • synonym: educational - relating to education or learning
  • synonym: psychological - relating to the mind or mental processes
  • synonym: therapeutic - relating to the treatment of illness or disorders
