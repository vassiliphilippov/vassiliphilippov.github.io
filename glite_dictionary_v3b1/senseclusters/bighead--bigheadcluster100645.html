bighead

noun
animal disease causing head swelling
8.1 8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbɪg.hed/ (AmE), /ˈbɪg.hed/ (BrE)
any of various diseases of animals, especially sheep, characterized by swelling (edema) of the head and neck

Examples

Sheep bighead disease
Farmers are cautioned to monitor their livestock for signs of bighead following prolonged rain seasons.

Treatment for bighead in animals often includes managing mineral imbalances in their diet.
