Saturnian

noun
inhabitant of Saturn
Pronunciation: / səˈtɜːrniən / (AmE), / səˈtɜːniən / (BrE)
a fictional or hypothetical being or inhabitant of the planet Saturn

Examples

A Saturnian alien
The explorers were surprised to meet a friendly Saturnian on their journey through the solar system.
In the movie, a Saturnian visits Earth to learn about human culture.
Legends spoke of ancient Saturnians who built cities among the planet's rings.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Earthling - an inhabitant of Earth
  • synonym: Martian - a fictional or hypothetical inhabitant of the planet Mars
