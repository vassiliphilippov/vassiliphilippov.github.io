harridan

noun
a bossy or scolding old woman
8.4 8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈhæ.rɪ.dən/ /; / /ˈhe.rɪ.dən/ (AmE), /ˈhæ.rɪ.dən/ (BrE)
an unpleasant, strict, or bossy old woman, often one who is scolding or vicious

Examples

A strict harridan
Everyone in the office was afraid of the harridan who ran the accounting department.
He referred to his strict neighbor as a harridan behind her back.
The children ran away from the old harridan who scolded them for playing near her garden.
