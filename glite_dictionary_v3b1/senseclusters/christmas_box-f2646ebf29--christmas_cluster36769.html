Christmas box

noun
Christmas gift given for services provided
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪs.məs bɑks / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs.məs bɒks / (BrE)
A gift given at Christmas, traditionally to people who have provided a service throughout the year.

Examples

A Christmas box gift

Many employers gave their staff a generous Christmas box to thank them for their hard work over the year.
The mail carrier received a Christmas box from several families in the neighborhood.
