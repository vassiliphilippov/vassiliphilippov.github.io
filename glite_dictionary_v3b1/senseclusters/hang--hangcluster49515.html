hang

verb
kill by hanging (with a rope)
Pronunciation: / hæŋ / (AmE), / hæŋ / (BrE)
to kill someone by tying a rope around their neck and removing support from beneath, causing death by suspension, especially as a legal punishment

Examples

To hang a criminal
Decades ago, murderers were often hanged as a form of capital punishment.
He was found guilty and hanged for his crimes in the town square.
The convicted spy will be hanged at dawn tomorrow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: execute - to put someone to death as a legal punishment
  • antonym: pardon - to officially forgive someone for an offense, erasing a sentence of execution
