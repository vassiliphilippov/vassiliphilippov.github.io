entozoon

noun
internal animal parasite (esp. worms)
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɛn.təˈzoʊ.ɑn / (AmE), / ˌɛntəˈzəʊ.ɒn / (BrE)
a parasite that lives inside the internal organs of an animal, especially referring to intestinal worms

An entozoon parasite
Infections caused by an entozoon often lead to malnutrition in livestock.
