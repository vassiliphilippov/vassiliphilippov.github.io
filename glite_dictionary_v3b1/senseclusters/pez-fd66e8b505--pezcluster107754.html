Pez

noun
candy dispenser toy
Pronunciation: / pɛz / (AmE), / pɛz / (BrE)
a small plastic dispenser, often shaped like a character's head, used to hold and release Pez candy tablets and popular as a collectible toy

Examples

Pez dispenser toy
He proudly displayed his rare Pez collection on a special shelf in his room.
As a child, she loved getting a new Pez dispenser with every holiday.
Collectors often trade Pez dispensers at conventions around the world.

  • synonym: toy - an object for children to play with
