Synonyms

  • gypsum board
    3.5 0.03/m
    gypsum board is the strict technical name for a gypsum panel used in construction, while drywall is the common everyday term.
  • plasterboard
    3.4 0.06/m
    plasterboard is the British term for the same gypsum panels, while drywall is the common American word used in everyday building.
  • Sheetrock
    3.5 0.45/m
    Sheetrock is a brand name often used like a generic word on job sites, while drywall is the general product name builders use.
  • wallboard
    3.9 0.29/m
    wallboard covers a range of interior wall panels made from various materials, while drywall specifically means plaster or gypsum panels.
