drywall

noun
panel for walls and ceilings
6.2 2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdraɪˌwɔl / (AmE), / ˈdraɪˌwɔːl / (BrE)
a large, flat board made of plaster or similar material, used to make the inside walls and ceilings of buildings

Examples

A drywall board

The contractor recommended using thicker drywall for better sound insulation.
After a leak, the drywall had to be replaced to prevent mold.
She learned how to patch a hole in the drywall herself.
The contractor installed new drywall in the renovated kitchen.
