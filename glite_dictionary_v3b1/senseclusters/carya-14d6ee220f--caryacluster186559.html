Carya

noun
genus of nut-bearing trees (hickories)
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːriə /; / ˈker.i.ə / (AmE), / ˈkɑːrɪə /; / ˈkɑː.ri.ə / (BrE)
a genus of large deciduous, nut-bearing trees (including hickories and pecans), native to North America and East Asia
