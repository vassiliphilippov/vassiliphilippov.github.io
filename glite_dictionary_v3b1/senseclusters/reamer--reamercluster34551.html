reamer

noun
citrus-juice extraction tool
Pronunciation: / ˈriːmɚ / (AmE), / ˈriːmə / (BrE)
A kitchen tool with a conical, ridged center used for extracting juice from citrus fruits by pressing and twisting the fruit against it.

Examples

Citrus reamer
A simple glass reamer can efficiently juice grapefruits and limes.
She used a reamer to extract the lemon juice for the recipe.
The reamer fits comfortably in the hand, making it easy to squeeze oranges.
