kitchen police

noun
enlisted person assigned to kitchen duties
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪtʃən pəˈlis / (AmE), / ˈkɪtʃɪn pəˈliːs / (BrE)
an enlisted person in the military who is assigned to help with cooking and cleaning duties in the kitchen

Examples

Kitchen police duties
After being late, he was put on kitchen police for the rest of the week.
Serving as kitchen police meant he spent hours washing dishes and peeling potatoes.
The new recruits rotated through their kitchen police duties every month.
