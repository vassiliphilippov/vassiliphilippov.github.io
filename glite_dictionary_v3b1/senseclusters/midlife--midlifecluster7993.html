midlife

adjective
middle-aged period
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌlaɪf / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌlaɪf / (BrE)
occurring in or relating to the middle period of a person's life, typically between young adulthood and old age

Examples

Midlife crisis
Many people experience a midlife crisis as they reflect on their achievements and goals.
She made a midlife decision to return to university and study art.
The book explores the challenges and opportunities of midlife transitions.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: elderly - being in old age
  • synonym: middle-aged - being in the middle period of life, usually between 40 and 65
  • degree lower: youthful - being young or characteristic of youth
