Petunia hybrida

noun
cultivated hybrid group of petunias
5.9
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈtuː.niː.ə/ /; / /pə.ˈtuː.njə/ (AmE), /pə.ˈtjuː.niː.ə/ /; / /pə.ˈtjuː.njə/ (BrE)
