partless

adjective
not participating
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrtləs / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtləs / (BrE)
not involved in or taking part in an activity, event, or dispute

Examples

Partless observer

The judge must be partless to ensure a fair trial for everyone involved.
He stayed partless in the family argument, refusing to take sides.
