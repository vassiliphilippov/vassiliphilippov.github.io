sparker

noun
wire screen to stop sparks from fire
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑrkər / (AmE), / ˈspɑːkə / (BrE)
a wire mesh or net designed to prevent sparks from escaping an open fireplace, stove, or smokestack

Examples

Fireplace sparker
Before lighting the fire, she double-checked that the sparker was securely in place.
He installed a sparker on the chimney to comply with safety codes.
The old cabin's fireplace was fitted with a sturdy sparker to keep embers from flying onto the rug.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spark guard - a screen placed in front of a fireplace to prevent sparks escaping
