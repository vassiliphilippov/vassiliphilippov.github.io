dakka

noun
loud repetitive noise
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdækə / (AmE), / ˈdækə / (BrE)
a loud, repetitive noise, especially the rapid sound of gunfire or impacts

Examples

Heavy dakka noise
The dakka from the machine guns filled the air during the battle scene.
We heard the constant dakka of construction work from across the street.
As the fireworks exploded, the dakka echoed through the night sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rattle - a rapid series of short, sharp noises
