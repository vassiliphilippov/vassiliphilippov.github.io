miscite

verb
to cite incorrectly
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪˈsaɪt / (AmE), / ˌmɪˈsaɪt / (BrE)
to cite a source incorrectly or inaccurately

Examples

To miscite a source
The article was retracted after the author miscited several important studies.
If you miscite your sources, your research may lose credibility.
She realized she had miscited the year of publication in her bibliography.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cite - to mention or refer to a source
  • synonym: misquote - to quote incorrectly
