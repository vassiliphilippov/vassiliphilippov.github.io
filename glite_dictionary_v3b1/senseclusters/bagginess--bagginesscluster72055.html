bagginess

noun
looseness or slackness
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbæɡinəs / (AmE), / ˈbæɡinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being loose, slack, or not fitting closely, especially in clothing or fabric

Examples

Bagginess of pants
The bagginess in the tent's fabric made it flap loudly in the wind.
Engineers checked the cables for any sign of bagginess before starting the lift.
Too much bagginess in the rope can make climbing dangerous.
The bagginess of her sweater made it perfect for lounging at home.
Some people prefer the bagginess of loose jeans for comfort and style.
He adjusted the bagginess of his jacket before heading out into the cold.

Related Terms

  • synonym: looseness - the state of not being tight or fitting closely
  • synonym: slackness - the state of being loose or not tight
  • antonym: tightness - the state of being firmly held or stretched
