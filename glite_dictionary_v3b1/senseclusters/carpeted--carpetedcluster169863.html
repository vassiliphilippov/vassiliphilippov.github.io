carpeted

adjective
covered with carpet or carpet-like layer
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːr.pɪ.t̬ɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.pɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
covered with carpet or as if with a carpet, often used in combination to describe something that appears to be covered like a carpet

Examples

A carpeted floor
After the renovation, every office was fully carpeted for comfort.
The carpeted stairs made the house feel warm and welcoming.
They walked barefoot across the soft, newly carpeted floor.

Related Terms

  • antonym: uncarpeted - not covered with carpet
