rug pad

noun
pad placed under a rug or carpet
1.9
Pronunciation: / rʌɡ pæd / (AmE), / rʌɡ pad / (BrE)
a thin pad placed under a rug or carpet to prevent slipping, provide cushioning, or protect the floor

Examples

A rug pad cushion
A quality rug pad can extend the life of your area rug by providing extra support.
Before laying down the new rug, make sure you place a rug pad underneath it.
We bought a rug pad to keep our living room carpet from sliding on the hardwood floor.
