range

noun
a connected series of hills or mountains
Pronunciation: / reɪndʒ / (AmE), / reɪndʒ / (BrE)
a line or group of hills or mountains forming a connected series

Examples

A mountain range
The Himalayas are the highest range of mountains in the world.
The Rocky Mountains are a prominent range stretching across North America.
We could see a snow-capped range in the distance as we drove.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mountain chain - a series of connected mountains or mountain ranges
  • synonym: mountain range - a line of mountains connected by high ground
