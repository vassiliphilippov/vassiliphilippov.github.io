pronation

noun
inward foot rolling
7.3
Pronunciation: /proʊ.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ /; / /prə.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /prəʊ.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ /; / /prə.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the inward rolling motion of the foot while walking or running, especially when the foot lands on the ground

Examples

Foot pronation in running
Wearing the right shoes can help reduce excessive pronation and prevent injuries.
A physical therapist can assess your walking pattern for signs of pronation.
Runners with high pronation may need special insoles for better support.
