shutterless

adjective
without shutters
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌtərləs / (AmE), / ˈʃʌtələs / (BrE)
lacking shutters, especially referring to a window or camera without a shutter

Examples

Shutterless windows
The abandoned building had shutterless windows that let in the cold wind.
Many modern cameras are now shutterless, using electronic sensors instead.
She admired the sunlight streaming through the shutterless glass doors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shuttered - having shutters, especially on windows or cameras
