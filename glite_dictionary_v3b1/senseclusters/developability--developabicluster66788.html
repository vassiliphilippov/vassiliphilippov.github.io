developability

noun
capacity for development
Pronunciation: / dɪˌvɛləpəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / dɪˌvɛləpəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being able to be developed or improved, especially in terms of land or projects

Examples

Land developability
The developability of the land was assessed before construction began.
Engineers studied the developability of the new software platform to ensure future upgrades would be possible.
Environmental concerns can limit the developability of certain rural areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: improvability - the quality of being able to be improved
  • synonym: potential - the possibility of becoming something in the future
  • antonym: undevelopable - not able to be developed
