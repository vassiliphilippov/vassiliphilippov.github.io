autoxidation

noun
reaction with oxygen
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɔtoʊˌɑksɪˈdeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəʊˌɒksɪˈdeɪʃən / (BrE)
a chemical process in which a substance reacts with oxygen from the air, often causing deterioration, spoilage, or changes in materials
