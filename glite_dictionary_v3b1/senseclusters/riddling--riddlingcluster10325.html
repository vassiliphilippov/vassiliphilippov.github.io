riddling

noun
shaking to separate
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪdlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɪdlɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of shaking or sifting to separate materials, especially to collect sediment in winemaking or mining

Examples

Champagne riddling
In traditional champagne production, riddling is used to move sediment toward the neck of the bottle.
The miners performed riddling to separate valuable minerals from the surrounding rock.
Modern machines have automated the riddling process in many wineries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: filtering - the process of removing unwanted substances from something
  • synonym: sifting - the act of separating fine material from coarse material by passing it through a sieve
