unrigorous

adjective
not strict or thorough
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɪɡərəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɪɡərəs / (BrE)
not strict, thorough, or careful in approach or method

Examples

An unrigorous method
The experiment was dismissed as unrigorous due to its lack of proper controls.
Her unrigorous approach to editing led to many mistakes in the final document.
Critics argued that the study's methods were unrigorous and unreliable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: careless - not giving enough attention or thought to avoiding harm or errors
  • synonym: lax - not sufficiently strict or severe
  • antonym: rigorous - strict, thorough, and careful
