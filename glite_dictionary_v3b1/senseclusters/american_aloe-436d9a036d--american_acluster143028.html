American aloe

noun
tropical American plant with fibrous leaves and tall flower spikes
6.0 6.2
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈmer.ɪ.kən ˈæ.ləʊ/ /; / /ə.ˈmer.ɪ.kən ˈæ.loʊ/ (AmE), /ə.ˈmer.ɪ.kən ˈæ.ləʊ/ (BrE)
A tropical American plant, chiefly of the genus Agave, with a basal rosette of fibrous sword-shaped leaves and tall flowering spikes, sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes or fiber.

The American aloe can live for decades before sending up its towering flower spike.
