blizzardy

adjective
snowy and windy
Pronunciation: / ˈblɪzərdi / (AmE), / ˈblɪzədi / (BrE)
characterized by conditions similar to a blizzard, with heavy snow and strong winds

Examples

Blizzardy weather
The mountain pass was closed due to the blizzardy conditions last night.
We stayed indoors all day because it was too blizzardy to travel safely.
Her memories of the trip include a particularly blizzardy afternoon spent by the fireplace.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - free from wind or storm
  • synonym: snowy - having a lot of snow
  • synonym: stormy - characterized by storms or turbulent weather
