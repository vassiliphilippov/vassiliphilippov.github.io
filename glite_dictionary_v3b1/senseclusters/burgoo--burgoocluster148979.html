burgoo

noun
social event where burgoo stew is served
7.5 8.9
Pronunciation: / bɝˈɡu / (AmE), / bɜːˈɡuː / (BrE)
A social gathering, especially in the southern United States, where a thick stew of meat and vegetables called burgoo is prepared and served.

Examples

Every summer, the town hosts a traditional burgoo that brings the community together.
