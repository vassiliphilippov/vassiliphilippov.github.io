projectile

noun
object fired or thrown at a target
Pronunciation: / prəˈdʒɛktʃəl /; / prəˈdʒɛk.tɪl / (AmE), / prəˈdʒɛkt.aɪl /; / prəˈdʒɛk.taɪl / (BrE)
an object that is thrown, fired, or propelled through the air or space, especially as a weapon

Examples

The soldier loaded a projectile into his slingshot before aiming at the target.

The military launched a projectile into the upper atmosphere for testing purposes.
The museum displayed a historical projectile used in early rocket experiments.
