squeakiness

noun
quality of producing a squeaky sound
4.0 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàskwiÀêkin…ôs / (AmE), / ÀàskwiÀêk…™n…™s / (BrE)
the quality or state of producing or tending to produce a high-pitched, squeaking sound.

Examples

The squeakiness of shoes
He was embarrassed by the squeakiness of his new shoes on the polished floor.
The squeakiness of the door annoyed everyone in the office.
The violin's squeakiness was due to the beginner's lack of technique.

Synonyms
