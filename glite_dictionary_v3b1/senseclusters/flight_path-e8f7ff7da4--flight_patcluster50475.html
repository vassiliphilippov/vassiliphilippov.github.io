flight path

noun
trajectory of an aircraft or projectile in the air
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: /flaɪt pæθ/ (AmE), /flaɪt pɑːθ/ /; / /flaɪt pæθ/ (BrE)
the route or path that an aircraft, rocket, or other object follows as it moves through the air

Examples
