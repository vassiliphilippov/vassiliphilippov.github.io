ludic

adjective
relating to play or playfulness
9.9 0.14/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàluÀê.d…™k / (AmE), / ÀàluÀê.d…™k / (BrE)
relating to play or playfulness, especially in behavior, activities, or attitude

Examples

A ludic attitude

Synonyms

  • sportive
    7.3 0.01/m
    sportive is lively and often means frolicking or teasing in a literary way, while ludic refers to playfulness in behavior or activities.
