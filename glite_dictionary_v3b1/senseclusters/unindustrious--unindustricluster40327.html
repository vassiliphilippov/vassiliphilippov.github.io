unindustrious

adjective
lacking industry
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈdʌstriəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈdʌstriəs / (BrE)
not having significant industry or manufacturing activity; lacking industrial development.

Examples

An unindustrious country

Many rural areas are still unindustrious and depend on agriculture for their economy.
Because the region was unindustrious, job opportunities in manufacturing were very limited.
