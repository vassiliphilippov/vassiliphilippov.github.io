upland

adjective
located in high or hilly country
Pronunciation: / ˈʌp.lænd / (AmE), / ˈʌp.lənd / (BrE)
(of land) situated in high or hilly country, away from low-lying areas.

Examples

Upland areas
The climate in upland regions is generally cooler than in the lowlands.
The river floods rarely reach the upland farms.
They hiked through the upland areas to get a better view of the valley.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lowland - situated in low or level country
