tightness

noun
strength of social norms

The degree to which social norms and rules constrain behaviour in a group or society; cultural rigidity and low tolerance for deviance.

Examples

Cultural tightness
Cross-cultural research measures national tightness by assessing adherence to social norms and tolerance for deviance.
Many immigrants described the community's tightness as comforting because everyone followed clear, predictable rules.
Policymakers argued that wartime tightness in social norms helped maintain order during the crisis.
