wake

noun
consequences after an event
3.0
Pronunciation: / weɪk / (AmE), / weɪk / (BrE)
the consequences or results that follow an event, especially a significant or catastrophic one

Examples

Wake of disaster
In the wake of the scandal, the company implemented stricter policies.
Many changes were made in the wake of the economic crisis.
The community came together to rebuild in the wake of the devastating hurricane.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aftermath - the consequences or aftereffects of a significant event
  • synonym: outcome - the result or effect of an action or event
