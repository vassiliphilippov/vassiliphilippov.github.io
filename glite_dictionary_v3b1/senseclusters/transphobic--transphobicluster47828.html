transphobic

adjective
prejudiced against transgender people
Pronunciation: / trænsˈfoʊbɪk / (AmE), / trænzˈfəʊbɪk / (BrE)
Showing or having a dislike of or prejudice against transgender people.

Examples

Transphobic attitude
Her transphobic attitude was called out during the meeting.
Many activists work to challenge transphobic policies in schools.
The company fired an employee for making transphobic remarks in the workplace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: homophobic - prejudiced against homosexual people
  • degree lower: transgender - identifying as a different gender from one's assigned sex at birth
