order Mecoptera

noun
an order of insects with beaklike heads
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdɚ məˈkɑptərə / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə mɪˈkɒptərə / (BrE)
An order of carnivorous insects characterized by long, membranous wings and elongated beaklike heads with chewing mouthparts at the tip.
