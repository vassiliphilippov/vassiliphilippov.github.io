common garden cress

noun
an annual herb used as salad green
9.9 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːm.ən ˈgɑːr.dən kres/ (AmE), /ˈkɒm.ən ˈgɑː.dən kres/ (BrE)
A small, fast-growing annual herb (Lepidium sativum) with pungent, peppery-tasting leaves, often used in salads and as a garnish.

Examples

Fresh common garden cress
Common garden cress is easy to grow in pots on a sunny windowsill.
She sprinkled fresh common garden cress over her salad for added flavor.
The chef used common garden cress as a garnish to give the dish a peppery kick.
