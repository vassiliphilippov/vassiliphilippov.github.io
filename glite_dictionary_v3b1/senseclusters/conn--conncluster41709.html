conn

verb
to direct the steering of a vessel
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɑn / (AmE), / kɒn / (BrE)
to direct or control the steering of a ship or aircraft; to give orders regarding its course

Examples

To conn a ship
She learned how to conn the vessel during her navy training.
The captain ordered him to conn the ship through the narrow channel.
The first mate was conning the ship when the fog rolled in.

Related Terms

  • synonym: steer - to guide the direction of a ship, car, etc.
