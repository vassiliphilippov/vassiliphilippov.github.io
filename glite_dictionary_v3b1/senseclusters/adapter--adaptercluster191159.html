adapter

noun
device for connecting incompatible equipment
4.6 2.9
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈdæp.tər/ (AmE), /ə.ˈdæp.tə/ (BrE)
a device that enables one piece of equipment to be used with another that would otherwise be incompatible, or allows something to be used for a different purpose than originally intended.

Examples

A plug adapter
He plugged his laptop charger into the wall with an adapter he bought at the airport.
The camera's memory card required a special adapter to fit into the computer.
You will need a power adapter to use this device in Europe.
I bought an adaptor to connect my headphones to the new laptop.
The travel adaptor lets you use your devices in different countries.
You will need an adaptor if your plug doesn't fit this socket.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adapter - alternative spelling for adaptor (device for compatibility)
  • synonym: connector - a device for joining electrical circuits together
  • synonym: convertor - a device that changes something into a different form
