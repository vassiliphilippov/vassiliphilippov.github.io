marble

verb
to make something look like marble
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrbəl / (AmE), / ˈmɑːbəl / (BrE)
to paint or stain something so that it resembles the veined appearance of marble

Examples

To marble paper
Bookbinders often marble the endpapers to add a decorative touch.
She learned how to marble fabric using traditional dyeing techniques.
The artist decided to marble the paper for a unique background effect.

Related Terms

  • synonym: marbleize - to make something look like marble, especially by painting or staining
