schizophrenically

adverb
in a disorganized way
Pronunciation: / ˌskɪtsəˈfrɛnɪkli / (AmE), / ˌskɪtsəˈfriːnɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to schizophrenia or is extremely disorganized or confused

Examples

Schizophrenically written story
The plot developed schizophrenically, making it hard for readers to follow the story.
He managed his schedule schizophrenically, jumping from one task to another without any plan.
The conversation shifted schizophrenically between unrelated topics, leaving everyone confused.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chaotically - in a completely disordered or confused way
  • antonym: orderly - in a neat, organized, or systematic way
