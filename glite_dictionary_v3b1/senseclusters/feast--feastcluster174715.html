feast

noun
something experienced with great delight
5.6
Pronunciation: / fiːst / (AmE), / fiːst / (BrE)
an event or thing that is experienced with great delight, especially appealing intensely to the senses

Examples

A feast for the eyes
The aroma coming from the kitchen was a feast for his nose as he entered the house.
The museum's latest exhibit is a feast for the senses, showcasing vibrant colors and textures.
The music festival was a feast of sound that left the audience mesmerized.

Related Terms

  • synonym: banquet - a large elaborate meal, often for celebration
  • synonym: treat - something special that gives great pleasure
