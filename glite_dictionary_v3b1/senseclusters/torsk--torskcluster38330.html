torsk

noun
edible fish related to cod
7.6
Pronunciation: / tɔːrsk / (AmE), / tɔːsk / (BrE)
A large edible marine fish of northern coastal waters, closely related to the cod.

Examples

Fresh torsk fish
Torsk can be found in cold northern waters and is prized for its firm flesh.
Fishermen in the North Atlantic often catch torsk alongside cod and haddock.
