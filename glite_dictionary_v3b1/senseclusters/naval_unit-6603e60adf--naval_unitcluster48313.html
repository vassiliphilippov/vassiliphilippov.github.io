naval unit

noun
military group in a navy
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪvəl ˈjuː.nɪt / (AmE), / ˈneɪvəl ˈjuː.nɪt / (BrE)
a military unit that forms part of a navy, such as a ship, fleet, or squadron

Examples

A naval unit patrols

Several naval units participated in the international maritime exercise.
The aircraft carrier is the largest naval unit in active service.
