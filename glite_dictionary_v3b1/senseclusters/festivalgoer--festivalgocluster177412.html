festivalgoer

noun
person at a festival
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛstəvəlˌɡoʊər / (AmE), / ˈfɛstəvəlˌɡəʊə / (BrE)
a person who attends a festival to enjoy its events and activities

Examples

Many festivalgoers attend
Every summer, thousands of festivalgoers travel to the city for the annual music festival.
The rain did not stop the enthusiastic festivalgoers from dancing in the open field.
Food trucks lined up to serve hungry festivalgoers between performances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attendee - a person who is present at an event
  • synonym: spectator - a person who watches an event or show
