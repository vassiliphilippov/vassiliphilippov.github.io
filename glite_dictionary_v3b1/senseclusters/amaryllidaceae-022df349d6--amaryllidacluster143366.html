Amaryllidaceae

noun
family of flowering plants (snowdrops, daffodils, etc.)
Pronunciation: / əˌmærɪlɪˈdeɪsiː /; / ˌæm.əˌrɪl.ɪˈdeɪ.siː / (AmE), / əˌmærɪlɪˈdeɪsiː /; / ˌæm.ərɪ.lɪˈdeɪ.siː / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants that includes snowdrops, daffodils, and narcissus; sometimes considered a subfamily of the lily family (Liliaceae)
