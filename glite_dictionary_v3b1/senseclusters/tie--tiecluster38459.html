tie

verb
finish a game with equal points
4.9 2.8
Pronunciation: / taɪ / (AmE), / taɪ / (BrE)
to finish a game or contest with the same number of points, votes, or other units as another competitor; to draw.

Examples

To tie the match
If both teams tie, they will share the trophy.
The game may tie if neither side scores another goal.
They tied the match 2-2 after an intense final.

Related Terms

  • synonym: draw - to finish a game with neither side winning; to end with equal scores
  • antonym: lose - to be defeated in a game or contest; to finish with fewer points
  • antonym: win - to achieve victory in a game or contest; to finish with more points
