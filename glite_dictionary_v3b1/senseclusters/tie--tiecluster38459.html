tie

verb
finish a game with equal points
2.8 14/m
Pronunciation: / taÉª / (AmE), / taÉª / (BrE)
to finish a game or contest with the same number of points, votes, or other units as another competitor; to draw.

Examples

To tie the match
If both teams tie, they will share the trophy.
The game may tie if neither side scores another goal.
They tied the match 2-2 after an intense final.

Synonyms
