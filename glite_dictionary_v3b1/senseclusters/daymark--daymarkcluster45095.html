daymark

noun
lighthouse identifier
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈdeɪ.mɑːrk/ (AmE), /ˈdeɪ.mɑːk/ (BrE)
a visible marker, pattern, or structure (such as on a lighthouse) that helps sailors identify a location during the day
