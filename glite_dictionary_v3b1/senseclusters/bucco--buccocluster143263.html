bucco

noun
Pirates fan or player
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌkoʊ / (AmE)
an informal term for a player or supporter of the Pittsburgh Pirates baseball team

Examples

A loyal bucco fan
Every loyal bucco wore black and gold to the big game at PNC Park.
The crowd erupted as a bucco hit a home run in the ninth inning.
She has been a proud bucco since she was a child, never missing a season.

Related Terms

  • antonym: opponent - a person or team competing against the Pirates
  • synonym: pirate - a player or supporter of the Pittsburgh Pirates
