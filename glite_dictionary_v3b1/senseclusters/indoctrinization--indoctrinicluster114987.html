indoctrinization

noun
teaching doctrines
9.3
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌdɑktrənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˌdɒktrɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of teaching or instilling a set of beliefs or doctrines, especially in a way that discourages questioning or critical thinking

After years of indoctrinization, it was difficult for people to question the official narrative.
