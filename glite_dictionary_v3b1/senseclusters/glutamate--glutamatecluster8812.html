glutamate

noun
a salt or ester of glutamic acid
Pronunciation: / ˈɡluːtəˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡluːtəmeɪt / (BrE)
a salt or ester of glutamic acid, commonly occurring in food and living organisms

Examples
