skip rope

noun
rope used for jumping exercise/game
Pronunciation: / skɪp roʊp / (AmE), / skɪp rəʊp / (BrE)
A length of rope, typically with handles at each end, that is swung around for a person to jump over as a physical activity or game.

Examples

A skip rope exercise
He used a skip rope to improve his balance and coordination.
Sarah bought a new skip rope for her daily workout routine.
The children played outside with a skip rope during recess.
