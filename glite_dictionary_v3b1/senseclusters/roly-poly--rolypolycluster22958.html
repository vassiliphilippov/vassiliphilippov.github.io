roly-poly

noun
self-righting rolling toy

Pronunciation: /ˈroʊ.liː ˈpɑː.liː/ /; / /ˈroʊ.liː ˈpoʊ.liː/ (AmE), /ˈrəʊ.liː ˈpɒl.iː/ /; / /ˈrəʊ.liː ˈpəʊ.liː/ (BrE)
