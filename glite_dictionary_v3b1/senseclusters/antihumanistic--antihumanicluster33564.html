antihumanistic

adjective
opposed to humanism
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˌhjuːməˈnɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌæntiˌhjuːməˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
opposed to or rejecting humanism or humanistic values and ideas

Examples

Antihumanistic ideas
The new law was criticized as antihumanistic by several advocacy groups.
His antihumanistic views made him unpopular among his colleagues in the philosophy department.
Some argue that the policy's antihumanistic nature ignores the needs of individuals.
The philosopher's antihumanistic perspective challenged the idea that individuals shape history.
Some critics argue that structuralist theories are inherently antihumanistic in their approach.
Her essay explored the antihumanistic tendencies in modern social theory.

Related Terms
