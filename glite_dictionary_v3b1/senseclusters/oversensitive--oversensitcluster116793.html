oversensitive

adjective
too easily upset or offended
3.1 4.6
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ˈsen.sɪ.tɪv/ /; / /oʊ.vər.ˈsen.sə.tɪv/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.ˈsen.sɪ.tɪv/ /; / /əʊ.və.ˈsen.sə.tɪv/ (BrE)
Too easily upset or offended by what others say or do; excessively sensitive or thin-skinned.

Examples

An oversensitive person
He became oversensitive to criticism after the incident at work.
Some people are oversensitive and may misinterpret your intentions.
You shouldn't be so oversensitive about harmless jokes.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: sensitive - easily affected by the feelings or attitudes of others
  • antonym: thick-skinned - not easily upset or offended
