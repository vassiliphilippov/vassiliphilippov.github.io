Too easily upset or offended by what others say or do; excessively sensitive or thin-skinned.

Examples

An oversensitive person
He became oversensitive to criticism after the incident at work.
Some people are oversensitive and may misinterpret your intentions.
You shouldn't be so oversensitive about harmless jokes.

Synonyms

  • touchy
    4.9 0.86/m
    touchy takes remarks personally and reacts strongly to criticism, while oversensitive is easily upset by what others say or do.
  • prickly
    5.7 0.84/m
    prickly seems quickly annoyed and defensive in conversation, while oversensitive feels hurt or offended by small comments.
  • tetchy
    8.1 0.03/m
