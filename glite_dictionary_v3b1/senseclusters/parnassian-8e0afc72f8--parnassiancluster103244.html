Parnassian

adjective
of poetry or poets
9.9
Pronunciation: / pɑrˈnæsiən / (AmE), / pɑːˈnæsiən / (BrE)
relating to poetry, poets, or to Mount Parnassus, especially in reference to classical or formal poetic style

Examples

Parnassian poetry style
Her Parnassian verses were praised for their elegance and classical form.
The critic admired the Parnassian qualities of his latest collection of poems.
Many poets aspired to a Parnassian ideal of artistic perfection.
