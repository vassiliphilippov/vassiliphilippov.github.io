percolator

noun
a type of coffeepot for brewing coffee
6.6 5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜr.kəˌleɪ.t̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.kə.leɪ.tə / (BrE)
a coffeepot in which boiling water rises through a tube and filters down through a basket of ground coffee, brewing the beverage

Examples

A percolator coffee pot
He prefers his morning coffee made in a percolator rather than a drip machine.
She bought a vintage percolator at the flea market for her kitchen.
The old percolator made a pleasant bubbling sound as the coffee brewed.
