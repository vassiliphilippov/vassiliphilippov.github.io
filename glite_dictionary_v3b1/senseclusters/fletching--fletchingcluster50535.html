fletching

noun
arrow feathering
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈflɛtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflɛtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of attaching feathers or vanes to an arrow to help it fly straight

Examples

Arrow fletching process
Traditional archers often do their own fletching to customize their arrows.
The museum displayed tools used for fletching in medieval times.
He spent the afternoon carefully fletching arrows for the upcoming competition.
