derangement

noun
state of disorder
8.5
Pronunciation: / dɪˈreɪndʒmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈreɪndʒmənt / (BrE)
a state in which things are disordered, confused, or out of their normal arrangement

Examples

Derangement of plans
The sudden derangement of the schedule caused confusion among the team members.
After the storm, there was a noticeable derangement of the garden furniture.
The files were in such derangement that it took hours to sort them.
The sudden change in plans led to complete derangement in the office.
After the accident, he suffered a brief period of mental derangement.
The derangement of the files made it difficult to find important documents.

  • synonym: confusion - a state of being unclear or disorganized
  • synonym: disorder - a lack of order or regular arrangement
  • antonym: order - a state in which everything is arranged properly
