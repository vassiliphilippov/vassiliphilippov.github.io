oosphere

noun
female gamete in lower plants
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊ.əˌsfɪr / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.əˌsfɪə / (BrE)
a female gamete in certain lower plants, such as algae and fungi, particularly before fertilization

Examples

The oosphere in plants
In many lower plants, each oosphere develops into a new organism after fertilization.
Scientists observed the movement of sperm cells toward the mature oosphere under the microscope.
The oosphere is fertilized by the male gamete during the reproductive cycle of certain algae.
