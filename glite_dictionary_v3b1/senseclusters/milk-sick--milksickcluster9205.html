milk-sick

adjective
affected by milk sickness
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlkˌsɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkˌsɪk / (BrE)
affected with or related to milk sickness, a condition caused by consuming milk or meat contaminated with toxins from certain plants, especially white snakeroot.

Examples

Milk-sick cattle
Historical accounts mention entire communities falling milk-sick during certain seasons.
Several settlers became milk-sick after drinking contaminated milk from local cows.
The veterinarian diagnosed the cow as milk-sick due to grazing on poisonous plants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: milk sickness - illness caused by consuming contaminated milk or meat
