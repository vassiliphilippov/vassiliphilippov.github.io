interior decorating

noun
decorating the inside of buildings or rooms
3.7 2.8
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈtɪr.iː.ər ˈdek.ə.reɪ.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈtɪə.rɪ.ə ˈdek.ə.reɪ.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
The activity or profession of decorating the inside of a building or room, especially with consideration to color, paint, fabrics, and furnishings.

Examples

Interior decorating a room
His passion for interior decorating is evident in every room of his house.
She decided to take a course in interior decorating to improve her skills.
The hotel hired a professional to oversee the interior decorating of all its suites.
