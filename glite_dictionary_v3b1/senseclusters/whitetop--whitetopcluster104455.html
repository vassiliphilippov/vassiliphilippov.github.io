whitetop

noun
plant with white flowers
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪtˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈwaɪtˌtɒp / (BrE)
a plant with white flowers, especially species in the genus Lepidium or Aegilops

Examples

Blooming whitetop plant
In early summer, the meadow was dotted with blooming whitetop.
Farmers often try to control the spread of whitetop in their fields.
The delicate white flowers of whitetop attract many pollinators.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hoary cress - a white-flowered plant in the genus Lepidium
  • synonym: mustard plant - a plant in the mustard family, often with white or yellow flowers
