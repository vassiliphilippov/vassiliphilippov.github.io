sheepdog

noun
dog bred to herd sheep
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈʃipˌdɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈʃiːpdɒɡ /; / ˈʃiːp.dɒɡ / (BrE)
a dog bred and trained to herd and guard sheep, often with a long-haired coat

Examples

A sheepdog on farm
Farmers often rely on a sheepdog to help manage their sheep.
Her sheepdog is trained to respond to whistle commands.

Among the most famous tasks for a sheepdog is guiding sheep through gates or into pens.
During the show, the sheepdog demonstrated impressive agility and discipline guiding the herd.
The farmer relies on his sheepdog to round up the flock every evening.
