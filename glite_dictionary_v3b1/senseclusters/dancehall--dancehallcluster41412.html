dancehall

noun
venue for dancing
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdænsˌhɔl / (AmE), / ˈdɑːnsˌhɔːl / (BrE)
a room or building where people gather to dance, especially a venue for Jamaican dancehall music

Examples

A crowded dancehall
The band played lively music at the crowded dancehall every Friday night.
Many young people enjoy going to a dancehall to listen to reggae and dance with friends.
The old dancehall was renovated and reopened as a popular community center.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ballroom - a large room used for formal dancing
  • synonym: nightclub - a place open at night for dancing and entertainment
