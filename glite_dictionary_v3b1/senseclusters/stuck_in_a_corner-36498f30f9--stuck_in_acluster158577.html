stuck in a corner

adjective
in a situation with no way out
Pronunciation: / stʌk ɪn ə ˈkɔrnər / (AmE), / stʌk ɪn ə ˈkɔːnə / (BrE)
in a difficult situation where there seem to be no options or ways out

Examples

Stuck in a corner alone
After losing his job and spending his savings, he felt stuck in a corner.
She tried every option, but soon she was stuck in a corner with no one to help.
Whenever I'm overwhelmed with responsibilities, I feel stuck in a corner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: between a rock and a hard place - in a situation with no good options
  • synonym: cornered - forced into a position with no escape; in a difficult situation
