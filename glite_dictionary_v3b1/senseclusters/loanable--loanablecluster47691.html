loanable

adjective
available to lend
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊnəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈləʊnəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be lent or available for borrowing, especially of money or resources

Examples

Loanable money
The library has a collection of loanable books for students.
Banks determine the amount of loanable funds they can offer each year.
Only certain equipment is loanable to employees for remote work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: borrowable - able to be borrowed
  • antonym: unavailable - not able to be used or obtained
