Taxodiaceae

noun
family of coniferous trees
9.9
Pronunciation: / tækˌsoʊdiˈeɪsiˌaɪ /; / tækˌsoʊdiˈeɪsi.i / (AmE), / takˌsəʊdiˈeɪsiːˌaɪ /; / tækˌsəʊdɪˈeɪsɪˌiː / (BrE)
a group of coniferous trees that were once considered a separate family but are now usually included in the Cupressaceae family
