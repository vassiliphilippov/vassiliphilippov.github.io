red clover

noun
a perennial clover with reddish flowers
2.6
Pronunciation: / rɛd ˈkloʊvər / (AmE), / rɛd ˈkləʊvə / (BrE)
a short-lived perennial clover with red-purple to pink flowers, commonly grown as forage for livestock

Examples

Red clover flowers
Farmers often plant red clover to enrich the soil with nitrogen and provide animal forage.
She recognized the distinctive red-purple flowers of red clover along the roadside.
The field was covered with blooming red clover that attracted many bees.
