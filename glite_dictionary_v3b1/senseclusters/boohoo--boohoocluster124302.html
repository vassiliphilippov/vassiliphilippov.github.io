boohoo

verb
to cry loudly
Pronunciation: / ˌbuːˈhuː / (AmE), / ˌbuːˈhuː / (BrE)
to cry loudly and noisily, often in a way that sounds like 'boohoo'

Examples

To boohoo loudly
The toddler started to boohoo when his toy broke in half.
She could not help but boohoo after hearing the sad news.
Instead of boohooing, he tried to stay calm and explain what happened.

Related Terms

  • antonym: laugh - to make sounds and movements of the face and body that show amusement
  • synonym: sob - to cry noisily while taking short, sudden breaths
  • synonym: wail - to make a long, loud cry of pain or sadness
