doubly

adverb
to twice the degree; twice as much
3.2 2.91/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆdÊŒbli / (AmE), / ËˆdÊŒbli / (BrE)
to twice the degree; in a twofold manner or with double intensity

Examples

To be doubly sure
His efforts were doubly rewarded when he won two awards at the ceremony.
Missing the bus felt doubly frustrating because it was raining heavily.
She was doubly grateful for their help after the long journey.
His kindness made her doubly grateful for the help she received.
She felt doubly responsible for the mistake because she had given the wrong instructions.
You should be doubly careful when handling these chemicals.

Synonyms

  • twofold
    3.7 0.55/m
    twofold often describes a doubled amount in formal writing, while doubly is the adverb speakers use to mean twice.

Related Terms

  • antonym: singly - to one degree or in a single amount
  • synonym: twice - two times; on two occasions or to two times the degree
