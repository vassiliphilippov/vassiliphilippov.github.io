post-free

adjective
with postage paid by sender
4.5 3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈpoʊst.friː/ (AmE), /ˈpəʊst.friː/ (BrE)
sent with the postage paid by the sender, so the recipient does not have to pay for delivery

Examples

If you order more than $50 worth of goods, your package will be delivered post-free.
The supplier offered to send the catalog post-free to all interested customers.
They advertised the books as being available post-free anywhere in the country.
