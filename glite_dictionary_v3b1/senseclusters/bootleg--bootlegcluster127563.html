bootleg

noun
illegally-made whiskey or liquor
8.4 0.46/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàbuÀêtÀål…õg / (AmE), / ÀàbuÀêt.l…õg / (BrE)
Whiskey or other alcoholic liquor made and distributed illegally, especially during Prohibition in the United States.

Examples

A bootleg whiskey

During Prohibition, people risked arrest to buy bootleg from hidden speakeasies.
The bootleg he offered tasted surprisingly smooth for illegal whiskey.
