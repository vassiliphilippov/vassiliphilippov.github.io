barbarian

noun
uncultured, crude person
6.4 1.18/m
Pronunciation: / b…ërÀàb…õri…ôn / (AmE), / b…ëÀêÀàb…õ…ôri…ôn / (BrE)
A person who is crude, uncouth, or lacking in culture or refinement.

Examples

A rude barbarian
He called his roommate a barbarian for eating with his hands at the formal dinner.
Her friends joked that she was a barbarian because she had never been to a museum.
Only a barbarian would ignore such beautiful works of art.

Synonyms

  • boor
    8.3 0.16/m
    a boor acts in rude, clumsy ways at social events, while a barbarian lacks culture and seems coarse and uncivilized.
  • churl
    9.9 0.08/m
    a churl behaves selfishly and speaks roughly to others, while a barbarian is crude and lacking in culture or refinement.
