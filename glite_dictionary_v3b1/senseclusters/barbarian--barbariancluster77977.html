barbarian

noun
uncultured, crude person
1.3
Pronunciation: / bɑrˈbɛriən / (AmE), / bɑːˈbɛəriən / (BrE)
A person who is crude, uncouth, or lacking in culture or refinement.

Examples

A rude barbarian
He called his roommate a barbarian for eating with his hands at the formal dinner.
Her friends joked that she was a barbarian because she had never been to a museum.
Only a barbarian would ignore such beautiful works of art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: philistine - a person who is hostile or indifferent to culture and the arts
