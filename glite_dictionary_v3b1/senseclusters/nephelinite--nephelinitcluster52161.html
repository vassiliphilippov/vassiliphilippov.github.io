nephelinite

noun
igneous rock with nepheline and pyroxene
Pronunciation: / nəˈfɛlɪˌnaɪt / (AmE), / nɪˈfɛlɪnaɪt / (BrE)
An igneous rock composed mainly of nepheline and pyroxene.

Examples

A nephelinite rock
A sample of nephelinite was collected from the volcanic region for analysis.
Geologists often study nephelinite to understand magma composition.
The distinct mineralogy of nephelinite sets it apart from other volcanic rocks.
