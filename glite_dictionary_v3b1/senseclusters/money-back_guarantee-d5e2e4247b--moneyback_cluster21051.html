money-back guarantee

noun
promise to refund if not satisfied
4.3 2.0
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌn.iː bæk gær.ən.ˈtiː/ /; / /ˈmʌn.ɪ bæk gær.ən.ˈtiː/ (AmE), /ˈmʌn.iː bæk gær.ən.ˈtiː/ /; / /ˈmʌn.ɪ bæk gær.ən.ˈtiː/ (BrE)
