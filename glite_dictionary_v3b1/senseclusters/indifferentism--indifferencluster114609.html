indifferentism

noun
lack of concern
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɪfərəntɪzəm / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɪfərəntɪzəm / (BrE)
a general lack of interest, concern, or preference about something, especially important matters

Examples

Widespread indifferentism in class
The students' indifferentism toward the election surprised their teachers and parents.
His indifferentism about the team's victory made his friends question his loyalty.
Many worry that growing indifferentism among young people could harm civic engagement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apathy - a lack of feeling, emotion, or interest
  • antonym: enthusiasm - intense and eager enjoyment or interest
  • synonym: indifference - lack of interest or concern
