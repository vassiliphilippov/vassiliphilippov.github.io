Malayan tapir

noun
large black-and-white tapir of Southeast Asia
Pronunciation: / məˈleɪən ˈteɪ.pɚ / (AmE), / məˈleɪ.ən ˈteɪ.pɪə / (BrE)
a large, herbivorous mammal native to the forests of Malaya and Sumatra, known for its distinctive black and white coloring
