Himantopus himantopus leucocephalus

noun
subspecies of stilt bird (Australia/NZ)
Pronunciation: / hɪˌmæntəpəs hɪˌmæntəpəs ˌluːkoʊˈsɛfələs / (AmE), / hɪˌmantəpʊs hɪˌmantəpʊs ˌluːkəʊˈsɛfələs / (BrE)
A subspecies of stilt found in the southwest Pacific region, especially Australia and New Zealand, characterized by mostly white plumage with black wings and a black nape of the neck.
