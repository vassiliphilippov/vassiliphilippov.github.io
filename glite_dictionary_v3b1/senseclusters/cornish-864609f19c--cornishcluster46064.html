Cornish

noun
Celtic language of Cornwall
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnɪʃ / (BrE)
a Celtic language spoken in Cornwall, England, now revived after having become extinct as a vernacular in the 18th century

Examples

Speak Cornish
He is fluent in both English and Cornish.
Many people are working to revive the Cornish language in Cornwall.
There are now Cornish classes available for learners of all ages.
