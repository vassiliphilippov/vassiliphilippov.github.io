post-communist

adjective
after the end of communism
Pronunciation: / poʊstˈkɑːmjənɪst / (AmE), / pəʊstˈkɒmjʊnɪst / (BrE)
Relating to a country, society, or period after the end of communism, especially following the collapse of a communist government or system.

Examples

Post-communist countries
Many Eastern European nations faced economic challenges in their post-communist period.
Researchers study how values shift in post-communist societies.
The post-communist government introduced sweeping reforms to encourage a market economy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: communist - relating to communism or a communist system
