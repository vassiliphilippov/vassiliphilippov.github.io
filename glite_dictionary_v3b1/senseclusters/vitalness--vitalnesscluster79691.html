vitalness

noun
state of being essential or indispensable
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪ.tl.nəs /; / ˈvaɪtlnəs / (AmE), / ˈvaɪ.tl.nɪs /; / ˈvaɪtlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely important or necessary, especially for life or success

Examples

She spoke passionately about the vitalness of protecting our environment for future generations.
The vitalness of clean drinking water cannot be overstated in any community.
