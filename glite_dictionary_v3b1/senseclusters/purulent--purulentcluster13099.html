purulent

adjective
containing or producing pus
Pronunciation: / ˈpjʊrələnt / (AmE), / ˈpjʊərʊlənt / (BrE)
containing or producing pus; characterized by the presence of pus.

Examples

Purulent sore
A purulent sore can be a sign of a bacterial infection.
The doctor examined the purulent discharge from the infected wound.
Treatment was required for the patient's purulent abscess.

Related Terms

  • synonym: suppurative - producing or associated with the generation of pus
