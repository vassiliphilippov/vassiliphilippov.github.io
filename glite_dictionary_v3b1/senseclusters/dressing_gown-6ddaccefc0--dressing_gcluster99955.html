dressing gown

noun
long robe for lounging or before dressing
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɛsɪŋ ɡaʊn / (AmE), / ˈdrɛsɪŋ ɡaʊn / (BrE)
a long, loose robe worn over nightwear, typically before getting dressed or while relaxing at home

Examples

A dressing gown at home
After a long bath, he relaxed in his favorite dressing gown and read the newspaper.
Her blue silk dressing gown looked both comfortable and elegant.
She slipped on her dressing gown before going downstairs to make coffee.
