overattachment

noun
excessive emotional bond
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərəˈtætʃmənt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərəˈtætʃmənt / (BrE)
a state of being excessively emotionally connected or dependent on someone or something

Examples

Emotional overattachment
His overattachment to his childhood home made it hard for him to move away.
Parents should be careful to avoid overattachment that might hinder a child's independence.
The therapist discussed the risks of overattachment to material possessions.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: attachment - an emotional connection or bond to someone or something
  • antonym: detachment - a lack of emotional involvement or connection
