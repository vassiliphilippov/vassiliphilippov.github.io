voice

noun
the ability to speak
4.7 1.0
Pronunciation: /vɔɪs/ (AmE), /vɔɪs/ (BrE)
the sound produced by a person when speaking or singing; the ability or quality of producing speech or song

Examples

A strong voice
A singer relies on a strong voice to perform on stage.
After the flu, he lost his voice and could barely whisper for days.
She raised her voice to address the entire auditorium clearly.
Many talented voices auditioned for the choir last week.
She is the leading voice in the opera company this season.
The conductor praised the young voice for her powerful performance.
After cheering all day, his voice was nearly gone.
He recognized his mother's voice on the other end of the phone.
Her voice was calm and soothing throughout the interview.

  • antonym: silence - the state or fact of not speaking
  • synonym: singer - a person who sings, especially professionally
  • synonym: speech - the act or ability of speaking
  • synonym: tone - the quality of a person's voice expressing a particular feeling or mood
