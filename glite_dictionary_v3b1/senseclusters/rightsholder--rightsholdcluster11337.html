rightsholder

noun
legal owner of rights
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪtˌhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈraɪtˌhəʊldə / (BrE)
a person or organization that legally owns rights to something, such as intellectual property or creative works

Examples

The rightsholder approves use
The rightsholder must approve any commercial use of the photograph.
Negotiations with the rightsholder delayed the release of the new edition.
Streaming services need agreements with every rightsholder before making music available online.

Related Terms

  • synonym: copyright holder - a person or entity that owns the copyright to a work
  • antonym: licensee - a person or company that has permission to use rights owned by someone else
