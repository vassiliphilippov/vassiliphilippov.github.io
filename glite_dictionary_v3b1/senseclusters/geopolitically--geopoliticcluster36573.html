geopolitically

adverb
in terms of global politics
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiːoʊpəˈlɪtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːəʊpəˈlɪtɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to the politics of geography or international relations

Examples

To be geopolitically important
The country is geopolitically significant because of its location between two major powers.
Decisions made in this region are often influenced geopolitically by neighboring countries.
The alliance was formed geopolitically to counterbalance the influence of rival nations.
