Juncus

proper noun
genus of marsh plants (rushes)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌŋkəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋkəs / (BrE)
A genus of perennial tufted marsh plants in the family Juncaceae, commonly known as rushes, found in temperate regions.

Examples

Juncus marsh plants
Juncus species are commonly found in wetlands and marshy environments worldwide.
