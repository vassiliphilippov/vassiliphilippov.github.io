walled

adjective
having a wall
Pronunciation: / wɔld / (AmE), / wɔːld / (BrE)
having a wall or thin outer layer, especially referring to biological structures such as cysts or cavities

Examples

Walled cyst
The doctor identified a walled cyst during the ultrasound examination.
Some bacteria are walled, while others lack a protective outer layer.
A walled cavity can help contain infection within a specific area of the body.

Related Terms

  • synonym: encapsulated - enclosed within a capsule or membrane
  • antonym: wall-less - lacking a wall or outer layer
