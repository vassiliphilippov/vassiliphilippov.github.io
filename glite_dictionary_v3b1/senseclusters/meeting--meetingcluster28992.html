meeting

noun
unexpected encounter or convergence
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmitɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
an occasion when people meet, especially by chance or unexpectedly

Examples

A chance meeting
Our chance meeting at the train station completely changed my travel plans.
She recalled a brief meeting with the author at a bookstore years ago.
Their unexpected meeting in the city felt almost like fate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: encounter - a meeting, especially one that happens by chance
  • antonym: parting - the act of leaving or separating after meeting
