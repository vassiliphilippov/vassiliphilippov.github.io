top-secret

adjective
classified at the highest security level
5.9 1.60/m
Pronunciation: / t…ëp ÀàsiÀêkr…ôt / (AmE), / t…íp ÀàsiÀêkr…ôt / (BrE)
classified at the highest level of security, so that only very few people are allowed to know about it

Examples

A top-secret mission
He was working on a top-secret mission abroad.
Only a handful of officials have access to top-secret files.
The government issued a top-secret report on national security.

Synonyms

  • top secret
    4.2 1.68/m

  • super-secret
    7.4 0.13/m

  • ultrasecret
    8.4 0.01/m
    ultrasecret is a single compact word that sounds rare or nonstandard, while top-secret is the common official security term.
