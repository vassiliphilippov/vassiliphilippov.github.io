puggle

noun
young monotreme or dog breed
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌɡəl / (AmE), / ˈpʌɡəl / (BrE)
a young echidna or platypus, or a dog that is a cross between a pug and a beagle

Examples

A puggle puppy
The zoo's new puggle is being cared for by the staff.
She adopted a playful puggle from the animal shelter last month.
A baby echidna, called a puggle, is rarely seen in the wild.

Related Terms

  • synonym: echidna - a spiny egg-laying mammal native to Australia and New Guinea
  • synonym: platypus - a small egg-laying mammal with a bill, native to Australia
  • synonym: puppy - a young dog
