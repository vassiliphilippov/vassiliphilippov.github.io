golden calf

noun
idol or object of undeserved worship
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɡoʊldən ˈkæf /; / ˈɡoʊldən kæf / (AmE), / ˌɡəʊldən ˈkɑːf /; / ˈɡəʊldən kɑːf / (BrE)
something (originally from the Bible) that is worshipped or valued too much, especially money or material things

Examples

The golden calf idol
In his speech, the pastor warned against making technology a modern golden calf.
Some people treat money as a golden calf, pursuing it above all else.
The film critic accused Hollywood of worshipping the box office as its new golden calf.
His pursuit of status turned into a golden calf that drove all his decisions.
In some cultures, celebrity has become a golden calf that people strive to emulate.
The novel critiques society's worship of the golden calf of success.

Related Terms

  • synonym: idol - an image or representation worshipped as a god
