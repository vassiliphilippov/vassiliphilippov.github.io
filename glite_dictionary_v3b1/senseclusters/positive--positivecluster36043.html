positive

adjective
showing presence of a condition (positive test result)
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːzətɪv / (AmE), / ˈpɒzətɪv / (BrE)
(of a test result) indicating the presence or existence of a particular condition, disease, or substance.

Examples

Positive test result
A positive biopsy confirmed the diagnosis of cancer.
His blood sample was positive for antibodies against the virus.
The patient received a positive test result for influenza.

Related Terms

  • antonym: negative - (of a test result) indicating the absence or non-existence of a suspected condition or pathogen
