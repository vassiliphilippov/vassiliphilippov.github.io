retroflexion

noun
the act of bending backward
7.5 8.7
Pronunciation: /re.troʊ.ˈflek.ʃən/ (AmE), /re.trəʊ.ˈflek.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act or condition of bending or turning a body part or organ backward

Examples

Retroflexion of the uterus
During the examination, the doctor noted a slight retroflexion of the uterus.
Some yoga poses require a gentle retroflexion of the neck or back.
The patient's back pain was caused by excessive retroflexion of the spine.
Abnormal retroflexion of the spine may cause discomfort or pain.
Surgical intervention might be required to correct severe retroflexion of internal organs.
The doctor diagnosed a retroflexion of the uterus during the examination.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extension - the act of straightening a limb or joint
  • synonym: flexion - the act of bending a limb or joint
  • synonym: retroversion - a turning backward, especially of an organ
