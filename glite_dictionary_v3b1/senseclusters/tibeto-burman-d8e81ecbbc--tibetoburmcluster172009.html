Tibeto-Burman

noun
branch of the Sino-Tibetan language family
8.5
Pronunciation: / tɪˌbɛtoʊ ˈbɜrmən / (AmE), / tɪˌbɛtəʊ ˈbɜːmən / (BrE)
A branch of the Sino-Tibetan family of languages spoken in regions from Tibet to the Malay Peninsula.

Examples

Tibeto-Burman language family
Many languages in northeastern India belong to the Tibeto-Burman branch.
Scholars have debated the classification of several Tibeto-Burman languages.
The spread of Tibeto-Burman languages influenced many cultures in South Asia.
