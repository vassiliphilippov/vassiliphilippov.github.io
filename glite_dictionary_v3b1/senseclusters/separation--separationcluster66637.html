separation

noun
distance between things
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɛpəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
a space or gap between two things; the distance that keeps things apart

Examples

Separation between wires
Engineers ensured a safe separation between the cables to prevent interference.
The separation between the two shelves was large enough to fit a vase.
There must be some separation between electrical and gas lines for safety.
Light filtered through the separation between the curtains early in the morning.
The separation between the two buildings was just wide enough for a person to walk through.
They repaired the separation in the fence to prevent animals from escaping.

  • synonym: interval - a space between two points or objects
