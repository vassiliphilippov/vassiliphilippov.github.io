condole with

verb
express sympathy to someone
Pronunciation: / kənˈdoʊl wɪθ / (AmE), / kənˈdəʊl wɪð / (BrE)
to express sympathy and sorrow to someone, especially for misfortune or loss

Examples

To condole with family
Many people visited to condole with the family after their loss.
Neighbors gathered to condole with him and offer their support.
She wrote a letter to condole with her friend on the passing of his father.
