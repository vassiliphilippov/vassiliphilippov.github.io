foxhunt

noun
sport of hunting foxes with hounds and horses
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑksˌhʌnt / (AmE), / ˈfɒksˌhʌnt / (BrE)
an organized sport in which riders on horseback follow hounds in pursuit of a fox

Examples

A traditional foxhunt
Every autumn, a traditional foxhunt takes place in the English countryside.
She participated in her first foxhunt last weekend and found it challenging.
The debate over banning the foxhunt continues to divide opinion in rural communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fox hunting - the practice or sport of hunting foxes with trained hounds
