pani

noun
Polish title for women
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpɑː.niː/ /; / /ˈpæ.ni/ (AmE), /ˈpɑː.niː/ /; / /ˈpæ.ni/ (BrE)
a Polish word used as a polite form of address or title for a woman, similar to 'Mrs.' or 'Ms.' in English

Examples

Pani Kowalska
In Poland, it is respectful to address a woman as pani followed by her last name.
The teacher was always called pani Kowalska by her students.
When entering the office, he greeted the receptionist with a polite pani.
