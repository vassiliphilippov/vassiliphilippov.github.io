superabound

verb
to exist in excess
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərəˈbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˌsuːpərəˈbaʊnd / (BrE)
to exist or be present in extremely large quantities or numbers

Examples

Apples superabound in orchards
During the harvest season, apples superabound in the orchards, filling every basket to the brim.
In the rainforest, plant and animal species superabound, making it one of the most diverse ecosystems.
Opportunities to learn and grow superabound at this innovative university.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abound - to exist in large numbers or amounts
  • antonym: lack - to be without or not have enough of something
  • synonym: overflow - to be so full that the contents go over the edge
