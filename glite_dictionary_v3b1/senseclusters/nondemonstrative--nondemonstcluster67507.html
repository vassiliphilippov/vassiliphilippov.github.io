nondemonstrative

adjective
not openly emotional
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑndɪˈmɑnstrətɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒndɪˈmɒnstrətɪv / (BrE)
not showing strong feelings or emotions, or not intended to prove something

Examples

Nondemonstrative person
Despite the exciting news, he remained nondemonstrative and calm.
Her nondemonstrative nature made it hard for others to know how she felt.
The teacher's nondemonstrative style kept the classroom atmosphere very formal.
