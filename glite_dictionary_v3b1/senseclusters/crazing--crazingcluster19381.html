crazing

noun
fine surface cracks
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkreɪ.zɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈkreɪ.zɪŋ/ (BrE)
a network of fine cracks that appears on the surface of materials like pottery, glass, or glaze

Examples

Crazing on pottery

Prolonged exposure to sunlight can cause crazing on painted surfaces.
