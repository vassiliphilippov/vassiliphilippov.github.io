catastrophically

adverb
in a disastrous manner
7.4 7.2
Pronunciation: / kəˌtæstrəˈfɪkəli / (AmE), / kəˌtæstrəˈfɪkəli / (BrE)

Examples

To fail catastrophically

The building collapsed catastrophically, leaving nothing but rubble behind.
Their plan went catastrophically wrong due to unforeseen circumstances.
