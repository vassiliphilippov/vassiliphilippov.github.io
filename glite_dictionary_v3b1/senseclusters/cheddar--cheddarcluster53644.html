cheddar

verb
to process cheese curds
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃe.dər/ (AmE), /ˈtʃe.də/ /; / /ˈtʃe.dər/ (BrE)
to perform the process in cheese-making where curds are cut, stacked, and turned to expel whey and develop texture.

Examples

To cheddar curds
The cheesemaker spent hours carefully cheddaring the curds to achieve the perfect texture.
Proper cheddaring is essential for making traditional English cheddar cheese.
After cheddaring, the curds are milled and salted before pressing.
