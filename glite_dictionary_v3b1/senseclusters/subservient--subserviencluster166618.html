subservient

adjective
serving as a means or aid
7.3
Pronunciation: / səbˈsɜːr.vi.ənt / (AmE), / səbˈsɜː.vi.ənt / (BrE)
serving or acting as a means or aid; useful in achieving a purpose

Examples

Subservient to a goal
Education is often seen as subservient to economic development.
Her careful planning was subservient to the project’s eventual success.
The tool was merely subservient to his larger goals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: instrumental - serving as a means or agent in achieving something
