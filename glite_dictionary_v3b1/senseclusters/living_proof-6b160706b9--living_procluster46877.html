living proof

noun
person or thing showing something is true
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪvɪŋ pruːf / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ pruːf / (BrE)
a person or thing that clearly shows something is true or possible, especially by their own existence or experience

Examples

Living proof of success
His recovery after the accident is living proof of modern medicine's capabilities.
Our company is living proof that small businesses can survive tough times.
She is living proof that you don't need a college degree to be successful.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evidence - something that shows, proves, or gives reason to believe something is true
