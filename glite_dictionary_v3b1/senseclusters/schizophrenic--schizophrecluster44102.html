schizophrenic

adjective
having or relating to schizophrenia
2.9
Pronunciation: / skɪt.səˈfrɛn.ɪk /; / ˌskɪtsəˈfrɛnɪk / (AmE), / skɪt.səʊˈfrɛn.ɪk /; / ˌskɪtsəˈfrɛnɪk / (BrE)
suffering from, relating to, or characteristic of schizophrenia, a serious mental disorder

Examples
