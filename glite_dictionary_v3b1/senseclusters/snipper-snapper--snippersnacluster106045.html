snipper-snapper

noun
cheeky or gossiping person
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɪpərˌsnæpər / (AmE), / ˈsnɪpəˌsnæpə / (BrE)
a person, especially a young one, who gossips, criticizes others, or behaves in a cheeky or impudent manner

Examples

A snipper-snapper child
Everyone avoids the snipper-snapper at work.
I can't believe that snipper-snapper told the manager about our private conversation.
The office snipper-snapper always has something snide to say about everyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whippersnapper - an impudent or presumptuous young person
