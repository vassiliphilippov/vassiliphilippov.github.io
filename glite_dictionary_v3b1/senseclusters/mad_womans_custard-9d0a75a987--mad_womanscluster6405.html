mad woman's custard

noun
humorous phrase for chaos or mess
Pronunciation: / mæd ˈwʊmənz ˈkʌstərd / (AmE), / mad ˈwʊmənz ˈkʌstəd / (BrE)
used humorously to describe something that is chaotically messy or disorganized

Examples

A mad woman's custard
After the kids finished playing, the living room looked like a mad woman's custard.
His desk is always such a mad woman's custard that no one else can find anything on it.
The party decorations ended up looking like a mad woman's custard after the wind blew through.
