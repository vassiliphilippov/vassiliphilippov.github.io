apophyseal

adjective
relating to an apophysis (bone projection)
Pronunciation: / əˌpɑːfəˈsiːəl / (AmE), / əˌpɒfɪˈsiːəl / (BrE)
relating to or associated with an apophysis, a natural bony outgrowth or projection on a bone.

Examples

Apophyseal bone growth
Adolescents are at risk of apophyseal injuries during rapid growth periods.
Doctors examined the apophyseal joints for signs of arthritis.
The X-ray showed an apophyseal fracture near the patient's pelvis.
