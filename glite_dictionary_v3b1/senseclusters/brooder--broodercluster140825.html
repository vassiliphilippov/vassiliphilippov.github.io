brooder

noun
heated box for chicks or infants
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːdər / (AmE), / ˈbruːdə / (BrE)
an apparatus with a controlled temperature, used to keep chicks or premature infants warm

Examples

Chick brooder
Farmers often invest in a high-quality brooder to improve chick survival rates.
The hospital uses a special brooder for premature babies in the neonatal unit.
The newly hatched chicks were placed in a brooder to keep them warm.
