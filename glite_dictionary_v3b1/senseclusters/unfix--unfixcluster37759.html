unfix

verb
to detach
6.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪks / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪks / (BrE)
to detach or remove something from a fixed position

Examples

To unfix a shelf
Before you can repaint the wall, you need to unfix the shelves.
The technician will unfix the panel to access the wiring behind it.
She carefully unfixed the poster from the door to avoid tearing it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detach - to remove or separate something from where it is attached
  • antonym: fix - to fasten or attach something firmly in place
  • synonym: remove - to take something away from a place
