spar

noun
type of cleavable mineral, e.g., calcite or feldspar
Pronunciation: / spɑr / (AmE), / spɑː / (BrE)
any of several nonmetallic minerals, such as calcite or feldspar, that are light in color, transparent or translucent, and cleavable.

Examples

Calcite spar
Geologists identified the translucent mineral as a type of spar based on its characteristics.
Some ancient tools were made from spar because it was easy to shape and split.
The crystal collection included a beautiful piece of spar with perfect cleavages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calcite - a common crystalline form of natural calcium carbonate, CaCO₃
  • synonym: feldspar - a group of rock-forming minerals that make up about 60% of the Earth's crust
