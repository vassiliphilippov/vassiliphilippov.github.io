Danubian

noun
person from Danube region
Pronunciation: / dəˈnuːbiən / (AmE), / dəˈnjuːbiən / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from the region of the Danube River

Examples

A Danubian person
The Danubian shared stories about life along the famous river.
Many Danubians celebrate traditional festivals unique to their region.
After years abroad, the Danubian returned to their village on the riverbank, proud of their heritage.
