boozer

noun
informal pub or bar
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːzər / (AmE), / ˈbuːzə / (BrE)
an informal term for a pub or bar where people drink alcohol

Examples

Local boozer
After the football match, everyone went to the boozer to celebrate the win.
He suggested meeting at the old boozer on the corner for a drink.
Locals often gather at their favorite boozer to catch up and relax.
