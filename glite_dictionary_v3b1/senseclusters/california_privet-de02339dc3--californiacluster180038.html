California privet

noun
a shrub used for hedges, Ligustrum ovalifolium
7.0
Pronunciation: / kəˌlɪfɔrniə ˈprɪvət / (AmE), / kəˌlɪfəˈnɪə ˈprɪvɪt / (BrE)
a semi-evergreen shrub (Ligustrum ovalifolium) native to Japan, often used for hedges due to its tendency to remain green longer than common privet, despite its unpleasantly scented flowers

Examples

The California privet hedge
Despite its malodorous flowers, California privet is a popular choice for suburban hedges.
The California privet hedge in our garden stays green all year round.
They planted California privet along the driveway for privacy and greenery.
