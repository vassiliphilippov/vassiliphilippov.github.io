antimilitary

adjective
opposed to military
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈmɪlɪˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈmɪlɪtəri / (BrE)
opposed to the military or to military policies and activities

Examples

Antimilitary views
The organization is known for its strong antimilitary stance and peaceful demonstrations.
Many students signed the petition to support antimilitary policies at their university.
Her antimilitary views often sparked debates with her friends who served in the army.

Related Terms

  • antonym: militaristic - supporting or favoring military values or policies
  • synonym: pacifist - opposed to war or violence as a means of settling disputes
