choy

noun
Asian leafy vegetable
3.7
Pronunciation: / tʃɔɪ / (AmE), / tʃɔɪ / (BrE)
a leafy green vegetable, especially varieties of Chinese cabbage, commonly used in Asian cooking

Examples

Fresh choy leaves

Stir-fried choy is a popular side dish in many Asian households.
The market sells several varieties of choy, including bok choy and gai lan.
