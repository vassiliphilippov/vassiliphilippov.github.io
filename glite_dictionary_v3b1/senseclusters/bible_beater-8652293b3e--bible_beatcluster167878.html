Bible beater

noun
person who aggressively promotes religion
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪbəl ˌbiːtər / (AmE), / ˈbaɪbəl ˌbiːtə / (BrE)
a person who aggressively promotes their religious beliefs or displays excessive religious fervor, especially in a way that is intrusive or unwelcome

Examples

Bible beater person
Although he disliked being labeled a Bible beater, his sermons were always passionate and forceful.
She was called a Bible beater because she constantly quoted scripture to her classmates.
Some people found the candidate’s Bible beater attitude off-putting during the debate.

Related Terms

  • antonym: agnostic - A person who claims neither faith nor disbelief in God
  • synonym: fundamentalist - A person who upholds strict, literal interpretation of scripture
  • synonym: zealot - A person who is fanatical in pursuing their religious, political, or other ideals
