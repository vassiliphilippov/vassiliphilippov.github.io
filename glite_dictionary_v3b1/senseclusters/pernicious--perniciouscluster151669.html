pernicious

adjective
causing harm in a subtle or gradual way

Pronunciation: /pər.ˈnɪ.ʃəs/ (AmE), /pə.ˈnɪ.ʃəs/ (BrE)

Examples

A pernicious effect
Long-term exposure to the chemical can have pernicious effects on human health.

Economic inequality can have a pernicious impact on social stability.
