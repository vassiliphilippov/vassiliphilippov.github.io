cantilever

verb
to extend outward supported at one end
8.3 8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkæntəˌlivɚ /; / ˈkæntɪˌliːvər /; / ˈkæntɪˌlivɚ / (AmE), / ˈkæntɪˌliːvə / (BrE)
to project, construct, or support something so that it extends outward, fixed at only one end (like a cantilever)
