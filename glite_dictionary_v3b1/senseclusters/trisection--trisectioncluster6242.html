trisection

noun
division into three parts
7.5
Pronunciation: / traɪˈsɛkʃən / (AmE), / traɪˈsɛkʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of dividing something into three equal parts

Examples

Trisection of a shape
The trisection of the cake ensured that everyone got an equal share.
In geometry class, we learned about the trisection of angles and its challenges.
Accurate trisection of a line segment can be useful in technical drawing.
