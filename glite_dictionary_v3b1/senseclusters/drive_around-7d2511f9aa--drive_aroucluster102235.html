drive around

verb
drive without a specific destination
Pronunciation: / draɪv əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / draɪv əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to drive a vehicle without any particular destination or purpose

Examples

To drive around the city
On weekends, we like to drive around the city just to relax and see what's new.
They spent the afternoon driving around the countryside, enjoying the fresh air.
When she felt bored, she would often drive around with no particular place in mind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cruise - to drive or ride at a relaxed pace, especially without a specific destination
