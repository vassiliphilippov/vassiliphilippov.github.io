go forward

verb
move ahead or proceed
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ ˈfɔrwərd / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ˈfɔːwəd / (BrE)
to move ahead or proceed in space, time, or progress

Examples

To go forward quickly
After much discussion, the project will finally go forward next month.
The timeline will go forward from January to December.
We must go forward despite the challenges we face.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advance - to move forward in position or progress
  • antonym: retreat - to move back or withdraw
