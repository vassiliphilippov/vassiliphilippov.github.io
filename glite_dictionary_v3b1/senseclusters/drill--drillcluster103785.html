drill

noun
hip-hop subgenre with dark, aggressive lyrics
7.5
Pronunciation: / drɪl / (AmE), / drɪl / (BrE)
A subgenre of hip hop, originating in the early 2010s, characterized by dark, confrontational lyrics, trap-influenced beats and a tense, staccato delivery.

The rapper's latest drill track sparked debates about the genre's violence and aesthetics.

Police say the song uses explicit lines common in drill, which critics link to real-world violence.
