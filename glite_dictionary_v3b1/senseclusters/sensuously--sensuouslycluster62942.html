sensuously

adverb
in a way that gratifies the senses
4.1 5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈsen.ʃʊə.sliː/ /; / /ˈsen.sjʊə.sliː/ (AmE), /ˈsen.sjʊə.sliː/ /; / /ˈsen.ʃʊə.sliː/ (BrE)
In a way that involves or provides aesthetic pleasure or gratification to the senses, especially in an enjoyable, luxurious, or eager manner.

Examples

To move sensuously
She moved sensuously to the rhythm of the soft music.
The chef described the dessert as a sensuously delightful experience.
The fabric felt sensuously smooth against her skin.
