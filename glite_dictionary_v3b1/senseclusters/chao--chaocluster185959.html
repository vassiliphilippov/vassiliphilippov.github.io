chao

interjection
informal farewell
Pronunciation: / tʃaʊ / (AmE), / tʃaʊ / (BrE)
an informal way to say goodbye, used especially in casual conversation

Examples

Say chao goodbye
She smiled and said chao before leaving the café.
At the end of the call, he waved and said chao to his friends.
Everyone shouted chao as the party came to an end.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bye - an informal way to say goodbye
  • antonym: hello - a greeting used when meeting someone
