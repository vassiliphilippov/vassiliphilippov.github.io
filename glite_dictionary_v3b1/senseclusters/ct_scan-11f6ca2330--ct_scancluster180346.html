CT scan

noun
detailed analysis
Pronunciation: / si ti skæn / (AmE), / siː tiː skæn / (BrE)
a thorough and detailed examination or analysis of something, often used figuratively to suggest close scrutiny.

Examples

A CT scan of politics
The journalist's article provided a CT scan of the country's political system.
Her research paper offered a CT scan of the challenges facing modern education.
The documentary gave viewers a CT scan of life in the remote village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: in-depth analysis - a thorough and comprehensive examination of something
  • antonym: overview - a general summary or review
