gank

verb
(slang) kill or defeat easily, esp. in gaming
Pronunciation: / ɡæŋk / (AmE), / ɡaŋk / (BrE)
(slang) In video games, to kill or defeat another character with little effort, often by surprise or with overwhelming force.

Examples

To gank an enemy
He managed to gank three players in the enemy base before anyone noticed.
If you go out alone, you're likely to get ganked by roaming opponents.
Our team kept ganking their carries, making it easy to win the match.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ambush - to attack by surprise, especially from a concealed position
