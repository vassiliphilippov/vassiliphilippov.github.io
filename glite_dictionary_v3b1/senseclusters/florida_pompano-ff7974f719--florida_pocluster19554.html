Florida pompano

noun
a species of edible marine fish
3.8
Pronunciation: / fləˈrɪdə ˈpɑmpəˌnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈflɒrɪdə ˈpɒmpənəʊ / (BrE)
A species of edible marine fish (Trachinotus carolinus) found along the western Atlantic coast from New England to Brazil, excluding the clear waters of the West Indies.

Examples

A Florida pompano fish
I spotted a school of Florida pompano swimming near the pier yesterday.
Restaurants in the region often serve fresh Florida pompano during the summer season.
The Florida pompano is a prized catch among recreational anglers along the Gulf Coast.
