junket

noun
official trip at public expense
7.6 7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒʌŋ.kɪt/ /; / /ˈdʒʌŋ.kət/ (AmE), /ˈdʒʌŋ.kɪt/ /; / /ˈdʒʌŋ.kət/ (BrE)
a trip or excursion, especially one taken for pleasure at another's expense, often seen as unnecessary or lavish
