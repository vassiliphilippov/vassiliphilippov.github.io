Ostara

proper noun
pagan spring festival
9.9
Pronunciation: / oʊˈstɑːrə / (AmE), / ɒˈstɑːrə / (BrE)
a spring festival celebrated in some pagan traditions, or the goddess associated with this festival

Examples

Ostara festival celebration
Many modern pagans celebrate Ostara as a festival of renewal and growth.
During Ostara, people often decorate eggs and welcome the arrival of spring.
The traditions of Ostara focus on themes of balance, fertility, and new beginnings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Easter - a Christian festival celebrating the resurrection of Jesus, often linked to spring themes
  • synonym: spring equinox - the time in spring when day and night are of equal length
