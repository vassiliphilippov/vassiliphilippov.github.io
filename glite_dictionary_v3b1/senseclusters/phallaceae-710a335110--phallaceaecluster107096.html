Phallaceae

noun
family of fungi (true stinkhorns)
9.9
Pronunciation: / fæˈleɪsiˌi / (AmE), / fæˈleɪsiːˌiː / (BrE)
A family of fungi in the order Phallales, commonly known as the true stinkhorns, characterized by their foul odor and distinct fruiting bodies.
