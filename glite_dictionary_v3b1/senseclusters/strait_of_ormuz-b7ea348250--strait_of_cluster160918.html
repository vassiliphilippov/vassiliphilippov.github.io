Strait of Ormuz

proper noun
strategic strait between Persian Gulf and Gulf of Oman
7.4
Pronunciation: / streɪt əv ɔrˈmuz / (AmE), / streɪt əv ɔːˈmʊz / (BrE)
a narrow, strategically important strait linking the Persian Gulf with the Gulf of Oman, serving as a vital route for oil shipments
