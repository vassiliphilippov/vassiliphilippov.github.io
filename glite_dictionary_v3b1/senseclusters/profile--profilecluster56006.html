profile

noun
biographical sketch; descriptive account of a person
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊ.faɪl / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ.faɪl / (BrE)
a description or account of a person, such as a short article or an online page showing information about them

Examples

Online profile
Each applicant's profile includes information about their education and previous experience.
She researched extensively to write an accurate profile of the famous scientist.
The magazine published a fascinating profile of the new CEO last week.
She updated her profile on the dating app to reflect her new job and hobbies.
His public profile showed dozens of photos, recent posts, and the groups he followed.
Companies often check a candidate's profile before interviews to learn about interests and experience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: account - a record or page used to access a service that represents a user's identity and settings
  • degree higher: biography - detailed written account of someone's life
  • degree lower: obituary - a notice of a person's death with a brief biography
  • synonym: online identity - a person's public identity or persona presented through internet profiles and activity
