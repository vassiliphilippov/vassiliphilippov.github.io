northern mammoth

noun
extinct hairy mammoth from the north
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrðərn ˈmæməθ / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən ˈmæməθ / (BrE)
a very hairy, extinct species of mammoth that lived in cold regions of the Northern Hemisphere

Examples

The northern mammoth fossil
Fossils of the northern mammoth have been found in Siberian permafrost.
Scientists believe the northern mammoth grazed on grasses similar to modern elephants.
The northern mammoth had a thick coat adapted to Ice Age climates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: woolly mammoth - a widely known extinct species of mammoth, Mammuthus primigenius, known for its thick coat
