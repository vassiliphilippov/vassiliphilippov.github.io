unflappable

adjective
calm and not easily upset
9.9 5.9
Pronunciation: /ən.ˈflæp.ə.bəl/ /; / /ʌn.ˈflæp.ə.bəl/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈflæp.ə.bəl/ /; / /ən.ˈflæp.ə.bəl/ (BrE)

Examples

The pilot's unflappable demeanor reassured the nervous passengers during the turbulent flight.
