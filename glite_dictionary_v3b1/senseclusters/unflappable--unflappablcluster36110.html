unflappable

adjective
calm and not easily upset
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈflæpəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈflæpəbəl / (BrE)
remaining calm and not becoming upset, especially in difficult situations; not easily disturbed or excited.

Examples

An unflappable person
Despite the criticism, the coach was unflappable and kept a positive attitude throughout the season.
Even during the chaotic meeting, Laura remained unflappable and handled every problem calmly.
The pilot's unflappable demeanor reassured the nervous passengers during the turbulent flight.

Related Terms

  • antonym: agitated - feeling or appearing troubled or nervous
  • synonym: imperturbable - not easily disturbed or excited; calm and composed
