northern casque-headed frog

noun
burrowing frog of the US southwest
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔɹðɚn ˈkæskˌhɛdɪd frɑg / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən ˈkɑːskˌhɛdɪd frɒg / (BrE)
A terrestrial, burrowing, nocturnal frog found in grassy terrain and scrub forests of the southwestern United States, noted for its very hard upper surface of the head.
