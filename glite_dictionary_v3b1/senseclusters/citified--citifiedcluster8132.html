citified

adjective
having urban customs or appearance
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪt̬.i.faɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɪt.ɪ.faɪd / (BrE)
characteristic of city life or people; having the manners, style, or appearance typical of someone from a city

Examples

Citified city style
He grew up in a small town, but his citified clothes made him stand out.
She sounded more citified after spending a year in New York.
The visitors brought a citified attitude to the rural community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: urbane - having the sophistication and elegance typical of city life
