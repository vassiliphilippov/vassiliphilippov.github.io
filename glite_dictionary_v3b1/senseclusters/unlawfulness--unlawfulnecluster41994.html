unlawfulness

noun
quality of being unlawful
1.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɔf(ə)lnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɔːf(ə)lnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of not conforming to the law; illegality.

Examples

The unlawfulness of actions
He was concerned about the unlawfulness of their actions under international law.
Protesters accused the government of unlawfulness in suppressing dissent.
The court examined the unlawfulness of the company's business practices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illegality - the state of being illegal
  • antonym: lawfulness - the quality of conforming to law
