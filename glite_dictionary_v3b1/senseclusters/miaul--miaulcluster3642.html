miaul

noun
cry or sound of a cat
9.9
Pronunciation: / miˈɑl / (AmE), / miˈaʊl / (BrE)
A cry or sound made by a cat, or any sound resembling this.

Examples

A miaul of a cat

She heard the miaul of a stray cat echoing in the alleyway.
The kitten's constant miaul woke me up early this morning.
