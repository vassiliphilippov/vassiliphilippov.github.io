capacitate

verb
make (sperm) able to fertilize an egg
9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈpæsɪteɪt / (AmE), / kəˈpæsɪteɪt / (BrE)
(of sperm) to undergo or to cause to undergo the physical changes needed to fertilize an egg.

Examples

To capacitate sperm
Certain chemicals in the oviductal fluid help to capacitate the sperm.
Mature sperm must capacitate in the female reproductive tract before they can fertilize an egg.
Without proper capacitateion, fertilization cannot occur.
