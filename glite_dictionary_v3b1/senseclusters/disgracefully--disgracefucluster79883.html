disgracefully

adverb
in a shameful or dishonorable way
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈɡreɪsfəli / (AmE), / dɪsˈɡreɪsfəli / (BrE)
in a way that causes shame or dishonor; in a shameful or dishonorable manner

Examples

To disgracefully neglect duties
She disgracefully neglected her duties, causing problems for the entire team.
The athlete behaved disgracefully after losing the game, yelling at the referee.
The government was criticized for acting disgracefully in handling the crisis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: honorably - in a way that brings honor or respect
  • synonym: shamefully - in a way that causes shame or humiliation
