decoupage

noun
art of decorating surfaces with paper cutouts and varnish
Pronunciation: / ˌdeɪkuˈpɑʒ /; / ˌdeɪˌkuːˈpɑːʒ / (AmE), / ˌdeɪkuːˈpɑːʒ /; / ˌdeɪˈkuːpɑːʒ / (BrE)
the art of decorating a surface or object by gluing cut-out paper designs and covering them with layers of varnish or lacquer
