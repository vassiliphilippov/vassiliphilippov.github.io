fishnet

noun
open-weave fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃˌnɛt / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃˌnɛt / (BrE)
an open-weave fabric with a net-like pattern, often used for stockings or tights

Examples

Fishnet stockings
She wore black fishnet stockings to the party.
The dancer's costume featured a sparkling fishnet overlay.
He bought a pair of gloves made from fishnet material for the concert.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lace - a delicate fabric made of yarn or thread in an open weblike pattern
  • synonym: mesh - a material made of a network of wire or thread
