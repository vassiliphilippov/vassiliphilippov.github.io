blue-purple

adjective
purple tinged with blue
Pronunciation: / bluː ˈpɜr.pəl / (AmE), / bluː ˈpɜː.pəl / (BrE)
Of a shade of purple that is tinged with blue; having a bluish-purple hue.

Examples

A blue-purple dress
She wore a dress in a striking blue-purple color that caught everyone's attention.
The artist chose a blue-purple shade for the evening sky in her painting.
The flowers bloom in various hues, from pale lavender to deep blue-purple.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: indigo - a deep purplish-blue color
  • degree lower: purple - a color intermediate between red and blue
