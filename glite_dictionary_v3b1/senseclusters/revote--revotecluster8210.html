revote

verb
to vote again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈvoʊt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈvəʊt / (BrE)
to hold a second or repeated vote on a proposal, decision, or election.

Examples

To revote on results
After a technical error, the committee decided to revote on the proposal.
The members agreed to revote to ensure the results were accurate.
If the outcome is unclear, we may need to revote next week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reconsider - to think about a decision again
  • degree lower: vote - to express a choice or opinion in an election or decision
