leaders

noun
those who direct a group or organization
Pronunciation: / ˈliːdərz / (AmE), / ˈliːdəz / (BrE)
the people who direct or guide a group, organization, or country; those in positions of authority.

Examples

World leaders
Several world leaders gathered to discuss climate change at the summit.
Student leaders play an important role in organizing school events.
The leaders of the company announced a new direction during the annual meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leadership - the state or position of being a leader, or the group which leads
