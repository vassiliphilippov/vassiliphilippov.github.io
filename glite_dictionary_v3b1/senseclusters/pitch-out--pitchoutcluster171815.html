pitch-out

noun
football lateral pass
6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈpɪtʃ.aʊt/ /; / /pɪtʃ aʊt/ (AmE), /ˈpɪtʃ.aʊt/ /; / /pɪtʃ aʊt/ (BrE)
a play in American football where the quarterback tosses the ball sideways or backward to a teammate, usually a running back

Examples

Football pitch-out
The quarterback executed a perfect pitch-out to the running back, who sprinted down the sideline.
During the final play, the team tried a risky pitch-out to gain extra yards.
A quick pitch-out can catch the defense off guard and create big opportunities.
