unencrypted

adjective
not encrypted
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈkrɪptɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈkrɪptɪd / (BrE)
not protected by encryption and able to be read or accessed without special decoding

Examples

Unencrypted data
Sensitive information should never be sent as unencrypted data.
The file was left unencrypted, making it vulnerable to unauthorized access.
Always check that your connection is secure and not unencrypted before sending personal details.

Related Terms

  • antonym: encrypted - protected by encryption and not readable without a key
  • synonym: plain - not encoded or encrypted; easily readable
