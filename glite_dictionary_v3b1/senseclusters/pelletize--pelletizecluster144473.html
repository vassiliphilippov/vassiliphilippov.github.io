pelletize

verb
to make into pellets
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈpel.ə.taɪz/ /; / /ˈpel.ɪ.taɪz/ (AmE), /ˈpel.ə.taɪz/ /; / /ˈpel.ɪ.taɪz/ (BrE)
to form or process a material or substance into small, rounded pellets

Examples

To pelletize waste
The company plans to pelletize agricultural waste to create a new type of biofuel.
Some animal feeds are easier to handle after they are pelletized in the factory.
Modern recycling plants often pelletize plastic before shipping it to manufacturers.
Many factories pelletize sawdust to create fuel for heating systems.
The company decided to pelletize the fertilizer for easier distribution and use.
Some pet food manufacturers pelletize ingredients to ensure consistent nutrition in every bite.
