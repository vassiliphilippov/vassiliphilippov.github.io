Bronx cheer

noun
insulting noise made to show contempt
5.7 7.6
Pronunciation: /brɑːŋks tʃɪr/ (AmE), /brɒŋks tʃɪə/ (BrE)
an insulting or mocking noise made by blowing through closed lips to show scorn or disapproval; a raspberry
