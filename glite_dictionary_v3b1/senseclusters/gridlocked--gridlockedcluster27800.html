gridlocked

adjective
blocked by congestion
4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈgrɪd.lɑːkt/ (AmE), /ˈgrɪd.lɒkt/ (BrE)
blocked or unable to move, especially because of severe traffic congestion or a complete standstill

Examples

Gridlocked traffic
The downtown streets were completely gridlocked after the concert ended.
We were gridlocked for over an hour and barely moved a few meters.
Emergency vehicles struggled to get through the gridlocked traffic during the parade.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - free of obstacles or congestion
  • synonym: congested - overcrowded or blocked, especially with traffic
  • synonym: jammed - stuck or unable to move due to being tightly packed
