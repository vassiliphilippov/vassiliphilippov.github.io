orchiopexy

noun
surgical correction of undescended testicle
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrkioʊˌpɛksi / (AmE), / ˈɔːkɪəʊˌpɛksi / (BrE)
a surgical procedure to move an undescended testicle into the scrotum and permanently fix it there

Examples

An orchiopexy surgery
After the diagnosis of an undescended testicle, the surgeon recommended an orchiopexy.
Many boys undergo orchiopexies before the age of two to correct this condition.
Recovery from an orchiopexy is usually quick, with most patients returning to normal activities within a week.

Related Terms

  • antonym: orchidectomy - surgical removal of one or both testicles
