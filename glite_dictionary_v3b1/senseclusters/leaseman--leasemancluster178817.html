leaseman

noun
tenant under lease
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈliːsmæn / (AmE), / ˈliːsmən / (BrE)
a person who holds the right to occupy or use land or property under a lease, especially a tenant or leaseholder of agricultural land

Examples

A leaseman on farmland
The leaseman was responsible for maintaining the fields throughout the year.
As a leaseman, he negotiated new terms with the property owner every few years.
The farm had been managed by the same leaseman for over a decade.

Related Terms

  • antonym: landlord - a person who owns and rents out land or property
  • synonym: leaseholder - a person who holds a lease on a property
  • synonym: tenant - a person who rents land or property from a landlord
