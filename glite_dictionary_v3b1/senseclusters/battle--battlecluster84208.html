Synonyms

  • combat
    4.1 9.48/m
    combat is a formal term for fighting to stop a threat or enemy, while battle suggests a longer, often large struggle.
  • fight
    1.0 153/m
    fight is a general word for physical or verbal fighting, while battle often means a longer or more organized struggle.
  • struggle
    2.7 12/m
    struggle describes working hard against difficulty or resistance, while battle names a direct fight that may be long or intense.
  • clash
    3.1 3.17/m
    clash often means a sharp disagreement or brief violent meeting, while battle refers to a sustained fight or long military struggle.
