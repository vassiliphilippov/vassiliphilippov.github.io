shenanigan

noun
mischievous or tricky act
Pronunciation: / ʃəˈnænɪɡən / (AmE), / ʃəˈnænɪɡən /; / ʃɪˈnænɪɡən / (BrE)
a mischievous, tricky, or deceitful act; behavior that causes trouble or annoyance, sometimes playful or intended to cheat

Examples

His constant shenanigans at school got him into a lot of trouble.

The teacher quickly put a stop to the students' shenanigans before they got out of hand.
After hours, the office was filled with laughter and harmless shenanigans among coworkers.
Their summer camp was known for the creative shenanigans the kids came up with every year.
