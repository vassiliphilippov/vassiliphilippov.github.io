deservingly

adverb
in a worthy manner
4.5
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzɜːrvɪŋli / (AmE), / dɪˈzɜːvɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is right or appropriate because of merit or worthiness

Examples

He was deservingly promoted
He was deservingly promoted after years of dedication to the company.
The team deservingly won the championship after an outstanding season.
Her efforts were deservingly praised by everyone at the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: justly - in a fair or deserved manner
  • synonym: rightfully - in a way that is morally or legally correct
  • antonym: unfairly - in a way that is not deserved or just
