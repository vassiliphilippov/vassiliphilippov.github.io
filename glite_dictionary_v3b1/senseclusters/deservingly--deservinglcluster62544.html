deservingly

adverb
in a worthy manner
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzɜːrvɪŋli / (AmE), / dɪˈzɜːvɪŋli / (BrE)

Examples

He was deservingly promoted

The team deservingly won the championship after an outstanding season.
Her efforts were deservingly praised by everyone at the ceremony.
