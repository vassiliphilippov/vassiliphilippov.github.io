wabe

noun
area around sundial
9.9
Pronunciation: / weɪb / (AmE), / weɪb / (BrE)
an invented word by Lewis Carroll meaning the area around a sundial

Examples

Around the wabe
In the poem, the creatures danced merrily around the wabe at sunrise.
The children imagined the wabe as a magical place filled with talking flowers.
He quoted Lewis Carroll, describing the grassy wabe surrounding the old sundial.
