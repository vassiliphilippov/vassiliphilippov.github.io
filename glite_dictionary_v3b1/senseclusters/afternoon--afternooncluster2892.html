afternoon

noun
part of the day between noon and evening
Pronunciation: / ˌæftɚˈnun / (AmE), / ˌɑːftəˈnuːn / (BrE)
The part of the day between noon and evening.

Examples

This afternoon
Let's meet for coffee this afternoon if you're free.
She usually goes for a walk in the afternoon.
The afternoon sunlight streamed through the window, warming the room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: evening - the part of the day between late afternoon and night
  • antonym: morning - the part of the day from sunrise to noon
