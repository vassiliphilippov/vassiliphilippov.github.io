subfamily Lutrinae

noun
animal subfamily (otters)
Pronunciation: / sʌbˌfæməli luːˈtraɪni / (AmE), / sʌbˌfæmɪli luːˈtraɪni / (BrE)
A taxonomic subfamily of carnivorous mammals comprising the otters.

Examples

The subfamily Lutrinae otters
All species in the subfamily Lutrinae are adapted to life in aquatic environments.
Recent studies have revised the classification of the subfamily Lutrinae within the mustelid family.
The subfamily Lutrinae includes the familiar river and sea otters found worldwide.
