Rothko

proper noun
Russian-American abstract painter
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːθkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈrɒθkəʊ / (BrE)
Mark Rothko (1903–1970), a Russian-born American abstract painter known for his works featuring large, soft-edged, horizontal bands of color.

Examples

Rothko paintings
Many art lovers admire Rothko for the emotional power of his color field paintings.
She wrote her thesis on the influence of Rothko in modern art.
The museum's new exhibit includes several works by Rothko from the 1950s.
