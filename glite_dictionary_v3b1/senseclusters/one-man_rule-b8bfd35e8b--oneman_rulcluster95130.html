one-man rule

noun
system of government by a single dictator
8.2 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw ån Àåm√¶n ÀàruÀêl / (AmE), / Ààw ån Àåm√¶n ÀàruÀêl / (BrE)
a system of government in which all power is held by a single individual who acts as an absolute dictator, without checks or opposition.

Examples

A one-man rule system
History has shown that one-man rule often leads to widespread corruption and repression.
Many citizens protested against the return of one-man rule after years of democracy.
The country suffered for decades under one-man rule and lacked basic freedoms.

Synonyms

  • monocracy
    9.9 0.00/m
    monocracy is a formal term for government by a single absolute leader, while one-man rule describes the same idea in plain language.
  • autocracy
    8.2 0.96/m
    autocracy is a system where one person holds unlimited power and authority, while one-man rule stresses sole control by an individual.
  • dictatorship
    4.8 10/m
    dictatorship is often used for rulers with absolute power, sometimes by a small group, while one-man rule means a single individual's total control.
  • absolutism
    8.1 0.61/m
    absolutism is a political system where a ruler has complete, unrestricted power, while one-man rule focuses on power held by one person.
  • despotism
    9.6 1.33/m
    despotism is rule by a harsh, often cruel ruler who rules without limits, while one-man rule only names single-person control.
  • tyranny
    6.7 7.83/m
    tyranny is cruel or oppressive rule that harms people, while one-man rule simply states that one person holds absolute power.
