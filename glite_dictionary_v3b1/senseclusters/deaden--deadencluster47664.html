deaden

verb
make less distinct or clear
9.2 5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈde.dən/ (AmE), /ˈde.dən/ (BrE)
to make something less distinct, clear, strong, or visible; to muffle or obscure

Examples

To deaden the sound
Fog can deaden the outlines of distant buildings, making them hard to see.
She tried to deaden her emotions by focusing on her work.
The thick curtains deaden the sound of traffic from the street.

Related Terms
