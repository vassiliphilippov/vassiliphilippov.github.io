become one flesh

verb
to unite intimately, often in marriage
6.3
Pronunciation: / bɪˈkʌm wʌn flɛʃ / (AmE), / bɪˈkʌm wʌn flɛʃ / (BrE)
(of two people) to unite intimately and completely, especially as a married couple.

Examples

To become one flesh in marriage
In many cultures, marriage is seen as a sacred act in which two people become one flesh.
The priest spoke about how the couple would become one flesh according to their faith.
They wanted their relationship to grow deeper, to truly become one flesh in both body and spirit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: marry - to become joined as husband and wife
  • antonym: separate - to stop being united or together as a couple
