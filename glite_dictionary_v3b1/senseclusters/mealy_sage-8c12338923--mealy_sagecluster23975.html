mealy sage

noun
herbaceous plant with blue flowers
7.8 9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːli seɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmiːli seɪdʒ / (BrE)
A herbaceous plant (Salvia farinacea), native to Texas and Mexico, known for its spikes of intense blue flowers and grayish-green foliage.

Examples

Mealy sage plant
Gardeners often choose mealy sage for its drought tolerance and easy care.
In Texas landscapes, mealy sage is frequently used as a decorative border plant.
