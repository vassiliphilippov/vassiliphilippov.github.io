no-lifer

noun
person with little or no social life
5.2 8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈnoʊ.laɪ.fər/ /; / /noʊ.ˈlaɪ.fər/ (AmE), /ˈnəʊ.laɪ.fə/ /; / /nəʊ.ˈlaɪ.fə/ (BrE)
a person who spends most of their time online or alone, with little to no social interaction or life outside of this activity

Examples

A no-lifer gamer
He was teased as a no-lifer because he spent all weekend gaming.
She decided not to be a no-lifer anymore and joined a local sports club.
Some people unfairly label hobbyists as no-lifers just because they prefer staying in.
