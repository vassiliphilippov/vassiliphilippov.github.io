interface

noun
point where two areas interact or overlap
6.3 5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntərˌfeɪs / (AmE), / ˈɪntəˌfeɪs / (BrE)
a point or area where two things meet or interact, or the boundary between them (including in science or abstract contexts)

Examples
