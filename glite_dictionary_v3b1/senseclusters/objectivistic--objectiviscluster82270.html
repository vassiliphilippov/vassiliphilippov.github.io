objectivistic

adjective
objective approach
Pronunciation: / əbˌdʒɛktɪˈvɪstɪk / (AmE), / əbˌdʒɛktɪˈvɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of objectivism or an objective approach, especially in philosophy or analysis

Examples

Objectivistic analysis
Her objectivistic analysis focused on facts rather than opinions.
The report took an objectivistic stance, avoiding any personal bias.
In philosophy, an objectivistic view values evidence over emotion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: objective - not influenced by personal feelings or opinions
  • antonym: subjective - based on personal feelings or opinions
