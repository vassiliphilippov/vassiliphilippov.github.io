caterpillar-tracked

adjective
fitted with caterpillar tracks
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkætərˌpɪlər trækt / (AmE), / ˈkatəpɪlə trækt / (BrE)
(of a vehicle or machine) fitted with continuous tracks or treads like those invented by Caterpillar, instead of wheels, for movement over rough ground.

Examples

A caterpillar-tracked vehicle
A caterpillar-tracked vehicle is better suited for uneven terrain than a wheeled one.
The caterpillar-tracked bulldozer was able to move easily across the muddy field.
They watched as the caterpillar-tracked excavator cleared the construction site.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tracked - fitted with tracks (continuous bands) for movement
  • antonym: wheeled - having wheels instead of tracks
