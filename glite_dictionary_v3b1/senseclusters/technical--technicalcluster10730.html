technical

adjective
relating to technique or skill
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛk.nɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˈtɛk.nɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to technique, skill, or proficiency in performing a practical task or art.

Examples

Technical skill
Her technical mastery on the piano impressed the judges at the competition.
The ballet dancer's technical skills set her apart from the rest of the troupe.
While his technical ability is strong, he still needs to develop a unique artistic style.

Related Terms

  • antonym: artistic - relating to art or creative skill, sometimes contrasted with technical skill
