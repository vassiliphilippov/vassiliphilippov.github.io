Synonyms

  • urging
    3.9 2.01/m
    urging is the act of strongly encouraging someone to act, while adjuration is a solemn, formal appeal asking for action.
  • appeal
    2.9 9.33/m
    appeal is an earnest request for help or action often made publicly, while adjuration is a solemn, personal urging.
  • plea
    3.9 9.99/m

  • imploration

  • pleading
