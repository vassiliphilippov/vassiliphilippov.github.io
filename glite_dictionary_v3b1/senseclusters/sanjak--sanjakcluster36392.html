sanjak

noun
Ottoman flag or banner
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːndʒæk / (AmE), / ˈsændʒæk / (BrE)
a flag or banner, especially one used as a military or administrative standard in the Ottoman Empire

Examples

Ottoman sanjak flag
The soldiers marched under the sanjak that represented their regiment.
Each province in the Ottoman Empire had its own distinctive sanjak displayed at official events.
The sanjak was carefully preserved in the museum as a symbol of historical heritage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: banner - a long strip of cloth bearing a slogan or design, used as a flag or symbol
  • synonym: standard - a flag or emblem used as a symbol, especially in military contexts
