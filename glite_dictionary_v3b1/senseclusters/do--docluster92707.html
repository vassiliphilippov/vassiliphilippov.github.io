do

noun
recommended action
Pronunciation: / duː / (AmE), / duː / (BrE)
a recommended action or behavior that is considered good or appropriate in a particular situation.

Examples

Important dos
Before starting the experiment, the teacher listed several important dos for safety.
When traveling abroad, one of the key dos is to respect local customs.
The guidebook includes a section on dos and don'ts for first-time visitors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: don't - an action or behavior that is advised against
  • synonym: rule - a guideline or instruction about what should be done
