promotor

noun
doctoral supervisor
8.9
Pronunciation: /prə.ˈmoʊ.tər/ /; / /proʊ.ˈmoʊ.tər/ (AmE), /prə.ˈməʊ.tə/ /; / /prəʊ.ˈməʊ.tə/ (BrE)
an academic supervisor, especially in some European universities, who formally supports and supervises a doctoral candidate

Examples

Doctoral promotor
The promotor guided the student through every stage of the doctoral process.
In the Netherlands, a promotor is required to formally support a PhD candidate.
Her promotor attended the defense and introduced her to the examination committee.
