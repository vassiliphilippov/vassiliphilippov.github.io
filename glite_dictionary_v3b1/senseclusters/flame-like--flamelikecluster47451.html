flame-like

adjective
resembling a flame
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪmˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈfleɪmˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the appearance or qualities of a flame

Examples

Flame-like colors
The artist painted the sky with flame-like streaks of orange and red at sunset.
Her hair had a flame-like quality, bright and wild in the sunlight.
The festival decorations included flame-like banners that danced in the wind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blazing - burning brightly and strongly
  • synonym: fiery - having the qualities or appearance of fire
