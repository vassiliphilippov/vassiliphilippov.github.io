Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrboʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɑːbəʊ / (BrE)
a combining form used in chemical and biological terms to indicate the presence of carbon or something containing carbon

Examples

Carbo- in chemistry words
The prefix carbo- is used in words like carbohydrate and carbonyl.
In biochemistry, carbo- often appears in terms related to carbon compounds.
Scientists use carbo- in naming molecules that contain carbon atoms.
