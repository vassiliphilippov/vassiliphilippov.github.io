non-poisonous

adjective
not containing poison; safe
5.5
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈpɔɪzənəs /; / nɑn ˈpɔɪzənəs / (AmE), / nɒnˈpɔɪzənəs /; / nɒn ˈpɔɪzənəs / (BrE)
not containing, producing, or involving poison; not harmful or dangerous to eat, touch, or use
