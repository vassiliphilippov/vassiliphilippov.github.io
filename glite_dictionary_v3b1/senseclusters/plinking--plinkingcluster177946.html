plinking

noun
casual target shooting
Pronunciation: / ˈplɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈplɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
informal shooting at small or nonstandard targets for enjoyment, often using small-caliber firearms

Examples

Plinking at cans
They spent the afternoon plinking at empty cans lined up on the fence.
Many people enjoy plinking as a relaxing way to practice their shooting skills.
The sound of plinking echoed across the field as the group aimed at bottles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: target shooting - the sport or activity of shooting at targets for practice or competition
