prerequisite

noun
something required in advance
6.6 6.9
Pronunciation: / priˈrɛkwɪˌzɪt / (AmE), / priˈrɛkwɪzɪt / (BrE)
a thing that is required as a prior condition for something else to happen or exist

Examples

A prerequisite for entry
A valid passport is a prerequisite for international travel.
Completion of Biology 101 is a prerequisite for enrolling in the advanced course.
Strong computer skills have become a prerequisite for many office jobs.
