Ansar

proper noun
Medinan supporters of Muhammad
9.9
Pronunciation: /æn.ˈsɑːr/ (AmE), /æn.ˈsɑː/ (BrE)
members of the group in Medina who supported Muhammad after his migration from Mecca in early Islamic history

Many historians credit the Ansar with helping to establish the early Muslim community.
Without the support of the Ansar, the migration from Mecca might not have succeeded.
