finder

noun
optical device for locating targets
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪndɚ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪndə / (BrE)
an optical device used to help a user locate or aim at an object of interest, especially in cameras or telescopes

Examples

Camera finder
He replaced the broken finder on his old camera with a modern one.
The photographer adjusted the finder to frame the perfect shot.
Through the telescope's finder, she quickly spotted the planet.
