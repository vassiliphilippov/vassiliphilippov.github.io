take each challenge as they come

verb
deal with problems one at a time
1.0
Pronunciation: / teɪk iːtʃ ˈtʃælɪndʒ æz ðeɪ kʌm / (AmE), / teɪk iːtʃ ˈtʃælɪndʒ æz ðeɪ kʌm / (BrE)
to deal with problems or difficulties one at a time, as they arise, rather than worrying about them all at once
