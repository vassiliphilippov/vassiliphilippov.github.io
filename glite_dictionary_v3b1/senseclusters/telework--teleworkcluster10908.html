telework

adjective
remote work-related
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛləˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌwɜːk / (BrE)
relating to work that is performed remotely using telecommunications technology instead of at a traditional workplace

Examples

Telework policy
The company introduced a telework policy to allow employees to work from home several days a week.
Many organizations now provide telework options to support flexible working arrangements.
A telework environment requires reliable internet and secure communication tools.

Related Terms

  • antonym: on-site - relating to work done at the physical workplace
  • synonym: remote - relating to work done away from a central office
