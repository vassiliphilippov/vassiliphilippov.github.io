snagger

noun
tool or person removing snags
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsnæɡər / (AmE), / ˈsnæɡə / (BrE)
a tool or person that removes snags or catches objects, especially in fishing or similar activities

Examples

Fishing snagger
The fisherman used a snagger to retrieve his lost hook from the river.
A snagger is essential for removing debris caught in fishing nets.
He worked as a snagger, clearing obstacles from the waterway to keep it safe for boats.

  • synonym: remover - a person or tool that takes something away
