dataflow

noun
diagram of data movement
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪtəˌfloʊ / (AmE), / ˈdeɪtəˌfləʊ / (BrE)
the movement or transfer of data between parts of a computer system, or a diagram showing how data moves
