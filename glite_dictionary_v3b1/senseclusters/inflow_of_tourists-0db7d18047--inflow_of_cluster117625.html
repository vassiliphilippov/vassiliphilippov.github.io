inflow of tourists

noun
arrival or increase in tourists to a place
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌfloʊ əv ˈtʊr.ɪsts / (AmE), / ˈɪn.fləʊ əv ˈtʊə.rɪsts / (BrE)
an arrival or increase in the number of people visiting a place for tourism

Examples

The inflow of tourists increases
Local authorities prepared for the expected inflow of tourists during the winter holiday season.
The sudden inflow of tourists strained transportation and accommodation services.
The summer festival led to a significant inflow of tourists in the town.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tourist arrivals - number of tourists arriving at a destination
