wood sage

noun
North American shrubby sage with spiked flowers
9.7 6.1
Pronunciation: /wʊd seɪdʒ/ (AmE), /wʊd seɪdʒ/ (BrE)
A perennial subshrub with serrated leaves and cream to pink or purple flowers in spikes, native to North America.
