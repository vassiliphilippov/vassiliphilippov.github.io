onyx

noun
banded black-and-white mineral
Pronunciation: / ˈɑnɪks / (AmE), / ˈɒnɪks / (BrE)
A type of chalcedony mineral with parallel bands of black and white, often used for cameos and ornaments.

Examples

Polished onyx stone
Her necklace featured alternating beads of white quartz and polished onyx.
Museum visitors admired the ancient bowl made of onyx and gold.
The jeweler showed us a beautiful cameo carved from onyx.
