slow lane

noun
relaxed, leisurely lifestyle
Pronunciation: / sloʊ leɪn / (AmE), / sləʊ leɪn / (BrE)
A way of life characterized by a relaxed, leisurely pace, often with less stress and pressure.

Examples

Life in the slow lane
After years of working long hours, he decided to switch to the slow lane.
Many retirees find happiness in the slow lane, spending more time with family and friends.
Some people thrive in the slow lane, valuing peace and simplicity over constant activity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fast lane - a way of life characterized by excitement and rapid activity
