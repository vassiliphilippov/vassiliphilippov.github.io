oligarch

noun
member of a ruling elite or oligarchy
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːlɪˌɡɑrk / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪˌɡɑːk / (BrE)
a very rich and powerful person, especially one who is part of a small group that controls a country or organization
