Examples

To discharge a gun
The old cannon discharged with a thunderous boom.
The soldier carefully discharged his rifle after the warning shot was given.
The trap is designed to discharge automatically when triggered.
He accidentally discharged the gun while cleaning it.
The soldier discharged his weapon after receiving the order.
To mark the start of the race, they discharged a pistol into the air.

Synonyms

  • fire
    1.0 49/m
    fire means to shoot a weapon directly and often intentionally, while discharge is a more formal word for making a weapon go off.
  • go off
    3.2 13/m
    go off describes a device that activates or explodes on its own, while discharge describes firing a weapon deliberately by a person.
