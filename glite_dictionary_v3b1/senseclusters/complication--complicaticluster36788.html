complication

noun
the process of making something more complex
1.0 3.4
Pronunciation: /kɑːm.plə.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /kəm.plɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /kɒm.plɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /kɒm.plə.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
