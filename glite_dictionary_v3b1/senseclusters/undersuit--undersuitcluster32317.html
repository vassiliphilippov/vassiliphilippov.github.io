undersuit

noun
spacesuit inner garment
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌsuːt / (AmE), / ˈʌndəsjuːt / (BrE)
a close-fitting garment worn under another suit (such as a spacesuit or drysuit) to provide warmth, protection, or help regulate temperature

Examples

A undersuit garment
The astronaut carefully put on the undersuit before donning the main spacesuit.
Each undersuit is equipped with tubes to help control the astronaut's body temperature.
Technicians inspected the undersuit for any damage before the spacewalk.
The diver put on a thick undersuit before donning his drysuit for the cold dive.
Wearing an undersuit helps motorcyclists stay warm during long rides in chilly weather.
She bought a new undersuit to wear beneath her ski outfit for extra insulation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: base layer - a layer of clothing worn next to the skin for warmth
  • synonym: coverall - a one-piece protective garment worn over clothes
  • antonym: outerwear - clothing worn over other clothes, especially outdoors
  • synonym: spacesuit - a suit designed to protect astronauts in space
