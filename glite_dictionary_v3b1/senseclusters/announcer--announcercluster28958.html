announcer

noun
automated voice or system for announcements
3.4 0.83/m
Pronunciation: / \u0259\u02c8na\u028ans\u0259r / (AmE), / \u0259\u02c8na\u028ans\u0259 / (BrE)
(in public spaces and digital systems) an automated or synthesized voice or system that makes public announcements, such as gate calls, platform warnings, or app notifications.

Examples

Automated announcer
The airport installed an automated announcer to provide timely gate and boarding updates to passengers.
Every morning the train station's announcer warned commuters about delays with a clear synthetic voice.
In the app, an automated announcer reads notifications aloud so visually impaired users can follow updates.

Synonyms

  • enunciator
    7.4 0.01/m
    enunciator often refers to a person or device that gives short alerts, while announcer is usually an automated or synthesized public voice system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automated voice - a synthetic or recorded voice used to deliver spoken messages without a live announcer
  • synonym: public-address system - a system of microphones and loudspeakers used to make announcements in a building or area
