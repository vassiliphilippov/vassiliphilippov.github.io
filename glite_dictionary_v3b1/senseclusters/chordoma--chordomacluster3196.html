chordoma

noun
rare bone cancer
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɔrˈdoʊmə / (AmE), / kɔːˈdəʊmə / (BrE)
a rare type of cancer that develops in the bones of the spine or base of the skull

Examples

A chordoma tumor
The patient was diagnosed with a chordoma at the base of his spine.
Treatment for chordoma often involves surgery and radiation therapy.
Because chordoma is so rare, it can be difficult to detect early.

Related Terms

  • antonym: benign tumor - a non-cancerous growth in the body
  • synonym: osteosarcoma - a type of cancer that starts in the bones
