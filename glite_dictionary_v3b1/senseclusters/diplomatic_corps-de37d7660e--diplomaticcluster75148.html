diplomatic corps

noun
body of foreign diplomats in a country
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪpləˈmætɪk kɔr / (AmE), / ˌdɪpləˈmætɪk kɔː / (BrE)
The collective body of diplomats representing foreign countries in a host nation.
