mattock

noun
digging hand tool with a flat blade
4.9 7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmæt.ək/ (AmE), /ˈmæt.ək/ (BrE)
a hand tool resembling a pickaxe, with a flat blade set at right angles to the handle, used for digging and chopping.

Examples

A mattock tool
He loosened the soil with a mattock before planting new shrubs.
She swung the mattock with steady blows to dig out the stubborn roots.
The old mattock hung on a hook in the shed, covered in rust.
