ricochet

noun
glancing rebound of a projectile
9.2 6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈrɪ.kə.ʃeɪ/ (AmE), /ˈrɪ.kə.ʃeɪ/ /; / /ˈrɪ.kə.ʃet/ (BrE)
a shot or projectile that rebounds off a surface at an angle rather than embedding or stopping

Examples

A ricochet bullet
The ricochet of the puck caught the goalie off guard.
The bullet struck the wall and made a loud ricochet before embedding in the ceiling.
We heard a ricochet as the stone skipped off the sidewalk.
