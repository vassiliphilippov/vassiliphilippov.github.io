imperial

noun
person of an empire
7.5
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɪriəl / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɪəriəl / (BrE)
a person or thing associated with an empire, especially someone holding power or authority in an empire

Examples

The imperials arrive
The imperials arrived in the city with a grand procession.
Many feared the power of the imperials during the height of the empire.
Artifacts belonging to the imperials are displayed in the national museum.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emperor - the ruler of an empire
  • antonym: subject - a person under the rule of an empire
