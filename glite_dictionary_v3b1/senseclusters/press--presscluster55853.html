press

verb
to push during childbirth
Pronunciation: / prɛs / (AmE), / prɛs / (BrE)
(of a woman in labor) to make strenuous pushing movements to help expel the baby during childbirth.

Examples

To press in labor
During labor, she was encouraged to press as strongly as possible.
He watched anxiously as his wife tried to press through the pain.
The midwife instructed her to press with each contraction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: push - to exert force to move something away from oneself, especially during childbirth
