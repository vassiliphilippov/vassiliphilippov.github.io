chestnut

noun
a deep reddish-brown color
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛs.nʌt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛs.nʌt / (BrE)
a deep reddish-brown color, like that of the nut of the chestnut tree

Examples

Chestnut color
His hair had turned from blond to chestnut over the years.
She painted the front door a warm shade of chestnut to match the autumn leaves.
The horse's coat was a rich chestnut that gleamed in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: auburn - a reddish-brown hair or color
  • degree lower: hazel - a light brown to yellowish-brown color, often used for eyes
  • synonym: mahogany - a reddish-brown color resembling mahogany wood
