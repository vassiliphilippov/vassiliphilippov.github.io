geocaching

noun
GPS treasure hunt
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːoʊˌkæʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːəʊˌkæʃɪŋ / (BrE)
an outdoor activity in which people use GPS devices to search for hidden containers called geocaches

Examples

Geocaching adventure
We spent the afternoon geocaching in the city park.
Many families enjoy geocaching as a way to explore new places together.
He discovered his first hidden container while geocaching in the mountains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orienteering - a sport involving navigation with a map and compass
  • synonym: treasure hunt - a game in which players search for hidden objects
