orientation

noun
introduction course for newcomers
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːriənˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɔːriənˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a course or session that introduces someone to a new situation, environment, or organization

Examples

Orientation session
All new students must attend the orientation before starting classes.
At the beginning of camp, an orientation session helps participants settle in and meet each other.
