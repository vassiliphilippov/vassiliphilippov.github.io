Synonyms

    8.6 0.4/m
    salvo is a group of shots fired together, often from artillery, while volley names any quick or simultaneous series of projectiles.
  • fusillade
    9.5 0.1/m
    fusillade is a long, rapid burst of shooting that keeps going, while volley is any quick or simultaneous series of shots.
  • burst
    5.6 1/m
    burst is a short, sudden discharge from a weapon, while volley covers any rapid series or many shots together.
  • massed fire
    8.8 0.00/m

  • concentrated fire
