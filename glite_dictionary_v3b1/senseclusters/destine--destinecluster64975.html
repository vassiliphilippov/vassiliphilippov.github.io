Synonyms

  • predestine
    9.2 0.3/m
    predestine means a higher power decides someone's future beforehand, while destine can mean being set by fate or by choice.
  • predestinate
    9.9 0.02/m
    predestinate is an older formal verb that says a divine power ordains events in advance, while destine can be more general.
  • predetermine
    5.8 1/m
    predetermine means people decide or influence outcomes before they happen, while destine often implies fate or intention beyond human choice.
  • fate
    7.4 0.2/m
    fate sends or leads events as if by destiny, while destine can mean being set by fate or by someone's plan.
