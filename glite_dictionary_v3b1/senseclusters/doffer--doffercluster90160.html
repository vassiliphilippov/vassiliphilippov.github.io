doffer

noun
textile removing machine
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdɑː.fər/ (AmE), /ˈdɒ.fə/ (BrE)
a machine or rotating roller in textile manufacturing that removes finished yarn, sliver, or full bobbins from spinning or carding machines

Examples

Textile doffer machine
The factory installed a new doffer to improve the efficiency of yarn removal.
A skilled technician checked the doffer to ensure it was operating smoothly.
Modern spinning mills often use an automatic doffer to handle full bobbins quickly.
