kickdown

noun
automatic gear shift
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪkˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈkɪkˌdaʊn / (BrE)
a device or action in an automatic transmission that shifts the car to a lower gear for faster acceleration

Examples

Automatic kickdown
When you press the accelerator hard, the car performs a kickdown to increase speed quickly.
The mechanic explained how the kickdown works in automatic transmissions for better performance.
A faulty kickdown can make it difficult for the car to accelerate smoothly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: downshift - to shift to a lower gear in a vehicle
