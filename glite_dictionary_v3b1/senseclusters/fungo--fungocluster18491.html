fungo

noun
baseball practice bat
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
a long, lightweight baseball bat used by coaches to hit balls to fielders during practice

Examples

A fungo bat
The coach grabbed a fungo and started hitting fly balls to the outfielders.
Using a fungo makes it easier to practice fielding high balls during training.
Every morning, the assistant would bring out the fungo for warm-up drills.
