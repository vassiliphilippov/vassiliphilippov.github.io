soft flag

noun
plant; Typha/reed mace/cattail
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɔft flæɡ / (AmE), / sɒft flæɡ / (BrE)
a common name for plants of the genus Typha, also known as reed maces or cattails, found in wetlands of America, Europe, North Africa, and Asia

Examples

The soft flag plant
