velvet plant

noun
ornamental plant with velvety purple-haired leaves
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛlvət plænt / (AmE), / ˈvɛlvɪt plɑːnt / (BrE)
A tropical ornamental plant known for its attractive velvety leaves covered with violet-purple hairs, especially from Java.

Examples

Purple velvet plant leaves
Botanists admire the unique purple hairs that cover the leaves of the velvet plant.
She placed a velvet plant on the windowsill to brighten up the room.
The velvet plant is popular among gardeners for its vividly colored, soft-textured leaves.
