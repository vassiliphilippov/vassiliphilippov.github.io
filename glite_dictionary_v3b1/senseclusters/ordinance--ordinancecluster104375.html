ordinance

noun
city or local government law
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrdɪnəns /; / ˈɔrdɪnəns / (AmE), / ˈɔːdɪnəns / (BrE)
an official rule, law, or order made by a government or authority, especially at the local level

Examples

Local ordinances
It is illegal to smoke in public parks under local ordinances.
Residents protested the proposed ordinance restricting pet ownership.
The city council passed a new ordinance to limit noise after 10 p.m.
Breaking the noise ordinance can result in a fine.
The city council passed an ordinance banning smoking in public parks.
The mayor issued an ordinance requiring all businesses to close by midnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: command - an authoritative order
  • synonym: order - a command or instruction given by someone in authority
  • synonym: regulation - an official rule or directive made by an authority
  • antonym: request - an act of asking politely or formally for something (less forceful than an 'ordinance')
  • synonym: statute - a written law passed by a legislative body
