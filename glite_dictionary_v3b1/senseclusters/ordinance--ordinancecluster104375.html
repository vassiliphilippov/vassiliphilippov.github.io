ordinance

noun
city or local government law

an official rule, law, or order made by a government or authority, especially at the local level

Examples

Local ordinances
It is illegal to smoke in public parks under local ordinances.
Residents protested the proposed ordinance restricting pet ownership.
The city council passed a new ordinance to limit noise after 10 p.m.
Breaking the noise ordinance can result in a fine.
The city council passed an ordinance banning smoking in public parks.
The mayor issued an ordinance requiring all businesses to close by midnight.

Synonyms

  • regulation
    3.4 61/m
    regulation is an official, detailed rule made by agencies to manage behaviour, while ordinance is a local law passed by a city council.
  • decree
    5.3 3.14/m
    decree is a strong, formal order issued by high authority, while ordinance is a local law set by municipal government.
  • edict
    7.2 2.01/m
    edict is a public proclamation that carries legal force from a ruler, while ordinance is a local law made by city officials.
  • fiat
    8.5 3.94/m
    fiat is an order given by someone in power without discussion, while ordinance is a law passed through local procedures.
