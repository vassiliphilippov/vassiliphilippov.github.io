than

conjunction
comparison conjunction
1.0
Pronunciation: / ðæn / (AmE), / ðæn / (BrE)
used to introduce the second part of a comparison, showing difference in degree or quality

Examples

Faster than anyone
He is much faster than anyone else on the team.
This book is more interesting than the one I read last week.
She would rather walk than take the bus to work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: as - used in comparisons to show equality
