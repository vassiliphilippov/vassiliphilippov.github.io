Taisho

proper noun
Japanese historical era
Pronunciation: / taɪˈʃoʊ / (AmE), / taɪˈʃəʊ / (BrE)
the period in Japanese history from 1912 to 1926, or something relating to that era

Examples

Taisho period
The Taisho period was marked by significant political change in Japan.
Many artists were influenced by Western styles during the Taisho era.
Some historic buildings from the Taisho period can still be seen in Tokyo today.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: Meiji - the Japanese era before Taisho, from 1868 to 1912
  • degree higher: Showa - the Japanese era after Taisho, from 1926 to 1989
