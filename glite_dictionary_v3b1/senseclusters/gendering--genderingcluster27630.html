gendering

noun
assigning gender roles
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛndərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛndərɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of assigning roles, behaviors, or expectations to people based on their gender

Examples

Gendering of toys
The gendering of toys can limit children's choices and interests from an early age.
Many educators are working to reduce gendering in classroom activities and materials.
Traditional gendering can influence children's interests and activities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: equality - the state of being equal, especially in status or rights
  • synonym: stereotyping - the act of assigning fixed ideas to people based on group membership
