City of God

noun
poetic/religious term for Heaven
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪti əv ˈɡɑd / (AmE), / ˈsɪti əv ˈɡɒd / (BrE)
a poetic or religious term referring to Heaven, regarded as the dwelling place of God and the blessed after death

Examples

Heavenly City of God
In medieval sermons, preachers often contrasted the Earthly City with the City of God.
Many believers hope one day to enter the City of God after this life.
The novel beautifully depicts the protagonist’s longing for the City of God.
