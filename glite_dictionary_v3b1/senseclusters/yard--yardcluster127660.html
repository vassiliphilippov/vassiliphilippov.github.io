yard

noun
unit of volume (cubic yard)
5.1 5/m
Pronunciation: / j…ërd / (AmE), / j…ëÀêd / (BrE)
A unit of volume used especially for sand, gravel, and similar materials, equal to one cubic yard (0.7646 cubic meters).

Examples

A cubic yard

They ordered five yards of gravel for the driveway construction.
