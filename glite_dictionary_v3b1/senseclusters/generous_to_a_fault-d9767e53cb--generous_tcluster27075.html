generous to a fault

adjective
excessively generous
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛnərəs tə ə fɔlt / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnərəs tə ə fɔːlt / (BrE)
excessively generous, to the point where it may cause problems or be considered unwise

Examples

Generous to a fault person
Her generous to a fault nature sometimes left her with little for herself.
My grandmother was generous to a fault, always giving away whatever she had to anyone in need.
Though generous to a fault, he often found himself taken advantage of by others.
