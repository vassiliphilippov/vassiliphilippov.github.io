plump down

verb
sit or drop down heavily
4.8 6.0
Pronunciation: / plʌmp daʊn / (AmE), / plʌmp daʊn / (BrE)
to sit or drop heavily or suddenly, often in a relaxed or careless way

Examples

To plump down on a chair

He plumped down his bag in the hallway and went to get a drink.
Instead of standing in line, the children plumped down on the grass to wait.
