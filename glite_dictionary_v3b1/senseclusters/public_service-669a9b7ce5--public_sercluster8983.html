public service

noun
service provided for the public good
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪk ˈsɝːvɪs /; / ˈpʌblɪk ˈsɜrvɪs / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
a service provided by the government or an official body for the benefit of the community, such as education, health care, or public transportation

Examples

Reliable public service is vital for a healthy, functioning society.
The government increased funding for public services such as healthcare and transportation.
