dryness

noun
detachment or lack of emotion
Pronunciation: / ˈdraɪ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈdraɪ.nəs / (BrE)
a manner of being unemotional, detached, or lacking warmth, especially in speech or behavior

Examples

Dryness in her voice
Her dryness during the meeting made her seem aloof to the others.
Some critics dislike the dryness of academic writing, preferring a warmer style.
The dryness in his tone suggested he was not emotionally involved.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detachment - the state of being objective or reserved in manner
  • antonym: warmth - emotional enthusiasm or affection; opposite of detachment in manner
