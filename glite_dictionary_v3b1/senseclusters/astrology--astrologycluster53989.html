astrology

noun
belief system relating celestial bodies to events
Pronunciation: / əˈstrɑlədʒi / (AmE), / əˈstrɒlədʒi / (BrE)
The study of the supposed influence of the positions and movements of celestial bodies on human affairs and natural events.

Examples

Study of astrology
Ancient civilizations often relied on astrology to make important decisions.
Despite its popularity, astrology is not considered a science by most researchers.
Many people read their astrology columns to learn what the stars predict for their future.

Related Terms

  • antonym: astronomy - the scientific study of celestial objects, space, and the universe
  • synonym: horoscope - forecast of a person's future based on astrology
