officer

noun
person with authority in the military
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːfəsər / (AmE), / ˈɒfɪsə / (BrE)
a person in the armed forces who holds a position of authority or command

Examples

Army officer
As an officer, she is responsible for the discipline and safety of her unit.
He trained for years to become a high-ranking officer in the navy.
The commanding officer addressed the troops before the mission began.

Related Terms
