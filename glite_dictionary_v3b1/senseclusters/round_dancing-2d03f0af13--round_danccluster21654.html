round dancing

noun
ballroom dance with a revolving movement
Pronunciation: / raʊnd ˈdænsɪŋ / (AmE), / raʊnd ˈdɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
A style of ballroom dance in which couples perform choreographed moves in a revolving movement, guided by a cuer.

Examples

Round dancing with couples
Many people enjoy round dancing because of its choreographed structure and social atmosphere.
The couple charmed the audience with their elegant movements during the round dancing portion of the evening.
They attended a workshop to improve their round dancing skills before the competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: waltz - a ballroom dance with a turning, revolving motion
