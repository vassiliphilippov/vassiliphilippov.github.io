Swarovski

proper noun
Austrian glassmaker
Pronunciation: / swəˈrɒvski / (AmE), / swəˈrɒvski / (BrE)
the surname of Daniel Swarovski, an Austrian glass cutter and businessman who founded the Swarovski company

Examples

Daniel Swarovski innovator
Daniel Swarovski revolutionized the glass industry with his innovative cutting techniques.
The legacy of Swarovski continues through the world-famous crystal company he established.
Many people associate the name Swarovski with high-quality crystal products.
