grub

verb
ask for and get for free; be a parasite
Pronunciation: / ɡrʌb / (AmE), / ɡrʌb / (BrE)
to ask for and get something, especially food or money, without paying for it; to act as a parasite

Examples

To grub for money
Don't let him grub off you—he needs to learn responsibility.
He always tries to grub a meal from his friends instead of buying his own.
She was known to grub money from relatives whenever she needed it.
