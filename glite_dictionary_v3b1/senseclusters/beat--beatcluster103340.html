beat

noun
route or area patrolled by police
Pronunciation: / bit / (AmE), / biːt / (BrE)
a route or area regularly patrolled by a police officer or security guard

Examples

A police beat
After years on the same beat, she recognized most shopkeepers in the neighborhood.
The new officer quickly learned every street on his beat.
The report said he left his beat without permission.

Related Terms

  • synonym: patrol - an act of walking or traveling around an area to guard or monitor it
