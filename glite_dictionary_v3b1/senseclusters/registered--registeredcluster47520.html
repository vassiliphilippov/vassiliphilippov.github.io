registered

adjective
officially recorded animal (breed association)
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdʒɪstɚd / (AmE), / ˈrɛdʒɪstəd / (BrE)
(of an animal) officially recorded with or certified by a recognized breed association, especially in a stud book.

Examples

Registered purebred dog
A registered Hereford bull was sold at the auction yesterday.
She breeds registered Arabian horses on her farm.
The kennel club only accepts registered dogs for this competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: purebred - an animal of pure breed, especially one registered with a breed association
