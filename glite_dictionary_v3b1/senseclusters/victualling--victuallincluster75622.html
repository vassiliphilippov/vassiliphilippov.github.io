victualling

verb
to supply provisions
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪtəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvɪtəlɪŋ / (BrE)
to supply food and provisions, especially for a ship or group of people

Examples

To victual a ship
The sailors spent hours victualling the ship before their long journey.
He was responsible for victualling the expedition with enough food and water.
Before setting out, the team focused on victualling to ensure they had all necessary supplies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: provisioning - supplying with necessary food and equipment
  • antonym: starving - suffering or dying from lack of food
