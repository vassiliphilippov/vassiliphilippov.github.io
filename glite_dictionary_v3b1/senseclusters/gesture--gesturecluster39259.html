Examples

To gesture toward someone

Synonyms

  • motion
    2.1 5/m
    motion is a simple hand or head movement to show or direct someone, while gesture is a broader movement to express or indicate meaning.
  • gesticulate
    7.6 0.5/m
    gesticulate means making lively hand or arm movements while speaking to add emphasis, while gesture is any movement to express or indicate.
  • signal
    3.0 10/m
    signal is an intentional sign, often silent or visual to give information, while gesture is a bodily movement to express or indicate something.
