synergistic

adjective
working together for enhanced effect
6.2 5.4
Pronunciation: /sɪn.ər.ˈdʒɪ.stɪk/ (AmE), /sɪn.ə.ˈdʒɪ.stɪk/ (BrE)
involving or resulting from people or things working together to produce a greater effect than they could separately

Examples

Synergistic teamwork
The project succeeded due to the synergistic collaboration of experts from different fields.
The researchers found that the drugs had a synergistic effect when combined.
These companies formed a synergistic partnership to innovate more effectively.
A synergistic team can achieve far more than individuals working separately.
Scientists are studying how the synergistic actions of different muscles improve athletic performance.
The two medications were found to have a synergistic effect when used together.

  • antonym: antagonistic - acting in opposition or hostility
  • synonym: cooperative - working jointly towards the same end
