wham

noun
sudden loud impact
3.6
Pronunciation: / wæm / (AmE), / wæm / (BrE)
a sudden loud sound or a forceful impact, often caused by something hitting another object

Examples

A loud wham
There was a loud wham when the book fell off the shelf.
The car door closed with a sharp wham that startled everyone nearby.
Suddenly, a wham echoed through the room as the window slammed shut.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crash - a loud sound caused by something breaking or hitting
  • antonym: silence - the absence of sound
