city-state

noun
independent city that is a sovereign state
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪtiˌsteɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɪtiˌsteɪt / (BrE)
A city that forms an independent and sovereign state, having its own government and jurisdiction.

Examples

An ancient city-state
Ancient Athens was a powerful city-state that played a leading role in Greek history.
Many historians study how city-states like Venice influenced European politics and trade.
Singapore is a modern city-state known for its efficient government and booming economy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: empire - A group of states or territories controlled by one ruler or government
  • synonym: nation-state - An independent state whose citizens are relatively homogeneous in factors like language or descent
