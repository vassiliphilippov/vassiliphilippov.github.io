boardwalk

noun
wooden walkway, often by the sea
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔrdˌwɑk / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdˌwɔːk / (BrE)
a walkway made of wooden boards, especially along or near a beach or waterfront

Examples

A boardwalk by the beach
The boardwalk is a popular place for families to gather on summer evenings.
Vendors lined the boardwalk, selling snacks and souvenirs to tourists.
We strolled along the boardwalk, watching the waves crash against the shore.
