Jacksonia

noun
genus of Australian shrubs with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / dʒækˈsoʊniə /; / ˌdʒækˈsoʊniə / (AmE), / dʒækˈsəʊniə /; / ˌdʒækˈsəʊniə / (BrE)
a genus of Australian shrubs with yellow flowers, usually lacking true leaves but having leaflike stems or branches; some species have spines
