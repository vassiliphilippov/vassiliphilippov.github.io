intersegmental

adjective
between segments
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərsɛɡˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəsɛɡˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
relating to or occurring between segments, especially in anatomy or linguistics

Examples

Intersegmental nerves
The intersegmental nerves connect different parts of the spinal cord.
Researchers studied the intersegmental boundaries in the developing embryo.
Insects rely on intersegmental muscles to coordinate movement between body sections.

Related Terms

  • antonym: segmental - relating to a segment or segments
