lookit

interjection
attention-getting exclamation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlʊkɪt / (AmE), / ˈlʊkɪt / (BrE)
an informal exclamation used to get someone's attention, introduce a statement, or express annoyance

Examples

Lookit, listen to me
Lookit, I told you already that the meeting starts at nine.
If you want to understand, lookit, it's really not that complicated.
Lookit, I don't have time to explain everything again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: listen - an exclamation used to get someone's attention before speaking
