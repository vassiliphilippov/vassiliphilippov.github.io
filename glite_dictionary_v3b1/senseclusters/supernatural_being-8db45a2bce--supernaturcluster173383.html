supernatural being

noun
being with powers beyond nature
Pronunciation: / ˌsuː.pɚˈnætʃ.ɚ.əl ˈbiː.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌsuː.pəˈnætʃ.ər.əl ˈbiː.ɪŋ / (BrE)
A being believed to exist beyond the laws of nature and to possess powers that can influence human events.

Examples

A supernatural being appears
He claimed to have seen a supernatural being appear suddenly in the old abandoned house.
Some cultures believe every forest is inhabited by a supernatural being that protects the land.
The legend describes a supernatural being who guides lost travelers back to their village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deity - a god or goddess, often considered a supernatural being
  • synonym: spirit - an incorporeal being, often believed to be the soul of a deceased person or supernatural agent
