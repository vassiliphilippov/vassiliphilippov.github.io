kill

verb
to cause intense pain (informal)
Pronunciation: / kɪl / (AmE), / kɪl / (BrE)
to cause great pain, exhaustion, or strong emotion (such as making someone laugh a lot or feel very tired)

Examples

My back kills me
After shoveling snow, his back was absolutely killing him.
My head is killing me—I think I need to lie down.
These high heels are killing my feet after a long day at work.
She kills every time she gets on stage—her audiences love her.
That movie killed me—I laughed so hard I cried.
The comedian's jokes absolutely killed me last night.
The long hike yesterday absolutely killed me.
Training for the marathon killed her, but she was proud of herself.
Working two jobs at once is really killing him these days.
