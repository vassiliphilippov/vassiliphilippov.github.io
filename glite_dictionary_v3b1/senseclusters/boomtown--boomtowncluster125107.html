boomtown

noun
rapidly growing town
4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈbuːm.taʊn/ (AmE), /ˈbuːm.taʊn/ (BrE)
a town that grows very quickly and becomes prosperous because of sudden economic success, often from a new industry or resource

Examples

A boomtown grows fast
The discovery of oil quickly transformed the small settlement into a bustling boomtown.
Many people moved to the boomtown hoping to find work and make their fortune.
After the tech company opened its headquarters, the area became a modern boomtown almost overnight.
The discovery of oil turned the small village into a boomtown.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gold rush town - a town that grew quickly due to a gold rush
  • synonym: prosperous city - a city that is wealthy and successful
