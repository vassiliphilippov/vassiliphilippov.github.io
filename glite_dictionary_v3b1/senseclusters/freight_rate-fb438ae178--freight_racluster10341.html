freight rate

noun
cost to transport goods by carrier
8.7
Pronunciation: / freɪt reɪt / (AmE), / freɪt reɪt / (BrE)
the charge or cost for transporting goods by a common carrier, such as by rail, truck, ship, or air

Examples

The freight rate cost
Companies compare freight rates to find the most cost-effective shipping options.
The freight rate for shipping containers has increased this year.
The contract specifies a fixed freight rate for all deliveries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shipping cost - the expense to send goods to a destination
