Brasov

proper noun
city in central Romania
6.8
Pronunciation: / brɑːˈʃɔv / (AmE), / brɑːˈʃɒv / (BrE)
A city in central Romania located in the foothills of the Transylvanian Alps, known for its medieval architecture and historic old town.
