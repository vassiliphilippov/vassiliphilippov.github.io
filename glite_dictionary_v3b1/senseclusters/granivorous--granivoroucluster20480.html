granivorous

adjective
eating seeds or grain
9.6
Pronunciation: / ɡrəˈnɪvərəs / (AmE), / ɡrəˈnɪvərəs / (BrE)
feeding mainly or exclusively on seeds or grain

Examples

Granivorous birds
Many desert birds are granivorous and rely on seeds as their main food source.
The granivorous habits of these rodents help control weed populations in the fields.
Finches are well known for their granivorous diet, using their beaks to crack open seeds.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carnivorous - feeding mainly on meat
  • synonym: herbivorous - feeding mainly on plants
