get into

verb
become involved or familiar with
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt ˈɪntu / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈɪntu / (BrE)
to become interested in or start to be involved with something; to familiarize oneself thoroughly with a subject or activity

Examples

To get into photography
After college, she really got into photography and spent hours learning different techniques.
He never used to care about politics, but lately he's gotten into following elections closely.
I found it hard to get into this novel at first, but it became fascinating halfway through.

Related Terms

  • synonym: become involved in - to start participating in or taking part in something
