superuniverse

noun
universe of universes
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsuː.pər.juː.nə.vɜːrs/ /; / /ˈsuː.pər.juː.nɪ.vɜːrs/ (AmE), /ˈsuː.pə.juː.nə.vɜː/ /; / /ˈsuː.pə.juː.nɪ.vɜː/ (BrE)
