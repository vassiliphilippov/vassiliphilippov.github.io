superuniverse

noun
universe of universes
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌjuːnɪvɜrs /; / ˌsuːpərˈjuːnɪvɜrs / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌjuːnɪvɜːs /; / ˌsuːpəˈjuːnɪvɜːs / (BrE)
a very large or hypothetical universe that contains many other universes, often used in science fiction or spiritual beliefs

Examples

A superuniverse of worlds
The science fiction novel explores a superuniverse where countless worlds are connected.
Scientists debated the possibility of a superuniverse beyond our own observable cosmos.
In the game, players travel between realities within a vast superuniverse.
Some spiritual texts describe a superuniverse as containing countless galaxies and worlds.
The concept of a superuniverse is central to certain cosmological teachings.
Followers believe that each superuniverse is managed by a unique spiritual administration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: multiverse - a collection of multiple possible universes
  • degree lower: universe - all of space, time, matter, and energy considered as a whole
