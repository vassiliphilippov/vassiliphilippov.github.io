zareba

noun
thorn bush enclosure
9.9
Pronunciation: / zəˈriːbə / (AmE), / zəˈriːbə / (BrE)
a fence or enclosure made of thorn bushes, especially used in parts of Africa to protect people or livestock

Examples

A zareba fence
The villagers built a zareba to protect their livestock from wild animals.
A sturdy zareba surrounded the camp, keeping out predators at night.
They repaired the zareba after a storm damaged part of the enclosure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stockade - a defensive barrier made of strong posts or timbers
