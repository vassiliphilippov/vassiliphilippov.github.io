pale blue

adjective
light shade of blue
2.1 1.0
Pronunciation: / peɪl bluː / (AmE), / peɪl bluː / (BrE)
A light or soft shade of blue; blue that has been diluted or whitened.

Examples

A pale blue shirt

In the early morning, the lake shimmered under a pale blue sky.
