pale blue

adjective
light shade of blue
Pronunciation: / peɪl bluː / (AmE), / peɪl bluː / (BrE)
A light or soft shade of blue; blue that has been diluted or whitened.

Examples

A pale blue shirt
He wore a pale blue shirt to the interview, hoping it would make a good impression.
Her bedroom walls were painted a soothing pale blue that made the space feel airy and light.
In the early morning, the lake shimmered under a pale blue sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: azure - a bright, clear blue color
  • degree higher: navy blue - a very dark shade of blue
  • synonym: sky blue - a pure, light blue like a clear sky
