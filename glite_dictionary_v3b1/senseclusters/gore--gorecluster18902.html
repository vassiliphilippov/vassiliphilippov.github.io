gore

verb
cut fabric into gores (dressmaking)
8.4
Pronunciation: / ɡɔɹ / (AmE), / ɡɔː / (BrE)
to cut (fabric) into triangular pieces called gores, especially to make it fit a particular shape, as in dressmaking

Examples

To gore fabric
