pinkish

adjective
somewhat pink in color
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪŋkɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋkɪʃ / (BrE)
having a slight or pale pink color; somewhat pink.

Examples

A pinkish color

The sky turned a beautiful pinkish hue as the sun set.
