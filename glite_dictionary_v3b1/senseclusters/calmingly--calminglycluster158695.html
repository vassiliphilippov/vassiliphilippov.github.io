calmingly

adverb
in a soothing manner
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈkɑːmɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that makes someone feel more relaxed or less anxious

Examples

Speak calmingly to children
She spoke calmingly to the frightened child until he stopped crying.
The teacher addressed the class calmingly after the fire alarm startled everyone.
He placed his hand calmingly on her shoulder to reassure her.

Related Terms

  • antonym: agitatedly - in a way that shows nervousness or excitement
  • synonym: reassuringly - in a way that removes doubt or fear
  • synonym: soothingly - in a way that calms or comforts
