animal fancier

noun
a person involved with keeping or breeding animals
Pronunciation: / ˈænɪməl ˈfænsiər / (AmE), / ˈænɪməl ˈfænsiə / (BrE)
a person who owns, breeds, shows, or judges animals, especially as a hobby or professional interest

Examples

Animal fancier attends shows
As an animal fancier, she attends dog shows across the country every year.
Many animal fanciers spend years perfecting the traits of specific breeds.
The club was formed by a group of local animal fanciers interested in exotic birds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breeder - a person who breeds animals, especially as an occupation
