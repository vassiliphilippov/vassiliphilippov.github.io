sanguine

adjective
having a healthy reddish complexion
Pronunciation: / ˈsæŋɡwɪn / (AmE), / ˈsaŋɡwɪn / (BrE)
having a healthy reddish color, especially of the complexion; ruddy.

Examples

Sanguine cheeks
After a day hiking in the mountains, his sanguine cheeks glowed in the evening sun.
Her sanguine complexion made her look healthy and full of life.
Many portraits from the era depict nobles with sanguine faces, signifying vitality and vigor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rubicund - having a ruddy or reddish complexion
  • synonym: ruddy - having a healthy red color
