fetoscope

noun
endoscopic instrument for visualising the fetus
7.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / fit59skoap / (AmE), / fit59sk59ap / (BrE)
(in medicine) a small endoscopic instrument inserted into the uterus during fetoscopic procedures to visualise the fetus or assist minimally invasive fetal surgery.

Examples

Surgical fetoscope use
During fetal surgery, the surgeon inserted a fetoscope to inspect the fetus directly.
The team used a small-diameter fetoscope to perform minimally invasive repair of the fetal membrane.
Residents practiced navigating a fetoscope through an artificial uterus model during simulation training.
