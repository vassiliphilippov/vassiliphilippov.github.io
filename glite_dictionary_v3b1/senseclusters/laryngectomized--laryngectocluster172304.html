laryngectomized

adjective
without a larynx
Pronunciation: / ləˌrɪŋɡɛk.təˌmaɪzd / (AmE), / ləˌrɪŋɡɛk.təˌmaɪzd / (BrE)
having had the larynx surgically removed, typically as a treatment for cancer

Examples

Laryngectomized patient
After becoming laryngectomized, he learned to communicate using an electronic device.
Support groups for laryngectomized individuals provide advice on adjusting to life after surgery.
The hospital offers special care for laryngectomized patients recovering from throat cancer.
