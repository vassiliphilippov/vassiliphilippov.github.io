laughable

adjective
provoking laughter; absurd or funny
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlæfəbəl / (AmE), / ˈlɑːfəbəl /; / ˈlɑːfəbl̩ / (BrE)
so absurd, funny, or unreasonable that it deserves to be laughed at; ridiculous

Examples

A laughable excuse
His explanation for being late was so laughable that nobody believed him.
Some of the movie's special effects were unintentionally laughable.
The suggestion that cats can do your taxes is simply laughable.
His claim to have finished all the work overnight was simply laughable.
Some of the excuses he gave were too laughable to believe.
The proposal was so laughable that no one at the meeting could take it seriously.

Related Terms

  • synonym: funny - amusing; causing laughter
  • synonym: ridiculous - deserving or inviting derision or mockery
  • antonym: serious - not funny or laughable; solemn or earnest
