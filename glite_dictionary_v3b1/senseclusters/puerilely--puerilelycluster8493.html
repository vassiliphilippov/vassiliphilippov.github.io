puerilely

adverb
childishly
Pronunciation: / ˈpjʊrəli / (AmE), / ˈpjʊərɪli / (BrE)
in a childish or immature manner

Examples

To puerilely behave
He answered the teacher's question puerilely, making everyone in the class laugh.
The debate quickly turned unproductive as both sides argued puerilely over minor details.
She reacted puerilely to the criticism, refusing to listen to any advice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: childishly - in a way that is typical of a child or immature
  • antonym: maturely - in a way that shows maturity or adult behavior
