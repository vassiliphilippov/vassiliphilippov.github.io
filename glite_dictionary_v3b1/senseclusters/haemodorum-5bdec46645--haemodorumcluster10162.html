Haemodorum

noun
genus of Australian herbs (Haemodoraceae)
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːməˈdɔːrəm / (AmE), / ˌhiːməˈdɔːrəm / (BrE)
A genus of perennial herbs in the family Haemodoraceae, native to Australia and known for their red roots.
