armyworm

noun
caterpillar pest of crops
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrmiˌwɜrm /; / ˈɑrmiˌwɜrm / (AmE), / ˈɑːmiˌwɜːm / (BrE)
a caterpillar (larva of certain moths) that moves in large groups and destroys crops, especially grains
