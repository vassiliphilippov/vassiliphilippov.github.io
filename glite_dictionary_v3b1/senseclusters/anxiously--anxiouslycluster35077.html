anxiously

adverb
in a worried or uneasy way
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋkʃəsli / (AmE), / ˈæŋkʃəsli / (BrE)
In a manner showing worry, nervousness, or unease.

Examples

Look anxiously at watch
He glanced anxiously at his watch as the meeting ran late.
Parents stood anxiously outside the school, waiting for news.
She waited anxiously for the results of the exam.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmly - in a relaxed and untroubled manner
  • synonym: nervously - in a manner showing nervousness or worry
