Examples

Look anxiously at watch

She waited anxiously for the results of the exam.

Synonyms

  • nervously
    2.3 10/m
    nervously shows physical signs like shaking or quick words, while anxiously expresses a more general worried or uneasy feeling.
  • worriedly
    2.0 0.53/m
    worriedly describes a worried, often quiet state of mind, while anxiously covers broader nervousness and unease in many situations.
  • apprehensively
    6.0 0.31/m
    apprehensively implies fear that something bad may happen, while anxiously means general worry or nervousness.
  • uneasily
    4.2 3.13/m
    uneasily suggests physical discomfort or awkwardness, while anxiously focuses more on worry and nervous concern.
  • tensely
    3.9 0.56/m
    tensely describes muscle tightness or strong strain, while anxiously describes inner worry and nervous feelings rather than physical tension.
