snotty

adjective
dirty with nasal mucus
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɑti / (AmE), / ˈsnɒti / (BrE)
Dirty or soiled with nasal mucus; covered with or containing nasal discharge.

Examples

Snotty nose
Please wash your face, it's all snotty from your cold.
She handed her snotty tissue to her mother for help.
The child came home with a snotty nose after playing outside.

Related Terms
