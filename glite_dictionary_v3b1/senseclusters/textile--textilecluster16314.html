textile

noun
material made by weaving or knitting fibres

A type of fabric or cloth produced by weaving, knitting, or felting fibres, used especially for making clothes, furnishings, and other goods.

Examples

A textile product
India has a long history of producing fine textiles for export and local use.
Modern textile factories use advanced technology to create strong and versatile fabrics.
The museum exhibited ancient textiles from different cultures around the world.

Synonyms

  • fabric
    1.0 27/m
    fabric refers to woven or knitted material used for clothing and curtains, while textile is a broader, often industrial term.
  • cloth
    1.0 27/m
    cloth is often a simple piece of woven material used for everyday items, while textile names the whole range of such materials.
