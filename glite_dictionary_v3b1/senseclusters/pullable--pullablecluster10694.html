pullable

adjective
able to be pulled
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊləbəl / (AmE), / ˈpʊləbəl / (BrE)
able to be pulled or moved by pulling

Examples

Pullable suitcase
The lightweight suitcase is easily pullable even when fully packed.
Make sure the door is pullable from both sides in case of emergency.
This toy is designed to be pullable by young children without much effort.

Related Terms

  • antonym: immovable - impossible to move
  • synonym: movable - able to be moved from one place to another
