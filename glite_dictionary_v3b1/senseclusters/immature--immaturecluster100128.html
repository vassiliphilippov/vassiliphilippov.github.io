Synonyms

    undeveloped is not fully grown or finished in form, while immature suggests something not yet mature or ready.
  • underdeveloped
    4.3 0.33/m
    underdeveloped lacks the normal level of growth or progress, while immature simply means not yet fully grown or mature.
  • embryonic
    7.2 0.80/m
    embryonic is at a very early, basic starting stage, while immature covers any stage before full growth or maturity.
  • fledgling
    6.4 1.46/m
    fledgling is young and inexperienced, often used for people or projects, while immature means not fully grown or mature.
