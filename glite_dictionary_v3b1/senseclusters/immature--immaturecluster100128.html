immature

adjective
not fully developed; not ripe
1.0 2.5
Pronunciation: /ɪ.mə.ˈtʃʊr/ /; / /ɪ.mə.ˈt.jʊr/ /; / /ɪ.mə.ˈtʃər/ (AmE), /ɪ.mə.ˈtʃʊə/ /; / /ɪ.mə.ˈtʃə/ /; / /ɪ.mə.ˈt.jʊə/ (BrE)
not fully developed or mature; at an early stage of growth or development (used for people, animals, plants, or things)

Examples
