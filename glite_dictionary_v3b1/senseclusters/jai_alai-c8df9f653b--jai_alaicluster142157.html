jai alai

noun
Basque ball game played with a basket
5.0
Pronunciation: / haɪ əˈlaɪ / (AmE), / haɪ əˈlaɪ / (BrE)
a game of Basque origin played on a court with a ball bounced off walls, using a curved wicker basket attached to the player's arm

Examples

A jai alai match
He watched a thrilling jai alai match while visiting Spain.
Several casinos in Florida offer regular jai alai games for visitors to enjoy.
The sport of jai alai is known for its fast pace and dangerous plays.
