misanthropic

adjective
disliking or distrusting humankind
7.2 7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪs.ænˈθrɑː.pɪk /; / ˌmɪsənˈθrɑːpɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɪs.ænˈθrɒp.ɪk /; / ˌmɪsænˈθrɒpɪk / (BrE)
disliking or distrusting people and avoiding human society

Examples
