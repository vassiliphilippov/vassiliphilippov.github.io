strophic

adjective
same melody for verses
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈstrɑː.fɪk/ /; / /ˈstroʊ.fɪk/ (AmE), /ˈstrɒ.fɪk/ /; / /ˈstrəʊ.fɪk/ (BrE)
