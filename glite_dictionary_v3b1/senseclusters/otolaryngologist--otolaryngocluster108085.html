otolaryngologist

noun
specialist in ear, nose, and throat disorders
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊtoʊˌlærɪnˈɡɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌəʊtəʊˌlærɪnˈɡɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the ear, nose, and throat

Examples

Otolaryngologist doctor
After losing his voice, he was referred to an otolaryngologist for evaluation.
She scheduled an appointment with an otolaryngologist for her chronic sinus issues.
The otolaryngologist examined my ears and recommended further tests.
