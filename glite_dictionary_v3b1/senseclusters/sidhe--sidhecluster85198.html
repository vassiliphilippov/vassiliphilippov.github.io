sidhe

noun
fairy mound in folklore
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʃiː / (AmE), / ʃiː / (BrE)
a mound or hill in Irish folklore believed to be the home of supernatural beings or fairies

Examples

Fairy sidhe mound
In Irish legends, the sidhe are said to be entrances to the world of fairies.
Locals avoid disturbing the old sidhe on the hillside, fearing bad luck.
The story describes a hero who visits a mysterious sidhe to seek wisdom.
