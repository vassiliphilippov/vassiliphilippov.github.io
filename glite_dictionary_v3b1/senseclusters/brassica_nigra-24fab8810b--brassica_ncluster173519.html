Brassica nigra

noun
plant producing seeds for mustard
7.5
Pronunciation: / brəˈsɪkə ˈnaɪɡrə / (AmE), / brəˈsɪkə ˈnɪɡrə / (BrE)
a Eurasian annual plant, commonly known as black mustard, cultivated for its pungent seeds used in making table mustard
