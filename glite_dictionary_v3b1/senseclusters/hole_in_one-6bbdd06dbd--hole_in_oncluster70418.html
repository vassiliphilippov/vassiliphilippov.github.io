hole in one

noun
golf shot going directly into the hole
5.8
Pronunciation: / hoʊl ɪn wʌn / (AmE), / həʊl ɪn wʌn / (BrE)
a shot in golf that lands the ball in the hole with a single stroke

Examples

A hole in one shot
After years of playing, she finally made her first hole in one on the seventh hole.
He was awarded a trophy for his incredible hole in one during the club championship.
Scoring a hole in one is a rare achievement that every golfer dreams about.
