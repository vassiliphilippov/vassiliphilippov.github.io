embracer

noun
person who accepts
5.8
Pronunciation: / ɛmˈbreɪsər / (AmE), / ɪmˈbreɪsə / (BrE)
a person who willingly accepts, supports, or adopts something or someone

Examples

Early embracer of change
As an early embracer of electric cars, he inspired others to follow his lead.
The company is looking for embracers of innovation to join their team.
She became a passionate embracer of healthy living after her diagnosis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adopter - a person who takes up or starts to use something new
  • antonym: opponent - a person who disagrees with or resists something
  • synonym: supporter - a person who approves of and encourages someone or something
