Scabiosa atropurpurea

noun
flowering plant with purple to crimson heads
9.9
Pronunciation: / skæˈbioʊsə ˌætroʊˌpɜrˈpjʊriə / (AmE), / skəˈbɪəʊsə ˌatrəʊˌpɜːˈpjʊərɪə / (BrE)
An Old World annual plant with fragrant purple to deep crimson flower heads, commonly known as the pincushion flower and naturalized in the United States.
