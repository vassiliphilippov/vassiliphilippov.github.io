kip down

verb
go to bed; prepare for sleep (informal, British)
Pronunciation: / kɪp daʊn / (AmE), / kɪp daʊn / (BrE)
(informal, British) To prepare for sleep or go to bed, especially temporarily or in a place that is not one's usual bed.

Examples

To kip down temporarily
After the party ended, several guests decided to kip down in the living room.
I'm so tired; I think I'll kip down on the sofa for a few hours.
We didn't book a hotel, so we had to kip down at a friend's place.
