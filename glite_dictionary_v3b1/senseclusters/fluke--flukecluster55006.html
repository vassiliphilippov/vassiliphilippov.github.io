fluke

noun
an unexpected stroke of luck
8.0
Pronunciation: / fluːk / (AmE), / fluːk / (BrE)
an unexpected stroke of luck; something good that happens by chance rather than by intention

Examples

A lucky fluke
His goal was a fluke, as the ball bounced off his knee and into the net.
They met again years later, but it was just a fluke rather than a planned reunion.
Winning the lottery was a complete fluke and not something she ever expected.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accident - something that happens unexpectedly or unintentionally
  • synonym: chance - luck, or the occurrence of events without apparent cause
  • antonym: misfortune - bad luck; an unlucky event
