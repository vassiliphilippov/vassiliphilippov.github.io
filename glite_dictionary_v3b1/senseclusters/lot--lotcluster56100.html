lot

noun
a large number or amount (e.g., a lot of money)
Pronunciation: / lɑt / (AmE), / lɒt / (BrE)
a large number or amount of something; a great deal (usually used with 'of')

Examples

A lot of money
She spent a lot of money on her new car.
There were lot of people at the concert last night.
We still have a lot of work to do before the deadline.
