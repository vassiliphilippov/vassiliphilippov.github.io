bruschetta

noun
Italian bread appetizer
Pronunciation: / bruːˈskɛtə / (AmE), / bruːˈskɛtə / (BrE)
an Italian appetizer of toasted or grilled bread topped with olive oil, tomatoes, herbs, or other ingredients

Examples

Toasted bruschetta bread
