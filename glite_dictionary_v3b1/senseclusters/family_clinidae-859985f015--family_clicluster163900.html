family Clinidae

noun
family of viviparous blennies
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli ˈklaɪnɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ˈklɪnɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of small, elongated, viviparous blennies found in temperate and tropical seas, known for inhabiting rocky reefs and kelp beds.
