marketeer

verb
to sell at markets
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːrkəˈtɪr / (AmE), / ˌmɑːkəˈtɪə / (BrE)
to sell or promote goods, especially by trading at markets or stalls

Examples

To marketeer goods
She marketeered her homemade jams at the local farmers' market every weekend.
Many artisans marketeer their crafts at festivals to reach new customers.
He marketeered antiques at street fairs to supplement his income.

Related Terms

  • antonym: buy - to purchase goods or services
  • synonym: vend - to sell goods, especially in a public place
