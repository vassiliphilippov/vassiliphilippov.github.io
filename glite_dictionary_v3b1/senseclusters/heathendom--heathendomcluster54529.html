heathendom

noun
non-Christian regions
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːðəndəm / (AmE), / ˈhiːðəndəm / (BrE)
non-Christian (pagan) peoples, lands, or beliefs, especially in historical or religious contexts

Examples

Medieval writers often described distant heathendom as mysterious and dangerous.
