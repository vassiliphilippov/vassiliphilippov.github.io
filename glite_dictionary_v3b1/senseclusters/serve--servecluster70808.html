serve

verb
deliver a legal document to someone
Pronunciation: / sɝv / (AmE), / sɜːv / (BrE)
(law) To formally deliver a legal document such as a warrant or summons to someone in accordance with legal procedures.

Examples

To serve a notice
Officers must follow strict procedures when serving a legal notice.
She was served a summons to appear in court next month.
The sheriff arrived to serve the warrant to the defendant.
