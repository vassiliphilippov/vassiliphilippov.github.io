denuclearization

noun
removal of nuclear weapons
Pronunciation: / diːˌnuːkliərɪˈzeɪʃən /; / diˌnuːkliərɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / diːˌnjuːkliərʌɪˈzeɪʃən /; / diˌnjuːkliərʌɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of removing or banning nuclear weapons, materials, or technology from a country, region, or organization

Examples

The denuclearization process
The international community supported the denuclearization of the region to promote peace and security.
Talks between the two countries focused on the denuclearization of the border area.
The agreement outlined steps toward the complete denuclearization of the peninsula.
The two countries agreed to begin talks on denuclearization to promote peace in the region.
International organizations monitor the denuclearization process to ensure compliance with treaties.
After years of negotiation, denuclearization became a central goal for the new administration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disarmament - the reduction or elimination of military weapons
  • antonym: nuclearization - the process of acquiring or deploying nuclear weapons
