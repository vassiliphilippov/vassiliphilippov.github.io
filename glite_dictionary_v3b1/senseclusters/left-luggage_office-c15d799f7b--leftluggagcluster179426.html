left-luggage office

noun
place to temporarily store baggage
5.4 2.9
Pronunciation: /left.ˈlʌg.ɪdʒ ˈɔːf.ɪs/ /; / /left.ˈlʌg.ə.dʒ ˈɔːf.ɪs/ /; / /left.ˈlʌg.ɪdʒ ˈɔːf.əs/ (AmE), /left.ˈlʌg.ɪdʒ ˈɒf.ɪs/ /; / /left.ˈlʌg.ə.dʒ ˈɒf.ɪs/ /; / /left.ˈlʌg.ɪdʒ ˈɒf.əs/ (BrE)
a place, usually at a railway station or airport, where travelers can leave their baggage or parcels for a short period and collect them later

Examples

The left-luggage office
I collected my backpack from the left-luggage office before catching my train.
The left-luggage office is located next to the main entrance of the station.
We left our suitcases at the left-luggage office while we explored the city.
