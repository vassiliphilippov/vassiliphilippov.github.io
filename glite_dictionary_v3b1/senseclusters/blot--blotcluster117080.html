blot

noun
a mark of disgrace or dishonor
6.3 0.20/m
Pronunciation: / blÉ‘t / (AmE), / blÉ’t / (BrE)
an act or event that damages a person's reputation or record; a disgrace or stain

Examples

A blot on his record
Even one mistake can leave a blot on your record.
Her generosity erased the blot of her earlier misdeeds.
The scandal was a blot on his distinguished career.
