girlie

adjective
stereotypically feminine
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɜrli / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːli / (BrE)
designed for or characteristic of girls, often in a stereotypically feminine or youthful way

Examples

Girlie pink dress
She chose a girlie dress with lots of pink and frills for the party.
The magazine featured girlie designs and advice for young women.
He thought the decorations were too girlie for his taste.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boyish - having qualities or appearance traditionally associated with boys
  • synonym: feminine - having qualities or appearance traditionally associated with women or girls
