unwinking

adjective
not blinking
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
not blinking; steady and unflinching (used for eyes or a gaze, either literally or figuratively)
