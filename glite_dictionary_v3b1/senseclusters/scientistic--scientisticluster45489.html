scientistic

adjective
falsely scientific
8.9
Pronunciation: /saɪ.ən.ˈtɪs.tɪk/ (AmE), /saɪ.ən.ˈtɪs.tɪk/ (BrE)
claiming to be scientific or using scientific language, but lacking real scientific basis or rigor

Examples

Scientistic claims
The article used a scientistic approach, but its arguments were not supported by real evidence.
Critics accused the policy makers of relying on scientistic jargon to justify their decisions.
Her explanation sounded scientistic, but experts quickly pointed out its flaws.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pseudo-scientific - appearing scientific but not based on real science
  • antonym: scientific - based on or using the methods of science
