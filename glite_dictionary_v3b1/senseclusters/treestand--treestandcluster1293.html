treestand

noun
Christmas tree holder
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtriːˌstænd / (AmE), / ˈtriːˌstænd / (BrE)
a sturdy base or support designed to hold a cut Christmas tree upright indoors

Examples

A treestand base
We bought a new treestand to make sure our Christmas tree would not tip over.
After placing the tree in the treestand, they filled it with water to keep the tree fresh.
The metal treestand was sturdy enough to support even the largest pine in the store.
