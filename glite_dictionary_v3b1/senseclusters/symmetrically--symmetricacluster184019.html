symmetrically

adverb
in a symmetrical or balanced way
1.9
Pronunciation: / sɪˈmɛtrɪkli / (AmE), / sɪˈmɛtrɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that is characterized by or exhibits symmetry; in a balanced or proportionate manner on opposite sides of a dividing line or around a center.

Examples

Arranged symmetrically
She arranged the flowers symmetrically in the vase for a pleasing display.
Snowflakes are often structured symmetrically around their center.
The chairs were symmetrically placed on either side of the table.

Related Terms

  • antonym: asymmetrically - in a manner lacking symmetry
  • synonym: symmetrical - having symmetry; balanced or proportionate
