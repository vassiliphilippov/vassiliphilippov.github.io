inscrutably

adverb
in a mysterious or incomprehensible manner
3.8 7.6
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈskruː.tə.blɪ/ /; / /ɪn.ˈskruː.tə.bliː/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈskruː.tə.bliː/ /; / /ɪn.ˈskruː.tə.blɪ/ (BrE)

Examples
