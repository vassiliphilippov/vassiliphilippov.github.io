rainbow trout

noun
flesh of a migratory Pacific trout used as food
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪnˌboʊ traʊt / (AmE), / ˈreɪnbəʊ traʊt / (BrE)
the flesh of a Pacific trout (Oncorhynchus mykiss) that migrates between salt and fresh water, used as food

Examples

The chef recommended seasoning the rainbow trout with lemon and herbs before baking.
