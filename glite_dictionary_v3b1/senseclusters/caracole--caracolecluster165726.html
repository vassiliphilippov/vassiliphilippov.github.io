caracole

verb
perform a half turn on a horse
Pronunciation: / ˈkærəˌkoʊl / (AmE), / ˈkarəkəʊl / (BrE)
(of a horse and its rider) to perform a half turn to one side, especially as a maneuver in dressage or historical cavalry tactics.

Examples

To caracole a horse
During the performance, several horses caracoled around the arena in perfect unison.
She practiced caracoling every day to improve her dressage routine.
The rider expertly made the horse caracole before the judges.
