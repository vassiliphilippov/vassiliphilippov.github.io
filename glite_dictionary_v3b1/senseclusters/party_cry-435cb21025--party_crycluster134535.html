party cry

noun
group chant or rallying call at events
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːrti kraɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːti kraɪ / (BrE)
a chant or call used to rally or inspire a group during celebrations or gatherings

Examples

Traditional party cry
Their traditional party cry brought energy and unity to the festival.
We all joined in the party cry as the victory was announced.
With a loud party cry, the crowd surged forward to support their team.
