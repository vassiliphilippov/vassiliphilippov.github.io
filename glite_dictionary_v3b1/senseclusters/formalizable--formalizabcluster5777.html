formalizable

adjective
able to be formalized
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrməˌlaɪzəbəl / (AmE), / ˈfɔːmələˌlaɪzəbəl /; / ˈfɔːməˌlaɪzəbəl / (BrE)
able to be made formal or expressed using a formal system or set of rules

Examples

Formalizable rules
The rules of chess are fully formalizable and can be written as precise instructions.
Some aspects of human language are not easily formalizable within strict grammatical systems.
The scientist argued that the process was formalizable using mathematical logic.
The agreement between the two companies was formalizable through a written contract.
Not every idea is easily formalizable into a set of clear rules or procedures.
Their partnership became formalizable once both parties agreed on the terms.

  • synonym: official - authorized or approved by an authority
  • synonym: systematic - done or acting according to a fixed plan or system
