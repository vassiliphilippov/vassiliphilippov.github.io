In a way that causes harm, injury, or is detrimental.

Examples

Act noxiously on plants
Certain chemicals can act noxiously when released into the environment.
His comments were meant noxiously to undermine her confidence.
The factory emitted smoke noxiously into the air, affecting the nearby residents.

Synonyms

  • harmfully
    4.3 0.06/m
    harmfully is a simple, common adverb used for causing general harm, while noxiously suggests poisonous, strong, or offensive harm.
  • injuriously
    7.6 0.02/m
    injuriously is used in formal or legal writing to mean causing injury or damage, while noxiously focuses on harmful, often toxic effect.
  • deleteriously
    9.0 0.05/m
    deleteriously is often used in science or medicine to mean causing harm or damage over time, while noxiously feels more immediately harmful.
  • detrimentally
    7.2 0.08/m
    detrimentally is used to say something has a damaging effect or disadvantage, while noxiously often implies direct physical or toxic harm.
