noxiously

adverb
in a harmful or injurious manner
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑk.ʃəs.li / (AmE), / ˈnɒk.ʃəs.li / (BrE)
In a way that causes harm, injury, or is detrimental.

Examples

Act noxiously on plants
Certain chemicals can act noxiously when released into the environment.
His comments were meant noxiously to undermine her confidence.
The factory emitted smoke noxiously into the air, affecting the nearby residents.
