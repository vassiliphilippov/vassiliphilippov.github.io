second

adverb
in the second place (order or sequence)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
Used to introduce the second point or item in a list or sequence.

Examples

Second, the next point
Second, I want to address the issue of transportation in our plan.
Second, we should discuss the results of the survey before making a decision.
First, let's go over the budget. Second, we can talk about the timeline.

Related Terms
