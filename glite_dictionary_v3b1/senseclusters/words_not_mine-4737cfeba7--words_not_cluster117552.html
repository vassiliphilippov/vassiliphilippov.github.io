words, not mine

x
used to distance oneself from others' words
7.6
Pronunciation: / wɝdz nɑt maɪn / (AmE), / wɜːdz nɒt maɪn / (BrE)
used to indicate that the speaker is repeating or referencing what someone else has said, not expressing their own view, often to show disagreement or distance from those words.

Examples

Those were words, not mine
He accused me of lying, but those were his words, not mine.
I never said the deal was a mistake—those are your words, not mine.
She claims the project failed because of me, but those are words, not mine.
