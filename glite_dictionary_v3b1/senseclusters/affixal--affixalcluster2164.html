affixal

adjective
relating to an affix in linguistics
4.8
Pronunciation: / əˈfɪksəl / (AmE), / əˈfɪksəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a linguistic affix; involving the use of an affix in word formation.

Examples

Affixal word formation
In English, the addition of '-ed' to form past tense verbs is an affixal process.
Prefixation and suffixation are two common types of affixal modifications in word formation.
The language has both affixal and non-affixal ways of expressing grammatical relationships.
