disquieted

adjective
feeling anxious or uneasy
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈkwaɪətɪd / (AmE), / dɪsˈkwaɪətɪd / (BrE)

Examples

Their disquieted expressions revealed their deep concern about the issue.
