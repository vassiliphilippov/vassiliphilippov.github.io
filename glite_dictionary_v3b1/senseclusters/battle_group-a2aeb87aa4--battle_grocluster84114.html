battle group

noun
multinational rapid-deployment formation
4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈbæt.əl gruːp/ /; / /ˈbæd.əl gruːp/ (AmE), /ˈbæt.əl gruːp/ (BrE)
a multinational military formation organized for rapid deployment and crisis-management operations (especially in the context of NATO or the EU).

Examples

EU battle group
The EU activated a battle group to lead the peacekeeping mission in the region.
NATO's new battle group trained for rapid deployment during the crisis response exercise.
Each multinational battle group includes infantry, logistics, and medical units ready to deploy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: EU battlegroup - a multinational EU military force established for rapid crisis response and peacekeeping missions
  • synonym: rapid reaction force - a military force maintained or organized for quick deployment during emergencies or crises
