rare-roasted

adjective
meat roasted until the inside is rare
2.4 4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛrˈroʊstɪd / (AmE), / ˌreəˈrəʊstɪd / (BrE)
(of meat) roasted so that the inside remains rare and red or pink.

Examples

Rare-roasted meat
Many steak lovers argue that rare-roasted meat retains more of its natural juices.
She prefers her lamb rare-roasted, with a warm red center.
The chef recommended the rare-roasted beef for a more flavorful and tender experience.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: medium-roasted - (of meat) roasted so the inside is pink but not as undercooked as rare
  • degree lower: rare - (of meat) cooked very lightly, so the inside is still red
