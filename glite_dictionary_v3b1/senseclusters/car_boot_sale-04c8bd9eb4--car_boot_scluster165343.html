car boot sale

noun
outdoor sale from car trunks (UK)
Pronunciation: / kɑr buːt seɪl / (AmE), / kɑː buːt seɪl / (BrE)
an outdoor market where people sell goods from the trunks of their cars, typically in a parking lot or open space

Examples
