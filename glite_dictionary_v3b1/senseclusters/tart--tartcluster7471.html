tart

noun
small open fruit pie
Pronunciation: / tɑrt / (AmE), / tɑːt / (BrE)
a small open pastry case filled with fruit, custard, or other sweet or savory ingredients, usually without a top crust

Examples

A fruit tart
I ordered a lemon tart with my afternoon tea.
She baked a delicious apple tart for dessert last night.
The bakery window displayed several colorful fruit tarts.
He baked a lemon tart with a buttery crust for the party.
She ordered a raspberry tart for dessert after her meal.
The bakery displayed dozens of colorful fruit tarts in the window.
