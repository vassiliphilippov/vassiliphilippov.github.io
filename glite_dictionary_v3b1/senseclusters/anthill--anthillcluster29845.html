anthill

noun
mound built by ants for their nest
Pronunciation: / ˈænthɪl / (AmE), / ˈænthɪl / (BrE)
A mound of earth, sand, or other material built by ants as they excavate their nest underground.

Examples

An anthill mound
After the rain, the anthill was partially washed away, exposing the tunnels underneath.
Children gathered around the anthill, watching the ants busily moving in and out.
He accidentally disturbed an anthill during his hike and was quickly surrounded by angry ants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ant nest - the underground home of ants
