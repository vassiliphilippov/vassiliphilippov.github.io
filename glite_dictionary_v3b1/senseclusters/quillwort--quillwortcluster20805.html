quillwort

noun
aquatic plant with quill-like leaves
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪlˌwɜrt / (AmE), / ˈkwɪl.wɜːt / (BrE)
a spore-bearing aquatic or marsh plant with grass-like leaves resembling quills, found worldwide except Polynesia; genus Isoetes

Examples

A quillwort plant
Many quillworts thrive in shallow freshwater lakes and ponds.
Scientists discovered a new species of quillwort in the mountain stream.
The leaves of the quillwort rise straight from the base, resembling green feathers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Isoetes - the genus of plants known as quillworts
