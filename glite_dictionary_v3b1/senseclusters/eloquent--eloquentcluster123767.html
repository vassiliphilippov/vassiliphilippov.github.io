eloquent

adjective
fluent, persuasive in speaking or writing
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɛləkwənt / (AmE), / ˈɛləkwənt / (BrE)
able to express oneself clearly, fluently, and persuasively.

Examples

An eloquent speaker
He is an eloquent spokesperson who easily wins people over.
Her eloquent speech moved the audience to tears.
The author's eloquent writing style made the novel a pleasure to read.

Related Terms

  • synonym: articulate - able to express thoughts and feelings easily and clearly
  • antonym: inarticulate - unable to express thoughts or feelings clearly
