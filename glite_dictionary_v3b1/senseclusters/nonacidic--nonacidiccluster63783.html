nonacidic

adjective
not acidic
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnəˈsɪdɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnəˈsɪdɪk / (BrE)
not having the properties of an acid; not acidic or sour in nature

Examples

A nonacidic diet
The doctor recommended a nonacidic diet to help with her stomach problems.
This type of fertilizer is best for plants that prefer nonacidic soil.
He chose a nonacidic juice to avoid irritating his throat.

Related Terms

  • antonym: acidic - having the properties of an acid; sour or sharp
  • degree higher: alkaline - having the properties of a base; not acidic
  • synonym: neutral - having a pH of 7; neither acidic nor basic
