blustery

adjective
windy and gusty (weather)
7.0 3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈblʌs.tər.iː/ /; / /ˈblʌs.triː/ (AmE), /ˈblʌs.tə.riː/ /; / /ˈblʌs.triː/ (BrE)

Examples
