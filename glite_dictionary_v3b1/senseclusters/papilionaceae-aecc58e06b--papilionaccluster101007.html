A former botanical family of leguminous plants with butterfly-shaped flowers, commonly included within the family Leguminosae (Fabaceae).

Examples

Papilionaceae family plants
Many plants in the Papilionaceae are important sources of protein for both humans and animals.
Modern botanists usually classify Papilionaceae under the broader family Fabaceae.
The distinctive butterfly-shaped flowers are a hallmark of the Papilionaceae family.

Synonyms

    9.9 0.02/m
