important

adjective
having authority or influence
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɔrtənt /; / ɪmˈpɔrtnt /; / ɪmˈpɔɹtənt / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɔːtnt /; / ɪmˈpɔːtənt / (BrE)
having great significance, value, or influence; being of considerable consequence or authority

Examples

An important decision
Being the eldest, she always felt important in family decisions.
He is an important figure in the company due to his leadership role.
Many important officials attended the international summit.
Family is the most important thing in his life.
It is important to follow the instructions carefully.
The meeting will cover several important issues facing the company.
Choosing the right school can be an important decision for a child's future.
It was important that the surgery be done as soon as possible.
This meeting is important to the future of the company.

  • synonym: powerful - having great power or influence
  • synonym: significant - having meaning or importance
