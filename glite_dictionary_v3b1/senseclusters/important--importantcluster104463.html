important

adjective
having authority or influence
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɔrtənt /; / ɪmˈpɔrtnt /; / ɪmˈpɔɹtənt / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɔːtnt /; / ɪmˈpɔːtənt / (BrE)
having great significance, value, or influence; being of considerable consequence or authority
