unceasing

adjective
never stopping; continuous
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsiːsɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈsiːsɪŋ / (BrE)
never stopping; continuing without pause or interruption

Examples

Despite the rain, the unceasing noise from the construction site kept everyone awake all night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ceaseless - without an end or pause; incessant
  • synonym: continuous - without interruption; ongoing, unbroken
  • antonym: intermittent - occurring at irregular intervals; not continuous
  • degree higher: unceasing - never stopping; continuous (antonym relation would be with 'intermittent')
