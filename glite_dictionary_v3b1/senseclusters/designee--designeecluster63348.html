designee

noun
appointed person
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛzɪgˈniː /; / ˌdɛzɪˈniː / (AmE), / ˌdɛzɪgˈniː /; / ˌdɛzɪˈniː / (BrE)
a person who has been officially chosen or appointed for a particular position, duty, or to receive something
