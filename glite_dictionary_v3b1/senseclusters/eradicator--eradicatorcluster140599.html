eradicator

noun
one who eliminates
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrædɪˌkeɪtər / (AmE), / ɪˈrædɪkeɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that completely removes or destroys something unwanted

Examples

Disease eradicator
The health organization became a leading eradicator of infectious diseases in the region.
Modern technology has made the eradicator of pests much more effective and environmentally friendly.
She was known as an eradicator of corruption within the company.

Related Terms

  • synonym: destroyer - a person or thing that destroys something
  • synonym: eliminator - a person or thing that removes something
  • antonym: preserver - a person who keeps something safe or prevents its destruction
