fastidiously

adverb
with great attention to detail
8.8
Pronunciation: / fæˈstɪdiəsli / (AmE), / fæˈstɪdiəsli /; / fəˈstɪdiəsli / (BrE)
in a way that shows great attention to detail, care, or cleanliness

Examples

To fastidiously clean
He fastidiously cleaned his workshop at the end of each day.
She arranged the flowers fastidiously, making sure every stem was perfectly placed.
The manuscript was fastidiously edited before publication to avoid any errors.
He fastidiously cleaned his workspace after finishing the project.
She fastidiously arranged the flowers in the vase until every petal was perfect.
The professor edited the manuscript fastidiously, leaving detailed notes on every page.

  • synonym: meticulously - in a way that shows great attention to detail; very thoroughly
