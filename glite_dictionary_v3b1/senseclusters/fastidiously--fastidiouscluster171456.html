Synonyms

  • punctiliously

    punctiliously means following rules and exact forms with careful detail, while fastidiously often adds concern for cleanliness or neatness.
  • painstakingly
    6.0 2.16/m
    painstakingly shows slow, thorough effort to complete tasks with care, while fastidiously also suggests picky concern with cleanliness.
  • scrupulously
    6.9 1.01/m
    scrupulously implies strict honesty or exactness in actions, while fastidiously emphasizes personal fussiness and cleanliness in detail.
  • fussily
    7.3 0.07/m

  • carefully
