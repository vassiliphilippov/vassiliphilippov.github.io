attack

noun
sudden episode of a condition
Pronunciation: / əˈtæk / (AmE), / əˈtæk / (BrE)
A sudden episode of a particular condition, especially an illness or symptom.

Examples

An attack of pain
An attack of dizziness forced her to sit down.
He suffered an attack of asthma during the night.
The child had a sudden attack of nausea after dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bout - a short period of intense activity or illness
