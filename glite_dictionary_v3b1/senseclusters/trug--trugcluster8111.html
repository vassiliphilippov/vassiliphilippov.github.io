trug

noun
garden basket
Pronunciation: / trʌɡ / (AmE), / trʌɡ / (BrE)
a shallow, wooden basket with a handle, used for carrying garden produce or tools

Examples

A trug basket
She carried the ripe tomatoes in a trug back to the kitchen.
The gardener placed her tools neatly inside the wooden trug before starting work.
After harvesting, he filled his trug with fresh herbs and flowers.

  • synonym: crate - a large container used for transporting or storing items
