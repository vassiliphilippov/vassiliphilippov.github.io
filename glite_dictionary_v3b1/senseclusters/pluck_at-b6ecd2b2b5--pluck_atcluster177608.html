pluck at

verb
pull repeatedly with fingers
5.3 4.9
Pronunciation: / plʌk æt / (AmE), / plʌk æt / (BrE)
to pull or tug repeatedly or lightly at something with the fingers

Examples

To pluck at fabric
Leaves plucked at her dress as she walked through the bushes.
She nervously plucked at the sleeves of her sweater during the interview.
The child plucked at his mother's hand for attention.
