keyless

adjective
not requiring a key
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːləs / (AmE), / ˈkiːləs / (BrE)
Not requiring or using a physical key to operate or unlock.

Examples

Keyless entry system
Many modern cars now feature keyless entry systems for added convenience.
She appreciated her keyless access to the gym, which let her enter at any hour.
The new office building installed keyless locks on all doors.
