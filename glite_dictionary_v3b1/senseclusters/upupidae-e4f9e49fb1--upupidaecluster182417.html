Upupidae

noun
family of birds, hoopoes
Pronunciation: / uːˈpuːpɪˌdiː / (AmE), / juːˈpjuːpɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of birds comprising the hoopoes, known for their distinctive crown of feathers.

Examples

Upupidae bird family
Ornithologists study the nesting habits of birds in the Upupidae family.
The Upupidae family is easily recognized by the hoopoe's striking crown of feathers.
The range of Upupidae includes parts of Europe, Asia, and Africa.
