nonretirement

noun
continued employment
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.rɪˈtaɪər.mənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.rɪˈtaɪə.mənt / (BrE)
the state or decision of continuing to work instead of retiring, especially beyond the typical retirement age

Examples

His nonretirement decision
His decision for nonretirement surprised his colleagues, who expected him to leave the company that year.
Many people choose nonretirement to stay active and engaged in their communities.
The trend of nonretirement is increasing as people live longer and healthier lives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: employment - the state of having paid work
  • antonym: retirement - the act of leaving one's job and stopping work permanently
