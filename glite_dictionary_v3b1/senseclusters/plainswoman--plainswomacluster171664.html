plainswoman

noun
woman from a plains region
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪnzwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈpleɪnzwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who lives in a plains region, especially the Great Plains of North America.

Examples

A plainswoman survivor
As a plainswoman, she knew how to survive the harsh winters of the Midwest.
Proud to be a plainswoman, she often spoke of the beauty of endless grasslands.
The novel tells the story of a plainswoman and her family's struggles during a drought.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plainsman - a man who lives on the plains
