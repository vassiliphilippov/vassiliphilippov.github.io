too

adverb
in addition; also; as well
1.0
Pronunciation: / tu / (AmE), / tuː / (BrE)
used to show that a statement applies to someone or something in addition to another person or thing; also; as well.

Examples

Me too
I want to come too, if that’s okay.
Not only is he smart, but he's funny too.
She likes chocolate, too.
