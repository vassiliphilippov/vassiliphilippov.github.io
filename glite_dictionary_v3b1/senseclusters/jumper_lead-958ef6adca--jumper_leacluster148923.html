jumper lead

noun
short wire for making temporary electrical connections
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌmpər liːd / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmpə liːd / (BrE)
A short length of wire with connectors at each end, used to establish a temporary electrical connection between two points.

Examples

A jumper lead cable
Always make sure the jumper lead is properly insulated before use.
He used a jumper lead to connect the battery terminals during the test.
The electronics kit came with several jumper leads in different colors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jumper cable - a thicker cable for connecting car batteries
