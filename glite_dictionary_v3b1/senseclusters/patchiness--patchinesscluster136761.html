patchiness

noun
unevenness in quality or performance
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpætʃi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈpætʃi.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of being uneven or inconsistent in quality or performance.

Examples

The patchiness of quality
Critics noted the patchiness in the quality of her latest novel.
The patchiness of the mobile network meant calls kept dropping during our journey.
There is significant patchiness in the distribution of resources across the region.

Related Terms

  • antonym: consistency - steadiness or regularity in quality or performance
  • antonym: evenness - the quality of being uniform or consistent
  • synonym: irregularity - the state of not being regular or uniform
