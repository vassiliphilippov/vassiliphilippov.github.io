cyclosis

noun
circulation of cytoplasm in a cell
Pronunciation: / saɪˈkloʊsɪs / (AmE), / sɪˈkləʊsɪs / (BrE)
the streaming movement of cytoplasm within a living cell, enabling the distribution of materials

Examples

cyclosis in cells
