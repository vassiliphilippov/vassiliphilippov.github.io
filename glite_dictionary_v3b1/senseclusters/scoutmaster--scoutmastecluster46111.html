scoutmaster

noun
leader of a Scout troop
Pronunciation: / ˈskaʊtˌmæstɚ / (AmE), / ˈskaʊtˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
the adult leader in charge of a troop of Scouts, responsible for their supervision and training

Examples

The scoutmaster leads
Every scoutmaster must ensure the safety of the Scouts during outdoor activities.
The scoutmaster organized a camping trip for the entire troop.
When the meeting ended, the scoutmaster encouraged everyone to practice their knots at home.
