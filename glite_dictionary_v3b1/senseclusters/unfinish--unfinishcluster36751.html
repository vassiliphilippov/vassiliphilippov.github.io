unfinish

verb
to leave incomplete
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪnɪʃ / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪnɪʃ / (BrE)
to leave something incomplete or not bring it to completion

Examples

To unfinish a project
She chose to unfinish her novel, leaving the story open-ended for readers.
The artist decided to unfinish the painting, giving it a raw and unique look.
Sometimes it is better to unfinish a task than to do it poorly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abandon - to leave something behind or give up on it
  • antonym: complete - to finish something fully
