Asplenium rhizophyllum

noun
a species of walking fern
Pronunciation: / æˈspləniəm raɪzoʊˈfɪləm / (AmE), / æˈsplɛniəm raɪzəʊˈfɪləm / (BrE)
A species of fern with narrow, lance-shaped fronds that produce roots at the tip; also known as walking fern.
