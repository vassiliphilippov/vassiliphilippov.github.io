precolonial

noun
era before colonization
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːkəˈloʊniəl / (AmE), / ˌpriːkəˈləʊniəl / (BrE)
the period or era before a region or people were colonized by foreign powers

Examples

Artifacts from the precolonial era reveal much about indigenous cultures.
