formally

adverb
in a formal manner
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrməli / (AmE), / ˈfɔːməli / (BrE)
in a formal or official way, following established rules or conventions

Examples

Dressed formally
He was dressed rather formally for the casual dinner party.
She formally introduced herself to the board at the start of the meeting.
The agreement was formally signed by both parties yesterday.
He was formally introduced to the board of directors.
The club will be formally recognized after the inspection.
The organization was formally approved by the government last year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: informally - in an informal manner; not following established conventions
  • synonym: officially - in a manner approved by authority or in an official capacity
