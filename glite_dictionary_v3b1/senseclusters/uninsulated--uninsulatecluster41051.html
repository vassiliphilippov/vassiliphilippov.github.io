uninsulated

adjective
not protected
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈɪnsəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈɪnsjʊleɪtɪd / (BrE)
not protected or separated from outside influences, effects, or harm; exposed or vulnerable

Examples

Uninsulated wires
The wires were left uninsulated, creating a potential safety hazard.
Many small businesses remained uninsulated from the effects of the economic downturn.
Without proper clothing, hikers can be uninsulated against the cold weather.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exposed - not protected or covered; open to harm
  • antonym: insulated - protected or separated from outside influences or harm
