Ascension of Christ

noun
the ascent of Jesus to heaven
Pronunciation: / əˈsɛnʃən əv kraɪst / (AmE), / əˈsɛnʃ(ə)n əv kraɪst / (BrE)
(in Christian belief) the ascent of Jesus Christ to heaven, occurring on the 40th day after his Resurrection.

Examples

The Ascension of Christ event
In Christian theology, the Ascension of Christ signifies his return to heaven after his resurrection.
Many churches hold special services on the anniversary of the Ascension of Christ.
The artist painted a magnificent depiction of the Ascension of Christ above the altar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Ascension Day - the Christian feast commemorating the Ascension of Christ
  • degree lower: Resurrection - the rising of Jesus from the dead
