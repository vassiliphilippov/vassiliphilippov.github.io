immaculately

adverb
in a flawless or spotless way
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmæk.jə.lət.li / (AmE), / ɪˈmæk.jʊ.lət.li / (BrE)
in a perfectly clean, neat, or flawless manner

Examples

Dressed immaculately
He was dressed immaculately for the interview, with not a crease in his suit.
She kept her home immaculately clean, impressing all her guests.
The table was set immaculately, with every piece of cutlery in its proper place.

Related Terms

  • antonym: messily - in a messy or untidy manner
  • synonym: perfectly - in a perfect manner
