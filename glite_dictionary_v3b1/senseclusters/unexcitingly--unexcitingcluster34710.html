unexcitingly

adverb
in an unexciting or dull way
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪkˈsaɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪkˈsaɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that is not exciting or interesting; in a dull or uninspiring manner.

Examples

Decorate unexcitingly simply
He decorated the room rather unexcitingly, sticking only to neutral colors and plain furniture.
The movie unfolded unexcitingly, failing to grab the attention of the viewers.
The speaker delivered her lecture unexcitingly, causing the audience to lose interest.

Related Terms

  • antonym: excitingly - in an exciting or stimulating manner
