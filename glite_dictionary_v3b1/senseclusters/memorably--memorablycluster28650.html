memorably

adverb
in a memorable way
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛmərəbli / (AmE), / ˈmɛmərəbli / (BrE)
In a way that is easily remembered or likely to be remembered for a long time, often because it is special, impressive, or unusual.

Examples

To memorably describe adventures
She memorably described her adventures in vivid detail.
The concert was memorably performed by the young orchestra.
The speech ended memorably, leaving the audience in awe.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forgettably - in a way that is easily forgotten
  • synonym: unforgettably - in a way that cannot be forgotten
