corn fritter

noun
fried cake with corn kernels
Pronunciation: / kɔrn ˈfrɪt̬ɚ / (AmE), / kɔːn ˈfrɪtə / (BrE)
a small fried cake made with batter and corn kernels, often served as a side dish or snack

Examples

A corn fritter snack
In the South, corn fritters are a popular addition to family dinners.
She prepared a batch of hot, golden corn fritters for breakfast.
The restaurant is famous for its spicy corn fritter appetizer.
