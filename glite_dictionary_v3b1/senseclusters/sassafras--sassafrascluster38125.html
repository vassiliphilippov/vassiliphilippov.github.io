sassafras

noun
aromatic North American tree
7.5 8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsæsəˌfræs / (AmE), / ˈsasəfrəs / (BrE)
A North American tree with aromatic leaves and bark, used as a source of sassafras oil and traditionally for flavoring and medicine.
