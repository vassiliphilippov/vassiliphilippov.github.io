curdy

adjective
like curds
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜrdi / (AmE), / ˈkɜːdi / (BrE)
having the appearance, texture, or consistency of curds; resembling curds

Examples

Curdy milk texture
The spoiled milk had a curdy consistency and smelled sour.
She noticed a curdy layer forming on top of the heated cream.
The sauce turned curdy after being cooked at too high a temperature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lumpy - having a lot of lumps or small, solid pieces
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface; not rough or lumpy
