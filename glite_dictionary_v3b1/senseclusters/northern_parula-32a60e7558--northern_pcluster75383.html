northern parula

noun
a North American species of wood warbler
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrðərn pəˈruːlə / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən pəˈruːlə / (BrE)
a small gray-blue wood warbler with a yellow throat and breast, found in eastern North America

Examples

A bright yellow throat makes the northern parula easy to identify among other warblers.
The migration of the northern parula is closely followed by bird enthusiasts in spring.
