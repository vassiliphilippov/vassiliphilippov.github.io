reloader

noun
ammunition refiller
7.2
Pronunciation: / riˈloʊdər / (AmE), / riˈləʊdə / (BrE)
a person or device that refills empty ammunition cases so they can be used again

Examples

Ammunition reloader
The experienced reloader carefully measured powder before assembling each cartridge.
He bought a new reloader to save money on ammunition for target practice.
Some hunters prefer to use a manual reloader to customize their bullets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loader - a person or machine that loads something
  • antonym: unloader - a person or device that removes a load
