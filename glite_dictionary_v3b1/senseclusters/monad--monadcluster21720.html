monad

noun
single-celled microorganism, esp. protozoan
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊ.næd / (AmE), / ˈmɒ.nad / (BrE)
(Biology) A single-celled microorganism, especially one that is a flagellate protozoan.

Examples

A single-celled monad
Flagellated monads play an important role in aquatic ecosystems.
Researchers identified a rare monad in the pond water sample.
Under the microscope, the monad moved swiftly using its slender flagellum.

  • synonym: protozoan - single-celled microscopic animal, eukaryotic organism
