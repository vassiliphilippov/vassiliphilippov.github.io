socializer

noun
person who participates in social activities
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊ.ʃəˌlaɪ.zər /; / ˈsoʊʃəlaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.ʃə.laɪ.zə /; / ˈsəʊʃəlaɪzə / (BrE)
a person who enjoys or regularly takes part in social activities

Examples

Tom isn't much of a socialiser, preferring quiet evenings to large gatherings.
