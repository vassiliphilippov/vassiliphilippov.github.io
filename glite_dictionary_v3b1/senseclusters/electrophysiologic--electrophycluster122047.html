electrophysiologic

adjective
electrical properties in biology
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˌfɪziəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˌfɪziəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to the electrical properties or activity of biological cells and tissues

Examples

Electrophysiologic properties
The electrophysiologic properties of nerve cells are essential for transmitting signals in the body.
Doctors use electrophysiologic tests to diagnose certain heart rhythm disorders.
Research on electrophysiologic responses helps us understand how muscles contract.
