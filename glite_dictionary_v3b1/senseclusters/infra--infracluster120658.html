infra

preposition
below in text
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnfrə / (AmE), / ˈɪnfrə / (BrE)
used in legal or technical writing to refer to material located later in the same document or text.

Examples

Section 4 infra
For further explanation, see the discussion infra in section 4.
The court's reasoning is provided infra under the heading 'Analysis'.
Details about the contract terms are outlined infra in the appendix.

Related Terms

  • antonym: supra - used to refer to material located earlier in the text
