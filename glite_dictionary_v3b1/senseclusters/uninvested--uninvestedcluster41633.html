uninvested

adjective
lacking official authority
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈvɛstɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈvɛstɪd / (BrE)
not having been given official authority, rank, or the formal powers of an office or position

Examples

Uninvested position
The committee remained uninvested with any real decision-making power until the official ceremony.
She acted as interim director but was still uninvested with the full responsibilities of the role.
Despite his nomination, he was uninvested and could not sign official documents.

Related Terms

  • antonym: invested - having been given official authority or rank
  • synonym: unappointed - not officially assigned to a position
