organize picket

verb
arrange a protest, often outside a workplace
7.4 3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈɔːr.ɡə.naɪz ˈpɪk.ɪt/ (AmE), /ˈɔː.ɡə.naɪz ˈpɪk.ɪt/ (BrE)
to arrange and carry out a protest, especially by workers, to express demands or support a cause, usually outside a workplace or building

Examples

Activists plan to organize picket tomorrow to demand climate action from city officials.
Teachers decided to organize picket at the district office to protest budget cuts.
The union leaders agreed to organize picket lines outside the factory gates.
