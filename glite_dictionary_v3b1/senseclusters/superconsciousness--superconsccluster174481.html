superconsciousness

noun
heightened awareness
9.9
Pronunciation: /suː.pə.ˈkɑːn.ʃəs.nəs/ (AmE), /suː.pə.ˈkɒn.ʃəs.nəs/ (BrE)
a higher or expanded state of consciousness or awareness, often believed to go beyond ordinary experience and sometimes seen as universal or collective
