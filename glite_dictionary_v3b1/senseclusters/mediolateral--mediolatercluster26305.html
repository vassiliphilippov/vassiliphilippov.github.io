mediolateral

adjective
middle to side
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmiːdioʊˈlætərəl / (AmE), / ˌmiːdiəʊˈlætərəl / (BrE)
relating to or extending from the middle to the side of a body or structure

Examples

Mediolateral body direction

A mediolateral approach was chosen to minimize damage to surrounding tissues.
The physical therapist measured the mediolateral movement of the patient's knee.
