fav

noun
informal favorite
Pronunciation: / feɪv / (AmE), / feɪv / (BrE)
an informal abbreviation for 'favorite', often used online or in text messages to refer to something liked best

Examples

A childhood fav
That movie is my all-time fav and I could watch it over and over.
She posted a picture of her fav dessert on social media last night.
Out of all the songs on the album, this one is definitely my fav.

Related Terms

  • synonym: favorite - the person or thing you like best
