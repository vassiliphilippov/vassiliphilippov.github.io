pachydermic

adjective
relating to pachyderms (thick-skinned animals)
Pronunciation: / ˌpækɪˈdɜrmɪk / (AmE), / ˌpakɪˈdɜːmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of pachyderms (thick-skinned animals such as elephants, rhinoceroses, and hippopotamuses)

Examples

Pachydermic animals
Her pachydermic patience with the children impressed everyone at the daycare center.
Scientists studied the pachydermic adaptations found in rhinoceroses for clues to their evolutionary history.
The pachydermic skin of the elephant helps protect it from predators and harsh conditions.
