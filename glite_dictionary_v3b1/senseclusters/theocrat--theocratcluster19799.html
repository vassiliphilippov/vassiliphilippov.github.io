theocrat

noun
supporter of religious rule
Pronunciation: / ˈθiːəˌkræt / (AmE), / ˈθiːəkræt / (BrE)
a person who supports or advocates government by religious leaders or according to religious law

Examples

Theocrat backs religion
The new law was strongly supported by the leading theocrat in the country.
As a theocrat, he believed that only religious leaders should govern the nation.
Debates between the theocrat and the secularist drew large crowds at the university.

Related Terms

  • antonym: secularist - a person who supports separation of religion from government
  • synonym: theocracy - a system of government ruled by religious leaders
