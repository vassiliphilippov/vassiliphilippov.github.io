turn indicator

noun
light on a vehicle showing intended turning direction
5.9 2.0
Pronunciation: /tɜːrn ˈɪn.də.keɪ.tər/ (AmE), /tɜːn ˈɪn.dɪ.keɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a blinking light on a motor vehicle that shows drivers and pedestrians which direction the vehicle intends to turn

Examples

Car turn indicator
It's important to always signal with your turn indicator to alert other drivers.
She forgot to use her turn indicator before changing lanes.
The left turn indicator stopped working, so he took the car to a mechanic.

Related Terms
