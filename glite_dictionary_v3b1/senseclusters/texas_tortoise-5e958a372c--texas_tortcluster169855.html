Texas tortoise

noun
species of tortoise native to Texas and Mexico
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛksəs ˈtɔːrtəs / (AmE), / ˈtɛksəs ˈtɔːtəs / (BrE)
a species of tortoise native to southern Texas and northern Mexico, closely related to the desert tortoise
