water-repellent

adjective
resistant to water penetration
6.4 3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtər rɪˈpɛlənt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə rɪˈpelənt / (BrE)

The tent is made from water-repellent fabric, making it suitable for camping in wet conditions.
