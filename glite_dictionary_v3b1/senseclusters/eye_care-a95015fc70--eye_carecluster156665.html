eye care

noun
treatment and maintenance of eye health
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ ˌkɛr / (AmE), / ˈaɪ ˌkɛə / (BrE)
the treatment, maintenance, and protection of eye health, including diagnosing and managing eye conditions

Examples

Good eye care
Good eye care includes regular checkups and protecting your eyes from injury.
He went to the clinic for a routine eye care appointment.
Regular eye care is important to prevent vision problems later in life.
