redeye

noun
brewed coffee with an espresso shot
Pronunciation: / c8r5bdcca6a / (AmE), / c8r5bdcca6a / (BrE)
(informal) a coffee drink made by adding a shot of espresso to a cup of brewed coffee.

Examples

Order a redeye coffee
Before the exam, she bought a strong redeye and studied until three in the morning.
At the cafe, Tom ordered a redeye instead of regular coffee to wake up quickly.
Baristas often ask if customers want a redeye or a stronger 'black eye' with two shots.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: black eye - a similar drink made by adding two espresso shots to brewed coffee (stronger than a redeye)
  • synonym: shot in the dark - a cup of brewed coffee with an added shot of espresso; term used interchangeably with redeye
