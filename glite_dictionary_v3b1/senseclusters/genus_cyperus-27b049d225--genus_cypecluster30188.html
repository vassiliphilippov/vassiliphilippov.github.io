genus Cyperus

noun
type genus of sedge family, grasslike herbs
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs saɪˈpɪr.əs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs sɪˈpɪər.əs / (BrE)
the type genus of the sedge family (Cyperaceae), comprising grasslike, rhizomatous herbs found worldwide except in very cold regions
