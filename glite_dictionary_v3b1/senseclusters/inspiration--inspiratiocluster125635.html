inspiration

noun
stimulus for creative activity
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnspəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪnspɪˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or feeling of being mentally stimulated to do or create something, especially something creative or positive

Examples

Inspiration for art
He found inspiration for his paintings in the beauty of nature.
Her latest novel was written under a sudden burst of inspiration.
Many inventors credit their success to a moment of inspiration.
His courage has been an inspiration to the whole team.
She found inspiration in the beauty of nature for her latest painting.
The speech was a true inspiration to everyone in the audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apathy - lack of interest, enthusiasm, or concern
  • antonym: discouragement - a loss of confidence or enthusiasm; dispiritedness
  • synonym: motivation - reason or enthusiasm for acting in a certain way
