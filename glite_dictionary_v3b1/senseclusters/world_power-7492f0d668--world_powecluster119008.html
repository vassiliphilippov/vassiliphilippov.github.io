world power

noun
a highly influential state globally
3.5
Pronunciation: / wɜrld ˈpaʊər / (AmE), / wɜːld ˈpaʊə / (BrE)
A state that possesses enough economic, military, or political strength to influence events and policies worldwide.

Examples

Major world power
Diplomatic relations between world powers often shape the global political landscape.
During the twentieth century, the United States established itself as a leading world power.
Emerging economies aspire to become recognized as a world power in the coming decades.
