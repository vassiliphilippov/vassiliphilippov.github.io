counter

noun
game piece for scoring or reserving
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.tə / (BrE)
a small object used in board or card games for keeping score, marking positions, or reserving spaces

Examples

Game counter
Each player was given ten counters to start the board game.
She carefully moved her counter to the next square on the track.
You must place your counter on a property to reserve it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: token - an object used as a substitute for currency or as a marker in a game
