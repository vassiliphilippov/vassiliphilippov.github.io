bewitched

adjective
enchanted; under a spell
5.0
Pronunciation: / bɪˈwɪtʃt / (AmE), / bɪˈwɪtʃt / (BrE)
Enchanted or under a magical spell; so attracted or fascinated by something or someone as if by magic.

Examples

Bewitched by magic
He stood bewitched by her performance, unable to look away for even a moment.
She gazed at the painting, completely bewitched by its vibrant colors and mysterious subject.
The audience listened in bewitched silence as the storyteller spun her tale.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enchanted - under a magical spell; delighted
  • antonym: unimpressed - not affected or interested; the opposite of enchanted
