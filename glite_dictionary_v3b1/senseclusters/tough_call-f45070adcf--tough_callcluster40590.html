tough call

noun
difficult decision or choice
3.5
Pronunciation: / tʌf kɔl / (AmE), / tʌf kɔːl / (BrE)
a difficult decision or choice, often because the options are similarly balanced or challenging.

Examples

A tough call to make
Choosing between honesty and loyalty proved to be a tough call for her.
It was a tough call to move abroad for work, but he decided to go.
The referee had to make a tough call during the final minutes of the game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hard choice - a decision that is difficult to make
