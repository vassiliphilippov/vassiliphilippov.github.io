rudimental

adjective
undeveloped; basic
Pronunciation: / ˌruːdəˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌruːdɪˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
existing in a basic, undeveloped, or elementary form; fundamental or at an early stage
