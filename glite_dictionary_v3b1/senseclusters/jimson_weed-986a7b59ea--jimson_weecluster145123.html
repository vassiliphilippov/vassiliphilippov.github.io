jimson weed

noun
poisonous annual weed with trumpet-shaped flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪmsən wid / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪmsən wiːd / (BrE)
A tall, coarse annual plant with foul-smelling leaves, large white or violet trumpet-shaped flowers, and prickly fruits; notably poisonous.

Examples

Tall jimson weed plant
Children should be kept away from jimson weed because all parts of the plant are toxic.
Native to the Americas, jimson weed is often recognized by its large, trumpet-shaped flowers.
The garden was overrun by jimson weed, which spread quickly after the rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Datura - genus of poisonous plants including jimson weed
  • synonym: thorn apple - another common name for jimson weed
