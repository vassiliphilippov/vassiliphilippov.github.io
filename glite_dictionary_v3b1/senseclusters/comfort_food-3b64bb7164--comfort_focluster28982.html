comfort food

noun
food giving a sense of wellbeing, often nostalgic
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌmfərt fuːd /; / ˈkʌm.fɚt fuːd / (AmE), / ˈkʌmfət fuːd /; / ˈkʌm.fət fuːd / (BrE)
food that is eaten to make you feel better emotionally, often reminding you of home or childhood

Examples

Comfort food like macaroni
After a long, stressful day, she turned to comfort food like macaroni and cheese to feel better.
Chicken soup is a classic example of comfort food for cold winter evenings.
Many people crave comfort food when they're homesick or feeling down.
Her favorite comfort food is homemade apple pie, which reminds her of her childhood.
On rainy days, many people crave comfort food like stew or mashed potatoes.
When I'm feeling down, a plate of comfort food like macaroni and cheese helps me feel better.
