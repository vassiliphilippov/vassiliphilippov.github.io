righteye flounder

noun
flounder with both eyes on the right side
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪt.aɪ ˈflaʊndər / (AmE), / ˈraɪt.aɪ ˈflaʊndə / (BrE)
A type of flounder with both eyes located on the right side of its head, belonging to the family Pleuronectidae.

Examples

The righteye flounder fish
Fishermen frequently catch righteye flounder in the waters off the North Atlantic coast.
Many commercial fishing operations target righteye flounder due to their culinary value.
Unlike its close relative, the righteye flounder has both eyes on the right side of its head.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flatfish - fish with a flattened body and both eyes on one side
  • antonym: lefteye flounder - flounder with both eyes on the left side of the head
