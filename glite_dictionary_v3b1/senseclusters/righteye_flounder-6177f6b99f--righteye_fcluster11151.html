righteye flounder

noun
flounder with both eyes on the right side
9.9 7.8
Pronunciation: /ˈraɪ.t.aɪ ˈflaʊn.dər/ (AmE), /ˈraɪ.t.aɪ ˈflaʊn.də/ (BrE)
