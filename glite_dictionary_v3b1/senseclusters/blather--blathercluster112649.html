blather

noun
foolish or nonsensical talk
Pronunciation: / ˈblæðɚ / (AmE), / ˈblɑːðə / (BrE)
talk or writing that is foolish, meaningless, or nonsensical; foolish gibberish.

Examples

Useless blather
Let's skip the blather and get straight to the point.
The politician's speech was nothing but blather from start to finish.
Tired of his constant blather, she tuned him out.

Related Terms

  • synonym: babble - continuous foolish or incoherent talk
  • synonym: nonsense - speech or writing that makes no sense
