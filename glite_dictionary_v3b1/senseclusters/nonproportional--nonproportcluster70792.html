nonproportional

adjective
not in constant ratio
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnprəˈpɔrʃənl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnprəˈpɔːʃənl / (BrE)
not having a constant ratio or not corresponding in size, amount, or degree

Examples

Nonproportional increase
The increase in cost was nonproportional to the amount of work completed.
A nonproportional response can sometimes lead to unfair outcomes.
The teacher explained that the data showed a nonproportional relationship between hours studied and test scores.
