inmate

noun
person confined to an institution
1.6 3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.meɪt /; / ˈɪnˌmeɪt / (BrE)
a person who is confined in a prison, hospital, or similar institution

Examples

Prison inmate
Most inmates are given daily routines to follow in the facility.
The hospital has a new wing specifically for psychiatric inmates.
The prison inmate awaited his court hearing anxiously.
Each inmate is allowed one hour of exercise outside each day.
The inmate spent years in prison before being released on parole.
The warden addressed concerns about the health of the inmate population.
As an inmate of the psychiatric ward, he followed a strict daily routine.
Long-term inmates at the sanatorium often developed close friendships with each other.
The hospital inmates gathered in the recreation room for their daily activities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: guard - a person who supervises and is responsible for the safety of inmates
  • synonym: patient - a person who is receiving medical care
  • synonym: prisoner - a person who is kept in prison as a punishment or while waiting for trial
  • synonym: ward - a person under the protection or care of another, especially in a hospital
