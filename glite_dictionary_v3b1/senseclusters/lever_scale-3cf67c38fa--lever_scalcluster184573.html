lever scale

noun
a type of portable weighing device
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛvɚ skeɪl / (AmE), / ˈliːvə skeɪl / (BrE)
a portable balance consisting of a pivoted bar with arms of unequal length, used to measure weight

Examples

A lever scale balance
Farmers often rely on a lever scale for quick weight checks in the field.
He carried a small lever scale in his toolkit for portable measurements.
The technician used a lever scale to weigh the chemicals accurately.
