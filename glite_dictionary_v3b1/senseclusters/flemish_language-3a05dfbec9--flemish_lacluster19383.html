Flemish language

noun
Dutch dialect spoken in Belgium
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈflɛmɪʃ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈflɛmɪʃ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A variety of Dutch spoken in Belgium, recognized as one of its official languages.

Examples

Flemish language in Belgium
Many Belgians in Flanders use the Flemish language at home and in schools.
She is fluent in both French and the Flemish language.
The Flemish language is widely spoken in the northern part of Belgium.
