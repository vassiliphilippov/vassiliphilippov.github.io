damascene

adjective
decorated with a wavy pattern of different metals
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdæm.əˌsin / (AmE), / ˈdæm.ə.siːn / (BrE)
(of metals) decorated or inlaid with a wavy pattern formed by different metals, often for ornamental effect.
