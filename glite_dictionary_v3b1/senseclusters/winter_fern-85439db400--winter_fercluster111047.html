winter fern

noun
poisonous biennial herb with fernlike leaves
7.9 7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪn.tər fɜːrn/ (AmE), /ˈwɪn.tə fɜːn/ (BrE)
a large, branching biennial herb with fernlike leaves and white flowers, native to Eurasia and Africa but also found in North America; all parts are extremely poisonous
