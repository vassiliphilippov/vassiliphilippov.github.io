body image

noun
perception of one's own body
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑdi ˌɪmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi ˌɪmɪdʒ / (BrE)
a person's perception or evaluation of their own physical appearance

Examples

Positive body image
Many people struggle with their body image due to unrealistic standards set by the media.
Social media can have both positive and negative effects on teenagers' body image.
Therapists often help clients improve a negative body image through counseling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: self-esteem - confidence in one's own worth or abilities; self-respect
  • synonym: self-perception - awareness of and opinion about oneself
