tolerantly

adverb
in a tolerant manner
4.1 0.13/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt…ëÀêl…ôr…ôntli / (AmE), / Ààt…íl…ôr…ôntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows willingness to accept behavior or opinions that are different from your own.

Examples

To tolerantly accept views
Despite the criticism, he responded tolerantly and explained his point of view calmly.
She listened tolerantly to the children arguing over the smallest details.
They behave tolerantly towards people with different beliefs.

Synonyms

    4.4 0.00/m
    broad-mindedly accepts different ideas and viewpoints in a positive, open way, while tolerantly shows calm acceptance of others' different opinions.
  • acceptingly
    4.6 0.01/m
    acceptingly takes something in without complaint, often quietly, while tolerantly means accepting different views with patience and fairness.
