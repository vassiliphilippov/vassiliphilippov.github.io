Culicoides

proper noun
genus of biting midges
Pronunciation: / ˌkjuːlɪˈkɔɪdiːz / (AmE), / ˌkjuːlɪˈkɔɪdiːz / (BrE)
a genus of small biting flies, commonly called midges, some of which transmit diseases to animals

Examples

Culicoides biting flies
Culicoides are often found near wetlands and can be a nuisance to both humans and animals.
Veterinarians monitor Culicoides populations because they can spread diseases among livestock.
Some species of Culicoides are responsible for transmitting the bluetongue virus in sheep.

Related Terms

  • synonym: midge - a small biting fly found near water or damp places
  • synonym: mosquito - a small flying insect that bites and can transmit diseases
