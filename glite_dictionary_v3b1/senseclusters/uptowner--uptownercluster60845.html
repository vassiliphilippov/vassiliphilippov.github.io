uptowner

noun
person from uptown
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈtaʊnər /; / ˈʌpˌtaʊnər / (AmE), / ʌpˈtaʊnə /; / ˈʌpˌtaʊnə / (BrE)
a person who lives in or is associated with the uptown (often wealthier or fashionable) part of a city

Examples

An uptowner resident
As an uptowner, she enjoyed the quiet streets and leafy parks far from the city center.
The new restaurant quickly became popular among uptowners looking for a trendy place to dine.
He always felt like an outsider when visiting downtown, even though he was a lifelong uptowner.
The exclusive party was filled with uptowners dressed in designer clothes.
As an uptowner, she was used to attending charity galas and art openings.
Some locals felt that the uptowner did not understand the struggles of other neighborhoods.

Related Terms

  • antonym: downtowner - a person who lives in or comes from the downtown area of a city
  • synonym: socialite - a person who is well known in fashionable society
