instrumentalize

verb
to add measurement devices
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnstrəˈmɛntəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˌɪnstrəˈmɛntəlaɪz / (BrE)
to equip a device, vehicle, or system with instruments or sensors for measurement or control

Examples

To instrumentalize a vehicle
The engineers instrumentalized the new aircraft to collect data during test flights.
Before the experiment, they instrumentalized the laboratory equipment with advanced sensors.
Scientists instrumentalize vehicles to monitor environmental conditions in remote areas.

  • synonym: outfit - to supply with equipment or gear
