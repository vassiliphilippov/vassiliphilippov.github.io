ejido

noun
communal village land
9.9
Pronunciation: /e.ˈhiː.doʊ/ /; / /ə.ˈhiː.doʊ/ (AmE), /e.ˈhiː.dəʊ/ /; / /ə.ˈhiː.dəʊ/ (BrE)
