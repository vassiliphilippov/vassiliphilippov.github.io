give it up

verb
stop doing something; quit
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɡɪv ɪt ˈʌp / (AmE), / ˌɡɪv ɪt ˈʌp / (BrE)
to stop doing something or to quit an activity, habit, or pursuit

Examples

To give it up smoking
After years of smoking, he finally decided to give it up for good.
If you can't win the game, sometimes it's best to give it up and move on.
She found it hard to give it up even though everyone advised her to quit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: quit - to stop doing something, especially permanently
