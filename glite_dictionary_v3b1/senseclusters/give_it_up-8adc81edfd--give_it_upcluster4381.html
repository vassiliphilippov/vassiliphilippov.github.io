Synonyms

    1.2 80/m
    give up is to stop doing something formally or broadly, while give it up is a more informal phrase for quitting.
  • quit
    1.0 30/m
    quit is a short neutral verb meaning to stop an activity, while give it up sounds more informal and conversational.
  • call it quits
    3.9 2/m
    call it quits is an idiom people use to decide to stop together, while give it up often names one person's choice.
  • throw in the towel
    4.8 1/m
    throw in the towel is an idiom for admitting defeat in a struggle, while give it up can mean simply quitting an activity.
  • throw in
    4.9 1/m

  • chuck up the sponge
