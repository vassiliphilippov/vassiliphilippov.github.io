  • call it quits
    3.9 1.09/m
    call it quits is an idiom people use to decide to stop together, while give it up often names one person's choice.
  • throw in the towel
    4.8 1.19/m

  • throw in
    4.9 0.97/m

  • chuck up the sponge
    9.9 0.00/m
    chuck up the sponge is a dated informal phrase for quitting after failure, while give it up is more modern and general.
  • abandon
    3.8 24/m

  • surrender
    4.2 4.58/m
