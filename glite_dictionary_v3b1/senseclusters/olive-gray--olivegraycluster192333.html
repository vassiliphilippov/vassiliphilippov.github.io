olive-gray

adjective
gray tinged with olive
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːlɪv ˌɡreɪ /; / ˈɑːlɪv ɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪv ˌɡreɪ /; / ˈɒlɪv ɡreɪ / (BrE)
