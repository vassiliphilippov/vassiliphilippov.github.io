bachelor

noun
unmarried man; first degree holder
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbætʃələr / (AmE), / ˈbætʃələ / (BrE)
a man who is not married or a person who has received a first university degree

Examples

Bachelor degree
After college, he remained a bachelor for many years before deciding to marry.
She received her bachelor in engineering from a prestigious university.
The party was organized to celebrate all the new bachelor graduates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: graduate - a person who has completed a degree at a university
  • synonym: single - not married or in a romantic relationship
  • antonym: spinster - an unmarried woman, especially an older one
