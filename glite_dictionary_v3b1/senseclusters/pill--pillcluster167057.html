pill

noun
small solid dose of medicine
2.2 1.0
Pronunciation: /pɪl/ (AmE), /pɪl/ (BrE)
a small, round, solid tablet of medicine that you swallow (including contraceptive tablets)

Examples

Swallow a pill
He took a pill every morning to control his blood pressure.
She swallowed the pill with a glass of water.
The doctor prescribed a new pill for her allergies.
Many women rely on the pill as their primary form of birth control.
She started taking the pill when she went away to college.
The doctor discussed different types of pill with her before she made a decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tablet - compressed medicinal solid, often larger and flat
