efficiently

adverb
in an efficient manner
Pronunciation: / ɪˈfɪʃəntli / (AmE), / ɪˈfɪʃəntli / (BrE)
In a way that achieves maximum productivity with minimum wasted effort or expense.

Examples

Work efficiently
She managed her time efficiently, balancing work and family life.
The team completed the project efficiently and ahead of schedule.
This machine operates more efficiently than the older model.

Related Terms

  • synonym: effectively - in an effective manner
  • antonym: inefficiently - in a way that is not efficient
