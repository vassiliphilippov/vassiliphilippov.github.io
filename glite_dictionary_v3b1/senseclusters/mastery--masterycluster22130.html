mastery

noun
great skill or complete command
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmæstəri / (AmE), / ˈmɑːstəri / (BrE)
great skill or knowledge in a particular subject or activity; complete control or command of something

Examples

Mastery of a skill
A deep understanding of language is essential for true mastery of translation.
His mastery in mathematics impressed his professors and peers alike.
She achieved mastery of the piano after years of relentless practice.

Related Terms

  • antonym: incompetence - lack of skill or ability
  • synonym: proficiency - high degree of skill or competence
