Synonyms

  • ornate
    7.0 3.44/m
    ornate is highly decorated with complex detail and formal style, while fancy means decorative and not plain in appearance.
  • florid
    8.6 0.31/m
    florid is richly decorated or uses flowery language and can feel excessive, while fancy simply means ornamented or not plain.
  • showy
    3.2 0.67/m
    showy draws attention by bright or loud styles and can seem ostentatious, while fancy means decorative without that loudness.
  • elegant
    3.3 16/m
    elegant looks graceful and refined and often stays simple, while fancy focuses on ornament and more decorative detail.
  • stylish
    2.0 6.83/m
    stylish follows current fashion and shows good taste, while fancy highlights ornament or decoration rather than modern simplicity.
