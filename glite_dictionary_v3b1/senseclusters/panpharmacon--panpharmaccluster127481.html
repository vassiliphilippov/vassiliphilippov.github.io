panpharmacon

noun
universal remedy
Pronunciation: / pænˈfɑːrməkɒn / (AmE), / pænˈfɑːməkɒn / (BrE)
a supposed remedy or solution that is believed to solve all problems or difficulties, not just medical ones

Examples

A panpharmacon for problems
Some people believe that technology will be a panpharmacon for all of society's challenges.
The new policy was presented as a panpharmacon, but it failed to address deeper issues.
There is no panpharmacon for complex economic problems, despite what some leaders claim.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cure-all - a solution that is supposed to solve every problem
  • synonym: panacea - a remedy for all diseases or problems
  • antonym: problem - a difficulty or issue that needs to be solved
