unfitted

adjective
not equipped
5.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪtɪd / (BrE)
not equipped, prepared, or provided with the necessary parts, fittings, or features.

Examples

An unfitted kitchen
The kitchen remained unfitted until the new cabinets arrived.
They moved into an unfitted apartment and had to buy all the appliances themselves.
The laboratory was still unfitted and lacked essential equipment for experiments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fitted - equipped or provided with the necessary parts
  • synonym: unfinished - not completed or fully made
