power station

noun
portable rechargeable battery generator
3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈpaʊ.ər ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /paʊr ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ˈpaʊ.ə ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /pɑː ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
A portable, rechargeable battery unit that provides AC or DC electricity to power appliances and electronic devices when mains power is unavailable.

Examples

Portable power station
We brought a compact power station to the campsite to run lights and a small fridge.
During the blackout, the family used a power station to charge phones and run a lamp.
Photographers often carry a lightweight power station to power camera batteries on remote shoots.

Related Terms

  • synonym: portable generator - a small device, often gas- or battery-powered, that produces electricity for appliances; functionally similar as a mobile power source
  • degree lower: power bank - a small portable battery used mainly to charge phones and other small devices; typically lower capacity than a power station
