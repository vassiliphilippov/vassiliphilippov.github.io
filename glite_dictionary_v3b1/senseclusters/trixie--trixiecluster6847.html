trixie

noun
three-selection bet
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪksi / (AmE), / ˈtrɪksi / (BrE)
a type of bet involving three selections that produces four bets: three doubles and one treble.

Examples

A football trixie
He decided to place a trixie on the day's football matches to increase his chances of winning.
A trixie can be a good option if you want to cover several outcomes with one bet.
She explained how a trixie works to her friend who was new to betting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accumulator - a bet combining multiple selections into one wager
  • synonym: patent - a bet involving three selections with seven bets in total
