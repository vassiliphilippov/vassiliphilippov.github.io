matzo ball

noun
Jewish dumpling made from matzo meal
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːtsoʊ bɔl /; / ˈmɑtsə bɔl / (AmE), / ˈmæt.səʊ bɔːl /; / ˈmatsə bɔːl /; / ˈmætsəʊ bɔːl / (BrE)
A dumpling made from matzo meal, eggs, and fat, traditionally served in soup as part of Jewish cuisine, especially during Passover.
