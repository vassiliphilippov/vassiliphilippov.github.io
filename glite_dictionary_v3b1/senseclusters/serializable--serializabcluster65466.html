serializable

adjective
fit for serialization
7.6
Pronunciation: /sɪr.ɪ.ə.ˈlaɪ.zə.bəl/ (AmE), /sɪə.rɪ.ə.ˈlaɪ.zə.bəl/ (BrE)
suitable to be published or presented in a series of parts or episodes

Examples

Serializable story
The editor found the story highly serializable and planned to publish it over several issues.
Her novel's structure made it easily serializable for a weekly magazine.
Writers often design plots to be serializable for television adaptation.
