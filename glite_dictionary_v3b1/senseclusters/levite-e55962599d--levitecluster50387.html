Levite

noun
member of the Hebrew tribe of Levi
Pronunciation: / ˈliːˌvaɪt / (AmE), / ˈliːˌvaɪt / (BrE)
a member of the Hebrew tribe of Levi, especially one who assisted the priests in the ancient Temple of Jerusalem

Examples

The Levite priest
During the ceremony, the Levite recited ancient prayers in Hebrew.
His ancestors were Levites who served in the Temple in Jerusalem.
The family traced its heritage back to a prominent Levite in biblical times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: priest - an ordained minister in a religious context
  • synonym: rabbi - a Jewish religious leader and teacher
