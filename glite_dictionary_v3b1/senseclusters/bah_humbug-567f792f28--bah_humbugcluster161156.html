Bah humbug

interjection
expression of disdain for festivity
Pronunciation: / bɑ hʌmbʌg / (AmE), / bɑː ˈhʌmbʌɡ / (BrE)
An exclamation used to express contempt or disapproval, especially towards festive cheer or enthusiasm.

Examples

Bah humbug expression
Every time someone wishes him Merry Christmas, he just mutters Bah humbug under his breath.
He played the role of Scrooge so convincingly, shouting Bah humbug at every bit of holiday cheer.
When asked to join the company holiday party, she replied with a curt Bah humbug.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Scrooge - a person who is mean or dislikes festivity, from Dickens
