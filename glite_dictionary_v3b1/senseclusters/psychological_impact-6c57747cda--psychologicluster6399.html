psychological impact

noun
effect on mental and emotional state
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkəˈlɑdʒɪkəl ˈɪmpækt / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkəl ˈɪmpakt / (BrE)
the effect that an event, situation, or experience has on a person's mental and emotional state

Examples

The psychological impact of events
Researchers studied the psychological impact of social media on teenagers.
The psychological impact of losing a loved one can last for years.
The disaster had a significant psychological impact on the survivors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: psychological effect - mental or emotional consequence; sometimes used synonymously
