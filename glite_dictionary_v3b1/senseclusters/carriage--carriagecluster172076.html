carriage

noun
passenger compartment of a train
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkærɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkærɪdʒ / (BrE)
a separate vehicle or compartment of a train for carrying passengers

Examples

Train carriage
She found an empty seat in the last carriage and settled in for the journey.
Smoking is prohibited in every carriage on this service.
The train was composed of several carriages, each reserved for different classes of passengers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coach - a railway carriage, especially one for passengers
  • antonym: freight car - a railcar for carrying goods rather than passengers
