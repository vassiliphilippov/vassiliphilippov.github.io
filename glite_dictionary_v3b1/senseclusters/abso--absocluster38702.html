abso

adverb
informal agreement
Pronunciation: / ˈæbsoʊ / (AmE), / ˈæbsəʊ / (BrE)
used informally to mean 'absolutely' or to strongly agree with something

Examples

Abso yes
Are you coming to the party tonight? Abso!
That was an abso amazing performance by the band.
Do you agree with the plan? Abso, it sounds perfect.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absolutely - completely or totally; used to strongly agree
  • synonym: definitely - without doubt; certainly
  • antonym: no - used to express disagreement or refusal
