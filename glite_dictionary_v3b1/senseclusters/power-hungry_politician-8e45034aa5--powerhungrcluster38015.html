power-hungry politician

noun
politician seeking excessive power
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊ.ɚ ˌhʌŋ.ɡri ˌpɑː.lɪˈtɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.ə ˌhʌŋ.ɡri ˌpɒl.ɪˈtɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
a politician who has an excessive or insatiable desire for power or control

Examples

Power-hungry politician seeks control
Many citizens grew wary of the power-hungry politician who promised to consolidate control.
People often mistrust a power-hungry politician who places ambition above all else.
The film depicted a power-hungry politician manipulating events behind the scenes.
