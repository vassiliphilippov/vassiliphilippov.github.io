strategy

noun
detailed plan to achieve a goal
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈstræt.ə.dʒiː/ (AmE), /ˈstræt.ə.dʒiː/ (BrE)

Examples

Business strategy

The company's new strategy aims to double its market share in the next five years.
They changed their strategy midway through the project to adapt to unexpected challenges.
