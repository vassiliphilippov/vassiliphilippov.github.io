walleye

noun
type of North American freshwater fish
7.9 2.95/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…ëÀêl.a…™ / (AmE), / Ààw…îÀêl.a…™ / (BrE)
A large, pike-like freshwater fish (Sander vitreus) of North America, valued for food and sport.

Examples

Catch a walleye
Fishermen travel from all over to catch walleye in the lake every spring.
The walleye is known for its glassy, reflective eyes and is a popular game fish.
We grilled the fresh walleye we caught yesterday for dinner.

Synonyms

  • Stizostedion vitreum
    9.9 0.00/m
    Stizostedion vitreum is the scientific name used in research and fish guides, while walleye is the common everyday name.
  • walleyed pike
    8.1 0.00/m
    walleyed pike is an alternate common name that highlights silvery eyes, while walleye is the usual name for this fish.
  • pike-perch
    8.9 0.00/m
    pike-perch describes a group of perch-like predatory fish such as zander, while walleye names the specific North American species.
