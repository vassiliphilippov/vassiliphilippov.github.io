fortify

verb
add alcohol to a drink
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔːr.t̬ə.faɪ / (AmE), / ˈfɔː.tɪ.faɪ / (BrE)

Examples

Some wines are fortified with brandy to enhance their alcohol content.
They decided to fortify the eggnog with a splash of rum for the party.
