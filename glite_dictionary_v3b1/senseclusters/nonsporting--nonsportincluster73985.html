nonsporting

adjective
not for sports
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈspɔrtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈspɔːtɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

Nonsporting event

He preferred nonsporting activities like painting and reading in his free time.
The event included both sporting and nonsporting competitions for all ages.
