ground

verb
connect electrically to ground
Pronunciation: / ɡraʊnd / (AmE), / ɡraʊnd / (BrE)
to connect an electrical device or system to the ground for safety or functional purposes

Examples

To ground a wire
Before installing the light fixture, always ground the wires properly.
It is important to ground your computer to prevent electrical shocks.
The electrician made sure to ground all the outlets in the new kitchen.
