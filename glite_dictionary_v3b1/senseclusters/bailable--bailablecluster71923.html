bailable

adjective
eligible for bail (legal)
6.2 6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪləbəl / (AmE), / ˈbeɪləb(ə)l /; / ˈbeɪləbəl / (BrE)
(of an offense, charge, or person) eligible for release on bail; allowing someone to be released on bail while awaiting trial
