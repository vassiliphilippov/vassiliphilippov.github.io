carnet

noun
book of tickets
8.2
Pronunciation: / kɑrˈneɪ / (AmE), / kɑːˈneɪ / (BrE)
a book or set of tickets, coupons, or vouchers sold together for repeated use, especially for public transport or events

Examples

A carnet of tickets
She bought a carnet of ten subway tickets to save time on her commute.
A carnet is often cheaper than buying individual bus tickets every day.
He showed his carnet to the conductor before taking his seat on the train.

Related Terms

  • antonym: single ticket - a ticket valid for one use only
  • synonym: ticket book - a booklet containing multiple tickets for repeated use
