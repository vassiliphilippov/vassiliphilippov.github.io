grate

noun
framework of bars covering an opening
6.4 3.2
Pronunciation: /greɪt/ (AmE), /greɪt/ (BrE)
a framework of metal bars, used to cover an opening or to hold fuel in a fireplace

Examples

A metal grate
Rainwater flowed into the drain through a metal grate on the street.
She placed the package behind the grate covering the fireplace.
The old building had a rusty grate guarding the basement window.
After the fire died out, ash collected under the grate.
She placed new coals on the grate to keep the room warm.
The logs were stacked neatly on the grate in the old stone fireplace.
