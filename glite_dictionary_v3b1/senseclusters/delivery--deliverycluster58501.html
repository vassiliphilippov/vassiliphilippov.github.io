delivery

noun
style of oral expression
1.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlɪvɚi / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪvəri / (BrE)
the manner or style in which someone expresses themselves orally, especially in public speaking or performance

Examples

Speech delivery
Critics praised the actor’s delivery of his lines for its natural flow and emotion.
Her delivery during the debate was calm and persuasive, capturing the audience's attention.
The comedian's delivery of each joke made the performance even funnier.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elocution - the skill of clear and expressive speech, especially of distinct pronunciation and articulation
  • synonym: presentation - the manner in which someone delivers a speech or performance
