nonreproductive

adjective
not able to reproduce
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌriːprəˈdʌktɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌriːprəˈdʌktɪv / (BrE)
not able to or not involved in producing offspring or reproduction
