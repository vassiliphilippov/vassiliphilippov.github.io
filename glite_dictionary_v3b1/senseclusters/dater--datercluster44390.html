dater

noun
person who assigns dates
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈdeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who determines or assigns the date or age of something, especially in history, archaeology, or science

Examples

Artifact dater
The museum hired a dater to establish the age of the newly discovered pottery.
A skilled dater can use carbon dating to determine when an artifact was made.
The research team relied on a dater to verify the timeline of the fossils.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chronologist - a person who studies or arranges events in order of time
  • synonym: historian - a person who studies or writes about history
