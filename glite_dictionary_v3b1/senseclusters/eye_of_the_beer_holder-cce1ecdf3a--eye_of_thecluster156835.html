eye of the beer holder

noun
humorous twist on 'eye of the beholder'
Pronunciation: / aɪ əv ðə bɪr ˈhoʊldər / (AmE), / aɪ əv ðə bɪə ˈhəʊldə / (BrE)
a humorous adaptation of 'eye of the beholder,' suggesting one's perception of beauty is influenced by the amount of beer one has drunk

Examples

The eye of the beer holder joke
After a few drinks, he joked that beauty is in the eye of the beer holder.
Her questionable taste in fashion was explained away by the phrase, 'eye of the beer holder.'
The sign in the pub said, 'Remember, it's all in the eye of the beer holder.'

Related Terms

  • synonym: eye of the beholder - the original idiom meaning beauty is subjective
