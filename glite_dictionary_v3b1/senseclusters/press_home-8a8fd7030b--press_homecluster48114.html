press home

verb
emphasize forcefully to convince
8.3 6.1
Pronunciation: / prɛs hoʊm / (AmE), / prɛs həʊm / (BrE)
to state or emphasize something forcefully and clearly in order to make someone understand or accept it

Examples

Press home a point
He pressed home his argument by citing several convincing statistics.
She repeatedly pressed home her point until everyone understood what she meant.
The minister tried to press home the importance of the new policy during his speech.
