Synonyms

  • out of the blue
    3.4 3.35/m
    out of the blue appears as a casual phrase for sudden events, while unexpectedly fits both formal and informal situations.
  • out of nowhere
    4.2 5.47/m
    out of nowhere paints a sudden, surprising appearance like something arriving from nowhere, while unexpectedly is more neutral.
  • unawares
    7.2 0.09/m
    unawares means acting or being found by surprise without awareness, while unexpectedly just says the event was not expected.
  • surprisingly
    2.5 30/m
    surprisingly signals the speaker's surprise or opinion about an event, while unexpectedly simply notes that it was not expected.
  • unforeseeably
    7.2 0.01/m
    unforeseeably describes something that cannot be predicted by anyone, while unexpectedly focuses on being not expected in that moment.
  • unpredictably
    4.8 0.57/m
    unpredictably suggests erratic or changeable behavior over time, while unexpectedly refers to a single unplanned event.
