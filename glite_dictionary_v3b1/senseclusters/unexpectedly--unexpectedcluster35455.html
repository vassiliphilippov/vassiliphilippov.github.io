unexpectedly

adverb
in an unanticipated or surprising way
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪkˈspɛktɪdli / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪkˈspɛktɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that is not expected or foreseen; without warning or planning

Examples

Happened unexpectedly
Rain unexpectedly began to fall during the outdoor ceremony.
She unexpectedly received a promotion at work yesterday.
The meeting ended unexpectedly after only half an hour due to a technical issue.
She unexpectedly received a letter from an old friend.
The lights went out unexpectedly during the meeting.
The weather changed unexpectedly, forcing us to cancel the picnic.

Related Terms

  • antonym: predictably - as expected; in a predictable way
  • synonym: suddenly - quickly and without warning
