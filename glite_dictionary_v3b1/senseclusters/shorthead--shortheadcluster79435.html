shorthead

noun
species with short head
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɔrtˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːtˌhɛd / (BrE)
an animal or fish species distinguished by having a relatively short head compared to related species

Examples

The shorthead redhorse
The shorthead redhorse is a common fish in North American rivers.
Scientists identified a new shorthead species in the lake during their research.
The shorthead variety is easily recognized by its compact skull shape.

  • antonym: longhead - a species with a relatively long head
