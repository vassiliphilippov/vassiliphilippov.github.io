windowing

noun
use of time slots
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɪndəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of assigning or using specific time periods for activities, deliveries, or communications

Examples

Windowing delivery times
The delivery service improved efficiency by implementing windowing for customer orders.
Airports often use windowing to manage takeoff and landing times for flights.
With windowing, each team was given a specific hour to access the conference room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scheduling - the process of planning activities at specific times
