protrusive

adjective
thrusting or projecting outward
7.8
Pronunciation: / proʊˈtruːsɪv / (AmE), / prəˈtruːsɪv / (BrE)
Thrusting or extending outward; projecting.

Examples

Protrusive teeth
His protrusive teeth made him self-conscious as a child.
She disliked the protrusive edges of the broken table.
The sculpture's protrusive nose drew everyone's attention.

Related Terms

  • antonym: recessive - going back or receding
