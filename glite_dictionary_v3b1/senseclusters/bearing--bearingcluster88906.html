bearing

noun
way of carrying one's body
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbeərɪŋ / (BrE)
the way a person carries or presents their body and manner, especially showing dignity or self-control

Examples

Confident bearing
Despite his casual clothes, his upright bearing conveyed authority.
She walked into the room with a confident bearing that impressed everyone.
Years of military service had given him a disciplined bearing.
Despite the chaos around her, she maintained a dignified bearing and never lost her composure.
He walked onto the stage with a bearing that inspired confidence in the audience.
Her calm bearing impressed everyone at the meeting, especially during the crisis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carriage - the manner in which someone moves or carries themselves
  • synonym: demeanor - outward behavior or bearing
  • synonym: posture - the position in which someone holds their body
