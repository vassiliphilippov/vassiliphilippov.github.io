overlocker

noun
sewing edge machine
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌlɑːkər / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəlɒkə / (BrE)
a sewing machine that trims and binds the edges of fabric to prevent fraying

Examples

A overlocker machine
She finished the raw edges of the fabric with an overlocker for a professional look.
The tailor recommended using an overlocker to prevent the seams from unraveling.
Many home sewers invest in an overlocker to improve the durability of their garments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: serger - a sewing machine for overcasting to prevent fabric edges from fraying
  • synonym: sewing machine - a machine used to stitch fabric and other materials together
