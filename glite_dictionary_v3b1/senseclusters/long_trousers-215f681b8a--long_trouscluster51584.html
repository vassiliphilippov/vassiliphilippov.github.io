long trousers

noun
trousers reaching to the ankles
2.7
Pronunciation: / lɔŋ ˈtraʊzɚz / (AmE), / lɒŋ ˈtraʊzəz / (BrE)
Trousers that extend down to the ankles or feet, covering the entire leg.

Examples

A pair of long trousers
He prefers to wear long trousers rather than shorts, even in summer.
She bought a new pair of long trousers for her job interview.
The school's dress code requires students to wear long trousers at all times.

  • antonym: shorts - trousers that end above the knee
