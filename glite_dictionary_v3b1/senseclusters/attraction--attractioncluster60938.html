attraction

noun
main entertainer or act drawing an audience
Pronunciation: / əˈtræk.ʃən /; / əˈtrækʃən / (AmE), / əˈtræk.ʃən /; / əˈtrækʃən / (BrE)
an event, show, performance, or place that draws public interest or visitors

Examples

Tourist attraction
She quickly became the biggest attraction at the comedy club.

The music festival's main attraction was a world-famous rock band.
The main attraction at the fair was the circus performance.
The museum's newest attraction is a virtual reality exhibit.
The theme park opened a new roller coaster as its latest attraction.
