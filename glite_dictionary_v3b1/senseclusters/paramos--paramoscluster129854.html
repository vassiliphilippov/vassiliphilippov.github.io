paramos

noun
Andean grasslands
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːrəˌmoʊs / (AmE), / ˈpærəməʊs / (BrE)
high, treeless plateaus or grasslands found in the Andes mountains, often characterized by unique ecosystems

Examples

The paramos grasslands
The unique plants and animals of the paramos have adapted to the cold, windy climate.
Scientists study the paramos to understand how climate change affects mountain ecosystems.
Many rivers in South America begin in the high paramos of the Andes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: savanna - a grassy plain in tropical and subtropical regions
  • synonym: steppe - large area of flat unforested grassland
