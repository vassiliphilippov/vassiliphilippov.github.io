candidacy

noun
state of being a candidate
Pronunciation: / ˈkændɪdəsi / (AmE), / ˈkændɪdəsi / (BrE)
the state of being a candidate, especially in an election or for a particular position

Examples

His candidacy for election
He withdrew his candidacy due to personal reasons.
Her candidacy for the mayoral election was announced last week.
The party officially endorsed his candidacy after a long debate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: candidature - the condition of being a candidate (used in British English)
