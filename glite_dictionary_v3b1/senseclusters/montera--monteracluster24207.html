montera

noun
bullfighter's hat
Pronunciation: / mɒnˈtɛrə / (AmE), / mɒnˈtɛərə / (BrE)
a traditional round hat with side flaps worn by Spanish bullfighters

Examples

The montera hat
The matador placed his montera on his head before stepping into the arena.
Spectators admired the ornate design of the montera during the opening ceremony.
He bought a souvenir montera after watching his first bullfight in Spain.
