nonrelease

noun
failure to open
9.8
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.rɪ.ˈliːs/ /; / /nən.rɪ.ˈliːs/ (AmE), /nɒn.rɪ.ˈliːs/ /; / /nən.rɪ.ˈliːs/ (BrE)
