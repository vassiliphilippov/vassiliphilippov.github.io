megalosaurus

noun
large carnivorous Jurassic dinosaur
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛɡ.ə.ləˈsɔr.əs / (AmE), / ˌmɛɡ.ə.ləˈsɔː.rəs / (BrE)
a large, carnivorous, bipedal dinosaur from the Jurassic or early Cretaceous period found in Europe

Examples

The megalosaurus skeleton
Children at the museum were fascinated by the skeleton of the megalosaurus.
Several fossils of megalosaurus have been discovered in England.
The megalosaurus was one of the first dinosaurs to be scientifically described.
