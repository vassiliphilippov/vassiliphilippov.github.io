rotini

noun
spiral pasta
3.5
Pronunciation: / roʊˈtini / (AmE), / rəʊˈtiːni / (BrE)
a type of pasta shaped like short, corkscrew spirals, often used in salads and pasta dishes

Examples

A rotini pasta
She made a salad with rotini and fresh vegetables.
The chef recommended using rotini because its shape holds sauces well.
We bought a box of rotini for tonight's pasta dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fusilli - a spiral-shaped pasta similar to rotini
  • antonym: penne - a tube-shaped pasta
