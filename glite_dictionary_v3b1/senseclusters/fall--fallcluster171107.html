fall

verb
hang or flow loosely in a particular way
Pronunciation: / fɔl / (AmE), / fɔːl / (BrE)
(of hair, clothing, etc.) to hang or flow loosely and gracefully in a particular way.

Examples

Her hair falls softly
Her hair fell softly over her shoulders, shining in the morning light.
The curtains fall in elegant folds to the floor.
The fabric falls nicely, giving the dress a graceful appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drape - to hang or arrange loosely or gracefully
  • synonym: hang - to be suspended or draped in a certain position
