trumpet creeper

noun
woody vine with trumpet-shaped red flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌmpət ˌkripər / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpɪt ˌkriːpə / (BrE)
a North American woody vine with pinnate leaves and large red, trumpet-shaped flowers, commonly grown as an ornamental plant

Examples

The trumpet creeper flower
Gardeners often plant trumpet creeper to attract hummingbirds with its showy blooms.
The trumpet creeper climbed gracefully along the garden fence, displaying its vivid red flowers.
We trimmed the trumpet creeper each spring to keep it from overtaking the old shed.
