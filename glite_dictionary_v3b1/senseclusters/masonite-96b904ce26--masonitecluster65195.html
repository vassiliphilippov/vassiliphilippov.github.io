Masonite

noun
type of hard fiberboard
9.7 6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈmeɪ.sən.aɪt/ /; / /ˈmæs.ə.naɪt/ (AmE), /ˈmeɪ.sən.aɪt/ (BrE)
