daymare

noun
daytime frightening thought
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdeɪ.meər/ (AmE), /ˈdeɪ.meə/ (BrE)
a frightening or disturbing experience or thought that happens while you are awake, similar to a nightmare but during the day

Examples

A daymare experience
After the accident, he was often troubled by daymares that made it hard to concentrate at work.
The vivid memory of her mistake replayed as a daymare throughout the afternoon.
Sometimes, stressful events can trigger daymares that feel just as real as nightmares.
After the accident, he described his panic attack as a daymare.
The memory of the event returned as a sudden daymare while she was at work.
He struggled to focus in class because a daymare kept replaying in his mind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anxiety - a feeling of worry or unease
  • synonym: hallucination - an experience of seeing or hearing something that is not actually there
  • antonym: nightmare - a frightening or unpleasant dream
