evoked potential

noun
electrical brain response to stimulation
9.3 6.7
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈvoʊkt pə.ˈten.ʃəl/ /; / /iː.ˈvoʊkt pə.ˈten.ʃəl/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈvəʊkt pə.ˈten.ʃəl/ /; / /iː.ˈvəʊkt pə.ˈten.ʃəl/ (BrE)
