misprise

verb
to misunderstand
Pronunciation: / mɪsˈpraɪz / (AmE), / mɪsˈpraɪz / (BrE)
to misunderstand or interpret something incorrectly

Examples

To misprise a message
He did not mean to offend, but she misprised his words and became upset.
It is easy to misprise someone's intentions when communication is unclear.
The letter was misprised by the recipient, leading to confusion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comprehend - to understand something fully
  • synonym: misinterpret - to understand something incorrectly
  • synonym: misunderstand - to fail to understand correctly
