balconette

noun
low-cut bra style
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌbælkəˈnɛt / (AmE), / ˌbælkəˈnɛt / (BrE)
a style of bra with wide-set straps and a low-cut cup that lifts and enhances the shape of the bust

Examples

Balconette bra style
She wore a balconette under her dress to create a flattering neckline.
The store offers several balconette styles in different colors and fabrics.
A balconette is ideal for tops with wide or low necklines.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sports bra - a bra designed for support during physical activity
