Alpine lift

noun
surface ski lift with bar for pulling riders uphill
8.6 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈæl.paɪn lɪft/ /; / /ˈælp.ɪn lɪft/ (AmE), /ˈæl.paɪn lɪft/ /; / /ˈælp.ɪn lɪft/ (BrE)
A type of surface ski lift in which riders hold a bar and are pulled up a slope while remaining on their skis or snowboard.
