telemark

noun
a specific skiing turn
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛlɪˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌmɑːk / (BrE)
a turn in skiing where the outside ski is placed ahead of the inside ski and both are progressively turned inward

Examples

A telemark turn
Learning how to execute a telemark is essential for classic cross-country skiers.
She performed a perfect telemark as she descended the slope.
The instructor demonstrated the telemark turn for the beginners.
