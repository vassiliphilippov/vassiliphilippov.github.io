gunslinging

noun
bold use of guns
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌnˌslɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnˌslɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or style of using guns, especially in a bold, skillful, or showy manner

Examples

Wild West gunslinging
The old Western was filled with dramatic gunslinging and tense standoffs.
He became famous for his daring gunslinging during the wild days of the frontier.
Modern action films often feature stylized gunslinging scenes inspired by classic Westerns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gunfighting - the act of fighting with guns, especially in a duel or shootout
  • antonym: peacemaking - the act of bringing about peace or resolving conflict
