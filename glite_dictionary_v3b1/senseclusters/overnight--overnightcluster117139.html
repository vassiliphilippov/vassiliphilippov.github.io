overnight

adverb
very quickly; in a short time
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɚˈnaɪt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈnaɪt / (BrE)
In a very short time or with great speed; very quickly.

Examples

Changed overnight
The news of his promotion spread overnight throughout the office.
Their fortunes changed overnight when the company was acquired.
You can't expect to see results overnight—it takes time.
