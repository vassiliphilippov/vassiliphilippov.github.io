Amaranthus hypochondriacus

noun
species of amaranth plant used for food
Pronunciation: / ˌæməˈrænθəs ˌhaɪpoʊˌkɑnˌdriˈækəs /; / ˌæməˈrænθəs haɪpəˌkɑndriˈækəs / (AmE), / ˌæməˈrænθəs ˌhɪpəʊˌkɒndrɪˈakəs /; / ˌæməˈranθəs hʌɪpəˌkɒndriˈakəs / (BrE)
a tall, showy plant (amaranth) whose leaves are sometimes eaten as greens and whose seeds are used as a cereal; also grown for its striking red flower spikes
