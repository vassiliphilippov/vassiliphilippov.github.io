purple orchis

noun
North American orchid with violet and white flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝː.pəl ˈɔːr.kɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.pəl ˈɔː.kɪs / (BrE)
A North American orchid with a spike of violet-purple and white flowers, with sepals and petals forming a hood.
