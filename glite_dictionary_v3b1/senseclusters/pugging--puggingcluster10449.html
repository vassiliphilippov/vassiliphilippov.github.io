pugging

noun
soil damage by trampling
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpʌɡɪŋ / (BrE)
damage to turf or soil caused by animals or heavy traffic on wet ground, resulting in churned, muddy depressions

Examples

Soil pugging damage
Heavy rainfall and cattle movement led to severe pugging in the pasture.
Farmers try to prevent pugging by rotating grazing areas during wet seasons.
The football field suffered from pugging after the tournament was held in the rain.

  • synonym: poaching - damage to soil or turf by trampling, especially by livestock
