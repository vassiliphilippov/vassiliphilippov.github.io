tropically

adverb
in a tropical manner
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɑːpɪkli / (AmE), / ˈtrɒpɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner characteristic of the tropics, especially referring to warmth, humidity, or vegetation typical of tropical regions.

Examples

In tropically warm places
Banana trees thrive in tropically warm climates.
The air was tropically humid throughout the rainy season.
We sat on the veranda, sipping cold drinks on a tropically hot afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tropical - relating to the tropics or characteristic of tropical regions
