unorthodoxy

noun
lack of conformity to accepted beliefs
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɔrθəˌdɑksi /; / ˌʌnˈɔr.θəˌdɑk.si /; / ʌnˈɔr.θəˌdɑk.si / (AmE), / ʌnˈɔːθədɒksi /; / ˌʌnˈɔː.θəˌdɒk.si /; / ʌnˈɔː.θəˌdɒk.si / (BrE)
the quality or state of not following established or accepted beliefs, customs, or practices (including in religion)
