true senna

noun
medicinal shrub; source of cathartic leaves
8.0
Pronunciation: /truː ˈsen.ə/ (AmE), /truː ˈsen.ə/ (BrE)
an erect shrub (Senna alexandrina) with racemes of tawny yellow flowers, whose dried leaves are used medicinally as a laxative
