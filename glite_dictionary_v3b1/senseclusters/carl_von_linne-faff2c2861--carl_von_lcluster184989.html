Carl von Linne

proper noun
Swedish botanist; creator of binomial nomenclature
Pronunciation: / kɑrl vɑn lɪˈneɪ / (AmE), / kɑːl von lɪˈneɪ / (BrE)
(1707–1778) Swedish botanist known for establishing the modern system of biological nomenclature.
