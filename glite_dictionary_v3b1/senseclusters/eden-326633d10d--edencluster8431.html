Eden

noun
an ideal paradise; place of perfect happiness
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈiː.dən / (AmE), / ˈiː.dən / (BrE)
a place or state of perfect happiness, bliss, and peace; often used metaphorically for an ideal paradise

Examples

A peaceful Eden
After years of hard work, she finally found her own Eden in a peaceful mountain cabin.
He described their summer together as an Eden he wished would never end.
The island was a hidden Eden, untouched by modern civilization and filled with natural beauty.
