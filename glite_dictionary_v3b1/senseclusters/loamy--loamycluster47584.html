loamy

adjective
having qualities of loam (soil)
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊmi / (AmE), / ˈləʊmi / (BrE)
having the characteristics of loam; consisting of or resembling loam, especially in soil.

Examples

Loamy soil
Experts recommend planting vegetables in loamy earth for optimal growth.
She scooped up a handful of loamy dirt to test its texture.
The garden thrives best in loamy soil that retains moisture but drains well.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: clayey - containing or resembling clay; a type of soil that retains water
  • degree lower: sandy - containing or resembling sand; a type of soil
