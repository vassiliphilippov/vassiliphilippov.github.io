runt

noun
small, weak person (disparaging)
9.9
Pronunciation: / rʌnt / (AmE), / rʌnt / (BrE)
the smallest or weakest member of a group, especially an animal in a litter or a small, weak person

Examples

The runt of litter
At school, he was often bullied for being the runt of the class.
She may be small, but don’t call her a runt; she's tough as nails.
The taller kids laughed and called him a runt, which hurt his feelings.
The runt of the litter often needs extra attention to grow strong.
Despite being the runt, the puppy eventually caught up in size with its siblings.
Farmers sometimes separate the runt to make sure it gets enough food.
The runt of the litter needed extra care to survive.
Although he was the runt in his class, he was very determined.
They adopted the runt because it was the smallest and cutest puppy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dwarf - a person of unusually short stature, especially due to medical condition
  • antonym: giant - a person of unusually great height or size
  • synonym: weakling - an animal or person who is physically weak
