sulfite

verb
to add sulfites
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌlfaɪt / (AmE), / ˈsʌlfaɪt / (BrE)
to treat or preserve food, drink, or materials by adding sulfite compounds as preservatives or antioxidants

Examples

To sulfite dried fruit
Food manufacturers often sulfite dried fruits to keep their color and freshness.
Some people are sensitive to foods that have been sulfited during processing.
Winemakers may sulfite wine to prevent unwanted fermentation and spoilage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: preserve - to treat food or materials to prevent decay
  • antonym: spoil - to become or make unfit for use or consumption
