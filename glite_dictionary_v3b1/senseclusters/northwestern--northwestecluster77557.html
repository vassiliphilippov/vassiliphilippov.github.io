northwestern

adjective
of the northwestern United States
Pronunciation: / ˌnɔrθˈwɛstərn / (AmE), / ˌnɔːθˈwɛstən / (BrE)
of or relating to the northwestern part of the United States, generally including Washington, Oregon, Idaho, and sometimes Montana and Wyoming.

Examples

Northwestern states
Heavy rainfall is common in the northwestern states during the winter.
Many tech companies are based in the northwestern region of the country.
The forests of the northwestern United States are rich in biodiversity.
