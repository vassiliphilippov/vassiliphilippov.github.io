underlining

noun
fabric layer inside garment
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌlaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌlaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
a layer of fabric sewn inside a garment to support, protect, or shape the outer material

Examples

Dress underlining
The tailor added a soft underlining to the dress to prevent the fabric from puckering.
A sturdy underlining helps the jacket keep its shape over time.
She chose a lightweight underlining so the skirt would remain comfortable in warm weather.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interfacing - a material used between layers of fabric to give structure
  • synonym: lining - a layer of fabric inside a garment for comfort or finish
