affricate

noun
a consonant sound combining a stop and a fricative
9.9 8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈæ.frə.kət/ /; / /ˈæ.frɪ.kət/ (AmE), /ˈæ.frɪ.kət/ /; / /ˈæ.frə.kət/ (BrE)
A speech sound consisting of a stop followed immediately by a fricative, both articulated at the same point, as in 'ch' in 'chair' and 'j' in 'joy'.
