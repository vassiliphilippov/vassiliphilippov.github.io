uprise

verb
to arise or come into existence
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈraɪz / (AmE), / ʌpˈraɪz / (BrE)
To come into existence, take form, or develop; to appear or arise.

Examples

A feeling uprises
A strange hush uprose in the hall as the announcement was made.
An era of optimism began to uprise among the people after the war ended.
Within the artist’s mind, new ideas seem to uprise effortlessly from nowhere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arise - to begin to exist or develop
  • antonym: dissolve - to disappear or cease to exist
  • synonym: originate - to come into existence; to have a specified beginning
