summerset

noun
acrobatic roll over the head
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌmərˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈsʌməsɛt / (BrE)
An acrobatic movement in which a person turns over completely in the air or on the ground, feet passing over the head and landing back on the feet.

Examples

To summerset quickly
Children love to do a quick summerset on the playground mats.
He landed awkwardly after attempting a backward summerset on the grass.
The gymnast performed a flawless summerset during her routine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: somersault - an acrobatic roll where the body turns head over heels
