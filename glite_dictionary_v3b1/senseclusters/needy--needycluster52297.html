needy

noun
poor or disadvantaged people collectively
1.0 1.37/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàniÀêdi / (AmE), / ÀàniÀêdi / (BrE)
people who are poor or lacking the basic necessities of life; the poor considered as a group.

Examples

Help the needy
During the winter, volunteers collect blankets for the needy.
Many charities focus their efforts on helping the needy and homeless.
The organization donates food and clothes to the needy in the city.

Synonyms

  • poor people
    1.0 6.88/m
    poor people are a common, everyday phrase for people without money, while needy refers to the poor as a group in charity contexts.
  • poor person
    1.0 0.47/m
    poor person names an individual who has little money, while needy refers to the poor as a group lacking basic needs.
  • pauper
    7.4 1.45/m
    pauper is an old-fashioned word for someone extremely poor who needs charity, while needy describes the poor as a social group.
