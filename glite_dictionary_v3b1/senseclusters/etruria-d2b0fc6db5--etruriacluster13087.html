Etruria

proper noun
ancient country in central Italy
Pronunciation: / ɪˈtruːriə / (AmE), / ɪˈtrʊəriə / (BrE)
an ancient country in central Italy, inhabited by the Etruscans and later assimilated by the Romans around 200 BC

Examples

Ancient Etruria
Ancient Etruria was famous for its art, architecture, and metalwork.
Many Roman roads were originally constructed in the territory of Etruria.
The artifacts from Etruria reveal a sophisticated civilization that influenced Roman culture.
