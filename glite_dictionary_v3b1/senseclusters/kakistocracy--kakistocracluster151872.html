kakistocracy

noun
rule by the worst
Pronunciation: / ˌkækɪˈstɑːkrəsi / (AmE), / ˌkækɪˈstɒkrəsi / (BrE)
a government or organization run by the least qualified, most incompetent, or most unprincipled people

Examples

A kakistocracy government
Many citizens feared their country was turning into a kakistocracy after years of corruption scandals.
Critics described the new administration as a kakistocracy due to its lack of experienced leaders.
Some historians argue that periods of kakistocracy have led to national decline.
