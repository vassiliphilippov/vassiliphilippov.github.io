ring mail

noun
armor made of interlinked metal rings
Pronunciation: / rɪŋ meɪl / (AmE), / rɪŋ meɪl / (BrE)
A type of flexible armor from the Middle Ages, made of interlinked metal rings.

Examples

A ring mail armor
He admired the craftsmanship involved in creating ring mail by hand.

The museum displayed a suit of ring mail worn by a medieval knight.
