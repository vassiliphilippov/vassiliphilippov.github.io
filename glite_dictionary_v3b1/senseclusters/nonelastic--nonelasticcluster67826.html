nonelastic

adjective
not stretchable
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnɪˈlæstɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪˈlæstɪk / (BrE)
not able to return to its original shape after being stretched or compressed

Examples

Nonelastic bandage
The bandage is made from a nonelastic fabric that does not stretch when wrapped.
Because the cord was nonelastic, it could not absorb any shock from sudden movements.
For this experiment, we need a nonelastic string to ensure accurate measurements.

Related Terms

  • antonym: elastic - able to return to original shape after being stretched
  • synonym: inelastic - not able to stretch or return to original shape
