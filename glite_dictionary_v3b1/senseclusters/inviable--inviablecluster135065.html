inviable

adjective
unable to survive
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvaɪəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈvaɪəbəl / (BrE)
not capable of surviving, working, or succeeding

Examples

An inviable embryo
The scientists concluded that the embryo was inviable and would not develop further.
Due to a lack of funding, the project soon became inviable.
Many experts warned that the proposal was inviable in the current economic climate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impractical - not sensible or realistic to carry out
  • synonym: unworkable - not able to function or be put into practice
  • antonym: viable - capable of working, surviving, or succeeding
