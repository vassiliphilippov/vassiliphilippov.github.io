philippic

noun
bitter denunciatory speech
6.6
Pronunciation: / fəˈlɪpɪk / (AmE), / fɪˈlɪpɪk / (BrE)
a speech or piece of writing that strongly denounces or criticizes someone or something

Examples

An angry philippic
His angry philippic shocked everyone at the meeting.
She launched into a philippic about the failings of modern society.
The senator delivered a fierce philippic against the corruption in government.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diatribe - a forceful and bitter verbal attack
  • synonym: tirade - a long, angry speech of criticism or accusation
