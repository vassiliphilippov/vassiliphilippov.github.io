busulfan

noun
chemotherapy drug
9.5
Pronunciation: / bjuːˈsʌlfæn / (AmE), / bjuːˈsʌlfæn / (BrE)
a chemotherapy drug used to treat certain blood cancers (such as leukemia and lymphoma) and to destroy bone marrow cells before a bone marrow or stem-cell transplant
