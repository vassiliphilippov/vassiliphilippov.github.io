wristband

noun
strap for attaching a wristwatch
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪstbænd /; / ˈrɪstˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈrɪstbænd /; / ˈrɪst.bænd / (BrE)
a band of cloth, leather, metal, or other material worn around the wrist, often as part of a watch, garment, or as an accessory
