crawfish

noun
spiny-shelled edible marine crustacean
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔˌfɪʃ /; / ˈkrɔːˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkrɔːfɪʃ /; / ˈkrɔːˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a small lobster-like crustacean found in water (freshwater or marine), often eaten as food
