heavy metal

noun
genre of loud, amplified rock music
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛvi ˈmɛtəl /; / ˌhɛvi ˈmɛtəl / (AmE), / ˌhɛvi ˈmɛtəl / (BrE)
a style of loud, aggressive rock music with strong rhythms and amplified distortion

Examples

Heavy metal music
He has been a fan of heavy metal since high school, especially bands from the 1980s.
Some people find heavy metal too loud, but others appreciate its energy and intensity.
The concert featured several heavy metal groups performing to a crowd of enthusiastic fans.
After hearing a heavy metal concert live, she became a fan of the genre.
Liam's favorite band plays heavy metal with thunderous guitar riffs and powerful vocals.
Not everyone enjoys the intensity of heavy metal, but its loyal fans are passionate.
