bell

noun
hollow metal object that rings
Pronunciation: / bɛl / (AmE), / bɛl / (BrE)
a hollow metal object that makes a ringing sound when struck, or a musical instrument made of such objects

Examples

A ringing bell
A small silver bell hung from the cat’s collar, tinkling as it moved.
Please ring the bell if you need assistance at the counter.
The church bell rang out at noon, echoing through the village.
She practiced the bell every day to prepare for the concert.
The orchestra's conductor signaled for the bell to begin the next movement.
The percussionist played the bell part during the symphony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chime - a set of bells tuned in harmony
  • synonym: glockenspiel - a percussion instrument made of tuned metal bars, similar in sound to bells
  • synonym: gong - a metallic percussion instrument that produces a resonant sound
