mystical

adjective
relating to mysticism; spiritual or mysterious
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪstɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈmɪstɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to mysticism or having a spiritual, mysterious, or supernatural quality that is beyond normal understanding
