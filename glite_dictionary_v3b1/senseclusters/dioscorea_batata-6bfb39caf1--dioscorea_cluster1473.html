Dioscorea batata

noun
Chinese yam; edible tuber-producing vine
9.9
Pronunciation: / di.ɑsˈkɔri.ə bəˈtɑtə / (AmE), / di.ɒsˈkɔːri.ə bəˈtɑːtə / (BrE)
A hardy Chinese vine valued for its edible tubers and glossy, cinnamon-scented, heart-shaped leaves, cultivated as both an ornamental climber and a food crop.

Examples

Dioscorea batata plant
Dioscorea batata is often grown in gardens for its attractive foliage and edible roots.
In warmer climates, Dioscorea batata can become a vigorously climbing ornamental plant.
The edible tubers of Dioscorea batata are used in various Asian dishes.
