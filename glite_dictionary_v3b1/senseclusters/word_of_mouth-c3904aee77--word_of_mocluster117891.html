word of mouth

noun
information spread by spoken communication
3.6 2.2
Pronunciation: /wɜːrd əv maʊθ/ /; / /wɜːrd ə maʊθ/ (AmE), /wɜːd ə maʊθ/ /; / /wɜːd əv maʊθ/ (BrE)
the process of spreading information or recommendations informally by spoken communication from one person to another

Examples

The movie's popularity spread quickly by word of mouth before it even had an official trailer.
