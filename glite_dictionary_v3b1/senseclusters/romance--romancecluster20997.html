romance

noun
novel with idealized or adventurous events
3.8
Pronunciation: / roʊˈmæns / (AmE), / rəʊˈmæns / (BrE)
a story, novel, or film that focuses on love or adventure, often with idealized or heroic events

Examples

A romance novel
He dreams of writing a sweeping historical romance set in ancient Persia.
Her favorite romance transports readers to a mythical kingdom full of daring knights and mysterious quests.
The library's collection includes several classic romances from the nineteenth century.
She loves reading romance novels on rainy afternoons.
The author's new romance quickly became a bestseller.
The latest movie is a romance set in Paris during springtime.

Related Terms

  • synonym: love story - a story about a romantic relationship
  • synonym: romantic novel - a novel focused on idealized love and relationships
  • antonym: tragedy - a story with a sad or disastrous ending, often contrasted with romance
