diphenylhydantoin

noun
anticonvulsant drug for epilepsy
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪ.fɛ.nɪl.haɪˈdæn.tɔɪn / (AmE), / ˌdɪ.fɛn.ɪl.haɪˈdæn.tɔɪn / (BrE)
a drug used as an anticonvulsant to treat epilepsy, also known as phenytoin

Examples

Diphenylhydantoin drug
After being diagnosed with epilepsy, she was prescribed diphenylhydantoin to control her seizures.
Many doctors favor diphenylhydantoin due to its effectiveness and manageable side effects.
The patient's blood levels of diphenylhydantoin must be monitored regularly to ensure safety.
