consummate

verb
make a marriage complete by intercourse
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.səˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.sjʊ.meɪt / (BrE)
to make (a marriage or relationship) complete by having sexual intercourse

Examples

To consummate a marriage
In some cultures, a marriage is not considered legal until the partners consummate their union.
The couple planned to consummate their marriage after the wedding ceremony.
They finally consummated their relationship during their honeymoon.
