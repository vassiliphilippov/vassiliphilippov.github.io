legal right

noun
a right protected by law
Pronunciation: / ˈliɡəl ˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈliːɡəl ˌraɪt / (BrE)
a right that is recognized and protected by law

Examples

A legal right
Access to clean water is considered a legal right in many countries.
Every citizen has a legal right to fair treatment under the law.
The company defended its legal right to use the trademark in court.

Related Terms

  • synonym: civil right - a personal right guaranteed to individuals by law
  • antonym: privilege - an advantage or right reserved exclusively to a particular person or group
