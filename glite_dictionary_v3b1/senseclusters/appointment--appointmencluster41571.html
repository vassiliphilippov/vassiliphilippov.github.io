appointment

noun
furnishings and equipment (esp. of ship or hotel)
8.6
Pronunciation: / əˈpɔɪntmənt / (AmE), / əˈpɔɪntmənt / (BrE)
(usually appointments) the furnishings and equipment of a room, building, or vehicle, especially those of a ship or hotel.

Examples

Hotel appointments
The conference hall was praised for its modern appointments and comfortable seating.
The hotel offered luxurious appointments in each room, including fine linens and custom furniture.
The yacht's elegant appointments impressed all the guests on board.
