pathogenically

adverb
in a disease-causing manner
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˌpæθəˈdʒɛnɪkli / (AmE), / ˌpæθəˈdʒɛnɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that causes or is capable of causing disease.

Examples

Act pathogenically
Certain bacteria act pathogenically, leading to serious infections in humans.
Some microorganisms can behave pathogenically under specific environmental conditions.
The virus spread pathogenically throughout the population, overwhelming the healthcare system.
