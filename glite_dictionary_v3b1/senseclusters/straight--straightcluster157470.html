straight

adjective
having no waves or curls (hair)
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /streɪt/ (AmE), /streɪt/ (BrE)
not curved, bent, or angled; in a direct line or position (used for lines, objects, posture, hair, teeth, rope, etc.)

Examples

Straight hair
After using the flat iron, her once wavy hair became perfectly straight.
He prefers to keep his hair straight because it's easier to manage.
She always wished her hair was straight like her sister's, instead of curly.
Draw a straight line from the top to the bottom of the page.
Her hair is naturally straight, without any waves or curls.
They decided to take the straight road through the valley.

The carpenter used a ruler to make sure the board was perfectly straight.
