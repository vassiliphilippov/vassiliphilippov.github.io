Dmitri Mendeleev

proper noun
Russian chemist; developer of the periodic table
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪmtri ˌmɛnˈdɛl.iˌɛf / (AmE), / ˈdɪmtri ˌmɛnˈdɛl.iːˌɛf / (BrE)
(1834–1907) Russian chemist who developed the periodic table of chemical elements and predicted the discovery of several new elements.

Examples

Dmitri Mendeleev periodic table
Dmitri Mendeleev is best known for his creation of the first widely recognized periodic table of elements.
In chemistry class, we learned how Dmitri Mendeleev predicted elements that had not yet been discovered.
Scientists still honor Dmitri Mendeleev for his contributions to the understanding of atomic structure.
