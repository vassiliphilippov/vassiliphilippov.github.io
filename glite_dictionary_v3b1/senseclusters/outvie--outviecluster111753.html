outvie

verb
surpass in rivalry or competition
Pronunciation: / aʊtˈvaɪ / (AmE), / aʊtˈvaɪ / (BrE)
to surpass or outdo others in competition or rivalry; to strive to be more successful than someone else

Examples

To outvie a rival
At the art exhibit, the painters seemed determined to outvie one another in creativity and originality.
During the holiday season, neighbors often outvie each other in decorating their homes.
The two companies tried to outvie each other with increasingly ambitious advertising campaigns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compete - to strive to gain or win something by defeating rivals
  • synonym: surpass - to be greater than or better than; exceed
