ipil

noun
tropical hardwood tree
Pronunciation: / ˈiːpɪl / (AmE), / ˈiːpɪl / (BrE)
a large tropical hardwood tree (Intsia bijuga) valued for its dense, durable timber used in construction and boatbuilding

Examples

Ipil wood plank
The carpenter selected a plank of dense ipil for the boat's keel because it resists rot.
Many traditional houses in the region are built using strong ipil wood for their foundations.
Because of its durability, ipil is often chosen for outdoor construction projects in tropical climates.
