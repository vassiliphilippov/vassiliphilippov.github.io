cocoa

noun
powder from roasted cacao beans for drinks or baking
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊ.koʊ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.kəʊ / (BrE)
a powder made from ground roasted cacao beans with most of the fat removed, used in making chocolate and drinks

Examples

Cocoa powder
A cup of hot cocoa is perfect on a cold winter night.
She added two tablespoons of cocoa to the cake mixture for a rich chocolate flavor.
The recipe calls for unsweetened cocoa instead of chocolate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cacao - the tropical tree and its seeds from which cocoa is made
