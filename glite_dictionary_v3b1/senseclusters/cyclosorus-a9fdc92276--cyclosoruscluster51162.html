Cyclosorus

noun
genus of tropical and subtropical ferns
9.9
Pronunciation: / saɪˈkloʊsərəs / (AmE), / sɪˈklɒsərəs / (BrE)
A small genus of terrestrial ferns found in tropical and subtropical regions of the Southern Hemisphere.

Examples

The Cyclosorus genus is distinguished by its circular arrangement of sori on the leaves.
