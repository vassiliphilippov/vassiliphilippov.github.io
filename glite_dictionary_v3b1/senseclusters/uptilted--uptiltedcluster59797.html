uptilted

adjective
angled upward
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌtɪltɪd / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌtɪltɪd / (BrE)
angled or turned upward from a horizontal position

Examples

An uptilted beak
The bird had an uptilted beak that made it look curious.
She admired the uptilted brim of his hat as he walked by.
The painting showed a face with an uptilted chin, full of confidence.

Related Terms

  • antonym: downturned - turned or directed downward
  • synonym: tilted - slanted or inclined from a straight position
  • synonym: upturned - turned or directed upward
