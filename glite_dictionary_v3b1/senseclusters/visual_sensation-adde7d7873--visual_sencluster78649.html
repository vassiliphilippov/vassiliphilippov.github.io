visual sensation

noun
the experience of seeing
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪʒuəl sɛnˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒuəl sɛnˈseɪʃən / (BrE)
the experience or perception of seeing something, produced by the stimulation of the eyes

Examples

Bright visual sensation
After staring at the sun, she experienced a temporary visual sensation of bright spots.
Some medications can alter your visual sensation, causing things to appear distorted.
The sudden flash produced a strong visual sensation in everyone present.
