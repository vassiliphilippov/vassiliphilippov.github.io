mill-girl

noun
girl working in a mill
Pronunciation: / mɪl ɡɜrl / (AmE), / mɪl ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a girl or young woman who works in a mill, especially a textile mill

Examples

A mill-girl worker
In 1845, the Lowell mill-girl wrote letters describing her daily routine.
Many mill-girls organized strikes to demand better working conditions.
The play tells the story of a young mill-girl dreaming of a different life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: millworker - a person who works in a mill
