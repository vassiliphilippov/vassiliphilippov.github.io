unworkably

adverb
impractically
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɜrkəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɜːkəbli / (BrE)
in a way that is not practical or cannot function properly

Examples

An unworkably slow system
The new software was unworkably slow on older computers, making it difficult to use.
Their proposal was unworkably complex for such a tight deadline.
The rules were unworkably strict, so very few people could participate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impractically - in a way that is not practical
  • antonym: workably - in a way that can function properly
