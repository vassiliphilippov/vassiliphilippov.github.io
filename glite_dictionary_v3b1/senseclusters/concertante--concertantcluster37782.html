concertante

adjective
with soloists and orchestra
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑnsərˈtɑnteɪ / (AmE), / ˌkɒnsəˈtænteɪ / (BrE)

Mozart wrote several concertante works that highlight both the orchestra and individual instruments.
