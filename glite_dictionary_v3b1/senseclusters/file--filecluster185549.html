file

noun
tool for smoothing or shaping surfaces
Pronunciation: / faɪl / (AmE), / faɪl / (BrE)
a hand tool with a roughened surface of small, sharp teeth, used to smooth or shape wood, metal, or other materials

Examples

A metal file
After shaping the metal, she finished the job with a file for a smooth finish.
Always clean your file after use to maintain its performance.
The carpenter used a file to smooth the rough edges of the wood.

Related Terms
