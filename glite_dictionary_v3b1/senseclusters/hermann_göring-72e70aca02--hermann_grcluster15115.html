Hermann Göring

proper noun
German Nazi politician and military leader
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɛrˌmɑn ˈɡɜrɪŋ / (AmE), / hɛəˈman ˈɡɜːrɪŋ / (BrE)
(1893–1946) A German politician and military leader in Nazi Germany, founder of the Gestapo, and a leading figure in mobilizing Germany for war.

Examples

Hermann Göring Nazi leader
Hermann Göring was a prominent figure in Nazi Germany, often seen alongside Adolf Hitler.
After the war, Hermann Göring was tried at the Nuremberg Trials for his role in war crimes.
The founding of the Gestapo is attributed to Hermann Göring, who consolidated secret police power in Nazi Germany.
