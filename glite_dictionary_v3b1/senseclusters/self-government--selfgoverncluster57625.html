self-government

noun
the right or system of governing oneself
4.2 3.5
Pronunciation: /self ˈgʌv.ərn.mənt/ /; / /səlf ˈgʌv.ərn.mənt/ (AmE), /self ˈgʌv.ən.mənt/ /; / /səlf ˈgʌv.ən.mənt/ (BrE)
The system or principle by which a group, community, or nation governs itself, especially without external control.

Examples

Local self-government
A strong tradition of self-government developed in the small community.
Many former colonies gained self-government in the twentieth century.
The region demanded self-government after years of being ruled by a distant authority.

  • antonym: colonial rule - government by a foreign power
  • synonym: independence - the condition of having no dependence on another
