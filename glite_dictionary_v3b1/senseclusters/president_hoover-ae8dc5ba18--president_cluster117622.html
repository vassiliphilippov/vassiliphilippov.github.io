President Hoover

proper noun
31st U.S. President (1929–1933)
Pronunciation: / ˌprɛzɪdənt ˈhuːvɚ / (AmE), / ˌprɛzɪdənt ˈhuːvə / (BrE)
the 31st President of the United States (1929–1933), under whose administration the stock market crashed and the Great Depression began
