unacademic

adjective
not scholarly
6.9
Pronunciation: /ʌn.æk.ə.ˈdem.ɪk/ /; / /ə.næk.ə.ˈdem.ɪk/ (AmE), /ʌn.æk.ə.ˈdem.ɪk/ /; / /ə.næk.ə.ˈdem.ɪk/ (BrE)
not following the standards, methods, or style expected in academic or scholarly work

Examples

Unacademic writing style
His writing style is considered unacademic because it lacks proper citations and structure.
The report was criticized for its unacademic tone and lack of evidence-based arguments.
She preferred a more unacademic approach, using stories instead of formal analysis.
