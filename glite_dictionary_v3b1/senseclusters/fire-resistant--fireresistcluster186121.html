fire-resistant

adjective
able to resist burning
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚ rɪˌzɪstənt / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə rɪˌzɪstənt / (BrE)
not easily damaged or destroyed by fire; able to resist burning to a significant degree.

Examples

Fire-resistant materials
All doors in the building are made from fire-resistant materials.
Firefighters wear fire-resistant suits for safety during emergencies.
The fire-resistant coating helps protect the structure during a blaze.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fireproof - not able to be burned or destroyed by fire
  • antonym: flammable - easily set on fire
