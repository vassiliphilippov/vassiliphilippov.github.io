Cabot

proper noun
Italian explorer who reached North America, 1497
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkæ.bət/ (AmE), /ˈkæ.bət/ (BrE)
The surname of John Cabot (c. 1450–1498), an Italian explorer who led the English expedition in 1497 that discovered parts of mainland North America.

Examples

John Cabot explorer
John Cabot sailed under the English flag, although he was originally from Italy.
Many historians believe that Cabot's 1497 voyage marked the first European landing on mainland North America since the Vikings.
The legacy of Cabot is still celebrated in parts of eastern Canada today.
