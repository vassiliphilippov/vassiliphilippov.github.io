slashy

adjective
full of slashing
Pronunciation: / ˈslæʃi / (AmE), / ˈslæʃi / (BrE)
involving or characterized by slashing, cutting, or violent attacks with sharp objects.

Examples

Slashy horror scenes
The movie was so slashy that many viewers had to look away during the violent scenes.
Critics described the novel as slashy due to its graphic descriptions of sword fights.
He avoided slashy video games because he did not like scenes with excessive cutting or blood.
