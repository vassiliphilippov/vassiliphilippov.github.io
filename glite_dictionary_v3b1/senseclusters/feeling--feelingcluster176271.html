feeling

noun
intuitive understanding of something
1.8 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfilɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
an intuitive or instinctive understanding, impression, or opinion about something

Examples

A feeling about it
His strong feeling for painting is evident in his work.
She has a natural feeling for languages and picks up new words easily.

I have a feeling that we will hear from her soon, even though she hasn't called.
She couldn't explain her feeling, but she trusted her instincts.
