suiting

noun
fabric for suits
Pronunciation: / ˈsuː.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsuː.tɪŋ / (BrE)
Fabric, typically of wool or a wool blend, used for making suits.

Examples

Suiting fabric
The tailor showed me several varieties of suiting before I chose a navy pinstripe.
They manufacture high-quality suiting for designers worldwide.
Wool suiting is popular for business wear because of its durability and appearance.
