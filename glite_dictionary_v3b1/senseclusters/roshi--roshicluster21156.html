roshi

noun
Zen Buddhist teacher
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊʃi / (AmE), / ˈrəʊʃi / (BrE)
a title given to a respected Zen Buddhist teacher or master

Examples

Zen roshi teacher
The roshi offered guidance to students during the meditation retreat.
After years of study, she was recognized as a roshi by her Zen community.
Many people traveled from afar to hear the teachings of the renowned roshi.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guru - a spiritual teacher or guide, especially in Hinduism
  • synonym: sensei - a teacher or instructor, especially in Japanese martial arts or Buddhism
