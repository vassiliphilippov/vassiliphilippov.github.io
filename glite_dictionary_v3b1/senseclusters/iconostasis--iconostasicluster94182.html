iconostasis

noun
church icon screen
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪkəˈnɑstəsɪs / (AmE), / ˌaɪkəˈnɒstəsɪs / (BrE)
a screen or partition decorated with icons, separating the altar from the nave in Eastern Orthodox churches

Examples

The iconostasis screen
The golden iconostasis was covered with beautiful religious paintings.
Visitors admired the intricate carvings on the iconostasis in the old cathedral.
During the service, the priest passed through the doors of the iconostasis to reach the altar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: altar screen - a decorative screen separating the altar from the rest of a church
