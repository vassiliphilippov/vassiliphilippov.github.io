Dermochelys coriacea

noun
leatherback sea turtle (scientific name)
Pronunciation: / dɜːr.moʊˈkiːlɪs kɔːr.iˈeɪ.ʃə / (AmE), / dɜː.məʊˈkiːlɪs kɒr.iˈeɪ.siə / (BrE)
the scientific name for the leatherback sea turtle, a large marine turtle with a flexible, leathery shell and the largest living turtle species
