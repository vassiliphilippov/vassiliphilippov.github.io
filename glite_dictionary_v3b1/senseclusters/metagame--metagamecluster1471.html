metagame

noun
using outside knowledge
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtəˌɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈmɛtəˌɡeɪm / (BrE)
the use or set of strategies, knowledge, or trends from outside the basic rules of a game that influence how it is played

Examples

The metagame strategy
Some players use the metagame to gain an advantage by predicting their opponents' moves.
In role-playing games, relying too much on the metagame can break the immersion for everyone.
The tournament rules discourage metagame tactics that exploit knowledge from previous matches.
Understanding the current metagame is essential for players who want to compete at a high level.
The metagame shifted after new rules were introduced, changing which strategies were most effective.
Many online forums discuss the evolving metagame to help players improve their performance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: roleplay - to act out the part of a character in a game or scenario
  • synonym: strategy - a plan of action designed to achieve a goal in a game or competition
  • synonym: tactic - a specific action or method used to achieve a short-term goal in a game
