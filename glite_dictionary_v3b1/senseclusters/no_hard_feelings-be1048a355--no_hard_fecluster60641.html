no hard feelings

x
no anger or resentment after a disagreement
2.0
Pronunciation: / noʊ hɑrd ˈfiːlɪŋz / (AmE), / nəʊ hɑːd ˈfiːlɪŋz / (BrE)
used to say that you are not angry or resentful about something, especially after a disagreement or argument

Examples

No hard feelings after argument
After their heated discussion, Sarah assured Tom that there were no hard feelings.
He apologized for the mistake, and I told him no hard feelings.
They parted ways professionally, each insisting there were no hard feelings.
