big oil

noun
large petroleum companies collectively
1.0
Pronunciation: / bɪɡ ɔɪl / (AmE), / bɪɡ ɔɪl / (BrE)
The major companies of the petroleum industry, especially regarded collectively as powerful or influential.

Examples

Big oil companies
Environmental groups are pressuring big oil to invest in renewable energy.
Lawsuits against big oil have increased in recent years over climate change liability.
The government faced criticism for its close ties to big oil.
