  • reflection factor
    7.4 0.00/m
    reflection factor is the fraction of incoming radiant energy reflected from a surface, while reflectance is the proportion named as a percentage or ratio.
  • reflectivity
    6.1 0.4/m
    reflectivity is the ability of a material to reflect light, usually shown as a fraction or percentage, while reflectance is the measured proportion of incident radiation.
  • reflective power
    6.0 0.00/m
    reflective power is the ability of a surface to reflect light measured as the ratio of reflected to incident light, while reflectance refers to that proportion.
  • albedo
    9.9 0.9/m
