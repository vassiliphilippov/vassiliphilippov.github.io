the proportion of incident light or other radiation that is reflected by a surface, typically expressed as a percentage or ratio

Examples

High reflectance surfaces
High reflectance surfaces are used to improve lighting efficiency in buildings.
Researchers measured the reflectance of different materials to determine which was most suitable for solar panels.
The reflectance of the snow made the landscape appear extremely bright.

Synonyms

  • coefficient of reflection
    8.2 0.00/m
    coefficient of reflection is the fraction of incoming light or radiation reflected by a surface, while reflectance is the same proportion shown as a percentage or ratio.
  • reflection factor
    7.4 0.00/m
    reflection factor is the fraction of incoming radiant energy reflected from a surface, while reflectance is the proportion named as a percentage or ratio.
  • reflectivity
    6.1 0.47/m
    reflectivity is the ability of a material to reflect light, usually shown as a fraction or percentage, while reflectance is the measured proportion of incident radiation.
  • reflective power
    6.0 0.00/m
    reflective power is the ability of a surface to reflect light measured as the ratio of reflected to incident light, while reflectance refers to that proportion.
  • albedo
    9.9 1.09/m
    albedo is the proportion of incident light reflected by a planet or moon surface, while reflectance is the general term for any surface's reflected fraction.
