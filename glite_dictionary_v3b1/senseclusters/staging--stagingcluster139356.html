staging

noun
system of scaffolds; temporary support structure
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsteɪdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
a temporary structure of scaffolds used to support people and materials during the construction or repair of a building or in theatrical productions

Examples

Building staging
Proper safety measures are required when erecting staging on a construction site.
The lighting crew positioned themselves on the staging high above the stage.
The workers assembled the staging before beginning repairs on the facade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scaffolding - a temporary structure on the outside of a building used by workers while building, repairing, or cleaning
