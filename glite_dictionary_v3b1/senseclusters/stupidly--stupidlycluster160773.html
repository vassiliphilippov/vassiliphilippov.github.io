stupidly

adverb
in a foolish or unintelligent manner
Pronunciation: / ˈstuːpɪdli / (AmE), / ˈstjuːpɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of intelligence or good judgment.

Examples

He stupidly made a mistake
He stupidly forgot to lock the door before leaving the house.
She stupidly ignored the warning signs on the mountain trail.
They stupidly spent all their savings on a risky investment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foolishly - in a foolish or unwise manner
  • antonym: wisely - in a way that shows experience, knowledge, or good judgment
