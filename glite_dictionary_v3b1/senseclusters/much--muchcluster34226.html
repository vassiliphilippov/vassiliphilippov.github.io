Synonyms

  • a lot
    1.0 625/m
    a lot is an informal phrase used in speech to mean very or many, while much is more general.
  • a great deal
    1.8 19/m
    a great deal expresses a large amount in both speech and writing, while much works in many formal and informal contexts.
  • considerably
    3.4 18/m
    considerably is used for significant, measurable change often in formal contexts, while much is a broader adverb of degree.
  • greatly
    1.0 35/m
    greatly often shows strong effect or feeling in formal speech, while much is more general and fits many situations.
  • often
    1.0 476/m
    often means frequently, referring to time or repetition, while much mainly describes degree or large amount.
