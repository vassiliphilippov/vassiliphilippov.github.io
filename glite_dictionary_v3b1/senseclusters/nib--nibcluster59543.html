nib

noun
horny beak tip of a bird
6.4
Pronunciation: / nɪb / (AmE), / nɪb / (BrE)
the hard, projecting tip of a bird's beak, especially the horny part

Examples

Bird's nib
Some birds have a specially adapted nib for cracking seeds.
The hawk used its nib to tear into its prey.
The ornithologist examined the distinct curve of the eagle's nib.
