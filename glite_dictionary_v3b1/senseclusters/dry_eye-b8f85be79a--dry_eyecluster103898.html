dry eye

noun
insufficient moisture in the eyes
Pronunciation: / draɪ aɪ / (AmE), / draɪ aɪ / (BrE)
a condition in which the eyes do not produce enough tears or moisture, leading to irritation or discomfort

Examples

Symptoms of dry eye
After spending hours reading, she noticed her dry eye symptoms getting worse.
People who wear contact lenses often complain about dry eye.
Windy weather can make dry eye much more uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dry eye syndrome - the chronic condition of dry eye, sometimes used interchangeably
