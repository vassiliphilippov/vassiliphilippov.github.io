dry eye

noun
insufficient moisture in the eyes
6.7 3.3
Pronunciation: /draɪ aɪ/ /; / /dʒraɪ aɪ/ (AmE), /draɪ aɪ/ /; / /dʒraɪ aɪ/ (BrE)
