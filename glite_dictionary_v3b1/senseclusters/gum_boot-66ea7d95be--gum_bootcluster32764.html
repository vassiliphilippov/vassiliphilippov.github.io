gum boot

noun
high rubber waterproof boot
3.2 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / …° åm buÀêt / (AmE), / …° åm buÀêt / (BrE)
A high waterproof boot made of rubber, typically used for protection against wet or muddy conditions.

Examples

A pair of gum boots
After the storm, the children splashed in puddles with their gum boots on.
Farmers often wear gum boots to keep their feet dry while working.
She pulled on her gum boots before heading out into the rainy field.
