salutary

adjective
beneficial to health; promoting well-being
Pronunciation: / ˈsæljəˌteri /; / ˈsæljəˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˈsæl.jʊ.tər.i /; / ˈsæljʊtəri / (BrE)
having a beneficial or helpful effect, especially for health or after something unpleasant
