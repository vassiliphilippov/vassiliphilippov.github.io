biddy

noun
gossipy old woman
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈbɪd.iː/ /; / /ˈbɪd.ɪ/ (AmE), /ˈbɪd.ɪ/ /; / /ˈbɪd.iː/ (BrE)
an old woman, especially one who is gossipy or meddlesome (informal, sometimes disparaging or humorous)

Examples

A nosy biddy
The neighbors called her a biddy because she always watched the street.
Some people thought the old biddy next door was too nosy for her own good.
He laughed and said he did not want to become a grumpy biddy in his old age.
The nosy biddy next door always comments on everyone's business.
A group of biddies gathered at the park to gossip about the neighbors.
He laughed and called his grandmother a biddy, but she didn't mind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crone - an old woman, often portrayed as unpleasant or ugly
  • antonym: gentlewoman - a woman of good breeding and manners
  • synonym: gossip - a person who enjoys talking about others' private lives
