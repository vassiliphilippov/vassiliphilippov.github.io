unplowed

adjective
not plowed (of fields or land)
3.0 5.0
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈplaʊd/ /; / /ən.ˈplaʊd/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈplaʊd/ /; / /ən.ˈplaʊd/ (BrE)
(of land or fields) not having been plowed or turned over with a plow.

Examples

Unplowed field
Heavy snowfall made many rural roads unplowed and difficult to drive on.
The unplowed fields stretched for miles beyond the village.
They decided to leave a portion of the land unplowed for wildlife habitats.

Local wildlife found shelter among the unploughed hedgerows bordering the property.
The farmer left several acres of unploughed land for wildflowers to grow naturally.
