defund

verb
to withdraw funding
5.9
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈfʌnd/ /; / /diː.ˈfʌnd/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈfʌnd/ /; / /diː.ˈfʌnd/ (BrE)
to reduce or stop giving money to support an organization, program, or activity

Examples
