nonscientific

adjective
not scientific
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.saɪənˈtɪfɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.saɪənˈtɪfɪk / (BrE)
not based on or related to science, scientific methods, or evidence

Examples

A nonscientific explanation
The committee rejected the nonscientific explanation because it lacked evidence.
Many nonscientific beliefs persist despite advances in research.
He warned against making nonscientific assumptions in the report.
Her explanation was dismissed as nonscientific because it relied on personal beliefs rather than evidence.
The museum offers both scientific and nonscientific exhibits to appeal to a wide audience.
Many nonscientific approaches, such as meditation, are used to improve well-being.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unscientific - not using or based on scientific methods
