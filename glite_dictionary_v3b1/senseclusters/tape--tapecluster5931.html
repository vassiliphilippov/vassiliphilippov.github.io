tape

noun
measuring instrument; flexible marked strip
1.7
Pronunciation: / teɪp / (AmE), / teɪp / (BrE)
A flexible strip of cloth, plastic, or metal marked with units and used for measuring length.

Examples

Tape measure
Please hand me the tape so I can measure the width of this shelf.
She always keeps a small tape in her purse for quick measurements.
The tailor pulled out his tape to check the length of the fabric.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tape measure - a flexible ruler used to measure length, marked in units
