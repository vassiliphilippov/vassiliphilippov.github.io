Chamorro

adjective
of the Chamorro people
Pronunciation: / tʃəˈmɔːroʊ / (AmE), / tʃəˈmɒrəʊ / (BrE)
relating to the Chamorro people, their language, culture, or traditions, especially of the Mariana Islands

Examples

Chamorro culture
The museum features a collection of Chamorro artifacts from the Mariana Islands.

Many Chamorro festivals include music, dance, and storytelling passed down through generations.
