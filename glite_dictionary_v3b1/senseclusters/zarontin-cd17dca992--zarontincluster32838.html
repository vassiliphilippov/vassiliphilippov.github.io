Zarontin

noun
anticonvulsant drug for epilepsy
9.9
Pronunciation: / zəˈrɑn.tɪn / (AmE), / zəˈrɒn.tɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked anticonvulsant drug used to treat petit mal epilepsy.

Examples

After starting Zarontin, his episodes of petit mal epilepsy became much less frequent.
Patients using Zarontin should follow their neurologist's dosage instructions carefully.
The doctor prescribed Zarontin to control her absence seizures.
