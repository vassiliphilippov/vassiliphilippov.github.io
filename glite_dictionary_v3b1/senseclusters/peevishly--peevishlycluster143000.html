peevishly

adverb
in an irritable or petulant manner
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpivɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈpiːvɪʃli / (BrE)
in a way that shows irritation or bad temper; in an irritable or petulant manner

Examples

Peevishly answered
He answered peevishly when asked about his unfinished work.
She peevishly complained about the noise coming from next door.
The child peevishly refused to eat his vegetables.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmly - in a relaxed or tranquil manner
  • synonym: irritably - in a manner showing annoyance or impatience
