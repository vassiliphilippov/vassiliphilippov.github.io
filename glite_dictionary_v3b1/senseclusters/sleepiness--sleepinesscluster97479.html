sleepiness

noun
state of feeling sleepy
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsliːpinəs / (AmE), / ˈsliːpinɪs / (BrE)
The condition of feeling ready to sleep or having a strong desire to sleep.

Examples

Sleepiness after lunch
Sleepiness often hits me after lunch, making it hard to focus on work.
Excessive sleepiness can be a symptom of certain medical conditions.
He fought off his sleepiness during the long meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alertness - the state of being attentive and watchful
  • synonym: drowsiness - feeling of being sleepy or lethargic
