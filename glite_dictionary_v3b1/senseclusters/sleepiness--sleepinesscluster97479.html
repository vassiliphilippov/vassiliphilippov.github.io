sleepiness

noun
state of feeling sleepy
1.0 1.84/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàsliÀêpin…ôs / (AmE), / ÀàsliÀêpin…™s / (BrE)
The condition of feeling ready to sleep or having a strong desire to sleep.

Examples

Sleepiness after lunch
Sleepiness often hits me after lunch, making it hard to focus on work.
Excessive sleepiness can be a symptom of certain medical conditions.
He fought off his sleepiness during the long meeting.

Synonyms

  • drowsiness
    1.8 0.68/m
    drowsiness is a common, mild feeling of wanting to sleep, while sleepiness names the general condition of readiness to sleep.
  • somnolence
    9.3 0.18/m
    somnolence is a medical or formal word for strong drowsiness, while sleepiness is the everyday feeling of wanting to sleep.
