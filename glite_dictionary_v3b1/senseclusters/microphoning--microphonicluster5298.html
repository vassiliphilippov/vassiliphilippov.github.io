microphoning

noun
process of converting sound to electrical signals
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪ.kroʊˌfoʊ.nɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪ.krəʊˌfəʊ.nɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of converting sound waves into electrical signals using a microphone

Examples

Microphoning technology
Advances in microphoning technology have improved the quality of live broadcasts.
Proper microphoning ensures clear and accurate audio recording during interviews.
The microphoning of the concert was handled by a team of experienced sound engineers.
