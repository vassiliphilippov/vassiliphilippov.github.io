orangutan

noun
large arboreal ape of Borneo and Sumatra
Pronunciation: / oʊˌræŋ.uːˈtæn /; / əˈræŋəˌtæn / (AmE), / ɒˌræŋ.uːˈtæn /; / ɒˈræŋʊtæn / (BrE)
a large, long-armed ape with reddish-brown hair that lives in the rainforests of Borneo and Sumatra
