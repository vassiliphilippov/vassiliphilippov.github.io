genu varum

noun
outward bowing of the legs at the knee
7.1 8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒiː.njuː ˈvɑːr.əm/ /; / /ˈdʒɛ.njuː ˈvɑːr.əm/ (AmE), /ˈdʒɛ.njuː ˈvɑː.rəm/ /; / /ˈdʒiː.njuː ˈvɑː.rəm/ (BrE)
a condition in which the legs bow outward at or below the knees; commonly known as bowleggedness

Examples

Genu varum condition
Genu varum can sometimes correct itself in young children as they grow older.
Surgery may be required in severe cases of genu varum to straighten the legs.
The doctor diagnosed the child with genu varum after noting the outward curve of his legs.
