Book of Nahum

proper noun
Old Testament book; Nahum's prophecy
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊk əv ˈneɪhəm / (AmE), / ˈbʊk əv ˈneɪhəm / (BrE)
A book of the Old Testament in the Bible, containing the prophecies of Nahum concerning the downfall of Nineveh.

Examples

The Book of Nahum
In our Bible study group, we discussed the themes of justice and vengeance found in the Book of Nahum.
Scholars often debate the historical accuracy of the events described in the Book of Nahum.
The Book of Nahum vividly describes the impending destruction of the city of Nineveh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Nahum - the prophet whose oracles are recorded in the Book of Nahum
  • synonym: Nineveh - ancient Assyrian city prophesied to fall in the Book of Nahum
