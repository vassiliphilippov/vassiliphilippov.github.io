oat

noun
seed of the grass Avena sativa
Pronunciation: / oʊt / (AmE), / əʊt / (BrE)
a tall grass (Avena sativa) grown for its seeds, which are used as food for people and animals

Examples

Oats for breakfast
Oats are a popular ingredient in breakfast cereals and oatmeal.
Farmers often plant oats as a nutritious crop for their livestock.
She bought a bag of oats to make homemade granola.
Farmers rotate oat crops with other grains to maintain soil health.
Many breakfast cereals contain oat as their main ingredient.
Whole-grain oat is considered a healthy dietary choice.
