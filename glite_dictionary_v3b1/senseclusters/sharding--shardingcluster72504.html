sharding

noun
breaking into shards
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑrdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of breaking something into sharp fragments or shards

Examples

Glass sharding
The sharding of the glass left dangerous pieces all over the kitchen floor.
After the impact, the sharding of the windshield made it difficult to see outside.
Careless handling resulted in the sharding of the ceramic plate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fragmentation - the process of breaking into smaller pieces
  • antonym: joining - the act of bringing things together
  • synonym: splintering - the act of breaking into thin, sharp pieces
