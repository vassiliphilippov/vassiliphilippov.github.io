raptorial

adjective
relating to birds of prey
4.0
Pronunciation: / ræpˈtɔriəl / (AmE), / ræpˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
relating to animals (especially birds) that hunt and feed on other animals; predatory

Examples

Raptorial bird claws
Eagles have strong, raptorial bills for tearing flesh.
Scientists studied the raptorial habits of hawks in the region.
The owl's raptorial talons are specially adapted for catching small mammals.
Many raptorial birds have sharp talons for catching their prey.
Scientists observed the raptorial behavior of hawks in their natural habitat.
The owl is a raptorial species that hunts small mammals at night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: herbivorous - feeding on plants, not animals
  • synonym: predatory - relating to or characterized by predation, especially animals that prey on others
