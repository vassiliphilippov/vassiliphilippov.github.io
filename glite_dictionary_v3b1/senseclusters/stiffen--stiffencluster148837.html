stiffen

verb
make or become stiff
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪfən / (AmE), / ˈstɪfən / (BrE)
to make or become physically stiff or less flexible

Examples

To stiffen the cream
If you stiffen the cream with gelatin, it will hold its shape better.
The child's posture stiffened as she heard the sudden noise behind her.
You can stiffen fabric by soaking it in a starch solution.
His back would stiffen with pain after a long day of work.
She tried to stiffen her resolve before making the announcement.
The mixture will stiffen as it cools, so stir constantly.

  • antonym: relax - to make or become less tense or stiff
