steamroller

verb
proceed forcefully, ignore opposition
7.6 5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈstiːm.roʊ.lər/ (AmE), /ˈstiːm.rəʊ.lə/ (BrE)
to forcefully overcome, defeat, or make something happen by using overwhelming power or pressure, often ignoring opposition
