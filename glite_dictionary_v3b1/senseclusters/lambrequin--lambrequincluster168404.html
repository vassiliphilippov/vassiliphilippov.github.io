lambrequin

noun
decorative drapery for window or shelf
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlæm.brə.kɪn / (AmE), / ˈlam.brɪ.kwɪn / (BrE)
a short decorative drapery or hanging, often along the upper part of a window or shelf edge, used as an ornament or to conceal fittings

Examples

Window lambrequin
Our designer suggested adding a velvet lambrequin above the window for a touch of elegance.
The antique bookshelf was adorned with a lace lambrequin that matched the room’s decor.
The living room curtains were topped with a richly embroidered lambrequin.
