antipyresis

noun
reduction or prevention of fever
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.ti.paɪˈriː.sɪs / (AmE), / ˌanti.paɪˈriː.sɪs / (BrE)
the reduction or prevention of fever, especially by the use of antipyretic medication

Examples

Physical antipyresis
Physical methods of antipyresis, such as cooling blankets, may sometimes be used in hospitals.
The doctor decided that antipyresis was necessary to prevent complications from the fever.
The patient was given medication for antipyresis to manage his high temperature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antipyretic - a drug used to reduce fever
