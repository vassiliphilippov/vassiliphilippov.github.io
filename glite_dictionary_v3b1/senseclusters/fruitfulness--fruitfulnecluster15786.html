fruitfulness

noun
intellectual productivity; creativity
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfrut.fəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈfruːt.fəl.nəs / (BrE)
the ability to produce many new and original ideas; intellectual productivity or creativity.

Examples

Fruitfulness of ideas
Her fruitfulness as a writer was evident in the dozens of novels she produced over her career.
Teachers try to foster fruitfulness in students by encouraging critical and creative thinking.
The fruitfulness of his imagination led to innovations in both science and art.

Related Terms

  • antonym: barrenness - the lack of productivity or results
  • synonym: creativity - the ability to create new ideas
  • synonym: fertility - the quality of producing abundantly
