outfitting

noun
equipping or preparing a ship
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌfɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌfɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of equipping and preparing a ship, especially by providing it with necessary fittings, gear, and supplies

Examples

Ship outfitting
During outfitting, engineers installed advanced navigation systems and safety gear.
Much of the expense was due to the outfitting required for long voyages.
The outfitting of the new ship took several months before it was ready to sail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fitting out - equipping and preparing a vessel for service
