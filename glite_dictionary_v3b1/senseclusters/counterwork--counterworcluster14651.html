counterwork

noun
work at service counter
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntərˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntəˌwɜːk / (BrE)
work performed at a service counter, especially involving serving customers or handling sales transactions

Examples

Busy counterwork shift
After her promotion, she spent less time on counterwork and more on managing the team.
He gained valuable experience through counterwork at the busy bakery every weekend.
Many students take part-time jobs that involve counterwork in cafes or retail stores.

Related Terms

  • synonym: customer service - assisting and serving customers in a business
  • synonym: sales - the activity of selling goods or services
