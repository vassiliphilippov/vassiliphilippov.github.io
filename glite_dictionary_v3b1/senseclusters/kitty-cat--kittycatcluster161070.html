kitty-cat

noun
informal term for a domestic cat
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪtiˌkæt /; / ˈkɪti ˌkæt / (AmE), / ˈkɪtiˌkat /; / ˈkɪti ˌkæt / (BrE)
an informal or affectionate word for a domestic cat, especially used by or when speaking to children

Examples

He adopted a stray kitty-cat and gave it a cozy home.

Every morning, the kitty-cat sits by the window and watches the birds.
She bought a new toy for her little kitty-cat to play with.
The children were delighted when they found a stray kitty-cat in the garden.
