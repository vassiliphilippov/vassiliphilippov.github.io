chestful

noun
lungful of air
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛstfʊl / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛstfʊl / (BrE)
an amount of air that fills the lungs when taking a deep breath

Examples

A chestful of air
He took a deep chestful of fresh mountain air before starting the hike.
After swimming to the surface, she gasped for a chestful of oxygen.
The runner paused to catch a chestful of air before continuing the race.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breath - the air taken into or expelled from the lungs
  • synonym: lungful - an amount of air that fills the lungs
