paranoidal

adjective
extremely suspicious
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈnɔɪdəl / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈnɔɪdəl / (BrE)
relating to or resembling paranoia, especially showing extreme suspicion or distrust of others

Examples

Paranoidal behavior
Her paranoidal behavior made it difficult for her to form close friendships.
The novel describes a paranoidal character who suspects everyone of plotting against him.
After the incident, he became increasingly paranoidal and avoided social gatherings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paranoid - having or showing unreasonable fear or distrust
  • antonym: trusting - showing belief in the honesty or reliability of others
