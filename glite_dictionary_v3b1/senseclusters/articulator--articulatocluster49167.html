articulator

noun
movable organ for speech production

Examples

Tongue articulator

Linguists study how each articulator contributes to the formation of different consonants and vowels.

Synonyms

  • speech organ
    4.1 0.00/m
    speech organ is any structure used to make speech sounds, often the tongue or lips inside the mouth, while articulator is the movable mouth organ that shapes sounds.
  • vocal organ
    3.8 0.02/m
    vocal organ includes voice sources like the larynx and also mouth parts used for sound, while articulator is the movable mouth organ.
  • organ of speech
    4.4 0.01/m
    organ of speech names any body part that helps produce voice, including vocal cords and larynx, while articulator is the movable mouth organ.
