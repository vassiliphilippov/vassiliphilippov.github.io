articulator

noun
movable organ for speech production
2.5 5.9
Pronunciation: /ɑːr.ˈtɪ.kjə.leɪ.tər/ /; / /ə.ˈtɪ.kjə.leɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ɑː.ˈtɪ.kjə.leɪ.tə/ /; / /ə.ˈtɪ.kjə.leɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a movable organ in the mouth, such as the tongue or lips, used in producing speech sounds
