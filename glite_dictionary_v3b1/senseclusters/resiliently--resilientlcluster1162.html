resiliently

adverb
in a flexible way
4.6
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɪliəntli / (AmE), / rɪˈzɪliəntli / (BrE)
in a way that quickly returns to its original shape or condition after being bent, stretched, or compressed

Examples

Rubber bends resiliently
The rubber band snapped back resiliently after being stretched to its limit.
Despite repeated pressure, the material responded resiliently and maintained its shape.
The athlete recovered resiliently from injury and returned to competition within weeks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elastically - in a way that stretches and returns to original shape
  • antonym: rigidly - in a stiff or inflexible way
