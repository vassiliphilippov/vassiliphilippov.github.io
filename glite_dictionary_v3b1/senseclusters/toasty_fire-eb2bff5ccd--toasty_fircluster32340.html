toasty fire

noun
pleasantly warm fire
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊsti ˈfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊsti ˈfaɪə / (BrE)
A fire that gives off a pleasantly warm and cozy feeling.

Examples

A warm toasty fire
After hiking all afternoon, they relaxed by a toasty fire with mugs of hot chocolate.
The mountain cabin was chilly until we lit a toasty fire in the fireplace.
There's nothing better than reading a good book beside a toasty fire on a winter evening.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cold - lacking warmth or heat
  • synonym: roaring fire - a large, blazing fire
