card

noun
baseball list of batters
3.8
Pronunciation: / kɑrd / (AmE), / kɑːd / (BrE)
an official record or list, especially in sports, such as a scorecard or batting order

Examples

A score card
Before the game, the coach filled out the card with the batting order.
If a player is replaced, the changes must be noted on the card.
The umpire collected both teams' cards to review the official lineups.
After finishing the round, each player must sign and submit their card to the tournament official.
If you lose your card, your score won't be accepted for the competition.
She checked her card and realized she had made a mistake on the 12th hole.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lineup - the list of the players set to play in a game, especially in baseball
  • synonym: scorecard - a card used to record scores in a game
