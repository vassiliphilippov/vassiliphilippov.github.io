vitamin E

noun
a fat-soluble antioxidant vitamin
3.4 2.42/m

a fat-soluble vitamin necessary for normal reproductive functions and an important antioxidant that protects cells from damage by free radicals.

Examples

Vitamin E supplements
Doctors may recommend vitamin E supplements to people with certain medical conditions.
Many foods, such as nuts and seeds, are good sources of vitamin E.
Regular intake of vitamin E helps protect the body's cells from oxidative stress.
