misarticulation

noun
incorrect articulation
8.6
Pronunciation: /mɪs.ɑːr.tɪ.ˈkjuː.leɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /mɪs.ɑː.tɪ.ˈkjuː.leɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
an incorrect or inappropriate way of articulating sounds, notes, or phrases, especially in music or speech

Examples

Musical misarticulation
The choir's misarticulation of the lyrics made the song difficult to understand.
Speech therapists often work with children to correct misarticulation of certain sounds.
The pianist's misarticulation affected the clarity of the fast passages in the performance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: articulation - the clear and precise production of sounds or notes
  • synonym: mispronunciation - the act of pronouncing a word incorrectly
