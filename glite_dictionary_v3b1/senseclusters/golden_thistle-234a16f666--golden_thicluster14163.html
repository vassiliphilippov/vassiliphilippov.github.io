golden thistle

noun
spiny Mediterranean herb with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldən ˈθɪsəl / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈθɪsl̩ / (BrE)
any of several spiny Mediterranean herbs of the genus Scolymus with yellow flower heads
