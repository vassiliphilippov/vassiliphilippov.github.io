placekicker

noun
football player who kicks for goals
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪsˌkɪkər / (AmE), / ˈpleɪsˌkɪkə / (BrE)
(in American football) a player who kicks the ball from a stationary position, usually to score a field goal or extra point.

Examples

The placekicker scores
During practice, the placekicker worked on improving his accuracy from long distance.
Every placekicker dreams of kicking a game-winning field goal in a championship game.
The team's placekicker made the winning field goal in the final seconds.
