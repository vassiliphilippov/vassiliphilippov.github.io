loft

noun
pigeon shelter (raised structure)
Pronunciation: / lɑft / (AmE), / lɒft / (BrE)
A raised shelter or structure where pigeons are kept.

Examples

A pigeon loft
A lock was installed on the loft to protect the birds from stray cats.
He built a wooden loft in his backyard to house dozens of pigeons.
The racing pigeons returned to their loft after a long flight.
