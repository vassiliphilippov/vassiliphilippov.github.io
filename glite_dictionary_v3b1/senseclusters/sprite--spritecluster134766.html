sprite

noun
flashes of red light above thunderstorms
2.7 9.9
Pronunciation: / spraɪt / (AmE), / spraɪt / (BrE)
a brief, luminous flash of red or pink light that appears high above thunderstorm clouds, caused by atmospheric electricity, often rising to altitudes up to 60 miles

Examples

A red sprite
Photographs of sprites often show them as bright red flashes far above lightning clouds.
Research aircraft are sometimes used to study sprites and other high-altitude lightning phenomena.
Scientists observed several sprites lighting up the sky above the thunderstorm last night.
