pressure

noun
a stressful or oppressive situation
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈpre.ʃər/ (AmE), /ˈpre.ʃə/ (BrE)
a difficult situation or strong demands that cause stress, urgency, or anxiety

Examples
