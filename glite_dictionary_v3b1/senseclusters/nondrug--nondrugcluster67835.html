nondrug

noun
nonpharmaceutical option
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑnˌdrʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈnɒnˌdrʌɡ / (BrE)
a substance, treatment, or product that is not a pharmaceutical drug, often used as an alternative to medication

Examples

A nondrug option
The doctor suggested a nondrug approach like physical therapy for her back pain.
Many patients prefer nondrug treatments to avoid possible side effects of medication.
Yoga and meditation are popular nondrug options for managing stress.

Related Terms

  • antonym: medication - a substance used for medical treatment, especially a drug
  • synonym: therapy - treatment intended to relieve or heal a disorder
