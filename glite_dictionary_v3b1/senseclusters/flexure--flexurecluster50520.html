flexure

noun
a bend or fold; especially in anatomy
Pronunciation: / ˈflɛk.ʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈflɛk.ʃə / (BrE)
a bend, fold, or curve, especially one made by bending or folding something; in anatomy, a curved part of an organ or structure.

Examples

A metal flexure
A visible flexure appeared in the thin metal rod after repeated stress.
Engineers designed the bridge to accommodate natural flexure caused by temperature changes.
The colon contains a sharp flexure known as the splenic flexure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bend - a curve or angle formed by bending
  • synonym: fold - a crease or plication created by doubling over
