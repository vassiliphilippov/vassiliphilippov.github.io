rattle on

verb
talk at length in a boring way
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈrætl ɑn / (AmE), / ˈrætl ɒn / (BrE)
to talk continuously and often in a tiresome or boring way, usually about trivial matters

Examples

To rattle on endlessly
He was still rattling on about his travels when I finally left.
She tends to rattle on about her weekend plans, even when no one is listening.
The lecturer rattled on for hours without pausing for questions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drone on - to speak continuously in a dull, monotonous manner
  • synonym: ramble - to talk or write at length in a confused or inconsequential way
