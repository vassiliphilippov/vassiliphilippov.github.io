right

noun
politically conservative group or parties
5.1
Pronunciation: / raɪt / (AmE), / raɪt / (BrE)
the group of people or political parties who support conservative or traditional views, especially in politics

Examples

The political right
Debates between the right and the left often dominate political discourse.
Many voters on the right oppose rapid changes to social policies.
The new law was supported mainly by members of the right.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conservative - favoring traditional views and values; tending to oppose change
  • antonym: left - those who support political or social liberalism or progressivism
