bum

adjective
poor-quality; flimsy or unreliable
Pronunciation: / bʌm / (AmE), / bʌm / (BrE)
Of very poor quality; flimsy, unreliable, or unsatisfactory.

Examples

A bum deal
My friend got a bum phone that stopped working after a week.
That was a bum deal; I expected much better service from them.
The movie had a bum script, and the acting was terrible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crummy - of poor quality; inferior
  • antonym: good - of high quality or standard
  • synonym: shoddy - of inferior quality or workmanship
