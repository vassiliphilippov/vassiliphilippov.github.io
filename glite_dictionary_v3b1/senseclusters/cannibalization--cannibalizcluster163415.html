cannibalization

noun
using parts from another
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌkænəbələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkænɪbələˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of taking parts from one machine, vehicle, or device to use in another, often to keep it working.

Examples

Cannibalization of parts
The airline resorted to cannibalization of older planes to keep its fleet operational.
Due to a shortage of spare parts, the team relied on cannibalization to repair the equipment.
Frequent cannibalization of machines can eventually leave some units unusable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: salvage - the act of saving or reusing parts from damaged items
