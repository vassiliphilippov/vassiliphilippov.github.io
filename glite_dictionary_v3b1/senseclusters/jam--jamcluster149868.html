jam

verb
push down forcefully
4.9
Pronunciation: / dʒæm / (AmE), / dʒam / (BrE)
to press or push something down forcefully and suddenly, especially with effort or urgency

Examples

To jam the button
He jammed the clutch pedal to avoid stalling the car.
Mark jammed the lid onto the jar to seal it tightly.
She jammed her finger on the elevator button in a hurry.
