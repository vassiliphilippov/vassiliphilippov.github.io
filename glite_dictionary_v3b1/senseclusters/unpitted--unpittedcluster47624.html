unpitted

adjective
with pit inside
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɪtɪd / (BrE)
not having had the pit or stone removed, especially in fruit such as olives or cherries

Examples

Unpitted olives
She accidentally bit into an unpitted cherry and almost hurt her tooth.
Always check if the olives are unpitted before serving them to children.
The recipe calls for unpitted dates, so be careful when eating them.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pitted - having had the pit or stone removed
