thoracodorsal

adjective
chest and back
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌθɔːrəkəˈdɔːrsəl / (AmE), / ˌθɒrəkəˈdɔːsəl / (BrE)

Examples

Knowledge of thoracodorsal anatomy is essential for certain surgical procedures.
