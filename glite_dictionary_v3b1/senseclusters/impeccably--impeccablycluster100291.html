impeccably

adverb
in a flawless manner
4.7
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɛkəbli / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɛkəbli / (BrE)

Examples

To dress impeccably

She delivered her speech impeccably, with every detail perfectly in place.
The chef's kitchen was impeccably clean after a long night of cooking.
