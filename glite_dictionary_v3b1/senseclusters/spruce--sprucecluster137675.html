spruce

adjective
neat and stylish in appearance
4.8 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / sprus / (AmE), / spruÀês / (BrE)
neat, elegant, and smart in appearance or manner; stylish and up-to-date.

Examples

A spruce outfit
He looked especially spruce in his tailored navy suit at the interview.
She wore a spruce outfit to the luncheon, catching everyone's eye.
The staff at the boutique always appear spruce and welcoming to customers.

Synonyms

  • spiffy
    7.8 0.64/m
    spiffy describes neat, stylish clothes in casual speech, while spruce is a more general word for elegant, up-to-date appearance.
  • natty
    8.1 0.12/m
    natty describes a smart, neatly dressed person often in slightly old-fashioned or British use, while spruce means stylish and up-to-date.
  • snappy
    3.8 0.66/m
    snappy describes bright, stylish clothes or quick-looking outfits in informal speech, while spruce means neat, elegant, and current.
  • dapper
    6.2 0.90/m
    dapper describes a neat, elegant look specifically for men, while spruce can describe any person or thing looking stylish.
