trifluoromethane

noun
a colorless gaseous compound, CHF3
Pronunciation: / traɪˌflʊəroʊˈmiːθeɪn / (AmE), / traɪˌflʊərəˈmiːθeɪn / (BrE)
A colorless, odorless gaseous haloform (CHF3) similar to chloroform, used in refrigeration and as a specialty solvent.

Examples
