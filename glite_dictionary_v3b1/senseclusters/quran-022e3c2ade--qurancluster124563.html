Quran

proper noun
sacred book of Islam
Pronunciation: / kɔːˈrɑːn, kəˈrɑːn / (AmE), / kɔːˈrɑːn, kəˈrɑːn / (BrE)
The sacred book of Islam, believed by Muslims to be the word of God as revealed to the Prophet Muhammad during his life in Mecca and Medina.
