wash drawing

noun
a drawing using monochrome watercolor washes
Pronunciation: / wɑʃ ˈdrɔɪŋ / (AmE), / wɒʃ ˈdrɔːɪŋ / (BrE)
a drawing made by applying a series of monochrome watercolor washes to paper, producing tonal effects

Examples

A wash drawing artwork
A delicate wash drawing can suggest atmosphere and depth using only variations in tone.
Her portfolio featured several wash drawings alongside her oil paintings and sketches.
The artist created a wash drawing to capture the subtle gradations of light in the landscape.
