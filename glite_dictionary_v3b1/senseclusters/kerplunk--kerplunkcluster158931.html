kerplunk

interjection
sound of a heavy fall
9.9
Pronunciation: / kərˈplʌŋk / (AmE), / kəˈplʌŋk / (BrE)
an exclamation used to imitate or react to the sound of something falling or hitting a surface with a heavy splash or thud

Examples

Kerplunk! It fell
The rock landed in the pond with a loud kerplunk that made everyone laugh.
As the book slipped off the shelf, it hit the floor with a sudden kerplunk.
He dropped his keys into the metal bucket and they fell with a distinct kerplunk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: splash - the sound of something hitting liquid
  • synonym: thud - a dull sound made by a heavy object hitting something
