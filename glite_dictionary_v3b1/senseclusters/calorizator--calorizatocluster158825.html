calorizator

noun
apparatus for heating beet-sugar juice
Pronunciation: / ˌkælərɪˈzeɪtər / (AmE), / ˌkæl.ə.rɪˈzeɪ.tə / (BrE)
an apparatus used in beet-sugar factories for heating juice to facilitate diffusion in the production process

Examples

The calorizator device
Operators monitor the temperature inside the calorizator to ensure optimal diffusion.
Regular maintenance of the calorizator is essential for consistent sugar production.
The factory installed a new calorizator to improve the efficiency of the juice extraction process.
