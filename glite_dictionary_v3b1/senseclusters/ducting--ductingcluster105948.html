ducting

noun
installation of ducts
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌktɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʌktɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of installing ducts or running cables, air, or other materials through ducts in a building

Examples

Ducting installation work
The ducting for the new air conditioning system was completed last week.
Proper ducting is essential to ensure good airflow throughout the building.
They inspected the ducting to check for any leaks or blockages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: piping - the system or process of installing pipes
  • synonym: ventilation - the provision of fresh air to a room or building
