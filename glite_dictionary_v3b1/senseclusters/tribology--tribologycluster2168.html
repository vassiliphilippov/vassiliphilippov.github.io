tribology

noun
study of friction, wear, and lubrication
9.1
Pronunciation: / traɪˈbɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / traɪˈbɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of engineering concerned with the study of friction, wear, and lubrication between surfaces in relative motion

Examples

Tribology study of friction
Tribology has become crucial in the design of modern car engines and other mechanical systems.
She specializes in tribology, focusing on new ways to reduce friction in machinery.
Understanding tribology helps engineers develop more durable and efficient bearings.
