yoke

noun
oppressive power or rule
Pronunciation: / joʊk / (AmE), / jəʊk / (BrE)
an oppressive force or system that restricts freedom

Examples

The yoke of oppression
After years of oppression, the nation finally cast off the yoke of dictatorship.
Revolutionaries fought to break the yoke of colonial rule.
The people suffered for decades under the yoke of a foreign empire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bondage - the state of being enslaved or under subjugation
  • antonym: freedom - the state of being free, not under control or oppression
  • synonym: shackle - a physical or figurative restraint or impediment
