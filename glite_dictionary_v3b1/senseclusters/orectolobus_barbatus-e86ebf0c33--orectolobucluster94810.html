Orectolobus barbatus

noun
a shark species with tassels around the mouth
Pronunciation: / ɔːrɛkˈtɒləbəs bɑːrˈbeɪtəs / (AmE), / ɒrɛkˈtɒləbəs bɑːˈbeɪtəs / (BrE)
a species of shark of the western Pacific, known for its flattened body, mottled skin, and distinctive tasseled appearance around the mouth
