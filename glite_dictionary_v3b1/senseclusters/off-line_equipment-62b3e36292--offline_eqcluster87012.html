off-line equipment

noun
devices not connected to a central computer
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɔf laɪn ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / ˈɒf laɪn ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
electronic devices that are not in direct communication with or under the control of a central processing unit

Examples

Off-line equipment devices
Because the printer is off-line equipment, it must be manually connected to upload print jobs.
Before being processed, the data must be transferred from the off-line equipment to the main system.
The laboratory uses off-line equipment to prepare samples before analysis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: online equipment - devices directly connected to and controlled by a central processing unit
