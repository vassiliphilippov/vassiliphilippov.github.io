germarium

noun
ovarian germ cell region
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒɚˈmɛəriəm / (AmE), / dʒɜːˈmɛəriəm / (BrE)
a region in the ovary of certain animals, especially insects, where germ cells develop into eggs

Examples

The germarium region
Scientists observed the germarium to track the early stages of egg formation in fruit flies.
The structure of the germarium varies among different insect species.
Damage to the germarium can affect the reproductive success of the animal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ovary - the organ in female animals that produces eggs
