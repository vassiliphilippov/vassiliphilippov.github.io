grison

noun
weasel-like mammal of Central and South America
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɪzən / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪzən / (BrE)
A carnivorous mammal of Central and South America, resembling a weasel, with a greyish-white back and dark underparts.

Examples

A grison animal
A grison can move swiftly through dense undergrowth while hunting.

The grison is rarely seen in the wild due to its secretive habits.
