confederative

adjective
relating to union in a confederacy or league
7.5 8.9
Pronunciation: / kənˈfɛdərətɪv / (AmE), / kənˈfɛdərətɪv / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by union in a confederacy or league; forming or having the nature of a confederation.

Examples

Confederative political system
Switzerland is often cited as a model of a confederative political structure.
The leaders agreed to pursue a confederative approach rather than a centralized government.
The treaty outlined a confederative system in which the states retained much of their autonomy.
