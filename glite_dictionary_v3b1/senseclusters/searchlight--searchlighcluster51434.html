searchlight

noun
a powerful directional beam of light
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜrtʃˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɜːtʃˌlaɪt / (BrE)
a powerful light with reflectors that projects a bright beam in a particular direction, used especially to search for objects or to illuminate areas at night

Examples

A searchlight beam
A powerful searchlight swept across the sky during the opening ceremony.
The prison guards scanned the yard with a searchlight throughout the night.
The ship's crew used a searchlight to locate survivors in the darkness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spotlight - a lamp projecting a narrow, intense beam of light on a place or person
