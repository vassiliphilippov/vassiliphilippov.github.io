polesman

noun
boat guide with pole
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊlzmən / (AmE), / ˈpəʊlzmən / (BrE)
a person who uses a long pole to move or steer a boat or raft, especially in shallow water

Examples

The polesman steers boat
The experienced polesman navigated the raft through the shallow waters with ease.
A skilled polesman is essential for guiding boats along narrow river channels.
Tourists watched as the polesman expertly pushed the boat away from the riverbank.

Related Terms

  • synonym: punter - a person who propels a boat with a pole
  • synonym: rower - a person who moves a boat using oars
