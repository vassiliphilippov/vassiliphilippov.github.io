castor sugar

noun
very finely granulated sugar
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkæs.tɚ ˈʃʊɡ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.stə ˈʃʊɡ.ə / (BrE)
A very finely granulated sugar, used especially in baking and formerly sprinkled from a castor.

Examples

A castor sugar jar
If you can't find castor sugar, you can pulse regular sugar in a food processor until fine.
She sprinkled a little castor sugar over the warm tart before serving.
The recipe calls for two tablespoons of castor sugar to ensure a light texture.
