salvationist

adjective
promoting salvation
8.4
Pronunciation: / sælˈveɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / sælˈveɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
relating to the belief in or promotion of salvation, especially in a Christian religious context.

Examples

Salvationist beliefs
The church's salvationist message focused on personal faith and redemption.
Her salvationist views influenced many people in the community to seek spiritual guidance.
A salvationist approach often emphasizes the importance of individual conversion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evangelical - relating to the preaching of the Christian gospel
  • antonym: secular - not connected with religious or spiritual matters
