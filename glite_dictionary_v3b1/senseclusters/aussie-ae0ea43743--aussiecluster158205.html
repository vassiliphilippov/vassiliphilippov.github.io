Aussie

noun
person from Australia (informal)
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɑː.si / (AmE), / ˈɒz.i / (BrE)
an informal term for a person from Australia; a native or inhabitant of Australia

Examples

An Aussie person
My friend Tom is an Aussie who moved to London last year.
The cafe was run by a cheerful Aussie who loved to chat with customers.
You can always trust an Aussie to know how to barbecue.
