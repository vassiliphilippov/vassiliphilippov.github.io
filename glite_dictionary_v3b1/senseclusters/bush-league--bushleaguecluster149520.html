bush-league

adjective
inferior or unsophisticated; not first-rate
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊʃ liːɡ / (AmE), / ˈbʊʃ liːɡ / (BrE)
of inferior quality or lacking sophistication; not meeting professional standards.

Examples

A bush-league mistake
Critics called the team's performance totally bush-league last night.
That was a bush-league move for someone with his experience.
The company apologized for delivering such bush-league customer service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amateurish - lacking professional skill or polish
  • antonym: professional - showing high level of skill or competence
