good-for-nothing

adjective
useless or worthless person or thing
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɡʊd fər ˈnʌθɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌɡʊd fə ˈnʌθɪŋ / (BrE)
Useless, worthless, or of no value; having little or no merit.

Examples

Good-for-nothing attitude
Many considered him a good-for-nothing who would never amount to anything.
She refuses to date a good-for-nothing who has no plans for the future.
The old, broken radio was just another good-for-nothing object cluttering the attic.

Related Terms

  • antonym: useful - able to be used to achieve something; having value
  • synonym: worthless - having no value or use
