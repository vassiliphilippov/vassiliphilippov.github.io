day-and-night

adjective
continuous or at all times
Pronunciation: / ˌdeɪ ən ˈnaɪt / (AmE), / ˌdeɪ ən ˈnaɪt / (BrE)
Lasting, occurring, or available at all times; continuous throughout the entire day and night.

Examples

The hospital provides day-and-night emergency services to all patients.
We need day-and-night surveillance to keep the area secure.
