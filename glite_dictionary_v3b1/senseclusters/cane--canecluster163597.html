cane

noun
switch/stick used for punishment
3.1 6.2
Pronunciation: /keɪn/ (AmE), /keɪn/ (BrE)
a long, thin stick or switch, especially one used as a whip or for punishing someone, formerly common in schools

Examples

A punishment cane
Corporal punishment with a cane has been banned in most schools.
He remembers the pain of being struck with a cane for misbehaving.
The teacher used a cane to discipline unruly students in the past.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rod - a straight, thin stick or bar, often used as an instrument for hitting
  • synonym: strap - a strip of leather used for striking as punishment
