baldness

noun
state of having no hair on the scalp
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔld.nəs / (AmE), / ˈbɔːld.nəs / (BrE)
the state of having little or no hair on the scalp, especially on the top of the head

Examples

Male baldness
He tried several treatments to prevent baldness but none were effective.
Male baldness often runs in families and becomes noticeable in middle age.
Some people embrace their baldness and feel confident without hair.
