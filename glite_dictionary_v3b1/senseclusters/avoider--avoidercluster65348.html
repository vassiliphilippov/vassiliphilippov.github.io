avoider

noun
person who avoids
4.6
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈvɔɪ.dər/ (AmE), /ə.ˈvɔɪ.də/ (BrE)
a person who tries to stay away from something or someone, especially to prevent involvement or confrontation

Examples

Avoider of conflict
As an avoider of conflict, he rarely argued with others.
The avoider in the group always found excuses to skip difficult conversations.
Being an avoider can sometimes make it harder to solve problems directly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confronter - a person who faces or deals with difficult situations directly
  • synonym: evader - a person who avoids or escapes something, often responsibility
