metering

noun
poetic rhythm pattern
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːtərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmiːtərɪŋ / (BrE)
the arrangement or use of rhythmic patterns, especially the pattern of stressed and unstressed syllables in poetry

Examples

Poetry metering
Careful metering can make a poem sound more musical and engaging to the reader.
The poet experimented with different metering to create a unique rhythm in each stanza.
Understanding metering is essential for anyone who wants to write traditional verse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prosody - the patterns of rhythm and sound in poetry
  • synonym: scansion - the analysis of a poem's meter and rhythm
