Synonyms

  • meter
    6.0 3.08/m
    a meter is the regular beat or foot pattern in verse used by poets, while metering means arranging those beats in lines.
  • poetic rhythm
    4.9 0.01/m
    poetic rhythm gives a poem its musical flow through stressed and unstressed beats, while metering focuses on arranging those patterns.
  • rhythmic pattern
    5.6 0.25/m
    a rhythmic pattern is a repeated order of stressed and unstressed syllables, while metering is the practice of setting that order in poems.
  • rhythm
    3.9 3.13/m
    rhythm is the general beat or timing heard in speech or music, while metering refers specifically to arranging those beats in poetry.
