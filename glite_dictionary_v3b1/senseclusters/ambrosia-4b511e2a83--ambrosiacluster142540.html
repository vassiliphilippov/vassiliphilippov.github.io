Ambrosia

noun
genus of plants (ragweeds)
Pronunciation: / æmˈbroʊʒə / (AmE), / æmˈbrəʊzɪə / (BrE)
A genus of plants in the family Asteraceae, commonly known as ragweeds, some species of which are major causes of seasonal allergies.

Examples

Ambrosia plants
Botanists studied the spread of Ambrosia in agricultural areas.
Many people suffer from allergies caused by Ambrosia pollen each autumn.
The Ambrosia genus includes several species native to North America.
