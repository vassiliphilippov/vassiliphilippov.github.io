swish

noun
a soft brushing or rustling sound
5.1 3.8
Pronunciation: /swɪʃ/ (AmE), /swɪʃ/ (BrE)
a soft, brushing or rustling sound, often produced by something moving quickly through the air or lightly touching a surface

Examples

A swish of the net
I heard the swish of the basketball going through the net.
The swish of the silk dress was barely audible as she walked across the room.
The gentle swish of leaves in the breeze soothed him to sleep.

  • synonym: rustle - a soft, muffled crackling sound like that made by dry leaves or paper
