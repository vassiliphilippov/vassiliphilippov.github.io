aeronautically

adverb
in a flight-related way
Pronunciation: / ˌɛroʊˈnɔːtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌeərəˈnɔːtɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the science or practice of flight or aircraft.

Examples

Designed aeronautically
The engineers designed the wings aeronautically to improve fuel efficiency.
This material performs aeronautically better than traditional metals in high-speed flight.
The new regulations require all parts to be tested aeronautically before approval.
