subnational

noun
regional government unit
6.4
Pronunciation: /sʌb.ˈnæʃ.ə.nəl/ /; / /sʌb.ˈnæʃ.nəl/ (AmE), /sʌb.ˈnæʃ.ə.nəl/ /; / /sʌb.ˈnæʃ.nəl/ (BrE)
a government, authority, or administrative unit within a country that operates below the national level, such as a state or province

Examples

A subnational authority
Each subnational is responsible for implementing education policies in its own region.
The agreement was signed by both the national and subnational authorities.
Funding for infrastructure projects is often distributed to subnational units like states or provinces.

Related Terms

  • antonym: national government - the central government of a country
  • synonym: regional government - a government that administers a specific region within a country
