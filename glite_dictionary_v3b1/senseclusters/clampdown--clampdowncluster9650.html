clampdown

noun
sudden restriction by authorities
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈklæmpˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈklæmpˌdaʊn / (BrE)
a sudden attempt by authorities to stop or control an undesirable activity

Examples

A strict clampdown
After the riots, the authorities imposed a strict clampdown on public gatherings.
The government announced a clampdown on illegal gambling operations across the city.
There has been a recent clampdown on tax evasion, with many companies under investigation.

  • synonym: crackdown - severe enforcement or repression
