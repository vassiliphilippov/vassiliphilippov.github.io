RhD

sym
blood type protein
Pronunciation: / ɑr eɪtʃ di / (AmE), / ɑː eɪtʃ diː / (BrE)
a protein on red blood cells that determines whether blood type is Rh positive or negative

Examples

RhD protein type
The doctor explained that her blood was RhD positive, which is important for transfusions.
If a pregnant woman is RhD negative, she may need special care.
Blood donors are tested for the RhD antigen to match recipients correctly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Rh factor - a group of antigens including RhD on red blood cells
  • synonym: antigen - a substance that causes the body to produce antibodies
