ventilatory

adjective
relating to ventilation, especially in the lungs
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛn.tɪ.ləˌtɔɹ.i / (AmE), / ˈvɛntɪlətəri / (BrE)
relating to or involving ventilation, especially the movement of air in and out of the lungs

Examples

Ventilatory response
Doctors monitored the child's ventilatory response during the procedure.
Physical activity increases ventilatory demand as the body requires more oxygen.
The patient was placed on a ventilatory support system after experiencing respiratory failure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ventilation - the process of supplying fresh air and removing stale air
