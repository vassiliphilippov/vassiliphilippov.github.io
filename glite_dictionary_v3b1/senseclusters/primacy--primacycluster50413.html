primacy

noun
first place in importance
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪməsi / (AmE), / ˈpraɪməsi / (BrE)
the state of being first in importance or having the highest rank

Examples

The primacy of importance
For centuries, the primacy of the church was challenged by emerging nation-states.
The primacy of education in a child's development cannot be overstated.
The company places primacy on customer satisfaction above all else.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inferiority - the condition of being lower in status or quality
  • synonym: supremacy - the state of being superior to all others in authority or power
