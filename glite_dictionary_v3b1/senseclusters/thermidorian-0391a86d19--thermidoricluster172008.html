Thermidorian

noun
supporter of Thermidorian Reaction
Pronunciation: / ˌθɜːrmɪˈdɔːriən / (AmE), / ˌθɜːmɪˈdɔːriən / (BrE)
a person who supported or was involved in the Thermidorian Reaction during the French Revolution

Examples

Thermidorian leader
The Thermidorian leaders played a key role in ending the Reign of Terror.
After the fall of Robespierre, many became known as Thermidorians for their support of the new government.
A prominent Thermidorian led the move to remove the Jacobin leaders from power.
