freight train

noun
train for transporting goods
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪt treɪn / (AmE), / ˈfreɪt treɪn / (BrE)
A train consisting of freight cars that are used to transport goods rather than passengers.

Examples

A freight train carrying coal
A freight train carrying coal derailed near the industrial park.
The long freight train rumbled through the countryside late at night.
You can hear the horn of a freight train from miles away.

Related Terms

  • antonym: passenger train - a train consisting of carriages for carrying people rather than goods
