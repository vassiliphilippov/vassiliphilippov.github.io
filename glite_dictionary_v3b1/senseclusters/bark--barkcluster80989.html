Examples

To bark loudly
Neighbors complained because the dogs would bark all night long.

Synonyms

  • yap
    5.2 0.06/m
    yap is a sharp, high-pitched bark often repeated by small dogs, while bark is a general loud dog sound.
  • bay
    6.8 0.45/m
    bay is a loud, long bark or howl used by hunting dogs and wolves, while bark is any sharp dog sound.
