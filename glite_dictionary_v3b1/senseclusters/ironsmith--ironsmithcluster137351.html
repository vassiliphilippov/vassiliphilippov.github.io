ironsmith

noun
ironworker
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪərnˌsmɪθ / (AmE), / ˈaɪənˌsmɪθ / (BrE)
a person who makes or repairs objects out of iron

Examples

The ironsmith crafts gates
The ironsmith crafted a sturdy gate for the village.
An experienced ironsmith can create tools, railings, and decorative items from raw iron.
They hired a local ironsmith to repair the old iron fence around the park.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blacksmith - a person who makes and repairs things in iron by hand
  • antonym: goldsmith - a person who makes or repairs things made of gold
