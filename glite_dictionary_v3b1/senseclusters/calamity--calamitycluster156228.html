calamity

noun
event causing great loss or disaster
8.0
Pronunciation: / kəˈlæm.ə.ti / (AmE), / kəˈlæm.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
An event causing great and often sudden damage or distress; a disaster.

Examples

A calamity event
After the flood, the government responded quickly to the unexpected calamity.
Losing both his parents in the accident was a personal calamity he never fully recovered from.
The economic calamity left thousands of people jobless and homeless.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catastrophe - an event resulting in great loss and misfortune
  • synonym: disaster - a sudden event causing great damage or loss
