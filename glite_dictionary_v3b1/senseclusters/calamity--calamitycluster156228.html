Examples

A calamity event
After the flood, the government responded quickly to the unexpected calamity.
Losing both his parents in the accident was a personal calamity he never fully recovered from.

Synonyms

  • disaster
    1.0 68/m
    disaster is a common word for a sudden event causing great damage, while calamity sounds slightly more formal and literary.
  • catastrophe
    7.0 11/m
    catastrophe is a sudden, very large and terrible event that causes heavy loss, while calamity is a general disaster causing great distress.
  • cataclysm
    9.2 0.98/m
    cataclysm is a violent upheaval that changes nature or society, while calamity is a broad word for any severe disaster.
  • tragedy
    3.0 36/m
    tragedy is an event that causes deep human suffering or loss, while calamity focuses more on large-scale damage or disaster.
