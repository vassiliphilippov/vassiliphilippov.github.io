slip up

verb
make a careless mistake
Pronunciation: / slɪp ʌp / (AmE), / slɪp ʌp / (BrE)
to make a careless or minor mistake

Examples

To slip up badly
Don't slip up during the interview, or you might lose your chance.
If you slip up on the details, the whole project could be delayed.
She slipped up by forgetting to send the email on time.
Be careful not to slip up when entering your password.
He slipped up by forgetting her birthday this year.
It's easy to slip up during a stressful situation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blunder - make a serious or careless mistake
  • synonym: err - to make a mistake or be incorrect
  • antonym: get it right - to do something without making a mistake
  • synonym: make a mistake - to do something incorrectly or in error
