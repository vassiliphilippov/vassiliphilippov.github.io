Examples

Covering fire support
During the retreat, tanks provided covering fire to shield the infantry.

Synonyms

  • suppressive fire
    7.8 0.02/m
    suppressive fire is sustained gunfire meant to stop enemy weapons from working, while covering fire focuses on protecting troops as they move.
  • supporting fire
    7.0 0.02/m
    supporting fire is gunfire from other units to help or protect troops, while covering fire specifically blocks enemy return fire during movement.
