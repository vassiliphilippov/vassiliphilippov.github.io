impartiality

noun
fairness; lack of bias
3.1 4.3
Pronunciation: /ɪm.pɑːr.ˈʃæl.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /ɪm.pɑː.ˈʃæl.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)

Examples

Complete impartiality

Journalistic impartiality is essential for maintaining public trust.
