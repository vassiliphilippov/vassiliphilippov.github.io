aortic stenosis

noun
narrowing of the aortic valve in the heart
5.4 5.5
Pronunciation: /eɪ.ˈɔːr.tɪk stɪ.ˈnoʊ.sɪs/ /; / /eɪ.ˈɔːr.tɪk sə.ˈnoʊ.sɪs/ (AmE), /eɪ.ˈɔː.tɪk stɪ.ˈnəʊ.sɪs/ /; / /eɪ.ˈɔː.tɪk sə.ˈnəʊ.sɪs/ (BrE)
