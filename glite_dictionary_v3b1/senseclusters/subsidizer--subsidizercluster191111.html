subsidizer

noun
one who provides a subsidy
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌb.sɪ.daɪ.zər /; / ˈsʌb.sɪˌdaɪ.zɚ / (AmE), / ˈsʌb.sɪ.daɪ.zə /; / ˈsʌb.sɪˌdaɪ.zə / (BrE)
