youngness

noun
the quality of being young
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈjʌŋ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈjʌŋ.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being young; the condition of having few years or being at an early stage of life or development.

Examples

The youngness of children
Despite his youngness, he was chosen to lead the project.
Her youngness made her perspective refreshing and full of hope.
The youngness of the team was both an asset and a challenge.

Related Terms

  • antonym: oldness - the quality or state of being old
  • synonym: youth - the period or state of being young
