lady slipper

noun
wildflower orchid with pouchlike lip
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪdi ˌslɪpər / (AmE), / ˈleɪdi ˌslɪpə / (BrE)
a wild orchid of North America with a distinctive inflated pouchlike lip, difficult or impossible to cultivate in gardens

Examples

Wild lady slipper orchid
Gardeners often find the lady slipper difficult to grow outside its natural habitat.
Several species of lady slipper are now protected due to their rarity.
The lady slipper is admired for its delicate beauty in the wild.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cypripedium - genus of orchids commonly known as lady's slippers
