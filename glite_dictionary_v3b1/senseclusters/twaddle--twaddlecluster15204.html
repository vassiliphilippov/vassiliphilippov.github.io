twaddle

noun
silly or trivial talk; nonsense
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɑː.dl̩ / (AmE), / ˈtwɒd.əl / (BrE)
silly or trivial talk or writing that lacks sense or meaning; nonsense.

Examples

Silly twaddle
Don't listen to that twaddle; none of it is true.
He dismissed the rumor as utter twaddle and refused to discuss it further.
Her speech was full of pretentious twaddle that failed to impress the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonsense - spoken or written words that have no meaning or make no sense
