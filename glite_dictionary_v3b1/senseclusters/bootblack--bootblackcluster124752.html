bootblack

noun
person who polishes shoes for pay
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːtˌblæk / (AmE), / ˈbuːtˌblæk / (BrE)
a person whose job is to polish shoes and boots, especially for payment

Examples

A bootblack polishes shoes
After school, he worked as a bootblack to earn extra money for his family.
In the old city, the bootblack polished boots with remarkable skill.
The bootblack set up his stand outside the grand hotel every morning.
