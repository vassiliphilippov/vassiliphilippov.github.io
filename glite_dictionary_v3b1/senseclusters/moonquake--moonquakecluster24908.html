moonquake

noun
lunar seismic event
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːnˌkweɪk / (AmE), / ˈmuːnˌkweɪk / (BrE)
a tremor or seismic event that occurs on the surface of the Moon

Examples

A strong moonquake
Astronauts recorded a strong moonquake during their mission on the lunar surface.
Scientists study moonquakes to learn more about the Moon's internal structure.
The equipment detected a minor moonquake just after sunrise on the Moon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: earthquake - a sudden shaking of the Earth's surface caused by seismic activity
