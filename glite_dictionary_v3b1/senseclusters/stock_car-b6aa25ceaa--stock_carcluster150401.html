stock car

noun
car held by a dealer for regular sale
Pronunciation: / stɑk kɑr / (AmE), / stɒk kɑː / (BrE)
a car kept in a dealer's inventory for general sale, as opposed to a custom-ordered vehicle

Examples

A stock car for sale
He chose a stock car from the lot instead of placing a special order.
Most buyers prefer to test drive a stock car before making a decision.
The dealership has several stock cars available for immediate purchase.
