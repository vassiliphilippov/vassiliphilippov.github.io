deadener

noun
noise-reducing material
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛdənər / (AmE), / ˈdɛdənə / (BrE)
a material or device used to reduce or block noise, vibration, or sensation

Examples

Sound deadener
The car doors were lined with a deadener to minimize road noise.
Adding a deadener under the floor helped reduce vibrations in the apartment.
The technician recommended a sound deadener to make the room quieter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dampener - a device that reduces vibration or noise
  • synonym: insulator - a material that prevents the passage of sound, heat, or electricity
