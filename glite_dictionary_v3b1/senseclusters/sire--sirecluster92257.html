sire

noun
male parent of an animal
Pronunciation: / saɪɚ / (AmE), / saɪə / (BrE)
the male parent of an animal, especially a domesticated animal such as a horse

Examples

Prize sire horse
Farmers carefully select which sire to use for breeding their livestock.
The champion stallion was a famous sire whose offspring won many races.
The pedigree documents list both the sire and the dam of each foal.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dam - the female parent of an animal
  • synonym: stud - a male animal, especially a horse, kept for breeding
