unfalsified

adjective
not disproved
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈfɔːlsɪfaɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈfɔːlsɪfaɪd / (BrE)
not shown to be false or untrue; not disproved by evidence or argument

Examples

Unfalsified theory
The scientist's hypothesis remains unfalsified despite numerous attempts to test it.
After years of investigation, the claim was still unfalsified by any evidence.
A theory is considered strong if it is testable and yet remains unfalsified.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disproved - shown to be false or incorrect
  • synonym: undisproved - not disproved or shown to be false
