villainize

verb
to portray as evil
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪlənˌaɪz / (AmE), / ˈvɪlənˌaɪz / (BrE)
to portray someone or something as evil, bad, or the cause of problems

Examples

To villainize a person
Some news outlets tend to villainize certain groups to attract more viewers.
It is unfair to villainize someone without understanding their side of the story.
The film refused to villainize any character, showing everyone's motivations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demonize - to make someone or something seem evil or threatening
  • antonym: praise - to express approval or admiration for someone or something
