Arecidae

noun
former subclass of monocot plants
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈrɛsɪˌdiː /; / əˈriːsɪˌdiː / (AmE), / əˈrɛsɪˌdiː /; / əˈriːsɪˌdiː / (BrE)
a subclass of monocotyledonous plants that includes palms, arums, duckweeds, and related families
