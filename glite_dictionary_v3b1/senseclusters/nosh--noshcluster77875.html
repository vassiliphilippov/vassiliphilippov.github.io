nosh

verb
eat a snack; eat lightly
Pronunciation: / nɑʃ / (AmE), / nɒʃ / (BrE)
to eat a snack or eat lightly, especially between meals

Examples

To nosh on chips
He tends to nosh throughout the day instead of having big meals.
She likes to nosh on fruit while watching television in the evening.
We stopped at a café to nosh before the show started.

Related Terms

  • antonym: feast - to eat a large or elaborate meal
  • synonym: snack - to eat a small amount of food between meals
