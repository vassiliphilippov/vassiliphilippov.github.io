intuitionistic

adjective
guided by intuition
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tuː.ɪ.ʃə.ˈnɪs.tɪk/ /; / /ɪn.tjuː.ɪ.ʃə.ˈnɪs.tɪk/ (AmE), /ɪn.tjʊ.ɪ.ʃə.ˈnɪs.tɪk/ /; / /ɪn.tuː.ɪ.ʃə.ˈnɪs.tɪk/ (BrE)
