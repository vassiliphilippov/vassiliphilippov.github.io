beam

noun
narrow guiding radio signal for aircraft
4.8
Pronunciation: / biːm / (AmE), / biːm / (BrE)
a radio signal transmitted in a narrow path to guide aircraft, especially for navigation in darkness or poor weather

Examples

Radio beam
During heavy fog, the plane relied on a guidance beam to land safely.
Modern navigation systems replaced the older beam approach for aircraft.
The pilot lined up with the runway by following the radio beam.

Related Terms

  • synonym: signal - a sound, gesture, or device that gives information or instructions
