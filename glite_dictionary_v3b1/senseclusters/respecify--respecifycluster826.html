respecify

verb
to define again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈspɛsɪfaɪ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈspɛsɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to define or state something again, often in a different or clearer way

Examples

To respecify project requirements
After receiving feedback, the team decided to respecify the project requirements.
The teacher asked the student to respecify his answer to make it clearer.
If the results are unclear, you may need to respecify your hypothesis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: redefine - to define again or differently
  • synonym: specify - to state or describe something clearly and exactly
