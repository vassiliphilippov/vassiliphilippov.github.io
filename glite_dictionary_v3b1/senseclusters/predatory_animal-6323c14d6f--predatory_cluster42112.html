predatory animal

noun
animal that hunts other animals
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛdəˌtɔri ˈænɪməl / (AmE), / ˈprɛdətri ˈænɪməl / (BrE)
an animal that hunts and kills other animals for food

Examples

Predatory animal attacks
Farmers are sometimes concerned about predatory animals attacking their livestock.
Many conservationists study the role of the predatory animal in maintaining the balance of ecosystems.
The lion is a well-known predatory animal that hunts in groups called prides.

Related Terms

  • synonym: predator - an animal that hunts, kills, and eats other animals
  • antonym: prey - an animal that is hunted and eaten by another animal
