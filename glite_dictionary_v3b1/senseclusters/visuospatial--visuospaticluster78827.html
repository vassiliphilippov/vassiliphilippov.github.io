visuospatial

adjective
visual-spatial ability
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌvɪʒuˌoʊˈspeɪʃəl / (AmE), / ˌvɪzjuːˈspɛɪʃəl / (BrE)
relating to the ability to perceive, analyze, or understand visual and spatial relationships

Examples

Visuospatial skills
Children develop visuospatial skills as they learn to draw shapes and recognize patterns.
The test measures your visuospatial ability by asking you to rotate objects in your mind.
Architects rely on strong visuospatial awareness when designing buildings and spaces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spatial - relating to space and the position, area, and size of things
  • synonym: visual - relating to seeing or sight
