hula

noun
Hawaiian dance with rhythmic movements
6.8 1.28/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàhuÀêl…ô / (AmE), / ÀàhuÀêl…ô / (BrE)
A traditional Polynesian dance, originally from Hawaii, characterized by rhythmic movement and gestures that tell a story, often performed to chant or music.

Examples

Traditional hula dance
He learned the basic steps of the hula during his vacation.
She performed a graceful hula at the cultural festival.
Tourists enjoy watching the traditional hula dancers in Hawaii.

Synonyms

  • hula-hula
    7.9 0.01/m
    hula-hula is a traditional Polynesian dance performed by a woman using hip and hand moves to tell stories, while hula is the broader Hawaiian dance form.
  • Hawaiian dancing
    3.4 0.00/m
    Hawaiian dancing is a general style from Hawaii with graceful hand and hip moves, while hula is a specific traditional storytelling dance.
