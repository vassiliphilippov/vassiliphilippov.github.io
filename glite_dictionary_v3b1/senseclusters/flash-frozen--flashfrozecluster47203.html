flash-frozen

adjective
quickly frozen to preserve quality
4.9
Pronunciation: / flæʃ ˈfroʊzən / (AmE), / flæʃ ˈfrəʊzən / (BrE)
(of food) preserved by freezing very quickly to retain flavor and nutritional value.

Examples

Flash-frozen food
Many ready meals use flash-frozen ingredients to maintain nutritional value.
Seafood is often flash-frozen at sea to lock in flavor and texture.
The vegetables are flash-frozen to ensure maximum freshness.
