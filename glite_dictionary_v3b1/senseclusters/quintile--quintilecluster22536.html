quintile

adjective
divided into five parts
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪntaɪl / (AmE), / ˈkwɪntaɪl / (BrE)
relating to or based on division into five equal groups, especially in statistical data analysis

Examples

Quintile range
The study analyzed quintile groups to compare health outcomes across the population.
Schools in the lowest quintile received additional funding to support students.
Researchers used quintile rankings to identify income disparities among households.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decile - relating to division into ten equal groups
  • synonym: quartile - relating to division into four equal groups
