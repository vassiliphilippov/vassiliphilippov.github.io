get the bullet

verb
be dismissed from a job
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt ðə ˈbʊlət / (AmE), / ɡɛt ðə ˈbʊlɪt / (BrE)
to be dismissed or fired from a job, or to lose an opportunity abruptly

Examples

To get the bullet at work
After the company restructuring, several employees got the bullet and had to look for new jobs.
He knew he'd get the bullet if he made another serious mistake at work.
When the project failed, the entire team feared they might get the bullet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: be fired - to lose one's job, typically due to dismissal
  • synonym: get the sack - to be dismissed from a job (especially British idiom)
