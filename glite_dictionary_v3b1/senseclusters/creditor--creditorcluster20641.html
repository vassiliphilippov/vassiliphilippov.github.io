creditor

noun
person or company owed money
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛdɪtər / (AmE), / ˈkrɛdɪtə / (BrE)
a person or organization to whom money is owed.

Examples

A creditor demands payment
After losing his job, John struggled to pay each creditor on time.
She negotiated with the creditor to extend her payment deadline.
The creditor threatened legal action if the debt was not settled within a month.

Related Terms

  • antonym: debtor - a person or organization that owes money
