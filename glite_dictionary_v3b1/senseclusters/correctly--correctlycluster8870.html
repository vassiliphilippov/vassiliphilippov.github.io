correctly

adverb
in an accurate or proper way
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɛktli / (AmE), / kəˈrɛktli / (BrE)
In a way that is accurate or free from mistakes; in accordance with the facts or rules.

Examples

Answer correctly on test
He answered all the questions correctly on the test.
Please make sure the address is written correctly on the envelope.
The instructions were followed correctly, and the machine started without any issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accurately - in a way that is correct and without any mistakes
  • antonym: incorrectly - in an inaccurate or wrong manner
