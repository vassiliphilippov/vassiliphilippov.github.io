crawl

noun
slow movement on hands and knees
2.7
Pronunciation: / krɔl / (AmE), / krɔːl / (BrE)
a slow movement by dragging the body or moving on hands and knees

Examples

A slow crawl
After the accident, he managed only a crawl across the room.
The baby's first move was a slow crawl toward her toys.
With traffic at a crawl, they were late to the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: creep - to move slowly with the body close to the ground
