imponderability

noun
unmeasurable factor
Pronunciation: / ɪmˌpɑndərəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ɪmˌpɒndərəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
something (a factor or quality) that cannot be measured, estimated, or weighed because it is too difficult or impossible to assess
