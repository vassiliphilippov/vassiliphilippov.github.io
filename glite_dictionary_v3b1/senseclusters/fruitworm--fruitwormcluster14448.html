fruitworm

noun
insect larva in fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈfruːtˌwɜrm / (AmE), / ˈfruːtˌwɜːm / (BrE)
a larva of certain insects that feeds on fruit, often causing damage to crops or trees

Examples

A fruitworm larva
The farmer lost much of his peach crop to a fruitworm infestation last summer.
Scientists are developing new methods to control fruitworm populations in orchards.
If you notice holes in your apples, a fruitworm may be the cause.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caterpillar - the larval stage of a butterfly or moth
  • synonym: pest - an animal or insect that damages crops or food
