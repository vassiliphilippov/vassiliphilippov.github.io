received

adjective
widely accepted as true or worthy
4.0 3.7
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈsiːvd/ /; / /rə.ˈsiːvd/ /; / /riː.ˈsiːvd/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈsiːvd/ /; / /rə.ˈsiːvd/ (BrE)
(of a belief, idea, or practice) generally accepted as true or authoritative, especially by most people in a society or group.
