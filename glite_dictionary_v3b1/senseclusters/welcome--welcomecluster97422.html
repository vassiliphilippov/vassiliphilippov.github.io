welcome

verb
greet someone on arrival
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛlkəm /; / ˈwɛl.kəm / (AmE), / ˈwɛlkəm /; / ˈwɛl.kəm / (BrE)
to greet or receive someone in a friendly and warm way, especially when they arrive

Examples

To welcome a guest
She always welcomes guests with a warm smile.
They welcomed the new student to the class with a small celebration.
We were happy to welcome our neighbors into our home for dinner.
The host stood at the door to welcome the arriving guests.
They welcome all new members with a handshake and a smile.
We were welcomed warmly by our friends after our long journey.
