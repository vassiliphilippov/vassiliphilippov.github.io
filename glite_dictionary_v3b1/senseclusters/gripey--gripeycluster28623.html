gripey

adjective
complaining frequently
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraɪpi / (AmE), / ˈɡraɪpi / (BrE)
often complaining or grumbling, especially in a persistent or annoying way

Examples

Gripey attitude
She became gripey after waiting in line for over an hour.
His gripey attitude made the meeting less enjoyable for everyone.
Try not to be so gripey about minor inconveniences.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerful - noticeably happy and optimistic
  • synonym: grumpy - easily annoyed or in a bad mood
