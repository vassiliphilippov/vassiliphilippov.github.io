Atriplex hymenelytra

noun
species of saltbush native to the southwestern US and Mexico
Pronunciation: / ˈætrɪplɛks ˌhaɪməniˈlaɪtrə / (AmE), / ˈætrɪplɛks ˌhɪmɛnɪˈliːtrə / (BrE)
A low-growing saltbush native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and Mexico, with blue-green, prickly-edged leaves often used for Christmas decorations.
