frictionally

adverb
by means of friction
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪkʃənəli / (AmE), / ˈfrɪkʃənəli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to or caused by friction

Examples

Frictionally sliding surfaces
The two blocks slid frictionally along the rough surface.
The wheels are frictionally engaged to prevent slipping on the track.
The machine operates frictionally, using resistance to slow down the moving parts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mechanically - in a manner relating to machines or physical forces
