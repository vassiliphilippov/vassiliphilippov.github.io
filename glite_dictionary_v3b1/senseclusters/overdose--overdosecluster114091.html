overdose

verb
take too much of a drug
1.0 3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈoʊ.vɜːr.doʊs/ (AmE), /ˈəʊ.və.dəʊs/ (BrE)
to take or administer an excessive amount of a drug or substance, especially to a dangerous or harmful extent

Examples

To overdose on drugs
Doctors warn that it is possible to overdose on certain vitamins.
He accidentally overdosed on painkillers after his surgery.
The singer was hospitalized after reportedly overdosing at a party.
