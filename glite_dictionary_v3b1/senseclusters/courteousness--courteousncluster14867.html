courteousness

noun
polite behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝːtiəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈkɜːtiəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being polite and showing respect for others

Examples

Courteousness towards guests
His courteousness towards guests made everyone feel welcome at the event.
The courteousness of the staff impressed all the customers at the restaurant.
She handled the complaint with great courteousness and professionalism.

Related Terms

  • synonym: politeness - the quality of being respectful and considerate
  • antonym: rudeness - lack of respect or courtesy
