Synonyms

  • let down
    2.9 10/m
    let down means disappointing someone by not helping or keeping a promise, while fail is a more general verb for not doing what is expected.
  • break a promise
    2.6 0.7/m
    break a promise means not keeping something you said you would do, while fail covers any case of not doing what is expected.
  • disappoint
    1.0 20/m
    disappoint means to cause someone sadness by missing hopes, while fail focuses on not doing what was expected or promised.
  • neglect
    3.8 12/m
    neglect means not giving proper care or attention over time, while fail often means not doing a single expected act.
