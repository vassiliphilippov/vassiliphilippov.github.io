too big for one's britches

adjective
overconfident or arrogant; self-important
8.8
Pronunciation: / tu bɪɡ fər wʌnz ˈbrɪtʃɪz / (AmE), / tuː bɪɡ fɔː wʌnz ˈbrɪtʃɪz / (BrE)
behaving as if you are more important or knowledgeable than you really are; overly confident or arrogant

Examples

To be too big for your britches
Don’t get too big for your britches just because you aced one test.
Since winning the award, he’s gotten too big for his britches and acts like he’s better than everyone.
Sometimes celebrities become too big for their britches after tasting a bit of fame.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrogant - having an exaggerated sense of one’s own importance or abilities
  • antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low estimate of one’s importance
