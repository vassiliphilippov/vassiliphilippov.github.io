slice bar

noun
iron tool for raking furnace clinkers
Pronunciation: / slaɪs bɑr / (AmE), / slaɪs bɑː / (BrE)
an iron bar used to loosen and rake clinkers out of furnaces

Examples

Heavy slice bar
He gripped the heavy slice bar and began chipping away at the hardened deposits.
Proper use of a slice bar can prevent dangerous blockages in industrial furnaces.
The worker used a slice bar to clear the clinkers from the bottom of the furnace.
