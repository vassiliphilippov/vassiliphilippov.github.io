undisturbed

adjective
not disturbed or interfered with
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.dɪˈstɝːbd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈstɜːbd / (BrE)
not interrupted, interfered with, or troubled; remaining calm or unchanged by outside influence.

Examples

Sleep undisturbed
He slept undisturbed through the night despite the storm outside.
She left the room and shut the door to remain undisturbed while working.
The ancient ruins had remained undisturbed for centuries beneath the jungle canopy.
