unrefusable

adjective
impossible to refuse
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈfjuzəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈfjuːzəbəl / (BrE)
impossible or extremely difficult to refuse or reject

Examples

An unrefusable offer
The job offer was so generous that it was simply unrefusable.
He received an unrefusable invitation to speak at the conference.
They made an unrefusable deal that everyone agreed to immediately.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irresistible - too attractive or appealing to be resisted
  • antonym: refusable - able to be refused or rejected
