call a lid

verb
end or stop an activity
Pronunciation: / kɔl ə lɪd / (AmE), / kɔːl ə lɪd / (BrE)
to bring something to an end or to stop or restrict an activity

Examples

To call a lid on talk
After an intense debate, the chair decided to call a lid on further discussion.
The manager called a lid on new proposals until next week.
We usually call a lid around 5 p.m., unless there’s urgent business.

Related Terms

  • synonym: call it a day - to stop working for the day; to stop an activity
  • synonym: wrap up - to finish or conclude something
