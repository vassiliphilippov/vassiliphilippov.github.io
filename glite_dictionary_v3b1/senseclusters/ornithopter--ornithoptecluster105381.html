ornithopter

noun
aircraft propelled by flapping wings
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrnəˌθɑptər / (AmE), / ˈɔːnɪˌθɒptə / (BrE)
an aircraft that flies by flapping wings like a bird

Examples

A ornithopter aircraft
Although rare, some inventors have built functioning ornithopters for demonstration purposes.
In science fiction novels, characters often travel using a ornithopter.
The engineer designed a ornithopter that mimics the motion of a bird’s wings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airplane - a powered flying vehicle with fixed wings
  • synonym: helicopter - an aircraft with rotating blades
