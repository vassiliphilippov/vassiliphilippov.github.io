macumba

noun
Afro-Brazilian religious practice
Pronunciation: / məˈkumbə /; / məˈkʊm.bə / (AmE), / məˈkʊmbə /; / məˈkʊm.bə / (BrE)
an Afro-Brazilian religious practice involving rituals, beliefs, and ceremonies of African origin, often including singing, chanting, dancing, and elements similar to voodoo
