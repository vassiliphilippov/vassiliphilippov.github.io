anti-smoking

noun
tobacco prevention efforts
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈsmoʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈsməʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
efforts, policies, or campaigns aimed at reducing or preventing the use of tobacco products

Examples

Anti-smoking policy
The government launched a new anti-smoking initiative to educate young people about the dangers of tobacco.
Many schools participate in anti-smoking programs to help students avoid starting the habit.
Public health organizations often support anti-smoking campaigns to reduce illness caused by tobacco use.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pro-smoking - supporting or promoting the use of tobacco products
  • synonym: smoking cessation - the process of quitting smoking
  • synonym: tobacco control - measures to regulate or restrict tobacco use
