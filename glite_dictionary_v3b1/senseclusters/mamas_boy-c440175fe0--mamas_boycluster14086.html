mama's boy

noun
boy overly attached to his mother
1.0 5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑː.məz bɔɪ/ /; / /ˈmæ.məz bɔɪ/ (AmE), /ˈmɑː.məz bɔɪ/ /; / /ˈmæ.məz bɔɪ/ (BrE)
a boy or man who is overly attached to or influenced by his mother, often seen as lacking independence

Examples

A mama's boy
Although he was often labeled a mama's boy, he was proud of his close relationship with his mother.
Everyone teased him for being a mama's boy because he called his mother several times a day.
Some people consider a mama's boy to be someone who always seeks his mother's approval before making decisions.
Everyone knew he was a mama's boy, always running home for advice on everything.
His friends teased him for being a mama's boy after he called his mother three times a day.
She broke up with him because she didn't want to date a mama's boy.
