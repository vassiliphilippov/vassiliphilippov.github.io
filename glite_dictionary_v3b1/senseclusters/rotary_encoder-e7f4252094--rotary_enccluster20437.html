rotary encoder

noun
device for measuring shaft rotation
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊˌtɛri ɪnˈkoʊdər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊtəri ɪnˈkəʊdə / (BrE)
an electro-mechanical device that converts the angular position or motion of a rotating shaft or axle to an analog or digital code

Examples

Rotary encoder device
A rotary encoder can help provide accurate feedback on shaft position in industrial machinery.
Engineers replaced the faulty sensor with a new rotary encoder to restore full motion control.
The robot's precise arm movements are made possible by a rotary encoder attached to each joint.
