chemical substance

noun
material with a specific chemical composition
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛmɪkəl ˈsʌbstəns / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪkəl ˈsʌbstəns / (BrE)
A material with a specific chemical composition, produced by or used in chemical reactions involving changes in atoms or molecules.

Examples

A chemical substance
Exposure to certain chemical substances can have serious health effects.
The storage of dangerous chemical substances requires special safety measures.
This factory produces several chemical substances used in the manufacture of pharmaceuticals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chemical - a substance used in or produced by a chemical process
