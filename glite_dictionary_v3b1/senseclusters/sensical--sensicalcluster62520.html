sensical

adjective
logical or reasonable
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnsɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsɪkəl / (BrE)
logical, reasonable, or making sense

Examples

A sensical explanation
Her explanation was clear and completely sensical.
The instructions were written in a sensical way that everyone could understand.
He tried to organize his thoughts into a sensical argument before the debate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: logical - reasonable and based on clear thinking
  • antonym: nonsensical - having no meaning or making no sense
  • synonym: reasonable - fair and sensible
