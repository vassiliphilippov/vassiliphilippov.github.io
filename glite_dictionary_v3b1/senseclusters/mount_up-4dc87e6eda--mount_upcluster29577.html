mount up

verb
get onto the back of a horse or similar
4.7
Pronunciation: / maʊnt ʌp / (AmE), / maʊnt ʌp / (BrE)
to get up onto the back of an animal, especially a horse, or onto something similar for riding

Examples

To mount up a horse
He watched the others mount up on their horses in preparation for the parade.
She quickly mounted up and rode off towards the stables.
The instructor told us to mount up before the lesson began.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dismount - to get down from a horse or similar
