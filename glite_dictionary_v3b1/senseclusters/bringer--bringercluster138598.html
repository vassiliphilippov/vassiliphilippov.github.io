bringer

noun
person who delivers
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪŋər / (AmE), / ˈbrɪŋə / (BrE)
a person or thing that delivers, causes, or introduces something

Examples

The bringer of hope
He was seen as the bringer of hope to the struggling community.
The dark clouds were a bringer of rain for the dry fields.
In many stories, a hero is the bringer of change.
The festival is said to honor the bringer of spring and new life.
She was called the bringer of hope by her community after the disaster.
In many stories, a mysterious bringer of gifts appears during the night.

  • synonym: messenger - a person who carries and delivers messages
