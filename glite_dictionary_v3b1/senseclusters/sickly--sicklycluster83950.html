sickly

adjective
often ill; unhealthy-looking
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪkli / (AmE), / ˈsɪkli / (BrE)
often ill or looking weak and unhealthy

Examples

A sickly smile

The sickly child missed a lot of school days due to frequent colds.
The old building had a sickly smell that made us hurry our visit.
After weeks of illness, his sickly appearance worried his family.
She gave me a sickly smile before excusing herself to rest.
The sickly child rarely played outside with the others.
