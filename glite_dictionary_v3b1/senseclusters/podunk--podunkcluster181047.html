podunk

adjective
small and unimportant
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊdʌŋk / (AmE), / ˈpəʊdʌŋk / (BrE)
informal, describing a place as small, unimportant, or typical of a remote or unsophisticated town

Examples

A podunk town
He grew up in a podunk town where everyone knew each other.
They dismissed her idea as something only a podunk school would try.
After moving from a podunk area, city life felt overwhelming to him.

Related Terms

  • synonym: backwater - a place regarded as being unimportant or isolated
  • antonym: metropolitan - relating to a large, important city
