interventricular foramen

noun
opening connecting brain ventricles
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərvɛnˈtrɪkjələr fəˈreɪmən / (AmE), / ˌɪntəvɛnˈtrɪkjʊlə fəˈreɪmən / (BrE)
either of the small openings connecting the third ventricle of the diencephalon with each lateral ventricle of the brain

Examples

The interventricular foramen opening
A tumor near the interventricular foramen can impede the normal movement of brain fluids.
During the procedure, the surgeon examined the patient's interventricular foramen for any signs of blockage.
The interventricular foramen allows cerebrospinal fluid to flow between the lateral and third ventricles.
