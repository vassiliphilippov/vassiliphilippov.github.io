Novatianism

noun
early Christian sect
9.9
Pronunciation: /noʊ.veɪ.ˈʃə.nɪ.zəm/ /; / /noʊ.ˈveɪ.ʃə.nɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /nəʊ.veɪ.ˈʃə.nɪ.zəm/ /; / /nəʊ.ˈveɪ.ʃə.nɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
a Christian sect and doctrine in the 3rd century that refused to readmit Christians who had lapsed or committed serious sins after baptism
