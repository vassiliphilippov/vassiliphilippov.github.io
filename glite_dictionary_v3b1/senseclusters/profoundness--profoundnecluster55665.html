profoundness

noun
intellectual depth or insight
Pronunciation: / prəˈfaʊnd.nəs /; / prəˈfaʊndnəs / (AmE), / prəˈfaʊnd.nɪs /; / prəˈfaʊndnɪs /; / prəˈfaʊnd.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of having great intellectual depth, insight, wisdom, seriousness, or intensity
