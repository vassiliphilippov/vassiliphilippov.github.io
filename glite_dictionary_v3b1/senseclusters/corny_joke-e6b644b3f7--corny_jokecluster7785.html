corny joke

noun
an unoriginal or predictable joke
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrni ʤoʊk / (AmE), / ˈkɔːni dʒəʊk / (BrE)
a joke that is silly, old-fashioned, or so overused that it is considered unoriginal or amusingly predictable

Examples

A corny joke
After the presentation, the speaker lightened the mood with a corny joke about computers.
She couldn't help but laugh at his latest corny joke, even though it was so predictable.
Whenever my uncle visits, he always tells a corny joke that makes everyone roll their eyes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dad joke - a simple, often predictable joke, typically told by fathers
  • antonym: witty remark - a clever or quick-witted comment
