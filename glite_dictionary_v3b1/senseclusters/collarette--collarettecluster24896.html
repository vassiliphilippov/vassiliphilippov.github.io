collarette

noun
neck ribbon for medals
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑləˈrɛt / (AmE), / ˌkɒləˈrɛt / (BrE)
a short ribbon or band worn around the neck to support a medal, badge, or insignia

Examples

Medal collarette
The knight wore a collarette to display his newly awarded medal at the ceremony.
Each recipient received a collarette to hold their badge during the official event.
She carefully fastened the collarette around her neck before stepping onto the stage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: necklace - a piece of jewelry worn around the neck
  • synonym: ribbon - a narrow strip of fabric used for decoration or to support something
