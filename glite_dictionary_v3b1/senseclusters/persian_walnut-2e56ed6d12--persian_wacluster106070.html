Persian walnut

noun
Eurasian tree with edible nuts and valued wood
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrʒən ˈwɔlnʌt / (AmE), / ˈpɜːʃən ˈwɔːlnʌt / (BrE)
A Eurasian walnut tree (Juglans regia) valued for its large edible nuts and hard, richly patterned wood; widely cultivated.

Examples

The Persian walnut tree
She enjoys the mild flavor of Persian walnuts in her baking.
The Persian walnut is grown in many countries for both its nuts and timber.
The furniture was made from the richly colored wood of the Persian walnut.
