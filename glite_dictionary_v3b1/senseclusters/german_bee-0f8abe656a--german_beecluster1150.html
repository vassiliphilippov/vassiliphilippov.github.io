German bee

noun
variety of honeybee from Germany
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɝːmən biː / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːmən biː / (BrE)
a dark-colored, ill-tempered variety of honeybee, originally bred in Germany, used in beekeeping

Examples

The German bee species
Many early American beekeepers imported the German bee due to its hardiness.
The German bee is known for its defensive behavior and tendency to swarm.
Unlike the Italian bee, the German bee can be difficult to manage in modern apiaries.
