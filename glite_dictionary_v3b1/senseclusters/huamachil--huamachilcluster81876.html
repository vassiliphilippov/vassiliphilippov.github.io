huamachil

noun
tropical American tree with edible pods and useful timber
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /wɑː.mə.ˈtʃɪl/ /; / /wɑː.mə.ˈtʃiːl/ (AmE), /wɑː.mə.ˈtʃɪl/ /; / /wɑː.mə.ˈtʃiːl/ (BrE)
