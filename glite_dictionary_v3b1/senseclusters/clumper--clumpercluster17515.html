clumper

noun
one who forms clumps
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈklʌmpər / (AmE), / ˈklʌmpə / (BrE)
a person or thing that forms, gathers, or moves in clumps or groups

Examples

A clumper of crops
The new machine worked as a clumper, efficiently grouping the harvested crops together.
In the forest, a clumper of mushrooms grew tightly packed at the base of the tree.
He acted as the clumper on the team, bringing everyone together for the group photo.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gatherer - a person or thing that collects or brings things together
  • antonym: scatterer - a person or thing that spreads things apart
