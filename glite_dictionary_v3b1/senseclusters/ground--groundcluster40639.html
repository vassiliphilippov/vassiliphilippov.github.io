ground

verb
apply a primer to a surface
6.5
Pronunciation: / ɡraʊnd / (AmE), / ɡraʊnd / (BrE)
to cover a surface, especially a canvas or panel, with a primer as preparation for painting

Examples

To ground a canvas
He grounds each panel with a special gesso for durability.
The artist carefully ground the canvas before starting her masterpiece.
You should always ground the wood with primer to ensure the paint adheres well.
