macular

adjective
relating to macules
Pronunciation: / ˈmækjələr / (AmE), / ˈmakjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or marked by macules, which are flat, discolored spots on the skin.

Examples

Macular rash
The dermatologist identified a macular rash on the patient's back.
A macular eruption can be a sign of an allergic reaction.
The child developed several macular spots after the illness began.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - free from spots or marks
  • synonym: maculate - spotted or stained; marked with spots
