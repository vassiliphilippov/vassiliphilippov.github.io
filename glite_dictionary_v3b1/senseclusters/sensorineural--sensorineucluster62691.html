sensorineural

adjective
relating to sensory nerves or sensation
5.9 7.2
Pronunciation: /sen.sə.rɪ.ˈnjʊr.əl/ (AmE), /sen.sə.rɪ.ˈnjʊə.rəl/ (BrE)
relating to or involving the sensory nerves or the neural process of sensation, especially used in reference to sensory organs such as the ear.

Examples

Sensorineural hearing loss
Damage to the sensorineural pathways can result in loss of certain sensory perceptions.
Specialists often distinguish between conductive and sensorineural types of hearing impairment.
The patient was diagnosed with sensorineural hearing loss after a series of tests.
