cycloolefin

noun
cyclic hydrocarbon with double bonds, non-aromatic
9.9
Pronunciation: /saɪ.kloʊ.ˈoʊ.lə.fɪn/ /; / /saɪ.kloʊ.ˈoʊ.lɪ.fɪn/ (AmE), /saɪ.klə.ˈəʊ.lɪ.fɪn/ /; / /saɪ.klə.ˈəʊ.lə.fɪn/ (BrE)
