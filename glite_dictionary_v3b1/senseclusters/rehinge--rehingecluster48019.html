rehinge

verb
to attach hinges again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈhɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈhɪndʒ / (BrE)
to attach new hinges to something again, usually after removing the old ones

Examples

To rehinge cabinet doors
The carpenter is rehinging the old cabinet doors to make them open smoothly.
After painting the door, she spent the afternoon rehinging it with new hardware.
They decided to rehinge the windows to improve their alignment and function.
After years of use, the carpenter had to rehinge the old cabinet doors.
They decided to rehinge the window to make it open more smoothly.
The antique box was restored when a craftsman rehinged its broken lid.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rehang - to hang something again, especially after removing it
  • antonym: unhinge - to remove the hinges from something
