report on

verb
give a detailed account of something
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpɔrt ɑn / (AmE), / rɪˈpɔːt ɒn / (BrE)
to give a detailed or formal account or description of something, especially as a journalist or an official observer.

Examples

To report on an event
Investigators are expected to report on the cause of the accident by next week.
She was asked to report on the progress of the community project at the meeting.
The local newspaper will report on the results of the election tomorrow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cover - to report the details of an event or subject as a journalist
