bomb

verb
attack with bombs
3.1 11/m
Pronunciation: / bÉ‘m / (AmE), / bÉ’m / (BrE)
to attack a place or target by dropping bombs on it

Examples

To bomb a city
During World War II, several cities were bombed heavily.
The air force planned to bomb the enemy's supply lines at dawn.
They threatened to bomb the airport if their demands were not met.
