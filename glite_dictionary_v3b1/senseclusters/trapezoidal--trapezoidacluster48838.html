trapezoidal

adjective
shaped like a trapezoid
Pronunciation: / træp.əˈzɔɪ.dl̩ / (AmE), / trəˌpiː.zɔɪˈdæl / (BrE)
having the shape of a trapezoid; resembling or relating to a trapezoid.

Examples

Trapezoidal shape
Her desk had a unique trapezoidal shape, wider at the back than the front.
The trapezoidal arrangement of the tables allowed more space for movement.
The architect designed a trapezoidal window for the modern house.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rectangular - having the shape of a rectangle
