signal the end

verb
indicate something is finished
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪɡnəl ði ɛnd / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡnəl ðiː end / (BrE)
to indicate that something has come to a conclusion or is finished

Examples

To signal the end
A sudden drop in temperature often signals the end of summer.
The closing bell signals the end of the trading day on Wall Street.
The final chapter signals the end of the book's epic journey.
