nonpolluted

adjective
free from pollution
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.pəˈluː.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.pəˈluː.tɪd / (BrE)

Examples

They chose to swim in a nonpolluted section of the river for safety.
Strict laws have kept the national park's lakes nonpolluted for decades.
