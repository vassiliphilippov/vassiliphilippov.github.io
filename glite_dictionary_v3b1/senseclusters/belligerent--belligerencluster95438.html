belligerent

adjective
hostile or aggressive
Pronunciation: / bəˈlɪdʒərənt / (AmE), / bɛˈlɪdʒərənt / (BrE)
hostile and aggressive; eager to argue or fight

Examples

Belligerent behavior
His belligerent attitude made negotiations difficult.
She responded in a belligerent tone to the criticism.
The customer became belligerent when his order was delayed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aggressive - ready or likely to attack or confront; characterized by aggression
  • antonym: peaceful - not involving conflict or hostility; calm
