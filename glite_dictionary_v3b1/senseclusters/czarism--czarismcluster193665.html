czarism

noun
absolute rule by one
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈzɑː.rɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈzɑː.rɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
A system or period of government ruled by a czar or tsar, especially in Russia before 1917, often characterized by autocratic or oppressive power and sometimes used figuratively for arbitrary rule.
