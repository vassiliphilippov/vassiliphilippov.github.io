Synonyms

  • aid
    1.0 54/m
    aid is emergency help sent quickly to people in crisis, while relief focuses on easing suffering and reducing hardship.
  • assistance
    1.0 65/m
    assistance is formal help that may include money or services for tasks, while relief is aid aimed at reducing people's suffering.
  • help
    1.0 151/m
    help is general support or action anyone offers to make things easier, while relief is targeted aid to reduce hardship.
  • support
    1.0 64/m
    support is ongoing financial or emotional backing that keeps people or projects going, while relief is short-term aid for immediate suffering.
