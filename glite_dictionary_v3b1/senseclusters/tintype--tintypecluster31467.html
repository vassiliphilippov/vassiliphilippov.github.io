tintype

verb
to photograph on metal
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪnˌtaɪp / (AmE), / ˈtɪnˌtaɪp / (BrE)
to make a photograph on a thin sheet of metal using the historic tintype (wet-plate) process

Examples

To tintype a portrait
The artist will tintype your portrait at the vintage photography booth.
Many reenactors enjoy being tintyped in period costumes at historical festivals.
She learned how to tintype after taking a workshop on nineteenth-century photography techniques.
