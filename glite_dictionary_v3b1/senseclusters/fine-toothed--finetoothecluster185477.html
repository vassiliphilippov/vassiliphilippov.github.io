fine-toothed

adjective
having fine, closely spaced teeth
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪnˌtuθt /; / ˈfaɪn tuθt / (AmE), / ˈfaɪnˌtuːθt /; / ˈfaɪn tʊθt / (BrE)
having small, thin, and closely spaced teeth, as on a comb or saw
