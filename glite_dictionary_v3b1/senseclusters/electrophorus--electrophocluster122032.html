electrophorus

noun
device generating static electricity
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑfərəs / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒfərəs / (BrE)
a simple device for generating repeated charges of static electricity, typically consisting of a metal plate and an insulator

Examples

An electrophorus device
An electrophorus can be assembled easily using a metal plate and an insulating material.
In the physics lab, students constructed an electrophorus for their experiment on electrostatics.
The scientist used an electrophorus to demonstrate the generation of static electricity.
