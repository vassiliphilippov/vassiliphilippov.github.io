bandtail

noun
wild pigeon of western North America
Pronunciation: / ˈbænd.teɪl / (AmE), / ˈbænd.teɪl / (BrE)
A wild pigeon found in western North America, often mistaken for the extinct passenger pigeon.

Examples

The bandtail pigeon
Conservationists are studying the habitat of the bandtail to better understand its population trends.
Many birdwatchers often mistake a bandtail for a passenger pigeon.
The bandtail is commonly found in forested mountain regions.
