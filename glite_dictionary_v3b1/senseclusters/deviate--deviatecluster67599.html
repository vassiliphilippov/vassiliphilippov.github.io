deviate

noun
person deviating from social norms (offensive, outdated)
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːviˌeɪt / (AmE), / ˈdiːvɪeɪt / (BrE)
a person whose behavior departs markedly from social norms, especially in sexual matters; now often considered offensive or outdated

Examples

A social deviate
He was labeled a deviate because his lifestyle did not match the era's social norms.
The psychologist wrote a paper on how society views the deviate through history.
Using the word deviate to describe someone is now widely regarded as offensive.
