desexualize

verb
suppress or redirect sexual urges or traits
Pronunciation: / diˈsɛkʃuəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / diːˈsɛksjʊəlaɪz /; / diˈsɛksjʊəlaɪz / (BrE)
To redirect or suppress sexual desires or characteristics, removing or neutralizing sexual content or urges.
