passion

noun
object of strong affection or devotion
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpæʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˈpæʃ.ən / (BrE)
an object, activity, or subject for which someone has a strong affection or devotion

Examples

A passion for music
Music has always been his greatest passion in life.
She turned her passion for baking into a successful business.
Traveling the world became a true passion for the adventurous couple.
