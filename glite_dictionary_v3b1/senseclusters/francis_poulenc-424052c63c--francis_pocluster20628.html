Francis Poulenc

proper noun
French composer and pianist (1899–1963)
Pronunciation: / ˈfræn.sɪs puˈlɛŋk / (AmE), / ˈfræn.sɪs puˈlɑ̃k / (BrE)
(1899–1963) French pianist and composer known for his innovative works in 20th-century classical music.
