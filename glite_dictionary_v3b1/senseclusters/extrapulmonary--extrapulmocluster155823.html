extrapulmonary

adjective
outside the lungs
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstrəˈpʌlməˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəˈpʌlmənəri / (BrE)
located or occurring outside the lungs

Examples

Extrapulmonary symptoms
The doctor was concerned about extrapulmonary symptoms that appeared during the illness.
Some infections can cause extrapulmonary complications affecting other organs.
Researchers studied the effects of extrapulmonary tuberculosis in different patient groups.
