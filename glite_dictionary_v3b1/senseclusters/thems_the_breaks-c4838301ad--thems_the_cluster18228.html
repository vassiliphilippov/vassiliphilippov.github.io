them's the breaks

x
expression of resigned acceptance of misfortune
Pronunciation: / ðɛmz ðə breɪks / (AmE), / ðɛmz ðə breɪks / (BrE)
Used to express acceptance of a disappointing or challenging situation, implying that such setbacks are an inevitable part of life.

Examples

Them's the breaks phrase
I didn't get the job I wanted, but them's the breaks.
We trained hard but still lost the championship—them's the breaks.
Your luggage got lost on your first trip abroad; them's the breaks.
