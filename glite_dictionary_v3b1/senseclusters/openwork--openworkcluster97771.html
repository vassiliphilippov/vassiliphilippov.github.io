openwork

noun
decorative work with patterns of openings
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊpənˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈəʊpənˌwɜːk / (BrE)
ornamental work in which a pattern of openings is created, as in embroidery, metalwork, or carved wood or stone

Examples

Delicate openwork patterns
She admired the lace tablecloth with its delicate openwork patterns.
The artist specialized in wooden openwork panels for architectural decoration.
The window was covered with an intricate openwork wrought-iron grille.
