hullo

noun
greeting; variant of 'hello'
9.9
Pronunciation: / həˈloʊ / (AmE), / həˈləʊ / (BrE)
An expression used as a greeting or to attract attention, similar to 'hello'.

Examples

A friendly hullo
A friendly hullo is often enough to start a conversation.
He gave a cheerful hullo as he entered the room.
She greeted her neighbor with a quick hullo before heading to work.
