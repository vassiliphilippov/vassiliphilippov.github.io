bedroom set

noun
suite of bedroom furniture
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdˌɹum sɛt / (AmE), / ˈbɛdˌɹuːm sɛt / (BrE)
A matching suite of furniture designed for use in a bedroom, typically including items such as a bed, dresser, and nightstands.

Examples

A bedroom set of furniture
The antique bedroom set was passed down from her grandmother.
They found a beautiful bedroom set on sale at the furniture store.
We bought a new bedroom set that includes a bed, two nightstands, and a dresser.
