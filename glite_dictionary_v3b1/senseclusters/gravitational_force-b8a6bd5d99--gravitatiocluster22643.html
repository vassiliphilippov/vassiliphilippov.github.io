Examples

Synonyms

  • gravity
    2.3 24/m
    gravity is the everyday name for the Earth's pull and making things fall, while gravitational force is the scientific term for that attraction.
  • gravitational attraction
    4.1 0.20/m
    gravitational attraction is the pulling action between masses, while gravitational force names the physical cause of that pull in physics.
  • gravitation
    4.1 0.68/m
    gravitation is the formal scientific term for mutual attraction of masses, while gravitational force highlights the measurable force acting on objects.
  • gravitational interaction
    6.8 0.11/m
