devout

adjective
sincerely earnest; heartfelt
5.1
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvaʊt / (AmE), / dɪˈvaʊt / (BrE)
(of a feeling or wish) deeply sincere and earnest; heartfelt.

Examples

Devout hope
His devout hope was that the communities would unite in peace.
She sent her devout wishes for our safe journey.
With devout attention, she listened to their concerns and offered heartfelt advice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heartfelt - deeply and sincerely felt; earnest
  • antonym: insincere - not expressing genuine feelings; not earnest
