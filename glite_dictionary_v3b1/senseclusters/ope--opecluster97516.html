ope

interjection
mild surprise exclamation
Pronunciation: / oʊp / (AmE)
an informal exclamation used to express mild surprise, a quick apology, or to get someone's attention, especially in the US Midwest.

Examples

Ope, sorry!
He said ope as he accidentally stepped in front of someone at the grocery store.
Walking through the crowded hallway, she muttered ope when she nearly bumped into a friend.
You might hear people say ope if they drop something or need to squeeze past you.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oops - an exclamation used after making a mistake or accident
  • synonym: sorry - an expression of apology
