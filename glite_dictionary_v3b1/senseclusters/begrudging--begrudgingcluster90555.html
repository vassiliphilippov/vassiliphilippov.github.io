begrudging

adjective
reluctant or resentful
Pronunciation: / bɪˈɡrʌdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / bɪˈɡrʌdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
showing reluctance or unwillingness, often with resentment or envy

Examples

A begrudging compliment
He offered a begrudging compliment after losing the competition.
Her begrudging acceptance of the new policy was clear in her tone.
They gave a begrudging nod of approval, still unhappy with the decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reluctant - unwilling or hesitant to do something
  • antonym: willing - ready or eager to do something
