pacer

noun
horse used to set pace in racing
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪsər / (AmE), / ˈpeɪsə / (BrE)
a horse used in harness racing, especially one that sets the pace or moves with a pacing gait

Examples

Harness pacer horse

The leading pacer set a fast tempo for the rest of the field.
Many harness racing fans enjoy watching a skilled pacer in action.
That stable is known for breeding champion pacers every year.
The pacer surged ahead of the other horses during the race.
