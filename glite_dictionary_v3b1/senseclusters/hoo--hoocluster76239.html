hoo

verb
to make owl cry
9.9
Pronunciation: / huː / (AmE), / huː / (BrE)
to make the low, hollow cry characteristic of an owl

Examples

Owls hoo at night
At dusk, we heard an owl hoo softly from the trees nearby.
The children tried to hoo like owls during their nature walk.
Sometimes, a distant hoo echoes through the forest at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hoot - to make the loud, deep sound of an owl
