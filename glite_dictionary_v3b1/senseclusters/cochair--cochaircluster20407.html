cochair

noun
joint chairperson
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊˌtʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌtʃɛə / (BrE)
a person who shares the position of chairperson with one or more others in a group or committee

Examples

A cochair of the committee
The two cochairs worked together to organize the annual conference.
As cochair of the committee, he shared responsibilities with his colleague.
The board elected new cochairs to lead the upcoming project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chairperson - the leader or presiding officer of a meeting or organization
