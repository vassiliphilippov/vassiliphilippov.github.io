Sicilian

proper noun
person from Sicily
Pronunciation: / sɪˈsɪliən / (AmE), / sɪˈsɪliən / (BrE)
a person from Sicily, an island region of Italy, or relating to Sicily or its people

Examples

The Sicilian welcomed us
The Sicilian greeted us warmly when we arrived in Palermo.
Many Sicilian dishes feature fresh seafood and local ingredients.
She is proud of her Sicilian heritage and traditions.
