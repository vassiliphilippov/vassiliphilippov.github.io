Whig Party

noun
US political party, 1834–1854
7.9
Pronunciation: /wɪg ˈpɑːr.tiː/ /; / /hwɪg ˈpɑːr.tiː/ /; / /wɪg ˈpɑːr.ti/ (AmE), /wɪg ˈpɑː.ti/ /; / /hwɪg ˈpɑː.ti/ (BrE)
a political party in the United States (1834–1854) that opposed the Democratic Party and supported a loose interpretation of the Constitution and high protective tariffs
