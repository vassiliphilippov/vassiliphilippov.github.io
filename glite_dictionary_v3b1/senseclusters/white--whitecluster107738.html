white

noun
the lightest color; the color of milk or snow
1.3
Pronunciation: / waɪt / (AmE), / waɪt / (BrE)
the quality or state of the color that is a blend of all visible wavelengths of light; the lightest achromatic color

Examples

A bright white
I prefer to wear white in the summer because it reflects the sunlight.
She painted her room a bright white to make it feel more spacious.
The bride's dress was a dazzling white that shimmered in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • antonym: black - the darkest color; absence of light
  • degree lower: gray - a color between black and white
