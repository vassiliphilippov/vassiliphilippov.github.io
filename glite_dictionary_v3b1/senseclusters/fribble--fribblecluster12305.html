fribble

verb
to waste time
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈfrɪbəl / (BrE)
to behave in a silly or frivolous way, especially by wasting time on unimportant things

Examples

To fribble away time
Instead of starting his homework, he chose to fribble away the entire evening.
She tends to fribble when she should be working on important projects.
If you fribble during meetings, you might miss crucial information.
