oleoresin capsicum

noun
resin from chili peppers; used in pepper spray
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊli.oʊˈrɛz.ɪn kəpˈsɪkəm / (AmE), / ˌəʊ.li.əʊˈrɛz.ɪn kæpˈsɪkəm / (BrE)
