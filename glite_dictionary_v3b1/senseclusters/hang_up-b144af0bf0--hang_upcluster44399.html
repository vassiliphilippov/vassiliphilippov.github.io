hang up

verb
suspend or attach something from above
Pronunciation: / hæŋ ʌp / (AmE), / hæŋ ʌp / (BrE)
to suspend something from above so that it is held in place off the ground or attached to a surface, often using a hook or similar device

Examples

To hang up clothes
He was busy hanging up the decorations for the party.
Please hang up your coat in the closet when you arrive.
She hung up the new calendar on the kitchen wall.

Related Terms

  • antonym: take down - remove something that is hanging or suspended
