letterhead

noun
stationery paper with printed heading
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛtɚˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈlɛtəˌhɛd / (BrE)
sheets of paper with the name, address, and other details of an organization or person printed at the top, used for official correspondence.

Examples

A letterhead paper
Every formal communication should be on letterhead to ensure authenticity.
Please print the invitation using our organization's letterhead.
The company sent out the contract on official letterhead to make it look professional.

  • synonym: stationery - writing materials such as paper and envelopes
