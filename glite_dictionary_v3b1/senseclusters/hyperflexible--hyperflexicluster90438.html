hyperflexible

adjective
extremely adaptable
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˈflɛksəbəl / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəˈflɛksɪbəl / (BrE)
very adaptable or easily adjusted to suit many different conditions, uses, or situations

Examples

Hyperflexible software
The new software is hyperflexible, making it easy to customize for different business needs.
Our team values hyperflexible plans that can quickly adapt to unexpected changes.
A hyperflexible schedule allows employees to work when it suits them best.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adaptable - able to change or be changed to fit new situations
  • antonym: rigid - not able to be changed or adapted easily
