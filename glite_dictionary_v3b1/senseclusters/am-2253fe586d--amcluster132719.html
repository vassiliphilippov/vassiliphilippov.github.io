AM

adverb
indicates time before noon
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ ˈɛm / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˈɛm / (BrE)
used to indicate the time between midnight and noon

Examples

Before 7 AM
She always wakes up before 7 AM on weekdays.
The train departs at 8 AM every morning.
Their meeting is scheduled for 10 AM sharp.

Related Terms

  • antonym: PM - used to indicate the time between noon and midnight
