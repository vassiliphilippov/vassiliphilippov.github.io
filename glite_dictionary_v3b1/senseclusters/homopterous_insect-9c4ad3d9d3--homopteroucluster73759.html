homopterous insect

noun
insect with membranous wings (ex-Order Homoptera)
9.9 8.6
Pronunciation: /hoʊ.ˈmɑːp.tər.əs ˈɪn.sekt/ (AmE), /hɒ.ˈmɒp.tə.rəs ˈɪn.sekt/ (BrE)
an insect belonging to the former order Homoptera, typically having membranous forewings and hind wings, such as aphids, cicadas, and leafhoppers.
