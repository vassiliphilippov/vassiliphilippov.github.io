adipocere

noun
waxy substance from decay
Pronunciation: / ˈædɪpoʊˌsɪr / (AmE), / ˈadɪpəˌsɪə / (BrE)
a waxy, grayish substance formed by the decomposition of body fat in moist conditions after death

Examples

The adipocere substance
The forensic scientist explained how adipocere forms on bodies buried in wet soil.
After several months underwater, the body was covered in a layer of adipocere.
The presence of adipocere helped investigators estimate the time since death.
