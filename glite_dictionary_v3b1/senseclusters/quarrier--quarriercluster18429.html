quarrier

noun
person who works in a quarry
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔriər / (AmE), / ˈkwɒriə / (BrE)
a person who works in or operates a quarry, extracting stone or other materials

Examples

A quarrier at work
As a young man, he trained as a quarrier before finding work in the city.
Generations of quarriers have worked in the ancient stone pits near the village.
The quarrier skillfully shaped the limestone blocks for the new building.
