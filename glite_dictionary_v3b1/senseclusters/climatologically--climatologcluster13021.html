climatologically

adverb
in terms of climate
Pronunciation: / ˌklaɪmətəˈlɑdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˌklaɪmətəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to the study or science of climate

Examples

Used climatologically
Climatologically, the desert receives less than 250 millimeters of rain each year.
The city is climatologically suited for growing a wide variety of crops.
This area is climatologically classified as temperate, with moderate rainfall and mild temperatures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meteorologically - in a way that relates to the science of weather
