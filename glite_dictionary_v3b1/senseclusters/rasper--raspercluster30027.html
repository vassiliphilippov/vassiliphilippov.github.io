rasper

noun
coarse shaping tool
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈræ.spər/ (AmE), /ˈræ.spə/ (BrE)
a coarse tool with sharp teeth used for shaping or smoothing wood, metal, or other hard materials

Examples

Woodworker's rasper
The woodworker used a rasper to shape the curved edge of the table.
Before painting, she smoothed the metal surface with a rasper to remove any rough spots.
A rasper is essential for quickly removing excess material from hard wood.
