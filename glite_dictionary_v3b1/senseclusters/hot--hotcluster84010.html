hot

adjective
very popular or successful
Pronunciation: / hɑt / (AmE), / hɒt / (BrE)
Very popular or successful; widely sought or in demand.

Examples

Hot trend
She's one of the hot young authors everyone's talking about this year.
That band's new album is really hot right now and selling out everywhere.
Tickets to the show are hot commodities, and they sold out within hours.
