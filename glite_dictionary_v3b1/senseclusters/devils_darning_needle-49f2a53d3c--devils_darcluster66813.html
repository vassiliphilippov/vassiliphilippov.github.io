devil's darning needle

noun
folk name for dragonfly (insect)
6.0 7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdev.əlz ˈdɑːr.nɪŋ ˈniː.dəl/ (AmE), /ˈdev.əlz ˈdɑː.nɪŋ ˈniː.dəl/ (BrE)
A dragonfly; a slender-bodied insect with iridescent wings, often seen near water, whose adults and nymphs feed on mosquitoes and other small insects.

Examples

Devil's darning needle insect
Folklore warns of the devil's darning needle, but it is harmless to humans.
Many people are fascinated by the shimmering wings of the devil's darning needle in summer.
The children watched a devil's darning needle hover above the pond.
