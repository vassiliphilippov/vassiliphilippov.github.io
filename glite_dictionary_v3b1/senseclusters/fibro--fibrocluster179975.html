fibro

noun
fibre-cement material
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪbrəʊ / (BrE)
a building material made from fibre-cement or asbestos-cement, or a house constructed from this material, especially in Australia

Examples

Fibro house
Many older Australian homes were built using fibro because it was affordable and easy to install.
The council warned residents about the dangers of disturbing fibro sheets containing asbestos.
They renovated their old fibro cottage to make it safer and more comfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: asbestos - a heat-resistant fibrous mineral used in building materials
  • antonym: weatherboard - a type of wooden siding used on buildings
