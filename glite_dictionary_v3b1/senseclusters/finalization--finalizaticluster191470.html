(in blockchain and distributed ledger technology) the process by which a block or transaction is confirmed by the network so that it is irreversible or immutable and cannot be changed without consensus.

Examples

Blockchain finalization
In blockchain, the finalisation of a block means its transactions cannot be changed later.
Network validators agreed, so the finalisation of the epoch was recorded on the ledger.
Low node participation delayed the finalisation of several transactions during the network upgrade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confirmation - the act of validating or agreeing that a transaction or block is valid and accepted by the network
  • synonym: finality - the state of being final or settled, especially the idea that a decision or record cannot be changed
