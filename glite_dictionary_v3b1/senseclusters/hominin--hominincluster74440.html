hominin

adjective
relating to hominins
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːmɪnɪn / (AmE), / ˈhɒmɪnɪn / (BrE)
relating to hominins, the group that includes modern humans and their extinct relatives after the split from chimpanzees

Examples

Hominin fossils
The hominin fossils found in the cave provided new insights into early human evolution.
Researchers analyzed hominin footprints to learn about ancient walking patterns.
A hominin skull was discovered, showing features distinct from modern humans.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ape - relating to non-human primates such as chimpanzees or gorillas
  • synonym: human - relating to people or Homo sapiens
