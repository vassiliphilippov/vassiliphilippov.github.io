missing stair

noun
neglected problem or person in a group
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪsɪŋ stɛr / (AmE), / ˈmɪsɪŋ stɛə / (BrE)
a person or issue within a group that is widely known but neglected or deliberately left unaddressed, often resulting in ongoing problems

Examples

The missing stair problem
She realized too late that the overlooked file was the missing stair in her plan.
The manager had become a missing stair in the company, as everyone worked around his unreliable behavior.
When problems persist because they're not addressed, you may have a missing stair in your midst.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elephant in the room - an obvious major problem or issue that everyone avoids discussing
