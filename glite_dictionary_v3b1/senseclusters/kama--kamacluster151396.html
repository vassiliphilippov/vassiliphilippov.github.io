kama

noun
Japanese sickle
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmə / (AmE), / ˈkæmə / (BrE)
a traditional Japanese sickle with a curved blade, used for cutting crops and as a weapon in martial arts

Examples

A kama sickle
The farmer used a kama to harvest rice in the traditional way.
In the demonstration, the sensei showed how a kama could be used for self-defense.
Collectors of martial arts weapons often display a pair of kama in their homes.
