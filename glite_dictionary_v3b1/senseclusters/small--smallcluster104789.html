Synonyms

  • little
    1.0 391/m
    little often feels warm or affectionate when describing size or amount, while small is the neutral, general word used everywhere.
  • modest
    3.3 21/m
    modest suggests moderate size, style, or status and a polite tone, while small simply means not large in size or amount.
  • slight
    1.2 39/m
    slight describes a small degree or effect and often seems unimportant, while small refers to low size or amount.
