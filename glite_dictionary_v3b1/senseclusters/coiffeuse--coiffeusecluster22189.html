coiffeuse

noun
female hairdresser
Pronunciation: / kwɑːˈfɜːz / (AmE), / kwɑːˈfɜːz / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to cut, style, and dress hair, especially in a salon; a female hairdresser.

Examples

A coiffeuse styles hair
As a skilled coiffeuse, she styled the bride’s hair with intricate braids and curls.
The French coiffeuse opened a chic new salon in the city center.
The famous actress booked an appointment with her personal coiffeuse before the gala.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coiffeur - a male or unspecified-gender hairdresser (loanword from French)
  • synonym: hairdresser - a person who cuts, styles, and dresses hair for a living
