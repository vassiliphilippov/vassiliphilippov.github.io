musicalness

noun
quality of sounding like music
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuː.zɪ.kəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmjuː.zɪ.kəl.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or property of having a sound that resembles or evokes music

Examples

The musicalness of sound
Critics praised the musicalness of the film's dialogue.
The musicalness of her voice made the reading especially enjoyable.
There is a certain musicalness to the sound of falling rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: musicality - the quality of being musical, especially skill or expressiveness in performance
