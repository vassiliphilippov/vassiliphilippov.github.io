redistributionist

noun
supporter of wealth sharing
Pronunciation: / ˌriːdɪˌstrɪbjuˈʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌriːdɪˌstrɪbjuˈʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or advocates for the more equal sharing of wealth or resources in society

Examples

A redistributionist leader
The redistributionist proposed new policies to reduce income inequality in the country.
Critics argued that the redistributionist approach would discourage investment and innovation.
As a redistributionist, she supported higher taxes on the wealthy to fund social programs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: capitalist - a person who supports private ownership and minimal government intervention in the economy
  • synonym: egalitarian - a person who believes in equal rights and opportunities for all
