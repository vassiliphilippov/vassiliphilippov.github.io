ebook

adjective
electronic book format
Pronunciation: / ˈiːbʊk / (AmE), / ˈiːbʊk / (BrE)
relating to, designed for, or published in the form of an electronic book.

Examples

Ebook version available
The library offers a wide selection of ebook resources for students and teachers.
She prefers the ebook version because it is easier to carry on her tablet.
Many ebook platforms allow users to highlight text and add notes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digital - relating to technology that uses computers or the internet
  • antonym: print - relating to books or materials produced on paper
