Synonyms

  • rendering
    5.1 0.89/m
    a rendering is a way of presenting or explaining an artwork, while a rendition is used for the actual musical or theatrical performance.
  • interpretation
    4.0 6.96/m
    an interpretation shows one person's choices about style and feeling, while a rendition names the performed version heard by listeners.
  • performance
    2.7 45/m
    a performance is the act of presenting a show to an audience, while a rendition highlights the performer's personal way of playing or acting.
  • representation
    4.5 0.67/m
    a representation is a public staging, often formal and theatrical, while a rendition is any performed or interpreted version in music or poetry.
