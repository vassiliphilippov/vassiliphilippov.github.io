disarmingly

adverb
in a reassuring way
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈɑrmɪŋli / (AmE), / dɪsˈɑːmɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that removes suspicion or makes people feel more comfortable and less defensive

Examples

To smile disarmingly
He smiled disarmingly and quickly put everyone at ease during the meeting.
Her disarmingly honest answer surprised the interviewer and made a strong impression.
The child asked for help disarmingly, making it impossible to refuse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: charmingly - in a pleasant and attractive way
  • antonym: suspiciously - in a way that causes distrust or doubt
