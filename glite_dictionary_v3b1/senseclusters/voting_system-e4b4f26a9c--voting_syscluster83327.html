voting system

noun

(especially in technology and elections) a technical system of hardware and/or software used to collect, transmit, store, or count votes, including electronic and online platforms for casting ballots.

Examples

Online voting system
Citizens voted securely using the city's voting system to submit ballots online.
Security researchers found vulnerabilities in a popular voting system that could allow remote tampering of results.
The county bought a cloud-based voting system to enable absentee voters to cast ballots from home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: e-voting - abbreviation for electronic voting, often including online and machine-based systems
  • synonym: electronic voting system - a system that uses electronic means to record or count votes
  • synonym: online voting - the practice of casting votes remotely over the internet
