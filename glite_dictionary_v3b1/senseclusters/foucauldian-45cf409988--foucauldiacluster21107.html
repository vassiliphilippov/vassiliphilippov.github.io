Foucauldian

noun
follower of Foucault
Pronunciation: / fuːˈkoʊldiən / (AmE), / fuːˈkəʊldiən / (BrE)
a person who supports, follows, or applies the theories and methods of Michel Foucault, especially in academic fields

Examples

A Foucauldian scholar
As a prominent Foucauldian, she often analyzes power structures in her research.
The conference attracted many Foucauldians interested in critical theory and social analysis.
He became known as a leading Foucauldian in the field of cultural studies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: poststructuralist - a person who supports or applies poststructuralism in philosophy or social science
  • synonym: structuralist - a person who supports or applies structuralism in philosophy or social science
