gurney

noun
wheeled hospital stretcher
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɝːni / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːni / (BrE)
a type of stretcher with wheels, used for moving patients in hospitals or medical emergencies

Examples

A gurney with wheels
Orderlies wheeled the gurney into the operating room.
She was lying on a gurney, waiting for X-rays in the hospital emergency department.
The patient was rushed through the corridor on a gurney after the accident.
