eating disorder

noun
medical condition of abnormal eating habits
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈiːtɪŋ dɪsˌɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈiːtɪŋ dɪsˌɔːdə / (BrE)
a medical condition marked by abnormal or disturbed eating habits, such as anorexia nervosa or bulimia

Examples

An eating disorder diagnosis
Her eating disorder was diagnosed during her teenage years, and she received counseling to help her recover.
People with an eating disorder may struggle with a distorted body image and unhealthy food habits.
The clinic specializes in treating young adults suffering from various eating disorders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anorexia nervosa - an eating disorder characterized by self-starvation and excessive weight loss
  • synonym: bulimia nervosa - an eating disorder marked by binge eating followed by purging
