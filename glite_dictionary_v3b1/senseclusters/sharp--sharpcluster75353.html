sharp

adjective
keenly felt, piercing (pain, sensation)
2.2 5.54/m
Pronunciation: /  É…ërp / (AmE), /  É…ëÀêp / (BrE)
(of pain or sensation) keen and intense, often felt as sudden and piercing, as if caused by a pointed edge.

Examples

Sharp pain
A sharp wind made walking along the coast uncomfortable.
She felt a sharp pain in her ankle after the fall.
The patient complained of sharp chest pains during the exam.
