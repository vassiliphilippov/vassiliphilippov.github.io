outbreed

verb

to breed with individuals from outside a particular group or population, often to increase genetic diversity

Examples

To outbreed animals
To prevent genetic disorders, scientists often outbreed laboratory animals with unrelated populations.
Some dog breeders choose to outbreed their dogs to introduce new traits.
Wild populations may outbreed naturally when their habitats overlap with others.
