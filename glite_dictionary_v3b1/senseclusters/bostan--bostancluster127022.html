bostan

noun
small vegetable garden
Pronunciation: / boʊˈstɑːn / (AmE), / bɒˈstɑːn / (BrE)
a small garden, especially one used for growing fruits and vegetables, often found in Turkish or Middle Eastern regions

Examples

A backyard bostan
The family grew fresh herbs in their backyard bostan.
Every morning, she watered the vegetables in her small bostan behind the house.
Local markets often sell produce grown in nearby bostans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: allotment - a small plot of land rented for growing food
  • antonym: field - an open area of land for crops or pasture
  • synonym: orchard - a piece of land planted with fruit trees
