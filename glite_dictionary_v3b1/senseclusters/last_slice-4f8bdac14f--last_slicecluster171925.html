last slice

noun
the final piece left (esp. of food)
Pronunciation: / læst slaɪs / (AmE), / lɑːst slaɪs / (BrE)
the final piece remaining from a whole, especially of food that has been divided into slices

Examples

The last slice of cake
She politely offered him the last slice of cake, knowing he loved chocolate.
There's always a debate about who deserves the last slice when we share a dessert.
Who will get the last slice of pizza at the party?

Related Terms

  • synonym: final portion - the last part of something divided
  • antonym: first slice - the initial piece taken from a whole, especially of food
