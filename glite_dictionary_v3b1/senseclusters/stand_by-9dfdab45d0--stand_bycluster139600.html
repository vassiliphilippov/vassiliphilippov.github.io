stand by

verb
be ready to help or act if needed
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: / stænd baɪ / (AmE), / stænd baɪ / (BrE)
to be ready and available to help or act if needed; to wait in readiness for instructions or action

Examples

To stand by for help
Medical staff will stand by in case of an emergency during the event.
Please stand by for further instructions from the crew.
The rescue team stood by all night, ready to assist if needed.
Please stand by for further announcements from the captain.
She had to stand by while her computer rebooted.
The emergency crew was told to stand by until they were needed.
