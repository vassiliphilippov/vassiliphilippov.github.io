reassuring

adjective
making someone feel less anxious
Pronunciation: / ˌriəˈʃʊrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈʃʊərɪŋ / (BrE)
Making someone feel less worried or anxious; restoring confidence or comfort.

Examples

A reassuring smile
Her kind words were incredibly reassuring during the stressful time.
It was reassuring to know that help was available if we needed it.
The doctor gave me a reassuring smile before the procedure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alarming - causing worry or fear
  • synonym: comforting - giving comfort or consolation
