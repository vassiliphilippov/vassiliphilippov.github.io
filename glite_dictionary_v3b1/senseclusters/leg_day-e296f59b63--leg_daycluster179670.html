leg day

noun
day focused on lower body exercise
Pronunciation: / lɛɡ deɪ / (AmE), / lɛɡ deɪ / (BrE)
a day set aside for exercising the muscles of the lower body, especially in weight training routines

Examples

Leg day workout
Many athletes dread leg day because it's especially challenging but important for overall fitness.
She never skips leg day, knowing that strong legs are key to her running performance.
Tomorrow is leg day, so I'll be focusing on squats and lunges at the gym.

Related Terms

  • antonym: upper body day - a gym day focusing on arms, chest, back, and shoulders
