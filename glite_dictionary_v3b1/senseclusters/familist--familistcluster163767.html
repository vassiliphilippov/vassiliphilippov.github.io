familist

noun
family-focused person
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməlɪst / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪlɪst / (BrE)
a person who places great importance on family relationships and values

Examples

A true familist
As a proud familist, he always made time for his relatives no matter how busy he was.
Her familist values influenced her decision to move closer to her parents.
The community was known for its strong familist traditions and close-knit families.

Related Terms

  • synonym: family-oriented - focused on or devoted to family life
  • antonym: individualist - a person who values independence over group or family ties
