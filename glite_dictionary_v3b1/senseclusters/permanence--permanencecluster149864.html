permanence

noun
the quality of lasting; durability
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝː.mə.nəns / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.mə.nəns / (BrE)
the state or quality of lasting or continuing without significant change for a long period

Examples

The permanence of agreement
He doubted the permanence of the agreement, fearing it would not last.
Many artists strive for permanence in their work by using archival materials.
The permanence of the monument symbolizes the nation's enduring values.

Related Terms

  • synonym: durability - the ability to withstand wear or decay over time
  • antonym: impermanence - the state of not lasting; transience
