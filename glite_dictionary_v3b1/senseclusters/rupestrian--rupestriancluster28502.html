rupestrian

adjective
rock art-related
Pronunciation: / ruːˈpɛstriən / (AmE), / ruːˈpɛstriən / (BrE)
relating to rock carvings or paintings found on cave walls or cliffs

Examples

Rupestrian art
The archaeologists discovered rupestrian paintings that revealed details about ancient life.
Many tourists visit the region to see its famous rupestrian art sites.
Scholars study rupestrian engravings to learn about early human communication.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cave art - prehistoric paintings or drawings on cave walls
  • synonym: petroglyphic - relating to images carved into rock surfaces
