LSD

noun
hallucinogenic drug; lysergic acid diethylamide
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɛl ˌɛs ˈdi / (AmE), / ˌɛl ˌɛs ˈdiː / (BrE)
A powerful hallucinogenic drug derived from lysergic acid, known for producing altered mental states and sensory perceptions.
