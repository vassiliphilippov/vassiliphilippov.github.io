no-frills dining

noun
simple, basic style of eating out
Pronunciation: / ˌnoʊ ˈfrɪlz ˈdaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnəʊ ˈfrɪlz ˈdaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
A style of eating or restaurant service that is deliberately simple, offering only basic food and minimal amenities, without unnecessary extras or luxury.

Examples
