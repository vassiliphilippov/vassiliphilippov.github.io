piloting

noun
running a pilot test

the act of testing or running a pilot version of a new product, service, program, or policy to evaluate its effectiveness before wider release.

Examples

Piloting a new program
The startup began piloting a new payment app in three major cities.
Before a national rollout, the company spent six months piloting the service with select customers.
The school district is piloting an online platform to support remote learning this semester.
