choo

verb
to imitate train sound
Pronunciation: / tʃuː / (AmE), / tʃuː / (BrE)
to make or imitate the sound 'choo', especially like a train's whistle or engine.

Examples

To choo like a train
The children loved to choo as they pushed their toy trains around the track.
He made the train choo loudly to entertain his little sister.
Whenever the locomotive passed, the crowd would choo in excitement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chug - to make a regular, muffled sound like an engine
