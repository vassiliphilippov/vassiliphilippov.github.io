mark

verb
make marks or underline text
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / mɑrk / (AmE), / mɑːk / (BrE)
to make a visible sign, line, or impression on a surface (such as a spot, scratch, or check)

Examples

To mark a box
Please mark any questions you find confusing so we can discuss them.
She carefully marked each key point with a red pen.
The teacher asked the students to mark the important sentences in the passage.
Please mark each item on the checklist after you complete it.
She carefully marked the correct answers on her exam paper.
You need to mark the tasks you have finished so we can track progress.
After she called everyone, she marked their names off the attendance list.
Once the task was done, he marked it off his checklist.
We mark attendees off the guest list as they arrive at the event.
