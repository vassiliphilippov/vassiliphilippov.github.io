sterned

adjective
with a specific stern
9.3
Pronunciation: / stɜrnd / (AmE), / stɜːnd / (BrE)
having a particular type of stern, especially used to describe the shape or design of a ship's rear end

Examples

Round-sterned boat
The museum displayed a round-sterned boat from the 19th century.
Engineers designed a sharp-sterned vessel to improve speed on the open sea.
A flat-sterned barge is ideal for carrying heavy loads on calm rivers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bowed - having a particular type of bow (front) on a ship
