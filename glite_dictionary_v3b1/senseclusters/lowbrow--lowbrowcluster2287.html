lowbrow

noun
person uninterested in intellectual pursuits
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊˌbraʊ / (AmE), / ˈləʊˌbraʊ / (BrE)
a person who is not interested in or does not appreciate intellectual or artistic matters

Examples

A lowbrow person
Although she loved pop culture, she resented being dismissed as a lowbrow.
He was criticized as a lowbrow with no appreciation for classical music or fine art.
The museum hopes to attract more than just lowbrow visitors interested in blockbuster exhibitions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: highbrow - a person with intellectual or cultured interests
  • synonym: philistine - a person who is indifferent or hostile to culture and the arts
