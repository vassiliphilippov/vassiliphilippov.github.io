Oma

noun
grandmother (German/Dutch)
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊmɑ / (AmE), / ˈəʊmɑ / (BrE)
a term for grandmother, especially used in families with German or Dutch heritage

Examples

Oma bakes
Every Sunday, we have lunch with my Oma.
My Oma taught me how to bake traditional German bread.
Whenever I visit, Oma tells stories about her childhood in Europe.
