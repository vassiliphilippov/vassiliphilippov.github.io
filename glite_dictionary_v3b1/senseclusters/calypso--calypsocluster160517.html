calypso

noun
Caribbean music style
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɪpsoʊ / (AmE), / kəˈlɪpsəʊ / (BrE)
a lively style of Caribbean music with rhythmic beats and often improvised, topical lyrics

Examples

Lively calypso music
The band played a lively calypso that got everyone on the dance floor.
She learned to play traditional calypso rhythms on the steel drums.
Many festivals in the Caribbean feature energetic calypso performances.
The steel band played a traditional calypso that filled the room with energy.
She learned to dance to calypso music during her trip to Trinidad.
Many calypsos tell stories about current events and local life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reggae - a popular music genre from Jamaica with a strong rhythm
  • synonym: soca - a genre of Caribbean music that blends soul and calypso
