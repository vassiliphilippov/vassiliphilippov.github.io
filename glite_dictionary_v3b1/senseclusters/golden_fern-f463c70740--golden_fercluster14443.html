golden fern

noun
tropical fern with golden undersides
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldən fɝn / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən fɜːn / (BrE)
a tropical American fern with golden coloring or golden sporangia on the underside of its fronds
