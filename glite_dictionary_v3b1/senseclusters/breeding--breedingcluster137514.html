breeding

noun
production of animals or plants by controlled mating
Pronunciation: / ˈbriːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbriːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of producing offspring, especially by controlled mating or hybridization in animals or plants

Examples

Animal breeding
Advances in plant breeding have improved yields and nutritional quality.
Selective breeding has led to new varieties of crops that are more resistant to disease.
The farm specializes in the breeding of rare cattle breeds.
Advances in science have improved our understanding of animal breeding and genetics.
Spring is the most active breeding season for many bird species.
The breeding of endangered species in captivity has helped to prevent their extinction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crossbreeding - the process of mating animals or plants of different breeds, varieties, or species
  • synonym: reproduction - the process by which living things produce offspring
