accommodate

verb
have enough room or space for
2.0 5.5
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈkɑː.mə.deɪt/ (AmE), /ə.ˈkɒ.mə.deɪt/ (BrE)
to provide space or a place for people or things; to have enough room for someone or something

Examples

We try to accommodate our guests' individual dietary requirements whenever possible.
