adamantium

noun
fictional strong metal
Pronunciation: / ˌædəˈmæntiəm / (AmE), / ˌædəˈmæntiəm / (BrE)
a fictional, indestructible metal featured in comic books and movies, especially in superhero stories

Examples

Adamantium claws
The villain tried to break the adamantium cage, but it was impossible.
Her shield was said to be made of pure adamantium in the story.
Scientists in the movie dreamed of creating adamantium for its legendary strength.
