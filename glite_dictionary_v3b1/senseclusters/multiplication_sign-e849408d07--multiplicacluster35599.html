multiplication sign

noun
symbol (×) for multiplication
7.1

Examples

The multiplication sign symbol
Be careful not to confuse the letter x with the multiplication sign when solving problems.
In mathematical equations, the multiplication sign is often represented by the symbol ×.
Some calculators use an asterisk instead of the multiplication sign for entering products.
