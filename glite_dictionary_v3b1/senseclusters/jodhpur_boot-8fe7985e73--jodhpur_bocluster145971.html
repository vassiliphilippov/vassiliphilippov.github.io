jodhpur boot

noun
ankle-high riding boot with side buckle
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑd.pɚ buːt / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒd.pə buːt / (BrE)
A short riding boot, typically ankle-length, that fastens with a strap and buckle at the side, worn especially for horseback riding.

Examples

A pair of jodhpur boots
For the horse show, she wore a new pair of jodhpur boots that matched her riding outfit.
He cleaned his jodhpur boots thoroughly before heading to the stable.
The shop specializes in equestrian gear, including jodhpur boots and breeches.
