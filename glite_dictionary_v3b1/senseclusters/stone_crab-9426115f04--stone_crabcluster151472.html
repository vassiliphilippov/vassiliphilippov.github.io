stone crab

noun
large edible crab of southern US coast
3.3
Pronunciation: / stoʊn kræb / (AmE), / stəʊn kræb /; / stəʊn krab / (BrE)
a large edible crab found in Florida and the southern United States, known for its hard shell and prized as seafood
