Synonyms

  • quiver
    4.5 5.34/m
    quiver is to shake with small, rapid movements often in the body or voice, while waver is an unsteady, trembling motion.
  • tremble
    2.5 18/m
    tremble means to shake slightly, often from fear or weakness, while waver implies a more unsteady, wavering motion.
  • quaver
    8.1 0.57/m
    quaver is to produce a trembling sound in the voice, while waver covers shaky motion or sound in objects and people.
  • shiver
    1.2 13/m
    shiver is to shake because of cold or fear with quick tremors, while waver means an unsteady, trembling motion in many contexts.
  • wobble
    3.8 3.50/m
    wobble is to move unsteadily side to side in an irregular way, while waver means a general unsteady or trembling motion.
  • shake
    1.0 50/m
    shake is to move or cause fast back-and-forth motion, while waver means a softer, unsteady trembling movement.
  • vibrate
    2.8 6.01/m
    vibrate is to move quickly with small, regular back-and-forth motions, while waver is an irregular, hesitant shaking or sound.
  • sway
    2.4 8.40/m
    sway is to move slowly and rhythmically from side to side, while waver is an unsteady, trembling motion that lacks steady rhythm.
