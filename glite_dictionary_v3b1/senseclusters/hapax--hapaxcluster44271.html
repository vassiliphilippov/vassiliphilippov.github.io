hapax

noun
unique occurrence
Pronunciation: / ˈhæpæks / (AmE), / ˈhæpaks / (BrE)
an occurrence, event, or item that happens or appears only once; a unique instance

Examples

A hapax event
The rare bird sighting was a true hapax for the region's ornithologists.
In the manuscript, the word appears as a hapax, making its meaning difficult to determine.
Winning the lottery was a hapax in his otherwise ordinary life.
