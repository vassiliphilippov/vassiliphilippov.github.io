ABC

noun
basic principles
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪˌbiːˈsiː / (AmE), / ˌeɪˌbiːˈsiː / (BrE)
the basic or fundamental principles of a subject, often used in the phrase 'the ABC of something'

Examples

ABC of cooking
Before you can master chess, you need to learn the ABC of the game.
The course covers the ABC of financial management for beginners.
He explained the ABC of photography to the new students.

Related Terms

  • synonym: basics - the essential facts or principles of a subject
  • antonym: details - the small parts or features of something, as opposed to the basics
  • synonym: fundamentals - the most important and basic parts of something
