relationality

noun
focus on relationships
8.7
Pronunciation: / rɪˌleɪʃəˈnæləti / (AmE), / rɪˌleɪʃəˈnæləti /; / rɪˌleɪʃəˈnælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or idea that things, people, or concepts are defined by their relationships or connections with others

The concept of relationality challenges the idea that people exist independently of their social connections.

Modern psychology often emphasizes relationality as a key factor in mental health.
The artist's work explores relationality through the connections between shapes and colors.
