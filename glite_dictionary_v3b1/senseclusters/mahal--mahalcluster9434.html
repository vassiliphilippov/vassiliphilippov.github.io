mahal

noun
land revenue unit
Pronunciation: / məˈhɑl / (AmE), / məˈhɑːl / (BrE)
a unit of land or estate used for revenue, administration, or taxation in historical South Asian records

Examples

Colonial mahal
Each mahal was responsible for paying taxes to the colonial administration.
The records showed that the village belonged to a different mahal after the reforms.
Historians study the boundaries of each mahal to understand land distribution in that era.

Related Terms

  • synonym: estate - a large area of land owned by one person or family
  • synonym: village - a small settlement, often part of a larger administrative unit
