cyanotype

noun
blue photographic print
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈsaɪ.ə.noʊ.taɪp/ /; / /ˈsaɪ.æn.ə.taɪp/ (AmE), /ˈsaɪ.ə.nəʊ.taɪp/ /; / /ˈsaɪ.æn.əʊ.taɪp/ (BrE)
a photographic print made using a process that produces a vivid blue image, often used for artistic or scientific purposes

Examples

A cyanotype print
The artist created a stunning cyanotype of leaves collected from the garden.
Many early engineers used the cyanotype process to reproduce technical drawings.
She framed a cyanotype of her favorite flower to hang in the hallway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: photograph - an image made by a camera and developed on paper
