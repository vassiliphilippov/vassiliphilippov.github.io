militantly

adverb
aggressively or combatively
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪl.ɪ.tənt.lɪ/ /; / /ˈmɪl.ɪ.tənt.liː/ (AmE), /ˈmɪl.ɪ.tənt.lɪ/ /; / /ˈmɪl.ɪ.tənt.liː/ (BrE)
in a forceful, aggressive, or combative way, especially in support of a cause or belief

Examples

Act militantly for change
The protesters acted militantly to demand changes from the government.
She spoke militantly about her beliefs, refusing to compromise.
The group militantly defended their position during the heated debate.
She spoke militantly about the need for immediate environmental action.
The group militantly opposed any compromise with the authorities.
He militantly defended his beliefs during the heated debate.

  • antonym: peacefully - in a calm or non-violent way
