have

noun
a wealthy person
Pronunciation: / hæv / (AmE), / hav / (BrE)
a person who possesses great material wealth; a wealthy person, as distinguished from those without wealth

Examples

A wealthy have
As the gap grows between haves and have-nots, social tensions may increase.
Society is often divided between the haves and the have-nots.
The tax policy is seen to favor the haves rather than the less fortunate.
