ticket

verb
to log an issue
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪkɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɪkɪt / (BrE)
to enter or record an issue, request, or problem as a ticket in a helpdesk or tracking system for attention or resolution

Examples

To ticket a problem
The support team ticketed the customer's complaint for further investigation.
All technical issues must be ticketed before the IT department can address them.
She ticketed the request so it would not be forgotten by the team.

Related Terms

  • antonym: resolve - to find a solution to a problem or issue
