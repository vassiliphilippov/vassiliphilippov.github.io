Cossack dancer

noun
performer of traditional Cossack dances
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːˌsæk ˈdænsər / (AmE), / ˈkɒsæk ˈdɑːnsə / (BrE)
a person who performs traditional dances originating from the Cossack people of Eastern Europe, characterized by energetic jumps, squats, and fast movements

Examples

Cossack dancers perform jumps
Many Cossack dancers wear brightly colored costumes and traditional boots when performing.
The Cossack dancer amazed the audience with his energetic leaps and swift footwork.
Young children learned the steps from a visiting Cossack dancer during the cultural festival.

  • synonym: folk dancer - a person who performs traditional folk dances
