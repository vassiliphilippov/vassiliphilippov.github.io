Spenglerian

noun
follower of Spengler
Pronunciation: / spɛŋˈlɛriən / (AmE), / spɛŋˈlɪəriən / (BrE)
a person who supports or follows the theories of Oswald Spengler, especially his idea that history moves in cycles of rise and decline

Examples

A Spenglerian thinker
As a dedicated Spenglerian, he believed that every civilization would eventually decline.
The professor's Spenglerian perspective influenced his interpretation of world history.
Many Spenglerians debate the current stage of Western civilization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: historian - a person who studies or writes about history
  • antonym: progressivist - a person who believes history moves in a linear, progressive direction
