fast-growing

adjective
increasing or spreading rapidly
Pronunciation: / fæst ˈɡroʊɪŋ / (AmE), / fɑːst ˈɡrəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
(of something) tending to increase, spread, or develop rapidly.

Examples

Fast-growing plant
Bamboo is a fast-growing plant that can reach full height in a single season.
Doctors grew concerned about the patient's fast-growing tumor.
The city is known for its fast-growing technology sector.

Related Terms

  • antonym: slow-growing - developing or increasing at a slow rate; antonym of fast-growing
