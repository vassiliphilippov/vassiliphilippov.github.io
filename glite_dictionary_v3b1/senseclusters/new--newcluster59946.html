Synonyms

  • fresh
    1.0 18/m
    fresh often means recently made or unused, used for food or ideas, while new means recently created or original.
  • novel
    5.1 12/m
    novel means original and unusual, often used in formal or literary contexts, while new is more general and common.
  • recent
    1.0 196/m
    recent means happening or starting not long ago, often about events, while new can mean both recent and original.
