unreviewable

adjective
cannot be evaluated
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈvjuː.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈvjuː.ə.bəl / (BrE)
impossible or impractical to examine, assess, or evaluate due to condition, inaccessibility, or technical limitations

Examples

Unreviewable file
The corrupted file was unreviewable by the editors, so they requested a replacement.
Due to missing data, the report was considered unreviewable by the committee.
Some online content is unreviewable because of privacy restrictions and technical barriers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inaccessible - not able to be reached or examined
  • antonym: reviewable - able to be examined or evaluated
