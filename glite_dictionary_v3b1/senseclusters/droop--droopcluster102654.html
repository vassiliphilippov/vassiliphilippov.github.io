droop

verb
hang or bend downward limply
Pronunciation: / druːp / (AmE), / druːp / (BrE)
to bend or hang down limply, often because of tiredness, weakness, or lack of support

Related Terms

  • synonym: flop - to fall, hang, or move in a loose or ungainly way
  • antonym: perk up - to become more lively or cheerful
  • synonym: sag - to sink, bend, or hang down under weight or pressure
  • antonym: stand - to be upright or vertical
  • synonym: wilt - to become limp through heat, loss of water, or lack of energy
