up to

adjective
occupied or engaged in doing something
Pronunciation: / ʌp tu / (AmE), / ʌp tʊ / (BrE)
busy or occupied with a particular activity, task, or scheme; engaged in doing something (often used in questions about someone's recent activities, or to suggest possible mischief).

Examples

Up to something
He looks like he's up to something mischievous again.
I wonder what you have been up to lately.
The kids were suspiciously quiet, so their mother asked what they were up to.
