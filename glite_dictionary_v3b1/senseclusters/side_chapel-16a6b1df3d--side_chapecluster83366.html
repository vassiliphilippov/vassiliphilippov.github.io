side chapel

noun
small chapel off the side aisle in a church
Pronunciation: / saɪd ˈtʃæpəl / (AmE), / saɪd ˈtʃæpəl / (BrE)
a small chapel located off the side aisle of a church, often used for private prayer or special services

Examples
