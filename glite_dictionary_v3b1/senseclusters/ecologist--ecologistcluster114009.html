ecologist

noun
person who studies ecology
Pronunciation: / ɪˈkɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ɪˈkɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies the relationships between living organisms and their environment

Examples

A ecologist studies nature
As an ecologist, she is passionate about wildlife conservation.
Many ecologists warn of the dangers of habitat destruction.
The ecologist spent years researching the rainforest's complex ecosystem.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ecology - the study of the relationships between living organisms and their environment
