storywise

adverb
regarding the story
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔːriˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈstɔːriˌwaɪz / (BrE)
with regard to the story or narrative aspect of something

Examples

Storywise speaking
Storywise, the novel was complex, but the characters were not very believable.
The film was impressive storywise, even though the special effects were lacking.
I enjoyed the show storywise, but the pacing felt a bit slow.
