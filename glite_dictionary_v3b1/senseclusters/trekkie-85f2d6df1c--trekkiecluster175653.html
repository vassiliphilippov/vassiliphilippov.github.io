Trekkie

noun
Star Trek fan
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛki / (AmE), / ˈtrɛki / (BrE)
a devoted or enthusiastic fan of the science fiction television series Star Trek

Many Trekkies dress up as their favorite characters at fan events.
As a lifelong Trekkie, she owns a large collection of memorabilia from the series.
