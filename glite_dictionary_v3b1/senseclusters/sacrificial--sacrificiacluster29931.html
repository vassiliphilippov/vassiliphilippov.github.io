sacrificial

adjective
relating to or used as a sacrifice
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌsækrəˈfɪʃəl / (AmE), / ˌsak.rɪˈfɪʃ.əl / (BrE)
used in or connected with a sacrifice, especially in a religious or ceremonial context

Examples

Sacrificial offering
A sacrificial offering was placed on the altar at dawn.
The expression 'sacrificial lamb' refers to someone who suffers for the benefit of others.
The priest wore a special sacrificial robe during the ancient ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sacrifice - an act of offering something valuable as a religious or symbolic gesture
