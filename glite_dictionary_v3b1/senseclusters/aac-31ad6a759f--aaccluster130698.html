Synonyms

  • autoclaved aerated concrete

  • autoclaved cellular concrete
    6.7 0.00/m
    autoclaved cellular concrete is an older alternate name found in some sources, while AAC is the modern common name for the same material.
  • autoclaved lightweight concrete
    7.0 0.00/m
    autoclaved lightweight concrete refers to light concrete cured in an autoclave, while AAC specifically means aerated, precast material used as blocks.
  • autoclaved concrete
    7.1 0.00/m
    autoclaved concrete is any concrete cured under heat and pressure in an autoclave, while AAC is a lightweight aerated variant.
