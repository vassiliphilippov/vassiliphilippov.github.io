cymule

noun
small, few-flowered cyme (botany)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪmjuːl / (AmE), / ˈsɪmjuːl / (BrE)
A small, usually few-flowered cyme; a cluster of flowers arranged in a particular way, typically found as a subdivision of a larger inflorescence.
