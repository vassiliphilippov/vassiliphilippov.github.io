Synonyms

  • made-to-order
    3.8 0.2/m
    made-to-order is produced only after a customer asks for it, while custom-made is created to fit a person's exact requirements.
  • bespoke
    6.9 0.3/m
    bespoke is mainly used for clothing and luxury items made by a craftsman, while custom-made is a broader term for items made to order.
  • tailor-made
    4.0 0.3/m
    tailor-made often describes clothing or plans made to fit someone perfectly, while custom-made applies to many objects made for one person.
  • custom
    4.4 6/m
    custom is a short, everyday adjective used in adverts and labels, while custom-made sounds more like a fully handcrafted item.
  • custom-built
    4.1 0.3/m
    custom-built usually refers to vehicles or structures built for a specific use, while custom-made describes items made to a person's requirements.
  • personalized
    4.1 0.01/m

  • individualized
    4.1 0.6/m
    individualized describes adapting services or plans to a single person, while custom-made refers to physically making an object to exact requirements.
