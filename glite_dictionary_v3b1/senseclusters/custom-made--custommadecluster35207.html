custom-made

adjective
made to individual specifications
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌstəm meɪd / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəm meɪd / (BrE)
(of an object) made according to the specific requirements or preferences of an individual.

Examples

Custom-made bicycle
His bicycle was custom-made to meet his unique specifications.
She ordered a custom-made suit for her wedding.
The company offers custom-made furniture tailored to your home's measurements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bespoke - made to order for a particular customer
  • antonym: ready-made - made in standard sizes and not to individual order
