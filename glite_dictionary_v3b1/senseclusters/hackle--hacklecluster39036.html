hackle

noun
animal neck hairs
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhækəl / (AmE), / ˈhækəl / (BrE)
the hairs along the back of an animal's neck that stand up when it is angry or frightened

Examples

Dog's hackles rise
When the dog barked, the cat's hackles rose immediately.
You could see the wolf's hackles standing up as it growled.
The horse's hackles bristled when it sensed danger nearby.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mane - the long hair on the neck of a horse, lion, or similar animal
