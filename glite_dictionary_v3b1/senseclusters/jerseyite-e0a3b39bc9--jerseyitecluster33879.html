Jerseyite

noun
person from Jersey
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɜːrziˌaɪt / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːziˌaɪt / (BrE)
a person who is from or lives on the island of Jersey in the Channel Islands

Examples

A Jerseyite from Jersey island
A friendly Jerseyite welcomed us to the island and recommended local restaurants.
Many Jerseyites celebrate their unique traditions during the annual Liberation Day.
The museum guide, a lifelong Jerseyite, shared fascinating stories about the island's history.
