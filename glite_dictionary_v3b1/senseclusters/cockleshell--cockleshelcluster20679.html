cockleshell

noun
ribbed sea shell
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑkəlˌʃɛl / (AmE), / ˈkɒkəlˌʃɛl / (BrE)
the shell of a small edible sea mollusk, typically ribbed and heart-shaped, found on sandy beaches

Examples

A cockleshell on sand
The children filled their buckets with colorful cockleshells they found along the shore.
A single cockleshell lay half-buried in the sand, glistening in the sunlight.
She decorated the picture frame with tiny cockleshells collected during her summer vacation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cockle - a small edible marine mollusk with a ribbed shell
  • synonym: seashell - the shell of a marine mollusk found on beaches
