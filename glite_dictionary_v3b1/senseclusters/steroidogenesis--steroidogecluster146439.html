steroidogenesis

noun
production of steroids
9.6
Pronunciation: / stɛˌrɔɪdoʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (AmE), / stɛˌrɔɪdəʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (BrE)
the biological process by which steroids are produced in the body

Examples

Steroidogenesis process
Scientists are investigating steroidogenesis to better understand hormone imbalances.
Certain medications can affect steroidogenesis in the adrenal glands.
A disruption in steroidogenesis may lead to various endocrine disorders.
