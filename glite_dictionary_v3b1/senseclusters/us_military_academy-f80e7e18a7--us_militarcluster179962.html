US Military Academy

proper noun
United States Army officer training school
Pronunciation: / ju ɛs mɪˈlɪtɛri əˈkædəmi / (AmE), / juː ɛs mɪˈlɪtəri əˈkadəmi / (BrE)
A federally funded institution for training men and women to become officers in the United States Army, often referred to as West Point.

Examples

The US Military Academy at West Point
Admission to the US Military Academy is highly competitive.
Many famous generals have studied at the US Military Academy.
She graduated from the US Military Academy in 2012.
