cubage

noun
volume measurement
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːbɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkjuːbɪdʒ / (BrE)
the measurement of the volume or cubic capacity of an object or space, especially for shipping or storage

Examples

The cubage of boxes
The shipping company calculated the cubage of each box to determine the total space needed.
Accurate cubage is essential when planning how to load goods into a storage unit.
Before moving, they measured the cubage of their furniture to ensure it would fit in the truck.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capacity - the maximum amount that something can contain
  • synonym: volume - the amount of space that something occupies
