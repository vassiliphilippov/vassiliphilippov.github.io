sink plug

noun
device blocks water draining from sinks or baths
5.0 2.2
Pronunciation: / sɪŋk plʌɡ / (AmE), / sɪŋk plʌɡ / (BrE)
A device used to block the drain of a sink, washbasin, or bath, preventing water from escaping.

Examples

A sink plug device
Always ensure the sink plug is pushed in tightly before filling the basin with water.
She pulled out the sink plug to let the water drain away.
The old sink plug was replaced because it had started leaking.
