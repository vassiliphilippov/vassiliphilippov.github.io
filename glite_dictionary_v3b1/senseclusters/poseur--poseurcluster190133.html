poseur

noun
person pretending to be something they are not
4.2 7.9
Pronunciation: /poʊ.ˈzɜːr/ /; / /pə.ˈzɜːr/ /; / /poʊ.ˈzʊr/ (AmE), /pə.ˈzɜː/ /; / /poʊ.ˈzɜː/ /; / /pə.ˈzʊə/ (BrE)
a person who pretends to be what they are not, often to impress others or fit in with a group

Examples

Many thought he was a poseur because he only dressed that way to fit in.
