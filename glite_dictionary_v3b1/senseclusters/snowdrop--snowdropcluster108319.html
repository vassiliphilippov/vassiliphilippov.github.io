snowdrop

noun
early spring flower
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊˌdrɑp / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌdrɒp / (BrE)
a small white flower that blooms in early spring, often appearing while snow is still on the ground

Examples

A snowdrop flower
The first snowdrop appeared in the garden at the end of winter.
Children were excited to spot a single snowdrop poking through the melting snow.
Each year, the arrival of the snowdrops signals that spring is near.
