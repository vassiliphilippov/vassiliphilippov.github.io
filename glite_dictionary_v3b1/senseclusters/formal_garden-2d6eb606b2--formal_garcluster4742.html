formal garden

noun
garden with symmetrical, geometric design
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrməl ˈɡɑrdn̩ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːməl ˈɡɑːdn̩ / (BrE)
A garden designed with regular, symmetrical layouts and geometric patterns, often featuring neatly arranged plants and precise lines.

Examples

A formal garden design
Each formal garden was designed to emphasize balance and perfect symmetry.
The palace grounds feature a beautifully maintained formal garden with neatly trimmed hedges and gravel paths.
Visitors admired the elaborate patterns in the formal garden at the heart of the estate.
