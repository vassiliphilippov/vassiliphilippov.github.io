family Muscicapidae

noun
family of Old World flycatchers (birds)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli ˌmʌsɪkəˈpeɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ˌmʌsɪkəˈpeɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of passerine birds known as the Old World or true flycatchers, found mainly in Europe, Asia, and Africa.
