overnight

noun
one-night stay
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈəʊvənaɪt / (BrE)
a stay at a place that lasts for one night only

Examples

A short overnight
We planned two overnights in small towns along the route.
The tour package includes all overnights and meals.
After several overnights in different cities, they finally reached their destination.

Related Terms

  • antonym: day trip - a trip completed within one day without staying overnight
  • synonym: stopover - a short stay at a place between parts of a journey
