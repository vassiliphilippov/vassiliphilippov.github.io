greige

adjective
gray-beige color
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪʒ / (AmE), / ɡreɪʒ / (BrE)
having a color that is a blend of gray and beige.

Examples

Greige walls
The designer recommended a greige sofa to match both the curtains and the rug.
Many modern homes use greige walls for a neutral and calming atmosphere.
Her favorite sweater is a soft greige that goes with almost anything.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beige - a pale sandy yellow-brown color
  • synonym: gray - a color between black and white
