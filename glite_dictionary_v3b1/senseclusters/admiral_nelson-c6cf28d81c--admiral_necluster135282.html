Admiral Nelson

proper noun
famous English admiral, defeated French at Trafalgar
Pronunciation: / ˈæd.mɚ.əl ˈnɛl.sən / (AmE), / ˈæd.mər.əl ˈnɛl.sən / (BrE)
(1758–1805) English admiral who defeated the French fleets of Napoleon and was mortally wounded at the Battle of Trafalgar.

Examples

Admiral Nelson at Trafalgar
