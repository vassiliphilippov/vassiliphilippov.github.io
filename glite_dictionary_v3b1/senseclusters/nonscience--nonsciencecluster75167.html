nonscience

noun
not based on science
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
a field, activity, idea, or method that is not based on scientific principles or methods

Examples

A nonscience field
The committee decided that astrology should be classified as nonscience rather than a legitimate discipline.
He argued that the proposal was based on nonscience and lacked any real evidence.
Many people confuse nonscience with scientific research, leading to misunderstandings.
The committee rejected the proposal because its methods were rooted in nonscience, not tested evidence.
Many people are misled by nonscience presented as fact on the internet.
It is important to distinguish between nonscience and real scientific research when evaluating claims.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pseudoscience - a system of beliefs mistakenly regarded as scientific
  • antonym: science - the systematic study of the structure and behavior of the physical world
