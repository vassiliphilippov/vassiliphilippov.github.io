superorder

noun
taxonomic rank above order in biology
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈsuːpərˌɔːdə / (BrE)
(biology) a taxonomic group ranking above an order and below a class or subclass.

Examples

A superorder group
Mammals are divided into several superorders based on evolutionary relationships.
Some scientists debate the boundaries between orders and superorders.
The classification of birds places them within the superorder Neognathae.
