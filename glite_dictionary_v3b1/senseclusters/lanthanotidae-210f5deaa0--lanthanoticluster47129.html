Lanthanotidae

noun
family of stout-bodied lizards
9.9
Pronunciation: / lænˌθænoʊˈtɪdiː /; / ˌlænθəˈnoʊtɪdiː / (AmE), / lanˌθænəʊˈtɪdiː /; / ˌlanθəˈnəʊtɪdiː / (BrE)
