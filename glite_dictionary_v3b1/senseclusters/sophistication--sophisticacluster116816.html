sophistication

noun
expert knowledge in a technical subject
1.0
Pronunciation: / səˌfɪstəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / səˌfɪstɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the quality of having a high level of knowledge or expertise in a specific technical or complex subject

Examples

Technical sophistication
Her sophistication in quantum mechanics made her a valuable member of the research team.
The sophistication required to design modern computer chips is truly impressive.
The financial analyst explained the market's sophistication to the board of directors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: naivety - lack of experience, wisdom, or judgment
