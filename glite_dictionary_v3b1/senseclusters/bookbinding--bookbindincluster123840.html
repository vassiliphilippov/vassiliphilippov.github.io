bookbinding

noun
the craft of binding books
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊkˌbaɪndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbʊkˌbaɪndɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or craft of assembling and securing the pages of a book within a cover
