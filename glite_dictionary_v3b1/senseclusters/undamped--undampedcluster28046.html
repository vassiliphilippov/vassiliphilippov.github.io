undamped

adjective
not reduced in intensity
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdæmpt / (AmE), / ʌnˈdæmpt / (BrE)
not reduced in force, intensity, or amplitude, especially in reference to vibrations or oscillations

Examples

Undamped vibrations
The scientist observed that the undamped vibrations persisted for several minutes.
In an ideal system, the undamped motion would continue indefinitely without energy loss.
The bell produced a clear, undamped tone that echoed through the hall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unattenuated - not weakened or diminished in strength
