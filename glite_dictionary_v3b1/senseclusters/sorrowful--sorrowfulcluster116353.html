sorrowful

adjective
feeling or expressing deep sadness
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔːroʊfəl / (AmE), / ˈsɒrəʊfəl / (BrE)
feeling or showing deep sadness, especially because of loss or disappointment

Examples

A sorrowful face
After hearing the news, the community gathered for a sorrowful memorial.
She gave a sorrowful sigh as she read the letter.
The sorrowful expression on his face revealed the depth of his grief.

Related Terms

  • antonym: joyful - feeling or expressing great happiness
  • synonym: sad - feeling or showing unhappiness or sorrow
