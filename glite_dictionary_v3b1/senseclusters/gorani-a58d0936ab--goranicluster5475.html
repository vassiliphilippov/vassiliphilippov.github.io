Gorani

proper noun
ethnic group in Iran/Iraq
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊˈrɑːni / (AmE), / ɡɔːˈrɑːni / (BrE)
a member of an ethnic group from western Iran and parts of Iraq, known for their distinct language and culture

Examples

The Gorani people
The Gorani have preserved their language and traditions for centuries.
Many Gorani live in the mountainous regions of western Iran.
Researchers are studying the unique customs of the Gorani community.
