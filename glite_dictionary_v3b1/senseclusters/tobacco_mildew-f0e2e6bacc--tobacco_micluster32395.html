tobacco mildew

noun
fungus-caused disease of tobacco
6.4
Pronunciation: / təˈbækoʊ ˈmɪlduː / (AmE), / təˈbækəʊ ˈmɪldjuː / (BrE)
a fungal disease that causes a downy mildew to form on growing tobacco plants

Examples

Tobacco mildew disease
Farmers implemented new treatments to combat the spread of tobacco mildew.
Researchers are developing resistant plants to reduce losses from tobacco mildew.
The crop was devastated by tobacco mildew after a period of heavy rains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: downy mildew - a group of plant diseases caused by fungi, resulting in a downy growth on leaves
