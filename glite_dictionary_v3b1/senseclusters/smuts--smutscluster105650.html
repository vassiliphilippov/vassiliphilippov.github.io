smuts

verb
to stain with soot
7.0
Pronunciation: / smʌts / (AmE), / smʌts / (BrE)
makes dirty or stains, especially with soot or a black powdery substance.

Examples

Chimney smuts the wall
The old chimney smuts the white walls every winter, leaving dark marks behind.
If you touch the lamp, the dust easily smuts your fingers.
The factory's smoke smuts the nearby buildings, making them look neglected.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clean - to remove dirt or stains
  • synonym: soil - to make something dirty
