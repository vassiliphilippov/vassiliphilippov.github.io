wise aleck

adjective
sarcastically clever
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪz ˌælɪk / (AmE), / ˈwaɪz ˌælɪk / (BrE)
showing annoying cleverness or sarcastic impertinence, especially in speech or behavior

Examples

Wise aleck remarks
Her wise aleck comments during the meeting annoyed her colleagues.
The student gave a wise aleck answer that made the teacher sigh in frustration.
He often uses a wise aleck tone when talking to his parents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impertinent - not showing proper respect; rude
  • antonym: respectful - showing politeness or deference
  • synonym: sarcastic - using irony to mock or convey contempt
