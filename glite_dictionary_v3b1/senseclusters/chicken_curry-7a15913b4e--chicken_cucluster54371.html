chicken curry

noun
dish of chicken with curry spices
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪk.ən ˈkɝː.i / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪk.ən ˈkʌr.i / (BrE)
A dish consisting of chicken cooked with curry spices, often in a sauce.

Examples

A chicken curry dish
Her homemade chicken curry was a hit at the dinner party.
I learned how to make a spicy chicken curry from my grandmother.
We ordered chicken curry and rice at the new Indian restaurant.
