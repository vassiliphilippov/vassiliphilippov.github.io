muff

noun
a handwarmer tube
7.2
Pronunciation: / mʌf / (AmE), / mʌf / (BrE)
a warm, tubular covering for the hands, usually made of fur or other soft material, into which both hands are placed for warmth

Examples

A fur muff
In old paintings, you can often see ladies holding a fur muff.
My grandmother gave me her antique muff as a keepsake.
She wore a velvet muff to keep her hands warm on the cold winter day.
