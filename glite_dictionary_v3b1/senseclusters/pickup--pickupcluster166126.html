pickup

noun
the ability to accelerate quickly (vehicle)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈpɪkʌp / (BrE)
the ability of a vehicle, especially a car, to accelerate quickly from a stop or at low speeds.

Examples

Strong pickup in cars
Drivers appreciate strong pickup when passing slower vehicles.
Her compact car may be small, but it has excellent pickup.
The new sports model has impressive pickup for highway merging.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acceleration - increase in the speed of something, especially a vehicle
