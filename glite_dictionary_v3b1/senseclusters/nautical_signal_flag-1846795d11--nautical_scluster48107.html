nautical signal flag

noun
flag used for ship-to-ship communication
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔtɪkəl ˈsɪɡnəl flæɡ / (AmE), / ˈnɔːtɪkəl ˈsɪɡnəl flaɡ / (BrE)
a flag used as part of an international code to send signals between ships at sea
