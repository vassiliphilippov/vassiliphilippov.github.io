whooo

interjection
imitative rushing sound
9.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / wuÀê / (AmE), / wuÀê / (BrE)
an imitative exclamation representing a long, whistling or rushing sound, such as wind or a fast-moving object

Examples

Whooo of the wind
The train sped by with a loud whooo that echoed through the station.
As the wind picked up, we heard a haunting whooo outside the window.
A soccer ball flew past my head with a sharp whooo during the game.
