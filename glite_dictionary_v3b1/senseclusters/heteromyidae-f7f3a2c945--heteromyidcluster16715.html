Heteromyidae

noun
family of burrowing rodents (pocket mice, kangaroo rats)
Pronunciation: / hɛtɚˈmoʊ.i.ɪˌdi /; / ˌhɛt̬əroʊˈmaɪɪˌdi / (AmE), / hɛt.əˈməʊ.ɪ.diː /; / ˌhɛtərəʊˈmaɪɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of small, burrowing rodents from the Americas, such as pocket mice and kangaroo rats, with fur-lined cheek pouches and strong hind legs for jumping
