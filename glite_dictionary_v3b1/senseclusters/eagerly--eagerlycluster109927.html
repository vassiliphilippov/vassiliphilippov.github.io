eagerly

adverb
with keen interest or enthusiasm
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈiːɡɚli / (AmE), / ˈiːɡəli / (BrE)
In a way that shows keen interest, enthusiasm, or impatience.

Examples

To eagerly wait
He eagerly volunteered to help with the project.
She eagerly opened the letter to see the results.
The children eagerly waited for the ice cream truck to arrive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enthusiastically - in a way that shows intense and eager enjoyment, interest, or approval
  • antonym: reluctantly - in an unwilling and hesitant way
