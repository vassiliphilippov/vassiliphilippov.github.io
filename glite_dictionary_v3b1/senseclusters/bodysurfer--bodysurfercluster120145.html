bodysurfer

noun
wave rider without board
4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑː.dɪ.sɜːr.fər/ (AmE), /ˈbɒd.ɪ.sɜː.fə/ (BrE)

Examples

An experienced bodysurfer

As a skilled bodysurfer, she could ride even the biggest waves without fear.
Many people watched the bodysurfer glide smoothly to the shore using only his body.
