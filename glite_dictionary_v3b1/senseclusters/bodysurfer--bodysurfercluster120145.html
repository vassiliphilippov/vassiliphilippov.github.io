bodysurfer

noun
wave rider without board
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːdiˌsɝːfər / (AmE), / ˈbɒdiˌsɜːfə / (BrE)
a person who rides waves in the sea using only their body, without a surfboard or other equipment

Examples

An experienced bodysurfer
The experienced bodysurfer caught wave after wave at the beach.
As a skilled bodysurfer, she could ride even the biggest waves without fear.
Many people watched the bodysurfer glide smoothly to the shore using only his body.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bodyboarder - a person who rides waves using a small board
  • synonym: surfer - a person who rides waves on a surfboard
