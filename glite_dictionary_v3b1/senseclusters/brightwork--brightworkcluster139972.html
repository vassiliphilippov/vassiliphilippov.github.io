brightwork

noun
superficial decoration
Pronunciation: / ˈbraɪtwɝːk / (AmE), / ˈbraɪtwɜːk / (BrE)
decorative but nonessential features or details added for show rather than function, especially in a figurative sense.

Examples

Website brightwork
The designer added a lot of brightwork to the website, but it did not improve usability.
Critics argued that the new policy was mostly brightwork with little real impact.
He prefers practical solutions over unnecessary brightwork in his projects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embellishment - a decorative detail or feature added to something
  • synonym: ornamentation - the act of adding decorative details
  • antonym: substance - the essential or most important part of something
