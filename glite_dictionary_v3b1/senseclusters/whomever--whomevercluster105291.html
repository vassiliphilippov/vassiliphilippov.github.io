whomever

pronoun
object pronoun
7.2
Pronunciation: / huːmˈɛvər / (AmE), / huːmˈɛvə / (BrE)
the person or people who receive the action, used as the object of a verb or preposition

Examples

Give tickets to whomever
You can give the tickets to whomever you think deserves them most.
The teacher will help whomever needs extra assistance after class.
Please send the invitation to whomever you wish to include.

Related Terms

  • antonym: whoever - the person or people who do the action, used as the subject
