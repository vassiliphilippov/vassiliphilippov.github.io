revote

noun
second voting round
4.4
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈvoʊt/ /; / /riː.ˈvoʊt/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈvəʊt/ /; / /riː.ˈvəʊt/ (BrE)
a second or repeated vote, typically held to resolve a dispute or correct an error in the original voting process

Examples

A second revote
After a technical issue, the board decided to hold a revote to ensure fairness.
The revote produced a different outcome than the original election.
Members demanded a revote when several ballots were found to be missing.
