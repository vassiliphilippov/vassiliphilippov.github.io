heroic

adjective
of or relating to ancient heroes or legends
Pronunciation: / həˈroʊɪk / (AmE), / hɪˈrəʊɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of the heroes and legends of ancient times, especially those celebrated in epic poetry or myth.

Examples

Heroic legend
His latest novel is set in a heroic age, featuring legendary warriors and great battles.
Many ancient myths are considered heroic stories that have shaped cultural traditions.
The museum's exhibition focuses on artifacts from the heroic period of Greek history.
