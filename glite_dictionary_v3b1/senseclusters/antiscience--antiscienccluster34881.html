antiscience

noun
opposition to science
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
an attitude, ideology, or movement that rejects or opposes science, scientific methods, or scientific consensus

Examples

The antiscience movement
The rise of antiscience movements has made it harder to promote public health initiatives.
Many experts worry that antiscience attitudes are spreading through social media platforms.
Educational programs aim to counter antiscience beliefs by teaching critical thinking and scientific literacy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pseudoscience - beliefs or practices mistakenly regarded as being based on scientific method
  • antonym: scientific skepticism - an approach that questions the validity of scientific claims using evidence and reason
