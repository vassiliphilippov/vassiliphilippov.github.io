air

noun
slight wind; refreshing breeze
Pronunciation: / ɛr / (AmE), / eə / (BrE)
the mixture of gases that people and animals breathe; the atmosphere or sky; a breeze or movement of wind

Examples

A gentle air drifted through the open windows, carrying the scent of flowers.
