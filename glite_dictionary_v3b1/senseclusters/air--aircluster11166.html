Synonyms

  • atmosphere
    3.5 30/m
    atmosphere is the technical layer of gases around a planet used in science, while air is the everyday word for the same mixture.
  • breeze
    1.0 17/m
    breeze is a gentle, pleasant wind you feel on your face, while air means the general mixture of gases and also the sky.
  • airflow
    3.5 2/m
    airflow is the movement of air through or around objects, often in machines, while air refers to the gas people breathe or the sky.
