diphenyl

adjective
with two phenyl groups
9.8
Pronunciation: / daɪˈfɛnəl / (AmE), / daɪˈfiːnɪl / (BrE)
containing or relating to two phenyl groups, especially as part of a chemical compound

Examples

Diphenyl compound
The chemist described the new substance as a diphenyl compound due to its two phenyl groups.
Researchers tested the properties of several diphenyl derivatives in the laboratory.
Adding a diphenyl group can change the chemical's reactivity and stability.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monophenyl - containing one phenyl group
