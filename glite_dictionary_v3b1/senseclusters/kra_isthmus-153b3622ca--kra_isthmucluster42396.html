Kra Isthmus

proper noun
isthmus linking Malay Peninsula and mainland Asia
Pronunciation: / krɑɪsθməs / (AmE), / krɑː ɪsθməs / (BrE)
a narrow isthmus in southern Thailand that connects the Malay Peninsula to the Asian mainland

Examples

The Kra Isthmus land
Some proposals have suggested building a canal across the Kra Isthmus to connect the Indian and Pacific Oceans.
The Kra Isthmus has played a key role in the region's trade routes for centuries.
The Kra Isthmus is considered the narrowest part of the Malay Peninsula.
