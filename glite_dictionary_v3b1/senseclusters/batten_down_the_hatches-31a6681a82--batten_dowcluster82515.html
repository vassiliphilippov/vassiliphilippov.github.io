batten down the hatches

verb
prepare for trouble or difficulty
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt̬.ən daʊn ðə ˈhætʃ.ɪz / (AmE), / ˈbæt.ən daʊn ðə ˈhætʃ.ɪz / (BrE)
