smokeproof

adjective
resistant to smoke
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsmoʊkˌpruːf / (AmE), / ˈsməʊkˌpruːf / (BrE)
designed to prevent the entry or passage of smoke

Examples

Smokeproof door
The hotel installed smokeproof doors to improve guest safety during emergencies.
A smokeproof stairwell can help people escape safely in case of a fire.
All new buildings must have at least one smokeproof exit according to the updated regulations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fireproof - designed to resist burning or withstand fire
  • antonym: smoky - filled with or smelling of smoke
