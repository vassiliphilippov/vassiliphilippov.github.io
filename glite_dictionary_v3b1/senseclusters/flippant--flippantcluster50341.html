flippant

adjective
disrespectfully humorous or lacking seriousness
7.1 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈflɪp.ənt/ (AmE), /ˈflɪp.ənt/ (BrE)
treating serious matters with inappropriate or disrespectful levity; not showing a serious or respectful attitude

Examples

Flippant remark
He made a flippant remark about the accident, upsetting those around him.
Her flippant attitude toward the rules frustrated the teacher.
It is important not to be flippant in situations that require sensitivity and respect.

Related Terms

  • synonym: facetious - treating serious issues with deliberately inappropriate humor
  • antonym: serious - showing careful thought or importance
