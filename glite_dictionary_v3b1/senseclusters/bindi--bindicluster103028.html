bindi

noun
spiky weed seed
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪndi / (AmE), / ˈbɪndi / (BrE)
a small, spiky seed or low-growing weed, especially Soliva sessilis, whose hard fruit can stick painfully into skin, shoes, or tires

Examples

Bindi weed seeds
After playing soccer, I found a bindi stuck in my sock and had to pull it out carefully.
Children often complain about stepping on a bindi when running barefoot on the grass.
The gardener warned us that bindis can quickly spread across the lawn if not removed.
