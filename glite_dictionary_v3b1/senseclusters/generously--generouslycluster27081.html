generously

adverb
in a generous manner
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛn.ɚ.əs.li / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛn.ər.əs.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows readiness to give more of something, especially money or time, than is strictly necessary or expected.

Examples

To generously give money
She always tips generously when dining out at restaurants.
The company donated generously to support the local hospital.
They generously offered their home to us during the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: liberally - in a generous or open-handed way
