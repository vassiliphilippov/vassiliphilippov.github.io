tea gown

noun
long, loose dress for informal afternoon wear
6.8 8.0
Pronunciation: / ti ˈɡaʊn / (AmE), / tiː ˈɡaʊn / (BrE)
a long, loose-fitting dress, traditionally worn by women in the late 19th and early 20th centuries for informal afternoon at-home occasions, especially tea.
