funnel

verb
to pour or channel through a funnel
7.8 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈfʌ.nəl/ (AmE), /ˈfʌ.nəl/ (BrE)
to move or pour something through or as if through a funnel, typically directing or channelling it into a narrow space

Examples

She carefully funneled the powder into the test tube without spilling any.
The company funnels money from different sources into the new project.
