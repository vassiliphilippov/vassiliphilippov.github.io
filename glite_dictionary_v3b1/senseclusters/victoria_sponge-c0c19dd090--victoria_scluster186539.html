Victoria sponge

noun
British sponge cake with jam (and cream) filling
8.1
Pronunciation: / vɪkˈtɔriə spʌndʒ / (AmE), / vɪkˈtɔːrɪə spʌndʒ / (BrE)
a light cake made from two layers of sponge cake sandwiched together with jam and often cream

Examples

A classic Victoria sponge
A classic Victoria sponge should have a layer of strawberry jam in the middle.
Mary Berry is famous for her perfect Victoria sponge recipes.
She baked a delicious Victoria sponge for the afternoon tea party.
