torso

noun
trunk of the body without head or limbs
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔrˌsoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɔːsəʊ / (BrE)
the main part of the human body, not including the head, neck, or limbs

Examples

The human torso

She kept her back straight, tightening her torso muscles during the exercise.
The statue was missing its torso and arms.
