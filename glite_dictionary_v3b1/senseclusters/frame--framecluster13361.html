frame

noun
scoring division in bowling
Pronunciation: / freɪm / (AmE), / freɪm / (BrE)
one of the divisions or periods of play in sports such as bowling or baseball

Examples

Bowling frame
After the fifth frame, he was ahead by ten points.
Each player has two chances to knock down the pins in every frame.
She bowled a perfect game, getting strikes in every frame.
He pitched a perfect frame, retiring all three batters he faced.
That was a wild frame, with both teams loading the bases twice.
The home team scored five runs in the first frame to take an early lead.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inning - division of play in baseball or cricket; period during which a team bats
