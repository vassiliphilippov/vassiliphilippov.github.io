claymation

noun
clay figure animation
Pronunciation: / kleɪˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / kleɪˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
a type of animation in which clay figures are moved and filmed frame by frame to create movement

Examples

Claymation movie
The children's favorite film was made entirely with claymation characters.
He learned how to create claymation videos by moving small clay models for each frame.
Many classic holiday specials use claymation to tell their stories in a unique way.

Related Terms

  • synonym: animation - the process of making movies or videos using drawings or models
  • synonym: stop-motion - an animation technique where objects are moved in small steps between frames
