corniced

adjective
with a cornice
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnɪst / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnɪst / (BrE)
having a decorative molding or cornice along the top edge of a wall, ceiling, or building

Examples

Corniced ceiling
The grand hall featured a beautifully corniced ceiling that impressed all the visitors.
They restored the old house and kept its original corniced walls for historical charm.
Light from the chandelier highlighted the corniced edges of the elegant dining room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ornamented - decorated with additional features
  • antonym: plain - without decoration or ornament
