associationist

adjective
of associationism theory
Pronunciation: / əˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / əˌsəʊsiˈeɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
relating to associationism or the theory that mental processes operate by the association of ideas.

Examples

Associationist theory
The associationist perspective influenced many early theories in psychology.
Her associationist approach focused on how memories are formed through linked experiences.
Some philosophers criticized the associationist explanation of thought as too simplistic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: associative - relating to or involving association, especially of ideas
