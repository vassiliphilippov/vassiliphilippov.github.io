soccer

noun
team sport played with a round ball
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑkər / (AmE), / ˈsɒkə / (BrE)
A team sport played by two teams of eleven players who attempt to score goals by kicking or heading a ball into the opposing team's net.

Examples

A soccer match
Many children dream of becoming professional soccer players.
She plays soccer with her friends every weekend in the park.
The World Cup is the biggest soccer tournament in the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: football - team sport played between two teams of 11 players using a round ball; known as 'football' outside North America
