slop chest

noun
ship's store for crew supplies
Pronunciation: / slɑp tʃɛst / (AmE), / slɒp tʃɛst / (BrE)
a store or chest on a merchant ship where clothing and small items are sold to the crew

Examples

A slop chest on ship
Every merchant vessel had a slop chest stocked with clothing and toiletries for sailors.
The first mate managed the slop chest and kept accounts of all transactions.
When he ran out of soap, he purchased more from the slop chest on board.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slop shop - a shop selling cheap ready-made clothes, especially for sailors
