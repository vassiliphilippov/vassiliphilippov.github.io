eye cup

noun
small vessel for eye washing or medication
9.2 5.4
Pronunciation: /aɪ kʌp/ (AmE), /aɪ kʌp/ (BrE)
a small cup with a rim shaped to fit the eye socket, used to apply medicated or cleansing solutions to the eyeball

Examples

A eye cup with solution
After filling the eye cup with saline, he gently leaned forward to bathe his eye.
She used an eye cup to rinse out the dust from her irritated eye.
The pharmacist recommended an eye cup for applying the prescription solution.
