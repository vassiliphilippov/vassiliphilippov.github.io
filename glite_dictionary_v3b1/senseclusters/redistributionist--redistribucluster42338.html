redistributionist

adjective
favoring wealth redistribution
8.2
Pronunciation: /riː.dɪ.strɪ.ˈbjuː.ʃən.ɪst/ /; / /rɪ.dɪ.strɪ.ˈbjuː.ʃən.ɪst/ (AmE), /riː.dɪ.strɪ.ˈbjuː.ʃən.ɪst/ /; / /rɪ.dɪ.strɪ.ˈbjuː.ʃən.ɪst/ (BrE)
supporting or relating to policies that redistribute wealth or resources to promote greater equality in society

Examples
