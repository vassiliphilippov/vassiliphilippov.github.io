redistributionist

adjective
favoring wealth redistribution
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌriːdɪˌstrɪbjuˈʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌriːdɪˌstrɪbjuˈʃənɪst / (BrE)
supporting or relating to policies that redistribute wealth or resources to promote greater equality in society

Examples

Redistributionist policies
The candidate's redistributionist platform appealed to voters concerned about economic inequality.
Critics argue that redistributionist policies can discourage investment and innovation.
Many social programs are based on redistributionist principles to help balance wealth in society.

Related Terms

  • synonym: egalitarian - supporting equal rights and opportunities for all people
  • antonym: laissez-faire - opposing government intervention in economic affairs
  • synonym: progressive - favoring social reform and new, liberal ideas
