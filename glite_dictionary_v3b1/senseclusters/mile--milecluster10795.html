Synonyms

  • international mile
    3.0 0.00/m
    international mile is the standardized length used in modern measures and tables, while mile is the general traditional unit in use.
  • statute mile
    3.0 0.02/m
    statute mile is the legally defined measurement set by law for land use, while mile is the ordinary name people use.
  • land mile
    2.0 0.00/m
    land mile is the common name used to distinguish the land measure from a nautical mile, while mile is the general unit of distance.
