faithlessly

In a disloyal or unfaithful manner; without loyalty or trustworthiness.

Examples

To act faithlessly
If you promise something, don't behave faithlessly when it becomes difficult.
She acted faithlessly by betraying her friend's secret to others.
The knight served his king faithlessly, conspiring with the enemy in secret.
