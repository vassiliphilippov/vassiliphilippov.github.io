shraddha

noun
Hindu ancestor ritual
Pronunciation: / ˈʃrɑːdə / (AmE), / ˈʃrʌdə / (BrE)
a Hindu ritual or ceremony performed to honor and pay respect to deceased ancestors

Examples

Annual shraddha ceremony
Every year, the family gathers to perform shraddha in memory of their ancestors.
During the shraddha ceremony, offerings of food and prayers are made for the departed.
Many Hindus believe that performing shraddha brings peace to the souls of their loved ones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: memorial - a ceremony to remember and honor someone who has died
  • synonym: ritual - a formal ceremony or series of actions performed in a set way
