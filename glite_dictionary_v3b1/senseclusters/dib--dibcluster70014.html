dib

to make planting holes

to make a small hole in the soil, especially for planting seeds, often using a pointed tool

Examples

To dib the ground
Gardeners often dib the soil before sowing each seed to ensure proper spacing.
She used a wooden stick to dib holes for the tomato plants in the garden.
After you dib the ground, drop a seed into each hole and cover it lightly with soil.
