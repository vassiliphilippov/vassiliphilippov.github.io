broken record

noun
someone who repeats the same thing persistently
3.1 4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈbroʊ.kən ˈrek.ərd/ /; / /ˈbroʊ.kɪn ˈrek.ərd/ (AmE), /ˈbrəʊ.kən ˈrek.ɔːd/ /; / /ˈbrəʊ.kɪn ˈrek.ɔːd/ (BrE)
