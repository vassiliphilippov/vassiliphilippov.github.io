pericardium

noun
membrane surrounding the heart
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛrɪˈkɑrdiəm / (AmE), / ˌpɛrɪˈkɑːdiəm / (BrE)
The membrane consisting of two layers that encloses and protects the heart.

Examples

The pericardium membrane
Fluid can accumulate in the pericardium, leading to serious health complications.
Inflammation of the pericardium is known as pericarditis.
The surgeon carefully examined the pericardium before proceeding with the heart operation.
