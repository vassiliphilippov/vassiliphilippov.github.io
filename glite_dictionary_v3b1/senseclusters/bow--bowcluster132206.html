bow

verb
yield or submit to others
5.5
Pronunciation: / baʊ / (AmE), / baʊ / (BrE)
to yield or submit to someone else's wishes, demands, or authority

Examples

To bow to pressure
Many companies bow to market trends to remain competitive.
She refused to bow to his unreasonable demands and stood her ground.
The government finally bowed to public pressure after weeks of protests.

Related Terms

  • antonym: resist - to oppose or stand firm against someone or something
  • synonym: submit - to yield to another's authority or will
