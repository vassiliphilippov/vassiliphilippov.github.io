showdown

noun
decisive face-to-face confrontation
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊˌdaʊn / (BrE)
a decisive confrontation or contest in which opposing parties face each other to settle a dispute or competition

Examples

The showdown game
The championship game was the long-awaited showdown between the league's top teams.
The two companies are heading for a showdown in court over the disputed patent.
There was an intense showdown between the protesters and the police at city hall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confrontation - a hostile or argumentative meeting between opposing parties
  • synonym: standoff - a deadlock or impasse between two evenly matched opponents
