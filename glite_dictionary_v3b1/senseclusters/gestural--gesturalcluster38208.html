gestural

adjective
relating to gestures or body language
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛs.tʃə.rəl /; / ˈdʒɛstʃərəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛs.tjʊ.rəl /; / ˈdʒɛstjʊərəl / (BrE)
relating to communication or expression using gestures or body movements instead of words

Examples

Gestural communication
American Sign Language is a gestural system used by the deaf community to communicate.
Her gestural expressions made her message clear, even to those who did not speak her language.
The teacher’s use of gestural communication helped the hearing-impaired students understand the lesson.
Her presentation included detailed gestural cues to emphasize her points.
Researchers analyzed the gestural signals used in early childhood communication.
The dancers used gestural movements to tell a story without words.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manual - performed or operated by the hands; often used of communication with hand signs
  • synonym: nonverbal - not involving or using words; not spoken
