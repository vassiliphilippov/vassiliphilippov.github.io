withdrawal

noun
act of taking out money or capital
3.8
Pronunciation: / wɪðˈdrɔəl / (AmE), / wɪðˈdrɔːəl / (BrE)
the act of taking money or other capital out, especially from a bank account or investment

Examples

Bank withdrawal
Large withdrawals from the savings account may require additional identification.
She made a withdrawal of $200 from the ATM yesterday.
You must fill out a form for any withdrawal over $1,000.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deposit - the act of putting money into a bank account
