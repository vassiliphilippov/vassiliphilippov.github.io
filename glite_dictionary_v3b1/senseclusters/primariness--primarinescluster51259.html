primariness

noun
state of being primary
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪmɛrinəs /; / ˈpraɪmərinəs / (AmE), / ˈpraɪmərɪnɪs /; / ˈprɪmərinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being first in importance, order, or fundamental in nature

Many debates focus on the primariness of environmental concerns over economic growth.
The primariness of safety in the workplace cannot be overstated.
Scholars discussed the primariness of certain events in shaping history.
He questioned the primariness of her role in the project’s success.
