backsplash

verb
to splash back
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌsplæʃ / (AmE), / ˈbækˌsplæʃ / (BrE)
to cause liquid to splash back toward someone or something after hitting a surface

Examples

To backsplash water
Be careful when pouring the soup so it does not backsplash onto your clothes.
The powerful faucet can backsplash water onto the counter if you turn it on too quickly.
Rain hitting the pavement may backsplash mud onto your shoes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: splash - to cause liquid to scatter or move in drops
