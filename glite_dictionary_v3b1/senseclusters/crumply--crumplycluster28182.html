crumply

adjective
soft crackling sound
7.6
Pronunciation: /ˈkrʌm.plɪ/ /; / /ˈkrʌm.pliː/ (AmE), /ˈkrʌm.plɪ/ /; / /ˈkrʌm.pliː/ (BrE)
making a soft crunching or crackling sound when pressed, moved, or stepped on

Examples

Crumply autumn leaves
The crumply leaves underfoot signaled the arrival of autumn in the park.
She loved the crumply texture of the old parchment as she turned each page.
His jacket made a crumply noise every time he moved his arms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crunchy - making a loud, crisp sound when crushed or chewed
  • synonym: rustling - making a soft, light sound like leaves or paper moving
  • antonym: smooth - having an even surface or making no sound when touched or moved
