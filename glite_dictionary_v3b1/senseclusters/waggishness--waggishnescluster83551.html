waggishness

noun
playful or witty humor
Pronunciation: / ˈwæɡɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈwaɡɪʃnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being humorous, playful, or mischievously witty

Examples

Child waggishness
Children often display a charming waggishness in their jokes and games.
His waggishness kept the atmosphere light during tense meetings.
She replied with her usual waggishness, making everyone in the room smile.
