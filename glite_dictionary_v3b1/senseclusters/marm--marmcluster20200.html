marm

noun
woman teacher (informal)
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɑrm / (AmE), / mɑːm / (BrE)
an informal or affectionate term for a woman teacher, especially in a school setting

Examples

School marm
The children always listened carefully when their marm read stories aloud in class.
Everyone respected the strict but fair marm who taught mathematics at the village school.
At the end of the year, the students gave their marm a bouquet of flowers to say thank you.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sir - a respectful or formal term for a male teacher
  • synonym: teacher - a person who teaches, especially in a school
