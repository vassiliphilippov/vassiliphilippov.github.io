benchrest

noun
precision shooting sport
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛntʃˌrɛst / (AmE), / ˈbɛntʃˌrɛst / (BrE)
a competitive shooting sport where rifles are fired from a rest on a bench to maximize accuracy

Examples

Benchrest shooting competition
He spent months practicing benchrest to prepare for the upcoming championship.
The club hosts weekly benchrest competitions for both beginners and experienced shooters.
Precision and consistency are crucial in benchrest shooting events.

Related Terms

  • antonym: field shooting - shooting at targets in outdoor, natural settings
  • synonym: target shooting - the sport of shooting at targets for accuracy
