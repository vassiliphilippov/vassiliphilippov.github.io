Pseudemys concinna

noun
species of large river turtle
Pronunciation: / suːˈdiːmɪs kɒnˈsɪnə / (AmE), / sjuːˈdiːmɪs kɒnˈsɪnə / (BrE)
A large species of river turtle found in the southern United States and northern Mexico, commonly known as the river cooter.
