MFR

noun
mean firing rate
Pronunciation: / ɛm ɛf ɑr / (AmE), / ɛm ɛf ɑː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for mean firing rate, the average rate at which a neuron produces electrical impulses over time

Examples

Neuron MFR measurement
The researchers measured the MFR to analyze the neuron's response to different stimuli.
A higher MFR can indicate increased neural activity in a specific brain region.
The experiment required precise calculation of the MFR for each recorded neuron.

Related Terms
