slip

noun
young and slender person
9.4
Pronunciation: / slɪp / (AmE), / slɪp / (BrE)
a young, slender, and often slight person, especially a child or adolescent.

Examples

A slip of a boy
At fifteen, he was still a slip and looked younger than his years.
She was a mere slip, but already showed signs of great strength.
Though only a slip, she fearlessly faced the crowd.
