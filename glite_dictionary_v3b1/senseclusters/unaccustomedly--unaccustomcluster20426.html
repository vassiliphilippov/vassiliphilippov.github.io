unaccustomedly

adverb
in an unusual way
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈkʌstəmdli / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈkʌstəmdli / (BrE)
in a way that is not usual, familiar, or typical for someone or something

Examples

Answer unaccustomedly quickly
He answered unaccustomedly quickly, surprising everyone in the room.
The city was unaccustomedly quiet after the festival ended.
She smiled unaccustomedly at her coworkers, brightening the mood in the office.
