superpatriotism

noun
fanatical or excessive patriotism
Pronunciation: / suːpɚˈpeɪtriˌɒtɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈpætriətɪzəm / (BrE)
Fanatical or excessive devotion to one's country, often characterized by intolerance of criticism or dissent.

Examples

Extreme superpatriotism
His superpatriotism often led him to see any criticism of the government as a personal attack.
Some critics warn that superpatriotism can stifle important debates within a democracy.
The leader's speech was filled with superpatriotism, stirring the crowd to fervent nationalism.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cosmopolitanism - the ideology that all human beings belong to a single community
  • synonym: nationalism - advocacy of political independence for a particular country
  • degree lower: patriotism - love for or devotion to one's country
