sand painting

noun
artwork made with colored sand, often by Native Americans
Pronunciation: / sænd ˈpeɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / sænd ˈpeɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
A picture or design created by arranging fine colored sands on a flat surface, especially as practiced by Native American peoples such as the Navaho.

Examples

Native American sand painting
He studied the traditional techniques used in Native American sand painting.
The museum displayed a beautiful sand painting created by a Navaho artist.
Visitors were fascinated by the vibrant colors of the sand painting during the cultural festival.
