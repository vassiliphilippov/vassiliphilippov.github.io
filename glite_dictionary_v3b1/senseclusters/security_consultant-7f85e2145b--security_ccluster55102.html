security consultant

noun
expert adviser on security measures
2.1
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkjʊrəti kənˈsʌltənt / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊərəti kənˈsʌltənt / (BrE)
a person who advises individuals or organizations on measures to protect property and prevent unauthorized access, especially by installing alarm systems and other security devices

Examples

Security consultant for buildings
As a security consultant, she often works with banks to reduce their vulnerability to robberies.
The security consultant recommended updating our alarm and surveillance systems for better protection.
We hired a security consultant to assess the risks in our new office building.
