prosecuting attorney

noun
official who prosecutes criminal cases for the government
4.5 4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈprɑː.sɪ.kjuː.tɪŋ ə.ˈtɜːr.niː/ (AmE), /ˈprɒ.sɪ.kjuː.tɪŋ ə.ˈtɜː.ni/ (BrE)
An official who conducts criminal prosecutions on behalf of the government or the state.

Examples
