malocchio

noun
evil eye curse
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːloʊˈkjoʊ / (AmE), / ˌmæləʊˈkjoʊ / (BrE)
a supposed curse or bad luck caused by a malevolent glare, also known as the evil eye in Italian folklore

Examples

The malocchio curse
In some Italian families, people wear charms to protect themselves from the malocchio.
She believed that her string of bad luck was caused by someone giving her the malocchio.
Stories about the malocchio have been passed down through generations in Mediterranean cultures.
