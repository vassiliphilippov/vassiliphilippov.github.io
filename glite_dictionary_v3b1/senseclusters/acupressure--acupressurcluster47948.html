acupressure

noun
therapy using finger pressure on body points
Pronunciation: / ˈæk.juˌprɛʃ.ər / (AmE), / ˈæk.juˌprɛʃ.ə / (BrE)
A technique of treating symptoms by applying pressure with the fingers to specific points on the body, believed to promote healing or relieve pain.

Examples

Acupressure therapy
She tried acupressure to relieve her headaches when medicine did not help.
Some spas offer acupressure as part of their wellness treatments.
The practitioner taught me how to use acupressure points to reduce stress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acupuncture - treatment using needles inserted into specific points in the body
