unbroken

adjective
not tamed or trained (of animals)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbroʊkən / (AmE), / ʌnˈbrəʊkən / (BrE)
(of an animal) not yet tamed, subdued, or trained for service or use.

Examples

Unbroken horse
The auction featured several young, unbroken colts.
The rancher showed us the unbroken mustangs in the field.
Training an unbroken horse requires patience and skill.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tamed - brought under control or made less wild (of animals)
