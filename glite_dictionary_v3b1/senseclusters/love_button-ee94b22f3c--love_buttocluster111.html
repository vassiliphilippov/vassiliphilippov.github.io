love button

noun
social media feature for expressing affection
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌv ˌbʌt.ən / (AmE), / ˈlʌv ˌbʌt.ən / (BrE)
a feature on social media platforms that allows users to express affection or support by clicking an icon, often represented by a heart

Examples

Love button on posts
She pressed the love button on her friend's vacation photo to show her support.
The new update introduced a love button alongside the like button on posts.
Whenever he sees a cute animal video, he always taps the love button.
