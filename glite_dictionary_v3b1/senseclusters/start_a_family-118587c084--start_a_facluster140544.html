start a family

verb
begin having children
Pronunciation: / stɑrt ə ˈfæməli / (AmE), / stɑːt ə ˈfæməli / (BrE)
to begin having children; to have one's first child or begin the process of raising children as a family

Examples

To start a family soon
After they bought their first house, they felt ready to start a family.
Many couples dream of the day they will start a family together.
Some people prefer to travel before deciding to start a family.
