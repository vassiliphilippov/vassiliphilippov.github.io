grainer

noun
grain producer or trader
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪnər / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪnə / (BrE)
a person who grows, harvests, buys, or sells grain such as wheat, corn, or barley

Examples

A grainer farmer
The grainer delivered sacks of wheat to the mill before dawn.
As a successful grainer, she supplied local bakeries with high-quality flour.
Many grainers gather at the annual harvest festival to trade their crops.

Related Terms

  • synonym: farmer - a person who owns or manages a farm
  • synonym: merchant - a person who buys and sells goods, especially in large amounts
