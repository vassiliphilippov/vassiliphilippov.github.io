canteen

noun
workplace cafeteria or restaurant
Pronunciation: / kænˈtin / (AmE), / kænˈtiːn / (BrE)
a place in a workplace, school, or institution where people can buy food, eat, and socialize

Examples

School canteen
Many students gather in the canteen to eat and socialize.
The company canteen serves hot meals to employees during lunch breaks.
The factory installed a new canteen to improve worker morale.
During their break, the workers gathered at the canteen for coffee and snacks.
She forgot her wallet and couldn't buy anything from the canteen at camp.
The school canteen offers a variety of healthy lunches for students every day.
Students gathered in the canteen after classes to chat and play board games.
The company upgraded its canteen to include table tennis and a coffee bar.
The hospital's canteen is a comfortable place for staff to unwind during breaks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cafeteria - a restaurant where you serve yourself and pay at a counter
  • synonym: common room - a lounge or recreation room, especially in an institution
  • synonym: mess hall - a place where soldiers eat meals
