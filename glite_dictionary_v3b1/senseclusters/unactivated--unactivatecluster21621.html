unactivated

adjective
not triggered
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈæktɪˌveɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈæktɪveɪtɪd / (BrE)
not stimulated or triggered to perform its function; in a resting or inactive physiological state, especially in biology or medicine

Examples

Unactivated cells
The unactivated cells remained in a dormant state until they encountered the specific antigen.
Scientists observed that unactivated enzymes did not catalyze the reaction under normal conditions.
In the study, unactivated immune responses were compared to those that had been stimulated.

Related Terms

  • antonym: activated - stimulated or triggered to perform its function
  • synonym: inactive - not active or not functioning
