Central American strap fern

noun
a fern species native to the Americas
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛntrəl əˈmɛrɪkən stræp fɜrn / (AmE), / ˌsɛntrəl əˈmɛrɪkən stræp fɜːn / (BrE)
A species of fern with shorter and narrower leaves than the Florida strap fern, found from Florida to the West Indies, Mexico, and south to Argentina.
