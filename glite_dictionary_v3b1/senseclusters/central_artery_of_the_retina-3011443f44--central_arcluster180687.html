central artery of the retina

noun
artery supplying blood to the retina
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntrəl ˈɑːrtəri əv ðə rɪˈtaɪnə / (AmE), / ˈsɛntrəl ˈɑːtəri əv ðə rɪˈtaɪnə / (BrE)
a branch of the ophthalmic artery that enters the eye alongside the optic nerve and supplies blood to the retina

Examples
