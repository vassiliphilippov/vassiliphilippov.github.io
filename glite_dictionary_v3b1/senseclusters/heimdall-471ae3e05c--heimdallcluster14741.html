Heimdall

proper noun
Norse god, guardian of Asgard
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪmˌdɑl / (AmE), / ˈhaɪmdɑːl / (BrE)
In Norse mythology, the god of dawn and light who serves as the guardian of Asgard.

Examples

Heimdall guards Asgard
In Norse mythology, Heimdall is famed for his keen senses and vigilance as Asgard's guardian.
Many tales describe Heimdall blowing his horn to alert the gods of approaching danger.
The rainbow bridge, Bifröst, is watched over by Heimdall to protect the gods from invaders.
