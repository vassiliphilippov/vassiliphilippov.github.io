shag

noun
a lively dance step with hopping movements
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʃæɡ / (AmE), / ʃaɡ / (BrE)
A lively dance step characterized by hopping on each foot in turn, popular in swing and jazz dances.

Examples

Shag dance step
He enjoys adding a few shag steps to his Lindy Hop sequences.
The dancers performed an impressive shag routine at the competition.
They learned the shag during their swing dance class.
