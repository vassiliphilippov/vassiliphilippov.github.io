decaffeinated

adjective
without caffeine
Pronunciation: / diˈkæfiˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / diˈkæfiːneɪtɪd / (BrE)
having had most or all of the caffeine removed, especially from coffee or tea

Examples

Decaffeinated coffee
He ordered a cup of decaffeinated coffee to avoid staying up all night.
Many people switch to decaffeinated tea in the afternoon to reduce their caffeine intake.
The restaurant offers both regular and decaffeinated options for coffee drinkers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: caffeinated - containing caffeine
