Examples

Synonyms

  • intended
    2.6 0.00/m
    intended is planned or done on purpose, while meant to be can mean either planned or seen as destined to happen.
  • destined
    4.1 0.15/m
    destined is seen as set by fate or plan, while meant to be is a casual way to say something seems meant to happen.
  • fated
    5.3 2.12/m
    fated is used when events seem doomed or fixed by fate, while meant to be is softer and often hopeful about outcomes.
  • bound
    4.2 2.39/m
    bound feels certain or likely to happen soon, while meant to be suggests a purpose or destiny rather than just likelihood.
  • foreordained
    9.9 0.06/m
    foreordained sounds like a fixed, often divine decision in advance, while meant to be is more everyday and less absolute.
