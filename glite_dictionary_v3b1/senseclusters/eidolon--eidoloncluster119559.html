eidolon

noun
phantom or idealized figure
Pronunciation: / aɪˈdoʊlɒn /; / aɪˈdoʊlən / (AmE), / aɪˈdəʊlɒn /; / aɪˈdəʊlən / (BrE)
an image, phantom, or idealized figure, especially a ghostly or imaginary presence in literature or the mind

Examples

A ghostly eidolon
The ancient legend spoke of an eidolon wandering the deserted halls at midnight.
In her dreams, an eidolon of her childhood friend appeared to offer comfort.
The artist painted an eidolon that seemed to float above the landscape, both real and unreal.
The ancient temple was said to be haunted by an eidolon that appeared at midnight.
In his novel, the hero is guided by an eidolon representing his lost love.
The artist painted an eidolon that captured the spirit of his imagination.
