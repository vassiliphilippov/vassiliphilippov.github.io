cantel

noun
rear of saddle
Pronunciation: / ˈkæntəl / (AmE), / ˈkæntəl / (BrE)
the raised rear part of a saddle that supports and secures the rider's seat

Examples

Saddle cantel
The old leather cantel provided extra support during the long ride across the fields.
She checked the cantel to make sure it was securely attached before saddling her horse.
A high cantel can help keep the rider in place on rough terrain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pommel - the raised front part of a saddle
