skiable

adjective
suitable for skiing
Pronunciation: / ˈskiːəbəl / (AmE), / ˈskiːəbəl / (BrE)
suitable or safe for skiing, usually because there is enough snow or good conditions

Examples

A skiable mountain
The mountain was finally skiable after a week of heavy snowfall.
We checked the weather report to see if the slopes would be skiable this weekend.
Some trails remain skiable well into the spring due to their high elevation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unskiable - not suitable or safe for skiing
