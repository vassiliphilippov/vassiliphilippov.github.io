waspish remark

noun
sharp or biting comment
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːspɪʃ rɪˈmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈwɒspɪʃ rɪˈmɑːk / (BrE)
a comment or statement that is sharply critical, biting, or sarcastic

Examples

A waspish remark
His waspish remark offended several members of the team.
She delivered another waspish remark that left everyone in stunned silence.
Tired of her waspish remarks, he decided not to engage further.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compliment - a polite or flattering remark
  • synonym: sarcastic remark - a comment expressing or marked by sarcasm
