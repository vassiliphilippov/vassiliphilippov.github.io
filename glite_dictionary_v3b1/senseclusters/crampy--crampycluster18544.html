crampy

adjective
uncomfortably small
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkræmpi / (AmE), / ˈkræmpi / (BrE)
uncomfortably small, restricted, or lacking enough space to move easily

Examples

Crampy room
The hotel room was so crampy that we could barely open our suitcases.
They ate dinner in a crampy kitchen with barely enough space for three chairs.
After moving into the crampy apartment, she missed her old, spacious home.

  • antonym: spacious - having plenty of space
