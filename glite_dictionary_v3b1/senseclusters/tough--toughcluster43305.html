tough

adjective
strongly made; durable
5.3
Pronunciation: / tʌf / (AmE), / tʌf / (BrE)
physically strong, durable, or able to withstand hardship or rough treatment

Examples

Tough shoes
The shoes are surprisingly tough for everyday wear.
They installed tough steel doors for better security.
This jacket is made from tough material that won't tear easily.
After years of walking barefoot, the tough soles of his feet could withstand any terrain.
Dancers often develop tough skin on their toes from constant practice.
This leather is so tough that it's nearly impossible to cut with ordinary scissors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: durable - able to withstand wear or damage; long-lasting
  • antonym: fragile - easily broken or damaged
  • synonym: resilient - able to withstand or recover quickly from difficult conditions
  • synonym: sturdy - strongly and solidly built or made
