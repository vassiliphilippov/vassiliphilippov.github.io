hit the bottle

verb
drink alcohol, often heavily
Pronunciation: / hɪt ðə ˈbɑt̬.əl /; / hɪt ðə ˈbɑt̬l̩ / (AmE), / hɪt ðə ˈbɒt.əl /; / hɪt ðə ˈbɒtl̩ / (BrE)
to start drinking alcohol heavily, especially as a way to deal with problems or stress

Examples

Many people turn to hit the bottle when faced with personal difficulties.
She promised her family she wouldn’t hit the bottle again.
