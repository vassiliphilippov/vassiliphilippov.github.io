immortalist

noun
believer in immortality
9.3
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɔrtəlɪst / (AmE), / ɪˈmɔːtəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes that humans can achieve immortality, especially through science or technology

Examples

A science immortalist
The immortalist community is growing as advances in biotechnology continue.
As an immortalist, she believes aging can be cured like any other disease.
Some immortalists invest in research aimed at extending human life indefinitely.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mortalist - a person who believes that death is inevitable for humans
  • synonym: transhumanist - a person who advocates for using technology to enhance human abilities
