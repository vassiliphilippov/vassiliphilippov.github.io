princess

noun
female royal; king's or queen's daughter
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪn.sɛs / (AmE), / ˈprɪn.səs / (BrE)
a female member of a royal family, especially the daughter of a king or queen or the wife of a prince

Examples

A royal princess
As a young princess, she was admired by everyone for her kindness and grace.
Every child at the party wanted to dress up as a princess from their favorite fairy tale.
The princess attended the charity gala with her parents, the king and queen.

Related Terms

  • antonym: prince - a male member of a royal family, especially the son of a king or queen
