Fringilla

noun
genus of finches (chaffinch, brambling)
Pronunciation: / frɪnˈdʒɪlə / (AmE), / frɪnˈdʒɪlə / (BrE)
A genus of small passerine birds in the finch family, including species such as the chaffinch and brambling.

Examples

The Fringilla genus
Researchers observed several chaffinches, members of the Fringilla genus, in the forest.
The Fringilla genus is well known for its colorful plumage and melodious songs.
The brambling, a type of Fringilla, migrates across Europe each year.
