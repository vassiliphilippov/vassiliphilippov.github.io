pulpitis

noun
tooth pulp inflammation
8.2
Pronunciation: / pʌlˈpaɪtɪs / (AmE), / pʌlˈpaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the dental pulp, the soft tissue inside a tooth

Examples

Pulpitis tooth pain
The dentist explained that pulpitis was causing the patient's severe tooth pain.
If left untreated, pulpitis can lead to more serious dental problems.
After a deep cavity, she developed pulpitis and needed immediate treatment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gingivitis - inflammation of the gums
  • synonym: toothache - pain in or around a tooth
