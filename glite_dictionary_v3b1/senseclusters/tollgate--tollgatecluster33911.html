tollgate

noun
gate for collecting tolls on a road or bridge
7.2 4.2
Pronunciation: /toʊl geɪt/ (AmE), /təʊl geɪt/ (BrE)
a gate or barrier across a toll road or bridge, operated to collect a fee before allowing passage

Examples

The tollgate collects fees
Cars lined up at the tollgate, waiting for their turn to pass through.
The old tollgate is now a small museum that tells the history of the road.
We stopped at the tollgate to pay the fee before crossing the bridge.
