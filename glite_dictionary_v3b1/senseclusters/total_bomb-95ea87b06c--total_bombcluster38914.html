A complete failure, especially in terms of financial results or public reception.

Examples

A total bomb failure
Critics expected the play to succeed, but it turned out to be a total bomb.
Despite the star-studded cast, the film became a total bomb at the box office.
The new software release was a total bomb with users and had to be quickly withdrawn.
