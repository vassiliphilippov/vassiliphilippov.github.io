brush-off

noun
curt or dismissive rejection
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbrʌʃ ˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈbrʌʃ ɒf / (BrE)
an abrupt or dismissive rejection or refusal to pay attention

Examples

Get the brush-off
After waiting all week for a reply, she finally realized she was getting the brush-off.
He tried to apologize, but her cold response was a clear brush-off.
Reporters received a polite brush-off when they asked about the rumors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rebuff - an abrupt or ungracious rejection
  • synonym: snub - an act of ignoring or dismissing someone
  • antonym: welcome - an act or manner of greeting or accepting
