go to the wall

verb
support someone in difficulty (idiom)
6.8
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ tə ðə wɑl / (AmE), / ɡəʊ tə ðə wɔːl / (BrE)

Examples

True friends are the ones who will go to the wall for you when times are tough.
When his colleague was unfairly accused, he decided to go to the wall for her.
