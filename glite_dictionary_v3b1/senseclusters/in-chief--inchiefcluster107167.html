in-chief

adjective
indicating the highest-ranking position
Pronunciation: / ɪn ˈtʃif / (AmE), / ɪn ˈtʃiːf / (BrE)
used after a title to indicate a person who holds the highest rank or most important position in a group or organization

Examples

Commander in-chief
As commander-in-chief, she made crucial decisions during the crisis.
He was recently promoted to editor-in-chief of the magazine.
The architect-in-chief oversaw every detail of the building's design.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chief - the leader or person in charge
