ballast

verb
to stabilize by adding ballast
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbæl.əst / (AmE), / ˈbæl.əst / (BrE)
to stabilize or make steady by adding ballast, especially in a vessel or aircraft

Examples

To ballast a ship
The engineers ballasted the ship to ensure a smoother voyage.
The old railroad tracks were carefully ballasted to withstand heavy loads.
They ballast the hot air balloon before every flight for safety.
