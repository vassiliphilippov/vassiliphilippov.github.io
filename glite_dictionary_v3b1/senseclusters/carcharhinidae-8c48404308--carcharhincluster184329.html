Carcharhinidae

noun
family of sharks (requiem sharks)
9.9
Pronunciation: /kɑːr.kə.ˈhɪn.ɪ.diː/ /; / /kɑːr.kə.ˈraɪ.nɪ.diː/ (AmE), /kɑː.kə.ˈhɪn.ɪ.diː/ /; / /kɑː.kə.ˈraɪ.nɪ.diː/ (BrE)
A family of sharks found worldwide, especially in tropical waters, including requiem sharks such as tiger sharks and soupfin sharks; their dorsal fins lack spines.

Examples

Carcharhinidae shark family
Carcharhinidae are found worldwide, particularly in tropical and subtropical waters.
Scientists observed several members of the Carcharhinidae off the coast during the expedition.
The Carcharhinidae family includes many well-known species such as the tiger shark.
