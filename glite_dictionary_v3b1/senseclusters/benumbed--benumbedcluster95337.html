benumbed

adjective
deprived of feeling or responsiveness
9.9 6.6
Pronunciation: / bɪˈnʌmd / (AmE), / bɪˈnʌmd / (BrE)
deprived of feeling, responsiveness, or sensitivity, especially as a result of overexposure or excessive stimulation

Examples

Benumbed by news
After watching nonstop news coverage, she felt benumbed to the tragedy.
His benumbed emotions made it hard for him to react to good or bad news.
The children became benumbed by repetitive lectures at school.
