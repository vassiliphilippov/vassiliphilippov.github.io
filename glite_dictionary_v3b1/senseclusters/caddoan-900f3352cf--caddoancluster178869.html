Caddoan

noun
family of North American indigenous languages
Pronunciation: / ˈkædoʊən / (AmE), / ˈkædəʊən / (BrE)
a family of North American indigenous languages spoken mainly in the Midwest, especially by the Caddo people

Caddoan languages
