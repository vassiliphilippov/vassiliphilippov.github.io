Dalit

proper noun
lowest Indian caste
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːlɪt / (AmE), / ˈdælɪt / (BrE)
a member of the lowest social caste in India, historically subjected to discrimination and formerly called 'untouchable'

Examples

A Dalit leader
Many Dalit leaders have played important roles in India's social reform movements.
The new law aims to protect the rights of every Dalit in the country.
She proudly identifies as a Dalit and works to end caste-based discrimination.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Brahmin - a member of the highest Hindu caste in India
  • synonym: untouchable - a historical term for a member of the lowest caste in India
