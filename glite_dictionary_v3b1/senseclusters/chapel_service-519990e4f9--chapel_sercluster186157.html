chapel service

noun
worship ceremony held in a chapel
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæpəl ˌsɝːvɪs / (AmE), / ˈtʃæpəl ˌsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
a religious ceremony or worship held in a chapel, typically involving prayers, readings, and hymns

Examples

A chapel service on holidays
Many families gather for a special chapel service on holidays.
Students are required to attend chapel service every Monday morning.
The chapel service included hymns, prayers, and a short sermon.
