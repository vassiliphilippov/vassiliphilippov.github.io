spatially

adverb
in relation to space
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈspeɪ.ʃə.li / (AmE), / ˈspeɪ.ʃəl.i / (BrE)

Examples

Objects spatially arranged
Objects in the image are spatially distributed across different areas.
The houses were spatially arranged in a perfect circle around the park.
The study examines how memories are spatially organized in the brain.
