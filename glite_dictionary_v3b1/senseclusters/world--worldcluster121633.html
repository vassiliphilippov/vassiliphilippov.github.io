world

noun
virtual, explorable online environment
4.2
Pronunciation: /wɜːrld/ (AmE), /wɜːld/ (BrE)
(in computing and gaming) a virtual or simulated environment created by software that users can explore, inhabit, or interact with.

Examples

A game world
The game's designers built a sprawling world filled with hidden quests, cities, and weather systems.
She lost track of time exploring new worlds on the virtual reality platform last night.
In the metaverse conference, companies discussed how users enter a shared world to socialize and work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: metaverse - a persistent, shared virtual space, often composed of multiple interconnected virtual worlds and services.
  • synonym: virtual world - a computer-based simulated environment that users can access and interact with, often via avatars.
