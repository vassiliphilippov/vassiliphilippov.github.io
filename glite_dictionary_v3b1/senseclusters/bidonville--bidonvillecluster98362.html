bidonville

noun
urban shantytown
9.9
Pronunciation: / biːˈdɔnvɪl / (AmE), / biːˈdɒnvɪl / (BrE)
a slum or shantytown, especially on the outskirts of a city in a French-speaking country

Examples

A bidonville slum
Many families live in the bidonville on the edge of the city.
The government launched a project to improve living conditions in the bidonville.
Children from the bidonville often face challenges accessing education and healthcare.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shantytown - an area of improvised housing on the outskirts of a city
  • synonym: slum - a densely populated urban area marked by poverty and poor housing
