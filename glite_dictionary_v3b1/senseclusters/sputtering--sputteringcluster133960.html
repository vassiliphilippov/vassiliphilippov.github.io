sputtering

noun
series of short explosive noises
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈspʌt̬.ɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspʌt.ər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
a series of short, explosive or spattering noises, often made by a malfunctioning engine, a flame, or other devices

Examples

Loud sputtering noise
Suddenly, a loud sputtering came from the fireplace as damp logs caught fire.
The sputtering of gunfire echoed across the empty field at dawn.
We heard the sputtering of the engine as it struggled to start on a cold morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spattering - making or causing repeated short bursts of noise or droplets
