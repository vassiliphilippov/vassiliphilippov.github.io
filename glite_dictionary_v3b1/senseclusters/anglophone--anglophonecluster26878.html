anglophone

adjective
English-speaking
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡləˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡləˌfəʊn / (BrE)
relating to people, countries, or cultures where English is the main language spoken

Examples

Anglophone countries
Many anglophone countries share similar legal and educational systems.
The conference attracted participants from both francophone and anglophone regions.
She grew up in an anglophone community despite living in a bilingual country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: english-speaking - using English as the main language
  • antonym: francophone - relating to French-speaking people or countries
