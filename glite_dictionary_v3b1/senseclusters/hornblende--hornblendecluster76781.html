hornblende

noun
dark green or black mineral of the amphibole group
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrnˌblɛnd / (AmE), / ˈhɔːnˌblɛnd / (BrE)
a dark green to black mineral of the amphibole group, consisting of calcium, sodium, magnesium, and iron silicates

Examples

Dark hornblende mineral
Metamorphic rocks often contain minerals such as mica, garnet, and hornblende.
The geologist identified the black crystals as hornblende within the rock sample.
Thin sections of the granite revealed flecks of hornblende mixed with quartz and feldspar.
