Entoloma aprile

noun
a spring-fruiting agaric mushroom species
Pronunciation: / ˌɛntoʊˈloʊmə əˈprɪleɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛntəˈləʊmə əˈprɪleɪ / (BrE)
A species of spring-fruiting agaric mushroom with a dark brown conical cap, found in woodland environments.
