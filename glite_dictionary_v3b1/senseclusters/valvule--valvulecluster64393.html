valvule

noun
small valve in a body or device
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈvælvjul / (AmE), / ˈvalvjuːl / (BrE)
a small valve, especially one found in a biological structure such as a heart or vein

Examples

A valvule in heart
A heart murmur can be caused by a defective valvule inside the heart.
Each valvule in the vein helps prevent blood from flowing backward.
The mechanic examined the tiny valvule in the fuel system to ensure it was working properly.
