whirly

noun
spinning toy
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrli / (AmE), / ˈwɜːli / (BrE)
a small toy or object that spins rapidly, such as a simple whirligig or spinner

Examples

A toy whirly
The child ran laughing across the yard while holding a colorful whirly in his hand.
She bought a bright whirly at the fair and watched it spin in the breeze.
A simple whirly can keep children entertained for hours on a windy day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spinner - a small toy that spins rapidly around a central point
  • synonym: whirligig - a toy that spins or whirls when blown or turned
