Thoughtful and thorough reflection or deliberation before making a decision or forming an opinion.

Examples

Careful consideration of options
After careful consideration, she chose to accept the new job offer.
Buying a house requires careful consideration of your finances and future plans.
The policy was adopted only after careful consideration of all possible outcomes.

Synonyms

    deliberation is formal discussion among people before deciding, while careful consideration is personal, thorough thought before forming an opinion.
  • consideration
    2.9 14/m
    consideration is the act of thinking or discussing something before a choice, while careful consideration is deeper and more thorough reflection before deciding.
  • reflection
    3.7 20/m
    reflection is quiet, inward thought to understand something, while careful consideration is thorough, careful thinking before making a decision.
  • weighing
    4.1 0.54/m

  • advisement
