careful consideration

noun
thoughtful, thorough reflection
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛr.fəl ˌkɒn.sɪˈdeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈkeə.fəl ˌkɒn.sɪˈdeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
Thoughtful and thorough reflection or deliberation before making a decision or forming an opinion.

Examples

Careful consideration of options
After careful consideration, she chose to accept the new job offer.
Buying a house requires careful consideration of your finances and future plans.
The policy was adopted only after careful consideration of all possible outcomes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deliberation - careful thought, especially in decision-making
  • synonym: reflection - serious thought or consideration
