careful consideration

noun
thoughtful, thorough reflection
1.0 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkeər.fəl kən.sɪ.də.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ˈkeə.fəl kən.sɪ.də.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
Thoughtful and thorough reflection or deliberation before making a decision or forming an opinion.

Examples
