transversal

adjective
cross-disciplinary, cross-cutting
Pronunciation: / trænzˈvɜr.səl / (AmE), / trænzˈvɜː.səl / (BrE)
spanning or relating to more than one subject, discipline, or area; cross-cutting across different fields rather than confined to a single domain.

Examples

Transversal skills
The curriculum emphasizes transversal skills, encouraging students to apply critical thinking across subjects.
Their research project took a transversal approach, combining insights from psychology, design, and computer science.
The new policy supports transversal initiatives that bridge health, education, and environmental programs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cross-cutting - affecting or relating to many different areas or groups; cutting across boundaries
  • synonym: cross-disciplinary - involving or combining two or more academic disciplines or fields of knowledge
  • synonym: interdisciplinary - relating to or involving two or more academic disciplines working together
