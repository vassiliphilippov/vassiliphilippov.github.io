cosmetic surgeon

noun
surgeon specializing in aesthetic procedures
Pronunciation: / kɑzˈmɛtɪk ˈsɝdʒən / (AmE), / kɒzˈmɛtɪk ˈsɜːdʒən / (BrE)
a medical doctor who performs surgical procedures to improve a person's appearance, especially of the face and body

Examples

Cosmetic surgeon improves appearance
Many people choose to visit a cosmetic surgeon to boost their self-confidence.
She consulted a cosmetic surgeon to discuss options for facial rejuvenation.
The cosmetic surgeon explained the risks and benefits of the procedure in detail.
