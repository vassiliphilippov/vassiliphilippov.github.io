antlike

adjective
resembling an ant
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈæntˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈæntˌlaɪk / (BrE)

Examples

Antlike insect body
The strange insect had an antlike head and thin, segmented legs.
Its antlike behavior made scientists wonder if it was mimicking ants for protection.
Some spiders have evolved an antlike appearance to avoid predators.
