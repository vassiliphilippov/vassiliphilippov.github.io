eye-catcher

noun
something or someone striking; attention-getter
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪˌkæt͡ʃər / (AmE), / ˈaɪˌkatʃə / (BrE)
A person or thing that is very noticeable or attractive and draws attention.

Examples

An eye-catcher dress
She wore an eye-catcher of a dress that made everyone stop and stare.
That colorful billboard is a definite eye-catcher in the city center.
The company's new sports car is a real eye-catcher on the highway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attention-grabber - something that grabs people's attention
  • antonym: wallflower - a person who is shy or unpopular and does not attract attention
