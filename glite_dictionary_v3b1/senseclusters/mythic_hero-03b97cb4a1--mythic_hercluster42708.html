mythic hero

noun
legendary or heroic figure in myths
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪθɪk ˈhɪroʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɪθɪk ˈhɪərəʊ / (BrE)
A legendary or heroic figure, often featured in myths or epic tales, known for extraordinary deeds or qualities.

Examples

A mythic hero story
Many cultures tell of a mythic hero whose bravery inspires future generations.
The mythic hero embarks on a perilous journey to recover a stolen artifact.
The story celebrates a mythic hero who defies the gods to protect his people.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demigod - a being with partial divine status, often a child of a god and a mortal
  • synonym: hero - a person admired for courage or noble qualities
