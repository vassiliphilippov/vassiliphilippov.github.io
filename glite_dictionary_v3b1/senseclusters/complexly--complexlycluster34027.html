complexly

adverb
in a complex manner
Pronunciation: / kəmˈplɛksli / (AmE), / kəmˈplɛksli / (BrE)
In a complex manner; involving many connected parts or complicated elements.

Examples

Explained complexly
She explained the theory complexly, making it difficult for beginners to understand.
The machinery was constructed complexly, requiring special training to operate.
The story unfolds complexly, with multiple plot twists and intertwined characters.
