straight-legged

adjective
having straight legs
7.8
Pronunciation: / streɪt ˈlɛɡd / (AmE), / streɪt ˈlɛɡd / (BrE)
having legs that are straight rather than bent or curved

Examples

Straight-legged jeans
He bought a pair of straight-legged jeans because he didn't like tight-fitting styles.
The coach praised the straight-legged posture of the gymnast during her routine.
The veterinarian noted that the puppy was unusually straight-legged for its breed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bow-legged - having outwardly curved legs
  • antonym: knock-kneed - having knees that touch but ankles apart when standing
