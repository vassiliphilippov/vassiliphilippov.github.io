shining sumac

noun
North American shrub with shiny leaves and red berries
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaɪnɪŋ ˈsuːmæk / (AmE), / ˈʃaɪnɪŋ ˈsjuːmæk / (BrE)
a nonpoisonous shrub of eastern North America with shiny compound leaves, green clustered flowers, and red berries

Examples

The shining sumac shrub
Gardeners often plant shining sumac for its attractive foliage and berries.
In autumn, the leaves of the shining sumac turn a brilliant red.
The woods behind the house are filled with shining sumac, especially in late summer.
