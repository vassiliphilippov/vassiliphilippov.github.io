noun
act of beheading
Pronunciation: / ˌdiːkəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdiːkɒˈleɪʃən / (BrE)

Examples

The decollation of the king was a turning point in the country's history.
Artists often depicted the decollation of saints in religious paintings.
The story describes the decollation as a symbol of martyrdom and sacrifice.
