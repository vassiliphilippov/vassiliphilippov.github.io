Hungarian sauce

noun
sauce with paprika, onion, and cream
3.1
Pronunciation: / hʌŋˈɡɛriən sɔs / (AmE), / hʌŋˈɡɛːriən sɔːs / (BrE)
A velouté-based sauce made with sautéed chopped onion, paprika, and cream, often served with meat or vegetables.

Examples

Hungarian sauce with cream
Hungarian sauce pairs perfectly with roasted vegetables or grilled meats.
I tried a new recipe last night that called for Hungarian sauce as the finishing touch.
The chef generously poured Hungarian sauce over the chicken to give it a rich, creamy flavor.
