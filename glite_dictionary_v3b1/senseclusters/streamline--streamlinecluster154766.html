streamline

verb
make more efficient or effective
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstriːmˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈstriːmˌlaɪn / (BrE)
to design or organize something in a way that makes it more efficient or effective; to simplify and improve a process

Examples

To streamline a process
Modern software can streamline many administrative tasks that used to take hours.
The company decided to streamline its operations to reduce costs and improve efficiency.
They hope to streamline the application process for future employees.

Related Terms

  • antonym: complicate - to make something more difficult or complex
  • synonym: simplify - make something simpler or easier to do or understand
