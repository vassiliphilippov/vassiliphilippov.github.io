legal battle

noun
court case involving legal dispute
Pronunciation: / ˈliːɡəl ˈbæt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈliːɡəl ˈbætl̩ / (BrE)
a court case in which two parties contest legal rights or issues, often involving considerable dispute or litigation

Examples

A legal battle in court
After years of appeals, their legal battle finally ended in victory.
She prepared herself for the emotional toll of the upcoming legal battle.
The company faced a long legal battle over intellectual property rights.

Related Terms

  • antonym: settlement - an agreement ending a legal dispute
