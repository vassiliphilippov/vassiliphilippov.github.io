gonal

adjective
with specified angles
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːnəl / (AmE), / ˈɡɒnəl / (BrE)
having a specified number of angles or sides, used in compound words to describe shapes

Examples

A gonal shape
A hexagonal tile is a six-gonal shape commonly used in bathroom floors.
The artist preferred gonal designs over circular ones for his modern sculptures.
In geometry, a decagonal figure has ten sides and ten angles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angular - having angles or sharp corners
  • antonym: circular - having the shape of a circle, without angles
