light a candle

verb
perform a ritual act of remembrance by igniting a candle
Pronunciation: / laɪt ə ˈkændl̩ / (AmE), / laɪt ə ˈkændl / (BrE)
to perform a ritual act of remembrance by igniting a candle, often as part of a religious or commemorative ceremony

Examples

To light a candle for peace
At the vigil, each person was invited to light a candle for peace.
Every year on her birthday, we light a candle in her memory.
Visitors to the memorial light a candle to pay their respects to those who have passed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commemorate - to honor the memory of someone or something, especially with a ceremony
