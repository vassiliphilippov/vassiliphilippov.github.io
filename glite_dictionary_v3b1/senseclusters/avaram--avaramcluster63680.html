avaram

noun
Indian shrub with yellow flowers, used in tanning
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːvərəm / (AmE), / ˈævərəm / (BrE)
an evergreen Indian shrub (Senna auriculata) with bright yellow flowers, whose bark is used in tanning; sometimes placed in the genus Cassia

Examples

The avaram shrub
Tanners in southern India often use bark from the avaram shrub.
The avaram plant is valued in traditional medicine and for its yellow flowers.
We planted an avaram in our garden to add a splash of color and support pollinators.
