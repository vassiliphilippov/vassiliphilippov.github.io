muckety muck

noun
important or influential person
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌkədi mʌk / (AmE), / ˈmʌkəti mʌk / (BrE)
an important or influential person, especially one who is wealthy or assertive; a bigwig

Examples

The muckety mucks met
Only the muckety mucks were aware of the plans before the public announcement.
She felt out of place among the muckety mucks at the exclusive fundraiser.
The muckety mucks always get the best seats at the restaurant, no matter how busy it is.

Related Terms

  • synonym: VIP - a person who is very important
  • synonym: bigwig - an important person
  • antonym: nobody - a person of no importance
