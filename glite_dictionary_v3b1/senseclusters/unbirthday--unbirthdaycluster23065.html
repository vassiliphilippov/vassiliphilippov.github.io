unbirthday

noun
non-birthday celebration
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɜːrθdeɪ / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɜːθdeɪ / (BrE)
a day that is not one's actual birthday, often celebrated playfully as an alternative occasion

Examples

An unbirthday party
They decided to have an unbirthday party to make an ordinary day special.
Every day that is not your birthday can be called an unbirthday.
The children exchanged small gifts on each other's unbirthday for fun.
