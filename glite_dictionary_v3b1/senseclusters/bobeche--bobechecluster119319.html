bobeche

noun
candle wax catcher
Pronunciation: / boʊˈbɛʃ / (AmE), / bəʊˈbɛʃ / (BrE)
a ring or cup placed at the base of a candle to catch dripping wax

Examples

Glass bobeche ring
The ornate chandelier featured a glass bobeche beneath each candle to catch the melting wax.
She carefully cleaned the silver bobeche before placing new candles in the holders.
Antique dealers often look for chandeliers with the original bobeche still intact.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drip tray - a tray used to catch drips from something above
