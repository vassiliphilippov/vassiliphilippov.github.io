whisper campaign

noun
secret effort to damage reputation
3.4 6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪspɚ kəmˈpeɪn / (AmE), / ˈwɪspə kəmˈpeɪn / (BrE)
a secretive attempt to spread rumors or negative information to harm someone's reputation

Examples

A whisper campaign spreads rumors
A whisper campaign can be difficult to combat because the rumors spread quietly and anonymously.
After months of a whisper campaign, her professional reputation was damaged beyond repair.
The senator's opponents started a whisper campaign to erode public trust before the election.

Related Terms

  • synonym: character assassination - the deliberate destruction of someone’s reputation
  • synonym: smear campaign - an attempt to discredit someone through false or exaggerated rumors
