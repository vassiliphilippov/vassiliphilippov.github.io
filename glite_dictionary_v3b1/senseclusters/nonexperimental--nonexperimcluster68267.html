nonexperimental

adjective
not based on experiments
6.0
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ɪk.spə.rɪ.ˈmen.təl/ /; / /nɑːn.ek.spə.rɪ.ˈmen.təl/ (AmE), /nɒn.ɪk.spə.rɪ.ˈmen.təl/ /; / /nɒn.ek.spə.rɪ.ˈmen.təl/ (BrE)
not involving or based on experiments; relying on observation rather than controlled testing

Examples

Nonexperimental research method
The researchers chose a nonexperimental approach to gather data from real-world situations.
A nonexperimental study cannot establish cause and effect as clearly as an experimental one.
Many social science surveys are nonexperimental and rely on observation rather than intervention.

Related Terms

  • antonym: experimental - involving controlled tests or experiments
  • synonym: observational - based on watching and recording rather than testing
