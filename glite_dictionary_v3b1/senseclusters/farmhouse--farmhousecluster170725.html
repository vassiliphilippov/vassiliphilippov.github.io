farmhouse

noun
house on a farm for a farmer's family
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑrmˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈfɑːmˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A house on a farm, typically used as the main residence by a farmer and their family.

Examples

Old farmhouse
The old farmhouse was surrounded by fields of corn and wheat.
They renovated the farmhouse to preserve its rustic charm.
We spent the weekend at a cozy farmhouse in the countryside.
