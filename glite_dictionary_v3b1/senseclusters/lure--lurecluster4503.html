lure

noun
appealing quality or feature
Pronunciation: / lʊr / (AmE), / ljʊə / (BrE)
an attractive quality or feature that tempts or entices someone by promising some kind of reward

Examples

The lure of money
Many people are drawn to the city by the lure of fame and fortune.
The lure of adventure inspired him to travel to distant lands.
The lure of easy money can be hard to resist for some people.

Related Terms

  • synonym: allure - the quality of being powerfully and mysteriously attractive or fascinating
  • synonym: attraction - the action or power of evoking interest, pleasure, or liking
  • antonym: repulsion - a feeling of intense distaste or disgust
