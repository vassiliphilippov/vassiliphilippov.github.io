advanceable

adjective
can be paid in advance
6.6
Pronunciation: / ədˈvænsəbəl / (AmE), / ədˈvɑːnsəbəl / (BrE)
capable of being paid or provided ahead of time as an advance, especially money or expenses

Examples

Advanceable payment
The company offers advanceable travel expenses for employees attending conferences.
Only advanceable costs will be covered before the project officially begins.
Her salary is advanceable if she submits a formal request to the finance department.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nonrefundable - not able to be returned or repaid
  • synonym: prepaid - paid for in advance
