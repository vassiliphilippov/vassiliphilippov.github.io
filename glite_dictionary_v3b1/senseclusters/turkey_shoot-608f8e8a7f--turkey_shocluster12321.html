turkey shoot

noun
an easy competition or contest, often involving shooting
8.4 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt…ùÀêki  ÉuÀêt / (AmE), / Ààt…úÀêki  ÉuÀêt / (BrE)
An event or competition in which the targets are very easy, originally referring to a shooting contest with live or artificial turkeys; often used figuratively to describe a situation where success is achieved with little effort due to lack of effective opposition.

Examples

A turkey shoot event
The debate was such a turkey shoot that the outcome was never in doubt.
The first match was a turkey shoot, with one team dominating entirely.
The old-fashioned turkey shoot raised money for the fire department.
