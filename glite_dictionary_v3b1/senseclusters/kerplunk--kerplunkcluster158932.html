kerplunk

adverb
with a heavy splash
9.9
Pronunciation: / kərˈplʌŋk / (AmE), / kəˈplʌŋk / (BrE)
with a sudden, heavy splash or thudding sound, often when something falls or lands

Examples

It landed kerplunk
The rock landed kerplunk in the middle of the puddle, sending water everywhere.
She dropped her keys and they fell kerplunk onto the wooden floor.
With a loud kerplunk, the book hit the bottom of the empty box.

Related Terms

  • synonym: splash - the sound of something hitting water or another liquid
  • synonym: thud - a dull, heavy sound made by an object falling
