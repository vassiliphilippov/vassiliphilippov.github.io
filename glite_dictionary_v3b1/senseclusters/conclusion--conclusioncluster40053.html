conclusion

noun
the act of deciding or judging
Pronunciation: / kənˈkluːʒən /; / kənˈkluʒən / (AmE), / kənˈkluːʒən / (BrE)
a decision, judgment, or opinion formed after thinking about the facts or arguments

Examples

Reach a conclusion
After listening to all the arguments, she finally reached her conclusion.
The evidence led the detective to an unexpected conclusion.
We must not jump to a conclusion before knowing all the facts.
After weeks of debate, the committee finally reached a conclusion about the proposal.
Based on the evidence presented, the detective drew a logical conclusion.
Her conclusion was that more research was needed before making a decision.

  • synonym: judgment - an opinion or decision that is based on careful thought
  • synonym: verdict - a decision or judgment given by a jury or court
