Pilobolus

proper noun
modern dance company
Pronunciation: / pɪˈlɑbələs / (AmE), / pɪˈlɒbələs / (BrE)
an American modern dance company, founded in 1971, known for its inventive, collaborative choreography and sculptural movement

Pilobolus dance company
