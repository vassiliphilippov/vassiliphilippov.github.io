ridgeway

noun
path on ridge
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪdʒˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈrɪdʒˌweɪ / (BrE)
a road or path that runs along the top of a ridge, often used for travel or hiking

Examples

A ridgeway path
The hikers followed the ridgeway to enjoy panoramic views of the countryside.
An old ridgeway runs along the top of the hills near the village.
We spent the afternoon walking the ridgeway above the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trail - a path or track made for walking or hiking
  • antonym: valley - a low area of land between hills or mountains
