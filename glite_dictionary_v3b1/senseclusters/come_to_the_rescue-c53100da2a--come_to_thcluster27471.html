Examples

To come to the rescue

Synonyms

  • come to one's rescue
    3.1 0.38/m
    come to one's rescue shows someone arrives quickly to help a particular person, while come to the rescue is more general.
  • come to (someone's) aid
    3.1 0.12/m
    come to (someone's) aid is a slightly more formal way of helping someone, while come to the rescue often sounds more urgent.
  • rescue
    1.0 22/m
    rescue means actively saving someone from danger, while come to the rescue highlights arriving to help at a critical moment.
  • save
    1.0 117/m
    save can mean protect someone or keep something for later, while come to the rescue means stepping in immediately to help in trouble.

Related Terms

  • synonym: save - to help someone out of danger or trouble
