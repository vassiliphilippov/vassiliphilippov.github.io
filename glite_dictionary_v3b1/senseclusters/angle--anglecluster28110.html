angle

noun
a particular point of view or bias
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡəl / (BrE)
a particular way of considering, describing, or presenting a situation, especially one that shows bias or a specific point of view

Examples

A new angle on news
She always finds an interesting angle to approach her essays.
The news article presented the story from a new angle to attract readers.
You need to consider the problem from every possible angle before making a decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bias - tendency to present information with prejudice for or against something
  • antonym: objectivity - the quality of being unbiased and impartial
  • synonym: perspective - a particular attitude or way of viewing something
