gustatory sensation

noun
sensation of taste
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌstəˌtɔri sɛnˈseɪʃn̩ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌstətəri sɛnˈseɪʃən / (BrE)
the sensation of taste perceived through the activation of taste buds by a soluble chemical stimulus

Examples

Gustatory sensation of sweetness
Loss of gustatory sensation can occur in certain medical conditions.
The spicy dish produced a strong gustatory sensation that lingered for several minutes.
Wine tasting relies not just on smell but also on subtle gustatory sensations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: olfactory sensation - the sensation of smell
  • synonym: taste - the sensation experienced when something is tasted
