hyperbaric

adjective
high air pressure
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˈbærɪk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəˈbærɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving air pressure that is higher than normal atmospheric pressure

Examples

A hyperbaric chamber
The diver entered a hyperbaric chamber to recover from decompression sickness.
Doctors sometimes use hyperbaric oxygen therapy to treat certain wounds.
A hyperbaric environment can help increase the amount of oxygen in the blood.
