Synonyms

  • embezzlement
    7.0 1.00/m
    embezzlement is the dishonest taking of money by someone who controls it, while misappropriation names wrongful use of entrusted funds broadly.
  • defalcation
    9.9 0.00/m
    defalcation is fraudulent taking of entrusted money in a professional role, while misappropriation covers wrongful use by anyone for personal benefit.
  • misapplication
    8.8 0.00/m
    misapplication is improper or dishonest use of funds for the wrong purpose, while misappropriation emphasises taking entrusted money for personal use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appropriation - the act of taking something for one's own use, often without permission
  • antonym: donation - the act of giving something freely
  • synonym: embezzlement - theft or misappropriation of funds placed in one's trust
