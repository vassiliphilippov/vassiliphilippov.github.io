pivotally

adverb
in a rotating way
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪvətəli / (AmE), / ˈpɪvətəli / (BrE)
in a manner that allows or involves rotation around a fixed point or pivot.

Examples

Moves pivotally on a hinge
The door swings pivotally on its central hinge, making it easy to open and close.
The sculpture is pivotally mounted, allowing viewers to turn it and see all sides.
The camera was pivotally attached to the tripod for smooth panoramic shots.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rotationally - in a way that involves rotation
