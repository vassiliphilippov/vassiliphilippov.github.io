repopularize

verb
to make popular again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈpɑːpjəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˌriːˈpɒpjʊləˌaɪz / (BrE)
to make something become popular again after a period of decline

Examples

To repopularize a trend
The documentary managed to repopularize interest in traditional folk music.
A new fashion trend can repopularize styles from previous decades.
Social media campaigns helped repopularize the classic board game among teenagers.
