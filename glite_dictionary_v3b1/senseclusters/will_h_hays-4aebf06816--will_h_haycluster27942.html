Will H. Hays

proper noun
U.S. film code creator (1879–1954)
Pronunciation: / wɪl eɪtʃ heɪz / (AmE), / wɪl eɪtʃ heɪz / (BrE)
(1879–1954) United States lawyer and politician who created the 'Hays Code,' regulating moral content in U.S. films from 1930 to 1966.
