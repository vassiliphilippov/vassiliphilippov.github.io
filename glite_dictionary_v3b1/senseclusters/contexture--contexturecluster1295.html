contexture

noun
woven fabric structure
9.9
Pronunciation: / kənˈtɛkstʃər / (AmE), / kənˈtɛkstjʊə / (BrE)
the arrangement or structure of woven threads in a fabric; the texture produced by weaving, especially in an archaic or specialized sense
