alas

interjection
expression of regret or sorrow
Pronunciation: / əˈlæs / (AmE), / əˈlæs / (BrE)
used to express sadness, regret, or disappointment, especially at a misfortune or bad news

Examples

Alas, poor choice
Alas, the old bridge could not withstand the storm and collapsed.
Alas, we arrived too late and missed the beginning of the performance.
He hoped for good news but, alas, it was not to be.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unfortunately - used to introduce bad news or disappointment
