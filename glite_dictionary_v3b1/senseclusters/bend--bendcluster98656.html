bend

verb
lean the upper body forward
Pronunciation: / bɛnd / (AmE), / bɛnd / (BrE)
to lean or move the upper part of your body forward and down from the waist

Examples

To bend forward
Before entering the shrine, visitors are expected to bend in respect.
She had to bend to look under the table for her keys.
The old man bent over to tie his shoelaces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stoop - to bend the body forward and downward
  • antonym: straighten - to make or become straight again
