abstracter

noun
automated abstractive summarizer
9.9
Pronunciation: /əb.ˈstræk.tər/ /; / /ˈæb.stræk.tər/ (AmE), /əb.ˈstræk.tə/ /; / /ˈæb.stræk.tə/ (BrE)
(in computing, especially natural language processing) a software tool, model, or algorithm that produces abstractive summaries of texts by generating new phrasing rather than selecting exact extracts.

Examples

AI abstracter tool
The research team trained an abstracter to produce concise summaries of scientific articles for reviewers.
Our newsroom deployed an abstracter that creates short, readable digests from long investigative reports.
Developers evaluated several abstracters and chose the one with the most fluent output for the app.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abstractive summarizer - a system that produces summaries by generating new text that captures the source meaning
  • synonym: summarizer - a tool or person that produces a shorter version of a longer text
