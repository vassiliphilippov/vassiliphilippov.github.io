containerboard

noun
paperboard for boxes
4.7
Pronunciation: / kənˈteɪnərˌbɔrd / (AmE), / kənˈteɪnəbɔːd / (BrE)
a strong type of paperboard used to make the sides and liners of corrugated shipping boxes

Examples

Strong containerboard paper

Advances in containerboard production have made boxes stronger and more environmentally friendly.
Most corrugated boxes are made from recycled containerboard to reduce waste.
