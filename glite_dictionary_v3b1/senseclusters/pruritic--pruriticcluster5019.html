pruritic

adjective
causing itching
9.2
Pronunciation: / pruˈrɪtɪk / (AmE), / pruːˈrɪtɪk / (BrE)
causing or relating to itching of the skin

Examples

Pruritic skin rash
The doctor prescribed a cream for her pruritic skin condition.
After hiking through the woods, he developed a pruritic rash on his legs.
Some allergic reactions can result in pruritic patches that are difficult to ignore.
