manifest

adjective
clear or obvious; easily perceived
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmænɪˌfɛst / (AmE), / ˈmanɪfɛst / (BrE)
clear or obvious to the eye or mind; easily perceived or noticed.

Examples

Manifest evidence
Her disappointment was manifest in her face.
His anger became manifest when he raised his voice.
The effects of the disease are manifest to everyone who visits the region.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hidden - not visible or easily noticed; concealed
