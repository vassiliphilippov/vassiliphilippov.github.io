scrapper

noun
scrap collector
Pronunciation: / ˈskræpər / (AmE), / ˈskræpə / (BrE)
a person who collects and sells scrap materials for reuse or recycling

Examples

Metal scrapper
The scrapper made his living by collecting old appliances and selling the metal.
Every morning, the local scrapper drives through the neighborhood looking for discarded items.
After the factory closed, he became a scrapper to support his family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recycler - a person or company that processes used materials for reuse
  • synonym: scavenger - a person who searches for and collects discarded items
