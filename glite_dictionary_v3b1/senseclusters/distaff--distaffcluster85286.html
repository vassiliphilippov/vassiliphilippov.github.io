distaff

noun
tool for spinning wool or flax
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪstæf / (AmE), / ˈdɪstɑːf / (BrE)
a staff on which wool or flax is wound for spinning by hand

Examples

A wooden distaff
In the old cottage, a wooden distaff leaned against the fireplace, a reminder of earlier days.
She skillfully wound the flax onto the distaff for spinning.
The spinner placed the wool on her distaff before beginning her work.
