overread

verb
to read too much into
8.2
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ˈriːd/ /; / /oʊ.vər.ˈred/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.ˈriːd/ /; / /əʊ.və.ˈred/ (BrE)
to interpret something as having more meaning or significance than it actually does

Examples

To overread a message
Try not to overread his comments; he was just joking.
She tends to overread every text message and worry about hidden meanings.
It is easy to overread a simple gesture and assume it means more than it does.

Related Terms

  • synonym: misinterpret - to understand something incorrectly
  • synonym: overinterpret - to interpret something in an excessively complex or detailed way
  • antonym: underread - to fail to notice or interpret the full meaning of something
