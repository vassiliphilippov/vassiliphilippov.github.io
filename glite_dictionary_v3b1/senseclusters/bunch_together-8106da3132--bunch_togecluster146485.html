bunch together

verb
form a close group or cluster
4.3
Pronunciation: / bʌntʃ təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / bʌntʃ təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to form into a close group or cluster; to come or bring close together into a compact mass

Examples

To bunch together closely
She asked the students to bunch together for the group photo.
The sheep bunched together at the sound of the barking dog.
When the rain started, the fans bunched together under the shelter.
