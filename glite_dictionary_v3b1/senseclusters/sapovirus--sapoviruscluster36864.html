sapovirus

noun
virus causing gastroenteritis
Pronunciation: / ˈsæpoʊˌvaɪrəs / (AmE), / ˈsæpəʊˌvaɪrəs /; / ˈsæpəʊˌvaɪərəs / (BrE)
a virus that causes stomach illness (gastroenteritis), especially in children
