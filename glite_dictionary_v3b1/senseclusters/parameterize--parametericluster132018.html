parameterize

verb
to set parameters
7.9
Pronunciation: / pəˈræməˌtaɪz /; / pəˈræməˌtraɪz / (AmE), / pəˈrɑːmɪtəraɪz / (BrE)
to express, describe, or set up something using parameters or variables, especially in mathematics, science, or systems
