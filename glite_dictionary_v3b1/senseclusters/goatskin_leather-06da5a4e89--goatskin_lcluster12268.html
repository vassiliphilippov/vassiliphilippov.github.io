goatskin leather

noun
leather from goat hide
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊtˌskɪn ˈlɛðər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊtskɪn ˈlɛðə / (BrE)
leather made from the hide of a goat, often used for bookbinding and fine goods due to its durability and softness.

Examples

Goatskin leather binding
Collectors prized the rare volume for its goatskin leather binding.
Her journal featured a beautifully embossed goatskin leather cover.
The artisan crafted gloves from soft goatskin leather.
