top boot

noun
tall boot reaching mid-knee
3.9
Pronunciation: / tɑp buːt / (AmE), / tɒp buːt / (BrE)
A tall boot that reaches halfway up to the knee; often worn for riding or as part of historical dress.

Examples

A pair of top boots
He wore black top boots with his formal riding attire.
In the painting, the gentleman is depicted in a waistcoat and top boots.
Top hats and top boots were both popular in 19th-century England.
