contested

adjective
disputed or subject to competition
2.6
Pronunciation: / kənˈtɛstɪd / (AmE), / kənˈtɛstɪd / (BrE)
subject to dispute, debate, or competition; not accepted as settled or final

Examples

A contested match
During the contested match, tensions between the teams ran high.
Ownership of the property remains contested in court.
The results of the contested election were not announced for several days.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disputed - questioned, argued about, or doubted
  • antonym: uncontested - not disputed or challenged
