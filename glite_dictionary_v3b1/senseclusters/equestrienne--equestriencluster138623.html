equestrienne

noun
female horse rider
Pronunciation: / ɪˌkwɛstriˈɛn / (AmE), / ɪˌkwɛstriˈɛn / (BrE)
a woman who rides horses, especially in competitions or performances

Examples

The equestrienne rides horses
The talented equestrienne won first place in the dressage competition.
As a young equestrienne, she spent hours training with her horse every day.
The circus featured a skilled equestrienne performing daring tricks on horseback.

Related Terms

  • synonym: equestrian - a person who rides horses
  • antonym: horseman - a man who rides or works with horses
  • synonym: horsewoman - a woman who rides or works with horses
