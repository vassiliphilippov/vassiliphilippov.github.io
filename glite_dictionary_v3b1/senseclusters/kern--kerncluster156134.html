kern

verb
adjust space between letters (typography)
6.8 8.3
Pronunciation: /kɜːrn/ (AmE), /kɜːn/ (BrE)
to adjust the space between letters in text, especially to make it look better in typography

Examples

To kern letters
In this software, you can manually kern every letter pair for custom typography.
