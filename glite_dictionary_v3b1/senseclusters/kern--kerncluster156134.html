kern

verb
adjust space between letters (typography)
Pronunciation: / kɜrn / (AmE), / kɜːn / (BrE)
to adjust the space between letters in text, especially to make it look better in typography

Examples

To kern letters
In this software, you can manually kern every letter pair for custom typography.
It is important to kern pairs like "A" and "V" for a balanced look.
The designer kerned the headline text to achieve perfect alignment.
Many fonts require you to kern certain letter pairs manually for the best appearance.
Once you kern the text properly, the final layout will look much more professional.
The designer decided to kern the headline so the letters would be more visually balanced.
