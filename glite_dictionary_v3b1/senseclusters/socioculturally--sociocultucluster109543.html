socioculturally

adverb
in a social and cultural way
7.9
Pronunciation: /soʊ.si.oʊ.ˈkʌl.tʃər.ə.li/ (AmE), /səʊ.si.əʊ.ˈkʌl.tʃə.rə.li/ (BrE)
