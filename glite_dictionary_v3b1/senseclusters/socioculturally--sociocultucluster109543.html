socioculturally

adverb
in a social and cultural way
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊsi.oʊˈkʌltʃɚəli / (AmE), / ˌsəʊsi.əʊˈkʌltʃərəli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to both social and cultural factors or influences

Examples

Socioculturally important event
The festival is socioculturally important, bringing together people from different backgrounds.
Policies must be socioculturally sensitive to address the needs of diverse communities.
The project was designed socioculturally to reflect local traditions and values.

Related Terms

  • synonym: culturally - in a way that relates to culture
  • synonym: socially - in a way that relates to society or social factors
