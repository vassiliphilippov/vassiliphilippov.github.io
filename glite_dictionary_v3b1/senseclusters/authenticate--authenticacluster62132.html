authenticate

verb
prove something is genuine
Pronunciation: / əˈθɛntɪˌkeɪt /; / ɔˈθɛntɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ɔːˈθɛntɪkeɪt / (BrE)
to prove that something is genuine or that someone is who they claim to be, especially by checking evidence or credentials
