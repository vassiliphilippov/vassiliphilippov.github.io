Nazirite

adjective
of Nazirite vow
Pronunciation: / ˈnæzəˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈnæzɪraɪt / (BrE)
relating to a Nazirite or the Nazirite vow in ancient Hebrew tradition

Examples

Nazirite tradition
The ancient scrolls describe Nazirite customs and rituals in great detail.
He followed a strict Nazirite lifestyle, abstaining from wine and cutting his hair.
The festival included a reenactment of traditional Nazirite ceremonies.
