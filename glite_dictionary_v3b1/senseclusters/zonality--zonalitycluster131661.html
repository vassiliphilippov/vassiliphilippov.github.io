zonality

noun
arrangement in zones
8.7
Pronunciation: / zoʊˈnælɪti / (AmE), / zəʊˈnælɪti / (BrE)
the state or quality of being arranged in distinct zones or bands, especially in nature or geography

Examples

Climate zonality
The zonality of climate patterns can be observed across different latitudes on Earth.
Scientists study the zonality of soil types to understand agricultural potential in various regions.
The zonality of vegetation is influenced by latitude and altitude.

Related Terms

  • antonym: uniformity - the quality of being the same throughout
  • synonym: zoning - the process or result of dividing an area into zones
