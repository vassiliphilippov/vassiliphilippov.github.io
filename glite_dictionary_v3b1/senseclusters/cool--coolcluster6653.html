cool

adjective
used for emphasis of a large amount
1.9
Pronunciation: / kuːl / (AmE), / kuːl / (BrE)
used before a number or amount, especially of money, to emphasize how large it is

Examples

A cool million
He won a cool thousand dollars at the casino last night.
She inherited a cool half a million from her aunt.
The company was sold for a cool five million euros.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neat - used to express an exact or round amount, especially of money
