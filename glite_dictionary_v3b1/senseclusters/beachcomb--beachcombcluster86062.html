beachcomb

verb
to search beach for items
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtʃˌkoʊm / (AmE), / ˈbiːtʃˌkəʊm / (BrE)
to walk along a beach searching for valuable or interesting items that have washed ashore

Examples

To beachcomb for shells
After the storm, many people came to beachcomb for shells and driftwood.
She likes to beachcomb early in the morning before the crowds arrive.
They spent the afternoon beachcombing and found several unusual pieces of sea glass.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forage - to search widely for food or provisions
  • synonym: scavenge - to search for and collect usable items from discarded material
