market square

noun
public space for markets in a town or city
2.1 0.22/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…ërk…™t skw…õr / (AmE), / Ààm…ëÀêk…™t skwe…ô / (BrE)
A public open area in a town or city where a market is held and goods such as food and merchandise are bought and sold.

Examples

The market square stalls
Every Saturday, local farmers sell their produce in the market square.
Many historic events took place in the city's central market square.
The market square was crowded with tourists and colorful stalls.

Synonyms

  • open-air market
    1.6 0.17/m
    an open-air market is a temporary outdoor event where vendors sell goods, while market square is a fixed public town area used for markets.
