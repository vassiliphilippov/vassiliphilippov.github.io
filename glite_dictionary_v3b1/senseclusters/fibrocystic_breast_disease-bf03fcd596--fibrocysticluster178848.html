fibrocystic breast disease

noun
benign breast tissue condition with cysts
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪ.broʊˈsɪs.tɪk brɛst dɪˈziz / (AmE), / ˌfʌɪ.brə(ʊ)ˈsɪs.tɪk brɛst dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
A benign condition in which the breast tissue develops cysts and fibrosis, often resulting in lumpiness and discomfort.

Examples

Fibrocystic breast disease symptoms
Her doctor reassured her that fibrocystic breast disease is not linked to an increased risk of cancer.
Many women experience fibrocystic breast disease during their reproductive years.
Symptoms of fibrocystic breast disease may include breast pain, tenderness, and lumpiness.
