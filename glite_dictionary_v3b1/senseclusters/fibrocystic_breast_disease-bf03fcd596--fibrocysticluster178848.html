fibrocystic breast disease

noun
benign breast tissue condition with cysts
3.3
Pronunciation: /faɪ.broʊ.ˈsɪs.tɪk brest dɪ.ˈziːz/ (AmE), /faɪ.brəʊ.ˈsɪs.tɪk brest dɪ.ˈziːz/ (BrE)
