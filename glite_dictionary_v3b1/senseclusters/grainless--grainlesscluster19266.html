grainless

adjective
without grains
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪnləs / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪnləs / (BrE)
having no grains or lacking a grainy texture or appearance

Examples

Grainless paper
The artist chose a grainless paper to achieve a flawless watercolor wash.
Modern digital cameras produce grainless images even in low light conditions.
She preferred grainless bread for its soft and uniform texture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: grainy - having a rough texture or appearance due to small particles
  • synonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface; not rough or grainy
