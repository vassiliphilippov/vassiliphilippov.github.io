munchkin

noun
cat with short legs
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌntʃkɪn / (AmE), / ˈmʌntʃkɪn / (BrE)
a breed of domestic cat known for its very short legs caused by a natural genetic mutation

Examples

Munchkin cat breed
The munchkin quickly became popular for its playful nature and unique appearance.
Many people are charmed by the short legs of the munchkin breed.
A munchkin can run and jump just like any other cat, despite its size.
