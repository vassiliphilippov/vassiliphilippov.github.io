graphic artist

noun
designer of printed or digital artworks
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræfɪk ˈɑrtɪst / (AmE), / ˈɡrafɪk ˈɑːtɪst / (BrE)
an artist who creates designs and artworks, often for print or digital media, such as posters, advertisements, or books

Examples

Graphic artist designs posters
Many graphic artists have shifted from traditional printmaking to digital media in recent years.
She works as a graphic artist, designing book covers and promotional materials for publishers.
The advertising agency hired a talented graphic artist to create their new campaign posters.
