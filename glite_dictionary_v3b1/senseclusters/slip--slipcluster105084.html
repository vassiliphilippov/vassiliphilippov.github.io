slip

noun
minor social or verbal mistake
Pronunciation: / slɪp / (AmE), / slɪp / (BrE)
a small mistake or error, especially one made by accident or carelessness

Examples

A slip of the tongue
Everyone makes a slip now and then when nervous in public.
He apologized for the slip he made during the meeting.
Her comment was an embarrassing slip at the dinner table.
A simple slip while typing led to a small but noticeable error.
Despite a single slip in her report, the overall work was excellent.
She made a slip of the tongue during the interview and called him by the wrong name.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blunder - a careless or serious mistake
  • synonym: error - a mistake
  • synonym: faux pas - an embarrassing social mistake
  • synonym: gaffe - an unintentional act causing embarrassment
  • synonym: lapse - a temporary failure of concentration, memory, or judgment
