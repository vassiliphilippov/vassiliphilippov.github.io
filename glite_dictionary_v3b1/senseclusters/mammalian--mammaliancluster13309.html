mammalian

adjective
relating to mammals
Pronunciation: / məˈmeɪliən / (AmE), / məˈmeɪlɪən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of mammals (animals of the class Mammalia)

Examples

Mammalian genetics
Scientists study mammalian genetics to understand human hereditary diseases.
The mammalian brain is capable of complex emotional responses.
Unlike reptiles, mammalian species nurse their young with milk produced by specialized glands.
