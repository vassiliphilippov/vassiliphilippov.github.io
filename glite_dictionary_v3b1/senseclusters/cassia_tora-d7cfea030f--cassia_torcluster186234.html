Cassia tora

noun
tropical herb with yellow flowers, sometimes a weed
Pronunciation: / ˌkæsiə ˈtɔrə / (AmE), / ˌkæsiə ˈtɔːrə / (BrE)
a tropical herb or subshrub with yellow flowers and slender curved pods, often considered a weed; sometimes used in herbal medicine
