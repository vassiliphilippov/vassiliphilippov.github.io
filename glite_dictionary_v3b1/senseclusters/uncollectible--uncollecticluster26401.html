uncollectible

noun
bad debt
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəˈlɛktəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəˈlɛktɪbəl / (BrE)
a debt or account that cannot be recovered or collected by a business or creditor

Examples

A business uncollectible
The accountant marked several uncollectibles in the company's financial records this quarter.
Banks often set aside reserves to cover potential uncollectibles each year.
After repeated attempts, the old invoice was finally classified as an uncollectible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bad debt - a debt that is unlikely to be paid or recovered
  • antonym: receivable - an amount of money owed to a business by its customers
