ladybeetle

noun
small, spotted beetle (ladybug)
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪdiˌbiːtəl / (AmE), / ˈleɪdiˌbiːtl̩ / (BrE)
A small, round beetle, often brightly colored and spotted, that feeds on aphids and other insect pests.

Examples

A ladybeetle on leaf
A ladybeetle landed on my finger as I was working in the garden.
Gardeners often appreciate the ladybeetle because it helps control aphid populations.
We found several red and black ladybeetles crawling on the rose bushes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ladybug - alternative name for ladybeetle, especially in American English
