broking

noun
arranging financial deals
Pronunciation: / ˈbroʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
the business or activity of arranging transactions, such as buying or selling assets, on behalf of others, especially in finance or insurance

Examples

Broking firm
She has worked in broking for over a decade.
The city is a major center for financial broking and investment services.
Many people enter broking after studying economics or finance at university.

  • synonym: trading - the act of buying and selling financial assets
