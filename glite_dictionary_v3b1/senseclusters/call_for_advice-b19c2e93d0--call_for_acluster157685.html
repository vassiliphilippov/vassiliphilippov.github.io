call for advice

verb
contact someone to seek guidance
1.0
Pronunciation: / kɔl fər ədˈvaɪs / (AmE), / kɔːl fə ədˈvaɪs / (BrE)
to contact someone, typically by phone, in order to seek guidance or help about a particular issue

Examples

To call for advice from mentor

He didn't hesitate to call for advice when the problem got complicated.
If you're unsure about the new policy, just call for advice at the help desk.
