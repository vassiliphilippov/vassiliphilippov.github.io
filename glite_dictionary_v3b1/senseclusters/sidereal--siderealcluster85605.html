sidereal

adjective
relating to stars or constellations
7.7
Pronunciation: / saɪˈdɪriəl / (AmE), / saɪˈdɪəriəl / (BrE)
relating to the stars or constellations, especially in astronomy (including time based on the stars)

Examples
