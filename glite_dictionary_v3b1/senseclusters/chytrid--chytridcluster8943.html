chytrid

noun
aquatic fungus
Pronunciation: / ˈkaɪtrɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɪtrɪd /; / ˈkaɪtrɪd / (BrE)
a type of fungus (from the phylum Chytridiomycota), often found in water or soil, that can infect and harm amphibians such as frogs
