resurrection plant

noun
desert plant that revives when watered
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛzəˈrɛkʃən plænt / (AmE), / ˌrɛzəˈrɛkʃən plɑːnt / (BrE)
a small desert plant that looks dead when dry but revives and turns green when moistened

Examples
