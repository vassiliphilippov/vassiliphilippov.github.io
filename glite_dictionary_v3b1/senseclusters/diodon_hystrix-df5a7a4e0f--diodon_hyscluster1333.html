Diodon hystrix

noun

9.9

A species of porcupinefish found worldwide in warm waters, known for erecting its spines when its body is inflated.

The Diodon hystrix can inflate its body and stand its spines to deter predators.
When threatened, a Diodon hystrix puffs up dramatically, making itself appear much larger.
