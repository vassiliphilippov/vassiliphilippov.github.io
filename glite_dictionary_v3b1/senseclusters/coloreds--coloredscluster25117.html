coloreds

noun
offensive racial slur
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlɚdz / (AmE), / ˈkʌlədz / (BrE)
an offensive term formerly used to refer to people of non-white racial backgrounds, especially Black people; now considered highly inappropriate

Examples

The coloreds term
The term coloreds appears in old documents but is now recognized as deeply offensive.
Using the word coloreds in conversation is unacceptable and hurtful.
Many organizations have removed references to coloreds from their historical records.

Related Terms

  • antonym: white - a term for people of European descent
