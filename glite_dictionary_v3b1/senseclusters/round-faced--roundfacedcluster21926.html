round-faced

adjective
having a round face
1.5
Pronunciation: / raʊnd feɪst / (AmE), / raʊnd feɪst / (BrE)
Having a face that is rounded in shape rather than angular or oval.

Examples

A round-faced child
As a child, she was chubby and round-faced, with bright blue eyes.
His round-faced appearance makes him look younger than his actual age.
The actor is known for his round-faced charm and friendly smile.
