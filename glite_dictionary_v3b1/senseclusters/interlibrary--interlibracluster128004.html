interlibrary

adjective
between libraries
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈlaɪˌbrɛri / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈlaɪbri / (BrE)
relating to cooperation, sharing, or activities between two or more libraries

Examples

Interlibrary loan system
The university offers an interlibrary loan system to help students access more resources.
Many research projects depend on interlibrary cooperation to gather rare materials.
She requested a book through the interlibrary service because her local library did not have it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intralibrary - within a single library
