downness

noun
fluffy quality
9.3
Pronunciation: /ˈdaʊn.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈdaʊn.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality or state of being covered with soft fine feathers or fuzz, especially resembling down

Examples

Downness of feathers
The downness of the baby bird made it feel incredibly soft in her hands.
He admired the downness of the new blanket, which reminded him of a cloud.
The kitten's downness was obvious when it curled up on the pillow.
