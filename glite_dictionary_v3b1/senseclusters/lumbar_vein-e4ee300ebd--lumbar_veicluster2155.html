lumbar vein

noun
vein in lower back region
6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈlʌm.bɑːr veɪn/ /; / /ˈlʌm.bər veɪn/ (AmE), /ˈlʌm.bɑː veɪn/ /; / /ˈlʌm.bə veɪn/ (BrE)
any of several veins that drain the posterior body wall and the lumbar vertebral venous plexuses in the lower back area
