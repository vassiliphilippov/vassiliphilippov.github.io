garb

verb
to dress; to clothe someone
4.2
Pronunciation: / ɡɑrb / (AmE), / ɡɑːb / (BrE)
to dress or provide someone with clothing, especially in a particular style or type of clothes

Examples

To garb in costume
She was garbed in a flowing white gown for the ceremony.
The actors were garbed in elaborate costumes for the historical drama.
Volunteers garbed the children in warm coats before sending them outside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dress - to put clothes on someone or oneself
  • antonym: undress - to remove someone's clothes
