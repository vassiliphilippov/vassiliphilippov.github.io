order Chiroptera

noun
scientific order of bats
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdɚ kaɪˈrɒptərə / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə kaɪˈrɒptərə / (BrE)
the scientific order of mammals that comprises all bats, including fruit bats and insectivorous bats

Examples

Order Chiroptera bats
Fruit bats belong to the suborder Megachiroptera within the order Chiroptera.
Scientists study the evolution of flight in mammals by examining fossils from the order Chiroptera.
The order Chiroptera includes over 1,400 species of bats worldwide.
