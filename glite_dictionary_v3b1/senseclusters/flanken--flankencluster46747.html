flanken

noun
beef flank cut
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈflæŋkən / (AmE), / ˈflæŋkən / (BrE)
a cut of beef from the flank, often used in Jewish cooking, especially for stews and soups

Examples

A flanken cut
She prepared a traditional stew with flanken and vegetables for the family dinner.
The recipe calls for several pieces of flanken to add rich flavor to the soup.
At the deli, he ordered a bowl of hot flanken with carrots and potatoes.
