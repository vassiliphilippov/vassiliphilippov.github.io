particolored

adjective
having sections of different colors
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːrtɪˌkʌlɚd /; / ˈpɑrtɪˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtɪˌkʌləd /; / ˌpɑːtɪˈkʌləd / (BrE)
Having sections, patches, or areas of different, usually bright, colors.

Examples

A particoloured kite fluttered brightly against the blue sky, catching everyone's attention.

The clown wore a particoloured jacket with bold red, yellow, and green patches.
