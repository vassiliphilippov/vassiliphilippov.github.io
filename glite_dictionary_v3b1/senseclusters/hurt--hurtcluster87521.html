hurt

adjective
injured; in pain from injury
1.0 2.79/m
Pronunciation: / h…ùt / (AmE), / h…úÀêt / (BrE)
suffering physical injury, especially as a result of an accident or violence

Examples

Hurt after a fall
Only a few soldiers returned from the conflict without being hurt.
She comforted her hurt friend after his fall on the icy pavement.
The paramedics rushed to help the hurt passengers after the accident.

Synonyms

  • injured
    1.0 1.66/m
    injured describes someone physically harmed by an accident or attack, while hurt is the general state of physical injury.
  • wounded
    2.8 0.30/m
    wounded usually refers to a more serious injury from violence or battle, while hurt covers general physical harm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: injured - physically harmed or wounded
  • antonym: unhurt - not injured or harmed
  • synonym: wounded - hurt by injury, especially in battle or accident
