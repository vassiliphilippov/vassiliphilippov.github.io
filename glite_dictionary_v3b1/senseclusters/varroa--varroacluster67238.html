varroa

noun
bee mite infestation
9.9
Pronunciation: / vəˈroʊ.ə / (AmE), / vəˈrəʊ.ə / (BrE)
an infestation of honey bee colonies by Varroa mites, or the disease caused by these mites

Examples

Varroa infestation
Beekeepers must regularly check their hives for signs of varroa to protect their colonies.
A severe varroa outbreak can devastate honey bee populations in a region.
Treatments are available to help control varroa and keep bee colonies healthy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: healthy - free from disease or infestation
  • synonym: parasitism - a relationship where one organism lives off another, causing harm
