itemizer

noun
device listing items
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪtəˌmaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈaɪtəˌmaɪzə / (BrE)
a device or program that lists or records items individually, often for receipts or reports

Examples

Cash register itemizer
The cashier used an itemizer to print a detailed receipt for each purchase.
Modern supermarkets rely on an itemizer to track every product sold at the register.
The software's itemizer function helps businesses keep accurate records of inventory.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aggregator - a device or program that collects items as a group
  • synonym: recorder - a device that records information
