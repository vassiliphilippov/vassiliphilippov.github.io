press

noun
tall furniture for hanging clothes
Pronunciation: / prɛs / (AmE), / prɛs / (BrE)
a tall piece of furniture for storing clothes, usually with doors and equipped with rails or hooks for hanging garments

Examples

A clothes press
She opened the press to choose a dress for the party.
The antique press in the hallway has beautiful brass handles.
They keep their winter coats in the old oak press upstairs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: closet - a small room or space for storing clothes
  • synonym: wardrobe - a large, tall cupboard for hanging clothes
