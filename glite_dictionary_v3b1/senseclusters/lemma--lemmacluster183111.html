In linguistics and natural language processing, the canonical base form of a word chosen to represent all its inflected or variant forms (used in lemmatization and lexical analysis).

Examples

Word lemma
In NLP, the lemma represents the canonical base form used for indexing and analysis.
The tokenizer produced thousands of tokens, but the lemmatizer reduced them to unique lemmas for evaluation.
When building a lexicon, choose the lemma that best represents all inflected word forms.
