tola

noun
South Asian weight unit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊlə / (AmE), / ˈtəʊlə / (BrE)
a traditional unit of weight used in South Asia, equal to about 11.66 grams

Examples

A tola of gold
The price of gold is often quoted per tola in Indian markets.
She bought two tolas of silver for her jewelry project.
Traditional scales in the shop still measure spices in tola units.
The gold necklace weighed five tola and was beautifully crafted.
In many South Asian markets, spices are still sold by the tola.
He inherited several silver coins, each weighing exactly one tola.
