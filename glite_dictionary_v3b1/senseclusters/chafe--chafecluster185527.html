chafe

noun
skin soreness from friction
7.1 5.4
Pronunciation: /tʃeɪf/ (AmE), /tʃeɪf/ (BrE)
soreness or irritation of the skin resulting from rubbing or friction, often accompanied by warmth or redness.

Examples

Painful chafe
After the long hike, he noticed a painful chafe on his inner thigh.
Athletes often use powders to prevent chafe during vigorous exercise.
She treated the chafe with a soothing ointment to reduce the soreness.
