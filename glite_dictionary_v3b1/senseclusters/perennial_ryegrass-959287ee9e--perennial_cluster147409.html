perennial ryegrass

noun
species of grass used for lawns and pastures
8.5
Pronunciation: / pəˈrɛniəl ˈraɪˌɡræs / (AmE), / pəˈrɛnɪəl ˈraɪɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A European perennial grass (Lolium perenne) widely grown for pasture, hay, and as a lawn grass.

Examples

A perennial ryegrass field
Farmers prefer perennial ryegrass for grazing because it recovers quickly after cutting.
In cooler climates, perennial ryegrass provides excellent turf for sports fields.
