keffiyeh

noun
Middle Eastern headscarf
9.8
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈfiː.jə/ /; / /kə.ˈfiː.ə/ (AmE), /kə.ˈfiː.jə/ /; / /kə.ˈfiː.ə/ (BrE)
a traditional Middle Eastern patterned scarf, usually worn on the head or neck for protection from sun and dust, and sometimes as a symbol of Palestinian identity
