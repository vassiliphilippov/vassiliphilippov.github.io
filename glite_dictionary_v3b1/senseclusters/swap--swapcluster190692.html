swap

noun
an act of exchange
9.8
Pronunciation: / swɑp / (AmE), / swɒp / (BrE)
an act of exchanging one thing for another, especially of equal value

Examples

A clothing swap
Our school organized a clothing swap to promote sustainability.
They made a swap of baseball cards at the playground.
We did a book swap with our neighbors last weekend.
After some negotiation, they agreed on a swop of seats for the journey.
Let's do a swop—I'll give you my book for your DVD.
The children arranged a swop of trading cards at lunchtime.
