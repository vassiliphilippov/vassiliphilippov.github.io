palisade

verb
to enclose with a defensive wall or fence
6.4 7.2
Pronunciation: /pæl.ɪ.ˈseɪd/ /; / /ˈpæl.ɪ.seɪd/ (AmE), /ˈpæl.ɪ.seɪd/ /; / /pæl.ɪ.ˈseɪd/ (BrE)
to surround, protect, or fortify an area by building a strong fence of stakes or posts around it
