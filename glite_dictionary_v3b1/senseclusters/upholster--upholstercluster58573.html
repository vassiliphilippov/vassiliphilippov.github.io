upholster

verb
cover furniture with padding and fabric
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈhoʊlstɚ / (AmE), / ʌpˈhəʊlstə / (BrE)
to provide furniture, especially seats, with padding, springs, webbing, and covering fabric or leather

Examples

To upholster a chair
She learned how to upholster chairs during her interior design course.
The company specializes in upholstering vintage armchairs with luxurious fabrics.
They hired a professional to upholster their new sofa in blue velvet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reupholster - to replace the covering and padding on furniture
