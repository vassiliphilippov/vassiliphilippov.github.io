work shift

noun
a period spent at work
Pronunciation: / wɝk ʃɪft / (AmE), / wɜːk ʃɪft / (BrE)
a period of time during which a person is scheduled to work, especially as part of a rota system

Examples

A work shift schedule
After his work shift, he likes to go for a run to relax.
My work shift starts at seven in the morning and ends at three.
She usually works the night work shift at the hospital.
