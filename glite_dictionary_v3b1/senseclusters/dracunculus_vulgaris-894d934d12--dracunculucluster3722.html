Dracunculus vulgaris

noun
a southern European arum plant with a purple spathe
9.9
Pronunciation: / drəˈkʌŋkjələs vʌlˈɡɛrɪs / (AmE), / dræˈkʌŋkjʊləs vʌlˈɡɑːrɪs / (BrE)
A species of arum native to southern Europe, known for its large, dark purple spathe and distinctive unpleasant odor; also called dragon arum.
