vertically

adverb
in a vertical direction
1.8 4.76/m
Pronunciation: / Ààv…ùÀê.t…™.kli / (AmE), / Ààv…úÀê.t…™.k…ôli / (BrE)
In a vertical direction; in a way that is upright and perpendicular to the horizontal.

Examples

To move vertically
The climber scaled the wall vertically using only his hands and feet.
The elevator moves vertically between floors in the building.
Trees grow vertically to reach for sunlight.

Synonyms

  • perpendicularly
    7.3 0.15/m
    perpendicularly means being at a right angle to a surface or line, while vertically means straight up or down.
  • uprightly
    4.3 0.01/m
    uprightly describes a thing or person standing in an upright position, while vertically refers to the general up-or-down direction.
