vertically

adverb
in a vertical direction
Pronunciation: / ˈvɝː.tɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˈvɜː.tɪ.kəli / (BrE)
In a vertical direction; in a way that is upright and perpendicular to the horizontal.

Examples

To move vertically
The climber scaled the wall vertically using only his hands and feet.
The elevator moves vertically between floors in the building.
Trees grow vertically to reach for sunlight.

Related Terms

  • antonym: horizontally - in a horizontal direction
