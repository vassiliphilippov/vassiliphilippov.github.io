Synonyms

  • quay
    8.2 0.4/m
    a quay is a reinforced platform built parallel to the shore for cargo and passengers, while a dock is any harbor structure.
  • pier
    1.4 6/m
    a pier is a narrow structure that sticks out into the water for access or mooring, while a dock sits at the shore.
  • landing
    2.6 3/m
