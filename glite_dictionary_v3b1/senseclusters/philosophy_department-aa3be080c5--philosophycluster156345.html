philosophy department

noun
university division for philosophy
Pronunciation: / fəˈlɑːsəfi dɪˌpɑːrtmənt / (AmE), / fəˈlɒsəfi dɪˌpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
an academic division within a college or university that is responsible for teaching and research in philosophy

Examples

The philosophy department
Many groundbreaking papers have come out of the university's philosophy department in recent years.

The philosophy department organized a public lecture on ethics last Friday.
