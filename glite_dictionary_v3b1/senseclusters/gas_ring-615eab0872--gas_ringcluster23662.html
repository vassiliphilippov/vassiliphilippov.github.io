gas ring

noun
circular gas stove burner
5.0
Pronunciation: / ɡæs rɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡæs rɪŋ / (BrE)
a circular burner on a gas stove, consisting of a metal ring with holes through which gas is burned

Examples

A gas ring burner
After lighting the gas ring, he turned the flame down to a simmer.
Make sure the gas ring is turned off before cleaning the stove.
She placed the kettle on the gas ring to boil some water for tea.
