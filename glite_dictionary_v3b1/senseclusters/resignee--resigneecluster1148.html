resignee

noun
person who resigns
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛzɪˈniː / (AmE), / ˌrɛzɪˈniː / (BrE)
a person who has formally given up a job or position by resigning

Examples

Company resignee
After months of consideration, the resignee left her position at the company.
The company organized a farewell party for the resignee on his last day.
Each resignee is required to complete an exit interview before leaving.
