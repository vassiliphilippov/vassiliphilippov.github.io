glorifier

noun
one who praises
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɔːrɪˌfaɪər / (AmE), / ˈɡlɔːrɪˌfaɪə / (BrE)
a person who praises or makes something seem more important or admirable

Examples

A glorifier of nature
The artist became a glorifier of nature through her beautiful landscape paintings.
Some see the film as a glorifier of violence, while others view it as a warning.
He was known as a glorifier of traditional values in his community speeches.
The historian was often called a glorifier of the nation's past achievements.
Some see him as a glorifier of outdated customs rather than a true reformer.
The documentary was criticized for being a glorifier of celebrity culture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: critic - a person who expresses disapproval or finds faults
  • synonym: praiser - a person who expresses approval or admiration
