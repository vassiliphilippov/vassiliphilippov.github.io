pedestrianize

verb
to make pedestrian-only
6.2
Pronunciation: / pəˈdɛstriəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / pɪˈdɛstriənaɪz / (BrE)
to convert a street or area so that only pedestrians are allowed and vehicles are excluded

Examples

To pedestrianize a street
The city council decided to pedestrianize the downtown area to reduce traffic and pollution.
After they pedestrianized the square, it became a popular place for outdoor cafes and events.
Many European cities have pedestrianized their historic centers to attract more tourists.

Related Terms

  • synonym: close - to block or restrict access to a street or area
  • antonym: open - to allow access, especially to vehicles
