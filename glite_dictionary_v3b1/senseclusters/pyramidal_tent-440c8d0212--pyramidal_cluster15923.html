pyramidal tent

noun
large tent shaped like a pyramid
Pronunciation: / pɪˈræmɪdəl tɛnt / (AmE), / pɪˈræmɪdəl tɛnt / (BrE)
a large tent with a square or rectangular base and sloping sides that meet at a single point at the top, forming a pyramid shape; typically designed to accommodate several people

Examples

A pyramidal tent camp
After hours of hiking, they pitched a pyramidal tent at the edge of the forest.
The pyramidal tent can comfortably provide shelter for six campers at once.
We stayed in a pyramidal tent during our camping trip in the mountains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tent - a portable shelter of fabric supported by poles and ropes
