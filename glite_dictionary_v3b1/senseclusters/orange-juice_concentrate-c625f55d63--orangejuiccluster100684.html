orange-juice concentrate

noun
frozen, concentrated orange juice product
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrɪndʒ ˌdʒus ˈkɑnsənˌtreɪt / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ ˌdʒuːs ˈkɒnsənˌtreɪt / (BrE)
A thick, syrupy form of orange juice from which most of the water has been removed, usually sold frozen, to be diluted with water before drinking.

Examples

Frozen orange-juice concentrate
She added water to the orange-juice concentrate to make a fresh pitcher of juice for breakfast.
The supermarket stocks several brands of orange-juice concentrate in the frozen foods aisle.
When camping, they like to bring orange-juice concentrate because it saves space and keeps well.
