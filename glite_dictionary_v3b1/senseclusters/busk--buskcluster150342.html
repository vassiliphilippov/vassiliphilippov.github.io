busk

verb
perform music in public for money
8.9
Pronunciation: / bʌsk / (AmE), / bʌsk / (BrE)
to play music or perform entertainment in a public place and solicit money from passers-by

Examples

To busk on street
Every weekend, local musicians busk near the city train station.
He used to busk on the streets of Paris to earn extra money.
Street performers often need a permit before they can busk in public areas.
