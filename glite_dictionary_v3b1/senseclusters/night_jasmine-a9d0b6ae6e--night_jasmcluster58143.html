night jasmine

noun
fragrant West Indian shrub with night-blooming flowers
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪt ˌdʒæzmɪn / (AmE), / ˈnaɪt ˌdʒæzmɪn / (BrE)
A West Indian evergreen shrub with clusters of funnel-shaped yellow-white flowers that emit fragrance at night.

Examples

The night jasmine flowers
Locals believe that night jasmine brings good luck when it blooms outside the house.
