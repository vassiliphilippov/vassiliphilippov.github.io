platforming

noun
building digital platforms
Pronunciation: / ˈplætfɔːrmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈplætfɔːmɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of designing, building, or operating a software or online platform to support services and third-party integration

Examples

Platforming software tools
The company's platforming strategy allowed developers to build new features on top of their service.
Effective platforming can attract more partners and expand the ecosystem.
They invested heavily in platforming to support third-party app integration.

  • synonym: platform development - the process of creating and maintaining a digital platform
