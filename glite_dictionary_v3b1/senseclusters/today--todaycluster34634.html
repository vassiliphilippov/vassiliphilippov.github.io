today

noun
the current day
Pronunciation: / təˈdeɪ / (AmE), / təˈdeɪ / (BrE)
the day that includes the present moment; the current day, as opposed to yesterday or tomorrow

Examples

Today only
Today is her birthday, so we're planning a surprise party.
I have a lot of meetings scheduled for today, so I'll be busy all afternoon.
What are your plans for today after work?
