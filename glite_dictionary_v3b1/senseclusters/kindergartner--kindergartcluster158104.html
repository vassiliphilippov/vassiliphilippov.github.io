kindergartner

noun
child attending kindergarten
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪndɚˌɡɑːrtnɚ / (AmE), / ˌkɪndəˈɡɑːtnə / (BrE)
a child who attends a kindergarten, especially in the first year of school

Examples

Young kindergartners play
At recess, the kindergartners played together on the playground.
Each kindergartner brought a favorite book for story time.
The kindergartner carefully painted a picture of her family in class.
