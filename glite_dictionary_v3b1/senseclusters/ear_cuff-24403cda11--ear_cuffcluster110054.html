ear cuff

noun
jewelry worn clipped to the ear rim
7.5
Pronunciation: / ɪr kʌf / (AmE), / ɪə kʌf / (BrE)
A piece of jewelry designed to be worn on the rim of the ear without the need for piercing.

Examples

A ear cuff jewelry
She wore a delicate ear cuff that wrapped around the edge of her ear.
The ear cuff complemented her other jewelry perfectly at the party.
Unlike traditional earrings, an ear cuff does not require the ear to be pierced.
