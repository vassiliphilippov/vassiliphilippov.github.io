  • accordion fold
    5.5 0.00/m
    Shows repeated parallel folds that open and close like bellows
    vs z-fold: a series of parallel folds alternately made in opposite directions, forming a zig-zag pattern like the bellows of an accordion
  • concertina fold
    6.7 0.00/m
