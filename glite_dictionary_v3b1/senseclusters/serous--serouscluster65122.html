serous

adjective
relating to or containing serum
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪr.əs / (AmE), / ˈsɪə.rəs / (BrE)
relating to, producing, or containing serum, especially a thin, watery bodily fluid found in many tissues and cavities of the body.

Examples

Serous fluid
A serous membrane lines the body's internal cavities, secreting a lubricating fluid.
An accumulation of serous exudate can indicate inflammation or injury.
The surgeon noted the presence of a serous fluid in the wound.
