attachment

noun
emotional bond or affection
Pronunciation: / əˈtætʃmənt /; / əˈtæt͡ʃmənt / (AmE), / əˈtætʃmənt /; / əˈtat͡ʃmənt / (BrE)
a feeling of affection, fondness, loyalty, or emotional connection towards a person, place, or cause

Examples

Strong attachment to family
Children often form a close attachment to their caregivers.
Her strong attachment to her hometown made it difficult for her to move away.
Over time, I developed a deep attachment to my coworkers.
Despite the controversy, his attachment to the political movement remained unshaken.
Her strong attachment to the charity inspired many others to volunteer.
The monk’s attachment to his faith guided every aspect of his life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adherence - faithful attachment or support
  • synonym: affection - a gentle feeling of fondness or liking
  • synonym: allegiance - loyalty or commitment to a group or cause
  • antonym: detachment - a lack of emotional involvement or interest
