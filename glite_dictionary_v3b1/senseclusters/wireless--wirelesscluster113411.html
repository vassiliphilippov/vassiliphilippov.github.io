wireless

adjective
not using wires; transmitting signals without cables
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪrləs / (AmE), / ˈwaɪəlɪs / (BrE)
using or involving the transmission of signals or power without the use of wires or cables

Examples

Wireless internet connection
He installed a wireless alarm system in his house for added security.
Many people prefer wireless headphones because they are more convenient to use.
The office is equipped with a wireless internet connection for all employees.

Related Terms

  • antonym: wired - using wires or cables for transmission
