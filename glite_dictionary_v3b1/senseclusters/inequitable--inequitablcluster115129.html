inequitable

adjective
unfair; unjust
1.3
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɛkwɪtəbl̩ / (AmE), / ɪˈnɛkwɪtəbl̩ / (BrE)
not fair; unjust or unequal in treatment or distribution

Examples

An inequitable pay scale
Many workers felt the inequitable pay scale discouraged motivation and loyalty.
The court ruled that the contract was inequitable and favored only one party.
They protested against the inequitable distribution of educational resources in the city.

Related Terms

  • antonym: equitable - fair and impartial
  • synonym: unfair - not just or equitable
