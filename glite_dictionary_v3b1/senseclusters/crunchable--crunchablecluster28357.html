crunchable

adjective
can be chewed crisply
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌntʃəbəl / (AmE), / ˈkrʌntʃəbəl / (BrE)
able to be chewed with a crisp, crackling sound or texture

Examples

A crunchable carrot
The fresh carrots were so crunchable that everyone enjoyed them at the picnic.
She prefers crunchable snacks like pretzels and crackers over soft pastries.
The chef added crunchable croutons to the soup for extra texture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crispy - having a firm, dry, and easily broken texture
  • antonym: soft - easy to bite or chew without making a sound
