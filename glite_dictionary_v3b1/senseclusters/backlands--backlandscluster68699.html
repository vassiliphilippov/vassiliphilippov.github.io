backlands

noun
remote rural areas
Pronunciation: / ˈbæklændz / (AmE), / ˈbæklændz / (BrE)
remote or sparsely populated regions far from cities or towns, often with difficult living conditions

Examples

The backlands region
Few families choose to settle in the backlands because of the harsh environment.
The novel describes the struggles of people living in the backlands of the country.
Travelers must prepare carefully before venturing into the backlands for exploration.

Related Terms

  • antonym: city - a large and densely populated urban area
  • synonym: hinterland - an area lying beyond what is visible or known, especially remote regions
  • synonym: wilderness - a wild and uninhabited area
