genetic profiling

noun
analysis of DNA for identification
2.8 4.0
Pronunciation: /dʒə.ˈne.tɪk ˈproʊ.faɪ.lɪŋ/ /; / /dʒɪ.ˈne.tɪk ˈproʊ.faɪ.lɪŋ/ (AmE), /dʒə.ˈne.tɪk ˈprəʊ.faɪ.lɪŋ/ /; / /dʒɪ.ˈne.tɪk ˈprəʊ.faɪ.lɪŋ/ (BrE)
