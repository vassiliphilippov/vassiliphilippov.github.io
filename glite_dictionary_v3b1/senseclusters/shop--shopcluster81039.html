shop

noun
school class for learning a trade
Pronunciation: / ʃɑp / (AmE), / ʃɒp / (BrE)
a class or course in school where students learn practical skills such as woodworking, mechanics, or other trades

Examples

Shop class
During high school, my favorite subject was shop because I enjoyed working with my hands.
He learned how to use power tools in shop class.
Many students create their first wood projects in shop.
