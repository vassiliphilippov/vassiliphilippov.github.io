chamber

noun
an enclosed space, natural or artificial
1.6 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃeɪm.bər/ (AmE), /ˈtʃeɪm.bə/ (BrE)
an enclosed space, either natural or artificial, such as inside a cave or in the body

Examples
