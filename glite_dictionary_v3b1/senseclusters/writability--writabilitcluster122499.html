writability

noun
ability to be written
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌraɪtəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌraɪtəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being able to be written to or modified, especially in computing or data storage

Examples

Writability of files
The writability of this document is restricted to administrators only.
Before saving changes, check the writability of the target folder.
Some filesystems allow you to adjust the writability of individual files for security.

Related Terms

  • synonym: editability - the quality of being able to be edited or changed
  • antonym: read-only - able to be read but not written or modified
