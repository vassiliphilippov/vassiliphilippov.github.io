tee up

verb
place a golf ball on a tee
9.2
Pronunciation: / ti ʌp / (AmE), / tiː ʌp / (BrE)
to place (a golf ball) on a tee in preparation for playing a shot

Examples

To tee up a ball
Before every drive, she tees up her ball and takes a deep breath.
He carefully teed up his ball before taking the first swing.
The caddie teed up the ball for the golfer on the first hole.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tee - a small stand used to support a stationary ball in sports like golf
