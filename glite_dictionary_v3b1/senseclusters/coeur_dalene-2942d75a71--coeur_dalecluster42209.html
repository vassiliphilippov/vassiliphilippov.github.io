Coeur d'Alene

noun
a Native American people of northern Idaho
Pronunciation: / koʊˌɜːr dəˈleɪn / (AmE), / kɜː dəˈleɪn / (BrE)
a member of a Native American people living primarily in northern Idaho near Coeur d'Alene Lake

Examples

Coeur d'Alene tribe member
