bestseller

noun
book or product with very high sales
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌbɛstˈsɛlɚ / (AmE), / ˌbɛstˈsɛlə / (BrE)
a book or other product that sells in very large numbers, especially soon after it is released

Examples

A bestseller book
Her debut novel quickly became a bestseller and topped the charts for months.
Many bestsellers are later adapted into major motion pictures.
The author’s new book is predicted to be the next summer bestseller.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blockbuster - something very successful, especially in entertainment
  • antonym: flop - something that is not successful, especially in entertainment or sales
