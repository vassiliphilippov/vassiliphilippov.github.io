cottony

adjective
resembling or as soft as cotton
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːtni / (AmE), / ˈkɒtəni / (BrE)
Resembling cotton in appearance or texture; as soft or fluffy as cotton.

Examples

Cottony soft texture
Fog settled over the hills, giving the landscape a cottony appearance.
She wrapped herself in a cottony robe after her shower, enjoying its soft warmth.
The plant's cottony fibers made it ideal for stuffing pillows and cushions.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: silky - having a smooth, soft texture like silk
  • synonym: velvety - having a soft, smooth texture like velvet
  • synonym: woolly - resembling wool
