nonradiative

adjective
not emitting radiation
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌreɪdiˈeɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌreɪdiˈeɪtɪv / (BrE)
not emitting energy as radiation or light; energy is transferred or converted within the system instead
