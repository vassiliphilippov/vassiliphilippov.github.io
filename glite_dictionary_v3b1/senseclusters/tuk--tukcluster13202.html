tuk

noun
gentle nudge
a short, quick push or gentle nudge given to attract attention or prompt action

Examples

A gentle tuk on the arm
She gave him a gentle tuk on the arm to let him know it was his turn.
A quick tuk on the shoulder made him look up from his book.
He responded to the soft tuk by turning around and smiling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: poke - a quick push with a finger or object
  • synonym: tap - a light touch or hit, often to get attention
