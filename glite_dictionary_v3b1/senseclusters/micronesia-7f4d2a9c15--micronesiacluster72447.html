Micronesia

proper noun
country in the western Pacific Ocean
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪ.kroʊˈniː.ʒə / (AmE), / ˌmaɪ.krəʊˈniː.zɪə / (BrE)
a country in the western Pacific Ocean, consisting of islands scattered over Micronesia, with a constitutional government in free association with the United States; independent since 1986.

Examples

Micronesia country
Micronesia consists of more than 600 islands spread across the Pacific Ocean.
After achieving independence in 1986, Micronesia established diplomatic relations with many countries.
The capital of Micronesia is Palikir, located on the island of Pohnpei.
