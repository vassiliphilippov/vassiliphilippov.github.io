oriental person

noun
offensive term for an East or Southeast Asian person
Pronunciation: / ˌɔriˈɛntəl ˈpɝsən / (AmE), / ˌɔːriˈɛntəl ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
An offensive and outdated term referring to a person from East Asia or Southeast Asia; now considered inappropriate and disrespectful.

Examples

Oriental person term
In the early 20th century, many documents used the phrase oriental person to refer to people from Asia.
She explained why calling her an oriental person rather than Asian was disrespectful.
The term oriental person is now widely recognized as offensive and should be avoided.

Related Terms
