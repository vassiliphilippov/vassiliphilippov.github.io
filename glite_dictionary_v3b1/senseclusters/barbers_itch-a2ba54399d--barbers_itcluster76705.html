barber's itch

noun
fungal infection of the beard area
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑrbərz ɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbəz ɪtʃ / (BrE)
A fungal infection of the skin, especially of the beard area of the face and neck, causing inflammation and irritation.

Examples

Barber's itch infection

Many cases of barber's itch respond well to antifungal creams and improved hygiene.
Traditional barbers had to be careful to prevent the spread of barber's itch among their customers.
