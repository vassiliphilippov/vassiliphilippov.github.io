Okapia

proper noun
genus including the okapi
Pronunciation: / oʊˈkæpiə / (AmE), / əʊˈkɑːpiə / (BrE)
A genus of mammals in the giraffe family, comprising the okapi, native to the rainforests of central Africa.

Examples

Scientists classified the newly discovered animal under the genus Okapia.
Taxonomists debated the placement of Okapia within the giraffe family for years.
The only living species in Okapia is the okapi, native to the Congo rainforest.
