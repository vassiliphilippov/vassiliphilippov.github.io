Synonyms

  • provide
    1.5 478/m
    provide is commonly used to give something needed in many situations, while supply is a general formal word for making things available.
  • give
    1.0 371/m
    give is a simple everyday verb for handing or creating things and feelings, while supply focuses on providing needed goods formally.
  • render
    3.9 4.92/m
    render is a formal way to provide help, service, or payment, while supply means to make goods or items available.
  • furnish
    3.7 9.62/m
    furnish is used for equipping rooms or people with furniture or tools, while supply means to give what is needed more generally.
  • issue
    2.5 63/m
    issue is to officially give documents or goods from an organization, while supply is a broader act of making things available to people.
  • ply
    6.6 0.47/m
    ply is to give someone things, often repeatedly or to persuade, while supply is a neutral word for providing what is needed.
