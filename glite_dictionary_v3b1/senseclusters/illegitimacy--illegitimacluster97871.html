illegitimacy

noun
state of being illegal or unlawful
Pronunciation: / ˌɪləˈdʒɪtəməsi / (AmE), / ˌɪlɪˈdʒɪtɪməsi / (BrE)
the state or quality of being illegal or not authorized by law

Examples

Illegitimacy of the law
Many activists protested against the illegitimacy of the newly enacted law.
The company was fined after the illegitimacy of its business practices was revealed.
The court questioned the illegitimacy of the evidence presented by the prosecution.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illegality - the state of being illegal
  • antonym: legitimacy - the state of being legal or authorized by law
