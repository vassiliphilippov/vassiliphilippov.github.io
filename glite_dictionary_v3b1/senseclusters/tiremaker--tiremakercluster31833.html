tiremaker

noun
maker of headdresses
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪərˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈtaɪəˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who makes tires or headdresses, especially women's headdresses or hair ornaments (archaic)

Examples

18th-century tiremaker
In the 18th century, a skilled tiremaker was essential for fashionable ladies attending court.
The museum displayed a collection of ornate headdresses crafted by a renowned tiremaker.
She aspired to become a tiremaker and design elegant hair ornaments for noblewomen.
