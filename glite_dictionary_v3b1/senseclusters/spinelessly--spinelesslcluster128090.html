spinelessly

adverb
without a spine
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪnlɪsli / (AmE), / ˈspaɪnlɪsli / (BrE)
in a manner that lacks a spinal column or backbone; without a spine, especially referring to animals or anatomical structures.

Examples

Jellyfish move spinelessly
The jellyfish drifted spinelessly through the clear ocean water.
Under the microscope, the juvenile worm moved spinelessly across the slide, its body soft and flexible.
Some sea creatures move spinelessly, allowing them to squeeze through narrow spaces.

  • synonym: invertebrately - in a way that lacks a backbone or spinal column
