dimetrodon

noun
Permian-era carnivorous reptile with a sail-like fin
Pronunciation: / daɪˈmɛtrəˌdɑn / (AmE), / daɪˈmiːtrədɒn / (BrE)
a large, carnivorous prehistoric reptile of the Permian period, characterized by a prominent sail-like fin on its back

Examples

A dimetrodon fossil
A fossil of a dimetrodon was discovered in a Texas quarry.
Children were fascinated by the illustration of a dimetrodon in their dinosaur book.
The dimetrodon used its sail to regulate body temperature, scientists believe.
