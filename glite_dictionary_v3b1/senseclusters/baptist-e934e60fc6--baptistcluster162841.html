Baptist

noun
member of a Christian denomination
Pronunciation: / ˈbæptɪst / (AmE), / ˈbæptɪst / (BrE)
a member of a Christian denomination that believes in baptism of believers by full immersion

Examples

Baptists church members
Many Baptists practice full-immersion baptism as part of their faith.
She was raised as a Baptist and attended church every Sunday.
The Baptist community organized a charity event for the town.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Catholic - member of the Roman Catholic Church
  • synonym: Methodist - member of a Protestant Christian denomination emphasizing methodical worship
