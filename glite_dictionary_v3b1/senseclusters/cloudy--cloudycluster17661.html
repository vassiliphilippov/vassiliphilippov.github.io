cloudy

adjective
vague or ill-defined; not clear
3.1 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈklaʊ.diː/ (AmE), /ˈklaʊ.diː/ (BrE)
not clear or having no definite form or limits; vague or ill-defined, especially in reference to ideas or situations

Examples

Cloudy explanation
His explanation of the issue remained cloudy and did not answer anyone's questions.
She wrote a cloudy essay that left the reader confused about her main point.
The committee reached only a cloudy understanding of the new regulations.
