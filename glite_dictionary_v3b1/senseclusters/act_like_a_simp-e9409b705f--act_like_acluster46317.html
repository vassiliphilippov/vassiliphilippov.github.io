act like a simp

verb
behave submissively to gain affection
5.8 8.5
Pronunciation: /ækt laɪk ə sɪmp/ (AmE), /ækt laɪk ə sɪmp/ (BrE)
to behave in an excessively submissive or attentive way, especially in an attempt to gain the affection or approval of someone, often someone one is attracted to

Examples

To act like a simp
He's always acting like a simp around her, buying gifts and doing favors hoping she'll notice him.
She accused him of acting like a simp when he rearranged his plans just to help her out.
Stop acting like a simp; you don't have to agree with everything she says.
