I'll be darned

interjection
expression of surprise or disbelief
Pronunciation: / aɪl bi ˈdɑrn(d) / (AmE), / aɪl bi ˈdɑːnd / (BrE)
Used to express surprise or disbelief.

Examples

I'll be darned!
I'll be darned if that isn't the strangest thing I've ever seen.
I'll be darned, I never thought you'd make it on time!
When I saw the final score, all I could say was 'I'll be darned.'

Related Terms
