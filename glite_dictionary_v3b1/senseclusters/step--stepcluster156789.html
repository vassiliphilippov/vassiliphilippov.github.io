step

noun
an act of moving by lifting and setting down the foot
1.0
Pronunciation: / stɛp / (AmE), / stɛp / (BrE)
an act of moving by lifting one foot and putting it down in a new position, especially as part of walking or running

Examples

A careful step
He took a cautious step forward in the dark room.
She counted each step as she climbed the staircase.
The child's first step was an exciting moment for her parents.
