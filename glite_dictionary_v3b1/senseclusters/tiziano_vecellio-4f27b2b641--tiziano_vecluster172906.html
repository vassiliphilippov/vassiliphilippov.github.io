Tiziano Vecellio

proper noun
Renaissance Venetian painter, known as Titian
Pronunciation: / tiˈtsjɑːnoʊ veˈtʃɛljoʊ / (AmE), / tɪˈtsiːɑːnəʊ veˈtʃɛliəʊ / (BrE)
