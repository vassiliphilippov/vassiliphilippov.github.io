denote

verb
be a sign or indication of
3.3
Pronunciation: / dɪˈnoʊt / (AmE), / dɪˈnəʊt / (BrE)
to be a sign or symbol of something; to represent or mean something

Examples

Red flag denotes danger
A red flag often denotes danger in many countries.
In mathematics, 'π' denotes the ratio of a circle’s circumference to its diameter.
The sign on the door denotes that smoking is not allowed.
By wearing white, she denoted her support for peace.
She denoted her feelings clearly during the meeting.
The gesture denotes his willingness to help.
A red octagon usually denotes a stop sign in traffic signals.
In chemistry, 'H2O' denotes water.
The word 'dog' denotes a domesticated canine animal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: announce - to make a public declaration about something
  • antonym: connote - to imply or suggest an additional meaning
  • synonym: indicate - to point out or show
