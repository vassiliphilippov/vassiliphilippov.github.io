milky

adjective
pale white or cloudy like milk
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlki / (AmE), / ˈmɪlki / (BrE)
pale white or cloudy in appearance, like milk; not clear or transparent.

Examples

A milky white color
A milky mist hung over the fields at dawn.
She chose a vase made of milky glass for the flowers.
The water in the pond appeared milky after the rainstorm.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - transparent; easy to see through
  • synonym: cloudy - not clear or transparent; murky
  • synonym: opaque - not able to be seen through; not transparent
