interactive multimedia

noun
multimedia system with interactive features
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈæktɪv ˌmʌltiˈmiːdiə / (AmE), / ˌɪntərˈæktɪv ˌmʌltɪˈmiːdiə / (BrE)
a computer-based system combining different forms of media, such as text, images, and sound, that allows users to access information and control the presentation interactively

Examples

An interactive multimedia tutorial
Our team developed an interactive multimedia tutorial that helps users learn new software step by step.
Schools increasingly use interactive multimedia in classrooms to engage students in the learning process.
The museum installed an interactive multimedia exhibit that lets visitors explore ancient artifacts in detail.
