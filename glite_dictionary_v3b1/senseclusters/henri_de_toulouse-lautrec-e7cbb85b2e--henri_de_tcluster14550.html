Henri de Toulouse-Lautrec

proper noun
French painter noted for Parisian nightlife scenes
Pronunciation: / ɑ̃ˈri də tuːˈluːs loʊˈtrɛk / (AmE), / ɑ̃ˈri də tuːˈluːs ləʊˈtrɛk / (BrE)
(1864–1901) French painter noted for his portrayals of Parisian nightlife, especially scenes from cafes and music halls in Montmartre.

Examples

Henri de Toulouse-Lautrec paintings
