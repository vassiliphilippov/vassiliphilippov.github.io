Elops saurus

noun
species of game fish, ladyfish
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈiːlɑps ˈsɔːrəs / (AmE), / ˈiːlɒps ˈsɔːrəs / (BrE)
A species of game fish of the family Elopidae, resembling the tarpon but smaller; also known as the ladyfish.

Examples

The Elops saurus fish
Anglers often prize the Elops saurus for its energetic fight when hooked.
The Elops saurus is commonly found in coastal waters of the western Atlantic Ocean.
Unlike the larger tarpon, the Elops saurus rarely exceeds a length of three feet.
