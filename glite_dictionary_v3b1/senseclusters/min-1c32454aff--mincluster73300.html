Min

proper noun
Egyptian god of fertility
Pronunciation: / mɪn / (AmE), / mɪn / (BrE)
An ancient Egyptian god associated with procreation, fertility, and reproduction.

Examples

Ancient Min god
Min was worshipped as the god of fertility in ancient Egypt.
Statues of Min were often carried in processions celebrating the harvest.
The cult of Min was especially prominent in the city of Akhmim.
