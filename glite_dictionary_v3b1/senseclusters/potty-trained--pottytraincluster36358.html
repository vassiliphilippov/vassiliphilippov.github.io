potty-trained

adjective
having learned to use the toilet
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːti treɪnd / (AmE), / ˈpɒti treɪnd / (BrE)
(of a child) having learned to use the toilet instead of a diaper or nappy.

Examples

Potty-trained child
It can take several months for a child to become fully potty-trained.
Most children are potty-trained by the time they start nursery school.
She was excited when her son finally became potty-trained at age two.
