untoned

adjective
lacking color variation
7.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtoʊnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtəʊnd / (BrE)
lacking noticeable variation in color or shade; appearing pale, flat, or uniform in appearance

Examples

Untoned colors

After a long winter, the landscape looked dull and untoned under the cloudy sky.
She preferred photographs with rich contrasts rather than those that appeared untoned and flat.
