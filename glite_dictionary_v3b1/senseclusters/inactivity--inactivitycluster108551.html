Synonyms

  • stagnancy
    7.3 0.02/m
    stagnancy describes a lack of change or progress in a place or system, while inactivity means simple lack of movement or action.
  • idleness
    4.5 1.01/m
    idleness is staying inactive from choice or laziness, while inactivity is a neutral state of no movement or action.
  • inertia
    6.5 2.03/m
