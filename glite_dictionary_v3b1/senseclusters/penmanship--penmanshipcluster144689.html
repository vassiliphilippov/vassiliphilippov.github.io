penmanship

noun
skill of beautiful handwriting
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛn.mənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈpɛn.mən.ʃɪp / (BrE)
the art or skill of writing by hand, especially in a clear or beautiful style

Examples

Beautiful penmanship
His penmanship is so elegant that people admire his letters.
She received an award for outstanding penmanship in her class.
Teachers often emphasize good penmanship when teaching young children how to write.

Related Terms

  • synonym: handwriting - writing done by hand, especially in contrast to print or type
