grafter

noun
tissue-transplant surgeon
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræftər / (AmE), / ˈɡrɑːftə / (BrE)
a surgeon or medical specialist who transplants tissue or attaches grafts, such as skin or organ grafts

Examples

Surgical grafter
The hospital hired an experienced grafter to perform the complex skin transplant for the burn patient.
As a skilled grafter, she specialized in delicate organ transplants.
The grafter carefully attached the donor tissue to ensure a successful recovery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: transplant surgeon - a doctor who performs organ or tissue transplants
