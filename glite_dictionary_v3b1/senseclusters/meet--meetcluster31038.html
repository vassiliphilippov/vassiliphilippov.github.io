  • converge
    5.5 2/m
    converge means coming together from different directions to a single point, while meet is a more general word for coming together.
  • gather
    1.0 50/m
    gather means collecting people or things into one place, often by choice, while meet is simply coming together.
  • assemble
    3.3 7/m
