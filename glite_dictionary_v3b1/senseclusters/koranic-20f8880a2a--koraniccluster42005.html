Koranic

adjective
of or relating to the Koran
Pronunciation: / kɔˈrɑːnɪk / (AmE), / kɒˈrɑːnɪk / (BrE)
Relating to the Koran, the sacred text of Islam.

Examples

Koranic text
His research focuses on the Koranic interpretation of ethical issues.
Many Koranic verses are recited during daily prayers.
The school offers a course on Koranic studies and Islamic history.
