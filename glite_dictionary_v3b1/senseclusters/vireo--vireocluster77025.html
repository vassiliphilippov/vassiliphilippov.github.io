vireo

noun
small insectivorous American songbird
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪrioʊ / (AmE), / ˈvɪəriəʊ / (BrE)
A small insect-eating American songbird, typically olive-grey in color, belonging to the Vireonidae family.
