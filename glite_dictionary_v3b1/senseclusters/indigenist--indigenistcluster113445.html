indigenist

adjective
supporting Indigenous rights
7.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɪdʒənɪst / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɪdʒənɪst / (BrE)
relating to the support or promotion of Indigenous peoples, their cultures, or their rights

Examples

Indigenist movement
The new law was praised by indigenist groups for protecting traditional lands.
Her indigenist perspective influenced the museum's approach to displaying cultural artifacts.
Many activists identify as indigenist in their efforts to promote language preservation.
