germy

adjective
containing germs; unsanitary
4.5 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd í…úrmi / (AmE), / Ààd í…úÀêmi / (BrE)
full of germs or microorganisms that can cause disease; dirty or unsanitary.

Examples

A germy kitchen
After a week without cleaning, the kitchen sponge became incredibly germy.
She always carries hand sanitizer for wiping down germy surfaces in public places.
The children refused to play in the germy sandbox at the park.

Synonyms

  • unsanitary
    3.7 0.77/m
    unsanitary describes places that are dirty and likely to spread disease, while germy is an informal word for being full of germs.
  • unhygienic
    4.8 0.19/m
    unhygienic describes actions or places that lack hygiene and can cause illness, while germy is a casual word meaning full of germs.
