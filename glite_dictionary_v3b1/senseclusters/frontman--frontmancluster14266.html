frontman

noun
figurehead owner
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈfrʌnt.mæn/ (AmE), /ˈfrʌnt.mæn/ (BrE)
a person who appears to be the owner or leader of something to conceal the true controllers, often in dishonest or illegal activities

Examples

Fake frontman

She acted as a frontman to protect the true leaders from public scrutiny.
