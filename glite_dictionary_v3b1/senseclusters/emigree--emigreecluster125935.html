emigree

noun
person who emigrates
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɛmɪˌɡri / (AmE), / ˈɛmɪˌɡriː / (BrE)
A person who leaves their own country to live permanently in another.

Examples

An emigree from country
As an emigree, she often felt torn between two cultures.
Many emigrees settled in the city, bringing with them new traditions and skills.
The book tells the story of a Russian emigree building a new life abroad.
