Jesus freak

noun
extremely enthusiastic Christian
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːzəs friːk / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːzəs friːk / (BrE)
A person who is extremely enthusiastic or outspoken about their Christian faith, often to the point of being perceived as excessive or zealous.
