collage

noun
collection of diverse items or elements
5.1 1.67/m
Pronunciation: / k…ôÀàl…ëÀê í / (AmE), / k…ôÀàl…ëÀê í / (BrE)
A combination or collection of various items or elements, especially those that are diverse or unrelated, brought together to form a whole.

Examples

A collage of memories
At her retirement party, they shared a collage of memories from her long career.
The article presented a collage of opinions from experts around the world.
The documentary is essentially a collage of personal stories, interviews, and archival footage.

Synonyms

  • montage
    6.2 3.67/m
    montage is an artistic sequence of images or scenes, while collage is a visual artwork made by sticking different materials together.
  • composition
    4.1 22/m
    composition is the arrangement of parts to form a whole, while collage combines diverse, often unrelated items in a visible layered artwork.
  • compilation
    3.5 2.79/m
    compilation is a gathered set of similar items collected in one place, while collage mixes varied elements into a single visual artwork.
