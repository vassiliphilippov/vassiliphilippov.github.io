picnic ham

noun
shoulder cut of pork, usually smoked
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪk.nɪk hæm / (AmE), / ˈpɪk.nɪk ham / (BrE)
a cut of pork from the shoulder of a hog, usually smoked and sometimes cured, often used as a less expensive alternative to traditional ham

Examples

A picnic ham cut
A picnic ham is often more affordable and still provides plenty of flavor.
He bought a picnic ham to serve at the family reunion.
The recipe called for a smoked picnic ham rather than a traditional cured one.
