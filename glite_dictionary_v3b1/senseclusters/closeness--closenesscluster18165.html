Synonyms

  • nearness
    1.0 0.73/m
    nearness feels simple and direct for physical distance, while closeness is a broader word for how near things are.
  • proximity
    4.2 11/m
    proximity sounds more formal and can mean near in space, time, or relation, while closeness focuses on physical nearness.
  • adjacency
    6.8 0.10/m
    adjacency means being next to or touching something, while closeness covers any general sense of being near in position.
