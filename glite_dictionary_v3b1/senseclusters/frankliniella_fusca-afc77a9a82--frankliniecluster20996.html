Frankliniella fusca

noun
species of thrips harmful to crops
9.9
Pronunciation: / fræŋkˌlɪniˈɛlə ˈfʌskə / (AmE), / fræŋkˌlɪniˈelə ˈfʌskə / (BrE)
A species of thrips, commonly known as the tobacco thrips, that is harmful to tobacco and peanut crops.

Examples

Frankliniella fusca insect
Farmers monitor their fields for Frankliniella fusca to prevent damage to tobacco plants.
Infestations of Frankliniella fusca can cause significant loss in peanut yields.
The spread of Frankliniella fusca has become a major concern for Southern growers.
