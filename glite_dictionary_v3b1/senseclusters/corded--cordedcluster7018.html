corded

adjective
having parallel raised lines (of textiles)
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrdɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɔːdɪd / (BrE)
(of textiles) having parallel raised lines or ridges, typically as part of the weave or surface texture.

Examples

Corded fabric texture
He preferred shirts with a corded finish for their unique look and feel.
She wore a jacket made of corded velvet that shimmered in the light.
The corded fabric gave the drapes a rich, textured appearance.

Related Terms
