accident of birth

noun
circumstance one is born into, not chosen
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈæk.sɪ.dənt əv bɝːθ / (AmE), / ˈæk.sɪ.dənt əv bɜːθ / (BrE)
a circumstance or condition, such as social status or nationality, into which a person is born, that affects their life or future opportunities and over which they have no control

Examples

An accident of birth
Many people attribute their success or struggle to an accident of birth.
She never forgot that her education was made possible by an accident of birth.
Your nationality is often determined entirely by an accident of birth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: privilege - an advantage or right held by a person or group
