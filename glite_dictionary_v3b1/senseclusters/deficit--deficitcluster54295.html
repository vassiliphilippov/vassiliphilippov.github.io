deficit

noun
deficiency in mental or brain functioning
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛfəsɪt / (AmE), / ˈdɛfɪsɪt / (BrE)
a deficiency or impairment in mental or neurological functioning, resulting in reduced ability or performance in a specific area

Examples

Cognitive deficit
Doctors are working to identify the cause of the cognitive deficit observed in some elderly patients.
Language deficits are common among children with certain learning disabilities.
The patient exhibited a significant deficit in short-term memory following the injury.
