golf cart

noun
small vehicle for golfers
Pronunciation: / ɡɑlf kɑrt / (AmE), / ɡɒlf kɑːt / (BrE)
a small motorized vehicle designed to carry golfers and their equipment around a golf course

Examples

A golf cart ride
He drove the golf cart from one hole to the next in just a few minutes.
The club requires all players to return their golf carts after finishing their rounds.
We loaded our bags onto the golf cart before starting the game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buggy - a small open vehicle, sometimes used for golf or recreation
