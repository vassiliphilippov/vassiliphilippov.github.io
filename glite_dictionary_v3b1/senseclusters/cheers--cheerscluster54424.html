cheers

noun
shouts of approval
1.4
Pronunciation: /tʃɪrz/ (AmE), /tʃɪəz/ (BrE)
shouts of approval, encouragement, or applause made by a group of people, especially at an event or performance

Examples

Loud cheers from fans
The crowd erupted in loud cheers as the singer took the stage.
After the final whistle, cheers echoed throughout the stadium.
The students' cheers encouraged the team to keep playing their best.

  • antonym: booing - shouts expressing disapproval or dislike
