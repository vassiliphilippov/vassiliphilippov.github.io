humidly

adverb
in a damp way
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhjuːmɪdli / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that is damp, moist, or filled with humidity

Examples

Air presses humidly
The jungle air pressed humidly against their skin as they walked deeper into the forest.
She spoke humidly of summer nights spent by the lake after a heavy rain.
The windows fogged up humidly as the storm continued outside.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dryly - in a way that lacks moisture
  • synonym: moistly - in a way that is slightly wet or damp
