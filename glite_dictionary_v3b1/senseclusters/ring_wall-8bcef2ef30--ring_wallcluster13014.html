ring wall

noun
defensive circular wall
7.5
Pronunciation: /rɪŋ wɔːl/ (AmE), /rɪŋ wɔːl/ (BrE)
a circular or oval wall built around a settlement or site for defense, especially in ancient times

Examples

Ancient ring wall
The ancient city was protected by a massive ring wall that encircled its entire perimeter.
Archaeologists discovered the remains of a ring wall dating back to the Bronze Age.
Villagers would retreat behind the ring wall whenever they feared an attack.
