ribbit

verb
to croak like a frog
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪbɪt / (AmE), / ˈrɪbɪt / (BrE)
to make the characteristic croaking sound of a frog

Examples

Frogs ribbit loudly
At night, dozens of frogs ribbit in the pond behind our house.
The children laughed as they tried to ribbit like frogs during the play.
You could hear a single frog ribbit in the quiet after the rain.
