platoon

noun
convoy of automated/connected vehicles
7.3
Pronunciation: /plə.ˈtuːn/ (AmE), /plə.ˈtuːn/ (BrE)
(in transportation and automated driving) a convoy of autonomous or connected vehicles travelling closely together under coordinated or automated control to improve efficiency, traffic flow, or safety.

Examples

A platoon of trucks
Automakers showcased a platoon of semi-autonomous trucks driving in sync along the highway.
Researchers tested a platoon of connected cars to measure fuel savings and traffic-flow improvements.
City planners hope that highway platoons will reduce congestion and improve road safety for everyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: convoy - a group of vehicles or ships traveling together, often for mutual support or efficiency.
  • synonym: vehicle platooning - the practice or technology of linking vehicles to travel closely together under coordinated control.
