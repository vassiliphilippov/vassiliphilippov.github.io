community college

noun
two-year local public college
3.0 53/m
Pronunciation: / k…ôÀàmjun…ôti Ààk…ël…™d í / (AmE), / k…ôÀàmjuÀên…™ti Ààk…íl…™d í / (BrE)
A nonresidential, two-year college offering courses that cater to the local community's educational and vocational needs, often leading to an associate degree or facilitating transfer to a four-year university.

Examples

A community college course
After graduating from high school, she enrolled at a community college to save money before transferring to a university.
Many community colleges offer flexible night classes for working adults.
The community college hosted a career fair to connect students with local employers.

Synonyms
