tollbooth

noun
small building for collecting tolls
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊlˌbuːθ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊlˌbuːθ / (BrE)
A small building at a tollgate where drivers stop to pay a fee to use a road, bridge, or tunnel.

Examples

A tollbooth building
Many highways are replacing the traditional tollbooth with an electronic payment system.
She reached for her change as she slowed down at the tollbooth on the bridge.
The long line of cars waited patiently at the tollbooth during rush hour.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tollgate - a point where a toll is collected on a road or bridge
