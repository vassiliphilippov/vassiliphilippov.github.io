mischievously

adverb
in a playful, naughty, or disobedient way
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪstʃəvəsli / (AmE), / ˈmɪs.tʃɪ.vəs.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows a playful desire to cause trouble or annoyance, often for fun.

Examples

To mischievously hide things
He mischievously hid his friend's backpack as a prank.
She grinned mischievously and refused to reveal her secret.
The puppy mischievously chewed on the new shoes while no one was looking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: naughtily - in a naughty or disobedient way
