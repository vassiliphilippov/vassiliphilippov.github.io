mucormycosis

noun
serious fungal infection
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmjuːkɔːrmaɪˈkoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌmjuːkɔːmaɪˈkəʊsɪs / (BrE)
a serious fungal infection caused by molds of the order Mucorales, often affecting people with weakened immune systems

Examples

mucormycosis infection
The patient developed mucormycosis after a long stay in the intensive care unit.
Doctors warned that people with diabetes are at higher risk for mucormycosis.
Early diagnosis and treatment are crucial for those suffering from mucormycosis.
