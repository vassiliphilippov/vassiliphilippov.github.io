sampler

noun
an assortment of different samples
Pronunciation: / ˈsæmplɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɑːmplə / (BrE)
a collection or assortment of different samples, especially of products, food, or artistic works, offered for testing or selection

Examples

Cheese sampler
She ordered a cheese sampler to share with the table.
The bakery offered a sampler of their most popular cookies to new customers.
The exhibition included a sampler of emerging artists' work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sample - a specimen or small part of something intended to show the quality or character of the whole
