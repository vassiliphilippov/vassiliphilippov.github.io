yoni

noun
female reproductive organ
Pronunciation: / ˈjoʊni / (AmE), / ˈjəʊni / (BrE)
a Sanskrit term for the female reproductive organs, especially the vulva or womb, often used in anatomical or spiritual contexts

Examples

The yoni in anatomy
In some yoga traditions, the yoni is honored as a symbol of creation and life.
The sculpture in the temple represents the yoni as a sacred form.
Medical texts sometimes use the term yoni when discussing female anatomy in historical contexts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lingam - a Sanskrit term for the male reproductive organ, especially in spiritual contexts
  • synonym: vulva - the external female genital organs
