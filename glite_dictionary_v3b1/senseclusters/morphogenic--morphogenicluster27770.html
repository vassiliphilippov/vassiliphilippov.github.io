morphogenic

adjective
pattern formation
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌmɔːrfəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɔːfəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
relating to the formation or development of patterns or structures, especially in non-living systems such as physical or chemical processes

Examples

Morphogenic pattern formation

Computational models help scientists understand morphogenic processes in geological formations.
