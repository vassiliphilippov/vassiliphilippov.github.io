fencepost

noun
off-by-one error
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛnsˌpoʊst / (AmE), / ˈfɛnsˌpəʊst / (BrE)
an error in counting or programming caused by misunderstanding whether to include endpoints, often resulting in an off-by-one mistake
