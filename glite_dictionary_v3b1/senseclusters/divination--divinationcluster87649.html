divination

noun
prediction by supernatural or divine means
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪvəˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪvɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or practice of trying to foretell the future or discover hidden knowledge by supernatural or mystical means

Examples
