Reaganite

adjective
of Reagan's policies
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪɡənaɪt / (AmE), / ˈreɪɡənaɪt / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of Ronald Reagan, his policies, or the ideas associated with him or his era

Examples

Reaganite policies
The senator's Reaganite approach to economic policy appealed to many voters in the 1980s.
Many historians debate the long-term effects of Reaganite foreign policy decisions.
Her Reaganite views on government spending were clear during the debate.

  • antonym: liberal - favoring progress and reform in politics
