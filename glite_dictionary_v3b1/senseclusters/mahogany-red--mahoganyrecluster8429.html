mahogany-red

adjective
brown with a red tinge, like mahogany
Pronunciation: / məˈhɑɡəni rɛd / (AmE), / məˈhɒɡəni rɛd / (BrE)
having a brown color tinged with red, resembling the shade of mahogany wood

Examples

Mahogany-red paint

The antique chair was upholstered in a rich mahogany-red fabric that matched the wood frame.
