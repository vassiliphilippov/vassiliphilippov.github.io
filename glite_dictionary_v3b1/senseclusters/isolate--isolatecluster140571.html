isolate

verb
separate experiences from their emotions (psychology)
2.5 6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˈaɪsəleɪt / (BrE)
to separate experiences from the emotions associated with them, often as a psychological defense mechanism

Examples

To isolate feelings
He learned to isolate unpleasant experiences, keeping the feelings locked away.
Some people isolate traumatic memories from how they feel about them.
Therapists may help patients who isolate their emotions from past incidents.
