spangle

verb
decorate with shiny objects
Pronunciation: / ˈspæŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈspæŋɡl̩ / (BrE)
to decorate or cover with small, shiny objects or spangles, often to create a glittering effect

Examples

To spangle a dress
She loved to spangle her costumes for the dance competition each year.
The designer chose to spangle the dress with silver sequins, making it shimmer under the lights.
Tiny crystals spangled the surface of the artwork, catching every ray of light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adorn - to decorate or add beauty to something
  • synonym: embellish - to make something more attractive by adding decorative details
