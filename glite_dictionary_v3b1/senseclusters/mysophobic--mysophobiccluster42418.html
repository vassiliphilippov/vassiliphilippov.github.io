mysophobic

adjective
afraid of dirt or contamination
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪsəˈfoʊbɪk / (AmE), / ˌmaɪsəˈfəʊbɪk / (BrE)
Abnormally afraid of dirt or contamination; suffering from the fear of germs or uncleanliness.
