detassel

noun
removal of tassels
Pronunciation: / diˈtæsəl / (AmE), / diˈtæsəl / (BrE)
the act or process of removing the tassels from plants, especially corn, to control pollination or produce hybrid seed

Examples

The detassel process
The detassel of corn is an important step in producing hybrid seeds.
Farm workers begin the detassel each summer to ensure the purity of the crop.
Proper detassel helps control which plants pollinate the corn in the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hybridization - the process of producing hybrids by cross-breeding plants
  • synonym: pollination - the transfer of pollen to enable fertilization in plants
