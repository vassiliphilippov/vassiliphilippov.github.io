latchkey

noun
unsupervised child at home
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈlætʃ.kiː/ (AmE), /ˈlætʃ.kiː/ (BrE)
a child who returns to an empty home after school because their parents are at work or otherwise absent

Examples

Latchkey child alone
After school, the latchkey waited alone until his mother finished her shift.
Many latchkey children learn to be independent at a young age.
The teacher noticed that several students in her class were latchkey kids.

Related Terms

  • synonym: latchkey child - a child who is regularly left at home without adult supervision
