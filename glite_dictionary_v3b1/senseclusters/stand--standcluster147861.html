stand

noun
a support or base
2.6 2.5
Pronunciation: / stænd / (AmE), / stænd /; / stand / (BrE)
a piece of furniture, structure, or booth used to support, hold, display, or sell things

Examples

A stand for books
A delicate vase sat atop an iron stand near the window.
She placed her microphone on the stand before starting her speech.
The statue rested securely on a wooden stand in the center of the gallery.
He bought a new telephone stand for the hallway.
She placed her alarm clock on the stand next to her bed.
The potted plant looked lovely sitting on the small wooden stand.
Each vendor set up a stand at the Saturday market.
The festival was filled with food stands offering snacks and drinks.
We bought fresh lemonade from the stand at the park.
