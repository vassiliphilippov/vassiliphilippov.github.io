Beezer

proper noun
British comic magazine
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːzə / (BrE)
the title of a British weekly comic magazine published from 1956 to 1993, featuring humorous cartoon strips for children

Examples

The Beezer comic
As a child, he eagerly awaited each new issue of the Beezer every week.
Her father collected Beezer comics and shared them with her on rainy afternoons.
Many classic British cartoon characters first appeared in the pages of the Beezer.

  • synonym: The Dandy - another well-known British children's comic magazine
