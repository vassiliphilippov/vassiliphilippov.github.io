trochlearis

noun
the fourth cranial nerve of the eye
9.9
Pronunciation: /trɑːk.ˈlɪə.rɪs/ /; / /trɑːk.ˈlɪ.rɪs/ (AmE), /trɒk.ˈlɪə.rɪs/ /; / /trɒk.ˈlɪə.rəs/ (BrE)
