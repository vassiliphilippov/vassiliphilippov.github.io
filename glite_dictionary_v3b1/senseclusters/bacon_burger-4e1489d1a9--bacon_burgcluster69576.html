bacon burger

noun
hamburger with bacon
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪkən ˌbɜːrɡər / (AmE), / ˈbeɪkən ˌbɜːɡə / (BrE)
A hamburger that includes bacon as one of its toppings.

Examples

A bacon burger with cheese
