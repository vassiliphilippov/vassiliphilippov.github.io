amanitin

noun
toxic mushroom compound
Pronunciation: / əˈmænɪtɪn / (AmE), / əˈmænɪtɪn / (BrE)
a highly poisonous compound found in certain mushrooms, which is toxic because it blocks RNA synthesis and is sometimes used in scientific research
