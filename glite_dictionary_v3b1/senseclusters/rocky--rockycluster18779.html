rocky

adjective
full of rocks or stones
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑki / (AmE), / ˈrɒki / (BrE)
Full of rocks or stones; containing or covered with rocks.

Examples

Rocky ground
The garden's soil is too rocky for most plants to grow well.
The hikers struggled to cross the rocky terrain that stretched for miles.
We set up our tent on a rocky beach overlooking the sea.
