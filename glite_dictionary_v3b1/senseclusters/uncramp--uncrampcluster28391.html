uncramp

verb
to make less crowded
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkræmp / (AmE), / ʌnˈkræmp / (BrE)
to make a space less cramped or crowded by increasing room or improving the arrangement.

Examples

To uncramp a room
We decided to uncramp the office by moving some desks into the hallway.
Opening the windows helped uncramp the small kitchen and made it feel more spacious.
They rearranged the furniture to uncramp the living room for the party.

Related Terms

  • antonym: crowd - to fill a space so that it becomes cramped
  • synonym: declutter - to remove unnecessary items to make a space tidier
