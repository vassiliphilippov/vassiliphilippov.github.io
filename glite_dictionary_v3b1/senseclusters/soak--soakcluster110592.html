soak

verb
heat metal before working
Pronunciation: / soʊk / (AmE), / səʊk / (BrE)
(metallurgy) To heat a metal and hold it at a specific temperature before working it.

Examples

To soak metal
Engineers carefully monitor the furnace when soaking the metal slabs ahead of further processing.
The technician will soak the steel ingots before forging to ensure uniform temperature.
To improve malleability, the alloy must be soaked prior to rolling.
