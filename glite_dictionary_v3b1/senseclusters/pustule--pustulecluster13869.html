pustule

noun
pus-filled skin blister
8.1 0.44/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp ås.t Éul / (AmE), / Ààp ås.tjuÀêl / (BrE)
A small, inflamed, raised area of skin filled with pus.

Examples

A pustule on skin
A pustule may indicate an infection that needs medical attention.
Several pustules appeared on his face during the allergic reaction.
The dermatologist examined the pustule on her arm and prescribed a topical cream.
