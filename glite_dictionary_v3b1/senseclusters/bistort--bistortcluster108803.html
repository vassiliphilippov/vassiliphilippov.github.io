bistort

noun
perennial meadow plant
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪstɔrt / (AmE), / ˈbɪstɔːt / (BrE)
a perennial plant with twisted roots, often found in damp meadows and used in herbal medicine

Examples

Wild bistort plant
The herbalist collected bistort for its medicinal properties.
In spring, pink flowers appear on the tall stems of bistort in the meadow.
Some traditional recipes use the roots of bistort as a thickener.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knotweed - a plant of the same family with jointed stems
