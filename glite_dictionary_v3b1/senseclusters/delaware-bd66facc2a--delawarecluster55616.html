Delaware

noun
Algonquian language of the Delaware (Lenape) people
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛləˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈdɛləwɛə / (BrE)
the Algonquian language traditionally spoken by the Delaware (Lenape) people of the eastern United States.

Examples

Delaware language
Few people today speak Delaware, but efforts are being made to revitalize the language.

The word for 'river' in Delaware is very different from that in neighboring languages.
