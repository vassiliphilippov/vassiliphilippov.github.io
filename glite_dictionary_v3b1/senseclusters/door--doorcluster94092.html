door

noun
space in a wall for entry or exit
Pronunciation: / dɔr / (AmE), / dɔː / (BrE)
a movable barrier or the opening in a wall, building, or vehicle that allows people to enter or leave

Examples

Open the door
Light spilled in from the door at the end of the hallway.
She stood in the door, waiting for her guests to arrive.
The cat darted through the open door and disappeared outside.
He forgot to lock the door before leaving the house.
Rain beat against the front door all night.
She opened the door quietly to avoid waking anyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doorway - an entranceway through a door
  • synonym: gate - a movable barrier in a fence or wall, typically giving access to a garden or field
  • antonym: window - an opening in a wall fitted with glass to let in light and air
