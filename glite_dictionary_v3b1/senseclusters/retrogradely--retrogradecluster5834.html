retrogradely

adverb
in a regressive way
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛtroʊˌɡreɪdli / (AmE), / ˈrɛtrəʊˌɡreɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that involves moving backward to an earlier or less advanced state

Examples

Retrogradely change laws
The reforms were implemented retrogradely, reversing many modern policies.
Some critics argue that the law changes society retrogradely rather than moving it forward.
Technological advances can be lost if a country develops retrogradely due to conflict.

Related Terms

  • antonym: progressively - in a way that moves forward or advances
  • synonym: regressively - in a manner that returns to a previous or less developed state
