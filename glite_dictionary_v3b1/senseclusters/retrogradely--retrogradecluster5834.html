retrogradely

adverb
in a regressive way
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈre.trə.greɪd.liː/ /; / /re.trə.ˈgreɪd.liː/ (AmE), /ˈre.trə.greɪd.liː/ /; / /re.trə.ˈgreɪd.liː/ (BrE)

Examples
