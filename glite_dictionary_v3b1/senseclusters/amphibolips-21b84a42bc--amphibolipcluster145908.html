Amphibolips

noun
genus of gall wasps causing oak galls
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæm.fəˈboʊ.lɪps / (AmE), / ˌam.fɪˈbəʊ.lɪps / (BrE)
A genus of gall wasps in the family Cynipidae, especially known for causing oak-apple galls.

Examples

Amphibolips wasp galls
Galls produced by Amphibolips can vary greatly in size and appearance.
Scientists studied several species of Amphibolips to understand gall formation on oaks.
The oak tree was covered in galls formed by Amphibolips wasps.
