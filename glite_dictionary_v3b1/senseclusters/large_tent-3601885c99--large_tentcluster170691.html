large tent

noun
big tent used for events or gatherings
1.9
Pronunciation: / lɑrdʒ tɛnt / (AmE), / lɑːdʒ tɛnt / (BrE)
a big tent used as a temporary shelter for outdoor events or gatherings

Examples

A large tent for events

The wedding reception was held in a beautiful large tent near the lake.
They rented a large tent to shelter guests from the rain during the outdoor event.
