roadability

noun
suitability for roads
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌroʊdəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌrəʊdəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of a vehicle or equipment that makes it suitable for use on roads

Examples

Roadability of a bus
The roadability of the new bus model was praised by transportation experts.
Engineers improved the roadability of the equipment to ensure safe travel on highways.
Before purchase, the roadability of each vehicle was carefully evaluated by the team.
