fad diet

noun
a popular but short-lived diet trend
6.3 5.6
Pronunciation: / fæd ˈdaɪət / (AmE), / fad ˈdaɪət / (BrE)
A diet that restricts certain foods or food groups and becomes very popular for a short period of time, often without scientific evidence supporting its effectiveness.

Examples

A fad diet trend
Nutritionists advise against following a fad diet without considering potential health risks.
She lost weight on a fad diet, but gained it back soon after stopping.
The popularity of the fad diet faded quickly when people saw little long-term success.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crash diet - a very restrictive diet intended for rapid weight loss
