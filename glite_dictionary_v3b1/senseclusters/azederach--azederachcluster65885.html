azederach

noun
tree with purple blossoms, inedible fruits
8.9 9.3
Pronunciation: /æ.zə.ˈdær.æk/ /; / /æ.zɪ.ˈdær.æk/ (AmE), /æ.zə.ˈdæ.rək/ /; / /æ.zɪ.ˈdæ.rək/ (BrE)
A tree native to northern India and China, known for its purple blossoms and small inedible yellow fruits, often grown in the southern United States as a shade tree.

Examples

The azederach tree
Although the azederach bears yellow fruits, they are not suitable for eating.

The garden was shaded by a mature azederach near the fence.
