cut down

verb
cause to fall by force or violence
Pronunciation: / kʌt daʊn / (AmE), / kʌt daʊn / (BrE)
to cause someone or something to fall to the ground, especially by force or violence

Examples

To cut down an enemy
During the raid, the police cut down several gunmen.
The boxer cut down his opponent with a swift punch.
The old tree was cut down by the strength of the wind.
