magnification

noun
ratio of image size to object size
4.9 4.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmæɡnəfɪˈkeɪʃən /; / ˌmæɡ.nɪ.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌmaɡnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən /; / ˌmaɡ.nɪ.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process of making something appear larger, or the degree to which it is enlarged, especially using a lens or microscope
