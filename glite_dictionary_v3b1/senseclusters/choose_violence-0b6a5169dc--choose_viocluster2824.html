choose violence

verb
opt for aggression or confrontation
Pronunciation: / tʃuz ˈvaɪə.ləns / (AmE), / tʃuːz ˈvaɪə.ləns / (BrE)
to decide to respond with aggressive or confrontational actions rather than peaceful or conciliatory ones

Examples
