brevis

adjective
short in form
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛvɪs / (AmE), / ˈbriːvɪs / (BrE)
used in scientific names to indicate that a species or feature is short in form or length

Examples

Brevis species name
The species name brevis was chosen because the plant has unusually short leaves.
In zoology, brevis often describes animals with compact or shortened body parts.
The Latin word brevis appears in many scientific names to highlight a short characteristic.

Related Terms

  • antonym: longus - used in scientific names to indicate something is long
