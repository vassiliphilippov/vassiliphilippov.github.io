laid low

adjective
unable to function due to illness
6.9
Pronunciation: / leɪd loʊ / (AmE), / leɪd ləʊ / (BrE)
Unable to function or out of action, typically due to illness or misfortune.

Examples

Be laid low by injury
Many athletes have been laid low by injuries this season.
She was laid low by a nasty bout of flu and missed the meeting.
The company was laid low by a devastating cyber attack.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bedridden - confined to bed due to illness or infirmity
  • synonym: incapacitated - unable to act or function
