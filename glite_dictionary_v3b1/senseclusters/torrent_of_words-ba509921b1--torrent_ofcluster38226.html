torrent of words

noun
rapid, continuous speech
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔr.ənt əv wɝdz / (AmE), / ˈtɒr.ənt əv wɜːdz / (BrE)
a rapid and continuous flow of speech or talk, often delivered with great intensity or emotion

Examples

A torrent of words flows

Caught up in excitement, she spilled out a torrent of words about her adventure.
The politician unleashed a torrent of words during the heated debate.
