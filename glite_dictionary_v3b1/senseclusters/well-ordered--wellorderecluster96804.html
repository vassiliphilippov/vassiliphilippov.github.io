well-ordered

adjective
systematically or logically arranged
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛl ˌɔrdɚd / (AmE), / ˈwɛl ˌɔːdəd / (BrE)
Arranged or organized in a systematic, orderly, or logical way.

Examples

A well-ordered system
A well-ordered system of government is necessary for stability.
She maintained a well-ordered routine to manage her busy schedule.
The files were kept in a well-ordered cabinet for easy access.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orderly - arranged or organized neatly and methodically
