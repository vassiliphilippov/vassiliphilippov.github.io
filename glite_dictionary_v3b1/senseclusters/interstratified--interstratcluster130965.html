interstratified

adjective
alternating layers
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈstrætɪfaɪd / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈstrætɪfaɪd / (BrE)
having layers of different materials alternately arranged within a structure or formation

Examples

Interstratified rock layers
The geologist identified interstratified layers of sandstone and shale in the core sample.
Some minerals are found in interstratified formations, making extraction more complex.
The museum displayed a rock with clearly interstratified bands of different colors.
