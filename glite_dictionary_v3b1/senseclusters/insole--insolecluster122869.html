insole

noun
inner sole of a shoe or boot
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌsoʊl / (AmE), / ˈɪn.səʊl / (BrE)
the inner part of a shoe or boot that lies directly beneath the foot and provides comfort or support

Examples

A insole in shoes
She added a new insole to her running shoes for extra comfort.
Special orthopedic insoles can help reduce foot pain.
The insole of my boot keeps sliding out when I walk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sole - the bottom part of a shoe or foot
