talking picture

noun
film with synchronized speech and singing
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔkɪŋ ˈpɪktʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɔːkɪŋ ˈpɪktʃə / (BrE)
(historical) A motion picture with synchronized recorded speech and singing; a film with a soundtrack, as opposed to a silent film.

Examples

Early talking pictures
Many early talking pictures incorporated musical performances to showcase the new technology.
She was amazed when she saw her first talking picture after years of silent films.
The first talking picture revolutionized the film industry in the late 1920s.

Related Terms

  • antonym: silent film - a film with no synchronized recorded sound
