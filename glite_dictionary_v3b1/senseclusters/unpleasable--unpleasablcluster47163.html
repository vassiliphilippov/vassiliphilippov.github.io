unpleasable

adjective
impossible to satisfy
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpliːzəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈpliːzəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to satisfy or make happy, no matter what is done

Examples

An unpleasable customer
No matter what we did, the unpleasable customer found something to complain about.
Her boss was so unpleasable that employees stopped trying to impress him.
It can be exhausting to work with someone who is completely unpleasable.
No matter how hard we tried, our manager remained unpleasable throughout the entire project.
Some customers are simply unpleasable and will always find something to complain about.
He has an unpleasable attitude that makes working with him very difficult.
