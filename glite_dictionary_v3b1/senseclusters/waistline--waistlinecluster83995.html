waistline

noun
the narrower part of the body between ribs and hips
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪstˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈweɪstˌlaɪn / (BrE)
the part of the body that is narrower between the ribs and hips, or the measurement around this area

Examples

The waistline measurement
Regular exercise can help reduce your waistline.
She measured her waistline before buying a new dress.
The jeans sit just below the waistline for a comfortable fit.
