abomasum

noun
fourth stomach compartment in ruminants
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæboʊˈmeɪsəm / (AmE), / ˌabəˈmeɪsəm / (BrE)
the fourth and final compartment of the stomach in ruminant animals, where true digestion takes place

Examples

The abomasum compartment
The abomasum is sometimes called the 'true stomach' in cattle and sheep.
Unlike the other compartments, the abomasum secretes digestive enzymes similar to those in a human stomach.
Veterinarians examined the cow's abomasum for signs of disease.
