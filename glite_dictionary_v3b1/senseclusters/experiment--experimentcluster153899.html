experiment

noun
test or trial to discover something
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspɛr.ə.mənt /; / ɪkˈspɛrəmənt / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɛr.ɪ.mənt /; / ɪkˈspɛrɪmənt / (BrE)
an attempt to try something new or different to see what happens

Examples

A life experiment
Moving to a new city was an experiment in starting over for her.
The company's latest experiment in remote work policies attracted a lot of attention.
Their experiment with flexible hours improved productivity and employee satisfaction.
As an experiment, she took a different route home to see if it was faster.
He viewed his new diet as an experiment to improve his health.
The company launched a marketing experiment to reach younger customers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: routine - a regular and unvarying procedure; not new
  • synonym: test - a procedure for assessing or proving something
  • synonym: trial - a test to assess something's performance or quality
