territorial

adjective
relating to a territory or area
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛrəˈtɔriəl /; / ˌtɛr.əˈtɔr.i.əl / (AmE), / ˌtɛrɪˈtɔːriəl /; / ˌtɛr.ɪˈtɔː.ri.əl / (BrE)
relating to or connected with a particular territory, area, or the territory of a state or ruler

Examples

Territorial rights
Many animals have territorial instincts and will defend their area from intruders.
The territorial government passed new environmental regulations last year.
They are engaged in a territorial dispute over the border between the two countries.
International law protects the territorial integrity of sovereign nations.
The country's territorial waters extend for 12 nautical miles off its coast.
The dispute centered on the territorial rights of indigenous peoples.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extraterritorial - existing or taking place outside the territory of a state or ruler
  • synonym: sovereign - independent and self-governing, especially in reference to states
