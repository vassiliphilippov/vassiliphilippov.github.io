scraplet

noun
small or puny person
Pronunciation: / ˈskræplɪt / (AmE), / ˈskræplɪt / (BrE)
an informal and often derogatory term for a very small or puny person or creature

Examples

Little scraplet
The coach doubted the scraplet could keep up with the rest of the team.
Despite being a scraplet, she surprised everyone with her determination.
He was teased at school for being a scraplet among his classmates.

  • synonym: runt - the smallest or weakest in a group, especially of animals
