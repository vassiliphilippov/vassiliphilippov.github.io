a prefix meaning 'under', 'below', or 'less than normal', used in words like 'hypothermia' or 'hypoglycemia'

Examples

Hypo in 'hypothermia'
The prefix hypo in 'hypoglycemia' means 'below normal' blood sugar levels.
Doctors use hypo to describe conditions where something is less than normal, like 'hypotension'.
In medical terms, hypo often signals a deficiency or lower amount, as in 'hypocalcemia'.
