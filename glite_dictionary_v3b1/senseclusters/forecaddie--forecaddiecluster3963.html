forecaddie

noun
golf ball collector
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔːrkædi / (AmE), / ˈfɔːkædi / (BrE)
a person employed to collect and return golf balls at a driving range or practice area

Examples

Driving range forecaddie
The forecaddie made sure all the golf balls were collected before the next group arrived.
After his shift, the forecaddie returned the buckets of balls to the clubhouse.
A forecaddie is essential for keeping the driving range organized and safe.

Related Terms
