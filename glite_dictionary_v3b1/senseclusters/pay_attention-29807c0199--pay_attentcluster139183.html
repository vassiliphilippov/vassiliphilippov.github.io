pay attention

verb
focus or concentrate on something
1.0
Pronunciation: / peɪ əˈtɛnʃən / (AmE), / peɪ əˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
to focus your mind or concentrate on someone or something; to listen or watch carefully

Examples

To pay attention carefully
Can you pay attention for a moment while I explain the instructions?
It’s important to pay attention in class if you want to understand the material.
She failed to pay attention to the warning signs and got lost.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ignore - deliberately pay no attention; refuse to notice
  • synonym: listen - to give attention to sound or speech
