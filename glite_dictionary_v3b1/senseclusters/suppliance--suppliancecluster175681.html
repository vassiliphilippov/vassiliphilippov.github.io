suppliance

noun
humble request
Pronunciation: / səˈplaɪəns / (AmE), / səˈplaɪəns / (BrE)
the act of making a humble request or plea, especially to someone in authority

Examples

A suppliance for help
Her heartfelt suppliance convinced the council to reconsider their decision.
The prisoner’s suppliance for mercy was met with silence from the judge.
In his letter, he made a humble suppliance for forgiveness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: demand - an insistent and forceful request
  • synonym: supplication - the act of asking or begging for something earnestly or humbly
