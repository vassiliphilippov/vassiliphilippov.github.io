consenting

adjective
having given consent or permission
Pronunciation: / kənˈsentɪŋ / (AmE), / kənˈsentɪŋ / (BrE)
having given permission or agreement to something, especially after considering the matter

Examples

Consenting adults
The experiment could only proceed with consenting participants.
The law requires that all consenting parties understand the agreement fully.
They are both consenting adults, capable of making their own decisions.
