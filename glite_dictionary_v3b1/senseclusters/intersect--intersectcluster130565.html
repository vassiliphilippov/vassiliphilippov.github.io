intersect

verb
cross or meet at a point
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.tərˈsɛkt / (AmE), / ˌɪn.təˈsɛkt / (BrE)
(of two or more lines, roads, or paths) to cross or meet at a point.

Examples

Lines intersect at point
At this point, the river and the railway intersect.
The lines intersect at a right angle on the graph.
The two roads intersect near the center of town.
