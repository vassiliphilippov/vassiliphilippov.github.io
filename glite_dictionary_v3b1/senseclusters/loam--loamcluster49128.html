loam

noun
fertile soil mixture
4.7 4.3
Pronunciation: / loʊm / (AmE), / ləʊm / (BrE)
A rich, fertile soil that is a mixture of sand, clay, and decaying organic matter, suitable for growing plants.

Examples

Fertile loam soil
A handful of loam feels slightly moist, crumbly, and rich in organic material.
The gardener recommends adding loam to improve the soil quality in your flower beds.
This region is famous for its fertile loam, which is ideal for growing vegetables.
