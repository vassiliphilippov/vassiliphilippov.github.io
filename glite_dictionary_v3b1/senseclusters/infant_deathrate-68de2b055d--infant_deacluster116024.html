infant deathrate

noun
deaths of infants under one year, per 1,000 births
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnfənt ˈdɛθˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪnfənt ˈdɛθˌreɪt / (BrE)
the number of deaths of infants under one year of age per 1,000 live births in a given period or population

Examples

High infant deathrate
A high infant deathrate often indicates poor access to nutrition and medical services.
Many developing countries are working to lower their infant deathrate through improved healthcare.
Public health initiatives have successfully reduced the infant deathrate in several regions over the past decade.
