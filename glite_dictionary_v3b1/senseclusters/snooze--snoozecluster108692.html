snooze

noun
alarm delay feature/duration
3.4 0.49/m
Pronunciation: / snuÀêz / (AmE), / snuÀêz / (BrE)
a device feature or the period of time by which an alarm or notification is temporarily silenced or delayed.

Examples

The snooze button
I hit the snooze and slept another nine minutes before the alarm sounded again.
Most calendar apps show a temporary snooze option to delay meeting reminders.
She set a one-hour snooze for notifications while she finished her work.
