economic theory

noun
principles explaining economic activities

Pronunciation: /ek.ə.ˈnɑː.mɪk ˈθiː.riː/ /; / /iː.kə.ˈnɑː.mɪk ˈθɪə.riː/ (AmE), /ek.ə.ˈnɒ.mɪk ˈθɪə.riː/ /; / /iː.kə.ˈnɒ.mɪk ˈθɪə.riː/ (BrE)
