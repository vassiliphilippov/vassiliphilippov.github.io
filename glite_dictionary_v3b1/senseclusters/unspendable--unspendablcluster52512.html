unspendable

adjective
cannot be spent
8.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈspɛndəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈspɛndəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be spent or used as money

Examples

An unspendable amount
The old currency notes became unspendable after the government introduced new designs.
Because the gift card had expired, its balance was now unspendable.
Some damaged coins are considered unspendable and must be exchanged at the bank.
