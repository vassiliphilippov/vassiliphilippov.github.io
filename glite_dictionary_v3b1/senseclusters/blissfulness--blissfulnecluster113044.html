blissfulness

noun
state of great happiness
Pronunciation: / ˈblɪsfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈblɪsfʊlnəs / (BrE)
A state of great joy and happiness; the quality of being extremely happy.

Examples

Complete blissfulness
Meditation helped her achieve a state of complete blissfulness after a stressful week.
She radiated blissfulness on her wedding day, smiling at everyone in the room.
The peaceful retreat gave him a sense of blissfulness he had never experienced before.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bliss - great joy or happiness
  • antonym: misery - a state of great suffering or unhappiness
