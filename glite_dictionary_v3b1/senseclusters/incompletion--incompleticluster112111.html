incompletion

noun
state of being unfinished
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnkəmˈpliːʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪnkəmˈpliːʃən / (BrE)
the state of being unfinished or not fully completed

Examples

Project incompletion
The project suffered from incompletion due to a lack of funding.
His essay was marked down for its incompletion and missing sections.
The building's incompletion was obvious, with scaffolding still surrounding it.
The construction project remained in a state of incompletion for several months.
She was frustrated by the incompletion of her application due to missing documents.
The artist's sudden illness led to the incompletion of his final painting.

  • synonym: incompleteness - the quality of not being complete
