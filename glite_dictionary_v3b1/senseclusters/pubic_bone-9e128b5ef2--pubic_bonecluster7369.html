pubic bone

noun
bone forming the front of the pelvis
5.0 0.15/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàpjuÀêb…™k bo än / (AmE), / ÀàpjuÀêb…™k b…ô än / (BrE)
one of the three sections of the hipbone, forming the front part of the pelvis in humans and many animals.

Examples

The pubic bone front
Doctors examined the patient for any damage to the pubic bone after the fall.
In anatomy class, we learned how the pubic bone connects to other parts of the pelvis.
The pubic bone can be fractured in accidents involving the pelvis.

Synonyms

  • pubis
    7.4 0.17/m

  • os pubis
    8.7 0.00/m
