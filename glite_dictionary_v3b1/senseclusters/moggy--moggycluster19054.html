moggy

noun
non-pedigree cat
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːɡi / (AmE), / ˈmɒɡi / (BrE)
a cat, especially one that does not belong to a particular breed or have a pedigree

Examples

A moggy cat
The children adopted a stray moggy from the animal shelter.
Her moggy loves to sleep in the sunniest spot in the house.
Unlike purebred cats, a moggy can have a mix of different features.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cat - a small domesticated feline animal
  • antonym: pedigree - an animal with a recorded ancestry or breed
