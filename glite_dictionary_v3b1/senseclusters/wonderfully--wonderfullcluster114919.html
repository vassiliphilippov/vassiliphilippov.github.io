wonderfully

adverb
in an excellent or impressive way
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌndɚfəli / (AmE), / ˈwʌndəfəli / (BrE)
In an excellent or impressive manner; extremely well or to a great extent.

Examples

Handled wonderfully
He handled the unexpected situation wonderfully, keeping everyone calm and reassured.
Jennifer sang wonderfully at the concert last night, impressing the entire audience.
The garden looked wonderfully vibrant after the spring rain.
