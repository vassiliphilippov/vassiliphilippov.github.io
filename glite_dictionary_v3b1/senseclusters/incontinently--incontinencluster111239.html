incontinently

adverb
immediately (archaic)
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɑntɪnəntli / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɒntɪnəntli / (BrE)
without delay; immediately or at once (archaic usage)

Examples

To leave incontinently
Upon hearing the alarm, he ran incontinently out of the building.
The messenger departed incontinently to deliver the urgent letter.
She answered incontinently, not pausing to consider her words.

Related Terms

  • antonym: eventually - after a period of time; in the end
  • synonym: immediately - without any delay; at once
  • synonym: instantly - at once; without delay
