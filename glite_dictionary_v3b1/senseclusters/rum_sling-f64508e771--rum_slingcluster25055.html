rum sling

noun
rum-based cocktail with sugar and water
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌm slɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrʌm slɪŋ / (BrE)
A cocktail consisting of rum mixed with sugar, water, and sometimes lemon juice.

Examples

A rum sling cocktail
After a long day, he liked to relax with a cold rum sling in hand.
She ordered a rum sling to enjoy with her dinner on the terrace.
The bartender prepared a classic rum sling garnished with a slice of lemon.
