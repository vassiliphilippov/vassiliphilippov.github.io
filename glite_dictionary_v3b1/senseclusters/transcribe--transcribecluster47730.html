transcribe

verb
arrange music for another instrument/medium
Pronunciation: / trænˈskraɪb / (AmE), / trænˈskraɪb / (BrE)
to rewrite or arrange a piece of music for an instrument or medium other than that originally intended

Examples

To transcribe music for piano
Many pianists enjoy performing Liszt’s works that transcribe famous orchestral pieces.
She transcribed the complex jazz solo for clarinet, preserving the spirit of the original.
The piece was originally written for violin, but the composer decided to transcribe it for piano.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrange - adapt a musical composition for performance by particular voices or instruments
