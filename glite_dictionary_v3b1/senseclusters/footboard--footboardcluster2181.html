footboard

noun
board at end of a bed
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊtˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈfʊtbɔːd / (BrE)
a vertical board or panel at the foot end of a bedstead

Examples

Bed footboard
Children sometimes sit on the footboard to tie their shoes in the morning.
He accidentally bumped his shin against the footboard while making the bed.
The antique bed had an ornate footboard made of carved oak.
