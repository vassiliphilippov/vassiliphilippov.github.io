eelblenny

noun
an eel-like subarctic coastal fish
Pronunciation: / ˈilˌblɛni / (AmE), / ˈiːlˌblɛni / (BrE)
A small, eel-like fish found in subarctic coastal waters, belonging to the family Pholidae.

Examples

Small eelblenny fish
Marine biologists observed several eelblennies hiding in underwater crevices.
The eelblenny burrows among rocks along the chilly northern coasts.
Unlike true eels, the eelblenny has a distinct dorsal fin and blunt head.
