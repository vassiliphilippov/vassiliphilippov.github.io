disassembler

noun
person who takes apart
Pronunciation: / dɪsəˈsæmbələr / (AmE), / dɪsəˈsæmbələ / (BrE)
a person who takes machines or devices apart, often to repair, inspect, or study them

Examples

A skilled disassembler
The disassembler carefully removed each screw to avoid damaging the device.
As a skilled disassembler, she could take apart any engine in under an hour.
The company hired a disassembler to inspect the faulty equipment for hidden defects.

Related Terms

  • antonym: assembler - a person who puts machines or devices together
  • synonym: mechanic - a person skilled in repairing and maintaining machines
