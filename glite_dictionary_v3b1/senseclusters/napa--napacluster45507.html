napa

noun
Chinese cabbage used in East Asian cooking
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑː.pə / (AmE), / ˈnɑː.pə / (BrE)
A type of Chinese cabbage with an elongated head and broad, pale green stalked leaves, commonly used as a vegetable in East Asian cooking.

Examples

Fresh napa cabbage
She picked up a fresh head of napa from the farmer's market for tonight's dumplings.
Stir-fried napa with garlic is a popular dish in many Chinese restaurants.
The kimchi was made with napa and a spicy red pepper paste.
