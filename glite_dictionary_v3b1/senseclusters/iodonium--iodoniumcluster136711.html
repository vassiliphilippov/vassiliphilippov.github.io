iodonium

noun
iodonium ion compound
Pronunciation: / aɪəˈdoʊniəm / (AmE), / aɪəˈdəʊniəm / (BrE)
a positively charged ion or compound containing iodine, used as a reagent in chemical reactions

Examples

iodonium ion compound

Many organic syntheses use iodonium salts as important intermediates.
