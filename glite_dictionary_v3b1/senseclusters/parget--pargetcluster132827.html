parget

noun
plaster for coating walls or lining chimneys
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːr.dʒɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɑː.dʒɪt / (BrE)
Plaster or a similar material used to coat the exterior of walls or line the inside of chimneys, especially in historical buildings.

Examples

Decorative parget on walls
Much of the medieval town's charm comes from the decorative parget seen on many buildings.
Restorers carefully reapplied parget to the old chimney to preserve its original look.
The historic cottage featured intricate parget designs on its exterior walls.
