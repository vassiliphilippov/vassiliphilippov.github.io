airtightness

noun
impermeability to air
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌtaɪtnəs / (AmE), / ˈeəˌtaɪtnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of not allowing air to pass in or out

Examples

Airtightness of windows
The airtightness of the windows helps reduce energy loss in the building.
Engineers tested the airtightness of the spacecraft before launch.
Good airtightness is essential for preserving the freshness of packaged food.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hermeticity - the state of being completely sealed against the entry or escape of air
  • antonym: porosity - the quality of allowing air or liquid to pass through
