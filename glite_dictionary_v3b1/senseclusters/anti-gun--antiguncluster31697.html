anti-gun

noun
gun control advocate
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈɡʌn / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈɡʌn / (BrE)
a person who is opposed to the ownership or use of guns, especially one who supports gun control laws

Examples

An anti-gun activist
The anti-gun spoke passionately at the rally for stricter firearm regulations.
Many anti-gun activists believe that reducing gun ownership will lower crime rates.
He was labeled an anti-gun after publishing his article on gun control.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gun control advocate - a person who supports laws to restrict gun ownership
  • antonym: pro-gun - a person who supports the right to own and use guns
