Ichthyosauridae

noun
family of extinct marine reptiles (Jurassic/Cretaceous)
Pronunciation: / ˌɪkθioʊˈsɔrɪˌdiː /; / ˌɪkθiˌoʊsɔːˈrɪdiː / (AmE), / ˌɪkθi.əˌsɔːˈrɪ.diː /; / ˌɪkθiˌəʊsɔːˈrɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of extinct marine reptiles from the Jurassic and Cretaceous periods, widely distributed and known for their fish-like bodies
