ruck

verb
to contest in rugby; to march with backpack
9.5
Pronunciation: / rʌk / (AmE), / rʌk / (BrE)
to engage in a physical contest for the ball in rugby or to march with a loaded backpack, especially as exercise

Examples

We will ruck ten miles with our backpacks as part of the training.
He enjoys rucking with friends on weekends to stay fit.
