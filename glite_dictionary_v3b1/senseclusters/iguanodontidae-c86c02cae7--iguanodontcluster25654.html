Iguanodontidae

noun
family of herbivorous dinosaurs (iguanodons)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˌɡwɑːnoʊˈdɒntɪdiː /; / ɪˌɡwɑːnəˈdɑːntɪdiː / (AmE), / ɪˌɡwɑːnəʊˈdɒntɪdiː /; / ɪˌɡwɑːnəˈdɒntɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of large herbivorous dinosaurs, including the iguanodons, known for their beaked jaws and thumb spikes, that lived during the Late Jurassic and Early Cretaceous periods
