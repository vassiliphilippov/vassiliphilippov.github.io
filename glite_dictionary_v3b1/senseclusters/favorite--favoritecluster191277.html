favorite

adjective
preferred over all others
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪvərɪt /; / ˈfeɪvərət / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvərɪt / (BrE)
liked or preferred over others; regarded with special favor

Examples

Favorite food

Of all her teachers, Mrs. Evans was the favorite because she was so kind.
Paris is a favorite destination for travelers from all over the world.
The new café is quickly becoming a favorite local hangout.
This song became a favorite among radio listeners last year.
Among all the desserts, chocolate cake is my favourite because my grandmother always makes it for me.
He was the manager's favourite player, often chosen for the most important matches.
She is clearly the favourite student in the class, always getting extra attention from the teacher.
