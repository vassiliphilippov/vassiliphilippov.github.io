deliberation

noun
AI/agent reasoning before action
Pronunciation: / dɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
(in computer science and artificial intelligence) the internal process by which an autonomous agent or system evaluates possible actions or plans and selects one to execute.

Examples

AI deliberation
The autonomous drone paused for deliberation before selecting the safest route.
During simulation, the agent's deliberation produced a cautious plan that avoided crowded areas.
Real-time deliberations in the robot were optimized to make faster safety decisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decision-making - the process of choosing between alternative actions or policies
  • synonym: planning - the process of formulating a sequence of actions to achieve a goal; often used in AI to produce plans
