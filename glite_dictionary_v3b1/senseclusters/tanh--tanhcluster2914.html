tanh

noun
hyperbolic tangent function
9.3
Pronunciation: / tæn / (AmE), / tæn / (BrE)
the hyperbolic tangent function, a mathematical function often used in mathematics and computer science

Examples

The tanh function
The tanh function is widely used as an activation function in deep learning models.
When plotting tanh, you can see it smoothly transitions between -1 and 1.
In calculus, the derivative of tanh is often used in solving differential equations.
