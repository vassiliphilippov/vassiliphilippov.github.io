Neverland

noun
state of fantasy
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛvərlænd / (AmE), / ˈnɛvəlænd / (BrE)
an imaginary place or state where someone escapes reality and avoids adult responsibilities, often associated with childish fantasy

Examples

Lost in Neverland dreams
After the breakup, he escaped to Neverland and refused to deal with his problems.
Some people live in Neverland rather than face the realities of adulthood.
She accused him of hiding in Neverland instead of taking responsibility for his actions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fantasy world - an imaginary or unreal place created by the mind
  • antonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist
