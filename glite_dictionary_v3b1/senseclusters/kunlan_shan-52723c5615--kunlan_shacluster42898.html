Kunlan Shan

proper noun
mountain range in western China
9.8
Pronunciation: / kunˈlɑn ʃɑn / (AmE), / kunˈlæn ʃæn / (BrE)
a mountain range in western China, extending eastward from the Indian border for about 1,000 miles

Examples

Kunlan Shan mountain range
Glaciers on the Kunlan Shan are important water sources for western China.
Many explorers have written about their journeys along the Kunlan Shan range.
The Kunlan Shan forms a major natural barrier between the northern Tibetan Plateau and the Tarim Basin.
