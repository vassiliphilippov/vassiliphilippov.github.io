langouste

noun
spiny lobster without claws, warm waters
Pronunciation: / lænˈɡuːst /; / lænˈguːst / (AmE), / lɑ̃ˈɡuːst /; / lɒŋˈɡuːst / (BrE)
a large edible spiny lobster found in warm seas, with a spiny shell and no large claws
