postmark

noun
official postal stamp showing date and place
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstˌmɑːk / (BrE)
an official mark stamped on a letter or package by a post office, showing the date and place of mailing

Examples

A postmark on a letter
Collectors often look for rare stamps and unique postmarks on old letters.
The postmark proved that the package was sent before the deadline.
The envelope still had the original postmark from Paris dated March 12.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cancellation - a mark that defaces a stamp to prevent reuse
