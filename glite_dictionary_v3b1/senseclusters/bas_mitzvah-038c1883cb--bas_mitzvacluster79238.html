bas mitzvah

noun
Jewish girl's 12th birthday ceremony
9.4
Pronunciation: / bɑs ˈmɪtsvə / (AmE), / bɑːs ˈmɪtsvə / (BrE)
(in Judaism) a ceremony marking the 12th birthday of a Jewish girl and recognizing her religious responsibilities as an adult.

Examples

A bas mitzvah ceremony
Her family was proud to celebrate her bas mitzvah at the synagogue.
Invitations were sent out for Emma’s bas mitzvah next month.
The rabbi spoke to the congregation during the bas mitzvah ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bar mitzvah - ceremony for a Jewish boy at age 13 assuming religious responsibilities
