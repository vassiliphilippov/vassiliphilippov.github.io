scrutinizer

noun
person who examines very carefully
Pronunciation: / ˈskruːt̬n̩ˌaɪzər /; / skruːˈtɪnəɪzər / (AmE), / ˈskruːtəˌnaɪzə /; / skruːˈtɪnʌɪzə / (BrE)
a person who examines or inspects something very carefully
