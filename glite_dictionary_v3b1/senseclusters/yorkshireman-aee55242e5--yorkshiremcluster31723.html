Yorkshireman

noun
man from Yorkshire
Pronunciation: / ˈjɔrkʃɪrmən / (AmE), / ˈjɔːkʃəmən / (BrE)
a man who comes from Yorkshire, a region in northern England

Examples

A Yorkshireman from England
As a proud Yorkshireman, he always supports his local cricket team.
The Yorkshireman shared stories about growing up in the countryside.
Every Yorkshireman I have met has a strong sense of regional identity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Yorkshire - a historic county in northern England
  • antonym: Yorkshirewoman - a woman who comes from Yorkshire
