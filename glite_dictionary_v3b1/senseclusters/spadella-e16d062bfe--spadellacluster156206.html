Spadella

noun
genus of marine worms
9.9
Pronunciation: / spəˈdɛl.ə / (AmE), / spəˈdɛl.ə / (BrE)
A genus of marine worms resembling sagittas but with a broader body and only one pair of lateral fins.

Examples

The genus Spadella
Marine biologists discovered a new species within the genus Spadella during the expedition.
The Spadella are distinguished from related genera by their broader bodies and single pair of lateral fins.
Unlike other chaetognaths, Spadella exhibits only one pair of lateral fins.
