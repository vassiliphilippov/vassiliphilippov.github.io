life cycle

noun
the stages of growth and reproduction in an organism
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪf ˌsaɪkəl /; / ˈlaɪf ˌsaɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˈlaɪf ˌsaɪkl /; / ˈlaɪf ˌsaɪ.kl̩ /; / ˈlaɪf ˌsaɪkəl / (BrE)
