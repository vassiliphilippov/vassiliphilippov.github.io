Lake Urmia

proper noun
saline lake in northwestern Iran
Pronunciation: / leɪk ɚˈmiːə / (AmE), / leɪk ɜːˈmiːə / (BrE)
a shallow, saline lake located in northwestern Iran, between the city of Tabriz and the eastern border of Turkey
