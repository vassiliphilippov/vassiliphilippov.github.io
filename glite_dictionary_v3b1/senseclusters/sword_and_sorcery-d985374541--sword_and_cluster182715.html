sword and sorcery

noun
fantasy subgenre with magic and heroics
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔrd ənd ˈsɔrsəri / (AmE), / ˈsɔːd ənd ˈsɔːsəri / (BrE)
a subgenre of fantasy fiction that typically features heroic characters, magical elements, and adventurous battles, often in a mythical or medieval setting

Examples

A sword and sorcery story
He built a collection of rare sword and sorcery novels from the 1960s and 1970s.
Many readers are drawn to the epic quests central to sword and sorcery tales.
The video game immerses players in a sword and sorcery adventure filled with wizards and monsters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: high fantasy - fantasy genre set in an alternate, epic world with grand themes
