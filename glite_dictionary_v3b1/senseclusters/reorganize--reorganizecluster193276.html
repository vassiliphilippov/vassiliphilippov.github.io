Synonyms

  • restructure
    4.4 4.92/m
    restructure means making large, formal changes to a system or company, while reorganize means arranging things in a new way to improve them.
  • rearrange
    2.1 2.45/m
    rearrange is moving or changing the order of items in a simple way, while reorganize often means planning a bigger change to improve structure.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: organise - to arrange systematically; to order
  • synonym: reorganize - to arrange or organize again (American spelling)
  • synonym: restructure - to change the structure of something, especially a company
