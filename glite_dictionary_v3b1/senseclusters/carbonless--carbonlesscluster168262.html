carbonless

adjective
without carbon emissions
4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːr.bən.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈkɑː.bən.ləs/ (BrE)
producing little or no carbon dioxide or other carbon-based greenhouse gases when used; causing no carbon emissions

Examples

Carbonless energy
The company invested in carbonless technologies to help fight climate change.
Switching to carbonless transportation options can significantly reduce your environmental impact.
Many governments are encouraging the use of carbonless energy sources like wind and solar power.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carbon-neutral - having a net zero carbon dioxide emissions
  • antonym: polluting - causing harmful substances to enter the environment
