keep options open

verb
remain undecided to allow flexibility
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: /kiːp ˈɑːp.ʃənz ˈoʊ.pən/ (AmE), /kiːp ˈɒp.ʃənz ˈəʊ.pən/ (BrE)
