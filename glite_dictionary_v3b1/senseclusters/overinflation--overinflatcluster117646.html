overinflation

noun
excessive increase
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪnˈfleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪnˈfleɪʃən / (BrE)
an excessive increase in importance, reputation, or expectations beyond what is justified or reasonable

Examples

Overinflation of reputation
The overinflation of his achievements led many to question his credibility.
Critics accused the media of overinflation of the company's success.
After the overinflation of expectations, the disappointing results surprised everyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exaggeration - the act of making something seem greater than it is
  • antonym: understatement - the act of making something seem less important than it is
