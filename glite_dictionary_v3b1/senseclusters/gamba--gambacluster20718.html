gamba

noun
bass viol instrument, like a cello
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæmbə / (AmE), / ˈɡæmbə / (BrE)
A viol that is the bass member of the viol family, having a range similar to the cello.

Examples

The gamba instrument
Many early music groups feature the gamba to recreate authentic Renaissance sounds.
She was fascinated by the rich, mellow tone of the gamba.
The musician played the gamba with exquisite precision in the Baroque ensemble.
