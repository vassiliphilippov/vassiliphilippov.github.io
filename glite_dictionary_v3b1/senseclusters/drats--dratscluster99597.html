drats

verb
to express mild annoyance
Pronunciation: / dræts / (AmE), / dræts / (BrE)
to express mild annoyance or frustration by saying 'drat'.

Examples

He drats at mistakes
Whenever his computer crashes, he drats and shakes his head in frustration.
She drats every time she forgets her keys at home.
The coach drats when the team misses an easy goal during practice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: complain - to express dissatisfaction or annoyance
  • antonym: praise - to express approval or admiration
