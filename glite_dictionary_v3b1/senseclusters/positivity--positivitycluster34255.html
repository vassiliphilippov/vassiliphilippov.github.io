positivity

noun
indisputable certainty; the state of being definite
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːzəˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˌpɒzɪˈtɪvɪti /; / ˌpɒzɪˈtɪvəti / (BrE)
the quality of being definite, certain, or confident in a statement or belief

Examples

Positivity in his answer
His statement carried a tone of positivity, making the conclusion indisputable.
The positivity of the evidence left no room for doubt.
The lawyer admired the positivity with which the facts were presented.
Her positivity helped the team remain hopeful during challenging times.
It can be difficult to maintain positivity in the face of repeated setbacks.
The manager's positivity was contagious, encouraging everyone to do their best.

  • antonym: doubt - lack of certainty or conviction
  • antonym: negativity - the quality or state of being negative or pessimistic
  • synonym: optimism - the tendency to expect the best or see the positive side
