bunt

noun
a wheat disease causing smelly smut spores
Pronunciation: / bʌnt / (AmE), / bʌnt / (BrE)
a disease of wheat or cereal crops, caused by a fungus, in which the grains are replaced by greasy, foul-smelling masses of spores
