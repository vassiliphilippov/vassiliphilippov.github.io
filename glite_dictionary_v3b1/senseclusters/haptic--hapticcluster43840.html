haptic

adjective
relating to the sense of touch
Pronunciation: / ˈhæptɪk / (AmE), / ˈhæptɪk / (BrE)
relating to the sense of touch, especially as distinct from sight or hearing.

Examples

Haptic feedback
Many smartphones use haptic feedback to simulate the feeling of pressing a button.
Researchers are developing haptic devices that allow users to 'feel' virtual objects.
The surgery simulator provided a realistic haptic experience for medical trainees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tactile - relating to or perceptible by the sense of touch
