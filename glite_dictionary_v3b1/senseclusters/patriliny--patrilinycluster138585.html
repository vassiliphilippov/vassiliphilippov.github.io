patriliny

noun
father-based descent
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpæ.trɪ.lɪ.niː/ /; / /ˈpæ.trə.lɪ.niː/ (AmE), /ˈpæ.trɪ.lɪ.niː/ /; / /ˈpæ.trə.lɪ.niː/ (BrE)
a system in which family descent and inheritance are traced through the father's or male line
