go down

verb
to decrease or become smaller
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ ˈdaʊn / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ˈdaʊn / (BrE)
to decrease in amount, size, value, or intensity; to become lower

Temperatures usually go down after sunset in this region.
