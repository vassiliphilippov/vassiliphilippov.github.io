fluidram

noun
British imperial unit of capacity (3.55 ml)
Pronunciation: / ˈfluːɪdˌræm /; / ˈfluːɪˌdræm / (AmE), / ˈfluːɪdˌræm /; / ˈfluːɪˌdræm / (BrE)
a small unit of liquid capacity, equal to about 3.55 milliliters, used in old British and medical measurements
