weed puller

noun
gardening tool for pulling weeds by the root
Pronunciation: / wid ˈpʊlɚ / (AmE), / wiːd ˈpʊlə / (BrE)
a hand-operated gardening tool with steel claws designed to grasp and remove weeds by the root

Examples

A weed puller tool
She bought a new weed puller to tackle the dandelions in her garden.
The weed puller is her favorite tool for keeping flowerbeds neat and tidy.
Using a weed puller makes it easier to remove stubborn weeds with their roots intact.
