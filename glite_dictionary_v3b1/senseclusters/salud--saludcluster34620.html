salud

noun
Spanish for health
Pronunciation: / sɑːˈluːð / (AmE), / sæˈluːd / (BrE)
a Spanish word meaning 'health', sometimes used in English to refer to well-being or as a toast wishing good health

Examples

Salud center
The clinic's name, Centro de salud, means 'health center' in Spanish.
At the party, everyone raised their glasses and shouted 'salud!' before drinking.
The public seminar taught basic hygiene and salud concepts to community members.

Related Terms

  • synonym: health - the state of being free from illness or injury
