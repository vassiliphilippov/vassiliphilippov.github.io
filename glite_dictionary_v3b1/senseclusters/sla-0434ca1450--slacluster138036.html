SLA

noun
3D printing process
Pronunciation: / ˌɛs ˌɛl ˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛs ˌɛl ˈeɪ / (BrE)
a 3D printing process called stereolithography that uses light to harden liquid resin layer by layer

Examples

SLA 3D printing
The engineer chose SLA to create highly detailed prototypes for the new product design.
Many jewelry designers use SLA printers to produce intricate wax models for casting.
Compared to FDM, SLA offers smoother surfaces and finer details in 3D printed objects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: FDM - a 3D printing process using melted filament to build objects layer by layer
  • synonym: stereolithography - a method of 3D printing using light to cure liquid resin
