communality

noun
shared community life
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːmjəˈnæləti / (AmE), / ˌkɒmjʊˈnælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of sharing things, interests, or values with others in a group or community

Examples

Strong communality in a group
The sense of communality in the neighborhood made everyone feel welcome and supported.
Shared gardens and group events helped foster communality among the residents.
The project succeeded because of the strong communality between team members.
A strong sense of communality helps unite the members of the group.
The festival fostered communality among people from different backgrounds.
Successful teams often rely on communality to achieve their goals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collectivism - the practice or principle of giving a group priority over each individual
  • synonym: community - a group of people with shared interests or values
  • antonym: individualism - the belief in the importance of individual rights and independence
  • synonym: solidarity - unity or agreement of feeling or action among individuals
