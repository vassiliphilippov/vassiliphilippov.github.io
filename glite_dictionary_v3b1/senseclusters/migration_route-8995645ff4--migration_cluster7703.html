migration route

noun
path regularly traveled by migrating birds or animals
2.4
Pronunciation: / maɪˈɡreɪʃən ruːt, maɪˈɡreɪʃən raʊt / (AmE), / maɪˈɡreɪʃ(ə)n ruːt, maɪˈɡreɪʃ(ə)n raʊt / (BrE)
the path or corridor regularly followed by migratory birds or animals during their seasonal movements

Examples

The migration route of birds
Changes in climate can disrupt the traditional migration route of many bird species.
Conservationists monitor the migration route of endangered cranes to protect key habitats along the way.
The Arctic tern follows a remarkable migration route from the Arctic to the Antarctic each year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flyway - a route regularly used by large numbers of migrating birds
