wuzzy

noun
timid person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌzi / (AmE), / ˈwʌzi / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is timid, easily frightened, or lacks courage

Examples

He is a wuzzy
He was called a wuzzy for not wanting to watch the scary movie with his friends.
Don't be such a wuzzy; it's just a harmless little spider.
Whenever there is a thunderstorm, my brother turns into a real wuzzy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brave - showing courage and not easily frightened
  • synonym: coward - a person who lacks courage
