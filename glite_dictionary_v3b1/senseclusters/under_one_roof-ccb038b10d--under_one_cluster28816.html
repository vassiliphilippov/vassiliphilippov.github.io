under one roof

adverb
in a single location or building
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɚ wʌn ɹuːf / (AmE), / ˌʌndə wʌn ruːf / (BrE)
In a single building or location, especially to denote that multiple people, businesses, or activities are brought together in one place.

Examples

All under one roof
Having the entire family under one roof made the holiday celebration even more special.
The company decided to consolidate its departments under one roof in a new office building.
The festival brought artists, musicians, and food vendors under one roof for the weekend.
