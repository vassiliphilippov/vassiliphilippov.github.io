monoenergetic

adjective
same energy particles
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːnoʊˌɛnərˈdʒɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəʊˌɛnəˈdʒɛtɪk / (BrE)
having or consisting of particles that all have the same energy

Examples

A monoenergetic beam
The researchers used a monoenergetic beam to ensure accurate measurement of the particles.
A monoenergetic source is important for certain types of physics experiments.
The detector was calibrated using monoenergetic gamma rays from a radioactive source.
