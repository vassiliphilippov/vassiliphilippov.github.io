cloying

adjective
excessively sweet or sentimental
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈklɔɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklɔɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
excessively sweet, rich, or sentimental to the point of discomfort or disgust

Examples

A cloying sweet taste
His cloying compliments made everyone uncomfortable.
The cloying smell of the artificial flowers gave me a headache.
The dessert was so cloying that I couldn't eat more than one bite.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: nauseating - causing a feeling of disgust or nausea
  • synonym: sickly - unpleasantly sweet or sentimental
  • degree lower: sweet - pleasantly or agreeably sweet to the taste or smell
