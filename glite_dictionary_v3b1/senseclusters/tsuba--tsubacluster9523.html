tsuba

noun
Japanese sword guard
Pronunciation: / tsuːˈbɑː / (AmE), / tsuːˈbɑː / (BrE)
a hand guard on a Japanese sword, often decorative as well as functional

Examples

A tsuba on sword
The samurai admired the detailed design of the tsuba on his new katana.
Collectors often seek antique swords with unique tsuba for their historical value.
The tsuba not only protected the hand but also showed the owner's status.

  • synonym: hilt - the handle of a sword or dagger
