day of reckoning

noun
time of judgment or consequences
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˌdeɪ əv ˈrɛkənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌdeɪ əv ˈrekənɪŋ / (BrE)
The time when one must face the consequences or judgement for past actions, often used in the context of a decisive or fateful event.

Examples

Courtroom day of reckoning
For years he escaped justice, but his day of reckoning finally came in court.
Many fear the day of reckoning when their past decisions finally catch up with them.
The company ignored the warnings, but a financial day of reckoning inevitably arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doomsday - a time or event of final judgment or destruction
  • synonym: judgment day - the time of God's final judgment of humankind
