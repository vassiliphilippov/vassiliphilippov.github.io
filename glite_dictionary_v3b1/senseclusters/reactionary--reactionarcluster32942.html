Examples

Reactionary views

Synonyms

  • reactionist
    9.9 0.00/m

  • antirevolutionary
    7.4 0.02/m
    antirevolutionary opposes revolutions and major political change, while reactionary opposes broader social or political reform and favors returning to old systems.
