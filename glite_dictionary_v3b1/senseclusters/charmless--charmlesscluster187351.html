charmless

adjective
without charm
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑːrmləs / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːmləs / (BrE)
lacking charm, appeal, or attractiveness

Examples

A charmless place
The charmless hotel room made her stay less enjoyable.
Despite its location, the restaurant was surprisingly charmless and dull.
He found the new office building rather charmless compared to the old one.
