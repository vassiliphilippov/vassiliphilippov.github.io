flowering fern

noun
Australasian fern with sporangia in clusters
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊ.ɚ.ɪŋ fɝːn /; / ˈflaʊərɪŋ fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ər.ɪŋ fɜːn /; / ˈflaʊərɪŋ fɜːn / (BrE)
a type of fern (especially of the genus Osmunda) with spore clusters that look like flowers

The flowering fern is often found in moist woodlands and along riverbanks.
