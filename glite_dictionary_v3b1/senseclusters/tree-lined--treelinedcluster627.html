tree-lined

adjective
with trees along sides
Pronunciation: / ˈtriːˌlaɪnd / (AmE), / ˈtriːˌlaɪnd / (BrE)
having rows of trees along both sides, especially of a road, path, or street

Examples

A tree-lined street

The university campus is famous for its beautiful tree-lined avenues.
In autumn, the tree-lined roads are covered with colorful fallen leaves.
