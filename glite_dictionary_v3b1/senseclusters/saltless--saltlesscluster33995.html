saltless

adjective
without salt
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔltləs / (AmE), / ˈsɔːltləs / (BrE)
not containing any salt or made without salt

Examples

Saltless soup
She prepared a saltless soup for her father, who was on a low-sodium diet.
The bakery offers saltless bread for customers with dietary restrictions.
After tasting the saltless meal, he realized he had forgotten to add any seasoning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: salty - containing or tasting of salt
  • synonym: unsalted - not having salt added
