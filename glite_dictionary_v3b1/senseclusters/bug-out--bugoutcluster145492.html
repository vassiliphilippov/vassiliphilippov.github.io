bug-out

noun
emergency evacuation
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌɡ aʊt / (AmE), / ˈbʌɡ aʊt / (BrE)
a rapid departure or evacuation, especially in response to an emergency or dangerous situation

Examples

A quick bug-out
When the alarm sounded, the team executed a swift bug-out to avoid the approaching storm.
They always keep a bug-out bag ready in case of sudden emergencies.
During the wildfire, the family made a quick bug-out to reach safety.

Related Terms

  • antonym: arrival - the act of coming to a place
  • synonym: evacuation - the act of leaving a place quickly for safety
  • synonym: retreat - the act of withdrawing from danger or difficulty
