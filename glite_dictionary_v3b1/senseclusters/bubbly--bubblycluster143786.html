bubbly

adjective
full of bubbles; carbonated or foaming
6.7 3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌb.li / (AmE), / ˈbʌb.li / (BrE)
containing or producing many bubbles, especially as a result of carbonation or fermentation

Examples

Bubbly water
He prefers his cider bubbly and cold, straight from the bottle.
The glass of bubbly water fizzed as she poured it over ice.
The surface of the homemade root beer was thick and bubbly after fermentation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flat - not fizzy or not containing bubbles, especially in drinks
  • synonym: frothy - full of or covered with a mass of small bubbles
