obsessive-compulsive

noun
person with obsessive-compulsive tendencies
Pronunciation: / əbˌsɛsɪv kəmˈpʌlsɪv / (AmE), / əbˌsɛsɪv kəmˈpʌlsɪv / (BrE)
a person who has obsessive thoughts and compulsive behavior, especially as a symptom of obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD)

Examples

An obsessive-compulsive cleaner
Her brother is an obsessive-compulsive who spends hours cleaning every surface in the house.
Some obsessive-compulsives find it difficult to leave home without completing their rituals.
The support group welcomes anyone who identifies as an obsessive-compulsive or has been diagnosed with OCD.

Related Terms

  • synonym: OCD - obsessive-compulsive disorder, a mental health condition
