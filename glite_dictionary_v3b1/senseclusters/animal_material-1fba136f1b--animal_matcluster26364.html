animal material

noun
material derived from animals
Pronunciation: / ˈænɪməl məˈtɪəriəl / (AmE), / ˈænɪməl məˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
any material that is derived from animals, such as fur, wool, leather, or proteins

Examples

Animal material like fur
Fur is a common animal material used in clothing.
Some researchers specialize in studying the properties of animal materials like silk and wool.
The laboratory tested the animal material to identify its origin.
