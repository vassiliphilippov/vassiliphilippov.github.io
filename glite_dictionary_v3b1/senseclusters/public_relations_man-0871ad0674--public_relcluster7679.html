public relations man

noun
manager of publicity for an organization
Pronunciation: / ˌpʌblɪk rɪˈleɪʃənz mæn / (AmE), / ˌpʌblɪk rɪˈleɪʃənz mæn / (BrE)
a man whose job is to manage and arrange publicity for a company, organization, or individual

Examples

Public relations man job
As a public relations man, he was responsible for shaping the company's public image.
Every politician relies on a skilled public relations man to handle media inquiries.
The public relations man issued a press release to address the recent controversy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: publicist - a person whose job is to generate and manage publicity for a company or individual
