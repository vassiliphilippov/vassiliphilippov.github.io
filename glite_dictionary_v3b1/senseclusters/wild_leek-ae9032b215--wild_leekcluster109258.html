wild leek

noun
North American wild onion plant
Pronunciation: / waɪld lik / (AmE), / waɪld liːk / (BrE)
A North American perennial plant (Allium tricoccum) with a slender bulb, broad leaves, and clusters of whitish flowers, often referred to as ramps.
