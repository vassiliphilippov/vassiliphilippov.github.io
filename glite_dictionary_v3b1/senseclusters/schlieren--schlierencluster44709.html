schlieren

noun
optical visualization method
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃlɪrən / (AmE), / ˈʃlɪərən / (BrE)
an optical technique used to visualize changes in fluid density or refractive index, often in scientific experiments

Examples

Schlieren imaging technique
The researchers used a schlieren setup to observe airflow patterns around the wing.
A schlieren image revealed the invisible heat waves rising from the hot plate.
Engineers rely on schlieren techniques to study shock waves in wind tunnels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interferometry - a technique using the interference of waves to make precise measurements
  • synonym: shadowgraph - an optical method for visualizing transparent media
