submittal

noun
formal document for approval
Pronunciation: / səbˈmɪtəl / (AmE), / səbˈmɪtəl / (BrE)
the act of formally presenting something (such as a document or item) for approval, review, or consideration, or the thing that is presented

Examples

A submittal document
The architect prepared a detailed submittal for the city council's review.
Each contractor must provide a submittal before starting any work on the project.
The committee rejected the initial submittal due to missing information.
The contractor's submittal of the building plans was reviewed by the city council.
Please ensure your submittal includes all the necessary documents before the deadline.
Late submittals may not be considered for the competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: presentation - the act of formally giving something to someone
  • synonym: proposal - a plan or suggestion put forward for consideration
  • synonym: submission - the act of presenting something for consideration or approval
