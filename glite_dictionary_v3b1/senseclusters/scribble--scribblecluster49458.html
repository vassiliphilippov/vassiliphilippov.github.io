scribble

noun
careless, aimless drawing
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈskrɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈskrɪbəl / (BrE)
a careless or aimlessly drawn mark or drawing, often done quickly and without purpose

Examples

A quick scribble
She absentmindedly filled the margin with a scribble of stars and circles.
The child's scribble covered the entire page in colorful loops.
What looked like a simple scribble was actually the start of her next masterpiece.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doodle - a rough, absent-minded drawing
