hydrofluosilicic acid

noun
a poisonous and corrosive acid used mainly in industry
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪdroʊˌfluːəˈsɪlɪsɪk ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪdrəʊˌfluːəˈsɪlɪsɪk ˈasɪd / (BrE)
an unstable, poisonous, and corrosive acid known chiefly through its salts; used mainly in water fluoridation and chemical manufacturing
