supranationalism

noun
authority above nations
9.6
Pronunciation: /suː.prə.ˈnæʃ.ə.nə.lɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /suː.prə.ˈnæʃ.ə.nə.lɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
the idea or practice of countries working together through organizations that have authority above individual national governments
