supranationalism

noun
authority above nations
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːprəˈnæʃənəlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌsuːprəˈnæʃənəlɪzəm / (BrE)
the idea or practice of countries working together through organizations that have authority above individual national governments

Examples

Supranationalism in organizations
The European Union is often cited as a leading example of supranationalism in practice.
Debates about supranationalism frequently arise when countries consider joining international organizations.
