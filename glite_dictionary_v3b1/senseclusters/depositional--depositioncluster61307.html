depositional

adjective
relating to deposition
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛpəˈzɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌdɛpəˈzɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to a deposition in law, especially an oral statement given under oath and recorded for use in court

Examples

Depositional testimony
The attorney requested all depositional records before preparing her case.
A depositional transcript can be crucial evidence in a trial.
He specialized in reviewing depositional documents for legal accuracy.
