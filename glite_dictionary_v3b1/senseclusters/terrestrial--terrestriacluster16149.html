terrestrial

noun
land-dwelling organism
4.0
Pronunciation: / təˈrɛstriəl / (AmE), / təˈrɛstriəl / (BrE)
an animal or plant that lives on land rather than in water or air

Examples

A terrestrial plant
Most mammals are terrestrials, living and feeding on land.
Desert terrestrials have adapted to survive with very little water.
Unlike fish, terrestrials breathe air and move on solid ground.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aerial - an animal or plant that lives in the air
  • antonym: aquatic - an animal or plant that lives in water
