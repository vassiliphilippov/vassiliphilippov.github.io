bottle

noun
container for feeding infants
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑː.təl/ (AmE), /ˈbɒ.təl/ (BrE)
A container with a flexible teat used to feed milk or formula to infants and very young children as a substitute for breastfeeding.

Examples

A baby bottle
Parents are advised to sterilize the bottle before each use.
The baby was happily drinking from her bottle in the living room.
When he was an infant, he refused to take the bottle at night.
