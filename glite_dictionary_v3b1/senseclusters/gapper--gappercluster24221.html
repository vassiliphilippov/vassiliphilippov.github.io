gapper

noun
person with tooth gap
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæpər / (AmE), / ˈɡæpə / (BrE)
a person, especially a child, who has a noticeable gap between their front teeth

Examples

A gapper smile
The little boy was proud to show off his gapper smile to his friends.
As a child, she was often called a gapper because of the space between her teeth.
The dentist explained that being a gapper is common and nothing to worry about.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gap-toothed - having a noticeable space between the front teeth
