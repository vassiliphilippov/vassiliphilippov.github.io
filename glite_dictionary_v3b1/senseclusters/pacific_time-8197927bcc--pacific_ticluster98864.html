Pacific Time

noun
U.S. west coast time zone
Pronunciation: / pəˈsɪfɪk taɪm / (AmE), / pəˈsɪfɪk tʌɪm / (BrE)
the standard time zone 8 hours behind Greenwich Mean Time, used in the western United States and parts of Canada

Examples

Pacific Time zone
During summer, Pacific Time shifts to daylight saving and is called PDT.
Flights often list both local and Pacific Time departures for clarity.
The meeting is scheduled for 10:00 a.m. Pacific Time, so check your local time zone.
