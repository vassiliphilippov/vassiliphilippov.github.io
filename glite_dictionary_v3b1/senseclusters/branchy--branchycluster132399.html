branchy

adjective
having many branches
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbræn.tʃi / (AmE), / ˈbrɑːn.tʃi / (BrE)
(of a tree, plant, or similar object) having many branches.

Examples

A branchy tree

The artist sketched a branchy silhouette against the winter sky.
We followed a narrow path through the branchy undergrowth.
