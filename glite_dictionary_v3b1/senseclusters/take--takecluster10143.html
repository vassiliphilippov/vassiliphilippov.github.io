take

verb
to use a mode of transportation or route
Pronunciation: / teɪk / (AmE), / teɪk / (BrE)

Examples

To take the train
Do you take the train to the city every morning?
She prefers to take a taxi when it rains.
We decided to take Route 66 on our road trip across the country.
