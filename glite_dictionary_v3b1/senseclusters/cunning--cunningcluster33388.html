cunning

adjective
charming or appealing in a cute way (archaic)
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʌnɪŋ / (BrE)
(archaic) Attractive, especially because of small size, prettiness, or quaintness; charmingly cute.

Examples

Cunning little dress
She wore a cunning little bonnet that suited her perfectly.
The kitten's cunning expression melted everyone's heart.
They lived in a cunning cottage at the edge of the village.
