Rudbeckia

noun
a genus of North American flowering plants
Pronunciation: / rʌdˈbɛkiə / (AmE), / rʊdˈbɛkɪə / (BrE)
a genus of North American perennial herbs with showy, cone-shaped yellow, orange, or brown flower heads, commonly known as coneflowers or black-eyed Susans
