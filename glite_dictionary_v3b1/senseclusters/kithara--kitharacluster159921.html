kithara

noun
ancient Greek instrument
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɪˈθɑːrə / (AmE), / kɪˈθɑːrə / (BrE)
an ancient Greek stringed musical instrument, similar to a lyre, used especially by professional musicians

Examples

Ancient kithara instrument
The ancient musician skillfully played the kithara at the festival.
Historians believe the kithara was used in important ceremonies and performances.
A replica kithara is displayed in the museum's collection of Greek artifacts.
