zookeeper

noun
person managing chaos
Pronunciation: / ˈzuːˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈzuːˌkiːpə / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who manages or controls a large, noisy, or disorderly group of people, especially children.

Examples

Classroom zookeeper
During recess she was the zookeeper trying to keep thirty noisy children in line.
At the family reunion, he felt like a zookeeper managing all the excited kids.
Whenever the classroom gets loud, the teacher jokes that she is a zookeeper.

Related Terms

  • synonym: herder - someone who keeps a group of people or animals together
  • synonym: supervisor - a person who oversees or manages others
