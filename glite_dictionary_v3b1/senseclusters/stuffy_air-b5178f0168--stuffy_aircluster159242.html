stuffy air

noun
unpleasant, warm air lacking freshness
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌfi ɛr / (AmE), / ˈstʌfi eə / (BrE)
air that is unpleasant because it is warm and does not have enough fresh air circulating

Examples

The stuffy air indoors
After an hour in the crowded train, the stuffy air made her feel dizzy.
Opening the windows quickly improved the stuffy air in the room.
The hotel gym was empty except for the stuffy air lingering from earlier workouts.
