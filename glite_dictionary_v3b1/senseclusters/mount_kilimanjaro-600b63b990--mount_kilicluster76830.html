Mount Kilimanjaro

proper noun
highest mountain in Africa, Tanzania
3.0
Pronunciation: / maʊnt ˌkɪlɪmənˈdʒɑːroʊ / (AmE), / maʊnt ˌkɪlɪmənˈdʒɑːrəʊ / (BrE)
