perpetrator

noun
person who commits a crime or wrongdoing
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːpəˌtreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpɪtreɪtə / (BrE)
a person who commits a crime or does something wrong

Examples

The perpetrator of crime
It took detectives weeks to identify the perpetrator responsible for the theft.
The perpetrator was caught on security camera near the scene of the incident.
The police are searching for the perpetrator of the violent crime.

Related Terms

  • synonym: offender - a person who commits an illegal act
  • antonym: victim - a person harmed as a result of a crime or wrongdoing
