Echeneididae

noun
family of fishes with a sucking disk (remoras)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkəˈnaɪɪˌdaɪ /; / ɛkəˌniːəˈdaɪdiː / (AmE), / ˌɛkɪˈniːɪˌdiː /; / ɛkɪˌniːɪˈdɪːdiː / (BrE)
a family of fishes, called remoras, with a sucking disk on their head used to attach to other fishes or ships
