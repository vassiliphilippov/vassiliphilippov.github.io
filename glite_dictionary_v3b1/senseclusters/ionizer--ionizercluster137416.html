ionizer

noun
device for charging particles
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈaɪ.ə.naɪ.zər/ (AmE), /ˈaɪ.ə.naɪ.zə/ (BrE)
a device that produces ions by adding electrical charge to particles, often used in industrial or scientific equipment

Examples

Industrial ionizer
The factory installed a new ionizer to reduce dust in the air during production.
An ionizer can help improve air quality by charging and removing particles.
Before painting the car, they used an ionizer to ensure the surface was properly prepared.
The technician installed a new ionizer in the laboratory equipment to improve performance.
A faulty ionizer can affect the accuracy of scientific measurements in mass spectrometry.
Researchers rely on the ionizer to prepare samples for detailed chemical analysis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electrostatic precipitator - a device that removes particles from air using electrical charges
  • synonym: ion source - a component that produces ions for scientific analysis
  • antonym: neutralizer - a device that removes or counteracts electrical charge
