royal flush

noun
highest poker hand (A, K, Q, J, 10 same suit)
7.1 4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈrɔɪ.əl flʌʃ/ /; / /ˈrɔɪ.əl flʊʃ/ (AmE), /ˈrɔɪ.əl flʌʃ/ /; / /ˈrɔɪ.əl flʊʃ/ (BrE)
