stadium

noun
large sports or entertainment arena
1.0 1.3
Pronunciation: /ˈsteɪ.dɪ.əm/ (AmE), /ˈsteɪ.dɪ.əm/ (BrE)
A large, usually open-air structure with tiered seating for sporting events, concerts, or other public entertainment.

Examples

A large stadium
From our seats at the top of the stadium, we could see the entire field.
Tens of thousands of fans cheered in the stadium during the concert.
The city built a new stadium to host the international football championship.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arena - a level area surrounded by seats for sports or entertainment
