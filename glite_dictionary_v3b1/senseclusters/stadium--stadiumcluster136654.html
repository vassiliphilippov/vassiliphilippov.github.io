Examples

A large stadium

Tens of thousands of fans cheered in the stadium during the concert.

Synonyms

    4.9 1.39/m
    an amphitheater has rising rows around a central open area for performances, while a stadium is larger for sports and concerts.
  • arena
    2.9 7.60/m
    an arena is often an enclosed building used for sports or concerts, while a stadium is usually large and open-air with tiered seating.
  • coliseum
    4.7 2.34/m
