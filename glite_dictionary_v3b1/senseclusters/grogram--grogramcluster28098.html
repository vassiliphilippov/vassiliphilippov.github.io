grogram

noun
a stiff, coarse fabric of silk and wool or mohair
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɑːɡrəm / (AmE), / ˈɡrɒɡrəm / (BrE)
a coarse fabric made of silk mixed with wool or mohair, often stiffened with gum for added rigidity
