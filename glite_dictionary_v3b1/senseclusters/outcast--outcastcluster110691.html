outcast

verb
to exclude from group
5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈaʊt.kæst/ (AmE), /ˈaʊt.kɑːst/ /; / /ˈaʊt.kæst/ (BrE)

Examples

The group was criticized for outcasting anyone who disagreed with their views.
