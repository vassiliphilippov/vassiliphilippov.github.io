mishandle

verb
manage badly; handle incorrectly
1.5 4.0
Pronunciation: /mɪ.ˈʃæn.dəl/ (AmE), /mɪ.ˈʃæn.dəl/ (BrE)
to deal with or manage something badly or incompetently, causing problems or damage

Examples

To mishandle a problem
He completely mishandled the negotiations, leading to a breakdown in talks.
If you mishandle these chemicals, you could cause a dangerous reaction.
The fragile package was mishandled and arrived broken.
He admitted that he had mishandled the situation and apologized to everyone involved.
If you mishandle these chemicals, they could cause serious harm.
The company was accused of having mishandled customer data, resulting in a major security breach.

Related Terms

  • antonym: handle - to deal with or manage a situation, person, or thing
  • synonym: mismanage - to manage something badly or incompetently
