aspic

noun
savory jelly for encasing food
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈæspɪk / (AmE), / ˈæspɪk / (BrE)
A savory jelly made from meat or fish stock, used as a mold to encase meats, seafood, eggs, or vegetables.

Examples

A aspic mold
She learned how to make aspic during her culinary course in France.
Slices of cold chicken were served in a savory aspic at the banquet.
The chef prepared a colorful vegetable aspic for the buffet table.
