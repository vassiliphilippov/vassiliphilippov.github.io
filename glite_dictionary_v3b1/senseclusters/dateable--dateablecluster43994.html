dateable

adjective
suitable for dating (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ˈdeɪtəbəl / (BrE)
informal: suitable or attractive for going on a romantic date; likely to be asked out or seen as a potential romantic partner.

Examples

Dateable person
After the breakup, many friends asked if she felt dateable again.
He joined the app hoping his new photos would make him more dateable.
Being honest about intentions can make someone appear less dateable to casual daters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: available - open or free to begin a romantic relationship; similar in dating contexts
  • antonym: unavailable - not open to dating or a relationship; opposite in dating contexts
