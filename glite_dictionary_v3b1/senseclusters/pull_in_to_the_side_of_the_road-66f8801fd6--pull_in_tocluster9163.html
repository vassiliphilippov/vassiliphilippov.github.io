pull in to the side of the road

verb
stop a vehicle at the roadside
Pronunciation: / pʊl ɪn tu ðə saɪd əv ðə roʊd / (AmE), / pʊl ɪn tu ðə saɪd əv ðə rəʊd / (BrE)
to steer a vehicle towards and stop near the edge of the road
