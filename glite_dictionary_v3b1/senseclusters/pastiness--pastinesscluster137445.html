pastiness

noun
thick, sticky texture
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪstinəs / (AmE), / ˈpeɪstinəs / (BrE)
the quality of having a thick, soft, and slightly sticky or doughy texture, often like paste

Examples

Pastiness of dough
The pastiness of the dough made it difficult to roll out for the pie crust.
He disliked the pastiness of the mashed potatoes and preferred them to be light and fluffy.
Too much flour can cause pastiness in cake batter, resulting in a dense texture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: smoothness - the quality of being even and free from lumps or roughness
  • synonym: stickiness - the quality of being sticky or adhesive
