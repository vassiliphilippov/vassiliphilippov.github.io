antidote

noun
remedy for poison
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.t̬ɪˌdoʊt / (AmE), / ˈæn.tɪ.dəʊt / (BrE)
a substance that counteracts or neutralizes the effects of a poison

Examples

An antidote for poison
Activated charcoal is sometimes used as an antidote for certain types of poisoning.
Doctors administered an antidote to counteract the snake venom.
Scientists are searching for an antidote to the new toxin.

Related Terms

  • antonym: poison - a substance that causes harm or death when introduced into a body
  • synonym: remedy - a medicine or treatment for a disease or injury
