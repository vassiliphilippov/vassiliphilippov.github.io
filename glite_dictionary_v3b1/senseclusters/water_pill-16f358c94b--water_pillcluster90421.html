water pill

noun
a medicine that helps the body lose excess water
9.0 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈwɔː.tər pɪl/ /; / /ˈwɑː.tər pɪl/ (AmE), /ˈwɔː.tə pɪl/ /; / /ˈwɒ.tə pɪl/ (BrE)
