subtitling

noun
adding captions to video
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌtaɪtlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌtaɪtlɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or profession of creating and adding written translations or captions (subtitles) to films, videos, or other media

Related Terms

  • synonym: captioning - the process of displaying text on a screen to provide additional information
  • antonym: dubbing - replacing the original spoken dialogue in a film with translated speech
  • synonym: translation - the process of converting text or speech from one language to another
