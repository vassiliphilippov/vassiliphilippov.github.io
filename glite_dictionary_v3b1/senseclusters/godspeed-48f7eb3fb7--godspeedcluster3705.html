Godspeed

noun
a wish for success or safety on a journey
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑdˌspiːd / (AmE), / ˈɡɒdˌspiːd / (BrE)
an expression of good wishes for a safe and successful journey or undertaking

Examples

Godspeed on journey
As the ship set sail, the captain wished his crew Godspeed on their voyage.
Friends gathered at the station to bid her Godspeed as she left for college.
We offer you Godspeed on your new adventure abroad.
