pedogenesis

noun
larval reproduction
9.9
Pronunciation: /piː.də.ˈdʒen.ɪs/ /; / /pe.də.ˈdʒen.ɪs/ (AmE), /piː.də.ˈdʒen.ɪs/ /; / /pe.də.ˈdʒen.ɪs/ (BrE)
a form of reproduction in which larvae or immature individuals produce offspring without reaching adult maturity

Examples

Larval pedogenesis
Certain species of midges exhibit pedogenesis, allowing their larvae to reproduce before becoming adults.
Scientists observed pedogenesis in the laboratory, where immature insects produced offspring directly.
The discovery of pedogenesis challenged traditional ideas about insect development and reproduction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parthenogenesis - reproduction without fertilization by a male
