cafeteria

noun
self-service restaurant or dining area
1.7 1.0
Pronunciation: /kæf.ə.ˈtɪr.ɪə/ /; / /kæf.ə.ˈtɪr.iː.ə/ (AmE), /kæf.ə.ˈtɪə.riː.ə/ /; / /kæf.ə.ˈtɪə.rɪə/ (BrE)
