cafeteria

noun
self-service restaurant or dining area
Pronunciation: / ˌkæfəˈtɪriə / (AmE), / ˌkæfəˈtɪəriə / (BrE)
a restaurant or dining area where customers serve themselves from a counter and pay for their meals before eating

Examples

Hospital cafeteria
She met her friends at the hospital cafeteria during her lunch break.
The school's cafeteria serves healthy meals for students every day.
We ate lunch in the cafeteria because it was quick and convenient.

Related Terms

  • synonym: canteen - a cafeteria or snack bar, especially in a workplace or school
