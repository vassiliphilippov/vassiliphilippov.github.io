biosolid

noun
fertilizer from sewage
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪoʊˌsɑːlɪd / (AmE), / ˈbaɪəʊˌsɒlɪd / (BrE)
treated sewage sludge that is used as fertilizer to improve soil quality

Examples

Treated biosolids fertilizer
The city processes wastewater to create biosolids that can be safely used on farms.
Applying biosolids to the fields improved the soil's nutrient content.
Some gardeners prefer to use biosolids as an eco-friendly alternative to chemical fertilizers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compost - decayed organic material used as fertilizer
  • synonym: fertilizer - a substance added to soil to help plants grow
