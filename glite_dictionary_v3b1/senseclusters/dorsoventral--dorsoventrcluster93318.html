dorsoventral

adjective
from back to belly
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌdɔrsoʊˈvɛntrəl / (AmE), / ˌdɔːsəʊˈvɛntrəl / (BrE)
Extending from the dorsal (back) to the ventral (belly or front) surface of an organism.

Examples

A dorsoventral axis
Flattened bodies often have a pronounced dorsoventral width, as seen in many fish species.
The dorsoventral axis is crucial in studying animal embryonic development.
The injury extended along the dorsoventral plane, affecting both the back and the underside.
