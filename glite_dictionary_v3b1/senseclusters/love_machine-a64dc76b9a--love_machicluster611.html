love machine

noun
person skilled in attracting romance
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌv məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈlʌv məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
a person, usually a man, who is very good at attracting romantic interest or is exceptionally skilled or energetic in romance or affection, often used humorously or affectionately.

Examples

Party love machine
Ever since his makeover, Tom has become a love machine at every party he attends.
She jokingly referred to her boyfriend as her love machine in front of their friends.
They called him a love machine because of his smooth dance moves and charm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ladies' man - a man who attracts or seeks the company of women; womanizer
  • synonym: womanizer - a man who often has romantic relationships with many women
