col

noun
mountain pass between two peaks
9.6 0.6/m
Pronunciation: / kÉ‘l / (AmE), / kÉ’l / (BrE)
A pass or depression between two mountain peaks, especially the lowest point on a mountain ridge between two summits.

The map marks a prominent col north of the main summit, used as a popular crossing point.
