denarcotize

verb
to remove narcotics
9.2
Pronunciation: / diːˈnɑːrkəˌtaɪz / (AmE), / diːˈnɑːkətaɪz / (BrE)
to eliminate or reduce the presence or influence of narcotics in a place, market, or system, often through policy or law enforcement

Examples

To denarcotize a city
The government launched a campaign to denarcotize the city and restore public safety.
Efforts to denarcotize schools include stricter monitoring and educational programs.
International cooperation is needed to denarcotize regions affected by drug trafficking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eradicate - to completely remove or destroy something
  • antonym: legalize - to make something legal, especially drugs
