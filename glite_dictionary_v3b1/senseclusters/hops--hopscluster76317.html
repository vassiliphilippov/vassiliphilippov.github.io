hops

noun
plant whose flowers flavor beer
6.0 4.5
Pronunciation: /hɑːps/ (AmE), /hɒps/ (BrE)
The dried flowers of a climbing plant (Humulus lupulus) used to add a bitter flavor and aroma to beer; also, the plant itself.

Examples

Dried hops flowers
Farmers in the region have been growing hops for generations to supply local breweries.
The unique flavor in this craft beer comes from the use of aromatic hops.
Without hops, beer would lack its distinctive bitterness and aroma.
