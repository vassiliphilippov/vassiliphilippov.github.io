favorability

noun
degree of approval
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌfeɪvərəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌfeɪvərəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the degree to which something or someone is liked, approved of, or viewed positively

Examples

Favorability rating
The candidate's favorability increased after the televised debate.
Surveys show high favorability for the new environmental regulations.
The product's favorability among young consumers surprised the marketing team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: approval - the belief that something or someone is good or acceptable
  • synonym: popularity - the state of being liked or supported by many people
  • antonym: unpopularity - the state of being disliked or not approved of
