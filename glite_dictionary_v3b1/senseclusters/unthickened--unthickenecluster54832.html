unthickened

adjective
not made thicker
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈθɪkənd / (AmE), / ʌnˈθɪkənd / (BrE)
not made thicker or more dense; remaining thin or fluid in consistency

Examples

An unthickened soup
The soup was still unthickened even after adding flour.
She preferred her yogurt unthickened for a lighter texture.
The paint remained unthickened and easy to spread on the canvas.

Related Terms

  • antonym: thickened - made thicker or more dense
  • synonym: thin - having little thickness or density
