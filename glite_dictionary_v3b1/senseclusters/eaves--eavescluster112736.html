eaves

noun
overhanging lower edge of a roof
7.1 4.3
Pronunciation: / ivz / (AmE), / iːvz / (BrE)
the part of a roof that projects beyond the side of a building, forming an overhanging edge at the lower boundary

Examples

The eaves of a roof
