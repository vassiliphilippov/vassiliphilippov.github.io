unsystematically

adverb
in a disorganized or unmethodical way
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnsɪstəˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌʌnsɪstəˈmætɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that lacks order, method, or system.

Examples

Arranged unsystematically
Books and papers were scattered unsystematically across his desk.
She arranged her notes unsystematically, making it difficult to find information quickly.
The research data were collected unsystematically, leading to unreliable results.

Related Terms

  • antonym: systematically - in an ordered, methodical manner
