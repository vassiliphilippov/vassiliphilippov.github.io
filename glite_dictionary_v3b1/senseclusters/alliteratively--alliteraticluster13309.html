alliteratively

adverb
in an alliterative manner
Pronunciation: / əˈlɪt̬ərətɪvli / (AmE), / əˈlɪtərətɪvli / (BrE)
In a manner that uses or resembles alliteration.

Examples

Written alliteratively
Children enjoy stories that are written alliteratively, as the repeated sounds make them fun to read aloud.
Her name was crafted alliteratively to be memorable and catchy.
The poet composed the verse alliteratively, using the same initial sounds throughout.
