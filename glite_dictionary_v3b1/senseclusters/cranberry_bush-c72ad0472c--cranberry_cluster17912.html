cranberry bush

noun
a North American shrub with red berries
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkrænˌbɛri bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈkranˌbɛri bʊʃ / (BrE)
A deciduous North American shrub or small tree with three-lobed leaves and red berries, often grown for its ornamental value.

Examples

The cranberry bush berries
Many birds are attracted to the fruit of the cranberry bush during winter.
She planted a cranberry bush near the fence for its vibrant foliage and berries.
The cranberry bush in our garden produced bright red berries in the fall.
