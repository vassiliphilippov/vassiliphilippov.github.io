snowbell

noun
small Japanese tree with white bell-shaped flowers
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊˌbɛl / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌbɛl / (BrE)
A small tree native to Japan, with bell-shaped white flowers, belonging to the genus Styrax.

Examples

The snowbell tree
Gardeners often choose the snowbell for its attractive appearance and manageable size.
The snowbell blooms beautifully in late spring, covering its branches with delicate white flowers.
The leaves of the snowbell turn a lovely yellow in the autumn months.
