dishcloth gourd

noun
plant with edible fruits and fibrous sponge interior
9.0 7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈdɪʃ.klɔːθ gɔːrd/ /; / /ˈdɪʃ.klɑːθ gʊərd/ (AmE), /ˈdɪʃ.klɒθ gɔːd/ /; / /ˈdɪʃ.klɒθ gʊəd/ (BrE)
a tropical climbing plant with large yellow flowers; its young fruits are edible and its mature fruit is dried to make sponges

Examples

The dishcloth gourd plant
Farmers in tropical regions often grow dishcloth gourd for both food and cleaning products.
She used the fibrous interior of the dishcloth gourd to scrub her pots and pans.
The mature fruit of the dishcloth gourd can be dried and used as a natural sponge.
