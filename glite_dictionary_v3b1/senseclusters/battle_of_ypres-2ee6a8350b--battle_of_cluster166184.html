Battle of Ypres

noun
major World War I battles at Ypres, Belgium
7.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbæt.əl əv ˈiː.prə/ /; / /ˈbæt.əl ə ˈiː.prə/ (AmE), /ˈbæt.əl əv ˈiː.prə/ /; / /ˈbæt.əl ə ˈiː.prə/ (BrE)
any of several major battles during World War I fought near the town of Ypres in Belgium, notable for the first large-scale use of poison gas by the Germans.
