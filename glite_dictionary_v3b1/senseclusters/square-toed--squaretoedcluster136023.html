square-toed

adjective
having a square-shaped toe
Pronunciation: / skwɛr toʊd / (AmE), / skweə təʊd /; / skwɛə təʊd / (BrE)
having a toe or front part that is square-shaped rather than rounded or pointed (especially of shoes or toes)

Examples

Square-toed shoes
He bought a pair of square-toed boots for the new job.
Her square-toed loafers stood out among the more classic styles.
The designer's latest collection features square-toed shoes in a variety of colors.
He bought a pair of square-toed leather boots for the wedding.
Her square-toed sandals gave her a unique and stylish look.
The square-toed style became popular again in the early 2000s.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pointed-toe - having shoes or boots with a toe that comes to a point
  • antonym: round-toed - having a shoe or toe shape with a rounded front
