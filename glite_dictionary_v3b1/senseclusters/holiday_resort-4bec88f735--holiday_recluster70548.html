holiday resort

noun
hotel or complex for vacations
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːlɪˌdeɪ rɪˈzɔrt / (AmE), / ˈhɒləˌdeɪ rɪˈzɔːt / (BrE)
A hotel or complex in a location designed for people to spend vacations or holidays, offering various recreational facilities and entertainment.

Examples

A holiday resort hotel
Many families choose a holiday resort that has activities for both adults and children.
The holiday resort offers all-inclusive packages, including meals and entertainment.
We stayed at a beautiful holiday resort by the beach for two weeks.
