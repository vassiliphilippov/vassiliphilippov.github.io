John Broadus Watson

proper noun
founder of behavioristic psychology

Pronunciation: /dʒɑːn ˈbroʊ.dəs ˈwɑːt.sən/ (AmE), /dʒɒn ˈbrəʊ.dəs ˈwɒt.sən/ (BrE)
(1878–1958) United States psychologist regarded as the founder of behaviorism, emphasizing the study of observable behavior.
