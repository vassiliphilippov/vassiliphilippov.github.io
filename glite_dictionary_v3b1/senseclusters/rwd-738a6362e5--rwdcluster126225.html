Pronunciation: / ɑr ˌdʌbəlˈjuː ˈdiː / (AmE), / ɑː ˌdʌbəlˈjuː ˈdiː / (BrE)
abbreviation for 'rear-wheel drive', a vehicle drivetrain where power is delivered to the rear wheels

Examples

RWD sports car
The new model is available with either RWD or all-wheel drive, depending on your preference.
Many classic muscle cars are known for their RWD layout, which provides strong acceleration.
Some drivers prefer RWD vehicles for their balanced handling and sporty feel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: AWD - abbreviation for 'all-wheel drive', where power is delivered to all wheels
  • antonym: FWD - abbreviation for 'front-wheel drive', where power is delivered to the front wheels
