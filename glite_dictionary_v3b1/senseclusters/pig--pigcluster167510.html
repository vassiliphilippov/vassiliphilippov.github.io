pig

verb
to clean pipelines
Pronunciation: / pɪɡ / (AmE), / pɪɡ / (BrE)
to clean, inspect, or maintain a pipeline by sending a device called a pig through it

Examples

To pig a pipeline
The engineers are pigging the pipeline to remove built-up debris and ensure smooth operation.
Regular pigging helps prevent blockages and extends the life of oil and gas pipelines.
After pigging the system, the maintenance team reported a significant improvement in flow efficiency.

Related Terms

  • antonym: block - to stop something from passing through a pipe or channel
  • synonym: flush - to clean something by forcing liquid through it
