windburned

adjective
having skin irritated by wind
8.8 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪnd.bɜːrnd/ (AmE), /ˈwɪnd.bɜːnd/ (BrE)
having redness or irritation of the skin caused by prolonged exposure to the wind.

Examples

Windburned skin
After hiking all day on the mountain, her face was windburned and sore.
Cover up or you'll get windburned out there in the cold wind.
He came back from sailing with windburned cheeks and chapped lips.
After a day sailing on the lake, his cheeks were windburnt and sore.
