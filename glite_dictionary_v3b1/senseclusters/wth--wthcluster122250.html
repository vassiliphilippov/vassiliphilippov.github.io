wth

x
informal exclamation
Pronunciation: / ˌdʌbəl.juː tiː eɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˌdʌbəl.juː tiː eɪtʃ / (BrE)
an informal abbreviation for 'what the heck' or 'what the hell', used to express surprise, confusion, or disbelief

Examples

Say wth in surprise
I opened my inbox and thought, wth, why are there so many emails?
He just quit his job without notice—wth is going on?
When the lights suddenly went out, everyone shouted, wth!
