irreprehensible

adjective
faultless; blameless
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrɪprɪˈhɛnsəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪrɪprɪˈhɛnsɪbəl / (BrE)
not deserving blame or criticism; completely faultless or blameless

Examples

An irreprehensible act

The judge praised his irreprehensible honesty during the trial.
Their irreprehensible behavior set a positive example for everyone in the group.
