Petaurista

noun
genus of large Asiatic flying squirrels
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpiːtəˈrɪstə / (AmE), / ˌpiːtəˈrɪstə / (BrE)
A genus of very large Asiatic flying squirrels known for their gliding ability.

Examples

A Petaurista squirrel
Researchers observed a Petaurista in the forests of Southeast Asia.
