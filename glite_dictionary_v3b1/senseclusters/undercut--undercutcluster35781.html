undercut

noun
hairstyle with short sides and longer top
5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈʌn.dər.kʌt/ (AmE), /ˈʌn.də.kʌt/ (BrE)
A hairstyle in which the hair on the sides and/or back is cut very short or shaved while the hair on top is left noticeably longer.

Examples

Trendy undercut hairstyle
Her trendy undercut made a bold statement at the concert.
He kept the top long while the barber shaved the sides into an undercut.
Many actors and musicians have popularized the modern undercut in recent years.
