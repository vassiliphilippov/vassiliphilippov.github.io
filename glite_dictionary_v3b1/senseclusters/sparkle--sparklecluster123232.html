sparkle

noun
brightness or liveliness in the eyes or face
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑrkəl / (AmE), / ˈspɑːkəl / (BrE)
a lively brightness or animation in the expression of the eyes or face, showing merriment or excitement

Examples

A sparkle in her eyes
Even at eighty, there was a mischievous sparkle in his gaze.
He greeted everyone with a sparkle that made him instantly likable.
The sparkle in her eyes revealed her excitement about the surprise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: twinkle - a gleam or glitter, particularly in the eyes, showing emotion or amusement
