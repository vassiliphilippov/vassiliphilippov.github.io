Paralipomenon

noun
archaic name for Books of Chronicles
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˌlaɪˈpɑːmɪˌnɑn / (AmE), / ˌparəˌlɪˈpɒmɪnɒn / (BrE)
(archaic) An obsolete name for the Old Testament books of I Chronicles and II Chronicles, considered supplementary to the Books of Kings.

Examples

The book Paralipomenon
In historical commentaries, you might encounter the word Paralipomenon instead of Chronicles.
Many early English Bibles refer to the Books of Chronicles as Paralipomenon.
The term Paralipomenon was once commonly used in biblical scholarship.
