synod

noun
church council or assembly
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪn.əd / (AmE), / ˈsɪn.əd / (BrE)
An assembly or council of church officials and theologians, usually convened to discuss and decide on ecclesiastical matters.

Examples

Church synod meeting
A new policy on clergy conduct was issued after the annual synod.
The synod will address pressing issues within the diocese this month.
The bishops gathered at the synod to debate church doctrine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: congress - a formal meeting or assembly, especially of delegates for discussion or action
  • synonym: council - assembly convened for discussion or decision-making
