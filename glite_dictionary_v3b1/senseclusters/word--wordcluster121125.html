word

noun
a promise or assurance
Pronunciation: / wɝːd /; / wɝd / (AmE), / wɜːd / (BrE)
a promise or assurance that something will be done or is true

Examples

Give your word
He gave his word that he would return before midnight.
She broke her word and didn’t show up for the meeting.
You have my word—I’ll take care of everything.
He gave his word that he would keep the information confidential.
I trusted her because she always kept her word.
You have my word that the repairs will be completed tomorrow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lie - an intentionally false statement
  • synonym: oath - a solemn promise, often invoking a divine witness
  • synonym: pledge - a formal or solemn promise or undertaking
  • synonym: promise - an assurance that one will do something or that something will happen
