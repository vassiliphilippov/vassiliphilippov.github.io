life line

noun
palm crease linked to lifespan in palmistry
1.6 6.4
Pronunciation: /laɪf laɪn/ (AmE), /laɪf laɪn/ (BrE)
a crease on the palm of the hand, supposed by palmists to indicate the duration of a person's life

Examples

The life line on palm
He examined the deep curve of his life line with curiosity after reading about palmistry.
Some people believe that a short life line means a shorter lifespan, though there is no scientific basis for this.
The palmist traced the life line on my hand and predicted a long, healthy future.
