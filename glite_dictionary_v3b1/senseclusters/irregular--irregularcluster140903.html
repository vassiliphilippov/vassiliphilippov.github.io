irregular

adjective
not having measurable dimensions (solid)
2.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrɛɡjələr / (AmE), / ɪˈrɛɡjʊlə / (BrE)
not even, smooth, or symmetrical in shape; not measurable by standard means

Examples

Irregular shape
A rock is an irregular solid, so its volume can't be calculated with a ruler.
Many natural materials are considered irregular because they lack clearly defined shapes.
To find the volume of an irregular object, scientists often use water displacement.
An irregular crack in the windshield spread quickly after the impact.
She admired the irregular outline of the handmade pottery bowl.
The path was hard to walk on because the stones were so irregular.

Related Terms

  • antonym: regular - (of solids) having clear, measurable dimensions
  • synonym: uneven - not level, smooth, or straight
