toolsy

adjective
like or full of tools
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːlzi / (AmE), / ˈtuːlzi / (BrE)
slang for having many tools, resembling tools, or appearing awkward in a way related to tools

Examples

A toolsy garage
His garage looked especially toolsy after he bought a new set of wrenches and drills.
The workshop had a toolsy vibe, with gadgets and equipment covering every surface.
He wore a toolsy belt that made him look ready for any repair job, even if he was a bit clumsy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awkward - lacking grace or skill
  • synonym: tool-filled - containing many tools
