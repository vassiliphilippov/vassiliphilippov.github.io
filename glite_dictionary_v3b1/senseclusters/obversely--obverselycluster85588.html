obversely

adverb
on the front side
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑbvɚsli / (AmE), / ˈɒbvɜːsli / (BrE)
on or toward the obverse or front side of an object, especially a coin or medal

Examples

Obversely stamped coin
The ancient coin was obversely stamped with the emperor's profile.
The award was obversely inscribed with the recipient's name and the date.
Each medal is obversely decorated with a national symbol.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reversely - on or toward the reverse or back side of an object
