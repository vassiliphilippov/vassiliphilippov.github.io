saxicolous plant

noun
plant growing among rocks
Pronunciation: / sækˈsɪkələs plænt / (AmE), / sækˈsɪkələs plɑːnt / (BrE)
A plant that grows among rocks or rocky habitats.

Examples

Saxicolous plants grow on rocks
Botanists studied the diversity of saxicolous plants in mountainous regions.
Many saxicolous plants have adapted to survive with minimal soil and water.
The rocky cliffs were home to several rare saxicolous plants.
