mahatma

noun
respected Hindu sage or spiritual leader
Pronunciation: / məˈhɑtmə / (AmE), / məˈhɑːtmə / (BrE)
A person respected for wisdom and holiness, especially a sage or spiritual leader in Hinduism.

Examples

A mahatma leader
Many people regarded Gandhi as a mahatma because of his compassion and leadership.
She found inspiration in the writings of the mahatma who taught the value of inner peace.
The village celebrated the visit of a mahatma who was famous for his spiritual teachings.
