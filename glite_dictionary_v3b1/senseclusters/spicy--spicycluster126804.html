spicy

adjective
provocative; likely to cause controversy
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪsi / (AmE), / ˈspaɪsi / (BrE)
bold or provocative; likely to cause strong reactions or controversy, especially about an opinion, comment, or online content (not necessarily sexual).

Examples

Spicy comments
Her spicy take on the budget debate sparked lively discussion across social media.
The columnist's spicy critique of the mayor made headlines and divided readers' opinions.
During the meeting he offered a spicier suggestion that challenged the team's usual approach.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bland - lacking strong features or qualities; uninteresting and not likely to provoke
  • synonym: controversial - causing disagreement or public argument
  • synonym: provocative - intended to provoke a reaction or strong feeling
