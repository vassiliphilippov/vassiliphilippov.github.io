hissy

noun
childish tantrum
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪsi / (AmE), / ˈhɪsi / (BrE)
an informal term for a short, childish outburst of anger or a tantrum.

Examples

Throw a hissy
She threw a hissy when her brother borrowed her phone without asking.
After losing the game, he had a little hissy on the field.
The toddler's hissy in the supermarket drew everyone's attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fit - a sudden display of strong emotion, especially anger
  • synonym: tantrum - a sudden, uncontrolled outburst of anger
