turnover

noun
loss of possession
3.4 3.44/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt…ùÀênÀåo äv…ö / (AmE), / Ààt…úÀênÀå…ô äv…ô / (BrE)
an event in sports when a team loses possession of the ball to the opposing team

Examples

A costly turnover
The team's third turnover in the first half cost them valuable points.
Reducing turnovers is essential for winning close basketball games.
After a costly turnover, the coach called a timeout to regroup the players.

Synonyms

  • takeaway
    4.8 0.25/m

Related Terms

  • antonym: possession - the state of having control of the ball in a game
