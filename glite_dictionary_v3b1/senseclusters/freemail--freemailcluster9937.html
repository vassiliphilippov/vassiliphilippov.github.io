freemail

noun
free advertising-supported email service
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːˌmeɪl / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌmeɪl / (BrE)
an email service that is provided free of charge, usually in exchange for exposure to advertisements

Examples

A freemail account

The popularity of freemail has increased as more users seek cost-effective communication solutions.
