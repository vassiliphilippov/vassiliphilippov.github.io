whisk

verb
beat or stir quickly, often with a whisk
3.8
Pronunciation: / wɪsk / (AmE), / wɪsk / (BrE)
to beat or stir a substance, especially eggs or cream, rapidly using a light, rapid movement or a wire whisk

Examples

To whisk eggs
Add the milk and whisk until the mixture is smooth.
He is whisking the cream to make it fluffy.
She quickly whisked the eggs for the omelette.
