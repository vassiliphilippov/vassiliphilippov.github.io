shopwalker

noun
retail store supervisor and customer assistant
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑpˌwɔkər / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpˌwɔːkə / (BrE)
an employee in a retail store whose job is to supervise sales staff and assist customers with problems or inquiries

Examples

The shopwalker helps customers
As a shopwalker, her main responsibility was to ensure customers received prompt service.
If you have a complaint, the shopwalker will be happy to help resolve it.
The shopwalker quickly noticed the customer's confusion and offered assistance.
