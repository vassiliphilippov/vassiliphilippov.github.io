moment

noun
great importance or significance
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
great importance, significance, or consequence; the quality of being important or influential

Examples

Moment of importance
Moral principles should always be treated as matters of the highest moment.

This is a decision of great moment for the future of the company.
