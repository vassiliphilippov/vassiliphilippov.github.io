cyclonic

adjective
relating to a cyclone or low-pressure system
1.0 5.6
Pronunciation: /saɪ.ˈkloʊ.nɪk/ /; / /saɪ.ˈkloʊ.nək/ (AmE), /saɪ.ˈkləʊ.nɪk/ /; / /saɪ.ˈkləʊ.nək/ (BrE)
relating to a cyclone or the weather conditions typical of cyclones, such as rotating winds and strong storms

Examples

Cyclonic storm winds
