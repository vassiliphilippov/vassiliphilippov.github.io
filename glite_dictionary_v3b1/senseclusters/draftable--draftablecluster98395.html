draftable

adjective
eligible for selection
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdræftəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈdrɑːftəbl̩ / (BrE)
eligible or able to be selected in a draft, such as for a sports team or military service

Examples

Draftable player
After recovering from his injury, the athlete was once again draftable by professional teams.
Only citizens between certain ages are considered draftable for military service.
The coach reviewed the list of draftable players before making his final selections.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eligible - having the right to be chosen or selected
  • antonym: ineligible - not having the right to be chosen or selected
