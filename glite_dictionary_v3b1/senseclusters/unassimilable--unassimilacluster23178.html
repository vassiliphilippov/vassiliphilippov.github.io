unassimilable

adjective
not able to be integrated
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈsɪmɪləbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈsɪmɪləbəl / (BrE)
impossible or very difficult to be absorbed or integrated into a group, system, or culture

Examples

Unassimilable customs
Some cultural practices may seem unassimilable to outsiders.
The new policy was considered unassimilable by many members of the organization.
Certain dietary habits are unassimilable in regions with different food traditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: assimilable - able to be absorbed or integrated into a group or system
  • synonym: incompatible - unable to exist or work together without conflict
