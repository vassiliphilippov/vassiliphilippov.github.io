buy

verb
obtain by paying money
Pronunciation: / baɪ / (AmE), / baɪ / (BrE)
to get or obtain something by paying money or giving something in exchange

This little money won't buy much in the city these days.

Sometimes people attempt to buy respect through charitable donations.
