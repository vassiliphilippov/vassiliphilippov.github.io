billowing

adjective
swelling in large waves or surges
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪl.oʊ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɪl.əʊ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
swelling outward or upward in large waves or surges, as if forming billows

Examples

Billowing sleeves
She wore a dress with billowing sleeves that floated gracefully as she moved.
Smoke was billowing from the factory chimneys after the explosion.
Thick, billowing clouds darkened the afternoon sky before the storm began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: billowy - swelling or surging in waves, like billows
