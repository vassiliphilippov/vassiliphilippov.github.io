dustproof

verb
to protect from dust
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌstˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈdʌstˌpruːf / (BrE)
to make something resistant to dust or prevent dust from entering or damaging it

Examples

To dustproof equipment
They need to dustproof the laboratory equipment before the experiment begins.
The company will dustproof all electronic devices for use in sandy environments.
It is important to dustproof your camera when traveling to dusty places.

  • synonym: waterproof - to make resistant to water
