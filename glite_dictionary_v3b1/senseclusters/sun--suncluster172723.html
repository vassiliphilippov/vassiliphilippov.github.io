sun

noun
the rays or light from the sun
Pronunciation: / sʌn / (AmE), / sʌn / (BrE)
the light or rays given off by the sun, especially as they are felt or seen on Earth

Examples

Bright sun light
The old photographs faded quickly in the strong sun pouring through the window.
Too much exposure to the sun can damage your skin.
We spent the entire afternoon basking in the warm sun on the beach.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shade - relative darkness caused by shelter from direct sunlight
  • synonym: sunlight - light from the sun, especially as it reaches the earth
