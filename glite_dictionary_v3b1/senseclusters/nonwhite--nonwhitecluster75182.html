nonwhite

noun
person not classified as white
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈwaɪt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈwaɪt / (BrE)
a person who is not classified as white, often used as a collective term for people of other racial backgrounds

Examples

A nonwhite
The study focused on the experiences of nonwhite students in higher education.
Many nonwhite communities have advocated for greater representation in government.
The report highlighted disparities in healthcare access for nonwhite populations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: person of color - a person who is not white, especially in the context of social identity
  • antonym: white - a person classified as white in terms of race
