dental practitioner

noun
a person qualified to practice dentistry
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛntəl prækˈtɪʃənɚ / (AmE), / ˈdɛntəl prækˈtɪʃənə / (BrE)
A person who is qualified and licensed to practice dentistry.

Examples

Licensed dental practitioners
Licensed dental practitioners are required to follow strict hygiene protocols.
The dental practitioner explained the procedure before extracting my wisdom tooth.
You should see a dental practitioner if you are experiencing consistent tooth pain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dentist - professional who is qualified to treat diseases and conditions of the teeth and gums
