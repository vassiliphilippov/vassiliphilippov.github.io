beatifically

adverb
in a blissful way
7.4
Pronunciation: / biˈætɪfɪkli / (AmE), / biˈætɪfɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that shows great happiness, serenity, or bliss, often with a peaceful or blessed expression

Examples

She smiled beatifically as she watched her grandchildren play in the garden.
The artist gazed beatifically at his finished painting, feeling a deep sense of peace.
After the ceremony, the priest looked beatifically at the congregation, radiating calm and joy.
