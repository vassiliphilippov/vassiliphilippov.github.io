formulaic nature

noun
quality of being predictable or conventional
8.1 5.8
Pronunciation: /fɔːr.mjə.ˈleɪ.ɪk ˈneɪ.tʃər/ (AmE), /fɔː.mjə.ˈleɪ.ɪk ˈneɪ.tʃə/ (BrE)
the quality or state of being made according to a fixed or conventional formula, often leading to predictability or lack of originality

Examples

The formulaic nature of movies
Critics often point to the formulaic nature of blockbuster movies as a downside.
Some listeners find the formulaic nature of pop music repetitive and uninspiring.
The formulaic nature of the report made it dull and unengaging.
