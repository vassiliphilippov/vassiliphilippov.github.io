collisional

adjective
caused by collisions
7.4
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɪʒənəl / (AmE), / kəˈlɪʒənəl / (BrE)
relating to or caused by collisions between objects or particles

Examples

Collisional processes
Scientists study collisional processes to understand how planets and moons formed.
The collisional dynamics of particles are important in physics experiments.
Astronomers analyze the collisional history of the asteroid belt to learn about the solar system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collision - an instance of two or more objects hitting each other
  • synonym: impact - the action of one object coming forcibly into contact with another
