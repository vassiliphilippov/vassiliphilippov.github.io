haricot

noun
French variety of bean or its seed
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhærɪkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈhæ.rɪ.kəʊ /; / ˈharɪkəʊ / (BrE)
a type of bean, especially used in French cooking; can refer to green beans or small dried beans
