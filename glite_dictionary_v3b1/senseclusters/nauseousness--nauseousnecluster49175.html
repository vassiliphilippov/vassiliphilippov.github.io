nauseousness

noun
feeling of nausea
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔːʃəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈnɔːzɪəsnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of causing nausea or a feeling of being sick or disgusted

Examples

Nauseousness of the smell
The nauseousness of the spoiled food made it impossible to stay in the room.
He complained about the nauseousness he felt after riding the roller coaster.
The chemical's strong odor added to the overall nauseousness of the laboratory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disgust - a strong feeling of dislike or revulsion
  • synonym: nausea - a feeling of sickness with an urge to vomit
  • antonym: pleasantness - the quality of being enjoyable or agreeable
