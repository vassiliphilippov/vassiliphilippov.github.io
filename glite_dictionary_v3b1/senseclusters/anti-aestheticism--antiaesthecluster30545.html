anti-aestheticism

noun
opposition to the Aesthetic art movement
7.4 9.2
Pronunciation: /æn.tiː.e.sˈθet.ɪ.zəm/ /; / /æn.tɪ.e.sˈθet.ɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ænti.e.sˈθet.ɪ.zəm/ /; / /æntiː.e.sˈθet.ɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
opposition to or rejection of the principles or values of the Aesthetic art movement, especially in art, literature, or culture

Examples

Anti-aestheticism in art
His work was notable for its strong sense of anti-aestheticism and disregard for classical beauty.
Some critics argued that the novel's anti-aestheticism was a deliberate challenge to traditional artistic values.
The exhibition celebrated anti-aestheticism by showcasing pieces that defied conventional standards of beauty.
