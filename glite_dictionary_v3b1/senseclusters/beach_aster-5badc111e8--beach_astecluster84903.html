beach aster

noun
succulent coastal plant with pink/lavender flowers
Pronunciation: / bitʃ ˈæstɚ / (AmE), / biːtʃ ˈɑːstə / (BrE)
a succulent perennial plant with basal leaves and hairy, sticky stems bearing a solitary flower head with narrow pink or lavender rays, found on coastal bluffs from Oregon to southern California

Examples

Beach aster flower
Gardeners appreciate the resilience of beach aster in dry, salty environments.
In spring, clusters of beach asters add a splash of color to the shoreline trails.
The beach aster thrives on the sandy bluffs along the Pacific coastline.
