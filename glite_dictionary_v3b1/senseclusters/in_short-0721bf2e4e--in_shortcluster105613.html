in short

adverb
to sum up briefly
Pronunciation: / ɪn ʃɔrt / (AmE), / ɪn ʃɔːt / (BrE)
used to indicate that you are giving only the main facts or a concise summary of something

Examples

In short summary
In short, the meeting was a waste of time.
In short, we need to invest more resources if we want the project to succeed.
His explanation was, in short, completely unconvincing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: briefly - in a concise manner; using few words
