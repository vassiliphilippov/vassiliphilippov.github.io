och

interjection
exclamation of surprise
Pronunciation: / ɑx / (AmE), / ɒx / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express surprise, mild pain, or emotion, especially in Scottish or Irish English

Examples

Och, I forgot
Och, I forgot to bring my umbrella and now it's raining.
He stubbed his toe and cried out, 'Och, that hurt!'
Och, what a beautiful view from the top of the hill!

Related Terms

  • synonym: oh - an exclamation expressing surprise or emotion
  • synonym: ouch - an exclamation expressing sudden pain
