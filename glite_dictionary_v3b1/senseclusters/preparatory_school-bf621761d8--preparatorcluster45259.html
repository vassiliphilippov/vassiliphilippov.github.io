preparatory school

noun
private school preparing students for higher education
Pronunciation: / prɪˈpɛrəˌtɔri skul / (AmE), / prɪˈpærətəri skuːl / (BrE)
A private school that prepares students, especially for admission to higher education, typically used for certain private secondary schools.

Examples

A preparatory school
He attended a preparatory school before enrolling at the university.
Many families choose a preparatory school to give their children the best start in education.
The preparatory school offered small classes and a strong academic program.

Related Terms

  • synonym: public school - in the UK, a private independent secondary school
