Dostoyevskian

adjective
of or like Dostoevsky's style or themes
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /dɑːs.tɔɪ.ˈev.ski.iː.ən/ /; / /də.stɔɪ.ˈev.ski.iː.ən/ (AmE), /dɒs.tɔɪ.ˈev.ski.iː.ən/ /; / /də.stɔɪ.ˈev.ski.iː.ən/ (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of the works, themes, or style of Fyodor Dostoevsky, especially involving existential themes, psychological depth, or moral dilemmas.

Examples

Dostoyevskian themes
Her novels often have a Dostoyevskian complexity, exploring the darkest corners of human psychology.
Some critics describe his characters as Dostoyevskian in their struggle with guilt and redemption.
The atmosphere of the film was distinctly Dostoyevskian, filled with existential angst and moral ambiguity.
