wall rock

noun
rock next to a vein or fault
Pronunciation: / wɔl rɑk / (AmE), / wɔːl rɒk / (BrE)
rock that is immediately adjacent to a vein, fault, or other geological structure.

Examples

Wall rock near vein
Geologists examined the wall rock to determine the history of mineralization in the area.
In mining operations, understanding the properties of the wall rock is crucial for safety and planning.
The wall rock showed clear signs of alteration from the heat of the vein intrusion.
