plasmatic quality

noun
fluid or formless characteristic
Pronunciation: / plæzˈmætɪk ˈkwɑːləti / (AmE), / plæzˈmætɪk ˈkwɒləti / (BrE)
A characteristic of being fluid, formless, or capable of dynamic change.

Examples

His abstract painting displayed a plasmatic quality that blurred the boundaries between shapes.

The dancer's movements had a plasmatic quality, shifting seamlessly from one pose to another.
