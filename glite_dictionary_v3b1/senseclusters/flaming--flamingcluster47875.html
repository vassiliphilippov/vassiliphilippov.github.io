flaming

adjective
burning brightly
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfleɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
burning with a bright flame or appearing very bright, hot, or intensely colored

Examples

Flaming torch
A flaming torch lit the entrance to the cave.
The flaming sunset painted the sky with shades of orange and red.
Firefighters rushed to put out the flaming car on the highway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blazing - burning fiercely or shining brightly
  • antonym: smoldering - burning slowly with smoke but no flame
