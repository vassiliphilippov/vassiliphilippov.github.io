Prosimii

noun
former primate suborder (lemurs, tarsiers, etc.)
Pronunciation: / proʊˈsɪmi.aɪ / (AmE), / prəʊˈsɪmɪ.aɪ / (BrE)
a former suborder of primates that included lemurs, lorises, and tarsiers, not recognized in all modern classifications

Examples

The Prosimii group
Recent taxonomic revisions have rendered the name Prosimii largely historical in modern primatology.
Some textbooks still refer to Prosimii despite newer classification systems.
The now-obsolete suborder Prosimii once grouped both lemurs and tarsiers together.
