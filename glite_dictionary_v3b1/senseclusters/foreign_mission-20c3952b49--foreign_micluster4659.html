foreign mission

noun
religious organization in another country
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrən ˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈfɒrən ˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
an organization consisting of missionaries sent to another country to conduct religious work

Examples

A foreign mission group
Many foreign missions provide not just religious services, but also education and healthcare in the local community.
She spent ten years volunteering with a foreign mission in Southeast Asia.
The church raised funds to support their foreign mission in Africa.

Related Terms

  • antonym: home mission - a religious mission carried out within one's own country
  • synonym: missionary - a person sent on a religious mission, especially to promote Christianity in a foreign country
