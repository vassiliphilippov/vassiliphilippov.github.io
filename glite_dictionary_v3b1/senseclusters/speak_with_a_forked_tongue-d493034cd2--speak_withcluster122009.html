speak with a forked tongue

verb
to speak deceitfully or insincerely
6.8 7.6
Pronunciation: /spiːk wɪð ə fɔːrkt tʌŋ/ /; / /spiːk wɪθ ə fɔːrkt tʌŋ/ (AmE), /spiːk wɪð ə fɔːkt tʌŋ/ /; / /spiːk wɪθ ə fɔːkt tʌŋ/ (BrE)
