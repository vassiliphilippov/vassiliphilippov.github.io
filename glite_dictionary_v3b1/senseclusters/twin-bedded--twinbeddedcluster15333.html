twin-bedded

adjective
having two single beds
Pronunciation: / twɪn ˈbɛdɪd / (AmE), / twɪn ˈbɛdɪd / (BrE)
(of a room) furnished with two single beds.

Examples

A twin-bedded room
Our children shared a spacious twin-bedded suite during the vacation.
The hotel offers both double-bedded and twin-bedded accommodations.
We booked a twin-bedded room for our stay in London.

Related Terms
