zoon

noun
asexually produced animal
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈzoʊ.ɒn / (AmE), / ˈzəʊ.ɒn / (BrE)
an individual animal produced by asexual reproduction, especially as part of a colonial organism such as coral

Examples

Coral zoon
Each zoon in the coral colony plays a different role in feeding and protection.
Scientists studied how a single zoon develops within a group of jellyfish.
The structure of the reef depends on the cooperation of every zoon in the colony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: polyp - a small, typically colonial animal such as those forming corals
  • synonym: zooid - an individual animal forming part of a colonial organism
