oooh

interjection
exclamation of pain
Pronunciation: / uː / (AmE), / uː / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express sudden mild pain, discomfort, or surprise

Examples

Oooh that hurts
When she touched the hot pan, she quickly said oooh and pulled her hand away.
He stubbed his toe on the table and let out a loud oooh in the kitchen.
As the cold water splashed on her face, she gasped, 'oooh, that's freezing!'

Related Terms

  • synonym: ouch - an exclamation expressing pain
  • synonym: ow - an exclamation used when something hurts
