pluperfect

noun
grammatical tense for completed action in the past
Pronunciation: / ˌpluˈpɝː.fɪkt / (AmE), / ˌpluːˈpɜː.fɪkt / (BrE)
a verb tense used to express an action that was completed before another past action or time

Examples

The pluperfect tense
French verbs require a pluperfect form in certain subordinate clauses.
In the sentence 'She had left', the verb is in the pluperfect.
The pluperfect is often introduced in advanced grammar lessons.
