beat off with a stick

verb
repel forcefully; fend off numerous things/people
Pronunciation: / bit ɔf wɪð ə stɪk / (AmE), / biːt ɒf wɪð ə stɪk / (BrE)
To repel or push away someone or something forcefully, especially when there are many of them; often used humorously to suggest being overwhelmed by attention or presence.
