kukri

noun
curved Gurkha knife
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːkri /; / kuːˈkuːri / (AmE), / ˈkʊkri /; / kuːˈkuːri / (BrE)
A curved knife from South Asia, especially associated with the Gurkhas of Nepal; traditionally made of steel with a razor-sharp edge and holding cultural and religious importance in Nepal.
