slap-bang

adverb
directly; exactly (position or place)
Pronunciation: / slæpˈbæŋ / (AmE), / slæpˈbaŋ / (BrE)
directly or exactly, often used to emphasize that something is in a particular place or position without deviation

Examples

Slap-bang in the center
She found herself slap-bang in the middle of the busiest street in town.
The ball landed slap-bang in the center of the field.
The cake was placed slap-bang on the dining table, right in front of everyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bang - exactly; precisely
  • synonym: directly - in a straight line or manner
