senti

noun
African currency subunit
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnti / (AmE), / ˈsɛnti / (BrE)
a monetary subunit used in some African countries, equal to one hundredth of the main currency unit

Examples

Fifty senti coins
The vendor asked for twenty senti for a small bag of fruit.
In Tanzania, one hundred senti make up one shilling.
The coin collection included several rare senti from different years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cent - a monetary unit equal to one hundredth of the main unit in many countries
