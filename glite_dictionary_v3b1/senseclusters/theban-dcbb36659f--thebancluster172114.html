Theban

noun
inhabitant of ancient Thebes
Pronunciation: / ˈθiː.bən / (AmE), / ˈθiː.bən / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of ancient Thebes, especially in Egypt.

Examples

A Theban mummy

Many myths center around legendary Theban rulers and their conflicts with neighboring cities.
The ancient Theban civilization left behind impressive monuments and tombs.
