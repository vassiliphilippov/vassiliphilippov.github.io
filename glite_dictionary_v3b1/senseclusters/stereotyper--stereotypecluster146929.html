stereotyper

noun
person who stereotypes
7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈste.r.i.oʊ.taɪ.pər/ /; / /ˈstɪr.i.oʊ.taɪ.pər/ (AmE), /ˈste.r.i.əʊ.taɪ.pə/ /; / /ˈstɪr.i.əʊ.taɪ.pə/ (BrE)
a person who forms or applies fixed, oversimplified ideas or beliefs about a group of people or things

Examples

Social stereotyper
He was labeled a stereotyper after making assumptions about people based on their appearance.
A good teacher avoids being a stereotyper and treats each student as an individual.
Calling someone a stereotyper can start an important conversation about fairness and respect.

Related Terms

  • antonym: individualist - someone who treats people as unique individuals
  • synonym: prejudiced person - someone who holds unfair or biased opinions about others
