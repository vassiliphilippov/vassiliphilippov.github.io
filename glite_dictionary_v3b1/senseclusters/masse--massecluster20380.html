masse

noun
a curved cue shot in billiards or pool
6.5
Pronunciation: / mæˈseɪ / (AmE), / mæˈseɪ / (BrE)
a shot in billiards or pool in which the cue ball is struck with the cue held almost vertically, causing it to curve or spin sharply around another ball

Examples

A masse shot
He impressed the crowd with his ability to make the cue ball curve using a masse.
Learning to control a masse is challenging, even for experienced billiards players.
The professional executed a perfect masse to avoid the blocking ball and sink the shot.
