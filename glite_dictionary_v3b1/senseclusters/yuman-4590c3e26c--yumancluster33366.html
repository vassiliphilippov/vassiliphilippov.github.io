Yuman

noun
a group of languages of the Hokan family
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːmən / (AmE), / ˈjuːmən / (BrE)
a group of closely related languages of the Hokan family spoken in southwestern United States (Arizona and California) and northern Mexico

Examples

Yuman languages
Anthropologists documented oral histories in several Yuman dialects during their fieldwork in northern Mexico.
Linguists have studied the Yuman language family to learn more about prehistoric migrations in the Southwest.
The Yuman languages are spoken by several indigenous tribes in Arizona and California.
