Giving the wrong idea or impression, making someone believe something that is not true.

Examples

Misleading information
His explanation was misleading, causing many to misunderstand the situation.
Some statistics can be misleading if they are taken out of context.
The advertisement was found to be misleading and was banned by regulators.

Synonyms

  • deceptive
    5.3 4.30/m
    deceptive presents a false or tricky appearance to hide the truth, while misleading causes someone to form an incorrect idea.
  • deceitful
    5.0 1.46/m
    deceitful acts with dishonest intent to fool others, while misleading gives a wrong impression not always meant to deceive.
