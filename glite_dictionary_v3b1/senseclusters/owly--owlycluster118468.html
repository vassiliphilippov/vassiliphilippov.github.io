owly

adjective
owl-like appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊli / (AmE), / ˈaʊli / (BrE)
looking or behaving like an owl, especially by being watchful, quiet, or having large, round eyes

Examples

Owly eyes
The child gave me an owly stare from behind his glasses.
Her owly expression made it clear she was paying close attention to everything.
He sat in the corner, quiet and owly, observing the whole room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alert - quick to notice and respond to things
  • synonym: watchful - paying close attention to possible danger or trouble
