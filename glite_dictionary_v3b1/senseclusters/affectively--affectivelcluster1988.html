affectively

adverb
emotionally
7.0
Pronunciation: / əˈfɛktɪvli / (AmE), / əˈfɛktɪvli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to feelings or emotions

Examples

To listen affectively
The therapist listened affectively to her client's concerns, showing genuine empathy.
Children often respond affectively to stories that reflect their own experiences.
The film was designed to engage viewers affectively as well as intellectually.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cognitively - in a way that relates to thinking or reasoning
  • synonym: emotionally - in a way that relates to emotions
