affectless

adjective
without emotion
Pronunciation: / ˈæfɛktləs / (AmE), / ˈæfɛktləs / (BrE)
showing no emotion or feeling; lacking visible emotional expression

Examples

Affectless expression
The actor delivered his lines in an affectless manner, making the scene feel cold and distant.
Her affectless expression gave no hint of how she truly felt about the news.
Despite the chaos around him, he remained affectless and calm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emotionless - not showing any emotion
  • antonym: expressive - showing feelings or emotions clearly
  • synonym: impassive - not feeling or showing emotion
