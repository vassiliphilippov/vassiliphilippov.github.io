more or less

adverb
to some extent; approximately
Pronunciation: / mɔɹ ər lɛs /; / mɔːr ər lɛs / (AmE), / mɔː ə lɛs /; / mɔːr ɔː lɛs / (BrE)
approximately; about; not exactly but close enough

Examples

More or less correct
