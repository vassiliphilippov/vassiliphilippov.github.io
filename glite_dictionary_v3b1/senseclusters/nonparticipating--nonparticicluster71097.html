nonparticipating

adjective
not sharing in profits
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.pɑrˈtɪs.əˌpeɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.pɑːˈtɪs.ɪ.peɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
not entitled to share in profits, dividends, or surplus; not participating in earnings or benefit distributions, especially in finance or insurance.

Examples

Nonparticipating policy
A nonparticipating policy does not pay dividends to the policyholder.
The company issued nonparticipating shares that do not receive extra profits.
He chose a nonparticipating insurance plan because it had lower premiums.
