Hindu

noun
follower of Hinduism
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪn.du / (AmE), / ˈhɪn.duː / (BrE)
A person who follows or practices Hinduism.

Examples

A Hindu believer
Hindu temples can be found throughout India and many parts of the world.
She was raised as a Hindu and celebrates Diwali every year.
The festival attracted both Hindu and non-Hindu participants from various countries.
