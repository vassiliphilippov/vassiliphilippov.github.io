pinkroot

noun
fungal onion disease
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪŋkˌruːt / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋkˌruːt / (BrE)
A fungal disease of onions in which the roots become pink and the plants are stunted.

Examples

Onion pinkroot disease
Farmers noticed that pinkroot was spreading rapidly through their onion crop.
Proper soil drainage helps prevent pinkroot in newly planted onion fields.
The presence of pinkroot can drastically reduce onion yields if not treated.
