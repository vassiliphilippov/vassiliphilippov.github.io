crowdfund

verb
to raise money online
Pronunciation: / ˈkraʊdˌfʌnd / (AmE), / ˈkraʊdˌfʌnd / (BrE)
to raise money for a project or venture by collecting small amounts from many people, typically via the internet

Examples

To crowdfund a project
They decided to crowdfund their new product idea after banks refused to give them a loan.
Many independent artists crowdfund their albums to connect directly with their fans.
The charity managed to crowdfund enough money to build a new community center.

Related Terms

  • synonym: donate - to give money or goods to help a person or organization
  • synonym: fundraise - to collect money for a cause or project
