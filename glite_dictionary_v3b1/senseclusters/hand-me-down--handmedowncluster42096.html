hand-me-down

adjective
secondhand item passed from one person to another
Pronunciation: / ˈhænd mi ˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈhænd mi ˌdaʊn / (BrE)
an object, especially a piece of clothing, that is passed on from one person to another after being used

Examples

Hand-me-down clothes
Many of my childhood toys were hand-me-downs from my older cousins.
She appreciated the vintage look of hand-me-down dresses.
We try to reduce waste by giving hand-me-down clothes to friends and family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: secondhand - previously owned or used by someone else
