anytime

adverb
at any time
Pronunciation: / ˈɛniˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈeniˌtaɪm / (BrE)
at any time; whenever it is convenient or possible

Examples

Anytime you want
You can visit us anytime you are in town.
Feel free to ask questions anytime during the presentation.
The store is open anytime between 9 a.m. and 9 p.m.

Related Terms

  • antonym: never - at no time; not ever
  • synonym: whenever - at whatever time; whenever it may be
