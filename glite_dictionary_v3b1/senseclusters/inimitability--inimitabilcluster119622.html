inimitability

noun
uniqueness; unmatchable quality
9.1
Pronunciation: / ɪˌnɪmɪtəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ɪˌnɪmɪtəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being so unique or excellent that it cannot be copied or matched

Examples

The inimitability of style
The inimitability of his writing style set him apart from other authors of his time.
Critics praised the inimitability of the artist's latest exhibition.
Her inimitability on stage drew audiences from around the world.

Related Terms

  • antonym: imitability - the quality of being able to be copied
  • synonym: originality - the ability to think or create in a new way
  • synonym: uniqueness - the quality of being one of a kind
