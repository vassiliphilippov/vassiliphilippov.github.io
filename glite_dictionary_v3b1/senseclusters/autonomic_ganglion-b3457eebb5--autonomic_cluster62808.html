autonomic ganglion

noun
nerve cell cluster in autonomic nervous system
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɔtəˈnɑmɪk ˈɡæŋɡliən / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈnɒmɪk ˈɡæŋɡliən / (BrE)
A mass of nerve cell bodies in the autonomic nervous system that relays signals to internal organs.

Examples

An autonomic ganglion mass
Damage to an autonomic ganglion can disrupt normal function of the digestive system.
Researchers studied the structure of a parasympathetic autonomic ganglion under the microscope.
The neurotransmitters are released at the autonomic ganglion before reaching the target organ.

  • synonym: sympathetic ganglion - a ganglion of the sympathetic nervous system
