unpleasant

adjective
causing discomfort or dislike
1.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈplɛzənt / (AmE), / ʌnˈplɛzənt / (BrE)
Not enjoyable or agreeable; causing discomfort, offense, or unhappiness.

Examples

An unpleasant smell
It's unpleasant to work in such a noisy environment.
She had an unpleasant experience at the dentist's office.
There was an unpleasant smell coming from the garbage bin.
