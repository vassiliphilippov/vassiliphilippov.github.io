Synonyms

  • disagreeable
    3.9 0.3/m
    disagreeable is used for things that cause mild annoyance or discomfort in everyday situations, while unpleasant is a general word for not enjoyable.
  • displeasing
    4.0 0.08/m
    displeasing often describes specific actions or sights that annoy or irritate people, while unpleasant is broader and covers many bad feelings.
  • unpleasing
    4.8 0.01/m
    unpleasing sounds rare and formal, and often describes things that fail to give pleasure, while unpleasant is the common everyday word.
  • distasteful
