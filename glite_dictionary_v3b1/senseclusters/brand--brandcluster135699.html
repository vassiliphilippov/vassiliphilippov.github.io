brand

noun
product or service name for identification
Pronunciation: / brænd / (AmE), / brænd / (BrE)
a type of product, service, company, or kind that is distinguished by a particular name, symbol, or set of qualities

Examples

A famous brand
Coca-Cola is a well-known brand recognized around the world.
Many customers remain loyal to a brand they trust.
The company spent millions to make their new brand a household name.
Her brand of leadership inspired confidence in her team.
There's a new brand of humor that's popular among young people.
This restaurant offers a unique brand of Italian cuisine.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generic - not associated with a particular brand
  • synonym: make - the manufacturer or specific type of something, especially vehicles
  • synonym: trademark - a symbol or word legally registered for use by a company
  • synonym: type - a category or class with common characteristics
