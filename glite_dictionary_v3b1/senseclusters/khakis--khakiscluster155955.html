khakis

noun
trousers or uniform made of khaki fabric
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkækiːz / (AmE), / ˈkɑːkiːz / (BrE)
trousers or a military uniform made of khaki-colored fabric, typically cotton or a cotton blend

Examples

Light khakis trousers
During summer, many soldiers were issued light khakis as their standard uniform.
He wore a crisp shirt tucked into his khakis for the interview.
She prefers wearing khakis to jeans because they feel more comfortable at work.
