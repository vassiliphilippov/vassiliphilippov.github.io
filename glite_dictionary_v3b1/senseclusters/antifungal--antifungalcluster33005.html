antifungal

noun
agent that prevents or destroys fungi
1.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /æn.tɪ.ˈfʌŋ.gəl/ /; / /æn.taɪ.ˈfʌŋ.gəl/ /; / /æn.tiː.ˈfʌŋ.gəl/ (AmE), /æn.tɪ.ˈfʌŋ.gəl/ /; / /æn.taɪ.ˈfʌŋ.gəl/ (BrE)
A substance that destroys or inhibits the growth of fungi, especially used to treat fungal infections.

Examples

Antifungal cream
Applying a topical antifungal cream can help relieve symptoms of ringworm.
Many antifungal medications can be purchased over the counter at the pharmacy.
The doctor prescribed an antifungal to treat the athlete's foot infection.
