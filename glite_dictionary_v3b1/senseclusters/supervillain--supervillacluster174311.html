supervillain

noun
fictional villain with superpowers
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsuː.pɚˌvɪl.ən / (AmE), / ˈsuː.pəˌvɪl.ən / (BrE)
A fictional villain, typically possessing superhuman powers or advanced technology, who opposes superheroes.

Examples

The supervillain fights heroes
In the latest comic, the supervillain battled his longtime superhero rival.
She designed her costume to look like a classic supervillain.
The supervillain threatened to destroy the city unless his demands were met.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hero - a person admired for courage or noble qualities, often opposing villains
  • degree lower: villain - a wicked or evil person, especially in fiction
