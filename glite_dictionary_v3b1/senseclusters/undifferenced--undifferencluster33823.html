undifferenced

adjective
not differenced
9.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdɪfərənst / (AmE), / ʌnˈdɪfərənst / (BrE)
not subjected to differencing; referring to raw or original data, especially in time-series analysis.

Examples

Undifferenced data set
The analyst examined the undifferenced data before deciding which statistical methods to apply.
Plotting the undifferenced series revealed a clear upward trend over time.
Researchers often compare undifferenced and differenced data to check for stationarity in time-series analysis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: differenced - having undergone differencing, a transformation in data analysis
  • synonym: raw - in its original, unprocessed state
