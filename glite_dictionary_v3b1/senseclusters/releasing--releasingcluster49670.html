releasing

adjective
emotionally purging or cathartic
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlisɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈliːsɪŋ / (BrE)
having the effect of causing someone to release or purge emotions, especially through art, music, or therapeutic experiences.

Examples

A releasing effect
Her poetry has a profoundly releasing effect on its readers, offering solace and understanding.
Many people found the movie's ending releasing, allowing them to confront their own fears.
Painting can be a releasing experience, helping people process their emotions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cathartic - providing psychological relief through open expression of strong emotions
