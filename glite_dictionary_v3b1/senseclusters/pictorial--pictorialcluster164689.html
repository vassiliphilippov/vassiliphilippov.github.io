pictorial

noun
photo-based magazine
4.9
Pronunciation: / pɪkˈtɔriəl / (AmE), / pɪkˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
a magazine or publication that mainly features photographs or illustrations rather than text

Examples

A pictorial magazine
He collected rare pictorials from the 1960s to add to his vintage magazine collection.
The library has a section dedicated to pictorials featuring famous photographers' work.
Many pictorials focus on travel destinations and showcase stunning images from around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illustrated magazine - a magazine with many illustrations
  • synonym: photo magazine - a magazine focused on photographs
  • antonym: textbook - a book containing mainly written content for study
