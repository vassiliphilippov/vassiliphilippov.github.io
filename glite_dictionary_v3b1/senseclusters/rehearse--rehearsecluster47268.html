rehearse

verb
to practice for a performance
2.3
Pronunciation: / rɪˈhɜrs / (AmE), / rɪˈhɜːs / (BrE)
to practice a play, piece of music, or other performance in preparation for a public presentation

Examples

To rehearse a play
She prefers to rehearse her speech several times before delivering it.
The actors will rehearse every evening until the opening night.
The orchestra began to rehearse the new symphony yesterday.
