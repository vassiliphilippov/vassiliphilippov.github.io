white perch

noun
a small North American freshwater fish
4.8
Pronunciation: / waɪt pɝtʃ / (AmE), / waɪt pɜːtʃ / (BrE)
a small, silvery North American freshwater fish (Morone americana) found in streams and used for food and sport

Examples

The white perch fish
Anglers often try to catch white perch in the streams along the eastern United States.
The white perch is popular both as a game fish and a source of food.
We spotted a school of white perch swimming near the riverbank.
