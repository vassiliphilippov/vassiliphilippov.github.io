smallness

noun
state of being small in status or size
4.2 0.20/m
Pronunciation: / Ààsm…îln…ôs / (AmE), / Ààsm…îÀêln…ôs / (BrE)
The quality or condition of being small, especially in terms of influence, power, or rank.

Examples

Smallness of influence
He was always aware of his smallness within the powerful organization.
She felt a sense of smallness standing before the vast, ancient mountains.
The smallness of the village prevented it from having its own school council.
