nonoptical

adjective
not related to light
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈɑptɪkəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈɒptɪkəl / (BrE)
not involving, using, or related to light or vision

Examples

Nonoptical methods
The laboratory developed nonoptical methods for measuring temperature in extreme environments.
Some animals rely on nonoptical cues, such as sound or smell, to navigate in the dark.
The engineer designed a nonoptical system to detect movement without using cameras or light sensors.
