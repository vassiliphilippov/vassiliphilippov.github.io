ruralism

noun
rural characteristic or trait
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrʊr.əˌlɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈrʊə.rəˌlɪz.əm / (BrE)
the characteristic or quality of being rural; rural traits or attitudes

Examples

A sense of ruralism
Critics say that his writing displays a certain ruralism that romanticizes country life.
The ruralism of the landscape was evident in its open fields and scattered farmhouses.
There is an increasing interest in ruralism as people seek alternatives to urban living.

Related Terms

  • antonym: urbanism - the characteristic or quality of being urban; urban traits or attitudes
