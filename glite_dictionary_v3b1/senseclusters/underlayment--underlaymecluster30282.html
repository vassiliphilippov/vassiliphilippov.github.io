underlayment

noun
pad or layer under carpet or flooring
8.5 5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈʌn.dər.leɪ.mənt/ (AmE), /ˈʌn.də.leɪ.mənt/ (BrE)
a material, such as a pad or layer, placed underneath flooring or carpet to provide support and insulation

Examples

Floor underlayment
It is important to install a quality underlayment before laying the new carpet.
The hardwood floor feels softer thanks to the foam underlayment beneath it.
This type of underlayment improves soundproofing and thermal insulation.
