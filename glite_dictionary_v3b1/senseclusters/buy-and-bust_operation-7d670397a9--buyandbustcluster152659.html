buy-and-bust operation

noun
undercover police operation to catch drug dealers
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ ən ˈbʌst ˌɑpəˌreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ ənd ˈbʌst ˌɒpəˌreɪʃən / (BrE)
an undercover police action in which officers pretend to purchase illegal drugs in order to arrest the seller

Examples

Undercover buy-and-bust operation
During the buy-and-bust operation, the undercover officer posed as a potential buyer.
Many dealers were wary after hearing about last week's buy-and-bust operation.
The police carried out a buy-and-bust operation that led to several arrests in the city.
