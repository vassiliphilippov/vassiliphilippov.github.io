untypicality

noun
the state of being untypical
Pronunciation: / ʌnˌtɪpɪˈkæləti / (AmE), / ʌnˌtɪpɪˈkælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being untypical; not conforming to what is usual or expected

Examples

The untypicality of behaviour
She was drawn to the untypicality of the old building, which stood out among newer structures.
The untypicality of his behavior surprised everyone at the meeting.
Their approach showed a refreshing untypicality compared to most teams in the industry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atypicality - the quality of being atypical; not representative of a type, group, or class
  • antonym: typicality - the quality or state of being typical; conformity to a type
