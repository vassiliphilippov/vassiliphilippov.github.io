Acrasiomycetes

noun
a class of cellular slime molds
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˌkreɪsiˌoʊmaɪˈsiːtiːz /; / ˌæk.rəˌsaɪ.oʊ.maɪˈsiːtiːz / (AmE), / əˌkrasɪəʊmaɪˈsiːtiːz /; / ˌakrəsiəʊmaɪˈsiːtiːz / (BrE)
a class of cellular slime molds (sometimes classified in the kingdom Protoctista) that are amoeboid and can form multicellular structures
