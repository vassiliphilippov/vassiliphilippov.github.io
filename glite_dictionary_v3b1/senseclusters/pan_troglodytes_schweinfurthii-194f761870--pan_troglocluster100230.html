Pan troglodytes schweinfurthii

noun
eastern subspecies of chimpanzee
Pronunciation: / pæn troʊˈɡlɒdəˌtiz ˌʃvaɪnˈfʊrθiː / (AmE), / pan trɒˈɡlɒdɪtiːz ˌʃvaɪnˈfʊəθiː / (BrE)
