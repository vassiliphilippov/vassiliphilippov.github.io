Scotch and soda

noun
a drink of Scotch whisky and soda water
4.7
Pronunciation: / skɑtʃ ənd ˈsoʊdə / (AmE), / skɒtʃ ənd ˈsəʊdə / (BrE)
A mixed drink consisting of Scotch whisky and club soda, usually served over ice.

Examples

A Scotch and soda drink
At the party, several guests requested a classic Scotch and soda as their drink of choice.
He ordered a Scotch and soda at the bar, preferring it to other cocktails.
She often relaxes with a Scotch and soda after a long day at work.
