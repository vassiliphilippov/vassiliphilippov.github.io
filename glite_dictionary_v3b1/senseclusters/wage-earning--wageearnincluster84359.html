wage-earning

adjective
earning money through paid work
5.1 3.1
Pronunciation: /weɪdʒ ˈɜːr.nɪŋ/ (AmE), /weɪdʒ ˈɜː.nɪŋ/ (BrE)
earning money through paid work, especially by the hour or in manual or industrial jobs

Examples

Wage-earning workers
His parents are both wage-earning workers in a local factory.
Many wage-earning families struggle to make ends meet each month.
The government introduced new policies to support the wage-earning population.
He grew up in a wage-earning household where both parents worked shifts.
Many wage-earning families find it difficult to save for emergencies.
The company offers benefits to all its wage-earning employees.
