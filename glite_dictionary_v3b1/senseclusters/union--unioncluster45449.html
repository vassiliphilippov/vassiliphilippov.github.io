union

noun
political unit formed by joining
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːniən / (AmE), / ˈjuːniən / (BrE)
a political unit formed by joining together previously independent states, peoples, or organizations

Examples

A political union
After years of negotiation, several small countries formed a lasting union.
The European Union is a prominent example of a modern political union.
The United States was originally a union of thirteen colonies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confederation - an organization consisting of a number of parties or groups united in an alliance or league
  • synonym: federation - an organization or group formed by uniting several groups, states, or organizations
