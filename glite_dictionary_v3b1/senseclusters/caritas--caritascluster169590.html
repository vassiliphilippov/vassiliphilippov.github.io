caritas

proper noun
Catholic charity network
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkærɪtɑs / (AmE), / ˈkærɪtæs / (BrE)
the name of a Catholic charitable organization or network, especially Caritas Internationalis, providing humanitarian aid and social services worldwide

Examples

Caritas organization
The local Caritas office organized a food drive for families affected by the flood.
Volunteers from Caritas provided medical care and shelter to refugees in the region.
Caritas Internationalis coordinates relief efforts in disaster zones around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Red Cross - an international humanitarian organization providing emergency assistance and disaster relief
  • synonym: charity - an organization that helps people in need
