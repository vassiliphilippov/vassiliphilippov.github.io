chimney plant

noun
European bellflower with tall stems
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪmni plænt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪmni plɑːnt / (BrE)
A bellflower native to southeastern Europe, known for its upright, chimney-like flowering stems.
