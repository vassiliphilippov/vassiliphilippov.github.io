chimney plant

noun
European bellflower with tall stems
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪmni plænt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪmni plɑːnt / (BrE)
A bellflower native to southeastern Europe, known for its upright, chimney-like flowering stems.

Examples

The chimney plant flower
Many gardeners appreciate the chimney plant for its tall, slender flowering spikes.
The chimney plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils of southeastern Europe.
We planted a chimney plant in the garden because of its beautiful blue flowers.
