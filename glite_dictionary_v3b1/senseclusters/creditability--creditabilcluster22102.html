creditability

noun
worthiness of praise
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɛdɪtəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌkrɛdɪtəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being worthy of praise, recognition, or credit for an achievement

Examples

Creditability of a report
The manager questioned the creditability of the report before approving the project.
Her creditability increased after she successfully led the team to victory.
Awards are often given to recognize the creditability of outstanding employees.
