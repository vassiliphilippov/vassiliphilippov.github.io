advised

adjective
considered or prudent (of decisions)
3.6
Pronunciation: / ədˈvaɪzd / (AmE), / ədˈvaɪzd / (BrE)
(of an action or decision) made or undertaken after careful consideration or advice; wise or prudent.

Examples

An advised choice
It would have been more advised to consult an expert before proceeding.
Only after an advised review of all options did they choose this route.
The company made an advised decision to postpone the launch until all tests were completed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ill-advised - not showing good judgment; unwise or imprudent
  • synonym: prudent - acting with or showing care and thought for the future
