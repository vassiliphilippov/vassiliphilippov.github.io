pluvial

noun
rainwater
Pronunciation: / ˈpluːviəl / (AmE), / ˈpluːviəl / (BrE)
rainwater; water that is produced by rainfall and collects naturally or is stored for use

Examples

Pluvial collection
The ancient reservoir was designed to store pluvial for use during the dry months.
Farmers rely on pluvial collected from rooftops to irrigate their crops in arid regions.
After the heavy storm, the village had enough pluvial to last through the summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rainwater - water that falls as rain and is collected
