sprag

noun
wedge or bar to stop rolling
9.4
Pronunciation: / spræɡ / (AmE), / spræɡ / (BrE)
a chock or bar wedged under a wheel or inserted between spokes to prevent a vehicle or machinery from rolling or moving

Examples

Wheel sprag
He forgot to remove the sprag before trying to move the wagon.
Rail workers often used a sprag to secure mining trucks on steep tracks.
The mechanic placed a sprag under the wheel to keep the cart from slipping.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chock - a wedge or block placed against a wheel to prevent movement
