sinewy

adjective
tough and muscular; like sinews
7.5 5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪn.juː.i/ /; / /ˈsɪn.juː.ɪ/ (AmE), /ˈsɪn.juː.i/ /; / /ˈsɪn.juː.ɪ/ (BrE)
