sinewy

adjective

Examples

Sinewy arms

Synonyms

  • wiry
    4.8 1.64/m
    wiry is lean, strong, and tough with little fat, while sinewy suggests a lean, muscular body with visible tough tendons.
  • muscular
    2.8 4.77/m
    muscular has well-developed, often larger muscles and clear strength, while sinewy describes a lean, tough look with less fat.
