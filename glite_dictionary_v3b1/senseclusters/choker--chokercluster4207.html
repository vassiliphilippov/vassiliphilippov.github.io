choker

noun
person who fails under pressure
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃoʊkər / (AmE), / ˈtʃəʊkə / (BrE)
a person who fails to perform effectively in a critical situation due to nervousness or pressure, especially in sport or competition

Examples

A choker in sports
After missing the final shot, the fans called him a choker.
Despite his skills, he has a reputation as a choker in big matches.
She tries not to be a choker when the contest gets tough.
