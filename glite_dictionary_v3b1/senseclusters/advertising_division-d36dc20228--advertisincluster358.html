advertising division

noun
department handling advertising in an organization
Pronunciation: / ˈæd.vɚˌtaɪ.zɪŋ dɪˈvɪʒ.ən / (AmE), / ˈæd.və.taɪ.zɪŋ dɪˈvɪʒ.ən / (BrE)
the part or department of a business organization responsible for planning and carrying out advertising activities

Examples

The advertising division
After restructuring, the advertising division was merged with the public relations team.
Budget cuts forced the company to downsize its advertising division last year.
She started her career in the advertising division of a large retail company.
