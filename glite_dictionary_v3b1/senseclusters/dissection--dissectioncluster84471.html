dissection

noun
thorough analytical examination
5.0
Pronunciation: / daɪˈsɛkʃən /; / dɪˈsɛkʃən, daɪˈsɛkʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈsɛkʃən /; / dɪˈsɛkʃən, daɪˈsɛkʃən / (BrE)
a detailed and systematic analysis or examination of something

Examples

Detailed dissection

The article offers a thorough dissection of the company’s financial performance over the last decade.
The professor’s dissection of the novel revealed its underlying themes and symbolism.
