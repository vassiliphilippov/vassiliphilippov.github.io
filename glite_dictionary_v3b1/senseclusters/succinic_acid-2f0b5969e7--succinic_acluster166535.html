succinic acid

noun
a dicarboxylic acid involved in metabolism
9.9
Pronunciation: / səkˈsɪnɪk ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / səkˈsɪnɪk ˈasɪd / (BrE)
A dicarboxylic acid (C4H6O4) found in living organisms, involved in metabolic processes such as the citric acid cycle.

Examples

Succinic acid is a key intermediate in the citric acid cycle, essential for cellular respiration.
