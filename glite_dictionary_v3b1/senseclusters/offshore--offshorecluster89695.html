offshore

adjective
blowing from land to sea (winds)
Pronunciation: / ˈɑfˌʃɔr / (AmE), / ˌɒfˈʃɔː / (BrE)
(of winds) blowing from the land towards the sea.

Examples

Offshore wind
Because the offshore breeze pushed the smoke away from land, the beaches remained clear.
Early morning offshore winds kept the harbor calm for the fishermen.
The surfers waited for offshore winds, which create ideal wave conditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: onshore - (of winds) blowing from the sea towards the land
