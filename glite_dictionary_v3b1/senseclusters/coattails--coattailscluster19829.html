coattails

noun
long back parts of coat
4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈkoʊt.teɪlz/ (AmE), /ˈkəʊt.teɪlz/ (BrE)
the long back parts of a coat or similar garment that hang down at the rear, often below the waist

Examples

Long coattails on a jacket
The butler made sure the gentleman's coattails did not touch the muddy ground.
He wore a tuxedo with long coattails to the formal evening event.
The dancer's coattails swirled dramatically as he spun across the stage.
