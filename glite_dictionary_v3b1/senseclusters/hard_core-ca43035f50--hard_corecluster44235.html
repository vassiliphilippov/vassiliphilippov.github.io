hard core

noun
most committed group members
Pronunciation: / hɑrd kɔr / (AmE), / hɑːd kɔː / (BrE)
the most dedicated and intensely committed members of a group, movement, or organization

Examples

A hard core group
A hard core of activists continued to protest even after most people lost interest.
Only the hard core of the club showed up to every meeting, rain or shine.
The band's hard core fans followed them on every tour across the country.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mainstream - ordinary members of a group, contrasted with the most dedicated nucleus
