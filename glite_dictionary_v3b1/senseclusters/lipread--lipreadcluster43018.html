lipread

verb
interpret speech by observing lips
4.0 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈlɪ.priːd/ (AmE), /ˈlɪ.priːd/ (BrE)
to understand spoken words by interpreting the movements of the speaker's lips, especially when hearing is impaired

Examples

To lipread words
It can be challenging to lipread when the speaker has a beard or mustache.
She learned to lipread after she began to lose her hearing.
The teacher spoke clearly to help the deaf student lipread during class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lipreading - the ability or activity of understanding speech by seeing the lips move
