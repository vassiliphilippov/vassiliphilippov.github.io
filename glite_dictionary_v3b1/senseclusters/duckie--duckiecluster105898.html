duckie

noun
term of endearment
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌki / (AmE), / ˈdʌki / (BrE)
an affectionate term used to refer to someone in a cute or endearing way, or a small duck

Examples

Sweet duckie
She called her little brother duckie when he was being especially cute.
The old man smiled and said, 'How are you today, duckie?' to the child.
At the pond, the children watched a tiny duckie swim after its mother.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dear - a term used to address someone affectionately
  • synonym: sweetie - an affectionate nickname for someone you like
