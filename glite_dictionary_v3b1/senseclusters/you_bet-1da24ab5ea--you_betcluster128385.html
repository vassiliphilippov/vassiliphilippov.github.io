you bet

adverb
expression of strong agreement
3.6
Pronunciation: / ju ˈbɛt / (AmE), / juː ˈbɛt / (BrE)
used to express strong agreement or to affirm that something is certain or assured

Examples

You bet I am
"Are you coming to the party?" "You bet! I wouldn't miss it for the world."
"Will it be finished by tomorrow?" "You bet, I'll have it done before noon."
If you need help moving, you bet I'll be there.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absolutely - used to emphasize complete agreement
  • antonym: no way - expression indicating strong disagreement or refusal
