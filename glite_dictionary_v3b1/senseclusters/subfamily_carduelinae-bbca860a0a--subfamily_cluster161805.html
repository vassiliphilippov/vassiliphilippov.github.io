subfamily Carduelinae

noun
a subgroup of finches (birds)
Pronunciation: / sʌbˌfæməli kɑrˌduːəˈlaɪni / (AmE), / sʌbˌfæməli kɑːdjuːəˈliːniː / (BrE)
A taxonomic subgroup within the finch family, comprising various seed-eating birds such as goldfinches and siskins.
