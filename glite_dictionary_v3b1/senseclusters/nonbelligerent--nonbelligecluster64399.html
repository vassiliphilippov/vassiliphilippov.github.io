nonbelligerent

adjective
not participating in a war
Pronunciation: / nɑn.bəˈlɪdʒ.ɚ.ənt / (AmE), / nɒn.bəˈlɪdʒ.ər.ənt / (BrE)
(of a country or group) not engaged in active warfare; not participating directly in armed conflict.

Examples

Nonbelligerent country
Several nonbelligerent countries attempted to mediate peace during the conflict.
Sweden maintained a nonbelligerent status throughout the war.
The treaty allowed nonbelligerent states to provide humanitarian aid to affected regions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: belligerent - engaged in war or conflict, as a country
