left-hand

adjective
on or toward the left side
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛft hænd / (AmE), / ˈlɛft hænd / (BrE)
Situated on, directed toward, or intended for the left side.

Examples

Left-hand side
He wrote a note in the left-hand margin of the page.
Please turn at the left-hand corner.
The steering wheel is on the left-hand side of the car.

Related Terms

  • antonym: right-hand - situated on or directed toward the right side
