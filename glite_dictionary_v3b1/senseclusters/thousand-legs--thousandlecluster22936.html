thousand-legs

noun
common name for millipede/centipede
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈθaʊzənd ˌlɛɡz / (AmE), / ˈθaʊzənd ˌlɛɡz / (BrE)
a common name for a millipede or centipede—a small, elongated creature with many legs

Examples

A thousand-legs insect
A thousand-legs crawled under the door and disappeared into the shadows.
He moved the rock and spotted a thousand-legs curling up underneath.
My little brother screamed when he saw a thousand-legs in the bathroom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: millipede - elongated arthropod with many legs, of the class Diplopoda
