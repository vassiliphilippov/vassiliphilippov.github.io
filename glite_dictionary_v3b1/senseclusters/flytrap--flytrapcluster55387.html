flytrap

noun
device for catching flies
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪˌtræp / (AmE), / ˈflaɪˌtræp / (BrE)
a device or contraption used for attracting and catching flies.

Examples

Sticky flytrap
After a week, the flytrap was packed with dead flies and needed to be replaced.
She bought a sticky flytrap to reduce insects in the dining room.
The old kitchen was full of flies, so we set up a flytrap near the window.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trap - a device or enclosure designed to catch and hold animals or objects
