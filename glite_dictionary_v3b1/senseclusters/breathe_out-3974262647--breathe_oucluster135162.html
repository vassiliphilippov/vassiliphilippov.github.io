breathe out

verb
expel air from the lungs
Pronunciation: / briːð aʊt / (AmE), / briːð aʊt / (BrE)
to expel air from the lungs; to exhale

Examples

To breathe out slowly
After running, she paused to breathe out slowly and relax.
He took a deep breath, then breathed out in a long sigh.
Remember to breathe out through your mouth during yoga.
