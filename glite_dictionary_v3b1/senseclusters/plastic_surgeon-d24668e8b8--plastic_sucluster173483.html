plastic surgeon

noun
doctor specializing in cosmetic or reconstructive surgery
1.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈplæ.stɪk ˈsɜːr.dʒən/ /; / /ˈplæ.stɪk ˈsɜːr.dʒɪn/ (AmE), /ˈplæ.stɪk ˈsɜː.dʒən/ (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in performing surgery to repair, reconstruct, or enhance parts of the body, especially the face and skin

Examples

Plastic surgeon repairs scars
After her accident, she consulted a plastic surgeon to help repair the scars.
Many celebrities have visited a plastic surgeon for cosmetic enhancements.
The plastic surgeon explained the risks and benefits of the procedure to his patient.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cosmetic surgeon - a doctor who performs surgery to improve appearance
  • synonym: reconstructive surgeon - a doctor who performs surgery to restore form or function after injury or illness
