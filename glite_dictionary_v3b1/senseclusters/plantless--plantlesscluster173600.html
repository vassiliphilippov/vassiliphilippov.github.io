plantless

adjective
without plants
Pronunciation: / ˈplæntləs / (AmE), / ˈplɑːntləs / (BrE)
having no plants or vegetation present

Examples

A plantless desert
The rocky island was completely plantless after the volcanic eruption.
Scientists studied the plantless soil to understand why nothing could grow there.
After the drought, the once green field became almost plantless.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barren - unable to support plant life
  • antonym: vegetated - covered with plants or vegetation
