ladleful

noun
amount in a ladle
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪdəlˌfʊl / (AmE), / ˈleɪdəlˌfʊl / (BrE)
the amount of food or liquid that fills one ladle

Examples

A ladleful of stew
He served each guest a generous ladleful of stew from the pot.
Add one ladleful of batter to the pan for each pancake.

The chef added a ladleful of sauce to each plate before serving.

She poured a ladleful of stew into each bowl for the guests.
He added another ladleful of sauce to the pasta to make it richer.
