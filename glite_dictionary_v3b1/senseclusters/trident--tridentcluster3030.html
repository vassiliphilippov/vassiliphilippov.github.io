trident

noun
a three-pronged spear
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪ.dənt / (AmE), / ˈtraɪ.dənt / (BrE)
a spear with three prongs, often used as a weapon or symbol in mythology and heraldry

Examples

A golden trident
A large golden trident stood atop the ancient temple as a symbol of the sea god.
In Greek mythology, Poseidon is often depicted wielding a powerful trident.
The fisherman used a trident to catch fish along the shore.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spear - a weapon with a long shaft and a pointed tip
