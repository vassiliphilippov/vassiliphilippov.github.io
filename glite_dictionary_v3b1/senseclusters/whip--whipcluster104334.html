whip

verb
defeat thoroughly (competition)
5.3
Pronunciation: / wɪp / (AmE), / wɪp / (BrE)
to defeat someone or something thoroughly, especially in a competition or contest

Examples

To whip a team
She whipped me at chess every time we played.
The defending champion whipped all competition in record time.
The home team whipped their rivals 6–0 in the final.

  • antonym: lose - to be defeated in a contest
