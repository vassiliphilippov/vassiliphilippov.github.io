Johnsonian

adjective
of Lyndon B. Johnson
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɑnsəˈniən / (AmE), / ˌdʒɒnsəˈniən / (BrE)
relating to Lyndon B. Johnson, his presidency, policies, or political style, especially the Great Society programs.

Examples

Johnsonian policies
The senator advocated a Johnsonian approach to expanding social welfare programs.
Many historians analyze the impact of Johnsonian reforms on American society.
Her speech reflected a distinctly Johnsonian optimism about government intervention.
