provision

verb
supply with necessary items
Pronunciation: / prəˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / prəˈvɪʒən / (BrE)
to supply someone or something with the necessary food, equipment, or other essential items

Examples

To provision a team
During the relief operation, volunteers helped provision displaced families with essential supplies.
The team worked hard to provision the remote research station before winter set in.
They must provision the ship with enough food and water for the long voyage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deprive - to prevent someone from having something
  • synonym: supply - to provide someone with something that is needed
