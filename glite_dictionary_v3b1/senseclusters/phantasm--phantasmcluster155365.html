phantasm

noun
illusory perception; apparition
5.3 8.8
Pronunciation: /fæn.ˈtæ.zəm/ (AmE), /fæn.ˈtæ.zəm/ (BrE)
something that appears to exist but is not real, such as an illusion, a figment of the imagination, or a ghostly figure

The legend spoke of a phantasm that haunted the old forest every moonless night.
