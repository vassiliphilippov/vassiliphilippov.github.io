Petasites fragrans

noun
European herb with scented pink-white flowers
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛtəˈsaɪtiːz ˈfreɪɡrənz / (AmE), / ˌpiːtəˈsaɪtiːz ˈfreɪɡrənz / (BrE)
A European perennial herb with broad leaves and vanilla-scented white to pink flowers, commonly known as winter heliotrope.
