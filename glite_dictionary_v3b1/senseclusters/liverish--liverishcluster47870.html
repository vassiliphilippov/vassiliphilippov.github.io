liverish

adjective
irritable or slightly ill, as if from indigestion
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪvərɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈlɪvərɪʃ / (BrE)
feeling slightly ill, irritable, or sickly, especially as if suffering from indigestion or a liver problem
