shrinker

noun
device that reduces size
6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈʃrɪŋ.kər/ (AmE), /ˈʃrɪŋ.kə/ (BrE)
a device or person that makes something smaller in size or amount

Examples

Metal shrinker
The technician used a shrinker to make the metal tube fit the new part.
After washing, she applied a fabric shrinker to restore the garment's original size.
A special shrinker is needed to adjust the plastic before installation.
The factory uses a shrinker to reduce the size of metal sheets.
As a skilled shrinker, she can alter clothes to fit perfectly.
This new shrinker helps minimize packaging waste in the warehouse.
