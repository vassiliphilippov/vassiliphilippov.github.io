insurance underwriter

noun
company selling and evaluating insurance risk
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈʃʊrəns ˈʌndərˌraɪtər / (AmE), / ɪnˈʃʊərəns ˈʌndəˌraɪtə / (BrE)
a financial institution or company that evaluates and accepts insurance risks and sells insurance policies

Examples

Insurance underwriter company
Many large companies work with an insurance underwriter to cover various business risks.
The insurance underwriter reviewed the application before issuing the policy.
You should contact your insurance underwriter to understand the details of your coverage.
