diffusionist

noun
diffusion specialist
8.9
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfjuːʒənɪst / (AmE), / dɪˈfjuːʒənɪst / (BrE)
a scientist or specialist who studies diffusion processes, such as the movement of particles, heat, or gases

Examples

A scientific diffusionist
The diffusionist presented her research on how gases spread through different materials.
As a diffusionist, he often collaborates with engineers to improve heat transfer in devices.
Many diffusionists work in laboratories studying the movement of atoms in metals.
