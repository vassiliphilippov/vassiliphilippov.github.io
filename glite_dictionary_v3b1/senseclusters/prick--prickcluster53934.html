prick

noun
an act of piercing with a sharp point
Pronunciation: / prɪk / (AmE), / prɪk / (BrE)
an act of piercing or puncturing something with a sharp point

Examples

A prick with a pin
A single prick with the pin was enough to pop the balloon.
He felt a sharp prick as the nurse gave him an injection.
The rose's thorn left a small prick on her finger.

Related Terms

  • synonym: puncture - the act of making a small hole with a sharp object
