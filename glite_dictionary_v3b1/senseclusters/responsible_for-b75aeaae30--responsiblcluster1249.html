Examples

Synonyms

  • culpable
    7.5 1.18/m
    culpable means deserving blame for a wrong act, while responsible for can simply mean being in charge or causing something.
  • at fault
    3.4 3.02/m
    at fault says someone caused a problem and deserves blame, while responsible for can also mean being in charge without blame.
  • liable
    5.5 9.36/m
    liable means legally answerable or may face a penalty, while responsible for is broader and may be non-legal control or cause.
