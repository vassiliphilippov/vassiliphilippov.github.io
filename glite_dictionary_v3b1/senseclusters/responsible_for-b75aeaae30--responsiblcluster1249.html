responsible for

adjective
being the agent or cause of something
1.0 2.3
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈspɑːn.sə.bəl fɔːr/ /; / /rɪ.ˈspɑːn.sə.bəl fər/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈspɒn.sə.bəl fɔː/ /; / /rɪ.ˈspɒn.sə.bəl fə/ (BrE)
having caused something to happen or having control over someone or something and being in charge of it; being the agent or cause of something.
