Synonyms

    8.4 0.09/m
    protuberant is a formal word for something sharply sticking out from a surface, while bulgy is a more common way to say it.
  • bulbous
    5.5 1/m
    bulbous describes a rounded, swollen shape like a plant bulb, while bulgy means any rounded part that sticks out.
  • bellied
    5.6 0.5/m

  • puffy
    2.2 3/m
    puffy looks soft, swollen, or filled with air, while bulgy suggests a firm rounded part that sticks out.
