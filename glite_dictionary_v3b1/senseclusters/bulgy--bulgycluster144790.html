bulgy

adjective
curving or swelling outward
4.3 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆbÊŒldÊ’i / (AmE), / ËˆbÊŒldÊ’i / (BrE)
curving or swelling outward; protruding in a rounded way

Examples

A bulgy mark
He noticed his new glasses left a slightly bulgy mark on his nose.
The frog's bulgy eyes gave it a comical appearance.
The suitcase was so full that its sides became bulgy and hard to close.

Synonyms

  • protuberant
    8.4 0.10/m
    protuberant is a formal word for something sharply sticking out from a surface, while bulgy is a more common way to say it.
  • bulbous
    5.5 1.21/m
    bulbous describes a rounded, swollen shape like a plant bulb, while bulgy means any rounded part that sticks out.
  • bellied
    5.6 0.53/m
    bellied often describes something with a belly-like curve, while bulgy is a general term for rounded protrusion.
  • puffy
    2.2 2.65/m
    puffy looks soft, swollen, or filled with air, while bulgy suggests a firm rounded part that sticks out.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bulging - swelling or sticking out in a rounded shape
  • antonym: sunken - curved inward or hollowed, the opposite of protruding
