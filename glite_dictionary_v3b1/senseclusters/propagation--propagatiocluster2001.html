Synonyms

  • dissemination
    6.6 3.51/m
    dissemination is the wide, active sharing of information to reach many people, while propagation focuses on ideas becoming adopted in new places.
  • diffusion
    5.8 2.30/m
    diffusion is the gradual spreading or dispersing of ideas or things across areas, while propagation implies active adoption by new people.
  • spreading
    2.3 0.02/m
    spreading is a general act of extending something over a larger area or group, while propagation stresses ideas being adopted.
  • circulation
    4.0 3.17/m
    circulation is movement or transmission within a system, like news or money, while propagation means ideas spread and are taken up in new areas.
