sweatiness

noun
state of sweating
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈswɛtinəs / (AmE), / ˈswɛtɪnɪs /; / ˈswɛtinɪs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being sweaty or producing sweat, often because of heat, activity, or sweating easily

After running for an hour, his sweatiness was obvious to everyone around him.
The sweatiness of the summer night kept me awake until morning.

The sweatiness of her palms was obvious during the job interview.
Some people are embarrassed by their sweatiness when exercising in public.
