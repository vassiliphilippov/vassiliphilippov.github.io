Rumohra adiantiformis

noun
a widely distributed tropical fern
9.9
Pronunciation: / ruːˈmoʊrə ˌædiˌæntɪˈfɔːrmɪs / (AmE), / ruːˈmɒrə ˌædiˌæntɪˈfɔːmɪs / (BrE)
