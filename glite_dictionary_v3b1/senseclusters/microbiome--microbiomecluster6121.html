microbiome

noun
community of microorganisms
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˈbaɪoʊm / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˈbaɪəʊm / (BrE)
the community of microorganisms (such as bacteria and fungi) and their genetic material living in a particular environment, especially in the body
