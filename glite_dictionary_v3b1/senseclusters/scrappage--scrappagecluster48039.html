scrappage

noun
vehicle disposal incentive
Pronunciation: / ˈskræpɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈskræpɪdʒ / (BrE)
a payment or incentive given to encourage the disposal of old vehicles or equipment, usually for recycling or replacement.

Examples

Scrappage payment
The government introduced a scrappage scheme to reduce pollution from old cars.
Many people took advantage of the scrappage offer when buying a new vehicle.
The scrappage program helped boost sales in the automotive industry.
