nontribal

adjective
not related to tribes
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈtraɪbəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈtraɪbəl / (BrE)
not related to, belonging to, or characteristic of a tribe or tribal group

Examples

Nontribal members
The organization welcomes both nontribal and tribal members to participate in its programs.
A nontribal perspective can help resolve conflicts that are rooted in group divisions.
The new law applies equally to nontribal citizens and those belonging to recognized tribes.
The government extended healthcare services to nontribal populations in remote areas.
Some traditions are unique to tribal groups, while others are common in nontribal societies.
The new policy aims to reduce disparities between tribal and nontribal communities.
