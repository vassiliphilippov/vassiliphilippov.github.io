Synonyms

    2.6 6/m
    an intersection is where roads or paths meet and cross, while a crossing can also mean a shallow place in a river.
  • crossroad
    2.0 4/m
    a crossroad is where roads meet and form a route, while a crossing can also mean a path or shallow river point.
  • junction
    3.0 3/m
    a junction is a point where roads or lines connect, while a crossing often names any place where two things physically cross.
  • crossway
    3.5 0.1/m
    a crossway is an older or less common term for where paths meet, while a crossing also refers to river shallows for walking.
