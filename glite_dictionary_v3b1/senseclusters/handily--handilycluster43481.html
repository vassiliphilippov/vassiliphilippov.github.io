handily

adverb
easily; without difficulty
Pronunciation: / ˈhændəli /; / ˈhændɪli / (AmE), / ˈhændɪli / (BrE)
easily or in a convenient way; without difficulty

Examples

To handily win
He handily finished the marathon well ahead of the other runners.
She completed the puzzle handily, impressing everyone with her skill.
The team won the match handily despite missing several key players.
She handily finished her assignment before the deadline.
The stores are handily located just across the street from the hotel.
The tools were handily arranged on the workbench for easy access.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conveniently - in a way that is easy or suitable
  • synonym: easily - without difficulty or effort
  • synonym: effortlessly - in a manner requiring no exertion
