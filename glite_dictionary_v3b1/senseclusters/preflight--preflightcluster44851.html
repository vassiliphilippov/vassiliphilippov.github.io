preflight

adjective
before takeoff
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːˌflaɪt / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌflaɪt / (BrE)
relating to checks or preparations made before an aircraft takes off

Examples

Preflight checklist
The pilot conducted a thorough preflight inspection of the aircraft.
Every preflight procedure must be completed to ensure passenger safety.
She reviewed the preflight checklist before starting the engines.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postflight - relating to checks or activities after an aircraft has landed
  • synonym: preparation - the act of getting ready for something
