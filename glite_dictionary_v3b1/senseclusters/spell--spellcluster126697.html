spell

verb
indicate or mean (often something negative)
Pronunciation: / spɛl / (AmE), / spɛl / (BrE)
(of an event or fact) to mean or result in (usually something negative or significant).

Examples

Spell disaster
For many farmers, a drought spells ruin for the entire season.
Poor planning often spells disaster in large projects.
The sudden drop in sales could spell financial trouble for the company.
