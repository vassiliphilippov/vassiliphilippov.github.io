dickybird

noun
small bird (children's term)
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪkiˌbɝd / (AmE), / ˈdɪkibɜːd / (BrE)
A small bird, especially as referred to when speaking to children.

Examples

A dickybird singing
Grandma told us to be quiet and listen to the dickybirds singing in the garden.
He drew a bright red dickybird in his coloring book.
The children pointed excitedly at the dickybird hopping on the lawn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bird - an animal with feathers and wings
