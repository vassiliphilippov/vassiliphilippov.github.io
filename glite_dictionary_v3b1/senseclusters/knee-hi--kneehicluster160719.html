knee-hi

noun
sock or stocking reaching below the knee
Pronunciation: / ˈniːˌhaɪ / (AmE), / ˈniːˌhaɪ / (BrE)
A sock or stocking that comes up to just below the knee.

Examples

A knee-hi sock
My grandmother prefers knee-hi stockings in the winter to keep her legs warm.
She wore a pair of knee-hi socks with her school uniform.
These boots look great with knee-hi socks peeking out at the top.
