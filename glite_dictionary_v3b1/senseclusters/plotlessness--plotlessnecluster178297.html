plotlessness

noun
lack of land plots
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈplɑtləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈplɒtləsnəs / (BrE)
the state of having no plots of land or allotments available for building or cultivation

Examples

Urban plotlessness
The rapid urban expansion led to plotlessness in many neighborhoods, making private gardens rare.
Residents complained about the plotlessness that followed the city's redevelopment plan.
After the farmland was sold, the village experienced a period of plotlessness and loss of community gardens.

Related Terms

  • antonym: allotment - a small plot of land rented for cultivation
  • synonym: landlessness - the condition of having no land
