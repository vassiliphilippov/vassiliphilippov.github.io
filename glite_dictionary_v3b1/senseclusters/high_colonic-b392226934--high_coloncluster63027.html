high colonic

noun
large-volume colon enema for cleansing
9.3
Pronunciation: / haɪ kəˈlɑnɪk / (AmE), / haɪ kəˈlɒnɪk / (BrE)
an enema procedure in which a large amount of fluid is injected high into the colon to cleanse it

Examples

A high colonic procedure
Her doctor warned against getting a high colonic without medical supervision.
Some people regularly receive a high colonic as part of a detoxification regimen.
The clinic offers high colonics to improve digestive health and overall well-being.

Related Terms
