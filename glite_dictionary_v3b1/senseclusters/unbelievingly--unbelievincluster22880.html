unbelievingly

adverb
in a manner showing disbelief
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnbɪˈlivɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌʌnbɪˈliːvɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner showing disbelief; without accepting something as true or real

Examples

Shake head unbelievingly
He shook his head unbelievingly when he heard the announcement.
She stared unbelievingly at the test results, unable to accept the truth.
They listened unbelievingly as the story unfolded, finding it hard to trust his words.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incredulously - in a manner unwilling or unable to believe something
