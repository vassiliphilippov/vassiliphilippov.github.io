two-humped

adjective
having two humps (on the back)
7.2
Pronunciation: / tuː ˈhʌmpt / (AmE), / tuː ˈhʌmpt / (BrE)
(of an animal) having two humps on its back.

Examples

A two-humped camel
The Bactrian camel is a two-humped species found in Central Asia.
The museum displayed a life-size model of a two-humped camel.
Unlike their one-humped relatives, two-humped camels are well adapted to colder climates.

Related Terms

  • antonym: single-humped - having one hump on the back (especially of camels)
