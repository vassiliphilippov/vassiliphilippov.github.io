restrictor

noun
limiting factor
Pronunciation: / rɪˈstrɪktər / (AmE), / rɪˈstrɪktə / (BrE)
something, such as a law, rule, device, or person, that limits or controls an activity, access, or freedom

Examples

Water restrictor
The government installed a restrictor on water usage during the drought.
A speed restrictor was fitted to the vehicle to ensure it could not exceed the legal limit.
The new policy served as a restrictor on access to sensitive information.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barrier - something that blocks or restricts movement or progress
  • antonym: enabler - something that makes an action or process possible
  • synonym: limiter - something that sets a limit or restricts
