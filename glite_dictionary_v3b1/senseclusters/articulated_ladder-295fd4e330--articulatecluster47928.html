articulated ladder

noun
ladder with locking jointed segments
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrtɪˈkjuːleɪtɪd ˈlædər / (AmE), / ˌɑːtɪˈkjuːleɪtɪd ˈladə / (BrE)
a ladder made of multiple segments connected by joints that can be locked into different positions

Examples

An articulated ladder segment
An articulated ladder can be adjusted into different shapes to suit various tasks.
He keeps an articulated ladder in his van for odd jobs around the house.
The firefighter quickly unfolded the articulated ladder to reach the second-story window.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extension ladder - a ladder that can be extended to reach higher places
