Nei Monggol

proper noun
autonomous region in northeastern China
Pronunciation: / neɪ mʊŋˈgoʊl / (AmE), / neɪ mɒŋˈɡɒl / (BrE)
an autonomous region in northeastern China, also known as Inner Mongolia, forming part of the People's Republic of China since 1911.

Examples

Nei Monggol region
In 1911, Nei Monggol became an integral part of China.
Many ethnic Mongols continue to live in Nei Monggol today.
The grasslands of Nei Monggol are famous for their beauty and vastness.
