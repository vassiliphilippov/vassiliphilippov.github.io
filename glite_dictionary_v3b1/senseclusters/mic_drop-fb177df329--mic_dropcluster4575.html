mic drop

noun
dramatic end to a statement or performance
4.2 6.4
Pronunciation: / maɪk drɑp / (AmE), / maɪk drɒp / (BrE)
an impressive or dramatic conclusion to a speech, performance, or statement, meant to signal that nothing more needs to be said or added

Examples

A mic drop moment
Her brilliant retort at the end of the debate was a real mic drop.
The comedian finished his set with a perfect mic drop moment that left the audience cheering.
When the CEO announced the company's record profits, it was a clear mic drop for the competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grand finale - an impressive final act or event
