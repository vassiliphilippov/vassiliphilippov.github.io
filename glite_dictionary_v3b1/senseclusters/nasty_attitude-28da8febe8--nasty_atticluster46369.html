nasty attitude

noun
very unpleasant or rude behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈnæsti æˈtɪtjud / (AmE), / ˈnɑːsti əˈtɪtjuːd / (BrE)
An extremely unpleasant, rude, or hostile manner or behavior; a way of acting marked by meanness or spite.

Examples

A nasty attitude problem
His nasty attitude was obvious to every customer he spoke with.
It's hard to get along with him when he has such a nasty attitude all the time.
She was fired because her nasty attitude made the workplace toxic for everyone.

  • antonym: pleasant attitude - friendly or positive manner or behavior
