anti-theft

noun
theft prevention device
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈθɛft / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈθɛft / (BrE)
a device, system, or measure designed to prevent theft or unauthorized removal of property

Examples

A car anti-theft
The new car comes equipped with an anti-theft to protect it from being stolen.
Many people install an anti-theft on their bicycles for extra security.
The store uses an anti-theft system to prevent shoplifting.
