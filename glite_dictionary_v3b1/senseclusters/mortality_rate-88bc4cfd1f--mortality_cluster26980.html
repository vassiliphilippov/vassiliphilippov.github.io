mortality rate

noun
rate of deaths per population
Pronunciation: / mɔrˈtæləti reɪt / (AmE), / mɔːˈtælɪti reɪt / (BrE)
the number of deaths in a particular population, scaled to the size of that population, typically expressed per 1,000 people per year

Examples

Infant mortality rate
Improvements in healthcare have led to a lower infant mortality rate worldwide.
Public health officials are working to reduce the mortality rate from infectious diseases.
The country's mortality rate has declined significantly over the past decade.

Related Terms

  • antonym: birth rate - the number of live births per 1,000 people per year in a population
  • synonym: death rate - the frequency of deaths in a particular population over a set period, synonym for mortality rate
