Synonyms

  • mortality
    5.9 4/m
    Reports the number of deaths adjusted for population size per thousand annually
    vs mortality rate: The number of deaths in a given population, scaled to the size of that population, typically expressed per thousand individuals per year.
  • death rate
    2.7 1/m
    Counts deaths occurring during a specific time period, shown per thousand annually
    vs mortality rate: the number of deaths in a population during a specific period, usually shown as deaths per 1,000 people per year
  • fatality rate
    4.9 0.2/m
    Measures the proportion of deaths within a population, expressed per thousand annually
    vs mortality rate: the proportion of deaths occurring in a specific population, typically expressed per 1,000 individuals per year
