aryl

adjective
containing aromatic ring
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrɪl / (AmE), / ˈærɪl / (BrE)
relating to or containing an aryl group, which is an aromatic ring used as a substituent in chemistry

Examples

Aryl compound
The reaction produced an aryl compound with a benzene ring attached to the main structure.
Scientists often study aryl groups because of their stability and unique chemical properties.
Adding an aryl substituent can change the reactivity of a molecule in organic synthesis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alkyl - relating to or containing an alkyl group, a type of hydrocarbon substituent
