capoeira

noun
Brazilian martial art
Pronunciation: / ˌkæp.wəˈeɪ.rə / (AmE), / ˌkap.uˈeɪ.rə / (BrE)
a Brazilian martial art that combines elements of dance, acrobatics, and music

Capoeira dance moves

The performance featured skilled capoeira artists moving to traditional Brazilian music.
Learning capoeira helped her connect with Brazilian culture and history.
