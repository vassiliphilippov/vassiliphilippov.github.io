black day

noun
day of misfortune or disaster
Pronunciation: / blæk deɪ / (AmE), / blæk deɪ / (BrE)
A day marked by significant misfortune, disaster, or sorrow.

Examples

A tragic black day
History will remember the signing of the treaty as a black day for the country.
The stock market crash was truly a black day for investors worldwide.
When the company filed for bankruptcy, it was a black day for its employees.
