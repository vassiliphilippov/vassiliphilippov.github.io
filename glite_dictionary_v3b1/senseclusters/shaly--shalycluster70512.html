shaly

adjective
like shale
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪli / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪli / (BrE)
containing, resembling, or relating to shale, a fine-grained sedimentary rock

Examples

Shaly soil
The geologist described the soil as shaly due to its layered texture.
The mountain's shaly slopes made climbing difficult after the rain.
They found fossils embedded in the shaly rock near the riverbank.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shale - a fine-grained sedimentary rock formed from mud
