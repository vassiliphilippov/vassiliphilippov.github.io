vee

verb
to form a V shape
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / viÀê / (AmE), / viÀê / (BrE)
to move into or arrange in a V-shaped formation, or to cause parts to diverge so they form a V shape

Examples

Birds vee in flight
The geese vee across the sky as they migrate south for the winter.
During the parade, the jets vee perfectly before breaking formation.
The dancers vee out from the center of the stage in the final act.

Related Terms

  • antonym: converge - to come together from different directions
  • synonym: fan out - to spread out from a central point
