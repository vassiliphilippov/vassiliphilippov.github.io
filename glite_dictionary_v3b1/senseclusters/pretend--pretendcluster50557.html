pretend

verb
put forward a claim or guess
2.3 3.2
Pronunciation: /prɪ.ˈtend/ /; / /prə.ˈtend/ (AmE), /prɪ.ˈtend/ /; / /prə.ˈtend/ (BrE)
to claim or assert something, such as a right, title, or opinion, often without certainty

Examples

To pretend a right
