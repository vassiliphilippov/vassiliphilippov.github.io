water ox

noun
Asian domesticated buffalo
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚ ɑks / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ɒks / (BrE)
an Asian buffalo, typically domesticated and used as a draft animal.

Examples

A water ox animal
A water ox can pull heavy loads through muddy terrain with ease.
Researchers studied how the water ox adapted to life in wet, tropical climates.
The villagers rely on the water ox to plow their rice fields each season.
