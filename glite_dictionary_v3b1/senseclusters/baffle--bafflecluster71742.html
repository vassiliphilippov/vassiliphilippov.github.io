baffle

noun
device for directing flow of fluid or energy
9.9 8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbæfəl / (AmE), / ˈbæfəl / (BrE)
a flat plate or device used to control or direct the flow of fluid, gas, or energy in a system

Examples

A baffle plate
Oil coolers often use baffles to guide liquid through the heat exchanger.
The engineer installed a baffle inside the tank to improve the mixing of fluids.
To reduce noise, a baffle was added to the ventilation system.
