craftily

adverb
in a cunning or sly manner
Pronunciation: / ˈkræftɪli / (AmE), / ˈkrɑːftɪli / (BrE)
in a clever and deceitful way; using cunning to achieve a goal

Examples

To craftily hide evidence
He craftily hid the evidence, making it hard for anyone to find.
She craftily avoided answering the difficult question by changing the subject.
The fox craftily crept into the chicken coop at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cunningly - in a clever but deceitful way
  • antonym: honestly - in a truthful and sincere way
