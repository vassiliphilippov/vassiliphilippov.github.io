lasting peace

noun
enduring state of peace
3.0 3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlæstɪŋ pis / (AmE), / ˈlɑːstɪŋ piːs / (BrE)
A state of tranquility or harmony that continues for a long time without being interrupted by conflict.

Examples

A lasting peace
Many leaders strive for a lasting peace in regions long affected by conflict.
Only through cooperation can we hope to achieve a truly lasting peace.
The treaty was intended to bring about a lasting peace between the two nations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harmony - a state of peaceful coexistence
  • antonym: temporary peace - a peace that does not last long
  • degree lower: truce - an agreement to stop fighting for a short time
