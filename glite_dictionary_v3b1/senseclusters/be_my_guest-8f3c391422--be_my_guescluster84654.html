be my guest

verb
polite invitation to do or use something
1.0
Pronunciation: / bi maɪ ɡɛst / (AmE), / bi maɪ ɡɛst / (BrE)
used to politely give someone permission to do something or to invite them to use something freely

Examples

Be my guest to eat
"May I have another slice of cake?" — "Be my guest."
Anyone who wants to join the discussion should be my guest and speak up.
If you’d like to try the new software, be my guest.
