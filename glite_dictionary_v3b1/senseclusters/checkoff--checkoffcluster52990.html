checkoff

noun
automatic payroll deduction
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛkˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛkˌɒf / (BrE)
a system in which money is automatically deducted from an employee's pay for a specific purpose, such as union dues

Examples

Checkoff system
The company uses a checkoff system to collect union dues from employees' paychecks.
Each month, a small amount is taken out through checkoff for the retirement fund.
Participation in the charity drive was made easy with an automatic checkoff from salaries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deduction - an amount subtracted from a total, especially from pay
  • synonym: withholding - the act of deducting money from an employee's wages
