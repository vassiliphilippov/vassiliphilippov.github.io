florid

adjective
having a reddish or rosy complexion
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔr.ɪd / (AmE), / ˈflɒr.ɪd / (BrE)
having a reddish or rosy complexion; inclined to a healthy, reddish color often associated with outdoor life

Examples

Florid cheeks
He had a florid complexion that seemed to radiate good health.
Her cheeks grew florid after a day in the cold wind.
The old sailor's florid face suggested a lifetime spent outdoors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pale - lacking color; having little or no color in the face
  • synonym: ruddy - having a healthy, red complexion
