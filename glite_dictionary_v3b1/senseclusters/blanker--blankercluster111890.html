blanker

noun
device that blocks
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈblæŋkər / (AmE), / ˈblæŋkə / (BrE)
a device or person that covers, blocks, or blanks something out, especially to prevent interference or access

Examples

Signal blanker
The technician installed a blanker to prevent signal interference.
A blanker was used to cover the opening during repairs.
He acted as a blanker to block the view from the window.
