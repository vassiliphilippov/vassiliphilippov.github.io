donkey engine

noun
small locomotive for switching rail cars
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑŋki ˌɛndʒɪn / (AmE), / ˈdɒŋki ˌɛndʒɪn / (BrE)
a small locomotive used for switching rolling stock in a railroad yard

Examples

Rail yard donkey engine
A donkey engine is essential for efficient operations in busy rail yards.
He spent his career operating a donkey engine in the city's main switching yard.
The old donkey engine chugged back and forth, rearranging the freight cars.
