downshifting

noun
simpler lifestyle choice
4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdaʊn.ʃɪf.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈdaʊn.ʃɪf.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
the practice of adopting a simpler, less stressful lifestyle, often by reducing work hours or material possessions

Examples

Lifestyle downshifting
After years in a high-pressure job, she considered downshifting to focus on her family and health.
Many people are attracted to downshifting as a way to reduce stress and find more meaning in life.
The trend of downshifting has grown as people seek balance between work and personal happiness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: minimalism - a lifestyle focused on simplicity and owning fewer possessions
  • synonym: simplification - the process of making something less complicated
