nick

noun
small notch or cut in a surface
6.9 3.0
Pronunciation: /nɪk/ (AmE), /nɪk/ (BrE)
a small cut or notch in a surface or material

Examples

A small nick mark
Be careful not to make a nick in the table while moving it.

The razor left a small nick on his chin.
