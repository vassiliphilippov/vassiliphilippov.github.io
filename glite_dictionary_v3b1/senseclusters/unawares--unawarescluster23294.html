unawares

adverb
suddenly and unexpectedly
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈwɛrz / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈwɛəz / (BrE)
suddenly and by surprise; without expecting or being aware of it

Examples

Caught unawares
The announcement arrived unawares, leaving everyone in the office surprised.
The heavy snowstorm took the hikers unawares as they crossed the mountain pass.
We were all caught unawares by the abrupt change in policy.
He was taken unawares by the surprise birthday party his friends organized.
She stumbled unawares into the middle of the heated debate.
The storm caught the hikers unawares while they were setting up camp.

  • synonym: unexpectedly - in a way not anticipated or predicted
