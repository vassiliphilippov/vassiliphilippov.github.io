kludge

verb
to make a quick fix
9.3 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / kluÀêd í / (AmE), / kluÀêd í / (BrE)
to put together a solution quickly in a clumsy or inelegant way, often as a temporary fix

Examples

To kludge a repair
We kludged a solution to keep the system running until the real fix arrived.
The team kludged together a working prototype just in time for the presentation.
He kludged the wiring so the lights would turn on, but it was far from perfect.
