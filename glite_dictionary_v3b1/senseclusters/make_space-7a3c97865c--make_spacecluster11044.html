make space

verb
create room for something
Pronunciation: / meɪk speɪs / (AmE), / meɪk speɪs / (BrE)
to create room or an area for something by removing obstacles or rearranging things

Examples

To make space at table
Can you make space at the table for another guest?
She moved the books to make space for the vase on the shelf.
We need to make space in the garage for the new car.
