zax

noun
roofing tool for slate
Pronunciation: / zæks / (AmE), / zæks / (BrE)
a tool with a blade and a hammer used by roofers for cutting and fitting slate tiles

Examples

A roofer's zax
The experienced roofer always carried his zax when working with slate tiles.
She learned how to use a zax to cut and shape the roofing slate.
A zax is essential for anyone repairing traditional slate roofs.
