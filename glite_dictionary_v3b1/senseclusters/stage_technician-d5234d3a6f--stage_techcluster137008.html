stage technician

noun
person handling theatre technical work
1.0 3.7
Pronunciation: /steɪdʒ tek.ˈnɪʃ.ən/ (AmE), /steɪdʒ tek.ˈnɪʃ.ən/ (BrE)
a person responsible for the technical operations required for a theatrical production, such as lighting, sound, set, and stage equipment

Examples

Stage technician backstage
A stage technician is essential backstage to ensure everything runs smoothly during the performance.
After the show, the stage technician quickly disassembled the lights and sound equipment.
He started his career as a stage technician before moving into theater management.
