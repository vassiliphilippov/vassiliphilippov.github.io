piloncillo

noun
unrefined cane sugar
Pronunciation: / piˌloʊnˈsiːjoʊ /; / ˌpiːloʊnˈsiːjoʊ / (AmE), / piˌlɒnˈsiːjoʊ /; / ˌpiːlɒnˈsiːjəʊ / (BrE)
a cone-shaped block of unrefined cane sugar used as a sweetener, especially in Mexican cooking
