marking ink

noun
ink for permanently marking items
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrkɪŋ ˌɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪŋ ˌɪŋk / (BrE)
an indelible ink used for marking clothes, linens, or other items to ensure the marks do not wash out

Examples

Marking ink for clothes
Make sure you use marking ink so the label doesn't wash off your clothes.
She bought a bottle of marking ink to label all her towels before moving into the dormitory.
The laundry staff used marking ink to write names on each student's uniform.
