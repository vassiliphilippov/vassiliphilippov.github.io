unemployed people

noun
people without jobs, as a group
Pronunciation: / ʌnɪmˌplɔɪd ˈpipəl / (AmE), / ʌnɪmˌplɔɪd ˈpiːpəl / (BrE)
people who are without a job and actively seeking work, considered collectively as a group.

Examples

Unemployed people group
Social services often focus on providing resources for unemployed people in the community.
The government announced new programs to support unemployed people during the economic downturn.
Training initiatives aim to reintegrate unemployed people into the workforce.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jobless - people or a person who does not have a job
