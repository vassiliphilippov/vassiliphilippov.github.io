meg

noun
megabyte (informal)
6.8
Pronunciation: / mɛɡ / (AmE), / mɛɡ / (BrE)
an informal term for a megabyte, a unit of digital information equal to 1,048,576 bytes

Examples

A 5 meg file
The file is only three megs, so it will download quickly.
He asked if I had a spare meg of storage on my USB drive.
Most songs are just a few megs each, so you can store hundreds on your phone.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: gigabyte - a unit of digital information equal to 1,073,741,824 bytes
  • degree lower: kilobyte - a unit of digital information equal to 1,024 bytes
  • synonym: megabyte - a unit of digital information equal to 1,048,576 bytes
