virtual tour

noun
online simulation of a real place
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrtʃuəl tʊr / (AmE), / ˈvɜːtjʊəl tʊə / (BrE)
an online or digital simulation of a physical place, allowing viewers to explore it remotely

Examples

A virtual tour online
Students took a virtual tour of the solar system as part of their science lesson.
Thanks to a detailed virtual tour, house hunters can visit properties without leaving their couches.
The museum offers a virtual tour that lets visitors explore its galleries from home.
