spinner

noun
person who spins fibers into threads
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪnɚ / (AmE), / ˈspɪnə / (BrE)
a person whose occupation is to spin fibers into threads or yarn, especially using a spinning wheel or similar device

Examples

A skilled spinner
In historical novels, a spinner is often depicted as sitting by the fire, twisting flax into thread.
The spinner worked diligently at her wheel, turning wool into fine yarn.
With the invention of factories, the traditional spinner gradually became less common.

Related Terms

  • synonym: weaver - a person who weaves fabric from threads
