nasofrontal

noun
nasal-frontal suture
Pronunciation: / ˌneɪzoʊˈfrʌntəl / (AmE), / ˌneɪzəʊˈfrʌntəl / (BrE)
the fibrous joint (suture) connecting the nasal bones and the frontal bone of the skull

Examples

Nasofrontal suture injury
The surgeon carefully examined the nasofrontal area for signs of injury.
A fracture at the nasofrontal suture can affect both the nose and forehead.
X-rays revealed a displacement at the nasofrontal junction after the accident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: suture - a joint between bones in the skull
