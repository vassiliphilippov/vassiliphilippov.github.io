owner-driver

noun
person who owns and drives their vehicle
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊnər ˈdraɪvər / (AmE), / ˈəʊnə ˈdraɪvə / (BrE)
a person who owns the vehicle they personally drive, especially in the context of cars or commercial vehicles

Examples

An owner-driver vehicle
As an owner-driver, he takes great pride in maintaining his car.
Many taxi companies are run by owner-drivers who manage their own vehicles.
The insurance policy is designed specifically for owner-drivers of commercial trucks.
