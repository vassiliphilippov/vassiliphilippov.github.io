encompassing

adjective
including many things; broad in scope
3.6 4.8
Pronunciation: /en.kˈʌm.pəs.ɪŋ/ /; / /ɪn.kˈʌm.pəs.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ɪn.kˈʌm.pəs.ɪŋ/ /; / /en.kˈʌm.pəs.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
