candlelight

noun
light from a candle
Pronunciation: / ˈkæn.dl̩ˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈkæn.dl̩.laɪt / (BrE)
the soft light produced by a burning candle

Examples

Soft candlelight
Old portraits in the gallery appeared mysterious in the dim candlelight.
The room was aglow with candlelight, casting soft shadows on the walls.
They enjoyed a romantic dinner by candlelight during the power outage.
