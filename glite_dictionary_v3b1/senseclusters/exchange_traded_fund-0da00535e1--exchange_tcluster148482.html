exchange traded fund

noun
investment fund traded on an exchange
Pronunciation: / ɪksˈʧeɪndʒ ˈtreɪdɪd fʌnd / (AmE), / ɪksˈʧeɪndʒ ˈtreɪdɪd fʌnd / (BrE)
an investment fund that is traded on a stock exchange, typically designed to track the performance of a specific market index

Examples

An exchange traded fund index
He invested in an exchange traded fund to diversify his portfolio.
Many investors prefer exchange traded funds for their low fees and flexibility.
The S&P 500 exchange traded fund is one of the most popular investment vehicles in the market.
