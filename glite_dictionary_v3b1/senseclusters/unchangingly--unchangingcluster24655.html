unchangingly

adverb
consistently, without change
4.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtʃeɪndʒɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃeɪndʒɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that does not change; consistently or steadily without variation

Examples

Remain unchangingly strict
The rules have remained unchangingly strict for decades despite many requests for reform.
He looked at her unchangingly, never letting his expression reveal his true feelings.
The mountain stood unchangingly against the sky, unaffected by the passing seasons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consistently - in a way that stays the same over time
  • synonym: steadily - in a regular and even way
  • antonym: variably - in a way that changes or varies
