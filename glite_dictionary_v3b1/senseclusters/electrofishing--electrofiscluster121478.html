electrofishing

verb
to catch fish with electricity
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˈfɪʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈfɪʃɪŋ / (BrE)
to catch or sample fish by applying an electric current to water, temporarily stunning the fish for collection or study

Examples

To electrofishing a stream
Researchers used electrofishing to study the fish population in the river without harming the animals.
The team will be electrofishing the stream next week to collect data for their environmental survey.
Before electrofishing, they made sure all safety equipment was in place to protect both people and fish.

  • synonym: netting - to catch fish using nets
