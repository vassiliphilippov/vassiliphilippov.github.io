this is deep

x
remark on profundity; often ironic
Pronunciation: / ðɪs ɪz dip / (AmE), / ðɪs ɪz diːp / (BrE)
used to remark that something is or appears to be very profound or insightful, sometimes with irony or humor

Examples

This is deep thought
After watching the movie, he turned to his friend and said, 'this is deep.'
She read the poem and whispered, 'this is deep,' moved by its hidden meaning.
Sometimes people post memes with the caption 'this is deep' to poke fun at over-the-top statements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insightful - showing clear understanding and perception
  • synonym: profound - showing great knowledge or insight
