flattener

noun
tool for flattening
Pronunciation: / ˈflætənər / (AmE), / ˈflætənə / (BrE)
a tool or machine used to make a surface flat or even

Examples

Metal flattener machine
The metalworker used a flattener to smooth the sheet of steel.
A flattener is essential in the process of making uniform metal plates.
After running the dough through the flattener, it was ready to be cut into shapes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: press - a machine that applies pressure to shape or flatten materials
  • synonym: roller - a tool or machine with a heavy cylinder used to flatten surfaces
