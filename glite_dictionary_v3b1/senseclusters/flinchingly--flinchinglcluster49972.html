flinchingly

adverb
with hesitation or fear
Pronunciation: / ˈflɪntʃɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈflɪntʃɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows hesitation, fear, or shrinking away from pain or discomfort

Examples

To flinchingly touch
She reached out flinchingly to touch the cold, wet surface of the stone.
The child answered the question flinchingly, afraid of making a mistake in front of the class.
He stepped flinchingly into the dark room, unsure of what he might find.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hesitantly - in a way that shows uncertainty or reluctance
