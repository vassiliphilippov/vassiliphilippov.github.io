vervain family

noun
the plant family Verbenaceae
Pronunciation: / vɚˈveɪn ˈfæməli / (AmE), / vəˈveɪn ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of tropical and subtropical herbs, shrubs, and trees known as Verbenaceae, found mainly in the New World
