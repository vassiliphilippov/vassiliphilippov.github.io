souvlaki

noun
Greek grilled meat on a skewer
Pronunciation: / suːˈvlɑːki / (AmE), / suːˈvlæki / (BrE)
A Greek dish consisting of small pieces of meat, often lamb, grilled on a skewer, sometimes served with pita and garnishes.

Examples

Greek souvlaki dish
At the festival, vendors sold souvlaki wrapped in warm pita bread.
She ordered a delicious souvlaki with a side of salad for lunch.
The restaurant is famous for its authentic Greek souvlaki cooked over charcoal.
