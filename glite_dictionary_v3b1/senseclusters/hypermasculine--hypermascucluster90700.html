hypermasculine

noun
person with extreme masculinity
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˈmæskjəlɪn / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəˈmæskjʊlɪn / (BrE)
a person, especially a man, who displays exaggerated or extreme male stereotypical behaviors or traits

Examples

A hypermasculine man
Some people see the action movie hero as a typical hypermasculine who never shows emotion.
The coach's hypermasculine attitude made it hard for players to express vulnerability.
He was criticized for acting like a hypermasculine in situations that called for empathy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: effeminate - having or showing qualities more often associated with women
  • synonym: macho - a man who is aggressively proud of his masculinity
