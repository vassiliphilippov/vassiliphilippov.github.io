sailor

noun
navy serviceman or crew member
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪlər / (AmE), / ˈseɪlə / (BrE)
a person who works in the navy, especially as a member of the crew of a warship

Examples

Navy sailor
Her father was a sailor in the navy for over twenty years.
Many sailors participated in the rescue mission during the storm.
The young sailor stood proudly on the deck of the destroyer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seaman - a person who works as a sailor, especially below the rank of officer
  • antonym: soldier - a person serving in an army
