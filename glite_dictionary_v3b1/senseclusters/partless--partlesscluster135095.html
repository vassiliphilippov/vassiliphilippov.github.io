partless

adjective
indivisible; whole
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrtləs / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtləs / (BrE)

Examples

Partless whole
The philosopher spoke of a partless reality that cannot be broken down further.
Some believe the soul is partless and eternal, unlike the body.
The artist aimed to capture a sense of partless unity in her sculpture.
