lofty

adjective
elevated in morals or intellect

Examples

Lofty ideals
Despite his lofty ambitions, he remained humble and approachable.
She admired his lofty ideals and commitment to justice.
The novel is written in a lofty and poetic style that challenges readers.
Her lofty ideals inspired those around her to strive for greatness.
