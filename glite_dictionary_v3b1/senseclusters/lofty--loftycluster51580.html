lofty

adjective
elevated in morals or intellect
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔfti / (AmE), / ˈlɒfti / (BrE)
of high moral, intellectual, or social status; elevated in character, ideals, or appearance

Examples

Lofty ideals

The lofty pillars of the ancient temple gave the building a sense of grandeur and dignity.
