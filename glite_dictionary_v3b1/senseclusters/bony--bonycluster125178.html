bony

adjective
very thin; bones showing
3.9 2.2
Pronunciation: /ˈboʊ.niː/ (AmE), /ˈbəʊ.niː/ (BrE)
very thin or having prominent bones so that the bones are easily seen

Examples

Bony arms
After months of illness, her bony frame was easily visible through her clothes.
He held out his bony hand to greet me.
The stray dog was so bony that you could count its ribs.
She wrapped her fingers around his bony wrist and felt how thin he had become.
The grilled fish was delicious, but the fillet was rather bony.
The stray dog looked weak and bony after weeks without proper food.
