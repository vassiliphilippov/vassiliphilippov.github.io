bulge out

verb
to swell or protrude outward
4.7
Pronunciation: / bʌldʒ aʊt / (AmE), / bʌldʒ aʊt / (BrE)
to swell or protrude outward in a rounded shape

Examples

To bulge out strongly
His suitcase was so full that it started to bulge out at the seams.
The sides of the balloon began to bulge out as more air was pumped in.
You could see her pockets bulging out with coins and candy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protrude - to extend beyond or above a surface
  • antonym: recede - to move or slope backward; to become less prominent
