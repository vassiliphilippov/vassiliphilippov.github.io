preexperiment

noun
preliminary test
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːɪkˈspɛrəmənt / (AmE), / ˌpriːɪkˈspɛrɪmənt / (BrE)
an activity or procedure carried out before the main experiment to test methods or equipment

Examples

A preexperiment test
The researchers performed a preexperiment to ensure all sensors were working correctly.
A preexperiment can help identify potential problems before the main study begins.
They adjusted the procedure after the preexperiment revealed some unexpected results.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: experiment - a scientific test to discover or prove something
  • synonym: pilot study - a small-scale preliminary study before the main research
