visit

verb
to inflict something unpleasant
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪzɪt /; / ˈvɪzət / (AmE), / ˈvɪzɪt / (BrE)
to inflict or impose something unpleasant, such as punishment or misfortune, on someone

Examples

To visit punishment
She vowed never to visit her frustrations on her children.
The dictator visited terror upon his enemies to maintain control.
The gods were said to visit plagues on those who angered them.
A severe drought visited the region, destroying crops and livelihoods.
The town was visited by a series of devastating floods in the early 20th century.
They believed the curse had visited his family for generations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: afflict - to cause pain or suffering to
  • synonym: inflict - to cause something unpleasant to be endured
  • synonym: punish - to subject someone to a penalty for wrongdoing
