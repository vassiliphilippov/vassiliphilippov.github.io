Revolutionary Armed Forces of Colombia

proper noun
political party formed by former FARC members
7.4
Pronunciation: /rev.ə.ˈluː.ʃə.ri ɑːrmd ˈfɔːr.sɪz əv kə.ˈlʌm.bi.ə/ (AmE), /rev.ə.ˈluː.ʃən.ri ɑːmd ˈfɔː.sɪz əv kə.ˈlʌm.bi.ə/ (BrE)
a political party formed by former members of the FARC guerrillas after the 2016–2017 peace process, officially known in Spanish as Fuerza Alternativa Revolucionaria del Común (FARC).
