baul

noun
Bengali mystic minstrel
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊl / (AmE), / ˈbaʊl / (BrE)
a member of a group of mystic minstrels from Bengal, known for their spiritual songs and unique philosophy

Examples

A baul singer
The baul captivated the audience with his soulful singing and rhythmic dance.
Many people travel to Bengal to listen to the spiritual songs of a baul.
She was inspired by the philosophy and music of the baul tradition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: minstrel - a singer or musician, especially one who performs folk music
  • synonym: mystic - a person who seeks spiritual truth or union with the divine
