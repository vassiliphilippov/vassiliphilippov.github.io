fashioner

noun
fashion designer
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfæʃənər / (AmE), / ˈfæʃənə / (BrE)
a person who designs clothing or creates new styles, especially in the fashion industry

Examples

A clothing fashioner
The young fashioner quickly gained recognition for her innovative designs.
As a talented fashioner, he introduced bold colors and unique fabrics to the runway.
Many aspiring designers look up to the famous fashioner for inspiration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: designer - a person who creates original plans or styles for products
  • antonym: follower - a person who adopts styles or ideas created by others
  • synonym: trendsetter - someone who starts or popularizes new styles or trends
