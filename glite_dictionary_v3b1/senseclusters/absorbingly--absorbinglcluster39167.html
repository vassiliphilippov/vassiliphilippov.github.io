absorbingly

adverb
in a captivating way
4.7
Pronunciation: / əbˈzɔrbɪŋli / (AmE), / əbˈzɔːbɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that fully holds your attention or interest

Examples

To absorbingly hold attention
The documentary was absorbingly presented, making even complex topics easy to follow.
She spoke absorbingly about her travels, and everyone listened closely.
The movie unfolds absorbingly, keeping the audience engaged from start to finish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: captivatingly - in a way that attracts and holds interest
  • antonym: dully - in a way that is not interesting or exciting
