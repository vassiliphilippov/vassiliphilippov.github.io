heterotopic

adjective
abnormally positioned
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛtərəˈtɑpɪk / (AmE), / ˌhɛtərəˈtɒpɪk / (BrE)
located in an abnormal or unusual position within the body

Examples

Heterotopic bone growth
The surgeon discovered a heterotopic bone growth near the patient's hip joint.
Doctors identified heterotopic tissue during the routine examination.
A heterotopic pregnancy occurs when an embryo implants outside the normal uterine location.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ectopic - located in an abnormal place or position
  • antonym: orthotopic - located in the normal or usual position
