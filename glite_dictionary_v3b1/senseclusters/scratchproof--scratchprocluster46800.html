scratchproof

adjective
resistant to scratches
Pronunciation: / ˈskrætʃˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈskrætʃˌpruːf / (BrE)

Examples

She bought scratchproof sunglasses so they would last longer during outdoor activities.
Many watches now feature scratchproof glass to keep them looking new.
