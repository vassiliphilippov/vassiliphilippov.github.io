Kievan Rus'

proper noun
medieval East Slavic state (9th–13th centuries)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːəvən ˈruːs / (AmE), / ˈkiːəvən ˈruːs / (BrE)
the medieval East Slavic state, founded in the 9th century and centered first in Novgorod, later in Kiev, considered a predecessor to modern Russia, Ukraine, and Belarus

Examples

Kievan Rus' state

Trade flourished in Kievan Rus' due to its position between Europe and Asia.
