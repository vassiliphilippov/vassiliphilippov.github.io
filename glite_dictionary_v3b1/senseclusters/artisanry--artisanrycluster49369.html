artisanry

noun
group of artisans
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrtɪzənri / (AmE), / ˈɑːtɪzənri / (BrE)
the group or class of skilled manual workers, especially those involved in traditional crafts or trades

Examples

Local artisanry community
The city is famous for its artisanry, especially in pottery and weaving.
Local artisanry has kept traditional woodworking techniques alive for generations.
The museum's new exhibit highlights the artisanry of the region's skilled metalworkers.

  • degree higher: working class - the social group consisting of people employed in manual or industrial work
