Thraupidae

noun
family of birds: tanagers
9.9
Pronunciation: / θræˈjupɪˌdi / (AmE), / θræˈjuːpɪdiː / (BrE)
A large family of small to medium-sized songbirds found mainly in the New World tropics, commonly known as tanagers.
