belong

verb
be suitable or accepted as part of something
Pronunciation: / bɪˈlɔŋ /; / bəˈlɔŋ / (AmE), / bɪˈlɒŋ / (BrE)
to feel accepted or to be a member of a group, organization, or place

Examples

Belong to a group
Despite her efforts, she never truly felt she belonged in the city.
Do you ever feel like you don't belong at work?
He struggled to find a place where he could belong.
After moving to the city, she finally felt she belonged to the community.
Does this team belong to your organization, or are they independent?
He belongs to a local chess club that meets every week.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exclude - to prevent from being included or accepted
  • synonym: fit in - to blend or integrate socially or culturally
  • synonym: join - to become a member or part of a group or organization
  • antonym: leave - to stop being a member of a group or organization
