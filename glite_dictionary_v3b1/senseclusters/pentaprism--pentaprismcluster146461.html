pentaprism

noun
five-sided optical prism
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛntəˌprɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈpɛntəˌprɪzəm / (BrE)
a five-sided optical prism used in cameras to reflect light and produce an upright image for the viewer

Examples

A pentaprism in camera
The camera uses a pentaprism to allow the photographer to see exactly what the lens sees.
A pentaprism is essential in many single-lens reflex cameras for producing an upright image.
Technicians carefully cleaned the pentaprism to ensure the optical path remained clear and accurate.
The pentaprism in a DSLR camera helps photographers see the scene as it will appear in the photo.
Without a pentaprism, the image in the viewfinder would appear upside down and reversed.
He cleaned the pentaprism carefully to ensure a clear and accurate view through the camera.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mirror - a surface that reflects light to form an image
  • synonym: prism - a transparent optical element with flat, polished surfaces that refract light
  • synonym: viewfinder - a device on a camera for showing the field of view
