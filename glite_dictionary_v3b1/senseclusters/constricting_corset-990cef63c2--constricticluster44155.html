constricting corset

noun
tight-fitting waist-shaping garment
Pronunciation: / kənˈstrɪktɪŋ ˈkɔr.sət / (AmE), / kənˈstrɪktɪŋ ˈkɔː.sɪt / (BrE)
a tightly fitting garment designed to compress and shape the waist
