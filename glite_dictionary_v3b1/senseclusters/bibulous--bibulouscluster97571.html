bibulous

adjective
excessively fond of drinking alcohol
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪbjələs / (AmE), / ˈbɪbjʊləs / (BrE)
(formal or literary) given to excessive drinking of alcohol; fond of or related to the consumption of alcoholic beverages.

Examples

A bibulous person
During the holidays, many enjoy bibulous celebrations with friends and family.
His bibulous remarks at dinner were met with uneasy laughter.
The novelist was famous for his bibulous lifestyle and late-night parties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drunken - affected by or given to drinking alcohol
  • antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol; abstaining from alcohol
