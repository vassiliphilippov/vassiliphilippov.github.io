raff

noun
derogatory for common people
Pronunciation: / ræf / (AmE), / ræf / (BrE)
a derogatory term for common people regarded as socially inferior or undesirable; the rabble or riffraff

Examples

The raff in town
The wealthy landowner looked down on the local raff and refused to speak with them.
Some believed the festival attracted only the raff from neighboring towns.
He warned his children not to associate with the raff at the market.

Related Terms

  • antonym: elite - a group of people considered to be the best or most powerful
  • synonym: rabble - a disorderly crowd; the common people seen as inferior
  • synonym: riffraff - people regarded as disreputable or undesirable
