rampantly

adverb
in a wild or uncontrolled way
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈræm.pənt.li / (AmE), / ˈræm.pənt.li / (BrE)
In an uncontrolled, wild, or unchecked manner.

Examples

Rampantly spreading rumors
Rumors about the scandal circulated rampantly on social media before any facts were confirmed.
The invasive species spread rampantly throughout the region, causing concern among conservationists.
Without proper maintenance, vines and weeds grew rampantly over the abandoned building.
