mythos

noun
fictional world lore
8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪ.θoʊs/ (AmE), /ˈmɪ.θɒs/ (BrE)
the collection of stories, themes, and background that form the foundation of a fictional world or series

Examples

Fantasy mythos
The fantasy series has a rich mythos that includes ancient gods and magical creatures.
Fans debated which parts of the mythos were considered official by the author.
The movie expanded the original mythos by introducing new characters and legends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: canon - the official set of stories or facts in a fictional universe
  • synonym: lore - traditional knowledge and stories about a subject
  • antonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist
