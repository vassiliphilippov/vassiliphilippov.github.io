fight

noun
aggressive determination; willingness to compete
Pronunciation: / faɪt / (AmE), / faɪt / (BrE)
energetic determination and willingness to compete or carry on in difficult situations, especially in the face of adversity

Examples

She shows fight
Despite her illness, she still has a lot of fight left in her.
His fight and passion inspired everyone on the field.
The team never lost their fight, even when they were behind.

  • synonym: spirit - courage and determination; liveliness
