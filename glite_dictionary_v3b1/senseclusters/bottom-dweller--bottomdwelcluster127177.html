bottom-dweller

noun
aquatic animal living on the bottom
9.9 5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑː.təm ˈdwel.ər/ (AmE), /ˈbɒ.təm ˈdwel.ə/ (BrE)
a fish or other aquatic animal that lives and feeds on or near the bottom of a body of water

Examples

A bottom-dweller fish
Many bottom-dwellers play a vital role in keeping aquatic ecosystems healthy by consuming decaying matter.
Scientists discovered a new species of bottom-dweller in the lake during their research expedition.
The bottom-dweller remained hidden beneath the rocks, searching for food late into the evening.
