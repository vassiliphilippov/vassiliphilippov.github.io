hop

noun
a small jump, often on one foot
2.1
Pronunciation: / hɑp / (AmE), / hɒp / (BrE)
a quick, small jump upward or forward, especially on one foot

Examples

A quick hop
He made a quick hop over the fence to retrieve the ball.
She lost her balance during the hop and had to start the routine again.
The child did a little hop on one foot to cross the puddle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jump - to push oneself off a surface and into the air by using the muscles in one's legs and feet
