home care

noun
household maintenance services
Pronunciation: / hoʊm kɛr / (AmE), / həʊm keə / (BrE)
services provided to maintain, clean, or repair a house or apartment, often performed by professionals or agencies

Examples

Weekly home care
They arranged for weekly home care to keep their apartment clean and tidy.
Professional home care services can help with repairs and regular maintenance tasks.
After moving in, she decided to invest in home care to maintain the property.

Related Terms

  • synonym: home repair - the act of fixing or restoring parts of a house
  • synonym: housekeeping - the management and cleaning of a home
