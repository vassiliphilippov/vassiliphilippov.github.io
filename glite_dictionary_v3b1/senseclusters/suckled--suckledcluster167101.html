suckled

adjective
breast-fed (of an infant)
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌkəld / (AmE), / ˈsʌkəld / (BrE)
(of an infant) having been breast-fed.

Examples

A suckled baby
After being suckled for several months, the infant was gradually weaned.
Many suckled babies develop a close bond with their mothers.
The newborn lamb was suckled by its mother during the first few weeks.

Related Terms
