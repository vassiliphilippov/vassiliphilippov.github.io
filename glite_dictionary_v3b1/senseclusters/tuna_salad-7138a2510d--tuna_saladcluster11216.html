tuna salad

noun
salad made with canned tuna
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːnə ˈsæləd / (AmE), / ˈtjuːnə ˈsæləd / (BrE)
A salad made mainly from chopped canned tuna, often mixed with mayonnaise and other ingredients such as celery or onion.

Examples

A bowl of tuna salad
For a quick dinner, I made a big bowl of tuna salad with crackers.
He packed a tuna salad sandwich for his lunch at work.
She added chopped celery and onions to her tuna salad for extra flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chicken salad - salad containing cooked chicken, often with mayonnaise and vegetables
  • synonym: egg salad - salad made with chopped hard-boiled eggs and mayonnaise
