craft

noun
skilled manual occupation or trade
4.6 3.1
Pronunciation: /kræft/ (AmE), /krɑːft/ /; / /kræft/ (BrE)
a skilled activity or occupation that involves making things by hand, or the people who do this work; also, skill in such work

Examples

A craft fair
After years of training, she finally mastered her craft as a jeweler.

The exhibition celebrates the traditional craft of pottery making.
