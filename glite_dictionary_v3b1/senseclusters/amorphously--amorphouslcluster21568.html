amorphously

adverb
without clear shape
Pronunciation: / əˈmɔrfəsli / (AmE), / əˈmɔːfəsli / (BrE)
in a way that lacks a clear shape, form, or structure

Examples

Spread amorphously over fields
The fog spread amorphously over the quiet fields in the early morning.
Paint spilled amorphously across the canvas, creating an abstract effect.
The crowd moved amorphously through the plaza, with no clear direction.
The fog drifted amorphously across the field, hiding the landscape in a shapeless mist.
The artist painted the background amorphously, giving it a dreamy and undefined quality.
Some materials cool amorphously, resulting in a glassy, non-crystalline structure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chaotically - in a way that is disordered or confused
  • antonym: clearly - in a way that is easy to see or understand
  • synonym: formlessly - in a way that has no definite form or shape
  • synonym: shapelessly - in a way that lacks a distinct or regular shape
  • antonym: structurally - in a way that has a clear structure or organization
