fawn

noun
a young deer
Pronunciation: / fɔn / (AmE), / fɔːn / (BrE)
a young deer, especially one that is less than a year old

Examples

A baby fawn
A frightened fawn darted away as we approached the stream.
The fawn followed its mother closely as they crossed the field.
We spotted a fawn resting in the tall grass near the forest edge.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: deer - a hoofed grazing or browsing animal, of the family Cervidae
