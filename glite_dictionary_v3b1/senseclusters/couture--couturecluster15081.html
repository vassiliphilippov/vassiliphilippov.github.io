couture

noun
high-end fashion design and dressmaking
9.1 3.27/m
Pronunciation: / kuÀàt är / (AmE), / kuÀêÀàtj ä…ô / (BrE)
the design and making of fashionable, high-quality clothes by leading fashion houses

Examples

High couture fashion
Many celebrities choose to wear couture gowns on the red carpet.
She dreams of working in couture after graduating from fashion school.
The exhibition showcased decades of stunning couture from renowned designers.

Synonyms

  • haute couture
    8.8 0.41/m
    haute couture makes one-off, custom-fitted garments for wealthy clients, while couture describes fashion made by top houses.
  • high fashion
    4.1 0.65/m
    high fashion shows trend-setting, often ready-to-wear luxury styles sold to many people, while couture means handmade designs by top fashion houses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: haute couture - the designing and making of exclusive, custom-fitted fashion
