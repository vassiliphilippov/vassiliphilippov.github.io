relay link

noun
amplifier for signal restoration
Pronunciation: / riˈleɪ lɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈrɪːleɪ lɪŋk / (BrE)
an amplifier that restores the strength of a transmitted signal, especially in telecommunications to maintain signal quality over long distances

Examples

A relay link amplifier
Engineers installed a new relay link to prevent the television broadcast from fading.
The relay link boosted the signal so it could travel across the entire region without loss of quality.
Using a relay link ensures that communications remain clear even over long fiber optic cables.

Related Terms

  • synonym: repeater - a device that receives and retransmits a signal
