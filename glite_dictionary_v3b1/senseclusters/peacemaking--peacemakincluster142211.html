peacemaking

noun
conflict resolution process
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːsˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpiːsˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of helping people or groups end conflict and establish peaceful relations

Examples

International peacemaking
The United Nations sent a team to assist with peacemaking in the war-torn region.
Effective peacemaking requires patience, understanding, and strong communication skills.
After years of conflict, both sides finally agreed to begin peacemaking talks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conciliation - the process of bringing disputing sides together
  • antonym: conflict - a serious disagreement or argument
  • synonym: mediation - the act of intervening to help resolve a dispute
