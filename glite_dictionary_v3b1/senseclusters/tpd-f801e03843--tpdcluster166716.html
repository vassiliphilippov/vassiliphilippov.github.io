TPD

sym
insurance disability benefit
Pronunciation: / ti pi di / (AmE), / tiː piː diː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for total and permanent disability, a benefit paid when someone is permanently unable to work due to serious injury or illness

Examples

TPD insurance payout
After his accident, he received a TPD payout that helped cover his living expenses.
Many workers add TPD cover to their insurance policies for extra protection.
Her claim for TPD was approved when doctors confirmed she could not return to work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disability insurance - insurance providing income in case of disability
  • antonym: life insurance - insurance paying out on the death of the insured
