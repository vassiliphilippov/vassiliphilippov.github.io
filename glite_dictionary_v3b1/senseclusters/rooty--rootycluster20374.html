rooty

adjective
earthy flavor or smell
7.1 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàruÀêti / (AmE), / ÀàruÀêti / (BrE)
having a taste, smell, or quality reminiscent of roots or root vegetables; earthy in flavor or aroma

Examples

Rooty flavor
The herbal tea had a distinctly rooty aroma that reminded me of fresh beets.
Some people enjoy the rooty flavor of traditional ginseng soups.
The forest floor gave off a rooty scent after the rain.
