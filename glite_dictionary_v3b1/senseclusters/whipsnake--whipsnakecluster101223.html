whipsnake

noun
fast-moving snake with a whiplike tail
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪpˌsneɪk / (AmE), / ˈwɪp.sneɪk / (BrE)
a small, fast-moving snake with a long, slender, whiplike tail, found in various regions and often belonging to the genera Masticophis or Demansia

Examples

A whipsnake species
Scientists observed a whipsnake hunting insects in the rocky terrain.
She spotted a slender whipsnake sunning itself on the path ahead.
The whipsnake darted quickly through the dry grass, barely visible as it moved.
