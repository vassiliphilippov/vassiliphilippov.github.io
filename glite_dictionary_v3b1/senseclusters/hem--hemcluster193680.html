hem

verb
to make a sound like 'ahem'
8.0 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / hÉ›m / (AmE), / hÉ›m / (BrE)
to make a short sound such as 'hem' or 'ahem', often to attract attention or clear the throat

Examples

Whenever she was nervous, she would hem before speaking.
