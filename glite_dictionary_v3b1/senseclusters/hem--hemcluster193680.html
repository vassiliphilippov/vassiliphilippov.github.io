hem

verb
to make a sound like 'ahem'
Pronunciation: / hɛm / (AmE), / hɛm / (BrE)
to make a short sound such as 'hem' or 'ahem', often to attract attention or clear the throat

Examples

To hem loudly
He hemmed loudly to let everyone know he had something to say.
The lecturer paused and hemmed, searching for the right words.
Whenever she was nervous, she would hem before speaking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ahem - an interjection used to attract attention or clear one's throat
