includible

adjective
able to be included
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkluːdəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈkluːdɪbəl / (BrE)
able to be included or suitable for inclusion in something

Examples

Only includible expenses
Only includible expenses will be reimbursed by the company.
The report lists all includible items for the tax deduction.
Please check if your purchase is includible under the new policy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eligible - qualified or suitable to be chosen
  • antonym: excludable - able to be excluded or left out
