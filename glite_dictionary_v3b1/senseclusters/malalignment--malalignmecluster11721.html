malalignment

noun
incorrect alignment
Pronunciation: / ˌmæl.əˈlaɪnmənt / (AmE), / ˌmæl.əˈlaɪnmənt / (BrE)
the condition of being incorrectly or imperfectly aligned, especially in bones or body parts

Examples

Bone malalignment
The X-ray revealed a malalignment of the patient's knee joint after the accident.
Orthodontic treatment can correct dental malalignment in children and adults.
Chronic pain may result from malalignment of the spine or other bones.

  • synonym: misalignment - incorrect or improper alignment
