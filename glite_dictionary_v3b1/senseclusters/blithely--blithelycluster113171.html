blithely

adverb
in a cheerful or careless manner
9.2 6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈblaɪð.liː/ /; / /ˈblaɪð.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈblaɪð.liː/ /; / /ˈblaɪð.lɪ/ (BrE)
In a way that shows a casual and cheerful indifference, often considered to be improper or lacking due thought.

Examples

To blithely ignore warnings
Despite the criticism, they continued blithely with their plans.
He blithely ignored the warning signs and carried on.
She walked blithely into the meeting, unconcerned about being late.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheerfully - in a happy or optimistic way
