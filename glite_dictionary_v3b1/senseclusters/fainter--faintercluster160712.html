fainter

noun
person who faints
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈfeɪn.tər/ (AmE), /ˈfeɪn.tə/ (BrE)
a person who loses consciousness briefly and suddenly, usually due to a temporary drop in blood flow to the brain

Examples

A fainter collapsed
The nurse rushed to help the fainter who had collapsed in the hallway.
During the crowded event, a fainter was quickly carried to a quiet area to recover.
First aid training teaches you how to assist a fainter safely and effectively.
