Piltdown man

noun
faked early human fossil discovery
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪltdaʊn mæn / (AmE), / ˈpɪltdaʊn man / (BrE)
an early human fossil discovered in England and once considered a missing link in human evolution, later shown to be a scientific hoax
