chocolate egg

noun
egg-shaped chocolate candy
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑklət ɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒklət ɛɡ / (BrE)
A sweet or confection made of chocolate and shaped like an egg, typically given as a gift or treat during Easter.

Examples

A chocolate egg
After dinner, they shared a small chocolate egg with coffee.
Many children look forward to finding a chocolate egg in their Easter basket.
She bought a large chocolate egg filled with candies as a gift for her niece.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Easter egg - an egg-shaped confection or a decorated real egg given at Easter
