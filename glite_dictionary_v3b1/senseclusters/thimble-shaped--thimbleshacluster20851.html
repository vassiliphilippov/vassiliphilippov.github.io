thimble-shaped

adjective
having the shape of a thimble
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈθɪmbəl ˌʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈθɪmbəl ˌʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape of a thimble; resembling a thimble in form, typically rounded and open at one end.

Examples

A thimble-shaped dome
The architect designed a thimble-shaped dome for the new gallery.
The delicate flower had a thimble-shaped blossom perched on a slender stem.
Tiny thimble-shaped containers were used to hold individual servings of spices.
