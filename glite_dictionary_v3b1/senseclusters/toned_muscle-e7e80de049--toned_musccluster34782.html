toned muscle

noun
firm and defined muscle
Pronunciation: / toʊnd ˈmʌsəl / (AmE), / təʊnd ˈmʌsl̩ / (BrE)
a muscle that is firm and has definition, usually as a result of exercise or physical training

Examples

A toned muscle definition
After months of training, his arms were noticeably more toned muscle than before.
Many fitness programs promise to help you achieve toned muscle in just a few weeks.
She worked hard at the gym to develop toned muscle throughout her body.
