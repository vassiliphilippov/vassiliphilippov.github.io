anti-American

adjective
opposed to the United States
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.ti.əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən / (AmE), / ˌænti.əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən / (BrE)
opposed to the United States or its policies, culture, or people

Examples

Anti-American views
He denied that his speech was anti-American, saying he merely disagreed with U.S. foreign policy.
Some politicians in Europe have expressed anti-American views in recent years.
The film was criticized for its perceived anti-American message.

Related Terms
