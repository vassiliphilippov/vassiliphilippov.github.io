short bone

noun
a bone with nearly equal dimensions; wrist or ankle bone
7.0 6.0
Pronunciation: / ʃɔrt boʊn / (AmE), / ʃɔːt bəʊn / (BrE)
a bone that has roughly equal length, width, and thickness, typically found in the wrist and ankle

Examples

A short bone in wrist
The carpals in the human wrist are classified as short bones because of their equal dimensions.
The tarsal bones of the ankle are a typical example of a short bone.
Unlike long bones, short bones provide support and stability with little movement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: long bone - a bone that is longer than it is wide, such as the femur or humerus
