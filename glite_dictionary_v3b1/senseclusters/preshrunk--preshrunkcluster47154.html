preshrunk

adjective
treated to prevent shrinking
Pronunciation: / priˈʃrʌŋk / (AmE), / priˈʃrʌŋk / (BrE)
treated with a process to prevent shrinking when washed or used

Examples

Preshrunk cotton shirt
The label says these preshrunk T-shirts will not get smaller in the wash.
He prefers preshrunk fabrics because they keep their original fit after cleaning.
Make sure your new pants are preshrunk to avoid any surprises after laundry day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shrink-resistant - not likely to shrink when washed
  • antonym: shrinkable - likely to become smaller when washed
