suborder Charadrii

noun
taxonomic suborder of shorebirds
Pronunciation: / sʌbˌɔrdər kəˈrædriːaɪ / (AmE), / sʌbˌɔːdə kəˈrædriːaɪ / (BrE)
A zoological taxonomic suborder comprising shorebirds such as plovers, sandpipers, avocets, phalaropes, coursers, and stone curlews.
