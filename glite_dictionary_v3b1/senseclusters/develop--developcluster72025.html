Synonyms

  • create
    3.4 30/m
    create means making something new by imagination or art, while develop implies a process to grow or improve an idea or product.
  • formulate
    4.5 3/m
    formulate is carefully expressing an idea or plan in clear words, while develop covers creating and growing that idea into something usable.
  • design
    2.6 60/m
    design is planning how something will look or work before making it, while develop covers both planning and producing the final product.
  • invent
    3.1 20/m
    invent is creating a completely new device or process by original thinking, while develop often means creating or improving over time.
  • generate
    3.2 30/m
