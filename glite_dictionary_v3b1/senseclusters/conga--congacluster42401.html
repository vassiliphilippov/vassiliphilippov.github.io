conga

noun
lively dance music (Afro-Cuban rhythm)
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑŋɡə / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡə / (BrE)
A style of lively music composed to accompany the conga dance, characterized by a syncopated rhythm and Afro-Cuban influences.

Examples

Lively conga music
She prefers listening to salsa and conga at Latin music festivals.
The band played a lively conga that got everyone on their feet.
The rhythmic beat of the conga filled the room with energy.

  • synonym: rumba - a genre of Cuban dance music
