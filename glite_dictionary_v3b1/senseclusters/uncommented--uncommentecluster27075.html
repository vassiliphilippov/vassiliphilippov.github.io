uncommented

adjective
without comment
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈkɑːmɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈkɒmɛntɪd / (BrE)
not remarked on, discussed, or given a comment, especially in public or official contexts

Examples

Uncommented statement
The report was released uncommented by the officials, leaving the public to draw their own conclusions.
Her suggestion went uncommented during the meeting, and no one responded to it.
The artist's controversial work remained uncommented in the press despite its popularity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discussed - talked about or commented on
  • synonym: ignored - deliberately not noticed or acknowledged
  • synonym: unremarked - not mentioned or noticed
