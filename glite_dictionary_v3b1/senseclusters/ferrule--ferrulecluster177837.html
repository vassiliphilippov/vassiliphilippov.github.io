ferrule

noun
metal cap or band added for reinforcement
9.9 7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈfe.rəl/ (AmE), /ˈfe.rəl/ (BrE)
a metal cap or band fitted around the end of a stick, handle, or pole to prevent splitting or to provide reinforcement

Examples

A ferrule on a stick

Many carpenters use a ferrule at the end of their wooden tools to make them last longer.
The old umbrella's handle was reinforced with a ferrule to prevent it from splitting.
