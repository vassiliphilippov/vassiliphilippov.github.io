Bureau of Customs

noun
government agency enforcing import tariffs
3.8
Pronunciation: / bjʊˈroʊ əv ˈkʌstəmz / (AmE), / bjʊəˈrəʊ əv ˈkʌstəmz / (BrE)
a government agency responsible for enforcing import tariffs and regulations on goods entering a country

Examples

The Bureau of Customs office
All imported goods must be declared to the Bureau of Customs upon arrival in the country.
Corruption scandals have occasionally plagued the Bureau of Customs in recent years.
The Bureau of Customs announced stricter regulations on imported electronics this year.
