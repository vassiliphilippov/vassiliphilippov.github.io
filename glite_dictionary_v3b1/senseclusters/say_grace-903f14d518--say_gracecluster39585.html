say grace

verb
offer a prayer before eating
2.4
Pronunciation: / seɪ ɡreɪs / (AmE), / seɪ ɡreɪs / (BrE)
to recite a short prayer of thanks before a meal

Examples

To say grace before meals
Before we eat, let's say grace together as a family.
It is their tradition to say grace before every meal, no matter where they are.
She forgot to say grace at lunch and remembered only after she started eating.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bless the food - to ask for a blessing over the food before eating
  • synonym: give thanks - to express gratitude, especially before a meal
