reassume

verb
to resume again
6.6
Pronunciation: /riː.ə.ˈsuːm/ /; / /riː.ə.ˈsjuːm/ (AmE), /riː.ə.ˈsjuːm/ /; / /riː.ə.ˈsuːm/ (BrE)
