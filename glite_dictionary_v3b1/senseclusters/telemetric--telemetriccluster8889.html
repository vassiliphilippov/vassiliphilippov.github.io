telemetric

adjective
measuring at a distance
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛləˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the measurement and transmission of data over a distance by electronic means

Examples

Telemetric data transmission
The hospital uses telemetric devices to monitor patients' heart rates from a central station.
Engineers installed a telemetric system to collect weather data from remote locations.
The spacecraft's telemetric signals allow scientists to track its journey in real time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: manual - operated or done by hand rather than automatically or remotely
  • synonym: remote - operated or controlled from a distance
