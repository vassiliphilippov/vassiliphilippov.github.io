lambada

noun
Brazilian dance style
8.4
Pronunciation: / lɑmˈbɑdə / (AmE), / læmˈbɑːdə / (BrE)
a lively Brazilian dance characterized by fast, rhythmic movements and close partner dancing

Examples

To dance the lambada
The couple performed the lambada with impressive energy at the dance competition.
Many tourists enjoy learning the lambada when visiting Brazil.
The music festival featured a live band playing songs for the lambada.
