chylomicron

noun
microscopic fat-transport particle in the blood
9.9
Pronunciation: / kaɪloʊˈmaɪkrɑn / (AmE), / kaɪləʊˈmɪkrɒn / (BrE)
A microscopic particle containing triglycerides formed in the intestines during digestion, which transports fats through the bloodstream.

Examples

A chylomicron particle
