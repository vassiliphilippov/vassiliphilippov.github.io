rolling

noun
the act of moving something on wheels
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlɪŋ / (BrE)
the action or process of moving or propelling something on wheels or by turning over and over

Examples

Smooth rolling luggage
Modern luggage eases travel with smooth rolling across airport floors.
She watched the rolling of barrels into the warehouse.
The rolling of the cart down the hill was effortless.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rotation - the action of rotating around an axis
  • antonym: sliding - the act of moving smoothly along a surface
