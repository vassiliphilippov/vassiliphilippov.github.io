uplight

noun
upward-facing lamp
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌlaɪt / (BrE)
a lamp or light fixture that directs light upward, usually to illuminate walls or ceilings

Examples

A hallway uplight
The new uplight in the hallway made the space feel brighter and more open.
She chose an uplight to highlight the artwork on the upper walls.
A single uplight can create a warm and inviting atmosphere in any room.
The designer installed an uplight in the corner to highlight the artwork on the wall.
A single uplight can make a small room feel much larger and brighter.
They chose an uplight to create a soft, ambient glow in the living room.
