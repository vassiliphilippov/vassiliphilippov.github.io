root beer float

noun
dessert drink: ice cream in root beer
Pronunciation: / ˈrut ˌbɪr floʊt / (AmE), / ˈruːt ˌbɪə fləʊt / (BrE)
a drink made by placing a scoop of ice cream in a glass of root beer, typically served as a dessert

Examples
