proportion

verb
to divide by ratio
5.3
Pronunciation: /prə.ˈpɔːr.ʃən/ (AmE), /prə.ˈpɔː.ʃən/ (BrE)
to divide or distribute something among parts or people according to a specific ratio or share

Examples

To proportion the work
The teacher proportioned the group project tasks based on each student's strengths.
They proportioned the food so that everyone at the picnic would get an equal share.
The inheritance was proportioned among the siblings according to their needs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: allocate - to distribute resources or duties for a particular purpose
  • synonym: distribute - to give out or share something among a number of people
  • antonym: hoard - to collect and keep a large amount of something for oneself
