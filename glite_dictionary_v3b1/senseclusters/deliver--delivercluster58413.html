Synonyms

  • give
    1.0 100/m
    give is a common verb used for offering or performing things like speeches or events, while deliver is more formal and focused on performance.
  • present
    2.8 104/m
    present is to show or introduce ideas or performances to an audience, while deliver often means performing or formally handing something over.
  • address
    4.4 17/m
    address is to speak formally to a group, often to state ideas or decisions, while deliver can mean giving or performing many kinds of content.
  • make a speech
    2.8 1.19/m
    make a speech is to give a formal talk at a ceremony or event, while deliver also applies to other performances or official statements.
  • speak in public
    3.9 0.20/m
    speak in public means to talk before people outside private settings, while deliver suggests a prepared speech or formal presentation to an audience.
  • speak
    3.2 80/m
    speak is to talk in general to others, while deliver specifically means giving or performing a prepared speech, idea, or decision before a group.
