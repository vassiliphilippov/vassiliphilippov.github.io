where to shove it

x
rude dismissal or rejection phrase
Pronunciation: / wɛr tu ʃʌv ɪt / (AmE), / weə tə ʃʌv ɪt / (BrE)
used as a rude way to tell someone that you reject their suggestion, demand, or request completely and with contempt

Examples

Say where to shove it
After the unfair criticism, he just said, 'You can guess where to shove it.'
If you don't like my decision, you know where to shove it.
She told her boss where to shove it when he demanded she work overtime again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: get lost - used to tell someone forcefully to go away
  • synonym: shove it - tell someone rudely to go away or reject their demand
