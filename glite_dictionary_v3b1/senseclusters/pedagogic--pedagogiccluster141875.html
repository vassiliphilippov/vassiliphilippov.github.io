Synonyms

  • pedagogical
    7.8 8.02/m
    pedagogical refers to the theory and practice of teaching in classrooms, while pedagogic describes general relations to teaching or instruction.
  • instructional
    3.4 25/m
    instructional is used for materials or activities meant to teach students, while pedagogic covers teaching methods and principles more broadly.
  • educational
    2.7 116/m
    educational describes things that give knowledge or help learning, while pedagogic focuses specifically on teaching methods and instruction principles.
