postgame

adjective
after a sports game
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstˌɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstˌɡeɪm / (BrE)
occurring or relating to the period after a sports game has ended

Examples

Postgame interview
The players attended a postgame press conference to answer questions from reporters.
Fans celebrated together at a local restaurant during the postgame festivities.
The coach reviewed the team's performance in a detailed postgame analysis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pregame - occurring or relating to the period before a sports game
