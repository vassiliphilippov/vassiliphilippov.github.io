naked lady

noun
a type of flowering amaryllis plant
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪkɪd ˈleɪdi /; / ˈneɪkɪd ˌleɪdi / (AmE), / ˈneɪkɪd ˈleɪdi /; / ˈneɪkɪd ˌleɪdi / (BrE)
a type of flowering plant with showy flowers, such as certain amaryllis or colchicum species, often called 'naked lady'

Unlike many bulbs, the leaves of the naked lady appear in spring and die back before the flowers emerge.
