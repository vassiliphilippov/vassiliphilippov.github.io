on the front burner

adjective
top priority; urgent issue
Pronunciation: / ɑn ðə frʌnt ˈbɝnɚ / (AmE), / ɒn ðə frʌnt ˈbɜːnə / (BrE)
considered to be a priority or urgent issue that needs immediate attention

Examples

On the front burner issue
Her career goals are now on the front burner after years of focusing on family.
The new marketing plan is on the front burner while older initiatives are on hold.
With the deadline approaching, this project is on the front burner for the whole team.

Related Terms

  • antonym: on the back burner - considered a low priority or to be delayed until later
