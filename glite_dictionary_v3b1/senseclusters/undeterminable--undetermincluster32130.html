undeterminable

adjective
impossible to determine with certainty
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.dɪˈtɜːr.mɪ.nə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈtɜː.mɪ.nə.bəl / (BrE)
not able to be definitely decided or ascertained; impossible to determine with certainty.

Examples

Undeterminable result
Due to conflicting evidence, the winner of the match remained undeterminable.
In some cases, a patient's time of death may be undeterminable.
The cause of the malfunction was undeterminable despite extensive investigation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: determinable - able to be definitely decided or ascertained
  • synonym: indeterminate - not exactly known, established, or defined
