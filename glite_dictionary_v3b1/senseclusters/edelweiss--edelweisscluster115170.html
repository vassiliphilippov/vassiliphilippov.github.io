edelweiss

noun
alpine plant with white star-shaped flowers
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪdəlˌwaɪs / (AmE), / ˈeɪd(ə)lˌvaɪs / (BrE)
a small white alpine perennial plant native to Europe, with woolly leaves and star-shaped clusters of glistening white bracts.

Examples

A edelweiss flower
Many tourists hike the Alps hoping to see a blooming edelweiss in the wild.
She pressed a dried edelweiss flower into her travel journal as a keepsake.
The edelweiss is often found growing in rocky mountain regions above the tree line.
