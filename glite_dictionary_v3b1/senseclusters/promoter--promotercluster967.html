promoter

noun
active supporter or advocate
Pronunciation: / prəˈmoʊtər / (AmE), / prəˈməʊtə / (BrE)
a person who actively supports or encourages a cause, event, or idea; an advocate or supporter

Examples

Promoter of change
As a promoter of animal rights, she organizes many events to raise awareness.
He has always been a strong promoter of environmental protection policies.
The charity's promoter worked tirelessly to gain more supporters for the cause.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advocate - person who publicly supports a cause or policy
  • antonym: opponent - person who disagrees with or resists a cause or idea
