African oil palm

noun
tropical palm tree yielding palm oil
6.4 5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈæf.rɪ.kən ɔɪl pɑːm / (AmE), / ˈæf.rɪ.kən ɔɪl pɑːm / (BrE)
A tropical palm tree of West Africa (Elaeis guineensis) cultivated for its oil-rich fruit, a major source of palm oil.

Examples

African oil palm plantations
Many rainforests have been cleared for African oil palm plantations.
The African oil palm is widely cultivated in Malaysia and Indonesia for palm oil production.
The fruit of the African oil palm is processed into both palm oil and palm kernel oil.
