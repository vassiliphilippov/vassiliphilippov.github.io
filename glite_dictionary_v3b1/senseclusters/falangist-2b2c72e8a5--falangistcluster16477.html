Falangist

noun
member of Franco's Spanish Falange party
Pronunciation: / fəˈlɑːn.dʒɪst / (AmE), / fəˈlændʒɪst / (BrE)
a member of the Spanish political party known as the Falange, particularly during the era of General Franco

Examples

Falangist party member
He openly identified as a Falangist, supporting Franco's regime.
Several Falangists attended the rally commemorating the party's formation.
The Falangist movement played a major role in Spanish politics during the 20th century.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fascist - supporter of fascism
  • antonym: republican - supporter of a republic (opposed to Falangists in the Spanish Civil War)
