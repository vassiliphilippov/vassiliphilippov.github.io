pinch bar

noun
metal lever for moving heavy objects
7.4 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / p…™nt É b…ër / (AmE), / p…™nt É b…ëÀê / (BrE)
A long, strong metal bar with a pointed end used as a lever, especially for moving heavy objects.

Examples

A pinch bar tool
A pinch bar can serve as a fulcrum when maneuvering industrial equipment.
It's easier to lift large stones with a pinch bar than by hand.
The workers used a pinch bar to roll the heavy wheel into place.

Synonyms

  • crowbar

  • wrecking bar
    6.5 0.02/m
