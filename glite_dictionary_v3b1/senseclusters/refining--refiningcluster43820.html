refining

noun
removing impurities from materials
2.8
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈfaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of removing unwanted substances or impurities from a material such as oil, metals, or sugar to make it pure or usable

Examples

Metal refining process
Modern techniques have greatly improved the refining of precious metals like gold and silver.
Sugar requires extensive refining before it is ready for consumption.
The refining of crude oil produces gasoline and other useful fuels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: purification - the process of making something pure by removing contaminants
