semitandem

noun
partly tandem bicycle
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈtændəm / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈtændəm / (BrE)
a bicycle or other vehicle designed for two people, with seats or controls arranged partly one behind the other and partly side by side

Examples

A semitandem bicycle
They enjoyed riding their new semitandem along the park trails together.
A semitandem allows both riders to pedal while sitting slightly offset from each other.
The cycling club organized a race featuring several unique bikes, including a semitandem.

Related Terms

  • antonym: side-by-side bicycle - a bicycle with seats arranged next to each other for two riders
  • synonym: tandem - a bicycle with seats arranged one behind the other for two riders
