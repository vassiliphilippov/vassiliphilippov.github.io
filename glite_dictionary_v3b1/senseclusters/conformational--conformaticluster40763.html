Synonyms

  • configurational
    9.0 0.13/m
    configurational describes the overall arrangement or pattern of parts, while conformational emphasizes the three-dimensional shape and folding of those parts.
  • configural
    8.9 0.06/m
    configural focuses on how parts relate as an integrated whole, while conformational refers more to specific physical shape or spatial form.
  • formational
    6.3 0.00/m
    formational relates to the process of forming or building structure, while conformational describes the settled shape or arrangement of those parts.
