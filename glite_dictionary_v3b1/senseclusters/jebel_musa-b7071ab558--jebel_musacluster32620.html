Jebel Musa

proper noun
promontory in northern Morocco
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɛbəl ˈmuːsə / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛbəl ˈmuːsə / (BrE)
a promontory in northern Morocco, located opposite the Rock of Gibraltar; regarded as one of the Pillars of Hercules in classical mythology

Examples

The Jebel Musa summit
During their trip to Morocco, they hiked to the summit of Jebel Musa for stunning views of the Mediterranean.
Historians believe that Jebel Musa is one of the legendary Pillars of Hercules mentioned in ancient texts.
The famous Jebel Musa can be seen from the coast of Spain on a clear day.
