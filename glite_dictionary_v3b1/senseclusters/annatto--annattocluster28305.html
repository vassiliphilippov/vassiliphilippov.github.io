annatto

noun
orange-red dye plant
8.2
Pronunciation: / əˈnɑːtoʊ / (AmE), / əˈnatəʊ / (BrE)
a tropical shrub or small tree (Bixa orellana) whose seeds produce an orange-red dye used in food and cosmetics

Examples

Annatto plant seeds
The cheese gets its yellow color from annatto added during production.
Farmers harvest annatto seeds to sell as natural food coloring.
Traditional dishes in Latin America often use annatto for its color and mild flavor.
