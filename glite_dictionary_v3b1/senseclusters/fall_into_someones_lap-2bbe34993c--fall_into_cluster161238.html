fall into someone's lap

verb
receive something unexpectedly or easily
4.5
Pronunciation: / fɔl ˈɪntuː ˈsʌmwʌnz læp / (AmE), / fɔːl ˈɪntuː ˈsʌmwʌnz læp / (BrE)
to be received unexpectedly or without effort, typically as a stroke of good luck

Examples

To fall into someone's lap unexpectedly
Sometimes, a great opportunity will fall into your lap when you least expect it.
The perfect apartment just fell into his lap after months of searching unsuccessfully.
When the inheritance fell into her lap, she was completely unprepared for the responsibility.
