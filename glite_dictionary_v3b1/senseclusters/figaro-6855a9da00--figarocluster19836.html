Figaro

proper noun
witty servant character
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːɡəˌroʊ / (AmE), / ˈfiːɡərəʊ / (BrE)
a clever and resourceful servant character in French plays and operas, especially in works by Beaumarchais and Mozart

Examples

Figaro the clever servant
The famous opera by Mozart features Figaro as the central character.
Many actors have enjoyed playing the witty Figaro on stage.
In literature, Figaro is known for his clever schemes and quick thinking.
