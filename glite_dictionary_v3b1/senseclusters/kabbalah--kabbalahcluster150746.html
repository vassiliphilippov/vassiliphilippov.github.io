kabbalah

noun
Jewish mystical/esoteric tradition
Pronunciation: / kəˈbɑlə / (AmE), / kəˈbɑːlə / (BrE)
A system of Jewish mystical and esoteric teachings, often associated with secret or occult knowledge.

Examples

Kabbalah teachings
He spent years studying kabbalah, hoping to uncover its hidden meanings.
Many people are drawn to kabbalah in search of deeper spiritual understanding.
The teachings of kabbalah have influenced numerous philosophies and religions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: occultism - study of hidden spiritual or supernatural matters
