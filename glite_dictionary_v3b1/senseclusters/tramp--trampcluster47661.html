tramp

noun
promiscuous person (often derogatory)
8.4
Pronunciation: / træmp / (AmE), / tramp / (BrE)
a person, typically a woman, who is considered promiscuous or engages in casual sexual relationships

Examples

Call her a tramp
He apologized for calling his ex-girlfriend a tramp during their argument.
Some people unfairly called her a tramp because of her lifestyle.
The movie challenges the stereotype of the so-called tramp and explores her personal story.

Related Terms

  • antonym: prude - a person who is very proper or modest, especially about sex
  • synonym: slut - a woman who has many casual sexual partners
