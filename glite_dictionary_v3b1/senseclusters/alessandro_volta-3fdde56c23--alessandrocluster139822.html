Alessandro Volta

proper noun
noted Italian physicist, inventor of the voltaic pile
6.2
Pronunciation: /æl.ə.ˈsæn.drə ˈvoʊl.tə/ /; / /ɑː.le.ˈsɑːn.droʊ ˈvoʊl.tə/ (AmE), /æl.ə.ˈsæn.drə ˈvəʊl.tə/ /; / /ɑː.le.ˈsɑːn.drəʊ ˈvəʊl.tə/ (BrE)
(1745–1827) Italian physicist known for his pioneering work in electricity and for inventing the voltaic pile, after whom the volt is named.
