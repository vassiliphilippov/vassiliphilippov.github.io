Alessandro Volta

proper noun
noted Italian physicist, inventor of the voltaic pile
Pronunciation: / əˌlɛːsɑnˈdroʊ ˈvoʊltə / (AmE), / ˌalɛˈsan.droʊ ˈvɒltə / (BrE)
(1745–1827) Italian physicist known for his pioneering work in electricity and for inventing the voltaic pile, after whom the volt is named.

Examples

Alessandro Volta inventor
Alessandro Volta's discoveries helped lay the foundation for modern electrical science.
During his experiments, Alessandro Volta invented the first chemical battery.
The volt, a unit of electric potential, is named after Alessandro Volta.
