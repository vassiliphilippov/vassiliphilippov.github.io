claim

noun
assertion of something as true
4.9 2.8
Pronunciation: /kleɪm/ (AmE), /kleɪm/ (BrE)

A claim of truth

She made a claim about seeing a rare bird in her backyard.
The company issued a claim that their product was the safest on the market.
