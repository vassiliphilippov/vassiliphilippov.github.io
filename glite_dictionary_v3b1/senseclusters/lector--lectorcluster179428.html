lector

noun
church official who reads scripture in services
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛktər / (AmE), / ˈlɛktə / (BrE)
a person who reads portions of scripture during a church service, especially one holding the minor order of reader in the Roman Catholic Church

Examples

Church lector
In some parishes, the lector is responsible for both readings and leading prayers.
She was appointed lector and now regularly reads Scripture in the church service.
The lector approached the lectern to read the first lesson during the Sunday Mass.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reader - a person who reads aloud to others, especially in a church
