hard to overlook

adjective
difficult to ignore or disregard
Pronunciation: / hɑrd tə oʊvɚˈlʊk / (AmE), / hɑːd tə əʊvəˈlʊk / (BrE)
difficult to ignore or not notice; impossible or nearly impossible to disregard.

Examples

Hard to overlook a problem
Her strong accent makes her presence in the meeting hard to overlook.
The evidence against him was hard to overlook, even for his defenders.
The mural’s bright colors are hard to overlook as you walk down the street.

Related Terms

  • antonym: easy to overlook - not difficult to ignore; easily missed or disregarded
  • synonym: obvious - easily seen or perceived; clear and apparent
  • synonym: unmissable - impossible to ignore or not notice
