basketmaking

noun
craft of making baskets
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbæskɪtˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪtˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the craft or activity of making baskets by hand, often using natural materials such as reeds or willow

Examples

Basketmaking with reeds
She has been practicing basketmaking for over ten years and sells her work at local markets.
Traditional basketmaking techniques are passed down through generations in some communities.
The art class offered a workshop on basketmaking using natural fibers and simple tools.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pottery - the craft of making objects from clay
  • synonym: weaving - the craft of interlacing materials to make objects
