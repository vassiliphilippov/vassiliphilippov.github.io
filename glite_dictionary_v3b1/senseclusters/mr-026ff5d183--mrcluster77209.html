Mr.

noun
title for a man
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪstər / (AmE), / ˈmɪstə / (BrE)
a formal title used before a man's surname or full name as a polite or respectful form of address

Examples

Mr. Smith
Mr. Smith will be leading the meeting today.
Please welcome Mr. Johnson to the stage.
The letter was addressed to Mr. James Turner.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Mrs. - title for a married woman
  • antonym: Ms. - title for a woman, regardless of marital status
