round-nose

noun
bullet with rounded tip
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊnd ˌnoʊz / (AmE), / ˈraʊnd ˌnəʊz / (BrE)
a bullet or projectile with a rounded, non-pointed tip, designed to reduce penetration or ricochet

Examples

Round-nose bullet
The police found a box of round-nose bullets at the scene of the investigation.
Many older rifles are designed to use round-nose ammunition for safety reasons.
Hunters sometimes prefer round-nose bullets to minimize the risk of over-penetration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: full metal jacket - a bullet with a soft core encased in a shell of harder metal
  • antonym: hollow-point - a bullet with a hollowed-out tip designed to expand on impact
