blog

noun
an online journal or website with regular posts
2.7 146/m
Pronunciation: / blÉ‘g / (AmE), / blÉ’g / (BrE)
a website or web page, typically maintained by an individual or group, where entries are regularly updated and displayed in chronological order, often in the style of a personal journal

Examples

A travel blog
Many people read his technology blog for expert advice and reviews.
She started a blog to share her travel adventures with friends and family.
Their cooking blog features new recipes and tips every week.

Synonyms

  • weblog
    3.3 1.17/m
    weblog is the longer, more formal name for an online journal used in writing, while blog is the short, common everyday word.
