blacklight

verb
to use ultraviolet light
Pronunciation: / ˈblækˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈblækˌlaɪt / (BrE)
to expose something to ultraviolet light in order to reveal stains, repairs, or other fluorescent marks

Examples

To blacklight a room
The detective decided to blacklight the room to search for hidden fingerprints.
Before buying the painting, the expert blacklighted it to check for any repairs.
Hotel staff often blacklight sheets to ensure they are properly cleaned.
