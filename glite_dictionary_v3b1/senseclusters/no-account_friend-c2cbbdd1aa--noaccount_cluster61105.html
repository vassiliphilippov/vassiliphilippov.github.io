no-account friend

noun
worthless or unreliable friend
Pronunciation: / ˌnoʊ əˈkaʊnt frɛnd / (AmE), / ˌnəʊ əˈkaʊnt frɛnd / (BrE)
A friend who is worthless or unreliable; someone whose friendship is of little value.

Examples

A no-account friend who lies
After what he did, I realized he was just a no-account friend.
She always ends up forgiving her no-account friend despite repeated disappointments.
You can't trust a no-account friend when you really need help.

Related Terms

  • antonym: trustworthy friend - a reliable and dependable friend
  • synonym: unreliable friend - a friend who cannot be depended on
