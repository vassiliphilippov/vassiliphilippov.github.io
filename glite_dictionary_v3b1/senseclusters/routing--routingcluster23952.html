routing

noun
wood shaping process
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈraʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of cutting, shaping, or hollowing out wood or similar material using a router tool

Examples

Wood routing
The routing of the edges gave the table a professional finish.
He spent the afternoon routing decorative grooves into the wooden panels.
Proper routing requires steady hands and the right equipment for clean results.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carving - the act of shaping wood or other material by cutting
  • synonym: planing - smoothing or shaping wood with a plane
