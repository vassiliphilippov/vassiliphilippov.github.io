stock certificate

noun
document proving share ownership
1.7
Pronunciation: / stɑk sərˈtɪfɪkət / (AmE), / stɒk səˈtɪfɪkət / (BrE)
an official document that certifies a person's ownership of shares in a corporation

Examples

A stock certificate document
He framed his grandfather's stock certificate as a piece of family history.
The company no longer issues physical stock certificates; everything is recorded digitally now.
You must present your stock certificate to transfer ownership of your shares.
