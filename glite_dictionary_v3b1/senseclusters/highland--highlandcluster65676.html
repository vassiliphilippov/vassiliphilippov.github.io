highland

adjective
of or relating to high or hilly country
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈhaɪlənd / (BrE)
(of a region or area) situated in or relating to high or hilly land.

Examples

Highland scenery
She preferred hiking in highland areas because of the breathtaking views.
The highland climate is known for its cool temperatures and frequent fog.
The highland villages are famous for their traditional festivals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lowland - of or relating to low or flat country
