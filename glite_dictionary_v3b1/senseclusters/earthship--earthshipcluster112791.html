earthship

noun
sustainable house type
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrθˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈɜːθˌʃɪp / (BrE)
a type of house constructed from natural and recycled materials, designed for sustainability and energy efficiency

Examples

Eco-friendly earthship home
They decided to build an earthship to live more sustainably and reduce their carbon footprint.
The earthship uses solar panels and rainwater collection to provide energy and water for the household.
Many people are interested in earthships as an alternative to traditional housing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conventional house - a standard house built with traditional materials and methods
  • synonym: eco-house - a house designed to be environmentally friendly
