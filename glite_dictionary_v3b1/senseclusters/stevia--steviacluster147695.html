stevia

noun
a South American sweet-leafed plant
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈstiːviə / (AmE), / ˈstiːviə / (BrE)
a plant with sweet-tasting leaves, used as a natural sweetener, or the sweetener made from its leaves

Examples

Stevia sweetener
Many people use stevia as a natural sweetener in their tea and coffee.
Some gardeners grow stevia in their backyard to harvest its leaves for homemade extracts.
The leaves of the stevia plant are incredibly sweet despite having no calories.
Farmers in Paraguay have been cultivating stevia for centuries.
Many people use stevia as a sugar substitute in their coffee and tea.
The leaves of the stevia plant are naturally sweet and calorie-free.
