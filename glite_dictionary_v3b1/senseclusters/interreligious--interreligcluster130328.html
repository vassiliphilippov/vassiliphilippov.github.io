interreligious

adjective
between religions
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərɪˈlɪdʒəs / (AmE), / ˌɪntərɪˈlɪdʒəs / (BrE)
involving or relating to people, activities, or dialogue between different religions

Examples

Interreligious dialogue
The conference focused on interreligious cooperation to address global challenges.
An interreligious marriage can bring together diverse cultural traditions.
Schools sometimes organize interreligious events to promote mutual respect among students.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interfaith - involving people or groups from different religions
  • antonym: intra-religious - occurring within a single religion
