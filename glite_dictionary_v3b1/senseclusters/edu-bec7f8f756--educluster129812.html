.edu

noun
academic email domain
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɒt ˌiː diː ˈjuː / (AmE), / ˈdɒt ˌiː djuː / (BrE)
an email address or online account ending with .edu, indicating affiliation with an educational institution, especially in the United States

Examples

A .edu email address
You need a .edu email address to access the university's online library resources.
Many companies offer discounts to anyone with a .edu account.
She created a new .edu email when she started her graduate program.

Related Terms

  • synonym: .ac.uk - a UK academic email domain for universities and colleges
  • antonym: personal email - an email address not linked to an institution or organization
