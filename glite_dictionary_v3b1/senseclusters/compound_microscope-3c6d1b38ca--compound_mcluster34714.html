compound microscope

noun
microscope with two lens systems
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːm.paʊnd ˈmaɪ.krə.skoʊp/ /; / /kəmˈpaʊnd ˈmaɪ.krə.skoʊp/ (AmE), /ˈkɒm.paʊnd ˈmaɪ.krə.skəʊp/ /; / /kəmˈpaʊnd ˈmaɪ.krə.skəʊp/ (BrE)
A microscope that uses two sets of lenses, the objective and the eyepiece, to magnify small objects with transmitted light.
