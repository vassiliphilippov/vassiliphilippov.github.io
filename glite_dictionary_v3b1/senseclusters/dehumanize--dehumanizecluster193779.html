dehumanize

verb
deprive of human qualities or dignity
3.3 6.0
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈhjuː.mə.naɪz/ /; / /diː.ˈhjuː.mə.naɪz/ (AmE), /diː.ˈhjuː.mə.naɪz/ /; / /dɪ.ˈhjuː.mə.naɪz/ (BrE)
to treat a person or group as if they are not human, by removing their dignity, individuality, or warmth

Long hours and endless paperwork can quickly dehumanize even the most dedicated professionals.

The harsh conditions of the prison system can dehumanise inmates.
Years of neglect and abuse dehumanised the workers at the factory.
