jinricksha

noun
goods-carrying cart
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒɪnˈrɪkʃə / (AmE), / dʒɪnˈrɪkʃə / (BrE)
a small two-wheeled cart, usually pulled by a person, used for carrying goods or luggage rather than passengers

Examples

Goods jinricksha
They loaded the boxes onto a jinricksha for delivery to the nearby shop.
The old market still uses jinrickshas to transport fresh produce every morning.
A worker pulled a jinricksha filled with luggage through the busy street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cart - a vehicle with either two or four wheels used for transporting goods
  • synonym: rickshaw - a light two-wheeled vehicle pulled by a person, often for carrying passengers
