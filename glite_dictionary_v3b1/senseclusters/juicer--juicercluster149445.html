juicer

noun
device for extracting juice from fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuːsər / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːsə / (BrE)
a device or machine used to extract juice from fruit or vegetables

Examples

Electric juicer machine
After squeezing a dozen limes, the juicer needed a good rinse.
She used a juicer to make fresh orange juice for breakfast.

She bought a new juicer to make fresh apple and carrot drinks every morning.
The juicer was easy to clean after preparing a large batch of orange juice.
Many health enthusiasts use a juicer to add more fruits and vegetables to their diet.
