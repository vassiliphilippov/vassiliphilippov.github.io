smeary

adjective
blurred or smudged
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɪri / (AmE), / ˈsmɪəri / (BrE)
covered with smears or appearing blurred, dirty, or messy due to smudges.

Examples

Smeary fingerprints
After painting, his hands were smeary with colorful streaks.
The photograph looked smeary because the lens was dirty.
She wiped the smeary fingerprints off the glass table.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clean - free from dirt, marks, or stains
  • synonym: smudged - marked with dirty streaks or smears
