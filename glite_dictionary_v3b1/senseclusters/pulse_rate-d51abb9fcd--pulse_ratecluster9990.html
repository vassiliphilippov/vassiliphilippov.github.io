pulse rate

noun
number of heartbeats per minute
Pronunciation: / pʌls reɪt / (AmE), / pʌls reɪt / (BrE)
The number of heartbeats per minute, typically measured to assess a person's health.

Examples

A low pulse rate
A low pulse rate can indicate good physical fitness in athletes.
After running up the stairs, his pulse rate increased significantly.
The doctor checked my pulse rate before starting the examination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heart rate - the speed of the heartbeat measured by the number of contractions per minute
