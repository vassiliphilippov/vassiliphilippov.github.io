ophthalmoscopic

adjective
eye examination method
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɑfθælməˈskɑpɪk / (AmE), / ˌɒfθælməˈskɒpɪk / (BrE)
relating to the examination of the inside of the eye using an ophthalmoscope

Examples

Ophthalmoscopic eye exam
The doctor used an ophthalmoscopic technique to examine the patient's retina for signs of disease.
An ophthalmoscopic examination can help detect early symptoms of glaucoma.
During the checkup, the nurse prepared the equipment for the ophthalmoscopic assessment.

  • synonym: ophthalmic - relating to the eye or its diseases
