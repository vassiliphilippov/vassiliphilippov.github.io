(law) The formal steps or actions taken as part of a legal process; the process of conducting judicial business, especially in court.

Examples

Court proceedings
All proceedings were recorded and reviewed by the judge.
Her lawyer explained every stage of the proceedings to her in detail.
The court proceedings are scheduled to begin next Monday.

Synonyms

  • legal proceeding
    4.8 0.69/m
    a legal proceeding is a single formal court action to decide a case, while proceedings refer to the full set of court actions and records.
  • judicial proceeding
    6.3 0.33/m
    a judicial proceeding is a court process that enforces rights and laws, while proceedings describes the overall formal process of conducting court business.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: investigation - the action of investigating something, especially as part of a legal process
  • synonym: lawsuit - a claim or dispute brought to a law court for adjudication
