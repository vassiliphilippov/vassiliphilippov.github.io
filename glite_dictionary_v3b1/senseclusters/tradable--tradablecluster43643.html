tradable

adjective
can be exchanged
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪdəbəl / (AmE), / ˈtreɪdəbəl / (BrE)
able to be bought, sold, or exchanged

Examples

Tradable goods
Many commodities are tradable on international exchanges.
The company focuses on creating highly tradable products for global markets.
Some services are not tradable because they cannot be exported or imported.
Gold is a highly tradable commodity worldwide.
The company only invests in tradable securities that can be sold quickly if needed.
Some digital assets are not tradable on public exchanges due to regulations.
