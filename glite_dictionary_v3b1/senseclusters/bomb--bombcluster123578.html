bomb

verb
fail badly at something (informal)
5.1 2/m
Pronunciation: / bÉ‘m / (AmE), / bÉ’m / (BrE)
to fail badly at something, especially an exam, test, or performance

Examples

To bomb an exam

If you don't prepare, you might bomb the final exam.
