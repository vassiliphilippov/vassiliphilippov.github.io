laughably

adverb
in a ridiculous or absurd way
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlæfəbli / (AmE), / ˈlɑːfəbli / (BrE)
in a way that is so absurd or inadequate as to cause laughter

Examples

A laughably small mistake
Even the smallest mistake was laughably exaggerated by his critics.
The proposal was laughably unrealistic given the time and budget constraints.
The team was laughably outmatched in the championship game.
