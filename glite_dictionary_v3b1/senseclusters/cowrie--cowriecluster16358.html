cowrie

noun
marine mollusk with glossy shell
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊ.ri / (AmE), / ˈkaʊ.ri / (BrE)
a tropical marine mollusk of the genus Cypraea, with a glossy, brightly marked, domed shell, often used as currency or ornamentation

Examples

A cowrie shell
A delicate necklace made of tiny cowries hung around her neck.
She collected a cowrie shell from the beach during her holiday in Fiji.
The trader used cowries as currency in West Africa before coins became common.
