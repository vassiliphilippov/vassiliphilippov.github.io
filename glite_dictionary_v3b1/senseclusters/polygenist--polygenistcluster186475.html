polygenist

noun
believer in separate origins
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːlɪˈdʒɛnɪst /; / pəˈlɪdʒənɪst / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˈdʒɛnɪst /; / pɒˈlɪdʒənɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes that different human races or groups have separate origins rather than a single common origin
