reattempt

verb
to try again
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˈtɛmpt / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈtɛmpt / (BrE)
to try to do something again, especially after failing or stopping previously

Examples

To reattempt the test
After failing the driving test, he decided to reattempt it the following month.
The team will reattempt the project after reviewing their mistakes.
She gathered her courage to reattempt the difficult climb.
