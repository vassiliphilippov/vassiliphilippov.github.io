Ding Dongs

noun
foolish people (informal)
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪŋ dɔŋz / (AmE), / ˈdɪŋ dɒŋz / (BrE)
an informal and often insulting term for people who are foolish or act in a ridiculous way

Examples

Those Ding Dongs at school
Those Ding Dongs forgot to bring the tickets to the concert again.
I can't believe the Ding Dongs tried to fix the car without reading the manual.
The meeting was delayed because a couple of Ding Dongs got lost on the way.

Related Terms

  • antonym: geniuses - people with exceptional intelligence or ability
  • synonym: idiots - people who are very foolish or lack good judgment
