unuttered

adjective
not spoken or expressed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʌtɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈʌtəd / (BrE)
not spoken aloud; not expressed in words or made explicit

Examples

An unuttered apology
He left the room in silence, his apology still unuttered.
Her disappointment remained unuttered, though her eyes revealed everything.
There were many unuttered thoughts during the tense negotiation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: explicit - stated clearly and in detail, leaving no room for confusion
  • synonym: unsaid - not spoken; left unexpressed
  • synonym: unspoken - not expressed in speech or explicitly stated
