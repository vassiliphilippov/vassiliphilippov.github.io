nonindependent

adjective
not able to stand alone
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌɪndɪˈpɛndənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌɪndɪˈpɛndənt / (BrE)
not able to stand alone as a separate word or morpheme and requiring attachment to another word in grammar

Examples

Nonindependent pronoun
In some languages, pronouns are nonindependent and must attach to verbs or nouns.
The suffix in this word is nonindependent and cannot appear by itself.
Clitics are a common example of nonindependent elements in grammar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dependent - relying on or requiring something else
  • antonym: independent - able to stand alone or function separately
