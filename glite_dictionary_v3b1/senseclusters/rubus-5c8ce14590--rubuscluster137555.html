Rubus

noun
genus of bramble plants including raspberries and blackberries
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈruːbəs / (AmE), / ˈruːbəs / (BrE)
a large genus of bramble plants in the rose family (Rosaceae) that bear aggregate berries, including raspberries, blackberries, and dewberries

Examples

Rubus bramble bushes
Gardeners often cultivate varieties of Rubus for their delicious berries.
Many wild Rubus plants can be found growing along country roadsides.
The fruits produced by Rubus species are an important food source for wildlife.
