azurine

adjective
sky-blue color
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈæʒəˌriːn / (AmE), / ˈazjʊˌriːn / (BrE)
having a bright blue or sky-blue color, like that of a clear sky

Examples

Azurine sky
The artist chose an azurine shade for the background of the painting.
Her dress was a stunning azurine that caught everyone's attention at the party.
We admired the azurine waters of the tropical lagoon under the midday sun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: azure - bright blue in color, like a clear sky
  • synonym: cerulean - deep blue in color, resembling the sky
