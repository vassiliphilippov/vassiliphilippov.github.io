concluding

adjective
ending or final
Pronunciation: / kənˈkluːdɪŋ / (AmE), / kənˈkluːdɪŋ / (BrE)
coming at or forming the end or final part of something; bringing to a close.

Examples

The concluding chapter
In the concluding chapter, the mystery is finally solved.
The speaker's concluding remarks summarized the key points of the conference.
They stood and applauded at the concluding scene of the play.
