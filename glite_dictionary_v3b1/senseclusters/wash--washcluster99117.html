Synonyms

  • clean
    1.0 65/m
    clean is a general verb for removing dirt by many methods, while wash uses water and often soap to remove dirt.
  • cleanse
    3.0 1.99/m
    cleanse sounds formal and often refers to cleaning the body or to ritual use, while wash is the everyday action with water.
