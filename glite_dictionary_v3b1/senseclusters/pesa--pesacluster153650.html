pesa

noun
East African coin
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪsə / (AmE), / ˈpeɪsə / (BrE)
a former monetary unit used in East African countries such as Kenya and Tanzania

Examples

Old pesa coin
He found an old pesa coin in the market.
Collectors often search for rare pesa coins from colonial East Africa.
The museum displayed a collection of pesa coins alongside other historical currencies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cent - a small unit of currency, often one hundredth of the main unit
  • synonym: shilling - a former unit of currency in East Africa
