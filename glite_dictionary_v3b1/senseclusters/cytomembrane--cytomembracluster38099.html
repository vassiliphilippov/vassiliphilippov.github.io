cytomembrane

noun
cell's enclosing membrane
4.4 6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪtoʊˈmɛmˌbreɪn / (AmE), / ˌsaɪtəʊˈmɛm.breɪn / (BrE)
the thin, double-layered membrane surrounding the cytoplasm of a cell, controlling the movement of substances in and out of the cell
