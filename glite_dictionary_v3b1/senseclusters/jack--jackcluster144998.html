jack

noun
playing card between ten and queen
7.0
Pronunciation: / dʒæk / (AmE), / dʒæk / (BrE)
one of four playing cards in a standard deck, each bearing a picture of a young man and ranking between the ten and the queen

Examples

Jack of hearts
He drew the jack of hearts and won the hand.
If you have a jack, queen, and king, you're almost there.
The jack is sometimes called the knave in old card games.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knave - another term for jack in cards
  • degree higher: queen - a playing card ranking just above the jack
  • degree lower: ten - a playing card ranking just below the jack
