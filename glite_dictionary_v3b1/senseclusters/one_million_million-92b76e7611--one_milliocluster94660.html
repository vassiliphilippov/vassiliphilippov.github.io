one million million

numeral
1,000,000,000,000 (one followed by twelve zeros)
Pronunciation: / wʌn mɪl.jən mɪl.jən / (AmE), / wʌn ˈmɪl.jən ˈmɪl.jən / (BrE)
the number represented as 1,000,000,000,000; in American English called a trillion, historically in British English called a billion.

Examples

A one million million number
If you stacked one million million coins, the tower would reach far into space.
Scientists estimate that the galaxy contains at least one million million stars.
The national debt has reached over one million million dollars this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trillion - the number 1,000,000,000,000 in American English
