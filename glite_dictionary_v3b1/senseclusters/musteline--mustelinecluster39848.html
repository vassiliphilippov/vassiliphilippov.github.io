musteline

noun
member of the weasel family (Mustelidae)
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌstəˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈmʌstɪˌlaɪn / (BrE)
A member of the weasel family; a mammal belonging to the family Mustelidae, such as a weasel, badger, otter, or mink.
