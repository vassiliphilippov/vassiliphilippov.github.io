unresponsively

adverb
without reacting
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈspɑːnsɪvli / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈspɒnsɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that does not react or respond to something

Examples

Acts unresponsively
The computer sat unresponsively after I clicked the button several times.
He stared unresponsively at the screen, lost in thought.
The device behaved unresponsively during the test, failing to register any commands.
She sat unresponsively while everyone else discussed the problem.
The patient lay unresponsively on the hospital bed after the accident.
He listened unresponsively to the news, showing no emotion at all.
The patient stared unresponsively at the ceiling after the accident.
He answered unresponsively when the doctor asked him simple questions.
During the meeting, she sat unresponsively, lost in her own thoughts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: actively - in a way that shows action or response
  • synonym: passively - in a way that does not show active response or resistance
  • antonym: responsively - in a way that reacts or responds quickly
  • synonym: unconsciously - in a way that lacks awareness or consciousness
  • synonym: unreactively - in a way that does not react
