spiritually

adverb
in a spiritual manner
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪrətʃuəli / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪtʃʊəli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to the human spirit or soul, as opposed to material or physical things; in a spiritual manner.

Examples

To be spiritually strong
Many people find yoga spiritually enriching as well as physically beneficial.
She described herself as not religious but deeply spiritually connected.
The ceremony was spiritually uplifting for everyone involved.

Related Terms

  • antonym: materially - in a material or physical manner
