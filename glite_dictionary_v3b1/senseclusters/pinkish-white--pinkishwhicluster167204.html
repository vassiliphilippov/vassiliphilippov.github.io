pinkish-white

adjective
white tinged with pink
4.0 2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪŋkɪʃ waɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋkɪʃ wʌɪt / (BrE)
Of a white color with a slight or pale tinge of pink.

Examples

Pinkish-white skin
His skin turned a pinkish-white after spending several hours in the cold.
She chose a pinkish-white rose for her wedding bouquet to match her dress.
The walls were painted a delicate pinkish-white that made the room feel warm and inviting.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: rosy - Having a pink or reddish color, especially in the cheeks
  • degree lower: white - The color of pure snow, milk, or paper
