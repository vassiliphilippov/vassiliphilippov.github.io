whitelist

noun
approved list
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪtˌlɪst / (AmE), / ˈwaɪtˌlɪst / (BrE)
a list of people, addresses, or items that are approved or allowed to access something

Examples

An email whitelist
Only email addresses on the whitelist can send messages to this group.
The IT department updated the whitelist to include new trusted websites.
If your application is not on the whitelist, it will be blocked by the system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: allowlist - a list of approved or permitted items or people
  • antonym: blacklist - a list of people or items that are not allowed or are blocked
