Fremontia

noun
genus of shrubs called flannelbush
Pronunciation: / friːˈmɒntiə / (AmE), / frɪˈmɒntiə /; / friːˈmɒntiə / (BrE)
a genus of flowering shrubs (flannelbush) native to western North America, known for their large yellow flowers and soft, felt-like leaves
