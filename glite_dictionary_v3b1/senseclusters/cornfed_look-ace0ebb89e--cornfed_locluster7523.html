cornfed look

noun
healthy, robust appearance
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnˌfɛd lʊk / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnfɛd lʊk / (BrE)
an appearance of being healthy, strong, and robust, often associated with rural or Midwestern upbringing

Examples

A cornfed look
After years on the farm, he had a real cornfed look about him.
Her cornfed look made her stand out in the urban crowd.
The football team was full of players with that classic cornfed look.
