undesignedly

adverb
accidentally, unintentionally
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈzaɪndli / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈzaɪndli / (BrE)
in an unplanned or accidental manner; not on purpose

Examples

Undesignedly reveal a secret

The vase was broken undesignedly when he bumped into the table.
He undesignedly insulted his friend by forgetting her birthday.
