Red Cloud

proper noun
Oglala Lakota chief and leader (1822–1909)
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛd klaʊd / (AmE), / ˈrɛd klaʊd / (BrE)
(1822–1909) A chief of the Oglala Lakota Sioux who led resistance against the United States government's expansion into Native American territory in Wyoming and Montana.
