life in Mayberry

noun
nostalgic, idealized simple life (US culture)
8.9
Pronunciation: / laɪf ɪn ˈmeɪˌbɛri / (AmE), / laɪf ɪn ˈmeɪbəri / (BrE)
A nostalgic reference to a simple, peaceful, and idealized way of life, as depicted in the fictional town of Mayberry from the American television show 'The Andy Griffith Show.'

Examples

Life in Mayberry style
Despite the city's chaos, she dreamed of settling somewhere that felt like life in Mayberry.
The politician promised to restore values reminiscent of life in Mayberry.
They decorated their home to evoke the warmth and community spirit of life in Mayberry.
