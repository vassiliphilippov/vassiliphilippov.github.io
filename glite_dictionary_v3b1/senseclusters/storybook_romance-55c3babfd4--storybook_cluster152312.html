storybook romance

noun
an idealized, perfect love story
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔːriˌbʊk roʊˈmæns / (AmE), / ˈstɔːrɪbʊk rəʊˈmæns / (BrE)
a love affair or relationship that seems perfect or ideal, like those often depicted in fairy tales or novels

Examples

A storybook romance love
She always dreamed of having a storybook romance just like in her favorite movies.
Their wedding was the culmination of their storybook romance.
They met in college and began a storybook romance that lasted a lifetime.
