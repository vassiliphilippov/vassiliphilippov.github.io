outness

noun
state of being outside
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtnəs / (AmE), / ˈaʊtnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being outside or external to something

Examples

Physical outness
The outness of the sculpture made it stand out in the open plaza.
Designers often play with outness and insideness to create unique architectural spaces.
The artist wanted to capture the feeling of outness in her landscape paintings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: externality - the state of being external or outside
  • antonym: insideness - the quality or state of being inside
