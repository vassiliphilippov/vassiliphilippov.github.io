decomposition

noun
separation of a substance into simpler parts (chemistry)

(Chemistry) the process of separating a substance into two or more simpler substances, which may differ from each other and from the original.

Examples

Chemical decomposition
Rapid decomposition can be observed when hydrogen peroxide is exposed to light and heat.
The decomposition of water into hydrogen and oxygen is an important chemical process.
Thermal decomposition of calcium carbonate produces calcium oxide and carbon dioxide.
