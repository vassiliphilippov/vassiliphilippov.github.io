GIA

noun
Algerian Islamist militant group (1990s)
Pronunciation: / dʒiː aɪ ˈeɪ / (AmE), / dʒiː aɪ ˈeɪ / (BrE)
a militant Islamist group (Armed Islamic Group of Algeria) formed in 1992 that conducted violent attacks to overthrow Algeria's secular government and establish an Islamic state

Examples

The GIA attacks
