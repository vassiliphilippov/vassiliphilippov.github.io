floater

noun
a person floating in water
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfloʊtər / (AmE), / ˈfləʊtə / (BrE)
a person or object that floats on water

Examples

Pool floater
At the lake, she was the only floater in a group of energetic swimmers.
Some people prefer being a floater and relaxing on their backs in the pool.
The lifeguard watched carefully as the floater drifted toward the deep end.
A plastic bottle can act as a makeshift floater in an emergency.
The child wore a floater on his arm while swimming in the pool.
We use a floater to mark the position of the fishing net.
