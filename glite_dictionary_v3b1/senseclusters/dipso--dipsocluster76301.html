dipso

x
relating to thirst
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪpsoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdɪpsəʊ / (BrE)
a combining form meaning 'thirst' or 'relating to abnormal thirst', used in medical terms such as dipsogenic or dipsomania

Examples

Dipso- in medical words
The prefix dipso- is found in medical terms related to thirst, such as dipsomania.
Doctors may use the combining form dipso- when describing conditions involving excessive thirst.
In medical vocabulary, dipso- helps form words that refer to abnormal thirst or drinking.
