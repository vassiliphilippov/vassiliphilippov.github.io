oralism

noun
speech-based deaf education
8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈɔːr.ə.lɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈɔː.rə.lɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
a method or belief in teaching deaf people to use speech and lip-reading instead of sign language

Examples

Oralism teaching method

Debates about oralism versus manualism have shaped deaf education for decades.
Some educators believe that oralism helps integrate students into mainstream society.
