comfort break

noun
brief pause, often to use restroom
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌmfərt breɪk / (AmE), / ˈkʌmfət breɪk / (BrE)
a short pause during an activity or event to allow people to use the restroom or have a brief rest

Examples

A comfort break
After two hours, the audience welcomed a quick comfort break.
Please let me know if you need a comfort break during the presentation.
The coach scheduled a comfort break halfway through the journey.
