shelving

noun
putting items on shelves
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛlvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɛlvɪŋ / (BrE)
the action or process of placing items onto shelves, especially in a shop, store, or warehouse

Examples

Shelving products
The new employee was trained in shelving products quickly and efficiently.
Proper shelving ensures that customers can easily find what they need in the store.
After the delivery arrived, the team focused on shelving all the new merchandise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: restocking - the process of replenishing items on shelves
  • synonym: stocking - the act of filling shelves with goods
