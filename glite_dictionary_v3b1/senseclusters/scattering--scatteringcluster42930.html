scattering

noun
localized light shower
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈskæt̬ɚɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskætərɪŋ / (BrE)
a light shower of rain or snow that falls in some areas but not others nearby

Examples

A scattering of rain
There was a scattering of rain across the city this afternoon.
Tomorrow's forecast predicts a scattering of brief showers in the region.
We had only a scattering of snowflakes before the sun came out again.
