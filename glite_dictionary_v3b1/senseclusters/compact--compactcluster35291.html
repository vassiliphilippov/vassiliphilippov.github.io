compact

noun
small cosmetics case with mirror
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpækt / (AmE), / ˈkɒmpækt / (BrE)
a small, flat case containing face powder and a mirror, designed to be carried in a handbag; often used for makeup touch-ups.

Examples

A makeup compact
She always carries a compact in her purse for quick makeup fixes.
She opened her compact to check her reflection before the meeting.
The elegant gold compact was a gift from her grandmother.
