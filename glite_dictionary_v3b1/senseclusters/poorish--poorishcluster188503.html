poorish

adjective
somewhat poor
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔrɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɔːrɪʃ / (BrE)
somewhat poor or not very wealthy; having only a little money or resources

Examples

Poorish family
They grew up in a poorish area but always found ways to have fun.
Although his salary was modest, he never complained about his poorish lifestyle.
The school serves children from mostly poorish backgrounds.
