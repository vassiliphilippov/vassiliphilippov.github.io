Synonyms

  • predestination
    9.9 0.36/m
    predestination is the doctrine that God decides people's fates in advance, while predestinarianism names the belief system holding that view.
  • predetermination
    9.7 0.09/m
    predetermination is the theological claim that events, especially salvation, are fixed beforehand, while predestinarianism is the doctrine expressing that idea.
  • foreordination
    9.9 0.01/m
    foreordination is the teaching that God has predetermined every event including souls' salvation, while predestinarianism names belief in that teaching.
  • preordination
    9.9 0.01/m
    preordination is the idea that things are decided by God before they happen, while predestinarianism is the organized belief in that idea.
  • predestiny
    9.9 0.01/m
    predestiny is the state that all events are fixed by fate or a higher power, while predestinarianism is the formal doctrine that teaches this.
