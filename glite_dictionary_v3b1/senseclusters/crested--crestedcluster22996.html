crested

adjective
having a crest or tuft on the head
6.1 0.16/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆkrÉ›stÉªd / (AmE), / ËˆkrÉ›stÉªd / (BrE)
(of a bird or animal) having a tuft, crest, or plume on the head, often ornamental; sometimes used of plants with a prominent ridge or tuft.

Examples

Crested bird
Many gardeners admire the crested iris for its unique ridged petals.
The crested duck floated gracefully on the pond, its head tuft standing out.
We spotted a crested oriole in the forest, easily identified by its prominent plume.

Synonyms

  • pileated
    8.6 0.07/m
    pileated is used for birds with a large, brushlike crest on the head, while crested is a more general term.
  • topknotted
    8.2 0.00/m
    topknotted is used for animals with a small ornamental bunch of feathers on the top of the head, while crested is broader.
  • tufted
    5.5 0.07/m
    tufted has small clusters or bunches of hairs or feathers across a surface, while crested means a single prominent head tuft.

Related Terms
