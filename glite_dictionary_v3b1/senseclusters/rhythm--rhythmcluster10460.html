Synonyms

  • rhythmic pattern
    5.6 0.25/m
    rhythmic pattern is a steady, regular sequence of stressed and unstressed beats in verse, while rhythm refers to that overall flow.
  • poetic rhythm
    4.9 0.01/m
    poetic rhythm gives verse a musical flow of stressed and unstressed syllables, while rhythm names that pattern in speech or poetry.
  • meter
    6.0 3.08/m
