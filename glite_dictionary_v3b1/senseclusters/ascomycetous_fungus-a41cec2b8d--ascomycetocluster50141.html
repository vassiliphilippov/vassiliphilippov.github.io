ascomycetous fungus

noun

A fungus belonging to the class Ascomycetes (or subdivision Ascomycota), characterized by producing spores inside a sac-like structure called an ascus.

Examples

An ascomycetous fungus infection
Many plant diseases are caused by an ascomycetous fungus infecting the host.
Penicillium is a well-known ascomycetous fungus used in the production of antibiotics.
When viewed under a microscope, the ascomycetous fungus revealed its distinctive spore sacs.
