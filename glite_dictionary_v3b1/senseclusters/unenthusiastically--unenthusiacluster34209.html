unenthusiastically

adverb
in a way that lacks enthusiasm
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˌθuziˈæstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˌθjuːziˈæstɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows no enthusiasm or excitement.

Examples

Answer unenthusiastically
He answered the questions unenthusiastically, barely making eye contact with the interviewer.
She unenthusiastically agreed to help with the project, clearly lacking interest.
The students unenthusiastically completed their homework, wishing they could be outside instead.
