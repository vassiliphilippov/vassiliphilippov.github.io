Ki

proper noun
Sumerian earth goddess
9.9
Pronunciation: / kiː / (AmE), / kiː / (BrE)
In Sumerian mythology, the goddess who personifies the earth and is considered the counterpart of the Akkadian Aruru.

Examples

Ki goddess earth
In ancient myths, Ki was revered as the goddess of the earth and a source of fertility.
Scholars often compare Ki to other earth deities from different cultures.
The temple was dedicated to Ki, honoring her role in Sumerian religion.
