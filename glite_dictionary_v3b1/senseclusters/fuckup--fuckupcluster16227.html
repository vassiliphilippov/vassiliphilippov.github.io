fuckup

noun
serious or embarrassing mistake
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌkˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈfʌkˌʌp / (BrE)
a serious or embarrassing mistake; a blunder, especially one caused by carelessness or incompetence

Examples

A big fuckup
After the meeting, everyone talked about John's fuckup during the presentation.
It was a costly fuckup, but at least we learned from our mistake.
That typo in the headline was a major fuckup for the editor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blunder - a careless or stupid mistake
  • synonym: mistake - an action or judgment that is wrong
  • antonym: success - the accomplishment of an aim or purpose
