gedanken

noun
mental reflections
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈdɑːnkən / (AmE), / ɡəˈdaŋkən / (BrE)
thoughts or ideas, especially those considered in the mind rather than acted upon

Examples

Deep gedanken
She spent the afternoon lost in her gedanken, barely noticing the world around her.
His gedanken often wandered to distant places and imaginary scenarios.
During meditation, he tried to let his gedanken pass by without judgment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reflections - serious thoughts or considerations
  • synonym: thoughts - ideas or mental processes
