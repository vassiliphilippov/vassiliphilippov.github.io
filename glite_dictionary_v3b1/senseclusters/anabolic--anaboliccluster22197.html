anabolic

adjective
relating to metabolic synthesis
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.əˈbɑ.lɪk / (AmE), / ˌæn.əˈbɒ.lɪk /; / ˌæn.əˈbɒl.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to the process in living things where complex molecules are built from simpler ones (as part of metabolism)

Examples

Anabolic steroids
Athletes sometimes take anabolic steroids to increase muscle mass, despite the health risks.
Insulin is considered an anabolic hormone because it stimulates the synthesis of proteins and fats.
The anabolic process builds complex molecules from simpler ones within the body.
During adolescence, the body undergoes rapid anabolic growth.
The use of anabolic steroids is banned in most professional sports.
This supplement claims to have anabolic effects that aid muscle recovery.
