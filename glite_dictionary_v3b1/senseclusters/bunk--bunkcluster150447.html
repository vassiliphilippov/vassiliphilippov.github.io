bunk

noun
long feeding trough for cattle
7.6
Pronunciation: / bʌŋk / (AmE), / bʌŋk / (BrE)
A long trough from which cattle or other livestock are fed.

Examples

Cattle bunk feeder
A wooden bunk can hold enough grain for a whole herd.
Cattle gathered around the bunk as feeding time approached.
The farmer filled the bunk with fresh hay every morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trough - a long, narrow receptacle for animals to eat or drink from
