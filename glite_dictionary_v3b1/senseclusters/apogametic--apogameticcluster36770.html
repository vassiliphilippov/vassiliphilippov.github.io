apogametic

adjective
development without fertilization
Pronunciation: / ˌæpəɡəˈmɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌapəɡəˈmɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to the development of an embryo without fertilization.

Examples

Apogametic reproduction
Research on apogametic reproduction has advanced our understanding of plant developmental biology.
Some ferns reproduce through an apogametic process, bypassing the need for fertilization.
The apogametic nature of these plants allows them to maintain genetic consistency across generations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parthenogenetic - relating to reproduction from an ovum without fertilization
  • antonym: sexual - involving fertilization or union of gametes
