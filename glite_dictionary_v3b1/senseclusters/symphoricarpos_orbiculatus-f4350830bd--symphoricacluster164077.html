Symphoricarpos orbiculatus

noun
North American shrub with coral-red berries
Pronunciation: / sɪmˌfɔrɪˈkɑːrpəs ɔːrˌbɪkjəˈleɪtəs / (AmE), / sɪmˌfɒrɪˈkɑːpɒs ɔːˌbɪkjʊˈlætəs / (BrE)
