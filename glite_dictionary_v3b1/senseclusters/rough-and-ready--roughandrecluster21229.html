rough-and-ready

adjective
crude but effective
Pronunciation: / ˌrʌf ənd ˈrɛdi / (AmE), / ˌrʌf ənd ˈrɛdi / (BrE)
simple and not carefully finished, but effective for its purpose

Examples

A rough-and-ready solution
His rough-and-ready manners sometimes offend people, but he gets things done.
The solution was rough-and-ready, but it worked in an emergency.
We put together a rough-and-ready shelter with some old tarps and branches.
