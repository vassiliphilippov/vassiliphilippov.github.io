unexaggerated

adjective
not overstated
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪɡˈzædʒəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪɡˈzædʒəreɪtɪd / (BrE)
not made to seem more important, serious, or impressive than it really is

Examples

An unexaggerated story
Her unexaggerated account of the event made it more believable.
The report was praised for its unexaggerated and honest tone.
He gave an unexaggerated description of the challenges they faced.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exaggerated - made to seem more important, serious, or impressive than it really is
  • synonym: understated - presented in a way that makes something seem less important or serious
