Synonyms

  • doubtful
    3.0 2.25/m
    doubtful shows general uncertainty about facts or outcomes, while skeptical means not easily convinced and questions claims.
  • unconvinced
    5.1 1.50/m
    unconvinced refuses to accept a claim without proof, while skeptical doubts claims and asks for clear evidence.
  • doubting
    4.4 0.03/m
    doubting expresses ongoing uncertainty or lack of trust, while skeptical describes a general habit of not being easily convinced.
  • incredulous
    6.8 2.41/m
    incredulous shows clear disbelief and surprise at a claim, while skeptical is a steadier, cautious doubt about truth.
  • questioning
    3.9 0.00/m
    questioning asks polite or curious questions to learn more, while skeptical doubts claims and looks for proof before believing.
  • disbelieving
    4.7 0.22/m
    disbelieving shows a refusal to accept something as true, while skeptical is a cautious stance that questions claims.
  • unbelieving
    5.9 0.26/m
    unbelieving lacks acceptance of certain beliefs, often in religion, while skeptical doubts claims more generally and asks for evidence.
