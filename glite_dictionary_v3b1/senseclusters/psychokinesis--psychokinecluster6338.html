psychokinesis

noun
supposed power to move objects with the mind
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkoʊkɪˈniːsɪs / (AmE), / ˌsʌɪkəʊkɪˈniːsɪs / (BrE)
the supposed ability to move objects with the mind without using physical force

Examples

Psychokinesis powers
Despite extensive testing, scientists have found no evidence supporting the existence of psychokinesis in controlled conditions.
Some people claim to possess psychokinesis, allowing them to bend spoons with their thoughts.
The movie features a character with psychokinesis who can levitate objects at will.

Related Terms

  • synonym: telekinesis - the supposed ability to move things at a distance with the mind
