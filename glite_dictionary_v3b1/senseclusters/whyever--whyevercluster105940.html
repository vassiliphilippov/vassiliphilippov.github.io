whyever

adverb
emphatic question word
8.2
Pronunciation: / waɪˈɛvər / (AmE), / waɪˈɛvə / (BrE)
used to emphasize surprise or confusion when asking the reason for something

Examples

Whyever ask
Whyever did you decide to walk home in the rain?
I can't imagine whyever they would cancel the event at the last minute.
Whyever would anyone refuse such a generous offer?
