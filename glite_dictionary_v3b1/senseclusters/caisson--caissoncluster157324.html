caisson

noun
two-wheeled military ammunition vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪsən /; / ˈkeɪ sɑn / (AmE), / ˈkeɪs(ə)n /; / ˈkeɪsɒn / (BrE)
a vehicle or large container used in the military to transport artillery ammunition, especially in the past

Examples
