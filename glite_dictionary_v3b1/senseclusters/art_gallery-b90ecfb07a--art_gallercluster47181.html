art gallery

noun
a place for exhibiting artworks
1.2 2.3
Pronunciation: /ɑːrt ˈgæl.ər.iː/ /; / /ɑːrt ˈgæl.ər.ɪ/ (AmE), /ɑːt ˈgæl.ə.rɪ/ /; / /ət ˈgæl.ə.rɪ/ (BrE)
a place, either a building or an online space, where works of art are displayed for the public to view and often to buy

Examples

  • synonym: online gallery - a website or platform that displays and sells artworks
  • synonym: virtual exhibition - an exhibition of artworks presented in a digital or online format
