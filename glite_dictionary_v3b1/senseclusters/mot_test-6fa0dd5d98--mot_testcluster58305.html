MOT test

noun
UK vehicle safety and emissions test
Pronunciation: / ˌɛm oʊ ˈti tɛst / (AmE), / ˌɛm əʊ ˈti tɛst / (BrE)
an annual test of vehicle safety, roadworthiness, and exhaust emissions required for most vehicles over a certain age in the UK

Examples

Book an MOT test
Every year, UK drivers must book an MOT test to keep their vehicles road legal.
My car failed its MOT test last week, so I need to get the brakes fixed.
She scheduled her MOT test early to avoid the winter rush.
