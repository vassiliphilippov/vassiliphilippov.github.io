reappropriation

noun
taking back a term
8.4
Pronunciation: /riː.ə.prə.ˈpriː.eɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rɪ.ə.prə.ˈpriː.eɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /riː.ə.prə.ˈprɪ.eɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rɪ.ə.prə.ˈprɪ.eɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act of taking back a word, symbol, or idea and giving it a new or different meaning, often by a group it once described negatively

Examples

Reappropriation of words
The reappropriation of certain words has helped marginalized groups reclaim their identity.
Through reappropriation, the symbol gained a positive meaning within the community.
Activists discussed the reappropriation of language as a tool for empowerment.

  • synonym: reclamation - the process of claiming something back or reasserting a right
