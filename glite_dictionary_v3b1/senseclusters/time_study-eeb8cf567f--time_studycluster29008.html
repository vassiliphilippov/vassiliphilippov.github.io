time study

noun
analysis of work to maximize efficiency
4.0
Pronunciation: / taɪm ˈstʌdi / (AmE), / taɪm ˈstʌdi / (BrE)
a detailed analysis of a specific job or process to identify the most efficient method by measuring the time and effort required

Examples

A detailed time study
A detailed time study revealed unnecessary steps in the packaging process.
After reviewing the time study, management proposed changes to improve productivity.
The manufacturing company conducted a time study to streamline its assembly line operations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: motion study - analysis of the physical movements in a job to improve efficiency
  • synonym: work study - analysis of work methods and productivity, often including time and motion study
