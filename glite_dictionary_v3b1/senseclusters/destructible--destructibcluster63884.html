destructible

adjective
able to be destroyed
3.2
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstrʌktəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈstrʌktɪbəl / (BrE)
Able to be destroyed; easily damaged or ruined.

Examples

Destructible objects
Some toys are intentionally designed to be destructible for safety reasons.
The ancient ruins consisted mainly of destructible materials like wood and clay.
The package was made from destructible paper, which tore easily during shipping.
