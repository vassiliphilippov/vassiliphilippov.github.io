berlin

noun
limousine or carriage with a glass partition
9.9
Pronunciation: / bərˈlɪn / (AmE), / bəˈlɪn / (BrE)
a type of limousine or carriage with a glass partition between the front and back seats, originally designed for comfort and privacy
