Falconiformes

noun
order of diurnal birds of prey
9.9
Pronunciation: /fæl.kə.ˈnɪ.fɑːr.miːz/ /; / /fæl.kə.ˈnɪ.fɔːr.miːz/ (AmE), /fæl.kə.ˈnɪ.fɔː.miːz/ (BrE)
