sincerely yours

adverb
standard closing for formal letters
5.4 1.9
Pronunciation: /sɪn.ˈsɪr.liː jʊrz/ /; / /sɪn.ˈsɪr.liː jɔːrz/ /; / /sɪn.ˈsɪr.liː jərz/ (AmE), /sɪn.ˈsɪə.liː jɔːz/ /; / /sɪn.ˈsɪə.liː jʊəz/ /; / /sɪn.ˈsɪə.liː jəz/ (BrE)
a standard formula used to politely close a formal letter, especially when the recipient's name is known to the sender

Examples

Sincerely yours closing
In formal letters, it is common to use sincerely yours as a closing.
Please let me know if you have any questions.Sincerely yours, John Smith.
She ended the letter with sincerely yours, as she always did in her business correspondence.
