yow

verb
to cry out loudly
9.9
Pronunciation: / jaʊ / (AmE), / jaʊ / (BrE)
to make a loud, sharp cry or wail, especially from pain, surprise, or distress

Examples

To yow in pain
The cat began to yow when it got its tail caught in the door.
He could not help but yow in pain after stubbing his toe on the table.
Startled by the sudden noise, she let out a loud yow that echoed through the house.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shriek - to make a high-pitched, loud cry
  • antonym: whisper - to speak very softly or quietly
  • synonym: yowl - to make a long, loud, mournful cry
