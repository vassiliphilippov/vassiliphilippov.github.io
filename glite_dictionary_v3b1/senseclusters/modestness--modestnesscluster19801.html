modestness

noun
moderation in price or expenditure
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑdɪstnəs / (AmE), / ˈmɒdɪstnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being moderate, especially in cost, size, or expenditure; not excessive or extravagant

Examples

The modestness of the house
She appreciated the modestness of his lifestyle despite his considerable wealth.
The modestness of the hotel rates made it popular among travelers on a budget.
The modestness of their wedding arrangements reflected their desire to avoid extravagance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extravagance - excessive or unrestrained spending
  • synonym: moderation - the avoidance of excess or extremes
