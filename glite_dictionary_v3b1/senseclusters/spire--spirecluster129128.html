spire

verb
to breathe (archaic)
9.9
Pronunciation: / spaɪr / (AmE), / spaɪə / (BrE)
to breathe or exhale, especially in a gentle or poetic sense (archaic).

Examples

To spire a breath

The poet wrote of lovers who spire their hopes into the night air.
He spired deeply, as if drawing strength from the morning breeze.
