drop

noun
an act of dropping something
Pronunciation: / drɑp / (AmE), / drɒp / (BrE)
a sudden downward movement or fall from a higher to a lower position

Examples

A sudden drop
After a quick drop at the designated point, the team drove away swiftly.
The drop of supplies made a huge difference to the stranded hikers.
The package was damaged during the drop from the drone.
Paratroopers train to land safely after a swift drop from the plane.
The climbers were careful to avoid a dangerous drop near the trail's edge.
The package was damaged after a drop from the delivery truck.
