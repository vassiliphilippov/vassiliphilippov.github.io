Entamoeba

proper noun
protozoan genus
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛntəˈmiːbə / (AmE), / ˌɛntəˈmiːbə / (BrE)
a genus of amoeboid protozoa, some species of which can infect humans and cause diseases such as amoebic dysentery

Examples

Entamoeba histolytica infection
Entamoeba histolytica is known to cause severe intestinal infections in humans.
Researchers studied the life cycle of Entamoeba to better understand how it spreads disease.
Proper sanitation can help prevent the transmission of Entamoeba in affected regions.
