Terebella

noun
genus of tube-forming marine worms
9.9
Pronunciation: / terəˈbɛlə / (AmE), / tɛrəˈbɛlə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Terebellidae; a group of tube-forming marine polychaete worms with numerous filamentous tentacles.

Examples

Terebella marine worm
Terebella species are often found living in tubes they construct from sand and mucus.
Many marine biologists study Terebella because of its unique tentacles and burrowing habits.
The colorful tentacles of Terebella can sometimes be seen waving from its tube on the ocean floor.
