bottlenose dolphin

noun
a common dolphin species with a rounded forehead
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːt̬lˌnoʊz ˈdɑːlfɪn / (AmE), / ˈbɒtlˌnəʊz ˈdɒlfɪn / (BrE)
A common species of dolphin with a rounded forehead and a prominent beak, found mainly in the northern Atlantic and Mediterranean.

Examples

A bottlenose dolphin swims
A bottlenose dolphin swam alongside the boat, delighting the holidaymakers.
Researchers have studied communication patterns among bottlenose dolphins for decades.
The bottlenose dolphin is known for its intelligence and playful behavior.
