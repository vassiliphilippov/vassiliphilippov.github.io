temazepam

noun
a benzodiazepine medication for insomnia
Pronunciation: / təˈmæzəˌpæm / (AmE), / tɛˈmazɪˌpæm / (BrE)
A benzodiazepine drug used to treat insomnia, especially for people who wake frequently during the night.

Examples

Temazepam for insomnia
My doctor prescribed temazepam to help with my chronic insomnia.
Taking temazepam helped her stay asleep through the night without frequent waking.
The patient was advised to use temazepam only as needed for short-term sleep issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Restoril - Trade name for temazepam, used to treat insomnia
  • synonym: diazepam - another benzodiazepine drug used for anxiety and sleep disorders
  • degree higher: insomnia - persistent difficulty falling asleep or staying asleep
