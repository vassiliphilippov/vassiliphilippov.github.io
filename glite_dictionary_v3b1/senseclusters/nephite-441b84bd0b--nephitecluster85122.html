Nephite

proper noun
Book of Mormon people
Pronunciation: / ˈniːfaɪt / (AmE), / ˈniːfaɪt / (BrE)
a member of a people described in the Book of Mormon, believed to be descendants of Nephi

Examples

A Nephite leader
The ancient Nephite civilization is a central focus in the Book of Mormon.
According to the story, a Nephite prophet recorded important teachings on golden plates.
Many people study the history of the Nephite people as part of their faith.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Lamanite - a member of another people described in the Book of Mormon
