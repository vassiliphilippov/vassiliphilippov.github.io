disagreeable

adjective
unpleasant in manner or interaction
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsəˈɡriəbəl / (AmE), / ˌdɪsəˈɡriːəbl / (BrE)
unpleasant or unfriendly in manner; causing discomfort or dislike in social interactions

Examples

Disagreeable person
He has a disagreeable habit of interrupting people when they are speaking.
She found her new colleague to be quite disagreeable during their first meeting.
The weather turned disagreeable just as we started our picnic, forcing us indoors.
