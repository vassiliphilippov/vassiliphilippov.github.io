freestyler

noun
freestyle swimmer
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːstaɪlər / (AmE), / ˈfriːstaɪlə / (BrE)
a competitive swimmer who specializes in the freestyle stroke, especially the front crawl, in swimming competitions

Examples

Champion freestyler swimmer
The young freestyler broke the national record in the 200-meter race.
As a dedicated freestyler, he trains twice a day to improve his speed and technique.
Many Olympic champions started as junior freestylers in local swim clubs.
