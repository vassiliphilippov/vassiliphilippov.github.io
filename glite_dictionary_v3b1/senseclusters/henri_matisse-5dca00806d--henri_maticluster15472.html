Henri Matisse

proper noun
French painter, leader of fauvism
7.2
Pronunciation: / ɑnˌri mæˈtis /; / ɑnˈri mæˈtis / (AmE), / ɒ̃ˈʁi maˈtis / (BrE)
(1869–1954) A French painter and sculptor, leader of the Fauvism art movement, known for his use of color and innovative art
