bump

verb
collide with; knock against
2.6
Pronunciation: / bʌmp / (AmE), / bʌmp / (BrE)
to collide with something or someone, usually unintentionally, often causing a jolt or shock

Examples

To bump your head
Be careful not to bump your head on the low doorway.
He accidentally bumped into a stranger while walking down the hall.
The bus bumped against the curb as it turned the corner.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: crash - to collide with something violently
  • synonym: knock - to strike something with a sharp blow
  • degree lower: touch - to come into contact with lightly
