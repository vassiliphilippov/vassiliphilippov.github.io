rotor

noun
revolving bar in a distributor (auto)
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊtər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊtə / (BrE)
the rotating bar inside a distributor that directs high-voltage current to the spark plugs of an internal combustion engine

Examples

Distributor rotor
If the rotor is worn, the engine may misfire or fail to start entirely.
Regular inspection of the distributor rotor can help prevent unexpected engine problems.
The mechanic replaced the faulty rotor in the car's distributor to restore proper ignition timing.
