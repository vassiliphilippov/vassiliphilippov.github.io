salpa

noun
minute transparent floating marine tunicate
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsælpə / (AmE), / ˈsælpə / (BrE)
A small, floating, transparent marine tunicate that has an opening at each end of its body.

Examples

A salpa jellyfish
During spring, blooms of salpa can play a key role in marine food webs.
Scientists discovered a large group of salpa drifting near the ocean's surface.
The transparent body of a salpa makes it challenging to spot in the water.
