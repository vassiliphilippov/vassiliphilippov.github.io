fortifiable

adjective
able to be strengthened
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔːrtɪˌfaɪəbəl / (AmE), / ˈfɔːtɪˌfaɪəbəl / (BrE)
capable of being strengthened or supported, especially by adding evidence, encouragement, or reinforcement

Examples

Fortifiable argument
The theory is fortifiable with additional data from recent experiments.
Her position became more fortifiable after she gathered expert opinions.
A fortifiable argument is more likely to convince a skeptical audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defensible - able to be supported or justified
  • antonym: vulnerable - easily harmed or weakened; not easily defended
