grog

noun
diluted rum drink
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡrɑɡ / (AmE), / ɡrɒɡ / (BrE)
A drink consisting of rum diluted with water, sometimes served hot and sweetened.

Examples

A mug of grog
She warmed up with a mug of grog on the chilly winter night.
The sailors were given grog after a long day at sea.
Traditionally, grog was used to make the rum last longer on ships.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rum - a distilled alcoholic liquor made from sugarcane byproducts
