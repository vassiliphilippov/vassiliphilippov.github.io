coordinate

adjective
equal in rank or importance
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊ.ɔr.də.nət / (AmE), / kəʊˈɔː.dɪ.nət / (BrE)
(of things or people) having the same rank, level, or importance.

Examples

Coordinate roles
Each department in the organization has a coordinate role in decision-making.
The committee needs coordinate efforts from all its members to succeed.
Their positions in the company are coordinate, with neither one outranking the other.

  • antonym: superior - higher in rank or quality
