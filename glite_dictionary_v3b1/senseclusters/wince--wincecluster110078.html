wince

noun
an involuntary grimace of pain or distress
Pronunciation: / wɪns / (AmE), / wɪns / (BrE)
an involuntary facial expression or movement showing pain, discomfort, or distress

Examples

A painful wince
A barely noticeable wince crossed his face when he heard the bad news.
She tried to hide her wince as the nurse inserted the needle.
The player gave a wince of pain after twisting his ankle.
A sharp wince crossed her face when she touched the hot pan.
His wince did not go unnoticed by the doctor during the examination.
With every injection, a small wince appeared on the child's face.
