flap

verb
pronounce by quickly striking tongue (linguistics)
7.8
Pronunciation: / flæp / (AmE), / flæp / (BrE)
(of an alveolar sound) to pronounce by rapidly striking the tongue against the alveolar ridge.

Examples

To flap a t sound
In American English, the 't' in 'butter' is often flapped between vowels.
Some dialects of Spanish flap the 'r' sound in certain positions.
When teaching pronunciation, linguists may ask students to flap the 'd' for accuracy.
