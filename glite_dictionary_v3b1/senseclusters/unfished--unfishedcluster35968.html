unfished

adjective
not fished
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪʃt / (BrE)
not yet exploited or affected by fishing activities

Examples

An unfished river
The remote river remained unfished for decades, allowing rare species to thrive.
Many conservationists hope to protect the last unfished areas of the ocean.
Tourists were excited to explore the unfished lake, known for its abundance of fish.

Related Terms

  • antonym: overfished - depleted by excessive fishing
  • synonym: untouched - not affected or changed by people
