productionist

noun
production crew member
7.6
Pronunciation: / prəˈdʌkʃənɪst / (AmE), / prəˈdʌkʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person responsible for the practical organization, technical tasks, or management of a film, television, or theatre production

Examples

Productionist on set
The productionist made sure all the equipment was ready before the actors arrived on set.
During the live performance, the productionist handled unexpected technical issues quickly and calmly.
A skilled productionist can keep a complex theatre production running smoothly from start to finish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: director - a person who supervises the creative aspects of a film, show, or play
  • synonym: producer - a person who oversees and manages the making of a film, show, or play
