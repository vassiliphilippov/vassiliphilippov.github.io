mail slot

noun
slot in a door for delivering mail
2.5 2.4
Pronunciation: /meɪl slɑːt/ (AmE), /meɪl slɒt/ (BrE)
A narrow opening, typically in a door, through which letters and other mail are delivered.

Examples

The mail slot in door

Please check the mail slot to see if any packages arrived while we were out.
The mail carrier pushed the envelopes through the mail slot in the front door.
