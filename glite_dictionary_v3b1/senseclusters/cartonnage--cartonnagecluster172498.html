cartonnage

noun
ancient funerary material
9.9
Pronunciation: /kɑːr.ˈtɑːn.ɪdʒ/ /; / /kɑːr.ˈtɑːn.ɑːʒ/ (AmE), /kɑː.ˈtɒn.ɪdʒ/ /; / /kɑː.ˈtɒn.ɑːʒ/ (BrE)
a material made from layers of linen or papyrus (sometimes with plaster), glued together and decorated, used in ancient Egypt to make coffins, masks, and other funerary objects
