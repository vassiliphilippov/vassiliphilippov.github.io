biddie

noun
elderly woman (informal)
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪdi / (AmE), / ˈbɪdi / (BrE)
an informal or affectionate term for an elderly woman, often implying she is talkative or meddlesome

Examples

A sweet biddie
A friendly biddie waved to us from her porch every morning.
The neighborhood biddie always knows everyone's business.
At the park, a cheerful biddie fed the birds and chatted with passersby.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crone - an old woman, often with negative connotations
  • synonym: old lady - an informal term for an elderly woman
