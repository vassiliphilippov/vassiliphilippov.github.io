Abbasid

adjective
of Abbasid dynasty
Pronunciation: / əˈbæsɪd / (AmE), / əˈbæsɪd / (BrE)

Examples

Abbasid culture

Historians often compare Abbasid administration with that of earlier Islamic dynasties.
