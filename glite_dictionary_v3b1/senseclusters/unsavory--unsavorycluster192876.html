unsavory

adjective
unpleasant in smell or taste
5.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈseɪvəri / (AmE), / ʌnˈseɪvəri / (BrE)
Having an unpleasant smell or taste.

Examples

Unsavory smell
Cheese that has sat out too long often turns unsavory both in taste and smell.
The unsavory odor from the garbage drifted through the open window.
The leftovers had become unsavory and had to be thrown away.
She grimaced at the unsavoury odor coming from the garbage bin.
The refrigerator contained some unsavoury leftovers that needed to be thrown out.
The soup had an unsavoury taste that made everyone avoid it.
