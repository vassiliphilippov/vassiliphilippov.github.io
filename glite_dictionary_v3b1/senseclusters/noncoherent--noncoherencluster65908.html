noncoherent

adjective
lacking cohesion
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkoʊˈhɪrənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkəʊˈhɪərənt / (BrE)
not holding together firmly; lacking physical cohesion or strength, especially of soil, rock, or other materials

Examples

Noncoherent soil
The engineer warned that the noncoherent soil could not support the weight of the new building.
After the landslide, the hillside became noncoherent and dangerous to approach.
The sample was classified as noncoherent because it easily crumbled when touched.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coherent - holding together firmly; logically or physically connected
  • synonym: loose - not firmly held together; not tight
