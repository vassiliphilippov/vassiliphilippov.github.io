manhunt

noun
organized search for a fugitive
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmænˌhʌnt / (AmE), / ˈmænˌhʌnt / (BrE)
An organized search, especially by police, for a person believed to have committed a crime.

Examples

Police manhunt
After the robbery, a nationwide manhunt was initiated to find the suspects.
The police launched a massive manhunt to catch the escaped convict.
The story made headlines as the manhunt continued for several days.

Related Terms

  • synonym: search - an attempt to find someone or something by looking or otherwise seeking carefully
