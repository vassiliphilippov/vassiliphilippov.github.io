corpuscular

adjective
relating to corpuscles or small particles
Pronunciation: / kɔrˈpʌs.kjə.lɚ / (AmE), / kɔːˈpʌs.kjʊ.lə / (BrE)
Relating to or consisting of corpuscles, especially small particles or cells such as blood cells.

Examples

Corpuscular blood count
A corpuscular count is often part of a routine blood test.
Physicists have proposed a corpuscular theory of light, viewing light as made of particles.
The doctor explained that anemia could result from a low corpuscular volume of red blood cells.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corpuscle - a minute body or cell in an organism, especially a red or white blood cell
