notochord

noun
flexible supporting structure in vertebrate embryos
9.9 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / Ààno ät…ôÀåk…îrd / (AmE), / Ààn…ô ät…ôÀåk…îÀêd / (BrE)
a flexible rodlike structure that forms the main support of the body in the embryos of vertebrates and in some primitive chordates

Examples

During early development, the notochord provides structural support for the growing embryo.

The notochord is eventually replaced by the vertebral column in most adult vertebrates.
