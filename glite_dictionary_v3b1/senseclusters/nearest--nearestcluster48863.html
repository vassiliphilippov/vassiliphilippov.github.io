nearest

adverb
closest in distance or relation
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪɹɪst / (AmE), / ˈnɪəɹɪst / (BrE)
at or to the shortest distance from a specified point; closest in space or relation

Examples

The nearest place
Of all the contestants, he came nearest to winning the prize.
She sat in the nearest chair to the window.
This is the nearest hospital to our house.

Related Terms

  • antonym: farthest - at or to the greatest distance; most distant
  • degree lower: near - close in space, time, or relationship
