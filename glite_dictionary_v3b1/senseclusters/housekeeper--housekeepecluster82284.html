housekeeper

noun
person employed to manage household tasks
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊsˌkipər / (AmE), / ˈhaʊskiːpə / (BrE)
a person, especially a servant, employed to manage domestic tasks and the running of a household

Examples

A hotel housekeeper
As a housekeeper, Maria ensured that every room was spotless and well-organized.
The wealthy family hired a housekeeper to manage their large estate.
Working as a housekeeper requires attention to detail and a strong work ethic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: butler - a male servant in charge of the dining room or household staff
  • synonym: maid - a female domestic servant
