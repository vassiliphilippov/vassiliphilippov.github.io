narcissistic personality

noun
personality marked by self-absorption and lack of empathy
1.9
Pronunciation: / nɑːrˌsɪsɪstɪk pɚˈsɑːnəˌlɪti / (AmE), / nɑːˌsɪsɪstɪk pəˈsɒnələti / (BrE)
a personality type characterized by excessive self-love, self-centeredness, and a lack of empathy for others

Examples

Narcissistic personality disorder
Her narcissistic personality made it challenging for her to accept criticism or admit mistakes.
People with a narcissistic personality often have difficulty maintaining healthy relationships.
The therapist diagnosed him with a narcissistic personality after evaluating his behavior and attitudes toward others.

Related Terms

  • antonym: altruistic personality - a personality type characterized by concern for the well-being of others
  • synonym: egotism - excessive self-importance or self-interest
