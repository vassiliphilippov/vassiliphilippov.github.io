bailer

noun
water-removal tool
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪlər / (AmE), / ˈbeɪlə / (BrE)
a tool or person used to remove water from a boat or container

Examples

A bailer removes water
After the storm, they used a bailer to remove water from the small fishing boat.
The old wooden bailer was kept under the seat in case the canoe started leaking.

The old fisherman always kept a bailer handy in case of leaks.
