Naga language

noun
a group of languages spoken by Naga peoples
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːɡə ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈnɑːɡə ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
Any of several languages of the Kamarupan group spoken by the Naga peoples in northeastern India and western Myanmar.

Examples

Naga language varieties
Linguists have documented several endangered varieties of Naga language.
Many children in Nagaland grow up speaking a Naga language at home.
The Naga language family exhibits great diversity among its dialects.
