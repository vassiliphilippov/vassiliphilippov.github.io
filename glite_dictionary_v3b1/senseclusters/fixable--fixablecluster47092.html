fixable

adjective
able to be settled
3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪk.sə.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈfɪk.sə.bəl/ (BrE)
able to be determined, settled, or made definite, such as a time, price, or arrangement.

Examples

Fixable price
The price for the service is fixable if both parties agree on the terms.
Is the schedule for the conference fixable this week, or do we need more time?
Once everyone responds, the meeting time will be fixable and sent to all participants.

  • antonym: uncertain - not able to be firmly decided or settled
