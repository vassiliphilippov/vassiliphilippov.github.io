Ulvales

noun
order of green algae
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʌlˈveɪliːz / (AmE), / ʌlˈveɪliːz / (BrE)
An order of green algae in the class Ulvophyceae, found mostly in marine and freshwater environments.

Examples

Ulvales green algae
Many edible seaweeds, such as sea lettuce, belong to the Ulvales.
Researchers collected samples of various species in the Ulvales order for genetic analysis.
The Ulvales are widely distributed in temperate and tropical coastal waters.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: Ulva - a genus of green algae commonly known as sea lettuce, within Ulvales
  • degree higher: Ulvophyceae - a class of green algae to which Ulvales belongs
