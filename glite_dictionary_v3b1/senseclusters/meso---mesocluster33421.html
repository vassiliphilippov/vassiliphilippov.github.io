meso-

x
chemical symmetry prefix
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːsoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmiːsəʊ / (BrE)
a chemical prefix indicating a compound with stereocenters that is achiral due to an internal plane of symmetry

Examples

Meso- compound symmetry
The chemist identified the compound as a meso- form because it had an internal plane of symmetry.
In stereochemistry, the meso- prefix is used for molecules that are achiral despite having stereocenters.
A meso- compound does not rotate plane-polarized light due to its symmetry.
