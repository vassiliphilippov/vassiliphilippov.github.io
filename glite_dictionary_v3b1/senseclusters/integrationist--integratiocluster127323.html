integrationist

adjective
supporting integration
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntəˈɡreɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
favoring or relating to the policy or practice of integrating different groups, systems, or institutions

Examples

Integrationist policies
The new law introduced several integrationist measures to promote equality in schools.
Her integrationist views helped bring together people from different backgrounds.
Many activists supported integrationist policies during the civil rights movement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inclusive - including all groups or people
  • antonym: segregationist - favoring the separation of different groups
