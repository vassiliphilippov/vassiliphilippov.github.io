faultlessness

noun
the state of being flawless or perfect
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔlt.lɪs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈfɔːlt.lɪs.nɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being without fault or error.

Examples

Complete faultlessness
He strives for faultlessness in every project he undertakes.
Some critics argue that faultlessness in art can sometimes lead to a lack of soul.
The faultlessness of her performance impressed the entire audience.

Related Terms
