overabuse

noun
excessive misuse
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərəˈbjuːs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərəˈbjuːs / (BrE)
excessive or extreme use, treatment, or mistreatment of something or someone beyond reasonable limits

Examples

Overabuse of medicine
The overabuse of antibiotics can lead to drug-resistant bacteria and serious health risks.
Many experts warn against the overabuse of social media by teenagers.
Strict regulations were introduced to prevent the overabuse of natural resources in the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abuse - improper or harmful use or treatment
  • synonym: misuse - use something in the wrong way or for the wrong purpose
  • antonym: restraint - moderation or self-control in use or behavior
