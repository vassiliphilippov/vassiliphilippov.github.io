PPI

noun
pixels per inch
Pronunciation: / pi pi aɪ / (AmE), / piː piː aɪ / (BrE)
a measurement of image resolution indicating the number of pixels per inch on a display or printed image

Examples

High PPI screen
A smartphone with a higher PPI will display images more sharply and clearly.
Graphic designers often check the PPI before printing to ensure good quality.
The monitor's PPI affects how detailed photos and videos appear on the screen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: DPI - dots per inch, a measurement of printing resolution
  • synonym: resolution - the amount of detail an image holds
