gloominess

noun
having a frowning or scowling appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈɡluːminəs / (AmE), / ˈɡluːminɪs / (BrE)
The quality of having a frowning, downcast, or scowling appearance.

Examples

Gloominess in his face
Despite the gloominess of their boss, the office remained positive.
The gloominess of her face made it clear she was upset about the news.
There was a certain gloominess in his expression that worried his friends.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfulness - the quality of being noticeably happy and optimistic
  • synonym: melancholy - a feeling of deep sadness or sorrow
