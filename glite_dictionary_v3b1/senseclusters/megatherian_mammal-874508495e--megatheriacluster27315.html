megatherian mammal

noun
large extinct ground sloth
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛɡəˈθɪriən ˈmæməl / (AmE), / ˌmiːɡəˈθɪəriən ˈmaməl / (BrE)
a large extinct ground sloth of the genus Megatherium, native to South America during the Pleistocene epoch

Examples

Large megatherian mammal
Many megatherian mammals roamed South America during the Ice Age, feeding mainly on leaves.
Scientists believe the megatherian mammal became extinct around 10,000 years ago.
The fossilized remains of a megatherian mammal were discovered in the Patagonian region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ground sloth - any of several extinct, ground-dwelling sloths of the Americas
