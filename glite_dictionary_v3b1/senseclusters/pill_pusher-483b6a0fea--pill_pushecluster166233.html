pill pusher

noun
informal term for a drug-dispensing professional
4.1 7.3
Pronunciation: /pɪl ˈpʊʃ.ər/ (AmE), /pɪl ˈpʊʃ.ə/ (BrE)
an informal or sometimes derogatory term for a healthcare professional, especially a pharmacist or doctor, who dispenses or prescribes medications

Examples

A trusted pill pusher
As a doctor, she found the label pill pusher insulting to her years of medical training.
He didn't want to be seen as just a pill pusher who hands out prescriptions without caring about his patients.
Some people refer to their pharmacists as pill pushers, but that's not really fair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pharmacist - a person qualified to prepare and dispense medicinal drugs
