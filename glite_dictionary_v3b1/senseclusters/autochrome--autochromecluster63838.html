autochrome

noun
early color photograph
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔtoʊˌkroʊm / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəˌkrəʊm /; / ˈɔːtəʊkrəʊm / (BrE)
an early type of color photograph, or the process for making it, using glass plates and dyed grains
