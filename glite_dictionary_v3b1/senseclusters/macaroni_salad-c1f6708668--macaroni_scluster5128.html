macaroni salad

noun
salad with macaroni as main ingredient
1.7 1.3
Pronunciation: / ˌmækəˈroʊni ˈsæləd / (AmE), / ˌmakəˈrəʊni ˈsaləd / (BrE)
A cold salad made from cooked macaroni pasta mixed with mayonnaise and various other ingredients such as vegetables, cheese, or meat.

Examples

A bowl of macaroni salad
At the deli, I usually order a side of macaroni salad with my sandwich.
She brought a large bowl of macaroni salad to the family picnic.
The macaroni salad was creamy and full of crunchy vegetables.
