sunglow

noun
warm sunlight glow
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌɡloʊ / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌɡləʊ / (BrE)
a warm, glowing light produced by the sun, especially during sunrise or sunset

Examples

Morning sunglow
The golden sunglow made the mountains look magical in the early morning.
She watched the fields turn orange under the soft sunglow of sunset.
A gentle sunglow filled the room as the day began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sunshine - the light and warmth that come from the sun
  • synonym: twilight - the soft light in the sky just after sunset or before sunrise
