prosthetic

noun
artificial body part
Pronunciation: / prɑsˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / prɒsˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
an artificial device that replaces a missing body part, such as a limb or tooth

Examples

A prosthetic arm
After the surgery, she was able to walk again with a prosthetic.
Modern prosthetics can closely mimic the movement of real limbs.
He chose a lightweight prosthetic to help him return to sports.

Related Terms

  • synonym: implant - a device placed inside the body to replace or support a part
  • antonym: natural limb - a body part that is not artificial or replaced
  • synonym: prosthesis - an artificial device to replace a missing body part
