Vorticist

adjective
of Vorticism movement
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɔrtɪsɪst / (AmE), / ˈvɔːtɪsɪst / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of Vorticism, an early 20th-century British art and literary movement focused on abstraction and dynamism.

Examples

Vorticist art style
The museum displayed several Vorticist works that captured the energy of modern life.
Her painting style is distinctly Vorticist, with bold lines and abstract forms.
Critics praised the Vorticist influence in his latest collection of art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Futurist - relating to Futurism, an early 20th-century art movement emphasizing speed and technology
  • antonym: Impressionist - relating to Impressionism, an art movement focused on light and color
