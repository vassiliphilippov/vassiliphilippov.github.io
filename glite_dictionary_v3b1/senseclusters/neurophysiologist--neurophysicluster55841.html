neurophysiologist

noun
nervous system specialist
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊˌfɪziˈɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˌfɪziˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist or doctor who studies the functions and activities of the nervous system, often by measuring its electrical activity

Examples

A neurophysiologist studies nerves
The neurophysiologist analyzed the patient's nerve conduction results to help diagnose the condition.
After reviewing the EEG, the neurophysiologist explained the findings to the medical team.
A neurophysiologist often works closely with neurologists and surgeons in hospitals.
The neurophysiologist used advanced equipment to measure brain activity during sleep.
After years of study, she became a respected neurophysiologist at the university hospital.
A neurophysiologist can help diagnose disorders affecting the nervous system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neurologist - a doctor specializing in diseases of the nervous system
  • synonym: physiologist - a scientist who studies how living organisms function
