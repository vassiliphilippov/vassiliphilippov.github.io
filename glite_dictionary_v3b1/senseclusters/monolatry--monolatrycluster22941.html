monolatry

noun
worship of one god (others may exist)
9.7 9.7
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑː.nə.leɪ.triː/ /; / /ˈmɑː.nə.lə.triː/ (AmE), /ˈmɒn.ə.leɪ.triː/ /; / /ˈmɒn.ə.lə.triː/ (BrE)
