funny paper

noun
newspaper comics section
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌni ˌpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈfʌni ˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
a section of a newspaper that contains comic strips or humorous cartoons

Examples
