plex

x
combining form: fold

Pronunciation: / plɛks / (AmE), / plɛks / (BrE)
a combining form meaning 'fold' or 'having a specified number of parts', used in compound words like duplex or triplex

Examples

Plex in 'duplex'
The word 'duplex' uses the combining form plex to indicate two connected units.
In scientific terms, 'triplex' means threefold, with plex showing the number of parts.
Architects sometimes refer to a 'multiplex' building, where plex signifies multiple sections.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duplex - having two parts or units
  • synonym: multiplex - having many parts or units
  • synonym: triplex - having three parts or units
