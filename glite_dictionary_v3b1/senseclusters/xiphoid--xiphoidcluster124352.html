xiphoid

adjective
sword-shaped; anatomical
Pronunciation: / ˈzaɪfɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈzɪfɔɪd / (BrE)
shaped like a sword or relating to the xiphoid process at the lower end of the sternum

Examples

Xiphoid cartilage
The xiphoid cartilage is located at the lower end of the sternum.
During the examination, the doctor pressed gently on the xiphoid region.
Pain near the xiphoid area can sometimes be mistaken for heart problems.
