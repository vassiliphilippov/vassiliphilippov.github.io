needle cast

noun
disease causing premature needle loss in conifers
9.4 6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈniː.dəl kæst/ (AmE), /ˈniː.dəl kɑːst/ (BrE)
a disease of coniferous trees in which the needles turn brown and fall off prematurely, typically caused by fungal infection
