gray

adjective
having gray or white hair from age
1.6 1.0
Pronunciation: /greɪ/ (AmE), /greɪ/ (BrE)
(of a person or animal) showing the effects of age, especially by having grey or white hair.

Examples

Gray hair
His once dark beard had grown gray over the years.
She noticed her father was looking more gray than she remembered.
The old dog, gray around the muzzle, slept by the fire.
Her once dark hair had become grey over the years.
His grey temples suggested a lifetime of wisdom and experience.
The old professor, with his grey beard, was respected by all the students.
