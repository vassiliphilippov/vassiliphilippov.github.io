mail boat

noun
boat carrying mail
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪl ˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈmeɪl ˌbəʊt / (BrE)
a boat used for transporting mail, especially to islands or remote areas

Examples

During the storm, the mail boat struggled to make its regular trip across the bay.
The mail boat arrives every morning to deliver letters to the islanders.
Tourists often enjoy riding the mail boat as it travels between small coastal villages.
