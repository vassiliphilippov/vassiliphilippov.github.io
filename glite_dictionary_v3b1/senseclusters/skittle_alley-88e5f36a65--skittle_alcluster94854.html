skittle alley

noun
lane for playing skittles
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪt̬l ˌæli / (AmE), / ˈskɪtl ˌæli / (BrE)
a long, narrow lane along which a ball is rolled toward skittles (pins) in the game of skittles
