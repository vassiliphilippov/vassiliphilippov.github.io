IPO

noun
a company's first public sale of stock
4.1
Pronunciation: / aɪ pi oʊ / (AmE), / aɪ piː əʊ / (BrE)
an occasion when a company offers its shares for sale to the public for the first time

Examples

The IPO launch
After its successful IPO, the company was listed on the stock exchange.
Investors eagerly anticipated the IPO of the popular social media platform.
The tech startup raised $200 million in its IPO last year.
