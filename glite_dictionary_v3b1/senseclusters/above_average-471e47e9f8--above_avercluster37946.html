above average

adjective
higher than the typical standard
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈbʌv ˈævərɪdʒ / (AmE), / əˈbʌv ˈævərɪdʒ / (BrE)
Better than the typical level or standard; higher in quality, quantity, or ability than most others considered.

Examples

Above average skills
He was hired because his skills were considered above average in the industry.
Her test results were above average compared to the rest of her class.
This year's profits are above average for the company.

Related Terms

  • antonym: average - the usual or typical amount, level, or rate
