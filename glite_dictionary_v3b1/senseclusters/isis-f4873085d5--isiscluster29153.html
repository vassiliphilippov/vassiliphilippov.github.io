Isis

proper noun
Egyptian goddess of fertility and motherhood
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ.sɪs / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.sɪs / (BrE)
An ancient Egyptian goddess associated with fertility, motherhood, magic, and the throne; sister and wife of Osiris and mother of Horus.

Examples

Ancient goddess Isis
Priests performed rituals dedicated to Isis to ensure a fruitful harvest.
Statues of Isis often depict her holding her infant son Horus.
The ancient Egyptians worshipped Isis as the ideal mother and wife.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Horus - Egyptian god, son of Isis and Osiris
  • synonym: Osiris - Egyptian god of the afterlife, husband of Isis
