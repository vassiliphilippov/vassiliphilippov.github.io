mignon

noun
small tender meat cut
8.2
Pronunciation: / miˈnjɑn / (AmE), / miˈnjɒn / (BrE)
a small, tender cut of meat, especially from beef or pork, often served as a delicacy

Examples

Filet mignon steak
The restaurant is famous for its grilled mignon served with a rich sauce.
She ordered a mignon because she preferred a smaller, more tender piece of meat.
At the banquet, each guest received a perfectly cooked mignon on their plate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: steak - a thick slice of meat, usually beef
