traveler's check

noun
cheque for international use, payable by agents
3.5 3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtrævələrz ˌtʃɛk /; / ˈtrævələrz tʃɛk / (AmE), / ˈtræv(ə)ləz ˌtʃɛk /; / ˈtrævələz tʃɛk / (BrE)
a cheque issued by a bank or financial company, usable internationally and payable upon presentation to an authorized agent as proof of identity
