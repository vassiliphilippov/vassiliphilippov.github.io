crab

verb
move sideways like a crab
4.1
Pronunciation: / kræb / (AmE), / kræb / (BrE)
to move sideways in a hurried or awkward manner, like a crab

Examples

To crab sideways
He had to crab sideways to fit through the tight opening in the rocks.
The boatman crabbed across the dock, avoiding the puddles left by the rain.
The children began to crab along the narrow ledge, careful not to fall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sidle - to move sideways, especially unobtrusively
