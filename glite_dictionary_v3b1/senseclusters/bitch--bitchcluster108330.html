bitch

noun
informal complaint or gripe
4.6
Pronunciation: / bɪtʃ / (AmE), / bɪtʃ / (BrE)
An informal complaint or cause for objecting about something, often expressed with annoyance or dissatisfaction.

Examples

A bitch about work
He always has a bitch about how long meetings take.
Her main bitch was about the cold food served in the restaurant.
If you have a bitch about the new policy, you should take it up with management.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gripe - informal complaint or grumble
