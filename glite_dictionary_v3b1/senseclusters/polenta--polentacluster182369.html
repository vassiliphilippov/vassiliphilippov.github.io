polenta

noun
boiled cornmeal dish
Pronunciation: / poʊˈlɛntə / (AmE), / pəˈlɛntə / (BrE)
A dish made from boiled cornmeal, often served as a thick mush or allowed to set and then sliced.

Examples

A bowl of polenta
I ordered the grilled chicken with polenta on the side.
Leftover polenta can be sliced and fried for a delicious snack.
She made a creamy polenta topped with roasted vegetables for dinner.
