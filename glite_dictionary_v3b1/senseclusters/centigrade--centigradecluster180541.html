centigrade

adjective
related to the Celsius temperature scale
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛn.tɪˌɡreɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɛn.tɪˌɡreɪd / (BrE)
relating to a temperature scale in which the freezing point of water is 0 degrees and the boiling point is 100 degrees

Examples

Centigrade temperature scale
Doctors often record body temperature in centigrade units.
The temperature outside was measured at 20 centigrade degrees.
The water reached a boil at 100 degrees centigrade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Celsius - temperature scale with 0° as freezing, 100° as boiling of water
