rent

noun
payment for use of property or service
1.0 1.2
Pronunciation: /rent/ (AmE), /rent/ (BrE)
A regular payment made for the use of property, land, equipment, or a service that belongs to someone else.

Examples

Pay rent monthly
Many students struggle to afford rent in large cities.
She forgot to pay the rent on time and received a warning.
The monthly rent for his apartment went up this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lease - a contract granting use of property
  • antonym: purchase - the act of buying and owning rather than renting
