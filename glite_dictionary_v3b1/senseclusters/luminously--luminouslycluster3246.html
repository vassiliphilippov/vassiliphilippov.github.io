luminously

adverb
clearly or brilliantly
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈluːmɪnəsli / (AmE), / ˈluːmɪnəsli / (BrE)
in a way that is clear, enlightening, or intellectually brilliant, making something easier to understand.

Examples

Explained luminously
The author luminously described the scientific process, making it accessible to everyone.
She spoke luminously about the importance of education during her inspiring lecture.
The documentary presents complex issues luminously, helping viewers understand the topic with ease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clearly - in a way that is easy to understand
  • antonym: obscurely - in a way that is difficult to understand
