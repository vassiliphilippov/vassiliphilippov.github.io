Achatinella

proper noun
Hawaiian land snail genus
Pronunciation: / əˌkætɪˈnɛlə / (AmE), / əˌkætɪˈnɛlə / (BrE)
a genus of small, colorful land snails native to Hawaii, many of which are critically endangered

Examples

Achatinella snails
The Achatinella snails are famous for their beautiful shells and unique patterns.
Conservationists are working to protect the remaining Achatinella species from extinction.
Many Achatinella snails can only be found in the forests of Oahu, Hawaii.
