American watercress

noun
mat-forming perennial plant of eastern US
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈwɔtərˌkrɛs / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈwɔːtəkres / (BrE)
an aquatic plant with edible, peppery leaves, found or grown in the United States and sometimes used in salads
