upswell

noun
sudden increase
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌswɛl / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌswɛl / (BrE)
a sudden or strong increase in amount, intensity, or emotion

Examples

An upswell of support
There was an upswell of support for the new policy after the announcement.
The community felt an upswell of pride following the team's victory.
An upswell in demand led to higher prices for the product.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decline - a gradual and continuous loss or decrease
  • synonym: surge - a sudden powerful forward or upward movement or increase
