sea pancake

noun
savory seafood pancake dish
Pronunciation: / si ˈpæn.keɪk / (AmE), / siː ˈpæn.keɪk / (BrE)
A savory pancake dish made with seafood or in a seafood style.

Examples

A sea pancake dish
Local restaurants often serve sea pancakes as a popular appetizer during seafood festivals.
She ordered a sea pancake filled with crab and spring onions for lunch.
The chef's specialty was a savory sea pancake with shrimp and scallops.
