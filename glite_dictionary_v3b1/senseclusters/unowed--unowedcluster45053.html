unowed

adjective
not required as payment
7.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈoʊd / (AmE), / ʌnˈəʊd / (BrE)
not required to be paid or not due as a debt or obligation

Examples

An unowed charge
After reviewing the account, the auditor confirmed the charges were unowed and should be removed.
The customer was relieved to learn that the disputed amount was unowed after the correction.
If a fee is unowed, you should not be asked to pay it.
