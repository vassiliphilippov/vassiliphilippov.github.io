Spock

noun
logical, unemotional person
Pronunciation: / spɑk / (AmE), / spɒk / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is extremely logical and unemotional, often lacking visible emotion

Examples

A real Spock in meetings
In group discussions, he is always the Spock who keeps everyone focused on facts.
Whenever emotions run high, she becomes the team's Spock and calmly analyzes the situation.
They called him Spock because he rarely showed excitement or frustration, no matter what happened.

Related Terms

  • antonym: emotional - showing strong feelings or emotions
  • synonym: robot - a person who acts in a mechanical or unemotional way
