ureteritis

noun
inflammation of the ureter
3.7
Pronunciation: / jʊˌriːtəˈraɪtɪs / (AmE), / jʊəˌriːtəˈraɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the ureter, the duct by which urine passes from the kidney to the bladder

Examples

Chronic ureteritis
Chronic ureteritis may require long-term treatment to address recurrent infections.
Symptoms of ureteritis can include pain, fever, and difficulty urinating.
The doctor diagnosed her with ureteritis after reviewing her test results.
