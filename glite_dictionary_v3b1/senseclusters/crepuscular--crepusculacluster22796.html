crepuscular

adjective
like twilight; dim or indistinct
9.4
Pronunciation: / krəˈpʌs.kjə.lɚ / (AmE), / krɪˈpʌs.kjʊ.lə / (BrE)
relating to or resembling twilight; dim or indistinct like twilight.

Examples

Crepuscular light
A crepuscular glow spread across the landscape as the sun set.
She enjoyed the crepuscular light that made everything look softly blurred.
The park was enveloped in a crepuscular haze after dusk.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bright - giving out or reflecting a lot of light; shining
  • synonym: twilight - the soft diffused light from the sky when the sun is below the horizon
