flapjack

noun
oat snack bar
Pronunciation: / ˈflæpˌdʒæk / (AmE), / ˈflæpˌdʒæk / (BrE)
a sweet, chewy bar made from oats, butter, and syrup, commonly eaten as a snack in the UK

Examples

Oaty flapjack bar
She packed a homemade flapjack in her lunchbox for a treat.
Would you like a cup of tea and a flapjack this afternoon?
The bakery sells delicious flapjacks with raisins and honey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biscuit - a small, sweet baked good, especially in British English
  • synonym: granola bar - a snack bar made from oats and other ingredients, often sweetened
