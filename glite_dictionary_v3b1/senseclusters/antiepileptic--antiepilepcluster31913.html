antiepileptic

noun
a drug for preventing seizures
5.1 4.5
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪk / (AmE), / ˌæntiˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪk / (BrE)
a drug used to prevent or control seizures, especially in the treatment of epilepsy

Examples

An antiepileptic drug
Patients often need to take antiepileptic medications for several years.
Some antiepileptic drugs can have significant side effects.
The doctor prescribed a new antiepileptic for her seizure disorder.
