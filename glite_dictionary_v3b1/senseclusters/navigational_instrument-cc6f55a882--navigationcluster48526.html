navigational instrument

noun
device used for navigation
Pronunciation: / ˌnævɪˈɡeɪʃənəl ˈɪnstrəmənt / (AmE), / ˌnavɪˈɡeɪʃ(ə)nəl ˈɪnstrʊmənt / (BrE)
a device or tool used to determine position, direction, or course when navigating

Examples

Modern navigational instrument
Advances in technology have greatly improved the accuracy of the modern navigational instrument.
Pilots rely on several navigational instruments during a night flight.
The ship's captain checked the navigational instrument before altering course.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compass - an instrument used for determining direction
  • synonym: sextant - an instrument used to measure angular distances for navigation
