legally

adverb
according to the law

in a way that is permitted or allowed by law; according to the law

Examples

To live legally

Synonyms

  • lawfully
    2.5 2.22/m
    lawfully is used in formal legal texts to mean allowed by law, while legally is the common word for law.
  • licitly
    8.2 0.03/m
    licitly sounds old or rare and appears in formal writing about law, while legally is the common everyday word for law.
  • de jure
    9.2 0.03/m
    de jure is a formal legal phrase used to say by law in court or texts, while legally fits everyday speech.
  • legitimately
    3.7 3.85/m
    legitimately often means lawful and proper in a social or moral sense, while legally focuses strictly on what the law allows.
