Eastern silvery aster

noun
species of North American flowering plant
Pronunciation: / ˈiːstɚn ˈsɪlvəri ˈæstɚ / (AmE), / ˈiːstən ˈsɪlvəri ˈɑːstə / (BrE)
A species of flowering plant in the aster family, native to eastern North America, known for its narrow, silvery leaves and purple flowers (Symphyotrichum concolor).
