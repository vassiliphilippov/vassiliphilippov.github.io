unrememberable

adjective
not easy to recall
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈmɛmbərəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈmɛmbərəbəl / (BrE)
impossible or very difficult to remember because it is not distinctive, interesting, or important

Many details from that year are unrememberable because nothing significant happened.
