collarette

noun
ring of skin scale
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑləˈrɛt / (AmE), / ˌkɒləˈrɛt / (BrE)
a narrow ring of scale or loose skin around the edge of a lesion or rash

Examples

Skin collarette
The doctor noticed a collarette of scale forming around the healing rash.
A faint collarette was visible at the edge of the patient's skin lesion.
The presence of a collarette can help in diagnosing certain skin conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scaling - the shedding or flaking of the outer layer of skin
