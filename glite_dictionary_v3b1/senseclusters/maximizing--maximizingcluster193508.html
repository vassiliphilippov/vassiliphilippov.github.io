maximizing

adjective
making as great as possible
Pronunciation: / ˈmæksɪˌmaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmæksɪmaɪzɪŋ /; / ˈmæksɪˌmaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
making as great as possible; increasing something to its highest degree or amount.

Examples

To maximize results
Researchers are interested in maximizing the effectiveness of the new vaccine.
She focused on maximizing her productivity by organizing her time efficiently.
The company is dedicated to maximizing profits without compromising quality.
Our new strategy is aimed at maximising customer satisfaction.
She is interested in maximising the impact of her charitable work.
The company focuses on maximising its profits through innovation and efficiency.

Related Terms

  • antonym: minimising - reducing something to the lowest possible level
  • antonym: minimizing - reducing to the smallest possible amount or degree
  • synonym: optimizing - making the best or most effective use of a situation or resource
