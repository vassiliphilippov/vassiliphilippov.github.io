serve as an escort

verb
formally accompany someone
2.2
Pronunciation: / sɜːrv æz ən ˈɛskɔrt / (AmE), / sɜːv æz ən ˈɛskɔːt / (BrE)

Examples

Officers often serve as an escort during high-profile events.
