bootlace

noun
long lace for fastening boots
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːtleɪs / (AmE), / ˈbuːtleɪs / (BrE)
a long lace used to fasten boots by threading through eyelets or hooks

Examples

A bootlace for boots
A sturdy bootlace can make all the difference during a long walk.
He replaced the broken bootlace before heading out on his hike.
She knelt down to tie her bootlace tightly before riding her bike.
