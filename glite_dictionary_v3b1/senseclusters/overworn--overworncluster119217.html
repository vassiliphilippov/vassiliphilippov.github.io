overworn

adjective
worn out from use
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈwɔrn / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈwɔːn / (BrE)
worn out or used so much that it has lost its value, freshness, or effectiveness

Examples

An overworn phrase
The overworn phrase no longer impressed the audience.
He replaced his overworn shoes after years of daily use.
The sofa looked overworn and faded after so many years in the living room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fresh - new or not used before
  • synonym: worn-out - damaged or no longer useful from long use
