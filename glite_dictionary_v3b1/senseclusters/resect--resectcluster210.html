resect

verb
to surgically remove part of an organ
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsɛkt / (AmE), / rɪˈsɛkt / (BrE)
to surgically remove part of an organ or structure

Examples

To resect a tumor
After examination, they decided to resect the tumor to prevent it from spreading.
In some cases, doctors may resect part of the lung to remove cancerous tissue.
The surgeon needed to resect a portion of the colon to treat the disease.
