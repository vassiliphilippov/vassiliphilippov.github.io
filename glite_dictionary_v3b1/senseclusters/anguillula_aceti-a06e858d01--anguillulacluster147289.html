Anguillula aceti

noun
minute nematode found in fermenting vinegar
9.9
Pronunciation: / ænˌɡwɪlˈjuːlə əˈsiːtaɪ / (AmE), / æŋˌɡwɪlˈjuːlə æˈsiːtiː / (BrE)
a minute nematode (vinegar eelworm) that feeds on organisms responsible for fermentation, especially in vinegar

Examples

Anguillula aceti vinegar eelworm
Children sometimes observe Anguillula aceti in science classes to learn about microscopic life.
The presence of Anguillula aceti is used as an indicator of fermentation in vinegar production.
Under the microscope, Anguillula aceti can be seen wriggling in unfiltered vinegar.
