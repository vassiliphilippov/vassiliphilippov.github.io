unstructured

adjective
not organized by a schema (data)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstrʌktʃəd / (AmE), / ʌnˈstrʌktʃəd / (BrE)
(in information technology) not organized according to a predefined model or schema; describing data (for example text, images, audio, or video) that does not fit a fixed table-like structure.

Examples

Unstructured data
Companies often analyze unstructured data such as emails and documents.
The research team used NLP to extract insights from a large unstructured text corpus.
Cloud providers offer tools to store and search vast amounts of unstructured multimedia content.

Related Terms

  • synonym: raw data - original unprocessed data, often lacking organization or labeling.
  • antonym: structured - data organized according to a defined schema or model (e.g., rows and columns).
