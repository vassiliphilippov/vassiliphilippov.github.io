John D. Rockefeller

proper noun
American oil magnate and philanthropist (1839–1937)
4.6
Pronunciation: /dʒɑːn ˈrɑː.kə.fel.ər/ (AmE), /dʒɒn ˈrɒ.kə.fel.ə/ (BrE)
