John D. Rockefeller

proper noun
American oil magnate and philanthropist (1839–1937)
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɑn ˌdi ˌrɑkəˈfɛlər / (AmE), / ˌdʒɒn ˌdi ˌrɒkəˈfɛlə / (BrE)
(1839–1937) An American industrialist and philanthropist who founded the Standard Oil Company and became one of the world's wealthiest individuals, noted for his charitable giving.

Examples

John D. Rockefeller oil tycoon
