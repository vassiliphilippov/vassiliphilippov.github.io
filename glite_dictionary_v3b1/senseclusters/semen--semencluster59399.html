semen

noun
male reproductive fluid containing sperm
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːmən / (AmE), / ˈsiːmən / (BrE)
The thick whitish fluid produced by the male reproductive organs that contains sperm and is ejaculated during orgasm.

Examples

A semen sample
During the fertility test, the doctor analyzed the semen sample for sperm count.
It is important to understand the role of semen in the reproductive process.
The laboratory stored semen at low temperatures for artificial insemination.
