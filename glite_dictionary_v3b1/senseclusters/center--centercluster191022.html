center

verb
place in the middle
3.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɛn.tə / (BrE)
to move or place something in the middle or at an equal distance from all sides

Examples

To center the picture
Please center the painting on the wall so it looks balanced.
She carefully centered the vase on the mantelpiece before stepping back to admire her work.
The text wasn't properly centered on the page, so the invitation looked off.
