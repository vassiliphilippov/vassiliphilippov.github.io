lost in action

adjective
military person missing during action
Pronunciation: / lɔst ɪn ˈækʃən / (AmE), / lɒst ɪn ˈækʃən / (BrE)
(of a member of the armed forces) missing and unaccounted for during a military operation, with their fate unknown.

Examples

Lost in action soldier
He is commemorated on the memorial wall as one of those lost in action in World War II.
Several pilots were reported lost in action after the conflict in the Pacific.
The family received the devastating news that their son was lost in action during the battle.

Related Terms

  • antonym: killed in action - killed during a battle or military operation
  • synonym: missing in action - unaccounted for after a battle, status unknown
