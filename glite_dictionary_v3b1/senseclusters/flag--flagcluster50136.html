flag

noun
a plant with sword-shaped leaves and bright flowers
1.5
Pronunciation: / flæɡ / (AmE), / flaɡ / (BrE)
a plant with long, sword-shaped leaves and upright stems bearing brightly colored flowers, typically with three petals and three drooping sepals; especially an iris.
