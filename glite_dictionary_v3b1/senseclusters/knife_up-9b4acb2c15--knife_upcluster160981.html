knife up

verb
cut or slice with a knife
Pronunciation: / naɪf ʌp / (AmE), / naɪf ʌp / (BrE)
to cut or slice something, especially food, using a knife.

Examples

To knife up vegetables
Before cooking, remember to knife up all the necessary ingredients.
He asked his assistant to knife up some cheese for the appetizer plate.
She used a sharp blade to knife up the tomatoes for the salad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slice - to cut into slices
