jarringly

adverb
in a shocking or disturbing manner
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑr.ɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈdʒɑː.rɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a way that is shocking, disturbing, or unpleasantly discordant.

Examples

Sounds jarringly different
Her voice contrasted jarringly with the gentle music in the background.
The abrupt ending of the movie felt jarringly out of place.
The modern decor clashed jarringly with the building's historic architecture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jarring - causing shock or disturbance
  • antonym: pleasantly - in a pleasing or agreeable manner
