cracker

noun
offensive US term for poor White Southerner
Pronunciation: / ˈkrækər / (AmE), / ˈkrækə / (BrE)
(offensive, chiefly US) A disparaging term for a poor White person, especially from the southern United States.

Examples

A poor cracker
He grew up in rural Georgia where the term cracker was often used for poor White farmers.
She found the label cracker both offensive and outdated when referring to her family.
The word cracker has a complicated and controversial history in the American South.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hillbilly - a disparaging term for a poor White person from a rural area, especially the Appalachians or Ozarks
  • synonym: redneck - a derogatory term for a White working-class person, especially from the southern United States
