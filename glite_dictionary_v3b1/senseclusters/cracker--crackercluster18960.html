cracker

noun
offensive US term for poor White Southerner
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkræk.ər/ (AmE), /ˈkræk.ə/ (BrE)
(offensive, chiefly US) A disparaging term for a poor White person, especially from the southern United States.

Examples

The word cracker has a complicated and controversial history in the American South.
