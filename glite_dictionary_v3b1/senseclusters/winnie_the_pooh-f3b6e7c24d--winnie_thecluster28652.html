Winnie the Pooh

proper noun
fictional bear from A.A. Milne's stories
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪni ðə pu / (AmE), / ˈwɪni ði ˈpuː / (BrE)
A fictional teddy bear and the main character in A.A. Milne's children's stories, known for his gentle and friendly nature.

Examples

Winnie the Pooh stories
He decorated his baby's room with a Winnie the Pooh theme.
