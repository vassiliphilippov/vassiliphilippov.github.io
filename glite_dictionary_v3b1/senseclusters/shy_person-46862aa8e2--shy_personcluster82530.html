shy person

noun
a person who is shy or timid
1.3
Pronunciation: / ʃaɪ ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ʃaɪ ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who is nervous or timid in the company of others and tends to avoid social interaction

Examples

A shy person
At parties, a shy person might feel overwhelmed by the large crowds.
Being a shy person, he found it difficult to speak up in meetings.
Her teachers knew she was a shy person, so they tried to encourage her participation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extrovert - a person who is outgoing and socially confident
  • synonym: introvert - a person who tends to be quiet and reserved
