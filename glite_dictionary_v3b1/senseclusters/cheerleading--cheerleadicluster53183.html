cheerleading

noun
uncritical support
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪrˌliːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪəˌliːdɪŋ / (BrE)
enthusiastic public support or promotion of a person, idea, or policy, often without critical evaluation

Examples

Media cheerleading
The newspaper's cheerleading for the new policy ignored its possible drawbacks.
Some fans accused the commentator of cheerleading instead of offering balanced analysis.
The report was dismissed as mere cheerleading for the company's latest product.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advocacy - active support for a cause or idea
  • antonym: criticism - the act of expressing disapproval or pointing out faults
