narcissistic

adjective
excessively self-centered or self-important
Pronunciation: / nɑr.sɪˈsɪs.tɪk / (AmE), / nɑː.sɪˈsɪs.tɪk / (BrE)
Having an excessive or inflated sense of one's own importance or abilities; excessively self-centered.

Examples

Narcissistic personality
His narcissistic attitude made it difficult for him to maintain genuine friendships.
Some psychologists warn about the dangers of narcissistic behavior in modern society.
The narcissistic celebrity constantly sought admiration from fans and the media.

Related Terms

  • synonym: egotistical - excessively self-centered or self-important
  • antonym: selfless - concerned more with the needs of others than with oneself
