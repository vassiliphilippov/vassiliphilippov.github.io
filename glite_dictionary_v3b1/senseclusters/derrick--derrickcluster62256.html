derrick

noun
type of crane with a movable boom
6.2 6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈde.rɪk/ (AmE), /ˈde.rɪk/ (BrE)
A type of crane with a movable boom that is used to lift or move heavy weights, especially on ships or at construction sites.

Examples

Ship derrick
A large derrick towered over the oil field.
The ship was equipped with its own derrick for loading and unloading cargo.
The workers used a derrick to lift the steel beams into place.
