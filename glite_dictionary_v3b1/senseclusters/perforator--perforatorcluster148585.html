perforator

noun
hole-making tool or vessel
7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈpɜːr.fə.reɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈpɜː.fə.reɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a tool or device that makes holes in materials, or a blood vessel that connects two others

Examples

Paper perforator
The technician used a perforator to create evenly spaced holes in the metal sheet.
During the operation, the doctor carefully avoided damaging the perforator connecting the two veins.
A perforator is essential in many crafts that require precise hole-making.

  • synonym: punch - a tool for making holes in materials
