gelée

noun
fruit jelly dessert
9.3
Pronunciation: / ʒəˈleɪ / (AmE), / ʒəˈleɪ / (BrE)
a clear, sweet jelly made from fruit juice and sugar, often used in desserts

Examples

Fruit gelée dessert
The chef decorated the cake with a shiny layer of raspberry gelée.
For dessert, we enjoyed a refreshing lemon gelée served in small glasses.
She learned to make strawberry gelée during her cooking class in Paris.

Related Terms

  • antonym: jam - a sweet spread made from fruit and sugar, usually less clear than jelly
  • synonym: jelly - a sweet, clear spread or dessert made from fruit juice and sugar
