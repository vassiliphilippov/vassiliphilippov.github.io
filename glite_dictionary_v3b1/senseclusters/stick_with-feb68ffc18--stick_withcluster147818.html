stick with

verb
continue to follow or support
1.1 3.0
Pronunciation: /stɪk wɪð/ /; / /stɪk wɪθ/ (AmE), /stɪk wɪð/ /; / /stɪk wɪθ/ (BrE)
to continue to do, support, or stay with something or someone, especially when it is difficult

Examples
