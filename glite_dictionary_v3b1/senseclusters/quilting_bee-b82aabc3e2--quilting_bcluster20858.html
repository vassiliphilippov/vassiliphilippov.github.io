quilting bee

noun
social gathering to make quilts
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪltɪŋ bi / (AmE), / ˈkwɪltɪŋ biː / (BrE)
a social gathering at which people, traditionally women, come together to make quilts, often in a communal and festive spirit

Examples

A quilting bee gathering
At the quilting bee, participants shared stories while stitching colorful fabric squares.
My grandmother attended a quilting bee every month with her friends in the church basement.
The quilting bee was both a productive and enjoyable way for neighbors to spend time together.
