cowberry

noun
evergreen shrub with edible red berries
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈkaʊbəri / (BrE)
a low, evergreen shrub found in northern regions that produces small, tart, red edible berries; also, the berry itself
