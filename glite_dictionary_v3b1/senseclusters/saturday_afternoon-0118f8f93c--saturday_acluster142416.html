Saturday afternoon

noun
period after noon on Saturday
Pronunciation: / ˈsæ.tɚ.deɪ ˌæf.tɚˈnuːn / (AmE), / ˈsæ.tə.deɪ ˌɑːf.təˈnuːn / (BrE)
the period of time after noon and before evening on Saturday

Examples

A sunny Saturday afternoon
On a sunny Saturday afternoon, the café was full of visitors.
The museum offers workshops every Saturday afternoon.
We spent a relaxing Saturday afternoon reading in the park.
