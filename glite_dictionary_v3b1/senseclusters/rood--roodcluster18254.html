rood

noun
representation of the crucifixion cross
Pronunciation: / rud / (AmE), / ruːd / (BrE)
a representation of the cross on which Jesus Christ was crucified, especially as used in church decoration or Christian art

Examples

A rood cross
Many ancient cathedrals once displayed a rood as a central symbol of faith.
Pilgrims gathered to pray before the great rood at the altar.
The medieval church features an ornate rood above the entrance to the chancel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crucifix - a representation of a cross with Jesus on it
