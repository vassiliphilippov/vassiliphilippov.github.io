Coushatta

proper noun
Native American group
Pronunciation: / kuːˈʃɑːtə / (AmE), / kuːˈʃɑːtə / (BrE)
a Native American people of the southeastern United States, especially Louisiana, or their language

Examples

Coushatta tribe
The Coushatta have preserved many of their traditional customs and crafts.
A reservation for the Coushatta people is located in Louisiana.
She is studying the Coushatta language as part of her research on Native American cultures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Creek - a related Native American people of the southeastern United States
  • synonym: Koasati - the language spoken by the Coushatta people
