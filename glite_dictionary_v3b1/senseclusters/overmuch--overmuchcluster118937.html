overmuch

noun
excessive or undue quantity
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈmʌtʃ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈmʌtʃ / (BrE)
an excessive or greater amount than is appropriate or necessary

Examples

An overmuch of praise
An overmuch of criticism can discourage even the most dedicated students.
He blamed the project’s failure on an overmuch of optimism and too little planning.
There was an overmuch of food at the banquet, more than anyone could possibly eat.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deficiency - a lack or shortage of something needed
  • synonym: excess - an amount that is more than necessary or permitted
  • synonym: surplus - an amount of something left over; more than needed
