brazier

noun
metal container for burning charcoal or coal
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪʒər / (AmE), / ˈbreɪzɪə / (BrE)
a large metal container in which coal or charcoal is burned to keep people warm when outdoors

Examples

A brazier with charcoal
A brazier stood at the entrance, filling the air with the smell of burning charcoal.
They gathered around the brazier to warm their hands on the chilly night.
Vendors heated their food on a small brazier by the roadside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stove - an apparatus for cooking or heating with fire
