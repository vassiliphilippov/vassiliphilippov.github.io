remembering

noun
the act of recalling past experiences
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmɛm.bɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈmɛm.bər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
The act or process of recalling or retaining information or experiences from the past.

Examples

Remembering good times
Remembering the good times they shared always brought a smile to her face.
For many people, remembering important dates is not an easy task.
He spent the afternoon quietly remembering his childhood adventures with his brother.
