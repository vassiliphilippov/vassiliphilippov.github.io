upbringing

noun
the way a child is raised
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌbrɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌbrɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
the way a child is raised, including the care, education, and values they receive from their parents or guardians

Examples

They credited their positive attitudes to a happy upbringing.
Despite his privileged upbringing, he remained humble and kind.
He attributed his success to the upbringing he received from his grandparents.
Her strict upbringing shaped her work ethic and values.
