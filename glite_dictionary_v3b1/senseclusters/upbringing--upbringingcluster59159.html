Synonyms

  • bringing up
    3.5 0.98/m
    bringing up is the common phrase for caring for and teaching a child, while upbringing refers to the child's whole background of care and values.
  • raising
    2.5 0.91/m
    raising is the action of looking after and bringing up children day to day, while upbringing describes their overall family influence.
  • rearing
    3.2 2.84/m
    rearing is a formal term for the practical care and training of young people, while upbringing covers values and family background.
  • parenting
    3.0 20/m
    parenting is the everyday activity and skills of raising children, while upbringing refers to the child's full background of care and values.
  • nurture
    3.5 0.25/m
    nurture is the care and encouragement that help a child grow emotionally, while upbringing includes wider teaching, rules, and family values.
