mushiness

noun
excessive or false sentimentality
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌʃi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmʌʃɪnɪs / (BrE)
a quality of being excessively sentimental or overly emotional, especially in a way that seems insincere or false

Examples

Movie mushiness
She disliked the mushiness of romantic comedies, preferring something more realistic.
The movie's mushiness made some viewers roll their eyes in disbelief.
There was a certain mushiness in his speech that seemed insincere to many listeners.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hardness - lack of tenderness or sentimentality
  • synonym: sentimentality - excessive expression of feelings of tenderness or sadness
