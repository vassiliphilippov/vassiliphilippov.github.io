enterprisey approach

noun
overly business-like or bureaucratic method
9.9 7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈen.tər.praɪ.ziː ə.ˈproʊtʃ/ /; / /ˈen.tər.praɪ.zɪ ə.ˈproʊtʃ/ (AmE), /ˈen.tə.praɪ.ziː ə.ˈprəʊtʃ/ /; / /ˈen.tə.praɪ.zɪ ə.ˈprəʊtʃ/ (BrE)
An approach to tasks or projects marked by overly formal, bureaucratic, or business-like qualities, often perceived as unnecessarily rigid or complex.

Examples

An enterprisey approach to work
Her enterprisey approach added unnecessary meetings and paperwork to the process.
Many startups avoid an enterprisey approach to maintain their agility and creativity.
The team preferred a more flexible style instead of the usual enterprisey approach.

Related Terms

  • antonym: innovative - featuring new methods; advanced and original
