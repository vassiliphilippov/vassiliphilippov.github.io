cephalometric

adjective
head measurement
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛfələˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɛfələˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the scientific measurement of the head, especially for medical or dental purposes

Examples

Cephalometric analysis
The orthodontist used cephalometric analysis to plan the treatment.
A cephalometric X-ray helps doctors assess jaw alignment before surgery.
Researchers collected cephalometric data to study facial growth patterns in children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cranial - relating to the skull or cranium
