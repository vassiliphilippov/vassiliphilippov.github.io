pother

verb
to make someone upset or troubled
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌðər / (AmE), / ˈpɒðə / (BrE)
to make someone upset, agitated, or troubled

Examples

To pother people

Please do not pother yourself over such a small disagreement.
The children pothered their babysitter with endless questions.
