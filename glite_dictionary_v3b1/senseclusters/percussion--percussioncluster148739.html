percussion

noun
tapping the body for medical diagnosis
4.5 6.0
Pronunciation: /pər.ˈkʌ.ʃən/ (AmE), /pə.ˈkʌ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act of tapping a part of the body as a diagnostic aid to assess the condition of underlying tissues or organs

Examples

Medical percussion exam
Percussion is a basic skill taught to medical students studying physical examination.
By gently performing percussion on the abdomen, the physician detected an abnormal sound.
The doctor used percussion to check for fluid in the lungs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: auscultation - listening to sounds within the body for diagnosis
  • synonym: palpation - examination by touch for medical diagnosis
