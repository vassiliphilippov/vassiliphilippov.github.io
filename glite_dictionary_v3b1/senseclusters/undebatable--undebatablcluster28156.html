undebatable

adjective
not open to debate
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈbeɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈbeɪtəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to question or argue against; not open to debate

Examples

An undebatable fact
The scientist presented undebatable facts that convinced everyone in the room.
It is undebatable that regular exercise benefits your health.
Her dedication to the project was undebatable and inspired her colleagues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indisputable - impossible to doubt or deny
  • antonym: questionable - open to doubt or debate
