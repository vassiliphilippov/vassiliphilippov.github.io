vectorization

noun
conversion to vectors
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌvɛktərəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌvɛktəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of converting data or images into a format that uses vectors for easier computation or processing

Examples

Image vectorization
The vectorization of handwritten notes makes them easier to edit and search on a computer.
Modern graphic design software relies heavily on vectorization for creating scalable images.
Machine learning models often require the vectorization of text data before analysis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digitization - the process of converting information into a digital format
  • antonym: rasterization - the process of converting vector images into a raster or bitmap format
