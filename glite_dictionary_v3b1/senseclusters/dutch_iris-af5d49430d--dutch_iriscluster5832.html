Dutch iris

noun
a bulbous iris with showy flowers
6.6
Pronunciation: / dʌtʃ ˈaɪrɪs /; / dʌtʃ ˈaɪ.rɪs / (AmE), / dʌtʃ ˈaɪərɪs /; / dʌtʃ ˈaɪ.rɪs / (BrE)
