swaggering

adjective
boldly confident and flamboyant
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈswæɡ.ɚ.ɪŋ /; / ˈswæɡərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈswæɡ.ər.ɪŋ /; / ˈswæɡərɪŋ / (BrE)
behaving in a bold, confident, and often arrogant or showy way
