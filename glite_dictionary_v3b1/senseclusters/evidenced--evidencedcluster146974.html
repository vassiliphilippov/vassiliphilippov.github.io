evidenced

adjective
supported by evidence
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɛv.ɪ.dənst / (AmE), / ˈɛv.ɪ.dənst / (BrE)
supported by evidence; shown to be true or probable by available facts.

Examples

Evidenced by emails
His guilt was evidenced through a series of emails presented during the trial.
The witness's statement was evidenced by video footage from the scene.
Their evidenced commitment to sustainability impressed the review panel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: substantiated - supported with proof or evidence
  • antonym: unsubstantiated - not supported by evidence
