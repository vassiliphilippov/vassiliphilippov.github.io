mind

noun
attention; notice (in certain phrases)
Pronunciation: / maɪnd / (AmE), / maɪnd / (BrE)
attention or notice, especially as used in the phrases 'pay mind' or 'give mind' to something or someone.

Examples

Pay mind
Don’t give that comment any mind; it wasn’t meant for you.
He walked away without so much as a mind to her feelings.
She paid his advice no mind, choosing to do it her own way.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attention - the act of focusing notice or concentration
