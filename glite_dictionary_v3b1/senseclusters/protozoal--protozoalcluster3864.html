protozoal

adjective
relating to protozoa
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊtəˈzoʊəl / (AmE), / ˌprəʊtəˈzəʊəl / (BrE)
of or relating to protozoa, especially diseases or conditions caused by protozoa

Examples

Protozoal disease
Malaria is a classic example of a protozoal disease transmitted by mosquitoes.
Researchers are developing new drugs to treat protozoal parasites in livestock.
The doctor suspected a protozoal infection after seeing the patient's symptoms and travel history.

Related Terms
