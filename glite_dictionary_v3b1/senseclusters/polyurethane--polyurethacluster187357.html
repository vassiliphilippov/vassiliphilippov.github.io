polyurethane

noun
synthetic polymer used in foams, paints, plastics
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑliˈjʊrəˌθeɪn / (AmE), / ˌpɒliˈjʊərəˌθeɪn / (BrE)
a synthetic material (polymer) used to make things like foams, adhesives, paints, and plastics
