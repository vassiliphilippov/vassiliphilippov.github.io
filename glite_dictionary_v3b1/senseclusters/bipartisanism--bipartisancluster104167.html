bipartisanism

noun
cooperation between parties
6.9
Pronunciation: /baɪ.pɑːr.ˈtɪ.zən.ɪz.əm/ (AmE), /baɪ.pɑː.ˈtɪ.zən.ɪz.əm/ (BrE)
the practice or support of cooperation and agreement between two major political parties

Examples

Bipartisanism in politics
The new law was a result of bipartisanism in the legislature.
Many citizens hope that bipartisanism will lead to more effective government policies.
Without bipartisanism, important reforms may struggle to pass in a divided congress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bipartisanship - cooperation between two political parties
  • antonym: partisanship - strong support for one political party, often without compromise
