kinsperson

noun
a person related by kinship
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪnˌspɜrsən / (AmE), / ˈkɪnˌspɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who is related to another by blood or marriage; a family member

Examples

A kinsperson in family
As a legal next of kin, any kinsperson can provide relevant family history information.
Only one kinsperson was able to attend the family reunion because of the travel restrictions.
She contacted her closest kinsperson when she received the important news.

Related Terms

  • synonym: family member - a person belonging to the same family
  • synonym: relative - a person connected by blood or marriage
