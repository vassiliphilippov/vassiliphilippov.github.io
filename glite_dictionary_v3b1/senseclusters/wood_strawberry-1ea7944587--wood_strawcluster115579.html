A small wild strawberry (Fragaria vesca) native to Europe and parts of Asia, bearing soft, sweet red fruit.

Examples

Wild wood strawberry
In many parts of Europe, wood strawberry plants grow wild in the meadows and woods.
The flavor of a wood strawberry is often considered sweeter than that of cultivated varieties.
We found ripe wood strawberries along the forest path and picked them for dessert.
