sorrowing

adjective
sad or grieving, often due to loss
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːroʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɒrəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
Experiencing or expressing deep sadness, especially because of loss or deprivation.

Examples

The sorrowing family
After the tragedy, the sorrowing community gathered to support one another.
His poetry often depicts sorrowing figures haunted by memories of lost love.
The sorrowing mother wept silently at her child's grave.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grieving - feeling great sadness, especially because of someone's death
  • antonym: joyful - feeling or expressing great happiness
