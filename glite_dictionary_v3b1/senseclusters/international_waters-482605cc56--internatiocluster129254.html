international waters

noun
areas of sea outside any country's jurisdiction
4.7 2.9
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.ˈnæʃ.ən.əl ˈwɔː.tərz/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.ˈnæʃ.ən.əl ˈwɔː.təz/ (BrE)
the areas of the sea that are not under the jurisdiction of any country and are open to all nations

Examples

International waters area
A dispute arose when the navy intercepted a vessel in international waters.
Fishing in international waters is subject to fewer regulations than in national waters.
Ships from many countries travel through international waters without restriction.

Related Terms

  • antonym: territorial waters - the part of the sea near a country's coast under its jurisdiction
