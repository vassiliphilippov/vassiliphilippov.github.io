femicide

noun
gender-based killing
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛməˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈfiːmɪˌsaɪd / (BrE)
the killing of a woman or girl because of her gender

Examples

Femicide cases rise
Activists are raising awareness about the rise in femicide across the region.
The new law aims to prevent femicide and protect women from violence.
Statistics show that femicide is a serious issue in many countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gender-based violence - violence directed at someone because of their gender
  • synonym: homicide - the killing of one person by another
