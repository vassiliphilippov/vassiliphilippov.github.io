burpee

noun
squat-jump-push-up exercise
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːpi / (AmE), / ˈbɜːpi / (BrE)
a physical exercise consisting of a squat, a jump, and a push-up performed in sequence

Examples

Ten burpees in a row
The coach asked everyone to do ten burpees as a warm-up before the game.
She included burpees in her daily workout to improve her strength and endurance.
After a set of burpees, he was already out of breath but felt accomplished.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jumping jack - an exercise involving jumping to a position with legs spread and arms overhead
  • synonym: push-up - an exercise in which a person raises and lowers the body using the arms
