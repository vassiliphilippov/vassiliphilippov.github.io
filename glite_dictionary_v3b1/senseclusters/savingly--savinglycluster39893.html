savingly

adverb
in a redeeming way
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪvɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈseɪvɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that provides a redeeming quality or acts as a saving grace in a situation.

Examples

Savingly kind
Her strictness was savingly balanced by her kindness to the children.
The film was dull, but it was savingly funny in a few scenes.
He criticized the plan, but savingly acknowledged its potential benefits.

Related Terms

  • antonym: damningly - in a way that condemns or criticizes harshly
  • synonym: redemptively - in a way that saves or redeems
