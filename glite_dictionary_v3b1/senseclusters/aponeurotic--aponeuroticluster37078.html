aponeurotic

adjective
relating to an aponeurosis (tendinous tissue)
Pronunciation: / ˌæp.ə.nʊˈrɑː.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌap.ə.njʊəˈrɒt.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to, or resembling an aponeurosis (a flat sheet or ribbon of tendon-like tissue that connects muscles to the parts they move)

Examples

Aponeurotic tissue
Aponeuroses are tough, aponeurotic tissues critical for muscle attachment.
The surgeon carefully repaired the aponeurotic layer during the procedure.
This muscle has a broad, aponeurotic origin from the lower part of the abdomen.
