handpick

verb
to select by hand
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌpɪk / (AmE), / ˈhændˌpɪk / (BrE)
to pick or gather something carefully by hand, often selecting the best items individually

Examples

To handpick vegetables
The chef likes to handpick the freshest vegetables from the market every morning.
Volunteers were handpicked for the project based on their experience and skills.
She spent the afternoon handpicking wildflowers for the wedding decorations.
