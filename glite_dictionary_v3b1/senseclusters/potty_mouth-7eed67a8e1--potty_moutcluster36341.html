potty mouth

noun
person who uses vulgar language
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑti ˌmaʊθ / (AmE), / ˈpɒti ˌmaʊθ / (BrE)
a person who frequently uses vulgar or coarse language

Examples

A potty mouth person
Even though he's a kind person, his potty mouth sometimes offends people.
My grandmother scolded me for being a potty mouth after I swore at the table.
Teachers warned students that a potty mouth would not be tolerated in class.
