water-lily family

noun
family of aquatic plants; Nymphaeaceae
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚ ˌlɪli ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌlɪli ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of dicotyledonous aquatic plants, known for their floating leaves and fragrant, often showy flowers, including the true water lilies (family Nymphaeaceae)
