CTE

noun
degenerative brain disease
Pronunciation: / si ti i / (AmE), / siː tiː iː / (BrE)
a progressive degenerative brain disease caused by repeated head injuries, often seen in athletes and military personnel

Examples

Football CTE diagnosis
Many former football players have been diagnosed with CTE after years of repeated head injuries.
Researchers are studying the link between CTE and contact sports like boxing and American football.
Symptoms of CTE can include memory loss, confusion, and changes in behavior.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concussion - a brain injury caused by a blow to the head
  • synonym: dementia - a general term for loss of memory and other mental abilities
