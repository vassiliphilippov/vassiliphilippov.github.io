gearless

adjective
without gears
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪrləs / (AmE), / ˈɡɪələs / (BrE)
not having gears or a gear mechanism

Examples

Gearless motor
The new elevator design features a gearless motor for quieter operation.
Many modern bicycles are gearless, making them simpler to maintain.
A gearless system can reduce mechanical noise in industrial machines.

Related Terms

  • antonym: geared - having gears or a gear mechanism
