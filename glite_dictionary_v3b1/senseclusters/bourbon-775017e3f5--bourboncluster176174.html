Bourbon

noun
member of the Bourbon royal family
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːbən / (AmE), / ˈbʊəbɒn / (BrE)
a member of a European royal family that ruled France, Spain, and other countries at various times since the 16th century

Examples

Bourbon king of France
King Louis XVI was a Bourbon who ruled France until the French Revolution.
Many palaces in Europe were built by the Bourbon monarchs.
The Spanish royal family is descended from a branch of the Bourbon dynasty.
