antagonistically

adverb
in a hostile or oppositional manner
Pronunciation: / ænˌtæɡəˈnɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ænˌtæɡəˈnɪstɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows hostility, opposition, or active resistance.

Examples

To act antagonistically
Debates in the committee often proceed antagonistically, making compromise difficult.
She responded antagonistically to every suggestion he made.
The two companies competed antagonistically for control of the market.

Related Terms

  • antonym: amicably - in a friendly or peaceable manner
  • synonym: hostilely - in a hostile manner
