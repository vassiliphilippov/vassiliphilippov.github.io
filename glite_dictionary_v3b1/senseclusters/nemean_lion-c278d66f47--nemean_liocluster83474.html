Nemean lion

noun
mythological lion slain by Hercules
Pronunciation: / nɪˈmiː.ən ˈlaɪ.ən / (AmE), / nɪˈmiː.ən ˈlaɪ.ən / (BrE)
an enormous lion in Greek mythology, slain by Hercules as the first of his twelve labors

Examples

The Nemean lion myth
Legends say the Nemean lion's fur was impervious to weapons, making it an almost invincible opponent.
Many ancient vases depict Hercules battling the Nemean lion with his bare hands.
The hero Hercules faced the Nemean lion as the first of his legendary twelve labors.
