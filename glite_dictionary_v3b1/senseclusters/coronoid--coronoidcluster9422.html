coronoid

noun
bony projection
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrəˌnɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɒrəˌnɔɪd / (BrE)
a triangular bony projection on a bone, especially found on the mandible (lower jaw) or the ulna (forearm)

Examples

Coronoid of mandible
The surgeon carefully examined the coronoid before proceeding with the operation.
A fracture of the coronoid can affect the movement of the jaw or elbow.
The X-ray revealed a small chip on the patient's coronoid after the accident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: process - a projection or outgrowth of tissue from a larger body
