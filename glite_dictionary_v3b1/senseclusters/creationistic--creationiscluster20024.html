creationistic

adjective
relating to creationism
Pronunciation: / kriːˌeɪʃəˈnɪstɪk / (AmE), / kriːˌeɪʃəˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to the belief that the universe and life were created by a divine or supernatural act

Examples

Creationistic belief
The debate featured both creationistic and scientific perspectives on the origin of life.
Some textbooks include creationistic explanations alongside evolutionary theory.
Her creationistic views influenced her interpretation of natural history.

Related Terms

  • synonym: creationist - a person who believes the universe was created by a divine act
  • antonym: evolutionary - relating to the theory that species develop over time through natural selection
