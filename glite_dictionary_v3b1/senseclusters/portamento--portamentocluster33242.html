portamento

noun
pitch sliding effect
9.3
Pronunciation: /pɑːr.tə.ˈmen.toʊ/ /; / /pɔːr.tə.ˈmen.toʊ/ (AmE), /pɔː.tə.ˈmen.təʊ/ (BrE)
a feature on electronic musical instruments that makes the pitch slide smoothly from one note to another

Examples

Synthesizer portamento
The synthesizer's portamento made the melody flow smoothly between each note.
By increasing the portamento setting, the keyboardist created a dreamy, sliding sound.
Some electronic instruments allow you to adjust the portamento for different musical effects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glissando - a continuous slide upward or downward between two notes
  • antonym: staccato - a style of playing notes sharply separated from each other
