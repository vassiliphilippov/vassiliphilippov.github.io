side horse

noun
gymnastics apparatus with pommels
7.8 7.0
Pronunciation: /saɪd hɔːrs/ (AmE), /saɪd hɔːs/ (BrE)
a gymnastic apparatus consisting of a leather-covered, cylindrical body with two upright handles (pommels) near the center, supported by steel legs at each end; used for exercises in men's gymnastics

Examples

The side horse apparatus
During training, he worked to perfect his technique on the side horse.
Judges gave high marks for the clean execution of skills on the side horse.
The gymnast performed a routine on the side horse with remarkable control and balance.
