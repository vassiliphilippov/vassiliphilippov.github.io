muni

noun
city transit system
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːni / (AmE), / ˈmjuːni / (BrE)
an informal term for a city's municipal public transportation system or one of its vehicles

Examples

Take the muni downtown
She caught the muni downtown to avoid the morning traffic.
Many residents rely on the muni for their daily commute.
The muni was delayed due to unexpected roadwork on the main route.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bus - a large vehicle that carries passengers by road
  • synonym: subway - an underground urban railway system
  • antonym: taxi - a car licensed to transport passengers for a fare
