puke

noun
contemptible or despicable person (insult, slang)
Pronunciation: / pjuk / (AmE), / pjuːk / (BrE)
a person regarded as contemptible or despicable; an insulting term for someone held in very low esteem

Examples

A real puke
He called the cheating player a puke and demanded he leave the game.
Only a real puke would lie to his friends like that.
The old coach was known for calling lazy athletes 'you little pukes!'
