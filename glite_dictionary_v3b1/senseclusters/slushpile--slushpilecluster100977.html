slushpile

noun
large, disorganized stack of items
9.9 8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈslʌʃ.paɪl/ (AmE), /ˈslʌʃ.paɪl/ (BrE)
A large, disorganized accumulation of items, especially unsolicited material such as manuscripts or, figuratively, unread emails or other work.

Examples

A slushpile of manuscripts
After vacation, my inbox was a slushpile of unanswered messages.
He dreaded sorting through the slushpile that had accumulated over the weekend.
It's easy to lose track of important information in a slushpile of unread emails.
