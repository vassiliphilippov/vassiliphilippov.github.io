recognize

verb
express appreciation or gratitude
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛkəɡˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈrɛkəɡnʌɪz / (BrE)
to show appreciation or formally acknowledge someone's actions, achievements, or efforts

Examples

To recognize hard work
He was recognized for his dedication and commitment to the organization.
The company held a ceremony to recognize employees for their years of hard work.
We should recognize her contribution to the success of the project.
Every year, the foundation recognizes individuals for their dedication to education.
She felt proud when her volunteer work was finally recognized by the community.
The company will recognize employees who have made outstanding contributions.
He felt his efforts were finally recognised by the company.
The Dean publicly recognised the team's hard work during the ceremony.
The school decided to recognise her outstanding contributions with a special award.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acknowledge - to accept or admit the existence or truth of; to express thanks or appreciation for
  • antonym: disregard - to pay no attention to; ignore or neglect
