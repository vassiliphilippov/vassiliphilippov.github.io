Achaemenid

noun
member of Persian dynasty
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈkiːmənɪd / (AmE), / əˈkiːmənɪd / (BrE)
a person who belonged to or lived under the rule of the Achaemenid dynasty, the first Persian Empire

Examples

An Achaemenid ruler
The Achaemenid Empire was known for its vast territory and efficient administration.
An Achaemenid ruler governed a diverse population across three continents.
Artifacts from the Achaemenid period reveal a blend of different cultural influences.
