clown

noun
comic entertainer, often in a circus
Pronunciation: / klaʊn / (AmE), / klaʊn / (BrE)
a person who entertains others by behaving in a comical or silly way, often in a circus

Examples

Circus clown
He acted like a clown during the meeting, making everyone laugh.
Some people are afraid of clown performances at the circus.
The clown made the children laugh with his silly tricks and bright costume.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jester - a historical figure who entertained at royal courts by joking and clowning
