relator

noun
person who connects others
Pronunciation: / rɪˈleɪtər / (AmE), / rɪˈleɪtə / (BrE)
a person who connects or brings others together, often acting as a mediator or facilitator

Examples

A relator connects people
As a relator, he was always introducing colleagues from different departments to each other.
The team valued her skills as a relator who could build strong connections among members.
At networking events, a good relator helps people find common interests quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: connector - a person who brings people together
  • antonym: isolate - a person who keeps others apart or separate
  • synonym: mediator - a person who helps others resolve disputes or reach agreement
