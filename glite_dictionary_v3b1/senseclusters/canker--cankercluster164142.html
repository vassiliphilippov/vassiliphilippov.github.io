canker

noun
mouth or lip ulcer
Pronunciation: / ˈkæŋkər / (AmE), / ˈkaŋkə / (BrE)
A small, painful ulcer or sore in the mouth or on the lips.

Examples

A mouth canker
My dentist explained that this painful canker on my tongue should heal within a week.
She often gets a canker inside her lower lip when she is stressed.
The canker made it difficult for him to enjoy spicy foods.
