legitimate

verb
declare (a child) legally legitimate
9.5 6.1
Pronunciation: /lə.ˈdʒɪ.tə.mət/ /; / /lɪ.ˈdʒɪ.tə.mət/ (AmE), /lɪ.ˈdʒɪ.tɪ.mət/ /; / /lə.ˈdʒɪ.tɪ.mət/ (BrE)
(of a child) to declare or make legally recognized as legitimate, especially one born outside marriage.

Examples

To legitimate a child
After they married, they legitimated their firstborn child.
Historically, some children could only be legitimated by royal decree.
The couple went to court to legitimate their daughter.
