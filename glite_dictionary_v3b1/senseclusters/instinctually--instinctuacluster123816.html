instinctually

adverb
by natural instinct
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈstɪŋktʃuəli / (AmE), / ɪnˈstɪŋktʃuəli / (BrE)
in a manner guided by natural instinct rather than conscious thought or reasoning

Examples

To instinctually react
He instinctually ducked when he heard the loud noise behind him.
Many animals instinctually protect their young from danger.
She instinctually knew something was wrong even before anyone said a word.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deliberately - in a way that is done on purpose or with careful thought
  • synonym: instinctively - in a way that is based on instinct
