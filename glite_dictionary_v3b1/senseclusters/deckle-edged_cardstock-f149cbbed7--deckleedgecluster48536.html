deckle-edged cardstock

noun
cardstock with a rough decorative edge
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛkəl ɛdʒd ˈkɑrdˌstɑk / (AmE), / ˈdɛkəl ɛdʒd ˈkɑːdˌstɒk / (BrE)
cardstock that has a rough or feathered decorative edge, resembling handmade paper

Examples

Deckle-edged cardstock paper
Artists often choose deckle-edged cardstock to add texture to their handmade greeting cards.
She loved the soft, irregular borders of deckle-edged cardstock for her watercolor paintings.
The invitations were printed on deckle-edged cardstock for an elegant touch.
