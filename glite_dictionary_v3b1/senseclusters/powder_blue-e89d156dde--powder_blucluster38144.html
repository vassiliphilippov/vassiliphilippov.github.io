powder blue

noun
a pale, greyish-blue color
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊdɚ bluː / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdə bluː / (BrE)
a pale blue color that has a slight greyish tint

Examples

A powder blue shirt
His tie was a subtle shade of powder blue that matched his shirt.
She wore a beautiful dress in powder blue to the wedding.
The painter chose powder blue for the walls to create a calming effect.
