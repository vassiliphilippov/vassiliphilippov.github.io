event horizon

noun
boundary around a black hole
7.3
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvɛnt həˈraɪzn̩ / (AmE), / ɪˈvɛnt həˈraɪzn̩ / (BrE)
the boundary surrounding a black hole beyond which nothing can escape, not even light

Examples

The event horizon of a black hole
No light can return from beyond the event horizon.
Physicists debate what happens to information at the event horizon.
The spacecraft vanished once it passed the event horizon of the black hole.

Related Terms

  • synonym: black hole - a region of space with gravity so strong that nothing can escape it
