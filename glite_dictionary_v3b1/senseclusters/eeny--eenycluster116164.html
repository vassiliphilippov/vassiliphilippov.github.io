eeny

adjective
counting rhyme word
Pronunciation: / ˈiːni / (AmE), / ˈiːni / (BrE)
used as the first word in the children's counting rhyme 'eeny, meeny, miny, moe' to begin a random selection.

Examples

The eeny rhyme
The children started their game with a loud eeny, meeny, miny, moe.
She chanted eeny to help decide who would go first in the race.
Whenever they could not choose, they used the eeny rhyme to make it fair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meeny - the second word in the counting rhyme 'eeny, meeny, miny, moe'
