figuration

noun
representation by symbol or allegory
1.0 6.3
Pronunciation: /fɪ.gjə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ /; / /fɪ.gə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /fɪ.gjə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ /; / /fɪ.gə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the representation of something in a symbolic, figurative, or allegorical way, especially in art or literature

Examples
