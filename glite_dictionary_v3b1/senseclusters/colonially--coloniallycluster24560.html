colonially

adverb
in a colonial way
Pronunciation: / kəˈloʊniəli / (AmE), / kəˈləʊniəli / (BrE)

Examples

Ruled colonially

Many local traditions were suppressed colonially during the period of foreign rule.
The country was governed colonially for over a century.
