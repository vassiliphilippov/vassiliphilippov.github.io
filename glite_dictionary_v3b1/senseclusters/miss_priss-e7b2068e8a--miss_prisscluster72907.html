Miss Priss

noun
overly proper or prim person
8.2
Pronunciation: / mɪs prɪs / (AmE), / mɪs prɪs / (BrE)
a person, especially a woman or girl, who is excessively concerned with being proper, polite, or prim

Examples

Miss Priss attitude
Don’t be such a Miss Priss about getting your shoes dirty.
Her classmates teased her, calling her Miss Priss because she followed every rule.
You don’t have to act like a Miss Priss just because we’re at a fancy restaurant.
