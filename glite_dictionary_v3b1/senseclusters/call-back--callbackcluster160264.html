call-back

noun
a return telephone call
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔlˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈkɔːlˌbæk / (BrE)
an act of telephoning someone again in response to an earlier call or message

Examples

Phone call-back
I missed his call but he left a message asking for a call-back later.
I'll give you a call-back as soon as I have more information.
The company promised a call-back within 24 hours.
