fleetingness

noun
quality of being brief or short-lived
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfliːtɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ˈfliːtɪŋnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of lasting for a very short time; transience or briefness

Examples

The fleetingness of moments
Artists often try to capture the fleetingness of a perfect moment in their work.
She was struck by the fleetingness of the sunset's vibrant colors.
The fleetingness of youth makes many people nostalgic for their younger years.

Related Terms

  • antonym: permanence - the state of lasting or remaining unchanged indefinitely
  • synonym: transience - the state of lasting only for a short time
