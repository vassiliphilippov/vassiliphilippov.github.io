like a bag of spanners

adjective
very unattractive/comical in appearance (British, informal)
9.9
Pronunciation: / laɪk ə bæɡ əv ˈspænɚz / (AmE), / laɪk ə bæɡ əv ˈspænəz / (BrE)
(informal, British) Very unattractive or comical in appearance, especially referring to someone's face.

Examples

Even dressed up, he still managed to look like a bag of spanners.
She joked that her hair made her look like a bag of spanners after the windy walk.
