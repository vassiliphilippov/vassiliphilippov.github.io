unpositive

adjective
not positive result
7.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpɑːzətɪv / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɒzətɪv / (BrE)
not indicating the presence of something, especially in medical or laboratory test results; negative.

Examples

Unpositive test result
The doctor explained that the test was unpositive for any signs of infection.
Her blood work came back unpositive, so no further treatment was needed.
A result that is unpositive usually means the condition is not present.
