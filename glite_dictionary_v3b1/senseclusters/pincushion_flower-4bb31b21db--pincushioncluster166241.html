pincushion flower

noun
ornamental annual with purple or crimson flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnˌkʊʃən ˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈpɪnˌkʊʃ.ən ˌflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
an annual plant of the teasel family, native to the Old World, with fragrant purple to deep crimson flower heads; commonly grown as an ornamental and naturalized in the United States.
