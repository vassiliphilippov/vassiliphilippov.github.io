Tsouic

noun
group of Formosan languages (Taiwan)
Pronunciation: / ˈtsoʊ ɪk / (AmE), / ˈtsəʊ ɪk / (BrE)
a group of Formosan languages consisting of Tsou, Kanakanabu, and Saaroa, spoken in Taiwan.

Examples

Tsouic languages group
Recent research has revealed new connections among the Tsouic languages.
The Tsouic group is important for understanding the diversity of Formosan languages.
