.edu

noun
educational domain
Pronunciation: / ˈdɒt ˌiːˈdiːˈjuː / (AmE), / ˈdɒt ˌiːˈdjuː / (BrE)
the top-level internet domain used by accredited educational institutions, especially in the United States

Examples

A university .edu website
Most American universities have websites ending with .edu to show they are educational institutions.
If you have a student email address with .edu, you may get discounts on software.
The college launched a new portal at their official .edu domain last semester.

Related Terms

  • antonym: .com - the top-level domain for commercial entities
  • synonym: .org - the top-level domain for non-profit organizations
