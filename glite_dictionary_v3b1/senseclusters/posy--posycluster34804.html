posy

noun
small bouquet of flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊzi / (AmE), / ˈpəʊzi / (BrE)
a small bunch of flowers, typically given as a gift

Examples

A posy of flowers
Children often present a small posy to their mothers on Mother's Day.
She gave her teacher a posy of wildflowers at the end of the school year.
The bride carried a delicate posy down the aisle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bouquet - an attractively arranged bunch of flowers
