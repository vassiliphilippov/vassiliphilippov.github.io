popup

noun
easy fly ball
4.2 0.01/m

Examples

A popup fly ball
The batter hit a popup that was easily caught by the second baseman.
With two outs, a popup ended the inning and stranded the runners on base.
He was frustrated after hitting a popup instead of driving the ball deep into the outfield.
