haute

adjective
upper; higher (French)
Pronunciation: / oʊt / (AmE), / əʊt / (BrE)
used before a place name or noun to mean 'upper' or 'higher', especially in geographical names, borrowed from French

Examples

Haute-Provence region
They visited the beautiful haute-Provence region during their summer vacation.
The river flows from the haute-Loire area down to the valley below.
Many French towns use haute in their names to indicate their higher elevation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lower - located below or beneath something else
  • synonym: upper - located above or higher than something else
