bridge

noun
dental device replacing missing teeth
9.9
Pronunciation: / brɪdʒ / (AmE), / brɪdʒ / (BrE)
A dental device consisting of an artificial tooth or teeth anchored to adjacent natural teeth to replace missing ones.

Examples

Dental bridge
After losing two of her teeth, she had a bridge fitted for a better smile.
Cleaning around a dental bridge requires the use of special flossing tools.
My dentist recommended a bridge to fill the gap left by my missing molar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: denture - removable replacement for missing teeth
