unbagged

adjective
not captured or taken
8.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbæɡd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbæɡd / (BrE)
not captured, killed, or taken by a hunter or collector; still free or at large.

Examples

Unbagged animals escape
The rare bird remained unbagged after several days of searching by the wildlife team.
Despite their efforts, the hunters left the forest with the deer still unbagged.
Many collectors dream of finding an unbagged specimen in the wild.
