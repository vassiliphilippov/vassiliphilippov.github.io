coalitional

adjective
relating to a coalition
6.9
Pronunciation: / koʊəˈlɪʃənəl / (AmE), / kəʊəˈlɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a coalition, especially a group formed by individuals or parties working together

Examples

Coalitional agreement
The parties reached a coalitional agreement to form a new government.
Their coalitional efforts led to a successful community project.
A coalitional approach can help diverse groups achieve common goals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collaborative - involving two or more people working together
  • antonym: individualistic - focused on individual action rather than group cooperation
