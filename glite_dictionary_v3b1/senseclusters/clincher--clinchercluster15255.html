clincher

noun
tool for clinching nails or rivets
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪntʃər / (AmE), / ˈklɪntʃə / (BrE)
a tool used to bend over and secure the ends of nails, bolts, or rivets

Examples

A clincher tool
A clincher is essential when working with horseshoes and nails.
After hammering in the nail, he finished the job with a clincher to bend it over.
The carpenter used a clincher to secure the rivets on the metal frame.
