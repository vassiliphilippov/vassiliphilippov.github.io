expeditor

noun
person who speeds workflow
Pronunciation: / ˈɛkspəˌdaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈɛkspɪˌdeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who organizes and speeds up the movement of goods, orders, or tasks, especially in a workplace

Examples

The expeditor speeds orders
The expeditor made sure all the orders left the kitchen on time.
As an expeditor, she coordinated shipments to ensure timely delivery to customers.
The restaurant hired an expeditor to improve communication between the kitchen and the waitstaff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coordinator - a person who organizes people or activities
  • synonym: facilitator - a person who makes a process easier or helps it run smoothly
