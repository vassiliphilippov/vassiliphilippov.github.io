unsell

verb
to fail to sell
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɛl / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛl / (BrE)
to fail to sell or to remain unsold, especially despite efforts to sell

Examples

The product unsells badly
The new product continued to unsell despite the company's advertising campaign.
Several items in the clearance section have unsold for months.
Even with a discount, the old model seemed to unsell at the electronics store.

Related Terms

  • synonym: remain - to continue to exist or stay in a particular state
  • antonym: sell - to exchange something for money
