fissile

adjective
easily split along grain or planes
6.1 8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪsəl / (AmE), / ˈfɪsaɪl / (BrE)
Capable of being split or divided easily along natural grain or planes of weakness, especially in crystals or wood.

Examples

Fissile rock layers
Some types of shale are highly fissile, making them easy to split into thin layers.
The mineral is fissile and breaks cleanly along flat surfaces.
This wood is too fissile for use in building strong furniture.
