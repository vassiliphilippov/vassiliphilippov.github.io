opencast

adjective
mined from the surface
5.6 5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈoʊ.pən.kæst/ (AmE), /ˈəʊ.pən.kɑːst/ (BrE)
(of mines or mining) worked from the exposed surface rather than from underground.

Examples

Opencast coal mine

Most of the area's coal extraction takes place in opencast sites rather than underground shafts.
The construction company specializes in opencast mining methods.
