thermospheric

adjective
of the thermosphere
Pronunciation: / ˌθɜrmoʊˈsfɪrɪk / (AmE), / ˌθɜːməʊˈsfɪərɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the thermosphere, the upper layer of Earth's atmosphere

Examples

Thermospheric temperatures
Scientists use satellites to measure thermospheric temperatures and study changes in the upper atmosphere.
Auroras are often visible in the thermospheric region during periods of high solar activity.
The thermospheric layer plays a key role in protecting Earth from harmful solar radiation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atmospheric - relating to the atmosphere
  • synonym: stratospheric - relating to the stratosphere, a layer of Earth's atmosphere
