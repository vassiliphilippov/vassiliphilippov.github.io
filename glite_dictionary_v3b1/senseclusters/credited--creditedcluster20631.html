credited

adjective
given credit for something
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛdɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkrɛdɪtɪd / (BrE)
(usually followed by 'to') Given credit for something, typically as the source or originator of an achievement, creation, or action.

Examples

Credited with success
The company is credited with revolutionizing the mobile phone industry.
The successful design was credited to her innovative approach.
This theory is often credited to Albert Einstein.
