breathing

noun
process of inhaling and exhaling air
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbriðɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbriːðɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of inhaling air into the lungs and exhaling it, enabling oxygen to enter the body and carbon dioxide to be expelled

Examples

Heavy breathing
After the long run, his breathing became heavy and rapid.
Practicing deep breathing can help reduce stress and anxiety.
The doctor listened carefully to her breathing during the examination.
