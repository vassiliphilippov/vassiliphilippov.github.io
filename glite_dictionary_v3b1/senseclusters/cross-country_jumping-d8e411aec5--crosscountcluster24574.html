cross-country jumping

noun
equestrian event over obstacles across terrain
4.8
Pronunciation: / krɔs ˈkʌntri ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ / (AmE), / krɒs ˈkʌntri ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ / (BrE)
A competitive horse-riding sport in which riders guide their horses across natural terrain and over various solid obstacles to test horsemanship and endurance.

Examples

Cross-country jumping competition
Many riders train for years to master the skills required for cross-country jumping.
She won first place in cross-country jumping at the national equestrian championships.
The course for cross-country jumping included water jumps and fallen logs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eventing - equine sport combining dressage, cross-country, and show jumping
  • antonym: show jumping - equestrian event over jumps in an arena
