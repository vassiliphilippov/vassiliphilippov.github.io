wanigan

noun
portable supply shelter
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwænɪɡən / (AmE), / ˈwænɪɡən / (BrE)
a portable shelter or boat used for storing supplies or as a kitchen, especially for workers in logging camps or on rivers
