fish lure

noun
artificial bait for fishing
8.7 2.4
Pronunciation: /fɪʃ lʊr/ /; / /fɪʃ lɜːr/ (AmE), /fɪʃ ljʊə/ /; / /fɪʃ lɔː/ /; / /fɪʃ lɜː/ (BrE)
An artificial bait, usually made of plastic or metal and often decorated with feathers, used to attract fish in angling.

Examples

Brightly colored fish lures
Brightly colored fish lures are often effective at attracting trout.
He tied a new fish lure to his line before casting into the lake.
The store sells a variety of fish lures for both saltwater and freshwater fishing.
