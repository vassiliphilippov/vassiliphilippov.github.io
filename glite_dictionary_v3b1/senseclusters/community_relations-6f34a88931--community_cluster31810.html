community relations

noun
organization's relationship with its community
Pronunciation: / kəˌmjunəti rɪˈleɪʃənz / (AmE), / kəˌmjuːnəti rɪˈleɪʃənz / (BrE)
the relationship and communication between an organization and the people living in the surrounding area or community it serves

Examples

Community relations work
Local authorities are launching new initiatives to strengthen community relations in the area.
Negative publicity from the incident damaged the company's community relations for years.
The charity organized open days to improve community relations with local residents.

Related Terms
