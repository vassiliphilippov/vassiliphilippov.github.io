rubrum

noun
species epithet use
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈruːbrəm / (AmE), / ˈruːbrəm / (BrE)
in biology, an informal name for a species using only the specific epithet 'rubrum' when the genus is understood from context

Examples

Botanists use rubrum
In the research notes, the botanist referred to the plant as rubrum since the genus was already established.
When discussing multiple samples, the team labeled them simply as rubrum for clarity.
The field guide listed the species as rubrum after mentioning the genus earlier in the text.
