assonate

verb
correspond in vowel sounds
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈæs.əˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈæs.ə.neɪt / (BrE)
(of words or syllables) to correspond in vowel sounds, especially so as to create assonance or a partial rhyme.

Examples

To assonate vowel sounds
In the last stanza, the lines cleverly assonate with each other.
Many English ballads assonate rather than use full rhyme.
The poet chose words that assonated to enhance the musicality of the verse.
