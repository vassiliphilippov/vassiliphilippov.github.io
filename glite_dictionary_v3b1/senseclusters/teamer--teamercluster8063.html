teamer

noun
driver of animal team
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàtiÀêm…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàtiÀêm…ô / (BrE)
a person who drives a team of animals, such as horses or oxen, pulling a wagon or plough

Examples

Teamer with oxen
The experienced teamer led his oxen across the muddy field at sunrise.
In the old days, a skilled teamer was essential for transporting goods over long distances.
The young boy watched the teamer harness the horses before starting the journey.
