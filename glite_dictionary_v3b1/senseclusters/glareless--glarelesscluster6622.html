glareless

adjective
without harsh light
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɛrləs / (AmE), / ˈɡlɛələs / (BrE)
not producing or reflecting a harsh or bright light that causes discomfort to the eyes

Examples

Glareless screen
The new computer screens are designed to be glareless for comfortable viewing.
We installed glareless lighting in the living room to create a relaxing atmosphere.
Her sunglasses have glareless lenses that protect her eyes on sunny days.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dazzling - extremely bright and difficult to look at
  • synonym: soft - gentle or not harsh in quality, especially of light
