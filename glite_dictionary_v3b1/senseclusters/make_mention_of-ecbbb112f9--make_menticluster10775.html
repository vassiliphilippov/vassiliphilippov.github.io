make mention of

verb
refer to or briefly mention
Pronunciation: / meɪk ˈmɛnʃən əv / (AmE), / meɪk ˈmɛnʃən ɒv / (BrE)
to refer to or briefly speak about something or someone, especially in passing

Examples

The article doesn't make mention of the recent developments in the case.
