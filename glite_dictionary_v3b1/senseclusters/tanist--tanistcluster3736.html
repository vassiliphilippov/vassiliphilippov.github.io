tanist

noun
designated successor
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtænɪst / (AmE), / ˈtænɪst / (BrE)
a person chosen as the official successor to an important position, especially the heir to a Celtic chief or king

Examples

After the chief's death, the tanist immediately assumed leadership of the tribe.
