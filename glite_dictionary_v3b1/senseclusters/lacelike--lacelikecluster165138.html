lacelike

adjective
resembling lace in appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪsˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈleɪs.laɪk / (BrE)
made of or resembling lace in appearance or texture.

Examples

Lacelike pattern
The artist created a lacelike pattern on the ceramic vase.
The insect's wings were almost transparent and beautifully lacelike.
Tiny frost crystals formed a lacelike design on the windowpane.
