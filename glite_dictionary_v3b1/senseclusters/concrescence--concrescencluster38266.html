concrescence

noun
fusion of parts
9.9
Pronunciation: / kənˈkrɛsəns / (AmE), / kɒnˈkrɛsəns / (BrE)
the growing together or fusion of separate parts into a single whole, especially in biology or anatomy

Examples

Concrescence of tissues
The concrescence of the roots made it difficult to separate the two plants.
In some flowers, the concrescence of petals creates a unique tubular shape.
Dental concrescence occurs when two teeth are joined by their cementum.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fusion - the process of joining two or more things together to form one
  • antonym: separation - the act of moving or being moved apart
