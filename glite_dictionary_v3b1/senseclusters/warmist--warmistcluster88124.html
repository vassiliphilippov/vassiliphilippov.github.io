warmist

noun
believer in global warming
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈwɑːr.mɪst/ /; / /ˈwɔːr.mɪst/ (AmE), /ˈwɔː.mɪst/ (BrE)
a person who believes that global warming is real and significant, often supporting action to address it

Examples

A warmist supporter
The warmist presented scientific data to support the need for reducing carbon emissions.
Some critics dismiss the concerns raised by a warmist as exaggerated.
At the conference, a well-known warmist debated with climate change skeptics.
Some critics dismiss climate activists as warmists in debates about environmental policy.
The article accused warmists of exaggerating the effects of climate change.
Many warmists advocate for stricter regulations to reduce carbon emissions.

  • antonym: climate change skeptic - a person who doubts or denies the significance of global warming
