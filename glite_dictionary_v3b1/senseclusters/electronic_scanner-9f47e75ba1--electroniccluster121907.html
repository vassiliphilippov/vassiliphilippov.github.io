electronic scanner

noun
radio device for scanning frequencies
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk ˈskænər / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk ˈskænə / (BrE)
A radio device that automatically scans a range of frequencies to detect signals or monitor communication channels.

Examples

Electronic scanner device
His hobby involves building circuits for his electronic scanner to improve its sensitivity.
I bought an electronic scanner to monitor weather broadcasts and aviation communications.
The police department used an electronic scanner to listen to emergency radio traffic during the incident.
