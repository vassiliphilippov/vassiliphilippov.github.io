maximalism

noun
style of excess
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmæksɪməlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈmæksɪməlɪzəm / (BrE)
a style or approach that emphasizes excess, complexity, and elaborate detail rather than simplicity

Examples

Interior maximalism
The designer's maximalism is clear in the bold patterns and rich textures throughout the room.
Some critics argue that maximalism can overwhelm the senses with too many details.
Her love of maximalism is reflected in her collection of colorful, eclectic furniture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: minimalism - a style or approach that favors simplicity and minimal elements
  • synonym: ornateness - the quality of being highly decorated or elaborate
