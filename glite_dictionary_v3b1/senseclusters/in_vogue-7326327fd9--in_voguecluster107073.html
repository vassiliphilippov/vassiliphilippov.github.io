in vogue

adjective
currently fashionable or popular
Pronunciation: / ɪn voʊɡ / (AmE), / ɪn vəʊɡ / (BrE)
Fashionable or popular at the present time; in the current style.

Examples

In vogue style
Minimalist design has been in vogue for several years now.
Social media platforms quickly spread whatever is in vogue among young people.
That style of dress is very in vogue this season.

Related Terms

  • antonym: out of fashion - not fashionable or no longer popular
  • synonym: trendy - very fashionable or up-to-date
