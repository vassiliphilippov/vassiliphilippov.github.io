Solutrean

adjective
Paleolithic European culture
9.9
Pronunciation: / səˈluːtriən / (AmE), / səˈluːtriən / (BrE)
relating to a Paleolithic culture in Europe known for its distinctive stone tools, especially in France and Spain

Examples

Solutrean stone tools
The Solutrean period is famous for its finely crafted stone blades and points.
Archaeologists discovered Solutrean tools at several sites in southwestern France.
The exhibit features Solutrean artwork dating back over 20,000 years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Magdalenian - relating to a later Upper Paleolithic culture in Europe
