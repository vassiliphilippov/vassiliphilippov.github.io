moonglow

noun
soft, moonlike light
4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈmuːn.gloʊ/ (AmE), /ˈmuːn.gləʊ/ (BrE)
a soft, gentle light like the light of the moon, either from the moon itself or something that looks similar

Examples

Soft moonglow at night
The garden was bathed in a gentle moonglow from the hidden lamps.
She admired the moonglow reflecting off the calm surface of the lake.
The artist tried to capture the soft moonglow in his painting of the night scene.
The lake shimmered under the soft moonglow late into the night.
They took a quiet walk, enjoying the moonglow on the empty street.
The old castle looked magical in the pale moonglow.

  • antonym: spotlight - a strong, focused beam of light
  • antonym: sunlight - the light that comes from the sun
