bowline knot

noun
non-slip loop knot
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊlɪn nɑt / (AmE), / ˈbəʊlɪn nɒt / (BrE)
a knot used to make a secure loop at the end of a rope, which will not slip or jam

Examples

A bowline knot loop
He quickly made a bowline knot to create a fixed loop at the end of the rope.
Learning the bowline knot is essential for anyone interested in sailing or climbing.
The sailor tied a bowline knot to secure the lifeline during the rescue.

Related Terms

  • antonym: slip knot - a knot that can be slipped out easily by pulling an end
