luminescent

adjective
emitting light not from heat
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌluːməˈnɛsənt / (AmE), / ˌljuːmɪˈnɛsənt / (BrE)
emitting light without being caused by heat; having or showing luminescence.

Examples

Luminescent glow
He admired the luminescent glow of fireflies on a summer night.
Many minerals are luminescent when exposed to ultraviolet light.
The jellyfish appeared luminescent in the dark ocean water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fluorescent - emitting light by fluorescence
  • synonym: phosphorescent - emitting light by phosphorescence
