jinricksha

noun
pulled passenger vehicle
Pronunciation: / dʒɪnˈrɪkʃə / (AmE), / dʒɪnˈrɪkʃə / (BrE)
a light two-wheeled vehicle pulled by a person, used for carrying passengers, especially in East Asia
