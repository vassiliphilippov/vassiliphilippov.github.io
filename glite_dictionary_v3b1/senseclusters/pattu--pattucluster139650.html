pattu

noun
South Indian folk song
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːtu / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtu / (BrE)
a traditional song in Tamil, Malayalam, or other South Indian languages, often with devotional or folk themes

Examples

Devotional pattu
The musicians performed a beautiful pattu during the festival celebrations.
She learned to sing a pattu from her grandmother, who knew many old melodies.
At the temple, the children joined in a lively pattu that everyone enjoyed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bhajan - a Hindu devotional song
  • synonym: folk song - a traditional song passed down through generations
