securitize

verb
to convert assets to securities
9.1
Pronunciation: /sɪ.ˈkjʊr.ə.taɪz/ /; / /sə.ˈkjʊr.ə.taɪz/ (AmE), /sɪ.ˈkjʊə.taɪz/ /; / /sə.ˈkjʊə.taɪz/ (BrE)
to convert financial assets, such as loans, into marketable securities that can be sold to investors

Examples

To securitize home loans
Banks often securitize home loans to raise capital for new lending.
The company decided to securitize its receivables to improve cash flow.
After the financial crisis, regulations on how firms securitize assets became stricter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bundle - to group financial assets together for sale
  • synonym: monetize - to convert something into money or a source of profit
