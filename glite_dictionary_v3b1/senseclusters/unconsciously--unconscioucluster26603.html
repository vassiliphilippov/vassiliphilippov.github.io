unconsciously

adverb
without awareness or intention
5.2 3.3
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈkɑːn.ʃəs.liː/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈkɒn.ʃəs.liː/ (BrE)
without being aware of something; in a way that is not deliberate or intentional

Examples

To unconsciously smile
He unconsciously smiled whenever her name was mentioned.
Many people unconsciously imitate the body language of those around them.
She unconsciously tapped her foot to the rhythm of the music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automatically - without conscious thought; as a reflex
  • antonym: consciously - with full awareness or intention
