bimonthly

adjective, adverb
occurring twice a month
Pronunciation: / baɪˈmʌnθli / (AmE), / baɪˈmʌnθli / (BrE)
happening or done twice a month (every two weeks)

Examples

Bimonthly meetings
The committee holds bimonthly meetings on the 1st and 15th of each month.
The magazine’s bimonthly issues always feature the latest trends.
We receive a bimonthly report from the project manager.
Employees receive their salaries bimonthly rather than on a monthly schedule.
Her bills are paid bimonthly to ensure she never misses a deadline.
The club meets bimonthly for workshops and discussions.
