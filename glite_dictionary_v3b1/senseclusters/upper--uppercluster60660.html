upper

noun
higher of two sleeping berths
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpɚ / (AmE), / ˈʌpə / (BrE)
the higher of two sleeping berths, especially on a train or a ship

Examples

Upper bunk bed
He was assigned the upper in the sleeping compartment, while his friend took the lower.
I requested an upper because I prefer to sleep higher up.
The upper offers more privacy but can be harder to climb into.
