oooo

interjection
ghostly exclamation
9.9
Pronunciation: / uː / (AmE), / uː / (BrE)
an exclamation imitating a spooky or ghostly moan, often used to scare or tease someone

Examples

Oooo to scare
The children crept up behind their friend and whispered oooo to give her a playful fright.
During the ghost story, someone in the dark made a long oooo sound to add to the atmosphere.
He waved his arms and called out oooo as he pretended to be a ghost at the party.
