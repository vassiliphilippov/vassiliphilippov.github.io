eightvo

noun
book size with eight leaves per sheet
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪtvoʊ / (AmE), / ˈeɪtvəʊ / (BrE)
A book size in which each sheet is folded three times to make eight leaves (sixteen pages).

Examples

An eightvo book
Collectors often seek out early printed books in eightvo format.
The auction catalog listed several first editions, including one in eightvo.
The librarian showed us a rare eightvo edition from the nineteenth century.
