clap

noun
sharp hand sound
1.0
Pronunciation: /klæp/ (AmE), /klæp/ (BrE)
a sharp sound made by striking the palms of the hands together, often to show approval or attract attention

Examples

A clap of hands
A single clap echoed through the hall before the audience erupted in applause.
He gave a loud clap to get everyone's attention during the meeting.
The sudden clap startled the birds resting on the fence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: applause - the act of showing approval by clapping hands
  • antonym: boo - a sound made to show disapproval
  • synonym: cheer - a shout of encouragement or approval
