spring-blooming

adjective
blooms or flowers in spring
2.3
Pronunciation: / sprɪŋ ˈbluːmɪŋ / (AmE), / sprɪŋ ˈbluːmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a plant) blooming or flowering during the spring season.

Examples

Spring-blooming flowers
Daffodils are classic spring-blooming plants admired for their early blossoms.
Many gardeners look forward to the arrival of spring-blooming flowers after a long winter.
The spring-blooming tulips added vibrant color to the garden in April.
