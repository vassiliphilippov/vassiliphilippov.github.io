mkt

noun
market price abbreviation
Pronunciation: / ɛm keɪ ti / (AmE), / ɛm keɪ ti / (BrE)
an abbreviation used in trading to refer to the market price or a transaction at the current market price

Examples

Mkt price order
The broker confirmed the shares were sold at mkt during the morning session.
Please execute the order at mkt to ensure a quick transaction.
He wrote mkt on the form to indicate the sale should happen at the current price.

Related Terms

  • synonym: market price - the current price at which an asset can be bought or sold
