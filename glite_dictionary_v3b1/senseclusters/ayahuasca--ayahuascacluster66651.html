ayahuasca

noun
psychoactive plant brew
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪjəˈwɑːskə / (AmE), / ˌaɪjəˈwæskə / (BrE)
a psychoactive or hallucinogenic drink made from Amazonian plants, used in traditional South American spiritual ceremonies
