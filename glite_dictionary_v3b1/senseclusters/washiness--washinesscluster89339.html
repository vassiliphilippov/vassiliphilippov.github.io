washiness

noun
paleness or weakness
8.8 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…ëÀê Éin…ôs / (AmE), / Ààw…í Éin…ôs / (BrE)
the quality of being pale, weak, or lacking in strength or color

Examples

Washiness of the sky
The washiness of the watercolor gave the landscape a dreamy, distant feel.
Critics noted the washiness in the artist's latest work, saying it lacked boldness.
After several washes, the shirt's color faded to a disappointing washiness.

    pallor is an unhealthy, often medical paleness of the skin, while washiness feels more like weak or faded color.
  • wanness
    9.7 0.00/m

  • pastiness
    6.7 0.00/m

  • ashiness
    7.4 0.02/m
    ashiness is a gray, powdery pale look from dry skin, while washiness implies general faintness or weak color.
