washiness

noun
paleness or weakness
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːʃinəs / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being pale, weak, or lacking in strength or color

Examples

Washiness of the sky
The washiness of the watercolor gave the landscape a dreamy, distant feel.
Critics noted the washiness in the artist's latest work, saying it lacked boldness.
After several washes, the shirt's color faded to a disappointing washiness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pallor - an unhealthy pale appearance
  • antonym: vividness - the quality of being bright or intense in color
