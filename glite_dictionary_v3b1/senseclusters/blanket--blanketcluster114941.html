blanket

noun
bed covering for warmth
Pronunciation: / ˈblæŋkət / (AmE), / ˈblaŋkɪt / (BrE)
A large piece of soft cloth, often woolen, used on a bed to keep a person warm.

Examples

A warm blanket
After the picnic, they sat together on a thick woolen blanket under the stars.
She pulled the blanket up to her chin to stay warm on the cold night.
The hotel provided an extra blanket for each guest during winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coverlet - a decorative bedspread, usually lighter than a blanket
  • degree higher: duvet - a soft quilt filled with down or feathers used as a bed covering
  • synonym: quilt - a warm bed covering with padding, stitched together
  • degree lower: sheet - a large rectangular piece of cloth for laying on or covering a bed
