affectionate

adjective
showing affection or fondness
4.8
Pronunciation: / əˈfɛk.ʃə.nət / (AmE), / əˈfɛk.ʃə.nət / (BrE)
showing fondness or tenderness towards someone or something; feeling or displaying affection.

Examples

Affectionate towards family
He has always been very affectionate towards his younger sister.
She gave her dog an affectionate hug before leaving for work.
Their affectionate relationship was obvious to everyone who knew them.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cold - lacking affection or warmth
  • synonym: fond - having affection or liking for someone or something
  • synonym: tender - showing gentleness, kindness, and affection
