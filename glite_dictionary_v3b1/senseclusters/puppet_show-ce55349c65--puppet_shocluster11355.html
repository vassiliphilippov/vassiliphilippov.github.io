puppet show

noun
a performance using puppets
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌpət ʃoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpʌpɪt ʃəʊ / (BrE)
a performance in which puppets are used to enact a story for an audience

Examples

A puppet show performance
She has fond memories of seeing her first puppet show as a child.
The children laughed and clapped during the puppet show at the fair.
The festival featured a traditional puppet show that delighted both adults and children.
