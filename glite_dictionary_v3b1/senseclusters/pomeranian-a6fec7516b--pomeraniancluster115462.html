Pomeranian

noun
small, long-haired toy dog breed
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːmɚˈeɪniən / (AmE), / ˌpɒm.əˈreɪ.ni.ən / (BrE)
a breed of very small, compact, long-haired dogs of the spitz type, often kept as pets

Examples

A Pomeranian dog
Many people choose a Pomeranian because of its lively personality and small size.
My aunt has a Pomeranian that loves to bark at delivery people.
The fluffy coat of a Pomeranian requires regular grooming to prevent matting.
