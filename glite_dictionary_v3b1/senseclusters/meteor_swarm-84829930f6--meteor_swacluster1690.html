meteor swarm

noun
group of meteoroids with similar paths
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːdiər swɔrm / (AmE), / ˈmiːtɪə swɔːm / (BrE)
a group of meteoroids traveling together along similar paths through space

Examples

A meteor swarm in space
A meteor swarm can cause a spectacular display of shooting stars in the sky.
Astronomers observed a meteor swarm passing close to Earth last night.
Scientists studied the meteor swarm to learn more about the formation of the solar system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meteor shower - a period when numerous meteors are observed to radiate from one point
