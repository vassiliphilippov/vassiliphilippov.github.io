solar constant

noun
rate of solar energy at Earth's atmosphere
4.9 6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊlər ˈkɑnstənt / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlə ˈkɒnstənt / (BrE)
the amount of solar radiation received per unit area at the outer layer of the Earth's atmosphere when the sun is at its mean distance from the Earth
