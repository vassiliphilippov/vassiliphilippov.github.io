progressive love

noun
love that evolves and adapts over time
Pronunciation: / prəˈɡrɛsɪv lʌv / (AmE), / prəˈɡrɛsɪv lʌv / (BrE)
A concept of love that develops, evolves, and adapts over time, embracing change and growth within a relationship.

Examples

Progressive love grows
In her essay, she describes progressive love as a journey of mutual evolution and flexibility.
Many modern couples advocate for progressive love rather than sticking to traditional relationship models.
They have chosen to practice progressive love that grows as their lives change together.
