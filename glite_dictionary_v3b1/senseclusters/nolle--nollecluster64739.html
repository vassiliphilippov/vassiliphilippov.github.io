nolle

verb
to dismiss a case
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːli / (AmE), / ˈnɒli / (BrE)
to formally end or dismiss a legal case or charge, especially by a prosecutor

Examples

To nolle charges
The judge agreed to nolle the case after reviewing the new evidence.
Due to a lack of witnesses, the prosecutor chose to nolle the charges.
The defense attorney requested that the court nolle the indictment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dismiss - to formally end a legal case or charge
  • antonym: prosecute - to officially continue with legal proceedings against someone
