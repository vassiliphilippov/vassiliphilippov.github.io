shoe

noun
a type of footwear covering the foot
2.4
Pronunciation: / ʃu / (AmE), / ʃuː / (BrE)
A covering for the foot, typically made of leather or another sturdy material, with a sole and often a heel, reaching just below the ankle.

Examples

A leather shoe
His shoe was untied, so he stopped to fix it.
Please take off your shoes before entering the house.
She bought a new pair of shoes for the wedding.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: boot - footwear covering the foot and ankle, often higher
  • degree lower: slipper - soft footwear for indoor use
