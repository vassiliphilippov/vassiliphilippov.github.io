healthiness

noun
state of being healthy
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlθi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθi.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being physically and mentally healthy

Examples

The healthiness of body
Doctors emphasize the healthiness of both mind and body for overall well-being.
She was admired for the healthiness of her lifestyle, including regular exercise and balanced meals.
The healthiness of the food served in schools is a growing concern for parents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: health - the condition of being free from illness or injury
  • antonym: unhealthiness - the quality or state of being unhealthy
