monochromatic design

noun
design using one color or shades of it
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːnəkroʊˈmætɪk dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəkrəˈmætɪk dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
A design that uses only one color or various shades of a single color.

Examples

A monochromatic design scheme
A monochromatic design can create a cohesive and calming atmosphere in any space.
Her artwork often exemplifies the simplicity of a monochromatic design.
The living room features a monochromatic design with different shades of blue throughout.

Related Terms

  • antonym: polychromatic - having many colors
