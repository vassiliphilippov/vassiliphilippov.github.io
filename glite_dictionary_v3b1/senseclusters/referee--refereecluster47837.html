referee

noun
sports official who enforces rules
2.7 5.46/m
Pronunciation: / Àår…õf…ôÀàri / (AmE), / Àår…õf…ôÀàriÀê / (BrE)
an official in sports responsible for ensuring fair play and enforcing the rules during a game or match

Examples

Soccer referee
During the heated contest, the referee tried to keep the players calm and focused on the rules.
If you disagree with the referee, you can appeal the decision after the game.
The referee blew the whistle to signal the end of the match.

Synonyms

  • ref
    7.4 2.76/m

Related Terms

  • synonym: umpire - an official who watches a game or match to enforce the rules (especially in cricket, tennis, baseball)
