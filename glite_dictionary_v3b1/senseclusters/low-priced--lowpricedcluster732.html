Examples

Low-priced housing

Synonyms

  • inexpensive
    1.0 12/m
    inexpensive is a neutral word for things that cost little, while low-priced describes items sold for a lower amount.
  • low-cost
    2.0 5.27/m
    low-cost is used for products or services that do not cost much, while low-priced focuses on a low selling price.
  • bargain-priced
    3.2 0.15/m
    bargain-priced refers to items sold below the usual price, while low-priced simply means they cost relatively little.
  • affordable
    2.1 21/m
    affordable means people can buy something within their budget, while low-priced only says the item costs little.
  • cheap
    1.0 59/m
    cheap often means low quality or badly made, while low-priced only describes a low cost without judging quality.
