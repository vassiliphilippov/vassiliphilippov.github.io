thumper

noun
slang for large firearm
Pronunciation: / ˈθʌmpər / (AmE), / ˈθʌmpə / (BrE)
a large-calibre firearm or handgun that produces a heavy recoil when fired, used as slang

Examples

A big thumper gun
He proudly showed off his new thumper at the shooting range.
The recoil from the old thumper nearly knocked him off his feet.
Hunters sometimes prefer a thumper for taking down large game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cannon - a large, heavy gun typically mounted on wheels
  • antonym: pistol - a small firearm designed to be held in one hand
