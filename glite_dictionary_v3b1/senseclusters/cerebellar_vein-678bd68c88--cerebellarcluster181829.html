cerebellar vein

noun
a vein draining the cerebellum
6.3
Pronunciation: / sɛr.əˈbɛl.ɚ veɪn / (AmE), / sɛr.əˈbɛl.ə veɪn / (BrE)
a vein that drains blood from the cerebellum of the brain

Examples

cerebellar vein drains blood
Damage to a cerebellar vein can lead to complications such as stroke or hemorrhage.
During surgery, care must be taken to avoid injury to the cerebellar vein.
The cerebellar vein plays an essential role in draining blood from the posterior region of the brain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cerebellum - part of the brain that coordinates voluntary movements
  • synonym: vein - a blood vessel that carries blood towards the heart
