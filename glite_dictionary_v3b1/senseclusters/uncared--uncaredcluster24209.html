uncared

adjective
neglected; unattended
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkɛrd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɛəd / (BrE)
not given attention, care, or maintenance; neglected or unattended.

Examples

An uncared place
The old house appeared uncared after years of abandonment.
She felt sad seeing so many uncared pets at the shelter.
The park became uncared when the city stopped regular maintenance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cared-for - looked after or maintained
  • synonym: neglected - not receiving proper care or attention
