nonnational

adjective
not a citizen
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈnæʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈnæʃənəl / (BrE)
not a citizen or legal national of a particular country; foreign, especially in legal or immigration contexts

Examples

Nonnational workers
The government introduced new policies affecting nonnational workers in the agricultural sector.
As a nonnational resident, she needed a special permit to stay in the country.
The university offers support services for nonnational students adjusting to life abroad.

Related Terms

  • antonym: citizen - a legally recognized subject or national of a country
  • synonym: foreign - from another country; not native
