laneway

noun
narrow urban passage
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪnˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈleɪnˌweɪ / (BrE)
a narrow road or passage between buildings, especially in a city or town

Examples

A narrow laneway
The children played soccer in the laneway behind their house.
A small café was hidden down a quiet laneway in the city center.
Residents decorated the laneway with colorful murals to brighten the neighborhood.
