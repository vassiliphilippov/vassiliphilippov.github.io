bunga

noun
loan interest (SE Asia)
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːŋɡə / (AmE), / ˈbuːŋɡə / (BrE)
interest charged on borrowed money, especially in financial contexts relating to Southeast Asia.

Examples

Bunga interest rate
The bank's bunga rates are higher than those in other countries.
He negotiated with the lender to reduce the bunga on his business loan.
In Indonesia, the bunga on savings accounts can vary widely between banks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interest - money charged for borrowing or earned by lending
