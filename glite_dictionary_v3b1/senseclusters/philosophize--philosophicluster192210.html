philosophize

verb
reason in a philosophical manner
Pronunciation: / fəˈlɑːsəˌfaɪz / (AmE), / fəˈlɒsəˌfaɪz /; / fɪˈlɒsəfaɪz / (BrE)
to discuss, consider, speculate, or reason about fundamental, abstract, or theoretical matters in a philosophical way

Examples

To philosophize about life
He loved to philosophize about the meaning of life over a cup of coffee.
In their late-night talks, the friends would philosophize until dawn about happiness and truth.
Rather than act, she would often philosophize about every possible outcome.
Rather than act immediately, he sat back and began to philosophise about the problem.
She tends to philosophise whenever faced with a difficult decision, considering every possible angle.
They would often philosophise late into the night about the meaning of life.

  • synonym: philosophize - to reason or speculate in a philosophical way (American spelling)
  • synonym: reflect - to think deeply or carefully about something
