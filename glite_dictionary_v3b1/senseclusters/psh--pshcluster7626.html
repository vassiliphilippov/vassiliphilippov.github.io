psh

interjection
sound of air release
Pronunciation: / pʃ / (AmE), / pʃ / (BrE)
a short, soft sound used to represent the sudden release of air or gas, often in comics or informal writing

Examples

Bottle opens with psh
When he opened the bottle, it made a quiet psh before the bubbles appeared.
The tire lost air with a sudden psh as it hit the sharp rock.
In the comic, the hero's jetpack started with a loud psh as he took off.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hiss - a long, sharp sound like air escaping
