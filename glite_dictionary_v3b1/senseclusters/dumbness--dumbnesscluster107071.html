dumbness

noun
state of being mentally slow
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌmnəs / (AmE), / ˈdʌmnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being mentally slow or lacking intelligence

Examples

Obvious dumbness
His comments betrayed a surprising dumbness about current events.
She tried to hide her dumbness in math class by avoiding difficult questions.
The film unfairly depicted rural life with a sense of exaggerated dumbness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intelligence - the ability to acquire and apply knowledge and skills
  • synonym: stupidity - lack of intelligence or common sense
