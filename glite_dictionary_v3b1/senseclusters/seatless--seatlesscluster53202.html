seatless

adjective
without a seat
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːtləs / (AmE), / ˈsiːtləs / (BrE)
lacking a seat or not having a place to sit

Examples

Seatless bicycle
The old bicycle was seatless and hard to ride.
We had to stand on the crowded, seatless bus during rush hour.
The seatless bench was removed from the park for repairs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: seated - having a seat or place to sit
