anchovy sauce

noun
sauce made with mashed anchovies
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.tʃoʊ.vi sɔs / (AmE), / ˈæn.tʃəʊ.vi sɔːs / (BrE)
A sauce made from mashed anchovies mixed into a white sauce, used as a savory condiment or topping.

Examples

Anchovy sauce recipe
My grandmother’s recipe calls for homemade anchovy sauce as the secret ingredient.
Serve the steamed vegetables with a drizzle of anchovy sauce for a savory kick.
The chef added a generous spoonful of anchovy sauce to enhance the flavor of the pasta.

Related Terms
