zinc phosphate dental cement

noun
type of dental cement
Pronunciation: / zɪŋk ˈfoʊs.feɪt ˈdɛn.tl səˈmɛnt / (AmE), / zɪŋk ˈfɒs.feɪt ˈdɛn.tl sɪˈmɛnt / (BrE)
