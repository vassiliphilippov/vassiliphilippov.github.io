Synonyms

  • judicial system
    3.9 2.30/m
    judicial system is the standard term for a country's courts and judges, while judiciary refers to the institution that applies the law.
  • judicial branch
    4.2 0.60/m
    judicial branch is the government part that interprets laws and decides cases, while judiciary names the courts and judges.
  • judicature
    9.9 0.08/m
    judicature refers to the process and practice of administering justice, while judiciary means the system of courts and judges.
  • judicatory
    9.9 0.05/m
    judicatory often means a specific tribunal or court body, while judiciary is the wider system of courts and judges.
