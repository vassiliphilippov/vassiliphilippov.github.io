associate

adjective
having partial or subordinate status
2.0 4.4
Pronunciation: / əˈsoʊsiət / (AmE), / əˈsəʊsiət / (BrE)
(of a rank, title, or position) having partial rights and privileges or subordinate status, often as a junior member or officer in an organization.

Examples

Associate member
As an associate member, he could attend meetings but not vote.
She was promoted to associate professor after several years as an assistant.
The club offers both associate and full memberships for different levels of participation.
