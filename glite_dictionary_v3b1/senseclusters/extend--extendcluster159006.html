extend

verb
exert oneself to the fullest
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɛnd / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛnd / (BrE)
to make use of one's abilities, effort, or resources to the utmost; to exert vigorously or to full capacity

Examples

To extend yourself
He always extends himself when preparing important presentations for his team.
She really extended herself to finish the marathon despite feeling exhausted.
You may need to extend your abilities if you want to master this challenge.

Related Terms
