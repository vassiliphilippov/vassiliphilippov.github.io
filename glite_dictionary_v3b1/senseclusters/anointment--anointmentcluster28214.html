anointment

noun
act of applying oil, often in ceremony
5.7
Pronunciation: / əˈnɔɪntmənt / (AmE), / əˈnɔɪntmənt / (BrE)
the act of applying oil or an oily liquid, especially as part of a religious ceremony

Examples

The anointment ceremony
During the ceremony, her anointment marked the beginning of her religious duties.
Many ancient traditions include the anointment of individuals to symbolize protection or blessing.
The anointment of the new king was held in a grand cathedral.
