WOD

noun
daily workout routine
7.7
Pronunciation: / wɑd / (AmE), / wɒd / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'workout of the day,' referring to a specific set of exercises assigned for a particular day, especially in fitness programs like CrossFit

Examples

CrossFit WOD routine
Today's WOD included running, push-ups, and squats.
She checked the gym's website every morning to see the new WOD.
After completing the challenging WOD, everyone felt accomplished and exhausted.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rest day - a day without scheduled exercise
  • synonym: workout - a session of physical exercise
