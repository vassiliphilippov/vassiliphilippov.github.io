frumpily

adverb
in an unfashionable or dowdy way
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfrʌmpɪli / (AmE), / ˈfrʌmpɪli / (BrE)
In an unfashionable or dowdy manner.

Examples

Dress frumpily
Despite her wealth, she always presented herself rather frumpily at public events.
He spoke frumpily about fashion, obviously uninterested in current trends.
She arrived at the party frumpily dressed in an oversized sweater and baggy pants.
