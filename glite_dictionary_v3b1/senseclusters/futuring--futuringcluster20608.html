futuring

adjective
forward-looking planning
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːtʃərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfjuːtʃərɪŋ / (BrE)
describing activities, thinking, or reports that are forward-looking and focused on anticipating or preparing for future possibilities

Examples

Futuring workshop
The organization held a futuring workshop to explore possible technological advances in the next decade.
Her futuring mindset helped the team prepare for unexpected changes in the industry.
Many companies are investing in futuring strategies to stay ahead of market trends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: predictive - relating to the ability to predict future events
  • antonym: retrospective - looking back on or dealing with past events
