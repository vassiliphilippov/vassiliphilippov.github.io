untuck

verb
to pull out from tucked position
3.9
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈtʌk/ /; / /ən.ˈtʌk/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈtʌk/ /; / /ən.ˈtʌk/ (BrE)
to pull something out from where it is tucked in, such as a shirt from pants or sheets from a bed
