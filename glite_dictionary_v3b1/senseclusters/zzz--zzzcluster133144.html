zzz

interjection
boredom or sleepiness
9.9
Pronunciation: / ziːz / (AmE), / ziːz / (BrE)
an informal written expression used to indicate boredom, dullness, or sleepiness, often to show something is uninteresting

Examples

zzz in a chat
The movie was so slow that I typed zzz in the group chat.
Whenever the lecture gets too technical, someone always posts zzz in the comments.
He replied with just zzz to show he was bored by the story.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exciting - causing great enthusiasm and interest
  • synonym: yawn - to open your mouth wide and breathe in deeply when tired or bored
