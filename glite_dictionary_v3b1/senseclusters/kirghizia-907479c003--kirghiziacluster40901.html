Kirghizia

proper noun
former name of Kyrgyzstan, Central Asian country
Pronunciation: / kɪrˈɡɪziə / (AmE), / kɜːˈɡɪziə / (BrE)
The former official name of Kyrgyzstan, a landlocked country in Central Asia, which was a republic of the Soviet Union until 1991.

Examples

Kirghizia country
After gaining independence in 1991, Kirghizia was renamed Kyrgyzstan.
During the Soviet era, Kirghizia was known for its mountainous landscapes and nomadic culture.
Many historical documents refer to the nation as Kirghizia before the dissolution of the Soviet Union.
