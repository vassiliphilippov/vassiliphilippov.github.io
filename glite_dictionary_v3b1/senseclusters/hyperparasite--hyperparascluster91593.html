hyperparasite

noun
parasite of a parasite
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˈpærəsaɪt / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəˈpærəsaɪt / (BrE)

Examples

A hyperparasite can help control pest populations by attacking their parasites.

The presence of a hyperparasite can complicate the life cycle of its host parasite.
