garnish

noun
decorative or flavor-enhancing addition to food or drink
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrnɪʃ /; / ˈɡɑːrnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːnɪʃ / (BrE)
an item added to food or drink for decoration or to enhance flavor

Examples

A lemon garnish
A lemon wedge is a common garnish for seafood dishes.
He placed a fresh parsley garnish on top of the soup.
The cocktail arrived with a cherry garnish on the rim of the glass.
A lemon slice can be used as a colorful garnish for drinks and dishes.
She placed edible flowers as a garnish to impress her dinner guests.
The chef added a sprig of parsley as a garnish on the plate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decoration - something used to decorate food or objects
  • synonym: embellishment - a decorative detail or feature added to something
