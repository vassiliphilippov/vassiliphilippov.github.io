geophagy

noun
the practice of eating earth or clay
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːəˌfeɪdʒi / (AmE), / dʒɪˈɒfədʒi / (BrE)
The practice of eating earth, clay, or chalk, sometimes observed among animals and certain human populations, often due to nutritional deficiency or cultural practices.

Examples

Geophagy practice
Reports of geophagy have been documented among certain tribal communities around the world.
Some animals display geophagy to obtain minerals absent from their regular diet.
The medical team investigated whether her geophagy was linked to iron deficiency anemia.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pica - compulsive eating of non-nutritive substances
