cultivatable

adjective
able to be farmed productively
Pronunciation: / ˌkʌl.tɪˈveɪ.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌkʌl.tɪˈveɪ.tə.bəl / (BrE)
(of land) able to be farmed or cultivated productively.

Examples

Cultivatable land
Due to poor soil quality, only a small portion of the area is cultivatable.
Experts assessed whether the newly cleared fields were truly cultivatable.
The government is investing in irrigation projects to make more cultivatable land available.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arable - land suitable for growing crops
  • synonym: cultivable - of land, able to be cultivated
