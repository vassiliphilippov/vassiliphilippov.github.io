winged

adjective
moving very fast; as if with wings
Pronunciation: / wɪŋd / (AmE), / wɪŋd / (BrE)
(literary) moving or capable of moving very fast; as if possessing wings.

Examples

Winged speed
He ran across the field on winged feet, reaching the finish line before anyone else.
The hero arrived like a winged messenger, swift and unexpected.
With winged speed, the news spread through the small town.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fleet - moving swiftly; very fast
  • antonym: slow - not moving quickly; moving at a low speed
