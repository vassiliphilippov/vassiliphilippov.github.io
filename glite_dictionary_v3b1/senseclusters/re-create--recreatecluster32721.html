re-create

verb
make again; reproduce
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌriˈkreɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈkreɪt /; / ˌriˈkriːeɪt / (BrE)
to make or produce something again, especially to copy or reproduce it as it was before

Examples

To re-create a scene
Many filmmakers strive to re-create historical events as accurately as possible.
The artist tried to re-create the beauty of the original painting.
They managed to re-create the atmosphere of the jazz age with music and decor.
He spent hours attempting to re-create his grandmother’s famous pie recipe.
Scientists are working to re-create ancient climate conditions in the lab.
The artist tried to re-create the lost mural from old photographs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: copy - to produce something that is similar or identical to another thing
  • synonym: replicate - to make an exact copy of; to duplicate
  • synonym: reproduce - to make a copy or representation of something
