foul

adjective
not hit between foul lines (baseball)
Pronunciation: / faʊl / (AmE), / faʊl / (BrE)
not hit between the foul lines; outside the area of fair play (in baseball)

Examples

A foul ball
That pitch was clearly foul, landing outside the left field line.
The batter hit a foul ball deep into the stands.
The umpire called the play foul after examining where the ball landed.
His foul tactics in business quickly earned him a bad reputation.
The player was penalized for making a foul tackle during the match.
The referee called a foul play when the runner was tripped intentionally.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fair - (in baseball) hit between the foul lines; opposite of foul
