mark

noun
symbol indicating disgrace or stigma
1.3
Pronunciation: / mɑrk / (AmE), / mɑːk / (BrE)
a symbol, sign, or notation used to identify, distinguish, or indicate something (including punctuation, labels, or company marks)

Examples

She checked for the manufacturer's mark on the back of the painting.
The prisoner was given a special mark to identify him.
Every sentence must end with a punctuation mark such as a period or question mark.
Please place a mark next to the correct answer on the sheet.
The teacher explained what each mark on the page meant.
