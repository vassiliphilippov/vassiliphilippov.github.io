spotted hyena

noun
species of African hyena with spots
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑdɪd haɪˈinə / (AmE), / ˈspɒtɪd haɪˈiːnə / (BrE)
a large African carnivorous mammal with a spotted coat, known for its distinctive howl and strong jaws

Examples

A group of spotted hyenas
A group of spotted hyenas surrounded the carcass, making high-pitched howls as they fed.
The spotted hyena is famous for its eerie, laughing vocalizations at night.
Unlike other predators in Africa, the spotted hyena often dominates its competitors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hyena - wild carnivorous animal of Africa and Asia, related to the dog family
