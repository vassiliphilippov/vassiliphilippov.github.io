utopianism

noun
belief in an ideal, perfect society
7.0
Pronunciation: / juːˈtoʊpiənɪzəm / (AmE), / juːˈtəʊpɪənɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
The belief in or pursuit of an ideally perfect society, often viewed as unrealistic or impractical.

Examples

Utopianism belief
Critics argue that utopianism ignores practical limitations and human nature.
Her passionate speeches often reveal a deep commitment to utopianism and hopes for a perfect world.
The movement was inspired by utopianism, envisioning a future free of poverty and injustice.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dystopianism - belief in or representation of a society that is frightening or undesirable
