apopemptic

adjective
addressed as a farewell to someone departing
Pronunciation: / ˌæpəˈmɛmptɪk / (AmE), / ˌapəˈmɛmptɪk / (BrE)
(of a speech, poem, hymn, etc.) addressed to or intended for someone who is departing, as a farewell.

Examples

Apopemptic farewell speech
Classical poets often wrote apopemptic verses for friends embarking on long journeys.
Her apopemptic speech brought tears to many who attended the farewell dinner.
The choir sang an apopemptic hymn as the guests prepared to leave.
