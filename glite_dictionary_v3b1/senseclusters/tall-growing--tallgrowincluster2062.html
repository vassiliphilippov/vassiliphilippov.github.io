tall-growing

adjective
growing to a tall height (plants)
4.9 2.8
Pronunciation: /tɔːl ˈgroʊ.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /tɔːl ˈgrəʊ.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
(of plants) growing to a tall and often slender height; having tall, spindly stems.

Examples

Tall-growing plants
Gardeners often stake tall-growing plants to prevent them from bending in the wind.
The tall-growing bamboo created a natural privacy screen along the fence.
This variety of hollyhock is known as a tall-growing species, reaching heights over six feet.
