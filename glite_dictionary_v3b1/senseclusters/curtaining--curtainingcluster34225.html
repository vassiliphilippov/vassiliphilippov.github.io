curtaining

noun
hanging curtains
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜrtənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɜːtənɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of hanging, drawing, or fitting curtains, or a covering made with curtains

Examples

Stage curtaining
The stage crew completed the curtaining before the performance began.
Heavy curtaining was used to block out the sunlight in the conference room.
They admired the elegant curtaining that framed the large windows in the hall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drapery - cloth or fabric arranged in loose folds, often used as a decorative covering
  • antonym: uncovering - the act of removing a cover or curtain
