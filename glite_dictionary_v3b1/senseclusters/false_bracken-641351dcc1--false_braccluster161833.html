false bracken

noun
Australian fern resembling bracken
9.8
Pronunciation: / fɔls ˈbrækən / (AmE), / fɔːls ˈbrækən / (BrE)
A fern of Queensland, Australia, that resembles the true bracken (Pteridium aquilinum).

Examples

false bracken leaves
The leaves of false bracken are similar in appearance to those of Pteridium aquilinum.
Unlike true bracken, false bracken is mainly found in Queensland's drier forests.
We spotted false bracken growing along the edge of the rainforest trail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bracken - a large coarse fern; especially Pteridium aquilinum
