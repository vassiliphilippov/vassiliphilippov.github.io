diatonically

adverb
using scale notes only
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪəˈtɑnɪkli / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəˈtɒnɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that uses only the notes of a given scale or key, without chromatic alterations

Examples

To play diatonically
The piece progresses diatonically, never straying from the notes of the G major scale.
She composed the entire song diatonically to keep the melody simple and clear.
The harmony moves diatonically through the key, avoiding any chromatic chords.
