Dillenia

noun
a genus of tropical flowering trees or shrubs
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlɛniə / (AmE), / dɪˈlɛniə / (BrE)
A genus of East Indian and Australian shrubs and trees, known for their panicles of large white or yellow flowers.
