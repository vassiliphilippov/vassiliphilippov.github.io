uphill

adjective
sloping upward
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈhɪl / (AmE), / ʌpˈhɪl / (BrE)
(of a road, path, or surface) sloping upward.

Examples

Uphill road
Cycling became much harder when the road turned uphill.
She prefers the uphill climb because it's more of a challenge.
The runners struggled along the uphill path to the finish line.

Related Terms

  • antonym: downhill - sloping downward
