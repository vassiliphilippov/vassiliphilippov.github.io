smooth-haired

adjective
having smooth, flat hair or fur
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsmuð ˌhɛrd / (AmE), / ˈsmuːð ˌheəd / (BrE)
(of an animal) having hair or fur that is short, flat, and feels smooth to the touch.

Examples

Smooth-haired cat
Many people prefer smooth-haired cats because their coats shed less and require little grooming.
The smooth-haired dachshund has a sleek, glossy coat that is easy to maintain.
We adopted a smooth-haired terrier from the animal shelter last weekend.

Related Terms

  • antonym: wire-haired - having coarse, wiry hair or fur (of an animal)
