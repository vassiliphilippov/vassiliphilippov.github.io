waltz

verb
to dance the waltz
6.9
Pronunciation: / wɔlts / (AmE), / wɔːlts / (BrE)
to dance a waltz, a type of ballroom dance performed in triple time

Examples

To waltz together
Couples waltzed in perfect synchronization during the competition.
He knows how to waltz gracefully across the dance floor.
They decided to waltz together at the end of the evening.
