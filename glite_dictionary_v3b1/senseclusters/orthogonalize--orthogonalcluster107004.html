orthogonalize

verb
to make independent
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɔːr.ˈθɑːg.ə.nəl.aɪz/ /; / /ɔːr.ˈθɔːg.ə.nəl.aɪz/ (AmE), /ɔː.ˈθɒg.ə.nəl.aɪz/ /; / /ɔː.ˈθɔːg.ə.nəl.aɪz/ (BrE)
to design or arrange ideas, features, or system components so that they are independent and do not affect each other

Examples

By orthogonalizing the design, the team made each feature easier to test and maintain.
It is important to orthogonalize your ideas so that changes in one area do not affect others.
