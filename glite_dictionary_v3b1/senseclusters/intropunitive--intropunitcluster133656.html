intropunitive

adjective
self-blaming tendency
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntroʊˈpjuːnətɪv / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəʊˈpjuːnɪtɪv / (BrE)
tending to direct punishment or blame toward oneself rather than others

Examples

An intropunitive person
Her intropunitive nature made her quick to blame herself for any mistake.
People with an intropunitive attitude often struggle with low self-esteem.
Therapists help clients recognize when their thinking becomes overly intropunitive after setbacks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extrapunitive - tending to direct punishment or blame toward others
  • synonym: self-critical - inclined to find fault with oneself
