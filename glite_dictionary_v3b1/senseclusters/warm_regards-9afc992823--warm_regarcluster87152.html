warm regards

noun
polite closing in letters or emails
1.1
Pronunciation: / wɔrm rɪˈɡɑrdz / (AmE), / wɔːm rɪˈɡɑːdz / (BrE)
a polite and friendly expression of good wishes, typically used to end written correspondence

Examples

warm regards in letters
After our meeting, she sent me a thank-you note with warm regards.
At the end of the letter, he wrote warm regards before signing his name.
She closed her email with warm regards to show her appreciation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kind regards - a polite way to end a letter or email, expressing good wishes
