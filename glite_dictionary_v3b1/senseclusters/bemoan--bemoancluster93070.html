bemoan

verb
express regret or sorrow
9.5
Pronunciation: / bəˈmoʊn / (AmE), / bɪˈməʊn / (BrE)
to express deep regret or sorrow over something

Examples

To bemoan a loss
Critics often bemoan the decline of traditional values in modern society.
Despite his success, he would sometimes bemoan the sacrifices he had made.
She bemoaned the lack of opportunities for young artists in the city.

Related Terms

  • antonym: celebrate - to show happiness or approval for something
  • synonym: deplore - to feel or express strong disapproval of
  • synonym: lament - to express grief or sorrow for something
