Italian honeysuckle

noun
a climbing shrub with yellow-white fragrant flowers
6.1
Pronunciation: / ɪˈtæliən ˈhʌniˌsʌkəl / (AmE), / ɪˈtælɪən ˈhʌniˌsʌkl / (BrE)
A deciduous climbing shrub with fragrant yellow-white flowers in axillary whorls, native to Europe and known for its ornamental value.

Examples

The Italian honeysuckle flowers
In early summer, the Italian honeysuckle produced a stunning display of white and yellow flowers.
She planted a Italian honeysuckle near the arbor to attract bees and butterflies.
The garden was filled with the sweet scent of Italian honeysuckle in full bloom.
