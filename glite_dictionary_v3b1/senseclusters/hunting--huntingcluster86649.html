hunting

noun
proactive search for cyber threats
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhʌntɪŋ / (BrE)
(in cybersecurity) the proactive search for hidden threats, attackers, or indicators of compromise within a computer network or systems using tools, analytics, and human investigation.

Examples

Cyber hunting
The security team began hunting across the network to find signs of the hidden intrusion.
Effective hunting uses logs, behavior analytics, and threat intelligence to locate advanced attackers.
Many companies now employ dedicated analysts for hunting in their security operations center.

Related Terms

  • synonym: searching - the act of looking thoroughly to find something; a broader, non-technical counterpart
  • synonym: threat hunting - the practice of proactively looking for cyber threats and intrusions inside networks or systems
