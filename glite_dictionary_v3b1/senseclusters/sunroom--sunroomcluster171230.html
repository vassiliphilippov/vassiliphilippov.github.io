sunroom

noun
room with glass walls for sunlight
3.4 0.64/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs ånÀåruÀêm / (AmE), / Ààs ånÀåruÀêm / (BrE)
A room in a house with large windows or glass walls, designed to let in a lot of sunlight.

Examples

A sunroom with windows
She loves reading her book in the comfort of the sunroom every afternoon.
The plants thrived in the bright sunroom even during winter.
They built a sunroom at the back of the house to enjoy the morning light.

Synonyms

  • sunporch
    4.6 0.09/m
    sunporch is an attached room enclosed mainly with glass for bright, airy relaxation, while sunroom is a house room that simply receives sunlight.
  • sun parlor
    5.8 0.00/m
    sun parlor is a glass lined room meant for quietly enjoying sun and views, while sunroom is the neutral term for any sunny house room.
  • sun lounge
    3.5 0.01/m
    sun lounge is a relaxed indoor space with large windows for sitting, while sunroom is a general house room that brings in sunlight.
  • solarium
    7.5 0.60/m
    solarium is a mainly glass room often with a glass roof for strong sun exposure, while sunroom is a simpler sunlit living space.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conservatory - a room with glass walls and roof for growing plants or relaxing in sunlight
  • synonym: solarium - a glass-enclosed porch or room designed for sunbathing or as a lounge
