change of pace

noun
a welcome variation from routine
2.7
Pronunciation: / tʃeɪndʒ əv peɪs / (AmE), / tʃeɪndʒ əv peɪs / (BrE)
an activity or experience that is different from what one usually does, providing a welcome variation from the usual routine

Examples

A change of pace
After months of city living, a week in the countryside was a welcome change of pace.
Cooking a new recipe can be a fun change of pace from your usual meals.
Joining a yoga class provided her with a healthy change of pace from her hectic schedule.

Related Terms

  • synonym: break - a brief period of rest from work or routine
