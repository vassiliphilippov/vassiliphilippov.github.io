abbreviation

noun
shortened form of a word or phrase
Pronunciation: / əˌbriː.viˈeɪ.ʃən /; / əˌbri.viˈeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / əˌbriː.viˈeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a shortened form of a word or phrase, made by omitting letters or using only initial letters
