fireless

adjective
without fire
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərləs / (AmE), / ˈfaɪələs / (BrE)
without fire, flames, or combustion; not having or producing fire

Examples

Fireless cabin
They spent a cold night in the fireless cabin.
The old stove was now completely fireless and covered in dust.
During the summer, the fireplace remained fireless and unused.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fiery - containing or producing fire
  • synonym: flameless - without flames or burning
