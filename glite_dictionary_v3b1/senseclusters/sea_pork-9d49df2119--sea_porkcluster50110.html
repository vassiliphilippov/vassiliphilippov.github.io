sea pork

noun
the flesh or meat of certain marine fish
7.5
Pronunciation: / siː pɔrk / (AmE), / siː pɔːk / (BrE)
the flesh of certain marine animals, such as porgy or porcupinefish, used as food; sometimes refers to either the fish itself or its meat

Examples

Sea pork meat
Adventurous diners often try sea pork when visiting coastal towns.
Fishermen sometimes refer to porgy as sea pork at the market.
The restaurant is famous for its delicious sea pork curry served with rice.
