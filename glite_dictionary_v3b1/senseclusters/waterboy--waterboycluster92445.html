waterboy

noun
person supplying water
4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈwɔː.tər.bɔɪ/ /; / /ˈwɑː.tər.bɔɪ/ (AmE), /ˈwɔː.tə.bɔɪ/ (BrE)
a person, usually a boy or young man, whose job is to provide drinking water to people, especially athletes, workers, or participants at events
