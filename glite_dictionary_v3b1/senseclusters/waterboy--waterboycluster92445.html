waterboy

noun
person supplying water
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtərˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
a person, usually a boy or young man, whose job is to provide drinking water to people, especially athletes, workers, or participants at events

Examples

Football waterboy
During the football game, the waterboy made sure every player stayed hydrated on the sidelines.
At the construction site, the waterboy refilled everyone's bottles and kept the crew hydrated all day.
The waterboy quickly handed out drinks to the runners as they passed the refreshment station.
During the match, the waterboy made sure every player stayed hydrated.
The coach thanked the waterboy for his quick response on the sidelines.
As a waterboy, he enjoyed being close to the action during games.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hydration - the process of providing or receiving adequate fluids
  • antonym: player - a person who takes part in a sports game
