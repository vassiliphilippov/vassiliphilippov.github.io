four-leaf clover

noun
rare clover with four leaflets; symbol of good luck
Pronunciation: / ˌfɔr ˌlif ˈkloʊvər / (AmE), / ˌfɔː ˌliːf ˈkləʊvə / (BrE)
a rare clover plant with four leaflets instead of the usual three, traditionally considered a symbol of good luck

Examples

A four-leaf clover plant
He spent the afternoon searching the field for a four-leaf clover but had no luck.
Many people believe that finding a four-leaf clover will bring them good fortune.
She pressed a four-leaf clover between the pages of her diary as a keepsake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shamrock - a clover with three leaves, symbol of Ireland
