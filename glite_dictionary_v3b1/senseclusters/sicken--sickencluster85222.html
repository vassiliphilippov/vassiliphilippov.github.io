sicken

verb
to become sick or ill
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪkən / (AmE), / ˈsɪkən / (BrE)
to become sick or ill; to fall ill

Examples

He sickened after dinner
After eating the spoiled food, he began to sicken within hours.
Several people may sicken if the flu continues to spread through the community.
She seemed fine all day, but suddenly started to sicken that evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fall ill - to become ill or sick
  • antonym: recover - to get well after being sick
