multitrack

adjective
using multiple tracks
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌtræk / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌtræk / (BrE)
involving or using several separate tracks for recording or playback, especially in audio production

Examples

Multitrack recording system
The studio installed new multitrack equipment to improve their recording capabilities.
With multitrack recording, each instrument can be edited separately for better sound quality.
Many modern songs are produced using multitrack techniques to layer vocals and instruments.
