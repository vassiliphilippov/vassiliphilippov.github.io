tumble

verb
perform gymnastic rolls and turns
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌmbəl / (AmE), / ˈtʌmbl̩ / (BrE)
to perform acrobatic movements that involve rolling and turning the body skillfully, especially in gymnastics

Examples

To tumble in gymnastics
Children love to tumble on the grass during recess.
She trained every day to tumble effortlessly in her gymnastics routine.
The gymnast began to tumble across the mat with a series of flips and rolls.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flip - a quick, acrobatic movement turning the body over
  • synonym: somersault - an acrobatic movement in which a person turns head over heels
