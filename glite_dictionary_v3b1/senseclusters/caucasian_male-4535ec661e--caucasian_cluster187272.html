Caucasian male

noun
a white male person
Pronunciation: / kɔːˈkeɪʒən meɪl / (AmE), / kɔːˈkeɪʒ(ɪ)ən meɪl / (BrE)
A man or boy who is classified as belonging to the white race, especially in demographic or social contexts.

Examples

Caucasian male group
As a Caucasian male, he was part of the largest demographic in the study.
The casting call specified that the role was for a Caucasian male aged 30 to 40.
The form required each applicant to state whether they were a Caucasian male or belonged to another group.

Related Terms
