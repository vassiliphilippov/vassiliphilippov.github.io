vena thoracica

noun
veins draining thoracic walls
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈviː.nə ˈθə.ræs.ɪ.kə/ (AmE), /ˈviː.nə ˈθə.ræs.ɪ.kə/ (BrE)
Any of several veins that drain blood from the thoracic walls.

Examples

The vena thoracica vein
A blockage in the vena thoracica can lead to complications involving blood return from the chest.
Surgeons must be careful not to damage the vena thoracica during operations on the thorax.
The vena thoracica carries blood from the chest wall to larger veins.
