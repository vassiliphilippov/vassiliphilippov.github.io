collaterally

adverb
indirectly or secondarily
7.1
Pronunciation: / kəˈlæt̬ɚəli / (AmE), / kəˈlæt.ər.əli / (BrE)
in a way that is secondary, indirect, or not the main focus or effect

Examples

Impacted collaterally
Several small businesses were collaterally impacted by the new tax regulations.
The decision to close the road affected nearby neighborhoods collaterally.
Her comments were not aimed at him, but he was collaterally offended.

Related Terms

  • antonym: directly - in a direct way, without anything in between
  • synonym: indirectly - not in a direct way
