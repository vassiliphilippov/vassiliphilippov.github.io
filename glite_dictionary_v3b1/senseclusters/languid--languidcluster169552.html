  • spiritless
    6.0 0.09/m
    spiritless feels empty and without drive in people or performances, while languid shows lazy, slow movements and low energy.
  • lethargic
    5.3 1/m
    lethargic means moving or acting very slowly from tiredness or illness, while languid implies a softer, relaxed lack of energy.
  • languorous
