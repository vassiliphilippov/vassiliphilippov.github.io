toquilla

noun
plant for hat straw
9.9
Pronunciation: / toʊˈkiːjə / (AmE), / təˈkiːjə / (BrE)
a tropical plant whose young leaves are used to make fine straw for weaving Panama hats and other goods

Examples

Toquilla plant leaves

Artisans carefully select young toquilla leaves to create high-quality straw hats.
The export of toquilla straw supports many local communities in South America.
