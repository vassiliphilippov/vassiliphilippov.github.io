urban sprawl

noun
expansion of cities into rural areas
8.5 4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈɜːr.bən sprɔːl/ (AmE), /ˈɜː.bən sprɔːl/ (BrE)
the uncontrolled and continuous expansion of urban areas into surrounding rural land, leading to a spread-out development pattern

Examples

Urban sprawl expansion
Many environmentalists are concerned about the effects of urban sprawl on local wildlife.
Policies have been introduced to control urban sprawl and encourage more sustainable cities.
The rapid urban sprawl has led to longer commutes and loss of green spaces.
