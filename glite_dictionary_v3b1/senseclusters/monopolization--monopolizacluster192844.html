monopolization

noun
exclusive control of a market
6.4
Pronunciation: / məˌnɑːpəlaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / məˌnɑːpələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / məˌnɒpələˈzeɪʃən /; / məˌnɒpəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
The act or process of gaining exclusive control over a market or commodity, excluding others from participation.
