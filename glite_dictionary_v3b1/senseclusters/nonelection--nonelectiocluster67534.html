nonelection

noun
period without elections
Pronunciation: / ˌnʌnɪˈlɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌnʌnɪˈlɛkʃən / (BrE)
a period or event during which no election is held

Examples

Nonelection year
The organization planned its major projects during a nonelection year to avoid political distractions.
Policy changes are often discussed in nonelection periods when there is less public scrutiny.
The mayor used the nonelection season to focus on long-term city improvements.
