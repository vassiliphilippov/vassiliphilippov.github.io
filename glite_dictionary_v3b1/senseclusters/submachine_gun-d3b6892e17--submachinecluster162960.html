submachine gun

noun
portable automatic firearm
Pronunciation: / sʌb.məˈʃin ˌɡʌn / (AmE), / sʌb.məˈʃiːn ˌɡʌn / (BrE)
A lightweight, portable automatic firearm designed to fire pistol-caliber cartridges, typically used by military, police, and security forces.

Examples

A submachine gun weapon
He carried a submachine gun as his primary weapon in close-quarters combat.
Modern submachine guns are often lightweight and easy to handle.
The police recovered a stolen submachine gun during the raid.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: machine gun - fully automatic gun designed for sustained rapid fire
