neroli oil

noun
aromatic oil from orange blossoms
4.3
Pronunciation: / nəˈroʊli ɔɪl / (AmE), / nəˈrəʊli ɔɪl / (BrE)
an aromatic yellow oil obtained from the blossoms of the bitter orange tree, used in perfumery and as a flavoring agent

Examples

neroli oil perfume
Many luxury perfumes contain neroli oil as one of their key ingredients.
She uses a few drops of neroli oil in her homemade skincare products for its soothing scent.
Some chefs add a hint of neroli oil to desserts for a delicate floral flavor.
