Synonyms

  • going
    1.0 0.25/m
    going describes the simple action of moving away from a place, while leaving often focuses on the event of departing from a person or place.
  • departure
    2.3 12/m
    departure means the moment or start of a journey away from a place, while leaving is any act of going away or departing.
  • exit
    1.0 6.60/m
    exit is a physical act of going out through a door or way, while leaving is the general act of going away.
  • going away
    3.2 2.61/m
    going away means leaving for a long time or permanently, while leaving can mean going away for any short or long time.
  • parting
    3.2 1.25/m
    parting is the act of saying goodbye with emotion or politeness, while leaving is simply going away from a person or place.
  • leave-taking
    7.0 0.10/m
    leave-taking is a formal, polite goodbye at the end of a visit, while leaving is the plain act of going away.
