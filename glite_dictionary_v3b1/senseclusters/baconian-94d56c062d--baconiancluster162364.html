Baconian

noun
follower of Francis Bacon
Pronunciation: / beɪˈkoʊniən / (AmE), / beɪˈkəʊniən / (BrE)
a follower or supporter of Francis Bacon, especially one who adopts his methods or ideas about scientific investigation

Examples

A true Baconian
As a dedicated Baconian, she valued careful observation and experimentation in her research.
The Baconian approach to science emphasizes evidence gathered through the senses.
Many modern scientists consider themselves Baconians in their commitment to empirical methods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: empiricist - a person who supports the theory that knowledge comes from experience
