Church of England

proper noun
national Christian church of England
3.7
Pronunciation: / tʃɝtʃ əv ˈɪŋɡlənd / (AmE), / tʃɜːtʃ əv ˈɪŋɡlənd / (BrE)
The national Christian church of England, with the Archbishop of Canterbury as its principal leader and the British monarch as its temporal head; also, any church elsewhere sharing its beliefs and practices.

Examples

The Church of England parish
After moving to Australia, she joined a local Church of England parish.
Many schools in the UK are affiliated with the Church of England.
The Church of England holds a special ceremony each year at Westminster Abbey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Anglican Church - a Christian church in communion with the Church of England
