wintergreen

noun
evergreen plant with spicy red berries, source of oil
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪntərˌɡrin /; / ˈwɪntɚˌɡrin / (AmE), / ˈwɪntəɡriːn /; / ˈwɪntəˌɡriːn / (BrE)
a low-growing North American evergreen shrub (Gaultheria procumbens) with spicy red berries and aromatic leaves, used to produce wintergreen oil

Examples

Wintergreen leaves and berries
She picked a few wintergreen leaves to make a soothing tea.
The aroma of wintergreen oil is commonly used in candies and medicinal products.
The woods were dotted with patches of wintergreen and their bright red berries.
Wintergreen oil is often used as a flavoring in candies and chewing gum.
Hikers found patches of wintergreen along the forest trail in the summer.
The aroma of wintergreen filled the room after she rubbed the leaves between her fingers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: teaberry - another term for wintergreen (Gaultheria procumbens)
