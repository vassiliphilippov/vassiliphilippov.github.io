capitellum

noun
small flower head
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkæpɪˈtɛləm / (AmE), / ˌkæpɪˈtɛləm / (BrE)
a small, compact flower head, especially as found in certain plants, being a diminutive form of capitulum

Examples

In spring, the meadow was dotted with the bright capitella of wildflowers.
