ould fella

noun
affectionate term for an old man
Pronunciation: / oʊld ˈfɛl.ə / (AmE), / əʊld ˈfel.ə / (BrE)
An affectionate or familiar term for an old man, especially in Irish English.

Examples

The ould fella
The ould fella always sat by the fire, telling stories from his youth.
The village honored the ould fella on his ninetieth birthday with a special celebration.
Whenever I needed advice, I'd go straight to the ould fella for help.
