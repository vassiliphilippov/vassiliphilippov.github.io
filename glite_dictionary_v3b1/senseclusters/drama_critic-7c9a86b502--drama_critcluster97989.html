drama critic

noun
person who reviews theatrical performances
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɑmə ˌkrɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˈdrɑːmə ˌkrɪtɪk / (BrE)
a person who reviews and evaluates theatrical performances for newspapers, magazines, or other media

Examples

A drama critic reviews plays
As a seasoned drama critic, she attends dozens of performances each month.
The drama critic wrote a glowing review of the play's opening night.
The theatre company invited a well-known drama critic to their latest production.
