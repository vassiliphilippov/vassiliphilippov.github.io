longe

noun
horse training rein
9.9
Pronunciation: / lʌndʒ / (AmE), / lɒndʒ / (BrE)
a long rein or rope used to train or exercise a horse from the ground by guiding it in circles

Examples

A longe line
The instructor attached the longe to the horse's halter before starting the lesson.
Using a longe helps trainers control young horses safely from a distance.
She learned to keep the longe taut while the horse trotted in a large circle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lunge line - a long rope used for exercising horses in a circle
