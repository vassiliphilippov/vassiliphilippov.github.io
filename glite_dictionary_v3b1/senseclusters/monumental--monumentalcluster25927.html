monumental

adjective
of great significance or importance
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːn.jəˈmen.t̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˌmɒn.jʊˈmen.təl / (BrE)
extremely important or having great significance; outstanding in size, scope, or achievement

Examples

Monumental achievement
Her novel is considered a monumental work in modern literature.
The decision to expand the company was a monumental step for the entire industry.
The scientists achieved monumental progress in their research this year.
