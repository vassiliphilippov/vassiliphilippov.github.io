transplacement

noun
job reassignment
8.9
Pronunciation: / trænsˈpleɪsmənt / (AmE), / trænsˈpleɪsmənt / (BrE)
the reassignment or relocation of a person, especially an employee, to a different job or location within an organization

Examples

Employee transplacement
The company announced the transplacement of several employees to its new branch office.
After the restructuring, many staff members faced transplacement to different departments.
He accepted the transplacement offer and moved to the firm's headquarters in another city.

Related Terms

  • antonym: promotion - the act of raising someone to a higher position or rank
  • synonym: transfer - the act of moving someone to a different job or location
