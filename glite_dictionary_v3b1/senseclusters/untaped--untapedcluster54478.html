untaped

adjective
not recorded on tape or film
5.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈteɪpt / (AmE), / ʌnˈteɪpt / (BrE)
not recorded on film, audio, or videotape

Examples

An untaped lecture
Because the lecture was untaped, students had to rely on their own notes.
Many of the artist's earliest performances are untaped and lost to history.
The meeting went untaped, so there is no official record of what was discussed.
