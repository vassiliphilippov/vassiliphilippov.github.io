restorationist

noun
supporter of restoration
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛstəˈreɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌrɛstəˈreɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports the return of a former government, ruler, or monarchy to power

Examples

Royal restorationist
The restorationist argued passionately for the return of the old royal family to the throne.
After the revolution, restorationists organized rallies demanding the reinstatement of the previous government.
Many restorationists believed that bringing back the monarchy would restore stability to the country.

Related Terms

  • antonym: republican - a person who supports a republic instead of a monarchy
  • synonym: royalist - a supporter of a monarchy or king
