foxhole

noun
military dugout shelter for one person
2.9 5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈfɑːks.hoʊl/ (AmE), /ˈfɒks.həʊl/ (BrE)
a small hole or pit dug in the ground by a soldier to provide shelter from enemy fire
