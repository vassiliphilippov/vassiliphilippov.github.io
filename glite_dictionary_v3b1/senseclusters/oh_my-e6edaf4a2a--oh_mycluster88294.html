oh my

interjection
expression of surprise or delight
1.1
Pronunciation: / oʊ maɪ / (AmE), / əʊ maɪ / (BrE)
used to express surprise, delight, or other strong emotion, often with varying intensity depending on context

Examples

Oh my goodness
Oh my, the weather changed so quickly today!
Oh my, what an adorable puppy you have!
When she saw the gift, she gasped, 'oh my, I wasn't expecting this!'

Related Terms

  • synonym: oh dear - expression used to show surprise or concern
  • synonym: oh my goodness - expression of surprise or mild shock
