earthstar

noun
a star-shaped fungus
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrθˌstɑr / (AmE), / ˈɜːθˌstɑː / (BrE)
a fungus of the family Geastraceae, having a round spore sac with an outer layer that splits open in the shape of a star

Examples

The earthstar fungus
Children were fascinated by the earthstar, which looked as though it had sprouted from a different planet.
The distinctive shape of the earthstar makes it easy to identify among other fungi.
We found an earthstar growing near the forest trail, its rays spread open like a starfish.
