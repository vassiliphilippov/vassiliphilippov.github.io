univocality

noun
complete agreement
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːnɪvoʊˈkæləti / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪvəʊˈkælɪti / (BrE)
the state of complete agreement or having only one clear meaning or opinion among people or groups

Examples

Univocality in the group
The committee reached univocality on the new policy after a long discussion.
True univocality is rare in large organizations with many different opinions.
The group's univocality made the decision-making process much faster and smoother.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consensus - general agreement among a group
  • antonym: discord - disagreement or lack of harmony
  • synonym: unanimity - complete agreement by everyone involved
