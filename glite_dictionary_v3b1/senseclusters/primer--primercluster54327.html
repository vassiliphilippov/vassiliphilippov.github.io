primer

noun
preparatory coat of paint or sealant
5.5 1.08/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆpraÉªmÉ™r / (AmE), / ËˆprÉªmÉ™ / (BrE)
a first or preparatory coat of paint or sealant applied to a surface to improve adhesion of subsequent layers or to protect the material

Examples

A paint primer
Applying a primer ensures that the paint adheres better to the wall.
He forgot to use primer before painting, so the finish looks uneven.
Most professionals recommend sanding the surface lightly after the primer dries.

Synonyms

  • primer coat
    5.2 0.02/m
    primer coat is the first paint layer applied to help later coats stick, while primer is the general product used for that job.
  • undercoat
    4.3 0.03/m
    undercoat is a base layer applied for build up or colour blocking before finishing, while primer is mainly a surface sealer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: undercoat - a coat of paint applied before the final coat
