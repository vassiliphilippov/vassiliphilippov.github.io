unworried

adjective
not feeling worried
2.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɝːid / (AmE), / ʌnˈwʌrid / (BrE)
not feeling anxious, troubled, or concerned; free from worry.

Examples

An unworried person
Despite the falling stock prices, the manager remained unworried and optimistic about the future.
She seemed completely unworried about the test results, even though others were anxious.
The children played in the park, unworried by the dark clouds gathering overhead.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carefree - free from anxiety and responsibility; without worry
  • antonym: worried - feeling or showing anxiety or concern
