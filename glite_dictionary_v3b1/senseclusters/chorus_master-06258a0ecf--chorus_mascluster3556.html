chorus master

noun
choir director
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔːrəs ˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈkɔːrəs ˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a person who directs and trains a choir or chorus, especially in preparation for performances

Examples

The chorus master rehearses singers
The chorus master worked tirelessly to prepare the singers for the opera's opening night.
As chorus master, she coordinated rehearsals and ensured every voice blended perfectly.
The orchestra and chorus master collaborated closely to achieve a harmonious performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: choirmaster - a person who leads and trains a choir
  • synonym: conductor - a person who directs a musical performance
