indicant

noun
sign or indicator
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndɪkənt / (AmE), / ˈɪndɪkənt / (BrE)
something that serves to indicate or suggest; a sign, symptom, or indication.

Examples

A clear indicant
Fever is one common indicant of infection in patients.
Physical symptoms act as an indicant of underlying health issues.
Rising unemployment rates can be an indicant of economic distress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indicator - something that indicates
  • synonym: symptom - a sign or indication of something, especially of disease
