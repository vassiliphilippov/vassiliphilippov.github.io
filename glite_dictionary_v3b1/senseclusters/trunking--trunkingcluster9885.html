trunking

noun
cable management system
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtrʌŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
a system of enclosed channels or conduits used to organize and protect electrical cables or wires

Examples

Electrical trunking system
The electrician installed trunking along the wall to keep the wires organized and safe.
Modern offices often use plastic trunking to hide and protect network cables.
Before painting the room, they checked that all the trunking was securely fixed in place.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cable tray - a support system for electrical cables
  • synonym: conduit - a tube or channel for protecting electrical wires
