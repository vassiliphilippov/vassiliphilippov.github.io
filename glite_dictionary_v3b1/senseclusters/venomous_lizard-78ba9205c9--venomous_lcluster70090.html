venomous lizard

noun
large lizard with venom glands
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛnəməs ˈlɪzərd / (AmE), / ˈvɛnəməs ˈlɪzəd / (BrE)
a large, heavy-bodied lizard, such as the Gila monster or beaded lizard, known for possessing venom glands

Examples

A venomous lizard bite
Scientists discovered that the bite of a venomous lizard can be harmful to humans.
The Gila monster is a well-known example of a venomous lizard found in North America.
Unlike most reptiles, venomous lizards have specialized glands that produce toxic saliva.
