cipher

noun
a secret code or method of writing
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪfər / (AmE), / ˈsʌɪfə / (BrE)
a secret or disguised way of writing; a code used to conceal the meaning of a message

Examples

A secret cipher
The diary was written in a simple cipher that took days to decode.
The spies communicated in a cipher to keep their messages safe from enemies.
Unless you can break the cipher, you won't be able to read the encrypted letter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: code - a system of words, letters, figures, or symbols used to represent others, especially for secrecy
  • antonym: decipher - to convert a coded message into normal language
