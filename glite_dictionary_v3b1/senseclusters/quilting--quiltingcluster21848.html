quilting

noun
stitching layers of fabric to form a design
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪl.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkwɪl.tɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of stitching together layers of fabric and a filling, often to create a decorative pattern or design

Examples

Quilting a blanket
He admired the intricate patterns created by the quilting on the blanket.
She spent hours working on the quilting for her new bedspread.
The museum featured exhibits on traditional quilting techniques from around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: quilt - a bed covering made of layers of fabric stitched together
