Easter

noun
Christian festival celebrating the resurrection of Jesus
3.2 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈiː.stər/ (AmE), /ˈiː.stə/ (BrE)
The most important Christian festival, celebrating the resurrection of Jesus Christ, observed on the Sunday following the first full moon after the vernal equinox.

Examples

The Easter festival
Children often hunt for eggs as part of traditional Easter festivities.
Churches hold sunrise services on Easter to mark the resurrection of Christ.
Many families gather for a special meal on Easter Sunday to celebrate together.
