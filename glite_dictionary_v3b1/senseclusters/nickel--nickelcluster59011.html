nickel

verb
to coat with a layer of nickel
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈnɪkəl / (BrE)
to cover or plate (a metal object) with a thin layer of nickel, especially by electrochemical means

Examples

To nickel a surface
Many old coins have been nickeled to restore their appearance.
Please make sure the handles are properly nickeled before assembly.
The company decided to nickel the car parts to make them more resistant to corrosion.
