intravascular

adjective
inside blood vessels
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈvæskjələr / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈvæskjʊlə / (BrE)
located or occurring within a blood vessel

Examples

Intravascular pressure
The doctor monitored the intravascular pressure during the surgery.
An intravascular injection delivers medication directly into the bloodstream.
Researchers studied the effects of intravascular clot formation in patients with heart disease.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extravascular - located or occurring outside a blood vessel
