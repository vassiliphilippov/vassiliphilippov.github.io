profits

noun
financial gain after expenses
1.0 2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːfɪts / (AmE), / ˈprɒfɪts / (BrE)
the money that is left after deducting all costs, expenses, and taxes from total revenue within a given period

Examples

Company profits
After deducting all expenses, the bakery reported healthy profits for the year.
Many investors are interested in companies with strong quarterly profits.
The company's profits rose significantly after introducing the new product line.

Related Terms

  • synonym: earnings - money obtained from work or investments, often used as a synonym for profits
  • antonym: losses - the amount by which expenses or costs exceed revenue
