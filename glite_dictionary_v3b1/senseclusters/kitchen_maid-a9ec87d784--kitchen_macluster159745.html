kitchen maid

noun
female kitchen servant
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪtʃən meɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɪtʃən meɪd / (BrE)
a female servant whose job is to help with cooking and cleaning in the kitchen, especially in a large house

Examples

A kitchen maid helps
The wealthy family employed a kitchen maid to assist with daily meal preparations.
As a young kitchen maid, she learned many traditional recipes from the head cook.
The kitchen maid started her work early each morning, cleaning and chopping vegetables.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: cook - a person who prepares food, especially as a job
  • synonym: scullery maid - a female servant responsible for cleaning dishes and kitchen areas
