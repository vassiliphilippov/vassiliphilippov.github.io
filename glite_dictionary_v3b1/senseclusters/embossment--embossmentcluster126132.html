embossment

noun
raised impression or design
8.4 6.6
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbɑsmənt /; / ɛmˈbɔsmənt / (AmE), / ɪmˈbɒsmənt / (BrE)
a raised design or pattern on a surface, made by pressing, carving, molding, or stamping so it stands out

Examples

A decorative embossment
A decorative embossment gives the invitation a touch of elegance.
He studied the embossment on the book's leather cover.
The certificate featured an official embossment in the lower corner.
She admired the delicate floral embossment on the silver vase.
The ancient temple walls were decorated with intricate embossments depicting historical scenes.
The book cover featured a beautiful embossment that made it stand out on the shelf.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emboss - to raise or mold into a surface by pressure
  • synonym: relief - sculptural technique where elements are raised from the background
