harness

verb
put a harness on an animal
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrnəs / (AmE), / ˈhɑːnɪs / (BrE)
to put a harness on (an animal, especially a horse) in order to attach it to a cart, plow, etc.

Examples

To harness a horse
Before the ride, she carefully harnessed the horse to the carriage.
He struggled to harness the restless pony before their journey.
The farmer harnessed the oxen to help plow the field.
