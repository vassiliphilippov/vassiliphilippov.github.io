dale

noun
broad valley in hills
8.5
Pronunciation: / deɪl / (AmE), / deɪl / (BrE)
A broad, open valley, especially one in a hilly area.

Examples

A dale valley
Legends tell of ancient castles hiding in the heart of the dale.
The hikers made their way across the green dale surrounded by gentle hills.
The river winds through the dale, providing water for the farms nearby.

Related Terms

  • synonym: valley - A low area of land between hills or mountains, often with a river flowing through it
