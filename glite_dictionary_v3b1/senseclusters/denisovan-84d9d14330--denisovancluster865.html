Denisovan

adjective
of ancient Denisovans
Pronunciation: / dəˈniːsəvən / (AmE), / dɛˈniːsəvən / (BrE)
relating to the Denisovans, an extinct species or subspecies of archaic humans known from fossils and genetic evidence.

Examples

Denisovan fossils found
Scientists discovered Denisovan tools in a Siberian cave, shedding light on their culture.
The Denisovan genome reveals interbreeding with both Neanderthals and modern humans.
Researchers compared Denisovan fossils with those of other ancient human species.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Neanderthal - relating to an extinct species of archaic humans closely related to modern humans
  • antonym: modern human - relating to Homo sapiens, the only surviving human species
