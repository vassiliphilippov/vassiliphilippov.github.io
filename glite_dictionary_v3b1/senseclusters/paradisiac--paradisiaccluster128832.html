paradisiac

adjective
relating to Paradise; heavenly
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈdiːziˌæk / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈdɪzɪˌæk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of Paradise; heavenly or blissful.

Examples

A paradisiac place
The artist tried to capture a paradisiac innocence in his painting.
The garden had a paradisiac beauty that left every visitor in awe.
They spoke of a paradisiac place where suffering did not exist.
