tea leaf

noun
dried leaf of tea plant
Pronunciation: / ti lif / (AmE), / tiː liːf / (BrE)
a dried leaf of the tea plant, used to make tea

Examples

Dried tea leaves
High-quality tea leaves are essential for making a flavorful brew.

The plantation workers carefully picked each tea leaf by hand.
