ethnic slur

noun
offensive term for race or ethnicity
Pronunciation: / ˈɛθ.nɪk slɝ / (AmE), / ˈɛθ.nɪk slɜː / (BrE)
an offensive word or expression used to insult or demean someone on the basis of their race, nationality, or language

Examples

An ethnic slur insult
He apologized publicly for making an ethnic slur during the interview.
The company enacted strict policies against any use of ethnic slurs in the workplace.
Using an ethnic slur in conversation is deeply hurtful and offensive.

  • synonym: racial slur - an offensive term related to someone's race
