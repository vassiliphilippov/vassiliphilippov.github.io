ozocerite

noun
waxy mineral made of hydrocarbons
9.0 9.9
Pronunciation: /oʊ.zə.ˈkɜːr.aɪt/ /; / /oʊ.zə.ˈkɑːr.aɪt/ (AmE), /əʊ.zə.ˈkɜːr.aɪt/ /; / /əʊ.zə.ˈkɑːr.aɪt/ (BrE)
