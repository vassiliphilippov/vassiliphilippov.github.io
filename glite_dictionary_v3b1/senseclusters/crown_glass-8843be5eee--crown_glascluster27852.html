crown glass

noun
optical glass with low dispersion and refractive index

A type of optical glass with low dispersion and a low refractive index, used especially in lenses.

Examples

Crown glass lens
Early telescope makers relied on crown glass before advanced materials were available.
Optical engineers often choose crown glass for its low refractive index in precision lenses.
The camera lens contains elements made from crown glass to minimize distortion.
