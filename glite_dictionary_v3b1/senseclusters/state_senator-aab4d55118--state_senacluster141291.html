state senator

noun
member of a state legislative senate
3.0
Pronunciation: / steɪt ˈsɛnətɚ / (AmE), / steɪt ˈsɛnətə / (BrE)
a member of the legislative upper chamber in a state government, especially in the United States

Examples

He was elected as a state senator after serving two terms in the House of Representatives.
Several state senators gathered for the annual legislative conference in the capital.
The state senator introduced a bill to improve education funding across the state.
