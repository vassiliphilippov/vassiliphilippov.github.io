mechanical drawing

noun
detailed technical scale drawing
2.0
Pronunciation: / məˈkænɪkəl ˈdrɔɪŋ / (AmE), / mɪˈkænɪkəl ˈdrɔːɪŋ / (BrE)
A scale drawing showing the details and dimensions of a machine, building, or other object, used especially in engineering and architecture.

Examples

A mechanical drawing of a machine
Accurate mechanical drawings are essential for assembling complex machines.
Students in architecture class must learn to read and produce a mechanical drawing.
The engineer created a mechanical drawing of the new engine part before manufacturing began.

  • synonym: drafting - the act or technique of producing technical drawings
