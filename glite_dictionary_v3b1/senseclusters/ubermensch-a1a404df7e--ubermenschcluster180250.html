Ubermensch

noun
person with exceptional powers or abilities
9.9 0.13/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàuÀêb…öÀåm…õn É / (AmE), / ÀàuÀêb…ôm…õn É / (BrE)
A person of extraordinary powers or abilities, often referring to Nietzsche's concept of an ideal or superior individual.

Examples

The Ubermensch ideal
Nietzsche's idea of the Ubermensch inspired many thinkers in the twentieth century.
She was described by her followers as an Ubermensch, able to overcome any challenge.
Some comic book heroes are modeled on the concept of the Ubermensch.
