axeman

noun
person using an axe
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈæksˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈæksmən / (BrE)
a person who uses an axe, especially a woodcutter or an executioner

Examples

A axeman chops wood
The axeman chopped wood for the campfire before sunset.
In old stories, the axeman was feared as the one who carried out executions.
The skilled axeman could split a log with a single swing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: executioner - a person who carries out a death sentence
  • synonym: woodcutter - a person who cuts down trees or chops wood
