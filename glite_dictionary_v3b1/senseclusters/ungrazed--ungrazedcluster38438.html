ungrazed

adjective
not eaten by animals
5.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡreɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡreɪzd / (BrE)
not eaten or fed on by grazing animals such as cattle or sheep

Examples

Ungrazed field
The ungrazed meadow was covered in wildflowers and tall grass.
After the fence was built, the land remained ungrazed for several years.
Scientists studied the differences between grazed and ungrazed areas in the pasture.
