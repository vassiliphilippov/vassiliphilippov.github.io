silk stocking

noun
sheer women's stocking
Pronunciation: / sɪlk ˈstɑkɪŋ / (AmE), / sɪlk ˈstɒkɪŋ / (BrE)
A women's stocking made from a smooth, sheer fabric such as silk, nylon, or rayon.

Examples

A pair of silk stockings
He bought her a pair of black silk stockings as a special gift.
She wore a beautiful dress with silk stockings to the party.
Vintage fashion often features women wearing silk stockings and elegant hats.
