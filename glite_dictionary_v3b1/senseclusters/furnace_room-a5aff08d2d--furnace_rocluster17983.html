furnace room

noun
room containing a building's furnace
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝːnɪs ruːm / (AmE), / ˈfɜːnɪs ruːm / (BrE)
A room, usually in the basement of a building, that contains a furnace used for heating the building.

Examples

The furnace room
Maintenance workers checked the furnace room for any signs of a gas leak.
The old house still had its original furnace room in the basement.
We stored our winter boots in the furnace room because it was always warm.
