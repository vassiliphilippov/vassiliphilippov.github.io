battle of Langside

proper noun
Scottish battle (1568), Mary Queen of Scots' defeat
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt̬l əv ˈlæŋsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv ˈlæŋsaɪd / (BrE)
a battle fought in 1568 near Glasgow in which Catholic forces supporting Mary Queen of Scots were defeated by Protestant forces

Examples
