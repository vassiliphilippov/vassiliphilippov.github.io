Gorgoniacea

noun
obsolete taxonomic group of sea fans and red corals
Pronunciation: / ɡɔːrˌɡoʊniˈeɪsiə / (AmE), / ɡɔːˌɡəʊnɪˈeɪsɪə / (BrE)
An obsolete order of colonial marine corals including sea fans and red corals, formerly used in classification.

Examples

Gorgoniacea sea fans
Scientists used to classify many sea fans under Gorgoniacea.
Specimens from the Gorgoniacea are often found in tropical oceans.
The Gorgoniacea were once distinguished by their fan-like colonial forms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: red coral - a type of coral known for its red or pink skeleton, used in jewelry
  • synonym: sea fan - a type of soft coral with a fan-shaped structure
