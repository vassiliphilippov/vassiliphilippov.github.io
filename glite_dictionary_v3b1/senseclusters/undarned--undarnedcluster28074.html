undarned

adjective
not mended
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdɑrnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈdɑːnd / (BrE)
not mended by darning; still having a hole or tear in fabric

Examples

An undarned hole
She noticed an undarned patch on the elbow of her sweater.
His old scarf remained undarned despite the small hole near the edge.
The undarned socks were uncomfortable to wear during the long walk.

Related Terms

  • antonym: repaired - mended or fixed
  • synonym: torn - having a hole or rip
