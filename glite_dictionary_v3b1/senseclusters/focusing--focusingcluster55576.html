focusing

noun
the act of bringing into focus
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊkəsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfəʊkəsɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of adjusting something so that it becomes clear or is brought into focus

Examples

Focusing a camera
She adjusted the lens, carefully focusing the camera on the butterfly.
The focusing of the microscope took several minutes to get a clear view.
With better focusing, the blurry shapes became crisp and recognizable on the screen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: focus - the point at which an image is sharp or clear
