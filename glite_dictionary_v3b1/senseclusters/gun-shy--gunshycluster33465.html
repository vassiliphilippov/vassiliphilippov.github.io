gun-shy

adjective
nervous or hesitant due to past experiences
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌn ʃaɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌn ʃaɪ / (BrE)
nervous or hesitant, especially because of previous bad experiences.

Examples

Be gun-shy after accident
After the accident, he became gun-shy about driving in the rain.
Investors are gun-shy after last year's market crash.
She was gun-shy about joining another project after her previous bad experience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cautious - being careful to avoid danger or risk
  • antonym: confident - feeling or showing certainty; not hesitant or nervous
