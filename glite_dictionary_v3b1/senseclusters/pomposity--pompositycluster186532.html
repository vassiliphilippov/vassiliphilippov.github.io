pomposity

noun
self-importance; pompous behavior
Pronunciation: / pɑmˈpɑsɪti / (AmE), / pɒmˈpɒsɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being pompous; self-importance or exaggerated dignity in manner, style, or language

Examples

His pomposity annoyed

Her speech was filled with pomposity, making it hard for the audience to relate.
The professor's pomposity alienated many of his students.
