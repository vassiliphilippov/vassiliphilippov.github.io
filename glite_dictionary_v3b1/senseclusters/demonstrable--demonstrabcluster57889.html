Examples

A demonstrable fact
Her innocence was demonstrable in the face of all the evidence.
The results provided demonstrable proof of his theory.
There is no demonstrable connection between the two incidents.
Her claims are not demonstrable by any scientific standards.
The benefits of the new method must be demonstrable before we adopt it.
There is no demonstrable evidence linking the suspect to the crime.

Synonyms

  • provable
    5.0 0.71/m
    provable is shown true by logical argument or evidence, while demonstrable is presented so others can clearly see or test it.
  • verifiable
    5.1 2.16/m
    verifiable can be checked by observing facts or tests, while demonstrable is shown true by clear evidence or argument.
  • confirmable
    5.1 0.13/m
    confirmable can be proved true or false by experiment or further tests, while demonstrable is shown to be true by evidence.
