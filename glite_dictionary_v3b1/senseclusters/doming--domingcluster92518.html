doming

noun
surface bulging
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈdoʊ.mɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈdəʊ.mɪŋ/ (BrE)
a process or effect in which a surface bulges outward to form a dome shape

Examples

Plastic doming
The plastic sheet showed clear doming after being exposed to heat.
Improper installation of the tiles resulted in noticeable doming across the floor.
Repeated pressure on the metal panel caused gradual doming over time.
