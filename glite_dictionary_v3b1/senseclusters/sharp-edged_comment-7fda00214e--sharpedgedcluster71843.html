sharp-edged comment

noun
critical or cutting remark
3.5 5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈʃɑːr.edʒd ˈkɑː.mənt/ (AmE), /ˈʃɑːr.edʒd ˈkɒ.mənt/ /; / /ˈʃɑːp.edʒd ˈkɒ.mənt/ (BrE)

Examples

Tired of his sharp-edged comments, the team asked for a more positive approach.
