flowering quince

noun
ornamental shrub with spiny branches and colorful flowers
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊɚɪŋ kwɪns / (AmE), / ˈflaʊərɪŋ kwɪns / (BrE)
An Asiatic ornamental shrub (genus Chaenomeles) with spiny branches and pink or red blossoms, often grown for its decorative flowers.
