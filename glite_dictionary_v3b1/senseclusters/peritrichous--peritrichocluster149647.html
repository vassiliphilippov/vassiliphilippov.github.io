peritrichous

adjective
having flagella all over the surface
4.2 9.9
Pronunciation: /pə.rɪ.ˈtrɪ.kəs/ (AmE), /pə.rɪ.ˈtrɪ.kəs/ (BrE)
(of a microorganism) having flagella distributed uniformly over the entire surface of the cell.

Examples

Peritrichous bacteria flagella
In microbiology class, we studied several peritrichous species under the microscope.
The peritrichous arrangement of flagella enables the bacterium to move efficiently in any direction.
Unlike monotrichous bacteria, peritrichous cells have flagella covering their entire surface.
