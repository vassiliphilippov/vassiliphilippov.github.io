natterjack

noun
a short-legged toad of western Europe
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈnætərˌdʒæk / (AmE), / ˈnatədʒak / (BrE)
a small brownish-yellow toad of western Europe with short legs, known for running rather than hopping

Examples

Conservationists are working to protect the rare habitats needed by the natterjack in the UK.
