contractee

noun
recipient of a contract
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːntrækˈtiː / (AmE), / ˌkɒntrækˈtiː / (BrE)
a person or organization that receives a contract and is responsible for fulfilling its terms

Examples

The contractee signs
The contractee is required to review all terms before signing the agreement.
If the contractee fails to meet the deadlines, penalties may apply.
Both the contractor and the contractee must keep copies of the signed contract.

Related Terms

  • synonym: client - a person or organization receiving services from a professional or company
  • antonym: contractor - a person or company that agrees to provide goods or services under a contract
