Sarda sarda

noun
species of tuna-like food fish
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑrdə ˈsɑrdə / (AmE), / ˈsɑːdə ˈsɑːdə / (BrE)
A medium-sized tuna-like food fish (Atlantic bonito) found in warm Atlantic and Pacific waters, less valued than tuna.

Examples

The Sarda sarda fish
Fishermen reported a large catch of Sarda sarda off the coast this morning.
The restaurant specializes in grilled Sarda sarda served with Mediterranean spices.
Unlike tuna, Sarda sarda is often used in traditional Atlantic coastal recipes.
