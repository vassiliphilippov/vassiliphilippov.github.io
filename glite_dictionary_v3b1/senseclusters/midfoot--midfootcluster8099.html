midfoot

noun
middle of foot
3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪd.fʊt/ (AmE), /ˈmɪd.fʊt/ (BrE)
the middle part of the foot located between the heel and the toes

Examples

Pain in the midfoot
The doctor examined the patient's midfoot for signs of swelling after the accident.
Pain in the midfoot can make walking and running very difficult.
A fracture in the midfoot may require several weeks to heal completely.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forefoot - the front part of the foot near the toes
  • antonym: hindfoot - the back part of the foot near the heel
