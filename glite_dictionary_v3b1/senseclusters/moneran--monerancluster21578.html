moneran

noun
single-celled organism from the former Monera kingdom
9.9
Pronunciation: / moʊˈnɪr.ən / (AmE), / məˈnɪə.rən / (BrE)
an organism formerly classified in the kingdom Monera, typically reproducing asexually and obtaining nutrients by absorption, photosynthesis, or chemosynthesis

Examples

A moneran organism
Scientists once classified both bacteria and blue-green algae as monerans.
The moneran can reproduce rapidly through simple cell division.
With advances in genetics, the classification of moneran became obsolete.
