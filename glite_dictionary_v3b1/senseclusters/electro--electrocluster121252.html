electro

noun
informal ECT term
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktroʊ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for electroconvulsive therapy, a medical treatment involving electrical stimulation of the brain for severe mental illness

Examples

An electro treatment
After months of therapy, the doctor recommended an electro to help with his severe depression.
Some patients are nervous about undergoing an electro, but it can be very effective.
The hospital offers electro as a last resort for treatment-resistant mental illness.
