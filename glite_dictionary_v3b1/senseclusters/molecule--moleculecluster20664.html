molecule

noun
a tiny piece or particle of anything
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑː.lɪ.kjuːl / (AmE), / ˈmɒl.ɪ.kjuːl / (BrE)
(nontechnical) A very tiny piece or particle of anything; a minute amount.

Examples

A molecule of hope
I don’t have a molecule of interest in their argument.
Not a molecule of evidence was left after the cleanup.
There wasn't a molecule of doubt in her mind about his honesty.
