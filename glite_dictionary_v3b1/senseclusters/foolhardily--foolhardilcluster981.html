foolhardily

adverb
recklessly boldly
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfuːlˌhɑːrdɪli / (AmE), / ˈfuːlhɑːdɪli / (BrE)
in a way that shows reckless or rash boldness, without considering the risks

Examples

To foolhardily jump in
He foolhardily jumped into the river without checking how deep it was.
The hikers foolhardily ignored the storm warnings and continued up the mountain.
She foolhardily invested all her savings in a risky business venture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cautiously - in a way that avoids risk or danger
  • synonym: recklessly - in a way that shows a lack of care about danger or risk
