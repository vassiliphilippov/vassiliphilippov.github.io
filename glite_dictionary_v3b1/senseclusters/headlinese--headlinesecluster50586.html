headlinese

noun
abbreviated style used in news headlines
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌlaɪniz / (AmE), / ˈhɛdlaɪniːz / (BrE)
the distinctive, abbreviated style of language used in newspaper headlines, characterized by short words, omissions, and condensed grammar

Many newspapers use headlinese to grab readers' attention quickly.
