trim

adjective
neat and smart in appearance
9.3 2.5
Pronunciation: /trɪm/ (AmE), /trɪm/ (BrE)
neat, tidy, or slim in appearance; well-groomed or simply designed

Examples

A trim figure
He kept his desk trim and organized at all times.
She wore a trim suit that gave her a professional look.
The trim garden reflected the owner's meticulous care.
His trim appearance made him look much younger than his actual age.
Regular swimming helps her stay trim and healthy.
She keeps her figure trim by jogging every morning.
Even after the storm, the crew worked hard to restore the yacht to its trim state.
She always keeps her office trim and organized, making it a pleasant place to work.
The hedges in front of the house are beautifully trim and well-maintained.
