signposting

noun
guiding language cues
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪnˌpoʊstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪnpəʊstɪŋ / (BrE)
the use of words or phrases to guide listeners or readers through the structure of a text or speech

Examples

Essay signposting
Effective signposting helps readers understand the main points of an essay.
The teacher emphasized the importance of signposting when giving presentations.
Without clear signposting, the audience may get lost during a long speech.

Related Terms

  • synonym: structure - the organization or arrangement of parts in a text or speech
  • synonym: transition - a word or phrase that links different parts of speech or writing
