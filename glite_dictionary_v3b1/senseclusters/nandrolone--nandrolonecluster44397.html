nandrolone

noun
synthetic anabolic steroid
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈnændroʊˌloʊn / (AmE), / ˈnændrəʊˌləʊn / (BrE)
a synthetic androgenic steroid used to treat testosterone deficiency, breast cancer, and osteoporosis

Examples

Nandrolone treatment
Nandrolone is sometimes used in the treatment of osteoporosis in postmenopausal women.
Some athletes have been banned for using nandrolone to enhance performance.
The doctor prescribed nandrolone to address his testosterone deficiency.
