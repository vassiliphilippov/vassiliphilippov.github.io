turf war

noun
bitter struggle for territory or control
Pronunciation: / tɝf wɔɹ /; / tɝf wɔr / (AmE), / tɜːf wɔː / (BrE)
a bitter struggle or conflict between groups for control over a particular area or sphere of influence

Examples

A turf war between gangs
Authorities intervened before the rival crews' turf war escalated into open violence.
Political factions began a fierce turf war within the organization, leading to instability.
The two companies became locked in a turf war over market share in the city.
A violent turf war erupted between rival gangs in the city.
Recent changes led to a subtle turf war among senior managers.
The two departments are locked in a turf war over who manages the new project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conflict - a serious disagreement or argument, typically a protracted one
  • synonym: power struggle - a fight for control or dominance between groups or individuals
  • antonym: truce - a suspension of fighting; a peace agreement
