francolin

noun
African-Asian game bird
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfræŋkəlɪn / (AmE), / ˈfræŋkəlɪn / (BrE)
a medium-sized game bird found in Africa and Asia, related to the partridge and often hunted for sport or food

Examples

A francolin bird
The guide pointed out a francolin hiding among the bushes during the safari.
Farmers sometimes see a francolin searching for seeds in their fields at dawn.
The distinctive call of the francolin can be heard early in the morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: partridge - a small, plump game bird related to the pheasant
  • synonym: pheasant - a large, long-tailed game bird
