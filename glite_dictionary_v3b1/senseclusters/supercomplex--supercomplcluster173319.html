supercomplex

noun
very complex system
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈkɑmplɛks / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈkɒmpleks / (BrE)
a system, organization, or structure made up of many interrelated parts and therefore extremely complicated

Examples

Transport supercomplex
The new transportation supercomplex connects trains, buses, and subways in one massive hub.
Researchers analyzed the genetic supercomplex responsible for regulating cell growth.
Managing the financial supercomplex of a multinational corporation requires advanced expertise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: network - a group or system of interconnected people or things
  • antonym: simplex - something simple or having a single part
  • synonym: system - a set of connected things or parts forming a complex whole
