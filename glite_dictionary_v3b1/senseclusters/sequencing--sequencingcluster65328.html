sequencing

noun
arranging in order
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːkwənsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsiːkwənsɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of arranging things in a specific or logical order

Examples

Sequencing of events
The sequencing of events in the story helped readers understand the plot.
Proper sequencing of tasks can make a project run more smoothly.
Teachers often use pictures to teach children about sequencing in daily routines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ordering - the act of arranging things in a sequence
  • antonym: randomization - the act of arranging things without a specific order
