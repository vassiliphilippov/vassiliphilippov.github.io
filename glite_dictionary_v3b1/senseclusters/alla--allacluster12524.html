alla

preposition
in the style of
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːlə / (AmE), / ˈalə / (BrE)
in the style or manner of, especially used to describe food or art prepared in a particular way

Examples

Pasta alla carbonara
We ordered pasta alla carbonara at the restaurant.
The chef prepared chicken alla cacciatora for the special menu tonight.
She painted the landscape alla impressionista, using quick, visible brushstrokes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: à la - in the manner or style of, especially in food or art
