Synonyms

  • deed of conveyance
    8.6 0.00/m
    a deed of conveyance is a written legal document used to transfer property, while conveyance also names that transfer process.
  • conveyance of title
    8.2 0.00/m
    a conveyance of title is the formal act that legally moves ownership of property, while conveyance is the general legal transfer term.
  • conveyancing
    9.1 0.04/m
    conveyancing is the legal process of moving property ownership between people, while conveyance can mean that process or the written document.
  • deed
    6.4 0.30/m
    a deed is a signed legal paper that gives property rights to someone, while conveyance can mean the same paper or the whole transfer process.
  • legal transfer
    4.2 0.06/m
    a legal transfer is the formal giving of ownership under law, while conveyance is the legal term often used for that transfer.
  • assignment
    5.3 2.65/m
    an assignment is a transfer of rights or interests in law, while conveyance usually refers to transferring property ownership or its document.
  • delivery
    2.3 19/m
    delivery is the act of handing over goods or title to someone, while conveyance is the legal transfer or document for property ownership.
