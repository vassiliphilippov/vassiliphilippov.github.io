simplex

noun
basic geometric shape
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmplɛks / (AmE), / ˈsɪmplɛks / (BrE)
the simplest possible geometric shape in a given dimension, such as a point, line segment, triangle, or tetrahedron

Examples

In three dimensions, a tetrahedron is the simplest simplex possible.
