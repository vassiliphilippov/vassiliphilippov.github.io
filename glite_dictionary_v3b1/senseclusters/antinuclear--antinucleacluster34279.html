antinuclear

adjective
opposed to nuclear power
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈnuːkliɚ / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈnjuːkliə / (BrE)
opposed to nuclear weapons or the use of nuclear energy

Examples

Antinuclear protest
The government faced strong antinuclear protests after announcing plans to build a new power plant.
Many citizens joined antinuclear groups to campaign against the use of atomic energy.
The antinuclear movement gained momentum during the late twentieth century.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nuclear-free - not allowing or containing nuclear weapons or energy
  • antonym: pro-nuclear - supporting the use of nuclear energy or weapons
