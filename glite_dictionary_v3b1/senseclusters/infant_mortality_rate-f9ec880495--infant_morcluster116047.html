infant mortality rate

noun
rate of infant deaths per 1,000 live births
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnfənt mɔrˈtæləti reɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪnfənt mɔːˈtælɪti reɪt / (BrE)
the number of deaths of infants under one year old per 1,000 live births in a given year or population

Examples

The infant mortality rate
A low infant mortality rate is seen as an indicator of a nation's overall health.
Regions with poor access to clean water often have higher infant mortality rates.
The infant mortality rate has declined significantly in many countries due to improved healthcare.
