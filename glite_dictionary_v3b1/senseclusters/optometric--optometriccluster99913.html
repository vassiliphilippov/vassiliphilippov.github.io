optometric

adjective
eye measurement science
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɑptəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌɒptəˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the practice or science of measuring eyesight and prescribing corrective lenses

Examples

Optometric exam
She scheduled an optometric exam to check her vision.
The clinic offers a wide range of optometric services for children and adults.
After his optometric assessment, he was prescribed new glasses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ophthalmic - relating to the eyes or vision
  • synonym: optical - relating to sight or light
