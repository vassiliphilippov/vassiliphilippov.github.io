weekend

noun
Saturday and Sunday; period of rest or leisure
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːkˌɛnd / (AmE), / ˈwiːkˌɛnd / (BrE)
the period from Friday evening or Saturday through Sunday, typically regarded as a time for rest or leisure from work or school

Examples

The weekend break
I'm planning to visit my parents this weekend for a family dinner.
Many people look forward to relaxing on the weekend after a busy week at work.
They usually go hiking with friends on weekends when the weather is nice.

Related Terms

  • antonym: weekday - any day of the week except Saturday and Sunday
