charcoal

noun
drawing made with black carbon sticks
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑrˌkoʊl / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːkəʊl / (BrE)
a drawing or sketch made with sticks of black carbon material, typically used by artists for its rich, deep lines and shading effects

Examples

A charcoal drawing

She created a stunning charcoal of her friend during the art class.
The artist's charcoal of a cityscape hung prominently in the gallery.
