sea poker

noun
a small, bony-plated northern Pacific fish
8.5
Pronunciation: / si ˈpoʊkər / (AmE), / si ˈpəʊkə / (BrE)
a small, slender fish up to 8 inches long, covered with bony plates, found mainly in deeper waters of the northern Pacific Ocean
