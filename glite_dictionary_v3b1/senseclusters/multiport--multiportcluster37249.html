multiport

noun
device with many ports
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltipɔrt / (AmE), / ˈmʌltipɔːt / (BrE)
a device or piece of hardware that provides multiple connection ports for attaching cables or peripherals

Examples

A multiport hub
The technician installed a multiport to connect all the office computers to the network.
She used a multiport to charge her phone, tablet, and headphones at the same time.
A multiport is essential for people who need to connect several devices to their laptop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adapter - a device used to connect different types of hardware
  • synonym: hub - a device that connects multiple computers or devices together
