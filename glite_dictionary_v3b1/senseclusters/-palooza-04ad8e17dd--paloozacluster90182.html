-palooza

x
informal abundance suffix
Pronunciation: / pəˈluːzə / (AmE), / pəˈluːzə / (BrE)
an informal suffix added to a word to make a playful name for a big, lively event or an overwhelming amount of something

Examples

A -palooza event
After the sale started, the store turned into a shopping-palooza with crowds everywhere.
The party was a dessert-palooza, featuring cakes, pies, and cookies on every table.
Our inbox became an email-palooza after the announcement went out to the whole company.
Our school is organizing a science-palooza with interactive experiments for everyone.
They called the neighborhood garage sale a junk-palooza because so many people participated.
The company held a tech-palooza to showcase the latest gadgets and innovations.
