pemphigoid

noun
autoimmune skin disease
9.9
Pronunciation: /pem.ˈfɪ.ɡɔɪd/ (AmE), /pem.ˈfɪ.ɡɔɪd/ (BrE)
a group of rare autoimmune skin diseases that cause blistering of the skin and mucous membranes

Examples

Pemphigoid skin blisters
The dermatologist confirmed the diagnosis of pemphigoid after reviewing the patient's test results.
Treatment for pemphigoid often involves medications to suppress the immune system.
Blisters caused by pemphigoid can appear on both the skin and inside the mouth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pemphigus - an autoimmune disease causing blisters on the skin and mucous membranes
