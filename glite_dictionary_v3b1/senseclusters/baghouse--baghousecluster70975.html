baghouse

noun
industrial dust filter
Pronunciation: / ˈbæɡˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈbæɡˌhaʊs / (BrE)
an industrial device that removes dust and particles from air by filtering it through fabric bags

Examples

A baghouse filter
The company installed a new baghouse to comply with environmental regulations.
A well-maintained baghouse can significantly reduce airborne dust in factories.
Technicians inspected the baghouse filters to ensure they were working properly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dust collector - a device that removes dust from air or gas
  • synonym: scrubber - an apparatus that removes pollutants from industrial exhaust
