nanostructure

noun
structure at nanoscale
Pronunciation: / ˈnænoʊˌstrʌktʃər / (AmE), / ˈnanəʊˌstrʌktʃə / (BrE)
a structure with at least one dimension measured in nanometers, typically used in nanotechnology or materials science

Examples

A nanostructure design
Scientists are studying how nanostructures can improve the efficiency of solar panels.
The unique properties of nanostructures make them valuable in medical research.
Engineers designed new materials by arranging nanostructures in specific patterns.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: microstructure - a structure at the microscopic scale, larger than nanostructure
  • synonym: nanomaterial - a material with structure at the nanometer scale
