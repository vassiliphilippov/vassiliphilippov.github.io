teletext

noun
TV text information system
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛliˌtɛkst / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌtɛkst / (BrE)
a television service that broadcasts or displays text information (such as news, weather, subtitles, or schedules) on TV screens
