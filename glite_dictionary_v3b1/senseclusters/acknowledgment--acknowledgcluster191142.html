acknowledgment

noun
statement of recognition or thanks
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /ək.ˈnɑː.lɪdʒ.mənt/ /; / /æk.ˈnɑː.lɪdʒ.mənt/ (AmE), /ək.ˈnɒ.lɪdʒ.mənt/ /; / /æk.ˈnɒ.lɪdʒ.mənt/ (BrE)
a statement or action that shows recognition of something or someone, especially by expressing thanks or appreciation

Examples

A polite acknowledgment
His polite nod was a brief acknowledgment of her presence in the room.
She included a heartfelt acknowledgment to her mentors in the book's introduction.
The project would not have succeeded without an acknowledgment of everyone's hard work.
She received public acknowledgement for her contributions to the project.
The letter offered a formal acknowledgement of their generous donation.
Winning the award was an acknowledgement of all his hard work.
He sent a brief email as an acknowledgement that he had received the documents.
She nodded in acknowledgement of his compliment, smiling politely.
The author's acknowledgement section thanked everyone who contributed to the book.
