PLA

noun
programmable logic device
8.2
Pronunciation: / pi ɛl eɪ / (AmE), / piː ɛl eɪ / (BrE)
a simple programmable electronic device used to implement logic functions in digital circuits

Examples

Digital PLA chip
The engineer used a PLA to quickly prototype the digital circuit design.
Students learned how to program a PLA as part of their electronics course.
A PLA can be configured to perform different logic functions depending on the requirements.
