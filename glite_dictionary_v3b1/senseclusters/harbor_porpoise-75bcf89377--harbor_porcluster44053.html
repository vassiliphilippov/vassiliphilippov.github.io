harbor porpoise

noun
a small coastal porpoise species
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːrbər ˈpɔrpəs / (AmE), / ˈhɑːbə ˈpɔːpɔɪs / (BrE)
a small porpoise (Phocoena phocoena) found in the coastal waters of the northern Atlantic and Pacific Oceans

Examples

A harbor porpoise group
A group of harbor porpoises surfaced briefly before disappearing beneath the waves.
Marine biologists are studying how noise pollution affects the behavior of harbor porpoises.
The harbor porpoise is often spotted swimming near the coasts of northern Europe.
