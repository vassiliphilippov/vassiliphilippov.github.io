Allhallowtide

noun
Christian season around All Saints' Day
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɔlˈhæloʊˌtaɪd / (AmE), / ˌɔːlˈhaləʊtaɪd / (BrE)
the period including and surrounding All Saints' Day, observed in some Christian traditions, typically encompassing October 31 (All Hallows' Eve), November 1 (All Saints' Day), and November 2 (All Souls' Day)

Examples

Allhallowtide season
Churches often hold special services throughout Allhallowtide to honor saints and remember the dead.
During Allhallowtide, many people visit the graves of loved ones and pray for the departed.
The traditions associated with Allhallowtide have influenced modern Halloween celebrations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: All Saints' Day - Christian festival honoring all saints, celebrated on November 1
