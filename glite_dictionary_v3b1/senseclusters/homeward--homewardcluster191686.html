homeward

adjective, adverb
toward home
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmwɚd / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmwəd / (BrE)
toward home or relating to returning home

Examples

Homeward journey
After months abroad, she finally set out homeward with a sense of relief.
As dusk fell, the hikers turned homeward along the forest path.
The dog wagged its tail and trotted homeward after exploring the park.
After a long day at work, she began her homeward journey.
The homeward voyage was much smoother than the outgoing trip.
We watched the flock take a homeward path at sunset.

Related Terms

  • synonym: homewards - in the direction of home; alternative form, chiefly British
  • antonym: outward - moving or directed away from home or a central place
