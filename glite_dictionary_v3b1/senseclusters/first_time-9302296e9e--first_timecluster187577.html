first time

noun
the initial experience of something
3.2
Pronunciation: / fɜrst taɪm / (AmE), / fɜːst taɪm / (BrE)
the first occasion on which something happens or someone experiences something

Examples

The first time abroad
It was her first time traveling abroad, and she was both excited and nervous.
Many people remember their first time driving a car vividly.
The restaurant specializes in guiding guests through their first time tasting traditional dishes.
