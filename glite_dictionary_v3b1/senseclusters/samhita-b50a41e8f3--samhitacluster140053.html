Samhita

noun
a collection of Vedic hymns in Hinduism
Pronunciation: / sʌmˈhiːtə / (AmE), / sʌmˈhiːtə / (BrE)
Any of the four collections of early Vedic hymns and texts that form a part of Hindu sacred literature.

Examples

The Samhita hymns
Each Samhita contains hymns and mantras used in rituals and ceremonies.
Scholars have translated several Samhitas to make ancient texts accessible worldwide.
The Rigveda is an important Samhita in Hindu scripture.
