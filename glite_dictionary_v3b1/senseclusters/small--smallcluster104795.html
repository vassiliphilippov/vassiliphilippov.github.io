small

noun
the narrow part of the lower back
Pronunciation: / smɔl / (AmE), / smɔːl / (BrE)
the slender or narrow part of the human back, just above the hips.

Examples

Small of the back
He placed his hand gently on the small of her back as they walked.
She felt a sharp pain in the small of her back after lifting the box.
The doctor instructed him to apply the ointment to the small of his back.
