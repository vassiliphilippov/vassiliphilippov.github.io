rehabilitation

noun
restoration of someone's reputation or honor
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌriː.əˌbɪl.əˈteɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌriː.həˌbɪl.ɪˈteɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of restoring someone's reputation or honor after it has been damaged or questioned, especially by officially clearing their name

Examples

Rehabilitation of reputation
After new evidence came to light, the court ordered the rehabilitation of the wrongly accused scientist.
His rehabilitation in the eyes of the public took years of persistent effort.
The official rehabilitation of her reputation followed a long legal battle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vindication - the action of clearing someone of blame or suspicion
