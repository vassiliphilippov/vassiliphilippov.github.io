tendrac

noun
spiny insectivorous mammal of Madagascar
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛn.dræk / (AmE), / ˈtɛn.dræk / (BrE)
a small, often spiny insectivorous mammal native to Madagascar, resembling a hedgehog

Examples

A tendrac animal
Researchers observed a tendrac foraging for insects at dusk.
The tendrac is well adapted to life among the forests of Madagascar.
With its spiny coat, the tendrac can be easily mistaken for a hedgehog.
