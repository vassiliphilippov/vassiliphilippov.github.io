retrospection

noun
reflection on past events
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛtrəˈspɛkʃən /; / ˌrɛtroʊˈspɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛtrəˈspɛkʃən /; / ˌrɛtrəʊˈspɛkʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of looking back on or thinking about past events or experiences

Examples

In retrospection of life
Her retrospection revealed how much she had grown since her childhood.
In moments of retrospection, he pondered the decisions that shaped his life.
The memoir is filled with deep retrospection about the author's early career.
A period of retrospection often follows major life changes.
The author's retrospection added depth to the memoir.
The film uses retrospection to reveal the main character's past.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anticipation - realization or consideration of something before it happens (opposite in time direction)
  • antonym: introspection - examination of one's own thoughts and feelings
  • synonym: reflection - serious thought or consideration
