spade

noun
tool for digging or lifting earth
4.4 1.0
Pronunciation: /speɪd/ (AmE), /speɪd/ (BrE)
A tool with a flat, typically rectangular blade and a long handle, used for digging or lifting earth, especially in gardening or construction.
