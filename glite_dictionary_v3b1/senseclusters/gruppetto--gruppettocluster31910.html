gruppetto

noun
cycling group behind
9.9
Pronunciation: /gruː.ˈpet.oʊ/ /; / /grʊ.ˈpet.oʊ/ (AmE), /gruː.ˈpet.əʊ/ /; / /grʊ.ˈpet.əʊ/ (BrE)
a group of cyclists who have fallen behind the main field and ride together to finish within the time limit in a race

Examples

Cyclists' gruppetto
A gruppetto formed on the steep climb as several riders struggled to keep up with the leaders.
The commentators noted that the gruppetto was working together to avoid elimination from the race.
Despite being in the gruppetto, she managed to finish the stage within the time limit.

Related Terms

  • antonym: breakaway - a small group of riders who have separated from the main field
  • antonym: peloton - the main group of riders in a cycling race
