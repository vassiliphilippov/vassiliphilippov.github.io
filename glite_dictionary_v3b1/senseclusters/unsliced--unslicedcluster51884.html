unsliced

adjective
not cut into slices
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈslaɪst / (AmE), / ʌnˈslaɪst / (BrE)
not cut into slices; whole and uncut.

Examples

Unsliced bread
He prefers to buy unsliced bread so he can cut thicker pieces at home.
The cheese was served unsliced on the platter for guests to cut themselves.
Please leave the cake unsliced until everyone arrives for the party.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sliced - cut into thin pieces
  • synonym: whole - not divided or cut; complete
