Landsmaal

noun
official rural-based Norwegian language form (Nynorsk)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːndsmɔl / (AmE), / ˈlændzmɑːl / (BrE)
an official written form of the Norwegian language, originally based on rural dialects and now known as Nynorsk

Examples

The Landsmaal language
After the language reform, Landsmaal gradually became known as Nynorsk.
His research focused on the development of Landsmaal from different regional dialects.
Many Norwegian writers in the 19th century advocated for the use of Landsmaal in their works.
