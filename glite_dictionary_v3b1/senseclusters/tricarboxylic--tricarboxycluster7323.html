tricarboxylic

adjective
with three carboxyl groups
Pronunciation: / ˌtraɪkɑrˈbɒksɪlɪk /; / ˌtraɪkɑːrˈbɒksɪlɪk / (AmE), / ˌtraɪkɑːˈbɒksɪlɪk / (BrE)
containing or relating to three carboxyl groups in a molecule, especially in reference to the tricarboxylic acid cycle in metabolism

Examples
