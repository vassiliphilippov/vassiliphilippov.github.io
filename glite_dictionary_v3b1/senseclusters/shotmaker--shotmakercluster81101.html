shotmaker

noun
person serving shots
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑtˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈʃɒtˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who prepares or serves alcoholic shots at a bar or party

Examples

Party shotmaker
At the party, everyone cheered as the shotmaker lined up colorful drinks on the counter.
The club hired a skilled shotmaker to impress guests with creative combinations.
During the celebration, she volunteered as the shotmaker and kept the energy high.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bartender - a person who serves drinks at a bar
  • synonym: server - a person who serves food or drinks to customers
