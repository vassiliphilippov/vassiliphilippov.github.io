shootaround

noun
basketball warm-up session
Pronunciation: / ˈʃuːtəˌraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈʃuːtəˌraʊnd / (BrE)

Fans watched the team’s shootaround to see their favorite athletes up close.
During the shootaround, the coach gave last-minute advice to the players.
