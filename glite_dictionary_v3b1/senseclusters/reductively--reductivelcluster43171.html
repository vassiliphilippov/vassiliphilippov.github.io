reductively

adverb
by chemical reduction
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdʌktɪvli / (AmE), / rɪˈdʌktɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that involves or causes chemical reduction, such as by adding electrons or removing oxygen from a compound

Examples

Reductively change a compound
The metal ions were reductively transformed during the experiment.
This reaction proceeds reductively when hydrogen gas is present.
Scientists studied how the compound changed reductively under different laboratory conditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: oxidatively - in a way that involves or causes chemical oxidation
