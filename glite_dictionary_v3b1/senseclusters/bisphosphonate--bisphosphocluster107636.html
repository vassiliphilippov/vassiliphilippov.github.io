bisphosphonate

noun
bone density drug
7.6
Pronunciation: /bɪs.ˈfɑːs.fə.neɪt/ /; / /bɪs.ˈfɔːs.fə.neɪt/ (AmE), /bɪs.ˈfɒs.fə.neɪt/ (BrE)
a type of medicine or drug used to slow bone loss and strengthen bones, often prescribed for osteoporosis

Examples
