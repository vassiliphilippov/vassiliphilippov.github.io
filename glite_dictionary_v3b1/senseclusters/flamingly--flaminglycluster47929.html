flamingly

adverb
very obviously or intensely
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪmɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈfleɪmɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely obvious, intense, or showy

Examples

Flamingly bright colors
She wore a flamingly bright dress that caught everyone's attention at the event.
His flamingly passionate speech inspired the entire audience.
The restaurant was decorated flamingly for the holiday celebration, with lights and bold colors everywhere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blatantly - in an open and unashamed manner
  • antonym: subtly - in a delicate or not obvious way
