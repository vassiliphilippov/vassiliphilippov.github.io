humidifier

noun
appliance that adds moisture to air
2.1 3.3
Pronunciation: / hjuˈmɪdəˌfaɪər / (AmE), / hjuːˈmɪdɪˌfaɪə / (BrE)
An electrical appliance that adds moisture to the air in a room or building.

Examples

An electric humidifier
After turning on the humidifier, the air felt much more comfortable.
Please clean the humidifier regularly to avoid mold buildup.
The humidifier in the bedroom helps prevent dry skin during winter.
