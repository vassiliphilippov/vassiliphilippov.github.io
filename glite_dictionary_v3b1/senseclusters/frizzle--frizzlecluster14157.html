frizzle

verb
fry until curled and crisp
2.1 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈfrɪ.zəl/ (AmE), /ˈfrɪ.zəl/ (BrE)
to cook food in hot oil or fat until it becomes crisp, browned, and sometimes curls up

Examples

To frizzle bacon
If you frizzle the ham too long, it turns tough and curly.
She loves to frizzle bacon until it's perfectly crispy for breakfast.
The chef will frizzle thin strips of onion as a garnish for the soup.
She frizzled the onions in the pan until they were golden and crispy.
The chef likes to frizzle bacon to add a crunchy texture to salads.
As the sausages frizzled in the skillet, the kitchen filled with a delicious aroma.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: burn - to cook something so much that it becomes blackened or charred
  • synonym: fry - to cook in hot fat or oil, typically in a shallow pan
  • synonym: sizzle - to make a hissing sound, as food does when frying
