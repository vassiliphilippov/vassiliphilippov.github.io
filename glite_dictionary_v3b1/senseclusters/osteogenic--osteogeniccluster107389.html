osteogenic

adjective
bone-forming
Pronunciation: / ˌɑstiəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌɒstiəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
relating to the formation or production of bone

Examples

Osteogenic activity
The doctor explained that osteogenic activity is crucial for bone repair after a fracture.
Researchers are studying osteogenic factors to improve treatments for osteoporosis.
Certain stem cells have osteogenic potential and can help regenerate damaged bone tissue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: osteoblastic - relating to the cells that form new bone
  • antonym: osteoclastic - relating to the cells that break down bone
