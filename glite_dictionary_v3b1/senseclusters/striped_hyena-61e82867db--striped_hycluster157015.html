striped hyena

noun
a hyena species with black stripes
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈstraɪpt haɪˈinə / (AmE), / ˈstraɪpt hʌɪˈiːnə / (BrE)
A medium-sized hyena (Hyaena hyaena) with a sandy-gray coat marked by black stripes, native to northern Africa, Arabia, and India.

Examples

The striped hyena animal
Conservationists are concerned about declining striped hyena populations due to habitat loss.
The striped hyena is nocturnal and avoids human settlements whenever possible.
Unlike its spotted relative, the striped hyena is more solitary by nature.
