preceptive

adjective
relating to instruction
Pronunciation: / prɪˈsɛptɪv / (AmE), / prɪˈsɛptɪv / (BrE)
relating to precepts or instruction, especially in the sense of giving or receiving guidance or rules

Examples

A preceptive comment
The professor's preceptive comments guided the students through complex ethical issues.
Her preceptive nature made her an excellent mentor for new employees.
A preceptive approach can help children learn the importance of honesty.

Related Terms

  • synonym: didactic - intended to teach or instruct
  • synonym: instructive - providing useful information or instruction
