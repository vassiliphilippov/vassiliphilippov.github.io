lush

adjective
sexually attractive (woman)
Pronunciation: / lʌʃ / (AmE), / lʌʃ / (BrE)
(of a person, especially a woman) attractive in a sexually appealing or voluptuous way.

Examples

A lush woman
Everyone at the party noticed the lush brunette in the sparkling red dress.
His latest novel describes a lush model who becomes the center of a scandal.
The movie featured a lush heroine who captivated the audience with her confidence.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plain - not attractive in appearance
  • synonym: voluptuous - (of a woman) curvaceous and sexually attractive
