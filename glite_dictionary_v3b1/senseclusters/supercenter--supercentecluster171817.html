supercenter

noun
large retail store
3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈsuː.pər.sen.tər/ (AmE), /ˈsuː.pə.sen.tə/ (BrE)

Examples

The new supercenter attracts shoppers from neighboring towns because of its wide selection.
Many families prefer shopping at a supercenter to save time and money.
