somnolently

adverb
in a drowsy or sleepy manner
8.1 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs…ëÀêmn…ôl…ôntli / (AmE), / Ààs…ímn…ôl…ôntli / (BrE)
In a drowsy or sleepy manner; appearing to be nearly asleep.

Examples

To answer somnolently
After a long day of travel, she answered the phone somnolently and barely understood the message.
He listened somnolently to the lecture, struggling to keep his eyes open.
The cat blinked somnolently in the afternoon sun, refusing to move from the windowsill.

Synonyms

  • drowsily
    2.4 0.27/m
    drowsily shows a slow, sleepy feeling in voice or movement, while somnolently sounds more formal and implies near sleep.
  • sleepily
    1.0 1.03/m
    sleepily looks like someone is tired and ready to sleep, while somnolently suggests a more formal, near-sleep state.
