sponge bath

noun
body washed with sponge or cloth
6.2 2.7
Pronunciation: /spʌndʒ bæθ/ (AmE), /spʌndʒ bɑːθ/ /; / /spʌndʒ bæθ/ (BrE)
a method of washing the body by using a wet sponge or washcloth instead of immersing oneself in a bathtub or shower

Examples

Take a sponge bath
She had to take a sponge bath after her surgery because she couldn't get her bandages wet.
The nurse gave the patient a sponge bath to keep him clean while he was bedridden.
When there is no shower available, a quick sponge bath is better than nothing.
