yodeler

noun
person who yodels
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈjoʊdələr / (AmE), / ˈjəʊdələ / (BrE)
a person who sings by rapidly alternating between chest and head voice and changing pitch in a distinctive style called yodeling, often associated with Swiss or Alpine folk music.
