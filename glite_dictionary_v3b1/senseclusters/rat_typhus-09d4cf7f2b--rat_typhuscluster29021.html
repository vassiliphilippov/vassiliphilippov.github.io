Synonyms

  • murine typhus

    murine typhus is the medical name used in papers and hospitals, while rat typhus is the common name for the same flea-borne illness.
  • endemic typhus
    8.8 0.00/m
    endemic typhus refers to cases that occur regularly in an area, while rat typhus names the flea-spread illness from rodents.
  • urban typhus
    8.9 0.00/m
