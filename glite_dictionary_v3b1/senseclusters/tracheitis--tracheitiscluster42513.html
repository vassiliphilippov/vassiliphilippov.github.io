tracheitis

noun
inflammation of the trachea
Pronunciation: / treɪˈkiː.aɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / trəˈkiː.ɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the trachea, usually caused by infection or irritation

Examples

Tracheitis infection
Antibiotics are sometimes prescribed to treat bacterial tracheitis in children.
Symptoms of tracheitis may include a fever, hoarseness, and difficulty breathing.
The doctor diagnosed her with tracheitis after examining her persistent cough and throat pain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bronchitis - inflammation of the bronchial tubes
  • synonym: laryngitis - inflammation of the larynx
