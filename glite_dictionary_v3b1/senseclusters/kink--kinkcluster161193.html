kink

noun
sharp bend or twist in a rope or wire
7.0
Pronunciation: / kɪŋk / (AmE), / kɪŋk / (BrE)
a sharp twist or bend in a rope, wire, or similar object, especially one produced when it is tightened after being looped

Examples

Kink in the rope
After pulling the cord too hard, a kink formed near the end.
Straighten out the kink in the wire before connecting it to the device.
There was a kink in the garden hose, which stopped the water flow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knot - a fastening made by tying a piece of string, rope, etc.
  • synonym: twist - a bend or curve in something that is usually straight
