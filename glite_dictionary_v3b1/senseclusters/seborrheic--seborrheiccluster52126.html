seborrheic

adjective
excess sebum
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛbəˈriːɪk / (AmE), / ˌsiːbəˈriːɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by excessive secretion of sebum, especially on the skin

Examples

Seborrheic skin
The dermatologist explained that seborrheic skin often appears shiny and greasy.
He was prescribed a special shampoo for his seborrheic scalp condition.
Many infants develop seborrheic dermatitis, commonly known as cradle cap.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dry - lacking moisture or oil
  • synonym: oily - having or producing a lot of oil
