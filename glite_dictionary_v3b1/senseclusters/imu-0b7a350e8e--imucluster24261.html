IMU

noun
Islamic militant group active in Central Asia
9.9
Pronunciation: / aɪ ɛm ju / (AmE), / aɪ ɛm ju / (BrE)
an Islamist militant group founded in 1996, known as the Islamic Movement of Uzbekistan, opposing Uzbekistan's secular regime and aiming to establish an Islamic state in Central Asia

Examples

IMU fighters
