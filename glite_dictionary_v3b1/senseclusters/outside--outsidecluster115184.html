outside

adjective
leading to or on the exterior of something
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈaʊtsaɪd / (BrE)
(of a door, window, etc.) leading to or located on the exterior of a building or room.

Examples

Outside door
Please make sure the outside door is locked when you leave.
The apartment has an outside staircase that leads to the second floor.
You should check the outside windows for any drafts before winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: external - located on or relating to the outside; outer
  • antonym: interior - situated within or inside; inner
