parasitization

noun
unauthorized resource use
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˌsaɪtəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpærəˌsaɪtɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process by which something, such as malicious software, occupies and uses a system's resources without authorization or benefit to the host

Examples

Parasitization of servers
The IT team detected the parasitization of the servers by hidden crypto-mining programs overnight.
Cybersecurity experts warned about the parasitization of personal computers through unauthorized background processes.
Regular system scans can help prevent the parasitization of your network by malicious software.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exploitation - the act of using something unfairly for one's own advantage
  • synonym: infection - the invasion and multiplication of harmful agents in a system
