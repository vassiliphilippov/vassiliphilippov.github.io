fibbing

noun
telling a small or harmless lie
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪbɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of telling a trivial or harmless lie, often about something unimportant

Examples

A fibbing story
He got in trouble at school for fibbing about why he was late.
Her parents didn't mind her fibbing as long as it didn't happen too often.
When asked about the missing cookies, her fibbing was easy to spot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lying - the act of making a false statement with the intention to deceive
  • antonym: truth-telling - the act of telling the truth; being honest
