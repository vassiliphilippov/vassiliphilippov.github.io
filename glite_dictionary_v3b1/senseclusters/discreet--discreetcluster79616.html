discreet

adjective
careful and prudent; avoiding unnecessary attention
4.3 4.7
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈskriːt/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈskriːt/ (BrE)
careful and prudent in speech or actions, especially to avoid causing offense or attracting attention
