replicator

noun
unit that is copied
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈre.plɪ.keɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈre.plɪ.keɪ.tə/ (BrE)
an entity, such as a gene or cultural item, that is copied and transmitted during replication

Examples

A gene replicator
A replicator can be a gene, a meme, or any unit that is faithfully copied.
Scientists study how a replicator spreads through a population over generations.
The concept of a replicator helps explain the evolution of both biological and cultural traits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gene - a unit of heredity that is transferred from parent to offspring
  • synonym: meme - a cultural element passed from one individual to another by imitation
