Fasciolopsis buski

noun
a parasitic intestinal fluke of humans and pigs
Pronunciation: / fæsiəˈlɑpsɪs ˈbʌski / (AmE), / fasɪəˈlɒpsɪs ˈbʌski / (BrE)
