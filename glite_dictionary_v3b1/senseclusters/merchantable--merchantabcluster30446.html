merchantable

adjective
suitable for sale
4.8 6.9

fit for sale; of a quality suitable to be sold, especially in commercial or legal contexts.

Examples

In order to be merchantable, food products must meet certain quality standards.
Only merchantable timber is selected by the forest contractors for processing.
