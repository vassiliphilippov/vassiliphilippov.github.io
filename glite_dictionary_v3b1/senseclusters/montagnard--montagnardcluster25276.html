montagnard

noun
hill-dwelling ethnic group
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɒntɑːnˈjɑːrd / (AmE), / ˌmɒntænˈjɑːd / (BrE)
a member of a hill-dwelling ethnic group, especially in Vietnam or historically in France

Examples

A montagnard villager
The montagnard people of Vietnam have preserved their traditional customs for generations.
Researchers visited several montagnard villages to study their unique languages and crafts.
During the colonial period, the French referred to certain ethnic minorities as montagnard communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: highlander - a person who lives in the highlands or mountainous regions
