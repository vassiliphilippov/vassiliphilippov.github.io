cohere

verb
stick or hold together; be united
3.8 5.0
Pronunciation: /koʊ.ˈhɪr/ /; / /kə.ˈhɪr/ (AmE), /kəʊ.ˈhɪə/ (BrE)
to stick or fit together as a unified whole, either physically or in ideas, organization, or design

Examples
