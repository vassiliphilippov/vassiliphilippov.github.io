a unit of energy equal to the work done by a power of 1,000 watts operating for one hour; commonly used to measure electricity consumption

Examples

One kW-hr unit
One kW-hr is the amount of energy used by a 1,000-watt device running for an hour.
Solar panels on the roof generated over 5 kW-hr today.
The monthly electricity bill showed a total consumption of 400 kW-hr.
