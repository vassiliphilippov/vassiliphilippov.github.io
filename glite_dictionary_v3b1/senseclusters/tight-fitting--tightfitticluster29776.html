Synonyms

  • close-fitting
    3.7 0.04/m
    close-fitting garments hug the body gently and allow movement, while tight-fitting means a firm close fit that leaves no slack.
  • form-fitting
    3.9 0.2/m
    form-fitting clothes follow body curves and show shape clearly, while tight-fitting just says something fits closely without shape focus.
  • fitted
    2.0 0.00/m
    fitted garments are made or altered to match measurements, while tight-fitting describes how closely they sit on the body.
  • tight
    1.0 0.3/m
    tight means held firmly or not loose in many situations, while tight-fitting refers specifically to close fit on a body or object.
  • skin-tight
    3.5 0.3/m
    skin-tight clothing presses very close to the skin and can feel extreme, while tight-fitting is a general close fit without that intensity.
  • snug
    3.5 0.2/m
