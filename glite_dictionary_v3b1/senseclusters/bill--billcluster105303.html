bill

verb
announce or advertise by posters
3.8
Pronunciation: / bɪl / (AmE), / bɪl / (BrE)
to advertise or announce someone or something publicly, especially by posters or notices

Examples

To bill a show

The actor was billed as the main attraction on all the placards.
The concert was heavily billed throughout the city with colorful posters.
She was billed as the headline speaker at the conference.
The concert was billed as the musical event of the year.
The new play is being billed on posters all over the city.
