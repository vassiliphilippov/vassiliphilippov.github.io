mug

verb
to rob with violence or threat in public
3.2 4.7
Pronunciation: /mʌg/ (AmE), /mʌg/ (BrE)
to attack and rob someone, especially in a public place, using violence or the threat of violence

Examples

To mug someone at night

The police have increased patrols in areas where people have been mugged recently.
Two men tried to mug him as he walked through the park at night.
