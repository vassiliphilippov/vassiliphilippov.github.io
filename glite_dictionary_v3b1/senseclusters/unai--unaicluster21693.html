unai

noun
a two-toed sloth
9.9
Pronunciation: / uːˈnaɪ /; / uːˈnɑː.i / (AmE), / juːˈnaɪ /; / juːˈnɑː.i / (BrE)
a two-toed sloth, a small, fast-moving animal with two long claws on each front foot, native to Central and South America

Examples

The unai animal
Compared to the three-toed variety, the unai is somewhat smaller and faster.
Researchers observed a wild unai feeding high in the rainforest canopy.
The unai is known for being more active at night than other sloths.
Researchers observed the unai moving slowly as it searched for leaves to eat.
The unai spends most of its life hanging from tree branches in the rainforest.
Unlike the three-toed sloth, the unai has only two claws on its front feet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sloth - a slow-moving tropical mammal that hangs upside down from trees
  • antonym: three-toed sloth - a sloth species with three claws on each front foot
  • synonym: two-toed sloth - a sloth species with two long claws on each front foot
