goldfish bowl

noun
glass bowl for keeping pet fish
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldˌfɪʃ boʊl / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊl(d)ˌfɪʃ bəʊl / (BrE)
A round, transparent container, typically made of glass, in which small pet fish such as goldfish are kept.

Examples

A goldfish bowl with water
After cleaning the goldfish bowl, he refilled it with fresh water.
She kept her pet goldfish in a classic goldfish bowl on her desk.
The sunlight reflected off the goldfish bowl, creating beautiful patterns on the table.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fish tank - a larger, often rectangular container for keeping fish
