new moon

noun
first appearance of crescent Moon
2.8 0.72/m
Pronunciation: / nu Ààmun / (AmE), / njuÀê ÀàmuÀên / (BrE)
The time when the Moon first becomes visible as a narrow waxing crescent after a period of invisibility.

Examples

The new moon phase
After several days of darkness, the new moon was finally visible in the evening sky.
Farmers used to plant their crops at the time of the new moon.
The festival is celebrated on the night of the new moon.

Synonyms

  • new phase of the moon
    4.1 0.00/m
    new phase of the moon marks the start of a lunar cycle, while new moon names the first visible narrow waxing crescent.

Related Terms

  • antonym: full moon - the time when the entire face of the Moon is illuminated
