hind

adjective
at or near the back (of an animal)
Pronunciation: / haɪnd / (AmE), / haɪnd / (BrE)
(of a part of an animal) situated at or nearer to the rear or back.

Examples

Hind legs
Frogs use their powerful hind legs to leap long distances.
The horse injured its hind leg while running in the field.
The veterinarian examined the dog's hind quarters for any signs of injury.

Related Terms

  • antonym: front - situated at or nearer to the front
  • synonym: rear - the back part of something, especially an animal's body
