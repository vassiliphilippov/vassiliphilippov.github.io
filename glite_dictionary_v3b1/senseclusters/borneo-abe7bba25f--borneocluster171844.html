Borneo

proper noun
large island in Southeast Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔr.ni.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɔː.ni.əʊ / (BrE)
the third largest island in the world, located in Southeast Asia, north of Java, and known for its dense jungle and rain forests; part of the Malay Archipelago, divided among Indonesia, Malaysia, and Brunei

Examples

Borneo island
Palm oil plantations have greatly changed the landscape of Borneo over recent decades.
She spent a month traveling across Borneo to study its biodiversity.
The rainforests of Borneo are home to many unique animal species.
