bitterly

adverb
in an extreme or intense way
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪt̬ɚli / (AmE), / ˈbɪtəli / (BrE)
In an extreme or intense manner, especially referring to unpleasant conditions or emotions.

Examples

Bitterly cold night
It was bitterly cold that night, with temperatures dropping well below freezing.
She was bitterly disappointed when she didn't get the job.
The wind blew bitterly around the corners, making it hard to walk outside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intensely - in a very strong or extreme way
  • antonym: mildly - to a small extent; not severely
