bitterly

adverb
in an extreme or intense way
1.0 2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪt̬ɚli / (AmE), / ˈbɪtəli / (BrE)
In an extreme or intense manner, especially referring to unpleasant conditions or emotions.

Examples

The wind blew bitterly around the corners, making it hard to walk outside.
