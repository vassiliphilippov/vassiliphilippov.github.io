suede leather

noun
leather with a velvety napped surface
3.8
Pronunciation: / sweɪd ˈlɛðər / (AmE), / sweɪd ˈlɛðə / (BrE)
Leather with a soft, velvety, napped surface, made by buffing the flesh side of the skin.

Examples

Suede leather furniture
Furniture upholstered with suede leather adds a touch of luxury to any room.
He bought new shoes crafted from dark brown suede leather for the wedding.
The jacket was made from a soft suede leather that felt elegant to the touch.
