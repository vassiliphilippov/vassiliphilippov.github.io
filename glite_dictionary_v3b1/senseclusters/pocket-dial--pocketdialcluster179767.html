pocket-dial

verb
accidentally call from pocket
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːkət ˌdaɪəl / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪt ˌdaɪəl / (BrE)
to accidentally call someone by pressing a mobile phone's buttons while it is in one's pocket or bag.

Examples

To pocket-dial a number
I accidentally pocket-dialed my boss while hurrying through the subway this morning.
She often pocket-dials her friends when her phone slides to the bottom of her bag.
He apologized after pocket-dialing his sister during the two-hour meeting at work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: call - to contact someone by telephone
