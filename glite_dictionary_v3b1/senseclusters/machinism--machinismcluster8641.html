machinism

noun
machine engineering skill
Pronunciation: / məˈʃiːnɪzəm / (AmE), / məˈʃiːnɪzəm / (BrE)
the art, practice, or skill of designing, building, or operating machines, especially in mechanical engineering

Examples

Machinism in engineering
She studied machinism at the technical college to learn how to design precise engine parts.
Advances in machinism have led to more efficient manufacturing processes in many industries.
His expertise in machinism made him a valuable member of the engineering team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: machinery - machines collectively or the components of a machine
  • synonym: mechanical engineering - the branch of engineering dealing with machines and mechanical systems
