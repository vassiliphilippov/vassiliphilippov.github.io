V-neck

noun
garment neckline shaped like a V
Pronunciation: / ˈviˌnɛk / (AmE), / ˈviː.nɛk / (BrE)
A neckline of a garment that is shaped like the letter V, usually plunging down in the front.

Examples

A V-neck sweater
He wore a stylish V-neck sweater over a white shirt.
I prefer V-neck T-shirts to crew necks during the summer.
The dress features a deep V-neck that adds a touch of elegance.

Related Terms
