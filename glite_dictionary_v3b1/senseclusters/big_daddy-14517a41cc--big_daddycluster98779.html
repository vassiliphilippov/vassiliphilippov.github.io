big daddy

noun
the most important or influential person
Pronunciation: / bɪɡ ˈdædi / (AmE), / bɪɡ ˈdædi / (BrE)
an important or influential person, especially the most powerful in a particular group or field

Examples

The big daddy of business
Everyone knew he was the big daddy of the boardroom decisions.
She called him the big daddy of the neighborhood after all he had done.
The big daddy of fast food chains is opening a new branch downtown.
