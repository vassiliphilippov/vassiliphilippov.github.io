prestation

noun
required payment or service
9.9
Pronunciation: / prɛˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / prɛˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
a payment, service, or gift given as part of a duty or obligation, especially in a formal, legal, or social context

Examples
