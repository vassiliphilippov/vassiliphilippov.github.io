overhead press

noun
exercise where weight is lifted overhead
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˌhɛd ˈprɛs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˌhɛd ˈprɛs / (BrE)
a weight training exercise in which a weight is lifted straight upward from shoulder level to above the head until the arms are fully extended

Examples

The overhead press exercise
During the competition, he managed to lift 100 kg in the overhead press.
Proper form is essential to avoid injury while performing an overhead press.
She added the overhead press to her workout routine to build stronger shoulders.
