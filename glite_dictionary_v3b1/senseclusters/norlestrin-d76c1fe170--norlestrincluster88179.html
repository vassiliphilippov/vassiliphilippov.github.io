Norlestrin

noun
oral contraceptive (trade name)
Pronunciation: / nɔrˈlɛstrɪn / (AmE), / nɔːˈlɛstrɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked oral contraceptive pill containing estradiol and norethindrone.

Examples

Norlestrin birth control
Her doctor prescribed Norlestrin as a method of birth control.
Many women have used Norlestrin to regulate their menstrual cycles.
Please inform your healthcare provider if you have any allergies before starting Norlestrin.
