fave

verb
to mark as favorite
8.2
Pronunciation: / feɪv / (AmE), / feɪv / (BrE)
to mark or save something as a favorite, especially on social media or online platforms

Examples

To fave a post
I always fave the recipes I want to try later on the app.
She faved my photo, so I knew she liked it.
If you fave this post, you can easily find it again in your profile.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bookmark - to save a webpage or item for later reference
  • antonym: ignore - to pay no attention to something
  • synonym: like - to indicate approval or enjoyment of something online
