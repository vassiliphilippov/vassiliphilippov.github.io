steamboat

noun
boat powered by a steam engine
4.6 4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈstiːm.boʊt/ (AmE), /ˈstiːm.bəʊt/ (BrE)
a boat propelled by a steam engine, especially one used on rivers in the 19th and early 20th centuries
