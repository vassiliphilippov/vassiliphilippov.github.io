superorganism

noun
complex unified system
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌɔːrɡəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌɔːɡənɪzəm / (BrE)
a large organization, system, or group of individuals that functions as a single entity, often used figuratively or in science fiction

Examples

Global superorganism
The internet has evolved into a global superorganism connecting billions of people and devices.
Some scientists describe an ant colony as a superorganism because the ants work together as one unit.
Modern cities can be seen as a superorganism with many systems working in harmony.
In the novel, the alien species forms a superorganism that thinks and acts as a single entity.
Scientists debate whether an ant colony can truly be called a superorganism due to its high level of cooperation.
The film depicts a city evolving into a conscious superorganism that influences every aspect of its citizens' lives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collective - a group acting as a single unit
  • synonym: hive mind - a collective consciousness shared by many individuals
  • antonym: individual - a single person or thing, not part of a group
  • synonym: organization - a group of people working together for a purpose
