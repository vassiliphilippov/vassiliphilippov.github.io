candied apple

noun
apple coated with caramelized sugar
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkændid ˈæpəl / (AmE), / ˈkændid ˈap(ə)l / (BrE)
An apple coated with a hard layer of caramelized sugar or syrup, often served on a stick as a sweet treat.

Examples

A candied apple treat
A candied apple is a classic treat at carnivals and festivals.
She made her own candied apples for Halloween.
The children enjoyed a candied apple at the autumn fair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: toffee apple - apple coated with a hard toffee shell
