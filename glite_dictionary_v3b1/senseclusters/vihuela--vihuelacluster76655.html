vihuela

noun
Mexican string instrument
9.9
Pronunciation: /viː.ˈweɪ.lə/ /; / /vɪ.ˈweɪ.lə/ (AmE), /viː.ˈweɪ.lə/ /; / /vɪ.ˈweɪ.lə/ (BrE)
a small, five-string Mexican guitar-like instrument with a rounded back, used mainly in mariachi music for rhythmic accompaniment

Examples

Mariachi vihuela
The musician played the vihuela to add rhythm to the lively mariachi performance.
A traditional mariachi band usually includes a vihuela along with trumpets and violins.
He learned to strum the vihuela as a child in his hometown in Mexico.
