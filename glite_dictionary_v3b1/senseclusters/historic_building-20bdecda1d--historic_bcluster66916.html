historic building

noun
building of historical importance
Pronunciation: / hɪˈstɔrɪk ˈbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / hɪˈstɒrɪk ˈbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
a building that is significant because of its historical, architectural, or cultural importance

Examples

A historic building site
Many tourists visit the historic building every year to learn about the city's past.
Renovations must preserve the character of the historic building located downtown.
The old library is considered a historic building and is protected by law.
