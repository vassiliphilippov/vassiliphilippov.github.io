iliahi

noun
Hawaiian sandalwood tree
Pronunciation: / iːliˈɑːhi / (AmE), / iːliˈɑːhi / (BrE)
a type of sandalwood tree native to Hawaii, prized for its fragrant wood used in crafts and ceremonies

Examples

Hawaiian iliahi tree
The iliahi was once abundant in Hawaiian forests but is now rare due to overharvesting.
Artisans carve beautiful bowls and jewelry from the fragrant wood of the iliahi.
During ceremonies, the scent of burning iliahi fills the air with a sweet aroma.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sandalwood - a fragrant wood from trees of the genus Santalum
