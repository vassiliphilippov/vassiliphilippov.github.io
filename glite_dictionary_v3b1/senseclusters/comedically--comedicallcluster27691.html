comedically

adverb
in a humorous way
Pronunciation: / kəˈmiːdɪkli / (AmE), / kəˈmiːdɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is related to or characteristic of comedy or humor

Examples

Act comedically
The character slipped comedically on a banana peel, causing the audience to burst out laughing.
She delivered her speech comedically, making even the most serious topics sound amusing.
The movie uses sound effects comedically to enhance the funny scenes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: humorously - in a funny or amusing way
  • antonym: seriously - in a way that is not intended to be funny
