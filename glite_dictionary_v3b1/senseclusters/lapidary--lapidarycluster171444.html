lapidary

noun
person who cuts and engraves gems
Pronunciation: / ˈlæpɪˌdɛri / (AmE), / ˈlæpɪdəri / (BrE)
a person who cuts, polishes, or engraves precious stones

Examples

A skilled lapidary

As a renowned lapidary, she has crafted jewelry for royalty and celebrities alike.

As a respected lapidary, she is often consulted about rare gemstones.
The lapidary carefully examined the uncut diamond before deciding how to shape it.
The museum employed a skilled lapidary to restore its collection of engraved jewels.
