mauresque

adjective
Moorish style
Pronunciation: / moʊˈrɛsk / (AmE), / mɔːˈrɛsk / (BrE)
relating to or resembling the art, architecture, or style of the Moors, especially in North Africa or Spain

Examples

Mauresque architecture
The palace featured mauresque tiles and intricate geometric patterns throughout its halls.
She admired the mauresque arches that decorated the entrance to the old city.
The artist incorporated mauresque motifs into his latest collection of paintings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Moorish - relating to the Moors or their culture
  • antonym: gothic - relating to a style of architecture from medieval Europe
