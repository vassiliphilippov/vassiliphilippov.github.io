speedster

noun
fast sports car
Pronunciation: / ˈspiːdstər / (AmE), / ˈspiːdstə / (BrE)
a light, fast sports car, often with an open top and two seats, designed for high speed

Examples

Classic speedster car
He restored a vintage speedster and took it to the summer classic car show.
The new speedster can accelerate from zero to sixty miles per hour in just four seconds.
She always dreamed of driving a red speedster along the coast with the top down.

Related Terms

  • synonym: convertible - a car with a roof that can be folded down or removed
  • synonym: roadster - a small, open-top sports car with two seats
  • antonym: sedan - a closed car with four doors and a separate trunk
