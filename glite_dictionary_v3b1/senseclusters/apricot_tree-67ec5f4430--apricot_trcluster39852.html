apricot tree

noun
tree producing apricots
Pronunciation: / ˈæp.rɪ.kɑt triː / (AmE), / ˈæp.rɪ.kɒt triː / (BrE)
an Asian tree with clusters of usually white blossoms and edible fruit resembling a peach, from which apricots are obtained

Examples

The apricot tree blossoms
In spring, the apricot tree is covered with delicate white blossoms.
She planted an apricot tree next to the fence hoping it would bear fruit in a few years.
The old apricot tree in our garden produces sweet fruit each summer.
