able to feel, detect, or respond easily to physical sensations, pain, or changes such as touch, light, or temperature; easily hurt or tender

Examples

Sensitive skin
Some animals are extremely sensitive to vibrations in the environment.
The device is designed to be sensitive enough to detect even slight changes in temperature.
This part of the skin is particularly sensitive to pain.
Some people's skin is especially sensitive to sunlight and may burn easily.
The microphone is highly sensitive, picking up even the faintest sounds in the room.
This new camera is sensitive to low light, allowing for better night photography.
After the dental procedure, her gums were sensitive and hurt when she brushed her teeth.
Be careful not to bump his arm; that area is still quite sensitive.
My skin is very sensitive after a sunburn, so even gentle fabrics irritate it.

Synonyms

  • tender
    3.4 1.82/m
    tender is easily sore or hurt in a body part, while sensitive also means quick to react to many small sensations.
  • sensible
    3.6 0.42/m
    sensible is able to be noticed by the senses, while sensitive reacts more strongly to touch, light, or pain.
  • sensate
    9.7 0.01/m
    sensate has physical feeling or perception, while sensitive describes both strong reaction to sensations and being easily hurt.
