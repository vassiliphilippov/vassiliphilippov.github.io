freelance

adjective
self-employed; working independently
1.7 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfriː.læns/ /; / /ˈfriː.ləns/ (AmE), /ˈfriː.lɑːns/ (BrE)
working for different organizations or clients on separate projects, rather than being permanently employed by one

Examples

Freelance designer job
As a freelance graphic designer, she chooses her own projects and clients.

Many tech professionals work freelance to enjoy more flexibility in their schedules.

Many writers choose to work on a freelance basis rather than being tied to one publisher.
She enjoys the flexibility of her freelance lifestyle.
