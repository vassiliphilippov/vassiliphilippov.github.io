prerenal

adjective
before the kidney
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈriːnəl / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈriːnəl / (BrE)
relating to or occurring before the kidney, especially in reference to blood flow or disease processes

Examples

Prerenal kidney failure
The doctor explained that prerenal causes of kidney failure are related to reduced blood flow.
Dehydration is a common prerenal factor leading to acute kidney injury.
They ruled out prerenal conditions before considering other types of kidney problems.
