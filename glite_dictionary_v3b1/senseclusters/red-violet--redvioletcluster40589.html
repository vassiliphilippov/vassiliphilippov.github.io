red-violet

adjective
violet tinged with red
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛdˈvaɪələt / (AmE), / ˌrɛdˈvaɪələt / (BrE)
Of a violet color with a tinge of red; the hue between red and violet on the color spectrum.

Examples

A red-violet color
Her bedroom walls are painted a soothing red-violet color.
She chose a red-violet dress that stood out at the party.
The artist mixed paints to create a unique red-violet shade for the sunset.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: magenta - a purplish-red color, close to red-violet
  • degree lower: violet - a bluish-purple color
