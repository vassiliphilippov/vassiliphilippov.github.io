Moehringia lateriflora

noun
a low-growing plant with small white flowers
Pronunciation: / moʊˌhɪrɪndʒiə ˌlætərəˈflɔrə / (AmE), / məʊˌhɪrɪndʒiə ˌlætərəˈflɔːrə / (BrE)
A low-growing herbaceous plant with clusters of small white flowers, each having four petals, native to temperate regions.
