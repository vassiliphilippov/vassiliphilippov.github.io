tonger

noun
oyster harvester
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːŋər / (AmE), / ˈtɒŋə / (BrE)
a person who collects oysters using long-handled tongs, especially in shallow waters

Examples

Oyster tonger
The experienced tonger set out before sunrise to harvest oysters from the bay.
Many families in the region have been tongers for generations, passing down their skills.
During oyster season, a skilled tonger can collect hundreds of oysters in a single day.

  • synonym: oysterman - a person who gathers or farms oysters
