European wildcat

noun
bushy-tailed wildcat native to Europe
Pronunciation: / jʊˌroʊpiən ˈwaɪldˌkæt / (AmE), / jʊəˌrəʊpiən ˈwaɪldˌkæt / (BrE)
a wildcat native to Europe, with a bushy tail and resembling the domestic cat; regarded as the ancestor of the domestic cat

Examples

The European wildcat habitat
Conservationists are working to protect the habitats of the endangered European wildcat.
It is widely believed that the European wildcat is the ancestor of the modern domestic cat.
The European wildcat is reclusive and rarely seen by people in its native forests.
