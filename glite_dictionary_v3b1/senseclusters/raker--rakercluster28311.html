raker

noun
small hand tool
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪkər / (AmE), / ˈreɪkə / (BrE)
a small handheld tool with tines used for loosening, smoothing, or gathering material such as soil, gravel, or leaves

Examples

Garden raker
He used a raker to clear the small stones from the garden bed.
The gardener kept a raker handy for loosening soil around the plants.
After trimming the bushes, she gathered the leaves with a raker.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rake - a tool with a long handle and tines for gathering leaves or smoothing soil
