report

noun
reputation; public estimation or opinion
2.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpɔrt / (AmE), / rɪˈpɔːt / (BrE)
the general opinion or reputation that people have about someone or something, especially as discussed or known by others

Examples

A good report
A bad report can ruin a politician's prospects.
She has a good report for honesty and kindness.
The scandal severely damaged his report in the community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disrepute - the state of being held in low esteem by the public
  • synonym: reputation - the beliefs or opinions held about someone or something
