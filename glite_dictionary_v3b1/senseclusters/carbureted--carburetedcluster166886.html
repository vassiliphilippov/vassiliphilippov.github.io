carbureted

adjective
with a carburetor
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrbjəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɑːbjʊˌreɪtɪd / (BrE)
having a carburetor that mixes fuel and air for an internal combustion engine

Examples

Carbureted engine
Older motorcycles often have carbureted engines that require manual adjustment.
The mechanic prefers working on carbureted cars because of their simpler design.
Many classic cars are carbureted rather than using modern fuel injection systems.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fuel-injected - using a fuel injection system instead of a carburetor
