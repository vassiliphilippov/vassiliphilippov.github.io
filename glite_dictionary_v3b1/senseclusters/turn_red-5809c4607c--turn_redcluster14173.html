turn red

verb
become red in the face from embarrassment
Pronunciation: / tɝn rɛd / (AmE), / tɜːn rɛd / (BrE)
to become visibly red in the face, usually because of embarrassment, shyness, shame, or anger

Examples

The little boy was turning red from embarrassment after forgetting his lines.
When she realized her mistake in front of the crowd, she immediately turned red.
