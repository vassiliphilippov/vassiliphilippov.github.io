radiochemical

adjective
radioactive chemistry
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌreɪdioʊˈkɛmɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌreɪdiəʊˈkɛmɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the chemistry of radioactive substances or the use of radioactivity in chemical processes

Examples

Radiochemical analysis
The laboratory uses radiochemical methods to detect trace elements in water samples.
A radiochemical reaction can help identify the presence of certain isotopes.
She specializes in radiochemical analysis of environmental samples.
