hymnology

noun
study of hymns
Pronunciation: / hɪmˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / hɪmˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study of hymns, including their history, composition, and use in worship

She specialized in hymnology and wrote several books about the history of church music.
The university offers a course in hymnology for students interested in religious music traditions.
His research in hymnology uncovered many forgotten hymns from the eighteenth century.
