piriform

adjective
pear-shaped
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪrɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈpɪrɪfɔːm / (BrE)
having the shape of a pear; pear-shaped

Examples

A piriform shape
The vase has a piriform body that widens at the base and narrows at the top.
Some birds lay piriform eggs that are wider at one end than the other.
The scientist described the organ as piriform due to its distinctive shape.
