Junkers

proper noun
Prussian landowning class
Pronunciation: / ˈjʊŋkərz / (AmE), / ˈjʊŋkəz / (BrE)
the conservative landed aristocracy of Prussia and eastern Germany, influential in politics and the military from the 19th to early 20th centuries

Examples

Prussian Junkers class
The Junkers played a major role in shaping Prussian military traditions and government policies.
Many historians argue that the influence of the Junkers delayed democratic reforms in Germany.
During the early 20th century, the Junkers maintained large estates and significant political power.
