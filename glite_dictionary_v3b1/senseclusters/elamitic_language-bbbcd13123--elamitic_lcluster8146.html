Elamitic language

noun
extinct language of the ancient Elamites
Pronunciation: / iˈlæmɪtɪk ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɪˈlæmɪtɪk ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
an extinct language spoken by the ancient Elamites of southwestern Iran, of uncertain linguistic affiliation

Examples

Elamitic language texts
Few historians are able to translate texts in the Elamitic language.
Scholars debate the classification of the Elamitic language due to its mysterious origins.
The discovery of tablets written in Elamitic language shed light on ancient Elamite culture.
