cyberwar

noun
digital warfare; attack on computer networks
2.2 5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈsaɪ.bər.wɔːr/ /; / /ˈsaɪ.bər.wɑːr/ (AmE), /ˈsaɪ.bə.wɔː/ (BrE)
