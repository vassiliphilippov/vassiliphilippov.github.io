rethread

verb
to thread again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈθrɛd / (AmE), / ˌriːˈθrɛd / (BrE)
to put a thread through something again, such as a needle or a hole

Examples

To rethread a needle
After the thread broke, she had to rethread the needle before continuing her sewing.
He carefully rethreaded the string through the small hole in the button.
If the thread slips out, simply rethread it and try again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thread - to pass a thread through a needle or hole
  • antonym: unthread - to remove a thread from a needle or hole
