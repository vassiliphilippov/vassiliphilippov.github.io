lilyturf

noun
Asiatic perennial herb; ground cover plant
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪl.iˌtɝf / (AmE), / ˈlɪl.iˌtɜːf / (BrE)
An Asiatic perennial herb with grasslike evergreen leaves and clusters of dark purple flowers, often grown as ground cover.

Examples

Lilyturf plant
Lilyturf is prized for its low-maintenance, evergreen foliage and grape-like flower clusters.
Many landscapers recommend lilyturf for shady areas where grass struggles to grow.
The garden was bordered with a dense patch of lilyturf to prevent soil erosion.
