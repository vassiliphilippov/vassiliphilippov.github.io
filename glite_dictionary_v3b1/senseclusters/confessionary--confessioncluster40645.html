confessionary

noun
place for confessions
Pronunciation: / kənˈfɛʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / kənˈfɛʃənəri / (BrE)
a place or structure in a church where confessions are made, especially to a priest

Examples

Church confessionary
The priest waited in the confessionary for parishioners to arrive before the evening service.
Visitors admired the ornate woodwork of the old confessionary in the cathedral.
She entered the confessionary quietly, seeking forgiveness for her mistakes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confessional - a small enclosed space in a church for hearing confessions
