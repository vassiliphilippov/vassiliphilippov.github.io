lug

noun
marine worm used as fishing bait
6.2
Pronunciation: / lʌɡ / (AmE), / lʌɡ / (BrE)
a marine worm with a row of tufted gills along each side of its back, often used as fishing bait

Examples

A lug worm
Fishermen often dig for lug on the shore to use as bait.
She baited the hook with a juicy lug and cast her line into the sea.
The beach was teeming with lug at low tide.
