axial motion

noun
rotation around an axis
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈæk.si.əl ˈmoʊ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈæk.si.əl ˈməʊ.ʃən / (BrE)
rotary movement of an object around its own axis

Examples

Axial motion of shaft
Proper lubrication is essential to maintain smooth axial motion in the rotating shafts of the machine.
The earth completes one axial motion every twenty-four hours, resulting in day and night.
The gyroscope resists changes to its axial motion due to the principle of conservation of angular momentum.

Related Terms

  • synonym: revolution - circular movement of an object around another axis
  • synonym: rotation - the action of rotating around an axis or center
