dispiritingly

adverb
in a discouraging way
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspɪrɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / dɪˈspɪrɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that causes someone to lose hope, confidence, or enthusiasm

Examples

React dispiritingly to news
The students reacted dispiritingly to the news that the trip was canceled.
He spoke dispiritingly about their chances of winning the competition.
The weather was dispiritingly gloomy throughout the entire weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discouragingly - in a way that makes someone lose confidence or enthusiasm
  • antonym: encouragingly - in a way that gives someone hope or confidence
