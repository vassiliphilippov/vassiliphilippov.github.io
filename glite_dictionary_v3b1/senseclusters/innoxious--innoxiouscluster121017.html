innoxious

adjective
harmless; not harmful
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɑkʃəs / (AmE), / ɪˈnɒkʃəs / (BrE)
Not harmful; having no adverse effect; harmless.

Examples

An innoxious snake
Despite its ominous appearance, the snake is actually innoxious to humans.
The chemical was found to be innoxious, posing no threat to humans or animals.
This additive is considered innoxious when used in small quantities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harmless - Not able or likely to cause harm
  • antonym: noxious - Harmful, poisonous, or very unpleasant
