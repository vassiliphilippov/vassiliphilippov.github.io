cut down

verb
intercept or tackle a player in sports
Pronunciation: / kʌt daʊn / (AmE), / kʌt daʊn / (BrE)
in sports, to intercept, stop, or tackle an opposing player, often to prevent progress or a goal

Examples

To cut down a striker
She quickly cut down her opponent, thwarting what could have been a game-winning play.
The coach praised the midfielder who cut down several attackers during the match.
The defender managed to cut down the striker just before he reached the goal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tackle - to seize and stop an opponent in sports to prevent progress
