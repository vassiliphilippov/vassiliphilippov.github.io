light syllable

noun
syllable with short vowel and no coda
Pronunciation: / laɪt ˈsɪləbəl / (AmE), / laɪt ˈsɪləbəl / (BrE)
A syllable that contains a short vowel as its nucleus and has no coda consonant, often important in phonology and poetic meter.
