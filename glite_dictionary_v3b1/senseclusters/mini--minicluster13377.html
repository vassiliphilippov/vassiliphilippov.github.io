mini

noun
small version
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪni / (AmE), / ˈmɪni / (BrE)
a small version of something, especially a product or item designed to be smaller than the standard size

Examples

A mini snack
The store sells minis of popular snacks for people who want smaller portions.
She collects minis of famous perfume bottles from around the world.
At the party, everyone enjoyed the minis because they were easy to eat in one bite.

Related Terms

  • antonym: full-size - the standard or normal size of something
  • synonym: miniature - a very small version or model of something
