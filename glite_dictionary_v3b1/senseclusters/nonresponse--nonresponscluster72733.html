nonresponse

noun
lack of reply
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.rɪˈspɑns / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.rɪˈspɒns / (BrE)
a lack of reply or answer, especially when someone does not respond to a survey or question

Examples

Survey nonresponse
The high rate of nonresponse made it difficult to draw conclusions from the survey.
Researchers must consider nonresponse when analyzing the accuracy of their data.
A nonresponse to the invitation was interpreted as a polite refusal.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reply - an answer or response to a question or request
  • synonym: silence - the absence of sound or response
