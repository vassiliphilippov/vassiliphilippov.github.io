reclaim

verb
to get something back; recover
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkleɪm / (AmE), / rɪˈkleɪm / (BrE)
to retrieve or recover (something previously lost, given, or paid); to claim back

Examples

To reclaim lost money
After quitting his job, he tried to reclaim some sense of purpose in his life.
She was determined to reclaim her lost luggage from the airline.
The organization wants to reclaim public land that has been abandoned for years.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forfeit - to lose or give up something as a penalty
  • synonym: recover - to get back something lost or taken
