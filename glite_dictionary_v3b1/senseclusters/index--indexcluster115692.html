index

verb
adjust for changes in a standard index
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌdɛks / (AmE), / ˈɪndɛks / (BrE)
to adjust (wages, prices, etc.) automatically according to changes in a standard index, such as inflation

Examples

To index wages
After the agreement, all student fees were indexed to the national income level.
Some countries index pensions to the cost of living to protect retirees from inflation.
The contract states that salaries will be indexed annually based on the consumer price index.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adjust - to change something slightly to achieve the desired result
