unshoveled

adjective
not cleared by shoveling
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃʌvəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃʌvəld / (BrE)
not cleared of snow or debris by shoveling

Examples

An unshoveled driveway
After the storm, the unshoveled driveway made it difficult to park the car.
The city issued fines for unshoveled sidewalks that blocked pedestrian access.
We had to walk carefully on the unshoveled path to avoid slipping.
