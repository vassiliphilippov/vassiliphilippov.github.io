Signorina

noun
Italian title for an unmarried woman
6.6
Pronunciation: / siˌnɔːˈriːnə / (AmE), / siˌnɔːˈriːnə / (BrE)
An Italian title or form of address for an unmarried woman, equivalent to 'Miss' in English.

Examples

Signorina in Italy
Excuse me, Signorina, could you help me with the menu?
Many Italians still address unmarried women as Signorina in formal situations.
The gentleman greeted the young Signorina with a warm smile.

Related Terms
