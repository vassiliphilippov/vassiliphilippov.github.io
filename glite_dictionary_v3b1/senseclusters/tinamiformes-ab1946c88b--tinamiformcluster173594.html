Tinamiformes

noun
order of birds (tinamous)
Pronunciation: / tɪˌnæməˈfɔrmiːz /; / tɪˌnæmɪˈfɔrmiːz / (AmE), / tɪˌnæmɪˈfɔːmiːz /; / tɪˌnamɪˈfɔːmiːz / (BrE)
an order of ground-dwelling birds (tinamous) found in Central and South America, belonging to the family Tinamidae
