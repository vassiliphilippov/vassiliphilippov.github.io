enlace

verb
to join or entangle together
7.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈleɪs / (AmE), / ɪnˈleɪs / (BrE)
to join, intertwine, or entwine together; to combine by winding or linking

Examples

To enlace vines
I watched as the vines slowly enlaced around the old fence.
The two organizations decided to enlace their resources for the project.
They hope to enlace their talents to create something truly unique.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intertwine - to twist or interweave together
