deploy

verb
to position or distribute strategically
Pronunciation: / dɪˈplɔɪ / (AmE), / dɪˈplɔɪ / (BrE)
to arrange or use people, resources, or equipment for a particular purpose, often in an organized or strategic way

Examples

To deploy emergency services
During the crisis, emergency services were deployed to affected areas.
The general decided to deploy additional troops along the border.
The tech company will deploy its new software across all offices next week.
The company will deploy new software next month to improve efficiency.
The government decided to deploy additional police officers during the festival.
Engineers deployed the latest technology to solve the complex problem.

  • synonym: utilize - to make practical use of something
