tweak

noun
a pinch or twist with the fingers
4.6 0.23/m
Pronunciation: / twiÀêk / (AmE), / twiÀêk / (BrE)
an act of pinching or slightly twisting something with the fingers

Examples

A playful tweak
He felt a sharp tweak on his shoulder when his friend tried to get his attention.
She gave his ear a playful tweak before running away.
The teacher gave the boy's nose a gentle tweak as a joke.

Synonyms

  • twiddle
    6.1 0.02/m
