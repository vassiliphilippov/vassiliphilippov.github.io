vocationalism

noun
focus on job training
Pronunciation: / voʊˈkeɪʃənəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / vəʊˈkeɪʃənəˌlɪzəm / (BrE)
the practice or policy of emphasizing training and education for specific trades or careers

Examples

Vocationalism in education
The new curriculum reflects a strong commitment to vocationalism in secondary education.
Critics argue that too much vocationalism can limit students' broader learning opportunities.
Many parents appreciate the school's emphasis on vocationalism for preparing students for the workforce.

Related Terms

  • antonym: academicism - emphasis on academic or theoretical education
  • synonym: vocational education - education that prepares people for specific trades or careers
