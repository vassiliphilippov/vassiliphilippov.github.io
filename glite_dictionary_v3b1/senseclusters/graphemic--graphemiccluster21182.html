graphemic

adjective
relating to graphemes
9.0
Pronunciation: / ɡræˈfiːmɪk / (AmE), / ɡræˈfiːmɪk / (BrE)
relating to graphemes, the smallest units of a writing system such as letters or characters

Examples

The graphemic system
The graphemic analysis of a language helps linguists understand its writing system.
Children often struggle with the graphemic rules when learning to spell new words.
Researchers compared the graphemic patterns of English and Japanese scripts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: phonemic - relating to phonemes, the smallest units of sound in a language
