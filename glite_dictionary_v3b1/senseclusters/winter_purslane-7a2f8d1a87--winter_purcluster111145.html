winter purslane

noun
a succulent edible herb native to western North America
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪn.tɚ ˈpɝːs.leɪn / (AmE), / ˈwɪn.tə ˈpɜːs.leɪn / (BrE)
a succulent herb (Claytonia perfoliata), sometimes grown for use in salads or as a pot herb, native to the Pacific coast of North America

Examples

Winter purslane plant
Winter purslane thrives in cool weather and can be harvested throughout the winter months.
Gardeners appreciate winter purslane because it grows well in poor soil and needs little maintenance.
He added a handful of winter purslane to the salad for its mild, fresh flavor.
