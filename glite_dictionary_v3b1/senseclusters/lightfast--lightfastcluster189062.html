lightfast

adjective
resistant to fading
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtˌfæst / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtˌfɑːst / (BrE)
not easily faded or changed in color when exposed to light

Examples

Lightfast inks
The museum uses only lightfast inks to preserve the quality of its prints.
If you want your painting to last, choose lightfast pigments for your colors.
Textiles made with lightfast dyes are ideal for rooms with lots of sunlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fade-resistant - not likely to lose color or brightness
  • antonym: fugitive - likely to fade or change color quickly
