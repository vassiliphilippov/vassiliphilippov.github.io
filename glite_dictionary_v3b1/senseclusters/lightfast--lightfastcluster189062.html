lightfast

adjective
resistant to fading
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtˌfæst / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtˌfɑːst / (BrE)
not easily faded or changed in color when exposed to light

Examples

Lightfast inks
The museum uses only lightfast inks to preserve the quality of its prints.

Textiles made with lightfast dyes are ideal for rooms with lots of sunlight.
