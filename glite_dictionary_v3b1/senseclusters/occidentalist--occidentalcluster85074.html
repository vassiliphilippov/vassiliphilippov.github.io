occidentalist

adjective
about the West
Pronunciation: / ˌɑksɪˈdɛntəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌɒksɪˈdɛntəlɪst / (BrE)
relating to ideas, attitudes, or representations of the West, especially in the context of analysis or critique of Western culture

Examples

Occidentalist critique
The article offered an occidentalist perspective on global politics, focusing on Western influence.
Her research takes an occidentalist approach to analyzing cultural trends in Europe and North America.
Some critics argue that occidentalist views can oversimplify the diversity within Western societies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: orientalist - relating to ideas or representations of the East
  • synonym: western - relating to the West or Western countries
