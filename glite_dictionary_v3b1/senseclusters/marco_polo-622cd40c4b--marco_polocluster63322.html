Marco Polo

proper noun
Venetian explorer and traveler of Asia
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːrkoʊ ˈpoʊloʊ / (AmE), / ˌmɑːkəʊ ˈpəʊləʊ / (BrE)
A Venetian explorer and traveler of the 13th century, known for his extensive journeys through Asia and his service to Kublai Khan.

Examples

Marco Polo explorer
Marco Polo wrote detailed accounts of his travels in China that fascinated readers across Europe.
