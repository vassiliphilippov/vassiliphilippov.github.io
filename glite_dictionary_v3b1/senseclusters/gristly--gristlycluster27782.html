gristly

adjective
containing tough, chewy tissue
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɪs.li / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪs.li / (BrE)
(of food, especially meat) containing tough tissue that is difficult to chew.

Examples

Gristly meat
He bit into a gristly piece of meat and immediately wished he had chosen the fish instead.
The steak was too gristly for me to enjoy, so I left most of it on my plate.
The stew was flavorful, but some chunks were a bit gristly and hard to chew.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tender - easy to cut or chew (food)
  • synonym: tough - difficult to cut or chew (food)
