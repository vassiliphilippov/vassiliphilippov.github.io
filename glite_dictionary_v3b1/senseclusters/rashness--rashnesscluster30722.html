rashness

noun
the quality of being rash or reckless
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈræʃ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈræʃ.nəs /; / ˈræʃ.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of acting or speaking without careful thought about the possible consequences; recklessness

Examples

His rashness in decisions
His rashness in making quick decisions often led to unnecessary problems.
History is full of leaders whose rashness brought about disastrous consequences.
The accident was a direct result of her rashness behind the wheel.
He acted with rashness, not considering how dangerous the shortcut might be.
Her rashness often led her into situations she would later regret.
The rashness of his decision surprised everyone at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: prudence - the quality of being cautious and wise
  • synonym: recklessness - lack of regard for danger or consequences
