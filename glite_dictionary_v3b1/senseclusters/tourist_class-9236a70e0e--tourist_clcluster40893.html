tourist class

noun
least expensive travel class
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtʊrɪst klæs / (AmE), / ˈtʊərɪst klɑːs / (BrE)
the least expensive accommodations on a ship, train, or airplane, typically offering basic comfort and few amenities
