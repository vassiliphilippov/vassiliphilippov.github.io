Colutea

noun
genus of Eurasian shrubs with yellow flowers and bladder pods
Pronunciation: / koʊˈluːtiə / (AmE), / kɒˈluːtɪə / (BrE)
a small genus of Eurasian shrubs, known for their yellow flowers and inflated, bladder-like seed pods.

Colutea yellow flowers
