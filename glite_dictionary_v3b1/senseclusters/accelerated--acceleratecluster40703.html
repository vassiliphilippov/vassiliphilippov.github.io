accelerated

adjective
made faster than usual; expedited
1.0 3.4
Pronunciation: / əkˈsɛlɚˌeɪtɪd / (AmE), / əkˈsɛl.əˌreɪ.tɪd / (BrE)

The company offers an accelerated training course for experienced professionals.
