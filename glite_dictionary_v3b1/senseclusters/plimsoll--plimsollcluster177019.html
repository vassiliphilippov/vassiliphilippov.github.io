plimsoll

noun
light canvas sports shoe with rubber sole
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈplɪmˌsoʊl / (AmE), / ˈplɪmsəl / (BrE)
a light canvas shoe with a rubber sole, typically worn for sports or physical education, especially in Britain

Examples
