ghazal

noun
Persian poetic form
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌzəl / (AmE), / ˈɡʌzæl / (BrE)
a form of poetry consisting of rhyming couplets and a refrain, often expressing themes of love or loss, originating from Persian tradition

Examples

A ghazal poem
She recited a moving ghazal at the poetry reading, captivating the entire audience.
The musician set a classic ghazal to music, blending traditional lyrics with modern melodies.
Many poets are inspired by the emotional depth found in a ghazal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ode - a lyric poem expressing praise or emotion
  • synonym: sonnet - a 14-line poem with a specific rhyme scheme
