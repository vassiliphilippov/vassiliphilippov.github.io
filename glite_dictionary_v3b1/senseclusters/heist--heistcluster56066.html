Synonyms

  • robbery
    2.3 17/m
    robbery is the legal term for stealing with force or threat, while heist is a planned, often clever theft.
  • holdup
    4.6 0.32/m
    holdup is a robbery that uses direct threats or weapons against people, while heist focuses on careful planning and complex stealing.
  • stickup
    7.9 0.24/m
    stickup is a sudden, armed robbery often at gunpoint, while heist suggests a planned operation that may avoid direct threats.
