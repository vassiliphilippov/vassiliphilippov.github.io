heist

noun
act of stealing; robbery
Pronunciation: / haɪst / (AmE), / haɪst / (BrE)
a robbery or act of stealing, especially one that is carefully planned

Examples

A bank heist
A group of thieves pulled off a daring heist at the art museum last night.
The diamond heist was meticulously planned and shocked the city.
The movie tells the story of an elaborate bank heist gone wrong.
After the daring heist, the thieves vanished without a trace.
That movie is about a complex heist that goes wrong.
The gang planned a heist on the city's main jewelry store.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burglary - unlawful entry into a building with intent to commit a crime, especially theft
  • synonym: robbery - the taking of property unlawfully from a person or place by force or threat
  • synonym: theft - the act of taking someone's property without permission
