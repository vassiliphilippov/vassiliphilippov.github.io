military press

noun
barbell exercise lifted overhead
3.3 4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪl.ɪ.tər.iː pres/ /; / /ˈmɪl.ɪ.tri pres/ (AmE), /ˈmɪl.ɪ.tri pres/ /; / /ˈmɪl.ɪ.tə.riː pres/ (BrE)
