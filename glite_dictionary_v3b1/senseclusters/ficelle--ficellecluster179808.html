ficelle

noun
thin string or twine
Pronunciation: / fiˈsɛl / (AmE), / fiˈsɛl / (BrE)
a thin string or twine used for tying or fastening things, especially in packaging or cooking

Examples

Tie with ficelle
She tied the parcel securely with a length of ficelle before sending it by post.
The chef used ficelle to hold the stuffed chicken together while it roasted.
He wrapped the newspapers with ficelle before placing them in the recycling bin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cord - a long, thin, flexible string or rope
  • degree higher: rope - a thick, strong cord made by twisting together strands of fiber
  • synonym: twine - strong string made by twisting two or more strands together
