loss

noun
the death of a loved one
1.8
Pronunciation: / lɔs /; / lɑs / (AmE), / lɒs / (BrE)
the experience of having someone close to you die or be taken away; a way of referring to someone's death

Examples

A great loss

The family was overwhelmed by the sudden loss of their mother.
We sent flowers to express our sympathy for their loss.
After his friend's sudden loss, he took time away from work to grieve.
Her family was deeply affected by the loss of her grandfather last year.
We express our condolences for your loss and hope you find comfort soon.
