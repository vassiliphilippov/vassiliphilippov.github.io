Newfoundland dog

noun
large, intelligent dog breed from Newfoundland
5.0 3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːfəndlənd dɔg / (AmE), / ˈnjuːfəndlənd dɒg / (BrE)
A very large breed of dog with a thick, coarse, usually black coat, known for its intelligence and strong swimming ability, originally bred in Newfoundland.

Examples

A Newfoundland dog breed
A well-trained Newfoundland dog can pull small carts or assist with heavy loads.
Her family owns a gentle Newfoundland dog with a glossy black coat.
The Newfoundland dog is famous for its lifesaving rescues in the water.
