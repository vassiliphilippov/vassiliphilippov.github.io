messaging

noun
communication strategy or content
3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈmes.ɪ.dʒɪŋ/ /; / /ˈmes.ə.dʒɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈmes.ɪ.dʒɪŋ/ /; / /ˈmes.ə.dʒɪŋ/ (BrE)
the content, key themes, or overall strategy used by a person or organization to communicate with a particular audience, especially in marketing, public relations, or political campaigns.

Examples

Political messaging
The campaign's messaging emphasized unity and economic growth to persuade undecided voters.
During onboarding, the new hire reviewed the company's messaging guidelines and tone-of-voice documents.
They adjusted their product messaging after customer feedback highlighted confusion about features.

Related Terms

  • synonym: communication strategy - a plan for delivering particular messages to specific audiences to achieve goals
  • synonym: message - the main idea or information that is communicated
