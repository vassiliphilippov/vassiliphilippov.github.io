pall of smoke

noun
a thick cloud of smoke
7.3 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / p…îl …ôv smo äk / (AmE), / p…îÀêl …ôv sm…ô äk / (BrE)
a thick, heavy cloud of smoke, often obscuring the view or creating a gloomy atmosphere

Examples

A pall of smoke

Firefighters struggled to see through the thick pall of smoke filling the building.
The pall of smoke drifted across the road, making it hard to see.

Synonyms
