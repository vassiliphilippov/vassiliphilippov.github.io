gestation

noun
period of development in the womb
6.1
Pronunciation: / dʒɛˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / dʒɛˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
the period during which a baby develops inside the womb, from conception to birth

Examples

Human gestation period
During gestation, proper nutrition is essential for both mother and baby.
The doctor explained that human gestation typically lasts about 40 weeks.
The elephant's gestation period is nearly two years, one of the longest in the animal kingdom.
A healthy diet is essential during gestation to support the development of the fetus.
Elephants have a much longer gestation period compared to most other mammals.
The doctor monitored the mother's health throughout the gestation of the baby.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pregnancy - the state of carrying a developing fetus within the body
