expressionlessness

noun
lack of emotion
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsprɛʃənləsnəs / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛʃənləsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of not showing any emotion or feeling in your face, voice, or actions
