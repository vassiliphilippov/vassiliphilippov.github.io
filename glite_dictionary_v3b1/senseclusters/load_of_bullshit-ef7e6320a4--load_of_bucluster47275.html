load of bullshit

noun
vulgar term for nonsense or lies
Pronunciation: / loʊd əv ˈbʊlˌʃɪt / (AmE), / ləʊd əv ˈbʊlʃɪt / (BrE)
A vulgar expression meaning something that is nonsense, untrue, or deliberately misleading.

Examples

A load of bullshit
Don't believe her story—it's just a load of bullshit.
Everything he said sounded like a load of bullshit to me.
The manager dismissed the accusations as a load of bullshit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonsense - words or ideas that have no meaning or make no sense
  • synonym: rubbish - nonsense or something without value; British informal synonym
