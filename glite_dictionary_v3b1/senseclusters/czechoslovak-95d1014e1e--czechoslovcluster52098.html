Czechoslovak

noun
person from former Czechoslovakia
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌtʃɛkəsləˈvɑk / (AmE), / ˌtʃɛkəsləˈvak / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of the former republic of Czechoslovakia, which existed in Central Europe from 1918 to 1992

Examples

A Czechoslovak citizen
Many Czechoslovaks emigrated to the United States during the 20th century.
My grandfather was a proud Czechoslovak who lived through many changes in Europe.
The Czechoslovak delegation attended the international peace conference in 1946.
