scarlet cup

noun
a red, cup-shaped spring fungus
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈskɑr.lɪt kʌp / (AmE), / ˈskɑː.lɪt kʌp / (BrE)
a small, bright red, cup-shaped fungus that typically appears in early spring, with a tough, saucer-like fruiting body and a pale exterior

Examples

A scarlet cup fungus
A single scarlet cup caught the sunlight, drawing attention to its vivid color.
Botanists often spot scarlet cups early in spring before other fungi appear.
