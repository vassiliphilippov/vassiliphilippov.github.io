oversocialized

adjective
excessively sociable
8.4
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ˈsoʊ.ʃə.laɪzd/ /; / /oʊ.vər.ˈsoʊ.ʃəl.aɪzd/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.ˈsəʊ.ʃəl.aɪzd/ /; / /əʊ.və.ˈsəʊ.ʃə.laɪzd/ (BrE)
having an excessive need or tendency for social interaction, often to a degree that may seem inappropriate or overwhelming

Examples

The camp counselor was so oversocialized that she organized group activities every hour.
