oversocialized

adjective
excessively sociable
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈsoʊʃəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈsəʊʃəlaɪzd / (BrE)
having an excessive need or tendency for social interaction, often to a degree that may seem inappropriate or overwhelming

Examples

Oversocialized party guest
Some people found his oversocialized behavior exhausting at every party.
Her oversocialized nature made it difficult for her to spend time alone.
The camp counselor was so oversocialized that she organized group activities every hour.

Related Terms

  • antonym: antisocial - avoiding the company of others; not sociable
  • degree lower: sociable - enjoying the company of others; friendly
