electrical storm

noun
cluster of dangerous heart arrhythmias
Pronunciation: / 6ac8l5bktr6ak59l st54rm / (AmE), / 6ac8l5bktr6ak59l st54d0m / (BrE)
(in medicine, cardiology) a rapid series or clustering of severe cardiac arrhythmias (for example, ventricular tachycardia or ventricular fibrillation) occurring within a short time and usually requiring urgent treatment.

Examples

Electrical storm in the heart
After his heart attack, doctors diagnosed an electrical storm and rushed him to intensive care.
The cardiology team used medication and ablation to control the patient's electrical storm.
Multiple episodes of ventricular tachycardia created an electrical storm that required urgent defibrillation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrhythmic storm - an alternative term for a rapid recurrence of life-threatening cardiac arrhythmias
