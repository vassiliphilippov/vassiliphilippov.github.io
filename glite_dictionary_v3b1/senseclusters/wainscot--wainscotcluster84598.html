wainscot

noun
wooden wall panels
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪnskət /; / ˈweɪnsˌkɑt / (AmE), / ˈweɪnskət / (BrE)
wooden panels or paneling that cover the lower part of a wall for decoration or protection
