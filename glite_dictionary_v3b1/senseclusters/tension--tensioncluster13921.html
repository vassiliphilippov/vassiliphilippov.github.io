Examples

Rope tension
As you pull the rope, the tension in it increases.
The bridge cables are under enormous tension to support the roadway.
The material can withstand high tension without snapping.
High tension is needed for the cable to support the weight of the bridge.
If you reduce the tension, the tent fabric will become loose and flap in the wind.

Yoga helps to relieve physical tension in the back and shoulders.

Synonyms

  • tautness
    5.3 0.14/m
    tautness describes something pulled tight with no slack, while tension also means the force or state that makes it tight.
  • tenseness
    6.4 0.01/m
    tenseness is when muscles or the body feel tight and not relaxed, while tension can mean the force causing that tightness.
  • tensity
    8.3 0.00/m
    tensity names the physical condition of being stretched or strained, while tension also refers to the pulling force behind it.
  • tightness
    5.5 0.81/m
    tightness is a feeling of being squeezed or constricted in the body, while tension is the state or force that stretches things.
