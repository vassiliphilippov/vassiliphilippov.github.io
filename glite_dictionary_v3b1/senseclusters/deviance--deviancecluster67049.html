deviance

noun
departure from social norms
Pronunciation: / ˈdiː.vi.əns / (AmE), / ˈdiː.vi.əns / (BrE)
behavior or the state of being different from what is normal or accepted, especially in a social or moral sense

Examples

Social deviance
Sociologists often study deviance to understand why people break social rules.
The film portrayed acts of deviance against traditional family values.
What is considered deviance can vary greatly between cultures.
His deviance from accepted customs made him stand out in the community.
Schools sometimes struggle with how to address deviance among students.
The study focuses on social deviance and how societies respond to it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conformity - behavior in accordance with accepted standards
  • synonym: deviation - the action of departing from an accepted standard
  • antonym: normality - the state of being normal; conformity to the norm
