Synonyms

  • shooting

    shooting is the act of using a gun or weapon to shoot, while firing is the formal noun often used in reports.
  • gunshot
    2.3 6/m
    gunshot is a single shot or its sound from a firearm, while firing refers to shooting generally or repeatedly.
  • discharge
    5.5 0.5/m
    discharge is a formal word for letting a weapon or device release a shot, while firing is more common in reports.
  • gunfire
    2.5 7/m
    gunfire is the sound or ongoing bursts of shots in combat, while firing names the act of shooting a weapon more neutrally.
  • fire
    5.9 50/m
    fire is used for planned shooting by armies or artillery, while firing is a general word for shooting any gun or weapon.
