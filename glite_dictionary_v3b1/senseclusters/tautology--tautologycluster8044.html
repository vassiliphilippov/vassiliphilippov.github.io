tautology

noun
useless repetition of meaning

Examples

Empty tautology
Critics argue that some political promises sound like empty tautology.
Saying 'he made it with his own hands' is a tautology because it is obvious.

Synonyms

  • redundancy
    5.2 1/m
    redundancy means something is unnecessary or repeated in many contexts, while tautology is specifically repeated wording that makes a statement needless.
