superfly

noun
very stylish person
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəflaɪ / (BrE)
a person who is extremely fashionable, stylish, or impressive in appearance or manner

Examples

A superfly at the party
Everyone admired the superfly who arrived in a bright red suit and matching shoes.
At the party, she was the superfly everyone wanted to talk to about fashion.
He always tries to dress like a superfly to stand out from the crowd.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fashionista - a person who is very interested in fashion
  • antonym: plain Jane - a person with an ordinary or unremarkable appearance
  • synonym: trendsetter - a person who leads the way in fashion or ideas
