feed the troll

verb
engage with an online provocateur
4.2
Pronunciation: / fid ðə troʊl / (AmE), / fiːd ðə trəʊl / (BrE)
to engage with someone who posts deliberately provocative or upsetting messages online, thereby encouraging further disruptive behavior

Examples

To feed the troll online
If you reply to his comments, you'll only feed the troll and make things worse.
It's best not to feed the troll in online discussions; just ignore them.
Many forums warn users not to feed the troll to keep conversations productive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: troll (internet) - someone who posts inflammatory or disruptive comments online
