playthrough

noun
full rehearsal run
4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈpleɪ.θruː/ (AmE), /ˈpleɪ.θruː/ (BrE)
a complete performance or practice of a play, scene, or piece of music from beginning to end

Examples

A playthrough rehearsal
The orchestra scheduled a playthrough of the entire symphony before the concert.
During the playthrough, the director made notes on the actors' timing and delivery.
We did a playthrough of the scene to make sure everyone knew their cues.
