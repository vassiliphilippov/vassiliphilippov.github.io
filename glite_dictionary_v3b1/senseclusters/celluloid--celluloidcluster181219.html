celluloid

noun
medium for photographic film or movies
2.6 8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛl.jə.lɔɪd /; / ˈsɛljəˌlɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɛl.jʊ.lɔɪd /; / ˈsɛljʊlɔɪd / (BrE)
a transparent, flammable material used in early photographic film and, by extension, a term for the movies or the film industry

Examples
