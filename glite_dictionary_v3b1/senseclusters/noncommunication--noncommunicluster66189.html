noncommunication

noun
lack of communication
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the absence or failure of communication between people or groups

Examples

Team noncommunication
Persistent noncommunication can lead to misunderstandings in a team.
The noncommunication between the two departments caused delays in the project.
After the argument, there was complete noncommunication between the friends for weeks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: communication - the act of sharing information or ideas
  • synonym: silence - the state of not speaking or making noise
