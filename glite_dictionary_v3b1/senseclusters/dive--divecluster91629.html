dive

noun
cheap, disreputable bar or club
Pronunciation: / daɪv / (AmE), / daɪv / (BrE)
a cheap, disreputable bar, nightclub, or dance hall, often considered shabby or unrespectable

Examples

A local dive bar
That old dive has sticky floors but the friendliest staff around.
They avoided the fancy bars and preferred a local dive with cheap drinks.
We ended up in a dive on the edge of town, listening to live blues music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bar - a place where alcoholic drinks are served
