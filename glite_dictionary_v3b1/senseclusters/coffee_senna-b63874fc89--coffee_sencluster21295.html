coffee senna

noun
tropical shrub with seeds used as coffee adulterant
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑː.fiː ˈsen.ə/ /; / /ˈkɔː.fiː ˈsen.ə/ (AmE), /ˈkɒf.iː ˈsen.ə/ /; / /ˈkɔː.fɪ ˈsen.ə/ (BrE)
