frostless

adjective
prevents frost
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɔstləs / (AmE), / ˈfrɒstləs / (BrE)
not affected by frost or designed to prevent the formation of frost, especially in appliances like refrigerators

Examples

Frostless fridge
The frostless refrigerator keeps food fresh without ice building up inside.
Many modern freezers are frostless, so you do not need to defrost them manually.
We bought a frostless model to avoid the hassle of cleaning out ice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frost-free - designed to prevent the buildup of frost
