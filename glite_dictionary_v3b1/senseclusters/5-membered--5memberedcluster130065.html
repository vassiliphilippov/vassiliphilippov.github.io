5-membered

adjective
having a five-membered ring (chemistry)
8.0
Pronunciation: / faɪvˈmɛmbɚd / (AmE), / faɪvˈmɛmbəd / (BrE)
(of a chemical compound) having a ring structure consisting of five atoms or groups.

Examples

5-membered ring structure
Imidazole is an example of a 5-membered aromatic ring system.
Many pharmaceuticals include at least one 5-membered ring in their structure.
The molecule contains a 5-membered heterocyclic ring, which is common in organic chemistry.
