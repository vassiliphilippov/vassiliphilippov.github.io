arsenal

noun
collection of resources or tools (figurative)

A collection of resources, tools, techniques, or capabilities that a person, group, or organization can use to achieve a purpose (figurative).

Examples

Arsenal of tools
The company expanded its arsenal of data analytics tools to accelerate decision-making across teams.
She added several public-speaking techniques to her professional arsenal before the conference.
The coach introduced a new set of drills into the team's defensive arsenal this season.
