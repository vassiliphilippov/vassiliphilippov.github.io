chemoreception

noun
detection of chemicals
Pronunciation: / ˌkiːmoʊrɪˈsɛpʃən / (AmE), / ˌkiːməʊrɪˈsɛpʃən / (BrE)
the process by which an organism or cell detects and responds to chemical signals or changes in its environment or body
