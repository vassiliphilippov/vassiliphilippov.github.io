catch one's breath

verb
pause to rest and recover breathing
Pronunciation: / kætʃ wʌnz brɛθ / (AmE), / katʃ wʌnz brɛθ /; / kætʃ wʌnz brɛθ / (BrE)
to pause to rest or recover normal breathing, especially after physical activity or a sudden shock

Examples

To catch his breath
After running up the stairs, he stopped to catch his breath before going into the meeting.
Give me a moment to catch my breath; that was a tough workout!
She paused halfway up the hill to catch her breath and admire the view.
After running up the stairs, I had to catch my breath before starting the meeting.
Give me a moment to catch my breath—that was a tough workout!
She paused on the path to catch her breath and admire the view.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recover - to return to a normal state after exertion, loss, or shock
  • synonym: rest - to cease work or movement in order to relax or recover
  • synonym: take a breather - to have a short rest or break
