catch one's breath

verb
pause to rest and recover breathing
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /kætʃ wʌnz breθ/ /; / /kætʃ wʌns breθ/ /; / /kætʃ wənz breθ/ (AmE), /kætʃ wʌnz breθ/ /; / /kætʃ wənz breθ/ /; / /kætʃ wʌns breθ/ (BrE)
to pause to rest or recover normal breathing, especially after physical activity or a sudden shock

Examples

To catch his breath
