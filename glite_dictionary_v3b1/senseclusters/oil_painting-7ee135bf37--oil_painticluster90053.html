oil painting

noun
digital artwork in oil style
Pronunciation: / ɔɪl ˈpeɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / ɔɪl ˈpeɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
a digital image or artwork created by software or artificial intelligence that imitates the visual style of a traditional oil painting, without using real oil-based paints.

Examples

AI oil painting
The app turned my photo into an oil painting with dramatic brushstrokes and rich color.
She printed the AI-generated oil painting and framed it for her living room wall last week.
At the gallery, a digital oil painting attracted visitors who expected traditional canvas and real oil paint.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digital art - art created or presented using digital technology
  • synonym: digital painting - an artwork created digitally using software or a tablet, rather than physical paint
