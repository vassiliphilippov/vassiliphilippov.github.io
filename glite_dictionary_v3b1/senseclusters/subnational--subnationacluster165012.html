subnational

adjective
relating to regions
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈnæʃənəl / (AmE), / sʌbˈnæʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to a part of a country, such as a state, province, or region, rather than the whole country

Examples

Subnational government
The new policy allows subnational authorities to manage their own education systems.
Many environmental initiatives are led by subnational governments rather than the national government.
Differences in healthcare can be seen at the subnational level across the country.

Related Terms

  • antonym: national - relating to a whole country
  • synonym: regional - relating to a particular region or area
