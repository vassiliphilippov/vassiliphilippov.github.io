Quercus prinus

noun
species of oak tree (chestnut oak) from eastern US
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɜːrkəs ˈpraɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈkwɜːkəs ˈpriːnəs / (BrE)
A medium to large deciduous tree native to the eastern United States, known as chestnut oak, valued for its durable wood used in timber, basketry, and chair seats.
