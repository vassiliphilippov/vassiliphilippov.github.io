peeve

verb
to annoy or irritate
6.8 0.1/m
Pronunciation: / piv / (AmE), / piÀêv / (BrE)
to annoy, irritate, or make someone resentful

Examples

To peeve someone
He was peeving his sister by playing loud music all night.
Her attitude peeved everyone in the group.
It really peeves me when people are late to meetings.
