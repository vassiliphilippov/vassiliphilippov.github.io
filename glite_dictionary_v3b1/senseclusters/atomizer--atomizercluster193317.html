atomizer

noun
device that sprays liquid as mist
5.2 4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈæt.ə.maɪ.zər/ (AmE), /ˈæt.ə.maɪ.zə/ (BrE)
A device that sprays or converts a liquid into a fine spray or mist, often used for perfumes or medicinal purposes.

Examples

Perfume atomizer
Before painting, he filled the atomiser with water to moisten the canvas.
She keeps an atomiser of perfume in her handbag for quick touch-ups during the day.
The nurse used a nasal atomiser to administer the medication to the patient.
Modern hair salons often use an atomizer to evenly distribute water or conditioner on hair.
She filled the atomizer with her favorite perfume before getting ready for the party.
The artist used an atomizer to apply a light mist of color to the canvas.

  • synonym: sprayer - device for spraying liquid in drops
