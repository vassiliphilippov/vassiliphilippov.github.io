ahoy

verb
to hail at sea
Pronunciation: / əˈhɔɪ / (AmE), / əˈhɔɪ / (BrE)
to call out 'ahoy' in order to hail or attract the attention of a ship or person, especially at sea

Examples

To ahoy a ship
The sailors tried to ahoy the passing vessel in the foggy morning.
He stood at the bow and ahoied the nearby fishing boat to warn them of rocks.
During the reenactment, the captain would often ahoy other ships for fun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hail - to call out to attract attention, especially at sea
  • antonym: ignore - to deliberately pay no attention to someone or something
