principate

noun
state ruled by prince
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪnsɪˌpeɪt / (AmE), / ˈprɪnsɪpeɪt / (BrE)
a territory or state ruled by a prince; a principality

Examples

A small principate
The small principate was known for its beautiful castles and rich history.
After years of conflict, the principate finally achieved independence from its neighboring kingdom.
The prince governed the principate with fairness and wisdom, earning the respect of his people.

Related Terms

  • antonym: kingdom - a country ruled by a king or queen
  • synonym: principality - a territory ruled by a prince
