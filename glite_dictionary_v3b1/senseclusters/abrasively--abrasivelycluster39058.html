abrasively

adverb
harshly or roughly
6.5
Pronunciation: / əˈbreɪsɪvli / (AmE), / əˈbreɪsɪvli / (BrE)
in a harsh, rough, or irritating manner, often causing discomfort or offense

Examples

Speak abrasively
The manager addressed the team abrasively, leaving many employees feeling upset.
She responded abrasively to the criticism, making the discussion tense.
His comments were delivered abrasively, which offended several people in the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gently - in a kind or soft manner
  • synonym: harshly - in a severe or rough manner
  • synonym: rudely - in an impolite or disrespectful way
