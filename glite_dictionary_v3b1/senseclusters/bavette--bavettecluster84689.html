bavette

noun
protective chest cloth
Pronunciation: / bəˈvɛt / (AmE), / bəˈvɛt / (BrE)
a small cloth or piece of material worn over the chest to protect clothing while eating or working

Examples

Chef's bavette apron
The chef wore a clean bavette to keep his uniform spotless while preparing food.
She tied a waterproof bavette around the toddler's neck before feeding him in the high chair.
Workers in the bakery use a bavette to protect their clothes from flour and dough.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apron - a garment worn over the front of clothes to keep them clean
  • synonym: bib - a cloth worn around the neck to protect clothing while eating
