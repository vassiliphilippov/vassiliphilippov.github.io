twinness

noun
state of being a twin
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪnnəs / (AmE), / ˈtwɪnnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being a twin or closely resembling a twin

Examples

The twinness of brothers
The twinness of the brothers made it hard for teachers to tell them apart.
She was fascinated by the twinness she shared with her sister, from looks to mannerisms.
Their twinness was evident in the way they finished each other's sentences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duality - the state of having two parts or aspects
  • antonym: uniqueness - the quality of being one of a kind
