sago palm

noun
dwarf Japanese cycad yielding sago
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪɡoʊ pɑm /; / ˈseɪɡoʊ ˌpɑm / (AmE), / ˈseɪɡəʊ pɑːm /; / ˈseɪɡəʊ ˌpɑːm / (BrE)
a plant (either a cycad or a palm tree) whose trunk yields sago, a starchy food substance
