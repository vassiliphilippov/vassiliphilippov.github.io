bell-bottom

adjective
flared at the bottom (of trousers)
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛl ˌbɑtəm / (AmE), / ˈbɛl ˌbɒtəm / (BrE)
(of trousers) having legs that become wider from the knee downward, creating a flared effect at the bottom.

Examples

Bell-bottom trousers
Many sailors in the past were known for their distinctive bell-bottom uniforms.
She wore her favorite pair of bell-bottom jeans to the party.
The fashion show featured models in bell-bottom trousers reminiscent of the 1970s.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flared - (of clothing) becoming wider at the end
