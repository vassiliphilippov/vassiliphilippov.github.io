valiant

noun
brave person
Pronunciation: / ˈvæliənt / (AmE), / ˈvæliənt / (BrE)
a person who shows great courage or bravery, especially in difficult situations

Examples

A valiant hero
The valiant led the rescue mission despite the dangerous conditions.
Stories are often told about a valiant who stood up for justice.
In the face of disaster, every valiant was remembered for their bravery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coward - a person who lacks courage or is easily frightened
  • synonym: hero - a person admired for courage or noble qualities
