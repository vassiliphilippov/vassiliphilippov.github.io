legate

noun
member of a diplomatic mission
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛɡ.eɪt / (AmE), / ˈlɛɡ.eɪt / (BrE)
a member of a diplomatic mission, especially one representing a government in a foreign country; more broadly, a diplomatic representative.

Examples

A legate from government
As a respected legate, she successfully resolved the diplomatic dispute.
Each legate had specific instructions from the foreign ministry before the conference.
The legate attended the negotiations on behalf of his country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ambassador - a diplomatic official of the highest rank sent by one country as its long-term representative to another
  • synonym: envoy - a person sent as a diplomatic representative on a special mission
