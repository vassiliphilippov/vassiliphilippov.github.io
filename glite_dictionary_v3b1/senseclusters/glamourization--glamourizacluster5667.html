glamourization

noun
the act of making something seem more attractive
Pronunciation: / ˌɡlæmərɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɡlæmərəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of making something or someone appear more attractive or appealing, often in a superficial or artificial way

Examples

The glamourization of crime
Many films contribute to the glamourization of crime, making it seem appealing to young audiences.
She criticized the glamourization of unhealthy lifestyles in fashion magazines.
The glamourization of violence in the media has raised concerns among parents and educators.

  • synonym: glamorization - US spelling: the act of making glamorous
