surreptitiousness

noun
secretive quality
Pronunciation: / ˌsɜːrəpˈtɪʃəsnəs / (AmE), / ˌsʌrəpˈtɪʃəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being secretive or acting in a hidden or stealthy way

Examples

The surreptitiousness of actions
The surreptitiousness of his actions made everyone wonder what he was hiding.
Her surreptitiousness allowed her to gather information without being noticed.
Because of the surreptitiousness involved, the plan remained a secret for months.

  • synonym: secrecy - the state of keeping something secret or hidden
