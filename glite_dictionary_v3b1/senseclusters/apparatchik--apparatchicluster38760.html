apparatchik

noun
official in a communist party bureaucracy
Pronunciation: / ˌæpəˈrɑːtʃɪk /; / əˈpɑːrətʃɪk / (AmE), / ˌæpəˈratʃɪk /; / əˈparətʃɪk / (BrE)
an official or bureaucrat in a political party or large organization, especially one who is blindly loyal and follows orders without question

Examples

A loyal apparatchik
As a loyal apparatchik, he carried out the party's orders without question.
Many Soviet apparatchiks lost their positions after the collapse of the Communist Party.
She criticized the rigid mindset typical of the average apparatchik.
As a loyal apparatchik, he always followed orders without question or hesitation.
Critics accused the council of being run by apparatchiks instead of true public servants.
The organization promoted her to apparatchik, entrusting her with overseeing compliance across all departments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bureaucrat - official in a bureaucracy
  • degree lower: party member - a member of a political party
