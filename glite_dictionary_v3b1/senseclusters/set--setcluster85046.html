set

noun
collection of similar things used as a group
Pronunciation: / sɛt / (AmE), / sɛt / (BrE)
a group or collection of things or people that belong together or are used together

Examples

A set of keys
He owns a set of rare coins from around the world.
She bought a beautiful set of dishes for her new apartment.
The detective examined the complete set of fingerprints found at the scene.
She quickly became popular among the fashion set in Paris.
The local set of artists often meets at that café downtown.
The smart set always attends that annual gala.

Related Terms

  • synonym: circle - a group of people with shared interests, often exclusive
  • synonym: clique - a small exclusive group of people
  • synonym: collection - a group of things collected together
  • synonym: kit - a set of articles or equipment needed for a specific purpose
  • degree lower: series - a number of things or events of the same kind coming one after another
