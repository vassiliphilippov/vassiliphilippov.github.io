Melanesian

noun
person from Melanesia
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛləˈniːʒən / (AmE), / ˌmɛləˈniːʒən / (BrE)
a person who is native to or descends from Melanesia, a region in the southwestern Pacific Ocean

Examples

A Melanesian person
A Melanesian greeted us warmly when we arrived on the island.
Many Melanesians maintain traditional customs alongside modern lifestyles.
The art of the Melanesian people is known for its vibrant colors and patterns.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Micronesian - a person from Micronesia, a region in the western Pacific Ocean
  • antonym: Polynesian - a person from Polynesia, a region in the central and southern Pacific Ocean
