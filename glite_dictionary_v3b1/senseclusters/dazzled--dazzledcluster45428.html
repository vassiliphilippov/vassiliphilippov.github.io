dazzled

adjective
overwhelmed by brilliance or power
Pronunciation: / ˈdæzəld / (AmE), / ˈdæzəld / (BrE)
Overwhelmed or stupefied by something impressively bright, beautiful, or powerful.

Examples

She stood dazzled by the sheer beauty of the mountain sunrise.
The audience was dazzled by the magician's incredible performance.
