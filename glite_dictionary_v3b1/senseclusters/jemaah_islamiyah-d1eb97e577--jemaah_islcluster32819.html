Jemaah Islamiyah

proper noun
Southeast Asian militant Islamist group
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈmɑ ɪsˌlɑːmiˈjɑː / (AmE), / dʒəˈmɑː ɪsˌlɑːmiˈjɑː / (BrE)
