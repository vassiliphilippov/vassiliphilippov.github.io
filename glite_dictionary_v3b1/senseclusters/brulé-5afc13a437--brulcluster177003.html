Brulé

adjective
burnt or caramelized
7.7
Pronunciation: / bruːˈleɪ / (AmE), / bruːˈleɪ / (BrE)
having a burnt or caramelized surface, especially in reference to food prepared with a layer of caramelized sugar.

Examples

Brulé sugar topping
The chef finished the dessert with a perfectly Brulé sugar topping.
She admired the Brulé crust on the classic French custard.
A Brulé finish adds a delightful crunch to many desserts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caramelized - having sugar that has been browned by heat
