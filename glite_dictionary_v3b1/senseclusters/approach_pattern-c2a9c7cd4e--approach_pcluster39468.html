approach pattern

noun
flight path before landing
6.2
Pronunciation: / əˈproʊtʃ ˈpætərn / (AmE), / əˈprəʊtʃ ˈpætən / (BrE)
the prescribed flight path that an aircraft follows while preparing to land at an airport

Examples

The approach pattern to landing
Clear communication is essential during the approach pattern to ensure the safety of all landing aircraft.
Due to heavy traffic, several planes had to circle in the approach pattern for twenty minutes.
The pilot received instructions to enter the approach pattern before beginning the descent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: traffic pattern - the general path aircraft follow around an airport for landing and takeoff
