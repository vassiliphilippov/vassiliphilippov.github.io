texture

noun
digital image applied to 3D surfaces
4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈteks.tʃər/ /; / /ˈtek.ʃər/ (AmE), /ˈteks.tʃə/ /; / /ˈtek.ʃə/ (BrE)
(in computer graphics) a digital image applied to the surface of a 2D or 3D model to give it color, detail, or surface appearance; often used as a 'texture map'.

Examples

3D texture
The game uses a high-resolution texture to render the rocky cliff.
She created several custom textures to give the character's clothing more realistic detail.
When the artist forgot to include a normal map, the texture looked flat on curved surfaces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: texture map - an image file used to add color or detail to the surface of a 3D model
  • synonym: texture mapping - the process of applying a texture (image) to a 3D surface in computer graphics
