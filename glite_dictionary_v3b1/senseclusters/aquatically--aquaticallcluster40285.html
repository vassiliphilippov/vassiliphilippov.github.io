aquatically

adverb
in a watery manner
Pronunciation: / əˈkwætɪkli / (AmE), / əˈkwatɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to water or takes place in water

Examples

To swim aquatically
The otter swims aquatically to catch fish in the river.
Some insects can move aquatically across the surface of ponds.
The plant grows aquatically, with its roots submerged in water.

Related Terms

  • antonym: terrestrially - in a way that relates to land
