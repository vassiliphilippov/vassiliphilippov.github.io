flush

verb
make level or even with another surface
9.9
Pronunciation: / flʌʃ / (AmE), / flʌʃ / (BrE)
to make (a surface or object) level or even with another; to align so that surfaces are on the same plane

Examples

To flush the tiles
She took extra time to flush the tiles so the floor looked even.
The carpenter made sure to flush the new shelf with the wall.
The mechanic carefully flushed the metal components to create a seamless joint.
