cupric sulfate

noun
a blue copper compound used in chemistry
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːprɪk ˈsʌlfeɪt / (AmE), / ˈkjuːprɪk ˈsʌlfeɪt / (BrE)
a blue crystalline chemical compound, copper(II) sulfate, used as a fungicide, algicide, and in agriculture, chemistry, industry, and chemical analysis
