enforcer

noun
person carrying out tough tasks for a superior
5.7 5.8
Pronunciation: /en.ˈfɔːr.sər/ /; / /ɪn.ˈfɔːr.sər/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈfɔː.sə/ /; / /en.ˈfɔː.sə/ (BrE)
