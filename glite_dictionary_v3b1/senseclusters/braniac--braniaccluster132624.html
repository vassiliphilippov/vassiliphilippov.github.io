braniac

noun
very intelligent person
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪniˌæk / (AmE), / ˈbreɪniˌæk / (BrE)
a very intelligent person, especially someone who excels in academic subjects

Examples

The braniac student
Everyone in the class turned to the braniac for help with the difficult math problem.
She was known as the braniac of her family, always reading and learning new things.
The quiz team relied on their resident braniac to answer the toughest questions.
