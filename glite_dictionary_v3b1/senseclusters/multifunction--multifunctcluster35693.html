multifunction

adjective
having many uses
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈfʌŋkʃən / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈfʌŋkʃən / (BrE)
having several different functions or able to perform multiple tasks

Examples

Multifunction printer
The new printer is a multifunction device that can print, scan, and copy documents.
She bought a multifunction kitchen appliance to save space and money.
Many modern tools are designed to be multifunction for greater convenience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: single-purpose - designed for only one function or use
  • synonym: versatile - able to adapt or be used for many different purposes
