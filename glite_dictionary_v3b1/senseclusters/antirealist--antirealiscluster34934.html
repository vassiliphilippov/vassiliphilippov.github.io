antirealist

noun
denier of objective reality
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈriːəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈrɪəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes that reality does not exist independently of perception, theory, or conceptual schemes

Examples

A famous antirealist thinker
The philosopher was known as an outspoken antirealist in debates about the nature of truth.
Some scientists argue from an antirealist perspective, claiming that theories shape what we consider real.
Her antirealist views led her to question the existence of an objective world outside our minds.

Related Terms

  • antonym: realist - a person who believes that reality exists independently of perception or theory
  • synonym: skeptic - a person who doubts or questions accepted beliefs
