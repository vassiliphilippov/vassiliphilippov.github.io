balsamic

adjective
relating to or containing balsam
Pronunciation: / bɔlˈsæmɪk / (AmE), / bɔːlˈsæmɪk / (BrE)
of, relating to, or containing balsam or having properties resembling balsam, especially in fragrance or soothing quality

Examples

Balsamic ointment
Her grandmother's balsamic ointment helped relieve minor skin irritations.
The forest air had a balsamic scent after the rain, reminiscent of fresh resin.
The room was filled with a balsamic aroma that was both sweet and soothing.
