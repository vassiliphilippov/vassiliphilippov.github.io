tackiness

noun
stickiness; adhesive quality
Pronunciation: / ˈtæk.i.nəs / (AmE), / ˈtæk.i.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being sticky or adhesive to the touch

Examples

Tackiness of glue
High humidity increased the tackiness of the adhesive labels.
She tested the paint for tackiness before adding a second coat.
The tackiness of the glue made it difficult to separate the two sheets of paper.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stickiness - the quality of being sticky
