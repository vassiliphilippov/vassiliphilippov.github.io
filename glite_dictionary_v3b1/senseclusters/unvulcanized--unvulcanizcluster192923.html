unvulcanized

adjective
not subjected to vulcanization (rubber)
9.9 8.2
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈvʌl.kə.naɪzd/ /; / /ən.ˈvʌl.kə.naɪzd/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈvʌl.kə.naɪzd/ /; / /ən.ˈvʌl.kə.naɪzd/ (BrE)
(of rubber) not having been subjected to the process of vulcanization.

Examples

Unvulcanized rubber
Disposal of unvulcanized rubber must follow special guidelines due to its different chemical properties.

The manufacturer requires only unvulcanized rubber for this specific bonding process.
Unvulcanised rubber is sticky and less durable than its treated counterpart.
Natural rubber is often transported in its unvulcanised form for processing.
The factory stores both unvulcanised and vulcanised rubber for different manufacturing needs.
