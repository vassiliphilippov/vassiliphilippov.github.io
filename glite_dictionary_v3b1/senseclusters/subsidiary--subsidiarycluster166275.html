subsidiary

noun
company controlled by another
5.1
Pronunciation: / səbˈsɪdiˌɛri /; / səbˈsɪd.iˌɛri / (AmE), / səbˈsɪdiːəri /; / səbˈsɪd.i.əri / (BrE)
a company or thing that is controlled by another, especially a company owned by a larger company

Examples

A subsidiary company
Each subsidiary has its own management team and business operations.
Our company became a wholly owned subsidiary after the takeover.
The multinational firm owns several subsidiaries around the world.
Many multinational corporations own several subsidiaries around the world.
She was a trusted subsidiary to the director throughout the project.
The regional office acts as a subsidiary to the main headquarters, following instructions from the top management.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assistant - a person who helps or aids another
  • antonym: parent company - a company that controls one or more subsidiaries
  • antonym: superior - a person higher in rank or authority
