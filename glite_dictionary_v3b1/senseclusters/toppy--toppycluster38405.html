toppy

adjective
unstable, top-heavy
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑpi / (AmE), / ˈtɒpi / (BrE)
likely to fall or tip over because the top is heavy or unbalanced

Examples

Toppy ladder
The ladder was so toppy that I was afraid to climb it.
Be careful stacking those books, or the pile will get toppy and fall over.
The sculpture looked toppy and seemed like it might tip at any moment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stable - firmly fixed or not likely to fall or change position
  • synonym: top-heavy - having a heavier or larger upper part, making it unstable
  • synonym: unstable - not firm or fixed; likely to fall or change position
