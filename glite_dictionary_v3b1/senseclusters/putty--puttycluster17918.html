putty

verb
apply putty to fill or seal
7.4 5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌti / (AmE), / ˈpʌti / (BrE)
to apply putty in order to fix, fill, or seal a joint or crack, especially in window frames or similar structures

Examples

To putty a crack
After scraping away the old sealant, she carefully puttied the new window pane.
He spent the afternoon puttying the cracks in the wooden frame before painting.
The handyman promised to putty the gaps around the glass for better insulation.
