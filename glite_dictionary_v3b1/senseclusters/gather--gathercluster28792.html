gather

noun
an act of collecting or drawing together
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæðɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡæðə / (BrE)
a small fold or pucker in cloth made by pulling the material together, used in sewing

Examples

A gather in a skirt
A delicate gather along the hem gave the skirt a soft look.
She admired the neat gather at the waist of the dress.
The seamstress adjusted each gather to ensure the fabric hung perfectly.
She stitched a gather along the edge of the fabric for ruffles.
The curtain's gathers gave it a soft and elegant appearance.
The dress had delicate gathers around the waist to add shape.

  • synonym: gathering - the act of coming together; an assembly or meeting
