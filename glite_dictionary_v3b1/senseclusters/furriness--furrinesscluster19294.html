furriness

noun
blurry quality
5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈfɜː.rɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈfɜː.rɪ.nəs/ (BrE)
a lack of sharpness or clarity in an image or sound, resulting in a fuzzy or blurred quality

Examples

Furriness in photos
The furriness of the old photograph made it hard to recognize the people in it.
Audio engineers try to reduce furriness in recordings to make voices sound clearer.
After adjusting the camera settings, the furriness in the video was greatly reduced.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fuzziness - the quality of being blurred or indistinct
  • antonym: sharpness - the quality of being clear and well-defined
