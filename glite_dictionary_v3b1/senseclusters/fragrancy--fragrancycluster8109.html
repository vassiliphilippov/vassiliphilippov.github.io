fragrancy

noun
pleasant smell; sweetness of scent
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪɡrənsi / (AmE), / ˈfreɪɡrənsi / (BrE)
the quality of having a pleasant, sweet smell; the state of being fragrant

Examples

Sweet fragrancy
She chose the candle for its gentle fragrancy, hoping it would freshen the room.
The fragrancy of the blooming roses filled the entire garden with delight.
The chef admired the fragrancy of freshly ground herbs in his kitchen.

  • antonym: odor - a distinctive smell, often unpleasant
