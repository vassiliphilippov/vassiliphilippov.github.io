snottily

adverb
in a nasal manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɑːtɪli / (AmE), / ˈsnɒtɪli / (BrE)
in a way that shows or involves mucus from the nose, often in a messy or nasal manner.

Examples

Blow nose snottily
The child blew his nose snottily after catching a cold at school.
She spoke snottily because her nose was blocked from allergies.
After running outside in winter, he sniffed snottily and wiped his face with his sleeve.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nasally - in a way that relates to the nose or sounds nasal
