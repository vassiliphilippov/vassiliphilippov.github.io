peripheral device

noun

An electronic device that is external to the main computer and connects to it to provide extra functions, such as input, output, or storage.

Examples

Adding a new peripheral device like a scanner can improve your computer’s functionality.
Each peripheral device must be properly connected and configured to work with your system.
The printer is a peripheral device that allows you to produce hard copies of documents.
