rockish

adjective
like rock music
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːkɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈrɒkɪʃ / (BrE)
having the style, sound, or qualities of rock music; somewhat like rock in musical character

Examples

Rockish guitar sound
Their latest single has a rockish feel, blending electric guitars with catchy melodies.
The festival featured several rockish bands that drew large crowds of music fans.
Although the song is mostly pop, it has a rockish edge in the chorus.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pop - a style of popular music different from rock
  • synonym: rock-inspired - influenced by or resembling rock music
