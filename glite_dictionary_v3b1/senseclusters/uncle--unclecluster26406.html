uncle

noun
male relative; parent's brother or aunt's husband
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʌŋ.kəl / (AmE), / ˈʌŋ.kəl / (BrE)
The brother of one's father or mother, or the husband of one's aunt.

Examples

My uncle visits
At the family reunion, we met all our uncles and aunts.
My uncle lives in Canada and visits us every summer.
She called her uncle for advice when she started her new job.
