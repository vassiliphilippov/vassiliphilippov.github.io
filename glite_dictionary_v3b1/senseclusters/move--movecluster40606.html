move

verb
to sell or get rid of goods
6.3 8.90/m
Pronunciation: / muÀêv / (AmE), / muÀêv / (BrE)
to sell or dispose of goods, especially by encouraging their sale or clearance

Examples

To move products
He struggled to move the unsold merchandise during the clearance sale.
The company is trying to move last season's stock before the new collection arrives.
They hoped to move all the used cars by the end of the month.
