Leptoptilus

noun
genus of large storks
Pronunciation: / lɛpˈtɒptɪləs /; / lɛpˈtɑptɪləs / (AmE), / lɛpˈtɪlɪəs /; / lɛpˈtɪləs / (BrE)
a genus of large storks, including adjutant birds and marabous, native to Africa and southern Asia
