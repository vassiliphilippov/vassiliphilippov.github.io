concelebration

noun
joint celebration
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːnsɛlɪˈbreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒnsɛlɪˈbreɪʃən / (BrE)
a joint celebration or ceremony carried out by two or more people or groups together, often in a non-religious or figurative sense

Examples

A concelebration event
The festival's opening was a concelebration involving musicians from around the world.
Their wedding became a concelebration of two families joining together in joy.
The community organized a concelebration to honor local heroes and volunteers.

  • synonym: collaboration - the act of working together with others
