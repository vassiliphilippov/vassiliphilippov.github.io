loosener

noun
tool or substance
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈluːsənər / (AmE), / ˈluːsənə / (BrE)
a tool or substance used to make something less tight or easier to move

Examples

Screw loosener
He sprayed a loosener on the stuck screw to help remove it.
The mechanic recommended using a loosener before trying to turn the rusty bolt.
A chemical loosener can make old paint easier to scrape off surfaces.

Related Terms
