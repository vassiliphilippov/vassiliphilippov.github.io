mole

noun
small burrowing mammal
4.8 1.0
Pronunciation: /moʊl/ (AmE), /məʊl/ (BrE)
a small, burrowing mammal with velvety fur, tiny eyes, and strong front feet adapted for digging

Examples

A mole tunnels
A mole dug a network of tunnels under the vegetable garden.
The soft fur of a mole helps it move easily through the soil.
We rarely see a mole above ground during the day.
