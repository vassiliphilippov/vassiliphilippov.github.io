Cicer

noun
genus of herbs including chickpea
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪsər / (AmE), / ˈsɪsə / (BrE)
A genus of Asiatic herbs in the pea family including the chickpea plant (Cicer arietinum).

Examples

Cicer arietinum plant
Many botanists study Cicer to understand genetic diversity in legumes.
The genus Cicer includes several species, with the chickpea being the most widely cultivated.
Wild species of Cicer are important for improving crop resistance to diseases.
