non-typical

adjective
not usual
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈtɪpɪkəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈtɪpɪkəl / (BrE)
not usual or not characteristic; different from what is typical or expected

Examples

A non-typical reaction
The scientist observed a non-typical reaction during the experiment.
Her response to the situation was non-typical compared to most people.
Doctors were puzzled by the patient's non-typical symptoms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atypical - not representative of a type, group, or class
  • antonym: typical - having the usual qualities of a particular type
