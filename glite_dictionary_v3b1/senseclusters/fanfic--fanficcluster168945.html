fanfic

noun
fan-created story
Pronunciation: / ˈfænˌfɪk / (AmE), / ˈfænˌfɪk / (BrE)
a story created by fans that uses characters or settings from existing books, movies, or other works

Examples

A fanfic story
She spent the weekend writing a new fanfic based on her favorite TV show.
Many readers enjoy sharing their fanfic stories online with other fans.
His first published work was actually a popular fanfic in the fantasy genre.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fan fiction - fictional stories written by fans using existing characters or worlds
  • antonym: original fiction - fictional stories with entirely new characters and settings
