peace officer

noun
law enforcement officer
3.5 0.27/m
Pronunciation: / pis Àà…ëf…ôs…ôr / (AmE), / piÀês Àà…íf…™s…ô / (BrE)

Examples

Peace officers enforce laws

Only a certified peace officer may issue a traffic citation in this state.
The sheriff is considered a peace officer under the law.
