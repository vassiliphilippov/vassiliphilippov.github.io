berth

noun
place for a ship to moor
Pronunciation: / bɜrθ / (AmE), / bɜːθ / (BrE)
a place in a harbour or dock where a ship can be moored or secured

Examples

Ship berth
Our ship finally found a berth alongside the main pier.
Reserve a berth in advance to ensure space for your boat.
The captain searched for an empty berth at the crowded marina.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mooring - the act or place of securing a ship or boat
