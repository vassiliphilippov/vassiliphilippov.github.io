biophysiological

adjective
biological and physiological
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪoʊˌfɪziəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌbaɪəʊˌfɪziəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)

Examples

Biophysiological responses
Researchers measured the biophysiological responses of participants during the experiment.
The doctor explained the biophysiological changes that occur under extreme stress.
Understanding biophysiological mechanisms is important for developing new medical treatments.
