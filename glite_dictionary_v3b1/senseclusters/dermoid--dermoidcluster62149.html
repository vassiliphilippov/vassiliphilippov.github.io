dermoid

adjective
skin-like (medical)
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɜrˌmɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɜːmɔɪd / (BrE)
relating to or resembling skin, especially used to describe certain cysts or tumors containing skin-like tissue

Examples

Dermoid cyst tissue
The doctor explained that a dermoid cyst can contain hair, skin, or even teeth.
A dermoid tumor is often found in the ovaries or near the eyes.
The pathologist identified the mass as a dermoid growth after examining the tissue.
