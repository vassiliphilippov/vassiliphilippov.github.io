conveyance

noun
transmission or communication of information
7.2
Pronunciation: / kənˈveɪəns / (AmE), / kənˈveɪəns / (BrE)
the act of transmitting or communicating information, ideas, or feelings from one person or place to another

Examples

Conveyance of ideas
Good teachers focus on the effective conveyance of knowledge to their students.
Her speech was remarkable for its clear conveyance of complex ideas.
The conveyance of accurate data is essential for scientific research.
