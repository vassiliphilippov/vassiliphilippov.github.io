Synonyms

  • transmission
    3.6 5.79/m
    transmission is the process of sending messages over channels, while conveyance is the general act of sharing ideas, feelings, or information.
  • imparting
    4.2 0.03/m
    imparting means teaching or giving knowledge to others, while conveyance means passing information or feelings more broadly between people.
  • communication
    2.3 17/m
    communication is the message or signal shared between people, while conveyance focuses on the act of transmitting ideas or feelings.
  • transmittal
    7.5 0.11/m
    transmittal is the formal sending of documents or files, while conveyance covers any act of passing information or feelings between people.
