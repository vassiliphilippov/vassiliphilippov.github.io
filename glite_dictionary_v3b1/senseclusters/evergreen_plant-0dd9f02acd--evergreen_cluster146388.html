evergreen plant

noun
plant with foliage year-round
5.6 2.1
Pronunciation: /ˈev.ər.griːn plænt/ (AmE), /ˈev.ə.griːn plɑːnt/ (BrE)
A plant whose leaves remain green and persist throughout the year, rather than falling off seasonally.

Examples

Many evergreen plants are valued for landscaping due to their persistent foliage.

The evergreen plant in the garden provided color even during the winter months.
