Iceni

proper noun
ancient British tribe
9.9
Pronunciation: / aɪˈsiːnaɪ / (AmE), / aɪˈsiːnaɪ / (BrE)
an ancient Celtic tribe that lived in eastern Britain during the Roman period, known for rebelling against Roman rule

Examples

The Iceni tribe
The Iceni are remembered for their fierce resistance against Roman occupation.
Boudica became the legendary leader of the Iceni during their revolt.
Archaeologists have discovered artifacts from the Iceni in eastern England.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Boudica - queen of the Iceni who led a revolt against the Romans
  • synonym: Britons - ancient Celtic peoples of Britain
