integumentary

adjective
relating to the skin or outer covering
5.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌtɛɡjəˈmɛntəri / (AmE), / ɪnˌtɛɡjʊˈmɛntri / (BrE)
relating to the skin or outer protective covering of a living organism; pertaining to the integument.

Examples

Integumentary system
Reptiles have specialized scales as part of their integumentary structures.
Some diseases can affect not only internal organs but also the integumentary system.
The integumentary system protects the body from external damage and water loss.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cutaneous - relating to or affecting the skin
