Synonyms

  • stand for
    2.8 10/m
    stand for means to publicly support or represent an idea in everyday speech, while advocate means to formally recommend a cause.
  • champion
    4.2 3/m
    champion means to actively defend and fight for a cause in public, while advocate means to publicly support or recommend it.
  • espouse
    7.8 2/m
    espouse means to adopt and support a belief, often in formal writing, while advocate means to publicly recommend a policy or action.
  • endorse
    4.2 9/m
    endorse means to give public or official approval, often for a person or product, while advocate means to publicly recommend a cause.
