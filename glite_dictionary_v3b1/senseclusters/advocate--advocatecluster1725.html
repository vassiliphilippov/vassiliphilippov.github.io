advocate

verb
recommend or support publicly
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈæd.vəˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈæd.və.keɪt / (BrE)
to publicly support or recommend a particular cause, policy, or action

Examples

To advocate change
Environmental groups strongly advocated reducing single-use plastics at the conference.
Many experts advocate taking regular exercise to maintain good health.
She continues to advocate for equal rights in the workplace.
Environmentalists advocate reducing plastic use to protect oceans.
She often advocates for better access to healthcare in rural areas.
The lawyer advocated on behalf of her client during the hearing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: oppose - to disapprove of and attempt to prevent something
  • synonym: recommend - to suggest that something is good or suitable
