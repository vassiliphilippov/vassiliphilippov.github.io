plotless

adjective
without a storyline
Pronunciation: / ˈplɑtləs / (AmE), / ˈplɒtləs / (BrE)
lacking a clear or structured storyline or sequence of events

Examples

A plotless story
The novel was criticized for being plotless and difficult to engage with.
Some experimental films are intentionally plotless to focus on mood and visuals.
Despite its talented cast, the play felt plotless and failed to hold the audience's attention.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coherent - having a clear and logical structure
  • synonym: disjointed - lacking a clear connection or order
