call to the bar

verb
admission as a barrister
7.1
Pronunciation: / kɔl tə ðə bɑr / (AmE), / kɔːl tə ðə bɑː / (BrE)
the formal process of being admitted as a barrister and authorized to practice law in a court

Examples

To call to the bar in London
After years of study, she was finally called to the bar in London.
He hopes to call to the bar after completing his legal training.
She was the first woman in her family to be called to the bar.
