knucklebones

noun
traditional children's game with jacks and a ball
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌkəlˌboʊnz / (AmE), / ˈnʌkəlˌbəʊnz / (BrE)
a traditional children's game in which small objects (originally animal knucklebones or jacks) are thrown and picked up in various groups between bounces of a small rubber ball

Examples

Playing knucklebones game
Children gathered on the playground to play knucklebones during recess.
My grandmother taught me how to play knucklebones when I was little.
The rules of knucklebones have remained almost unchanged for generations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jacks - a game played with small metal or plastic objects and a ball, similar to knucklebones
