whensoever

conjunction
at any time that
Pronunciation: / ˌwɛnsoʊˈɛvər / (AmE), / ˌwɛnsəʊˈɛvə / (BrE)
at any time that; whenever

Examples

Whensoever you arrive
You may visit the library whensoever you wish, day or night.
The king declared that help would be given whensoever his people called.
Return to the castle whensoever the moon rises, and the guards will let you in.
