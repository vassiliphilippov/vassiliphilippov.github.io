stew

noun
dish of meat or fish cooked with vegetables
1.0 5.98/m
Pronunciation: / stuÀê / (AmE), / stjuÀê / (BrE)
A dish consisting of meat or fish and vegetables cooked slowly in liquid.

Examples

A beef stew
On cold days, nothing is better than a hot stew.
She served a delicious beef stew for dinner.
The aroma of the bubbling stew filled the kitchen.

Synonyms

  • ragout
    7.9 0.19/m
    A ragout is a richly seasoned, more refined meat-and-vegetable dish often served in restaurants, while a stew is a simpler home-cooked slow dish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: casserole - a dish in which food is baked and served, often referring to a similar type of stewed or baked meal
  • degree lower: soup - a liquid food made by boiling meat, fish, or vegetables
