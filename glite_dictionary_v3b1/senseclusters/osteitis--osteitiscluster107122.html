osteitis

noun
inflammation of bone tissue
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːstiˈaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌɒstiːˈaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of bone tissue, usually caused by infection, injury, or degeneration

Examples

Chronic osteitis inflammation
Chronic osteitis can result from repeated trauma or untreated bacterial infections.
Symptoms of osteitis may include localized pain, swelling, and tenderness in the affected area.
The patient was diagnosed with osteitis following a severe bone infection.
