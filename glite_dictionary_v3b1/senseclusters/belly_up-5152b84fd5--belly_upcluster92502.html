Synonyms

  • go bankrupt
    3.5 3/m
    go bankrupt means becoming legally unable to pay debts, while belly up is a casual way to say the same business failure.
  • go under
    4.4 2/m
    go under describes a business sinking or failing financially, while belly up is a more informal, idiomatic way to say it.
  • fold
    5.3 4/m
    fold means a company stops operating because of money problems, while belly up is a colorful informal phrase meaning the same.
  • fail
    5.1 17/m
    fail can mean a business stops working in many ways, while belly up specifically means it closes or goes bankrupt.
  • founder
    6.8 0.2/m
    founder means a plan or project collapses completely, while belly up mainly describes a business going bankrupt or closing.
  • collapse
    3.9 30/m
    collapse means to fall apart suddenly in many contexts, while belly up is an informal phrase for business bankruptcy or failure.
