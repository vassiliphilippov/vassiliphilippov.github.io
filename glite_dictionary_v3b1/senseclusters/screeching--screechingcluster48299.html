screeching

noun
high-pitched shrill noise
Pronunciation: / ˈskritʃɪŋ /; / ˈskriːtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskriːtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
a loud, high-pitched, and piercing noise or cry

Examples

The screeching tires
He covered his ears to block out the screeching of the car tires on the wet road.
The jungle was filled with the screeching of exotic birds at dawn.
The sudden screeching of the alarm startled everyone in the building.
Sudden screeching from the playground made the teachers rush over.
The screeching of the brakes startled everyone in the street.
We were kept awake by the screeching of owls outside our window.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scream - a loud, piercing cry
  • synonym: screech - a loud, harsh, high-pitched sound
  • synonym: wail - a prolonged, high-pitched cry of pain, grief, or anger
  • antonym: whisper - a soft, quiet manner of speaking
