schoolbag

noun
bag for schoolbooks and supplies
Pronunciation: / ˈskuːlˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈskuːlˌbæɡ / (BrE)
a bag used for carrying school books and supplies.

Examples

A schoolbag with books
His new schoolbag has extra pockets for his art supplies and water bottle.
She packed her lunch and books into her schoolbag before leaving for class.
The teacher noticed a forgotten schoolbag lying under the desk after school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: backpack - a bag carried on the back, often used by students
  • synonym: satchel - a bag with a shoulder strap, used for carrying books
