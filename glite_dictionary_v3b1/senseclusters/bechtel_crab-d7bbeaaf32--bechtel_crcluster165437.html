Bechtel crab

noun
cultivated crabapple tree with double pink flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛk.təl kræb / (AmE), / ˈbɛk.təl kræb / (BrE)
A cultivated variety of crabapple tree derived from the Iowa crab, known for its large double pink blossoms.
