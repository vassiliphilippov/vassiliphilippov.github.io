greater butterfly orchid

noun
a south European orchid species
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪ.tɚ ˈbʌt̬.ɚ.flaɪ ˈɔːr.kɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪ.tə ˈbʌt.ə.flaɪ ˈɔː.kɪd / (BrE)
a south European orchid (Platanthera chlorantha) with dark green, relatively large flowers, less fragrant than those of the lesser butterfly orchid

Examples

The greater butterfly orchid flower
The greater butterfly orchid is native to calcareous grasslands across Europe.
Unlike its close relative, the greater butterfly orchid has larger but less scented flowers.
We spotted several greater butterfly orchids growing at the edge of the forest.
