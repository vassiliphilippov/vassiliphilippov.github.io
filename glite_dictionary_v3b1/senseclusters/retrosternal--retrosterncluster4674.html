retrosternal

adjective
behind the sternum
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛtroʊˈstɜrnəl / (AmE), / ˌrɛtrəʊˈstɜːnəl / (BrE)
located or occurring behind the breastbone (sternum)

Examples

Retrosternal burning pain
The patient complained of a retrosternal burning sensation after eating a large meal.
Doctors often check for retrosternal pain when diagnosing heart conditions.
A retrosternal mass was detected during the chest X-ray examination.
