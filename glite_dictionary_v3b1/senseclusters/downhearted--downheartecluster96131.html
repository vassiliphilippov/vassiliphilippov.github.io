downhearted

adjective
feeling sad or discouraged
Pronunciation: / ˌdaʊnˈhɑrtɪd / (AmE), / ˌdaʊnˈhɑːtɪd / (BrE)
feeling sad, discouraged, or lacking in hope; low in spirits

Examples

Feeling downhearted today
After losing the match, the whole team felt downhearted on the way home.
Despite feeling downhearted, he managed to put on a brave face for his family.
She tried to cheer up her downhearted friend after the disappointing news.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerful - noticeably happy and optimistic
  • synonym: despondent - in low spirits from loss of hope or courage
  • synonym: disheartened - having lost confidence or hope; discouraged
