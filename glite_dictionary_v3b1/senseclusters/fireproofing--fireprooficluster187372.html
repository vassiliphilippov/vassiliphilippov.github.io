fireproofing

noun
making fire-resistant
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərˌpruːfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌpruːfɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or material used to make something resistant to fire or prevent it from burning easily

Examples

Fireproofing material
The new regulations require fireproofing for all high-rise buildings.
They applied a special fireproofing spray to the wooden beams in the attic.
Proper fireproofing can greatly reduce the risk of damage during a fire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flameproofing - the process of making something resistant to flames
  • synonym: insulation - material used to prevent heat, sound, or electricity from escaping
