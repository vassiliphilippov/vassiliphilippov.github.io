picture

verb
to represent visually or in words
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪktʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃə / (BrE)
to show or represent someone or something in a picture or as if in a picture

Examples

To picture a person
She pictured her childhood home in the vivid painting.
The artist pictures a dreamlike landscape in his latest work.
The film pictures the struggles of immigrants in great detail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depict - to represent or show someone or something in a picture or story
  • synonym: portray - to represent someone or something in a work of art or literature
