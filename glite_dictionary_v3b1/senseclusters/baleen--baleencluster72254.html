baleen

noun
whale jaw material; used in corsets, fans
9.9 0.22/m
Pronunciation: / b…ôÀàlin / (AmE), / b…ôÀàliÀên / (BrE)
A tough, flexible, horny substance from the upper jaws of certain whales, formerly used in products such as corsets and fans.

Examples

Whale baleen plates
Blue whales use plates of baleen to filter food from the ocean.
In the past, baleen was prized for its strength and flexibility.
The corset was stiffened with strips of baleen to give it shape.

Synonyms

  • whalebone
    8.3 0.16/m
    whalebone is the historical name for the flexible whale jaw material used in corsets and umbrellas, while baleen is the zoological term.

Related Terms
