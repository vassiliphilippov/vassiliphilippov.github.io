woa

interjection
surprise exclamation
Pronunciation: / woʊ / (AmE), / wəʊ / (BrE)
an informal exclamation used to express surprise or to get someone's attention

Examples

Woa! Look at that
He shouted woa when he saw the huge cake on the table.
Woa, I did not expect you to arrive so early this morning.
The crowd gasped and someone yelled woa as the magician revealed his trick.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whoa - an exclamation used to express surprise or to command a stop
  • synonym: wow - an exclamation expressing astonishment or admiration
