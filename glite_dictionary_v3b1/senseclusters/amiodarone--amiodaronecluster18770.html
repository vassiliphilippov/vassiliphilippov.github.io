amiodarone

noun
antiarrhythmic heart drug
9.9 8.9
Pronunciation: /æ.mɪ.oʊ.ˈdɑː.r.oʊn/ /; / /ə.mɪ.ə.ˈdɑː.r.oʊn/ (AmE), /æ.mɪ.ə.ˈdɑː.rəʊn/ /; / /ə.mɪ.ə.ˈdɑː.rəʊn/ (BrE)
a powerful antiarrhythmic drug used to treat serious irregular heart rhythms, usually prescribed when other treatments have failed

Examples

Because amiodarone can have serious side effects, its use is closely monitored by specialists.
