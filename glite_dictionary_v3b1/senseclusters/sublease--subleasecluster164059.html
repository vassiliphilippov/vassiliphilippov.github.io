sublease

noun
a lease from a tenant to another
6.1 4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈsʌ.bliːs/ (AmE), /ˈsʌ.bliːs/ (BrE)
a lease agreement made by a tenant (lessee) who rents out all or part of the leased property to another person

Examples

A sublease agreement
He offered a sublease on his office space while he worked remotely abroad.
It's important to read the sublease carefully before moving into a sublet apartment.
The tenant signed a sublease with a friend to cover the last six months of her rental agreement.
