overwrought

adjective
extremely agitated or emotional
7.8 0.73/m
Pronunciation: / Àåo ä.v…öÀàr…ît / (AmE), / Àå…ô ä.v…ôÀàr…îÀêt / (BrE)
extremely nervous or agitated, especially as a result of emotion; in a highly excited or stressed state.

Examples

An overwrought person
After hearing the bad news, she became so overwrought that she could not speak.
His overwrought nerves made it impossible for him to relax before the interview.
The overwrought atmosphere in the courtroom was felt by everyone present.

Synonyms

  • overanxious
