YWCA

noun
women's community center
Pronunciation: / ˌwaɪ ˌdʌbəljuː siː ˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌwʌɪ ˌdʌbəljuː siː ˈeɪ / (BrE)
a local building or organization run by the Young Women's Christian Association, offering classes, accommodation, or community activities

Examples

A YWCA building
She left her keys at the YWCA after the evening fitness class and couldn't find them.
The local YWCA offers affordable accommodation for women traveling alone.
Many community events are held at the YWCA throughout the year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: YMCA - a community center run by the Young Men's Christian Association
  • synonym: community center - a place where people from a local area can meet for social or recreational activities
