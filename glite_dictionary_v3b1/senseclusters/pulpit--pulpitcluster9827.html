pulpit

noun
raised platform in a church for speaking
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌlpɪt / (AmE), / ˈpʊlpɪt / (BrE)
a raised platform in a church or other building from which the preacher or speaker addresses the congregation or audience

Examples

Church pulpit
She addressed the audience from the pulpit during the special service.
The minister stood at the pulpit and began his sermon.
Visitors admired the ornate carvings on the old stone pulpit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lectern - a stand for holding a book, typically for reading during a service
