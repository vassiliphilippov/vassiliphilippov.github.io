cash machine

noun
device for withdrawing cash with a card
Pronunciation: / ˈkæʃ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈkæʃ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine, typically outside a bank, that allows customers to withdraw cash by using a bank card and PIN

Examples

Use a cash machine
Always cover your PIN when using a cash machine in public.
He withdrew fifty pounds from the cash machine outside the supermarket.
The cash machine was out of order, so I had to go into the bank.
