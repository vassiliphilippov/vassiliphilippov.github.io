taunting

noun
mocking or provoking act
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɔːntɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of mocking, ridiculing, or provoking someone in a scornful or contemptuous manner

Examples

Loud taunting
Despite the taunting, she remained calm and composed throughout the event.
His taunting was meant to provoke a reaction from his classmates.
The constant taunting from the opposing team made it hard to concentrate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insult - a disrespectful or scornful remark or action
  • synonym: mockery - the act of mocking or making fun of someone or something
  • antonym: praise - the expression of approval or admiration
