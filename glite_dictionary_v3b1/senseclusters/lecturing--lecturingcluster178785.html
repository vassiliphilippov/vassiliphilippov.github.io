lecturing

noun
teaching by giving a lecture
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛktʃərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɛktʃərɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of teaching or explaining a subject by delivering a formal talk, especially to a class.

Examples

A lecturing session
His lecturing style is both engaging and informative.
She will be lecturing on ancient history throughout the semester.
The university hired him for his expertise in lecturing large groups.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lecture - a formal talk on a subject, given to an audience or class
