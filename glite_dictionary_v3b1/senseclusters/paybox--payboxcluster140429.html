paybox

noun
payment deposit box
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈpeɪˌbɒks / (BrE)
a secure box or device where payments, especially cash, are deposited

Examples

Bus paybox
Please place your bus fare in the paybox next to the driver.
The parking lot uses a paybox system for collecting fees after hours.
If the office is closed, you can leave your payment in the paybox by the door.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cash box - a container for holding cash, especially in public places
  • synonym: drop box - a box where items or payments can be left securely
