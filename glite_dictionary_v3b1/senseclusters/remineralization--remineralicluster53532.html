remineralization

noun
conversion to minerals
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌmɪnərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌmɪnərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process by which organic matter is broken down and converted back into inorganic minerals usable by organisms

Examples

Ocean remineralization
The remineralization of organic material in the ocean helps sustain marine life by recycling nutrients.
Soil health depends on the remineralization of plant and animal remains into nutrients for new growth.
Scientists study remineralization to understand how ecosystems maintain their nutrient balance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mineralization - the process of converting organic matter into inorganic minerals
