livestream

verb
to broadcast live online
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪvstriːm / (AmE), / ˈlaɪvstriːm / (BrE)
to broadcast an event or activity over the internet in real time as it happens

Examples

To livestream a concert
The band decided to livestream their concert so fans around the world could watch.
She will livestream her cooking class every Saturday afternoon.
Many conferences now livestream their sessions for remote participants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broadcast - to transmit a program or event to an audience
  • antonym: record - to capture audio or video for later playback
