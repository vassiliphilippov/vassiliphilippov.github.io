blindman's buff

noun
game where a blindfolded person tries to catch others
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈblaɪndˌmænz ˈbʌf / (AmE), / ˈblaɪndmənz ˈbʌf / (BrE)
a children's game in which one player, blindfolded, tries to catch and identify the other players

Examples

Play blindman's buff game
At the party, the children played blindman's buff in the garden until it started to rain.
Laughing and stumbling, Sara tried to catch her friends in a lively game of blindman's buff.
We all took turns being blindfolded while playing blindman's buff during recess.
