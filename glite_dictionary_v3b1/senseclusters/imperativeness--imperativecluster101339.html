imperativeness

noun
quality of being imperative or pressing
5.4
Pronunciation: / ɪmˌpɛrəˈtɪvnəs / (AmE), / ɪmˌpærəˈtɪv.nəs /; / ɪmˌpɛrəˈtɪvnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being urgent, commanding, or demanding immediate attention
