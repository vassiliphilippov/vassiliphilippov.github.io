cretin

noun
offensive term for an unintelligent person
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛt̬n̩ /; / ˈkrɛtɪn / (AmE), / ˈkriːtɪn / (BrE)
an offensive term for a person who is considered very stupid or foolish

Examples

A cretin insult
Don't be such a cretin; use your common sense for once.
He acted like a complete cretin during the meeting, ignoring everyone's suggestions.
She couldn't believe the cretin who parked in the disabled spot without a permit.
He called me a cretin just because I made a small mistake.
Only a cretin would try to fix the wiring without turning off the power.
Sometimes he acts like a complete cretin when he's tired.
