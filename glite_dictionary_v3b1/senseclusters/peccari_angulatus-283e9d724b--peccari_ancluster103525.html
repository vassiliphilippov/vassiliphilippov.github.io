Peccari angulatus

noun
a species of peccary, collared peccary
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛkəˌraɪ æŋˈɡjuːlətəs / (AmE), / ˌpɛkəˌraɪ æŋˈɡjuːlətəs / (BrE)
A species of peccary, dark grey with a faint white collar, found in semi-desert areas of Mexico and the southwestern United States; also known as the collared peccary.
