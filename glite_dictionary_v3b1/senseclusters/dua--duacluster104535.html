dua

noun
Islamic prayer
9.5
Pronunciation: / duːˈɑː / (AmE), / duːˈɑː / (BrE)
a prayer or supplication, especially in Islamic tradition, asking God for help or blessings

Examples

A dua for success
Before the exam, he whispered a heartfelt dua for success and confidence.
Many people make a dua after finishing their daily prayers.
She taught her children to say a dua for protection every morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prayer - a request or expression addressed to God or a deity
  • synonym: supplication - the act of asking or begging for something earnestly
