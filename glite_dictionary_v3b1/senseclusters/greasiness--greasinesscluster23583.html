greasiness

noun
quality of being greasy or oily
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriː.sinəs / (AmE), / ˈɡriː.si.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being covered with, or containing, a greasy or oily substance

Examples

The greasiness on hands
He disliked the greasiness left on his hands after fixing the car.
The greasiness of the pizza made it difficult to hold without a napkin.
You can remove greasiness from your hair by using a clarifying shampoo.
