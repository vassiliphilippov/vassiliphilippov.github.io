bolero

noun
Spanish dance in triple time
Pronunciation: / boʊˈlɛroʊ / (AmE), / bəˈlɛərəʊ / (BrE)
a lively Spanish dance in triple time, traditionally performed to guitar and castanets accompaniment

Examples

Spanish bolero dance
She learned the bolero while studying Spanish folk dances.
The dancers performed a traditional bolero in colorful costumes during the festival.
The graceful movements of the bolero captivated the entire audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flamenco - a Spanish art form involving singing, guitar playing, dance, and handclaps
