in the same breath

adverb
almost simultaneously; unexpectedly together
5.4 4.4
Pronunciation: /ɪn ðə seɪm brɛθ/ /; / /ɪn ðiː seɪm brɛθ/ (AmE), /ɪn ðə seɪm brɛθ/ /; / /ɪn ðiː seɪm brɛθ/ (BrE)
used to say that two things are said or done almost at the same time, often when this is surprising or contradictory
