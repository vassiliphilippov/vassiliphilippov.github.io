loma

noun
West African people or language
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊmə / (AmE), / ˈləʊmə / (BrE)
a member of a West African ethnic group mainly found in Liberia and Guinea, or the language spoken by this group

Examples

Loma people
The loma have a rich tradition of music and storytelling in their communities.
She is studying the loma language as part of her research on West African cultures.
Many loma live in rural areas of northern Liberia and southern Guinea.
