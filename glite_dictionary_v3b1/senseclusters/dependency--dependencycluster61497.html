dependency

noun
psychological or physical reliance on a substance
5.8 6.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɛndənsi / (AmE), / dɪˈpɛndənsi / (BrE)
A state of needing or being abnormally tolerant to a habit-forming substance, especially drugs or alcohol.

Examples

Drug dependency
Her struggle with opioid dependency affected every aspect of her life.
Overcoming a drug dependency often requires medical intervention and strong social support.
The clinic specializes in treating alcohol dependency with counseling and group therapy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: addiction - the condition of being addicted to a particular substance or activity
  • antonym: independence - the state of not depending on something, especially a substance or drug
