triennially

adverb
every three years
Pronunciation: / traɪˈɛniəli / (AmE), / traɪˈɛnɪəli / (BrE)
every three years or once in a period of three years

Examples

Held triennially
The art exhibition is organized triennially and attracts visitors from around the world.
Elections for the committee are held triennially to ensure fresh perspectives.
The report is published triennially to provide updated data on climate change.
