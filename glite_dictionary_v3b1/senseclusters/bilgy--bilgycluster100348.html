bilgy

adjective
smelling like bilge water
6.8 8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbɪl.dʒiː/ (AmE), /ˈbɪl.dʒiː/ (BrE)
Smelling unpleasant, like bilge water; having a foul, stagnant odor associated with the bottom of a ship.

Examples

A bilgy smell
The inside of the abandoned boat was bilgy and stank of decay.
The storage room had grown bilgy after years of neglect and leaking pipes.
They opened the hatch to a rush of bilgy air, making everyone cough.
