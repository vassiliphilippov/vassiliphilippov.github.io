Synonyms

  • crossbreed
    4.6 0.18/m
    crossbreed means making an animal or plant by mating different breeds or species, while hybridize is the general term for creating hybrids.
  • interbreed
    6.5 0.08/m
    interbreed refers to breeding between different varieties or species to mix genes, while hybridize focuses on producing hybrids.
  • cross
    3.9 1.37/m
    cross means to breed plants or animals from different varieties or species in a general way, while hybridize implies making hybrids intentionally.

Related Terms
