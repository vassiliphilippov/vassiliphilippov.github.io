incisura

noun
anatomical notch or small hollow
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɪʒərə / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɪʒjʊərə / (BrE)
(Anatomy) A notch or small hollow in the margin or edge of a bone or organ.

Examples

The incisura in bone
Surgeons must take care to avoid the mental incisura during mandibular procedures.
The cardiac incisura can be seen as a small notch at the junction of the esophagus and the stomach.
The superior temporal incisura is an important surgical landmark in brain anatomy.
