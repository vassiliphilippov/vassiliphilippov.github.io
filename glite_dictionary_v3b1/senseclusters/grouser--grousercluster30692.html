grouser

noun
habitual complainer
9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈɡraʊ.sər/ (AmE), /ˈɡraʊ.sə/ (BrE)
a person who complains or grumbles frequently, especially in an annoying or habitual way

Examples

A constant grouser
Everyone in the group knew him as the grouser who always found something to complain about.
Instead of helping solve the problem, she acted like a grouser and criticized every suggestion.
The team grew tired of the constant negativity from their resident grouser.

Related Terms

  • synonym: complainer - a person who expresses dissatisfaction or annoyance
  • synonym: grumbler - someone who complains in a low voice or mutters discontent
  • antonym: optimist - a person who tends to be hopeful and confident about the future
