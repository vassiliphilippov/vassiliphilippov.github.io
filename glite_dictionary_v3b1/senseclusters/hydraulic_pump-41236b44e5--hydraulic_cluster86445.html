hydraulic pump

noun
device for moving fluid to higher pressure/level
2.3 3.4
Pronunciation: /haɪ.ˈdrɔː.lɪk pʌmp/ /; / /haɪ.ˈdrɑː.lɪk pʌmp/ (AmE), /haɪ.ˈdrɒ.lɪk pʌmp/ (BrE)
a device that uses the energy of moving water or fluid to move a portion of that fluid to a higher elevation or pressure

Examples

A hydraulic pump device
A broken hydraulic pump can severely disrupt irrigation on farms.
The engineers installed a hydraulic pump to transport water up the hillside.
This model of hydraulic pump is used in many hydroelectric power stations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: water pump - a device for moving water, especially from one place to another
