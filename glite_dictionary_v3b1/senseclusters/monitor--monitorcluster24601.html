Synonyms

  • computer monitor
    2.7 1/m
    computer monitor shows a computer's visual output directly, while monitor can mean any device that displays images from various electronic sources.
  • visual display unit
    4.1 0.00/m
    visual display unit is a technical British term for a computer screen, while monitor is the common word for the same device.
  • computer display
    2.5 0.2/m
    computer display refers to the screen that shows a computer's output, while monitor may also mean the whole device with controls and electronics.
  • computer screen
    2.1 5/m
    computer screen is the flat surface where you see computer images, while monitor can mean the full unit around that screen.
  • video display
    3.4 0.2/m
    video display is any electronic screen or device that shows moving images, while monitor usually refers to a specific computer or control display.
  • screen
    1.0 90/m
    screen is a general surface or display for films, TVs, or computers, while monitor is usually a dedicated electronic device for computers.
