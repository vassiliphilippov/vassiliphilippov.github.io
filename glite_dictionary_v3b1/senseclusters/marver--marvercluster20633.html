marver

noun
glassblowing tool
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːrvər / (AmE), / ˈmɑːvə / (BrE)
a flat tool or surface used in glassblowing to shape and smooth hot glass

Examples

Glassblower's marver
The glassblower rolled the molten glass on the marver to achieve a smooth surface.
Before shaping the vase, she pressed the hot glass against the marver to cool it slightly.
A steel marver is commonly used in modern glass studios for its durability.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blowpipe - a long tube used to blow air into molten glass
  • synonym: punty - a metal rod used to hold and shape hot glass
