gladdon iris

noun
species of iris with purple flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæd.ən ˈaɪ.rɪs / (AmE), / ˈɡlæd.ən ˈaɪ.rɪs / (BrE)
A species of iris with purple flowers and foul-smelling leaves, native to southern and western Europe and North Africa.

The gladdon iris is often found growing wild along Mediterranean coastlines.
