villus

noun
minute projection on mucous membrane

A minute, finger-like projection from the surface of a mucous membrane, especially in the small intestine, that increases surface area for absorption.

Examples

Small villus in intestine
Damage to the villus can lead to malabsorption disorders.
Each villus in the small intestine helps to absorb nutrients from digested food.
The surface area of the intestine is greatly increased by the presence of villi.
