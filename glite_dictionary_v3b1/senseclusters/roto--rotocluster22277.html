roto

noun
newspaper photo section
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊtəʊ / (BrE)
the rotogravure section of a newspaper, especially the pages featuring photographs or special articles.

Examples

Sunday roto
He always checked the roto for the latest sports photographs on Sunday mornings.
The celebrity interview appeared in the weekend roto alongside colorful feature stories.
She clipped her favorite picture from the roto to add to her scrapbook.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rotogravure - a printing process used for high-quality images in newspapers and magazines
  • synonym: supplement - an additional section of a newspaper or magazine
