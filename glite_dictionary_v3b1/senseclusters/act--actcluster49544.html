act

noun
short performance in a show
2.9
Pronunciation: / ækt / (AmE), / ækt / (BrE)
a short performance by an entertainer or group of entertainers, especially as part of a longer show or program

Examples

Comedy act
Her comedy act had the whole audience laughing nonstop.
The magician ended his act with an impressive escape from a locked box.
They performed their dance act at the school talent show.

Related Terms

  • synonym: number - a single item or performance within a show
  • synonym: routine - a sequence of actions regularly followed or a set performance
