reestimate

noun
revised estimate
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈɛstəˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈɛstɪmeɪt / (BrE)
a new or revised estimate made after considering additional information or changes

Examples

A project reestimate
After receiving updated costs, the team prepared a reestimate for the project budget.
The architect's reestimate reflected the changes requested by the client.
A reestimate was necessary when the timeline for completion was extended.
