eejit

interjection
annoyed exclamation
Pronunciation: / ˈiːdʒɪt / (AmE), / ˈiːdʒɪt / (BrE)
an exclamation used to call someone foolish or express annoyance at their actions

Examples

Eejit! Watch out
When he forgot his keys for the third time, she shouted, 'eejit!'
You absolute eejit, you left the door wide open all night.
He tripped over his own feet and his friend called him an eejit in jest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: idiot - a person who acts foolishly
