suppliantly

adverb
humbly or pleadingly
Pronunciation: / səˈplaɪəntli / (AmE), / səˈplʌɪəntli / (BrE)
in a humble or pleading manner, as if asking for something earnestly

Examples

To ask suppliantly
She asked suppliantly for forgiveness, her voice trembling with emotion.
The child looked suppliantly at his mother, hoping for another piece of cake.
He waited suppliantly at the door, hoping to be invited inside.

Related Terms

  • antonym: arrogantly - in a proud or self-important way
  • synonym: humbly - in a modest or respectful way
  • synonym: pleadingly - in a way that shows you are asking for something urgently
