sinfully

adverb
excessively or guiltily
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪn.fəl.ɪ/ /; / /ˈsɪn.fəl.iː/ (AmE), /ˈsɪn.fəl.ɪ/ /; / /ˈsɪn.fəl.iː/ (BrE)
