cattle

noun
interchangeable, replaceable servers
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkæt.əl/ (AmE), /ˈkæt.əl/ (BrE)
(in computing, especially cloud and DevOps) servers or virtual machines treated as interchangeable, disposable units that are replaced automatically rather than individually maintained.

Examples

Cattle servers
In our cloud setup, broken machines are treated like cattle and replaced instead of repaired.
The DevOps team prefers immutable infrastructure where instances are disposable cattle, not unique pets.
When an instance fails, automated systems simply terminate the cattle and spin up replacements.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pet (computing) - a machine treated as unique and maintained individually; contrasted with 'cattle' in cloud operations
  • synonym: server - a computer or virtual machine that provides data, resources, or services to other computers on a network
