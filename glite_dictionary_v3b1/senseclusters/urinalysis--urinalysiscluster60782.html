urinalysis

noun
analysis of urine for diagnosis
6.0
Pronunciation: / jʊˌrɪˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / jʊəˌrɪˈnaləsɪs / (BrE)
The chemical analysis of urine, typically for medical diagnosis.

Examples

Urinalysis test
After his urinalysis, the patient received a detailed report on kidney function.
Routine urinalysis can help detect early stages of certain diseases.
The doctor ordered a urinalysis to check for signs of infection.
