Chinatown

proper noun
Chinese district
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪnəˌtaʊn / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪnəˌtaʊn / (BrE)
a district in a city where many Chinese people live and Chinese businesses, restaurants, and shops are found

Examples

Visit Chinatown for food
We visited Chinatown to try some authentic dim sum and explore the local shops.
The annual parade in Chinatown attracts thousands of visitors every year.
Many tourists enjoy the vibrant atmosphere and unique culture of Chinatown.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Japantown - a district in a city where many Japanese people live and have businesses
  • synonym: Little Italy - a district in a city where many Italian people live and have businesses
