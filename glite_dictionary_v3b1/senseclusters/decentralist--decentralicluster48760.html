decentralist

adjective
supporting decentralization
Pronunciation: / diːˈsɛntrəlɪst / (AmE), / diːˈsɛntrəlɪst / (BrE)
favoring or relating to the transfer of authority from a central body to local or regional units

Examples

Decentralist policy
The new policy was praised by decentralist leaders who wanted more local control.
A decentralist approach can help communities address their unique needs more effectively.
Many political parties have both centralist and decentralist factions within them.

Related Terms

  • antonym: centralist - favoring concentration of power in a central authority
  • synonym: federalist - supporting a system where power is shared between central and regional authorities
