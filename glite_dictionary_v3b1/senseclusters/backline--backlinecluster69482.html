backline

noun
defensive players group
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈbækˌlaɪn / (BrE)
the group of players positioned behind the main line, usually responsible for defense in a team sport

Examples

Soccer backline
The coach praised the backline for their strong performance throughout the match.
In rugby, the backline must react quickly to stop fast attacks from the opposition.
A solid backline can make a big difference in close games.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defense - the part of a team responsible for preventing the opponent from scoring
  • antonym: forward - a player positioned near the opponent's goal, mainly responsible for attacking
