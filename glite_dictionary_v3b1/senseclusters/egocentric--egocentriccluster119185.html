egocentric

adjective
self-centered; concerned only with oneself
2.7 5.9
Pronunciation: /iː.ɡoʊ.ˈsen.trɪk/ /; / /iː.ɡəʊ.ˈsen.trɪk/ (AmE), /iː.ɡəʊ.ˈsen.trɪk/ /; / /iː.ɡoʊ.ˈsen.trɪk/ (BrE)
Thinking only of oneself, without regard for the feelings or desires of others; self-centered.

Examples

Egocentric attitude
Her egocentric behavior made it difficult for her to empathize with her friends.
In a team environment, being egocentric often leads to poor collaboration and conflict.
The novel's main character is so egocentric that he rarely considers anyone else's needs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: altruistic - showing a disinterested and selfless concern for the well-being of others; unselfish
  • synonym: selfish - lacking consideration for others; concerned chiefly with one's own personal profit or pleasure
