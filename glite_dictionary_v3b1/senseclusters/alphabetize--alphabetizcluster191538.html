alphabetize

verb
arrange in alphabetical order
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈæl.fə.bəˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈæl.fə.bɪ.taɪz /; / ælˈfəbɪtaɪz / (BrE)
to arrange words or items in the order of the letters of the alphabet

Examples

To alphabetize a list
Please alphabetize the names before submitting the roster.
The librarian had to alphabetize hundreds of books on the new shelf.
We decided to alphabetize our DVDs so we could find movies more easily.
I spent an hour alphabetising my collection of books.
Please alphabetise the files before you leave for the day.
The names were alphabetised for easier reference in the directory.
