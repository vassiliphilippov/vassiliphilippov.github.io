hostelling

noun
staying in hostels
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑstəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɒstəlɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or practice of staying in hostels while traveling, especially as a budget-friendly way to see different places

Examples

Hostelling in Europe
During her gap year, she spent months hostelling through Southeast Asia with friends.
Many students choose hostelling as an affordable way to see new countries.
He met people from all over the world while hostelling across Europe.
Many young travelers enjoy hostelling across Europe to save money and meet new people.
The rise of hostelling has made it easier for students to explore different countries on a budget.
She learned about different cultures while hostelling with friends during her summer break.
