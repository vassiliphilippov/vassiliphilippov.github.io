bacteriolytic

adjective
destroying bacteria by lysis
Pronunciation: / bækˌtɪəriəˈlɪtɪk / (AmE), / bækˌtɪəriəˈlɪtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or causing the destruction of bacteria by lysis.

Examples

A bacteriolytic reaction
A strong bacteriolytic reaction was observed in the treated samples.
Researchers studied the bacteriolytic activity of several new antibiotics against resistant strains.
The enzyme lysozyme exhibits a bacteriolytic effect on certain bacterial cell walls.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bacteriostatic - Inhibiting the growth of bacteria without destroying them
