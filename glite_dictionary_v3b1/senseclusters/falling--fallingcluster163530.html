falling

noun
act of dropping
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːlɪŋ / (BrE)
an act or instance of dropping or coming down from a higher place or position

Examples

The falling of snow
The falling of the snow created a peaceful scene in the village.
Repeated falling can be dangerous for elderly people living alone.
The falling of the curtain signaled the end of the performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: descent - an act of moving downward
  • antonym: rise - an act of moving upward
