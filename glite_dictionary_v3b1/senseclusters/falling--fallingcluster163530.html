Synonyms

  • fall
    1.0 9.42/m
    fall is the common word for a single movement down from high places, while falling names the action or process of coming down.
  • drop
    1.0 2.81/m
    drop is a sudden downward movement often quick and sharp, while falling can mean any coming down from a higher place.
  • tumble
    2.9 2.78/m
    tumble is a rolling or turning fall that looks uncontrolled, while falling covers any drop from a higher position.
  • spill
    1.7 0.54/m
    spill is an informal quick fall, often from standing or while moving, while falling is the neutral name for coming down.
  • descent
    3.7 8.23/m
    descent is a controlled or gradual movement downward or a slope, whereas falling is a sudden act of coming down.
