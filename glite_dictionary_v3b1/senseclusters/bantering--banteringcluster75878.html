bantering

adjective
playful and teasing in tone
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbæn.tɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbæn.tər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
playful and clever in tone, marked by light teasing or joking

Examples

Bantering tone
He replied in a bantering tone, making everyone in the room laugh.
The host's bantering comments put the nervous guests at ease.
Their conversation was light and bantering, filled with gentle teasing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: facetious - treating serious issues with deliberately inappropriate humor; flippant
  • antonym: serious - not joking or playful; solemn or earnest in tone
