Eelam

noun
proposed Tamil state in Sri Lanka
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈiːlɑːm / (AmE), / ˈiːlɑːm / (BrE)
A proposed independent state in Sri Lanka, chiefly sought by Tamil separatist groups, especially the Tamil Tigers.

Examples

Independent Eelam state
Many refugees still dream of returning to an independent Eelam.
The Tamil Tigers fought for the creation of Eelam in Sri Lanka.
The conflict over Eelam shaped Sri Lankan politics for decades.
