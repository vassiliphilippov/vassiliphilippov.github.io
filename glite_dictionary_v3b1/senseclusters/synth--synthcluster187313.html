synth

verb
to create electronic sounds
6.9
Pronunciation: / sɪnθ / (AmE), / sɪnθ / (BrE)
to create or modify a sound electronically using a synthesizer

Examples

To synth a beat
She loves to synth new sounds for her electronic music tracks.
The band asked their keyboardist to synth a bassline for the next song.
He spent hours trying to synth the perfect drum effect for the intro.

Related Terms

  • antonym: record - to capture and store sound or music
  • synonym: synthesize - to produce sound electronically, especially with a synthesizer
