device

noun
artistic method or technique
2.9
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
a method or technique used in a work of art to produce a particular effect or achieve a specific purpose

Examples

Storytelling device
Flashback is a common device employed in storytelling to provide background information.
Repetition is often used as a poetic device to create rhythm and emphasis.
The author uses irony as a device to highlight the characters' misunderstandings.

  • synonym: technique - a way of carrying out a particular task, especially in art or literature
