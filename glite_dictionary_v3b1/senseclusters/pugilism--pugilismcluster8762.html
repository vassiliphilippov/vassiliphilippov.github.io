pugilism

noun
the sport or practice of boxing
Pronunciation: / ˈpjudʒəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈpjuːdʒɪlɪzəm / (BrE)
the practice or sport of fighting with the fists, especially as a form of boxing

Examples

Pugilism sport
He became fascinated by the history and techniques of pugilism.
In the 18th century, pugilism was considered both brutal and honorable.
The golden age of pugilism attracted large crowds to prizefighting events.
