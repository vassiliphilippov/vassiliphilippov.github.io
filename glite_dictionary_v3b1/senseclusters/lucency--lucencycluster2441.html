lucency

noun
clarity of expression
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈluːsənsi / (AmE), / ˈluːsənsi / (BrE)
the quality of being clear and easy to understand in thought or expression

Examples

Lucency of speech
The lucency of his explanation made the complex topic much easier to grasp.
Her essay was praised for its lucency and logical structure.
Teachers value lucency in students' written work to ensure ideas are communicated clearly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clarity - the quality of being clear and easy to understand
  • antonym: obscurity - the state of being difficult to understand
