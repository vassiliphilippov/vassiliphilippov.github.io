first gentleman

noun
husband of a female chief executive
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝst ˈdʒɛntlmən / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ˈdʒɛntl̩mən / (BrE)
the husband of a female chief executive, such as a president or governor, especially when acting as the official host of state functions

Examples

The first gentleman role
After the election, her husband became the country's first gentleman, a role he embraced with enthusiasm.
As first gentleman, he launched several initiatives to support local charities.
The first gentleman attended many state events throughout the governor's term.
