high

noun
a peak level or point
4.1
Pronunciation: / haɪ / (AmE), / haɪ / (BrE)
a point or level that is higher than all others, especially in terms of amount, degree, or achievement

Examples

Reach a career high
His career hit a high when he won the top prize.
Last night the stock market reached a new high for the year.
Temperatures will remain in the highs throughout the week.

Related Terms

  • antonym: low - a point or level that is lower than all others; an opposite of 'high' in this context
  • synonym: peak - the highest point or level of something
