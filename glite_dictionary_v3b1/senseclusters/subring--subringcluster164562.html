subring

noun
subset ring structure
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌrɪŋ / (BrE)
a subset of a mathematical ring that is itself a ring under the same operations

Examples

A subring of integers
The set of even integers is a classic example of a subring of the integers.
If a subring contains the multiplicative identity, it is called a unital subring.
Students learned how to determine whether a subset is a subring in their algebra class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ideal - a special subset of a ring with additional properties
  • synonym: ring - a set with two operations, addition and multiplication, following certain rules
