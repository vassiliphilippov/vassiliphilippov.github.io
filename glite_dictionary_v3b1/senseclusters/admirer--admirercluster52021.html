admirer

noun
someone who admires another
1.0
Pronunciation: / ədˈmaɪɚ / (AmE), / ədˈmaɪərə / (BrE)
a person who admires someone, often showing respect, affection, or romantic interest

Examples

A secret admirer
He received a letter from an anonymous admirer who praised his recent performance.
She blushed when she realized her secret admirer had sent her flowers.
The actress has many devoted admirers around the world.
He became her most devoted admirer after seeing her perform on stage.
Many admirers gathered outside the theater hoping to catch a glimpse of the actress.
She received flowers from an anonymous admirer every Valentine’s Day.

Related Terms

  • antonym: critic - a person who judges or finds fault; someone who is often negative
  • synonym: fan - a person who has a strong interest in or admiration for someone or something
