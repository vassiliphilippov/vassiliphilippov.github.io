Synonyms

  • refrain
    4.2 4/m
    refrain is a quiet self control choice to not act in a moment, while abstain suggests a firmer decision often for health or principle.
  • eschew
    8.9 1/m
    eschew is a formal long term avoidance for moral reasons, while abstain is choosing not to do something in a particular situation.
  • avoid
    1.0 90/m
