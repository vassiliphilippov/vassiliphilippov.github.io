woozily

adverb
dizzily or unsteadily
Pronunciation: / ˈwuːzɪli / (AmE), / ˈwuːzɪli / (BrE)
in a way that feels dizzy, unsteady, or confused

Examples

Walk woozily after ride
After the long roller coaster ride, she walked woozily toward the exit.
He answered the phone woozily, still half asleep from his nap.
The patient sat up woozily after the operation, needing help to stand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dizzily - in a way that feels dizzy or lightheaded
  • antonym: steadily - in a way that is firm, stable, or not shaky
