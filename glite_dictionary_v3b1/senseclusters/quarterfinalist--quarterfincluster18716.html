quarterfinalist

noun
competitor in quarterfinals
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˌkwɔrtərˈfaɪnəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌkwɔːtəˈfaɪnəlɪst / (BrE)
a person or team that has reached the quarterfinal round in a competition

Examples

A quarterfinalist team
After winning three matches, the team became a quarterfinalist in the tournament.
Each quarterfinalist received a medal for reaching this stage of the competition.
He was thrilled to be named a quarterfinalist in the international chess championship.
