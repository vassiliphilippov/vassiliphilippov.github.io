bargewoman

noun
woman operating a barge
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrdʒˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈbɑːdʒˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who operates or works on a barge, usually transporting goods by water.

Examples

A bargewoman on river
As a bargewoman, she spent most of her days traveling along Europe's rivers.
The bargewoman expertly guided the vessel through the narrow canal locks.
The history museum featured the stories of many bargewomen from the early 20th century.
