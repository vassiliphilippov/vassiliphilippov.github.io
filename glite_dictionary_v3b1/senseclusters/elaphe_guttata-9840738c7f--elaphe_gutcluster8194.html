Elaphe guttata

noun
large harmless U.S. snake (corn snake)
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlæfi ɡʌˈtɑtə / (AmE), / ɪˈlæfi ɡʌˈtɑːtə / (BrE)
A large harmless snake of the southeastern United States, commonly found on farms; also known as the corn snake.
