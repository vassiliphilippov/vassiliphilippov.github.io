essence

noun
lightweight hydrating skincare product
Pronunciation: / ˈɛsəns / (AmE), / ˈɛsəns / (BrE)
a lightweight, usually water‑based skincare product containing active ingredients, applied after cleansing and toning to hydrate and prepare the skin for serums and moisturizers.

Examples

Skincare essence
Her evening routine includes a lightweight essence applied after toner and before serum.
The dermatologist recommended a hydrating essence to restore skin barrier after acne treatments.
Travel-sized essence bottles are handy for keeping skin hydrated during long flights and layovers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: serum - a concentrated skincare product applied to the skin to deliver active ingredients; often used after an essence or toner.
