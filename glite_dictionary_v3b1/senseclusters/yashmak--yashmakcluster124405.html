yashmak

noun
Muslim women's face veil
Pronunciation: / jæʃˈmɑk / (AmE), / jæʃˈmæk / (BrE)
A veil worn by some Muslim women to cover the face, leaving only the eyes visible, especially in Turkey.

Examples

A yashmak veil
Photographs from the era show women draped in dark robes, each with a yashmak concealing her face.
She wore a traditional yashmak during religious ceremonies.
The travelogue described women in Istanbul wearing the yashmak in the early twentieth century.
