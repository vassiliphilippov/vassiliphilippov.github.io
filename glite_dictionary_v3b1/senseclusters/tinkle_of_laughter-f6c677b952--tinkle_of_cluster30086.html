tinkle of laughter

noun
light, cheerful sound of laughter
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪŋ.kəl əv ˈlæf.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɪŋ.kəl əv ˈlɑːf.tə / (BrE)
a light, high, and cheerful sound of laughter, often resembling the gentle ringing of a bell

Examples

A soft tinkle of laughter
As the children played, a tinkle of laughter drifted through the open window.
He remembered the tinkle of laughter that always followed her clever jokes.
Her tinkle of laughter brightened up the otherwise quiet room.
