flashlight

noun
portable battery-powered electric lamp
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈflæʃˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈflaʃlʌɪt / (BrE)
A small portable electric lamp powered by batteries, typically used to provide light in the dark.

Examples

A flashlight in hand
During the power outage, she used a flashlight to find her way around the house.
He shone his flashlight into the dark cave, looking for any signs of movement.
I always keep a flashlight in my car in case of emergencies.
