underthink

verb
to analyze too simply
8.2
Pronunciation: /ən.dər.ˈθɪŋk/ /; / /ʌn.dər.ˈθɪŋk/ (AmE), /ən.də.ˈθɪŋk/ /; / /ʌn.də.ˈθɪŋk/ (BrE)
to not think enough about something or to consider it too simply, missing important details

Examples

To underthink a problem
If you underthink the problem, you might miss important factors that affect the outcome.
