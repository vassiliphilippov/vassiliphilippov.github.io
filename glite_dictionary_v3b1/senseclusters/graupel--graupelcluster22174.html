graupel

noun
soft snow pellets
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraʊpəl / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊpəl / (BrE)
small, soft pellets of snow formed when supercooled water droplets freeze onto falling snowflakes

Examples

Soft graupel pellets
After the storm, the ground was covered with graupel instead of regular snow.
The weather forecast predicted graupel in the afternoon as temperatures dropped.
Children were surprised to see graupel falling, as it looked different from normal snow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hail - small balls of ice that fall from the sky during storms
  • synonym: sleet - a mixture of rain and snow or partially melted snow
  • degree lower: snowflake - a single crystal of snow
