hatchback

noun
a car with a rear door that opens upwards
4.4 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈhætʃ.bæk/ (AmE), /ˈhætʃ.bæk/ (BrE)
a car with a door across the full width at the back that opens upward to provide access to the cargo area

Examples

The new model is available as a hatchback or a sedan.
They bought a small hatchback for commuting around the city.
Several compact cars are available as hatchbacks for added versatility.
She bought a new hatchback to make grocery shopping easier.
The hatchback design provides more storage space than a sedan.
