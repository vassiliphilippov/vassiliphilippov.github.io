photographically

adverb
using photographic means
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtəˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəˈɡræfɪkli / (BrE)
by means of photography; using or relating to the process of taking or producing photographs

Examples

To photographically record
The evidence was photographically documented for use in the investigation.
The landscapes were photographically captured during the expedition.
The museum's artifacts were photographically cataloged for record-keeping.
