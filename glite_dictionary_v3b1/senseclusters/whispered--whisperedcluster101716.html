whispered

adjective
spoken very softly; uttered in a whisper
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪspərd / (AmE), / ˈwɪspəd / (BrE)
spoken or produced in a quiet, soft voice without vocal cord vibration; uttered in a whisper.

Examples

A whispered warning
A whispered warning reached him just in time to avoid trouble.
She spoke in a whispered tone so as not to disturb the sleeping child.
The two friends exchanged whispered words during the long lecture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: murmured - spoken very softly or quietly, often in a low tone
