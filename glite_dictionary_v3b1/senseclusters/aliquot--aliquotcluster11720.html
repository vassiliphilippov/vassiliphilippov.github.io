aliquot

adjective
an exact divisor or factor
9.5 8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈæl.ə.kwət/ /; / /ˈæl.ɪ.kwɑːt/ (AmE), /ˈæl.ɪ.kwɒt/ /; / /ˈæl.ə.kwɒt/ (BrE)
(of a part or portion) containing an exact number of times within a larger whole; an exact divisor or factor of a quantity.

Examples

Aliquot part of 20
By definition, 5 is an aliquot part of 20, because 20 divided by 5 is 4.
In mathematics, an aliquot divisor of 12 includes 1, 2, 3, 4, and 6.
Only the aliquot portions of the reagent should be used to ensure precise measurement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: divisor - a number by which another number is to be divided
  • synonym: factor - a number that divides another number exactly
