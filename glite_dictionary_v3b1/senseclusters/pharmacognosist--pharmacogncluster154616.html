pharmacognosist

noun
expert in medicinal plants
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfɑːrməˈkɑːɡnəsɪst / (AmE), / ˌfɑːməkɒɡˈnəʊsɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies medicinal drugs obtained from plants and other natural sources

Examples

The pharmacognosist studies plants
The pharmacognosist traveled to remote forests to discover new medicinal plants.
A skilled pharmacognosist can identify useful compounds in everyday herbs.
Universities often employ a pharmacognosist to teach about natural drug sources.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pharmacognosy - the study of medicinal drugs from natural sources
  • synonym: pharmacologist - a person who studies the effects of drugs on living organisms
