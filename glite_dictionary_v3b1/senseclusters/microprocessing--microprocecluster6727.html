microprocessing

noun
processing on small scale
6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.kroʊ.prɑː.sɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.krəʊ.prɒ.sɪŋ/ (BrE)
the processing or manipulation of materials, samples, or signals on a microscopic or very small scale, especially in science or industry

Examples

Microprocessing of samples
Advances in microprocessing have enabled the creation of smaller and more efficient electronic devices.
The laboratory specializes in microprocessing techniques for developing new materials at the nanoscale.
Precise microprocessing is essential for manufacturing high-performance microchips.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: processing - the act of treating or preparing something by a series of actions
