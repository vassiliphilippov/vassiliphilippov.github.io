Micmac language

noun
Algonquian language spoken by the Mi'kmaq
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪkˌmæk ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmɪkˌmak ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Algonquian language traditionally spoken by the Mi'kmaq people of northeastern Canada and parts of the northeastern United States
