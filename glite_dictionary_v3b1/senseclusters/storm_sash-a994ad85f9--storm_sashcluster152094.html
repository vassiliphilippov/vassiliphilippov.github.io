storm sash

noun
outer window for weather protection
7.1
Pronunciation: / stɔrm sæʃ / (AmE), / stɔːm sæʃ / (BrE)
an extra window installed outside an ordinary window to protect against severe weather or cold

Examples

The storm sashes
Every autumn, we install the storm sashes to prepare for harsh weather.
The carpenter recommended adding a storm sash for extra insulation.
The old farmhouse had a storm sash to keep out the icy winter winds.
