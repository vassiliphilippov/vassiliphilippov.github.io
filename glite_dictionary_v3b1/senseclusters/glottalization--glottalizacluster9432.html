glottalization

noun
creaky voice quality
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɡlɑːt̬ələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɡlɒtələˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the production of speech sounds by using the vocal cords in a special way, such as tightly constricting or briefly closing them
