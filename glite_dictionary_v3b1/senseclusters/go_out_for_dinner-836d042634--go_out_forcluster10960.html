go out for dinner

verb
to eat dinner at a restaurant
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ aʊt fɔr ˈdɪnər / (AmE), / ɡəʊ aʊt fə ˈdɪnə / (BrE)
to leave one's home to eat dinner at a restaurant or similar establishment; to dine out
