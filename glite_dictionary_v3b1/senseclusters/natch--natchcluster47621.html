natch

adverb
informal 'naturally'
9.9
Pronunciation: / nætʃ / (AmE), / nætʃ / (BrE)
used informally to mean 'naturally' or 'of course', expressing that something is obvious or expected

Examples

He forgot, natch
He forgot his keys again, natch, because he was in a hurry.
She aced the test, natch, since she studied all week.
They brought snacks to the movie night, natch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: naturally - as expected; of course
  • synonym: obviously - in a way that is easy to see or understand
