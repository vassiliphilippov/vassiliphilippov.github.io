hoagie

noun
large sandwich on a long roll
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊ.ɡi / (AmE), / ˈhəʊ.ɡi / (BrE)
A large sandwich made of a long crusty roll split lengthwise and filled with meats, cheese, and various toppings; known by different names in different regions of the US.

Examples

An Italian hoagie
At the picnic, there was a giant Italian hoagie for everyone to share.
I've never tried a Philly-style hoagie before, but it looks delicious.
She ordered a turkey hoagie with extra lettuce and tomatoes for lunch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grinder - a large sandwich made on a long roll, especially in New England
  • synonym: hero - a large sandwich on a long Italian roll
  • synonym: submarine - a large sandwich on a long roll, filled with meats, cheese, and more
