perspicuous

adjective
clearly expressed; easy to understand
7.9 0.12/m
Pronunciation: / p…ôrÀàsp…™kju…ôs / (AmE), / p…ôÀàsp…™kjuÀê…ôs / (BrE)
(of language or speech) clearly expressed and easily understood; transparent in meaning.

Examples

A perspicuous summary
A perspicuous summary is essential in scientific writing to ensure clarity for readers.
Her perspicuous analysis made the complex issue much easier to comprehend.
The instructions were written in a perspicuous style that left no room for confusion.

Synonyms

  • lucid
    4.5 1.07/m
    lucid is clear and easy to follow in speech or writing, while perspicuous sounds more formal and literary.
  • pellucid
    8.6 0.03/m
    pellucid is very clear and transparent in style, while perspicuous is similar but used more in formal description.
  • limpid
    6.4 0.04/m
    limpid is smoothly clear and easy to picture in writing, while perspicuous feels more strictly logical and formal.
  • crystal clear
    2.5 1.75/m
    crystal clear is an informal phrase that shows something obvious immediately, while perspicuous sounds formal and learned.
  • transparent
    3.5 3.09/m
    transparent is used for actions or processes that are easy to see through, while perspicuous focuses on clear language.
