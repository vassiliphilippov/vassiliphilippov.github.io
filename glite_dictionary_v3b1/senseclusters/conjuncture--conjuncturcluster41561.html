conjuncture

noun
critical combination of circumstances
Pronunciation: / kənˈdʒʌŋktʃər / (AmE), / kənˈdʒʌŋktʃə / (BrE)
a combination of events or circumstances, especially one that is critical or significant

Examples

A critical conjuncture
At such a delicate conjuncture, any mistake could have serious consequences.
The current political conjuncture has created opportunities for significant reform.
The economist analyzed the conjuncture of global markets and its impact on trade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crisis - a time of intense difficulty or danger
