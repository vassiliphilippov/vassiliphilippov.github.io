umbel

noun
flower cluster with stalks from a common point
Pronunciation: / ˈʌmbəl / (AmE), / ˈʌmbəl / (BrE)
a cluster of flowers with stalks of almost equal length that spring from a common center, forming a flat or rounded shape, as seen in the carrot family

Examples

A flat umbel
Bees are often attracted to the flat-topped umbel of the cow parsley.
Many plants in the parsley family are recognized by their umbel inflorescences.
The wild carrot produces a distinctive umbel of small white flowers.
