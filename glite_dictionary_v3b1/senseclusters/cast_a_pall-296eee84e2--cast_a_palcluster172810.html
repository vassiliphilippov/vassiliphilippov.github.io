cast a pall

verb
create a gloomy atmosphere
9.9 8.2
Pronunciation: / kæst ə pɔl / (AmE), / kɑːst ə pɔːl / (BrE)

Examples

To cast a pall on a meeting

The failure of the project cast a pall over the company's annual celebration.
The tragic news cast a pall over the entire community.
