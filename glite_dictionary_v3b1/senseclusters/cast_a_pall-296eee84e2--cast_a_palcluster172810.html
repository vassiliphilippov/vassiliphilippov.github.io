cast a pall

verb
create a gloomy atmosphere
9.9
Pronunciation: / kæst ə pɔl / (AmE), / kɑːst ə pɔːl / (BrE)
to cause a gloomy or depressing atmosphere to spread over a situation or event

Examples

To cast a pall on a meeting
Her absence cast a pall on the usually cheerful meeting.
The failure of the project cast a pall over the company's annual celebration.
The tragic news cast a pall over the entire community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cast a shadow - to make something less positive or cause unhappiness
