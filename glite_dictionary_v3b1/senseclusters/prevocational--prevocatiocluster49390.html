prevocational

adjective
preparing for work
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːvoʊˈkeɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌpriːvəʊˈkeɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to training or education that prepares someone for work or vocational training

Examples

Prevocational courses
The school offers prevocational courses to help students develop job skills.
Many teenagers attend prevocational programs before choosing a specific career path.
A prevocational class can introduce students to basic workplace expectations and skills.

Related Terms

  • antonym: academic - relating to education focused on general knowledge rather than job skills
  • degree higher: vocational - relating to education or training for a specific job or career
