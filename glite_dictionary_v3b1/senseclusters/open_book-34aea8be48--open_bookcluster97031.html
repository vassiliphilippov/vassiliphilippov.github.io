open book

noun
a person easy to understand
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊ.pən bʊk / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.pən bʊk / (BrE)
a person whose feelings, thoughts, or motives are easy to understand; someone who is transparent and does not hide anything

Examples

An open book person
Despite his fame, the athlete was an open book about his personal struggles.
Maria is an open book; you always know exactly how she feels.
When it comes to sharing her opinions, she's truly an open book.

Related Terms

  • antonym: closed book - a person or subject that is difficult or impossible to understand
