unsoftened

adjective
not treated for hardness
6.5
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈsɑːf.ən/ /; / /ʌn.ˈsɔːf.ən/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈsɒf.ənd/ /; / /ən.ˈsɒf.ənd/ (BrE)
not treated to remove minerals such as calcium and magnesium, especially in water; still containing hardness minerals.

Examples

Unsoftened water
The pipes became clogged with scale from the unsoftened water.
Using unsoftened water in the washing machine can cause mineral buildup over time.
The laboratory required unsoftened water for certain chemical experiments.
