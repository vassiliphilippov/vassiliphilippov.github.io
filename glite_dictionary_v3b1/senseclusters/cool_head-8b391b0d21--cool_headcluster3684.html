cool head

noun
calmness under pressure
1.2
Pronunciation: / kul hɛd / (AmE), / kuːl hɛd / (BrE)
the ability to remain calm and not become agitated or upset in difficult or stressful situations

Examples

A cool head in crisis
A cool head is essential when negotiating high-stakes deals.
Even in the midst of chaos, she managed to keep a cool head.
He lost his cool head and shouted during the crisis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: composure - the state of being calm and in control; synonym of 'cool head'
  • antonym: panic - a sudden strong feeling of fear that prevents reasonable thought and action; antonym of 'cool head'
