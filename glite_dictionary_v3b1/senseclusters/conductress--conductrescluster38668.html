conductress

noun
female conductor
Pronunciation: / kənˈdʌktrəs / (AmE), / kənˈdʌktrɪs / (BrE)
a woman who conducts, especially a female conductor on a bus, train, or orchestra

Examples

A conductress on bus
In the early 1900s, it was common to see a conductress managing the bus route.
She was the first conductress to lead the city's philharmonic orchestra.
The conductress collected tickets from the passengers as the tram moved through the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conductor - a person who directs or is in charge, especially of a vehicle or orchestra
