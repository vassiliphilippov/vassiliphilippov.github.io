unrated

adjective
without a rating
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈreɪtɪd / (BrE)
not having been given a rating, score, or official classification

Examples

An unrated movie
The movie was released in an unrated version with additional scenes.
Some online games remain unrated because they have not been reviewed by the board.
He submitted an unrated essay since the teacher had not yet graded it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rated - having been given a rating or classification
  • synonym: unclassified - not assigned to a category or class
