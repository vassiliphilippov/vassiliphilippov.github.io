tough guy

noun
aggressive or bullying man
1.0
Pronunciation: / tʌf ɡaɪ / (AmE), / tʌf ɡaɪ / (BrE)
a man who acts or appears strong, aggressive, or intimidating; someone who tries to look tough or scare others

Examples

A tough guy act
Despite acting like a tough guy, he was actually quite insecure inside.
Everyone knew him as the tough guy who always started fights at school.
The movie cast him as a tough guy with a dark past.
Despite his reputation as a tough guy, he is gentle with animals.
He tried to act like a tough guy in front of his friends.
The movie's main character is a classic tough guy who never backs down from a fight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bully - a person who habitually seeks to harm or intimidate those whom they perceive as vulnerable
  • antonym: coward - a person who lacks courage to do or endure dangerous or unpleasant things
  • antonym: softie - a man who is emotionally sensitive or lacks toughness
