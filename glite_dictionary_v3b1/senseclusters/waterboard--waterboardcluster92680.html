waterboard

noun
restraint board for torture
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtərˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a board or platform used to restrain a person during the act of waterboarding, where water is poured over the face to simulate drowning

Examples

A waterboard device
The prisoner was strapped to a waterboard during the controversial interrogation procedure.
Human rights organizations have condemned the use of the waterboard in detention centers.
The old waterboard was displayed in the museum as a reminder of past interrogation methods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: restraint - a device used to hold someone in place
  • synonym: torture device - an object used to inflict pain or suffering as punishment or interrogation
