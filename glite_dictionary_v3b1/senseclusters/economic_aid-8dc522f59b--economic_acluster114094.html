economic aid

noun
financial or material support for economic improvement
Pronunciation: / ɪˈkɑːnəmɪk eɪd / (AmE), / iːˈkɒnəmɪk eɪd / (BrE)
money or other material support given to help a country, organization, or individual improve their economic situation

Examples

International economic aid
International economic aid has helped rebuild infrastructure in the region after the disaster.
Many developing countries rely on economic aid from wealthier nations to support public services.
The government pledged more economic aid to support small businesses during the recession.

Related Terms

  • synonym: development aid - assistance given to support economic development in countries
  • synonym: financial aid - money given to help fund education or other financial need
