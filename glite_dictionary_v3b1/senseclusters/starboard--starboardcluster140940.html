starboard

noun
right side of a ship or aircraft
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːrˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈstɑːbəd / (BrE)
the right side of a ship or aircraft when facing forward toward the bow or nose

Examples

The starboard side
Please keep clear of the starboard side during docking procedures.
The captain ordered the crew to shift the cargo to starboard to balance the ship.
When the whale appeared off the starboard bow, everyone rushed to the rail.
