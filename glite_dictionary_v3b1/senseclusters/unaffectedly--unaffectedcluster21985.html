unaffectedly

adverb
calmly, without reaction
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈfɛktɪdli / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈfɛktɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that shows no visible emotional reaction or disturbance; calmly and without being influenced.

Examples

React unaffectedly
He accepted the criticism unaffectedly, without showing any sign of disappointment.
Despite the chaos around her, she continued her work unaffectedly and stayed focused.
The child listened unaffectedly to the story, not reacting to the scary parts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calmly - in a peaceful and relaxed way
  • antonym: emotionally - in a way that shows strong feelings
