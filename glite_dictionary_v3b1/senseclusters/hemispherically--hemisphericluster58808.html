hemispherically

adverb
by hemisphere
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛmɪˈsfɛrɪkli / (AmE), / ˌhɛmɪˈsfɛrɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or affects one half of the Earth, especially a hemisphere such as the northern or southern half.

Examples

Hemispherically different climates
Climate patterns can differ hemispherically due to the tilt of the Earth's axis.
The disease spread hemispherically, affecting only the southern half of the planet.
Bird migration often occurs hemispherically, with different species moving between the north and south.

Related Terms

  • antonym: globally - in a way that relates to the whole world
  • synonym: regionally - in a way that relates to a specific region
