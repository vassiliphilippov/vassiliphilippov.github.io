flatline

noun
period of no activity
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈflætˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈflætˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a continuous straight line on a monitor indicating no activity, especially no heartbeat, or a period of no change or progress.

Examples

A heart flatline
The patient's monitor showed a sudden flatline, causing the doctors to rush into action.
After months of steady sales, the company experienced a worrying flatline in revenue.
The conversation hit a flatline and everyone sat in silence for several minutes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: growth - an increase or development in activity or progress
  • synonym: stagnation - a state of not moving or changing
  • synonym: standstill - a situation in which all activity or movement stops
