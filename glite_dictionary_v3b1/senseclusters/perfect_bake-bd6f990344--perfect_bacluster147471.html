perfect bake

noun
ideal result of baking
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜːr.fɪkt beɪk / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.fɪkt beɪk / (BrE)
A baked good that has been cooked under ideal conditions, resulting in the best possible texture, taste, and appearance.

Examples

A perfect bake result
Achieving a perfect bake requires practice and careful attention to detail.
The judges praised her cake for its perfect bake and flawless crumb.
With the right oven temperature, you can achieve a perfect bake every time.
