woodgrain

noun
pattern or texture of wood fibers
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊdˌɡreɪn / (AmE), / ˈwʊdˌɡreɪn / (BrE)
the pattern or texture visible on the surface of wood, created by the direction and arrangement of its fibers

Examples

Woodgrain pattern
He chose a laminate floor with a realistic woodgrain pattern.
The artist enhanced the woodgrain of the doors to highlight the natural beauty of the oak.
The table's woodgrain gives it a unique and elegant appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grain - the arrangement of fibers in a material
