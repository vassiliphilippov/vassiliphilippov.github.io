Cliftonia

noun
genus of evergreen shrubs or trees in SE US
Pronunciation: / klɪfˈtoʊniə / (AmE), / klɪfˈtəʊniə /; / klɪfˈtəʊnɪə / (BrE)
a genus of evergreen shrubs or small trees (titi) found in wetlands of the southeastern United States
