agency

noun
business or organization providing a service
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.dʒən.si / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.dʒən.si / (BrE)
a business, organization, or government unit that provides a service or acts for others

Examples

Travel agency
A real estate agency can help you buy or sell a house.
She contacted an employment agency to help her find a job.
The travel agency arranged their entire itinerary for the holiday.
After college, she applied for a job at a government agency focused on public health.
Each federal agency submits its own annual budget proposal to Congress.
The Environmental Protection Agency announced new regulations on air quality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: company - an organization that sells goods or services in order to make money
  • synonym: department - a division of a larger organization such as a government, university, business, or shop
  • synonym: firm - a business organization, especially one involving a partnership of two or more people
