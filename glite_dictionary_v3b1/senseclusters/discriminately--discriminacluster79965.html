discriminately

adverb
with unfair bias
7.8
Pronunciation: / dɪˈskrɪmɪnətli / (AmE), / dɪˈskrɪmɪnətli / (BrE)
in a way that treats people or groups differently, often unfairly or with prejudice.

Examples

Discriminately treated workers
The company was accused of hiring discriminately based on applicants' backgrounds.
Laws are in place to prevent schools from treating students discriminately.
It is illegal to act discriminately against someone because of their age or gender.

Related Terms

  • antonym: impartially - in a way that is fair and without bias
  • synonym: unfairly - in a way that is not just or equal
