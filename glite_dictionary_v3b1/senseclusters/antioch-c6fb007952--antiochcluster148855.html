Antioch

proper noun
ancient city in southern Turkey, historic Christian center
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈæntiˌɑk / (AmE), / ˈæntiɒk / (BrE)
An ancient city in what is now southern Turkey, formerly a major commercial center and capital of Syria, and an early center of Christianity.

Examples

Antioch ancient city
Antioch played a key role in the spread of early Christianity throughout the Roman Empire.
Archaeologists have discovered impressive Roman mosaics in the ruins of ancient Antioch.
The Apostle Paul began several missionary journeys from Antioch.
