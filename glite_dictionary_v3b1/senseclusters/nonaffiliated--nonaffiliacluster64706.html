nonaffiliated

adjective
not connected to group
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnəˈfɪlieɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌnɒnəˈfɪlieɪtɪd / (BrE)
not officially connected or associated with any group, organization, or party

Examples

Nonaffiliated member
He remained nonaffiliated throughout his career, never joining any professional association.
Many nonaffiliated voters feel that no political party represents their views.
The club welcomes both members and nonaffiliated guests to its events.

Related Terms

  • antonym: affiliated - officially connected or associated with a group or organization
  • synonym: independent - not belonging to or controlled by a group or organization
