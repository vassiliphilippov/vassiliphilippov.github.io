Arabis turrita

noun
European cress with stiff erect stems
Pronunciation: / əˈræbɪs tərˈaɪtə / (AmE), / əˈræbɪs tʌˈrɪtə / (BrE)
A European cress with stiff, upright stems, sometimes classified in the genus Turritis.

Examples

Arabis turrita plant
Botanists recorded several specimens of Arabis turrita along the mountain trail.
Some texts classify Arabis turrita under the genus Turritis instead of Arabis.
The wildflowers included Arabis turrita, which stood out for its tall, rigid stalks.
