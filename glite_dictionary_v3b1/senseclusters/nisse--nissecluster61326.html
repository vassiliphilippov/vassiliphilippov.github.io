nisse

noun
Scandinavian household elf
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪsə / (AmE), / ˈnɪsə / (BrE)
a household spirit or elf from Scandinavian folklore, often believed to protect families and associated with Christmas

Examples

A helpful nisse
In many Scandinavian homes, a nisse is believed to bring good luck during the winter holidays.
Children left a bowl of porridge for the nisse on Christmas Eve to keep him happy.
The old stories say a mischievous nisse might play tricks if not treated kindly.
