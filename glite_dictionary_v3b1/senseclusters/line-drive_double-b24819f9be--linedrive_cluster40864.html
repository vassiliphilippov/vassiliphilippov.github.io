line-drive double

noun
double in baseball from a line drive
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪn ˌdraɪv ˈdʌbəl / (AmE), / ˈlaɪn ˌdraɪv ˈdʌbəl / (BrE)
A double scored in baseball when the batter hits a line drive that results in safely reaching second base.

Examples

A line-drive double
His line-drive double in the seventh inning brought in two more runs.
The batter hit a line-drive double into the gap between the outfielders.
With two outs, she smashed a line-drive double down the left-field line.

Related Terms

  • synonym: double - a hit in baseball allowing the batter to reach second base
  • synonym: line drive - a sharply hit baseball that travels straight and low
