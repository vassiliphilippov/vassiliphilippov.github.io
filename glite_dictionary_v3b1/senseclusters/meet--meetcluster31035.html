meet

verb
to be introduced or become acquainted
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /miːt/ (AmE), /miːt/ (BrE)
to come together with someone, either for the first time, by arrangement, or socially

Examples

To meet a friend
It was a pleasure to meet you at the conference last week.
She can't wait to meet her favorite author during the book signing event.
They first met while traveling abroad and quickly became friends.
Hundreds of fans met outside the stadium to welcome the team.
Let's meet for coffee tomorrow afternoon.
We usually meet once a month to discuss the project.
Let's meet at the café after work to discuss the project.
The committee will meet next Tuesday to make a decision.
We often meet friends for brunch on Sundays.

Related Terms

  • antonym: avoid - to keep away from or not meet
  • synonym: encounter - to meet unexpectedly or face to face
  • synonym: gather - to come together as a group, often for a specific purpose
  • antonym: separate - to move apart or stop meeting
