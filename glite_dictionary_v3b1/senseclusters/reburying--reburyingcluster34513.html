reburying

noun
the act of burying again
Pronunciation: / riˈbɛriɪŋ / (AmE), / riˈbɛriɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of burying something or someone again, especially after being exhumed or uncovered

Examples

The reburying of coffins
After the flood, the community took part in the reburying of the exposed coffins.
Authorities approved the reburying of the archaeological findings after documentation.
The reburying of ancient remains must be handled with great respect and ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burial - the act of placing a dead body in the ground
  • antonym: exhumation - the act of digging out something, especially a body, from the ground
