mingler

noun
sociable person
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪŋɡlər / (AmE), / ˈmɪŋɡlə / (BrE)
a person who enjoys or is skilled at moving among people and socializing at events or gatherings

Examples

Party mingler
At the party, she was a natural mingler, chatting with almost everyone in the room.
Being a good mingler can help you make valuable connections at business events.
He is not much of a mingler and prefers quiet evenings at home.

Related Terms

  • antonym: introvert - a person who prefers solitary activities and avoids large social gatherings
  • synonym: socializer - a person who enjoys meeting and talking to others
