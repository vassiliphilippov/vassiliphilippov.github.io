piñon

noun
pine tree or seed
Pronunciation: / piːˈnjoʊn / (AmE), / piːˈnjɒn / (BrE)
a type of pine tree native to the southwestern United States, or its edible seed

Examples

Piñon pine tree
The piñon forests provide important habitat for many animals in the Southwest.
She roasted piñon seeds to add a nutty flavor to her salad.
During our trip, we learned how to identify a piñon tree by its short needles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pine - a type of evergreen tree with needle-like leaves
  • synonym: pine nut - the edible seed of certain pine trees
