mean deviation

noun
statistical measure of average absolute deviation
7.1 0.02/m

The arithmetic mean of the absolute differences between each value in a set and the mean of that set; a measure of statistical dispersion.

Examples

The mean deviation value
In basic statistics, students often learn to compute the mean deviation before tackling more complex measures like standard deviation.
The mean deviation provides a simple way to measure variation without considering the direction of the differences.
To assess the consistency of the data, calculate the mean deviation for the given set of numbers.

Synonyms

  • average deviation
