goniometric

adjective
angle measurement
Pronunciation: / ˌɡoʊniəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌɡəʊniəˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving the measurement of angles

Examples

Goniometric measurement
The surgeon relied on goniometric data to assess the patient's joint movement.
A goniometric analysis was performed to determine the exact angle of the slope.
Physical therapists often use goniometric tools to track progress in rehabilitation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angular - relating to angles or having angles
