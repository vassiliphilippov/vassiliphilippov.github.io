Milesian philosophy

noun
ancient Ionian school of philosophy
8.8 9.4
Pronunciation: /maɪ.ˈliː.zɪ.ən fə.ˈlɑː.sə.fiː/ /; / /maɪ.ˈliː.ʒən fə.ˈlɑː.sə.fiː/ (AmE), /maɪ.ˈliː.ʒən fɪ.ˈlɒs.ə.fiː/ /; / /maɪ.ˈliː.zɪ.ən fɪ.ˈlɒs.ə.fiː/ (BrE)
the school of philosophy founded in ancient Ionia, focusing on explanations of nature and the cosmos, associated with thinkers such as Thales, Anaximander, and Anaximenes
