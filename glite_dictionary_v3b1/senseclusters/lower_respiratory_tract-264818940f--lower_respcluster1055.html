lower respiratory tract

noun
bronchi and lungs; part of the respiratory system
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊ.ɚ rɪˈspɪr.əˌtɔr.i trækt / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.ə rɪˈspɪr.ət.ri trækt / (BrE)
the part of the respiratory system comprising the bronchi and lungs

Examples

The lower respiratory tract
Doctors examined the patient's lower respiratory tract using a bronchoscope to check for abnormalities.
Pneumonia is an infection of the lower respiratory tract that can cause serious illness.
Smoking damages the lower respiratory tract and increases the risk of lung disease.

Related Terms

  • antonym: upper respiratory tract - the part of the respiratory system comprising the nose, nasal cavity, and throat
