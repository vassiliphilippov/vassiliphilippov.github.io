waste not, want not

x
saying: careful use prevents need
7.6
Pronunciation: / weɪst nɑt wɑnt nɑt / (AmE), / weɪst nɒt wɒnt nɒt / (BrE)
If you do not waste anything, you will not be left in need; careful use of resources prevents future lack.

Examples

Waste not, want not principle
If you live by the principle of waste not, want not, you'll rarely find yourself in need.
My grandmother always reminded us, waste not, want not, every time we finished a meal.
The company adopted a waste not, want not approach to reduce expenses and conserve resources.
