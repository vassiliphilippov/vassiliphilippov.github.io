unflinchingly

adverb
without fear or hesitation
7.1
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈflɪn.tʃɪŋ.liː/ /; / /ʌn.ˈflɪn.tʃɪŋ.liː/ (AmE), /ə.ˈflɪn.tʃɪŋ.liː/ /; / /ʌn.ˈflɪn.tʃɪŋ.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that does not show fear, hesitation, or avoidance when facing something difficult or dangerous

Examples

She described the tragic events unflinchingly, refusing to hide the painful truth.
