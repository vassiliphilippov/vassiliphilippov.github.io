currently indisposed

adjective
temporarily unavailable; unable to attend
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝːrəntli ˌɪndɪsˈpoʊzd / (AmE), / ˈkʌrəntli ˌɪndɪsˈpəʊzd / (BrE)
Unable to attend or participate at the present moment, typically for reasons of health or personal matters.

Examples

Currently indisposed at work
I'm afraid the manager is currently indisposed and can't take your call.
She apologized for being currently indisposed and promised to reschedule the meeting.
The professor is currently indisposed, so today’s lecture has been postponed.
