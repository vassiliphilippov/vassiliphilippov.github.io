beatbox

noun
vocal percussion art
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtbɑks / (AmE), / ˈbiːtbɒks / (BrE)
the art or practice of creating rhythmic sounds and percussion effects using only the mouth and voice

Examples

Beatbox performance
He amazed the crowd with his impressive beatbox skills during the performance.
Learning to beatbox takes practice and a good sense of rhythm.
The group added a beatbox section to their a cappella song for extra energy.
