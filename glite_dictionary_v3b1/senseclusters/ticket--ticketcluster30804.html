ticket

noun
support or issue-tracking record
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈtɪk.ət/ /; / /ˈtɪk.ɪt/ (AmE), /ˈtɪk.ɪt/ /; / /ˈtɪk.ət/ (BrE)
a record or entry in a customer-support, helpdesk, or issue-tracking system that documents a reported problem, request, or task to be investigated and resolved.

Examples

A support ticket
She opened a ticket with IT to report the network outage affecting several colleagues.
The support agent updated the ticket with steps taken and assigned it to the engineer.
After we closed the ticket, the customer confirmed the problem was completely resolved.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bug report - a report describing a software defect that needs fixing
  • synonym: issue - a problem or fault reported for attention and resolution
  • synonym: service request - a user's request for a service or assistance recorded for handling
