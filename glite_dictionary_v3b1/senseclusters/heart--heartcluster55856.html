heart

noun
organ that pumps blood
1.0
Pronunciation: / hɑrt / (AmE), / hɑːt / (BrE)
the hollow muscular organ in the chest that pumps blood through the body by rhythmic contraction and dilation

Examples

A beating heart
After running up the stairs, his heart was pounding in his chest.
Doctors explained that the heart supplies oxygen-rich blood to every part of the body.
The patient needed a transplant because his heart was failing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cardiac - relating to the heart (medical adjective)
