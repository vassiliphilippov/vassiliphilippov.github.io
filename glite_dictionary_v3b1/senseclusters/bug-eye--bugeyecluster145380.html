bug-eye

noun
large protruding eyes
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌɡˌaɪ / (AmE), / ˈbʌɡˌaɪ / (BrE)
a person or thing with large, protruding eyes or headlights that resemble the eyes of a bug

Examples

Bug-eye headlights
The old sports car had distinctive bug-eye headlights that made it stand out on the road.
He was teased at school for his bug-eye appearance, but he learned to embrace it.
Some insects have bug-eye features that help them see in many directions at once.

Related Terms

  • antonym: normal - not unusual or prominent in appearance
  • synonym: pop-eye - a person with eyes that bulge outward
