co-present

adjective
present together
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊˈprɛzənt / (AmE), / ˌkəʊˈprɛzənt / (BrE)
being present together with one or more others in the same place at the same time

Examples

Co-present at a meeting
Both managers were co-present at the meeting to discuss the new project.
The teacher required all group members to be co-present during the presentation.
Researchers found that participants were more engaged when they were co-present in the same room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: absent - not present in a place
  • synonym: together - with each other in the same place
