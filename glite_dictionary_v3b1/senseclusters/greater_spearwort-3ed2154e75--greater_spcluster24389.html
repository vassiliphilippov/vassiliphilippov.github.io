greater spearwort

noun
European semiaquatic plant with spear-shaped leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪtər ˈspɪər.wɜːrt / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtə ˈspɪə.wɜːt / (BrE)
a semiaquatic European plant of the buttercup family, with spear-shaped leaves and yellow flowers; Ranunculus lingua.

Examples

The greater spearwort plant
Botanists identified the plant as greater spearwort due to its long, spear-shaped leaves.
In spring, greater spearwort produces striking yellow flowers along the riverbank.
The pond was edged with tall, leafy greater spearwort swaying gently in the breeze.
