surfy

adjective
good for surfing
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːfi / (AmE), / ˈsɜːfi / (BrE)
having waves or water conditions that are suitable for surfing

Examples

Surfy beach waves
The beach was especially surfy after last night's storm, attracting many experienced surfers.
We checked the forecast to see if the conditions would be surfy enough for our lesson.
On calm days, the bay is peaceful, but today it looks quite surfy and exciting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - without waves or roughness
  • synonym: wavy - having many waves or undulations
