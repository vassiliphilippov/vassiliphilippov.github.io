death stare

noun
fierce, intimidating look
2.5
Pronunciation: / dɛθ stɛr / (AmE), / dɛθ steə / (BrE)
A fierce and intimidating look directed at someone, usually expressing anger or disapproval.

Examples

A death stare of anger
She shot her little brother a death stare after he spilled juice on her homework.
The coach's death stare was enough to silence the whole team.
When I told the joke, my mother gave me a death stare from across the table.

  • synonym: scowl - an angry or bad-tempered expression
