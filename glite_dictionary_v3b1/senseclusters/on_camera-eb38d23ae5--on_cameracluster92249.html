on camera

adverb
within view of a camera; being filmed
Pronunciation: / ɑn ˈkæmɚə / (AmE), / ɒn ˈkæmərə / (BrE)
appearing or taking place within the view of a movie or television camera; being filmed or broadcast

Examples

On camera appearance
Her first appearance on camera made her very nervous.
Please make sure you are on camera before you begin speaking.
The actor remained completely in character while on camera.

Related Terms

  • antonym: off camera - not within the view of a camera; not being filmed or broadcast
