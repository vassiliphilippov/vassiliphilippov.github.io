pink-collar

adjective
relating to women's traditional jobs
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪŋk ˌkɑːlər / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋk ˌkɒlə / (BrE)
Relating to jobs or workers in industries or positions traditionally held by women, such as nursing, teaching, or clerical work.

Examples

Pink-collar jobs
Many pink-collar positions are still predominantly occupied by women despite social progress.
She worked in a pink-collar job as an elementary school teacher for twenty years.
The union is fighting for better wages for pink-collar employees in the healthcare sector.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blue-collar - relating to manual or industrial work
  • antonym: white-collar - relating to professional or office work
