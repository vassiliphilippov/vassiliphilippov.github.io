simulant

noun
artificial replica
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmjələnt / (AmE), / ˈsɪmjʊlənt / (BrE)
an artificial being or object made to closely imitate a real person or thing, especially in science fiction

Examples

Robot simulant
The scientist created a simulant that could perfectly mimic human emotions.
In the futuristic city, simulants worked alongside humans in many industries.
The movie explores what happens when a simulant begins to question its own identity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: android - a robot with a human appearance
  • antonym: human - a real person, not artificial
  • synonym: replica - an exact copy or model of something
