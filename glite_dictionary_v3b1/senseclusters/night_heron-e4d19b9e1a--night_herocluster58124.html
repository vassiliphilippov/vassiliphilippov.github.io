night heron

noun
nocturnal or crepuscular heron
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪt ˌhɛr.ən / (AmE), / ˈnaɪt ˌhɛr.ən / (BrE)
A heron that is active at night or during twilight, belonging to the genera Nycticorax or related groups.

Examples

The night heron bird
The night heron is known for its distinctive black cap and red eyes.
Unlike other herons, the night heron prefers to hunt during the evening and early morning.
We spotted a night heron standing quietly by the riverbank as dusk fell.

  • synonym: heron - a long-legged wading bird
