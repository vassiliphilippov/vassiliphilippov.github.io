snippy remark

noun
curt or sharp comment
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɪpi ɹəˈmɑɹk / (AmE), / ˈsnɪpi rɪˈmɑːk / (BrE)
a curt or sharp comment, often considered rude or dismissive

Examples

A snippy remark
Her snippy remark caught everyone by surprise, as she is usually very polite.
She couldn't help but make a snippy remark during the heated meeting.
Whenever he's stressed, his snippy remarks tend to offend his colleagues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curt remark - a brief and often rude comment
  • antonym: polite remark - a courteous, considerate comment
