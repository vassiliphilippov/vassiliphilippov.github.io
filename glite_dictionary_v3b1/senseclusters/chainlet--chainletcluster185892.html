chainlet

noun
short sequence
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪnlət / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪnlət / (BrE)
a short series or sequence of related things or events, often forming part of a larger chain

Examples

A chainlet of events
A chainlet of errors in the report caused confusion among the team.
The detective noticed a chainlet of clues that pointed to the suspect.
She wore a delicate chainlet around her neck as a simple accessory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chain - a series of connected things or events
  • synonym: sequence - a set of related things that follow each other in order
