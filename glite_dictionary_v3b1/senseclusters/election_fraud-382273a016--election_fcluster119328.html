election fraud

noun
illegal interference with election results
3.4
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛkʃən frɔd / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛkʃən frɔːd / (BrE)
Illegal interference with the process or results of an election, typically by misrepresentation or tampering.

Examples

Election fraud allegations
Allegations of election fraud sparked widespread protests throughout the country.
Strict laws are designed to prevent election fraud and protect voters’ rights.
The candidate denied any involvement in election fraud during the campaign.

Related Terms

  • antonym: election integrity - the adherence to ethical and legal standards in elections
  • synonym: vote rigging - the act of dishonestly organizing an election to get a particular result
