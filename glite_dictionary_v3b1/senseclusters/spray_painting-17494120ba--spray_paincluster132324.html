spray painting

noun
applying paint with a spray device
Pronunciation: / spreɪ ˈpeɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / spreɪ ˈpeɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
The act or technique of applying paint to a surface by means of a spray, typically using a sprayer or aerosol device.

Examples

Spray painting technique
Graffiti artists often use spray painting to create vibrant murals on city walls.
Many people prefer spray painting furniture for a smooth and even coat.
The car's finish looked flawless after professional spray painting at the auto shop.
