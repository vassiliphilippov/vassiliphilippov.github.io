vallecular

adjective
of a small groove
Pronunciation: / vəˈlɛkjələr / (AmE), / vəˈlɛkjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to a small depression or groove, especially in anatomical structures

Examples

The vallecular area
The surgeon carefully inspected the vallecular area for any signs of abnormal tissue.
A vallecular cyst can sometimes cause difficulty in swallowing.
During the examination, the doctor noted a slight swelling in the vallecular region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grooved - having a long, narrow cut or depression
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface; not grooved
