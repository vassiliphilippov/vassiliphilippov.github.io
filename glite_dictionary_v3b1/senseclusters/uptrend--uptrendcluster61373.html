uptrend

noun
rising tendency
Pronunciation: / ˈʌptrɛnd / (AmE), / ˈʌptrɛnd / (BrE)
a general increase or upward tendency in something, such as popularity, activity, or performance over time

Examples

A steady uptrend
There has been a noticeable uptrend in renewable energy use over the past decade.
The school reported an uptrend in student participation in extracurricular activities this year.
Analysts observed an uptrend in consumer confidence following the new policy announcement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: downtrend - a general decrease or downward tendency
  • synonym: rise - an increase in amount, number, or level
  • synonym: upswing - a period of improvement or increase
