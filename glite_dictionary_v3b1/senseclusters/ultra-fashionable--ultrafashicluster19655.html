ultra-fashionable

noun
very stylish people
Pronunciation: / ˌʌltrəˈfæʃənəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌltrəˈfæʃnəbəl / (BrE)
people who are extremely fashionable and regarded as very stylish or trendsetting

Examples

The ultra-fashionable crowd
The ultra-fashionable gathered at the exclusive launch party, each wearing the latest designer trends.
She dreams of joining the ranks of the ultra-fashionable in the world of high fashion.
Magazines often feature the ultra-fashionable to showcase new styles and inspire readers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fashionista - a person who is very interested in fashion
  • antonym: old-fashioned - not in style; out of date
  • synonym: trendsetter - a person who leads the way in fashion or ideas
