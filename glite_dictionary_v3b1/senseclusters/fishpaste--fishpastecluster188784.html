fishpaste

noun
spreadable paste made from fish or shellfish
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃˌpeɪst / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃˌpeɪst / (BrE)
A soft, spreadable food made by blending fish or shellfish into a paste, often used as a sandwich filling or condiment.

Examples

A fishpaste spread
Asian markets often carry several varieties of fishpaste used in soups and dumplings.
He spread fishpaste on his toast for breakfast.
The recipe calls for two tablespoons of fishpaste to add flavor.
