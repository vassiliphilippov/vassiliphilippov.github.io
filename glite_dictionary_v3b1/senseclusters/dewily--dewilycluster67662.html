dewily

adverb
in a moist manner
8.8 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàduÀê…™li / (AmE), / ÀàdjuÀê…™li / (BrE)
in a way that is moist, fresh, or covered with dew

Examples

Grass shines dewily
The leaves sparkled dewily after the cool night.
She looked at him dewily, her eyes bright and fresh.
The garden glistened dewily in the soft morning sunlight.

Synonyms

  • moistly
    2.8 0.07/m

  • damply
    2.5 0.10/m
    damply describes an often cool or unwanted slight wetness on things, while dewily suggests fresh morning wetness from dew.
