go along to get along

verb
avoid conflict by conforming
4.0 6.6
Pronunciation: /goʊ ə.ˈlɔːŋ tə get ə.ˈlɔːŋ/ (AmE), /gəʊ ə.ˈlɒŋ tə get ə.ˈlɒŋ/ (BrE)
to act in a way that conforms or agrees with others in order to avoid conflict or maintain harmony

Examples

To go along to get along
