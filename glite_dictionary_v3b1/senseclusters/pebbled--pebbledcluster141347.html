pebbled

adjective
bumpy surface texture
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛbəld / (AmE), / ˈpɛbəld / (BrE)
having a surface covered with small, rounded bumps or resembling the texture of pebbles

Examples

Pebbled leather bag
She carried a pebbled leather bag to the party.
The pebbled path made walking a bit uncomfortable in thin shoes.
The artist chose a pebbled canvas to add extra texture to the painting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: smooth - having an even, flat surface without bumps
  • synonym: textured - having a noticeable surface feel or pattern
