committee meeting

noun
meeting held by a committee
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɪti ˈmitɪŋ / (AmE), / kəˈmɪti ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
a meeting of a committee, usually held to discuss and make decisions on administrative matters

Examples

Committee meeting decisions

She missed the committee meeting due to a prior engagement.
The quarterly committee meeting was scheduled to discuss the new budget proposal.
