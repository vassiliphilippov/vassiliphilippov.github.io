foul

adjective
offensive or unpleasant smell
4.4
Pronunciation: / faʊl / (AmE), / faʊl / (BrE)
having a very unpleasant or offensive smell or taste; offensive to the senses

Examples

Foul smell
A foul stench wafted up from the stagnant pond.
After being left out all night, the milk turned completely foul.
The garbage had a foul smell that filled the entire alley.
Rotting food can have a foul taste and smell that is hard to forget.
She was offended by his foul language during the argument.
The foul odor coming from the drain made everyone cover their noses.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clean - free from dirt or impurities; not offensive
  • synonym: disgusting - extremely unpleasant or revolting
  • antonym: fragrant - having a pleasant or sweet smell
  • synonym: putrid - decaying and emitting a fetid smell
