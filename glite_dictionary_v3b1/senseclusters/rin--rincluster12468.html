rin

noun
Japanese length unit
9.9
Pronunciation: /rɪn/ (AmE), /rɪn/ (BrE)
a traditional Japanese unit of length equal to one thousandth of a shaku, used in measuring small distances

Examples

A rin measurement
The craftsman measured the joint to within a single rin before gluing the pieces together.
In traditional carpentry, accuracy down to a rin can make a significant difference.
The old ruler still had markings for every rin, showing its use in precise work.
