thorny challenge

noun
difficult or problematic situation
Pronunciation: / ˈθɔrni ˈtʃælɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈθɔːni ˈtʃælɪndʒ / (BrE)

Examples

A thorny challenge arises

Solving the city's traffic problems has proven to be a thorny challenge for urban planners.
