songy

adjective
like a song
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔŋi / (AmE), / ˈsɒŋi / (BrE)
having qualities or characteristics typical of a song, such as melody or musicality

Examples

A songy voice
Her voice was very songy and pleasant to listen to.
The poem had a songy rhythm that made it easy to remember.
He played a songy tune on his guitar that everyone enjoyed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: melodic - having a pleasant tune or melody
  • synonym: musical - having the qualities of music
