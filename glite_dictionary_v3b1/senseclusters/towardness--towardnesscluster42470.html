towardness

noun
orientation or focus
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔːrdnəs / (AmE), / ˈtəʊədnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being directed or oriented toward a person, goal, or object

Examples

Towardness to goals
The towardness of the team helped them achieve their ambitious goals ahead of schedule.
Her towardness in pursuing her studies impressed all of her professors.
A clear sense of towardness can motivate people to overcome obstacles.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aimlessness - the state of lacking direction or purpose
  • synonym: focus - concentration of attention or effort on something
  • synonym: orientation - the act of positioning or aligning toward something
