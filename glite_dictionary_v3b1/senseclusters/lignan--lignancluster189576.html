lignan

noun
plant compound
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪɡnæn / (AmE), / ˈlɪɡnan / (BrE)
a type of natural compound found in plants, especially seeds and grains, often associated with health benefits

Examples

Plant lignan compound
Flaxseeds are one of the richest sources of lignan in the human diet.
Scientists study lignan content in foods to understand their potential health effects.
Some grains and vegetables also contain significant amounts of lignan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flavonoid - a group of plant compounds with antioxidant properties
  • synonym: phytoestrogen - a plant-derived compound with estrogen-like activity
