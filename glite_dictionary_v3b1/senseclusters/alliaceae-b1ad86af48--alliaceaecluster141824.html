Alliaceae

noun
family of flowering plants (onions, garlic, etc.)
Pronunciation: / ˌæl.iˈeɪ.si.i /; / ˌæliˈeɪsiːˌiː / (AmE), / ˌæl.iˈeɪ.siː.iː /; / ˌæliˈeɪsiːˌiː / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants that includes onions, garlic, leeks, chives, and related plants; sometimes considered a subfamily of Liliaceae
