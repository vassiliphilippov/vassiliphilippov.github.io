self-luminous

adjective
emitting light by itself
4.3 6.7
Pronunciation: /self ˈluː.mɪ.nəs/ /; / /self ˈluː.mə.nəs/ (AmE), /self ˈluː.mɪ.nəs/ /; / /self ˈluː.mə.nəs/ (BrE)
emitting light by itself, not by reflecting or needing light from another source

Examples
