fossil fool

noun
someone resistant to change; outdated person
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑsəl ˌfuːl / (AmE), / ˈfɒsəl ˌfuːl / (BrE)
a person who is outdated or stubbornly resists change, especially by clinging to old ideas or technologies

Examples

A fossil fool resists change

The company risks becoming a fossil fool by ignoring modern trends in the market.
