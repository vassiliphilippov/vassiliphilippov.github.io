first up

adverb
the first item or person in a sequence
Pronunciation: / fɝst ʌp / (AmE), / fɜːst ʌp / (BrE)
used to indicate the first item, person, or topic to be presented or discussed in a sequence

Examples

First up is a reading
First up is a reading from our guest author.
First up, the committee will address the budget concerns before moving to other topics.
Let’s look at the quarterly report first up in today’s agenda.
