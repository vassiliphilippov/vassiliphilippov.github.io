cat's-claw

noun
a shrub with curved pods, West Indies and Florida
8.9 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkæts klɔ / (AmE), / ˈkats klɔː / (BrE)
an erect shrub native to the West Indies and Florida, having few or no spines, racemes of white to yellow flowers, and curved pointed pods with black shiny seeds
