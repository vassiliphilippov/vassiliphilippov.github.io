heterostructure

noun
layered material structure
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhet.ə.r.oʊ.strʌk.tʃər/ (AmE), /ˈhet.ə.rəʊ.strʌk.tʃə/ (BrE)
a structure formed by joining layers of different materials, especially semiconductors, to achieve specific electronic properties

Examples

A heterostructure design
The efficiency of the new solar cell is increased by its heterostructure design.
Researchers developed a heterostructure to combine the properties of different semiconductors.
A heterostructure allows for better control of electron flow in electronic devices.

Related Terms

  • antonym: homostructure - a structure made from layers of the same material
  • synonym: superlattice - a periodic structure of layers of two or more materials
