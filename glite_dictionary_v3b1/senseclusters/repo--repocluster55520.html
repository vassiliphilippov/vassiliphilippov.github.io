repo

verb
to repossess property
Pronunciation: / ˈriːpoʊ / (AmE), / ˈriːpəʊ / (BrE)
to repossess property, especially a vehicle, because the owner has failed to make required payments

Examples

To repo a car
If you miss another payment, the bank might repo your car.
They had to repo several vehicles after the owners defaulted on their loans.
The company will repo the equipment if the contract is not honored.

Related Terms

  • synonym: repossess - to take back property because of missed payments
