jutting

adjective
extending out; projecting outward
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌtɪŋ / (BrE)
extending out above or beyond a surface or boundary; projecting outward

Examples

A jutting balcony
