pavilion

noun
large, sumptuous tent
1.3
Pronunciation: / pəˈvɪliən /; / pəˈvɪljən / (AmE), / pəˈvɪliən /; / pəˈvɪljən / (BrE)
a large tent or open-sided building, often used outdoors for events, gatherings, or shelter
