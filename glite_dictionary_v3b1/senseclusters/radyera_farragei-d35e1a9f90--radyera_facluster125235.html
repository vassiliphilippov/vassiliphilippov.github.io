Radyera farragei

noun
Australian shrub with purple flowers
Pronunciation: / ˌræd.iˈɛr.ə fəˈrædʒ.aɪ / (AmE), / ˌræd.iˈɛə.rə fəˈrædʒ.iː / (BrE)
A shrub native to southern and western Australia, with unlobed or shallowly lobed toothed leaves and purple flowers; sometimes classified in the genus Hibiscus.
