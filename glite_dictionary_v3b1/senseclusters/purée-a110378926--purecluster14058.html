purée

verb
to blend until smooth
4.1
Pronunciation: / pjʊˈreɪ / (AmE), / ˈpjʊəreɪ / (BrE)
to make food smooth and soft by crushing or blending it into a thick liquid or paste

Examples

To purée vegetables
She used a blender to purée the vegetables for the soup.
After boiling the fruit, he began to purée it for the dessert.
You should purée the beans until they are completely smooth and creamy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blend - to mix or combine into a smooth substance
  • antonym: chop - to cut food into small pieces
  • synonym: mash - to crush food into a soft mass
