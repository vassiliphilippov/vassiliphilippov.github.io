fishing eagle

noun
a fish-eating eagle of Europe and Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃɪŋ ˌiːɡəl / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃɪŋ ˌiːɡl̩ / (BrE)
a large eagle found in southeast Europe and central Asia, known for catching and eating fish

Examples

The fishing eagle hunts fish
Conservation efforts have helped increase the population of the fishing eagle in Central Asia.
The fishing eagle soared above the river, searching for prey.
We watched a fishing eagle swoop down and snatch a fish from the lake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: osprey - a large fish-eating bird of prey with long, narrow wings
