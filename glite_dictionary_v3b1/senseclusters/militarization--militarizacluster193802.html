militarization

noun
process of preparing for war or military conflict
4.2 5.8
Pronunciation: /mɪl.ɪ.tə.rɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /mɪl.ɪ.tə.rɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of equipping, preparing, or organizing a country, region, or group for military conflict or operations

Examples

The militarization of police
Concerns have grown over the militarization of local police forces in recent years.
The militarization of the border led to heightened tensions between the two countries.
The government's announcement signaled an increase in the militarization of the region.
Many citizens protested against the government's rapid militarisation policies.
The militarisation of the police force is a controversial issue in many democracies.
The militarisation of the region has raised tensions among neighbouring countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: armament - the process of supplying military weapons and equipment
  • antonym: demilitarisation - removal of military forces from an area
  • antonym: demilitarization - the process of removing military forces or influence
  • synonym: militarization - act of assembling and putting into readiness for war (American spelling)
