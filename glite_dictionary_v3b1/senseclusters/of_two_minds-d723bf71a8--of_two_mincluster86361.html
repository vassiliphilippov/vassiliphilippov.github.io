of two minds

adjective
uncertain or undecided, conflicted
Pronunciation: / ʌv tuː maɪndz / (AmE), / ɒv tuː maɪndz / (BrE)
uncertain or undecided, having conflicting opinions or feelings about something

Examples

To be of two minds
I'm of two minds about accepting the promotion, since it means relocating.
She was of two minds when choosing between graduate schools.
We're of two minds about getting a dog this year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decided - having made a firm choice or decision
  • synonym: uncertain - not sure; having doubts
