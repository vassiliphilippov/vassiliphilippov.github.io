playable

adjective
able to be played or used
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈpleɪ.ə.bəl / (BrE)
capable of being played or suitable for play; able to be used, performed, or acted upon in the context of a game, sport, instrument, or media.

Examples

A playable piano
She found the old piano surprisingly playable despite its age.
The course was muddy, but still playable after the rain.
The new game has a very playable demo available online.
