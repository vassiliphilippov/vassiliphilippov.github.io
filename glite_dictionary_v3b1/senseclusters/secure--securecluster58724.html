secure

adjective
safe; free from danger or risk
1.9 3.2
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkjʊr / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
protected or safe from danger, attack, or harm; not likely to be tampered with or broken into

Examples

A secure building
She felt secure knowing the doors were locked and the alarm was set.
The company wants to ensure its data is secure from cyberattacks.
This vault is one of the most secure places in the building.
Make sure your home is secure before you leave for vacation.
The company uses a secure server to protect customer data from hackers.
This system provides a secure environment for storing sensitive information.
