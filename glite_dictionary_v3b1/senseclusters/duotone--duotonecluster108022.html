duotone

noun
two-tone image
Pronunciation: / ˈduːoʊˌtoʊn / (AmE), / ˈdjuːəʊˌtəʊn / (BrE)
an image or print produced using two different colors or tones, often for artistic effect

Examples

A duotone print
The designer chose a duotone effect to give the poster a modern look.
Many vintage photographs were printed in duotone to enhance contrast and mood.
You can create a striking duotone image by combining blue and yellow tones.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monochrome - an image created using only one color or tone
  • degree higher: tritone - an image or print made using three different colors or tones
