duotone

noun
two-tone image
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈduː.oʊ.toʊn/ /; / /ˈdjuː.oʊ.toʊn/ (AmE), /ˈdjuː.əʊ.təʊn/ /; / /ˈduː.əʊ.təʊn/ (BrE)
an image or print produced using two different colors or tones, often for artistic effect

Examples
