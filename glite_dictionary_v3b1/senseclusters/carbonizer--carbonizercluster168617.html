carbonizer

noun
charcoal-making machine
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrbəˌnaɪzər /; / ˈkɑːrbənaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈkɑːbənʌɪzə / (BrE)
a person or device that converts organic material (such as wood or agricultural waste) into charcoal by heating it without air
