unexotic

adjective
not foreign or unusual
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪɡˈzɑːtɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪɡˈzɒtɪk / (BrE)
not exotic; ordinary, familiar, or typical of a place (not foreign or unusual)

Examples

Unexotic plants
The garden featured only unexotic plants that were common in the region.
He preferred unexotic foods that reminded him of home.
Their vacation was filled with unexotic activities like hiking and picnicking in local parks.
The food at the restaurant was tasty but rather unexotic compared to international cuisine.
He preferred an unexotic vacation spot close to home instead of traveling abroad.
Her style is intentionally unexotic, focusing on comfort and simplicity.
