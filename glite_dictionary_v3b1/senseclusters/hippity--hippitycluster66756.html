hippity

noun
small playful hop
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪpɪti / (AmE), / ˈhɪpɪti / (BrE)
a small, quick jump or hop, often used in a playful or childlike way

Examples

A quick hippity
The rabbit gave a quick hippity across the garden path.
With a cheerful hippity, the child jumped over the puddle.
Each hippity of the frog brought it closer to the pond.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hop - a short, quick jump
  • degree higher: leap - a long or high jump
