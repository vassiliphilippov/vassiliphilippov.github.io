savory stew

noun
hearty, flavorful meat and vegetable dish
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪvəri stu / (AmE), / ˈseɪvəri stjuː / (BrE)
A hot dish made by simmering meat and vegetables in a seasoned, flavorful broth.

Examples

A savory stew dish
A bowl of savory stew is perfect on a chilly afternoon.
She prepared a savory stew with beef, potatoes, and carrots for dinner.
The aroma of savory stew filled the entire house as it simmered on the stove.
