agrodolce

noun
Italian sweet-sour sauce
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːɡroʊˈdoʊltʃeɪ / (AmE), / ˌaɡrəʊˈdɒltʃeɪ / (BrE)
a sweet-and-sour Italian sauce or relish, usually made with vinegar and sugar, used to flavor vegetables, fish, or meat

Examples

Agrodolce sauce
The chef drizzled agrodolce over the grilled fish to enhance its flavor.
Roasted vegetables taste delicious when topped with a homemade agrodolce.
She prepared a tangy agrodolce using vinegar, sugar, and dried fruit for the dish.
