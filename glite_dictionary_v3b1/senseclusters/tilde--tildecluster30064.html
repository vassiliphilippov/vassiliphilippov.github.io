tilde

noun
the ~ symbol
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪl.deɪ /; / ˈtɪldə / (AmE), / ˈtɪl.də /; / ˈtɪldə / (BrE)
the symbol (~) used in writing, mathematics, or computing, often to show approximation, negation, or for other special uses

Examples

Math tilde symbol
To enter a tilde on your keyboard, press Shift and the key next to 1.

The tilde is often used in mathematics to show that two values are approximately equal.

In some programming languages, the tilde is used as a bitwise operator.
