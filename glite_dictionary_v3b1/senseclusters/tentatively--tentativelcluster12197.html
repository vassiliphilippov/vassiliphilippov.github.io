tentatively

adverb
uncertain or hesitant manner
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛntətɪvli / (AmE), / ˈtɛntətɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that shows uncertainty or hesitation; not done with finality or confidence.

Examples

To smile tentatively at someone
He smiled tentatively, not sure if his joke had been understood.
She reached out tentatively to shake his hand, unsure of his reaction.
The committee has tentatively approved the proposal, pending further review.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confidently - in a manner showing certainty or confidence
  • synonym: hesitantly - in a hesitant or unsure manner
