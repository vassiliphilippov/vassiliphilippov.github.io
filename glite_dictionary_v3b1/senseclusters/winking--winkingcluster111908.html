winking

noun
rapid, repeated eye closing
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
Closing the eyes repeatedly and rapidly, often as a reaction to light or irritation.

Examples

Rapid winking in wind
In the harsh wind, she was winking constantly to protect her eyes.
The allergy caused him to start winking uncontrollably during the meeting.
The bright lights on the stage left the actor winking for several minutes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blinking - closing and opening the eyes quickly and repeatedly
