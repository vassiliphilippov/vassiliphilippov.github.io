half-breed

noun
offensive term for a person of mixed racial ancestry
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhæfˌbrid /; / ˈhæfˌbrid, ˈhɑfˌbrid / (AmE), / ˈhɑːfˌbriːd /; / ˈhɑːf.briːd / (BrE)
an offensive term for a person of mixed racial or ethnic ancestry, especially with one parent of Indigenous descent and one of European descent

Examples

A half-breed person
Historically, the term half-breed was often used in a derogatory way in North America.
Many Native Americans found the label half-breed deeply insulting and dehumanizing.
The use of half-breed is now widely recognized as offensive and inappropriate.
He identified as a proud half-breed, celebrating both cultures.
She felt like a half-breed in a world that demands conformity.
The novel's protagonist is a half-breed struggling for acceptance.
