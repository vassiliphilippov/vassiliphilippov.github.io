area

noun
region of the body with specific function or supply
Pronunciation: / ˈɛriə / (AmE), / ˈɛːriə / (BrE)
a part of an animal's body that has a special function or is supplied by a particular artery or nerve

Examples

Sensitive area of the arm
An injury to this area of the brain can affect movement and speech.
Surgeons carefully avoided the area supplied by the major nerve.
The doctor explained that the sensation was lost in the area innervated by the damaged nerve.
