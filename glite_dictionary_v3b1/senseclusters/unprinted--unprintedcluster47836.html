unprinted

adjective
without printed design
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈprɪntɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈprɪntɪd / (BrE)
not having any printed design, pattern, or text; plain and undecorated.

Unprinted paper

The packaging was completely unprinted, making it easy to recycle.
