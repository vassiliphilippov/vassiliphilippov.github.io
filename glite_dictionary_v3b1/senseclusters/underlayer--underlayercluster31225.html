underlayer

noun
inner clothing layer
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌleɪər / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌleɪə / (BrE)
a garment or layer of material worn beneath other clothing, usually for warmth or to absorb sweat

Examples

Thermal underlayer
She wore a thermal underlayer beneath her sweater to stay warm during the hike.
A moisture-wicking underlayer helps keep athletes dry during intense workouts.
In cold climates, a good underlayer is essential for comfort and protection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: base layer - the first layer of clothing worn next to the skin
  • antonym: outerwear - clothing worn over other clothes, especially outdoors
