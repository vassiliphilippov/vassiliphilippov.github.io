casualty

noun
military loss of people or equipment
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkæʒuˌælti / (AmE), / ˈkaʒjʊəlti / (BrE)
a reduction in number of military personnel or equipment as a result of loss, injury, or destruction

Examples

Military casualty numbers
Naval reports indicated a severe casualty rate among the fleet’s destroyers.
The battalion suffered a significant casualty in equipment after the attack.
The exercise simulated a casualty situation to prepare soldiers for combat losses.
