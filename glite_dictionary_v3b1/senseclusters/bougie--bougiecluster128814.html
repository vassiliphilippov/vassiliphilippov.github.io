bougie

noun
pretentious person
9.3 0.08/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàbuÀê íi / (AmE), / ÀàbuÀê íi / (BrE)
a person who is seen as valuing material comfort, wealth, or social status, often in a pretentious way

Examples

He is a bougie
He was called a bougie because he only shops at expensive stores.
Some people think drinking fancy coffee makes you look bougie.
Her friends teased her for being bougie after she refused to eat at a fast food place.
