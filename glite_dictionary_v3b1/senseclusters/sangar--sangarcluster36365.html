sangar

noun
stone shelter or wall
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsæŋ.gɑːr/ /; / /ˈsæŋ.gər/ (AmE), /ˈsæŋ.gɑː/ /; / /ˈsæŋ.gə/ (BrE)
