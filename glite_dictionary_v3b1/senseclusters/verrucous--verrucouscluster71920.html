verrucous

adjective
wart-like surface
Pronunciation: / vəˈruːkəs / (AmE), / vəˈruːkəs / (BrE)
having a rough, wart-like surface or appearance

Examples

Verrucous skin growth
The dermatologist described the growth as verrucous due to its rough texture.
A verrucous plaque was observed on the patient's lower leg during the examination.
Some skin conditions can cause verrucous changes that resemble warts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface
  • synonym: warty - having the appearance or texture of a wart
