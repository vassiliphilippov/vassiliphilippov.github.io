tubular

adjective
shaped like or consisting of tubes
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːbjələr / (AmE), / ˈtjuːbjʊlə / (BrE)
shaped like a tube or consisting of hollow tubes, often allowing fluids or air to pass through

Examples

Tubular shape
Many sea creatures have tubular bodies to help them move through water efficiently.
The instrument produces sound as air passes through its tubular structure.
The plumber repaired the tubular pipes under the kitchen sink.
