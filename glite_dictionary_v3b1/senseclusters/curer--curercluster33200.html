curer

noun
person or device that cures
Pronunciation: / ˈkjʊrər / (AmE), / ˈkjʊərə / (BrE)
a person or device that preserves food or hardens materials by curing processes such as drying, salting, or chemical treatment

Examples

A curer of meat
The curer used traditional methods to preserve the fish for the winter months.
A modern curer can harden concrete quickly using special chemicals.
In the factory, the curer ensures that all products are properly treated before packaging.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curing - the process of preserving or hardening by drying, salting, or chemical treatment
  • synonym: preserver - a person or thing that keeps something in good condition
