solidify

verb
to become solid
3.1 3.6
Pronunciation: /sə.ˈlɪ.də.faɪ/ /; / /sə.ˈlɪ.dɪ.faɪ/ (AmE), /sə.ˈlɪ.dɪ.faɪ/ /; / /sə.ˈlɪ.də.faɪ/ (BrE)
to make or become solid or more solid; to change from a liquid or gas to a solid state

Examples

To solidify a mixture
Allow the mixture to cool so that it will solidify into a gel.
The lava quickly began to solidify after the eruption.
When metal is poured into a mold, it will solidify as it cools.
After being left out overnight, the cement mixture began to solidify rapidly.
Allow the chocolate to cool so it can solidify into a firm shape.
The decision helped solidify their partnership for years to come.

  • antonym: liquefy - to become liquid
