undergraduate

noun
student not yet with a first degree
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈɡrædʒuət / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈɡrædʒuət / (BrE)
a university or college student who has not yet earned a first degree

Examples

An undergraduate student
As an undergraduate, she studied biology at the university.
Many undergraduates participate in research projects during their studies.
The program is open to both undergraduate and graduate students.

Related Terms

  • antonym: graduate - a person who has successfully completed a degree
