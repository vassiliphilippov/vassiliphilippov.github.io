unfruitfulness

noun
lack of productivity
7.0 0.01/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàfruÀêtf…ôln…ôs / (AmE), /  ånÀàfruÀêtf…ôln…ôs / (BrE)
the state of not producing anything useful, such as crops, offspring, or results; barrenness or lack of productivity

Examples

The unfruitfulness of land
The unfruitfulness of the land made farming nearly impossible for the villagers.
After months of unfruitfulness, the team decided to change their strategy.
He was frustrated by the unfruitfulness of his repeated attempts to solve the problem.
The unfruitfulness of the soil made it difficult for farmers to grow any crops.
After several years of unfruitfulness, the couple decided to seek medical advice.
The project was abandoned due to the unfruitfulness of their efforts.

Synonyms

  • fruitlessness
    7.3 0.01/m
    fruitlessness means failing to bring results or success, while unfruitfulness highlights a lack of useful production or productivity.
  • unproductiveness
    7.1 0.01/m
    unproductiveness describes a general lack of output or achievement, while unfruitfulness stresses absence of useful or productive results.
  • barrenness
    8.1 0.14/m
    barrenness often refers to land or inability to have children, while unfruitfulness refers to lack of useful production or results.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barrenness - the state of being unable to produce anything
  • antonym: fertility - the ability to produce fruit, offspring, or results
  • antonym: productivity - the state of being productive or producing results
  • synonym: sterility - the inability to produce offspring or crops
