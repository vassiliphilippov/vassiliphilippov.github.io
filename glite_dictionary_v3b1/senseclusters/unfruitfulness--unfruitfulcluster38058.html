unfruitfulness

noun
lack of productivity
7.0
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈfruːt.fəl.nəs/ /; / /ə.ˈfruːt.fəl.nəs/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈfruːt.fəl.nəs/ /; / /ə.ˈfruːt.fəl.nəs/ (BrE)
the state of not producing anything useful, such as crops, offspring, or results; barrenness or lack of productivity
