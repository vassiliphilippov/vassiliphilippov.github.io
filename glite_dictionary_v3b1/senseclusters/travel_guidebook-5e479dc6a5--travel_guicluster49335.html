travel guidebook

noun
book with advice for travelers
Pronunciation: / ˈtrævəl ˈɡaɪdˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈtrævəl ˈɡaɪdbʊk / (BrE)
a book containing information, advice, and recommendations for travelers about a particular place or travel in general

Examples

A travel guidebook for Japan
Before visiting Japan, she bought a travel guidebook to help plan her trip.
He always packs a travel guidebook in his suitcase for reference during his adventures.
The travel guidebook recommended several hidden gems that tourists often miss.
