bury

verb
try to forget; dismiss from mind
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛri / (AmE), / ˈbɛri / (BrE)
to dismiss from the mind or deliberately try to forget

Examples

To bury bad memories
Despite his efforts, he could not bury the memory of that terrible day.
He wanted to bury his anger and move on with his life.
She tried hard to bury all thoughts of her past mistakes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forget - to fail to remember; to lose remembrance of
  • antonym: remember - to recall to the mind by an act or effort of memory
