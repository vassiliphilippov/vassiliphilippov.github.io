BBS

noun
computer messaging and file sharing system
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈbiː.biː.es/ /; / /biː.biː.ˈes/ (AmE), /ˈbiː.biː.es/ /; / /biː.biː.ˈes/ (BrE)
a computer system that allows users to exchange messages, share files, and access information remotely, typically over a telephone line; short for 'bulletin board system'

Examples

Old BBS network
Before the Internet became widespread, BBS communities were popular among computer enthusiasts.
He set up his own BBS so friends could share games and tips.
Many people used to connect to their local BBS to exchange messages and download files.
