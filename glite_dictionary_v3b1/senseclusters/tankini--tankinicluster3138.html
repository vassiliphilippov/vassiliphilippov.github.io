tankini

noun
two-piece swimsuit
3.7
Pronunciation: / tæŋˈkiːni / (AmE), / tæŋˈkiːni / (BrE)
a two-piece swimsuit for women, consisting of a tank top and a bikini bottom

Examples

A tankini swimsuit
She bought a new tankini for her summer vacation at the seaside.
Many women prefer a tankini because it offers more coverage than a bikini.
The store has a wide selection of tankinis in different colors and patterns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bikini - a two-piece swimsuit with a bra top and brief bottom
  • antonym: one-piece - a swimsuit made from a single piece of fabric covering the torso
