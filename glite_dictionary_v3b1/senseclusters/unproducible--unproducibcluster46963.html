unproducible

adjective
impossible to produce
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnprəˈduːsəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnprəˈdjuːsəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be produced, created, or brought into existence

Examples

An unproducible item
The original painting is unproducible because the artist never revealed their technique.
Some rare minerals are unproducible in a laboratory setting due to their unique natural conditions.
The evidence was declared unproducible after it was destroyed in the accident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impossible - not able to be done or achieved
  • antonym: producible - able to be produced or created
  • synonym: unattainable - not able to be reached or achieved
