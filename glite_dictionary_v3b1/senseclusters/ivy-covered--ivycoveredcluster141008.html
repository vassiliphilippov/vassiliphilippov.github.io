ivy-covered

adjective
covered with ivy
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪviˌkʌvɚd / (AmE), / ˈaɪviˌkʌvəd / (BrE)
(of a surface, especially a building or wall) covered with or overgrown by ivy.

Examples

Ivy-covered wall
Sunlight filtered softly through the leaves that clung to the ivy-covered wall.
The old university campus was dotted with ivy-covered halls and libraries.
They bought an ivy-covered cottage at the edge of the village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ivied - overgrown or covered with ivy
