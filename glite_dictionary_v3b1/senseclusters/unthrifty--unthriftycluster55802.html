unthrifty

adjective
wasteful; not thrifty
8.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈθrɪfti / (AmE), / ʌnˈθrɪfti / (BrE)
not careful with money or resources; wasteful or lacking in thrift

Examples

Unthrifty spending habits
His unthrifty spending habits made it hard for him to save any money.
The unthrifty use of resources led to shortages in the community.
She realized her unthrifty choices were affecting her long-term financial goals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extravagant - spending more than necessary; lacking restraint in use of resources
  • antonym: thrifty - using money and resources carefully and not wastefully
  • synonym: wasteful - using resources carelessly or extravagantly
