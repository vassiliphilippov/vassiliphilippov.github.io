mucho

adverb
very much (informal)
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːtʃoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmuːtʃəʊ / (BrE)
used informally to mean 'a lot' or 'very much', especially as a playful borrowing from Spanish

Examples

Thanks mucho
Thanks mucho for picking me up from the airport so late at night.
I appreciate your help mucho with organizing the event.
She said she liked the gift mucho and smiled brightly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: a little - to a small extent or degree
  • synonym: a lot - to a great extent or degree
  • synonym: very much - to a high degree
