Synonyms

  • vacant
    2.6 6.72/m
    vacant is used for an empty seat, job, or room that has no current person, while unoccupied covers many unused spaces.
  • empty
    1.0 76/m
    empty means having nothing inside or no belongings, while unoccupied more often describes a space or position with no people.
  • uninhabited
    4.0 1.03/m
    uninhabited describes a place where no one lives, often wild or deserted, while unoccupied can mean simply unused.
  • unpopulated
    4.8 0.30/m
    unpopulated refers to an area with no residents or settlements, while unoccupied can describe rooms, seats, or jobs too.
