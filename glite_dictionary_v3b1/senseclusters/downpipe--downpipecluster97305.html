downpipe

noun
rainwater drainage pipe
3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈdaʊn.paɪp/ (AmE), /ˈdaʊn.paɪp/ (BrE)
a pipe that carries rainwater from the roof of a building down to the ground or a drainage system

Examples

Rainwater downpipe
After the heavy rain, water poured out of the downpipe onto the driveway.
The plumber replaced the old downpipe to prevent leaks along the wall.
Leaves and debris can easily block a downpipe if it is not cleaned regularly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drainpipe - a pipe that carries waste water or rainwater away from a building
  • synonym: gutter - a channel at the edge of a roof for carrying away rainwater
