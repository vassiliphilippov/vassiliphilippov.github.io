parfum

noun
concentrated perfume
Pronunciation: / pɑːrˈfʌm / (AmE), / paːˈfœ̃ / (BrE)
a highly concentrated and long-lasting type of perfume

Examples

Luxury parfum
She prefers to wear a light parfum rather than a strong cologne.
The new parfum from the designer lasts all day with just one spray.
He bought an expensive parfum as a gift for his mother.
