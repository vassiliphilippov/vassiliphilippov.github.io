biradially

adverb
in a biradial manner
Pronunciation: / baɪˈreɪdiəli / (AmE), / baɪˈreɪdiəli / (BrE)
In a manner characterized by having two planes of symmetry; with biradial symmetry.

Examples

Split biradially in symmetry
Certain ctenophores split their body plan biradially as a result of evolutionary specialization.
Some sea anemones are organized biradially, which can be observed in their internal structure.
The jellyfish develops biradially symmetric tentacles during its growth stages.
