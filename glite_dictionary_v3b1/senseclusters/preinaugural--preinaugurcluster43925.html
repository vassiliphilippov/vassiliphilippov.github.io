preinaugural

adjective
before inauguration
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːɪˈnɔːɡjərəl / (AmE), / ˌpriːɪˈnɔːɡjʊərəl / (BrE)
occurring or existing before an official inauguration or ceremony

Examples

A preinaugural event
The city hosted several preinaugural celebrations in anticipation of the new mayor's swearing-in.
Journalists covered the preinaugural meetings held by the incoming administration.
Many guests arrived early to attend the preinaugural dinner before the official ceremony.
