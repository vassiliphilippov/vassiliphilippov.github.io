country people

noun
people from rural areas
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌntri ˌpipəl / (AmE), / ˈkʌntri ˌpiːpl / (BrE)
People who live in or come from rural areas, as opposed to urban or city dwellers; rustics.

Examples

Country people in villages
Many country people rely on farming as their main source of income.
She preferred the simple, honest ways of country people to the bustle of the city.
The country people gathered every Saturday at the village market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rustics - people from the countryside, considered unsophisticated
  • antonym: urbanites - people who live in a city
