country people

noun
people from rural areas
3.6 2.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌn.trɪ ˈpiː.pəl/ (AmE), /ˈkʌn.trɪ ˈpiː.pəl/ (BrE)
People who live in or come from rural areas, as opposed to urban or city dwellers; rustics.
