Nabatean

proper noun
ancient Arab people
Pronunciation: / ˌnæbəˈtiːən / (AmE), / ˌnæbəˈtiːən / (BrE)
a member of an ancient Arab people who lived in northern Arabia and the southern Levant, known for building Petra

Examples

Nabatean people
The Nabatean civilization is famous for building the city of Petra.
Archaeologists discovered ancient inscriptions left by the Nabatean people in the desert.
Many tourists visit Jordan to see the impressive ruins created by the Nabatean culture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Arab - a member of a Semitic people originally from the Arabian Peninsula
  • synonym: Petra - an ancient city in Jordan built by the Nabateans
