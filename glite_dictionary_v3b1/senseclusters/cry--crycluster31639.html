cry

noun
rallying slogan or catchphrase
6.9
Pronunciation: / kraɪ / (AmE), / kraɪ / (BrE)
a word or phrase that is used as a rallying call or slogan for a cause, movement, or campaign

Examples

A battle cry
"Equal rights for all" became the cry of a generation seeking justice.
The revolutionaries adopted "Freedom now!" as their cry during the protests.
Throughout the campaign, their cry of "Change!" echoed at rallies across the nation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catchphrase - a well-known sentence or phrase, typically one that is associated with a particular famous person
  • synonym: slogan - a short and striking or memorable phrase used in advertising or promotion
  • synonym: watchword - a word or phrase expressing a person's or group's guiding principle
