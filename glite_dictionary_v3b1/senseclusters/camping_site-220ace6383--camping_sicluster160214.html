camping site

noun
place for camping with tents or caravans
Pronunciation: / ˈkæmpɪŋ saɪt / (AmE), / ˈkæmpɪŋ saɪt / (BrE)
A place where people can set up tents or park caravans for camping, typically with basic facilities.

Examples

A camping site with tents
After a long hike, they were relieved to reach the designated camping site.
The camping site offered clean showers and a small shop for groceries.
We found a lovely camping site by the lake for our summer holiday.
