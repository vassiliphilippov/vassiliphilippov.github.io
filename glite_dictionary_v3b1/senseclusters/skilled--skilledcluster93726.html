skilled

adjective
having special knowledge or ability
Pronunciation: / skɪld / (AmE), / skɪld / (BrE)
having or showing the special knowledge, ability, or training needed to perform a particular activity well

Examples

Skilled workers
Finding skilled workers for the job took several weeks.
She is a skilled painter who specializes in portraits.
The surgery was a success thanks to a very skilled medical team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expert - having or involving great knowledge or skill
  • antonym: unskilled - not having or requiring special training or ability
