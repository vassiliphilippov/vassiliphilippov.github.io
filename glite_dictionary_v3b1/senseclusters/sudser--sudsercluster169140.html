sudser

noun
foaming cleaning product
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌdsər / (AmE), / ˈsʌdzə / (BrE)
a soap, detergent, or cleaning product that creates a lot of suds or foam when used with water

Examples

Dish sudser
She poured a generous amount of the new kitchen sudser onto the sponge to wash dishes.
The sudser made the bathroom smell fresh and filled the tub with bubbles.
He prefers a sudser that is gentle on his hands but tough on grease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detergent - a cleaning substance that removes dirt and grease
  • synonym: soap - a substance used for washing and cleaning
