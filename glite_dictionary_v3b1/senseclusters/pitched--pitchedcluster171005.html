pitched

adjective
set at a slant or angle
4.9
Pronunciation: / pɪtʃt / (AmE), / pɪtʃt / (BrE)
(of a roof or surface) set at a slant or angle rather than flat or level.

Examples

Pitched roof
A pitched driveway allows rainwater to drain easily.
The house had a traditional pitched roof covered with red tiles.
You must install special gutters for a pitched roof.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flat - having a level surface with no slope
