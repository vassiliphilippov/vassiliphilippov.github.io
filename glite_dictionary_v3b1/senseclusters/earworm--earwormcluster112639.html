earworm

noun
catchy tune in mind
Pronunciation: / ˈɪrˌwɜrm / (AmE), / ˈɪəˌwɜːm / (BrE)
a catchy song or tune that keeps repeating in your mind and is hard to forget

Examples

A catchy earworm song
That new commercial jingle is such an earworm that I can't stop singing it.
After hearing the song once, it became an earworm stuck in my head all day.
Some earworms can last for hours, making it hard to concentrate on anything else.
That new pop song is such an earworm that I keep singing it all day.
After hearing the commercial, the jingle became an earworm stuck in my head for hours.
She complained that the melody was a real earworm and she couldn't get rid of it.

  • synonym: obsession - an idea or thought that continually preoccupies the mind
