even-textured

adjective
of uniform or consistent texture
Pronunciation: / ˈiːvən ˈtɛkstʃərd / (AmE), / ˈiːvən ˈtɛkstʃəd / (BrE)
having a uniform or consistent texture throughout, without irregularities or roughness

Examples

Even-textured fabric
She prefers fabrics that are even-textured for making dresses, as they look more refined.
The artist praised the even-textured surface of the canvas, saying it made painting easier.
The cake turned out perfectly even-textured, with no lumps or air pockets.

  • synonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface
