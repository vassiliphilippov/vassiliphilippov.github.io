vibrance

noun
brightness and energy
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪbrəns / (AmE), / ˈvaɪbrəns / (BrE)
the quality of being bright, lively, or full of energy

Examples

City vibrance
The vibrance of the city at night made it feel alive and exciting.
She chose colors with great vibrance to decorate her new apartment.
Spring brings a renewed vibrance to the countryside after the long winter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dullness - the quality of lacking brightness or energy
  • synonym: vibrancy - the state of being full of energy and life
