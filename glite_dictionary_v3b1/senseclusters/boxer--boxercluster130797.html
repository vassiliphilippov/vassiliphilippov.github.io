boxer

noun
person who competes in boxing
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːksər / (AmE), / ˈbɒksə / (BrE)
a person who takes part in the sport of boxing, fighting with their fists according to set rules

Examples

Professional boxer
Several retired boxers attended the charity event to promote youth sports.
She admired the discipline and determination of the young boxer she saw in the gym.
The boxer trained intensively for months before his championship bout.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fighter - a person who fights, especially as a professional boxer or soldier
