lungless

adjective
without lungs
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌŋləs / (AmE), / ˈlʌŋləs / (BrE)
not having lungs or lacking lung organs

Examples

A lungless animal
Certain species of salamanders are completely lungless and rely on their skin for breathing.
The lungless amphibian was discovered in a remote rainforest region.
Scientists study lungless animals to understand alternative methods of respiration.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pulmonate - having lungs
