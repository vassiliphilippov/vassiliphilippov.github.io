Synonyms

  • frothy
    3.2 0.80/m
    frothy looks full of soft foam on top of liquids like beer or soap, while foamy can describe any surface with bubbles.
  • bubbly
    3.2 1.48/m
    bubbly has many rising gas bubbles, often from carbonation, while foamy means covered with soft or thick bubbles.
  • spumy
    9.9 0.01/m
    spumy describes foam that is thin and frothy and is a rare word, while foamy is common for any bubbly surface.
