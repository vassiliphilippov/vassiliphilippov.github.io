undetectable

adjective
impossible to detect
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈtɛktəbl̩ /; / ˌʌndɪˈtɛktəbl / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈtɛktəbl̩ /; / ˌʌndɪˈtɛktəbl / (BrE)
impossible or almost impossible to discover, notice, or detect

Examples

An undetectable smell
Her perfume was so light it was almost undetectable.
The differences between the two paintings are undetectable to the untrained eye.
The spy used technology to remain undetectable during his mission.
The drug left undetectable traces in her bloodstream after 24 hours.
The flaw in the system was undetectable to all but the most experienced engineers.
The spy wore a disguise that made him undetectable in the crowd.

  • synonym: invisible - impossible to see
