Vichy

proper noun
French town; WWII capital; mineral springs
Pronunciation: / ˈviːʃi / (AmE), / ˈviːʃi / (BrE)
a town in central France known for its hot mineral springs and as the capital of unoccupied France during World War II

Examples

Vichy spa town
During World War II, Vichy served as the administrative center of unoccupied France.
Many tourists visit Vichy to experience its famous mineral springs and spas.
The Vichy government collaborated with Nazi Germany during the war.
