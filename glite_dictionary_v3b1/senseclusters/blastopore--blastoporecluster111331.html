blastopore

noun
opening formed in early embryo development
8.4 9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈblæstəˌpɔr / (AmE), / ˈblæstəpɔː / (BrE)
the opening that forms during early embryonic development, leading into the archenteron (the primitive gut) of an embryo
