tinned meat

noun
meat preserved in a can or tin
Pronunciation: / tɪnd ˈmit / (AmE), / tɪnd ˈmiːt / (BrE)
meat that has been preserved and sealed in a tin or can for long-term storage.

Examples

A can of tinned meat
During the hike, they survived on bread and tinned meat for several days.
We always keep a few cans of tinned meat in the pantry for emergencies.
When fresh food ran out, tinned meat became their main source of protein.

  • synonym: canned meat - meat preserved in a can; North American usage
