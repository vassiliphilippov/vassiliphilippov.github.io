vinculum

noun
connecting line or band
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪŋkjələm / (AmE), / ˈvɪŋkjʊləm / (BrE)
a line or band that connects or binds things, especially a horizontal line in mathematics or a band in anatomy

Examples

Anatomy vinculum
In anatomy, a vinculum is a small band of tissue connecting a tendon to a bone.
The teacher explained that the vinculum in a fraction separates the numerator from the denominator.
Surgeons must be careful not to damage the vinculum during hand operations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: band - a flat, thin strip or loop used to bind or connect
  • synonym: bar - a straight line or strip, often used in mathematics
