listenable

adjective
easy to listen to
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪsənəbl / (AmE), / ˈlɪsənəbl / (BrE)
pleasant or easy to listen to (especially music or audio)

Examples

A listenable song
The new album is very listenable and appeals to a wide audience.
Her voice is so listenable that I could hear her sing all day.
This podcast is both informative and highly listenable for long drives.
The new album is very listenable and has received positive reviews from critics.
Despite the background noise, her speech was still listenable to the audience.
He prefers music that is listenable rather than experimental or harsh.
