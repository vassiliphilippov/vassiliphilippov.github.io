undervest

noun
upper body underwear
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌvɛst / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌvɛst / (BrE)
a piece of underwear worn on the upper body beneath a shirt or other clothing

Examples

A clean undervest
He put on a clean undervest before getting dressed for work.
During winter, wearing an undervest helps keep you warm under your clothes.
She bought a pack of cotton undervests for her children to wear to school.

Related Terms
