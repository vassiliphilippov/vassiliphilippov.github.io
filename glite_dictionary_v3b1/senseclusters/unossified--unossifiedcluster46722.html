unossified

adjective
not rigid or fixed
9.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɑsəˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɒsɪfaɪd / (BrE)
not rigid, fixed, or fully developed; still flexible or open to change, often used figuratively.

Examples

Unossified ideas
The organization remained unossified, allowing new ideas to shape its direction.
Her teaching methods are still unossified, adapting to the needs of each class.
A truly unossified approach can help a team respond quickly to unexpected challenges.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rigid - not able to change or adapt; inflexible
