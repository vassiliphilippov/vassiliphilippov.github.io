txt

verb
to send a message
6.2
Pronunciation: / tɛkst / (AmE), / tɛkst / (BrE)
to send someone a short written message electronically, especially by mobile phone

Examples

To txt a friend
I'll txt you the address after I finish the meeting.
She prefers to txt her friends instead of calling them.
Can you txt me when you arrive at the station?
