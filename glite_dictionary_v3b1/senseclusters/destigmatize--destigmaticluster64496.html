destigmatize

verb
to remove stigma
7.5
Pronunciation: / diˈstɪɡməˌtaɪz / (AmE), / diˈstɪɡmətaɪz / (BrE)
to remove the negative associations, shame, or social disapproval attached to something

Examples

To destigmatize mental illness
The organization works to destigmatize mental illness through education and public awareness campaigns.
Open conversations can help destigmatize seeking therapy for emotional problems.
Many activists are trying to destigmatize addiction by sharing personal stories and promoting understanding.
Many organizations work to destigmatize mental health issues through education and open discussion.
The documentary helped destigmatize addiction by sharing real stories of recovery.
Efforts to destigmatize seeking therapy have made it easier for people to ask for help.

Related Terms

  • synonym: normalize - to make something accepted as normal or standard
  • antonym: stigmatize - to mark something with shame or disapproval
