stopping point

noun
the temporal end; conclusion
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːpɪŋ pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈstɒpɪŋ pɔɪnt / (BrE)
the time or moment when something comes to an end; the conclusion of a period, process, or event.

Examples

A stopping point in time
Each segment of the interview has a stopping point to give participants a break.
It's important to set a clear stopping point when working on long projects.
We agreed that midnight would be the stopping point for the negotiations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: end - the final point or conclusion of something
  • antonym: start - the beginning or initial point of something
