Examples

Sustain the weight

Synonyms

  • hold up
    3.4 8.23/m

  • prop up
    3.6 4.26/m
    prop up means place something under or against an object to stop it falling, while sustain means support it continuously.
  • hold
    1.0 184/m
    hold means grasp or carry something in your hands or arms, while sustain focuses on supporting weight to prevent collapse.
