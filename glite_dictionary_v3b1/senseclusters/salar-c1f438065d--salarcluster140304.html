Salar

proper noun
Turkic ethnic group
Pronunciation: / sɑːˈlɑːr / (AmE), / sɑːˈlɑː / (BrE)
a member of the Salar people, a Turkic ethnic group living mainly in Qinghai and Gansu provinces in China

Examples

A Salar elder
A Salar elder described traditional songs and ceremonies from his mountain village to the visiting students.
The Salar have preserved their unique language and customs for centuries.
Many Salar communities celebrate important festivals with music and dance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Han - a member of the largest ethnic group in China
  • synonym: Uyghur - a member of a Turkic ethnic group primarily living in Xinjiang, China
