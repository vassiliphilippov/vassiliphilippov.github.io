brightish

adjective
somewhat bright
Pronunciation: / ˈbraɪtɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbraɪtɪʃ / (BrE)
somewhat bright in color, light, or appearance; moderately cheerful or lively

Examples

Brightish color
The living room was painted a brightish yellow that made it feel welcoming.
Her mood was brightish despite the cloudy weather outside.
He wore a brightish shirt that stood out in the crowd.
The curtains let in a brightish light that made the room feel cheerful.
She wore a brightish blue dress that stood out at the party.
The sky was a brightish gray, hinting at sunshine behind the clouds.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dull - lacking brightness or liveliness
