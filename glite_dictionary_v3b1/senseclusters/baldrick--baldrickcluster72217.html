baldrick

noun
belt worn across body for sword or bugle
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔl.drɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɔːl.drɪk / (BrE)
a wide, ornamented belt worn over one shoulder and across the chest to support a sword or bugle at the opposite hip

Examples

A baldrick belt
In many old paintings, a baldrick can be seen slung across the chest of soldiers.
She adjusted the baldrick on the statue to make the display more historically accurate.
The knight wore a richly decorated baldrick to carry his sword during the ceremony.
