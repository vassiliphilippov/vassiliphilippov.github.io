nightfall

noun
the time when night begins
2.1 3.05/m
Pronunciation: / Ààna…™tÀåf…îl / (AmE), / Ààna…™tÀåf…îÀêl / (BrE)
the time in the evening when it becomes dark and night begins, just after sunset

Examples

After nightfall comes darkness
By nightfall, the temperature had dropped and the city lights were twinkling.
They set up their tents quickly as nightfall approached in the mountains.
We should try to finish the hike before nightfall so we don't get lost in the dark.

Synonyms

  • twilight
    2.4 4.85/m
    twilight is the soft light after sunset that slowly fades, while nightfall is the exact point when it becomes dark and night starts.
  • sundown
    1.2 2.17/m
    sundown is the time the sun drops below the horizon, while nightfall is when it grows dark and true night begins.
