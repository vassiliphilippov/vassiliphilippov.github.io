out of touch

adjective
unaware, or lacking contact or communication
Pronunciation: / aʊt əv tʌtʃ / (AmE), / aʊt əv tʌtʃ / (BrE)
not aware of current issues, feelings, or situations, or lacking contact or communication with people or events

Examples

Out of touch with news
If you don't read the news regularly, you can quickly become out of touch with world events.
Many believe that politicians are out of touch with the concerns of ordinary people.
Since leaving university, I've felt completely out of touch with my old friends.

Related Terms

  • antonym: in touch - having or maintaining communication or awareness
