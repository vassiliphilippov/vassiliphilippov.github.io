hydropic

adjective
swollen with fluid
Pronunciation: / haɪˈdrɑpɪk / (AmE), / hɪˈdrɒpɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by swelling caused by excess fluid in body tissues

Examples

Hydropic tissue swelling
The doctor observed hydropic changes in the patient's kidney tissue under the microscope.
Certain diseases can cause cells to become hydropic due to fluid accumulation.
The report described the liver as having a hydropic appearance after the infection.
