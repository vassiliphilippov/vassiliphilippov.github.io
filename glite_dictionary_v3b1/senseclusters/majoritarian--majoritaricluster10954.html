majoritarian

adjective
majority rule principle
7.7
Pronunciation: /mə.dʒɔːr.ɪ.ˈter.i.ən/ /; / /mə.dʒə.rɪ.ˈter.i.ən/ (AmE), /mə.dʒɒr.ɪ.ˈteə.ri.ən/ /; / /mə.dʒɒr.ɪ.ˈter.i.ən/ (BrE)
relating to or based on the principle that the majority's decision should prevail
