upstroke

noun
an upward stroke or movement
Pronunciation: / ˈʌp.stroʊk / (AmE), / ˈʌp.strəʊk / (BrE)
a stroke or movement that is made in an upward direction, especially in writing or playing a musical instrument

Examples

A gentle upstroke
He used a gentle upstroke on the guitar to create a softer sound.
The calligrapher's upstroke was delicate and precise, contrasting with the bold downstroke.
While writing, pay attention to each upstroke and try to keep them even.

Related Terms

  • antonym: downstroke - a stroke made in a downward direction
