monophasic

adjective
single-phase
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑnəˈfeɪsɪk /; / ˌmɑːnəˈfeɪsɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəˈfeɪsɪk / (BrE)
having or involving only one phase or stage, especially in medication or a process

Examples

This medication is administered in a monophasic dose rather than in multiple stages.
