medalist

noun
person who has won a medal
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɛdəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who has been awarded a medal, especially in a sporting competition

Examples

Olympic medalist
As a proud medalist, he gave an inspiring speech to young athletes.
She became a three-time medalist at the World Championships.
The Olympic gold medalist was greeted by cheering fans at the airport.
He became a gold medallist after his outstanding performance in the finals.
Several former medallists attended the ceremony to present awards to this year's winners.
The Olympic medallist was greeted with cheers on her return home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: champion - person who wins first place in a competition
  • antonym: loser - a person who does not win
  • synonym: winner - a person who wins something
