adjective

Examples

    4.8 0.82/m
    glitzy is glittery and glamorous but often surface-level, while jazzy feels lively and stylish for clothes or looks.
  • showy
    3.2 0.67/m
    showy attracts attention by being elaborate or overly decorative, while jazzy feels more playful and fashionably bright for clothing.
  • flashy
