Diwali

proper noun
Hindu festival of lights
4.8
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈwɑː.liː/ /; / /dɪ.ˈvɑː.liː/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈwɑː.liː/ /; / /dɪ.ˈvɑː.liː/ (BrE)
an important Hindu festival of lights celebrated in autumn, symbolizing the victory of light over darkness and marking the new year in some Indian communities

Examples

Diwali festival lights
Families decorate their homes with lamps and candles during Diwali to celebrate the victory of light over darkness.
Many people exchange gifts and sweets with friends and relatives on Diwali.
Fireworks light up the night sky as communities come together to mark Diwali each year.
During Diwali, families light oil lamps and set off fireworks to celebrate.
Many people clean and decorate their homes before Diwali begins.
Shops and streets are filled with colorful lights and sweets for Diwali.
