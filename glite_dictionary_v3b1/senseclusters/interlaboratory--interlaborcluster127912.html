interlaboratory

adjective
between laboratories
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈlæbrəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈlæbrətəri / (BrE)
involving or conducted between two or more laboratories

Examples

Interlaboratory comparison
The interlaboratory comparison ensured that results were reliable across different research centers.
An interlaboratory study was organized to validate the new testing method.
They published the findings of their interlaboratory experiment in a scientific journal.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intralaboratory - occurring within a single laboratory
