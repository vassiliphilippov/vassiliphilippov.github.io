Muenster

noun
semisoft pale-yellow cheese
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌnstər / (AmE), / ˈmʌnstə / (BrE)
A semisoft, pale-yellow cheese with a mild flavor, commonly made from cow's milk.

Examples

Slices of Muenster cheese
I put slices of Muenster on my sandwich for a creamy texture.
She prefers Muenster cheese because of its mild flavor.
The cheeseboard featured Muenster alongside brie and gouda.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheddar - a hard pale-yellow to sharp-tasting cheese
