musk rose

noun
a Mediterranean rose with musky fragrance
5.3 5.8
Pronunciation: /mʌsk roʊz/ (AmE), /mʌsk rəʊz/ (BrE)
a species of rose native to the Mediterranean, with curved or climbing branches and loose clusters of musky-scented flowers
