divide

verb
to perform a division (mathematics)
3.9
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvaɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪd / (BrE)
to perform a mathematical operation in which a number is separated into equal parts or groups; to find out how many times one number is contained in another

Examples

Can you divide 48 by 6 for me?
If you divide 100 by 5, the answer is 20.
The teacher asked the students to divide twelve by four.
