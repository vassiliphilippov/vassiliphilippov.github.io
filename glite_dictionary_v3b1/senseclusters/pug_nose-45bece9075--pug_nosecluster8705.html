pug nose

noun
short, upturned nose
2.5
Pronunciation: / pʌɡ ˈnoʊz / (AmE), / pʌɡ ˈnəʊz / (BrE)
A nose that is short and flattened, with a tip that turns upward.

Examples

A pug nose
He wrinkled his pug nose in distaste at the smell.
She was known for her distinctive pug nose and lively eyes.
The actor’s pug nose gave him a boyish charm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: snub nose - a short, turned-up nose
