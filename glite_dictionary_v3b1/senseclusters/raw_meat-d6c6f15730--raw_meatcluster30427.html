raw meat

noun
uncooked meat
Pronunciation: / rɔː mit / (AmE), / rɔː miːt / (BrE)
meat that has not been cooked; uncooked meat.

Examples

Raw meat uncooked
It is dangerous to eat raw meat because of the risk of foodborne illness.
Sushi often contains slices of raw meat such as fish or beef.
The chef used only fresh raw meat to prepare the tartare.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cooked meat - meat that has been heated until done
