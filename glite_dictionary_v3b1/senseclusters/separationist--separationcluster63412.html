separationist

noun
person who supports separation from a larger group
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛpəˈreɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌsɛpəˈreɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who advocates or supports the separation of a group from a larger entity, such as a nation or an established church

Examples

The separationist argued for independence from the central government.
