flush

adverb
level or even with a surface
9.5
Pronunciation: / flʌʃ / (AmE), / flʌʃ / (BrE)
even or level with another surface; in the same plane (adverb)

The ball struck the wall flush and bounced straight back.
The boxer was knocked out after being hit flush on the chin.
