along

adverb
progressing; moving forward
Pronunciation: / əˈlɔŋ / (AmE), / əˈlɒŋ / (BrE)
moving forward or progressing to a more advanced stage, or continuing onward in a direction

Examples

Well along in progress
She's well along in her studies and will graduate next year.
The project is coming along nicely and should be finished soon.
Their plans are moving along more quickly than expected.
Please keep along as we walk through the museum.
The hikers continued along despite the rain.
The parade moved along the main street, cheered by the crowd.

Related Terms

  • synonym: onward - moving forward; continuing in a forward direction
