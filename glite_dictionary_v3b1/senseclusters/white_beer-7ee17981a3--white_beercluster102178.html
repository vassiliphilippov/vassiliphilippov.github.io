white beer

noun
a pale, wheat-based beer
Pronunciation: / waɪt bɪr / (AmE), / waɪt bɪə / (BrE)
a pale, cloudy beer made from wheat and fermented with top-fermenting yeast, often effervescent

Examples

A pint of white beer
He ordered a pint of white beer to pair with his seafood platter.
On hot summer days, many people enjoy a chilled glass of white beer.
The brewery is famous for its signature white beer, which is light and slightly fruity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lager - a type of beer fermented at low temperatures
  • synonym: wheat beer - a beer brewed with a significant proportion of wheat
