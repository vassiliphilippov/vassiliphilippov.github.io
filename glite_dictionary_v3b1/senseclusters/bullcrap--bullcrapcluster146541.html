bullcrap

verb
to deceive with nonsense
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈbʊl.kræp/ (AmE), /ˈbʊl.kræp/ (BrE)
to try to deceive or mislead someone by saying things that are false, exaggerated, or nonsensical

Examples

To bullcrap your boss
Don't try to bullcrap me—I know exactly what happened.
He was clearly bullcrapping when he claimed he had met the president.
She tried to bullcrap her way out of trouble by making up a story.

Related Terms

  • antonym: honest - telling the truth and not lying or deceiving
  • synonym: lie - to say something that is not true
  • synonym: mislead - to cause someone to have a wrong idea or impression
