four-flusher

noun
person who bluffs or pretends
4.9 9.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfɔːr.flʌ.ʃər/ /; / /ˈfɔːr.flʊ.ʃər/ (AmE), /ˈfɔː.flʌ.ʃə/ /; / /ˈfɔː.flʊ.ʃə/ (BrE)
