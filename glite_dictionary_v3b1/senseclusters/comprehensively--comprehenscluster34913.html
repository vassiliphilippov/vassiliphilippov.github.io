comprehensively

adverb
in an all-inclusive manner
1.3 4.0
Pronunciation: / kəmˈprɛhənsɪvli / (AmE), / kəmˈprɛhənsɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that includes or deals with all or nearly all elements or aspects of something.

Examples

To comprehensively assess risks
Our team assessed the risks comprehensively before making any decisions.
She updated the manual comprehensively to cover recent changes in technology.
The report examines the issue comprehensively, leaving no aspect unexplored.

  • synonym: thoroughly - completely and with great attention to detail
