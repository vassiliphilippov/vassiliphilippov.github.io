paddleboat

verb
to use a pedal boat
Pronunciation: / ˈpædəlˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈpædəlˌbəʊt / (BrE)
to travel in or operate a small boat powered by pedals or paddles, especially for recreation.

Examples

To paddleboat on water
Every summer, families paddleboat across the calm lake in the city park.
We decided to paddleboat along the river to enjoy the sunset together.
Tourists often paddleboat on the pond to see the ducks up close.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pedal - to move a vehicle by pushing pedals with your feet
  • synonym: row - to move a boat using oars
