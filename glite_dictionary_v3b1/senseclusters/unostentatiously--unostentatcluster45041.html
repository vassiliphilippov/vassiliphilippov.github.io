unostentatiously

adverb
without attracting attention
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌɑːstənˈteɪʃəsli / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌɒstənˈteɪʃəsli / (BrE)
in a way that does not attract attention or display showiness

Examples

Unostentatiously donate money
He donated to the charity unostentatiously, never seeking public recognition for his generosity.
The artist lived unostentatiously, preferring a quiet life away from the spotlight.
She entered the room unostentatiously, hoping not to interrupt the ongoing discussion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discreetly - in a careful and unobtrusive way
  • synonym: modestly - in a way that is not intended to attract attention
  • antonym: ostentatiously - in a way that is showy or meant to attract notice
