portfolio

noun
collection of creative works for display
Pronunciation: / pɔrˈtfoʊlioʊ / (AmE), / pɔːtˈfəʊliəʊ / (BrE)
A collection of examples of a person's creative work, compiled to demonstrate their ability and shown to potential employers or clients.

Examples

Artist's portfolio
Photographers often update their portfolio to showcase their latest projects.
She spent weeks assembling her portfolio before applying to art school.
The designer's portfolio impressed the interviewers with its variety and creativity.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: portfolio career - a career constructed from multiple jobs or projects, rather than a single full-time role
  • synonym: work sample - an individual example of someone's work given for evaluation
