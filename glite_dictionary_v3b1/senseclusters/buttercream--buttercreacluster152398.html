buttercream

noun
sweet cake icing
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌt̬ɚˌkriːm / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəˌkriːm / (BrE)
a sweet, creamy icing made from butter and sugar, often used to decorate cakes and pastries

Examples

Buttercream frosting
She decorated the cupcakes with swirls of buttercream.
The birthday cake was covered in a thick layer of chocolate buttercream.
He learned how to make vanilla buttercream for his baking class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frosting - a sweet topping for cakes and pastries
  • antonym: ganache - a rich chocolate icing or filling
