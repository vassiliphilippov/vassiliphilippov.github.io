yes man

noun
a person who always agrees (often with a superior)
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛs ˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈjɛs ˌmæn / (BrE)
a person who always agrees with those in authority, often to gain approval or avoid conflict, rather than offering honest opinions

Examples

A yes man agrees
He's a classic yes man, never challenging the boss's decisions.
Many companies discourage hiring yes men to promote a culture of innovation.
The new manager doesn't want a yes man; she values honest feedback from her team.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rebel - a person who opposes or resists authority
  • synonym: sycophant - a person who acts obsequiously toward someone to gain advantage
  • synonym: toady - a person who flatters or acts subservient to someone important
