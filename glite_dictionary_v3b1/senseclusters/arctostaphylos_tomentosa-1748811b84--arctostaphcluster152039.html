Arctostaphylos tomentosa

noun
a Californian woolly-twigged shrub
Pronunciation: / ɑːrktoʊˈstæfɪloʊs toʊˈmɛntoʊsə / (AmE), / ɑːktəˈstæfɪləs təʊˈmɛntəʊsə / (BrE)
an erect, openly branched shrub native to California, whose young twigs are covered with woolly hairs
