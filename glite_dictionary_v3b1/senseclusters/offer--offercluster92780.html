Synonyms

  • provide
    1.5 478/m
    provide is to give or supply what is needed, while offer is to present something for someone to accept or refuse.
  • supply
    1.6 37/m
    supply means giving items to meet a need or demand, while offer means presenting something for someone to accept.
  • extend
    3.5 12/m
    extend is used for offering help, invitations, or greetings in a polite way, while offer is broader and neutral.
  • proffer
    8.1 2.28/m
    proffer means hold out something for acceptance in formal or old-fashioned speech, while offer is the common, neutral verb people use.
  • render
    3.9 4.92/m
    render often means give a service, help, or payment in a formal setting, while offer is more general everyday presenting.
  • furnish
    3.7 9.62/m
    furnish usually means supply a place or person with needed things or furniture, while offer means present something for choice.
