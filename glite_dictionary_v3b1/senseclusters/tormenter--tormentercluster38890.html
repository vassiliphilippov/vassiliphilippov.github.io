tormenter

noun
someone who causes pain or distress
Pronunciation: / tɔrˈmɛntər / (AmE), / tɔːˈmɛntə / (BrE)
a person who deliberately causes pain or distress to another

Examples

A cruel tormenter
Childhood memories of a cruel tormenter can linger for years.
He finally confronted his longtime tormenter and demanded an apology.
The playground tormenter made recess miserable for several children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bully - a person who habitually seeks to harm or intimidate those whom they perceive as vulnerable
  • antonym: victim - a person harmed or tormented by others
