promotion system

noun
system for advancing participants to higher status
Pronunciation: / prəˈmoʊʃən ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / prəˈməʊʃən ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
a set of rules or procedures by which individuals are advanced to higher positions or ranks within an organization, institution, or competition

Examples

Sports promotion system
Many sports leagues operate with a promotion system that rewards successful teams with advancement to higher divisions.
The company revised its promotion system to ensure more transparency and fairness.
The university's promotion system is based on academic achievement and years of service.
