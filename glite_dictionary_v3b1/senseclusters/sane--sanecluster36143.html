sane

adjective
having sound judgment
Pronunciation: / seɪn / (AmE), / seɪn / (BrE)
having or showing good judgment; sensible and reasonable

Examples

A sane decision
After a heated argument, we needed a sane voice to calm things down.
Her decision to leave the company was considered quite sane by her colleagues.
It's important to have a sane approach to managing stress at work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: irrational - not logical or reasonable
  • synonym: rational - based on or in accordance with reason or logic
  • synonym: sensible - chosen in accordance with wisdom or prudence
