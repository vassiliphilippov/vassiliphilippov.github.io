presser

noun
tool for pressing
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛsər / (AmE), / ˈprɛsə / (BrE)
a device or tool used to apply pressure to something, often to flatten or shape materials

Examples

A garlic presser
She used a presser to flatten the fabric before sewing.
The chef reached for the garlic presser to crush the cloves quickly.
A book presser helps keep pages flat while they dry after restoration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flattener - a tool used to make something flat
  • synonym: press - a machine or tool that applies pressure to something
