Synonyms

    4.1 12/m
    death penalty is the common modern term used in news and speech, while capital punishment is the formal legal phrase for the same punishment.
  • death sentence
    5.2 2.31/m

  • execution
    6.5 13/m
    execution is the act of carrying out a condemned person's death, while capital punishment is the legal system that allows that killing.
