parmigiana

noun
Italian baked dish
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːrmɪˈʒɑːnə / (AmE), / ˌpɑːmɪˈʒɑːnə / (BrE)
an Italian dish made with layers of breaded meat or vegetables, tomato sauce, and cheese, baked until golden

Examples

Eggplant parmigiana
For dinner, she made a delicious eggplant parmigiana with fresh basil and mozzarella.
The restaurant's chicken parmigiana is served with a side of spaghetti.
He learned to cook parmigiana from his grandmother's traditional Italian recipe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: casserole - a baked dish with mixed ingredients, often with a topping
  • synonym: lasagna - an Italian baked dish with layers of pasta, sauce, and cheese
