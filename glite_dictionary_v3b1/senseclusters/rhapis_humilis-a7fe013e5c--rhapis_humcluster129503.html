Rhapis humilis

noun
species of slender-stemmed Chinese lady palm
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈræpɪs hjuːˈmɪlɪs / (AmE), / ˈræpɪs hjuːˈmɪlɪs / (BrE)
A species of Chinese lady palm distinguished by its slender stems and finer sheath fibers compared to Rhapis excelsa.
