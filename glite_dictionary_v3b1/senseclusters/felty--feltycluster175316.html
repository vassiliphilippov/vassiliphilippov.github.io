felty

adjective
felt-like texture
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛlti / (AmE), / ˈfɛlti / (BrE)
having a texture or appearance similar to felt, often soft and slightly fuzzy

Examples

Felty surface
The plant's leaves have a felty surface that feels soft to the touch.
She wore a felty hat that kept her warm during the winter.
The kitten's fur was so felty that everyone wanted to pet it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface
