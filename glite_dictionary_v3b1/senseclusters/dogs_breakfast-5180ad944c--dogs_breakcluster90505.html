dog's breakfast

noun
a complete mess; a botched job
Pronunciation: / dɔɡz ˈbrɛkfəst /; / dɔɡz ˈbrɛkfɪst / (AmE), / dɒɡz ˈbrɛkfəst / (BrE)
a situation or job that is very messy, confused, or done very badly

Examples

A dog's breakfast situation
She tried to fix the leaking tap but made a complete dog's breakfast of it.
That report was a dog's breakfast—nothing was clear or organized.
The new manager turned the simple project into a dog's breakfast within days.
After the meeting, the agenda was still a complete dog's breakfast.
His attempt to fix the engine made things even more of a dog's breakfast.
The new software rollout turned into a dog's breakfast due to lack of preparation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dog's dinner - something messy or badly executed (variant idiom)
  • synonym: shambles - a state of total disorder
  • antonym: success - the opposite of a botched or messy job
