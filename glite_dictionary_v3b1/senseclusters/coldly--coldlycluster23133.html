coldly

adverb
in an unemotional or unfriendly way
1.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈkoʊld.liː/ (AmE), /ˈkəʊld.liː/ (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of warmth, emotion, or friendliness.

Examples

To coldly refuse
Although invited to join, she coldly refused and walked away.
He coldly dismissed their concerns during the meeting.
She replied coldly, making it clear she had no interest in further discussion.
