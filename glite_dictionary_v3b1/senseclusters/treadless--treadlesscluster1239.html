treadless

adjective
untouched by footsteps
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛdləs / (AmE), / ˈtrɛdləs / (BrE)
not marked or worn by footsteps; untouched or unused by people walking on it

Examples

Treadless path
The hikers were excited to find a treadless trail deep in the forest.
After the snowfall, the field remained treadless until the children came out to play.
She preferred the treadless sand dunes, untouched by any footprints.

Related Terms

  • synonym: untrodden - not walked on or trampled by people
  • antonym: worn - damaged or changed by frequent use or movement
