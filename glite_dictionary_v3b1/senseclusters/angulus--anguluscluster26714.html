angulus

noun
anatomical angle
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡjələs / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡjʊləs / (BrE)
an anatomical angle or corner, especially referring to a specific part of a bone or organ in medical contexts

Examples

Stomach angulus
The surgeon carefully examined the angulus of the stomach for any abnormalities.
A fracture was detected at the angulus of the patient's jawbone.
The radiologist pointed out a shadow near the angulus of the scapula on the X-ray.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angle - the space between two intersecting lines or surfaces
  • synonym: corner - the point where two lines or surfaces meet
