snow eater

noun
warm wind melting snow in Rockies
6.8
Pronunciation: / snoʊ ˈiːtər / (AmE), / snəʊ ˈiːtə / (BrE)
A warm, dry wind that blows down the eastern slopes of the Rocky Mountains, rapidly melting snow.

Examples

A snow eater wind
After weeks of winter, a sudden snow eater brought spring-like temperatures to the valley.
Farmers welcomed the snow eater as it thawed the fields earlier than expected.
The arrival of a snow eater can transform a landscape overnight, leaving little trace of the previous snowfall.
