Dard

noun
Indic languages of the Kashmir and Afghan region
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɑrd / (AmE), / dɑːd / (BrE)
A group of related Indic languages spoken in Kashmir, eastern Afghanistan, and northern Pakistan.

Examples

Dard language group
He grew up speaking a dialect of Dard in a remote mountain village.
Linguists have studied the grammar of Dard to trace the history of Indo-Aryan languages.
Many communities in northern Pakistan communicate using different varieties of Dard languages.
