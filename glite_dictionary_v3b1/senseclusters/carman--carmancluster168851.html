carman

noun
cart or wagon driver
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrmən / (AmE), / ˈkɑːmən / (BrE)
a person who drives or works with carts or wagons, especially for transporting goods in the past

Examples

A carman with a cart
In Victorian London, a carman would deliver coal to homes using a horse-drawn wagon.
The museum displayed the uniform once worn by a nineteenth-century carman.
Before trucks became common, a carman played a vital role in city transport.
