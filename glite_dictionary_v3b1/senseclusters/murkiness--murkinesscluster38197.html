murkiness

noun
lack of clarity or transparency (in information)
Pronunciation: / c8m5dd0kin59s / (AmE), / c8m5cd0kin59s / (BrE)
the quality of being unclear, opaque, or difficult to understand in information, communication, or decision-making; lack of transparency.

Examples

Murkiness of information
The murkiness of the official report left citizens uncertain about the government's actions.
Investors complained about the murkiness surrounding the company's quarterly earnings and guidance.
We criticized the murkiness in his explanation because it hid important details we needed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clarity - the quality of being clear and easy to understand; opposite of murkiness
  • synonym: obscurity - the state of being unclear or not well known
  • synonym: opacity - the quality of being difficult to understand or see through; lack of transparency
