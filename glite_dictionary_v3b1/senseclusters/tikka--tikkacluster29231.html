tikka

noun
forehead mark
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪkə / (AmE), / ˈtɪkə / (BrE)
a mark of colored powder or paste placed on the forehead for religious or decorative purposes, especially in South Asian cultures

Examples

A red tikka on the forehead
She applied a red tikka to her forehead before the festival began.
The priest placed a tikka on each devotee as part of the ceremony.
Wearing a tikka is a common tradition during many Hindu celebrations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bindi - a decorative mark worn on the forehead by Hindu women
