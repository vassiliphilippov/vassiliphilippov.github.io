hyaluronic

adjective
containing hyaluronic acid
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪələˈrɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪələˈrɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or containing hyaluronic acid, a substance naturally found in connective tissues and skin

Examples

Hyaluronic acid serum
The serum contains hyaluronic compounds to help keep your skin hydrated.
Doctors often use hyaluronic injections to treat joint pain in arthritis patients.
Many beauty products advertise their hyaluronic properties for improved skin moisture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hyaluronic acid - a substance found in connective tissues and skin that retains moisture
