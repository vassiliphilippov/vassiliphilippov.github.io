digital display

noun
device showing electronic characters or numbers
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪdʒɪtəl dɪˈspleɪ / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒɪtəl dɪˈspleɪ / (BrE)
a device that shows information using characters such as numbers or letters, typically on an electronic screen

Examples

The clock features a bright digital display that's easy to read, even in the dark.
