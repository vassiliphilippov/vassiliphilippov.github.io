frosty relationship

noun
cold or unfriendly relationship
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɑsti rɪˌleɪʃənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈfrɒsti rɪˌleɪʃənʃɪp / (BrE)
a relationship characterized by a lack of warmth or friendliness, often showing signs of coldness or tension

Examples

A frosty relationship
Despite working together daily, their frosty relationship never really improved.
The team's continued failures led to a frosty relationship between the coach and players.
There was a frosty relationship between the two colleagues after the argument.

Related Terms

  • antonym: amicable relationship - a relationship characterized by friendliness or goodwill
  • degree higher: hostile relationship - a relationship marked by open enmity or aggression
