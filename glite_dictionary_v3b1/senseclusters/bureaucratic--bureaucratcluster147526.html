bureaucratic

adjective
relating to bureaucracy or bureaucrats
2.9
Pronunciation: / bjʊˌroʊˈkrætɪk / (AmE), / bjʊəˌrəˈkrætɪk / (BrE)
Relating to the business of running an organization or government, often with excessive rules and red tape.

Examples

Bureaucratic rules
He criticized the government's bureaucratic regulations as unnecessarily complex and outdated.
Many employees were frustrated by the bureaucratic approach to solving simple problems.
The company's bureaucratic procedures slowed down the decision-making process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: administrative - relating to the running of a business, organization, etc.
  • antonym: efficient - being effective without wasting time or effort
