sponge cloth

noun
soft, porous cleaning fabric
3.4
Pronunciation: / spʌndʒ klɔθ / (AmE), / spʌndʒ klɒθ / (BrE)
a soft, porous fabric, often with a loose honeycomb weave, used especially for cleaning or wiping surfaces

Examples

A sponge cloth for cleaning
A sponge cloth works well for absorbing water and drying dishes.
Make sure to rinse out the sponge cloth after cleaning the bathroom sink.
She used a sponge cloth to wipe the spilled juice off the kitchen counter.
