nig

noun
offensive racial slur
Pronunciation: / nɪɡ / (AmE), / nɪɡ / (BrE)
an extremely offensive and derogatory term used as a racial slur; do not use this word

Examples

The word nig
The use of the word nig is widely condemned and considered deeply offensive.

Many organizations have banned the use of nig in all communications.
