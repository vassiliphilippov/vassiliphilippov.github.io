revising

noun
editing or rewriting process
1.4
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈvaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of editing or rewriting something to improve its content or clarity

Examples

Good revising process
Good revising is essential for producing clear and effective writing.
She spent hours revising her essay before the deadline.
The teacher emphasized the importance of careful revising during the writing process.
