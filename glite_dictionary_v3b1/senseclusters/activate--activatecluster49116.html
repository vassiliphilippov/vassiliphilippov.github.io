activate

verb
aerate sewage to aid decomposition
Pronunciation: / ˈæk.təˌveɪt / (AmE), / ˈæk.tɪ.veɪt / (BrE)
(of sewage treatment) to aerate sewage in order to encourage the growth of organisms that decompose organic matter.

Examples

To activate sewage
Engineers designed the plant to activate effluent before it enters the next treatment stage.
Properly activated sewage ensures the safe removal of contaminants by microorganisms.
The facility uses a modern system to activate sewage and accelerate the breakdown of waste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aerate - to introduce air into a material (soil, liquid, etc.)
