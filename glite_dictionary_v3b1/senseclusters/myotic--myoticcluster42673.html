myotic

adjective
causing constriction of the pupil
Pronunciation: / maɪˈɑːtɪk / (AmE), / maɪˈɒtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or causing the constriction of the pupil of the eye.

Examples

Myotic effect on pupils
Exposure to certain chemicals may have a myotic effect on the eyes.
Pilocarpine is a common myotic used in ophthalmology.
The doctor prescribed a myotic agent to reduce the patient's pupil size.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mydriatic - relating to or causing dilation of the pupil of the eye
