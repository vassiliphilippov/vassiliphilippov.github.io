neurogenetic

adjective
nervous system genetics
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to the genetics of the nervous system or the genetic factors that influence its development and function

Examples

Neurogenetic factors
Researchers are studying neurogenetic factors that contribute to certain types of epilepsy.
A neurogenetic disorder can affect brain development in children.
Advances in neurogenetic research may lead to new treatments for inherited neurological diseases.
