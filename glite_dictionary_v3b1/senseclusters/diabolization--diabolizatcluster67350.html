diabolization

noun
portraying as evil
9.7
Pronunciation: / daɪˌæbələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / daɪˌæbələˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of portraying someone or something as evil or devilish

Examples

The diabolization of enemies
The diabolization of the opposing side in the media led to increased hostility.
Some critics argue that the film relies too much on the diabolization of its main villain.
Political campaigns often use diabolization to sway public opinion against their rivals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demonization - the act of making someone or something seem evil
  • antonym: glorification - the act of praising or representing as admirable
  • synonym: vilification - the act of speaking or writing about someone in a very negative way
