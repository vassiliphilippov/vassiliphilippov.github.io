of a piece

adjective
consistent; matching in style or character
Pronunciation: / əv ə piːs / (AmE), / ɒv ə piːs / (BrE)
(of things) consistent with or matching each other in style, character, or quality.

Examples

Of a piece style
Her new novel is of a piece with her earlier works, both in tone and theme.
The architect designed all the buildings to be of a piece with one another.
Their approach to management is of a piece with the company's overall culture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: at odds with - in disagreement or conflict with
  • synonym: in keeping with - in agreement or harmony with
