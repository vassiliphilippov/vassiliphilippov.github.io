underplate

noun
supporting plate
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌpleɪt / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌpleɪt / (BrE)
a plate or dish placed beneath another, usually for support or decorative purposes

Examples

An underplate beneath bowl
The waiter placed an elegant underplate beneath each soup bowl at the banquet.
She bought a set of gold-rimmed underplates to match her dinnerware for special occasions.
Using an underplate can help protect the tablecloth from spills and heat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: charger - a large decorative plate placed under a dinner plate
  • synonym: saucer - a small dish placed under a cup
