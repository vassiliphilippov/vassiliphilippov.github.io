work shoe

noun
protective heavy shoe for work
1.0
Pronunciation: / wɝk ʃu / (AmE), / wɜːk ʃuː / (BrE)
A sturdy, heavy shoe designed to protect the foot during hard physical work.

Examples

A work shoe
Before starting the job, make sure your work shoes are in good condition.
He wore sturdy work shoes to the construction site every day.
The thick soles of her work shoes protected her feet from sharp objects.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: work boot - a heavier boot designed for work, offering more protection than a shoe
