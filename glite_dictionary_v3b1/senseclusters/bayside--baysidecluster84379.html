bayside

noun
area beside a bay
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsaɪd / (BrE)
the area of land located next to or along the edge of a bay

Examples

Walk along the bayside
They bought a house on the bayside to enjoy the beautiful water views.
The festival was held at the bayside, attracting visitors from all over the city.
Children played along the bayside while their parents relaxed nearby.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lakeside - the area next to a lake
  • synonym: seaside - the area next to the sea or ocean
