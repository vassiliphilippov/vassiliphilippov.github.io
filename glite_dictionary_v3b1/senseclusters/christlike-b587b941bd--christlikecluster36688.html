Christlike

adjective
resembling Christ; compassionate
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkraɪst.laɪk / (AmE), / ˈkraɪst.laɪk / (BrE)
Having the qualities or spirit of Christ; showing kindness, compassion, and selflessness.

Examples

Christlike compassion
Her Christlike compassion inspired many people in the community to help others.
In a moment of Christlike forgiveness, he chose to pardon those who wronged him.
The charity aims to encourage Christlike attitudes of generosity and service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: christian - showing the qualities or teachings related to Christ or Christianity
  • antonym: unchristian - not resembling or showing the qualities of Christ
