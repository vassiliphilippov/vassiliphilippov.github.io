roll bar

noun
vehicle stability bar
3.5 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / Ààro äl b…ër / (AmE), / Ààr…ô äl b…ëÀê / (BrE)
a strong metal bar fitted in a vehicle to improve safety, especially to protect people if the vehicle rolls over or to improve stability during turns

Examples

Racing vehicles often have a reinforced roll bar for added protection and stability.
After the accident, the damaged roll bar had to be replaced before the truck could be driven again.
