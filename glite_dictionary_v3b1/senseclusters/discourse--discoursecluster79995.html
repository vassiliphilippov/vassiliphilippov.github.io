discourse

noun
religious address or sermon
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪsˌkɔrs / (AmE), / ˈdɪs.kɔːs / (BrE)
an address or sermon on a religious subject, usually delivered during a church service

Examples

Religious discourse
After the discourse, the congregation reflected quietly on the message.
Her Sunday discourse focused on the importance of forgiveness.
The priest delivered a thoughtful discourse on compassion during the service.
