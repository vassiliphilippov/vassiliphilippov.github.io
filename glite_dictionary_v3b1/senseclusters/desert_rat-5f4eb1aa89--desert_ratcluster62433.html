desert rat

noun
North American desert-dwelling leaping rodent
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛzɚt ˌræt / (AmE), / ˈdezət ˌræt / (BrE)
any of various leaping rodents found in the deserts of North America and Mexico, especially the largest members of the family Heteromyidae.

Examples

A desert rat animal
Many desert rats feed primarily on seeds and plants found in their sandy habitats.
Researchers tracked a desert rat as it hopped swiftly across the dunes.
The desert rat has adapted to survive in arid environments with scarce water.
