frying

noun
cooking in fat or oil
Pronunciation: / ˈfraɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfraɪɪŋ / (BrE)
The act or process of cooking food in hot fat or oil, usually in a shallow pan.

Examples

Frying food quickly
Frying at high temperatures can make food crispier but also increases oil absorption.
Many classic dishes rely on deep frying for their distinctive texture.
She avoided frying and preferred to steam her vegetables for a healthier diet.

Related Terms

  • antonym: roasting - cooking food with dry heat, especially in an oven
