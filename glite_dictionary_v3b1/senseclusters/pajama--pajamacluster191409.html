pajama

noun
loose-fitting nightwear
4.5
Pronunciation: / pəˈdʒɑmə /; / pəˈdʒæmə / (AmE), / pəˈdʒɑːmə /; / pɪˈdʒɑːmə / (BrE)
A loose-fitting jacket and trousers worn for sleeping or lounging, usually made of soft fabric and worn as nightclothes.

Examples

A pair of pajamas
After dinner, he put on his pajamas and settled in to watch a movie.
Every Christmas, the children receive new pajamas from their grandparents.
She changed into her favorite pajamas before going to bed.
He changed into his pyjamas before going to bed.
She bought a new pair of silk pyjamas for the trip.
The children wore matching pyjamas at the sleepover.
