unsustainability

noun
inability to continue
7.4
Pronunciation: /ʌn.sə.steɪ.nə.ˈbɪl.ə.tɪ/ /; / /ən.sə.steɪ.nə.ˈbɪl.ə.tɪ/ (AmE), /ʌn.sə.steɪ.nə.ˈbɪl.ə.tɪ/ /; / /ən.sə.steɪ.nə.ˈbɪl.ə.tɪ/ (BrE)
the quality or state of not being able to be continued or maintained over time

Examples

The unsustainability of practices
