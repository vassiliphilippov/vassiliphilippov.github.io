fogey

noun
old-fashioned, conservative person
6.6 7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfoʊ.giː/ /; / /ˈfoʊ.gɪ/ (AmE), /ˈfəʊ.giː/ /; / /ˈfəʊ.gɪ/ (BrE)
