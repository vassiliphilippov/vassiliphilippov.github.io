fodder

verb
to feed animals (with fodder)
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːdər / (AmE), / ˈfɒdə / (BrE)
to give food such as hay or straw to domesticated animals.

Examples

To fodder the cows
Each morning, the farmer would fodder the cattle before sunrise.
The task of foddering the animals was shared among the children.
They carefully foddered the horses with a mix of hay and oats.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feed - to give food to animals or people
