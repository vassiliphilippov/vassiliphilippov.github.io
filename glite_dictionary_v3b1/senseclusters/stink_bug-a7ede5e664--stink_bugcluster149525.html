stink bug

noun
odor-releasing insect
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪŋk ˌbʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋk ˌbʌɡ / (BrE)
a type of insect that emits a strong, unpleasant odor as a defense mechanism

Examples

A stink bug releases smell
When threatened, a stink bug releases a foul smell to deter predators.
We found a stink bug crawling on the kitchen wall and quickly opened the window.
The garden was full of stink bugs during the late summer months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shield bug - an insect of the same family, often with a shield-shaped body
