downvote

verb
to rate negatively
4.9
Pronunciation: /daʊn.ˈvoʊt/ (AmE), /daʊn.ˈvəʊt/ (BrE)
to give a negative vote or rating to content on a website or app, usually to show disapproval or disagreement

Examples

To downvote a comment
If you think a comment is unhelpful, you can downvote it to lower its visibility.
Many users decided to downvote the post because it contained false information.
She accidentally downvoted a photo she actually liked.
If you disagree with a comment, you can downvote it to show your opinion.
Some users downvote posts that break the forum rules.
He was disappointed when his answer was quickly downvoted by the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disapprove - to have a negative opinion of something
  • synonym: dislike - to indicate that you do not like something online
  • antonym: upvote - to give a positive rating to content online
