million

noun
very large indefinite number (hyperbole)
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪljən / (AmE), / ˈmɪljən / (BrE)
An extremely large but indefinite number, often used as hyperbole rather than the exact figure.

Examples

Million times told
I've told you a million times to turn off the lights when you leave.
She has million things to do before the trip.
There must have been a million people in the crowd at the concert.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: thousand - a large but smaller indefinite number
  • synonym: zillion - an extremely large but indefinite number (slang)
