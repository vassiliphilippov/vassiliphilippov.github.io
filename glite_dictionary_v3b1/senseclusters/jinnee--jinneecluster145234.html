jinnee

noun
supernatural spirit in Islamic tradition
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪni / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪni / (BrE)
(in Islam) an invisible spiritual being, often believed to inhabit the earth and sometimes influence humans, as described in the Koran.

Examples

A jinnee in Islam
According to tradition, each person may be accompanied by a personal jinnee.
She believed that a mischievous jinnee was responsible for the strange happenings in her house.
The old legends speak of a jinnee who guarded the hidden treasure for centuries.

  • synonym: jinn - supernatural beings in Islamic belief
