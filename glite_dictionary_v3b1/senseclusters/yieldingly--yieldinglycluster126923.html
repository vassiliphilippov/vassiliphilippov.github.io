yieldingly

adverb
in an obedient or compliant manner
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈjiːldɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈjiːldɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows willingness to comply or give in; in an obedient or compliant manner.

Examples

To yieldingly accept criticism
He accepted the criticism yieldingly, without any signs of resistance.
She nodded yieldingly to his request, agreeing without protest.
The dog lay down yieldingly at its owner's feet after being called.
