SSP

noun
ad management platform
Pronunciation: / ˌɛs ɛs ˈpi / (AmE), / ˌɛs ɛs ˈpi / (BrE)
a technology platform that enables online publishers to manage, sell, and optimize advertising space programmatically

Examples

Online SSP platform
The publisher used an SSP to automate the sale of their website's ad inventory.
By integrating an SSP, the company increased its advertising revenue through programmatic deals.
Many online media companies rely on an SSP to manage and optimize their digital ad spaces.

Related Terms

  • antonym: DSP - a demand-side platform for advertisers to buy digital ad space programmatically
  • synonym: ad exchange - a digital marketplace for buying and selling advertising space
