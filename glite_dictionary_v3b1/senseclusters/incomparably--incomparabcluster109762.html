incomparably

adverb
to an unmatched or unparalleled degree
2.5
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɑːmpərəbli / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɒmpərəbli / (BrE)
In a way or to a degree that is beyond comparison; to a degree that nothing else matches.

Examples

Performed incomparably better
She performed incomparably better than her competitors in the final round.
The chef's signature dish is incomparably delicious.
The view from the mountaintop was incomparably beautiful at sunrise.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comparably - in a way that can be compared
  • synonym: unmatched - not matched or equaled; unsurpassed
  • synonym: unparalleled - having no equal; exceptional
