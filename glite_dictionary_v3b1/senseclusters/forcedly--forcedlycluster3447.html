forcedly

adverb
using physical force
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrstɪdli / (AmE), / ˈfɔːsɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that involves the use of physical force or violence

Examples

Forcedly removed
The protesters were forcedly removed from the building by security personnel.
The door was forcedly opened after several attempts failed to unlock it.
During the rescue, the team forcedly broke the window to reach the trapped child.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forcibly - using physical force or violence
  • antonym: gently - in a soft or mild manner, without force
