embrace

verb
hold someone closely in one's arms
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbreɪs / (AmE), / ɪmˈbreɪs / (BrE)
to hold (someone) tightly in your arms, usually as an expression of affection

Examples

To embrace a friend
After hearing the good news, she couldn't help but embrace her friend.
The mother rushed to embrace her son after his long journey.
They embraced warmly at the airport, happy to see each other again.
