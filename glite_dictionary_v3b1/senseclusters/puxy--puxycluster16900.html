puxy

verb
to rot and soften
9.9
to become soft and spongy as a result of decay, especially in wood or similar material

Examples

Old beams puxy
After years of rain and neglect, the old barn's beams began to puxy and lose their strength.
If you leave untreated wood outside, it will eventually puxy and crumble.
The wooden steps had puxied so much that they were unsafe to walk on.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decay - to gradually decompose or break down
  • antonym: preserve - to keep something from decaying or spoiling
  • synonym: rot - to decay and break down due to natural processes
