gudge

noun
easily deceived person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡʌdʒ / (AmE), / ɡʌdʒ / (BrE)
an old term for a fool or someone who is easily deceived or tricked

Examples

A gudge in tales
In old stories, the gudge was always the one who fell for every trick.
They thought I was a gudge, but I quickly proved them wrong.
No one wants to be called a gudge in front of their friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dupe - a person who is easily tricked or deceived
  • synonym: fool - a person lacking good sense or judgment
  • antonym: trickster - a person who deceives or cheats others
