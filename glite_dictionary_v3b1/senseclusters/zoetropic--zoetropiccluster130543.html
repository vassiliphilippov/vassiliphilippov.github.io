zoetropic

adjective
like a zoetrope
9.9
Pronunciation: / zoʊ.əˈtroʊ.pɪk / (AmE), / zəʊ.ɪˈtrəʊ.pɪk / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a zoetrope, especially in producing the illusion of motion from static images

Examples

Zoetropic effect
The filmmaker used a zoetropic technique to bring the old photographs to life.
Her sculpture had a zoetropic quality, making it seem as if it was moving when viewed from different angles.
The museum displayed a zoetropic installation that fascinated visitors with its spinning images.

Related Terms

  • synonym: animated - appearing to move or full of motion
  • antonym: static - not moving or changing; still
