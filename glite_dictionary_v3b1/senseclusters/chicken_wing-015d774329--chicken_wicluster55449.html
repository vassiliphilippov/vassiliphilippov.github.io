chicken wing

noun
wing of a chicken used as food
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪkən wɪŋ /; / ˈtʃɪkɪn wɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn wɪŋ / (BrE)
the wing of a chicken, especially when cooked and eaten as food (often served as a snack or appetizer, sometimes with sauces)

Examples

Spicy chicken wings
He ordered a plate of spicy chicken wings at the bar.
She prefers baked chicken wings to fried ones for a healthier option.
The recipe calls for marinating the chicken wings overnight for extra flavor.
He prefers his chicken wings extra crispy and covered in hot sauce.
The menu includes a variety of chicken wing flavors to choose from.
We shared a plate of spicy chicken wings at the pub.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buffalo wing - spicy deep-fried chicken wing, often served as an appetizer
  • synonym: drumstick - the lower joint of a chicken leg, often eaten as food
  • synonym: wing - the part of a bird used for flying; also used for edible chicken parts
