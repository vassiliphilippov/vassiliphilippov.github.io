Tremella

noun
hydrating cosmetic ingredient
8.1
Pronunciation: / trəˈmɛl.ə /; / trəˈmɛlə / (AmE), / trɪˈmɛl.ə /; / trɪˈmiːlə / (BrE)
a moisturizing ingredient or extract from the Tremella mushroom, used in skin-care products to help skin retain moisture
