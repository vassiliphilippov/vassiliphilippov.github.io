patronage

noun
support or sponsorship by a patron
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪtrənɪdʒ, ˈpætrənɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpætrənɪdʒ / (BrE)
the support, encouragement, or financial aid that an individual or organization bestows to another

Examples

Patronage of the arts
Her patronage of the arts has made her well-known in cultural circles.
The museum relies on the patronage of local businesses to fund its exhibitions.
Without royal patronage, many artists would have struggled to survive in that era.

Related Terms

  • antonym: opposition - active resistance or dissent; contrast to support
  • synonym: sponsorship - the position of being a sponsor; financial or other support
  • synonym: support - the act of helping or backing someone or something
