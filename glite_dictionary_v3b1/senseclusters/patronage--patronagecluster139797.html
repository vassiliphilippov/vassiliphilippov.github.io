Synonyms

  • support
    1.0 64/m
    support is general help or money given to meet needs, while patronage often comes from a named patron and is more formal.
  • sponsorship
    3.5 4.64/m
    sponsorship is official financial or promotional support for an event or person, while patronage is broader personal backing.
  • backing
    2.6 0.06/m
    backing is active help or approval, often public or political, while patronage implies ongoing support from a patron.
  • favor
    1.0 48/m
    favor is a helpful act or special preference shown to someone, while patronage usually means formal or financial support.
