In a way that is driven by instinct rather than conscious thought or reasoning.

Examples

To act instinctively
He instinctively knew something was wrong as soon as he entered the room.
She instinctively reached out to catch the falling glass before it hit the ground.
The dog instinctively protected its owner from danger.

Synonyms

  • intuitively
    6.1 2.99/m
    intuitively is based on a quick inner sense or feeling about something, while instinctively is a natural, more bodily reaction.
  • automatically
    2.9 7.43/m
    automatically is done as a regular or mechanical action without thought, while instinctively is driven by natural, often biological, impulses.
  • reflexively
    5.4 1.31/m
    reflexively is an immediate, involuntary response often at the body level, while instinctively is a broader, sometimes emotional, natural urge.
