exceptionalist

noun
believer in uniqueness
9.6
Pronunciation: /ɪk.ˈsep.ʃə.nə.lɪst/ /; / /ɪk.sep.ʃə.ˈnæl.ɪst/ (AmE), /ɪk.ˈsep.ʃə.nə.lɪst/ /; / /ɪk.sep.ʃə.ˈnæl.ɪst/ (BrE)
a person who believes that something, especially their country, is unique, superior, or not subject to usual rules or standards

Examples

An exceptionalist leader
The exceptionalist argued that his country's history made it different from all others.
Critics accused the leader of being an exceptionalist who ignored international agreements.
As an exceptionalist, she believed her culture should not be compared to others.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nationalist - a person who strongly identifies with and supports their nation
  • antonym: universalist - a person who believes in universal principles applying to all
