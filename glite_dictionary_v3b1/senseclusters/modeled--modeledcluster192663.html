modeled

adjective
created as a 3D digital model
4.4 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / \u02c8m\u0251\u02d0dld / (AmE), / \u02c8m\u0252dld / (BrE)
(in computer graphics and CAD) created or represented as a three-dimensional digital model using modelling software; having an origin as a digital 3D model.

Examples

Modeled character
The game's protagonist was carefully modeled to capture realistic facial expressions and skin detail.
The design team presented a modeled prototype that allowed clients to inspect the product before manufacturing.
The architect showed a modeled version of the building so the committee could evaluate sunlight and shadows.
