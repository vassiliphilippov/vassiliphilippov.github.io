lab bench

noun
table used for scientific work
2.5
Pronunciation: / læb bɛntʃ / (AmE), / læb bɛntʃ / (BrE)
A long, sturdy table in a laboratory, used for scientific work and experiments.

Examples

A lab bench surface
At school, students are expected to keep their lab benches clean after each experiment.
