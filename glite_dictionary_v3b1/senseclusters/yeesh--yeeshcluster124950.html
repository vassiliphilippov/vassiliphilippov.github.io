yeesh

interjection
expression of discomfort
Pronunciation: / jiʃ / (AmE), / jiːʃ / (BrE)
used to express surprise, discomfort, or mild disgust at something unpleasant or awkward

Examples

Yeesh, that's bad
Yeesh, I can't believe how much homework we have this weekend.
He spilled coffee all over his shirt during the meeting—yeesh.
When I saw the price of the concert tickets, I thought, yeesh, that's expensive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ew - used to express disgust
  • synonym: yikes - used to show surprise or mild shock
