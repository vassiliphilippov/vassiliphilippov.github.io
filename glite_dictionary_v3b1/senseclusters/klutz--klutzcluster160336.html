klutz

noun
a clumsy or awkward person
Pronunciation: / klʌts / (AmE), / klʌts / (BrE)
A clumsy or awkward person.

Examples

A klutz person
Don't let Tom carry the cake—he's such a klutz!
Even as a child, she was a klutz and always got bumps and bruises.
I tripped over my own feet like a real klutz during the dance recital.
