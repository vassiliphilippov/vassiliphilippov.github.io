practice

noun
application of an idea or theory
Pronunciation: / ˈpræktɪs / (AmE), / ˈpræktɪs / (BrE)
the act of putting an idea, theory, or plan into effect

Examples

Practice an idea
The scientist was eager to put her new theory into practice.
There is often a gap between the ideals of education and their practice in the classroom.
While the plan seemed perfect on paper, its practice proved much more challenging.

Related Terms

  • antonym: theory - a system of ideas intended to explain something
