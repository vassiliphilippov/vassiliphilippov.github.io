Sophora japonica

noun
deciduous tree with creamy-white flowers, native to East Asia
9.4
Pronunciation: / soʊˈfɔrə dʒæˈpoʊnɪkə / (AmE), / səˈfɔːrə dʒæˈpəʊnɪkə / (BrE)
A deciduous tree native to China and Japan, known for its round crown, dark green compound leaves, and clusters of fragrant creamy-white flowers.
