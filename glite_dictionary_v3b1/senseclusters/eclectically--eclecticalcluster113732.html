eclectically

adverb
in a mixed style
Pronunciation: / ɪˈklɛktɪkli / (AmE), / ɪˈklɛktɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that combines ideas, styles, or elements from a wide variety of sources

Examples

To eclectically mix styles
The artist eclectically blends classical and modern techniques in her paintings.
Their home is eclectically furnished with pieces from different countries and eras.
He eclectically chooses music, enjoying everything from jazz to electronic dance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diversely - in a varied or diverse manner
  • antonym: uniformly - in a consistent or unvarying way
