tsaritsa

noun
wife or widow of a czar
8.9
Pronunciation: / tsɑˈriːtsə / (AmE), / tsɑːˈriːtsə / (BrE)
the wife or widow of a czar, especially in Imperial Russia

Examples

The tsaritsa of Russia
Following the death of the czar, the tsaritsa lived in seclusion for many years.
Historians have written extensively about the influence wielded by the last tsaritsa of Russia.
The young tsaritsa was admired for her wisdom and grace at the Russian court.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: czar - an emperor of Russia before 1917
  • synonym: czarina - the wife of a Russian czar
  • synonym: empress - the wife or widow of an emperor; a female ruler of an empire
