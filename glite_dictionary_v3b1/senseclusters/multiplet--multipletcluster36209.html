multiplet

noun
group of spectral lines
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltɪplət / (AmE), / ˈmʌltɪplɪt / (BrE)
a group of closely related spectral lines or energy states, especially in physics or chemistry

Examples

Spectral multiplet
The NMR spectrum displayed a multiplet indicating the presence of several interacting protons.
Physicists analyzed the multiplet to determine the energy levels of the atom.
A clear multiplet appeared in the data due to spin-orbit coupling effects.
