ladylikeness

noun
quality of being ladylike
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪdiˌlaɪknəs / (AmE), / ˈleɪdɪˌlaɪknɪs / (BrE)
the quality or behavior considered appropriate to a refined or well-mannered woman

Examples

Ladylikeness and manners
Despite her athletic achievements, she never lost her sense of ladylikeness.
Her ladylikeness was evident in every gesture and word she spoke.
The school prided itself on teaching decorum and ladylikeness to all its students.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ladylike - having the manners and appearance traditionally associated with a lady
  • antonym: unladylike - not typical of a lady; not proper or refined in manner
