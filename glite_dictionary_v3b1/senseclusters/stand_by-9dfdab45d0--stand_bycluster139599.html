stand by

verb
do nothing in a situation
1.0
Pronunciation: / stænd baɪ / (AmE), / stænd baɪ / (BrE)
to do nothing or take no action, especially when something bad is happening and intervention is expected.

Examples

To stand by and watch
I couldn't just stand by and watch him being bullied.
Many people stood by as the incident unfolded, unwilling to help.
She refused to stand by while her friend was in trouble.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intervene - become involved to influence what happens
  • antonym: step in - take action to become involved
