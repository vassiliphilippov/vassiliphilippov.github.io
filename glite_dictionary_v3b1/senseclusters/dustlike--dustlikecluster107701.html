dustlike

adjective
resembling dust in fineness
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌstˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdʌst.laɪk / (BrE)
as fine and powdery as dust; resembling or having qualities of dust

Examples

Dustlike particles
A dustlike substance coated the surface of the ancient book.
After the fire, the air was filled with dustlike particles.
The pollen appeared almost dustlike in the sunlight.
