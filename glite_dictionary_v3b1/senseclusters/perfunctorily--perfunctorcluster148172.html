In a way that shows little interest, care, or enthusiasm; done as a formality or routine rather than with real attention.

Examples

To perfunctorily greet customers
After years at the job, he now greeted customers perfunctorily rather than with genuine warmth.
She shook his hand perfunctorily, barely glancing in his direction.
The students completed their assignments perfunctorily, clearly lacking motivation.
