disconnectedly

adverb
without connection
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪskəˈnɛktɪdli / (AmE), / ˌdɪskəˈnɛktɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that is not connected, either logically or emotionally; lacking smooth flow or involvement

Examples

To speak disconnectedly
He spoke disconnectedly, jumping from one topic to another without any clear link.
The wires hung disconnectedly from the ceiling after the renovation work.
Her thoughts drifted disconnectedly as she stared out the window.
He answered the questions disconnectedly, making it hard to follow his thoughts.
The story was told disconnectedly, with events out of order and missing details.
During the interview, she spoke disconnectedly about her experiences, jumping from topic to topic.
He answered the questions disconnectedly, barely making eye contact with the interviewer.
During the meeting, she spoke disconnectedly, as if her mind was elsewhere.
The students listened disconnectedly, not really paying attention to the lecture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coherently - in a logical and connected way
  • antonym: engagedly - in a way that shows active interest or involvement
  • synonym: indifferently - in a way that shows a lack of interest or concern
  • synonym: randomly - in a way that happens without a specific pattern or plan
  • synonym: separately - in a way that is apart or not together
