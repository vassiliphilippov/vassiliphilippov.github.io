plushly

adverb
luxuriously, richly
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈplʌʃli / (AmE), / ˈplʌʃli / (BrE)
in a soft, luxurious, or richly comfortable manner

Examples

The room is plushly decorated
The hotel lobby was plushly decorated with marble floors and golden chandeliers.
They dined plushly in a private room with velvet curtains and fine china.
The apartment was plushly furnished, making it feel warm and inviting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: luxuriously - in a way that is very comfortable and expensive
  • antonym: plainly - in a simple or unadorned manner
  • synonym: richly - in a way that is full, elaborate, or expensive
