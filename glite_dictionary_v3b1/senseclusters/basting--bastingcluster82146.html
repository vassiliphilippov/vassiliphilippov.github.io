basting

noun
temporary sewing stitch
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪstɪŋ / (BrE)
A loose, temporary stitch used to hold layers of fabric together before the final sewing.

Examples

Basting stitch
Good basting makes it easier to assemble a complex garment.
She used basting to hold the pieces of fabric together before sewing the seam.
The tailor removed the basting stitches after finishing the dress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tacking - temporary stitching to hold fabric in place
