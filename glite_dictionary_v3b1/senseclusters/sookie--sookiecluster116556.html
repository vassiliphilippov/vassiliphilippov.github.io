sookie

noun
term of endearment
9.9
an affectionate or informal name used to address someone in a loving or endearing way

Examples

Call her sookie
He called her sookie as they walked hand in hand along the beach.
Whenever his little sister was upset, he would comfort her by saying, 'It's okay, sookie.'
She smiled when her grandfather affectionately referred to her as sookie during their conversation.
