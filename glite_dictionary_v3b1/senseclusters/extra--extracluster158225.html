Synonyms

  • additional
    1.0 110/m
    additional is used to describe something added to what already exists, while extra is more general and informal.
  • supplementary
    4.7 3/m
    supplementary is used to add materials or help to complete something, while extra is more general and simple.
  • supplemental
    5.0 6/m
    supplemental is a formal word for added support or items in reports, while extra is casual everyday speech.
  • surplus
    3.4 0.1/m
    surplus means more goods or stock than needed, often in stores or budgets, while extra is more general.
  • excess
    4.1 10/m
    excess describes too much of something and often has a negative tone, while extra is neutral and simple.
  • spare
    2.1 4/m
    spare refers to a replacement or unused item kept for use, while extra is any additional thing in general.
