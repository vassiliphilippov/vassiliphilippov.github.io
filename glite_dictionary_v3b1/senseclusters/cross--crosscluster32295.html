cross

verb
breed from different varieties or species
1.0
Pronunciation: / krɑs / (AmE), / krɒs / (BrE)
to breed animals or plants using parents of different varieties or species

Examples

To cross two plants
Farmers often cross two different apple varieties to create new flavors.
Mendel famously crossed pea plants with different traits in his experiments.
The scientist plans to cross wheat with a disease-resistant strain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crossbreed - to produce an animal or plant by mating individuals of different varieties or species
  • synonym: interbreed - to breed or cause to breed between different types, especially species or varieties
