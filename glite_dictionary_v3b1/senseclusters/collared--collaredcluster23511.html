collared

adjective
with a collar
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑlɚd / (AmE), / ˈkɒləd / (BrE)
having a collar, especially referring to clothing or something worn around an animal's neck

Examples

Collared shirt
He wore a crisp collared shirt to the interview.
The dog was easily identified by its bright red collared neck.
Many school uniforms require students to wear collared tops.
