Teresian

noun
follower of St. Teresa
9.9
Pronunciation: / təˈriːziən / (AmE), / təˈriːziən / (BrE)
a member or follower of a religious order founded by or dedicated to St. Teresa, especially St. Teresa of Ávila

Examples

A devoted Teresian
She became a devoted Teresian after reading about the life of St. Teresa of Ávila.
The Teresians gathered for their annual retreat at the monastery.
As a Teresian, he followed the spiritual teachings of St. Teresa closely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Carmelite - a member of a Roman Catholic religious order founded on Mount Carmel
