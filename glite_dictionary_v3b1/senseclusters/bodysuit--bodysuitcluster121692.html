bodysuit

noun
one-piece garment
3.2 0.59/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…ëÀêdiÀåsuÀêt / (AmE), / Ààb…ídiÀåsuÀêt / (BrE)
a close-fitting, one-piece garment that covers the torso and sometimes the arms and legs, worn as underwear, outerwear, or for activities like dance or gymnastics

Examples

A bodysuit garment
She wore a bodysuit under her dress to create a smooth silhouette.
The dancer's sparkling bodysuit caught everyone's attention during the performance.
Many athletes prefer a bodysuit for comfort and support during competitions.
She dressed her newborn in a soft cotton bodysuit before putting him to bed.
The store offers a variety of bodysuits in different colors and patterns for babies.
A bodysuit makes diaper changes much easier for new parents.
She wore a black bodysuit to the dance class.
The gymnast's bodysuit was decorated with sparkling sequins for the competition.
Many fashion models prefer a bodysuit for its sleek and comfortable fit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corset - a close-fitting undergarment designed to shape the waist and support the torso
  • synonym: jumpsuit - a one-piece garment with sleeves and legs, often worn as clothing or uniform
  • synonym: leotard - a tight-fitting one-piece garment covering the torso, often worn for dance or exercise
  • synonym: onesie - a one-piece garment for infants, similar to a bodysuit
  • synonym: romper - a one-piece garment for babies or young children
  • synonym: unitard - a one-piece garment covering the body and legs, sometimes the arms
