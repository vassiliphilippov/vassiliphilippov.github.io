fibrofatty

noun
fibrous and fatty tissue
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪbroʊˈfæti / (AmE), / ˌfaɪbrəʊˈfæti / (BrE)
a mass, lesion, or deposit made up of both fibrous and fatty tissue, often found in medical contexts

Examples

Fibrofatty mass
The surgeon removed a fibrofatty mass from the patient's abdomen during the operation.
A fibrofatty deposit was detected in the artery during the medical examination.
Doctors are studying how fibrofatty lesions develop in heart disease patients.

  • synonym: fibrous tissue - tissue composed mainly of fibers, especially collagen
