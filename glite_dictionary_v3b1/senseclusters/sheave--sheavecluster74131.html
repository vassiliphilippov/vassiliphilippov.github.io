sheave

noun
grooved pulley wheel
9.4 0.06/m
Pronunciation: /  Éiv / (AmE), /  ÉiÀêv / (BrE)
a wheel with a groove around its edge for holding a belt, rope, or cable, used in machinery to change direction or transmit force

Examples

Pulley sheave
The engineer inspected the sheave to ensure the cable would run smoothly.
A broken sheave can cause the entire pulley system to malfunction.
They lubricated the sheave regularly to prevent wear and tear.
