réchauffé

noun
reheated dish, often leftovers
Pronunciation: / reɪˈʃoʊfeɪ / (AmE), / reɪˈʃɒfeɪ / (BrE)
a dish consisting of food that has been reheated, typically leftovers given a new preparation or seasoning

Examples

A rechauffe dish
Last night's stew was turned into a delicious réchauffé for lunch.
The chef presented a clever réchauffé that tasted better than the original meal.
We often have a réchauffé on Mondays, using Sunday roast leftovers.
