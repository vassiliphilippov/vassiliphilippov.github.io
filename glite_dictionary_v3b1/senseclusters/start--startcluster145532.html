start

noun
opportunity to be a starter in a game
Pronunciation: / stɑɹt / (AmE), / stɑːt / (BrE)
an opportunity for a player to be in the starting lineup in a game or competition

Examples

A start for the team
After an impressive performance at practice, she earned her first start with the team.
He was disappointed not to get the start in such an important match.
The young quarterback is hoping for another start this weekend after last week's win.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appearance - an occasion when a player takes part in a game
