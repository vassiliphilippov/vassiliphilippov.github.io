carissa

noun
a shrub with white flowers and dark fruits
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɪsə / (AmE), / kəˈrɪsə / (BrE)
A tropical or subtropical shrub of the genus Carissa, having fragrant white flowers and red to purple-black plumlike fruits.

Examples

The carissa shrub
The carissa is popular in landscaping for its ornamental appearance and tough, evergreen leaves.
The botanical garden features a beautiful carissa with clusters of fragrant white blossoms.
We picked some ripe carissas to make jam from the purple-black fruits.
