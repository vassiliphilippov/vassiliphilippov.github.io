Godzilla

noun
huge or powerful thing
Pronunciation: / ɡɑdˈzɪlə / (AmE), / ɡɒdˈzɪlə / (BrE)
an informal term for something or someone extremely large, powerful, or destructive, likened to the fictional monster

Examples

A Godzilla project
The new shopping mall is a Godzilla that towers over all the nearby buildings.
Their company became a Godzilla in the tech industry after the merger.
That truck is a real Godzilla compared to the other vehicles on the road.

Related Terms

  • synonym: behemoth - something enormous and powerful
  • synonym: giant - a very large or powerful person or thing
  • antonym: minnow - something very small or insignificant
