extrasystole

noun
premature heartbeat; cardiac arrhythmia
9.6 8.6
Pronunciation: /ek.strə.ˈsɪs.toʊl/ (AmE), /ek.strə.ˈsɪs.təʊl/ (BrE)
a premature heartbeat, occurring earlier than the next expected regular heart contraction, resulting in a brief cardiac arrhythmia
