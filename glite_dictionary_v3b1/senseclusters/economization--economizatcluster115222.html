economization

noun
reducing spending
Pronunciation: / ɪˌkɑːnəməˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌkɒnəmaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of reducing spending or using resources more efficiently to save money or effort

Examples

Economization of costs
The company implemented strict economization measures to improve its financial health.
Household economization became necessary after the rise in utility costs.
Through careful economization, the project was completed under budget.

Related Terms

  • synonym: saving - the act of reducing expenses or conserving resources
  • antonym: waste - the careless or unnecessary use of resources
