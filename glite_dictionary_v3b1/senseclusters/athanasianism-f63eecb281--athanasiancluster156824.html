Athanasianism

noun
Christian doctrine: Christ of same substance as God the Father
9.5 9.9
Pronunciation: /ə.θæ.nə.ˈzeɪ.ə.nɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ə.θæ.nə.ˈzeɪ.ə.nɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
