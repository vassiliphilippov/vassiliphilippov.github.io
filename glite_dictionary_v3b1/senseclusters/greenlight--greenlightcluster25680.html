greenlight

verb
give approval to a project
1.9 3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈgriːn.laɪt/ (AmE), /ˈgriːn.laɪt/ (BrE)
to give official permission for a project, especially a film or television show, to proceed

Examples

To greenlight a project
Management will only greenlight the project if the costs stay within budget.
The studio decided to greenlight the new science fiction series after reviewing the pilot episode.
They have not yet greenlighted the proposal, but discussions are ongoing.
