Synonyms

  • approval

  • recognition
    2.8 7.38/m
    recognition is official or public acceptance of status or rights, while acceptance covers being welcomed or agreeing to something.
  • acknowledgment
    4.2 5.33/m

  • credence
    7.1 2.78/m

  • assent
