wheelie

verb
to lift front wheel
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːli / (AmE), / ˈwiːli / (BrE)
to ride a bicycle or motorcycle with the front wheel lifted off the ground, usually as a stunt

Examples

To wheelie a motorcycle
He tried to wheelie his new motorcycle along the quiet suburban road.
She learned how to wheelie her bike after weeks of practice.
The crowd cheered as he managed to wheelie for several meters down the street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pop a wheelie - to perform a wheelie on a bicycle or motorcycle
  • synonym: stunt - an action showing skill or daring, often for entertainment
