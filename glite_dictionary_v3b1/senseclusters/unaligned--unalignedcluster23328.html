unaligned

adjective
not in line or agreement
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈlaɪnd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈlaɪnd / (BrE)
not in agreement or not arranged in a straight line with something else

Examples

Unaligned opinions
The two political parties remained unaligned on the proposed reforms.
Some of the tiles were unaligned, making the floor look uneven.
Her views were unaligned with those of her colleagues on the project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aligned - in agreement or arranged in a straight line
  • synonym: disagreeing - not having the same opinion or view
