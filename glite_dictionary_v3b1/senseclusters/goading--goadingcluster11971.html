goading

noun
provoking or urging someone to act
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊ.dɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊ.dɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of provoking, urging, or encouraging someone to do something, often by taunting or persistent urging

Examples

Constant goading
His constant goading finally convinced her to try the new sport.
She resisted the goading from her friends to make a speech.
The goading he received pushed him to prove everyone wrong.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discouragement - the act of dissuading or deterring someone
  • synonym: encouragement - the act of giving support or confidence
  • synonym: prodding - persistent urging or prompting
