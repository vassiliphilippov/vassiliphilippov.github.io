counterwork

noun
opposing actions
7.7 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààka änt…ôrÀåw…úrk / (AmE), / Ààka änt…ôÀåw…úÀêk / (BrE)
actions or efforts intended to oppose, prevent, or neutralize something

Examples

Counterwork government plans
The committee's counterwork prevented the new policy from being implemented too quickly.
Effective counterwork can slow down the progress of harmful initiatives.
Their counterwork included organizing protests and writing critical articles.

Synonyms

    6.3 0.09/m

  • countermeasure
    6.5 1.50/m
    countermeasure is a planned device or action used to prevent damage or attack, while counterwork covers wider efforts to oppose threats.
  • neutralization
    6.7 0.06/m
    neutralization is the process of making an effect harmless or inactive, while counterwork means active efforts to oppose or prevent that effect.
  • countermove
    6.7 0.10/m
    countermove is a quick response action to stop an enemy or opponent, while counterwork implies broader, longer efforts to oppose something.
