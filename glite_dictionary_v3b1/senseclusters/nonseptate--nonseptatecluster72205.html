nonseptate

adjective
without partitions
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈsɛpteɪt / (AmE), / nɒnˈsɛpteɪt / (BrE)
not divided by septa or partitions, especially in biological structures such as cells or hyphae

Examples

Nonseptate hyphae
Some fungi have nonseptate hyphae, allowing their cytoplasm to flow freely throughout the structure.
The biologist identified the sample as nonseptate under the microscope due to the absence of internal walls.
In certain algae, the filaments are nonseptate, making them appear as long, continuous tubes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: septate - divided by septa or partitions
