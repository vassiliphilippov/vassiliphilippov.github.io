misogynist

noun
person who dislikes women
Pronunciation: / mɪˈsɑdʒənɪst / (AmE), / mɪˈsɒdʒənɪst / (BrE)
a person who dislikes, despises, or is strongly prejudiced against women

Examples

A misogynist man
He was criticized as a misogynist due to his frequent derogatory remarks about women.
Some writers have been accused of expressing misogynist ideas in their work.
The company took a stand against the misogynist behavior reported by several employees.

Related Terms

  • antonym: feminist - a person who supports women's rights and gender equality
  • degree higher: misanthrope - a person who dislikes humankind in general
