TFR

noun
bank transfer abbreviation
6.7
Pronunciation: / ti ɛf ɑr / (AmE), / ti ɛf ɑː / (BrE)
an abbreviation on bank statements indicating a transfer of money from one account to another

Examples

Bank TFR
The bank statement listed a TFR of $500 to the joint account.
She noticed a TFR entry when reviewing her monthly transactions online.
A regular TFR is set up to move funds into his savings each month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: transfer - the act of moving money from one account to another
