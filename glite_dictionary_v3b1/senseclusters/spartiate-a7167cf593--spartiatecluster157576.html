Spartiate

adjective
of ancient Sparta
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑːrtiˌeɪt / (AmE), / ˈspɑːtɪeɪt / (BrE)
relating to Sparta or its citizens, especially the elite ruling class of the ancient city-state.

Examples

Spartiate customs
The Spartiate tradition emphasized discipline and military training above all else.
Only those born to Spartiate families could participate in the government of ancient Sparta.
The historian described the Spartiate way of life as strict and communal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Spartan - relating to Sparta or its people
