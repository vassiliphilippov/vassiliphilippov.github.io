moonlight

verb
work a second job, often at night
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmunˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈmuːnˌlaɪt / (BrE)
to have a second job in addition to one's main employment, especially secretly or at night

Examples

To moonlight as a waiter
During the week, he works at the bank but he also moonlights as a guitarist in a local bar.
Many teachers choose to moonlight in the evenings to supplement their income.
She moonlighted as a waitress while pursuing her degree.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freelance - to work as an independent, often on side jobs
  • synonym: side hustle - an additional job or income source besides one's main job
