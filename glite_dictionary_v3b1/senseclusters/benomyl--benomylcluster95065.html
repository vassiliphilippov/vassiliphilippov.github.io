benomyl

noun
fungicide, cell inhibitor
9.9
Pronunciation: / bəˈnoʊmɪl / (AmE), / bəˈnəʊmɪl / (BrE)
a chemical compound used as a fungicide to control fungal diseases in plants and in laboratory research to disrupt cell division
