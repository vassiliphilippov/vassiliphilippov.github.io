baby fever

noun
strong desire to have a child
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪ.bi ˌfiː.vɚ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi ˌfiː.və / (BrE)
A strong, often sudden, desire to have a baby.

Examples

Baby fever feeling
After visiting her sister's newborn twins, she couldn't shake her baby fever.
He never understood what baby fever felt like until he held his niece for the first time.
Many people experience baby fever when friends begin to have children of their own.

  • synonym: broody - desiring to have children
