lamia

noun
mythical blood-drinking creature
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪ.mi.ə / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.mi.ə / (BrE)
(in folklore) a corpse or female demon that rises at night to drink the blood of the living.

Examples

The lamia drinks blood
According to legend, the lamia would prowl the villages after dark in search of victims.
Some believed garlic could keep a lamia from entering their home at night.
Stories about the lamia have frightened children for generations across Eastern Europe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ghoul - an evil spirit or phantom associated with graveyards and consuming human flesh
  • synonym: vampire - a creature from folklore that drinks the blood of the living
