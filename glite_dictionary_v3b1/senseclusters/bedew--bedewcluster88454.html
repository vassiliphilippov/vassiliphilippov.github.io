bedew

verb
cover with drops of dew
7.7
Pronunciation: / bɪˈdu / (AmE), / bɪˈdjuː / (BrE)
to cover or sprinkle with drops of dew or a dew-like moisture

Examples

To bedew with drops
His brow was bedewed with sweat after the long run.
Morning mist would bedew the garden every sunrise, creating a sparkling view.
Tears began to bedew her cheeks as she listened to the sad song.
