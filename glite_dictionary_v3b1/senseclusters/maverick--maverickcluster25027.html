maverick

noun
independent-minded person
Pronunciation: / ˈmævərɪk / (AmE), / ˈmævərɪk / (BrE)
a person who thinks and acts independently, often refusing to follow conventional rules or group opinions

Examples

A political maverick
As a political maverick, she often challenged her party's traditional views.
He was considered a maverick in the scientific community for questioning established theories.
The startup hired a maverick who brought fresh ideas and broke the mold.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonconformist - a person who does not conform to prevailing ideas or practices
