star

verb
mark with an asterisk
3.6
Pronunciation: / stɑr / (AmE), / stɑː / (BrE)
to mark something with a star or asterisk, or to mark something as important or saved by clicking a star icon

Examples

To star a message
In this grammar handbook, incorrect forms are starred for students to avoid.
Please star the items that need further review before the meeting.
The editor decided to star all the key terms in the glossary for emphasis.
Many developers star popular repositories on GitHub to bookmark them for later.
She starred the important emails so she could find them quickly when needed.
Before the project deadline, he started starring helpful tutorials and notes to revisit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: asterisk - mark with an asterisk symbol (*)
  • synonym: bookmark - to save a web page or item so you can find it again later
  • synonym: favorite - to mark something as preferred or saved for quick access
