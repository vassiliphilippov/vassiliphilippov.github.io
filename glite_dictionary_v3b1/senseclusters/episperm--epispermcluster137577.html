episperm

noun
outer seed coat in flowering plants
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɛpɪˌspɜrm / (AmE), / ˈɛpɪˌspɜːm / (BrE)
the outer protective layer or seed coat of seeds in flowering plants

Examples

The episperm layer
Damage to the episperm can make seeds more susceptible to disease.
The episperm plays a crucial role in protecting the embryo within the seed.
When studying bean seeds, you can easily see the episperm covering the surface.
