logistic

adjective
relating to logistics
Pronunciation: / ləˈdʒɪstɪk / (AmE), / ləˈdʒɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving the organization of the movement, supply, and maintenance of equipment, personnel, or goods.

Examples

Proper logistic planning
Proper logistic planning ensured that all the necessary equipment arrived on time.
The team faced significant logistic challenges in delivering supplies to remote areas.
They hired a logistic expert to streamline the company's distribution process.
