drag

noun
inhalation of smoke from a cigarette, etc.
Pronunciation: / dræɡ / (AmE), / dræɡ / (BrE)
an act of inhaling smoke from a cigarette, pipe, or similar device

Examples

A drag on a cigarette
He took a long drag on his cigarette before answering the question.
She offered me a drag of her cigar during the break.
Tom flicked away the ash and took another drag from his pipe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: puff - an act of inhaling and exhaling smoke from a cigarette, etc.
