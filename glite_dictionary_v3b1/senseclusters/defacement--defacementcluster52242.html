defacement

noun
unauthorized change to a website
7.6
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfeɪsmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈfeɪsmənt / (BrE)
(in computing) the unauthorized alteration of a website or web page, typically to display vandalistic, political, or malicious content.

Examples

Website defacement
Hackers carried out defacement of several municipal websites during the protest.
After the attack, the university reported the defacement and began restoring the affected web pages.
Visitors noticed a crude political message in the site's header, evidence of the defacement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hacking - unauthorized access to computer systems; often associated with attacks that may include website defacement
  • synonym: vandalism - deliberate damage or spoiling of property or content for malicious or political reasons
