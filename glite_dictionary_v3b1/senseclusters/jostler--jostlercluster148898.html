jostler

noun
vibrating device
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑslər / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒslə / (BrE)
a machine or device that shakes, vibrates, or agitates objects or material to move, sort, or separate them

Examples

A jostler machine
The factory uses a jostler to keep small parts moving along the assembly line.
A powerful jostler helps separate different sizes of stones during processing.
Technicians adjusted the jostler to ensure the materials were evenly distributed.
