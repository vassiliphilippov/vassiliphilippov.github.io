draw

noun
a shallow gully or small valley
Pronunciation: / drɔ / (AmE), / drɔː / (BrE)
a natural gully or shallow valley, typically less steep or deep than a ravine, often forming a route for water or passage

Examples

A forest draw
A deer emerged quietly from the draw at dusk.
The cattle grazed in the draw, shaded from the midday sun.
We hiked down the draw to reach the stream at its bottom.
