Tadzhik

noun
member of a Central Asian ethnic group
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːdʒɪk /; / tɑːdˈʒiːk / (AmE), / ˈtadʒɪk /; / tædˈʒiːk / (BrE)
a person who is a member of the Tajik ethnic group or a native/inhabitant of Tajikistan and neighboring regions

Many Tadzhik communities have maintained their traditional customs.
The Tadzhik language is closely related to Persian.
