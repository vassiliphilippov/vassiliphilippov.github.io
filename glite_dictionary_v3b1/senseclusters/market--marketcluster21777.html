market

noun
place for selling groceries or food items
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑːr.kɪt/ /; / /ˈmɑːr.kət/ (AmE), /ˈmɑː.kɪt/ /; / /ˈmɑː.kət/ (BrE)
