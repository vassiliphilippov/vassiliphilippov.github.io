market

noun
place for selling groceries or food items
1.0 48/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…ërk…ôt / (AmE), / Ààm…ëÀêk…™t / (BrE)
a place where people buy and sell goods, such as food or other products

Examples

A busy market
After work, she stopped by the market to pick up groceries for dinner.
Every Saturday morning, I go to the local market to buy fresh fruits and vegetables.
The old town market is famous for its selection of cheeses and baked goods.
Every Saturday, the town holds a market in the main square.
She bought fresh fruit and vegetables at the local market.
Vendors set up their stalls early in the morning before the market opened.

Synonyms

  • bazaar
    3.4 1.42/m
