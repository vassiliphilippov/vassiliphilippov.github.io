royal

adjective
chartered or authorized by royalty
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔɪ.əl / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪ.əl / (BrE)
(of an organization or institution) established, chartered, or authorized by a king, queen, or other monarch.

Examples

Royal Navy
He works for the Royal Mint, which produces coins for the United Kingdom.
Many historic organizations in Britain have the Royal prefix to signify their connection to the monarchy.
The Royal Society was founded with a charter from King Charles II.

Related Terms

  • synonym: regal - belonging to or befitting a king
