Benadryl

noun
antihistamine drug for allergies and motion sickness
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛnəˌdrɪl / (AmE), / ˈbɛnəˌdrɪl / (BrE)
A trademarked antihistamine drug used to relieve symptoms of allergies such as hay fever and to treat motion sickness.

Examples

Benadryl allergy medicine
He keeps Benadryl in his travel bag in case he gets motion sickness.
My doctor recommended Benadryl to help with my seasonal allergies.
You can take Benadryl to reduce itching caused by insect bites.
