declension

noun
inflectional category of nouns/pronouns/adjectives
Pronunciation: / dɪˈklɛnʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈklɛnʃən / (BrE)
the pattern or system of grammatical changes (inflections) in nouns, pronouns, or adjectives to show case, number, or gender
