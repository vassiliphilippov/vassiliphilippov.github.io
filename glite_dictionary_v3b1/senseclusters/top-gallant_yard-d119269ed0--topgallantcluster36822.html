top-gallant yard

noun
spar above topsail for topgallant sail
Pronunciation: / tɑp ˈɡæl.ənt jɑrd / (AmE), / tɒp ˈɡæl.ənt jɑːd / (BrE)
a horizontal spar on a sailing ship from which the topgallant sail is set, located above the topsail yard

Examples

The top-gallant yard on ship
During the storm, a rope snapped and the top-gallant yard began to swing dangerously.
Sailors worked together to secure the top-gallant yard before the weather worsened.
The crew climbed to the top-gallant yard to unfurl the sail as the wind picked up.
