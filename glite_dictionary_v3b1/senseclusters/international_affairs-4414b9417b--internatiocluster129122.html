international affairs

noun
relations and events between nations
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntɚˈnæʃənəl əˈfɛrz / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈnæʃənəl əˈfeəz / (BrE)
matters, events, or relations involving two or more countries, especially concerning politics, economics, or diplomacy

Examples

International affairs management
His job in government involves managing international affairs and maintaining diplomatic relationships.
She has a keen interest in international affairs and reads news from several countries.
The conference addressed major challenges currently facing international affairs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: domestic affairs - matters occurring within a single country
  • synonym: foreign affairs - matters and relations involving other countries
