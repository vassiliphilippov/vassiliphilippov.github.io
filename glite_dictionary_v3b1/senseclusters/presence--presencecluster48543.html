presence

noun
dignified or impressive manner
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛzəns / (AmE), / ˈprɛzəns / (BrE)
A dignified or impressive manner or conduct, especially one that commands attention or respect.

Examples

A strong presence
Although he spoke little, his presence made others take notice.
She walked into the room with such presence that everyone stopped to look.
The actor had a commanding presence on stage, captivating the entire audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bearing - the way a person carries themself; manner, deportment
  • synonym: demeanor - outward behavior or manner
