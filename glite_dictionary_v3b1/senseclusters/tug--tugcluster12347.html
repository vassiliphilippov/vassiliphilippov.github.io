tug

noun
small powerful boat for towing ships
Pronunciation: / tʌɡ / (AmE), / tʌɡ / (BrE)
a small, powerful boat used for towing or pushing ships and barges, especially in harbors or along rivers

Examples

A tug boat
A tug waited at the dock, ready to assist arriving vessels.
Several tugs were needed to tow the giant oil tanker out to sea.
The tug maneuvered the massive ship safely into the harbor.
