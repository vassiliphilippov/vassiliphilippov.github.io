record company

noun
company that produces and sells music recordings
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛkərd ˌkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˈrɛkɔːd ˌkʌmpəni / (BrE)
a company that produces, markets, and sells musical recordings, often managing artists and their releases

Examples

A record company signs artists
Many independent artists now release music without the help of a record company.
She signed a contract with a major record company after her first single became popular.
The record company handled the distribution and promotion of the new album.

Related Terms

  • synonym: record label - a brand or trademark associated with the marketing of music recordings
