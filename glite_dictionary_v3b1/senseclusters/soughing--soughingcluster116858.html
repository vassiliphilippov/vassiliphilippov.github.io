soughing

adjective
making a soft murmuring or sighing sound
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌfɪŋ / (BrE)
(of wind or trees) making a soft murmuring, rustling, or sighing sound.

Examples

A soughing wind
A soughing wind swept gently through the pine forest, creating a calming atmosphere.
At night, I found the soughing of the sea outside my window soothing as I drifted to sleep.
The soughing branches overhead made it difficult to hear anything else in the garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: murmuring - making a low, continuous sound
  • synonym: rustling - making a soft, muffled crackling sound
