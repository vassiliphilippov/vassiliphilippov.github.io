match-up

noun
pairing for competition or comparison
Pronunciation: / ˈmætʃ.ʌp / (AmE), / ˈmatʃ.ʌp / (BrE)
An event or situation in which people or things are paired against each other, especially for competition or comparison.

Examples

A match-up game
Many fans are excited about the heavyweight match-up this weekend.
The match-up of these two companies will affect the entire industry.
Tomorrow's basketball match-up will be broadcast live on television.

Related Terms

  • synonym: competition - an organized event in which people compete
  • synonym: contest - an event in which people compete for supremacy
