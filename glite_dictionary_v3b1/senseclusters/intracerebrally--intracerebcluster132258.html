intracerebrally

adverb
within the brain
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəsəˈrɛbrəli / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəsəˈriːbrəli / (BrE)
in a way that occurs or is administered within the brain

Examples

Injected intracerebrally
The medication was injected intracerebrally to target the affected brain region directly.
Researchers studied the effects of the virus when delivered intracerebrally in animal models.
The treatment was administered intracerebrally to bypass the blood-brain barrier.
