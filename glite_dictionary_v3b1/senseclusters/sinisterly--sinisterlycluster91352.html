sinisterly

adverb
toward the left
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪnɪstərli / (AmE), / ˈsɪnɪstəli / (BrE)
in a direction toward the left side, especially in heraldry.

Examples

Sinisterly facing left
In heraldic art, the lion is often shown looking sinisterly to indicate a specific family lineage.
The knight's shield was divided, with the eagle facing sinisterly as per tradition.
On the ancient banner, the animal was positioned sinisterly, symbolizing a turn to the left.
