linga

noun
Hindu religious symbol
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪŋɡə / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋɡə / (BrE)
a symbolic representation of divine energy and the god Shiva, especially in Hinduism

Examples

Sacred linga in temple
Devotees gathered at the temple to pray before the sacred linga.
The priest carefully poured water over the linga during the morning ritual.
Many temples in India have a central linga as a symbol of Shiva.

  • synonym: symbol - an object that represents an idea or quality
