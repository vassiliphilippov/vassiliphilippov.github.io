apostolic delegate

noun
Holy See representative without diplomatic status
Pronunciation: / ˌæpəˈstɑlɪk ˈdɛlɪˌɡeɪt / (AmE), / ˌapəˈstɒlɪk ˈdɛlɪɡət / (BrE)
a representative of the Holy See appointed to a country that has no formal diplomatic relations with the Vatican

Examples

Apostolic delegate to country
An apostolic delegate does not hold official diplomatic status in the host country.
The apostolic delegate attended the ceremony on behalf of the Vatican.
The Pope appointed a new apostolic delegate to oversee relations with local Catholic communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nuncio - a diplomatic representative of the Holy See with ambassadorial status
