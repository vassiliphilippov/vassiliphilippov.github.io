Thomas Robert Malthus

proper noun
English economist and demographer (1766–1834)
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈtɑː.məs ˈrɑː.bərt ˈmæl.θəs/ (AmE), /ˈtɒ.məs ˈrɒ.bət ˈmæl.θəs/ (BrE)
(1766–1834) An English economist and demographer known for his theory that population growth would eventually outpace food supply, leading to societal strain.
