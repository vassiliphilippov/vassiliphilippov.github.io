chain

noun
series of connected metal rings or links
Pronunciation: / tʃeɪn / (AmE), / tʃeɪn / (BrE)
a series of metal rings or links connected together, used for fastening, pulling, supporting, or as a restraint

Examples

Metal chain link
She wore a delicate gold chain around her neck.
The dog was kept outside with a heavy chain attached to its collar.
The old gate was secured with a rusty iron chain.
The old castle's dungeon still held rusted chains attached to the stone walls.
The prisoner struggled against the chains that bound his ankles.
They removed the chains from her wrists after the trial was over.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cable - a thick, strong rope or wire
  • synonym: fetters - chains or manacles for restraining prisoners
  • synonym: handcuffs - a pair of locking restraints for the wrists
  • antonym: rope - a strong, thick line made of fibers or wire twisted together
  • synonym: shackle - metal fastening for restraining someone
