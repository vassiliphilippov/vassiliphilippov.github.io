hiring

noun
renting for temporary use
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪɚɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of renting something, such as a car or equipment, for temporary use, especially in British English

Examples

Hiring a car
The hiring of bicycles is popular among tourists visiting the city.
We arranged the hiring of a van for our weekend trip.
Due to high demand, the hiring of camping equipment must be booked in advance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: purchase - the act of buying something to own it permanently
  • synonym: rental - the act of paying to use something temporarily
