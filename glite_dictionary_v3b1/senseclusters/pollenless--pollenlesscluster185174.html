pollenless

adjective
without pollen
5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈpɑː.lən.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈpɒl.ən.ləs/ (BrE)
not having, producing, or covered with pollen (usually describing a plant, flower, or environment)

Examples

pollenless flowers
Many gardeners prefer pollenless lilies to avoid triggering allergies.
The new pollenless sunflower variety is ideal for people sensitive to pollen.
Researchers developed a pollenless plant to help reduce the spread of certain allergens.
The laboratory maintained a strictly pollenless environment to prevent contamination of samples.
Many allergy sufferers prefer pollenless flowers in their homes during spring.
After the storm, the air felt refreshingly pollenless and clean.
