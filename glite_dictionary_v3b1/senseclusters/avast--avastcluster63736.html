avast

interjection
nautical command to stop
Pronunciation: / əˈvæst / (AmE), / əˈvɑːst / (BrE)
a command used to tell someone to stop or cease an action, especially on ships

Examples

Say avast now
The captain yelled 'avast!' as the sailors rushed to lower the sails.
Hearing the command 'avast!', the crew immediately stopped what they were doing.
In old pirate movies, you often hear someone shout 'avast!' to get everyone's attention.

Related Terms

  • antonym: go - to move or proceed
  • synonym: stop - to cease moving or doing something
