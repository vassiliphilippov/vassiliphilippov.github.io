sprawl

noun
extensive area of urban development
Pronunciation: / sprɔl / (AmE), / sprɔːl / (BrE)
A continuous area of urban development, typically characterized by rapid expansion and a lack of planning.

Examples

Urban sprawl
From the air, the sprawl of Los Angeles seems to stretch endlessly toward the horizon.
Many environmentalists criticize the unchecked sprawl around major urban centers.
The city has become a vast sprawl of neighborhoods, shopping malls, and highways.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conurbation - an extended urban area, typically consisting of several towns merging with the suburbs of a central city
  • synonym: urbanization - the process by which towns and cities grow larger
