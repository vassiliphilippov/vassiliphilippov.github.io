floozie

noun
promiscuous woman, prostitute (informal)
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfluzi / (AmE), / ˈfluːzi / (BrE)
an informal and sometimes derogatory term for a woman regarded as sexually promiscuous, often implying a prostitute

Examples

A floozie at bar
He left the party with a floozie he had just met at the bar.
She bristled at being called a floozie for wearing a short skirt.
The novelist's depiction of a city street included gamblers, drunks, and floozies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harlot - derogatory term for a promiscuous woman or prostitute
  • synonym: prostitute - a person who has sex with others for payment
