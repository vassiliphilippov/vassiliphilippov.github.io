prairie fairy

noun
mythical prairie-dwelling creature
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛr.i ˌfɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈprɛə.ri ˌfeə.ri / (BrE)
A mythical creature associated with prairies, often featured in folklore as a small, magical being connected with nature.

Examples

The prairie fairy legend
Legends say the prairie fairy appears to those who respect the land.
She decorated her room with pictures of the prairie fairy and wildflowers.
The children searched the tall grasses, hoping to catch a glimpse of the prairie fairy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fairy - small, imaginary being of human form with magical powers
