educational institution

noun
organization for education, like a school or university
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɛdʒəˈkeɪʃənl ˌɪnstəˈtuːʃən / (AmE), / ˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃənl ˌɪnstɪˈtjuːʃən / (BrE)
An organization such as a school, college, or university that is devoted to education and learning.

Examples

A educational institution for students
Many international students choose to study at a prestigious educational institution.
She hopes to work at an educational institution after completing her degree.
The government provides funding for every educational institution in the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: school - a place where children or young people receive education
  • synonym: university - an institution of higher education and research
