tassel

verb
to form tassels
Pronunciation: / ˈtæsəl / (AmE), / ˈtæsəl / (BrE)
to produce or develop tassels, especially referring to the flowering part of plants like corn or sorghum

Examples

Corn plants tassel
The corn plants began tasseling earlier than expected due to the warm weather.
Farmers monitor when their crops start tasseling to plan the next steps in cultivation.
Once the sorghum is tasseling, it signals the start of the pollination period.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flower - to produce flowers or bloom
