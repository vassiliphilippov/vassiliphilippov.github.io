Culex pipiens

noun
common house mosquito species
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːlɛks pɪˈpiːɛnz / (AmE), / ˈkjuːlɛks pɪˈpiːɛnz / (BrE)
A species of mosquito commonly found in temperate regions, known as the common house mosquito, often associated with transmitting diseases.

Examples

Culex pipiens mosquito species
Culex pipiens is commonly found breeding in stagnant water near homes and gardens.
Health officials monitor Culex pipiens populations to prevent outbreaks of West Nile virus.
The larvae of Culex pipiens develop in standing water and can cause significant nuisance during summer months.
