goniometry

noun
measurement of angles
Pronunciation: / ˌɡoʊniˈɑːmətri / (AmE), / ˌɡəʊniˈɒmətri / (BrE)
the measurement of angles, especially in joints or geometric figures

Examples

Goniometry of joints
Physical therapists use goniometry to assess a patient's range of motion.
Accurate goniometry is essential for evaluating joint injuries in athletes.
In geometry class, students learned about goniometry to measure different angles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angle measurement - the process of determining the size of an angle
  • synonym: trigonometry - the branch of mathematics dealing with the relationships between angles and sides of triangles
