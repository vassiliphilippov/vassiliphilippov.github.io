sunfall

noun
time of sunset
2.7 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs ånÀåf…îl / (AmE), / Ààs ånÀåf…îÀêl / (BrE)
the time of day when the sun sets below the horizon; sunset

Examples

Sunfall over hills
The sky turned orange at sunfall over the quiet hills.
They reached the village just before sunfall, as the last light faded from the sky.
Birds returned to their nests at sunfall, filling the air with gentle songs.

Synonyms

  • sunset
    1.0 15/m
    sunset is the evening moment when light fades and colours glow on the sky, while sunfall is a less common name for that time.
  • sundown
    1.2 2.17/m
    sundown is an informal, often poetic word for the evening when the sun goes down, while sunfall is a rarer synonym.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dusk - the time just after the sun has set, when the sky is partly dark
  • antonym: sunrise - the time in the morning when the sun appears above the horizon
  • synonym: sunset - the time in the evening when the sun disappears below the horizon
