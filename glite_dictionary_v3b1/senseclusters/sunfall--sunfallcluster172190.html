sunfall

noun
time of sunset
2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈsʌn.fɑːl/ /; / /ˈsʌn.fɔːl/ (AmE), /ˈsʌn.fɔːl/ (BrE)

Examples

Birds returned to their nests at sunfall, filling the air with gentle songs.
