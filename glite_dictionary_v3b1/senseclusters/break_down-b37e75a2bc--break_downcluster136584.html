break down

verb
(of a machine/vehicle) stop working
1.0 2.7
Pronunciation: /breɪk daʊn/ (AmE), /breɪk daʊn/ (BrE)
(of a machine, system, or process) to stop working or functioning properly; to fail or collapse

Examples

The car breaks down

My car broke down on the highway during rush hour.

My car broke down on the way to work this morning.

The old lawn mower broke down halfway through cutting the grass.
