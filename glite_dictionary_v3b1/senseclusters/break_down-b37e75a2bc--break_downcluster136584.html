Synonyms

  • fail
    2.2 17/m
    fail means to stop working or operating properly, while break down often describes a machine's sudden malfunction.
  • crash
    3.2 7/m
    crash stops suddenly, often for computers or systems, while break down can be slower or more general in use.
  • cut out
    4.1 2/m
    cut out stops working suddenly, often an engine or part, while break down covers many types of failure or fault.
  • conk out
    8.1 0.3/m
    conk out suddenly fails and sounds informal, while break down is more neutral and used in many contexts.
  • go down
    3.4 9/m
    go down stops operating, often temporarily for services or sites, while break down refers to any machine or system failure.
  • give out
    4.1 3/m
    give out stops working or becomes used up from wear or use, while break down usually means a fault or failure.
  • die
    4.6 20/m
    die stops operating completely and often permanently, while break down can be temporary and is often repairable.
  • come down
    4.2 0.3/m
    come down becomes unavailable, often for websites or networks, while break down covers machines and wider kinds of failure.
  • collapse
    3.9 30/m
    collapse falls apart or fails suddenly on a larger scale, while break down usually means a machine or system stops working.
