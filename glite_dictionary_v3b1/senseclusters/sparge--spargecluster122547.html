sparge

noun
act of sprinkling or spraying liquid
Pronunciation: / spɑrdʒ / (AmE), / spɑːdʒ / (BrE)
an act of sprinkling or spraying liquid, especially water, over something; commonly used in brewing to refer to rinsing grain with water to extract sugars

Examples

A gentle sparge
A gentle sparge ensured all the grains were thoroughly rinsed during the brewing process.
He performed a quick sparge over the garden to keep the plants hydrated in the heat.
The technician instructed us to use a warm water sparge to extract maximum flavor from the malt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spray - to send out liquid in very small drops
  • synonym: sprinkle - to scatter small drops or particles over a surface
