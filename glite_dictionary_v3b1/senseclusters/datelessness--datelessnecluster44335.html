datelessness

noun
lack of dates
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪtləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈdeɪtləsnəs / (BrE)
the state of not having romantic or social dates with a partner.

Examples

His datelessness continues
He joked about his datelessness at the party, but it clearly bothered him.
Her long period of datelessness ended when she joined a local hiking group.
Many people experience datelessness after moving to a new city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: singleness - the state of being single or not in a romantic relationship
