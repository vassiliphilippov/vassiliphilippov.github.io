burdenless

adjective
free from any burden or load
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜrdnˌlɪs / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdnlɪs / (BrE)
Not encumbered with a physical burden or any load; free from a burden.

Examples

A burdenless life
After donating most of her possessions, she felt remarkably burdenless and free.
Meditation helped him achieve a burdenless state of mind.
The hikers moved swiftly, their packs nearly empty and their bodies burdenless.

Related Terms
