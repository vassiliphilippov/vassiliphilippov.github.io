drawing card

noun
attraction or item used to draw customers
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɔɪ.ɪŋ kɑrd /; / ˈdrɔ.ɪŋ ˌkɑrd /; / ˈdrɔɪŋ ˌkɑrd / (AmE), / ˈdrɔː.ɪŋ kɑːd /; / ˈdrɔː.ɪŋ ˌkɑːd /; / ˈdrɔːɪŋ ˌkɑːd / (BrE)
a person, thing, or feature that attracts people or attention
