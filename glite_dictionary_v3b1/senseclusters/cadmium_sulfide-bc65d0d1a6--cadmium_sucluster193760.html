cadmium sulfide

noun
yellow pigment compound (CdS)
4.1 6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkæd.miː.əm ˈsʌl.faɪd/ (AmE), /ˈkæd.mɪ.əm ˈsʌl.faɪd/ (BrE)
a yellow inorganic crystalline compound of cadmium and sulfur (CdS), used as a pigment in paints and plastics and in electronic devices such as some types of solar cells
