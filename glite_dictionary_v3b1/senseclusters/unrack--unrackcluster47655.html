unrack

verb
to remove from rack
7.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈræk / (AmE), / ʌnˈræk / (BrE)
to remove something from a rack or take it out of a racked position

Examples

To unrack a coat
She unracked her coat from the hallway stand before leaving the house.
The technician carefully unracked the server to perform maintenance.
After the race, he unracked his bicycle and rode home.
