erratically

adverb
in an irregular or unpredictable way
8.7
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrætɪkli / (AmE), / ɪˈrætɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that is irregular, unpredictable, or lacking a fixed pattern.

Examples

To drive erratically
He drove erratically after the storm made the roads slippery.
Her moods changed erratically, making her difficult to predict.
The stock market has behaved erratically over the past few weeks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irregularly - in an irregular manner
  • antonym: predictably - in a predictable or expected manner
