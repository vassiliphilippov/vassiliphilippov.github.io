in toto

adverb
completely; as a whole
Pronunciation: / ɪn ˈtoʊtoʊ / (AmE), / ɪn ˈtəʊtəʊ / (BrE)
completely; in its entirety; as a whole, without anything left out or excluded.

Examples

Believed in toto
Her testimony was believed in toto by the jury.
The recommendations were accepted in toto by the committee.
They decided to adopt the proposal in toto rather than modifying it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: entirely - completely; with nothing left out
  • antonym: in part - partially; not completely
