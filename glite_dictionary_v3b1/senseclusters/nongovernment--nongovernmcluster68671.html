nongovernment

adjective
not governmental
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈɡʌvərnmənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈɡʌvənmənt / (BrE)
not related to, controlled by, or part of the government

Examples

Nongovernment funding
The project was funded by a nongovernment source to ensure impartiality.
Several nongovernment organizations work to protect the environment in that region.
He prefers to work for nongovernment agencies because of their flexibility.

Related Terms

  • antonym: governmental - relating to or controlled by the government
  • synonym: independent - not influenced or controlled by others, especially by the government
