oatmeal

noun
food made from cooked oats
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊtˌmiːl / (AmE), / ˈəʊtmiːl /; / ˈəʊtˌmiːl / (BrE)
a soft food or porridge made by cooking oats in water or milk, usually eaten for breakfast

Examples

A bowl of oatmeal
He eats a bowl of oatmeal every morning for breakfast.
Many people find oatmeal both filling and nutritious.
My grandmother likes her oatmeal with honey and fresh fruit.
Oatmeal is often enjoyed with fresh fruit and honey for breakfast.
It is healthier to choose plain oatmeal instead of instant flavored packets.
She makes a bowl of oatmeal every morning before work.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: gruel - a thin liquid food of oatmeal or other meal boiled in milk or water
  • synonym: porridge - a soft food made by boiling grains in water or milk
