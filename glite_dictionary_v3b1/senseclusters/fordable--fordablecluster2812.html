fordable

adjective
shallow enough to be crossed (of water)
3.5 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈfɑːr.də.bəl/ /; / /ˈfɔːr.də.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈfɔː.də.bəl/ (BrE)
(of a river or stream) shallow enough to be crossed by walking, riding, or driving through it.

Examples
