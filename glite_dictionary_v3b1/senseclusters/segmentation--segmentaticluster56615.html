segmentation

noun
division into parts or sections
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛɡ.mənˈteɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɛɡ.mɛnˈteɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of dividing something into separate parts or sections

Examples

Segmentation of a market
Cell segmentation is a crucial step in analyzing microscope images.
Geographic segmentation enables businesses to tailor their services to different regions.
The segmentation of the market allows companies to better target their customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: division - the action of separating something into parts
  • antonym: integration - the action of combining or unifying parts into a whole
