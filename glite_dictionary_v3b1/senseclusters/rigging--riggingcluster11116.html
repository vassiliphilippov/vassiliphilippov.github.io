rigging

noun
system of ropes and masts on a ship
6.4 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈrɪ.gɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈrɪ.gɪŋ/ (BrE)
the system of ropes, cables, and chains used on a ship to support the masts and control the sails

Examples

Ship rigging system
Restoring the old yacht included replacing much of its original rigging.
Sailors inspected the rigging before setting off on their voyage.
The storm damaged the rigging and left the ship adrift at sea.
She climbed the rigging to untangle a rope near the top of the mast.
Storms can easily damage the rigging on even the strongest ships.
The sailors repaired the rigging before setting out to sea.
