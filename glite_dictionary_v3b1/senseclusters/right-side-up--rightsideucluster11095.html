right-side-up

adjective
in the correct upright position
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˌraɪt saɪd ˈʌp / (AmE), / ˌraɪt saɪd ˈʌp / (BrE)
(of an object) having its top part uppermost; in the correct upright position.

Examples

Right-side-up picture
Make sure the painting is right-side-up before hanging it on the wall.
She accidentally placed the book right-side-up after flipping through it upside down.
The movers were careful to keep all the boxes right-side-up during transport.

Related Terms

  • antonym: upside-down - with the top part facing downward; inverted
