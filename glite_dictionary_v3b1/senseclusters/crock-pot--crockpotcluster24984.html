crock-pot

noun
slow-cooked dish
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɑkˌpɑt / (AmE), / ˈkrɒkˌpɒt / (BrE)
a meal or dish that has been cooked slowly in a crock-pot or slow cooker

Examples

A crock-pot meal
She prepared a delicious crock-pot of beef stew for the family dinner.
At the party, everyone enjoyed the homemade crock-pot full of spicy chili.
He brought a vegetarian crock-pot to share at the office lunch.

Related Terms

  • antonym: roast - a dish cooked by roasting, usually in an oven
  • synonym: stew - a dish of meat and vegetables cooked slowly in liquid
