mythically

adverb
in a mythical way
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪθɪkli / (AmE), / ˈmɪθɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to myths, legends, or magical stories

Examples

Mythically created heroes
The city was mythically founded by a pair of twin brothers raised by wolves.
Some animals are mythically believed to have magical powers in ancient cultures.
The mountain is mythically said to be the home of the gods.

Related Terms

  • antonym: factually - in a way based on facts or reality
  • synonym: legendary - in a way that relates to legends or famous stories
