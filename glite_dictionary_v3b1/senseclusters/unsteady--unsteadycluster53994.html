unsteady

verb
to make unstable
4.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstɛdi / (AmE), / ʌnˈstɛdi / (BrE)
to make something less stable or balanced

Examples

To unsteady the table
The sudden movement unsteadied the vase, causing it to wobble dangerously on the shelf.
Strong waves unsteady the boat, making it difficult for passengers to stand.
A loud noise from outside unsteadied her hand as she poured the tea.
