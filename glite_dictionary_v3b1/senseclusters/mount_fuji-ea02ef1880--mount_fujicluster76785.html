Mount Fuji

proper noun
Japan's highest mountain; sacred volcano
2.2 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / ma änt ÀàfuÀêd íi / (AmE), / ma änt ÀàfuÀêd íi / (BrE)
a dormant volcanic mountain in south central Honshu, Japan, known as the country's highest peak and a site of cultural and religious significance

Examples

Mount Fuji summit
Many tourists climb Mount Fuji each year to watch the sunrise from the summit.
On clear days, Mount Fuji can be seen from Tokyo, over 100 kilometers away.
The snow-capped peak of Mount Fuji is often depicted in traditional Japanese art.

Synonyms

  • Fuji
    4.9 1.88/m
    Fuji is the common short name used by people and visitors, while Mount Fuji is the mountain's full, formal name.
  • Fuji-san
    7.3 0.00/m
    Fuji-san is the respectful Japanese name used by locals and at shrines, while Mount Fuji is the English full name.
  • Fujiyama
    7.4 0.04/m
    Fujiyama is an older anglicized form often seen in foreign art and speech, while Mount Fuji is the correct modern name.
