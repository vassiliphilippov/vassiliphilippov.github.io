lymphoblastic leukemia

noun
type of leukemia with lymphoblasts
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪm.foʊˈblæs.tɪk lʊˈkiː.mi.ə / (AmE), / ˌlɪm.fəʊˈblæs.tɪk ljuːˈkiː.mi.ə / (BrE)
A form of leukemia in which the abnormal white blood cells are primarily immature lymphoblasts.

Examples

Lymphoblastic leukemia treatment
He underwent chemotherapy to combat the aggressive nature of his lymphoblastic leukemia.
Recent advances in treatment have improved survival rates for patients with lymphoblastic leukemia.
The doctor diagnosed the child with lymphoblastic leukemia after noticing a high number of lymphoblasts in the blood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leukemia - cancer of blood-forming tissues
  • degree lower: myeloblastic leukemia - a form of leukemia involving myeloblasts
