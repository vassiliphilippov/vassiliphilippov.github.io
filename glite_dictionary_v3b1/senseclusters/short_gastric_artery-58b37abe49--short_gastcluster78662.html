short gastric artery

noun
artery supplying stomach's greater curvature
5.3
Pronunciation: / ʃɔrt ˈɡæs.trɪk ˈɑr.t̬ə.ri / (AmE), / ʃɔːt ˈɡæs.trɪk ˈɑː.tə.ri / (BrE)
any of several small arteries branching from the splenic artery to supply the greater curvature of the stomach

Examples

Damage to the short gastric artery can result in gastric ischemia.

The anatomical course of the short gastric artery can vary between individuals.
