tax bracket

noun
income range subject to a tax rate
Pronunciation: / tæks ˈbrækət / (AmE), / tæks ˈbrakɪt / (BrE)
A range of incomes taxed at a particular rate, used to categorize taxpayers according to the amount of income they earn.

Examples

A tax bracket range
Different countries define tax brackets in various ways to ensure a progressive system.
If your income increases, you may move into a higher tax bracket.
She consulted an accountant to find out which tax bracket she was in.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tax rate - the percentage at which an individual or corporation is taxed
