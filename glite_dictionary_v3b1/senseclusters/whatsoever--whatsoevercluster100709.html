whatsoever

determiner
emphatic determiner
Pronunciation: / ˌwʌtsoʊˈɛvər / (AmE), / ˌwɒtsəʊˈɛvə / (BrE)
used after a noun to emphasize that there is not even the smallest amount or type of something

Examples

No interest whatsoever
He has no interest in sports whatsoever and prefers to read instead.
There was no evidence whatsoever to support the claim.
She received no help whatsoever during the project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whatever - any or all of a thing, no matter what
