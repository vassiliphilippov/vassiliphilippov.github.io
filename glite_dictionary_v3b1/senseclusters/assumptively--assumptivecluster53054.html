assumptively

adverb
based on assumption
Pronunciation: / əˈsʌmptɪvli / (AmE), / əˈsʌmptɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that is based on assumption or supposition rather than on proof

Examples

To assumptively believe
She assumptively believed the meeting was canceled without confirming with anyone.
The report was assumptively written before all the data was collected.
He assumptively took the lead, thinking no one else wanted the job.

Related Terms

  • synonym: presumptively - in a way that is presumed or taken for granted
  • synonym: speculatively - in a way that is based on guesswork or conjecture
