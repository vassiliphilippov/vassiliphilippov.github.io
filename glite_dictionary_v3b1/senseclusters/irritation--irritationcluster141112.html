irritation

noun
behavior causing anger or resentment
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrəˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪrɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
unfriendly or provocative behavior that causes anger or resentment in others

Examples

Irritation tactics
Her sarcastic remarks were a form of irritation meant to provoke her classmates.
Repeated irritation from the neighbors eventually led to an argument.
The manager's constant irritation towards his subordinates created a tense work environment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aggravation - the act of making a situation worse or more severe, often causing annoyance or resentment
  • synonym: annoyance - the feeling or state of being annoyed; a nuisance or source of irritation
  • antonym: appeasement - the act of making someone less angry by giving in to their demands
