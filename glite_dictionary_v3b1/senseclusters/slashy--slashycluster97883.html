slashy

adjective
about same-sex fan fiction
Pronunciation: / ˈslæʃi / (AmE), / ˈslæʃi / (BrE)
relating to fan fiction or works that depict romantic or sexual relationships between same-sex fictional characters, especially in popular media.

Examples

Slashy fan stories
Many fans enjoy writing slashy stories about their favorite television characters.
The online community is known for its slashy artwork and creative writing.
She recommended a particularly slashy novel to her friends at the book club.

Related Terms

  • antonym: canon - official or original storylines in fiction
  • synonym: slash - fan fiction about same-sex relationships between fictional characters
