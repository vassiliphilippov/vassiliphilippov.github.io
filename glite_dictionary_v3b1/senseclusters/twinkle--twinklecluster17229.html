twinkle

noun
lively brightness in the eyes or face
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪŋkəl / (AmE), / ˈtwɪŋkəl / (BrE)
A bright or lively expression in the eyes or face that suggests merriment or animation.

Examples

A twinkle in her eyes
As she accepted the award, a proud twinkle lit up her face.
He spoke with a twinkle that made everyone in the room smile.
There was a mischievous twinkle in her grandfather's eyes as he told the story.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sparkle - a lively or bright expression seen in someone's eyes or face
