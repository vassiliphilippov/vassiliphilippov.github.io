point of no return

noun
stage after which reversal is impossible
2.3 4.4
Pronunciation: /pɔɪnt əv noʊ rɪ.ˈtɜːrn/ /; / /pɔɪnt əv noʊ riː.ˈtɜːrn/ (AmE), /pɔɪnt əv nəʊ rɪ.ˈtɜːn/ (BrE)
