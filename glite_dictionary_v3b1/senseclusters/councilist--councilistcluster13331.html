councilist

noun
council member
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊnsəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈkaʊnsəlɪst / (BrE)
a member of a council, especially an elected member of a municipal or organizational governing body

Examples

City councilist
The newly elected councilist promised to improve local services for the community.
Each neighborhood sends a councilist to participate in city planning meetings.
As a councilist, she worked hard to represent the interests of her district.

Related Terms

  • synonym: councilor - an elected member of a council
  • synonym: representative - a person chosen to act or speak for others
