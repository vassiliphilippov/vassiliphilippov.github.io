aircraft engine

noun
engine that powers an aircraft
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌkræft ˈɛn.dʒɪn / (AmE), / ˈeəˌkrɑːft ˈɛn.dʒɪn / (BrE)
an engine that supplies power to an aircraft, enabling it to fly

Examples

Modern aircraft engine
Fuel efficiency is a major concern in the design of any modern aircraft engine.
Technicians spent hours repairing the aircraft engine before the next scheduled flight.
The new fighter jet is equipped with a powerful aircraft engine capable of supersonic flight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jet engine - an engine that discharges a fast stream of gases to generate thrust, used in jets
  • degree lower: piston engine - a type of engine with pistons, used in some aircraft
