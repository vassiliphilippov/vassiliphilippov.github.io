hemoconcentration

noun
increased blood concentration
9.3 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåhiÀêmo äÀåk…ëÀêns…ônÀàtre…™ É…ôn / (AmE), / ÀåhiÀêm…ô äÀåk…íns…ônÀàtre…™ É…ôn / (BrE)
an increase in the concentration of blood cells and solids due to a loss of plasma

Examples

Hemoconcentration in blood
Severe vomiting and diarrhea can cause hemoconcentration in children.
Doctors monitor hemoconcentration to assess the severity of a patient's dehydration.
After intense exercise, temporary hemoconcentration may occur due to fluid loss.

Related Terms
