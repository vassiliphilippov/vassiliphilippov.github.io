calendarist

noun
calendar system expert
Pronunciation: / ˈkæləndərɪst / (AmE), / ˈkælɪndərɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies, designs, or advocates for calendar systems

Examples

Expert calendarist theory
The calendarist presented his ideas for a more efficient leap year system at the conference.
As a renowned calendarist, she was invited to consult on the international date reform project.
Historians often rely on the work of a calendarist to understand ancient timekeeping methods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chronologist - a person who studies or arranges events in order of time
