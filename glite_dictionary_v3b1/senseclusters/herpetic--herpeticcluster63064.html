herpetic

adjective
caused by herpes
Pronunciation: / hərˈpɛtɪk / (AmE), / hɜːˈpɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or caused by the herpes virus

Examples

Herpetic infection
The doctor diagnosed her with a herpetic infection after examining the blisters.
A herpetic sore can be painful and may take several days to heal.
He was prescribed antiviral medication to treat the herpetic outbreak.

Related Terms

  • synonym: viral - caused by a virus
