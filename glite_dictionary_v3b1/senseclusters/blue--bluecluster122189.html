blue

adjective
color between green and violet
1.0
Pronunciation: / bluː / (AmE), / bluː / (BrE)
of the color between green and violet in the visible spectrum; resembling the color of a clear, unclouded sky

Examples

Blue sky
A blue sky stretched endlessly above the city.
She wore a blue dress to match her eyes.
The lake was a deep, blue color under the midday sun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: azure - bright blue color like the sky on a clear day
  • degree lower: green - color between blue and yellow
  • degree higher: violet - color between blue and purple
