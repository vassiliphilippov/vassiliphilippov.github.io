assist

noun
help or support; assistance
Pronunciation: / əˈsɪst / (AmE), / əˈsɪst / (BrE)
help or support given to someone in order to achieve something or make a task easier; assistance.

Examples

An assist with work
Her quick assist allowed the project to be completed on time.
I really appreciated your assist with moving the heavy furniture.
The teacher gave an assist to students struggling with the assignment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assistance - the action of helping or supporting someone
  • synonym: help - the act of making it easier for someone to do something
  • antonym: obstruction - the act of hindering or preventing progress
