foldaway

adjective
able to be folded for storage
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊldəˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈfəʊldəˌweɪ / (BrE)
(of furniture or objects) able to be folded up for convenient storage when not in use.

Examples

A foldaway table
She prefers a foldaway table for her small kitchen.
The apartment comes furnished with a foldaway bed to save space.
We stored the foldaway chairs in the closet after the party.
