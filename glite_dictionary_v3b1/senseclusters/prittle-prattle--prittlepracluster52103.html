prittle-prattle

noun
trivial or nonsensical talk
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪt(ə)lˌpræt(ə)l / (AmE), / ˈprɪt(ə)lˌprat(ə)l / (BrE)
trivial or foolish talk; chatter about unimportant things

Examples

Constant prittle-prattle
He was annoyed by the constant prittle-prattle from his younger siblings.
She tries to avoid prittle-prattle and prefers meaningful conversation.
The meeting dissolved into prittle-prattle about office gossip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: babble - talk rapidly and continuously in a foolish, excited way
  • synonym: chatter - rapid, trivial talk
