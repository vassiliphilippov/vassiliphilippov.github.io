bioethicist

noun
ethics expert in medicine
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪoʊˈɛθɪsɪst / (AmE), / ˌbaɪəʊˈɛθɪsɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies or gives advice on ethical issues in biology, medicine, or life sciences

Examples

As a bioethicist, she often speaks at conferences about medical research ethics.

The hospital consulted a bioethicist before making the decision.
A bioethicist may help resolve difficult questions about patient care and research.
Universities often invite a bioethicist to speak about new developments in genetic engineering.
