born

verb
to come into existence
Pronunciation: / bɔrn / (AmE), / bɔːn / (BrE)
to come into existence as a result of being given birth to

Examples

Born in Paris
He was born in Paris and moved to London as a child.
A new idea was born during their late-night discussion.
Many animals are born in the spring when food is plentiful.

  • synonym: emerge - to come into view or existence
