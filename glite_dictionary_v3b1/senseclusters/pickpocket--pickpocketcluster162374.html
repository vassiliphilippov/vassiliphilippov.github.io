pickpocket

noun
a thief who steals from people's pockets
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkˌpɑːkɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɪkˌpɒkɪt / (BrE)
a person who steals money or valuables from the pockets or bags of people in public places

Examples

A pickpocket steals wallets
A skilled pickpocket can steal a wallet without the victim noticing a thing.
She always keeps her bag zipped to avoid pickpocket theft in crowded areas.
The police arrested a pickpocket who had been targeting tourists near the train station.

Related Terms

  • synonym: robber - a person who steals, especially using force or threat
  • synonym: thief - a person who steals
