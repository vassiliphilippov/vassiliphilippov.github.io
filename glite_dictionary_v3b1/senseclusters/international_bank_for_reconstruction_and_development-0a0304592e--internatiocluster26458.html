International Bank for Reconstruction and Development

proper noun
UN agency providing loans for development
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntɚˈnæʃənəl bæŋk fɚ rɪˌkɑnstrəkˈʃən ənd dɪˈvɛləpmənt / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈnæʃənəl bæŋk fə rɪˌkɒnstrʌkˈʃən ənd dɪˈvɛləpmənt / (BrE)
A United Nations agency established to provide loans and financial assistance for the development of member countries, especially developing nations.
