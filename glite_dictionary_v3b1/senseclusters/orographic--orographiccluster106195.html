orographic

adjective
mountain-related
Pronunciation: / ˌɔrəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌɒrəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to the physical features, structure, or description of mountains and mountain ranges

Examples

Orographic map
The orographic map showed the elevation changes across the entire region.
Scientists studied orographic rainfall patterns caused by the nearby mountain range.
The team analyzed orographic influences on local climate and weather conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mountainous - having many mountains or relating to mountains
  • synonym: topographic - relating to the arrangement of the physical features of an area
