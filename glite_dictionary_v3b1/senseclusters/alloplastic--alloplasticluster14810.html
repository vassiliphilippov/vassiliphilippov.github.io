alloplastic

adjective
using artificial materials
Pronunciation: / ˌæləˈplæstɪk / (AmE), / ˌaləˈplastɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving the use of artificial or synthetic materials, especially in surgical procedures

Examples

Alloplastic implant material
The doctor recommended an alloplastic material for the bone graft.
Advances in alloplastic implants have improved outcomes in reconstructive surgery.
Some patients prefer alloplastic options over using their own tissue for repairs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: autoplastic - relating to the use of the patient's own tissues in surgery
  • synonym: synthetic - made by chemical synthesis, especially to imitate a natural product
