makeup

noun
cosmetics applied to the face
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪk.ʌp / (AmE), / ˈmeɪk.ʌp / (BrE)
Cosmetics such as powder, lipstick, and eye shadow applied to the face to enhance or alter appearance.

Examples

Put on makeup
Before the play, the actors spent an hour putting on makeup.
Many people use makeup to boost their confidence or highlight their features.
She wore minimal makeup for a natural look at the interview.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cosmetics - preparations applied to the body, especially the face, to improve appearance
