interspinous

adjective
between spines
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈspaɪnəs / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈspaɪnəs / (BrE)
situated or occurring between two spines, especially the bony projections of vertebrae

Examples

The interspinous space
The doctor examined the interspinous space for signs of swelling after the injury.
An interspinous device was used to stabilize the patient's lower back.
Pain in the interspinous region can indicate ligament strain between the vertebrae.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intervertebral - situated between the vertebrae
