A small North American juniper tree (Juniperus virginiana) with a conical crown, reddish-brown bark that peels in strips, and sharp needle-like leaves, native to areas east of the Rocky Mountains.

Examples

The eastern red cedar tree
Birds are attracted to the blue berries produced by the eastern red cedar.
The eastern red cedar is often planted as a windbreak in rural areas.
The aromatic wood of the eastern red cedar is used for making pencils and chests.
