dusting powder

noun
powder applied to skin after bathing
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌstɪŋ ˌpaʊdər / (AmE), / ˈdʌstɪŋ ˌpaʊdə / (BrE)
a fine, usually scented powder applied to the skin, typically after bathing, to absorb moisture and prevent irritation

Examples

A dusting powder bottle
