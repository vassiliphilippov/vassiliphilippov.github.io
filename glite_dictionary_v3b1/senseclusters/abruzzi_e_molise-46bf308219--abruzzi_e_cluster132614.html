Abruzzi e Molise

proper noun
historical region in central Italy
Pronunciation: / ɑːˌbrut.si eɪ moʊˈliːseɪ / (AmE), / aːˌbrʊt.tsi ɛ mɒˈliːzeɪ / (BrE)
a historical mountainous region of central Italy, bordering the Adriatic Sea, now divided into the regions of Abruzzo and Molise.
