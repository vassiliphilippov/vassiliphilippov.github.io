foxtrot

noun
a type of ballroom dance
8.5 5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfɑːks.trɑːt/ /; / /ˈfɔːks.trɔːt/ (AmE), /ˈfɒks.trɒt/ (BrE)
a ballroom dance in quadruple time, characterized by smooth, progressive movements and a combination of slow and quick steps.
