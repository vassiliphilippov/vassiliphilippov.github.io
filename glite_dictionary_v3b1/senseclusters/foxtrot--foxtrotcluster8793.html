foxtrot

noun
a type of ballroom dance
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːkstrɑːt / (AmE), / ˈfɒkstrɒt / (BrE)
a ballroom dance in quadruple time, characterized by smooth, progressive movements and a combination of slow and quick steps.

Examples

A smooth foxtrot
At the ballroom competition, their foxtrot was praised for its elegance and precision.
She learned the steps of the foxtrot before trying more complex dances like the tango.
They swept gracefully across the dance floor, performing the foxtrot to the sound of a jazz band.

Related Terms

  • synonym: waltz - a ballroom dance in triple time with a strong accent on the first beat
