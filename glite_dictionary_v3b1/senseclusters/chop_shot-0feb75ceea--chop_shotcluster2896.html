chop shot

noun
a tennis stroke made with backspin
Pronunciation: / tʃɑp ʃɑt / (AmE), / tʃɒp ʃɒt / (BrE)
a tennis stroke made with a downward motion, imparting backspin to the ball

Examples

A chop shot in tennis
He used a chop shot to keep his opponent on the defensive.
Her impressive chop shot sent the ball skidding low over the net.
It's difficult to return a well-executed chop shot in a fast-paced match.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slice - a stroke in tennis that imparts spin to the ball, often with a downward motion
