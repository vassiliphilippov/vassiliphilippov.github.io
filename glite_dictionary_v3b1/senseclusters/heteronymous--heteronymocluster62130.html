heteronymous

adjective
different sides of eyes
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛtəˈrɑnəməs / (AmE), / ˌhɛtəˈrɒnɪməs / (BrE)
in medicine, affecting different or opposite sides of the visual fields in both eyes

Examples

Heteronymous visual defect
A heteronymous visual field defect can indicate damage at the optic chiasm.
The patient was diagnosed with heteronymous hemianopia after experiencing vision loss in both eyes.
Doctors look for heteronymous patterns when assessing certain neurological conditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: homonymous - affecting the same side of the visual field in both eyes
