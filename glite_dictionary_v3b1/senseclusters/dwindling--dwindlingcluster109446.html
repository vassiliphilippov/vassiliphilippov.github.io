dwindling

adjective
gradually becoming less
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdwɪndlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdwɪndlɪŋ / (BrE)
becoming gradually smaller, fewer, or less; diminishing steadily over time

Examples

Dwindling numbers
Attendance at the club has been dwindling for the past few months, causing concern among its members.
The company is facing a dwindling supply of raw materials, threatening its production.
With their dwindling savings, they started looking for new sources of income.
