fly high

verb
achieve great success or prominence
5.5
Pronunciation: / flaɪ haɪ / (AmE), / flaɪ haɪ / (BrE)
to be very successful or to feel very happy and excited, especially because of achievement

Examples

To fly high in success

After winning the competition, she was flying high for weeks.
He always flies high when he hears good news about his family.
The team flew high after their impressive victory yesterday.
After years of hard work, she finally began to fly high in her chosen field.
When you feel confident and motivated, you can really fly high.
With their latest album topping the charts, the band continued to fly high.
