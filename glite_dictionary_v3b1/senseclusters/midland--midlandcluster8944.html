midland

noun
interior region of a country
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈmɪd.lənd / (BrE)
an area forming the interior part of a country, especially away from the coasts or borders

Examples

The midland region
Many farmers live in the midland where the land is rich and fertile.
The midland region is known for its rolling hills and vast plains.
Trade routes often passed through the midland to avoid the dangers of the coast.
