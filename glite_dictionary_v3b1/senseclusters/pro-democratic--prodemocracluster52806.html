pro-democratic

adjective
supporting democracy
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊˌdɛməˈkrætɪk / (AmE), / ˌprəʊˌdɛməˈkrætɪk / (BrE)
supporting or favoring democracy or democratic principles

Examples

Pro-democratic reforms
The new government introduced several pro-democratic reforms to increase citizen participation.
Many young people joined pro-democratic movements to demand fair elections.
International organizations praised the country's pro-democratic changes after years of authoritarian rule.

Related Terms

  • antonym: antidemocratic - opposed to democracy or democratic principles
  • synonym: democratic - relating to or supporting democracy
