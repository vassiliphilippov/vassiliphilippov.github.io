greystone

noun
gray stone building
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪstəʊn / (BrE)
a building or large house made of gray stone, especially common in some urban areas

Examples

A greystone building
The historic greystone on the avenue was converted into luxury apartments.
They bought a beautiful old greystone with tall windows and ornate details.
Many neighborhoods are known for their rows of classic greystones lining the streets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brownstone - a townhouse built of reddish-brown sandstone
