stuff you

x
rude expression of angry dismissal
6.4
Pronunciation: / stʌf ju / (AmE), / stʌf juː / (BrE)
A rude and dismissive way of telling someone to go away or expressing contempt, anger, or frustration.

Examples

Stuff you!
After hearing her unfair criticism, he turned and said, 'stuff you!' before leaving the room.
If you don't like my decision, then stuff you—I'm not changing my mind.
She muttered 'stuff you' under her breath after the argument with her colleague.

Related Terms

  • synonym: get lost - go away; leave me alone (informal)
  • synonym: sod off - a rude way of telling someone to go away
  • antonym: thank you - expression of gratitude (polite antonym in usage)
