foul

verb
hit a ball outside the fair area (sports)
6.7 3.4
Pronunciation: /faʊl/ (AmE), /faʊl/ (BrE)
(in baseball and similar sports) to hit (a ball) so that it lands outside the designated fair area.

Examples

To foul a ball
It's frustrating when you keep fouling balls instead of making solid contact.
She accidentally fouled the ball into the stands during her first at-bat.
The batter managed to foul off three pitches in a row.
