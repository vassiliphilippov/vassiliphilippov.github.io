sloshy

adjective
wet and muddy
4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈslɑː.ʃiː/ /; / /ˈslɑː.ʃɪ/ (AmE), /ˈslɒ.ʃiː/ /; / /ˈslɒ.ʃɪ/ (BrE)
wet, soft, or muddy with enough water to make a squelching or splashing sound when walked on

Examples

Sloshy field
The field became sloshy after hours of heavy rain, making it hard to walk across.
Her boots were soaked from the sloshy ground along the riverbank.
We avoided the sloshy path and took the drier route through the woods.

  • synonym: muddy - covered in or full of mud
  • synonym: soggy - very wet and soft
