doggishly

adverb
in a doglike way
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔːɡɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡɪʃli / (BrE)
in a manner characteristic of a dog, such as eagerly, loyally, or by begging.

Examples

Waits doggishly for food
He waited doggishly by the door, hoping for a treat or a kind word.
She looked up at her teacher doggishly, eager to please and ready to help.
The child followed his older brother doggishly, imitating every move with loyal enthusiasm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eagerly - in a way that shows keen interest or enthusiasm
  • synonym: obediently - in a manner showing willingness to follow orders
