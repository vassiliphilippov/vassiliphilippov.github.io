contractionary

adjective
causing tightening
8.2
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈtræk.ʃə.nər.iː/ (AmE), /kən.ˈtræk.ʃə.nə.riː/ (BrE)

Examples

Some herbs are believed to have contractionary properties that affect smooth muscle tissue.
