PMS

noun
premenstrual symptoms in women
3.8 1.46/m
Pronunciation: / piÀê …õm …õs / (AmE), / piÀê …õm …õs / (BrE)
a set of physical and emotional symptoms occurring in many women before the start of menstruation, such as mood swings, fatigue, and headaches

Examples

PMS symptoms
His sister warned him that her PMS made her more sensitive during that time of the month.
Many women experience PMS symptoms such as irritability and bloating before their period.
She took vitamins to help manage her PMS.

Synonyms

  • premenstrual syndrome
    3.6 0.17/m
    premenstrual syndrome is the full medical name used in formal contexts, while PMS is the common abbreviation used in speech and on labels.
  • PMT
    6.7 0.00/m
    PMT is an older abbreviation for premenstrual tension used in some contexts, while PMS is the more common modern term.
