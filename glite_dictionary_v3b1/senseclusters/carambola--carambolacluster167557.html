carambola

noun
East Indian tree with star-shaped fruit
7.2
Pronunciation: / kɛrəŋˈboʊlə / (AmE), / kærəmˈbəʊlə / (BrE)
An East Indian tree (Averrhoa carambola) bearing deeply ridged, yellow-brown fruit, also known as starfruit.

Examples

Carambola tree
Farmers in tropical regions often cultivate carambola for its unique appearance and tart flavor.
Slices of carambola add a decorative touch to fruit salads because of their star shape.
The carambola tree produces fruit that is crisp and juicy when ripe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: starfruit - another name for the carambola fruit
