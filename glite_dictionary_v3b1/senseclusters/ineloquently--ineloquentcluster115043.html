ineloquently

adverb
in an inarticulate or ungraceful way
5.1
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɛləkwəntli / (AmE), / ɪˈnɛləkwəntli / (BrE)
In a manner lacking eloquence; in an inarticulate or ungraceful way.

Examples

To speak ineloquently
Despite her knowledge, she answered ineloquently and left the panel unimpressed.
He apologized ineloquently, stumbling over his words and struggling to express himself clearly.
The candidate spoke ineloquently during the debate, failing to persuade the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awkwardly - in a clumsy or ungraceful manner
  • antonym: eloquently - in a fluent or persuasive manner
