otogenic

adjective
ear development
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊtəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌəʊtəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
relating to the formation or development of the ear, especially during embryonic growth

Examples

Otogenic disorder
The doctor explained that the patient's hearing loss was due to an otogenic disorder present from birth.
Researchers are investigating the otogenic factors that influence ear structure in developing embryos.
Certain genetic mutations can disrupt otogenic processes, leading to congenital ear defects.
