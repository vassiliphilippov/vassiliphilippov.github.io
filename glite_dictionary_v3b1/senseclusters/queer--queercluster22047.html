queer

verb
question or deconstruct traditional views
Pronunciation: / kwɪr / (AmE), / kwɪə / (BrE)
to question or deconstruct traditional or normative ideas, especially regarding gender, sexuality, or identity.

Examples

To queer ideas
The workshop helped participants queer their understanding of gender roles.
Their goal was to queer their understanding of history by examining marginalized perspectives.
This book encourages readers to queer their understanding of identity and belonging.

Related Terms

  • antonym: affirm - to state positively; to assert as valid
  • synonym: challenge - to question or dispute established ideas
