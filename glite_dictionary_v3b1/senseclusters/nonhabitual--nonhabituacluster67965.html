nonhabitual

adjective
not regular
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnhəˈbɪtʃuəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnhəˈbɪtʃuəl / (BrE)
not done regularly or as a habit; occurring only occasionally

Examples

A nonhabitual action
Her nonhabitual visits to the gym made it hard to see progress.
The judge considered the defendant's nonhabitual behavior when deciding the sentence.
Doctors asked about any nonhabitual symptoms that had appeared recently.

Related Terms

  • antonym: habitual - done regularly or repeatedly
  • synonym: occasional - happening from time to time
