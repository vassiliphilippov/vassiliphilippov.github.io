Sebastian Cabot

proper noun
Italian-born explorer (1476–1557) active in England and Spain
8.8
Pronunciation: / səˈbæstʃən ˈkæbət / (AmE), / sɪˈbæstʃən ˈkæbət / (BrE)
(1476–1557) Italian-born explorer, son of John Cabot, who led English and Spanish expeditions, searching for the Northwest Passage and exploring parts of South America.

Examples

Sebastian Cabot explorer
Sebastian Cabot played a significant role in early European exploration of the Americas.
In 1544, Sebastian Cabot published a new map of the world based on his voyages.
Many historians credit Sebastian Cabot with important contributions to both English and Spanish expeditions.
