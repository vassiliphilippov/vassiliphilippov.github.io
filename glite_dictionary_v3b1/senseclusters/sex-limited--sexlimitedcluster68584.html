sex-limited

adjective
expressed differently in two sexes (biology)
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛks ˌlɪmɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈseks ˌlɪmɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a characteristic or trait) expressed differently in the two sexes, typically due to genetic factors.

Examples

Sex-limited trait
Baldness in humans is a sex-limited trait, more common in males than in females.
Some diseases have a sex-limited expression and affect only one sex due to hormonal differences.
The study focused on the sex-limited inheritance of certain feather patterns in birds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sex-linked - (of a trait) associated with a gene located on a sex chromosome
