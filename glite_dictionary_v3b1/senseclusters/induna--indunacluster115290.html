induna

noun
Zulu leader or commander
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈduːnə / (AmE), / ɪnˈduːnə / (BrE)
a traditional leader or official in southern African communities, such as a commander, adviser, or headman

As an induna, he was respected for his wisdom and fairness.
