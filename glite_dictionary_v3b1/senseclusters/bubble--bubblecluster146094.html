bubble

verb
flow with bubbling noise
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌb.əl / (AmE), / ˈbʌb.əl / (BrE)
(of water or liquid) to flow in an irregular current with a bubbling or gurgling sound.

Examples

A stream bubbles along
A small brook bubbled through the meadow behind their house.
The stream began to bubble over the rocks, filling the air with its soft music.
We could hear water bubbling quietly as it wound its way through the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: babble - to make a continuous murmuring sound, like a brook
