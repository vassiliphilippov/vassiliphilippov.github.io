baillie

noun
Scottish land manager
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪli / (AmE), / ˈbeɪli / (BrE)
a person responsible for managing land or property, especially in Scotland

Examples

Estate baillie
The baillie oversaw the daily operations of the estate with great care.
Villagers often consulted the baillie about disputes over land boundaries.
As a respected baillie, he ensured the property was well maintained and profitable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bailiff - an official who manages property or keeps order, especially in a court
  • synonym: landlord - a person who owns and rents out land, buildings, or apartments
