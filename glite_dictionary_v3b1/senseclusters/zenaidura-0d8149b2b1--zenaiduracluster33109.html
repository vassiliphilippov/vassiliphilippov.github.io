Zenaidura

noun
former bird genus for mourning doves
Pronunciation: / zɛˌneɪɪˈdʊrə / (AmE), / zɛˌneɪɪˈdjʊərə / (BrE)
A former genus of birds in the pigeon family, including the mourning dove; now usually included in the genus Zenaida.

Examples

The Zenaidura bird
Modern taxonomy now places species formerly known as Zenaidura in the genus Zenaida.
Several field guides published in the early 20th century still refer to Zenaidura in their listings.
The genus Zenaidura was once used to classify the common mourning dove found across North America.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mourning dove - a species of dove native to North America, known for its plaintive coo
