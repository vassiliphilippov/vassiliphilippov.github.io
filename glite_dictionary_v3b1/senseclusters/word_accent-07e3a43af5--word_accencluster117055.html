word accent

noun
pattern of stresses in a word
Pronunciation: / wɜrd ˈæk.sɛnt / (AmE), / wɜːd ˈæk.sənt / (BrE)
the pattern or arrangement of stressed and unstressed syllables in a polysyllabic word

Examples

Word accent pattern
In English, primary word accent usually falls on the first syllable of many nouns.
Incorrect word accent can make speech harder to understand for listeners.
