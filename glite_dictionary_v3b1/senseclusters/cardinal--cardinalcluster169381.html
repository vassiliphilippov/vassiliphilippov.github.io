cardinal

noun
a vivid red color
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrdnəl / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdɪnəl / (BrE)
a vivid red color, similar to that of a cardinal bird's plumage or a cardinal's robe

Examples

Cardinal red

The autumn leaves turned a brilliant shade of cardinal as the season progressed.
The bride wore a dress of cardinal that captured everyone's attention.
