furtherance

noun
the advancement or promotion of something
Pronunciation: / ˈfɚðɚəns /; / ˈfɝːðɚəns / (AmE), / ˈfɜːðərəns / (BrE)
the act or process of promoting, supporting, or advancing something

Examples

In furtherance of goals
His diligent work was in the furtherance of scientific knowledge.
Many volunteers contributed to the furtherance of the organization's mission.
The charity is committed to the furtherance of education for underprivileged children.
He worked tirelessly in the furtherance of civil rights throughout his career.
The committee was established for the furtherance of scientific research in the country.
They contributed generously to the furtherance of educational opportunities for underprivileged children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advancement - the process of promoting the growth or development of something
  • antonym: hindrance - something that impedes or obstructs progress
  • synonym: promotion - activity that supports or encourages a cause or aim
