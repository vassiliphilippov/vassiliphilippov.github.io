asynchronous

adjective
not occurring at the same time
6.7
Pronunciation: / əˈsɪŋkrənəs / (AmE), / əˈsɪŋ.krə.nəs / (BrE)
not happening, existing, or moving at the same time or rate; not synchronous

Examples

Asynchronous events
The server can handle multiple asynchronous requests simultaneously.
Their clocks are asynchronous, which causes coordination problems.
Unlike traditional classes, asynchronous online courses let students complete work on their own schedule.

Related Terms

  • antonym: synchronous - occurring or existing at the same time or rate
