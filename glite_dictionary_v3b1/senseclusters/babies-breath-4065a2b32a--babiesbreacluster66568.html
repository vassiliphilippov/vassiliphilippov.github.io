babies'-breath

noun
ornamental plant with tiny white or pink flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪbiz brɛθ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪbiz brɛθ / (BrE)
A tall plant of the genus Gypsophila, especially Gypsophila paniculata, with small lance-shaped leaves and numerous tiny white or pink flowers, often used in floral arrangements.

Examples
