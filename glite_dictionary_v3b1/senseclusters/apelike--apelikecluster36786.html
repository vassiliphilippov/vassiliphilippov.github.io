apelike

adjective
imitative or resembling ape behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪpˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈeɪplaɪk / (BrE)
Imitating others in a servile or unoriginal manner; resembling the behavior of an ape.

Examples

Apelike copying
He adopted an apelike manner, echoing his boss’s words and movements precisely.
Her apelike copying of every trend made her seem unoriginal.
The student’s apelike mimicry of the teacher amused the whole class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imitative - tending to copy others; not original
