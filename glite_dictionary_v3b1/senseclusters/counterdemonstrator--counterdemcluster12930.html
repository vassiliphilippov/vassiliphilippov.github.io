counterdemonstrator

noun
person who opposes a protest with a counter-protest
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌkaʊntərˈdɛmənˌstreɪtər / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntəˈdɛmənstreɪtə / (BrE)
a person who takes part in a public protest to oppose another demonstration

Examples

A counterdemonstrator protests

Hundreds of counterdemonstrators gathered across the street from the main protest, chanting and holding signs.
Police kept a close watch as counterdemonstrators arrived to voice their opposition to the rally.
