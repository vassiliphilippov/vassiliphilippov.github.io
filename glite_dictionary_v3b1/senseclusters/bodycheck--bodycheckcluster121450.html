bodycheck

verb
to block with body
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑdiˌtʃɛk / (AmE), / ˈbɒdiˌtʃɛk / (BrE)
to block or stop an opponent in a sport, especially ice hockey, by using one's body

Examples

To bodycheck an opponent
The defender tried to bodycheck the forward to prevent a goal.
Players are taught how to bodycheck safely to avoid injuries during the game.
He was penalized for attempting to bodycheck an opponent from behind.

Related Terms

  • antonym: avoid - to keep away from or prevent contact with
  • synonym: tackle - to stop an opponent by physically challenging them
