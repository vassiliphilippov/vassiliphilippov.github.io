obeche

noun
light African wood for veneers and plywood
6.2
Pronunciation: / oʊˈbiːtʃi / (AmE), / əʊˈbiːtʃi / (BrE)
The pale, lightweight wood of an African tree, used especially for making veneers and plywood.

Examples

Obeche wood panel
Because obeche is easy to work with, it’s popular for model-making and furniture.

They chose obeche veneer to give the cabinets a smooth, pale finish.
