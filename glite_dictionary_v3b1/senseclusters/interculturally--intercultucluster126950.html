interculturally

adverb
between cultures
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈkʌltʃərəli / (AmE), / ˌɪntəkˈʌltʃərəli / (BrE)
in a manner that involves or relates to interaction between different cultures

Examples

Work interculturally
The team worked interculturally to develop a project that respected everyone's traditions.
Learning to think interculturally is important in today's globalized world.
She communicates interculturally with colleagues from many different countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cross-culturally - involving or comparing two or more different cultures
