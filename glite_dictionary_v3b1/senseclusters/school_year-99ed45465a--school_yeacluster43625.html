school year

noun
period when school is in session
Pronunciation: / skuːl jɪr / (AmE), / skuːl jɪə / (BrE)
the period of the year during which schools are open and regular classes are held

Examples

The school year
During the school year, the library has extended evening hours.
Many students look forward to summer break at the end of the school year.
The school year typically begins in September and ends in June.
