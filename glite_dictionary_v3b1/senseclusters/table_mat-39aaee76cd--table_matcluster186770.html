table mat

noun
pad to protect a table under a dish
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪbəl mæt / (AmE), / ˈteɪbl̩ mæt / (BrE)
A small, flat pad placed under dishes or plates to protect a table from heat, moisture, or scratches.

Examples

A table mat protector
He wiped the crumbs off the table mat after breakfast before putting it away.
She placed a table mat under the hot casserole to protect the wooden surface.
The restaurant used woven table mats at every setting for a rustic look.

Related Terms

  • synonym: placemat - a mat set under a plate to protect the table and decorate the setting
