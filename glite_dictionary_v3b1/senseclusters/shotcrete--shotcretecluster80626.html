shotcrete

noun
sprayed concrete
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑtˌkriːt / (AmE), / ˈʃɒtˌkriːt / (BrE)
concrete or mortar that is sprayed onto a surface at high speed, often used to reinforce structures

Examples

A layer of shotcrete
The engineers used shotcrete to quickly stabilize the hillside after the landslide.
Applying shotcrete is an efficient way to reinforce tunnel walls during construction.
The swimming pool was built using shotcrete for a smooth and durable finish.
