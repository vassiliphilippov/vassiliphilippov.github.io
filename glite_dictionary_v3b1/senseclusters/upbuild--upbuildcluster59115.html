upbuild

verb
to build up
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌbɪld / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌbɪld / (BrE)
to construct or build up a physical structure, especially by adding to or improving it

Examples

To upbuild a playground
The community worked together to upbuild a new playground for the children.
They hope to upbuild the old warehouse into a vibrant art center.
Volunteers helped upbuild homes for families in need after the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: construct - to build or put together
  • antonym: demolish - to destroy or tear down a structure
  • synonym: erect - to build or put up a structure
