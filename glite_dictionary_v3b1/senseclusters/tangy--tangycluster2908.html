tangy

adjective
having a sharp, pleasantly sour taste
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtæŋi / (AmE), / ˈtaŋi / (BrE)
having a strong, sharp taste or flavor that is pleasantly sour or acidic

Examples

A tangy barbecue sauce
A tangy barbecue sauce can really liven up grilled chicken.
She loves the tangy taste of Greek yogurt with fresh fruit.
The lemonade had a tangy flavor that was both refreshing and delicious.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sour - having an acidic taste like lemon or vinegar
  • antonym: sweet - having a taste like sugar or honey
