Synonyms

  • endeavor
    5.2 2/m
    endeavor is more formal and means trying very hard in careful ways, while strive is a general strong effort to reach goals.
  • struggle
    2.8 40/m
    struggle is a forceful, often difficult fight to achieve something, while strive means making a great effort to succeed.
  • strain
    4.2 4/m
    strain means exerting force to the limit, often causing tiredness, while strive means making a strong effort toward a goal.
  • push
    1.5 30/m
    push is making a determined effort, often to move something or persuade others, while strive is about personal hard work.
  • aim for
    2.8 4/m
    aim for means set a target and try to reach it, while strive focuses on putting in great effort to achieve.
