bridgeway

noun
connecting passageway
6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbrɪdʒ.weɪ/ (AmE), /ˈbrɪdʒ.weɪ/ (BrE)

Examples

A glass bridgeway offers a scenic view as you walk between the towers.
