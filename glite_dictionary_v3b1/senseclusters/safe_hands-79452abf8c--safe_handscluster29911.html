safe hands

noun
trustworthy or reliable care; capable guardianship
Pronunciation: / seɪf hændz / (AmE), / seɪf hændz / (BrE)
used to refer to a person or group who can be trusted to look after someone or something carefully and responsibly

Examples

You can relax knowing the company is in safe hands with the new manager.
