groomer

noun
person who prepares others
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɡruːmər / (AmE), / ˈɡruːmə / (BrE)
a person who prepares, trains, or develops someone for a particular role, position, or responsibility

Examples

A groomer for leaders
As a dedicated groomer, she helped her assistant develop the skills needed for promotion.
The company assigned a groomer to guide new employees through their first year.
He became a respected groomer for future leaders in the organization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coach - someone who trains or instructs others, especially in skills or performance
  • synonym: mentor - an experienced person who advises and trains someone less experienced
