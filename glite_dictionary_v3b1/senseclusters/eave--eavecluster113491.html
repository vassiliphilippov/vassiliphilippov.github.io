eave

verb
to overhang like a roof
8.6 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / iÀêv / (AmE), / iÀêv / (BrE)
to overhang or project outward like the edge of a roof

Examples

Trees eave the path
The old cottage eaves over the garden, providing shade in the summer.
Branches eaved above the path, sheltering us from the rain.
The balcony eaves slightly, creating a small covered area below.

Synonyms

  • overhang
    5.1 1.89/m
    overhang is a projecting part of a building that sticks out, while eave means to project outward like a roof edge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overhang - to extend or hang over the edge of something
  • antonym: recede - to move back or away from a position
