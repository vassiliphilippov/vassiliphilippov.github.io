sunbird

noun
nectar-feeding bird
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌbɝd / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌbɜːd / (BrE)
a small, brightly colored bird from Africa and Asia that feeds on nectar from flowers, similar to a hummingbird

Examples
