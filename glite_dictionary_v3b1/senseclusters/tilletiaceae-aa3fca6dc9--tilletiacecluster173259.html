Tilletiaceae

noun
family of smut fungi
Pronunciation: / tɪˌliːtiˈeɪsiː /; / tɪˌliːtiˈeɪsiːˌiː / (AmE), / tɪˌliːtɪˈeɪsɪiː /; / tɪˌliːtɪˈeɪsɪˌiː / (BrE)
