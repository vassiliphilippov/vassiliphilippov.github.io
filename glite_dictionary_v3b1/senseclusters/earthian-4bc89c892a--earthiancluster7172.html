Earthian

noun
person from Earth
Pronunciation: / ˈɜːrθiən / (AmE), / ˈɜːθiən / (BrE)
a person or being from Earth, especially as contrasted with beings from other planets in science fiction

Examples

An Earthian explorer
The ambassador was the first Earthian to set foot on the Martian surface.
In the story, the Earthian explorers encountered a civilization unlike any they had known.
Aliens often view Earthians as mysterious and unpredictable in science fiction novels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Earthling - a human or inhabitant of Earth
  • antonym: alien - a being from another planet
