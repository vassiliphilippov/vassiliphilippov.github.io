coachman

noun
driver of a horse-drawn carriage
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊtʃ.mən / (AmE), / ˈkəʊtʃ.mən / (BrE)
a man whose job is to drive a horse-drawn coach or carriage, especially in the past

Examples

The coachman drives horses
During the storm, the coachman struggled to keep the horses calm on the slippery road.
In Victorian novels, a coachman is often responsible for both the carriage and the stables.
The coachman tipped his hat politely before opening the door for the passengers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: driver - person who drives a vehicle
  • antonym: passenger - person riding in a vehicle but not controlling it
