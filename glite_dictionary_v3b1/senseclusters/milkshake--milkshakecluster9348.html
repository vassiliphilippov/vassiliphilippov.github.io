milkshake

noun
a cold drink of milk and flavoring, often with ice cream
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlkˌʃeɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkʃeɪk / (BrE)
a sweet, cold drink made by blending milk with flavoring, and usually with ice cream or fruit

Examples

A milkshake with banana
After school, we made a banana milkshake using the blender.
He ordered a chocolate milkshake to go with his burger and fries.
The restaurant offers a strawberry milkshake topped with fresh whipped cream.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frappé - a blended iced drink, often with coffee or flavorings
  • synonym: smoothie - a thick, cold drink of blended fruit and sometimes yogurt or juice
