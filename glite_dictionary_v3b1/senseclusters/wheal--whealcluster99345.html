wheal

noun
raised mark on the skin, often from allergy
9.0 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / wiÀêl / (AmE), / wiÀêl / (BrE)
A small, raised, red or pale area on the skin, often itchy and caused by injury or an allergic reaction.

Examples

A wheal on skin
Doctors examined the wheal and confirmed it was a typical response to the injection.
She developed a large wheal on her arm after being stung by a bee.
The allergic reaction caused multiple itchy wheals to appear on his skin.
