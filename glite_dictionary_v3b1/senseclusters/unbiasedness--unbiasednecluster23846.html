unbiasedness

noun
freedom from bias
5.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbaɪəstnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈbaɪəstnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being fair and impartial; not influenced by personal opinions or prejudice (also, in statistics, the property of being correct on average without systematic error)
