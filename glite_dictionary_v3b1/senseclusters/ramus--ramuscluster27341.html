ramus

noun
vertical part of the lower jaw
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪ.məs / (AmE), / ˈreɪ.məs / (BrE)
the upright or vertical part at the back of the mandible (jawbone) in humans and other animals

Examples

The ramus of jaw
On the X-ray, the ramus of the jaw appeared clearly defined.
Swelling was observed near the ramus after the injury to his lower jaw.
The dentist explained that the fractured ramus would require surgical intervention.
