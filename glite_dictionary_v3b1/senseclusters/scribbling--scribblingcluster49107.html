scribbling

noun
messy handwriting
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈskrɪblɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskrɪblɪŋ / (BrE)
messy or quick, careless writing or drawing that is hard to read or has little meaning

Examples

Messy scribbling on paper
The teacher could not read the student's scribbling on the homework assignment.
Doctors are often joked about for their scribbling on medical charts.
Her notes were just a page of scribbling after the long meeting.
The notebook was full of scribbling from her daydreams during class.
He covered the whiteboard with random scribbling while explaining his ideas.
Children often leave scribbling on the walls when they find a marker.

Related Terms

  • antonym: artwork - a painting, drawing, or other artistic creation
  • synonym: doodle - a simple, aimless drawing made while thinking or bored
  • antonym: penmanship - the quality or style of someone's handwriting
  • synonym: scrawl - irregular or untidy handwriting
