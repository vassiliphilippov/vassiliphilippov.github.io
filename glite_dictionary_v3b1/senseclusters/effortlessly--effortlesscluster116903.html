effortlessly

adverb
with ease; without effort
Pronunciation: / ˈɛfərtləsli / (AmE), / ˈɛfətləsli / (BrE)
In a way that requires no physical or mental exertion; with ease.

Examples

To play effortlessly
He plays the piano effortlessly, as if he were born to do it.
She finished the puzzle effortlessly, impressing everyone at the table.
The athlete ran effortlessly ahead of his competitors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: easily - without difficulty or effort
  • antonym: laboriously - with considerable effort or difficulty
