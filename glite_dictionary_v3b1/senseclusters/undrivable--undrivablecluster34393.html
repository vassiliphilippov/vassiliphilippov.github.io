undrivable

adjective
not able to be driven
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdraɪvəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈdraɪvəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be driven, usually because of damage, poor condition, or other issues

Examples

Undrivable car
After the accident, the vehicle was completely undrivable and had to be towed away.
Heavy flooding left many roads undrivable for several days.
The old truck became undrivable after years of neglect and rust.

Related Terms

  • antonym: driveable - able to be driven
  • synonym: unusable - not able to be used
