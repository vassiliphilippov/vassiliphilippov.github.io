inharmonious

adjective
not harmonious; discordant
Pronunciation: / ɪn.hɑrˈmoʊ.ni.əs /; / ˌɪn.hɑːrˈmoʊ.ni.əs / (AmE), / ɪn.hɑːˈməʊ.ni.əs /; / ˌɪn.hɑːˈməʊ.ni.əs / (BrE)
lacking harmony or agreement; not forming a pleasing or consistent whole
