Joneses

noun
people named Jones
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒoʊnzɪz / (AmE), / ˈdʒəʊnzɪz / (BrE)
the members of a family or household whose surname is Jones, used as a plural form of Jones

Examples

The Joneses next door
The Joneses next door invited us for tea after they moved in last month.
Every summer, the Joneses host a big barbecue for the whole neighborhood.
We often see the Joneses walking their dog in the park on weekends.
