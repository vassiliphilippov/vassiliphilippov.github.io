captivatingly

adverb
in a fascinating or bewitching way
Pronunciation: / ˈkæp.tɪ.veɪ.tɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈkæp.tɪ.veɪ.tɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a way that attracts and holds attention; in a bewitching or fascinating manner.

Examples

To speak captivatingly
He spoke captivatingly about his travels, holding everyone's attention during the entire dinner.
The actress smiled captivatingly at the audience, earning a round of applause.
The novel was written so captivatingly that I couldn't put it down.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dully - in a manner that fails to attract or hold attention
  • synonym: fascinatingly - in a way that intensely attracts attention or interest
