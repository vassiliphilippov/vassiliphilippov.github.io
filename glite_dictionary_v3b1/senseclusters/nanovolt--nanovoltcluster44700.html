nanovolt

noun
one billionth of a volt
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnænoʊˌvoʊlt / (AmE), / ˈnanəʊvɒlt / (BrE)
A unit of electric potential equal to one billionth of a volt (10⁻⁹ volts).

Examples

A nanovolt unit
Researchers reported fluctuations in the magnetic field of only a few nanovolts.
Some scientific instruments are sensitive enough to detect changes as small as one nanovolt.
The voltage drop across the component was measured in nanovolts.
