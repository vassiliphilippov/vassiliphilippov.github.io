neuroplasty

noun
surgical repair of nerves
Pronunciation: / ˈnʊroʊˌplæsti / (AmE), / ˈnjʊərəʊˌplæsti / (BrE)
A surgical procedure in which damaged or injured nerves are repaired, reconstructed, or otherwise modified.

Examples

Peripheral neuroplasty surgery
Advances in microsurgery have improved the outcomes of peripheral neuroplasty procedures.
After her injury, the doctor recommended neuroplasty as the best option to repair the damaged nerve.
The patient underwent neuroplasty to restore function after suffering nerve damage in his arm.
