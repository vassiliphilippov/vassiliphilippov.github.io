steam-heat

verb
heat using steam
Pronunciation: / stiːm hiːt / (AmE), / stiːm hiːt / (BrE)
to heat something using steam, typically by circulating steam through pipes or radiators

Examples

To steam-heat rooms
Old apartment buildings often steam-heat their rooms during winter.
They steam-heated their lunch using the pipes in the basement.
We decided to steam-heat the warehouse for better climate control.
