prickly-leafed

adjective
having leaves with sharp points or spines
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪkli ˌliːft / (AmE), / ˈprɪkli ˌliːft / (BrE)
Having leaves that are covered with or characteristic of prickles (sharp points or spines).

Examples

Prickly-leafed plants
Many desert plants are prickly-leafed to deter herbivores from feeding on them.
She carefully avoided the prickly-leafed shrubs along the hiking trail to prevent scratches.
The prickly-leafed holly bush made a natural barrier around the garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prickly - covered with or having many prickles
  • synonym: spiny - covered with spines or thorns
