decubitus

noun
pressure sore
Pronunciation: / dɪˈkjuːbɪtəs / (AmE), / dɪˈkjuːbɪtəs / (BrE)
a sore or ulcer that forms on the skin due to prolonged pressure, especially in bedridden patients

Examples

Decubitus sore
The nurse checked the patient's back for signs of a decubitus.
Proper repositioning can help prevent the development of a decubitus in immobile patients.
Treatment of a decubitus often requires careful wound care and regular monitoring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bedsore - an ulcer caused by prolonged pressure on the skin
  • synonym: pressure ulcer - a sore caused by continuous pressure on the skin
