decubitus

noun
pressure sore
9.9
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈkjuː.bɪ.təs/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈkjuː.bɪ.təs/ (BrE)

Examples

Treatment of a decubitus often requires careful wound care and regular monitoring.
