sliding board

noun
a playground slide for children
Pronunciation: / ˈslaɪdɪŋ bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈslaɪdɪŋ bɔːd / (BrE)
a children's playground apparatus consisting of a smooth, sloping surface for sliding down

Examples

Children's sliding board
My daughter loves to play on the sliding board at the park after school.
Rain had made the sliding board too slippery to use safely.
The children ran to the sliding board as soon as they reached the playground.
