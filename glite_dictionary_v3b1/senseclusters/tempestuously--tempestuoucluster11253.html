tempestuously

adverb
in a stormy way
8.9
Pronunciation: / tɛmˈpɛstʃuəsli / (AmE), / tɛmˈpɛstjʊəsli / (BrE)
in a stormy, violent, or turbulent manner, like a tempest or severe storm

Examples

Waves crash tempestuously
The sea churned tempestuously as the hurricane approached the coast.
She argued tempestuously, her emotions as wild as the storm outside.
Clouds raced tempestuously across the sky, blocking out the sun.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmly - in a peaceful or quiet manner
  • synonym: violently - in a forceful or intense manner
