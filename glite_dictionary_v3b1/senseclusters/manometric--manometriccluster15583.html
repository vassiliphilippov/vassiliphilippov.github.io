manometric

adjective
measuring pressure
Pronunciation: / ˌmænəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌmænəˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the measurement of pressure, especially using a manometer

Examples

Manometric pressure measurement
The scientist recorded the manometric data to monitor changes in gas pressure during the experiment.
A manometric method was used to determine the pressure inside the sealed container.
Technicians rely on manometric instruments to ensure accurate pressure measurements in the laboratory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barometric - relating to the measurement of atmospheric pressure
