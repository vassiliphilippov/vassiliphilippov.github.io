Filipina

noun
woman from the Philippines
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪlɪˈpiːnə / (AmE), / ˌfɪlɪˈpiːnə / (BrE)
a woman or girl who is a native or citizen of the Philippines

Examples

A Filipina woman
Maria is a talented Filipina who works as an engineer in Manila.
The documentary highlights the achievements of a young Filipina scientist.
Many Filipinas have made significant contributions to the arts and culture of their country.
The Filipina chef prepared a delicious traditional meal for her guests.
As a proud Filipina, she celebrates her culture wherever she goes.
Many Filipinas work abroad to support their families back home.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Filipino - a man or boy who is a native or citizen of the Philippines
  • synonym: Philippine - relating to the Philippines or its people
