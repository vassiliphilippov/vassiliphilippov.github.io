dorsiflexion

noun
movement bending upward (especially foot/hand)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdɔrsəˈflɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɔːsɪˈflɛkʃən / (BrE)
the movement that bends a part of the body, especially the foot or hand, upward or backward toward the dorsum (upper surface)

Examples

Proper dorsiflexion of ankle
Proper dorsiflexion of the ankle is crucial for running efficiently.
The physical therapist measured the patient's dorsiflexion to assess ankle mobility.
You may feel a stretch in your calf when performing dorsiflexion exercises.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plantarflexion - the movement opposite to dorsiflexion, bending the foot downward at the ankle
