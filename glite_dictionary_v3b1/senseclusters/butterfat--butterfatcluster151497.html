butterfat

noun
fatty substance in milk
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌt̬ərˌfæt / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəˌfat / (BrE)
The natural fat present in milk, and the main constituent from which butter is made.

Examples

Natural butterfat in milk
