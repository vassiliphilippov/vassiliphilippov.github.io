Heimdallr

proper noun
Norse god of dawn and guardian of Asgard
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪmˌdɑl / (AmE), / ˈhaɪm.dal / (BrE)
(in Norse mythology) the god of dawn and light, who serves as the vigilant guardian of Asgard.

Examples

Heimdallr the guardian
In Norse legends, Heimdallr is known for his keen senses and unwavering vigilance.
Many stories describe Heimdallr as the herald of Ragnarok, destined to sound the Gjallarhorn.
The rainbow bridge, Bifröst, is guarded by Heimdallr to prevent the giants from invading Asgard.
