demi-

x
prefix: half or partly
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛmi / (AmE), / ˈdɛmi / (BrE)
a prefix meaning half or partly, used to form words indicating partial or incomplete status

Examples

Demi- prefix meaning half
A demigod is a mythical being who is half-god and half-human.
The chef served a demitasse of strong coffee after the meal.
She wore a demiskirt that reached just below her knees.
