drain

noun
pipe for carrying away liquid
Pronunciation: / dreɪn / (AmE), / dreɪn / (BrE)
A pipe or channel that carries away waste liquid or water from a place.

Examples

Street drain
After the heavy rain, water quickly disappeared down the street drain.
He lifted the metal cover to check if the drain underneath was clear.
The kitchen sink was blocked because the drain was clogged with food scraps.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sewer - underground pipe that carries wastewater
