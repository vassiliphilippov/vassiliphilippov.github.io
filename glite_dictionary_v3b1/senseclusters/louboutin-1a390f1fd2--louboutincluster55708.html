Louboutin

proper noun
luxury shoe brand
Pronunciation: / luːbuːˈtæ̃ / (AmE), / luːbuːˈtæ̃ / (BrE)
a luxury brand of shoes, especially high heels, known for their red soles and designed by Christian Louboutin

Examples

Louboutin heels
She wore her favorite Louboutin heels to the gala and received many compliments.
The signature red soles made it clear her shoes were Louboutin.
He surprised her with a pair of Louboutin pumps for her birthday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Jimmy Choo - a luxury brand of shoes and accessories
  • synonym: Manolo Blahnik - a high-end shoe brand known for elegant designs
