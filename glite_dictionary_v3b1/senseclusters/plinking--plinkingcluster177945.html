plinking

noun
sharp metallic sounds
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈplɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈplɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
a series of short, sharp, metallic or percussive sounds, often repeated or rhythmic

Examples

Plinking of raindrops
The plinking of raindrops on the metal roof was oddly soothing.
She listened to the plinking from the old piano in the next room.
A steady plinking from the loose gutter kept him awake all night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clinking - a sharp, ringing sound made by metal objects striking each other
  • synonym: tinkling - a light, ringing sound like small bells
