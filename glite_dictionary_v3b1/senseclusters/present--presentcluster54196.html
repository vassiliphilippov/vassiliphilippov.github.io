present

noun
the time happening now
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛzənt / (AmE), / ˈprɛz(ə)nt / (BrE)
the period of time that is happening now, as distinct from the past or the future

Examples

The present moment
For the present, the museum will remain closed to visitors.
In his speech, he urged everyone to live in the present moment.
She tries to focus on the present instead of worrying about the future.

  • antonym: past - the time before the present
