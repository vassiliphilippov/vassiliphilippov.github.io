incumbent

adjective
currently holding office or position
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkʌmbənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈkʌmbənt / (BrE)
(of a person) currently holding a particular office or position, especially in politics or business.

Examples

Incumbent president
It is rare for an incumbent president to lose the nomination of their own party.
Many voters were dissatisfied with the incumbent senator's performance.
The incumbent mayor announced she would seek re-election this year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: challenger - a person who competes against the incumbent for a position
