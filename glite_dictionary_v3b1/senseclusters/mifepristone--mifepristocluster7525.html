mifepristone

noun
abortion-inducing medication
8.5 7.7
Pronunciation: /mɪ.fə.ˈprɪs.toʊ/ (AmE), /mɪ.fə.ˈprɪs.təʊ/ (BrE)
A synthetic steroid drug used to induce abortion, especially in the early stages of pregnancy, by blocking the action of progesterone.
