Hemingway

noun
concise writing style
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈhem.ɪŋ.weɪ/ (AmE), /ˈhem.ɪŋ.weɪ/ (BrE)
a style of writing characterized by short sentences, simple vocabulary, and understated expression, inspired by Ernest Hemingway

Examples

Hemingway style
The author adopted a Hemingway in her new novel, using short, direct sentences.
Critics praised the Hemingway of his essays for their clarity and simplicity.
Writers often study Hemingway to learn how to express ideas with fewer words.

Related Terms

  • synonym: minimalism - a style using very few words or elements
  • antonym: ornate - using elaborate or complex language
