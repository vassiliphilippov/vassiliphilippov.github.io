networker

noun
person who builds connections
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛtˌwɜrkər / (AmE), / ˈnɛtˌwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person who actively builds and maintains relationships for business, professional, or social purposes

Examples

A skilled networker at events
At the conference, the best networker made dozens of new contacts in just one day.
She is a skilled networker who always knows the right people to call.
Many networkers use social media to expand their professional circles.
As a skilled networker, he attends many conferences to meet new business partners.
The event was full of networkers exchanging business cards and ideas.
Being a good networker can help you find new job opportunities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: connector - a person who brings people together
  • antonym: introvert - a person who is shy or reserved in social situations
