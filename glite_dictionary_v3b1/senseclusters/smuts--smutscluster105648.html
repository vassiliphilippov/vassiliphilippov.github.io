smuts

noun
fungal crop diseases
Pronunciation: / smʌts / (AmE), / smʌts / (BrE)
fungal diseases of cereal crops and grasses that produce dark, powdery spore masses on affected plants

Examples

Wheat smuts disease
The wheat crop was severely damaged by smuts that spread quickly after the rainy season.
Farmers use resistant varieties to protect their fields from smuts and other fungal diseases.
Early detection of smuts can help prevent major losses in cereal production.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blight - a plant disease causing withering and death of tissues
  • synonym: rusts - fungal diseases causing reddish or brownish spots on plants
