gas bomb

noun
bomb releasing chemical gas on explosion
Pronunciation: / ɡæs bɑm / (AmE), / ɡas bɒm / (BrE)
a bomb filled with chemical agents that are dispersed as gas when the bomb explodes

Examples

A gas bomb explosion
After the explosion, the area was filled with toxic fumes from a single gas bomb.
During the raid, several gas bombs were dropped on the enemy positions.
The military banned the use of gas bombs in combat due to their indiscriminate effects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chemical weapon - a weapon that uses chemicals to cause harm or death
