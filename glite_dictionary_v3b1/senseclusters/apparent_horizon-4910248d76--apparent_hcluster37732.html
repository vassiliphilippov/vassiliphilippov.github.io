Synonyms

  • visible horizon
    4.1 0.00/m
    visible horizon is the clearly seen line where earth and sky meet, while apparent horizon names the same line as seen from a single point.
  • sensible horizon
    7.6 0.00/m
    sensible horizon is the line where sky and sea or land appear to meet for an observer, while apparent horizon is the same line seen from one point.
  • horizon
    2.0 22/m
    horizon is the general line or imaginary circle where earth and sky seem to touch, while apparent horizon shows that line from a single viewpoint.
