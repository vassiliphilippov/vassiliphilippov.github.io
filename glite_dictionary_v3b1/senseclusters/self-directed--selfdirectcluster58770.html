self-directed

adjective
acting on one's own initiative
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf.dɪˈrɛk.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf.dɪˈrɛk.tɪd / (BrE)
(of a person) making decisions and acting according to one's own initiative, not influenced or controlled by others.

Examples

Self-directed learner
Many employers seek self-directed employees capable of managing their own tasks.
She is a self-directed learner who excels without constant supervision.
The students in the program are encouraged to become more self-directed in their studies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: independent - free from outside control; self-reliant
