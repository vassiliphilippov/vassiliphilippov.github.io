DMP

noun
disaster management plan
7.5
Pronunciation: /diː.em.piː/ /; / /diː.əm.piː/ (AmE), /diː.em.piː/ /; / /diː.əm.piː/ (BrE)

Examples

School DMP plan

The government reviewed the DMP after the hurricane to identify areas for improvement.
