struggle

verb
make a strenuous effort
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstrʌɡəl / (AmE), / ˈstrʌɡl / (BrE)
to make a strenuous or determined effort to achieve something or overcome difficulty

Examples

To struggle with problems
After losing his job, he continued to struggle to pay the bills every month.
She was struggling to stay awake during the long lecture.
The injured bird struggled to fly but couldn't lift off the ground.
Despite the strong currents, he continued to struggle towards the shore.
She had to struggle to stay awake during the long meeting.
The company is struggling to survive in an increasingly competitive market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fight - to try hard to defeat or achieve something
  • antonym: yield - to give way to pressure or opposition
