piece of gum

noun
a single portion of chewing gum
Pronunciation: / pis əv ɡʌm / (AmE), / piːs əv ɡʌm / (BrE)
a small portion or unit of chewing gum, usually intended for a single use

Examples

A piece of gum
He always carries a piece of gum in his pocket.
She offered me a piece of gum after lunch.
There was a piece of gum stuck to my shoe.
