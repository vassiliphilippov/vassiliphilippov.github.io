flimmer

noun
faint flicker of light
9.9 0.01/m
a brief or faint flicker or shimmer of light, often appearing unsteady or wavering

Examples

A flimmer of light
A flimmer of silver ran across the lake as the sun slipped behind clouds.
She noticed a flimmer in the distance, barely visible through the evening mist.
The candle's flimmer cast gentle shadows on the old stone walls.
