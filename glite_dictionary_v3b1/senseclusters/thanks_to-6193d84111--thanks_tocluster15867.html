thanks to

preposition
because of; due to
1.0
Pronunciation: / θæŋks tu / (AmE), / θæŋks tə / (BrE)
because of; as a result of the help, action, or presence of someone or something.

Examples

Thanks to the help
Thanks to the quick response of the firefighters, the building was saved from serious damage.
The hospital staff worked tirelessly, and many patients recovered thanks to their dedication.
We finished the project ahead of schedule thanks to everyone's hard work.
