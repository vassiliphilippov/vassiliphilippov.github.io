ashore

adverb
towards or onto the shore
Pronunciation: / əˈʃɔr / (AmE), / əˈʃɔː / (BrE)
towards or onto the shore from a body of water

Examples

Washed ashore by waves
After the storm, debris was washed ashore by the waves.
The small boat finally made its way ashore after a long day at sea.
We spotted several seals coming ashore to rest on the rocks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: afloat - floating on water or at sea
