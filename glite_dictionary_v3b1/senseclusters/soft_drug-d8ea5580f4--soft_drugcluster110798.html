soft drug

noun
relatively mild, non-addictive drug
Pronunciation: / sɔft drʌɡ / (AmE), / sɒft drʌɡ / (BrE)
a drug that is regarded as relatively mild in its effects and not likely to cause addiction

Examples

Use of soft drugs

The doctor explained the difference between a soft drug like cannabis and stronger substances.
