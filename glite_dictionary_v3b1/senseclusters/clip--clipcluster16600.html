clip

noun
a quick, sharp blow
Pronunciation: / klɪp / (AmE), / klɪp / (BrE)
a quick, sharp, and slanting blow, especially with the hand

Examples

A sharp clip
He was caught off guard by the sudden clip during the scuffle.
She landed a swift clip on his ear when he made a rude remark.
The coach gave him a gentle clip on the head for not paying attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: punch - a forceful blow with the fist
  • synonym: slap - a sharp blow with the open hand
