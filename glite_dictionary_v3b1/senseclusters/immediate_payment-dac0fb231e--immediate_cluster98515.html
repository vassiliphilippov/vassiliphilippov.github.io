immediate payment

noun
payment made instantly at time of purchase
2.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmiːdiət ˈpeɪmənt / (AmE), / ɪˈmiːdiət ˈpeɪmənt / (BrE)
payment for goods or services made instantly, typically at the time of purchase, in cash or by check.

Examples

Immediate payment required
Many online services ask for immediate payment to process your order.
She offered immediate payment by check to secure the reservation.
The store requires immediate payment for all purchases made on sale days.
