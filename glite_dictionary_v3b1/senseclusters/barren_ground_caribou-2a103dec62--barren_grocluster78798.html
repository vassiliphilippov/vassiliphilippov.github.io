barren ground caribou

noun
a caribou subspecies from northern Canada
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛrən ɡraʊnd kəˈriːbuː / (AmE), / ˈbærən ɡraʊnd kəˈriːbuː / (BrE)
a subspecies of caribou (Rangifer tarandus groenlandicus) native to the tundra regions of northern Canada, notable for its long migrations
