hedge

verb
enclose with a hedge
5.9
Pronunciation: / hɛdʒ / (AmE), / hɛdʒ / (BrE)
to enclose or surround (an area or property) with a hedge or hedges

Examples

To hedge a yard
Before building the fence, they first hedged the property with shrubs.
The farmer hedged his fields to protect the crops from animals.
They decided to hedge the garden to create more privacy from the street.
