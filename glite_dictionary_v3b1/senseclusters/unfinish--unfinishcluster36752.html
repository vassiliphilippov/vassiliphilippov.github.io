unfinish

verb
to strip surface finish
9.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪnɪʃ / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪnɪʃ / (BrE)
to remove a surface finish such as varnish, paint, or polish from an object

Examples

To unfinish wood
The craftsman decided to unfinish the old chair before repainting it.
You should unfinish the cabinet to remove the damaged varnish before applying a new coat.
After years of use, they chose to unfinish the table to restore its original wood surface.

Related Terms

  • antonym: refinish - to apply a new finish to a surface
  • synonym: strip - to remove a covering or layer from something
