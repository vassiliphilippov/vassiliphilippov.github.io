Senecio aureus

noun
herbaceous plant with yellow flowers (golden ragwort)
Pronunciation: / sɪˌniʃioʊ ɔˈreɪəs / (AmE), / sɛˌnɛsɪəʊ ɔːˈriːəs / (BrE)
A species of herbaceous plant of the eastern United States to Texas, with golden-yellow flowers, sometimes invasive, also known as golden ragwort.
