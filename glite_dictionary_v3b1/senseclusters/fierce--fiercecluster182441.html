Synonyms

  • ferocious
    5.5 3.76/m
    ferocious describes very violent, savage behavior in animals or people, while fierce covers strong force, passion, or intense competition.
  • raging
    3.9 0.08/m
    raging describes uncontrolled, violent force like a storm or anger, while fierce shows strong intensity or aggression in many contexts.
  • intense
    3.7 41/m
    intense means very strong or deep feeling, while fierce often adds aggression, force, or sharp danger to that strength.
  • fiery
    3.5 4.49/m
    fiery shows hot passion or a quick temper in people, while fierce can mean strong force, violence, or intense competition.
