comarca

noun
judicial district
Pronunciation: / koʊˈmɑrkə / (AmE), / kəʊˈmɑːkə / (BrE)
a judicial district or territorial division served by a court of law, especially in Portuguese and some Latin American legal systems

Examples

Comarca court
The trial was held in the main courthouse of the comarca where the crime occurred.
Each comarca has its own set of judges and legal staff to handle local cases.
Lawyers must be familiar with the rules specific to each comarca in the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: district - an area of a country or city, often for administrative or legal purposes
  • degree higher: province - a principal administrative division of certain countries
