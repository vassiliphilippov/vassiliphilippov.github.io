viscosimetry

noun
measurement of viscosity
Pronunciation: / vɪˌskoʊˈsɪmɪtri / (AmE), / vɪˌskɒˈsɪmɪtri / (BrE)
the measurement of the viscosity of a fluid, usually by means of a viscometer

Examples

Accurate viscosimetry measurement
Accurate viscosimetry is essential in quality control for the food and beverage industry.
Advances in viscosimetry have improved the production process for many polymers.
The laboratory specializes in viscosimetry to analyze different lubricating oils.
