nothing short of

x
emphatic phrase meaning 'exactly as stated'
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌθɪŋ ʃɔrt ʌv / (AmE), / ˈnʌθɪŋ ʃɔːt ɒv / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something is exactly or absolutely as stated, and not less or different in any way

Examples

Nothing short of miraculous
Her recovery after such a serious accident was nothing short of miraculous.
The team's performance in the final was nothing short of outstanding.
Winning the award at her age is nothing short of remarkable.
