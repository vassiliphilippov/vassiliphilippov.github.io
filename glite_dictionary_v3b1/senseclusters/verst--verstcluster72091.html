verst

noun
old Russian unit of length (1.067 km)
9.9
Pronunciation: / vɜrst / (AmE), / vɜːst / (BrE)
a Russian unit of length, formerly used especially in Russia, equal to 1.067 kilometers (about two-thirds of a mile)

Examples

Twenty versts marched
He marched over twenty versts through the snow to deliver the message.
Historical documents often measured land distances in versts.
The old map indicated that the next village was five versts away.
