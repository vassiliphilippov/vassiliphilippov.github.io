roll

noun
act of throwing dice
4.6
Pronunciation: / roʊl / (AmE), / rəʊl / (BrE)
an act of throwing dice, especially as one turn in a game

Examples

A lucky roll
A double six on the first roll gave her an early lead.
He won the game with his final roll of the dice.
Please wait for your turn to roll before moving your piece.
