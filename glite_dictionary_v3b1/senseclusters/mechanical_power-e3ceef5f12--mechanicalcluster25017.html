mechanical power

noun
rate at which mechanical work is performed
4.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈkæn.ɪ.kəl ˈpaʊ.ər/ /; / /mə.ˈkæn.ɪ.kəl paʊr/ (AmE), /mə.ˈkæn.ɪ.kəl ˈpaʊ.ə/ /; / /mə.ˈkæn.ɪ.kəl pɑː/ (BrE)
