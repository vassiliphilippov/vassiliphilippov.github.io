sclerosing leukoencephalitis

noun
rare brain inflammation caused by measles virus

A rare, chronic, and progressive brain inflammation caused by the measles virus, mainly affecting children and young adults, and typically fatal within several years.

Examples

Sclerosing leukoencephalitis after measles
Sclerosing leukoencephalitis is a devastating complication that can develop years after a measles infection.
Doctors may consider sclerosing leukoencephalitis when a young patient presents with progressive neurological decline.
Early measles vaccination significantly reduces the risk of sclerosing leukoencephalitis in children.
