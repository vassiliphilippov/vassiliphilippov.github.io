all that

adjective
self-important; overly impressive (idiom)
Pronunciation: / ɔl ðæt / (AmE), / ɔːl ðæt / (BrE)
(informal) Having an exaggerated sense of self-importance or impressiveness.

Examples

All that and more
After winning the talent show, she thought she was all that for weeks.
Don't act like you're all that; we're all equal here.
He walked into the meeting acting like he was all that and expected everyone to listen.
