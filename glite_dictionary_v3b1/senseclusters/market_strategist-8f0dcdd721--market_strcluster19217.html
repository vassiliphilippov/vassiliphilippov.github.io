market strategist

noun
person who plans marketing campaigns
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrkət ˈstrætədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪt ˈstrætədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person skilled in developing and planning marketing campaigns for businesses or products

Examples

A market strategist plans campaigns
As a market strategist, she stays up to date on the latest consumer trends.
Many businesses rely on a skilled market strategist to improve their competitive edge.
The company's new market strategist developed a campaign that significantly increased brand awareness.
