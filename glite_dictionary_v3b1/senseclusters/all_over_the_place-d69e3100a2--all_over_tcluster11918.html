all over the place

adverb
disorganized or scattered; confused
Pronunciation: / ɔl ˈoʊvɚ ðə pleɪs / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈəʊvə ðə pleɪs / (BrE)
in a state of confusion or disorganization; scattered in many directions

Examples

All over the place thoughts
After just one cup of coffee, my thoughts were all over the place.
His arguments in the debate were all over the place, making it hard to follow his main point.
The kids left their toys all over the place before bedtime.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disorganized - lacking order or structure
  • antonym: tidy - organized and in order
