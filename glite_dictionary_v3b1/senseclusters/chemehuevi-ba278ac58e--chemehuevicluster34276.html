Chemehuevi

proper noun
Native American people
Pronunciation: / ˌtʃɛmɪˈweɪvi / (AmE), / ˌtʃɛmɪˈweɪvi / (BrE)
a Native American people of the southwestern United States, especially in the Mojave Desert, or their Uto-Aztecan language

Examples

The Chemehuevi people
The Chemehuevi have a long history in the Mojave Desert region.
Many Chemehuevi traditions are still practiced by the community today.
Linguists are working to preserve the Chemehuevi language for future generations.
