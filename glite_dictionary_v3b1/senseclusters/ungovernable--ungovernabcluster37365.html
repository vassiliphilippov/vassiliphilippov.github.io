ungovernable

adjective
impossible to control or govern
4.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡʌvərnəbl̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡʌvənəbl / (BrE)
impossible to control or govern; difficult or impossible to restrain.

Examples

An ungovernable crowd
Despite many attempts, the crowd remained ungovernable during the protest.
Her ungovernable curiosity led her to explore dangerous places.
The class was so ungovernable that the school had to call in extra help.

Related Terms

  • antonym: manageable - easy to control or deal with
  • synonym: unruly - difficult to control or discipline
