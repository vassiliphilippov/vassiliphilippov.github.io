reordination

noun
rearrangement or reordering
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌɔːrdɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌɔːdɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of arranging or ordering something again, especially to restore a proper sequence

Examples

Reordination of documents
The reordination of the files was necessary after the computer system crashed.
Following the renovation, a complete reordination of the library shelves was undertaken.
The team discussed the reordination of tasks to improve workflow efficiency.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrangement - the action of putting things in a particular order
  • antonym: disorder - a lack of order or organization
  • synonym: reordering - the act of arranging again in a new order
