reordination

noun
rearrangement or reordering
8.7
Pronunciation: /riː.ɔːr.dɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rə.ɔːr.dɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /riː.ɔː.dɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rə.ɔː.dɪ.ˈneɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act or process of arranging or ordering something again, especially to restore a proper sequence
