fishtail

noun
tapered shape
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃˌteɪl / (BrE)
a shape or part that is wide at one end and tapers to a narrow point, resembling a fish's tail

Examples

Dress fishtail
The wedding gown had a beautiful fishtail that trailed behind the bride as she walked.
The artist designed a sculpture with a distinctive fishtail at its base.
Her hairstyle was finished with a braid ending in a neat fishtail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: taper - a gradual decrease in width or thickness
