thou

noun
thousandth of an inch
Pronunciation: / ðaʊ / (AmE), / ðaʊ / (BrE)
a unit of length equal to one thousandth of an inch, used especially in engineering and manufacturing

Examples

A thou of an inch
The engineer specified a tolerance of just one thou for the metal sheet.
A difference of several thou can affect the performance of precision machinery.
He measured the gap and found it was only five thou wide.
