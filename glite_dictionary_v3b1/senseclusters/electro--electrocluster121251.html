electro

noun
electronic music genre
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktroʊ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrəʊ / (BrE)
a style of electronic music characterized by the use of drum machines, synthesizers, and electronic sounds

Examples

Electro music festival
The DJ mixed classic electro tracks with modern electronic beats at the festival.
Many fans of electro appreciate its use of vintage drum machines and synthesizers.
He started producing electro music after being inspired by 1980s artists.

Related Terms

  • synonym: house - a style of electronic dance music with a steady four-on-the-floor beat
  • synonym: techno - a genre of electronic dance music with repetitive beats
