effortfully

adverb
with great effort
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɛfərtfəli / (AmE), / ˈɛfətfəli / (BrE)
in a way that requires or shows a lot of effort

Examples

To effortfully work
He effortfully completed the long hike up the mountain.
The student effortfully solved the complex math problem after several attempts.
She effortfully carried the groceries up three flights of stairs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: easily - without difficulty or effort
  • synonym: laboriously - in a way that takes a lot of time and effort
