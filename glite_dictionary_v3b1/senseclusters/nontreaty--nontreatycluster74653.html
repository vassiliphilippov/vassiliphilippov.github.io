nontreaty

noun
informal agreement
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈtriːti / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈtriːti / (BrE)
an agreement or arrangement between parties that does not have the formal status of a treaty

Examples

A nontreaty agreement
The two countries signed a nontreaty to outline their cooperation without legal obligations.
A nontreaty can help parties work together when a formal treaty is not possible.
They preferred a nontreaty arrangement to keep the process flexible and informal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agreement - a mutual arrangement or contract between parties
  • antonym: treaty - a formal agreement between countries or parties
