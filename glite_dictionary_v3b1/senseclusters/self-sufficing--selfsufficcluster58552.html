self-sufficing

adjective
able to provide for oneself
7.0
Pronunciation: / sɛlf səˈfaɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / sɛlf səˈfaɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
able to provide for one's own needs without assistance from others; self-sufficient.

Examples

Self-sufficing households
During the crisis, many households tried to become self-sufficing in basic necessities.
She took pride in being truly self-sufficing, never asking for outside support.
The monastery was a self-sufficing community, growing its own food and making its own clothes.
