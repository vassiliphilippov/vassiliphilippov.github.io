Carpenteria

noun
genus of flowering shrub native to California
Pronunciation: / kɑːr.pənˈtɪəri.ə / (AmE), / kɑː.pənˈtɪə.ri.ə / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the shrub family Hydrangeaceae, consisting of a single species native to California, sometimes placed in the Saxifragaceae family.

Carpenteria flowering plant
