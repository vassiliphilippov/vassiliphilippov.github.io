stone mimicry plant

noun
succulent plant resembling stones
4.9 9.2
Pronunciation: / stoʊn ˈmɪməkri plænt / (AmE), / stəʊn ˈmɪməkri plɑːnt / (BrE)
a highly succulent, stemless, clump-forming plant with grey-green leaves that closely resemble lumps of granite, native to South Africa
