merry-go-round

noun
rotating amusement ride with seats
2.1 3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛri ɡoʊ ˌraʊnd /; / ˈmɛri ɡoʊ raʊnd / (AmE), / ˈmɛri ɡəʊ ˌraʊnd /; / ˈmɛri ɡəʊ raʊnd / (BrE)
a large, rotating amusement ride with seats (often shaped like animals) that people can sit on for entertainment

Examples

Children's merry-go-round
A brightly painted merry-go-round stood in the center of the amusement park.
The children laughed as they rode the merry-go-round at the fair.
We spent the afternoon riding the merry-go-round and eating cotton candy.
After waiting in line, we finally got to ride the merry-go-round at the festival.
She smiled as the merry-go-round spun, its lights twinkling in the evening.
The children ran excitedly toward the merry-go-round as soon as they entered the park.
