menagerie

noun
collection of wild animals for exhibition
7.1 6.8
Pronunciation: / məˈnædʒəri / (AmE), / məˈnædʒəri /; / mɪˈnædʒəri / (BrE)
a collection of wild animals kept for exhibition or study, or the place where they are kept and shown to the public
