supercow

noun
exceptional performer
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərkaʊ / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəkau / (BrE)
an informal term for a person or thing that performs exceptionally well or is extremely productive

Examples

He is a supercow at work
After the deadline, his teammates joked that he was a supercow for completing so much alone.
She became the office supercow by finishing all her projects ahead of schedule.
Whenever a big challenge arises, Mark turns into a supercow and gets everything done.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overachiever - a person who performs better or achieves more than expected
  • antonym: slacker - a person who avoids work or effort
  • synonym: superstar - a person who is extremely successful or talented
