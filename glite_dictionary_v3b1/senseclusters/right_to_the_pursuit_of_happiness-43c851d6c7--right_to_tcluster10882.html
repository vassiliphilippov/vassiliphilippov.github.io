right to the pursuit of happiness

noun
fundamental right to seek happiness
3.7
Pronunciation: / raɪt tə ðə pərˈsut əv ˈhæpinəs / (AmE), / raɪt tə ðə pəˈsjuːt əv ˈhæpinəs / (BrE)
the fundamental right to seek personal fulfillment and happiness, often regarded as an essential human right and famously included in the United States Declaration of Independence

Examples

The right to the pursuit of happiness declared
Debates over civil liberties often reference the right to the pursuit of happiness as a core value.
Many believe that every citizen should have the right to the pursuit of happiness regardless of their background.
The Declaration of Independence asserts the right to the pursuit of happiness as a fundamental human entitlement.
