aye

interjection
expression of agreement
7.3
Pronunciation: / aɪ / (AmE), / aɪ / (BrE)
used to express agreement or to vote yes, especially in formal or official situations

Examples

Say aye to agree
When the chair asked for votes, everyone responded with a loud aye.
The motion passed after a majority shouted aye during the meeting.
If you agree with the proposal, please say aye now.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nay - used to show disagreement or to vote no
  • synonym: yes - used to show agreement or affirmation
