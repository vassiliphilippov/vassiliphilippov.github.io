California black walnut

noun
a tree species native to California
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkælɪˈfɔrnjə blæk ˈwɔlnʌt / (AmE), / ˌkælɪˈfɔːniə blæk ˈwɔːlnʌt / (BrE)
a medium-sized tree native to California, with aromatic compound leaves and edible nuts, scientifically known as Juglans hindsii
