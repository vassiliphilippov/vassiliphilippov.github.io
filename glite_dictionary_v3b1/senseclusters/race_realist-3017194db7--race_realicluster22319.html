race realist

noun
person who believes in inherent racial differences
9.9
Pronunciation: / reɪs ˈriːəlɪst / (AmE), / reɪs ˈrɪəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes that inherent differences exist between human races, typically emphasizing these differences in social or scientific discussions; often associated with controversial or pseudoscientific views on race

Examples

Race realists debate science
Debates featuring race realists often stir heated arguments about science and society.
Some public figures have been criticized for expressing race realist opinions in their writings.
The professor's identification as a race realist drew widespread condemnation from colleagues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: race science - the study of supposed biological differences between races, now widely discredited
  • synonym: racist - a person who believes that some races are inherently superior to others
