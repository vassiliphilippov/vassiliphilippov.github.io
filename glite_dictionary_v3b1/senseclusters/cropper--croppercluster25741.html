cropper

noun
small farmer or tenant
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɑːpər / (AmE), / ˈkrɒpə / (BrE)
a person who farms or harvests crops, especially a small farmer or sharecropper

Examples

A cotton cropper
Landowners often depended on croppers to plant and harvest the fields.
Many croppers struggled to make ends meet after a bad harvest.
The community was once full of small croppers and their families.
The new cropper made it possible to harvest the entire field in one day.
Many farms now use a mechanical cropper instead of hiring seasonal workers.
The cropper worked from sunrise to sunset during the busy harvest season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harvester - a person or machine that gathers crops
  • synonym: reaper - a person or machine that cuts and collects crops
  • synonym: sharecropper - a tenant farmer who gives a part of each crop as rent
