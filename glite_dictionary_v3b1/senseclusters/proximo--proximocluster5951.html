proximo

noun
next month (archaic)
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːksɪˌmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈprɒksɪməʊ / (BrE)
an old-fashioned term referring to the next or upcoming month, especially in dates

Examples

The 10th of proximo
The meeting is scheduled for the 10th of proximo.
Please submit your report by the 1st of proximo.
Rent payments are due on the first day of proximo each year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: next month - the month following the current one
  • antonym: ultimo - the previous month, especially in dates
