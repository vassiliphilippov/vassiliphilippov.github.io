drip-feed

verb
give information or supplies gradually
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɪp fiːd / (AmE), / ˈdrɪp fiːd / (BrE)
to provide information, resources, or supplies gradually and in small amounts over a period of time.

Examples

To drip-feed information
The government chose to drip-feed the information to the public to prevent unrest.
The trainer decided to drip-feed new techniques instead of introducing them all at once.
To keep customers engaged, the company will drip-feed updates over the next few months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trickle - to provide or come in small, slow amounts
