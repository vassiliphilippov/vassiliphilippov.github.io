arrowleaf

adjective
arrowhead-shaped leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈæroʊˌliːf / (AmE), / ˈærəʊˌliːf / (BrE)
having leaves shaped like an arrowhead, with a pointed tip and two spreading lobes at the base

Examples

Arrowleaf shape
The plant is easily recognized by its arrowleaf foliage, which stands out among other species.
Botanists often describe certain aquatic plants as having arrowleaf forms.
The arrowleaf pattern helps the leaves shed water efficiently after rain.
