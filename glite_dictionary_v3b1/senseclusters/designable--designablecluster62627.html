designable

adjective
able to be designed
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzaɪnəbl / (AmE), / dɪˈzaɪnəbl / (BrE)

Examples

A designable interface

Many modern products are designable to fit the unique needs of each customer.
With advanced tools, even complex structures are now designable by engineers.
