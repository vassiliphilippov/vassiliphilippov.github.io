trust territory

noun
territory under international supervision

Pronunciation: /trʌst ˈter.ɪ.tɔːr.i/ /; / /trʌst ˈter.ə.tɔːr.i/ (AmE), /trʌst ˈter.ɪ.tə.riː/ /; / /trʌst ˈter.ə.tə.riː/ (BrE)
