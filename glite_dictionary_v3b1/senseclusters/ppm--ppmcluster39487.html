ppm

sym
parts per million
Pronunciation: / pi pi ɛm / (AmE), / piː piː em / (BrE)
a unit of measurement meaning parts per million, used to express very small concentrations of substances

Examples

2 ppm chlorine
The laboratory measured only 2 ppm of chlorine in the pool water.
Air quality standards limit carbon monoxide to 9 ppm in indoor environments.
The fish cannot survive if ammonia levels exceed 1 ppm in the tank.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: percent - a unit meaning parts per hundred
  • degree lower: ppb - parts per billion, a smaller unit of concentration
