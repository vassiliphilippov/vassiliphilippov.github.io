subseries

noun
smaller series within series
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌsɪriːz / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌsɪəriːz / (BrE)
a smaller series that is part of a larger series, often within books, television, or other media

Examples

Book subseries
The detective novels include a subseries that focuses on the main character's early years.
Fans were excited when the studio announced a new subseries set in the same universe.
Each subseries explores a different aspect of the overall storyline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: miniseries - a short television series with a predetermined number of episodes
  • degree higher: series - a set of related things, such as books or shows, released in order
