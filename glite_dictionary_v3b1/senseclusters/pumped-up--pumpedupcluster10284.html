pumped-up

adjective
excited and full of energy
Pronunciation: / pʌmpt ˈʌp / (AmE), / pʌmpt ˈʌp / (BrE)
filled with excitement and enthusiasm, especially as a result of adrenaline or anticipation; highly energized or motivated.

Examples

A pumped-up athlete
He was so pumped-up about his new job that he could hardly sit still.
She always gets really pumped-up when she hears her favorite song.
The team was pumped-up before the big championship game.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - not affected by excitement or strong emotion
  • synonym: excited - feeling or showing excitement
