adjudication

noun
formal legal decision or judgment
4.4 6.6
Pronunciation: /ə.dʒuː.dɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ə.dʒuː.dɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
