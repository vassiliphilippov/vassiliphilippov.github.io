baidarka

noun
Aleut kayak
Pronunciation: / baɪˈdɑrkə / (AmE), / baɪˈdɑːkə / (BrE)
a small, covered kayak traditionally built and used by the Aleut people of Alaska for hunting and travel

Examples

A baidarka kayak
The museum displayed a traditional baidarka made from driftwood and animal skins.
He learned to paddle a baidarka while visiting the Aleutian Islands.
Researchers studied the design of the baidarka to understand its efficiency in rough waters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: canoe - a lightweight narrow boat, typically pointed at both ends and open on top
  • synonym: kayak - a small, narrow watercraft propelled by a double-bladed paddle
