predation

noun
the act of hunting and eating prey
Pronunciation: / prɪˈdeɪʃən / (AmE), / prɪˈdeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of hunting, killing, and eating another animal for food

Examples

Animal predation
Changes in the ecosystem led to a decrease in predation on smaller mammals.
Many species have evolved defenses to protect themselves from predation.
The lion's predation on the antelope is a classic example of the food chain in action.

Related Terms

  • antonym: herbivory - eating plants (by animals)
  • synonym: predator - an animal that hunts and eats other animals
