brushy

adjective
covered with bushes or thickets
Pronunciation: / ˈbrʌʃi / (AmE), / ˈbrʌʃi / (BrE)
covered with or consisting of bushes or thickets

Examples

Brushy hills
The trail became difficult to follow as we entered the brushy hills.
They cleared the brushy area behind the house to build a garden.
Wildlife is abundant in the brushy regions near the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bosky - covered with bushes or thickets, especially in a literary style
  • synonym: wooded - covered with trees or woods
