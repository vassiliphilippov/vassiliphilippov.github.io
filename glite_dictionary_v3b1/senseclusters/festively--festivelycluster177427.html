festively

adverb
in a celebratory way
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛstɪvli / (AmE), / ˈfɛstɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that is suitable for a celebration or festival, showing joy or festivity

Examples

To festively decorate
The entire street was festively lit with colorful lights for the parade.
Guests arrived festively dressed, ready to enjoy the New Year's Eve party.
The children sang festively as they decorated the tree together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: joyfully - in a happy or cheerful way
  • antonym: solemnly - in a serious or formal way
