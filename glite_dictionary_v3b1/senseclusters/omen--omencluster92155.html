omen

noun
a sign or event predicting the future

an event or sign thought to indicate what will happen in the future, often regarded as a warning or prediction

Examples

A lucky omen
Finding a four-leaf clover is often taken as a lucky omen.
Many people considered the sudden storm an omen of disaster.
The sighting of a black cat was once regarded as an evil omen.

  • presage

  • portent
    8.7 0.87/m

  • augury
    9.9 0.24/m

  • sign
