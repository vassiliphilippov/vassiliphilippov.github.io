sheen

noun
a soft, smooth shine or gloss
4.2 3.78/m
Pronunciation: /  Éin / (AmE), /  ÉiÀên / (BrE)
a soft, smooth shine on a surface, especially one that looks attractive or healthy

Examples

A sheen on hair
A soft sheen covered the surface of the newly painted car.
Her hair caught the light and gleamed with a healthy sheen.
The polished marble floor had a brilliant sheen in the afternoon sunlight.
After polishing, the silverware regained its brilliant sheen.
Her satin dress had a delicate sheen that shimmered in the candlelight.
The marble countertop had a subtle sheen that caught the morning light.
