surface-to-air missile system

noun
missile system targeting aircraft from the ground or sea
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːfɪs tu æɹ ˈmɪsaɪl ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈsɜːfɪs tʊ ˈeə ˈmɪsaɪl ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
a coordinated set of shipboard or land-based equipment that launches missiles designed to destroy aircraft or other airborne targets

Examples

A surface-to-air missile system
A malfunction in the surface-to-air missile system left the base vulnerable to aerial attacks.
Many countries invest heavily in developing new surface-to-air missile systems for national defense.
The warship was equipped with an advanced surface-to-air missile system to defend against enemy aircraft.

Related Terms

  • synonym: surface-to-air missile - missile launched from the ground or a ship to destroy aircraft
  • antonym: surface-to-surface missile system - system for launching missiles from the ground or sea to hit surface targets
