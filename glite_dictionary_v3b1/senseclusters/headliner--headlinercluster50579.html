headliner

noun
main performer at an event or show
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌlaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌlaɪnə / (BrE)
a performer who is the main attraction at an event or show and receives top billing

Examples

The headliner performer
Many fans arrived early to see the headliner perform his greatest hits.
She was thrilled to be chosen as the headliner for the summer music festival.
The headliner took the stage to an enthusiastic and cheering crowd.

Related Terms

  • synonym: star - a leading or principal performer
  • antonym: supporting act - a performer who appears before the headliner
