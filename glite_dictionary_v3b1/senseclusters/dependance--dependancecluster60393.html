dependance

noun
state of reliance or being controlled by another
7.5
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɛndəns / (AmE), / dɪˈpɛndəns / (BrE)
the state of relying on or being controlled by someone or something else

Examples

Emotional dependance
He struggled to break free from his emotional dependance on others.
Her financial dependance on her parents made it difficult for her to move out.
The company’s dependance on imported materials increased its vulnerability to supply chain disruptions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dependence - the usual spelling; state of relying on or being controlled by someone or something else
  • antonym: independence - the state of not being subject to control by others
