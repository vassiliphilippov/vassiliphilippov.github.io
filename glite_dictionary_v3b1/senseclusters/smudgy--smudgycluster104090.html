smudgy

adjective
covered with smudges or stains
Pronunciation: / ˈsmʌdʒi / (AmE), / ˈsmʌdʒi / (BrE)
covered with or marked by smudges; smeared so as to be dirty or blurred.

Examples

A smudgy mark
After wiping her eyes, her makeup became smudgy and uneven.
The child handed me a drawing with smudgy pastel colors all over the page.
The old photograph had a smudgy appearance due to years of handling.
