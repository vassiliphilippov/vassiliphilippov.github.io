Athabascan

noun
member of a North American indigenous group
9.9
Pronunciation: /æθ.ə.ˈbæs.kən/ /; / /æθ.ə.ˈbæs.kæn/ (AmE), /æθ.ə.ˈbæs.kən/ /; / /æθ.ə.ˈbæs.kæn/ (BrE)
