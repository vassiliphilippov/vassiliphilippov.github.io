interaural

adjective
between the ears
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈɔrəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈɔːrəl / (BrE)
situated between or relating to the space or comparison between the ears

Examples

Interaural time difference
The interaural time difference allows humans to determine the direction of a sound source.
Researchers measured the interaural level difference to study how we perceive spatial audio.
Hearing aids can be adjusted to account for interaural differences in sound perception.
