unlegislated

adjective
not established by law
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɛdʒɪsleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɛdʒɪsleɪtɪd / (BrE)
not established, regulated, or authorized by law or legislation

Examples

An unlegislated rule
The new guidelines remain unlegislated and are not yet enforceable.
Many issues discussed in the meeting were still unlegislated at the end of the year.
Without official approval, the proposal is considered unlegislated and has no legal standing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: illegal - not allowed by law
  • antonym: legislated - established or regulated by law
