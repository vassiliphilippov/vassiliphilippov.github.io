balefulness

noun
quality of being harmful or evil
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪl.fəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈbeɪl.f(ə)l.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being harmful, menacing, or evil

Examples

The balefulness of glare
She was unnerved by the balefulness of his glare across the room.
Stories of ancient curses often speak of a balefulness that haunts the unlucky.
The villain's balefulness was evident in every malicious plot he devised.

Related Terms

  • antonym: benevolence - the quality of being well-meaning or kindly
  • synonym: malignity - the state of being very harmful or malignant
