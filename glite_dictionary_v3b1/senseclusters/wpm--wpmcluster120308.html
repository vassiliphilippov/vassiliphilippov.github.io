wpm

noun
words per minute (rate measurement)
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˌdʌbəl.juː piː ˈɛm / (AmE), / ˌdʌbəl.juː piː ˈɛm / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'words per minute', used to indicate the number of words produced or processed in one minute, especially in typing, reading, or speaking

Examples

wpm typing speed
Her typing speed is measured at 80 wpm, which is above average.
Improving your wpm can make writing essays and taking notes much faster.
Professional transcriptionists can often reach over 100 wpm with high accuracy.
