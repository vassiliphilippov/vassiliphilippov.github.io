clean up

verb
stop using drugs or addictive substances
3.9 0.43/m
Pronunciation: / klin  åp / (AmE), / kliÀên  åp / (BrE)
to stop using drugs or other addictive substances, especially after a period of abuse

Examples

To clean up after rehab
After rehab, she managed to clean up and stay sober for five years.
He struggled for years before he finally cleaned up and started a new life.
Many former addicts say that deciding to clean up was the hardest but best choice they ever made.
