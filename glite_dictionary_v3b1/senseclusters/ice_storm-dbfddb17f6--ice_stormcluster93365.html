ice storm

noun
a storm with freezing rain and ice accumulation
3.2 2.5
Pronunciation: /aɪs stɔːrm/ (AmE), /aɪs stɔːm/ (BrE)
A storm in which freezing rain coats surfaces with ice, often causing hazardous conditions.

Examples

An ice storm warning
Driving was extremely dangerous during the ice storm due to slick roads and falling branches.
Power outages were widespread after the ice storm damaged utility lines across the region.
The ice storm last night left the trees coated in a thick layer of ice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freezing rain - rain that freezes on contact, forming a layer of ice
