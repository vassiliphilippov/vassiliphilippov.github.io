bandelette

noun
narrow ring-shaped molding on column
Pronunciation: / bæn.dəˈlɛt / (AmE), / bænd.ɪˈlɛt / (BrE)
A narrow molding forming a ring, typically found at the top of a column in classical architecture.

Examples

A bandelette molding
Architectural drawings indicate that a bandelette should encircle each column just below the capital.
At the top of the pillar, a bandelette separated the shaft from the capital.
Restorers carefully cleaned the original bandelette on the ancient temple column.
