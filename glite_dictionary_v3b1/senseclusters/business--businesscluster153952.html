business

noun
matter of concern or responsibility
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs / (BrE)
a matter that is someone's concern or responsibility; something that should rightfully involve or interest a person

Examples

None of your business
He told me it was none of my business and refused to answer.
What I do in my free time is strictly my business.
You should really mind your own business instead of interfering.
