excretion

noun
bodily waste eliminated from the body
2.2
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈskriʃən / (AmE), / ɪkˈskriːʃən / (BrE)
waste matter, such as urine, feces, or sweat, that is discharged from the body

Examples
