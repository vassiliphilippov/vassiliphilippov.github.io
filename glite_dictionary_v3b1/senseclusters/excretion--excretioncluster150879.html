Synonyms

    excreta names bodily waste like urine or feces used in scientific writing, while excretion means the release or process of that waste.
  • excretory product
    6.1 0.00/m
    excretory product is a technical term for a specific waste such as urine or sweat, while excretion is the body's waste discharge.
  • body waste
    2.7 0.02/m
    body waste refers to waste matter like urine, feces, or sweat in everyday use, while excretion names the body's act of sending out waste.
  • human waste
    3.5 0.50/m
    human waste means waste produced by people, often in public-health contexts, while excretion is the general bodily discharge of waste.
  • excrement
    6.1 1.43/m
    excrement usually means solid feces and is more specific or blunt, while excretion includes all body wastes and the process of losing them.
