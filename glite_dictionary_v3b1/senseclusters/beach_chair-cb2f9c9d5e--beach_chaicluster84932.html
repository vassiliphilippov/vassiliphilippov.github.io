beach chair

noun
folding outdoor chair for the beach
Pronunciation: / bitʃ tʃɛr / (AmE), / biːtʃ tʃɛː / (BrE)
A folding chair with a wooden or metal frame and a canvas or fabric seat, designed for use outdoors, especially on the beach.

Examples

A beach chair outdoors
I relaxed in a beach chair while watching the waves roll in.
She packed her beach chair and umbrella for the afternoon at the shore.
The old wooden beach chair was surprisingly comfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deck chair - a folding chair with a frame and fabric seat, commonly used outdoors or on decks
