Synonyms

    2.0 3/m
    take up often describes lifting with a scooping motion or in careful contexts, while pick up is the common everyday action of taking something by hand.
  • scoop up
    2.8 2/m
    scoop up means to lift quickly in a sweeping, cupped motion with hand or container, while pick up is a neutral hand lift.
  • lift
    1.0 1/m
    lift means to remove something from a surface carefully or skillfully, while pick up is the more general everyday action of taking by hand.
  • lift up
    1.1 5/m
    lift up means to raise something to a higher position, often fully off the ground, while pick up means taking something from a surface by hand.
  • scoop
    1.9 6/m
