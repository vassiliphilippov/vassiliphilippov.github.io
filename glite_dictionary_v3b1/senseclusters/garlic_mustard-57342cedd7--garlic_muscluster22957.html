garlic mustard

noun
European herb smelling of garlic
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrlɪk ˈmʌstərd / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːlɪk ˈmʌstəd / (BrE)
a European herb (Alliaria petiolata) of the mustard family, having leaves that smell like garlic when crushed

Examples

Crushed garlic mustard leaves
Crushed garlic mustard leaves release a strong odor similar to garlic.
The woods behind our house were overrun with garlic mustard, crowding out native wildflowers.
Volunteers spent the morning pulling up garlic mustard to help restore the local ecosystem.
