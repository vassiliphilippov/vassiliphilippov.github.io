pedant

noun
person overly concerned with rules or learning
7.4 6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈpe.dənt/ (AmE), /ˈpe.dənt/ (BrE)
A person who is excessively concerned with minor details, rules, or displaying academic learning.

Examples

A pedant corrects errors

He can be such a pedant about grammar that it's hard to enjoy a conversation.
The meeting was delayed by a pedant who argued every tiny point of procedure.
