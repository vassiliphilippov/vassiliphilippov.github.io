black-backed gull

noun
large gull with a black back and wings
Pronunciation: / ˈblæk ˌbækt ɡʌl / (AmE), / ˈblæk ˌbækt ɡʌl / (BrE)
a large white gull species with a black back and wings, found in coastal regions of the Northern Hemisphere

Examples

A black-backed gull soars
A black-backed gull soared above the harbor, its striking black wings glinting in the sunlight.
During the nature hike, the guide pointed out several black-backed gulls resting on the rocky shore.
The fisherman watched as a black-backed gull swooped down to snatch a fish from the water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: herring gull - a common large gull with grey back
  • degree lower: lesser black-backed gull - a smaller species of black-backed gull
