birdhouse

noun
shelter for birds; nesting box
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːdˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A small structure or box placed outdoors to provide birds with a place to nest or shelter.

Examples

A birdhouse in garden
A bluebird made its nest inside the old birdhouse last spring.
Several colorful birdhouses lined the garden fence.
We hung a new birdhouse in the backyard to attract sparrows.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: aviary - a large enclosure for keeping birds
  • synonym: nest box - a box provided for birds to nest in
