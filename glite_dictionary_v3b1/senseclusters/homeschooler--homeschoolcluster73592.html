homeschooler

noun
person teaching at home
3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈhoʊm.skuː.lər/ (AmE), /ˈhəʊm.skuː.lə/ (BrE)
a person, often a parent, who teaches their own children at home instead of sending them to a traditional school

Examples

Homeschooler teaches kids
As a dedicated homeschooler, he planned daily lessons for his children.
The local library offers resources for every homeschooler in the community.
Many homeschoolers join online groups to share ideas and support each other.

  • synonym: teacher - a person who teaches others, especially in a school
