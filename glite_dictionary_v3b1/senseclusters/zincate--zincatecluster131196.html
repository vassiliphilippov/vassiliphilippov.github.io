zincate

verb
to coat with zinc
Pronunciation: / ˈzɪŋkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈzɪŋkeɪt / (BrE)
to treat or coat a metal surface with a zinc-containing solution to deposit a thin layer of zinc

Examples

To zincate metal
Before painting, technicians zincate the aluminum parts to prevent corrosion.
It is important to zincate the surface to ensure proper adhesion of the next coating.
The factory routinely zincates metal components as part of the finishing process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: galvanize - to coat metal with a protective layer of zinc
