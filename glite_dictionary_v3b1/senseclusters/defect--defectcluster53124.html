defect

noun
a fault or deficiency

Examples

A defect in design
His plan was well thought out, but there was one serious defect in the logic.
Honesty is important, but a lack of confidence can be a defect in leadership.
The product was returned because it had a manufacturing defect.
Even a minor defect can decrease the value of a product.
She tried to hide the defect on her cheek with makeup.
The fruit had a small defect on its surface, but it was still edible.
Certain genetic defects can lead to serious health problems if left untreated.
Regular screening can help identify heart defects early in childhood.
The doctor explained that the vision problem was caused by a defect in the retina.
