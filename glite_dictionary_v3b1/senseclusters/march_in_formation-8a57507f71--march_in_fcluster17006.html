march in formation

verb
walk in a structured, organized group
2.5
Pronunciation: / mɑrtʃ ɪn fɔˈrmeɪʃən / (AmE), / mɑːtʃ ɪn fɔːˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
to walk in a structured, organized arrangement, typically as part of a group, especially for military or ceremonial purposes

Examples

To march in formation together
At the parade, the cadets marched in formation down the main street.
Protesters decided to march in formation to make their demonstration more organized.
The children learned to march in formation as part of their school drill exercises.
