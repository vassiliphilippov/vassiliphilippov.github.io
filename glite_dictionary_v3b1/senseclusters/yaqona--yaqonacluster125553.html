yaqona

noun
Fijian kava drink
Pronunciation: / jɑːˈkoʊnə / (AmE), / jɑːˈkɒnə / (BrE)
a traditional Fijian beverage made from the root of the kava plant, often consumed during ceremonies

Examples

A bowl of yaqona
During the welcome ceremony, the chief offered us a bowl of yaqona to drink.
Tourists are often invited to share yaqona as part of the Fijian cultural experience.
The preparation of yaqona is an important ritual in many Fijian villages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kava - a drink made from the root of a tropical plant, used in Pacific Island cultures
