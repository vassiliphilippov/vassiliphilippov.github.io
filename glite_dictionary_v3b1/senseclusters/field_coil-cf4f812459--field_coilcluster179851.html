field coil

noun
coil generating a magnetic field in motors/generators
5.1 5.1
Pronunciation: /fiːld kɔɪl/ (AmE), /fiːld kɔɪl/ (BrE)
an electric coil wound around a field magnet, used to produce a magnetic field in an electric machine such as a motor or generator
