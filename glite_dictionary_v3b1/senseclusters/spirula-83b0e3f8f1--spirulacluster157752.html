Spirula

noun
genus of small deep-sea cephalopods
9.9
Pronunciation: /spɪ.ˈruː.lə/ (AmE), /spɪ.ˈruː.lə/ (BrE)
a genus of small deep-sea cephalopods known for their many-chambered internal spiral shells, resembling those of extinct belemnites
