Synonyms

    uncertain is not clearly decided or fixed, while doubtful suggests something people question or think may be untrue.
  • in question
    5.8 7/m
    in question is seen as doubted or under review by others, while doubtful suggests people suspect it is not true.
  • dubious
    6.5 4/m
    dubious often feels suspicious or untrustworthy, while doubtful means something may be untrue or not clearly proved.
  • questionable
    3.9 8/m
    questionable often suggests poor quality or possible dishonesty, while doubtful focuses on being uncertain or unlikely to be true.
  • unlikely
    1.9 20/m
    unlikely means having little chance of happening, while doubtful often adds a feeling that something is wrong or not genuine.
  • implausible
    7.0 1/m
