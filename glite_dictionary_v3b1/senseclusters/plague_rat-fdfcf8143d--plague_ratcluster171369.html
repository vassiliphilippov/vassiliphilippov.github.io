plague rat

noun
corrupt or undesirable person (offensive)
9.9
Pronunciation: / pleɪɡ ræt / (AmE), / pleɪɡ ræt / (BrE)
an offensive term for a person considered corrupt, undesirable, or whose presence is likened to the spread of disease

Examples

A plague rat insult
After the scandal, he felt like a plague rat among his peers.
He was called a plague rat for spreading rumors at work.
The media labeled the politician a plague rat for his corrupt dealings.
