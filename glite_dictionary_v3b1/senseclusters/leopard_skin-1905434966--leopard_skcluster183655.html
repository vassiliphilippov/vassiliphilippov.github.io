leopard skin

noun
spotted animal pelt
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛpərd skɪn / (AmE), / ˈlɛpəd skɪn / (BrE)
the spotted fur or pelt of a leopard, or a fabric or pattern that resembles it

Examples

Leopard skin pattern
She wore a striking leopard skin dress to the party.
The chair was upholstered in a bold leopard skin pattern.
Hunters once prized real leopard skin for its beauty and rarity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: animal print - a fabric or pattern imitating animal fur or skin
  • synonym: zebra skin - the striped pelt of a zebra or a pattern resembling it
