durian

noun
tree bearing spiny edible fruit
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʊriən / (AmE), / ˈdjʊəriən / (BrE)
A tree of southeastern Asia that bears large, edible, oval fruit with a hard, spiny rind.

Examples

Durian tree
Farmers cultivate the durian tree in many parts of Southeast Asia.
Some people travel long distances to sample fresh durian during its harvesting season.
The durian is famous for both its strong smell and unique flavor.
