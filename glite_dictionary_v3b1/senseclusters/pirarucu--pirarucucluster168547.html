pirarucu

noun
Amazonian fish
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpiːrəruːˈkuː / (AmE), / ˌpɪrəruːˈkuː / (BrE)
a very large freshwater fish native to the Amazon River, prized for its edible meat

Examples

The large pirarucu
The chef prepared grilled pirarucu for the special dinner menu.
Fishermen in Brazil catch pirarucu because it is highly valued for its meat.
The pirarucu can grow to over two meters long in the wild.
