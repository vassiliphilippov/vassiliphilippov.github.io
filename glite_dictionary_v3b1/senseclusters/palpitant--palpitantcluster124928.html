palpitant

adjective
slightly trembling or throbbing
Pronunciation: / ˈpæl.pɪ.tənt / (AmE), / ˈpal.pɪ.tənt / (BrE)
trembling, throbbing, or quivering with a slight and rapid motion; palpitating.

Examples

A palpitant heart
The air was heavy and palpitant with anticipation before the winner was announced.
The patient’s palpitant pulse worried the doctor during the examination.
Through the mist, they saw the palpitant flicker of a distant lantern.

Related Terms

  • synonym: palpitating - beating or throbbing rapidly or strongly
