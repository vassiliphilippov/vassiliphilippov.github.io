reengage

verb
to connect again
4.1
Pronunciation: /riː.ɪn.ˈɡeɪdʒ/ /; / /rɪ.ɪn.ˈɡeɪdʒ/ (AmE), /riː.ɪn.ˈɡeɪdʒ/ /; / /rɪ.ɪn.ˈɡeɪdʒ/ (BrE)
to cause a machine part or mechanism to connect or operate again after being disengaged.

Examples

To reengage the gear

After the safety check, the technician was able to reengage the machine's power supply.
If the gears slip, you may need to reengage them manually to continue operating the device.
