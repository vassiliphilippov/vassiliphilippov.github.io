inkjet

adjective
for inkjet printers
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋkˌdʒɛt / (AmE), / ˈɪŋkˌdʒɛt / (BrE)
relating to or designed for use with an inkjet printer or the inkjet printing method

Examples

Inkjet paper
This inkjet paper is specially made to absorb the liquid ink from your printer.
She bought an inkjet cartridge to replace the empty one in her home printer.
The brochure was printed on an inkjet machine for high-quality color images.

Related Terms

  • antonym: laser - relating to or designed for use with a laser printer
