paraparetic

adjective
partial lower limb paralysis
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəpəˈrɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌpærəpəˈrɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by partial paralysis of the lower limbs

Examples

Paraparetic symptoms
After the accident, the patient became paraparetic and required physical therapy to regain strength.
Doctors noted that the paraparetic symptoms were caused by damage to the spinal cord.
The clinic specializes in treating paraparetic conditions resulting from neurological injuries.
