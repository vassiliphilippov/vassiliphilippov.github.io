photometric

adjective
relating to measurement of light
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the measurement of light or the intensity of light

Examples

Photometric analysis
A photometric analysis revealed subtle variations in brightness across the painting.
The research team used photometric data to estimate the distance of the galaxy.
The telescope is equipped with sensitive photometric instruments to measure starlight.
