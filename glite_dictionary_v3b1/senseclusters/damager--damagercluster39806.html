damager

noun
one who causes harm
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdæmɪdʒər / (AmE), / ˈdæmɪdʒə / (BrE)
a person or thing that causes harm or damage to something

Examples

The damager of property
The heavy rain was the biggest damager of the old bridge this year.
As a careless driver, he became a frequent damager of parked cars.
Insects can be a serious damager to crops if not controlled.

Related Terms

  • synonym: destroyer - a person or thing that destroys something
  • antonym: protector - a person or thing that protects from harm or damage
