PET scanner

noun
medical imaging device (positron emission tomography)
4.3 3.0
Pronunciation: /pet ˈskæn.ər/ (AmE), /pet ˈskæn.ə/ (BrE)
a device that produces cross-sectional images of metabolic processes in the body using positron emission tomography

Examples

PET scanner machine
A PET scanner can detect changes in cellular activity before physical symptoms appear.
The hospital recently installed a new PET scanner to improve cancer diagnosis.
Training is required for technicians to operate a PET scanner safely and effectively.
