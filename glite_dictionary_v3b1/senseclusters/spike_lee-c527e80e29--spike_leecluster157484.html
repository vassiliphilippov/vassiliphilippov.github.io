Spike Lee

proper noun
American filmmaker and cultural commentator
Pronunciation: / spaɪk liː / (AmE), / spaɪk liː / (BrE)
(born 1957) An American film director, producer, writer, and actor known for exploring the experiences of African Americans through his films.
