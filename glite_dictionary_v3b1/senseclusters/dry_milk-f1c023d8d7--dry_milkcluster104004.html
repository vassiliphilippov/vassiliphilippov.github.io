dry milk

noun
powdered milk made by dehydration
4.7
Pronunciation: / draɪ mɪlk / (AmE), / draɪ mɪlk / (BrE)
powdered milk produced by removing the water content from liquid milk, allowing for long-term storage

Examples

Dry milk powder
Baking recipes sometimes call for dry milk as a convenient substitute.
Many families buy dry milk because it lasts longer than fresh milk.
To make a cup of dry milk, simply add water according to the instructions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: powdered milk - milk that has been evaporated and dried to a powder form
