godown

noun
warehouse (India, Malaysia)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊdaʊn / (BrE)
a warehouse, especially in India or Malaysia, where goods are stored

Examples

A godown warehouse
During the monsoon season, the godown was often flooded, damaging stored goods.
The company kept most of its supplies in a large godown near the port.
Traders inspected the spices before moving them from the godown to local shops.

Related Terms

  • synonym: warehouse - a large building where goods are stored before distribution
