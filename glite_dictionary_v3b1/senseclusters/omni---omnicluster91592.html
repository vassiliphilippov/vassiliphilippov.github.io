omni-

x
prefix meaning all
Pronunciation: / ˈɑmni / (AmE), / ˈɒmni / (BrE)
a prefix meaning all or everywhere, used to form compound words

Examples

Omni- prefix meaning all
The prefix omni- is used in words like 'omniscient', meaning all-knowing.
In science fiction, an omni-tool is a device that can do everything.
The word 'omnipresent' uses the omni- prefix to mean present everywhere.
