drawspan

noun
movable bridge section
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɔˌspæn / (AmE), / ˈdrɔːˌspæn / (BrE)
the movable section of a drawbridge that can be raised or lowered to allow passage beneath

Examples

The drawspan section
The drawspan was raised to allow the cargo ship to pass through the waterway.
Engineers inspected the drawspan regularly to ensure it operated safely and smoothly.
When the drawspan is lowered, cars and pedestrians can cross the bridge as usual.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drawbridge - a bridge with a section that can be raised or lowered
  • antonym: fixed span - a bridge section that does not move
