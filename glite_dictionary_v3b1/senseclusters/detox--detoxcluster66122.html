detox

verb
to remove toxins
3.9 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàdiÀêt…ëks / (AmE), / ÀàdiÀêt…íks / (BrE)
to remove harmful substances from the body, often by following a special diet or treatment

Examples

To detox with diet
After the holidays, she decided to detox by eating only fruits and vegetables for a week.
Some people detox to feel healthier and have more energy.
He plans to detox from caffeine by gradually drinking less coffee each day.

Synonyms

  • detoxify
    5.3 0.24/m
    detoxify is a medical treatment that removes drugs or alcohol from a person, while detox often means following a diet or general cleansing.
  • clean one's system
    5.1 0.01/m
    clean one's system is a casual phrase for removing harmful substances, while detox suggests a planned diet or treatment to cleanse the body.
  • flush out toxins
    4.4 0.04/m
    flush out toxins refers to using fluids or processes to force out poisons, while detox covers broader medical or dietary cleansing plans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cleanse - to make something clean or free from impurities
  • synonym: purify - to remove contaminants or impurities
