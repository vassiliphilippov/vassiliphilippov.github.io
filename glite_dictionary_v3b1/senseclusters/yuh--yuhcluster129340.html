yuh

interjection
informal yes
9.9
Pronunciation: / jə / (AmE), / jə / (BrE)
an informal way to say yes or to show agreement

Examples

Yuh, that's right
A: Are you coming to the party? B: Yuh, I wouldn't miss it.
When asked if he understood the instructions, he simply replied, 'yuh.'
She gave a quick 'yuh' when her friend offered her a ride home.
