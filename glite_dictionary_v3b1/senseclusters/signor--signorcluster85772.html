signor

noun
Italian courtesy title for a man
Pronunciation: / siˈnyɔr / (AmE), / sɪˈnjɔː / (BrE)
a title of respect used before the name of an Italian man, or used to address him directly

Examples

Signor Rossi
At the opera, the signor greeted the audience with a broad smile.
Please wait, Signor Rossi will see you now.
The letter was addressed to Signor Bianchi in Rome.

Related Terms

  • synonym: signora - Italian courtesy title for a married woman
  • synonym: signorina - Italian courtesy title for an unmarried woman
  • synonym: sir - English courtesy title for a man
