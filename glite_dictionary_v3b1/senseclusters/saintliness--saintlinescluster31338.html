saintliness

noun
quality of being like a saint
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪntlinəs / (AmE), / ˈseɪntlɪnəs / (BrE)
the quality of having the virtues, goodness, or holiness associated with a saint

Examples

The saintliness of a person
He was remembered for his selflessness and extraordinary saintliness during difficult times.
Her saintliness earned her the admiration of everyone in the village.
The teacher's patience and saintliness were evident in her gentle words and actions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: holiness - the state of being holy
  • antonym: wickedness - the quality of being evil or morally wrong
