stiflingly

adverb
oppressively or suffocatingly
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪflɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈstaɪflɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that makes it difficult to breathe or feel comfortable due to heat, lack of air, or restriction

Examples

The air is stiflingly hot
The air in the crowded subway was stiflingly hot and made everyone uncomfortable.
She found the rules at her new school stiflingly strict and hard to follow.
During the summer, the attic became stiflingly warm by midday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oppressively - in a way that is overwhelming or hard to endure
  • antonym: refreshingly - in a way that is pleasantly cool or invigorating
  • synonym: suffocatingly - in a way that causes difficulty in breathing
