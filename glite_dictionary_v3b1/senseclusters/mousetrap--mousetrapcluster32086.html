mousetrap

noun
device for catching mice
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊsˌtræp / (AmE), / ˈmaʊs.træp / (BrE)
a device designed to catch or kill mice

Examples

A mousetrap under the sink
He set a mousetrap under the sink after spotting droppings.
The old farmhouse had several mousetraps hidden in the corners.
They checked the mousetrap every morning to see if it had caught anything.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trap - a device for catching animals
