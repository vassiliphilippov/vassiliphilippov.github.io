linkage

noun
joining records across datasets
6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈlɪŋ.kɪdʒ/ /; / /ˈlɪŋ.kədʒ/ (AmE), /ˈlɪŋ.kɪdʒ/ /; / /ˈlɪŋ.kədʒ/ (BrE)
(in data science and research) the process of identifying and joining records from different data sources that refer to the same entity, creating an integrated dataset for analysis.

Examples

Data linkage process
Public health researchers performed linkage of hospital and census records to analyze long-term patient outcomes.
The team used privacy-preserving methods when conducting linkage between administrative databases and survey data.
Successful data linkage allowed policymakers to track program impacts across multiple service providers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: data integration - the process of combining data from different sources to provide a unified view
  • synonym: record linkage - the specific practice of matching records that refer to the same person or event across datasets
