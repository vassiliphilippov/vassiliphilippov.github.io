Gaius Cassius Longinus

proper noun
Roman senator; led conspiracy against Caesar
Pronunciation: / ˈɡaɪ.əs ˈkæʃ.i.əs lɒnˈdʒaɪ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪ.əs ˈka.si.əs lɒŋˈɪ.nəs / (BrE)
