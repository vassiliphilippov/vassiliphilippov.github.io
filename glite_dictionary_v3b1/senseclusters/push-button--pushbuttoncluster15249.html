push-button

adjective
operated by button
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊʃˌbʌtən / (AmE), / ˈpʊʃˌbʌtən / (BrE)
operated or controlled by pressing a button or buttons.

Examples

Push-button controls
The office lights are push-button for easy access and energy saving.
He admired the convenience of the new push-button ignition in his car.
Many modern appliances now feature push-button controls for user-friendly operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automatic - working by itself with little or no direct human control
  • antonym: manual - operated by hand rather than by electricity or automation
