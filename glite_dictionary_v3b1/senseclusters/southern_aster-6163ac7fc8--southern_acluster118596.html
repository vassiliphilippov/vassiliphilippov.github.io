southern aster

noun
a plant of the aster family (Eurybia genus)
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌðərn ˈæstər / (AmE), / ˈsʌðən ˈæstə / (BrE)
A plant of the aster family, especially Eurybia hemispherica and Eurybia paludosa, native to the southeastern United States.

Examples

The southern aster plant
Gardeners often choose southern aster for its late-season blooms and attractive lavender flowers.
The southern aster attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies to the garden.
The southern aster thrives in moist woodlands across the southeastern United States.
