die

verb
to yearn for something intensely
Pronunciation: / daɪ / (AmE), / daɪ / (BrE)
(informal) To feel an intense longing or desire for something; to yearn deeply.

Examples

To die for chocolate
I'm dying for a cup of coffee after that long meeting.
She was dying to find out the results of the competition.
We're dying to go on vacation after such a busy year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crave - to feel a powerful desire for something
  • synonym: yearn - to have an intense feeling of longing for something
