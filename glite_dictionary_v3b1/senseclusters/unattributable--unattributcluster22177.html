unattributable

adjective
not able to be attributed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.əˈtrɪbjətəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈtrɪbjʊtəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be attributed to a particular source, author, or cause.

Examples

Unattributable source
Some quotes in the report are unattributable because the speakers requested anonymity.
The source of the rumor remained unattributable despite numerous investigations.
The strange noise was unattributable to any known equipment in the lab.

Related Terms

  • antonym: attributable - able to be ascribed or assigned to a cause, source, or author
