lute

verb
to seal with substance
9.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / luÀêt / (AmE), / luÀêt / (BrE)
to seal or cover something with a substance to make it airtight or watertight

Examples

To lute a joint
The technician will lute the glass cover to ensure no air can enter.
In traditional pottery, artisans lute the joints with clay to prevent leaks.
To fix the leak, the plumber carefully luted the pipe connections.
