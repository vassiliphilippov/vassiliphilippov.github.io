multisector

adjective
involving many sectors
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈsɛktər / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈsɛktə / (BrE)
involving or relating to several different sectors or areas of activity

Examples

Multisector approach
The city developed a multisector plan to address transportation, housing, and education together.
A multisector approach is needed to solve complex social problems involving health and employment.
International aid organizations often use multisector strategies to maximize their impact.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cross-sector - involving more than one sector
  • antonym: single-sector - involving only one sector
