radiographic

adjective
relating to or produced by radiography
4.4 5.0
Pronunciation: /reɪ.di.ə.ˈgræf.ɪk/ /; / /reɪ.di.oʊ.ˈgræf.ɪk/ (AmE), /reɪ.dɪ.ə.ˈgræf.ɪk/ /; / /reɪ.di.əʊ.ˈgræf.ɪk/ (BrE)
relating to or produced by radiography, especially using X-rays to view the internal structure of something

Examples

A radiographic scan
A radiographic scan revealed the presence of a tumor in her lung.
Routine dental check-ups often include radiographic examinations to detect cavities.
The doctor reviewed the radiographic images to diagnose the fracture.
