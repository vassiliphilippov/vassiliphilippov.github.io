zimmi

noun
protected non-Muslim
Pronunciation: / ˈzɪmi / (AmE), / ˈzɪmi / (BrE)
a non-Muslim living under Muslim rule who is granted protected status and certain rights in exchange for paying a special tax under Islamic law

Examples

A zimmi pays tax
Under Islamic rule, a zimmi was required to pay the jizya tax for protection.
The rights and responsibilities of a zimmi were defined by Islamic legal codes.
Historical records show that many zimmis lived peacefully within Muslim societies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dhimmi - a non-Muslim subject with protected status under Islamic law
