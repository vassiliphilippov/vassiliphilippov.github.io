plainclothes

adjective
not in uniform
4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈpleɪn.kloʊðz/ /; / /ˈpleɪn.kloʊz/ (AmE), /ˈpleɪn.kləʊðz/ /; / /ˈpleɪn.kləʊz/ (BrE)
wearing ordinary clothes instead of a uniform, especially to avoid being recognized as a police officer or official

Examples

Plainclothes police officer
A plainclothes officer blended in with the crowd during the investigation.
The plainclothes detectives gathered evidence without drawing attention to themselves.
She did not realize the man was a plainclothes police officer until he showed his badge.

Related Terms

  • antonym: uniformed - wearing a uniform, especially as part of a job
