Republic of Kenya

proper noun
country in eastern Africa
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈkɛnjə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈkiːnjə / (BrE)
An East African country, officially called the Republic of Kenya, known for its diverse wildlife, landscapes, and as the site of important archaeological discoveries.
