Gethsemane

proper noun
period of great suffering
Pronunciation: / ɡɛθˈsɛməni / (AmE), / ɡɛθˈsɛməni / (BrE)
a situation or period of intense suffering, anguish, or spiritual trial, especially one that tests a person's faith or resolve

Examples

Personal Gethsemane moment
After losing his job and home, he went through his own Gethsemane before finding hope again.
The months of illness were a true Gethsemane for her and her family.
Many people experience a personal Gethsemane during times of deep loss or crisis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ordeal - a very difficult or painful experience
  • antonym: relief - the removal or easing of pain or distress
  • synonym: trial - a test of patience or endurance
