mailbox

noun
virtual email inbox
1.6
Pronunciation: /ˈmeɪl.bɑːks/ (AmE), /ˈmeɪl.bɒks/ (BrE)
A virtual folder or inbox in an email or messaging system where incoming electronic messages are stored and managed.

Examples

Email mailbox
She checked her mailbox on the phone and found several unread messages from clients.
After switching providers, his company moved thousands of user mailbox entries to the new server.
Make sure you empty your mailbox before the migration to avoid losing important emails.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inbox - a folder that receives incoming electronic messages
  • antonym: outbox - a folder where outgoing electronic messages are stored before sending
