low-lying

adjective
at a small elevation above the ground
1.7 3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊˌlaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈləʊˌlaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
situated at a low elevation, often near water and likely to flood
