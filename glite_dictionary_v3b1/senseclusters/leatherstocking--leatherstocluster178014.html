leatherstocking

noun
American frontiersman
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛðərˌstɑːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɛðəˌstɒkɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

In the novel, a leatherstocking guides settlers through forests and across rivers.
Many stories from the 1800s feature a leatherstocking as the main character.
