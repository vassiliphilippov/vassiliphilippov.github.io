nibong

noun
Southeast Asian palm
9.9
Pronunciation: / niˈbɔŋ / (AmE), / niˈbɒŋ / (BrE)
a tropical palm tree native to Southeast Asia, valued for its strong wood and sap

Examples

Strong nibong wood
The villagers built their houses using strong nibong poles.
A nibong tree can be found growing near the riverbank in many rural areas.
The sap from the nibong is sometimes collected and used in traditional recipes.
