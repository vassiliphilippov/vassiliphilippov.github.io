archangel

noun
a plant with edible stems and medicinal roots
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrktʃˌeɪndʒəl / (AmE), / ˈɑːkˌeɪndʒəl / (BrE)
A biennial herb (Angelica archangelica) cultivated for its edible stems, which are candied, and for its roots, which have medicinal uses.

Examples

Archangel plant stems
Farmers in northern Europe have cultivated archangel for centuries.
The candied stems of archangel are often used to decorate cakes and pastries.
Traditional remedies sometimes include the root of archangel for its supposed medicinal benefits.
