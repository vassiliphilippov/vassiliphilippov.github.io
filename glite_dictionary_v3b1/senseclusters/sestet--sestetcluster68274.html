sestet

noun
a group of six lines of verse
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɛsˈtɛt / (AmE), / sɛsˈtɛt / (BrE)
a group of six lines of verse, especially forming a unit in a poem (especially the last six lines of an Italian sonnet); also, any group of six things
