proletarian

noun
member of the working class
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊ.lɪˈtɛr.i.ən / (AmE), / ˌprəʊ.lɪˈteə.ri.ən / (BrE)
a member of the working class, especially one who does not own property and must sell their labor to survive

Examples

A proletarian worker
As a proud proletarian, he believed in fighting for workers' rights.
Many classic novels explore the struggles faced by the proletarian class.
The political party claimed to represent the interests of the proletarian majority.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bourgeois - a member of the middle class, especially relating to materialistic values or conventional attitudes
  • synonym: worker - a person who works, especially one engaged in manual or industrial labor
