latently

adverb
in a hidden way
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪtəntli / (AmE), / ˈleɪtəntli / (BrE)
in a way that exists but is not yet active, developed, or visible

Examples

To latently exist
The virus can remain latently present in the body for many years without causing symptoms.
Her talent for painting was latently waiting to be discovered until she took her first art class.
Some problems may exist latently before they become obvious to everyone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: openly - in a way that is not hidden or secret
  • synonym: secretly - in a way that is hidden from others
