ill-fitting

adjective
not fitting properly
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪlˈfɪt̬ɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪlˈfɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
not fitting properly; not the right size or shape for someone or something

Examples

An ill-fitting lid
An ill-fitting lid can cause the container to leak.
He wore an ill-fitting suit to the interview, which made him look uncomfortable.
The ill-fitting shoes gave her blisters after only an hour of walking.

Related Terms

  • antonym: well-fitting - fitting properly or appropriately
