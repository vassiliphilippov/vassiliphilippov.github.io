townscape

noun
view of a town
Pronunciation: / ˈtaʊnˌskeɪp /; / ˈtaʊnskeɪp / (AmE), / ˈtaʊnˌskeɪp /; / ˈtaʊnskeɪp / (BrE)
the visual appearance or a view of a town, including its buildings, streets, and open spaces (in reality or as shown in art or photos)

Examples

A townscape view
The artist painted a beautiful townscape featuring the old church and cobblestone streets.
Photographers often capture the unique townscape of small European villages.
From the hilltop, we admired the evening lights illuminating the entire townscape.
The historic buildings give the townscape a unique and charming character.
Modern developments have changed the townscape significantly over the past decade.
Artists often paint the townscape to capture the atmosphere of the place.
