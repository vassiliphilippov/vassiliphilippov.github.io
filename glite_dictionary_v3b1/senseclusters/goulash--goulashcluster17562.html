goulash

noun
a paprika-flavoured meat stew
Pronunciation: / ˈɡuːlɑʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡuːlæʃ / (BrE)
a rich stew typically made with meat, vegetables, and flavoured with paprika, originating from Hungary

Examples

Hungarian goulash stew
Hungarian goulash is famous for its spicy kick and hearty texture.
She served a bowl of steaming goulash for dinner on a cold winter night.
The restaurant’s special goulash draws food lovers from all over the city.
