mandoline

noun
stringed musical instrument
Pronunciation: / ˌmændəˈliːn / (AmE), / ˌmændəˈliːn / (BrE)
a small stringed musical instrument with a rounded or pear-shaped body, usually plucked with a plectrum, similar to a mandolin

Examples

Play the mandoline
She played the mandoline at the folk festival, filling the air with lively music.
The musician carefully tuned his mandoline before the performance began.
A beautiful mandoline was displayed in the window of the antique shop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guitar - a larger stringed instrument played by plucking or strumming
  • synonym: mandolin - a similar small stringed instrument, often with a flat back
