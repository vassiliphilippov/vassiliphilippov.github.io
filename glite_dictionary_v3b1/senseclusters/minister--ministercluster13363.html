minister

verb
perform duties of a religious minister
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnɪstər / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪstə / (BrE)
to perform the duties of a religious minister, especially by providing spiritual care or leadership

Examples

To minister in church
He decided to minister in a remote village after completing his seminary studies.
She has ministered to the needs of her congregation for many years.
The priest spent his weekends ministering to the sick and elderly.
