unglamorous

adjective
not attractive or exciting
5.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡlæmərəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡlæmərəs / (BrE)
not attractive, exciting, or interesting; dull or lacking in prestige

Examples

An unglamorous job
Despite the unglamorous nature of cleaning, it is essential to public health.

Working overnight shifts at the warehouse is an unglamorous but necessary job.
Cleaning the warehouse is a very unglamourous part of the job.
Many essential but unglamourous jobs are overlooked by the public.
She accepted the unglamourous task of organizing files for the office.
