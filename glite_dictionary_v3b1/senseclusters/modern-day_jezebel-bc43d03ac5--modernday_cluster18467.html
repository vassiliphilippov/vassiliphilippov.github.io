modern-day Jezebel

noun
promiscuous or manipulative woman
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑdərn deɪ ˈdʒɛzəbəl / (AmE), / ˈmɒdən deɪ ˈdʒɛzəbəl / (BrE)
A woman regarded as being promiscuous or manipulative, likened to the biblical Jezebel.

Examples

A modern-day Jezebel woman
She was unfairly called a modern-day Jezebel because she defied social norms.
Some critics labeled the character a modern-day Jezebel for her daring lifestyle.
The tabloids portrayed her as a modern-day Jezebel, though she insisted her actions were misunderstood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Jezebel - an impudent, shameless, or morally unrestrained woman (from the Bible)
  • antonym: saint - a woman of exceptional holiness or virtue
  • synonym: seductress - a woman who seduces someone, often by using her sexuality
