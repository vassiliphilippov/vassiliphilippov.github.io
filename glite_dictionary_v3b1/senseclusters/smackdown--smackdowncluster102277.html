smackdown

verb
to defeat decisively
Pronunciation: / ˈsmækˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈsmækˌdaʊn / (BrE)
to defeat someone decisively or to severely humiliate them in a contest, argument, or public exchange

Examples

To smackdown a rival
The debate team managed to smackdown their opponents with strong arguments and quick responses.
Fans cheered as their favorite player smackdown the competition in the final round.
She was determined not to let her rival smackdown her in front of the whole class.
