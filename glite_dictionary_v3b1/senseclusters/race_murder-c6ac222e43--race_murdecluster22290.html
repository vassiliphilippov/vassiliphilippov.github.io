race murder

noun
systematic killing of a racial group
Pronunciation: / reɪs ˈmɜrdər / (AmE), / reɪs ˈmɜːdə / (BrE)
the systematic killing of a racial or cultural group, especially with the intention to eradicate them

Examples

A race murder crime
Acts of race murder are universally condemned as crimes against humanity.
Scholars have studied cases of race murder in twentieth-century history.
The term race murder was used to describe atrocities committed during the conflict.
