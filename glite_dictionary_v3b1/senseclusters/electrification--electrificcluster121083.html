electrification

noun
the process of supplying electricity
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktrəfəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of providing a region, building, or system with electric power

Examples

Railway electrification
Major investments in electrification are needed to modernize the country's railways.
The electrification of rural areas transformed the quality of life for many communities.
The government's plan aims to speed up the electrification of public transportation systems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electrify - to supply with electricity
