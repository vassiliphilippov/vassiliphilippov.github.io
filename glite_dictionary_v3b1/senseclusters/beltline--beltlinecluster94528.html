beltline

noun
vehicle body line
6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbelt.laɪn/ (AmE), /ˈbelt.laɪn/ (BrE)
the horizontal line on a vehicle's body where the windows meet the lower panels, separating the upper and lower parts of the bodywork

Examples

Car beltline
The designer raised the beltline to give the car a sportier appearance.
A lower beltline can make the interior feel more spacious and open.
The new model features a chrome strip along the beltline for added style.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shoulder line - the contour line running along the side of a vehicle, often above the beltline
