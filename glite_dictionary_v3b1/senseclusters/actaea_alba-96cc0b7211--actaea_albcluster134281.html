Actaea alba

noun
North American herb with white poisonous berries
Pronunciation: / ækˈtiːə ˈælbə / (AmE), / ækˈtiːə ˈælbə / (BrE)
A North American plant species with white, poisonous berries, also known as white baneberry or doll's eyes.

The white, eye-like berries of Actaea alba make it easy to identify in the forest.
