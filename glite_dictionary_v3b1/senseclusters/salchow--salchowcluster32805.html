salchow

noun
figure skating jump
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsælkaʊ / (AmE), / ˈsælkəʊ / (BrE)
a figure skating jump in which the skater takes off from the back inside edge of one skate, spins in the air, and lands on the back outside edge of the other skate

Examples

A perfect salchow jump
The crowd cheered as she landed a flawless salchow in the middle of her routine.
He practiced the salchow for months before finally mastering the jump.
A double salchow is often included in competitive figure skating programs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: axel - a figure skating jump with a forward takeoff and one and a half turns
  • synonym: toe loop - a figure skating jump that starts from the back outside edge with assistance from the toe pick
