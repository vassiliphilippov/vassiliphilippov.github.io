encyclical letter

noun
official papal letter to bishops
9.5 8.7
Pronunciation: /en.ˈsɪ.klɪ.kəl ˈlet.ər/ (AmE), /en.ˈsɪ.klɪ.kəl ˈlet.ə/ (BrE)
An official letter sent by the pope to all Roman Catholic bishops around the world, usually addressing important Church doctrine or practice.

Examples

The encyclical letter from pope
Many theologians study the moral teachings found in each encyclical letter from the Vatican.
The most recent encyclical letter addressed the environmental responsibilities of all Catholics.
The pope issued an encyclical letter calling for peace and social justice.
