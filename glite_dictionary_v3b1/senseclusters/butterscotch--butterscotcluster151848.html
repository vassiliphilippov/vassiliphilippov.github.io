butterscotch

noun
hard candy made with butter and brown sugar
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌt̬.ɚˌskɑtʃ / (AmE), / ˈbʌt.əˌskɒtʃ / (BrE)
A sweet, hard, brittle candy made from butter and brown sugar.

Examples

A piece of butterscotch
He loved the rich flavor of butterscotch and always kept a few pieces in his pocket.
She handed me a butterscotch from the candy jar as a treat.
The bakery sells homemade butterscotch in decorative tins during the holidays.
