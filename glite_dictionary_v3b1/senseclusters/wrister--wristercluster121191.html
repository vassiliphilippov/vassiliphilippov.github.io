wrister

noun
ice hockey wrist shot
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪstər / (AmE), / ˈrɪstə / (BrE)
a type of shot in ice hockey made by quickly flicking the wrist to propel the puck

Examples

A quick wrister shot
The player scored with a quick wrister that surprised the goalie.
She practiced her wrister every day to improve her accuracy on the ice.
A well-placed wrister can be just as effective as a slapshot in a game.

Related Terms
