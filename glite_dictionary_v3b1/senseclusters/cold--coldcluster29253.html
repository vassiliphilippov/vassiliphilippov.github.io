cold

adjective
not warm in color; cool-toned
1.4 2.7
Pronunciation: / koʊld / (AmE), / kəʊld / (BrE)
(of a color or shade) having a quality that lacks warmth or suggests coolness; often associated with blues, greens, and similar tones.

Examples

Cold blue tones
Designers often recommend using cold hues in small spaces to make them feel larger.
Her abstract painting features a cold blue that dominates the entire canvas.
The room was painted in cold colors to create a sense of calm.
