Yas

proper noun
island in Abu Dhabi
Pronunciation: / jɑːs / (AmE), / jɑːs / (BrE)
a man-made island in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates, famous for its theme parks, hotels, and motor-racing circuit

Examples
