puppet leader

noun
leader controlled by another
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌp.ɪt ˈliː.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈpʌp.ɪt ˈliː.də / (BrE)
a leader or ruler who has the outward appearance of authority but is actually controlled by another, typically more powerful, person or group

Examples

A puppet leader controlled
After the coup, the new president was widely seen as a puppet leader for the military regime.
Many citizens distrusted their puppet leader, believing real decisions came from foreign officials.
The invading power set up a puppet leader to give an illusion of local governance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: figurehead - a person with a nominal leadership role but no real power
