unsmudge

verb
to remove smudges
7.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsmʌdʒ / (AmE), / ʌnˈsmʌdʒ / (BrE)
to remove smudges or smears from a surface, making it clean or clear again

Examples

To unsmudge a photo
She carefully unsmudged the photograph with a soft cloth before framing it.
He tried to unsmudge the window so the view would be clearer.
After spilling ink, I unsmudged my hands with soap and water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clean - to make something free from dirt or marks
  • antonym: smudge - to make something dirty or blurry with a smear
