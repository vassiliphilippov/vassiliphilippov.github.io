ferritin

noun
iron-storing protein in the body
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛr.ɪ.tɪn / (AmE), / ˈfɛr.ɪ.tɪn / (BrE)
a protein that stores and releases iron in the body, mainly found in the liver, spleen, and intestines

Examples

Ferritin protein level
Ferritin is stored mainly in the liver and released as the body needs iron.
Low ferritin may indicate iron deficiency even when hemoglobin is normal.
The doctor ordered a blood test to check her ferritin levels.
