Parian

noun
unglazed porcelain
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛəriən / (AmE), / ˈpeəriən / (BrE)
a fine, unglazed porcelain made to imitate Parian marble, especially used for figurines in the 19th century

Examples

Parian porcelain figurine
The antique shop had a beautiful Parian bust displayed in the window.
Collectors often seek out rare Parian figurines from the Victorian period.
She inherited a delicate Parian statue that had belonged to her grandmother.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bisque - unglazed porcelain with a matte finish
  • synonym: marble - a hard, white or colored stone used in sculpture and architecture
