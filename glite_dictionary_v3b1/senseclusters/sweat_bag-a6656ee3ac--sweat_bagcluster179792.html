sweat bag

noun
porous bag cooling water by evaporation
7.8
Pronunciation: / swɛt bæg / (AmE), / swɛt bæɡ / (BrE)
a porous bag, usually made of canvas, used to hold water and cool it by evaporation

Examples

A sweat bag hangs
A sweat bag hung from the tree branch, dripping moisture as it cooled the contents inside.
Early travelers often relied on a sweat bag to prevent their drinking water from becoming too warm.
The explorer filled his sweat bag with water to keep it cool during the crossing.
