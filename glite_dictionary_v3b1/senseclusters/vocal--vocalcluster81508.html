vocal

adjective
having the ability to produce speech or sound
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈvoʊkəl / (AmE), / ˈvəʊkəl / (BrE)
having or using the ability to produce speech or sound, especially with the voice

Examples

Vocal animals
All vocal creatures communicate in some way, whether by song or speech.
Birds use their vocal organs to produce a variety of sounds.
The patient retained his vocal abilities after the surgery.
