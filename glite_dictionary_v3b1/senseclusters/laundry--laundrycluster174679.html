laundry

noun
place where clothes are washed and ironed
1.0 3.13/m
Pronunciation: / Ààl…înd…πi / (AmE), / Ààl…îÀêndri / (BrE)
a place or business where clothes, linens, and other textiles are washed and ironed

Examples

A hotel laundry
Many hospitals have an on-site laundry to handle their large amount of linens.
She took her shirts to the local laundry to have them washed and pressed.
The hotel offers a laundry service for guests who need their clothes cleaned.

Synonyms

  • washhouse
    8.1 0.01/m
    a washhouse is an older building used for washing clothes, while a laundry is a business that washes and often irons garments.
  • launderette
    3.5 0.06/m
    a launderette has coin-operated machines where customers wash and dry their own clothes, while a laundry usually offers staff washing and ironing services.
  • Laundromat
    3.2 1.69/m
    a Laundromat is a self-service, coin- or card-operated laundry common in the US, while a laundry often offers full-service washing and ironing.
