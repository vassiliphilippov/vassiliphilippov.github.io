Synonyms

  • banker's draft
    5.7 0.01/m
    a banker's draft is a bank-guaranteed cheque often drawn on another bank, while a cashier's check is paid from the issuing bank's own funds.
  • treasurer's check
    7.2 0.00/m
    a treasurer's check is drawn by a bank officer on the bank's funds, while a cashier's check is issued by the bank to guarantee payment.
  • banker's check
    4.1 0.00/m
    a banker's check is payable on demand and seen as more secure than a personal cheque, while a cashier's check guarantees payment to the payee.
