cashier's check

noun
bank-issued guaranteed check
3.9 3.7
Pronunciation: /kæ.ˈʃɪrz tʃek/ /; / /kə.ˈʃɪrz tʃek/ (AmE), /kæ.ˈʃɪəz tʃek/ /; / /kə.ˈʃɪəz tʃek/ (BrE)
a check issued by a bank on its own account rather than that of a customer, guaranteeing payment to the payee

Examples

A cashier's check
Some landlords require a cashier's check for the security deposit instead of a personal check.
The lawyer requested payment by cashier's check to ensure the funds would clear immediately.
To buy the car, he brought a cashier's check from his bank as the safest method of payment.
A cashier's cheque is generally considered more secure than a personal cheque.
She paid for the new car with a cashier's cheque issued by her bank.
Some landlords require a cashier's cheque for the first month's rent and security deposit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bank draft - an order to pay money drawn by one bank on another
  • synonym: certified check - a check guaranteed by a bank; the bank verifies the funds and sets them aside
