smooth-bodied

adjective
having a smooth body or surface
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsmuð ˌbɑdɪd / (AmE), / ˈsmuːð ˌbɒdɪd / (BrE)
having a body or surface that is smooth to the touch, lacking roughness, projections, or noticeable texture.

Examples

smooth-bodied fish
Some fish are smooth-bodied, making them difficult to hold.
The smooth-bodied snake glided effortlessly across the rocks.
The scientist observed a smooth-bodied insect under the microscope.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rough-bodied - having a rough or coarse body or surface
  • synonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface
