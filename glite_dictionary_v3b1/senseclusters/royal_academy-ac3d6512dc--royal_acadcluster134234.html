Royal Academy

proper noun
British arts institution in London
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔɪəl əˈkædəmi / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪəl əˈkadəmi / (BrE)
An honorary institution in London, founded in 1768, dedicated to the promotion of the arts of painting, sculpture, and architecture in Britain.
