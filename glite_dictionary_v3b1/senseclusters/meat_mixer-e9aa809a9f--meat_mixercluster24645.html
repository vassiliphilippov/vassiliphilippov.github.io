meat mixer

noun
utensil for mixing meat, esp. for sausages
4.3 2.7
Pronunciation: /miːt ˈmɪks.ər/ (AmE), /miːt ˈmɪks.ə/ (BrE)
a kitchen or industrial utensil with a steel tank and paddles for mixing meat, especially when preparing sausages

Examples

A meat mixer machine
After grinding the beef, she added spices and used a meat mixer to combine everything thoroughly.
Most commercial kitchens use a meat mixer when making large batches of sausage.
The butcher placed the seasoned pork in the meat mixer to blend it evenly.
