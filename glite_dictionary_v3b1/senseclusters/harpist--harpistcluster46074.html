harpist

noun
person who plays the harp
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrpɪst / (AmE), / ˈhɑːpɪst / (BrE)
a person who plays the harp, especially professionally or as a serious hobby

Examples

A harpist plays harp
At the concert, a local harpist introduced children to the instrument.
She is a talented harpist who regularly plays with the city orchestra.
The harpist performed a beautiful solo during the wedding ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: musician - a person who plays a musical instrument or is musically talented
