mare

noun
dark region on the moon's surface
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛəɹi / (AmE), / ˈmɑːreɪ / (BrE)
a large, dark, basaltic plain on the surface of the moon, formed by ancient volcanic eruptions

Examples

Lunar mare
A telescope reveals the detailed boundaries of the mare across the Moon's surface.
Scientists studied the composition of the lunar mare to learn about the Moon's volcanic history.
The astronauts landed near a mare visible from Earth with the naked eye.
