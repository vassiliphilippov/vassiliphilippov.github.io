medical care

noun
professional treatment for illness or injury
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdɪkəl kɛr / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl keə / (BrE)
the professional treatment and management of illness, injury, or health conditions by trained medical personnel

Examples

Medical care services
After her accident, she received immediate medical care at the nearest hospital.
All citizens have the right to receive medical care regardless of their income.
Many rural communities lack access to basic medical care.
