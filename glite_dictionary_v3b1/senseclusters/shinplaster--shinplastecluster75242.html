shinplaster

noun
low-value, insecure paper money
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪnˌplæstər / (AmE), / ˈʃɪnˌplɑːstə / (BrE)
A piece of paper money of little value, especially one issued with insufficient security; commonly used in the 19th century.
