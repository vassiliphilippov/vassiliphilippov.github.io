central vein of suprarenal gland

noun
main draining vein of the adrenal gland
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntrəl veɪn əv ˌsuːprəˈriːnəl ɡlænd / (AmE), / ˈsɛntrəl veɪn əv ˌsjuːprəˈriːnəl ɡlænd / (BrE)
the main vein that drains blood from the adrenal (suprarenal) gland, emptying into the inferior vena cava on the right and into the left renal vein on the left

Examples

The central vein of suprarenal gland
During surgery, the central vein of suprarenal gland must be carefully identified to control bleeding.
In the dissection, the left central vein of suprarenal gland was found joining the left renal vein.
The right central vein of suprarenal gland drains directly into the inferior vena cava.
