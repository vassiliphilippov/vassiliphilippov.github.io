faraway

adjective
distant in thought
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑr.əˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈfɑː.rə.weɪ / (BrE)
looking as if thinking about something distant or lost in thought; not present mentally

Examples

Faraway look
During the meeting, her thoughts became faraway and she hardly heard what was said.
His expression was faraway, lost in memories of years past.
She had a faraway look in her eyes as she gazed out the window.
