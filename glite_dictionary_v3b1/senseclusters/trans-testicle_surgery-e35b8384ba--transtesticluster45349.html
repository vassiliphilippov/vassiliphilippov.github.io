trans-testicle surgery

noun
gender-affirming testicular surgery
Pronunciation: / trænz ˈtɛstɪkəl ˈsɝːdʒəri / (AmE), / tranz ˈtɛstɪkəl ˈsɜːdʒəri / (BrE)
