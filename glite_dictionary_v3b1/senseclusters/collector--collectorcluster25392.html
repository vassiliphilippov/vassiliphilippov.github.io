collector

noun
person who collects things
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɛktər / (AmE), / kəˈlɛktə / (BrE)
a person who accumulates or gathers particular items, often as a hobby or for study

Examples

Stamp collector
My grandfather is an avid collector of rare stamps from around the world.
She became known as a collector of vintage jewelry after years of searching flea markets.
The museum hired a collector to acquire antique coins for its upcoming exhibition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accumulator - a person who gathers things together
