forever war

noun
prolonged, seemingly endless conflict
Pronunciation: / fəˈrɛvɚ wɔr / (AmE), / fəˈrɛvə wɔː / (BrE)
a war or conflict that continues for a very long time with no apparent end or resolution

Examples

A forever war
Living through a forever war can deeply affect an entire generation.
Many historians debate the beginning and end of the forever war in the region.
The country was exhausted by the forever war that dragged on for decades.
