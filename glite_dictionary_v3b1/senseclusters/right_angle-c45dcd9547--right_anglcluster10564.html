right angle

noun
an angle of 90 degrees
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪt ˌæŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈraɪt ˌæŋɡl̩ / (BrE)
an angle of exactly 90 degrees, formed where two lines are perpendicular to each other

Examples

A right angle corner
He bent the wire into a perfect right angle using a special tool.
The two walls met at a right angle in the corner of the room.
When drawing a rectangle, each corner forms a right angle.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: acute angle - an angle less than 90 degrees
  • degree higher: obtuse angle - an angle greater than 90 degrees but less than 180 degrees
