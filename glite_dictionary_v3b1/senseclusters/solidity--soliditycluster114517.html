solidity

noun
state or quality of being solid
2.0 4.0
Pronunciation: / səˈlɪdədi /; / səˈlɪdəti / (AmE), / səˈlɪdəti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being solid, firm, or not hollow (physically strong or dense)

Examples

Physical solidity
Her sculpture impressed everyone with the solidity and strength of its materials.

The solidity of the marble block made it perfect for sculpture.
Unlike the hollow statue, the new one had solidity throughout.
