whoa

interjection
surprise or stop
Pronunciation: / woʊ / (AmE), / wəʊ / (BrE)
used to express surprise, amazement, or to tell someone or something to stop suddenly

Examples

Whoa! That's amazing
When the car came around the corner too fast, she yelled whoa!
He opened the gift and said whoa in amazement at what he saw.
Whoa, slow down and explain that again so I can understand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stop - to cease movement or action
  • synonym: wow - used to express amazement or admiration
