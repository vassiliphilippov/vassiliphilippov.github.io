borecole

noun
a type of curly-leaved cabbage
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔrˌkoʊl / (AmE), / ˈbɔːkəʊl / (BrE)
A type of cabbage with tough, curly leaves that do not form a dense head, also known as kale.

Examples

Fresh borecole leaves
Borecole thrives in cold weather and is harvested well into winter.
The farmer grows several varieties of borecole in his garden each year.
We added chopped borecole to the soup for extra flavor and nutrition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kale - a hardy cabbage with curly leaves, often used synonymously with borecole
