Tesla coil

noun
high-voltage, high-frequency transformer
6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtes.lə kɔɪl/ /; / /ˈtez.lə kɔɪl/ (AmE), /ˈtes.lə kɔɪl/ (BrE)
an electrical device consisting of a step-up transformer with an air core, used to produce high voltages at high frequencies

Examples

A Tesla coil device
He built a small Tesla coil for his science fair project.
Modern researchers still use the Tesla coil to study the properties of high-frequency electricity.
The Tesla coil can generate impressive sparks during public demonstrations.
