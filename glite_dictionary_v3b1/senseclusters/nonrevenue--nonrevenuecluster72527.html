nonrevenue

noun
unpaid service trip
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈrɛvəˌnu / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈrɛvəˌnjuː / (BrE)
a trip, vehicle, or passenger movement that does not generate income because it does not carry paying customers

Examples

Nonrevenue trip
The bus company scheduled a nonrevenue trip to move vehicles back to the depot.
Airlines often operate nonrevenue flights to reposition aircraft between airports.
Crew members sometimes travel on nonrevenue journeys when they are not working a paid flight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deadhead - a trip made by a vehicle without passengers or cargo for pay
  • antonym: revenue - income from business activities or services
