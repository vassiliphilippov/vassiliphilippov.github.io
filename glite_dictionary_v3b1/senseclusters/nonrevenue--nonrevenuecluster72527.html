nonrevenue

noun
unpaid service trip

Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈrev.ə.njuː/ /; / /nən.ˈrev.ə.njuː/ /; / /nɑːn.ˈrev.ə.nuː/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈrev.ə.njuː/ /; / /nən.ˈrev.ə.njuː/ /; / /nɒn.ˈrev.ə.nuː/ (BrE)
a trip, vehicle, or passenger movement that does not generate income because it does not carry paying customers

Crew members sometimes travel on nonrevenue journeys when they are not working a paid flight.
