Asclepias curassavica

noun
tropical milkweed plant species
Pronunciation: / əˈskliːpiəs ˌkjʊrəˈsævɪkə / (AmE), / əˈskliːpɪas ˌkjʊərəˈsavɪkə / (BrE)
a tropical herbaceous plant with orange-red flowers and long seed pods, commonly known as tropical milkweed; often found as a weed in the tropics

Examples

Asclepias curassavica plant
Asclepias curassavica is popular with gardeners for its bright flowers that attract butterflies.
In many regions, Asclepias curassavica is considered an invasive weed.
The long seed pods of Asclepias curassavica resemble the wings of a swallow.
