mantel shelf

noun
fireplace shelf
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmæntəl ʃɛlf / (AmE), / ˈmæntl ʃɛlf / (BrE)
a shelf above a fireplace, often used for displaying decorative objects or photographs

Examples

The mantel shelf
She arranged candles and vases on the mantel shelf to decorate the living room.
The clock on the mantel shelf chimed every hour, filling the house with gentle sounds.
Family photos lined the mantel shelf above the fireplace, creating a warm atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fireplace - an open space in a wall for a fire
  • synonym: mantelpiece - the structure or shelf above a fireplace
