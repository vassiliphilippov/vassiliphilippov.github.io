wheelchair

noun
a chair with wheels for people who cannot walk
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːl.tʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈwiːl.tʃeə / (BrE)
a chair fitted with wheels, used by individuals who are unable to walk or have difficulty walking, often propelled by the user or someone else

Examples

A wheelchair user
He pushed his grandfather's wheelchair through the park on a sunny afternoon.
She relied on a wheelchair after her surgery to get around the hospital.
The museum is easily accessible to visitors in a wheelchair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stretcher - a portable frame for carrying injured or ill people
