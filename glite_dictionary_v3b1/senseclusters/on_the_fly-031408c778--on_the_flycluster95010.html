on the fly

adverb
dynamically at runtime
4.6
Pronunciation: /ɑːn ðə flaɪ/ /; / /ɑːn ðiː flaɪ/ (AmE), /ɒn ðə flaɪ/ /; / /ɒn ðiː flaɪ/ (BrE)
(computing) performed, generated, or processed dynamically during execution (at runtime) rather than being precomputed or stored in advance.

Examples

Create files on the fly
The server generates thumbnails on the fly when a user uploads a large image.
Our video player transcodes streams on the fly to match slower network connections.
You can create reports on the fly from the live database without stopping the service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: at runtime - during the execution of a program or process
  • synonym: dynamically - happening or produced as something runs or changes, not fixed beforehand
  • synonym: in real time - occurring immediately as events happen, with little or no delay
