Lucius Domitius Ahenobarbus

proper noun
birth name of Nero, Roman Emperor (37–68 AD)
Pronunciation: / ˈluːʃi.əs doʊˈmɪʃi.əs æˌhɛnəˈbɑːrbəs / (AmE), / ˈluːsi.əs dəʊˈmɪti.əs əˌhɛnəˈbɑːbəs / (BrE)
The birth name of Nero, Roman Emperor from 54 to 68 AD, notorious for his extravagance and alleged cruelty.
