trifling

adjective
of little value or importance
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪflɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtraɪflɪŋ / (BrE)
of little value or importance; insignificant or trivial

Examples

A trifling matter
He dismissed her concerns as a trifling issue that did not deserve attention.
The amount of money involved was so trifling that he didn't bother to record it.
They spent hours debating trifling details instead of addressing the main problem.

Related Terms

  • antonym: important - of great significance or value
  • synonym: insignificant - too small or unimportant to be worth considering
