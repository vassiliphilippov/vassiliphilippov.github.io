intranatal

adjective
during birth
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈneɪtəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈneɪtəl / (BrE)
occurring or existing during the process of birth

Examples

The intranatal period
The nurse carefully recorded all intranatal events during the delivery.
Certain complications can arise from intranatal factors affecting the newborn.
Continuous monitoring of intranatal conditions is essential for a safe childbirth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perinatal - relating to the time shortly before and after birth
  • antonym: postnatal - occurring after birth
  • antonym: prenatal - occurring before birth
