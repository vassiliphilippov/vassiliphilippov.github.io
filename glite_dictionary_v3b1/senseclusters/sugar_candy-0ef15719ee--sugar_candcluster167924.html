sugar candy

noun
sweet made by boiling pure sugar
Pronunciation: / ˈsʊɡɚ ˈkændi / (AmE), / ˈsʊɡə ˈkændi / (BrE)
A sweet food made by boiling pure sugar and allowing it to harden, often forming clear, crystalline pieces.

Examples

A sugar candy piece
Children love eating sugar candy at fairs and festivals.
He made homemade sugar candy with just sugar and water.
The shop sells brightly colored sugar candy in glass jars.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rock candy - crystalline candy made from concentrated sugar solution
