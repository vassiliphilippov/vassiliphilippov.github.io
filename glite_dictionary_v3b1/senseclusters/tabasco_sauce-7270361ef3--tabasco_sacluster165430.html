Tabasco sauce

noun
a brand of hot, pepper-based sauce
2.8
Pronunciation: / təˈbæsku sɔs / (AmE), / təˈbæskəʊ sɔːs / (BrE)
a very spicy sauce made from fully-aged red peppers and vinegar, known for its distinctive heat and flavor

Examples

A dash of Tabasco sauce
A dash of Tabasco sauce gives the cocktail a spicy kick.
He always puts Tabasco sauce on his scrambled eggs to add extra heat.
They ran out of Tabasco sauce at the barbecue, so the food wasn't as spicy as usual.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hot sauce - a general term for spicy sauces made from chili peppers
