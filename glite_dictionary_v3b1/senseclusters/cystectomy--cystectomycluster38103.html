cystectomy

noun
bladder removal surgery
Pronunciation: / sɪsˈtɛktəmi / (AmE), / sɪsˈtɛktəmi / (BrE)
a surgical operation to remove all or part of the urinary bladder

Examples

Cystectomy for bladder cancer
The doctor explained that a cystectomy was necessary to treat the patient's bladder cancer.
After her cystectomy, she needed to adjust to a new way of urinating.
A cystectomy can be performed using traditional surgery or minimally invasive techniques.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: bladder - the organ that stores urine in the body
  • synonym: nephrectomy - surgical removal of a kidney
