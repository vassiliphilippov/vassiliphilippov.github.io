probing

adjective
searching and thorough inquiry or investigation
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈprəʊbɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a question, inquiry, or investigation) searching and thorough, aiming to uncover the truth or details.
