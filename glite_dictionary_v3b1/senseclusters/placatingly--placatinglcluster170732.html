placatingly

adverb
in a calming, pacifying way
9.9
Pronunciation: / pləˈkeɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / pləˈkeɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that is intended to calm or pacify someone who is angry or upset.

Examples

To smile placatingly
He smiled placatingly after realizing his comment had offended her.
She spoke placatingly to her upset child, hoping to ease his frustration.
The manager nodded placatingly, trying to calm the angry customer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: provocatively - in a manner intended to provoke
  • synonym: soothingly - in a soothing manner
