fowl pest

noun
acute viral disease of domestic fowl
Pronunciation: / faʊl pɛst / (AmE), / faʊl pɛst / (BrE)
Either of two acute viral diseases affecting domestic fowl, marked by loss of appetite, high fever, and discoloration of the comb.

Examples

Fowl pest disease
Early detection of fowl pest is crucial to control its spread among domestic birds.
The farmers lost several chickens to fowl pest during last year's outbreak.
Veterinarians advised strict biosecurity measures to prevent fowl pest on poultry farms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Newcastle disease - a contagious viral bird disease affecting many domestic and wild avian species
  • synonym: avian influenza - a viral infection affecting birds, also known as bird flu
