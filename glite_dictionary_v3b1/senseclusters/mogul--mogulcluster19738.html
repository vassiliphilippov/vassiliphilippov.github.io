mogul

noun
powerful businessperson
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊɡəl / (AmE), / ˈməʊɡəl / (BrE)
a very wealthy or powerful person in business or industry

Examples

A business mogul
Many people dream of becoming a real estate mogul, but few achieve that level of success.
She was considered a media mogul after acquiring several major news outlets.
The tech mogul announced the launch of a new startup in Silicon Valley.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tycoon - a wealthy, powerful person in business
