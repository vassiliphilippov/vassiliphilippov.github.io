keynoter

noun
main speaker
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːˌnoʊtər / (AmE), / ˈkiːˌnəʊtə / (BrE)
a person who delivers the main speech at an event or conference

Examples

Conference keynoter
The keynoter captivated the audience with a powerful message about innovation.
As the keynoter, he set the tone for the entire conference with his opening speech.
Many attendees were eager to hear the keynoter share her experiences in the industry.
