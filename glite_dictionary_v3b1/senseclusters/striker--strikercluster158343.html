striker

noun
attacking football player
Pronunciation: / ˈstraɪkər / (AmE), / ˈstraɪkə / (BrE)
a player whose main role is to score goals for their team in football (soccer)

Examples

Football striker
As the leading striker, she scored 25 goals last season.
The coach praised the striker for his decisive goal in the final match.
The team signed a new striker to improve their chances of scoring goals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forward - an attacking player in football or hockey
  • antonym: goalkeeper - the player who defends the goal in football
