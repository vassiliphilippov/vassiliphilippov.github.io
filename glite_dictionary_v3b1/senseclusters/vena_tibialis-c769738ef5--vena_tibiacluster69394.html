vena tibialis

noun
vein of the lower leg
9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈviː.nə tɪ.ˈbaɪ.ə.lɪs/ (AmE), /ˈviː.nə tɪ.ˈbaɪ.ə.lɪs/ (BrE)
A vein in the lower leg that drains blood from the leg and empties into the popliteal vein.

Examples

Vena tibialis vein
Damage to the vena tibialis can result in improper drainage of blood from the calf muscles.
During surgery, the surgeon carefully avoided the vena tibialis to prevent excessive bleeding.
The vena tibialis plays a crucial role in returning blood from the lower leg to the heart.
