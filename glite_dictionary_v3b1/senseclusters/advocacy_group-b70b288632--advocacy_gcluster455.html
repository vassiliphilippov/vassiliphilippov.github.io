advocacy group

noun
organization seeking to influence policy
6.0 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈæd.və.kə.siː gruːp/ /; / /ˈæd.və.kə.sɪ gruːp/ (AmE), /ˈæd.və.kə.sɪ gruːp/ /; / /ˈæd.və.kə.siː gruːp/ (BrE)
An organization that seeks to influence public policy or decisions by lobbying or campaigning for a particular cause or interest.

Examples
