An organization that seeks to influence public policy or decisions by lobbying or campaigning for a particular cause or interest.

Examples

Many advocacy groups campaign
Many advocacy groups submit reports to lawmakers to shape new healthcare policies.
She joined an advocacy group that supports education reform in urban areas.
The environmental advocacy group organized a campaign to protect local forests.

Synonyms

  • interest group
    3.1 6.28/m
    an interest group organizes people with shared aims to influence public policy through coordinated action, while an advocacy group focuses on campaigning for a cause.
  • pressure group
    4.1 0.64/m
    a pressure group uses strong tactics like lobbying or public protest to push for policies, while an advocacy group can rely more on campaigning and education.
  • lobby group
    3.9 0.14/m
    a lobby group focuses on persuading legislators and officials directly, while an advocacy group also uses public campaigning and broader awareness work.
