Namoi River

proper noun
river in southeastern Australia
Pronunciation: / ˈnæmɔɪ ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈnæmɔɪ ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in southeastern Australia that flows northwest to join the Darling River

Examples
