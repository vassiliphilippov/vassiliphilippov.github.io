phut

noun
soft popping sound
Pronunciation: / fʌt / (AmE), / fʌt / (BrE)
a soft, short popping or explosive sound, often made by a small object or device.

Examples

A quiet phut
The old engine gave a quiet phut before stopping completely.
With a gentle phut, the balloon burst in her hands.
He heard a faint phut as the cap popped off the bottle.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: bang - a sudden loud noise
  • synonym: pop - a short, sharp, explosive sound
