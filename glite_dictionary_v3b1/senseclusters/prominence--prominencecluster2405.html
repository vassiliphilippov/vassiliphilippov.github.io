prominence

noun
a part that juts out; a projection
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑː.mə.nəns / (AmE), / ˈprɒm.ɪ.nəns / (BrE)
a part that sticks out or protrudes from its surroundings, such as a hill or anatomical bulge

Examples

A bony prominence
A slight prominence could be felt on the surface of the bone.
From the valley, the prominence of the hill was clearly visible against the skyline.
The explorer climbed to the top of the rocky prominence to survey the landscape.

Related Terms

  • synonym: projection - something that juts out from a surface or structure
  • synonym: protuberance - a thing that protrudes from something else
