deplorable

adjective
deserving strong disapproval
Pronunciation: / dɪˈplɔrəbəl /; / dɪˈplɔːrəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈplɔːrəbəl / (BrE)
shockingly bad, unacceptable, or deserving strong condemnation

Examples

Deplorable behavior
His deplorable behavior at the meeting led to widespread criticism from his colleagues.
It is deplorable that so many children go to bed hungry every night.
The living conditions in the camp were absolutely deplorable and shocked the inspectors.
His behavior at the meeting was utterly deplorable and shocked everyone present.
The building was found to be in a deplorable state of disrepair.
The refugees were living in deplorable conditions with little food or water.
Many felt the company's handling of the crisis was simply deplorable.
The refugees were forced to live in deplorable conditions for months.
The school board apologized for the deplorable state of the gymnasium.
