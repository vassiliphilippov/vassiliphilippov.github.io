faggot

verb
join fabric by decorative stitch
Pronunciation: / ˈfæɡət / (AmE), / ˈfæɡət / (BrE)
to ornament or join pieces of fabric together using a decorative openwork stitch known as faggoting

Examples

To faggot fabric edges
After learning the technique, he faggoted a scarf to match the handmade blouse.
She carefully faggoted the edges of the dress to create a delicate pattern.
The blouse was expertly faggoted at the seams, giving it an elegant finish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: faggoting - the process or result of joining fabric with an openwork stitch
