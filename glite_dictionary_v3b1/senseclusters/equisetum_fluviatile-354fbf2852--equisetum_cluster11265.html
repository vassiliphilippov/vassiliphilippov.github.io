Equisetum fluviatile

noun
species of horsetail plant
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkwɪˈsiːtəm fluːˈvaɪ.ətɪl / (AmE), / ˌɛkwɪˈsiːtəm fluːˈvɪətʌɪl / (BrE)
A species of horsetail plant commonly found in wetlands across Eurasia and northern North America, also known as water horsetail.

Examples

Equisetum fluviatile plant
Equisetum fluviatile thrives in shallow ponds and marshes throughout northern Europe.
Botanists studied the growth patterns of Equisetum fluviatile in Canadian wetlands.
The stems of Equisetum fluviatile are hollow and easily recognized by their segmented appearance.
