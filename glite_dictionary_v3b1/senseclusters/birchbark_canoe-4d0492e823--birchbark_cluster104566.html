birchbark canoe

noun
canoe made with birch bark
7.0 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈbɜːrtʃ.bɑːrk kə.ˈnuː/ /; / /ˈbɜːrtʃ.bɑːrk kən.ˈuː/ (AmE), /ˈbɜːtʃ.bɑːk kə.ˈnuː/ (BrE)
