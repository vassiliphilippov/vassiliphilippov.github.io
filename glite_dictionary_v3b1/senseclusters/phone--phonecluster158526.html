phone

noun
device for transmitting sound over distances
1.0
Pronunciation: / foʊn / (AmE), / fəʊn / (BrE)
A device that converts sound into electrical signals for transmission over distances, then reconverts received signals back into sounds.

Examples

A mobile phone
His phone rang just as he was about to leave for work.
She left her phone on the kitchen counter and missed three important calls.
You can reach me on my phone if anything urgent comes up.

Related Terms

  • synonym: telephone - a device for transmitting voices over a distance using wires or radio
