Synonyms

  • inhabitant
    2.8 14/m
    inhabitant is a person or animal living in a place long-term, while resident is a general term for someone who lives there.
  • dweller
    3.2 5.31/m
    dweller is someone who lives in a particular place and often appears with location names, while resident means someone who lives there.
  • occupier
    4.1 0.26/m
    occupier is a person who holds or lives in a property, often in a legal way, while resident means someone living there.
  • occupant
    3.3 6.27/m
    occupant is a person who uses or stays in a space for a period, while resident lives there as their home.
