workingman

noun
male manual or industrial laborer
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrkɪŋˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkɪŋˌmæn / (BrE)
a man who is employed to do manual or industrial labor, especially as opposed to managerial or professional work

Examples

A workingman in factory
As a lifelong workingman, he understood the struggles of manual labor.
The factory owner promised better conditions for every workingman on the payroll.
The union fights for the rights of the average workingman in the industry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: worker - person who works, especially one engaged in physical labor
  • antonym: workingwoman - a female who performs manual or industrial labor
