periapical

adjective
around tooth root
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛriˈæpɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌpɛriˈæpɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the area surrounding the tip of the root of a tooth

Examples

Periapical X-ray
The dentist ordered a periapical X-ray to check for infection.
A periapical abscess can cause severe pain and swelling in the jaw.
Treatment of periapical lesions often requires root canal therapy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apical - relating to the tip or apex of a structure
