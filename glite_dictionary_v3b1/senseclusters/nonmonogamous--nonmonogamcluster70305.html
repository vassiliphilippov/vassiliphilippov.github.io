nonmonogamous

adjective
having multiple partners
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnməˈnɑɡəməs / (AmE), / ˌnɒnməˈnɒɡəməs / (BrE)
having more than one romantic or sexual partner at the same time; not limited to one partner

Examples

Nonmonogamous relationships
Some animal species are nonmonogamous and form bonds with several mates during a breeding season.
A nonmonogamous relationship allows partners to date or be involved with others openly.
Researchers observed that the birds' nonmonogamous behavior increased their chances of reproductive success.
They agreed to have a nonmonogamous relationship and date other people openly.
Some people prefer a nonmonogamous lifestyle because it allows for more freedom.
Being nonmonogamous requires clear communication and trust between all partners.

  • synonym: polyamorous - having romantic relationships with more than one person at the same time, with consent
  • synonym: polygamous - having more than one spouse or mate at the same time
