unprotected

adjective
lacking protection or defense
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.prəˈtɛk.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.prəˈtɛk.tɪd / (BrE)
Not having protection or defense against harm or danger.

Examples

Unprotected data
Sensitive data stored without encryption is extremely unprotected from cyberattacks.
She felt unprotected walking alone at night in an unfamiliar city.
The small animals were left unprotected during the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defenseless - lacking the means to protect oneself
  • antonym: protected - having protection against harm or danger
