exclamation mark

noun
punctuation mark indicating strong feeling
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkskləˈmeɪʃən mɑrk / (AmE), / ˌɛkskləˈmeɪʃən mɑːk / (BrE)
the punctuation mark (!) used at the end of a sentence to indicate strong feeling or a loud command

Examples

An exclamation mark symbol
Don't forget to add an exclamation mark at the end of that sentence.
She used an exclamation mark to show her surprise.
The teacher explained when to use an exclamation mark in writing.
