prio

verb
to give priority
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpraɪəʊ / (BrE)
to give priority to something or treat it as more important than other things.

Examples

To prio work
I always prio my health over work when I am feeling sick.
The manager asked the team to prio the most urgent tasks for today.
You should prio your assignments if you want to finish everything on time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neglect - to fail to give proper attention to
  • synonym: prioritize - to arrange or deal with in order of importance
