specialty crop

noun
crop grown for specific or niche markets
1.8 3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈspeʃ.əl.tiː krɑːp/ /; / /ˈspeɪ.ʃəl.tiː krɑːp/ /; / /ˈspeʃ.əl.tɪ krɑːp/ (AmE), /ˈspeʃ.əl.tiː krɒp/ /; / /ˈspeɪ.ʃəl.tiː krɒp/ /; / /ˈspeʃ.əl.tɪ krɒp/ (BrE)
