for chrissakes

adverb
expression of frustration
9.9
Pronunciation: / fər krɪsˈseɪks / (AmE), / fə krɪsˈseɪks / (BrE)
used informally to express frustration, annoyance, or urgency, often to emphasize a request or command

Examples

Quiet down for chrissakes
Turn down the music, for chrissakes, I'm trying to sleep.
For chrissakes, can you please answer your phone when I call?
Just tell me the truth, for chrissakes, so we can move on.

Related Terms

  • synonym: for Pete's sake - an informal expression of frustration or urgency
  • synonym: for goodness' sake - used to express annoyance or urgency without blasphemy
