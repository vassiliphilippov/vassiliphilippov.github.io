redback

noun
Texas banknote (1839–1840)
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌbæk / (BrE)
a paper banknote issued by the Republic of Texas in 1839–1840, named for its red-printed back

Examples

A rare redback note
The collector was thrilled to find a rare redback at the antique fair.
A redback from the Republic of Texas is a valuable piece of history.
Historians studied the design of the redback to learn more about early Texan currency.
