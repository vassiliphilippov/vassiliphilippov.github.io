leapfrog

verb
vault over or move past by jumping
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈliːp.frɔːɡ / (AmE), / ˈliːp.frɒɡ / (BrE)
to move forward by jumping over something or someone, especially by vaulting with hands and legs as in the children's game; also, to surpass or overtake others by sudden progress.

Examples

To leapfrog a hurdle
He leapfrogged the hurdles with impressive agility during the practice run.
Her company managed to leapfrog its competitors with an innovative new product.
The students took turns trying to leapfrog over the tall bench in gym class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vault - to jump over something in a single movement, using hands or a pole
