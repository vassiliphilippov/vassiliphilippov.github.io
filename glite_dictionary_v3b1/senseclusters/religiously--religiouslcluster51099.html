religiously

adverb
in a manner relating to religion
4.8
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlɪdʒəsli / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəsli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or is based on religion.

Examples

Religiously observed rules
Some laws are followed religiously in accordance with ancient customs.
The festival is celebrated religiously every year by the entire community.
The paintings in the temple are religiously inspired masterpieces.

Related Terms

  • antonym: spiritually - in a spiritual manner rather than a religious one
