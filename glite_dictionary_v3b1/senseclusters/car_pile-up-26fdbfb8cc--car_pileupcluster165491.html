car pile-up

noun
multi-car traffic accident
7.2
Pronunciation: / kɑr ˈpaɪl ʌp / (AmE), / kɑː ˈpaɪl ʌp / (BrE)
a road accident involving several cars crashing into each other, often at high speed or in a chain reaction

Examples

A car pile-up accident
Morning commuters were stuck in traffic after a car pile-up blocked both lanes.
Police say icy roads contributed to the car pile-up that involved eight vehicles.
There was a massive car pile-up on the highway due to the thick fog.

Related Terms

  • synonym: car accident - an accident involving cars, not necessarily multiple vehicles
  • synonym: car crash - a collision involving one or more cars
