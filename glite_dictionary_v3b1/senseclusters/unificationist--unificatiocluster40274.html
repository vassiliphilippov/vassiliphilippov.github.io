unificationist

noun
Unification Church member
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːnɪfɪˈkeɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪfɪˈkeɪʃənɪst / (BrE)

Examples

She became a unificationist after learning about the movement's teachings in college.
