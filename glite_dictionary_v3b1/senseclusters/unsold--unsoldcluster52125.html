unsold

adjective
not yet sold
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsoʊld / (AmE), / ʌnˈsəʊld / (BrE)
not disposed of by being purchased; not yet sold.

Examples

Many unsold books
Many of the books were left unsold after the event ended.
The house remained unsold despite several viewings.
The store offered discounts on unsold inventory at the end of the season.
