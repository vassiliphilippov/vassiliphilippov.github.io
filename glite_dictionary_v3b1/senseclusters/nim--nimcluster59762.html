nim

noun
strategy game involving removal of objects
8.0 8.9
Pronunciation: /nɪm/ (AmE), /nɪm/ (BrE)
a game in which objects such as matchsticks are arranged in rows and players take turns removing one or more; the aim is either to take or avoid taking the last object, depending on the variation.
