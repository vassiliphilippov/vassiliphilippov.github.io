parish-pump

adjective
concerned with local or parochial matters
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpærɪʃ pʌmp / (AmE), / ˈpærɪʃ pʌmp / (BrE)
concerned with local issues or narrow, parochial matters, especially in politics; lacking broader or national perspective

Examples

Parish-pump politics
Critics accused the council of focusing too much on parish-pump politics at the expense of wider reforms.
His parish-pump mentality meant he rarely considered national interests when making decisions.
The election debate quickly descended into a parish-pump squabble about minor local issues.
