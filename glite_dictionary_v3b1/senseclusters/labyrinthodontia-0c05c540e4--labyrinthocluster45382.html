Labyrinthodontia

noun
extinct group of prehistoric amphibians
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌlæb.əˌrɪn.θoʊˈdɑn.ʃi.ə /; / ˌlæb.əˌrɪn.θəˈdɒn.ʃi.ə / (AmE), / ˌlæb.ər.ɪnˈθɒd.n.ti.ə /; / ˌlæb.ɪˌrɪn.θɒˈdɒn.ʃɪ.ə / (BrE)
an extinct group of large amphibians from the Devonian to Triassic periods, with solid, flattened skulls and conical teeth with a complex internal structure
