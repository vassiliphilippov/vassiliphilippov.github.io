overdraw

noun
wasted pixel rendering in graphics
8.4 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / \u02cco\u028av\u0259r\u02c8dr\u0254 / (AmE), / \u02cc\u0259\u028av\u0259\u02c8dr\u0254\u02d0 / (BrE)
(in computer graphics and UI development) wasted rendering work caused by drawing pixels that are later hidden or replaced; a measure of rendering inefficiency.

Examples

High overdraw in graphics
The app suffers from high overdraw, causing slower frame rates.
Developers used a profiler to measure overdraw and found many hidden layers being rendered unnecessarily.
To improve performance, the team reduced overdraw by flattening overlapping transparent views in the UI.

  • synonym: rendering inefficiency - unnecessary GPU/CPU work caused by drawing pixels that will not be visible to the user
