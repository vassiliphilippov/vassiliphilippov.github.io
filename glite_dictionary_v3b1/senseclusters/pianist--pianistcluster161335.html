pianist

noun
person who plays the piano
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːənɪst / (AmE), / ˈpɪənɪst / (BrE)
A person who plays the piano, especially as a profession or with notable skill.

Examples

A pianist plays piano
As a professional pianist, he practices for several hours every day.
The competition attracted talented pianists from around the world.
The young pianist dazzled the audience with her technical skill and emotion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: keyboardist - a person who plays keyboard instruments
