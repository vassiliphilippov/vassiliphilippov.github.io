Taxus

noun
genus of yew trees and shrubs
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtæksəs / (AmE), / ˈtaksəs / (BrE)
A genus of evergreen coniferous trees and shrubs known as yews, native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.
