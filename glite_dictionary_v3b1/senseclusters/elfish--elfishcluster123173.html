elfish

adjective
playfully mischievous, like an elf
Pronunciation: / ˈɛl.fɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈɛl.fɪʃ / (BrE)
Mischievous or playful in a way that is reminiscent of an elf; usually in a good-natured manner.

Examples

Elfish smile
He looked at me with an elfish twinkle in his eye, plotting some harmless prank.
Her elfish grin made it impossible to stay annoyed with her for long.
The writer's elfish sense of humor gave the story a lighthearted charm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impish - Mischievous in a playful or naughty way
