foil

noun
transparent sheet for projection
Pronunciation: / fɔɪl / (AmE), / fɔɪl / (BrE)
a transparent sheet bearing a positive photograph or drawing, intended to be viewed with a projector

Examples

Projector foil slide
He carefully mounted the map onto a foil to ensure clarity during his presentation.
The teacher placed a foil on the overhead projector to display the diagram to the class.
Vintage photos are sometimes preserved as a foil for use in slide projectors.
