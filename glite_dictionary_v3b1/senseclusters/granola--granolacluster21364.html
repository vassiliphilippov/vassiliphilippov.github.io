granola

noun
cereal of oats, nuts, and dried fruits
1.6
Pronunciation: / ɡrəˈnoʊlə / (AmE), / ɡrəˈnəʊlə / (BrE)
a breakfast cereal or snack made from rolled oats, nuts, dried fruit, and sweeteners such as honey or brown sugar, typically baked until crisp

Examples

A granola bar
Many hikers carry granola bars for an energy boost during long walks.
She sprinkled granola on her yogurt for a healthy breakfast.
The recipe calls for a cup of homemade granola to add some crunch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: muesli - a cereal dish made from raw oats, fruits, and nuts, usually not baked
