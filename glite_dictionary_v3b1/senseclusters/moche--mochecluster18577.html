moche

adjective
of Moche culture
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtʃeɪ / (AmE), / ˈməʊtʃeɪ / (BrE)
relating to the ancient Moche culture of Peru, especially its art, pottery, or archaeological artifacts

Examples

Moche pottery art
The museum's display included many moche vessels decorated with scenes of warriors and animals.
Archaeologists discovered a moche temple with intricate murals in northern Peru.
She studied moche pottery to learn more about ancient Andean societies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Inca - relating to the Inca civilization of ancient Peru
  • synonym: pre-Columbian - relating to the cultures of the Americas before the arrival of Columbus
