execution speed

noun
rate at which a computer executes instructions
4.3 4.0
Pronunciation: /ek.sɪ.ˈkjuː.ʃən spiːd/ (AmE), /ek.sɪ.ˈkjuː.ʃən spiːd/ (BrE)
(computer science) the rate at which a computer or processor executes instructions, typically measured in instructions per second.

Some algorithms are designed specifically to maximize execution speed on modern hardware.
