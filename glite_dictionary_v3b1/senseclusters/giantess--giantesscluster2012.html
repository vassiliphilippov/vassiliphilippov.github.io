giantess

noun
female giant in mythology or stories
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪəntɛs / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪəntɛs / (BrE)
an imaginary female being of great size and strength, often appearing in mythology or fairy tales

Examples

The giantess in stories
Artists often depict the giantess with long flowing hair and enormous hands.
In the story, a giantess towers over the trees, her footsteps causing the ground to tremble.
The old myth spoke of a giantess who lived among the mountains and protected her village.
