dust storm

noun
storm with blowing dust or sand
Pronunciation: / dʌst stɔrm / (AmE), / dʌst stɔːm / (BrE)
A storm accompanied by strong winds that lift and carry large amounts of dust or sand into the air, reducing visibility.

Examples

A dust storm warning
During the summer, a severe dust storm can make travel across the desert extremely dangerous.
Farmers lost several crops when a powerful dust storm hit the region last month.
The dust storm swept across the town, blotting out the sun and lowering visibility to near zero.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sandstorm - a windstorm carrying sand through the air
