phonebook

noun
directory of telephone numbers
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊnˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈfəʊnˌbʊk / (BrE)
a book or electronic directory listing names, addresses, and telephone numbers of people or businesses in alphabetical order
