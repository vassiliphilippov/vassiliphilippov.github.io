supertramp

noun
wandering person
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərtræmp / (AmE), / ˈsuːpətræmp / (BrE)
a person who travels from place to place without a permanent home or job, often living by wandering or odd jobs

Examples

A wandering supertramp
The story tells of a supertramp who crosses continents in search of meaning.
After losing his job, he became a supertramp, living on the road for years.
Many admired the supertramp lifestyle, but few could handle its hardships.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drifter - a person who moves from place to place without a fixed home or job
  • antonym: settler - a person who stays in one place and establishes a home
  • synonym: vagabond - a person who wanders from place to place without a home or job
