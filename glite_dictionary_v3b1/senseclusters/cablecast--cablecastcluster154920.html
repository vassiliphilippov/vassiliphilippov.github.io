cablecast

noun
cable TV broadcast
6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈkeɪ.bəl.kæst/ (AmE), /ˈkeɪ.bəl.kɑːst/ (BrE)
a television or radio program that is transmitted over a cable system rather than broadcast over the air

Examples

Many viewers prefer a cablecast for its higher picture quality and exclusive content.
The documentary's cablecast attracted a large audience interested in environmental issues.
