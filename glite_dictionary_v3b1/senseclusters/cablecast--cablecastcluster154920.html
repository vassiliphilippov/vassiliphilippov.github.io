cablecast

noun
cable TV broadcast
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪbəlˌkæst / (AmE), / ˈkeɪbəlˌkɑːst / (BrE)
a television or radio program that is transmitted over a cable system rather than broadcast over the air

Examples

A live cablecast
The local news cablecast covered the city council meeting in detail.
Many viewers prefer a cablecast for its higher picture quality and exclusive content.
The documentary's cablecast attracted a large audience interested in environmental issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broadcast - a program transmitted by radio or television to the public
  • antonym: webcast - a program transmitted over the internet
