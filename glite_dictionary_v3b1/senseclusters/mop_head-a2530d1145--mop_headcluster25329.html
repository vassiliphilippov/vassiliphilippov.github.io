mop head

noun
absorbent cleaning part of a mop
Pronunciation: / mɑp hɛd / (AmE), / mɒp hɛd / (BrE)
the absorbent part at the end of a mop used for cleaning floors or surfaces.

Examples

A mop head for cleaning
Over time, the mop head collected dust and hair from the floor.
She bought a replacement mop head for her old cleaning handle.
The mop head was soaked, so I wrung it out before continuing to clean.
