Linosyris vulgaris

noun
a southern European perennial plant resembling goldenrod
Pronunciation: / lɪˌnɒsɪrɪs vʌlˈgɛrɪs / (AmE), / lɪˌnɒsɪˈrɪs vʌlˈgɑːrɪs / (BrE)
An early-flowering perennial plant native to southern and southeastern Europe, with flower heads that resemble those of goldenrod.
