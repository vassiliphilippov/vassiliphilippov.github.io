marmoset

noun
small South American monkey with claws
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːrməˌzɛt / (AmE), / ˈmɑːməzɛt / (BrE)
A small, soft-furred monkey native to South and Central America, characterized by having claws instead of nails.

Examples

A small marmoset
A marmoset is much smaller than most other monkey species.
Researchers observed the social behavior of a group of marmosets in the rainforest.
The marmoset is known for its agility as it leaps between tree branches.
