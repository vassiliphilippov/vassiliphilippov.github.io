santo

noun
saint statue
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːntoʊ / (AmE), / ˈsæntəʊ / (BrE)
a carved or painted statue or image of a saint, especially used in religious devotion in Spanish-speaking cultures

Examples

Wooden santo statue
The old mission displayed a beautifully painted santo of Saint Francis in its chapel.
Collectors often seek out antique santos from colonial-era churches in Latin America.
During the festival, villagers carried a wooden santo through the streets in a lively procession.

Related Terms

  • synonym: icon - a religious image or representation, especially in Eastern Christianity
  • synonym: relic - an object of religious veneration, often associated with a saint
