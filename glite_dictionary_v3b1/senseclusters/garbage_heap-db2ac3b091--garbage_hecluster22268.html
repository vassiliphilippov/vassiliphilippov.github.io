garbage heap

noun
pile of garbage or refuse
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrbɪdʒ hip / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbɪdʒ hiːp / (BrE)
a large pile or accumulation of refuse or discarded materials

Examples

A garbage heap pile
After the festival, crews worked until dawn to clear the garbage heap left behind by revelers.
Environmentalists expressed concern about toxins leaking from the sprawling garbage heap near the river.
The abandoned lot was nothing more than a garbage heap filled with broken furniture and empty cans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: landfill - a site for the disposal of waste materials by burial
  • synonym: rubbish heap - a pile of waste or discarded materials (chiefly British)
