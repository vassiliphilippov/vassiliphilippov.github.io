tour

noun
virtual/online guided walkthrough
3.5 9.93/m
Pronunciation: / t58r / (AmE), / t8a59 / (BrE)
a digital or virtual guided walkthrough or presentation of a place or space, viewable online or in virtual reality, often interactive and designed to simulate visiting the location.

Examples

A virtual tour
Prospective buyers can take a 360-degree tour of the property online before visiting.
The museum offers an interactive tour you can follow on your phone in augmented reality.
During the webinar, the architect gave a narrated tour of the new building using VR headsets.
