gulden

noun
former currency unit of Suriname
9.8 8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈgʌl.dən/ /; / /ˈgʊl.dən/ (AmE), /ˈgʌl.dən/ /; / /ˈgʊl.dən/ (BrE)
the former basic unit of currency in Suriname, equal to 100 cents, replaced by the Surinamese dollar in 2004

Examples

Surinamese gulden
Before 2004, the gulden was used for all transactions in Suriname.
