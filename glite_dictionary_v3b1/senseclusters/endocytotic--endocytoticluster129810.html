endocytotic

adjective
relating to endocytosis
Pronunciation: / ˌɛndoʊsaɪˈtɑtɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛndəʊsaɪˈtɒtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving endocytosis, the process by which a cell takes in material from its surroundings

Examples

Endocytotic process
The endocytotic process allows cells to absorb nutrients efficiently.
Researchers studied the endocytotic pathways involved in drug delivery to cells.
Defects in endocytotic mechanisms can lead to various cellular disorders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: endocytic - relating to endocytosis
  • antonym: exocytotic - relating to exocytosis, the process of a cell releasing material
