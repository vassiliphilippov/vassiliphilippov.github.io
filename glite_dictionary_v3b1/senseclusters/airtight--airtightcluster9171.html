airtight

adjective
free from flaws or weaknesses
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr.taɪt /; / ˈer.taɪt / (AmE), / ˈeə.taɪt / (BrE)
impossible to find fault with or challenge; completely flawless or secure (used about arguments, explanations, etc.)
