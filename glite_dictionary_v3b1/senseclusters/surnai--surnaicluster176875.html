surnai

noun
South Asian wind instrument
9.9
a traditional double-reed wind instrument used in South Asian music, often played at ceremonies and celebrations

Examples

To play the surnai
The musician played the surnai beautifully during the festival procession.
A lively tune on the surnai welcomed guests to the traditional wedding.
The sound of the surnai echoed through the village at dawn.
