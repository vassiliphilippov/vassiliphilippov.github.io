necro

noun
informal necromancer
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈnek.roʊ/ /; / /ˈniː.kroʊ/ (AmE), /ˈnek.rəʊ/ /; / /ˈniː.krəʊ/ (BrE)
an informal term for a necromancer, especially a character who summons or controls the dead in games or fiction

Examples

A necro summons skeletons
He always plays a necro in fantasy video games because he likes summoning skeletons.
The story's main villain is a powerful necro who commands an army of undead.
During the campaign, our party was saved by a friendly necro with healing spells.
