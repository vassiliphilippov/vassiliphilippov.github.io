flexibility

noun
the quality of bending or adapting easily
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌflɛksəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌflɛksɪˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality of being able to bend or be changed easily without breaking; the ability to adapt to different situations

Examples

Physical flexibility
Good stretching routines can improve your flexibility and reduce the risk of injury.
Her flexibility in handling new tasks made her a valuable team member.
The flexibility of the material allows it to be molded into different shapes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pliability - the quality of being easily bent without breaking
  • antonym: rigidity - the quality of being stiff or inflexible
