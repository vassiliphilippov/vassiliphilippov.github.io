birth trauma

noun
emotional injury caused by events during birth
2.9 5.8
Pronunciation: /bɜːrθ ˈtrɔː.mə/ /; / /bɜːrθ ˈtrɑː.mə/ (AmE), /bɜːθ ˈtrɔː.mə/ /; / /bɜːθ ˈtraʊ.mə/ (BrE)
psychological or emotional injury said to be caused to an infant by distressing events during birth, sometimes cited as influencing later mental health

Examples

Infant birth trauma
Some psychologists believe that birth trauma can leave lasting psychological effects on an individual.
The concept of birth trauma is controversial within the psychiatric community.
Therapists sometimes explore early experiences of birth trauma to help patients understand their mental health issues.
