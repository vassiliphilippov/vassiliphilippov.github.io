susurrus

noun
soft murmuring or whispering sound
Pronunciation: / suˈsɜr.əs / (AmE), / suˈsʌr.əs / (BrE)
A soft, indistinct murmuring or whispering sound.

Examples

A susurrus of voices
A susurrus of voices filled the quiet library during the exam.
He lay awake, listening to the susurrus of leaves rustling outside his window.
The susurrus of the audience grew louder as the performer took the stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: murmur - a low, continuous sound, often of voices
  • synonym: whisper - a soft, hushed sound produced by speaking very quietly
