camp

noun
deliberately exaggerated or affected style
5.0
Pronunciation: / kæmp / (AmE), / kamp / (BrE)
an aesthetic style or sensibility that is appreciated because it is deliberately exaggerated, artificial, or affected, often viewed as so bad or outmoded that it becomes amusing or fashionable

Examples

Camp style
Critics described the performance as a celebration of camp rather than serious drama.
She decorated her apartment with a sense of camp that made every visitor smile.
The movie's costumes and dialogue were pure camp and delighted the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kitsch - art or objects considered to be in poor taste because of excessive garishness or sentimentality, but sometimes appreciated ironically
