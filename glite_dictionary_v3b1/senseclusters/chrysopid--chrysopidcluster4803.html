chrysopid

noun
pale green lacewing insect
4.6 9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkrɪs.ə.pɪd/ (AmE), /ˈkrɪs.ə.pɪd/ (BrE)
A pale green insect of the lacewing family, known for its unpleasant smell and carnivorous larvae, belonging to the family Chrysopidae.

Examples

A chrysopid larva
A single chrysopid larva can eat hundreds of insect eggs and pests in its lifetime.
Entomologists studied the behavior of chrysopids in controlling pest populations.
The garden was filled with chrysopids, which are effective predators of aphids.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lacewing - any insect of the order Neuroptera with lacy wings, especially the green lacewing
