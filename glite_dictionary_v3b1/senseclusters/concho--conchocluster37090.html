concho

noun
metal ornament
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːntʃoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɒntʃəʊ / (BrE)
a decorative, usually round, metal ornament used on clothing, accessories, or saddles, especially in Western wear

Examples

A concho ornament
The cowboy's belt was decorated with a silver concho.
She added a turquoise concho to her leather handbag for a Western touch.
The saddle featured several engraved conchos along its edge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ornament - a decorative object added to something for embellishment
  • synonym: stud - a small decorative knob or fastener on clothing or accessories
