count noun

noun
noun that can be counted
Pronunciation: / kaʊnt naʊn / (AmE), / kaʊnt naʊn / (BrE)
a noun that refers to people or things that can be counted and forms a plural, such as 'book' or 'apple'.

Examples

A count noun example
In English, most everyday objects are referred to using count nouns.
Please give an example of a count noun and a mass noun.
The word 'dog' is a count noun, since you can say 'two dogs'.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mass noun - a noun denoting something not countable, like 'water' or 'information'
