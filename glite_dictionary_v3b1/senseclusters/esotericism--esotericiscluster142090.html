esotericism

noun
specialized knowledge
Pronunciation: / ˌɛsəˈtɛrɪˌsɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌiːsəʊˈtɛrɪsɪzəm /; / ˌiːsəˈtɛrɪsɪzəm / (BrE)
knowledge, ideas, or practices that are secret, specialized, or only understood by a small group of people

Examples

Many people are drawn to esotericism in search of deeper meaning beyond conventional religion.

Some people are drawn to the esotericism of secret societies and their hidden rituals.
Critics argued that the novel's esotericism limited its appeal to a wider audience.
