Hitchiti

noun
member of a Native American people of Georgia
Pronunciation: / hɪˈtʃiːti / (AmE), / hɪˈtʃiːti / (BrE)
A member of a Native American people who formerly lived in Georgia and were part of the Creek Confederacy.

Examples

Hitchiti tribe member
Archaeologists have found Hitchiti artifacts in several areas of Georgia.
Many Hitchiti descendants still honor their cultural heritage.
The Hitchiti were once an important part of the Creek Confederacy.
