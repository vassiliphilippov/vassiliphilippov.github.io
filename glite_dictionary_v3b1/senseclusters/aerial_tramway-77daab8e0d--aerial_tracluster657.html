aerial tramway

noun
cable transport system for passengers or goods
8.4 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈer.iː.əl ˈtræm.weɪ/ /; / /ˈer.ɪ.əl ˈtræm.weɪ/ (AmE), /ˈeə.ri.əl ˈtræm.weɪ/ (BrE)
A transport system in which passengers or goods are carried in cabins or cars suspended from and moved along overhead cables supported by towers.

Examples

The aerial tramway cabin
During winter, the aerial tramway remains a popular way to reach the ski resort.
Engineers inspected the cables of the aerial tramway to ensure passenger safety.
The aerial tramway carried tourists up the mountain for breathtaking views.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cable car - vehicle that moves along cables, often used for short steep routes
  • synonym: funicular - a railway that moves up and down a steep slope using cables
