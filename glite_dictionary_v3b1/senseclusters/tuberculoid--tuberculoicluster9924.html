tuberculoid

adjective
resembling tuberculosis; characteristic of tuberculosis
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtuː.bər.kjuː.lɔɪd/ /; / /ˈtuː.bər.kjʊ.lɔɪd/ (AmE), /ˈtjuː.bə.kjʊ.lɔɪd/ /; / /ˈtuː.bə.kjʊ.lɔɪd/ (BrE)
