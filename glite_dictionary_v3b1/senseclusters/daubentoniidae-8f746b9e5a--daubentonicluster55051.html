Daubentoniidae

noun
family of primates containing only the aye-aye
Pronunciation: / dɔːbɛnˈtoʊniˌaɪdiː /; / dɔːbɛnˌtɔniˈɪədiˌi / (AmE), / dɔːbɛnˈtəʊnɪˌiːdiː /; / dɔːbɛnˌtɒniˈɪədiː / (BrE)
a family of primates that includes only the aye-aye, a rare lemur native to Madagascar
