bongo

noun
a large African forest antelope
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɒŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
a large forest antelope of central Africa, having a reddish-brown coat with white stripes and spiral horns

Examples

A wild bongo
A solitary bongo moved quietly through the dense undergrowth.
Conservationists are working to protect the endangered bongo from poaching.
The bongo's striking stripes help camouflage it in the forest shadows.
