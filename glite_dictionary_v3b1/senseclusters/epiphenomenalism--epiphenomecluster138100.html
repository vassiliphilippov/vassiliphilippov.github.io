epiphenomenalism

noun
mind as by-product
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪfəˈnɑːmənəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪfəˈnɒmɪnəˌlɪzəm / (BrE)
the theory that mental events are caused by physical processes in the brain but do not themselves influence physical events

Related Terms

  • antonym: materialism - the doctrine that only physical matter exists
