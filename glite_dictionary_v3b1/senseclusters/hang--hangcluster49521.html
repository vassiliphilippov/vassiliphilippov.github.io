hang

noun
gymnastic exercise supported by arms
Pronunciation: / hæŋ / (AmE), / hæŋ / (BrE)
a gymnastic exercise in which the body is suspended by the arms from a bar or rings, with the weight supported by the arms

Examples

A bar hang
He practiced his hang to improve his upper body endurance.
Strength is required to maintain a steady hang during the routine.
The gymnast performed a perfect hang on the parallel bars before dismounting.
