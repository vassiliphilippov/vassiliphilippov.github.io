bouncy

adjective
able to spring back or rebound easily
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊnsi / (AmE), / ˈbaʊnsi / (BrE)
Able to rebound easily after being compressed or dropped; elastic or springy.

Examples

Bouncy ball
She loves how her hair feels light and bouncy after washing it.
The children enjoyed playing with the bouncy rubber ball in the park.
This mattress has a bouncy feel that makes it comfortable to sleep on.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elastic - able to resume its normal shape after being stretched or compressed
  • synonym: resilient - able to recover shape or position quickly
  • synonym: springy - able to return quickly to its original shape after being pressed
