afterglow

noun
faint glow after sunset
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈæf.tɚˌɡloʊ / (AmE), / ˈɑːf.təˌɡləʊ / (BrE)
a faint light or glow sometimes visible in the sky after the sun has set

Examples

Evening afterglow
Photographers gathered to capture the magical afterglow that followed the stormy evening.
The sunset ended, but a beautiful afterglow lingered on the horizon.
We watched the pink and orange afterglow as night slowly set in.

Related Terms

  • synonym: twilight - the soft light in the sky when the sun is below the horizon
