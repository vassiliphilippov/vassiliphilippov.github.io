whereon

adverb
on what basis
Pronunciation: / wɛrˈɑn / (AmE), / weərˈɒn / (BrE)
on what or upon what; used in questions to ask about the thing or basis on which something is done or based.

Examples

Whereon is this based
She asked whereon he had based his decision during the meeting.
Can you tell me whereon this theory depends for its validity?
The judge inquired whereon the lawyer was relying for his argument.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whereupon - immediately after which
