bright

adjective
clear and resonant (of sound)
2.5
Pronunciation: / braɪt / (AmE), / braɪt / (BrE)
(of a sound) clear, sharp, and resonant; having a lively and penetrating tone.

Examples

Bright sound
She preferred a less bright tone when recording her vocals for the track.
The guitar's bright timbre made it stand out in the mix.
The new speakers produced a bright sound that filled the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brilliant - very bright and clear in tone; sparkling (of sound)
  • antonym: dull - lacking brightness or clarity in sound; muffled or muted
