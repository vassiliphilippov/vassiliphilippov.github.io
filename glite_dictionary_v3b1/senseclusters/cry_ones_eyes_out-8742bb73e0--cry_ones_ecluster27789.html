cry one's eyes out

verb
cry intensely or heavily (idiom)
Pronunciation: / kraɪ wʌnz aɪz aʊt / (AmE), / kraɪ wʌnz aɪz aʊt / (BrE)
to cry very intensely or for a long time, usually because of great sadness

Examples

To cry their eyes out
After losing the championship, the whole team cried their eyes out in the locker room.
I cried my eyes out watching that heartbreaking film last night.
She cried her eyes out when she heard the tragic news.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bawl one's eyes out - to cry very loudly or intensely, usually from distress
