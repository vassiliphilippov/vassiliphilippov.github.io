foodservice

adjective
for commercial kitchens
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfuːdˌsɜːrvɪs / (AmE), / ˈfuːdˌsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
relating to products, equipment, or services intended for use in restaurants, cafeterias, or catering rather than at home

Examples

Foodservice equipment
The company supplies foodservice equipment to restaurants and hotels across the country.
They purchased foodservice containers designed to withstand frequent use in busy kitchens.
Many manufacturers offer both home and foodservice versions of their products.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commercial - intended for business or industrial use
  • antonym: domestic - intended for use in the home
