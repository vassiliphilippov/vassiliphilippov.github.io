overcomplicated

adjective
unnecessarily complex
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈkɑːmplɪˌkeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈkɒmplɪkeɪtɪd / (BrE)
made more complex than necessary, often resulting in confusion or difficulty

Examples

An overcomplicated system
The new software interface is so overcomplicated that users struggle to find basic functions.
He overcomplicated the recipe by adding too many unnecessary steps.
Sometimes, simple solutions work best, but we tend to make things overcomplicated.
