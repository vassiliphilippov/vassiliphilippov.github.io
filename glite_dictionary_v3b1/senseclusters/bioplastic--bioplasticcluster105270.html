bioplastic

adjective
from renewable sources
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊˈplæstɪk / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.əʊˈplæstɪk / (BrE)
made from or relating to plastic materials produced from biological, renewable sources instead of petroleum

Examples

Bioplastic packaging
The company switched to bioplastic packaging to reduce its environmental impact.
Many bioplastic products are made from corn starch or sugarcane.
Using bioplastic materials helps decrease reliance on fossil fuels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biodegradable - able to be broken down naturally by microorganisms
  • antonym: petrochemical - made from chemicals derived from petroleum
