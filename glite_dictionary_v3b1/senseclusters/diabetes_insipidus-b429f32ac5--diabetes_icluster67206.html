Examples

Diabetes insipidus symptoms
The patient was diagnosed with diabetes insipidus after experiencing extreme thirst and excessive urination.
Treatment for diabetes insipidus often includes medication to replace or mimic vasopressin.
Unlike diabetes mellitus, diabetes insipidus does not involve high blood sugar levels.
