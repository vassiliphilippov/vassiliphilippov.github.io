arcanist

noun
holder of secret knowledge
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˈkeɪnɪst / (AmE), / ɑːˈkeɪnɪst / (BrE)
a person who possesses or is skilled in secret or mysterious knowledge, especially in specialized crafts or alchemy

Examples

Master arcanist of secrets
The arcanist was famous for creating unique glazes that no one else could replicate.
In ancient times, an arcanist might have been sought after for their mysterious skills.
Only the arcanist knew the true process behind the legendary porcelain.
