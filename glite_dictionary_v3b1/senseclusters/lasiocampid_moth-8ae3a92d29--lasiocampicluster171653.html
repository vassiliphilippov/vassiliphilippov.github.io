lasiocampid moth

noun
moth of the family Lasiocampidae
7.6 9.1
Pronunciation: /lə.sɪ.oʊ.ˈkæm.pɪd mɔːθ/ (AmE), /lə.sɪ.əʊ.ˈkæm.pɪd mɒθ/ (BrE)
A medium-sized, stout-bodied moth of the family Lasiocampidae, typically neutral-colored and having comb-like antennae.

The lasiocampid moth is easily recognized by its stout, hairy body and feathery antennae.
