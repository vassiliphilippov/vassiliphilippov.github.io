tress

noun
a braid or lock of hair
Pronunciation: / trɛs / (AmE), / trɛs / (BrE)
a lock or section of hair, especially one that has been braided or twisted

Examples

A long tress of hair
He admired the thick tress resting against her back.
She wore her long golden tress swept over one shoulder.
The princess's silken tress was perfectly braided for the festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braid - an interlaced strand of hair or thread
  • synonym: plait - a braid of hair or other material
