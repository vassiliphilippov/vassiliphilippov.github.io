steam iron

noun
steam-based hair straightener
3.2
Pronunciation: / stiːm ˈaɪən / (AmE), / stiːm ˈaɪə(n) / (BrE)
A hair-styling device that uses steam to straighten, set, or shape hair, often claimed to reduce heat damage compared with dry-heat straighteners.

Examples

A steam iron for hair
She used a steam iron on small sections to smooth frizz before the party.
The stylist prefers a steam iron because it leaves hair shinier and less damaged than a hot plate.
Manufacturers advertised the new steam iron as ideal for creating long-lasting straight styles without extreme heat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flat iron - a pair of heated plates used to straighten hair
  • synonym: hair straightener - a tool used to make hair straight, often called a flat iron
