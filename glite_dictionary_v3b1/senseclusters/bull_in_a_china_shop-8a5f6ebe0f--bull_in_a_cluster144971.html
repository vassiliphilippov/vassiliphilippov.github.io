bull in a china shop

noun
clumsy or tactless person in a sensitive situation
6.8
Pronunciation: / bʊl ɪn ə ˈtʃaɪnə ʃɑp / (AmE), / bʊl ɪn ə ˈtʃaɪnə ʃɒp / (BrE)
a person who is clumsy or tactless in a delicate or sensitive situation

Examples

Like a bull in a china shop
As a negotiator, you can't afford to act like a bull in a china shop.
His direct manner made him seem a bull in a china shop at the diplomatic dinner.
Whenever Mark tries to help with decorations, he's like a bull in a china shop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clumsy - having or showing a lack of skill or grace
  • antonym: tactful - showing sensitivity and diplomacy in dealing with others
