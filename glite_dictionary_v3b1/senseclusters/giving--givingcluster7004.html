giving

verb
to provide or offer
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
present participle of 'give', meaning to hand over, provide, or offer something to someone

Examples

She is giving help
She is giving her time to help the local community every weekend.
By giving clear instructions, the teacher made the assignment much easier to understand.
They are giving away free samples at the entrance of the store.

Related Terms

  • synonym: offering - to present or give something for acceptance
  • synonym: providing - to supply or make available
  • antonym: receiving - to be given or to accept something
