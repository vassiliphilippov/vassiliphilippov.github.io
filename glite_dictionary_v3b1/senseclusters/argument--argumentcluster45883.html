argument

noun
logical reasoning or set of reasons
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrɡjəmənt /; / ˈɑːrɡjumənt / (AmE), / ˈɑːɡjʊmənt / (BrE)
a statement or series of reasons used to persuade others or show that something is true or false

Examples

Her argument was based on scientific research and facts.

The lawyer presented a clear argument to support his case.
Our team had a long argument over which strategy to use for the project.

The politicians were engaged in a heated argument about healthcare reform.
He presented a strong argument to support his proposal at the meeting.
Her main argument was based on new research data and statistics.
The lawyer's closing argument convinced the jury of his client's innocence.
