leveling

noun
complete destruction of a building
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛvəɫɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɛvəlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of completely destroying a building so that it is flat with the ground

Examples

Leveling a building
After the earthquake, the leveling of several buildings was necessary for safety reasons.
The leveling of the old factory made way for a new park in the neighborhood.
The town council approved the leveling of the abandoned warehouse last month.

  • synonym: demolition - the act of deliberately destroying a building
