retest

verb
to check market level
5.6
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈtest/ /; / /rə.ˈtest/ (AmE), /riː.ˈtest/ /; / /rə.ˈtest/ (BrE)
to return to a previously broken support or resistance level in a market to check if it still holds.

Examples

To retest support
After breaking through resistance, the stock price retested the level before continuing upward.
Traders watched closely as the currency pair retested its previous support zone.
It is common for a market to retest a key level after a significant move.
