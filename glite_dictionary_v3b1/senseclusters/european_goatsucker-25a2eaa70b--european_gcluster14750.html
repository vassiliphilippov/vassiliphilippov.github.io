European goatsucker

noun
a European nocturnal bird, the nightjar
Pronunciation: / jʊˈroʊpiən ˈɡoʊtˌsʌkər / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən ˈɡəʊtˌsʌkə / (BrE)
