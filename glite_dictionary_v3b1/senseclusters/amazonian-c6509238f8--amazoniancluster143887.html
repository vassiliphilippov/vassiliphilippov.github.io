Amazonian

noun
tall, strong woman
Pronunciation: / ˌæməˈzoʊniən / (AmE), / ˌæməˈzəʊniən / (BrE)
a member of the mythical tribe of warrior women called Amazons, or figuratively, a tall and strong woman

Examples

Amazonian warrior
The actress played an Amazonian who led her people into battle.
Standing over six feet tall, she was often described as an Amazonian by her friends.
In ancient myths, an Amazonian was known for her bravery and strength.

Related Terms

  • synonym: warrior - a person who fights in battles or wars
