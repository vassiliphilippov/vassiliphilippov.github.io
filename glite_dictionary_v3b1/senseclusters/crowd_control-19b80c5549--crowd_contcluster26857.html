crowd control

noun
game ability to disable multiple enemies
4.6
Pronunciation: /kraʊd kən.ˈtroʊl/ (AmE), /kraʊd kən.ˈtrəʊl/ (BrE)
(in video games) abilities or effects that restrain, disable, or otherwise manage multiple enemies at once during combat.

Examples

Crowd control spells
The mage used crowd control to immobilize several foes during the dungeon boss fight.
Good team composition includes healers and reliable crowd control to survive waves of enemies.
Players debated whether the new patch nerfed crowd control abilities that dominate competitive play.

Related Terms

  • synonym: area of effect - an ability that affects multiple targets in a zone; often used to apply crowd control to groups
  • synonym: stun - an effect that temporarily prevents an enemy from acting; a common type of crowd control
