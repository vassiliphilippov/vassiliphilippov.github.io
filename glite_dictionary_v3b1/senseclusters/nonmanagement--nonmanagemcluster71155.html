nonmanagement

noun
absence of management
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈmænɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈmænɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
the absence or lack of management or managerial control in an organization or situation

Examples

Total nonmanagement failure
The project failed due to complete nonmanagement at every level.
Without proper oversight, nonmanagement led to confusion among the staff.
A culture of nonmanagement can result in missed deadlines and disorganization.

Related Terms

  • antonym: management - the act or process of controlling or organizing something
  • synonym: mismanagement - poor or improper management
