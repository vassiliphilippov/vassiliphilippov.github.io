potbellied

adjective
having a large, rounded belly
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑtˌbɛlid / (AmE), / ˈpɒtˌbɛlid / (BrE)
Having a large, rounded belly that protrudes noticeably.

Examples

A potbellied man
He became noticeably potbellied after years of neglecting exercise.
The potbellied man struggled to button his jacket over his stomach.
The potbellied pig wandered around the farm, searching for food.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paunchy - having a large, protruding belly
  • antonym: slim - thin or slender in form
