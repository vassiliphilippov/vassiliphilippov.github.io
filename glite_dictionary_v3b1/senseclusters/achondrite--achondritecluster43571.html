achondrite

noun
stony meteorite without chondrules
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈkɑnˌdraɪt / (AmE), / əˈkɒn.draɪt / (BrE)
a stony meteorite that does not contain chondrules, typically formed from differentiated planetary material

Examples

A achondrite meteorite
Scientists discovered that the new meteorite was an achondrite lacking the characteristic round grains found in chondrites.
The museum's collection includes an achondrite that originated from a differentiated asteroid.
Unlike chondrites, an achondrite formed after its parent body experienced melting and segregation.
