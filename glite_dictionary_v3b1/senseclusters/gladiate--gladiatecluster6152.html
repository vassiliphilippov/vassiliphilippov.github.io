gladiate

adjective
sword-shaped (botany)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlædiˌeɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡlædɪeɪt / (BrE)
having the shape of a sword, especially referring to leaves or plant parts in botany

Examples

Gladiate leaves
The plant's gladiate leaves made it popular in the sunny rock garden.
Botanists often describe certain iris species as having gladiate foliage.
The gladiate form of the leaf helps the plant capture more sunlight efficiently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ensiform - shaped like a sword, especially of leaves
