mango

noun
large evergreen tropical tree
Pronunciation: / ˈmæŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmæŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
a large evergreen tropical tree of the genus Mangifera, especially Mangifera indica, cultivated for its large oval fruit

Examples
