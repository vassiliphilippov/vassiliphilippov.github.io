glumly

adverb
in a sullen or dejected manner
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlʌmli / (AmE), / ˈɡlʌmli / (BrE)
in a sullen, dejected, or depressed manner

Examples

To glumly accept news
He glumly accepted the bad news without saying much.
She stared glumly out of the window as rain poured down.
The children walked home glumly after losing the match.
