ruffianly

adjective
violent or lawless in manner
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌfiənli / (AmE), / ˈrʌfiənli / (BrE)
behaving in a violent, rough, or lawless manner; characteristic of a ruffian

Examples

Ruffianly behaviour
He spoke in a ruffianly tone that made everyone feel uneasy.
The film portrayed the ruffianly behavior of the gang members in vivid detail.
The more ruffianly elements in the crowd started causing trouble as night fell.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lawless - disregarding laws; anarchic
  • synonym: violent - using or involving physical force
