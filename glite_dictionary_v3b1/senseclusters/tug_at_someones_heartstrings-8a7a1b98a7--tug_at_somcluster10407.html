tug at someone's heartstrings

verb
evoke deep emotion
Pronunciation: / tʌɡ æt ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈhɑːrtˌstɹɪŋz / (AmE), / tʌɡ ət ˈsʌmwənz ˈhɑːtˌstɹɪŋz / (BrE)

Examples
