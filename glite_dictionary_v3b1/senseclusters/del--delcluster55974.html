del

noun
vector calculus symbol
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɛl / (AmE), / dɛl / (BrE)
a mathematical symbol (∇) used in vector calculus to denote operations such as gradient, divergence, and curl

Examples

Del operator
In vector calculus, the del operator is essential for finding gradients and divergences.
The symbol del is written as an upside-down triangle and is used in many equations.
To calculate the curl of a field, you must apply the del operator to the vector function.
