rebag

verb
to bag again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈbæɡ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈbæɡ / (BrE)
to put something back into a bag or to bag something again

Examples

To rebag groceries
After checking the contents, she had to rebag the groceries.
The workers needed to rebag the sand after the original bags tore open.
Please rebag the apples so they do not fall out during transport.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pack - to put things into a container or bag for storage or transport
  • antonym: unbag - to remove something from a bag
