best deal in town

noun
most favorable price or offer available locally
2.3
Pronunciation: / bɛst dil ɪn taʊn / (AmE), / bɛst diːl ɪn taʊn / (BrE)
used to refer to the most favorable price or offer available in a particular area or among competitors

Examples

The best deal in town now
If you are looking for new phones, we've got the best deal in town right now.
Shoppers flock to this outlet because it always has the best deal in town.
We compared prices everywhere, and this car really is the best deal in town.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bargain - something bought or offered at a lower than usual price
  • antonym: rip-off - something that is unreasonably expensive
