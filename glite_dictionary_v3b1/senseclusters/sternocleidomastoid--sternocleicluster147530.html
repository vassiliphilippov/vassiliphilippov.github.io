sternocleidomastoid

noun
neck muscle that rotates and flexes the head
Pronunciation: / ˌstɜr.noʊˌklaɪ.dəˈmæs.tɔɪd /; / stɝː.noʊˌklaɪ.doʊˈmæs.tɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌstɜː.nəʊˌklaɪ.dəʊˈmæs.tɔɪd /; / stɜː.nəʊˌklaɪ.dəˈmæs.tɔɪd / (BrE)
either of the two thick muscles on each side of the neck that help rotate and flex the head
