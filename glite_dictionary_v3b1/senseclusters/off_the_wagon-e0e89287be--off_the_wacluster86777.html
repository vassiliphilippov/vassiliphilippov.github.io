off the wagon

adjective
resumed drinking alcohol after abstinence
Pronunciation: / ɔf ðə ˈwæɡən / (AmE), / ɒf ðə ˈwæɡən / (BrE)
Having resumed drinking alcohol after a period of abstinence.

Examples

Off the wagon again
After three years of sobriety, she found herself off the wagon.
Ever since the holidays, he's been off the wagon and drinking again.
It's hard watching a friend go off the wagon after so much progress.

Related Terms

  • antonym: on the wagon - abstaining from alcohol
