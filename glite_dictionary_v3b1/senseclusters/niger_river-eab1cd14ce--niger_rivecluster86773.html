Niger River

proper noun
major river in western Africa
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪdʒər ˌrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈnaɪdʒə ˌrɪvə / (BrE)
A major river in western Africa, flowing through several countries and emptying into the Gulf of Guinea in the Atlantic Ocean.

Examples

The Niger River basin
Many cities, including Niamey, are situated along the banks of the Niger River.
The Niger River is a vital waterway for agriculture in Mali and Niger.
The delta of the Niger River supports a rich diversity of wildlife.
