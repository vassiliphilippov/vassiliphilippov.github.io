nigga

noun
offensive racial slur for Black people
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈnɪ.gə/ (AmE), /ˈnɪ.gə/ (BrE)
A highly offensive term for a Black person, considered extremely insulting and derogatory, sometimes used informally within the Black community.

Examples

The word nigga
