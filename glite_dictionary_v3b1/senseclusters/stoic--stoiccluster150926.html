stoic

adjective
showing no visible reaction to hardship or pain
Pronunciation: / ˈstoʊɪk / (AmE), / ˈstəʊɪk / (BrE)
enduring pain or hardship without showing feelings or complaint; appearing unaffected by pleasure or pain

Examples

Stoic attitude
Despite the bad news, she remained stoic and did not shed a single tear.
He showed stoic endurance while facing the difficult challenges at work.
Her stoic patience inspired her friends during the long wait for results.

Related Terms

  • antonym: emotional - showing strong feelings or emotions
  • synonym: impassive - showing no emotion; not affected by pain or pleasure
