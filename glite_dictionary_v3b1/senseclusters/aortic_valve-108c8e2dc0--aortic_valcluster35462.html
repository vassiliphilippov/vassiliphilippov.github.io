aortic valve

noun
heart valve between left ventricle and aorta
3.7
Pronunciation: / eɪˌɔrtɪk ˈvælv / (AmE), / eɪˌɔːtɪk ˈvælv / (BrE)
the heart valve between the left ventricle and the aorta that prevents blood from flowing back into the heart

Examples

The aortic valve function
A defective aortic valve can lead to serious heart complications if left untreated.
Doctors replaced her aortic valve with an artificial one to restore normal blood flow.
The surgeon carefully examined the aortic valve for signs of damage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pulmonary valve - the valve between the right ventricle and the pulmonary artery
