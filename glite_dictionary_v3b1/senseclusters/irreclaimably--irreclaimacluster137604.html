irreclaimably

adverb
beyond recovery
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrɪˈkleɪməbli / (AmE), / ˌɪrɪˈkleɪməbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be recovered, reformed, or restored

Examples

Documents irreclaimably lost
After the fire, the documents were irreclaimably lost and could not be replaced.
The old traditions have been irreclaimably forgotten in the modern city.
The forest was irreclaimably altered by decades of illegal logging.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hopelessly - in a way that shows no possibility of improvement
  • synonym: irretrievably - in a way that cannot be recovered or regained
  • antonym: recoverably - in a way that can be recovered or restored
