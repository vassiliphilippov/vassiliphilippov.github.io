check register

noun
record of checks and deposits in an account
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛk ˌrɛdʒɪstɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛk ˌrɛdʒɪstə / (BrE)
a record used to keep track of checks written and deposits made in a checking account, usually in numeric order

Examples

A check register book
Many people now use online banking instead of keeping a paper check register.
She balanced her account by carefully updating her check register every week.
Whenever you write a new check, record the details in your check register.

Related Terms

  • synonym: checkbook - book containing blank checks and sometimes a register
