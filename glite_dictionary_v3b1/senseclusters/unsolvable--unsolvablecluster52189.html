unsolvable

adjective
impossible to solve
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɑːləbl̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɒləbl̩ / (BrE)
impossible to solve; not able to be resolved or worked out.

Examples

An unsolvable problem
Some mysteries remain unsolvable despite years of investigation.
The mathematician was frustrated by the seemingly unsolvable equation.
They faced an unsolvable conflict that led to their separation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insoluble - impossible to solve or explain; not able to be dissolved
  • antonym: solvable - able to be solved or resolved
