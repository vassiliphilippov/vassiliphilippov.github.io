dandy fever

noun
tropical mosquito-borne disease (dengue fever)
Pronunciation: / ˈdændi ˌfiːvər / (AmE), / ˈdændi ˌfiːvə / (BrE)
an infectious tropical disease transmitted by mosquitoes and marked by fever, rash, and pain in the head and joints; another name for dengue fever

Examples

Dandy fever disease
Early tropical medicine texts often used the term dandy fever for what is now called dengue fever.
Joint pain and skin rashes are common symptoms of dandy fever.
The region reported several cases of dandy fever after the rainy season began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dengue fever - viral tropical disease causing fever and pain, transmitted by mosquitoes
