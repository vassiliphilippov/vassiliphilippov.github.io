Vigna aconitifolia

noun
species of edible legume native to India
Pronunciation: / ˈviːɡnə ˌækəˌnaɪtɪˈfəʊliə / (AmE), / ˈvɪɡnə ˌækəˌnɪtɪˈfəʊlɪə / (BrE)
A species of legume native to India, grown for its edible seeds and used as food, forage, and soil conditioner.
