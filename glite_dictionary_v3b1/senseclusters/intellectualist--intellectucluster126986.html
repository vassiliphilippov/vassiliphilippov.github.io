intellectualist

noun
public thinker or scholar
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who values or engages in intellectual activities, critical thinking, or scholarly debate

Examples

Active intellectualist debate
The intellectualist contributed thoughtful articles to several academic journals.
As an intellectualist, she often participated in public debates about social issues.
Many admired the intellectualist for his deep analysis of political events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intellectual - a person who uses the mind creatively or critically
  • antonym: philistine - a person who is indifferent or hostile to culture and the arts
  • synonym: scholar - a person who studies a subject in great detail, especially at a high level
