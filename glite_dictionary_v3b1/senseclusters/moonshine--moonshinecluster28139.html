moonshine

noun
light from the Moon
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːnˌʃaɪn / (AmE), / ˈmuːnʃaɪn / (BrE)
the light that comes from the Moon, especially as seen at night

Examples

Bright moonshine
The garden was bathed in moonshine that filtered through the trees.

They walked along the beach, guided only by soft moonshine.
