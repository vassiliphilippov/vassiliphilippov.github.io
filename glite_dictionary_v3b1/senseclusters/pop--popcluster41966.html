pop

verb
hit a baseball in a high, easily caught way
4.1 5.5
Pronunciation: /pɑːp/ (AmE), /pɒp/ (BrE)
(baseball) to hit a ball high into the air but not far, so it is easy to catch.

Examples

To pop a fly ball
He didn't want to pop another easy fly ball during the game.
In the ninth inning, she accidentally popped it to center field and was caught out.
The batter managed to pop the ball straight up to the catcher.

Related Terms
