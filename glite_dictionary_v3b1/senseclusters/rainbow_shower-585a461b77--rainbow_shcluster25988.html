rainbow shower

noun
hybrid ornamental tree with colorful flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪnˌboʊ ˈʃaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈreɪnbəʊ ˈʃaʊ.ə / (BrE)
A deciduous ornamental hybrid tree of southeastern Asia and Hawaii, noted for its racemes of cream, orange, or red flowers.

Examples

The rainbow shower tree
The rainbow shower is prized in Hawaiian landscapes for its stunning multicolored blossoms.
Tourists admired the rainbow shower trees lining the boulevard, each covered in vibrant flowers.
We planted a rainbow shower in our garden, and it flourished in the summer heat.
