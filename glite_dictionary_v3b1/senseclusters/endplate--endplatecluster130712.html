endplate

noun
terminal region of a motor neuron
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɛndˌpleɪt / (AmE), / ˈɛndˌpleɪt / (BrE)
the flattened terminal region of a motor neuron that transmits neural impulses to a muscle fiber

Examples

Motor endplate region
Damage to the endplate can result in impaired muscle function.
Researchers studied the structure of the endplate in different muscle types.
The neurotransmitter is released at the endplate during nerve impulse transmission.
