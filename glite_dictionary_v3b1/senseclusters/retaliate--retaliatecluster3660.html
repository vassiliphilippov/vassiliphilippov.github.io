Synonyms

  • get back at
    5.6 1.58/m
    get back at is an informal way to punish someone for a wrong, while retaliate is more neutral and formal.
  • get even
    5.4 1.64/m
    get even is a casual phrase meaning to settle a score against someone, while retaliate sounds more formal and general.
  • pay back
    3.2 0.33/m
    pay back is often used for revenge or repayment, while retaliate is mainly a formal word for returning harm.
  • pay (someone) back in (their) own coin
    8.3 0.00/m
    pay (someone) back in (their) own coin means treating someone the same bad way they used, while retaliate is any return of harm.
  • avenge
    4.9 2.90/m
    avenge is to punish a wrong often on behalf of someone else, while retaliate is a personal act of returning harm.
  • fight back
    3.3 7.42/m
