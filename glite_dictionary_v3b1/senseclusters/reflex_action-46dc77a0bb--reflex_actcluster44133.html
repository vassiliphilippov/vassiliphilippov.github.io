reflex action

noun
automatic unlearned response to stimulus
Pronunciation: / ˈriː.flɛks ˈæk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈriː.flɛks ˈæk.ʃən / (BrE)
an automatic, unlearned response to a stimulus, occurring without conscious thought

Examples

A reflex action response
A baby's sucking motion when fed is a natural reflex action.
The doctor tested his knee jerk reflex action with a small hammer.
Touching a hot surface triggers a reflex action that pulls your hand away instantly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reflex - an automatic physical response to a stimulus
