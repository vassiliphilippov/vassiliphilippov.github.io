tarn

noun
mountain lake, glacial origin
8.8
Pronunciation: / tɑrn / (AmE), / tɑːn / (BrE)
A small mountain lake, especially one formed by glaciers.

Examples

A tarn lake
After the glacier retreated, it left behind a deep tarn filled with clear water.
Mist hung low over the surface of the tarn at sunrise, creating a mysterious atmosphere.
The hikers stopped to rest beside the tranquil tarn nestled in the valley.
