keypunch

noun
data punching machine
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːˌpʌntʃ / (AmE), / ˈkiːˌpʌntʃ / (BrE)
a machine that creates holes in cards or paper tape to record data for computers or tabulating equipment

Examples

Keypunch machine
The technician demonstrated how the keypunch was used to prepare data cards for the computer.
Before digital input, workers relied on the keypunch to process large amounts of information.
She learned to operate the keypunch as part of her first job in data entry.
