shamanize

verb
practice shamanism; act as a shaman
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑməˌnaɪz /; / ˈʃæməˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːmənaɪz / (BrE)
to practice shamanism or act as a shaman, especially by entering altered states to interact with the spirit world

Examples

She traveled to Mongolia to learn how to shamanise from local spiritual leaders.
