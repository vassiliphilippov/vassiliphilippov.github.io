zigzag

adjective, adverb
having sharp turns or angles
5.5 3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈzɪɡˌzæɡ / (AmE), / ˈzɪɡˌzæɡ / (BrE)
having or moving in a pattern with sharp turns or angles, from side to side (in a zigzag way)
