screener

noun
software tool that filters data or content
5.1 0.14/m
Pronunciation: / c8skrid0n59r / (AmE), / c8skrid0n59 / (BrE)
a software tool or online service that screens, filters, or sorts data, items, or content according to user-defined criteria.

Examples

Stock screener
The finance app offers a screener that filters stocks by market cap and sector.
The clinic uses an online screener to decide which patients need immediate testing and care.
Journalists used a candidate screener to filter social media posts that mentioned the scandal.

Synonyms

  • filterer
    5.3 0.00/m
    a filterer removes unwanted material or information through a physical or simple filter, while a screener is software that sorts content by user rules.

Related Terms

  • synonym: filter - a tool or process that removes unwanted items from a collection
  • synonym: screen - to examine or check items and separate those that meet particular criteria
