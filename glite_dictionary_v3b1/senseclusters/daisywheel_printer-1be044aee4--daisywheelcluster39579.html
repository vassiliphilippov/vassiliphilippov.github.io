daisywheel printer

noun
printer using a rotating daisy-shaped wheel
8.9 7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈdeɪ.zi.wiːl ˈprɪn.tər/ (AmE), /ˈdeɪ.zi.wiːl ˈprɪn.tə/ (BrE)
