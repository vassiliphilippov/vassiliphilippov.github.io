sergeant fish

noun
large dark-striped tropical fish
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑrdʒənt fɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɑːdʒənt fɪʃ / (BrE)
a large, dark-striped tropical fish found worldwide in coastal to open waters, valued both as food and as a game fish, related to remoras

Examples

A sergeant fish species
Fishermen in tropical regions often catch sergeant fish for both sport and food.
The sergeant fish is easily recognized by its bold, dark stripes running along its body.
We spotted a school of sergeant fish swimming near the coral reef.
