fantasticalness

noun
quality of being fantastical or whimsical
Pronunciation: / fænˈtæstɪkəlnəs / (AmE), / fanˈtastɪkəlnəs / (BrE)
The state or quality of being fantastical; fancifulness or whimsicality, especially in imagination or appearance.

Examples

The fantasticalness of stories
Critics admired the fantasticalness that infused his writing with vivid and surreal images.
She decorated her room with a fantasticalness that reflected her imaginative nature.
The fantasticalness of the play made it a favorite among both children and adults.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fancifulness - the quality of being imaginative or unreal
  • synonym: fantasy - imaginative fiction involving magic or the supernatural; the faculty of imagining
  • antonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist
