Caricaceae

noun
family of tropical trees and shrubs
Pronunciation: / ˌkæɹɪˈkeɪsiˌi /; / ˌkɛrɪˈkeɪsiˌi / (AmE), / ˌkærɪˈkeɪsɪˌiː /; / ˌkærɪˈkeɪsiːiː / (BrE)
a family of tropical trees and shrubs, native to America and Africa, with milky sap and large, palmately lobed leaves (botany)
