unsatisfying

adjective
not giving satisfaction
1.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsætɪsfaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈsætɪsfaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
Not giving satisfaction or failing to meet expectations.

Examples

An unsatisfying ending
After reading the book, he felt the plot was somewhat unsatisfying.
She found her new job unsatisfying because it lacked creative challenges.
The ending of the movie was quite unsatisfying and left many viewers disappointed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disappointing - failing to fulfill hopes or expectations
