Miwok

noun
a Native American people of central California
Pronunciation: / ˈmiː.wɑːk / (AmE), / ˈmiː.wɒk / (BrE)
A member of a Native American people traditionally living in the central Sierra Nevada region of California.

Examples

A Miwok person
Many Miwok descendants still live in the region today.
She is studying the culture and traditions of the Miwok.
The Miwok built their villages along the rivers in what is now California.
