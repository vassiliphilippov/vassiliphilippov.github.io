unintegrated

adjective
not incorporated into a larger group
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈɪntəˌɡreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈɪntɪˌɡreɪtɪd / (BrE)
not joined or incorporated into a larger group; separated or kept apart from others or from the main group.

Examples

Unintegrated communities
Many minority communities felt unintegrated within the broader society.
The committee remained unintegrated, with each member working separately on their tasks.
The museum's collection is still largely unintegrated with little effort to connect the exhibits.

Related Terms

  • antonym: integrated - combined into a whole or made part of a larger group
  • synonym: segregated - separated or set apart from others or from the majority group
