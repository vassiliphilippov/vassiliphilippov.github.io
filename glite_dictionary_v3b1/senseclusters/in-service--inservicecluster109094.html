in-service

adjective
currently employed
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn ˌsɜrvɪs / (AmE), / ˈɪn ˌsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
currently employed or actively serving in a job, position, or official duty, especially in public services or the military

Examples

In-service police officers
The school offers special training programs for in-service teachers to improve their skills.
All in-service police officers must complete annual fitness tests.
The government announced a pay raise for in-service military personnel this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: on duty - currently working or performing one's job
  • antonym: retired - no longer working after leaving a job or career
