Zygomycotina

noun
division of fungi producing zygospores
Pronunciation: / zaɪˌɡoʊmaɪkoʊˈtaɪnə /; / zaɪɡoʊmaɪkoʊˈtaɪnə / (AmE), / zaɪˌɡəʊmaɪkəʊˈtɪnə /; / zaɪɡəʊmaɪkəʊˈtiːnə / (BrE)
a former division of fungi characterized by the production of zygospores (now mostly classified within the phylum Mucoromycota)
