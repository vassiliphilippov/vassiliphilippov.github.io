mossie

noun
slang for mosquito
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɒsi / (BrE)
an informal term for a mosquito, especially used in Australian English

Examples

A mossie bite
We had to use repellent to keep the mossies away at night.
After the rain, there were so many mossies buzzing around the campsite.
She woke up with several bites from mossies on her arms and legs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mosquito - a small flying insect that bites and sucks blood
