confrontational

adjective
aggressive or argumentative manner
Pronunciation: / kənˌfrʌn.t̬əˈneɪ.ʃən.əl / (AmE), / kənˌfrʌn.təˈneɪ.ʃən.əl / (BrE)
tending to deal with situations in an aggressive or argumentative way, rather than avoiding conflict

Examples

A confrontational attitude
Her confrontational approach to management often led to heated debates in the office.
Some people appreciate a direct style, but others find him too confrontational.
The politician became increasingly confrontational during the televised debate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aggressive - ready or likely to attack or confront; characterized by aggression
  • antonym: nonconfrontational - not inclined to confrontation; avoiding conflict
