firebird

noun
a brightly colored tropical American bird
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərˌbɝd /; / ˈfaɪɚˌbɝd / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəbɜːd /; / ˈfaɪəˌbɜːd / (BrE)
a brightly colored bird, especially one with vivid red or fiery plumage (such as some tropical American birds)
