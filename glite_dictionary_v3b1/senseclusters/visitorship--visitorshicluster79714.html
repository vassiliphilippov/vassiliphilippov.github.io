visitorship

noun
office of a Visitor
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪzɪtərʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈvɪzɪtəʃɪp / (BrE)
the office, authority, or period of service of a Visitor, especially one who inspects or oversees an institution such as a university or charity

Examples

University visitorship
During his visitorship, he reviewed the university's financial records and policies.
The college made several important changes under her visitorship.
His visitorship was marked by a commitment to transparency and fairness in administration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inspectorship - the office or position of an inspector
  • synonym: tenure - the period during which someone holds a position or office
