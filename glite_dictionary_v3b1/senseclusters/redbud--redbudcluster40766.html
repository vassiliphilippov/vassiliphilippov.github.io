redbud

noun
a North American tree with pink flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌbʌd / (AmE), / ˈrɛdbʌd / (BrE)
a small tree of eastern North America, similar to the Judas tree, that usually has pink flowers and grows in damp, sheltered woodlands

Examples

The redbud tree
Each spring, the redbud covers itself in a blanket of vibrant pink blossoms.
The forest was dotted with clusters of redbud trees in full bloom.
We planted a redbud in our backyard to attract more butterflies and bees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Judas tree - a small Eurasian tree with pink flowers, related to the redbud
