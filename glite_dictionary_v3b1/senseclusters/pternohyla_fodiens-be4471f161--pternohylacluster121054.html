Pternohyla fodiens

noun
burrowing nocturnal frog species of U.S. southwest
9.9
Pronunciation: / ptɜːr.noʊˈhaɪ.lə ˈfoʊ.di.ɛnz / (AmE), / ptɜː.nəˈhaɪ.lə ˈfəʊ.di.ɛnz / (BrE)
A species of burrowing nocturnal frog native to the southwestern United States, characterized by a very hard upper surface of the head and found in grassy areas and scrub forests.
