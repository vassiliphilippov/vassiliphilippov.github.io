signable

adjective
able to be signed
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪnəbəl / (AmE), / ˈsaɪnəbəl / (BrE)
able to be signed or suitable for signing, especially referring to documents or contracts

Examples

Signable contract
The agreement is signable after all parties have reviewed the terms.
Please ensure the document is signable before sending it to the client.
Only signable forms will be accepted by the admissions office.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unsigned - not having been signed
  • synonym: valid - legally acceptable or officially approved
