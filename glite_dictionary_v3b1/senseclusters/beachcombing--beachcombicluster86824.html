beachcombing

verb
to search beach for items
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtʃˌkoʊmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbiːtʃˌkəʊmɪŋ / (BrE)
to walk along a beach searching for useful or interesting items such as shells, sea glass, or driftwood

Examples

To go beachcombing
They spent the afternoon beachcombing and found several beautiful shells.
On vacation, he enjoyed beachcombing for sea glass and unusual stones.
After the storm, many people went beachcombing to see what had washed ashore.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collecting - gathering items of interest or value
  • synonym: scavenging - searching for and collecting discarded or useful items
