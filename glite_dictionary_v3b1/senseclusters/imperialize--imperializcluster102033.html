imperialize

verb
to make imperial
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɪriəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɪəriəlaɪz / (BrE)
to give something the appearance, style, or character of an empire, making it grand or imperial in form or decoration

Examples

To imperialize a building
The architect wanted to imperialize the building by adding grand arches and marble statues.
They decided to imperialize the ceremony with elaborate decorations and regal music.
The artist's latest work attempts to imperialize everyday objects, giving them a majestic look.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embellish - to make something more attractive by adding decorative details
  • antonym: simplify - to make something plainer or less complex
