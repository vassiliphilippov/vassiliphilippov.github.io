sandboard

verb
to ride down sand dunes
Pronunciation: / ˈsændˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈsændˌbɔːd / (BrE)
to slide down sand dunes on a board, similar to snowboarding but on sand.

Examples

To sandboard down dunes
We decided to sandboard down the tallest dune at the desert park.
Tourists often sandboard for fun when visiting the coastal dunes.
She learned how to sandboard during her vacation in Morocco.

Related Terms

  • antonym: climb - to go up or ascend, especially a slope or hill
  • synonym: snowboard - to ride down snow-covered slopes on a board
