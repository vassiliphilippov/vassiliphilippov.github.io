Mahonia aquifolium

noun
evergreen shrub, also called Oregon grape
9.9
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈhoʊ.niː.ə æk.wɪ.ˈfoʊ.liː.əm/ (AmE), /mə.ˈhəʊ.niː.ə æk.wɪ.ˈfəʊ.liː.əm/ (BrE)
