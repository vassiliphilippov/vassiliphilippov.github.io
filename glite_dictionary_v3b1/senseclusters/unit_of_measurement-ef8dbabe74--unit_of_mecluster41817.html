unit of measurement

noun
fixed standard quantity for measurement
Pronunciation: / ˈjuː.nɪt əv ˈmɛʒ.ər.mənt / (AmE), / ˈjuː.nɪt əv ˈmɛʒ.ə.mənt / (BrE)
a fixed quantity used as a standard for measuring things like length, mass, or volume
