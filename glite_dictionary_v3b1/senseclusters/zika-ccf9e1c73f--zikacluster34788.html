Zika

noun
mosquito-borne virus
4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈzɪ.kə/ /; / /ˈziː.kə/ (AmE), /ˈziː.kə/ /; / /ˈzɪ.kə/ (BrE)
a virus transmitted by mosquitoes that can cause fever, rash, and other symptoms, sometimes leading to birth defects

Examples

Zika virus infection

Pregnant women are advised to avoid areas where Zika is spreading rapidly.
Researchers are working to develop a vaccine to prevent Zika infections.
