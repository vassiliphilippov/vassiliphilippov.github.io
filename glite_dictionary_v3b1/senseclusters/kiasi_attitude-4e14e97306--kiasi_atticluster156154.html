kiasi attitude

noun
risk-averse or overly cautious attitude
9.9
Pronunciation: / kiˈɑːsi ˈætɪˌtjud / (AmE), / kiˈɑːsi ˈatɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
an excessively cautious or risk-averse outlook; a tendency to avoid taking risks

Examples

A kiasi attitude to risk
Her kiasi attitude meant she always took the safest path, even if it meant missing opportunities.
Many entrepreneurs warn that a kiasi attitude can hinder success in a competitive market.
The team's kiasi attitude prevented them from innovating and trying bold new ideas.

Related Terms

  • antonym: recklessness - the state of taking risks without concern for the outcome
  • synonym: risk aversion - the tendency to avoid taking risks
