cast of characters

noun
group of actors in a production
Pronunciation: / kæst əv ˈkɛrɪktɚz / (AmE), / kɑːst əv ˈkærɪktəz / (BrE)
the group of actors who perform in a play, movie, or other dramatic work

Examples

A cast of characters in a play
A talented cast of characters brought new life to the classic play.
She was impressed by the diversity in the cast of characters for the upcoming film.
The cast of characters took their final bows at the end of the performance.
