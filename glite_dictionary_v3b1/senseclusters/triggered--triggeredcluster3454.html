triggered

adjective
emotionally upset; offended (slang)
8.6
Pronunciation: / c8tr6a615a / (AmE), / c8tr6a6159 / (BrE)
(informal, slang) emotionally upset, offended, or distressed by something, often a reminder of a past trauma; used especially in online or conversational contexts.

Examples

Feeling triggered emotionally
The debate left many students feeling triggered and unable to continue calmly.
She felt unexpectedly triggered when the movie showed a scene from her past.
Online threads quickly became hostile, and several users claimed they were triggered.

Related Terms

  • synonym: offended - feeling hurt or insulted by something said or done
  • antonym: unaffected - not emotionally changed or disturbed by something
  • synonym: upset - emotionally disturbed or distressed
