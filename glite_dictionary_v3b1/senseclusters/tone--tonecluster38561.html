Synonyms

  • timbre

    timbre is the sound quality that makes instruments or voices sound different, while tone also includes pitch and character.
  • quality
    6.0 6.17/m
    quality in music means the timbre that marks a voice or note, while tone adds pitch and overall expressive character.
  • pitch
    3.7 20/m
    pitch is how high or low a sound seems to the ear, while tone includes pitch plus sound colour and character.
  • tenor
    5.8 1.90/m
    tenor means the general meaning or sense of speech or writing, while tone focuses on sound, voice, or emotional character.
  • note
    3.7 24/m
    note is a small expressive detail in voice or writing, while tone refers to the overall sound, pitch, or character.

  • degree lower: semitone - a musical interval of half a tone
  • synonym: timbre - the character or quality of a musical sound or voice
