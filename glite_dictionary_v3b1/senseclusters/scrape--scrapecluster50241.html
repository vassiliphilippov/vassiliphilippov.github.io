scrape

verb
to make or inscribe by scratching
5.4
Pronunciation: / skreɪp / (AmE), / skreɪp / (BrE)
to form or inscribe (something) by scratching or rubbing a surface, often with a sharp or hard object

Examples

To scrape a pattern
He carefully scraped a simple pattern into the wooden bench with his pocketknife.
The children scraped their initials into the wet cement sidewalk.
Years ago, people would scrape warnings onto cave walls to alert others of danger.

Related Terms

  • synonym: engrave - to cut or carve a design or words into a hard surface
