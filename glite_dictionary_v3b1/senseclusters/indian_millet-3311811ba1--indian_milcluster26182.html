Indian millet

noun
forage grass of North America
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən ˈmɪlɪt /; / ˈɪndiən ˈmɪlət / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈmɪlɪt / (BrE)
a drought-tolerant grass grown in dry regions, used as animal feed or as a cereal crop
