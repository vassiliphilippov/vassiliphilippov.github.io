squidgy

adjective
soft and wet
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɪdʒi / (AmE), / ˈskwɪdʒi / (BrE)
soft, wet, and easily squeezed or squashed

Examples

The squidgy grass
After the rain, the children loved jumping on the squidgy grass in the park.
She pressed her finger into the squidgy cake to check if it was baked properly.
The clay was still squidgy and easy to shape into different forms.

Related Terms

  • antonym: firm - solid and not easily pressed or squashed
  • synonym: soggy - wet and soft, often unpleasantly so
