lamellar mixture

noun
mixture with substances in layers
3.9 8.4
Pronunciation: /lə.ˈmel.ər ˈmɪks.tʃər/ (AmE), /lə.ˈmel.ə ˈmɪks.tʃə/ (BrE)
a mixture in which the substances are arranged in distinct, parallel layers rather than being fully combined.

Examples

A lamellar mixture layer
In a lamellar mixture, each component forms a separate, visible layer within the material.
Microscopists identified several lamellar mixtures in the sample, indicating incomplete blending of ingredients.
The product failed quality control due to the presence of a persistent lamellar mixture instead of a homogeneous blend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heterogeneous mixture - a mixture in which the components are not uniformly distributed
  • antonym: homogeneous mixture - a mixture with uniform composition throughout
