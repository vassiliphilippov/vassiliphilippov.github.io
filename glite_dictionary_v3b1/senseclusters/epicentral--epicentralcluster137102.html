epicentral

adjective
at earthquake epicenter
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈsɛntrəl / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈsɛntrəl / (BrE)
relating to or located at the epicenter of an earthquake

Examples

Epicentral area
The epicentral region suffered the most severe damage after the earthquake struck.
Scientists measured the intensity of shaking in the epicentral zone to assess the quake's impact.
Rescue teams focused their efforts on the epicentral area where buildings had collapsed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: epicenter - the point on the Earth's surface directly above an earthquake's origin
