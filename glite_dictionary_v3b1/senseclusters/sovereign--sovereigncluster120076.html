sovereign

adjective
independent and self-governing (of a state)
5.1 5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈsɑː.vər.ɪn/ /; / /ˈsɑː.vər.ən/ (AmE), /ˈsɒv.ər.ɪn/ /; / /ˈsɒv.ər.ən/ (BrE)
(of a state or political body) having independent authority and not being controlled by any other state or external power.

Examples

Sovereign state
Some regions are seeking to become sovereign entities, independent from central government control.
Switzerland is known as a sovereign nation, with full control over its internal affairs.
The new constitution reaffirmed the country's status as a sovereign state.

Related Terms
