fax

noun
electronic document transmission
2.3
Pronunciation: / fæks / (AmE), / fæks / (BrE)
a document sent electronically over a telephone line and printed on paper at the receiving end

Examples

A fax message
Please send the signed contract by fax as soon as possible.
The company still uses faxes to communicate with some international partners.
He received a fax confirming his hotel reservation for the conference.

Related Terms

  • antonym: email - a message sent electronically from one computer to another
  • synonym: facsimile - an exact copy or reproduction, especially of a document
