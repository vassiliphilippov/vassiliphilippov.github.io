and

conjunction
sentence connector
Pronunciation: / ænd / (AmE), / ænd / (BrE)
used at the beginning of a sentence to add a new comment or continue a narrative in speech or writing

Examples

And then she said
And then she realized she had left her keys at home.
And what surprised me most was how quickly the story changed tone in the final act.
And finally, I would like to thank everyone for their support.

Related Terms

  • synonym: also - used to add information or continue a point
  • antonym: but - used to introduce a contrast or exception
