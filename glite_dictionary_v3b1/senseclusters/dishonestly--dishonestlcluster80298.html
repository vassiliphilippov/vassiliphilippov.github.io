dishonestly

adverb
in a deceitful or untruthful way
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈɑːnəstli / (AmE), / dɪsˈɒnɪstli / (BrE)
in a way that is intended to deceive or cheat others; in a corrupt or untruthful manner

Examples

To act dishonestly
He obtained the promotion dishonestly, without regard for fairness or integrity.
She dishonestly altered the figures in the report to cover up her mistake.
The politician was accused of acting dishonestly during the contract negotiations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deceitfully - in a way that is intended to deceive others
  • antonym: honestly - in a truthful or sincere way
