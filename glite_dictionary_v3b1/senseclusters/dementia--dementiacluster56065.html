dementia

noun
serious mental deterioration (medical condition)
2.2 5.5
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈmen.ʃiː.ə/ /; / /dɪ.ˈmen.ʃə/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈmen.ʃə/ /; / /dɪ.ˈmen.ʃiː.ə/ (BrE)
