jejunoileitis

noun
inflammation of parts of the small intestine
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒəˌdʒunoʊˌɪliˈaɪtɪs / (AmE), / dʒɪˌdʒuːnəʊˌɪliˈaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the jejunum and the ileum, which are parts of the small intestine

Examples

Jejunoileitis inflammation
Crohn's disease can sometimes present as jejunoileitis in affected individuals.
Long-term jejunoileitis may lead to complications such as malabsorption and intestinal obstruction.
