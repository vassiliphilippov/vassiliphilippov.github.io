ontologist

noun
ontology specialist
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɑnˈtɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ɒnˈtɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a specialist who designs, builds, or maintains ontologies, especially in computer science and artificial intelligence

Examples

A computer ontologist
The company hired an ontologist to organize its vast database of information.
As an ontologist, she collaborates with software developers to improve data integration.
An experienced ontologist can help make complex information easier to search and analyze.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knowledge engineer - a person who develops knowledge-based systems
  • synonym: taxonomist - a person who classifies things, especially in science
