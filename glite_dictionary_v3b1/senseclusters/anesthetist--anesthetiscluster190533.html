anesthetist

noun
medical specialist giving anesthesia
Pronunciation: / əˈnɛsθətɪst /; / əˈnisθətɪst / (AmE), / əˈniːsθətɪst / (BrE)
A medical professional trained to administer anesthetics to patients undergoing surgical or other medical procedures.

Examples

The anesthetist administers anesthesia
Every anesthetist must ensure the patient is fully prepared for anesthesia.
The anesthetist monitored the patient's vital signs during surgery.
You should discuss any allergies with the anesthetist before your operation.
Before surgery, the anaesthetist explained the steps of the procedure to the family.
Every hospital requires a skilled anaesthetist as part of the surgical team.
The anaesthetist carefully monitored the patient's vital signs during the operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anesthesiologist - a doctor specializing in anesthesia and perioperative medicine
