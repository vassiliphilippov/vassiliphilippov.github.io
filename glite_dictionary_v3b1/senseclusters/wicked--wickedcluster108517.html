wicked

adjective
mischievously playful
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪkɪd / (AmE), / ˈwɪkɪd / (BrE)
Behaving in a naughtily or annoyingly playful way; mischievous or impish.

Examples

Wicked grin
His wicked grin let everyone know he was up to no good.
She told a wicked joke that had the whole class giggling.
The children played a wicked prank on their babysitter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impish - inclined to do mildly naughty things for fun
  • synonym: mischievous - causing or showing a fondness for causing trouble in a playful way
