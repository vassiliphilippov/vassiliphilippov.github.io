chip

verb
hit a short shot (sports, esp. golf)
Pronunciation: / tʃɪp / (AmE), / tʃɪp / (BrE)
(in golf and certain other sports) to hit a ball with a short, light stroke so that it rises into the air and lands quickly, especially near the hole or target.

Examples

To chip the ball
After missing the fairway, he had to chip over a sand trap to reach the hole.
He expertly chipped the puck over the goalie and into the net.
She managed to chip the ball onto the green from a tricky position.

Related Terms

  • synonym: putt - to hit a golf ball gently along the ground towards the hole
