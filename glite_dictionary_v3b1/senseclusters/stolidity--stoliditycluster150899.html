stolidity

noun
lack of emotional response
9.9 8.0
Pronunciation: /stə.ˈlɪ.dɪ.tɪ/ /; / /stɑː.ˈlɪ.dɪ.tɪ/ (AmE), /stə.ˈlɪ.dɪ.tɪ/ /; / /stɒ.ˈlɪ.dɪ.tɪ/ (BrE)
