redfish

noun
a North Atlantic rockfish
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈrɛd.fɪʃ / (BrE)
a fish with reddish coloring, called redfish (can refer to several different species, such as North Atlantic rockfish, a male salmon after spawning, or a large edible fish off the US coast)
