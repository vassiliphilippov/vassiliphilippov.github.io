diner

noun
restaurant resembling a dining car
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈdaɪnə / (BrE)
A small, usually inexpensive restaurant, often resembling a railway dining car, that serves simple meals quickly.

Examples

A roadside diner
Late at night, the only place still open was a small diner near the highway.
The classic American diner often features chrome decor and a long counter with stools.
We stopped at a roadside diner for breakfast during our road trip.

  • synonym: restaurant - an establishment where meals are served to customers
