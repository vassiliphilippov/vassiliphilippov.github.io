mourning dove

noun
a type of North American wild dove
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrnɪŋ ˌdoʊv / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnɪŋ ˌdʌv / (BrE)
A North American wild dove (Zenaida macroura) known for its soft, mournful cooing call.
