barf bag

noun
bag for vomiting, usually while traveling
9.1
Pronunciation: / bɑrf bæɡ / (AmE), / bɑːf bæɡ / (BrE)
a bag provided for use in case someone needs to vomit, typically while traveling

Examples

A barf bag on plane
During the turbulent flight, several passengers reached for the barf bag in their seat pockets.
The flight attendant quickly handed him a barf bag when he started feeling nauseous.
You should take a barf bag with you if you get carsick easily.
