comprende

verb
to understand (Spanish)
Pronunciation: / kəmˈprɛndeɪ / (AmE), / kəmˈprɛndeɪ / (BrE)
used in English, from Spanish, to mean 'do you understand?', often for emphasis or effect.

Examples

Listen carefully and follow the instructions, comprende?
