Polyporus squamosus

noun
a bracket fungus with a scaly cap
9.9
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈlɪp.ə.rəs skwə.ˈmoʊ.səs/ (AmE), /pɒ.ˈlɪp.ə.rəs skwə.ˈməʊ.səs/ (BrE)
A widely distributed bracket fungus of the Northern Hemisphere, with a scaly cap that turns nearly black when mature, sometimes with a lateral stalk.
