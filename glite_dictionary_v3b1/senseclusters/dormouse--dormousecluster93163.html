dormouse

noun
small squirrel-like rodent that hibernates
6.9 0.15/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd…îrÀåma äs / (AmE), / Ààd…îÀêma äs / (BrE)
a small rodent with a furry tail, resembling a squirrel, found in Europe and Asia, known for becoming inactive during cold weather

Examples

A dormouse in forest
Children listened to a story about a shy dormouse who made friends with forest animals.
In the forest, we spotted a dormouse curled up and sleeping in a tree hollow.
The dormouse becomes torpid during winter, surviving by hibernating for months.
