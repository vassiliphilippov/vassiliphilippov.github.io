lie

verb
be in a horizontal position
Pronunciation: / laɪ / (AmE), / laɪ / (BrE)
to be in a horizontal or resting position, or to be situated in a place

Examples

To lie on the bed
He had lain awake on the bed for hours, staring at the ceiling.
Several coats were lying on the floor by the door.
The cat likes to lie in the sun all afternoon.
After a long day, she decided to lie down for a short nap.
He likes to lie on the grass and watch the clouds float by.
The cat will often lie in the sun near the window.
A beautiful valley lies between the mountains.
The answer lies in careful observation of the data.
The keys lay on the kitchen table all night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recline - to lean or lie back in a relaxed position
  • antonym: sit - to be in a position in which your body is resting on a chair or the ground but not lying
  • antonym: stand - to be in an upright position on one's feet
