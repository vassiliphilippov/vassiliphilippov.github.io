imaginative

adjective
creative; showing originality
4.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmædʒɪnətɪv / (AmE), / ɪˈmædʒɪnətɪv / (BrE)
Having or showing creativity or originality in thought or action; marked by the ability to invent or conceive new ideas.

Examples

An imaginative solution
He came up with an imaginative solution to the budget crisis.
Her imaginative approach to problem-solving impressed everyone on the team.
The book features an imaginative world full of strange and wonderful creatures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: creative - having the ability or power to create
  • synonym: inventive - showing creativity or resourcefulness
  • antonym: uninspired - lacking creativity or originality
