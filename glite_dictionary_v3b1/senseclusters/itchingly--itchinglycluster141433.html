itchingly

adverb
in an itchy manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɪtʃɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈɪtʃɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that causes or relates to an itching sensation

Examples

Itchingly uncomfortable skin
The rash on his skin made him feel itchingly uncomfortable all night.
She waited itchingly for the mosquito bite to stop bothering her.
After wearing the wool sweater, he scratched his neck itchingly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irritatingly - in a way that causes irritation or discomfort
