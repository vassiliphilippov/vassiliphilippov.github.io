Sphyrnidae

noun
family of hammerhead and bonnethead sharks
9.9
Pronunciation: / sfɪrˈnɪdiː / (AmE), / sfɪəˈnɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of sharks including the hammerhead and bonnethead sharks, characterized by distinctive, laterally extended heads.

Examples

The Sphyrnidae family
Many species within the Sphyrnidae family inhabit warm coastal waters around the world.
Scientists have studied Sphyrnidae to better understand shark evolution and behavior.
The Sphyrnidae family is easily recognized by the unique shape of their heads.
