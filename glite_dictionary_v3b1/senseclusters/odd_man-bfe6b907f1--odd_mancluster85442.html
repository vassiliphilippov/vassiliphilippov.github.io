odd man

noun
outsider in group
Pronunciation: / ɑd mæn / (AmE), / ɒd mæn / (BrE)
a person who is different from or does not fit in with others in a group

Examples

The odd man out
He always felt like the odd man at family gatherings.
In the new school, she became the odd man until she made friends.
During the meeting, Mark realized he was the odd man with a different opinion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: insider - a person who is accepted as part of a group
  • synonym: misfit - a person who does not fit in with others
  • synonym: outsider - a person who does not belong to a particular group
