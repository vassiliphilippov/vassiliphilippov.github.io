raspatory

noun
coarse shaping tool
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈræspəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈrɑːspətəri / (BrE)
a coarse file or rasp used for shaping or smoothing wood, metal, or other hard materials

Examples

Wood raspatory
The carpenter used a raspatory to smooth the rough edges of the wooden beam.
After shaping the metal, she finished the surface with a raspatory for a finer texture.
A raspatory is essential for detailed work in both woodworking and metalworking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: file - a tool with a rough surface for smoothing or shaping
  • synonym: rasp - a coarse file with sharp teeth for shaping materials
