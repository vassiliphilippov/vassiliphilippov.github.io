parity

noun
state of being equal
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛr.ɪ.ti / (AmE), / ˈpar.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the state or condition of being equal, especially in terms of status, value, or function

Examples

Gender parity
Currency parity was reached after months of economic negotiations.
The government is working to achieve parity between rural and urban schools.
There is still no parity in pay between men and women in many industries.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disparity - a great difference; lack of equality
  • synonym: equality - the state of being equal, especially in status, rights, or opportunities
