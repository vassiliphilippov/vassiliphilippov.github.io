Bartlett

proper noun
pear variety
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑrtlɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɑːtlɪt / (BrE)
a variety of pear with smooth yellow or green skin, also known as the Williams pear

Examples

Bartlett (Williams) pear
She sliced a ripe Bartlett for the fruit salad.
The Bartlett is popular for its sweet flavor and juicy texture.
Farmers harvested crates of Bartlett pears at the end of summer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Anjou - a different variety of pear with green or red skin
  • synonym: Williams pear - another name for the Bartlett pear, especially outside North America
