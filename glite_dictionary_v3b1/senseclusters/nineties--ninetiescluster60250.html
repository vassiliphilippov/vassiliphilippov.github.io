nineties

noun
decade 1990–1999
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪntiːz /; / ˈnaɪn.tiz / (AmE), / ˈnaɪntiz /; / ˈnaɪn.tiːz / (BrE)
the decade from 90 to 99 in a century (for example, 1990 to 1999 or 1890 to 1899)

Examples

The nineties fashion
Fashion trends that were popular in the nineties have made a comeback.
He grew up during the nineties, so he remembers cassette tapes and dial-up internet.
Many popular TV shows from the nineties are still watched today.
Many technological advancements took place during the nineties.
The city underwent rapid growth in the nineties of the nineteenth century.
Writers of the nineties often captured the optimism of the era.
