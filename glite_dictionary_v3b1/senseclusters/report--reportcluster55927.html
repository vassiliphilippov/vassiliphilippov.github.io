Examples

To report the news

Synonyms

  • report on
    3.3 28/m
    report on gives a formal, detailed account by a journalist or official observer, while report names the general act of presenting information.
  • cover
    3.3 47/m
    cover means to report or discuss a subject in journalism or writing, while report focuses on giving official or formal information.
  • account
    3.2 11/m
    account records or describes events in words and numbers, while report often implies an official or job-related presentation of information.
  • describe
    1.0 213/m
    describe gives details about how something looks or works, while report often refers to giving formal information for a job or duty.
