fix

verb
preserve tissue for microscopic study
Pronunciation: / fɪks / (AmE), / fɪks / (BrE)
to kill, preserve, and harden (tissue) in order to prepare it for microscopic examination

Examples

To fix tissue
After harvesting, the technician quickly fixed the tissue in a chemical solution.
It is important to fix the specimen as soon as possible to prevent decay.
The scientist used formalin to fix the tissue samples before analysis.
