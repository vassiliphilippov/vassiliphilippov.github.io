labeling

noun
attaching labels
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪbəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈleɪbəlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of attaching or assigning labels to something, especially to identify or describe it

Examples

Product labeling
The labeling on the package listed all the ingredients clearly for consumers.
Strict labeling regulations require companies to provide nutritional information on food products.
Accurate labeling helps customers choose the right medication at the pharmacy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: marking - the act of making a mark to identify or distinguish something
  • synonym: tagging - the act of attaching tags to something for identification
