decade

noun
ten-year period
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛˌkeɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɛk.eɪd / (BrE)
A period of ten years.

Examples

A decade of years
She spent a decade working on that novel before publishing it.
The city has changed dramatically over the past decade.
Trends in technology often shift rapidly from one decade to the next.
