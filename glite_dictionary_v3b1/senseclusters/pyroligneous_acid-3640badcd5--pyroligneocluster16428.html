pyroligneous acid

noun
liquid from wood distillation
9.9
Pronunciation: /paɪ.rə.ˈlɪg.nɪ.əs ˈæs.əd/ (AmE), /paɪ.rə.ˈlɪg.nɪ.əs ˈæs.ɪd/ (BrE)
