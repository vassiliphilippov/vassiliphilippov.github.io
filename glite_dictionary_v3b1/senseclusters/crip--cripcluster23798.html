crip

noun
slang for disabled person
Pronunciation: / krɪp / (AmE), / krɪp / (BrE)
a slang term for a person with a physical disability, sometimes used in a reclaimed or empowering way within the disability community

Examples

Proud crip activist
She is a proud crip who advocates for disability rights.
Some activists use the word crip to reclaim and empower their identity.
The conference welcomed many crips sharing their experiences and perspectives.
