nonharmonic

adjective
musical note not fitting harmony
5.5
Pronunciation: / nɑn.hɑrˈmɑ.nɪk / (AmE), / nɒn.hɑːˈmɒ.nɪk / (BrE)
(of a note) not fitting into the prevailing harmony of a musical passage; not harmonic.

Examples

Nonharmonic note
He struggled to identify which notes were nonharmonic during the analysis exercise.
That measure features a nonharmonic note that stands out from the chord.
The composer frequently adds nonharmonic tones to create tension in the melody.
