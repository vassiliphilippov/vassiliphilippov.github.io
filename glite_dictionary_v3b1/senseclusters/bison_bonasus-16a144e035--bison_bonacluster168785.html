Bison bonasus

noun
European bison species
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪsən boʊˈneɪsəs / (AmE), / ˌbaɪsən bəˈneɪsəs / (BrE)
The European bison, a large wild bovine native to Europe, distinguished by its smaller and higher-set head compared to the North American bison.
