small

adjective
composed of tiny or fine particles
Pronunciation: / smɔl / (AmE), / smɔːl / (BrE)
(of a substance) consisting of fine or very tiny particles; finely divided.

Examples

Small particles
A small dust cloud rose as the car drove down the dirt road.
The chef sifted a small amount of flour onto the counter before kneading the dough.
This face scrub uses small grains to gently exfoliate your skin.
