soldier of fortune

noun
mercenary fighter
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊldʒɚ əv ˈfɔːrtʃən /; / ˈsoʊldʒər əv ˈfɔrtʃən / (AmE), / ˈsəʊldʒə əv ˈfɔːtʃuːn / (BrE)
a person who fights in wars for money, especially for countries or groups other than their own; a mercenary or adventurer

Examples

As a young man, he dreamed of becoming a soldier of fortune and seeing the world.
He made his living as a soldier of fortune in South America.
The novel tells the story of a soldier of fortune caught between loyalties.
