biracialism

noun
two-race cooperation
7.3
Pronunciation: /baɪ.ˈreɪ.ʃə.lɪ.zəm/ /; / /baɪ.ˈreɪ.ʃəl.ɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /baɪ.ˈreɪ.ʃə.lɪ.zəm/ /; / /baɪ.ˈreɪ.ʃəl.ɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
the belief in or practice of recognizing, cooperating, or sharing power between two racial groups in a society or institution
