unrestricted

adjective
not limited or controlled
1.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /ʌn.rɪ.ˈstrɪk.tɪd/ /; / /ən.rɪ.ˈstrɪk.tɪd/ (AmE), /ʌn.rɪ.ˈstrɪk.tɪd/ /; / /ən.rɪ.ˈstrɪk.tɪd/ (BrE)
