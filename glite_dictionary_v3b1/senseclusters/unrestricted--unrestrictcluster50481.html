unrestricted

adjective
not limited or controlled
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈstrɪktɪd /; / ˌʌn.rɪˈstrɪk.tɪd /; / ʌn.rɪˈstrɪk.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈstrɪktɪd /; / ˌʌn.rɪˈstrɪk.tɪd /; / ʌn.rɪˈstrɪk.tɪd / (BrE)
