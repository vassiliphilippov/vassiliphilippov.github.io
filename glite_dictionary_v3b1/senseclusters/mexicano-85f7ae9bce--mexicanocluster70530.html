Mexicano

noun
Mexican or person of Mexican descent in the US
Pronunciation: / meɪ.hiˈkɑː.noʊ / (AmE), / meɪ.hɪˈkɑː.nəʊ / (BrE)
A Mexican or person of Mexican descent living in the United States, especially in the context of culture and identity.

Examples

A Mexicano in USA
He identifies proudly as a Mexicano and supports local Latino organizations.
Many Mexicanos in Texas celebrate both American and Mexican holidays.
The restaurant is owned and operated by a family of Mexicano heritage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Chicano - An American of Mexican origin or descent, especially one who identifies with a particular political and cultural identity.
