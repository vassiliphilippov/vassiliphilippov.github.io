unexceptionably

adverb
without objection
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪkˈsɛpʃənəbli / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪkˈsɛpʃənəbli / (BrE)
in a way that gives no cause for objection or criticism; perfectly acceptable or unobjectionable

Examples

To act unexceptionably
The committee found the report unexceptionably thorough and approved it without hesitation.
Her conduct during the meeting was unexceptionably professional and respectful.
The contract was written unexceptionably, leaving no room for dispute among the parties.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exceptionably - in a way that gives cause for objection
  • synonym: unobjectionably - in a way that does not cause objection
