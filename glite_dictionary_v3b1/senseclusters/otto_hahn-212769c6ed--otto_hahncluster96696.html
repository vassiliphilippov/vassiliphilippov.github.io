Otto Hahn

proper noun
German chemist, co-discoverer of nuclear fission
7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈɑː.təʊ hɑːn/ /; / /ˈɑː.toʊ hɑːn/ (AmE), /ˈɒt.əʊ hɑːn/ (BrE)
(1879–1968) A German chemist famous for co-discovering nuclear fission, sharing this work with Lise Meitner.
