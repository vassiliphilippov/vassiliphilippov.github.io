non-formal

adjective
casual or unofficial
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈfɔrməl / (AmE), / nɒnˈfɔːməl / (BrE)
not official, strictly organized, or formal; casual or relaxed in style or manner

Examples

Non-formal meeting
The training program offered a non-formal approach to learning new skills.
We prefer a non-formal dress code for our weekly team meetings.
Her non-formal attitude made everyone feel comfortable at the event.

Related Terms

  • antonym: formal - official or strictly organized in style or manner
  • synonym: informal - not formal; relaxed or casual
