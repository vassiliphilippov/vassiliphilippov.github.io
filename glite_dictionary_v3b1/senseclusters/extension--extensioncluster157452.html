extension

noun
an agreed extra period of time
5.3
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɛnʃən / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛnʃən / (BrE)
an additional period of time allowed beyond an original deadline for completing a task or making a payment, usually by agreement

Examples

Extension of a deadline
She was granted an extension on her rent payment after discussing her situation with the landlord.
The company requested an extension to file its year-end reports due to unforeseen delays.
The team needed an extension to finish the project because new requirements were added midway.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deadline - the latest time or date by which something should be completed
  • synonym: deferral - an action of delaying something; postponement
