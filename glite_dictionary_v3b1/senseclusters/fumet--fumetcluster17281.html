fumet

noun
concentrated fish stock
Pronunciation: / fuːˈmeɪ / (AmE), / ˈfjuːmeɪ / (BrE)
a concentrated and flavorful stock, especially made from fish, used to enhance the taste of dishes in cooking

Examples

Fish fumet sauce
The chef prepared a delicate fumet to use as the base for the seafood risotto.
A splash of fumet can bring out the flavors in a fish stew.
She simmered fish bones and vegetables to make a homemade fumet for her recipe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broth - a liquid in which meat, fish, or vegetables have been cooked
  • synonym: stock - a flavored liquid base for soups and sauces
