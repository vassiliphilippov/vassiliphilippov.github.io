Santa Claus

proper noun
legendary figure who brings Christmas gifts
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsæn.tə klɔz / (AmE), / ˈsantə klɔːz /; / ˈsæn.tə klɔːz / (BrE)
a legendary figure who brings gifts to children at Christmas, often depicted as a jolly old man in red
