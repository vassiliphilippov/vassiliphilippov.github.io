scrub

noun
inept person (slang)
Pronunciation: / skrʌb / (AmE), / skrʌb / (BrE)
informal slang: a person regarded as inept, unskilled, or worthless, especially in gaming or competitive situations.

Examples

A gaming scrub
After losing again, he muttered that the opponent was just a scrub.
The coach told the team not to treat the rookie like a scrub after a single mistake.
In online matches, players often taunt newcomers by calling them scrubs to provoke a reaction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: noob - a new or inexperienced player regarded as unskilled (slang)
  • antonym: pro - an experienced, highly skilled player; opposite of a scrub
