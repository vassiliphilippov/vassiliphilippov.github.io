desmoid

adjective
benign fibrous tumor
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛzmɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɛzmɔɪd / (BrE)
relating to a type of benign fibrous tumor that arises in connective tissue

Examples

Desmoid tumor cells
The surgeon explained that the desmoid growth was not cancerous but could still cause problems.
A desmoid tumor may develop in the abdominal wall after surgery or injury.
Doctors monitor desmoid tumors closely because they can grow and affect nearby organs.

Related Terms
