deathmatch

noun
intense confrontation
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛθˌmætʃ / (AmE), / ˈdɛθˌmætʃ / (BrE)
an intense and often violent competition or confrontation between opponents, sometimes used figuratively for fierce contests in sports, politics, or business

Examples

Business deathmatch
The championship game turned into a fierce deathmatch between the two rival teams.
In the world of business, their merger talks became a corporate deathmatch with only one winner.
The video game features a multiplayer deathmatch mode where players compete until only one remains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duel - a contest or fight between two people or groups
  • antonym: friendly - a competition or match without high stakes or hostility
  • synonym: showdown - a decisive confrontation or contest
