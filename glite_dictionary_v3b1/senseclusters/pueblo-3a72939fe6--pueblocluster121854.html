Pueblo

noun
member of a southwestern Native American people
Pronunciation: / ˈpwɛbloʊ / (AmE), / ˈpwɛbləʊ / (BrE)
a member of any of several Native American peoples of the southwestern United States, known for living in villages (pueblos) built of adobe and stone
