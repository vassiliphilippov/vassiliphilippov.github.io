addressee

noun
person to whom something is addressed
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌæd.rɛˈsi / (AmE), / ˌæd.rɛˈsiː / (BrE)
the person to whom something, such as a letter or message, is addressed

Examples

The addressee of a letter
During her speech, she looked directly at the addressee of her remarks.
Please ensure the addressee's name is spelled correctly on the envelope.
The letter was returned to the sender because the addressee had moved away.
