soap pad

noun
abrasive pad containing soap for cleaning
4.6
Pronunciation: / soʊp pæd / (AmE), / səʊp pad / (BrE)
a small abrasive pad that contains soap, used for cleaning tough stains on pots, pans, and other surfaces

Examples

A soap pad for cleaning
If the pan is burned, try cleaning it with a soap pad and hot water.
She always keeps a box of soap pads next to the sink for scrubbing dishes.
The stubborn grease came off easily with a soap pad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scouring pad - a pad used for cleaning, often without soap
