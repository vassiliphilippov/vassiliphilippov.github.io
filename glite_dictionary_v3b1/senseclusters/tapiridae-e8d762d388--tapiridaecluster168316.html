Tapiridae

noun
biological family of tapirs
Pronunciation: / təˈpɪrəˌdiː /; / təˈpɪrɪˌdeɪ / (AmE), / təˈpɪərɪˌdiː / (BrE)
the biological family that includes tapirs and their extinct relatives
