intern

verb
confine or detain, especially during war
9.2 6.9
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈtɜːrn/ /; / /ˈɪn.tɜːrn/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈtɜːn/ /; / /ˈɪn.tɜːn/ (BrE)
