neuroglial cell

noun
supporting cell in the nervous system
3.5 6.1
Pronunciation: /nʊr.oʊ.ˈɡlaɪ.əl sɛl/ /; / /njʊr.oʊ.ˈɡlaɪ.əl sɛl/ (AmE), /njʊə.rə.ˈɡlaɪ.əl sɛl/ /; / /njuː.rə.ˈɡlaɪ.əl sɛl/ (BrE)
a cell in the nervous system that supports and protects neurons, forming the neuroglia

Examples

A neuroglial cell supports neurons
A neuroglial cell helps to maintain the chemical environment around nerve cells.
Damage to neuroglial cells can impair the central nervous system's ability to repair itself.
The brain contains millions of neuroglial cells that support neuron function.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glial cell - a cell of neuroglia; synonym for neuroglial cell
  • antonym: neuron - a nerve cell that transmits electrical impulses
