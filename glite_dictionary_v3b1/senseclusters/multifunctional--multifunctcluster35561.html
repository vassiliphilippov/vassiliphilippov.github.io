multifunctional

noun
device with many uses
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈfʌŋkʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈfʌŋkʃənəl / (BrE)
a device or product that can perform several different functions or tasks.

Examples

A multifunctional printer
We decided to buy a multifunctional for the office to save space and money.
The new multifunctional prints, scans, and copies documents quickly.
Many homes now have a multifunctional instead of separate printers and scanners.

Related Terms

  • synonym: all-in-one - a device that combines multiple functions in one unit
  • antonym: single-purpose - a device designed for only one function
