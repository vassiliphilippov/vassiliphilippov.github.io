commander

noun
MTG deck leader (Commander format)
8.6
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈmæn.dər/ (AmE), /kə.ˈmɑːn.də/ /; / /kə.ˈmæn.də/ (BrE)
(in the trading card game Magic: The Gathering) the legendary creature or card chosen to lead a player's deck in the Commander format; it begins the game in the command zone and imposes special deck-building and gameplay rules.

Examples

Magic commander card
In casual play, your commander is the legendary creature you build around.
She moved her commander to the command zone before the tournament began.
A commander's color identity determines which cards may be included in that deck.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leader - a person or thing that leads or directs others; the card that leads a Commander deck
  • synonym: legendary creature - a card type in Magic: The Gathering representing a unique character; often used as a commander in the Commander format
