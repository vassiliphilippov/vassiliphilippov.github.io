retreat

noun
a quiet, private place
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtrit / (AmE), / rɪˈtriːt / (BrE)
a quiet or secluded place where you can relax or be alone

Examples

Many people dream of having a seaside retreat far from the city's noise.
The library became his favorite retreat when he needed peace to study.
After a stressful week, she found comfort in her cozy little retreat.
He often escapes to his mountain retreat for some peace and quiet.
The garden at the back of the house became her private retreat.
