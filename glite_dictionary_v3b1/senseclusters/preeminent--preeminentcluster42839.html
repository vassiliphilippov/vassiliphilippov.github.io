preeminent

adjective
outstanding or distinguished above others
7.4
Pronunciation: / priˈɛmənənt / (AmE), / priˈɛmɪnənt / (BrE)
Surpassing all others; very distinguished in some way.

Examples

The preeminent expert
She is regarded as the preeminent authority on Renaissance art in the academic world.
The preeminent concern at the meeting was the safety of the community.
This institute is known for its preeminent research in climate science.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eminent - famous and respected within a particular sphere
  • antonym: obscure - not well known; not prominent or famous
  • synonym: prominent - important and well-known
