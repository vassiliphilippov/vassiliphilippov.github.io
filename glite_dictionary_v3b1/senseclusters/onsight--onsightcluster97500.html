onsight

noun
first-try climb
Pronunciation: / ˈɑnˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɒnˌsaɪt / (BrE)
a successful attempt to climb a route on the first try without prior practice or information about it

Examples

An onsight climb
She was thrilled to achieve an onsight of the challenging mountain route.
Completing an onsight requires both skill and the ability to read the rock as you climb.
Many climbers consider an onsight the purest form of ascent.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: flash - a successful climb on the first try with some prior information
  • degree lower: redpoint - a successful climb after previous attempts or practice
