interwoven

adjective
closely linked or combined
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈwoʊvən / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈwəʊvən / (BrE)
closely connected or combined together, so that the parts are difficult to separate; intricately linked or blended.

Examples

Closely interwoven cultures
Different cultures became interwoven through centuries of migration and trade.
The story's themes are interwoven with elements of folklore and tradition.
Their personal and professional lives were so interwoven that they were hard to separate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intertwined - twisted or closely connected together
