by-catch

noun
unintentional catch in fishing
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪˌkætʃ / (AmE), / ˈbaɪˌkatʃ / (BrE)
marine animals or other organisms unintentionally caught while fishing for a different species

Examples

Reduce by-catch animals
Efforts to reduce by-catch have led to changes in fishing gear and practices.
The environmental impact of by-catch is a major concern for marine conservationists.
Trawling can result in a high amount of by-catch, including sea turtles and unwanted fish.
