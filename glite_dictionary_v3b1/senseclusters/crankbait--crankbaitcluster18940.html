crankbait

noun
wobbling fishing lure
8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈkræŋk.beɪt/ (AmE), /ˈkræŋk.beɪt/ (BrE)
a type of fishing lure with a hard body designed to wobble and attract fish when pulled through the water

Examples

A crankbait lure
He caught a large bass using a bright-colored crankbait near the rocks.
Many anglers prefer a crankbait when fishing in deep water for aggressive fish.
The unique wobble of a crankbait can trigger strikes from curious fish.
He caught a large bass using a bright crankbait near the rocks.
Many anglers prefer a crankbait when fishing in deep water for predatory fish.
The wobbling action of a crankbait can attract fish even in murky conditions.
