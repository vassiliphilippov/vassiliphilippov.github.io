copper

noun
informal term for a policeman
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːpər / (AmE), / ˈkɒpə / (BrE)
(informal, sometimes dated, chiefly British) A police officer.

Examples

He tried to outrun the copper, but he was easily caught.
The copper on the corner blew his whistle to stop traffic.
