pubescent

adjective
having reached puberty but not yet mature
Pronunciation: / pjuˈbɛsənt / (AmE), / pjuːˈbɛsənt / (BrE)
Having reached the stage of puberty but not yet fully mature; undergoing the physical changes of adolescence.

Examples

Pubescent teenager
During the pubescent years, teenagers become more aware of their changing bodies.
Some pubescent boys develop deeper voices and facial hair.
The doctor explained that pubescent children often experience rapid growth spurts.
