carroting

noun
fur treatment process
Pronunciation: / ˈkærətɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkærətɪŋ / (BrE)
a process of treating animal fur, especially rabbit fur, with chemicals to prepare it for making felt

Examples

Carroting animal fur
The factory was known for its expertise in carroting before producing high-quality felt hats.
Workers wore protective gear during the carroting of rabbit pelts to avoid chemical exposure.
Advances in technology have reduced the need for traditional carroting in the felt industry.
