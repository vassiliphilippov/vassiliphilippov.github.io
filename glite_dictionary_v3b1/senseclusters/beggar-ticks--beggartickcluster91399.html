beggar-ticks

noun
plant of the genus Bidens
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛɡər tɪks / (AmE), / ˈbɛɡə tɪks / (BrE)
Any of several plants of the genus Bidens, having yellow flowers and small prickly fruits that stick to fur and clothing.

Examples

Wild beggar-ticks plants
After our walk, the dog's fur was full of beggar-ticks stuck to it.
She picked the beggar-ticks off her socks after hiking through the meadow.
The field was covered in beggar-ticks, their yellow blossoms bright against the green grass.
