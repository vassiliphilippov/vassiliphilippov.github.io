specification

noun
detailed description of requirements or standards
2.1 4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌspɛsəfɪˈkeɪʃən /; / ˌspɛs.ə.fəˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌspɛsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən /; / ˌspɛs.ɪ.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən /; / ˌspesɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
a detailed description or document listing the requirements, features, or standards for something (such as a product, job, or contract)
