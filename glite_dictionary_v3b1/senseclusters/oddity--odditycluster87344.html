oddity

noun
unusual or strange person or thing
5.3 1.04/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…ëÀê.d…™.ti / (AmE), / Àà…íd.…™.ti / (BrE)
a person or thing that is strange or unusual; something out of the ordinary, sometimes considered worth noticing or collecting

Examples

A rare oddity
Among his collections, the rare coin was the most prized oddity.
She was considered an oddity in her small town because of her peculiar hobbies.
The museum displayed several oddities collected from around the world.
