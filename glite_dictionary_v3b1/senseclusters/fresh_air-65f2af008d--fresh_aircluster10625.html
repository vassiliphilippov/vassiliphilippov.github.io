fresh air

noun
clean outdoor air
1.0
Pronunciation: / frɛʃ ɛr / (AmE), / frɛʃ eə / (BrE)
clean and invigorating outdoor air, especially considered refreshing or beneficial

Examples

To breathe fresh air
After a long day indoors, she stepped outside to enjoy some fresh air.
He takes a walk every morning just to breathe the fresh air.
Opening the window let fresh air into the stuffy room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stale air - air that is not fresh and may be stuffy or unhealthy
