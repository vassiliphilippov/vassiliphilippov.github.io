walnut blight

noun
disease affecting English walnut trees
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔlnət ˌblaɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːlnʌt ˌblaɪt / (BrE)
a bacterial disease affecting English walnut trees, causing lesions on leaves, shoots, and nuts

Examples

Walnut blight disease
Farmers are concerned about walnut blight destroying a large portion of their harvest this season.
Researchers are developing new treatments to protect walnut orchards from walnut blight.
Symptoms of walnut blight include dark, sunken spots on the tree's leaves and young fruit.
