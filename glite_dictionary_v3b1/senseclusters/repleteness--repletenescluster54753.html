repleteness

noun
state of fullness
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpliːtnəs / (AmE), / rɪˈpliːtnəs / (BrE)
the state of being completely full or well supplied with something

Examples

A sense of repleteness
After the large meal, a feeling of repleteness made it hard to move.
The pantry's repleteness ensured the family would not go hungry during the storm.
He smiled in quiet repleteness after finishing his favorite dessert.
