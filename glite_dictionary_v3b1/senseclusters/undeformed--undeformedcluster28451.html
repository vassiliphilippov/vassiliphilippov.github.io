undeformed

adjective
not altered in shape
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈfɔrmd / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈfɔːmd / (BrE)
not changed in shape or form; remaining in its original or natural condition

Examples

An undeformed object
The ancient vase was discovered undeformed, which is rare for such fragile artifacts.
Scientists examined the undeformed crystals to learn about the original geological conditions.
The metal rod remained undeformed after being subjected to high pressure in the experiment.
