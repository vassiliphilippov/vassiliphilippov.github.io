overlimit

noun
excess usage fee
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌlɪmɪt / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəlɪmɪt / (BrE)
an extra charge or penalty applied when usage or quantity exceeds an allowed limit

Examples

Overlimit fee charge
If you use more data than your plan allows, an overlimit may be added to your bill.
The credit card company charged an overlimit fee when he exceeded his spending limit.
Customers are warned about the possibility of an overlimit if they go beyond their monthly quota.

Related Terms

  • synonym: penalty - a punishment or fee for breaking a rule or limit
  • synonym: surcharge - an additional charge added to the usual cost
