reckon

verb
to calculate or count
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛkən / (AmE), / ˈrɛkən / (BrE)
to make a mathematical calculation or computation; to count or estimate numerically

Examples

Reckon the total
He paused to reckon the distance they had traveled so far.
It took her only a moment to reckon the total cost of the groceries.
The accountants must reckon every expense before closing the accounts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calculate - to determine mathematically
  • synonym: estimate - to roughly calculate or judge the value, number, or amount
