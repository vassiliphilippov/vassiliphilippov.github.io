balsamic

adjective
relating to sweet vinegar
Pronunciation: / bælˈsɑːmɪk / (AmE), / bælˈsæmɪk / (BrE)
describing a type of dark, sweet, and tangy vinegar made from grape must, often used in salads and cooking

Examples

Balsamic vinegar
She prefers a balsamic dressing on her salads for its rich, tangy flavor.
The chef reduced balsamic vinegar to make a sweet glaze for the roasted vegetables.
A splash of balsamic adds depth to tomato-based sauces and marinades.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vinegary - having the taste or qualities of vinegar
