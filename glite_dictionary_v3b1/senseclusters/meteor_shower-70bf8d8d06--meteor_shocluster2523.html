meteor shower

noun
a celestial event with multiple meteors
2.7 2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈmiː.tɪ.ər ˈʃaʊ.ər/ /; / /ˈmiː.tɪ.ər ˈʃoʊ.ər/ (AmE), /ˈmiː.tɪ.ə ˈʃaʊ.ə/ /; / /ˈmiː.tɪ.ɔː ˈʃəʊ.ə/ (BrE)
