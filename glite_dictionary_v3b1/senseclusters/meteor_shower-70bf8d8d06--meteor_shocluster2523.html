meteor shower

noun
a celestial event with multiple meteors
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːdiər ˌʃaʊər / (AmE), / ˈmiːtiə ˌʃaʊə / (BrE)
an event when many meteors (shooting stars) are seen in the night sky, appearing to come from one point
