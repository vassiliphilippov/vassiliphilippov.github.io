straight-shooting honesty

noun
blunt, direct truthfulness
Pronunciation: / streɪt ˈʃuːtɪŋ ˈɑːnəsti / (AmE), / streɪt ˈʃuːtɪŋ ˈɒnəsti / (BrE)
Honesty that is blunt, direct, and unpretentious, often delivering the truth without sugarcoating.

Examples

Straight-shooting honesty style
Her straight-shooting honesty made her both respected and feared in the boardroom.
His straight-shooting honesty can sometimes cause discomfort, but you always know where you stand.
I prefer straight-shooting honesty over polite lies any day.
