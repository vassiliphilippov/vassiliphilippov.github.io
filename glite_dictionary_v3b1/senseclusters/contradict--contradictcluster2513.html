Synonyms

    deny is to say something is not true or refuse to accept it, while contradict is to state or show the opposite of someone.
  • controvert
    8.3 0.06/m
    controvert is to argue strongly against a claim in formal discussion, while contradict is to express the opposite of someone in speech.
  • disconfirm
    7.4 0.04/m
