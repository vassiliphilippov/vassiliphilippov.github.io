Thevetia

noun
genus of poisonous tropical American shrubs or trees
9.9
Pronunciation: / θəˈviːtiə /; / θəˈviːʃiə / (AmE), / θəˈviːtiə /; / θɪˈviːtiə / (BrE)
a genus of poisonous evergreen shrubs and trees native to tropical America, with entire leaves and large cymose flowers
