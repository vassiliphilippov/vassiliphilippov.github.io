cherrywood

adjective
reddish-brown color
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛriˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛriˌwʊd / (BrE)
having a reddish-brown color similar to that of wood from a cherry tree, often used for stains or finishes

Examples

Cherrywood color
The designer recommended a cherrywood stain for the new dining table to add warmth to the room.
They painted the doors in a cherrywood shade to match the antique furniture.
Her favorite bookshelf was finished in a rich cherrywood color that complemented the decor.
