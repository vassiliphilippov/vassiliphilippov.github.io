Micrococcaceae

noun
family of pigmented, spherical bacteria
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˌkɑːkˈkeɪsiː /; / ˌmaɪkroʊˌkɑːkəˈsiːiː / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊkɒkˈkeɪsɪiː /; / ˌmʌɪkrəʊˌkɒkəˈsiːiː / (BrE)
