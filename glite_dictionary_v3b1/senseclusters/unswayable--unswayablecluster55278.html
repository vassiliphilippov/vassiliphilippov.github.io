unswayable

adjective
not able to be influenced
8.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsweɪəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈsweɪəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be influenced, changed, or persuaded; firmly fixed in opinion or determination

Examples

An unswayable opinion
Despite the criticism, his unswayable belief in his project never faltered.
The judge remained unswayable by public opinion throughout the trial.
Her unswayable loyalty to her friends earned her their deep respect.
Despite the criticism, her unswayable determination helped her achieve her goals.
The statue remained unswayable even during the strongest earthquake.
His unswayable beliefs made it difficult for others to change his mind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adamant - refusing to be persuaded or to change one's mind
  • antonym: flexible - willing to change or adapt
  • synonym: immovable - impossible to move or change
  • synonym: steadfast - firm and unwavering
  • synonym: unwavering - firm and not changing in opinion or decision
