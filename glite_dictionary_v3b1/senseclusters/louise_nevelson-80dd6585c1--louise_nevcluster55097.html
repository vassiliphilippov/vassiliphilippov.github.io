Louise Nevelson

proper noun
American sculptor known for wooden assemblages
8.8
Pronunciation: / luˈis ˈnɛvəlˌsən / (AmE), / luˈiːz ˈnɛvəlsən / (BrE)
(1899–1988) an American sculptor, born in Russia, known for her large-scale wooden assemblages and monochromatic painted creations.
