surfable

adjective
suitable for surfing
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːfəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈsɜːfəbl̩ / (BrE)
suitable or good for surfing, especially referring to waves or beaches where surfing is possible

Examples

Surfable waves
After days of flat water, the waves finally became surfable this morning.
The beach is famous for its consistently surfable conditions throughout the year.
We checked several spots, but only one had surfable waves suitable for beginners.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rideable - able to be ridden, especially by a surfboard or similar
  • antonym: unsurfable - not suitable for surfing
