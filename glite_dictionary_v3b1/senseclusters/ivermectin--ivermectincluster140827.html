ivermectin

noun
antiparasitic drug
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪvərˈmɛktɪn / (AmE), / ˌaɪvəˈmɛktɪn / (BrE)
a drug used to treat infections caused by certain parasites in humans and animals

Examples

Ivermectin medicine
The veterinarian administered ivermectin to the dog to treat its parasite infection.
Some countries use ivermectin in public health programs to control river blindness.
The patient recovered quickly after starting a course of ivermectin tablets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anthelmintic - a drug that destroys or expels parasitic worms
  • synonym: antiparasitic - a drug used to treat parasitic infections
