galled

adjective
sore from rubbing or abrasion
Pronunciation: / ɡɔld / (AmE), / ɡɔːld / (BrE)
(of skin) sore or painful as a result of being rubbed or abraded.

Examples

The galled skin
After a long hike, his heels were galled by the stiff new boots.
Her galled skin needed ointment to heal properly.
The harness left the horse’s shoulders galled and raw.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abraded - worn or scraped by rubbing
  • synonym: chafed - (of skin) made sore by rubbing against something
