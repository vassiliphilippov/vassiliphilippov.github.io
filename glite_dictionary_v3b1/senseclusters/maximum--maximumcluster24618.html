maximum

adjective
greatest or highest possible
Pronunciation: / ˈmæk.sə.məm / (AmE), / ˈmæk.sɪ.məm / (BrE)
Being the greatest, highest, or most complete possible in degree or amount.

Examples

Maximum effort needed
He reached the maximum level of achievement in his field.
The machine should not be operated at its maximum speed for long periods.
The room has a maximum capacity of 50 people.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maximal - greatest or most complete possible (less common variant)
  • antonym: minimum - the least or smallest amount possible
