intimate

noun
close friend; confidant
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.tɪ.mət / (AmE), / ˈɪn.tɪ.mət / (BrE)
a close friend with whom one shares personal or private matters

Examples

A trusted intimate
He has only a few intimates who truly understand him.
She considered her sister her closest intimate and always trusted her with her secrets.
Unlike acquaintances, an intimate can be relied upon in difficult times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confidant - a person to whom private matters are confided
