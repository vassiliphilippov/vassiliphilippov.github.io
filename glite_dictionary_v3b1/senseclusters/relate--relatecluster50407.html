Synonyms

  • concern
    2.1 45/m
    concern means to be relevant or to affect someone, while relate means simply to be connected or linked in meaning.
  • pertain
    4.6 10/m
    pertain appears in formal documents and law, while relate is a common verb for general connections.
  • bear on
    5.1 3.65/m
    bear on refers to something that affects or connects to an issue directly, while relate is more general about connection.
  • refer
    3.0 14/m
    refer means to point to or be about a topic directly, while relate focuses on being connected or linked in meaning.
  • tie in
    4.1 0.69/m
    tie in means to connect things so they fit together in a story or plan, while relate is a general link verb.
  • connect
    2.3 25/m
    connect means to join or link two things directly, while relate describes a broader or more abstract connection between ideas.
  • link
    3.0 34/m

  • link up
    3.5 0.10/m
    link up means to join or connect people or systems, often physically or online, while relate is about general connections.
