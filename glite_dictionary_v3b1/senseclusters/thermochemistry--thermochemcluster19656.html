thermochemistry

noun
study of heat in chemical reactions
Pronunciation: / ˌθɝːmoʊˈkɛmɪstri / (AmE), / ˌθɜːməʊˈkɛmɪstri / (BrE)
the branch of chemistry concerned with the relationship between chemical reactions and the heat involved

Examples

Thermochemistry study
His research in thermochemistry focuses on measuring the heat released during combustion.
She is studying thermochemistry to understand how energy changes in chemical reactions.
The results of the thermochemistry experiment helped explain the reaction's temperature change.
