high-flier

noun
flies at high altitude
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪˌflaɪər / (AmE), / ˈhaɪˌflaɪə / (BrE)
an animal or aircraft that flies at a high altitude or frequently soars well above the ground

Examples

Eagle high-flier
The eagle is a remarkable high-flier, often spotted soaring above mountain peaks.
Many modern jets are designed to be high-fliers for long-distance travel.
The falcon's reputation as a high-flier makes it a favorite among bird watchers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: low-flier - an animal or aircraft that flies at a low altitude
  • synonym: soarer - an animal or aircraft that glides or soars at great heights
