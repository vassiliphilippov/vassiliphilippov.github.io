G. K. Chesterton

proper noun
English writer and creator of Father Brown
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiː keɪ ˈtʃɛstərtən / (AmE), / ˌdʒiː keɪ ˈtʃɛstətən / (BrE)
(1874–1936) an English writer and critic, known for his conservative views, Catholic faith, essays, and the Father Brown detective stories.
