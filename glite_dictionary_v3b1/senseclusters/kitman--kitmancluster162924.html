kitman

noun
equipment crew member
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪtmæn / (AmE), / ˈkɪtmæn / (BrE)
a person responsible for looking after, transporting, and setting up instruments and technical equipment for a performing group

Examples

Band kitman
The kitman arrived early to prepare all the instruments before the band members showed up.
After the show, the kitman carefully packed away the guitars and sound equipment.
Every successful tour depends on a reliable kitman to handle the technical setup.

Related Terms

  • synonym: roadie - a person who helps with equipment for a band on tour
  • synonym: technician - a person skilled in setting up and maintaining technical equipment
