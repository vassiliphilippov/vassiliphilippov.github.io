permissibly

adverb
in a permitted manner
Pronunciation: / pərˈmɪsəbli / (AmE), / pəˈmɪsəbli / (BrE)
In a way that is allowed by rules, laws, or principles.

Examples

Act permissibly under rules
The company acted permissibly under the existing regulations.
The contract was permissibly amended according to the agreed procedure.
You may permissibly bring your own food to the event if you have dietary restrictions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: impermissibly - in a way that is not permitted
