dirty

adjective
offensive or indecent (language, jokes, etc.)
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdɝːti / (AmE), / ˈdɜːti / (BrE)
(of language, jokes, books, etc.) containing or referring to sexual matters in a way that is considered rude or indecent.

Examples

Dirty joke
He was suspended from class for using dirty language.
Some parents think certain movies are too dirty for children to watch.
The comedian was known for telling dirty jokes in his stand-up routines.
