loftily

adverb
in a proud or elevated manner

In a manner that shows or suggests a proud, superior, or elevated attitude.

Examples

To speak loftily
He loftily dismissed the suggestion as beneath his consideration.
She spoke loftily about her achievements, impressing the audience with her confidence.
The judge addressed the courtroom loftily, maintaining an air of detachment throughout the proceedings.
