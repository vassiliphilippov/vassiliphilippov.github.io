borrowed time

noun
period beyond expected limit, often with risk
4.0 4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑːr.oʊd taɪm/ /; / /ˈbɔːr.oʊd taɪm/ (AmE), /ˈbɒr.əʊd taɪm/ (BrE)
a period after an expected end or limit, especially when one continues to exist or function despite serious danger or risk
