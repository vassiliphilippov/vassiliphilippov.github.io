repin

noun
social media repost
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈpɪn / (AmE), / ˌriːˈpɪn / (BrE)
a repost or share of another user's pin on a social media platform, especially Pinterest

Examples

A repin on Pinterest
Her summer salad tutorial received a repin from thousands of home cooks last week.
The photo quickly gained popularity after it got its first repin on Pinterest.
A single repin can help your content reach a much wider audience online.

Related Terms

  • antonym: original post - the first version of a post before it is shared or repinned
  • synonym: repost - to post something again or share someone else's post
  • synonym: share - to post something so others can see it on social media
