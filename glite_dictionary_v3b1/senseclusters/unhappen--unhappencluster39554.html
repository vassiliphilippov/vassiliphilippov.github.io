unhappen

verb
to cancel event
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhæpən / (AmE), / ʌnˈhæpən / (BrE)
to make something not happen, either by canceling it or by reversing it as if it never occurred

Some mistakes cannot simply unhappen, no matter how much we regret them.
If only it were possible to unhappen the accident, everything would be different.
