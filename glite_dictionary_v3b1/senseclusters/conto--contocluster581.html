conto

noun
former Portuguese currency unit
Pronunciation: / ˈkɒn.toʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.təʊ / (BrE)
A unit of currency in Portugal equal to 1,000 escudos, used prior to the adoption of the euro.

Examples

A conto in Portugal
He remembered when a single conto was a significant amount of money.
In 1990, a car might have cost ten contos in Portugal.
The old house was sold for fifty contos before the euro was introduced.
