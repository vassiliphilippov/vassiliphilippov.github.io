fanboyism

noun
excessive devotion
8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈfæn.bɔɪ.ɪ.zəm/ /; / /ˈfæn.bɔɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈfæn.bɔɪ.ɪ.zəm/ /; / /ˈfæn.bɔɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
excessive or uncritical enthusiasm and devotion toward a person, group, product, or idea, often ignoring faults or criticism

Examples

Fanboyism for comics
His fanboyism for the comic book series made him defend it against any criticism.
The debate quickly turned heated due to the fanboyism of both sides.
She recognized her own fanboyism when she bought every piece of merchandise from the show.
His fanboyism for the tech company made him ignore any negative reviews.
The debate quickly turned into fanboyism as both sides refused to admit any flaws.
Critics accused the magazine of fanboyism in its coverage of the new movie.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bias - an unfair preference for or against something
  • synonym: fandom - the state of being a fan of someone or something
  • antonym: objectivity - the quality of being unbiased and impartial
