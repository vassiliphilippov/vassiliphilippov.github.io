Synonyms

  • hero-worship
    6.5 0.09/m
    hero-worship means admiring a person as perfect and beyond fault, while fanboyism is enthusiasm that can be toward products or groups.
  • idolatry
    7.6 0.27/m
    idolatry is an extreme, often formal love that treats someone or something as perfect, while fanboyism is more casual pop culture devotion.
  • fanaticism
    7.4 1.69/m
    fanaticism is extreme, often intolerant belief that rejects other views, while fanboyism is milder, uncritical enthusiasm for people or products.
