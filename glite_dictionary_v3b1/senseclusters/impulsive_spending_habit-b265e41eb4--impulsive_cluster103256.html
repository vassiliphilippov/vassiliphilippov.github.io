impulsive spending habit

noun
tendency to spend money without thinking
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpʌlsɪv ˈspɛndɪŋ ˈhæbɪt / (AmE), / ɪmˈpʌlsɪv ˈspɛndɪŋ ˈhæbɪt / (BrE)
a tendency to spend money without careful thought or planning, often on sudden urges

Examples

Impulsive spending habit causes debt
His impulsive spending habit often left him with very little savings at the end of the month.
Many people try to break their impulsive spending habit by setting strict budgets.
She blamed her credit card debt on her impulsive spending habit developed during college.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impulse buying - the act of buying things without planning
