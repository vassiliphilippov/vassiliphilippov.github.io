undipped

adjective
not immersed in liquid
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdɪpt / (AmE), / ʌnˈdɪpt / (BrE)
not having been dipped or immersed in a liquid.

Examples

Undipped candles
The undipped candles were set aside for later use.
He preferred his strawberries undipped so he could taste their natural flavor.
The undipped brushes were ready for the children to use in the art class.
