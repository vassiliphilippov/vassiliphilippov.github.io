stablewoman

noun
woman who cares for horses in a stable
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪ.bəlˌwʊ.mən / (AmE), / ˈsteɪ.bəlˌwʊ.mən / (BrE)
a woman employed in a stable to take care of horses

Examples

A stablewoman cares
After years as a stablewoman, she knew every horse by name and temperament.
The stablewoman arrived early each morning to feed and groom the horses.
The farm recently hired a new stablewoman to help during foaling season.
