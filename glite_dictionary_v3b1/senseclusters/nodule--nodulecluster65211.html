nodule

noun
small rounded protuberance on a plant
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑdʒuːl /; / ˈnɑːdʒuːl / (AmE), / ˈnɒd.juːl / (BrE)
a small, rounded lump or swelling, especially on or in the body, plants, or rocks

Examples

Geologists found a manganese nodule on the ocean floor.
