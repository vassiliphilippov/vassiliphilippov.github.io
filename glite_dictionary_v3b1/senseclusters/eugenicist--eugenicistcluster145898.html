eugenicist

adjective
favoring eugenics
9.9
Pronunciation: /juː.ˈdʒe.nɪ.sɪst/ (AmE), /juː.ˈdʒe.nɪ.sɪst/ (BrE)
supporting or relating to the idea of improving the human population by controlled breeding for desirable traits

Examples

Eugenicist ideas
The committee rejected the eugenicist proposal as unethical and discriminatory.
Some early 20th-century policies were influenced by eugenicist thinking about genetics and society.
Her eugenicist views were widely criticized by the scientific community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anti-eugenic - opposed to the principles or practice of eugenics
  • synonym: eugenic - relating to or aiming at the improvement of the human race by selective breeding
