nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory

noun
anti-inflammatory drug without steroids
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.stɛˈrɔɪ.dl ˌæn.ti.ɪnˈflæm.əˌtɔr.i / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.stəˈrɔɪ.dəl ˌæn.ti.ɪnˈflæm.əˌtɔː.ri / (BrE)
A drug or substance that reduces inflammation without containing steroids, commonly used to relieve pain and swelling.

Examples

A nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug
Ibuprofen is a widely used nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory in the United States.
Many people take nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs for headaches or muscle pain.
Patients should consult a doctor before using a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory for long periods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: NSAID - nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug; common abbreviation and synonym
  • antonym: steroidal anti-inflammatory - anti-inflammatory containing steroids
