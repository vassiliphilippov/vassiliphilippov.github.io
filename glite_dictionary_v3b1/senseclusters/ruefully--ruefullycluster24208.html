ruefully

adverb
with regret or sorrow
Pronunciation: / ˈruː.fə.li / (AmE), / ˈruː.fə.li / (BrE)
in a way that expresses regret, sorrow, or pity

Examples

He ruefully admitted

He glanced ruefully at the broken vase on the floor.
She smiled ruefully, acknowledging her mistake.
