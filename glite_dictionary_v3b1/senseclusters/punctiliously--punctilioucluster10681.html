punctiliously

adverb
with great attention to detail
Pronunciation: / pʌŋkˈtɪliəsli / (AmE), / pʌŋkˈtɪlɪəsli / (BrE)
In a manner showing great attention to detail or correct behavior; very carefully and precisely.

Examples

To punctiliously follow rules
He punctiliously observed all the customs of the host country.
She punctiliously recorded every participant’s response in her research notes.
The lawyer punctiliously followed every procedure outlined in the contract.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carefully - with attention and caution
  • antonym: negligently - in a careless or neglectful manner
