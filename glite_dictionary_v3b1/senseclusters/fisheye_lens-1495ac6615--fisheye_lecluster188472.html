fisheye lens

noun
wide-angle, strongly distorting camera lens
4.4 5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃ.aɪ lɛnz / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃ.aɪ lɛnz / (BrE)
a camera lens with a very wide angle of view, producing strong visual distortion intended to create a wide panoramic or hemispherical image

Examples

A fisheye lens photo
He used a fisheye lens to capture the entire stadium in one shot.
Photographers often use a fisheye lens for creative architectural or landscape images.
The fisheye lens creates a dramatic, curved effect along the edges of the photo.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wide-angle lens - a lens with a wider field of view than a standard lens
