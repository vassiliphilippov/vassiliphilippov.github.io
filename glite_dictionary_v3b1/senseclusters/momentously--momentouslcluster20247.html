momentously

adverb
in a significant or important manner
1.8 5.6
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈmen.təs.liː/ (AmE), /mə.ˈmen.təs.liː/ (BrE)

Examples

To speak momentously

The decision to launch the satellite was momentously announced to the press.
The treaty was momentously signed, marking a new era in international relations.
