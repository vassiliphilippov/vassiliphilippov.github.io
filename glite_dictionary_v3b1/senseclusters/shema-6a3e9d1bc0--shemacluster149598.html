Shema

noun
central Jewish prayer of faith
9.3
Pronunciation: / ʃəˈmɑ / (AmE), / ʃəˈmɑː / (BrE)
a central Jewish prayer affirming the oneness of God, traditionally recited daily, especially by observant Jewish men

Examples

The Shema prayer
As a child, Leah learned the words of the Shema from her grandmother.
Every morning and evening, he makes sure to recite the Shema as part of his prayers.
The Shema is considered by many to be the most important declaration of Jewish faith.
