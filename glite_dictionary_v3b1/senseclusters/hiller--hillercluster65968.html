hiller

noun
soil-ridging tool
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪlər / (AmE), / ˈhɪlə / (BrE)
a person or machine that forms soil into ridges or hills for planting crops

Examples

A hiller machine

After tilling the field, she used a hiller to shape the soil into neat ridges.

The hiller moved steadily down the field, shaping the earth into neat rows for potatoes.
After buying a new hiller, the farmer was able to prepare his land much faster.
She worked as a hiller during the planting season, making ridges for the seeds.
