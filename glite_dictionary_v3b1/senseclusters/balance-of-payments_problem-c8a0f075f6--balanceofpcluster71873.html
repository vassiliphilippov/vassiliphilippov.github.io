balance-of-payments problem

noun
economic issue from import payments exceeding export receipts
1.7 6.2
Pronunciation: / ˌbæləns əv ˈpeɪmənts ˈprɑːbləm / (AmE), / ˌbæləns əv ˈpeɪmənts ˈprɒbləm / (BrE)
a situation in which a country's payments for imports exceed its receipts from exports, causing economic difficulties

Examples

A balance-of-payments problem
A persistent balance-of-payments problem may lead to currency devaluation and inflation.
Government intervention was required to address the balance-of-payments problem and restore stability.
The country faced a severe balance-of-payments problem after oil prices surged.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trade deficit - an excess of imports over exports in a country's trade balance
