want

noun
a feeling of desire or longing
Pronunciation: / wɑnt / (AmE), / wɒnt / (BrE)
a feeling of desire or longing for something that is needed or wished for

Examples

A want for love
His every want was anticipated by the attentive staff.
In times of hardship, the want for food became overwhelming.
She felt a want for companionship after moving to a new city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: desire - a strong feeling of wanting to have something
  • degree lower: need - a requirement or necessity for something
  • synonym: wish - a desire or hope for something to happen
