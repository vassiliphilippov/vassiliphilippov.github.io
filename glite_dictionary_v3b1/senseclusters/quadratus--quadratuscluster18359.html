quadratus

noun
square-shaped muscle
9.9
Pronunciation: / kwɑːˈdreɪtəs / (AmE), / kwɒˈdreɪtəs / (BrE)
a name for several square-shaped muscles in the human body, such as in the lower back or forearm

Examples

Quadratus lumborum muscle
The quadratus lumborum helps support the spine during movement.
In anatomy class, we learned about the quadratus muscles of the forearm.
Injuries to the quadratus can cause lower back pain in athletes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: muscle - a tissue in the body that contracts to produce movement
