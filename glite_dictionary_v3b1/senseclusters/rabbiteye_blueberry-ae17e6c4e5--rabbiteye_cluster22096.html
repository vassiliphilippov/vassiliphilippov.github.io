rabbiteye blueberry

noun
a southeastern US blueberry shrub and its fruit
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈræb.i.taɪ ˈbluːˌbɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈræb.ɪt.aɪ ˈbluːˌbɛr.i / (BrE)
