teletype machine

noun
telegraph-connected typewriter-printer
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛləˌtaɪp məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪtaɪp məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
A device that prints text messages sent and received over a telegraph network, operating similarly to a typewriter.

Examples

The teletype machine prints
Every message from the control room appeared on the teletype machine in a steady stream of paper.
Historians preserved an old teletype machine used during World War II for sending urgent cables.
The reporter typed her story quickly on the teletype machine to send it to headquarters.
