chechia

noun
North African felt cap
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛtʃiə / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛtʃiə / (BrE)
a traditional brimless felt cap, usually red, worn in North Africa, especially Tunisia

Examples

A red chechia
During the festival, many men wore a chechia as part of their traditional clothing.
He bought a handmade chechia from a market in Tunis as a souvenir.
The red chechia is a symbol of Tunisian national identity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beret - a round, flat, soft cap, often associated with France
  • synonym: fez - a flat-topped conical red hat with a tassel, worn in the Middle East and North Africa
