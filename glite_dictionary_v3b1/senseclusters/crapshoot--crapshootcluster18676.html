crapshoot

noun
risky and uncertain venture
8.5 7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkræp.ʃuːt/ (AmE), /ˈkræp.ʃuːt/ (BrE)
an activity or venture in which the outcome is highly uncertain and unpredictable, often depending largely on chance

Examples

A crapshoot venture
Getting funding for a new tech startup can be a total crapshoot.
Predicting the winner of the election turned out to be a crapshoot this year.
Whether or not your application is approved sometimes feels like a crapshoot.
