ungendered

adjective
without grammatical gender
7.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdʒɛndərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈdʒɛndəd / (BrE)
not having grammatical gender or not marked as masculine, feminine, or neuter in language or grammar

Examples

Ungendered nouns
English nouns are generally ungendered, unlike those in French or German.
Some linguists prefer to use ungendered terms when describing people in academic writing.
The new pronoun was introduced as an ungendered alternative to traditional gendered forms.

Related Terms
