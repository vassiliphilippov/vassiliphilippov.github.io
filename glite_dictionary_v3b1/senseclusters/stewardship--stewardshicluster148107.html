stewardship

noun
management and governance of data assets
4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈstuː.ərd.ʃɪp/ /; / /ˈstjuː.ərd.ʃɪp/ (AmE), /ˈstjuː.əd.ʃɪp/ /; / /ˈstuː.əd.ʃɪp/ (BrE)
the careful and responsible management and protection of something valuable, such as property, resources, the environment, or data

Examples

Stewardship of resources
The company established stewardship of data to ensure quality and privacy across departments.
Effective stewardship of customer records requires clear ownership, documented lineage, and regular audits by IT.
Under her stewardship, the new data governance framework reduced duplicate records and compliance risk.
The organization is known for its excellent stewardship of community funds and resources.
Environmental stewardship is essential to preserve forests for future generations.
Under her stewardship, the museum expanded its collection and improved visitor experiences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: data governance - the overall control and policies that determine how data is managed and used across an organisation
  • synonym: data management - the practice of collecting, storing, protecting, and utilising data efficiently and securely
  • synonym: management - the act of controlling or organizing something
  • antonym: neglect - the failure to care for or manage something properly
