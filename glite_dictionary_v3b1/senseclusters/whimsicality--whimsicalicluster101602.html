whimsicality

noun
quality of being fanciful or playful
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌwɪmzɪˈkæləti / (AmE), / ˌwɪmzɪˈkælɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being playfully unusual, imaginative, or unpredictable
