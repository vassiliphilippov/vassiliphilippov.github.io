supralinear

adjective
more than proportional
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːprəˈlɪniər / (AmE), / ˌsuːprəˈlɪniə / (BrE)
increasing more than proportionally in response to an increase in input or stimulus

Examples

A supralinear response
The scientist observed a supralinear response in the chemical reaction as the temperature increased.
When the input doubled, the system's output grew in a supralinear fashion, exceeding expectations.
A supralinear relationship was found between exercise intensity and heart rate in the study.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: linear - increasing in direct proportion to input
  • antonym: sublinear - increasing less than proportionally to input
