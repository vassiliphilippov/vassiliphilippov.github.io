Phobos

proper noun
largest moon of Mars
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊboʊs / (AmE), / ˈfəʊbɒs / (BrE)
the larger and innermost of the two natural satellites of Mars

Examples

Phobos moon of Mars
Scientists have observed that Phobos is gradually spiraling closer to Mars.
Some missions to Mars plan to land spacecraft on Phobos in the future.
The surface of Phobos is covered with craters and deep grooves.
