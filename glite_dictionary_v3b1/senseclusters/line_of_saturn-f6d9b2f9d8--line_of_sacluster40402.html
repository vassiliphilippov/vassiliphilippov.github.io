line of Saturn

noun
palm crease said to show destiny
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪn əv ˈsætɚn / (AmE), / ˈlaɪn əv ˈsatən / (BrE)
a crease on the palm, also called the fate line, which palmists claim indicates potential for success or destiny

Examples

The line of Saturn on palm
He grew curious about the meaning of his line of Saturn after reading a book on palmistry.
Her line of Saturn was deep and clear, which the palmist said signified a successful career.
Some believe that a broken line of Saturn suggests unexpected changes in one's life path.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fate line - the palmistry line associated with a person's destiny or fate
