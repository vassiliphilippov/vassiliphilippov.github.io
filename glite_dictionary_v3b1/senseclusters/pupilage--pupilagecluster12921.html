pupilage

noun
state of wardship
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpjuːpɪlɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpjuːpɪlɪdʒ / (BrE)
the state or period of being under the care and supervision of a guardian, especially as a minor; wardship.

Examples

Pupilage under a guardian
After his parents' death, he was placed under the pupilage of his uncle.
The court determined the terms of her pupilage until she reached adulthood.
During his pupilage, all major decisions were made by his appointed guardian.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guardianship - the position of being legally responsible for someone, especially a child
  • antonym: independence - the state of being self-reliant and not under the care of another
  • synonym: wardship - the state of being under the protection of a legal guardian
