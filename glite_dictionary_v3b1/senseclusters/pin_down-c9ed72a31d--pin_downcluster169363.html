pin down

verb
attach with or as if with a pin
Pronunciation: / pɪn daʊn / (AmE), / pɪn daʊn / (BrE)
to attach something with, or as if with, a pin, making it stay in a fixed position

Examples

Pin down a poster
Can you pin down that map to the noticeboard?
She pinned down her scarf so it wouldn't blow away in the wind.
The teacher pinned down the students' artworks along the hallway wall.
