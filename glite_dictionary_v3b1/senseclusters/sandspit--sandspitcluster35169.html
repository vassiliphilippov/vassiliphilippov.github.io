sandspit

noun
narrow sandy strip
5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈsænd.spɪt/ (AmE), /ˈsænd.spɪt/ (BrE)
a narrow strip of sand that extends into a body of water from the shore

Examples

A sandspit on shore
The children built a sandcastle on the sandspit that reached far into the lake.
Birds gathered on the sandspit to rest during their migration.
At low tide, we could walk along the entire sandspit without getting our feet wet.
