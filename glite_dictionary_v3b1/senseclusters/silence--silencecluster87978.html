silence

noun
absence of sound
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪləns / (AmE), / ˈsaɪləns / (BrE)
a complete absence of sound or speech; the state of being silent

Examples

A deep silence
A sudden silence filled the room when the principal entered.
After the storm passed, a deep silence fell over the village.
She enjoyed the silence of the empty library.
After the announcement, a heavy silence filled the room.
He raised his hand, asking for silence before the ceremony began.
The silence in the library was interrupted only by the turning of pages.

Related Terms

  • antonym: noise - a loud or unpleasant sound
  • synonym: quiet - a lack of noise or bustle
