CPD

noun
city police abbreviation
Pronunciation: / si pi di / (AmE), / siː piː diː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for a city's police department, often used in news and reports to refer to the local police force

Examples

CPD officers
The CPD issued a statement following the incident downtown.
Local residents praised the CPD for their quick response to the emergency.
After the parade, the CPD managed traffic to ensure everyone's safety.

Related Terms
