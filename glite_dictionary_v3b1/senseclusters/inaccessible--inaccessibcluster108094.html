Synonyms

  • unavailable
    3.4 5.86/m
    unavailable is not ready to be used or obtained now, while inaccessible means hard or impossible to reach, enter, obtain, or understand.
  • unreachable
    4.9 0.75/m
    unreachable is impossible or very hard to get to because of location or situation, while inaccessible can mean hard to enter, use, or understand.
  • unapproachable
    7.1 0.04/m
    unapproachable is set apart or hard to get near, sometimes socially distant, while inaccessible focuses on being unable to reach, use, or understand.
  • out of reach
    4.0 2.77/m
    out of reach means physically or figuratively beyond one's reach right now, while inaccessible is more absolute or broadly hard to access or use.
  • unattainable
    6.3 1.63/m
    unattainable describes goals or levels that cannot be achieved, while inaccessible is used more broadly for places, things, or ideas one cannot reach or use.
  • unobtainable
    6.1 0.42/m
    unobtainable is impossible to get or buy, often for objects, while inaccessible covers both physical access and difficulty of use or understanding.
  • unprocurable
    9.9 0.00/m
    unprocurable is hard to obtain, often in formal or old texts, while inaccessible is a common, broader word for things you cannot reach or use.
  • incomprehensible
    8.8 4.12/m
    incomprehensible is impossible or very hard to understand, while inaccessible can mean both hard to understand and hard to reach or use.
  • unintelligible
    8.6 52/m

  • impenetrable
