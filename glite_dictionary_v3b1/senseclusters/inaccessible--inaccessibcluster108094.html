inaccessible

adjective
impossible or very difficult to reach
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.əkˈsɛs.ə.bəl /; / ˌɪn.ækˈsɛs.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌɪn.əkˈses.ə.bəl /; / ˌɪn.əkˈsɛs.ə.bəl / (BrE)
not able to be reached, entered, obtained, or understood; very difficult or impossible to approach or use

Examples

An inaccessible place
Many online resources are still inaccessible to people with disabilities.
The mountain summit remained inaccessible due to heavy snowfall and dangerous winds.
The philosopher's writing style is so complex that some readers find it inaccessible.
Much of the ancient manuscript remains inaccessible to modern readers.
The remote mountain village was almost inaccessible during the winter months.
There are resources deep underground that are currently inaccessible with today's technology.

  • synonym: unattainable - not able to be achieved or obtained
  • synonym: unreachable - not able to be reached
