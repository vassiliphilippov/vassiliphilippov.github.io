optimistically

adverb
in an optimistic manner
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːptɪˈmɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɒptɪˈmɪstɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that shows hope and confidence about the future; with optimism.

Examples

Speak optimistically about future
Despite the setbacks, he spoke optimistically about the team's chances next year.
She looked optimistically at the test results, hoping for good news.
The company is optimistically projecting increased sales for the upcoming quarter.
