assimilation

noun
absorption of nutrients into the body
6.0 4.7
Pronunciation: /ə.sɪ.mə.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ə.sɪ.mɪ.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ə.sɪ.mə.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ə.sɪ.mɪ.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process by which the body absorbs nutrients after digestion has broken food down into simpler forms

Examples

Nutrient assimilation

Proper assimilation of nutrients is essential for overall health and well-being.
The assimilation of vitamins occurs mainly in the small intestine.
