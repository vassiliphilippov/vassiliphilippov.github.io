troglodytism

noun
secluded or uncultured behavior
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɑːɡləˌdaɪtɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈtrɒɡləˌdaɪtɪzəm / (BrE)
the practice or tendency of living in seclusion or behaving in a rude, uncultured, or socially backward way, often used figuratively.

Examples

Social troglodytism
His refusal to attend community events revealed a stubborn troglodytism that worried his neighbors.
The professor criticized the troglodytism of those who reject new ideas and avoid social interaction.
Some saw her preference for solitude as harmless, while others called it troglodytism.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boorishness - rude or insensitive behavior
  • synonym: seclusion - the state of being private and away from other people
  • antonym: sociability - the quality of enjoying the company of others
