civilianize

verb
to make civilian
Pronunciation: / sɪˈvɪljəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / sɪˈvɪljənaɪz / (BrE)
to convert something or someone from military to civilian use or status

Examples

To civilianize military roles
The government plans to civilianize several administrative roles within the defense department.
After the conflict ended, the country worked to civilianize its police force.
Many military vehicles were civilianized and sold to the public after the war.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demilitarize - to remove military control or influence from something
  • antonym: militarize - to give a military character or use to something
