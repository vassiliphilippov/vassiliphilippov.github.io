downshifting

noun
changing to lower gear
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊnˌʃɪftɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnˌʃɪftɪŋ / (BrE)
the action of changing a vehicle's transmission to a lower gear, usually to slow down or increase power.

Examples

Smooth downshifting
He practiced downshifting smoothly as he approached the sharp curve in the road.
Proper downshifting can help you control your speed when driving downhill.
The instructor explained the importance of downshifting before overtaking another vehicle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gear change - the act of shifting gears in a vehicle
  • antonym: upshifting - the action of changing a vehicle's transmission to a higher gear
