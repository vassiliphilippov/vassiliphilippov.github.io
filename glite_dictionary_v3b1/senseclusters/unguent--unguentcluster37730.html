unguent

noun
medicated ointment for skin
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʌŋɡwənt / (AmE), / ˈʌŋɡwənt / (BrE)
A soft, semisolid preparation, usually containing medicine, that is applied to the skin as a remedy or to soothe irritation.

Examples

Medicinal unguent
Apply the unguent twice daily to the affected area for best results.
Historically, soldiers carried an unguent for treating wounds during battle.
The herbalist prepared an unguent to soothe the patient's rash.
