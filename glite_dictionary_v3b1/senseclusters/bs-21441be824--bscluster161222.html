BS

nonsense; deceptive talk (slang)

Informal, crude: nonsense, false statements, or deceptive talk; abbreviation of 'bullshit'.

Examples

Complete BS
The politician's excuse was obvious BS, and voters demanded clearer evidence for the policy.
Don't give me that BS; I want to see the actual sales figures for the quarter.
She called the company's ad BS in an email to the board of directors.

Synonyms

  • bullshit
    9.3 0.80/m

  • bullcrap
    9.7 0.21/m
    bullcrap is a milder rude slang people use to avoid harsher language, while BS is the cruder, blunt abbreviation.
  • bollocks
    9.9 0.04/m
    bollocks is strong British vulgar slang for nonsense often heard in speech, while BS is an informal abbreviation used more widely.
