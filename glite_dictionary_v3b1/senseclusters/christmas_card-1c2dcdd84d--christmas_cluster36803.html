Christmas card

noun
decorative card sent at Christmas
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪs·məs kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs·məs kɑːd / (BrE)
a decorative card sent as a greeting at Christmas, typically featuring holiday scenes or messages

Examples

A Christmas card greeting
