elimination tournament

noun
tournament where losers are eliminated each round
5.0 3.7
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɪməˈneɪʃən tɔːr.nə.mənt / (AmE), / ɪˌlɪmɪˈneɪʃən ˈtɔː.nə.mənt / (BrE)
a tournament in which participants who lose a match are removed from the competition in successive rounds until a winner is determined

Examples

An elimination tournament round
After losing in the second round of the elimination tournament, her team was out of the competition.
Many sports leagues organize their playoffs as an elimination tournament to create excitement and suspense.
The annual chess championship uses an elimination tournament format to determine the best player.
