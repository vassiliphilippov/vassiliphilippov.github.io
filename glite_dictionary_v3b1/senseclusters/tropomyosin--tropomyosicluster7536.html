tropomyosin

noun
muscle protein allergen
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌtroʊpoʊˈmaɪəsɪn / (AmE), / ˌtrɒpəʊˈmaɪəsɪn / (BrE)
a protein found in muscle and some shellfish that helps muscles contract, stabilizes cell structure, and can cause allergic reactions in some people
