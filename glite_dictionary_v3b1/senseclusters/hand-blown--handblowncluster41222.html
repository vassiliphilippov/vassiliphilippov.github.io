hand-blown

adjective
made by hand blowing
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhænd ˌbloʊn / (AmE), / ˈhænd ˌbləʊn / (BrE)
made by shaping glass by blowing air through a tube by hand, not by machine

Examples

Hand-blown glass
The artist displayed a collection of hand-blown glass ornaments at the gallery.
Each hand-blown vase is unique because of the traditional glassmaking process.
They admired the delicate patterns in the hand-blown wine glasses on the table.

Related Terms

  • antonym: machine-made - produced by a machine, not by hand
  • synonym: mouth-blown - made by blowing air into glass by mouth
