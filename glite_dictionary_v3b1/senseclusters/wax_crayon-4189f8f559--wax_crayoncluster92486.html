wax crayon

noun
colored wax stick for drawing or writing
Pronunciation: / wæks ˈkreɪ.ɑn / (AmE), / wæks ˈkreɪ.ən / (BrE)
a writing or drawing tool made of colored wax, used especially by children

Examples

A wax crayon drawing
He accidentally broke his favorite wax crayon while drawing a tree.
Teachers often use a wax crayon to mark student work.
The child colored the picture with a red wax crayon.
