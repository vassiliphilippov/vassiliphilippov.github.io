snakey

adjective
resembling a snake
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsneɪki / (AmE), / ˈsneɪki / (BrE)
having a surface, pattern, or appearance that resembles a snake, especially with scales or winding shapes

Examples

Snakey pattern
The artist painted a snakey design that twisted across the entire wall.
She wore a dress with a bold, snakey print to the party.
The river followed a snakey path through the valley, curving back and forth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scaly - covered with or resembling scales
  • synonym: serpentine - having a shape or movement like a snake
