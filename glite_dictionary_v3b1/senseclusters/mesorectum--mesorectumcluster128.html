mesorectum

noun
rectal fatty tissue
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛzoʊˈrɛktəm / (AmE), / ˌmiːsəʊˈrɛktəm / (BrE)
the fatty tissue surrounding the rectum that contains blood vessels, lymph nodes, and connective tissue

Examples

The mesorectum tissue
The surgeon carefully dissected the mesorectum during the procedure.
Cancer cells can sometimes spread through the mesorectum to nearby lymph nodes.
Preserving the mesorectum is important in certain types of rectal surgery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rectum - the final section of the large intestine
