creepies

noun
small crawling creatures
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːpiz / (AmE), / ˈkriːpiz / (BrE)
small animals or insects that creep or crawl, such as bugs or worms, used informally

Examples

Bugs and creepies
The children shrieked when they saw creepies crawling under the log in the garden.
She doesn't like camping because there are too many creepies in the tent at night.
After the rain, the sidewalk was covered with all sorts of creepies moving about.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bugs - small insects, especially those considered pests
  • synonym: creepy-crawlies - small insects or creatures that crawl, often considered unpleasant
