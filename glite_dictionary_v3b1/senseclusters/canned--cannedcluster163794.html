canned

adjective
preserved in a can or jar
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /kænd/ (AmE), /kænd/ (BrE)
(of food or drink) preserved and sealed in a can or jar for long-term storage.

Examples

Canned fruit
He prefers the taste of canned peaches over the fresh ones.
Many people keep a supply of canned soup in their pantry for emergencies.
The recipe calls for fresh tomatoes, but canned ones can be used as a substitute.
