fire hall

noun
fire station venue
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚ hɔl /; / ˈfaɪər hɔl / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə hɔːl / (BrE)
a building where firefighters and their equipment are based, often also used for community events or meetings

Examples

Local fire hall
The annual pancake breakfast was held at the local fire hall last Saturday.
Volunteers gathered in the fire hall to organize the town's emergency response plan.
After the parade, everyone met at the fire hall for refreshments and music.
The local community gathered at the fire hall for the annual safety demonstration.
Volunteers helped repaint the old fire hall to make it more welcoming.
After the storm, the fire hall served as a shelter for displaced families.

Related Terms

  • synonym: community center - a place where people from a local community can meet for social events
  • synonym: fire station - a building where fire engines and equipment are kept and firefighters work
