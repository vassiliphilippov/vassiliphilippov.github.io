possession

noun
state of being controlled by emotion or supernatural force
3.5
Pronunciation: / pəˈzɛʃ.ən / (AmE), / pəˈzɛʃ.ən / (BrE)
the state of being controlled by a strong emotion or by a supernatural force, such as a spirit or demon

Examples

Spirit possession
During the ceremony, several participants seemed to fall into a state of possession.
Some cultures believe that sudden changes in behavior may be caused by possession by spirits.
The film tells the disturbing story of a young girl’s possession by a malevolent demon.
