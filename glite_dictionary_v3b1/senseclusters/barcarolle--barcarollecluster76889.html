barcarolle

noun
Venetian gondolier's boat song
Pronunciation: / ˌbɑːrkəˈroʊl / (AmE), / ˌbɑːkəˈrɒl / (BrE)
a traditional song sung by Venetian gondoliers, or a musical composition in a similar style with a gentle rhythm reminiscent of rowing

Examples

A barcarolle song
He hummed a barcarolle as they drifted gently down the canal.
Offenbach's most famous piece is the delightful barcarolle from 'The Tales of Hoffmann'.
The orchestra performed a well-known barcarolle that evoked the charm of Venice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: serenade - a song sung or played in the open air, typically for a lover
