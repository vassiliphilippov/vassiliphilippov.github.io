homebuilt

noun
individually built machine
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌbɪlt / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌbɪlt / (BrE)
a vehicle, aircraft, or machine that is constructed by an individual, not produced in a factory.

Examples

A homebuilt car
He proudly displayed his homebuilt at the local car show.
Many aviation enthusiasts enjoy flying their own homebuilt aircraft.
Building a homebuilt requires patience, skill, and careful planning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: factory-made - produced in a factory, not by an individual
  • synonym: kit - a set of parts to be assembled by the buyer
