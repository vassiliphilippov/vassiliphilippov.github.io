abdominal muscle

noun
muscle of the abdomen
Pronunciation: / əbˈdɑːmɪnəl ˈmʌsəl / (AmE), / əbˈdɒmɪnəl ˈmʌsl̩ / (BrE)
any of the muscles located in the abdomen, involved in movement and support of the trunk

Examples

Strong abdominal muscle
An injury to an abdominal muscle can cause significant discomfort during movement.
Regular sit-ups can help strengthen your abdominal muscle and improve core stability.
She felt a sharp pain in her abdominal muscle after lifting the heavy box.
