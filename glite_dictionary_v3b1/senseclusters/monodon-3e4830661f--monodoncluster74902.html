Monodon

noun
genus of whales including the narwhal
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑː.nəˌdɑn / (AmE), / ˈmɒn.əˌdɒn / (BrE)
the genus of toothed whales that includes the narwhal, belonging to the family Monodontidae
