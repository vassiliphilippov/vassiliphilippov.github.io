broody hen

noun
hen ready to incubate eggs
5.4 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàbruÀêdi h…õn / (AmE), / ÀàbruÀêdi h…õn / (BrE)
A domestic hen that is ready to incubate eggs and tends to sit on them to hatch chicks.

Examples

A broody hen sits
If you want to hatch chicks naturally, a broody hen is essential.
She separated the broody hen from the others to prevent them from disturbing her eggs.
The farmer noticed that the broody hen refused to leave her nest for several days.
