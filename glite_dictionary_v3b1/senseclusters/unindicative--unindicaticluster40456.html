unindicative

adjective
not showing evidence
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈdɪkətɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈdɪkətɪv / (BrE)
not serving as a sign, indication, or evidence of something

Examples

Unindicative results
The test results were unindicative of any serious health problems.
Her calm expression was unindicative of the excitement she felt inside.
Market trends this month are unindicative of the company's long-term performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: uninformative - not providing useful or relevant information
