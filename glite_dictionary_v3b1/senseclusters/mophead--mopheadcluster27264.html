mophead

noun
hydrangea with round flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑpˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈmɒpˌhɛd / (BrE)
a type of hydrangea plant with large, rounded clusters of flowers that resemble a mop head

Examples

A blue mophead
The gardener admired the blue mophead blooming in the corner of the yard.
She prefers mophead hydrangeas because of their full, globe-shaped flowers.
A single pink mophead can add a splash of color to any garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hydrangea - a flowering plant with large, showy flower clusters
  • antonym: lacecap - a type of hydrangea with flat flower clusters
