tantra

noun
mystical system in Hinduism and Buddhism
Pronunciation: / ˈtæntrə / (AmE), / ˈtæntrə / (BrE)
a religious and philosophical system, especially in Hinduism and Buddhism, focusing on spiritual practices and rituals intended to achieve enlightenment and the realization of the oneness of self and the world, often involving mantras, mudras, and sometimes esoteric or erotic rites

Examples

Hindu tantra practice
Many Tibetan monks incorporate tantra into their religious practices.
She spent years studying tantra and its spiritual rituals in India.
The book explores the philosophical foundations and rituals of tantra in Eastern traditions.
