fluorogenic

adjective
producing fluorescence
Pronunciation: / ˌflʊrəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌflɔːrəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
producing fluorescence or capable of becoming fluorescent after a chemical reaction

Examples

Fluorogenic compound
The fluorogenic compound became brightly visible under ultraviolet light during the experiment.
Researchers used a fluorogenic substrate to detect enzyme activity in the sample.
A fluorogenic marker was added to the solution to monitor the chemical reaction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fluorescent - emitting light when exposed to radiation such as ultraviolet light
