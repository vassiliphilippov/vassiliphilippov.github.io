goad

noun
pointed tool for prodding animals
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊd / (AmE), / ɡəʊd / (BrE)
a pointed stick or instrument used to prod animals into motion

Examples

An ox goad
A sharp goad lay beside the oxen’s yoke, ready if they slowed down.
He carved a new goad after the old one broke during the plowing season.
The farmer used a goad to herd the cattle into the barn.
