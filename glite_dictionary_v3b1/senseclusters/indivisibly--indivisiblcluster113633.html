indivisibly

adverb
inseparably
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˈvɪzəbli / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈvɪzəbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be divided or separated into parts

Examples

Connected indivisibly
The two countries are indivisibly connected by their shared history and culture.
Justice and fairness are indivisibly linked in the foundation of the law.
The artist saw beauty and truth as indivisibly joined in her work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: divisibly - in a way that can be divided
  • synonym: inseparably - in a way that cannot be separated
