suffocatingly

adverb
in an oppressive way
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌfəˌkeɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈsʌfəkeɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that makes it difficult to breathe or feel free, often due to heat, lack of air, or restriction

Examples

A suffocatingly hot room
The small, windowless office felt suffocatingly cramped after a few hours.
She found the rules at her new school suffocatingly strict and hard to follow.
It was suffocatingly hot in the crowded subway car during rush hour.

Related Terms

  • antonym: freely - in a way that is open and without restriction
  • synonym: oppressively - in a way that is overwhelming or hard to endure
  • synonym: stiflingly - in a way that is very hot or stuffy and hard to breathe
