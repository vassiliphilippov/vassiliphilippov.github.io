methenamine

noun
antibacterial drug for urinary tract infections
Pronunciation: / məˈθɛnəˌmiːn / (AmE), / mɛˈθɛnəmiːn / (BrE)
a white crystalline compound used chiefly as an antibacterial agent to treat urinary tract infections
