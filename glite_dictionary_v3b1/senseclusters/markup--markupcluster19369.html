markup

noun
typesetting instructions added to text
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrkˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkʌp / (BrE)
written instructions or symbols added to copy, indicating how the text should be typeset or formatted before printing.

Examples

Text markup symbols
Before digital tools, manual markup was essential to communicate formatting needs to typesetters.
She learned the traditional markup symbols used in publishing during her internship.
The editor used red ink to add markup to the author's manuscript.
