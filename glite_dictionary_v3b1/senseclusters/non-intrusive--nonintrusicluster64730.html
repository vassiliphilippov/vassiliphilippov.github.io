non-intrusive

adjective
not disruptive
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn ɪnˈtruːsɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒn ɪnˈtruːsɪv / (BrE)
not causing disruption, disturbance, or damage; not involving entry or interference

Examples

A non-intrusive method
The new security system is non-intrusive and does not disturb daily activities.
We prefer non-intrusive advertising that does not interrupt the user experience.
Her non-intrusive approach made everyone feel comfortable during the meeting.
The doctor recommended a non-intrusive test that would not require any surgery.
Many people prefer non-intrusive security checks at airports to avoid delays and discomfort.
The new software runs in the background and is completely non-intrusive to users.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intrusive - causing disruption or unwanted attention
  • antonym: invasive - involving entry into the body or disruption of something
  • synonym: unobtrusive - not attracting attention or causing disturbance
