appear

verb
seem to be true or probable
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈpɪr / (AmE), / əˈpɪə / (BrE)
to give the impression of being or to seem (not necessarily true)

Examples

Appear to be true
From the evidence, it appears he left the country yesterday.
It appears that her invitation was lost in the mail.
Though the sky is cloudy, it appears we will avoid rain today.
He appeared unhappy with the results of the exam.
It may appear that she is angry, but she is just tired.
The task appears to be more complicated than we first thought.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disappear - to cease to be visible or apparent
  • synonym: look - to appear or seem in a particular way
  • synonym: seem - to give the impression of being something
