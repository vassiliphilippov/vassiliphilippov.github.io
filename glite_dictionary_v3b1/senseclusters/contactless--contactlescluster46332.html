contactless

noun
no-touch payment method
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑnˌtæktləs / (AmE), / ˈkɒntæktləs / (BrE)
a card, device, or payment method that allows transactions or data exchange without physical contact

Examples

A contactless card
Many people prefer using contactless for quick and easy payments at the supermarket.
The new bus system accepts contactless to make boarding faster for passengers.
You can pay for your coffee with contactless instead of using cash or coins.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chip-and-pin - a payment method using a card chip and PIN code
  • synonym: tap-and-go - a payment method where you tap a card or device to pay
