zócalo

proper noun
Mexico City main square
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊkəˌloʊ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊkələʊ / (BrE)
the main central public square in Mexico City, officially known as Plaza de la Constitución

Examples

Mexico City zócalo
Thousands of people gathered in the zócalo to celebrate Independence Day.
The historic cathedral overlooks the bustling zócalo in the heart of Mexico City.
Street performers entertained crowds in the zócalo on a sunny afternoon.
