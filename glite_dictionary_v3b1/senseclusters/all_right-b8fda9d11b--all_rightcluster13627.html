all right

adjective
satisfactory; acceptable condition
Pronunciation: / ɔl ˈraɪt / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈraɪt / (BrE)
satisfactory or acceptable; in good enough condition or adequate for the purpose.

Examples

She feels all right
After taking a short rest, she felt all right again.
The car is old, but it's still all right for short trips.
The food at the new restaurant was all right, but nothing special.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bad - of poor quality or unsatisfactory
  • synonym: fine - of high quality or satisfactory; acceptable
  • synonym: okay - satisfactory or acceptable; in good enough condition
