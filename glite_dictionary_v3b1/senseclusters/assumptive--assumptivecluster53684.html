assumptive

adjective
excessively forward or presumptuous
9.1
Pronunciation: / əˈsʌmp.tɪv / (AmE), / əˈsʌmp.tɪv / (BrE)
behaving in a way that is too confident or forward, especially by taking things for granted without justification

Examples

Assumptive attitude
Her assumptive behavior made others uncomfortable at the dinner party.
It is important not to come across as assumptive when dealing with clients.
The new intern was criticized for being too assumptive during team meetings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assuming - having or showing an arrogant attitude
  • antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low estimate of one's importance
  • synonym: presumptuous - showing too much confidence or taking liberties beyond one's place
