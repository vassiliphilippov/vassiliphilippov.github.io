untreated

adjective
not given medical treatment
5.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtritɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtriːtɪd / (BrE)
not having received medical attention, care, or treatment.

Examples

Untreated injuries
Many injuries remained untreated after the earthquake due to a lack of medical staff.
She was concerned about the effects of drinking untreated water while traveling.
The infection worsened because it was left untreated for several weeks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: treated - having received medical care or treatment
