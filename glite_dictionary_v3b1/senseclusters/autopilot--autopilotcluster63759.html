A mental state in which actions are carried out automatically and without conscious thought, typically due to habit or lack of attention.

Examples

On autopilot at work
After so many years in the job, he operated on autopilot most days.
During his long commute, he often drives on autopilot and hardly remembers the journey.
She completed the routine tasks on autopilot, barely aware of what she was doing.
