topdressing

noun
soil surface layer
4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtɑːp.dres.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈtɒp.dres.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
a layer of material, such as compost, fertilizer, or sand, spread over soil or grass to improve its quality or appearance
