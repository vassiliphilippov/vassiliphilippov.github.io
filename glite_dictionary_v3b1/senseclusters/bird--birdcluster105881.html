bird

noun
flesh of a bird used as food
Pronunciation: / bɝd / (AmE), / bɜːd / (BrE)
The flesh of a bird or fowl, wild or domestic, used as food.

Examples

Roast bird
He prefers the taste of wild bird to farmed chicken.
Roast bird was served with potatoes and vegetables for dinner.
The restaurant specializes in dishes made from game bird.

Related Terms

  • synonym: poultry - domesticated birds kept for their eggs or meat; also their flesh as food
