Chinese wood oil

noun
yellow oil from tung seeds used in wood products
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪniz wʊd ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪniːz wʊd ɔɪl / (BrE)
a yellow drying oil derived from the seeds of the tung tree, used in varnishes, paints, and wood finishes

Examples

Chinese wood oil finish
Many artists prefer Chinese wood oil because it dries quickly and gives a durable finish.
The restoration process included applying several coats of Chinese wood oil to the antique cabinet.
Traditional furniture makers often use Chinese wood oil to protect and enrich the wood's appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tung oil - a drying oil obtained from the seeds of the tung tree (often synonymous with Chinese wood oil)
