thumper

noun
vibrating construction machine
Pronunciation: / ˈθʌmpər / (AmE), / ˈθʌmpə / (BrE)
a large machine that produces strong vibrations, used in construction or road building to compact soil or materials

Examples

Road thumper machine
The crew used a thumper to compact the gravel before laying the asphalt.
A powerful thumper made the ground shake as it worked on the new road.
Before pouring the concrete, they ran a thumper over the soil to ensure stability.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compactor - a machine used to compress or compact soil or materials
  • synonym: roller - a heavy machine with a large cylinder used to flatten surfaces in construction
