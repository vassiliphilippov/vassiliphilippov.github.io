cozy up

verb
ingratiate oneself, often insincerely
6.0 5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈkoʊ.ziː ʌp/ /; / /ˈkoʊ.ziː əp/ (AmE), /ˈkəʊ.ziː ʌp/ /; / /ˈkəʊ.ziː əp/ (BrE)
to try to make someone like you or treat you well, often in order to gain a personal advantage, sometimes in an insincere way

Examples

To cozy up to someone
Don't cozy up to her just because she has connections.
He's trying to cozy up to the new boss in hopes of getting a promotion.
She was obviously cozying up to him to get invited to the party.
