intention

noun
plan or aim to act
1.0 1.9
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈten.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈten.ʃən/ (BrE)
a plan, aim, or purpose; what you mean or plan to do

Examples

Good intention
Despite his good intentions, the plan did not succeed.
It's always been my intention to travel the world after graduation.
She made her intention clear with a simple, direct statement.
Despite their good intentions, things did not go as planned.
He announced his intention to run for president next year.
Her intention was to finish the project before the deadline.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accident - an event that happens by chance, not intended
  • synonym: aim - a result that someone tries to achieve
  • synonym: goal - the object of a person's ambition or effort; an aim or desired result
  • synonym: intent - something that is intended; purpose or aim
  • synonym: plan - a detailed proposal for doing or achieving something
  • synonym: purpose - the reason for which something is done or created
