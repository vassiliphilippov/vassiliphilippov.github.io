biocatalyst

noun
substance catalyzing a biological reaction
2.8 6.2
Pronunciation: /baɪ.oʊ.ˈkæt.ə.lɪst/ (AmE), /baɪ.əʊ.ˈkæt.ə.lɪst/ (BrE)
