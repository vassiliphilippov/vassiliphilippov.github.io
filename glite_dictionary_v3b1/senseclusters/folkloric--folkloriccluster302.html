folkloric

adjective
traditional customs
Pronunciation: / foʊkˈlɔːrɪk / (AmE), / fəʊkˈlɒrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the traditional beliefs, stories, music, or customs of a community or culture

Examples

Folkloric stories and music
The museum displayed folkloric costumes from different regions of the country.
She specializes in folkloric tales that have been passed down for generations.
The dance troupe performed a folkloric piece inspired by rural traditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modern - relating to the present or recent times
  • synonym: mythical - relating to myths or legends
  • synonym: traditional - following customs or beliefs passed down through generations
