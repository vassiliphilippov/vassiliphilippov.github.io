grassed

adjective
covered with grass
2.3
Pronunciation: / ɡræst / (AmE), / ɡrɑːst / (BrE)
covered with grass or having a surface of grass.

Examples

Grassed slope

The children played soccer on the newly grassed field behind the school.
A narrow grassed path led from the house to the garden gate.
