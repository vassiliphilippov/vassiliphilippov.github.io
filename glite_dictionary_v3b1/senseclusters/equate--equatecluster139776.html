equate

verb
be or show as equal, esp. in math
Pronunciation: / ɪˈkweɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈkweɪt / (BrE)
(in mathematics) To be or show as equal or equivalent in value, amount, function, or meaning.

Examples

To equate x to 10
In this equation, we equate x to 10 to find the solution.
Mathematicians often equate two expressions to prove they are identical.
You must equate both sides of the equation to solve for y.

Related Terms

  • synonym: equal - being the same in quantity, size, degree, or value
