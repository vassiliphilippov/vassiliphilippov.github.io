suicide

noun
the act of killing oneself
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːəˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈsuːɪsaɪd / (BrE)
the act of intentionally killing oneself

Examples

Commit suicide
He talked openly about his past thoughts of suicide during the interview.
News reports highlighted an increase in suicide rates among teenagers.
She struggled with depression for years before ultimately committing suicide.

Related Terms

  • antonym: murder - the unlawful killing of another person with intent
  • degree lower: self-harm - the act of deliberately injuring your own body without suicidal intent
