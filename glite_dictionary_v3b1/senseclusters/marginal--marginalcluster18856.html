marginal

adjective
at or near an edge or border
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːrdʒɪnəl / (AmE), / ˈmɑːdʒɪnəl / (BrE)
located at or forming the border or edge of something.

Examples

Marginal land
A narrow marginal path runs along the riverbank.
Only a marginal area of grass separated the forest from the fields.
The developer wants to build on the marginal land around the lake.

Related Terms

  • antonym: central - located at or forming the center; opposite of marginal location
