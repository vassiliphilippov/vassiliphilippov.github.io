country house

noun
large countryside estate house
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌntri haʊs / (AmE), / ˈkʌntri haʊs / (BrE)
a large and impressive house in the countryside, often on a private estate

Examples

A grand country house
He inherited a grand country house that had been in his family for generations.
The old country house was surrounded by acres of lush gardens and woodland.
They spent the summer relaxing at their country house in the hills.
