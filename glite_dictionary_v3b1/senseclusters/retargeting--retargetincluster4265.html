retargeting

noun
online ad strategy
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈtɑːrɡətɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈtɑːɡɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a marketing strategy that displays ads to people who have previously visited a website or interacted with a brand online

Examples

Online retargeting ads
Many online retailers use retargeting to encourage customers to complete their purchases.
After visiting the travel website, she noticed retargeting ads for flights on other sites.
Effective retargeting can increase the chances of turning visitors into buyers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: remarketing - a marketing technique targeting previous visitors with ads
  • degree lower: targeting - focusing marketing efforts on a specific group
