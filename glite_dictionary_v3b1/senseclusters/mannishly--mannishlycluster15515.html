mannishly

adverb
in a masculine way
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmænɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈmænɪʃli / (BrE)
in a way that is characteristic of or resembles a man, especially in appearance or behavior

Examples

mannishly dressed
She wore her hair mannishly short and dressed in a suit.
The actress walked mannishly across the stage, surprising the audience with her bold style.
He spoke mannishly, using a deep voice and confident gestures during the performance.

  • synonym: masculinely - in a masculine or manly manner
