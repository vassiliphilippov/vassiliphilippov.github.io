oxpecker

noun
African tick-eating bird
Pronunciation: / ˈɑksˌpɛkər / (AmE), / ˈɒksˌpɛkə / (BrE)
a small African bird that eats ticks and insects found on large mammals such as buffalo, rhinos, and cattle
