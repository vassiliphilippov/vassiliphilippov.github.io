supernormal

adjective
beyond normal or scientific explanation
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈnɔrməl / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈnɔːməl / (BrE)
beyond the range of the normal or scientifically explainable; exceeding what is considered typical or natural.

Examples

Supernormal powers
Some people claim to have supernormal abilities, such as telepathy or clairvoyance.
The child's supernormal memory amazed even the most skeptical scientists.
The experimenters were searching for evidence of supernormal perception in their subjects.
