gun

noun
the firing of a gun as a signal or salute
Pronunciation: / ɡʌn / (AmE), / ɡʌn / (BrE)
the discharge of a firearm used as a signal or salute, especially in sporting events or military ceremonies.

Examples

A starting gun
He heard the gun and knew the race had officially started.
The athletes waited for the gun before beginning the race.
The ceremony included a twenty-one gun salute in honor of the president.

Related Terms

  • synonym: salute - a gesture or action in recognition or honor, especially firing of guns as a military honor
