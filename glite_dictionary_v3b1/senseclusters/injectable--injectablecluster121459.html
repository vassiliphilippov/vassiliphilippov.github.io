injectable

noun
medicine for injection
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʒɛktəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒɛktəbəl / (BrE)
a medicine or substance that is designed to be administered by injection with a needle

Examples

An injectable medicine
The doctor prescribed an injectable to treat her severe allergy symptoms.
Some vaccines are only available as injectables rather than pills.
Before traveling, he received an injectable to protect against certain diseases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: injection - the act of putting a substance into the body using a needle
  • antonym: oral medication - medicine taken by mouth
