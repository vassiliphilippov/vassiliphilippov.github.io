rechamber

verb
to modify for new cartridge
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈtʃeɪmbər / (AmE), / ˌriːˈtʃeɪmbə / (BrE)
to modify a firearm or device so that it can use a different cartridge or chamber size

Examples

To rechamber a rifle
The gunsmith will rechamber the old rifle to fit modern ammunition.
After purchasing the firearm, he decided to rechamber it for a more common caliber.
It is possible to rechamber certain pistols to improve their performance or compatibility.
