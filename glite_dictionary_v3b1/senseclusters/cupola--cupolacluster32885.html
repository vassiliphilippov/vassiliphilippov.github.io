cupola

noun
dome-shaped roof structure
8.7 0.7/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàkjuÀêp…ôl…ô / (AmE), / ÀàkjuÀêp…ôl…ô / (BrE)
A small roof or ceiling structure in the shape of a dome, often placed on top of a larger roof or dome as an architectural feature.

Examples

A rooftop cupola
Sunlight streamed through the glass cupola at the top of the rotunda.
The architect designed a small cupola to crown the central hall of the mansion.
The old town hall is famous for its green copper cupola that rises above the skyline.
