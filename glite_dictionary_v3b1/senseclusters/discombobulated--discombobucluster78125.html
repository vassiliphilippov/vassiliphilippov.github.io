discombobulated

adjective
confused; thrown into disorder
8.0 7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪskəmˈbɑbjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌdɪskəmˈbɒbjʊˌleɪtɪd / (BrE)
