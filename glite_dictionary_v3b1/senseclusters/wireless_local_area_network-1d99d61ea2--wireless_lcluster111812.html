wireless local area network

noun
Wi-Fi-based local area network
4.2 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààwa…™r.l…™s Ààlo ä.k…ôl Àà…õr.i…ô Ààn…õt.w…ùk / (AmE), / Ààwa…™…ô.l…ôs Ààl…ô ä.k…ôl Ààe…ô.ri.…ô Ààn…õt.w…úÀêk / (BrE)
A local area network that transmits and receives data wirelessly using high-frequency radio signals over short distances, typically following the Ethernet protocol.

Examples

A wireless local area network setup
Security measures are important when setting up a wireless local area network in a public space.
The new office was equipped with a wireless local area network to provide internet access to all employees.
You can connect your laptop to the wireless local area network for fast and convenient web browsing.
