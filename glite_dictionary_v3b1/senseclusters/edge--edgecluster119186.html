edge

noun
sharp border of a surface
Pronunciation: / ɛdʒ / (AmE), / ɛdʒ / (BrE)
the outer line, border, or limit of an object, area, or surface, often sharp or distinct

Examples

Be careful not to cut yourself on the edge of the metal sheet.
