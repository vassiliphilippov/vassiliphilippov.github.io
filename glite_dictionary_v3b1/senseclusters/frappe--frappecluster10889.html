frappe

noun
a thick milkshake with ice cream
6.6 6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfræp /; / fræˈpeɪ / (AmE), / ˈfræpeɪ / (BrE)
a cold, thick milkshake or blended drink made with ice, milk, and flavorings such as coffee or fruit (sometimes with ice cream)

Examples

Thick frappe milkshake
In New England, a frappe always contains ice cream, unlike a regular milkshake.
She ordered a chocolate frappe to cool off on the hot afternoon.
The diner is famous for its thick and creamy frappes.
On a hot afternoon, she enjoyed a cold frappe at the café.
He ordered a strawberry frappe with extra whipped cream.
Many people like to start their day with an iced coffee frappe in the summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: milkshake - a cold drink made of milk, a sweet flavouring such as fruit or chocolate, and usually ice cream, mixed together until it is thick and creamy
  • synonym: smoothie - a thick, cold drink made from blended fruit and juice or milk
