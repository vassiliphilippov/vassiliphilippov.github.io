tetragram

noun
word or sequence of four letters
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛtrəˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈtɛtrəɡræm / (BrE)
a word or sequence written with four letters in an alphabetic writing system

Examples

A tetragram word
Each tetragram in the puzzle had to be filled in with a different set of four letters.
Scrabble players often look for high-scoring tetragrams to quickly use up their tiles.
The word 'lamp' is a simple example of a tetragram.
