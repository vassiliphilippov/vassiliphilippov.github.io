ochred

adjective
colored with ocher
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊkərd / (AmE), / ˈɒkəd / (BrE)
colored or stained with ocher, a yellow-brown natural pigment

Examples

Ochred walls
The walls of the cave were ochred by prehistoric artists using natural pigments.
Her hands became ochred after working with the earthy paint all afternoon.
The old manuscript had ochred edges from years of exposure to dust and minerals.
