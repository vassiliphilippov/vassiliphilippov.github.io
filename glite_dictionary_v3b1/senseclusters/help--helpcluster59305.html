help

verb
be unable to stop oneself (with negative)
3.1
Pronunciation: / hɛlp / (AmE), / hɛlp / (BrE)
(always with negative) to be unable to prevent oneself from doing something; to find it impossible not to do something

Examples

Can't help laughing
He tried to ignore the noise, but he just couldn't help hearing it.
I couldn't help laughing at his joke, even though it was silly.
She can't help worrying about her exams.

Related Terms

  • antonym: restrain - to prevent oneself from acting on an impulse
