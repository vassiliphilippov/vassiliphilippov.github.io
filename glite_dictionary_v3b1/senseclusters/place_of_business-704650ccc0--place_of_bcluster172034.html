place of business

noun
online or virtual business presence
3.7
Pronunciation: /pleɪs əv ˈbɪz.nəs/ /; / /pleɪs əv ˈbɪz.nɪs/ (AmE), /pleɪs əv ˈbɪz.nəs/ /; / /pleɪs əv ˈbɪz.nɪs/ (BrE)
(of a business) an online or virtual presence (such as a website or mobile app) that functions as the business's storefront or primary location for offering goods or services.

Examples

Online place of business
Many startups now list their place of business as an online storefront instead of a shop.
Customers expect a clear return policy on the company's place of business before purchasing online.
For tax purposes, some jurisdictions treat a business website as its official place of business.

Related Terms

  • antonym: business premises - a physical building or property used for business activities
  • synonym: online storefront - a website or app where a business displays and sells products or services
