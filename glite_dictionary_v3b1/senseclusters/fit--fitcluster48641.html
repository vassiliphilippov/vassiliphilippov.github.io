fit

adjective
in good health; strong and healthy
Pronunciation: / fɪt / (AmE), / fɪt / (BrE)
in good physical and mental health; strong, healthy, and active

Examples

Fit and healthy
After months of training, she felt fit enough to run the marathon.
He joined a gym to get fit before his hiking trip.
Regular exercise helps me stay fit both physically and mentally.
