sukkah

noun
Jewish festival hut
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsʊ.kə/ (AmE), /ˈsʊ.kə/ (BrE)
a temporary hut with a roof of branches or leaves, built and used during the Jewish festival of Sukkot
