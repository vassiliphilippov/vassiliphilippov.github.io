tiger rattlesnake

noun
a rattlesnake species from Arizona and Mexico
5.0 7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈtaɪ.gər ˈræt.əl.sneɪk/ (AmE), /ˈtaɪ.gə ˈræt.əl.sneɪk/ (BrE)
A species of rattlesnake with irregular crossbands on its back, found in arid foothills and canyons of southern Arizona and Mexico.
