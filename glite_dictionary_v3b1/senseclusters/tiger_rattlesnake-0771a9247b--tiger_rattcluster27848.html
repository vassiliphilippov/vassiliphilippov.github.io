tiger rattlesnake

noun
a rattlesnake species from Arizona and Mexico
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪɡɚ ˈræt̬lˌsneɪk / (AmE), / ˈtaɪɡə ˈrætlˌsneɪk / (BrE)
A species of rattlesnake with irregular crossbands on its back, found in arid foothills and canyons of southern Arizona and Mexico.

Examples

The tiger rattlesnake species
Hikers in southern Arizona should watch for tiger rattlesnakes in rocky canyons.
Researchers tagged a tiger rattlesnake to study its movements in the desert.
The tiger rattlesnake is known for its distinctive cross-banded back and small rattle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rattlesnake - a venomous American pit viper with a segmented rattle at the end of its tail
