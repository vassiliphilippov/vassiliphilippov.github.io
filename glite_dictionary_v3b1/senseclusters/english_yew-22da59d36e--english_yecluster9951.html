English yew

noun
a long-lived European yew tree species
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ juː / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ juː / (BrE)
a species of yew tree native to Europe, known for its longevity and slow growth; Taxus baccata
