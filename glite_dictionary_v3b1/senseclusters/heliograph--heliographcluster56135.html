heliograph

verb
send signal with reflected sunlight
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhiː.li.əˌɡræf / (AmE), / ˈhiː.li.əˌɡrɑːf / (BrE)
to send a message by reflecting sunlight with a mirror, usually as a form of signal communication

Examples

To heliograph a message
During the expedition, they heliographed an urgent message across the valley.
She was taught how to heliograph in case radio communication was lost.
The scouts heliographed their position to the base camp using a small mirror.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flash - to shine or send a brief light signal
