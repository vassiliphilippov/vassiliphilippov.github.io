block grant

noun
large government grant for broad purposes
5.2 3.3
Pronunciation: /blɑːk grænt/ (AmE), /blɒk grɑːnt/ (BrE)
a grant of money from a central government to a local or regional authority to be used for a broad purpose, typically with few restrictions

Examples

A block grant from government
Debate continues over whether block grants provide enough oversight to ensure funds are used effectively.
Many education initiatives are funded through federal block grants to local school districts.
The state received a block grant to expand its healthcare services for low-income families.
