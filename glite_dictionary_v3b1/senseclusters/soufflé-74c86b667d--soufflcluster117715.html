soufflé

verb
to make puffed dish
Pronunciation: / suːˈfleɪ / (AmE), / suːˈfleɪ / (BrE)
to prepare or cook a dish by folding beaten egg whites into a base mixture so that it puffs up when baked

Examples

To soufflé eggs
She decided to soufflé the spinach for dinner to impress her in-laws.
If you soufflé the chocolate mixture carefully, it will rise beautifully in the oven.
He learned how to soufflé eggs during his cooking class in Paris.
