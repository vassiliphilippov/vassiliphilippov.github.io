meningococcemia

noun
blood infection by bacteria
9.9
Pronunciation: / məˌnɪŋɡoʊkɑkˈsiːmiə / (AmE), / mɛˌnɪŋɡəʊkɒkˈsiːmiə / (BrE)
a serious infection in which meningococcal bacteria are present in the bloodstream

Examples

Meningococcemia infection
Doctors must act quickly when meningococcemia is suspected to prevent severe complications.
The hospital treated several cases of meningococcemia after the outbreak in the community.
Early symptoms of meningococcemia can resemble those of the flu, making diagnosis challenging.

Related Terms

  • antonym: meningitis - inflammation of the membranes covering the brain and spinal cord
  • synonym: septicemia - a serious bloodstream infection caused by bacteria
