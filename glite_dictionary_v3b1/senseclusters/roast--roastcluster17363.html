roast

noun
humorous mocking criticism, often at an event
2.5 7.3
Pronunciation: / roʊst / (AmE), / rəʊst / (BrE)
a speech or event in which a person is subjected to humorously insulting and critical comments, often as entertainment; negative or mocking criticism

Examples

A comedy roast
After enduring that brutal roast, he admitted he had never felt more embarrassed and amused at the same time.
She wasn't offended by the roast; in fact, she laughed the hardest at the harshest remarks.
The comedian hosted a roast where celebrities took turns making scathing jokes about each other.

  • antonym: praise - expression of approval or admiration
