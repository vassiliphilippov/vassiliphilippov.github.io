apply

verb
request formally (job, school, etc.)
3.2
Pronunciation: / əˈplaɪ / (AmE), / əˈplaɪ / (BrE)
to make a formal request, especially in writing, for something such as a job, permission, or admission

Examples

To apply for a job
Hundreds of candidates applied for the position at the company.
She decided to apply for the scholarship before the deadline.
You can apply online for a visa if you have all the required documents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: request - to ask for something formally or politely
  • antonym: withdraw - to remove or take back (especially a request or application)
