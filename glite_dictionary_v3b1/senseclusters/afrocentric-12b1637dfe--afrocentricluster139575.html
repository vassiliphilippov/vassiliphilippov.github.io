Afrocentric

noun
supporter of Afrocentrism
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌæfroʊˈsɛntrɪk / (AmE), / ˌæfrəʊˈsɛntrɪk / (BrE)
a person who supports or promotes Afrocentrism, emphasizing African culture, history, or perspectives

Examples

An Afrocentric leader
As an Afrocentric, she organized lectures showing African contributions to world history.
The conference attracted many Afrocentrics interested in promoting African heritage.
He became an outspoken Afrocentric after studying African civilizations in college.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Afrocentrist - a person who advocates for Afrocentrism
  • antonym: Eurocentric - a person who focuses on European culture or history
