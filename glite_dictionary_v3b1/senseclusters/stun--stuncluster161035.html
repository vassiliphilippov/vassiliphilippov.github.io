stun

verb
make someone or something senseless or dazed
Pronunciation: / stʌn / (AmE), / stʌn / (BrE)
to make someone or something senseless, dazed, or temporarily unconscious, usually by a blow or shock

Examples

To stun an opponent
Boxers are trained to recover quickly if they are stunned by a punch in the ring.
The fisherman used an electric current to stun the fish before catching them.
The loud explosion managed to stun everyone in the building for a few moments.
A falling branch struck the animal, temporarily stunning it.
He was stunned by a sudden punch before he could react.
The force of the blow managed to stun the attacker for several minutes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: daze - make someone unable to think clearly
  • synonym: knock out - to make someone unconscious, esp. by a blow
  • antonym: revive - restore consciousness or alertness
