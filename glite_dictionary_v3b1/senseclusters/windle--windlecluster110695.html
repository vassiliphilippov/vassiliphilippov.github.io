windle

noun
bundle of yarn
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndəl / (AmE), / ˈwɪndəl / (BrE)
a small coil or bundle of thread, yarn, or similar material wound together for storage or use

Examples

A windle of yarn
She kept a neat windle of yarn in the basket for quick repairs.
The weaver reached for a windle to continue her work on the loom.
Each windle was carefully labeled with the type and color of thread.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ball - a rounded mass of thread or yarn wound together
  • synonym: skein - a length of thread or yarn wound in a loose long coil
