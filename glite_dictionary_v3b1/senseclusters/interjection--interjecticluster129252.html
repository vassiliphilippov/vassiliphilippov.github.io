interjection

noun
abrupt exclamatory word or phrase
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntɚˈdʒɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈdʒɛkʃən / (BrE)
a word or phrase used as an abrupt exclamation expressing emotion, such as surprise, pain, or joy

Examples

The interjection 'wow'
In English, the interjection 'wow' conveys surprise or admiration.
Teachers often explain how an interjection can add emotion to a sentence.
The word 'ouch' is an interjection used to express pain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exclamation - an abrupt or sudden utterance expressing strong emotion
