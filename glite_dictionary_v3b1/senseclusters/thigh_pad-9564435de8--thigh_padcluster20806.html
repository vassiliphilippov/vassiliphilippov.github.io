thigh pad

noun
protective pad for the thigh (sports)
4.6
Pronunciation: / θaɪ pæd / (AmE), / θaɪ pad / (BrE)
a protective pad worn over the thigh to prevent injury, especially in sports such as football and cricket

Examples

A thigh pad in football

The cricketer adjusted his thigh pad before facing the next ball.
