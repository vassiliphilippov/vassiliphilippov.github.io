hyperphosphatemia

noun
high blood phosphate
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˌfɑsfəˈtiːmiə / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəˌfɒsfəˈtiːmiə / (BrE)
a medical condition in which there is an abnormally high level of phosphate in the blood

Examples

High hyperphosphatemia level
The doctor diagnosed the patient with hyperphosphatemia after reviewing the blood test results.
Chronic kidney disease can often lead to hyperphosphatemia if not properly managed.
Treatment for hyperphosphatemia may include dietary changes and medications to lower phosphate levels.
