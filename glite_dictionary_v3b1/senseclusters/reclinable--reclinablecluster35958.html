reclinable

adjective
can lean back
4.2
Pronunciation: / rɪˈklaɪnəbəl / (AmE), / rɪˈklaɪnəbəl / (BrE)
able to be adjusted so that it leans or lies back at an angle

Examples

Reclinable chair
The reclinable chair allowed her to relax comfortably after a long day.
Passengers appreciated the reclinable seats on the overnight train.
He bought a reclinable sofa for the living room to watch movies in comfort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adjustable - able to be changed to different positions or settings
  • antonym: fixed - not able to be changed or moved
