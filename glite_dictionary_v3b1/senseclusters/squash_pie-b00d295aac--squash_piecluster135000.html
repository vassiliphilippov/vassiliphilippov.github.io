squash pie

noun
sweet pie made with winter squash
Pronunciation: / skwɑːʃ paɪ / (AmE), / skwɒʃ paɪ / (BrE)
A sweet pie similar to pumpkin pie, made with winter squash purée instead of pumpkin.

Examples

A squash pie dessert
If you can't find pumpkin, a squash pie makes an excellent substitute.
My grandmother always bakes a delicious squash pie for Thanksgiving dinner.
We used butternut squash to make the filling for our homemade squash pie.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pumpkin pie - a sweet pie made from pumpkin purée
