scorepad

noun
blank music notebook
Pronunciation: / ˈskɔːrˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈskɔːpæd / (BrE)
a pad or notebook with blank staff paper used for writing musical notation

Examples

Music scorepad
The composer quickly jotted down his ideas in his scorepad during the rehearsal break.
She always carries a scorepad in her bag in case inspiration strikes.
Before composing at the piano, he prepared a fresh scorepad to write out melodies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manuscript paper - blank paper printed with staff lines for writing music
  • synonym: notebook - a book of blank pages for writing notes
