domino

noun
cloak and mask for masquerade
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːmɪˌnoʊ /; / ˈdɑːməˌnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdɒmɪnəʊ / (BrE)
a loose cloak and/or mask worn at a masquerade to hide your identity, especially in the past

Examples

In many Venetian carnivals, the classic black domino is a popular choice.
