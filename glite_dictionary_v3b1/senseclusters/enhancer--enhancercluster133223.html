enhancer

noun
something that improves
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈhænsər / (AmE), / ɪnˈhɑːnsə / (BrE)
a substance or thing that improves or increases the quality, value, or effect of something else

Some shampoos contain a shine enhancer to make hair look healthier.
