lanceman

noun
maker of lances
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlænsˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈlɑːnsmən / (BrE)
a person who makes, sells, or repairs lances or similar pole weapons

Examples

Skilled lanceman repairs weapons
The castle employed a skilled lanceman to maintain the knights' weapons.
A lanceman crafted new lances for the upcoming tournament.
During the festival, the lanceman displayed his finest pole weapons to the crowd.

Related Terms

  • synonym: armourer - a person who makes or repairs weapons and armor
  • synonym: blacksmith - a person who makes and repairs things in iron by hand
