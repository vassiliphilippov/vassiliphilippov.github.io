semideveloped

adjective
partly processed
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˌdɪˈvɛləpt / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˌdɪˈvɛləpt / (BrE)
partly processed but not fully developed, especially referring to photographic film or paper that has not completed the development process

Examples

Semideveloped film
The technician realized the semideveloped film would not produce clear images.
She found a box of semideveloped photographs in her grandfather's attic.
If the paper is only semideveloped, the picture will appear faint and incomplete.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: undeveloped - not processed or developed at all
