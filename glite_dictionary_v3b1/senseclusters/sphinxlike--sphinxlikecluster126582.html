sphinxlike

adjective
resembling a sphinx
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsfɪŋksˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈsfɪŋksˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having the appearance, form, or qualities of a sphinx, especially being monumental or statue-like with a human head and a lion's body

Examples

Sphinxlike statue
The ancient temple was guarded by two sphinxlike figures carved from stone.
Her expression was so calm and sphinxlike that no one could guess her thoughts.
A sphinxlike monument stood at the center of the plaza, attracting many tourists.

Related Terms

  • synonym: monumental - large, impressive, and resembling a monument
  • synonym: statuesque - having a tall, dignified, and graceful appearance like a statue
