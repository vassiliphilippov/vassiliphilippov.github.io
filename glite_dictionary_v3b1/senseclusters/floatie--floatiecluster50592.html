floatie

noun
inflatable swimming aid
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfloʊti / (AmE), / ˈfləʊti / (BrE)
an inflatable object, usually worn on the arms, used to help someone float in water

Examples

Bright floaties on arms
The little boy wore bright yellow floaties while learning to swim in the pool.
Parents often use floaties to help their children feel safe in the water.
She forgot her floaties at home, so she stayed in the shallow end.

Related Terms

  • synonym: armband - an inflatable band worn on the arm to help with swimming
  • synonym: life jacket - a vest designed to keep a person afloat in water
