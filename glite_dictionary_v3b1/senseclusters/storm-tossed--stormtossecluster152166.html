storm-tossed

adjective
buffeted by storms or adversities
7.9 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / Ààst…îrm Àåt…ëst / (AmE), / Ààst…îÀêm Àåt…íst / (BrE)
Pounded or buffeted repeatedly by storms or, figuratively, by many difficulties or adversities.

Examples

Storm-tossed sea
After months of storm-tossed negotiations, the two countries finally reached an agreement.
The storm-tossed ship struggled to stay afloat in the raging sea.
The poet described his life as a storm-tossed journey, filled with challenges and hardships.
