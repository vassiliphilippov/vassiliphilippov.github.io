cornucopian

noun
optimist about resources
Pronunciation: / ˌkɔrnjuˈkoʊpiən / (AmE), / ˌkɔːnjuˈkəʊpiən / (BrE)
a person who believes that human innovation and technology will ensure abundant resources and overcome natural limitations

Examples

A cornucopian thinker
As a cornucopian, she argued that technology would solve future food shortages.
The panel included a cornucopian who believed in endless resource growth.
Critics of the cornucopian view warn about the risks of overpopulation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: optimist - a person who expects positive outcomes
  • antonym: pessimist - a person who expects negative outcomes
