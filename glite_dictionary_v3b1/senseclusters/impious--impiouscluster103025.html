impious

adjective
lacking respect for religion or God
9.9 7.6
Pronunciation: /ˈɪm.pɪ.əs/ /; / /ɪm.ˈpaɪ.əs/ (AmE), /ˈɪm.pɪ.əs/ /; / /ɪm.ˈpaɪ.əs/ (BrE)
showing a lack of respect or reverence for God, religion, or anything considered sacred

Examples

Impious behavior

Some viewed her refusal to observe the tradition as impious behavior.
The novel was banned for being considered impious by religious authorities.
