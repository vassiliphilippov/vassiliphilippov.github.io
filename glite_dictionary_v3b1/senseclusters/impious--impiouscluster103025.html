impious

adjective
lacking respect for religion or God
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmpiəs /; / ˈɪm.pi.əs / (AmE), / ˈɪmpɪəs /; / ˈɪm.pɪ.əs / (BrE)
showing a lack of respect or reverence for God, religion, or anything considered sacred

Examples

Impious behavior
He was accused of being impious and disrespectful toward established traditions.
Her impious remarks offended the deeply religious audience.
The play was banned for its impious treatment of sacred rituals.
His impious laughter shocked the elders during the solemn ceremony.
Some viewed her refusal to observe the tradition as impious behavior.
The novel was banned for being considered impious by religious authorities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irreverent - showing lack of respect for people or things that are generally taken seriously
  • antonym: pious - showing reverence and devotion to religion
