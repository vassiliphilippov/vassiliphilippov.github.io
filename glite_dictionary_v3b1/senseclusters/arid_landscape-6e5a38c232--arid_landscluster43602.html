arid landscape

noun
extremely dry and barren land
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈærɪd ˈlændˌskeɪp / (AmE), / ˈærɪd ˈlændskeɪp / (BrE)
A region of land that is extremely dry and lacking in vegetation, often appearing barren.

Examples

An arid landscape
After months without rain, the once green valley turned into an arid landscape.
Artists are often inspired by the colors and shapes of the arid landscape.
The arid landscape of the region made agriculture nearly impossible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: desert - an arid, sandy region with little or no vegetation
