multimodal

adjective
using multiple methods
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈmoʊdəl / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈməʊdəl / (BrE)
involving or using two or more different methods, especially of transport, in a single process or journey

Examples

Multimodal transport
The company chose a multimodal approach, combining rail and sea transport for faster delivery.
A multimodal system can reduce costs by using the most efficient transport methods for each part of the journey.
They planned a multimodal route that included both trucking and air freight to meet the deadline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intermodal - involving two or more different modes of transport in a journey
  • antonym: unimodal - involving only one mode or method
