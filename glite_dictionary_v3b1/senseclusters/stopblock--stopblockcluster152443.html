stopblock

noun
workpiece positioning device
7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈstɑːp.blɑːk/ /; / /ˈstɑːp.blək/ (AmE), /ˈstɒp.blɒk/ /; / /ˈstɒp.blək/ (BrE)
a device attached to a tool or workbench to position and stop a workpiece at a fixed point for repeated operations

Examples

Workbench stopblock
He set a stopblock on the saw fence to make identical cuts on the wooden boards.
Using a stopblock helps ensure each piece of wood is cut to the exact same length.
The carpenter adjusted the stopblock before starting the series of repetitive cuts.
