pop

noun
a short, sharp explosive sound
3.2
Pronunciation: / pɑp / (AmE), / pɒp / (BrE)
a short, sharp, explosive sound, like that made by a burst or a cork being drawn from a bottle

Examples

A loud pop
A sudden pop from the engine made everyone jump.
She opened the bottle with a quick pop and poured the wine.
There was a loud pop as the balloon burst.
