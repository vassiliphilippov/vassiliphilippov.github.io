chucklehead

noun
silly person
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌkəlˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌkəlˌhɛd / (BrE)
a person who is silly or acts foolishly

Examples

A silly chucklehead
Don't be such a chucklehead and remember to lock the door next time.
She laughed and called her brother a chucklehead for wearing mismatched socks.
Only a chucklehead would try to fix the computer by hitting it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fool - a person who lacks good sense or judgment
  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence
  • synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
