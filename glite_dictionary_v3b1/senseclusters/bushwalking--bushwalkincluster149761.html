bushwalking

noun
hiking in bushland
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊʃˌwɔːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of walking for pleasure through natural countryside or bushland, especially in Australia

Examples

bushwalking in nature
They spent the weekend bushwalking in the national park.
Many Australians enjoy bushwalking as a way to explore nature and stay fit.
Before going bushwalking, make sure you have enough water and a map of the area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hiking - the activity of going for long walks in the countryside
  • synonym: trekking - the activity of making a long and difficult journey on foot
