thirtyish

adjective
about age thirty
Pronunciation: / ˈθɜːrtiɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈθɜːtiɪʃ / (BrE)
appearing to be about thirty years old or in one's early thirties

Examples

Thirtyish man
The new manager is a thirtyish woman with lots of energy.
He looked thirtyish, though his actual age was never mentioned.
A group of thirtyish friends gathered to celebrate the reunion.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: middle-aged - between young adulthood and old age, typically 40–65 years old
  • degree lower: twentyish - appearing to be about twenty years old
