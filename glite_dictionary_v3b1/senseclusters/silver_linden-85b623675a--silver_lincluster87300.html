silver linden

noun
a tree with silvery-white leaf undersides
9.9 7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvɚ ˈlɪndən / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˈlɪndɪn / (BrE)
a large deciduous tree native to eastern Europe and Asia Minor, with leaves that have a silvery-white underside; often planted as an ornamental tree

Examples

Many city streets are lined with silver linden trees for shade and beauty.
The silver linden is prized for its attractive foliage in parks and gardens.
