footrest

noun
a support for resting the feet
1.9 1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊtˌrɛst / (AmE), / ˈfʊtˌrɛst / (BrE)
A low support on which a person who is sitting can rest their feet.

Examples

A footrest under chair
After a long day, he relaxed in the recliner and put his feet up on the footrest.
She bought a comfortable footrest to use under her desk at work.
The armchair came with a matching footrest for extra comfort.
