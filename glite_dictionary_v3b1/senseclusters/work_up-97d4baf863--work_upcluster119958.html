work up

verb
become agitated or upset
7.5
Pronunciation: / wɝk ʌp / (AmE), / wɜːk ʌp / (BrE)
to become agitated, excited, or emotionally upset, often as a result of thinking or talking about something

Examples

To work up over mistakes
He tends to work up over small mistakes.
She was worked up about the delay in her travel plans.
Try not to get too worked up before your presentation.
