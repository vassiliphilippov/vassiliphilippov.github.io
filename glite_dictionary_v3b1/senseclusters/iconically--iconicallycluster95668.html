iconically

adverb
in the style of icons
7.5
Pronunciation: / aɪˈkɑːnɪkli / (AmE), / aɪˈkɒnɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that resembles or is characteristic of religious icons, especially in a formal or stylized way

Examples

Iconically painted saints
The artist painted the figure iconically, using gold backgrounds and stylized features.
In the museum, several portraits are displayed iconically to evoke a sense of reverence.
The saint was depicted iconically, with a solemn expression and a radiant halo.

Related Terms

  • antonym: realistically - in a way that represents things as they are in real life
  • synonym: symbolically - in a way that uses symbols to represent ideas
