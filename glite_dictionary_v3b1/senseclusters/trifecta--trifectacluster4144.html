trifecta

noun
set of three successes
7.0
Pronunciation: / traɪˈfɛktə / (AmE), / traɪˈfɛktə / (BrE)
a set of three achievements, successes, or related things, often considered together as a group

Examples

Achieve a trifecta
Completing the marathon, raising money for charity, and inspiring others was his personal trifecta this year.
The restaurant won a trifecta of awards for food, service, and atmosphere.
Landing her dream job, buying a house, and getting married made for a memorable trifecta in her life.

  • synonym: hat trick - three successes or achievements, especially in sports
