bullocky

adjective
strong and powerful like a bullock
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊl.ə.ki / (AmE), / ˈbʊl.ə.ki / (BrE)

Examples

Her bullocky strength surprised everyone during the lifting contest.
The wrestler's bullocky build made him a formidable opponent.
