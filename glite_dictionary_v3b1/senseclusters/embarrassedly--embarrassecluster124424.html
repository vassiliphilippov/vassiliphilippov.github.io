embarrassedly

adverb
showing embarrassment
4.6
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbærəstli / (AmE), / ɪmˈbærəstli / (BrE)
in a way that shows embarrassment or self-consciousness

Examples

To embarrassedly admit
He embarrassedly admitted that he had forgotten her birthday.
She looked away embarrassedly when her name was called in front of the class.
They embarrassedly tried to explain the misunderstanding to their guests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awkwardly - in a way that shows discomfort or lack of confidence
  • antonym: confidently - in a way that shows confidence or self-assurance
