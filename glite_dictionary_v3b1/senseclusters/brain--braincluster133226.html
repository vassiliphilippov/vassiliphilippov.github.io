brain

noun
intellectual ability; intelligence
Pronunciation: / breɪn / (AmE), / breɪn / (BrE)
intelligence, mental ability, or the mind

Examples

A sharp brain
If you want to succeed in this industry, you need both brains and determination.
She has the brains to solve even the most complex problems.
They recognized his brain and creativity during the interview.
After the long exam, my brain felt exhausted.
Meditation helps him clear his brain of stressful thoughts.
She has a sharp brain and quickly solved the puzzle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mind - the element of a person that enables them to be aware, to think, and to feel
  • antonym: stupidity - lack of intelligence or common sense
  • synonym: wit - keen intelligence; mental sharpness
