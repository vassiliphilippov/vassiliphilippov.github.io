cincher

noun
tightening belt or device
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪntʃər / (AmE), / ˈsɪntʃə / (BrE)
a belt or device used to make clothing or another object tight or secure

Examples

A waist cincher
She wore a cincher to give her dress a better fit.
The cincher helped keep the heavy load secure during transport.
Many costumes use a cincher to create a more defined waistline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: belt - a band worn around the waist to hold up clothing
  • synonym: corset - a tightly fitting undergarment worn to shape the waist
