autoinhibited

adjective
restrained by self-control
9.4
Pronunciation: /ɑː.toʊ.ɪn.ˈhɪ.bɪ.tɪd/ /; / /ɔː.toʊ.ɪn.ˈhɪ.bɪ.tɪd/ (AmE), /ɔː.təʊ.ɪn.ˈhɪ.bɪ.tɪd/ (BrE)
unable to express feelings, impulses, or behavior due to internal self-control or self-consciousness.

Examples

Autoinhibited person
She became autoinhibited after years of being told not to show her emotions.
His autoinhibited nature made it difficult for him to participate in group discussions.
People who are autoinhibited may struggle to express their true feelings in public.

Related Terms

  • synonym: self-restrained - holding back one's own actions or feelings
  • antonym: uninhibited - free to express feelings or behavior without restraint
