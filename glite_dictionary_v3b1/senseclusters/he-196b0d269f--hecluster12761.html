He

noun
chemical symbol for helium
Pronunciation: / hiː / (AmE), / hiː / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for helium, a very light, colorless, odorless inert gas element occurring in natural gases and used in balloons, scientific equipment, and as a coolant

Examples

Liquid He coolant
Liquid He is used in cooling superconducting magnets.
The balloon was filled with He to make it float.
They analyzed the composition of the gas and detected the presence of He.
