docker

noun
port laborer loading/unloading ships
2.7 3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈdɑː.kər/ (AmE), /ˈdɒ.kə/ (BrE)
a person whose job is to load and unload ships at a port or dock

Examples

As a young man, he found a job as a docker at the busy port.
