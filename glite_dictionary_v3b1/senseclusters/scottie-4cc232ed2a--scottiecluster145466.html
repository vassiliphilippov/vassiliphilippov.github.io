Scottie

noun
a small Scottish terrier breed
Pronunciation: / ˈskɑti / (AmE), / ˈskɒti / (BrE)
A breed of small, long-haired terrier, usually black, with erect ears and tail, originally from Scotland.

Examples

A Scottie dog
Her Scottie barked happily as she entered the house.
President Roosevelt was famously photographed with his Scottie, Fala.
The family adopted a Scottie from the local animal shelter.
