unrushed

adjective
not hurried
3.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrʌʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈrʌʃt / (BrE)
not hurried or done quickly; calm and relaxed in pace or manner

Examples

An unrushed walk
They took an unrushed walk through the park, enjoying the fresh air.
His unrushed approach to problem-solving often led to better results.
The chef prepared each dish in an unrushed manner, focusing on quality.
