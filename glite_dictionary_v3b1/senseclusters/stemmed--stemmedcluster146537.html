stemmed

adjective
reduced to word stems (NLP)
Pronunciation: / stɛmd / (AmE), / stɛmd / (BrE)
(in computing, especially natural language processing) having had words reduced to their stems (base forms) by an automated process called stemming, so different inflected forms are treated alike.

Examples

Stemmed words
The search engine stores stemmed terms so queries match plural and tense variations.
In preprocessing, she converted all documents to lower case and stemmed words to reduce vocabulary size.
Researchers found that the classifier performed better on stemmed inputs when training data was limited.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lemmatized - processed so words are reduced to dictionary forms (lemmas); similar to stemming but often linguistically more accurate
  • synonym: stem - in linguistics, the base part of a word from which inflected or derived forms are made; the root element used by stemming algorithms
