Viola canadensis

noun
a North American perennial plant (Canada violet)
Pronunciation: / vaɪˈoʊlə kəˌnæˈdɛnsɪs / (AmE), / vɪˈəʊlə kəˌnæˈdɛnsɪs / (BrE)
A tall North American perennial plant with heart-shaped leaves and white flowers marked with purple streaks, also known as Canada violet.
