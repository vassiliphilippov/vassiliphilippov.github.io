stoplight

noun
traffic control signal at intersections
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑpˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈstɒpˌlaɪt / (BrE)
a set of lights, typically red, yellow, and green, used to control the flow of traffic at road intersections

Examples

Busy stoplight intersection
A malfunctioning stoplight caused a major traffic jam this morning.
He waited for the stoplight to change before crossing the street.
The stoplight at the corner turned red just as we arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: signal - device to indicate or direct action
  • synonym: traffic light - set of colored lights controlling vehicle traffic
