Life Saver

noun
ring-shaped hard candy
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪf ˌseɪvər / (AmE), / ˈlaɪf ˌseɪvə / (BrE)
A brand of ring-shaped hard candy with a hole in the center, resembling a life preserver.

Examples

A Life Saver candy
I haven't had a Life Saver since I was in school.
