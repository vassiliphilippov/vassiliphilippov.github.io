kick-out

noun
goalkeeper restart kick
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪk aʊt / (AmE), / ˈkɪk aʊt / (BrE)
a restart kick that returns the ball to play, especially one taken by a goalkeeper in sports like soccer or football

Examples

Goalkeeper kick-out
After making a save, the goalkeeper performed a quick kick-out to start a counterattack.
The referee signaled for a kick-out after the ball went out of play.
A powerful kick-out can help a team regain possession and move up the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goal kick - a method of restarting play in soccer when the ball goes out over the goal line
  • antonym: throw-in - a way to restart play in soccer by throwing the ball from the sideline
