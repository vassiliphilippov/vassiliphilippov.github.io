James Prescott Joule

proper noun
English physicist; pioneer of thermodynamics
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒeɪmz ˈprɛskət dʒuːl / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪmz ˈprɛskət dʒuːl / (BrE)
