incomparable

adjective
so good as to have no equal; not able to be compared
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɑːmpərəbl̩ / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɒmpərəbəl / (BrE)
so good or unusual as to have no equal, or not able to be compared because of fundamental differences.

Examples

An incomparable performance
Her performance on stage was truly incomparable and left the audience in awe.
The beauty of the mountain at sunrise is simply incomparable to anything else.
The treasures in the museum are of incomparable historical value.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comparable - able to be compared; similar in some respects
  • synonym: matchless - having no equal; unsurpassed
  • synonym: peerless - without equal; unrivaled
