wanton

adjective
sexually unrestrained, promiscuous
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑn.tən / (AmE), / ˈwɒn.tən / (BrE)
(of a person or their behavior) sexually unrestrained or having many casual sexual relationships.

Examples

Wanton lifestyle
He accused her of wanton behavior, but others defended her personal choices.
Rumors about her wanton lifestyle quickly spread through the small town.
The play was criticized for its wanton depiction of sexuality and indulgence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: promiscuous - having or involving many sexual partners
