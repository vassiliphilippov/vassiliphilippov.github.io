cane

verb
to beat with a cane
4.7
Pronunciation: / keɪn / (AmE), / keɪn / (BrE)
to beat or strike someone with a cane, typically as a punishment

Examples

To cane a student
He was caned for arriving late to class several times.
In some schools, teachers used to cane children as punishment.
The principal threatened to cane the students for misbehavior.
