ammoniate

verb
treat with ammonia
Pronunciation: / əˈmoʊniˌeɪt / (AmE), / əˈmɒniˌeɪt / (BrE)
to treat or combine with ammonia or an ammonium compound

Examples

To ammoniate animal feed
Farmers often ammoniate feed to improve its nutritional value for livestock.
The company ammoniates certain chemical products as part of their production process.
The laboratory technician decided to ammoniate the sample to increase its solubility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ammonia - a colorless gas with a pungent smell, used in cleaning, fertilizing etc.
