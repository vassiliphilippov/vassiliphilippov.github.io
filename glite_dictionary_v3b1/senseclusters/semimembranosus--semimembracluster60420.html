semimembranosus

noun
thigh hamstring muscle
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˌmɛmbrəˈnoʊsəs / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˌmɛmbrəˈnəʊsəs / (BrE)
a large muscle at the back of the thigh that is part of the hamstring group

Examples

The semimembranosus muscle
The semimembranosus helps bend the knee and extend the hip during walking.
Injuries to the semimembranosus are common among athletes who play soccer or run track.
Physical therapy can help strengthen the semimembranosus after a hamstring strain.
