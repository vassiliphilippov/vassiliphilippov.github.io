acumen

noun
keen insight; shrewdness
Pronunciation: / əˈkjumən, ˈækjəmən / (AmE), / ˈakjʊmən, əˈkjuːmən / (BrE)
The ability to make good judgments and quick decisions, especially in a particular domain.

Examples

Business acumen
He demonstrated remarkable acumen when solving complex legal cases.
Her business acumen quickly made her a leader in the industry.
The manager's political acumen helped the company navigate tricky negotiations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insight - the capacity to gain an accurate and deep understanding of something
  • antonym: stupidity - lack of intelligence or common sense
