nevoid elephantiasis

noun
congenital thickening of skin from lymph vessel issues
9.9
Pronunciation: /nɪ.ˈvɔɪd e.lɪ.fən.ˈtaɪ.ə.sɪs/ (AmE), /nɪ.ˈvɔɪd e.lɪ.fən.ˈtaɪ.ə.sɪs/ (BrE)
A congenital condition characterized by unilateral thickening of the skin on an extremity due to enlargement and obstruction of lymph vessels.
