screenless

adjective
without a display
Pronunciation: / ˈskriːnləs / (AmE), / ˈskriːnləs / (BrE)
not having or using a display screen

Examples

Screenless device
The new smart speaker is a screenless device controlled by voice.
Many people prefer screenless gadgets to reduce distractions in their homes.
A screenless design can make technology more accessible for visually impaired users.

Related Terms

  • synonym: displayless - not having a display
  • antonym: touchscreen - a display that also serves as an input device
