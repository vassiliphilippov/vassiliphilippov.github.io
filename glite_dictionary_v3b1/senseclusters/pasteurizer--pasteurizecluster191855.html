pasteurizer

noun
machine for pasteurizing liquids
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpæs.tʃɚˌaɪ.zɚ /; / ˈpæstʃəˌraɪzər / (AmE), / ˈpæs.tjʊəˌraɪ.zə /; / ˈpɑːstjʊəˌraɪzə / (BrE)
A machine or apparatus used for pasteurizing liquids, especially milk, by heating them to a specific temperature to destroy harmful microorganisms.
