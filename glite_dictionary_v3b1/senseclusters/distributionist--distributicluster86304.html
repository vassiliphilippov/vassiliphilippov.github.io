distributionist

adjective
supporting broad distribution
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstrɪbjuːʃənɪst / (AmE), / dɪˈstrɪbjʊʃənɪst / (BrE)
relating to or supporting the economic theory that property and resources should be widely distributed among the population.

Examples

Distributionist ideas
The party's distributionist policies aimed to reduce economic inequality in the country.
Many activists supported distributionist reforms to ensure fair access to land and resources.
Her distributionist views were influenced by the writings of early 20th-century economists.

Related Terms

  • antonym: capitalist - supporting private ownership and control of property and resources
  • synonym: egalitarian - believing in equal rights and opportunities for all people
