batting average

noun
rate of success in repeated attempts
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbæɾɪŋ ˌævɚɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbæt.ɪŋ ˌæv.ər.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
The percentage or proportion of times that an effort, attempt, or activity is successful, by analogy to the baseball statistic.

Examples

Company batting average
Our company's batting average for winning proposals has doubled this quarter.
The salesperson's batting average improved after they changed their approach.
When applying for grants, your batting average is just as important as the total number of applications.

Related Terms

  • synonym: success rate - the proportion of attempts that are successful
