air of expectation

noun
a mood of eager anticipation
Pronunciation: / ɛr ʌv ˌɛkspɛkˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / eə ɒv ˌɛkspɛkˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a feeling or atmosphere in a place that suggests that people are eagerly waiting for something to happen.

Examples

An air of expectation
She entered the hall, which was filled with an air of expectation.
The room had an air of expectation as everyone waited for the announcement.
There was an air of expectation in the theater before the curtain rose.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anticipation - a feeling of excitement about something that is going to happen
