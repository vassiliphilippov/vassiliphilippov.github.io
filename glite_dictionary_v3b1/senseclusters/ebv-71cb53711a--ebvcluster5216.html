EBV

noun
herpesvirus causing mononucleosis, linked to cancers
8.4
Pronunciation: /iː biː viː/ (AmE), /iː biː viː/ (BrE)
the herpesvirus that causes infectious mononucleosis and is associated with certain cancers, especially in Africa and China

Examples

EBV virus infection
Doctors believe that EBV is the primary cause of infectious mononucleosis in teenagers.
Research suggests a link between EBV infection and certain types of cancer in Africa.
Testing for EBV antibodies can help diagnose long-term or unusual illnesses.
