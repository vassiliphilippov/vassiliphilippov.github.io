tooth fairy

noun
mythical figure who collects children's lost teeth
4.0 3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtuθ ˌfɛri /; / tuθ ˈfɛri / (AmE), / ˈtuːθ ˌfeəri /; / tuːθ ˈfeəri / (BrE)
a mythical character in children's stories who is said to visit when a child loses a baby tooth, leaving money or a small gift under the pillow

Examples

The tooth fairy story

The story of the tooth fairy is told in many families across the world.
