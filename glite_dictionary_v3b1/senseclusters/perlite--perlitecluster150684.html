perlite

noun
lightweight volcanic glass
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɜːlaɪt / (BrE)
a lightweight, porous volcanic material used in gardening to improve soil and in construction as insulation or filler

Examples
