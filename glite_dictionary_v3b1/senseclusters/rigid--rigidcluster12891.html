rigid

adjective
not able to be bent; stiff
2.3 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈrɪ.dʒɪd/ /; / /ˈrɪ.dʒəd/ (AmE), /ˈrɪ.dʒɪd/ /; / /ˈrɪ.dʒəd/ (BrE)
not able to be bent or changed in shape; stiff and inflexible

Examples
