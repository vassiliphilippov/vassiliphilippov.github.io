strings attached

x
with conditions or obligations added
Pronunciation: / strɪŋz əˈtætʃt / (AmE), / strɪŋz əˈtætʃt / (BrE)
(of an offer, arrangement, or agreement) accompanied by special conditions or obligations.

Examples

No strings attached
He agreed to help, but only if there were absolutely no strings attached.
Sometimes, generous-sounding donations have hidden strings attached.
The scholarship offer came with several strings attached that limited how the money could be used.
