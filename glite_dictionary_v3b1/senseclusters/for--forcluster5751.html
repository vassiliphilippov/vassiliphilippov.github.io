for

preposition
in support of
Pronunciation: / fɔr / (AmE), / fɔː / (BrE)
used to show support, approval, or agreement with someone or something

Examples

Vote for a proposal
She voted for the proposal because she believed it would help the community.
Are you for or against the new school rules?
The committee members spoke for the changes during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: against - opposing or not in favor of something
