jennet

noun
small Spanish horse
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛnɪt / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnɪt / (BrE)
a small, light Spanish horse, especially one used for riding in the past

Examples

A Spanish jennet horse
In medieval Spain, nobles often rode a jennet for its speed and agility.
The museum displayed a painting of a knight mounted on a jennet.
Farmers prized the jennet for its gentle nature and endurance on long journeys.
