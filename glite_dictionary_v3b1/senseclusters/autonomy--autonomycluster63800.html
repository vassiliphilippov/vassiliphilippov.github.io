autonomy

noun
independence of machines or AI
6.3
Pronunciation: / əˈtɑnəmi / (AmE), / ɔːˈtɒnəmi / (BrE)
(in computing, robotics, and AI) the ability of a machine, robot, or software system to perform tasks and make decisions without direct human control or continuous human guidance.

Examples

Robot autonomy
Self-driving cars demonstrate increased autonomy in complex urban traffic during rush hour.
Engineers test the drone's autonomy by giving it unforeseen tasks without human guidance.
Concerns about safety rise as household robots gain more autonomy over daily chores.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automation - the use of machines or software to perform tasks with minimal human involvement
  • synonym: independence - freedom from external control; related concept when applied to agents or systems
