consuming

adjective
intensely absorbing; overwhelming
Pronunciation: / kənˈsuːmɪŋ / (AmE), / kənˈsjuːmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a feeling or interest) so intense that it takes up all of one’s attention or energy.

Examples

A consuming curiosity
His consuming curiosity led him to travel the world in search of answers.
She had a consuming desire to succeed in her chosen field.
The novel explores the consuming nature of grief after loss.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overwhelming - very great in amount or intensity
