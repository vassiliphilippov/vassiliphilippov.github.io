audio drama

noun
dramatized story performance using only sound
4.2 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…îdiÀåo ä Ààdr…ëm…ô / (AmE), / Àà…îÀêdi…ô ä Ààdr…ëÀêm…ô / (BrE)
A dramatized performance presented solely through sound, broadcast on radio or distributed via audio media such as tapes or CDs.

Examples

A audio drama performance
She enjoys listening to a science fiction audio drama on her commute every morning.
The BBC has produced many well-known audio dramas that captivated listeners across generations.
The podcast blends elements of mystery and suspense in a serialized audio drama format.

Synonyms

    3.5 0.09/m

  • radio theater
    4.4 0.01/m
    radio theater is presented with actors, music, and sound effects on radio or audio releases, while audio drama names any dramatized performance heard only as sound.
