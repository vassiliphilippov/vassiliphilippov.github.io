messiah

noun
expected saviour or deliverer
5.5
Pronunciation: / məˈsaɪ.ə / (AmE), / mɪˈsaɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person who is expected to bring deliverance or salvation; an expected saviour or liberator

Examples

The messiah comes
In difficult times, people often look for a messiah to lead them out of crisis.
Many saw the young politician as a messiah promising change and hope for the country.
The team hoped their new coach would be the messiah to turn around their losing streak.

Related Terms

  • synonym: liberator - a person who liberates a person or place from imprisonment or oppression
  • synonym: saviour - a person who saves someone or something
