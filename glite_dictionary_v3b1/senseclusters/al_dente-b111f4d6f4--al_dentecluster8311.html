al dente

adjective
firmly cooked pasta or rice
6.8 5.3
Pronunciation: /æl den.ˈteɪ/ /; / /ɑːl den.ˈteɪ/ (AmE), /æl den.ˈteɪ/ /; / /ɑːl den.ˈteɪ/ (BrE)
(of pasta or rice) cooked so that it is still firm to the bite, not too soft.

Examples

Al dente pasta
For the best flavor and texture, cook the spaghetti al dente as instructed on the package.
She prefers her pasta al dente, with a slight bite to it rather than mushy.
The risotto should be creamy but the rice grains must remain al dente.

Related Terms

  • antonym: overcooked - cooked too long so as to become too soft
