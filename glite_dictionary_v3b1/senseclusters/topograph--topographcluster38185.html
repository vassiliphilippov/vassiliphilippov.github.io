topograph

noun
detailed map of features
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑpəˌɡræf / (AmE), / ˈtɒpəˌɡrɑːf / (BrE)
a detailed map or representation that shows the physical features of a specific place or area

Examples

A topograph map
The geologist used a topograph to plan the expedition through the mountain range.
Students examined the topograph to identify rivers and valleys in the region.
A detailed topograph can reveal subtle changes in elevation across the landscape.
