unformulated

adjective
not in final mixture
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈfɔːrmjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈfɔːmjʊleɪtɪd / (BrE)
not prepared as a finished product or mixture, containing only the active ingredient without added components.

Examples

Unformulated chemicals
The laboratory ordered unformulated chemicals to create custom solutions for their experiments.
Some pesticides are sold in an unformulated state and must be mixed before use.
Buying unformulated products allows manufacturers to control the final composition of their goods.

Related Terms

  • antonym: formulated - prepared as a finished product or mixture
  • synonym: raw - in a natural or unprocessed state
