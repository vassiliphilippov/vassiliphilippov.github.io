waterproofing

noun
the process of making something waterproof
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚˌpruːfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌpruːfɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of making something impervious to water by applying a special treatment or material

Examples

Waterproofing a jacket
Modern jackets often use advanced waterproofing technologies to keep you dry.
Proper waterproofing is essential for roofs in rainy climates.
The contractor applied waterproofing to the basement walls to prevent flooding.

Related Terms

  • antonym: leakage - the act or process of liquid escaping
  • synonym: sealant - a material used to block the passage of fluids
