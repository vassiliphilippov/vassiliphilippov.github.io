ill-humored

adjective
unfriendly or bad-tempered
7.6 5.6
Pronunciation: /ɪl.ˈhjuː.mərd/ /; / /ɪl ˈhjuː.mərd/ (AmE), /ɪl.ˈhjuː.məd/ /; / /ɪl ˈhjuː.məd/ (BrE)
often showing irritation or surliness; unfriendly or bad-tempered in manner.
