angleworm

noun
earthworm used as fishing bait
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡəlˌwɜrm / (AmE), / ˈaŋɡəlˌwɜːm / (BrE)
A common earthworm, especially one used as bait by anglers for fishing.

Examples

Common angleworms
After the rain, many angleworms appeared on the sidewalk.
Fishermen prefer using angleworms because they are lively and attractive to fish.
The soil in the garden is healthier because angleworms help to aerate it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: earthworm - common burrowing worm that aerates soil
