quale

noun
subjective experience
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑːleɪ / (AmE), / ˈkweɪli / (BrE)
a quality or property as it is experienced by a conscious mind, especially in discussions of perception or consciousness

Examples

A quale of redness
The redness of a rose is a classic example of a quale in philosophy.
Some argue that pain has a unique quale that cannot be explained by science alone.
Each person's experience of the color blue is a distinct quale in their mind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: phenomenon - an observable event or experience
  • synonym: sensation - a physical feeling or perception resulting from something that happens to the body or mind
