security

noun
financial safety or independence
1.0
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkjʊrɪti / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊərəti / (BrE)
freedom from worry or danger, especially relating to having enough money to cover one's needs now and in the future

Examples

Financial security
A steady income is essential for financial security.
Her retirement savings gave her a sense of security for the future.
They valued the security of owning their own home.

Related Terms

  • antonym: insecurity - lack of assurance or safety, especially financial
  • synonym: safety - the state of being safe from danger or risk
