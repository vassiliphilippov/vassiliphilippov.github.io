obsessivity

noun
extreme compulsiveness or preoccupation
6.3 8.0
Pronunciation: /əb.ses.ˈɪv.ɪ.tɪ/ (AmE), /əb.ses.ˈɪv.ɪ.tɪ/ (BrE)
the quality or state of being extremely compulsive or preoccupied with a particular idea, activity, or subject

Examples

Extreme obsessivity about time
He was aware of his own obsessivity regarding punctuality, but found it hard to change.
Her obsessivity with cleanliness led her to clean the house several times a day.
The obsessivity observed in patients with certain disorders can be difficult to manage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compulsivity - the quality of being driven to behave in a repetitive and uncontrollable way
  • synonym: obsession - an idea or thought that continually preoccupies a person's mind
