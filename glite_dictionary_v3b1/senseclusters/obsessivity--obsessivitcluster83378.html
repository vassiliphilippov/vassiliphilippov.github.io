Examples

Extreme obsessivity about time

Her obsessivity with cleanliness led her to clean the house several times a day.

Synonyms

  • obsession
    5.1 14/m
    obsession is a strong, often uncontrollable focus on a thought or person, while obsessivity describes that lasting compulsive quality.
  • fixation
    7.5 1.49/m
    fixation is an unhealthy, narrow attachment that harms normal life, while obsessivity names the broader state of compulsive preoccupation.
  • preoccupation
    5.6 5.61/m
    preoccupation is being absorbed in a thought or worry without strong compulsion, while obsessivity means extreme, persistent compulsive focus.
