unimpeachable

adjective
not able to be doubted or criticized
5.4 6.5
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ɪm.ˈpiː.tʃə.bəl/ (AmE), /ʌn.ɪm.ˈpiː.tʃə.bəl/ (BrE)
so good, honest, or reliable that it cannot be doubted or criticized
