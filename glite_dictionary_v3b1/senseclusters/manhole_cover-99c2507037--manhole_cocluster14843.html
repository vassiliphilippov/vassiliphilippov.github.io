manhole cover

noun
metal lid for a manhole
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmænˌhoʊl ˌkʌvər / (AmE), / ˈmænˌhəʊl ˌkʌvə / (BrE)
a heavy, usually circular, flat metal lid that covers the opening of a manhole in a street or pavement

Examples

Heavy manhole cover
Replacing a broken manhole cover is essential for public safety.
She tripped when the manhole cover was left slightly ajar in the pavement.
The workers removed the manhole cover to access the sewer below.
