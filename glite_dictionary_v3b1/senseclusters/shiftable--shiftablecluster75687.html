shiftable

adjective
able to be reassigned
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪftəbəl / (AmE), / ˈʃɪftəbəl / (BrE)
able to be transferred or reassigned from one person, group, or account to another.

Examples

Shiftable costs
The extra costs are shiftable to the customer if necessary.
In some companies, tasks are shiftable among team members to balance workloads.
Responsibility for the error was not shiftable and remained with the manager.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fixed - not able to be changed or moved
  • synonym: transferable - able to be moved or passed from one person or place to another
