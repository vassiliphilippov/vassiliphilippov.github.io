EntleBucher

noun
Swiss working dog breed
Pronunciation: / ˈɛntləbjuːkər / (AmE), / ˈɛntləbjuːkə / (BrE)
A breed of small, sturdy working dog from Switzerland, recognized as the smallest of the four Sennenhund breeds.
