papillon

noun
breed of small toy spaniel dog
Pronunciation: / ˌpæpɪˈjɒn / (AmE), / ˌpæpɪˈjɒn / (BrE)
A small, slender breed of toy spaniel with large, erect ears resembling butterfly wings and a long, silky coat, often white with brown or black markings.

Examples

Papillon dog breed
A papillon is known for its graceful movement and alert expression.
Their new papillon won first prize at the dog show.
We adopted a papillon because we love their playful and friendly nature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spaniel - a breed of dog with a long silky coat and drooping ears
