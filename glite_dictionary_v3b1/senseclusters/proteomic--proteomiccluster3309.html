proteomic

adjective
study of proteins
8.1
Pronunciation: / proʊtiˈoʊmɪk / (AmE), / prəʊtiˈəʊmɪk / (BrE)
relating to the large-scale study or analysis of proteins in living organisms

Examples

Proteomic analysis
The team used proteomic techniques to discover new biomarkers for cancer.
Advances in proteomic research have improved our understanding of cellular processes.
A proteomic approach can reveal how proteins interact in complex diseases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: genomic - relating to the study of genomes
  • synonym: proteomics - the large-scale study of proteins
