sideway

adverb
obliquely; from the side
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪd.weɪ / (BrE)
in a sideways direction; from or towards the side

Examples

Look sideway quickly
She glanced sideway at him, unsure of what he was thinking.
The artist painted the subject sideway to create a dramatic effect.
The sunlight came in sideway through the open window, casting long shadows on the floor.
He glanced sideway at me before answering the question.
The crab moves sideway across the sand to avoid predators.
Without warning, the car skidded sideway on the icy road.
