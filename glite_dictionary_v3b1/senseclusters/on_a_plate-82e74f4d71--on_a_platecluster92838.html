on a plate

adverb
offered very easily, without effort
1.0 4.2
Pronunciation: /ɑːn ə pleɪt/ /; / /ən ə pleɪt/ (AmE), /ɒn ə pleɪt/ /; / /ən ə pleɪt/ (BrE)
