sidebar

noun
secondary panel on a website or app
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈsaɪd.bɑːr/ (AmE), /ˈsaɪd.bɑː/ (BrE)
A vertical or horizontal panel at the side of a website or app screen that contains secondary content such as navigation, widgets, links, or metadata.

Examples

Website sidebar
The blog's sidebar features recent posts and a subscribe box.
She customized the site's sidebar to show tags, archives, and a search field.
On mobile, the sidebar collapses into a menu to save screen space for content.

Related Terms

  • synonym: column - a vertical section of a page used to arrange content
  • synonym: panel - a distinct area of a user interface that groups controls or information
