conchoid

noun
a mathematical curve defined by a locus

Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːn.tʃɔɪd/ /; / /ˈkɑːn.kɔɪd/ (AmE), /ˈkɒn.kɔɪd/ /; / /ˈkɒn.tʃɔɪd/ (BrE)
