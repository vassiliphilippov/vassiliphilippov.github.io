herb mercury

noun
Eurasian weed, once medicinal
7.6
Pronunciation: / hɜrb ˈmɜrkəri /; / hɜrb ˈmɜrkjəri / (AmE), / hɜːb ˈmɜːkjʊri /; / hɜːb ˈmɜːkjʊəri / (BrE)
a small annual plant (Mercurialis annua), often considered a weed, formerly used in herbal medicine as a purgative, diuretic, or treatment for syphilis
