skittle

noun
old British bowling-type game
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈskɪtl̩ / (BrE)
a game, mainly played in Britain, in which players try to knock down pins by rolling or throwing a ball at them, similar to bowling

Examples

A game of skittle
On rainy days, the children would often play skittle in the hall.
The pub had a special alley for skittle competitions every weekend.
We decided to organize a skittle tournament for the village fair.

Related Terms
