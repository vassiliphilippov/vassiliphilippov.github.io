Russula

noun
a genus of fungi with brittle, colorful caps
7.7 8.5
Pronunciation: /ˈrʌs.jə.lə/ /; / /ˈrʌs.juː.lə/ (AmE), /ˈrʌs.jʊ.lə/ /; / /ˈrʌs.juː.lə/ (BrE)
a large genus of mushrooms with stout stems, brittle caps of various colors, and white spores, lacking a ring (annulus) and volva
