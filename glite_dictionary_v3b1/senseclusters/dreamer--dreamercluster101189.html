dreamer

noun
person dreaming while asleep
Pronunciation: / ˈdriːmər / (AmE), / ˈdriːmə / (BrE)
a person who is experiencing a dream while asleep

Examples

Sleeping dreamer
Scientists study the brain activity of a dreamer during rapid eye movement sleep.
She watched her brother, the peaceful dreamer, sleeping soundly in his bed.
The dreamer awoke suddenly, startled by a vivid nightmare.
