makeshift doorstop

noun
temporary improvised door holder
7.8 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmeɪks.ʃɪft ˈdɔːr.stɑːp/ (AmE), /ˈmeɪks.ʃɪft ˈdɔː.stɒp/ (BrE)
