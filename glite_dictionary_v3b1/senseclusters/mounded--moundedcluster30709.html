mounded

adjective
shaped in a pile
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊndɪd / (AmE), / ˈmaʊndɪd / (BrE)
formed into a rounded pile or small hill, or having a raised, rounded shape

Examples

Mounded potatoes
The mashed potatoes were mounded high on each plate at the dinner table.
A mounded layer of sand protected the small plants from the wind.
She admired the mounded snow on the branches after the storm.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flat - having a level or even surface
  • synonym: heaped - piled up in a large, often messy way
