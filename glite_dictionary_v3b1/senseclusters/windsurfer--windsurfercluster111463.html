windsurfer

noun
board with sail
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndˌsɝːfər / (AmE), / ˈwɪndˌsɜːfə / (BrE)
a board with an attached sail that is used for riding on water, powered by the wind

Examples

A windsurfer board
He carried his windsurfer down to the beach to catch the morning breeze.
The windsurfer glided smoothly across the lake, powered by a strong gust of wind.
After renting a windsurfer, she spent the afternoon learning how to balance and steer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sailboard - a board with a sail used for windsurfing
  • degree lower: surfboard - a long, narrow board used for riding waves
