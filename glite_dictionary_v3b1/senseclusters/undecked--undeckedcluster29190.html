undecked

adjective
without a deck
7.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdɛkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈdɛkt / (BrE)
not having a deck or platform, especially referring to a boat or structure that is open on top

Examples

Undecked boat
The fishermen used an undecked boat to reach the shallow waters near the shore.
Because the structure was undecked, rainwater easily collected inside.
Sitting in the undecked canoe, they enjoyed an unobstructed view of the sky.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decked - having a deck or platform
  • synonym: open - not covered or enclosed
