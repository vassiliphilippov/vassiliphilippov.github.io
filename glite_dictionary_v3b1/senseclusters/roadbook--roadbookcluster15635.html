roadbook

noun
guidebook with maps and road information
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊd.bʊk / (AmE), / ˈrəʊd.bʊk / (BrE)
a guidebook giving information about the roads of a country or region, often including maps and route details

Examples

A roadbook with maps
Before setting out, he made notes in his roadbook about possible rest stops.
She consulted the roadbook to plan the next stage of their journey.
The roadbook included detailed maps and recommendations for scenic routes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atlas - a book of maps or charts, often including road maps
