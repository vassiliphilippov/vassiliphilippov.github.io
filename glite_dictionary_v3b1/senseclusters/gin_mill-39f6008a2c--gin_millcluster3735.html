gin mill

noun
informal bar or tavern
Pronunciation: / dʒɪn mɪl / (AmE), / dʒɪn mɪl / (BrE)
an informal or slang term for a bar, tavern, or pub, especially one that is cheap or disreputable

Examples

Many of these gin mills attracted a lively, if sometimes rowdy, crowd in the evenings.
The novel's main character spends his nights wandering from one gin mill to another.
After work, they all headed to the gin mill for a few drinks and lively conversation.
He preferred the cozy atmosphere of a small gin mill to the fancy cocktail bars downtown.
The old gin mill on the corner has been in business for decades, attracting regulars every night.
