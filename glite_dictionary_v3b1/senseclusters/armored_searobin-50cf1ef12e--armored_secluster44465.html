armored searobin

noun
a sea robin fish with bony scutes and barbels
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈɑːr.mərd siː ˈrɑː.bɪn/ (AmE), /ˈɑː.məd siː ˈrɒb.ɪn/ (BrE)
