co-ordinated

adjective
intentionally matched in style or color
7.7
Pronunciation: / koʊˈɔrdɪneɪtɪd / (AmE), / kəʊˈɔːdɪneɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of colors, designs, objects, etc.) intentionally matched in style, color, or arrangement to create a pleasing combination.

Examples

Co-ordinated outfit
She wore a co-ordinated outfit with matching shoes and bag.
The designer chose co-ordinated cushions and curtains for a harmonious look.
The office decor features co-ordinated colors to create a welcoming atmosphere.
