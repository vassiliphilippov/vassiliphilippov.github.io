pneumonitis

noun
inflammation of the lungs
Pronunciation: / nuːˌmoʊˈnaɪtɪs / (AmE), / njuːˌməʊˈnaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the lungs, typically caused by infection or an allergic reaction

Examples

Pneumonitis lung inflammation
Doctors prescribed medication to reduce the lung inflammation associated with pneumonitis.
Radiation therapy can sometimes cause a condition known as pneumonitis.
The patient developed pneumonitis after being exposed to toxic fumes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pneumonia - infection causing inflammation of the air sacs in the lungs
