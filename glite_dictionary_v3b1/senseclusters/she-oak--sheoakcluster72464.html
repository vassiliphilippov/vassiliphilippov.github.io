she-oak

noun
Australian tree of the genus Casuarina
Pronunciation: / ˈʃiˌoʊk / (AmE), / ˈʃiːˌəʊk / (BrE)
any of several Australian trees of the genus Casuarina, typically with slender, jointed branchlets and scale-like leaves

Examples

Australian she-oak tree
Indigenous Australians have long used she-oak wood for crafting tools and instruments.
The dry landscape was dotted with resilient she-oak trees.
Wind whistled softly through the fine needle-like leaves of the she-oak.
