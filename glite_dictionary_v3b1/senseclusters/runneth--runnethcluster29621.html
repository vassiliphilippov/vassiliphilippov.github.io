runneth

verb
archaic manages
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnɪθ / (AmE), / ˈrʌnɪθ / (BrE)
archaic third person singular present form of 'run', meaning to manage, operate, or be in charge of something.

Examples

He runneth the house
He runneth the household with wisdom and fairness, as was expected in those days.
The steward runneth the estate with strict and careful rules.
In the old text, it is written that she runneth the market every morning.
