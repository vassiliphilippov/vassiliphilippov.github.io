special military operation

noun
significant or secret military action
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛʃəl ˌmɪləˌtɛri ˌɑpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈspɛʃəl ˌmɪlɪtəɹi ˌɒpəˈɹeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
a significant military action, often involving secrecy or specific objectives, sometimes used as an official term for military intervention

Examples

A special military operation
Reports about the special military operation were scarce and highly controlled.
The general refused to discuss the details of the special military operation with the media.
The government launched a special military operation to achieve its objectives in the region.
