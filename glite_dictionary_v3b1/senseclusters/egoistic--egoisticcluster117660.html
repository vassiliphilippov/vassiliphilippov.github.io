egoistic

adjective
thinking only of yourself; selfish
3.4 5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌiːɡoʊˈɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌiːɡəʊˈɪstɪk / (BrE)
thinking only of yourself and what is best for you, rather than considering other people; selfish or self-centered.

Examples

Egoistic attitude
Her egoistic attitude often made collaboration within the team difficult.
It was an egoistic decision that ignored the needs of everyone else involved.
People who are overly egoistic frequently find themselves isolated from friends and family.

Related Terms

  • antonym: altruistic - showing a selfless concern for the well-being of others; unselfish
  • synonym: selfish - lacking consideration for others; concerned chiefly with personal profit or pleasure
