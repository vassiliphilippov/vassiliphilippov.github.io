Lycium

noun
a genus of spiny shrubs (boxthorn, wolfberry)
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪsiəm / (AmE), / ˈlɪsiəm /; / ˈlɪsɪəm / (BrE)
a genus of mostly spiny, deciduous or evergreen shrubs found in temperate and subtropical regions, including species such as boxthorn, wolfberry, and goji berry
