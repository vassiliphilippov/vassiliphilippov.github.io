jerk

noun
weightlifting movement of raising weight overhead
Pronunciation: / dʒɝk / (AmE), / dʒɜːk / (BrE)
a movement in weightlifting in which a weight is raised from shoulder height to above the head by straightening the arms

Examples

A weightlifting jerk
He improved his jerk technique by training with a coach.
She completed a clean and jerk to secure the gold medal.
The jerk requires strong coordination between the legs and arms.
