cosmically

adverb
in a universal way
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑzmɪkli / (AmE), / ˈkɒzmɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the universe or is extremely vast or significant

Examples

Cosmically large universe
The discovery was cosmically important for our understanding of space.
They felt cosmically insignificant when looking up at the night sky.
The two friends were cosmically lucky to meet at just the right moment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: locally - in a way that relates to a specific place or area
  • synonym: universally - in a way that applies to the whole universe or to everyone
