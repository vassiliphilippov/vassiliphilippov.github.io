soldier-proof

adjective
extremely durable or resistant; able to withstand rough use
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊldʒɚ pruːf / (AmE), / ˈsəʊldʒə pruːf / (BrE)
(of an object or material) made to be extremely durable or resistant, especially able to withstand the rough treatment typical in military use.

Examples

Soldier-proof material
The new phone case is so durable it's practically soldier-proof.
The water-resistant watches are advertised as being completely soldier-proof.
We need soldier-proof gear for this expedition into the rainforest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: childproof - designed so that children cannot tamper with or access
  • synonym: foolproof - incapable of going wrong or being misused
