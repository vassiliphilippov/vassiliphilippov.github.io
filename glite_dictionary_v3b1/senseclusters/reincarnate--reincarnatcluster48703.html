reincarnate

verb
cause to appear in a new form
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈɪnkɑːrneɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈɪnkɑːneɪt / (BrE)
to cause something, especially an idea, product, or form, to appear or exist again in a new or different form

Examples

To reincarnate an idea
Classic songs are often reincarnated as fresh covers by new artists.
The company hopes to reincarnate its classic toy line for today's children.
The designer managed to reincarnate the brand with a modern look.
