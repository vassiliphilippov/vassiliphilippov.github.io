pugmark

noun
animal footprint
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌɡˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈpʌɡˌmɑːk / (BrE)
a footprint of a wild animal, especially a large cat, used for tracking in the wild

Examples

Fresh pugmark in mud
The forest guide pointed to a fresh pugmark in the mud, indicating a tiger had passed recently.
Researchers photographed the pugmarks to estimate the number of leopards in the area.
After the rain, it was easier to spot pugmarks along the jungle trail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: footprint - an impression left by a foot or paw on a surface
  • synonym: track - a mark left on the ground by a moving animal or person
