sealift

noun
transport by ship
6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈsiː.lɪft/ (AmE), /ˈsiː.lɪft/ (BrE)
the transportation of goods or people by ship, especially in large quantities or for military purposes

Examples

A sealift operation
The government organized a massive sealift to bring food and medicine to the disaster area.
During the war, a sealift was used to transport troops and equipment across the ocean.
A successful sealift ensured that the island community received supplies before winter arrived.
