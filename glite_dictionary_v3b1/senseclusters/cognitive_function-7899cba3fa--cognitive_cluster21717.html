Synonyms

  • cognitive process
    4.4 1.85/m
    cognitive process is a series of mental activities like thinking or learning that act over time, while cognitive function is the mind's ability to perform those activities.
  • mental process
    4.2 0.60/m
    mental process is any inner activity such as thinking, remembering, or perceiving in the mind, while cognitive function refers to the mind's working ability.
  • cognitive operation
    6.4 0.04/m
