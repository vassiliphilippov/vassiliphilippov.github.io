Spencerian

noun
follower of Spencer
Pronunciation: / spɛnˈsɛriən / (AmE), / spɛnˈsɪəriən / (BrE)
a person who supports or follows the philosophical or social views of Herbert Spencer

Examples

A Spencerian thinker
As a dedicated Spencerian, she believed in the power of individual freedom and minimal government.
The debate featured a Spencerian who argued for social progress through natural competition.
Many nineteenth-century thinkers identified as Spencerians and promoted his ideas in public policy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Darwinist - a person who supports the theories of Charles Darwin
  • antonym: socialist - a person who supports or advocates socialism
