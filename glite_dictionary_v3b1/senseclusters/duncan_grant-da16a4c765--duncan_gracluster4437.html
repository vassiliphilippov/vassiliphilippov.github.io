Duncan Grant

proper noun
Scottish painter and Bloomsbury Group member
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌŋkən ˈɡrænt / (AmE), / ˈdʌŋkən ˈɡrɑːnt / (BrE)
(1885–1978) Scottish painter, a member of the Bloomsbury Group, known for his influential modernist works and collaborations.

Examples

Duncan Grant painter
Duncan Grant was celebrated for his bold use of color and innovative style in the early 20th century.
As part of the Bloomsbury Group, Duncan Grant contributed to the British cultural and artistic renaissance.
Several portraits by Duncan Grant are featured in major art museums across the UK.
