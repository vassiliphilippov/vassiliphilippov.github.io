plantlike

adjective
motionless, rooted
Pronunciation: / ˈplæntˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈplɑːntˌlaɪk / (BrE)
very still or motionless, as if rooted in place like a plant

Examples

Plantlike stillness
She stood plantlike at the doorway, unable to speak as the crowd cheered.
The children remained plantlike during the game of statues, barely even blinking.
He waited plantlike in the lobby, hoping not to be noticed by anyone passing by.
