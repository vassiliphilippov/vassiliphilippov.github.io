horsiness

noun
horse-like quality
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔːrsinəs / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsinəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being like a horse, especially in appearance, smell, or manner

Examples

Horsiness of the barn
The barn had a distinct horsiness that lingered long after the animals had left.
She loved the horsiness of the countryside, with its fields and stables.
A faint horsiness clung to his boots after a day at the ranch.
