surf

noun
waves breaking on the shore
3.9
Pronunciation: / sɝf / (AmE), / sɜːf / (BrE)
the mass or line of foam formed by waves breaking on the shore of a sea or ocean

Examples

The surf crashes
Children played in the surf at the edge of the beach.
The sound of the surf helped her fall asleep.
We watched the surf pound the rocks all afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wave - a ridge of water moving across the surface of a sea or lake
