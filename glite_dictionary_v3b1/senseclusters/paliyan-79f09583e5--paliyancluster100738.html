Paliyan

proper noun
indigenous group in India
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːliˌjɑːn / (AmE), / ˈpɑːliˌjɑːn / (BrE)
a member of an indigenous people living in the southern regions of India, especially in Tamil Nadu and Kerala

Examples

A Paliyan person
The Paliyan have traditionally lived in the forests of Tamil Nadu.
Researchers have studied the unique customs of the Paliyan community.
Many Paliyan people continue to practice their traditional way of life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Adivasi - a member of the indigenous peoples of India
