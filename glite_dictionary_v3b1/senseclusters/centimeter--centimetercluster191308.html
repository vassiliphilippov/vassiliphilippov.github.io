centimeter

noun
metric unit of length (1/100 meter)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntɪˌmitər /; / ˈsɛn.tɪˌmiː.tər / (AmE), / ˈsɛntɪˌmiːtə /; / ˈsɛntɪmiːtə / (BrE)
a metric unit of length equal to one hundredth of a meter (0.01 meter), commonly used to measure small distances

Examples

One centimeter length
Her height increased by three centimeters over the summer.
Please use a ruler marked in centimeters when drawing your diagram.
The fabric measured exactly 50 centimeters wide.
Please measure the fabric in centimetres for accuracy.
She grew five centimetres taller in a year.
The width of the notebook is about 15 centimetres.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: meter - the base unit of length in the metric system
  • degree lower: millimeter - a metric unit of length equal to one thousandth of a meter
  • degree lower: millimetre - a metric unit of length equal to one thousandth of a meter
