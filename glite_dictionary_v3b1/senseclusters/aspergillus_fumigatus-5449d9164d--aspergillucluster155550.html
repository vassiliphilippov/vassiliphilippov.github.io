Aspergillus fumigatus

noun
species of mold causing aspergillosis
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌæspərˈdʒɪləs ˌfjuːmɪˈɡeɪtəs / (AmE), / ˌaspəˈdʒɪləs ˌfjuːmɪˈɡɑːtəs / (BrE)
a species of mold that can cause aspergillosis, especially in birds and humans
