dirgeful

adjective
sorrowful or mournful
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdɜrdʒfəl / (AmE), / ˈdɜːdʒfʊl / (BrE)
full of sorrow or mournfulness, especially in a way that resembles a funeral song

Examples

A dirgeful melody
The dirgeful melody echoed through the empty church, filling everyone with a sense of loss.
Her voice took on a dirgeful quality as she recited the poem at the memorial.
A dirgeful atmosphere settled over the town after the tragic news spread.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerful - noticeably happy and optimistic
  • synonym: mournful - feeling or expressing sadness or grief
