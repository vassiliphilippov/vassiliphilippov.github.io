pickpocketing

verb
to steal secretly
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkˌpɑːkɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪkˌpɒkɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
to steal money or small valuables from someone's pocket, bag, or clothing without them noticing

Examples

To pickpocket a tourist
The police caught him pickpocketing tourists at the busy market.
She was accused of pickpocketing during the crowded festival.
Security cameras recorded the suspect pickpocketing several people on the subway.

Related Terms

  • antonym: return - to give something back to its owner
  • synonym: rob - to take property from a person or place by force or threat
  • synonym: steal - to take something that does not belong to you without permission
