Synonyms

  • move
    1.0 332/m
    move is to change position or place, while go is a general verb for leaving or moving in a direction.
  • travel
    1.0 123/m
    travel means going over a distance for a trip, while go covers any leaving or simple motion in many situations.
  • go away
    1.0 17/m
    go away tells someone to leave or describes leaving a place, while go is the basic verb for moving or leaving.
  • depart
    1.3 13/m
    depart is a formal word for leaving a place, while go is more general and used in everyday speech for movement.
  • set out
    2.4 6.66/m
    set out means to begin a journey with a clear plan, while go simply means to move or leave without detail.
  • locomote
    7.9 0.01/m
    locomote is a rare, technical verb for physical moving, while go is common everyday speech for moving or leaving.
