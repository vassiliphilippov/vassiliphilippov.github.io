superfat

adjective
extremely overweight
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌfæt / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌfæt / (BrE)
very fat or extremely overweight, often used informally or in slang

Examples

Superfat cartoon cat
The cartoon character was drawn as a superfat cat who loved to eat.
He joked about being superfat after eating three slices of cake at the party.
Some people use the word superfat to describe extremely large animals in funny stories.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: fat - having a lot of body fat
