hollow-horned

adjective
having hollow horns
6.3 8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈhɑː.loʊ hɔːrnd/ /; / /ˈhɑː.loʊ hɑːrnd/ (AmE), /ˈhɒl.əʊ hɔːnd/ (BrE)
(of animals) having horns that are hollow rather than solid throughout.

Examples

Hollow-horned animals
Many hollow-horned ruminants are important in agriculture.
The hollow-horned cattle are domesticated for both their milk and meat.
Unlike deer, which have solid antlers, antelopes are hollow-horned animals.
