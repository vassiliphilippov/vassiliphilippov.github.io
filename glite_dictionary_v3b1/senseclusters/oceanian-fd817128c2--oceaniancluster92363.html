Oceanian

noun
person from Oceania
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊʃiˈæniən / (AmE), / ˌəʊʃiˈɑːniən / (BrE)
a person who is from, lives in, or is native to Oceania

Examples

An Oceanian artist
The museum featured art created by a renowned Oceanian from Fiji.
As an Oceanian, she is proud of her cultural heritage and traditions.
Many Oceanians speak several languages, reflecting the region's diversity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Pacific Islander - a person from the islands of the Pacific Ocean
