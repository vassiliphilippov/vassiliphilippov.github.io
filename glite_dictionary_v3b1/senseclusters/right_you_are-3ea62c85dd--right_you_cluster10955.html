right you are

interjection
expression of agreement or affirmation
Pronunciation: / raɪt ju ɑr / (AmE), / raɪt juː ɑː / (BrE)
used to express agreement or affirmation; you are correct.

Examples

Yes, right you are
"Right you are," said the manager, nodding in agreement.
If you think it's finished, then right you are.
Whenever she corrected him, he would respond with "right you are!" and a smile.
