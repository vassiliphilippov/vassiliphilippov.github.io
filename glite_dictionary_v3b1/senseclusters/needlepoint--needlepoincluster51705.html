needlepoint

noun
type of embroidery using canvas and yarn
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈniː.dl̩.pɔɪnt /; / ˈniː.dl̩ˌpɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈniː.dl.pɔɪnt /; / ˈniː.dlˌpɔɪnt / (BrE)
a type of embroidery made by hand with a needle on canvas or a pattern, often creating decorative designs
