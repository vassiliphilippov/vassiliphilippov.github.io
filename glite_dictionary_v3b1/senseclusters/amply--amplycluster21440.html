amply

adverb
to a large or sufficient degree
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈæmpli / (AmE), / ˈampli / (BrE)
in a way that is more than enough; fully or sufficiently

Examples

To be amply rewarded
His efforts were amply rewarded with a promotion and a bonus.
The room was amply decorated for the wedding reception, with flowers everywhere.
Your explanation should amply cover all the main points for the committee.
She was amply rewarded for her years of hard work.
The needs of the refugees were amply provided for by relief agencies.
The room was amply large enough to fit everyone comfortably.
