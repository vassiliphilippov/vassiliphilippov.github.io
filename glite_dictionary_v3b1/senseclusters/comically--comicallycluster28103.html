comically

adverb
in a humorous or funny way
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmɪkli / (AmE), / ˈkɒmɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is amusing or intended to be funny

Examples

To act comically
He slipped on the ice comically, just like in an old slapstick movie.
She reacted comically to the unexpected news, making everyone laugh.
The magician's tricks failed comically but still entertained the children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: humorously - in a humorous manner
  • antonym: seriously - in a serious manner
