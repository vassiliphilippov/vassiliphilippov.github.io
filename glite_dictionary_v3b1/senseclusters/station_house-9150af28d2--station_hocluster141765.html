station house

noun
police headquarters building
5.7 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈsteɪ.ʃən haʊs/ /; / /ˈsteɪ.ʃən haʊz/ (AmE), /ˈsteɪ.ʃən haʊs/ /; / /ˈsteɪ.ʃən haʊz/ (BrE)
A building that serves as the headquarters for police in a particular district, where police officers are based and detainees are taken.

Examples

The station house building
After the arrest, the suspect was taken to the station house for questioning.
Several officers gathered at the station house before their morning patrol.
The old station house at the corner was renovated into a museum.
