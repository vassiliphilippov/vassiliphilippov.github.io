surmullet

noun
a brightly colored tropical fish with chin barbels
Pronunciation: / sɜrˈmʌlɪt / (AmE), / sɜːˈmʌlɪt / (BrE)
a brightly colored tropical fish, especially of the genus Mullus, distinguished by a pair of chin barbels

Examples

A surmullet fish
A display tank at the aquarium features several species of surmullet.
Divers often spot surmullets foraging along sandy sea floors using their chin barbels.
The Mediterranean surmullet is prized for its delicate flavor and bright coloration.

Related Terms
