Phlebotomus papatasii

noun
species of disease-carrying sand fly
9.9
Pronunciation: / fləˈbɑtəˌmoʊs ˌpæpəˈteɪsaɪ / (AmE), / fləˈbɒtəməs ˌpæpəˈtɑːsiː / (BrE)
A species of small sand fly whose bloodsucking females can transmit sandfly fever and leishmaniasis.

Examples

Phlebotomus papatasii sand fly
Phlebotomus papatasii is recognized as a primary vector of leishmaniasis in many tropical regions.
