horizontality

noun
the state of being horizontal
Pronunciation: / həˌrɪzənˈtæləti / (AmE), / hɒˌrɪzənˈtælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being horizontal or parallel to the horizon

Examples

The horizontality of lines
Modernist architecture often values horizontality over vertical expression.
The horizontality of the building’s lines was emphasized by the wide, flat roof.
The landscape painting conveys a sense of horizontality through its wide, sweeping brushstrokes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: verticality - the quality or state of being vertical
