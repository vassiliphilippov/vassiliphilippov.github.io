grown-up

adjective
for adults
Pronunciation: / ˈɡroʊn ʌp / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊn ʌp / (BrE)
intended for or suitable for adults rather than children

Examples

Grown-up movie
The movie had a grown-up storyline that was not appropriate for young children.
She prefers reading grown-up books instead of comics these days.
The restaurant has a grown-up atmosphere, making it ideal for a quiet dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adult - suitable for or relating to adults
  • antonym: childish - suitable for or typical of children
