twinning

noun
pairing or forming twins
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtwɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of forming twins or pairing two things, especially cities or organizations, in a similar way

Examples

City twinning
The twinning of the two cities led to many cultural exchanges.
In biology, twinning refers to the natural process of producing twins.
The twinning of organizations can help share knowledge and resources between them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pairing - the act of joining two things together as a pair
  • antonym: separation - the act of moving or being moved apart
