otoscopy

noun
ear examination
6.5
Pronunciation: / oʊˈtɑːskəpi / (AmE), / əʊˈtɒskəpi / (BrE)
a medical examination of the ear canal and eardrum using a special instrument called an otoscope

Examples

Otoscopy examination
The doctor performed an otoscopy to check for signs of infection in the ear.
During the otoscopy, the physician noticed redness and swelling in the ear canal.
A routine otoscopy can help detect problems like earwax buildup or eardrum damage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: otoscope - an instrument used to examine the ear
