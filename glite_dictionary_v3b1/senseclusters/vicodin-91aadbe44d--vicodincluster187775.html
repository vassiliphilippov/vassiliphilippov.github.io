Vicodin

proper noun
pain relief medication
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪkədɪn / (AmE), / ˈvɪkədɪn / (BrE)
a brand name for a prescription medicine containing hydrocodone and acetaminophen, used to relieve moderate to severe pain

Examples

Vicodin tablets
After his dental surgery, the dentist prescribed Vicodin to help manage the pain.
She was warned not to drive while taking Vicodin because it can cause drowsiness.
Doctors monitor patients closely when they are prescribed Vicodin due to its potential for addiction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acetaminophen - a common pain reliever and fever reducer
  • synonym: hydrocodone - an opioid pain medication
  • antonym: ibuprofen - a non-opioid pain reliever
