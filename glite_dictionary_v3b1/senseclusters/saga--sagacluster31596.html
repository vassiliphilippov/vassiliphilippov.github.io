saga

noun
a long heroic or family narrative
6.4 6.65/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs…ëÀê…°…ô / (AmE), / Ààs…ëÀê…°…ô / (BrE)
A long, involved narrative about heroic deeds, families, or events, especially a traditional story from medieval Iceland; more generally, any lengthy prose story resembling such an account.

Examples

An epic saga
The Icelandic saga of Egil Skallagrimsson is famous for its vivid storytelling.
The Star Wars films are often referred to as a cinematic saga spanning decades and galaxies.
The novel traces the saga of three generations of a family living in rural Texas.
