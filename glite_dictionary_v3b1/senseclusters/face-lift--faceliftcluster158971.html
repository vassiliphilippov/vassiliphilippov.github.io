face-lift

noun
cosmetic update; modernization
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪsˌlɪft / (AmE), / ˈfeɪs.lɪft / (BrE)
a small change or set of changes made to the appearance of something (for example a building, product, or website) to make it look more modern or attractive.

Examples

A face-lift for buildings
The old city hall got a much-needed face-lift that brightened its exterior and fixed the roof.
After the update, the company's website received a subtle face-lift to improve navigation and style.
The cereal brand gave its packaging a quick face-lift before the product relaunched nationwide.

Related Terms

  • synonym: makeover - a complete change of appearance or style, often to look more attractive
  • synonym: redesign - to change the design or appearance of something, especially to improve it
  • synonym: renovation - the process of improving a building or structure, often including cosmetic changes
