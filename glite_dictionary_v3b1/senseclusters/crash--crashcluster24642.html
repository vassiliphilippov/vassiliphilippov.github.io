Synonyms

  • free fall
    4.6 0.3/m
    a free fall is a quick uncontrolled plunge in value in markets, while a crash is a sudden large decline causing wider damage.
  • collapse
    4.1 16/m
    a collapse is a complete breakdown of a company or system, while a crash is a sharp market fall often starting a chain of failures.
