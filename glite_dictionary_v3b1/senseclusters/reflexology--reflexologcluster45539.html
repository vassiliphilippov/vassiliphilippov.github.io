reflexology

noun
massage-based therapy using pressure points
Pronunciation: / ˌriːflɛkˈsɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌriːflɛkˈsɒlədʒi / (BrE)
A therapeutic method involving massage and finger pressure on specific points of the feet, hands, or head, believed to correspond to different areas of the body and relieve tension or illness.

Examples

Reflexology foot massage
He booked a reflexology session to address his chronic headaches.
Many people use reflexology to alleviate stress and promote relaxation.
The spa offers a range of therapies, including reflexology and aromatherapy.

  • synonym: massage - manual manipulation of soft tissues for relaxation or health
