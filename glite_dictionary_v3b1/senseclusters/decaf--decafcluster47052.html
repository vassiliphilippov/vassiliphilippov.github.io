decaf

noun
coffee without caffeine
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːˌkæf / (AmE), / ˈdiː.kæf / (BrE)
coffee that has had most or all of the caffeine removed.

Examples

A cup of decaf
I ordered a decaf because I can’t have caffeine late in the day.
She prefers decaf even in the morning.
There’s always a pot of decaf brewing at the office.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coffee - a hot drink made from roasted and ground coffee beans
