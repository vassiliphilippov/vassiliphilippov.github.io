on my watch

adverb
while I am responsible
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɑn maɪ wɑtʃ / (AmE), / ɒn maɪ wɒtʃ / (BrE)
during the period when I am responsible or in charge of a situation

Examples

On my watch no mistakes
No mistakes will be tolerated on my watch.
Nothing like that happened on my watch when I was the manager.
The security breach occurred on my watch, and I take responsibility.
