Nell Gwynn

proper noun
English actress; mistress of Charles II
Pronunciation: / nɛl ɡwɪn / (AmE), / nɛl ɡwɪn / (BrE)
(1650–1687) An English actress and comedienne celebrated as a prominent figure in Restoration theatre and known for being the mistress of King Charles II.
