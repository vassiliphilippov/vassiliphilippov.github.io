Gigantopithecus

proper noun
extinct giant ape
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒaɪˌɡæntoʊpɪˈθiːkəs / (AmE), / dʒɪˌɡæntəʊpɪˈθiːkəs / (BrE)
an extinct genus of giant ape that lived in Asia during the Pleistocene epoch

Examples

Gigantopithecus fossils
Scientists believe that Gigantopithecus may have stood up to three meters tall.
Fossilized teeth of Gigantopithecus have been found in caves in southern China.
The extinction of Gigantopithecus is thought to be linked to climate change and habitat loss.
