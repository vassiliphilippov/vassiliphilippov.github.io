course

noun
layer of bricks or masonry
7.0 0.98/m
Pronunciation: / k…îrs / (AmE), / k…îÀês / (BrE)
A continuous horizontal layer of bricks, stones, or other masonry units in a wall or structure.

Examples

A brick course
A decorative stone course runs along the top of the structure.
The mason laid another course to strengthen the foundation.
They built the wall using three courses of bricks.

Synonyms

  • row
    1.0 33/m
    A row is a simple line or horizontal layer for people or objects, while a course is the continuous masonry layer in a wall.
