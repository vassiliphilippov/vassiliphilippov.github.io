planetarium

noun
apparatus for showing planetary movements
4.2 4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌplænəˈtɛriəm /; / plænəˈtɛriəm / (AmE), / ˌplænɪˈteəriəm /; / plænɪˈteərɪəm / (BrE)
a building, room, or device where images of stars, planets, and other celestial bodies are shown, usually on a domed ceiling, for education or entertainment
