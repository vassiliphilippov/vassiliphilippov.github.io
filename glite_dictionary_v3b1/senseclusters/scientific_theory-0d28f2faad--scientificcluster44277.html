A well-substantiated explanation of some aspect of the natural world, based on a body of facts that have been repeatedly confirmed through observation and experiment.

Examples

A well-established scientific theory must be supported by extensive evidence from many experiments.
The theory of evolution is one of the most thoroughly tested scientific theories in biology.
