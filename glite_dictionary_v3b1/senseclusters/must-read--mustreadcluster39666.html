must-read

noun
highly recommended reading
3.7 1.04/m
Pronunciation: / m åst Ààrid / (AmE), / m åst ÀàriÀêd / (BrE)
a book, article, or publication considered essential or highly recommended to read

Examples

A must-read book

Her latest novel is a must-read for anyone interested in historical fiction.
This article is a must-read for all new employees joining the company.
