Examples

A entreaty for help

Their entreaties for help were met with silence from the authorities.

Synonyms

  • imploration
    8.5 0.00/m

  • plea
    3.9 9.99/m
    a plea is a humble or formal request often made to someone in power, while an entreaty shows deep personal feeling.
  • appeal
    2.9 9.33/m
    an appeal is a public or formal request for help or action, while an entreaty is more private and emotional.
  • supplication
    8.7 1.12/m
