codfish ball

noun
fried ball of cod and potatoes
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑdˌfɪʃ bɔl / (AmE), / ˈkɒdˌfɪʃ bɔːl / (BrE)
A small ball of seasoned mashed potatoes mixed with flaked salt cod, usually breaded and fried.

Examples
