extracranial

adjective
outside the skull
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstrəˈkreɪniəl / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəˈkreɪniəl / (BrE)
located or occurring outside the skull

Examples

Extracranial tumor
The surgeon identified an extracranial tumor near the base of the skull.
Ultrasound can help detect extracranial blockages in the carotid arteries.
The patient was diagnosed with an extracranial hemorrhage after the accident.
