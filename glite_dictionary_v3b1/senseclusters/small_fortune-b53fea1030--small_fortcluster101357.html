small fortune

noun
a large sum of money
Pronunciation: / smɔl ˈfɔrtʃən / (AmE), / smɔːl ˈfɔːtʃuːn / (BrE)
a large amount of money, typically more than expected or more than is usual for a particular situation

Examples

A small fortune made
He made a small fortune selling rare vintage records online.
She spent a small fortune on designer shoes last year.
The repairs to the roof are going to cost us a small fortune.
