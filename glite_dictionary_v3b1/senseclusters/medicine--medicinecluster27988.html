medicine

noun
the science and profession of treating diseases
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdəsən / (AmE), / ˈmɛdsɪn /; / ˈmɛdɪsɪn / (BrE)
the science or field of diagnosing, treating, and preventing diseases and injuries

Examples

Advancements in medicine have significantly increased average life expectancy.
Many students study medicine with the hope of becoming doctors.
She decided to pursue a career in medicine rather than surgery.
