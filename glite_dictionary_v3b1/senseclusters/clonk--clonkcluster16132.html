clonk

verb
to hit with dull sound
8.3
Pronunciation: / klɑŋk / (AmE), / klɒŋk / (BrE)
to hit or strike something, producing a dull, heavy sound

Examples

To clonk your head
He accidentally clonked his head on the low doorway as he entered the room.
She clonked the metal pot onto the counter, making everyone jump.
The ball clonked against the wall with a heavy, echoing sound.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bang - to strike something loudly or forcefully
  • degree lower: tap - to hit lightly and quickly
  • synonym: thump - to hit with a heavy, dull sound
