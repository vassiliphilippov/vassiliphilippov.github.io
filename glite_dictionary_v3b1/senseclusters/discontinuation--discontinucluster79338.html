discontinuation

noun
the act of stopping or interrupting
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪs.kənˌtɪn.juˈeɪ.ʃən /; / ˌdɪskənˌtɪnjuˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪs.kɒnˌtɪn.juˈeɪ.ʃən /; / ˌdɪskənˌtɪnjuˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of stopping or ending something, either temporarily or permanently
