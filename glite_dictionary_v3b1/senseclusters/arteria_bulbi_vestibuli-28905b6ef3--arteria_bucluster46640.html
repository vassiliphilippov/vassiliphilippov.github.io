arteria bulbi vestibuli

noun
artery supplying bulb of vestibule (female anatomy)
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɑːr.ˈtɪə.ri.ə ˈbʌl.bi ˌvɛs.tɪˈbjuː.li/ (AmE), /ɑː.ˈtɪə.ri.ə ˈbʌl.bi ˌvɛs.tɪˈbjuː.li/ (BrE)
