burdened

adjective
weighed down by work or difficulties
3.8 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…ùÀêd…ônd / (AmE), / Ààb…úÀêd…ônd / (BrE)
carrying a heavy load of work, difficulties, or responsibilities; feeling weighed down by something.

Examples

Burdened with debt
After the financial crisis, many families were burdened with debt.
He walked out of the meeting looking burdened and worried.
She felt burdened by the weight of her responsibilities at work.
