reading

noun
public recitation or performance of literature
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈriːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈriːdɪŋ / (BrE)
an event where literature or written works are recited or performed publicly, often by the author or reader

Examples

Poetry reading event
A reading of Shakespearean sonnets was scheduled for the cultural festival.
She attended a reading at the bookstore, where several writers performed their latest work.
The library hosted a reading of the author's new poems last Friday evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recitation - the act of reciting a memorized or prepared text, often publicly
