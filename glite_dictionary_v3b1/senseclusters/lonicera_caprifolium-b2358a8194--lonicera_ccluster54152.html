Lonicera caprifolium

noun
species of honeysuckle; climbing shrub
9.9
Pronunciation: / loʊˈnɪsərə ˌkæprɪˈfoʊliəm / (AmE), / ləˈnɪsərə ˌkæprɪˈfəʊliəm / (BrE)
