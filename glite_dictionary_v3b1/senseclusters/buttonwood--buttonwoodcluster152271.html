buttonwood

noun
large North American plane tree
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtnˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈbʌtnˌwʊd / (BrE)
a very large, spreading plane tree (Platanus occidentalis) native to eastern and central North America and Mexico, also known as the American sycamore

Examples

A buttonwood tree
A row of buttonwoods lines the riverbank, their broad leaves whispering in the breeze.
Early settlers used buttonwood wood for making furniture and tools.
The old buttonwood in the park provides shade for dozens of people each summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plane tree - a tree of the genus Platanus, related to buttonwood
  • synonym: sycamore - a large North American plane tree (Platanus occidentalis)
