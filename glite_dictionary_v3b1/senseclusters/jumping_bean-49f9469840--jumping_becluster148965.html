jumping bean

noun
seed that jumps due to larva inside
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ biːn / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ biːn / (BrE)
the seed of certain Mexican shrubs that contains a moth larva, which moves inside and causes the seed to jump or tumble

Examples

A jumping bean seed
A jumping bean can writhe and twitch because of the moth larva inside.
Children are fascinated when the jumping bean suddenly moves on its own.
Souvenir shops in Mexico sometimes sell boxes of jumping beans to tourists.
