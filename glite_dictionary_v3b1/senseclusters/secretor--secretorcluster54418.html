secretor

noun
one who releases substances
7.8
Pronunciation: /ˈsiː.krə.tər/ (AmE), /ˈsiː.krə.tə/ (BrE)
a person or organism that produces and releases a particular substance, especially a bodily fluid, into bodily fluids such as saliva or mucus

Examples

Researchers studied how secretors and nonsecretors differ in their immune responses.
