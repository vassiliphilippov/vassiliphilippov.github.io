zanjero

noun
water official
9.9
Pronunciation: /zæn.ˈhe.roʊ/ /; / /zæn.ˈdʒe.roʊ/ (AmE), /zæn.ˈhe.rəʊ/ /; / /zæn.ˈdʒe.rəʊ/ (BrE)
a person (often an official or worker) who manages, oversees, or maintains irrigation ditches or water supply, especially in the southwestern United States
