workability

noun
practical feasibility
4.8 0.1/m

the degree to which something, such as a plan or idea, can be successfully put into practice

Examples

Workability of a plan
The engineers discussed the workability of the new design before starting construction.
We need to consider the workability of this plan given our limited resources.
Her report questioned the workability of implementing such strict regulations in a short time.
