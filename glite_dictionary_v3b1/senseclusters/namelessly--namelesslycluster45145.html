namelessly

adverb
indescribably
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪmləsli / (AmE), / ˈneɪmləsli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be described or named, often because it is too intense or unclear.

Examples

Namelessly afraid
She felt namelessly anxious as she waited for the results.
He wandered namelessly through the city, searching for something he could not define.
The music moved her namelessly, stirring emotions she could not put into words.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indescribably - in a way that cannot be described
  • synonym: vaguely - in a way that is not clear or definite
