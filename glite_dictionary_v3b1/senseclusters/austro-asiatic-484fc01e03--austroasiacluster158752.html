Austro-Asiatic

adjective
language family of SE Asia
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːstroʊ ˌeɪʒiˈætɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːstrəʊ ˌeɪzɪˈatɪk / (BrE)
Belonging to or denoting a family of languages spoken in southern and southeastern Asia, including Vietnamese, Khmer, and Mon.

Examples

Austro-Asiatic language family
Many indigenous tribes in India speak languages belonging to the Austro-Asiatic family.
The migration of Austro-Asiatic speakers influenced the linguistic landscape of Southeast Asia.
Vietnamese and Khmer are prominent representatives of the Austro-Asiatic language group.
