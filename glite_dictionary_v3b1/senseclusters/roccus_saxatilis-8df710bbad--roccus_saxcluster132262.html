Roccus saxatilis

noun
species of striped bass (fish)
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈrɑːk.əs sæk.sə.ˈtɪl.ɪs/ (AmE), /ˈrɒk.əs sæk.sə.ˈtɪl.ɪs/ (BrE)
A species of marine food and game fish, also known as the striped bass, with dark longitudinal stripes; it migrates upriver to spawn and is sometimes placed in the genus Morone.

The diet of Roccus saxatilis consists mainly of smaller fish and crustaceans.
