bizarrely

adverb
strangely or unusually
4.4
Pronunciation: / bɪˈzɑːrli / (AmE), / bɪˈzɑːli / (BrE)
in a very strange, unusual, or unexpected way

Examples

To bizarrely behave
The artist bizarrely combined kitchen utensils and old shoes in her latest sculpture.
He dressed bizarrely for the party, wearing a cape and mismatched socks.
The meeting ended bizarrely when the lights suddenly went out and everyone started laughing.
He bizarrely wore a winter coat in the middle of summer.
Bizarrely, the quietest student in class became the lead singer of a rock band.
The story ended bizarrely, leaving everyone confused and surprised.
