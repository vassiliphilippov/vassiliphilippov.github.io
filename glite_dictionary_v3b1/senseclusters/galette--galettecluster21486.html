galette

noun
French butter cookie
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈlɛt / (AmE), / ɡaˈlɛt / (BrE)
a crisp, flat butter cookie or pastry from France, especially Brittany

Examples

A butter galette
She served a homemade galette with coffee after dinner.
The bakery is famous for its traditional Breton galette made with rich butter.
At the market, we bought a box of crisp galette cookies to take home.
