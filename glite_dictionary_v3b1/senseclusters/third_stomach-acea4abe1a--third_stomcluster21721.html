third stomach

noun
third compartment of a ruminant's stomach
9.3 7.2
Pronunciation: /θɜːrd ˈstʌ.mək/ (AmE), /θɜːd ˈstʌ.mək/ (BrE)
the third compartment of the stomach in a ruminant animal, situated between the reticulum and abomasum

Examples
