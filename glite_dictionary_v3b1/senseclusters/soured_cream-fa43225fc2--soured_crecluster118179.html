soured cream

noun
fermented cream with a tart flavor
5.8 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈsaʊ.ərd kriːm/ /; / /saʊrd kriːm/ (AmE), /ˈsaʊ.əd kriːm/ /; / /saʊd kriːm/ (BrE)
