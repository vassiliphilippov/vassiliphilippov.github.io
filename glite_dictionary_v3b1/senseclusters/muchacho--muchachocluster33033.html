muchacho

interjection
addressing a young man
Pronunciation: / muˈtʃɑːtʃoʊ / (AmE), / muˈtʃɑːtʃəʊ / (BrE)
used informally to address or call out to a young man or boy, especially in Spanish-speaking contexts

Examples

Hey muchacho
Hey muchacho, can you help me carry these boxes to the car?
Relax, muchacho, everything is going to be fine.
The coach shouted, 'muchacho, pay attention!' during the practice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buddy - informal way to address a male friend
  • synonym: lad - informal term for a boy or young man
