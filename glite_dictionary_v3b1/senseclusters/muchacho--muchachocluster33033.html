muchacho

interjection
addressing a young man
9.1
Pronunciation: /mu.ˈtʃɑː.tʃoʊ/ /; / /mə.ˈtʃɑː.tʃoʊ/ (AmE), /mu.ˈtʃɑː.tʃəʊ/ /; / /mə.ˈtʃɑː.tʃəʊ/ (BrE)
used informally to address or call out to a young man or boy, especially in Spanish-speaking contexts

Examples
