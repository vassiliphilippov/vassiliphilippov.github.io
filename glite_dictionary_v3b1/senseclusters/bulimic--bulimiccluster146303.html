bulimic

adjective
relating to or suffering from bulimia
7.2 6.5
Pronunciation: /bjuː.ˈlɪ.mɪk/ /; / /buː.ˈlɪ.mɪk/ /; / /bə.ˈlɪ.mɪk/ (AmE), /bjuː.ˈlɪ.mɪk/ /; / /buː.ˈlɪ.mɪk/ /; / /bə.ˈlɪ.mɪk/ (BrE)
relating to or suffering from bulimia, an eating disorder characterized by episodes of binge eating followed by compensatory behaviors such as vomiting or excessive exercise.

Examples

Bulimic behavior
She struggled with bulimic behaviors throughout her teenage years.
Support groups for bulimic individuals can be very helpful during treatment.
The clinic provides therapy for bulimic patients seeking recovery.

  • antonym: anorexic - relating to or suffering from anorexia, an eating disorder characterized by restricted food intake
