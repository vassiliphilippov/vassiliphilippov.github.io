bloomer

noun
an embarrassing mistake
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːmɚ / (AmE), / ˈbluːmə / (BrE)
an embarrassing or foolish mistake

Examples

A big bloomer
He told a funny story about his biggest bloomer as a young politician.
I made a real bloomer at the meeting by forgetting my boss's name.
That typo was quite a bloomer in yesterday's newspaper.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blunder - a careless or serious mistake
  • synonym: gaffe - a social or diplomatic error
  • antonym: triumph - a great success or achievement (opposite of mistake)
