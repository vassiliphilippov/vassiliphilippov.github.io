Continental

adjective
relating to continental Europe
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːn.tɪˈnen.t̬əl / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.tɪˈnen.təl / (BrE)
relating to Europe, especially as distinct from Britain or Ireland; characteristic of continental Europe.

Examples

Continental breakfast
In France, a Continental breakfast typically includes croissants and coffee.
Many Continental customs differ from those in the United Kingdom.
She enjoys Continental fashion, especially styles from Paris and Milan.
