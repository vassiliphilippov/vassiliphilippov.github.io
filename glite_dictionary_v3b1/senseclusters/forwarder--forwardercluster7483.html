forwarder

noun
shipping agent
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrwərdər / (AmE), / ˈfɔːwədə / (BrE)
a person or company that arranges the transport and shipping of goods for others

Examples

Freight forwarder
The forwarder arranged customs clearance for the imported electronics.
We hired a forwarder to manage the logistics of our overseas shipments.
A reliable forwarder can help reduce delays in international trade.
The forwarder arranged for the goods to be shipped overseas by air.
Our company relies on a trusted forwarder to handle all international deliveries.
A good forwarder can simplify the process of exporting products to new markets.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carrier - a company that actually transports goods or people
  • synonym: freight forwarder - a company specializing in organizing shipments for individuals or corporations
