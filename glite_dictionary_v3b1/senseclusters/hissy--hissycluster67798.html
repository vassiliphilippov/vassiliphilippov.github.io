hissy

adjective
making a hissing sound
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪsi / (AmE), / ˈhɪsi / (BrE)
making or showing a hissing sound, often as a sign of anger or annoyance

Examples

Hissy snake
The snake became hissy when it felt threatened by the loud noise.
Our cat gets hissy whenever a stranger enters the house.
She gave a hissy response when asked about her missing homework.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - not showing anger or agitation
  • synonym: spiteful - showing or caused by malice or anger
