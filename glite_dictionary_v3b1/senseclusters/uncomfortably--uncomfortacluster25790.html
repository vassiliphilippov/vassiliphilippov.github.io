uncomfortably

adverb
in a physically uncomfortable way
2.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkʌmfərtəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈkʌmfətəbli / (BrE)
In a way that causes physical discomfort or makes someone feel uneasy.

Examples

Shift uncomfortably in seat
He shifted uncomfortably in his seat, trying to relieve the pain in his back.
It was uncomfortably hot in the crowded train carriage.
She sat uncomfortably on the hard wooden bench during the ceremony.
