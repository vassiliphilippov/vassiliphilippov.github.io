untwist

verb
make straight or loose after twisting
5.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtwɪst / (AmE), / ʌnˈtwɪst / (BrE)
to make something straight or loose after it has become twisted or tangled

Examples

To untwist a rope
After washing, gently untwist the wet towel before hanging it up to dry.
Can you help me untwist this necklace chain?
She paused to untwist the cords tangled behind her computer desk.
