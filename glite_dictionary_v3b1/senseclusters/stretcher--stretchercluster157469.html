stretcher

noun
device for carrying people, especially the injured
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɛtʃər / (AmE), / ˈstrɛtʃə / (BrE)
a lightweight framework with a canvas or fabric surface, used for carrying ill, injured, or deceased people

Examples

Medical stretcher
After collapsing at the marathon, she was taken away on a stretcher by medical staff.
During the rescue, two firefighters lifted the patient onto a stretcher for transport to the hospital.
The paramedics quickly placed the injured hiker on a stretcher and carried him to the ambulance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gurney - a wheeled stretcher used in hospitals
