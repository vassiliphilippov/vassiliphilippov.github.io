boatside

noun
side of a boat
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊtsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈbəʊtsaɪd / (BrE)
the side of a boat, especially the area near the edge or rail

Examples

Fishing by the boatside
He leaned over the boatside to pull in the net.
Water splashed against the boatside as the storm grew stronger.
She tied the rope securely to the boatside before stepping onto the dock.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gunwale - the upper edge of the side of a boat
  • synonym: hull - the main body of a boat or ship
