key staging post

noun
important stop or location
Pronunciation: / ki ˈsteɪdʒɪŋ poʊst / (AmE), / ki ˈsteɪdʒɪŋ pəʊst / (BrE)

Examples

The small town became a key staging post for relief supplies after the storm.
The village acted as a key staging post for migrating birds on their long journey.
