square deal

noun
fair and honest treatment
Pronunciation: / skwɛr diːl / (AmE), / skweə diːl / (BrE)
fair and honest treatment of people in a situation or agreement

Examples

A square deal
She felt she did not get a square deal after all her hard work.
The manager promised everyone a square deal during the upcoming layoffs.
Workers expect a square deal when negotiating their contracts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: raw deal - unfair or harsh treatment
