Telugu

noun
Dravidian language of southeastern India
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛlʊɡu /; / ˈtɛlɪˌɡu / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪɡuː / (BrE)
a Dravidian language spoken mainly in the Indian states of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana, southeastern India
