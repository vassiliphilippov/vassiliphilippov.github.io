fount

noun
type size and style in printing
9.0 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / faÊŠnt / (AmE), / faÊŠnt / (BrE)
a particular size and style of a typeface within a type family, traditionally referring to all the characters in that style and size

Examples

Fount of type
Each fount was carefully organized in the typesetter's drawer.
The museum displays a rare fount used in 19th-century publications.
The printer requested another fount of 12-point italic to complete the job.

Synonyms

  • font
    2.8 6.32/m

  • face
    7.8 3.10/m
    face refers to the design or look of letters in a type family, whereas fount focuses on a specific size and style.
