awe

noun
great respect or wonder
7.8 3.4
Pronunciation: / ɑː /; / ɔ / (AmE), / ɔː / (BrE)
a feeling of great respect mixed with wonder or fear, often inspired by something impressive or beautiful

Examples

In awe of nature
His first glimpse of the mountains filled him with awe.
She listened to the concert in silent awe of the musicians' talent.
The children gazed in awe at the fireworks lighting up the night sky.
He listened in awe to the scientist’s fascinating explanation of the universe.
Her voice filled the audience with awe as she sang the anthem.
The children gazed at the towering mountains in awe.
