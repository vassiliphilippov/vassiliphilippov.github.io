decease

noun
formal act of dying; death
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsis / (AmE), / dɪˈsiːs / (BrE)
the act or event of dying; death, especially as a formal or legal term.

Examples

Her decease was sudden
All property will be transferred to her heirs upon her decease.
The lawyer requested a copy of the will following his client's decease.
The sudden decease of the president caused uncertainty throughout the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: death - the end of life; cessation of all vital functions
