Examples

To act opportunistically
The software updates opportunistically when a stable internet connection is detected.
Sensors in the network transmit data opportunistically to save energy and bandwidth.
The device backs up files opportunistically whenever it finds an unmetered Wi-Fi connection.
The company expanded opportunistically into new markets when competitors withdrew.
He acted opportunistically to secure the last available ticket for the concert.
Some animals feed opportunistically, eating whatever food is easiest to find.
The bacteria spread opportunistically when the patient's immune system was compromised.
Some investors act opportunistically during market downturns to buy undervalued stocks.
The plant grows opportunistically in disturbed soil where other species cannot compete.
