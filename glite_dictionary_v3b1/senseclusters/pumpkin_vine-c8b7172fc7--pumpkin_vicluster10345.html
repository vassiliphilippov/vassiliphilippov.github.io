pumpkin vine

noun
the plant that produces pumpkins
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌmp.kɪn vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈpʌmp.kɪn vaɪn / (BrE)
a vigorous climbing or trailing plant that produces pumpkins, known for its large, round, orange fruits with firm skin and many seeds

Examples

The pumpkin vine grows
After a few weeks, bright orange pumpkins started to emerge along the pumpkin vine.
He carefully guided the pumpkin vine onto a trellis to keep the fruit off the ground.
The pumpkin vine stretched across the garden, its broad leaves shading the soil beneath.
