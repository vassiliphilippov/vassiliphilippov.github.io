wave aside

verb
dismiss or reject something
3.9
Pronunciation: / weɪv əˈsaɪd / (AmE), / weɪv əˈsaɪd / (BrE)
to dismiss or reject something as unimportant, often with a gesture

Examples

To wave aside concerns
Despite the warnings, the manager waved aside all concerns from the team.
He tried to wave aside their suggestions, insisting his plan was best.
She waved aside the criticism and continued with her work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brush off - to reject or ignore someone or something
  • synonym: dismiss - to decide not to consider something
