margherita

noun
classic pizza with tomato and cheese
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːrɡəˈriːtə / (AmE), / ˌmɑːɡəˈriːtə / (BrE)
a pizza topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil

Examples

A margherita pizza

The restaurant is famous for its wood-fired margherita with fresh basil leaves.
We shared a large margherita and a salad for lunch at the Italian café.
