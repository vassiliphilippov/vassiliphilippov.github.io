vena labialis

noun
vein draining vulvar lips
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪ.nə ləˈbeɪ.li.əs / (AmE), / ˈviː.nə læˈbeɪ.li.əs / (BrE)
A vein that drains blood from the lips of the vulva.

Examples

Vena labialis of the vulva
