Cordaites

noun
extinct genus of Paleozoic trees
Pronunciation: / kɔrˈdaɪtiːz / (AmE), / kɔːˈdaɪtiːz / (BrE)
an extinct genus of tall Paleozoic trees that were structurally intermediate between cycads and conifers
