livid

adjective
pale, ghostly light or color
6.6 7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪvɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪd / (BrE)
(of light or color) having a pale, deathlike, or ghostly luminosity; bluish-grey or pallid in appearance, often suggesting unnaturalness or eeriness.

Examples

Livid glow

The old photograph captured a livid light falling across the empty room.
The storm brought livid clouds that glowed eerily against the night sky.
