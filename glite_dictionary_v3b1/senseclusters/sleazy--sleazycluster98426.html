sleazy

adjective
morally corrupt or sordid
6.5 2/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàsliÀêzi / (AmE), / ÀàsliÀêzi / (BrE)
morally low, dishonest, or sordid, especially in appearance, behavior, or atmosphere.

Examples

Sleazy business deals

The nightclub had a sleazy reputation and attracted all kinds of shady people.
