fintech

adjective
financial technology
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪnˌtɛk / (AmE), / ˈfɪnˌtɛk / (BrE)
relating to financial technology, especially services, products, or companies that use software and digital tools in finance

Examples

Fintech solutions
Many fintech companies are changing how people manage their money online.
The fintech sector is attracting significant investment from venture capitalists.
Banks are adopting fintech solutions to improve customer experience and efficiency.

  • antonym: traditional - not using modern technology; conventional
