shipboard system

noun
system operating on a naval ship
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪpbɔrd ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpbɔːd ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
A system that is installed and operates as an integrated unit on board a naval ship.

Examples

A shipboard system failure
A failure in a single shipboard system could compromise the safety of the entire crew.
Modern naval vessels rely on advanced shipboard systems for navigation and communication.
The engineers performed routine checks on each shipboard system before departure.
