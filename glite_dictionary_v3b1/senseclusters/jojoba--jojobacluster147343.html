jojoba

noun
plant-based oil
7.6
Pronunciation: /hoʊ.ˈhoʊ.bə/ /; / /hə.ˈhoʊ.bə/ (AmE), /həʊ.ˈhəʊ.bə/ /; / /hə.ˈhəʊ.bə/ (BrE)
a liquid wax obtained from the seeds of the jojoba plant, used in cosmetics and as an industrial lubricant

Examples

Some industrial machines use jojoba as a lubricant due to its stability at high temperatures.
