monorchidism

noun
condition where only one testis descends
9.9
Pronunciation: / məˈnɔrkɪˌdɪzəm / (AmE), / məˈnɔːkɪˌdɪzəm / (BrE)
a medical condition in which only one testis descends into the scrotum, while the other remains undescended

Examples

Monorchidism condition
Monorchidism is less common than the complete failure of both testes to descend.
The veterinarian diagnosed the dog with monorchidism after discovering only one testis in the scrotum.
Treatment for monorchidism may involve surgery to locate and correct the undescended testis.
