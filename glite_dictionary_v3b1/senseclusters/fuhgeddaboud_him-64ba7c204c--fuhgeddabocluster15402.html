fuhgeddaboud him

Pronunciation: / fəˈgɛdəˌbaʊt ɪm / (AmE), / fəˈgɛdəˌbaʊt ɪm / (BrE)
(informal, slang) Used to dismiss or suggest disregarding a particular man or issue, often with emphasis, in the style of a New York accent; means 'forget about him.'

Examples

Whenever trouble comes his way, he just says, 'fuhgeddaboud him.'
