countermission

noun
opposing mission
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntərˌmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntəˌmɪʃən / (BrE)
a mission or operation carried out to oppose or prevent the success of another mission

Examples

A countermission operation
The commander launched a countermission to intercept the enemy's supply convoy.
A successful countermission can prevent the opposing side from achieving its objectives.
They planned a countermission after learning about the rival team's strategy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: counterattack - an attack made in response to an enemy's attack
  • antonym: mission - a specific task or operation assigned to a person or group
