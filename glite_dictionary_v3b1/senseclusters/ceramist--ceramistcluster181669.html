ceramist

noun
a person who makes ceramics or pottery
Pronunciation: / səˈræmɪst / (AmE), / səˈræmɪst / (BrE)
a person who makes pottery or other objects from ceramic, especially by shaping clay and firing it in a kiln

Examples

A ceramist makes pottery
As a skilled ceramist, she was known for her beautifully glazed plates.
Many art galleries feature works by contemporary ceramists from around the world.
The ceramist spent hours shaping the clay before firing the vase in the kiln.

Related Terms

  • synonym: potter - a person who makes pots, bowls, etc., from clay
