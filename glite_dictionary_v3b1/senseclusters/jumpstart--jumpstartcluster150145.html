jumpstart

noun
starting a vehicle's battery with jumper cables
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌmpˌstɑrt / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmpˌstɑːt / (BrE)
An act of starting a vehicle with a weak or dead battery by connecting it to another vehicle's battery with jumper cables.

Examples

A jumpstart for the engine
After leaving the lights on overnight, his truck required a jumpstart to get going.
She learned how to give a jumpstart in her driver's education class.
When my car wouldn't start this morning, I needed a jumpstart from a passing motorist.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jump-start - to start a vehicle by connecting it to another vehicle's battery
