scum

noun
despicable or worthless people
1.0
Pronunciation: / skʌm / (AmE), / skʌm / (BrE)
a term used to refer to people regarded as despicable, worthless, or contemptible

Examples

Call them scum
He called the criminals scum who deserved no sympathy.
Some considered the corrupt officials to be nothing but scum.
The protesters shouted that the company executives were scum for polluting the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rabble - a disorderly or worthless crowd; the riffraff
  • synonym: vermin - offensive term for people regarded as despicable or causing harm
