hysterosalpingogram

noun
X-ray of uterus and Fallopian tubes
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪstɪˌroʊsælˈpɪŋɡəˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˌhɪstərəʊˌsælpɪŋˈɡəʊɡræm / (BrE)
an X-ray image of the uterus and Fallopian tubes, usually performed to examine the structure or detect blockages, especially when investigating infertility

Examples

A hysterosalpingogram test
A hysterosalpingogram can help determine the cause of a woman's infertility.
She was nervous about her upcoming hysterosalpingogram, but the test was over quickly.
The doctor recommended a hysterosalpingogram to check for possible blockages in the Fallopian tubes.
