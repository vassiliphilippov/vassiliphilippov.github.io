industrial

adjective
relating to highly developed industries
1.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʌstriəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʌstriəl / (BrE)
relating to industry, manufacturing, or production (including people, places, or things connected with industry)

Examples

The city has transformed into an industrial center over the past few decades.
Several industrial plants have been built along the river.
The city saw rapid growth during the industrial revolution.
The region is now a major center of industrial production.
