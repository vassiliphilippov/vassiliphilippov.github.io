hydropower industry

noun
sector producing power from water
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪdroʊˌpaʊ.ɚ ˈɪn.də.stri / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ.drəʊˌpaʊ.ə ˈɪn.də.stri / (BrE)
the sector comprising all industries involved in generating electricity and energy from falling or running water
