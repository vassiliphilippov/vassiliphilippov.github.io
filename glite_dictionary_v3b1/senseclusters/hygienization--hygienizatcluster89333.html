hygienization

noun
data cleaning process
8.2
Pronunciation: / haɪˌdʒiːnəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / haɪˌdʒiːnʌɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of cleaning, standardizing, and removing errors from data or records to ensure accuracy and reliability

Examples

Data hygienization
The IT department scheduled a regular hygienization of all customer records to maintain data quality.
Effective hygienization helps organizations avoid costly mistakes caused by inaccurate information.
After the hygienization process, the database contained only verified and up-to-date entries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: data cleansing - the process of detecting and correcting errors in data
  • synonym: data validation - the process of ensuring data is accurate and useful
