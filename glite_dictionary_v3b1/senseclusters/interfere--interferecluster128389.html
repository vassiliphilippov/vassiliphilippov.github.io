to become involved in a situation in a way that prevents, hinders, or disrupts what would normally happen

Examples

To interfere with work
Loud noises from outside can interfere with my concentration when I study.
Please do not interfere with the ongoing investigation.
She didn't want to interfere in her friend's personal matters.
Parents should avoid interfering too much in their children's friendships.
Please don't interfere with the investigation, as it could compromise the results.
