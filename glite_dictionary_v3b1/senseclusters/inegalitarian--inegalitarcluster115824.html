inegalitarian

adjective
promoting inequality
9.3
Pronunciation: / ɪnɪˌɡælɪˈtɛriən / (AmE), / ɪnɪˌɡælɪˈteəriən / (BrE)
characterized by or supporting inequality or unfair treatment among people or groups

Examples

Inegalitarian system
The new policy was criticized as inegalitarian because it favored certain groups over others.
An inegalitarian society often leads to social unrest and dissatisfaction among its citizens.
Many activists campaign against inegalitarian practices in education and employment.
Critics argued that the new law was inegalitarian and would increase the gap between rich and poor.
The organization opposes inegalitarian practices in education and employment.
Many people protested against the inegalitarian structure of the political system.

Related Terms

  • antonym: egalitarian - believing in or promoting equality for all people
  • synonym: unequal - not the same in amount, value, or status
  • synonym: unfair - not just or equitable
