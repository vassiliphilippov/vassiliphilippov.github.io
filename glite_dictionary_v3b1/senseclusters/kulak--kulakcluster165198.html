kulak

noun
greedy exploiter
9.9
Pronunciation: / kuˈlɑk / (AmE), / kuˈlæk / (BrE)
a derogatory term for a person regarded as greedy or selfish, especially one accused of exploiting others for personal gain

Examples

Called a kulak
During the crisis, some accused him of being a kulak for hoarding supplies.
The politician was labeled a kulak by his opponents for prioritizing his own interests.
In the village, anyone who prospered too much risked being called a kulak by neighbors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: altruist - a person who is selfless and concerned for the well-being of others
  • synonym: exploiter - a person who takes advantage of others for personal gain
