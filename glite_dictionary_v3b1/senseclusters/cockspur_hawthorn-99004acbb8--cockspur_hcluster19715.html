cockspur hawthorn

noun
species of hawthorn tree with long thorns
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑkˌspɜr ˈhɔˌθɔrn / (AmE), / ˈkɒkˌspɜː ˈhɔːθɔːn / (BrE)
a species of hawthorn tree native to eastern North America, distinguished by its long, straight thorns and white flowers

Examples

The cockspur hawthorn tree
Gardeners often choose the cockspur hawthorn as a barrier plant due to its thorny nature.
The cockspur hawthorn is valued for its spring blossoms and dense foliage in landscaping.
You can easily recognize a cockspur hawthorn by the formidable thorns along its branches.
