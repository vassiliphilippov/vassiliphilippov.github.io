squeezable

adjective
able to be easily compressed
Pronunciation: / ˈskwizəbəl / (AmE), / ˈskwiːzəbl / (BrE)
Able to be easily compressed, pressed, or shaped by squeezing.

Examples

Squeezable container
Look for squeezable containers if you want to avoid spills in the kitchen.

This bottle is squeezable, making it easy to dispense ketchup quickly.
