redeployment

noun
the redistribution of forces or resources
Pronunciation: / ˌriː.dɪˈplɔɪ.mənt / (AmE), / ˌriː.dɪˈplɔɪ.mənt / (BrE)
the act of withdrawing and reallocating resources, personnel, or military forces to be used more effectively in a different place or role

Examples

The redeployment of staff
After the restructuring, the company announced the redeployment of several employees to new departments.
The redeployment of medical staff improved patient care in the overwhelmed emergency room.
The army ordered the redeployment of troops to the northern border in response to rising tensions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deployment - the movement of forces or resources into position for action
