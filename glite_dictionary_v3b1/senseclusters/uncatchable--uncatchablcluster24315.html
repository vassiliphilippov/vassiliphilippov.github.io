uncatchable

adjective
impossible to catch
4.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkætʃəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈkætʃəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to catch or capture

Examples

An uncatchable rabbit
The rabbit was so fast it seemed uncatchable to the children chasing it.
For years, the legendary thief remained uncatchable by the police.
The soccer player's speed made him almost uncatchable on the field.
