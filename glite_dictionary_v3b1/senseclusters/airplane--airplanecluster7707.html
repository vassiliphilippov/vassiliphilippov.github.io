airplane

noun
powered fixed-wing aircraft
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌpleɪn / (AmE), / ˈeəˌpleɪn / (BrE)
A powered flying vehicle with fixed wings, propelled by engines or jets, used for transporting people or goods through the air.

Examples

A airplane flies
From my window seat, I watched the shadow of the airplane glide over the countryside below.
She has a keen interest in how airplanes are designed and built.
The airplane took off smoothly despite the rainy weather.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aircraft - any vehicle capable of atmospheric flight
  • synonym: plane - common short form for airplane; aircraft with fixed wings
