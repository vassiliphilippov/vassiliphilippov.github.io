carnivorously

adverb
in a meat-eating way
Pronunciation: / kɑrˈnɪvərəsli / (AmE), / kɑːˈnɪvərəsli / (BrE)
in a manner characteristic of eating meat or like a carnivore

Examples

Eat carnivorously
The lion looked carnivorously at the herd of antelope grazing nearby.
He tore into the steak carnivorously, barely pausing between bites.
The hawk circled carnivorously above the field, searching for its next meal.

Related Terms

  • antonym: herbivorously - in a manner characteristic of eating plants
  • synonym: voraciously - in an eager or greedy manner when eating
