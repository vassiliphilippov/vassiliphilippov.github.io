snowplow

verb
to clear snow
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊˌplaʊ / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌplaʊ / (BrE)
to remove snow from a surface, such as a road or driveway, using a snowplow vehicle.

Examples

To snowplow the street
After the heavy snowfall, the city quickly snowplowed the main roads to keep traffic moving.
Our driveway was snowplowed before we woke up this morning.
The workers snowplowed the parking lot several times during the blizzard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plow - to clear or turn over earth or snow with a plow
  • synonym: shovel - to move or remove with a shovel
