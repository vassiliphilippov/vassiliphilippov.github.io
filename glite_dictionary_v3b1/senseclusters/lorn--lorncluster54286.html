lorn

adjective
lonely or abandoned
Pronunciation: / lɔrn / (AmE), / lɔːn / (BrE)
feeling or appearing lonely, abandoned, or forsaken

Examples

A lorn cottage
The lorn cottage stood empty at the edge of the moor.
She gazed out the window with a lorn expression, missing her family.
A lorn dog wandered the deserted streets after the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: desolate - feeling or showing misery, unhappiness, or loneliness
  • synonym: forlorn - sad and abandoned or lonely
