Post-impressionist

noun
artist of the Post-Impressionist movement
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊst.ɪmˈprɛʃ.ə.nɪst / (AmE), / ˌpəʊst.ɪmˈprɛʃ.ən.ɪst / (BrE)
an artist who belonged to the Post-Impressionist movement, rejecting Impressionism in favor of new artistic directions

Examples

Post-impressionists artists
Many Post-impressionists experimented with bold colors and abstract forms.
Paul Cézanne is often considered a key Post-impressionist who influenced modern art.
The museum features an exhibition dedicated to the works of Post-impressionist painters.
