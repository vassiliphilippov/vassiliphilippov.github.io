unlikeness

noun
dissimilarity; lack of resemblance
4.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlaɪknəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈlaɪknɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being different or dissimilar; lack of resemblance

Examples

The unlikeness of things
Her theories stress the unlikeness of individual experience rather than universal similarity.
The unlikeness of the two paintings made it clear they were by different artists.
There is a certain unlikeness between twins that only close friends can spot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dissimilarity - the state of being unlike or different
  • antonym: likeness - the quality of being alike; similarity
