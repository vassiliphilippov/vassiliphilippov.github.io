complaint

noun
formal legal accusation; initial pleading in a case
2.3
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpleɪnt / (AmE), / kəmˈpleɪnt / (BrE)
(Law) a formal statement made to a court or authority that someone has done something wrong, starting a legal case

Examples

File a complaint
A police officer must investigate any criminal complaint submitted by a citizen.
The judge reviewed the complaint before deciding if the case could proceed.
The prosecutor filed a complaint against the defendant for fraud.
After receiving the complaint, the court scheduled a preliminary hearing next month.
The attorney filed a complaint against the company for breach of contract.
The judge dismissed the complaint because it failed to state a valid legal claim.

  • synonym: charge - an official statement accusing someone of a crime
