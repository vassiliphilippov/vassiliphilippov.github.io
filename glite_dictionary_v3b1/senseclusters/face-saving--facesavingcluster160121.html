face-saving

noun
action taken to preserve dignity
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪs ˌseɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfeɪs ˌseɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
An action or measure taken to avoid embarrassment or loss of dignity.

Examples

Face-saving measure
As a face-saving measure, the politician resigned before he could be dismissed.
The company issued a face-saving apology to avoid public backlash.
They reached a compromise that offered both parties a face-saving way out.

Related Terms

  • antonym: embarrassment - a feeling or situation of self-consciousness or awkwardness
  • antonym: humiliation - loss of dignity and self-respect
