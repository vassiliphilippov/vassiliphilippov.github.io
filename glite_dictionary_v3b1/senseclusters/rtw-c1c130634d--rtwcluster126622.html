ready-to-wear clothing
6.9
Pronunciation: / ɑr ti ˈdʌbəl.ju / (AmE), / ɑː tiː ˈdʌbəl.juː / (BrE)
abbreviation for 'ready-to-wear', referring to clothing made in standard sizes and sold finished, not tailored to individual measurements

Examples

RTW collection
The fashion show will feature the designer's latest RTW collection for the upcoming season.
Many shoppers prefer RTW because it is more affordable and immediately available.
Unlike bespoke suits, RTW garments are produced in standard sizes for quick purchase.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bespoke - custom-made clothing tailored to individual measurements
  • synonym: ready-to-wear - clothing made in standard sizes and sold finished
