Xhosa language

noun
Bantu language spoken in South Africa
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊsə læŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkɒsə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A Bantu language spoken primarily in South Africa, closely related to Zulu and noted as the country's second most common home language.
