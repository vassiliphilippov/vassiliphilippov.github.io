preflight

verb
to check before use
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːˌflaɪt / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌflaɪt / (BrE)
to perform checks or tests on an aircraft, system, or file before it is used or published

Examples

To preflight a plane
Technicians always preflight the aircraft to ensure all systems are working properly.
You should preflight your document before sending it to the printer to avoid errors.
The team will preflight the software update before releasing it to users.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inspect - to examine something carefully
  • synonym: test - to check the quality or performance of something
