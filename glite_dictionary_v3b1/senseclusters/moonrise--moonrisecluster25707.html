moonrise

noun
appearance of the moon
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːnˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈmuːnˌraɪz / (BrE)
the time or event when the moon first appears above the horizon in the sky

Examples

The moonrise time
We climbed the hill to watch the moonrise over the city skyline.
The photographer captured a stunning moonrise reflected on the calm lake.
As darkness fell, the children waited eagerly for the moonrise to begin their night hike.
We planned our hike to reach the summit by moonrise for the best view.
Photographers gathered on the beach to capture the beautiful moonrise over the ocean.
The festival began at moonrise, creating a magical atmosphere for everyone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: moonset - the time when the moon disappears below the horizon
  • synonym: sunrise - the time when the sun first appears above the horizon
