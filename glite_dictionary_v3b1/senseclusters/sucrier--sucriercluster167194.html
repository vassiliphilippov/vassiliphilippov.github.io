sucrier

noun
Caribbean bird or sugar bowl
Pronunciation: / suːˈkriːeɪ / (AmE), / suːˈkriːeɪ / (BrE)
a small songbird of the Caribbean, especially the bananaquit, or a container used for holding sugar

Examples

A sucrier bird
The bright yellow sucrier flitted among the flowers in the garden.
She placed the ornate sucrier on the table next to the teapot.
Birdwatchers in the Caribbean often spot the sucrier feeding on nectar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bananaquit - a small Caribbean bird known for feeding on nectar
  • synonym: sugar bowl - a small container for serving sugar
