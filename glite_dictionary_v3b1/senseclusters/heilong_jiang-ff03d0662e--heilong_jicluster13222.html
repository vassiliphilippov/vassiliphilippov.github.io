Heilong Jiang

proper noun
Chinese name for the Amur River between China and Russia
9.0
Pronunciation: / heɪˈlɔŋ ˈdʒjɑːŋ / (AmE), / heɪˈlɒŋ ˈdʒjɑːŋ / (BrE)
the Chinese name for a river in northeast Asia (also known as the Amur River) forming part of the border between China and Russia and flowing into the Sea of Okhotsk

Examples

The Heilong Jiang river
Many rare species of fish inhabit the waters of the Heilong Jiang.
The Heilong Jiang serves as a major natural boundary between northeastern China and Russia.
Trade along the Heilong Jiang has historically linked Chinese and Russian communities.
