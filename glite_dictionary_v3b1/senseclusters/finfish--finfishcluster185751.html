finfish

adjective
about true fish
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪnˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈfɪnˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
relating to or intended for true fish with fins, as opposed to shellfish, especially in fisheries or aquaculture contexts

Examples

Finfish farming
The new regulations apply only to finfish farms, not to shellfish operations.
Researchers are studying finfish diseases to improve aquaculture health management.
The company specializes in finfish feed for sustainable fish farming.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shellfish - aquatic animals with shells, such as shrimp, crabs, or clams
