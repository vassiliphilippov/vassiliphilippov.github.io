crevalle

noun
large coastal fish
Pronunciation: / krəˈvæli / (AmE), / krəˈvæli / (BrE)
a large, fast-swimming marine fish found in warm coastal waters, also known as a jack (especially the crevalle jack)

Examples

A large crevalle fish
The fisherman proudly displayed a large crevalle he caught near the pier.
Schools of crevalle are often seen chasing baitfish in shallow waters.
Many anglers consider the crevalle a challenging and exciting catch.
Fishermen were excited to spot a school of crevalle near the reef.
The crevalle is known for its strength and speed when hooked.
Many anglers travel to tropical coasts hoping to catch a large crevalle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jack - a type of large marine fish found in warm seas
  • synonym: snapper - a type of marine fish often found in warm coastal waters
  • synonym: tuna - a large, fast-swimming fish found in warm seas
