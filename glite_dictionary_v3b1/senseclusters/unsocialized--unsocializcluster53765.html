unsocialized

adjective
lacking social experience
7.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsoʊʃəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsəʊʃəlaɪzd / (BrE)
not used to being with others or lacking social experience and learned social behaviors

Examples

An unsocialized puppy
The unsocialized puppy was nervous around strangers and avoided being petted.
Children who are unsocialized may struggle to make friends at school.
After years of working alone, he became somewhat unsocialized and found group meetings difficult.
The unsocialized puppy was fearful of people and other animals.
Researchers observed unsocialized behavior in children raised in isolation.
Some unsocialized individuals struggle to adapt to group settings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antisocial - not wanting the company of others
  • synonym: feral - living in a wild state, not domesticated or socialized
  • antonym: sociable - enjoying being with and talking to other people
  • antonym: socialized - having learned and adopted social norms and behaviors
