intensifying

adjective
increasing in strength or intensity
4.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɛnsəˌfaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛnsɪfaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
becoming stronger, greater, or more intense.

Examples

An intensifying feeling
She felt an intensifying sense of excitement as the concert began.
The intensifying debate has drawn attention from international media.
The intensifying storm forced residents to evacuate the area immediately.

Related Terms

  • antonym: weakening - becoming less strong or intense
