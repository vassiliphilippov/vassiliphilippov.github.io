afo

noun
leg and foot brace
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ ɛf ˈoʊ / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ɛf ˈəʊ / (BrE)
a brace worn on the lower leg and foot to support the ankle and help with walking, short for ankle–foot orthosis

Examples

Wear an afo brace
After her injury, she wore an afo to help stabilize her ankle while walking.
The doctor recommended an afo to improve his mobility and prevent falls.
Children with certain muscle conditions may benefit from using an afo for support.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brace - a device that supports a weak or injured body part
  • synonym: orthosis - a medical device used to support or correct the function of a limb
