writing style

noun
distinctive manner of writing
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪtɪŋ ˌstaɪl / (AmE), / ˈraɪtɪŋ ˌstaɪl / (BrE)
the distinctive manner in which a person expresses ideas in writing; a way of using language and structure in written work

Examples
