lollipop lady

noun
woman who helps children cross roads near schools
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːliˌpɑp ˌleɪdi / (AmE), / ˈlɒl.iˌpɒp ˌleɪ.di / (BrE)
a woman employed to help children cross the road safely near a school, especially by holding up a circular stop sign

Examples

The lollipop lady stops traffic
Every morning, the lollipop lady stands outside the school rain or shine.
Parents appreciated how friendly the lollipop lady was with the students.
The lollipop lady helped the children cross the busy street safely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lollipop man - a man who helps children cross the road safely near a school
