non-skilled

adjective
without special training
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈskɪld / (AmE), / nɒnˈskɪld / (BrE)
not requiring special training, education, or skill

Examples

Non-skilled job
Many non-skilled positions are available for people without formal education.
The factory hired several non-skilled workers to help with basic assembly tasks.
He started in a non-skilled job but later received training for a higher position.

Related Terms

  • antonym: skilled - having or requiring special training or ability
  • synonym: unskilled - not having or requiring special skill or training
