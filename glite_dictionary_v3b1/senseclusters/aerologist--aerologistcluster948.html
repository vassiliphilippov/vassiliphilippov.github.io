aerologist

noun
atmosphere scientist
9.8
Pronunciation: /eə.ˈrɑː.lə.dʒɪst/ (AmE), /eə.ˈrɒ.lə.dʒɪst/ (BrE)
a scientist who studies the atmosphere and its phenomena, such as weather and climate

Examples

The aerologist studies weather
The aerologist presented new findings about atmospheric pressure at the conference.
Before the flight, an aerologist checked the weather conditions for safety.
Many aerologists work closely with meteorologists to improve weather forecasts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meteorologist - a scientist who studies weather and atmospheric conditions
