multilayered

adjective
having many layers
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈleɪərd / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈleɪəd / (BrE)
having many layers or levels, either physically or in meaning or complexity

Examples

A multilayered cake
The cake was beautifully multilayered, with different flavors in each section.
Her argument was so multilayered that it took time to fully understand all the points.
The film's multilayered narrative kept the audience thinking long after it ended.

  • antonym: simple - easy to understand or not complicated
