bistort

noun
edible plant leaves
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪstɔrt / (AmE), / ˈbɪstɔːt / (BrE)
the leaves or young shoots of the bistort plant, used as a vegetable or wild green in cooking

Examples

Bistort leaves in cooking
In spring, villagers gathered young bistort leaves to cook in stews and savory puddings.
The chef added chopped bistort to the soup for a fresh, earthy flavor.
Wild bistort is sometimes foraged and used in traditional regional dishes.
