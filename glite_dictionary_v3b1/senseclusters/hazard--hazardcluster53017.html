hazard

noun
obstacle on a golf course
8.4 6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhæzɚd / (AmE), / ˈhæzəd / (BrE)
An obstacle, such as a sand trap or water, on a golf course designed to make play more challenging.

Examples

A water hazard
A water hazard runs along the left side of the ninth fairway.
Golfers must carefully avoid each hazard on the course to keep a good score.
The ball landed in the hazard, making the shot more difficult.
