ice-cream cake

noun
cake-shaped ice cream dessert
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪs ˌkrim keɪk / (AmE), / ˈaɪs ˌkriːm keɪk / (BrE)
A dessert made by molding ice cream into the shape of a cake, often layered with cake or cookie elements and decorated like a traditional cake.

Examples

An ice-cream cake dessert
For my birthday, my friends surprised me with an ice-cream cake decorated with sprinkles.
The bakery offers several flavors of ice-cream cake, including chocolate chip and strawberry.
We ordered an ice-cream cake for the summer party because it's refreshing and fun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ice cream - a sweet frozen food made from milk products and flavorings
