ice-cream cake

noun
cake-shaped ice cream dessert
1.0 1.5
Pronunciation: /ˈaɪs.kriːm keɪk/ /; / /aɪs.ˈkriːm keɪk/ (AmE), /ˈaɪs.kriːm keɪk/ /; / /aɪs.ˈkriːm keɪk/ (BrE)
