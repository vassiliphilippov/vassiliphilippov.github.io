transferee

noun
person to whom property or rights are transferred
8.2 0.08/m
Pronunciation: / Àåtr√¶nsf…ôÀàri / (AmE), / Àåtr√¶nsf…ôÀàriÀê / (BrE)
(law) a person to whom a title, property, or right is transferred by another party.

Examples

Transferee of property
In the case of a share sale, the seller is the transferor and the buyer is the transferee.
Once the contract is executed, the transferee becomes responsible for the property.
The transferee must sign all documents to complete the ownership change.

Synonyms
