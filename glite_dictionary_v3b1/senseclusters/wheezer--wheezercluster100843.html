wheezer

noun
person or device making wheeze
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːzər / (AmE), / ˈwiːzə / (BrE)
a person or thing that makes a wheezing sound, especially because of breathing problems

Examples

A wheezer breathing hard
The doctor could hear the wheezer struggling to catch his breath during the examination.
She used a special wheezer to help her breathe more easily at night.
The old engine was a real wheezer, making loud noises every time it started.
The doctor listened carefully to the wheezer before prescribing medication.
Old engines can become real wheezers when they are about to break down.
As a child, he was known as a wheezer because of his frequent asthma attacks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: asthmatic - a person suffering from asthma, often with wheezing
  • antonym: healthy - in good health, not suffering from illness
  • synonym: inhaler - a device used to deliver medication for breathing difficulties
