imperceptibly

adverb
in an almost unnoticeable way
9.9 6.0
Pronunciation: /ɪm.pər.ˈsep.tə.bliː/ /; / /ɪm.pər.ˈsep.tɪ.bliː/ (AmE), /ɪm.pə.ˈsep.tə.bliː/ /; / /ɪm.pə.ˈsep.tɪ.bliː/ (BrE)

Examples
