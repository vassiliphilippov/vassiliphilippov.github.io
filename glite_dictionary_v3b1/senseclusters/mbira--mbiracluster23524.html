mbira

noun
African thumb piano
9.9
Pronunciation: / əmˈbiːrə / (AmE), / əmˈbiːrə / (BrE)
an African musical instrument with metal keys that are plucked with the fingers to produce sound

Examples

African mbira instrument
The musician played a beautiful melody on the mbira during the cultural festival.
She bought a handmade mbira as a souvenir from her trip to Zimbabwe.
Children gathered around to listen as their grandfather played the mbira by the fire.
