Eriobotrya japonica

noun
evergreen tree bearing the loquat fruit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛri.oʊˈbɒtri.ə dʒəˈpoʊnɪkə / (AmE), / ˌɛri.əˈbɒtrɪ.ə dʒəˈpəʊnɪkə / (BrE)
an evergreen tree native to China and Japan, cultivated for its fuzzy yellow fruit with a large stone, commonly known as the loquat
