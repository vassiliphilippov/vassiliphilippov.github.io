proprietorship

noun
sole ownership of a business
2.4
Pronunciation: / prəˈpraɪət̬ɚˌʃɪp / (AmE), / prəˈprʌɪətəʃɪp / (BrE)
An unincorporated business owned and run by one person, who is entitled to all profits and liable for all losses.

Examples

A proprietorship business
A proprietorship is simple to set up, but the owner assumes all financial risk.
He decided to run his company as a proprietorship rather than form a corporation.
Many small shops operate as a proprietorship with just one employee—the owner.

Related Terms

  • antonym: partnership - a business organization owned by two or more people
  • synonym: sole proprietorship - a business owned and operated by a single person (emphasizing one-person ownership)
