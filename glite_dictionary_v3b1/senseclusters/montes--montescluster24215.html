montes

noun
planetary mountain ranges
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɒnteɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɒntiːz / (BrE)
a term used in astronomy to refer to mountain ranges on planets or moons

Examples

Montes on planets
Scientists have discovered new montes on the surface of Jupiter's moon Io.
Olympus Montes is one of the most prominent features on Mars.
The spacecraft sent back images of icy montes on a distant moon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mountain range - a series of connected mountains
