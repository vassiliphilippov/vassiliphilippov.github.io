reach a stalemate

verb
reach a deadlock or impasse
4.3 5.0
Pronunciation: /riːtʃ ə ˈsteɪl.meɪt/ (AmE), /riːtʃ ə ˈsteɪl.meɪt/ (BrE)

Examples

Talks between management and workers reached a stalemate when neither side would compromise.
The peace negotiations reached a stalemate, leaving the conflict unresolved.
