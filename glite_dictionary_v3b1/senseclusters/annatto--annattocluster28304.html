annatto

noun
natural food coloring
7.6
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈnæt.oʊ/ (AmE), /ə.ˈnæt.əʊ/ (BrE)
a natural red-orange coloring and flavoring made from the seeds of a tropical plant, used in food and cosmetics
