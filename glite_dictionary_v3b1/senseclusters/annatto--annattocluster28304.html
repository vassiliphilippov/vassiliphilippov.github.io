annatto

noun
natural food coloring
Pronunciation: / əˈnɑːtoʊ / (AmE), / əˈnatəʊ / (BrE)
a natural red-orange coloring and flavoring made from the seeds of a tropical plant, used in food and cosmetics

Examples

Annatto coloring in cheese
The cheese gets its yellow hue from annatto.
Many traditional Latin American dishes use annatto to add both color and flavor.
Cosmetic companies sometimes use annatto as a natural dye in their products.

Related Terms

  • synonym: colorant - a substance used to give color to food or other products
  • synonym: turmeric - a yellow spice used as a coloring and flavoring in food
