stowing

noun
the act of packing or storing away
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈstoʊ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstəʊ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of packing or storing something away, especially carefully or efficiently

Examples

Careful stowing luggage
After stowing his luggage, he settled into his seat for the long flight.
Efficient stowing of equipment saves time during setup and teardown.
The stowing of emergency supplies must be done quickly before the storm arrives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: storage - the action or method of storing something for future use
  • antonym: unpacking - the act of taking things out that were packed or stored
