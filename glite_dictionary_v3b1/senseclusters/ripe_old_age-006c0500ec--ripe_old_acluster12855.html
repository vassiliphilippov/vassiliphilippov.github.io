ripe old age

noun
advanced or long life
5.4
Pronunciation: / raɪp oʊld eɪdʒ / (AmE), / raɪp əʊld eɪdʒ / (BrE)
an age that is advanced in years, often suggesting a long, healthy, or well-lived life

Examples

Live to ripe old age
He continued painting well into his ripe old age.
Her grandmother lived to the ripe old age of ninety-eight.
Many hope to remain healthy at a ripe old age.
