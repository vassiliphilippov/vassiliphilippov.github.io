rocket propellant

noun
material used to propel rockets
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːkɪt prəˈpɛlənt / (AmE), / ˈrɒkɪt prəˈpɛl(ə)nt / (BrE)
a material or substance used to provide thrust for a rocket, usually by being explosive or reactive
