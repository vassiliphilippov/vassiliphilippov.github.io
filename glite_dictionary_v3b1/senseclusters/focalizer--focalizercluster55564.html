focalizer

noun
energy-concentrating device
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊkəˌlaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈfəʊkəlaɪzə / (BrE)
a device or component that concentrates light, sound, or other energy onto a single point or small area

Examples

Laser focalizer
The technician adjusted the focalizer to ensure the laser beam hit the exact target.
A high-quality focalizer can greatly improve the efficiency of solar panels.
The new speaker system uses a focalizer to direct sound precisely toward the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: condenser - a device that focuses light or other energy
  • antonym: diffuser - a device that spreads out light or energy
