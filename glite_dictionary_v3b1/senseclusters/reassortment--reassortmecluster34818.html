reassortment

noun
new arrangement
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˈsɔrtmənt / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈsɔːtmənt / (BrE)
the act or process of arranging items, elements, or parts into a new assortment or distribution

Examples

Office reassortment
The reassortment of office furniture created a more open and collaborative workspace.
After the merger, a reassortment of responsibilities was necessary among the staff.
The annual reassortment of products keeps the store's inventory fresh and appealing to customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: redistribution - the process of distributing something again in a different way
  • synonym: reorganization - the act of arranging something in a new way
