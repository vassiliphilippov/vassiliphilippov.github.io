spirit animal

noun
animal representing a person's traits or guide
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪrɪt ˌænɪməl /; / ˈspɪrɪt ˌænəməl / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪt ˌænɪməl / (BrE)
an animal that is believed to embody a person's character, traits, or serve as their guide

Examples

A spirit animal guide
Many people choose a spirit animal that inspires them to be their best selves.
My spirit animal is the owl because I love staying up late and learning new things.
She joked that the sloth was her spirit animal on lazy Sundays.
During the retreat, each participant learned about their spirit animal and its meaning.
He jokes that his spirit animal is a sloth because he loves to nap.
Many people consider the owl to be their spirit animal because of its wisdom.
