demonstration

noun
act of showing or displaying something
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛmənˈstreɪʃən /; / ˌdɛm.ənˈstreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛmənˈstreɪʃ(ə)n /; / ˌdɛm.ənˈstreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
an act of showing or explaining how something works or is done, especially to an audience

Examples

Demonstration of a product
Before buying the machine, the customer requested a demonstration of its features.
The teacher gave a brilliant demonstration of the new software in class.
There will be a live demonstration of glassblowing at the museum today.
A cooking demonstration was held to show customers how to prepare the new dish.
The salesperson performed a demonstration of the vacuum cleaner’s features.
The teacher gave a live demonstration of the science experiment to the class.

  • synonym: exhibition - a public display of works of art or other items of interest
  • synonym: presentation - the act of giving or showing something to an audience
