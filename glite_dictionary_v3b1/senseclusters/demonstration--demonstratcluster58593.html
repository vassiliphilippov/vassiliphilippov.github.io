demonstration

noun
act of showing or displaying something
1.0 2.3
Pronunciation: /dem.ən.ˈstreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /dem.ən.ˈstreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
an act of showing or explaining how something works or is done, especially to an audience

Examples

A cooking demonstration was held to show customers how to prepare the new dish.
The salesperson performed a demonstration of the vacuum cleaner’s features.
