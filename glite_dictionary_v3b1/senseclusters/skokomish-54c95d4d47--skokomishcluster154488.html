Skokomish

proper noun
Native American reservation
Pronunciation: / skoʊˈkoʊmɪʃ / (AmE), / skəˈkəʊmɪʃ / (BrE)
the federally recognized Skokomish Indian Reservation and the community located on it in Mason County, Washington
