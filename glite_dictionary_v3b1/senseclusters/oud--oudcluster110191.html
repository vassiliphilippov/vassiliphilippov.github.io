oud

noun
fragrant wood or instrument
9.5
Pronunciation: / uːd / (AmE), / uːd / (BrE)
a fragrant resinous wood used in perfumes, or a pear-shaped Middle Eastern stringed instrument

Examples

A oud instrument
The perfume contains a hint of oud that gives it a rich, woody scent.
He learned to play the oud while studying music in Cairo.
Artisans carefully harvest oud for use in high-end fragrances and incense.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agarwood - a fragrant dark resinous wood used in incense and perfumes
  • synonym: lute - a plucked string instrument with a rounded body
