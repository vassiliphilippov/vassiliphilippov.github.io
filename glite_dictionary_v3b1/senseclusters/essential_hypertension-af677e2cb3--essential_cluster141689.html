Examples

Essential hypertension diagnosis
Doctors frequently encounter cases of essential hypertension in adults during routine check-ups.
Patients diagnosed with essential hypertension often require long-term management to control their blood pressure.
Unlike secondary hypertension, essential hypertension develops without any clear underlying cause.
