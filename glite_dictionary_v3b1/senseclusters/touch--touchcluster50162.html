touch

noun
moment of physical contact
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /tʌtʃ/ (AmE), /tʌtʃ/ (BrE)
the act, moment, or sensation of making physical contact with something or someone; the ability to feel by contact

Examples

A gentle touch
He cherished the gentle touch of his mother's hand when he was a child.
She woke at the light touch of rain against her face.
The sudden touch of cold water made her shiver.
His touch on the switch turned on the entire system.
The slightest touch of her hand made the baby smile.
With one gentle touch, the artist transformed the painting.
Cold air on his skin gave him an unpleasant touch.
She missed the gentle touch of her mother's hand.
The soft touch of velvet made her smile with delight.
