Emersonian

noun
follower of Emerson
9.9
Pronunciation: /e.mər.ˈsoʊ.nɪ.ən/ (AmE), /e.mə.ˈsəʊ.nɪ.ən/ (BrE)
a person who admires or follows the ideas and philosophy of Ralph Waldo Emerson, especially individualism and transcendentalism

Examples

An Emersonian thinker
As an unapologetic Emersonian, she trusted her own judgment over popular opinion.
The professor described himself as a modern Emersonian who values self-reliance and nature.
Many Emersonians gather annually to discuss the impact of his writings on American thought.
