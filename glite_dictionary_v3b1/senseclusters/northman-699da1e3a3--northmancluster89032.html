Northman

noun
male inhabitant of Scandinavia (historical)
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔɹθ.mən / (AmE), / ˈnɔːθ.mən / (BrE)
(historical) A male inhabitant of Scandinavia, especially during the Viking Age.
