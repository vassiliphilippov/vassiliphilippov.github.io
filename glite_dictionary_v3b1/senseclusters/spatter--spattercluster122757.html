spatter

noun
sharp, explosive noise or splashing sound
4.8 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈspæ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈspæ.tə/ (BrE)
the noise or act of liquid or small particles hitting a surface in drops or spots

Examples

A spatter of rain
A spatter of applause followed the speaker’s remarks.
She was startled by the spatter of gravel as the car sped away.
There was a sudden spatter of rain against the window.
He cleaned up the spatter of sauce that landed on the counter.
Rain left a spatter of droplets across the windshield.
There was a spatter of paint on the floor near the canvas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burst - a sudden, brief outbreak or spurt, especially of sound or activity
  • degree lower: dribble - to fall in small drops or a small stream
  • synonym: splash - an act of liquid striking a surface and scattering
  • synonym: sputter - make a series of soft explosive or spitting sounds
