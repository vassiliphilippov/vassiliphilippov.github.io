fluoro marker

noun
fluorescent highlighter pen
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈflʊəroʊ ˈmɑrkər / (AmE), / ˈflʊərəʊ ˈmɑːkə / (BrE)
A highlighter pen that fluoresces under ultraviolet (UV) light, used to mark or highlight text visibly and vividly.

Examples

A fluoro marker pen
My teacher recommends using a fluoro marker to help key points stand out in your notes.
She bought a pack of fluoro markers in different colors for her study sessions.
Under the blacklight, every line highlighted with a fluoro marker glowed brightly.
