Pacific Ocean

proper noun
the world's largest ocean
1.0
Pronunciation: / pəˈsɪfɪk ˈoʊʃən / (AmE), / pəˈsɪfɪk ˈəʊʃən / (BrE)
the largest and deepest ocean in the world, located between Asia and Australia in the west and the Americas in the east

Examples

The Pacific Ocean coast
A tsunami originating in the Pacific Ocean can affect coastlines thousands of miles away.
Many island nations, such as Fiji and Samoa, are located in the Pacific Ocean.
The Pacific Ocean covers more area than all of Earth's land combined.

  • antonym: Atlantic Ocean - the second-largest ocean, located between the Americas and Europe/Africa
