knuckle dragger

noun
insulting term for a primitive or unsophisticated person
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌkəl ˌdræɡər / (AmE), / ˈnʌkəl ˌdræɡə / (BrE)
an insulting term for a person considered slow-witted, unsophisticated, or backward in their attitudes or behavior

Examples

A knuckle dragger insult
Despite his appearance, he is far from being a knuckle dragger in conversation.
She refused to work with anyone she considered a knuckle dragger in the office.
Some critics unfairly label him a knuckle dragger because of his traditional views.

Related Terms

  • synonym: luddite - a person opposed to new technology or ways of working
  • synonym: neanderthal - a person regarded as uncultured or primitive
  • antonym: sophisticate - a person with refined tastes and manners
