gentoo

noun
penguin with white stripe
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛntu / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛntuː / (BrE)
a medium-sized penguin with a white stripe across the top of its head and a bright orange bill

Examples

Gentoo penguin
The gentoo is known for its speed in the water and distinctive white headband.
We saw a group of gentoo penguins waddling along the rocky shore in Antarctica.
A gentoo can be identified by its bright orange beak and the white stripe on its head.
