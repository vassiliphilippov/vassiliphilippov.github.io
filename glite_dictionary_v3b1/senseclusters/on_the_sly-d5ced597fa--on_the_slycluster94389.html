on the sly

adverb
secretly; furtively
Pronunciation: / ɑn ðə slaɪ / (AmE), / ɒn ðə slaɪ / (BrE)
in a secret or sneaky way, without others knowing

Examples

To act on the sly
He managed to take some snacks on the sly during the meeting.
She had been meeting her friend on the sly because her parents disapproved.
The children watched television on the sly after their bedtime.
He sometimes eats extra snacks on the sly after dinner.
She saved money on the sly for a vacation without telling anyone.
They met on the sly because their families disapproved.

  • synonym: secretly - without others knowing; in secret
