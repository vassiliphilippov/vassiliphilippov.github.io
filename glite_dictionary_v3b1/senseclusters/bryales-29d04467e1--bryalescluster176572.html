Bryales

noun
an order of mosses
Pronunciation: / braɪˈeɪliːz / (AmE), / braɪˈeɪliːz /; / braɪˈɑːliːz / (BrE)
an order of mosses characterized by a spore case separated from the capsule wall by a hollow intercellular space
