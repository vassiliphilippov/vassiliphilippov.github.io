minicam

noun
small video camera
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪniˌkæm / (AmE), / ˈmɪniˌkæm / (BrE)
a very small, portable camera used for recording or broadcasting video, often used for journalism or surveillance

Examples

With her lightweight minicam, she could easily capture events as they happened.
The documentary crew relied on a minicam for filming in tight spaces.

She used a minicam to record her experiment without disturbing the animals.
A hidden minicam captured the thief entering the office late at night.
