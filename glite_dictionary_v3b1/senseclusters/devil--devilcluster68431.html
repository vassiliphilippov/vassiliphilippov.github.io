devil

noun
mischievous or rowdy person
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈdɛv.əl / (BrE)
An informal term for a person, usually a young man, who is mischievous or rowdy.

Examples

A little devil
My nephew is a real devil, always causing trouble at family gatherings.
That devil broke my window playing football in the street.
The boys were little devils when they started that prank at school.

Related Terms

  • antonym: angel - a person who is very virtuous or well-behaved (opposite sense)
  • synonym: rascal - a mischievous or cheeky person
