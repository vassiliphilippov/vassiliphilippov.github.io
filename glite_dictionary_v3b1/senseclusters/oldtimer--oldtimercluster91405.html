oldtimer

noun
experienced person; veteran
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊldˌtaɪmər /; / ˈoʊldˌtaɪmɚ / (AmE), / ˈəʊl(d)ˌtaɪmə /; / ˈəʊldˌtaɪmə / (BrE)
