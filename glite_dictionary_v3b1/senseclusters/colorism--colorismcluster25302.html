colorism

noun
skin color prejudice
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlɚˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈkʌlərɪzəm / (BrE)
prejudice or discrimination against individuals based on the shade or tone of their skin, often within the same ethnic or racial group

Examples

Colorism in communities
Many communities struggle with colorism and its impact on social relationships.
The film addresses colorism by showing how people are treated differently based on their skin tone.
Activists are working to raise awareness about colorism and promote acceptance of all skin colors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inclusivity - the practice of including people of all backgrounds
  • synonym: prejudice - unfair dislike or preference for a person or group
  • synonym: racism - prejudice or discrimination based on race
