non-responsible

adjective
not accountable
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn rɪˈspɑnsəbəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒn rɪˈspɒnsəbəl / (BrE)
not having responsibility or not being accountable for something

Examples

Non-responsible party
The contract clearly states that he is a non-responsible party in the agreement.
As a non-responsible employee, she was not held liable for the error.
The company designated him as non-responsible for the financial decisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irresponsible - not showing a proper sense of responsibility
  • antonym: responsible - having a duty to deal with something or being accountable
