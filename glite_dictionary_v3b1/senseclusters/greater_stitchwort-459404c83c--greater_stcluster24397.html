greater stitchwort

noun
a flowering plant with white star-shaped flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪtər ˈstɪtʃˌwɝt / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtə ˈstɪtʃˌwɜːt / (BrE)
a low-growing plant of the pink family found in Europe and temperate Asia, with slender stems and small white star-shaped flowers (Stellaria holostea)
