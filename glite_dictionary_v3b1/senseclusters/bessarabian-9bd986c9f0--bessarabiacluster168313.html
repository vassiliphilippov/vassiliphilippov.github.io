Bessarabian

noun
person from Bessarabia
Pronunciation: / ˌbɛsəˈreɪbiən / (AmE), / ˌbɛsəˈreɪbiən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Bessarabia, a historical region in Eastern Europe

Examples

A Bessarabian person
The museum featured artifacts donated by a proud Bessarabian who had immigrated decades ago.
Many Bessarabians celebrate their cultural heritage with traditional music and dance.
She learned about her Bessarabian ancestors through old family photographs and letters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Moldovan - a person from Moldova, a country that includes part of Bessarabia
