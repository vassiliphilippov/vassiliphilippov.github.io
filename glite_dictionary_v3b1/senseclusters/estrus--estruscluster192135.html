estrus

noun
period of sexual receptivity in female mammals
8.4 8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈes.trəs/ (AmE), /ˈes.trəs/ /; / /ˈiː.strəs/ (BrE)
A recurring period of sexual receptivity and fertility in many female mammals, during which they are most likely to mate.

Examples

Female estrus period

The female dog was in oestrus and attracted several males from the neighborhood.
