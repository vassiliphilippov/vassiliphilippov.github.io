fazed

adjective
disturbed or unsettled
Pronunciation: / feɪzd / (AmE), / feɪzd / (BrE)
disturbed, unsettled, or disconcerted by something unexpected or challenging.

Examples

Not fazed by noise
Despite the loud noises outside, he remained completely fazed.
She wasn't fazed by the criticism and continued with her work.
The team's captain appeared fazed after the unexpected loss.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perturbed - feeling anxious or unsettled
  • antonym: unfazed - not disturbed or disconcerted
