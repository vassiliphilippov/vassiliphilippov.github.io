agreement

noun
correspondence between results or observations
Pronunciation: / əˈɡriːmənt / (AmE), / əˈɡriːmənt / (BrE)
correspondence or consistency between different measurements, observations, or statements

Examples

Agreement between results
The experiment showed good agreement across all measurements.
The results are in agreement with earlier studies.
There was no agreement between the two sets of data.
