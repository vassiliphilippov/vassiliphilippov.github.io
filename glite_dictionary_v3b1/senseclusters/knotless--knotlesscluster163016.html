knotless

adjective
without knots
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑtləs / (AmE), / ˈnɒtləs / (BrE)
having no knots; smooth or free from tied lumps or hard spots, especially in wood or rope

Examples

Knotless wood surface
The craftsman preferred knotless wood for making fine furniture.
She admired the knotless rope because it was easier to handle and looked neat.
A knotless thread is ideal for delicate sewing projects.

Related Terms

  • antonym: knotty - full of knots; having many hard lumps or tied spots
  • synonym: smooth - having an even surface without lumps or knots
