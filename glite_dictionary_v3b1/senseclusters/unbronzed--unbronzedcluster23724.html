unbronzed

adjective
without bronze color
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbrɑːnzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbrɒnzd / (BrE)
not having a bronze color or not covered with bronze

Examples

The unbronzed skin
Despite spending hours at the beach, his skin stayed unbronzed all summer.
The statue was left unbronzed, giving it a unique and unfinished look.
She admired her friend's unbronzed complexion after months of avoiding the sun.
