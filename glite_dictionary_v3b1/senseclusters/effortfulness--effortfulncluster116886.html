effortfulness

noun
the quality of requiring deliberate effort
Pronunciation: / ˈɛfərtfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈɛfətfʊlnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of requiring deliberate effort to accomplish something

Examples

The effortfulness of work
He admired the effortfulness of her performance, recognizing how much hard work it required.
The effortfulness of learning a new language can be daunting at first.
There is a certain effortfulness involved in breaking old habits and forming new ones.

Related Terms

  • antonym: effortless - not requiring effort; achieved with ease
