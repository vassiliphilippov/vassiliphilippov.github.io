circumneutral

adjective
near-neutral pH
Pronunciation: / ˌsɜrkəmˈnuːtrəl / (AmE), / ˌsɜːkəmˈnjuːtrəl / (BrE)

A circumneutral environment helps prevent corrosion in metal pipes.
