request

noun
an act of asking for something
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈkwest/ /; / /riː.ˈkwest/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈkwest/ (BrE)
an act of asking for something to be given or done, especially politely or formally; a formal message or petition

Examples

A request form

He made a formal request for additional funding.
The waiter responded quickly to our request for more water.
She submitted an official request to access the documents.
The committee denied his request for more funding.
You must put the request in writing to the managing director.
