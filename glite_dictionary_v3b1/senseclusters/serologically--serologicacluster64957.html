serologically

adverb
by blood serum analysis
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪrəˈlɑdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˌsɪərəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the study or analysis of blood serum and immune responses

Examples

To serologically test blood
The disease was serologically identified using a blood test.
Patients were serologically screened for evidence of past infection.
The virus was serologically confirmed in several laboratory samples.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immunologically - in a way that relates to the immune system
