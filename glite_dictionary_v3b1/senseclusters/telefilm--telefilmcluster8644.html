telefilm

noun
film made for TV broadcast
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛl.i.fɪlm / (AmE), / ˈtɛl.i.fɪlm / (BrE)
A film produced specifically to be broadcast on television rather than shown in cinemas.

Examples

A telefilm on TV
Many classic mysteries have been adapted into telefilms for a wider TV audience.
She prefers to work on telefilms rather than theatrical releases.
The director's latest telefilm received high ratings when it aired last night.
