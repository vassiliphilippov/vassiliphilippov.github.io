thermohaline

adjective
temperature and salt effects
9.3
Pronunciation: /θɜːr.moʊ.ˈheɪ.laɪn/ /; / /θɜːr.moʊ.ˈheɪ.liːn/ (AmE), /θɜː.mə.ˈheɪ.laɪn/ /; / /θɜː.mə.ˈheɪ.liːn/ (BrE)
