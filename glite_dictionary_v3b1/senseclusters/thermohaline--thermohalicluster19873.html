thermohaline

adjective
temperature and salt effects
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌθɜːrmoʊˈheɪlaɪn / (AmE), / ˌθɜːməʊˈheɪlaɪn / (BrE)
relating to the combined effects of temperature and salt content on the properties or movement of water, especially in the ocean

Examples

Thermohaline circulation
Scientists study thermohaline circulation to understand how heat and salt drive ocean currents.
The thermohaline properties of seawater influence marine life and climate patterns.
Changes in thermohaline conditions can affect the distribution of nutrients in the ocean.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oceanic - relating to the ocean
  • synonym: saline - containing or relating to salt
