unsauced

adjective
without sauce
5.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɔst / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɔːst / (BrE)
not covered or served with sauce

Examples

Unsauced chicken
He ordered his chicken unsauced to enjoy the natural flavor of the meat.
The chef served the vegetables unsauced for a lighter, healthier option.
Some diners prefer their pasta unsauced to better appreciate the texture of the noodles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plain - not mixed with other substances or covered with sauce
  • antonym: sauced - covered or served with sauce
