Synonyms

  • replace
    1.8 89/m
    replace means putting a new or different item in the same place, while substitute means acting in someone's or something's place.
  • stand in for
    3.2 0.62/m
    stand in for means temporarily taking someone's role during an absence, while substitute can mean any replacement person or thing.
  • sit in for
    4.2 0.69/m
    sit in for means doing someone's duties for a short time, often attending meetings or classes, while substitute can be broader.
  • fill in
    3.3 3.06/m
    fill in means covering someone's job for a short time, usually in an informal way, while substitute is a general replacement.
