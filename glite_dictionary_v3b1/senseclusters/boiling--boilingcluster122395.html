boiling

noun
cooking in boiling liquid
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔɪ.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪ.lɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of cooking food by immersing it in a liquid that has been brought to its boiling point

Examples

Boiling pasta
Add pasta to the boiling and cook until tender but firm.
Proper boiling of vegetables preserves more nutrients than overcooking.
The boiling of eggs takes about ten minutes for a hard yolk.
