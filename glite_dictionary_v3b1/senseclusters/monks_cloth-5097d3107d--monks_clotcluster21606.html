monk's cloth

noun
heavy fabric with basket weave
Pronunciation: / mʌŋks klɑθ / (AmE), / mʌŋks klɒθ / (BrE)
a heavy cotton or linen fabric with a coarse basket weave, often used for embroidery or drapery
