sequence

noun
series of related shots in a film
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsiː.kwəns / (AmE), / ˈsiː.kwəns / (BrE)
a part of a film consisting of a series of related shots that together develop a single subject or idea

Examples

Film sequence
That dream sequence used surreal images to express the character's fears.
The car chase sequence was one of the most exciting parts of the movie.
The director filmed the entire opening sequence in one continuous shot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scene - a division of a film or play with continuous action in one place
