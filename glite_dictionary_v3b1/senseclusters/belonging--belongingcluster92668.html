belonging

noun
feeling of being accepted or connected
4.1
Pronunciation: / bəˈlɔŋɪŋ / (AmE), / bɪˈlɒŋɪŋ / (BrE)
a feeling of being happy or comfortable as part of a group or relationship; a sense of acceptance and connection

Examples

Sense of belonging
Many people search for belonging in social groups throughout their lives.
Promoting diversity in the workplace can help foster a sense of belonging among employees.
She experienced a deep belonging when she joined the community choir.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alienation - feeling of being isolated or estranged
  • synonym: inclusion - the act of including or being included
