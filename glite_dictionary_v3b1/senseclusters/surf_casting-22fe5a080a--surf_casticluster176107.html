surf casting

noun
casting a fishing line from the shore into the surf
7.3
Pronunciation: / sɝf ˈkæstɪŋ / (AmE), / sɜːf ˈkɑːstɪŋ / (BrE)
The practice of fishing by casting bait or a lure from the shore into the ocean beyond the breaking waves.

Examples

Surf casting from shore
He demonstrated his surf casting technique on the rocky coastline.
Many anglers enjoy surf casting at dawn, hoping to catch striped bass as the tide comes in.
Proper equipment is crucial for successful surf casting from sandy beaches.
