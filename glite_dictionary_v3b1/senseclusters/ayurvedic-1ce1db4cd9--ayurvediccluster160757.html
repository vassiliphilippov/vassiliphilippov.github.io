Ayurvedic

noun
Ayurveda-based remedy
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪjərˈveɪdɪk / (AmE), / ˌaɪjʊəˈveɪdɪk / (BrE)
a medicine, treatment, or product made according to the principles of Ayurveda, a traditional Indian system of medicine

Examples

An Ayurvedic remedy
She prefers to use an Ayurvedic for minor illnesses instead of modern medicine.
The shop sells a variety of Ayurvedics for common health problems.
Many people trust an Ayurvedic to help with digestion and stress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: herbal remedy - a medicine made from plants
  • antonym: homeopathic - a product or treatment based on homeopathy
