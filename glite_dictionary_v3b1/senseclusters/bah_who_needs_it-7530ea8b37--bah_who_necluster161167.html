Bah, who needs it?

interjection
expression of indifference or dismissal
6.5
Pronunciation: / bɑ hu nidz ɪt / (AmE), / bɑː huː niːdz ɪt / (BrE)
An exclamation used to express indifference or the view that something is unnecessary or unimportant.

Examples

Say Bah, who needs it?
He failed his driving test, but with a smile, he shrugged, 'Bah, who needs it?'
I missed the concert, but honestly, Bah, who needs it? I was tired anyway.
When the expensive dress sold out, she just said, 'Bah, who needs it?' and walked away.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whatever - used to indicate indifference
  • synonym: who cares? - expression indicating lack of concern or interest
