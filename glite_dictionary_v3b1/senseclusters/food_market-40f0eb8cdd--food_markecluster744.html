food market

noun
place where food and groceries are sold
1.1 0.35/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàfuÀêd Àåm…ëÀêrk…™t / (AmE), / ÀàfuÀêd Àåm…ëÀêk…™t / (BrE)
A market or store where food and groceries are sold to the public.

Examples

Local food market
After moving to the neighborhood, Sarah quickly found her favorite food market nearby.
He went to the food market to buy fresh vegetables for dinner.
The city hosts a popular food market every Saturday, featuring local produce and baked goods.

Synonyms

  • grocery store
    1.0 14/m
    a grocery store is a single shop selling food and household items, while a food market can be larger with multiple sellers.
  • grocery
    1.0 22/m
    a grocery is a small shop selling food and household supplies, while a food market includes more stalls or a larger shopping area.
  • food shop
    1.0 0.12/m
    a food shop sells mainly ready-to-eat or prepared foods in one shop, while a food market sells a wider range of groceries and stalls.
