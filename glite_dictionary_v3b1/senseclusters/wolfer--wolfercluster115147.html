wolfer

noun
wolf hunter or trapper
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊlfər / (AmE), / ˈwʊlfə / (BrE)
a person who hunts or traps wolves, especially for their pelts

Examples

A skilled wolfer
The experienced wolfer shared stories of tracking wolves across the snowy plains.
In the 19th century, a wolfer could earn a living by selling wolf pelts.
The wolfer checked his traps every morning before sunrise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hunter - a person who hunts wild animals
  • synonym: trapper - a person who catches animals in traps for their fur
