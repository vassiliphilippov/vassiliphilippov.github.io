snippiness

noun
curt or rude manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɪpinəs / (AmE), / ˈsnɪpinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being curt, short, or slightly rude in manner or speech

Examples

His snippiness in speech
His snippiness during the meeting surprised everyone who expected a friendly discussion.
I could sense a bit of snippiness in her reply to my question.
The teacher's snippiness made the students hesitant to ask for help.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curtness - the quality of being brief and unfriendly in speech
  • antonym: politeness - the quality of being respectful and considerate in manner or speech
