californium

noun
radioactive chemical element (Cf)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkæləˈfɔrniəm / (AmE), / ˌkælɪˈfɔːniəm / (BrE)
a radioactive chemical element (symbol Cf), produced artificially and used mainly in scientific research and as a neutron source

Examples
