Kleinian

noun
follower of Klein
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈklaɪniən / (AmE), / ˈklaɪniən / (BrE)
a person who supports or follows the psychoanalytic theories of Melanie Klein

Examples

A Kleinian researcher
As a dedicated Kleinian, he often referenced Klein's work in his research.
The debate between a Kleinian and a Freudian can be intense in psychoanalytic circles.
Many modern therapists identify as Kleinian in their approach to child psychology.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Freudian - a person who supports or follows the theories of Sigmund Freud
  • synonym: psychoanalyst - a person trained in psychoanalysis
