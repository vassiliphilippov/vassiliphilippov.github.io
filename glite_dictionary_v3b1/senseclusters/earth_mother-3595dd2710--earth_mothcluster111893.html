earth mother

noun
nurturing, nature-oriented person
4.5
Pronunciation: /ɜːrθ ˈmʌð.ər/ (AmE), /ɜːθ ˈmʌð.ə/ (BrE)
a (usually female) person who is strongly connected to nature and is caring or nurturing, often interested in gardening, natural living, or environmentalism.

Examples

Community earth mother
She is known as the earth mother of the community, always planting herbs and sharing food.
At the festival, the speaker praised the earth mother who taught sustainable gardening to families.
After the storm, neighbors turned to their local earth mother for seeds, advice, and comfort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Mother Earth - the personification of the Earth as a nurturing mother; sometimes used interchangeably in figurative speech
  • synonym: nature lover - a person who enjoys and cares for the natural environment
