oil color

noun
artist's paint made with oil and pigment
2.6
Pronunciation: / ɔɪl ˈkʌlər /; / ɔɪl ˈkʌlɚ / (AmE), / ɔɪl ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
a type of paint consisting of pigment mixed with oil, used by artists for painting

Examples

Oil colors on palette
She mixed several oil colors on her palette before starting the portrait.
The artist preferred using oil color for its rich texture and vibrant hues.
Unlike watercolors, oil color takes much longer to dry.

She prefers to work with oil colour rather than watercolour.
The artist mixed the oil colour on his palette before starting the portrait.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oil paint - paint in which pigment is suspended in oil
  • antonym: watercolor - paint in which the pigments are suspended in a water-based solution
