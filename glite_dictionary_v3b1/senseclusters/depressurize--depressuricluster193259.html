depressurize

verb
reduce air or gas pressure
5.4 5.1
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈpreʃ.ə.raɪz/ (AmE), /diː.ˈpreʃ.ə.raɪz/ (BrE)
to reduce the air or gas pressure in something, especially in a sealed space or container

Examples

To depressurize a tank
Always follow safety protocols when you depressurize a gas tank to avoid accidents.
If the cabin loses integrity, it will automatically depressurize to match the outside pressure.
The engineers had to depressurize the chamber before opening it for maintenance.
If the pilot accidentally depressurises the cabin, oxygen masks will drop automatically.
Technicians must depressurise the tank before they can safely enter for maintenance.
The crew depressurised the module to simulate conditions in outer space.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depressurize - to reduce the air pressure in a container; American spelling
  • antonym: pressurise - to increase or maintain air pressure in a container
