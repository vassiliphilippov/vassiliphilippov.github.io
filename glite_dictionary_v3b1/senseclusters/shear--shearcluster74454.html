shear

noun
machine for cutting sheet metal
Pronunciation: / ʃɪr / (AmE), / ʃɪə / (BrE)
a heavy cutting tool or machine used to cut sheet metal by passing a blade through it

Examples

Metal shear machine
Make sure the shear is sharp before attempting to cut metal plates.
The worker used a shear to cut through the thick sheet of aluminum quickly.
They purchased a new hydraulic shear for the workshop to handle larger jobs.
