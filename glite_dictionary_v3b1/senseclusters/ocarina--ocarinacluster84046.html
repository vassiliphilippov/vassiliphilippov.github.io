ocarina

noun
egg-shaped ceramic wind instrument
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊkəˈriːnə / (AmE), / ˌɒkəˈriːnə / (BrE)
An egg-shaped wind instrument made of terracotta, having a mouthpiece and finger holes.

Examples

An ocarina instrument
Children made and decorated their own ocarinas in art class.
He played a haunting melody on his ocarina as the sun set.
The delicate sound of the ocarina echoed through the garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flute - a woodwind instrument producing sound from the flow of air across an opening
