neurovascular

adjective
nerves and blood vessels
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊˈvæskjələr / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˈvæskjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to both the nerves and blood vessels

Examples

Neurovascular surgery
The surgeon specializes in neurovascular procedures to treat complex brain conditions.
A neurovascular disorder can affect both nerve function and blood flow.
Advanced imaging techniques help diagnose neurovascular diseases more accurately.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neurological - relating to the nervous system
  • synonym: vascular - relating to blood vessels
