eau de toilette

noun
lightly scented perfume
Pronunciation: / oʊ də twɑːˈlɛt / (AmE), / əʊ də twɑːˈlɛt / (BrE)
a lightly scented perfume that contains a lower concentration of aromatic substances than cologne or perfume

Examples

Light eau de toilette
He prefers eau de toilette because it has a subtler scent than regular perfume.
She sprayed a light mist of eau de toilette before leaving the house.
The gift set included a bottle of eau de toilette and a matching body lotion.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: cologne - a scented liquid with typically a lower fragrance concentration than eau de toilette
  • degree higher: perfume - a fragrant liquid typically with a higher concentration of aromatic compounds than eau de toilette
