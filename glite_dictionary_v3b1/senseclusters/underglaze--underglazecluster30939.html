underglaze

noun
pottery decoration layer
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌɡleɪz / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌɡleɪz / (BrE)
a colored decoration or the method of applying it to pottery before the final glaze and firing
