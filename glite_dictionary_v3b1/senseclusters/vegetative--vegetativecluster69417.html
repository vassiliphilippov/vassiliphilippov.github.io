(of reproduction) relating to asexual reproduction, especially in plants, involving processes such as budding, fragmentation, or runners rather than the union of gametes.

Examples

Vegetative propagation
Many gardeners use vegetative propagation to grow new plants from cuttings.
Mosses often employ vegetative means of reproduction when conditions aren't favorable for spores.
Strawberry plants can multiply through vegetative reproduction by sending out runners.
