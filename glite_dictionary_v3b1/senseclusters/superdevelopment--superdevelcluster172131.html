superdevelopment

noun
excessive development
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərdɪˈvɛləpmənt / (AmE), / ˌsuːpədɪˈvɛləpmənt / (BrE)
a state or process of development that greatly exceeds normal or expected levels

Examples

The superdevelopment of area
The superdevelopment of the coastal area resulted in the loss of natural habitats.
Concerns about superdevelopment have led to stricter city planning regulations.
Some residents opposed the superdevelopment of their quiet neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overdevelopment - development to an excessive or harmful degree
  • antonym: underdevelopment - a lack of adequate development
