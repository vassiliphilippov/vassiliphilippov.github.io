interforce

noun
joint military force
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntərˌfɔrs / (AmE), / ˈɪntəˌfɔːs / (BrE)
a combined military force or operation made up of units from different services or countries

Examples

An interforce team
The United Nations sent an interforce to help maintain peace in the conflict zone.
An interforce operation requires careful coordination between different military branches.
After the disaster, an interforce was quickly assembled to provide humanitarian aid.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alliance - a formal agreement between countries or groups to work together
  • synonym: coalition - an alliance of different groups or nations for a common purpose
  • antonym: unit - a single group or division within a military organization
