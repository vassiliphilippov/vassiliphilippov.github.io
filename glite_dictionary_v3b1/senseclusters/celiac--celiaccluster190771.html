celiac

adjective
relating to celiac disease
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːliˌæk /; / ˈsiːliæk / (AmE), / ˈsiːliˌak /; / ˈsiːliæk / (BrE)
relating to celiac (coeliac) disease, especially indicating inability to eat gluten because of the condition or describing things prescribed for it.

Examples

Celiac diet
A strict celiac regimen can help prevent health complications for those affected.
She follows a celiac diet to manage her condition.
The restaurant offers several celiac menu options for guests with gluten intolerance.
As a coeliac child, he must avoid foods containing wheat, barley, or rye.
The restaurant offers a special menu for coeliac customers to ensure their safety.
Her coeliac condition means she always checks food labels for gluten.

Related Terms

  • synonym: celiac disease - an autoimmune disorder where ingestion of gluten leads to damage in the small intestine
  • synonym: gluten-free - not containing gluten
