celiac

adjective
relating to celiac disease
4.4 5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈsiː.li.æk/ /; / /sɪ.ˈliː.æk/ (AmE), /ˈsiː.li.æk/ /; / /sɪ.ˈliː.æk/ (BrE)
relating to celiac (coeliac) disease, especially indicating inability to eat gluten because of the condition or describing things prescribed for it.

Examples
