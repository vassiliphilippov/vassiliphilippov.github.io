sugarbush

noun
South African shrub
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʊɡərˌbʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈʃʊɡəbʊʃ / (BrE)
a South African flowering shrub of the Proteaceae family, especially Protea repens, known for its large, nectar-rich flowers

Examples

Sugarbush flowers
The sugarbush is famous for its beautiful, nectar-filled blossoms that attract many birds and insects.
Gardeners often plant sugarbush to add color and wildlife to their landscapes.
During spring, the hillsides are covered with blooming sugarbush shrubs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protea - a genus of South African flowering plants in the Proteaceae family
