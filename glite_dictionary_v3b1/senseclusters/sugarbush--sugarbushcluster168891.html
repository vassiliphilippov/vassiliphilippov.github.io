sugarbush

noun

Examples

Sugarbush flowers
The sugarbush is famous for its beautiful, nectar-filled blossoms that attract many birds and insects.
Gardeners often plant sugarbush to add color and wildlife to their landscapes.
During spring, the hillsides are covered with blooming sugarbush shrubs.
