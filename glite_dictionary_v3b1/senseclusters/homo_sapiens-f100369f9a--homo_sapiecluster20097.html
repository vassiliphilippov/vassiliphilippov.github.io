Homo sapiens

noun
the species of modern humans
Pronunciation: / ˌhoʊmoʊ ˈseɪpiənz /; / hoʊmoʊ ˈseɪpiənz / (AmE), / ˌhəʊməʊ ˈseɪpiənz /; / həʊməʊ ˈsapiːənz / (BrE)
the species of modern humans, known for advanced cognitive abilities, language, and upright posture
