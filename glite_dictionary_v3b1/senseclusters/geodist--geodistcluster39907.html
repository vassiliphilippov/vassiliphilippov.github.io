geodist

noun
geographic distance measure
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːoʊˌdɪst / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːəʊˌdɪst / (BrE)
a measure of the distance between two points on the earth's surface, often calculated using geographic coordinates

Examples

A geodist value

Mapping software can quickly calculate the geodist from your location to any destination.
Researchers used the geodist to analyze travel patterns between cities.
