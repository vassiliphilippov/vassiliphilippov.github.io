cootie

noun
imaginary germ (children)
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːti / (AmE), / ˈkuːti / (BrE)
an imaginary germ or louse, often mentioned by children during play to tease or avoid others

Examples

Catch cooties playing
The children ran away, shouting that he had cooties after he touched the ball.
She laughed and pretended to avoid her friend because of imaginary cooties.
In the playground, kids sometimes tease each other about having cooties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: germ - a very small living thing that can cause disease
  • synonym: louse - a small insect that lives on the skin of people or animals
