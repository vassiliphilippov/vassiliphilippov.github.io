woot

noun
joyful exclamation
9.9
Pronunciation: / wuːt / (AmE), / wuːt / (BrE)
an exclamation or shout expressing joy, excitement, or approval, especially in online communication

Examples

A loud woot
When she saw her exam results, she let out a loud woot in the hallway.
The crowd erupted with a collective woot as the band took the stage.
He typed woot in the chat after winning the online game.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boo - an exclamation showing disapproval or disappointment
  • synonym: yay - an exclamation of happiness or excitement
