roasted

adjective
publicly mocked or ridiculed
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊstɪd / (AmE), / ˈrəʊstɪd / (BrE)
publicly ridiculed or humorously mocked, especially in a formal roast, by comedians, or on social media.

Examples

Roasted by comedians
She was roasted on social media after posting the controversial video.
At the comedy night, the celebrity was lovingly roasted by old friends and comic rivals.
After his disastrous presentation the team roasted him with jokes during the coffee break.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mock - to make fun of someone or something in a cruel or humorous way
  • antonym: praise - express warm approval or admiration; opposite of ridiculing
  • synonym: ridicule - to subject someone to contemptuous or dismissive language or behavior
