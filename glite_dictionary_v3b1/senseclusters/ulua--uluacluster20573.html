ulua

noun
large tropical fish
Pronunciation: / ˈuːluːə / (AmE), / ˈuːluːə / (BrE)
a large predatory fish found in tropical waters, especially around Hawaii, known for its strength and size

Examples

Catch a giant ulua

The ulua is prized for its fighting ability and impressive size.
