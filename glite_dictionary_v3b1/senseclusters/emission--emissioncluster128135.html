Synonyms

  • emanation
    8.3 0.51/m
    an emanation is something that comes out from a source and often names the thing itself, while emission is the process of sending it out.
  • emittance
    8.2 0.01/m
    emittance is the act or measure of how well something gives off energy, while emission is a general release of gas, light, heat, or sound.
  • discharge
    4.4 4.26/m
    a discharge is material, often waste, that comes out from something, while emission describes the act or process of releasing substances or energy.
