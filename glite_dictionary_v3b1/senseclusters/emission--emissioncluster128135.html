emission

noun
act of discharging a substance
5.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɪʃ.ən /; / ɪˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˈmɪʃ.ən /; / ɪˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of releasing or sending out something (such as gas, light, heat, sound, or other substances) from a source

Excessive emission of sweat can indicate an underlying medical condition.
The patient experienced frequent emissions of mucus due to the infection.

  • synonym: ejection - the act of being thrown out or expelled
