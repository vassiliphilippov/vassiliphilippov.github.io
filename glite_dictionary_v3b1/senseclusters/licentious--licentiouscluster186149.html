licentious

adjective
sexually unrestrained; lacking moral restraint
1.0
Pronunciation: / laɪˈsɛnʃəs / (AmE), / laɪˈsɛnʃəs / (BrE)
lacking moral restraint, especially in sexual conduct; disregarding accepted rules or standards of behavior or morality.

Examples

Licentious behavior
He was dismissed for his licentious behavior at the workplace.
Some critics accused the artist of leading a licentious lifestyle.
The novel was banned in several countries for its licentious content.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immoral - not conforming to accepted standards of morality
  • antonym: virtuous - having or showing high moral standards
