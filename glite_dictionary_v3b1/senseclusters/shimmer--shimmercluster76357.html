Examples

Synonyms

  • glimmer
    3.9 1.71/m
    glimmer is a faint, unsteady light that barely shows, while shimmer is a soft, wavering glow across a surface.
  • flicker
    3.6 7.32/m
    flicker is a quick, small, unsteady flashing that often comes and goes, while shimmer is a gentle, continuous wavering light.
  • twinkle
    1.5 2.28/m
    twinkle is a small bright flash like stars or eyes, while shimmer is a soft, wavering glow that does not flash.
  • gleam
    1.9 9.49/m
    gleam is a soft but steady bright reflection from a surface, while shimmer is a wavering, flickering shine.

Related Terms
