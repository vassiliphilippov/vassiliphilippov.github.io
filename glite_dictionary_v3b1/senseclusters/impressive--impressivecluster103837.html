impressive

adjective
making a strong or vivid impression
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈprɛsɪv / (AmE), / ɪmˈprɛsɪv / (BrE)
causing admiration, awe, or respect because it is notable, skillful, or effective

Examples

Her speech was truly impressive, leaving the entire audience in awe.
The athlete's impressive performance broke several records at the tournament.
The building's architecture is impressive, combining both modern and classical elements.
