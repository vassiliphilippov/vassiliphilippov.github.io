metritis

noun
inflammation of the uterus lining
Pronunciation: / məˈtraɪtɪs / (AmE), / mɪˈtraɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the lining of the uterus, especially the endometrium

Examples

Metritis after birth
After giving birth, she developed metritis and required antibiotics for treatment.
The veterinarian diagnosed the cow with metritis after observing signs of infection.
Untreated metritis can lead to serious reproductive complications in women.
