representative

adjective
serving as a symbol or example
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (AmE), / ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (BrE)
serving as a typical example or specimen of a group, quality, or idea

Examples

Representative sample
Many researchers believe this sample is not representative of the whole population.
The bald eagle is representative of the United States.
This painting is representative of the artist's early style.
Her artwork is highly representative of the modernist movement.
The speech was not representative of the organization's values.
This survey included opinions from a representative sample of the population.

Related Terms

  • antonym: atypical - not representative of a type, group, or class
  • synonym: symbolic - serving as a symbol or emblem of something
  • synonym: typical - having the distinctive qualities of a particular type of person or thing
  • antonym: uncharacteristic - not typical of a particular person, group, or thing
