waterskiing

noun
sport of skiing on water behind a boat
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːtərˌskiːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌskiːɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport of moving across water on skis while being pulled by a motorboat

Examples

Waterskiing sport
He learned waterskiing last summer during his vacation in Florida.
She has been waterskiing since she was a teenager and competes in local contests.
The lake is perfect for outdoor activities such as waterskiing and kayaking.
