toehold

noun
small foothold for climbing
8.5 6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊˌhoʊld / (AmE), / ˈtəʊˌhəʊld / (BrE)
A small support or ledge just large enough for the toe to rest on, used especially in climbing.

Examples

Climber's toehold
She found a narrow toehold that helped her pull herself up.
The climber searched for a toehold on the sheer rock face.
Without a proper toehold, making progress up the wall was nearly impossible.
