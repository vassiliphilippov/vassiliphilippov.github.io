bastardy proceeding

noun
legal action regarding paternity of child born out of wedlock
Pronunciation: / ˈbæstɚdi proʊˈsiːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːstədi prəˈsiːdɪŋ / (BrE)
a legal action to determine the paternity of a child born out of wedlock and to arrange financial support for the child

Examples

A bastardy proceeding case
A bastardy proceeding can result in the legal father being required to pay child support.
During the bastardy proceeding, several witnesses testified about the relationship between the parties.
The court scheduled a bastardy proceeding after the mother filed a claim for child support.
