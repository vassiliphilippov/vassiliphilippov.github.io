eyelid

noun
fold of skin covering the eye
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪˌlɪd / (AmE), / ˈaɪlɪd / (BrE)
either of the two movable folds of skin that protect and cover the eye

Examples

The eyelid moves
After the surgery, her eyelid healed quicker than expected.
She gently closed her eyelid to keep out the light.
Swelling caused his eyelid to droop over his eye.
