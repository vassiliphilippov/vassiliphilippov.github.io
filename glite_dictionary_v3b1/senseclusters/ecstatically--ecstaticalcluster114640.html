ecstatically

adverb
in an ecstatic manner
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstætɪkli / (AmE), / ɪkˈstætɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner showing or feeling overwhelming happiness or joyful excitement

Examples

Laugh ecstatically
He laughed ecstatically after hearing the good news.
She smiled ecstatically as she opened the acceptance letter.
The fans cheered ecstatically when their team scored the winning goal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ecstatic - feeling or expressing overwhelming joy or excitement
