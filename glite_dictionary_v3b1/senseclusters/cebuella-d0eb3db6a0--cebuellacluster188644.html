Cebuella

proper noun
genus of pygmy marmosets
Pronunciation: / sɛˈbjuːɛlə / (AmE), / siːˈbjuːɛlə / (BrE)
A genus of small South American monkeys known as pygmy marmosets, among the smallest primates in the world.
