ballistic capsule

noun
spacecraft following a ballistic trajectory
4.0 7.3
Pronunciation: /bə.ˈlɪs.tɪk ˈkæp.səl/ (AmE), /bə.ˈlɪs.tɪk ˈkæp.səl/ /; / /bə.ˈlɪs.tɪk ˈkæp.sjuːl/ (BrE)

Examples
