lurky

adjective
secretive or suspicious
9.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààl…ùÀêki / (AmE), / Ààl…úÀêki / (BrE)
acting in a secretive, suspicious, or hidden way, as if lurking

Examples

Lurky shadows
The old alley had a lurky atmosphere that made people walk faster.
He gave me a lurky look before disappearing into the crowd.
There was something lurky about the way the cat watched from the shadows.
