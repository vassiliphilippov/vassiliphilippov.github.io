vitrine

noun
glass display case or cabinet
4.3
Pronunciation: / viˈtrin / (AmE), / ˈviːtrɪn / (BrE)
A glass case or cabinet for displaying and storing valuable or decorative objects, often found in shops, museums, or private homes.

Examples

A glass vitrine
She admired the porcelain figurines arranged neatly in the glass vitrine in the living room.
The museum's new exhibit features ancient artifacts displayed in a large vitrine at the entrance.
The rare coins were kept in a vitrine to protect them from dust and handling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cabinet - a storage cupboard or display case, usually with shelves
  • synonym: display case - a case for displaying objects
