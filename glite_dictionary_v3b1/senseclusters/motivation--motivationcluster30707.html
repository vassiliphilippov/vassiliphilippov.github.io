Examples

Student motivation
Good teachers know how to inspire motivation in their students.
His main motivation for volunteering was to help others.
She lost her motivation to exercise after the race was over.

Synonyms

  • drive
    3.4 14/m
    drive is a strong inner urge to succeed, while motivation is a general reason or willingness that makes someone act.
  • motive
    3.0 30/m
    motive is a specific reason, often hidden, that explains action, while motivation is the broader desire or encouragement to act.
  • inducement
    6.3 1/m
    inducement is an outside offer or incentive to persuade someone, while motivation is the inner reason or desire to do something.
  • impetus
    6.7 6/m
    impetus is a force or spark that starts action, often external, while motivation is the ongoing inner reason to act.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apathy - lack of motivation or interest
  • antonym: discouragement - the act of making someone less confident or willing to do something
  • synonym: drive - strong motivation to achieve something
  • synonym: incentive - something that encourages or motivates someone to take action
