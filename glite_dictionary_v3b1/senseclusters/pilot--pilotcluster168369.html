pilot

verb
to guide or operate
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪlət / (AmE), / ˈpaɪlət / (BrE)
to control or guide a vehicle, project, or process, especially as a leader or operator

Examples

To pilot a project
She piloted the new software rollout to ensure a smooth transition for the team.
The captain skillfully piloted the ship through the narrow channel during the storm.
He was chosen to pilot the experimental project because of his experience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: follow - to go after or be guided by someone else
  • synonym: lead - to guide or direct a group or activity
  • synonym: navigate - to plan and direct the course of a vehicle or process
