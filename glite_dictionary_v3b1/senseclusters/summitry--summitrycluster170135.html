summitry

noun
meetings of leaders
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌmətri / (AmE), / ˈsʌmɪtri / (BrE)
the practice or activity of holding meetings between heads of government or state, especially to discuss important issues

Examples

Modern summitry meetings
Modern summitry often brings together world leaders to address urgent global challenges.
The success of international summitry depends on the willingness of countries to cooperate.
Media coverage of summitry highlights the importance of dialogue between nations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diplomacy - the management of international relations by negotiation
  • synonym: negotiation - discussion aimed at reaching an agreement
