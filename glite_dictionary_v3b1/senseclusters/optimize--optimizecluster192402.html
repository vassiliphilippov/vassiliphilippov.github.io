optimize

verb
make as effective or efficient as possible
Pronunciation: / ˈɑptɪˌmaɪz /; / ˈɑp.tɪ.maɪz /; / ˈɑːptəˌmaɪz /; / ˈɑːptɪˌmaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɒptɪmaɪz /; / ˈɒp.tɪ.maɪz / (BrE)
to make something as effective, efficient, or useful as possible; to improve something to its best possible state

Examples

To optimize performance
The company hired a consultant to optimize their production processes.
We are constantly working to optimize our website for faster loading speeds.
You need to optimize your workflow to save time and resources.
Engineers are working to optimize the design for greater fuel efficiency.
She optimized the storage system to save both time and money.
We need to optimize our website to load faster for users.
By adjusting the engine settings, the mechanic was able to optimize the car's performance.
The software helps companies optimize their resource allocation and reduce costs.
We need to optimize our workflow to meet project deadlines more efficiently.
