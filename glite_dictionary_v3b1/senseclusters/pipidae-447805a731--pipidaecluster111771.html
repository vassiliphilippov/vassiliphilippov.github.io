Pipidae

noun
family of tongueless aquatic frogs
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪpɪˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɪpɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of aquatic frogs that lack tongues, found mainly in sub-Saharan Africa and South America
