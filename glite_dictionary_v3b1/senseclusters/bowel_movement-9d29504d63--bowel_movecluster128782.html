bowel movement

noun
the discharge of waste from the body
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊəl ˌmuːvmənt / (AmE), / ˈbaʊəl ˌmuːvmənt / (BrE)
the act of passing waste matter from the bowel out of the body

Examples

Regular bowel movements
Regular bowel movements are a sign of good digestive health.
She became concerned after three days without a bowel movement.
The doctor asked if he had a bowel movement that day.
