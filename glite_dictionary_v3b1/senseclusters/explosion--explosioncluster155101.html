explosion

noun
golf shot from a sand bunker
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsploʊʒən / (AmE), / ɪkˈspləʊʒən / (BrE)
in golf, a shot played from a sand bunker that lifts sand along with the ball, typically used to escape from the hazard.

Examples

Explosion shot in golf
She practiced her explosion shots to improve her ability to get out of sand traps.
The crowd cheered when he played a perfect explosion out of the bunker.
The golfer used an explosion to loft the ball onto the green from the deep bunker.
