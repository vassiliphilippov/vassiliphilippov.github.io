bio-break

noun
short informal break for biological needs
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪ.oʊˌbreɪk / (AmE), / ˈbaɪ.əʊˌbreɪk / (BrE)
an informal short break taken for personal or biological needs, especially to use the restroom or attend to bodily functions

Examples

Take a bio-break
After an hour into the meeting, they paused for a bio-break.
Can we schedule a bio-break between the two presentations?
The event included regular bio-breaks to keep attendees comfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comfort break - a break to attend to personal physical needs
  • synonym: restroom break - a short break to use the restroom
