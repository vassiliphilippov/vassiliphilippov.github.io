corroded

adjective
damaged by chemical action
4.4
Pronunciation: / kəˈroʊdɪd / (AmE), / kəˈrəʊdɪd / (BrE)
damaged or worn away by chemical action, especially by acid or oxidation

Examples

The corroded metal
The battery terminals were badly corroded, making it difficult to start the car.
The metal pipes were severely corroded after years of exposure to salty air.
We discovered that the bridge supports had become corroded and needed urgent repair.
