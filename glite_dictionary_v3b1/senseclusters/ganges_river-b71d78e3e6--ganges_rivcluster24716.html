Ganges River

proper noun
major river in India; sacred in Hinduism
Pronunciation: / ˈɡænˌdʒiz ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈɡæn.dʒiːz ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
A major river in northern India and Bangladesh, originating in the Himalayas and flowing east into the Bay of Bengal; considered sacred by Hindus.
