A line on a map or diagram connecting points that have the same value of a particular quantity, such as temperature or pressure.

Examples

An isometric line on map
On the weather map, each isometric line indicates areas experiencing the same atmospheric pressure.
Scientists analyze isometric lines to understand gradients and spatial distribution in physical geography.
The contour map used isometric lines to display regions with equal elevation above sea level.
