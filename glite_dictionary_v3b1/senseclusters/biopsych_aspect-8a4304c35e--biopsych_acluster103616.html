biopsych aspect

noun
biological and psychological interrelation
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊˈsaɪk ˈæs.pɛkt / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪ.əˈsʌɪk ˈæs.pɛkt / (BrE)
