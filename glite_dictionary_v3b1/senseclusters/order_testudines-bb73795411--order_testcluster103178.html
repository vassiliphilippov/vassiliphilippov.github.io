order Testudines

noun
order of tortoises and turtles
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər tɛˈstjuːdɪniːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə tɛˈstjuːdɪniːz / (BrE)
A taxonomic order of reptiles that includes all tortoises and turtles.

Examples

Order Testudines reptiles
All living turtles and tortoises are classified within the order Testudines.
Paleontologists study fossils from the order Testudines to learn more about their early evolution.
The order Testudines is distinguished by the presence of a bony or cartilaginous shell.
