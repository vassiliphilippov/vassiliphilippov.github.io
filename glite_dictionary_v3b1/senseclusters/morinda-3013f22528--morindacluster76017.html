Morinda

proper noun
tropical plant genus
9.9
Pronunciation: / məˈrɪndə / (AmE), / məˈrɪndə / (BrE)
a genus of tropical plants, some species of which are used in traditional medicine

Examples

The Morinda plant

Researchers are studying the health benefits of Morinda extracts in modern medicine.
The fruit of Morinda citrifolia is sometimes called noni.
