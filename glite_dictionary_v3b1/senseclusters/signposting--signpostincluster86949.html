signposting

noun
directional signage
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪnˌpoʊstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪnpəʊstɪŋ / (BrE)
the system or use of signs and markers that provide directions or information in public places, roads, or buildings

Examples

Road signposting
Clear signposting in the museum helped visitors find each exhibit easily.
The lack of signposting on the hiking trail made it difficult to stay on the correct path.
Airport signposting was updated to assist international travelers with finding their gates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: navigation - the act of planning and following a route
  • synonym: wayfinding - the process or activity of navigating or finding one's way in a space
