fathomless

adjective
immeasurably deep
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfæðəmləs / (AmE), / ˈfæðəmləs / (BrE)
so deep or great that it cannot be measured or fully understood

Examples

The fathomless ocean
The diver gazed into the fathomless blue of the ocean below.
She felt a fathomless sadness that she could not explain to anyone.
The mystery of space remains fathomless to scientists and dreamers alike.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bottomless - so deep as to have no apparent bottom
  • antonym: shallow - not deep; having little depth
