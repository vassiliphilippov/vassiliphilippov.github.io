gussie

verb
to dress up showily
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌsi / (AmE), / ˈɡʌsi / (BrE)
to dress up or make oneself or something look more attractive, often in a showy or elaborate way

Examples

To gussie up clothes
He likes to gussie up his car with bright decals and shiny rims.
Before the event, they gussied up the hall with balloons and streamers.
She always gussies herself up for family gatherings, wearing her best clothes and jewelry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dress up - to put on formal or special clothes
  • antonym: plain - not decorated or elaborate
  • synonym: spruce up - to make something or someone look cleaner or tidier
