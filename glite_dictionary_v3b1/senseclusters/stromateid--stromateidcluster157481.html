stromateid

noun
small marine fish of the Stromateidae family
9.9
Pronunciation: / stroʊˈmeɪtiːd / (AmE), / strɒˈmeɪtɪɪd / (BrE)
a small marine fish with a short, compressed body and weak spines, belonging to the family Stromateidae
