uncarded

adjective
not carded (fibers)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkɑrdɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɑːdɪd / (BrE)
not processed by carding, especially referring to fibers or wool that have not been cleaned or disentangled.

Examples

Uncarded wool fibers
The factory received several bags of uncarded wool from the farm.
Spinners often prefer uncarded fibers for certain traditional techniques.
Because it was uncarded, the wool contained bits of grass and seeds.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carded - processed by carding to clean and align fibers
  • synonym: raw - in a natural, unprocessed state
