focused

adjective
light rays converging to a point
2.1 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈfoʊ.kəst/ /; / /ˈfoʊ.kɪst/ (AmE), /ˈfəʊ.kəst/ /; / /ˈfəʊ.kɪst/ (BrE)

Examples
