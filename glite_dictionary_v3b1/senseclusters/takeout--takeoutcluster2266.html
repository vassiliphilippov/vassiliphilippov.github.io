takeout

adjective
food to be eaten off the premises
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪkˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈteɪkaʊt / (BrE)
(of food or drink) intended to be taken away from the place where it is bought and eaten elsewhere.

Examples

Takeout coffee cup
She picked up some takeout coffee on her way to the office.
The restaurant has a special takeout menu available for busy customers.
We ordered takeout pizza for dinner after a long day at work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: takeaway - food to be taken and eaten elsewhere; chiefly British term
