takeout

adjective
food to be eaten off the premises

Examples

Takeout coffee cup

Synonyms

  • take-away
    2.3 0.03/m
    take-away is the common British word for food prepared to eat off the premises, while takeout is the American equivalent.
  • grab and go
    3.7 0.06/m
    grab and go describes ready-to-eat items sold for quick immediate use, while takeout refers to food bought to eat elsewhere more generally.
