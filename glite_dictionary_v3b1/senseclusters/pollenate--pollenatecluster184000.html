pollenate

verb
fertilize by transferring pollen
4.9 4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑləˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɒləˌneɪt / (BrE)
to fertilize a plant by transferring pollen from the male part to the female part

Examples

To pollenate plants
Farmers sometimes use brushes to pollenate plants by hand in greenhouses.
If there are no insects, the wind may still pollenate certain types of crops.
Many bees help to pollenate flowers in the garden each spring.
