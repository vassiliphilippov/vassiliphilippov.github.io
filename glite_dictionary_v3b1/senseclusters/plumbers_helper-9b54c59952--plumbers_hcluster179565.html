plumber's helper

noun
tool for clearing clogged drains (plunger)
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈplʌmɚz ˈhɛlpɚ / (AmE), / ˈplʌməz ˈhɛlpə / (BrE)
a tool with a rubber suction cup attached to a stick, used to clear blockages from drains and pipes
