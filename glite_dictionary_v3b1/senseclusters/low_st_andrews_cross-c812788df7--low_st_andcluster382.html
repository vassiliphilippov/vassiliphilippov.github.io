low St Andrew's cross

noun
low shrubby plant with cross-shaped yellow flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / loʊ ˌsɛnt ˈændruːz krɑs / (AmE), / ləʊ ˌsənt ˈændruːz krɒs / (BrE)
A low, shrubby plant native to Bermuda, the southeastern United States, the West Indies, and eastern Mexico, with yellow flowers that have four petals arranged in the shape of a cross.
