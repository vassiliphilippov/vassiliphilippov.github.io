amla

noun
Indian gooseberry fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːmlə / (AmE), / ˈæmlə / (BrE)
a small, sour, green fruit native to India, also known as Indian gooseberry, often used in cooking and traditional medicine

Examples

Fresh amla fruit
Many people in India eat amla raw or use it in pickles and chutneys.
Drinking amla juice is popular for its health benefits.
The market sells fresh amla during the winter season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gooseberry - a small, round, tart fruit from the Ribes genus
