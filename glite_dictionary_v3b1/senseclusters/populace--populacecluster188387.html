populace

noun
the general public or people collectively
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːpjələs / (AmE), / ˈpɒpjʊləs / (BrE)
the people living in a particular country or area, considered as a whole

Examples

The populace of a country
The country's leaders must listen to the concerns of the populace.
The mayor made promises to improve the lives of the entire populace.
There was widespread discontent among the populace after the new law was introduced.
