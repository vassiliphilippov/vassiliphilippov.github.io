thin person

noun
very slim or scrawny person
Pronunciation: / θɪn ˈpɜrsən / (AmE), / θɪn ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who is unusually slim and appears scrawny or underweight

Examples

A very thin person
Despite eating a lot, he has always been a thin person.
She worried that being a thin person made her look unhealthy.
Some clothes are designed to suit a thin person better.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fat person - a person who is overweight
  • synonym: skinny person - a person who is extremely thin
