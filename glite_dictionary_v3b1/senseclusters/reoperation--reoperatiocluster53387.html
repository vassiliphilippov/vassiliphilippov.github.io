reoperation

noun
repeat surgery
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌɑːpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌɒpəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
a second or additional surgical operation performed after an earlier one, usually because of complications or incomplete results

Examples

A reoperation surgery
After the initial procedure failed, the doctor recommended a reoperation to resolve the issue.
The risk of complications increases with each reoperation performed on the same area.
Careful planning is essential before scheduling a reoperation for complex cases.
The patient required a reoperation after complications developed from the first surgery.
A reoperation of the software system was necessary to fix the persistent errors.
Engineers planned a reoperation of the equipment to ensure it met safety standards.

Related Terms

  • synonym: redo - to do something again
  • synonym: repeat - to do something again in the same way
  • synonym: revision surgery - a surgical procedure to correct or improve the outcome of a previous surgery
