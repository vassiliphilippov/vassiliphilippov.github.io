wildlife expedition

noun
journey to study or observe wildlife
1.0 3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪldˌlaɪf ˌɛkspəˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈwaɪldlʌɪf ˌɛkspɪˈdɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
a journey organized to study or observe wild animals in their natural habitat

Examples
