wildlife expedition

noun
journey to study or observe wildlife
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪldˌlaɪf ˌɛkspəˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈwaɪldlʌɪf ˌɛkspɪˈdɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
a journey organized to study or observe wild animals in their natural habitat

Examples

A wildlife expedition trip
During our wildlife expedition, we saw elephants, lions, and many unique plants.
She dreamed of leading a wildlife expedition in the remote forests of Borneo.
The team set off on a wildlife expedition to observe rare birds in the Amazon rainforest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: safari - an expedition to observe or hunt animals, especially in Africa
