division Eumycota

noun
taxonomic division: true fungi
9.6
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvɪʒən juːmaɪˈkoʊtə / (AmE), / dɪˈvɪʒən juːmaɪˈkəʊtə / (BrE)
A principal taxonomic division of true fungi, comprising eukaryotic, heterotrophic, walled organisms, as distinguished from slime molds.

Examples

division Eumycota fungi
Researchers discovered a new species belonging to division Eumycota in the forest soil.
The division Eumycota includes all of the true fungi known to science.
Unlike slime molds, members of division Eumycota have cell walls composed of chitin.

Related Terms
