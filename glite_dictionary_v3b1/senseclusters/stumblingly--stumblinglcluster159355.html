stumblingly

adverb
awkwardly or unsteadily
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌmblɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈstʌmblɪŋli / (BrE)
in an awkward or unsteady manner, as if stumbling or faltering

Examples

To speak stumblingly
He answered the difficult question stumblingly, searching for the right words.
The actor delivered his lines stumblingly during the first rehearsal.
She walked stumblingly across the rocky path in the dark.

Related Terms

  • antonym: smoothly - in an even or graceful way
