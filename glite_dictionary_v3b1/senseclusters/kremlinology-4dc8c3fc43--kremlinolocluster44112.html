Kremlinology

noun
study of Russian politics
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɛmlɪˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌkrɛmlɪˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study and analysis of the politics, policies, and inner workings of the Soviet or Russian government, especially by interpreting official statements and actions

Examples

Soviet Kremlinology
During the Cold War, many Western analysts specialized in Kremlinology to understand Soviet intentions.
Her background in Kremlinology made her a valuable advisor on Russian affairs.
The professor taught a course on Kremlinology that examined the history of Soviet leadership.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Sovietology - the study of the Soviet Union's politics and society
  • synonym: political science - the study of government and political processes
