companioning

noun
providing company
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpænjənɪŋ / (AmE), / kəmˈpænjənɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of accompanying or providing company to someone, especially to offer support or comfort

Examples

Companioning the elderly
Companioning the elderly can help reduce their feelings of loneliness.
Volunteers are trained in companioning to support patients in hospice care.
Her job involves companioning people with disabilities during community activities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accompanying - the act of going somewhere with someone
  • antonym: isolating - the act of keeping someone alone or apart
  • synonym: supporting - the act of giving help or encouragement
