planted

adjective
deeply rooted or firmly fixed

(of ideas, feelings, etc.) deeply rooted or firmly fixed so that they are difficult to change or remove.

Examples

Planted belief
His distrust of outsiders is a planted conviction that goes back to his childhood.
Such planted ideas are hard to change, even with new evidence.
There is a planted sense of duty in her that influences all her decisions.
