senate race

noun
election campaign for a senate seat
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnət reɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɛnət reɪs / (BrE)
an election campaign in which candidates compete for a seat in a senate

Examples

The senate race campaign
Close polls indicate the senate race is likely to be very competitive.
He announced his candidacy for the upcoming senate race.
The senate race in Texas attracted national attention this year.
