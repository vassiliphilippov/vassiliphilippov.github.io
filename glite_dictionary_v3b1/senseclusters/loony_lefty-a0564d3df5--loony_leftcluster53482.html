loony lefty

noun
derogatory term for extreme left-wing person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈluːni ˈlɛfti / (AmE), / ˈluːni ˈlɛfti / (BrE)
A disparaging term for someone with extreme or eccentric left-wing political views.

Examples

A loony lefty politician
He was labeled a loony lefty after proposing radical changes at the council meeting.
Online debates often devolve into name-calling, with opponents quick to use terms like loony lefty.
Some newspapers dismiss protesters as loony lefties without listening to their arguments.
