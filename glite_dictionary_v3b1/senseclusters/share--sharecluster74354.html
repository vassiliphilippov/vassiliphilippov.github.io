share

noun
portion of assets or property
Pronunciation: / ʃɛr / (AmE), / ʃeə /; / ʃɛə / (BrE)
a part or portion of something that is divided among people (such as money, property, credit, or food)

Examples

A share of money
Each partner received an equal share of the profits.
His share in the family business was transferred to his daughter.
She demanded her share of the inheritance after the will was read.
Don't worry, everyone will get their fair share of the credit.
Each team member was responsible for their share of the group assignment.
She did more than her share of the household chores last weekend.
Each child gets a share of the chocolate cake.
He felt he had done more than his fair share of the work.
She paid her share of the rent on the first of the month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: allotment - a share or portion assigned to someone
  • synonym: contribution - something given or done to help achieve a result
  • synonym: portion - a part or share of a whole
  • antonym: whole - the complete amount or entirety; antonym of share in the sense of undivided
