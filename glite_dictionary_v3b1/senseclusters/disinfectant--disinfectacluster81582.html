disinfectant

noun
substance that kills microorganisms
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛktənt / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛktənt / (BrE)
a substance or agent that destroys microorganisms to prevent infection or disease, especially on surfaces.

Examples

Disinfectant spray
Exposure to sunlight can act as a natural disinfectant for water in outdoor tanks.
The hospital uses a powerful disinfectant to keep patient rooms germ-free.
You should always clean the kitchen counters with a disinfectant to reduce the risk of illness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antiseptic - substance that prevents the growth of microorganisms, especially on living tissue
