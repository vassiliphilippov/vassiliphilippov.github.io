orneriness

noun
quality of being ornery or irritable
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrnəri.nəs / (AmE), / ˈɔːnəri.nɪs / (BrE)
a disposition to be irritable, stubborn, or difficult; mean-spirited disagreeableness or contrariness

Examples

His orneriness shows
Despite his orneriness, his friends knew he had a good heart beneath his gruff exterior.
Her orneriness made it difficult for anyone to work with her on group projects.
The dog's orneriness was evident whenever anyone tried to move him from his favorite spot.

Related Terms

  • antonym: agreeableness - the quality of being pleasant and cooperative
  • synonym: stubbornness - the quality of being unwilling to change or yield
