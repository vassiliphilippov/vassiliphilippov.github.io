reexamine

verb
look at again; examine again
3.8 3.1
Pronunciation: /riː.ɪɡ.ˈzæm.ən/ /; / /riː.ɪɡ.ˈzæm.ɪn/ (AmE), /riː.ɪɡ.ˈzæm.ɪn/ /; / /riː.ɪɡ.ˈzæm.ən/ (BrE)

The court decided to reexamine the evidence before reaching a verdict.
