impolitically

adverb
without tact
Pronunciation: / ˌɪmpəˈlɪtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɪmpəˈlɪtɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is not politically wise or tactful

Examples

Impolitically spoken words
The ambassador answered the question impolitically, causing tension between the two countries.
She acted impolitically during the meeting, ignoring the concerns of her colleagues.
Making such a statement impolitically can damage your reputation in public office.

Related Terms

  • antonym: politically - in a way that is wise or tactful in politics
  • synonym: tactlessly - in a way that lacks sensitivity or diplomacy
