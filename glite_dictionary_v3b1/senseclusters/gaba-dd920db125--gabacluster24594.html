GABA

noun
dietary supplement for relaxation/sleep
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈgɑː.bə/ /; / /ˈgæ.bə/ (AmE), /ˈgæ.bə/ (BrE)
a dietary supplement form of gamma-aminobutyric acid sold or taken to promote relaxation, reduce anxiety, or aid sleep (effectiveness may be disputed).

Examples

GABA supplement
Many people take GABA supplements before bed to help with sleep and relaxation.
She bought a bottle of GABA capsules after reading online reviews about improved sleep quality.
Doctors note that orally taken GABA may not cross the blood–brain barrier effectively.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neurotransmitter - a chemical substance that transmits nerve impulses in the nervous system
  • synonym: supplement - a product taken to add nutritional value or for a specific health purpose
