fibrocystic disease of the breast

noun
benign breast condition with cysts and fibrous tissue
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪbroʊˈsɪstɪk dɪˈziz əv ðə brɛst / (AmE), / ˌfʌɪbrə(ʊ)ˈsɪstɪk dɪˈziːz əv ðə brɛst / (BrE)
a benign condition of the breast characterized by the presence of one or more cysts and fibrous tissue, often causing lumpiness or discomfort

Related Terms

  • synonym: mastopathy - general term for diseases of the breast, especially noncancerous
