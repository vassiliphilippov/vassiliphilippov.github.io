unificationist

noun
supporter of unification
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːnɪfɪˈkeɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪfɪˈkeɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or promotes the process of uniting separate groups, organizations, or countries into one

Examples

Unificationist movement
The unificationist presented a plan to bring the two communities together under one government.
As a dedicated unificationist, she worked tirelessly to merge the rival organizations.
Many unificationists believe that cooperation leads to greater strength and prosperity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: separatist - a person who supports separation from a larger group
  • synonym: unionist - a person who supports the union of groups or countries
