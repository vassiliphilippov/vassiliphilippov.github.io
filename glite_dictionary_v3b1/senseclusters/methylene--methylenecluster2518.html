methylene

noun
bivalent chemical radical CH₂
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛθ.əˌlin / (AmE), / ˈmɛθ.ɪliːn / (BrE)
a bivalent chemical radical (CH₂) derived from methane, occurring in many organic compounds
