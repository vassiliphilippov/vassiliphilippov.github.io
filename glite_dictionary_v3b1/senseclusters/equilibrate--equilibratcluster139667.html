equilibrate

verb
bring into balance
2.8
Pronunciation: / ɪˈkwɪləˌbreɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈkwɪlɪˌbreɪt /; / ɪˈkwɪlɪbreɪt / (BrE)
to bring something into balance or a state of equilibrium, or to become balanced
