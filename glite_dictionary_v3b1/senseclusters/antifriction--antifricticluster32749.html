antifriction

adjective
reducing friction
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈfrɪkʃən / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈfrɪkʃən / (BrE)
designed to reduce or prevent friction between moving parts

Examples

Antifriction bearing
The engineer recommended using antifriction materials to extend the life of the machinery.
Modern bicycles often include antifriction bearings to make pedaling smoother.
Applying an antifriction coating can help prevent wear and tear on moving parts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: frictional - causing or relating to friction
  • synonym: lubricating - reducing friction by applying a substance
