haunter

noun
persistent worry or regret
9.3
Pronunciation: /ˈhɔːn.tər/ /; / /ˈhɑːn.tər/ (AmE), /ˈhɔːn.tə/ (BrE)
something that repeatedly causes distressing memories, thoughts, or feelings, or is a persistent source of worry or regret

For years, the loss acted as a silent haunter in his daily life.
