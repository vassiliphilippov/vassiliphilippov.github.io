haunter

noun
persistent worry or regret
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔntər / (AmE), / ˈhɔːntə / (BrE)
something that repeatedly causes distressing memories, thoughts, or feelings, or is a persistent source of worry or regret

Examples

A haunter of memories
The memory of his mistake became a lifelong haunter that he could never escape.
Guilt over the argument was a constant haunter in her thoughts.
For years, the loss acted as a silent haunter in his daily life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comfort - something that brings relief or ease
  • synonym: torment - something that causes great pain or distress
