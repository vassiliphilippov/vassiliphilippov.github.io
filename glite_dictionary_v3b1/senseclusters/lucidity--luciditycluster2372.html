lucidity

noun
clarity or clearness of mind
Pronunciation: / luˈsɪdəti / (AmE), / luˈsɪdəti /; / luːˈsɪdəti / (BrE)
the quality of being clear in thought or expression; clarity of mind or communication

Examples

Clear lucidity of thought
Moments of lucidity returned to him after several days of confusion.
She spoke with remarkable lucidity despite her illness.
The patient's sudden lucidity surprised the doctors and family.
He spoke with remarkable lucidity, even after his long illness.
The lucidity of her explanation made the complex topic easy to grasp.
The essay was praised for its structure and lucidity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confusion - the state of being unclear or bewildered
