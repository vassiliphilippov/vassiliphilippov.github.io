hurst

noun
wooded hill
Pronunciation: / hɜrst / (AmE), / hɜːst / (BrE)
a small hill or rise in the land that is covered with trees or woodland

Examples

A hurst hill
The old castle stood on a hurst surrounded by ancient oaks.
We hiked up the hurst to get a better view of the countryside.
Wildflowers covered the hurst in the spring, making it a favorite spot for locals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grove - a small group of trees
  • synonym: knoll - a small rounded hill
