year of grace

noun
any year of the Christian era
Pronunciation: / jɪr əv ɡreɪs / (AmE), / jɪə əv ɡreɪs / (BrE)
Any year since the birth of Christ, used especially in formal, religious, or historical contexts to refer to a year in the Christian calendar.

Examples

The year of grace 1920
He was born in the year of grace 1920, at the dawn of a new era.
In this year of grace, we enjoy freedoms unknown to our ancestors.
The treaty was signed in the year of grace 1648.
