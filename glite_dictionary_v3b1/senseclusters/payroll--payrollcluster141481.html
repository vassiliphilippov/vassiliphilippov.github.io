payroll

noun
total amount paid to employees
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪˌroʊl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪˌrəʊl /; / ˈpeɪ.rəʊl / (BrE)
the total amount of money that a company pays to its employees as wages and salaries

Examples

Monthly payroll cost
After the merger, the combined payroll exceeded two million dollars per month.
Many small businesses struggle to meet payroll during tough economic times.
The company's payroll increased significantly after it hired more staff.
Accurately calculating payroll is essential for any business to remain compliant with tax laws.
Due to budget cuts, the school district reduced its overall payroll by 10 percent.
The company's payroll has increased significantly over the last year.

  • synonym: wages - money paid regularly for work
