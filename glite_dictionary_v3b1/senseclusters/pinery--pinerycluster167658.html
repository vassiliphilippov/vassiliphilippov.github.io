pinery

noun
pine tree forest
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪnəri / (AmE), / ˈpaɪnəri / (BrE)
a place where pine trees grow, especially a forest or plantation of pines

Examples

A quiet pinery
The hikers set up camp in a peaceful pinery near the lake.
Birdsong echoed through the tall trees of the pinery at dawn.
Many animals make their homes in the dense pinery outside the village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grove - a small group of trees
  • synonym: pine forest - a forest consisting mainly of pine trees
