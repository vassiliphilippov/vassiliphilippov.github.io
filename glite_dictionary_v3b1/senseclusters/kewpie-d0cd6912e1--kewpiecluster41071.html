Kewpie

noun
plump baby-faced person
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːpi / (AmE), / ˈkjuːpi / (BrE)
a person, especially a child, with a plump, round, and cherubic baby-like face

Examples

A Kewpie face
Her round cheeks and big eyes made her look like a Kewpie in the family photo.
The actor was often described as a Kewpie because of his youthful, chubby face.
With her Kewpie smile, she charmed everyone at the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cherub - a person, especially a child, with an innocent, angelic face
  • antonym: waif - a person, especially a child, who is thin and looks delicate
