purchasable

adjective
able to be bought
2.8 0.08/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ùÀê.t É…ôs.…ô.b…ôl / (AmE), / Ààp…úÀê.t É…ô.s…ô.b…ôl / (BrE)
able to be bought or acquired through payment; available for purchase

Examples

Purchasable items online
Many of the artworks in the gallery are purchasable by interested collectors.
Only purchasable items can be added to your online shopping cart.
The software you are using is not purchasable anymore; it has been discontinued.

Synonyms

  • for sale
    1.0 10/m
    for sale is used on items offered now to buyers, while purchasable is a neutral adjective meaning able to be bought.
  • sellable
    3.1 0.07/m
    sellable describes something that can be sold or is marketable, while purchasable stresses that it can be bought.
  • vendable
    6.8 0.00/m
    vendable sounds formal and rare, meaning marketable, while purchasable is common and simply says something can be bought.
