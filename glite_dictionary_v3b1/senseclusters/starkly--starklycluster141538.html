starkly

adverb
in a sharply distinct or contrasting way
7.4 5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrk.li /; / ˈstɑrkli / (AmE), / ˈstɑːk.li /; / ˈstɑːkli / (BrE)
in a way that is very clear, sharp, or obvious, often without any softening or decoration

Examples
