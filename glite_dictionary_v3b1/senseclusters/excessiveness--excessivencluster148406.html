excessiveness

noun
quality of being excessive
3.8
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsɛsɪvnəs / (AmE), / ɪkˈsɛsɪvnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of exceeding what is normal, reasonable, or permitted; immoderation.

Examples

The excessiveness of sugar
A diet characterized by excessiveness in sugar can lead to health problems.
She was criticized for the excessiveness of her demands during negotiations.
The excessiveness of the spending alarmed many policymakers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excess - an amount that is more than necessary, permitted, or desirable
  • antonym: moderation - the avoidance of excess or extremes
