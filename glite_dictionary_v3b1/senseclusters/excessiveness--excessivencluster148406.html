Synonyms

    8.6 0.01/m
    immoderation is a lack of control or restraint in actions, while excessiveness describes going beyond reasonable or permitted limits.
  • excess
    4.4 5.72/m
    excess often refers to too much of something you can see or measure, while excessiveness names the general state of being immoderate.
  • inordinateness
    9.9 0.01/m

  • exorbitance
    8.8 0.00/m
    exorbitance suggests a very high or unreasonable amount such as prices, while excessiveness is a general idea of too much.
  • extravagance
    7.0 0.47/m
    extravagance shows wasteful or showy spending or behavior, while excessiveness simply means going beyond reasonable limits.
