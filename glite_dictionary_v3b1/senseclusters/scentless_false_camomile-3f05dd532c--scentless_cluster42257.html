scentless false camomile

noun
European annual weed with white flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntləs fɔls kəˈmoʊmiːl / (AmE), / ˈsɛntləs fɔːls kəˈməʊmaɪl / (BrE)
A widespread European annual weed with white flowers and finely divided leaves, often naturalized and sometimes cultivated in eastern North America; sometimes classified in genus Matricaria.

Examples

Gardeners sometimes mistake scentless false camomile for true chamomile due to its similar appearance.
