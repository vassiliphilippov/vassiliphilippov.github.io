post-paid

adjective
postage paid by sender
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstˈpeɪd / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈpeɪd / (BrE)
(of mail) having the postage paid by the sender before delivery.

Examples

Post-paid mail
All orders over $50 will be shipped post-paid to your address.
If you include a post-paid envelope, we will return your documents free of charge.
The package was clearly marked as post-paid by the sender.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prepaid - postage paid in advance
