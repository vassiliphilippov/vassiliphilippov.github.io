Arawakan

adjective
of the Arawak-speaking peoples or their languages
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈrɑːwəkən / (AmE), / əˈraʊəkən / (BrE)
relating to the peoples who speak the language of the Arawak, or to their languages

Examples

Arawakan cultures
Anthropologists study the Arawakan cultures to understand pre-Columbian history.
The Arawakan languages are widely distributed across northern South America.
The Arawakan population was profoundly impacted by European colonization.
