act like a diva

verb
behave in a demanding or dramatic way
5.4 6.5
Pronunciation: /ækt laɪk ə ˈdiː.və/ (AmE), /ækt laɪk ə ˈdiː.və/ (BrE)
to behave in an overly dramatic, demanding, or self-important manner, especially in situations that do not warrant such behavior.

Examples

To act like a diva
He started to act like a diva after being promoted, making unreasonable demands of his team.
Please don't act like a diva just because your project was praised; stay grounded.
Whenever she doesn't get her way, she tends to act like a diva in front of everyone.
