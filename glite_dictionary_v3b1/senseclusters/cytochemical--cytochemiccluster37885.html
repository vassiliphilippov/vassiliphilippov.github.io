cytochemical

adjective
cell chemistry
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪtoʊˈkɛmɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌsaɪtəʊˈkɛmɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the chemical composition or chemical processes of cells or their components

Examples

cytochemical staining technique
The cytochemical staining technique helps identify specific cell structures under the microscope.
Researchers used cytochemical methods to detect enzyme activity in tissue samples.
A cytochemical analysis can reveal important information about cellular function and disease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biochemical - relating to the chemical processes in living organisms
