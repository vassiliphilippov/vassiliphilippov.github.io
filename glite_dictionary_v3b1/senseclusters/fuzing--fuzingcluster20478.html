fuzing

verb
to set a fuze
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfjuːzɪŋ / (BrE)
to set, adjust, or configure a fuze’s timing or mode so that a device will function as intended

Examples

Proper fuzing of the device is essential for the safety of the operation.
Before deployment, the team double-checked that they had correctly fuzed each explosive.
