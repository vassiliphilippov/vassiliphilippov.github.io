bass

noun
lowest-range musical instrument in a family
Pronunciation: / beɪs / (AmE), / beɪs / (BrE)
the musical instrument with the lowest range in its family, such as the bass in a string or brass section

Examples

Bass guitar
Electric bass guitars are common in rock and jazz music.
She learned to play the bass in her school band.
The orchestra added a new bass to strengthen the lower harmonies.
