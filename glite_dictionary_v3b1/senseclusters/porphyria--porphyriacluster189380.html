porphyria

noun
genetic disorder causing abdominal pain and confusion
4.6
Pronunciation: / pɔrˈfɪri.ə / (AmE), / pɔːˈfɪəri.ə / (BrE)
a rare genetic disorder affecting the metabolism, often resulting in abdominal pain, mental confusion, and other symptoms due to buildup of certain chemicals in the body

Examples

Porphyria symptoms
Genetic testing confirmed that her symptoms were caused by porphyria.
The patient was diagnosed with porphyria after experiencing severe abdominal cramps and confusion.
Treatment for porphyria requires careful management of metabolic triggers.
