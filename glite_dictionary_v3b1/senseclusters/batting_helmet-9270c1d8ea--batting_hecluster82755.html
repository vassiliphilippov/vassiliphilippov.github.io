batting helmet

noun
protective helmet worn by a baseball batter
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbæɾɪŋ ˌhɛlmɪt / (AmE), / ˈbætɪŋ ˌhɛlmɪt / (BrE)
a protective helmet worn by a batter in baseball to safeguard the head from pitched balls
