trippin'

verb
to stumble; informal
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪpɪn / (AmE), / ˈtrɪpɪn / (BrE)
to stumble or catch one's foot while walking, causing a fall or near-fall; informal pronunciation of 'tripping'.

Examples

Trippin' on a step
She was trippin' on the loose tile and nearly dropped her coffee.
Watch your step so you don't end up trippin' over the curb.
He laughed after trippin' on his shoelaces in front of everyone.

  • synonym: stumble - to trip or lose balance while walking
