frijolillo

noun
poisonous shrub with blue flowers and red beans
9.9
Pronunciation: / fri.hoʊˈli.joʊ / (AmE), / fri.hɒˈliː.jəʊ / (BrE)
A shrub or small tree with pinnate leaves that are poisonous to livestock, bearing dense clusters of fragrant blue flowers and red beans.

Examples

Frijolillo plant
Cattle should not graze where frijolillo grows, as the plant is highly toxic to them.
Local guides warned us that the red beans of frijolillo are not safe for consumption.
The brilliant blue flowers of the frijolillo stand out in the springtime landscape.
