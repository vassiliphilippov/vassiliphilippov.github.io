bald-faced

adjective
shamelessly open; blatant
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔld ˌfeɪst / (AmE), / ˈbɔːld ˌfeɪst / (BrE)
shamelessly open, blatant, or undisguised; brazen (used of actions, statements, or people)

Examples

Bald-faced lie
He told a bald-faced lie to avoid getting into trouble.
Her bald-faced arrogance shocked everyone at the meeting.
It was a bald-faced admission of guilt in front of the entire audience.
His bald-faced lies were easily spotted by everyone.
It was a bald-faced violation of the company's rules.
She made a bald-faced attempt to take credit for the project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barefaced - openly shameless or undisguised
  • synonym: brazen - bold and without shame
  • antonym: disguised - concealed or hidden
  • synonym: shameless - without embarrassment or remorse; brazen
  • antonym: subtle - not obvious or blatant; delicate
