beef jerky

noun
seasoned, dried strips of beef
3.4
Pronunciation: / biːf ˈdʒɜrki / (AmE), / biːf ˈdʒɜːki / (BrE)
strips of beef that have been seasoned and dried to preserve them, typically eaten as a snack.

Examples

A bag of beef jerky
He bought some beef jerky for the long road trip.
Homemade beef jerky tends to be less salty than store-bought varieties.
I always keep a bag of beef jerky in my desk for a quick snack.
