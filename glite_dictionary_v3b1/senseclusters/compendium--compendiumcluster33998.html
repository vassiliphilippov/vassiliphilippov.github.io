compendium

noun
concise, comprehensive summary or collection
5.7 8.8
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpɛndiəm / (AmE), / kəmˈpɛndiəm /; / kəmˈpɛndɪəm / (BrE)
a book or resource that collects and summarizes information on a subject, often bringing together many works or facts
