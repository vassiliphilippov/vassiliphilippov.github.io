eye bank

noun
facility for storing corneas for transplant
Pronunciation: / aɪ bæŋk / (AmE), / aɪ baŋk / (BrE)
a medical facility where human corneas are stored and preserved for transplantation

Examples

The eye bank facility
After his grandmother passed away, her family donated her corneas to the local eye bank.
Doctors retrieved the donated cornea from the eye bank before performing the transplant surgery.
The hospital's eye bank plays a crucial role in restoring the sight of patients with corneal damage.

Related Terms
