modder

noun
game/software modifier
6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑː.dər/ (AmE), /ˈmɒd.ə/ (BrE)
a person who changes or customizes video games, software, or hardware, especially to add new features or improve them

Examples

A modder changes games
The popular modder added new characters and levels to the old game.
Many gamers rely on a skilled modder to fix bugs or add features.
After months of work, the modder released a graphics overhaul for the community.

Many modders share their creations online for others to download and use.
As a skilled modder, she added new levels and characters to her favorite game.
