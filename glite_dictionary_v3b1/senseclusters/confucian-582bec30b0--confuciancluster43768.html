Confucian

noun
follower of Confucius
Pronunciation: / kənˈfjuːʃən / (AmE), / kənˈfjuːʃən / (BrE)
A person who believes in or follows the teachings of Confucius.

Examples

A Confucian thinker
During the discussion, he described himself as a committed Confucian.
Many Confucians emphasize the importance of family values in their daily lives.
Modern Confucians often seek to apply ancient wisdom to contemporary social issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Confucianism - the system of ethical and philosophical teachings founded by Confucius
  • antonym: Taoist - a follower of the philosophical tradition of Taoism
