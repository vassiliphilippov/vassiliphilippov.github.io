wadding

noun
soft protective or insulating material
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɒdɪŋ / (BrE)
soft material, often cotton or wool, used to protect, cushion, or pack objects, or as a filler in garments or quilting

Examples

Soft wadding material
Old firearms often required a piece of wadding between the powder and the bullet.
She wrapped the fragile vase in wadding before packing it in the box.
The tailor used cotton wadding to line the winter coat for extra warmth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: padding - soft material used to protect or cushion
  • synonym: stuffing - material used to fill cushions or soft toys
