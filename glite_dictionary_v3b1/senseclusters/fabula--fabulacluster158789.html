fabula

noun
moral story
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfæbjələ / (AmE), / ˈfæbjʊlə / (BrE)
a story or tale, especially one that conveys a moral lesson

Examples

A moral fabula
The professor explained the structure of a fabula in narrative theory.
Each fabula in the collection teaches children an important lesson about kindness.
Writers often use a fabula to communicate values through engaging stories.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fable - a short story, typically with animals, conveying a moral
  • synonym: myth - a traditional story explaining a belief or natural phenomenon
  • synonym: parable - a simple story used to illustrate a moral or spiritual lesson
