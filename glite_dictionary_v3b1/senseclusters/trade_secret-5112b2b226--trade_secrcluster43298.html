trade secret

noun
confidential business method or formula
Pronunciation: / treɪd ˈsiːkɹət / (AmE), / treɪd ˈsiːkɹɪt / (BrE)
A confidential method, formula, or process used by a business to gain a competitive advantage over others.

Examples

A trade secret formula
Coca-Cola's formula is one of the most famous trade secrets in the world.
The lawsuit alleges that the employee stole a trade secret and sold it to a competitor.
The recipe for the sauce is a closely guarded trade secret at the company.

Related Terms

  • synonym: industrial secret - a secret concerning manufacturing techniques or processes
