sugar-forming suffix
Pronunciation: / oʊs / (AmE), / əʊz / (BrE)
a suffix used in chemistry to form the names of sugars and some related compounds

Examples

The suffix ose in sugars
The suffix ose is found at the end of many sugar names, such as lactose and sucrose.
In biochemistry, compounds ending with ose are usually carbohydrates.
