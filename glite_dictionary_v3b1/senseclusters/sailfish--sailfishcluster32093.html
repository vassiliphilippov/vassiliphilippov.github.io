sailfish

noun
large saltwater fish with a sail-like dorsal fin
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪlˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈseɪlˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a large, fast marine fish with a long, pointed bill and a tall, sail-like fin, popular with anglers as a game fish
