unaccommodating

adjective
not willing to help or oblige
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.əˈkɑː.mə.deɪ.tɪŋ /; / ˌʌn.əˈkɑː.məˌdeɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈkɒ.mə.deɪ.tɪŋ /; / ˌʌn.əˈkɒm.əˌdeɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
not willing to help or cooperate; not helpful or obliging
