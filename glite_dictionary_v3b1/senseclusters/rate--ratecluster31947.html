rate

verb
estimate or assign value or rank
2.2 2.7
Pronunciation: / reɪt / (AmE), / reɪt / (BrE)
to assign or be considered as having a particular value, quality, or rank

Examples

To rate a movie
On a scale of one to ten, how would you rate your experience with our service?
The critics rated the movie as outstanding for its originality.
The player is rated among the top five in the league.
His skills hardly rate a mention in the report.
The restaurant rates highly among food critics.
This film rates as one of the best comedies of the year.
Critics rated the film as one of the year's best.
How would you rate your experience with our customer service?
It's difficult to rate the candidates when they have such different skills.
