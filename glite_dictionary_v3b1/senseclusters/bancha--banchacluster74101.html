bancha

noun
Japanese green tea
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːntʃɑː / (AmE), / ˈbæntʃə / (BrE)
a Japanese green tea made from mature tea leaves, typically milder and less expensive than sencha

Examples

A cup of bancha
After dinner, she poured everyone a cup of bancha to help them relax.
Many Japanese families drink bancha daily because of its mild flavor and low cost.
He prefers bancha over other teas because it is less bitter and easy to drink.

Related Terms

  • synonym: green tea - tea made from unoxidized leaves, generally lighter in flavor
  • degree higher: sencha - a common Japanese green tea made from younger leaves
