Willis Tower

proper noun
skyscraper in Chicago, formerly Sears Tower
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪl.ɪs ˈtaʊ.ər/ /; / /ˈwɪl.ɪs taʊr/ (AmE), /ˈwɪl.ɪs ˈtaʊ.ə/ /; / /ˈwɪl.ɪs ˈtəʊ.ə/ (BrE)

Examples
