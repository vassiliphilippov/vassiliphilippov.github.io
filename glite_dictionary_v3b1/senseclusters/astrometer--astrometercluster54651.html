astrometer

noun
star-measuring instrument
Pronunciation: / əˈstrɑːmətər / (AmE), / əˈstrɒmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument used to measure the positions or brightness of stars

Examples

Precision astrometer
The scientist calibrated the astrometer before beginning the night's observations.
Using an astrometer, they accurately measured the brightness of several distant stars.
The new astrometer allowed astronomers to map star positions with greater precision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: photometer - an instrument for measuring the intensity of light
  • synonym: telescope - an instrument for observing distant objects, especially in astronomy
