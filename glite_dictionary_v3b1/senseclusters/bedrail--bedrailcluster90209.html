bedrail

noun
bar on bed side
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdˌreɪl / (AmE), / ˈbɛdˌreɪl / (BrE)
a bar attached to the side of a bed to prevent someone from falling out or to help them move

Examples

Children's beds often have a bedrail to keep them safe while sleeping.
