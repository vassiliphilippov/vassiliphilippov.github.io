Battle of Ypres

proper noun
World War I battles near Ypres, Belgium
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbæɾəl əv ˈiːprə / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv ˈiːprə / (BrE)
any of several significant battles during World War I near Ypres in Belgium, especially known for intense and often indecisive fighting between Allied and German forces.

Examples

The Battle of Ypres in Belgium

Many soldiers lost their lives during the Battle of Ypres in World War I.
