hunt with a jacklight at night
6.9
Pronunciation: / dʒæk / (AmE), / dʒæk / (BrE)
To hunt animals at night using a jacklight (a bright light to spot and dazzle them).

Examples

To jack deer
In the 19th century, poachers would jack deer in the woods at night.
It is illegal to jack rabbits using high-powered lamps in many states.
They were caught jacking game in the national forest last fall.
