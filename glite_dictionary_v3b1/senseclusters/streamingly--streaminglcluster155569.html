streamingly

adverb
via streaming technology
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈstriːmɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈstriːmɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that uses streaming technology to transmit, play, or deliver content continuously over the internet.

Examples

Streamingly released movie
The movie was released streamingly so viewers could watch it at home on the same day as in theaters.
Many concerts are now available streamingly for fans who cannot attend in person.
The lecture was delivered streamingly to students around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digitally - in a way that uses digital technology
  • antonym: offline - not connected to or using the internet
