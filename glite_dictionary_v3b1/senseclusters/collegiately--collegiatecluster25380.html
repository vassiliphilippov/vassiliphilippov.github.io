collegiately

adverb
in a college-related way
Pronunciation: / kəˈlidʒiətli / (AmE), / kəˈliːdʒiətli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to college or college students, especially in activities or competition

Examples

Competed collegiately in sports
The athletes competed collegiately before moving on to professional leagues.
She participated collegiately in debate tournaments during her university years.
Many musicians perform collegiately before launching their professional careers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: professionally - in a way that relates to a profession or career
