capably

adverb
in a competent manner
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪ.pə.bli / (AmE), / ˈkeɪ.pə.bli / (BrE)
In a competent and skillful manner; with ability.

Examples

To capably answer questions
He answered all the difficult questions capably during the interview.
She managed the entire project capably and delivered excellent results.
The team handled the challenging situation capably under pressure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: competently - in a competent, efficient manner
  • antonym: ineptly - in an awkward or incompetent manner
