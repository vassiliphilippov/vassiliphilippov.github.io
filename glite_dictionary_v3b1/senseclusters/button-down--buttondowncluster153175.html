button-down

adjective
shirt with collar fastened by buttons
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtənˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈbʌtənˌdaʊn / (BrE)
(of a shirt or collar) having the ends of the collar fastened down by buttons.

Examples

A button-down shirt
Classic button-down collars are a staple in men's business fashion.
He wore a crisp button-down shirt to the interview.
I prefer button-down styles because they look neat even without a tie.

Related Terms

  • synonym: button-up - (of a shirt) having buttons down the front
