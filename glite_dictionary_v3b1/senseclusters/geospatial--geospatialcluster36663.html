geospatial

adjective
relating to geographic location
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiːoʊˈspeɪʃəl / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːəʊˈspeɪʃəl / (BrE)
relating to the geographic location and characteristics of objects, data, or activities on the earth's surface

Examples

Geospatial data
The company uses geospatial data to map the spread of diseases across regions.
Advances in geospatial technology have improved disaster response and planning.
Researchers rely on geospatial analysis to study changes in land use over time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geographical - relating to the natural features or location of an area
  • synonym: spatial - relating to space or the position, area, and size of things
