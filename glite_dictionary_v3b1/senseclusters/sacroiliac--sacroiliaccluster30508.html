sacroiliac

adjective
pelvic joint area
Pronunciation: / ˌsækrəʊˈɪliæk / (AmE), / ˌsækrəʊˈɪliæk / (BrE)
relating to the joint between the sacrum and the ilium bones in the pelvis

Examples

Sacroiliac joint pain
The patient complained of pain in the sacroiliac region after the accident.
Physical therapy can help relieve sacroiliac joint discomfort.
Inflammation of the sacroiliac area is common in some types of arthritis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pelvic - relating to the pelvis
