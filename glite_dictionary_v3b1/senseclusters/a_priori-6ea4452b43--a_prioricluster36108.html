a priori

adjective, adverb
from theoretical deduction, not observation
7.2 7.8
Pronunciation: /ə praɪ.ˈɔːr.iː/ /; / /eɪ praɪ.ˈɔːr.aɪ/ (AmE), /ə praɪ.ˈɔːr.iː/ /; / /eɪ praɪ.ˈɔːr.aɪ/ (BrE)
based on reasoning or knowledge that comes from theoretical deduction, not from experience or observation (used as an adjective or adverb)

Examples

a priori knowledge
