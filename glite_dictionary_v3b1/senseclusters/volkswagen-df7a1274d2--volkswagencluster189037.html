Volkswagen

noun
German car brand
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈvoʊksˌwɑːɡən / (AmE), / ˈfɒlksˌvaːɡən / (BrE)
a car produced by the German automobile manufacturer Volkswagen, known for models like the Beetle and Golf

Examples

A Volkswagen car
She bought a used Volkswagen because it was reliable and affordable.
The classic Volkswagen Beetle is famous for its unique shape and history.
Many students drive a Volkswagen to campus every day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: car - a road vehicle with an engine, four wheels, and seats for a small number of people
