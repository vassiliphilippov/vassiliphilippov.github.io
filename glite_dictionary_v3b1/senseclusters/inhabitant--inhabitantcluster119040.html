Synonyms

  • resident
    2.0 0.4/m
    resident lives in a place as someone's long-term home, while inhabitant simply means a person or animal living there.
  • habitant
    5.4 0.01/m
    habitant names a person or animal living in a place but sounds older or regional, whereas inhabitant is the common modern word.
  • dweller
    3.2 2/m
    dweller describes someone living in a particular spot like a cave or city, while inhabitant is more general.
  • denizen
    7.9 0.9/m
    denizen often names animals, plants, or people found in a place and sounds more formal, while inhabitant is everyday and neutral.
  • occupier
    4.1 0.3/m
    occupier is someone who lives in or holds a place, often implying long-term use, while inhabitant covers any resident.
  • occupant
    3.3 3/m
    occupant uses a room, seat, or building for a time, often temporary, while inhabitant means someone living there.
