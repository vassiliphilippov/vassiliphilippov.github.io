opioid

noun
pain-relieving drug
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊpiˌɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈəʊpiˌɔɪd / (BrE)
a drug (natural or synthetic) that relieves pain by acting on opioid receptors and can be addictive
