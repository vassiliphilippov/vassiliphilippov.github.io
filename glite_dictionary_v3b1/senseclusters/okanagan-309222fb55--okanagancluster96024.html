Okanagan

proper noun
Salishan indigenous language
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊkəˈnɑːɡən / (AmE), / ˌəʊkəˈnɑːɡən / (BrE)
the Salishan language, also known as Nsyilxcən, traditionally spoken by the Okanagan people of British Columbia and Washington
