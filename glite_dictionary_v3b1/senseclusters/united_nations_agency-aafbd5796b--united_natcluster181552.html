United Nations agency

noun
organization within the United Nations
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌjuː.naɪ.t̬ɪd ˈneɪ.ʃənz ˈeɪ.dʒən.si / (AmE), / ˌjuː.nɪ.tɪd ˈneɪ.ʃənz ˈeɪ.dʒən.si / (BrE)
an organization that is part of the United Nations and is responsible for a specific area, such as health, education, or development

Examples

Several United Nations agencies
Several United Nations agencies collaborated on the humanitarian relief project.
The World Health Organization is a United Nations agency focused on international public health.
UNICEF, as a United Nations agency, works to improve children's lives around the globe.
