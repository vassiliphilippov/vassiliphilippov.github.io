anglaise

noun
eyelet cotton fabric
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɑ̃ˈɡleɪz / (AmE), / ɑ̃ˈɡleɪz / (BrE)
a white cotton fabric or embroidery featuring small patterned eyelets, often used for clothing or decoration

Examples

Anglaise cotton fabric

She chose a blouse of white anglaise for its light and airy feel.
Curtains of anglaise added a charming touch to the cottage windows.
