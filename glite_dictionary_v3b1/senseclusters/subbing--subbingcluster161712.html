subbing

noun
creating or syncing subtitles
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌbɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of creating, translating, timing, or synchronizing subtitles for video content, especially performed by fans or volunteer groups.

Examples

Subbing anime episodes
The team's weekly subbing sessions produced accurate English subtitles for the anime episodes.
She learned timing and translation techniques to improve her subbing for indie film festivals.
Volunteer subbing communities often coordinate via Discord to finish episodes quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fansubbing - the creation and distribution of unofficial subtitles for media by fans.
  • synonym: subtitling - the process of creating subtitles for film or video.
