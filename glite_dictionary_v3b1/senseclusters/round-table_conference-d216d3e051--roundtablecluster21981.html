round-table conference

noun
meeting for open discussion among equals
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊnd ˌteɪbəl ˈkɑːn.fɚ.əns / (AmE), / ˈraʊnd ˌteɪ.bəl ˈkɒn.fər.əns / (BrE)
a meeting in which participants of equal status gather to discuss and exchange views on a particular subject

Examples

Round-table conference meeting
At yesterday’s round-table conference, experts from different fields shared their views on artificial intelligence.
She was invited to participate in a round-table conference on global economic development.
The university organized a round-table conference to address environmental issues facing the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: panel discussion - a formal, organized discussion among a group of experts or panelists
