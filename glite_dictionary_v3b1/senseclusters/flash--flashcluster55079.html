flash

noun
decorative or identifying patch of color on clothing
Pronunciation: / flæʃ / (AmE), / flæʃ / (BrE)
a bright patch of color, often sewn onto clothing or equipment, used for decoration or identification, especially on uniforms

Examples

Uniform flash
All the uniforms had a green flash sewn onto the left sleeve.
Her scout group was proud of the red flash on their hats.
The captain wore a flash on his shoulder to indicate his regiment.
