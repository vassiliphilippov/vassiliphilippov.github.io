Cassiope

noun
genus of small, cold-region evergreen shrubs
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkæsiˈoʊpi / (AmE), / ˌkæsiˈəʊpiː / (BrE)
a genus of small, evergreen shrubs with moss-like leaves and nodding white or pink flowers, found in cold northern regions
