calmness

noun
state of being calm, especially under stress
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmnəs / (AmE), / ˈkɑːmnɪs /; / ˈkɑːmnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being free from agitation, excitement, or strong emotion; serenity

Examples

His calmness under pressure
His calmness in emergencies helps reassure everyone around him.
Meditation can help you develop greater calmness in your daily life.
She was admired for her calmness even during the chaotic meeting.
Despite the chaos outside, there was a sense of calmness inside the building.
He handled the stressful situation with remarkable calmness and patience.
The calmness of the early morning made it perfect for meditation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: agitation - state of being excited or disturbed
  • synonym: composure - mental calmness under stress
  • synonym: serenity - the state of being calm, peaceful, and untroubled
