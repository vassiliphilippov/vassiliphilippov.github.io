horse mackerel

noun
a European Atlantic food fish
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrs ˌmækərl /; / hɔrs ˈmækɚəl / (AmE), / ˌhɔːs ˈmæk.ər.əl /; / hɔːs ˈmækərəl / (BrE)
a type of fish found in the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans, often used as food (can refer to several related species)
