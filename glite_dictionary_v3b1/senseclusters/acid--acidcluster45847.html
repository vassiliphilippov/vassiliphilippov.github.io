acid

adjective
harsh or sharply critical (figurative)
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / ˈæsɪd / (BrE)
(of remarks or tone) harsh, bitter, or sharply critical in manner or content.

Examples

Acid comment
His acid reply silenced the room immediately.
She wrote an acid review that left the new play in ruins.
The meeting ended with a burst of acid comments from the chairman.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acerbic - (of speech or comment) sharp and forthright
  • synonym: caustic - (of remarks) severely critical or sarcastic
  • antonym: mild - (of language or tone) gentle, not harsh or critical
