male, pale and stale

adjective
lacking diversity; dominated by older white men
Pronunciation: / meɪl peɪl ənd steɪl / (AmE), / meɪl peɪl ənd steɪl / (BrE)
used to describe a group or environment that is dominated by older white men and perceived as lacking diversity or originality

Examples

Male, pale and stale leadership
Many young professionals are frustrated by the male, pale and stale leadership in their industry.
The awards panel was described as male, pale and stale, raising concerns about representation and inclusion.
The new board of directors was criticized as male, pale and stale by many advocacy groups.
