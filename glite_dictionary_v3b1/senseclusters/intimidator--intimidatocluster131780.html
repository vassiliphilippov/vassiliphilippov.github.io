intimidator

noun
person who threatens
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɪməˌdeɪtər / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who frightens or threatens others to gain control or make them do something

Examples

The intimidator threatens others
The intimidator made everyone in the office feel nervous during meetings.
After several complaints, the manager confronted the intimidator about his behavior.
No one wanted to sit near the intimidator in class because he often threatened others.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bully - a person who uses strength or power to harm or intimidate others
  • antonym: protector - a person who defends or shields others from harm
