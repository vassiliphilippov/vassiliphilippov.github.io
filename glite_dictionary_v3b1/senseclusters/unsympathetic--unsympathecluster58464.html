unsympathetic

adjective
not showing sympathy or understanding
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌsɪmpəˈθɛtɪk /; / ˌʌnsɪmpəˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌsɪmpəˈθɛtɪk /; / ˌʌnsɪmpəˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
not showing or expressing sympathy, concern, or understanding for others; lacking compassion

Examples

Unsympathetic response
Her unsympathetic attitude made the difficult situation worse for everyone involved.
The judge appeared unsympathetic, refusing to listen to the defendant's explanation.
The manager was unsympathetic to our concerns about the new policy.
He gave an unsympathetic shrug and walked away.
Her response to my problem was completely unsympathetic and cold.
The manager was criticized for his unsympathetic attitude toward employees' concerns.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sympathetic - showing sympathy or understanding
  • synonym: unkind - lacking kindness; harsh or cruel
