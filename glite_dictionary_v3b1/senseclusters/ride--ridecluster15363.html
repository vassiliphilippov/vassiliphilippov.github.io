ride

verb
travel over or through an area
3.0
Pronunciation: / raɪd / (AmE), / raɪd / (BrE)
to travel on or in a vehicle, animal, bicycle, or similar means of transport

Examples

To ride a bike

Many students ride the bus to school every morning.

Yesterday, we rode the ferry across the river to visit the museum.
He loves to ride his mare through the countryside every weekend.
She learned to ride a horse when she was ten years old.
Tourists can ride camels across the desert as part of the adventure.

  • synonym: travel - to go from one place to another, typically over some distance
  • antonym: walk - to move on foot at a regular pace
