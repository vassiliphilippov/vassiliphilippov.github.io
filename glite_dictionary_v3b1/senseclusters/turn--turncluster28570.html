turn

noun
unexpected change or development
1.0
Pronunciation: / tɝn / (AmE), / tɜːn / (BrE)
an unexpected or sudden change in a situation, event, or story

Just when you thought the hero would win, the story took another turn.
The investigation took a dark turn after new evidence was uncovered.
The plot took a surprising turn that left the audience speechless.
