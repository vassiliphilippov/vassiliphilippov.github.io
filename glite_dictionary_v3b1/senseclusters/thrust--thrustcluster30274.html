thrust

noun
force pushing something forward

Examples

Engine thrust

Synonyms

  • propulsion
    6.2 5.56/m
    propulsion is the process or force that pushes things forward, while thrust is the direct engine push in physics.
  • motive power
    5.5 0.03/m
    motive power is the energy that drives a machine, while thrust is the specific forward push from an engine or nozzle.
  • driving force
    4.1 3.27/m
    driving force is a general cause that makes something move, while thrust is the concrete mechanical push by engines.
