watch out

verb
be vigilant; take care
3.0 11/m
Pronunciation: / wÉ‘tÊƒ aÊŠt / (AmE), / wÉ’tÊƒ aÊŠt / (BrE)
to be vigilant or take care in order to avoid danger or trouble

Examples

To watch out for danger
Watch out for icy patches on the road during winter.
She told him to watch out because the stairs were slippery.
You should always watch out when crossing the street, especially at night.
