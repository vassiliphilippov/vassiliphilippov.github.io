polarimetric

adjective
measuring polarization
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊlərɪˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌpəʊlərɪˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the measurement or analysis of the polarization of light or other waves

Examples

A polarimetric analysis
The polarimetric analysis revealed new details about the planet's atmosphere.
Scientists use polarimetric techniques to study the properties of different materials.
A polarimetric sensor can detect changes in the polarization of reflected light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: polarization - the orientation of waves, especially light, in particular directions
