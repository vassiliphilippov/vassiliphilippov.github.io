unashamed

adjective
not feeling shame or embarrassment
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈʃeɪmd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈʃeɪmd / (BrE)
not feeling or showing any shame or embarrassment about one's actions or behavior.

Examples

Unashamed honesty
He was unashamed of his past mistakes and spoke openly about them.
She gave an unashamed account of her actions during the trial.
The artist's unashamed style challenged traditional standards of beauty.
