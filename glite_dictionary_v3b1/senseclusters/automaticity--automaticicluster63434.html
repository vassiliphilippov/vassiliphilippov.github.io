automaticity

noun
ability to operate automatically
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːtəˌməˈtɪsəti / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˌməˈtɪsəti / (BrE)
the quality or state of operating automatically, without manual control or conscious intervention

Examples

Automaticity of machines
The automaticity of the new coffee machine means you only need to press one button.
With practice, typing can reach a level of automaticity where you no longer think about each key.
The automaticity in the system allows it to adjust settings without human help.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automation - the use of machines to perform tasks without human intervention
  • antonym: manual control - operation by direct human input
