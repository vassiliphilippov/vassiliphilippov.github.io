Pomo

noun
a member of a Native American people of northern California
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊməʊ / (BrE)
A member of a Native American people of northern California, living along the Russian River valley and nearby Pacific coast.

Examples

Pomo people
A Pomo community event was held along the banks of the Russian River.
Many Pomo elders work to preserve their language and cultural practices.
The Pomo have a rich tradition of basket weaving that is highly regarded worldwide.
