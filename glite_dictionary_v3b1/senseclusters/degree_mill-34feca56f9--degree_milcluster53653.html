degree mill

noun
unaccredited institution awarding fake degrees
Pronunciation: / dɪˈɡri mɪl / (AmE), / dɪˈɡriː mɪl / (BrE)
an institution that awards academic degrees with little or no academic study and is often not properly accredited

Examples

A degree mill certificate
After paying a hefty fee, he received a certificate from a degree mill within a week.
Many employers are cautious of candidates holding qualifications from a degree mill.
The government cracked down on the fraudulent activities of the degree mill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diploma mill - an institution selling academic diplomas with little or no study
