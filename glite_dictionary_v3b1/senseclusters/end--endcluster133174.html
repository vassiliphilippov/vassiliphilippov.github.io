end

noun
goal or objective
Pronunciation: / ɛnd / (AmE), / ɛnd / (BrE)
an aim or purpose that a plan or action is intended to achieve

Examples

A means to an end
Every strategy was devised with a clear end in mind.
He worked tirelessly to achieve his end of reforming the education system.
They believed that the end justified the means in their aggressive campaign.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goal - an aim or desired result
  • antonym: means - the method or way of achieving something; often contrasted with 'end'
  • synonym: purpose - the reason for which something is done or created
