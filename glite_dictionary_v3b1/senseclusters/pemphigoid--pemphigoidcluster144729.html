pemphigoid

adjective
blistering skin disease
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛmfɪˌɡɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɛmfɪˌɡɔɪd / (BrE)
relating to or having the characteristics of pemphigoid disease, especially producing blisters similar to those seen in pemphigoid.

Examples

Pemphigoid rash appears
The patient developed a pemphigoid rash that required immediate medical attention.
Doctors recognized the pemphigoid blisters as a sign of an autoimmune disorder.
A pemphigoid reaction can sometimes be triggered by certain medications.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bullous - characterized by large fluid-filled blisters
