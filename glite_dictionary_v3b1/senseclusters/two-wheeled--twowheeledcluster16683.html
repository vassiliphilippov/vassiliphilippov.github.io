two-wheeled

adjective
vehicle with two wheels
4.7
Pronunciation: / tuːˈwiːld / (AmE), / tuːˈwiːld / (BrE)
(of a vehicle) having or relating to two wheels.

Examples

A two-wheeled bicycle
He rode his two-wheeled bicycle to school every morning.
Many delivery workers use a two-wheeled cart to transport boxes.
The museum displayed an ancient two-wheeled chariot from Greece.

Related Terms

  • antonym: four-wheeled - (of a vehicle) having four wheels
