swooper

noun
one who descends quickly
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈswuːpər / (AmE), / ˈswuːpə / (BrE)
a person, animal, or thing that moves quickly downward or descends suddenly, especially through the air

Examples

A bird swooper
The eagle was a skilled swooper, catching its prey with incredible speed.
As a child, he dreamed of being a superhero and acting as a fearless swooper from rooftops.
The drone acted like a swooper, descending rapidly to deliver the package.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diver - a person or animal that plunges headfirst into water or air
  • degree lower: glider - an aircraft or animal that moves smoothly through the air without engine power
