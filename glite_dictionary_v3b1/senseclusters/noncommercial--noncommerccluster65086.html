noncommercial

adjective
not intended for commercial or profit purposes
4.4 0.75/m
Pronunciation: / Àån…ënk…ôÀàm…úr É…ôl / (AmE), / Àån…ínk…ôÀàm…úÀê É…ôl / (BrE)
Not connected with or engaged in commercial activities or enterprises; not intended for profit.

Examples

A noncommercial project
He produced the film as a noncommercial project to raise awareness about climate change.
Many noncommercial radio stations rely on donations rather than advertising revenue.
The organization operates on a strictly noncommercial basis.

Synonyms

  • nonprofit
    3.3 8.33/m
    nonprofit describes organizations or activities that do not aim to make money, while noncommercial means not connected with commercial activity.
  • not-for-profit
    3.4 1.88/m
    not-for-profit names organizations set up to serve a social goal, while noncommercial simply means not for profit.
  • non-profit-making
    3.5 0.01/m
    non-profit-making describes something not run to earn profit, while noncommercial focuses on lack of commercial business aim.
  • nonbusiness
    4.5 0.00/m
    nonbusiness refers to things not related to business activity, while noncommercial stresses not being involved in commerce or profit.
