noncommercial

adjective
not intended for commercial or profit purposes
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkəˈmɜrʃəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkəˈmɜːʃəl / (BrE)
Not connected with or engaged in commercial activities or enterprises; not intended for profit.

Examples

A noncommercial project
He produced the film as a noncommercial project to raise awareness about climate change.
Many noncommercial radio stations rely on donations rather than advertising revenue.
The organization operates on a strictly noncommercial basis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: commercial - connected with or engaged in commerce or business activities
