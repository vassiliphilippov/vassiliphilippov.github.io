subliterate

noun
person with low literacy
9.5
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈlɪtərət / (AmE), / sʌbˈlɪtərət / (BrE)
a person whose reading and writing skills are below the standard level of literacy

Examples

A subliterate worker
Many subliterates struggle to fill out job application forms without help.
The program aims to help subliterates improve their basic reading and writing skills.
Libraries often offer special classes for subliterates in the community.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: illiterate - a person unable to read or write
  • degree higher: literate - a person able to read and write
