magic

noun
the art of using supernatural powers
1.0 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈmædʒ.ɪk/ (AmE), /ˈmædʒ.ɪk/ (BrE)
the power or art of using supernatural forces, or a performance using tricks that seem supernatural

Examples

Ancient magic
In many cultures, magic is performed by shamans or witches.
The ancient priest claimed to possess powerful magic that could heal the sick.
The story is about a kingdom where magic is forbidden by law.
For those who don't know the secret, the trick seems like real magic.
The magician performed an incredible piece of magic that amazed everyone in the room.
To a child, technology can be just as astonishing as magic.
