nonpolluting

adjective
environmentally safe
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.pəˈluː.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.pəˈluː.tɪŋ / (BrE)
not causing pollution or harm to the environment

Examples

Nonpolluting energy sources
Electric cars are considered nonpolluting because they do not emit harmful gases.
The company invested in nonpolluting technologies to reduce its environmental impact.
Many people prefer nonpolluting energy sources like wind and solar power.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clean - not producing pollution or harmful substances
  • antonym: polluting - causing pollution or environmental harm
