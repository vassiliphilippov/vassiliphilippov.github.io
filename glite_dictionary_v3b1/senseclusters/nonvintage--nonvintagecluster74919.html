nonvintage

adjective
not from one year
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈvɪntɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈvɪntɪdʒ / (BrE)
describing wine or spirits made from a blend of grapes or ingredients from different years, not a single harvest

Examples

Nonvintage sparkling wine
This sparkling wine is nonvintage, meaning it combines grapes from several different years.
Many champagnes are labeled as nonvintage to ensure a consistent taste every year.
The nonvintage port was created by blending wines from multiple harvests.
