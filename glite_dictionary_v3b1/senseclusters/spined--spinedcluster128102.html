spined

adjective
having a spine
Pronunciation: / spaɪnd / (AmE), / spaɪnd / (BrE)
having a spine or a raised binding edge, especially referring to books or bound volumes

Examples

Spined book

Collectors often seek out rare, leather-spined editions for their shelves.
The publisher released a special spined version of the novel for its anniversary.
