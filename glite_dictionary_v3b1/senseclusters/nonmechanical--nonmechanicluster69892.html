nonmechanical

adjective
lacking mechanical skills
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnməˈkænɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnmɪˈkænɪkəl / (BrE)
(of a person) lacking mechanical skills or aptitude; not good at understanding or working with machines or technical devices.

Examples

Nonmechanical person
Because she is nonmechanical, she prefers creative hobbies over fixing appliances.
His nonmechanical nature made assembling the furniture a frustrating experience.
The company offered special training for nonmechanical staff who struggled with the new equipment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mechanically minded - having an aptitude for understanding or working with machinery
