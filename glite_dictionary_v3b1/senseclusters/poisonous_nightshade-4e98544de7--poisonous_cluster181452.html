poisonous nightshade

noun
a highly toxic perennial plant with violet flowers and red berries
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔɪzənəs ˈnaɪtʃeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪzənəs ˈnaɪtʃeɪdʒ / (BrE)
A perennial plant of the nightshade family, having violet flowers and bright red berries, highly toxic if ingested.

Examples

Bright poisonous nightshade berries
Gardeners are advised to remove poisonous nightshade from areas where children play.
Research has examined the medicinal properties and dangers of poisonous nightshade.
The berries of poisonous nightshade can be fatal if eaten.
