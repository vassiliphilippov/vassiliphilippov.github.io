divvy up

verb
divide or share out
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪvi ʌp / (AmE), / ˈdɪvi ʌp / (BrE)
to divide or distribute something among several people; to share out

Examples

To divvy up money
After the fundraising event, the group divvied up the proceeds evenly.
Let's divvy up the bill so everyone pays their fair share.

After the project ended, they had to divvy up the remaining funds.
Let's divvy up the pizza so everyone gets two slices.
The manager divvied up the tasks among the new employees.
