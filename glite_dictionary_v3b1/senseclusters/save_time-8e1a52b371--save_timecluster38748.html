save time

verb
to use time efficiently; avoid wasting time
Pronunciation: / seɪv taɪm / (AmE), / seɪv taɪm / (BrE)
to avoid wasting time or to use time more efficiently when doing something

Examples

To save time on work
If you plan your route ahead, you can save time during your commute.
She always prepares her ingredients in advance to save time when cooking dinner.
Using a dishwasher instead of washing dishes by hand really saves time.
