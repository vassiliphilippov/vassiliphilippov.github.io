turnover rate

noun
rate of employee replacement
4.0 0.66/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt…úrÀåno äv…ôr re…™t / (AmE), / Ààt…úÀênÀå…ô äv…ô re…™t / (BrE)
the rate at which employees leave a workforce and are replaced by new employees within a specific period

Examples

Employee turnover rate
A low turnover rate often indicates satisfied employees and good management.
Human resources implemented new policies to reduce the annual turnover rate.
The company's high turnover rate made it difficult to maintain a stable team.

Synonyms

  • employee turnover
    3.3 0.18/m
    employee turnover is the common HR term for staff leaving and being replaced, while turnover rate names the measured speed used in reports.
  • turnover
    4.2 4.44/m
    turnover is a short, general word for staff leaving and can also mean business sales, while turnover rate is the specific measured rate.
  • churn
    5.1 0.25/m
    churn often refers to customers or users leaving a service or being replaced, while turnover rate refers specifically to employees leaving and being replaced.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attrition rate - the rate at which staff or employees leave a company, often measured over a specific period
