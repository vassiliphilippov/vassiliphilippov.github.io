arctic

adjective
extremely cold
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrk.tɪk / (AmE), / ˈɑːk.tɪk / (BrE)
Extremely cold; resembling the conditions of the Arctic region.

Examples

Arctic weather
After stepping outside on that arctic morning, her breath instantly turned to mist in the frigid air.
The wind blowing off the lake made the city feel arctic in the middle of January.
They huddled by the fire, escaping the arctic chill that crept through the old cabin.
