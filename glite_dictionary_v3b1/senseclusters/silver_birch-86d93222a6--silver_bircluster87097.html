silver birch

noun
European birch tree with silvery bark
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvər bɜrtʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə bɜːtʃ / (BrE)
A European birch tree (Betula pendula) with silvery white, peeling bark and drooping branches.

Examples

A silver birch tree
In the garden, a majestic silver birch stands near the pond.
The pale bark of the silver birch glowed faintly in the moonlight.
They planted a row of silver birches along the driveway for shade and beauty.
