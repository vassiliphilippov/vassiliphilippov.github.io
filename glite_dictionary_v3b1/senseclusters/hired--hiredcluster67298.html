hired

adjective
rented or chartered for exclusive use
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪɚd / (AmE), / ˈhaɪəd / (BrE)
(of a vehicle or service) hired for the exclusive temporary use of a person or group, especially for transport.

Examples

Hired car
The hired bus picked up the tour group at 8 a.m.
The company arranged for a hired coach to take employees to the event.
We traveled to the mountains in a hired van with other families.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chartered - rented or hired for exclusive use, especially a vehicle
