hock-joint

noun
joint in the hind leg of hoofed mammals
Pronunciation: / hɑk dʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / hɒk dʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
the tarsal joint on the hind leg of a hoofed mammal, corresponding to the human ankle

Examples

The hock-joint of a dog
A sprain in the hock-joint can cause a dog to limp noticeably.
Many hoofed animals rely on a strong hock-joint for jumping and running.
The veterinarian examined the horse’s hock-joint for signs of swelling or injury.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hock - the joint in the hind leg of a quadruped, especially a horse, that corresponds to the human ankle
