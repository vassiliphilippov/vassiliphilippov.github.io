cowherb

noun
a European annual plant; sometimes a weed or soapwort
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààka äÀåh…úrb / (AmE), / Ààka äÀåh…úÀêb / (BrE)
a European annual plant with pale rose-colored flowers, sometimes grown as an ornamental or found as a weed in grainfields; introduced to North America, and sometimes classified as a soapwort

Examples

The cowherb plant
Gardeners sometimes cultivate cowherb for its delicate rose-colored flowers.
Some botanists classify cowherb as a type of soapwort due to its similar properties.
The cowherb is often seen growing wild along European grainfields.
