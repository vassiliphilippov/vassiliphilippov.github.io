CCITT

noun
telecom standards body
Pronunciation: / siː siː aɪ tiː tiː / (AmE), / siː siː aɪ tiː tiː / (BrE)
an international organization that established standards for telecommunications, now succeeded by ITU-T

Examples

CCITT organization
The CCITT played a crucial role in developing early digital communication protocols.
Many fax machines were built to comply with CCITT standards.
After 1992, the responsibilities of CCITT were taken over by ITU-T.
