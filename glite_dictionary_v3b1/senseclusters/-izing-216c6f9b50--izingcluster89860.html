-izing

x
suffix for ongoing action
4.3
Pronunciation: / aɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / aɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
suffix used to form present participles or adjectives from verbs ending in -ize, indicating an ongoing action or process.

Examples

-izing workers
The company is modernizing its offices to create a more comfortable workspace.
By organizing the files, she made it easier to find important documents.
He spent the afternoon optimizing the website for faster loading times.
