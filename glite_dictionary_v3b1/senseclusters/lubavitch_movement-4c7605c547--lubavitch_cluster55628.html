Lubavitch movement

noun
Hasidic Jewish group with missionary outreach
Pronunciation: / luˈbɑːvɪtʃ muˌvmənt / (AmE), / luˈbævɪtʃ ˈmuːvmənt / (BrE)
a large Hasidic Jewish group known for its missionary outreach, hospitality, technological engagement, optimism, and strong emphasis on religious study

Examples

The Lubavitch movement outreach
Many young people are inspired by the outreach and warmth of the Lubavitch movement.
The Lubavitch movement is known for sending its emissaries to Jewish communities around the world.
The influence of the Lubavitch movement extends far beyond its Russian roots.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Chabad - another name for the Lubavitch movement, emphasizing the intellectual approach
  • degree higher: Hasidism - Jewish religious movement emphasizing mysticism and piety
