snuffle

noun
noisy nasal breathing
1.4 5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈsnʌf.əl/ (AmE), /ˈsnʌf.əl/ (BrE)
the act or sound of breathing noisily through the nose, especially when it is blocked or congested

Examples

A loud snuffle
He had a snuffle from his cold all through the meeting.
The child's constant snuffle worried his mother.
You could hear her snuffle as she tried to breathe through a blocked nose.
