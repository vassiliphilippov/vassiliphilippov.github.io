rockist

noun
advocate of rock music's superiority
1.0 8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːkɪst / (AmE), / ˈrɒkɪst / (BrE)
A person who regards rock music, especially classic rock, as superior to other genres, often with a preference for traditional musicianship and authenticity.

Examples

A rockist fan
Many critics dismissed his views as those of a typical rockist.
She was accused of being a rockist for her disdain toward modern pop albums.
The music magazine often features debates between rockists and popists.
