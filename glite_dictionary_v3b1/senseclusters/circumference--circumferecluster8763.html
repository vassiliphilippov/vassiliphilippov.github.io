circumference

noun
distance around a circle
2.7
Pronunciation: / sərˈkʌmfərəns /; / sɚˈkʌmfɚəns /; / sərˈkʌm.fɚ.əns / (AmE), / səˈkʌmfərəns /; / səˈkʌm.fər.əns / (BrE)
the distance around the edge or the boundary line of a circle or other round object

Examples

The circumference of a circle
The circumference of the circular table was measured to find how much material was needed for the edge.
The runners completed a lap around the circumference of the field.
To figure out the circumference of a circle, multiply its diameter by pi.
He paced the circumference of his property to check the fences.
The circumference of the planet makes it difficult to imagine traveling all the way around it.
The teacher asked us to measure the circumference of the circle using a piece of string.
The circumference of the circular garden was measured to install a new fence.
The wheel has a circumference of two meters, making it ideal for the machine.
You can calculate the circumference of a circle if you know its diameter.
