spray can

noun
pressurized container for spraying substances
3.6
Pronunciation: / spreɪ kæn / (AmE), / spreɪ kæn / (BrE)
A small container that holds a substance under pressure and dispenses it as a fine spray, usually by means of a propellant gas when a button or trigger is pressed.

Examples

A spray can of paint
He shook the spray can before painting the graffiti on the wall.
Please use the spray can to touch up those scratches on the car.
She bought a spray can of air freshener for the bathroom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aerosol can - a pressurized can that releases a substance as a fine spray
