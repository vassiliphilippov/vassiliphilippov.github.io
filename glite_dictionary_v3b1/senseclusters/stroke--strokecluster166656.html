stroke

noun
a sudden, chance event
6.7
Pronunciation: / stroʊk / (AmE), / strəʊk / (BrE)
a sudden event or occurrence, often happening by chance and without an apparent cause

Examples

A stroke of luck
It was only by a fortunate stroke that she caught the last train home.
That promotion was a stroke of good luck I never saw coming.
Winning the lottery was a real stroke of luck for the Smith family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accident - an unexpected event, especially one causing damage or injury
  • synonym: fortune - chance or luck as an arbitrary force affecting human affairs
