cactus

noun
succulent plant with spines
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkæktəs / (AmE), / ˈkæktəs / (BrE)
A succulent plant of the family Cactaceae, typically found in arid regions of the Americas and usually having spines instead of leaves.

Examples

A cactus plant
After a long hike, they found shade beside a large cactus in the arid valley.
She accidentally pricked her finger on a cactus while arranging her collection of succulents.
The desert landscape was dotted with tall cactus plants reaching toward the sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: succulent - a plant with thick, fleshy tissues for storing water
