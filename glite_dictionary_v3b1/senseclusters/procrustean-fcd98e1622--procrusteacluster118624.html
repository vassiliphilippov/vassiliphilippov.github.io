Procrustean

adjective
enforcing rigid conformity; arbitrary standard
Pronunciation: / proʊˈkrʌstiən / (AmE), / prəˈkrʌstɪən / (BrE)
enforcing uniformity or conformity without regard to natural variation or individuality, especially in a harsh or arbitrary way; relating to or resembling Procrustes' method of forcing victims to fit his bed by stretching or cutting them.

Examples

A Procrustean method
Critics say the new policy adopts a Procrustean approach to employee evaluation.
Her Procrustean teaching style left no room for creative solutions.
The company enforced a Procrustean dress code that allowed no personal expression.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flexible - able to change or be adapted easily
  • synonym: rigid - strict; not flexible or adaptable
