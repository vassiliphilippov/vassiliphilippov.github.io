sandboxing

noun
SEO ranking restriction
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈsænd.bɑːk.sɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈsænd.bɒk.sɪŋ/ (BrE)
a process in which a new website is temporarily restricted in search rankings or visibility by a search engine

Examples

Google sandboxing new sites
Many webmasters believe that sandboxing affects new sites for several months after launch.
The team noticed a drop in traffic and suspected sandboxing by the search engine.
SEO experts often discuss strategies to minimize the impact of sandboxing on new websites.
