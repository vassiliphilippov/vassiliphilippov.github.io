neutered

adjective
having reproductive organs removed
6.8 3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈnuː.tərd/ /; / /ˈnjuː.tərd/ (AmE), /ˈnjuː.təd/ /; / /ˈnuː.təd/ (BrE)
(of an animal) having had its reproductive organs, such as testicles or ovaries, removed so that it cannot reproduce.

Examples

Neutered pet
After the surgery, the cat was officially neutered and could no longer have kittens.
Most veterinarians recommend having your dog neutered for health and behavioral reasons.
The animal shelter only allows adoption of neutered pets to help control the population.
