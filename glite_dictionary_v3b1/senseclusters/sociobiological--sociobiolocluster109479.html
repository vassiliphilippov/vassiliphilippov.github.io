sociobiological

adjective
relating to sociobiology (biological basis of social behavior)
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊsiˌoʊbaɪəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌsəʊsiəˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or based on sociobiology, the study of the biological basis of social behavior in animals and humans

Examples

The sociobiological approach
E.O. Wilson is considered the founder of the sociobiological approach to animal behavior.
Her dissertation examined sociobiological explanations for patterns of human cooperation.
Some critics argue that sociobiological theories can be misused to justify social inequalities.
