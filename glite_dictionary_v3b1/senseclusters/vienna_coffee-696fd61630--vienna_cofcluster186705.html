Vienna coffee

noun
coffee with whipped cream
Pronunciation: / viˈɛnə ˈkɑfi / (AmE), / viˈenə ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
A type of coffee or espresso topped with whipped cream.

Examples

A Vienna coffee with cream
After dinner, she treated herself to a hot Vienna coffee.
He ordered a Vienna coffee with his breakfast at the café.
The rich whipped cream on top of a Vienna coffee makes it a decadent treat.
