damaging

adjective
causing harm to reputation or interests
7.1 3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈdæm.ɪ.dʒɪŋ/ /; / /ˈdæm.ə.dʒɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈdæm.ɪ.dʒɪŋ/ /; / /ˈdæm.ə.dʒɪŋ/ (BrE)
intended or likely to harm someone's reputation, credibility, or interests, especially in a way that is discrediting or critical without offering constructive suggestions.

Examples

Damaging comments
Her damaging remarks during the meeting threatened her colleague's reputation.
The article contained several damaging allegations that could discredit the candidate.
Unfounded accusations can have a damaging impact on a person's career.
