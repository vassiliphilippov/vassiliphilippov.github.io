untappable

adjective
impossible to tap
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtæpəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈtæpəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to tap, intercept, or access, especially referring to phone lines or resources

Examples

Untappable phone lines
The company installed untappable phone lines to protect sensitive information.
With the latest technology, their communications became completely untappable.
The resource was considered untappable due to its remote location and strong security.

Related Terms

  • synonym: secure - protected against unauthorized access or attack
  • antonym: vulnerable - easily accessed or attacked
