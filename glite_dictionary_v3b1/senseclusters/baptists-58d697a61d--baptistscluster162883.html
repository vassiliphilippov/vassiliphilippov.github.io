Baptists

noun
Protestant Christian denomination emphasizing believer's baptism
3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbæp.tɪsts/ /; / /ˈbæp.təsts/ (AmE), /ˈbæp.tɪsts/ /; / /ˈbæp.təsts/ (BrE)
