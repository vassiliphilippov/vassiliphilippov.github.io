sewing-machine stitch

noun
stitch made by a sewing machine
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊɪŋ məˈʃin stɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊɪŋ məˈʃiːn stɪtʃ / (BrE)
a stitch made by a sewing machine, often using one or more threads

Examples

A sewing-machine stitch
A sewing-machine stitch can be stronger and more uniform than a traditional hand stitch.
For added durability, use a zigzag sewing-machine stitch when working with stretch fabrics.
The seamstress adjusted the tension to create a perfect sewing-machine stitch on the new dress.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hand stitch - a sewing stitch made by hand, not by machine
