jangling

adjective
making a harsh metallic noise
8.1 5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒæŋ.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒæŋ.lɪŋ / (BrE)
making a harsh, discordant ringing noise, typically like that of metal objects striking together

Examples

Jangling metal coins
She was startled by the jangling of loose coins in the cup as the bus jerked to a stop.
The jangling keys in his pocket annoyed everyone in the quiet room.
The jangling sound of spurs echoed through the empty corridor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clanging - making a loud, resonant, metallic sound
  • degree lower: jingling - making a light, ringing sound as of small metal objects shaking together
