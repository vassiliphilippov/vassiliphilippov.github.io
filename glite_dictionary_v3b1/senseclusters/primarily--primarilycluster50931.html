Synonyms

  • mainly
    1.0 35/m
    mainly is the common everyday word for saying something is true in most cases, while primarily sounds slightly more formal.
  • chiefly
    3.0 4.37/m
    chiefly is often used in formal or older texts to mean mostly, while primarily is more common in modern general use.
  • principally
    4.0 4.88/m
    principally is a formal word used in reports or explanations, while primarily is slightly less formal and more spoken.
  • predominantly
    3.7 11/m
    predominantly suggests a strong majority or clear trend, while primarily points to the main reason or purpose.
  • mostly
    1.0 106/m
    mostly is an informal, frequent choice to say something happens in most cases, while primarily sounds a bit more formal.
  • largely
    2.4 65/m
    largely speaks about size or extent more than a precise cause, while primarily highlights the chief reason or focus.
  • for the most part
    3.2 20/m
    for the most part is a casual phrase that allows exceptions, while primarily feels firmer and more formal in writing.
  • in the main
    3.7 1.11/m
    in the main is a slightly old-fashioned phrase meaning generally, while primarily is more neutral and fits many formal contexts.
