sucka

interjection
slang insult
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌkə / (AmE), / ˈsʌkə / (BrE)
an insulting exclamation used to taunt or mock someone as foolish, weak, or contemptible

Examples

Sucka! Got you!
He shouted sucka after scoring the winning point in the basketball game.
You thought you could beat me? Not today, sucka!
She laughed and called him a sucka when he fell for her prank.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely or lacks good sense
  • synonym: loser - a person who is unsuccessful or looked down upon
