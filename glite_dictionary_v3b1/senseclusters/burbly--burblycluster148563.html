burbly

adjective
bubbling sound
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜːrbli / (AmE), / ˈbɜːbli / (BrE)
making or having a bubbling or gurgling sound, like water flowing over rocks

Examples

Burbly stream
The mountain stream sounded burbly as it wound through the forest.
She loved the burbly laughter of the children playing in the pool.
A burbly fountain added a cheerful touch to the quiet garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bubbly - making or full of bubbles; lively or effervescent
  • synonym: gurgling - making a low, bubbling sound like water flowing
