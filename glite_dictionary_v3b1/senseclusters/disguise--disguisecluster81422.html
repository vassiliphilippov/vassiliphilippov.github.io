disguise

verb
to make unrecognizable by altering appearance
3.0
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈɡaɪz / (AmE), / dɪsˈɡaɪz / (BrE)
to change someone’s or something’s appearance so that they are not recognized

Examples

To disguise your voice
A strong perfume can sometimes disguise unpleasant odors in a room.
She used makeup to disguise her identity during the investigation.
The thief tried to disguise himself with a fake beard and sunglasses.
