pride oneself

verb
feel satisfied with own qualities or achievements
Pronunciation: / praɪd wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / praɪd wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to feel satisfaction or pleasure about one's own qualities, achievements, or possessions

Examples

To pride oneself on work
As a craftsman, he prides himself in the quality of his work.
She prides herself on always arriving on time for meetings.
They prided themselves on their hospitality and attention to detail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: take pride in - to feel proud of (something or someone)
