dubious

adjective
open to doubt or suspicion
6.5 8.03/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàduÀêbi…ôs / (AmE), / ÀàdjuÀêbi…ôs / (BrE)
not certain, trustworthy, or reliable; causing or feeling doubt or suspicion

Examples

  • suspicious
    2.7 13/m
    suspicious looks like it hides wrong or illegal things, while dubious means it creates general doubt or mistrust.
  • doubtful
    3.0 2.25/m

  • uncertain

  • dubitable
