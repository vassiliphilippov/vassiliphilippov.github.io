Anomalopidae

noun
family of flashlight fishes
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˌnoʊməˈlɒpɪˌdiː /; / əˌnoʊməˈlɑːpɪˌdiː / (AmE), / əˌnɒmələʊˈpɪdiː /; / əˌnɒməˈlæpɪˌdiː / (BrE)
a family of marine fishes known as flashlight fishes, with light-producing organs beneath their eyes
