common horsetail

noun
a common species of horsetail plant
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən ˈhɔrˌsteɪl / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈhɔːsteɪl / (BrE)
a widespread species of horsetail plant (Equisetum arvense) found in Eurasia, Greenland, and North America, recognized for its jointed, reed-like stems
