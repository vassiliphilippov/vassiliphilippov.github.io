Penstemon

noun
genus of flowering plants (beardtongues)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnstɪmən /; / ˈpɛnstəˌmɑn / (AmE), / ˈpɛnstiːmən /; / ˈpɛnstɪˌmɒn / (BrE)
a genus of North American plants with showy, colorful, tubular or trumpet-shaped flowers, also known as beardtongues
