level-headed approach

noun
calm and rational attitude
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛvəl ˌhɛdɪd əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈlɛvəl ˌhɛdɪd əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
A calm and sensible way of thinking or dealing with a situation, especially when under pressure or in a crisis.

Examples

A level-headed approach to problems
A level-headed approach to solving problems can prevent unnecessary conflict.
During the negotiations, her level-headed approach helped the team reach a fair agreement.
It's important to maintain a level-headed approach when responding to criticism at work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: emotional approach - a way of dealing with something led by feelings rather than reason
  • synonym: rational approach - a logical and reasoned way of dealing with a problem
