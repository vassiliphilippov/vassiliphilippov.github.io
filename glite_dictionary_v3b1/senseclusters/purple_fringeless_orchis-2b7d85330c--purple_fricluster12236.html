purple fringeless orchis

noun
a North American orchid species with violet flowers
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːpəl ˈfrɪndʒləs ˈɔːrkɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˈfrɪndʒləs ˈɔːkɪs / (BrE)
a species of orchid native to northeastern and alpine eastern North America, closely related to purple fringed orchids, with rosy-purple or violet flowers and finely toothed leaf divisions
