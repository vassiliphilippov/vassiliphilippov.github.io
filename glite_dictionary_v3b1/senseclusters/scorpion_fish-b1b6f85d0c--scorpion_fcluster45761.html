scorpion fish

noun
marine fish with venomous spines
Pronunciation: / ˈskɔrpiən ˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈskɔːpiən ˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
A marine fish with a tapering body, an armored head, and venomous spines, found especially in warm seas.

Examples

A scorpion fish sting
Care must be taken, as the spines of a scorpion fish can deliver a painful sting.
Scuba guides warn beginners not to touch any scorpion fish they see underwater.
The diver spotted a brightly colored scorpion fish resting on the coral reef.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lionfish - a species of scorpion fish with long, showy fins and venomous spines
