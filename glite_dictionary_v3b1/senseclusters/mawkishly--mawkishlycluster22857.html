mawkishly

adverb
in an overly sentimental way
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔːkɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈmɔːkɪʃli / (BrE)
in an overly sentimental or sickly emotional manner

Examples

To feel mawkishly sad
She responded mawkishly to the movie, wiping away tears at every sad scene.
The speech ended mawkishly, with exaggerated praise and forced emotion.
The writer sentimentalized his childhood memories mawkishly throughout the article.

  • synonym: sentimentally - in a sentimental manner; showing tender emotions
