adverb

Examples

To feel mawkishly sad

The writer sentimentalized his childhood memories mawkishly throughout the article.

Synonyms

  • drippily
    9.2 0.00/m

  • mushily
    7.4 0.00/m

  • cloyingly
    8.4 0.04/m
