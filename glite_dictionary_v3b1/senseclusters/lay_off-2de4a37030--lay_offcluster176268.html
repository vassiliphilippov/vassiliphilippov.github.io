lay off

verb
dismiss from a job (economically motivated)
1.0 2.8
Pronunciation: /leɪ ɔːf/ (AmE), /leɪ ɒf/ /; / /leɪ ɔːf/ (BrE)

The company had to lay off 200 workers due to declining sales.
