ironstone

noun
durable white earthenware
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪərnˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈaɪənˌstəʊn / (BrE)
a type of hard, durable white earthenware used for making dishes and tableware, especially popular in 19th-century England
