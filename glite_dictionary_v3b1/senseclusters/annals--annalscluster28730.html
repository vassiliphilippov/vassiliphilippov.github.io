annals

noun
chronological historical records
7.4 6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.əlz / (AmE), / ˈæn.əlz / (BrE)
official records of events arranged in chronological order, especially year by year; can also refer to journals or publications that record the activities of organizations

Examples

The annals of history
According to the annals of the town, the festival has been held for over two centuries.
His name appears frequently in the annals of nineteenth-century literature.
The scientist's discoveries will go down in the annals of medical history.
Her groundbreaking discovery was featured in the annals of the Academy of Sciences.
The annals provide a detailed history of the organization's progress and activities.
The society publishes its research findings in the annals each year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chronicle - a detailed and factual written record of events in chronological order
  • synonym: history - the study or record of past events
  • synonym: journal - a periodical publication recording transactions of a learned society
