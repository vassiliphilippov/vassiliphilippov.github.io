whush

interjection
call for silence
Pronunciation: / wʊʃ / (AmE), / wʊʃ / (BrE)
an exclamation used to tell someone to be quiet or to urge silence

Examples

Whush! Be quiet
The teacher raised her finger and said whush to quiet the noisy class.
He whispered whush as they entered the silent library.
With a quick whush, she signaled everyone to stop talking during the movie.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hush - an exclamation used to urge silence
  • synonym: shush - an exclamation used to tell someone to be quiet
