mongrelize

verb
make into a mongrel by mixing breeds or types
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋɡrəˌlaɪz /; / ˈmɑŋɡrəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋɡrəˌlaɪz /; / ˈmʌŋɡrɪlaɪz / (BrE)
to make something into a mongrel, typically by crossbreeding or mixing breeds or types, often implying loss of purity

Examples

To mongrelize a breed
Critics argue that careless crossbreeding can mongrelize distinct species.
Some breeders refuse to mongrelize their purebred dogs.
The garden variety was mongrelized by mixing imported plants with local ones.
Some animal rights activists argue that to mongrelise a rare species endangers its unique traits.
The breeder refused to mongrelise the pedigree dogs by allowing indiscriminate mating.
They worried that crossbreeding might mongrelise the traditional cattle stock.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hybridize - to cross-breed different species or varieties
  • synonym: mongrelize - variant American spelling; cause to become a mongrel
  • antonym: purify - make pure or more pure, often used about breed or lineage
