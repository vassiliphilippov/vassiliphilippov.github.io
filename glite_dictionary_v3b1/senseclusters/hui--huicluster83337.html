hui

noun
Māori meeting
Pronunciation: / ˈhuːi / (AmE), / ˈhuːi / (BrE)
a meeting or gathering, especially in Māori culture, for discussion or decision-making

Examples

A Maori hui
The elders called a hui to discuss important issues facing the community.
Everyone was invited to the hui at the marae on Saturday afternoon.
Decisions about the new project were made during the hui last week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assembly - a group of people gathered together for a common purpose
  • synonym: conference - a formal meeting for discussion
