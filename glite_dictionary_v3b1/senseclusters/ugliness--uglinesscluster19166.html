ugliness

noun
unattractiveness; unpleasant appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈʌɡlinəs / (AmE), / ˈʌɡlinɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being unpleasant or unattractive in appearance.

Examples

Physical ugliness
She was hurt by the remarks about her supposed ugliness.
The artist transformed ugliness into something meaningful through his sculpture.
The old house had a certain ugliness that made it infamous in the neighborhood.

  • synonym: ugly - unpleasant or unattractive in appearance
