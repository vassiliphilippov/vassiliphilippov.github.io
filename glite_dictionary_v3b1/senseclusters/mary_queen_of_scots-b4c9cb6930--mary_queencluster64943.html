Mary Queen of Scots

proper noun
Scottish queen (1542–1587), executed in England

Pronunciation: /ˈmeə.ri kwiːn əv skɑːts/ /; / /ˈmeə.ri kwiːn əv skɒts/ (AmE), /ˈmeə.ri kwiːn əv skɒts/ (BrE)
