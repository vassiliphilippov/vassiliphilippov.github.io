thimbled

adjective
fitted with thimble
Pronunciation: / ˈθɪmbəld / (AmE), / ˈθɪmbəld / (BrE)
having or provided with a thimble or thimbles, especially as a protective fitting or reinforcement.

Examples

Thimbled cable end
The thimbled end of the cable made it easy to attach.
For extra durability, the rope was thimbled before being used on the boat.
Always use a thimbled wire when connecting heavy loads to prevent fraying.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reinforced - strengthened or supported with additional material
