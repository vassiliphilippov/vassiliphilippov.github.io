turkeyfoot

noun
turkey's footprint
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜːrkiˌfʊt / (AmE), / ˈtɜːkiˌfʊt / (BrE)
the footprint or track left by a turkey, especially as an impression in soft ground

Examples
