superheroine

noun
female superpowered hero
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈhɛroʊɪn / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈhɛrəʊɪn / (BrE)
a female character, especially in stories, films, or comics, who has superhuman powers and fights evil or protects others; sometimes used for a woman admired for extraordinary courage or achievements

Many young girls look up to their favorite superheroine as a role model.

After saving the family from the burning house, she was hailed as a real-life superheroine.
Many comic books now feature a strong superheroine as the main character.
