superheroine

noun
female superpowered hero
4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈsuː.pər.hɪ.rɪn/ /; / /suː.pər.ˈhɪ.rɪn/ (AmE), /ˈsuː.pə.hɪə.rɪn/ /; / /suː.pə.ˈhɪə.rɪn/ (BrE)
