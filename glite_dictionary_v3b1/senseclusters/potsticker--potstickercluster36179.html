potsticker

noun
pan-fried dumpling
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑtˌstɪkər / (AmE), / ˈpɒtˌstɪkə / (BrE)
a Chinese dumpling that is pan-fried on one side and steamed on the other, often filled with meat or vegetables

Examples

A plate of potstickers
We ordered a plate of potstickers to share as an appetizer.
The chef taught us how to make homemade potstickers with pork and cabbage filling.
She dipped her potsticker in soy sauce before taking a bite.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dumpling - a small piece of dough filled with meat or vegetables and cooked by boiling, steaming, or frying
  • synonym: gyoza - a Japanese dumpling similar to a potsticker, usually pan-fried
