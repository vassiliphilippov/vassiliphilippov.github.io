bustle about

verb
move energetically and busily
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌs.əl əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / ˈbʌs.əl əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to move around actively and energetically, often while busy with tasks

Examples

To bustle about quickly
All morning, workers bustled about the office, making sure the event ran smoothly.
She bustled about the kitchen, preparing food for the party guests.
The teachers bustled about the classroom to get everything ready for the exam.

  • synonym: rush around - move quickly and in a busy way
