kanaka

noun
Pacific Island worker
9.9
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈnæk.ə/ /; / /ˈkæn.ə.kə/ (AmE), /kə.ˈnæk.ə/ /; / /ˈkæn.ə.kə/ (BrE)
a worker from the Pacific Islands, especially employed on plantations in Australia and other regions during the 19th and early 20th centuries

Examples
