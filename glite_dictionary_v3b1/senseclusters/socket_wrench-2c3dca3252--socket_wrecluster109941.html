socket wrench

noun
tool with handle and interchangeable sockets
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑk.ɪt ˌrɛntʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɒk.ɪt ˌrɛntʃ / (BrE)
a tool consisting of a handle with interchangeable sockets of various sizes used to turn nuts and bolts

Examples

A socket wrench set
A socket wrench set includes several different socket sizes for various tasks.
He tightened the bolt using a socket wrench from his toolbox.
It is easier to reach tight spaces with a socket wrench than with a regular spanner.
