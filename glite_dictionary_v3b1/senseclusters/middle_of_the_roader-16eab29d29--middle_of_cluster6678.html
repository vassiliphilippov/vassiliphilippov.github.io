middle of the roader

noun
person with moderate or centrist views
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪd.əl əv ðə ˈroʊ.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɪd.əl əv ðə ˈrəʊ.də / (BrE)

Examples

A middle of the roader politician
As a middle of the roader, she tries to find common ground between conservative and liberal parties.
He's known as a middle of the roader who rarely supports controversial policies.
The candidate appealed to middle of the roaders who were undecided about the upcoming election.
