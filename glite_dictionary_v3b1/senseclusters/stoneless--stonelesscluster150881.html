stoneless

adjective
without a stone or pit (fruit)
Pronunciation: / ˈstoʊn.ləs / (AmE), / ˈstəʊn.ləs / (BrE)
(of fruit) having had the stone or pit removed.

Examples

Stoneless fruit
For easier eating, choose stoneless olives in your salads.

The recipe calls for stoneless dates to make the process faster.
