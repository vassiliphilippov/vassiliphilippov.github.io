hairsplitter

noun
person making overly fine distinctions
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrˌsplɪtər / (AmE), / ˈheəˌsplɪtə / (BrE)
a person who makes excessively fine or subtle distinctions, especially in argument or reasoning

Examples

A hairsplitter argues
He's a notorious hairsplitter who will argue over the smallest possible difference in meaning.
Sometimes being a hairsplitter is seen as a sign of intelligence, but it often leads to unnecessary arguments.
The debate dragged on as one hairsplitter after another refused to accept any compromise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pedant - a person excessively concerned with minor details or rules
  • synonym: quibbler - a person who raises petty objections
