switcher

noun
person who changes
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈswɪtʃə / (BrE)

Examples

Job switcher

As a switcher, she adapted quickly to new environments and challenges.
The company values switchers who bring diverse experiences from different industries.
