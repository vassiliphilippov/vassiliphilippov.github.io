mean

verb
to signify or represent (a meaning)
Pronunciation: / miːn / (AmE), / miːn / (BrE)
to have (a particular meaning); to signify or represent something, especially a word, symbol, or expression.

Examples

A word means something
In mathematics, π means the ratio of a circle’s circumference to its diameter.
The red light usually means you should stop your car.

Related Terms

  • synonym: denote - to be the name or symbol for; to mean
  • synonym: signify - to be a sign of; to represent or mean
