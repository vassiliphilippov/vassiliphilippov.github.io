applecart

noun
one's plans or arrangements
9.9 6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈæp.əl.kɑːrt/ (AmE), /ˈæp.əl.kɑːt/ (BrE)
used to refer to a person's carefully made plans or arrangements, especially when they are disrupted or disturbed.

Examples

Upset the applecart
Don't let unexpected news disturb your applecart and ruin your plans.
Every time we're close to finishing, something comes along and overturns the applecart.
He worked for weeks on the project, but one mistake was enough to upset his applecart.
