wassailer

noun
person who enjoys festive, noisy drinking
8.4
Pronunciation: / wəˈseɪlər /; / ˈwɑːˌseɪlər / (AmE), / ˈwɒseɪlə /; / ˈwɒs.əɪlər / (BrE)
a person who takes part in festive, often noisy, drinking and celebration, especially as part of a tradition
