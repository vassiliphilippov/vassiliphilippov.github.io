sixth-rate

adjective
of very poor quality
Pronunciation: / sɪksθ reɪt / (AmE), / sɪksθ reɪt / (BrE)
of very poor or extremely low quality; inferior compared to others of its type.

Examples

A sixth-rate movie
His writing was criticized as sixth-rate by most of the reviewers.
Nobody wants to pay top dollar for a sixth-rate hotel room.
The concert was a sixth-rate performance that left the audience disappointed.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: fifth-rate - of poor quality; slightly better than sixth-rate
  • antonym: first-rate - of the highest quality
  • degree higher: second-rate - of inferior or less than best quality
