high

noun
area of high atmospheric pressure
9.9 5.2
Pronunciation: /haɪ/ (AmE), /haɪ/ (BrE)
an area of the atmosphere where the pressure is higher than in the surrounding regions, often bringing clear weather; a high-pressure system

Examples

A weather high
A large high settled over the Midwest, bringing sunny skies for several days.
Meteorologists predict that the high will move eastward by Thursday afternoon.
The Bermuda high often influences summer weather on the east coast.
