dinner theater

noun
theater where dinner is served with the show
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪnər ˌθiətər /; / ˈdɪnɚ ˌθiətər / (AmE), / ˈdɪnə ˌθɪətə / (BrE)
A theater in which a meal is served to the audience as part of the admission price, typically while a play or performance is presented.
