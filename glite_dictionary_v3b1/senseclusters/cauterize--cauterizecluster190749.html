cauterize

verb
burn or destroy tissue for medical purposes
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔːtəˌraɪz /; / ˈkɔtəˌraɪz /; / ˈkɔːtəraɪz / (AmE), / ˈkɔːtəraɪz / (BrE)
to burn, sear, or freeze tissue (usually to stop bleeding or prevent infection), using heat, electricity, or chemicals
