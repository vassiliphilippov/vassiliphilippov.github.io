courtesy flush

noun
extra flush to reduce restroom odors
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝːtəsi flʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈkɜːtəsi flʌʃ / (BrE)
an extra flush of a toilet, performed to reduce odors or maintain cleanliness while using the restroom

Examples

A courtesy flush
He always does a courtesy flush when using someone else's bathroom.
Remember to give a courtesy flush if you're worried about the smell.
Some people think a courtesy flush is more considerate when sharing a restroom.
