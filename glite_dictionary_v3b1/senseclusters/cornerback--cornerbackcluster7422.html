cornerback

noun
defensive football player position
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnərˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnəbæk / (BrE)
a defensive player in American or Canadian football who covers wide receivers and defends against passing plays, typically stationed outside the linebackers

Examples

A cornerback covers receivers
He was drafted as a cornerback after a stellar college career.
Most cornerbacks are known for their speed and agility.
The team's best cornerback intercepted the pass in the final minute.

Related Terms

  • synonym: safety - defensive player who plays deeper than the cornerbacks
  • antonym: wide receiver - offensive player covered by the cornerback
