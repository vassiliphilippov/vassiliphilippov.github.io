teres minor muscle

noun
upper arm muscle, aids rotation and adduction
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛr.iːz ˈmaɪ.nɚ ˈmʌs.əl / (AmE), / ˈtɛr.iːz ˈmaɪ.nə ˈmʌs.əl / (BrE)
a small, narrow muscle of the upper arm that helps adduct and rotate the arm laterally

Examples
