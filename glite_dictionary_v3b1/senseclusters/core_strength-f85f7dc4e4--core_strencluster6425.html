core strength

noun
abdominal and back muscle strength
5.2
Pronunciation: / kɔr strɛŋkθ / (AmE), / kɔː strɛŋθ / (BrE)
Muscle strength in the abdomen and back, essential for stability and movement.

Examples

Core strength exercises
Athletes focus on building core strength to enhance performance and reduce injury.
If your core strength is weak, you may experience back pain during physical activity.
Yoga and Pilates are both excellent for improving core strength.
