quark

noun
fresh, soft cheese
Pronunciation: / kwɑrk / (AmE), / kwɑːk / (BrE)
A soft, fresh, unripened cheese with a smooth texture, made from pasteurized milk, a starter, and rennet.

Examples

A spoonful of quark
I spread some quark on my toast for a healthy breakfast.
Many German desserts use quark as a main ingredient for their creamy texture.
The recipe calls for 200 grams of quark mixed with herbs and spices.

Related Terms
