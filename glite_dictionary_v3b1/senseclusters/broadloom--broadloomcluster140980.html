broadloom

adjective
carpet woven in a wide piece
8.2 5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɔdˌlum / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːd.luːm / (BrE)
(of rugs or carpets) woven in a single, wide piece, usually intended for wall-to-wall installation rather than as individual rugs.

Examples

Broadloom carpet style
The hotel corridors are covered with a plush, red broadloom carpet.
They decided to install broadloom in the living room for a seamless look.
This store specializes in fitted broadloom as well as traditional area rugs.
