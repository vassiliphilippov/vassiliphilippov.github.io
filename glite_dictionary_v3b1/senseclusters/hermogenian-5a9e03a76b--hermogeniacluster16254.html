Hermogenian

noun
follower of Hermogenes
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɜrˌmɑːˈdʒiːniən / (AmE), / hɜːˌmɒˈdʒiːniən / (BrE)
a person who follows or supports the ideas or works of Hermogenes, especially in rhetoric or law

Examples

A Hermogenian scholar
As a dedicated Hermogenian, she emphasized structure and clarity in her legal arguments.
The professor was known as a Hermogenian for his admiration of classical rhetorical techniques.
Many students became Hermogenians after studying the works of Hermogenes in their rhetoric class.
