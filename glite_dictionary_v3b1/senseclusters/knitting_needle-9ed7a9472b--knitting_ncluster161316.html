knitting needle

noun
slender rod used for knitting by hand
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪtɪŋ ˌniːdəl / (AmE), / ˈnɪtɪŋ ˌniːdl / (BrE)
A slender, pointed rod, usually used in pairs, for knitting by hand.

Examples

Metal knitting needles
Metal knitting needles can make a clicking sound when you work quickly.
My grandmother gave me a pair of bamboo knitting needles for my birthday.
She slipped the stitches carefully from one knitting needle to the other.
