confidingly

adverb
in a trusting manner
7.8
Pronunciation: / kənˈfaɪdɪŋli / (AmE), / kənˈfaɪdɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows trust or confidence in someone.

Examples

To speak confidingly
He spoke confidingly to the counselor about his hopes and fears.
She smiled confidingly at her best friend, sharing a secret only they knew.
The child looked confidingly at her mother when she felt unsure.
