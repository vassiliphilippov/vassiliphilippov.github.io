Synonyms

    shivering is a shaking response to cold or excitement, while trembling is often caused by fear, anxiety, or physical weakness.
  • quiver
    3.9 0.3/m
    quiver is a slight, quick shake from strong emotion, while trembling is a more general, often longer involuntary shaking.
  • tremor
    5.3 2/m
    tremor is a medical or sudden involuntary movement often linked to illness, while trembling is a common slight shaking from fear or cold.
  • shaky
    2.1 3/m

  • shake
