trembling

adjective
shaking slightly and uncontrollably
1.9 2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛmblɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtrɛmblɪŋ / (BrE)
shaking slightly and uncontrollably, typically as a result of fear, anxiety, cold, or weakness

Examples

A trembling voice
A trembling voice betrayed his anxiety during the interview.
She held out her trembling hands to show how nervous she was.
The puppy appeared cold and trembling after being out in the rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: quaking - shaking or trembling, especially from fear or cold
  • synonym: shaking - moving with slight, rapid, tremulous movements
  • antonym: steady - firmly fixed, supported, or balanced; not shaking or moving
