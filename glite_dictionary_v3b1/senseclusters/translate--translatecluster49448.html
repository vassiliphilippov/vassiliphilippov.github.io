translate

verb
to synthesize protein from mRNA instructions
5.4 6.1
Pronunciation: / trænˈsleɪt / (AmE), / trænzˈleɪt / (BrE)
(in biochemistry) to synthesize a protein by determining the amino acid sequence using the information encoded in messenger RNA.

Examples

To translate mRNA
During protein synthesis, ribosomes translate the genetic information carried by mRNA into a specific amino acid sequence.
If the mRNA is mutated, the cell may translate a faulty protein.
Scientists study how cells translate mRNA to understand genetic diseases.
