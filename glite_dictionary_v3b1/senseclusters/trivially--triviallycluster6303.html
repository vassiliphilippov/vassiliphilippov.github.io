trivially

adverb
in an obvious or simple manner
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪviəli / (AmE), / ˈtrɪvɪəli /; / ˈtrɪviəli / (BrE)
in a way that is obvious, requires little effort, or is unimportant

These concerns were dismissed trivially by the committee as unimportant.
