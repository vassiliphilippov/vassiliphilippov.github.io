mycotic

adjective
caused by fungus
Pronunciation: / maɪˈkɑtɪk / (AmE), / maɪˈkɒtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or caused by a fungus, especially in a medical context

Examples

A mycotic infection
The doctor suspected a mycotic infection after reviewing the patient's symptoms.
Treatment for mycotic diseases often involves antifungal medications.
A mycotic ulcer can develop if a fungal infection is left untreated.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bacterial - relating to or caused by bacteria
  • synonym: fungal - relating to or caused by a fungus
