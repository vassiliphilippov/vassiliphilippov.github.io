Indigofera suffruticosa

noun
shrub used for indigo dye
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˈɡoʊfərə ˌsʌfruːtɪˈkoʊsə / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈɡəʊfərə ˌsʌfruːtɪˈkəʊsə / (BrE)
a shrub native to the West Indies and South America, cultivated as a source of indigo dye
