tibial

adjective
of or near the tibia bone
8.8 5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪbiəl / (AmE), / ˈtɪbɪəl / (BrE)
Relating to or situated near the tibia, the larger bone in the lower leg.

Examples

Tibial pain
Her tibial pain worsened after running the marathon.
Swelling around the tibial area suggested soft tissue damage.
The x-ray revealed a fracture along the tibial shaft.

Related Terms

  • synonym: femoral - relating to the femur bone in the thigh
  • synonym: tibia - the inner and usually larger of the two bones in the lower leg
