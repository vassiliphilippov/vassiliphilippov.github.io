noncontagious

adjective
not able to be spread (of disease)
2.3 3.6
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.kən.ˈteɪ.dʒəs/ /; / /nən.kən.ˈteɪ.dʒəs/ (AmE), /nɒn.kən.ˈteɪ.dʒəs/ /; / /nɒn.kɒn.ˈteɪ.dʒəs/ (BrE)
(of a disease) not capable of being transmitted from one person to another; not contagious.
