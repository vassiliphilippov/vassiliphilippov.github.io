noncontagious

adjective
not able to be spread (of disease)
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.kənˈteɪ.dʒəs / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.kənˈteɪ.dʒəs / (BrE)
(of a disease) not capable of being transmitted from one person to another; not contagious.

Examples

Noncontagious disease
Doctors confirmed that her skin condition was noncontagious and posed no public health risk.
It's important to know whether an illness is noncontagious before deciding on quarantine measures.
Unlike the flu, a cold sore is a noncontagious lesion caused by stress or injury.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contagious - (of a disease) able to be passed from one person to another by contact
