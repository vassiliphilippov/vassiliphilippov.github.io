subphylum Urochorda

noun
taxonomic group of tunicates
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsʌb.faɪ.ləm juː.rə.ˈkɔːr.də/ /; / /ˈsʌb.faɪ.ləm juː.roʊ.ˈkɔːr.də/ (AmE), /ˈsʌb.faɪ.ləm juː.rə.ˈkɔː.də/ (BrE)
A major subdivision of the phylum Chordata, comprising tunicates or sea squirts, marine animals characterized by a sac-like body and a notochord during some stage of development.
