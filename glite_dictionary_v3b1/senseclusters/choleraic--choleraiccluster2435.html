choleraic

adjective
relating to or resembling cholera
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːləˈreɪɪk / (AmE), / ˌkɒləˈreɪɪk / (BrE)
relating to, caused by, or resembling cholera, a severe infectious disease causing profuse diarrhea and dehydration

Examples

Choleraic infection
Her symptoms were so severe and sudden that the doctor suspected a choleraic infection.
Sanitation was crucial to prevent the spread of choleraic disease in the refugee camp.
The hospital opened a special ward for choleraic patients during the outbreak.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cholera - a severe infectious disease causing vomiting and diarrhea
