detox

noun
break from digital devices
4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈdiː.tɑːks/ (AmE), /ˈdiː.tɒks/ (BrE)
a period or process of refraining from using digital devices, social media, or online services in order to reduce stress or improve well-being.

Examples

A digital detox
After feeling burnt out, she scheduled a three-day detox from social media to clear her mind.
The company encouraged employees to take a digital detox each evening to reduce screen fatigue.
Over the weekend, he tried a phone detox and discovered how much free time he had left.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digital detox - a period of intentionally avoiding digital devices and online platforms to reduce stress or distraction
  • synonym: social-media break - a deliberate temporary stop to using social media
