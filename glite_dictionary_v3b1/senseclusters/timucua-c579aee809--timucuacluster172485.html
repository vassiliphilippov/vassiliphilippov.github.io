Timucua

proper noun
Native American people
Pronunciation: / tɪˈmuːkwə / (AmE), / tɪˈmuːkwə / (BrE)
a Native American people who lived in northern Florida and their now-extinct language

Examples

Timucua people
Artifacts from the Timucua are displayed in the local history museum.
The Timucua once inhabited large areas of northern Florida before European contact.
Researchers study the Timucua language to learn more about the region's history.
