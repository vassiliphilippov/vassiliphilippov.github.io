violinmaker

noun
violin craftsman
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪəlɪnˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈvaɪəlɪnˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who makes or repairs violins by hand

Examples

A violinmaker crafts violins
The violinmaker spent months perfecting the sound of each instrument.
Many musicians travel to Italy to meet a famous violinmaker for custom violins.
She apprenticed with a master violinmaker to learn the traditional techniques.

Related Terms

  • antonym: musician - a person who plays a musical instrument
