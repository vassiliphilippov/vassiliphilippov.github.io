yard ape

noun
mischievous, playful child
6.6
Pronunciation: / jɑrd eɪp / (AmE), / jɑːd eɪp / (BrE)
an informal and sometimes playful term for a lively, mischievous child

Examples

She's always been a bit of a yard ape, climbing everything in sight.
