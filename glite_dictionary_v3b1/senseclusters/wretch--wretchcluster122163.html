wretch

noun
unfortunate or pitiable person
Pronunciation: / rɛtʃ / (AmE), / rɛtʃ / (BrE)
a person who is in a very unhappy or unfortunate state, often eliciting pity or compassion from others.

Examples

A poor wretch
After the disaster, many wretches wandered the streets in search of aid.
She helped the injured wretch who had collapsed on the side of the road.
The poor wretch was shivering in the cold, with nothing but rags to cover him.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outcast - a person who has been rejected by society
  • synonym: pauper - a very poor person
