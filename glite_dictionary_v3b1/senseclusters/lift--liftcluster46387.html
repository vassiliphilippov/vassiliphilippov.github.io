Synonyms

  • raise
    1.0 42/m
    raise is used to move something higher in general, while lift focuses on physically moving something by hand or machine.
  • elevate
    3.9 11/m
    elevate sounds formal and often means raise to a higher level, while lift is common and physical everyday action.
  • hoist
    4.7 5.61/m
    hoist means to raise using ropes or machinery, while lift is more general and covers hands or many simple tools.
  • pick up
    1.0 49/m
    pick up means to lift something from a surface with your hands, while lift covers both hands and machines.
  • heave up
    4.5 0.27/m
    heave up means to lift something heavy with effort, often by pushing or pulling, while lift is neutral about effort.
  • boost
    2.5 0.98/m
    boost often means help something up from below or improve size or power, while lift is plain physical raising.
