larcenist

noun
someone who commits larceny
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːrsənɪst / (AmE), / ˈlɑːsənɪst / (BrE)
a person who commits larceny; a thief

Examples

A larcenist steals goods
As a known larcenist, he was often under suspicion when valuables went missing.
The larcenist was sentenced to three years in prison for her repeated offenses.
The police arrested the larcenist after reviewing the security footage.
