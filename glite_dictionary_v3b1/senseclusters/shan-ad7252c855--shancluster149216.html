Shan

noun
a Tai language of Laos and Myanmar
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʃɑn / (AmE), / ʃan / (BrE)
A Tai language spoken mainly in the Louangphabang Province of Laos, and by Shan people in Myanmar and surrounding regions.

Shan language
