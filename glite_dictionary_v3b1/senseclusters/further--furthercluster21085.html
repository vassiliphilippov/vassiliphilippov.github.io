further

adverb
in addition; moreover
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝːðər / (AmE), / ˈfɜːðə / (BrE)
in addition; moreover; used to introduce a new point or add information

Examples

Further information added
Further, we have to consider the impact on local businesses before making a decision.
No further questions were asked after the presentation was finished.
The team will review the proposal; further, they plan to meet with stakeholders next week.
