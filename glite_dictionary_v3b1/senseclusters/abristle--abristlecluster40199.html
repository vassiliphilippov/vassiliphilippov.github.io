abristle

adjective
irritated or defensive
Pronunciation: / əˈbrɪsəl / (AmE), / əˈbrɪsəl / (BrE)
showing anger, irritation, or defensive readiness, as if bristling in manner or expression

Examples

Abristle with anger
She became abristle when the manager questioned her decision in front of the team.
His tone was abristle with irritation after hearing the unfair criticism.
Whenever someone mentions the topic, he goes abristle and refuses to discuss it further.
