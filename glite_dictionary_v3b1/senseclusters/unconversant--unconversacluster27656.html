unconversant

adjective
not familiar
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈvɜrsənt / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈvɜːsənt / (BrE)
not familiar with or knowledgeable about a particular subject or area

Examples

Unconversant with technology
She was unconversant with the rules of the new game and needed help.
Many people are unconversant in the technical language used by experts.
He admitted he was unconversant with the details of the contract.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ignorant - lacking knowledge or awareness in general or about something
