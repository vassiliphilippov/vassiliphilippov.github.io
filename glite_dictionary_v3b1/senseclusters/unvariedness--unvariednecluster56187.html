unvariedness

noun
state of being unvaried
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈværidnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈvɛːrɪdnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being uniform or lacking in variation

Examples

The unvariedness of menu
Critics complained about the unvariedness of the restaurant’s menu.
The unvariedness of his routine made each day feel exactly the same.
There was a certain unvariedness in her voice that made her speech less engaging.

Related Terms

  • synonym: uniformity - the quality of always being the same
  • antonym: variety - the quality of being different or diverse
