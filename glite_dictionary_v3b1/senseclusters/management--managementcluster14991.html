management

noun
the act of managing
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmænɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ˈmænɪdʒm(ə)nt / (BrE)
The act or process of managing, controlling, or supervising something, such as a business or organization.

Examples

Project management
Effective management is essential for the success of any project.
Environmental management requires cooperation between different sectors.
He took over the management of the company last year.
