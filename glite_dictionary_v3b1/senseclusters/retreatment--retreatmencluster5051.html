retreatment

noun
additional processing
6.9
Pronunciation: / riˈtriːtmənt / (AmE), / riˈtriːtmənt / (BrE)
the process of subjecting something to an additional treatment to restore, improve, or purify it

Examples

Water retreatment
The company ordered a retreatment of the water to ensure it met safety standards.
After the initial process failed, a retreatment was necessary to achieve the desired quality.
Some materials require retreatment to remove all impurities before use in manufacturing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neglect - the act of not giving care or attention to something
  • synonym: treatment - the process of applying a method to improve or change something
