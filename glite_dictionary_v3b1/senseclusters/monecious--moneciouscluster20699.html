monecious

adjective
having both male and female organs (biology)
8.8 8.3
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈniː.ʃəs/ (AmE), /mə.ˈniː.ʃəs/ (BrE)
Having both male and female reproductive organs in the same individual, especially in plants.

Examples

Monecious plant species
Corn is a classic example of a monecious plant species.
Many squash plants are monecious, bearing both male and female flowers on the same individual.
Some algae are monecious, enabling self-fertilization within a single organism.
