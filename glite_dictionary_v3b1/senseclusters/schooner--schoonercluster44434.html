schooner

noun
type of sailing vessel
Pronunciation: / ˈskuːnər / (AmE), / ˈskuːnə / (BrE)
a sailing ship with two or more masts, typically with the foremast shorter than the mainmast, used especially in the past

Examples

A sailing schooner
During their expedition, they traveled up the coast in a sturdy schooner.
He gazed at the model schooner displayed in the maritime museum.
The old schooner sailed gracefully into the harbor at sunset.
