fwd

noun
forwarded message
7.5
Pronunciation: / ɛf ˈdʌbəljuː ˈdiː / (AmE), / ɛf ˈdʌbəljuː ˈdiː / (BrE)
an email or message that has been forwarded from another sender, often marked with 'FWD:' in the subject line

Examples

Email fwd
I received a fwd from my colleague with the latest project updates.
Please check the attached fwd for the original message from the client.
He accidentally sent the fwd to the wrong mailing list.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forward - to send a message received from someone else to another person
  • antonym: reply - a response to a message or email
