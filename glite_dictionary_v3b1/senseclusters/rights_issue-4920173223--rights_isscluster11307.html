rights issue

noun
share offering to existing shareholders
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪts ˌɪʃu / (AmE), / ˈraɪts ˌɪsjuː / (BrE)
an offering of new shares to existing shareholders, giving them the right to buy additional shares at a discount before they are offered to the public

Examples

A rights issue offer
After the rights issue, the proportion of shares held by existing investors increased.
Shareholders were given the opportunity to buy extra stock during the rights issue.
The company announced a rights issue to raise funds for its expansion plans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: share issue - the act of offering new shares for sale
