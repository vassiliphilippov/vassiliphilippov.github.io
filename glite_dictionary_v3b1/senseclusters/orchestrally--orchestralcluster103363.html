orchestrally

adverb
in a coordinated way
Pronunciation: / ɔːrˈkɛstrəli / (AmE), / ɔːˈkɛstrəli / (BrE)
in a manner that is carefully planned and coordinated, like an orchestra performance.

Examples

Orchestrally planned event
The event was orchestrally managed, with every detail planned in advance.
All the departments worked orchestrally to ensure the project's success.
The rescue operation was carried out orchestrally, showing impressive teamwork and coordination.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chaotically - in a disorganized or confused way
  • synonym: systematically - in a methodical and organized way
