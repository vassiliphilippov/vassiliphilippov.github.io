oversensitize

verb
to make too sensitive
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪtaɪz / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈsɛnsɪtaɪz / (BrE)
to make someone or something excessively sensitive to a particular stimulus or influence

Examples

To oversensitize the skin
Repeated exposure can oversensitize the skin to sunlight, leading to irritation.
Some chemicals may oversensitize people to allergens in the environment.
Loud noises can oversensitize children, making them react strongly to even small sounds.

Related Terms

  • antonym: desensitize - to make less sensitive to a stimulus
  • degree lower: sensitize - to make sensitive to a stimulus
