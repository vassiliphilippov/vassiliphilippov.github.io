onus

noun
burden or responsibility
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊnəs / (AmE), / ˈəʊnəs / (BrE)
a duty, responsibility, or burden that is difficult or unpleasant to bear

Examples

The onus of proof
After the error was discovered, the onus shifted to the IT department to find a solution.
As the manager, the onus of making important decisions rests on her shoulders.
The onus is on the applicant to provide evidence of their qualifications.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burden - a heavy load or responsibility
  • synonym: responsibility - the state or fact of being accountable for something
