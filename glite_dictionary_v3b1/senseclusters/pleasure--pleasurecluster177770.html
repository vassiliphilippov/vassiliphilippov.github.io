pleasure

verb
to give enjoyment
Pronunciation: / ˈplɛʒər /; / ˈplɛʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈplɛʒə / (BrE)
to give someone enjoyment, happiness, or satisfaction

Examples

To pleasure a friend
He always tries to pleasure his friends by organizing fun activities.
The chef hoped to pleasure the guests with his signature dessert.
She found joy in pleasuring others through small acts of kindness.
The music pleasured the guests throughout the evening.
She hoped her gift had pleasured her friend after a difficult week.
The beautiful scenery pleasured everyone on the long drive.
