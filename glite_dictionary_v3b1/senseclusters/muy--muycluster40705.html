muy

adverb
Spanish for 'very'
9.9 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / muËˆi / (AmE), / muËˆi / (BrE)
used in Spanish to mean 'very', for emphasis before adjectives or adverbs.

Examples

The muy spicy salsa

His Spanish is muy impressive for someone who just started learning.
