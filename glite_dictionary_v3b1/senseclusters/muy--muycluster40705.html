muy

adverb
Spanish for 'very'
Pronunciation: / muˈi / (AmE), / muˈi / (BrE)
used in Spanish to mean 'very', for emphasis before adjectives or adverbs.

Examples

The muy spicy salsa
The salsa at this restaurant is muy spicy, so be careful if you do not like heat.
She said the weather in Spain was muy pleasant during her vacation.
His Spanish is muy impressive for someone who just started learning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: very - to a high degree; extremely
