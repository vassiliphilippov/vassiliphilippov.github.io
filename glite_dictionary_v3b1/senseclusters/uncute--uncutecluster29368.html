uncute

adjective
unpleasant or inappropriate
8.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkjuːt / (AmE), / ʌnˈkjuːt / (BrE)
informal: behaving or speaking in a way that is unpleasant, inappropriate, or not charming, often displeasing others

Examples

Uncute comment
It was really uncute of him to make that joke at her expense.
Her uncute comments during the meeting made everyone uncomfortable.
Posting that photo without asking was pretty uncute and upset your friend.

Related Terms

  • antonym: charming - pleasant or attractive in manner
  • synonym: rude - impolite or offensive in manner or speech
