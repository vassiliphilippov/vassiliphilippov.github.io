nonconformity

noun
failure to conform to accepted standards
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑːnkənˈfɔːrməti /; / ˌnɑn.kənˈfɔr.mə.ti /; / ˌnɑːnkənˈfɔrməti / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkənˈfɔːməti /; / ˌnɒn.kənˈfɔː.mə.ti / (BrE)
the refusal or failure to conform to accepted standards, customs, or beliefs, especially in society or religion
