adventurously

adverb
with risk-taking spirit
3.9
Pronunciation: / ədˈvɛntʃərəsli / (AmE), / ədˈvɛntʃərəsli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows a willingness to take risks or try new and exciting things

Examples

To adventurously invest
He invested adventurously in several start-up companies despite the risks.
They decided to travel adventurously by backpacking across South America.
The chef cooked adventurously, combining unusual flavors in her dishes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boldly - in a brave or daring way
  • antonym: cautiously - in a careful or risk-avoiding way
