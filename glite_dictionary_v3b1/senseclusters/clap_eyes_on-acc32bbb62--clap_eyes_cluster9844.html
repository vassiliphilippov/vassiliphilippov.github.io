clap eyes on

verb
see or notice (idiom)
Pronunciation: / klæp aɪz ɑn / (AmE), / klæp aɪz ɒn / (BrE)
to see or notice someone or something, often for the first time

Examples

To clap eyes on a jewel

When I first clapped eyes on him, I knew he looked familiar.
