five-hitter

noun
baseball: game with five hits allowed by pitcher
5.2 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈfaɪv.hɪt.ər/ (AmE), /ˈfaɪv.hɪt.ə/ (BrE)
(Baseball) A game in which a pitcher allows the opposing team exactly five hits.

Examples

A five-hitter game
Despite allowing five hits, his control during the five-hitter kept the opponent scoreless.
Last night's game was his second five-hitter of the season.
The pitcher threw a brilliant five-hitter to lead his team to victory.
