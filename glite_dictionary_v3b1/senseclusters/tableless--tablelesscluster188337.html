tableless

adjective
without a table
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪbələs / (AmE), / ˈteɪbələs / (BrE)
not having a table or not furnished with a table in a room or setting

Examples

Tableless room
The new apartment was completely tableless, so they had to eat on the floor.
After selling their old furniture, the living room looked strangely tableless and empty.
A tableless office can feel more spacious but may lack practical work surfaces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bare - lacking furniture or decoration
  • antonym: furnished - provided with furniture
