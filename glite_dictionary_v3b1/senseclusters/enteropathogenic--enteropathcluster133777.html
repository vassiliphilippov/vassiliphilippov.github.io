enteropathogenic

adjective
causing intestinal disease
Pronunciation: / ˌɛntəroʊˌpæθəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛntərəʊˌpæθəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
causing disease in the intestines, especially by infection

Examples

Enteropathogenic bacteria
Some enteropathogenic bacteria are responsible for outbreaks of diarrhea in young children.
