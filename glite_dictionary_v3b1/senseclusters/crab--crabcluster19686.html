crab

noun
marine crustacean with pincers and broad shell
Pronunciation: / kræb / (AmE), / kræb / (BrE)
a marine crustacean with a broad, flat shell, a small abdomen folded under the thorax, and five pairs of legs, the first pair usually ending in pincers, and eyes on short stalks

Examples

A crab scuttles
A large crab scuttled across the rocks as the tide came in.
She ordered fresh crab at the seafood restaurant for dinner.
We watched the fishermen haul in their traps, full of live crab.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lobster - large marine crustacean with long body and large pincers
  • synonym: shrimp - small, slender marine crustacean
