bate

verb
soak hides in solution to soften or clean
8.9
Pronunciation: / beɪt / (AmE), / beɪt / (BrE)
(of a hide or skin) to soak in a special solution to soften it and remove chemicals used in previous treatments, especially in tanning.

Examples

To bate animal skins
Before further processing, the workers bate the animal skins in large wooden vats.
If you don't bate the leather properly, it may remain stiff and difficult to dye.
The tannery bated the hides overnight to ensure all residues were removed.
