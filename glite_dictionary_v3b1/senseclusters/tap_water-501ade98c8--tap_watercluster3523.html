tap water

noun
water supplied from a tap or faucet
1.3 1.0
Pronunciation: /tæp ˈwɔː.tər/ (AmE), /tæp ˈwɔː.tə/ (BrE)
water that comes directly from a tap or faucet, typically used for drinking, cooking, or cleaning, as supplied by a public or private water system

Examples

Some people prefer to filter their tap water before drinking it.
