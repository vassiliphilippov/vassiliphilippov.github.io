microprobe

noun
small space probe
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌproʊb / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌprəʊb / (BrE)
a very small unmanned spacecraft or device sent into space to collect scientific data from planets, comets, or other celestial bodies

Examples

Space microprobe
The research team designed a microprobe to analyze the atmosphere of Mars.
A microprobe was deployed to collect samples from the surface of the asteroid.
Scientists celebrated the successful landing of the microprobe on the distant comet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: probe - an unmanned spacecraft sent to explore space or celestial bodies
  • synonym: satellite - an artificial object placed in orbit around a planet
