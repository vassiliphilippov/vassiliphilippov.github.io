foramen of Monro

noun
passage connecting brain ventricles
9.9
Pronunciation: / fəˈreɪmən əv ˈmɒnroʊ / (AmE), / fəˈreɪmən əv ˈmɒnrəʊ / (BrE)
the small passage connecting each lateral ventricle of the brain to the third ventricle

Examples

The foramen of Monro passage
A blockage at the foramen of Monro can lead to increased intracranial pressure.
Cerebrospinal fluid flows through the foramen of Monro into the third ventricle.
The surgeon located the tumor near the patient's left foramen of Monro.
