lounger

noun
person who idles or loafs
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlaʊndʒər / (AmE), / ˈlaʊndʒə / (BrE)
a person who spends time in a lazy or idle way

Examples

A lazy lounger
Don't be such a lounger; there's plenty of work to do around here.
He became a habitual lounger at the local coffee shop, rarely getting anything done.
The beach was filled with loungers soaking up the afternoon sun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: idler - a person who avoids work or spends time lazily
  • synonym: loafer - a person who is habitually lazy or does not work
  • antonym: workaholic - a person who compulsively works hard and long hours
