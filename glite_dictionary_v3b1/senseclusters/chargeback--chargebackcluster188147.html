chargeback

verb
to reverse a payment
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑrdʒˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːdʒˌbæk / (BrE)
to reverse a payment or transfer a cost back to the person, account, or department responsible for it

Examples

To chargeback expenses
The finance team will chargeback the software subscription to the marketing department next month.
If the expense was made in error, we can chargeback the amount to the correct account.
The company decided to chargeback the travel costs to the employee's department.

Related Terms

  • antonym: charge - to ask for payment for something
  • synonym: refund - to return money paid for a product or service
  • synonym: reimburse - to pay back money to someone
