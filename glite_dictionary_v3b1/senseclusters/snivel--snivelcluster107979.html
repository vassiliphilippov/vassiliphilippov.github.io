snivel

noun
tearful whining or complaining
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɪvəl / (AmE), / ˈsnɪvəl / (BrE)
the act or sound of whining or complaining in a tearful or self-pitying way

Examples

A sad snivel
His constant snivel about unfair treatment annoyed everyone in the office.
She ignored the child's snivel and explained that rules apply to everyone.
There was not a single snivel from him despite the difficult conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whine - a long, high-pitched complaining cry or sound
