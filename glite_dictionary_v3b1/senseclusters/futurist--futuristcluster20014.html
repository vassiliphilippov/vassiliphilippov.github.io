futurist

noun
someone who predicts the future
5.6 5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈfjuː.tʃə.rɪst/ /; / /ˈfjuː.tʃ.rɪst/ (AmE), /ˈfjuː.tʃə.rɪst/ /; / /ˈfjuː.tʃ.rɪst/ (BrE)
