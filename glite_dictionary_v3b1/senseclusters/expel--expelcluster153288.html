expel

verb
force out a substance
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspɛl / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɛl / (BrE)
to force out or discharge a substance, such as gas or liquid, from a body or container

Examples

The old factory expelled smoke into the atmosphere every day.
