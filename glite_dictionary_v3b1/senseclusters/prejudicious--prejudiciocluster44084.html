prejudicious

adjective
showing or tending toward prejudice
4.3 7.4
Pronunciation: /pre.dʒuː.ˈdɪ.ʃəs/ /; / /pre.dʒʊ.ˈdɪ.ʃəs/ (AmE), /pre.dʒʊ.ˈdɪ.ʃəs/ /; / /pre.dʒuː.ˈdɪ.ʃəs/ (BrE)
tending to favor preconceived ideas or prejudices; exhibiting or resulting from prejudice.

Examples

A prejudicious attitude
A prejudicious attitude can damage workplace harmony.
He made a prejudicious assumption without knowing the full story.
The hiring process was criticized for being prejudicious against certain applicants.
