vinchuca

noun
South American insect
9.9
Pronunciation: / vɪnˈtʃuːkə / (AmE), / vɪnˈtʃuːkə / (BrE)
a bloodsucking insect native to South America that transmits Chagas disease

Examples

The vinchuca insect
The vinchuca is a major concern in rural areas of Argentina.
Health workers educate families about how to prevent vinchuca infestations in their homes.
Scientists are studying ways to control the spread of the vinchuca to reduce Chagas disease.
