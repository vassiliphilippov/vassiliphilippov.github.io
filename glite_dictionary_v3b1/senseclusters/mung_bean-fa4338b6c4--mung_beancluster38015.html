mung bean

noun
edible seed of an Asian plant
6.5
Pronunciation: / mʌŋ ˌbin /; / mʌŋ bin / (AmE), / mʌŋ ˌbiːn /; / mʌŋ biːn / (BrE)
an edible, small green bean (seed) from an Asian plant, often used in cooking and for sprouting

Examples

Cooked mung beans
I added soaked mung beans to the salad for extra protein.
Stir-fried noodles often include sprouts made from mung bean seeds.
The recipe calls for two cups of cooked mung beans as the main ingredient.
Mung beans are often sprouted and added to stir-fries or soups.
She made a salad using fresh mung beans and herbs.
The recipe called for soaking mung beans overnight before cooking.
In many Asian dishes, mung bean sprouts are a common ingredient.
Our chef added fresh mung beans to the salad for a crunchy texture.
She bought a bag of mung beans to grow her own sprouts at home.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: bean - an edible seed, typically kidney-shaped, of various plants in the legume family
  • synonym: bean sprout - a sprout from a mung bean, commonly used in cooking
