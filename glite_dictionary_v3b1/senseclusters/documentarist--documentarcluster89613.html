documentarist

noun
documentary creator
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌdɑːkjəˈmɛntərɪst / (AmE), / ˌdɒkjʊˈmɛntərɪst / (BrE)
a person who creates documentary films, photographs, or programs that record real events, people, or places

Examples

The documentarist films life
The documentarist spent months following the lives of local fishermen for her new project.
As a skilled documentarist, he strives to capture the truth behind every image he takes.
Many aspiring filmmakers look up to the work of a renowned documentarist in the industry.
The documentarist interviewed dozens of people for her latest film about climate change.
As a passionate documentarist, he strives to reveal untold stories from around the world.
The festival awarded the best documentarist for his innovative approach to storytelling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: director - a person who supervises the making of a film
  • synonym: documentarian - a person who makes documentary films
  • synonym: documentary filmmaker - a person who directs or produces documentary films
  • synonym: photojournalist - a journalist who uses photographs to tell news stories
