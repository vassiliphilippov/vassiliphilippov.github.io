documentarist

noun
documentary creator
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˌdɑː.kjə.ˈmen.tər.ɪst/ /; / /ˌdɑː.kjʊ.ˈmen.tər.ɪst/ (AmE), /ˌdɒk.jʊ.ˈmen.tə.rɪst/ /; / /ˌdɒk.jə.ˈmen.tə.rɪst/ (BrE)
a person who creates documentary films, photographs, or programs that record real events, people, or places

Examples

The documentarist films life

As a skilled documentarist, he strives to capture the truth behind every image he takes.

The documentarist interviewed dozens of people for her latest film about climate change.
As a passionate documentarist, he strives to reveal untold stories from around the world.
The festival awarded the best documentarist for his innovative approach to storytelling.
