deer hunt

noun
an act of hunting deer
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪr ˌhʌnt / (AmE), / ˈdɪə ˌhʌnt / (BrE)
an act or period of hunting deer, typically as a sport or for food

Examples

A deer hunt trip
During the deer hunt, they followed tracks through the freshly fallen snow.
Each autumn, people travel to the forest for the annual deer hunt.
He prepared his equipment for the early morning deer hunt with his friends.
