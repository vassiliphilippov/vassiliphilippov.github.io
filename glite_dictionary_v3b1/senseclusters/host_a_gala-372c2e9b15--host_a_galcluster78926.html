host a gala

verb
organize a formal event
Pronunciation: / hoʊst ə ˈɡælə / (AmE), / həʊst ə ˈɡɑːlə / (BrE)
to organize and preside over a formal social event, often to raise funds or celebrate an occasion

Examples

To host a gala event
It was an honor to host a gala in celebration of the hospital's 100th anniversary.
She was chosen to host a gala dinner for international diplomats.
The charity will host a gala next spring to support local education programs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: attend a gala - to be present at a formal event or celebration
  • synonym: organize - to arrange or coordinate an event or activity
