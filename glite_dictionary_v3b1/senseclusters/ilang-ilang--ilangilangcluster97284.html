ilang-ilang

noun
essential oil from ilang-ilang flowers
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌiːlɑːŋ ˈiːlɑːŋ / (AmE), / ˌiːlæŋ ˈiːlæŋ / (BrE)
An essential oil distilled from the flowers of the ilang-ilang tree, used especially in perfumery.

Examples

Some aromatherapy blends use ilang-ilang for its calming scent.
The fragrance of ilang-ilang is a key ingredient in many luxury perfumes.
