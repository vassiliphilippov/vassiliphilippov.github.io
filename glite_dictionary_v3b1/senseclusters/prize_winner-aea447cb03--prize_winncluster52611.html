prize winner

noun
person who has won a prize
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪz ˌwɪnər / (AmE), / ˈpraɪz ˌwɪnə / (BrE)
a person who has won a prize, especially in a contest, competition, or lottery

Examples

Each prize winner will be notified by email and asked to claim their reward within a week.
The prize winner smiled as he accepted the trophy in front of the cheering crowd.
