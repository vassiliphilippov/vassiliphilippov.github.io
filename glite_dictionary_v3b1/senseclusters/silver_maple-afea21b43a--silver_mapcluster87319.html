silver maple

noun
a North American maple tree with silvery underside leaves
5.1 4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪl.vər ˈmeɪ.pəl/ (AmE), /ˈsɪl.və ˈmeɪ.pəl/ (BrE)
a North American maple tree (Acer saccharinum) with five-lobed, light green leaves that are silvery white underneath, valued for its brittle, light-brown wood

Examples

Silver maples tree
Many city streets are lined with silver maples because of their rapid growth.
The silver maple in our backyard provides plenty of shade in the summer.
The leaves of the silver maple turn a beautiful yellow in the autumn.
