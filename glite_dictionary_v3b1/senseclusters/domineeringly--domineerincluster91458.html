domineeringly

adverb
in an overbearing or controlling way
Pronunciation: / ˌdɑː.məˈnɪr.ɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˌdɒ.mɪˈnɪə.rɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a manner that shows an overbearing or controlling attitude toward others.

Examples

To speak domineeringly
He spoke domineeringly to his employees, expecting absolute obedience.
She domineeringly insisted that all decisions be made her way.
The manager domineeringly dismissed any ideas that were not his own.
