santur

noun
hammered string instrument
9.9
Pronunciation: / sænˈtʊr / (AmE), / sænˈtʊə / (BrE)
a stringed musical instrument, similar to a hammered dulcimer, played by striking the strings with small mallets

Examples

To play the santur
The musician played a haunting melody on the santur during the concert.
She learned to play the santur from her grandfather, who was a master of the instrument.
The unique sound of the santur added a special touch to the traditional music performance.
