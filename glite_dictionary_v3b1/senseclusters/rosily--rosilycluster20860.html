rosily

adverb
in a rosy manner
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊzɪli / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is pink or reddish in color, or with a rosy or flushed appearance

Examples

Clouds glow rosily
The sunset colored the clouds rosily as the day came to an end.
After her run, her cheeks glowed rosily in the cool morning air.
The baby's face was rosily flushed from playing outside in the sun.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pallidly - in a way that is pale or lacking color
  • synonym: reddishly - in a way that is somewhat red in color
