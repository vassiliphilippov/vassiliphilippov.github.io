ecotourist

noun
environmentally conscious traveler
4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈiː.koʊ.tʊr.ɪst/ /; / /ˈe.koʊ.tʊr.ɪst/ (AmE), /ˈiː.kəʊ.tʊə.rɪst/ /; / /ˈe.kəʊ.tʊə.rɪst/ (BrE)
a person who travels to natural areas to enjoy nature and help protect the environment

Examples

An ecotourist visits nature
The ecotourist chose a guided hike to minimize their impact on the forest.
As an ecotourist, she always supports local conservation projects during her trips.
Many ecotourists prefer destinations that protect wildlife and natural habitats.
Many ecotourists choose to visit rainforests to observe wildlife and support conservation projects.
The lodge was designed to attract ecotourists interested in sustainable travel and local culture.
Ecotourists often participate in activities like birdwatching and guided nature walks.
