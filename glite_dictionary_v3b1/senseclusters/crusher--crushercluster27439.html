crusher

noun
machine that crushes materials
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈkrʌʃə / (BrE)
a machine or device designed to break up or compress something into smaller pieces, especially by squeezing or pounding

Examples

A crusher machine
A can crusher is a useful tool in recycling operations.
The laboratory uses a jaw crusher to prepare rock samples for analysis.
The stone crusher at the quarry makes the work much faster.
