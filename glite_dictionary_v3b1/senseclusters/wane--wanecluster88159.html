wane

noun
gradual decline or decrease
8.3
Pronunciation: / weɪn / (AmE), / weɪn / (BrE)
A gradual decline in size, strength, power, or number.

Examples

On the wane
After the festival ended, excitement was on the wane throughout the city.
The power of the empire was already in wane by the early 18th century.
There has been a noticeable wane in the popularity of print newspapers over the past decade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decline - gradual and continuous loss of strength, numbers, quality, or value
  • antonym: increase - an instance of becoming greater or larger
