hold dear

verb
to value or love greatly
Pronunciation: / hoʊld dɪr / (AmE), / həʊld dɪə / (BrE)
to feel great affection or love for someone or something; to value highly

Examples

To hold dear a friend
He always held dear the friendship they shared during difficult times.
Many people hold dear the traditions passed down from their ancestors.
She holds dear the memories of her childhood spent in the countryside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cherish - to hold something or someone in deep affection
  • synonym: treasure - to value highly or treat as very special
