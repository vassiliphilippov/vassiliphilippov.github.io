A-line

noun
skirt or dress flared from waist to hem
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ ˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈeɪ laɪn / (BrE)
A style of women's clothing, especially a skirt or dress, that fits closely at the hips and gradually widens towards the hem, giving the impression of the letter 'A'.

Examples

An A-line dress
Many brides prefer the flattering silhouette of an A-line wedding gown.
She chose an elegant A-line dress for the evening party.
The boutique features several A-line skirts in bright, summery fabrics.
