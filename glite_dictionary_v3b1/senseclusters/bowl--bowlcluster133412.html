bowl

noun
large open-air stadium or arena
Pronunciation: / boʊl / (AmE), / bəʊl / (BrE)
a large, open-air stadium or arena used for sports or public entertainments, typically with a rounded or oval shape

Examples

Football bowl stadium
The Super Bowl is held every year in a different bowl across the country.
The new football bowl can seat over 70,000 fans.
We watched the concert live at the Hollywood bowl last summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arena - a central area for sports, entertainment, or other events with seats all around
  • synonym: stadium - an arena with tiers of seats for spectators at sports events
