session

noun
meeting period of a group or organization
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˈseʃ.ən / (BrE)
a period during which a group, such as a legislature or court, meets to conduct its business

Examples

Court session
Each session of the conference will focus on a different theme.
The annual budget is discussed during the first session of parliament.
The court resumed its session after a short break.

  • synonym: meeting - an assembly of people for a particular purpose
