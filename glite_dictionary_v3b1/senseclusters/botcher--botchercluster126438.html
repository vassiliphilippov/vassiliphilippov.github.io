botcher

noun
incompetent or unskilled person
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːtʃər / (AmE), / ˈbɒtʃə / (BrE)
a person who performs tasks badly or incompetently; someone who makes mistakes due to lack of skill

Examples

A botcher at work
After seeing the mess, it was clear a botcher had attempted the repairs.
Nobody trusted the botcher to fix the leaking pipe again after last time's disaster.
The report was riddled with errors made by the botcher in charge of the project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bungler - a person who does things clumsily or badly
  • antonym: expert - a person with a high level of knowledge or skill
