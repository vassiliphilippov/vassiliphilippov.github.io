terministic

adjective
about terminology
Pronunciation: / ˌtɜːrmɪˈnɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌtɜːmɪˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving the use or choice of terms or terminology

Examples

Terministic language use
The professor discussed the terministic differences between scientific and everyday language.
Her analysis focused on the terministic strategies used in political speeches.
Writers often make terministic choices that shape how readers interpret their work.
