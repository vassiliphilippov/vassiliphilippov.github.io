spoonful

noun
amount that fills a spoon
1.0 2.15/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàspunÀåf äl / (AmE), / ÀàspuÀênf äl / (BrE)
An amount of something that fills a spoon.

Examples

One spoonful of sugar

He tasted the soup and decided it needed another spoonful of salt.
She stirred a spoonful of honey into her tea every morning.
