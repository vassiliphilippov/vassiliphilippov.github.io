winterkill

verb
to die from winter cold
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪn.tər.kɪl/ (AmE), /ˈwɪn.tə.kɪl/ (BrE)
to die or cause to die as a result of severe winter weather, especially from cold or frost

Examples

Young trees winterkill
Many young trees can winterkill if temperatures drop suddenly in early spring.
Farmers worry that a harsh winter might winterkill their wheat crops.
Some fish species winterkill in shallow lakes when the ice blocks oxygen.
