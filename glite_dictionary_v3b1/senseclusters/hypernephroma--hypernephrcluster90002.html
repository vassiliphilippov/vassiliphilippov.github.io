hypernephroma

noun
kidney cancer tumor
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərnɪˈfroʊmə / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpənɪˈfrəʊmə / (BrE)
a malignant tumor of the kidney, also known as renal cell carcinoma

Examples

A hypernephroma tumor
The surgeon explained that the hypernephroma needed to be removed as soon as possible.
After several tests, the patient was diagnosed with hypernephroma in the left kidney.
Treatment options for hypernephroma often include surgery and targeted therapies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carcinoma - a malignant tumor that starts in epithelial tissue
  • synonym: renal cell carcinoma - a common type of kidney cancer
