button up

verb
fall silent; refuse to speak
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtən ˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈbʌtən ˌʌp / (BrE)
to refuse to talk or to stop speaking suddenly; to fall silent

Examples

To button up suddenly
As soon as the teacher looked at him, he buttoned up and wouldn't say another word.
The suspect buttoned up when the police started asking tough questions.
Whenever the subject of money comes up, she tends to button up completely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shut up - to stop talking or making noise
