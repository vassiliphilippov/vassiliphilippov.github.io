preparer

noun
person who prepares
Pronunciation: / prɪˈpɛrər / (AmE), / prɪˈpeərə / (BrE)
a person who gets something ready or makes arrangements for it

Examples

The preparer of documents
The preparer checked all the documents before the meeting.
Each tax preparer must ensure the forms are accurate and complete.
As the event preparer, she made sure everything was ready on time.
A professional preparer can help you organize your documents for the application process.
The tax preparer explained each step of the filing procedure to her client.
As the event preparer, he made sure everything was ready before the guests arrived.

  • synonym: planner - a person who makes plans for something
