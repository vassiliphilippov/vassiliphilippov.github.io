terrorist attack

noun
violent act for political or religious aims
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛrərɪst əˈtæk / (AmE), / ˈtɛrərɪst əˈtak / (BrE)
a sudden act of violence against civilians intended to achieve political or religious goals through terror

Examples

Terrorist attack on city
Governments around the world have increased security measures following a series of terrorist attacks.
The city was left in shock after the terrorist attack that targeted a busy shopping center.
The victims of the terrorist attack were honored in a memorial service attended by thousands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attack - an aggressive and violent action against a person or place
  • synonym: terrorism - the unlawful use of violence for political aims
