natural phenomenon

noun
event in nature, not artificial
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃrəl fəˈnɑːməˌnɑːn / (AmE), / ˈnatʃ(ə)rəl fɪˈnɒmɪnən /; / ˈnatʃrəl fɪˈnɒmɪnən / (BrE)
an event or process that happens in nature and is not caused by humans

Examples

Volcano eruptions are awe-inspiring examples of a natural phenomenon.
