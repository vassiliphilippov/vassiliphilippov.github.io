Stinger

noun
portable shoulder-fired missile system
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪŋər / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋə / (BrE)
a portable surface-to-air missile system using infrared guidance and an impact fuse, designed to be fired from the shoulder at low-flying aircraft
