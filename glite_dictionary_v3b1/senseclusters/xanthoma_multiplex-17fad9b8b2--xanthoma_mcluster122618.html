xanthoma multiplex

noun
widespread xanthomas on skin
Pronunciation: / zænˈθoʊmə ˈmʌltɪˌplɛks / (AmE), / zænˈθəʊmə ˈmʌltɪplɛks / (BrE)
A medical condition characterized by widespread xanthomas, often found particularly on the elbows and knees, and typically associated with lipid metabolism disorders.

Examples

Xanthoma multiplex on elbows
Xanthoma multiplex is often seen in patients suffering from familial hypercholesterolemia.
The dermatologist diagnosed her with xanthoma multiplex after observing several lipid deposits on her elbows and knees.
Treatment for xanthoma multiplex may involve addressing the underlying lipid disorder.
