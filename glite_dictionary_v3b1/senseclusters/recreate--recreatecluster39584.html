recreate

verb
give new life or energy to
Pronunciation: / ˌriː.krieɪt / (AmE), / ˌriː.kriˈeɪt / (BrE)
to give new life, energy, or freshness to someone or something; to refresh or revitalize

Examples

To recreate the mind
A short walk in the fresh air can often recreate a tired mind.
A vacation by the sea helped to recreate his spirit after months of hard work.
The new therapy aims to recreate patients weakened by illness.

  • synonym: refresh - to restore strength or animation to
  • synonym: revive - to bring back to life or consciousness
