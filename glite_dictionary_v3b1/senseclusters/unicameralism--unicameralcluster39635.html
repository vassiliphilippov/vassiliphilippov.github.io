unicameralism

noun
single-chamber legislature
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːnɪˈkæmərəˌlɪzəm /; / ˌjuːnɪˈkæmərəlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪˈkæmərəlɪzəm / (BrE)
the system or belief in having a single legislative or parliamentary chamber in a government
