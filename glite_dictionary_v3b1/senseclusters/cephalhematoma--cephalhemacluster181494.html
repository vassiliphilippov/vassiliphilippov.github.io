cephalhematoma

noun

A collection of blood between a newborn's scalp and the skull bone, usually caused by birth trauma.

Examples

A cephalhematoma in newborn
A large cephalhematoma may be concerning, but it typically does not require surgical intervention.
Most cephalhematomas resolve on their own within a few weeks after birth.
The doctor diagnosed the swelling as a cephalhematoma after examining the newborn's head.
