ecch

noun
expression of disgust
Pronunciation: / ɛk / (AmE), / ɛk / (BrE)
a sound or expression made to show disgust or distaste

Examples

A loud ecch
She wrinkled her nose and let out a loud ecch when she smelled the spoiled milk.
The children shouted ecch as they saw the slimy worms on the sidewalk.
He made an ecch after tasting the bitter medicine his mother gave him.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ugh - an exclamation of disgust or dislike
  • synonym: yuck - an informal exclamation of distaste or disgust
