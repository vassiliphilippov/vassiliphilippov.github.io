pseudomembranous

adjective
false membrane-like
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːdoʊˈmɛmbrənəs / (AmE), / ˌsjuːdəʊˈmɛmbrənəs / (BrE)
describing tissue that appears to have a false membrane, often as a result of disease or infection

Examples

Pseudomembranous tissue
The colonoscopy revealed pseudomembranous patches lining the patient's intestine.
Doctors identified a pseudomembranous layer covering the affected tissue.
The infection led to the development of pseudomembranous inflammation in the throat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: membranous - having the characteristics of a membrane
