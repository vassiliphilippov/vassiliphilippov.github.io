sanzen

noun
Zen meditation session
Pronunciation: / sɑnˈzɛn / (AmE), / sænˈzɛn / (BrE)
a period of seated meditation practiced in Zen Buddhism

Examples

Morning sanzen session
Every morning, the monks gathered in silence for sanzen before beginning their daily tasks.
Visitors to the temple were invited to join a sanzen to experience Zen meditation.
During sanzen, participants focus on their breathing and clear their minds of distractions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meditation - the practice of focusing the mind for relaxation or spiritual purposes
  • synonym: zazen - seated meditation in Zen Buddhism
