viva

interjection
exclamation of praise
Pronunciation: / ˈviːvə / (AmE), / ˈviːvə / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express praise, support, or celebration for someone or something

Examples

Viva the team
The fans shouted viva as their team scored the winning goal.
During the festival, people lined the streets and cried viva to celebrate the tradition.
With a loud viva, the crowd welcomed the new leader to the stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bravo - an exclamation used to express approval or praise
  • synonym: hurrah - an exclamation of joy or approval
