network architecture

noun
design of an artificial neural network
4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈnet.wɜːrk ˈɑːr.kɪ.tek.tʃər/ (AmE), /ˈnet.wɜːk ˈɑː.kɪ.tek.tʃə/ (BrE)
(in machine learning) the arrangement and connection pattern of layers, modules, and operations in an artificial neural network that determines how it processes information and learns.

Examples

Neural network architecture
Researchers compared several network architectures to see which model generalized best on new images.
She adjusted the network architecture by adding dropout layers to reduce overfitting during training.
Edge devices require compact network architectures that balance accuracy with low memory and power use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: model architecture - the design of a machine-learning model, especially the arrangement of layers and operations in a neural model
  • synonym: network topology - the arrangement of components and connections; in ML, the layout of layers and connections in a neural network
