malic

adjective
of malic acid
Pronunciation: / ˈmælɪk / (AmE), / ˈmælɪk / (BrE)
relating to or derived from malic acid, an organic acid found in many fruits such as apples

Examples

Malic acid
The tart taste of green apples is due to their high malic acid content.
Winemakers monitor malic acid levels to control the flavor of their wines.
Some skincare products use malic acid for its exfoliating properties.
