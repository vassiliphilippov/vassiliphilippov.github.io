sweatband

noun
band inside a hat to absorb sweat
Pronunciation: / ˈswɛtˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈswɛtˌbænd / (BrE)
A band of fabric or leather sewn inside the crown of a hat to absorb sweat.

Examples

Hat sweatband
After years of use, the sweatband inside his cap had become discolored.
The sweatband in my favorite hat keeps the sweat from dripping down my face.
This hat's sweatband is made of soft leather for extra comfort.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hatband - a decorative band on the outside of a hat
