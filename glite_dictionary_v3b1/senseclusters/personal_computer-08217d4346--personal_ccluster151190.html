personal computer

noun
a computer for individual use
2.4 3.53/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ùÀês…ônl k…ômÀàpjuÀêt…ö / (AmE), / Ààp…úÀês…ônl k…ômÀàpjuÀêt…ô / (BrE)
A small digital computer intended for use by one person at a time, typically based on a microprocessor.

Examples

A personal computer system
I bought a new personal computer for my home office last week.
Many students use a personal computer to complete their assignments and research online.
The company's IT department is responsible for maintaining every personal computer in the building.

Synonyms

  • PC
    1.8 20/m
    PC is the common short name people use for small home or office computers, while personal computer is the formal full term.
  • microcomputer
    4.7 2.14/m
    microcomputer is a technical term for small single-user machines used in labs or schools, while personal computer is the everyday name.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: mainframe - a large high-speed computer, especially one supporting numerous workstations
