failure

noun
inability to pay debts (insolvency)
1.8 2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈfeɪ.ljər/ /; / /ˈfeɪ.lər/ (AmE), /ˈfeɪ.ljə/ /; / /ˈfeɪ.lə/ (BrE)
