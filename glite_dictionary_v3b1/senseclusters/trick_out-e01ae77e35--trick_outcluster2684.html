trick out

verb
decorate or dress up elaborately
6.8
Pronunciation: / trɪk aʊt / (AmE), / trɪk aʊt / (BrE)

Examples

To trick out outfits

She tricked out her costume with feathers and glitter for the masquerade ball.
The car was tricked out with colorful lights and custom wheels.
