superior

adjective
of high quality or performance
Pronunciation: / suˈpɪriər / (AmE), / suːˈpɪəriə / (BrE)
of higher quality, ability, or performance than others of the same kind

Examples

Superior quality
Her superior skills made her the top candidate for the job.
The chef only uses ingredients of superior quality in his dishes.
This computer model offers superior performance compared to its competitors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excellent - extremely good or outstanding
  • antonym: inferior - of lower quality or ability
