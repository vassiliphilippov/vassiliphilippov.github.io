reassessment

noun
a new appraisal or evaluation
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˈsɛsmənt / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈsɛsmənt / (BrE)
a new appraisal or evaluation of something, especially after a period of time or a change in circumstances

Examples

A reassessment of strategy
A major reassessment of the company's strategy led to significant changes in management.
After reviewing new evidence, the committee called for a reassessment of the school's safety policies.
The environmental impact report is due for a reassessment given recent developments.
