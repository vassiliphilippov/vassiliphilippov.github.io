untarred

adjective
not coated with tar
6.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtɑrd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɑːd / (BrE)
not covered or treated with tar, especially referring to roads or surfaces

Examples

The children played on the untarred playground, getting their shoes muddy.
