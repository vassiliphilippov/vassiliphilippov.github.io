amyris

noun
fragrant tree resin
Pronunciation: / əˈmaɪrɪs / (AmE), / əˈmaɪrɪs / (BrE)
a fragrant essential oil or resin obtained from the wood of Amyris trees, used in perfumery and cosmetics

Examples

Amyris oil fragrance
The perfumer added a drop of amyris to the blend for a warm, woody aroma.
Many luxury candles contain amyris for its subtle, balsamic scent.
Cosmetic companies value amyris as a natural fragrance ingredient in their products.

Related Terms

  • synonym: balsam - an aromatic resin used in perfumes and medicines
  • synonym: sandalwood - a fragrant wood and oil used in perfumes and incense
