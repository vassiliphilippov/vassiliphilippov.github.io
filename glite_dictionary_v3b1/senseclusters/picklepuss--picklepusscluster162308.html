picklepuss

noun
person with a habitually gloomy expression
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkəlˌpʊs / (AmE), / ˈpɪkəlˌpʊs / (BrE)
a person who habitually has a sullen or gloomy expression

Examples

A picklepuss face
Don't be such a picklepuss—try to enjoy the party!
Her picklepuss demeanor made her seem unapproachable to new colleagues.
No one expects a smile from old picklepuss in the morning.
