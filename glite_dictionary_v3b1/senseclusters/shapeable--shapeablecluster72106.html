shapeable

adjective
able to be molded
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪpəbəl / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪpəbəl / (BrE)
able to be formed or molded into a particular shape

Examples

Shapeable clay
The artist prefers shapeable materials like clay for her sculptures.
This metal is highly shapeable when heated to the right temperature.
Children enjoy playing with shapeable dough to create different figures.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rigid - not able to be bent or changed in shape
