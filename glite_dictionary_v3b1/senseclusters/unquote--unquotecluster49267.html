unquote

verb
to remove quotation marks
6.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkwoʊt / (AmE), / ʌnˈkwəʊt / (BrE)
to remove quotation marks from a word or phrase in written text

Examples

To unquote a name
Please unquote the author's name in the draft before publication.
The editor asked me to unquote the phrase to match the style guide.
If you unquote the title, it will look more consistent with the rest of the text.

Related Terms

  • antonym: quote - to enclose words or phrases in quotation marks
