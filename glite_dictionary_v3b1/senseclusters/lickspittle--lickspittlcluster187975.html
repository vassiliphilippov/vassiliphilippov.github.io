lickspittle

adjective
excessively flattering
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪkˌspɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈlɪkˌspɪtəl / (BrE)
showing excessive flattery or behaving in an overly servile and obsequious manner toward someone in authority
