opencast mining

noun
surface extraction of minerals or coal
5.6 4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈoʊ.pən.kæst ˈmaɪ.nɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈəʊ.pən.kɑːst ˈmaɪ.nɪŋ/ (BrE)
