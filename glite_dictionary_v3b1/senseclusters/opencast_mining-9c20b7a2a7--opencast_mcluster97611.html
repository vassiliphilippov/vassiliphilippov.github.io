opencast mining

noun
surface extraction of minerals or coal
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊpənˌkæst ˈmaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈəʊpənˌkɑːst ˈmaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of extracting ore, coal, or other minerals from a large open pit near the surface, rather than from underground mines

Examples

Opencast mining operation
Opencast mining has a significant impact on the landscape and surrounding ecosystems.
Strict regulations now govern opencast mining operations to minimize environmental damage.
The company switched from underground to opencast mining to reduce costs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: strip mining - surface mining method involving removal of soil and rock overlying a mineral deposit
