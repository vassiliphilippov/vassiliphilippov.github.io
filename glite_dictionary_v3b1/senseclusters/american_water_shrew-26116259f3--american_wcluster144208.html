American water shrew

noun

A species of water shrew (Sorex palustris) found in North America, known for its semi-aquatic habits and foraging in streams and wetlands.

Examples

The American water shrew species
Because of its dense fur, the American water shrew is well adapted to cold water environments.

The American water shrew can be found in streams and wetlands across the northern United States and Canada.
