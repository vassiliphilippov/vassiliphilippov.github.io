forward

adverb
toward the future; ahead in time
1.0 87/m
Pronunciation: / Ààf…îrw…ôrd / (AmE), / Ààf…îÀêw…ôd / (BrE)
toward the future or a later time; from a point in time onward; in the direction of progress

Examples

We are always looking forward to new opportunities.

Synonyms

  • onward
    2.7 3.74/m
    onward is used for continuing movement in time or space without stopping, while forward points toward future time or progress.
  • henceforward
    7.5 0.08/m
    henceforward is a formal word used in legal texts to mean from this moment on, while forward is more general and common.
