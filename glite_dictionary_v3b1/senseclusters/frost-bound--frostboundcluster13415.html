frost-bound

adjective
hardened by frost; immovable due to frost
3.2 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈfrɔːst.baʊnd/ /; / /ˈfrɑːst.baʊnd/ (AmE), /ˈfrɒst.baʊnd/ (BrE)
(of the ground or a region) made hard, immovable, or impassable by frost.

Examples

Frost-bound ground
During January, the river became frost-bound, halting all boat traffic.
The fields were frost-bound and impossible to plough until the thaw.
We trekked across the frost-bound landscape under a pale winter sun.
