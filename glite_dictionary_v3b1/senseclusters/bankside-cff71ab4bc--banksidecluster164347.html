Bankside

proper noun
London riverside district
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbæŋksaɪd / (AmE), / ˈbæŋksaɪd / (BrE)
a district in London situated along the south bank of the River Thames, known for its cultural and historical landmarks

Examples

Bankside London
Many tourists visit Bankside to see the famous Globe Theatre and Tate Modern.
The riverside walk along Bankside offers beautiful views of the city.
