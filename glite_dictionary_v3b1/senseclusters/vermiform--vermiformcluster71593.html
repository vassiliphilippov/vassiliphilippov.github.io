vermiform

adjective
worm-shaped; cylindrical and thin
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrməˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈvɜːmɪfɔːm / (BrE)
resembling a worm in shape; long, thin, and cylindrical.

Examples

Vermiform shape
Microscopically, the parasite exhibits a vermiform appearance under the microscope.
Some marine animals have a vermiform body plan that allows them to burrow easily into sediment.
The vermiform appendix is a narrow, tube-like structure attached to the large intestine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wormlike - resembling or characteristic of a worm
