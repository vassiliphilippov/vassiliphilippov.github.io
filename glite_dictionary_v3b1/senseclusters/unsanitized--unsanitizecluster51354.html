unsanitized

adjective
not censored
6.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsænɪtaɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsænɪtaɪzd / (BrE)
not edited, censored, or altered to remove explicit, offensive, or sensitive content; left in its original form

Examples

Unsanitized footage
The documentary showed unsanitized footage of the events as they happened.
He preferred to hear the unsanitized truth, even if it was uncomfortable.
The report was released in its unsanitized form, including all controversial details.
