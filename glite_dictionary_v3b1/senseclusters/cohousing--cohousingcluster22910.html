cohousing

adjective
shared living community
5.3
Pronunciation: / koʊˈhaʊzɪŋ / (AmE), / kəʊˈhaʊzɪŋ / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a community where people live in private homes but share common spaces and resources.

Examples

Cohousing project
The new cohousing development encourages residents to participate in community activities.
Many families are attracted to the cohousing lifestyle for its sense of shared responsibility.
A cohousing arrangement often includes communal gardens and kitchens.
