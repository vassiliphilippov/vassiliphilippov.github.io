eyebrow

noun
the line of hair above the eye
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪˌbraʊ / (AmE), / ˈaɪbraʊ / (BrE)
the line or arch of hair growing above the eye on the human face

Examples

Thick eyebrows
His thick eyebrows made his expression look serious.
She raised her eyebrow in surprise when she heard the news.
The makeup artist carefully shaped each eyebrow for the photoshoot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brow - the forehead or the arch of hair above the eye
  • synonym: eyelash - one of the short hairs growing on the edge of the eyelid
