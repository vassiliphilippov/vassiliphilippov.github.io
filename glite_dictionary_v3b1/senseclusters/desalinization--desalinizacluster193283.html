desalinization

noun
removal of salt from water
5.6 5.1
Pronunciation: /diː.seɪ.lɪn.ɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /dɪ.sæl.ɪn.ɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /dɪ.sə.lɪn.ɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /diː.seɪ.lɪn.ɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of removing salt, especially from sea water, to make it suitable for drinking or irrigation
