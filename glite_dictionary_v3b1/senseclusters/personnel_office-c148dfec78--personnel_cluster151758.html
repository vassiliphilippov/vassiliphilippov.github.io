personnel office

noun
department handling employee management
3.5
Pronunciation: / pɚˈsɑːnəl ˈɑːfɪs / (AmE), / pəˈsɒnəl ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
the department in a company or organization responsible for hiring, training, and managing employees, including personnel policies

Examples

The personnel office
All job applications must be submitted to the personnel office by the end of the week.
She contacted the personnel office to inquire about training opportunities within the company.
The personnel office updated its policies to improve employee satisfaction.

Related Terms
