twee

adjective
affectedly quaint or dainty
9.9
Pronunciation: / twi / (AmE), / twi / (BrE)
Excessively or affectedly quaint, dainty, or sentimental.

Examples

A twee style
He found the village too twee for his taste, preferring something less quaint.
Her twee manner of speaking made her sound insincere to some people.
The café was decorated in a twee style, with pastel colors and lace curtains.
