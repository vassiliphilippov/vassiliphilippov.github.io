josser

noun
gullible person
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑsər / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒsə / (BrE)
a person who is easily tricked or deceived; a gullible or naive individual

A josser falls for tricks

Don't act like a josser and fall for obvious scams online.
The clever salesman spotted a josser and quickly convinced him to buy the useless gadget.
