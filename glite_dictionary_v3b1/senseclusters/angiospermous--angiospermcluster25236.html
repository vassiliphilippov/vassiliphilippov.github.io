angiospermous

adjective
relating to flowering plants (angiosperms)
8.5 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌændʒiˈoʊspɚməs / (AmE), / ˌandʒiˈəʊspɜːməs / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of plants that are angiosperms (flowering plants whose seeds are enclosed within a fruit or ovary)

Examples

Angiospermous plants
Botanists often study the evolutionary advantages of angiospermous species over gymnosperms.
Many of the world's crops are angiospermous plants, including rice, wheat, and corn.
The angiospermous structure of this flower distinguishes it from ferns and mosses.
