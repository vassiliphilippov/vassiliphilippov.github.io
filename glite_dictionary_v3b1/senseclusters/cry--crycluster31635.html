cry

noun
an act of weeping
Pronunciation: / kraɪ / (AmE), / kraɪ / (BrE)
an act or session of weeping, typically as an expression of sadness or strong emotion

Examples

A good cry
After hearing the sad news, she had a long cry in her room.
He tried to hold back his tears, but eventually he needed a cry.
Sometimes a good cry can help relieve stress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sob - an act of crying noisily and uncontrollably
