pride of place

noun
most important or prominent place or position
7.5
Pronunciation: / praɪd əv pleɪs / (AmE), / praɪd əv pleɪs / (BrE)
the position of greatest importance or prominence; the most conspicuous or honored part or location

Examples

Pride of place in room
His trophy collection occupies pride of place in the hallway, where everyone can see it.
The architect's latest design was given pride of place in the exhibition.
The family photograph took pride of place on the mantelpiece in the living room.
