banteringly

adverb
in a playful, teasing way
Pronunciation: / ˈbæn.tɚ.ɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈbæn.tər.ɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a playful or teasing manner; using banter.

Examples

To speak banteringly
He always speaks banteringly to his colleagues, making the office atmosphere more relaxed.
She responded banteringly to his teasing remark, showing she wasn't offended.
The team captain addressed the players banteringly, lightening the mood before the match.
