megestrol

noun
synthetic hormonal drug, used in cancer treatment
9.3
Pronunciation: / məˈdʒɛstrɔl / (AmE), / mɪˈdʒɛstrɒl / (BrE)
a synthetic hormonal drug used especially to treat certain cancers such as endometrial carcinoma and to stimulate appetite in specific medical conditions

Examples

Megestrol acetate medicine
Megestrol is often prescribed to increase appetite in patients suffering from severe weight loss due to chronic illness.
Research has shown that megestrol may be effective in improving quality of life for certain cancer patients.
The oncologist explained that megestrol could help manage symptoms of endometrial carcinoma.
