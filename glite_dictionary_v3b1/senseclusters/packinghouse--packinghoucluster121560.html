packinghouse

noun
building for processing and packing food
Pronunciation: / ˈpækɪŋˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈpækɪŋˌhaʊs / (BrE)
a building or factory where food products, especially meat or fruit, are processed and packed for shipment
