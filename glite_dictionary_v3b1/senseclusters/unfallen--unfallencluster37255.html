unfallen

adjective
not conquered
7.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɔlən / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɒlən / (BrE)
not conquered, captured, or brought under enemy control, especially referring to places or groups during conflict.

Examples

Unfallen city
The city stood proud and unfallen despite the enemy's repeated attacks.
After the long campaign, only a few unfallen villages remained in the region.
Historians wrote about the unfallen fortress that resisted every invasion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fallen - conquered or captured, especially in war
  • synonym: unconquered - not defeated or taken by force
