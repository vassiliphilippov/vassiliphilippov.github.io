projector

noun
device for projecting images or video
Pronunciation: / prəˈdʒɛktɚ /; / prəˈdʒɛktər / (AmE), / prəˈdʒɛktə / (BrE)
a device that uses light to project images or video onto a screen or surface for people to see

Examples

A projector device
She struggled with the old projector before finally getting the film to play.
The projector displayed the presentation slides clearly on the wall.
We rented a projector for the outdoor movie night in the park.
Modern offices often have a digital projector for presentations and meetings.
The teacher used a projector to display the lesson on the classroom wall.
We set up the projector in the backyard for an outdoor movie night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overhead projector - device projecting images of transparencies on a screen
  • synonym: screen - surface onto which images are projected
  • synonym: slide projector - device for projecting photographic slides
