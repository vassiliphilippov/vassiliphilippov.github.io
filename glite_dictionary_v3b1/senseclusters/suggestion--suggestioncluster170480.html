Synonyms

  • proposal
    2.9 55/m
    proposal is a formal plan put forward for discussion or decision, while suggestion is a more general idea offered to consider.
  • proposition
    4.2 0.13/m
    proposition is a formal plan or idea put to someone for agreement, while suggestion is a broader, everyday proposal to consider.
  • recommendation
    2.5 45/m
    recommendation is advice that says what someone should do, while suggestion is a neutral idea left for people to consider.
  • proffer
    8.2 0.17/m
    proffer is a formal offer presented for acceptance or rejection, while suggestion is a spoken or written idea for someone to consider.
  • idea
    1.0 552/m
    idea is a thought or plan formed in the mind and not always shared, while suggestion is an idea put forward for others to consider.
