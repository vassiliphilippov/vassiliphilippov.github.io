spiel

noun
glib sales talk or persuasive speech
9.3
Pronunciation: / spiːl / (AmE), / spiːl / (BrE)
a lengthy or glib talk or speech, especially one intended to persuade or promote something

Examples

A sales spiel
After listening to her long spiel, I still wasn't convinced to buy anything.
I quickly recognized his pitch as just another marketing spiel.
The salesman launched into his usual spiel about the benefits of the product.
