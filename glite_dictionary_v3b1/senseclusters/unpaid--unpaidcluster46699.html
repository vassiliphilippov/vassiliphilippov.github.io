unpaid

adjective
done without pay or compensation
3.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpeɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpeɪd / (BrE)
done without receiving payment; work or services provided for free

Examples

Unpaid work
Many charities rely on unpaid volunteers to carry out their work.
She took an unpaid internship to gain experience in the field.
The unpaid extras in the movie enjoyed being part of the production.
Most internships at the organization are unpaid, but they offer valuable experience.
She worked as an unpaid volunteer at the local animal shelter every weekend.
The committee relies heavily on the efforts of unpaid members.

Related Terms

  • antonym: paid - receiving payment or salary
  • synonym: voluntary - done by choice, not for payment
