pyromancy

noun
magic that uses fire
Pronunciation: / \u02c8pa\u026ar\u0259\u02ccm\u00e6nsi / (AmE), / \u02c8p\u026a\u0259r\u0259\u02ccm\u00e6nsi / (BrE)
(in fantasy and gaming) the practice, art, or ability of creating, controlling, or casting magic that uses fire.

Examples

Fantasy pyromancy spells
The young sorcerer practised pyromancy to summon a ring of protective flame during the ritual.
Players specialise in pyromancy when they want high damage over time in the game.
Her spellbook contained ancient pyromancies that called fire elementals from the molten depths.
