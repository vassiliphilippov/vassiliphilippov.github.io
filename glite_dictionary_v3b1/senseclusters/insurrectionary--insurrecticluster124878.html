insurrectionary

adjective
relating to insurrection or rebellion
5.7 8.4
Pronunciation: /ɪn.sə.ˈrek.ʃə.riː/ (AmE), /ɪn.sə.ˈrek.ʃə.riː/ (BrE)
Relating to, characterized by, or inclined to insurrection; associated with rebellion against authority or government.

Examples

Insurrectionary beliefs
During the trial, his insurrectionary beliefs were closely scrutinized by the authorities.
The government declared a state of emergency after several insurrectionary acts occurred in the city.
The group published insurrectionary pamphlets calling people to rise against the regime.
