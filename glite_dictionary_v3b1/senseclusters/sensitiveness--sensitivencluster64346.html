sensitiveness

noun
sensitivity to emotional feelings
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛn.sɪ.tɪv.nəs /; / ˈsɛn.sə.tɪv.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsɪtɪvnɪs /; / ˈsɛnsɪtɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being easily affected by emotions or sensitive to the feelings of others

Examples

Sensitiveness to feelings
He appreciated her sensitiveness to the emotions of those around her.
Her sensitiveness to criticism made her cautious about sharing her opinions.
Teachers must be aware of students' sensitiveness to harsh words and feedback.
He sometimes wishes he could suppress his sensitiveness at work to deal with criticism.
Her sensitiveness made her very aware of her friends' unspoken feelings.
The therapist praised the child's remarkable sensitiveness to other people's emotions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: insensitivity - lack of concern for others' feelings; inability to be easily affected emotionally
  • synonym: sensitivity - the quality of being sensitive; responsiveness to others' feelings or to external stimuli
