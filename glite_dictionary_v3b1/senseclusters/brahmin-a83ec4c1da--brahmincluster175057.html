Brahmin

noun
mutated two-headed cattle (Fallout game)
9.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbrɑː.mɪn/ (AmE), /ˈbrɑː.mɪn/ (BrE)
(in the Fallout video game series) a large, mutated two-headed (or multi-headed) bovine creature kept as livestock and commonly encountered in the game's post-apocalyptic wasteland.

Examples

Brahmin in Fallout
A roaming Brahmin blocked the road, its two heads snapping in all directions.
Farmers in the wasteland kept several Brahmins for milk and meat despite their mutations.
Players shot at the aggressive Brahmin to protect their camp during the night raid.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cattle - domesticated bovine animals; the in-game Brahmin are a fictional, mutated form of cattle.
