ALK

proper noun
cancer-related gene
Pronunciation: / eɪ ɛl keɪ / (AmE), / eɪ ɛl keɪ / (BrE)
a gene and its protein product, anaplastic lymphoma kinase, a receptor tyrosine kinase linked to certain cancers

Examples

ALK gene mutation
The doctor ordered a test to check for ALK mutations in the patient's lung tumor.
Targeted therapies have been developed for cancers with abnormal ALK activity.
Researchers are studying how ALK gene changes contribute to the development of lymphoma.

Related Terms

  • synonym: EGFR - a gene encoding the epidermal growth factor receptor, often mutated in cancers
  • synonym: ROS1 - a gene encoding a receptor tyrosine kinase, associated with some cancers
