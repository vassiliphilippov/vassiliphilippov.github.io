cartwright

noun
maker and repairer of carts and wagons
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrtˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɑːt.raɪt / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make and repair carts and wagons

Examples

A cartwright repairs carts
As a young apprentice, he learned the trade of a cartwright from his father.
His grandfather was a skilled cartwright in the small village.
The museum features tools used by a nineteenth-century cartwright.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wheelwright - a person who makes or repairs wooden wheels for carts and wagons
