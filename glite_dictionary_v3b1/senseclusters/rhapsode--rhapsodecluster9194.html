rhapsode

noun
public poetry performer
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈræpˌsoʊd / (AmE), / ˈræpsəʊd / (BrE)
a person who recites or performs poetry or prose in public, often with dramatic expression

Examples

Public rhapsode performance
The rhapsode captivated the crowd with his passionate delivery of classic poems.
At the festival, a young rhapsode performed original verses to enthusiastic applause.
She dreamed of becoming a rhapsode and sharing her poetry on stages around the world.
