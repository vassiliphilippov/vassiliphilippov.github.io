California beauty

noun
shrub with yellow flowers native to California
Pronunciation: / kælɪˈfɔrnjə ˈbjuːti / (AmE), / kælɪˈfɔːnɪə ˈbjuːti / (BrE)
a handsome evergreen shrub native to California and northern Mexico, with downy lobed leaves and showy yellow flowers

Examples

The California beauty shrub
Botanists admire the striking yellow blossoms of the California beauty.
The California beauty adds a splash of color to many gardens in the region.
We planted a California beauty by the porch for its vibrant flowers and attractive leaves.
