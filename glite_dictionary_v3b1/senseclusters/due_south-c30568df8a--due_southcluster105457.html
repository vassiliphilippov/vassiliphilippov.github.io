due south

noun
compass direction, directly south
2.3 0.44/m
Pronunciation: / duÀê sa äŒ∏ / (AmE), / djuÀê sa äŒ∏ / (BrE)
the direction that corresponds to 180 degrees on a compass; directly south.

Examples

Due south direction
If you walk due south from here, you'll arrive at the river.
The campsite is located exactly due south of the lake.
They headed due south until they reached the coast.
