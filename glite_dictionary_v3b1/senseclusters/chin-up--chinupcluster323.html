chin-up

noun
pull-up exercise using a bar
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪn ʌp / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪn ʌp / (BrE)
An exercise in which a person pulls themselves up with their arms on a horizontal bar until their chin is level with the bar.

Examples

Do ten chin-ups
He does ten chin-ups every morning as part of his workout routine.
I could barely manage a single chin-up in gym class yesterday.
She improved her strength until she could perform multiple consecutive chin-ups.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pull-up - An exercise where a person pulls themselves up until their chin is above a bar, often with palms facing away
