bedgown

noun
loose home garment
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdˌɡaʊn / (AmE), / ˈbɛdˌɡaʊn / (BrE)
a loose, comfortable garment, often worn by women at home, especially in the past

Examples

A bedgown garment
In the chilly morning, she wrapped herself in a warm bedgown before making tea.
The old portrait showed a woman reading by the fire in a simple bedgown.
During the 18th century, it was common for women to wear a bedgown at home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dressing gown - a robe worn over nightwear at home
  • synonym: nightgown - a loose garment worn in bed, especially by women
