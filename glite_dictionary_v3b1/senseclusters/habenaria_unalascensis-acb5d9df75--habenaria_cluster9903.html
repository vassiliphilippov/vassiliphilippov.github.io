Habenaria unalascensis

noun
a North American orchid species
Pronunciation: / həˌbɛnˈɛəriə ˌjuːnəˌlæsˈkɛnsɪs / (AmE), / hæˌbɛnˈɛːrɪə ˌjuːnəˌlæˈskɛnsɪs / (BrE)
A species of orchid native to North America, characterized by smaller flowers and found from Alaska to Baja California and east to the Dakotas and Colorado.

Examples

Habenaria unalascensis orchid species
Habenaria unalascensis is commonly found in moist meadows and open forests in western North America.
Botanists studying alpine ecosystems often encounter Habenaria unalascensis among other native orchids.
The flowers of Habenaria unalascensis are notably smaller than those of the coastal rein orchid.
