steerswoman

noun
woman who steers a ship
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪrzwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈstɪəzˌwʊm.ən / (BrE)
A woman whose job or role is to steer a ship.

Examples

The steerswoman guides
As a skilled steerswoman, she was trusted to handle the yacht in any weather.
During the regatta, the steerswoman kept the crew coordinated and on course.
The steerswoman guided the boat safely through the narrow channel.
