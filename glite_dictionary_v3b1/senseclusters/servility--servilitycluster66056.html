servility

noun
excessive submissiveness
4.1
Pronunciation: / sɚˈvɪl.ɪ.ti / (AmE), / səˈvɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
excessive willingness to serve or please others; abject or cringing submissiveness

Examples

Abject servility
Critics accused the ambassador of servility in his negotiations with the foreign government.
Her servility toward her boss was obvious to everyone in the office.
The ruler demanded servility rather than genuine respect from his subjects.

Related Terms

  • antonym: independence - freedom from the control or influence of others
  • synonym: submissiveness - willingness to submit to the will of others
