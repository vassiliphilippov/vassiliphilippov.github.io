Examples

Abject servility

Synonyms

  • subservience
    8.1 0.44/m
    subservience shows a strong, unquestioning readiness to obey and serve others, while servility adds a cringing, overly eager desire to please.
  • obsequiousness
    9.9 0.07/m
    obsequiousness is praising and flattering others to gain favor, while servility is more about abject, needy willingness to serve or please.
  • submissiveness
    6.0 0.29/m
    submissiveness is a willing acceptance of another's authority or wishes, while servility feels low and cringing in tone.
  • slavishness
    8.5 0.04/m
    slavishness is blind copying or a total lack of independence, while servility focuses on cringing readiness to serve or please others.
