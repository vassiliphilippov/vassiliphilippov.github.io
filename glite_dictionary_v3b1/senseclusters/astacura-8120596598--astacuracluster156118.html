Astacura

noun
former infraorder of crayfish
Pronunciation: / ˌæstəˈkjʊrə / (AmE), / ˌæstəˈkjʊərə / (BrE)
A former infraorder of freshwater crayfish, now generally classified within the infraorder Astacidea.

Examples

The Astacura infraorder
In older scientific literature, crayfish were often grouped within the Astacura infraorder.
Researchers studying freshwater ecosystems sometimes reference historical groupings such as Astacura.
The classification of Astacura has been subject to revision in modern taxonomy.
