ski lift

noun
device transporting skiers uphill
6.9 2.3
Pronunciation: / ski lɪft / (AmE), / skiː lɪft / (BrE)
a mechanical device, often consisting of a cable and seats or cabins, used to transport skiers up a slope

Examples

A ski lift ticket

The ski lift stopped temporarily due to strong winds.
We waited in line for the ski lift to take us to the top of the mountain.
