colorfulness

noun
brightness of colors
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlɚfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈkʌləfʊlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of having bright, vivid, or many different colors

Examples

Colorfulness of the parade
The colorfulness of the parade costumes amazed the children watching from the sidewalk.
She admired the colorfulness of the autumn leaves in the park.
The artist is known for the colorfulness in his abstract paintings.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dullness - the quality of lacking brightness or vividness
  • synonym: vividness - the quality of being very bright or intense in color
