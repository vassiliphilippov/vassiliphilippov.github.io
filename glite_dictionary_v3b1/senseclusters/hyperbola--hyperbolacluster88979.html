hyperbola

noun
open curve in mathematics/conic section
9.4 0.03/m

A type of open curve formed when a plane cuts through both halves of a double cone; each branch is mirror-symmetrical and extends to infinity.

Examples

A hyperbola curve
In mathematics class, we studied the properties of a hyperbola and how it differs from other conic sections.
The intersection of a double cone and a plane at a steep angle produces a hyperbola.
The orbits of some comets are shaped like a hyperbola, allowing them to escape the Sun's gravity.
