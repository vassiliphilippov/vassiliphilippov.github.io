gimlet

noun
small tool for boring holes
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪmlət / (AmE), / ˈɡɪmlɪt / (BrE)
A small hand tool with a pointed screw tip and a cross handle, used for boring holes, especially in wood.

Examples

Wood gimlet tool
Before power tools, carpenters relied on the gimlet for small holes in wood.
He drilled a neat hole in the plank using a gimlet.
The old toolbox contained a rusty gimlet among other traditional tools.

Related Terms

  • synonym: auger - a tool for boring larger holes in wood or earth
