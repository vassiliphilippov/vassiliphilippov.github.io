inbetweener

noun
animation frame artist
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnbɪˈtwiːnər / (AmE), / ˌɪnbɪˈtwiːnə / (BrE)
a person who draws the intermediate frames between key drawings in animation to create smooth motion

Examples

Animation inbetweener
The studio hired an experienced inbetweener to help finish the animated film on time.
As an inbetweener, she carefully drew the frames that made the character's movement look natural.
Many artists start as an inbetweener before becoming a lead animator.

Related Terms

  • synonym: animator - a person who creates animated sequences or characters
  • antonym: key animator - an artist who draws the main frames in animation
