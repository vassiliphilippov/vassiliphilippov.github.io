fund

verb
convert short-term debt into long-term bonds
2.9
Pronunciation: / fʌnd / (AmE), / fʌnd / (BrE)
to convert short-term floating debt into long-term debt that bears fixed interest and is represented by bonds

Examples

To fund debt
Many countries fund their outstanding liabilities through the issuance of long-term bonds.
The central bank funded the nation's floating debt to secure more favorable interest rates.
The government decided to fund its short-term debts in order to stabilize the economy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: refinance - to finance something again, typically replacing an existing loan with a new one
