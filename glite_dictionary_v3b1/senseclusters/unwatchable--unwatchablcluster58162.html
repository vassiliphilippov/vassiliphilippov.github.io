unwatchable

adjective
too disturbing to watch
5.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɑtʃəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɒtʃəbəl / (BrE)
so shocking, disturbing, or unpleasant that people cannot or do not want to watch it

Examples

Unwatchable violence
The horror movie was so graphic that many people found it unwatchable.
Some critics called the violent scenes in the series unwatchable for sensitive viewers.
He turned off the documentary because it was simply unwatchable for him.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disturbing - causing anxiety or discomfort
  • antonym: entertaining - enjoyable and interesting to watch
  • synonym: unbearable - too unpleasant or painful to endure
