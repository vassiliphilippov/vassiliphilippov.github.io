Darvon

noun
brand name for a pain-relieving drug
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːrvɑn / (AmE), / ˈdɑːvɒn / (BrE)
A brand name for a mildly narcotic analgesic drug related to methadone but considered less addictive, used to relieve mild to moderate pain.

Examples

Darvon pain relief
After reports of side effects, Darvon was withdrawn from the market in many countries.
He asked the pharmacist if Darvon was still available for his chronic pain.
The doctor prescribed Darvon to help manage her postoperative pain.
