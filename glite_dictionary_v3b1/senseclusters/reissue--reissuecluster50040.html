reissue

verb
publish or issue again
4.4 3.6
Pronunciation: / riˈɪʃu / (AmE), / riˈɪʃuː /; / riˈɪsju / (BrE)
to publish, release, or distribute something again, sometimes with changes or updates

Examples

To reissue a book
The publisher decided to reissue the classic novel with a new introduction.
The software company plans to reissue the update to fix several bugs.
You can request the bank to reissue your lost credit card.
Many out-of-print books are now reissued through digital platforms.
The publisher decided to reissue the novel with a new introduction and cover art.
They plan to reissue the band's classic album on vinyl next year.
