preseason

noun
training period before sports season
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpriˌsiːzən / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌsiːzn / (BrE)
the period before the start of an official sports season, used for practice and preparation

Examples

Preseason games
Players often compete for starting positions in preseason games.
The coach was satisfied with their performance throughout the preseason.
The team worked on new strategies during the preseason.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postseason - period after the regular sports season
