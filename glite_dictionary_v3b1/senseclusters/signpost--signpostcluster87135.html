signpost

noun
post showing directions or information
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪnˌpoʊst / (AmE), / ˈsaɪnˌpəʊst / (BrE)
a post with a sign, especially one that shows directions or gives information about a place

Examples

A signpost with directions
A new signpost was installed to help tourists navigate the town.
The village was easy to find thanks to the clear signpost at the crossroads.
We followed the signpost to reach the hiking trail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guidepost - a post with signs showing directions
