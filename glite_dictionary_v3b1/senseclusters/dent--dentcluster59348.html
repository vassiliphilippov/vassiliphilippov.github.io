dent

verb
make a hollow or depression
5.7
Pronunciation: / dɛnt / (AmE), / dɛnt / (BrE)
to make a hollow or depression in the surface of something by hitting or pressing it

Examples

To dent the surface
Be careful not to dent the refrigerator door when moving it.
She accidentally dented the side of the filing cabinet with her chair.
The accident dented the front bumper of my car.
