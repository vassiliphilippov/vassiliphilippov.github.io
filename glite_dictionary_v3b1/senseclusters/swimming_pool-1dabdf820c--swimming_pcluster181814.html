swimming pool

noun
large artificial pool for swimming
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪmɪŋ puːl / (AmE), / ˈswɪmɪŋ puːl / (BrE)
A large structure filled with water, designed for people to swim in.

Examples

A swimming pool area
Children splashed and played in the swimming pool during the hot afternoon.
The hotel has an outdoor swimming pool that guests can use year-round.
We installed a heated swimming pool in our backyard last summer.
