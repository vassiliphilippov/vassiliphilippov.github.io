applesauce

noun
nonsense; silly talk
Pronunciation: / ˈæpəlˌsɔs / (AmE), / ˈap(ə)lˌsɔːs / (BrE)
nonsense talk or writing; words without real meaning or value.

Examples

That is applesauce
Don't listen to that applesauce—stick to the facts.
Everything he said about the project was pure applesauce and didn't make any sense.
She dismissed the rumors as nothing more than applesauce.
