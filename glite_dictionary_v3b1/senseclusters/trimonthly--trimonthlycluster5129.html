trimonthly

noun
three-month publication
Pronunciation: / traɪˈmʌnθli / (AmE), / traɪˈmʌnθli / (BrE)
a magazine, journal, or publication that is issued once every three months

Examples

A trimonthly magazine
The science club publishes a trimonthly filled with research updates and interviews.
She subscribes to a trimonthly that covers the latest trends in technology.
Each trimonthly features a special section dedicated to reader questions and answers.
