adermin

noun
B vitamin important in metabolism
8.7
Pronunciation: / əˈdɜːrmɪn / (AmE), / əˈdɜːmɪn / (BrE)
a B vitamin, also known as vitamin B6, that is essential for the metabolism of amino acids and starch

Examples

Adermin vitamin B6
Adermin deficiency can result in neurological disorders and skin problems.
Doctors may prescribe adermin supplements to treat certain vitamin deficiencies.
