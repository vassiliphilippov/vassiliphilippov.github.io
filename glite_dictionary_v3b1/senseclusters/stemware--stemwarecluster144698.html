stemware

noun
glasses with stems
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛmˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈstɛmˌweə / (BrE)
drinking glasses with a stem and base, such as wine glasses or champagne flutes

Examples

Fine stemware glasses

After the party, she carefully washed each piece of stemware by hand.
He bought a new set of crystal stemware for special occasions.
