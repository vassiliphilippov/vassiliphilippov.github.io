builder

noun
no-code software tool
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪldər / (AmE), / ˈbɪldə / (BrE)
(in computing) a software tool or online platform that allows users to create websites, apps, or other digital content without writing code, typically using visual interfaces, templates, or drag-and-drop components.

Examples

Website builder
She used a builder to design her personal website without coding.
The startup launched a drag-and-drop builder that lets small businesses create online stores quickly.
While learning web design, he experimented with a visual builder to prototype page layouts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: no-code platform - a platform that enables creation of software or applications without traditional programming.
  • synonym: website builder - a tool or service for creating websites, often with templates and drag-and-drop editing.
