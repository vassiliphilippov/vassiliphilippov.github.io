bare-knuckle

adjective
without protection
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛrˌnʌkəl / (AmE), / ˈbeəˌnʌkəl / (BrE)
done without gloves, protection, or safety measures, especially in fighting or competition

Examples

Bare-knuckle fight
The fighters entered the ring for a bare-knuckle contest that drew a large crowd.
He admired her bare-knuckle approach to business negotiations, never backing down from a challenge.
Old bare-knuckle boxing matches were often much more dangerous than modern bouts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gloved - wearing gloves, especially for protection
  • synonym: unprotected - not protected or shielded
