bare-knuckle

adjective
without protection
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛrˌnʌkəl / (AmE), / ˈbeəˌnʌkəl / (BrE)

Examples

Bare-knuckle fight

He admired her bare-knuckle approach to business negotiations, never backing down from a challenge.
Old bare-knuckle boxing matches were often much more dangerous than modern bouts.
