flute

noun
high-pitched woodwind instrument
1.5 6/m
Pronunciation: / fluÀêt / (AmE), / fluÀêt / (BrE)
a high-pitched woodwind instrument consisting of a slender tube closed at one end, with finger holes and an opening near the closed end across which the breath is blown
