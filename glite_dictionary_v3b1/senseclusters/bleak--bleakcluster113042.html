bleak

adjective
unpleasantly cold and damp
5.1 4.1
Pronunciation: /bliːk/ (AmE), /bliːk/ (BrE)
(of weather or a place) cold and damp in an unpleasant or depressing way.

Examples

They trudged through the bleak landscape, shivering in the cold drizzle.
