Atlantic bonito

noun
species of tuna-like food fish
8.2 6.3
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈlæn.tɪk bə.ˈniː.toʊ/ /; / /æt.ˈlæn.tɪk bə.ˈniː.toʊ/ (AmE), /ə.ˈlæn.tɪk bə.ˈniː.təʊ/ /; / /æt.ˈlæn.tɪk bə.ˈniː.təʊ/ (BrE)
A medium-sized, tuna-like food fish (Sarda sarda) found in warm waters of the Atlantic Ocean and the Mediterranean, valued less than tuna.

Examples

The Atlantic bonito fish
Compared to tuna, Atlantic bonito has a milder flavor and softer texture.

The chef prepared grilled Atlantic bonito with lemon and herbs for the guests.
