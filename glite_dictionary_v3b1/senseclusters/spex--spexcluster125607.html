spex

noun
student comedy show
9.9
Pronunciation: / spɛks / (AmE), / spɛks / (BrE)
a comedic musical or theatrical performance, often satirical and featuring songs and sketches, traditionally staged by university students in Sweden and Finland

Examples

Student spex show
The university's annual spex drew a sold-out crowd with its witty songs and colourful costumes.
Many students auditioned for this year's spex, hoping to perform in the popular comedy sketches.
Attending a spex is a beloved tradition for students in Sweden and Finland.

  • synonym: revue - a light theatrical entertainment with songs, dances, and sketches
