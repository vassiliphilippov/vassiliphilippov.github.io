emergent

noun
plant above surface
7.0
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈmɜːr.dʒənt/ /; / /ˈiː.mər.dʒənt/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈmɜː.dʒənt/ (BrE)
a plant or organism that rises above the surrounding surface, especially above water or other vegetation

Examples

Tall emergents
Tall emergents like reeds and cattails are common in this marsh.
The biologist studied the emergents that towered above the water lilies.
Many birds nest in the branches of emergents growing in the wetland.
