disty

noun
informal distributor
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪsti / (AmE), / ˈdɪsti / (BrE)
an informal term for a distributor, especially a company that supplies goods to retailers or resellers

Examples

An electronics disty
The electronics disty delivered the new smartphones to stores ahead of the holiday rush.
Our company works closely with a reliable disty to ensure fast product distribution.
If the disty runs out of stock, retailers may face empty shelves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distributor - a company that supplies goods to retailers or resellers
  • antonym: retailer - a business that sells goods directly to consumers
