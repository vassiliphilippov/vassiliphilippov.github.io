biomorphic

adjective
resembling living forms
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪoʊˈmɔːrfɪk / (AmE), / ˌbaɪəʊˈmɔːfɪk / (BrE)
having shapes or forms that resemble living organisms or natural life forms

Examples

Biomorphic shapes
The artist created a biomorphic painting inspired by the shapes of plants and animals.
Modern furniture often features biomorphic designs that mimic the curves of nature.
The museum displayed several biomorphic sculptures that looked like living creatures.

Related Terms

  • antonym: geometric - having shapes based on mathematical forms like circles or squares
  • synonym: organic - having forms or shapes similar to those found in nature
