deportable

adjective
able to be moved
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɔːrtəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈpɔːtəbəl / (BrE)
able to be transported or carried away; movable (archaic or rare)

Examples

Deportable goods
In the 18th century, only deportable goods could be shipped overseas.
The old law referred to certain property as deportable, meaning it could be removed from the premises.
Because of its fragile design, the antique vase was not deportable without professional packing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: immovable - not able to be moved
  • synonym: portable - able to be easily carried or moved
