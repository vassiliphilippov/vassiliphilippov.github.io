how

interjection
expressing surprise
1.0
Pronunciation: / haʊ / (AmE), / haʊ / (BrE)
used as an exclamation before an adjective or clause to express surprise or strong emotion about degree or quality

Examples

How amazing
How amazing it is to see the city lights from this viewpoint!
How quickly the weather changed caught everyone by surprise.
How generous of you to help us move on such short notice!
