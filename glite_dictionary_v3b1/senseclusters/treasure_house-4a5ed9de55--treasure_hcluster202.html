treasure house

noun
place where valuables are stored
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛʒər haʊs / (AmE), / ˈtrɛʒə haʊs / (BrE)
a building or place where valuable objects or treasures are stored

Examples

A treasure house of jewels
Centuries ago, kings hid their jewels in a secret treasure house beneath the castle.
The library is a true treasure house of ancient knowledge.
The museum is a treasure house of rare manuscripts and artifacts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: repository - a place where things are stored and can be found
  • synonym: storehouse - a building for storing goods
