remainder

noun
piece of leftover material or cloth
5.7 2.3
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmeɪndɚ /; / rɪˈmeɪn.dɚ / (AmE), / rɪˈmeɪndə /; / rɪˈmeɪn.də / (BrE)
the part of something that is left after the rest has been used, removed, or taken away

Examples

The remainder of the cake
Only a small remainder of blue velvet was left on the shelf.
She picked up a colorful remainder from the cloth store for her craft project.
The shop sold the remainder of the fabric roll at a discount.
After dividing the cake, only a small remainder was left for later.
She donated most of her clothes and kept the remainder for herself.
The remainder of the day was spent reading and relaxing.
