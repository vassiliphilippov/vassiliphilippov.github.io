concentration

noun
intense mental focus
1.0 2.8
Pronunciation: /kɑːn.sən.ˈtreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /kɒn.sən.ˈtreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act or ability of focusing all your attention or mental effort on something

Examples

Concentration on work
His concentration on the chess game was so intense he didn't notice the time passing.
Noise in the office makes concentration difficult for many employees.
She needs complete concentration when studying for her exams.
Athletes require intense concentration during important competitions.
Noise from outside made concentration almost impossible during the meeting.
She studied with complete concentration for hours before the exam.
