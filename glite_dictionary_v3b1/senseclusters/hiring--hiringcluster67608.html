hiring

verb
to employ someone
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪɚɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
present participle of hire: to employ someone for a job or position.

Examples

Hiring new staff
The company is hiring new engineers to expand its research team.
She has been hiring seasonal workers every summer for her shop.
Many businesses are hiring more staff to meet increased demand during the holidays.

Related Terms

  • antonym: firing - to dismiss someone from a job
  • synonym: recruiting - to find and attract people for a job
