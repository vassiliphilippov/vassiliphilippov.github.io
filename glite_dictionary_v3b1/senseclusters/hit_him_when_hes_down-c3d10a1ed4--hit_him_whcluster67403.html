hit him when he's down

verb
criticize or attack someone when vulnerable
Pronunciation: / hɪt hɪm wɛn hiz daʊn / (AmE), / hɪt hɪm wɛn hiːz daʊn / (BrE)
to attack or criticize someone when they are already in a weakened or vulnerable state

Examples

To hit him when he's down hard
Criticizing him after the loss would just hit him when he's down.
Her harsh words really hit him when he's down during a tough time.
It's unfair to hit him when he's down instead of helping him recover.
