operative

noun
private detective or agent
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɑp(ə)rətɪv /; / ˈɑːpərətɪv / (AmE), / ˈɒp(ə)rətɪv /; / ˈɒpərətɪv / (BrE)
a person who works as a detective, agent, or spy, especially for a government

Examples

Secret operative
As a government operative, she worked undercover in several high-profile cases.
Private detectives and other operatives assisted the police during the investigation.
The agency sent an experienced operative to follow the suspect.
After years as an operative, she finally retired from the world of espionage.
No one suspected that he was actually an undercover operative working for the government.
The intelligence agency sent an operative to gather information on the foreign diplomats.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agent - person who does secret or undercover work, often for a government
  • synonym: detective - a person who investigates crimes and gathers information
  • synonym: spy - a person who secretly collects and reports information about others
