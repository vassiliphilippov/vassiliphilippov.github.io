nickelodeon

noun
early movie theater
Pronunciation: / ˌnɪkəˈloʊdiən / (AmE), / ˌnɪkəˈləʊdiən / (BrE)
a small, early 20th-century movie theater that charged five cents for admission, often showing silent films

Examples

Silent nickelodeon theater
In the early 1900s, families would visit the nickelodeon to enjoy the latest silent films.
The old building downtown was once a bustling nickelodeon filled with eager moviegoers.
For just five cents, you could watch a short film at the local nickelodeon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cinema - a place where movies are shown to an audience
  • synonym: theater - a building where plays or movies are presented
