stater

noun
ancient Greek gold or silver coin
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪtər / (AmE), / ˈsteɪtə / (BrE)
an ancient Greek coin made of silver or gold, used as a standard unit of currency in various Greek city-states

Examples

The value of a single stater could support a family for weeks in ancient Greece.
