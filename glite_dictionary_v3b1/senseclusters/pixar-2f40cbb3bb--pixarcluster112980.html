Pixar

noun
animated film by Pixar
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪksɑr / (AmE), / ˈpɪksɑː / (BrE)
an animated film produced by the Pixar company, often known for high-quality storytelling and animation

Examples

A Pixar movie
We watched a classic Pixar last night and everyone loved the animation.
Every new Pixar seems to set a higher standard for animated movies.
My kids want to see the latest Pixar as soon as it comes out.
