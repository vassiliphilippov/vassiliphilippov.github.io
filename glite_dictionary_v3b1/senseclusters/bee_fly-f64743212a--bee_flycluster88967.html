bee fly

noun
nectar-eating fly resembling a bee
4.9 6.9
Pronunciation: /biː flaɪ/ (AmE), /biː flaɪ/ (BrE)
A hairy, nectar-feeding fly that resembles a bee; its larvae are parasitic on the larvae of bees and related insects.

Examples

A bee fly can be mistaken for a real bee due to its fuzzy appearance and flight pattern.
