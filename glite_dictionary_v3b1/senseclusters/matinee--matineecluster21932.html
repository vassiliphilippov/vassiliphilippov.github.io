matinee

noun
daytime theatre performance
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌmæ.tɪˈneɪ / (AmE), / ˈmæt.ɪ.neɪ / (BrE)
A theatrical performance or entertainment held during the daytime, especially in the afternoon.

Examples

A matinee show
Children are often taken to a matinee because the earlier time is more convenient for families.
The Saturday matinee was sold out within hours of the box office opening.
We decided to attend the matinee so we could have dinner afterward.
