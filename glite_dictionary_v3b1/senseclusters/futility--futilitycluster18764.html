futility

noun
pointlessness; uselessness
Pronunciation: / fjuˈtɪlɪti / (AmE), / fjʊˈtɪlɪti / (BrE)
The fact or quality of being pointless or ineffective; uselessness, especially because no result can be achieved.

Examples

A futility of effort
After several failed attempts, the group felt a sense of futility.
He realized the futility of arguing with someone who refused to listen.
The novel explores the futility of trying to change the past.

Related Terms

  • antonym: usefulness - the quality of being useful or able to achieve a practical result
  • synonym: uselessness - the quality of having no useful result or effect
