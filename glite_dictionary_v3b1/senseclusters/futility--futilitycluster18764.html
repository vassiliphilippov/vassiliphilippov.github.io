The fact or quality of being pointless or ineffective; uselessness, especially because no result can be achieved.

Examples

A futility of effort
After several failed attempts, the group felt a sense of futility.
He realized the futility of arguing with someone who refused to listen.
The novel explores the futility of trying to change the past.

Synonyms

  • ineffectiveness
    5.4 0.67/m
    ineffectiveness shows a lack of ability to produce a desired result, while futility means actions fail because no outcome is possible.
  • uselessness
    4.3 0.48/m
    uselessness describes having no practical value or use, while futility focuses on efforts that cannot produce any real result.
  • pointlessness
    6.2 0.14/m
    pointlessness means something has no purpose or meaning, while futility stresses that trying will not achieve the intended result.
