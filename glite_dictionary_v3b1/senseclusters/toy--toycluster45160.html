toy

noun
a very small breed of pet dog
6.4
Pronunciation: / tɔɪ / (AmE), / tɔɪ / (BrE)
a very small breed of dog, kept mainly as a pet; often used to refer to dogs in the toy group distinguished by their small size

Examples

A toy dog
Her favorite breed is the Yorkshire Terrier, which is classified as a toy.
Many people choose a toy for apartment living due to their small size.
The toy was so tiny that it could fit in the palm of her hand.
