diffusionist

adjective
based on diffusion
8.4
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfjuːʒənɪst / (AmE), / dɪˈfjuːʒənɪst / (BrE)
relating to or based on the process of diffusion, especially the spreading of something from one place to another.

Examples

Diffusionist theory
The diffusionist approach explains how cultural traits spread between societies.
Scientists used a diffusionist model to predict the movement of gases in the experiment.
Her research is grounded in diffusionist theories about the spread of technology.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diffusive - tending to spread out or move from an area of high concentration to low concentration
