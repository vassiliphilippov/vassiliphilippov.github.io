landed estate

noun
large privately owned property (usually rural)
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlændɪd ɪˈsteɪt / (AmE), / ˈlændɪd ɪˈsteɪt / (BrE)
an extensive area of land, typically in the countryside, owned and retained for the exclusive use of one person, family, or organization

Examples

A large landed estate
He spent his childhood on a vast landed estate in the English countryside.
Maintaining a landed estate requires significant resources and careful management.
The family inherited a landed estate that has been in their name for centuries.
