positionality

noun
importance of position
9.4
Pronunciation: / pəˌzɪʃəˈnæləti / (AmE), / pəˌzɪʃəˈnæləti / (BrE)
the importance or advantage that comes from a particular position or arrangement, especially in a strategic or competitive context

Examples

Positionality in a game
The positionality of the players on the field can determine the outcome of the match.
In business negotiations, understanding your positionality can help you make better decisions.
Chess masters often discuss the positionality of pieces rather than just their immediate value.
