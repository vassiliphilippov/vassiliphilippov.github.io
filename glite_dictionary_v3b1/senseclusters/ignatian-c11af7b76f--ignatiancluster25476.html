Ignatian

adjective
of St. Ignatius or Jesuits
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪɡˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
relating to St. Ignatius of Loyola or the Jesuit order founded by him

Examples

Ignatian spirituality
The university follows an Ignatian approach to education, emphasizing service and reflection.
Many students participate in Ignatian retreats to deepen their spiritual lives.
The Ignatian tradition encourages finding God in all things.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Jesuit - relating to the Society of Jesus or its members
