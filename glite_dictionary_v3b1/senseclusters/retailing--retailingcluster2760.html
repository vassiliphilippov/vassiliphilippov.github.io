retailing

noun
selling goods directly to consumers
3.0 2.5
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈteɪ.lɪŋ/ /; / /riː.ˈteɪ.lɪŋ/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈteɪ.lɪŋ/ /; / /riː.ˈteɪ.lɪŋ/ (BrE)
