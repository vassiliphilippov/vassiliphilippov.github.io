layback

noun
reclining position
6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈleɪ.bæk/ (AmE), /ˈleɪ.bæk/ (BrE)
a backward-leaning or reclining position, often for balance, relaxation, or in certain sports techniques

Examples

A relaxing layback
After the long hike, he enjoyed a relaxing layback on the grassy hill.
The climber used a layback technique to move up the crack in the rock face.
She settled into a comfortable layback on the sofa to watch her favorite show.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sit up - to move from a lying or reclining position to a sitting position
