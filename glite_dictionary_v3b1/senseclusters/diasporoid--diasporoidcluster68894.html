diasporoid

noun
one feeling disconnected from homeland or community
Pronunciation: / daɪˈæspəˌrɔɪd / (AmE), / daɪˈaspərɔɪd / (BrE)
a person who feels disconnected from their community or homeland, often due to being part of a diaspora or feeling rootless

Examples

A diasporoid person
After moving abroad, she sometimes saw herself as a diasporoid among her new friends.
His poetry explores the mindset of a perpetual diasporoid, longing for a place to call home.
Many diasporoids seek out cultural groups to regain a sense of belonging.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diaspora - a group of people living outside their ancestral homeland
