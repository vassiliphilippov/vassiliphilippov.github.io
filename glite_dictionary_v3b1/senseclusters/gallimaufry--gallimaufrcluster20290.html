gallimaufry

noun
motley assortment; miscellaneous collection
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɡælɪˈmɔːfri / (AmE), / ˌɡælɪˈmɔːfri / (BrE)
A confused or miscellaneous collection; a motley assortment of things.

Examples

A gallimaufry of leftovers
For dinner, she served a gallimaufry of leftovers collected over the week.
His notes were a gallimaufry of ideas, sketches, and half-finished poems.
The museum's latest exhibition is a gallimaufry of paintings, sculptures, and photographs from various eras.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hodgepodge - a confused mixture; a jumble
  • synonym: medley - a varied mixture of things; a miscellany
  • antonym: order - the opposite of disorder or jumble; a regular arrangement
