kitten

noun
young domestic cat
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪt̬n̩ / (AmE), / ˈkɪt.ən / (BrE)
a young domestic cat, especially one less than a year old

Examples

A playful kitten
As the kitten grew, it became more playful and curious around the house.
She adopted a stray kitten from the animal shelter last weekend.
The children were thrilled to see the newborn kitten sleeping in the basket.
