frig

noun
informal refrigerator
8.0
Pronunciation: / frɪɡ / (AmE), / frɪɡ / (BrE)
an informal variant spelling of 'fridge', meaning a refrigerator used to keep food and drinks cold

Examples

A cold frig
She opened the frig to grab a cold drink before heading outside.
We need to clean out the frig because something smells bad in there.
Can you check if there is any milk left in the frig?
