adscription

noun
assignment or affiliation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ædˈskrɪpʃən / (AmE), / ædˈskrɪpʃən / (BrE)
the state of being assigned or attached to a person, place, group, or position, often by birth or authority

Examples

Adscription to a group
Her adscription to the royal household limited her career choices throughout her life.
The adscription of new members to the committee was decided by the board.
In some societies, social status is determined by adscription rather than achievement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: affiliation - the state of being officially attached to a group or organization
  • synonym: assignment - the act of being given a specific role or position
  • antonym: separation - the act of being removed or detached from a group or position
