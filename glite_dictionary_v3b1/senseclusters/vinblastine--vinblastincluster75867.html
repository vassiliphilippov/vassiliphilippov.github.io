vinblastine

noun
anticancer drug from periwinkle plant
8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈvɪn.blæs.tiːn/ /; / /vɪn.ˈblæs.tiːn/ (AmE), /ˈvɪn.blæs.tiːn/ /; / /vɪn.ˈblæs.tiːn/ (BrE)
