humerus

noun
bone of the upper arm
9.9 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈhjuː.mə.rəs/ /; / /ˈhjuː.mə.rɪs/ (AmE), /ˈhjuː.mə.rəs/ /; / /ˈhjuː.mə.rɪs/ (BrE)
The long bone in the upper arm or forelimb, extending from the shoulder to the elbow.

Examples

The humerus bone
A break in the humerus can seriously affect arm movement.
The surgeon examined the humerus to determine the extent of the fracture.
X-rays revealed a clean break in her upper humerus.
