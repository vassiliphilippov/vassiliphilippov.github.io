katha

noun
South Asian land unit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːtə / (AmE), / ˈkɑːtə / (BrE)
a traditional unit of area used in parts of South Asia, especially India, Bangladesh, and Nepal, with a value that varies by region

Examples

A katha of land
They bought two katha of land to start their new farm.
In some regions, a katha can mean a different area, so always check local measurements.
The price of one katha of land has increased significantly in recent years.
