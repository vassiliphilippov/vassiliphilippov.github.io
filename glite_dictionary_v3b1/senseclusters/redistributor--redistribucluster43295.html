redistributor

noun
one who resells products
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˌriːdɪˈstrɪbjətər /; / ˌriːdɪˈstrɪbjutər / (AmE), / ˌriːdɪˈstrɪbjʊtə /; / ˌriːdɪˈstrɪbjuːtə / (BrE)
a person or organization that distributes something again or in a new way, such as products, content, or goods

Examples

A redistributor of goods
The company became a leading redistributor of open-source software in the region.
As a redistributor, she specialized in packaging digital content for educational institutions.
Many small businesses act as a redistributor by buying products in bulk and selling them to local shops.
The nonprofit acts as a redistributor of donated clothes to families in need.
A central redistributor collects surplus food and sends it to local shelters.
As a redistributor, the agency ensures resources reach the most vulnerable communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distributor - a person or company that supplies goods to stores or other businesses
  • antonym: manufacturer - a person or company that makes goods for sale
  • synonym: retailer - a business or person that sells goods to the public
