macassar

noun
striped tropical hardwood
Pronunciation: / məˈkæsər / (AmE), / məˈkæsə / (BrE)
a dark, richly striped tropical hardwood, also known as Macassar ebony, used for furniture and decorative objects

Examples

Macassar wood furniture
The cabinet was crafted from rare macassar, giving it a luxurious appearance.
Collectors value furniture made with macassar for its unique striped pattern.
The artist chose macassar for the sculpture because of its striking dark and light streaks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ebony - a dense black hardwood from several tree species
  • synonym: mahogany - a reddish-brown tropical hardwood used for furniture
