yat

noun
obsolete Cyrillic letter
Pronunciation: / jæt / (AmE), / jæt / (BrE)
a letter formerly used in the Old Church Slavonic and early Cyrillic alphabets, now obsolete

Examples

Linguists study the historical use of yat in Slavic manuscripts.
You can still find the yat in old Church Slavonic texts.
