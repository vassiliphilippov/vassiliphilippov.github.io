all-devouring

adjective
eating both plants and animals
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɔl.dɪˈvɔɹ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌɔːl.dɪˈvɔː.ɹɪŋ / (BrE)
(of animals) eating both plants and animals; omnivorous.

Examples

All-devouring animals
In the forest, raccoons exhibit all-devouring habits, eating everything from plants to insects to eggs.
Some bears are all-devouring creatures, consuming berries as well as small animals when available.
The all-devouring appetite of pigs allows them to survive in many different environments.
