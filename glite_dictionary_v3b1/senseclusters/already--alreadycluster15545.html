already

adverb
before a specified or expected time
Pronunciation: / ɔlˈrɛdi / (AmE), / ɔːlˈrɛdi / (BrE)
before the time that has been mentioned or was expected; by this or that time.

Examples

Have you already finished
Are you already finished with your homework?
He was surprised to find that the meeting had already started.
She had already left when I arrived at the party.

Related Terms

  • antonym: yet - up to now or until the present time
