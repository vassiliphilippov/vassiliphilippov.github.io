sensitive

noun
emotionally responsive person
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnsətɪv / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsətɪv / (BrE)
a person who is easily affected by the feelings, moods, or needs of others

Examples

The sensitive in class
Among the group, the sensitive was always the first to notice when someone felt left out.
Many sensitives find crowded places overwhelming because they pick up on so many emotions.
As a sensitive, she often needs time alone to recover from social events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: empath - a person who can deeply understand or feel others' emotions
  • antonym: stoic - a person who does not show or feel much emotion
