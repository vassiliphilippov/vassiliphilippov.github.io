James II

proper noun
King of England, Scotland, and Ireland (1685–1688)
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒeɪmz səˈkənd / (AmE), / ˌdʒeɪmz ðə ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
King of England, Scotland, and Ireland from 1685 to 1688, the last Stuart to hold the English throne, deposed in the Glorious Revolution.

Examples

James II king
