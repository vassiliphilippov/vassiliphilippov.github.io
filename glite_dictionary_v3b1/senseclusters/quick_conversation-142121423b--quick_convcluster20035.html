quick conversation

noun
brief, informal chat or discussion
Pronunciation: / kwɪk ˌkɑnvərˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / kwɪk ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃən / (BrE)
a brief and informal exchange of words or ideas

Examples

Sometimes a quick conversation is all it takes to clear up a misunderstanding.
We had a quick conversation in the hallway before the meeting began.
