superfinishing machine

noun
machine for fine surface finishing in manufacturing
7.4 7.6
Pronunciation: /suː.pər.ˈfɪn.ɪ.ʃɪŋ mə.ˈʃiːn/ /; / /ˈsuː.pər.fɪn.ɪ.ʃɪŋ mə.ˈʃiːn/ (AmE), /suː.pə.ˈfɪn.ɪ.ʃɪŋ mə.ˈʃiːn/ /; / /ˈsuː.pə.fɪn.ɪ.ʃɪŋ mə.ˈʃiːn/ (BrE)
a machine tool used to improve the surface finish and geometry of a workpiece by removing a thin surface layer with an abrasive stone or tape, after previous finishing processes

Examples

A superfinishing machine tool
After grinding, the workpieces were processed through a superfinishing machine for a mirror-like finish.
Technicians must calibrate the superfinishing machine regularly to ensure optimal performance.
The new superfinishing machine significantly improved the surface quality of the metal rods.
