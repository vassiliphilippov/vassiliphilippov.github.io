dance music

noun
music made for dancing
3.0 1.3
Pronunciation: /dæns ˈmjuː.zɪk/ /; / /dɑːns ˈmjuː.zɪk/ (AmE), /dɑːns ˈmjuː.zɪk/ /; / /dæns ˈmjuː.zɪk/ (BrE)
