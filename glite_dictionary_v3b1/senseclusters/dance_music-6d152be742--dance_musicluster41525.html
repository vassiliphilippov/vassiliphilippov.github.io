dance music

noun
music made for dancing
3.0
Pronunciation: / dæns ˈmjuː.zɪk /; / ˈdæns ˌmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / dɑːns ˈmjuː.zɪk /; / ˈdɑːns ˌmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
music with a strong rhythm, made for people to dance to, often played at parties or social events
