misrecognition

noun
failure to recognize
8.1
Pronunciation: /mɪs.rek.ə.ˈgnɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /mɪs.rek.ə.ˈgnɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act or process of failing to correctly identify or recognize someone or something

Examples

Face misrecognition
The security system failed due to misrecognition of the employee's face.
Cases of misrecognition in eyewitness testimony can lead to wrongful convictions.
Automatic translation tools sometimes suffer from misrecognition of spoken words.

  • antonym: recognition - the act of correctly identifying someone or something
