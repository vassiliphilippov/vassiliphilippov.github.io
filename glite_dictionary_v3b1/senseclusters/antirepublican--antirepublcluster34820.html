antirepublican

adjective
opposed to republicanism
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪrɪˈpʌblɪkən / (AmE), / ˌæntɪrɪˈpʌblɪkən / (BrE)
opposed to republican principles or to the Republican party

Examples

Antirepublican views
The organization released an antirepublican manifesto criticizing the current government.
His antirepublican views were evident in his speeches against the party's policies.
Some newspapers published antirepublican articles following the controversial election results.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antimonarchist - opposed to monarchy or monarchist principles
  • antonym: prorepublican - supporting republican principles or the Republican party
