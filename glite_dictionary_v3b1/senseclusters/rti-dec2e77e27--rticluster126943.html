RTI

noun
right to information law
5.2
Pronunciation: /ɑːr tiː aɪ/ (AmE), /ɑː tiː aɪ/ (BrE)
a law or policy that allows people to access information held by government bodies

Examples

RTI request

The RTI process helps citizens hold the government accountable for its actions.
Many journalists use RTI applications to investigate government decisions.
