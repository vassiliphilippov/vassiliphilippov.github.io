dumbfound

verb
to astonish or bewilder
Pronunciation: / ˌdʌmˈfaʊnd / (AmE), / ˌdʌmˈfaʊnd / (BrE)
to greatly astonish or bewilder someone; to make someone so surprised or confused that they are unable to respond

Examples

To dumbfound someone
Her response to the difficult question dumbfounded the interviewer.
I was completely dumbfounded when I heard the unexpected news.
The magician's final trick dumbfounded the entire audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astonish - to surprise greatly
  • synonym: bewilder - to cause someone to become perplexed or confused
  • antonym: enlighten - to give someone greater knowledge or understanding about a subject
