dumbfound

verb
to astonish or bewilder
3.7 5.6
Pronunciation: /dʌm.ˈfaʊnd/ /; / /ˈdʌm.faʊnd/ (AmE), /dʌm.ˈfaʊnd/ /; / /ˈdʌm.faʊnd/ (BrE)
