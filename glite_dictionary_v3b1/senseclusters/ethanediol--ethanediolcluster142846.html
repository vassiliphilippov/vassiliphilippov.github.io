ethanediol

noun
a toxic liquid antifreeze and solvent
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌiːθəˈniːˌdaɪˌɑl / (AmE), / ˌiːθəˈniːˌdʌɪɒl / (BrE)
a sweet, colorless, poisonous liquid used chiefly as antifreeze and as a solvent
