clunker

noun
failure or dud
5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈklʌŋ.kər/ (AmE), /ˈklʌŋ.kə/ (BrE)
a thing, especially a vehicle or product, that is very poor in quality or a complete failure.

Examples

A clunker product
The old car he bought for cheap turned out to be a real clunker and broke down constantly.
Everyone was disappointed when the new phone model was a clunker with many problems.
Despite the hype, the play was a clunker and closed after just one week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dud - something that fails to work properly or is unsuccessful
  • synonym: lemon - a product, especially a car, that is defective or unsatisfactory
  • antonym: success - something that achieves a desired result or is very good
