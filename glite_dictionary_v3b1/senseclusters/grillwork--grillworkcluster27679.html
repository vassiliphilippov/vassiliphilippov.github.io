grillwork

noun
decorative or functional mesh of crossed bars or wires
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɪlˌwɝk / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪlˌwɜːk / (BrE)
an ornamental or functional structure made of crossed bars, wires, or strips, used as a screen, grate, or decorative feature

Examples

He admired the intricate grillwork on the garden gate as he passed by.
The ornate grillwork on the old bank's windows was crafted by a local blacksmith.
Wrought iron grillwork added an elegant touch to the facade of the building.
