dogface

noun
butterfly with dog-like markings
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔɡˌfeɪs / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡˌfeɪs / (BrE)
a North American butterfly (Zerene cesonia) with wing markings that resemble a dog's face

Examples

Yellow dogface butterfly
The bright yellow dogface fluttered among the wildflowers in the summer meadow.
We spotted a rare dogface resting with its wings open, showing the unique pattern.
Many nature enthusiasts hope to see a dogface during butterfly watching trips in North America.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sulphur butterfly - a butterfly of the subfamily Coliadinae, often yellow or orange
