headquarters

noun
main office of an organization
3.0 18/m
Pronunciation: / Ààh…õdÀåkw…îrt…ôrz / (AmE), / Ààh…õdÀåkw…îÀêt…ôz / (BrE)
The main office or center of operations for an organization, where administrative activities are managed.

Examples

Company headquarters
During the crisis, all decisions came directly from military headquarters.
She was transferred to headquarters after five years in regional offices.
The company's global headquarters are located in London.

Synonyms

  • HQ
    3.4 1.55/m
    HQ is an abbreviation used in speech and writing for the same site, while headquarters is the full formal word.
  • main office
    1.5 0.58/m
    main office is the place for daily administrative work in a company, while headquarters covers overall company control and official base.
  • central office
    3.5 0.82/m
    central office handles administrative tasks for the company center, while headquarters is the main decision and command center.
  • home office
    3.0 1.79/m
    home office often names the main administrative center in formal or British use, while headquarters is the organization's main base.
  • home base
    3.4 0.59/m
    home base sounds informal and often military or sports, serving as a local starting point, while headquarters is the formal company center.
