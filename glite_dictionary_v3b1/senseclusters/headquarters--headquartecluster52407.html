headquarters

noun
main office of an organization
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌkwɔrtərz / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌkwɔːtəz / (BrE)
The main office or center of operations for an organization, where administrative activities are managed.

Examples

Company headquarters
During the crisis, all decisions came directly from military headquarters.
She was transferred to headquarters after five years in regional offices.
The company's global headquarters are located in London.

Related Terms

  • antonym: branch - a local office or subsidiary of a larger organization
