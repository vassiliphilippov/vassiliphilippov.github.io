decerebrate

verb
remove the cerebrum surgically
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɛrəˌbreɪt /; / diˈsɛrəˌbreɪt / (AmE), / dɪˈsɛrəˌbreɪt /; / diˈsɛrɪbreɪt / (BrE)
to remove or destroy the brain or part of the brain, especially for scientific or medical purposes
