Georgian

noun
person from the US state of Georgia
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔrdʒən / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːdʒən / (BrE)
a native or resident of the US state of Georgia

Examples

A proud Georgian
Many Georgians are proud of their Southern heritage and hospitality.
She became a Georgian after moving to Atlanta with her family.
The football team was cheered on by hundreds of loyal Georgians.
