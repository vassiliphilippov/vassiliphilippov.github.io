hoghead

noun
insult for a coarse person
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑɡˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈhɒɡˌhɛd / (BrE)
an informal and often offensive term for a stupid or coarse person

Examples

He is a hoghead
He called his brother a hoghead after he broke the window playing ball inside.
Don't be such a hoghead and listen to what I'm saying for once.
The coach shouted at the hoghead who kept ignoring the team's strategy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blockhead - a person who is stupid or slow to understand
  • synonym: fool - a person lacking good sense or judgment
  • antonym: gentleman - a polite or refined man
