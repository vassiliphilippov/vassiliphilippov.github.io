cut

noun
a hurtful or wounding remark
5.3
Pronunciation: / kʌt / (AmE), / kʌt / (BrE)
a remark or comment that is intended to hurt someone's feelings; an emotionally wounding statement

Examples

A sharp cut
He couldn't ignore the stinging cut she delivered during dinner.
Her sarcastic cut lingered in his mind long after their conversation ended.
The comedian's clever cut drew laughter from the audience, but embarrassment from the target.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compliment - a polite expression of praise or admiration
  • synonym: insult - a disrespectful or scornful remark
