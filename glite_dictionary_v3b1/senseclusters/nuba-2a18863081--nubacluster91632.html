Nuba

proper noun
people of Sudan
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːbə / (AmE), / ˈnjuːbə /; / ˈnuːbə / (BrE)
a member of, or the group of, indigenous peoples living in the Nuba Mountains region of Sudan
