caramel

adjective
moderate yellow-brown color
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkærəˌmɛl / (AmE), / ˈkærəməl / (BrE)
of a moderate yellow-brown color, resembling that of caramelized sugar.

Examples

Caramel colored eyes
His eyes were a striking caramel color in the sunlight.
She wore a caramel sweater that complemented her skin tone perfectly.
The new sofa comes in a rich caramel shade, adding warmth to the room.

  • degree higher: chocolate - deep brown color, like chocolate
  • synonym: tan - pale tone of brown, used for color
