courtyard

noun
open area surrounded by buildings or walls
1.0 1.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkɔːr.tjɑːrd/ /; / /ˈkɔːrt.jɑːrd/ (AmE), /ˈkɔː.tjɑːd/ /; / /ˈkɔːt.jɑːd/ (BrE)
