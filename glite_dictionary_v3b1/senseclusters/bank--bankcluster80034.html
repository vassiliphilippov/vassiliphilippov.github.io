bank

noun
lateral tilting of an aircraft in flight
8.6
Pronunciation: / bæŋk / (AmE), / bæŋk / (BrE)
A flight maneuver in which an aircraft tips laterally about its longitudinal axis, especially when turning.

Examples

A bank in flight
During the airshow, the pilot executed a sharp bank to the left.
Sudden banks can cause discomfort for some passengers.
The aircraft maintained a steady bank as it circled the airport.
