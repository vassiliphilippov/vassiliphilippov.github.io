cedi

noun
Ghanaian currency unit
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪdi / (AmE), / ˈsiːdi / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Ghana, equal to 100 pesewas.

Examples

The cedi currency
He exchanged his dollars for cedi before traveling to Ghana.
The cedi has remained stable against major world currencies this year.
The price of the book is twenty cedis in Accra.
