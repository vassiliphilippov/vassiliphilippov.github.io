rush hour

noun
busy commuting times of day
2.3 2.0
Pronunciation: /rʌʃ ˈaʊ.ər/ /; / /rʌʃ ˈaʊr/ (AmE), /rʌʃ ˈaʊ.ə/ (BrE)
the times at the beginning and end of the working day when traffic is heavy because many people are traveling to or from work

Examples

During rush hour traffic
It can take twice as long to get to work during rush hour.
On Fridays, rush hour usually starts earlier because people leave work sooner.
We try to schedule meetings after rush hour to avoid heavy traffic.
