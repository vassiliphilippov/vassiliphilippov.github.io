light

adjective
moderate or limited in consumption or habit
Pronunciation: / laɪt / (AmE), / laɪt / (BrE)
not consuming or doing something in large amounts; moderate in one's habits or intake

Examples

Light smoker
Although he used to smoke heavily, he's now just a light smoker.
She is a light eater and rarely has large meals.
We enjoyed a light supper before the concert.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abstemious - not self-indulgent, especially with food or drink
  • antonym: heavy - doing something in large amounts, e.g., 'a heavy drinker' or 'heavy eater'
