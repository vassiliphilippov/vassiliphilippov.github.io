caladium

noun
tropical ornamental plant with colorful leaves
Pronunciation: / kəˈleɪdiəm / (AmE), / kəˈleɪdɪəm / (BrE)
A tropical plant of the genus Caladium, grown for its large, colorful, and decorative leaves often patterned in white, pink, or red.

Examples

A caladium plant
Many gardeners love caladiums for their strikingly patterned foliage.
She planted a caladium in the shaded corner of her garden for a splash of color.
The vibrant leaves of the caladium brightened up the patio all summer long.
