Buddhist

noun
follower of Buddhism
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːdɪst / (AmE), / ˈbʊdɪst / (BrE)
a person who follows the teachings of Buddha or practices Buddhism

Examples

A Buddhist monk
As a Buddhist, he strives to follow the principles of compassion and nonviolence.
Her grandmother is a devout Buddhist who meditates every morning.
Many Buddhists observe the practice of mindfulness in their daily lives.
