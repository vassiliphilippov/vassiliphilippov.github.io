refreshed

adjective
feeling renewed energy or freshness
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfrɛʃt / (AmE), / rɪˈfrɛʃt / (BrE)
feeling full of renewed energy, strength, or freshness after rest or relaxation

Examples

Feel refreshed after sleep
After a good night's sleep, she felt completely refreshed and ready to tackle the day.
He took a short break and came back to work looking refreshed.
The shower left him refreshed after a long hike.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exhausted - very tired, lacking energy
  • synonym: rested - feeling relaxed and no longer tired after resting
