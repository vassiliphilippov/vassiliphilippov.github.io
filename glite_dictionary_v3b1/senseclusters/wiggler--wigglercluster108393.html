wiggler

noun
mosquito larva
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪɡlər / (AmE), / ˈwɪɡlə / (BrE)
the larva of a mosquito, often seen moving with wiggle-like motions in standing water

Examples

Mosquito wiggler
A microscope revealed hundreds of tiny wigglers swimming in the water sample.
Birds feed on wigglers before they develop into adult mosquitoes.
The pond was full of wigglers right after the rainy season began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: larva - an immature form of an insect that undergoes metamorphosis
