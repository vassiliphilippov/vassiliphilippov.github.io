interfold

adjective
nested or interleaved
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntərˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈɪntəˌfəʊld / (BrE)
folded or arranged so that separate sheets or layers are nested or interleaved for easy dispensing

Examples

Interfold napkins
The dispenser uses interfold napkins so you can easily pull out one at a time.
Many paper towels are interfold to make them more convenient for public restrooms.
The packaging contains interfold tissues that are simple to grab without touching the rest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interleaved - arranged with alternating layers or sheets
  • antonym: stacked - placed one on top of another without interleaving
