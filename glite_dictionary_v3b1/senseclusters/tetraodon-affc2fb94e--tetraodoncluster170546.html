Tetraodon

noun
pufferfish genus
Pronunciation: / tɛˈtrɑːədɑn / (AmE), / tɛˈtræədɒn / (BrE)
a type of pufferfish belonging to the genus Tetraodon, often kept in freshwater or brackish aquariums

Examples

A Tetraodon fish
The aquarium featured a rare Tetraodon that fascinated visitors with its unique shape.
Many hobbyists enjoy keeping Tetraodon species because of their interesting behavior.
A single Tetraodon can be the highlight of a well-maintained freshwater tank.
