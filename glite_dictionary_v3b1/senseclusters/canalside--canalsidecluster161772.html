canalside

adjective
beside a canal
Pronunciation: / kəˈnælˌsaɪd / (AmE), / kəˈnælˌsaɪd / (BrE)
situated beside or next to a canal, especially referring to buildings or locations on the bank of a canal.

Examples

Canalside house
They enjoyed a peaceful walk along the canalside path in the evening.
A new apartment complex was built on the canalside, offering beautiful water views.
The canalside market attracts many visitors every weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lakeside - situated beside a lake
  • synonym: riverside - located beside a river
