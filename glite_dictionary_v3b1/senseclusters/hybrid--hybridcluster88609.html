hybrid

noun
car using two types of power sources
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪ.bɹɪd / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ.brɪd / (BrE)
a car that is powered by both an internal combustion engine and an electric motor, typically to improve fuel efficiency and reduce emissions

Examples

Hybrid car
More drivers are choosing a hybrid to save on fuel costs and reduce their carbon footprint.
She traded in her old sedan for a hybrid last summer.
The new hybrid offers a smooth transition between electric and gasoline power.

Related Terms

  • antonym: electric car - a car powered exclusively by electricity
  • synonym: hybridize - to produce a hybrid; to combine two different things
