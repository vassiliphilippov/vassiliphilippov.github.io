go through with

verb
finish something despite difficulty
1.1
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ ˈθruː wɪð / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ˈθruː wɪð / (BrE)
to do or complete something you have planned or promised, especially when it is difficult or you have doubts
