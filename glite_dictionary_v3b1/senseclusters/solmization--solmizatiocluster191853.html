solmization

noun
naming musical notes with syllables
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊlməˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌsoʊlmɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌsɑlməˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɒlmɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the system or act of using syllables (like do, re, mi) to name the notes of a musical scale, especially for teaching or singing
