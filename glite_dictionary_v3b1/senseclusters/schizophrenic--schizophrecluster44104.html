schizophrenic

adjective
contradictory behavior
Pronunciation: / ˌskɪtsəˈfrɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌskɪtsəˈfriːnɪk / (BrE)

Examples

Schizophrenic behavior
The company's schizophrenic approach to marketing confused both customers and employees.
Her schizophrenic mood swings made it difficult to predict how she would react.
The weather this week has been truly schizophrenic, changing from sunshine to storms within hours.
