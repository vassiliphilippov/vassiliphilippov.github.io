budget-zilla

noun
project that greatly overspends
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌdʒɪtˌzɪlə / (AmE), / ˈbʌdʒɪtˌzɪlə / (BrE)
an informal term for a project or venture that dramatically exceeds its intended budget; a financial undertaking that becomes much more expensive than planned

Examples

A budget-zilla project
Nobody anticipated the software upgrade would be a budget-zilla when we first approved it.
Our new marketing campaign is turning into a budget-zilla, costing us double what we planned.
The construction became a budget-zilla after months of unexpected expenses and delays.
