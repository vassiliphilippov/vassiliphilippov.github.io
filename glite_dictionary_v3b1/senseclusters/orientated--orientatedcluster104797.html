orientated

adjective
adjusted in relation to surroundings or purpose
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːr.i.ənˈteɪ.t̬ɪd / (AmE), / ˌɔː.ri.ənˈteɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
adjusted or positioned in relation to surroundings, circumstances, or a specific purpose; often used in combination (e.g., value-orientated)

Examples

Value-orientated approach
Our discussion was more environmentally orientated than last year's meeting.
The new employee quickly became orientated to the company's workflow.
This training program is heavily skills-orientated to prepare students for the job market.
