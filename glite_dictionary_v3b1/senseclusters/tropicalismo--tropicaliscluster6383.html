tropicalismo

noun
Brazilian art movement
Pronunciation: / ˌtroʊpɪkəˈlizməʊ / (AmE), / ˌtrɒpɪkəˈlɪzməʊ / (BrE)
a Brazilian artistic movement that blends traditional Brazilian culture with modern and international influences, especially in music and art

Examples

Tropicalismo music style
The influence of tropicalismo can be heard in the band's experimental rhythms and lyrics.
Many artists in the 1960s embraced tropicalismo to challenge traditional cultural norms in Brazil.
The exhibition explores how tropicalismo changed the direction of Brazilian music and visual arts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: modernism - an artistic movement embracing new ideas and forms
  • synonym: tropicália - another name for the tropicalismo movement in Brazil
