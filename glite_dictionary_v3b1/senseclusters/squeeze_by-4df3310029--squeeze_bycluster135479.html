squeeze by

verb
manage to survive with difficulty
4.3
Pronunciation: / skwiz baɪ / (AmE), / skwiːz baɪ / (BrE)
to manage to survive or get by with only just enough resources, money, or effort

Examples

To squeeze by barely
After his hours were cut, he barely squeezed by until he found a new job.
Even with two jobs, she can only squeeze by each month.
Many students squeeze by on a tight budget while at university.
