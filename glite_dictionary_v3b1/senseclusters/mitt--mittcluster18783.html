mitt

noun
fingerless glove
1.8
Pronunciation: / mɪt / (AmE), / mɪt / (BrE)
a glove that covers the hand but does not have separate sections for each finger, except for the thumb; usually worn for warmth or protection

Examples

A warm mitt
He put on a thick mitt before shoveling snow in the driveway.
The child lost one mitt while playing outside in the winter.
Baseball players often use a special mitt to catch the ball.
He put on a thick mitt before shoveling snow from the driveway.
The chef wore a heat-resistant mitt to take the tray out of the oven.
She lost one mitt while playing outside in the snow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: glove - a piece of clothing that covers the hand and has separate sections for each finger
  • synonym: mitten - a glove with one section for the thumb and one for the other fingers
