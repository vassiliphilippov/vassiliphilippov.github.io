votive

noun
small ceremonial candle
Pronunciation: / ˈvoʊtɪv / (AmE), / ˈvəʊtɪv / (BrE)
a small candle, often used in religious ceremonies or for making wishes or offerings

Examples

Votive candle
She placed a votive on the altar and whispered a silent prayer.
Dozens of votives flickered in the church during the evening service.
He bought a scented votive to light during meditation at home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: candle - a stick of wax with a wick for burning to give light
  • synonym: offering - something given as a religious gift or sacrifice
