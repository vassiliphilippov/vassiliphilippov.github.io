ciabatta

noun
Italian white bread
4.2
Pronunciation: / tʃəˈbɑtə / (AmE), / tʃaˈbatə / (BrE)
a type of Italian white bread with a crisp crust and a light, airy texture

Examples

A ciabatta loaf
She bought a fresh ciabatta loaf to serve with dinner.
The sandwich was made with grilled vegetables and slices of ciabatta.
At the bakery, I always choose ciabatta for its airy texture and crisp crust.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baguette - a long, thin French bread with a crisp crust
  • synonym: focaccia - a flat Italian bread often seasoned with olive oil and herbs
