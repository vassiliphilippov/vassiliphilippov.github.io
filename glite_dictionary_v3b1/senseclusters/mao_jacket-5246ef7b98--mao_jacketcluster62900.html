Mao jacket

noun
Chinese jacket with high collar
2.0 6.4
Pronunciation: /maʊ ˈdʒæk.ɪt/ /; / /maʊ ˈdʒæk.ət/ (AmE), /maʊ ˈdʒæk.ɪt/ /; / /maʊ ˈdʒæk.ət/ (BrE)
