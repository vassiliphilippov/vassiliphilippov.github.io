Synonyms

  • take a picture
    1.4 8/m
    take a picture is the common everyday phrase people use to make an image, while photograph sounds more formal and appears in writing.
  • shoot
    3.2 18/m
    shoot can mean taking a photo or filming with a camera, while photograph usually refers to making a still image.
  • snap
    2.8 2/m
    snap means taking a quick, informal photo in the moment, while photograph is a neutral verb for making a picture.
