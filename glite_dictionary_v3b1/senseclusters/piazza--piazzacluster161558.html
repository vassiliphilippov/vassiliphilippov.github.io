piazza

noun
public square or open marketplace
7.4
Pronunciation: / piˈɑːtsə / (AmE), / piˈætsə / (BrE)
a public square, especially in an Italian town, open area or marketplace for people to gather

Examples

A piazza square
Many local festivals are held in the historic piazza every summer.

We enjoyed our morning coffee at a little café on the main piazza.
