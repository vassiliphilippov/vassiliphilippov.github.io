Neptunian

adjective
of Neptune
8.6
Pronunciation: / nɛpˈtuːniən / (AmE), / nɛpˈtjuːniən / (BrE)
relating to the planet Neptune or the Roman god Neptune

Examples

Neptunian planet
The Neptunian atmosphere is composed mainly of hydrogen, helium, and methane.
Mythology often describes Neptunian powers as controlling the seas and storms.
Scientists are fascinated by the intense winds found on Neptunian planets.
