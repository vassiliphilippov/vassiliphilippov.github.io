lose one's touch

verb
to lose a previously held ability or skill
3.1 6.0
Pronunciation: / luːz wʌnz tʌtʃ / (AmE), / luːz wʌnz tʌtʃ / (BrE)
to lose the ability or skill that one once had, usually in a particular activity or field

Examples

To lose his touch

I feel like I've lost my touch when it comes to baking cakes.
She used to win every negotiation, but lately she's afraid she's losing her touch.
