minimize

verb
downplay importance
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnɪˌmaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪmaɪz /; / ˈmɪnɪˌmaɪz / (BrE)
to make something seem less important or serious; to play down the significance of something

Examples

To minimize problems
It is wrong to minimize the challenges that people with disabilities face.
She tried to minimize the impact of the scandal during the press conference.
The company spokesperson attempted to minimize the environmental damage caused by the new factory.
She tried to minimize the impact of her mistake during the meeting.
The politician sought to minimize the seriousness of the scandal in his speech.
We should not minimize the challenges that lie ahead.

Related Terms

  • synonym: belittle - to make someone or something seem unimportant
  • synonym: downplay - to make something seem less important
  • antonym: exaggerate - to make something seem more important or significant
