assurgent

adjective
rising upward, especially from the sea
5.3
Pronunciation: / əˈsɜrdʒənt / (AmE), / əˈsɜːdʒənt / (BrE)
rising upward, especially from the sea; ascending, as depicted in heraldry or marine biology.

Examples

Assurgent island

The family crest features a dolphin assurgent against a blue shield.
