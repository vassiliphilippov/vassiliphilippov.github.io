heat-releasing

adjective
emitting heat in a reaction or process
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːt rɪˌliːsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhiːt rɪˌliːsɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a chemical reaction or substance) occurring or formed with the release of heat

Examples

Heat-releasing reaction
Scientists studied the heat-releasing properties of the new compound in the laboratory.
The combustion of gasoline is a heat-releasing reaction that powers car engines.
When calcium oxide reacts with water, the heat-releasing process can be felt through the container.

Related Terms

  • antonym: endothermic - (of a process or reaction) accompanied by the absorption of heat
  • synonym: exothermic - (of a process or reaction) accompanied by the release of heat
