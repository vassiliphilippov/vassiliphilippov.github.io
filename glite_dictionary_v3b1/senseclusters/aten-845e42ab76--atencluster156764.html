Aten

noun
ancient Egyptian sun god
9.9 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…ëÀêt…õn / (AmE), / Àà…ëÀêt…õn / (BrE)
the sun or solar disc worshipped as a deity in ancient Egypt, especially as the focus of a monotheistic cult under Pharaoh Akhenaten

Examples

The Aten sun god
Depictions of the Aten show rays of sunlight ending in hands reaching down to the royal family.
Scholars still debate the nature of the Aten cult and its brief period of prominence in Egypt.
The ancient Egyptians under Akhenaten worshipped the Aten as the sole god.
