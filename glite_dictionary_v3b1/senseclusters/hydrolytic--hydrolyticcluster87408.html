hydrolytic

adjective
involving hydrolysis
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪdrəˈlɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪdrəˈlɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving hydrolysis, a chemical process in which a compound is broken down by water

Examples

Hydrolytic reaction
The enzyme catalyzed a hydrolytic reaction that split the molecule into two parts.
Certain detergents work through hydrolytic processes to break down stains.
Researchers studied the hydrolytic activity of the new compound in water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hydrolysis - a chemical reaction where water breaks down a compound
