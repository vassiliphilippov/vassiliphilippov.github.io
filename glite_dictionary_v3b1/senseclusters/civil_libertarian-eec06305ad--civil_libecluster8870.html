civil libertarian

noun
someone who advocates civil liberties
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪvəl ˌlɪbəˈtɛriən / (AmE), / ˈsɪvəl ˌlɪbəˈtɛːrɪən / (BrE)
a person who actively supports the protection and expansion of civil liberties, often advocating minimal government interference in personal freedoms

Examples

A civil libertarian activist
As a civil libertarian, she voiced concerns about the government's surveillance programs.
Many civil libertarians argued against the new security law for restricting free speech.
The debate attracted civil libertarians from across the country to discuss individual rights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: libertarian - a person advocating liberty and minimal government intervention
