administrative

adjective
relating to administration or management
Pronunciation: / ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪtɪv / (AmE), / ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪtɪv / (BrE)
relating to the organization, management, or running of a business, institution, or other enterprise.

Examples

Administrative work
She works in an administrative role at the university, managing schedules and paperwork.
The school requires more support staff for its administrative functions.
There are high administrative costs associated with running a large corporation.
