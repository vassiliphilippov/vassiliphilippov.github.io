intrepidity

noun
fearless courage; bravery
9.7
Pronunciation: / ɪn.trəˈpɪd.ə.ti / (AmE), / ɪn.trəˈpɪd.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
brave or fearless courage; resolute fearlessness in the face of danger or difficulty

Examples

The intrepidity of explorers
Few explorers have matched the intrepidity of those who first crossed the Antarctic.
Her intrepidity in the face of overwhelming odds inspired everyone on the team.
The journalist was praised for her intrepidity in reporting from dangerous conflict zones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bravery - courageous behavior or character
  • antonym: cowardice - lack of bravery; timidity
