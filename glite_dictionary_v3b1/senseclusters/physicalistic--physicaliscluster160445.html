physicalistic

adjective
relating to physicalism
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪzɪkəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌfɪzɪkəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to the belief or doctrine that everything can be explained in terms of physical matter and laws

Examples

Physicalistic explanation
The philosopher defended a physicalistic explanation for mental processes in his latest book.
Many scientists adopt a physicalistic perspective when studying the origins of life.
Her research challenges the physicalistic view that consciousness is only a product of the brain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dualistic - relating to the belief that reality consists of two fundamental substances, such as mind and matter
  • synonym: materialistic - relating to the belief that only physical matter exists
