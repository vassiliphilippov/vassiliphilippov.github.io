Teflon layer

noun
non-stick coating layer (esp. in cookware)
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛflɑn ˈleɪər / (AmE), / ˈtɛflɒn ˈleɪə / (BrE)
a layer of Teflon applied as a non-stick coating, especially on cookware surfaces

Examples

The Teflon layer
Always avoid using metal utensils to preserve the Teflon layer on your pots and pans.
Over time, the Teflon layer can wear out if abrasive sponges are used.
The Teflon layer on the frying pan makes cleaning up afterward very easy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: non-stick coating - a coating that prevents substances from sticking to surfaces, often used in cookware
