hieroglyphics

noun
unreadable writing
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪks / (AmE), / ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪks / (BrE)
writing or symbols that are difficult to read or understand, often appearing mysterious or unintelligible.

Examples

Hieroglyphics handwriting
The doctor's notes looked like hieroglyphics to anyone who tried to read them.
After the rain smudged the ink, the address on the envelope became hieroglyphics.
My little brother's homework was covered in hieroglyphics that only he could understand.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clarity - the quality of being clear and easy to understand
  • synonym: gibberish - unintelligible or meaningless writing or speech
  • synonym: scrawl - messy or illegible handwriting
