come down

verb
become ill, usually with 'with'
Pronunciation: / kʌm daʊn / (AmE), / kʌm daʊn / (BrE)
(of a person) to become ill with a particular disease or condition, usually used with 'with'.

Examples

Come down with a cold
He started to come down with a sore throat on Monday.
Many students came down with a cold after the exam period.
She might come down with the flu if she's not careful.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contract - to catch or develop a disease
  • antonym: recover - to return to a normal state of health
