lantern

verb
to light with lanterns
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈlæn.tərn/ (AmE), /ˈlæn.tən/ (BrE)
to provide with a lantern or lanterns, or to light or mark with a lantern.

Examples

To lantern a path
They lanterned the garden walkway every evening during the festival.
The old street was beautifully lanterned for the holiday celebration.
Volunteers lanterned the path to guide visitors safely at night.
