striking

noun
the act of hitting or making contact
Pronunciation: / ˈstraɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstraɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of hitting or making contact between two things, especially with force.

Examples

Striking of the bell
Her hand was sore from the striking of the punching bag during training.
Repeated striking of the bell signaled the end of the match.
The striking of the clock could be heard throughout the quiet house.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hit - a successful strike or contact between two things
  • antonym: miss - a failure to make contact
