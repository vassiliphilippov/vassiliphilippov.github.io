overextension

noun
excessive joint movement
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪkˈstɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪkˈstɛnʃən / (BrE)
the movement of a joint or limb beyond its normal range, often resulting in strain or injury

Examples

Elbow overextension
The athlete's sudden overextension of her elbow led to a painful injury.
Physical therapists warn against overextension during stretching exercises to prevent damage.
He experienced overextension in his wrist after lifting heavy weights incorrectly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flexion - bending a joint to decrease the angle between bones
  • synonym: hyperextension - extension of a joint beyond its normal limit
