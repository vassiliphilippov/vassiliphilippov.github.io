MiB

noun
unit of digital information (1,048,576 bytes)
7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈme.bɪ.baɪt/ /; / /mɪb/ /; / /em aɪ biː/ (AmE), /ˈme.bɪ.baɪt/ /; / /mɪb/ /; / /em aɪ biː/ (BrE)
a unit of information used in computing, equal to 1,048,576 bytes or 1024 kibibytes

Examples
