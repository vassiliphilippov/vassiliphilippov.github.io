beyond words

adjective
indescribably beautiful or impressive
1.0
Pronunciation: / biˈjɑnd wɝdz / (AmE), / bɪˈjɒnd wɜːdz / (BrE)
so beautiful, impressive, or emotionally affecting that it cannot be described in words; indescribable.

Examples

Feelings beyond words
His generosity in our time of need was truly beyond words.
Standing at the edge of the Grand Canyon, the view was simply beyond words.
The artist's performance was beyond words and left the audience in awe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indescribable - too unusual, extreme, or beautiful to be described satisfactorily
