cloudbusting

noun
weather modification method
Pronunciation: / ˈklaʊdˌbʌstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklaʊdˌbʌstɪŋ / (BrE)
a method or attempt to influence or disperse clouds, often to cause rain or change the weather

Examples

Cloudbusting for rain
The team used cloudbusting in hopes of bringing rain to the dry region.
Some scientists remain skeptical about the effectiveness of cloudbusting techniques.
After several attempts at cloudbusting, the weather finally changed and it started to rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cloud seeding - a technique to encourage precipitation by dispersing substances into clouds
  • synonym: weather modification - the act of artificially changing weather conditions
