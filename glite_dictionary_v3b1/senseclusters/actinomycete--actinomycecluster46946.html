actinomycete

noun
a type of bacteria from Actinomycetales
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌæk.tɪ.noʊˈmaɪ.siːt / (AmE), / ˌæk.tɪ.nəʊˈmʌɪ.siːt / (BrE)
any of a group of bacteria belonging to the order Actinomycetales, some of which are pathogenic to humans and animals

Examples

Actinomycetes bacteria group
Researchers are studying actinomycetes for their ability to produce antibiotics.
Soil rich in actinomycetes can have a distinctive earthy smell due to their metabolic activity.
The doctor explained that the infection was caused by a rare actinomycete bacterium.
