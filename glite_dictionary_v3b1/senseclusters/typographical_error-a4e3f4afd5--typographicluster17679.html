typographical error

noun
mistake in printed material
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˌtaɪpəˈɡræfɪkəl ˈɛrər / (AmE), / ˌtʌɪpəˈɡrafɪkəl ˈɛrə / (BrE)
An error in printed material caused by a mistake in typing, setting, or mechanical printing processes.

Examples

A typographical error in text
A single typographical error can change the meaning of a legal document.
Please let us know if you spot any typographical errors in this book.
The newspaper printed an apology for the typographical error in yesterday's headline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: typo - an abbreviated form of 'typographical error', common in informal usage
