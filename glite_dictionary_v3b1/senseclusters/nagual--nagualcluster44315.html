nagual

noun
shape-shifting folk figure
9.9
Pronunciation: / nɑːˈwɑːl / (AmE), / nəˈwɑːl / (BrE)
in Mesoamerican folklore, a person believed to have the power to transform into an animal

Examples

A nagual transforms
According to legend, a nagual can take the form of a jaguar or other animal.
The villagers believed the mysterious events were caused by a nagual living nearby.
Stories about the nagual have been passed down for generations in rural Mexico.
