maidan

noun
public square
9.1
Pronunciation: / maɪˈdɑːn / (AmE), / maɪˈdɑːn / (BrE)
a public square or large open area in a city, especially used for gatherings, markets, or demonstrations in Eastern Europe and South Asia

Examples

City maidan market
The city's main maidan was filled with people celebrating the national holiday.
Vendors set up their stalls every morning at the bustling maidan in the center of town.
During the protest, thousands gathered peacefully in the maidan to demand change.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: park - a public green area in a town, used for recreation
  • synonym: plaza - a public square or open area in a city
  • synonym: square - an open area in a city, often used for public events
