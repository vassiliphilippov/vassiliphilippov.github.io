external iliac artery

noun
major artery branching to the thigh
5.9
Pronunciation: /ɪk.ˈstɜːr.nəl ˈɪl.ɪ.æk ˈɑːr.tə.riː/ /; / /ɛk.ˈstɜːr.nəl ɪ.ˈlaɪ.æk ˈɑːr.tə.riː/ (AmE), /ɪk.ˈstɜː.nəl ˈɪl.ɪ.æk ˈɑː.tə.riː/ /; / /ɛk.ˈstɜː.nəl ɪ.ˈlaɪ.æk ˈɑː.tə.riː/ (BrE)
either of the two main arteries located on each side of the body that branch from the common iliac artery and continue as the femoral artery in the thigh.

Examples
