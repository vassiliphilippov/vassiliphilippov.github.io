stiltedness

noun
unnatural formality
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪltɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈstɪltɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being stiff, unnatural, or overly formal in style, speech, or manner

Examples

Stiltedness in speech
The stiltedness of the conversation made it difficult for anyone to relax.
Critics noted the stiltedness in the actor's performance during the opening scene.
Her writing lost its stiltedness as she became more confident in her style.

Related Terms

  • synonym: formality - the quality of being formal or stiff in manner
  • antonym: naturalness - the quality of being natural and relaxed
