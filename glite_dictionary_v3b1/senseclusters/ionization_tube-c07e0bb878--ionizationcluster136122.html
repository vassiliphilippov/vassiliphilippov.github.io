ionization tube

noun
instrument for measuring ionizing radiation
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪənaɪˈzeɪʃən tuːb / (AmE), / ˌaɪənaɪˈzeɪʃən tjuːb / (BrE)
a device used to measure the amount of ionizing radiation by detecting ions produced within a gas-filled tube

Examples

Ionization tube device
Accurate readings from an ionization tube are crucial for nuclear facility monitoring.
Routine safety checks include testing with an ionization tube to ensure there are no harmful radiation leaks.
The scientist used an ionization tube to measure the radioactivity in the laboratory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Geiger counter - an instrument for detecting and measuring ionizing radiation, also based on ionization in a tube
