diathermic

adjective
producing therapeutic heat
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪəˈθɜrmɪk / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəˈθɜːmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or producing heat in body tissues by means of high-frequency electric currents, especially in medical treatment.

Examples

Diathermic instrument
The surgeon used a diathermic instrument to stop the bleeding during the operation.
A diathermic treatment can help relieve deep muscle pain in some patients.
Modern physiotherapy clinics often use diathermic devices for therapeutic heating.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diathermy - the medical technique of heating body tissues using high-frequency electric currents
  • synonym: thermogenic - producing heat, especially in the body
