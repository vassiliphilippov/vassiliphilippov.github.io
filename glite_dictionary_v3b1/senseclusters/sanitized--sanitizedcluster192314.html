Synonyms

  • sanitary
    2.8 1/m
    sanitary is used to describe places or tools kept clean for health, while sanitized emphasizes that something was actively cleaned.
  • sterile
    4.4 2/m
    sterile means completely free of microbes due to special methods, while sanitized means cleaned to reduce germs, not always totally sterile.
  • germfree
    4.4 0.00/m
    germfree describes something with no germs at all, often informal, while sanitized describes an item that was cleaned to remove germs.
  • uncontaminated
    5.2 0.1/m
    uncontaminated means not made impure by pollution or contact, while sanitized implies a deliberate cleaning to kill germs.
