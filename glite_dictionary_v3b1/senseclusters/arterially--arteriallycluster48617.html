arterially

adverb
by way of an artery
7.7
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˈtɪriəli / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriəli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to or by way of an artery

Examples

Arterially injected medicine
The medication was delivered arterially to reach the affected tissue quickly.
Doctors sometimes inject contrast dye arterially during certain imaging procedures.
The treatment was administered arterially rather than through a vein for better results.

Related Terms

  • antonym: venously - in a manner relating to or by way of a vein
