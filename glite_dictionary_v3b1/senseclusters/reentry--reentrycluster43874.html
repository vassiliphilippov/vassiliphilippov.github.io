reentry

noun
return to atmosphere
Pronunciation: / riˈɛntri / (AmE), / riˈɛntri / (BrE)
the act of entering again, especially when a spacecraft returns into the Earth's atmosphere from space

Examples

Spacecraft reentry
The spacecraft's reentry was closely monitored by mission control.
Engineers designed special heat shields to protect the capsule during reentry.
After months in orbit, the astronauts prepared for reentry into Earth's atmosphere.

  • antonym: exit - the act of leaving a place
