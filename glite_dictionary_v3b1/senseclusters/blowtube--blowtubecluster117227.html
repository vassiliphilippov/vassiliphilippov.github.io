blowtube

noun
tube for shooting darts by blowing
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbloʊˌtuːb / (AmE), / ˈbləʊˌtjuːb / (BrE)
A tube through which darts or other projectiles are propelled by blowing through one end.

Examples

Paper dart blowtube
Children made their own blowtube from bamboo to shoot tiny paper darts.
The hunter skillfully aimed his blowtube at the distant target.
Using a blowtube requires steady breath and good aim.
