blue cheese

noun
cheese with blue mold
Pronunciation: / bluː tʃiːz / (AmE), / bluː tʃiːz / (BrE)
a type of cheese in which blue mold gives veins or spots of blue or green color and a distinctive strong flavor

Examples

A blue cheese salad
Many people enjoy the strong flavor of blue cheese on their salads.
She added crumbled blue cheese to the steak for extra taste.
The aroma of blue cheese can be intense, but it’s popular among cheese lovers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Gorgonzola - an Italian type of blue cheese
  • synonym: Roquefort - a famous type of French blue cheese
