softshell

noun
technical outerwear fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔftˌʃɛl / (AmE), / ˈsɒftˌʃɛl / (BrE)
a flexible, weather-resistant fabric or jacket, or a turtle with a soft, leathery shell instead of a hard one
