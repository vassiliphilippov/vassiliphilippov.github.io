unnailed

adjective
not nailed down
8.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈneɪld / (AmE), / ʌnˈneɪld / (BrE)
not fastened or secured with nails

Examples

Unnailed boards
After the repairs, several planks were still unnailed and needed to be secured.
The picture frame fell because it was left unnailed on the shelf.
During the inspection, the builder noticed an unnailed board on the porch.
