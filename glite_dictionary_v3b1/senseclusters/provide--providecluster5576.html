provide

verb
to put up or mount (e.g., resistance, a fight)
Pronunciation: / prəˈvaɪd / (AmE), / prəˈvʌɪd / (BrE)
to put up, mount, or offer something such as a resistance or a fight, usually in response to a challenge or demand

Examples

To provide resistance
Despite being outnumbered, the soldiers provided strong resistance for several days.
She provided no excuse and accepted the decision quietly.
The community provided help in the form of protests and rallies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: offer - to present or put forward something for consideration
  • synonym: withstand - to stand up against; resist successfully
