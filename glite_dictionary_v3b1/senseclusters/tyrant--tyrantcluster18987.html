tyrant

noun
cruel and oppressive ruler
1.0 4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈtaɪ.rənt/ (AmE), /ˈtaɪ.rənt/ (BrE)
a person who uses power or authority in a cruel or oppressive way

Examples
