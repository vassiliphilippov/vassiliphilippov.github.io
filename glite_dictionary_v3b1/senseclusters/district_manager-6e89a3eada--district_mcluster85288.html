district manager

noun
supervising manager for a district
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪstrɪkt ˈmænɪdʒər / (AmE), / ˈdɪstrɪkt ˈmænɪdʒə / (BrE)
a manager who is responsible for overseeing operations and sales activities within a specific geographic district, often supervising multiple local managers or stores

Examples

District manager role
After years as a store supervisor, she was promoted to district manager for the northern region.
As a district manager, he oversees the hiring and training of all local staff in his territory.
The district manager scheduled monthly visits to each store to ensure company standards were followed.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: branch manager - manager in charge of a single branch or location
  • degree higher: regional manager - manager overseeing a larger region, generally higher than district manager
