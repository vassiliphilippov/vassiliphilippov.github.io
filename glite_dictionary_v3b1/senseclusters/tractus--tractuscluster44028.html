tractus

noun
short written pamphlet
Pronunciation: / ˈtræktəs / (AmE), / ˈtræktəs / (BrE)
a short written treatise or pamphlet, especially on a religious or political subject

Examples

Political tractus
The activist distributed a tractus to inform people about the upcoming election.
During the meeting, she read from a tractus discussing the importance of community service.
He found an old tractus in the library that argued for social reform.

Related Terms

  • synonym: booklet - a small, thin book with a paper cover
  • synonym: tract - a short pamphlet or leaflet, often on a religious or political topic
