portable

noun
a small, easily carried typewriter
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔrtəbəl / (AmE), / ˈpɔːtəbəl / (BrE)
a small, lightweight typewriter designed to be easily carried, often with its own case

Examples

A portable typewriter
Journalists in the past often depended on a trusty portable for their reports.
She packed her portable before leaving for her writing retreat in the mountains.
The antique shop displayed a 1950s portable in a leather case.
