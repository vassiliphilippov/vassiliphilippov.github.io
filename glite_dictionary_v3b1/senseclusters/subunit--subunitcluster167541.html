subunit

noun
smaller component part
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌjuːnɪt / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌjuːnɪt / (BrE)
a smaller part or section that forms part of a larger unit or structure

Examples

Organizational subunit
Each subunit of the organization is responsible for a different region.
The protein is made up of several subunits that work together.
The project was divided into manageable subunits to speed up completion.
Each subunit of the protein has a specific function.
The machine is made up of several subunits that work together.
Researchers studied how each subunit interacts within the larger complex.

Related Terms

  • synonym: component - a part or element of a larger whole
  • synonym: module - a separate part that can be combined with others
  • synonym: section - a distinct part of something larger
  • antonym: whole - something complete, with all its parts
