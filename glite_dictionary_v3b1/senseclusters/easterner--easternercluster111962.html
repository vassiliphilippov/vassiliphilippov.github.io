easterner

noun
person from the eastern region
Pronunciation: / ˈiːstərnər / (AmE), / ˈiːstə.nə / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from the eastern part of a country, especially the United States

Examples

An easterner from America
As an easterner, she was not used to the dry desert climate of the West.
He could always tell who was an easterner by their accent and mannerisms.
Many easterners move to the coast for better job opportunities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: westerner - person from the western region, especially of the US
