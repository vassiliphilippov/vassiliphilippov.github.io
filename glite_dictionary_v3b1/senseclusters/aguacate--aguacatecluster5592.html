aguacate

noun
pear-shaped tropical fruit; avocado
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːɡwəˈkɑːteɪ / (AmE), / ˌɑːɡwəˈkɑːteɪ / (BrE)
A pear-shaped tropical fruit with green or blackish skin and rich yellowish pulp surrounding a large seed, also known as an avocado.
