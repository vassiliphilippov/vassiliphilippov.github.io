Ashanti

proper noun
Akan kingdom in Ghana
8.1
Pronunciation: / əˈʃɑːnti / (AmE), / əˈʃɑːnti / (BrE)
a historical kingdom of the Akan people in southern Ghana, dominant from the 17th to 19th centuries

Examples

Ashanti kingdom
The powerful Ashanti kingdom played a major role in West African history.
Many cultural traditions in Ghana have roots in the Ashanti people.
The British fought several wars against the Ashanti during the 19th century.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Akan - an ethnic group in West Africa to which the Ashanti belong
  • synonym: Ghana - a country in West Africa where the Ashanti kingdom was located
