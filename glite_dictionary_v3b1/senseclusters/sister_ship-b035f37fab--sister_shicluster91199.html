sister ship

noun
a ship of the same type as another
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪstər ʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈsɪstə ʃɪp / (BrE)
a ship that is one of two or more of the same design, built at the same time or to the same specifications as another

Examples

A sister ship design
Each sister ship in the fleet shares the same basic design and capabilities.
Our vessel passed its sister ship in the harbor this morning.
The Titanic and the Olympic were sister ships built for the White Star Line.
