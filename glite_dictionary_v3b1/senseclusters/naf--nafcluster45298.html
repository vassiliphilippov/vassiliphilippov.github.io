naf

noun
tasteless thing
Pronunciation: / naf / (BrE)
an informal British term for a person or thing considered tasteless, worthless, or of poor quality

Examples

A piece of naf
He thought the souvenir was just a piece of naf and left it behind.
Some people love that style, but others find it a bit naf.
The market was full of cheap naf that nobody really wanted.

Related Terms

  • synonym: junk - something of little value or poor quality
  • synonym: rubbish - something considered worthless or of poor quality
  • antonym: treasure - something of great value or high quality
