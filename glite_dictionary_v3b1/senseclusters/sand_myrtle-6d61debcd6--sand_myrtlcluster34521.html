sand myrtle

noun
evergreen shrub with white flowers
7.2
Pronunciation: / sænd ˈmɜːrtəl / (AmE), / sænd ˈmɜːtəl / (BrE)
a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the eastern United States, grown for its white star-shaped flowers and glossy leaves

Examples

A sand myrtle shrub

The sand myrtle thrives in sandy soils along the coastal plains.
