decentralization

noun
distribution of computing and data across nodes

(in computing) the distribution of data storage, processing, or control across multiple independent networked nodes rather than relying on a single central server or authority.

Examples

Network decentralization
Many startups promote decentralization to reduce single points of failure in cloud infrastructure.
The new protocol enables secure decentralization of user data across multiple independent servers.
Experts argue that decentralization can improve privacy but complicate regulatory compliance for companies.
Tech firms are pursuing decentralisation of data to improve privacy and resilience.
The startup built a decentralisation layer so users control their own identity and keys.
Researchers studied how decentralisation improves fault tolerance across thousands of networked devices.

Related Terms

  • antonym: centralisation - the concentration of control or decision-making under a single authority
  • antonym: centralization - the concentration of control or services under a single authority or server; opposite of decentralization
  • synonym: distributed computing - computing in which tasks or storage are shared across multiple networked machines
