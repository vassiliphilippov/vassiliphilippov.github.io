Monera

noun
former kingdom of prokaryotes
9.9
Pronunciation: / moʊˈnɪrə / (AmE), / məʊˈnɪərə / (BrE)
a former biological kingdom comprising prokaryotic organisms, such as bacteria and blue-green algae, now classified under Bacteria and Archaea.

Examples

Monera kingdom
Many biology textbooks once included Monera as one of the five kingdoms of life.
The term Monera is rarely used in modern biological classification systems.
With advances in genetics, the kingdom Monera was divided into Bacteria and Archaea.
