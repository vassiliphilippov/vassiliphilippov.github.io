chap

noun
informal word for boy or man
Pronunciation: / tʃæp / (AmE), / tʃap / (BrE)
an informal term for a boy or man, especially in British English

Examples

A friendly chap
He seems like a friendly chap who gets along with everyone.
Some chaps in the village helped us fix the car.
There's a chap waiting for you outside the office.
