Oktoberfest

noun
German beer festival held in autumn
4.9
Pronunciation: / oʊkˈtoʊbɚˌfɛst / (AmE), / ɒkˈtəʊbəˌfɛst / (BrE)
an annual German festival held in autumn, celebrated with beer, food, and music, particularly in Munich

Examples

Munich Oktoberfest festival
During Oktoberfest, breweries release special seasonal beers just for the occasion.
Every year, thousands of people travel to Munich to experience the lively Oktoberfest.
The local community hosted its own Oktoberfest, complete with German food and traditional music.
