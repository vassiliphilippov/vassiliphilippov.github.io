nasopharynx

noun
upper part of the pharynx behind the nose
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˌneɪzoʊˈfærɪŋks / (AmE), / ˌneɪzəʊˈfærɪŋks / (BrE)
the upper part of the pharynx, connecting the nasal cavity above the soft palate

Examples

The nasopharynx area
A doctor may examine the nasopharynx to check for signs of inflammation.
Infections in the nasopharynx can lead to ear problems in children.
The nasopharynx is located behind the nose and above the soft palate.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: oropharynx - middle part of the pharynx, behind the mouth
