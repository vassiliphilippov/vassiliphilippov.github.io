lanthorn

noun
roof structure with windows
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlænθən / (AmE), / ˈlænθən / (BrE)
an architectural structure on a roof or dome, with windows or openings, that lets in light or air

Examples

Roof lanthorn
A small lanthorn crowned the cathedral's dome, casting morning light into the central hall.
The architect designed a lanthorn to provide natural ventilation for the grand hall below.
Restorers carefully repaired the old lanthorn to preserve the building's historic character.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cupola - a small dome on top of a building
  • synonym: roof lantern - a glass structure on a roof that lets in light
