Neuropteris

proper noun
extinct seed fern genus
Pronunciation: / nuːˈrɑːptərɪs / (AmE), / njʊəˈrɒptərɪs / (BrE)
a genus of extinct seed ferns known from fossil records, especially from the Carboniferous period
