pregnant chad

noun
partially punched paper ballot fragment
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛɡnənt tʃæd / (AmE), / ˈprɛɡnənt tʃæd / (BrE)
A chad on a punched paper ballot that has been indented or dimpled but is still attached at all four corners.

Examples

A pregnant chad on ballot
During the recount, officials examined every pregnant chad to determine voter intent.
Election workers debated whether a pregnant chad should count as a valid vote.
Several ballots were disqualified due to the presence of a pregnant chad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hanging chad - a chad still attached to a ballot at only one or more corners
