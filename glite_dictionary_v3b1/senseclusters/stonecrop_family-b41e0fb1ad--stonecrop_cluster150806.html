stonecrop family

noun
family of succulent herbs and shrubs
Pronunciation: / ˈstoʊnˌkrɑp ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈstəʊnˌkrɒp ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of chiefly succulent herbs and shrubs, including the stonecrops (genus Sedum) and related plants, known for their ability to thrive in dry conditions
