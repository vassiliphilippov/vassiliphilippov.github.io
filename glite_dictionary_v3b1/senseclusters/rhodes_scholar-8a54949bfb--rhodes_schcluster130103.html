Rhodes scholar

noun
recipient of the Rhodes Scholarship
Pronunciation: / roʊdz ˈskɑːlər / (AmE), / rəʊdz ˈskɒlə / (BrE)
A student who has been awarded a Rhodes Scholarship to study at the University of Oxford.

Examples

A Rhodes scholar award
Becoming a Rhodes scholar is considered one of the highest academic achievements for university students.
He was selected as a Rhodes scholar and spent two years studying at Oxford.
Many Rhodes scholars go on to have distinguished careers in public service and academia.
