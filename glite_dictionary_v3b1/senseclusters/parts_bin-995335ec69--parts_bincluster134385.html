parts bin

noun
container for storing spare parts
Pronunciation: / pɑrts bɪn / (AmE), / pɑːts bɪn / (BrE)
a container or compartment used for storing spare parts, especially in workshops or manufacturing settings

Examples

A parts bin
Every workstation has its own parts bin for easy access to replacement components.
