elephant-tusk

noun
plant with curved-beak flowers
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɛl.ɪ.fənt tʌsk / (AmE), / ˈɛl.ɪ.fənt tʌsk / (BrE)
an annual plant of the southern United States to Mexico, with large whitish or yellowish flowers mottled with purple and a distinctive long, curving beak

Examples

The elephant-tusk flower
Elephant-tusk is valued for its unusual beak-like blooms that add exotic charm to gardens.
During his trip to Texas, he photographed a wild elephant-tusk in full bloom.
The gardener admired the striking flowers of the elephant-tusk growing near the fence.
