wryneck

noun
neck condition causing twisted head
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪˌnɛk / (AmE), / ˈraɪnɛk / (BrE)
A condition in which the head is twisted to one side due to muscle contraction in the neck.

Examples

A wryneck condition
Long-term wryneck can lead to discomfort and restricted movement in the neck.
She wore a special collar to help relieve her wryneck condition.
The doctor diagnosed the child with wryneck after observing the abnormal tilt of his head.
