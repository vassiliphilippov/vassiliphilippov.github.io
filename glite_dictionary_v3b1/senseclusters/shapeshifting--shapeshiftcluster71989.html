shapeshifting

adjective
able to change form
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪpˌʃɪftɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪpˌʃɪftɪŋ / (BrE)
able to change form or appearance, especially by transforming into different shapes or beings.

Examples

Shapeshifting creature
The shapeshifting character in the story could become any animal at will.
Legends often describe shapeshifting beings who deceive humans by changing their appearance.
The movie featured a shapeshifting villain who was difficult to recognize.

Related Terms

  • antonym: static - not changing or moving
  • synonym: transforming - changing completely in form or appearance
