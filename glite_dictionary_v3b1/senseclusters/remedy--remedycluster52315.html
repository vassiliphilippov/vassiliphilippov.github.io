remedy

noun
medicine or treatment to cure or ease pain
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛmədi / (AmE), / ˈrɛmɪdi / (BrE)
A medicine, treatment, or therapy that cures disease or relieves pain.

Examples

A home remedy
A hot drink with honey is a common home remedy for a sore throat.
Doctors are searching for an effective remedy for the disease.
She took an herbal remedy to help with her headaches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cure - A treatment that fully restores health
  • antonym: poison - A substance that is harmful or lethal when introduced to the body
  • synonym: therapy - Treatment intended to relieve or heal a disorder
