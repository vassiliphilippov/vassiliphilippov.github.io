Synonyms

  • weeping
    1.4 1/m
    weeping often means quietly shedding tears as an emotional response, while crying usually refers to shedding tears with sound and pain.
  • cry
    1.0 12/m
    a cry is a single act or short session of tears or sound, while crying is the general action of shedding tears.
  • sobbing
    1.0 3/m
    sobbing is intense crying with loud gasps and shaking, while crying can be milder and may only mean shedding tears.
