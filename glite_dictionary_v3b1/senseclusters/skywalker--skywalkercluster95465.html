skywalker

noun
person at great heights
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈskaɪ.wɔː.kər/ (AmE), /ˈskaɪ.wɔː.kə/ (BrE)
a person who walks or works at great heights, especially on bridges or tall buildings

Examples

A skywalker on beams
The daring skywalker balanced on the steel beam as the city bustled far below.
Many skywalkers helped construct the skyscraper, working hundreds of feet above the ground.
Becoming a skywalker requires both skill and a strong sense of balance.
