calcarine fissure

noun
groove on the occipital lobe in the brain
7.9 9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkæl.kə.raɪn ˈfɪʃ.ər/ /; / /ˈkæl.kə.raɪn ˈfɪʒ.ər/ (AmE), /ˈkæl.kə.raɪn ˈfɪʃ.ə/ /; / /ˈkæl.kə.raɪn ˈfɪʒ.ə/ (BrE)
a deep groove on the medial surface of the occipital lobe in the brain, marking the location of the primary visual cortex
