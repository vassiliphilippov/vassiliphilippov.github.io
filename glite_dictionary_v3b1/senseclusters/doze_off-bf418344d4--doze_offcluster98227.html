Synonyms

  • drop off
    3.0 0.41/m
    drop off means falling asleep briefly and without meaning to, while doze off is a common informal phrase for the same quick sleep.
  • nod off
    4.1 0.54/m
    nod off means falling asleep briefly without planning to, while doze off feels like a light, accidental nap.
  • drift off
    3.8 1.85/m
    drift off means gradually moving into sleep in a slow, gentle way, while doze off often happens more suddenly and briefly.
  • drowse off
    5.5 0.02/m

  • nod out
    7.9 0.04/m
    nod out means falling asleep from tiredness or drug effect, sometimes deeply, while doze off usually means a short unplanned sleep.
