auto race

noun
competition between automobiles
Pronunciation: / ˈɔtoʊ reɪs / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊ reɪs / (BrE)
a competition in which automobiles compete to finish a course in the shortest time or achieve the highest speed

Examples

An auto race event
He spent years preparing his car for the international auto race next month.
Thousands of fans gathered to watch the annual auto race at the famous speedway.
Winning an auto race requires skill, precision, and nerves of steel.
