churro

noun
fried dough pastry
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʊroʊ / (AmE), / ˈtʃʊərəʊ / (BrE)
a sweet, long, fried dough pastry, usually coated with sugar and sometimes eaten with chocolate

Examples

Hot churros with chocolate

The bakery sells fresh churros every morning with a dusting of cinnamon sugar.

She enjoyed a hot churro dipped in chocolate sauce at the fair.
Vendors sell fresh churros on many street corners in Spain.
After dinner, we shared a plate of cinnamon-sugar churros for dessert.
