Heinrich Himmler

proper noun
Nazi leader, SS and Gestapo chief
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪn.rɪk ˈhɪm.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪn.rɪç ˈhɪm.lə / (BrE)
(1900–1945) German Nazi leader who was chief of the SS and the Gestapo, overseeing the genocide of six million Jews during the Holocaust.
