cellarage

noun
storage area in a cellar
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛlərɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsɛlərɪdʒ / (BrE)
an area or space in a cellar used for storing goods, especially wine or other provisions

Examples

Wine cellarage
The old manor's extensive cellarage was perfect for aging wine.
The restaurant boasts a large cellarage for its impressive wine collection.
They kept the preserves in the cool cellarage beneath the house.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cellar - an underground room or rooms used for storage
