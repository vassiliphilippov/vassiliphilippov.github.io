refillable

adjective
can be filled again
Pronunciation: / riˈfɪləbəl / (AmE), / riˈfɪləbəl / (BrE)
able to be filled again after being emptied

Examples

A refillable bottle
She bought a refillable pen to avoid throwing away plastic.
Many coffee shops offer discounts if you bring a refillable cup.
The refillable soap dispenser is both convenient and eco-friendly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disposable - intended to be used once and thrown away
  • synonym: reusable - able to be used more than once
