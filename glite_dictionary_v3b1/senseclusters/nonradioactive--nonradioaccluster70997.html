nonradioactive

adjective
not emitting radiation
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnrˌeɪdi.oʊˈæktɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnrˌeɪdi.əʊˈæktɪv / (BrE)
Not emitting harmful radiation; not radioactive.

Examples

A nonradioactive sample
All soil samples tested were found to be completely nonradioactive.
The company offers a nonradioactive tracer for environmental monitoring.
We use nonradioactive materials in all stages of the experiment to ensure safety.
