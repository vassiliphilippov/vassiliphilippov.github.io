recommence

verb
to begin again after a pause

Examples

Synonyms

  • resume
    3.1 19/m
    resume means returning to an activity after a short pause, while recommence sounds more formal and literary.
  • restart
    2.3 5/m
    restart often means starting again from the beginning, while recommence is more formal and can apply to events or speech.
  • reinitiate
    5.9 0.08/m
    reinitiate is a formal, technical word for beginning a process again, while recommence is more general and used in speech or writing.
