thioridazine

noun
antipsychotic medication
9.9
Pronunciation: / θaɪəˈrɪdəˌziːn / (AmE), / θaɪəˈrɪdəziːn / (BrE)
a drug used as a tranquilizer to treat schizophrenia and other psychotic disorders

Examples
