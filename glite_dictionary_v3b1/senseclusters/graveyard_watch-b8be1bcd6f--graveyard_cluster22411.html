graveyard watch

noun
night shift from midnight to 8 a.m.
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪvˌjɑrd wɑtʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪvjɑːd wɒtʃ / (BrE)
a work shift or duty period that takes place during the late night hours, typically from midnight to 8 a.m., especially in maritime or industrial contexts
