decider

noun
person making decisions
4.4
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsaɪdər / (AmE), / dɪˈsaɪdə / (BrE)
a person or group who makes important choices or decisions

Examples

The main decider
The final decider in the process is the board of directors.
As the project manager, she was the main decider for all major changes.
In a democracy, the voters are the ultimate deciders of leadership.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decision-maker - a person who makes decisions, especially in an organization
  • antonym: follower - a person who accepts the decisions of others
