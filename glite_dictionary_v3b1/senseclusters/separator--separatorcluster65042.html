separator

noun
thing that divides
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛpəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈsɛpəreɪtə / (BrE)
an object, device, or symbol that divides or keeps things apart

Examples

Room separator
A curtain can serve as a separator between two areas in a shared room.
The software uses a semicolon as a separator in data files.
He placed a wooden separator between the garden beds to keep the plants organized.
A comma is often used as a separator in lists to make them clearer.
The fence acted as a separator between the two gardens.
In the factory, a machine called a separator removes impurities from the oil.

  • synonym: divider - something that separates or marks a division
