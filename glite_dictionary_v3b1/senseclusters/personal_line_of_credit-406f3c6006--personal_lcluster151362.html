personal line of credit

noun
individual credit borrowing limit
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝsənl ˌlaɪn əv ˈkrɛdɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl ˌlaɪn əv ˈkrɛdɪt / (BrE)
an arrangement with a bank or other financial institution that allows an individual to borrow up to a set limit as needed

Examples

A personal line of credit
A personal line of credit can offer more flexibility than a traditional loan.
She applied for a personal line of credit to finance her home renovations.
With his personal line of credit approved, he was able to handle unexpected expenses easily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: credit limit - the maximum amount of credit that a financial institution extends to a client
  • synonym: line of credit - a credit facility extended by a bank to a customer that permits the customer to draw up to a specified amount
