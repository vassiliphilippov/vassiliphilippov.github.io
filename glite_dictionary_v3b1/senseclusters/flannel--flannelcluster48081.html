flannel

noun
washcloth for face or body
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈflænəl / (AmE), / ˈflænəl / (BrE)
A small piece of soft cloth used for washing the face or body, especially in the bath.

Examples

A flannel for washing
Could you pass me a flannel? I want to wash my face.
She prefers using a flannel to soap up in the shower.
The hotel provides each guest with a clean flannel every day.
