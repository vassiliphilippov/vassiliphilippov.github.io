Herbert Kitchener

proper noun
British field marshal and WWI Minister of War
Pronunciation: / ˈhɝːbərt ˈkɪtʃənɚ / (AmE), / ˈhɜːbət ˈkɪtʃɪnə / (BrE)
(1850–1916) British field marshal, colonial administrator, and Minister of War during the First World War.
