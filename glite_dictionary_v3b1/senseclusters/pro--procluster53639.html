pro

noun
professional athlete
Pronunciation: / proʊ / (AmE), / prəʊ / (BrE)
a person who is very skilled or works professionally in a particular field

Examples

A tennis pro
After college, he signed a contract and became a pro in the league.
Many young athletes dream of making it as a pro someday.
The tournament is open to both pros and amateurs.
After years of practice, she finally became a pro at playing the piano.
They decided to call a pro to handle the complicated repairs.
He plays tennis like a pro, even though he is still a student.

Related Terms

  • antonym: amateur - a person who engages in a sport or activity for pleasure rather than financial benefit
  • synonym: expert - a person with a high level of knowledge or skill
