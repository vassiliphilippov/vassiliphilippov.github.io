sunk

adjective
irretrievably lost; doomed
6.7 6.0
Pronunciation: /sʌŋk/ (AmE), /sʌŋk/ (BrE)
Having no hope of recovery or success; doomed to failure or extinction.

Examples

A sunk cause

He knew he was sunk when the evidence against him became overwhelming.
With the company's debts piling up, the investors realized they were sunk.
