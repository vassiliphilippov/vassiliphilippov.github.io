abuelita

noun
Spanish for grandmother
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːbweɪˈliːtə / (AmE), / ˌabweɪˈliːtə / (BrE)
a Spanish term for grandmother, sometimes used in English to refer affectionately to one's grandmother

Examples

My abuelita cooks
Every summer, we visited my abuelita in her small village in Mexico.
My abuelita makes the best homemade tamales for the holidays.
She learned traditional recipes by cooking alongside her abuelita as a child.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grandmother - the mother of one's parent
