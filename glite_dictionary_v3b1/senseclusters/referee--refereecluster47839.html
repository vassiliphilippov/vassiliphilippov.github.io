referee

verb
act as an official in a sports contest
3.2 0.40/m
Pronunciation: / Àår…õf…ôÀàri / (AmE), / Àår…õf…ôÀàriÀê / (BrE)
to act as an official who oversees a sports contest and enforces the rules

Examples

To referee a match

She will referee several matches at the upcoming tournament.
