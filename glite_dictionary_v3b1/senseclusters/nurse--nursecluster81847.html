nurse

verb
to breastfeed a baby or young animal
2.3 4.59/m
Pronunciation: / n…úrs / (AmE), / n…úÀês / (BrE)
to feed a baby or young animal at the breast; to give suck to

Examples

To nurse a baby
It is important for mothers to nurse their newborns shortly after birth.
She decided to nurse her baby herself rather than use formula.
The lioness began to nurse her cubs in the den.

Synonyms

  • breastfeed
    2.6 2.14/m
    breastfeed means feeding a baby directly at the mother's breast, while nurse is a more general word for feeding young.
  • suckle
    5.5 1.09/m
    suckle is used for animals or babies taking milk from a breast or udder, while nurse means to feed a young.
  • give suck
    9.8 0.02/m
    give suck is an older phrase for feeding at the breast, whereas nurse is the common modern verb used today.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breastfeed - to feed a baby with milk from the breast
  • antonym: wean - to gradually stop feeding a baby with its mother's milk
