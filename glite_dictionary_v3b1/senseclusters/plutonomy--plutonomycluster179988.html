plutonomy

noun
rule by the wealthy
9.9
Pronunciation: / pluːˈtɑːnəmi / (AmE), / pluːˈtɒnəmi / (BrE)
an economic or political system where most wealth and power are held by the rich, and economic growth mainly benefits them

Examples

In a plutonomy, most of the wealth is concentrated among a small elite.
Critics warn that a plutonomy can lead to greater social and economic inequality.

  • synonym: plutocracy - a society or system ruled by the wealthy
