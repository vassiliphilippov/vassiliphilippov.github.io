Siege Perilous

noun
legendary fatal seat at King Arthur's Round Table
9.9
Pronunciation: / siːdʒ ˈpɛrələs / (AmE), / siːdʒ ˈpɛrɪləs / (BrE)
the legendary seat at King Arthur's Round Table reserved for the knight destined to find the Holy Grail; said to be fatal to anyone else who sat in it

Examples

The Siege Perilous seat
According to legend, only the purest knight could sit in the Siege Perilous without being struck down.
Sir Galahad was said to be the only one worthy to occupy the Siege Perilous.
The Siege Perilous at the Round Table symbolized the ultimate test of virtue and destiny.
