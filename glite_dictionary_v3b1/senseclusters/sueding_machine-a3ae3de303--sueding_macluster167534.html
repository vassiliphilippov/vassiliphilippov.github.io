sueding machine

noun
textile machine for softening fabric
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːdɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈsjuːdɪŋ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
A machine used in the textile industry during the finishing process to give fabric a softer surface and improved appearance.

Examples

A sueding machine in factory
After passing through the sueding machine, the fabric felt much softer to the touch.
Technicians inspected the sueding machine before starting the finishing process.
The sueding machine is essential in achieving a velvet-like finish on cotton fabrics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sueding - the process of finishing fabric to create a soft surface
