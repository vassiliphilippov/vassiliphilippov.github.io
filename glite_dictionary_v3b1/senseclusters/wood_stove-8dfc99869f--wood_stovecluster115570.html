wood stove

noun
stove burning wood
2.0
Pronunciation: / wʊd stoʊv / (AmE), / wʊd stəʊv / (BrE)
a stove that burns wood as fuel to provide heat or for cooking

Examples

A wood stove heater
They cooked soup on the wood stove during the power outage.
The old cabin was kept warm by a large wood stove in the corner.
She added more logs to the wood stove to keep the house cozy all night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fireplace - an open space in a wall for a fire to heat a room
  • antonym: gas stove - a stove that uses gas as fuel
  • synonym: heater - a device that produces heat
