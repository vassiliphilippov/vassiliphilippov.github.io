RV

noun
motor vehicle for camping/living in
3.0 2.77/m
Pronunciation: / …ër Ààvi / (AmE), / …ëÀê ÀàviÀê / (BrE)
a motor vehicle equipped for living in, used for camping or travel

Examples

A RV camper
He parked his RV next to the lake and enjoyed a quiet week of fishing and relaxation.
The family decided to rent an RV for their road trip instead of staying in hotels.
We spent the summer driving our RV across the country and camping in national parks.

Synonyms

  • recreational vehicle
    3.4 0.31/m
    recreational vehicle is the full formal name used in writing or documents, while RV is the short, spoken abbreviation people use.
  • motorhome
    3.0 0.27/m
    motorhome is a drivable living vehicle with beds and a kitchen, while RV is the broader term for similar camping vehicles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: camper - motor vehicle equipped for living in, used while traveling
  • synonym: motorhome - large motor vehicle with living space inside
