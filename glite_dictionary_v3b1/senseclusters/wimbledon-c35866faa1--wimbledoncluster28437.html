Wimbledon

proper noun
London suburb, site of tennis championships
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪmbəldən / (AmE), / ˈwɪmbəldən / (BrE)
A suburb of London, known especially as the location of the All England Club, where the annual Wimbledon tennis championships are held on grass courts.

Examples

Wimbledon tennis championships
Each summer, the world's top tennis players compete at Wimbledon.
He traveled to Wimbledon to watch the championships live for the first time.
The grass courts at Wimbledon are renowned for their speed and elegance.
