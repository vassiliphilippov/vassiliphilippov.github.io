rubbish dump

noun
place for dumping waste materials
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌbɪʃ dʌmp / (AmE), / ˈrʌbɪʃ dʌmp / (BrE)
a place where waste materials are collected and left, often in a large open area

Examples

A rubbish dump site
Do not leave your trash in the open; use the designated rubbish dump.
Residents complained about the smell coming from the rubbish dump on the edge of town.
The city is planning to convert the old rubbish dump into a park.

Related Terms

  • synonym: landfill - site for the disposal of waste material by burial
  • antonym: recycling centre - place where recyclable materials are collected and processed
