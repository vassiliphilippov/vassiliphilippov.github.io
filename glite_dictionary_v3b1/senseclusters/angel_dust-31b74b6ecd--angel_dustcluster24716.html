angel dust

noun
slang for phencyclidine (PCP), hallucinogenic drug
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪndʒəl dʌst / (AmE), / ˈeɪndʒəl dʌst / (BrE)
A slang term for the drug phencyclidine (PCP), originally used as a veterinary anesthetic, taken illicitly for its hallucinogenic effects.

Years ago, angel dust was sometimes used as an anesthetic for animals.
