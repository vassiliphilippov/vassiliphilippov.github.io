operationalization

noun
making operational
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːpəˌreɪʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɒpəˌreɪʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making a system, plan, or idea ready for use or putting it into operation

Examples

Researchers discussed the operationalization of abstract concepts for their study.
