operationalization

noun
making operational
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːpəˌreɪʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɒpəˌreɪʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making a system, plan, or idea ready for use or putting it into operation

Examples

Operationalization of a system
The operationalization of the new software system required extensive staff training.
Successful operationalization of the policy improved efficiency across all departments.
Researchers discussed the operationalization of abstract concepts for their study.

Related Terms

  • synonym: execution - the carrying out of a plan or order
  • synonym: implementation - the act of putting a plan or system into effect
