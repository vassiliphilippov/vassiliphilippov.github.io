rooted

adjective
having root/administrator access (computing)
Pronunciation: / ˈruːtɪd / (AmE), / ˈruːtɪd / (BrE)
(computing) (of a device, system, or account) having root or administrator access, giving unrestricted control over the operating system and system files.

Examples

Rooted Android phone
Many users install custom apps on a rooted Android phone to change system settings.
The technician warned that a rooted device can be more vulnerable to malware if misconfigured.
After she rooted her tablet, she could remove preinstalled apps and adjust hidden settings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: administrator (admin) access - the level of system permission that allows configuration and modification of protected system settings
  • synonym: root access - unrestricted administrative privileges on a computer or device
