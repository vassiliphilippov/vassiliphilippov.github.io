hotelier

noun
owner or manager of a hotel
Pronunciation: / hoʊˈtɛljər / (AmE), / həʊˈtɛljeɪ / (BrE)
a person who owns or manages a hotel.

Examples

A hotelier owns hotels
As a successful hotelier, she owns several boutique hotels across Europe.
The hotelier greeted each guest personally at the entrance of the luxury resort.
The city honored the hotelier for his contribution to the local tourism industry.

Related Terms

  • antonym: guest - person staying at a hotel or similar establishment
  • synonym: innkeeper - a person who owns or manages an inn
