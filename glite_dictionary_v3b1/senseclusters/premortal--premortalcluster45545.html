premortal

adjective
before birth
Pronunciation: / priːˈmɔrtəl / (AmE), / priːˈmɔːtəl / (BrE)
relating to a supposed existence or state before birth or before mortal life, especially in theological contexts

Examples

Premortal existence
Some religious traditions teach of a premortal existence where souls live before entering the physical world.
The concept of a premortal life is discussed in certain theological texts.
He wrote an essay exploring beliefs about the premortal state of the soul.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postmortal - relating to the period after death
  • synonym: prenatal - relating to the period before birth
