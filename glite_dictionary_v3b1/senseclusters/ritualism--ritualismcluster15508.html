ritualism

noun
study or use of religious or magical rites

Pronunciation: /ˈrɪtʃ.ʊə.lɪ.zəm/ /; / /ˈrɪt.jʊə.lɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈrɪtʃ.ʊə.lɪ.zəm/ /; / /ˈrɪt.jʊə.lɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
