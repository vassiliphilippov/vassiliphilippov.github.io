ritualism

noun
study or use of religious or magical rites
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪtʃuəlˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃuəlɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
the study or use of religious or magical rites and ceremonies, often with excessive emphasis on prescribed forms

Examples

Study of ritualism
Her fascination with ritualism led her to participate in ceremonies from many different traditions.
Some critics argue that organized religion is often reduced to mere ritualism without genuine faith.
The anthropologist devoted his life to the study of ritualism in ancient cultures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ritual - a religious or solemn ceremony consisting of a series of actions performed according to a prescribed order
