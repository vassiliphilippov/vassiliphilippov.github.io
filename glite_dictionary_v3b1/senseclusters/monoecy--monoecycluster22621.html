monoecy

noun
both sexes on one plant
9.9
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈnoʊ.ɪ.siː/ /; / /mə.ˈnoʊ.ə.siː/ (AmE), /mə.ˈnəʊ.ɪ.siː/ /; / /mə.ˈnəʊ.ə.siː/ (BrE)

Examples

Plant monoecy
Corn is a common example of a plant that exhibits monoecy.
