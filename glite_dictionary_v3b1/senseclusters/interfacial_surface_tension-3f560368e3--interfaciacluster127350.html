interfacial surface tension

noun
surface tension between two liquids
7.3 5.9
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.ˈfeɪ.ʃəl ˈsɜːr.fəs ˈten.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.ˈfeɪ.ʃəl ˈsɜː.fɪs ˈten.ʃən/ (BrE)
