retainer

noun
dental appliance for maintaining teeth alignment
5.6
Pronunciation: / rɪˈteɪnɚ / (AmE), / rɪˈteɪnə / (BrE)
a dental device worn in the mouth to keep teeth or a dental prosthesis in place after orthodontic treatment

Examples

Dental retainer
After getting her braces removed, Maria wore a retainer to keep her teeth straight.
Losing his retainer meant he had to get a replacement to maintain his dental alignment.
The dentist recommended using a retainer at night for several months.
