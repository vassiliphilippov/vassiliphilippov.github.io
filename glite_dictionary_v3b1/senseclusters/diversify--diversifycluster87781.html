diversify

verb
make or become more diverse
Pronunciation: / daɪˈvɜr.sɪ.faɪ /; / daɪˈvɜːrsəˌfaɪ /; / dəˈvɜrsɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / daɪˈvɜː.sɪ.faɪ /; / daɪˈvɜːsɪˌfaɪ /; / daɪˈvɜːsɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to make or become more varied by adding different types or elements, especially to reduce risk or increase opportunities
