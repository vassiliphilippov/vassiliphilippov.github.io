dark-green

adjective
deep shade of green color
1.0
Pronunciation: / dɑrk ˈɡrin / (AmE), / dɑːk ˈɡriːn / (BrE)
having a deep shade of green, darker than the standard green, like that of pine needles or dense foliage

Examples
