wood horsetail

noun
a species of horsetail plant
6.0 7.7
Pronunciation: /wʊd ˈhɔːr.steɪl/ (AmE), /wʊd ˈhɔː.steɪl/ (BrE)
A species of horsetail plant (Equisetum sylvaticum) found in Eurasia (excluding southern Russia) and northern North America, typically growing in moist woodland areas.

Examples

A wood horsetail plant

Gardeners sometimes struggle to remove wood horsetail because its roots spread quickly underground.
The wood horsetail is often found in damp, shady forests across northern Europe and North America.
