edible seed

noun
a seed that can be safely eaten
Pronunciation: / ˈɛdəbəl sid / (AmE), / ˈɛdɪbl̩ siːd / (BrE)
a seed that can be safely eaten, often used as food or seasoning

Examples

Edible seeds like sesame
Many edible seeds, such as sesame and chia, are rich in nutrients and healthy fats.
Pumpkin is grown both for its flesh and its edible seeds, which can be roasted and eaten.
The chef sprinkled toasted edible seeds over the salad for extra flavor and texture.
