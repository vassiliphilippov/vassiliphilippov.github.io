blain

noun
inflammatory swelling or sore
9.9
Pronunciation: / bleɪn / (AmE), / bleɪn / (BrE)
An inflammatory swelling or sore, especially one on the skin.

Examples

Painful blains on feet
During the harsh winter, many villagers suffered from blains on their feet.
The old medical text described a blain as a painful swelling of the skin.
The shepherd's hands were covered with blains from working in the cold and wet.
