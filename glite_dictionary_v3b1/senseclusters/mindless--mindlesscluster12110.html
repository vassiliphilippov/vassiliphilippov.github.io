mindless

adjective
requiring little mental effort
4.8 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪnd.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪnd.ləs/ (BrE)
(of a task or activity) requiring very little mental effort or thought; not engaging the intellect.

Examples

Mindless work
Filing paperwork can be a mindless activity if you do it every day.
He switched to watching a mindless TV show to relax after work.
Many people enjoy doing mindless chores to clear their heads.

Related Terms
