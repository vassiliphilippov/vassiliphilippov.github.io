send a message

verb
to give a clear signal or indication
1.9 2.9
Pronunciation: /send ə ˈmes.ɪdʒ/ /; / /send ə ˈmes.ə.dʒ/ (AmE), /send ə ˈmes.ɪdʒ/ /; / /send ə ˈmes.ə.dʒ/ (BrE)
to communicate an idea, attitude, or intention to others indirectly, often through actions or signals rather than direct words
