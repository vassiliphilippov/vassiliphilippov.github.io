peg

noun
wooden pin for fastening or securing
7.4
Pronunciation: / pɛɡ / (AmE), / pɛɡ / (BrE)
a small pin or cylindrical object used to fasten, secure, or mark something

Examples

A peg in the wall
He hammered a peg into the wall to hang his coat.
She marked the boundary with a peg driven into the ground.
The tent was held down with a sturdy wooden peg.
Each time I win a round, I move my peg forward on the score track.
She pressed the peg into the wall to mark where the frame would hang.
The board game uses colored pegs to keep track of each player's score.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pin - a thin piece of metal or wood used for fastening or attaching things
  • degree higher: stake - a strong wooden or metal post driven into the ground as a marker or support
