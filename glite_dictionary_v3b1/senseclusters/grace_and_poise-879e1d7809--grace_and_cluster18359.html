grace and poise

noun
elegance and composure in movement
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪs ənd pɔɪz / (AmE), / ɡreɪs ənd pɔɪz / (BrE)
elegant and composed manner, especially in movement or behavior

Examples

With grace and poise
Despite the chaotic situation, he moved among the guests with remarkable grace and poise.
She danced across the stage with grace and poise that captivated the entire audience.
The young diplomat handled the difficult questions with grace and poise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elegance - refined grace or dignified propriety
