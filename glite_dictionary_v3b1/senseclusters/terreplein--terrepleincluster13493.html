terreplein

noun
level area behind parapet on rampart
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛrˌpleɪn / (AmE), / ˈtɛə.pleɪn / (BrE)
A level area on the top of a rampart or fortification, behind the parapet, where artillery or heavy guns may be mounted.
