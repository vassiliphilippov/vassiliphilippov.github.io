rambutan tree

noun
tropical tree bearing rambutan fruit
2.8
Pronunciation: / ræmˈbuːtən triː / (AmE), / ræmˈbuːtən triː / (BrE)
A tropical tree native to Southeast Asia that produces spiny red fruit called rambutans.

Examples

A rambutan tree fruit
Farmers carefully prune each rambutan tree to encourage healthy fruit production.
The rambutan tree thrives in warm, humid climates and requires plenty of rainfall.
We planted a rambutan tree in our backyard and enjoyed its fruit during the summer.
