enologist

noun
wine making specialist
9.9
Pronunciation: / iˈnɑːlədʒɪst /; / iˈnɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / iːˈnɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
A specialist in the science and study of wine making.

Examples

An enologist studies wine
As an enologist, she travels to vineyards around the world, studying different grape varieties.
The enologist recommended a new fermentation process to improve the wine's flavor.
The winery hired a skilled enologist to oversee their entire wine production.
As an oenologist, she travels to vineyards around the world to consult on wine production.
The oenologist carefully monitored the fermentation process to ensure the wine developed its best flavor.
The winery hired an experienced oenologist to improve their overall wine quality.
