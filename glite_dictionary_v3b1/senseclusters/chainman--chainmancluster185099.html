chainman

noun
surveying assistant
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪnmæn / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪnmən / (BrE)
a person who assists a surveyor by measuring distances with a chain or tape during land surveys

Examples

The chainman measures distances

A skilled chainman is essential for efficient and precise land surveying work.

During the survey, the chainman recorded each distance as the team moved along the boundary.
A skilled chainman is essential for ensuring precise land measurements on construction sites.
