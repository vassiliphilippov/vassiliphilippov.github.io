unframed

adjective
not having a frame
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfreɪmd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfreɪmd / (BrE)
not provided with a frame; not enclosed in or surrounded by a frame.

Examples

Unframed photograph
I prefer photographs that are unframed for a more modern look.
The artist displayed several unframed drawings in the gallery.
We received an unframed painting that we plan to hang as it is.
