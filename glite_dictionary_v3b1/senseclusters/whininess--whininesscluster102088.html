whininess

noun
annoying complaining
4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈwaɪ.nɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈwaɪ.niː.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈwaɪ.nɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈwaɪ.niː.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of complaining in an annoying, childish, or high-pitched way

Examples

Constant whininess
Her constant whininess made it hard for her friends to enjoy the trip.
The teacher addressed the students' whininess about the amount of homework.
He tried to ignore his brother's whininess during the long car ride.
The whininess in his voice made it hard to take his complaints seriously.
Parents often struggle with the whininess of young children when they are tired.
The dog's constant whininess signaled that it wanted to go outside.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfulness - the quality of being happy and positive
  • synonym: grumbling - the act of complaining in a low voice
  • synonym: petulance - the quality of being childishly sulky or bad-tempered
  • synonym: whining - the act of making a high-pitched, complaining sound
