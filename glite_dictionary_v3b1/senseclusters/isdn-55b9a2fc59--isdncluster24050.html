ISDN

adjective
digital network technology
6.0
Pronunciation: / aɪ ɛs diː ɛn / (AmE), / aɪ ɛs diː ɛn / (BrE)
relating to or using Integrated Services Digital Network technology for digital transmission of voice and data over telephone lines

Examples

ISDN line upgrade
The company upgraded to an ISDN line to improve the quality of their video conferences.
Many businesses relied on ISDN connections before broadband became widely available.
The technician installed an ISDN modem to enable faster data transfer over the phone line.

Related Terms

  • antonym: analog - relating to non-digital signal transmission
  • synonym: broadband - high-speed internet connection technology
