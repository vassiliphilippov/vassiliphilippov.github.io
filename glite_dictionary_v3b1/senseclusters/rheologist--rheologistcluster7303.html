rheologist

noun
scientist of flow
9.4
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈɑː.lə.dʒɪst/ /; / /rɪ.ˈɑː.lə.dʒɪst/ (AmE), /riː.ˈɒl.ə.dʒɪst/ /; / /rɪ.ˈɒl.ə.dʒɪst/ (BrE)
a scientist who studies the flow and deformation of matter, especially liquids and soft solids

Examples

A rheologist studies flow
The rheologist used advanced equipment to measure the thickness of the liquid.
As a rheologist, she investigates how materials respond to stress and strain.
A rheologist can help develop products like lotions and paints by understanding their flow properties.
