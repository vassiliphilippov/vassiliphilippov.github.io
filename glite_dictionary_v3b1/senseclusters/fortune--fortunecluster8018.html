fortune

verb
to make prosperous
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrtʃuːn / (AmE), / ˈfɔːtʃuːn / (BrE)
to give wealth or prosperity to someone or something; to make prosperous.

Examples

To fortune a city
The coalition's investments fortuned several small towns, transforming their economies within a few years.
A generous inheritance fortuned the family beyond their wildest dreams.
The discovery of oil fortuned the region, bringing rapid development and growth.
