berating

noun
a severe rebuke or scolding
Pronunciation: / bəˈreɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / bəˈreɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a harsh scolding or severe rebuke

Examples

A berating from father
After his mistake, he received a lengthy berating from his father.
She dreaded the inevitable berating she would get when she arrived home late.
The manager's public berating of the employee made everyone uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rebuke - an expression of sharp disapproval or criticism
