wax figure

noun
a statue of a person made of wax
5.9
Pronunciation: / wæks ˈfɪɡjɚ / (AmE), / wæks ˈfɪɡjə / (BrE)
a statue or likeness of a person made from wax, typically life-sized and often representing a famous figure

Examples

A wax figure statue
He posed next to a wax figure of his favorite movie star for a photo.
Some wax figures in the hall looked so real that visitors mistook them for people.
The museum's most popular wax figure is a lifelike representation of Queen Elizabeth II.
