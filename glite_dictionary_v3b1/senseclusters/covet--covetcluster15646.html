covet

verb
desire something belonging to others
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌvət / (AmE), / ˈkʌvɪt / (BrE)
To strongly desire something, especially something that belongs to someone else.

Examples

To covet a prize
He secretly covets his neighbor's beautiful garden.
Many people covet a job that offers both security and fulfillment.
The priceless artifact was coveted by collectors around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: desire - to want or wish for something strongly
  • synonym: envy - to wish for something that someone else has
