Platanus wrightii

noun
species of tree native to Arizona region
9.9
Pronunciation: / pləˈtænəs ˈraɪti.aɪ / (AmE), / pləˈtænəs ˈraɪti.aɪ / (BrE)
A medium-sized tree native to Arizona and nearby areas, distinguished by its deeply lobed leaves and fruit clusters of 3 to 5.
