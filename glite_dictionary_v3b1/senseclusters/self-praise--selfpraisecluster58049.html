self-praise

noun
the act of praising oneself
Pronunciation: / sɛlfˈpreɪz / (AmE), / sɛlfˈpreɪz / (BrE)
the act of praising oneself, especially excessively or in a boastful manner

Examples

Self-praise is bad
A little self-praise can sometimes boost your confidence, but too much is off-putting.
His constant self-praise annoyed everyone at the meeting.
The coach discouraged self-praise and encouraged teamwork instead.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boasting - talking with excessive pride and self-satisfaction
  • antonym: humility - the quality of having a modest view of one's importance
