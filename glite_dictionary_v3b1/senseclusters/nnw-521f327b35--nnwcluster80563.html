NNW

noun
compass point between north and northwest
Pronunciation: / ɛn ɛn ˈdʌbəlju / (AmE), / ɛn ɛn ˈdʌbəljuː / (BrE)
the compass point that is halfway between north and northwest, at 337.5° on a compass

Examples

NNW compass point
Sail west until you reach a point directly NNW of the island.
The hikers turned toward the NNW to avoid the storm.
The wind is coming from the NNW at 15 miles per hour.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: N - the compass point north, at 0° or 360°
  • degree higher: NW - the compass point northwest, at 315°
