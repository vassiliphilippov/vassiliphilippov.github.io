needlepoint embroidery

noun
decorative embroidery on canvas
Pronunciation: / ˈniːdəlˌpɔɪnt ɪmˈbrɔɪdəri / (AmE), / ˈniːdəlˌpɔɪnt ɪmˈbrɔɪd(ə)ri / (BrE)
a type of embroidery in which yarn is stitched through a stiff open canvas to create decorative patterns, often resembling tapestry

Examples

Needlepoint embroidery patterns
Many antique chairs are upholstered with intricate needlepoint embroidery designs.
She spent months working on a beautiful needlepoint embroidery for her living room.
The craft store offers workshops in needlepoint embroidery for beginners.
