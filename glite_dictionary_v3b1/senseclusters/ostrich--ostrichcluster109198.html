ostrich

noun
person who avoids facing reality
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑː.strɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈɒs.trɪtʃ / (BrE)
a person who refuses to face or accept unpleasant facts, often by pretending a problem does not exist

Examples

Ostrich attitude
Don't be an ostrich—ignoring your health issues won't make them go away.
He acted like an ostrich, refusing to admit there was a problem with the project.
Many politicians are ostriches when it comes to addressing climate change.

Related Terms

  • synonym: denialist - a person who refuses to accept an established truth
