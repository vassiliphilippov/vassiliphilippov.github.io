nymphomaniac

noun
woman with extreme sexual desire
9.9
Pronunciation: / nɪm.foʊˈmeɪ.ni.æk /; / ˌnɪm.foʊˈmeɪ.ni.æk / (AmE), / nɪm.fəˈmeɪ.ni.æk /; / ˌnɪm.fəˈmeɪ.ni.æk / (BrE)
