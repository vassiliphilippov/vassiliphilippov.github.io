marriage proposal

noun
an offer of marriage
Pronunciation: / ˈmærɪdʒ prəˈpoʊzəl / (AmE), / ˈmærɪdʒ prəˈpəʊzəl / (BrE)
an act of formally asking someone to marry you

Examples

A marriage proposal at dinner

She was nervous as she prepared for her own marriage proposal to her longtime partner.
The couple announced their engagement after a surprise marriage proposal in Paris.
