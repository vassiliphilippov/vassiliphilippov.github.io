creaser

noun
person who makes creases
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːsər / (AmE), / ˈkriːsə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make creases or folds in materials such as cloth, paper, or cardboard

Examples

A skilled creaser

As a skilled creaser, she could fold paper with incredible precision.
Each pair of pants was handled by a creaser before being sent to the store.
