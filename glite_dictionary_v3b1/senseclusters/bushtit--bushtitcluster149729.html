bushtit

noun
a small songbird of western North America
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌʃˌtɪt / (AmE), / ˈbʌʃˌtɪt / (BrE)
a very small, active songbird of western North America, with greyish plumage, belonging to the family Aegithalidae (Psaltriparus minimus)

Examples

A bushtit bird
A flock of bushtits darted through the branches, searching for insects.
Despite their small size, bushtits are constantly on the move in the shrubbery.
We spotted a bushtit building a delicate nest in the garden.
