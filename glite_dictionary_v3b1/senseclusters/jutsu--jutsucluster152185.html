jutsu

noun
clever technique
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuːtsu / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːtsu / (BrE)
an informal term for a clever method, trick, or technique used to achieve a goal, often in a creative or skillful way

Examples

Coding jutsu
The programmer used a clever jutsu to solve the problem in just a few lines of code.
She impressed everyone with her negotiation jutsu during the meeting.
Marketing teams sometimes talk about growth jutsu to mean clever tricks that boost engagement quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: technique - a way of carrying out a particular task, especially a skillful one
  • synonym: trick - a clever or skillful action or method
