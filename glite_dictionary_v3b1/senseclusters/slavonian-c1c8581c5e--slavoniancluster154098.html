Slavonian

noun
person from Slavonia
Pronunciation: / sləˈvoʊniən / (AmE), / sləˈvəʊniən / (BrE)
a person who comes from Slavonia, a region in eastern Croatia

Examples

A Slavonian cook
The Slavonian shared traditional recipes from her homeland with the group.
Many Slavonians celebrate their cultural festivals with music and dance.
He is proud to be a Slavonian and often visits his family in eastern Croatia.
