carry a torch

verb
have unrequited romantic feelings
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkæri ə tɔrtʃ / (AmE), / ˈkæri ə tɔːtʃ / (BrE)
to have strong, often unrequited romantic feelings for someone, especially for a long time

Examples

To carry a torch for someone
Even after all these years, she still carries a torch for her college boyfriend.
He tried to move on, but it was clear he carried a torch for his ex-wife.
Whenever they meet, you can tell she's carrying a torch by the way she looks at him.

Related Terms

  • antonym: get over - to recover from romantic feelings for someone
  • synonym: pine (for) - to yearn deeply or long for someone, especially with unrequited love
