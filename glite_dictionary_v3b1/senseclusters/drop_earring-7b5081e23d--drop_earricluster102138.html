drop earring

noun
earring that hangs below the earlobe
6.5
Pronunciation: / drɑp ˈɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / drɒp ˈɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
An earring designed to hang below the earlobe, often featuring a pendant ornament.

Examples

A pair of drop earrings
I prefer drop earrings over studs for formal occasions.
She wore a stunning pair of drop earrings to the party last night.
The delicate drop earring caught the light as she moved.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stud earring - an earring that sits directly on the earlobe without hanging
