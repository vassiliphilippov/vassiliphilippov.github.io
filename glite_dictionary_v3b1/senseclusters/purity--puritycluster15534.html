purity

noun
class homogeneity measure (ML)
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈpjʊr.ɪ.ti/ /; / /ˈpjʊr.ə.ti/ (AmE), /ˈpjʊə.rɪ.ti/ /; / /ˈpjɔː.rɪ.ti/ (BrE)
(in data analysis, machine learning) a measure of how homogeneous a sample, subset, or cluster is with respect to a particular class label; high purity means most items belong to the same class.

Examples

Cluster purity
The leaf nodes achieved high purity, indicating strong class homogeneity in the dataset.
We measured the decision tree's purity after each split to choose the best feature.
Low cluster purity showed that many samples were mislabelled or overlapping between classes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: entropy - a measure (in information theory) of disorder or uncertainty; often inversely related to purity in classification
  • synonym: homogeneity - the quality of being composed of similar or identical parts; similarity within a set
