yerba

noun
aromatic herb
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛrbə / (AmE), / ˈjɜːbə / (BrE)
an herb or aromatic plant, especially one used for cooking or medicinal purposes

Examples

Fresh yerba herb
She added fresh yerba to the soup to enhance its flavor.
Traditional healers often use yerba in natural remedies for common ailments.
In some cultures, yerba is dried and brewed as a medicinal tea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: herb - a plant used for flavoring, food, medicine, or perfume
  • synonym: spice - a substance from a plant used to flavor food
