trackman

noun
railroad track worker
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtrækmən / (AmE), / ˈtrækmən / (BrE)
a person whose job is to maintain, inspect, or repair railroad tracks

Examples

Railroad trackman
The trackman walked along the rails, looking for any loose bolts or damage.
After the storm, a trackman was sent to inspect the railway for debris.
Each morning, the trackman ensures the tracks are safe for the first train.
The trackman started his shift early to inspect the rails before the first train arrived.
After the storm, the trackman worked overtime to repair the damaged tracks.
Each trackman is responsible for a specific section of the railway line.

Related Terms

  • antonym: engineer - a person who operates or maintains engines or technical equipment
  • synonym: railway worker - a person employed to work on a railway
