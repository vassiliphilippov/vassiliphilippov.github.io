William S. Burroughs

proper noun
American writer and Beat Generation figure
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪljəm ɛs ˈbɜroʊz / (AmE), / ˈwɪljəm ɛs ˈbɜːrəʊz / (BrE)
(1914–1997) an American writer known for his influential works on the life of drug addicts and his role in the Beat Generation.
