Upper Egypt

proper noun
southern region of Egypt, south of Cairo
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpɚ ˈiːdʒɪpt / (AmE), / ˈʌpə ˈiːdʒɪpt / (BrE)
the southern part of Egypt, extending from south of Cairo to the border with Sudan, historically one of the two main divisions of Ancient Egypt.

Examples

Upper Egypt region
Ancient pharaohs often ruled over both Upper Egypt and Lower Egypt.
Archaeological sites in Upper Egypt attract scholars and tourists alike.
The Nile flows northward through Upper Egypt before reaching the Mediterranean Sea.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Lower Egypt - the northern part of Egypt, around the Nile Delta and north of Cairo
