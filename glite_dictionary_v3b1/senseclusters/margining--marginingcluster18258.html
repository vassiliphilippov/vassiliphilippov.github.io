margining

noun
setting page margins
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrdʒɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːdʒɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of creating, setting, or adjusting the margins or borders of a page, layout, or printed material

Examples

Margining a page
The editor spent hours margining the document to ensure it looked professional.
Proper margining is important for readability in printed materials.
She double-checked the margining before submitting her assignment.

  • synonym: layout - the arrangement of text and images on a page
