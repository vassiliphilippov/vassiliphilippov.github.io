nonalcohol

noun
person who abstains
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈælkəˌhɔl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈælkəhɒl / (BrE)
a person who does not drink alcohol or who abstains from alcoholic beverages

Examples

A nonalcohol at parties
As a proud nonalcohol, she always ordered sparkling water at parties.
The club welcomed both nonalcohols and those who occasionally drank.
After his health scare, he became a strict nonalcohol for the rest of his life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: drinker - a person who drinks alcoholic beverages
  • synonym: teetotaler - a person who never drinks alcohol
