charming scallywag

noun
playfully mischievous and appealing person
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑrmɪŋ ˈskæliˌwæɡ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːmɪŋ ˈskælɪwæɡ / (BrE)
A person who is mischievous or playfully dishonest but has an appealing or likeable quality.

Examples

A charming scallywag at party
Everyone at the party adored the charming scallywag, even though he played pranks on nearly everyone.
She has always been a charming scallywag, quick to laugh after her playful tricks.
That charming scallywag managed to turn his mischief into an advantage yet again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rascal - a mischievous or cheeky person
  • synonym: rogue - a dishonest or unprincipled but likable person
