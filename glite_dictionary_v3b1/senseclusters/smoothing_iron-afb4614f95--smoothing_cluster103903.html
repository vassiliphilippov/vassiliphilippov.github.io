smoothing iron

noun
device for removing creases from fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈsmuðɪŋ ˌaɪərn / (AmE), / ˈsmuːðɪŋ ˌaɪən / (BrE)
A device with a flat, heated metal base used to remove creases from clothes and fabrics.

Examples

A smoothing iron device
A traditional smoothing iron needs to be heated on a stove.
Modern smoothing irons come with adjustable temperature controls.
She pressed her shirt using a smoothing iron before the interview.
