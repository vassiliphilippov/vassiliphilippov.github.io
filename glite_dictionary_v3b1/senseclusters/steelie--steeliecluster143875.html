steelie

noun
steel ball or wheel cover
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈstiːli / (AmE), / ˈstiːli / (BrE)
a small steel ball used in games like marbles, or a steel wheel cover on a car

Examples

A steelie marble
He won three steelies in the marble game.
The car looked classic with shiny steelies on all four wheels.
Kids often trade steelies because they are heavier than regular marbles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hubcap - a cover for the central part of a car wheel
  • synonym: marble - a small glass or clay ball used in children's games
