centipede

noun
segmented predatory arthropod
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛn.təˌpid / (AmE), / ˈsɛntɪpiːd / (BrE)
A small, elongated arthropod with a segmented body and many legs, the front pair modified to grasp prey.

Examples

A centipede insect
A centipede scurried beneath the old log as I lifted it.
Many people are startled by the rapid movements of a centipede.
The biologist carefully captured the centipede for further study.
