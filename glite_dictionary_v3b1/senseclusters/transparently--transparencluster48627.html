transparently

adverb
in an obvious or easily understood way
1.0
Pronunciation: / trænˈsperəntli / (AmE), / trænˈspærəntli / (BrE)
in a manner that is easy to understand or see through; plainly or obviously

Examples

Transparently obvious reason
His motives were transparently selfish, as everyone could see.
She explained the process transparently, leaving no room for confusion.
The company has dealt with its clients transparently to maintain their trust.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obviously - in a way that is easily perceived or understood
  • antonym: opaquely - in a way that is not easily understood or seen through
