goddamn

adjective, adverb
vulgar intensifier for strong emotion
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑdˌdæm / (AmE), / ˈɡɒd.dæm /; / ˈɡɒdˌdæm / (BrE)
(informal, vulgar) used to strongly emphasize anger, annoyance, or surprise
