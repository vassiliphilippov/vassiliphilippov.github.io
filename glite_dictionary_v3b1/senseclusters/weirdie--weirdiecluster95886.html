weirdie

noun
strange or eccentric person
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪrdi / (AmE), / ˈwɪədi / (BrE)
a person who behaves in an unpleasantly strange or eccentric way

Examples

A weirdie person
She's a bit of a weirdie, but her creativity always impresses the team.
Some kids at school called him a weirdie because of his unusual hobbies.
The new student seemed like a weirdie at first, but soon made many friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eccentric - a person with unconventional and slightly strange views or behavior
  • synonym: weirdo - a person regarded as strange or eccentric
