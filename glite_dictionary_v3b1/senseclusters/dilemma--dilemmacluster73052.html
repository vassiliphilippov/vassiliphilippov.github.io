dilemma

noun
difficult choice between undesirable options
4.2 3.5
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈlem.ə/ /; / /daɪ.ˈlem.ə/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈlem.ə/ /; / /daɪ.ˈlem.ə/ (BrE)
a situation in which a difficult choice must be made between two or more equally undesirable alternatives

Examples

A dilemma situation
Choosing between keeping his job and moving abroad for love was a real dilemma for Mark.
She faced a moral dilemma when deciding whether to tell the truth or protect her friend.
The committee was in a dilemma over which candidate to select for the position.
