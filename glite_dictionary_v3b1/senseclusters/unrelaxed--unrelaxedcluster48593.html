unrelaxed

adjective
not relaxed; tense or strained
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈlækst / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈlækst / (BrE)
not relaxed; tense, strained, or under continual pressure or effort.

Examples

Feeling unrelaxed and tense
Despite the peaceful setting, he remained unrelaxed throughout the evening.
Her unrelaxed posture revealed her anxiety before the interview.
The unrelaxed atmosphere made it difficult to enjoy the conversation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: relaxed - free from tension or anxiety
