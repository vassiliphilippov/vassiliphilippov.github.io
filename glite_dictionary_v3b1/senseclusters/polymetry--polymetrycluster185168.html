polymetry

noun
simultaneous use of different metres in music

Pronunciation: /pɑː.lɪ.ˈmiː.trɪ/ /; / /pɑː.lə.ˈmiː.trɪ/ (AmE), /pɒ.lɪ.ˈmiː.trɪ/ /; / /pɒ.lə.ˈmiː.trɪ/ (BrE)
