beep

noun
a short high-pitched electronic signal
Pronunciation: / biːp / (AmE), / biːp / (BrE)
a short, high-pitched electronic sound, often used as a signal or warning

Examples

A loud beep
Every time you press a button, the machine emits a loud beep.
I heard a beep from my phone, signaling a new message.
The microwave lets out a beep when your food is ready.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buzz - a continuous low, humming sound
