strand wolf

noun
brown hyena of southern Africa
5.0
Pronunciation: / strænd wʊlf / (AmE), / strɑːnd wʊlf / (BrE)
Another name for the brown hyena, a wild carnivorous mammal native to southern Africa.

Examples

The strand wolf animal
Farmers in Namibia sometimes encounter a strand wolf near their livestock.
The strand wolf is rarely seen during the day as it is primarily nocturnal.
The strand wolf plays an important role in the ecosystem by scavenging carrion.
