concentrator

noun
device that increases concentration
6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːn.sən.treɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈkɒn.sən.treɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a device (or sometimes a person) that gathers or increases the concentration of a substance or signal

Examples

Oxygen concentrator
The hospital purchased a new concentrator to provide patients with purified oxygen.
A solar concentrator focuses sunlight onto a small area to generate heat or electricity.
The technician adjusted the concentrator to increase the purity of the chemical solution.
The hospital installed a new oxygen concentrator for patients with breathing difficulties.
A water concentrator can purify and condense moisture from the air in dry climates.
The technician checked the signal concentrator to ensure all data was being transmitted correctly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amplifier - a device that increases the strength of signals
  • synonym: collector - a device or person that gathers things together
  • antonym: diluter - a device or substance that makes something less concentrated
