Able to stick firmly to something, especially by adhering strongly or clinging tightly.

Examples

Tenacious glue
The tenacious glue held the two parts firmly together for weeks.
The tenacious weeds clung persistently to the garden wall.
Velcro is designed to be tenacious, making it perfect for items that need to stay attached.

Synonyms

  • adhesive
    3.5 0.22/m
    adhesive is a material or surface quality that helps things stick, while tenacious describes firm, lasting clinging or grip.
