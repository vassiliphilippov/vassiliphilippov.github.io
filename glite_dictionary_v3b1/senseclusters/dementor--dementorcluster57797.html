dementor

noun
joy-draining person
8.7
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈmen.tər/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈmen.tə/ (BrE)
a person or thing that causes gloom, discouragement, or drains others' joy or hope, especially in a figurative sense

Examples

Office dementor
Every time he entered the room, his negative attitude made him seem like a dementor to the team.
Social media can sometimes act as a dementor, draining your happiness with constant bad news.
She refused to let the office dementor ruin her good mood on Friday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: killjoy - a person who spoils the enjoyment of others
  • antonym: optimist - a person who tends to be hopeful and positive
