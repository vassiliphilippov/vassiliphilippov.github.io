segregate

verb
to separate from a group
2.4 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈse.grə.geɪt/ /; / /ˈse.grə.gət/ (AmE), /ˈse.grə.geɪt/ /; / /ˈse.grə.gət/ (BrE)
to separate or set apart from others or from the main group, often for a particular purpose or characteristic

Examples
