summerlike

adjective
resembling summer
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌmərˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈsʌməˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having qualities or characteristics similar to those of summer, especially warmth or brightness

Examples

A summerlike day
The air felt so summerlike that we decided to have a picnic in the park.
In March, the city experienced unusually summerlike temperatures for several days.
Her dress was light and summerlike, perfect for the warm afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: summery - typical of or suitable for summer
  • antonym: wintry - typical of or resembling winter
