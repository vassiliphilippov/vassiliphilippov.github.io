diaphany

noun
transparency or clearness
9.9
Pronunciation: / daɪˈæfəni / (AmE), / daɪˈæfəni / (BrE)
the quality of being transparent or allowing light to pass through; clearness or translucency

Examples

Diaphany of mist
The diaphany of the morning mist made the landscape appear dreamlike.
She admired the diaphany of the delicate curtains as sunlight filtered through them.
Artists often use materials with diaphany to create effects of light and shadow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: opacity - the quality of not letting light pass through; lack of transparency
  • synonym: transparency - the quality of being able to see through something
