sultana

noun
wife of a sultan
Pronunciation: / sʌlˈtɑːnə / (AmE), / sʌlˈtɑːnə / (BrE)
the wife or a female relative of a sultan, especially in former Muslim states.

Examples

The sultana of Turkey
The sultana played an important role in the royal court's decisions.
Many stories describe the wisdom and influence of the sultana in the palace.
The sultana was respected by both the people and the royal family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: queen - the female ruler of a country or the wife of a king
  • antonym: sultan - a Muslim sovereign or ruler
