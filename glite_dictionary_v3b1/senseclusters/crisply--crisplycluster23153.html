crisply

adverb
in a precise, clear way
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪspli / (AmE), / ˈkrɪspli / (BrE)
in a clear, precise, or sharply defined manner

Examples

To speak crisply

The artist's lines are drawn crisply, giving the image a strong sense of detail.
The instructions were delivered crisply, making them easy to follow.
