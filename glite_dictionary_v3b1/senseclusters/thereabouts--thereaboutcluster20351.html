thereabouts

adverb
approximately that time, place, or number
4.4
Pronunciation: / ðɛr.əˈbaʊts / (AmE), / ðeər.əˈbaʊts / (BrE)
At or near that time, date, quantity, or place; approximately that.

Examples

Noon or thereabouts
He plans to arrive at noon or thereabouts.
She was born in 1975 or sometime thereabouts.
The repairs will cost $500, give or take a hundred dollars thereabouts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: approximately - close to, but not exactly
