brontosaurus

noun
large herbivorous Jurassic dinosaur
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌbrɑːn.təˈsɔːr.əs / (AmE), / ˌbrɒn.təˈsɔː.rəs / (BrE)
a huge four-legged herbivorous dinosaur commonly found in North America during the late Jurassic period

Examples

A brontosaurus skeleton

Scientists once debated whether the brontosaurus was a separate species from the apatosaurus.
The fossilized skeleton of a brontosaurus was displayed in the museum's dinosaur exhibit.
