disingenuous

adjective
not candid or sincere; falsely frank

not candid or sincere, typically by pretending to know less about something than one really does; giving a false impression of honesty or straightforwardness.

Examples

A disingenuous apology
His disingenuous apology failed to convince anyone that he truly regretted his actions.
It was disingenuous of her to pretend she had no prior knowledge of the plan.
She gave a disingenuous answer, knowing full well she was avoiding the real issue.
