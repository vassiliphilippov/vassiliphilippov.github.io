television tube

noun
cathode-ray tube in a television
8.2 5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛləˌvɪʒən tuːb / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌvɪʒən tjuːb / (BrE)
a cathode-ray tube used in a television set to display images by translating electronic signals into visible pictures on a screen
