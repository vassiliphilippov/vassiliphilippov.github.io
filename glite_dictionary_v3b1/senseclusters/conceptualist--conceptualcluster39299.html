conceptualist

noun
proponent of ideas in art
8.1
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɛptʃuəlɪst / (AmE), / kənˈsɛptʃuəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who emphasizes ideas and concepts, especially in art or philosophy, rather than traditional techniques or materials

Examples

A conceptualist artist
The conceptualist challenged viewers to think about the meaning behind the installation.
As a leading conceptualist, she prioritized ideas over traditional painting techniques.
Many young artists today are influenced by conceptualist approaches to creativity.
As a leading conceptualist, she inspired others to think deeply about the meaning behind their work.
The exhibition featured several conceptualists who challenged traditional artistic techniques.
He was known as a conceptualist in the field of philosophy, always questioning established ideas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conceptual artist - an artist who focuses on the idea or concept behind the work
  • antonym: pragmatist - a person who focuses on practical results rather than ideas
  • synonym: theorist - a person who develops ideas or theories
  • antonym: traditionalist - a person who values traditional methods or styles
