flirtatious

adjective
showing playful romantic or sexual interest
Pronunciation: / flɝːˈteɪʃəs / (AmE), / flɜːˈteɪʃəs / (BrE)
Behaving in a way that shows playful sexual attraction or romantic interest; inclined to flirt.

Examples

Flirtatious smile
His flirtatious comments made everyone laugh, but she rolled her eyes.
She gave him a flirtatious smile across the crowded room.
The waitress was friendly but not at all flirtatious with her customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coquettish - behaving in a playful, teasing manner to attract attention, especially romantically; like a coquette
