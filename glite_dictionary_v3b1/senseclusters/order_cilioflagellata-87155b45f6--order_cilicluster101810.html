order Cilioflagellata

noun
taxonomic order of single-celled organisms
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər sɪliˌoʊflædʒəˈleɪtə / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə sɪlɪˌəʊflædʒɪˈleɪtə / (BrE)
a former taxonomic order of single-celled organisms, variably classified in protozoans or algae, now often placed in the phylum Pyrrophyta

Examples

The order Cilioflagellata group
Research on the order Cilioflagellata has led to a better understanding of plankton diversity.
