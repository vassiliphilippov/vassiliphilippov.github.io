physiotherapy

noun
treatment using physical methods
3.0 3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪzi̯oʊˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / ˌfɪziəʊˈθerəpi / (BrE)
the treatment of disease, injury, or deformity by physical methods such as exercise, massage, and other techniques, rather than by drugs or surgery

Examples

Physiotherapy sessions
After his knee surgery, he attended physiotherapy sessions twice a week.
Her doctor recommended physiotherapy to help recover flexibility after the accident.
Many stroke patients benefit greatly from regular physiotherapy.

  • synonym: physical therapy - the American English term for physiotherapy
