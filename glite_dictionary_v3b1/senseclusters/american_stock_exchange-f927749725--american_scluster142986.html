American Stock Exchange

noun
New York stock exchange (the AMEX)

Pronunciation: /ə.ˈmɛr.ɪ.kən stɑːk ɪks.ˈtʃeɪndʒ/ /; / /ə.ˈmer.ɪ.kən stɑːk ɪks.ˈtʃeɪndʒ/ (AmE), /ə.ˈmɛr.ɪ.kən stɒk ɪks.ˈtʃeɪndʒ/ /; / /ə.ˈmer.ɪ.kən stɒk ɛks.ˈtʃeɪndʒ/ (BrE)
a stock exchange in New York City, formerly known as the New York Curb Exchange, handling stocks and securities not listed on the New York Stock Exchange.

Examples
