Samaritan

noun
member of ancient Samaria people
7.9
Pronunciation: / səˈmærɪtən / (AmE), / səˈmærɪtən / (BrE)
a member of a people inhabiting Samaria in biblical times, especially known from biblical and later Jewish and Christian traditions

Examples

Ancient Samaritan people
Archaeologists have discovered artifacts that shed light on Samaritan practices in ancient Samaria.
In the parable, a Samaritan is portrayed as helping a wounded traveler.
The Samaritan community maintained distinct religious traditions from those of other Israelites.
