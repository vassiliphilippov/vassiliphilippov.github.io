dialogically

adverb
involving dialogue
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪəˈlɑːdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that involves dialogue or an exchange of ideas between people

Examples

To respond dialogically
The students were asked to respond dialogically to each other's ideas during the seminar.
Effective learning often happens when people interact dialogically rather than just listening passively.
The debate was structured so that participants could engage dialogically and build on each other's arguments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conversationally - in a way that relates to conversation
  • antonym: monologically - in a manner involving only one speaker or viewpoint
