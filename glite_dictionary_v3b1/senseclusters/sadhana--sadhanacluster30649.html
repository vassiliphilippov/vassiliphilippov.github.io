sadhana

noun
disciplined practice
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːdɑːnə / (AmE), / ˈsɑːdɑːnə / (BrE)
a regular spiritual or disciplined practice, especially in yoga or Hinduism, aimed at personal growth or enlightenment

Examples

Daily sadhana practice
Her daily sadhana of meditation helped her stay calm and focused throughout the year.
He approached his piano practice as a sadhana, dedicating hours each day to improvement.
Completing a marathon required months of physical and mental sadhana.
Every morning, she dedicated time to her sadhana before starting the day.
The teacher explained that sadhana is essential for spiritual progress in yoga.
He found peace through daily sadhana and mindful breathing exercises.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discipline - training or practice to develop skill or self-control
  • antonym: laziness - lack of effort or discipline
  • synonym: meditation - the practice of focusing the mind for relaxation or spiritual purposes
  • synonym: ritual - a set of fixed actions performed regularly, often for religious reasons
  • synonym: routine - a regular way of doing things
