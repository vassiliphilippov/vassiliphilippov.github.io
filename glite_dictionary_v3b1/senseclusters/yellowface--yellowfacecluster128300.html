yellowface

noun
offensive Asian imitation
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊˌfeɪs / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊˌfeɪs / (BrE)
the offensive practice of a non-Asian person imitating Asian appearance, culture, or mannerisms, especially in performance or media

Examples

Yellowface in movies
Many people now recognize yellowface as a harmful and outdated practice in film and theater.
The actor apologized for participating in yellowface roles earlier in his career.
Critics condemned the use of yellowface in the new television series.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blackface - the offensive practice of non-Black people imitating Black appearance or culture
  • antonym: representation - the depiction or portrayal of someone or something in a particular way
