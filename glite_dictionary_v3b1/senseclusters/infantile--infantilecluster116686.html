infantile

adjective
relating to infants or infancy
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.fən.taɪl / (AmE), / ˈɪn.fən.taɪl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of infants or infancy; typical of a baby or young child.

Examples

Infantile amnesia
He suffered from infantile amnesia and could not remember anything from early childhood.
Her artwork showed an infantile style, with bright colors and simple shapes.
The doctor diagnosed the child with infantile paralysis, which affected his muscle control.

Related Terms

  • synonym: childish - typical of a child; immature
  • antonym: mature - fully developed physically; showing adult qualities
