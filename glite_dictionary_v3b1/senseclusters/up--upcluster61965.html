up

adjective
showing increase or improvement
Pronunciation: / ʌp / (AmE), / ʌp / (BrE)
showing an increase, improvement, or functioning normally (such as a market, trend, or system)

Examples

Sales are up
Confidence in the economy tends to be up during times of low unemployment.
It's been an up year for the tech industry, with many companies reporting growth.
The stock market was up for the third consecutive day.
If the network isn't up, you won't be able to access your email.
The IT team worked quickly to get the website up again after the outage.
The server has been up all night without interruption.
