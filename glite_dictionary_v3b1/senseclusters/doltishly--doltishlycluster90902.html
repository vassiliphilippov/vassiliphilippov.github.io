doltishly

adverb
in a stupid or foolish manner
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊltɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈdəʊltɪʃli / (BrE)
In a stupid or foolish manner; showing a lack of intelligence or good sense.

Examples

To doltishly ignore signs
He doltishly ignored the warning signs posted all along the path.
He doltishly spent all his money on cheap trinkets at the fair.
The mistake was made because she approached the problem doltishly, without thinking it through.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foolishly - in a way that lacks good sense or judgement
  • synonym: stupidly - in a stupid manner
