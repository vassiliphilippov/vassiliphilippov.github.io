Uganda

proper noun
country in East Africa
Pronunciation: / juˈɡændə / (AmE), / juˈɡɑːndə / (BrE)
A landlocked country in East Africa, which became independent from the United Kingdom in 1962.

Examples

Uganda country in Africa
