earth god

noun
deity of earth and fertility
Pronunciation: / ɝːθ ɡɑd / (AmE), / ɜːθ ɡɒd / (BrE)
A deity believed to preside over the earth, particularly associated with fertility and vegetation in mythology or religion.

Examples

An earth god worshipped
In ancient mythology, people worshipped an earth god to ensure bountiful harvests.
The earth god is often depicted with symbols of growth and nature.
Villagers offered sacrifices to the earth god during planting season.
