upline

noun
earlier supply stage
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a stage, participant, or supplier earlier in a production, distribution, or supply chain, closer to the original source.

Examples

Upline supplier
The factory had to stop production because an upline partner failed to deliver raw materials on time.
Problems in the upline supply chain can affect every company further down the line.
Our manager contacted the upline distributor to resolve the shipment issue quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: supplier - a person or company that provides goods or services
