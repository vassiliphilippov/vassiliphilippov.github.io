genus Tyto

noun
genus of barn owls (Tytonidae)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈtaɪtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈtɪtəʊ / (BrE)
A genus of owls in the family Tytonidae, commonly known as barn owls, characterized by a heart-shaped facial disc.
