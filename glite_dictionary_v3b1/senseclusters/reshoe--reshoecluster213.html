reshoe

verb
to fit with new shoes
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈʃuː / (AmE), / ˌriːˈʃuː / (BrE)
to fit with new shoes again, especially referring to horses or certain machines

Examples

To reshoe a horse
The farrier will reshoe the horse before the next race.
It is important to reshoe your horse regularly to keep its hooves healthy.
The mechanic had to reshoe the machine after the old parts wore out.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shoe - to fit a horse or machine with shoes
  • antonym: unshoe - to remove shoes from a horse or machine
