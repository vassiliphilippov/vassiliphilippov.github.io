garden rhubarb

noun
edible-stalked plant used in cooking
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrdn ˈruːbɑrb / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn ˈruːbɑːb / (BrE)
A plant with thick, edible red or green stalks, often cooked and used in pies, sauces, or preserves.

Fresh garden rhubarb
