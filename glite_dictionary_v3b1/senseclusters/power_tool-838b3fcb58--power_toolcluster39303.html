power tool

noun
electrically or motor-driven tool
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊər tuːl /; / ˈpaʊ.ɚ tuːl / (AmE), / ˈpaʊə tʊːl /; / ˈpaʊ.ə tuːl / (BrE)
