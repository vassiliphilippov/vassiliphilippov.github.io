equivocation

noun
use of ambiguous language to deceive
Pronunciation: / ɪˌkwɪvəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌkwɪvəˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the use of ambiguous or vague language to deceive, mislead, or avoid giving a clear answer
