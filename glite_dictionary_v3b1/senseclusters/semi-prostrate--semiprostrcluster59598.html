semi-prostrate

adjective
partly lying flat (botany)
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈprɑːstreɪt / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈprɒstreɪt / (BrE)
(of a plant or part of a plant) lying flat on the ground for part of its length, but not completely prostrate.

Examples

Semi-prostrate plant
He selected a semi-prostrate variety of juniper for the rock garden.
The semi-prostrate branches of the shrub made it ideal for ground cover.
This species is characterized by its semi-prostrate growth habit and evergreen foliage.
