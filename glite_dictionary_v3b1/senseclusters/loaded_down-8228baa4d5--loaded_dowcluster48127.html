loaded down

adjective
carrying a heavy load
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊdəd daʊn / (AmE), / ˈləʊdɪd daʊn / (BrE)
carrying or supporting a heavy physical weight or burden

Examples

Loaded down with bags
After shopping, my backpack was loaded down and felt twice as heavy as before.
She was loaded down with groceries and could barely walk to her car.
The donkey is loaded down with supplies for the long journey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burdened - carrying a heavy load or burden
  • synonym: overloaded - carrying or bearing more than can be easily supported
