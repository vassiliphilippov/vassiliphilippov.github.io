Japanese bittersweet

noun
ornamental Asian vine with orange-yellow fruit
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈpænɪs ˈbɪtərˌswit / (AmE), / dʒəˈpænɪs ˈbɪtəˌswiːt / (BrE)
an ornamental climbing vine native to Asia, with orange-yellow fruit and a scarlet covering around the seeds, naturalized in North America

Examples
