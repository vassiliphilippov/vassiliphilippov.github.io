Examples

To indemnify workers
Employers are required to indemnify their workers for injuries sustained on the job.
The company agreed to indemnify its directors against any legal costs incurred in the lawsuit.
The insurance policy will indemnify the homeowners against damage caused by fire.
After the lawsuit, the firm was required to indemnify the customers for damages caused by its product.
The company agreed to indemnify its employees for expenses incurred during business trips.
The insurance policy will indemnify the homeowner against any future losses.

Synonyms

  • compensate
    4.2 7.55/m

  • recompense
    7.4 0.08/m
    recompense is a formal word for paying or making amends for loss or work, while indemnify promises legal protection by covering losses.

Related Terms

  • antonym: expose - to leave someone unprotected from potential loss or harm
  • synonym: insure - to provide compensation in case of loss, damage, or liability, especially through an insurance policy
  • antonym: penalize - to subject someone to a penalty or punishment
  • synonym: reimburse - to pay back money to someone that they have spent or lost
