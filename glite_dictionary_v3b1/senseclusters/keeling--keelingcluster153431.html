keeling

verb
to tip over
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkiː.lɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈkiː.lɪŋ/ (BrE)
turning or falling over, especially of a boat or ship losing balance and tipping to one side

Examples

Boat keeling over
The small boat was keeling dangerously in the rough water.
As the wind picked up, the sailboat started keeling to one side.
He lost his balance and felt himself keeling over onto the sand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capsize - to overturn in the water
  • antonym: right - to return to an upright position
