allonge

noun
paper for endorsements
9.9
Pronunciation: /əlʌnʒ/ (AmE), /əlʌnʒ/ (BrE)
a slip of paper attached to a document, especially to provide space for additional endorsements or signatures

Examples

A contract allonge
The lawyer attached an allonge to the contract to allow for more signatures.
When the check ran out of space, an allonge was added for further endorsements.
Banks often require an allonge when there is not enough room for all necessary signatures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: endorsement - a signature or statement added to a document
