mammographic

adjective
of breast X-rays
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌmæməˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌmaməˈɡrafɪk / (BrE)
relating to or produced by mammography, an X-ray technique used to examine the breasts

Examples

Mammographic scans
The radiologist carefully examined the mammographic scans for any unusual findings.
A mammographic screening is recommended for women over the age of forty.
Technicians must ensure that mammographic images are clear and accurate for diagnosis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mammography - an X-ray examination of the breasts
  • synonym: radiographic - relating to X-ray imaging
