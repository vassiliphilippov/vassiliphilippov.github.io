filmlike

adjective
resembling a thin layer
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪlmˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈfɪlmˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the qualities of a thin layer or membrane, especially as a coating or residue on a surface

Examples

Filmlike layer
A filmlike substance formed on the pond after the rain.
The medication left a filmlike coating on the inside of the glass.
After cleaning, a filmlike residue was still visible on the window.

Related Terms

  • synonym: membranous - having the characteristics of a thin, flexible layer
  • antonym: thick - having a large distance between opposite surfaces
