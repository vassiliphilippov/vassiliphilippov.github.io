snagger

noun
person who grabs opportunities
Pronunciation: / ˈsnæɡər / (AmE), / ˈsnæɡə / (BrE)
a person who quickly gets or secures desirable things, often by taking advantage of opportunities

Examples

A deal snagger
As a skilled snagger, he always managed to get the best deals during sales.
The snagger in the group secured the last available table at the busy restaurant.
She prided herself on being a snagger of rare collectibles at auctions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grabber - a person who seizes things quickly or eagerly
  • synonym: opportunist - a person who takes advantage of opportunities for personal gain
