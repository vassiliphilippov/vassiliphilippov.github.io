tofu

noun
cheeselike food from soybeans
1.9 2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈtoʊ.fuː/ (AmE), /ˈtəʊ.fuː/ (BrE)
A soft, white, cheeselike food made by coagulating soy milk and pressing the resulting curds, commonly used in East Asian and vegetarian cuisine.

Examples

Soft tofu block
Many vegans use tofu as a substitute for meat in their diets.
She added cubes of tofu to the vegetable stir-fry for extra protein.
The recipe calls for firm tofu, which holds its shape when cooked.
