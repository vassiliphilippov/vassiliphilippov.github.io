melodramatist

noun
overly dramatic person
8.8
Pronunciation: /me.lə.ˈdræm.ə.tɪst/ /; / /mə.lə.ˈdræm.ə.tɪst/ (AmE), /me.lə.ˈdrɑː.mə.tɪst/ /; / /mə.lə.ˈdrɑː.mə.tɪst/ (BrE)
a person who behaves in an overly emotional or exaggerated way, often making ordinary situations seem dramatic

Examples

Overreacting melodramatist
Whenever a minor issue arose, he would become a complete melodramatist and make it seem like a crisis.
Her friends teased her for being a melodramatist when she reacted so strongly to the lost keys.
At family gatherings, his melodramatist tendencies always made small disagreements seem like major arguments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drama queen - a person who reacts to situations in an exaggerated, emotional way
  • antonym: realist - a person who accepts situations as they are and deals with them practically
