emasculate

adjective
having unmanly or feminine qualities
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmæskjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈmæskjʊlət / (BrE)
(of a person, action, or thing) having qualities regarded as unmanly or lacking traditional masculine strength.

Examples

Emasculate style
Critics called his writing style too emasculate for a magazine aimed at men.
In the novel, the once powerful king is portrayed as emasculate after his defeat.
The leader worried that compromise would make the government appear emasculate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: effeminate - having or showing qualities regarded as more typical of women than men
