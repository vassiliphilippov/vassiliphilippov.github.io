courtly

adjective
refined and dignified; suitable for a royal court
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrtli / (AmE), / ˈkɔːtli / (BrE)
having a very refined, elegant, or dignified manner or appearance, especially in a way regarded as suitable for a royal court

Examples

Courtly manners
Despite his humble origins, his courtly behavior set him apart from others.
She gave him a courtly bow before leaving the room.
The ambassador's courtly manners impressed everyone at the banquet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gallant - brave, heroic, or chivalrous, often elegant
  • antonym: uncouth - lacking good manners or refinement
