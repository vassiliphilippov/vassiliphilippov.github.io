Synonyms

  • blossoming
    2.5 0.54/m
    blossoming is the period when buds open and bright flowers appear across a plant, while blooming describes the plant making flowers.
  • flowering
    2.0 3.24/m
    flowering is the stage when a plant produces its flowers for reproduction, while blooming is a general term for plants making flowers.
  • efflorescence
    9.3 0.01/m
    efflorescence is a formal or scientific term for the time flowers open on plants, while blooming is common everyday wording.
  • florescence
    8.4 0.05/m

  • bloom
