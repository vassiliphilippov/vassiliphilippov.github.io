civic duty

noun
responsibilities of a citizen
2.6 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪv.ɪk ˈduː.tiː/ /; / /ˈsɪv.ɪk ˈdjuː.tiː/ (AmE), /ˈsɪv.ɪk ˈdjuː.ti/ /; / /ˈsɪv.ɪk ˈdʒuː.ti/ (BrE)
the responsibilities or obligations of a citizen to their community or country, such as obeying laws, voting, or serving on a jury

Examples

Civic duty to vote
He believes that paying taxes is an essential civic duty for all responsible citizens.
Jury service is a civic duty that helps uphold the justice system.
Voting in elections is considered a civic duty in many democratic societies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: civic responsibility - a citizen's obligation to contribute to society or community
  • antonym: civic right - a right afforded to citizens by law
