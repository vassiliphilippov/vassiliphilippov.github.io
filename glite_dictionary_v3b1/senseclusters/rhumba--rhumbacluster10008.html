rhumba

noun
syncopated dance music style
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌmbə / (AmE), / ˈrʌmbə / (BrE)
A style of syncopated music in duple time, suitable for dancing the rumba.

Examples

Rhumba music plays
She learned to dance the rhumba during her vacation in Cuba.
The band played a lively rhumba at the party, and everyone joined in.
The musicians performed a traditional rhumba that filled the room with energy.

Related Terms
