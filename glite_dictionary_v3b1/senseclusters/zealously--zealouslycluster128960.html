zealously

adverb
in an enthusiastic and energetic way
Pronunciation: / ˈziːləsli / (AmE), / ˈziːləsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows great energy and enthusiasm for something.

Examples

To zealously pursue goals
He zealously pursued his ambition to become a scientist.
She zealously defended her beliefs during the heated debate.
The volunteers zealously organized the charity event, ensuring every detail was perfect.

Related Terms

  • antonym: indifferently - in an uncaring or uninterested way
  • synonym: zealously - in a zealous way; showing enthusiasm
