unreadiness

noun
lack of preparation
5.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɛdinəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɛdinəs / (BrE)
the state of not being prepared or willing to do something

Examples

Her unreadiness for interview
Her unreadiness for the interview was obvious when she could not answer basic questions.
The team's unreadiness led to mistakes during the important match.
He admitted his unreadiness to take on such a big responsibility at work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: readiness - the state of being fully prepared or willing
  • synonym: unpreparedness - the state of not being ready or equipped
