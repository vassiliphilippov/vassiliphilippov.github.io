cognizable

adjective
capable of being known or recognized
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːɡ.nə.zə.bəl /; / ˈkɑːɡnəzaɪzəbəl / (AmE), / ˈkɒɡ.nɪ.zə.bəl /; / ˈkɒɡnʌɪzəbəl / (BrE)
