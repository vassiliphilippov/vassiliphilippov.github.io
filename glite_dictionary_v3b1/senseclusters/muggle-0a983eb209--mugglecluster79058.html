Muggle

noun
non-expert person
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌɡəl / (AmE), / ˈmʌɡl̩ / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who lacks knowledge, skill, or involvement in a particular activity or field; a non-expert

Examples

A computer Muggle
As a complete Muggle with computers, he asked colleagues to install the software for him.
She felt like a Muggle at the science conference, surrounded by experts.
In the world of chess, I am just a Muggle trying to learn the basics.

Related Terms

  • antonym: expert - a person with a high level of knowledge or skill in a particular area
  • synonym: novice - a person new to and inexperienced in a job or situation
