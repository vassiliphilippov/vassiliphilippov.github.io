pee-on

noun
derogatory term for a person of low rank or status
Pronunciation: / ˈpiː.ɑn / (AmE), / ˈpiː.ɒn / (BrE)
(slang, derogatory) An unimportant person or someone of low rank or status, often considered insignificant or looked down upon.

Examples

A pee-on person
At the meeting, he was treated like a pee-on by the senior staff.
I'm tired of being a pee-on who gets no recognition for my hard work.
She refused to let anyone talk to her as if she were just a pee-on.
