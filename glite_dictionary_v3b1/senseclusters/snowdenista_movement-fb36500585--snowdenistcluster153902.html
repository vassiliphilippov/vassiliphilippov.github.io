Snowdenista movement

noun
movement for privacy and whistleblower rights
Pronunciation: / ˌsnoʊdn̩ˈistə ˈmuːvmənt / (AmE), / ˌsnəʊdn̩ˈiːstə ˈmuːvmənt / (BrE)
A movement advocating for increased privacy protections and whistleblower rights, inspired by the actions of Edward Snowden.

Examples

The Snowdenista movement grows
After the disclosures, many joined the Snowdenista movement to support open government and protection for whistleblowers.
Members of the Snowdenista movement often advocate for stronger digital privacy laws.
The Snowdenista movement has gained traction online.

Related Terms

  • synonym: privacy movement - activism in favor of protecting personal privacy
  • synonym: whistleblower - a person who exposes wrongdoing within an organization
