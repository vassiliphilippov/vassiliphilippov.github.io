minilesson

noun
brief teaching session
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪniˌlɛsən / (AmE), / ˈmɪniˌlɛsən / (BrE)
a short instructional session focused on a specific topic or skill

Examples

A minilesson on commas
The teacher started the day with a minilesson on using commas correctly.
Each minilesson helps students focus on one important writing skill at a time.
Before the main activity, the coach gave a quick minilesson about teamwork.
