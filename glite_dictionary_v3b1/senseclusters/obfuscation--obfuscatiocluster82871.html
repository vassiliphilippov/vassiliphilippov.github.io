obfuscation

noun
the act of making something unclear or difficult to see
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɑb.fəˈskeɪ.ʃən /; / ˌɑːbfəˈskeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɒb.fʌˈskeɪ.ʃən /; / ˌɒb.fjuˈskeɪ.ʃən /; / ˌɒbfʌˈskeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of making something unclear, confusing, or difficult to understand, often deliberately
