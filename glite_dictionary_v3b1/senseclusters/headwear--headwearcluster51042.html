headwear

noun
hats and helmets
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌwɛə / (BrE)
items such as hats, helmets, or caps that are worn on the head

Examples

Protective headwear
Cyclists are required to wear protective headwear for safety.
Traditional ceremonies often include special headwear as part of the costume.
She bought new headwear for the winter to keep warm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hat - a covering for the head, often with a brim
  • synonym: helmet - a hard or protective covering for the head
