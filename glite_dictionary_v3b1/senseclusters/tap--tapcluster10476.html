tap

noun
plug for a cask bunghole
1.5 1.0
Pronunciation: /tæp/ (AmE), /tæp/ (BrE)
a device for controlling the flow of liquid from a pipe, cask, or container; a faucet or spout

Examples

A kitchen tap
A brass tap was used to seal the opening of the wine cask.
She removed the tap to let the ale flow into her mug.
The old wooden barrel had a tap tightly fitted into its bunghole.
Make sure you close the tap tightly after using it.
She turned on the tap and waited for the water to warm up.
The kitchen tap was leaking and needed to be fixed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: faucet - a device for controlling the flow of liquid from a pipe (US synonym)
  • synonym: spigot - a device for controlling the flow of liquid from a container
  • synonym: stopper - an object used to seal a container
