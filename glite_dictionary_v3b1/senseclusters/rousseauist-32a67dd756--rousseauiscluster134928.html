Rousseauist

noun
follower of Rousseau
9.9
Pronunciation: / ruːˈsoʊɪst / (AmE), / ruːˈsəʊɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or follows the ideas and philosophy of Jean-Jacques Rousseau

Examples

A Rousseauist thinker
As a dedicated Rousseauist, he valued the importance of nature in education.
The political movement was influenced by several prominent Rousseauists in the eighteenth century.
Her views on society reflected her identity as a Rousseauist.

Related Terms

  • antonym: realist - a person who accepts a situation as it is and deals with it accordingly
  • synonym: romanticist - a supporter of the Romantic movement in art and literature
