displeasing

adjective
causing dislike or annoyance
3.5
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈplizɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪsˈpliːzɪŋ / (BrE)
causing dislike or annoyance; not pleasant or agreeable.

Examples

A displeasing look
She gave him a displeasing look after his rude comment.
The committee found the proposal rather displeasing and suggested several changes.
The room was painted in a displeasing shade of yellow that nobody liked.
