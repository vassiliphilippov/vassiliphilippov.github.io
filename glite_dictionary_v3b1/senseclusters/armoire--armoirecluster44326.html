armoire

noun
large freestanding cupboard or wardrobe
8.1 1.00/m
Pronunciation: / …ëÀêrÀàmw…ëÀêr / (AmE), / Àà…ëÀêmw…ëÀê / (BrE)
A large, often ornate, freestanding cupboard or wardrobe, originally used for storing weapons but now typically used for clothes or household items.

Examples

An antique armoire
She kept her winter coats neatly hung inside the antique armoire in her bedroom.
The movers struggled to carry the heavy oak armoire up the narrow stairs.
We found a beautiful French armoire at the flea market, perfect for the living room.

Synonyms

  • wardrobe
    1.0 1.99/m
    a wardrobe is a tall, modern closet for hanging clothes, while an armoire is an older, freestanding, often ornate cupboard used for clothes or household items.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wardrobe - a large cupboard for hanging clothes
