simple leaf

noun
a leaf not divided into parts
7.9 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪm.pəl liːf/ (AmE), /ˈsɪm.pəl liːf/ (BrE)
a leaf consisting of a single, undivided blade, not separated into leaflets

Examples

A simple leaf blade
A simple leaf is attached to the stem by a single petiole.
The oak tree has a simple leaf with a single, undivided blade.
Unlike a compound leaf, a simple leaf does not have multiple leaflets.
