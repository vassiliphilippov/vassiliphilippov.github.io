crescent

noun
curved shape like a quarter moon
5.2 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkres.ənt/ /; / /ˈkrez.ənt/ (AmE), /ˈkres.ənt/ /; / /ˈkrez.ənt/ (BrE)
a curved shape that is wide in the middle and tapers to points at both ends, resembling the shape of the moon in its first or last quarter

Examples

A sliver of cheese was cut in a crescent shape for the appetizer plate.
He admired the elegant crescent formed by the delicate silver necklace.
The crescent of land curved around the bay, sheltering the small village from the wind.
