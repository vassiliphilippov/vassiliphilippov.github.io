hike

noun
long walk for exercise or pleasure
Pronunciation: / haɪk / (AmE), / haɪk / (BrE)
a long walk, especially in the countryside, taken for exercise or pleasure

Examples

A mountain hike
A sudden rainstorm forced us to end our hike early.
The group paused for lunch during their five-mile hike.
We planned a hike in the mountains this weekend.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: stroll - a slow, relaxed walk, especially for pleasure
  • degree higher: trek - a long and often difficult journey on foot
  • synonym: walk - an act of travelling on foot, especially for recreation
