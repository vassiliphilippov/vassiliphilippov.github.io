mediastinitis

noun
inflammation of mediastinum
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmiːdiˌæstəˈnaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌmiːdiːˌæstəˈnaɪtɪs / (BrE)
a serious inflammation of the tissues in the mediastinum, the central part of the chest between the lungs

Examples
