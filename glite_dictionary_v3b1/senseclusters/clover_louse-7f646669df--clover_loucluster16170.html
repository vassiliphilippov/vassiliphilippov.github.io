clover louse

noun
insect pest of clover plants
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊvər laʊs / (AmE), / ˈkləʊvə laʊs / (BrE)
an insect pest that infests and feeds on clover plants, often causing damage in gardens or fields

Examples

Clover louse insect
A severe infestation of clover louse can quickly destroy an entire patch of clover.
Farmers monitor their fields regularly for signs of clover louse activity.
The gardener discovered a group of clover lice damaging the young shoots.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aphid - small sap-sucking insect; common garden pest
