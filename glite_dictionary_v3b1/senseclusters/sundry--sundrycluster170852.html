sundry

adjective
various; of different kinds
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌndɹi / (AmE), / ˈsʌndɹi / (BrE)
of various kinds; made up of different sorts or types, especially when grouped together unexpectedly or in no set order

Examples

Various sundry items
Among the sundry items on her desk were old notebooks, pens, and mysterious trinkets.
The market sells sundry goods from local artisans and farmers.
We ordered bread, cheese, and sundry other snacks for the picnic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assorted - of various kinds put together
  • synonym: miscellaneous - consisting of various kinds
  • antonym: uniform - of the same kind throughout
