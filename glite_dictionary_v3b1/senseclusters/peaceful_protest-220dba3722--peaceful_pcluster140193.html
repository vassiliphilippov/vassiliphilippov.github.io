peaceful protest

noun
non-violent demonstration or protest
Pronunciation: / ˈpisfəɫ ˈproʊtɛst / (AmE), / ˈpiːs.fəl ˈprəʊ.tɛst / (BrE)
a demonstration or public gathering in support of or opposition to a cause, conducted without violence

Examples

A peaceful protest march
A peaceful protest marched through downtown streets, drawing support from local businesses.
The university respects the right of students to stage a peaceful protest on campus.
They joined a peaceful protest to call for climate action in their city.

Related Terms

  • antonym: riot - a violent public disturbance
