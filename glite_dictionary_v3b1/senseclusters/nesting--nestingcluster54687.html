nesting

noun
building a nest
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnɛstɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of building or occupying a nest, especially by birds during breeding season

Examples

Bird nesting season
During spring, many birds begin their nesting in trees and bushes.
The park is closed to protect the nesting sites of endangered species.
Researchers observed the nesting behavior of swans along the riverbank.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breeding - the process of animals producing offspring
  • antonym: migration - the seasonal movement of animals from one region to another
