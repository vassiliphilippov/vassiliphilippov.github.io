sash fastener

noun
device for locking double hung windows
9.9
Pronunciation: / sæʃ ˈfæstnɚ / (AmE), / sæʃ ˈfɑːstnə / (BrE)
a device used to lock the sashes of a double hung window in the closed position

Examples

The sash fastener locks

The old wooden windows were fitted with a new sash fastener for added security.
When restoring the historic house, we replaced several broken sash fasteners on the upstairs windows.
