planetary mixer

noun
mixing machine with rotating agitator
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈplænəˌtɛri ˈmɪksər / (AmE), / ˈplænətəri ˈmɪksə / (BrE)
a type of mixing machine with a stationary bowl and an agitator that moves rapidly around the bowl to combine ingredients efficiently

Examples

A planetary mixer machine
A commercial planetary mixer can mix everything from bread dough to delicate meringues.
She prefers using a planetary mixer for making cakes because it handles thick batters well.
The bakery installed a new planetary mixer to improve the consistency of their dough.
