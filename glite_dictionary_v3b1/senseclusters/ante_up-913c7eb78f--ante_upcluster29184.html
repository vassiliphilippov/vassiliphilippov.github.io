ante up

verb
pay money owed or due
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.ti ʌp / (AmE), / ˈæn.ti ʌp / (BrE)
to pay an amount of money owed or due, especially as part of settling a debt or participating in a shared expense

Examples

To ante up money
It's time to ante up and settle your part of the bill.
She refused to ante up until she saw the product delivered.
The players were told to ante up before the next round.
