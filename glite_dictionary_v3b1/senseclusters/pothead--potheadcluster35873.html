pothead

noun
habitual marijuana smoker
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑtˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈpɒtˌhɛd / (BrE)
a person who habitually smokes marijuana

Examples

A pothead smoker
Despite being a pothead, he managed to keep up with his studies.
Everyone knew that Jake was a pothead in college.
Her parents worried she would become a pothead if she kept hanging out with that group.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stoner - a person who regularly uses marijuana
  • antonym: teetotaler - a person who abstains from alcoholic drinks and often other intoxicants
