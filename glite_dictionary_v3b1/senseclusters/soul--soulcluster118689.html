soul

noun
deep feeling or emotion
Pronunciation: / soʊl / (AmE), / səʊl / (BrE)
deep feeling or emotional intensity, especially as expressed in art, music, or performance

Examples

Soul in music
He plays the guitar with real soul, making every note count.
Her painting is full of soul and raw emotion.
The singer performed with such soul that the audience was moved to tears.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emotion - a strong feeling such as love, anger, or joy
  • synonym: passion - strong emotion or enthusiasm
