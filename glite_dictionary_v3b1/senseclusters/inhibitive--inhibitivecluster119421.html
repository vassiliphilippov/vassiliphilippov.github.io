inhibitive

adjective
preventing or restraining
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈhɪbətɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈhɪbɪtɪv / (BrE)
tending to prevent, restrain, or slow down an action or process

Examples

An inhibitive effect
The new policy has an inhibitive effect on small business growth.

High costs can be inhibitive for many people considering higher education.
