nonsteroid

noun
a compound that is not a steroid
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈstɛr ɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈstɪə rɔɪd / (BrE)
an organic compound that does not contain a steroid structure; especially, a substance that is not a steroid

Examples

A nonsteroid drug
Many anti-inflammatory drugs are classified as nonsteroids to distinguish them from corticosteroids.
Researchers are developing new nonsteroid treatments for various hormonal conditions.
The medication is a nonsteroid, reducing the risk of certain side effects associated with steroid drugs.
