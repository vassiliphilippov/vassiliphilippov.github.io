hammerstone

noun
stone tool for striking
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhæmərstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈhæməstəʊn / (BrE)
a stone used as a tool for striking or shaping other stones, especially in prehistoric times

Examples

A hammerstone tool
The archaeologist discovered a hammerstone at the ancient excavation site.
Early humans used a hammerstone to shape tools from flint.
A worn hammerstone was found among the remains of the prehistoric settlement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anvil stone - a stone used as a surface for working other stones
  • synonym: hand axe - a prehistoric stone tool with two faces used for cutting
