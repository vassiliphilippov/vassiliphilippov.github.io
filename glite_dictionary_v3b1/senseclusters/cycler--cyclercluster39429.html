cycler

noun
bicycle rider or cycling device
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪklər / (AmE), / ˈsaɪklə / (BrE)
a person who rides a bicycle or a device that automatically cycles through a set of processes

Examples

Automatic cycler device
The cycler rode up the steep hill with impressive speed.
Our laboratory uses a thermal cycler to automate the heating and cooling cycles.
Every morning, the dedicated cycler commutes to work regardless of the weather.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cyclist - a person who rides a bicycle
  • synonym: machine - a device with moving parts that performs a task
