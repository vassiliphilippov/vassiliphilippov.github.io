codfish cake

noun
savory patty of cod and potatoes
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑdˌfɪʃ keɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɒdˌfɪʃ keɪk / (BrE)
A fried or baked savory patty made from flaked salt cod and mashed potatoes, often seasoned and breaded.

Examples

A codfish cake recipe
My grandmother taught me her secret recipe for codfish cake.
She served homemade codfish cakes for breakfast with baked beans.
The restaurant is known for its crispy codfish cakes and tangy tartar sauce.
