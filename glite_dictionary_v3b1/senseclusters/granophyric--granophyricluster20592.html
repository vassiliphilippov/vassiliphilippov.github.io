granophyric

adjective
rock texture type
Pronunciation: / ˌɡrænəˈfaɪrɪk / (AmE), / ˌɡrænəˈfɪrɪk / (BrE)
describing a rock texture characterized by intergrown quartz and feldspar crystals

Examples

Granophyric rock texture
The geologist identified the granophyric structure in the thin section of the igneous rock.
Under the microscope, the sample revealed a clear granophyric texture with intergrown minerals.
Rocks with a granophyric texture often form during the late stages of magma crystallization.
