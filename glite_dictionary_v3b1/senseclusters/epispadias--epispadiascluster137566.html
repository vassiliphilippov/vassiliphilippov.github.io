epispadias

noun
congenital abnormality of the urethra (upper surface of penis)
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /e.pɪ.ˈspeɪ.diː.əs/ (AmE), /e.pɪ.ˈspeɪ.diː.əs/ (BrE)
A congenital medical condition in which the urethra opens on the upper surface of the penis instead of at its tip.
