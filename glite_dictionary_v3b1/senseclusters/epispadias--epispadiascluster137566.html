epispadias

noun
congenital abnormality of the urethra (upper surface of penis)
Pronunciation: / ˌiːpɪˈspeɪdiəs / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈspeɪdɪəs / (BrE)
A congenital medical condition in which the urethra opens on the upper surface of the penis instead of at its tip.

Examples

Epispadias condition
Parents of children with epispadias may benefit from counseling and support groups.
Surgical correction of epispadias often requires a multidisciplinary team of specialists.
The newborn was diagnosed with epispadias after a routine physical examination.
