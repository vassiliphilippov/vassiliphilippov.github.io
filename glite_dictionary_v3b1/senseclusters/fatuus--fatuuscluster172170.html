fatuus

noun
foolish person
Pronunciation: / ˈfætʃuːs / (AmE), / ˈfætjuːs / (BrE)
an archaic term for a foolish or silly person

Examples

A fatuus character
In the old tale, the fatuus was always the source of comic relief.
He was dismissed as a fatuus for believing every rumor he heard.
The letter described the king's advisor as a fatuus unfit for his position.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely or imprudently
  • antonym: sage - a wise person
