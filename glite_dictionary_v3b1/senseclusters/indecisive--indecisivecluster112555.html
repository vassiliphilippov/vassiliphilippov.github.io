Examples

Synonyms

  • irresolute
    8.2 0.18/m
    irresolute is a formal word for lacking firmness in choices, while indecisive is the more common, everyday term for that trait.
  • vacillating
    8.1 0.04/m
    vacillating shows a back-and-forth change of opinion over time, while indecisive simply means unable to decide confidently.
  • wavering
    5.5 0.28/m
    wavering describes being unsteady or doubtful in action, while indecisive emphasizes a wider lack of decision or resolve.
  • hesitant
    3.2 5.60/m
    hesitant means slow to act from doubt or fear, while indecisive suggests a general inability to make firm choices.
  • undecided
    2.9 4.14/m
    undecided describes not yet having made a choice, while indecisive implies a personal lack of firmness or decision habit.
