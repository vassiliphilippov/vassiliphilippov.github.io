bar chart

noun
chart showing data with bars
Pronunciation: / bɑr tʃɑrt / (AmE), / bɑː tʃɑːt / (BrE)
a diagram in which numerical values are represented by the length of bars to compare different quantities or categories

Examples

A bar chart shows data
A bar chart makes it easy to compare the performance of different teams.
She created a bar chart to visualize the survey responses by age group.
The results were presented in a bar chart that compared the sales figures for each month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: histogram - a graphical representation of the distribution of numerical data, similar to a bar chart but with continuous data
