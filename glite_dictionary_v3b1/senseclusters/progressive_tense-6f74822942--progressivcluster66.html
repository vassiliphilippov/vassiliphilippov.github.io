progressive tense

noun
verb tense for ongoing actions
7.8
Pronunciation: / prəˈɡrɛsɪv tɛns / (AmE), / prəˈɡrɛsɪv tɛns / (BrE)
A tense of verbs used to describe actions that are continuing or ongoing at a particular time.

Examples

The progressive tense form
English learners often struggle with when to use the progressive tense.
In the sentence "She is running," the verb is in the progressive tense.
The progressive tense is used to talk about actions that are happening now.
