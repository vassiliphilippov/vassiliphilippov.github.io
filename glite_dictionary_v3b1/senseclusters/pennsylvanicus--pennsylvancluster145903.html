pennsylvanicus

adjective
from Pennsylvania
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛn.səlˈveɪ.nɪ.kəs / (AmE), / ˌpɛn.sɪlˈveɪ.nɪ.kəs / (BrE)
used in scientific names to indicate that a species is from Pennsylvania or associated with Pennsylvania

Examples

Pennsylvanicus species name
The scientific name Tamias pennsylvanicus refers to a chipmunk species found in Pennsylvania.
Many plants with the epithet pennsylvanicus are native to the forests of Pennsylvania.
The Latin adjective pennsylvanicus is often used in taxonomy to indicate origin.
