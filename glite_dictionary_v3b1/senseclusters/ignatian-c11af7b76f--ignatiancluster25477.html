Ignatian

noun
Jesuit follower
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪɡˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
a person who follows or is associated with the teachings or institutions of St. Ignatius of Loyola or the Jesuit order

Examples

An Ignatian teacher
As an Ignatian, she values service and reflection in her daily life.
The school encourages every Ignatian to participate in community outreach programs.
He met many fellow Ignatians during the international Jesuit conference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Jesuit - a member of the Society of Jesus, a Catholic religious order
