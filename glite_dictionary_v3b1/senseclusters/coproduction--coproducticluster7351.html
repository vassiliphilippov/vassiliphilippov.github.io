coproduction

noun
collaborative service creation
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊprəˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / ˌkəʊprəˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
the process of creating or delivering services or policies through collaboration between professionals and service users, citizens, or communities

Examples

Coproduction with citizens
The city launched a coproduction initiative to involve residents in designing local parks.
Effective coproduction can lead to better public services that meet community needs.
Through coproduction, patients and doctors worked together to improve healthcare delivery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collaboration - the act of working together with others to achieve a common goal
  • antonym: isolation - the state of being alone or working separately
  • synonym: partnership - a relationship where two or more parties work together
