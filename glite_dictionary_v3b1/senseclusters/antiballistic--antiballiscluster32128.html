antiballistic

adjective
missile-intercepting
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌænti bəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌænti bəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
designed to intercept, counter, or destroy incoming ballistic missiles

Examples

Antiballistic missile system
The government invested in antiballistic technology to strengthen national security.
An antiballistic missile system was deployed to protect the capital from potential threats.
Engineers are developing more advanced antiballistic defenses to counter new missile types.
