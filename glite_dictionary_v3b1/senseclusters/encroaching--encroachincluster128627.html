encroaching

adjective
gradually invading or intruding
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈkroʊtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈkrəʊtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
gradually invading or intruding without right or permission

Examples

The encroaching development
Lawmakers discussed the impact of encroaching developments on protected natural habitats.
She was concerned about the encroaching weeds overtaking her carefully tended garden.
The encroaching sea forced the village to build higher walls for protection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: invasive - tending to spread undesirably or harmfully
  • synonym: trespassing - entering someone's property without permission
