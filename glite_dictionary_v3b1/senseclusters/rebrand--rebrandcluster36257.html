rebrand

noun
change of brand identity
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈbrænd / (AmE), / ˌriːˈbrænd / (BrE)
the process or result of changing the name, image, or design of a company, product, or organization to create a new identity

Examples

A company rebrand
The company announced a major rebrand to attract younger customers and modernize its image.
After the scandal, the organization underwent a complete rebrand to regain public trust.
Their recent rebrand included a new logo, updated website, and fresh marketing materials.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: branding - the process of creating and promoting a particular image or identity for a company or product
  • synonym: rebranding - the act of giving a new image or identity to something
  • synonym: relaunch - the act of starting something again with changes or improvements
