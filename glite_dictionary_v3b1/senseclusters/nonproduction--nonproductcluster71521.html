nonproduction

adjective
for testing or development
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnprəˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnprəˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
not used for actual production but for testing, development, or other purposes separate from live operations

Examples

Nonproduction environment
The software was first installed in a nonproduction environment to ensure it worked correctly.
Developers often use nonproduction databases to avoid affecting real customer data.
Before launching the new feature, the team ran tests on a nonproduction server.

Related Terms

  • synonym: development - used for creating or improving something before release
  • antonym: production - used for actual, live operations
  • synonym: test - used for checking or evaluating something
