reapplication

noun
applying again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of applying again for something, such as a job, permit, loan, or program, after a previous attempt.

Examples

Job reapplication
After her first attempt was unsuccessful, she submitted a reapplication for the university program.
The company allows reapplication six months after an initial job rejection.
His reapplication for the visa included additional supporting documents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: application - the act of formally asking for something, usually in writing
