reapplication

noun
applying again
4.1
Pronunciation: /riː.ə.plɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rə.ə.plɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /riː.ə.plɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /rə.ə.plɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act of applying again for something, such as a job, permit, loan, or program, after a previous attempt.

Examples

Job reapplication

His reapplication for the visa included additional supporting documents.
