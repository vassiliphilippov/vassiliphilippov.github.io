retrohepatic

adjective
behind the liver
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛtroʊhɪˈpætɪk / (AmE), / ˌrɛtrəʊhɪˈpætɪk / (BrE)
located behind or at the back of the liver

Examples

Retrohepatic area
The surgeon examined the retrohepatic portion of the inferior vena cava.
A tumor was found in the retrohepatic space during the abdominal scan.
Bleeding in the retrohepatic area can be difficult to control surgically.
