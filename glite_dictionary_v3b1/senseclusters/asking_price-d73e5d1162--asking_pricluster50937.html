asking price

noun
price set for sale of something
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈæskɪŋ praɪs / (AmE), / ˈɑːskɪŋ praɪs / (BrE)
the price at which something is offered for sale, especially in real estate or auctions

Examples

The asking price
Despite several offers, the car did not sell at its original asking price.
She refused to pay the asking price and negotiated for a discount instead.
The asking price for the house was significantly higher than its appraised value.

Related Terms

  • synonym: offer price - the price at which something is offered, typically in business or finance
  • antonym: selling price - the final price for which something is sold
