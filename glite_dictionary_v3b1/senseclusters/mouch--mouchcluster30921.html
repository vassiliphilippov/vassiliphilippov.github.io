mouch

verb
to get without paying
Pronunciation: / maʊtʃ / (AmE), / maʊtʃ / (BrE)
to take or ask for something, especially food or money, without paying or giving anything in return

Examples

To mouch a snack
He tried to mouch a cigarette from his friend during the break.
She would often mouch snacks from her coworkers instead of bringing her own lunch.
It's not polite to mouch money from people at every opportunity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beg - to ask for something as a gift or as charity
  • antonym: pay - to give money in exchange for something
  • synonym: scrounge - to try to get something from someone without paying
