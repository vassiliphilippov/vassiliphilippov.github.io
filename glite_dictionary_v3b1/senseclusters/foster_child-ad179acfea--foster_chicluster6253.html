foster child

noun
child raised by non-biological parents
4.0 3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑstɚ tʃaɪld / (AmE), / ˈfɒstə tʃaɪld / (BrE)
A child who is raised by people who are not their biological or adoptive parents, usually through a formal arrangement.

Examples

A foster child
After moving to the city, they welcomed a foster child into their home.
Every foster child deserves a safe and supportive environment.
She grew up as a foster child in several different families.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adopted child - a child legally taken as one's own
  • antonym: biological child - a child born to their parents by birth
