interagency support

noun
assistance provided between government agencies
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntɚˌeɪdʒənsi səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / ˌɪntərˌeɪdʒənsi səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
the provision of logistical or administrative assistance by one or more military services to other departments or agencies of a government

Examples

Effective interagency support
Effective interagency support is essential during national emergencies to ensure all agencies can function smoothly.
The committee discussed improvements in interagency support for future relief operations.
The military provided critical interagency support to other government departments during the crisis.
