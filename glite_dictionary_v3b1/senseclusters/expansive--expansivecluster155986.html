expansive

adjective
impressive and ambitious in scale
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspænsɪv / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɑːnsɪv /; / ɪkˈspænsɪv / (BrE)
very large, wide, or covering a broad area; impressive or ambitious in scale or scope
