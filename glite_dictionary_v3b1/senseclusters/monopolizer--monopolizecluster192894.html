monopolizer

noun
one who gains exclusive control over something
3.9 5.9
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈnɑː.pə.laɪ.zər/ (AmE), /mə.ˈnɒp.ə.laɪ.zə/ (BrE)
a person or organization that gains exclusive control over the supply or trade of a good or service

Examples

Market monopolizer
As a potential monopolizer, the company faced intense scrutiny from regulators.
Laws were enacted to prevent any single monopolizer from controlling the energy market.
The monopolizer quickly bought out all the competing small businesses in the area.
Many economists warn against the power of a monopoliser in reducing market competition.
Regulators broke up the oil monopoliser to encourage fairer prices for consumers.
The telecommunications industry was once dominated by a single monopoliser that controlled all phone lines.

  • synonym: monopolist - person or organization with a monopoly
