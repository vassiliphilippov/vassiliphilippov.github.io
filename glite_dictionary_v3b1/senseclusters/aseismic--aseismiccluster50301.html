aseismic

adjective
not affected by earthquakes
Pronunciation: / eɪˈsaɪzmɪk / (AmE), / eɪˈsiːzmɪk / (BrE)
not affected by earthquakes or not producing earthquakes

Examples

Aseismic zone
The building was constructed in an aseismic zone, so earthquake damage is unlikely.
Scientists classified the area as aseismic after decades without any recorded tremors.
Engineers prefer to build critical infrastructure in aseismic regions whenever possible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: earthquake-resistant - designed to withstand earthquakes
  • antonym: seismic - relating to or caused by earthquakes
