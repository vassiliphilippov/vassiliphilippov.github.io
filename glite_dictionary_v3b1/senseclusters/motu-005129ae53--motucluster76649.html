Motu

proper noun
Papua New Guinea language
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtu / (AmE), / ˈməʊtuː / (BrE)
an Austronesian language spoken in and around Port Moresby and the Central Province of Papua New Guinea

Examples

Motu language
