gee

noun
unit of force equal to Earth's gravity
Pronunciation: / dʒi / (AmE), / dʒi / (BrE)
a unit of force equal to the force of gravity at the Earth's surface, used to indicate the acceleration experienced by a body

Examples

Five gees of force
At high speeds, fighter pilots may endure up to nine gees of acceleration.
Cars on this roller coaster can pull up to 4 gees in tight turns.
The astronauts experienced three gees during liftoff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: g-force - acceleration felt as weight; gravitational force equivalent
