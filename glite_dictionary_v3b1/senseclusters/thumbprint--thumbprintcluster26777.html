thumbprint

noun
digital certificate fingerprint
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈθʌm.prɪnt/ (AmE), /ˈθʌm.prɪnt/ (BrE)
a short digital fingerprint (usually a hash) of a certificate or cryptographic key, used to verify identity or integrity.

Examples

Digital thumbprint
During setup, compare the server's thumbprint to the known value to verify identity.
The administrator recorded the SSH key's thumbprint in the inventory for later checks.
The certificate thumbprint displayed in the dialog helped her confirm it was genuine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fingerprint - a unique digital identifier (often a hash) used to verify certificates or keys
  • synonym: hash - a fixed-size cryptographic digest computed from data, often used as a fingerprint
