cold-blooded

adjective
deliberately cruel or unfeeling
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊldˈblʌdɪd / (AmE), / ˌkəʊldˈblʌdɪd / (BrE)
showing or feeling no sympathy or pity; deliberately cruel or callous

Examples

Cold-blooded killer
She showed a cold-blooded determination to succeed, regardless of who got hurt.
The crime was a cold-blooded act of violence carried out without remorse.
The murderer was described as cold-blooded in his disregard for human life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: callous - emotionally insensitive or unfeeling
  • antonym: compassionate - showing sympathy and concern for others
