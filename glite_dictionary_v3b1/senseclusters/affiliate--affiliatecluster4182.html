affiliate

noun
online marketer earning commissions
4.5 3.23/m
Pronunciation: / 59c8f6alicce6at / (AmE), / 59c8f6alie6at / (BrE)
(in online marketing) a person or organization that promotes a company's products or services and earns a commission for sales or leads generated through their links or referrals.

Examples

An affiliate marketer
She became an affiliate for the brand and earned commissions from each referred sale she generated.
Many bloggers rely on links from affiliates to earn steady income through product referrals and reviews.
The company removed a slow-performing affiliate from its program after months of poor conversion rates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: partner - a person or organization that works with a company to promote or sell its products
  • synonym: referrer - someone who directs customers to a business, often receiving a commission or reward
