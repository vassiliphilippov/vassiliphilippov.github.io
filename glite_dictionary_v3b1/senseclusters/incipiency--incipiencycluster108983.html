incipiency

noun
the beginning or emerging stage of something
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɪpiənsi / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɪpiənsi / (BrE)
the state or fact of beginning to exist or become apparent

Examples

The incipiency of change
Economic reforms were evident in their incipiency, with only minor changes taking place at first.
Many historians debate the incipiency of the civil rights movement in America.
The incipiency of the project was marked by uncertainty and excitement.

  • synonym: inception - the beginning of something
