powermonger

noun
power-obsessed person
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊərˌmʌŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈpaʊəˌmʌŋɡə / (BrE)
a person who seeks or uses power in an obsessive or aggressive way

Examples

A powermonger leader
The new manager was seen as a powermonger by the staff.
Many believed the politician was a powermonger who cared only about control.
In group projects, a powermonger often tries to dominate every decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authoritarian - a person who enforces strict obedience to authority
  • antonym: democrat - a person who supports democracy and equal power sharing
  • synonym: dictator - a ruler with total power over a country
