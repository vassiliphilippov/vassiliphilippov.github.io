nonsculptural

adjective
not like sculpture
8.2
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈskʌlptʃərəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈskʌlptʃərəl / (BrE)
not relating to, resembling, or characteristic of sculpture or sculptural art

Examples

A nonsculptural design
The architect chose a nonsculptural approach, emphasizing straight lines and simple shapes.
Her artwork is distinctly nonsculptural, focusing on painting rather than three-dimensional forms.
Unlike the ornate statues, the new monument is deliberately nonsculptural in its design.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sculptural - relating to or resembling sculpture
