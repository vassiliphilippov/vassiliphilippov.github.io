Ideally; if possible or if one could choose, in a way that is more desirable or suitable.

Examples

Used preferably typed
All applications must be submitted in English, preferably typed rather than handwritten.
Store the medication at room temperature, preferably in a dry place.
You should arrive early, preferably before 9 a.m.

Synonyms

    3.5 10/m
    ideally describes the best or perfect situation you hope for, while preferably states a more realistic choice you want if possible.
  • rather
    1.0 103/m
    rather shows a personal preference or degree, often in 'would rather' phrases, while preferably asks for the more desirable or suitable option.
