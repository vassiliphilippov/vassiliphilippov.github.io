three-dimensionality

noun
the quality of having three dimensions
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌθriː daɪˌmɛnʃəˈnæləti / (AmE), / ˌθriː daɪˌmɛnʃəˈnæləti /; / ˌθriː dɪˌmɛnʃəˈnælɪti / (BrE)
