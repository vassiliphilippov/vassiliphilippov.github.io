symptom

noun
sign indicating something's existence
1.0 2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmptəm / (AmE), / ˈsɪmptəm / (BrE)
a sign or indication of the existence of something, especially an undesirable situation or condition

Examples

A symptom of trouble
Fever can be a symptom of many different illnesses.
Increased anxiety is often a symptom of chronic stress.
The company's declining sales were seen as a symptom of larger economic problems.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cause - the reason something happens, as distinct from a sign
