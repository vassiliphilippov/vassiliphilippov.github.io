monthly

noun
periodical published every month
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌnθli / (AmE), / ˈmʌnθli / (BrE)
A magazine or periodical that is published once each month.

Examples

A monthly magazine
Several monthlies rely on subscriptions to fund their in-depth journalism.
She looked forward to receiving her favorite monthly in the mail every month.
The science monthly features articles from leading researchers around the world.
