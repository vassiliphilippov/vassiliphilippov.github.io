yeah

particle
seeking agreement
Pronunciation: / jæ / (AmE), / jɛə / (BrE)
used at the end of a statement to ask for confirmation or agreement, similar to 'right?'

Examples

Coming tonight, yeah?
You're coming to the party tonight, yeah?
We should leave before it gets dark, yeah?
This is the right address, yeah?

Related Terms

  • synonym: okay - used to check for agreement or understanding
  • synonym: right - used at the end of a statement to seek agreement
