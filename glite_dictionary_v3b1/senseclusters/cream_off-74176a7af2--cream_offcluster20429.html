cream off

verb
select the best for oneself
8.9
Pronunciation: / krim ɔf / (AmE), / kriːm ɒf / (BrE)
to remove the best people or the most desirable things from a group, especially for one's own advantage

Examples

To cream off the best students

The company tends to cream off the top students from leading universities.
They accused the agency of trying to cream off the most lucrative contracts for itself.
