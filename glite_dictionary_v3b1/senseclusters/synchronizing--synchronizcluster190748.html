synchronizing

noun
keeping data up-to-date across devices

(in computing) the process of keeping data, files, or system states identical and up-to-date across two or more devices, services, or storage locations.

Examples

Device synchronizing
The app's automatic synchronizing of photos across devices saved her precious memories.
Engineers scheduled nightly synchronizing of databases to prevent inconsistencies after updates.
Users complained when the slow network caused delayed synchronizing of contacts and calendar events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: replication - the process of copying data from one system to another; similar in purpose to synchronizing in many computing contexts.
  • synonym: synchronization - the process of causing things to occur or be maintained in the same state or time; often used for keeping data consistent.
