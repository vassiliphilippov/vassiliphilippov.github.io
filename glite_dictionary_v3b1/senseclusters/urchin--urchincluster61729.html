urchin

noun
spiny sea animal
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrtʃɪn /; / ˈɜːrtʃɪn / (AmE), / ˈɜːtʃɪn / (BrE)
a small sea animal with a round, spiny shell, found on the ocean floor

Examples

Sea urchin
The diver carefully avoided stepping on a sharp urchin while exploring the reef.
Children collected empty urchin shells as souvenirs from the beach.
A colorful urchin clung to the rocks in the shallow tide pool.
The diver carefully picked up a bright red urchin from the sandy ocean floor.
Many fish feed on the spines of the urchin despite their sharpness.
We saw a cluster of purple urchins clinging to the rocks at low tide.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sea urchin - a marine animal with a spherical, spiny shell
