too fatty

adjective
containing excessive fat
3.1
Pronunciation: / tu ˈfæti / (AmE), / tuː ˈfæti / (BrE)
containing more fat than is desirable or healthy.

Examples

A too fatty pizza
Being too fatty, the pizza upset her stomach.
I avoid meals that are too fatty for health reasons.
The sauce was too fatty for my taste, so I left it on the plate.
