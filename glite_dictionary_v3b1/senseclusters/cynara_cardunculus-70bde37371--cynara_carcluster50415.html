Cynara cardunculus

noun
southern European edible plant (cardoon)
Pronunciation: / saɪˈnɛr.ə kɑrˈdʌŋ.kjʊ.ləs / (AmE), / sɪˈnɑː.rə kɑːˈdʌŋ.kjʊ.ləs / (BrE)
A southern European plant with spiny leaves and purple flowers, cultivated for its edible leafstalks and roots; also known as cardoon.

Examples

The thick stalks of the Cynara cardunculus are often used in Mediterranean cooking.
