time plan

noun
installment payment scheme
6.3
Pronunciation: / taɪm plæn / (AmE), / taɪm plæn / (BrE)
a system of paying for goods by making regular installments over a period of time

Examples

A time plan payment
He paid for the furniture on a time plan, spreading the cost over twelve months.
Many customers choose the time plan option when buying expensive appliances.
The store offers a time plan to make it easier for people to afford big purchases.
