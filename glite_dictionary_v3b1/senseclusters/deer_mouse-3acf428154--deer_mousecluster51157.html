deer mouse

noun
North American small brownish mouse
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪr ˌmaʊs / (AmE), / ˈdɪə ˌmaʊs / (BrE)
A small brownish mouse of North America, especially the widely distributed species Peromyscus maniculatus, known for its agility and deer-like coloring.

Examples

The deer mouse animal
Researchers often study the deer mouse because of its adaptability to various environments.
The deer mouse is known for carrying hantavirus in some regions.
We spotted a deer mouse scurrying across the forest floor at dusk.
