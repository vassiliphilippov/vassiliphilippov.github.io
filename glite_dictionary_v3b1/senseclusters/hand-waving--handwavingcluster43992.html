hand-waving

noun
gesturing with hands
4.9
Pronunciation: /hænd ˈweɪ.vɪŋ/ (AmE), /hænd ˈweɪ.vɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of moving one's hands to attract attention, indicate direction, or emphasize what is being said

Examples

Helpful hand-waving
The teacher's hand-waving helped students understand which direction to look.
He used a lot of hand-waving to emphasize his excitement during the presentation.
Her hand-waving made it easy for the audience to follow her instructions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gesticulation - the act of making gestures, especially while speaking
  • synonym: signal - a movement or gesture used to convey information
