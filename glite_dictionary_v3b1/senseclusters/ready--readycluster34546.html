Synonyms

  • prepared
    1.3 3/m
    prepared is slightly more formal and often shows prior action, while ready is more general and used in many situations.
  • ready and waiting
    3.0 0.2/m
    ready and waiting means fully prepared and standing by to act at once, while ready is a general state of preparedness.
  • at the ready
    3.5 1/m
    at the ready is an idiom used after tools or weapons to show immediate availability, while ready describes general preparedness.
  • on the ready
    5.3 0.01/m
    on the ready is a less common phrase meaning alert and available to respond quickly, while ready is more common and everyday.
