Wardian

noun
plant glass container
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔːrdiən / (AmE), / ˈwɔːdiən / (BrE)
a glass container designed to protect and transport plants, especially over long distances; short for Wardian case

Examples

A Wardian for orchids
The botanist carefully placed rare orchids inside the Wardian for the journey.
Collectors displayed their ferns in a beautiful Victorian Wardian on the windowsill.
During the expedition, a Wardian was used to transport delicate seedlings safely.
