cerulean

adjective
deep sky-blue color
8.1 0.41/m

Deep blue in color, resembling the clear sky on a bright day, sometimes with a purplish tint.

Examples

Cerulean sky
She wore a cerulean dress that matched the clear sky.
The artist painted the sea in a vivid cerulean that captivated everyone.
The mountain lake reflected the cerulean hue of an autumn afternoon.

Synonyms

  • azure
    5.8 0.03/m
    azure is a bright, deep blue often used in art and formal writing, while cerulean suggests a sky-like blue with a slight purple tint.
  • sky-blue
    2.1 0.25/m
    sky-blue is a common everyday name for clear sky color, while cerulean feels slightly deeper and often shows a purple tint.
