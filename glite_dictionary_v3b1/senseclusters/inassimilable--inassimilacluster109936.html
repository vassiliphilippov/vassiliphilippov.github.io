inassimilable

adjective
not able to assimilate
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnəˈsɪmɪləbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪnəˈsɪmɪləbəl / (BrE)
not capable of being assimilated or changed to match surrounding sounds or forms, especially in language or phonology

Examples

The linguist explained why the cluster was inassimilable in rapid speech.
