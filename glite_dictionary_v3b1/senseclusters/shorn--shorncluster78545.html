shorn

adjective
having hair or wool cut off
6.6 0.21/m
Pronunciation: /  É…îrn / (AmE), /  É…îÀên / (BrE)
(of hair, wool, or similar) cut or clipped off, especially as if with shears.

Examples

The monk appeared shorn of his usual thick hair, following the temple's ritual.
