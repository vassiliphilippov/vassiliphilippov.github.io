Synonyms

  • push
    1.0 108/m
    push is a normal, everyday action to move something with force, while shove is rougher, hurried, and more forceful.
  • thrust
    3.9 7.78/m
    thrust is a sudden, forward push that forces something quickly, while shove is rough, hurried, and often casual.
