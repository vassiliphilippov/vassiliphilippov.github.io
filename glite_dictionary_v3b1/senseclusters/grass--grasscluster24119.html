grass

verb
feed (an animal) with grass
Pronunciation: / ɡræs / (AmE), / ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
to feed (an animal) with grass or allow it to graze on grass

Examples

To grass a horse
Horses are often grassed in open meadows to keep them healthy.
The farmer decided to grass the cows in the south field for the summer.
They grassed the sheep early in the morning before the sun became too hot.
