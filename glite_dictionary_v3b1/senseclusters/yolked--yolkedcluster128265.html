yolked

verb
to add egg yolk
4.8
Pronunciation: / joʊkt / (AmE), / jəʊkt / (BrE)

Examples

The chef yolked the sauce to make it creamier and more flavorful.
Before baking, he yolked the dough to improve its texture.
