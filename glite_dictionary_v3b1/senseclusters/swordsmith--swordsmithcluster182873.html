swordsmith

noun
maker of swords
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔrdˌsmɪθ / (AmE), / ˈsɔːdˌsmɪθ / (BrE)
a person who makes or repairs swords as a craft or profession

Examples

The swordsmith shapes blades
The swordsmith carefully shaped the blade over the hot coals.
Many warriors trusted the local swordsmith to craft their weapons.
A skilled swordsmith can repair even a badly damaged sword.

Related Terms

  • synonym: armorer - a person who makes or repairs armor and weapons
  • synonym: blacksmith - a person who makes and repairs things in iron by hand
