cesarian

adjective
relating to caesarean delivery
5.5 3.8
Pronunciation: /sə.ˈzɪr.iː.ən/ /; / /siː.ˈzɪr.iː.ən/ (AmE), /sə.ˈzɪə.riː.ən/ /; / /siː.ˈzɪə.riː.ən/ (BrE)
relating to a surgical operation in which a baby is delivered through an incision in the mother's abdomen and uterus; relating to a caesarean section.

Examples

Cesarian operation
Her recovery after the cesarian operation was quick and uneventful.
The doctor recommended a cesarian delivery due to medical complications.
The patient was prepared for a cesarian birth as the safest option for her baby.
