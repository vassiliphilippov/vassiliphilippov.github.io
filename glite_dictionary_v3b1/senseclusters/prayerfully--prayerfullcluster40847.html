prayerfully

adverb
with reverence or hope
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛərfəli / (AmE), / ˈpreəfəli / (BrE)
in a way that shows deep reverence, focus, or hope, as if in prayer

Examples

Look prayerfully at sky
She looked prayerfully at the sky, hoping for good news.
He waited prayerfully outside the hospital room for any update on his friend.
The children listened prayerfully as their teacher spoke about kindness and hope.
She prayerfully considered her options before making a difficult decision.
The congregation sat prayerfully in silence as the ceremony began.
He approached the challenge prayerfully, seeking guidance and strength.
