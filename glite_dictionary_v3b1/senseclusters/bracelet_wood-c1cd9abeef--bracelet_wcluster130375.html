bracelet wood

noun
West Indian shrub/tree with decorative seeds
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪslət wʊd / (AmE), / ˈbreɪslət wʊd / (BrE)
A small West Indian shrub or tree whose hard, glossy yellow and brown patterned seeds are used to make bracelets.
