Baconian

noun
believer in Bacon authorship
9.9
Pronunciation: /beɪ.ˈkoʊ.nɪ.ən/ /; / /bə.ˈkoʊ.nɪ.ən/ (AmE), /bə.ˈkəʊ.nɪ.ən/ /; / /beɪ.ˈkəʊ.nɪ.ən/ (BrE)
