scat

noun
animal droppings
Pronunciation: / skæt / (AmE), / skæt / (BrE)
the droppings or feces of a wild animal, often used for identification in nature studies

Examples

Bear scat
The ranger pointed out fresh scat on the trail, indicating a bear had passed by recently.
Researchers collected samples of scat to study the diet of local wolves.
Hikers are often taught to recognize animal scat as a sign of nearby wildlife.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dung - the excrement of animals, especially farm animals
  • synonym: feces - solid waste matter discharged from the bowels
