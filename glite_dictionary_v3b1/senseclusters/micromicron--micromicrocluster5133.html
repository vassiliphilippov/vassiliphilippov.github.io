micromicron

noun
one trillionth of a meter
9.9
Pronunciation: /maɪ.krə.ˈmaɪ.krən/ /; / /ˈmaɪ.krə.maɪ.krən/ (AmE), /maɪ.krə.ˈmaɪ.krɒn/ /; / /ˈmaɪ.krə.maɪ.krɒn/ (BrE)
A metric unit of length equal to one trillionth of a meter (1 × 10⁻¹² meter); also known as a picometer.

Examples

One micromicron length
Advances in microscopy now allow scientists to study structures only a few micromicrons wide.
Some older scientific papers use the term micromicron instead of picometer.
The wavelength of certain X-rays can be measured in micromicrons.
