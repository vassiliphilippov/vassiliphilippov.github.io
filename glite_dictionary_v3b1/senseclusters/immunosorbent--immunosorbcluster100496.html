immunosorbent

adjective
binding antibodies or antigens
8.8
Pronunciation: /ɪ.mjuː.nə.ˈsɑːr.bənt/ /; / /ɪ.mjuː.nə.ˈsɔːr.bənt/ (AmE), /ɪ.mjuː.nə.ˈsɔː.bənt/ (BrE)
able to bind specific antibodies or antigens, often used in laboratory tests to detect immune responses

Examples

Immunosorbent surface
The laboratory used an immunosorbent surface to capture the target antibodies from the blood sample.
An immunosorbent assay is commonly used to test for the presence of specific viruses.
Researchers developed a new immunosorbent material to improve the accuracy of diagnostic tests.
