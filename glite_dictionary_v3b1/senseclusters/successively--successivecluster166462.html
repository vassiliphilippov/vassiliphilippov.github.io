Synonyms

  • consecutively
    4.1 0.6/m
    consecutively means one thing follows another without a break, while successively simply stresses the items happen in sequence.
  • sequentially
    4.5 1/m
    sequentially describes actions that follow a set order, while successively focuses on items coming one after another.
  • one after another
    2.3 2/m
    one after another shows events happening one by one in a row, while successively names the same sequence simply.
  • in turn
    3.3 30/m
