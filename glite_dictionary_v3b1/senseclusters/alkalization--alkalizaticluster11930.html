alkalization

noun
increase in alkalinity
Pronunciation: / ˌælkəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌælkəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making something more alkaline or increasing its pH (reducing acidity)

Examples

Soil alkalization
