bosonic

adjective
relating to bosons
9.9
Pronunciation: /boʊ.ˈzɑː.nɪk/ /; / /boʊ.ˈsɑː.nɪk/ (AmE), /bəʊ.ˈzɒ.nɪk/ /; / /bəʊ.ˈsɒ.nɪk/ (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of bosons, which are particles in physics that follow Bose-Einstein statistics

Examples

Bosonic particles
The bosonic nature of photons allows them to occupy the same quantum state.
Researchers studied the bosonic properties of particles in the new experiment.
Superfluidity is a phenomenon that can occur in bosonic systems at very low temperatures.
