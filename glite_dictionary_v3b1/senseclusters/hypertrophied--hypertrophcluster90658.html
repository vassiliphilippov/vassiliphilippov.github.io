hypertrophied

adjective
excessively enlarged (medical)
9.8 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / ha…™Ààp…úr.tr…ôÀåfid / (AmE), / ha…™Ààp…úÀê.tr…ô.fiÀêd / (BrE)
(of an organ or body part) excessively enlarged due to an increase in the size of its constituent cells.

Examples

Hypertrophied muscle tissue
A hypertrophied muscle may result from excessive weight training.
Doctors noted the presence of hypertrophied adrenal glands in the autopsy report.
The patient's heart was hypertrophied due to years of high blood pressure.
