breed

noun
a special type or kind of person or thing
Pronunciation: / brid / (AmE), / briːd / (BrE)
A particular type or kind of person or thing, especially when distinguished by notable characteristics.

Examples

A new breed of leader
In politics, a new breed of leader has emerged, more open and transparent than before.
She considered herself part of a rare breed of artists who valued originality above fame.
The company is looking for a new breed of manager who can handle rapid change.
