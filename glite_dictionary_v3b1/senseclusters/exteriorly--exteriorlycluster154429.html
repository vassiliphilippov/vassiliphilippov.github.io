exteriorly

adverb
on the outside
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɪriɚli / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɪəriəli / (BrE)
on or from the outside; in an external manner or position

Examples

Look exteriorly plain
The house looks plain exteriorly, but the interior is beautifully decorated.
The damage was only visible exteriorly and did not affect the structure inside.
The sculpture is smooth exteriorly, hiding the complex framework beneath.

Related Terms

  • synonym: externally - on or from the outside
  • antonym: interiorly - on or from the inside
