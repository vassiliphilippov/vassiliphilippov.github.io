iron carbide

noun
hard, brittle steel and cast-iron compound
5.0 5.7
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪərn ˈkɑrˌbaɪd / (AmE), / ˌaɪən ˈkɑːbaɪd / (BrE)
a hard, brittle chemical compound (Fe₃C) that is a constituent of steel and cast iron
