overcharge

noun
excessive charge or fee
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈtʃɑrdʒ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈtʃɑːdʒ / (BrE)
an amount of money that is charged in excess of a fair or correct price

Examples

An overcharge fee
If you notice an overcharge on your bill, contact customer service immediately.
Many customers complained about the frequent overcharge on their electricity invoices.
The hotel imposed an overcharge for the late checkout, which I refused to pay.

Related Terms

  • synonym: surcharge - an additional charge or payment
  • antonym: undercharge - an amount charged that is less than the correct price
