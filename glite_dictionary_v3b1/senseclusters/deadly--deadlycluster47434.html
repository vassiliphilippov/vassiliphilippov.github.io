Synonyms

  • lethal
    4.8 9/m
    lethal describes weapons, poisons, or doses that kill directly, while deadly also means extremely dangerous or harmful.
  • fatal
    3.7 12/m
    fatal describes an outcome or injury that causes death, whereas deadly can mean both fatal and generally extremely dangerous.
  • mortal
    6.0 1/m
    mortal often means causing death or being human and vulnerable, while deadly focuses on causing death or great danger.
  • deathly
    6.8 0.2/m
    deathly usually means very like death, pale, or very quiet, whereas deadly means able to cause death.
