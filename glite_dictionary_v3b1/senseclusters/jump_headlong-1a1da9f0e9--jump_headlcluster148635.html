jump headlong

verb
begin something impulsively or recklessly

To begin or undertake something quickly and without careful thought, especially in a reckless or impulsive manner.

Examples

He jumped headlong into the new job without reading the contract carefully.
Instead of planning, she jumped headlong into organizing the event on a whim.
Many young entrepreneurs jump headlong into business ventures with little experience.
