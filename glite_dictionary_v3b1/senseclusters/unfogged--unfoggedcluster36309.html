unfogged

adjective
clear of fog
4.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɑgd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɒgd / (BrE)
not covered or obscured by fog; made clear or free of fog or mist

Examples

The unfogged windshield
After the rain stopped, the car's windshield was finally unfogged and safe to drive with.
She waited until her glasses were unfogged before stepping outside into the cold morning.
The view from the mountain became unfogged as the sun rose and burned away the mist.

Related Terms

  • antonym: foggy - full of or covered with fog
