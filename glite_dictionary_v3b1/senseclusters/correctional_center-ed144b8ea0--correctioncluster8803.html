correctional center

noun
facility for rehabilitating offenders
2.5
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɛkʃənl ˈsɛntər / (AmE), / kəˈrɛkʃənəl ˈsɛntə / (BrE)
a place where people who have been convicted of crimes are held, with a focus on rehabilitating them

Examples

A correctional center
After his conviction, Mark was transferred to a correctional center to serve his sentence.
The correctional center offers job training and education classes for inmates seeking a fresh start.
Volunteers visit the correctional center every week to run support groups for offenders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prison - a building where people are kept as punishment for a crime
  • synonym: rehabilitation center - a place where people receive treatment or education to re-enter society
