photogenic

adjective
looking attractive in photographs
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
(of a person or thing) looking attractive in photographs or on film.

Examples

Photogenic person
Although the building is impressive, it is not particularly photogenic in pictures.
Many people think she is very photogenic and should consider a career in modeling.
Some animals, like golden retrievers, always look photogenic no matter the situation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unphotogenic - not looking attractive in photographs
