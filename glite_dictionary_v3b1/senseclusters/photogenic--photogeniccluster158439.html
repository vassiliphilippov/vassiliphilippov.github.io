photogenic

adjective
looking attractive in photographs
1.0 3.0
Pronunciation: /foʊ.tə.ˈdʒen.ɪk/ /; / /foʊ.toʊ.ˈdʒen.ɪk/ (AmE), /fəʊ.tə.ˈdʒen.ɪk/ /; / /fəʊ.təʊ.ˈdʒen.ɪk/ (BrE)

Examples
