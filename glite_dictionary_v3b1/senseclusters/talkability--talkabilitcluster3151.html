talkability

noun
ease to discuss
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌtɔːkəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌtɔːkəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being easy or appealing to talk about, especially in conversation or media

Examples

Talkability of a topic
The talkability of the new movie made it a popular topic on social media.
Marketers aim to increase the talkability of their products to boost word-of-mouth promotion.
A high level of talkability can help a campaign spread quickly among the public.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discussability - the quality of being suitable for discussion
  • synonym: noteworthiness - the quality of being interesting or worthy of attention
