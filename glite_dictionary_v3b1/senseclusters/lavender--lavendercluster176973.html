lavender

adjective
pale purple in color
Pronunciation: / ˈlævəndɚ / (AmE), / ˈlævəndə / (BrE)
of a pale purple color, like that of the lavender flower.

Examples

Lavender dress
As the sun set, the sky turned a beautiful lavender hue.
She wore a lavender dress to the spring festival, which complemented the flowers in the garden.
The walls of her bedroom were painted a soft lavender shade.
