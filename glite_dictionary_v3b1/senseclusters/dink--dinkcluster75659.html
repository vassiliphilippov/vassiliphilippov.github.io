dink

noun
a soft, short shot in tennis
Pronunciation: / dɪŋk / (AmE), / dɪŋk / (BrE)
(in tennis or similar sports) a soft, gentle hit that causes the ball to drop quickly and short after crossing the net.

Examples

A soft dink shot
His dink landed just out of reach of his opponent.
Instead of smashing, he surprised everyone with a delicate dink over the net.
She used a clever dink to win the point at the net.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lob - a high, arching shot in tennis
