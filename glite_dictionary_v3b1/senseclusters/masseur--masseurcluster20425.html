masseur

noun
male professional who gives massages
4.7
Pronunciation: / mæˈsɜr / (AmE), / mæˈsɜː / (BrE)
A man whose job is to give people massages.

Examples

A skilled masseur
After the long flight, a skilled masseur helped ease my stiff muscles.
He works as a masseur at a luxury spa in the city center.
Hiring a professional masseur can greatly improve recovery after sports injuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: massage therapist - person who gives massages (gender-neutral term)
  • antonym: masseuse - female professional who gives massages
