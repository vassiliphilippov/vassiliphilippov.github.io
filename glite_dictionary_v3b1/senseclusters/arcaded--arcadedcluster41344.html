arcaded

adjective
with arches
8.6
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈkeɪdɪd / (AmE), / ɑːˈkeɪdɪd / (BrE)

Examples

An arcaded building

The museum's arcaded entrance gave the building a grand and classical appearance.
