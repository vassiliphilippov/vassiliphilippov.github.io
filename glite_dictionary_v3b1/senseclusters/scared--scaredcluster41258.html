scared

adjective
afraid; feeling fear
1.0
Pronunciation: / skɛrd / (AmE), / skɛəd / (BrE)
feeling afraid or frightened; fearful because of danger, pain, or uncertainty.

Examples

To feel scared
Even though he was scared, he decided to confront his fears.
She was scared to walk home alone after dark.
The thunderstorm made the dog scared and he hid under the bed.
