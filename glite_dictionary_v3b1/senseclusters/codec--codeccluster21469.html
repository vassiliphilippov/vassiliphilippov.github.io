codec

noun
media compression tool
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊdɛk / (AmE), / ˈkəʊdɛk / (BrE)
a device or software that encodes, decodes, compresses, or decompresses digital audio or video files

Related Terms

  • synonym: compressor - a device or program that reduces the size of data files
  • synonym: decoder - a device or program that converts encoded data back to its original form
  • synonym: encoder - a device or program that converts data into a different format
