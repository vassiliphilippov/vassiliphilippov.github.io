disentitlement

noun
loss of eligibility
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsɪnˈtaɪtəlmənt / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɪnˈtaɪtəlmənt / (BrE)
the act or state of losing the right or eligibility to receive a benefit, service, or privilege

Examples

The new policy resulted in the disentitlement of several employees from health benefits.
After the investigation, her disentitlement from the scholarship was confirmed.
