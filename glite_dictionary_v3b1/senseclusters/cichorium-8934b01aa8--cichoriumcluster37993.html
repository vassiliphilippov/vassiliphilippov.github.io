Cichorium

noun
genus of plants including chicory and endive
8.9
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkɔriəm / (AmE), / sɪˈkɔːrɪəm / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the daisy family, including chicory and endive, often cultivated for their edible leaves and roots.
