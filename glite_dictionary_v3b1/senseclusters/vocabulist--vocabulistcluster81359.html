vocabulist

noun
word list compiler
9.9
Pronunciation: / voʊˈkæbjəlɪst / (AmE), / vəˈkæbjʊlɪst / (BrE)
a person who compiles, studies, or is interested in lists of words and their meanings

Examples

A vocabulist compiles dictionaries
The vocabulist spent years assembling a comprehensive dictionary.
As a dedicated vocabulist, she enjoyed collecting rare and unusual words.
Every vocabulist values accuracy and clarity in their word lists.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lexicographer - a person who compiles dictionaries
  • synonym: philologist - a person who studies language in written historical sources
