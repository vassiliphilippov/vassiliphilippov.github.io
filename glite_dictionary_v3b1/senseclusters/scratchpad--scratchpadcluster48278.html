scratchpad

noun
notepad for quick notes
Pronunciation: / ˈskrætʃˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈskrætʃˌpæd / (BrE)
a small notebook or pad used for making quick notes or calculations

Examples

A scratchpad for notes
He always keeps a scratchpad on his desk for jotting down ideas during meetings.
Before solving the math problem, she used her scratchpad to work out the calculations.
The artist filled his scratchpad with quick sketches and notes for future projects.

Related Terms

  • antonym: journal - a book for recording personal experiences or thoughts
  • synonym: notebook - a book of blank pages for writing notes
