spinning jenny

noun
early multi-spindle spinning machine
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪnɪŋ ˌdʒɛni / (AmE), / ˈspɪnɪŋ ˌdʒɛni / (BrE)
an early machine for spinning with multiple spindles, used in the textile industry during the Industrial Revolution

Examples

The spinning jenny machine
Before the spinning jenny, spinning large amounts of yarn by hand was a slow process.
Historians often cite the spinning jenny as a key factor in the early Industrial Revolution.
The invention of the spinning jenny revolutionized the textile industry in the eighteenth century.
