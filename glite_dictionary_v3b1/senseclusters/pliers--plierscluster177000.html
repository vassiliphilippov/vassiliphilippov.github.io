pliers

noun
tool for gripping or bending
Pronunciation: / ˈplaɪɚz / (AmE), / ˈplaɪəz / (BrE)
A hand tool with two hinged arms and typically serrated jaws, used for gripping, bending, or cutting objects.

Examples

A pair of pliers
He used a pair of pliers to pull out the bent nail.
Make sure you hold the wire tightly with pliers before cutting it.
The mechanic handed me some pliers to help loosen the bolt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forceps - a hand tool for holding or grasping objects, used especially in medical or laboratory contexts
