nip off

verb
remove by pinching or snipping
Pronunciation: / nɪp ɔf / (AmE), / nɪp ɒf / (BrE)
to remove or sever something by pinching or snipping, especially small parts such as leaves or buds

Examples

To nip off leaves
Gardeners often nip off faded blooms to encourage new growth.
She carefully nipped off the dead leaves from the rose bush.
Use scissors to nip off any excess thread along the seam.

Related Terms

  • synonym: snip off - to cut off with small, quick cuts
