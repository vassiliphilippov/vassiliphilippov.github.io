Dior

proper noun
French fashion designer
3.9
Pronunciation: / diˈɔr / (AmE), / diˈɔː / (BrE)
the surname of Christian Dior, a renowned French fashion designer and founder of the House of Dior, famous for post-war haute couture.

Examples

Christian Dior
The elegant dress she wore was designed by Dior for the spring collection.
Many celebrities choose Dior gowns for major red carpet events.
After World War II, Dior introduced the 'New Look' that changed women's fashion.
