richness

noun
full and pleasing quality (sound, taste, etc.)
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪtʃ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃ.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being full, deep, and pleasing to the senses, such as in taste, sound, or appearance

Examples

Richness of taste
She admired the richness of colors in the autumn forest.
The richness of the chocolate cake delighted everyone at the party.
The orchestra was praised for the richness of its sound.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fullness - the quality of being full or complete, often used for sound or appearance
  • antonym: poverty - the quality of being lacking or deficient in richness or fullness
