non-U

adjective
not of the upper classes (esp. in speech)
Pronunciation: / nɑn ju / (AmE), / nɒn juː / (BrE)
Not characteristic of the upper classes, especially in language or manners; associated with the middle or lower classes.

Examples

Non-U language
Her preference for non-U expressions often surprised her upper-class friends.
Some still regard certain words as non-U and avoid them at formal gatherings.
Using paper napkins instead of linen ones was considered distinctly non-U in that social circle.
