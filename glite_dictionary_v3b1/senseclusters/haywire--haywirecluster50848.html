haywire

adjective
not functioning properly; out of control
6.0 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈheɪ.waɪ.ər/ (AmE), /ˈheɪ.waɪ.ə/ (BrE)
(of a system, machine, or situation) not working or behaving as it should; out of control or malfunctioning.

Examples

Her plans went haywire after the unexpected storm ruined the festival.
When the signal started acting haywire, the technician tried to reset the equipment.
