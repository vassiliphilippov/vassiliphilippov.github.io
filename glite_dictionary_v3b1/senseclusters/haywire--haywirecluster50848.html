haywire

adjective
not functioning properly; out of control
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪˌwaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈheɪˌwaɪə / (BrE)
(of a system, machine, or situation) not working or behaving as it should; out of control or malfunctioning.

Examples

The system goes haywire
Ever since the power outage, the computer has gone completely haywire.
Her plans went haywire after the unexpected storm ruined the festival.
When the signal started acting haywire, the technician tried to reset the equipment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amiss - not quite right; out of order
  • synonym: malfunctioning - not working correctly
  • antonym: working - functioning as intended
