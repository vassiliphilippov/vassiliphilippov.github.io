perforator

noun
piercing vessel or structure
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrfəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈpɜːfəreɪtə / (BrE)
a vessel or structure that passes through or pierces tissue, especially in the body

Examples

A perforator vessel
The surgeon carefully located the perforator before proceeding with the operation.
A damaged perforator can lead to complications in reconstructive surgery.
Doctors use imaging techniques to identify each perforator supplying blood to the tissue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: penetrator - something that enters or pierces another substance
