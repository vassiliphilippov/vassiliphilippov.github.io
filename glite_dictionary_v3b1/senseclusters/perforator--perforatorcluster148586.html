perforator

noun
piercing vessel or structure
8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈpɜːr.fə.reɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈpɜː.fə.reɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a vessel or structure that passes through or pierces tissue, especially in the body

Examples

A perforator vessel

A damaged perforator can lead to complications in reconstructive surgery.
