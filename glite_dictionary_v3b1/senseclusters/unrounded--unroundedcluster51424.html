unrounded

adjective
not curved or rounded
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈraʊndɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈraʊndɪd / (BrE)
not having a curved or smoothly rounded shape; having flat, sharp, or angular edges or corners

Examples

Unrounded corners
The architect chose unrounded corners to give the building a modern, geometric look.
Her handwriting was characterized by sharp, unrounded letters that stood out on the page.
The furniture's unrounded edges made it look sleek but less comfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angular - having sharp corners or edges
  • antonym: rounded - having a smooth, curved shape
