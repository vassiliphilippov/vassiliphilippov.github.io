overturn

noun
the act of turning something over
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌtɜrn / (AmE), / ˈəʊvətɜːn / (BrE)
an act of causing something to turn over or be upset; a state of being overturned

Examples

A boat overturn
After the boat's overturn, everyone was accounted for and rescued safely.
Investigators examined the cause of the sled's overturn on the icy hill.
The truck's sudden overturn blocked traffic for several hours.
