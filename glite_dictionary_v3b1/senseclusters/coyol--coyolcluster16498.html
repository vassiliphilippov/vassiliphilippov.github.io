coyol

noun
tropical American palm tree with edible nuts
9.9
Pronunciation: / koʊˈjɔl / (AmE), / kɔɪˈɒl / (BrE)
A tropical American palm tree (Acrocomia mexicana) with edible nuts and a strong fiber used for various purposes.

Examples

Coyol palm tree
Baskets woven from coyol fiber are both sturdy and attractive.
Farmers harvest coyol nuts in the dry season to make traditional drinks.
The coyol is valued in Central America for both its nuts and its fibrous leaves.
