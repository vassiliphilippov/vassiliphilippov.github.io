Dunkirk

noun
a desperate last-ditch effort to avoid defeat
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌnˌkɜrk / (AmE), / ˈdʌn.kɜːk / (BrE)
a situation or event in which a desperate, determined effort is made as the only alternative to defeat or disaster

Examples

A business Dunkirk
After months of setbacks, the company had to pull a Dunkirk to avoid bankruptcy.
Facing overwhelming odds, the activists launched a Dunkirk to stop the demolition of the historic building.
The team staged a Dunkirk when they fought back in the final minutes of the game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: last-ditch effort - a final attempt to achieve something when everything else has failed
