Examples

Airtight container
Make sure the lid is airtight before shaking the bottle.

They designed an airtight seal to prevent any leaks.

Synonyms

  • gas-tight
    5.8 0.02/m
    gas-tight is sealed so no gas can pass through, while airtight means completely closed against air or gas.
  • hermetic
    8.9 0.51/m
    hermetic is completely sealed and often used for very tight, isolated containers, while airtight simply means closed to air or gas.
  • leakproof
    3.2 0.05/m
    leakproof keeps liquids or gas from escaping and usually refers to designed containers, while airtight focuses on stopping any air or gas.
