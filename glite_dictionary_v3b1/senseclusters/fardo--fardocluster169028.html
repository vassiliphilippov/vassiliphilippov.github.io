fardo

noun
large bundle or bale
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑrdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈfɑːdəʊ / (BrE)
a large bundle or bale, especially of goods or clothing, often prepared for transport or storage

Examples

A fardo bundle
The workers unloaded each fardo from the truck and stacked them in the warehouse.
Each fardo contained dozens of used clothes ready for resale in the market.
It took three people to lift the heavy fardo onto the shipping pallet.
