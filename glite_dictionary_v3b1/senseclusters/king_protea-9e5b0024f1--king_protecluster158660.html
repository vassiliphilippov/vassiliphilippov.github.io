king protea

noun
South African shrub with large artichoke-like flowers
Pronunciation: / kɪŋ proʊˈtiːə / (AmE), / kɪŋ prəʊˈtiːə / (BrE)
A South African shrub (Protea cynaroides) with large, cup-shaped flowers that resemble artichokes.

Examples

A king protea flower
A single king protea can dominate a floral arrangement with its striking appearance.
Gardeners often cultivate the king protea for its unusual, artichoke-like blossoms.
The king protea is the national flower of South Africa and admired for its beauty.

Related Terms
