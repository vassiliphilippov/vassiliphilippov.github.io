belvedere

noun
Eurasian shrub with purple-red autumn foliage
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛlvəˌdɪr / (AmE), / ˈbɛlvɪdɪə / (BrE)
A densely branched Eurasian plant, whose foliage turns purple-red in autumn.
