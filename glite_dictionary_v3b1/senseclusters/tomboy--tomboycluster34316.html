tomboy

noun
girl with boyish behavior
2.3 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈtɑːm.bɔɪ/ (AmE), /ˈtɒm.bɔɪ/ (BrE)
a girl who enjoys rough or energetic activities and behaves in a way considered typical of boys

Examples

A tomboy girl
As a child, she was a bit of a tomboy who loved climbing trees and playing football.
Her parents encouraged her to be herself, whether she felt like a tomboy or not.
People were surprised that the tomboy from elementary school had become a fashion designer.
