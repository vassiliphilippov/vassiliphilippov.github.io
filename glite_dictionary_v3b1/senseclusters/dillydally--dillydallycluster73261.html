dillydally

verb
waste time by delaying or hesitating
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪliˌdæli / (AmE), / ˈdɪlɪˌdæli / (BrE)
to waste time by being slow or indecisive; to delay taking action, especially because of hesitation or indecision

Examples

To dillydally in the morning
He tends to dillydally in the mornings instead of getting ready for work.
Stop dillydallying and finish your homework before dinner.
They dillydallied so long that they missed their bus.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hasten - to move or act quickly
  • synonym: procrastinate - to delay or postpone action
