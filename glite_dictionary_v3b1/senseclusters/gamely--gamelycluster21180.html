gamely

adverb
in a brave or determined manner
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪmli / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪmli / (BrE)
in a brave, spirited, or determined manner, especially in the face of difficulty

Examples

To gamely face challenges
Despite being injured, she continued the race gamely and finished with a smile.
He gamely accepted the challenge, even though he knew it would be difficult.
The team fought gamely until the very end, refusing to give up.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bravely - in a courageous manner
  • antonym: timidly - in a shy or lacking confidence manner
