head for the hills

verb
run away to escape danger or trouble
3.6 6.2
Pronunciation: /hed fər ðə hɪlz/ /; / /hed fɔːr ðə hɪlz/ (AmE), /hed fə ðə hɪlz/ /; / /hed fɔː ðə hɪlz/ (BrE)
to leave or run away quickly to escape danger or trouble

Examples

To head for the hills quickly
