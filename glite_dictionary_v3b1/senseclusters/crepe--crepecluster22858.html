crepe

noun
thin pancake, often with a filling
Pronunciation: / kreɪp / (AmE), / kreɪp / (BrE)
A small, very thin pancake, usually made from wheat flour and eaten with a sweet or savory filling.

Examples

A sweet crepe
She ordered a chocolate crepe with strawberries for dessert.
The street vendor filled the hot crepe with ham and cheese.
They made a batch of delicious crepes for Sunday brunch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pancake - a thin, flat cake made from batter
