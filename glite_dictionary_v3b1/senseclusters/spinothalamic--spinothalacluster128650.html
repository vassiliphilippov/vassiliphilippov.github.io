spinothalamic

adjective
spinal cord to thalamus
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌspaɪnoʊθəˈlæmɪk / (AmE), / ˌspaɪnəʊθəˈlæmɪk / (BrE)
relating to the nerve pathway that connects the spinal cord to the thalamus in the brain

Examples

Spinothalamic pathway
The spinothalamic pathway is essential for transmitting pain signals to the brain.
Damage to the spinothalamic tract can result in loss of temperature sensation.
Researchers studied the spinothalamic system to better understand chronic pain disorders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spinothalamic tract - a nerve pathway transmitting pain and temperature from the spinal cord to the thalamus
