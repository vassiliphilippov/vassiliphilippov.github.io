autocatalytic

adjective
relating to autocatalysis in chemistry
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɔtoʊˌkætəˈlɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəʊˈkætəlɪtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or involving autocatalysis, a process in which a chemical reaction is catalyzed by one of its own products.

Examples

Autocatalytic chemical reaction
Many biochemical pathways feature at least one autocatalytic step that regulates their progress.
Researchers studied the autocatalytic behavior observed during the chemical process.
The reaction is autocatalytic, meaning it speeds up as more product is formed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autocatalysis - a chemical reaction catalyzed by one of its own products
