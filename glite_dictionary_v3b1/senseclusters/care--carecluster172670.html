  • caregiving
    3.6 0.00/m
    caregiving focuses on helping sick or elderly people with daily needs, while care can also mean looking after objects or people generally.
  • keeping
    1.5 1.68/m
    keeping means holding or protecting something under your responsibility, while care implies active looking after and sometimes medical or practical treatment.
  • upkeep
    3.3 1.60/m

  • maintenance
    3.6 26/m
