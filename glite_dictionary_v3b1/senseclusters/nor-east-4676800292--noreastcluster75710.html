nor'-east

adverb
toward the northeast (literary/nautical variant)
Pronunciation: / nɔrˈist / (AmE), / nɔːˈiːst / (BrE)
To, toward, or in the direction of the northeast; used especially in literary or nautical contexts as a variant of 'northeast'.
