diesel-hydraulic

noun
locomotive with diesel-powered hydraulic drive
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːzəl haɪˈdrɑlɪk / (AmE), / ˈdiːzəl haɪˈdrɒlɪk / (BrE)
a locomotive that uses a hydraulic transmission system powered by a diesel engine

Examples

A diesel-hydraulic locomotive
Engineers debated the advantages of diesel-hydraulic systems over traditional designs.
The railway museum displayed a restored diesel-hydraulic from the 1960s.
Unlike diesel-electric locomotives, a diesel-hydraulic uses fluid to transmit power.
