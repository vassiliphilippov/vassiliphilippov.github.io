netlist

verb
to create netlist file
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛtlɪst / (AmE), / ˈnɛtlɪst / (BrE)
to convert a circuit schematic or hardware design into a netlist, listing all components and their electrical connections

Examples

To netlist a design
Before manufacturing, the engineer will netlist the entire circuit to check for errors.
It is important to netlist your design to ensure all connections are correctly documented.
The software can automatically netlist complex hardware schematics in just a few seconds.
