leanness

noun
the state of having little body fat
Pronunciation: / ˈliːn.nəs / (AmE), / ˈliːn.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having little body fat; thinness or lack of excess flesh

Examples

Physical leanness
A diet focused on reducing body fat can help achieve leanness.
His leanness made him an ideal candidate for the long-distance running team.
The model's leanness was often praised by fashion designers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fatness - the state of having much body fat; obesity
  • synonym: thinness - the state of being thin, especially related to the body
