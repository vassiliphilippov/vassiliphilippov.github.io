gainsay

verb
deny or contradict
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɡeɪnˈseɪ / (AmE), / ˌɡeɪnˈseɪ / (BrE)
to deny or contradict a fact or statement; to declare to be untrue or invalid

Examples

To gainsay a fact
It is difficult to gainsay the importance of education in modern society.
No one dared to gainsay the evidence presented in court.
She calmly gainsaid every accusation made against her.

Related Terms

  • antonym: affirm - to state that something is true
  • synonym: contradict - to assert the opposite of a statement
  • synonym: deny - to state that something is not true
