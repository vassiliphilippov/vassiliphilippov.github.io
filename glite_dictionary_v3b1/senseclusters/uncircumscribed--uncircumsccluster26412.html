uncircumscribed

adjective
unrestricted; unlimited
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈsɜːrkəmskraɪbd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈsɜːkəmskraɪbd / (BrE)
not limited, restricted, or enclosed by boundaries; unrestricted or free.

Examples

Uncircumscribed freedom
The artist enjoyed uncircumscribed freedom in her creative process.
His influence over the project was uncircumscribed, allowing him to make all major decisions.
They valued an uncircumscribed approach to learning, encouraging curiosity and exploration.
