trifle

noun
something of little importance
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪfl̩ /; / ˈtraɪ.fəl / (AmE), / ˈtraɪf(ə)l /; / ˈtraɪ.fəl / (BrE)
something that is not important or has little value

Examples

A small trifle
He dismissed the error as a mere trifle not worth discussing.
She doesn't waste her time on trifles and always focuses on what matters most.
To argue over such a trifle seems completely pointless to me.
He lost his temper over a mere trifle, surprising everyone in the room.
Please don't concern yourself with such a trifle when there are bigger issues to solve.
She dismissed their complaints as a trifle, not worth further discussion.

  • synonym: trivial - of little value or importance
  • synonym: triviality - something trivial; an unimportant thing
