viridian

noun
blue-green pigment
8.6
Pronunciation: / vəˈrɪdiən / (AmE), / vɪˈrɪdiən / (BrE)
a blue-green pigment or color, especially one resembling fresh pine leaves, used in painting and art

Examples

Viridian paint
The painter chose viridian to capture the lushness of the forest in her landscape.
He mixed a little viridian into the blue to create a more natural shade for the lake.
The walls were painted a soft viridian that brought a calming atmosphere to the room.
