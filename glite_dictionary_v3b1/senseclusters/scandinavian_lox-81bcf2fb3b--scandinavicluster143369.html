Scandinavian lox

noun
salt-cured Scandinavian salmon
Pronunciation: / skænˌdeɪviˈeniən lɑks / (AmE), / skænˌdeɪviˈeɪniən lɒks / (BrE)
Salt-cured salmon prepared in the traditional Scandinavian style, usually thinly sliced and served cold.

Examples

Thin sliced Scandinavian lox
At the brunch buffet, I enjoyed the Scandinavian lox with capers and red onion.
She prepared homemade Scandinavian lox following a family recipe from Sweden.
Slices of Scandinavian lox were arranged artfully on the appetizer platter.
