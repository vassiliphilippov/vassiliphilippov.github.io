subprefecture

noun
district below prefecture
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbprɪˈfɛktʃər / (AmE), / ˌsʌbprɪˈfɛktʃə / (BrE)
an administrative district or office that is below the level of a prefecture in some countries

Examples

The subprefecture office
The new policy was announced by the head of the subprefecture during a community meeting.
Each subprefecture is responsible for managing local services and administration.
After the reorganization, our town became part of a different subprefecture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: municipality - a city or town with its own local government
  • degree higher: prefecture - an administrative district or office in certain countries, often larger than a subprefecture
