perspiration

noun
the process of producing sweat
3.0 0.6/m

The process of the sweat glands in the skin producing and secreting a salty fluid, especially as a way to regulate body temperature.

Examples

Heavy perspiration
After running a mile, a thin layer of perspiration covered her forehead.
His shirt was soaked with perspiration after working out in the gym.
Intense perspiration is common during strenuous physical activity.
