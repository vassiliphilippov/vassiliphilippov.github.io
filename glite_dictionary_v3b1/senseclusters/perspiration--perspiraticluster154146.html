perspiration

noun
the process of producing sweat
3.0 0.64/m
Pronunciation: / Àåp…ùÀê.sp…ôÀàre…™. É…ôn / (AmE), / Àåp…úÀê.sp…™Ààre…™. É…ôn / (BrE)
The process of the sweat glands in the skin producing and secreting a salty fluid, especially as a way to regulate body temperature.

Examples

Heavy perspiration
After running a mile, a thin layer of perspiration covered her forehead.
His shirt was soaked with perspiration after working out in the gym.
Intense perspiration is common during strenuous physical activity.

Synonyms

  • sweating
    1.1 0.06/m
    sweating is the common word for body moisture when you get hot or exercise, while perspiration sounds more formal.
  • sudation
    9.7 0.00/m
    sudation is a technical medical term for sweat produced by glands, while perspiration is the more common clinical or everyday word.
  • hidrosis
    9.9 0.00/m
    hidrosis is the clinical specialist name used in medicine for sweat conditions, while perspiration is the general term for sweating.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sweat - the salty liquid produced by sweat glands
