transindividual

noun
collective phenomenon
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtrænzˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəl / (AmE), / ˌtrænzˌɪndɪˈvɪdjʊəl / (BrE)
a social or psychological entity, process, or phenomenon that arises across and beyond individual persons, involving collective dynamics

Examples

A transindividual force
Sociologists study how a transindividual emerges when people act together in large groups.
The transindividual can influence decisions in ways that no single person could predict.
During the protest, the transindividual formed as people coordinated chants and actions together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collective - a group or entity formed by individuals acting together
  • antonym: individual - a single person or entity, considered separately from a group
