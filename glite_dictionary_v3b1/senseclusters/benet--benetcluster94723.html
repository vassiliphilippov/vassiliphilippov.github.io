benet

verb
to bless (archaic)
Pronunciation: / bɪˈnɛt / (AmE), / bɪˈnɛt / (BrE)
to bless or make blessed, especially in an archaic or religious context

Examples

To benet the land
The old manuscript described how the monk would benet the fields before planting.
In the ceremony, the leader benetted each participant with a gentle touch.
It was believed that to benet a home would bring peace and prosperity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bless - to ask for divine favor or protection for someone or something
  • antonym: curse - to call for harm or misfortune to come to someone
