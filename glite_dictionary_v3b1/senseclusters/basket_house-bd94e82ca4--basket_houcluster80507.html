basket house

noun
house designed in a basket-like style
Pronunciation: / ˈbæskət haʊs / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪt haʊs / (BrE)
a house designed to resemble or imitate the appearance or structure of a basket, often as a unique architectural style

Examples

A basket house
The architect is famous for designing a basket house that stands out in the neighborhood.
The newly constructed basket house became a local tourist attraction.
They spent their vacation in a rustic basket house near the river.
