Synonyms

  • go back on
    4.1 1.02/m

  • break a promise
    2.6 0.41/m
    break a promise means not doing what you promised, while renege is a more formal term often used in writing or law.
  • back out
    3.4 1.78/m
    back out means to change your mind and withdraw from an agreement, while renege focuses on failing to fulfill a commitment.
  • breach
    4.5 1.92/m
    breach means to break a rule or contract in a formal, legal way, while renege usually refers to breaking a promise.
