Datura suaveolens

noun
South American plant with fragrant trumpet flowers
Pronunciation: / dəˈtʊrə swɑːveɪˈoʊlɛnz / (AmE), / dəˈtʃʊərə swɑːviːˈəʊlɛnz / (BrE)
A South American plant cultivated for its large, trumpet-shaped, nocturnally fragrant flowers, also known as angel's trumpet.
