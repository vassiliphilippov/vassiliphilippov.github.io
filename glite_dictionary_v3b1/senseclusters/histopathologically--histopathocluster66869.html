histopathologically

adverb
by tissue examination
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪstoʊˌpæθəˈlɑdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˌhɪstəʊˌpæθəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the microscopic examination of diseased tissue.

Examples

To histopathologically examine tissue
The diagnosis was histopathologically confirmed after examining the tissue sample.
The disease was histopathologically classified as malignant by the laboratory.
Doctors determined the infection type histopathologically using advanced staining techniques.

Related Terms

  • synonym: microscopically - in a way that involves the use of a microscope
