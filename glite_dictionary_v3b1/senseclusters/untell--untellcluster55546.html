untell

verb
to withhold information
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtɛl / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɛl / (BrE)
to choose not to tell or to withhold information, a story, or a fact.

Examples

To untell a secret
He chose to untell the details of the incident, keeping them private.
Sometimes it is better to untell certain facts to avoid hurting others.
After thinking it over, she decided to untell the story she had planned to share.

Related Terms

  • synonym: withhold - to refuse to give or share something
