mean

adjective
very good; impressive
Pronunciation: / miːn / (AmE), / miːn / (BrE)
(informal) Very good or impressive; of the highest quality.

Examples

A mean guitar solo
He gave a mean performance on the piano last night.
She plays a mean game of chess and rarely loses a match.
That chef can cook a mean lasagna—it's the best I've tasted.

Related Terms

  • antonym: awful - very bad or unpleasant
  • synonym: excellent - of the highest quality; extremely good
  • synonym: superb - of very high quality; excellent
