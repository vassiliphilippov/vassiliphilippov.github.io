burse

noun
student grant
Pronunciation: / bɜrs / (AmE), / bɜːs / (BrE)
a grant of money given to a student to help pay for education; a bursary or scholarship

Examples

A student burse
After applying to several programs, he was delighted to receive a burse for his studies.
The university offers a burse each year to outstanding students from low-income families.
Winning the burse allowed her to focus on her research without financial worries.
