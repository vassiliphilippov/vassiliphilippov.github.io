cumulant

noun
statistical distribution measure
9.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàkjuÀêmj…ôl…ônt / (AmE), / ÀàkjuÀêmj äl…ônt / (BrE)
a statistical value that characterizes the shape or features of a probability distribution, such as its mean, variance, or skewness

Examples

The cumulant of distribution
The third cumulant of a distribution measures its skewness.
Researchers calculated the fourth cumulant to analyze the distribution's kurtosis.
Understanding each cumulant helps statisticians describe complex data sets more precisely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moment - a statistical measure related to the shape of a distribution
  • synonym: variance - a measure of how much values in a distribution differ from the mean
