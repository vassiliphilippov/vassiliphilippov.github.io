treasurer's check

noun
bank check issued from bank's own funds
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛʒərərz tʃɛk /; / ˈtrɛʒərz ˌtʃɛk / (AmE), / ˈtrɛʒ(ə)rəz tʃɛk /; / ˈtrɛʒərəz ˌtʃɛk / (BrE)
a check drawn by a bank officer on the bank's own funds rather than on a customer's account
