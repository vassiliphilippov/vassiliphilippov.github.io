preaxial

adjective
in front of axis
Pronunciation: / priˈæksiəl / (AmE), / priˈæksiəl / (BrE)
located in front of or on the anterior side of an axis, especially in anatomical structures

Examples

The preaxial side
The thumb is considered a preaxial digit in the human hand.
In some animals, the big toe is the most preaxial part of the foot.
Doctors examined the preaxial border of the limb for signs of injury.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postaxial - located behind or on the posterior side of an axis
