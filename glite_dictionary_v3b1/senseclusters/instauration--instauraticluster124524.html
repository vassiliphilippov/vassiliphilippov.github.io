instauration

noun
restoration or renewal
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnstəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪnstɔːˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of restoring or renewing something to its original state

Examples

Instauration of the building
The instauration of the historic theater attracted many visitors to the city.
After years of neglect, the instauration of the gardens was a major community project.
The museum celebrated the instauration of its oldest exhibit after extensive repairs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: destruction - the act of causing so much damage that something no longer exists
  • synonym: renewal - the process of making something new or fresh again
  • synonym: restoration - the act of bringing something back to its original condition
