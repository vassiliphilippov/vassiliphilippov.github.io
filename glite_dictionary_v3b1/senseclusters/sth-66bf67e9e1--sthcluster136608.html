A hormone produced by the anterior pituitary gland that promotes growth in humans; also known as somatotropic hormone or growth hormone.

Examples

The STH hormone
STH plays a crucial role in regulating metabolism and supporting bone development.
An excess of STH can result in abnormal height in adults, a condition called acromegaly.
Doctors sometimes measure STH levels to diagnose growth disorders in children.
