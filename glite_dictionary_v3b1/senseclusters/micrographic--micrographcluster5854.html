micrographic

adjective
very small writing
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
written, printed, or formed with extremely small letters or relating to art or images made from tiny script

Examples

Micrographic handwriting art
The scientist used a micrographic technique to record data in a tiny notebook.
She admired the micrographic artwork, which revealed hidden words when viewed up close.
His micrographic handwriting made his notes difficult for others to read.

Related Terms

  • antonym: macroscopic - large enough to be visible to the naked eye
  • synonym: miniature - very small in size or scale
