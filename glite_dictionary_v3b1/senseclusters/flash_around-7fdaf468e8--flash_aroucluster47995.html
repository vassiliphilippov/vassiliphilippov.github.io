flash around

verb
spread quickly (online)
4.2
Pronunciation: /flæʃ ə.ˈraʊnd/ /; / /flæʃ ə.ˈraʊn/ (AmE), /flæʃ ə.ˈraʊnd/ /; / /flæʃ ə.ˈraʊn/ (BrE)
(of news, images, or other content) to spread or circulate quickly among people or across the internet.

Examples

Short, funny clips flash around social media within hours of being uploaded.
After the scandal, the video flashed around the internet before officials issued a statement.
By midnight the photo was flashing around several community forums and chat groups.
