Gobiidae

noun
family of small fishes (gobies)
Pronunciation: / ˈgoʊ.bi.aɪˌdi / (AmE), / ˈgəʊ.bɪ.aɪ.diː / (BrE)
A large family of small, often bottom-dwelling fishes (gobies) found in marine, brackish, and freshwater habitats worldwide.
