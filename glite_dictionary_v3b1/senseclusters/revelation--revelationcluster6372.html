Synonyms

  • revealment
    6.7 0.01/m
    revealment names the act of making something visible or announced, while revelation is the surprising making known of something hidden.
  • disclosure
    4.1 13/m
    disclosure is the formal act of telling secret information, while revelation often means a surprising making known to many.
  • divulgence
    8.4 0.01/m
    divulgence is an older or rare word for telling private facts, while revelation usually describes a striking or unexpected disclosure.
  • uncovering
    3.2 0.01/m
    uncovering is the action of finding and showing what was hidden, while revelation stresses the surprise of something newly known.
  • exposure
    3.9 6.91/m
    exposure is making something secret visible often with harm or risk, while revelation focuses on the act of surprising disclosure.
  • eye opener
    3.9 0.04/m
    eye opener is an event or fact that surprises and opens someone's eyes, while revelation names the act of revealing information.
