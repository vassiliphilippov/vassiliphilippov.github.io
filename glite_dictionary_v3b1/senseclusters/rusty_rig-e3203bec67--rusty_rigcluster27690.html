rusty rig

noun
Australian tree resembling the banyan
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌsti rɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈrʌsti rɪɡ / (BrE)
An Australian tree resembling the banyan, often planted for ornamental purposes; also introduced into South Africa for use as brushwood.
