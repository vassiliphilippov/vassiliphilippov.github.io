Synonyms

  • foreboding
    8.0 1/m
    foreboding is a dark, worrying sense that trouble is coming, while premonition is a strong inner feeling that predicts an event.
  • presentiment
    9.1 0.2/m
    presentiment is a vague, quick feeling that something bad is near, while premonition is a clearer, stronger warning about the future.
  • boding
    8.9 0.01/m
