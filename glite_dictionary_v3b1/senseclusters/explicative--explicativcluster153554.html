explicative

noun
explanatory remark
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsplɪkətɪv / (AmE), / ɪkˈsplɪkətɪv / (BrE)
a word, phrase, or statement that explains or makes something clear; an explanatory remark

Examples

An explicative comment
The professor added an explicative to help students understand the complex theory.
Her explicative at the end of the article clarified several confusing points.
Without an explicative, the instructions would have been difficult to follow.

  • synonym: explanation - a statement that makes something clear
