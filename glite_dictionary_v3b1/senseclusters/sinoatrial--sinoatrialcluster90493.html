sinoatrial

adjective
of the sinus node
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪnoʊˈeɪtriəl / (AmE), / ˌsaɪnəʊˈeɪtrɪəl / (BrE)
relating to the sinus node of the heart, which regulates the heartbeat rhythm

Examples

Sinoatrial node
The sinoatrial node is responsible for initiating each heartbeat in a healthy heart.
Doctors monitor sinoatrial function to assess heart rhythm disorders.
A problem with the sinoatrial region can lead to irregular heartbeats.

Related Terms

  • antonym: atrioventricular - relating to the atria and ventricles of the heart
  • synonym: sinus node - the natural pacemaker of the heart
