Synonyms

  • unacceptable
    3.2 12/m
    unacceptable strongly breaks rules or standards in formal settings, while not acceptable describes anything that fails to meet rules or expectations.
  • impermissible
    7.7 0.3/m
    impermissible is used in laws and rules to show something is not allowed, while not acceptable can mean rule-break or poor quality.
  • unallowable
    6.4 0.01/m
    unallowable is a formal term saying rules forbid something, while not acceptable is broader and may cover poor quality or unsuitability.
  • forbidden
    3.2 4/m
    forbidden is strongly banned by law, rule, or custom, while not acceptable can mean either banned or simply unsuitable for use.
  • unsuitable
    2.6 0.9/m
    unsuitable is not right or fitting for a purpose or person, while not acceptable also covers not meeting rules or quality.
  • inappropriate
    3.7 16/m
    inappropriate feels wrong in a situation or social setting, while not acceptable is wider and can include safety or rule issues.
  • improper
    4.1 5/m
    improper breaks accepted moral or legal standards in a context, while not acceptable may mean either unsuitable or not allowed.
  • objectionable
    6.7 1/m
    objectionable causes people to feel upset or offended, while not acceptable states something fails rules, standards, or common approval.
  • out of the question
    2.7 3/m
