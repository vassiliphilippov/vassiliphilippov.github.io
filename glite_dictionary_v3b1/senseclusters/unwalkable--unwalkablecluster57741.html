unwalkable

adjective
not suitable for walking
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɔkəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɔːkəbl / (BrE)
not suitable or possible to walk through or across, especially due to obstacles or danger

Examples

Unwalkable street
After the heavy snowfall, the sidewalks became completely unwalkable for several days.
The construction site was so cluttered that it was unwalkable without special equipment.
Many rural roads are unwalkable during the rainy season due to deep mud.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impassable - impossible to travel through or across
  • antonym: walkable - easy or suitable to walk through or across
