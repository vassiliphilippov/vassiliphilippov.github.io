gatherer

noun
person who collects or gathers
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæðɚɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡæðərə / (BrE)
a person who collects or gathers things, especially as part of their work or way of life

Examples

A berry gatherer
As a gatherer, her main job was to pick wild berries and roots from the forest.
In many indigenous cultures, people played the role of both hunter and gatherer.
The museum displayed tools used by ancient gatherers thousands of years ago.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collector - a person who collects things as a hobby or job
