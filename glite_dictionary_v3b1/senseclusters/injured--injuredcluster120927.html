injured

adjective
emotionally hurt or offended
7.0 2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈɪn.dʒərd/ (AmE), /ˈɪn.dʒəd/ (BrE)
feeling emotionally hurt, upset, or offended, especially because of someone's actions or words

Examples

Injured feelings
He felt deeply injured by their harsh criticism and avoided speaking with them for days.
His injured pride prevented him from apologizing, even though he knew he was wrong.
She gave him an injured look, as if his comment had genuinely wounded her feelings.

  • synonym: hurt - emotionally pained or distressed
  • synonym: offended - feeling resentful or hurt due to perceived insult or disregard
