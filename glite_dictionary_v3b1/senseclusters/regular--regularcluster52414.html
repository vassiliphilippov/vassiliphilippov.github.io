  • periodic
    3.9 8/m
    periodic happens at set intervals and sounds formal, while regular covers set times or routines in everyday speech.
  • recurrent
    4.7 3/m
    recurrent appears again and again over time, often without fixed schedule, while regular implies set times or planned routine.
  • usual
    1.0 70/m
