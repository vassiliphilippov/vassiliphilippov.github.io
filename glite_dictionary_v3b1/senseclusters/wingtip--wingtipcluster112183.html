wingtip

noun
decorated dress shoe
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪŋˌtɪp / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋˌtɪp / (BrE)
a style of dress shoe with a pointed toe cap shaped like wings, often decorated with perforations

Examples

Wingtip shoes
He wore polished wingtip shoes to the wedding for a classic look.
The store offers several styles, but the black wingtip is their bestseller.
His grandfather's old wingtip shoes were still in excellent condition after many years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brogue - a sturdy shoe with decorative perforations
  • synonym: oxford - a type of lace-up dress shoe
