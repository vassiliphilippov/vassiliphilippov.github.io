winna

verb
informal 'will not'
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪnə / (BrE)
an informal or dialect spelling of 'will not' or 'want to', used in some regional varieties of English

Examples

I winna do it
I winna go to the party if it rains.
She winna listen to any advice, no matter what I say.
They winna agree to the plan unless we make some changes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: will not - the negative form of 'will'
  • synonym: won't - contraction of 'will not'
