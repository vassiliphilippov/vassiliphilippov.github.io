market day

noun
fixed day for a public market
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrkət deɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪt deɪ / (BrE)
a fixed or regular day on which a public market is held, usually in a town or village square

Examples

The market day
Every Thursday is market day in the village, attracting people from nearby towns.
On market day, the square was filled with stalls selling fresh produce and crafts.
They scheduled their visit to coincide with market day for the best local shopping experience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: market - a regular gathering for the purchase and sale of goods
