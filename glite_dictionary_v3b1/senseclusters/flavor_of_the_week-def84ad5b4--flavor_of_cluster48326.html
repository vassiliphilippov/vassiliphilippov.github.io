flavor of the week

noun
a trendy or temporary choice
4.0 3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈfleɪ.vər əv ðə wiːk/ /; / /ˈfleɪ.vər əv ðiː wiːk/ (AmE), /ˈfleɪ.və əv ðə wiːk/ /; / /ˈfleɪ.və əv ðiː wiːk/ (BrE)
something or someone that is currently fashionable or popular for a short time; also, a literal flavor offered for a limited period, especially in food service.

Examples

The flavor of the week trend
Every few months, a new snack food becomes the flavor of the week before fading away.
The bakery offered pistachio as their flavor of the week and it quickly sold out.
The committee praised her idea, but it felt like just another flavor of the week to me.
