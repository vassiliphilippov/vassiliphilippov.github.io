mitigate

verb
make less severe or serious

Pronunciation: /ˈmɪ.tɪ.ɡeɪt/ /; / /ˈmɪ.tə.ɡeɪt/ (AmE), /ˈmɪ.tɪ.ɡeɪt/ /; / /ˈmɪ.tə.ɡeɪt/ (BrE)
to make something less severe, harmful, or painful; to reduce the bad effects of something
