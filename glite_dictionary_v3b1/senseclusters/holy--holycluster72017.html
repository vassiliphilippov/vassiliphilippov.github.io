holy

adjective
connected with religion or divinity
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊli / (AmE), / ˈhəʊli / (BrE)
connected to a god or religion; regarded as sacred, pure, or having a divine quality

Examples

Holy place
Many people make a pilgrimage to holy places seeking peace and spiritual growth.
She treated the old scriptures as holy objects, showing them the utmost respect.
The site is considered holy by three major religions, attracting pilgrims from around the world.
The church is considered a holy place by the local community.
Many people travel to holy sites for spiritual reasons.
He tried to live a holy life by helping others and avoiding wrongdoing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: profane - relating to that which is not sacred or biblical; secular rather than religious
  • synonym: sacred - regarded with great respect and reverence by a particular religion, group, or individual
