Synonyms

  • hallowed
    7.7 0.20/m

  • consecrated
    7.6 0.00/m
    consecrated is made holy for religious use by a ceremony, while holy can describe a wider divine or pure quality.
  • sanctified
    7.4 0.00/m

  • blessed
