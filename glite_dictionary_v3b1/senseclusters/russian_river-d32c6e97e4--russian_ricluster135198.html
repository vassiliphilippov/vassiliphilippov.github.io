Russian River

proper noun
river in northern California
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌʃən ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈrʌʃən ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in northern California that flows south and then west to the Pacific Ocean, known for its vineyards and recreational activities

Examples

The Russian River floods
Heavy rains caused the Russian River to flood its surrounding areas last winter.
Many vineyards line the banks of the Russian River in Sonoma County.
The Russian River is a popular destination for kayaking during the summer months.
