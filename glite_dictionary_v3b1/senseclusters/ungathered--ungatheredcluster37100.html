ungathered

adjective
not collected or brought together
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡæðɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡæðəd / (BrE)
not brought together; not collected into one place or group

Examples

Ungathered papers
He looked around at the ungathered papers scattered across his desk.
Research was delayed due to many ungathered statistics from regional offices.
The ungathered crops were left to rot in the field after the storm.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gathered - collected or brought together
  • synonym: uncollected - not collected; left where it is
