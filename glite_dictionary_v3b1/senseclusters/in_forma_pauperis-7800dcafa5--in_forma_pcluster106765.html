in forma pauperis

noun
court fee waiver request
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɪn ˈfɔːr.mə pɔːˈper.ɪs/ (AmE), /ɪn ˈfɔː.mə pɔːˈper.ɪs/ (BrE)
a legal request to proceed in court without paying fees due to inability to afford them

Examples

File an in forma pauperis motion
The judge granted her motion to proceed in forma pauperis because she could not afford the court fees.
Filing in forma pauperis allows individuals with limited means to access the legal system.
He submitted his appeal in forma pauperis after losing his job and income.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fee waiver - an official permission to not pay required fees
