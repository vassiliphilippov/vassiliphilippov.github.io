cold stare

noun
unfriendly, angry look
4.1
Pronunciation: / koʊld stɛr / (AmE), / kəʊld steə / (BrE)
a fixed and unfriendly look that expresses anger or disapproval

Examples

A cold stare
After arriving late, he was met by his boss's cold stare.
His cold stare made it clear that he was not pleased with the situation.
She fixed him with a cold stare that made him fall silent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glare - an angry or fierce stare
  • synonym: look daggers at - to look at someone in a very angry way
