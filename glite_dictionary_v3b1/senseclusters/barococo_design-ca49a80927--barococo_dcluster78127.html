barococo design

noun
style blending Baroque and Rococo elements
9.4 8.5
Pronunciation: /bə.rə.ˈkoʊ.koʊ dɪz.ˈaɪn/ /; / /bæ.rə.ˈkoʊ.koʊ dɪz.ˈaɪn/ (AmE), /bə.rə.ˈkəʊ.kəʊ dɪz.ˈaɪn/ /; / /bæ.rə.ˈkəʊ.kəʊ dɪz.ˈaɪn/ (BrE)
A style of art, architecture, or decoration that combines features of both Baroque and Rococo movements, often resulting in ornate and extravagant expressions.

Examples

Barococo design style
Several modern hotels now use barococo design as a signature style, blending opulence and whimsy.
She decorated her bedroom in a barococo design, mixing classic curves with playful flourishes.
The lobby's barococo design featured gilded mirrors and swirling plasterwork on every wall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Rococo - A highly ornamental and decorative late Baroque style of the 18th century
