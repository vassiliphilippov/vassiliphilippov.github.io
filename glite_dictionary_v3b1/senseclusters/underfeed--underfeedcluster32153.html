to supply less than the required amount of material to a machine, process, or system.

Examples

To underfeed a machine
If you underfeed the machine, it may stop working properly.
The technician realized he had accidentally underfed the fuel to the engine.
Continuous underfeeding of raw materials can slow down the entire production process.

Synonyms
