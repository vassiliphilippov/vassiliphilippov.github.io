medium-large

adjective
fairly large, but not the largest
Pronunciation: / ˌmiːdiəmˈlɑrdʒ / (AmE), / ˌmiːdiəmˈlɑːdʒ / (BrE)
fairly large in size, but not the largest among possible sizes

Examples

A medium-large coffee
She ordered a medium-large coffee because the extra-large was too much for her.
The hotel room had a medium-large bed that was just the right size for two people.
We adopted a medium-large dog from the shelter, perfect for our apartment.
