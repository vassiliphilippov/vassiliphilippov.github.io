prolific

adjective
producing many works; highly productive
7.0 7.2
Pronunciation: / prəˈlɪfɪk / (AmE), / prəˈlɪfɪk / (BrE)
Producing a large amount of something, especially works of art or literature; highly productive intellectually.

Examples

Prolific writer
His prolific mind produced inventions that changed the industry.
She is a prolific author who has published more than fifty novels.
The artist remained prolific even in the later years of her career.
