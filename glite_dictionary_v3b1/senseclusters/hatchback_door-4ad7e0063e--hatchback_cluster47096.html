hatchback door

noun
rear upward-opening door on a hatchback car
3.7 3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhætʃˌbæk dɔr / (AmE), / ˈhætʃˌbæk dɔː / (BrE)
a sloping rear car door, typically hinged at the roof, that opens upwards to provide access to the cargo area in a hatchback vehicle

Examples

The hatchback door opens
Rainwater leaked through a gap in the hatchback door.

The mechanic replaced the hatchback door after the accident damaged it.
