rainmaking

noun
attempt to produce rain
6.3 7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪnˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈreɪnˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of attempting to cause rain to fall, especially by artificial or ritual means

Examples

Artificial rainmaking
Modern rainmaking techniques have helped alleviate water shortages in some countries.
The scientist specialized in rainmaking by using silver iodide to seed clouds.
Traditional tribes sometimes performed rainmaking ceremonies during droughts.
