juice

noun
vitality or creative energy
6.8 2.76/m
Pronunciation: / d íus / (AmE), / d íuÀês / (BrE)
energetic vitality, especially as a source of creativity or enthusiasm

Examples

Creative juices
A walk in the fresh air helps stimulate my mental juices.
He needed some coffee to get his juices going in the morning.
Her creative juices were flowing while she worked on the painting.
