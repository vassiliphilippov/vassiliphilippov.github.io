Examples

To plump down

Synonyms

  • plop
    3.6 1.0/m
    plop sets something down with a soft, quick sound when it lands, while plump means to set down more heavily.
  • plunk
    6.0 1/m
    plunk puts or drops something with a loud, heavy sound, while plump means to set down heavily but not always loudly.
  • plonk
    7.8 0.1/m
    plonk drops something down noisily and often carelessly onto a surface, while plump means to set down heavily without showing care.
  • flop
    2.3 3/m
    flop falls or lies down heavily in a relaxed or tired way, while plump focuses on setting or dropping down with weight.
  • slump
    4.0 6/m
    slump sinks or sits down heavily with a bent, drooping posture, while plump means to set or drop down with a noise.
