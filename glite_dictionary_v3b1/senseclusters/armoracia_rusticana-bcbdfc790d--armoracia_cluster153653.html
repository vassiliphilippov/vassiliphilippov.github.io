Armoracia rusticana

noun
species of plant, horseradish
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrməˈreɪʃə rʌˈstɪkænə / (AmE), / ˌɑːməˈreɪsɪə rʌsˈtɪkənə / (BrE)
a coarse Eurasian plant cultivated for its thick, white, pungent root, commonly known as horseradish
