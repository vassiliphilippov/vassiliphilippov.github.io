asystolic

adjective
no heartbeat
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪsɪˈstɑlɪk / (AmE), / ˌeɪsɪˈstɒlɪk / (BrE)
having no electrical activity in the heart, resulting in no heartbeat

Examples

Asystolic heart activity
The monitor showed the patient was asystolic and immediate resuscitation was started.
After several minutes without a pulse, the team confirmed he was asystolic.
If a patient becomes asystolic, emergency intervention is required right away.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cardiac arrest - sudden loss of heart function
  • synonym: pulseless - having no detectable pulse
