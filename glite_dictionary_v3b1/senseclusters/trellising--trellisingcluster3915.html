trellising

noun
plant support system
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛlɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtrɛlɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
the framework or process of supporting climbing plants or vines by training them to grow on a trellis or similar structure

Examples

Vineyard trellising system
The vineyard installed new trellising to help the grapevines grow upright and receive more sunlight.
Proper trellising can improve air flow and reduce disease in tomato plants.
Gardeners often use trellising to train climbing roses along fences or walls.
