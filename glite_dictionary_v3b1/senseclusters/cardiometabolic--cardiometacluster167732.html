cardiometabolic

adjective
heart and metabolism
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑrdioʊˌmɛtəˈbɑlɪk / (AmE), / ˌkɑːdiəʊˌmɛtəˈbɒlɪk / (BrE)
relating to the combined health or risk factors of the heart and metabolism

Examples

Cardiometabolic risk factors
Doctors are concerned about cardiometabolic risks in patients with obesity and high blood pressure.
A healthy diet can help reduce cardiometabolic complications over time.
Researchers are studying the link between sleep and cardiometabolic health.
