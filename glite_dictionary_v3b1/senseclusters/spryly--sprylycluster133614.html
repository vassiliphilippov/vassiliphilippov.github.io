spryly

adverb
in a lively way
Pronunciation: / ˈspraɪli / (AmE), / ˈspraɪli / (BrE)
in a lively, energetic, or nimble manner

Examples

To move spryly
Despite his age, he danced spryly at the family wedding.
The cat jumped spryly onto the windowsill to watch the birds outside.
She moved spryly through the crowded market, weaving between shoppers with ease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: energetically - in a way that shows energy and enthusiasm
  • antonym: lazily - in a slow or relaxed way, without energy
