using whatever methods or resources are available; improvised or flexible in approach, often without strict planning.

Examples

Catch-as-catch-can method
During the blackout, cooking meals became a catch-as-catch-can affair for everyone.
The meeting was scheduled in a catch-as-catch-can fashion as people arrived at different times.
With limited supplies, their building process was strictly catch-as-catch-can.
