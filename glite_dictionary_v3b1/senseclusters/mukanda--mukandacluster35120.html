mukanda

noun
initiation camp for boys
Pronunciation: / muˈkɑːndə / (AmE), / muˈkændə / (BrE)
a traditional initiation camp or period of seclusion where boys undergo circumcision and receive instruction in cultural knowledge and adult responsibilities

Examples

Mukanda camp
The boys spent several weeks at the mukanda learning about their community's traditions.
After completing the mukanda, the boys were welcomed back as young men.
Elders from the village led the mukanda and taught important life skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: circumcision - the surgical removal of the foreskin, often as a cultural or religious practice
  • synonym: rite of passage - a ceremony marking an important stage in someone's life
