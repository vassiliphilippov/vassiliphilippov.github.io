driving school

noun
a school for learning to drive vehicles
Pronunciation: / ˈdraɪvɪŋ skuːl / (AmE), / ˈdraɪvɪŋ skuːl / (BrE)
a school or institution that teaches people how to drive motor vehicles, especially cars

Examples

A driving school lesson
Maria enrolled in a driving school to prepare for her license test.
Most teenagers take lessons at a driving school before getting their permits.
The driving school provides both classroom instruction and hands-on practice.
