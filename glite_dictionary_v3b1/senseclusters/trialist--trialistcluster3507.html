trialist

noun
clinical study participant
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈtraɪəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who participates as a subject in a medical or scientific trial, such as a clinical study

Examples

Medical trialist
The trialist received regular check-ups throughout the duration of the clinical study.
Each trialist was carefully monitored for side effects after taking the new medication.
As a trialist, he contributed valuable data to the research project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: participant - a person who takes part in something
  • synonym: volunteer - a person who freely offers to take part in an activity
