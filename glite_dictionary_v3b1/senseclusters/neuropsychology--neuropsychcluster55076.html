neuropsychology

noun
study of brain-behavior relationships
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊsaɪˈkɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊsaɪˈkɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of how the structure and function of the brain relate to psychological processes and behavior

Examples

Study of neuropsychology
Recent advances in neuropsychology have improved our understanding of memory and learning disorders.

The conference featured experts in neuropsychology from universities around the world.
