lexical meaning

noun
meaning of a word as it relates to real-world concepts
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛksɪkəl ˈmiːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɛksɪkəl ˈmiːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the meaning of a word as it refers to real-world concepts or objects, rather than its grammatical or structural role

Examples

Lexical meaning of words
Distinguishing between grammatical and lexical meaning is important in language analysis.
Some words have more than one lexical meaning depending on context.
The lexical meaning of 'cat' is a type of four-legged animal, not just its grammatical use.
