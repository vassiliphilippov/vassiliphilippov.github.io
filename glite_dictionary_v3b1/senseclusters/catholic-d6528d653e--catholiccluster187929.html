Catholic

adjective
relating to the Roman Catholic Church
Pronunciation: / ˈkæθəlɪk / (AmE), / ˈkæθlɪk / (BrE)
Relating to the Roman Catholic Church or its members; supporting or characteristic of Catholicism.

Examples

Catholic school values
Many schools with a Catholic ethos emphasize religious education.
She was raised in a Catholic family and attends Mass every Sunday.
The new pope introduced reforms to modernize the Catholic Church.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Catholicism - the faith, practice, and church order of the Roman Catholic Church
  • antonym: Protestant - relating to Protestantism or its members
