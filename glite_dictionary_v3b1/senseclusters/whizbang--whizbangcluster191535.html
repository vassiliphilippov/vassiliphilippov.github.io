whizbang

noun
flashy impressive gadget or product
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪzbæŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɪzbæŋ / (BrE)
an impressive, flashy, or showy thing - especially a gadget, product, or feature - marketed for its novelty or advanced capabilities.

Examples

A whizbang gadget
The startup unveiled a whizbang smartwatch that promised features nobody really needed but everyone wanted.
She bought several whizbangs at the fair, though most of them stopped working after a few uses.
Critics called the movie's special effects a whizbang distraction from the weak, underdeveloped plot.
