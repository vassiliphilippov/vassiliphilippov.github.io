mistune

verb
to tune incorrectly
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈtuːn / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈtjuːn / (BrE)
to tune a musical instrument incorrectly or to the wrong pitch

Examples

To mistune a violin
He accidentally mistuned the violin, making it sound unpleasant during the performance.
If you mistune your instrument, the entire band may sound off.
She realized she had mistuned the piano after hearing the first few notes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detune - to make a musical instrument out of tune
  • antonym: tune - to adjust a musical instrument to the correct pitch
