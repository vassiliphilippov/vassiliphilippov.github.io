com

noun
serial port on computer
7.9
Pronunciation: /kɑːm/ (AmE), /kɒm/ (BrE)
a computer serial communications port, often labeled COM, used to connect external devices such as modems

Examples

Com port connection
The technician asked me to check if the modem was connected to the com port.
Older computers often have more than one com port for different devices.
You need a special cable to connect the printer to the com port on your PC.
