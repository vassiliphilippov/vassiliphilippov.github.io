deed of conveyance

noun
legal document transferring property ownership
Pronunciation: / did əv kənˈveɪəns / (AmE), / diːd əv kənˈveɪəns / (BrE)
a legal document that transfers ownership of property from one person to another

Examples

The deed of conveyance
Before closing on the house, both parties reviewed the deed of conveyance carefully.
She filed the deed of conveyance with the county clerk to ensure the transaction was official.
The lawyer prepared the deed of conveyance to transfer the property to the new owners.

  • synonym: title deed - a deed or document showing ownership of property
