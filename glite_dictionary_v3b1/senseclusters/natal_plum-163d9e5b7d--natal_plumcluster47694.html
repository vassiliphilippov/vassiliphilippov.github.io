natal plum

noun
edible, scarlet fruit of a South African shrub
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪt̬l plʌm / (AmE), / ˈneɪ.təl plʌm / (BrE)
An edible, scarlet, plumlike fruit of a South African shrub, often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Examples

A ripe natal plum
Birds are attracted to the bright red natal plum growing on the shrub outside our window.
The natal plum has a sweet, cranberry-like flavor when fully ripe.
They planted a natal plum hedge along the garden path for both its fruit and flowers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carissa - the South African shrub that bears the natal plum
