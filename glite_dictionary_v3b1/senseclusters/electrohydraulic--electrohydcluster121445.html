electrohydraulic

noun
electrically controlled hydraulics
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊhaɪˈdrɑlɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊhaɪˈdrɒlɪk / (BrE)
a machine, device, or system that uses electrical control together with hydraulic power to operate

Examples

Electrohydraulic machine
The engineer repaired the electrohydraulic system that controls the lift in the warehouse.
Modern construction equipment often uses electrohydraulic units for precise movement and control.
The technician tested the electrohydraulic device to ensure it responded correctly to electrical signals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electromechanical - involving both electrical and mechanical processes
  • synonym: hydraulic - operated by the movement of liquid, especially water or oil
