kiwifruit

noun
green-fleshed fruit
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːwiˌfruːt /; / ˈkiwiˌfrut / (AmE), / ˈkiːwiˌfruːt / (BrE)
a small, oval fruit with brown fuzzy skin, bright green flesh, and tiny black seeds
