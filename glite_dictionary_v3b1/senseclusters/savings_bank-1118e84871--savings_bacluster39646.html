savings bank

noun
financial institution for savings deposits
2.8 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈseɪ.vɪŋz bæŋk/ /; / /ˈseɪ.vɪŋz beɪŋk/ (AmE), /ˈseɪ.vɪŋz bæŋk/ /; / /ˈseɪ.vɪŋz beɪŋk/ (BrE)
a financial institution that accepts savings deposits and pays interest, traditionally focused on encouraging personal savings, and now often offering services similar to commercial banks

Examples

Open a savings bank account
He decided to open an account at the local savings bank to start building his emergency fund.
Her grandparents deposited money for her college fund in a savings bank years ago.
Many savings banks now provide online banking and competitive loan rates.
