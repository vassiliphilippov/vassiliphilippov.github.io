wheeze

verb
breathe with a whistling sound
Pronunciation: / wiz / (AmE), / wiːz / (BrE)
to breathe with difficulty, producing a whistling or rattling sound in the chest

Examples

To wheeze after running
After running up the stairs, he began to wheeze from the effort.
Her chest tightened, and she started to wheeze in the cold air.
The old man would often wheeze during the night due to his asthma.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gasp - draw in breath sharply
  • synonym: pant - breathe quickly and with difficulty
