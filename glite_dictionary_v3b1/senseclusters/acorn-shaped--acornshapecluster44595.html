acorn-shaped

adjective
shaped like an acorn
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪˌkɔrn ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈeɪˌkɔːn ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape of an acorn; rounded at the bottom and tapering to a point at the top

Examples

Acorn-shaped charm
He wore a pendant with an acorn-shaped charm around his neck.
Some ancient helmets were distinctly acorn-shaped in appearance.
The architect designed an acorn-shaped dome for the church.
