chart

noun
visual display of information
1.0
Pronunciation: / tʃɑrt / (AmE), / tʃɑːt / (BrE)
a sheet of information in the form of a table, graph, or diagram, specially arranged for easy reference or to show relationships between facts or figures

Examples

A bar chart
A bar chart can help you compare the popularity of different products.
She studied the chart to understand the company's performance over the past year.
The financial analyst presented the sales data using a colorful chart.

Related Terms

  • synonym: graph - a diagram showing the relation between variable quantities
  • synonym: table - an arrangement of data in rows and columns
