barrow-girl

noun
female street vendor (of a barrow)
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbæroʊ ɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈbærəʊ ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a woman who sells fruit and vegetables from a barrow, typically in a street market

Examples

A barrow-girl sells fruit
During the 19th century, a barrow-girl was a common sight in London markets.
The barrow-girl called out her prices as she arranged apples and oranges on her cart.
The smiling barrow-girl handed me a ripe pear with a cheerful greeting.
