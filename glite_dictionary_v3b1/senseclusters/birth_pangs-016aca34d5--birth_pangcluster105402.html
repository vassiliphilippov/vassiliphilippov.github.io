birth pangs

noun
labor pains during childbirth
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɝθ pæŋz / (AmE), / bɜːθ pæŋz / (BrE)
recurrent sharp pains experienced by a woman during childbirth, caused by contractions of the uterus

Examples

Birth pangs start
As the birth pangs started, she knew the baby would be arriving soon.
She gripped the bed as the birth pangs became more frequent and intense.
The doctor reassured her that the birth pangs were a normal part of labor.
