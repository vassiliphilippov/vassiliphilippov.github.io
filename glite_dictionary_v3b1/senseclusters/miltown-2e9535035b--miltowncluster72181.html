Miltown

noun
trade name for a sedative drug
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlˌtaʊn / (AmE), / ˈmɪltaʊn / (BrE)
A trademarked name for meprobamate, a sedative and tranquilizer used to treat anxiety and muscle tension.

Examples

Miltown medicine
After several sleepless nights, her doctor prescribed Miltown to help her relax.
He was cautioned not to drive while taking Miltown due to its sedative effects.
In the 1950s, Miltown became one of the most widely used tranquilizers in the United States.
