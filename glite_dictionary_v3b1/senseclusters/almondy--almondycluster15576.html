almondy

adjective
pale almond color
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːlməndi / (AmE), / ˈɑːməndi / (BrE)
having a pale brown or creamy off-white color similar to that of an almond's skin or flesh

Examples

Almondy color walls
The living room was decorated in soft almondy tones to create a warm atmosphere.
She wore an almondy dress that complemented her complexion perfectly.
The artist mixed several paints to achieve the exact almondy shade he wanted for the portrait.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ivory - a creamy-white color
