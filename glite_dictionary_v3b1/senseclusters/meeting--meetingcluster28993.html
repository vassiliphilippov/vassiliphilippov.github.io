Synonyms

  • coming together
    3.5 3.50/m
    coming together is a general joining or gathering of people or things, while a meeting often implies a planned event or discussion.
  • meeting of minds
    4.2 0.07/m
    meeting of minds is deep agreement and shared ideas between people, while a meeting can be any gathering without full agreement.
  • merging
    3.0 0.06/m
    merging is the process of joining things into one, often permanently, while a meeting is a coming together that may be temporary.
