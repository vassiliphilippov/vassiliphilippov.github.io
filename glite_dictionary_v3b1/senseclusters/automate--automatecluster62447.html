automate

verb
make automatic by using machines or computers
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːtəˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəmeɪt / (BrE)
to make a process, system, or task operate automatically by using machines or computers instead of people

Examples

To automate office tasks
Many repetitive office tasks can now be automated with specialized software.
The company decided to automate its assembly line to increase efficiency.
We are automating the billing process to reduce errors and save time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automatize - to make automatic, often used synonymously with 'automate'
  • antonym: manual - done by hand, not automatic
