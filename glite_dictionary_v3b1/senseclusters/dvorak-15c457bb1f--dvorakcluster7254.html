Dvorak

noun
cyclone intensity method
Pronunciation: / ˈdvɔːræk / (AmE), / ˈdvɔːræk / (BrE)
a satellite-based method for estimating the intensity of tropical cyclones, developed by Vernon Dvorak

Examples

Dvorak technique
Meteorologists used the Dvorak technique to estimate the hurricane's strength from satellite images.
The Dvorak method has become a standard tool for tracking tropical cyclones worldwide.
Advances in technology have improved the accuracy of the Dvorak analysis for storm intensity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: satellite imagery - images of Earth taken from satellites
  • synonym: tropical cyclone - a rotating storm system over tropical oceans
