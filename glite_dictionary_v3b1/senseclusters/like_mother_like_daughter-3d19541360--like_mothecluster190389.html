Pronunciation: / laɪk ˈmʌðɚ, laɪk ˈdɔtɚ / (AmE), / laɪk ˈmʌðə, laɪk ˈdɔːtə / (BrE)
used to say that a daughter has qualities or behaviors similar to her mother

Examples

They both have a passion for gardening; like mother, like daughter.
Whenever I see them laugh together, I think, 'like mother, like daughter.'
