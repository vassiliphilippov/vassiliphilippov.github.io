phonatory

adjective
relating to vocal sound
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊnəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈfəʊnətəri / (BrE)
relating to the production of sound by the vocal cords

Examples

Phonatory system

Damage to the larynx can affect phonatory function and make speaking difficult.
Researchers studied the phonatory mechanisms involved in singing high notes.
