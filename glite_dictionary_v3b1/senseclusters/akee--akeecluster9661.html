akee

noun
tropical tree with edible fruit
7.0
Pronunciation: / əˈkiː / (AmE), / əˈkiː / (BrE)
An evergreen tree (Blighia sapida), native to West Africa but widely cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions for its fragrant flowers and distinctive red fruit, which is used as food after proper preparation.
