notification

noun
formal request for payment
Pronunciation: / ˌnoʊtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnəʊtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
a formal request for payment, especially one given in writing to remind someone of a debt or obligation

Examples

Payment notification
A second notification was issued when the first one was ignored.
After receiving the notification, he settled his outstanding balance immediately.
The company sent a notification of overdue payment to the client.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demand - an insistent and formal request for payment of a debt
  • synonym: reminder - something that causes someone to remember a payment is due
