Iyar

noun
Jewish calendar month (April–May)
9.9
Pronunciation: / iˈjɑr / (AmE), / iˈjɑː / (BrE)
the eighth month of the civil year and the second month of the ecclesiastical year in the Jewish calendar, usually occurring in April and May

Examples

The month Iyar
Many Jews observe the custom of counting the Omer during the month of Iyar.
Passover ends in the middle of Iyar each year.
The holiday of Lag BaOmer falls on the 18th day of Iyar.
