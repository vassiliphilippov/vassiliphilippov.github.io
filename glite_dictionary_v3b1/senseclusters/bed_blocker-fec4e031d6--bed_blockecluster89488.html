bed blocker

noun
person or thing that blocks access to a bed
Pronunciation: / bɛd ˈblɑkɚ / (AmE), / bɛd ˈblɒkə / (BrE)
a person or object that prevents others from using a bed or takes up excessive space in a bed

Examples

Dog bed blocker
Every morning I wake up cramped because my dog is a notorious bed blocker.
Her collection of pillows has become the ultimate bed blocker for anyone sharing her bed.
The cat is such a bed blocker that I can barely find room to sleep.
