dandruff

noun
flaking of scalp skin
Pronunciation: / ˈdæn.drəf / (AmE), / ˈdæn.drəf / (BrE)
a condition in which small white flakes of dead skin appear on the scalp and may fall onto the hair or shoulders

Examples

Dandruff flakes on shoulders
He was embarrassed by the dandruff visible on his shoulders.
If you notice persistent dandruff, consult a dermatologist.
Special shampoos are often used to treat dandruff.
Frequent washing with a medicated shampoo can help control dandruff.
He was embarrassed when someone pointed out the dandruff in his hair.
She wore a dark sweater, making her dandruff much more noticeable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seborrhea - excessive discharge of sebum from the sebaceous glands, often linked to dandruff
  • synonym: seborrheic dermatitis - A medical condition causing flaking and itching of the skin, especially the scalp
