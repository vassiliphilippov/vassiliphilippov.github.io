reappointment

noun
follow-up appointment
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˈpɔɪntmənt / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈpɔɪntmənt / (BrE)
the act of arranging or scheduling another appointment, especially as a follow-up with a professional or service

Examples

Doctor reappointment
The dentist's office called to confirm my reappointment for next Tuesday.
After missing her session, she quickly scheduled a reappointment with her therapist.
You can request a reappointment online if you need to see the doctor again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appointment - a scheduled meeting or arrangement
  • antonym: cancellation - the act of calling off a scheduled meeting
