Advil

noun
trademarked pain and fever reliever
2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈæd.vɪl/ (AmE), /ˈæd.vɪl/ (BrE)
A trademarked name for ibuprofen, a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used to relieve pain, reduce inflammation, and lower fever.
