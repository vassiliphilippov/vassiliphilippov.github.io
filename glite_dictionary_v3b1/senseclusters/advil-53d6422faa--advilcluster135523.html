Advil

noun
trademarked pain and fever reliever
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈæd.vɪl / (AmE), / ˈæd.vɪl / (BrE)
A trademarked name for ibuprofen, a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used to relieve pain, reduce inflammation, and lower fever.

Examples

Advil pain relief
After spraining her ankle, she took Advil for the pain.
My doctor recommended Advil to help reduce my fever and headache.
You can buy Advil over the counter at most pharmacies.
