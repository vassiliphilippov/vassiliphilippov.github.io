Haldol

noun
trademarked antipsychotic drug (haloperidol)
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhælˌdɒl / (AmE), / ˈhaldɒl / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for haloperidol, a tranquilizer used to treat psychotic disorders and Tourette's syndrome.

Examples

Haldol medication
In severe cases of agitation, Haldol can be administered as an emergency medication.
Some patients taking Haldol may experience drowsiness as a side effect.
The doctor prescribed Haldol to help manage the patient's schizophrenia symptoms.
