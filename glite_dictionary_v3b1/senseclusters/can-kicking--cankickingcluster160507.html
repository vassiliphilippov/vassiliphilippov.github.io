can-kicking

noun
postponing a decision or problem
9.9 7.6
Pronunciation: /kæn ˈkɪk.ɪŋ/ /; / /kən ˈkɪk.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /kæn ˈkɪk.ɪŋ/ /; / /kən ˈkɪk.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the action of delaying or avoiding dealing with a difficult issue or making a decision, especially by postponing it for later
