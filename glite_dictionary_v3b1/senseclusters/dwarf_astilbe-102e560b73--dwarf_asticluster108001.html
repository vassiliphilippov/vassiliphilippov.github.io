dwarf astilbe

noun
mat-forming evergreen Asian ground cover plant
Pronunciation: / dwɔrf əˈstɪlbi / (AmE), / dwɔːf əˈstɪlbi / (BrE)
A mat-forming evergreen plant native to Asia, with finely divided leaves and small pink to burgundy flowers, often grown as ground cover in gardens.
