slab

verb
to cover with slabs
6.7
Pronunciation: /slæb/ (AmE), /slæb/ (BrE)
to cover or form something with a thick, flat piece or layer, especially of stone or concrete

Examples

To slab a walkway

After mixing the clay, the artist slabbed it onto the table to begin shaping her sculpture.
They slab the dough onto the tray before cutting it into pieces.
