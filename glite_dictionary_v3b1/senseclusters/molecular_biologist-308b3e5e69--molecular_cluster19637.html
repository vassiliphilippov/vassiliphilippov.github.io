molecular biologist

noun
scientist specializing in molecular biology
5.1 4.0
Pronunciation: / məˈlɛkjələr baɪˈɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / məˌlɛk.jʊ.lə baɪˈɒl.ə.dʒɪst / (BrE)
A scientist who studies the structure and function of molecules essential to life, such as DNA, RNA, and proteins.

Examples

Molecular biologist studies DNA
As a molecular biologist, he spends much of his time analyzing DNA sequences.
Her team includes a renowned molecular biologist specializing in gene therapy.
The conference attracted dozens of young molecular biologists from top universities around the world.
