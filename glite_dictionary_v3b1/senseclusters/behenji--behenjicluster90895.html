behenji

noun
traditional or conservative woman (informal, Indian English)
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪ.hen.dʒi / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.hen.dʒi / (BrE)
(informal, Indian English) A woman regarded as traditional, conservative, or socially old-fashioned.

Examples

A behenji woman
Despite her modern job, people still see her as a behenji because of her conservative outlook.
Even though she is young, the behenji in her prefers modest clothing and traditional values.
It's hard to ignore the behenji side of me when I visit my family.
