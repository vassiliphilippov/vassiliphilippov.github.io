sesamoiditis

noun
inflammation of sesamoid bones
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛsəˌmɔɪˈdaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌsɛsəmɔɪˈdaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the small sesamoid bones, usually occurring in the foot and causing pain or discomfort

Examples

Sesamoiditis foot pain
The runner developed sesamoiditis after increasing her training intensity too quickly.
Wearing proper footwear can help prevent sesamoiditis in athletes and dancers.
A doctor diagnosed him with sesamoiditis after he complained of pain in the ball of his foot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arthritis - inflammation of the joints
  • synonym: tendinitis - inflammation of a tendon
