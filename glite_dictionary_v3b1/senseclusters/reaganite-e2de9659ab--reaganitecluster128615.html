Reaganite

noun
supporter of Reagan
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪɡəˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈreɪɡənaɪt / (BrE)
a person who supports or advocates the policies and ideals of Ronald Reagan, especially his conservative political views

Examples

A Reaganite supporter
During the 1980s, many young voters identified as Reaganites because of his economic policies.
The candidate's views appealed to every Reaganite in the audience.
As a proud Reaganite, she often quoted the former president in debates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conservative - a person who supports traditional political views
  • antonym: liberal - a person who supports progressive or left-leaning political views
