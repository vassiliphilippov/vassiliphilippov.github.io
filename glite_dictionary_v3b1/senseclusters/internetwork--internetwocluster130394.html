internetwork

verb
to connect networks
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈnɛtwɜrk / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈnɛtwɜːk / (BrE)
to connect two or more separate computer networks so that devices on them can exchange data

Examples

To internetwork systems
The company decided to internetwork its regional offices to improve communication and data sharing.
Engineers worked overnight to internetwork the new data centers with the existing infrastructure.
By internetworking their systems, the universities created a collaborative research environment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disconnect - to separate or break the connection between networks or devices
  • synonym: network - to connect computers or devices together
