hardness

noun
lack of compassion or feeling
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːrd.nəs /; / ˈhɑrd.nəs / (AmE), / ˈhɑːd.nəs / (BrE)
lack of emotion, compassion, or tenderness; excessive sternness or severity in character or treatment

Examples

Emotional hardness
Despite his outward hardness, he truly cared about his friends.
His hardness prevented him from showing any sympathy to those in need.
She was criticized for the hardness of her response to the tragedy.
During training, the hardness of the instructors was legendary among the recruits.
Some people mistook his hardness for a lack of compassion.
The hardness of her tone left no room for argument.

  • synonym: insensitivity - lack of sensitivity or emotional response
  • antonym: kindness - the quality of being friendly, generous, or considerate
  • antonym: leniency - the quality of being more merciful or tolerant than expected
  • synonym: severity - the quality of being severe; strictness or harshness
