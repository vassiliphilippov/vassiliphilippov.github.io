Iberia

proper noun
ancient region in the Caucasus (now Georgia)
Pronunciation: / aɪˈbɪriə / (AmE), / aɪˈbɪəriə / (BrE)
an ancient region south of the Caucasus Mountains, roughly corresponding to present-day Georgia

Examples

Ancient Iberia in Georgia
Archaeologists have discovered artifacts from Iberia dating back to the early medieval period.
The ancient kingdom of Iberia played a crucial role in the history of the Caucasus region.
The rulers of Iberia were instrumental in the spread of Christianity in the area now known as Georgia.
