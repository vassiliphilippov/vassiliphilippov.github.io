Orphean

adjective
enchantingly musical
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈɔːr.fi.ən/ /; / /ˈɔːr.fɪ.ən/ (AmE), /ˈɔː.fi.ən/ /; / /ˈɔː.fɪ.ən/ (BrE)
having an enchanting, deeply musical, or lyrical quality, especially in a way that captivates or entrances; relating to Orpheus, especially in reference to music or poetry that is enchanting or moving
