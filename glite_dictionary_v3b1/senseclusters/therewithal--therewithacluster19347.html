therewithal

adverb
in addition; besides
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌðɛrwɪˈðɔl / (AmE), / ˌðɛːwɪˈðɔːl / (BrE)
In addition to that; besides; together with that.

Examples

And therewithal
His reputation was ruined, and therewithal his prospects for future advancement disappeared.
She completed her duties, and therewithal took on new responsibilities without complaint.
The king granted him land, and therewithal bestowed a generous pension.

  • synonym: moreover - in addition to what was said
