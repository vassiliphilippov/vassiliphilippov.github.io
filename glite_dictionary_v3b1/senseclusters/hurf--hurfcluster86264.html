hurf

verb
to bluster online
9.9 0.00/m
to speak or write in a blustering or nonsensical way, especially online or in debates

Examples

To hurf online
During the heated discussion, he began to hurf without making any real points.
People sometimes hurf on social media when they do not have all the facts.
She was tired of listening to him hurf about topics he barely understood.
