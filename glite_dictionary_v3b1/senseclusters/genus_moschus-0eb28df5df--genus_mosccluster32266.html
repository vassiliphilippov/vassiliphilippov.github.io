genus Moschus

noun
genus of musk deer mammals
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈmɑːskəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈmɒskəs / (BrE)
A genus of small, hoofed mammals known as musk deer, native to Asia, distinguished by the male's musk gland.

Examples

Genus Moschus musk deer
Biologists study the genus Moschus to understand the unique adaptations of musk deer.
Conservation efforts have focused on protecting animals within the genus Moschus due to poaching for musk.
The genus Moschus includes several species of musk deer found in mountainous regions of Asia.
