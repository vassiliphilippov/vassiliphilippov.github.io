unsayable

adjective
too taboo to say
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈseɪəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈseɪəbl / (BrE)
so inappropriate, offensive, or taboo that it should not be spoken aloud

Examples

Unsayable truth
There are some unsayable truths that people avoid discussing in public.
He paused, realizing his thoughts were too unsayable to share with the group.
The comedian avoided unsayable topics to keep the show appropriate for all ages.

Related Terms

  • antonym: speakable - able to be spoken or mentioned
  • synonym: taboo - prohibited or restricted by social custom
  • synonym: unmentionable - too embarrassing or taboo to be mentioned
