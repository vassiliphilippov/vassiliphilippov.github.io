penal facility

noun
institution for confining offenders
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːnəl fəˈsɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˈpiːnəl fəˈsɪlɪti / (BrE)
A building or institution where people are confined as punishment for crimes and to protect the public.

Examples

A penal facility
After overcrowding became an issue, the government built a new penal facility outside the city limits.
Prison reform advocates are calling for major changes in how penal facilities are managed.
The criminal was sentenced to five years in a penal facility for armed robbery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jail - a place for the confinement of people accused or convicted of a crime
  • synonym: prison - a building where people are kept as a punishment for a crime or while they are waiting for trial
