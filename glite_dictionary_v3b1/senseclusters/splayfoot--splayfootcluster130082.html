splayfoot

noun
foot with fallen arch; flattened, widened shape
Pronunciation: / ˈspleɪˌfʊt / (AmE), / ˈspleɪˌfʊt / (BrE)
A foot with a fallen arch resulting in an abnormally flattened and widened shape.

Examples

A splayfoot problem
A splayfoot can lead to discomfort and difficulty walking if not treated.
After years of wearing the wrong shoes, he developed a noticeable splayfoot.
Children with splayfoot may need special insoles for support.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flatfoot - a condition in which the arch of the foot collapses, with the entire sole coming into complete or near-complete contact with the ground
