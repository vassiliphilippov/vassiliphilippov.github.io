caricature plant

noun
tropical shrub with face-like leaf patterns
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkærɪkətʃɚ plænt / (AmE), / ˈkærɪkətjʊə plɑːnt / (BrE)
a tropical shrub (Graptophyllum pictum) native to the Old World, known for its purple or red tubular flowers and leaves marked with patterns resembling a human face

Because of its unique appearance, the caricature plant is popular in tropical landscaping.
