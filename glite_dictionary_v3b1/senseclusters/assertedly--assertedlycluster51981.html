assertedly

adverb
claimed as true
Pronunciation: / əˈsɜːrtɪdli / (AmE), / əˈsɜːtɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that is claimed or stated to be true, often without proof

Examples

Document assertedly signed
The document was assertedly signed by the company director, but no one witnessed it.
He was assertedly present at the meeting, though there is no official record.
The new policy was assertedly designed to help small businesses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: allegedly - in a way that is claimed to be true but not proven
  • synonym: supposedly - as is claimed, though not necessarily true
