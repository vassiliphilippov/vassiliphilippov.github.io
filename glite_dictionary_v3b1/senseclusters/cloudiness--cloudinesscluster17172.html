Synonyms

  • overcast
    2.5 0.09/m
    overcast is a dark, fully clouded sky condition often used in weather reports, while cloudiness names the sky being covered with clouds.
  • overcast sky
    2.5 0.27/m
    overcast sky describes a sky entirely covered by clouds with no sun, while cloudiness names the general state of the sky being cloudy.
  • cloud cover
    3.4 0.92/m
    cloud cover measures how much of the sky is covered by clouds, often as a percentage, while cloudiness describes a cloudy sky state.
