parfait

noun
layered dessert with ice cream and syrup
3.7 4.1
Pronunciation: /pɑːr.ˈfeɪ/ (AmE), /pɑː.ˈfeɪ/ (BrE)
a dessert made of layers of ingredients such as ice cream, yogurt, cream, fruit, and sometimes granola, usually served in a tall glass

Examples

A parfait dessert
She ordered a strawberry parfait for dessert after dinner.
The restaurant serves a delicious chocolate parfait layered with nuts and caramel.
You can easily make a fruit parfait at home using yogurt and berries.
For a healthy snack, she prepared a parfait with yogurt, berries, and granola.
The restaurant served a chocolate parfait topped with whipped cream and nuts.
He enjoyed a refreshing fruit parfait on a hot summer afternoon.
