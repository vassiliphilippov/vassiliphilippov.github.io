pigtailed

adjective
hair in two tails
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪɡˌteɪld / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡˌteɪld / (BrE)
wearing the hair tied into two sections, usually at the sides or back, resembling tails

Examples

Pigtailed girl hairstyle
The pigtailed girl smiled as she skipped down the street.
He wore a pigtailed wig for the costume party.
Several pigtailed children played together in the schoolyard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braided - with hair woven into braids
  • antonym: ponytailed - with hair tied back in a single tail
