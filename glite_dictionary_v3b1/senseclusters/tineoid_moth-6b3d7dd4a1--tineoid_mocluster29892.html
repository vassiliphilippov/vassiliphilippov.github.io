tineoid moth

noun
small dull-colored moth with chewing mouthparts
Pronunciation: / taɪˈniːɔɪd mɒθ / (AmE), / tɪˈniːɔɪd mɒθ / (BrE)
a small, dull-colored moth with chewing mouthparts, typically belonging to the superfamily Tineoidea

Examples
