eggplant parmesan

noun
Italian-American eggplant and cheese dish
Pronunciation: / ˈɛgˌplænt ˌpɑːrməˈzɑːn / (AmE), / ˈɛgˌplɑːnt ˌpɑːməˈzæn / (BrE)
An Italian-American dish consisting of breaded and fried eggplant slices layered with tomato sauce and cheese, then baked.

Examples

Baked eggplant parmesan
Her homemade eggplant parmesan is always a hit at family gatherings.
I'm trying a new recipe for baked eggplant parmesan this weekend.
We ordered eggplant parmesan at the Italian restaurant last night and it was delicious.
