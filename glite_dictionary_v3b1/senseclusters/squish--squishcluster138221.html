squish

noun
soft sucking noise, e.g. in mud
4.2 3.7
Pronunciation: /skwɪʃ/ (AmE), /skwɪʃ/ (BrE)
a soft, sucking noise made when stepping on or compressing something wet or soft, such as mud

Examples

A squish in mud
As she walked through the marsh, each step made a distinct squish.
He stepped into the mud with a loud squish under his boot.
The squish of wet grass echoed as we crossed the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: squelch - a crushing or sucking sound, especially when walking on wet ground
