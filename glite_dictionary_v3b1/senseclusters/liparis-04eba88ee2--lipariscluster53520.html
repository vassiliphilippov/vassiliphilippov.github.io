Liparis

noun
a genus of orchids
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪˌpɛərɪs / (AmE), / ˈlɪpərɪs / (BrE)
A genus of terrestrial and epiphytic orchids found in a wide range of climates from tropical to temperate regions.

Examples

Liparis orchid flower
Many Liparis orchids have glossy leaves and small, delicate flowers.
Several species within the Liparis genus thrive in both tropical forests and temperate woodlands.
The botanist carefully catalogued the newly discovered Liparis orchid from the rainforest.
