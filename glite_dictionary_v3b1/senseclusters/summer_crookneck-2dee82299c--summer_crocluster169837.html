summer crookneck

noun
yellow squash with curved neck and warty skin
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌmɚ ˈkrʊknɛk / (AmE), / ˈsʌmə ˈkrʊknɛk / (BrE)
A type of yellow summer squash with a thin, curved neck and slightly warty skin, commonly used in cooking.

A summer crookneck squash
