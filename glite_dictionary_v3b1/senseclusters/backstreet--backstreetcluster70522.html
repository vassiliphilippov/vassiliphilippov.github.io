backstreet

adjective
illegal or secret
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌstriːt / (AmE), / ˈbækˌstriːt / (BrE)
secret, illegal, or disreputable and not officially recognized or approved, especially referring to activities or businesses.

Examples

Backstreet business
The police raided a backstreet clinic that was operating without a license.
He found work in a backstreet factory that paid workers under the table.
Many backstreet businesses avoid taxes and government regulations.
