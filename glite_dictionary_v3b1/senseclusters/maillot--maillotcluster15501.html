maillot

noun
one-piece women's swimsuit
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪ.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪ.ɒt / (BrE)
A woman's one-piece swimsuit, especially in a style resembling a leotard.

Examples

A red maillot
She looked elegant in her red maillot by the pool.
The fashion magazine featured the latest designer maillots for summer.
Unlike bikinis, a maillot provides more coverage and support.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bikini - two-piece women's swimsuit
  • synonym: swimsuit - garment worn for swimming
