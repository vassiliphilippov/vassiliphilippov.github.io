unpicturable

adjective
cannot be pictured
9.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpɪktʃərəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɪktʃərəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to represent or imagine in a picture or image

Examples

An unpicturable idea
The concept of infinity is often considered unpicturable because it cannot be shown visually.
Some emotions are so complex that they seem unpicturable in any form of art.
For many, the true nature of time remains unpicturable despite scientific explanations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: depictable - able to be represented in a picture or image
  • synonym: unimaginable - impossible to imagine or comprehend
