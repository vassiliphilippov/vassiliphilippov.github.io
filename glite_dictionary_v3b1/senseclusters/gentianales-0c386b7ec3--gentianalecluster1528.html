Gentianales

noun
order of flowering plants
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɛnʃiəˈneɪliːz /; / ˌdʒɛn(t)ʃiəˈneɪliːz / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛnʃɪəˈneɪliːz /; / ˌdʒɛn(t)ʃɪəˈneɪliːz / (BrE)
