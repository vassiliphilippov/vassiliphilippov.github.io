Socialist Labor Party

proper noun
U.S. socialist political party, founded 1874
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəlɪst ˈleɪbər ˈpɑːrti / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəlɪst ˈleɪbə ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
a political party in the United States, founded in 1874, that advocated for the peaceful establishment of socialism
