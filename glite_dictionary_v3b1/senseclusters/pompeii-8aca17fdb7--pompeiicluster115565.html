Pompeii

proper noun
ancient city buried by Vesuvius eruption
Pronunciation: / pɑmˈpeɪ.i / (AmE), / pɒmˈpeɪ.i / (BrE)
an ancient Roman city near Naples, Italy, which was buried by the eruption of Mount Vesuvius in AD 79

Examples

Ancient Pompeii city
Archaeologists have uncovered remarkably preserved houses in Pompeii.
The eruption of Mount Vesuvius in 79 AD destroyed Pompeii in a matter of hours.
Thousands of tourists visit Pompeii each year to see its ancient ruins.
