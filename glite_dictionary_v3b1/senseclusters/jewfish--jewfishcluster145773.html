jewfish

noun
large marine food fish of Australia
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuːˌfɪʃ /; / ˈdʒuˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːˌfɪʃ /; / ˈdʒuːfɪʃ / (BrE)
a large marine fish found in warm seas, valued as seafood and known for its size and thick head
