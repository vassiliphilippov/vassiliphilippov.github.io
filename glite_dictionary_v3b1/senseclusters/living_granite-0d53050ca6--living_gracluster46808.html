living granite

noun
succulent plant resembling granite
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪvɪŋ ˈɡrænət / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ ˈɡranɪt / (BrE)
a highly succulent, stemless, clump-forming plant with grey-green leaves resembling lumps of granite, native to South Africa

Examples

Living granite plant
In South Africa, living granite plants are admired for their camouflage against predators.
She added a living granite to her succulent collection for its unique appearance.
The living granite thrives in arid conditions and blends in with surrounding rocks.
