manifoldness

noun
variety; multiplicity
Pronunciation: / ˈmænɪˌfoʊldnəs / (AmE), / ˈmænɪfəʊldnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having many and various parts or aspects

Examples

Cultural manifoldness
The manifoldness of cultures in the city makes it a fascinating place to live.
Scientists were amazed by the manifoldness of life forms discovered in the rainforest.
The manifoldness of his talents impressed everyone at the audition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diversity - the state of being diverse or varied
  • synonym: multiplicity - the state of being multiple or having many parts
  • antonym: uniformity - the quality of being the same or consistent
