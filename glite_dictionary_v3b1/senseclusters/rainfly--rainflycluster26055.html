rainfly

noun
protective tent covering
3.2 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈreɪn.flaɪ/ (AmE), /ˈreɪn.flaɪ/ (BrE)
a piece of waterproof material, usually a sheet or flap, that covers a tent to protect it from rain

Examples

A rainfly on tent
Before it started raining, we made sure the rainfly was securely attached to the tent.
If you forget your rainfly, you risk getting wet inside your tent on rainy nights.
The rainfly kept us dry during the night when a storm passed over our campsite.
