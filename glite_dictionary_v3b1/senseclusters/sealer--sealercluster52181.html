sealer

noun
material for coating and sealing surfaces
5.0 0.61/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàsiÀêl…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàsiÀêl…ô / (BrE)
a substance or material applied to a porous surface to form a hard, protective coating, often before painting or finishing

Examples

Wood sealer coat
Before painting, apply a sealer to ensure the surface is smooth and protected.
She used a clear sealer to preserve the natural look of the wood table.
The contractor recommended a water-resistant sealer for the concrete patio.

Synonyms

  • sealant
    4.2 0.75/m
    sealant fills joints and stops water or air, while sealer is a coating applied to porous surfaces before painting to protect them.
  • sealing material
    3.4 0.01/m
    sealing material fills cracks or joins to stop leaks, while sealer is spread over porous surfaces as a protective coating before painting.
  • seal
    3.7 5.32/m
    a seal can be a device or tight coating that blocks moisture, while sealer is a liquid coating used on porous surfaces before finishing.
