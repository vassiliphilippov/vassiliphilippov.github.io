mindful of

adjective
aware and considerate of
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪnd.fəl əv / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnd.fəl ɒv / (BrE)
conscious of and attentive to something, especially in a way that shows care or consideration.

Examples

Mindful of the environment
The company is mindful of its impact on the environment and looks for sustainable solutions.
When planning the menu, the chef was mindful of the guests' dietary restrictions.
You should always be mindful of your tone when giving feedback to colleagues.
