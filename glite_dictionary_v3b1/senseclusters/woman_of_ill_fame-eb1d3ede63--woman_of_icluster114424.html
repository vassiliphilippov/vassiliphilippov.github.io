woman of ill fame

noun
disreputable or immoral woman
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊmən əv ɪl feɪm / (AmE), / ˈwʊmən əv ɪl feɪm / (BrE)
a woman known for having an immoral or disreputable reputation, especially regarding sexual conduct

Examples

In many old novels, a woman of ill fame is often shunned by the townspeople.
Rumors quickly spread, labeling her a woman of ill fame even without proof.
