dental caries

noun
tooth decay; cavity formation
1.0 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈden.təl ˈker.iːz/ /; / /ˈden.təl ˈker.ɪz/ (AmE), /ˈden.təl ˈkeə.riːz/ /; / /ˈden.təl ˈkeə.rɪz/ (BrE)
