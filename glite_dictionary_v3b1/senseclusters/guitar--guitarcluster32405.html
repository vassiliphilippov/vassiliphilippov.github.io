guitar

noun
stringed musical instrument
Pronunciation: / ɡɪˈtɑr / (AmE), / ɡɪˈtɑː / (BrE)
A stringed musical instrument, typically with six strings, played by strumming or plucking.

Examples

Acoustic guitar
He strummed his guitar softly by the campfire.
She learned to play the guitar when she was a teenager.
The band features a singer, a drummer, and a guitar player.

Related Terms

  • synonym: banjo - a stringed instrument with a circular body and long neck
  • synonym: violin - a bowed string instrument with four strings
