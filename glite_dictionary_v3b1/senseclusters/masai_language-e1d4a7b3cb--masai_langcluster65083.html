Masai language

noun
language of the Masai people
Pronunciation: / məˈsaɪ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / məˈsaɪ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Nilotic language spoken by the Masai people in Kenya and northern Tanzania

Examples

Masai language spoken
