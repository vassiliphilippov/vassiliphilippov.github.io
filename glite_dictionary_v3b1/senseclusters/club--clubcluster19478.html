club

noun
nightlife venue with entertainment, food, and drinks
Pronunciation: / klʌb / (AmE), / klʌb / (BrE)
a place, especially one open late at night, that provides entertainment, dancing, food, and drinks; a nightclub or similar venue

Examples

A night club
He worked as a bartender at a popular club near the waterfront.
She spent every weekend dancing at her favorite club downtown.
The new jazz club has a fantastic atmosphere and great live music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nightclub - an establishment for late-night entertainment, music, and dancing
