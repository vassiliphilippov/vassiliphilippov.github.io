sheepshank

noun
a knot for shortening rope
Pronunciation: / ˈʃipˌʃæŋk / (AmE), / ˈʃiːpˌʃæŋk / (BrE)
a knot used to shorten a rope or take up slack without cutting it

Examples

A sheepshank knot
If your rope is too long, a sheepshank can provide a temporary fix.
The sailor tied a sheepshank to shorten the loose rope on deck.
Using a sheepshank, he quickly adjusted the length of the line without cutting it.
