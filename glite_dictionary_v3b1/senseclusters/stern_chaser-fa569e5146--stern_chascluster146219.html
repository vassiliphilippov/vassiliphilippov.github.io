stern chaser

noun
naval gun for firing astern
8.5 8.8
Pronunciation: /stɜːrn ˈtʃeɪ.sər/ /; / /stɜːrn ˈtʃeɪ.zər/ (AmE), /stɜːn ˈtʃeɪ.sə/ (BrE)
A naval gun mounted at the stern of a ship, used to fire at a pursuing enemy vessel.
