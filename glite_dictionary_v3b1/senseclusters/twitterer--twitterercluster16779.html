twitterer

noun
a bird that twitters
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪt̬ɚɚ / (AmE), / ˈtwɪtərə / (BrE)
a bird that makes a series of short, high-pitched sounds or calls

Examples

A small twitterer
Early in the morning, a lone twitterer perched on the windowsill greeted the dawn.
She loved listening to every twitterer in the park as they announced the arrival of spring.
The twitterer flitted from branch to branch, filling the garden with cheerful notes.
