yawningly

adverb
in a boring way
Pronunciation: / ˈjɔnɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈjɔːnɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows or causes boredom or tiredness

Examples

Yawningly boring movie
The movie was yawningly predictable and failed to hold my attention.
He delivered his speech in a yawningly monotonous tone that made the audience restless.
The book's plot moved yawningly slowly, making it hard to finish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boringly - in a dull or uninteresting manner
  • antonym: excitingly - in a way that causes excitement
