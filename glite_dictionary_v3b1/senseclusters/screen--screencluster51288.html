screen

verb
prevent from entering or passing through
4.8 3.2
Pronunciation: /skriːn/ (AmE), /skriːn/ (BrE)
to block, shield, hide, or protect someone or something from view, danger, or harm

Examples

Thick hedges screened the house from the road, ensuring privacy for the residents.
