ninjitsu

noun
Japanese art of espionage and stealth
Pronunciation: / nɪnˈdʒɪtsu / (AmE), / nɪnˈdʒɪtsu / (BrE)
A traditional Japanese art of espionage and stealth involving techniques for spying, camouflage, and unconventional warfare.

Examples

Traditional ninjitsu art
He spent many years mastering ninjitsu at a renowned martial arts school.
Her knowledge of ninjitsu gave her an edge in escaping detection.
The film depicted various ancient ninjitsu techniques, including silent movement and disguise.
