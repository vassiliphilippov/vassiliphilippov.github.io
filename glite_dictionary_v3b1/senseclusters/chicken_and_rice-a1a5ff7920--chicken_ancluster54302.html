chicken and rice

noun
dish of chicken cooked with rice
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪkən ənd ˈraɪs / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn ənd ˈraɪs / (BrE)
A dish consisting of rice and chicken cooked together, often seasoned and sometimes combined with other ingredients.

Examples

A chicken and rice dish
