cliffed

adjective
with cliffs
7.0
Pronunciation: / klɪft / (AmE), / klɪft / (BrE)
having cliffs or steep rock faces along its surface or edge

Examples

A cliffed coastline
The cliffed coastline made access to the beach difficult.
Birds nested along the cliffed edges of the island, far from predators.
The hikers admired the dramatic, cliffed landscape stretching along the shore.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flat - having a level surface without raised areas
  • synonym: rocky - covered with or consisting of rocks
