cichlid

noun
a family of tropical freshwater fish
9.9 9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪklɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɪklɪd / (BrE)
Any of a large family of chiefly freshwater fishes native to tropical America, Africa, and Asia, many of which are kept as aquarium fish.

Colorful cichlids
