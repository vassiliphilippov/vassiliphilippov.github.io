remount

noun
fresh horse, esp. for replacement in battle
Pronunciation: / ˌriˈmaʊnt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈmaʊnt / (BrE)
a fresh horse, especially one provided to replace a horse killed or injured in battle

Examples

A fresh remount
He chose a sturdy remount from the fresh batch delivered to the regiment.
The cavalry unit waited anxiously for a remount after losing several horses in combat.
Without a reliable remount, the messenger could not continue his urgent journey.
