preset

noun
saved setting
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌsɛt / (BrE)
a saved setting or configuration that can be quickly applied to a device or software

Examples

A preset option
She chose a preset on her camera to take better night photos.
The music app offers several presets for different genres like rock or jazz.
You can save your favorite settings as a preset for future use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: configuration - an arrangement of settings or options
  • synonym: default - a standard setting automatically used by a device or software
