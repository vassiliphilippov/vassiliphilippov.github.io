make over

verb
reuse after alteration
4.3
Pronunciation: / meɪk ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / meɪk ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to use something again after altering or adapting it, especially in a new or different form

Examples

To make over old clothes
