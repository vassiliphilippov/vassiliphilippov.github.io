Wordle

verb
to play Wordle
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːdəl / (AmE), / ˈwɜːdəl / (BrE)
to play the online Wordle puzzle, usually by trying to guess the day's five-letter word in a limited number of attempts

Examples

To Wordle each morning
Every morning they Wordle together, comparing guesses and celebrating quick solutions.
She likes to Wordle during her coffee break to challenge her mind.
Many people Wordle daily and share their results with friends online.
