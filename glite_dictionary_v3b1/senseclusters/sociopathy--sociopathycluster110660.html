Synonyms

  • sociopathic personality
    9.5 0.01/m
    sociopathic personality describes a person's pattern of cold, rule-breaking behaviour, while sociopathy names the condition behind those same traits.
  • antisocial personality disorder
    6.5 0.17/m
    antisocial personality disorder is the formal clinical diagnosis for long-term rule-breaking and lack of empathy, while sociopathy is a common term for that condition.
  • psychopathic personality
    9.0 0.07/m
    psychopathic personality often describes a person who is cold and violent without guilt, whereas sociopathy focuses more on general lack of empathy and rule-breaking.
  • psychopathy
    9.6 2.66/m
    psychopathy often includes bold charm and selfish traits, while sociopathy is mainly about lack of empathy and breaking social rules.

Related Terms

  • antonym: empathy - the ability to understand and share the feelings of others
  • synonym: psychopathy - a personality disorder marked by persistent antisocial behavior and lack of remorse
