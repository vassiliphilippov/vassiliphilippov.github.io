sociopathy

noun
antisocial personality disorder
9.2
Pronunciation: /soʊ.sɪ.ˈæp.ə.θiː/ /; / /soʊ.ʃɪ.ˈæp.ə.θiː/ (AmE), /səʊ.ʃɪ.ˈɑː.pə.θiː/ /; / /səʊ.sɪ.ˈɑː.pə.θiː/ (BrE)
a personality disorder or condition where someone shows a lack of empathy, ignores social rules, and behaves in an antisocial way

Examples

Sociopathy personality disorder
The psychologist explained that sociopathy often leads to difficulties in forming genuine relationships.
Some experts debate whether sociopathy and psychopathy are truly different conditions.
A lack of remorse is a common trait in people diagnosed with sociopathy.
The film's villain displays clear signs of sociopathy through his manipulative and remorseless actions.
Some experts argue that sociopathy is shaped more by environment than by genetics.
Her complete lack of guilt after hurting others suggested possible sociopathy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antisocial personality disorder - a mental disorder involving persistent antisocial behavior and lack of empathy
  • antonym: empathy - the ability to understand and share the feelings of others
  • synonym: psychopathy - a personality disorder marked by persistent antisocial behavior and lack of remorse
