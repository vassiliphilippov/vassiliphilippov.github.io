yenta

noun
gossipy or meddlesome woman
7.8 9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈjen.tə/ (AmE), /ˈjen.tə/ (BrE)
(informal) a woman who is gossipy or meddlesome, especially one who habitually gets involved in other people's affairs
