newshound

noun
eager journalist
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːzˌhaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌhaʊnd / (BrE)
a journalist who is very eager and persistent in finding and reporting news stories

Examples

A top newshound
The experienced newshound chased every lead to get the latest scoop.
As a dedicated newshound, she never missed a breaking story.
Every newsroom needs at least one newshound who thrives on uncovering the truth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: journalist - a person who collects, writes, or edits news stories
  • synonym: reporter - a person who writes or broadcasts news stories
