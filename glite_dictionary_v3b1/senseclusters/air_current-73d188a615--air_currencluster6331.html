air current

noun
flow or movement of air
3.3 0.43/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…õr Àåk…ùr…ônt / (AmE), / Ààe…ô Àåk år…ônt / (BrE)
A flow of air, especially when moving with noticeable speed or force, from one place to another.

Examples

Strong air current
A sudden air current swept through the open window, scattering papers across the room.
Hot air currents can cause turbulence for small aircraft during takeoff and landing.
The soaring eagle effortlessly rode the air current above the canyon.

Synonyms

  • current of air
    3.5 0.10/m
    current of air is air moving from high pressure to low pressure, while an air current is a general flow between places.
  • airflow
    3.5 2.46/m
    airflow describes how air moves through or around things, while an air current is a moving flow between places, often stronger.
  • airstream
    4.2 0.02/m
    airstream is a steady, well-defined wind moving over large areas, while an air current is any flow between places, often local.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wind - moving air, especially outdoors and naturally occurring
