self-fertilization

noun
union of gametes from one individual
5.7 5.2
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlfˌfɜrtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˌfəːtɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process in which an organism's own male and female gametes unite to produce offspring
