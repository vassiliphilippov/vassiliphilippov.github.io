knackery

noun
animal waste by-products
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnækəri / (AmE), / ˈnækəri / (BrE)
the animal carcasses, offal, or waste material produced by a business that processes dead or unwanted animals, especially for fertilizer or other by-products

Examples

Knackery waste
The knackery was collected and sent to the plant for processing into fertilizer.
After the animals died, their remains became part of the knackery used by local farmers.
Strict regulations control how knackery is handled to protect public health and the environment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rendering - the process of converting animal waste into usable materials
