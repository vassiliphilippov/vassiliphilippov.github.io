corporate chaos

noun
business-related disorder or confusion
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔːr.pə.rət ˈkeɪ.ɑs / (AmE), / ˈkɔː.pər.ət ˈkeɪ.ɒs / (BrE)
