SMSgt

noun
U.S. Air Force senior noncommissioned officer rank
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛs ˌɛm ˈɛs ˌdʒiː ˈtiː / (AmE), / ˌɛs ˌɛm ˈɛs ˌdʒiː ˈtiː / (BrE)
abbreviation for Senior Master Sergeant, a senior noncommissioned officer rank in the U.S. Air Force, comparable to master sergeant in the Army.

Examples

SMSgt rank
After twenty years of service, he was promoted to SMSgt last month.
Every SMSgt is responsible for mentoring junior enlisted personnel in the Air Force.
