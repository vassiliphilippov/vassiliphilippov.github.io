gelée

noun
savory jelly for dishes
8.6
Pronunciation: / ʒəˈleɪ / (AmE), / ʒəˈleɪ / (BrE)
a savory jelly made from meat or fish stock or gelatin, used to set, glaze, or encase dishes

Examples

Salmon gelée layer
The chef decorated the cold salmon with a layer of gelée to give it a glossy finish.
Slices of chicken were beautifully arranged and set in a savory gelée for the appetizer.
She learned to prepare a traditional French gelée to encase vegetables for the holiday meal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aspic - a savory jelly made with meat stock, often used to encase food
  • synonym: jelly - a soft, clear food made from gelatin, often sweet or savory
