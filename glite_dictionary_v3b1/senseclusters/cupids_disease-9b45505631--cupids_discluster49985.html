Cupid's disease

noun
humorous term for being in love
8.1 9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːpɪdz dɪˈziːz / (AmE), / ˈkjuːpɪdz dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
A humorous or metaphorical way to refer to being in love, likening it to catching a contagious illness.

Examples

Caught Cupid's disease
After meeting her, he claimed he was suffering from Cupid's disease and couldn't think of anything else.
They teased him about his odd behavior, suggesting he had come down with Cupid's disease.
Whenever Valentine's Day arrives, it seems like everyone catches Cupid's disease.
