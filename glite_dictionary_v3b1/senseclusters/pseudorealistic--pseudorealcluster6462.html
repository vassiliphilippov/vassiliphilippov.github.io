pseudorealistic

adjective
seemingly realistic
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːdoʊriəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌsjuːdəʊriːəˈlɪstɪk /; / ˌsjuːdəʊriəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
appearing to be realistic or lifelike, but actually using stylized or simplified techniques instead of true realism

Examples

Pseudorealistic graphics
The video game uses pseudorealistic graphics to create an immersive but stylized world.
Her paintings have a pseudorealistic quality, blending real details with imaginative elements.
The documentary's pseudorealistic approach made viewers question what was fact and what was fiction.
The movie used pseudorealistic animation to create lifelike characters with a unique artistic touch.
Many video games feature pseudorealistic graphics that balance realism and stylization for better performance.
The artist's pseudorealistic paintings suggest real scenes but with simplified shapes and bold colors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artificial - not natural or real; made to seem like something real
  • degree higher: photorealistic - extremely realistic, like a photograph
  • antonym: realistic - representing things as they really are
  • degree lower: stylized - depicted in a non-realistic, artistic way
