pseudorealistic

adjective
seemingly realistic
8.3
Pronunciation: /suː.doʊ.riː.ə.ˈlɪs.tɪk/ /; / /sjuː.doʊ.riː.ə.ˈlɪs.tɪk/ (AmE), /sjuː.dəʊ.riː.ə.ˈlɪs.tɪk/ /; / /suː.dəʊ.riː.ə.ˈlɪs.tɪk/ (BrE)
appearing to be realistic or lifelike, but actually using stylized or simplified techniques instead of true realism

Examples

  • degree lower: stylized - depicted in a non-realistic, artistic way
