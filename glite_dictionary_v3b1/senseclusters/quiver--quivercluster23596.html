quiver

noun
container for arrows
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪvɚ / (AmE), / ˈkwɪvə / (BrE)
a container for holding arrows, usually worn on the back, hip, or belt by archers.

Examples

An archer's quiver
A full quiver hung across his back as he entered the tournament.
He fashioned a leather quiver to carry his arrows during the hunt.
The archer reached for an arrow from her quiver before taking a shot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrow - a shaft shot from a bow
