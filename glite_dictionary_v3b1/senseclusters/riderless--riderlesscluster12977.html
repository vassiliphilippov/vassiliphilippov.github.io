riderless

adjective
without a rider
4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈraɪ.dər.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈraɪ.də.ləs/ (BrE)
without a rider or person controlling it, especially referring to an animal or vehicle

Examples

Riderless horse
The riderless horse galloped across the field, startling everyone nearby.
During the parade, a riderless bicycle rolled down the street after slipping from its owner's hands.
A riderless scooter was found at the edge of the park late last night.
A solemn riderless horse followed the procession, its empty saddle a symbol of loss.
The riderless horse walked quietly behind the casket during the military funeral.
Tradition calls for a riderless horse to honor fallen leaders in state ceremonies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unmanned - without a person operating or controlling it
  • synonym: unmounted - not having a rider or person on top
