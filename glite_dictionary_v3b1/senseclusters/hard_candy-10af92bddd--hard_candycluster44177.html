hard candy

noun
brittle, solid sweet confection
3.7 1.0
Pronunciation: /hɑːrd ˈkæn.diː/ (AmE), /hɑːd ˈkæn.dɪ/ (BrE)
A sweet confection made from sugar that is solid and brittle when cooled, designed to be sucked rather than chewed.

Examples

A bowl of hard candy
Every Halloween, my grandmother gives us a bowl of assorted hard candy to share.
The bowl was filled with colorful hard candy wrapped in shiny paper.
You should be careful not to bite down on hard candy, as it can damage your teeth.
