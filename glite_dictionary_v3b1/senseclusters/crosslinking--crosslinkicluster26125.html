crosslinking

noun
linking web pages
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔsˌlɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌlɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of creating hyperlinks between different web pages or websites to improve navigation or search ranking

Examples

Website crosslinking
Effective crosslinking can help users find related information more easily on a website.
The marketing team focused on crosslinking to boost the site's search engine ranking.
Good crosslinking between articles keeps visitors engaged and increases page views.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hyperlinking - adding clickable links to web content
  • synonym: interlinking - connecting multiple web pages or sites with links
