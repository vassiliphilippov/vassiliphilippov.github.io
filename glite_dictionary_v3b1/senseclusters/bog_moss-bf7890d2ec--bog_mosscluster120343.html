bog moss

noun
a type of Sphagnum moss found in bogs
9.1
Pronunciation: / bɑɡ mɑs / (AmE), / bɒɡ mɒs / (BrE)
any of various pale or ashy mosses of the genus Sphagnum, whose decomposed remains form peat

Examples

Bog moss plants
Bog moss plays a crucial role in the formation of peat bogs.

You can often find bog moss carpeting the ground in damp, acidic environments.
