haaa

interjection
sigh of relief
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɑː / (AmE), / hɑː / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express a long sigh of relief, exhaustion, or resignation

Examples

A long haaa of relief
After finishing the marathon, she let out a long haaa and sat down to rest.
He sighed haaa when he finally found his lost keys under the couch.
The teacher gave a quiet haaa after grading all the exams late at night.
