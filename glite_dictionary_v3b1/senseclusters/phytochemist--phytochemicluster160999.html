phytochemist

noun
chemist specializing in plant chemistry
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪtoʊˈkɛmɪst / (AmE), / ˌfʌɪtəʊˈkɛmɪst / (BrE)

Examples

A phytochemist studies plants

She became a leading phytochemist after years of research on medicinal herbs.
The phytochemist analyzed the leaves to identify active plant compounds.
