underbody

noun
the underside of an animal's body
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌbɑdi / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌbɒdi / (BrE)
the underside or belly of an animal's body, especially referring to the softer part underneath

Examples

Animal underbody
Predators often target the underbody of their prey, where it is less protected.
She gently stroked the dog's underbody to check for any injuries.
The cat's underbody is covered in white fur.
