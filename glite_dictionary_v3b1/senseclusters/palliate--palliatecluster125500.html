palliate

verb
to ease symptoms or pain
3.8 7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈpæl.ɪ.eɪt/ /; / /ˈpæl.ɪ.ət/ (AmE), /ˈpæl.ɪ.eɪt/ /; / /ˈpæl.ɪ.ət/ (BrE)
to make a disease or its symptoms less severe without removing the cause; to reduce the intensity of pain or discomfort

Examples

To palliate pain
Doctors may try to palliate the symptoms of the disease with medication.
Hospice care aims to palliate suffering and improve the quality of life for terminally ill patients.
The new treatment helped palliate her chronic pain, but it did not cure the underlying illness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alleviate - to make (pain, suffering, or a problem) less severe
  • synonym: relieve - to reduce or remove (pain, distress, etc.)
