henna

noun
reddish-brown dye from a shrub
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛnə / (AmE), / ˈhɛnə / (BrE)
a reddish-brown dye made from the powdered leaves of a tropical shrub, used especially to color hair and skin

Examples

Henna hair dye
She used henna to color her hair a vibrant shade of auburn.
Some people choose henna as a natural alternative to chemical hair dyes.
Traditional brides in India often decorate their hands with henna designs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dye - a substance used to add color to or change the color of something
