keto

noun
state of ketosis
4.2 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàkiÀêto ä / (AmE), / ÀàkiÀêt…ô ä / (BrE)
a metabolic state in which the body burns fat and produces ketones for energy instead of carbohydrates

Examples

Reach keto state
After three days of fasting, he finally reached keto and felt less hungry and more energetic.
Many people follow a low-carb diet to enter keto and promote fat loss.
Athletes sometimes use keto to improve their endurance and energy levels.
