pyrolignic

adjective
produced by heating wood (technical, chemistry)
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪrəˈlɪdʒnɪk / (AmE), / ˌpʌɪrəˈlɪdʒnɪk / (BrE)
(of a substance) produced by the action of heat on wood, especially through destructive distillation.

Examples

Pyrolignic acid

The sharp odor was traced to pyrolignic oils released by the burning wood.
