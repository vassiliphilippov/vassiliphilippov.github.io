put forward

verb
propose as a candidate
3.3
Pronunciation: / pʊt ˈfɔrwərd / (AmE), / pʊt ˈfɔːwəd / (BrE)
to suggest someone or something as a candidate for an honor, award, or position

Examples

To put forward a candidate
She was put forward for the award by her colleagues due to her outstanding work.
The committee decided to put forward his name as a nominee for the prize.
Three candidates were put forward for the vacant position at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nominate - to formally propose as a candidate
  • antonym: withdraw - to remove from consideration as a candidate
