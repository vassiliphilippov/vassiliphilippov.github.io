Synonyms

  • nominate
    3.4 20/m
    nominate is a formal official suggestion used in committees or awards, while put forward is a more general way to suggest a candidate.
  • propose
    3.4 2/m
    propose often means formally suggest a person in meetings or votes, while put forward is used more widely for any candidate suggestion.
  • put up
    4.0 0.3/m
    put up is an informal phrase used to name someone for a post, while put forward is a neutral phrase used in formal settings.
