field guide

noun
book for identifying natural objects outdoors
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːld ˌɡaɪd / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ˌɡaɪd / (BrE)
A book providing descriptions and illustrations to help identify natural objects, such as plants, animals, or rocks, in their natural environment.

Examples

A field guide to birds
He brought a field guide to birds on his hiking trip.
This field guide is full of detailed illustrations of local wildflowers.
Using a field guide, she was able to identify the mushroom species.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guidebook - a book that gives information or instruction about a subject or place
