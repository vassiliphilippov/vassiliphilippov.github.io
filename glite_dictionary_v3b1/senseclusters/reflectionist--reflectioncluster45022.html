reflectionist

noun
introspective person
9.6
Pronunciation: / rɪˈflɛkʃənɪst / (AmE), / rɪˈflɛkʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who regularly thinks deeply about their own thoughts, feelings, or experiences

Examples

A thoughtful reflectionist
As a reflectionist, he often kept a journal to record his thoughts and feelings.
The reflectionist in the group encouraged everyone to consider their motivations before making decisions.
Her reflectionist nature made her a thoughtful and considerate friend.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extrovert - a person who is outgoing and focused on the external world
  • synonym: introspect - to examine one's own thoughts or feelings
