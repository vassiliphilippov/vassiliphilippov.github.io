Botrychium multifidum

noun
a species of European fern
Pronunciation: / boʊˈtrɪkiəm ˌmʌltɪˈfaɪdəm / (AmE), / bəʊˈtrɪkɪəm ˌmʌltɪˈfɪdəm / (BrE)
A species of European fern with leathery, sparsely hairy fronds, commonly known as leather grapefern.
