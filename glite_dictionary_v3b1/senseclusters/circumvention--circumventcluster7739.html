circumvention

noun
the act of evading by going around
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɝːkəmˈvɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɜːkəmˈvɛnʃən / (BrE)
The act of avoiding a rule, problem, or restriction by cleverly going around it rather than dealing with it directly.

Examples

Clever circumvention of rules
His clever circumvention of the security system impressed the team.
The company's circumvention of regulations resulted in hefty fines.
There are laws in place to prevent the circumvention of tax obligations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compliance - the act of conforming to a rule or law
  • synonym: evasion - the act of avoiding something, especially by cleverness or trickery
