derp

noun
silly or foolish person (slang)
8.0
Pronunciation: / dɝːp / (AmE), / dɜːp / (BrE)
A person who is silly or acts in a foolish or stupid manner, often in an amusing or cute way.

Examples

A total derp
Don't be such a derp—read the instructions before you start!
She made a silly face in the photo and looked like a total derp.
Whenever he forgets his keys for the third time, we just call him a derp.

Related Terms

  • antonym: genius - a person with outstanding intelligence or skill
  • synonym: idiot - a very stupid or foolish person
