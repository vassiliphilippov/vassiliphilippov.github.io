unemancipated

adjective
not legally independent
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪˈmænsɪˌpeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈmænsɪpeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not freed from control or authority; still under the power or responsibility of another person or entity

Examples

Unemancipated minor
A child is considered unemancipated until reaching the age of majority.
The court ruled that the teenager was still unemancipated and under her parents' care.
Many laws treat unemancipated minors differently from adults in legal matters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dependent - relying on someone else for support or authority
  • antonym: emancipated - freed from control or authority; legally independent
