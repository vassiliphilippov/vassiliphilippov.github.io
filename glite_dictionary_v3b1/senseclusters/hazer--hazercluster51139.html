hazer

noun
stage mist machine
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪzər / (AmE), / ˈheɪzə / (BrE)
a machine that produces a fine mist or haze to enhance lighting effects on stage or in events

Examples

Stage hazer machine
The lighting crew used a hazer to create a subtle mist across the stage before the concert began.
Without the hazer, the beams of light would not have been as visible during the performance.
They rented a hazer for the event to make the dance floor lighting more dramatic.

Related Terms
