yellow pine

noun
hard yellowish wood from certain pines
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊ paɪn / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ paɪn / (BrE)
a type of pine tree with hard, yellowish wood, or the wood from this tree, often used in construction and furniture
