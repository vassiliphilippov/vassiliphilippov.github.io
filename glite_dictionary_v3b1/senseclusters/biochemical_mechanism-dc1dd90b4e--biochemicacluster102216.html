biochemical mechanism

noun
chemical process in living organisms
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊˈkɛm.ɪ.kəl ˈmɛk.əˌnɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.əʊˈkɛm.ɪ.kəl ˈmɛk.əni.zəm / (BrE)
