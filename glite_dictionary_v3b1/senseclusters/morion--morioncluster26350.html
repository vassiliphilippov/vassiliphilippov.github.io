morion

noun
16th-century metal helmet
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔriən / (AmE), / ˈmɒrɪən / (BrE)
a metal helmet with a crest or comb, worn by common soldiers in the 16th and early 17th centuries, especially in Europe.
