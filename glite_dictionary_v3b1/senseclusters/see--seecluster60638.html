see

verb
experience or live through
1.0 407/m
Pronunciation: / si / (AmE), / siÀê / (BrE)
to experience or be present at something happening or changing

Examples

To see hard times
During his career, he saw many hardships that tested his resolve.
She hopes never to see such difficult times again.
The city has seen several economic crises over the years.
That museum has seen a surge in visitors since it reopened.
The last decade saw remarkable advances in artificial intelligence.
We hope to see positive changes in the community this year.
