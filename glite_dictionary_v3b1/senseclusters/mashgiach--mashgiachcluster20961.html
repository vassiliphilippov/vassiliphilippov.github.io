mashgiach

noun
kosher food supervisor
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɑʃˈɡiːəx / (AmE), / mɑʃˈɡiːəx / (BrE)
a supervisor who ensures that food is prepared according to Jewish dietary laws (kashrut)

Examples

Kosher mashgiach
The mashgiach arrived early to inspect the kitchen before food preparation began.
A certified mashgiach is required for the restaurant to maintain its kosher status.
During Passover, the mashgiach pays extra attention to ensure all ingredients are permitted.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kosher - food that meets Jewish dietary laws
  • synonym: supervisor - a person who oversees work or activity
