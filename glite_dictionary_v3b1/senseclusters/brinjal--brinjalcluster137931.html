brinjal

noun
eggplant; aubergine (vegetable)
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪndʒɑl / (AmE), / ˈbrɪndʒɑːl / (BrE)
A variety of eggplant, particularly the large, glossy edible fruit, used as a vegetable; commonly known as aubergine or eggplant in other varieties of English.

Examples

Fresh brinjal curry
I love cooking brinjal in a spicy curry with fresh herbs.
She bought a kilo of brinjal at the local market for dinner.
The farmer harvested several brinjal plants from his vegetable garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aubergine - word for eggplant, chiefly in British English
  • synonym: eggplant - large purple edible fruit of the nightshade family, also known as aubergine
