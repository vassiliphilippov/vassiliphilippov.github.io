wall-to-wall

adjective
completely filled or crowded
4.2 0.52/m
Pronunciation: / w…îl t…ô w…îl / (AmE), / w…îÀêl t…ô w…îÀêl / (BrE)
(of a place or area) completely filled with people or things; extremely crowded or packed.

Examples

Wall-to-wall people
By midnight, the club was wall-to-wall with people dancing and laughing.
During the big sale, the store became wall-to-wall with shoppers hunting for bargains.
The room was wall-to-wall with enthusiastic fans eager for the concert to begin.

  • full to the gills

  • thronged

  • crowded
