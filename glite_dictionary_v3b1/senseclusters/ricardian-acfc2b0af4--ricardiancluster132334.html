Ricardian

adjective
of Ricardo or Richard III
9.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɑːrdiən / (AmE), / rɪˈkɑːdiən / (BrE)
relating to the economic theories of David Ricardo or to supporters of Richard III

Examples

Ricardian theory
The Ricardian model is important in international trade theory.
Many economists debate the relevance of Ricardian ideas in today's global economy.
Some historians study Ricardian supporters who defend the reputation of Richard III.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Keynesian - relating to the economic theories of John Maynard Keynes
  • synonym: Ricardianism - the economic theories of David Ricardo
