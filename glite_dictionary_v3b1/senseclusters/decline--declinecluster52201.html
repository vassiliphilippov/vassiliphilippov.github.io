decline

verb
to inflect a word for grammatical case, number, etc.
Pronunciation: / dɪˈklaɪn / (AmE), / dɪˈklaɪn / (BrE)
to inflect a noun, pronoun, or adjective to show its case, number, gender, etc., especially in languages with extensive inflection

Examples

To decline a noun
Can you decline the word 'puer' through all its cases?
It can be difficult for learners to decline nouns in Latin correctly.
Many Indo-European languages decline adjectives as well as nouns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conjugate - to inflect a verb for tense, person, number, etc.
