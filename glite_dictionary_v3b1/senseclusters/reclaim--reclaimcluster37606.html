reclaim

verb
recover materials from waste for reuse
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkleɪm / (AmE), / rɪˈkleɪm / (BrE)
to recover useful materials from waste products so they can be reused

Examples

To reclaim materials
Efforts to reclaim metals from old electronics have increased to reduce mining.
Many companies now reclaim plastic and turn it into new products.
The city has a facility that reclaims water from sewage for landscaping use.
