unobservant

adjective
not noticing things; inattentive
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəbˈzɜrvənt / (AmE), / ˌʌnəbˈzɜːvənt / (BrE)
not noticing or paying attention to things; not alert or attentive to details.

Examples

Being unobservant at work
Being unobservant can sometimes lead to missing important details at work.
His unobservant nature made it easy for surprises to catch him off guard.
Julia was so unobservant that she didn't notice the new painting in the hallway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inattentive - not paying close attention
  • antonym: observant - quick to notice things; alert and attentive
