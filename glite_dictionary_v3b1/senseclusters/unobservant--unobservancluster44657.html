unobservant

adjective
not noticing things; inattentive
2.3 5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəbˈzɜrvənt / (AmE), / ˌʌnəbˈzɜːvənt / (BrE)
not noticing or paying attention to things; not alert or attentive to details.

Examples

Julia was so unobservant that she didn't notice the new painting in the hallway.
