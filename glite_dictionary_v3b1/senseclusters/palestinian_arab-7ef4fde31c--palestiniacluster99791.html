Palestinian Arab

noun
descendant of Arabs from Palestine
Pronunciation: / pəˌlɛstəˈnɪən ˈɛrəb / (AmE), / ˌpalɪˈstiːnɪən ˈarəb / (BrE)
A person who descends from the Arab population that originally inhabited Palestine.

Examples

A Palestinian Arab person
He is a Palestinian Arab who now resides in Jordan.
Many Palestinian Arabs live in the West Bank and Gaza Strip.
The culture of Palestinian Arabs includes unique traditions and cuisine.

  • synonym: Palestinian - a native or inhabitant of Palestine
