unslaked

adjective
not quenched with water
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsleɪkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈsleɪkt / (BrE)
not treated with water or not quenched, especially referring to lime that has not been slaked with water.

Examples

Unslaked lime
The workers handled the unslaked lime with extreme caution to avoid chemical burns.
If unslaked lime comes into contact with water, it reacts violently and produces heat.
Always store unslaked materials in a dry place to prevent dangerous reactions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: quicklime - calcium oxide, often in unslaked form
  • antonym: slaked - treated with water, especially referring to lime
