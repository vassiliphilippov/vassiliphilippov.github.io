pipe fitting

noun
device for joining pipes together
3.1
Pronunciation: / paɪp ˈfɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / paɪp ˈfɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
A device or component, typically with threaded ends, used to connect sections of pipe together.

Examples

Metal pipe fitting
Corrosion on a pipe fitting can eventually lead to water damage if not replaced.
The plumber inspected each pipe fitting to ensure there were no leaks in the new installation.
You need the correct size of pipe fitting to connect those two sections securely.
