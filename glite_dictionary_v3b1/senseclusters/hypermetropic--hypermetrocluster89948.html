hypermetropic

adjective
able to see distant objects more clearly than near ones
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərməˈtroʊpɪk / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəməˈtrɒpɪk / (BrE)
(of a person or their eyes) able to see distant objects more clearly than close ones; affected by hypermetropia (farsightedness).

Examples

Hypermetropic eyes
Children who are hypermetropic may experience eye strain when reading for long periods.
His hypermetropic vision made it difficult to focus on objects close to his face.
The optometrist diagnosed her as hypermetropic and prescribed corrective lenses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: farsighted - able to see distant objects more clearly than near ones; synonym for hypermetropic
  • antonym: myopic - unable to see distant objects clearly; opposite of hypermetropic
