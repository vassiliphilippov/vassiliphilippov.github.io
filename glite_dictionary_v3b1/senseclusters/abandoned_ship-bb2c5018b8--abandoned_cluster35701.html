abandoned ship

noun
ship deserted by its crew
4.7
Pronunciation: / əˈbændənd ʃɪp / (AmE), / əˈbændənd ʃɪp / (BrE)
a ship left deserted by its crew and owners, especially on the high seas

Examples

An abandoned ship
After the disaster, the coast guard found an abandoned ship with no sign of its crew.
Pirates sometimes searched for valuables on an abandoned ship drifting near the coast.
The legal status of an abandoned ship can become complicated in international waters.
