accommodation

noun
place to stay; lodging
Pronunciation: / əˌkɑməˈdeɪʃən /; / əˌkɑːməˈdeɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌkɒməˈdeɪʃən / (BrE)
a place to stay or live, or the act of providing lodging or services

Examples

Hotel accommodation
During our road trip, we searched for affordable accommodation along the highway.
The hotel provides comfortable accommodation for business travelers and tourists alike.
The university offers on-campus accommodation for first-year students.
Finding suitable accommodation in the city center can be expensive.
The hotel offers accommodation for up to 200 guests during the summer season.
We arranged accommodation and meals for all the conference attendees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lodging - a place to sleep or live temporarily
  • synonym: shelter - protection or cover, often temporary housing
