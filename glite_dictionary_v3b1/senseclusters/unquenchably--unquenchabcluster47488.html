unquenchably

adverb
insatiably
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkwɛntʃəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈkwɛntʃəbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be satisfied, stopped, or extinguished

Examples

To unquenchably desire
He pursued knowledge unquenchably, always eager to learn more.
Her curiosity burned unquenchably, driving her to explore new places.
The team worked unquenchably toward their goal, refusing to give up.

Related Terms

  • synonym: endlessly - in a way that never ends
  • synonym: insatiably - in a way that cannot be satisfied
  • antonym: satisfiably - in a way that can be satisfied
