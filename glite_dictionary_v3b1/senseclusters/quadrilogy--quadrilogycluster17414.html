quadrilogy

noun
series of four works
8.0
Pronunciation: / kwɑːˈdrɪlədʒi / (AmE), / kwɒˈdrɪlədʒi / (BrE)
a series of four related works, such as books, movies, or plays, that form a connected set

Examples

A quadrilogy of films
The director released the final film in the quadrilogy to critical acclaim.
Fans eagerly collected all four novels in the fantasy quadrilogy.
The quadrilogy explores the main character's journey across four different time periods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tetralogy - a series of four related works, especially in classical drama
  • degree lower: trilogy - a series of three related works
