hairball

noun
clump of hair in an animal's stomach
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈheəˌbɔːl /; / ˈheə.bɔːl / (BrE)
a compact mass of hair that forms in the stomach or intestines of animals, especially from licking or grooming their fur
