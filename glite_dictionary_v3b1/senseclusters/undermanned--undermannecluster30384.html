Examples

Synonyms

  • understaffed
    3.4 0.53/m
    understaffed means having too few workers to do required tasks, while undermanned suggests not enough people to do a job properly.
  • short-staffed
    3.9 0.19/m
    short-staffed says a team has fewer staff than needed for its work, while undermanned highlights inability to do tasks properly.
  • short-handed
    4.4 0.22/m
    short-handed feels informal and often describes lacking helpers during work, while undermanned is a neutral term for lacking staff needed.
  • understrength
    5.5 0.04/m
    understrength means lacking people, strength, or resources for effectiveness, while undermanned focuses specifically on not having enough staff.
