irritative

adjective
causing irritation or stimulation
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɪrɪˌteɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˈɪrɪtətɪv / (BrE)
(of a physical stimulus) causing irritation or stimulation, especially in tissues or organs.

Examples

Irritative substances
Certain plants release irritative substances as a defense mechanism.
Prolonged exposure to dust may cause an irritative response in the airways.
The doctor explained that some chemicals have an irritative effect on the skin.

  • synonym: irritant - a substance that causes irritation
