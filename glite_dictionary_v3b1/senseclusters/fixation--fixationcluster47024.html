fixation

noun
firm attachment or fastening
Pronunciation: / fɪkˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / fɪkˈseɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of firmly attaching, securing, or fastening something in a set position

Examples

Secure fixation
Proper fixation of street signs ensures they remain stable during high winds.
Surgical fixation of fractured bones often involves metal plates or screws.
The fixation of the shelf to the wall required drilling holes and using strong anchors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attachment - the act of attaching something
  • antonym: release - the act of letting something go
