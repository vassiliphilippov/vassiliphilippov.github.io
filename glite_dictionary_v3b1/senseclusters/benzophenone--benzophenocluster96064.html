benzophenone

noun
chemical compound
Pronunciation: / ˌbɛnzoʊfəˈnoʊn / (AmE), / ˌbɛnzəʊˈfiːnəʊn / (BrE)
a chemical compound used as a photoinitiator or photosensitizer in UV-curing processes, inks, and polymer production

Examples

Benzophenone in ink
Manufacturers add benzophenone to inks to act as a photoinitiator during UV curing processes.
The laboratory ordered more benzophenone for their polymer research experiments.
Exposure to benzophenone is carefully controlled in industrial settings to ensure worker safety.
