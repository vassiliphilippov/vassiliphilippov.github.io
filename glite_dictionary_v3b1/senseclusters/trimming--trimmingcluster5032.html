trimming

noun
the act of cutting or shaping to size
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtrɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of cutting or shaping something to achieve a desired size or form

Examples

Hair trimming
I gave the hedge a careful trimming to keep it neat.
Proper trimming of fabric is essential for a tidy hem.
The gardener did a quick trimming before the guests arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pruning - removing superfluous parts, especially from plants, to promote growth
  • synonym: shaping - the act of giving something a particular shape
