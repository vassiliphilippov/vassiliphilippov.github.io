imipramine hydrochloride

noun
tricyclic antidepressant medication
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɪprəˌmin haɪˌdrɑːkləˌraɪd / (AmE), / ɪˈmɪprəmiːn haɪˌdrɒklaɪd / (BrE)
