Morris chair

noun
adjustable reclining armchair
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔːrɪs tʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈmɒrɪs tʃɛə / (BrE)
an armchair with a reclining, adjustable back and loose cushions, typically wooden-framed

Examples

A Morris chair armchair
He spent the afternoon reading in his Morris chair by the window.
She inherited a vintage Morris chair from her grandparents.
The living room was furnished with a classic oak Morris chair and a matching footstool.
