transcribe

verb
write in a different script
5.3
Pronunciation: / trænˈskraɪb / (AmE), / trænˈskraɪb / (BrE)
to write or represent spoken or written words in a different script or alphabet

Examples

To transcribe into English letters
It took several months to transcribe the Sanskrit prayers into the Roman script for publication.
Scholars will transcribe the old Greek texts using the Cyrillic script for linguistic comparison.
The ancient manuscript was carefully transcribed into the modern Latin alphabet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: transliterate - to write words in the characters of another alphabet
