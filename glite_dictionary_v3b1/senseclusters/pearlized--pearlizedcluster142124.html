pearlized

adjective
shiny like a pearl
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈpɜːlaɪzd / (BrE)
having a shiny, iridescent surface that resembles the appearance of a pearl

Examples

Pearlized nail polish
She chose a pearlized nail polish for the special occasion.
The car's pearlized finish sparkled in the sunlight, attracting everyone's attention.
He admired the pearlized buttons on his new shirt, which shimmered softly under the lights.

  • antonym: matte - having a dull, non-shiny surface
