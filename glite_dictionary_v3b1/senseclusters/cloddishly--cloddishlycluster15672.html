cloddishly

adverb
awkwardly or clumsily
Pronunciation: / ˈklɑːdɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈklɒdɪʃli / (BrE)
in a clumsy, awkward, or dull manner

Examples

Walks cloddishly
He stumbled cloddishly into the room, nearly knocking over a chair.
The new worker handled the delicate equipment cloddishly, causing concern among his colleagues.
She danced cloddishly at the party, but her friends cheered her on anyway.
