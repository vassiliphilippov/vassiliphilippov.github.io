actional

adjective
expressing action
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈækʃənəl / (AmE), / ˈækʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or expressing an action, especially in grammar or philosophy

Examples

An actional verb
In this sentence, the actional verb indicates what the subject is doing.
Some languages distinguish between actional and stative verbs in their grammar.
The philosopher discussed the difference between actional and non-actional statements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: active - showing or involving action or movement
  • antonym: stative - describing a state rather than an action
