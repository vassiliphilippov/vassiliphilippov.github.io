sophisto type

noun
person with sophisticated tastes
6.2
Pronunciation: / səˈfɪstoʊ taɪp / (AmE), / səˈfɪstəʊ taɪp / (BrE)
a person who has sophisticated or refined tastes, especially in art, culture, or lifestyle

Examples

The new restaurant caters to the sophisto type who appreciates fine wine and modern art.
