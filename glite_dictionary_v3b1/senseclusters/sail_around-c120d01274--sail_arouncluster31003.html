sail around

verb
navigate by boat in a circuit or area
1.1
Pronunciation: / seɪl əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / seɪl əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to travel by boat or yacht in a circuit or along a route, often around a particular place or area

Examples

To sail around the coast
Last summer, she sailed around the coast of Greece with her friends.
They dreamed of sailing around the world after retirement.
We spent the afternoon sailing around the lake, enjoying the sunshine.
