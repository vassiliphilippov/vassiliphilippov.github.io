Everglade

proper noun
Florida wetland region
Pronunciation: / ˈɛvərˌɡleɪd / (AmE), / ˈɛvəˌɡleɪd / (BrE)
a large tropical wetland region in southern Florida known for its unique ecosystem and wildlife

Examples

The Everglade region
Many rare birds and reptiles can be found in the Everglade region of Florida.
Scientists study the unique ecosystem of the Everglade to learn about wetland conservation.
The Everglade attracts tourists who want to experience its natural beauty and wildlife.

Related Terms

  • synonym: swamp - an area of low-lying, uncultivated ground where water collects
  • synonym: wetland - land consisting of marshes or swamps
