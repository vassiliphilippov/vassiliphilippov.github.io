low

verb
make the sound of a cow; moo
Pronunciation: / loʊ / (AmE), / ləʊ / (BrE)
(of a cow or similar animal) to make a deep, long sound; to moo

Examples

Cows low softly
I heard a cow low softly in the early morning mist.
The cattle began to low as the farmer approached with food.
The distant lowing of the herd echoed across the valley.
The cows lowed softly as the farmer approached with their feed.
In the quiet night, the sound of cattle lowing could be heard across the fields.
As the storm approached, the herd began lowing more loudly than usual.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bellow - to make a loud, deep sound, especially by an animal
  • synonym: moo - make the sound characteristic of cattle
