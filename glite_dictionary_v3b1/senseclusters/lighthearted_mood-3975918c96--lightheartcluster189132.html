lighthearted mood

noun
a cheerful, carefree atmosphere
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtˌhɑrtɪd ˌmud / (AmE), / ˈlaɪthɑːtɪd ˌmuːd / (BrE)
a cheerful and carefree atmosphere; a state where people feel happy and relaxed

Examples

A lighthearted mood
During the meeting, her jokes helped set a lighthearted mood among the team.
The comedy show created a lighthearted mood that lasted all evening.
We enjoyed dinner in a lighthearted mood, with lots of laughter and friendly conversation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: good mood - a state of feeling happy or content
  • antonym: serious mood - a thoughtful or solemn atmosphere
