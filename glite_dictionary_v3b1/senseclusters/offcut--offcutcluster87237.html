offcut

noun
leftover material piece
Pronunciation: / ˈɔfˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈɒfˌkʌt / (BrE)
a leftover piece of material remaining after the main part has been cut off or used

Examples

A offcut piece
The carpenter saved every offcut for future small projects.
She found an offcut of fabric that was perfect for making a patch.
Many artists use offcuts to create unique pieces of art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scrap - a small piece of material left after cutting
  • synonym: waste - material that is not needed and is thrown away
