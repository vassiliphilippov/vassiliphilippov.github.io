mantra

noun
sacred utterance or prayer; repeated statement
Pronunciation: / ˈmæn.trə / (AmE), / ˈmæn.trə / (BrE)
a word, sound, or phrase that is repeated, especially in meditation, prayer, or as a way to motivate or inspire yourself

Examples

A mantra for peace
'Stay positive' became his daily mantra during tough times.
She repeated the mantra quietly to herself as she meditated.
The monks chanted a sacred mantra in the temple at dawn.
He recited the mantra every morning to stay focused and motivated.
She repeated her favorite mantra to calm herself before the big presentation.
‘Quality over quantity’ became a mantra in the office after the new manager arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catchphrase - a well-known phrase often associated with a group or individual
  • synonym: chant - a repeated rhythmic phrase, often sung or spoken for spiritual purposes
  • synonym: slogan - a short, memorable phrase used in advertising or promotion
