secernate

verb
distinguish or mark as different
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛsərˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɛsəˌneɪt / (BrE)
To distinguish or mark as different; to separate by recognizing differences.

Examples

To secernate different ideas
A good teacher can secernate genuine questions from mere distractions in class.
Despite their similarities, the botanist was able to secernate the two plant species.
Modern technology allows us to secernate subtle differences in DNA sequences.
