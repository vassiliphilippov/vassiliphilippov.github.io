uncontrovertibly

adverb
undeniably
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkɑːntrəˈvɜrtəbli / (AmE), / ˌʌnkɒntrəˈvɜːtəbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be disputed or denied; unquestionably or undeniably.

Examples

The evidence uncontrovertibly proves
The evidence uncontrovertibly proves that the theory is correct.
He was uncontrovertibly chosen as the leader due to his experience.
The results uncontrovertibly show a significant improvement after the new policy was introduced.

Related Terms

  • antonym: arguably - in a way that can be supported by argument but not certain
  • synonym: indisputably - in a way that cannot be challenged or doubted
  • synonym: undeniably - in a way that cannot be denied
