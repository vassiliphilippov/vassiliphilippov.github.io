go-to resource

noun
preferred or reliable source
Pronunciation: / ˈgoʊ tu ˈrisɔrs / (AmE), / ˈgəʊ tuː ˈriːsɔːs / (BrE)
a source that is regularly relied upon as the most trustworthy or useful for information or support

Examples

The go-to resource for students
The library has long been the go-to resource for students needing credible references.
Whenever I need a new recipe, this blog is my go-to resource.
Wikipedia is my go-to resource for quick fact-checking and general information.
