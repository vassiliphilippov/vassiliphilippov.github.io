Lhasa

proper noun
capital city of Tibet, sacred in Buddhism
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːsə / (AmE), / ˈlɑːsə / (BrE)
The capital city of Tibet, regarded as a sacred center of Tibetan Buddhism and historically known as the Forbidden City for its former inaccessibility to outsiders.

Examples

Lhasa city
For centuries, Lhasa was off-limits to foreign visitors.
Pilgrims from all over Tibet travel to Lhasa to worship at its holy sites.
The Potala Palace is one of Lhasa's most iconic landmarks.
