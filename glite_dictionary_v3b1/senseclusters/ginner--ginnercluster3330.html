ginner

noun
cotton separator
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪnər / (AmE), / ˈɡɪnə / (BrE)
a person or machine that separates cotton fibers from their seeds

Examples

The ginner separates cotton
The ginner ensured that the cotton was clean and ready for processing.
Modern farms often use a mechanical ginner to speed up cotton production.
During harvest, the ginner played a crucial role in preparing the cotton for market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cotton gin - a machine that removes seeds from cotton fibers
