ricasso

noun
blunt blade section
9.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɑːsoʊ / (AmE), / rɪˈkɑːsəʊ / (BrE)
a blunt, unsharpened section of a blade just above the guard, often used for gripping

Examples

The ricasso section
The fencer placed his finger on the ricasso for better control of the sword.
Some historical swords feature a long ricasso to allow for two-handed use.
The blacksmith carefully shaped the ricasso before sharpening the rest of the blade.
