keylock

noun
lock opened by key
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːlɑk / (AmE), / ˈkiːlɒk / (BrE)
a lock that is operated by inserting and turning a key

Examples

A keylock lock
He inserted the key into the keylock to open the cabinet.
The old desk still has its original keylock and brass key.
Make sure the keylock is secure before leaving the office.

Related Terms

  • antonym: combination lock - a lock opened by entering a sequence of numbers or symbols
  • synonym: padlock - a detachable lock with a U-shaped bar
