loaner

noun
temporary replacement car
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊnər / (AmE), / ˈləʊnə / (BrE)
a car that is lent temporarily as a replacement when one's own vehicle is being repaired

Examples

Loaner car
My mechanic gave me a loaner while they fixed my transmission.
She drove a loaner for two days after her accident.
The dealership provided a loaner so I could get to work while my car was in the shop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: replacement car - a car provided as a substitute when another is out of service
