Examples

Regularization in models
Regularization is often used in machine learning to improve a model's ability to predict new data.
By applying regularization, the algorithm avoids fitting noise in the training set.
L1 and L2 are common types of regularization techniques in statistics and data science.
To prevent overfitting, the team applied L2 regularisation when training the neural network.
Many textbooks explain regularisation as adding a penalty term to the model's loss function.
They compared dropout and weight-decay regularisation techniques to see which improved validation accuracy.
