swelteringly

adverb
extremely hot manner
Pronunciation: / ˈswɛltərɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈswɛltərɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely hot and uncomfortable

Examples

Swelteringly hot day
The air was swelteringly hot, making it hard to concentrate during the afternoon meeting.
We walked through the swelteringly humid streets, searching for a place to cool down.
It was swelteringly warm in the classroom, and everyone was eager for the lesson to end.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coolly - in a way that is pleasantly or refreshingly cool
  • synonym: oppressively - in a way that is uncomfortably intense or stifling
