Camellia

noun
genus of tropical evergreen shrubs or small trees
Pronunciation: / kəˈmiliə / (AmE), / kəˈmiːliə / (BrE)
a genus of tropical Asiatic evergreen shrubs or small trees, including several species grown for their attractive flowers or for tea production

Examples

Camellia flower
Botanists study the different species within the Camellia genus for their unique characteristics.
She planted several Camellia bushes for their elegant blooms.
The Camellia genus includes both ornamental garden plants and the source of tea leaves.
