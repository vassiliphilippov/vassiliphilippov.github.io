nuanced

adjective
subtle distinctions
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːˌɑnst / (AmE), / ˈnjuːˌɑːnst / (BrE)
showing or having small and subtle differences or details

Examples

A nuanced argument
Her argument was thoughtful and nuanced, considering many sides of the issue.
The artist's use of color is very nuanced, creating a sense of depth and emotion.
He gave a nuanced explanation that captured the complexity of the situation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blunt - not subtle or detailed; direct and straightforward
  • synonym: detailed - including many small parts or facts
  • synonym: subtle - not obvious; delicate or precise
