superstructure

noun
structure above a ship's main deck
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌstrʌktʃər / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌstrʌktʃə / (BrE)
the part of a building, ship, or other structure that is above its base, foundation, or main deck
