private security force

noun
group hired for private security
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪvət sɪˈkjɚəti fɔrs / (AmE), / ˈpraɪvət sɪˈkjʊərəti fɔːs / (BrE)
a group of personnel privately employed to protect the safety or property of a business, organization, or individual

Examples

A private security force
During the festival, a private security force maintained order inside the event grounds.
Some wealthy neighborhoods rely on a private security force rather than the local police.
The company hired a private security force to monitor its warehouses after several break-ins.
