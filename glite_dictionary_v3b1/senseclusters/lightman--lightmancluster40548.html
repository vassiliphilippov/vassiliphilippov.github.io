lightman

noun
lighting technician
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtmæn / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtmæn / (BrE)
a person who manages, operates, installs, or maintains lighting in theater, film, television, or similar settings

Examples

Theater lightman
The lightman adjusted the spotlights before the show began.

A skilled lightman can completely change the mood of a scene with lighting.

A skilled lightman is essential for setting up complex lighting at concerts.
