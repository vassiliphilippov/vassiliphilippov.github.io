cry wolf

verb
raise a false alarm repeatedly
Pronunciation: / kraɪ wʊlf / (AmE), / kraɪ wʊlf / (BrE)
to raise a false alarm or repeatedly claim danger or trouble when none exists, so that subsequent warnings are likely to be ignored

Examples

To cry wolf falsely
His habit of crying wolf made everyone skeptical of his warnings.
If you cry wolf too often, people will stop believing you when something is actually wrong.
She realized her mistake after crying wolf so many times and being ignored when she truly needed help.

Related Terms

  • synonym: false alarm - an untrue warning about danger or trouble
  • synonym: raise the alarm - to warn people about danger or trouble
