unlifted

adjective
not stolen or removed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɪftɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɪftɪd / (BrE)
not taken, stolen, or removed; left in its original place, especially referring to something that could have been stolen.

Examples

Unlifted jewelry
The jewelry remained unlifted in the display case after the busy market day.
Despite the open door, all the valuables were found unlifted by the owner.
Security cameras showed the merchandise was still unlifted after the event.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stolen - taken without permission; illegally removed
  • synonym: untouched - not changed or affected; left as it was
