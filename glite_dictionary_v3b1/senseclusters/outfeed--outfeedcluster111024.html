outfeed

noun
machine exit point
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌfiːd / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌfiːd / (BrE)
the part of a machine where material exits after being processed

Examples

Outfeed table
The boards moved from the saw to the outfeed conveyor for stacking.
Be careful not to block the outfeed area while the machine is running.
After processing, the finished parts are collected at the outfeed section.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discharge - the act or place of releasing material from a machine
  • antonym: infeed - the part of a machine where material enters for processing
