plane

verb
smooth or shape with a plane
5.8
Pronunciation: / pleɪn / (AmE), / pleɪn / (BrE)
to make a surface smooth or flat by shaving off thin layers, especially using a carpenter's plane

Examples

She spent the afternoon planing down the wooden surface to remove any uneven spots.
The carpenter planes the rough boards until they are smooth enough for furniture making.

  • synonym: shave - to remove thin layers from a surface
