urban area

noun
a city or town area
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɝːbən ˌɛriə / (AmE), / ˈɜːbən ˌeəriə / (BrE)
a region characterized by high population density and significant built infrastructure, typically constituting a city or town

Examples

An urban area
Public transportation is usually more accessible in an urban area than in a rural one.
The city council plans to expand green spaces throughout the entire urban area.
The majority of the population now lives in an urban area rather than in the countryside.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rural area - a region outside towns and cities, with low population density
