western red cedar

noun
reddish-brown paint or stain
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈwɛs.tərn rɛd ˈsiː.dər/ /; / /ˈhwɛs.tərn rɛd ˈsiː.dər/ (AmE), /ˈwɛs.tən rɛd ˈsiː.də/ (BrE)
a paint or wood-stain color named after the Western red cedar tree, denoting a warm reddish-brown tone used in interior design and finishes.

Examples

Western red cedar color

The cabinet maker recommended a western red cedar finish to bring out the wood grain.
