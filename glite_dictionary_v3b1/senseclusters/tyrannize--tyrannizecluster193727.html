tyrannize

verb
exercise cruel or oppressive power
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪrəˌnaɪz /; / tɪˈrænəˌzaɪz / (AmE), / ˈtɪrənaɪz /; / tɪˈranʌɪz /; / ˈtɪərənaɪz / (BrE)
to rule or treat people in a cruel and oppressive way, like a tyrant
