reprogram

verb
to reallocate funds
4.7
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈproʊ.græm/ /; / /riː.prə.ˈgræm/ (AmE), /riː.ˈprəʊ.græm/ /; / /riː.prə.ˈgræm/ (BrE)
to change the planned use or allocation of money or resources, especially within a budget or organization

Examples

To reprogram the budget
The city council voted to reprogram funds from road repairs to public health initiatives.
After the disaster, the organization had to reprogram its budget to address urgent needs.
The department will reprogram some of its resources to support the new project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reallocate - to assign money or resources to a different purpose
  • synonym: redirect - to send something to a different place or purpose
