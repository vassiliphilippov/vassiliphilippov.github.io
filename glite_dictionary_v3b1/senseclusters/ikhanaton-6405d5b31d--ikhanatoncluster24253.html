Ikhanaton

proper noun
Egyptian pharaoh who promoted sun worship
Pronunciation: / iːˈkɑːnəˌtɒn / (AmE), / iːˈkænətɒn / (BrE)
an early ruler of Egypt who rejected the traditional gods and introduced the worship of the sun disc Aten; died in 1358 BC

Examples

Ikhanaton the pharaoh
Ikhanaton is remembered for his attempt to shift Egyptian religion to the worship of a single deity, Aten.
Artifacts from the reign of Ikhanaton often depict the sun disc prominently.
Many historians debate whether Ikhanaton's reforms had a lasting impact on Egyptian society.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Akhenaten - Egyptian pharaoh known for promoting the worship of Aten
