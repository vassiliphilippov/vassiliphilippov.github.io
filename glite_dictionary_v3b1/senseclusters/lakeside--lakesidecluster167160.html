lakeside

noun
the land beside a lake
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪk.saɪd / (AmE), / ˈleɪk.saɪd / (BrE)
the land along the edge of a lake

Examples

The lakeside view
The hotel offers stunning lakeside views from every room.
They built a small cabin right on the lakeside for weekend getaways.
We enjoyed a picnic at the lakeside as the sun set behind the hills.
