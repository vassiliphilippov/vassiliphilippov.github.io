Etna

proper noun
an active volcano in Sicily, Italy
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɛtnə / (AmE), / ˈɛtnə / (BrE)
a large, active volcano on the east coast of Sicily, Italy; the highest and one of the most active volcanoes in Europe

Examples

Mount Etna
Mount Etna is famous for its frequent eruptions and is a popular site for tourists.
Scientists monitor Etna closely to study volcanic activity and predict eruptions.
The fertile soil around Etna supports many vineyards and orchards.
