unrepresentativeness

noun
lack of accurate representation
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪvnəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of not accurately reflecting or representing a group, population, or whole.

Examples

The unrepresentativeness of sample
The unrepresentativeness of the sample led to misleading conclusions in the research study.
Critics pointed out the unrepresentativeness of the panel, which did not include younger voices.
Because of its unrepresentativeness, the poll failed to capture the opinions of rural communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bias - a tendency to favor one side or perspective, leading to unfair results
  • antonym: representativeness - the quality of accurately reflecting a group or whole
