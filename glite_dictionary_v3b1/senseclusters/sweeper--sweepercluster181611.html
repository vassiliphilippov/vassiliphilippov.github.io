sweeper

noun
cleaning device with revolving brushes
4.4 1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈswipər / (AmE), / ˈswiːpə / (BrE)
a cleaning device equipped with revolving brushes, used to pick up dirt and debris from carpets or floors as it is pushed.

Examples

Carpet sweeper
Before vacuum cleaners became common, households relied on a sturdy sweeper for daily cleaning.
She pushed the sweeper over the carpet to pick up crumbs after dinner.
The hotel uses a mechanical sweeper in the hallways to keep the floors clean.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vacuum cleaner - an electrical cleaning device that uses suction to remove dirt from floors and carpets
