boss around

verb
to act bossy or domineering towards someone
1.0 3.7
Pronunciation: /bɔːs ə.ˈraʊnd/ /; / /bɔːs ə.ˈraʊn/ (AmE), /bɒs ə.ˈraʊnd/ (BrE)
