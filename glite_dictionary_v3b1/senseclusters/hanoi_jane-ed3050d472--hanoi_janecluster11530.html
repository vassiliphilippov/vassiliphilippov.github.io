Hanoi Jane

noun
derogatory nickname for Jane Fonda linked to anti-war activism
Pronunciation: / həˈnɔɪ ˈdʒeɪn / (AmE), / həˈnɔɪ ˈdʒeɪn / (BrE)
a disparaging nickname for Jane Fonda, especially referencing her anti-Vietnam War activism and visit to Hanoi during the Vietnam War

Examples

Hanoi Jane nickname
Many veterans still resent the nickname Hanoi Jane when discussing Jane Fonda's actions during the Vietnam War.
Some view the label Hanoi Jane as an unfair characterization of her anti-war protest.
The photo that led to her being called Hanoi Jane remains a point of controversy.
