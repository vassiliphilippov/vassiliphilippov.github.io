noma

noun
severe ulcerative disease of the mouth or genitals
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊmə / (AmE), / ˈnəʊmə / (BrE)
A severe gangrenous disease causing ulceration of the mucous membranes of the mouth or genitals, most commonly affecting malnourished children.

Examples

Severe noma disease
Malnutrition and poor oral hygiene can contribute to the development of noma in young children.
Medical teams in remote regions work to prevent noma by improving nutrition and sanitation.
The child was diagnosed with noma after presenting with severe mouth ulcers and tissue destruction.
