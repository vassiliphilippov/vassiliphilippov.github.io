multicore

noun
cable with multiple wires
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌkɔr / (AmE), / ˈmʌltɪˌkɔː / (BrE)
a cable containing several insulated wires or conductors within a single outer covering, used for audio or electrical connections

Examples

A multicore for speakers
The technician connected the speakers using a multicore to simplify the setup.
A multicore is often used in recording studios to organize multiple audio signals.
He labeled each wire in the multicore to avoid confusion during installation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cable - a thick wire or group of wires covered in plastic, used to carry electricity or electronic signals
  • antonym: single-core - a cable with only one conductor inside
