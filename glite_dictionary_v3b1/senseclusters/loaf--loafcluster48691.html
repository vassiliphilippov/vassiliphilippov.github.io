loaf

noun
shaped mass of food (not only bread)
3.3
Pronunciation: / loʊf / (AmE), / ləʊf / (BrE)
a shaped mass of food, especially bread, that is baked or formed into a block

Examples

A loaf of bread
He prepared a delicious meat loaf for dinner last night.
She molded the cheese into a large loaf before aging it.
The bakery sold a beautifully shaped sugar loaf at the holiday market.
Please slice the loaf before serving it at the table.
She bought a fresh loaf of whole wheat bread from the bakery.
The aroma of a baking loaf filled the entire kitchen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: roll - a small bread portion shaped and baked separately
