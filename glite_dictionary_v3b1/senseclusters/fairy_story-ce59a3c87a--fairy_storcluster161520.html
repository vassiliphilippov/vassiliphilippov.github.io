fairy story

noun
children's story about fairies
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛri ˌstɔri / (AmE), / ˈfeəri ˌstɔːri / (BrE)
A story involving fairies and magical beings, typically told to entertain children.

Examples

A classic fairy story
As a child, she begged her grandmother to tell her a fairy story every night.
He dismissed the rumors as nothing but an old fairy story.
Many fairy stories feature magical forests and enchanted castles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fairy tale - a story involving magical beings or fantastical events, often for children
