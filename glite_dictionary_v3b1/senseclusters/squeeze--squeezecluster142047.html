squeeze

verb
fit into a small space with difficulty
2.8 12/m
Pronunciation: / skwiz / (AmE), / skwiÀêz / (BrE)
to force oneself or something into a small or tight space, often with difficulty

Examples

To squeeze through a gap
He squeezed through the narrow opening in the fence.
I managed to squeeze my suitcase under the bed.
She tried to squeeze into the last available seat on the crowded bus.
