Synonyms

  • unforeseeable
    6.7 0.39/m
    unforeseeable is used for events that cannot be predicted, while unpredictable covers things that change or happen without warning.
  • erratic
    6.1 4.71/m
    erratic describes behavior that is wildly changeable and inconsistent, while unpredictable simply means not able to be known in advance.
  • volatile
    7.0 4.45/m
    volatile means likely to change suddenly and possibly become dangerous, while unpredictable is a more neutral word for sudden change.
  • irregular
    3.5 2.49/m
    irregular describes things that do not follow a pattern or schedule, while unpredictable means you cannot guess what will happen.
