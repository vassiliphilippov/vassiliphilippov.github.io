boiled dinner

noun
corned beef and vegetables simmered together
Pronunciation: / bɔɪld ˈdɪnər / (AmE), / bɔɪld ˈdɪnə / (BrE)
A meal consisting of corned beef simmered with onions, cabbage, and typically other vegetables such as potatoes and carrots.

Examples

A boiled dinner meal
Every year on St. Patrick's Day, my grandmother makes a traditional boiled dinner for the whole family.
Leftover boiled dinner can be used to make a hearty soup the next day.
The restaurant's specialty is boiled dinner served with homemade bread and mustard.
