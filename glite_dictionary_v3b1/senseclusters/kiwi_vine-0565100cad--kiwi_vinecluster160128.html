kiwi vine

noun
climbing plant grown for kiwi fruit
4.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààkiwi va…™n / (AmE), / ÀàkiÀêwi va…™n / (BrE)
A climbing plant native to China, cultivated especially in New Zealand for its edible, fuzzy-skinned fruit with green flesh.

Examples
