kiwi vine

noun
climbing plant grown for kiwi fruit
3.8 4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkiː.wiː vaɪn/ /; / /ˈkiː.wɪ vaɪn/ (AmE), /ˈkiː.wiː vaɪn/ /; / /ˈkiː.wɪ vaɪn/ (BrE)
A climbing plant native to China, cultivated especially in New Zealand for its edible, fuzzy-skinned fruit with green flesh.

Examples

A kiwi vine plant

Many orchards in New Zealand are filled with rows of kiwi vines.
The kiwi vine requires a strong trellis to support its vigorous growth.
