Helvetian

noun
native or inhabitant of ancient Switzerland
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɛlˈviːʃən / (AmE), / hɛlˈviːʃən / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of ancient Switzerland, especially of the Celtic Helvetii tribe.

Examples

The Helvetians tribe
Julius Caesar's writings mention the migration of the Helvetians into Gaul.
She was fascinated by the customs of the early Helvetian people.
The museum displayed ancient artifacts belonging to a proud Helvetian.
