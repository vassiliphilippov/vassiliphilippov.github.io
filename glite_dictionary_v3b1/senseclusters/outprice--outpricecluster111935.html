outprice

verb
to cost more
6.8
Pronunciation: /aʊt.ˈpraɪs/ /; / /ˈaʊt.praɪs/ (AmE), /aʊt.ˈpraɪs/ /; / /ˈaʊt.praɪs/ (BrE)
to set a price higher than another item or competitor, often making it less attractive or unaffordable

Examples
