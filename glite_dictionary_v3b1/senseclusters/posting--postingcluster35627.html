posting

noun
public notice or advertisement displayed
1.0 1.8
Pronunciation: /ˈpoʊ.stɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈpəʊ.stɪŋ/ (BrE)
a message, notice, or item shared publicly, either on a physical board or online (such as a social media post or forum message)

Examples
