hotspot

noun
place of political unrest or potential violence
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑtˌspɑt / (AmE), / ˈhɒtˌspɒt / (BrE)
a place where political unrest or potential violence is particularly likely to occur

Examples

A conflict hotspot
Journalists are often sent to hotspots around the world to report on emerging crises.
Several countries in the region have become hotspots for political conflict in recent years.
The government is monitoring potential hotspots to prevent the outbreak of violence.
