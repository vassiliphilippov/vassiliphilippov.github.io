spike heel

noun
very high, narrow heel on a woman's shoe
Pronunciation: / spaɪk hiːl / (AmE), / spaɪk hiːl / (BrE)
a very high, thin heel on a woman's shoe, tapering to a small point at the bottom

Examples

A spike heel shoe
Her new boots had a sharp spike heel that made her appear even taller.
She found it difficult to walk quickly while wearing shoes with a spike heel.
The spike heel broke off when she stepped on an uneven pavement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stiletto heel - a very thin, high heel on women's shoes
