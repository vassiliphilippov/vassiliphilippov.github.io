symmetrical

adjective
having symmetry; evenly balanced
4.3
Pronunciation: / sɪˈmɛtrɪkəl / (AmE), / sɪˈmɛtrɪkəl / (BrE)
having parts that are the same or match on both sides; showing symmetry

Examples

Symmetrical patterns
Butterflies often have symmetrical patterns on their wings.
Her face was remarkably symmetrical, making her features very balanced.
The artist admired the symmetrical design of the building.
Her face is remarkably symmetrical, making it appealing to artists and photographers.
The architect designed a symmetrical building with identical wings on each side.
The pattern on the rug was perfectly symmetrical around the center point.

Related Terms

  • antonym: asymmetrical - not having symmetry; lacking balanced proportions
  • synonym: balanced - having equal distribution of weight or elements
  • synonym: even - being equal or uniform on both sides
