cassata

noun
Italian layered dessert
8.2
Pronunciation: / kəˈsɑtə / (AmE), / kəˈsɑːtə / (BrE)
an Italian dessert made of sponge cake layered with sweetened ricotta cheese and candied fruit

Examples

Italian cassata dessert

The bakery is famous for its traditional Sicilian cassata filled with candied fruit.
She learned to make cassata from her grandmother's old recipe book.
