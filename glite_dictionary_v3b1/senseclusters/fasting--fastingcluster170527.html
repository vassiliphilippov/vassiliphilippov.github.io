fasting

noun
abstaining from food
2.0 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈfæ.stɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈfɑː.stɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act or practice of abstaining from food, especially for a set period of time or for religious or medical reasons

Examples

Fasting during Ramadan
During Ramadan, many Muslims observe fasting from sunrise to sunset.
Some people practice intermittent fasting to improve their health.
Strict fasting before medical tests is often required.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abstinence - the practice of abstaining from some or all kinds of food or drink
  • antonym: feasting - the act of eating and drinking sumptuously
