warmth of the hearth

noun
comforting heat from a fireplace
9.9
Pronunciation: / wɔrmθ əv ðə hɑrθ / (AmE), / wɔːmθ əv ðə hɑːθ / (BrE)
The comforting heat and sense of coziness provided by a fireplace, often associated with feelings of home and security.

Examples

The warmth of the hearth
After a long walk in the snow, she welcomed the warmth of the hearth.
Gathering around the warmth of the hearth became a cherished family tradition.
The warmth of the hearth made the long winter nights much more bearable.
