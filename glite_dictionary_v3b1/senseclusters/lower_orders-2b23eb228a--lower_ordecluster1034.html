lower orders

noun
the lower social or economic classes
7.0 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / Ààlo ä.…ö Àå…îr.d…öz / (AmE), / Ààl…ô ä.…ô Àå…îÀê.d…ôz / (BrE)
the people belonging to the lower social or economic classes in a society

Examples

The lower orders
The government's reforms brought some improvements for the lower orders but left the wealthy untouched.
There was resentment among the lower orders towards those who held power and privilege.
Victorian novels often depict the struggles faced by the lower orders in urban society.
