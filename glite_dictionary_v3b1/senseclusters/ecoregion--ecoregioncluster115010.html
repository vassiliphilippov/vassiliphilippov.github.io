ecoregion

noun
distinct ecological area
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈiːkoʊˌriːdʒən / (AmE), / ˈiːkəʊˌriːdʒən / (BrE)
a large area of land or water defined by its particular climate, plants, and animals
