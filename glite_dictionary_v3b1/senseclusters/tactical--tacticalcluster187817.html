tactical

adjective
relating to tactics or strategy
Pronunciation: / ˈtæk.tɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˈtæk.tɪ.kəl / (BrE)
connected with tactics; done or planned in order to achieve a particular aim or advantage

Examples

A tactical change
Making a tactical change to the lineup improved the team's performance.
She was praised for her tactical awareness during the negotiation.
The general made a tactical decision to retreat and regroup.
