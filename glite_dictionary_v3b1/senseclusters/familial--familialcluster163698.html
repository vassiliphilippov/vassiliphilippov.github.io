familial

adjective
occurring in or affecting a family, often by heredity
7.8 4.8
Pronunciation: /fə.ˈmɪl.jəl/ /; / /fə.ˈmɪl.ɪ.əl/ (AmE), /fə.ˈmɪl.jəl/ /; / /fə.ˈmɪl.ɪ.əl/ (BrE)
relating to or typical of a family, especially because of heredity or shared traits

Examples

Certain familial traits
Certain familial traits, such as eye color, are determined genetically.
The doctor asked about any familial history of heart disease.
There is a strong familial tendency for high blood pressure in their family.
Researchers are interested in the familial factors that may influence the development of certain diseases.
The study focused on familial patterns in the transmission of genetic disorders.
They observed a strong familial resemblance among all the siblings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: family - a group consisting of parents and children
  • synonym: hereditary - (of a characteristic or disease) passed from parent to offspring through genes
  • antonym: sporadic - (of a disease or occurrence) appearing only occasionally or in isolated instances
