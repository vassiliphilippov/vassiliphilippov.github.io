comebacker

noun
baseball ball hit to pitcher
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌmˌbækər / (AmE), / ˈkʌmˌbækə / (BrE)
a ball in baseball that is hit directly back toward the pitcher by the batter

Examples

A fast comebacker
The pitcher quickly reacted and caught the comebacker before it could hit the ground.
A hard comebacker can be dangerous if the pitcher is not paying attention.
He was injured last season when a comebacker struck him on the leg.

Related Terms

  • synonym: line drive - a ball hit hard and straight in the air
