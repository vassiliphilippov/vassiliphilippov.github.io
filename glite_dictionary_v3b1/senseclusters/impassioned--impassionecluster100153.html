impassioned

adjective
full of intense emotion
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpæʃənd / (AmE), / ɪmˈpæʃənd / (BrE)
showing or expressing strong feelings or intense emotion.

Examples

An impassioned speech
He wrote an impassioned letter to the editor about climate change.
Her impassioned speech moved the audience to tears.
The lawyer delivered an impassioned plea for justice.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dispassionate - not influenced by strong emotion; impartial
  • synonym: fervent - having or showing very strong and sincere feelings
  • synonym: passionate - having or showing strong feelings or emotions
