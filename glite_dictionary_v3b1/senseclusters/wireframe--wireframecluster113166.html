wireframe

noun
3D line model
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈwaɪ.ər.freɪm/ /; / /ˈwaɪr.freɪm/ (AmE), /ˈwaɪ.ə.freɪm/ /; / /ˈwaɪ.freɪm/ (BrE)
a visual model of a three-dimensional object using only lines and vertices to show its basic structure, especially in computer graphics

Examples

3D wireframe model
The designer created a wireframe of the car before adding any colors or textures.
By examining the wireframe, the team could easily spot errors in the 3D model's structure.
A simple wireframe helps visualize the basic shape of an object during the early stages of design.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blueprint - a detailed technical drawing or plan
  • antonym: rendering - a fully detailed image or model in computer graphics
