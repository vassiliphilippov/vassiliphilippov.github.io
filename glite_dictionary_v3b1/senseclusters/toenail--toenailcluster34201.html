toenail

verb
to nail at an angle
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊˌneɪl / (AmE), / ˈtəʊˌneɪl / (BrE)
to fasten two pieces of wood together by driving a nail at an angle through one piece into the other.

Examples

To toenail a board
The carpenter began to toenail the joists to secure the frame.
You should toenail the boards for extra stability in the structure.
He quickly toenailed the support beam into place before moving on.
