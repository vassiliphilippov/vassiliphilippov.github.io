bravo

noun
cry of approval or praise
5.3 3.97/m
Pronunciation: / Ààbr…ëÀêvo ä / (AmE), / Ààbr…ëÀêv…ô ä / (BrE)
A shout of approval or praise, especially from an audience at the end of a performance.

Examples

A loud bravo

She smiled as the hall echoed with cheers and bravo for her performance.
The audience erupted in bravo after the singer finished the final note.
