otologist

noun
ear specialist doctor
5.5
Pronunciation: / oʊˈtɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / əʊˈtɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
A physician who specializes in diagnosing and treating diseases of the ear.

Examples

An otologist treats ears
After several failed treatments, he was finally referred to an experienced otologist.
Many patients seek help from an otologist for persistent hearing problems.
The otologist recommended surgery to treat her chronic ear infections.

Related Terms

  • synonym: audiologist - a specialist in hearing and balance issues
  • synonym: ear specialist - a doctor specializing in ear problems
  • degree lower: ophthalmologist - a doctor specializing in eye and vision care
