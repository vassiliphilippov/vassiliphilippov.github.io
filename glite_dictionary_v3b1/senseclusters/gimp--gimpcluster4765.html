gimp

noun
decorative sewing trim
Pronunciation: / ɡɪmp / (AmE), / ɡɪmp / (BrE)
a narrow, decorative trim or braid used in sewing or upholstery, often for edging or embellishment

Examples

Decorative gimp trim
The dressmaker added a silver gimp to the hem of the evening gown.
Antique chairs are often finished with decorative gimp along the edges of the upholstery.
She chose a red gimp to accent the curtains in her living room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braid - a narrow band of fabric used for trimming or decoration
  • synonym: piping - a narrow tube of fabric used as a decorative edge
