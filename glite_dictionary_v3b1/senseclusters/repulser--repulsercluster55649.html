repulser

noun
one who repels
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpʌlsər / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌlsə / (BrE)
a person or thing that drives others away or causes a feeling of repulsion

Examples

Insect repulser
The chemical spray acted as a repulser to keep insects away from the crops.
His rude comments made him a repulser in social situations.
The loud alarm served as a repulser for potential intruders.

Related Terms

  • antonym: attractor - something that draws others in
  • synonym: repellent - something that drives away or keeps something off
