remineralization

noun
restoring minerals
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌmɪnərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌmɪnərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of restoring minerals to a substance, especially to tooth enamel or soil

Examples

Tooth remineralization
Dentists recommend fluoride toothpaste to support the remineralization of weakened tooth enamel.
The remineralization of soil is important for healthy plant growth in agriculture.
After acid exposure, saliva helps with the natural remineralization of teeth.

Related Terms
