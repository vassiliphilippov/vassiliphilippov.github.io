or thereabout

adverb
approximately at that time or place
Pronunciation: / ɔr ˈðɛr.əˌbaʊt / (AmE), / ɔː ˈðeərəˌbaʊt / (BrE)
approximately at that time, place, or number; about that point.

Examples

Arrive at 8 p.m. or thereabout
He’s due to arrive at 8 p.m. or thereabout, but it might be a little later.
The repairs will cost $500 or thereabout, depending on the parts needed.
The village was founded in the year 1200 or thereabout, according to historical records.

Related Terms

  • synonym: about - approximately
  • antonym: exactly - in a precise manner, not approximately
  • synonym: or thereabouts - approximately that time, place, or number (variant form)
