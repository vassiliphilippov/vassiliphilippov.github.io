abnormality

noun
departure from normal behavior or rules
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˌæbˌnɔrˈmæləti / (AmE), / ˌæb.nɔːˈmæl.ə.ti / (BrE)
Behavior or quality that departs from what is considered normal, especially by breaching rules, etiquette, customs, or morality.

Examples

Social abnormality
His disregard for social norms was seen as an abnormality within his conservative community.
Such abnormality in her behavior surprised everyone at the formal dinner.
The court ruled that repeated abnormality in conduct was a violation of the school's code.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anomaly - something that deviates from what is standard or expected
  • antonym: normalcy - state of being normal
