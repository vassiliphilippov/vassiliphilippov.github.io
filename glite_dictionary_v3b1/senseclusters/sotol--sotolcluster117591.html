sotol

noun
desert plant species
Pronunciation: / soʊˈtoʊl / (AmE), / səʊˈtɒl / (BrE)
a perennial desert plant of the genus Dasylirion, with stiff, spiky leaves, native to northern Mexico and the southwestern United States
