crewless

adjective
without a crew
Pronunciation: / ˈkruːləs / (AmE), / ˈkruːləs / (BrE)
without any people operating or working on board a vehicle, vessel, or aircraft

Examples

A crewless ship
The crewless ship sailed across the ocean using advanced navigation systems.
A crewless aircraft can perform surveillance missions without risking human lives.
Scientists launched a crewless submarine to explore the deep sea.

Related Terms

  • antonym: manned - operated by people on board
  • synonym: unmanned - operated without people on board
