neorealist

adjective
realistic artistic style
Pronunciation: / ˌniːoʊˈriːəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌniːəʊˈrɪəlɪst / (BrE)
relating to neorealism, a style in art, film, or literature that focuses on realistic, everyday life and social issues.

Examples

Neorealist film style
The director's neorealist approach gave the movie a raw and authentic feeling.
Many critics praised the neorealist elements in the novel for their honest depiction of poverty.
Her paintings, influenced by neorealist ideas, show ordinary people in everyday situations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: realist - relating to realism, focusing on accurate representation of life
  • antonym: romantic - relating to a style emphasizing emotion and imagination over realism
