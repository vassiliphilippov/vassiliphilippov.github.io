suppressor

noun
noise-reducing device
Pronunciation: / səˈprɛsər / (AmE), / səˈprɛsə / (BrE)
a device that reduces noise or controls unwanted effects, especially the sound of a firearm being fired

Examples

Gun suppressor attachment
The technician installed a suppressor on the rifle to reduce its noise during testing.
Many countries have strict regulations on owning a suppressor for firearms.
The suppressor helped make the machine operate more quietly in the laboratory.

Related Terms

  • antonym: amplifier - a device that increases the strength of signals or sounds
  • synonym: silencer - a device attached to a firearm to reduce noise
