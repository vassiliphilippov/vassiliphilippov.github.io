spile

noun
plug or peg for closing a barrel hole
9.9 0.01/m

A small plug or wooden peg used to close a hole in a barrel, cask, or flask.

Examples

Barrel spile
A wooden spile was removed to allow the liquid to be poured out.
The cooper inserted a spile to seal the cask after filling it with wine.
Traditional barrels often have a spile to regulate the flow of contents.
