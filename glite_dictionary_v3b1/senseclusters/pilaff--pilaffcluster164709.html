pilaff

noun
seasoned rice dish, often with meat or seafood
Pronunciation: / pɪˈlɑf / (AmE), / pɪˈlæf / (BrE)
A dish of rice cooked in a seasoned broth, often with onions, vegetables, and sometimes meat or seafood.

Examples

Seafood pilaff
For dinner, she prepared a seafood pilaff with shrimp and mussels.
The cookbook includes several recipes for vegetable pilaff from different regions.
We ordered a delicious pilaff with lamb and aromatic spices at the restaurant.
