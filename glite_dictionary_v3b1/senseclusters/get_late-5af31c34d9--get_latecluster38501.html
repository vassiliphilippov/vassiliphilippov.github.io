get late

verb
approach a late hour; become late
2.0
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt leɪt / (AmE), / ɡɛt leɪt / (BrE)
to reach or approach a late hour; to become late in the day or night

Examples

It is getting late
I realized it was getting late, so I decided to head home.
It's getting late; we should probably finish this up soon.
Whenever it gets late, the streets in this area become very quiet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: be late - to be after the expected or usual time
  • synonym: run late - to be delayed or behind schedule
