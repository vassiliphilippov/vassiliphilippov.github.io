decal

noun
digital texture or sticker in graphics
5.7
Pronunciation: / \u02c8di\u02d0\u02cck\u00e6l / (AmE), / \u02c8di\u02d0k\u00e6l / (BrE)
(in computer graphics, video games, and 3D modelling) a digital image or texture applied to a surface to add markings, labels, or visual detail without altering the underlying geometry.

Examples

Game decal texture
The game's level designers added weather stains and graffiti decals to make the city feel lived-in.
To save memory, the artist painted detail with layered decals instead of increasing model polygon counts.
She applied a bright neon decal to the ship's hull texture during the render pass.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sticker - a small image or label applied to a surface (also used for digital decorations)
  • synonym: texture - an image used in 3D graphics to add surface detail or color
