underdeveloped

adjective
lacking economic and industrial development
2.4 4.1
Pronunciation: /ʌn.dər.dɪ.ˈvel.əpt/ (AmE), /ʌn.də.dɪ.ˈvel.əpt/ (BrE)
(of a country or region) having a low level of industrialization, infrastructure, and economic development, often due to a lack of capital investment.

Examples

Underdeveloped country
Educational opportunities are limited in underdeveloped communities.
Infrastructure in many underdeveloped regions is inadequate for growth.
The country remains underdeveloped despite efforts to attract foreign investment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: developed - having advanced industrialization and infrastructure
  • degree higher: industrialized - having a highly developed industry and economy
