Ozarks

proper noun
mountainous region in the southern United States
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊˌzɑrks / (AmE), / ˈəʊzɑːks / (BrE)
a region of low, forested mountains in northwestern Arkansas, southeastern Missouri, and northeastern Oklahoma, known for its rugged terrain and natural beauty

Examples

The Ozarks region
Several towns in the Ozarks are known for their folk music festivals.
The natural springs in the Ozarks attract tourists year-round.
We spent our summer vacation hiking and fishing in the Ozarks.
