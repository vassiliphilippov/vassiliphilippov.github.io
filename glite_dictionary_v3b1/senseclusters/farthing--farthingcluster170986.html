farthing

noun
former British coin, quarter of a penny
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑrðɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɑːðɪŋ / (BrE)
a former British coin worth a quarter of a penny; also used to mean a very small amount of money
