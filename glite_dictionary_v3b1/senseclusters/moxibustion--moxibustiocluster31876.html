moxibustion

noun
herbal heat therapy
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑksɪˈbʌs.tʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɒksɪˈbʌs.tʃən / (BrE)
a traditional therapy in which dried herbs are burned near the skin to promote healing or relieve pain

Examples

Moxibustion therapy
Many people use moxibustion as a complementary treatment for chronic pain.
The practitioner demonstrated how moxibustion is performed using dried mugwort.
Some patients report feeling relaxed after a session of moxibustion therapy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acupuncture - a traditional therapy using needles inserted into the skin
  • synonym: massage - the practice of rubbing and kneading the body to relieve tension or pain
