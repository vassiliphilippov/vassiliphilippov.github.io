greenness

noun
environmental friendliness, sustainability
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrin.nəs / (AmE), / ˈɡriːn.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being environmentally friendly or sustainable; the degree to which something avoids harm to the environment.

Examples

Greenness of a company
The company's report highlights the greenness of its supply chain to appeal to investors.
Consumers now compare the greenness of products before deciding which brand to buy.
The city's new policy increased the greenness of urban development through green roofs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eco-friendliness - the property of causing little or no harm to the environment
  • antonym: pollution - the presence or introduction of harmful substances or products into the environment; opposite of environmental friendliness
  • synonym: sustainability - the practice of meeting present needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet theirs
