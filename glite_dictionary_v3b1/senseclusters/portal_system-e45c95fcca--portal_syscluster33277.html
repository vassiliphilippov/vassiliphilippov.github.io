portal system

noun
centralized web/intranet access platform
Pronunciation: / c8p54r.t2c59l c8s6as.t59m / (AmE), / c8p54d0.t59l c8s6as.t59m / (BrE)
a centralized software platform (web or intranet) that provides users with a single point of access to multiple online services, resources, and tools, often featuring a dashboard and single sign-on.

Examples

University portal system
The university implemented a portal system to provide one login for email, courses, and the library.
Employees access the company's HR forms and benefits information through the new portal system.
Before the semester, staff trained students to navigate the portal system and check their examination schedules.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enterprise portal - a corporate portal that centralizes access to company applications and information
  • synonym: web portal - an online gateway that aggregates links, tools, and services for users
