oversaturated

adjective
excessively full
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈsætʃəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈsætʃəreɪtɪd / (BrE)
containing or having more of something than can be absorbed, handled, or is considered normal

Examples

Oversaturated market
The market became oversaturated with similar products, making it hard for new brands to succeed.
Her painting looked unrealistic because the colors were oversaturated and too bright.
If the soil is oversaturated with water, the plants may not survive.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: saturated - containing as much of something as possible
  • antonym: underfilled - not filled to capacity
