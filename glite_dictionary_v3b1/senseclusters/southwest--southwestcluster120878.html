southwest

adjective
coming from the southwest
Pronunciation: / ˌsaʊθˈwɛst / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˈwɛst / (BrE)
(of wind, direction, or location) coming from, facing, or located in the southwest

Examples

Southwest wind
Clouds started moving in from the southwest during the afternoon.
The pilot adjusted the plane's course to compensate for the strong southwest breeze.
The storm was accompanied by southwest winds that reached 30 miles per hour.
A southwest breeze brought warm air into the valley.
She positioned her chair to face the southwest for the best light in the afternoons.
The house has a southwest view that overlooks the mountains.
