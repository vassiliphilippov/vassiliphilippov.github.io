submarining

noun
seat belt sliding
9.9
Pronunciation: /sʌb.mə.ˈriː.nɪŋ/ /; / /səb.mə.ˈriː.nɪŋ/ (AmE), /sʌb.mə.ˈriː.nɪŋ/ (BrE)
the forward sliding of a vehicle occupant under the lap part of a seat belt during a collision, increasing injury risk

Examples
