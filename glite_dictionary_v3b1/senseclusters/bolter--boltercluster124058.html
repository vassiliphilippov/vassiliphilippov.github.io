bolter

noun
one who runs away
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊltər / (AmE), / ˈbəʊltə / (BrE)
a person or animal that suddenly runs away or leaves unexpectedly, especially in a startling or uncontrolled manner

Examples

A horse bolter
The horse became a bolter after being startled by a loud noise during the race.
She was known as a bolter because she often left parties without saying goodbye.
When the gate opened, the dog acted like a bolter and dashed into the street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deserter - a person who abandons a place or group
  • synonym: runaway - a person or animal that escapes or leaves suddenly
  • antonym: stayer - a person or animal that remains in place
