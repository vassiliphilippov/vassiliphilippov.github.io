arrowleaf

noun
plant with arrow-shaped leaves
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈæroʊˌliːf / (AmE), / ˈærəʊˌliːf / (BrE)
a plant with leaves shaped like an arrowhead, commonly found in wetlands or near water

Examples

Arrowleaf plant
The pond was surrounded by arrowleaf and other aquatic plants.
We identified the arrowleaf by its distinctive pointed leaves growing near the water's edge.
Many birds nest among the thick clusters of arrowleaf in the marsh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrowhead - a wetland plant with arrow-shaped leaves
  • synonym: cattail - a tall wetland plant with long, flat leaves
