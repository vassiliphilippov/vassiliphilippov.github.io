unadulterated

adjective
complete; pure; not mixed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈdʌltəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈdʌltəˌreɪtɪd / (BrE)
complete and total; not diluted or mixed with anything else; used to emphasize the extent or purity of something

Examples

Unadulterated nonsense
His story was unadulterated nonsense from start to finish.
The investigation revealed the unadulterated truth about the scandal.
We only use unadulterated ingredients in our recipes to ensure the best quality.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adulterated - mixed with inferior or impure substances
  • synonym: pure - not mixed with anything else; free of contamination
