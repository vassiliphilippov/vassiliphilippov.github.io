religious text

noun
writing regarded as sacred in a religion
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlɪdʒəs tɛkst / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəs tɛkst / (BrE)
a book or other piece of writing that is considered sacred and venerated in a religion

Examples

A religious text book
Many people turn to their religious texts for spiritual guidance and inspiration.
Scholars study religious texts to understand ancient beliefs and practices.
The Bible is a well-known religious text in Christianity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scripture - the sacred writings of a religion
  • antonym: secular text - a writing not connected with religious matters
