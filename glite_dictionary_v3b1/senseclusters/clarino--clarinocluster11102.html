clarino

noun
synthetic leather material
9.6
Pronunciation: /klə.ˈriː.noʊ/ (AmE), /klə.ˈriː.nəʊ/ (BrE)
a trademarked synthetic microfiber material used as a durable substitute for natural leather in products like shoes and upholstery

Examples

Clarino sports gloves
Many sports gloves are made from clarino because it is lightweight and durable.
The sofa was upholstered in clarino to provide a leather-like look without using animal products.
She prefers clarino shoes for running since they are easy to clean and long-lasting.
