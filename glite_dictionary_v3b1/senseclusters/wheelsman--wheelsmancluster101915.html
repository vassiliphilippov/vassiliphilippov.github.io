wheelsman

noun
criminal getaway driver
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːlzmən / (AmE), / ˈwiːlzmən / (BrE)
a person who drives a vehicle for criminals, especially as the getaway driver after a crime

Examples

Getaway wheelsman
The wheelsman waited in the car while the others robbed the store.
Police identified the wheelsman from security camera footage after the heist.
During the trial, the wheelsman admitted to driving the getaway car for the gang.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accomplice - a person who helps another commit a crime
  • synonym: getaway driver - a person who drives a vehicle to help criminals escape after a crime
  • antonym: police officer - a person who enforces the law and prevents crime
