slightness

noun
lack of importance; triviality
Pronunciation: / ˈslaɪtnəs / (AmE), / ˈslaɪtnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being unimportant or insignificant; triviality

Examples

The slightness of the problem
His criticism had a certain slightness that made it easy to ignore.
She dismissed the comment with a wave of her hand, annoyed by its slightness.
The slightness of the issue made everyone wonder why it caused such a fuss.

Related Terms

  • antonym: significance - the quality of being worthy of attention; importance
  • synonym: triviality - the quality or state of being trivial; lack of seriousness or importance
