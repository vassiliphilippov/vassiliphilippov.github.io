palatine artery

noun
artery supplying the palate
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpæləˌtaɪn ˈɑːrtəri / (AmE), / ˈpæləˌtaɪn ˈɑːtəri / (BrE)
an artery supplying blood to the palate and neighboring areas of the mouth and face

Examples

Palatine artery supply
Damage to the palatine artery can cause significant bleeding during dental procedures.
Infections in the palate region may involve branches of the palatine artery.
The surgeon identified the palatine artery before making the incision in the patient's mouth.
