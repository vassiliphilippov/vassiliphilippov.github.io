catchfly

noun
flowering plant with sticky stems
Pronunciation: / ˈkætʃˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈkætʃˌflʌɪ /; / ˈkætʃ.flʌɪ / (BrE)
a flowering plant of the genus Silene, with sticky stems that can trap small insects
