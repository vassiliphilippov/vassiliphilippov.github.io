charlotte russe

noun
dessert of ladyfingers and Bavarian cream
Pronunciation: / ʃɑrˈloʊt ˈrus / (AmE), / ʃɑːˈlɒt ˈruːs / (BrE)
A dessert made of ladyfingers lined around a mold and filled with Bavarian cream.
