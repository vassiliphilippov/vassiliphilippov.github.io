make someone laugh

verb
cause someone to laugh or be amused
Pronunciation: / meɪk ˈsʌmwʌn læf / (AmE), / meɪk ˈsʌmwən lɑːf / (BrE)
to cause someone to feel amused or to laugh by saying or doing something funny.

Examples

To make someone laugh loudly
He loves to make someone laugh with his impressions of celebrities.
Her silly jokes never fail to make someone laugh during stressful meetings.
Telling funny stories can really make someone laugh and lift the mood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amuse - to entertain or make someone smile or laugh
  • antonym: make someone cry - to cause someone to shed tears, typically from sadness
