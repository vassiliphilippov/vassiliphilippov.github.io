inkless

adjective
without ink
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋkləs / (AmE), / ˈɪŋkləs / (BrE)
not containing or using ink

Examples

Inkless pen
He wrote his notes with an inkless pen during the exam.
The inkless printer uses heat to create images on special paper.
After years in storage, the marker was completely inkless and would not write.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inked - containing or marked with ink
