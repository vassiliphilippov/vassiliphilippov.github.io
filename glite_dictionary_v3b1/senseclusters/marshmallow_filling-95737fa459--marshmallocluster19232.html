marshmallow filling

noun
soft sweet filling made from marshmallows
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrʃˌmæloʊ ˈfɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːʃˌmæl.əʊ ˈfɪl.ɪŋ / (BrE)
a soft, sweet mixture made from marshmallows, used as a filling in desserts or confectionery

Examples

Sweet marshmallow filling
His favorite treat was a doughnut oozing with sweet marshmallow filling.
She spooned fluffy marshmallow filling onto the cookies before sandwiching them together.
The cake was layered with rich marshmallow filling between the chocolate sponges.
