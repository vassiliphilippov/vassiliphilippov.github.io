spike lavender oil

noun
essential oil from spike lavender
Pronunciation: / spaɪk ˈlævəndɚ ɔɪl / (AmE), / spaɪk ˈlavəndə ɔɪl / (BrE)
a pale yellow essential oil obtained from spike lavender, used for scenting soaps and cosmetics

Examples

A drop of spike lavender oil
She added a drop of spike lavender oil to her homemade soap for a calming fragrance.
Some artists use spike lavender oil as a natural solvent in oil painting.
Unlike regular lavender oil, spike lavender oil has a sharper scent and is often used in perfumery.
