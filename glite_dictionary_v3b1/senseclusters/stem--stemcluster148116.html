stem

verb
to turn skis inward (skiing term)
Pronunciation: / stɛm / (AmE), / stɛm / (BrE)
to turn or position the front ends of skis inward so that they form an angle, typically for slowing down or controlling speed

Examples

To stem the skis
Learning to stem is important for safety when skiing on steeper slopes.
The instructor taught the beginners how to stem their skis to control their speed on the slope.
You should stem your skis before making a sharp turn.
