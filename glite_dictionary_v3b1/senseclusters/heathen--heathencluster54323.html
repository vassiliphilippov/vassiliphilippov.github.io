heathen

noun
person not belonging to a major religion
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhiðən / (AmE), / ˈhiːð(ə)n / (BrE)
a person who does not belong to a widely held religion, especially someone who is not a Christian, Jew, or Muslim as regarded by those groups

Examples

A heathen tribe
During the Middle Ages, many European texts referred to non-Christians as heathen.
He accused his neighbors of acting like heathens because they never went to church.
The missionaries tried to convert the local heathen population to Christianity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: believer - a person who believes in a particular religion or set of religious ideas
  • synonym: infidel - a person who does not believe in a particular religion
