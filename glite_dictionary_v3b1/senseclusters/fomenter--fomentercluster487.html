fomenter

noun
person who stirs up trouble
Pronunciation: / foʊˈmɛntər / (AmE), / fəʊˈmɛntə / (BrE)
a person who instigates or stirs up trouble, especially political unrest or dissent

Examples

Fomenter of trouble
His speeches marked him as a fomenter of discord in the country.
Revolutionaries often view themselves as heroes, while authorities see them as fomenters of chaos.
The police arrested the fomenter of the violent demonstration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agitator - a person who urges others to protest or rebel
  • antonym: peacemaker - a person who brings about peace, especially by reconciling adversaries
