eternal patrol

noun
unceasing vigilance or watchfulness
9.6
Pronunciation: / ɪˈtɝːnəl pəˈtroʊl / (AmE), / ɪˈtɜːnəl pəˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
a state of constant or never-ending vigilance or watchfulness, often used to describe a duty that is unceasing or everlasting

Examples

On eternal patrol duty
Her dedication to her work made it seem like she was on an eternal patrol of the hospital corridors.
Some believe the fallen sailors are on eternal patrol beneath the waves forever.
The guards continued their eternal patrol throughout the long, silent night.
