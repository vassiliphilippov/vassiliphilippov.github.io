train

verb
teach a machine-learning model
4.2
Pronunciation: /treɪn/ (AmE), /treɪn/ (BrE)
(in computing and machine learning) to cause a model or algorithm to learn patterns from data by adjusting its parameters so it performs a task (for example, classification or prediction).

Examples

To train a model
Data scientists will train a neural network on millions of labeled images.
We trained the speech-recognition model overnight using hundreds of hours of audio.
The team is training the algorithm to recognize defects in production images.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fit - (in statistics/machine learning) to adjust a model to data
  • synonym: teach - to give knowledge or instruction; to cause to learn
