leggy

adjective
having long legs
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛɡi / (AmE), / ˈlɛɡi / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) having long legs in proportion to the rest of the body.

Examples

Leggy model
That breed of dog is famous for being particularly leggy, allowing it to run swiftly.
The leggy basketball player easily reached the ball above the others.
The model was described as leggy and elegant on the runway.
