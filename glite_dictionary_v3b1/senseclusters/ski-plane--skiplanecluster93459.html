ski-plane

noun
airplane equipped with skis for snow landings
3.0 5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈskiː.pleɪn/ (AmE), /ˈskiː.pleɪn/ /; / /ˈʃiː.pleɪn/ (BrE)
An airplane fitted with skis, enabling it to land and take off on snow or ice.

Examples

A ski-plane landing
During winter, the only access to the village is by ski-plane or snowmobile.
Our expedition was supplied with food and gear delivered by a ski-plane.
The scientists arrived at the remote Arctic research station by ski-plane.
