blech

noun
expression of disgust
Pronunciation: / blɛk / (AmE), / blɛk / (BrE)
an informal exclamation or sound expressing disgust or strong dislike

Examples

A loud blech
A sudden blech rose from the crowd when the band played the awful song.
He took one bite of the spoiled food and let out a loud blech.
Whenever she sees bugs in her salad, she can't help but say blech.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ugh - an exclamation expressing disgust or dislike
  • synonym: yuck - an informal exclamation of distaste or disgust
