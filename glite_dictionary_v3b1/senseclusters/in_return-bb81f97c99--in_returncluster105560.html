in return

adverb
as an exchange or reciprocation
3.0
Pronunciation: / ɪn ɹɪˈtɝn / (AmE), / ɪn ɹɪˈtɜːn / (BrE)
as an exchange or as a response to something given or done

Examples

In return for help
Businesses expect some benefits in return for their investments.
He offered advice and, in return, received gratitude from the team.
She helped me with my homework, and I baked cookies for her in return.
