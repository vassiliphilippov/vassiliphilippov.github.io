barrage balloon

noun
large tethered balloon used to block enemy aircraft
6.3 6.8
Pronunciation: /bə.ˈrɑːʒ bə.ˈluːn/ (AmE), /bə.ˈrɑːʒ bə.ˈluːn/ /; / /bæ.ˈrɑːʒ bə.ˈluːn/ (BrE)
