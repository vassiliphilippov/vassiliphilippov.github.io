bubble-like

adjective
isolated or sheltered
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌbəl laɪk / (AmE), / ˈbʌbəl laɪk / (BrE)
isolated or sheltered from outside reality, as if enclosed in a protective or insulated environment

Examples

Bubble-like world
The children grew up in a bubble-like environment, rarely interacting with people outside their community.
His bubble-like perspective made it difficult for him to understand the challenges others faced.
Many students at the elite school led bubble-like lives, unaware of the world beyond their campus.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exposed - open to outside influences or experiences
  • synonym: insulated - protected from outside influences
