beef plant

noun
South American ornamental plant with colorful foliage
8.8
Pronunciation: / biːf plænt / (AmE), / biːf plɑːnt / (BrE)
A South American plant with green to purple or red branches, ornamental foliage, and spikes of small woolly flowers with dry membranous bracts.
