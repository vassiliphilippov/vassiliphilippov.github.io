admeasurer

noun
person who measures shares
Pronunciation: / ædˈmɛʒərər / (AmE), / ədˈmɛʒərə / (BrE)
a person who measures, divides, or certifies the size or amount of something, especially land, goods, or property

Examples

A land admeasurer
The admeasurer carefully calculated each family's portion of the farmland.
An experienced admeasurer was hired to ensure the goods were divided fairly.
After the survey, the admeasurer marked the boundaries for each new property owner.
The admeasurer carefully recorded the ship's dimensions before issuing the certificate.
Before the property could be sold, an admeasurer was called to verify its boundaries.
Every vessel must be checked by an admeasurer to ensure it meets legal requirements.

  • synonym: inspector - a person who examines or checks something officially
  • synonym: surveyor - a person who measures land and property
