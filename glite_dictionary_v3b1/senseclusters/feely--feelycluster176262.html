feely

noun
sensory toy
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːli / (AmE), / ˈfiːli / (BrE)
a small object or toy designed to be touched, often with different textures, for sensory or tactile experience

Examples

A feely toy

Children with sensory needs often enjoy playing with a feely because of its unique textures.
She keeps a feely on her desk to squeeze when she feels stressed.
