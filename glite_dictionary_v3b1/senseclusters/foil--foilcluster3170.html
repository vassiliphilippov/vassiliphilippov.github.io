foil

noun
a contrasting person or thing that highlights another's qualities
5.5 4.7
Pronunciation: /fɔɪl/ (AmE), /fɔɪl/ (BrE)
a person or thing that contrasts with and so emphasizes and enhances the qualities of another.

Examples

A perfect foil
Her reserved personality made him an ideal foil for her outgoing nature.
In the painting, the dark background serves as a foil to the vibrant colors in the foreground.
The villain in the movie acted as a perfect foil to the charming hero.
