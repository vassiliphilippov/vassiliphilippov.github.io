achylia gastrica

noun
absence of gastric juices in the stomach
9.0
Pronunciation: / əˌkaɪliə ɡæsˈtrɪkə / (AmE), / əˌkɪliə ɡæsˈtrɪkə / (BrE)
A medical condition in which gastric juice is completely or partially absent from the stomach.

Examples

Achylia gastrica condition
Achylia gastrica can lead to difficulties in digesting food due to insufficient stomach juices.
Doctors monitor those with achylia gastrica for signs of malnutrition and related complications.
The patient was diagnosed with achylia gastrica after tests showed a lack of gastric secretions.
