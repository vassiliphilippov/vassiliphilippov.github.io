Synonyms

  • carry
    1.0 113/m
    carry means holding and moving something, often by hand or on your body, while take means moving or leading more generally.
  • convey
    3.5 1.68/m
    convey moves or carries something, often in a formal way, while take is more general and used in everyday speech.
  • transport
    1.4 18/m
    transport moves people or things by vehicle or as a business, while take is general and can be done by anyone.
  • transfer
    2.7 32/m
    transfer moves something between places, positions, or groups, often in a formal way, while take is simpler and more everyday.
  • lead
    1.0 86/m
    lead goes ahead and shows the way, while take often means to move or carry someone without guiding them.
  • guide
    1.0 22/m
    guide shows the way or directs a path, while take means to move or carry but does not imply instruction.
  • conduct
    4.3 0.67/m
    conduct leads or escorts people in a formal, careful way, while take is a general, less formal word for moving someone.

Related Terms
