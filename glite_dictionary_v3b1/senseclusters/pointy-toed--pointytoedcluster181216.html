pointy-toed

adjective
having a pointed toe (esp. shoes)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔɪnti toʊd / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪnti təʊd / (BrE)
(of a shoe) having a narrow, pointed toe.

Examples

Pointy-toed shoes
Many people find pointy-toed shoes uncomfortable for everyday wear.
She wore her favorite pointy-toed boots to the party last night.
The designer's new collection features several pointy-toed styles for both men and women.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pointed - having a sharp or tapered end
  • antonym: rounded-toed - having a rounded toe
