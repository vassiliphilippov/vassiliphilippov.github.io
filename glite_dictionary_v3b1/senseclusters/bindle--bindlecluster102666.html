bindle

noun
bundle of belongings
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪndəl /; / ˈbɪndl̩ / (AmE), / ˈbɪndəl /; / ˈbɪndl̩ / (BrE)
a bundle of belongings wrapped in cloth and carried, especially by a traveler or hobo

Examples

A hobo's bindle
The old man carried his bindle as he walked along the railroad tracks.
She packed a small bindle with food and clothes for her journey.
In classic stories, a hobo is often shown with a bindle slung over his shoulder.
The hobo slung his bindle over his shoulder and set off down the tracks.
Each traveler carried a small bindle containing their most important possessions.
She made a makeshift bindle to carry her clothes on the journey.
