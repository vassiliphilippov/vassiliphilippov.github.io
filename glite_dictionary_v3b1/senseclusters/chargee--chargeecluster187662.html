chargee

noun
person given responsibility
9.3
Pronunciation: / tʃɑːrˈdʒiː / (AmE), / tʃɑːˈdʒiː / (BrE)
a person who is given responsibility for someone or something, especially for care or supervision

Examples

A chargee for the class
The teacher appointed a chargee to oversee the class while she was away.
As the chargee of the project, he made sure every task was completed on time.
During the event, the museum named her the chargee of the children's activity area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: supervisor - a person who oversees or manages others
