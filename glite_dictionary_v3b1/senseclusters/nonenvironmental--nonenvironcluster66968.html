nonenvironmental

adjective
not about environment
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
not related to the environment or environmental issues

Examples

Nonenvironmental research
The grant was awarded for nonenvironmental research in the field of technology.
They decided to focus their efforts on nonenvironmental concerns such as education and healthcare.
Most of the budget was allocated to nonenvironmental projects this year.
