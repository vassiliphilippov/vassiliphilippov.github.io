penalty

noun
punishment in sports for rule violation
1.0 1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnəlti / (AmE), / ˈpɛnəlti / (BrE)
(in games and sports) a punishment or disadvantage imposed on a player or team for breaking the rules.

Examples

Penalty kick
A handball in the box often results in a penalty against the defending team.
She received a two-minute penalty for tripping an opponent in the hockey match.
The referee imposed a penalty on the team for delay of game.
