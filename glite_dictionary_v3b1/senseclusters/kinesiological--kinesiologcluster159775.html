kinesiological

adjective
relating to kinesiology
8.9
Pronunciation: / kɪˌnisiəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / kɪˌniːsiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to kinesiology, especially methods involving muscle testing for diagnosis or treatment.

Examples

Kinesiological muscle testing
The therapist used kinesiological techniques to assess the patient's muscle strength.
A kinesiological evaluation can help identify imbalances in the body.
She specializes in kinesiological approaches to physical rehabilitation.
