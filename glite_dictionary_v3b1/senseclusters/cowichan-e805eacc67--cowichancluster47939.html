Cowichan

noun
hand-knitted wool sweater
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊɪtʃən / (AmE), / ˈkaʊɪtʃən / (BrE)
a heavy, hand-knitted wool sweater with bold patterns, traditionally made by the Cowichan people of Vancouver Island

Examples

A warm Cowichan sweater
He wore a thick Cowichan to stay warm during the chilly autumn evening.
The traditional Cowichan features bold animal and geometric designs in natural wool colors.
She bought a handmade Cowichan as a souvenir from her trip to Vancouver Island.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cardigan - a knitted sweater that opens at the front
  • synonym: pullover - a sweater that is put on by pulling it over the head
