goofiness

noun
silly behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈɡuːfinəs / (AmE), / ˈɡuːfinəs / (BrE)
silly or playful behavior; the quality of being funny, lighthearted, or prone to making foolish mistakes

Despite his goofiness, he was very good at his job.
His goofiness always made his friends laugh, even during stressful times.
The actor's goofiness on stage brought a lighthearted mood to the play.
Despite her goofiness, she managed to complete the project successfully.
