singleness

noun
quality of devoted single-minded focus
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋɡəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of focusing with complete commitment on one aim or objective

Examples

Singleness of mind
He approached the challenge with remarkable singleness of mind, ignoring all distractions.
Her singleness of purpose inspired the whole team to strive for their goal.
The singleness with which she pursued justice impressed everyone around her.

Related Terms

  • synonym: single-mindedness - the quality of being determined to achieve a single aim
