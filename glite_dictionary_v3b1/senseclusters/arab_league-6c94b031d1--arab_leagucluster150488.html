Arab League

proper noun
international organization of Arab states
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɛˌræb liːɡ / (AmE), / ˈærəb liːɡ / (BrE)
an international organization of Arab states founded in 1945 to enhance political, economic, cultural, and social cooperation among its members

Examples
