telecommunication system

noun
system for distant electronic communication
Pronunciation: / tɛləˌkəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃən ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪkəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
a system that enables the transmission of information over long distances by electronic means, such as telephone, radio, or internet networks

Examples

Modern telecommunication system
Modern hospitals rely on an advanced telecommunication system to coordinate patient care and respond to emergencies.
The new telecommunication system allows employees to work remotely with high-speed internet and secure phone access.
Upgrading the national telecommunication system has improved connectivity across rural areas.

  • synonym: communication system - a system for sending and receiving information
