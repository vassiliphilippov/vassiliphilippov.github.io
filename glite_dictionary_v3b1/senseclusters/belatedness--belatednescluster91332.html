belatedness

noun
state of being late
Pronunciation: / bɪˈleɪtɪdnəs / (AmE), / bɪˈleɪtɪdnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being late or delayed

Examples

His belatedness at ceremony
His belatedness was noticed by everyone at the ceremony.
The belatedness of the response made the apology seem less sincere.
She apologized for her belatedness in sending the thank-you note.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lateness - the fact or quality of arriving or happening after the expected time
  • antonym: promptness - the quality of being on time or without delay
