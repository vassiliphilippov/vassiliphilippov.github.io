decline

verb
to slope downward
Pronunciation: / dɪˈklaɪn / (AmE), / dɪˈklaɪn / (BrE)
(of a surface or object) to slope downward or become lower in level.

Examples

The path declines gently
At the edge of the city, the land declines toward the river.
The old theater's roof declines slightly to the back.
The path begins to decline steeply after the ridge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: descend - to move downward; go or come down
