osteosarcoma

noun
malignant bone tumor
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɑstioʊsɑrˈkoʊmə / (AmE), / ˌɒstiəʊsɑːˈkəʊmə / (BrE)
a malignant tumor that develops in bone, typically affecting children and young adults

Examples

Bone osteosarcoma
Early detection of osteosarcoma can improve the chances of successful treatment.
Many children diagnosed with osteosarcoma require surgery followed by chemotherapy.
The doctor confirmed that the patient had osteosarcoma in his left femur.
