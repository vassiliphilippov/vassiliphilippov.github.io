feather-brain

noun
person who is foolish or scatterbrained
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛðɚˌbreɪn / (AmE), / ˈfɛðəˌbreɪn / (BrE)
A person who is foolish or scatterbrained; someone who is not sensible or who forgets things easily.

Examples

A feather-brain person
He lost his keys again—what a feather-brain!
Jenny can be such a feather-brain when it comes to remembering her appointments.
Stop acting like a feather-brain and pay attention to the instructions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airhead - a silly or foolish person
  • synonym: scatterbrain - a person who is forgetful or disorganized
  • synonym: simpleton - a person lacking in common sense
