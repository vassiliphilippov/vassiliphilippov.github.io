matchlessness

noun
state of having no equal
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmætʃləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈmatʃləsnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being without equal; having no rival or peer.

Examples

The matchlessness of skill
Historians often remark on the matchlessness of the ancient city's architecture.
The sheer matchlessness of her voice captivated audiences across the world.
Their matchlessness in the field set a new standard for all future competitors.
