plaster

noun
adhesive dressing for wounds
Pronunciation: / ˈplæstɚ / (AmE), / ˈplɑːstə / (BrE)
a small piece of adhesive material used to cover and protect a minor wound or cut

Examples

A plaster for cuts
Do you have a plaster? I think I'm getting a blister on my heel.
He put a plaster on his finger after he cut it while chopping vegetables.
The nurse quickly covered the small scrape with a plaster.

Related Terms

  • synonym: band-aid - an adhesive bandage for covering small wounds (mainly American English)
