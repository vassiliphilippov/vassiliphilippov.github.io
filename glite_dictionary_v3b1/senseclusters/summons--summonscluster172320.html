summons

noun
magical invocation or summoned creature
Pronunciation: / ˈsəmənz / (AmE), / ˈsʌmənz / (BrE)
(in fantasy, gaming, and fiction) a magical invocation or spell that calls a creature or entity into existence, or the creature/entity brought forth by that invocation.

Examples

A magical summons
In the final battle, the wizard unleashed a massive summons that turned the tide.
Before the boss fight, our party prepared to coordinate several summons for maximum crowd control.
The ancient ritual produced a glowing summons that obeyed her command without question.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conjuration - the act of calling a supernatural being or using a spell to summon it
  • synonym: summon - to call someone or something to be present, especially by magic in fantasy contexts
