summons

noun
magical invocation or summoned creature
8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈsʌ.mənz/ (AmE), /ˈsʌ.mənz/ (BrE)
(in fantasy, gaming, and fiction) a magical invocation or spell that calls a creature or entity into existence, or the creature/entity brought forth by that invocation.
