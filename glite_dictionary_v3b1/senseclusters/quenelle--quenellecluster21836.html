quenelle

noun
delicate poached dumpling
9.9
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈnel/ (AmE), /kə.ˈnel/ (BrE)
a small, delicate, oval-shaped portion of soft food (such as a dumpling, mousse, or sorbet), usually made of finely ground meat, fish, vegetables, or other ingredients, and often poached or shaped with spoons

The chef placed a perfectly shaped quenelle of chicken on each plate.
For the starter, we enjoyed a delicate salmon quenelle with a creamy sauce.

  • synonym: meatball - a small ball of ground meat, often mixed with other ingredients
