quenelle

noun
delicate poached dumpling
Pronunciation: / kəˈnɛl / (AmE), / kəˈnɛl / (BrE)
a small, delicate, oval-shaped portion of soft food (such as a dumpling, mousse, or sorbet), usually made of finely ground meat, fish, vegetables, or other ingredients, and often poached or shaped with spoons
