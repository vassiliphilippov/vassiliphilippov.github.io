poster girl

noun
female symbol or example of a cause
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstər ɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstə ɡɜːl / (BrE)
A woman or girl who is a prominent or typical example of a particular quality, cause, or group.

Examples

The poster girl for fashion
In the 1990s, she was the poster girl for grunge fashion trends.
She became the poster girl for environmental activism after her passionate speeches went viral.
The actress is often described as the poster girl for health and fitness.
