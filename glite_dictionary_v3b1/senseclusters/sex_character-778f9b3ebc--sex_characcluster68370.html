sex character

noun
trait associated with biological sex
2.4
Pronunciation: / sɛks ˈkærɪktər / (AmE), / sɛks ˈkarɪktə / (BrE)
a characteristic, either anatomical or psychological, that is strongly associated with one biological sex as opposed to the other

Examples

Primary sex characters
Primary sex characters include organs like ovaries or testes.
Some researchers have studied how psychological sex characters influence behavior.
The development of secondary sex characters, such as facial hair in men, occurs during puberty.
