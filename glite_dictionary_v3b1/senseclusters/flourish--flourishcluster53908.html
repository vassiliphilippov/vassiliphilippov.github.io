flourish

verb
grow or develop vigorously
2.8 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈflʌ.rɪʃ/ /; / /ˈflɜː.rɪʃ/ /; / /ˈflʊ.rɪʃ/ (AmE), /ˈflʌ.rɪʃ/ (BrE)

Examples

Despite the harsh conditions, the young trees managed to flourish.
