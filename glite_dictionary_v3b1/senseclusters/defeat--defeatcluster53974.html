defeat

noun
feeling of frustration after failure
5.8 2.6
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈfiːt/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈfiːt/ (BrE)
a sense of frustration or discouragement experienced when one fails to achieve a goal or is prevented from succeeding

Examples

Feeling of defeat
After losing the final match, a crushing sense of defeat settled over the team.
He struggled to hide his defeat when he failed the entrance exam again.
The politician’s speech revealed a rare moment of defeat and vulnerability.
