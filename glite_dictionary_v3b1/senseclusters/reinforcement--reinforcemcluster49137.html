reinforcement

noun
device or material for added strength
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: /riː.ɪn.ˈfɔːr.smənt/ /; / /rɪn.ˈfɔːr.smənt/ (AmE), /riː.ɪn.ˈfɔː.smənt/ /; / /rɪn.ˈfɔː.smənt/ (BrE)
something (such as a device, material, or extra troops) that is added to provide extra strength or support
