legal status

noun
position as defined by law
Pronunciation: / ˈliɡəl ˈsteɪtəs / (AmE), / ˈliːɡəl ˈsteɪtəs / (BrE)
The position or standing of a person, organization, or thing as recognized and defined by law.

Examples

Legal status under law
Her citizenship application will change her legal status under immigration law.
His legal status as a permanent resident allows him to work in the country.
The organization is seeking legal status as a registered charity.
