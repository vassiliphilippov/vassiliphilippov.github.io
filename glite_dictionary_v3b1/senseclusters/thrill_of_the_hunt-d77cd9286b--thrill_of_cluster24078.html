thrill of the hunt

noun
excitement from pursuing or searching
5.1 5.5
Pronunciation: /θrɪl əv ðə hʌnt/ /; / /θrɪl əv ðiː hʌnt/ (AmE), /θrɪl əv ðə hʌnt/ /; / /θrɪl əv ðiː hʌnt/ (BrE)
