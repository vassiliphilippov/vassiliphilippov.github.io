bubba

noun
rough, unsophisticated man
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌbə / (AmE), / ˈbʌbə / (BrE)
an informal term for a large, rough, or unsophisticated man, often implying a lack of refinement or education

Examples

A big bubba
The crowd cheered as the burly bubba lifted the heavy log with surprising ease.
He was known as a friendly bubba who always helped his neighbors with their chores.
Some people use the word bubba to describe a man who prefers simple pleasures and outdoor activities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gentleman - a man of good manners and refinement
  • synonym: redneck - an unrefined or unsophisticated rural person, often from the southern United States
