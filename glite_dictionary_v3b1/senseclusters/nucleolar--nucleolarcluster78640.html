nucleolar

adjective
of the nucleolus
9.1
Pronunciation: / nuːˈkliːələr / (AmE), / njuːˈkliːələ / (BrE)
relating to or associated with the nucleolus, a small structure within the cell nucleus

Examples

Nucleolar region
The nucleolar region is easily visible under an electron microscope.
Researchers observed nucleolar activity increasing during rapid cell growth.
Certain diseases can cause changes in nucleolar structure and function.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nuclear - relating to the nucleus of a cell
