cesta

noun
woven basket
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛstə / (AmE), / ˈsɛstə / (BrE)
a basket, often woven, used for carrying, storing, or displaying objects

Examples

A fruit cesta
She placed fresh bread in the cesta before serving breakfast to her family.
The market vendor displayed colorful fruits in a large cesta on the stall.
He carried a woven cesta filled with vegetables from the garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: basket - a container made of interwoven material used for carrying or holding things
