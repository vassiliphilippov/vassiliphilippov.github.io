collectability

noun
suitability for collecting
5.7
Pronunciation: / kəˌlɛktəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / kəˌlɛktəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being suitable or desirable for collecting, especially as a hobby or investment

Examples

Collectability of antiques
The collectability of old comic books often depends on their condition and rarity.
Experts debated the collectability of the newly released limited-edition coins.
Some people are drawn to antiques because of their high collectability and potential value.
