John F. Kennedy International Airport

proper noun
major airport in New York City
2.5
Pronunciation: / dʒɑn ɛf ˈkɛnədi ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəl ˈɛrˌpɔrt / (AmE), / dʒɒn ɛf ˈkɛnədi ˌɪntəˈnæʃnəl ˈeəpɔːt / (BrE)
