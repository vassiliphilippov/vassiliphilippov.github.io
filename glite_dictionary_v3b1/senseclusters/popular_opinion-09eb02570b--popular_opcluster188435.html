popular opinion

noun
belief shared by most people
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːpjələr əˈpɪnjən / (AmE), / ˈpɒpjʊlə əˈpɪnjən / (BrE)
A belief or sentiment that is held by the majority of people in a community or society.

Examples

The popular opinion
Changes in popular opinion can greatly influence election outcomes.
Despite popular opinion, the scientist's theory was later proven correct.
The government considers popular opinion when making important decisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: public opinion - beliefs or attitudes held by the general public
