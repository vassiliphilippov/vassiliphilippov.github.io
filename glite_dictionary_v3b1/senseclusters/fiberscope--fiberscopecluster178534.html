fiberscope

noun
flexible fiber-optic medical instrument
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪbərˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˈfʌɪbəskəʊp / (BrE)
A flexible optical instrument using fiber optics, used especially in medicine to visually examine internal organs.

Examples

A fiberscope device
Advances in fiberscope technology have improved the accuracy of minimally invasive surgeries.
Cleaning the fiberscope properly is crucial to prevent infections after procedures.
The doctor used a fiberscope to examine the patient's airway.
