comprende

verb
to command understanding
9.9
Pronunciation: / komˈprɛndeɪ / (AmE), / kɒmˈprɛndeɪ / (BrE)
used as an imperative command in Spanish meaning 'understand!' or 'do you get it?'.

Examples

Teacher says 'comprende'
The teacher pointed at the board and said 'comprende' to make sure everyone was following.
He raised his voice and shouted 'comprende' when the instructions were not clear.
After explaining the rules, she looked at the group and asked 'comprende?'

Related Terms

  • antonym: ignore - to pay no attention to something
  • synonym: understand - to grasp the meaning of something
