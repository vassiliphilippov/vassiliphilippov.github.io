psittacosis

noun
disease from birds causing atypical pneumonia
7.0 9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪtəˈkoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌsɪtəkəʊˈsɪs /; / ˌsɪtəˈkəʊsɪs / (BrE)
an infectious disease caused by the bacterium Chlamydia psittaci, usually transmitted from birds (especially parrots) to humans, causing flu-like symptoms
