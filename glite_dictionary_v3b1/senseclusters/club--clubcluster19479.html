club

noun
stout stick used as weapon or tool
6.8
Pronunciation: / klʌb / (AmE), / klʌb / (BrE)
a stout heavy stick, often thicker at one end, used as a weapon or for hitting something

Examples

A wooden club
He walked through the dark alley gripping a club for protection.
She fashioned a makeshift club from a broken table leg.
The ancient warriors were armed with nothing but a club and shield.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: baton - a short stick or staff, often used as a weapon or symbol of authority
  • synonym: cudgel - a short thick stick used as a weapon
