succulence

noun
the quality of being juicy
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌkjələns / (AmE), / ˈsʌkjʊləns / (BrE)
the quality of being juicy and appetizing, especially in food

Examples

The succulence of food
Chefs prize the succulence of fresh seafood for its rich, moist texture.
Overcooking can destroy the succulence of a good steak.
The succulence of the ripe peaches made them a favorite at the market.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dryness - the state of lacking moisture or juice
  • synonym: juiciness - the quality of being full of juice
