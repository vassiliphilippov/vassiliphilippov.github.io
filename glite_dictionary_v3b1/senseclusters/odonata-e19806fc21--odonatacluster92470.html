Odonata

noun
order of insects: dragonflies and damselflies
Pronunciation: / oʊˈdɑːnətə /; / ˌoʊdəˈneɪtə / (AmE), / əʊˈdɒnətə /; / ˌəʊdəˈneɪtə / (BrE)
an order of insects that includes dragonflies and damselflies, with elongated bodies, large eyes, and two pairs of transparent wings
