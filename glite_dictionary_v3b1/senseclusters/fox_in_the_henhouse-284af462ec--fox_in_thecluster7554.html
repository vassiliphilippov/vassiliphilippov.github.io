fox in the henhouse

noun
person posing threat in a vulnerable situation
6.5 6.7
Pronunciation: /fɑːks ɪn ðə ˈhen.haʊs/ /; / /fɑːks ɪn ðiː ˈhen.haʊs/ /; / /fɑːks ən ðə ˈhen.haʊs/ (AmE), /fɒks ɪn ðə ˈhen.haʊs/ /; / /fɒks ɪn ðiː ˈhen.haʊs/ /; / /fɒks ən ðə ˈhen.haʊs/ (BrE)
a person who is a danger or threat in a place where others are vulnerable, especially in a position of trust or responsibility

Examples
