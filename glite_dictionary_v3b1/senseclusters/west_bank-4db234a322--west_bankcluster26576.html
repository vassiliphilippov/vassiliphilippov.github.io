West Bank

proper noun
territory west of the Jordan River
5.3
Pronunciation: / wɛst bæŋk / (AmE), / wɛst bæŋk / (BrE)
an area of land in the Middle East, situated on the west side of the Jordan River between Israel and Jordan, largely inhabited by Palestinians

Examples

The West Bank area
