epoch-making

adjective
of historic, era-defining importance
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɛpək ˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈiːpɒk ˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
having great significance or importance, especially in bringing about or marking the beginning of a new era or development.

Examples

An epoch-making event
The launch of the internet is considered an epoch-making moment in human history.
The scientist's epoch-making research changed the course of modern medicine.
Their epoch-making agreement paved the way for a new era of peace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: epochal - highly significant or important, especially in defining an era
