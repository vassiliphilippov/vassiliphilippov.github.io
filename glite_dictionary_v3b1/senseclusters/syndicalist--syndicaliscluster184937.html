syndicalist

noun
supporter of syndicalism or workers' control
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪndɪkəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈsɪndɪkəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or advocates for syndicalism, a movement that seeks to transfer the ownership and control of the means of production and distribution to workers' unions

Examples

A syndicalist supporter
He read widely about syndicalist theory and the history of labor movements.
Many early twentieth-century syndicalists aimed to empower workers through direct action.
The factory strike was organized by a prominent syndicalist known for radical views.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anarchist - a person who believes in or tries to bring about anarchy
