life support

noun
medical equipment sustaining life
1.0 2.8
Pronunciation: / laɪf səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / laɪf səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
medical equipment that keeps a person alive by assisting or replacing vital bodily functions, such as breathing or circulation, when they cannot function independently

Examples

On life support machines
After several days on life support, the patient showed signs of improvement.
He was kept alive by life support machines in the intensive care unit.
The doctors placed her on life support after she stopped breathing on her own.

Related Terms

  • synonym: life support system - a system or device for keeping a person alive when they are seriously ill or injured
