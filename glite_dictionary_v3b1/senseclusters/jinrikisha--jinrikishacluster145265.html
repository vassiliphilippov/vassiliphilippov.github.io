jinrikisha

noun
two-wheeled passenger cart pulled by a person
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒɪnˈrɪkʃə / (AmE), / dʒɪnˈrɪkʃə / (BrE)
a small, two-wheeled cart for one passenger, traditionally pulled by a person, especially in East Asia

Examples

The museum had a restored jinrikisha on display as part of its transportation exhibit.
