Examples

The value-added tax rate

Many European countries charge a value-added tax on most consumer products.
The value-added tax is often included in the price displayed on the shelf.

Synonyms

  • VAT
    5.0 0.90/m
    VAT is a tax charged on the value added at each production stage, while value-added tax is the formal name used in reports.
  • GST
    4.2 0.11/m
    GST is the common abbreviation for goods and services tax collected at several production stages, while value-added tax is the full formal name.
  • goods and service tax
    3.6 0.00/m
    goods and service tax is a tax collected by the seller from the final buyer at sale, while value-added tax is charged at each production stage.
