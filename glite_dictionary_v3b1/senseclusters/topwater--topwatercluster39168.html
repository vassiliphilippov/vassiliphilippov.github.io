topwater

adjective
surface fishing
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑpˌwɔtər / (AmE), / ˈtɒpˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
describing fishing lures or techniques that remain on or near the surface of the water

Examples

Topwater lure
He prefers using topwater lures when fishing for bass at dawn.
The angler demonstrated a topwater technique to attract fish near the surface.
Many fishermen find topwater action exciting because they can see the fish strike.

Related Terms

  • antonym: subsurface - located or occurring below the surface of the water
  • synonym: surface - relating to the top layer of water
