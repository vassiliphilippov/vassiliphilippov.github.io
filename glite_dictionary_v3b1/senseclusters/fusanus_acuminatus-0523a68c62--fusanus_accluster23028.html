Fusanus acuminatus

noun
Australian tree with edible fruit and seeds
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfjuːˈseɪnəs ˌækjʊˈmɪnətəs / (AmE), / ˌfjuːˈsænəs ˌækjʊˈmɪnətəs / (BrE)
An Australian tree whose edible flesh and nut-like seeds are consumed, also known as quandong.
