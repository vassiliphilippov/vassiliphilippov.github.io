Shetlander

noun
small hardy pony
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛtləndər / (AmE), / ˈʃɛtləndə / (BrE)
a small, strong pony breed originally from the Shetland Islands, known for its hardiness and thick coat

Examples

A Shetlander pony
The children enjoyed riding the gentle Shetlander at the farm during their holiday.
A Shetlander can survive harsh weather thanks to its thick winter coat.
She won a ribbon for her well-groomed Shetlander at the local horse show.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: horse - a large domesticated animal used for riding and work
