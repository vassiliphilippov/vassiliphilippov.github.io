rage

noun
violent or furious natural force
7.6
Pronunciation: / reɪdʒ / (AmE), / reɪdʒ / (BrE)
a violent or furious natural force, especially as manifested in the elements such as wind, sea, or storm

Examples

The storm's rage
As the storm's rage subsided, a calm fell over the village.
The hurricane struck the coast with unbridled rage, uprooting trees and demolishing houses.
The poet described winter as a time when the wind's rage battered the old farmhouse.

  • synonym: fury - intense and violent force, especially of nature
