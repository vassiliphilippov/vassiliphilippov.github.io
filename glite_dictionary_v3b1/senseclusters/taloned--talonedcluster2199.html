taloned

adjective
having talons or claws
Pronunciation: / ˈtælənd / (AmE), / ˈtælənd / (BrE)
(of an animal) having talons or claws, especially sharp ones used for seizing prey.

Examples

Sharp taloned animal
Owls are known for their strong, taloned toes, which help them catch small mammals.
The eagle's taloned feet gripped the branch tightly as it watched for prey.
The myth spoke of a taloned beast that hunted in the night.
