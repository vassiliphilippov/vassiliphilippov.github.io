cytometry

noun
cell measurement science
8.2
Pronunciation: / saɪˈtɑmətri / (AmE), / saɪˈtɒmətri / (BrE)
the scientific measurement and analysis of cells, often using specialized instruments

Examples

Cytometry analysis
Modern laboratories use cytometry to quickly analyze thousands of cells in a single sample.
Advances in cytometry have improved the diagnosis of many blood disorders.
Researchers rely on cytometry to study the immune system at the cellular level.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cell analysis - the examination and study of cells
  • synonym: flow cytometry - a technique for counting and analyzing microscopic particles like cells
