regulation

noun
official rule or directive
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛɡjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛɡjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
an official rule or directive made and maintained by an authority

Examples

A safety regulation
Failure to comply with the regulation may result in a fine.
The new safety regulation requires all workers to wear protective helmets.
These regulations were introduced to protect consumers from unsafe products.
According to school regulation, students must wear uniforms during class hours.
All employees must follow the regulation requiring safety helmets on the construction site.
The new environmental regulation limits emissions from factories to protect air quality.

  • degree higher: law - a system of rules recognized by a community
  • synonym: order - an authoritative command or instruction
  • synonym: rule - a prescribed guide for conduct or action
