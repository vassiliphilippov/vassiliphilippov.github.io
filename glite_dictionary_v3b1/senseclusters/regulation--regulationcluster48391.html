Examples

A safety regulation
Failure to comply with the regulation may result in a fine.
The new safety regulation requires all workers to wear protective helmets.
These regulations were introduced to protect consumers from unsafe products.
According to school regulation, students must wear uniforms during class hours.
All employees must follow the regulation requiring safety helmets on the construction site.
The new environmental regulation limits emissions from factories to protect air quality.

Synonyms

  • rule
    1.0 189/m
    rule is a general instruction or guideline used in many settings, while regulation is an official legal rule kept by authorities.
  • directive
    6.1 8.49/m
    directive is an instruction sent from an authority to use or change something, while regulation is a formal rule applied across a system.
  • ordinance
    6.7 8.46/m
    ordinance is a local government law for a city or town, while regulation is a broader official rule set by authorities.
  • edict
    7.2 2.01/m
    edict is a public proclamation that announces a law or command, while regulation is a rule that authorities maintain and enforce.
  • fiat
    8.5 3.94/m
    fiat is a decree issued by someone in power without debate, while regulation is a formal rule usually made through procedures.
  • mandate
    4.9 16/m
    mandate is an order or official requirement telling someone to act, while regulation is a standing rule created and kept by authorities.

  • synonym: order - an authoritative command or instruction
