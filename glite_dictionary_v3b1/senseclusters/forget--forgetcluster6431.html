forget

verb
fail to do something as intended
Pronunciation: / fərˈɡɛt / (AmE), / fəˈɡɛt / (BrE)
to fail to remember to do something that you intended or were supposed to do

Examples

Forget to call
I forgot to bring my umbrella, so I got soaked in the rain.
Please don't forget to lock the door when you leave.
She always forgets to send birthday cards to her friends.

Related Terms

  • antonym: remember - to keep something in mind or recall to do it
