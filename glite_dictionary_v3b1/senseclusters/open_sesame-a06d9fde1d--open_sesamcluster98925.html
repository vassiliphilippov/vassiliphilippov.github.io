open sesame

noun
magical command to unlock or open
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊpən ˈsesəˌmi /; / ˌoʊpən ˈsɛsəmi / (AmE), / ˈəʊpən ˈsesəmi /; / ˌəʊpən ˈsɛsəmi / (BrE)
a magic phrase or thing that gives access or success easily (from the story of Ali Baba)

Technology often acts as an open sesame for business expansion in modern markets.
