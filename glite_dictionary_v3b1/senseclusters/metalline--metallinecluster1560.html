metalline

adjective
containing metal
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtəˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈmɛtəˌliːn / (BrE)
containing, bearing, or yielding metal, especially used to describe ores, veins, or rocks with metal content

Examples

Metalline ore sample
The geologist identified a metalline layer in the rock formation, indicating the presence of valuable minerals.
They discovered a metalline deposit that was rich in copper and zinc.
The prospector was excited to find a metalline vein running through the hillside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: metallic - having the characteristics or nature of metal
  • antonym: nonmetallic - not containing or resembling metal
