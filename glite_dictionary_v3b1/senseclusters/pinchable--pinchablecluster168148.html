pinchable

adjective
cute and squeezable
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪntʃəbəl / (AmE), / ˈpɪntʃəbəl / (BrE)
so cute or attractive that it makes someone want to pinch, especially a cheek or similar feature

Examples

Pinchable cheeks
The puppy's pinchable ears made everyone smile at the animal shelter.
His niece has the most pinchable cheeks you have ever seen.
Those pinchable dimples always appear when she laughs.
