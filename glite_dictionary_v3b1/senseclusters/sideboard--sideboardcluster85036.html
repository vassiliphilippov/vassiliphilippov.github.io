sideboard

noun
board forming side of bed or crib
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdbɔːd / (BrE)
a board forming the side of a bed or crib, enclosing and supporting the structure

Examples

Bed sideboard
For safety, ensure the sideboard is properly attached to the crib.
She sanded the sideboard of the antique bed before repainting it.
The old crib's sideboard was loose and needed repairs.
