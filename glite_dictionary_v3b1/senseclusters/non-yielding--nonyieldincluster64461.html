non-yielding

adjective
not productive
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈjiːldɪŋ / (AmE), / nɒnˈjiːldɪŋ / (BrE)
not producing crops, results, or returns; failing to give any useful output or benefit

Examples

Non-yielding land
The non-yielding land was left uncultivated for several years.
Investors avoided the non-yielding project because it showed no signs of profit.
After repeated attempts, the scientist gave up on the non-yielding experiment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barren - unable to produce plants or fruit
  • antonym: productive - producing or able to produce large amounts or results
  • synonym: unproductive - not producing useful results or goods
