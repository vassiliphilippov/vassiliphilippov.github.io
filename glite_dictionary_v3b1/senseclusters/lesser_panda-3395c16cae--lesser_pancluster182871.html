lesser panda

noun
reddish-brown mammal, also red panda
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛsər ˈpændə / (AmE), / ˈlɛsə ˈpandə / (BrE)
A small, reddish-brown, raccoon-like mammal native to the eastern Himalayas and southwestern China, also known as the red panda.

Examples

The lesser panda in trees
The lesser panda spends most of its time in trees, feeding on bamboo and berries.
The zoo's new exhibit features two lesser pandas from the eastern Himalayas.
Unlike the giant panda, the lesser panda has a long, bushy tail and reddish fur.

Related Terms

  • synonym: red panda - another common name for the lesser panda (Ailurus fulgens)
