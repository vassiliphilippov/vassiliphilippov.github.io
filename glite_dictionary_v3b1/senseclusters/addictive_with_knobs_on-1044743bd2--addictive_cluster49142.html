addictive with knobs on

adjective
extremely addictive (informal idiom)
9.6
Pronunciation: / əˈdɪktɪv wɪð nɑbz ɑn / (AmE), / əˈdɪktɪv wɪð nɒbz ɒn / (BrE)
(informal, chiefly British) Extremely addictive; far more addictive than usual or than something previously mentioned.

Examples

That television series is addictive with knobs on; I binged the entire season in two days.
The app is addictive with knobs on—I can't put my phone down since I started using it.
This new candy is addictive with knobs on, even more than the one we tried last year.
