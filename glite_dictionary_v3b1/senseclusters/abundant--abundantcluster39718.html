abundant

adjective
present in large amounts; plentiful
Pronunciation: / əˈbʌndənt / (AmE), / əˈbʌndənt / (BrE)
existing or available in large quantities; more than enough; plentiful.

Examples

An abundant supply
The region has abundant rainfall throughout the year, making it ideal for agriculture.
There is abundant evidence to support his theory.
We enjoyed an abundant dinner with food from every corner of the globe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: copious - in large quantities; abundant
  • synonym: plentiful - existing in large amounts; more than enough
  • antonym: scarce - insufficient for the demand; in short supply
