unirrigated

adjective
without artificial watering
6.4
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ɪ.ˈrɪ.ɡeɪ.tɪd/ /; / /ə.nɪ.ˈrɪ.ɡeɪ.tɪd/ (AmE), /ʌn.ɪ.ˈrɪ.ɡeɪ.tɪd/ /; / /ə.nɪ.ˈrɪ.ɡeɪ.tɪd/ (BrE)
