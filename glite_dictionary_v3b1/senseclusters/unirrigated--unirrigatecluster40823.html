unirrigated

adjective
without artificial watering
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɪrɪˌɡeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɪrɪɡeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not supplied with water by artificial means such as pipes, channels, or sprinklers

Examples

The unirrigated land
The unirrigated land produced much less food than the fields with modern irrigation systems.
Farmers struggled to grow crops on unirrigated plots during the long, hot summer.
Many wild plants thrive in unirrigated areas where water is scarce.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dry - lacking moisture or water
  • antonym: irrigated - supplied with water by artificial means
