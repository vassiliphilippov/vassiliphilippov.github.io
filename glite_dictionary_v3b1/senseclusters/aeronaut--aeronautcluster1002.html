aeronaut

noun
person who operates a balloon or airship
9.6 0.14/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…õr.…ôÀån…ît / (AmE), / Ààe…ô.r…ô.n…îÀêt / (BrE)
a person who pilots or travels in an airship or other lighter-than-air aircraft, especially a balloon

Examples

An aeronaut in a balloon
In the 19th century, being an aeronaut was considered both adventurous and dangerous.
Many spectators gathered to watch the celebrated aeronaut launch his giant gas balloon.
The daring aeronaut guided his hot air balloon across the mountain range.
