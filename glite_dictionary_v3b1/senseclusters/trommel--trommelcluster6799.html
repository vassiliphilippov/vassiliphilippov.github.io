trommel

noun
German drum
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɑːməl / (AmE), / ˈtrɒməl / (BrE)
a drum used in military or folk music, especially in German-speaking countries

Examples

Marching band trommel
The parade featured a musician playing a traditional trommel at the front of the marching band.
During the folk festival, children learned to keep rhythm on a small trommel.
He bought a handmade trommel as a souvenir from his trip to Germany.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drum - a percussion instrument sounded by being struck
