dumbhead

noun
slang insult
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌmˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈdʌmˌhɛd / (BrE)
a slang term for a person who is considered very stupid or foolish

Examples

A dumbhead person
Stop being such a dumbhead and read the instructions before you start.
He laughed and called his friend a dumbhead after the silly mistake.
You don't have to be a dumbhead to forget your keys once in a while.

Related Terms

  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence
  • synonym: idiot - a person who is very stupid or lacks common sense
