untruthful

adjective

Synonyms

    9.1 0.33/m
    mendacious is habitually false and formal in tone, while untruthful is a general word for not telling the truth.
  • deceitful
    5.0 1.46/m
    deceitful acts aim to mislead others deliberately, often by tricking people, while untruthful simply means not telling the truth.
  • dishonest
    2.5 6.87/m
    dishonest describes someone likely to lie, cheat, or steal for gain, while untruthful focuses only on not telling the truth.
  • disingenuous
    7.8 3.74/m
    disingenuous sounds polite and shows hidden motives or false friendliness, while untruthful means not telling the truth directly.
  • uncandid
    9.1 0.03/m
    uncandid means not open or straight in speech, often hiding bias or facts, while untruthful means not telling the truth.
  • deceptive
    5.3 4.30/m
    deceptive usually describes things or actions that give a false impression, whereas untruthful describes words or people that are not honest.
