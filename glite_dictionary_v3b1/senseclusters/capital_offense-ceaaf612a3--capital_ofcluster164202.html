capital offense

noun
crime punishable by the death penalty
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkæpɪtl əˈfɛns / (AmE), / ˈkæpɪtəl əˈfɛns / (BrE)
a crime so serious that it is punishable by death under the law

Examples

A capital offense
He was convicted of a capital offense and faced a lengthy court trial.
The murder was considered a capital offense in that state, carrying the possibility of the death penalty.
Treason is often classified as a capital offense under federal law.
