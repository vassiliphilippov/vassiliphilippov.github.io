driverless

adjective
without a human driver
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdraɪvərləs / (AmE), / ˈdraɪvələs / (BrE)
operating or moving without a human driver controlling it

Examples

Driverless car
The city is testing driverless buses on several routes.
Many experts believe that driverless cars will reduce traffic accidents in the future.
A driverless train arrived at the station right on time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: manual - operated or controlled by a person
  • synonym: self-driving - capable of driving itself without human input
