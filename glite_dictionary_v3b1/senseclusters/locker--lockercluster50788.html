locker

noun
secure online storage space
3.3 0.51/m
Pronunciation: / Ààl…ëÀêk…ôr / (AmE), / Ààl…ík…ô / (BrE)
A secure online storage space, typically accessed via a website or app, for storing and retrieving digital documents, credentials, or files.

Examples

Digital locker app
She uploaded her passport and itinerary to her locker so she could access them abroad.
The university provides every student with a secure locker for storing academic documents online.
Before the meeting, he shared the presentation via the team's encrypted locker link.
