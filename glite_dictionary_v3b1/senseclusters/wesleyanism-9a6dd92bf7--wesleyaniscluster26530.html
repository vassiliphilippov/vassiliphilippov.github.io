Wesleyanism

noun
principles of Methodism founded by John Wesley
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛz.li.əˌnɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈwɛz.lɪ.ə.nɪz.əm / (BrE)
The religious principles and practices taught by John Wesley, forming the foundation of the Methodist movement.
