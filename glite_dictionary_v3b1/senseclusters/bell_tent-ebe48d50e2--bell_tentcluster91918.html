bell tent

noun
a tent with a bell-like shape
Pronunciation: / bɛl tɛnt / (AmE), / bɛl tɛnt / (BrE)
a tent with a circular base and a high central pole, giving it a bell-like shape

Examples

A bell tent for camping
A bell tent is ideal for festivals because it offers plenty of space.
The guests stayed in a luxurious bell tent during the outdoor wedding.
We set up a bell tent by the lake for our weekend camping trip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tent - a portable shelter made of cloth, supported by poles and ropes
