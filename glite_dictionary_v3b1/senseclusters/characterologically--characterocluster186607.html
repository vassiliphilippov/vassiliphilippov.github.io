characterologically

adverb
relating to character
Pronunciation: / ˌkærɪktərəˈlɑdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˌkærɪktərəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to a person's character or personality structure

Examples

Described characterologically
The therapist described the patient as characterologically unable to form close relationships.
They are characterologically similar, both being highly independent and reserved.
Some people are characterologically more adaptable to change than others.

Related Terms

  • synonym: psychologically - in a way that relates to the mind or mental processes
