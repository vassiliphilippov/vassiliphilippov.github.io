observationally

adverb
by observing
Pronunciation: / ˌɑbzɚˈveɪʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌɒbzəˈveɪʃənəli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to or is based on observation

Examples

To observe carefully
Observationally, the scientist noted changes in the plant's growth over several weeks.
The two groups appeared similar observationally, but their test results differed significantly.
He approached the problem observationally, carefully watching before drawing any conclusions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: empirically - in a way based on experience or observation rather than theory
  • antonym: theoretically - in a way based on theory rather than observation or experience
