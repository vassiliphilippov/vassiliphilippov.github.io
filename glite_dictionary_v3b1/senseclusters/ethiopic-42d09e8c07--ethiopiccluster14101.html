Ethiopic

adjective
of ancient Ethiopia
Pronunciation: / ˌiːθiˈɑːpɪk / (AmE), / ˌiːθiˈɒpɪk / (BrE)
relating to ancient Ethiopia, its people, languages, or writing system

Examples

Ethiopic culture

Scholars study Ethiopic manuscripts to learn about ancient African history.
