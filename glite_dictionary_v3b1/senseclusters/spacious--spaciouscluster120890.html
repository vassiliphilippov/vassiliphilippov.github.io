Synonyms

  • roomy
    2.1 3.14/m
    roomy is informal and common for places with lots of floor space, while spacious sounds neutral and slightly more elegant.
  • commodious
    7.4 0.29/m
    commodious is a more formal word suggesting roomy comfort in houses, while spacious is simpler and common in everyday speech.
  • capacious
    7.0 0.60/m
    capacious is often used for containers or luggage that hold a lot, while spacious describes open areas like rooms or halls.
