hobo

noun
travelling worker; itinerant laborer
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊˌboʊ /; / ˈhoʊboʊ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊbəʊ / (BrE)
a person who travels from place to place, often homeless and looking for temporary work, especially by riding freight trains

In stories and movies, the life of a hobo is sometimes romanticized as one of freedom and adventure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drifter - a person who moves from place to place without a fixed home or job
  • synonym: tramp - a person who travels from place to place, often seeking work or begging
  • synonym: vagrant - a person without a settled home or job who wanders from place to place
