Illimani

proper noun
mountain peak in Bolivia's Andes
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪliˈmɑːni / (AmE), / ˌɪliˈmɑːni / (BrE)
a mountain peak in the Andes in Bolivia, rising to 21,201 feet (6,438 meters) and considered a major landmark southeast of La Paz.

Examples

Illimani mountain peak
Climbers from around the world attempt to reach the summit of Illimani each year.
On clear mornings, the peak of Illimani glows beautifully in the first light of day.
The snow-capped Illimani is visible from many parts of La Paz, dominating the city’s skyline.
