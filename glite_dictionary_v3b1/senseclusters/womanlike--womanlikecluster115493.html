womanlike

adjective
resembling a woman
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊmənˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈwʊmənˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having qualities, appearance, or manner that are like those of a woman

Examples

A womanlike manner
Her handwriting is so delicate and womanlike that it could easily be mistaken for calligraphy.
The painting depicted a womanlike creature emerging from the mist, both mysterious and graceful.
The statue has a distinctly womanlike figure, with soft facial features and flowing robes.
He spoke to her in a womanlike voice, soft and gentle.
Her womanlike compassion helped calm the frightened child.
The queen displayed a womanlike grace and dignity in court.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feminine - having qualities or appearance traditionally associated with women
  • antonym: manlike - having qualities traditionally associated with men
  • synonym: womanly - having qualities regarded as typical of a woman
