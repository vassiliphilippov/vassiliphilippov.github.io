womanlike

adjective
resembling a woman
8.7 5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈwʊm.ən.laɪk/ (AmE), /ˈwʊm.ən.laɪk/ (BrE)
having qualities, appearance, or manner that are like those of a woman

Examples

A womanlike manner
Her handwriting is so delicate and womanlike that it could easily be mistaken for calligraphy.

The statue has a distinctly womanlike figure, with soft facial features and flowing robes.
He spoke to her in a womanlike voice, soft and gentle.
Her womanlike compassion helped calm the frightened child.
The queen displayed a womanlike grace and dignity in court.
