Synonyms

  • alpha-adrenergic receptor
    7.4 0.00/m
    alpha-adrenergic receptor is the standard medical name for these sympathetic nerve receptors, while alpha receptor is a shorter general term.
  • alpha-adrenoceptor
    9.3 0.00/m
    alpha-adrenoceptor names the receptor that reacts to certain agents and affects muscles including intestinal relaxation, while alpha receptor is the general sympathetic receptor name.
