Federal Trade Commission

proper noun
US agency regulating competition and consumer protection
3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfed.ər.əl treɪd kə.ˈmɪʃ.ən/ (AmE), /ˈfed.ə.rəl treɪd kə.ˈmɪʃ.ən/ (BrE)
A US government agency that enforces antitrust laws and maintains fair and free competition, also educating the public about issues like identity theft.

Examples

Federal Trade Commission approval
