zigzaggy

adjective
full of sharp turns
Pronunciation: / ˈzɪɡˌzæɡi / (AmE), / ˈzɪɡˌzæɡi / (BrE)
having a shape, path, or pattern with many sharp turns or angles

Examples

Zigzaggy road
The zigzaggy road up the mountain made the drive exciting but challenging.
She drew a zigzaggy line across the paper to decorate her project.
The river followed a zigzaggy course through the valley.

Related Terms

  • antonym: straight - without bends or curves
  • synonym: winding - having many bends or curves
