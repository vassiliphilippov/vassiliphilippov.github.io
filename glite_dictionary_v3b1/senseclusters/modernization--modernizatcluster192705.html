modernization

noun
the process of making modern
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːdərnəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɒdənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making something more modern in style, methods, or attitude

Examples

Economic modernization
Economic modernization often requires significant changes in infrastructure and education.
The modernization of the library included digital catalogs and new computers.
The government is investing heavily in the modernization of public transportation.
Rapid modernisation has transformed many cities in East Asia in recent decades.
Success depended on the modernisation of outdated equipment in the factory.
The government announced a new plan for the modernisation of the public transportation system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: innovation - the introduction of something new
  • synonym: modernization - the process of becoming or making more modern (American spelling)
  • antonym: preservation - the act of keeping something in its original state
  • synonym: update - to make something more modern, usually with new information or equipment
