sounding

adjective
deep or resonant in sound
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsaʊndɪŋ / (BrE)
having a deep, resonant, or impressive sound; producing or characterized by a loud or notable volume

Examples

Sounding voice
At the concert, we admired the rich, sounding notes of the organ.
The river rushed over the rocks with a sounding roar, filling the valley with echoes.
To the left, the sounding waves crashed endlessly against the high cliffs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sonorous - imposingly deep and full (of sound)
