dobson

noun
aquatic larva of the dobsonfly
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑb.sən / (AmE), / ˈdɒb.sən / (BrE)
a large soft-bodied insect (the dobsonfly) or its aquatic larva, often used as fishing bait

Examples

A dobson insect

Fishermen often use dobson larvae as live bait for catching bass and trout.
The male dobson is known for its impressively long jaws.
