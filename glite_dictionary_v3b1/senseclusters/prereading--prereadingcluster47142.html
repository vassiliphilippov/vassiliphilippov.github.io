prereading

noun
assigned preparatory texts
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈriːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈriːdɪŋ / (BrE)
texts or materials assigned to be read before a class, meeting, or event to prepare participants.

Examples

Prereading materials
The professor emailed the prereading to students a week before the first lecture.
Be sure to complete the prereading so you can participate in the group discussion.
Our manager expects everyone to finish the prereading before the meeting starts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: homework - schoolwork assigned to be done outside class
  • synonym: prework - tasks or assignments to be completed before an event
