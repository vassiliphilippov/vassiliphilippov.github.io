get the goods

verb
obtain incriminating information
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt ðə ɡʊdz / (AmE), / ɡɛt ðə ɡʊdz / (BrE)
to obtain evidence, especially damaging or incriminating information, about someone

Examples

To get the goods on someone
If a journalist can get the goods on the corrupt official, the story will make headlines.
She finally got the goods on her rival by hiring a private investigator.
The detective worked undercover to get the goods needed for an arrest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dig up dirt - to find and reveal negative or scandalous information about someone
  • synonym: expose - to reveal the truth about someone, especially wrongdoing
