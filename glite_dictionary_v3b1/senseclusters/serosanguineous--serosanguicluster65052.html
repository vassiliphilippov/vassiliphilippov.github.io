serosanguineous

adjective
containing blood and serum
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪroʊˌsæŋɡwɪˈniːəs / (AmE), / ˌsɪərəʊˌsæŋɡwɪˈniːəs / (BrE)
containing or relating to both blood and a clear yellowish fluid (serum), especially in bodily fluids or discharges

Examples

Serosanguineous fluid
The nurse observed a serosanguineous fluid leaking from the surgical incision.
Doctors monitor wounds for serosanguineous drainage as a sign of healing or infection.
A serosanguineous discharge is often seen in the early stages of wound recovery.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: sanguineous - containing or relating to blood
  • degree lower: serous - containing or relating to serum
