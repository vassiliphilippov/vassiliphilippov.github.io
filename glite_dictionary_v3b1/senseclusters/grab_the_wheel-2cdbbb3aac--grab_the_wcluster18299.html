grab the wheel

verb
suddenly seize vehicle's steering wheel
Pronunciation: / ɡræb ðə wil / (AmE), / ɡræb ðə wiːl / (BrE)
to suddenly take control of the steering wheel of a vehicle, especially in an emergency or urgent situation

Examples

To grab the wheel suddenly
He instinctively grabbed the wheel when the bus hit a patch of ice.
In a moment of panic, she grabbed the wheel to avoid the obstacle.
When the driver fainted, Mark grabbed the wheel and steered the car to safety.

Related Terms
