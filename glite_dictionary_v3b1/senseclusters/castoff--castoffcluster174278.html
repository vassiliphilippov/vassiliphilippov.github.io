castoff

adjective
discarded or secondhand
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkæstˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈkɑːstˌɒf / (BrE)
no longer wanted and therefore discarded or given away, often used to describe secondhand items

Examples

Castoff clothes
He furnished his apartment with castoff furniture from friends and family.
The charity collects castoff clothes to distribute to those in need.
She found a beautiful castoff lamp at the flea market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discarded - thrown away or rejected as no longer useful
  • antonym: new - not previously used or owned
  • synonym: secondhand - previously owned or used by someone else
