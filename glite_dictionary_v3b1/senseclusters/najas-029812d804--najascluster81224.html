Najas

proper noun
genus of aquatic plants (water nymphs)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪdʒəs / (AmE), / ˈneɪdʒæs / (BrE)
A genus of aquatic plants commonly known as water nymphs, constituting the sole genus of the family Naiadaceae.

Examples

Najas aquatic plants
Botanists studying water nymphs often refer to members of the genus Najas in their research.
Several species of Najas can be found in freshwater lakes and ponds around the world.
The aquatic plant Najas plays an important role in underwater ecosystems as shelter for small fish.
