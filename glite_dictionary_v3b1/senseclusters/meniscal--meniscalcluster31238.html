meniscal

adjective
relating to a meniscus
9.4
Pronunciation: / məˈnɪskəl / (AmE), / mɪˈnɪskəl / (BrE)
relating to or having the shape of a meniscus, especially the curved surface of a liquid in a container.

Examples

Meniscal curve in glass
The scientist carefully observed the meniscal surface to read the liquid's volume accurately.
A meniscal curve can cause errors if not accounted for during measurement.
The meniscal shape of the water in the tube was clearly visible at eye level.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concave - curved inward like the inside of a bowl
  • antonym: convex - curved outward like the outside of a sphere
