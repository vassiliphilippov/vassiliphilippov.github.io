auxiliary research submarine

noun
submarine for research purposes
1.3
Pronunciation: / ɔːɡˈzɪljɛri rɪˈsɜrtʃ səbˌmərin / (AmE), / ɔːɡˈzɪljəri rɪˈsɜːtʃ sʌbˌmɑːriːn / (BrE)
A submarine designed and used primarily for research purposes, often as a support vessel for scientific investigations under water.

Examples

An auxiliary research submarine
An auxiliary research submarine can operate at depths that are unsafe for human divers.
The team deployed an auxiliary research submarine to collect samples from the ocean floor.
The university's new auxiliary research submarine is equipped with advanced sonar technology.
