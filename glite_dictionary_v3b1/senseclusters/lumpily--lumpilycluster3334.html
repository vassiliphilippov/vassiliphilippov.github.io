lumpily

adverb
in a clumsy way
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌmpɪli / (AmE), / ˈlʌmpɪli / (BrE)
in a heavy, clumsy, or awkward manner, as if with lumps or unevenness

Examples

Walks lumpily
He moved lumpily across the dance floor, stepping on his partner's feet several times.
The mashed potatoes were prepared lumpily, with uneven chunks throughout the dish.
She packed her bag lumpily, so it would not close properly.

  • antonym: gracefully - in a smooth and elegant way
