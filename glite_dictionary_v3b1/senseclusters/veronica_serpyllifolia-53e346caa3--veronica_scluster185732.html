Veronica serpyllifolia

noun
a perennial herb with blue flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / vəˌrɑnɪkə sərˌpɪlɪˈfoʊliə / (AmE), / vəˌrɒnɪkə sɜːˌpɪlɪˈfəʊliə / (BrE)
A perennial, low-growing herb with small, opposite leaves and racemes of blue flowers, found throughout Eurasia and the New World; known as thyme-leaved speedwell.
