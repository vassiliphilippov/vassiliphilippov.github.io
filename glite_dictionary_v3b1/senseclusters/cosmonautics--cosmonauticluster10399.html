cosmonautics

noun
space travel science
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑzməˈnɔtɪks / (AmE), / ˌkɒzməˈnɔːtɪks / (BrE)
the science and technology of space travel and exploration beyond Earth's atmosphere

Examples

Study of cosmonautics
He decided to pursue a career in cosmonautics after reading about early space missions.
Advances in cosmonautics have made it possible to send probes to distant planets.
Universities now offer specialized degrees in cosmonautics for aspiring space engineers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aeronautics - the science of flight within Earth's atmosphere
  • synonym: astronautics - the science of the design and operation of spacecraft
