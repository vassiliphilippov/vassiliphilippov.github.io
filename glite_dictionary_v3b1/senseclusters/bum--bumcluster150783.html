bum

noun
contemptible person
5.4
Pronunciation: / bʌm / (AmE), / bʌm / (BrE)
a person who is lazy, idle, or regarded as contemptible

Examples

A lazy bum
Get that bum out of here before he causes more trouble.
He's always making excuses—what a bum!
I can't believe you trusted that bum with your money.
Don’t be such a bum—get up and find a job!
He spent most of his twenties as a bum, doing nothing all day.
Some people called him a bum, but he was just taking some time off.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loafer - a person who is habitually lazy and does not work
  • synonym: rat - a despicable or treacherous person
  • synonym: rotter - a contemptible person (chiefly British)
  • synonym: slacker - a person who avoids work or effort
  • antonym: workaholic - a person who compulsively works long and hard
