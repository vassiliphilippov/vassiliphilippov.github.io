intergenerational

adjective
between generations
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənəl / (BrE)
happening between or involving people from different generations

Examples

Intergenerational wealth transfer
The study focused on intergenerational wealth transfer within families over several decades.
Programs that encourage intergenerational activities can help bridge the gap between young and old.
Researchers are interested in the intergenerational impact of education on social mobility.
The intergenerational project encouraged teenagers and older adults to share their life experiences.
Many families benefit from intergenerational support, with grandparents helping to care for grandchildren.
The museum organized an intergenerational event to connect children with local elders.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intragenerational - occurring within the same generation
  • synonym: multigenerational - involving several generations
