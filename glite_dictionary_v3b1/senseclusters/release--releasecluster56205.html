Synonyms

    4.4 2/m
    discharge sends out or emits a substance or energy from a container or system, while release means letting something escape.
  • liberate
    5.8 0.9/m
    liberate often refers to a chemical process that frees gas or energy, while release is the general act of letting substances escape.
  • eject
    4.1 1/m
    eject forcefully pushes a liquid or substance out in a sudden burst, while release is broader and may be gentle or slow.
  • expel
    5.0 2/m
    expel forces a substance out of a body or container, often by pressure, while release can mean letting it escape naturally.
  • exhaust
    4.8 0.9/m
    exhaust expels gas or vapour from an engine or system as used output, while release simply means letting a substance escape.
  • free
    7.5 0.2/m
    free in science describes a process that lets energy or gas be produced, while release is a general letting something escape.
