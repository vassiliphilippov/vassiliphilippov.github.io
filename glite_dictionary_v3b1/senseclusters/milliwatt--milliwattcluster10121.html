milliwatt

noun
unit of power; one thousandth of a watt
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪliwɑt / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪwɒt / (BrE)
A unit of power equal to one thousandth of a watt.

Examples

One milliwatt unit
A laser pointer typically operates at less than five milliwatts to ensure safety.
Even a small increase of several milliwatts can significantly affect battery life in wireless sensors.
The device consumes just one milliwatt of power, making it extremely energy-efficient.
