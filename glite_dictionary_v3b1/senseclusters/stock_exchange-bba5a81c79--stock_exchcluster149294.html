stock exchange

noun
organized market for trading securities
3.0
Pronunciation: / stɑk ɪkˈstʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / stɒk ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
an organized market where stocks, bonds, and other securities are bought and sold by brokers

Examples

A stock exchange market
After graduating, she got a job as a trader at a major stock exchange.
Many companies list their shares on a stock exchange to raise capital.
The New York stock exchange is the largest in the world by market capitalization.
