hot

adjective
having a burning or spicy taste
1.0 17/m
Pronunciation: / hÉ‘t / (AmE), / hÉ’t / (BrE)
(of food or drink) having a burning taste caused by strong spices, especially chili peppers; spicy.

Examples

Hot peppers
Many people enjoy eating hot peppers with their meals.
She warned me that the sauce was extremely hot before I tried it.
The curry was so hot that I had to drink a glass of milk.

Synonyms

  • spicy
    1.0 6.40/m
    spicy describes food with a sharp, burning taste from spices, while hot means food with strong chili heat.
  • peppery
    3.9 0.44/m
    peppery describes a pepper-like, sharp pungent taste, while hot means strong chili heat that burns the mouth.
  • fiery
    3.6 3.10/m
    fiery means very hot and burning like flames, while hot usually means spicy food that gives a burning taste.
