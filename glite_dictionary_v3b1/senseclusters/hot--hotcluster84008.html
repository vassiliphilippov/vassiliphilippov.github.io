hot

adjective
having a burning or spicy taste
Pronunciation: / hɑt / (AmE), / hɒt / (BrE)
(of food or drink) having a burning taste caused by strong spices, especially chili peppers; spicy.

Examples

Hot peppers
Many people enjoy eating hot peppers with their meals.
She warned me that the sauce was extremely hot before I tried it.
The curry was so hot that I had to drink a glass of milk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spicy - having a strong taste from spices, especially hot ones
