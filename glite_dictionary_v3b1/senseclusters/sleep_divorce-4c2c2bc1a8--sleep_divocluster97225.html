sleep divorce

noun
when couples sleep separately due to sleep issues
Pronunciation: / slip dɪˈvɔrs / (AmE), / sliːp dɪˈvɔːs / (BrE)
an arrangement in which a couple chooses to sleep in separate beds or rooms because of conflicting sleep habits or schedules
