wassail

noun
spiced ale or wine drink, esp. at Christmas
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːseɪl / (AmE), / ˈwɒseɪl / (BrE)
A hot drink made from sweetened ale or wine mixed with spices and roasted apples, traditionally served at Christmas celebrations.

Examples

A bowl of wassail
A steaming bowl of wassail sat at the center of the festive table.
At the Christmas party, they served traditional wassail with roasted apples and spices.
He ladled hot wassail into mugs as carolers sang outside the house.
