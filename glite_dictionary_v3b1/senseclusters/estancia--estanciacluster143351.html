estancia

noun
South American ranch
9.1
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈstæn.sjə/ /; / /es.ˈtæn.sjə/ (AmE), /es.ˈtæn.sjə/ /; / /ə.ˈstæn.sjə/ (BrE)
a large rural estate or cattle ranch in South America, especially in Argentina or Uruguay, often used for raising livestock

Examples

A cattle estancia
They spent a week riding horses on a traditional estancia in Argentina.
The estancia covered thousands of acres and was home to hundreds of cattle.
Tourists can experience rural life by staying at an estancia in Uruguay.
The family spent their summers working on the estancia in the Argentine pampas.
Cattle roamed freely across the vast estancia owned by her grandfather.
Tourists can visit a traditional estancia to experience rural life in Uruguay.
