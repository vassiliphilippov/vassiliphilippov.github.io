prestudy

noun
preliminary research
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːˌstʌdi / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌstʌdi / (BrE)
a preliminary investigation or research carried out before the main study to gather initial information or test methods

Examples

A prestudy phase
Before launching the full experiment, the researchers completed a prestudy to refine their methods.
The prestudy revealed several issues that needed to be addressed before the main project.
Funding was secured for a prestudy to assess the feasibility of the proposed research.

Related Terms

  • antonym: main study - the primary research conducted after preliminary investigations
  • synonym: pilot study - a small-scale preliminary study conducted before the main research
