scene

noun
a view or landscape; visual appearance of a place
Pronunciation: / siːn / (AmE), / siːn / (BrE)
a view, landscape, or situation as seen in real life or depicted in art or photography

Examples

A beautiful scene
From the top of the hill, the sunset created an unforgettable scene.
He spent hours painting the scene outside his window.
Tourists stopped to admire the breathtaking scene along the riverbank.
She enjoys photographing scenes from nature during her travels.
The artist exhibited several scenes capturing urban life at sunset.
Their mural features colorful scenes of the local marketplace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: picture - an image, especially one created by painting or drawing
  • synonym: prospect - a wide view over a region, often scenic
  • synonym: view - the visual perception of a place or landscape
