recognition

noun
official acceptance or acknowledgment
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛkəɡˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛkəɡˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
the action or process of officially accepting or acknowledging something as true, valid, or existing, including granting someone the right to speak in a formal meeting

Examples

International recognition
His achievements led to international recognition of his work.
The government's recognition of the new policy was announced yesterday.
The treaty marked the recognition of the two countries' borders.
After several attempts, she was granted recognition to address the assembly.
No motion can be proposed without proper recognition by the presiding officer.
The senator waited impatiently until he finally received recognition from the chair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acknowledgment - the act of accepting or admitting the existence or truth of something
