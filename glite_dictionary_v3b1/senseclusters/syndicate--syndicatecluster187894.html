syndicate

noun
business association of companies
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪndɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɪndɪkət / (BrE)
an association of companies or organizations formed to carry out a specific business project or to promote a shared interest

Examples

A bank syndicate
A group of banks formed a syndicate to finance the new railway project.
The newspaper was distributed by a national syndicate of publishers.
The oil companies worked together as a syndicate to explore the region for new resources.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consortium - an association of companies for a common purpose
