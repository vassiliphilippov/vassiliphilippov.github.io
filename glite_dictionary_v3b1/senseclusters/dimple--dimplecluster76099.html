dimple

verb
make a small indentation or depression
5.5 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈdɪm.pəl/ (AmE), /ˈdɪm.pəl/ (BrE)
to make a small, rounded indentation or depression on a surface

Examples

To dimple metal
