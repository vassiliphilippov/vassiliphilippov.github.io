RH

noun
abbreviation for releasing hormone
8.9
Pronunciation: / ɑr eɪtʃ / (AmE), / ɑː ɒtʃ / (BrE)
An abbreviation for 'releasing hormone', any of several hormones produced in the hypothalamus that stimulate the release of specific hormones from the anterior pituitary gland.

Examples

RH hormone release
Measurement of RH secretion provides insight into hypothalamic function.
Scientists are studying how different RH molecules affect endocrine disorders.
The doctor explained that RH levels in the blood signal the pituitary to release other hormones.
