auth

noun
access permission
7.5
Pronunciation: /ɔːθ/ (AmE), /ɔːθ/ (BrE)
official permission or rights to access resources or perform actions, especially in computing or administration

Examples

Admin auth required
You need admin auth to install new software on the company servers.
Without proper auth, users cannot access confidential files on the network.
The IT department manages all auth for employee accounts and permissions.

  • synonym: authorization - official permission to do something or access something
