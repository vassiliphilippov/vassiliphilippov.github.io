precontrol

adjective
before main control
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːkənˈtroʊl / (AmE), / ˌpriːkənˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
relating to checks, settings, or measures carried out before a main control phase or operation

Examples

Precontrol checks
The engineers performed precontrol checks to ensure the equipment was ready for operation.
Accurate precontrol measures can prevent problems during the main process.
All precontrol procedures must be completed before starting the primary system.
