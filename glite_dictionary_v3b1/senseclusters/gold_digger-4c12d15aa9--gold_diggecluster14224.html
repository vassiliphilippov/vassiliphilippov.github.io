gold digger

noun
person who digs or pans for gold
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊld ˌdɪɡər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊld ˌdɪɡə / (BrE)
a person who dig or pans for gold, typically in a gold field

Examples

A gold digger in California
During the gold rush, thousands of gold diggers flocked to California in search of fortune.
Many gold diggers lived in makeshift camps near the mining sites.
The veteran gold digger taught newcomers how to pan the river for nuggets.
