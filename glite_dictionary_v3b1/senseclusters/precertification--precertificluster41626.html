precertification

noun
provisional certification
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˌsɜːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːˌsɜːtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
a provisional certification granted before full approval, allowing limited use or testing while final certification is pending

Examples

Precertification testing phase
The new software underwent precertification before being released to a small group of users.
Hospitals often require precertification from insurance companies before certain procedures are performed.
The company received precertification to test its product in select markets while awaiting final approval.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authorization - official permission or approval to do something
  • degree higher: certification - official approval or confirmation of something
