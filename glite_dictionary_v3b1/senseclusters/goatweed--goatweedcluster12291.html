goatweed

noun
plant name
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊtˌwiːd / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊtˌwiːd / (BrE)
a common name for several plants, especially species in the genera Croton or Euphorbia, sometimes used in traditional medicine
