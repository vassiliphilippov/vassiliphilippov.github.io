ferret

noun
domesticated mammal used for hunting rodents
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛrət / (AmE), / ˈfɛrɪt /; / ˈfɛrət / (BrE)
a small mammal of the weasel family, often domesticated or found in the wild

Examples

A pet ferret

Conservation programs are working hard to save the prairie ferret from extinction.
Researchers tracked the movements of several ferrets released into the wild.
The black-footed ferret is one of the most endangered mammals in North America.
