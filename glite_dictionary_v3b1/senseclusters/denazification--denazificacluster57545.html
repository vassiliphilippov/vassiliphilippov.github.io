denazification

noun
removal of Nazis and Nazi ideology
9.5
Pronunciation: / diˌnɑtsəfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / diˌnɑːtsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
The process of removing Nazis from positions of authority and influence, and eradicating Nazi ideology and practices from public life.

Examples

Denazification policies
Denazification policies were enforced by the Allies in Germany after World War II.
Historians debate the overall effectiveness of denazification efforts in post-war Europe.
The process of denazification was met with resistance in some regions.
