erythematosus

adjective
relating to skin redness
Pronunciation: / ˌɛrɪˌθɛməˈtoʊsəs /; / ˌɛrɪθəˈmeɪtəsəs / (AmE), / ˌɛrɪˌθiːməˈtəʊsəs /; / ˌɛrɪθəˈmætəsəs / (BrE)
reddened or inflamed, especially describing skin or tissue affected by redness (erythema)

Examples

Related Terms

  • synonym: erythematous - showing or marked by redness of the skin
  • synonym: inflamed - red, swollen, and often painful due to infection or injury
  • antonym: pale - having little color or lacking redness
