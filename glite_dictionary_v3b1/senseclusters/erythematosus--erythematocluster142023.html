erythematosus

adjective
relating to skin redness
9.9
Pronunciation: /ə.rɪ.θə.ˈmæt.ə.səs/ (AmE), /ə.rɪ.θə.ˈmæt.ə.səs/ (BrE)
reddened or inflamed, especially describing skin or tissue affected by redness (erythema)

Examples

Erythematosus rash
The doctor noted an erythematosus rash on the patient's arms during the examination.
Systemic lupus erythematosus is a chronic autoimmune disease affecting many organs.
The skin appeared erythematosus after exposure to the allergen.
The dermatologist noted several erythematosus areas on the patient's cheeks.
A rash with erythematosus features can indicate an underlying autoimmune condition.
The nurse monitored the erythematosus patches for any signs of infection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inflamed - red, swollen, and often painful due to infection or injury
  • antonym: pale - having little color or lacking redness
