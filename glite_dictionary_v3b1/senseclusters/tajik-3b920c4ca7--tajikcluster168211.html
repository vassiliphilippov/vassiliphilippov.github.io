Tajik

noun
Iranian language of Tajikistan
Pronunciation: / tɑːˈdʒik / (AmE), / tæˈdʒiːk / (BrE)
the Iranian language spoken mainly in Tajikistan and parts of Iran, closely related to Farsi

Examples

Tajik language
Many schools in Tajikistan teach their classes in Tajik rather than Russian.
She is fluent in both Tajik and Russian, making her an excellent interpreter.
The official documents were translated from Tajik into English for the conference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Farsi - the Persian language, especially as spoken in Iran
