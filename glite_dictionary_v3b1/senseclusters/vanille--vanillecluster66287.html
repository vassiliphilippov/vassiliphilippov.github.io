vanille

noun
vanilla plant or pod
8.6
Pronunciation: / væˈniː / (AmE), / vaˈniː / (BrE)
the orchid plant or its pod that produces the aromatic substance used as a spice, commonly known as vanilla.

Examples

Vanille pod
The chef added a vanille pod to the custard for a rich flavor.
She bought dried vanille beans at the market to use in her baking.
Farmers in Madagascar carefully harvest vanille to export around the world.
