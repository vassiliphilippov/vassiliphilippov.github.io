ovalize

verb
to become oval
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəlaɪz / (BrE)
to become oval in shape or to make something take on an oval form

Examples

To ovalize metal
If the metal tube is squeezed too hard, it may ovalize and lose its original shape.
Engineers check that the structure does not ovalize under heavy loads.
Repeated pressure can ovalize the opening of the pipe over time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deform - to change shape, often in a way that is not desired
  • antonym: round - to make or become round in shape
