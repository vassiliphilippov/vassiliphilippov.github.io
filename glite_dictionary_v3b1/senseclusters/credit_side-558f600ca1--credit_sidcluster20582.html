credit side

noun
right side of a financial statement (credits)
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛdɪt saɪd / (AmE), / ˈkrɛdɪt saɪd / (BrE)
the side of a financial statement or account that records payments received or amounts credited

Examples

The credit side of account
If the credit side exceeds the debit side, the account has a credit balance.
Revenue from product sales appears on the credit side of the company's balance sheet.
The accountant carefully checked each entry on the credit side of the ledger.

Related Terms

  • antonym: debit side - the side of a financial statement that records payments made or amounts debited
