kudo

verb
to praise informally
8.4 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàkuÀêdo ä / (AmE), / ÀàkjuÀêd…ô ä / (BrE)
to give praise or public approval to someone, especially in an informal way

Examples

To kudo a friend
Many fans kudo their favorite artists online after a great concert.
She wanted to kudo her colleague for the excellent presentation at the meeting.
People often kudo athletes on social media for their achievements.

Synonyms

  • praise
    1.0 20/m
    praise means saying you admire someone's actions in many situations, while kudo is informal public praise used in casual talk.

  • antonym: criticize - to express disapproval or point out faults
  • synonym: praise - to express approval or admiration for someone
