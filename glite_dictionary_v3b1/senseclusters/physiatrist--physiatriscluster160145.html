physiatrist

noun
rehabilitation doctor
8.7
Pronunciation: /fɪ.ˈziː.ə.trɪst/ /; / /fɪ.ˈzɪə.trɪst/ (AmE), /fɪ.ˈziː.ə.trɪst/ /; / /fɪ.ˈzɪə.trɪst/ (BrE)

Examples

A physiatrist often works with physical therapists to support patient recovery.
