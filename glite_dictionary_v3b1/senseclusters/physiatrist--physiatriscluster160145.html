physiatrist

noun
rehabilitation doctor
8.7
Pronunciation: / fɪziˈætrɪst / (AmE), / fɪziˈætrɪst / (BrE)
a medical doctor who specializes in physical medicine and rehabilitation, helping patients recover physical function after injury or illness

Examples

A physiatrist doctor
After her stroke, the physiatrist designed a therapy plan to help her regain mobility.
The hospital hired a new physiatrist to lead the rehabilitation department.
A physiatrist often works with physical therapists to support patient recovery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: orthopedist - a doctor specializing in the musculoskeletal system
  • synonym: rehabilitation physician - a doctor specializing in physical rehabilitation
