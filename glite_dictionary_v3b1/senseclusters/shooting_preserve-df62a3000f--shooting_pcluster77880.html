shooting preserve

noun
area for regulated hunting
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃuːtɪŋ prɪˌzɜrv / (AmE), / ˈʃuːtɪŋ prɪˌzɜːv / (BrE)
An area of land where hunting game is legally permitted during specific times of the year.

Examples

A shooting preserve area
Many shooting preserves require visitors to obtain permits before participating in hunts.
The estate was converted into a shooting preserve where guests could hunt pheasants in autumn.
The local community debated opening a new shooting preserve to attract more tourists.
