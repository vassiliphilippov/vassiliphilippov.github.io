earflap

noun
flap on a hat to protect the ears
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɪrˌflæp / (AmE), / ˈɪəˌflæp / (BrE)
one of two pieces of material attached to the sides of a cap or hat, designed to cover and protect the ears, especially in cold weather

Examples

A earflap on a hat
Most aviator caps have earflaps for extra protection against the wind.
She tied the earflaps under her chin before stepping outside.
The hat's earflaps kept my ears warm during the winter hike.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flap - a piece of material that hangs loosely, often attached on one side
