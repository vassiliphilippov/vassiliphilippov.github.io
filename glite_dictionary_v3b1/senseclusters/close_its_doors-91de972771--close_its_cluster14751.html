close its doors

verb
cease business operations permanently
4.0 0.75/m
Pronunciation: / klo äz …™ts d…îrz / (AmE), / kl…ô äz …™ts d…îÀêz / (BrE)
(of a business or organization) to permanently cease operations or go out of business.

Examples

To close its doors permanently
After 50 years in business, the bakery was forced to close its doors due to rising costs.
Several small theaters had to close their doors during the economic downturn.
The tech startup announced it would close its doors by the end of the month.
