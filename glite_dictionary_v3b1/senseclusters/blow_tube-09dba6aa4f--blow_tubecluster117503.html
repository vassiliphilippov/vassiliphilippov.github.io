blow tube

noun
tube for shooting darts by blowing
6.1
Pronunciation: / bloʊ tuːb / (AmE), / bləʊ tjuːb / (BrE)
A tube used to shoot darts or other projectiles by blowing air through it.

Examples

Dart blow tube
He made a blow tube from a plastic straw to shoot paper darts.
Kids used a homemade blow tube to launch tiny projectiles across the room.
The indigenous tribe demonstrated how to hunt with a blow tube.
