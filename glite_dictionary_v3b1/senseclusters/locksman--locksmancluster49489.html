locksman

noun
canal lock operator
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑksmən / (AmE), / ˈlɒksmən / (BrE)
a person responsible for operating and maintaining the locks on a canal or waterway

Examples

The locksman opens gates
The locksman carefully monitored the water levels before opening the gates for the barge.
As the boat approached, the locksman signaled to the crew to prepare for entry.
During the busy summer months, the locksman works long hours to assist passing vessels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lockkeeper - a person who manages and operates a canal lock
