Synonyms

  • substitutable
    5.9 0.09/m
    substitutable can be used in the same role without changing results, while replaceable simply means it can be swapped for another.
  • interchangeable
    4.8 2.96/m
    interchangeable refers to two items working equally well in place of each other, while replaceable only means one can be changed.
  • swappable
    3.9 0.04/m
    swappable is an informal word for items you can swap in casual settings, while replaceable is the standard neutral adjective.
