miswire

verb
to connect incorrectly
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈwaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈwaɪə / (BrE)
to connect wires or systems incorrectly, causing malfunction or error

Examples

To miswire a circuit
If you miswire the circuit, the device may not work properly or could be damaged.
Early trauma can miswire a child's brain, affecting emotional development.
The technician accidentally miswired the panel, causing the lights to flicker.
If you miswire the circuit, the device will not work properly.
The technician accidentally miswired the panel, causing a short circuit.
Be careful not to miswire the connections when installing the new light fixture.
