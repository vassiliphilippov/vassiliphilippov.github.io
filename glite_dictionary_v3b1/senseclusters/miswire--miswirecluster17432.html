miswire

verb
to connect incorrectly
8.1
Pronunciation: /mɪs.ˈwaɪ.ər/ /; / /mɪs.ˈwaɪr/ (AmE), /mɪs.ˈwaɪə/ (BrE)

Examples

To miswire a circuit
If you miswire the circuit, the device may not work properly or could be damaged.
