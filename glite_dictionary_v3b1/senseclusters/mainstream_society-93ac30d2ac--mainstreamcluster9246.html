mainstream society

noun
dominant group and norms in society
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪnˌstriːm səˈsaɪəti / (AmE), / ˈmeɪnstriːm səˈsaɪəti / (BrE)
the dominant cultural group and social norms that are accepted by the majority of people within a society

Examples

Mainstream society values
After years of living abroad, she found it difficult to reconnect with mainstream society.
His unconventional beliefs set him apart from mainstream society.
Many artists challenge the values of mainstream society in their work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: counterculture - a culture with values and norms opposed to those of mainstream society
