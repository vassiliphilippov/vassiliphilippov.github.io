its

pronoun
possessive of 'it'
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪts / (AmE), / ɪts / (BrE)
the possessive form of 'it', used to show that something belongs to or is related to a thing, animal, or idea previously mentioned

Examples

Its meaning
The company changed its logo to attract more customers.
After the storm, the tree lost many of its branches.
The cat licked its paw while sitting in the sun.
