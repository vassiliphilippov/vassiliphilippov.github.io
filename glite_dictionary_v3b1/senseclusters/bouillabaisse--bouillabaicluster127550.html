bouillabaisse

noun
Mediterranean fish stew
Pronunciation: / ˌbuːjəˈbeɪs / (AmE), / ˌbuːjəˈbɛs / (BrE)
a highly seasoned Mediterranean soup or stew made with various kinds of fish and shellfish, typically cooked with tomatoes, onions or leeks, flavored with saffron, garlic, and herbs

Examples

Traditional bouillabaisse soup
His grandmother taught him how to prepare traditional bouillabaisse using fresh seafood from the market.
She ordered the bouillabaisse at the French restaurant, eager to try the authentic flavors of Marseille.
The rich aroma of saffron filled the kitchen as the bouillabaisse simmered on the stove.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fish stew - a dish of fish cooked in seasoned liquid
