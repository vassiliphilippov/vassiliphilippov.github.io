bichon

noun
small curly-haired dog
Pronunciation: / biˈʃɑn / (AmE), / biˈʃɒn / (BrE)
a small breed of dog with a curly white coat, known for being friendly and kept as a pet

Examples

A bichon dog
The bichon greeted everyone at the door with a wagging tail and a happy bark.
After its bath, the bichon's white fur looked especially fluffy and clean.
Many families choose a bichon because of its gentle nature and playful personality.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bulldog - a medium-sized dog breed with a muscular build and wrinkled face
  • synonym: poodle - a breed of dog with a curly coat, often intelligent and active
