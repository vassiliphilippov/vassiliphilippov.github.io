importance

noun
quality of being significant or notable
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɔrtəns / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɔːtəns / (BrE)
the quality of being significant, valuable, or having a high position or influence

Examples

The importance of health
Many people underestimate the importance of regular exercise for good health.
She realized the importance of spending time with her family.
The CEO stressed the importance of meeting the project deadline.
She enjoyed her sudden importance among the guests at the banquet.
The ambassador's arrival was an event of great importance.
The company president is a person of considerable importance within the industry.

Related Terms

  • antonym: obscurity - the state of being unknown or unimportant
  • synonym: prominence - the state of being important or famous
  • synonym: significance - the quality of being worthy of attention; importance
  • antonym: triviality - the quality of being trivial or unimportant
