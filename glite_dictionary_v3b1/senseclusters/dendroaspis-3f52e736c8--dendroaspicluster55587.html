Dendroaspis

noun
genus of venomous African snakes (mambas)
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛndroʊˈæspɪs / (AmE), / ˌdɛndrəʊˈaspɪs / (BrE)
a genus of large, highly venomous snakes known as mambas, native to Africa
