about

adverb
approximately; nearly (not exact)
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
used to show that a number, amount, or time is not exact; approximately or nearly

Examples

About ten minutes
My watch says it's about two o'clock.
The repairs will cost about $100, give or take.
There were about fifty people at the wedding.
It’s about finished, just a few more touches are needed.
She was about to leave when the phone rang.
The dinner is about ready, so please set the table.

Related Terms

  • synonym: almost - very nearly, not quite
  • synonym: approximately - used to show that something is almost, but not completely, accurate or exact
  • antonym: exactly - precisely; in an exact manner
  • synonym: nearly - close to but not completely
