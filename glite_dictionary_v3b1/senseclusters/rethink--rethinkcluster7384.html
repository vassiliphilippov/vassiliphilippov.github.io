rethink

verb
to reconsider or change one's mind
2.7 2.9
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈθɪŋk/ /; / /rɪ.ˈθɪŋk/ (AmE), /riː.ˈθɪŋk/ /; / /rɪ.ˈθɪŋk/ (BrE)
