granuloma venereum

noun
sexually transmitted ulcerative disease
9.9
Pronunciation: /græn.jə.ˈloʊ.mə və.ˈnɪr.iː.əm/ (AmE), /græn.jə.ˈləʊ.mə və.ˈnɪə.riː.əm/ (BrE)
A rare sexually transmitted disease caused by bacteria of the genus Calymmatobacterium, marked by ulcerative lesions in the genital and groin area.

Examples

Granuloma venereum infection
He experienced sores in the groin area that were later confirmed as granuloma venereum.
Proper diagnosis of granuloma venereum requires laboratory testing to identify the bacteria.
The clinic reported several new cases of granuloma venereum last year.
