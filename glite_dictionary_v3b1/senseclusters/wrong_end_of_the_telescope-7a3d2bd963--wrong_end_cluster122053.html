wrong end of the telescope

noun
distorted or misguided perspective
6.8 7.5
Pronunciation: /rɔːŋ ɛnd əv ðə ˈtel.ə.skoʊp/ (AmE), /rɒŋ ɛnd əv ðə ˈtel.ɪ.skəʊp/ (BrE)
