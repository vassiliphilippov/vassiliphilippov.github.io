pomological

adjective
fruit science
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊməˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌpɒməˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study or science of fruit and fruit trees

Examples

Pomological study
The university offers a pomological course for students interested in fruit cultivation.
Her pomological research focuses on improving the quality of citrus fruits.
A pomological survey was conducted to document the region's apple varieties.
