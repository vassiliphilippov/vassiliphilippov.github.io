cigaret

noun
thin tobacco roll for smoking
Pronunciation: / sɪgəˈrɛt / (AmE), / sɪgəˈrɛt / (BrE)
A thin roll of finely ground tobacco wrapped in paper for smoking.

Examples

A cigaret pack
