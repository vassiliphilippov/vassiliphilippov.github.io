shooty

adjective
full of shoots
Pronunciation: / ˈʃuːti / (AmE), / ˈʃuːti / (BrE)
producing or covered with shoots, especially new stems or growth in plants

Examples

Shooty plant stems
The shooty branches made the young tree look especially healthy this spring.
After a week of rain, the garden became noticeably more shooty and green.
Pruning encourages roses to become shooty with lots of new stems.

  • synonym: sprouting - beginning to grow new shoots or leaves
