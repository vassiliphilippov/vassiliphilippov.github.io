scarlet haw

noun
shrub or tree with red berries in eastern US
Pronunciation: / ˈskɑːrlət hɔ / (AmE), / ˈskɑːlɪt hɔː / (BrE)
a shrub or small tree of the eastern United States with few thorns, white flower clusters, and bright orange-red berries
