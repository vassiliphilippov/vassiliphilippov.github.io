juicy

adjective
lucrative; offering large rewards
5.3 0.61/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd íusi / (AmE), / Ààd íuÀêsi / (BrE)
(of an offer, contract, or opportunity) very lucrative or offering large rewards.

Examples

Juicy job offer
It's a juicy assignment that everyone in the office wants.
She was offered a juicy contract to join the rival company.
They secured a juicy deal that will boost their revenues for years.

Synonyms
