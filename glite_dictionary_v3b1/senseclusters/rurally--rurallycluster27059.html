rurally

adverb
in a countryside way
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈrʊrəli / (AmE), / ˈrʊərəli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to or is characteristic of the countryside rather than the city

Examples

Living rurally
The family chose to live rurally to enjoy a quieter and more peaceful environment.
Many traditions are still preserved in communities that are organized rurally.
She grew up rurally, surrounded by fields and forests far from the city.

Related Terms

  • antonym: urbanely - in a manner characteristic of a city or town
