cross-section

adjective
plane cut at right angles; its shape
4.0 4.3
Pronunciation: /krɔːs ˈsek.ʃən/ /; / /krɑːs ˈsek.ʃən/ (AmE), /krɒs ˈsek.ʃən/ (BrE)
representing or relating to a plane made by cutting across something at right angles to its length, or the shape revealed by such a cut

Examples

A cross-section view
A cross-section view reveals the layers within the tree trunk.

The textbook included detailed cross-section diagrams of the human brain.
