serve someone right

verb
to get a deserved consequence
Pronunciation: / sɝv ˌsʌmwʌn ˈraɪt / (AmE), / sɜːv ˌsʌmwʌn ˈraɪt / (BrE)
(of a consequence) to be justly deserved by someone, typically because of their actions or behavior.

Examples

It would serve him right
It would serve him right if the plan failed after all his boasting.
Losing the match served him right after he cheated in the last round.
Some think it serves her right for breaking the rules so many times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deserve - to be worthy of something, especially a punishment or reward
