zag

noun
sharp angular turn
7.4
Pronunciation: / zæɡ / (AmE), / zæɡ / (BrE)
A sharp, angular turn, especially as one of a series in alternating directions.

Examples

A sharp zag
After every zig, there was a matching zag along the fence.
Each zag in the mountain trail made the hike more challenging.
The path had a sudden zag that caught him off guard.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: zig - a sharp turn in the opposite direction, often paired with 'zag' in 'zigzag'
