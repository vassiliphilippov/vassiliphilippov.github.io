homeroom

noun
classroom for administrative meetings in school
2.8 1.11/m
Pronunciation: / Ààho ämÀåruÀêm / (AmE), / Ààh…ô ämÀåruÀêm / (BrE)
a classroom where students in a particular grade or group meet regularly for administrative purposes, usually at the start of the school day, under the supervision of a designated teacher

Examples

The homeroom class
At 8:00 a.m., all students must report to their homeroom for attendance.
Every Monday, the homeroom teacher distributes weekly announcements and permission slips.
She decorated her homeroom with colorful posters for her new class.

Synonyms

  • home room

    home room appears as two words in some older texts or informal notes, while homeroom is the single-word term schools use today.
