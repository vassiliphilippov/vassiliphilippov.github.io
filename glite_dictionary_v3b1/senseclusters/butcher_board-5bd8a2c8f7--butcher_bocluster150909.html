butcher board

noun
thick hardwood slab for work surfaces
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊtʃər bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbʊtʃə bɔːd / (BrE)
a thick slab made by bonding together laminated strips of unpainted hardwood, used as a durable work surface

Examples

A butcher board surface
Always clean your butcher board thoroughly to maintain its quality and hygiene.
He refinished the old butcher board to use as a cutting surface in his workshop.
The kitchen island featured a large butcher board countertop for meal preparation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: butcher block - a type of thick wooden slab traditionally used by butchers as a cutting surface
