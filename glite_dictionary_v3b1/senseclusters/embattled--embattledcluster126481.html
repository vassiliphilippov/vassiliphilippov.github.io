embattled

adjective
having battlement-like indentations
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbætəld / (AmE), / ɪmˈbatəld / (BrE)
(of a structure or object) having repeated square indentations resembling those in a battlement; crenellated.

Examples

Embattled walls
The ancient castle had embattled walls overlooking the valley.
The designer added an embattled cornice to give the house a medieval appearance.
The town hall features an embattled parapet as part of its gothic revival style.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crenellated - having a notched or indented pattern like a battlement
