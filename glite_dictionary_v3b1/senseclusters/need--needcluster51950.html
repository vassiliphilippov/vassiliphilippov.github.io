Synonyms

  • require
    1.1 140/m
    require is more formal and often used for rules or official situations, while need covers general necessary or important things.
  • demand
    2.1 14/m
    demand is stronger and shows force or expectation, while need is neutral for something necessary.
  • call for
    3.2 17/m
    call for is a phrase saying a situation demands a response or quality, while need names the necessary thing.
  • take
    1.0 200/m
    take is used in everyday speech to say how much time or effort something requires, while need is general.
  • want
    5.8 140/m
    want normally means to desire something, while need means something is necessary or important and not just wanted.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lack - to be without or deficient in
  • synonym: require - to need something for a particular purpose
  • degree lower: want - to wish for something, often less strictly necessary than 'need'
