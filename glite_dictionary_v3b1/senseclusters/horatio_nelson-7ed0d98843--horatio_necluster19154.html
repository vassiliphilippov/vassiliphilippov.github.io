Horatio Nelson

proper noun
famous English naval admiral (1758–1805)
Pronunciation: / həˈreɪʃioʊ ˈnɛlsən / (AmE), / həˈreɪʃiəʊ ˈnɛlsən / (BrE)
(1758–1805) English admiral renowned for his victories against the French during the Napoleonic Wars and who died at the Battle of Trafalgar.

Examples

Horatio Nelson naval hero
Horatio Nelson is celebrated as one of Britain's greatest naval heroes for his leadership at Trafalgar.
Books about Horatio Nelson often describe his daring tactics and brilliant naval strategies.
The statue of Horatio Nelson in Trafalgar Square honors his memory and achievements.
