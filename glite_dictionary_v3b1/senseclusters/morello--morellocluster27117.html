morello

noun
variety of sour cherry tree
9.9
Pronunciation: / məˈrɛloʊ / (AmE), / məˈrɛləʊ / (BrE)
A variety of cultivated sour cherry tree bearing fruit with dark skin and juice.

Examples

Morello cherry tree
The morello is prized by bakers for its tart, richly colored fruit.
Unlike sweet cherries, the morello is often used for preserves and jams.
We planted a morello in our garden to enjoy homemade cherry pies.
