Synonyms

  • faraway
    2.8 2.73/m
    faraway feels like something seen or imagined far away in space or time, while far-off stresses great actual distance.
  • distant
    2.8 24/m
    distant refers to things physically far away or not close in feeling, while far-off highlights very great space or time distance.
  • remote
    3.6 20/m
    remote describes places far from people or services and quiet, while far-off simply means very distant in space or time.
  • far-flung
    6.3 1.91/m
    far-flung describes things spread widely across distant places, while far-off means single points or times that are very distant.
