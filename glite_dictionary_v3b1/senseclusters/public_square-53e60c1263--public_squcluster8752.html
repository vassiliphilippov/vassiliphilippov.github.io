public square

noun
an open meeting area in a town or city
3.0 2.1
Pronunciation: /ˈpʌb.lɪk skwer/ /; / /ˈpʌb.lɪk skweər/ (AmE), /ˈpʌb.lɪk skweə/ (BrE)
