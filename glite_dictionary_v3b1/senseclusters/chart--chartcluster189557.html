chart

verb
plan or map out in detail
Pronunciation: / tʃɑɹt /; / tʃɑrt / (AmE), / tʃɑːt / (BrE)
to make a chart, map, or detailed plan of something; to record or organize information visually or strategically

Examples

The scientists are charting the spread of the disease across the region.
