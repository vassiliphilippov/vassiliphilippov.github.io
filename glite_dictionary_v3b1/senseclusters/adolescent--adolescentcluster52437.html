(of behaviour or attitudes) displaying or suggesting a lack of maturity; immature or childish.

Examples

Adolescent behavior
His adolescent response to criticism made resolving the issue difficult.
She regretted her adolescent outburst after reflecting on the situation.
The film was filled with adolescent humor that appealed mostly to teenagers.

Synonyms

  • immature
    3.5 4.45/m
    immature describes behavior that lacks emotional growth and self-control, acting without thought, while adolescent suggests childish or underdeveloped attitudes.
  • childish
    2.8 7.22/m
    childish shows behavior like tantrums or silly games in adults, while adolescent labels immature attitudes tied to teenage years.
  • infantile
    6.5 1.07/m
    infantile means very baby-like and extreme child behavior, while adolescent means immature but often linked to teenage development.
  • puerile
    9.1 0.58/m
    puerile sounds formal and strongly negative, meaning silly or stupid, while adolescent is a more neutral word for immature behavior.
  • juvenile
    4.2 3.40/m
    juvenile can simply mean relating to youth or law, while adolescent focuses on immature behavior or childish attitudes.

  • antonym: mature - fully developed; showing adult qualities
  • synonym: puerile - childishly silly or trivial; immature
