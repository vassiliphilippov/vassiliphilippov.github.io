adolescent

adjective
immature or childish
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌædəˈlɛsənt / (AmE), / ˌadəˈlɛsənt / (BrE)
(of behaviour or attitudes) displaying or suggesting a lack of maturity; immature or childish.

Examples

Adolescent behavior
His adolescent response to criticism made resolving the issue difficult.
She regretted her adolescent outburst after reflecting on the situation.
The film was filled with adolescent humor that appealed mostly to teenagers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: juvenile - immature or childish
  • antonym: mature - fully developed; showing adult qualities
  • synonym: puerile - childishly silly or trivial; immature
