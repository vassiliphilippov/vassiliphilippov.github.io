introitus

noun
opening to a bodily canal or organ
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtroʊɪtəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈtrəʊɪtəs / (BrE)
the entrance or opening to a canal or hollow organ in the body, especially the opening of the vagina

Examples

Vaginal introitus
Proper hygiene of the vaginal introitus is important to prevent infection.
Scarring around the introitus may cause discomfort during intercourse.
The gynecologist examined the introitus for signs of inflammation.
