Araucaria excelsa

noun
evergreen tree of Australia and Norfolk Island
Pronunciation: / əˌraʊˈkɛəriə ɛkˈsɛlsə / (AmE), / ˌarɔːˈkɛːrɪə ɪkˈsɛlsə / (BrE)
An evergreen tree native to Australia and Norfolk Island in the South Pacific, also known as the Norfolk Island pine, often grown as an ornamental plant.

Examples

A potted Araucaria excelsa
Many people keep a potted Araucaria excelsa as a decorative houseplant.
The Araucaria excelsa can grow to impressive heights when planted outdoors.
The unique, symmetrical shape of the Araucaria excelsa makes it popular during the holiday season.
