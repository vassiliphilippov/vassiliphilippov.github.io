epenthetic

adjective
relating to the insertion of sounds in words
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpənˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛpɛnˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
(of a sound or letter) inserted into a word, or relating to epenthesis.
