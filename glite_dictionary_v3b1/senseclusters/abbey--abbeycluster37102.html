abbey

noun
monastery or convent ruled by an abbot or abbess
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈæbi / (AmE), / ˈæbi / (BrE)
a large religious building or complex, such as a monastery or convent, often including a church and governed by an abbot or abbess
