clean and jerk

noun
weightlifting movement with barbell
9.9 3.4
Pronunciation: /kliːn ənd dʒɜːrk/ /; / /kliːn ænd dʒɜːrk/ (AmE), /kliːn ənd dʒɜːk/ /; / /kliːn ænd dʒɜːk/ (BrE)
a weightlifting movement in which a barbell is lifted to shoulder height (the clean) and then thrust overhead in one motion (the jerk)
