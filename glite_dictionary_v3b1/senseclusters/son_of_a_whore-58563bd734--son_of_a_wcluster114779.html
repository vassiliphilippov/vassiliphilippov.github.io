son of a whore

noun
highly offensive insult; contemptible person
9.9
Pronunciation: / sʌn əv ə hɔɹ / (AmE), / sʌn əv ə hɔː / (BrE)
An extremely offensive and vulgar insult directed at a person regarded as dishonorable, irritating, or contemptible.

Examples

Call someone son of a whore
He called me a son of a whore during the argument, which shocked everyone in the room.
If you cross him, you'll hear every insult in the book, including son of a whore.
Nobody wants to work with that son of a whore after what he did to the team.
