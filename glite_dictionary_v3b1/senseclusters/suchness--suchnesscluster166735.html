suchness

noun
essential nature
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌtʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈsʌtʃnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being just as it is; the essential nature of something

Examples

The suchness of reality
The philosopher discussed the suchness of reality in his lecture.
Meditation helps some people appreciate the pure suchness of each moment.
Artists often try to capture the suchness of their subjects in their work.
