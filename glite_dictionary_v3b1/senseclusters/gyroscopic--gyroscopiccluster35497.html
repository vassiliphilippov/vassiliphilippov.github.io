gyroscopic

adjective
relating to a gyroscope
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒaɪrəˈskɑːpɪk / (AmE), / ˌdʒaɪrəˈskɒpɪk / (BrE)
relating to or having the characteristics of a gyroscope, especially in maintaining orientation or stability.

Examples

Gyroscopic technology
Modern smartphones use gyroscopic technology to detect changes in orientation.
The drone's gyroscopic sensors help it maintain balance even in strong winds.
The engineer checked the gyroscopic stability of the navigation system before launch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gyroscope - a device for measuring or maintaining orientation
