pieceworker

noun
worker paid per task
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːswɜrkər / (AmE), / ˈpiːswɜːkə / (BrE)
a person who is paid based on the amount of work or number of items completed, rather than by the hour or day

Examples

A pieceworker in factory
The factory employs several pieceworkers to assemble electronic devices quickly.
As a pieceworker, she earned more money by completing extra garments each day.
Many pieceworkers prefer the flexibility of being paid for each item they finish.
The factory hired several pieceworkers to increase production during the busy season.
As a pieceworker, she earned more by working faster and producing more garments each day.
Many pieceworkers prefer the system because it rewards efficiency and skill.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hourly worker - a person paid based on hours worked
  • synonym: piecework - work paid according to the amount produced
  • antonym: salaried worker - a person who is paid a fixed regular salary
  • synonym: wage earner - a person who earns money by working for wages
