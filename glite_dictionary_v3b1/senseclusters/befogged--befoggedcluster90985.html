befogged

adjective
physically covered with fog or condensation
6.8
Pronunciation: /bɪ.ˈfɑːgd/ /; / /bə.ˈfɑːgd/ /; / /bɪ.ˈfɔːgd/ (AmE), /bɪ.ˈfɒgd/ /; / /bə.ˈfɒgd/ /; / /bɪ.ˈfɔːgd/ (BrE)
Covered, blurred, or made cloudy by fog, steam, or condensation so that it is difficult to see through.

Examples

Befogged window
The bathroom mirror became befogged after her hot shower this morning.
He wiped the befogged windshield to see the dark road more clearly.
Windows on the train were befogged in the cold, hiding the snowy landscape outside.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - free from cloudiness, fog, or obstruction; easily seen through.
  • synonym: fogged - covered with condensation or fog so that visibility is reduced.
