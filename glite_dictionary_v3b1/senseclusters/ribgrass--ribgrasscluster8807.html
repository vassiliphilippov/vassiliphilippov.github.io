ribgrass

noun
a plantain with narrow ribbed leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪbˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈrɪbˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
an Old World plantain (Plantago lanceolata) with long, narrow ribbed leaves, widely found in temperate regions

Examples

Common ribgrass plant
Cows often graze on ribgrass because it is common in pastures.
