hydrastine

noun
alkaloid from goldenseal
Pronunciation: / haɪˈdræstiːn / (AmE), / haɪˈdræstiːn / (BrE)
a natural alkaloid compound found in the goldenseal plant, used in medicine for its potential therapeutic effects

Examples

Hydrastine compound
Researchers are studying hydrastine for its possible effects on blood pressure.
The roots of goldenseal contain both hydrastine and berberine.
Pharmaceutical companies extract hydrastine from plants for use in certain medications.
