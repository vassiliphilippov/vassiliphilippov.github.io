osmose

verb
to pass by osmosis
Pronunciation: / ɑzˈmoʊz / (AmE), / ɒzˈməʊz / (BrE)
to pass or diffuse through a membrane by the process of osmosis

Examples

To osmose through membranes
Water molecules osmose through the cell membrane to balance concentrations.
Certain nutrients can osmose into plant roots from the surrounding soil.
In biology class, we learned how gases osmose across cell walls.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absorb - to take in or soak up
  • synonym: diffuse - to spread or scatter widely
