mulligan

noun
Irish meat and vegetable stew
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌlɪɡən / (AmE), / ˈmʌlɪɡən / (BrE)
A stew, typically made with meat and vegetables, regarded as the Irish version of burgoo.

A bowl of mulligan
