pirouette

verb
turn on one foot in dance
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪr.uˈɛt / (AmE), / ˌpɪr.uˈɛt / (BrE)
to perform a full turn on the toe or ball of one foot, especially in ballet or dance.

Examples

To pirouette gracefully
As the music swelled, she pirouetted in the spotlight, drawing applause.
He pirouettes with remarkable balance during the performance.
The ballerina pirouetted gracefully across the stage.
