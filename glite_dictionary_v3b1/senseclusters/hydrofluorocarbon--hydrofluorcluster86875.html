hydrofluorocarbon

noun
compound of hydrogen, fluorine, and carbon

A compound consisting of hydrogen, fluorine, and carbon, used as a refrigerant and emitted as a by-product of industrial manufacturing.

Examples

Hydrofluorocarbon refrigerants
Many air conditioners now use hydrofluorocarbon refrigerants to reduce ozone depletion.
Regulations are being proposed to phase out certain hydrofluorocarbons due to their impact on climate change.
The company developed a new hydrofluorocarbon blend that is less harmful to the environment.
