treater

noun
person giving treats
Pronunciation: / ˈtriːtər / (AmE), / ˈtriːtə / (BrE)
a person who gives treats, especially during celebrations or holidays

Examples

Halloween treater
On Halloween night, each treater stood by the door with a bowl of candy.
The generous treater surprised everyone at the office with homemade cookies.
During the festival, every child hoped to meet a friendly treater handing out sweets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: giver - a person who gives something to others
  • antonym: recipient - a person who receives something
