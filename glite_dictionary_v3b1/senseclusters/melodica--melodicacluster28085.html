melodica

noun
blown keyboard instrument
7.3
Pronunciation: / məˈlɑdɪkə / (AmE), / məˈlɒdɪkə / (BrE)
a small keyboard instrument that is played by blowing air through a mouthpiece while pressing keys

Examples

A melodica instrument
The music teacher introduced the melodica to the class as a fun way to learn melodies.
He carried his melodica to the park and played songs for his friends.
During the concert, the band featured a solo on the melodica that delighted the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harmonica - a small musical instrument played by blowing air through reeds
