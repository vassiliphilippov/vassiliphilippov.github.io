upcut

adjective
cutting upward
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpkʌt / (AmE), / ˈʌpkʌt / (BrE)
describing a tool, blade, or cutting action that moves chips or material upward as it cuts

Examples

Upcut blade
An upcut blade helps remove sawdust from the groove as you work.
For clean edges on the bottom of the board, use an upcut router bit.
The technician recommended an upcut tool for better chip evacuation during milling.

Related Terms

  • antonym: downcut - describing a tool or action that directs material downward while cutting
