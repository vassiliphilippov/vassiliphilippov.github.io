turpentine

noun
oleoresin from pines, used as solvent
7.6 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈtɜːr.pən.taɪn/ (AmE), /ˈtɜː.pən.taɪn/ (BrE)
a substance from pine trees, used as a solvent, paint thinner, or in making varnishes

Examples

Natural turpentine solvent
Some varnishes are made with natural turpentine as a key ingredient.
