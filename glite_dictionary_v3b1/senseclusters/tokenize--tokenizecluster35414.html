tokenize

verb
to create digital token
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊkəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈtəʊkənaɪz / (BrE)
to replace sensitive information with a digital token for secure storage, authentication, or processing, especially in computing or finance

Examples

The company decided to tokenize all sensitive data before storing it in the cloud.
After you tokenize the information, only the token is used for future transactions.
