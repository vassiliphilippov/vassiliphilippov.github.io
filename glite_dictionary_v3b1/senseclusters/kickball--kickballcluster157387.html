kickball

noun
ball game like baseball
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪkˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈkɪkˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a game similar to baseball in which players kick a rubber ball instead of hitting it with a bat

Examples

A game of kickball
Every Friday, the students gather on the field to play kickball after school.
She joined a local kickball league to meet new people and stay active.
The rules of kickball are easy to learn, making it popular with children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baseball - a bat-and-ball game played between two teams
  • synonym: soccer - a game played by kicking a ball to score goals
