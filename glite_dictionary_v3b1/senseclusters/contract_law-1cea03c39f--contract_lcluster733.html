contract law

noun
area of law dealing with contracts
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑnˌtrækt lɔ / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.trækt lɔː / (BrE)
The area of law that deals with the rights and obligations of parties who enter into contracts.
