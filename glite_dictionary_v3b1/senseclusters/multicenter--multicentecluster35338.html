multicenter

noun
study at multiple sites
5.3
Pronunciation: /mʌl.tɪ.ˈsen.tər/ /; / /ˈmʌl.tɪ.sen.tər/ (AmE), /mʌl.tɪ.ˈsen.tə/ /; / /ˈmʌl.tɪ.sen.tə/ (BrE)
a study, trial, or project conducted at several different centers or institutions simultaneously

Examples

A multicenter trial

Researchers designed a multicenter to compare treatment outcomes across various regions.
Funding for the multicenter was provided by several international health organizations.
