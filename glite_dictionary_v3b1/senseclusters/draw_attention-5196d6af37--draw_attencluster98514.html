draw attention

verb
attract notice or interest
Pronunciation: / drɔ ətɛnʃən / (AmE), / drɔː əˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
to make people notice something or someone; to attract notice or interest

Examples

To draw attention quickly
Her unusual hairstyle always seemed to draw attention wherever she went.
The artist hoped his mural would draw attention to environmental issues.
The politician tried to draw attention away from the scandal by making a new announcement.
