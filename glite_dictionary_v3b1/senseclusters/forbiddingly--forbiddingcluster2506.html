forbiddingly

adverb
in a menacing or unfriendly manner
8.6
Pronunciation: / fərˈbɪdɪŋli / (AmE), / fəˈbɪdɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that is threatening, unfriendly, or creating a sense of danger or unwelcomeness.

Examples

Looked forbiddingly
She looked at us forbiddingly, making it clear we were not welcome.
The castle stood forbiddingly on the cliff, daring anyone to approach.
The sky grew forbiddingly dark before the storm began.

Related Terms

  • antonym: invitingly - in a friendly or welcoming manner
  • synonym: menacingly - in a threatening or intimidating manner
