cylindric

adjective
having the form of a cylinder
Pronunciation: / sɪˈlɪndrɪk / (AmE), / sɪˈlɪndrɪk / (BrE)
having the form or shape of a cylinder; cylindrical.

Examples

Cylindric shape
Some plant stems grow in a nearly cylindric shape, which aids their structural strength.
The engineer designed a cylindric tank for storing gas under pressure.
The vase on the table has a distinct cylindric profile, making it stand out among the others.
