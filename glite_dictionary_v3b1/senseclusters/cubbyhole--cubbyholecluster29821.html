cubbyhole

noun
small secluded room or compartment
9.9 5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌbiˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈkʌbiˌhəʊl /; / ˈkʌbɪˌhəʊl / (BrE)
a small, enclosed space or compartment, often used for storage, work, or as a cozy nook

Examples

A small cubbyhole
He retreated to his cubbyhole at the back of the shop to finish his paperwork.
Teachers each have a cubbyhole in the staff room where their mail is left.
The small apartment had a cubbyhole that she used as a reading nook.
Each student has a cubbyhole for their belongings in the classroom.
She tucked her keys into the cubbyhole by the door before leaving.
The post was left in a little cubbyhole outside the office.

  • synonym: niche - a shallow recess, especially one in a wall to display a statue or other ornament
  • synonym: nook - a small corner or recess, especially one offering seclusion or security
