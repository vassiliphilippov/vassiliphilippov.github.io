tufting

noun
fabric-making technique
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈtʌf.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈtʌf.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
a method of making fabric by inserting loops or clusters of yarn to create a raised texture or pattern

Examples

Decorative tufting on cushions
The handmade pillow features beautiful tufting that adds texture to the design.
Carpet manufacturers often use tufting to create soft and durable floor coverings.
She learned the art of tufting to make her own decorative wall hangings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embroidery - decorating fabric with needle and thread
  • synonym: weaving - making fabric by interlacing threads
