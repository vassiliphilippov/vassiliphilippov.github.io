left-slanting

adjective
slanting backward (handwriting)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛft ˌslæntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɛft ˌslɑːntɪŋ / (BrE)
(of handwriting) having the letters slanting backward from right to left.

Examples

Left-slanting handwriting
A left-slanting script is often associated with introverted or cautious individuals.
His signature was easily recognized by its left-slanting letters and unique loops.
The graphologist noted the left-slanting style in her handwriting sample.

Related Terms

  • antonym: right-slanting - (of handwriting) having the letters slanting forward from left to right
