intravital

adjective
within living organisms
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈvaɪtəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈvaɪtəl / (BrE)
relating to processes or observations that take place within a living organism

Examples

Intravital imaging
The scientist conducted intravital imaging to study blood flow in real time.
Advances in intravital techniques have improved our understanding of immune cell behavior.
Using intravital microscopy, researchers observed how cancer cells move within living tissue.

Related Terms

  • antonym: in vitro - performed or taking place outside a living organism
  • synonym: in vivo - performed or taking place in a living organism
