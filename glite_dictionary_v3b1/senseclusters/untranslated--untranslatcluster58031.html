untranslated

adjective
not converted to protein
Pronunciation: / ˌʌntrænzˈleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌntrænzˈleɪtɪd / (BrE)
not converted from messenger RNA into a protein, especially describing regions of RNA not used in protein synthesis

Examples

Untranslated region
The scientist analyzed the untranslated regions of the gene for regulatory elements.
Mutations in untranslated sequences can impact how efficiently a protein is produced.
Researchers focus on untranslated mRNA segments to understand gene expression control.

Related Terms

  • antonym: translated - converted from mRNA into a protein during protein synthesis
