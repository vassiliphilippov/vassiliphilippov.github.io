public speaker

noun
person who gives speeches to an audience
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪk ˈspiːkər / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk ˈspiːkə / (BrE)
a person who gives speeches or addresses to an audience, especially in public settings

Examples

A public speaker gives speeches
A good public speaker knows how to engage listeners and clearly convey a message.
After years of practice, she became a confident public speaker at conferences and seminars.
The public speaker captured the audience's attention with his powerful delivery.
