Rosidae

noun
subclass of flowering plants
9.9
Pronunciation: / roʊˈsaɪdiː / (AmE), / rəʊˈsɪdiː / (BrE)
a large group (subclass) of flowering plants, mostly trees, shrubs, and herbs with separate petals, including the rose family and many others
