butt

noun
large cask (about 126 gallons) for liquids
9.9
Pronunciation: / bʌt / (AmE), / bʌt / (BrE)
a large cask, especially one holding about 126 gallons (equivalent to two hogsheads), used chiefly for storing wine or other liquids

Examples

A butt of wine
A butt of wine was delivered to the manor each autumn.
Antique butts from the 18th century can still be found in some European wineries.
The sherry was aged for years in a massive butt deep within the cellar.
