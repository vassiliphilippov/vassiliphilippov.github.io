trammel

noun
restraint for training horses to amble
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtræməl / (AmE), / ˈtræməl / (BrE)
A device or restraint used for training a horse to amble, typically by restricting its movement.

Examples

Horse trammel
Historically, a trammel was often used in the early stages of a horse's training.
The trainer attached a trammel to the horse's legs to help it learn the amble gait.
Using a trammel can encourage smoother and more controlled movement in horses being taught to amble.
