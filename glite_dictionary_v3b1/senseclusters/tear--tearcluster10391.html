tear

verb
(of eyes) to fill with or produce tears
Pronunciation: / tɪr / (AmE), / tɪə / (BrE)
(of the eyes) to fill with tears or to produce tears, often as a response to emotion or irritation.

Examples

Eyes tear from onions
Allergy season causes many people's eyes to tear frequently.
The cold wind made my eyes tear as I walked outside.
Whenever she cuts onions, her eyes begin to tear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cry - to shed tears, typically as an expression of emotion
  • synonym: weep - to shed tears, usually as a result of emotion
