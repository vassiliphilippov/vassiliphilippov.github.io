Rudbeckia hirta

noun
a North American daisy-like flower; black-eyed Susan
8.8
Pronunciation: / ruːdˈbɛkiə ˈhɜːrtə / (AmE), / ruːdˈbɛkiə ˈhɜːtə / (BrE)
a North American flowering plant of the daisy family, with yellow to orange ray flowers and dark centers, known as the black-eyed Susan and the state flower of Maryland
