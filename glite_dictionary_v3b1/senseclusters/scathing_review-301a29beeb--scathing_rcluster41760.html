scathing review

noun
an extremely harsh and critical review
Pronunciation: / ˈskeɪðɪŋ rɪˈvju / (AmE), / ˈskeɪðɪŋ rɪˈvjuː / (BrE)
a review that is extremely harsh and critical in its evaluation

Examples

A scathing review
After reading the scathing review, the author was hesitant to work with that editor again.
Her latest play was the subject of a scathing review that criticized nearly every aspect of the production.
The film received a scathing review in the national newspaper, calling it the year's biggest disappointment.

  • antonym: favorable review - a review expressing approval or praise
