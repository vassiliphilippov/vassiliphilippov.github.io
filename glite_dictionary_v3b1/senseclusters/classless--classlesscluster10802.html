classless

adjective
not divided by social class
Pronunciation: / ˈklæs.lɪs / (AmE), / ˈklɑːs.lɪs / (BrE)
(of a society or system) not divided into social classes; characterized by or promoting social equality.

Examples

A classless society
Her ideas reflect a vision of a future that is completely classless.
Many politicians profess to support a classless education system.
They dreamed of building a truly classless society.

Related Terms

  • synonym: egalitarian - believing in or based on the principle that all people are equal
