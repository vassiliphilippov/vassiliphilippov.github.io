penologist

noun
expert in prison management theory and practice
Pronunciation: / pɛˈnɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / pɪˈnɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies or is an expert in the theory and practice of prison management

Examples

A penologist studies prisons
As a penologist, she researches the effectiveness of different rehabilitation programs in prisons.
Many penologists believe that education plays a critical role in reducing recidivism.
The prison hired a penologist to advise on new management strategies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: criminologist - a person who studies crime and criminals
