altitude

noun
height above sea or ground level
Pronunciation: / ˈæl.təˌtuːd / (AmE), / ˈæl.tɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
the height of an object or point in relation to sea level or ground level

Examples

High altitude
At this altitude, the temperature drops significantly compared to sea level.
Commercial airplanes typically fly at an altitude of around 35,000 feet.
The mountain climbers struggled with the thin air at such a high altitude.

Related Terms

  • antonym: depth - distance below surface level
  • synonym: elevation - height above a given level, especially sea level
