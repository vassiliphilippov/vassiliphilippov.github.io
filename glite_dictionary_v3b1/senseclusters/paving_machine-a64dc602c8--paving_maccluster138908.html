paving machine

noun
machine for laying pavement
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪvɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈpeɪvɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
A machine used for laying pavement, such as roads or walkways, typically by spreading and smoothing materials like asphalt or concrete.

Examples

Modern paving machines
Modern paving machines can lay asphalt evenly and efficiently, making construction projects much faster.
Operators must be well-trained to handle a paving machine safely on busy urban streets.
The crew used a paving machine to quickly resurface the road after the harsh winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paver - machine that lays asphalt or concrete for roads and walkways
