Anguillula

noun
genus of nematode worms
Pronunciation: / ˌæŋɡwɪˈluːlə / (AmE), / ˌaŋɡwɪˈljuːlə /; / ˌaŋɡwɪˈluːlə / (BrE)
a genus of small nematode worms in the family Cephalobidae, sometimes called vinegar eels, found in soil or decaying matter
