voodoo

verb
bewitch or affect by voodoo magic
9.3 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàvuÀêduÀê / (AmE), / ÀàvuÀêduÀê / (BrE)
to bewitch, enchant, or affect someone or something as if by voodoo magic

Examples

To voodoo someone
He claimed his unlucky streak started after someone voodooed him.
Rumors spread that the opposing team had been voodooed before the playoffs.
Some superstitious locals believe the old house was voodooed years ago.
