frank

verb
stamp mail with a postmark
9.9 8.9
Pronunciation: / fræŋk / (AmE), / fræŋk / (BrE)
to stamp or mark mail to show that postage has been paid or is not required

Examples

To frank a letter
All outgoing mail must be franked to ensure proper delivery.
She spent the afternoon franking hundreds of envelopes in the office.
The clerk will frank your letter at the counter before posting it.
The secretary franked all the invitations before sending them out to the guests.
In the past, members of parliament could frank their mail without paying postage.
Please make sure the package is franked before dropping it in the mailbox.
