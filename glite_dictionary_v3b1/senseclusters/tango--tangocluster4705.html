tango

verb
to dance the tango
Pronunciation: / ˈtæŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtæŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
to dance the tango, a lively ballroom dance from Latin America, usually with a partner

Examples

To tango with a partner
Last night, they tangoed until midnight at the club.
She loves to tango in elegant ballrooms around the world.
They learned to tango before their wedding reception.
They tangoed across the dance floor with great skill.
At the festival, couples tango late into the night, enjoying the lively music.
She learned to tango during her trip to Buenos Aires.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foxtrot - to dance the foxtrot, a smooth progressive ballroom dance
  • synonym: waltz - to dance the waltz; to perform a waltz
