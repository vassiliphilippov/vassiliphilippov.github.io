stim

verb
to self-soothe repetitively
9.6
Pronunciation: / stɪm / (AmE), / stɪm / (BrE)
to perform repetitive movements, sounds, or actions, often to self-regulate sensory or emotional state, especially in neurodivergent individuals

Examples

To stim by rocking
Some people stim by rocking back and forth when they feel overwhelmed.
He likes to stim by humming softly to himself during stressful situations.
Children may stim by flapping their hands or spinning objects repeatedly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fidget - to make small movements, often because of nervousness or boredom
