stolidly

adverb
in an unemotional or impassive manner
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑː.lɪd.li / (AmE), / ˈstɒl.ɪd.li / (BrE)
in a way that shows little or no emotion or sensitivity; unemotionally or impassively

Examples

To stand stolidly
Despite the chaos around him, the guard stood stolidly at his post.
He listened stolidly to the bad news, betraying no sign of distress.
She stolidly repeated her answer, unfazed by the judge's stern tone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: emotionally - with strong emotion
  • synonym: impassively - without showing emotion
