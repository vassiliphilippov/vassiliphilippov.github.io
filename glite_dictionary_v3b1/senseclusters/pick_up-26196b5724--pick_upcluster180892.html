pick up

verb
improve after being poor or bad
3.7
Pronunciation: / pɪk ʌp / (AmE), / pɪk ʌp / (BrE)
to improve, increase, or become stronger, better, or more positive (about situations, mood, energy, speed, etc.)

Examples

Listening to her favorite music can pick up her mood instantly.
The sunshine really picked up my spirits after a long, rainy week.
