bromoform

noun
a colorless liquid chemical compound
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbroʊməˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊməˌfɔːm / (BrE)
A heavy, colorless liquid compound (CHBr₃) with a sweet odor, used as a solvent and in laboratories.

Examples

A bromoform liquid
As a haloform, bromoform is structurally similar to chloroform and iodoform.
Laboratory workers handle bromoform with care because of its potential health effects.
The density of bromoform makes it useful for separating mineral samples.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chloroform - a similar chemical compound (CHCl₃) used as an anesthetic and solvent
