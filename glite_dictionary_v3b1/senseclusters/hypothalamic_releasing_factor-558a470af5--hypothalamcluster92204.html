hypothalamic releasing factor

noun
hormone stimulating pituitary secretion
9.9
Pronunciation: / haɪpəˌθæləmɪk rɪˈlisɪŋ ˈfæktɚ / (AmE), / haɪpəˌθæləmɪk rɪˈliːsɪŋ ˈfæktə / (BrE)
any of several hormones secreted by the hypothalamus that stimulate the release of specific hormones from the anterior pituitary gland

Examples
