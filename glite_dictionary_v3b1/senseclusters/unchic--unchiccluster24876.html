unchic

adjective
not fashionable
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃiːk / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃiːk / (BrE)
not stylish or fashionable; lacking in elegance or trendiness

Examples

An unchic style
Although comfortable, her shoes were considered unchic by her fashion-conscious friends.
The restaurant's decor was clean but rather unchic compared to the trendy places nearby.
He chose an unchic jacket for the event, prioritizing warmth over style.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chic - stylish and fashionable
  • synonym: unfashionable - not in style or not following current trends
