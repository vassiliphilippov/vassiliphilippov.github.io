pock

verb
to scar with pockmarks
9.6 0.83/m
Pronunciation: / pÉ‘k / (AmE), / pÉ’k / (BrE)
to mark or scar with pockmarks or small pits, especially on the skin

Examples

To pock a surface
Over time, acid rain began to pock the statue's surface.
The smallpox outbreak left his face pocked for life.
Years of acne can pock otherwise healthy skin.
