Anemone sylvestris

noun
Eurasian herb with white nodding flowers
9.0
Pronunciation: / əˈnɛməni sɪlˈvɛstrɪs / (AmE), / əˈnɛməni sɪlˈvɛstrɪs / (BrE)
A perennial Eurasian herb with solitary, nodding, fragrant white flowers, commonly found in woodlands and gardens.
