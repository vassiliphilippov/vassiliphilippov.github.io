crocheting

noun
making fabric with a hooked needle
1.1
Pronunciation: / kroʊˈʃeɪtɪŋ /; / kroʊˈʃeɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / krəʊˈʃeɪtɪŋ /; / krəʊˈʃeɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
