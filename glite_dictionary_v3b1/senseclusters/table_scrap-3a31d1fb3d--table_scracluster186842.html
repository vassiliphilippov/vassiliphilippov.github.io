table scrap

noun
leftover food from a meal
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪbəl skræp / (AmE), / ˈteɪbəl skræp / (BrE)
a small amount of leftover food from a meal, often discarded or given to animals

Examples

Leftover table scraps

Most table scraps end up in the trash unless they are composted.
