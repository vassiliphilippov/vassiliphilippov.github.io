hillocky

adjective
full of small hills
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪləki / (AmE), / ˈhɪləki / (BrE)
full of small hills or mounds

Examples

Hillocky terrain
The hillocky terrain made it difficult for the cyclists to maintain a steady speed.
Farmers found the hillocky fields challenging to plow with their old equipment.
We enjoyed hiking across the hillocky countryside during our summer vacation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hilly - having many hills
