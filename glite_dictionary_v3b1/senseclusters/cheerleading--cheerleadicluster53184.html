cheerleading

noun
encouragement or support
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪrˌliːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪəˌliːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of giving encouragement or moral support to someone, especially to help them continue or succeed

Examples

Friend's cheerleading
Her constant cheerleading helped her friend stay motivated during a difficult project.
The team appreciated the cheerleading from their supporters after the tough loss.
Sometimes a little cheerleading is all someone needs to keep going.

Related Terms

  • antonym: criticism - the act of expressing disapproval or pointing out faults
  • synonym: encouragement - words or actions that give someone confidence or support
  • synonym: support - help or assistance given to someone
