fiercely

adverb
with intense or violent force
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪrsli /; / ˈfɪr.sli / (AmE), / ˈfɪəsli /; / ˈfɪəs.li / (BrE)
in a strong, intense, or forceful way; showing great energy, emotion, or aggression

Examples

To fiercely protect
She glared fiercely at anyone who dared disturb her concentration.
The debate became fiercely competitive as both sides refused to back down.
The wolves fiercely defended their territory from intruders.
He fiercely defended his beliefs during the heated debate.
She was fiercely proud of her children and never hesitated to praise them.
The wind blew fiercely throughout the night, rattling the windows.

  • synonym: violently - with great force or intensity
