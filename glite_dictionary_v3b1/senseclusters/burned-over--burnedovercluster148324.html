burned-over

adjective
destroyed by fire
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːnd ˌoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɜːnd ˌəʊvə / (BrE)

Examples

The firefighters surveyed the burned-over land after the wildfire had passed.
