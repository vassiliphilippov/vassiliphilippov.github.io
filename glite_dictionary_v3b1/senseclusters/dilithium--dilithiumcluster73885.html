dilithium

noun
molecule with two lithium atoms
9.9
Pronunciation: / daɪˈlɪθiəm / (AmE), / daɪˈlɪθiəm / (BrE)
a chemical species containing two lithium atoms, especially the diatomic molecule Li2 or an organolithium compound with two lithium atoms
