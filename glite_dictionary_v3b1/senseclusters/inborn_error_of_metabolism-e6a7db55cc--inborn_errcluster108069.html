inborn error of metabolism

noun
genetic metabolic disorder
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnˌbɔrn ˈɛrər əv məˈtæbəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌɪnˌbɔːn ˈɛrə əv məˈtæbəlɪzəm / (BrE)
a disease caused by an inherited defect, usually a missing or insufficient enzyme, leading to abnormal metabolism

Examples

An inborn error of metabolism disease

Early diagnosis of an inborn error of metabolism can be crucial for effective treatment.
Phenylketonuria is a classic example of an inborn error of metabolism.
