slogan

noun
memorable phrase of a group or organization
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsloʊ.ɡən / (AmE), / ˈsləʊ.ɡən / (BrE)
A short and memorable phrase used by a group, organization, or individual to express a principle, purpose, or aim.

Examples

A slogan for campaign
Every political party creates its own slogan to motivate and unite supporters.
Protesters chanted their slogans in the city square throughout the night.
The company launched a new advertising campaign featuring the slogan 'Just do it.'

Related Terms

  • synonym: catchphrase - a well-known sentence or phrase, especially one associated with a particular person
  • synonym: motto - a brief statement expressing principles or ideals of an individual or group
