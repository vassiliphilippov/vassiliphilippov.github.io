layer

noun
thin sheet or stratum, often of cells
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪ.ɚ /; / ˈleɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.ə /; / ˈleɪə / (BrE)
a sheet, stratum, or thickness of material that covers a surface or lies between other substances

Related Terms

  • synonym: coating - a thin layer covering a surface
  • antonym: core - the central or innermost part, often contrasted with outer layers
  • synonym: sheet - a thin, flat layer of something
  • synonym: stratum - a single level of material or tissue in a layered system
