Synonyms

  • give voice
    4.3 1/m
    give voice is an idiom for expressing feelings or opinions publicly and clearly, while voice is a general verb for stating them.
  • speak out
    3.4 11/m
    speak out means expressing opinions openly and without fear in public, while voice simply means to state or express them.
  • verbalize
    4.8 0.9/m
    verbalize means putting thoughts into words, often privately or roughly, while voice stresses stating ideas aloud, especially in public.
  • utter
    2.9 10/m
    utter means to say a sound or word aloud, often briefly or formally, while voice focuses on expressing opinions publicly.
  • vocalize
    4.4 0.6/m
    vocalize means to make sounds or sing using the voice, often about sound itself, while voice means to express ideas aloud.
