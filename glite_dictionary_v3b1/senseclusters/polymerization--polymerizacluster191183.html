polymerization

noun
process of forming a polymer from monomers
9.6
Pronunciation: / pəˌlɪmɚɪˈzeɪʃən /; / pəˌlɪmər.aɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / pəˌlɪməraɪˈzeɪʃən /; / pəˌlɪmər.aɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a chemical process in which small molecules called monomers are combined to form a polymer or polymeric compound
