hognose bat

noun
a small Mexican bat with a long nose
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑɡˌnoʊz bæt / (AmE), / ˈhɒɡˌnəʊz bæt / (BrE)
A small bat native to Mexico, distinguished by its long, slender nose and small ears.

Examples

A hognose bat species
A hognose bat was spotted roosting in a cave near the Mexican border.
Scientists study the hognose bat to understand how its nose helps it find food.
The hognose bat is one of the smallest bat species in the world.
