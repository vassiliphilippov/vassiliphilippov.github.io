villanelle

noun
archaic rustic person
Pronunciation: / ˌvɪləˈnɛl / (AmE), / ˌvɪləˈnɛl / (BrE)
an archaic term for a peasant or rustic person, especially one seen as simple or unsophisticated

Examples

A humble villanelle
In the old tale, a humble villanelle outsmarted the wealthy landowner.
The poet described the villanelle as cheerful and content with a simple life.
During the festival, every villanelle wore traditional clothing and danced in the square.

Related Terms

  • antonym: noble - a person of high social rank
  • synonym: peasant - a poor farmer or agricultural laborer
  • synonym: villager - a person who lives in a village
