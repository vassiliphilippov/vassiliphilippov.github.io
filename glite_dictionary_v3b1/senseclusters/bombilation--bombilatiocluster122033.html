bombilation

noun
continuous buzzing or droning sound
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌbɑːmbɪˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌbɒmbɪˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
A continuous droning or buzzing sound, like that made by bees.

Examples

The bombilation of bees
The bombilation of machinery filled the silent workshop with life.
There was a persistent bombilation in the garden as the bees gathered nectar.
We listened to the soft bombilation of insects on a warm summer evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buzz - a low, continuous humming or murmuring sound
  • synonym: drone - a low, monotonous sound, like that of a humming insect
