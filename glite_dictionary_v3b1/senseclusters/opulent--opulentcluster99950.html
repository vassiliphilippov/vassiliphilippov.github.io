opulent

adjective
luxurious and ostentatious
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑpjələnt / (AmE), / ˈɒpjʊlənt / (BrE)
luxurious and expensive; characterized by great wealth and ostentation

Examples

An opulent mansion
The billionaire lived in an opulent mansion overlooking the ocean.
The hotel's opulent furnishings impressed everyone who entered the lobby.
They hosted an opulent gala with gold-plated decorations and fine cuisine.

Related Terms

  • antonym: austere - severe or strict in manner, attitude, or appearance; lacking luxury
  • synonym: luxurious - extremely comfortable or elegant, especially when involving great expense
