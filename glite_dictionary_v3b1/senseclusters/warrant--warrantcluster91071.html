warrant

noun
written guarantee for product/service
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrənt / (AmE), / ˈwɒrənt / (BrE)
a written guarantee that a product or service will be provided or will meet specified standards

Examples

A product warrant
Customers received a written warrant as assurance of the service quality.
Please retain your warrant in case you need repairs or replacement.
The product comes with a two-year warrant covering all major defects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contract - a legally binding agreement
  • antonym: disclaimer - a statement denying responsibility
  • synonym: guarantee - a formal promise that certain conditions will be fulfilled
