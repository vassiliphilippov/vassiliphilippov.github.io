Mali franc

noun
former basic currency unit of Mali
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːli ˈfræŋk / (AmE), / ˈmɑːli ˈfræŋk / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency formerly used in Mali before the introduction of the West African CFA franc

Examples

Old Mali franc coins
A collector paid a high price for rare 1960s Mali franc coins.
Before 1984, prices in Mali were listed in Mali francs rather than in CFA francs.
The Mali franc was replaced by the West African CFA franc in 1984.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: West African CFA franc - the current currency used by Mali and other West African nations
  • synonym: franc - the basic monetary unit of several countries, originally France
