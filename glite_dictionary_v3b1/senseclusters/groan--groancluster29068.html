groan

noun
sound of pain or disapproval
6.1
Pronunciation: / ɡroʊn / (AmE), / ɡrəʊn / (BrE)
a low, mournful sound expressing pain, discomfort, or disapproval

Examples

A loud groan
A loud groan echoed through the room as the patient tried to sit up.
She let out a groan of frustration when her computer crashed again.
There was a collective groan from the audience at the bad joke.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheer - a shout of encouragement or approval
  • synonym: moan - a long, low sound expressing physical or mental suffering
