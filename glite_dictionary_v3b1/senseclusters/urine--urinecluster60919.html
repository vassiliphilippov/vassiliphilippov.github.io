urine

noun
liquid waste excreted by the kidneys
Pronunciation: / ˈjʊrɪn / (AmE), / ˈjʊərɪn / (BrE)
the pale yellowish liquid waste that is excreted from the body by the kidneys, stored in the bladder, and passed through the urethra

Examples

Yellow urine
If you drink lots of water, your urine may appear clearer.
The doctor ordered a test of my urine to check for infection.
The veterinarian collected a sample of the cat's urine for analysis.
