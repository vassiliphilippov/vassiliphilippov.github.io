E

noun
abbreviation for east (compass direction)
Pronunciation: / iː / (AmE), / iː / (BrE)
The abbreviation for 'east,' the cardinal compass point at 90 degrees, directly opposite west.

Examples

E on a map
The city is located 10 miles to the E of the river.
The sun always rises in the E.
Turn the map so that E is facing right.
