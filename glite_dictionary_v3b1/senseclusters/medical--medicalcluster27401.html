medical

noun
physical examination (esp. British)
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl / (BrE)

Examples

A full medical

All new employees must undergo a medical before starting work.
She failed her annual medical due to high blood pressure.
