casual

adjective
happening by chance; unplanned
Pronunciation: / ˈkæʒuəl / (AmE), / ˈkæʒjʊəl / (BrE)
happening by chance or without prearrangement; not planned or intended

Examples

A casual meeting
Her discovery of the hidden room was completely casual and unplanned.
The event was a casual gathering, not a formal meeting.
We had a casual conversation on the train, neither of us expecting to see each other again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accidental - happening by accident or chance, not planned
  • antonym: intentional - done on purpose; planned
