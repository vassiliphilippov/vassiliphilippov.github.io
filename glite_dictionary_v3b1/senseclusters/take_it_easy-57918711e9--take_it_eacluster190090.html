take it easy

verb
relax; avoid stress or effort
1.0
Pronunciation: / teɪk ɪt ˈiːzi / (AmE), / teɪk ɪt ˈiːzi / (BrE)
to relax and avoid making much effort or becoming stressed; to be calm and not worry

Examples

To take it easy after surgery
After the surgery, the doctor advised me to take it easy for a few weeks.
When work gets overwhelming, remember to take it easy and breathe.
Why don’t you take it easy this evening and watch a movie?

Related Terms

  • synonym: relax - to become less tense or anxious; to rest
  • antonym: stress - a state of mental or emotional strain or tension
  • synonym: unwind - to relax after stress or tension
