blow one's load

(informal, sometimes vulgar) To use up all one's effort, resources, or energy at once, often prematurely; to reveal all one has too soon. Also used as a vulgar slang term meaning to ejaculate.

Examples

To blow one's load early
He blew his load at the beginning of the debate and had nothing left to say.
She tends to blow her load all at once when she's excited about a new project.
The team blew their load in the first half and couldn't keep up later.
