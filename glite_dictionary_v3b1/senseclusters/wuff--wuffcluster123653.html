wuff

verb
to make soft dog sound
9.9
Pronunciation: /wʌf/ /; / /wʊf/ (AmE), /wʊf/ /; / /wʌf/ (BrE)
to make a soft, low barking sound like a dog, often as an imitation or gentle warning

Examples

To wuff softly
The puppy would wuff softly whenever someone walked past the window.
At night, the old dog sometimes wuffs in his sleep, dreaming of chasing rabbits.
She laughed when her friend tried to wuff like a small dog to get the baby's attention.
