bed and breakfast

noun
small lodging with breakfast provided
5.1
Pronunciation: / bɛd ənd ˈbrɛkfəst / (AmE), / bɛd ənd ˈbrɛkfəst / (BrE)
a small lodging establishment that offers overnight accommodation and breakfast, but usually no other meals

Examples

A bed and breakfast stay
She prefers a bed and breakfast over a large hotel for its personal touch.
The bed and breakfast served homemade scones with breakfast every morning.
We stayed in a charming bed and breakfast during our trip to the countryside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guesthouse - a private house offering accommodation, similar to a bed and breakfast
