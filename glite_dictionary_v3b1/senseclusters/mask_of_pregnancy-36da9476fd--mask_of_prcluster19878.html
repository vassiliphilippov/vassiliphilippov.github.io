mask of pregnancy

noun
facial discoloration during pregnancy
Pronunciation: / ˈmæsk əv prəˈɡnænsi / (AmE), / ˈmɑːsk əv prəˈɡnænsi / (BrE)
a tan or brownish discoloration of the face occurring in women, especially during pregnancy or when using oral contraceptives
