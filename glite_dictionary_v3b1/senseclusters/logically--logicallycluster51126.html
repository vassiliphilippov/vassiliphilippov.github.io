logically

adverb
in a logical manner
2.4 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kliː/ /; / /ˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kəl.iː/ (AmE), /ˈlɒ.dʒɪ.kliː/ /; / /ˈlɒ.dʒɪ.kəl.iː/ (BrE)

Examples

Think logically

Logically, the evidence points to only one possible conclusion.
Logically, we should leave early to avoid the rush hour traffic.
