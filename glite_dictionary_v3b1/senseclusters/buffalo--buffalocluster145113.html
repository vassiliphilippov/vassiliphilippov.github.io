Examples

Synonyms

  • American buffalo
    2.3 0.01/m
    American buffalo is the common regional name for the North American species, while buffalo is used both for the live animal and its meat.
  • American bison
    3.3 0.05/m
    American bison is the formal scientific name used in books and museums, while buffalo is the common everyday name and meat term.
  • bison
    3.9 2.48/m
    bison is the shorter, widely used name by scientists and people, while buffalo also often names the meat sold in shops.
