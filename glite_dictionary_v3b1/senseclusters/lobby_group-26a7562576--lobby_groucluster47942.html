lobby group

noun
group seeking political influence
3.9 0.14/m
Pronunciation: / Ààl…ëÀê.bi …°ruÀêp / (AmE), / Ààl…íb.i …°ruÀêp / (BrE)
An organization that attempts to influence legislators or government officials to support laws or policies in its interest.

Examples

A lobby group influences laws
A powerful lobby group was behind the new tax reforms proposed in parliament.
Several lobby groups attended the meeting to present their concerns to the committee.
The environmental lobby group organized a campaign to urge lawmakers to pass stricter pollution controls.
