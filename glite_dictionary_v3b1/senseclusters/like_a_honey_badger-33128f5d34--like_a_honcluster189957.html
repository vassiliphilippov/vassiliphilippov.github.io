like a honey badger

adverb
fearless; bravely or fiercely
7.3
Pronunciation: / laɪk ə ˈhʌni ˈbædʒər / (AmE), / laɪk ə ˈhʌni ˈbadʒə / (BrE)
bravely or fiercely, without fear or hesitation, often in the face of challenges or danger

Examples

Whenever he feels threatened, he reacts like a honey badger and stands his ground.
