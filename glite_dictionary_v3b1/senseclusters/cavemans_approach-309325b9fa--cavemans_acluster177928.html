caveman's approach

noun
primitive or outdated method
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪvˌmænz əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈkeɪvmænz əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a primitive, unsophisticated, or outdated method of doing something, often implying a lack of modern understanding or technique

Examples

A caveman's approach to problems
His caveman's approach to negotiation frustrated his colleagues.
The team quickly rejected her caveman's approach to marketing in favor of new strategies.
Using a caveman's approach to problem-solving rarely leads to effective solutions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: primitive approach - a basic or unsophisticated method
