iff

conjunction
if and only if
Pronunciation: / ɪf / (AmE), / ɪf / (BrE)
used in mathematics and logic to mean 'if and only if', indicating that two statements are both necessary and sufficient for each other

Examples

The iff statement
A number is even iff it is divisible by two.
A function is continuous at a point iff the limit exists and equals the function value.
A matrix is invertible iff its determinant is not zero.
