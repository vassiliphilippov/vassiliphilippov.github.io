pyrometric cone

noun
ceramic cone for measuring kiln temperature
9.9
Pronunciation: /paɪ.rə.ˈmet.rɪk koʊn/ (AmE), /paɪ.rə.ˈmet.rɪk kəʊn/ (BrE)
