assumed name

noun
business name used instead of legal name
2.5 5.5
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈsuːmd neɪm/ /; / /ə.ˈsjuːmd neɪm/ (AmE), /ə.ˈsjuːmd neɪm/ /; / /ə.ˈʃuːmd neɪm/ (BrE)
(law) a name under which a business or corporation operates that is different from its legal, registered name.

Examples

Business assumed name
ABC Corporation filed paperwork to conduct business under the assumed name "Main Street Books."
He opened a restaurant using an assumed name instead of his own company name.
Many states require companies to register their assumed names for transparency.
