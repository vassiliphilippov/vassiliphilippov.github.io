finance minister

noun
government minister overseeing finances
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪnæns ˌmɪnɪstɚ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪnæns ˌmɪnɪstə / (BrE)
the government minister in charge of a country's public finances, including taxation and spending

Examples

The finance minister
After the economic crisis, the country appointed a new finance minister.
India’s finance minister announced new tax reforms this week.
The finance minister presented the annual budget to parliament yesterday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chancellor of the exchequer - British government minister in charge of economic and financial matters
  • synonym: treasurer - a person responsible for managing financial affairs, especially in government or organizations
