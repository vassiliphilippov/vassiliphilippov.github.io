Vicksburg

noun
a key Civil War battle in 1863
8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈvɪks.bɜːrg/ /; / /ˈvɪks.bərg/ (AmE), /ˈvɪks.bɜːg/ (BrE)
a decisive battle in the American Civil War in 1863, in which Union forces besieged and captured the city of Vicksburg, leading to Confederate surrender after nearly seven weeks
