unspeakability

noun
inexpressible quality
9.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˌspiːkəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ʌnˌspiːkəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being impossible or too terrible to express in words

Examples

The unspeakability of disaster
The unspeakability of the disaster made it difficult for anyone to talk about what happened.
She was overwhelmed by the unspeakability of her loss and could not find the words to describe it.
Many novels struggle to capture the unspeakability of certain human experiences.

Related Terms

  • antonym: expressibility - the quality of being able to be expressed in words
  • synonym: indescribability - the quality of being impossible to describe
  • synonym: ineffability - the quality of being too great or extreme to be expressed in words
