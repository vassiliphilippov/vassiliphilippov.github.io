ham it up

verb
overact or exaggerate performance
Pronunciation: / hæm ɪt ʌp / (AmE), / hæm ɪt ʌp / (BrE)
to overact or exaggerate one's performance, especially in a theatrical or dramatic way

Examples

To ham it up on stage
She couldn't resist hamming it up during the school play's funny scenes.
The actor really hammed it up in the comedy sketch, making everyone laugh.
Whenever he's on stage, he loves to ham it up for the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overact - to act a role in an exaggerated way
  • antonym: underplay - to act a role in a restrained or subtle way
