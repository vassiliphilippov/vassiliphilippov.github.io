French dressing

noun
type of salad dressing
1.0
Pronunciation: / frɛntʃ ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / frɛntʃ ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
A type of salad dressing typically made from oil and vinegar, often with added mustard and garlic.

Examples

French dressing salad
He mixed up a homemade French dressing using olive oil and Dijon mustard.
I always put French dressing on my salads for a tangy flavor.
The restaurant serves a classic Caesar salad topped with French dressing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vinaigrette - salad dressing made with oil and vinegar, sometimes with seasoning
