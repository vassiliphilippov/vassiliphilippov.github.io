controversy

noun
prolonged public dispute or debate
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːn.trəˌvɝː.si / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.trəˌvɜː.si / (BrE)
A prolonged public disagreement or heated discussion, often involving strong opposing views.

Examples

Public controversy
The artist’s latest work has stirred significant controversy in the art world.
The new law sparked a controversy among politicians and citizens alike.
There is ongoing controversy regarding the safety of genetically modified foods.

  • synonym: dispute - A disagreement or argument about something important
