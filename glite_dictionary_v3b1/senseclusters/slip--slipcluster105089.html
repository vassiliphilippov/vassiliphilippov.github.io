slip

noun
narrow, flat piece of material
3.7
Pronunciation: / slɪp / (AmE), / slɪp / (BrE)
a narrow, flat piece of material, such as a small strip of paper or cloth

Examples

A slip of paper
A slip of cloth was used to bandage the wound.
She scribbled her phone number on a slip of paper and handed it to me.
The teacher distributed a slip for each student to fill out their information.
