mockumentary

noun
comedic fake documentary
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːkjuˈmɛntəri / (AmE), / ˌmɒkjʊˈmɛntəri / (BrE)
a film or television program that imitates the style of a documentary to create a humorous or satirical effect

Examples

A funny mockumentary
The new series is a hilarious mockumentary about a small-town theater group.
Many people believed the mockumentary was real until the credits rolled.
The director is famous for creating a mockumentary that satirizes the music industry.

Related Terms

  • antonym: documentary - a factual film or television program presenting real events
  • synonym: parody - a humorous imitation of a style or genre
