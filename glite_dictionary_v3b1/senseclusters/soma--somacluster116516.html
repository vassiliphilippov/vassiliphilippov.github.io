soma

noun
neuron cell body
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊmə / (AmE), / ˈsəʊmə / (BrE)
the cell body of a neuron, excluding its axons and dendrites

Examples

Neuron soma
The soma contains the nucleus and most of the organelles of the neuron.
Damage to the soma can affect the entire function of the nerve cell.
Researchers observed changes in the soma after the experimental treatment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: axon - the long, threadlike part of a neuron that carries impulses away from the cell body
  • synonym: cell body - the main part of a cell, especially a neuron, containing the nucleus
  • antonym: dendrite - a branched extension of a neuron that receives signals from other cells
