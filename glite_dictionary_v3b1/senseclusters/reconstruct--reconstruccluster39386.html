Synonyms

  • rebuild
    2.8 20/m
    rebuild means to build something again after damage, while reconstruct means to make it like the original structure again.
  • restore
    3.5 10/m
    restore returns an item to its original working or look, while reconstruct involves rebuilding parts or whole structures after destruction.
  • rehabilitate
    4.9 0.4/m
    rehabilitate brings a building or system back to good working order for use, while reconstruct rebuilds it after major damage.
  • recondition
    5.4 0.2/m
    recondition repairs machinery or used goods to a usable state, while reconstruct rebuilds destroyed structures from parts or plans.
