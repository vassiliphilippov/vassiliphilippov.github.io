codifier

noun
person who assigns codes
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊdəˌfaɪər / (AmE), / ˈkəʊdɪˌfaɪə / (BrE)
a person who assigns codes or classification numbers to items, records, or data to organize and manage them

Examples

Data codifier
The company hired a codifier to standardize the labeling of all inventory items.
As a codifier, she developed a new system for classifying medical records efficiently.
Every library needs a skilled codifier to keep its catalog organized and up to date.

Related Terms

  • synonym: classifier - a person or thing that arranges items into categories
  • synonym: organizer - a person who arranges or structures things systematically
