lone gunman

noun
single shooter acting alone
4.2
Pronunciation: / loʊn ˈɡʌnmən / (AmE), / ləʊn ˈɡʌnmən / (BrE)
a single person who commits a shooting, acting without accomplices

Examples

The lone gunman shoots
After hours of searching, authorities apprehended the lone gunman near the city park.
Reports indicate a lone gunman was responsible for the attack.
The police have confirmed that the suspect was a lone gunman acting independently.
