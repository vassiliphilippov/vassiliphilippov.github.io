epidural

adjective
on or outside the dura mater
8.1 5.7
Pronunciation: /e.pɪ.ˈdɜːr.əl/ /; / /e.pɪ.ˈdʊr.əl/ (AmE), /e.pɪ.ˈdjʊə.rəl/ /; / /e.pɪ.ˈdjɔː.rəl/ (BrE)
Situated on or outside the dura mater, the outermost membrane covering the brain and spinal cord.

Examples

Epidural space
An epidural catheter may be used to deliver pain relief during childbirth.
The scan revealed an epidural abscess pressing on the spinal cord.
The surgeon injected the anaesthetic into the epidural space before beginning the procedure.
