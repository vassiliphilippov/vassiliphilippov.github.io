unsustained

adjective
not maintained
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnsəˈsteɪnd / (AmE), / ˌʌnsəˈsteɪnd / (BrE)
not maintained, continued, or kept up over a period of time

Examples

Unsustained effort
The team's early success was unsustained and quickly faded as the season progressed.
Her interest in the new hobby was unsustained after the first few weeks.
Economic growth proved unsustained due to a lack of investment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sustained - maintained or continued over time
  • synonym: unmaintained - not kept in a good or working condition
