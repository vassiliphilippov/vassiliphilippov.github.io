bloodsucking

adjective
feeding by drawing blood
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌdˌsʌkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈblʌdˌsʌkɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an animal, insect, or person) feeding by sucking blood, or (figurative) exploiting others in a parasitic way

Examples

Bloodsucking leeches
He was bitten by several bloodsucking leeches while swimming in the lake.
Mosquitoes are well-known bloodsucking insects that can spread disease.
Some bats are bloodsucking mammals that feed on livestock.
A bloodsucking insect can transmit several diseases to humans.
Some companies have bloodsucking practices that exploit their employees.
The landlord was accused of being a bloodsucking profiteer during the housing crisis.
