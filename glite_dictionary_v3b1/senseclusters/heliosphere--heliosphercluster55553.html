heliosphere

noun
the region around the sun formed by the solar wind
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːli.oʊˌsfɪr / (AmE), / ˈhiːlɪəˌsfɪə / (BrE)
the region surrounding the sun and the solar system, within the boundary where the solar wind meets interstellar space

Examples

The heliosphere boundary
Cosmic rays from outside the heliosphere can pose a danger to astronauts.
Researchers are studying the boundaries of the heliosphere to better understand space weather.
The Voyager spacecraft have crossed the edge of the heliosphere into interstellar space.
