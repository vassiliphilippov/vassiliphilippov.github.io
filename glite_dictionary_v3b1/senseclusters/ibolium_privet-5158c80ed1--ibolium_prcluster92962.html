ibolium privet

noun
hybrid privet shrub for hedging
8.9
Pronunciation: / aɪˈboʊliəm ˈprɪvɪt / (AmE), / aɪˈbəʊliəm ˈprɪvɪt / (BrE)
A fast-growing, densely branched hybrid shrub of Ligustrum ovalifolium and Ligustrum obtusifolium, often used for hedging.

Examples

Dense ibolium privet hedge
Gardeners often choose ibolium privet for its resistance to pests and adaptability to urban environments.
The ibolium privet is prized for its rapid growth and dense foliage, making it ideal for privacy hedges.
We planted an ibolium privet border along the driveway last spring, and it has filled out nicely.
