promptbook

noun
annotated playscript for prompter
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑmptˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈprɒmptˌbʊk / (BrE)
a copy of a playscript containing notes and directions used by the prompter in a theatrical production

Examples

The promptbook notes
During rehearsals, the director referred to the promptbook to clarify stage directions for the cast.
Every promptbook needs to be updated for changes before opening night.
The prompter's promptbook included cues, lighting notes, and all actor movements.
