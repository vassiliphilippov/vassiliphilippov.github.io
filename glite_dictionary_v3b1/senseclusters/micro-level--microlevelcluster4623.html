micro-level

noun
smallest scale of analysis
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.kroʊ ˈlev.əl/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.krəʊ ˈlev.əl/ (BrE)
the smallest scale of analysis in a field, focusing on individuals, households, or small groups rather than larger systems

Examples

Micro-level study group
The study focused on the micro-level to understand how family decisions impact education.
At the micro-level, researchers analyze the behavior of individuals within a community.
Policy changes can have different effects when examined at the micro-level compared to the national scale.

Related Terms

  • synonym: individual - a single person or thing, often the focus at the micro-level
  • antonym: macro-level - the largest scale of analysis, focusing on large groups or systems
