retweet

verb
to share a post
2.6
Pronunciation: / riˈtwiːt /; / ˌriːˈtwiːt / (AmE), / riˈtwiːt /; / ˌriːˈtwiːt / (BrE)
to share or repost someone else's tweet or post on Twitter (or a similar social media platform) so your followers can see it

Examples

She retweeted the news article so her friends could read it too.

If you find an interesting article, you can retweet it so your followers see it too.

If you agree with the message, feel free to retweet it.
