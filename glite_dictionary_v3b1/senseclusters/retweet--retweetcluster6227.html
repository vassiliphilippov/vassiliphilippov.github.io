retweet

verb
to share a post

Examples

Synonyms

  • reshare
    3.3 0.05/m
    reshare posts someone else's content on social sites to show it to your followers, while retweet is the Twitter-specific word for that action.
  • repost
    2.8 1.58/m
    repost shares or publishes content again on websites or social media, while retweet refers specifically to resharing on Twitter for followers.
