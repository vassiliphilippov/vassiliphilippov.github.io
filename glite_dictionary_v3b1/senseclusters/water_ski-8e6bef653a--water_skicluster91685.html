water ski

noun
equipment for gliding on water behind a boat
5.5 2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈwɔː.tər skiː/ /; / /ˈwɑː.tər skiː/ (AmE), /ˈwɔː.tə skiː/ /; / /ˈwɒ.tə skiː/ /; / /ˈwɔː.tə ʃiː/ (BrE)
A long, flat, narrow piece of equipment worn on the foot and used for gliding over the surface of water while being towed by a boat.

Examples

A water ski boot
He lost his balance and fell off the water ski into the lake.
Newcomers often rent a water ski when first trying the sport.
She strapped the water ski to her feet before the boat started moving.
