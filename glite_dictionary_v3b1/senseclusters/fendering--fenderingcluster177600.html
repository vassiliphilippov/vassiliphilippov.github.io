fendering

noun
protective vehicle panels
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈfen.dər.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈfen.də.rɪŋ/ (BrE)
protective panels or guards attached to a vehicle or bicycle, especially around wheel arches, to prevent damage or spray

Examples

Car fendering
The mechanic inspected the fendering for any signs of rust or damage after the accident.
Modern bicycles often have lightweight fendering to keep riders clean in wet conditions.
They replaced the damaged fendering around the truck's wheel arches after the minor collision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fender - a part of a vehicle that covers and protects the wheel area
  • synonym: mudguard - a guard over a wheel to protect against mud or water spray
