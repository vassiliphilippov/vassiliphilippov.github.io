belvedere

noun
structure for enjoying a view
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛlvəˌdɪr / (AmE), / ˈbɛlvɪdɪə / (BrE)
a structure, such as a gazebo or pavilion, built to provide a beautiful view of the surrounding area

Examples

Rooftop belvedere
The architect designed a rooftop belvedere to take advantage of the city skyline.
The garden's belvedere offers stunning views of the entire valley.
They enjoyed afternoon tea in the shaded belvedere overlooking the lake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gazebo - a small building with open sides in a garden, often used for relaxation
