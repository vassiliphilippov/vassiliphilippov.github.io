Synonyms

  • contend
    4.6 11/m

  • contest
    4.6 5.56/m
    contest is to formally challenge a decision or result, while dispute is to argue about or question that decision's truth.
  • gainsay
    9.9 0.23/m
    gainsay is to deny or directly say something is untrue, while dispute is to argue or question its validity.
  • argue
    1.1 26/m

  • quarrel
    2.6 2.47/m

  • altercate
