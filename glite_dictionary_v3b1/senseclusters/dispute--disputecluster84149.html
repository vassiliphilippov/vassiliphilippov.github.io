Synonyms

  • challenge
    4.3 30/m
    challenge is to formally question or oppose something, while dispute is to argue about or question its truth.
  • call into question
    4.5 2/m
    call into question is to express doubt about something publicly, while dispute is to argue about its truth or validity.
  • contend
    4.6 5/m
    contend is to state an opinion and argue for it, while dispute is to argue or question someone's claim.
  • contest
    4.6 3/m
    contest is to formally challenge a decision or result, while dispute is to argue about or question that decision's truth.
  • gainsay
    9.9 0.07/m

  • argue

  • quarrel
    2.6 2/m
