allowance

noun
the act of permitting something
Pronunciation: / əˈlaʊəns / (AmE), / əˈlaʊəns / (BrE)
the act of permitting or allowing something; official or formal permission

Examples

Allowance of late arrivals
His allowance of late arrivals created a more relaxed atmosphere in the office.
The allowance of noise after 10 p.m. was met with complaints from neighbors.
There was much debate about the allowance of cell phones during the exam.
