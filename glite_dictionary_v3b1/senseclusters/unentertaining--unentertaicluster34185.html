unentertaining

adjective
not enjoyable
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌɛnərˈteɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌɛntəˈteɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
not amusing, interesting, or enjoyable to watch, listen to, or experience

Examples

An unentertaining lecture
The lecture was so unentertaining that several students struggled to stay awake.
Many critics found the play unentertaining despite its talented cast.
He stopped reading the book because it was too unentertaining for his taste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boring - not interesting or exciting
  • synonym: dull - lacking interest or excitement
  • antonym: entertaining - amusing, interesting, or enjoyable
