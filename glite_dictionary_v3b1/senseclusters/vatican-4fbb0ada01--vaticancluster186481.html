Vatican

proper noun
residence of the Pope in Vatican City
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈvæt̬.ɪ.kən /; / ˈvætɪkən / (AmE), / ˈvæt.ɪ.kən /; / ˈvætɪkən / (BrE)
