gusle

noun
Balkan string instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈɡuːslə / (AmE), / ˈɡuːslə / (BrE)
a traditional single-stringed bowed musical instrument from the Balkans, used to accompany epic poetry, folk songs, and storytelling
