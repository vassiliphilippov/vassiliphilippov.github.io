hurl

verb
make a thrusting forward movement
7.1
Pronunciation: / hɜrl / (AmE), / hɜːl / (BrE)
to make a forceful or thrusting forward movement, especially suddenly or with great energy

Examples

To hurl himself forward
He hurled himself into the debate, determined to make his point heard.
The soldier hurled himself at the enemy line, surprising everyone with his courage.
With a sudden burst of energy, she hurled forward to block the door before it closed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thrust - to push or drive with force, as in a forward movement
