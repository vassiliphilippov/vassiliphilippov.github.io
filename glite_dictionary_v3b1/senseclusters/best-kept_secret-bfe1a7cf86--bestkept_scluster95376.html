best-kept secret

noun
something valuable not widely known
Pronunciation: / ˌbɛstˌkɛpt ˈsiːkrət / (AmE), / ˌbɛstˌkɛpt ˈsiːkrɪt / (BrE)
something known to only a few people, often considered especially good or valuable, and not widely publicized or recognized.

Examples

A best-kept secret place
For years, the museum remained a best-kept secret among local art lovers.
Hidden in a quiet neighborhood, this bakery is truly the city's best-kept secret.
That small island is considered one of the Mediterranean's best-kept secrets.
