dependent

adjective
addicted to a drug
1.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɛndənt / (AmE), / dɪˈpɛndənt / (BrE)
Addicted to a drug and unable to function normally without it.

Examples

Dependent on drugs
After years of substance abuse, he became dependent on painkillers.
Patients who are dependent on opioids often require supervised withdrawal.
She realized she was dependent on sleeping pills to get through the night.
After years of using prescription painkillers, he became dependant on them to get through the day.
Some patients are dependant on medication for both physical and psychological relief.
The centre offers support to those dependant on alcohol or other substances.

  • synonym: dependent - unable to stop taking a drug; addicted
