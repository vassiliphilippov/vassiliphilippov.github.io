vodun

noun
West African religion
9.9
Pronunciation: / voʊˈduːn / (AmE), / ˈvɒdʊn / (BrE)
a traditional West African religion centered on spirits, ancestor worship, and rituals

Examples

Practice vodun rituals
Many communities in Benin and Togo still practice vodun as part of their daily lives.
The rituals of vodun often involve music, dance, and offerings to spirits.
Anthropologists have studied vodun to better understand West African cultural traditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: voodoo - a religion derived from vodun, practiced chiefly in Haiti and parts of the Caribbean
