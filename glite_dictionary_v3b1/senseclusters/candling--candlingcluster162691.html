candling

noun
making candles
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkændlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkændlɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of making candles by dipping wicks into melted wax or pouring wax into molds

Examples

Candling wax
She took a workshop on candling and now makes her own scented candles at home.
Traditional candling involves repeatedly dipping a wick into hot wax to build up layers.
Many people enjoy candling as a relaxing and creative hobby during the winter months.
