diphenhydramine

noun
antihistamine medication
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪfɛnˈhaɪdrəˌmin / (AmE), / ˌdaɪfenˈhɪdrəmiːn / (BrE)
an antihistamine drug used to relieve symptoms of allergy, hay fever, and motion sickness

Examples

Diphenhydramine allergy medicine
Diphenhydramine is commonly sold under the brand name Benadryl.
Because diphenhydramine can cause drowsiness, you should avoid driving after taking it.
The doctor recommended diphenhydramine to help with her hay fever symptoms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antihistamine - a drug that counteracts the effects of histamine
