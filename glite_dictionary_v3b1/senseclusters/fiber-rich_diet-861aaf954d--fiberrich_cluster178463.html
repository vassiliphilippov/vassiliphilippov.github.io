fiber-rich diet

noun
diet high in dietary fiber
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪbɚ rɪtʃ ˈdaɪət / (AmE), / ˈfaɪbə rɪtʃ ˈdaɪət / (BrE)
a diet that contains a large amount of dietary fiber, beneficial for digestion and bowel health

Examples

A fiber-rich diet
A fiber-rich diet can help prevent constipation and maintain a healthy digestive system.
Nutritionists often recommend a fiber-rich diet to lower the risk of heart disease.
Switching to a fiber-rich diet made her feel more energetic and improved her overall wellness.
