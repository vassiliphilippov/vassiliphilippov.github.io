blellum

verb
to chatter foolishly
to talk foolishly or chatter continuously, often in a silly or pointless way

Examples

To blellum in class
He would often blellum during class, distracting everyone with his pointless chatter.
Instead of listening, she began to blellum about unrelated topics at the dinner table.
The children blellum excitedly as they waited for the show to start.

Related Terms

  • synonym: babble - to talk quickly and continuously in a foolish or excited way
  • antonym: be silent - to not speak or make noise
  • synonym: prattle - to talk at length in a foolish or inconsequential way
