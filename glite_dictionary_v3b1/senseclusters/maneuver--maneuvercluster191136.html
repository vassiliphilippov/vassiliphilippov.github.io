maneuver

noun
military training exercise
Pronunciation: / məˈnuːvɚ /; / məˈnuːvər / (AmE), / məˈnjuːvə /; / məˈnuːvə / (BrE)
a large-scale military training exercise intended to simulate the conditions of war or prepare troops for combat
