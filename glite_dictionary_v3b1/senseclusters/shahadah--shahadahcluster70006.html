shahadah

noun
Islamic declaration of faith
Pronunciation: / ʃəˈhɑdə / (AmE), / ʃəˈhɑːdə / (BrE)
the Islamic declaration of faith, professing that there is no god but Allah and Muhammad is his Messenger, considered the first pillar of Islam

Examples

The shahadah prayer
Muslims declare the shahadah daily as an affirmation of their faith.
Reciting the shahadah is a fundamental step in converting to Islam.
The shahadah is often inscribed in Islamic art and architecture.
