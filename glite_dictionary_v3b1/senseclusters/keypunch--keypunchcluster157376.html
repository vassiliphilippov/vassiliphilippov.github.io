keypunch

verb
to enter data by machine
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːˌpʌntʃ / (AmE), / ˈkiːˌpʌntʃ / (BrE)
to enter data by typing on a keyboard, especially using a keypunch machine for punched cards

Examples

To keypunch cards
In the early days of computing, operators would keypunch thousands of cards each day.
She keypunched the data from the forms into the system for processing.
Before modern computers, students learned to keypunch their assignments onto cards.
In the 1960s, workers would keypunch thousands of cards each day for data processing.
She learned to keypunch quickly to keep up with the demands of her office job.
Before computers had screens, people had to keypunch information onto cards for storage.
