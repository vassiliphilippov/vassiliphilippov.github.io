plug

noun
wad of chewing tobacco
Pronunciation: / plʌɡ / (AmE), / plʌɡ / (BrE)
A quantity of chewing tobacco pressed into a compact, chewable mass.

Examples

A plug of tobacco
He took a large plug of tobacco from his pocket and began to chew.
Some baseball players keep a plug in their cheek during games.
The old store sold snuff, loose tobacco, and the occasional plug behind the counter.
