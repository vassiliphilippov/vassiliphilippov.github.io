extractive

adjective
relating to resource extraction from the earth
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstræktɪv / (AmE), / ɪkˈstræktɪv / (BrE)
relating to or involving the removal of natural resources from the earth, such as minerals, oil, or gas

Examples

Extractive industries
Many conflicts in the region are driven by interests in extractive resources such as copper and gold.
New regulations aim to make extractive industries more environmentally responsible.
The country's economy is heavily dependent on its extractive sector, especially mining and oil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extract - to remove or obtain something, especially using effort
