allegretto

adjective, adverb
moderately fast tempo in music
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæləˈɡrɛtoʊ /; / əˌlɛˈɡrɛtoʊ / (AmE), / ˌalɪˈɡrɛtəʊ /; / alɪˈɡrɛtəʊ / (BrE)
played or to be played at a moderately quick tempo (allegretto), faster than andante but slower than allegro
