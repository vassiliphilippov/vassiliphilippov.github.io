factor

noun
number that divides another exactly
Pronunciation: / ˈfæk.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈfæk.tə / (BrE)
a number or symbol that divides another number exactly, or that is multiplied with others to produce a given product (mathematics)

Examples

Prime numbers have only two factors: one and themselves.
