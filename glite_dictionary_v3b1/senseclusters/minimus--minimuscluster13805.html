minimus

noun
smallest part
9.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪ.nɪ.məs/ /; / /ˈmɪ.nə.məs/ (AmE), /ˈmɪ.nɪ.məs/ /; / /ˈmɪ.nə.məs/ (BrE)
the smallest or least part of something, especially used for the smallest muscle or digit in anatomy

Examples

Abductor minimus muscle
The abductor minimus is the smallest muscle in that group.
In some animals, the minimus refers to the tiniest toe or finger.
Doctors examined the patient's minimus for signs of injury after the accident.
