passenger train

noun
train that carries passengers
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsəndʒɚ treɪn / (AmE), / ˈpæsɪndʒə treɪn / (BrE)
A train designed and operated to carry people rather than goods.

Examples

A passenger train
Many commuters rely on the passenger train to get to work every day.
The passenger train arrived at the station exactly on time.
We decided to take a passenger train across the country for a scenic journey.

Related Terms

  • antonym: freight train - a train that carries goods rather than passengers
