call in

verb
summon someone to enter
2.7 2.6
Pronunciation: /kɔːl ɪn/ /; / /kɑːl ɪn/ (AmE), /kɔːl ɪn/ (BrE)
to ask or summon someone to come in, help, or take part, especially an expert, professional, or replacement

The company decided to call in a consultant to resolve the issue.
We had to call in a plumber when the pipe burst last night.
