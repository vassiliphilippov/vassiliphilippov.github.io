horned chameleon

noun
African chameleon with horn-like head structures
Pronunciation: / hɔrnd kəˈmiːliən / (AmE), / hɔːnd kəˈmiːliən / (BrE)
a species of chameleon distinguished by prominent horn-like structures on its head, native to parts of Africa
