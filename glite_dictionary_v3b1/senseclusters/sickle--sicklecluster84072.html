sickle

noun
agricultural cutting tool with curved blade
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪkəl / (BrE)
A hand tool with a curved blade and short handle, used for cutting grass or crops.

Examples

A sickle blade
She found an old, rusted sickle in the barn while cleaning.
The farmer used a sickle to harvest the wheat by hand.
Traditional rice harvesting in the region is still done with a sickle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scythe - tool with a long, curved blade for mowing grass or reaping crops
