MPEG

noun
video and audio compression standard
4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈem.peg/ /; / /ˈem.piː.iː.dʒiː/ (AmE), /ˈem.peg/ /; / /ˈem.piː.iː.dʒiː/ (BrE)
