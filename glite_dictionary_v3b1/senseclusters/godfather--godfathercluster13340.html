godfather

noun
male sponsor or mentor
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑdˌfɑðɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡɒdˌfɑːðə / (BrE)
A man who acts as a sponsor or mentor, especially one who has a relationship similar to that of a male sponsor to his godchild.

Examples

A political godfather
As a political godfather, she guided many young leaders to success.
He has always considered Mr. Smith his godfather in the business world.
The artist acknowledged his godfather for inspiring his creative journey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mentor - an experienced and trusted adviser
