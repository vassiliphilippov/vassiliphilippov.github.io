Peripatetic

noun
follower of Aristotle or his philosophy
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛrəpəˈtɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌpɛrɪpəˈtɛtɪk / (BrE)
A follower of Aristotle or an adherent of Aristotelian philosophy.

Examples

A Peripatetic philosopher
Several Peripatetics gathered at the academy to discuss logic and ethics.
She became a Peripatetic after studying the works of Aristotle.
The Peripatetic argued passionately for Aristotle's view of virtue.

Related Terms
