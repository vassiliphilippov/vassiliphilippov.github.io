fowl

noun
bird flesh used as food
Pronunciation: / faʊl / (AmE), / faʊl / (BrE)
the flesh of a bird, especially chicken or other domestic or wild bird, when used as food

Examples

Roast fowl dinner
Roast fowl is often served during festive occasions.
She preferred the taste of wild fowl over domestic chicken.
The chef prepared a delicious dish using fowl from the local farm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: poultry - domestic birds raised for their meat or eggs
