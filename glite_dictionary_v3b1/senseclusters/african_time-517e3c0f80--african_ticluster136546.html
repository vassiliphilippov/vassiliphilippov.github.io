African time

noun
relaxed attitude to punctuality
4.9 7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈæf.rɪ.kən taɪm / (AmE), / ˈæf.rɪ.kən taɪm / (BrE)
A humorous or informal term referring to a relaxed or flexible approach to punctuality, especially in some African cultures.
