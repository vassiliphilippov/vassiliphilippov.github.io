clubroom

noun
room for club meetings or activities
Pronunciation: / ˈklʌbˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈklʌbˌruːm / (BrE)
a room used for meetings or activities of a club

Examples

The clubroom meeting
After classes, they gathered in the clubroom to plan the upcoming charity fundraiser.
Every Friday, the chess club meets in the clubroom to play matches and discuss strategies.
The school provided each student organization with its own clubroom for meetings and events.
