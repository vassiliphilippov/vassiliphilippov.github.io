dandelion

noun
wild plant with yellow flowers and fluffy seeds
4.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈdæn.də.laɪ.ən/ /; / /ˈdæn.dɪ.laɪ.ən/ (AmE), /ˈdæn.də.laɪ.ən/ /; / /ˈdæn.dɪ.laɪ.ən/ (BrE)
A common wild plant of the genus Taraxacum, with deeply notched leaves, bright yellow flowers, and globe-shaped clusters of fluffy seeds.

Examples

A dandelion flower
Children love to blow the fluffy seeds off a dandelion on a windy day.
The leaves of a dandelion can be used in salads or herbal teas.
We found a bright yellow dandelion growing between the cracks in the sidewalk.
