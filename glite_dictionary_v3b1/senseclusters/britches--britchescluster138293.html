britches

noun
informal term for trousers or breeches
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪtʃɪz / (AmE), / ˈbrɪtʃɪz / (BrE)
(informal) Loose-fitting trousers, especially as a variant pronunciation or spelling of 'breeches'.

Examples

Old britches
As a child, I remember my grandfather always wore old britches around the farm.
Don’t get too big for your britches, young man!
He pulled up his britches and got ready to work in the field.
