mouth-to-mouth

noun
emergency resuscitation method
2.0 3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈmaʊθ.tə.maʊθ/ /; / /ˈmaʊθ.tuː.maʊθ/ /; / /ˈmaʊð.tə.maʊð/ (AmE), /ˈmaʊθ.tə.maʊθ/ /; / /ˈmaʊθ.tuː.maʊθ/ (BrE)
a method of emergency resuscitation in which a person breathes directly into another's mouth to provide oxygen

Examples

Mouth-to-mouth resuscitation
Knowing how to perform mouth-to-mouth can help save lives in emergencies.
The instructor demonstrated proper mouth-to-mouth technique during the CPR class.
The paramedic quickly began mouth-to-mouth when she saw the patient had stopped breathing.
