refrigeration dryer

noun
device removing moisture from compressed air
9.9 4.4
Pronunciation: /rɪ.frɪdʒ.ə.ˈreɪ.ʃən ˈdraɪ.ər/ (AmE), /rɪ.frɪdʒ.ə.ˈreɪ.ʃən ˈdraɪ.ə/ (BrE)
