upturn

verb
to turn upside down
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌtɜrn / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌtɜːn / (BrE)
to turn something so that the bottom is on top or facing upward

Examples

To upturn a box
The child tried to upturn the bucket to see what was inside.
Strong winds can upturn small boats on the lake.
She accidentally upturned her cup, spilling coffee everywhere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overturn - to turn something over so that it is upside down
  • antonym: right - to return something to an upright position
