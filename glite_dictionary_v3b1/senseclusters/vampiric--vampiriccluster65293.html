vampiric

adjective
exploitative; draining
8.4
Pronunciation: / væmˈpɪrɪk / (AmE), / væmˈpɪrɪk / (BrE)
figuratively, acting in a way that drains energy, resources, or life from others in an exploitative manner

Examples

Vampiric control
The vampiric behavior of the landlord left tenants struggling to make ends meet.
Critics described the new tax as vampiric, draining money from the poorest citizens.
Her vampiric attitude toward friendships left people feeling exhausted and used.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exploitative - taking unfair advantage of others
  • antonym: generous - willing to give and share unselfishly
  • synonym: parasitic - living off or exploiting others
