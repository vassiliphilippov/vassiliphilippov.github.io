schmalz

noun
excessive sentimentality in art or music
Pronunciation: / ʃmɑlts / (AmE), / ʃmɑːlts / (BrE)
Excessive sentimentality in art or music, especially when considered cloying or mawkish.

Examples

Too much schmalz
Critics complained that the musical score added too much schmalz to the drama.
Some listeners enjoy a bit of schmalz in love songs, while others find it irritating.
The movie was full of schmalz and predictable tear-jerking scenes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: saccharine - excessively sweet or sentimental, especially in tone
  • synonym: sentimentality - tenderness, sadness, or nostalgia that is excessive or self-indulgent
