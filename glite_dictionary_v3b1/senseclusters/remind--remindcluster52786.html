remind

verb
prompt by suggesting forgotten words
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmaɪnd / (AmE), / rɪˈmaɪnd / (BrE)
to assist someone during speech or performance by prompting them with forgotten or missed words

Examples

To remind a speaker
If you can't recall the next verse, I'll remind you.
She kept her script nearby to remind her in case she stumbled.
The stage manager was ready to remind the actor if he forgot his lines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prompt - to supply forgotten words to a speaker or actor
