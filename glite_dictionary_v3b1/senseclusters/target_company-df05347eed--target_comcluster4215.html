target company

noun
company sought for acquisition
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑrɡət ˌkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˈtɑːɡɪt ˌkʌmpəni / (BrE)
a company that has been selected by another business as a desirable candidate for acquisition, typically in the context of a takeover bid

Examples

The target company for takeover
Hostile takeovers often place pressure on the management of the target company.
The target company received several offers from potential buyers last year.
The shareholders of the target company must approve any merger proposal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acquiree - a company that is acquired in a business deal
