reserve

verb
arrange or book in advance
2.0 2.7
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈzɜːrv/ /; / /riː.ˈzɜːrv/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈzɜːv/ /; / /riː.ˈzɜːv/ (BrE)
to arrange for something to be kept for your use at a future time; to book in advance

Examples

To reserve a table
