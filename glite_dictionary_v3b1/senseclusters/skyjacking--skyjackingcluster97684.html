skyjacking

verb
to hijack an aircraft
Pronunciation: / ˈskaɪˌdʒækɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskaɪˌdʒækɪŋ / (BrE)
to illegally take control of an aircraft in flight, especially by force or threat

Examples

To skyjack a plane
The authorities responded quickly when the group attempted skyjacking the passenger jet.
International laws have become stricter to prevent skyjacking incidents on commercial flights.
The movie tells the story of a daring skyjacking that changed aviation security forever.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commandeer - to take control of something, especially for military or official use
  • synonym: hijack - to illegally seize control of a vehicle
