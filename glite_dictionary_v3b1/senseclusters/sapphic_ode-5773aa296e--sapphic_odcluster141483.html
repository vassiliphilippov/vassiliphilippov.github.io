Sapphic ode

noun
ode in Sapphic meter; specific stanzaic poem form
Pronunciation: / ˈsæfɪk oʊd / (AmE), / ˈsæfɪk əʊd / (BrE)
A lyric poem composed in stanzas following the Sapphic metre, typically with four lines per stanza, often reflecting themes of passion or emotion.

Examples

A Sapphic ode poem
Her latest collection includes a masterful Sapphic ode dedicated to the theme of unrequited love.
In literature class, we analyzed a famous Sapphic ode by imitating its stanzaic structure and meter.
The poet experimented with various classical forms, eventually composing a Sapphic ode that won critical acclaim.
