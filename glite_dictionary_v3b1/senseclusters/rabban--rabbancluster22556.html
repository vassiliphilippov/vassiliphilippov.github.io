rabban

noun
Jewish honorific title
9.9
Pronunciation: / rɑːˈbɑːn / (AmE), / rəˈbæn / (BrE)
a title of honor for a Jewish teacher or scholar, especially used in ancient times

Examples

Rabban Simeon

Many students admired the wisdom of their rabban during lessons on ancient texts.
In ancient times, a rabban was highly respected in the Jewish community.
