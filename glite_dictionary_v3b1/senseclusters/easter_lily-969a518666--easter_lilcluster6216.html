Easter lily

noun
white spring-blooming lily plant
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈiːstər ˌlɪli / (AmE), / ˈiːstə ˌlɪli / (BrE)
A tall lily (Lilium longiflorum) with large, white, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in the spring, often associated with Easter celebrations.

Examples

White Easter lilies
Each spring, churches display Easter lilies near the altar to symbolize purity and hope.
She bought a beautiful Easter lily for her grandmother's garden.
The fragrant blossoms of the Easter lily filled the room with a delicate scent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lily - a plant with large, often fragrant flowers, genus Lilium
