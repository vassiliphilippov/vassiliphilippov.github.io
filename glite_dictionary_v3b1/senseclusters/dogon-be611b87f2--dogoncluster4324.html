Dogon

noun
ethnic group member
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊɡɒn / (AmE), / ˈdəʊɡɒn / (BrE)
a member of the Dogon people, an ethnic group living mainly in Mali, West Africa

Examples

A Dogon guide

The Dogon are known for their unique architecture and vibrant festivals.
Many tourists visit Mali to learn about the traditions of the Dogon.
