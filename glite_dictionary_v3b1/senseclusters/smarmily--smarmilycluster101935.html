smarmily

adverb
in an insincerely flattering way
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɑːrməli / (AmE), / ˈsmɑːmɪli / (BrE)
In an excessively flattering, insincerely polite, or ingratiating manner.

Examples

To smarmily praise
He smarmily complimented his boss, hoping for a promotion.
She smarmily agreed with everything the teacher said, trying to gain favor.
The salesperson smarmily assured us that the car was the best on the market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unctuously - in a way that is excessively or insincerely flattering
