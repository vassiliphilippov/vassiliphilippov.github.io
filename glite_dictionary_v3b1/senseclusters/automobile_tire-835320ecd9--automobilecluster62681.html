automobile tire

noun
rubber covering for a car wheel
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːtəmoʊˌbiːl ˈtaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəməˌbiːl ˈtaɪə / (BrE)
A rubber covering fitted around the rim of a car wheel, providing traction and absorbing shock while driving.
