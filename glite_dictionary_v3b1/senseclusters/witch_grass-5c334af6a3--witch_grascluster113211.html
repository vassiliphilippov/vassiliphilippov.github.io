witch grass

noun
a North American weedy grass species
Pronunciation: / wɪtʃ ɡræs / (AmE), / wɪtʃ ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a type of weedy grass, found in North America or Europe, that spreads quickly and is often unwanted in gardens or fields
