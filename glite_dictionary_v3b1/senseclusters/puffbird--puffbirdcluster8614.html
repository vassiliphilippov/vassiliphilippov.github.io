puffbird

noun
tropical American bird with fluffed feathers
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌf.bɝːd / (AmE), / ˈpʌf.bɜːd / (BrE)
A brownish tropical American bird noted for its large head and fluffed-out feathers, belonging to the family Bucconidae.

Examples

A puffbird species
Researchers observed a rare species of puffbird deep in the Amazon rainforest.
The puffbird sat quietly on a branch, its feathered head appearing unusually large.
With its fluffed-out plumage, the puffbird is easily recognized among tropical birds.
