monstrous

adjective
shockingly brutal or cruel
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑnstrəs / (AmE), / ˈmɒnstrəs / (BrE)
Extremely cruel, brutal, or shocking in a way that evokes horror or outrage; exceptionally evil.

Examples

Monstrous crime
His crimes were so monstrous that even his allies were horrified.
She could not forgive such a monstrous betrayal by someone she trusted.
The dictator's monstrous acts drew condemnation from the entire world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atrocious - shockingly wicked or cruel
  • synonym: heinous - utterly wicked or evil
  • antonym: merciful - marked by compassion or forgiveness
