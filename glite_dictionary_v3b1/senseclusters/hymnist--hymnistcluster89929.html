hymnist

noun
composer of hymns
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪmnɪst / (AmE), / ˈhɪmnɪst / (BrE)
a person who writes or composes hymns, especially for religious worship

Examples

Hymnist writes songs
The famous hymnist wrote many songs for the church choir.
As a dedicated hymnist, she contributed several new pieces to the annual hymnal.
Many churches honor the work of a hymnist by singing their compositions during services.
