consigliere

noun
trusted advisor
Pronunciation: / kənˌsɪliˈɛri /; / kənˌsiːliˈɛri / (AmE), / kɒnˌsɪliˈɛəreɪ / (BrE)
a trusted advisor or counselor, especially to a leader; often used for an official adviser in a criminal organization such as the Mafia

Examples

The consigliere advises
The president relied on his consigliere for advice during difficult negotiations.
As the family's consigliere, she always offered calm and wise counsel.
He became the unofficial consigliere to the startup's founder, guiding many key decisions.
The consigliere played a key role in negotiating peace between rival families.
As the trusted consigliere, he always offered wise counsel to the boss.
Without a skilled consigliere, the organization struggled to resolve internal conflicts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advisor - someone who gives advice, especially professionally
  • antonym: boss - the leader of an organization or group
  • synonym: counselor - a person who gives guidance or advice
  • antonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group
