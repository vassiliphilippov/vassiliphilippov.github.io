machan

noun
brother-in-law (South Asia)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːtʃʌn / (AmE), / ˈmɑːtʃʌn / (BrE)
a brother-in-law, especially as used as a kinship term in some South Asian languages and English varieties

Examples

During the wedding, the machan played an important role in the traditional ceremonies.
