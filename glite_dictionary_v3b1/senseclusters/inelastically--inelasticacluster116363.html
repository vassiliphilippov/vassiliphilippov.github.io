inelastically

adverb
without conserving energy
9.7
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ɪ.ˈlæs.tɪ.klɪ/ /; / /ɪn.ə.ˈlæs.tɪ.klɪ/ (AmE), /ɪn.ɪ.ˈlæs.tɪ.klɪ/ /; / /ɪn.ə.ˈlæs.tɪ.klɪ/ (BrE)
in a way that kinetic energy is not conserved during a collision, often resulting in energy loss as heat or deformation

Examples

Collides inelastically
When the clay balls collided inelastically, they stuck together and lost some energy as heat.
In physics experiments, objects may bounce inelastically if they deform and do not return to their original shape.
The two vehicles crashed inelastically, causing significant damage and absorbing much of the impact energy.
