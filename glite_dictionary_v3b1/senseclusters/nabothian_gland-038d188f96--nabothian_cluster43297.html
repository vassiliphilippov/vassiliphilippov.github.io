nabothian gland

noun
mucus-secreting gland of the cervix
9.9
Pronunciation: /nə.ˈboʊ.θi.ən glænd/ (AmE), /nə.ˈbɒ.θi.ən glænd/ (BrE)
one of the small mucus-secreting glands of the uterine cervix, whose ducts may become obstructed and form cysts

Examples

A nabothian gland cyst
A nabothian gland can sometimes be seen during a routine gynecological examination.
Blockage of a nabothian gland may lead to the formation of a small cervical cyst.
Most cases of nabothian gland cysts are harmless and do not require treatment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nabothian cyst - a mucus-filled cyst formed from a nabothian gland in the cervix
