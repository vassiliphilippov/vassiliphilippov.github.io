prefatory

adjective
serving as a preface or introduction
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛfəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈprɛfətri / (BrE)
serving as an introduction or preface, especially in a book or speech

Examples

A prefatory remark
After a brief prefatory remark, the lecturer began her main presentation.
The prefatory chapters set the stage for the rest of the book.
The author included a prefatory note to explain the context of the story.
