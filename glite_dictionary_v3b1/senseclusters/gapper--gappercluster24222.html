gapper

noun
stock with price gap
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæpər / (AmE), / ˈɡæpə / (BrE)
a stock or security that opens trading at a price significantly higher or lower than its previous closing price

Examples

A gapper stock
The financial news highlighted several gappers that caught traders' attention at the market open.
Experienced investors often look for gappers to find potential trading opportunities.
A sudden earnings report turned the company into a major gapper overnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gap down - when a stock opens at a lower price than its previous close
  • synonym: gap up - when a stock opens at a higher price than its previous close
