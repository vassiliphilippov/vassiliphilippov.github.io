disprover

noun
person who disproves claims or arguments
8.1
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈpruvər / (AmE), / dɪsˈpruːvə / (BrE)
a person who refutes an argument or claim by offering contrary evidence or reasoning

Examples

A disprover of claims
Every claim needs a potential disprover to ensure its robustness.
In debates, a skilled disprover relies on logic and detailed analysis.
The scientist acted as a disprover of the popular theory by presenting new evidence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: refuter - a person who proves something to be wrong or false
