Schrodinger wave equation

noun
main equation of quantum mechanics
Pronunciation: / ˈʃroʊdɪŋər weɪv ɪˈkweɪʒən / (AmE), / ˈʃrɜːdɪŋə weɪv ɪˈkweɪʒ(ə)n / (BrE)
