Synonyms

  • incorporation
    4.9 4.73/m
    incorporation often means adding parts into an existing organization, while integration stresses them working together as a single functioning whole.
  • merging
    3.0 0.06/m
    merging describes two entities joining to become one new unit, while integration focuses on combining parts so they work smoothly together.
  • consolidation
    5.5 5.25/m
    consolidation is combining parts to create a stronger, more coherent whole, while integration focuses on making them work together daily.
  • mix
    1.0 24/m
    mix often means physically or casually combining different things, while integration is a planned process to make parts work together.
  • mixture
    1.2 12/m
    mixture is the result of different things joined together, often without order, while integration makes parts function as a single system.
