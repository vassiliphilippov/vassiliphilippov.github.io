Coccyzus erythropthalmus

noun
species of North American cuckoo
Pronunciation: / kɑkˈsaɪzəs ˌɛrɪˌθrɒpˈθælməs / (AmE), / kɒkˈsaɪzəs ˌɛrɪˌθrɒpˈθælməs / (BrE)
A species of North American cuckoo, known as the black-billed cuckoo, which builds nests and rears its own young.
