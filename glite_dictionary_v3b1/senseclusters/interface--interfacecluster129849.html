interface

verb
to connect systems
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntərˌfeɪs / (AmE), / ˈɪntəfeɪs /; / ˈɪntəˌfeɪs / (BrE)
to connect or link systems, devices, or programs so they can work together or exchange information

Examples

To interface with software

Our app can interface directly with most popular payment platforms.
The new app can easily interface with your existing calendar software.
Engineers worked late to interface the two computer systems before the deadline.
You need to interface the device with the network to access all its features.

  • antonym: disconnect - to separate or break the connection between things
  • synonym: integrate - to combine parts to make a whole or work together
