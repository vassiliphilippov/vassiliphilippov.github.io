ultra

adverb
extremely; very
Pronunciation: / ˈʌltrə / (AmE), / ˈʌltrə / (BrE)
used informally to mean extremely or to a very high degree, for emphasis

Examples

Ultra fast car
The new phone is ultra thin and fits easily in your pocket.
She was ultra careful when handling the fragile vase.
His jokes are ultra funny and always make everyone laugh.
