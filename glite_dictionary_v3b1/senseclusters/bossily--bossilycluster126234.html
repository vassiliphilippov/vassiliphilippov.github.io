bossily

adverb
in a domineering way
4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑː.sɪ.lɪ/ /; / /ˈbɔː.sɪ.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈbɒs.ɪ.lɪ/ (BrE)
in a manner that shows a tendency to order people around or act like a boss

Examples

To speak bossily
He bossily told everyone what to do without listening to their ideas.
The manager spoke bossily to the staff during the meeting.
She bossily arranged the chairs and expected everyone to follow her instructions.
