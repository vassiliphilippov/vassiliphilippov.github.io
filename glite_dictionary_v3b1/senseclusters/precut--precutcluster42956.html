precut

adjective
already cut
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌkʌt / (BrE)
already cut into pieces or sections before being sold or used

Examples

Precut vegetables
The store sells precut fruit to make healthy snacks more convenient.
He bought a package of precut wood pieces for his craft project.
Using precut vegetables saved her a lot of time in the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ready-made - prepared in advance for immediate use
  • antonym: whole - not divided or cut into parts
