Parana River

proper noun
South American river, Rio de la Plata tributary
Pronunciation: / pɑːˈrɑːnə ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / pəˈrɑːnə ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
A major river in South America that flows through Brazil, Paraguay, and Argentina, and is a tributary of the Rio de la Plata.

Examples

The Parana River basin
