Mormonism

noun
religious beliefs of the Mormon Church
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔːr.məˌnɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈmɔː.məˌnɪz.əm / (BrE)
the religious doctrines, beliefs, and practices of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints and related groups, based chiefly on the Book of Mormon
