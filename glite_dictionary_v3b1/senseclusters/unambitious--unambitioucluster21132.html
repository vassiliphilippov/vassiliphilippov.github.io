unambitious

adjective
not having ambition or desire to succeed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.æmˈbɪʃ.əs / (AmE), / ˌʌn.æmˈbɪʃ.əs / (BrE)
not having or showing a strong desire to succeed or achieve something; lacking ambition.

Examples

An unambitious person
He was criticized as unambitious because he was content with a simple life.
Her unambitious attitude surprised her friends, who expected her to be more driven.
Many unambitious employees are happy just meeting the minimum requirements of their jobs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ambitious - having a strong desire for success or achievement
  • degree higher: lazy - unwilling to work or use energy
