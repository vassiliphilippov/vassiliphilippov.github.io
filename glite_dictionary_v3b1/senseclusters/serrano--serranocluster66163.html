serrano

noun
hot chili pepper
Pronunciation: / səˈrɑːnoʊ / (AmE), / səˈrɑːnəʊ / (BrE)
a small, hot chili pepper commonly used in Mexican and Southwestern cuisine

Examples

Fresh serrano pepper
She added a chopped serrano to the salsa for extra heat.
The recipe calls for two fresh serranos to give the dish a spicy kick.
He prefers using serrano peppers instead of jalapeños in his guacamole.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: habanero - a very hot chili pepper
