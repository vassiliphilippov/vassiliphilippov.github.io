ciabatta

noun
light slip-on shoe
Pronunciation: / tʃəˈbɑtə / (AmE), / tʃəˈbætə / (BrE)
a light, casual slip-on shoe, often resembling a slipper.

Examples

Ciabatta shoes
She slipped her feet into a pair of ciabatta before heading to the garden.
His old ciabatta were perfect for relaxing around the house on weekends.
After a long day, he enjoyed changing into his soft ciabatta for comfort.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boot - a sturdy shoe that covers the ankle
  • synonym: loafer - a slip-on shoe with no laces
  • synonym: slipper - a comfortable slip-on shoe worn indoors
