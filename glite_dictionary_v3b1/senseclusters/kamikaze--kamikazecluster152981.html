kamikaze

adjective
recklessly self-destructive
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːməˈkɑːzi / (AmE), / ˌkæmɪˈkɑːzi / (BrE)
acting in a reckless, self-destructive, or dangerously bold way, often without regard for personal safety.

Examples

Kamikaze move
He made a kamikaze decision to invest all his savings in a risky business.
The driver's kamikaze maneuver shocked everyone on the road.
Her kamikaze approach to the exam left her exhausted but satisfied.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cautious - careful to avoid danger or risk
  • synonym: reckless - not caring about danger or consequences
