Able to absorb liquid or take up a substance, especially without repelling it.

Examples

Receptive paper
The artist prefers paper that is highly receptive to ink for detailed drawings.
The filter has a receptive surface that allows rapid absorption of water.
This type of fabric is especially receptive to dye, resulting in brighter colors.

Synonyms

  • absorbent
    3.4 0.97/m
    absorbent soaks up and holds liquids easily for storage, while receptive takes in liquids without repelling them but may not hold them firmly.
  • absorptive
    6.8 0.21/m
    absorptive is a technical word for a material that soaks up liquids or energy, while receptive simply means able to take them in.
