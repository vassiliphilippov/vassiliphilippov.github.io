baby

noun
term of endearment for a loved one
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪ.bi / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi / (BrE)
A person who is loved very much, especially used as a term of affection for a romantic partner or loved one.

Examples

My baby darling
Don't worry, baby, everything will be fine.
He whispered, 'baby, I miss you' before leaving for the airport.
She called him baby and wrapped her arms around him.
