nutting

noun
gathering nuts
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnʌtɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of gathering nuts from trees or bushes, especially as a leisure pursuit

Examples

To nutting in autumn
Every autumn, families enjoy nutting in the countryside to collect fresh walnuts and hazelnuts.
The children spent the afternoon nutting under the tall chestnut trees near the river.
In the past, nutting was a popular outdoor activity during the fall season.
