Dominican peso

noun
currency of the Dominican Republic
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdoʊmɪˈniːkən ˈpeɪsoʊ / (AmE), / ˌdɒmɪˈniːkən ˈpeɪsəʊ / (BrE)
