hoolock

noun
Asian gibbon species
Pronunciation: / ˈhuːlɑk / (AmE), / ˈhuːlɒk / (BrE)
a type of gibbon native to the forests of South and Southeast Asia, known for its loud calls

Examples

The hoolock gibbon
The hoolock is an endangered primate found in the forests of Bangladesh and India.
Researchers observed a family of hoolocks swinging gracefully from tree to tree.
Early in the morning, the loud calls of the hoolock echo through the jungle.
