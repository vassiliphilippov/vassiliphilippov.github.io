fullback

noun
offensive football position; blocking running back
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊlˌbæk /; / ˈfʊl.bæk / (AmE), / ˈfʊlbæk /; / ˈfʊl.bæk / (BrE)
(in American football) a player on the offensive team who lines up behind the quarterback and is mainly responsible for blocking and sometimes running with the ball or catching passes

Examples

Powerful fullback run
As a fullback, he was known for his strength and reliable short-yardage gains.
She earned a scholarship to play fullback at a top college football program.
The team's fullback led an effective block for the running back to score a touchdown.
As a rookie fullback, he quickly learned the importance of protecting the quarterback.
Our coach decided to use the fullback on short-yardage plays.
The team's fullback excelled at blocking defenders to open lanes for the running back.
