hyphenated

adjective
joined by hyphen
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪfəˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈhaɪfənˌeɪtɪd / (BrE)
joined or connected by a hyphen between words or parts

Examples

Hyphenated compound word
The editor prefers hyphenated words in formal writing to avoid confusion.
Her last name is hyphenated because it combines both of her parents' surnames.
In English, some adjectives are hyphenated when placed before a noun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compound - a word made up of two or more parts joined together
  • antonym: unhyphenated - not joined by a hyphen
