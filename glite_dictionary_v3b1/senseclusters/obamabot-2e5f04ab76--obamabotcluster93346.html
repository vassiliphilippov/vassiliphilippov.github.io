Obamabot

noun
slang for Obama supporter
Pronunciation: / oʊˈbɑːməˌbɑt / (AmE), / əʊˈbɑːməˌbɒt / (BrE)
a slang term for an uncritical or blindly loyal supporter of Barack Obama, especially online

Examples

Online Obamabot supporter
Some people on the forum were labeled Obamabots because they defended every policy without question.
The article criticized the group of Obamabots for ignoring any negative news about the administration.
He was called an Obamabot after he dismissed all opposing viewpoints in the debate.

Related Terms

  • antonym: critic - a person who expresses disapproval or finds fault
  • synonym: fanboy - a person who is extremely enthusiastic or devoted to a particular person or thing
