evaluator

noun
person who assesses value or quality
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvæljueɪtər / (AmE), / ɪˈvæljueɪtə / (BrE)
a person who assesses the value, quality, or importance of something

Examples

A skilled evaluator
A panel of evaluators will judge the competition based on creativity and presentation.
As an experienced evaluator, she is trusted to provide fair and accurate feedback.
The evaluator carefully reviewed each student's project before giving a final grade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assessor - a person who evaluates something or someone
  • synonym: critic - someone who evaluates and forms opinions on artistic works
