adrenocorticotrophic hormone

noun
hormone from anterior pituitary stimulating adrenal cortex
Pronunciation: / əˌdrinoʊˌkɔrtɪkoʊˈtroʊfɪk ˈhɔrmoʊn / (AmE), / əˌdriːnəʊˌkɔːtɪkəʊˈtrɒfɪk ˈhɔːməʊn / (BrE)
a hormone produced by the anterior pituitary gland that stimulates the adrenal cortex to secrete corticosteroids

Examples
