harlequin snake

noun
venomous, brightly banded New World snake
5.0 7.8
Pronunciation: /ˈhɑːr.lɪ.kwɪn sneɪk/ /; / /ˈhɑːr.lə.kwɪn sneɪk/ (AmE), /ˈhɑː.lɪ.kwɪn sneɪk/ /; / /ˈhɑː.lə.kwɪn sneɪk/ (BrE)
