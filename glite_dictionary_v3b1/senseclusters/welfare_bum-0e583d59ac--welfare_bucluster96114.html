welfare bum

noun
derogatory term for person on welfare
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛlfɛr bʌm / (AmE), / ˈwɛlfeə bʌm / (BrE)
(offensive, informal) A derogatory term for someone who relies on government assistance, especially one perceived as not seeking employment or abusing the system.

Examples

A welfare bum
She refused to let anyone label her a welfare bum, despite her circumstances.
The media portrayal of welfare bums often contributes to negative stereotypes about the poor.
The politician was criticized for calling unemployed citizens welfare bums during his speech.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scrounger - a person who borrows or lives off others, especially instead of working
