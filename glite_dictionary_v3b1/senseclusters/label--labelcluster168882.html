label

noun
word or phrase for identification
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪ.bəl / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.bəl / (BrE)
a word or phrase used to describe or identify something or someone

Examples

The label of genius
He disliked the label of 'genius' because it set high expectations.
Some people find it unfair when a negative label is attached to a whole group.
The museum gave each artifact a label describing its origin and age.
