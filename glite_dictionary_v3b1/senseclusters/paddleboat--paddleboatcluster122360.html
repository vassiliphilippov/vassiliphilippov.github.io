paddleboat

noun
steamboat with paddle wheels
Pronunciation: / ˈpædəlˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈpædəlˌbəʊt / (BrE)
a large boat, often powered by steam, that moves using one or more paddle wheels, especially on rivers

Examples

A river paddleboat
The old paddleboat offered scenic tours along the wide river every summer.
Tourists enjoyed dinner and music on the historic paddleboat as it glided past the city lights.
A restored paddleboat is now a floating museum on the waterfront.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ferry - a boat that carries people or vehicles across water
  • synonym: steamboat - a boat powered by a steam engine
