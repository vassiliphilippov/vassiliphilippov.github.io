10-membered

adjective
having a ring of ten members (chemistry)
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛn ˌmɛmbɚd / (AmE), / ˈtɛn ˌmɛmbəd / (BrE)
(of a chemical compound) having a ring structure composed of ten atoms or groups.

Examples

A 10-membered ring
A 10-membered carbocyclic ring is less common than six- or five-membered ones.
Chemists have synthesized a new 10-membered lactone to study its properties.
The molecule contains a 10-membered ring that is crucial to its biological activity.
