Examples

To familiarize yourself with terms
She took time to familiarize herself with the scientific terms before the meeting.
Teachers often familiarize students with exam formats in advance.
The company offers orientation sessions to help new employees familiarize themselves with workplace procedures.

Synonyms

  • acquaint
    4.0 0.95/m
    acquaint makes someone briefly aware or introduces them to something, while familiarize makes them more deeply knowledgeable and ready to use it.
  • orient
    4.2 0.55/m
    orient shows new people the place, rules, or tasks so they can start well, while familiarize makes them fully aware of details.
  • accustom
    3.6 11/m
    accustom brings repeated exposure so something feels normal and easy, while familiarize makes someone aware or knowledgeable about it.
