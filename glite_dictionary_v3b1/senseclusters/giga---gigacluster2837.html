in computing, a prefix meaning 2^30 (1,073,741,824) units, especially used for bytes or bits

Examples

Giga- prefix in computing
In older computer manuals, giga- was often used to mean 2^30 bytes instead of one billion.
A hard drive label might use the giga- prefix to indicate 2^30 bytes per gigabyte.
The confusion between giga- as 2^30 and 10^9 has led to the adoption of the 'gibi-' prefix.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gibi- - a prefix meaning exactly 2^30 units in computing
