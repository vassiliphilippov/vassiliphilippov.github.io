candy corn

noun
triangular Halloween candy resembling a corn kernel
Pronunciation: / ˈkændi kɔrn / (AmE), / ˈkændi kɔːn / (BrE)
a small, triangular, yellow, orange, and white candy made of sugar and shaped to resemble a kernel of corn, typically associated with Halloween in the United States

Examples

A bag of candy corn
Every October, stores fill their shelves with bags of candy corn.
She decorated the cupcakes with tiny pieces of candy corn for a festive touch.
Some people love candy corn, while others refuse to eat it.
