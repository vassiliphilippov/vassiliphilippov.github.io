insanity

noun
severe mental illness; loss of sanity
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsæn.ə.ti /; / ɪnˈsænəti / (AmE), / ɪnˈsæn.ɪ.ti /; / ɪnˈsænəti / (BrE)
a state of severe mental illness, or extremely foolish or irrational behavior

Examples

Severe insanity state
Some crimes are committed during periods of insanity when the person cannot distinguish right from wrong.
The defendant pleaded insanity to avoid a prison sentence.
The novel explores the fine line between genius and insanity.
The court had to determine if his actions were caused by insanity or by intent.
Some people thought her decision to quit her job was pure insanity.
Throughout history, the definition of insanity has changed many times.
