authority

noun
people or groups in power; officials
Pronunciation: / əˈθɔr.ə.t̬i / (AmE), / ɔːˈθɒr.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
(usually plural) a group of people or an organization who have the power to make decisions and enforce laws or rules.

Examples

Local authorities
Local authorities are investigating the source of the contamination.
The authorities closed the road due to hazardous weather conditions.
The authorities imposed strict travel restrictions during the outbreak.

Related Terms

  • synonym: government - the group of people who govern a country or state
  • synonym: official - a person holding public office or having official duties
