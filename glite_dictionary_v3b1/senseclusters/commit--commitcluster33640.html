commit

verb
carry out an illegal or bad act
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɪt / (AmE), / kəˈmɪt / (BrE)
to carry out or perpetrate (especially an act regarded as wrong or illegal)

Examples

To commit a crime
He was caught trying to commit a theft at the store.
It is a serious offense to commit fraud against the government.
Many people feel regret after they commit a crime.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perpetrate - to carry out or perform (a harmful, illegal, or immoral action)
