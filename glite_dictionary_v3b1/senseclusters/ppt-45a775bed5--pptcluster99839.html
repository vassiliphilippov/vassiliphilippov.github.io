PPT

noun
parts per trillion
8.0
Pronunciation: / pi pi ti / (AmE), / piː piː tiː / (BrE)
a unit meaning 'parts per trillion', used to measure extremely small concentrations of a substance in a mixture or solution

Examples

2 PPT chemical
The laboratory detected only 2 PPT of the chemical in the water sample.
Regulations limit the presence of certain toxins to less than 5 PPT in food products.
Scientists measured the pollutant at 10 PPT, indicating a very low concentration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: PPB - parts per billion, a unit for measuring very small concentrations
  • synonym: PPM - parts per million, a unit for measuring small concentrations
