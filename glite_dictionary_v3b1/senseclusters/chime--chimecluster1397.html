chime

noun
musical bell sound
Pronunciation: / tʃaɪm / (AmE), / tʃaɪm / (BrE)
a clear, musical sound produced by a bell or a set of bells

Examples

The chime of bells
The gentle chime of the wind chimes soothed everyone on the porch.
At noon, the chime from the church tower could be heard across the town.
She smiled when she heard the familiar chime announcing a new message on her phone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clang - a loud, resonant metallic sound
  • synonym: peal - a loud ringing of bells
