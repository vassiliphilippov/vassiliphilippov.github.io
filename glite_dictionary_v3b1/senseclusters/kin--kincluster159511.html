kin

noun
relatives; family group
7.7
Pronunciation: / kɪn / (AmE), / kɪn / (BrE)
a group of people who are related to each other by blood or marriage; relatives or family members

Examples

He invited all his kin to the family reunion.
It is important to respect your elders and your kin.
