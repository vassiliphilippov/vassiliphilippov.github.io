inflatable

noun
air-filled object
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfleɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈfleɪtəbəl / (BrE)
an object made to be filled with air or gas so that it becomes larger and can be used or played with

Examples

A big inflatable
The children jumped on the large inflatable shaped like a castle at the fair.
We packed an inflatable for the pool so everyone could relax on the water.
After the party, they deflated the inflatable and stored it in the garage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: balloon - a thin rubber bag that is filled with air or gas
  • synonym: raft - a flat inflatable structure used for floating on water
