clover-leaf roll

noun
yeast-raised roll with a clover shape
9.9 5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊvər liːf roʊl / (AmE), / ˈkləʊvə liːf rəʊl / (BrE)
A small yeast-raised dinner roll made by baking three small balls of dough together in each cup of a muffin pan, resulting in a roll with a clover-like shape.

Examples

A clover-leaf roll
Bakers often brush a clover-leaf roll with melted butter before baking for a golden crust.
She served hot clover-leaf rolls with butter at Thanksgiving dinner.
The clover-leaf roll is easy to pull apart because of its three sections.
