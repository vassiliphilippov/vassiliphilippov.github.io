stope

verb
to mine ore underground
9.9
Pronunciation: / stoʊp / (AmE), / stəʊp / (BrE)
to excavate ore from underground by creating or working a step-like cavity called a stope

Examples

To stope a tunnel
The miners will stope the new section of the tunnel next week.
Modern equipment allows crews to stope more efficiently and safely than in the past.
They carefully stope the ore to avoid collapsing the surrounding rock.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fill - to make something full, especially a cavity or space
  • synonym: mine - to extract minerals or other geological materials from the earth
