glucosidase

noun
sugar-breaking enzyme
8.9
Pronunciation: /gluː.kə.ˈsaɪ.deɪs/ /; / /gluː.koʊ.ˈsaɪ.deɪs/ (AmE), /gluː.kə.ˈsaɪ.deɪs/ /; / /gluː.kəʊ.ˈsaɪ.deɪs/ (BrE)
