gate

verb
to control using a gate or valve
6.6
Pronunciation: / ɡeɪt / (AmE), / ɡeɪt / (BrE)
to control or regulate (a signal, flow, or process) using a gate, valve, or similar device

Examples

To gate a signal
Digital circuits frequently gate incoming data to synchronize with the system clock.
Industrial processes often gate the flow of chemicals to ensure safety.
The technician used a switch to gate the electrical signal during testing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: regulate - to control or maintain the rate or speed of a machine or process
