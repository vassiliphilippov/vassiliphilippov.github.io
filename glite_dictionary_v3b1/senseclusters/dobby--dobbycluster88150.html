dobby

noun
patterned woven fabric
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːbi / (AmE), / ˈdɒbi / (BrE)
a fabric woven with small geometric patterns, often created using a special loom attachment called a dobby

Examples

A dobby fabric
The tailor recommended a dobby fabric for its subtle texture and durability.
She admired the small geometric designs woven into the dobby cotton of her new blouse.
Many dress shirts are made from dobby because of its elegant patterned appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jacquard - a fabric with complex woven patterns made on a Jacquard loom
  • antonym: plain weave - a simple woven fabric without patterns
