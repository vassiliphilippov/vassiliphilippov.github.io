gas guzzler

noun
car with high fuel consumption
Pronunciation: / ɡæs ˈɡʌz.lɚ / (AmE), / ɡæs ˈɡʌz.lə / (BrE)
a car that uses a lot of fuel and is not fuel-efficient

Examples

A gas guzzler car
He traded his gas guzzler for a hybrid car to help the environment.
Many people avoid buying a gas guzzler because of rising fuel prices.
The old SUV was a notorious gas guzzler, costing a fortune to fill up every week.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fuel-efficient - using little fuel compared to others of the same type
