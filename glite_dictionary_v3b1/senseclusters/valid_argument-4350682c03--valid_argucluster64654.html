valid argument

noun
logically sound reasoning
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈvælɪd ˈɑːrɡjəmənt / (AmE), / ˈvælɪd ˈɑːɡjʊmənt / (BrE)
a point or line of reasoning that is logically sound and reasonable

Examples

A valid argument in debate

We should always consider every valid argument before making a decision.
