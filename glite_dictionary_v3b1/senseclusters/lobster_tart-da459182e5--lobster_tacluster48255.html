lobster tart

noun
pastry filled with cooked lobster
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːbstɚ tɑrt / (AmE), / ˈlɒbstə tɑːt / (BrE)
A tart consisting of a pastry shell filled with cooked lobster, often served as an appetizer or main course.

Examples

A lobster tart dish
Her recipe for lobster tart blends flaky pastry with tender pieces of lobster.
The chef's signature dish was a delicious lobster tart with a creamy herb sauce.
We ordered a lobster tart as a starter at the fancy seafood restaurant.
