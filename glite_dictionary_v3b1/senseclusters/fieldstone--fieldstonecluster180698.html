fieldstone

noun
naturally occurring stone used for building
7.9 6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfiːld.stoʊn/ /; / /ˈfɪld.stoʊn/ (AmE), /ˈfiːld.stəʊn/ /; / /ˈfɪld.stəʊn/ (BrE)
A stone found naturally on the surface of fields and often used in building or landscaping without being cut or shaped.

Examples

Large fieldstone slabs

Many old farmhouses used locally gathered fieldstone in their foundations.
The cottage was built entirely of fieldstone, giving it a rustic charm.
