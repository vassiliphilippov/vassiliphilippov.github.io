Synonyms

  • depend upon
    2.3 3/m
    depend upon is slightly more formal and often used in writing, while depend on is common in speech.
  • depend
    1.1 80/m
    depend is the base verb used with many structures, while depend on shows what something is influenced or decided by.
  • hinge on
    4.2 2/m
    hinge on is used when outcomes rely completely on one thing, while depend on can be used more generally.
  • hinge upon
    5.5 0.08/m
    hinge upon sounds slightly formal and shows full reliance on one factor, while depend on fits everyday use.
  • hinge
    4.3 0.6/m
    hinge often appears with on or upon to show full reliance, while depend on is more neutral and common.
  • hang upon
    5.4 0.00/m
    hang upon feels old-fashioned and formal, often found in writing, while depend on is modern and common in speech.
  • turn on
    4.1 0.9/m
    turn on can mean depend in some contexts but also means activate, while depend on only means be influenced or decided by.
  • ride
    5.3 11/m
    ride can mean to depend on in informal speech, but depend on is a clearer, neutral phrase for general use.
