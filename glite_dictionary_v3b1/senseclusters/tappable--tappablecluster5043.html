tappable

adjective
can be tapped
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtæpəbəl / (AmE), / ˈtæpəbəl / (BrE)
able to be tapped, especially by touching a screen or surface to activate a function

Examples

Tappable screen icon
Each icon on the screen is tappable for quick access.
The new app design makes every button easily tappable with your finger.
If a link is not tappable, users may get frustrated trying to use the site.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clickable - able to be clicked, especially with a mouse or pointer
  • antonym: untappable - not able to be tapped or activated by touch
