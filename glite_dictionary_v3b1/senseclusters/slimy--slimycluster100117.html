slimy

adjective
covered with or like slime
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈslaɪmi / (AmE), / ˈslaɪmi / (BrE)
covered with or resembling slime; having a wet, sticky, or slippery texture like slime

Examples

Slimy surface
A slimy substance oozed from the bottom of the old bucket.
She recoiled at the slimy feel of the fish in her hands.
The rocks by the river were covered in a slimy layer of algae.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dry - free from moisture or liquid; not wet
  • synonym: slippery - difficult to hold or stand on because it is smooth, wet, or slimy
  • synonym: sticky - having the quality of adhering or sticking to a surface
