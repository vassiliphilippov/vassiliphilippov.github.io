drivel

noun
saliva coming from the mouth
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɪvəl / (AmE), / ˈdrɪvəl / (BrE)
Saliva that flows or spills from the mouth, especially unintentionally.

Examples

A bit of drivel
She was so exhausted that a little drivel escaped while she dozed in her chair.
The infant's face was covered in drivel after his nap.
The old man wiped the drivel from his chin with a handkerchief.
