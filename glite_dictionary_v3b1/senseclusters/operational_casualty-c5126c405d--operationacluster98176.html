operational casualty

noun
military loss of equipment in operations
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːpəˈreɪʃənl ˈkæʒuəlti / (AmE), / ˌɒpəˈreɪʃənl ˈkæʒuəlti / (BrE)
the loss or destruction of military equipment during field operations, especially as a result of enemy action or mishaps
