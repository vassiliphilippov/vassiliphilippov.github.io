untasted

adjective
not yet tasted
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈteɪstɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈteɪstɪd / (BrE)
not having been tasted; not tried or sampled by tasting.

Examples

An untasted meal
His lunch remained untasted as he hurried off to his next meeting.
She poured a glass of wine and left it untasted on the counter.
The cake sat on the table, completely untasted after the guests left.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tasted - having been tried or experienced by tasting
  • synonym: untouched - not yet touched or handled; undisturbed
