collectibility

noun
desirability to collectors
7.2 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / k\u0259\u02ccl\u025bkt\u0259\u02c8b\u026al\u026ati / (AmE), / k\u0259\u02ccl\u025bkt\u026a\u02c8b\u026al\u026ati / (BrE)
The quality of an object that makes it desirable to collectors, often determined by rarity, condition, provenance, or historical interest.

Examples

Collectibility of antiques
The limited-edition vinyl's collectibility increased after the band announced they would never press it again.
Experts said the toy's original box significantly improved its collectibility among serious collectors.
Condition, rarity, and provenance all affect an item's collectibility on the resale market.

Synonyms

  • collectability
    5.7 0.01/m
    collectability often describes how suitable an item is for hobbyists or investors, while collectibility focuses on desirability to collectors due to rarity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collectible - an item that is desirable to collectors because of rarity, condition, or appeal
