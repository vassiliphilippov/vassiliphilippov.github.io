Pneumocystis

noun
fungus causing pneumonia
9.9
Pronunciation: /nuː.mə.ˈsɪs.tɪs/ /; / /njuː.mə.ˈsɪs.tɪs/ (AmE), /njuː.mə.ˈsɪs.tɪs/ /; / /nuː.mə.ˈsɪs.tɪs/ (BrE)
a genus of fungi that can cause lung infections, especially in people with weakened immune systems

Examples

Pneumocystis lung infection
Doctors diagnosed her with a lung infection caused by Pneumocystis after her immune system became weakened.
People with HIV are at higher risk of developing pneumonia from Pneumocystis.
Treatment for Pneumocystis often involves special antibiotics and supportive care in the hospital.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Pneumocystis pneumonia - a type of pneumonia caused by Pneumocystis fungi
  • synonym: fungus - a group of organisms including molds, yeasts, and mushrooms
