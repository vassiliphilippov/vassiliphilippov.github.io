bouldering

noun
climbing without ropes
4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈboʊl.dər.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈboʊl.dɪr.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈbəʊl.də.rɪŋ/ (BrE)
a form of rock climbing performed on small rock formations or artificial walls without the use of ropes or harnesses

Examples

Bouldering at gyms
Many people try bouldering at indoor gyms before climbing outdoors.
She improved her strength and technique through regular bouldering sessions.
Safety mats are placed under the wall to protect climbers during bouldering.

Related Terms

  • synonym: free climbing - climbing using only hands and feet for support, with ropes for safety
  • synonym: rock climbing - the sport of climbing natural rock formations or artificial rock walls
