meter

noun
unit of length (SI)
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːtɚ /; / ˈmiːtər / (AmE), / ˈmiːtə / (BrE)
the basic unit of length in the International System of Units, equal to approximately 39.37 inches or 1.094 yards

Examples

He ran a distance of one hundred metres in record time.
The Olympic pool must be exactly 50 metres long.
The building stands over thirty metres tall.
