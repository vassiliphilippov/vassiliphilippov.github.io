parapharyngeal

adjective
near the pharynx
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəfəˈrɪndʒiəl / (AmE), / ˌpærəfəˈrɪndʒiəl / (BrE)
located near or beside the pharynx in the throat

Examples

A parapharyngeal abscess
A parapharyngeal abscess can cause severe pain and swelling in the neck.
The surgeon examined the parapharyngeal space for signs of infection.
Tumors in the parapharyngeal region are rare but can be serious.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peripharyngeal - situated around the pharynx
