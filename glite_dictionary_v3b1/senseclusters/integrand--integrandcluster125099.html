integrand

noun
function to integrate
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntəˌɡrænd / (AmE), / ˈɪntɪɡrænd / (BrE)
a function that is to be integrated in a mathematical operation

Examples

The integrand function
In the integral ∫x^2 dx, the integrand is x squared.
To solve the problem, first identify the integrand in the equation.
The complexity of the integrand can affect the difficulty of integration.
