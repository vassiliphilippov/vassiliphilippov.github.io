liking

noun
fondness or preference
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
a feeling of pleasure and enjoyment; a fondness or preference for something or someone

Examples

A liking for food
After trying the dish, he developed a liking for spicy food.
My liking for hiking grew as I spent more weekends outdoors.
She has always had a liking for classical music and attends concerts regularly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dislike - a feeling of distaste or aversion
  • synonym: preference - a greater liking for one alternative over another
