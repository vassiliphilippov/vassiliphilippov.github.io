chipmunk

noun
small striped ground squirrel
2.3 1.6
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃɪp.məŋk/ /; / /ˈtʃɪp.mʌŋk/ (AmE), /ˈtʃɪp.mʌŋk/ /; / /ˈtʃɪp.məŋk/ (BrE)
a small ground-dwelling rodent of North America and Asia, known for its striped back and cheek pouches, belonging to the squirrel family

Examples

The chipmunk stuffed its cheeks with seeds before scurrying back to its burrow.
We watched a chipmunk gather acorns near the picnic table.
