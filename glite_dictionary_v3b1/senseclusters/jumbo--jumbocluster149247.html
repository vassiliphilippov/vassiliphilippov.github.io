jumbo

noun
very large thing
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌmboʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmbəʊ / (BrE)
a very large person, animal, or thing

Examples

A jumbo muffin
The bakery sells a jumbo muffin that is twice the size of a regular one.
He won a jumbo teddy bear at the carnival by playing a game.
They ordered a jumbo pizza to share with the whole group.

Related Terms

  • antonym: miniature - a very small version of something
