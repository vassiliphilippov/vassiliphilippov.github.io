immunoblot

verb
to test for proteins
9.2
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmjunəˌblɑt / (AmE), / ɪˈmjuːnəˌblɒt / (BrE)
to perform an immunoblot assay on a sample to detect or identify specific proteins

Examples

To immunoblot samples
The researchers will immunoblot the samples to check for the presence of the target protein.
After extracting the proteins, the next step is to immunoblot them for analysis.
She learned how to immunoblot during her laboratory training at the university.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ELISA - a test that uses antibodies and color change to identify a substance
  • synonym: western blot - a laboratory method used to detect specific proteins in a sample
