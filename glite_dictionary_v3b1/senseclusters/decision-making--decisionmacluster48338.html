decision-making

noun
the process of making decisions
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɪʒ.ən ˌmeɪ.kɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈsɪʒ.ən ˌmeɪ.kɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of making choices or reaching conclusions, especially after considering various alternatives

Examples

Good decision-making process
Good decision-making is critical for effective leadership in any organization.
He realized that stress was affecting his decision-making abilities.
The policy aims to involve more people in the decision-making process.

Related Terms
