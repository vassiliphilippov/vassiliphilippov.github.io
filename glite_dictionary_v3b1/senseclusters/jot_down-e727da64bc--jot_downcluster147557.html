jot down

verb
write something quickly and briefly
8.0
Pronunciation: / dʒɑt daʊn / (AmE), / dʒɒt daʊn / (BrE)
to write something quickly and briefly, usually as a short note or reminder

Examples

To jot down notes
During the meeting, he jotted down a few important points in his notebook.
She quickly jotted down the phone number before she forgot it.
Whenever I have a new idea, I try to jot down my thoughts instantly.
Can you jot down your contact details for me before you leave?
He jotted down the directions to the new restaurant on a napkin.
Whenever an idea pops into her head, she jots down a quick note in her phone.
