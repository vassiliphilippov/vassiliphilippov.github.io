lecture

a long and serious talk given to someone in order to criticize or warn them, often as a form of discipline

Examples

A lecture on behavior
After he came home late, his mother gave him a lecture on responsibility.
He received a stern lecture from his boss after missing the meeting.
No one wants to sit through another lecture about keeping their room tidy.

Synonyms

  • talking to
    4.2 0.02/m
    talking to is an informal one-on-one scolding used to warn someone, while lecture is a longer formal talk that criticizes.
  • speech
    4.8 8.94/m
    speech is a formal public address often given to many people, while lecture is a serious talk meant to warn or scold individuals.
