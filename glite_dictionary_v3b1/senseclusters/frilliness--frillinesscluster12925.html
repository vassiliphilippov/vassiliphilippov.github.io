frilliness

noun
ornate quality
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪlinəs / (AmE), / ˈfrɪlɪnəs /; / ˈfrɪlinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being excessively ornate, decorated, showy, or fussy (in appearance, style, or behavior)

Examples

He tried to hide his nervousness with jokes and a certain frilliness in his manner.
Some people prefer a simple style without any frilliness or unnecessary decoration.
