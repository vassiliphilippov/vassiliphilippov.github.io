frilliness

noun
ornate quality
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪlinəs / (AmE), / ˈfrɪlɪnəs /; / ˈfrɪlinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being excessively ornate, decorated, showy, or fussy (in appearance, style, or behavior)
