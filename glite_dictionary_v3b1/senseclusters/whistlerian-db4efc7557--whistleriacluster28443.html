Whistlerian

noun
admirer of Whistler
9.9
Pronunciation: / wɪsˈtlɪriən / (AmE), / wɪsˈtlɪəriən / (BrE)
a person who admires, follows, or supports the painter James McNeill Whistler or his artistic style

Examples

A true Whistlerian
As a proud Whistlerian, she collected books and prints related to the artist's life.
The museum's new exhibit attracted many Whistlerians eager to see rare paintings.
He considered himself a Whistlerian and often discussed Whistler's influence on modern art.
