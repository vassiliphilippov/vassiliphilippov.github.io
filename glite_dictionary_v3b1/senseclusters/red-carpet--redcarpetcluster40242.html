red-carpet

adjective
special or VIP treatment
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛd ˌkɑrpɪt / (AmE), / ˈrɛd ˌkɑːpɪt / (BrE)
Giving special or VIP treatment; showing exceptional hospitality or honor to someone, often as if they were an important guest.

Examples

A red-carpet welcome
She was treated to a red-carpet welcome on her first day at work.
The guests received red-carpet service at the luxury hotel throughout their stay.
The mayor promised to give the ambassadors a truly red-carpet reception.

Related Terms

  • synonym: VIP treatment - exceptional service or honor reserved for important people
  • antonym: ordinary - usual, not given special status or treatment
