miasma

noun
unhealthy or unpleasant vapor
6.2
Pronunciation: / miˈæzmə / (AmE), / miˈæzmə / (BrE)
An unpleasant or unhealthy smell or vapor, especially one that was formerly believed to cause disease.

Examples

A miasma of smoke
A thick miasma hung over the swamp, making it hard to breathe.
In the novel, a miasma of smoke filled the dimly lit room.
The miasma from the landfill drifted across the town during the summer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aura - a distinctive atmosphere or quality, often pleasant
  • synonym: effluvium - an unpleasant or harmful odor or discharge
