bong

noun
a deep resonant bell sound
Pronunciation: / bɑŋ / (AmE), / bɒŋ / (BrE)
a dull, resonant sound, especially the stroke of a bell

Examples

A bell bong
A deep bong resounded from somewhere in the darkness, making everyone turn their heads.
At midnight, the final bong from the bell tower signaled the start of the new year.
The bong of the church clock echoed through the quiet village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clang - a loud, resonant metallic sound
  • degree lower: ding - the sound of a small bell
