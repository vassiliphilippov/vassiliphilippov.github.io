lichen

verb
to cover with lichen
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪkən / (AmE), / ˈlɪkən / (BrE)
to become covered with lichen or to cause something to be covered with lichen.

Examples

Wall lichened over
The old stone wall had lichened over the decades, giving it a greenish appearance.
After years of neglect, the statue slowly lichened in the damp garden.
The wooden fence lichened quickly in the moist climate near the forest.
