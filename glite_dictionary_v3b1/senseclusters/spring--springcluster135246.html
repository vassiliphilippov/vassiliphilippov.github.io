spring

verb
to rebound or bounce back after impact
Pronunciation: / sprɪŋ / (AmE), / sprɪŋ / (BrE)
to move suddenly or quickly, especially by jumping, leaping, or bouncing

Examples

To spring up quickly
The coin sprung out of his hand when he accidentally hit it.
The tennis ball sprang off the wall and rolled across the court.
Watch how the trampoline mat springs back into shape after each jump.
He springs forward at the sound of the starting gun.
Rabbits often spring across the field when startled.
The cat sprang onto the windowsill and stared outside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bounce - to rebound off a surface after hitting it
  • synonym: bound - to move by leaping or jumping
  • synonym: leap - to jump or spring a long way, to a great height, or with great force
  • synonym: rebound - to spring back after hitting something
