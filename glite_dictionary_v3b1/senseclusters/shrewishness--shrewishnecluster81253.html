shrewishness

noun
the state of being nagging or scolding
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃruɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈʃruːɪʃnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being bad-tempered, nagging, or scolding, especially in a woman

Examples

Her shrewishness showed
Despite her reputation for shrewishness, she had a kind heart beneath her harsh exterior.
He could no longer tolerate the shrewishness that dominated their conversations at home.
Her shrewishness made it difficult for anyone to work with her in the office.

  • antonym: meekness - the quality of being gentle or submissive
