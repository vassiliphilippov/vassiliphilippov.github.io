lip-gloss

noun
shiny cosmetic for lips
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪp ɡlɑs / (AmE), / ˈlɪp ɡlɒs / (BrE)
A cosmetic gel or liquid applied to the lips to give them a shiny, glossy appearance.

Examples

Shiny lip-gloss
My sister collects every flavor of lip-gloss she can find.
She applied a layer of lip-gloss before leaving for the party.
The new lip-gloss promises longer-lasting shine and a hint of color.
