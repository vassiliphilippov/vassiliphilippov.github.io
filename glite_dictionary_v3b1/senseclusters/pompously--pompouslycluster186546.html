pompously

adverb
in an arrogant or self-important manner
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːmpəsli / (AmE), / ˈpɒmpəsli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows self-importance or arrogance; in an overly grand or dignified way

Examples

To speak pompously
He pompously corrected everyone at the meeting as if he knew everything.
She entered the room pompously, expecting everyone to notice her presence.
The manager spoke pompously about his minor achievements, hoping to impress the staff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrogantly - in an arrogant manner
  • antonym: modestly - in a modest or unassuming manner
