Queensland bottletree

noun
Australian tree with swollen trunk and cream flowers
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkwiːnzˌlænd ˈbɑtlˌtri / (AmE), / ˈkwiːnzˌlænd ˈbɒtlˌtriː / (BrE)
a large tree native to Queensland, Australia, with cream-colored flowers blotched with red inside, known for its bulbous trunk; sometimes placed in the genus Sterculia
