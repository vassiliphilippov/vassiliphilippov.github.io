southward

adverb
toward the south
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊθwərdz / (AmE), / ˈsaʊθwədz / (BrE)
toward the south; in the direction of the south.

Examples

Moving southwards
After crossing the river, the hikers continued southwards through the valley.
The birds migrate southwards every autumn to escape the cold.
The storm is moving southwards and is expected to reach the coast by morning.
