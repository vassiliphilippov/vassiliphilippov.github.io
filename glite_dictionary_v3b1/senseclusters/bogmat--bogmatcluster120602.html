bogmat

noun
North American aquatic plant
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑɡˌmæt / (AmE), / ˈbɒɡˌmæt / (BrE)
A North American aquatic plant with narrow, flat, sickle-shaped submerged fronds.

Examples

The bogmat plant
Biologists surveyed the stream for bogmat populations as part of their ecological study.
He pressed a sample of bogmat between sheets of paper to preserve it for identification.
The thick bogmat covered almost the entire pond surface, making it difficult to see the water.
