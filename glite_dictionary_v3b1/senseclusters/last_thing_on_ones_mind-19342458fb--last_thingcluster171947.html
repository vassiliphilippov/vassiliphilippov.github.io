last thing on one's mind

noun
something not a priority or concern
Pronunciation: / læst θɪŋ ɑn wʌnz maɪnd / (AmE), / lɑːst θɪŋ ɒn wʌnz maɪnd / (BrE)
used to indicate that something is not a priority, concern, or likely thought for someone at a particular time

Examples

The last thing on her mind
At that moment, food was the last thing on her mind.
Dealing with paperwork was the last thing on their mind after the long trip.
When I'm on holiday, work is the last thing on my mind.

Related Terms

  • antonym: top of one's mind - something that is a major priority or concern
