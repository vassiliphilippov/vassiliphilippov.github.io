field glass

noun
small binocular telescope for outdoor viewing
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːld ɡlæs / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
a small binocular telescope, used especially for observing distant objects outdoors

Examples

A field glass device
From the hilltop, I used my field glass to spot deer grazing in the distance.
He raised his field glass to study the hawk soaring above the field.
The naturalist packed his field glass and notebook before heading into the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: binoculars - an optical device for viewing distant objects with both eyes
