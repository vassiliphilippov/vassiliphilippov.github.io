Molinist

noun
follower of Molina
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊlɪnɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɒlɪnɪst / (BrE)
a person who follows the theological views of Luis de Molina, especially regarding free will and divine grace

Examples

A Molinist thinker

In the seminar, a Molinist explained the concept of middle knowledge to the audience.
The debate between the Molinist and the Calvinist centered on the nature of free will.
