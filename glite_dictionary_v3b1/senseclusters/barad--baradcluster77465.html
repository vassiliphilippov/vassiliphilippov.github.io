barad

noun
hailstones
9.9
frozen pellets or lumps of ice that fall from the sky during a storm; hail

Examples

Heavy barad falls
The sudden barad left the streets covered in small white ice pellets.
Farmers worried that the barad would ruin their crops before harvest.
Children ran inside quickly when the barad began to fall during the afternoon storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hail - frozen balls of ice that fall from clouds during storms
