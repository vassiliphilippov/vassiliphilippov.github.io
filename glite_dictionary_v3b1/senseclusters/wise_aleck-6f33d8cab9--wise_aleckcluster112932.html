wise aleck

noun
sarcastic know-it-all
8.1
Pronunciation: /waɪz ə.ˈlek/ (AmE), /waɪz ə.ˈlek/ (BrE)
a person who annoys others by acting as if they know everything or by making clever, sarcastic remarks

Examples

A wise aleck at school
Nobody likes a wise aleck who always has a sarcastic comment for everything.
During class, the wise aleck kept interrupting the teacher with clever remarks.
She told the wise aleck to keep his opinions to himself for once.

Related Terms

  • antonym: humble - not thinking you are better or smarter than others
  • synonym: know-it-all - a person who acts as if they know everything
  • synonym: smart aleck - a person who is irritatingly clever or sarcastic
