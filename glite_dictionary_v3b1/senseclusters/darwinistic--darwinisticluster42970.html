darwinistic

adjective
relating to Darwin's theories
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌdɑːrwɪˈnɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌdɑːwɪˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to the theories or ideas of Charles Darwin, especially the theory of evolution by natural selection

Examples

Darwinistic theory
The professor gave a darwinistic interpretation of animal behavior in her lecture.
Some critics argue that a strictly darwinistic view ignores cultural influences on human development.
The debate centered on whether the process was truly darwinistic or influenced by other factors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: darwinian - relating to Charles Darwin or his theories
  • antonym: lamarckian - relating to the evolutionary theories of Jean-Baptiste Lamarck
