flow

noun
menstrual discharge; menstruation
3.3
Pronunciation: / floʊ / (AmE), / fləʊ / (BrE)
the monthly discharge of blood from the uterus in women who are not pregnant, occurring from puberty until menopause; menstruation.

Examples

Heavy menstrual flow
Doctors may ask if a woman's flow is unusually heavy or irregular during check-ups.
She always carries supplies just in case her flow starts unexpectedly at work.
Some women experience cramps and mood changes along with their monthly flow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: menstruation - the monthly process in which blood flows from a woman's uterus
  • synonym: period - the regular monthly bleeding in women; menstruation
