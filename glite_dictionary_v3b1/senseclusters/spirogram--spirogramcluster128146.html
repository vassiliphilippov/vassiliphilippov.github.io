spirogram

noun
recording of breathing patterns
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪɹoʊˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈspaɪərəʊˌɡræm / (BrE)
A graphic recording showing the amount and pattern of air breathed in and out, typically produced by a spirometer during a lung function test.

Examples

A spirogram test
A spirogram is essential for diagnosing respiratory disorders like asthma or COPD.
Technicians reviewed the patient's spirogram to determine if there were any abnormalities in her breathing pattern.
The doctor's analysis of my spirogram showed that my lung function has improved.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spirometer - an instrument for measuring the air capacity of the lungs
