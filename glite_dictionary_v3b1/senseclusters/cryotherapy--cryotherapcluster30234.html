cryotherapy

noun
cold-based treatment
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkraɪoʊˌθɛrəpi / (AmE), / ˈkraɪəʊˌθɛrəpi / (BrE)
a medical treatment that uses extreme cold to destroy abnormal or unwanted tissue, such as warts or skin lesions

Examples

Cryotherapy for warts
The doctor recommended cryotherapy to remove the patient's stubborn wart.
After several sessions of cryotherapy, the skin lesion disappeared completely.
Many athletes use cryotherapy to help reduce muscle soreness and speed up recovery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cryosurgery - a surgical procedure using extreme cold to destroy tissue
  • antonym: radiotherapy - the treatment of disease using radiation
