neath

preposition
poetic under
9.9
Pronunciation: / niːθ / (AmE), / niːθ / (BrE)
an old or poetic word meaning under or beneath something

Examples

Neath the tree
The roots grew neath the ancient oak tree.
A gentle stream flowed neath the stone bridge in the valley.
He found shelter neath the wide branches during the sudden rain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: above - at a higher level or position
  • synonym: beneath - under or below something
