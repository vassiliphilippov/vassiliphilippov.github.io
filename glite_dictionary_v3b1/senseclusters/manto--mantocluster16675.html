manto

noun
loose outer cloak
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːntoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmantoʊ / (BrE)
a loose outer garment or cloak worn over other clothes for warmth or protection

Examples

A warm manto
She draped a heavy manto over her shoulders to protect against the cold wind.
The traditional costume included a beautifully embroidered manto worn during festivals.
He admired the elegant manto displayed in the museum's collection of historical garments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cape - a sleeveless outer garment that drapes over the back and shoulders
  • synonym: cloak - a loose outer garment worn over indoor clothing
  • antonym: jacket - a short coat worn on the upper body
