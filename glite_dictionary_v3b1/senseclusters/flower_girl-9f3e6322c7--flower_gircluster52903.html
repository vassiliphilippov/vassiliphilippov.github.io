flower girl

noun
woman or girl selling flowers on the street
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊ.ɚ ɡɝːl / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ə ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a woman or girl who sells flowers on the street, often from a stall or basket

Examples

Street flower girl
A young flower girl stood in the rain, hoping to sell her last handful of daisies.
In Victorian London, flower girls were a common sight on busy street corners.
The flower girl offered me a bouquet of fresh roses as I passed by the station.
