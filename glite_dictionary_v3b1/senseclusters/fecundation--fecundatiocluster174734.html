fecundation

noun
fertilization by union of gametes
8.4 8.0
Pronunciation: /fiː.kən.ˈdeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /fɪ.kən.ˈdeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /fiː.kən.ˈdeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /fɪ.kən.ˈdeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process by which male and female gametes physically unite, resulting in fertilization in animals or plants

Examples

Animal fecundation
After fecundation, the embryo begins to develop within the seed.
In flowering plants, fecundation occurs after pollen reaches the ovule.
Successful fecundation in mammals requires the sperm to reach the egg first.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fertilization - the act or process of fertilizing, especially by fusion of gametes
