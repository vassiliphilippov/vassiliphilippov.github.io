planter

noun
person who plants seeds or seedlings
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈplæn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈplɑːn.tə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to plant seeds or seedlings in the ground, especially as part of agricultural work

Examples

A skilled planter
As a skilled planter, she ensured every seedling was carefully set into the prepared earth.
Many planters are hired during the spring season when fields need to be cultivated.
The planter worked quickly, sowing row after row under the hot sun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: farmer - a person who owns or manages a farm
