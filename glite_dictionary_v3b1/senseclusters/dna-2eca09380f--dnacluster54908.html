DNA

noun
molecule that carries genetic information
2.2 2.7
Pronunciation: /diː.en.ˈeɪ/ (AmE), /diː.en.ˈeɪ/ (BrE)
a molecule present in the nuclei of cells, composed of two long strands forming a double helix, that carries genetic information in living organisms
