resaw

noun
machine for cutting boards
Pronunciation: / ˈriːˌsɔ / (AmE), / ˈriːˌsɔː / (BrE)
a saw or machine, often a bandsaw, used to cut thick boards into thinner pieces

Examples

A resaw machine
The carpenter used a resaw to turn thick planks into thinner boards for the project.
After purchasing a new resaw, the workshop was able to process lumber much more efficiently.
A high-quality resaw is essential for making precise cuts in woodworking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bandsaw - a saw with a long, sharp blade consisting of a continuous band of toothed metal
  • synonym: table saw - a woodworking tool consisting of a circular saw blade mounted on an arbor
