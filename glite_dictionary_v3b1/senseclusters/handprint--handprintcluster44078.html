handprint

noun
visible influence
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌprɪnt / (AmE), / ˈhændˌprɪnt / (BrE)
a visible mark, influence, or lasting effect that someone leaves on something, especially as evidence of their involvement.

Examples

An artist's handprint
The artist's handprint can be seen in every detail of the mural.
Each leader leaves a unique handprint on the organization they guide.
Her handprint on the community project inspired others to get involved.

Related Terms

  • synonym: footprint - a mark or effect left by someone, often used metaphorically
  • synonym: imprint - a mark or influence left by someone or something
