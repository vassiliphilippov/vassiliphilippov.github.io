sleepyheaded

adjective
feeling very drowsy or sleepy
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsliːpiˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈsliːpiˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
feeling very drowsy or on the verge of falling asleep; inclined to sleep

Examples

A sleepyheaded student
A group of sleepyheaded students yawned through the early morning lecture.
I felt so sleepyheaded after staying up too late last night.
The sleepyheaded child could barely keep his eyes open during dinner.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alert - fully awake and attentive
  • synonym: drowsy - feeling sleepy and lethargic
