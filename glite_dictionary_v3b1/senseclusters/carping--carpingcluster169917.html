carping

noun
constant petty criticism
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɑːpɪŋ / (BrE)
persistent petty and unjustified criticism, especially over trivial matters

Examples

Constant carping criticism
Her constant carping about the smallest mistakes made it hard for anyone to enjoy the meeting.
Instead of offering constructive feedback, his carping only served to lower morale.
The actor grew tired of the media's carping over minor details of his performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: criticism - the act of expressing disapproval or noting the problems of something
