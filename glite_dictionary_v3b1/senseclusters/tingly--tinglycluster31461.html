tingly

adjective
slightly excited feeling
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪŋɡli / (AmE), / ˈtɪŋɡli / (BrE)
having a slight, pleasant prickling or stinging feeling, often caused by excitement, anticipation, or a mild physical sensation

Examples

Feeling tingly sensation
She felt tingly with excitement as the curtain rose and the orchestra began.
The news made him tingly with anticipation for what was to come.
A tingly sensation ran through her as she waited for the results.
After sitting in the same position for too long, my foot felt tingly when I stood up.
The mint in the toothpaste left a tingly feeling in my mouth.
She described the excitement as a tingly sensation running down her spine.

  • synonym: nervous - feeling worried or anxious
  • antonym: numb - lacking physical sensation
  • synonym: prickly - having a sensation like being lightly pricked
