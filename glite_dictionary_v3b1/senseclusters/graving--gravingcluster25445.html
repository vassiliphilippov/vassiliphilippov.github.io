graving

verb
to carve designs
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
cutting or etching letters or designs into a hard surface, especially metal or stone

Examples

To grave a name
She spent the afternoon graving initials into the silver locket.
The artist was graving intricate patterns onto the metal plate for hours.
Workers are graving names into the memorial stone for the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carving - shaping or cutting a material to form a design
  • synonym: engraving - cutting or carving a design onto a hard surface
