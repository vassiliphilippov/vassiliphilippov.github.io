double-deck rail car

noun
rail car with two passenger levels
4.2 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Àåd åb.…ôl Àåd…õk Ààre…™l k…ër / (AmE), / Àåd åb.…ôl Àåd…õk Ààre…™l k…ëÀê / (BrE)
a railway passenger car with two levels of seating to increase capacity, as opposed to the usual single level.

Examples

A double-deck rail car design
Engineers designed a new double-deck rail car for long-distance travel to improve comfort and seating capacity.
On busy routes, double-deck rail cars help reduce crowding while offering scenic views from the upper level.
The commuter train used a double-deck rail car to accommodate more passengers during rush hour.

Synonyms

  • bilevel railcar

  • double-deck carriage
    4.2 0.00/m
