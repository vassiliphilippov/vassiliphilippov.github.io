gaslighting

adjective
manipulative, undermining
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæsˌlaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡæsˌlaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
describing behavior that manipulates someone into doubting their own perceptions, memories, or sanity

Examples

Gaslighting tactics
Her partner's gaslighting behavior made her question her own memories and feelings.
The manager's gaslighting comments left the team feeling confused and insecure.
Friends noticed the gaslighting tactics and encouraged her to trust her instincts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manipulative - controlling or influencing someone in a clever or unfair way
  • antonym: supportive - providing encouragement or emotional help
