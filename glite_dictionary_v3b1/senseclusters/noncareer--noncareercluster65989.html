noncareer

noun
temporary government appointee
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkəˈrɪr / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkəˈrɪə / (BrE)
a person appointed to a government position who is not part of the regular, permanent civil service, often serving temporarily or politically

Examples

Noncareer ambassador
The ambassador was a noncareer who had previously worked in the private sector.
Many noncareer officials serve only during a particular administration.
Unlike career diplomats, a noncareer may not have formal training in foreign service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appointee - a person who has been appointed to a position
  • antonym: career - a permanent member of the civil service
