pledge taker

noun
person recording fundraising pledges
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈplɛdʒ ˌteɪkər / (AmE), / ˈplɛdʒ ˌteɪkə / (BrE)
a person, typically a volunteer, who records contributions pledged during a fundraising campaign, often by telephone

Examples

A pledge taker volunteer
As a pledge taker, she spent her evening answering calls and noting down donations for the radio drive.

The company recruited over twenty pledge takers to help with their annual charity fundraiser.
