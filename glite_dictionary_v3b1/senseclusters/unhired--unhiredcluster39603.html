unhired

adjective
not employed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhaɪɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhaɪəd / (BrE)
not employed or not selected for a job or position

Examples

Unhired worker
After several interviews, he remained unhired and continued his job search.
The company received many applications, but some candidates were left unhired.
Programs were created to help unhired graduates find employment opportunities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hired - employed or given a job
  • synonym: unemployed - not having a paid job
