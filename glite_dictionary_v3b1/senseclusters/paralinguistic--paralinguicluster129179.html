paralinguistic

adjective
nonverbal communication
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˌlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌpærəˌlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to nonverbal elements of communication, such as tone of voice, facial expressions, or gestures

Examples

Paralinguistic signals
Her paralinguistic signals, like a raised eyebrow, revealed her true feelings.
Teachers often rely on paralinguistic cues to understand students' emotions in class.
The actor's paralinguistic skills made his performance more believable and engaging.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonverbal - not involving words or speech
  • antonym: verbal - relating to words or speech
