Numidian

noun
inhabitant of ancient Numidia
9.9
Pronunciation: / nuˈmɪdiən / (AmE), / njuˈmɪdiən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of ancient Numidia, a region of North Africa during Roman times

Examples

A Numidian warrior
A Numidian was often employed as a mercenary in ancient military forces.
Artifacts from Numidian settlements have been discovered by archaeologists in present-day Algeria.
The Numidian cavalry played a crucial role in Hannibal's campaigns against Rome.
