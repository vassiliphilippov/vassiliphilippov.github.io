split the baby

verb
reach a compromise in a tough dispute
8.8 6.5
Pronunciation: / splɪt ðə ˈbeɪ.bi / (AmE), / splɪt ðə ˈbeɪ.bi / (BrE)
to reach a compromise by dividing the outcome between opposing parties, especially in a situation where a clear win for either side is not possible

Examples
