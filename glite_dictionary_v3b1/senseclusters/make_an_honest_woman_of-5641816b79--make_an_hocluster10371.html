make an honest woman of

verb
marry a woman to legitimize a relationship
5.6
Pronunciation: / meɪk ən ˈɑnɪst ˈwʊmən ʌv / (AmE), / meɪk ən ˈɒnɪst ˈwʊmən ɒv / (BrE)
(of a man) to marry a woman, especially after a romantic or sexual relationship, so as to legitimize the relationship or ensure her respectability according to traditional standards.

Examples

To make an honest woman of her
After years of dating, he asked her to marry him and finally make an honest woman of her.
He joked that he would make an honest woman of her by proposing marriage.
Some people still think a man should make an honest woman of his partner if they've been living together.
