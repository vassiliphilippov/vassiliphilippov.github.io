strawless

adjective
without straw
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɔːləs / (AmE), / ˈstrɔːləs / (BrE)
without straw or lacking any straw.

Examples

A strawless stable
After the animals left, the stable was completely strawless and needed cleaning.
The old mattress was strawless, making it uncomfortable to sleep on.
During the drought, many fields became strawless and barren.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bare - not covered or filled; empty
  • antonym: straw-filled - containing or filled with straw
