machination

noun
secret and cunning plot
Pronunciation: / ˌmæk.əˈneɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌmæk.ɪˈneɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
A secret and complicated plan, often with the intention of causing harm or achieving an illicit goal.

Examples

Secret machination plan
His clever machination nearly succeeded in deceiving the entire company.
She became entangled in the political machinations of the royal court.
The villain's machination was finally exposed after months of investigation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conspiracy - a secret plan by a group to do something unlawful
  • synonym: plot - a secret plan to achieve something, often illegal
  • synonym: scheme - a secret or underhanded plan
