thiamin

noun
vitamin B1; essential nutrient
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈθaɪ.ə.mɪn / (AmE), / ˈθaɪ.ə.mɪn / (BrE)
a vitamin of the B complex (vitamin B1) that is essential for metabolism and helps prevent beriberi

Examples

The thiamin vitamin
A deficiency of thiamin can lead to beriberi, a serious medical condition.
Doctors recommend thiamin supplements for patients with certain nutritional deficiencies.
Many cereals are fortified with thiamin to promote better health.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: niacin - another B vitamin (B3) essential for metabolism
  • synonym: vitamin B1 - another name for thiamin, a B-group vitamin
