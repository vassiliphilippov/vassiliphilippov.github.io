unfeignedly

adverb
in a sincere, genuine way
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfeɪnɪdli / (AmE), / ʌnˈfeɪnɪdli / (BrE)
In a sincere and genuine manner; without pretense or insincerity.

Examples

To unfeignedly congratulate
He unfeignedly congratulated his rival, proving his sportsmanship.
She spoke unfeignedly about her feelings, leaving no doubt about her sincerity.
The community unfeignedly mourned the loss of their beloved leader.

Related Terms

  • synonym: genuinely - in a real and honest manner; without falseness
  • antonym: insincerely - in a way that is not honest or genuine; with pretense
  • synonym: sincerely - in a sincere and honest way, without pretending
