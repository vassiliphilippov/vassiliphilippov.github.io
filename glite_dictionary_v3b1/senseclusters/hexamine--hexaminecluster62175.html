hexamine

noun
urinary antiseptic compound
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛksəˌmiːn / (AmE), / ˈhɛksəmiːn / (BrE)
a medicinal compound used as a urinary antiseptic to prevent or treat urinary tract infections

Examples

Hexamine tablets for infection
The doctor prescribed hexamine to help prevent future urinary tract infections.
Some travelers carry hexamine tablets as a precaution against urinary problems.
Long-term use of hexamine should be monitored by a healthcare professional.

Related Terms

  • synonym: methenamine - a compound used to treat urinary tract infections
