guestimate

verb
estimate roughly (informal)
6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈge.stɪ.meɪt/ /; / /ˈges.tɪ.meɪt/ (AmE), /ˈge.stɪ.meɪt/ /; / /ˈges.tɪ.meɪt/ (BrE)
to make a rough or approximate estimate; to calculate or judge something using informal or partial information.

Examples

To guestimate the cost
Before the meeting, the manager asked the team to guestimate how long the rollout would take.
They guestimated the attendance at around two hundred people based on ticket sales.
Without precise figures, she was guestimating project costs while preparing the proposal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: estimate - to calculate roughly; an approximate calculation or judgment
  • degree lower: guess - to form an opinion or answer without sufficient information; less precise than an estimate
