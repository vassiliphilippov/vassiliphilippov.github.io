segue

noun
smooth transition from one thing to another
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛɡ.weɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɛɡ.weɪ / (BrE)
a smooth transition from one state, subject, or section to another

Examples

A smooth segue
Her speech had an awkward segue between two unrelated points.
The conversation included a clever segue from serious topics to lighthearted banter.
The musician made a seamless segue from one song to the next during the concert.

Related Terms

  • synonym: transition - the process or a period of changing from one state to another
