designer shoe

noun
luxury fashion footwear
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzaɪnər ʃu / (AmE), / dɪˈzaɪnə ʃuː / (BrE)
a shoe made by a well-known fashion designer or luxury brand, often expensive and stylish

Examples

Designer shoes brand
Although expensive, designer shoes are prized for their beauty and craftsmanship.
She saved for months to buy her first pair of designer shoes from a Paris boutique.
The red carpet was filled with celebrities showing off their latest designer shoes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: luxury shoe - expensive, high-quality footwear intended as a status symbol
  • degree lower: sneaker - a type of casual shoe, often sporty and comfortable
