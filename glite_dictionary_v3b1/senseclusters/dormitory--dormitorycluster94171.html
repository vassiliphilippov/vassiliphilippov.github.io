dormitory

noun
student housing at a college or university
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔːrməˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈdɔːmɪtri / (BrE)
a building in a college or university where students live while studying

Examples

College dormitory building
After her classes, she returned to her dormitory to study with friends.
Most freshmen are required to live in a dormitory during their first year at college.
The university renovated its oldest dormitory to provide more comfortable accommodations.
