basepath

noun
baseball runner's path
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪsˌpæθ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsˌpɑːθ / (BrE)
in baseball, the path a runner follows between two bases on the field

Examples

Run the basepath
The runner dashed along the basepath to try to beat the throw to third base.
After hitting the ball, she quickly sprinted down the basepath toward first base.
The grounds crew carefully maintained the basepaths before every game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baseline - the line marking the boundary between bases in baseball
