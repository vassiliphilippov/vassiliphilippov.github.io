yock

verb
to laugh loudly
9.9
Pronunciation: / jɑk / (AmE), / jɒk / (BrE)
to laugh loudly or heartily, often in response to something funny or amusing

Examples

To yock loudly
The comedian's joke made the whole audience yock for several minutes.
She couldn't help but yock when her friend slipped on the banana peel.
They yocked so hard at the movie that people turned to stare.
