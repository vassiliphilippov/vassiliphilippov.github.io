sketchpad

noun
drawing paper pad
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈskɛtʃˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈskɛtʃˌpæd / (BrE)

Examples

A sketchpad for drawing
She always keeps a sketchpad in her bag to draw whenever inspiration strikes.
The artist filled his sketchpad with quick drawings of city scenes.
He flipped through his old sketchpad and smiled at his early work.
