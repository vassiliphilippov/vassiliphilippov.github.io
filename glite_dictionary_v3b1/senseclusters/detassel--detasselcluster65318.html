detassel

noun
corn tassel remover
9.1
Pronunciation: / diˈtæsəl / (AmE), / diˈtæsəl / (BrE)
a person who removes the tassels from corn plants, usually as a seasonal agricultural worker

Examples

A detassel worker
During the summer, she worked as a detassel in the cornfields near her hometown.
Many teenagers take jobs as a detassel to earn extra money during school break.
The farm hired a new detassel to help with the hybrid corn production.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harvester - a person who gathers crops at harvest
