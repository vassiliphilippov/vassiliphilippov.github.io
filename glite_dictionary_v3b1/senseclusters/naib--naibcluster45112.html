naib

noun
local deputy official
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪɪb / (AmE), / ˈnaɪɪb / (BrE)
a local governor or deputy official in some Islamic countries, especially in North Africa

Examples

Regional naib
The naib was responsible for maintaining order in the region.
After the governor left, the naib took charge of the administration.
In some towns, the naib acted as a judge and mediator for local disputes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deputy - a person appointed to assist or act for another
  • synonym: governor - an official in charge of a region or province
