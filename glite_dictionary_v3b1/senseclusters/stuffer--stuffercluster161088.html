stuffer

noun
advertisement enclosed in mailings
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌfər / (AmE), / ˈstʌfə / (BrE)
an advertising leaflet or circular inserted with other mail, typically for promotional purposes

Examples

Mail stuffer ad
Every catalog mailing came with a colorful stuffer advertising seasonal discounts.
She tossed the stuffer into the recycling bin without reading it.
The company included a stuffer in each envelope to promote their new product line.
