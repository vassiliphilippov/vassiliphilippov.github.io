unibrow

noun
joined eyebrows
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːniˌbraʊ / (AmE), / ˈjuːnɪˌbraʊ / (BrE)
a single continuous eyebrow formed when the two eyebrows grow together above the nose

Examples

A unibrow above nose
She decided to pluck her unibrow before the school photo was taken.
Some people consider a unibrow to be a unique and attractive feature.
His thick unibrow made him stand out in the crowd.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: eyebrow - the strip of hair growing above the eye
  • synonym: monobrow - a single eyebrow formed by the joining of both eyebrows
