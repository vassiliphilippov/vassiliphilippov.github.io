pre-employment

adjective
before hiring
Pronunciation: / ˌpriː ɪmˈplɔɪmənt / (AmE), / ˌpriː ɪmˈplɔɪmənt / (BrE)
relating to activities, checks, or requirements that take place before someone is hired for a job

Examples

Pre-employment screening
All candidates must pass a pre-employment drug test before starting work.
The company conducts pre-employment training sessions for new hires.
A thorough pre-employment screening helps employers find the right person for the job.

Related Terms

  • antonym: post-employment - relating to activities or requirements after someone is hired
