litany of complaints

noun
long list of grievances
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪtəni əv kəmˈpleɪnts / (AmE), / ˈlɪtəni əv kəmˈpleɪnts / (BrE)
a lengthy and often repetitive list of grievances or criticisms

Examples

A litany of complaints
He delivered a litany of complaints during the meeting, covering everything from salaries to working conditions.
Social media users posted a litany of complaints after the product launch did not meet expectations.
The manager had to listen patiently as the employee recited her litany of complaints about the new policy.
