herpetological

adjective
study of reptiles
Pronunciation: / ˌhɜrpɪtəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌhɜːpɪtəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the scientific study of reptiles and amphibians

Examples

Herpetological study
The museum has a large herpetological collection featuring rare snakes and frogs.
He attended a herpetological conference to present his research on salamanders.
Their herpetological studies helped protect endangered turtle species in the region.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ornithological - relating to the study of birds
  • synonym: zoological - relating to the study of animals
