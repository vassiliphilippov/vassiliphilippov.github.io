Rosmarinus

proper noun
former plant genus
Pronunciation: / ˌrɒzməˈraɪnəs /; / ˌroʊzməˈraɪnəs / (AmE), / ˌrɒzməˈraɪnəs / (BrE)
the former botanical genus name for rosemary and related aromatic shrubs, now classified under the genus Salvia
