Mauna Kea

proper noun
highest peak in Hawaii, dormant volcano
Pronunciation: / ˌmaʊnə ˈkeɪə / (AmE), / ˌmaʊ.nə ˈkeɪ.ə / (BrE)
a dormant volcano in north-central Hawaii; the highest peak in the Hawaiian Islands.

Examples

Mauna Kea volcano
Astronomers travel to Mauna Kea for its world-class observatories.
Many native Hawaiians consider Mauna Kea a sacred mountain.
Snow occasionally falls on the summit of Mauna Kea despite its tropical location.
