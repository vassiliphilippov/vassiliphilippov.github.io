Graecophile

adjective
admirer of Greek culture or Greece
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːkəˌfaɪl / (AmE), / ˈɡriːkəˌfaɪl / (BrE)
Having or showing a strong admiration for Greece, its people, or its culture.

Examples

Graecophile attitude
Her Graecophile interests led her to specialize in Hellenic studies.
Many Graecophile societies promote the preservation of Greek heritage and art.
The new director is a noted Graecophile who collects ancient Greek manuscripts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Philhellene - a person who loves or supports Greece and Greek culture
