social drinker

noun
person drinking alcohol moderately, socially
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəl ˈdrɪŋkər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəl ˈdrɪŋkə / (BrE)
a person who drinks alcoholic beverages moderately, usually only in social situations

Examples

A social drinker at parties
As a social drinker, she rarely touches alcohol outside of gatherings with friends.
He considers himself a social drinker, only having a glass of wine at parties.
Many people describe themselves as social drinkers rather than regular drinkers.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: heavy drinker - a person who frequently drinks large amounts of alcohol
  • antonym: teetotaler - a person who abstains completely from drinking alcohol
