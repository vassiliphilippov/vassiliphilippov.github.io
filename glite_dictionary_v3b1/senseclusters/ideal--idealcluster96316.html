ideal

adjective
perfect; embodying the highest standard
Pronunciation: / aɪˈdiəl / (AmE), / aɪˈdɪəl / (BrE)
representing or embodying the highest standard of perfection or excellence; perfect or most suitable in every way

Examples

Ideal solution
He described the city as an ideal place to raise a family.
She is the ideal candidate for the position because of her experience and skills.
The climate in this region is ideal for growing grapes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flawed - imperfect; not meeting the highest standard
  • synonym: perfect - having all the required or desirable elements; as good as possible
