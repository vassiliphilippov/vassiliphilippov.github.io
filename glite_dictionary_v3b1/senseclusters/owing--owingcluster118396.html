owing

adjective
remaining to be paid; owed
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
(of money or a sum) yet to be paid; unpaid; owed as a debt.

Examples

Bills owing
All the bills owing must be cleared before you move out.
The invoice shows $200 owing for last month's work.
There is still some amount owing on the loan.

Related Terms

  • antonym: paid - settled; no longer owed
