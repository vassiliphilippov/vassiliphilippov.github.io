Synonyms

  • unpaid
    1.8 2/m
    unpaid describes money that has not been paid yet by someone, while owing describes a sum still recorded as debt.
  • outstanding
    3.9 4/m
    outstanding means an amount still unpaid and waiting to be cleared, while owing describes money that is owed as a debt.
  • due
    1.3 20/m
    due refers to money or a date when payment must happen, while owing focuses on sums that are still debts.
  • payable
    4.1 0.8/m
