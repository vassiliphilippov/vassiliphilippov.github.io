retentive

adjective
able to remember things easily
4.1
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtɛntɪv / (AmE), / rɪˈtɛntɪv / (BrE)
having the ability to remember things easily or keep information in mind for a long time

Examples

Retentive memory
Even after many years, her retentive brain could recall small details from childhood.
His retentive mind made him an excellent scholar.
She has a remarkably retentive memory for faces and names.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forgetful - having a tendency to forget things
