flagstick

noun
marker pole with flag
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈflæɡˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈflæɡˌstɪk / (BrE)
a pole with a small flag attached, used to mark a specific spot, boundary, or hazard, especially outside of golf

Examples

A flagstick marker
The construction crew placed a flagstick at the edge of the new pavement.
A bright flagstick warned hikers about the unstable ground ahead.
Surveyors used a flagstick to mark the property boundary during the inspection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: marker - an object used to indicate a position or boundary
  • synonym: signpost - a post with a sign giving information or directions
