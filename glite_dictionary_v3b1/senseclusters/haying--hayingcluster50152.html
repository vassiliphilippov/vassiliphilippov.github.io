haying

noun
the harvesting of hay
6.2 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈheɪ.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈheɪ.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process or season of cutting, drying, and collecting grass to make hay for animal feed

Examples

Haying season
During haying season, everyone in the village lends a hand in the fields.
The farmers started haying early in the morning to avoid the afternoon heat.
Wet weather can delay haying and reduce the quality of the crop.
Haying usually takes place in early summer when the grass is at its peak.
Good weather is crucial for successful haying, as rain can ruin the drying grass.
The whole family helped out during haying each year on the farm.
