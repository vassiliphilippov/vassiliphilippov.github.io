Scophthalmus aquosus

noun
a thin, translucent North American flounder
Pronunciation: / skoʊfˈθælməs æˈkwoʊsəs / (AmE), / skɒfˈθælməs akˈwəʊsəs / (BrE)
A species of very thin, translucent flounder found along the Atlantic coast of North America, commonly known as the windowpane.
