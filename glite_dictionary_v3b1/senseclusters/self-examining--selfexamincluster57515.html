self-examining

adjective
introspective; reflective about oneself
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf ɪɡˈzæmɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf ɪɡˈzæmɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
given to examining or reflecting on one's own thoughts, feelings, or sensory experiences

Examples

Self-examining personality
She has a naturally self-examining personality, always reflecting on her own motivations.
The therapy encourages a self-examining attitude toward past relationships and habits.
Writers often become self-examining when describing personal experiences in their memoirs.
