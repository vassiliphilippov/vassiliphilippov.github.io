expressionlessly

adverb
without emotion
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsprɛʃənləsli / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛʃənləsli / (BrE)
in a way that shows no emotion or facial expression

Examples

Look expressionlessly
She stared expressionlessly at the painting, giving no hint of her thoughts.
The guard stood expressionlessly at his post, unmoved by the commotion.
He answered the difficult question expressionlessly, revealing nothing to the interviewer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: emotionally - in a way that shows strong feelings
  • synonym: impassively - in a way that does not show emotion
