ceramicist

noun
someone who makes ceramics or pottery
9.5 6.6
Pronunciation: /sə.ˈræm.ɪ.sɪst/ /; / /sɪ.ˈræm.ɪ.sɪst/ (AmE), /sə.ˈræm.ɪ.sɪst/ /; / /sɪ.ˈræm.ɪ.sɪst/ (BrE)
A person who makes ceramic items, especially pottery, by shaping clay and firing it in a kiln.

Examples

A ceramicist makes pottery
As a skilled ceramicist, she creates both functional and decorative pieces.
The ceramicist spent hours perfecting the curves of each bowl before firing them.
The gallery featured works by a renowned local ceramicist.
