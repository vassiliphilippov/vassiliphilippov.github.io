tableside

adjective, adverb
at the dining table
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪbəlˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈteɪbəlˌsaɪd / (BrE)
happening or done at or next to a dining table, especially in a restaurant, so people can watch
