Synonyms

  • product launch
    3.2 0.3/m
    product launch is an event that introduces a new product with promotion, while release is the broader act of making something public.
  • rollout
    4.4 1/m
    rollout is a planned, often gradual introduction and distribution to users, while release is a single act of making something public.
  • unveiling
    4.1 1/m
    unveiling is a formal event where something new is shown to people, while release is the general making of that thing public.
  • press release
    2.5 4/m
    press release is an official statement sent to the media to give information, while release is any public announcement or product availability.
  • publication
    3.2 6/m
    publication is the act of issuing written or recorded material to the public, while release covers products, statements, or announcements more widely.
  • announcement
    1.5 30/m
    announcement is a public message that gives news or plans, while release is a broader act of making products or statements available.
  • disclosure
    4.1 8/m
    disclosure is revealing new or secret information, often with legal or surprising tone, while release simply makes something publicly available.
