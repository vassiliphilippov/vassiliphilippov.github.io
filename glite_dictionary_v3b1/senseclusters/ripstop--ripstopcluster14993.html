ripstop

noun
tear-resistant fabric
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪpˌstɑp / (AmE), / ˈrɪpˌstɒp / (BrE)
a strong woven fabric with a special reinforcing technique that makes it resistant to tearing and ripping

Examples

A ripstop tent
The tent is made from ripstop to withstand harsh weather conditions.
Hikers often choose jackets with ripstop for extra durability on the trail.
My new duffel bag uses ripstop so it does not tear easily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: canvas - a heavy-duty, plain-woven fabric
  • synonym: nylon - a strong synthetic fabric often used for outdoor gear
