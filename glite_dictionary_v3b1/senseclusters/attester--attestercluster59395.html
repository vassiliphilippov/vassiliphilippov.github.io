attester

noun
device/software that produces cryptographic attestation
Pronunciation: / 59c8t5bst59r / (AmE), / 59c8t5bst59 / (BrE)
(in computing, especially digital attestation) a device or software component that produces cryptographic proof of a computer system's state and presents it to a remote verifier.

Examples

System attester
The platform's attester reports a trusted measurement to the remote verifier during startup.
Developers updated the server's attester to include TPM-based measurements before deployment.
During the audit, the cloud provider produced logs from several attesters showing measured boot values.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prover - in security, a component that proves or demonstrates a system state to a verifier
  • antonym: verifier - a component that checks and validates attested measurements or proofs from an attester
