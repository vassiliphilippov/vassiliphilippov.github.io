storage

noun
place where goods are kept
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔːrɪdʒ /; / ˈstɔrɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈstɔːrɪdʒ / (BrE)
a place, activity, or process for keeping goods, materials, or possessions until they are needed
