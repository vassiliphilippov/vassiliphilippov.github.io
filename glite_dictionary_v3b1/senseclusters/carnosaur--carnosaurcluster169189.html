carnosaur

noun
large carnivorous bipedal dinosaur
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrnəˌsɔr / (AmE), / ˈkɑːnəˌsɔː / (BrE)
a large carnivorous bipedal dinosaur belonging to a group characterized by massive size and sharp claws, such as Allosaurus or similar theropods

Examples

A carnosaur dinosaur
In the museum exhibit, a towering carnosaur skeleton greets visitors at the entrance.
Scientists believe the carnosaur was one of the top predators of its time.
The fossilized remains of a carnosaur were discovered in the desert last summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: theropod - any member of a major group of bipedal saurischian dinosaurs
