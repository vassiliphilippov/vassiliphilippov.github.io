hunting dog

noun
dog trained or bred for hunting
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌntɪŋ dɔg / (AmE), / ˈhʌntɪŋ dɒg / (BrE)
a dog that is trained or bred for hunting game animals or birds

Examples

A hunting dog breed
A well-trained hunting dog can make a big difference on a day in the field.
She adopted a retired hunting dog and gave it a loving home.
The trainer demonstrated how the hunting dog followed the scent trail through the forest.
