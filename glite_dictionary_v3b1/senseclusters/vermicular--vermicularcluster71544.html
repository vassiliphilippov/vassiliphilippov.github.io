vermicular

adjective
marked with worm-like designs
6.8
Pronunciation: / vərˈmɪkjələr / (AmE), / vəˈmɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
decorated or marked with wavy, worm-like designs, especially in architectural or artistic contexts

Examples

Vermicular designs
Historians admired the vermicular designs etched into the marble columns.
The artist added a vermicular pattern to the surface for a textured effect.
The old building was notable for its vermicular stonework around the entrance.
