nonrotating

adjective
not spinning
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈroʊteɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈrəʊteɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
not turning or spinning around an axis; stationary in terms of rotation

Examples

A nonrotating platform
The satellite was placed on a nonrotating platform to ensure accurate measurements.
A nonrotating object will not experience centrifugal force.
The experiment required a nonrotating base to minimize any unwanted movement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rotating - turning or spinning around an axis
  • synonym: stationary - not moving or not intended to be moved
