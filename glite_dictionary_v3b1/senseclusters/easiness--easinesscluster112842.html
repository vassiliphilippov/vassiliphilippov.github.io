easiness

noun
relaxed quality or manner
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈiːzi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈiːzɪnɪs / (BrE)
a relaxed and natural quality in behavior or style, free from stiffness or constraint

Examples

Easiness in her manner
Despite the formal setting, she maintained a sense of easiness throughout the event.
Her easiness in conversation put everyone at ease during the meeting.
There was an obvious easiness in his smile that made him approachable.

Related Terms
