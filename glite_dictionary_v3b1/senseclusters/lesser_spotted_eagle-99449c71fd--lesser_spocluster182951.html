lesser spotted eagle

noun
a species of medium-sized eagle
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛsər ˈspɑt̬ɪd ˈiɡəl / (AmE), / ˈlɛsə ˈspɒtɪd ˈiːɡl̩ / (BrE)
a medium-sized species of eagle (Clanga pomarina) found in Europe and Asia, characterized by brown plumage and lighter spots.

Examples

The lesser spotted eagle bird
A nature reserve was established to protect the habitat of the lesser spotted eagle in the region.
During migration, the lesser spotted eagle travels thousands of kilometers to reach its wintering grounds.
The lesser spotted eagle can often be seen soaring over forests and marshlands.
