common bean

noun
edible bean plant (Phaseolus vulgaris)
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən bin /; / ˈkɑmən ˌbin / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən biːn /; / ˈkɒmən ˌbiːn / (BrE)
a plant (Phaseolus vulgaris) grown for its edible seeds or pods, used as food
