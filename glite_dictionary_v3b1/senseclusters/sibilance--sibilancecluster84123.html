sibilance

noun
hissing breath sound
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪbɪləns / (AmE), / ˈsɪbɪləns / (BrE)
a high-pitched wheezing or hissing sound from the airways heard during breathing, indicating narrowing or obstruction

Examples

Lung sibilance
The doctor noted a distinct sibilance when listening to the patient's lungs with a stethoscope.
Persistent sibilance may indicate an underlying respiratory condition that needs medical attention.
After the asthma attack, the nurse could hear sibilance during the child's exhalation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stridor - a harsh, grating sound when breathing, often due to airway obstruction
  • synonym: wheeze - a whistling or rattling sound in the chest when breathing
