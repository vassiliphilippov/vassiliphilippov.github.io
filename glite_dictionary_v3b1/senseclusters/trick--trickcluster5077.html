trick

noun
deceitful action or scheme
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: /trɪk/ (AmE), /trɪk/ (BrE)
a cunning or deceitful act or scheme intended to mislead or cheat someone; or to deceive or mislead someone using cunning methods

Examples

A trick to cheat
Be careful, that offer might be a trick to scam you.
He played a trick on his friend by hiding his keys.
The magician wowed the audience with an amazing trick.
He tried to win the game by playing a trick on his opponents.
She caught on quickly and realized it was only a harmless trick.
The magician amazed the children with a clever trick during his performance.

  • synonym: prank - a playful trick or practical joke
