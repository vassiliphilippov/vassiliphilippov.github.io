Jagatai

noun
Turkic literary language of medieval Central Asia
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒæɡətaɪ / (AmE), / ˈdʒæɡətaɪ / (BrE)
a Turkic literary language formerly used in medieval Central Asia, named after a son of Genghis Khan.

Examples

Jagatai language
Historians study Jagatai texts to understand the culture of medieval Central Asia.
Many classical Central Asian poems were originally written in Jagatai.
The Jagatai language influenced several modern Turkic languages in the region.
