chilled

adjective
relaxed or cold
Pronunciation: / tʃɪld / (AmE), / tʃɪld / (BrE)
relaxed and calm, or made cold in temperature

Examples

A chilled evening
After a long week, we had a chilled night at home with friends.
The drinks were chilled and ready to serve at the party.
He always seems so chilled even when things get stressful.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calm - not feeling nervous or excited
  • synonym: cold - having a low temperature
  • synonym: relaxed - free from tension or anxiety
  • antonym: tense - feeling nervous or anxious
  • antonym: warm - having a moderately high temperature
