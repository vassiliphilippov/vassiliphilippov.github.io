ambulance-chase law

noun
aggressive, often unethical, client-seeking legal practice
Pronunciation: / ˈæmbjələns ˌtʃeɪs lɔ / (AmE), / ˈæmbjʊləns ˌtʃeɪs lɔː / (BrE)
the practice of actively seeking clients as a lawyer by targeting those involved in accidents or traumatic events, often considered unethical

Examples

Ambulance-chase law practice
Many see ambulance-chase law as damaging to the profession's public image.
She decided to avoid ambulance-chase law and built a reputation as an ethical attorney.
Working in ambulance-chase law made him uncomfortable due to its aggressive practices.
