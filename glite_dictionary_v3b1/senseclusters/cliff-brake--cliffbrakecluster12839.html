cliff-brake

noun
a small rock-dwelling fern
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪf ˌbreɪk / (AmE), / ˈklɪf ˌbreɪk / (BrE)
Any of several small ferns that grow on rocks, found in tropical and warm temperate regions.

Examples

A cliff-brake fern

We spotted a rare cliff-brake during our hike in the canyon.
