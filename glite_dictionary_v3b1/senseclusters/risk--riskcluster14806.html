risk

noun
possibility of danger or loss
Pronunciation: / rɪsk / (AmE), / rɪsk / (BrE)
the possibility or chance that something bad, dangerous, or harmful may happen

Examples

A risk of injury
He was aware of the risk but decided to proceed anyway.
Investing in stocks carries a certain risk of losing money.
Smoking increases your risk of developing heart disease.
Doctors assess a patient's risk before recommending vaccination.
Frequent travel increases your risk of being exposed to new viruses.
The risk of infection rises in crowded places.
Climbing the mountain in winter involves a significant risk of avalanches.
Investing all your money in one company carries a high risk.
She was willing to take a risk to achieve her dreams.

Related Terms

  • synonym: danger - the possibility of harm or injury
  • synonym: hazard - a potential source of harm or adverse effect
  • antonym: safety - the condition of being protected from danger
