sop

noun
a conciliatory concession
8.6 0.34/m
Pronunciation: / sÉ‘p / (AmE), / sÉ’p / (BrE)
a thing given or done as a concession to appease someone, often to lessen anger or dissatisfaction

Examples

A sop to critics
The apology served as a sop to the critics, but did little to solve the real issue.
The small tax cut was merely a sop to an angry public.
They offered the free gifts as a sop to dissatisfied customers.

Synonyms

  • concession
    5.1 4.31/m
    concession is yielding or agreeing to a demand in negotiation, while sop is a small gift or gesture to calm someone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concession - a thing granted, often to appease
  • synonym: placate - to make someone less angry or hostile
  • antonym: provocation - action or speech that makes someone angry
