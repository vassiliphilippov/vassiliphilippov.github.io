spending money

noun
money for incidental expenses
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛndɪŋ ˌmʌni / (AmE), / ˈspɛndɪŋ ˌmʌni / (BrE)
money kept to spend on small, incidental expenses or personal purchases.

Examples

Spending money for shopping
I set aside part of my paycheck as spending money each week.
She always keeps a little spending money in her purse for coffee and snacks.
The children were thrilled to receive some spending money for their school trip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: allowance - regular sum of money given for personal spending
  • synonym: pocket money - money given to a child by parents for small expenses
