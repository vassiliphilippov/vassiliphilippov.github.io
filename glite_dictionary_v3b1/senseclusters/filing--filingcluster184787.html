filing

noun
small particle removed by a file
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
a small particle or shaving removed from a surface by a file, especially in metalworking or woodworking

Examples

Metal filings
After sharpening the blade, he carefully cleared away the steel filings.
The mechanic brushed the metal filings off the workbench before starting a new job.
Tiny filings from the saw collected in a pile beneath the table.
