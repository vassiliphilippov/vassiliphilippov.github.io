outercourse

noun
sexual stimulation without penetration
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtɚˌkɔrs / (AmE), / ˈaʊtəˌkɔːs / (BrE)
sexual activity involving stimulation without vaginal penetration, often used as a method to avoid pregnancy or sexually transmitted infections.
