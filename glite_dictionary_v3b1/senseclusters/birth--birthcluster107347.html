birth

noun
the beginning or start of something
1.4
Pronunciation: / bɜrθ / (AmE), / bɜːθ / (BrE)
the start or coming into existence of something, especially life or a new period

Examples

The birth of a nation
Following the revolution, the country celebrated the birth of a democracy.
The birth of his first novel launched his writing career.
The invention of the internet marked the birth of the digital age.

Related Terms

  • antonym: death - the end of life or existence
  • synonym: origin - the point or place where something begins
