blearily

adverb
unclearly, sleepily
Pronunciation: / ˈblɪrəli / (AmE), / ˈblɪərəli / (BrE)
in a way that shows tiredness, confusion, or blurred vision, often after waking up or when exhausted

Examples

To blearily stare
He stared blearily at the clock, struggling to remember what day it was.
After a long night of studying, she answered the question blearily in class.
The children looked blearily around the room when the lights came on early in the morning.
He stared blearily at the computer screen after staying up all night.
She answered the phone blearily, still half asleep from her nap.
The students looked blearily at the teacher during the early morning class.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alertly - in a way that shows clear thinking and attention
  • synonym: drowsily - in a sleepy or sluggish manner
  • synonym: sleepily - in a way that shows you are tired or about to fall asleep
