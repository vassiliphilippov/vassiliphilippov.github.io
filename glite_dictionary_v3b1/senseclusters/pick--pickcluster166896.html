Synonyms

    3.5 6.28/m
    pluck pulls something quickly and sharply with the fingers, while pick means removing fruit or flowers one by one from where they grow.
  • gather
    1.6 9.15/m
    gather involves searching for and collecting many wild fruits or plants, while pick focuses on removing individual items from where they grow.
  • harvest
    1.6 10/m
    harvest collects crops or natural products at ripeness on a larger scale, while pick refers to taking individual fruits or flowers by hand.
