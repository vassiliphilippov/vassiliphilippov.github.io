pollinosis

noun
allergic rhinitis caused by pollen
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːlɪˈnoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˈnəʊsɪs / (BrE)
A seasonal allergic inflammation of the nasal passages caused by an allergic reaction to pollen, also known as hay fever.

Examples

Pollinosis symptoms
His doctor diagnosed him with pollinosis after he complained of persistent sneezing and itchy eyes.
Many people suffer from pollinosis during the spring when pollen counts are high.
Treatments for pollinosis typically involve antihistamines and avoiding exposure to pollen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hay fever - common term for seasonal allergy to pollen (allergic rhinitis)
