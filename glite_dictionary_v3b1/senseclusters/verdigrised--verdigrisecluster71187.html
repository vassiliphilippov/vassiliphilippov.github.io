verdigrised

adjective
green with patina
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrdɪˌɡraɪzd / (AmE), / ˈvɜːdɪˌɡraɪzd / (BrE)
covered with or colored by a greenish patina that forms on copper or brass surfaces over time

Examples

Verdigrised copper surface
The ancient coins were verdigrised after centuries buried underground.
A verdigrised copper roof gave the old building its distinctive green color.
The artist admired the verdigrised surface of the weathered statue in the park.
