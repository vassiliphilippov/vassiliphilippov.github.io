gymnemic

noun
plant-derived acid
Pronunciation: / dʒɪmˈniːmɪk / (AmE), / dʒɪmˈniːmɪk / (BrE)
a compound, especially an acid, derived from Gymnema plants and used to reduce sweetness perception or in herbal medicine
