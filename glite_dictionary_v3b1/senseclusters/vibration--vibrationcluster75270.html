vibration

noun
an atmosphere or feeling sensed instinctively
2.8
Pronunciation: / vaɪˈbreɪʃən / (AmE), / vaɪˈbreɪʃən / (BrE)
a feeling or atmosphere that someone senses in a place, situation, or from a person, often perceived instinctively

Examples

A good vibration
I always get a good vibration from that café; it's so welcoming.
Sometimes you pick up a strange vibration from someone you've just met.
There was a tense vibration in the air as the meeting began.
