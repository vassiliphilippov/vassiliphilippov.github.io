beadboard

noun
grooved wood paneling
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːdbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbiːdbɔːd / (BrE)
a type of wooden paneling featuring narrow, vertical grooves, often used for walls or ceilings in interior design

Examples

Wooden beadboard paneling
They installed beadboard in the bathroom for a classic look.
The kitchen walls were covered with white beadboard to create a cozy, cottage-style atmosphere.
Adding beadboard to the ceiling gave the room a charming, traditional feel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paneling - decorative or protective wood panels for walls or ceilings
  • synonym: wainscoting - wooden paneling on the lower part of a wall
