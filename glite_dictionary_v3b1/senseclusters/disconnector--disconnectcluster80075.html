disconnector

noun
quick-release device
Pronunciation: / dɪs.kəˈnɛktər / (AmE), / dɪs.kəˈnɛktə / (BrE)
a device or fitting that allows hoses, pipes, or cables to be quickly and easily separated from each other

Examples

Hose disconnector
The technician used a disconnector to quickly separate the cables during maintenance.
A special disconnector makes it easy to detach the hose without any tools.
Before replacing the filter, she released the disconnector on the fuel line.

Related Terms

  • antonym: connector - a device for joining two things together
  • synonym: coupling - a device that connects two parts together
