Lettish

noun
older term for Latvian language
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛtɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈlɛtɪʃ / (BrE)
an older name for the Latvian language, the official language of Latvia, belonging to the Baltic branch of the Indo-European family

Examples

Lettish language
Few people use the term Lettish today, preferring 'Latvian' instead.
He decided to learn Lettish before traveling to Riga.
Some old grammar books refer to the language as Lettish.
