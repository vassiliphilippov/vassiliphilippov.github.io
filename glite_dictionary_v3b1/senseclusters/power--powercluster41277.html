a number that indicates how many times another number (the base) is to be multiplied by itself; also called exponent

Examples

To the power of
In the expression 5 to the second power, 5 is the base and 2 is the exponent.
Raising 2 to the third power gives you 8.
The power of a number is shown as a small superscript.
