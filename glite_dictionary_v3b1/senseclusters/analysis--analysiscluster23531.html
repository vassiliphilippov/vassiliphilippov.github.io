analysis

noun
literary criticism by structural examination
Pronunciation: / əˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / əˈnæləsɪs / (BrE)
A method of literary criticism that examines and interprets the structure and elements of a piece of writing.

Examples

Literary analysis essay
Her analysis of the poem focused on its use of metaphor and symbolism.
Students are asked to write an analysis of Shakespeare’s sonnets for their final assignment.
The professor's analysis revealed hidden themes and motifs in the novel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: critique - a detailed analysis and assessment of something, especially a literary, philosophical, or political theory
  • synonym: interpretation - the act of explaining the meaning of something, especially in literature or art
