purse

verb
contract or pucker (lips or surface)
Pronunciation: / pɜrs / (AmE), / pɜːs / (BrE)
to contract or pucker the lips or another part of the body into wrinkles or folds

Examples

The child pursed her face as she tasted the sour lemon.
He pursed his lips to blow out the candle on the cake.

The child pursed her lips before giving her grandmother a kiss.
