gatekeeper

noun
person who guards an entrance
5.1 5.63/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…°e…™tÀåkiÀêp…ôr / (AmE), / Àà…°e…™tÀåkiÀêp…ô / (BrE)
a person or organization that controls access to a place, person, or thing

Examples

The gatekeeper controls access
A uniformed gatekeeper stood at the main entrance, ready to answer visitors' questions.
As the gatekeeper of the estate, he was responsible for monitoring all who entered and left.
The gatekeeper checked everyone's identification before allowing them onto the premises.
An effective gatekeeper balances security with openness to new ideas.
In many industries, editors serve as gatekeepers to publication.
The gatekeeper insisted that only authorized personnel could enter the premises.
