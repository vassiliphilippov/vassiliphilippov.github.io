burnout

noun
car tire spinning stunt
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜrnaʊt / (AmE), / ˈbɜːnaʊt / (BrE)
an act of spinning a vehicle's wheels rapidly so they produce smoke and leave marks of burned rubber on the road

Examples

Car burnout show
The crowd cheered as the driver performed a spectacular burnout before the race began.
After the event, the parking lot was covered in black marks from repeated burnouts.
He showed off his car's power by doing a quick burnout at the intersection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: donut - a driving maneuver where a car spins in tight circles, leaving tire marks
  • synonym: drift - to slide a car sideways through a turn, often intentionally
