elk-wood

noun
North American deciduous tree with umbrella-like leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈɛlkˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈɛlkˌwʊd / (BrE)
A small deciduous tree of eastern North America with creamy white flowers and large, umbrella-like leaves at the ends of branches.

Examples

The elk-wood tree
Botanists are researching how the large leaves of elk-wood help it thrive in shaded forests.
The elk-wood in their backyard blooms with white flowers in late spring.
We identified the young sapling as an elk-wood by its umbrella-shaped foliage.
