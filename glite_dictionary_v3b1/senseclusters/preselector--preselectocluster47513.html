preselector

a person who selects or screens candidates, entries, or items in advance of a final selection process

Examples

Job preselector

As a preselector, she was responsible for narrowing down hundreds of entries to a manageable shortlist.
Each preselector used specific criteria to decide which candidates would move to the next round.
