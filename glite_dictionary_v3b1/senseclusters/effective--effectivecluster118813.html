Synonyms

  • efficacious
    8.6 1.13/m
    efficacious is a formal word often used for medicines or treatments that produce the intended result, while effective is general.
  • effectual
    6.8 0.25/m
    effectual is a formal or old-fashioned word for something that actually works to bring results, while effective is the everyday term.
  • potent
    5.5 9.49/m
    potent describes a very strong force or drug with strong effects, while effective means it achieves a specific intended result.
  • successful
    1.6 129/m
    successful means achieving a goal or positive outcome, while effective focuses on producing the intended result or working well.
