pool ball

noun
ball used to play pool
Pronunciation: / pul bɑl / (AmE), / puːl bɔːl / (BrE)
a ball used in the game of pool, typically numbered and colored for specific play

Examples

A pool ball set
Every pool ball must be racked at the start of the game.
He carefully aimed at the pool ball before making his shot.
The striped pool ball rolled across the green felt and dropped into the pocket.
