Opiliones

noun
an order of arachnids (harvestmen)
Pronunciation: / oʊˌpɪliˈoʊniːz / (AmE), / əʊˌpɪliˈəʊniːz / (BrE)
An order of arachnids commonly known as harvestmen, characterized by their small, rounded bodies and long, slender legs.

Examples

The Opiliones species
Scientists have discovered several new species within the Opiliones in the Amazon rainforest.
The long, slender legs of Opiliones help them navigate through leaf litter and forest floors.
Unlike spiders, Opiliones do not produce silk or venom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harvestman - any member of the Opiliones order, commonly known as a daddy longlegs
