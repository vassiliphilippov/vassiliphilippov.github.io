Synonyms

  • reassuring
    3.1 4/m
    reassuring gives clear words or facts that restore confidence and reduce worry, while comforting offers warm support to calm anxiety.
  • assuring
    3.4 0.00/m
    assuring promises certainty or guarantees to remove doubt, while comforting focuses on emotional warmth and easing worry.
  • consoling
    3.4 0.03/m
    consoling gives gentle words and presence to ease deep sadness, while comforting can also calm smaller worries and fears.
  • consolatory
    7.8 0.01/m
    consolatory feels formal or literary and comforts after loss, while comforting sounds more everyday and less formal in speech.
  • soothing
    2.9 4/m
    soothing reduces pain or strong upset with gentle touch or sounds, while comforting often uses words and emotional support.
  • calming
    2.6 0.3/m
    calming lowers anger, stress, or a loud scene with quiet actions, while comforting focuses on personal warmth and reassurance.
