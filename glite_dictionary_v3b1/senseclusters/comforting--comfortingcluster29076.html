comforting

adjective
providing comfort or solace
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌmfərtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʌmfətɪŋ / (BrE)
making someone feel less worried, upset, or anxious; giving comfort or reassurance

The warm blanket was comforting on a cold winter night.
Her words had a comforting effect during the difficult times.
It is comforting to know that help is only a phone call away.
The sound of rain falling outside was strangely comforting to him.
