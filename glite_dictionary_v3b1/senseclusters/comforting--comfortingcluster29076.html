Synonyms

  • reassuring

    reassuring gives clear words or facts that restore confidence and reduce worry, while comforting offers warm support to calm anxiety.
  • assuring
    3.4 0.00/m
    assuring promises certainty or guarantees to remove doubt, while comforting focuses on emotional warmth and easing worry.
  • consoling
    3.4 0.04/m
    consoling gives gentle words and presence to ease deep sadness, while comforting can also calm smaller worries and fears.
  • consolatory
    7.8 0.07/m

  • soothing
    2.9 5.95/m

  • calming
    2.6 0.51/m
