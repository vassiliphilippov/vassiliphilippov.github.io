chinois

noun
conical kitchen sieve
9.1
Pronunciation: / ʃiːnˈwɑː / (AmE), / ʃiːnˈwɑː / (BrE)
a conical fine-mesh sieve used in cooking to strain sauces, soups, or stocks for a smooth texture

Examples

A chinois strainer
The chef pressed the sauce through a chinois to achieve a silky consistency.
After simmering the soup, she strained it using a chinois for a refined finish.
A chinois is essential in professional kitchens for making smooth purees and stocks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: colander - a bowl-shaped kitchen utensil with holes for draining food
  • synonym: strainer - a kitchen tool used to separate solids from liquids
