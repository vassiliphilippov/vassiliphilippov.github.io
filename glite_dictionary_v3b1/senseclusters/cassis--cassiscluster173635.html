cassis

noun
large marine snail
9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈsiːs / (AmE), / kaˈsiːs / (BrE)
a large tropical marine snail of the genus Cassis, or its heavy, decorative shell

Examples

Cassis shell display
The collector displayed a beautiful cassis shell on the living room shelf.
Divers often search for cassis in the warm waters near coral reefs.
A single cassis can be the highlight of a shell collection due to its size and color.
