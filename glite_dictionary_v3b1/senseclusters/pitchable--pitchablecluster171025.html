pitchable

adjective
able to be tuned
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪtʃəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈpɪtʃəbl̩ / (BrE)
capable of being tuned to or producing a specific musical pitch

Examples

Pitchable guitar string
The new guitar is highly pitchable, making it easy for musicians to adjust the sound.
Some traditional flutes are not pitchable and cannot be tuned to match other instruments.
A pitchable instrument is essential for playing in an orchestra where tuning is important.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tunable - able to be adjusted to a particular pitch or note
  • antonym: untunable - not able to be tuned to a specific pitch
