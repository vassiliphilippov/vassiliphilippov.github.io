hallmark

noun
distinctive feature or characteristic
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔlˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈhɔːlˌmɑːk / (BrE)
a distinctive feature or characteristic that is typical of a person, place, or thing

Examples

A hallmark of quality
Attention to detail is the hallmark of a good craftsman.
Honesty was the hallmark of her leadership style.
The hallmark of the brand is its simple, elegant design.

  • synonym: feature - a distinctive attribute or aspect
  • synonym: trait - a distinguishing quality or characteristic
