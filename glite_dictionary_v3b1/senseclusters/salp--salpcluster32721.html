salp

noun
small barrel-shaped marine tunicate
7.1 8.8
Pronunciation: / sælp / (AmE), / sælp / (BrE)
A small, transparent, barrel-shaped marine animal belonging to the group of tunicates, which moves by pumping water through its body.

Examples

A salp animal
A single salp filters plankton from the water as it moves with the current.
Chains of salps can sometimes be seen drifting near the surface of the sea.
The researcher collected several salps during the oceanic expedition for further study.
