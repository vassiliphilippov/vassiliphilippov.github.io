Mexican sunflower

noun
a tall flowering plant of the genus Tithonia
4.0 5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈmek.sɪ.kən ˈsʌn.flaʊ.ər/ (AmE), /ˈmek.sɪ.kən ˈsʌn.flaʊ.ə/ (BrE)
