Mexican sunflower

noun
a tall flowering plant of the genus Tithonia
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛksɪkən ˈsʌnˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈmɛksɪkən ˈsʌnˌflaʊə / (BrE)
a tall, coarse herb or shrub of Mexico to Panama, of the genus Tithonia, with large flower heads resembling sunflowers and yellow to orange-scarlet rays
