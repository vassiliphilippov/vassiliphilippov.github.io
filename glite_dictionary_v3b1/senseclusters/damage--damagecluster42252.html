damage

noun
(informal) cost or price; bill total
Pronunciation: / ˈdæmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdamɪdʒ / (BrE)
(informal) the amount of money required to pay for something; the cost or price, especially of a bill.

Examples

Pay the damage
After shopping, she checked her wallet to see if she could cover the damage.
Could you tell me what the damage is for dinner tonight?
When the bill arrived, he nervously asked, 'So what's the damage?'

Related Terms

  • synonym: cost - the amount paid to buy or do something
  • synonym: price - the amount of money for which something is sold
