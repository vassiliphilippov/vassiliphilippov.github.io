home theater

noun
audio-video setup for cinema-like home experience
Pronunciation: / hoʊm ˈθiːətər /; / hoʊm ˈθiətɚ / (AmE), / həʊm ˈθɪətə / (BrE)
A system of audio and video equipment designed to reproduce the experience of watching movies in a cinema, but at home.

Examples

A home theater system
Having a home theater makes movie nights much more exciting.
The salesman recommended the latest home theater system for improved audio quality.
We installed a home theater in our basement to enjoy movies with surround sound.
Many electronics stores sell complete home theatre packages with surround sound speakers.
She invited friends over to watch the game on her new home theatre system.
We're upgrading our living room to add a home theatre for the best movie nights.
