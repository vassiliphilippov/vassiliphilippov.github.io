scintillation

noun
a brilliant display of wit
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪn.təlˈeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɪn.tɪˈleɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a brilliant or sparkling display of wit, cleverness, or lively intelligence

Examples

Scintillation of wit
Her dinner parties are renowned for their scintillation and sparkling conversation.
The speaker's scintillation during the debate kept the audience thoroughly entertained.
While the play was uneven, its occasional scintillation delighted critics.
