tenuousness

noun
thin or weak quality
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈten.ju.əs.nəs/ /; / /ˈten.ju.ə.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈten.ju.əs.nəs/ /; / /ˈten.ju.ə.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being thin, weak, or having low density, especially in a physical sense (such as air, structure, or form)

Examples

The tenuousness of the atmosphere at high altitudes makes breathing more difficult for climbers.
