undecided

adjective
not yet decided; uncertain
1.6 2.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈsaɪdɪd /; / ˌʌn.dɪˈsaɪdɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈsaɪdɪd /; / ˌʌn.dɪˈsaɪdɪd / (BrE)
not having made a decision or choice yet; still open or uncertain

Examples

Still undecided
After hearing both arguments, she remained undecided about which side to support.
Many voters are still undecided just days before the election.
The match was so close that the result was undecided until the final moments.
Many undecided voters waited until the last day to choose a candidate.
She remains undecided about accepting the job offer.
The committee is still undecided on the location for the next meeting.
After hours of discussion, the committee was still undecided on the proposal.
Her plans for the summer are undecided as she weighs different options.
The election results remain undecided until all the votes are counted.
