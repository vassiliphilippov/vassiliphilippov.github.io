Bradypodidae

noun
family of true sloths
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌbrædɪˈpɑːdəˌdiː / (AmE), / ˌbrædɪˈpɒdɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of mammals comprising the true sloths, native to Central and South America.

Examples

Bradypodidae family mammals
Members of the Bradypodidae are commonly known as three-toed sloths.
Scientists have studied the evolution of the Bradypodidae to understand the development of slow movement in mammals.
The Bradypodidae are well adapted to their arboreal lifestyle in South American rainforests.
