depressionary

adjective
causing depression
8.2
Pronunciation: / dɪˈprɛʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / dɪˈprɛʃənəri / (BrE)
relating to or causing psychological depression or a prolonged low mood.

Examples

Depressionary mood swings
The long, dark winter had a depressionary effect on many people in the town.
She avoided depressionary news stories to protect her mental health.
The film's depressionary tone made it difficult for some viewers to enjoy.
