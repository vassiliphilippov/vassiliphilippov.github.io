flight

noun
limited software rollout
5.6
Pronunciation: /flaɪt/ (AmE), /flaɪt/ (BrE)
a staged, limited release of a software feature or update to a subset of users so developers can test performance, collect feedback, and monitor for problems before wider deployment.

Examples

A flight release
The company launched the payment update in flight to a small group of long-term users.
During the flight, engineers monitored logs and rolled back changes when errors appeared.
Product managers scheduled the A/B test and put the redesign into flight tomorrow morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: canary release - a technique of deploying a change to a small subset of users to reduce risk
  • synonym: pilot - a small-scale, preliminary trial of a product or feature before full launch
  • synonym: rollout - the introduction or deployment of a new product or feature to users
