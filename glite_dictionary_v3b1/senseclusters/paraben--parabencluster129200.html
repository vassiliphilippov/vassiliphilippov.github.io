paraben

noun
preservative compound
Pronunciation: / ˈpærəˌbɛn / (AmE), / ˈpærəbɛn / (BrE)
a chemical compound used as a preservative in cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and food products to prevent the growth of bacteria and mold
