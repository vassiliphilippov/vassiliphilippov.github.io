crown jewel

noun
precious stone in a monarch's regalia
8.0
Pronunciation: / kraʊn ˈdʒuːəl / (AmE), / kraʊn ˈdʒuːəl / (BrE)
a precious stone that forms an important and valuable part of a monarch's ceremonial regalia

Examples

Royal crown jewel
Many tourists visit the tower to catch a glimpse of the crown jewels on display.
The Koh-i-Noor diamond is considered a crown jewel of the British monarchy.
The theft of a crown jewel from the royal palace caused a major scandal.
