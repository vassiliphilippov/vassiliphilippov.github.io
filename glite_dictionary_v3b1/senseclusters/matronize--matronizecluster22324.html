matronize

verb
treat in a condescending, motherly way
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪ.trəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmeɪ.trə.naɪz / (BrE)
to treat (someone) in a condescending, motherly, or patronizing way

Examples

To matronize a person
He didn't mean to matronize you, but his tone came across as condescending.
Please don't matronize me; I am perfectly capable of making my own decisions.
She tended to matronize her younger colleagues, which they found annoying.

Related Terms

  • synonym: patronize - to treat with apparent kindness that betrays a feeling of superiority
