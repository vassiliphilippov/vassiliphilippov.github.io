Dactylorhiza fuchsii

noun
European orchid species with spotted leaves and flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdæktɪləˈraɪzə ˈfʊksi.aɪ / (AmE), / ˌdæktɪləˈraɪzə ˈfʊksi.aɪ / (BrE)
A species of European orchid with lance-shaped leaves spotted with purple and flowers ranging in color from pink to white or mauve, marked with deep red or purple spots or lines.
