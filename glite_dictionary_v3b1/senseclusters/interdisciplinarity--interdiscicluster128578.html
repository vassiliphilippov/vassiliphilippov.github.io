interdisciplinarity

noun
combining disciplines
7.7
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.dɪs.ə.plɪ.ˈnær.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.dɪs.ə.plɪ.ˈnær.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
the quality or practice of involving two or more academic disciplines, subjects, or fields in study, research, or activities
