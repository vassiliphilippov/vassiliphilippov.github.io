shrew

noun
bad-tempered, nagging woman (offensive)
9.1 0.42/m
Pronunciation: /  Éru / (AmE), /  ÉruÀê / (BrE)
An offensive term for a woman who is regarded as bad-tempered, scolding, or nagging.

Examples

A shrew of a woman
Despite her intelligence, some unfairly labeled her a shrew for speaking her mind.
He grew weary of the constant bickering with the village shrew.
The play features a strong-willed shrew who defies traditional expectations.
