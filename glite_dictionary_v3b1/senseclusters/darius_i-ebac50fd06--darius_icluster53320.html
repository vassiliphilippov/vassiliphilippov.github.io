(c. 550–486 BC) King of Persia who expanded the Achaemenid Empire and led the first invasion of Greece, being defeated at the Battle of Marathon.

Examples

Darius I king of Persia
Darius I commissioned the construction of the famous Royal Road to improve communication across his empire.
