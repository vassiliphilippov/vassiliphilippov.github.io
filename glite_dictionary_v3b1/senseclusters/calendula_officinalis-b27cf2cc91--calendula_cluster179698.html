Calendula officinalis

noun

8.1
Pronunciation: / kəˌlɛn.dʒə.lə əˌfɪs.ɪˈnæl.ɪs / (AmE), / kəˌlɛndjʊlə ˌɒfɪsɪˈneɪlɪs / (BrE)
a species of flowering plant known as the common marigold, widely grown for its yellow or orange blooms and used in gardens and herbal remedies

Examples
