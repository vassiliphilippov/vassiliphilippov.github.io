cesspool

noun
underground tank for sewage
7.7 1.26/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs…õsÀåpul / (AmE), / Ààs…õs.puÀêl / (BrE)
an underground, covered tank or pit for the collection and storage of liquid waste and sewage, especially from a house

Examples

A cesspool overflow
After the heavy rains, the cesspool overflowed, causing a public health concern.
Many rural homes still rely on a cesspool rather than a modern sewer system.
The inspector warned that the old cesspool needed to be replaced immediately.
