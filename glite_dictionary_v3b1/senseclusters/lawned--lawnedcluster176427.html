lawned

adjective
covered with grass
Pronunciation: / lɔnd / (AmE), / lɔːnd / (BrE)
having or covered with a lawn or lawns, especially referring to an area of grass.

Examples

Lawned garden
The hotel is surrounded by beautifully lawned gardens.
They enjoyed a picnic on the lawned area behind the museum.
The lawned courtyard was perfect for outdoor events and gatherings.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bare - not covered with grass or plants
  • synonym: grassy - covered with or full of grass
