maldistribution

noun
uneven distribution
Pronunciation: / ˌmældɪstrɪˈbjuːʃən / (AmE), / ˌmældɪstrɪˈbjuːʃən / (BrE)
an uneven or improper distribution of something, especially within a system, leading to inefficiency or reduced performance

Examples

Maldistribution of coolant
Engineers discovered that maldistribution of coolant was causing the system to overheat.
The report highlighted the maldistribution of resources between urban and rural areas.
Airflow maldistribution in the ventilation system led to uncomfortable temperatures in some rooms.

Related Terms

  • antonym: distribution - the act of spreading or sharing something among recipients
  • synonym: imbalance - a lack of balance or equality
