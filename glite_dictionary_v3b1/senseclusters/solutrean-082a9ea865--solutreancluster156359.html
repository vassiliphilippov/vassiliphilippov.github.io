Solutrean

noun
Paleolithic culture

Pronunciation: /sə.ˈluː.triː.ən/ /; / /soʊ.ˈluː.triː.ən/ (AmE), /sə.ˈluː.triː.ən/ /; / /sɒ.ˈluː.triː.ən/ (BrE)

Examples

The Solutrean culture
The Solutrean is famous for its beautifully crafted stone blades and points.
