stop payment

noun
order to bank not to pay a check
1.0
Pronunciation: / stɑp ˈpeɪmənt / (AmE), / stɒp ˈpeɪmənt / (BrE)
an order given by a depositor to a bank instructing it not to pay a specified check or draft

Examples

A stop payment order
After discovering an error, Jane called her bank to place a stop payment on the check.
If you think your checkbook was stolen, you should request a stop payment immediately.
The company issued a stop payment on the lost check to prevent unauthorized cashing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cancelled check - a check that has been marked as cancelled so it cannot be cashed
