farming

noun
repetitive in-game resource gathering
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈfɑːr.mɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈfɑː.mɪŋ/ (BrE)
(in video games) the activity of repeatedly performing tasks or defeating enemies to collect in-game rewards such as money, items, or experience points.

Examples

Farming gold
He spent hours farming gold in the RPG to upgrade his equipment.
Many players prefer farming low-level monsters because they drop needed crafting materials.
The update reduced the efficiency of farming, making resource runs longer and more repetitive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grind - to perform repetitive in-game tasks to gain experience, items, or currency; used interchangeably in gaming contexts.
