Cypriniformes

noun
order of freshwater fishes (carps, minnows, etc.)
Pronunciation: / saɪˌprɪnɪˈfɔrmiːz / (AmE), / sɪˌprɪnɪˈfɔːmiːz / (BrE)
an order of mostly freshwater fishes that includes carps, minnows, loaches, and suckers.

Examples

The Cypriniformes order
Many species within the Cypriniformes are popular in aquariums around the world.
Scientists study the evolutionary adaptations of Cypriniformes to changing freshwater environments.
The order Cypriniformes includes well-known fish such as goldfish and carp.
