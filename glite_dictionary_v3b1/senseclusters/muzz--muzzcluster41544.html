muzz

noun
animal's snout
9.9
Pronunciation: / mʌz / (AmE), / mʌz / (BrE)
the mouth, muzzle, or snout of an animal; sometimes used to refer to the face (archaic or dialectal)

Examples

A dog's muzz
The dog pressed its muzz against the window, watching the children play outside.
After a long walk, the horse's muzz was covered in dust from the trail.
In old stories, a wolf's muzz is often described as sharp and fierce.

Related Terms

  • synonym: muzzle - the projecting part of the face, including the nose and mouth, of an animal
  • synonym: snout - the long nose and area around the mouth of some animals
