fritterer

noun
fritter maker or seller
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪtərər / (AmE), / ˈfrɪtərə / (BrE)
a person who makes, sells, or cooks fritters, which are small fried cakes or patties

Examples

Street fritterer
The famous fritterer at the market always has a long line of customers.
As a skilled fritterer, she experimented with new recipes every week.
Children gathered around the fritterer to watch him fry the sweet treats.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cook - a person who prepares food
  • synonym: vendor - a person who sells something
