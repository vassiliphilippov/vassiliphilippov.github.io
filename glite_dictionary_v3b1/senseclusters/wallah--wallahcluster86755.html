wallah

noun
person in charge of a task or place
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːlə / (AmE), / ˈwɒlə / (BrE)
(usually in combination) a person who is in charge of, works at, or is associated with a particular thing or place.

Examples

A ticket wallah
At the festival, the ticket wallah kept the queue moving efficiently.
He asked the chai wallah for a cup of tea at the station.
The kitchen wallah prepared breakfast for everyone before dawn.
