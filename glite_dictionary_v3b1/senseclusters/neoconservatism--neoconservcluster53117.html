neoconservatism

noun
return to traditional policies or values
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌnioʊkənˈsɝːvətɪzəm /; / ˌniːoʊkənˈsɜːrvəˌtɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌniːəʊkənˈsɜːvətɪzəm / (BrE)
a political ideology that supports traditional values, free-market capitalism, strong national defense, and an active foreign policy
