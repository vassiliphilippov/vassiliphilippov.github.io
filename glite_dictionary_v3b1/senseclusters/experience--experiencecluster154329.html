experience

noun
knowledge or skill gained by doing
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspɪriəns / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɪəriəns / (BrE)
the knowledge or skill gained through direct involvement in events or activities over time

Examples

Work experience
Nothing can prepare you for this job like hands-on experience.
She has a lot of experience working with children in different settings.
Through the experience of living abroad, he learned to adapt to new cultures.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inexperience - lack of knowledge or skill from not having done something before
  • synonym: knowledge - information, understanding, or skill that you get from experience or education
