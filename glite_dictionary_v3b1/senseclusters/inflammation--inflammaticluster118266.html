inflammation

noun
bodily response with redness, swelling, heat, pain
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.fləˈmeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.fləˈmeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
A localized physical condition in which part of the body becomes reddened, swollen, hot, and often painful, especially as a reaction to injury or infection.

Examples

Chronic inflammation
Chronic inflammation may lead to more serious health problems if left untreated.
Ibuprofen can help reduce inflammation and relieve pain caused by muscle strain.
The doctor examined the patient's knee and noticed significant inflammation around the joint.

Related Terms

  • synonym: infection - the invasion of the body by harmful microorganisms
  • synonym: swelling - an abnormal enlargement of a part of the body, typically as a result of accumulation of fluid
