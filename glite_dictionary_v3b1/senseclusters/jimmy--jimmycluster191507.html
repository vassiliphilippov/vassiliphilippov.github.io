jimmy

verb
make a makeshift repair
Pronunciation: / c8d926ami / (AmE), / c8d926ami / (BrE)
to make or assemble a temporary or improvised repair or device using available materials, often quickly and in an informal way.

Examples

To jimmy a fix
When the pipe burst, they had to jimmy a temporary seal with duct tape and a rag.
She jimmied the radio into working order with a paperclip and some electrical tape.
After hours of jimmying, the team produced a makeshift antenna that received a station.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jerry-rig - to assemble a makeshift or improvised solution quickly
  • synonym: jury-rig - to make a temporary or improvised repair, especially using whatever is at hand
