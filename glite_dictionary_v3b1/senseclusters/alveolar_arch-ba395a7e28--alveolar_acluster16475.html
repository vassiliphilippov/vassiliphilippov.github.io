Synonyms

  • alveolar ridge
    7.5 0.02/m
    alveolar ridge is the common dental term for the curved bony border holding teeth, while alveolar arch names the same curved part more anatomically.
  • alveolar process
    8.4 0.11/m
    alveolar process is the anatomical bone projection that forms the jaw border holding tooth sockets, while alveolar arch refers to that curved jaw ridge.
  • alveolar bed
    7.7 0.00/m
    alveolar bed is the part of jawbone that contains tooth sockets and supports teeth or implants, while alveolar arch names the curved ridge around them.
  • gum ridge
    6.7 0.00/m
    gum ridge is a more informal term people may use for the bony jaw border, while alveolar arch is the anatomical name.
