wiring

noun
system of electrical wires
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
the system of wires used to distribute electricity in a building, machine, or device

Examples

House wiring
Faulty wiring was responsible for the power outage last night.
The old house needs new wiring to meet current safety standards.
The technician inspected the wiring in the control panel for signs of damage.
