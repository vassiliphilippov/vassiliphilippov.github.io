cattle pen

noun
enclosure for confining cattle
Pronunciation: / ˈkæt̬l̩ pɛn / (AmE), / ˈkatl̩ pɛn / (BrE)
an enclosure or fenced area used to confine cattle

Examples

A cattle pen
A sturdy cattle pen is essential for managing large groups of livestock.
After the storm, the ranchers found several cows had escaped the cattle pen.
The workers cleaned out the cattle pen before bringing in the next herd.
