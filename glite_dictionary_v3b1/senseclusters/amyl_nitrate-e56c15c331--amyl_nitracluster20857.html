amyl nitrate

noun
a vasodilator chemical used medically
Pronunciation: / ˈæmɪl ˌnaɪˌtreɪt / (AmE), / ˈamɪl ˌnaɪtreɪt / (BrE)
a chemical compound used as a vasodilator, sometimes medically to treat angina pectoris

Examples
