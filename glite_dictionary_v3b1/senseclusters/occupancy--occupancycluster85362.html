occupancy

noun
number of users
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːkjəpənsi / (AmE), / ˈɒkjʊpənsi / (BrE)
the number of people who are present in or using a space at a particular time

Examples

Maximum occupancy
The apartment building has an occupancy of 120 residents.
Fire regulations limit the occupancy of the hall to 300 people.
During the festival, the stadium reached its maximum occupancy.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: capacity - the maximum number of people a space can hold
  • antonym: vacancy - the state of being unoccupied or available
