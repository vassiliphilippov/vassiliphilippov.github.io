Canarium

noun
tropical tree genus
Pronunciation: / kəˈnɛəriəm / (AmE), / kəˈneəriəm / (BrE)
a genus of tropical trees whose nuts or resin are used for food, oil, or other products

Examples

Canarium tree nuts
The Canarium tree produces edible nuts popular in Southeast Asian cuisine.
Farmers harvest resin from Canarium trees to make varnish and incense.
Many species in the Canarium genus are valued for their nutritious seeds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: resin - a sticky substance produced by some trees
