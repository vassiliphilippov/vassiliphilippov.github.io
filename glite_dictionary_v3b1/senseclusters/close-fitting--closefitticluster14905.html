close-fitting

adjective
fitting tightly but comfortably
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊsˌfɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkləʊsˌfɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
fitting tightly to the body or an object, but not uncomfortably so.

Examples

Close-fitting gloves
In winter, many people prefer close-fitting gloves to keep their hands warm.
She wore a close-fitting dress that accentuated her figure.
The close-fitting lid kept the container airtight.

Related Terms

  • antonym: loose-fitting - fitting in a way that leaves extra room, not tight
