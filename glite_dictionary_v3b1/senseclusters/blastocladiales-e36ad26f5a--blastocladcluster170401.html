Blastocladiales

noun
an order of fungi with unique asexual reproduction
Pronunciation: / ˌblæstoʊklæˈdiːəˌliːz /; / blæstəˌklædiˈeɪliːz / (AmE), / ˌblastəʊklæˈdiːəliːz /; / ˌblastəʊklædɪˈeɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of fungi that reproduce asexually by thick-walled resting spores, which produce zoospores when they germinate; sometimes classified in Oomycetes
