loin of lamb

noun
cut of meat from lamb's loin
6.1
Pronunciation: / lɔɪn əv læm / (AmE), / lɔɪn əv læm / (BrE)
A cut of meat taken from the loin section of a lamb, considered tender and often used for roasting or grilling.

Examples

A loin of lamb roast
A perfectly cooked loin of lamb should be pink in the center and juicy.
He ordered the loin of lamb with rosemary potatoes at the restaurant.
The chef recommended marinating the loin of lamb overnight for extra flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leg of lamb - a cut from the hind leg of lamb
  • synonym: rack of lamb - a cut from the rib section of lamb
