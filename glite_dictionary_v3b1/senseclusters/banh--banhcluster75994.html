banh

noun
Vietnamese cake or bread
9.9
Pronunciation: / bæn / (AmE), / bæn / (BrE)
a Vietnamese term for a variety of cakes, breads, or pastries, often made from rice or wheat flour

Examples

A sweet banh
At the market, she bought a delicious banh filled with mung bean paste.
He learned to make traditional banh for the Lunar New Year celebration.
The bakery offered several types of banh, both sweet and savory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bread - a staple food made from flour and water, usually baked
  • synonym: cake - a sweet baked dessert, often made with flour and sugar
  • synonym: pastry - a baked food made from dough, often sweet or filled
