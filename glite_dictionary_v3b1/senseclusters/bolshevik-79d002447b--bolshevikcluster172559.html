Bolshevik

adjective
relating to Bolshevism or the Bolsheviks
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊlʃəˌvɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɒlʃəvɪk / (BrE)
relating to Bolshevism or its supporters, especially to the Russian Bolshevik movement or its principles

Examples

Bolshevik ideas
Many exiles fled abroad to escape Bolshevik persecution after the revolution.
She wrote a thesis on Bolshevik ideology during the early Soviet period.
The Bolshevik regime implemented sweeping social and economic reforms after 1917.

Related Terms

  • synonym: communist - relating to communism or a member of a Communist party
