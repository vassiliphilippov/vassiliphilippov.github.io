actuated

adjective
moved to act; motivated
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈæktʃuˌeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈæktjʊeɪtɪd / (BrE)
moved to take action; motivated or prompted to act.

Examples

He was actuated by duty
He was actuated by a strong sense of duty to his community.
Her decision was actuated by a desire to make a difference.
The machinery is actuated by a hydraulic system.
