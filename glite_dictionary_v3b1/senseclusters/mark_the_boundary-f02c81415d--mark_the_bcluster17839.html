mark the boundary

verb
indicate a dividing line
Pronunciation: / mɑrk ðə ˈbaʊndəri / (AmE), / mɑːk ðə ˈbaʊndəri / (BrE)
indicate the dividing line or limit that separates two areas or entities.

Examples

To mark the boundary line
A white fence marks the boundary between the two properties.
Tall trees mark the boundary of the old estate.
The river naturally marks the boundary between the two countries.
