pepperbox

noun
container for pepper
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛpərˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈpɛpəbɒks / (BrE)
a small container with holes in the top used for sprinkling ground pepper onto food

Examples

A table pepperbox
She reached for the pepperbox and shook pepper over the salad before serving.
The antique pepperbox on the table was made of silver and beautifully engraved.
Please pass the pepperbox so I can add some spice to my soup.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pepper shaker - a container with holes in the top for sprinkling pepper
  • antonym: salt shaker - a container with holes in the top for sprinkling salt
