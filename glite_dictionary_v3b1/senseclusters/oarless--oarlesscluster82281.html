oarless

adjective
without oars
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrləs / (AmE), / ˈɔːləs / (BrE)
without oars; lacking the equipment needed to row a boat

Examples

An oarless boat
The oarless boat drifted slowly down the river.
After losing the paddles, they were left oarless in the middle of the lake.
He stared at the oarless canoe, wondering how they would get back to shore.
