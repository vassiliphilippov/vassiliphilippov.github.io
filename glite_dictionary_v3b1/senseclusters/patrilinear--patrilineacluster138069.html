patrilinear

adjective
tracing descent through the male line
Pronunciation: / ˌpætrɪˈlɪniɚ / (AmE), / ˌpætrɪˈlɪniə / (BrE)
Relating to, based on, or tracing descent through the male line.

Examples

Patrilinear family line
Anthropologists have studied patrilinear kinship systems in various societies.
In some traditional cultures, inheritance is determined by patrilinear rules.
Membership in the tribe is patrilinear, passed down from fathers to sons.
