canary-yellow

adjective
light yellow shade, like a canary
4.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈnɛri ˈjɛloʊ / (AmE), / kəˈneəri ˈjɛləʊ / (BrE)
Of a light to moderate yellow color, resembling that of a canary bird.

Examples

A canary-yellow bike
He bought a canary-yellow bike so he would be easily seen by motorists.
She wore a canary-yellow dress to the wedding, which immediately caught everyone's eye.
The kitchen walls were painted a cheerful canary-yellow to brighten up the space.
