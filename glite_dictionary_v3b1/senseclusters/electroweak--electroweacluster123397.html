electroweak

adjective
unified fundamental forces
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˈwiːk / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈwiːk / (BrE)
relating to the unified theory or interaction of electromagnetic and weak nuclear forces in physics

Examples

Electroweak force
The electroweak interaction is a key concept in modern particle physics.
Scientists study electroweak processes to understand how fundamental forces are connected.
The discovery of the Higgs boson confirmed important aspects of electroweak theory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electromagnetic - relating to the interaction of electric and magnetic fields
  • synonym: weak nuclear - relating to the weak nuclear force, one of the four fundamental forces
