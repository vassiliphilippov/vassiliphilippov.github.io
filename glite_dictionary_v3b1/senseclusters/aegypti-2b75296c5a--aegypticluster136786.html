Aegypti

proper noun
Latin for Egyptians
9.9
Pronunciation: / aɪˈdʒɪptaɪ / (AmE), / aɪˈdʒɪptaɪ / (BrE)
the Latin nominative plural form meaning 'the Egyptians', used in classical texts to refer to the people of Egypt

Examples

The Aegypti people
In many Roman histories, the Aegypti are described as skilled builders and artists.
The ancient text praised the wisdom of the Aegypti in matters of science and religion.
Poets of the era often referenced the Aegypti when describing the wonders of the Nile.
