mineral deficiency

noun
lack of essential mineral nutrient
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnərəl dɪˈfɪʃənsi / (AmE), / ˈmɪnərəl dɪˈfɪʃənsi / (BrE)
A lack of an essential mineral nutrient in the diet, which can cause health problems or disrupt normal body functions.

Examples

Mineral deficiency in diet
Eating a varied diet helps prevent mineral deficiency in children and adults.
Low energy and muscle cramps can be symptoms of a mineral deficiency.
The doctor diagnosed the patient with a mineral deficiency after reviewing her blood test results.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nutrient deficiency - a shortage of any nutritional substance needed for health
  • synonym: vitamin deficiency - a lack of essential vitamin(s) in the body
