Synonyms

  • step down

    step down means leaving an important job to let someone else take over, while step aside can also mean moving physically away.
  • step out of someone's way

    step out of someone's way means moving aside so someone can pass easily, while step aside also means leaving a role or position.
  • bow out
    4.9 0.79/m

  • stand down
