tenuity

noun
the thinness or slenderness of something
Pronunciation: / təˈnuːəti / (AmE), / tɪˈnjuːɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being very thin or slender; slightness of thickness or diameter.

Examples

Tenuity of the wire
Engineers had to account for the tenuity of the metal wires in the experiment.
She admired the tenuity of the artist's brushstrokes on the delicate canvas.
The tenuity of the thread made it nearly invisible against the fabric.

Related Terms

  • antonym: thickness - the state of being thick in dimension
  • synonym: thinness - the state of being thin in dimension
