frazil

noun
needle-shaped ice crystals
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈfreɪzɪl / (BrE)
loose, needle-shaped ice crystals that form in water, especially in rivers or lakes, during freezing conditions

Examples

Scientists studied the properties of frazil to better understand river ice formation.
