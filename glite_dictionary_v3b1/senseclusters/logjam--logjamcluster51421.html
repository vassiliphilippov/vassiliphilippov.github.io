logjam

noun
a blockage or impasse preventing progress
9.4 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈlɑːg.dʒæm/ (AmE), /ˈlɒg.dʒæm/ (BrE)
a situation in which progress is stopped by a serious blockage or obstacle, especially due to complicated or unusual activity

Political logjam

Efforts to fix the system finally broke through the logjam.
The talks reached a logjam that took weeks to resolve.
