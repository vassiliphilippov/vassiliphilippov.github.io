Denisovan

noun
extinct ancient human
9.9
Pronunciation: / dəˈniːsəvən / (AmE), / dɛˈniːsəvən / (BrE)
an extinct species or subspecies of ancient humans known from fossils in Asia, especially Siberia and Tibet
