Sunni

noun
major branch of Islam
5.3 2.71/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàsuÀêni, Ààs åni /; / ÀàsuÀêni, Ààs äni / (AmE), / Ààs äni, Ààs åni /; / Ààs äni, ÀàsuÀêni / (BrE)
a member of the largest branch of Islam, which accepts the first four caliphs as the rightful successors to Muhammad

Examples

Synonyms

  • Sunni Muslim
    3.2 0.42/m
    Sunni Muslim is the full everyday phrase used to describe followers of Sunni Islam, while Sunni is the shorter group label.
  • Sunnite
    8.9 0.01/m
    Sunnite is an older or less common form used in some texts, while Sunni is the modern standard name most people use.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Shia - the second largest branch of Islam, distinct from Sunni Islam
  • antonym: Shiite - a member of the branch of Islam that supports Ali and his descendants as the rightful successors to Muhammad
