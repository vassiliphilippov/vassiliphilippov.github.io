GTD

noun
pregnancy-related disorder
Pronunciation: / dʒi ti di / (AmE), / dʒiː tiː diː / (BrE)
a group of rare pregnancy-related disorders in which abnormal tissue grows inside the uterus, known as gestational trophoblastic disease

Examples

GTD diagnosis
The doctor explained that GTD can sometimes be detected through unusual symptoms during pregnancy.
Early diagnosis of GTD is important for effective treatment and recovery.
Specialists monitored her closely after she was diagnosed with GTD following her pregnancy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gestational trophoblastic disease - a group of disorders involving abnormal growth of cells inside the uterus during pregnancy
