Synonyms

  • self-confident
    3.2 1.0/m
    self-confident shows belief in one's own abilities, while confident can mean belief in self or in a situation.
  • self-assured
    4.1 1/m
    self-assured sounds calm and sure about abilities, while confident is a general feeling of certainty about self or things.
  • assured
    4.1 0.00/m
    assured is often formal and shows calm certainty, while confident is a common word for feeling sure about something.
  • sure
    1.0 400/m
    sure expresses no doubt about a fact, while confident also covers trust in your own skills or future outcomes.
  • certain
    1.0 40/m
    certain is stronger and often about facts or certainty, while confident can mean both self-belief and surety about things.
  • secure
    3.6 6/m
    secure means safe and free from worry, while confident focuses on being sure or believing in yourself or a result.
