bratling

noun
young child (mildly disparaging)
Pronunciation: / ˈbræt.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbræt.lɪŋ / (BrE)
a small or young child, often used in a mildly disparaging or playful way

Examples

A bratling child
The teacher tried to keep the group of bratlings under control during the field trip.
She laughed as the little bratling demanded another cookie with a cheeky grin.
After school, the park was filled with bratlings playing tag and shouting happily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brat - a badly behaved child
  • synonym: child - a young person who is not yet an adult
