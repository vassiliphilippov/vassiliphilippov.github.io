ring armor

noun
flexible armor of interlinked rings
Pronunciation: / rɪŋ ˈɑrmər / (AmE), / rɪŋ ˈɑːmə / (BrE)
(historical) Flexible armor made of interlinked metal rings, used especially in the Middle Ages for protection in battle.
