Venn diagram

noun
diagram showing relationships between sets
4.9
Pronunciation: / vɛn ˈdaɪ.əˌɡræm /; / vɛn ˈdaɪəˌgræm / (AmE), / vɛn ˈdaɪ.əɡram /; / vɛn ˈdaɪəɡræm / (BrE)
a diagram made of overlapping circles that shows the relationships and shared elements between different sets, especially in mathematics or logic

Examples

A Venn diagram with circles
Her presentation included a colorful Venn diagram illustrating the overlap among project teams.
The math teacher drew a Venn diagram on the board to explain the concept of union and intersection.
We used a Venn diagram to compare the similarities and differences between mammals and birds.
The data is best represented visually in a Venn diagram showing the intersection of the two groups.
The teacher used a Venn diagram to illustrate the similarities and differences between mammals and reptiles.
You can use a Venn diagram to compare the features of two stories.

Related Terms
