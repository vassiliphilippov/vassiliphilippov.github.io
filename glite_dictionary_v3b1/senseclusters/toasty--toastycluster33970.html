toasty

adjective
warm and crisp
2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈtoʊ.stiː/ /; / /ˈtoʊ.stɪ/ (AmE), /ˈtəʊ.stiː/ /; / /ˈtəʊ.stɪ/ (BrE)

Examples

Toasty bread
