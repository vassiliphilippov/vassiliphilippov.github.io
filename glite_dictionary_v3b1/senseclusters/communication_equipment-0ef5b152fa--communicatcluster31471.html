communication equipment

noun
devices used to transmit and receive information
1.9
Pronunciation: / kəˌmjunəˈkeɪʃən ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
the physical devices and facilities used to transmit, receive, and process information, such as telephones, radios, and computers
