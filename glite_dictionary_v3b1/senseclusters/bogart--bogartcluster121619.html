bogart

noun
selfish person
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊɡɑrt / (AmE), / ˈbəʊɡɑːt / (BrE)
a person who selfishly keeps or uses something and refuses to share it with others

Examples

A bogart with snacks
Don't be a bogart—let someone else have a turn with the game controller.
He was called a bogart for keeping all the snacks to himself during the movie.
Nobody likes a bogart at a party where everyone is supposed to share.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hog - a person who takes more than their share
  • antonym: share - to give a portion to others
