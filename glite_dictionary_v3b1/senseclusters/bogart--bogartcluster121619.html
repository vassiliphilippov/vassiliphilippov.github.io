bogart

noun
selfish person
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈboʊ.ɡɑːrt/ (AmE), /ˈbəʊ.ɡɑːt/ (BrE)
a person who selfishly keeps or uses something and refuses to share it with others

Examples

A bogart with snacks

He was called a bogart for keeping all the snacks to himself during the movie.
Nobody likes a bogart at a party where everyone is supposed to share.
