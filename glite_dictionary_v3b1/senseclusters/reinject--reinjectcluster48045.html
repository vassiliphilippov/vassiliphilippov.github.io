reinject

verb
to inject again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˈdʒɛkt / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈdʒɛkt / (BrE)
to inject something again, especially a substance into a body or system after an initial injection

Examples

To reinject medicine
If the treatment is not effective, doctors may reinject the medication after a few days.
Technicians had to reinject coolant into the system to prevent overheating.
The nurse will reinject the vaccine if the first dose was not administered properly.
