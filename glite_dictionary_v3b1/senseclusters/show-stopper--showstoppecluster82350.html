show-stopper

noun
something strikingly impressive or attractive
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊˌstɑːpər / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊˌstɒpə / (BrE)
something or someone that is strikingly impressive, attractive, or has great popular appeal

Examples

A real show-stopper
Every garden needs a show-stopper like the bright blue delphinium.
Her red gown was a real show-stopper at the gala event.
The new sports car design is a show-stopper that grabbed everyone's attention at the exhibition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dud - something that fails or is unimpressive
  • synonym: sensation - something that causes great excitement or attracts much attention
