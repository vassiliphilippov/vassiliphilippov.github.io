autogenetic

adjective
relating to autogenesis (self-originating)
Pronunciation: / ˌɔtoʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or resulting from autogenesis, especially the process by which something originates or develops from within itself rather than from an external cause.

Examples

Autogenetic process
Some minerals form through autogenetic processes deep within the Earth's crust.
The scientist discussed autogenetic theories of the origin of life in his paper.
The species' autogenetic development allows it to adapt quickly to changing environments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autogenic - self-generated or self-produced, especially by internal causes
