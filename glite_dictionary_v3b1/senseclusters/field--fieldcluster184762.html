field

noun
area of active military operations
1.1
Pronunciation: / fiːld / (AmE), / fiːld / (BrE)
an area where military operations or battles take place

Examples

Battle field
He wrote letters home from the field describing the harsh conditions of war.
Many soldiers were sent to the field during the early stages of the campaign.
Medical units are stationed close to the field to treat wounded personnel.
After the battle, the field was littered with abandoned weapons and uniforms.
Historians visited the famous field where the decisive conflict took place.
Many soldiers lost their lives on the field during the war.
