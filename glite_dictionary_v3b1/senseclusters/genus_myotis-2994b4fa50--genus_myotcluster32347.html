genus Myotis

noun
a genus of widely distributed bats
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs maɪˈoʊtɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs maɪˈəʊtɪs / (BrE)
the largest and most widely distributed genus of bats, comprising species known as mouse-eared bats

Examples

The genus Myotis bats
Researchers discovered a new species within the genus Myotis in Southeast Asia.
The genus Myotis includes more than a hundred species found across six continents.
The genus Myotis is commonly referred to as mouse-eared bats due to their distinctive ear shape.
