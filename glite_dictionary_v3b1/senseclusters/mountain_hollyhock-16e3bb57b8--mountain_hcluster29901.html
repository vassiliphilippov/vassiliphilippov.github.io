mountain hollyhock

noun
a perennial plant resembling hollyhock
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntn ˈhɑliˌhɑk / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn ˈhɒlɪˌhɒk / (BrE)
