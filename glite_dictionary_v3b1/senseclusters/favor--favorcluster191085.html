favor

noun
an act of kindness or support
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪvər / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvə / (BrE)
an act of kindness or support, often done to help someone without expecting anything in return

Examples

Do me a favor
Could you do me a favor and lend me your pen?
He returned the favor by helping her with her project.
She did me a huge favor by watching my kids last night.
Could you do me a favour and check this letter for errors?
He asked for no favour in return for his help.
She offered to babysit as a favour to her friend.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disfavour - state of being out of favour or not being liked or approved
  • synonym: kindness - the quality of being friendly, generous, and considerate
  • synonym: service - an act of helpful activity
