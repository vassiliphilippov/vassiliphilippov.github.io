terrorist group

noun
group using violence for political aims
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛrərɪst ɡruːp / (AmE), / ˈtɛrərɪst ɡruːp / (BrE)
An organized group that uses violence and intimidation, especially against civilians, in pursuit of political aims.

Examples

A terrorist group attacks
Authorities are concerned about the recruitment efforts of a new terrorist group in the region.
Several countries have added the organization to their list of designated terrorist groups.
The government condemned the attack carried out by a terrorist group on the capital.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: guerrilla group - an irregular armed group fighting by unconventional methods
  • synonym: terrorist organization - an organization that engages in acts of terrorism
