oleaginous

adjective
containing or producing oil; oily
8.3
Pronunciation: / oʊliˈædʒənəs / (AmE), / əʊliˈadʒɪnəs / (BrE)
containing, producing, or covered with oil or grease; oily or greasy.

Examples
