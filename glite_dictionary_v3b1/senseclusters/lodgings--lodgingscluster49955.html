lodgings

noun
temporary accommodation
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑdʒɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈlɒdʒɪŋz / (BrE)
a place to live temporarily, especially one rented for a short period such as rooms in a house, hotel, or inn

Examples

Cheap lodgings
His lodgings consisted of a small attic room above a bakery.
The university provides lodgings for new students arriving from abroad.
We found affordable lodgings near the city center during our trip.

  • antonym: residence - the place where someone lives permanently
