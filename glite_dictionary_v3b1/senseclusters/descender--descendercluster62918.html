descender

noun
a person who descends
8.4
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɛndər / (AmE), / dɪˈsɛndə / (BrE)
a person or thing that descends from a higher place to a lower one.

Examples

Mountain descender
As an experienced descender, she navigated the steep slope with ease.
Each descender wore a helmet for safety while rappelling down the cliff.
The first descender reached the base camp ahead of the others.

Related Terms

  • antonym: climber - someone who climbs up, especially mountains or rocks
