penetrative

adjective
able to enter or pierce
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛn.əˌtreɪ.tɪv / (AmE), / ˈpɛn.ɪ.trə.tɪv / (BrE)
having the power to enter, pierce, or affect deeply; able to penetrate.

Examples

Penetrative wind
