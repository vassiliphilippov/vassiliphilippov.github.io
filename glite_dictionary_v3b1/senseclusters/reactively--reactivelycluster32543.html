reactively

adverb
in a reactive way
Pronunciation: / riˈæktɪvli / (AmE), / riˈæktɪvli / (BrE)
in a manner that readily undergoes or participates in a chemical or physical reaction.

Examples

Reactively dissolves in water
The compound dissolved reactively when mixed with water, releasing heat and gas.
Some metals behave reactively in the presence of oxygen, leading to rapid oxidation.
The solution must be handled carefully because it can respond reactively to other chemicals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: actively - in a way that involves action or participation
  • antonym: inertly - in a way that shows little or no ability to react
