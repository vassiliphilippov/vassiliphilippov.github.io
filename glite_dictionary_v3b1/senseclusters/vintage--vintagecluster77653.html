vintage

noun
year's wine yield from a vineyard
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪntɪdʒ /; / ˈvɪn.tɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈvɪntɪdʒ /; / ˈvɪn.tɪdʒ / (BrE)
the wine made in a particular year or season, or the year itself as a mark of its age and quality

Examples
