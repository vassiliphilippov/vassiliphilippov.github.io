arduously

adverb
with great effort or difficulty
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrdʒuəsli / (AmE), / ˈɑːdjuəsli / (BrE)
In a way that requires great effort or difficulty; with hard work and persistence.

Examples

To study arduously
He studied arduously for the exams, determined to improve his grades.
She climbed the steep mountain trail arduously, refusing to give up despite the obstacles.
The team arduously worked on the project for months before achieving success.
