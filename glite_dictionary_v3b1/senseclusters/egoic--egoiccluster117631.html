egoic

adjective
relating to the ego
9.0
Pronunciation: / iˈɡoʊɪk / (AmE), / iˈɡəʊɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the ego or self-centeredness

Examples

egoic attitude
Her egoic attitude often prevented her from listening to others' ideas.
Meditation can help reduce egoic thinking and promote a sense of connection.
The leader's egoic decisions led to conflict within the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: egotistical - having an exaggerated sense of self-importance
  • antonym: selfless - putting others' needs before one's own
