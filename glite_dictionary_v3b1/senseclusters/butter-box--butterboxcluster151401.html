butter-box

noun
insincere, flattering person
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtər ˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈbʌtə ˌbɒks / (BrE)
a person who flatters excessively or is insincere in a superficial, charming manner

Examples

A butter-box flatterer
Although he called himself a leader, everyone knew he was just a butter-box who sought approval.
In group discussions, the butter-box always praises ideas he hasn't even considered.
They grew tired of the butter-box pretending to care about their opinions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flatterer - a person who gives excessive compliments, often insincerely
  • antonym: straight-shooter - someone who is honest and forthright
  • synonym: sycophant - a person who acts obsequiously toward someone important to gain advantage
