clerking

noun
activity of recording business transactions
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈklɜrkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklɑːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of recording business transactions, especially as part of an office or administrative job

Examples

Clerking office work
His job mainly involves clerking and maintaining company records.
She spent most of her summer clerking at her uncle's law firm.
With the increase in digital systems, traditional clerking methods are becoming less common.
