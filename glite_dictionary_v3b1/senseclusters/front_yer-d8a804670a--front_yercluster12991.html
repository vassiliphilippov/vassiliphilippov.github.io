front yer

noun
historical Cyrillic letter representing reduced front vowel
9.9
Pronunciation: /frʌnt jɜːr/ /; / /frʌnt jʌr/ (AmE), /frʌnt jɜː/ /; / /frʌnt jeə/ (BrE)
The Cyrillic letter Ь/ь, historically used to represent a short front vowel in Old Church Slavonic and now indicating a palatalized consonant in several Slavic languages.
