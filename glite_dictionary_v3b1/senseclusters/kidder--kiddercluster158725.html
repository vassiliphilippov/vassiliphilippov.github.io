kidder

noun
young goat herder
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪdər / (AmE), / ˈkɪdə / (BrE)
a person who tends or looks after young goats, especially in an archaic or regional context

Examples

A young goat kidder
The village kidder led the young goats to the hillside every morning.
In olden times, a kidder was responsible for keeping the kids safe from predators.
The farmer's son became the family's kidder during the spring kidding season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goatherd - a person who tends goats
  • synonym: shepherd - a person who tends sheep
