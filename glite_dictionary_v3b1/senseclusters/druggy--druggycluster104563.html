druggy

noun
slang for drug user
Pronunciation: / ˈdrʌɡi / (AmE), / ˈdrʌɡi / (BrE)
a slang term for a person who frequently uses illegal drugs

Examples

A druggy teen
The school organized a program to help druggy teens find healthier ways to cope.
He was labeled a druggy because of his reputation in the neighborhood.
Some people unfairly judge anyone with tattoos as a druggy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: addict - a person who is dependent on a substance, especially drugs
  • antonym: teetotaler - a person who never uses alcohol or drugs
  • synonym: user - a person who uses illegal drugs
