screw bean

noun
the twisted pod of the screwbean mesquite
Pronunciation: / skruː biːn / (AmE), / skruː biːn / (BrE)
the spirally twisted, sweet-tasting pod of the screwbean mesquite, used as animal fodder or ground into meal for feed

Examples

Screw bean pod
Locals would collect screw bean and grind it into meal for making porridge.
The cattle were fed screw bean pods during the dry season to supplement their diet.
The twisted shape of the screw bean pod makes it easily recognizable among other mesquite species.
