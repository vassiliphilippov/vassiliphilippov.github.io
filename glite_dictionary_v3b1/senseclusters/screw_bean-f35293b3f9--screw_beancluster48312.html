screw bean

noun
the twisted pod of the screwbean mesquite
Pronunciation: / skruː biːn / (AmE), / skruː biːn / (BrE)
the spirally twisted, sweet-tasting pod of the screwbean mesquite, used as animal fodder or ground into meal for feed
