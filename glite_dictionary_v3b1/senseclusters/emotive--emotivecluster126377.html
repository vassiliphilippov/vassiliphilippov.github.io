emotive

adjective
causing or expressing strong emotion
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmoʊtɪv / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊtɪv / (BrE)
causing or characterized by strong emotion; arousing or expressing emotion

Examples

An emotive speech
His emotive speech moved many in the audience to tears.
She used emotive language to persuade the listeners.
The film is particularly emotive, tackling complex issues with sensitivity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emotional - characterized by or showing emotion
  • antonym: unemotional - not showing or affected by emotion
