sweet flag

noun
perennial marsh plant with aromatic roots
Pronunciation: / swit flæɡ / (AmE), / swiːt flaɡ / (BrE)
A perennial marsh plant (Acorus calamus) with sword-shaped leaves and aromatic roots, often found in wetlands.
