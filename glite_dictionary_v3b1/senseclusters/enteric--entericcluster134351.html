enteric

adjective
of or relating to the intestines
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈtɛrɪk /; / ɪnˈtɛrɪk / (AmE), / ɛnˈtɛrɪk /; / ɪnˈtɛrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or occurring in the intestines; intestinal

Examples

Enteric diseases
Enteric diseases are common in areas with poor sanitation.

The doctor described it as an enteric infection caused by contaminated food.
Certain vaccines protect against enteric diseases such as typhoid and cholera.
Research focuses on the enteric nervous system and its role in digestive health.
The patient was diagnosed with an enteric infection after complaining of severe abdominal pain.
