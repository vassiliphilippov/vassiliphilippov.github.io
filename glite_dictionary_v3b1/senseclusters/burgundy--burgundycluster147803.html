burgundy

noun
dark purplish-red color
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːɡəndi / (AmE), / ˈbɜːɡəndi / (BrE)
a dark purplish-red or blackish-red color, named after the wine of the Burgundy region in France

Examples

Dark burgundy color
His tie added a touch of burgundy to his otherwise plain suit.
She wore a stunning dress in burgundy to the gala.
The living room walls were painted a rich shade of burgundy.
