yellow goatfish

noun
a goatfish species with a yellow stripe
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊ ˈɡoʊtˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈɡəʊtˌfɪʃ / (BrE)

The yellow goatfish is easily recognized by the bright stripe running along its side.
