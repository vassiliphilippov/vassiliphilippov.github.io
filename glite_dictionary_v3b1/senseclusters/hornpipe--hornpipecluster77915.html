hornpipe

noun
a British dance performed by sailors
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrnˌpaɪp / (AmE), / ˈhɔːnˌpaɪp / (BrE)
a traditional British solo dance, often associated with sailors, characterized by lively steps and distinctive rhythms

Examples

Sailor hornpipe dance
During the festival, children competed in a lively hornpipe contest.
Many folk dance groups include the hornpipe in their repertoire of traditional dances.
The sailor expertly performed a hornpipe on the deck, to the delight of the crew.
