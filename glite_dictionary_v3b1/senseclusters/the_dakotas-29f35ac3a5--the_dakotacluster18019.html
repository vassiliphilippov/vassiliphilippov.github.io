the Dakotas

proper noun
North and South Dakota
Pronunciation: / ðə dəˈkoʊtəz /; / ðə dəˈkoʊdəz / (AmE), / ðə dəˈkəʊtəz / (BrE)
the two U.S. states of North Dakota and South Dakota, sometimes also referring to the region that was once the Dakota Territory
