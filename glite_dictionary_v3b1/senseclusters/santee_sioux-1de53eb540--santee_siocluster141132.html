Santee Sioux

noun
member of the eastern branch of the Sioux people
Pronunciation: / ˌsænˈti ˈsu / (AmE), / ˌsænˈti ˈsjuː / (BrE)
a member of the eastern branch of the Sioux people, traditionally living in the central United States

Examples

Santee Sioux people
A delegation of Santee Sioux met with government officials to discuss historical treaties.
She is studying the traditions of the Santee Sioux for her research project.
The Santee Sioux once inhabited large areas in what is now Minnesota.
