Algerie

proper noun
French name for Algeria (country in Africa)
7.4
Pronunciation: / al.ʒeˈʁi / (AmE), / al.ʒeˈʁi / (BrE)
The French name for Algeria, a republic in northwestern Africa on the Mediterranean Sea.
