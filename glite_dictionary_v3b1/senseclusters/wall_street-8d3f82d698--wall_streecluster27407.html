Wall Street

noun
the US financial markets sector
2.7
Pronunciation: /wɔːl striːt/ (AmE), /wɔːl striːt/ (BrE)
the U.S. financial industry, financial markets, or the people and companies involved in them (especially in New York City)

Examples

Wall Street investors
Many investors keep a close eye on Wall Street to track the latest economic trends.
