critique

noun
critical evaluation essay or article
4.4
Pronunciation: / krɪˈtik / (AmE), / krɪˈtiːk / (BrE)
a detailed analysis and evaluation of something, especially a work of art, literature, or theory (often in writing or speech)

Examples

A critique of the book
After reading the critique, the playwright made changes to the script.
She wrote a thoughtful critique of the latest novel.
The film received a harsh critique in the newspaper.
After reading his film critique, I decided not to watch the movie.
Her critique of the proposal highlighted several important weaknesses.
The editor wrote a thorough critique of the new novel for the magazine.

Related Terms

  • antonym: praise - to express approval or admiration
  • synonym: review - an assessment or appraisal of a work, often less thorough than a critique
