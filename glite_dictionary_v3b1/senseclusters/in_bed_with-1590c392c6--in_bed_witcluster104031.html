Pronunciation: / ɪn bɛd wɪð / (AmE), / ɪn bɛd wɪð / (BrE)
colluding with or having a close association, especially one considered improper or secretive, with someone or an organization

Examples

In bed with the government
Critics argued that the media was in bed with the government, compromising its independence.
Many suspected the company was in bed with corrupt officials to win contracts.
The investigation revealed several executives had been in bed with the competition for years.
