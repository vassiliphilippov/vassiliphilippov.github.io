air attache

noun
military air force official at an embassy
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr ˌætəˈʃeɪ / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌatəˈʃeɪ / (BrE)
a military officer attached to a diplomatic mission who serves as an expert on air force matters

Examples

As air attache, she advised the ambassador on all matters relating to aviation security.
Several foreign air attaches attended the international air show as official observers.
The embassy's air attache coordinated joint air defense exercises with the host country.
