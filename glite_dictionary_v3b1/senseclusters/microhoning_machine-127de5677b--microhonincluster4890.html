microhoning machine

noun
precision machine for fine surface finishing
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.krə.hoʊ.nɪŋ mə.ˈʃiːn/ /; / /ˈmaɪ.kroʊ.hoʊ.nɪŋ mə.ˈʃiːn/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.krə.həʊ.nɪŋ mə.ˈʃiːn/ /; / /ˈmaɪ.krəʊ.həʊ.nɪŋ mə.ˈʃiːn/ (BrE)
a precision machine tool that uses an abrasive stone or tape to remove a thin amorphous surface layer from a workpiece, improving its surface finish and geometry

Examples

A microhoning machine tool
After initial grinding, the component was passed through a microhoning machine to remove any remaining surface irregularities.
Recent advancements in microhoning machine technology have increased precision in manufacturing small components.
The engineer operated the microhoning machine to achieve an exceptionally smooth finish on the metal part.
