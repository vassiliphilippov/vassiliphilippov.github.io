defogger

noun
image clarity tool
6.1
Pronunciation: / diˈfɑɡər / (AmE), / diˈfɒɡə / (BrE)
a software tool or digital filter that removes haze or fog from photographs or video to improve clarity

Examples

Photo defogger
The photo editing software includes a powerful defogger to enhance landscape images.
After applying the defogger, the mountains in the background became much clearer.
Many photographers use a defogger to improve visibility in misty or hazy scenes.
