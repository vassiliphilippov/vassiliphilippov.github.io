novaculite

noun
sharpening stone rock
9.9
Pronunciation: / noʊˈvækjəˌlaɪt / (AmE), / nəʊˈvækjʊˌlaɪt / (BrE)
a hard, fine-grained rock composed mainly of silica, used for sharpening tools and making whetstones

Examples

A piece of novaculite
The craftsman used a piece of novaculite to sharpen his woodworking tools.
Archaeologists discovered ancient blades made from novaculite at the excavation site.
Because of its hardness, novaculite is prized for making high-quality whetstones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sandstone - a sedimentary rock used for building and sharpening
  • synonym: whetstone - a stone used for sharpening cutting tools
