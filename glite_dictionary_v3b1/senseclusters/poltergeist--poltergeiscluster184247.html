poltergeist

noun
noisy ghost causing disturbances
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊl.tərˌɡaɪst / (AmE), / ˈpɒl.təˌɡaɪst / (BrE)
A ghost or supernatural being said to cause physical disturbances, such as loud noises and objects being moved or thrown around.

Examples

A poltergeist moves objects
Some people claim to have witnessed a poltergeist move objects across the room.
Stories about poltergeists have inspired countless horror movies and ghost tales.
The family believed a poltergeist was responsible for the mysterious banging noises in their house.
