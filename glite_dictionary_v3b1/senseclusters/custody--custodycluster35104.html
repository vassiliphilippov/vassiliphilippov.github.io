Synonyms

  • detention
    3.9 7/m
    detention is being held by police, often briefly while officers decide what to do, while custody is the state of such confinement.
  • detainment
    6.7 0.3/m
    detainment is the act of holding someone for a short time by authorities, while custody is the formal state of being kept by police.
  • confinement
    5.9 4/m
    confinement is being kept in a limited space, often physical or private, while custody is official detention by police or authorities.
  • incarceration
    8.0 4/m
    incarceration is being put in prison as punishment, while custody is being held by police and may be shorter or before trial.
  • imprisonment
    4.0 4/m
    imprisonment is serving time in prison as a legal punishment, while custody is being kept in police detention or short confinement.
  • captivity
    3.7 5/m
    captivity is being held against your will, often by enemies or animals, while custody is formal detention by authorities.
