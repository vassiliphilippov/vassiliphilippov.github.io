ovoflavin

noun
a B vitamin essential for health
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvoʊˈfleɪvɪn / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈfleɪvɪn / (BrE)
a B vitamin, also known as riboflavin, that is necessary for body growth and helps prevent skin lesions and weight loss

Examples

The ovoflavin vitamin
A deficiency in ovoflavin can lead to cracked lips and other skin problems.
Eggs are a good source of ovoflavin, which is important for metabolic health.
Some breakfast cereals are fortified with ovoflavin to help meet daily vitamin requirements.

  • synonym: vitamin B2 - another name for ovoflavin, a type of B vitamin
