Busycon

noun
large spiral sea snail
Pronunciation: / ˈbjuːzɪˌkɒn /; / ˈbɪzɪˌkɑn / (AmE), / ˈbjuːzɪˌkɒn /; / ˈbɪzɪˌkɒn / (BrE)
a large marine snail or whelk of the genus Busycon, known for its thick, spiral shell
