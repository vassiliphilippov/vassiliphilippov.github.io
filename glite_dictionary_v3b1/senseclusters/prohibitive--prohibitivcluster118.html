Examples

A prohibitive price
Many medicines remain prohibitive in price for people without insurance.

Synonyms

  • prohibitory
    7.9 0.05/m
    prohibitory is strict or meant to stop actions, while prohibitive usually describes a very high price that prevents people from buying.
