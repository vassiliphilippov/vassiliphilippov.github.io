unfaced

adjective
without facial features
8.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfeɪst / (AmE), / ʌnˈfeɪst / (BrE)
lacking a face, facial features, or a distinct front surface; blank or featureless.

Examples

Unfaced statue
The statue in the garden was unfaced, giving it a mysterious and haunting appearance.
After years of erosion, the once detailed carving became completely unfaced.
The artist intentionally left the portrait unfaced to invite viewers to imagine their own expressions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blank - empty or lacking detail
  • antonym: detailed - having many features or details
  • synonym: featureless - having no distinct features or details
