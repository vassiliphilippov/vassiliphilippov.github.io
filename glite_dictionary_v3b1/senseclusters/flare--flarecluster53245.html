flare

noun
short football pass to a back moving sideline
6.3
Pronunciation: / flɛr / (AmE), / fleə / (BrE)
In American football, a short forward pass to a back who is moving toward the sidelines.

Examples

Flare pass play
During practice, they worked on timing the flare routes to improve quick passing.
The coach called for a flare late in the fourth quarter to catch the defense off guard.
The quarterback completed a flare to the running back, gaining five yards.
