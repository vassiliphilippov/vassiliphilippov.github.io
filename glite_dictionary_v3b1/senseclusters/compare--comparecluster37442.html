compare

verb
to be comparable in quality or status
4.0
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpɛr / (AmE), / kəmˈpɛə / (BrE)
to be as good as or similar in quality, amount, or status to someone or something else

Examples

Compare with the best
His recent work simply does not compare with his earlier masterpieces.
No other city compares to Paris for its romantic atmosphere.
Nothing in the restaurant compares with their signature dessert.
