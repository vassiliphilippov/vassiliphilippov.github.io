enfranchisement

noun
granting of citizenship or voting rights
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈfræn.tʃaɪz.mənt /; / ɪnˈfræn.tʃaɪz.mənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈfræn.tʃaɪz.mənt /; / ɪnˈfræn.tʃaɪzmənt / (BrE)
the act of giving someone the rights of citizenship, especially the right to vote

Related Terms

  • antonym: disenfranchisement - the act of depriving someone of the right to vote or other rights
  • synonym: emancipation - the act of being set free from legal, social, or political restrictions; liberation
  • synonym: suffrage - the right to vote in political elections
