sarcosomal

adjective
relating to sarcosomes
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɑrˈkoʊsəməl / (AmE), / sɑːˈkəʊsəməl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of sarcosomes, which are specialized mitochondria found in muscle cells.
