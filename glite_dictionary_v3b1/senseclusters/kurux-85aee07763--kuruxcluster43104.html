Kurux

noun
a Dravidian language of eastern India
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːruːʃ / (AmE), / ˈkuːrʊks / (BrE)
a Dravidian language spoken by the Kurukh people in eastern India, especially in Jharkhand, Chhattisgarh, and Odisha

Examples

Kurux language

The Kurux language is listed as vulnerable by UNESCO.
