Jimmy Hoffa

proper noun
notorious American labor leader and union president
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪmi ˈhɑfə / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪmi ˈhɒfə / (BrE)
(1913–1975) An American labor leader who served as president of the Teamsters Union, known for his imprisonment for jury tampering and his mysterious disappearance, widely presumed murdered.

Examples

Jimmy Hoffa union leader
As president of the Teamsters Union, Jimmy Hoffa became one of America’s most powerful labor leaders.
Many books and films have explored the mysterious case of Jimmy Hoffa's disappearance.
The fate of Jimmy Hoffa remains one of the great unsolved mysteries in American history.
