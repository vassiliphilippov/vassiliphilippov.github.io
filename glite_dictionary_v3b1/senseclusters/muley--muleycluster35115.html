muley

noun
hornless animal
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːli / (AmE), / ˈmjuːli / (BrE)
a hornless animal, especially a male deer or cow without antlers or horns

Examples

A muley cow
The rancher pointed out a muley among the herd of cattle in the field.
Hunters sometimes prefer to track a muley because it stands out from other deer.
We saw a muley grazing peacefully near the edge of the forest.

Related Terms

  • antonym: antlered - having antlers or horns
  • synonym: polled - naturally hornless, especially in cattle
