nonmalignant tumor

noun
tumor that is not cancerous
Pronunciation: / nɑn.məˈlɪɡ.nənt ˈtu.mɚ /; / ˌnɑn.məˈlɪɡ.nənt ˈtuː.mɚ / (AmE), / nɒn.məˈlɪɡ.nənt ˈtjuː.mə /; / ˌnɒn.məˈlɪɡ.nənt ˈtjuː.mə / (BrE)
A tumor that is not cancerous and does not spread to other parts of the body.

Examples
