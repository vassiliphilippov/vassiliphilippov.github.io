stock-take

noun
instance of inventory checking
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑkˌteɪk / (AmE), / ˈstɒkˌteɪk / (BrE)
an instance or act of counting and checking a store's inventory of goods

Examples

Annual stock-take
During the stock-take, several discrepancies were discovered in the inventory.
The company conducts a stock-take every quarter to ensure accurate records.
We prepared for the annual stock-take by organizing the warehouse shelves.
