lechuguilla

noun
desert spiky plant
Pronunciation: / ˌle.tʃuˈɡi.jə / (AmE), / ˌle.tʃuˈɡiː.jə / (BrE)
a small, spiky plant native to desert regions of North America, especially the Chihuahuan Desert

Examples

The lechuguilla plant
The lechuguilla is well adapted to survive in the harsh conditions of the Chihuahuan Desert.
Researchers studied the roots of the lechuguilla to understand how it conserves water.
Hikers are often careful to avoid the sharp leaves of the lechuguilla while walking in the desert.
