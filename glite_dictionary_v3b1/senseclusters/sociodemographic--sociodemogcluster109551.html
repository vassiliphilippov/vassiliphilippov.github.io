sociodemographic

adjective
social and demographic
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊsiˌoʊˌdɛməˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌsəʊsiəʊˌdɛməˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to both social and demographic characteristics of a population or group

Examples

Sociodemographic characteristics
The study examined the sociodemographic backgrounds of the survey participants.
Understanding sociodemographic factors can help improve public health strategies.
The report highlights sociodemographic differences between urban and rural communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demographic - relating to the structure of populations
  • synonym: socioeconomic - relating to social and economic factors
