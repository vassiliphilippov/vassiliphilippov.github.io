gun dog

noun
dog trained to assist hunters
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡʌn dɑɡ / (AmE), / ɡʌn dɒɡ / (BrE)
A dog that is specially trained to assist hunters by retrieving game or flushing birds when hunting with guns.

Examples

A gun dog retrieves
Her family's gun dog is skilled at flushing pheasants from the undergrowth.
Labradors and spaniels are often trained as gun dogs for hunting expeditions.
The hunter brought his faithful gun dog along to help retrieve the birds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: retriever - a breed of gun dog trained to retrieve shot game
