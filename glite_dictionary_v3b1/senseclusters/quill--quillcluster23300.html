quill

verb
to write with a quill
Pronunciation: / kwɪl / (AmE), / kwɪl / (BrE)
to write or produce something using a quill pen, especially by hand.

Examples

To quill a letter
The poet quilled several verses by candlelight before going to bed.
She carefully quilled her signature at the bottom of the letter.
In the old library, scholars quilled manuscripts for future generations to read.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pen - to write or compose with a pen
  • antonym: type - to write using a typewriter or keyboard
