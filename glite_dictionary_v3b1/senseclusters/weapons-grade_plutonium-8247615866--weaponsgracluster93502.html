weapons-grade plutonium

noun
plutonium suitable for nuclear weapons
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛpənz ɡreɪd pluˈtoʊniəm / (AmE), / ˈwɛpənz ɡreɪd pluːˈtəʊniəm / (BrE)
Plutonium, primarily consisting of isotope 239, of high purity and suitable for use in nuclear weapons.

Examples

Weapons-grade plutonium isotope
Recycling weapons-grade plutonium reduces the risk of nuclear proliferation.
Scientists studied the properties of weapons-grade plutonium for use in atomic research.
The country has strict controls over its stockpile of weapons-grade plutonium.
