approval

noun
acceptance or agreement
1.0 1.5
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈpruː.vəl/ (AmE), /ə.ˈpruː.vəl/ (BrE)
official agreement or permission to do something

Examples

Official approval needed
She waited anxiously for her parents' approval of her decision.
The plan received approval from the city council last night.
The product was sent to customers on approval so they could decide if they wanted to keep it.
She waited anxiously for her manager's approval before proceeding with the plan.
The committee granted its approval to the new policy last night.
Without parental approval, you cannot participate in the trip.
