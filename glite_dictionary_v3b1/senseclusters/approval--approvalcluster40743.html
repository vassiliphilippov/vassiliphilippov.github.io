Examples

Official approval needed
She waited anxiously for her parents' approval of her decision.
The plan received approval from the city council last night.
The product was sent to customers on approval so they could decide if they wanted to keep it.

Synonyms

  • authorization

  • consent
    3.2 20/m
    consent is a person's explicit agreement often required in situations like medical choices, while approval is the official permission to proceed.
  • sanction
    5.7 2/m

  • seal of approval
    3.9 0.8/m

  • blessing
