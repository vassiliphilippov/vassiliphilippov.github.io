pattern

noun
template or model for making something
Pronunciation: / ˈpætərn / (AmE), / ˈpætən / (BrE)
a model, template, or example used as a guide or standard for making or doing something

Examples

A dress pattern
She used a pattern to cut the fabric for the new dress.
The carpenter followed the pattern to make identical chair legs.
This pattern is perfect for beginners learning how to sew.
His leadership style became a pattern for future generations of managers.
She used her mentor's career as a pattern to shape her own choices.
The school adopted a successful curriculum as a pattern for its own program.
He is considered the pattern of good behavior in the classroom.
The book became a pattern for later detective novels.
Their organization set a new pattern for customer service in the industry.

  • synonym: model - something or someone that serves as an example to be copied
