Eucalyptus australis

noun
Australian eucalyptus species
8.3
Pronunciation: / juːˈkliːptəs ɔːˈstreɪlɪs / (AmE), / juːˈkliːptəs ɔːˈstreɪlɪs / (BrE)
a species of eucalyptus tree native to Australia, known for its aromatic leaves and adaptability
