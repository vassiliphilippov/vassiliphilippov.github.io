Katsushika Hokusai

proper noun
famous Japanese painter and printmaker
8.8
Pronunciation: / kɑːtsuːˈʃiːkə hoʊˈkuːsaɪ / (AmE), / kat.suːˈʃiː.kə həʊˈkuː.saɪ / (BrE)
(1760–1849) A renowned Japanese painter and printmaker, best known for his woodblock print series 'Thirty-Six Views of Mount Fuji', which influenced Western art and the Impressionists.
