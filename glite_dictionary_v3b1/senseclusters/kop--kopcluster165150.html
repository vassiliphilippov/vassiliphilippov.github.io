kop

noun
stadium supporters' section
9.1
Pronunciation: / kɑp / (AmE), / kɒp / (BrE)
a section or terrace in a sports stadium, especially in the UK, where passionate supporters gather to watch and cheer for their team

Examples

The kop crowd
The crowd in the kop erupted in cheers when their team scored the winning goal.
Many lifelong fans prefer to watch matches from the kop for its lively atmosphere.
Songs and chants from the kop can be heard throughout the entire stadium during big games.
The fans packed the kop to support their team during the crucial match.
Chants echoed from the kop as the players entered the field.
He always tries to get a spot on the kop for the best atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • antonym: grandstand - a large covered seating area at a sports stadium
  • synonym: stand - a section of seats or standing area in a stadium
  • synonym: terrace - a stepped area in a stadium for standing spectators
