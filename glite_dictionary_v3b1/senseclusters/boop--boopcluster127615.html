boop

noun
playful light touch
8.3
Pronunciation: /buːp/ (AmE), /buːp/ (BrE)
a light, playful touch, especially on the nose or cheek, often done affectionately

Examples

A gentle boop on the nose
She gave her dog a gentle boop on the nose to make him smile.
With a quick boop, he touched his friend's cheek and laughed.
The baby giggled every time her mother gave her a little boop on the forehead.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tap - a light touch or hit, usually with a finger
