under the circumstances

adverb
in view of the current situation
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚ ðə ˈsɝː.kəmˌstænsɪz / (AmE), / ˈʌndə ðə ˈsɜː.kəmˌstɑːnsɪz / (BrE)

Examples
