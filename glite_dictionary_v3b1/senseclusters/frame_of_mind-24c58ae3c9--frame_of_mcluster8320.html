frame of mind

noun
temporary mental or emotional state
7.0
Pronunciation: / freɪm əv maɪnd / (AmE), / freɪm əv maɪnd / (BrE)
a person's temporary mental or emotional state; the mood someone is in at a particular time

Examples

A frame of mind change
After the argument, he wasn't in any frame of mind to continue the conversation.
Her positive frame of mind helped her overcome many challenges at work.
You need to be in the right frame of mind before making such an important decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mood - a temporary state of mind or feeling
  • synonym: temper - a person's state of mind seen in terms of their being angry or calm
