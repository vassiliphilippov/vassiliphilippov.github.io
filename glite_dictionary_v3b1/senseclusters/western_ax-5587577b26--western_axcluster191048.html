Western ax

noun
ax with two cutting edges
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛstərn æks / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən æks / (BrE)
a type of ax with cutting edges on both sides of the head, commonly associated with Western styles of toolmaking.
