toroidal

adjective
doughnut-shaped; shaped like a torus
Pronunciation: / tɔˈrɔɪ.dəl / (AmE), / tɔːˈrɔɪ.dəl / (BrE)
having the shape of a torus; doughnut-shaped.

Examples

Toroidal shape
Donuts are classic examples of toroidal geometry.
Some lifebuoys are designed with a nearly toroidal cross-section to keep them afloat.
The magnetic field in a fusion reactor is often toroidal in shape.

Related Terms

  • synonym: torus - a surface or solid shaped like a ring or doughnut
