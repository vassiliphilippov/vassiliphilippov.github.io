concert dance

noun
stage dance performance as art
5.1 4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːn.sərt dæns/ (AmE), /ˈkɒn.sət dɑːns/ (BrE)
A style of dance performed on stage as a theatrical art form, usually choreographed and presented to an audience, often as ballet or modern dance.
