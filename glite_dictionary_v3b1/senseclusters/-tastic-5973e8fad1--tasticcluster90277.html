-tastic

x
humorous intensifying suffix
8.8
Pronunciation: / tæstɪk / (AmE), / tæstɪk / (BrE)
an informal and humorous suffix added to words to mean 'extremely', 'full of', or 'excellent' in relation to that word, often for emphasis or exaggeration

Examples

A -tastic ending
Her new dress is absolutely fashion-tastic and caught everyone's attention at the party.
The movie was action-tastic, with nonstop car chases and explosions.
That pizza was cheese-tastic, with more cheese than I've ever seen before.
Her new dress was absolutely sparkle-tastic and caught everyone's attention at the party.
The movie was action-tastic, with nonstop excitement from start to finish.
He made a cheese-tastic pizza loaded with four different kinds of cheese.
Her birthday cake was absolutely choco-tastic and everyone loved it.
The team's performance was super-tastic in the final match.
We had a movie-tastic night with popcorn and friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: -ful - a suffix meaning 'full of' or 'characterized by'
  • synonym: -licious - a playful suffix meaning 'delicious' or 'very appealing'
  • synonym: fantastic - excellent or wonderful
