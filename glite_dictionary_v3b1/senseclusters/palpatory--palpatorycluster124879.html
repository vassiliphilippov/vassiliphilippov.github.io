palpatory

adjective
relating to medical examination by touch
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpæl.pəˌtɔr.i / (AmE), / ˈpælpəˌtəri / (BrE)
relating to or involving palpation, especially as a method of medical examination by touch

Examples

Palpatory medical examination

The doctor performed a palpatory assessment of the abdomen to detect any tenderness.
