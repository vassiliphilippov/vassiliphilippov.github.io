beatdown

noun
intense workout
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈbiːtˌdaʊn / (BrE)
an intense and exhausting physical training session or workout that leaves participants very tired

Examples

A gym beatdown
Yesterday's beatdown at the gym pushed everyone to their physical limits.
After the coach's beatdown, the team could barely walk out of the locker room.
He signed up for a Saturday morning beatdown to challenge himself and improve his endurance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: training - the process of practicing physical exercises to improve fitness
  • synonym: workout - a session of physical exercise
