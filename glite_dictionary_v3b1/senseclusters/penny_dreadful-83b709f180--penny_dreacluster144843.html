penny dreadful

noun
cheap sensational storybook (19th-century Britain)
6.0 7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈpe.nɪ ˈdred.fəl/ /; / /ˈpe.nɪ ˈdred.fʊl/ (AmE), /ˈpe.nɪ ˈdred.fəl/ /; / /ˈpe.nɪ ˈdred.fʊl/ (BrE)
a cheaply produced and sensational storybook, especially one published in 19th-century Britain, featuring lurid and melodramatic tales intended to entertain the masses
