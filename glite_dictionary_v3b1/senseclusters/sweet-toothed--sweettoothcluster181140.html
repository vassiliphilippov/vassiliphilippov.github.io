sweet-toothed

adjective
fond of sweet foods
Pronunciation: / ˈswit tuːθt / (AmE), / ˈswiːt tuːθt / (BrE)
having a strong liking for sweet foods or flavors

Examples

A sweet-toothed person
If you’re sweet-toothed, you might enjoy baking your own desserts.
My brother is sweet-toothed and always asks for ice cream after dinner.
She’s so sweet-toothed that she keeps a jar of candy on her desk.

Related Terms

  • antonym: savory-toothed - having a preference for savory foods (contrast)
  • synonym: sugar-loving - having a liking for sugary foods
