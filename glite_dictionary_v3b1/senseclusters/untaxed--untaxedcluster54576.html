untaxed

adjective
not subject to taxation
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtækst / (AmE), / ʌnˈtækst / (BrE)
(of goods, funds, or income) not subject to taxation; not taxed.

Examples

Untaxed income
Charitable donations may sometimes be treated as untaxed by the government.
He purchased untaxed goods at a duty-free shop.
The company benefited from untaxed income earned abroad.

Related Terms

  • antonym: taxed - having had a tax imposed
