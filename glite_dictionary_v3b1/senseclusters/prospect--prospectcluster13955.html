prospect

noun
a wide or scenic view
3.9 2.06/m
Pronunciation: / Ààpr…ëÀêspekt / (AmE), / Ààpr…íspekt / (BrE)
a view or scene, especially of a wide area or landscape

Examples

A beautiful prospect
From the balcony, we enjoyed the prospect of the distant hills and lake.
The hotel was chosen for its excellent prospect across the city skyline.
The mountain cabin offered a breathtaking prospect of the valley below.
