career

noun
a person's main occupation or profession
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɪr / (AmE), / kəˈrɪə / (BrE)
the job, profession, or series of jobs that a person does during their working life

Examples

A long career
He dedicated his entire career to public service and education.
She hopes to build a successful career in medicine after graduating from university.
Switching career paths in your thirties can be both challenging and rewarding.
After a twenty-year career as a teacher, he decided to retire.
She hopes to have a successful career in medicine.
The award recognizes his outstanding career in scientific research.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hobby - an activity done regularly in one's leisure time for pleasure
  • synonym: job - a paid position of regular employment
  • synonym: profession - a type of job that requires special education or training
  • synonym: vocation - a strong feeling of suitability for a particular career or occupation
