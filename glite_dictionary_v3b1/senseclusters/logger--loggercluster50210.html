logger

noun
person who cuts down trees
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔːɡər / (AmE), / ˈlɒɡə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to cut down trees for timber

Examples

A logger cuts trees
As a skilled logger, she could identify the best trees to cut.
Many rural communities depend on loggers for local employment.
The logger spent months deep in the forest harvesting timber.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lumberjack - a person whose job is cutting down trees and transporting the timber
