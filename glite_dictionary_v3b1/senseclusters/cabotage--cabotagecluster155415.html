cabotage

noun
navigation or transport within a country's borders
Pronunciation: / ˈkæb.əˌtɑʒ /; / ˈkæbətɑʒ / (AmE), / ˈkab.ə.tɑːʒ /; / ˈkabətɑːʒ / (BrE)
the right or practice of operating or regulating transport (such as ships or aircraft) within a country's borders, usually excluding foreign operators
