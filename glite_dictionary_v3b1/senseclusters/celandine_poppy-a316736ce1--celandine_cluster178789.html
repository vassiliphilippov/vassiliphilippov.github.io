celandine poppy

noun
North American yellow-flowered woodland herb
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛlənˌdaɪn ˈpɑpi / (AmE), / ˈsɛləndiːn ˈpɒpi / (BrE)
A perennial herbaceous plant native to woodlands of eastern North America, with yellow flowers and lobed leaves.
