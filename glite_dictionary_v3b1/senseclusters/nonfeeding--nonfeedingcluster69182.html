nonfeeding

adjective
not eating
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈfiːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈfiːdɪŋ / (BrE)
not eating or not consuming food during a particular period or stage.

Examples

Nonfeeding period
During hibernation, the bear enters a nonfeeding state to conserve energy.
The insect's larval stage is nonfeeding and lasts for several weeks.
Doctors monitored the patient's nonfeeding period to ensure proper hydration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fasting - abstaining from all or some kinds of food or drink
  • antonym: feeding - eating or consuming food
