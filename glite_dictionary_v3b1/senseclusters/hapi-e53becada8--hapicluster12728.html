Hapi

proper noun
Egyptian river god
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːpi / (AmE), / ˈhɑːpi / (BrE)
the ancient Egyptian god associated with the annual flooding of the Nile, bringing fertility to the land

Examples

Hapi floods Nile
Ancient Egyptians prayed to Hapi to ensure the Nile would flood and nourish their crops.
Statues of Hapi were often placed near the riverbanks as offerings for a good harvest.
The annual festival honoring Hapi celebrated the life-giving waters of the Nile.

Related Terms
