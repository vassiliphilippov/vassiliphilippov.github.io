resiliently

adverb
with quick recovery
4.5
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈzɪl.jənt.liː/ /; / /rɪ.ˈzɪl.ɪənt.liː/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈzɪl.jənt.liː/ /; / /rɪ.ˈzɪl.ɪənt.liː/ (BrE)
in a manner that shows the ability to recover quickly from difficulties or setbacks

Examples

She acts resiliently
Despite the sudden loss, she handled the situation resiliently and continued with her work.
The community rebuilt resiliently after the devastating storm destroyed many homes.
He faced criticism resiliently, refusing to let it affect his confidence.
