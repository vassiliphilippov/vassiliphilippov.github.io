oligarchically

adverb
in an oligarchic way
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːlɪˈɡɑːrkɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɒlɪˈɡɑːkɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to or is characteristic of an oligarchy, where power is held by a small group.

Examples

Govern oligarchically
The nation was governed oligarchically, with decisions made by only a handful of powerful families.
Critics argued that the company was run oligarchically, limiting input from most employees.
Some believe the organization operates oligarchically, concentrating authority among a select few.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autocratically - in a manner relating to rule by one person with absolute power
  • antonym: democratically - in a manner relating to rule by the people or majority
