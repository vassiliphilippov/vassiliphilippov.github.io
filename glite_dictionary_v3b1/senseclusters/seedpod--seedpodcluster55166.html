seedpod

noun
pod containing seeds, often splitting open
2.2 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈsiːd.pɑːd/ (AmE), /ˈsiːd.pɒd/ (BrE)
a pod containing seeds, especially the fruit of leguminous plants that splits open when ripe to release the seeds

Examples

A seedpod splits open
Several birds were feeding on the seedpods that had fallen to the ground.
The peas are harvested once the seedpod has dried on the plant.
When the seedpod bursts open, it scatters seeds across the soil.
