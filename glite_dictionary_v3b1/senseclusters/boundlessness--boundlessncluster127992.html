boundlessness

noun
state of having no limits
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊnd.ləs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈbaʊnd.ləs.nəs / (BrE)
The state or quality of having no bounds or limits; infiniteness.

Examples

The boundlessness of space
Children are often drawn to the boundlessness of their imaginations.
She admired his boundlessness when it came to creative ideas.
The boundlessness of the universe has fascinated scientists for centuries.

Related Terms

  • antonym: finiteness - the state or quality of having bounds or limits
  • synonym: infinity - the state of being limitless; endlessness
