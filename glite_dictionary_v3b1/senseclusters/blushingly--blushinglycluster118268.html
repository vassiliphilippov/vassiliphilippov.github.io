blushingly

adverb
with embarrassment
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌʃɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈblʌʃɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows embarrassment or shyness, often with a red face

Examples

He blushingly admitted
He blushingly admitted that he had forgotten her birthday.
The student answered the question blushingly after being called on in class.
She blushingly accepted the award in front of the large audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confidently - in a way that shows confidence or self-assurance
  • synonym: shyly - in a way that shows shyness or timidity
