saraband

noun
slow, stately dance or its music
Pronunciation: / ˈsær.əˌbænd /; / ˈsærəˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈsær.əˌbænd /; / ˈsærəbænd / (BrE)
a slow, stately dance in triple time from the 17th and 18th centuries, or the music composed for such a dance
