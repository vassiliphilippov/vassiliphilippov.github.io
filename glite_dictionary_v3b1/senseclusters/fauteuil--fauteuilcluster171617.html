fauteuil

noun
upholstered French-style armchair
8.4
Pronunciation: / foʊˈtɜɪl / (AmE), / fəʊˈtɜːj / (BrE)
An upholstered armchair, especially one of a style originating in France with open sides and a wooden frame.

Examples

An antique fauteuil
She sat reading in an antique fauteuil near the window.
The living room featured a beautiful fauteuil covered in silk fabric.
The ornate fauteuil complemented the elegant décor of the drawing room.
