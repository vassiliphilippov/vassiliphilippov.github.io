immunization

noun
process of making immune, usually by vaccine
6.7 3.1
Pronunciation: /ɪ.mjuː.nə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ɪ.mjuː.nɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɪ.mjuː.nɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ɪ.mjuː.nə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of making someone immune to a disease, especially by introducing a vaccine or similar agent
