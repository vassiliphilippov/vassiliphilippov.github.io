Synonyms

  • rebirth
    7.2 0.70/m
    rebirth is the general belief that a soul starts a new human life after death, while reincarnation names that idea in religions like Hinduism and Buddhism.
  • transmigration
    9.9 0.21/m
    transmigration is the idea of a soul passing into another body, sometimes animal, while reincarnation often refers to rebirth in human lives.
  • metempsychosis
    9.9 0.04/m
    metempsychosis is an old or technical term for a soul moving into another body, while reincarnation is the common religious word for rebirth.
