uncontested

adjective
not disputed or challenged
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.kənˈtɛst.ɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kənˈtɛs.tɪd / (BrE)
Not disputed, opposed, or challenged; accepted without question or competition.

Examples

An uncontested victory
He inherited the land through uncontested succession, facing no opposition from his relatives.
Her victory in the race was uncontested, as no one else registered to compete.
The CEO held uncontested authority over all major decisions in the company.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contested - disputed or opposed
  • synonym: undisputed - not disputed or doubted
