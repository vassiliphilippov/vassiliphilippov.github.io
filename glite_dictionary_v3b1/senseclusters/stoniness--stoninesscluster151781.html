stoniness

noun
hardness or coldness
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstoʊninəs / (AmE), / ˈstəʊninəs / (BrE)
the quality of being like stone, especially in hardness, coldness, or lack of emotion

Examples

Stoniness in her voice
Her voice had a certain stoniness that made her seem unapproachable.
The stoniness of the landscape made it difficult for plants to grow.
He answered the question with stoniness, showing no sign of emotion.
