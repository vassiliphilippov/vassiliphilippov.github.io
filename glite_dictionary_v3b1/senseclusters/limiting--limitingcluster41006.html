limiting

adjective
restrictive; placing a constraint
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪmɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɪmɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
restricting the extent, freedom, or possibilities of someone or something; placing a constraint or boundary

Examples

Limiting rules
A limiting belief can prevent you from achieving your goals.
Some people find uniforms limiting because they restrict personal expression.
The company found the new regulations to be very limiting for its business operations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: permissive - allowing a great deal of freedom
  • synonym: restrictive - imposing restrictions or limitations
