noninvolvement

noun
lack of participation
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnɪnˈvɑlvmənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪnˈvɒlvmənt / (BrE)
the state of not taking part in or being connected with something

Examples

Government noninvolvement
The government maintained its noninvolvement in the neighboring country's civil war.
Her strict noninvolvement in office politics helped her focus on her work.
The club's policy of noninvolvement in controversial issues kept its reputation neutral.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detachment - the state of being uninvolved or emotionally distant
  • antonym: involvement - the act of being engaged or taking part in something
  • antonym: participation - the act of taking part in something
