sewer slide

noun
dangerous or badly designed waterslide
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːɚ slaɪd / (AmE), / ˈsjuːə slaɪd / (BrE)
a waterslide that is dangerous or poorly designed, often perceived as unsafe or unpleasant

Examples

A sewer slide ride
After seeing the cracks and poor maintenance, no one wanted to go on the sewer slide.

We joked about the old, rusty waterslide, calling it the sewer slide because it looked so unsafe.
