furball

noun
hairball from animals
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝːˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈfɜːˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a ball of hair that forms in the stomach of an animal, especially a cat, and is coughed up

Examples

Cat furball
My cat left a furball on the living room rug.
It's common for cats to cough up a furball after grooming themselves.
The veterinarian explained how to help prevent furballs in long-haired cats.
