nebular

adjective
cloud-like or cloudy in appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛbjələr / (AmE), / ˈnɛbjʊlə / (BrE)
resembling a cloud; having a cloudy or indistinct appearance

Examples

Nebular glow
A faint, nebular glow surrounded the distant star in the telescope's view.
Mist rose from the lake in nebular formations at dawn.
The artist chose soft, nebular colors to create an ethereal sky in the painting.
