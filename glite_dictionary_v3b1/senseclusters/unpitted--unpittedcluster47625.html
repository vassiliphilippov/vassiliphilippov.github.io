unpitted

adjective
smooth, not pitted
5.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɪtɪd / (BrE)
not marked by pits, holes, or small depressions; having a smooth or intact surface

Examples

Unpitted surface
The ancient statue's surface remained unpitted despite centuries of exposure to the elements.
After cleaning, the metal appeared unpitted and free of any corrosion.
The glass was surprisingly unpitted after the hailstorm, with no visible damage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pitted - marked with small holes or depressions
  • synonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface
