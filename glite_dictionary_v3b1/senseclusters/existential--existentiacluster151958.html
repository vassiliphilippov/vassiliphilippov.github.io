existential

adjective
relating to existentialism or existence
Pronunciation: / ˌɛɡ.zɪˈstɛn.ʃəl / (AmE), / ˌɛɡ.zɪˈstɛn.ʃəl /; / ˌɛk.sɪˈstɛn.ʃəl / (BrE)
relating to existence, especially as a philosophical or psychological concept (such as questions about the meaning of life or individual freedom)
