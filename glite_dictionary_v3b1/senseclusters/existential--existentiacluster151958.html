existential

adjective
relating to existentialism or existence
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɛɡ.zɪˈstɛn.ʃəl / (AmE), / ˌɛɡ.zɪˈstɛn.ʃəl /; / ˌɛk.sɪˈstɛn.ʃəl / (BrE)
relating to existence, especially as a philosophical or psychological concept (such as questions about the meaning of life or individual freedom)

Related Terms

  • synonym: existentialism - a philosophical theory emphasizing individual existence, freedom, and choice
  • synonym: experiential - relating to experience, especially as opposed to theoretical knowledge
  • synonym: ontological - relating to the nature of being or existence
