staffing

noun
number of employees
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstæfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɑːfɪŋ / (BrE)
the arrangement or process of providing, assigning, or hiring employees for work in an organization

Examples

Staffing a company
The hospital is struggling with low staffing during the holiday season.
Proper staffing is essential to ensure customer satisfaction in busy restaurants.
Due to increased demand, the company reviewed its staffing needs for the upcoming year.
The company improved its staffing to ensure every department had enough workers during busy periods.
Proper staffing is essential for maintaining high-quality customer service in the hotel industry.
Due to budget cuts, the school had to reduce its staffing for the upcoming semester.
The hospital is facing staffing shortages due to increased patient numbers.
Good staffing is essential for providing quality customer service in any business.
Seasonal businesses often adjust their staffing levels throughout the year.

  • synonym: workforce - all the people employed in an organization or area
