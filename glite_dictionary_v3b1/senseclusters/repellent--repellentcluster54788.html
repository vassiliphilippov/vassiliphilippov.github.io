repellent

adjective
causing aversion or disgust
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpɛlənt / (AmE), / rɪˈpɛlənt /; / rɪˈpɛl.ənt / (BrE)
causing strong dislike or disgust; extremely unpleasant or offensive

Examples

Repellent behavior
Her repellent behavior ensured she had very few friends.
Many people found his arrogant attitude repellent and refused to work with him.
The smell coming from the trash bin was so repellent that everyone stayed away.
His repellent behavior made everyone avoid him at the party.
She found the smell of rotten eggs absolutely repellent.
The idea of cruelty to animals is deeply repellent to her.
