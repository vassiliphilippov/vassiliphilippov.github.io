Air National Guard

noun
U.S. Air Force reserve component
4.2
Pronunciation: /er ˈnæʃ.ə.nəl gɑːrd/ /; / /er ˈnæʃ.nəl gɑːrd/ (AmE), /eə ˈnæʃ.ə.nəl gɑːd/ /; / /eə ˈnæʃ.nəl gɑːd/ (BrE)
a reserve military force composed of civilian volunteers who support the United States Air Force, providing rapid mobilization during war and assistance during national emergencies

Examples
