orthotist

noun
brace specialist
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrθətɪst / (AmE), / ˈɔːθətɪst / (BrE)
a healthcare professional who designs, fits, and maintains orthopedic braces and supports for patients

Examples

The orthotist fits braces
The orthotist carefully measured the patient's leg before creating a custom brace.
After surgery, she visited an orthotist to be fitted with a supportive device.
Children with scoliosis often work with an orthotist to improve their posture.
