Smurf

noun
network attack type
9.9
Pronunciation: / smɝːf / (AmE), / smɜːf / (BrE)
a type of network attack that overwhelms a target with spoofed ICMP packets, causing a denial of service

Examples

Smurf attack online
The company's servers were brought down by a Smurf that generated massive network traffic.
Security experts recommend filtering ICMP traffic to prevent a Smurf from disrupting operations.
After the Smurf attack, the IT team implemented new firewall rules to protect the network.

Related Terms

  • synonym: DDoS attack - a distributed denial-of-service attack that disrupts network services
  • synonym: ping flood - a network attack using excessive ICMP echo requests
