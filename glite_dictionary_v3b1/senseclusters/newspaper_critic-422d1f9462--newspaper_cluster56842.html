newspaper critic

noun
journalist who reviews for a newspaper
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːzˌpeɪpər ˈkrɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpə ˈkrɪtɪk / (BrE)
