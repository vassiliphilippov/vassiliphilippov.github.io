visitation

noun
an affliction or catastrophe
8.6 6.5
Pronunciation: /vɪ.zɪ.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /vɪ.zɪ.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a disaster, catastrophe, or distressing event, especially seen as a punishment or supernatural occurrence

Examples

A tragic visitation
Many in the village believed the illness was a visitation brought on by their collective misdeeds.
The early settlers dreaded every visitation of locusts that could destroy their crops.
The town suffered a visitation of flooding after weeks of heavy rain.
Medieval chroniclers wrote about the visitation of the Black Death as divine retribution.
Superstitious people believed the epidemic was a visitation for the village's past sins.
The town was devastated by a visitation of floods that ruined crops and homes.

  • synonym: calamity - an event causing great and often sudden damage or distress
  • synonym: catastrophe - a sudden event causing great damage or suffering
  • synonym: tribulation - a cause of great trouble or suffering
