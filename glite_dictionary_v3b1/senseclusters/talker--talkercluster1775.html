talker

noun
a person who talks, often a lot
2.1 2.77/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt…îk…ö / (AmE), / Ààt…îÀêk…ô / (BrE)
a person who talks, especially one who talks a lot or who gives speeches in public

Examples

A talker in public
He is more of a talker than a doer, often making grand promises.
Lisa is a great talker and always keeps conversations lively at parties.
The talker at the conference captivated everyone with her stories.
