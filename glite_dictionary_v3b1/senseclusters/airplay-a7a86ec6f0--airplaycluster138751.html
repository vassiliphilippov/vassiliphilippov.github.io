AirPlay

noun
Apple streaming technology
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌpleɪ / (AmE), / ˈeəˌpleɪ / (BrE)
a wireless technology developed by Apple for streaming audio, video, and other media between compatible devices

Examples

AirPlay music to speakers
She used AirPlay to stream music from her phone to the speakers.
With AirPlay, you can mirror your iPad screen on your television easily.
The new speakers support AirPlay, making it simple to play music wirelessly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Bluetooth - a wireless technology for exchanging data over short distances
  • synonym: Chromecast - a Google device for streaming media to a TV
