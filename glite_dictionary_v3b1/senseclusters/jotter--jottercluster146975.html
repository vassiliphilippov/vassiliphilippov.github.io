jotter

noun
small notebook for notes
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑːtər / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒtə / (BrE)
a small notebook used for making brief or rough notes

Examples

A small jotter
A small jotter lay on the desk, filled with scribbled numbers and reminders.
He flipped through his jotter looking for yesterday's shopping list.
She always carries a jotter in her bag to write down ideas.
