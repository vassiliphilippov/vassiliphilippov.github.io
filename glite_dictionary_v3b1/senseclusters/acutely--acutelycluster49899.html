acutely

adverb
suddenly or sharply; with pointed quality
5.2 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / …ôÀàkjuÀêtli / (AmE), / …ôÀàkjuÀêtli / (BrE)
in a way that changes direction or degree suddenly and sharply; with a sharp or pointed quality

Examples

Acutely angled hat

The mountain path turns acutely to the right just beyond the old oak.
