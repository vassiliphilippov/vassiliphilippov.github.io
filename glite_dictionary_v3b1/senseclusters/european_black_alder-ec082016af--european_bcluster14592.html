European black alder

noun
European deciduous tree with dark bark and woody catkins
4.8
Pronunciation: / jʊˈroʊpiən blæk ˈɔldər / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən blæk ˈɔːldə / (BrE)
a medium-sized deciduous tree (Alnus glutinosa) native to Europe and western Asia, with dark bark, rounded leaves hairy beneath, and woody fruiting catkins

Examples

Wood from the European black alder is valued for its resistance to decay in water.
