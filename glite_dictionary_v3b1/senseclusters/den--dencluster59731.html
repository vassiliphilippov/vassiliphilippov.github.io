den

verb
to shelter in a den
4.2
Pronunciation: /den/ (AmE), /den/ (BrE)
(of an animal) to take shelter or live in a den, especially during certain seasons

Examples

Foxes den underground
During the harsh winter, foxes den underground to stay warm and safe.
Some animals den together in groups for added protection from predators.
The mother bear will den in a secluded cave until her cubs are ready to emerge.
During the harsh winter, the wolves denned deep in the forest for safety.
The mother fox dens with her kits to protect them from predators.
After a long day, the animals denned together in the burrow to rest.

  • synonym: hibernate - to spend the winter in a dormant state
  • antonym: roam - to move about or travel aimlessly
