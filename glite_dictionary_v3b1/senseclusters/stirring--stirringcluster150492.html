stirring

noun
beginning feeling
Pronunciation: / ˈstɜrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɜːrɪŋ / (BrE)
a beginning sign or feeling of an emotion, idea, or action starting to develop.

Examples

A stirring of hope
He felt the first stirrings of hope after hearing the good news.
The stirrings of rebellion could be sensed among the students.
There were stirrings of excitement in the crowd as the band took the stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hint - a slight or indirect indication or suggestion
  • antonym: outburst - a sudden strong expression of emotion
  • synonym: trace - a very small amount or sign of something
