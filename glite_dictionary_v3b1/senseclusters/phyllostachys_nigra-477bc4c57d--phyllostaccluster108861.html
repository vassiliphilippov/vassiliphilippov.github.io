Phyllostachys nigra

noun
species of bamboo with black stems
9.9
Pronunciation: / fɪˌlɒstəˈkɪs ˈnaɪɡrə / (AmE), / fɪˌlɒstəˈkɪs ˈnɪɡrə / (BrE)
A species of small bamboo with slender green stems that turn shiny black as they mature, commonly grown as an ornamental plant.

Examples

Phyllostachys nigra bamboo plant
After several years, the stems of Phyllostachys nigra turn from green to a deep black.
Many landscape designers use Phyllostachys nigra as a striking ornamental plant.
The glossy black culms of Phyllostachys nigra add dramatic contrast to the garden.
