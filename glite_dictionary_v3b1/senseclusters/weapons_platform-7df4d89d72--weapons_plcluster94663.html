weapons platform

noun
software or network for cyberattacks
7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈwep.ənz ˈplæt.fɔːrm/ (AmE), /ˈwep.ənz ˈplæt.fɔːm/ (BrE)
(in cybersecurity) software or a networked infrastructure used to host, coordinate, or deploy cyber weapons, automated exploits, or large-scale attacks.

Examples

Cyber weapons platform
Security researchers warned that the new botnet served as a weapons platform for automated cyberattacks.
The company sold access to its control servers, essentially renting a weapons platform to attackers.
Nation-states deploy sophisticated malware frameworks as a persistent weapons platform in espionage campaigns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: botnet - a network of compromised computers controlled to perform coordinated malicious actions
  • synonym: cyber weapon - software, tools, or exploits designed to damage, disrupt, or gain unauthorized access to computer systems
