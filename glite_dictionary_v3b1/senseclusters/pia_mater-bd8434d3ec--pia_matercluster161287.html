pia mater

noun
innermost membrane covering the brain and spinal cord
Pronunciation: / ˌpiːə ˈmeɪtər / (AmE), / ˌpiːə ˈmɑːtə / (BrE)
the delicate, highly vascular inner layer of the three membranes (meninges) covering the brain and spinal cord

Examples

The pia mater layer
Bleeding underneath the pia mater can cause serious neurological symptoms.
The pia mater closely adheres to the contours of the brain, following every groove and ridge.
The surgeon carefully separated the pia mater from the underlying brain tissue during the procedure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arachnoid mater - the middle of the three meninges covering the brain and spinal cord
  • synonym: dura mater - the tough outermost membrane of the three meninges
