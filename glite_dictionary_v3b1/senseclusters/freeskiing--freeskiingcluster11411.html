freeskiing

verb
to perform ski tricks
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːˌskiːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌskiːɪŋ / (BrE)
to ski in a style that involves performing tricks, jumps, or using terrain features for creativity and skill

Examples

To freeski in the park
He spent the afternoon freeskiing at the terrain park, practicing new tricks on the rails.
Many young athletes enjoy freeskiing because it allows them to express creativity on the slopes.
After learning the basics, she started freeskiing to challenge herself with jumps and spins.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alpine skiing - to ski down slopes in a traditional style without tricks
  • synonym: freestyle skiing - to ski while performing acrobatic tricks and jumps
