heating system

noun
system for warming buildings
1.0 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈhiː.tɪŋ ˈsɪs.təm/ /; / /ˈhiː.təŋ ˈsɪs.təm/ (AmE), /ˈhiː.tɪŋ ˈsɪs.təm/ /; / /ˈhiː.təŋ ˈsɪs.təm/ (BrE)
