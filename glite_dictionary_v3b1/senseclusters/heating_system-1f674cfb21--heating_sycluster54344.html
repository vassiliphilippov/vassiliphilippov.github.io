heating system

noun
system for warming buildings
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːtɪŋ ˌsɪstəm /; / ˈhidɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈhiːtɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
a system or apparatus that provides heat to a building or part of a building

Examples

Their apartment building uses a central heating system that keeps every unit warm in winter.
We decided to upgrade the old heating system to a more energy-efficient model.

Related Terms

  • antonym: air conditioning - a system for controlling temperature and humidity, usually cooling
  • synonym: boiler - a device for generating steam or hot water for heating
  • synonym: central heating - system where heat is provided from a single source, usually for the whole building
  • synonym: furnace - an enclosed apparatus for producing heat
