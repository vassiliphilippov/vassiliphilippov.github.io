Norway rat

noun
large brown domestic rat, major pest
4.9 4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔr.weɪ ræt / (AmE), / ˈnɔː.weɪ ræt / (BrE)
a large brownish-gray rat (Rattus norvegicus) commonly found in urban areas worldwide, known as a major domestic pest

Examples

Unlike the black rat, the Norway rat prefers to live at ground level or in burrows.
