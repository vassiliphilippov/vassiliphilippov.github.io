blossom

noun
flower, especially of a tree or showy plant
Pronunciation: / ˈblɑːsəm / (AmE), / ˈblɒsəm / (BrE)
a flower or mass of flowers, especially one of a fruit tree or other plant with showy or colorful parts

Examples

Apple tree blossom
A single white blossom appeared on the apple tree this morning.
The cherry trees are covered in pink blossom every spring.
The garden is full of the sweet scent of orange blossom in May.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flower - the reproductive structure in flowering plants; more general term
