blossom

noun
flower, especially of a tree or showy plant
1.0 7.08/m
Pronunciation: / Ààbl…ëÀês…ôm / (AmE), / Ààbl…ís…ôm / (BrE)
a flower or mass of flowers, especially one of a fruit tree or other plant with showy or colorful parts

Examples

Apple tree blossom
A single white blossom appeared on the apple tree this morning.
The cherry trees are covered in pink blossom every spring.
The garden is full of the sweet scent of orange blossom in May.

Synonyms

  • bloom
    1.0 6.64/m
    bloom is the plant's flower or its flowering time, while blossom refers to the showy flowers on fruit trees.
  • flower
    1.0 75/m
    flower is the general colorful plant part that makes seeds or looks pretty, while blossom often means clusters on fruit trees.
