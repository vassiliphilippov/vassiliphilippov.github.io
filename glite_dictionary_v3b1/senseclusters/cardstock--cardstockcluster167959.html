cardstock

noun
thick craft paper
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrdˌstɑk / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdˌstɒk / (BrE)
thick, stiff paper that is stronger than regular paper and used for crafts, cards, and invitations

Examples

Thick cardstock paper
She printed the invitations on white cardstock for a more professional look.
The children used colored cardstock to make greeting cards for their parents.
You need sturdy cardstock if you want your project to last longer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: construction paper - colored, stiff paper used for crafts
  • synonym: paperboard - a thick, stiff paper material used for packaging
