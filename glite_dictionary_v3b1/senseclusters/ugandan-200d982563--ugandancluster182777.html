Ugandan

proper noun
person from Uganda
Pronunciation: / juːˈɡændən / (AmE), / juːˈɡændən / (BrE)
a person from Uganda or relating to Uganda, a country in East Africa

Examples

Ugandan community
The Ugandan community in the city celebrates their national holidays together.
She met a Ugandan student at the international conference last year.
Many Ugandan dishes feature plantains and beans as main ingredients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Kenyan - a person from Kenya, a neighboring country in East Africa
  • antonym: foreigner - a person from another country
