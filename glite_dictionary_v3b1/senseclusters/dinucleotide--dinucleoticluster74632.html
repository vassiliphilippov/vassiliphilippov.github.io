dinucleotide

noun
two joined nucleotides
Pronunciation: / daɪˈnuːkliəˌtaɪd / (AmE), / daɪˈnjuːkliəˌtaɪd / (BrE)
a molecule consisting of two nucleotides joined together by chemical bonds
