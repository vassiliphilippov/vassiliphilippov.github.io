harshen

verb
make or become harsher
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrʃən / (AmE), / ˈhɑːʃən / (BrE)
to make or become harsh or harsher in quality, tone, or appearance

Examples

To harshen criticism
Criticism can harshen if mistakes repeat themselves.
The cold wind began to harshen his features as he walked outside.
The drought will only harshen the already difficult living conditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: soften - make or become less harsh or severe
