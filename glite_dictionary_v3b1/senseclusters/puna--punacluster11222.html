puna

adjective
Andean plateau region
Pronunciation: / ˈpuːnə / (AmE), / ˈpuːnə / (BrE)
relating to the high, cold plateau region of the central Andes or to its characteristic plants, animals, or climate

Examples

Puna grasslands

Many puna plants have developed special features to survive the cold and dry conditions.
