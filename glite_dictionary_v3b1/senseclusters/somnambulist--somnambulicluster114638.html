somnambulist

noun
person who walks in their sleep
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɑmˈnæmbjəlɪst / (AmE), / sɒmˈnæmbjʊlɪst / (BrE)
a person who walks about while asleep; a sleepwalker

Examples

A somnambulist at night
As a child, she was a frequent somnambulist and often wandered into the kitchen at night.
The doctor explained that a somnambulist can perform complex actions while still asleep.
They installed alarms because their son is a somnambulist who sometimes leaves the house while sleepwalking.
