marriage offer

noun
an offer to marry someone
Pronunciation: / ˈmærɪdʒ ˈɔfər / (AmE), / ˈmærɪdʒ ˈɒfə / (BrE)
a proposal made to someone with the intention of entering into marriage with them

Examples

A marriage offer
After declining several marriage offers, she finally accepted one.
His marriage offer was both sincere and unexpected.
She received a surprising marriage offer from her childhood friend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: proposal - a formal suggestion, especially of marriage
