piteous

adjective
deserving or causing pity
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪtiəs / (AmE), / ˈpɪtɪəs / (BrE)
deserving or causing feelings of pity; evoking compassion through suffering or distress

Examples

A piteous expression
Her piteous expression made it impossible for anyone to refuse her request.
The child's piteous cries moved everyone in the room to try and help.
They watched the piteous sight of the injured dog limping along the road.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pitiable - deserving pity
  • synonym: pitiful - arousing pity or compassion
  • synonym: wretched - in a very unhappy or unfortunate state
