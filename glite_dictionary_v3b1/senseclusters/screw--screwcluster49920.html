screw

verb
turn or rotate with a twisting motion
Pronunciation: / skruː / (AmE), / skruː / (BrE)
to fasten, insert, or turn something with a twisting motion, like using a screw

Examples

Related Terms

  • synonym: twist - to turn something, especially repeatedly, so that it takes on a spiral shape
  • antonym: unscrew - to loosen or remove by turning in the opposite direction
