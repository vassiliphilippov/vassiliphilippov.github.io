modesty

noun
the quality of being proper and unassuming
3.8 1/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…ëÀêd…ôsti / (AmE), / Ààm…íd…ôsti / (BrE)
the quality of behaving, dressing, or speaking in a way that is considered proper, decent, and not intended to attract attention or show off.

Examples

Modesty in dress
A sense of modesty is valued in many traditional societies.
He accepted the award with great modesty and thanked everyone who helped him.
Her modesty prevented her from taking credit for the team's success.
