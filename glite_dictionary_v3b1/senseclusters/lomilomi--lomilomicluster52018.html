lomilomi

verb
to massage Hawaiian style
Pronunciation: / ˌloʊmiˈloʊmi / (AmE), / ˌlɒmiˈlɒmi / (BrE)
to massage someone using the traditional Hawaiian lomilomi technique, which involves rhythmic movements and gentle pressure.

Examples

To lomilomi tired muscles
After a long hike, he decided to lomilomi his tired legs to help them recover.
The spa offers therapists trained to lomilomi clients using authentic Hawaiian methods.
She learned how to lomilomi her family members as part of a cultural tradition.
