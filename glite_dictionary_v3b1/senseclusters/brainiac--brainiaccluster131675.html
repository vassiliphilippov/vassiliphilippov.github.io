brainiac

noun
an exceptionally intelligent person
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪniˌæk / (AmE), / ˈbreɪnɪak / (BrE)
a person who is exceptionally intelligent or very good at academic or intellectual pursuits

Examples

A brainiac student
Although everyone expected the brainiac to ace the exam, she was still nervous beforehand.
Don't be intimidated by the brainiac in your class—everyone learns at their own pace.
He was always considered the brainiac of the group, solving problems nobody else could understand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Einstein - an informal term for a highly intelligent person, especially used ironically
  • antonym: dullard - a slow or stupid person
  • synonym: genius - a person with exceptional intellectual or creative power or other natural ability
