Babesia

noun

A genus of protozoan parasites, some species of which infect the red blood cells of mammals and cause babesiosis.

Examples

Babesia infection
Infection with Babesia can lead to symptoms similar to malaria.
The life cycle of Babesia involves both a mammalian host and a tick vector.
