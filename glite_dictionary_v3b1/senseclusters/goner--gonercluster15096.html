goner

noun
person who is doomed or beyond hope
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːnər / (AmE), / ˈɡɒnə / (BrE)
a person who is doomed or beyond hope, especially in a situation where survival or success seems impossible

Examples

A goner in danger
After the car crashed into the river, everyone thought he was a goner, but he managed to escape.
If the doctors don't find a cure soon, I'm afraid I'm a goner.
When she saw the boss coming, she knew she was a goner for missing the deadline.

Related Terms

  • antonym: survivor - a person who survives and is not doomed
  • synonym: toast - a person in trouble or doomed to fail (informal)
