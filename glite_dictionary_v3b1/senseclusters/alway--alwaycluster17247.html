alway

adverb
at all times
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːlweɪ / (AmE), / ˈɔːlweɪ / (BrE)
at all times; on every occasion (archaic form of 'always')

Examples

Alway polite
He was alway courteous to guests, no matter the hour.
The castle gates stood alway open to travelers seeking shelter.
She believed that hope should alway guide one's actions.
