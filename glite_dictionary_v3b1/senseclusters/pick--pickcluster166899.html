pick

verb
strike lightly with a picking motion
Pronunciation: / pɪk / (AmE), / pɪk / (BrE)
to pluck or play the strings of a musical instrument with the fingers or a pick

Examples

To pick the strings
He began to pick the strings of the guitar gently to tune it.
She picked at the windowpane until the ice started to crack.
The child picked the drum with his finger, producing a quiet sound.
He watched the musician expertly pick each note on the banjo.
She used her fingers to pick the strings of the guitar gently.
The boy began to pick at the loose threads on his sweater.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pluck - to pull or pick off, especially with a quick movement
  • synonym: tap - to strike lightly and quickly
