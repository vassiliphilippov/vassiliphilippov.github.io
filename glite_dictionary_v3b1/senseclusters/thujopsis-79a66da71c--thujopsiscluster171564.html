Thujopsis

noun
genus of conifers related to Thuja
Pronunciation: / θuːˈdʒɑːpsɪs / (AmE), / θjuːˈdʒɒpsɪs / (BrE)
A monotypic genus of coniferous trees closely related to Thuja, consisting of a single species native to Japan.
