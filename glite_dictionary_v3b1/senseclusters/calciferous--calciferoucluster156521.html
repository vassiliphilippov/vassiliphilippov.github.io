calciferous

adjective
containing calcium or calcium compounds
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌkælsɪˈfɛrəs / (AmE), / ˌkalsɪˈfɛːrəs / (BrE)
bearing, producing, or containing calcium or calcium compounds such as calcium carbonate or calcite

Examples

Calciferous soil
Certain soils with a calciferous nature can influence the types of plants that grow there.
Geologists collected calciferous samples to study their mineral content.
The region is known for its calciferous rocks, which are rich in calcium carbonate.
