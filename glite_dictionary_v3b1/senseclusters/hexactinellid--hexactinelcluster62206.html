hexactinellid

noun
glass sponge
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɛkˌsæktəˈnɛlɪd / (AmE), / hɛkˌsæktɪˈnɛlɪd / (BrE)
a deep-sea sponge with a skeleton made of silica, commonly known as a glass sponge

Examples

A hexactinellid sponge
Scientists discovered a new species of hexactinellid living on the ocean floor.
The delicate structure of a hexactinellid makes it look almost like glass.
Many hexactinellids are found in deep, cold waters around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sponge - a simple aquatic animal with a porous body
