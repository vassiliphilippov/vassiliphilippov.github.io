grisette

noun
Belgian farmhouse ale
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡrɪˈzɛt / (AmE), / ɡrɪˈzɛt / (BrE)
a light, refreshing Belgian farmhouse-style ale, originally brewed for miners and similar to a saison

Examples

A cold grisette beer
After a long day in the fields, the workers shared a cold grisette together.
The brewery introduced a new grisette inspired by traditional Belgian recipes.
Many craft beer enthusiasts enjoy the light and refreshing taste of a grisette in summer.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: lager - a type of beer that is light in color and flavor
  • synonym: saison - a Belgian farmhouse-style ale with a fruity and spicy flavor
  • degree higher: tripel - a strong pale Belgian ale with higher alcohol content
