lorazepam

noun
antianxiety medication; benzodiazepine tranquilizer
Pronunciation: / lɔˈræzəˌpæm / (AmE), / lɒˈræzəˌpam / (BrE)
a medication of the benzodiazepine class used as a tranquilizer to treat anxiety, insomnia, and other conditions

Examples

lorazepam medication
He was given lorazepam before surgery to help him relax.
Patients should not drink alcohol when taking lorazepam due to potential side effects.
The doctor prescribed lorazepam to help manage her severe anxiety symptoms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antianxiety - intended to reduce anxiety
  • synonym: diazepam - another benzodiazepine used as a tranquilizer (Valium)
