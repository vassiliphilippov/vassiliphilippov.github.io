good spirit

noun
benevolent supernatural being
2.7
Pronunciation: / ɡʊd ˈspɪrɪt / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
A benevolent supernatural being or force, believed to do good or bring good fortune.

Examples

A good spirit brings luck
Legends say a good spirit lives near the old well, bringing luck to those who visit.
Many cultures believe that a good spirit protects children from harm.
The villagers left offerings to the good spirit of the forest.

Related Terms

  • antonym: evil spirit - a malevolent supernatural being or force
