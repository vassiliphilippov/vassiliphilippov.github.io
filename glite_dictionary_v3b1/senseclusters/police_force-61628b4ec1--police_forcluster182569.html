police force

noun
organization of police officers
2.0 6.08/m
Pronunciation: / p…ôÀàlis f…îrs / (AmE), / p…ôÀàliÀês f…îÀês / (BrE)
the official organization of police officers responsible for maintaining law and order in a particular area

Examples

The police force
Following the incident, the entire police force underwent new training procedures.
The city expanded its police force to ensure better security for residents.
The local police force responded quickly to the emergency call.

Synonyms

  • police
    1.0 248/m
    police is the everyday name for officers who stop crime and keep order, while police force sounds more formal and refers to the whole group.
  • constabulary
    8.1 0.69/m
    constabulary is a British word for a local police organization, while police force is a general term used in many countries.
  • police department
    1.3 5.87/m
    police department is the local government office that runs the police in a city, while police force names the whole group of officers.
  • law enforcement agency
    3.1 2.82/m
    law enforcement agency covers any organization that enforces laws, while police force usually means the local uniformed police group.
