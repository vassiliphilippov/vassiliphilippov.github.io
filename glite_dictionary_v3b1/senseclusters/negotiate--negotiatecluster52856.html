negotiate

verb
sell or transfer a financial instrument
Pronunciation: / nɪˈɡoʊʃiˌeɪt /; / nəˈɡoʊʃieɪt / (AmE), / nɪˈɡəʊʃieɪt / (BrE)
to sell, transfer, or exchange a financial instrument such as a check or security

Examples

To negotiate a check
Banks often negotiate securities on behalf of their clients.
She helped the company negotiate several promissory notes for immediate cash.
The firm decided to negotiate its bonds before the end of the quarter.
Banks often negotiate checks on behalf of their clients.
He attempted to negotiate the promissory note before its maturity date.
The company decided to negotiate the bond to a foreign investor.

  • synonym: sell - to exchange something for money
  • synonym: transfer - to change ownership of something, especially formally or legally
