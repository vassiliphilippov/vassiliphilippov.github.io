mesothelial

adjective
lining body cavities
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛsəˈθiːliəl / (AmE), / ˌmɛsəʊˈθiːliəl / (BrE)
relating to the thin layer of cells that lines body cavities and covers internal organs

Examples

Mesothelial layer
A mesothelial layer lines the inside of the chest and abdominal cavities.
Doctors examined the mesothelial cells for signs of disease.
The mesothelial tissue helps protect organs from friction and injury.
