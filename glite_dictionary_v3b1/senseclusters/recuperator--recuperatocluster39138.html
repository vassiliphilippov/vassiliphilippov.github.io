recuperator

noun
heat recovery device
8.6
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkuːpəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / rɪˈkjuːpəˌreɪtə / (BrE)
a device that recovers heat from exhaust gases to improve energy efficiency in industrial processes

Examples

Industrial recuperator
The engineers installed a recuperator to capture waste heat from the furnace exhaust.
By using a recuperator, the plant significantly reduced its overall energy consumption.
Modern factories often rely on a recuperator to improve their energy efficiency and lower costs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heat exchanger - a device that transfers heat between two or more fluids
  • synonym: regenerator - a device that stores and releases heat to improve efficiency
