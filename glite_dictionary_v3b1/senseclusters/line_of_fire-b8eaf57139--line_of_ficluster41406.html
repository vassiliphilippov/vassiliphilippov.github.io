line of fire

noun
path of a discharged projectile
4.1 4.5
Pronunciation: /laɪn əv faɪr/ /; / /laɪn əv ˈfaɪ.ər/ /; / /laɪn ə faɪr/ (AmE), /laɪn əv ˈfaɪ.ə/ /; / /laɪn ə ˈfaɪ.ə/ (BrE)
