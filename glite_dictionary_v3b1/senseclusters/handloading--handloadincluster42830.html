handloading

noun
manual ammunition assembly
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌloʊdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhændˌləʊdɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of manually assembling ammunition by loading individual components into cartridge cases

Examples

Handloading bullets
Many shooting enthusiasts take up handloading to customize their ammunition for better accuracy.
He learned handloading from his father, who carefully measured each powder charge.
Safety is crucial when practicing handloading to avoid dangerous mistakes.

Related Terms
