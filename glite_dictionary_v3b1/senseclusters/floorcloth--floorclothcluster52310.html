floorcloth

noun
decorative floor covering
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔrˌklɔθ / (AmE), / ˈflɔːˌklɒθ / (BrE)
a heavy, durable cloth used as a decorative or protective covering for a floor

Examples

A painted floorcloth
The old kitchen still had a painted floorcloth covering the wooden boards.
She chose a colorful floorcloth to brighten up the entryway.
After cleaning, the floorcloth looked almost as good as new.
They spread a colorful floorcloth in the hallway to protect the wooden boards.
The artist painted a unique pattern on the old floorcloth in her studio.
After the party, the floorcloth was covered in crumbs and needed to be cleaned.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carpet - a floor covering made from thick woven fabric
  • synonym: rug - a thick woven piece of fabric used as a floor covering
