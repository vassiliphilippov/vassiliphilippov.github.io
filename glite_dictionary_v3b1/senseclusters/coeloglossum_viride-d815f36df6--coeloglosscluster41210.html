Coeloglossum viride

noun
a species of orchid
Pronunciation: / ˌsiːoʊˈɡlɑːsəm ˈvɪrɪdeɪ / (AmE), / ˌsiːələˈɡlɒsəm ˈvɪrɪdiː / (BrE)
A species of orchid with hooded, long-bracted green to yellow-green flowers often tinged with purple.
