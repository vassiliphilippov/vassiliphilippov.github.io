Synonyms

  • give
    1.0 1200/m
    give is a general verb for giving or offering something to someone, while hand emphasizes placing into someone's hands or custody.
  • pass
    1.0 40/m
    pass means to move or transfer something along from one person to another, while hand focuses on placing into custody.
  • pass on
    2.7 6/m
    pass on means to give or tell something to the next person, while hand often implies direct physical passing or custody.
  • hand over
    2.8 14/m
    hand over means to give possession or control, often because you must, while hand is a more general act of giving.
  • hand off
    3.7 1/m
    hand off means to transfer responsibility or an object, often quickly or informally, while hand covers ordinary giving or custody.
  • turn over
    3.2 12/m
    turn over means to give someone or something to an authority or for custody, while hand is a simple act of giving.
