ingrate

noun
ungrateful person
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌɡreɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪŋˌɡreɪt / (BrE)
a person who does not show gratitude or appreciation

Examples

An ingrate person
After all I did for him, he turned out to be an ingrate and walked away without saying thanks.
It's harsh to label someone an ingrate for a single oversight.
She called her children ingrates after they forgot her birthday again.

Related Terms

  • antonym: grateful - feeling or showing thanks
  • synonym: ingrateful - lacking gratitude; unthankful
