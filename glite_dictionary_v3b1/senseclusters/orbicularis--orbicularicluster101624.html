orbicularis

noun
ring-shaped muscle
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɔːrˌbɪkjəˈlɛrɪs / (AmE), / ɔːˌbɪkjʊˈlɑːrɪs / (BrE)
a ring-shaped muscle, especially one around the eye or mouth, that controls opening and closing movements

Examples

Orbicularis around the eye
The orbicularis muscle helps you close your eyelids tightly.
Damage to the orbicularis can affect a person's ability to speak or eat.
The doctor explained how the orbicularis works to control facial expressions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sphincter - a circular muscle that closes a passage in the body
