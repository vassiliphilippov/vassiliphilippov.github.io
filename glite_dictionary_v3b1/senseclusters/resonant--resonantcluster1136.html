resonant

adjective
evocative; suggestive; bringing to mind
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛzənənt / (AmE), / ˈrɛzənənt / (BrE)
making you think of or remember a particular feeling, idea, or image; evocative.

Examples

Resonant memories
He spoke in a tone resonant with nostalgia, reminding everyone of summers long past.
Her words were resonant of her childhood in Italy, painting vivid pictures in our minds.
The film's soundtrack is resonant of classic jazz, transporting the audience to another era.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evocative - bringing strong images, memories, or feelings to mind
