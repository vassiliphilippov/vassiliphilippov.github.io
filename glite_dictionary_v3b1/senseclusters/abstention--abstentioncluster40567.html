abstention

noun
deliberate avoidance, especially of alcohol
9.4 5.8
Pronunciation: /əb.ˈsten.ʃən/ /; / /æb.ˈsten.ʃən/ (AmE), /əb.ˈsten.ʃən/ /; / /æb.ˈsten.ʃən/ (BrE)
the practice of deliberately choosing not to take part in something, especially the avoidance of alcohol or other pleasures as a matter of principle

Examples

Abstention from alcohol
Abstention from all forms of indulgence is a core value in certain religious traditions.
His long period of abstention from alcohol was admirable.
The policy encouraged abstention as a way to promote a healthier lifestyle.
