pot roast

noun
beef cut/dish for slow simmering
Pronunciation: / pɑt ɹoʊst / (AmE), / pɒt ɹəʊst / (BrE)
A large piece of beef, suitable for slow cooking by simmering in liquid in a covered pot, commonly served as a main dish.

Examples

A pot roast dinner
After hours of slow cooking, the pot roast was tender enough to cut with a fork.
She prepared a pot roast with carrots and potatoes for Sunday dinner.
The smell of the pot roast simmering all afternoon made everyone hungry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braise - to cook food slowly in liquid in a closed container
  • synonym: stew - to cook meat or vegetables slowly in liquid in a closed dish or pan
