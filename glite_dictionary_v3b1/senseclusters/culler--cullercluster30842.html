culler

noun
animal remover
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlər / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
a person or thing that removes animals from a group, usually to control population size or health

Examples

Wildlife culler
The culler was hired to manage the deer population in the park.
A skilled culler selects only the sick animals to remove from the herd.
Wildlife officials sent a culler to help control the overpopulation of rabbits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hunter - a person who hunts wild animals
  • antonym: protector - a person who keeps animals safe from harm
