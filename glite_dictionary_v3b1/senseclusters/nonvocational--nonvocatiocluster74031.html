nonvocational

adjective
not job-related
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnvəˈkeɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnvəˈkeɪʃənəl / (BrE)
not related to training for a specific job, trade, or occupation

Examples

Nonvocational courses
The university offers a wide range of nonvocational courses in the arts and sciences.
He chose a nonvocational program because he wanted a broader education.
Many students enroll in nonvocational classes to explore their personal interests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: academic - relating to education or scholarship, not practical job training
  • antonym: vocational - relating to training for a specific job or occupation
