debagging

noun
removal of covering
9.3
Pronunciation: / diːˈbæɡɪŋ / (AmE), / diːˈbæɡɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of removing a protective bag or covering from an object, especially during unpacking or unveiling

Examples

Debagging a sculpture
The workers began the debagging of the new sculpture early in the morning.
After debagging the equipment, the team inspected it for any damage.
The debagging process revealed the shiny surface of the restored car.
