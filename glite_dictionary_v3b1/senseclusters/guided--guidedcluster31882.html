guided

adjective
controlled or directed along a path
Pronunciation: / ˈɡaɪdɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪdɪd / (BrE)
(of a device or vehicle) controlled or directed along a specific path or towards a target, especially after being launched or set in motion.

Examples

A guided tour
A guided tour of the museum offers more detailed information than exploring on your own.
Thanks to its guided navigation system, the drone can avoid obstacles autonomously.
The army deployed several guided missiles during the exercise.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unguided - not subject to direction or control after launching
