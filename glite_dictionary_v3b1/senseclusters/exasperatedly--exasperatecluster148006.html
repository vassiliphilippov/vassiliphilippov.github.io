exasperatedly

adverb
with annoyance
7.2
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzæspəˌreɪtɪdli / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɑːspəˌreɪtɪdli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows strong annoyance or irritation

Examples

To speak exasperatedly
He threw up his hands exasperatedly when no one listened to his advice.
She exasperatedly explained the instructions for the third time.
The teacher sighed exasperatedly as the class continued to talk over her.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmly - in a peaceful or relaxed way
  • synonym: irritably - in an annoyed or easily angered way
