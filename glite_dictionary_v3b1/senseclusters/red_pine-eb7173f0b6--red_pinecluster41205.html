red pine

noun
New Zealand timber tree (rimu)
Pronunciation: / rɛd paɪn / (AmE), / rɛd paɪn / (BrE)
A tall New Zealand timber tree, known for its reddish wood; also called rimu.

Examples

New Zealand red pine
Furniture made from red pine is valued for its attractive appearance and durability.

The native forests are home to the majestic red pine, which grows up to 50 meters tall.
