humpf

verb
to express annoyance
Pronunciation: / hʌmf / (AmE), / hʌmf / (BrE)
to make a short, low sound or gesture to show annoyance, doubt, or disapproval

Examples

She humpfs every time someone interrupts her reading.
After losing the game, the player just humpfed and walked off the field.
