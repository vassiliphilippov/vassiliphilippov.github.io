clip-on

adjective
attachable by a clip
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪp ɑn / (AmE), / ˈklɪp ɒn / (BrE)
designed to be attached by means of a clip, rather than by piercing, sewing, or fixing permanently.

Examples

Clip-on tie
He wore a clip-on bow tie to the formal dinner.
She prefers clip-on earrings because her ears aren't pierced.
The sunglasses come with a convenient clip-on attachment for prescription glasses.
