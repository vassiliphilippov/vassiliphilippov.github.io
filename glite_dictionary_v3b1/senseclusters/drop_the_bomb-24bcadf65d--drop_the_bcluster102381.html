drop the bomb

verb
reveal shocking news
Pronunciation: / drɑp ðə bɑm / (AmE), / drɒp ðə bɒm / (BrE)
to reveal shocking news or make a dramatic and unexpected announcement

Examples

To drop the bomb suddenly
Just before the event ended, he dropped the bomb that he was getting married.
Nobody expected him to drop the bomb about the company closing during lunch.
She dropped the bomb when she told her parents she was moving abroad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: break the news - to announce important or shocking news to someone
  • synonym: spill the beans - to reveal secret information unintentionally or suddenly
