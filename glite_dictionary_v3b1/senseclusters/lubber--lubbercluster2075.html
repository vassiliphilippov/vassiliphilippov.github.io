lubber

noun
awkward or clumsy person
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌbər / (AmE), / ˈlʌbə / (BrE)
an awkward or clumsy person, often considered to be inexperienced or lacking social grace

Examples

Clumsy lubber
Despite being called a lubber by his friends, Tom tried hard to improve his dancing.
Everyone laughed when the big lubber tripped over his own feet at the party.
The pirate captain had no patience for any lubber who could not climb the rigging.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clumsy - lacking grace or skill in movement or action
  • synonym: oaf - a stupid, clumsy, or uncultured person
