driftless

adjective
without glacial deposits
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɪftləs / (AmE), / ˈdrɪftləs / (BrE)
not covered by glacial drift or deposits, especially referring to a geographic region unaffected by glaciers.

Examples

Driftless region
The driftless region features steep hills and deep valleys unlike the surrounding flatlands.
Scientists study the driftless area to understand landscapes untouched by glaciers.
Farmers in the driftless zone often deal with rocky soils and uneven terrain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: glaciated - covered or affected by glaciers or glacial deposits
