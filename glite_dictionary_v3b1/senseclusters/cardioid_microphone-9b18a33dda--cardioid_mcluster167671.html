cardioid microphone

noun
directional microphone with heart-shaped pickup pattern
7.5 4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːr.dɪ.ɔɪd ˈmaɪ.krə.foʊn/ (AmE), /ˈkɑː.dɪ.ɔɪd ˈmaɪ.krə.fəʊn/ (BrE)
A microphone that is most sensitive to sound coming from the front and sides, with reduced pickup from the rear, following a heart-shaped (cardioid) pattern of sensitivity.

Examples

A cardioid microphone pattern
At the press conference, each speaker used a cardioid microphone to ensure clear and focused audio.
Engineers often recommend a cardioid microphone for podcasting in untreated rooms.
The cardioid microphone is ideal for recording vocals because it reduces background noise from the sides and rear.

Related Terms

  • antonym: omnidirectional microphone - microphone sensitive to sound from all directions
  • degree higher: supercardioid microphone - microphone with a more directional polar pattern than cardioid
