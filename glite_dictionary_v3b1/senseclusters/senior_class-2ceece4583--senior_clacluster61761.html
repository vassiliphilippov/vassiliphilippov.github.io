senior class

noun
final-year students in school or college
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːniɚ klæs / (AmE), / ˈsiːniə klɑːs / (BrE)
the group of students in their final year of high school or college

Examples

The senior class president
During the assembly, the senior class president gave a moving farewell speech.
The senior class planned a trip to celebrate their graduation.
This year's senior class has the highest number of honor students in a decade.

Related Terms

  • antonym: freshman class - students in their first year of high school or college
  • degree lower: junior class - students in the year before the final year of high school or college
