ad hominem

adjective
argument attacking a person, not their ideas
9.1 6.9
Pronunciation: /æd ˈhɑː.mɪ.nəm/ /; / /əd ˈhɑː.mɪ.nəm/ (AmE), /æd ˈhɒm.ɪ.nəm/ /; / /əd ˈhɒm.ɪ.nəm/ (BrE)
(Of an argument or reaction) directed against a person rather than the position they are maintaining.

Examples

Ad hominem attack
Critics warned against relying on ad hominem reasoning in serious discussions.
She accused him of making an ad hominem attack instead of addressing her points.
The debate quickly devolved into ad hominem arguments that ignored the actual issue.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ad rem - relating to the matter at hand, as opposed to 'ad hominem'
  • synonym: personal attack - an attack directed against a person rather than a position or argument
