Nut

proper noun
Egyptian sky goddess
9.9
Pronunciation: / nʌt / (AmE), / nʌt / (BrE)
An ancient Egyptian goddess who personified the sky, often depicted as a woman arching over the earth.

Examples

Nut goddess
In Egyptian mythology, Nut was believed to swallow the sun every evening.
Priests in ancient Egypt often performed rituals dedicated to Nut.
The ceiling of the tomb was painted with images of Nut stretching across the sky.
