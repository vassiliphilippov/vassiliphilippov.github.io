anthroposophy

noun
philosophy by Rudolf Steiner, often in education
9.9
Pronunciation: / ænˌθroʊpəˈsɑfi / (AmE), / ænˌθrəʊpəˈsɒfi / (BrE)
A system of beliefs and practices developed by Rudolf Steiner, aiming to integrate practical and psychological approaches, especially in child-centered education.

Examples

Anthroposophy education system
Critics of anthroposophy question its integration of spiritual beliefs with scientific concepts.
Many Waldorf schools are founded on the principles of anthroposophy.
She became interested in anthroposophy after reading about Rudolf Steiner's educational philosophy.
