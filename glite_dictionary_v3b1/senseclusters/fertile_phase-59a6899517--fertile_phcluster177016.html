fertile phase

noun
period of highest fertility in menstrual cycle
4.1 4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈfɜːr.təl feɪz/ /; / /ˈfɜːr.taɪl feɪz/ (AmE), /ˈfɜː.taɪl feɪz/ /; / /ˈfɜː.təl feɪz/ (BrE)
