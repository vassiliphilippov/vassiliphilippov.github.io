bookfair

noun
event for displaying and selling books
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊkˌfɛr / (AmE), / ˈbʊkˌfeə / (BrE)
an event where books are displayed and sold, often organized by publishers, booksellers, or institutions, sometimes to raise funds or support a cause

Examples

A bookfair event
Our school is hosting a bookfair next week, featuring local authors and publishers.
She bought several new novels at the annual bookfair downtown.
The bookfair attracted thousands of visitors interested in literature and rare editions.
The school is hosting a bookfair to support its library renovation project.
Volunteers helped organize the annual bookfair to raise money for literacy programs.
We bought several rare novels at the local bookfair last weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: book sale - event or sale where books are sold, often for fundraising
  • synonym: trade fair - large exhibition at which businesses promote their products and services
