bookfair

noun
event for displaying and selling books
3.8 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbʊk.feər/ (AmE), /ˈbʊk.feə/ (BrE)
an event where books are displayed and sold, often organized by publishers, booksellers, or institutions, sometimes to raise funds or support a cause

Examples

The bookfair attracted thousands of visitors interested in literature and rare editions.
The school is hosting a bookfair to support its library renovation project.
Volunteers helped organize the annual bookfair to raise money for literacy programs.
