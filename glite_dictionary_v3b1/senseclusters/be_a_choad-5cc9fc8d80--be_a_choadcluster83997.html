be a choad

verb
act foolishly or obnoxiously (slang)
Pronunciation: / bi ə tʃoʊd / (AmE), / biː ə tʃəʊd / (BrE)
To act in a foolish, irritating, or obnoxious way.

Examples

To be a choad at meeting
If you be a choad at the meeting, people won't take you seriously.
Please don't be a choad about this; just help us out.
She told her brother not to be a choad when guests were over.
