Ovibos moschatus

noun
large Arctic mammal; musk ox
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɪˌboʊs moʊˈskɑːtəs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvɪˌbɒs mɒsˈkɑːtəs / (BrE)
A large, shaggy-coated bovid mammal native to the Arctic regions of Canada and Greenland, intermediate in size and anatomy between an ox and a sheep; also known as the musk ox.
