yard donkey

noun
engine-powered winch system for hauling logs
3.2 9.9
Pronunciation: / jɑrd ˈdɑŋki / (AmE), / jɑːd ˈdɒŋki / (BrE)
a winch or system of winches powered by an engine, used for hauling logs from the place they are felled to a landing or skid road

Examples

The yard donkey engine
A new engine was installed in the yard donkey to improve its hauling efficiency.
The old logging company still uses a yard donkey to pull heavy timber up the steep hillside.
Workers operated the yard donkey to move logs from the cutting area to the loading zone.
