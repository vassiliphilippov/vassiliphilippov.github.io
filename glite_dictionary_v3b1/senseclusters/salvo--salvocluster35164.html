Synonyms

  • volley
    6.8 0.5/m
    a volley is many projectiles fired together by soldiers or weapons, while a salvo often refers to artillery or formal salutes.
  • fusillade
    9.5 0.1/m
    a fusillade is a heavy, continuous burst of gunfire that keeps going, while a salvo is usually a planned, single round of shots.
  • burst
    5.6 1/m
    a burst is a short, sudden discharge of shots from one gun, while a salvo is often a larger, coordinated round from many weapons.
