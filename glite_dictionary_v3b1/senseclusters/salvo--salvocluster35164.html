salvo

noun
simultaneous discharge of weapons
9.9 6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsælvoʊ / (AmE), / ˈsælvəʊ / (BrE)
a simultaneous or rapid series of shots or blows, especially from firearms or artillery

Examples

A salvo of gunfire
A salvo of gunfire erupted as the parade began.
The artillery fired a salvo that reverberated across the valley.
They answered the enemy's attack with a fierce salvo of their own.

Related Terms
