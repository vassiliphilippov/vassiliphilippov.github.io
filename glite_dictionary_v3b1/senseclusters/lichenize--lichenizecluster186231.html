lichenize

verb
to cover with lichens
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪkəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈlɪkənaɪz / (BrE)
to become covered with lichens or to cause something to be covered with lichens

Examples

To lichenize a surface
The old stone wall slowly lichenized after years of exposure to rain and shade.
If left untouched, the wooden bench will eventually lichenize in the damp forest.
The statue in the park has lichenized, giving it a weathered and natural look.

Related Terms

  • synonym: encrust - to cover or coat with a hard layer
