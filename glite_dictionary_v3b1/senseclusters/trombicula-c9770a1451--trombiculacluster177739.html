Trombicula

noun
genus of mites; chiggers
Pronunciation: / trɒmˈbɪkjələ /; / trɒmˈbɪkjʊlə / (AmE), / trɒmˈbɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
a genus of mites (Trombiculidae) whose larvae, called chiggers, can cause skin irritation or dermatitis in humans and animals
