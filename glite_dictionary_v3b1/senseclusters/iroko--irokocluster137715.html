iroko

noun
West African tree spirit
9.9
Pronunciation: / iˈroʊkoʊ / (AmE), / ɪˈrəʊkəʊ / (BrE)
a spirit or sacred presence believed to inhabit the iroko tree in West African, especially Yoruba, traditional religion

Examples

Iroko spirit worship
In Yoruba tradition, the iroko is honored as a powerful spirit of the forest.
Offerings were placed at the base of the iroko to seek blessings and protection.
Stories about the iroko are passed down through generations in West African communities.
