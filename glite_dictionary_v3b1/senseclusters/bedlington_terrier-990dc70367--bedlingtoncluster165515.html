Bedlington terrier

noun
lamb-like breed of small terrier dog
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdlɪŋtən ˈtɛriər / (AmE), / ˈbɛdlɪŋtən ˈtɛriə / (BrE)
A small breed of dog, originally from England, with a curly, pale coat and a distinctive lamb-like appearance.
