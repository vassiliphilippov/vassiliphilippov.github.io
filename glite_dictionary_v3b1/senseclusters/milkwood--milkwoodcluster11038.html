milkwood

noun
wood from milkwood tree
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlkwʊd / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkwʊd / (BrE)
the wood or timber from a milkwood tree, used for fuel, carving, or building materials

Examples

Milkwood timber
The craftsman selected milkwood for its durability and attractive grain.
They built a sturdy bench out of milkwood for the garden.
Local artists often use milkwood to carve decorative bowls and utensils.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hardwood - wood from broad-leaved trees, used for furniture and construction
  • synonym: timber - wood prepared for use in building and carpentry
