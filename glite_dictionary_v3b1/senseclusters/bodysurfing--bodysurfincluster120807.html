bodysurfing

noun
riding waves without board
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑdiˌsɝfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɒdiˌsɜːfɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport of riding waves in the sea using only your body, without a board or other equipment

Examples

A bodysurfing competition
He spent the afternoon bodysurfing at the local beach.
Many people enjoy bodysurfing because it requires no special equipment.
She learned bodysurfing during her summer vacation by the ocean.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bodyboarding - the sport of riding waves on a small board while lying down
  • synonym: surfing - the sport of riding waves on a surfboard
