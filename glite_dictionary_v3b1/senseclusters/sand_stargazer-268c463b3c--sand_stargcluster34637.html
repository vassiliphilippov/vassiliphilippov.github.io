sand stargazer

noun
small tropical fish with stalked eyes that burrows in sand
6.9 8.4
Pronunciation: /sænd ˈstɑːr.geɪ.zər/ (AmE), /sænd ˈstɑː.ɡeɪ.zə/ (BrE)
a small, pale fish found in tropical waters of the Americas, noted for its eyes on stalks atop the head and a habit of burrowing in sand to ambush prey
