eye-deceiving

adjective
visually deceptive; creating illusion of reality
Pronunciation: / aɪ dɪˈsiːvɪŋ / (AmE), / aɪ dɪˈsiːvɪŋ / (BrE)
(of art or images) creating the illusion of reality; visually deceptive.

Examples

Eye-deceiving art
She specializes in eye-deceiving paintings that trick the viewer at first glance.
The eye-deceiving mural on the ceiling made the room appear much taller.
Trompe-l'oeil is an eye-deceiving style that has captivated artists for centuries.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: realistic - closely resembling real life, especially in art or appearance
  • synonym: trompe-l'oeil - art technique that creates lifelike optical illusions
