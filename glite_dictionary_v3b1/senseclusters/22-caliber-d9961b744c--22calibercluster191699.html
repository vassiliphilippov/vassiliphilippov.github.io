.22-caliber

adjective
describing a 0.22-inch bore in guns/ammo
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˌtwɛnti ˈtuː ˈkælɪbər /; / ˈtwɛnti tuː ˈkæləbər / (AmE), / ˌtwɛnti ˈtjuː ˈkælɪbə /; / ˈtwɛnti tuː ˈkalɪbə / (BrE)
