beautifully

adverb
in a beautiful manner
Pronunciation: / ˈbjuːtəfli / (AmE), / ˈbjuːtɪfəli / (BrE)
in a beautiful or pleasing manner; with beauty

Examples

To play beautifully
She played the violin beautifully at the concert last night.
The garden was beautifully maintained throughout the year.
The poem is beautifully written and deeply moving.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beautiful - pleasing to the senses or mind
  • antonym: ugly - unpleasant or repulsive in appearance
