sundari

noun
mangrove tree species
Pronunciation: / suːnˈdɑːri / (AmE), / sʌnˈdɑːri / (BrE)
a mangrove tree (Heritiera fomes) native to the Sundarbans region, valued for its strong, durable wood

Examples

Sundari tree wood
The sundari is an important source of timber for local communities in the Sundarbans.
Many animals find shelter among the roots of the sundari trees along the riverbanks.
Conservationists are working to protect the endangered sundari from overharvesting and habitat loss.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mangrove - a tree or shrub that grows in coastal saline or brackish water
