sad

adjective
feeling or showing unhappiness
Pronunciation: / sæd / (AmE), / sæd / (BrE)
feeling or showing sorrow; unhappy or causing unhappiness

Examples

Sad eyes
His sad eyes told me everything I needed to know about how he was feeling.
It was a sad day for the community when the old library closed.
She felt sad when her best friend moved away to another city.

Related Terms

  • antonym: joyful - feeling or expressing great happiness
  • degree higher: miserable - extremely unhappy or uncomfortable
  • synonym: unhappy - feeling sad or not cheerful
