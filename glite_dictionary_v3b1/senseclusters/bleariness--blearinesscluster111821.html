bleariness

noun
blurry or tired look
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈblɪr.i.nəs / (AmE), / ˈblɪə.ri.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being blurry, unfocused, or showing signs of tiredness, especially in the eyes or appearance

Examples

The bleariness in eyes
The bleariness in her eyes made it clear she had not slept well.
After staring at the computer for hours, he noticed a growing bleariness in his vision.
Morning bleariness faded as the sunlight filled the room and he became more alert.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blur - a state of being unclear or indistinct
  • antonym: clarity - the quality of being clear and easy to see or understand
