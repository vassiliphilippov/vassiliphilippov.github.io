crucifier

noun
harsh persecutor
9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkruː.sɪ.faɪ.ər/ (AmE), /ˈkruː.sɪ.faɪ.ə/ (BrE)
a person who harshly punishes, criticizes, or oppresses someone, especially in a severe or relentless way

Examples

Media crucifier
The media became her crucifier, criticizing her every move after the controversy.
He felt like a crucifier when he harshly judged his colleague for a minor mistake.
Online trolls can act as crucifiers, relentlessly attacking people on social media.

Related Terms

  • synonym: persecutor - someone who treats another person cruelly or unfairly, especially because of their beliefs
  • antonym: protector - someone who defends or shields others from harm
  • synonym: tormentor - a person who causes great pain or suffering to others
