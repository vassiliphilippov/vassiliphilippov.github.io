starburst

noun
radiating pattern
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːrˌbɝst /; / ˈstɑːrbɜrst / (AmE), / ˈstɑːbɜːst / (BrE)
a bright, radiating shape or pattern with lines or rays spreading out from a central point, like an exploding star

Examples

A bright starburst
The artist painted a large starburst in the center of the mural to catch attention.
Fireworks exploded in the sky, creating a colorful starburst above the city.
She decorated the invitation with a golden starburst to make it look festive.
The fireworks exploded in a brilliant starburst above the city skyline.
She decorated the invitation with a golden starburst around the event title.
A starburst of light shone from the chandelier, filling the room with sparkle.

Related Terms

  • antonym: circle - a round shape with all points equidistant from the center
  • synonym: sunburst - a design or pattern with rays radiating from a central point
