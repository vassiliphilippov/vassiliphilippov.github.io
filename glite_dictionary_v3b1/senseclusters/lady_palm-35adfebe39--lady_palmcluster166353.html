lady palm

noun
ornamental palm of the genus Rhapis
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪdi pɑm / (AmE), / ˈleɪdi pɑːm / (BrE)
a small palm of the genus Rhapis, often cultivated as a houseplant for its ornamental foliage

Examples

A lady palm plant
I bought a new lady palm to place by the window in my living room.
The lady palm is a popular choice for brightening up office spaces.
Unlike many other indoor plants, the lady palm thrives in low light conditions.
