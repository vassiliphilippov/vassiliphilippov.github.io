makara

noun
Indian zodiac sign
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌkərə / (AmE), / ˈmʌkərə / (BrE)
the zodiac sign in Indian astrology corresponding to Capricorn, often symbolized as a sea-goat

Examples

Makara zodiac sign
According to his birth chart, his zodiac sign is makara.
In Indian astrology, makara is associated with discipline and ambition.
The symbol for makara is often depicted as a creature with the body of a fish and the head of a goat.
