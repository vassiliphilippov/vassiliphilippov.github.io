fine

adjective
satisfactory; acceptable in condition
1.0
Pronunciation: / faɪn / (AmE), / faɪn / (BrE)
Satisfactory or acceptable in quality or condition; in good enough state or not causing concern.

Examples

Feeling fine
He felt tired after the race, but otherwise he was fine.
I thought the meeting went fine, even though it ran a bit long.
The doctor said her test results were fine, so there’s nothing to worry about.

Related Terms

  • synonym: all right - satisfactory; in an acceptable or good enough condition
  • degree higher: excellent - of the highest quality
  • synonym: okay - satisfactory or acceptable
  • antonym: poor - of low quality or condition; unsatisfactory
