salesmanship

noun
skill in selling or persuading
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈseɪlz.mən.ʃɪp/ (AmE), /ˈseɪlz.mən.ʃɪp/ (BrE)
the skill or ability of persuading people to buy products or services; expertise in selling

Examples

Good salesmanship skills
Despite his lack of experience, his natural salesmanship helped him close several big deals.
John's salesmanship impressed the company, and he was quickly promoted to team leader.
The art of salesmanship involves understanding customers' needs and persuading them ethically.
