Fat Tuesday

noun
the day before Lent marked by festivities
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfæt ˈtuːzdeɪ /; / fæt ˈtuːzdeɪ / (AmE), / ˌfæt ˈtjuːzdeɪ /; / fæt ˈtjuːzdeɪ / (BrE)
the day before Ash Wednesday, celebrated with feasting and festivities before Lent, also known as Mardi Gras
