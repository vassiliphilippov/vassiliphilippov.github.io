sensitometer

noun
instrument for measuring light sensitivity of film
8.1 8.8
Pronunciation: /sɛn.sɪ.ˈtɑː.mə.tər/ /; / /sən.sɪ.ˈtɑː.mə.tər/ (AmE), /sɛn.sɪ.ˈtɒm.ə.tə/ /; / /sən.sɪ.ˈtɒm.ə.tə/ (BrE)
an instrument used to measure the sensitivity of photographic film or other light-sensitive materials over a range of exposures

Examples

A sensitometer device
A sensitometer can reveal subtle changes in the sensitivity of photographic emulsions over time.
Modern sensitometers allow precise calibration of photographic equipment in a laboratory setting.
The technician used a sensitometer to ensure the film had the proper exposure range.
