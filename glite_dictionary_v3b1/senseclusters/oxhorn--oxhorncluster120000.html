oxhorn

noun
horn used for signaling
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɑksˌhɔrn / (AmE), / ˈɒksˌhɔːn / (BrE)
a horn taken from an ox, often used as a wind instrument or for signaling

Examples

Blow an oxhorn
The shepherd blew his oxhorn to call the flock back to the pen.
In ancient times, an oxhorn was used to signal the start of a meeting.
The museum displayed a beautifully carved oxhorn among its musical instruments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bugle - a simple brass instrument used for signals
  • synonym: trumpet - a brass musical instrument with a bright sound
