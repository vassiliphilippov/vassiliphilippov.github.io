candid

adjective
honest and straightforward
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkændɪd / (AmE), / ˈkændɪd /; / ˈkandɪd / (BrE)
honest and open; telling the truth without hiding anything, even if it is difficult or embarrassing

Examples

Candid opinion

His candid remarks surprised everyone at the meeting.
I appreciate your candid feedback about my presentation.
She gave a candid assessment of the project's chances of success.
