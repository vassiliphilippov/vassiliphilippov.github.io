Synonyms

  • encircle
    3.4 2/m
    encircle means to close tightly around in a clear circular line, while ring means to form a circle around something.
  • environ
    8.8 0.02/m
    environ sounds formal and means to surround completely like a wall, while ring is common and means to form a circle around.
  • surround
    1.7 50/m
    surround is a common word for being on every side, often for attack or protection, while ring means to form a circle around.
  • circle
    2.0 3/m
    circle can mean to draw or move around something without closing fully, while ring means to form and close a circle around.
