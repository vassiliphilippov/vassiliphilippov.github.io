irregular basis

noun
not happening at regular intervals
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrɛɡjələr ˈbeɪsɪs / (AmE), / ɪˈrɛɡjʊlə ˈbeɪsɪs / (BrE)
at intervals that are not consistent or predictable; not happening at regular times

Examples

On an irregular basis
Garbage collection in the area occurs on an irregular basis, which frustrates the residents.
Staff meetings are held on an irregular basis due to everyone's busy schedules.
The club meets on an irregular basis, sometimes twice a month and sometimes not at all.

Related Terms

  • antonym: regular basis - at consistent or predictable intervals
