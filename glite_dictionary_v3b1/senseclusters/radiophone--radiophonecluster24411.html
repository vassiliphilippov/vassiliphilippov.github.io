radiophone

noun
telephone using radio waves
1.0 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈreɪ.di.oʊ.foʊn/ /; / /ˈreɪ.dɪ.oʊ.foʊn/ (AmE), /ˈreɪ.di.əʊ.fəʊn/ /; / /ˈreɪ.dɪ.əʊ.fəʊn/ (BrE)
