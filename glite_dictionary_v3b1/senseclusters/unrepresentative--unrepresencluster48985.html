unrepresentative

adjective
not typical or not accurately representing
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (BrE)
Not typical of a particular group or class; not accurately representing the whole.

Examples

An unrepresentative sample
His outburst was completely unrepresentative of his usual behavior.
The committee's decisions are often seen as unrepresentative of its members.
The survey results were unrepresentative of the views of the general population.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atypical - not representative; not usual
  • antonym: representative - typical of a particular group or class
