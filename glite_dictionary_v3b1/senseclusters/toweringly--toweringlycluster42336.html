toweringly

adverb
extremely impressively
Pronunciation: / ˈtaʊərɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈtaʊərɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely impressive, tall, or outstanding

Examples

Toweringly high mountain
The mountain rose toweringly above the surrounding hills, dominating the landscape.
He was toweringly successful in his career, earning respect from everyone in his field.
Her toweringly ambitious ideas changed the direction of the entire project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exceptionally - to an unusually high degree
  • antonym: ordinarily - in a normal or usual way
  • synonym: remarkably - in a way that is worthy of attention
