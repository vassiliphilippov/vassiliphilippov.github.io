rerun

noun
repeat broadcast of a program
Pronunciation: / ˈriˌrʌn / (AmE), / ˈriːˌrʌn / (BrE)
a television or radio program that is broadcast again after its original showing

Examples

A rerun episode
Every summer, TV channels show reruns of popular sitcoms.
Most late-night programs this week are just reruns instead of new episodes.
She watched an old rerun of her favorite detective series last night.
