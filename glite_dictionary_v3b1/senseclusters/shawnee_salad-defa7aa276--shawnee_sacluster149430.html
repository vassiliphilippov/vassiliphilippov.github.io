Shawnee salad

noun
edible herb with white flowers
Pronunciation: / ʃɔːˈniː ˈsæləd / (AmE), / ʃɔːˈniː ˈsaləd / (BrE)
A showy perennial herb with white flowers; its leaves are sometimes used as edible greens in the southeastern United States.

Examples

Shawnee salad leaves
After learning to identify Shawnee salad, he began using its leaves in hearty salads.
Locals in the Southeast sometimes serve Shawnee salad as part of traditional meals.
The forager picked young Shawnee salad leaves to add to her spring greens mix.
