fluosilicate

noun
a salt of fluosilicic acid
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌfluː.oʊˈsɪl.ɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ˌfluː.əʊˈsɪl.ɪ.keɪt / (BrE)
a salt derived from fluosilicic acid, typically containing the hexafluorosilicate ion.
