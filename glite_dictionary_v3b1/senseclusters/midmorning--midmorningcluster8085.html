midmorning

noun
late morning period
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪdˈmɔrnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌmɪdˈmɔːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the period in the morning between breakfast and noon

Examples

A midmorning break
We usually take a short break around midmorning.
By midmorning, the sun was already high and the temperature had risen.
She scheduled the meeting for midmorning to give everyone time to prepare.

Related Terms

  • antonym: afternoon - the period of the day after noon
  • degree lower: early morning - the period shortly after sunrise
  • degree higher: midday - the middle of the day; noon
