lacking the basic resources, opportunities, or conditions that are considered necessary for a good quality of life or equal chance of success, especially compared to others in society.

Examples

Disadvantaged communities
Many disadvantaged communities lack access to proper healthcare facilities.
Students from disadvantaged backgrounds may receive additional financial aid.
The charity works to support disadvantaged children in inner-city schools.

Synonyms

  • underprivileged
    4.4 1.32/m
    underprivileged lacks social rights and chances, while disadvantaged focuses on missing resources, opportunities, or fair conditions compared to others.
  • less fortunate
    3.3 1.94/m
    less fortunate refers gently to people with less money, advantages, or chances, while disadvantaged highlights lack of basic resources or opportunity.
  • deprived
    3.9 1.23/m
    deprived lacks basic necessities like food, shelter, or a healthy home, while disadvantaged covers missing resources and chances compared with others.
  • needy
    1.5 3.19/m
    needy needs help and support for daily life, while disadvantaged stresses lack of broader opportunities and fair conditions in society.
