water girl

noun
female assistant providing water (in sports)
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔt̬ɚ ɡɝl / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a girl or young woman who provides drinking water to athletes or participants, typically during sporting events

Examples

The water girl helps
During halftime, the water girl made sure every player had a bottle of water.
She volunteered as the team's water girl for the whole summer season.
The water girl quickly ran onto the field when a player signaled for a drink.

Related Terms

  • synonym: water boy - a boy or young man who provides drinking water to athletes
