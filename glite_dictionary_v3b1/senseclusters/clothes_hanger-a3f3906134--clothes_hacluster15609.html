clothes hanger

noun
a device for hanging clothes
Pronunciation: / kloʊðz ˈhæŋər / (AmE), / kləʊðz ˈhæŋə / (BrE)
a frame shaped like human shoulders, used for hanging garments to keep them neat

Examples

A clothes hanger frame
