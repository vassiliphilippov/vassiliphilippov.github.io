sensibilize

verb
make sensitive or aware
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnsɪbəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsɪbɪlaɪz /; / ˈsɛnsɪbɪˌlaɪz / (BrE)
to make someone or something sensitive or aware, especially to certain stimuli or issues

Examples

To sensibilize the public
Campaigns like these help sensibilize the public to the effects of climate change.
Exposure to various cultures can sensibilize students to different perspectives.
The training program aims to sensibilize employees to the importance of workplace diversity.
The campaign aims to sensibilise the public to environmental issues.
The training sessions are designed to sensibilise staff to cultural differences in the workplace.
Through her work, she hopes to sensibilise people to the challenges faced by the disabled community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sensitize - make sensitive or aware, especially to physical or emotional stimuli
