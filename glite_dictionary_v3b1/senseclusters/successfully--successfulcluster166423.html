successfully

adverb
in a successful way
Pronunciation: / səkˈsɛs.fəli / (AmE), / səkˈsɛs.fəli / (BrE)
In a way that achieves the desired result or brings about success.

Examples

To successfully complete
After months of practice, she successfully completed her first marathon.
He negotiated the contract successfully, ensuring favorable terms for his company.
The new treatment was successfully tested on thousands of patients.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unsuccessfully - in a manner that does not bring about success
