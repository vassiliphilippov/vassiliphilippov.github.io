sandbagging

noun
flood protection activity
4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈsænd.bæɡ.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈsænd.bæɡ.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of filling, moving, or placing sandbags to protect against flooding or to create a temporary barrier or weight
