sandbagging

noun
flood protection activity
Pronunciation: / ˈsændˌbæɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsændˌbæɡɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of filling, moving, or placing sandbags to protect against flooding or to create a temporary barrier or weight

Examples

Sandbagging against floods
After heavy rain, the community organized sandbagging to protect the town from rising floodwaters.
Volunteers spent hours sandbagging the riverbank before the storm arrived.
The city provided free materials for sandbagging to help residents safeguard their homes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flood defense - measures taken to prevent or reduce flooding
  • synonym: reinforcement - the act of strengthening or supporting something
