Cotacachi

proper noun
Andean volcano in northern Ecuador
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊtəˈkɑːtʃi / (AmE), / ˌkəʊtəˈkɑːtʃi / (BrE)
an inactive volcano in northern Ecuador, part of the Andes, last known to have erupted in 1955
