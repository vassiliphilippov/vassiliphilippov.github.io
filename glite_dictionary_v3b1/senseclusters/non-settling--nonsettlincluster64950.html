non-settling

adjective
not finalized (finance)
7.7
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈsɛt̬lɪŋ / (AmE), / nɒnˈsɛtlɪŋ / (BrE)
not completed, cleared, or finalized, especially in reference to financial transactions or transfers

Examples

Non-settling payment
The bank flagged several non-settling transactions that required further investigation.
A technical error led to a number of non-settling payments at the end of the day.
Clients were notified about the non-settling status of their recent wire transfers.
