boyishness

noun
the quality of being like a boy
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔɪɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪɪʃnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being characteristic of a boy, especially in appearance or behavior

Examples

Boyishness charm
Her boyishness charmed everyone at the party, making her seem playful and approachable.
The actor was praised for bringing a sense of boyishness to his otherwise serious role.
There is a certain boyishness in his smile that never faded, even as he grew older.
