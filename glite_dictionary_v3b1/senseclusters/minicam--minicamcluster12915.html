minicam

noun
tiny medical camera
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪniˌkæm / (AmE), / ˈmɪniˌkæm / (BrE)
a very small camera, especially one used in medical procedures to view inside the body

Examples

Medical minicam
The doctor inserted a minicam to examine the patient's stomach lining during the procedure.
A minicam provided a clear view of the internal organs for the surgical team.
Technicians checked the minicam before the operation to ensure it was working properly.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: camera - a device for taking photographs or recording video
  • synonym: endoscope - a medical instrument with a camera for viewing inside the body
