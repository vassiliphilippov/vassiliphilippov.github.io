playbox

noun
box for a child's toys and things
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈpleɪˌbɒks / (BrE)
a box for storing a child's toys and personal belongings, especially used in boarding schools

Examples

A playbox for toys
After playtime, Emma neatly put her toys back in her playbox.
At the boarding school, each child was assigned a small playbox for their personal items.
He kept his favorite action figures in his playbox under the bed.
