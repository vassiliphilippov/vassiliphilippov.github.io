Filipino time

noun
tendency to be late; relaxed punctuality
Pronunciation: / fɪˈliːpinoʊ taɪm / (AmE), / fɪˈliːpiːnəʊ taɪm / (BrE)
the tendency, especially among Filipinos or in Filipino culture, to be late or have a relaxed attitude toward punctuality

Examples

Filipino time culture
Joking about Filipino time, he assured everyone there was no need to rush.
She told her guests to arrive on Filipino time so that no one would be too early.
The meeting didn't start until thirty minutes after the scheduled time, which was just Filipino time.
