social movement

noun
organized group seeking social change
1.0 2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəl ˈmuːvmənt / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəl ˈmuːvmənt / (BrE)
a large, organized group of people who work together to achieve social or political change

Examples

A social movement group
