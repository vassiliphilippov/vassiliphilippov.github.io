jump rope

noun
rope for skipping and jumping over
1.0 1.4
Pronunciation: /dʒʌmp roʊp/ (AmE), /dʒʌmp rəʊp/ (BrE)
A length of rope, often with handles at each end, used for skipping over as it is swung under the feet and over the head.

Examples

A jump rope with handles
Children were laughing and playing with a jump rope at recess.
She bought a new jump rope to practice her skipping skills at the gym.
Using a jump rope is a great way to improve cardiovascular fitness.
