vasomotion

noun
blood vessel contractions
9.9
Pronunciation: /veɪ.zə.ˈmoʊ.ʃən/ (AmE), /veɪ.zə.ˈməʊ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the rhythmic contraction and relaxation of blood vessel walls that regulates blood flow

Examples

Rhythmic vasomotion in arteries
Scientists observed vasomotion in small arteries to study how tissues receive oxygen.
Abnormal vasomotion can contribute to certain cardiovascular diseases.
The rhythmic pattern of vasomotion helps maintain steady blood flow in the body.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vasoconstriction - narrowing of blood vessels due to contraction of muscular wall
  • antonym: vasodilation - widening of blood vessels due to relaxation of muscular wall
