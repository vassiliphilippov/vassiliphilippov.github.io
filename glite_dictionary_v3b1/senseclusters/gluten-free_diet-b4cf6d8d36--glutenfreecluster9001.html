gluten-free diet

noun
diet excluding foods with gluten
2.7 2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡluːtən friː ˈdaɪət / (AmE), / ˈɡluːtən friː ˈdaɪət / (BrE)
a diet that excludes foods containing gluten, often prescribed for people with celiac disease or gluten intolerance

Examples

A gluten-free diet

Many restaurants now offer gluten-free diet options on their menus.
