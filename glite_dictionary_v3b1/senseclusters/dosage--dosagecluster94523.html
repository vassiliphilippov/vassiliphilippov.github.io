dosage

noun
amount of medicine or substance taken
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊsɪdʒ /; / ˈdoʊ.sɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdəʊsɪdʒ /; / ˈdəʊ.sɪdʒ / (BrE)
the amount of a medicine, drug, or similar substance taken or given at one time or over a period

Examples

The dosage of medicine
An incorrect dosage of the drug can be dangerous.
Be sure to follow the prescribed dosage on the label for best results.
She asked the doctor if her dosage needed to be adjusted.
Missing a dosage can reduce the effectiveness of the treatment.
The doctor increased the dosage to help manage her symptoms.
You should not take more than the recommended dosage of this medication.
