ska

noun
Jamaican music style
5.1
Pronunciation: /skɑː/ (AmE), /skɑː/ (BrE)
a lively style of Jamaican music characterized by strong rhythms, offbeat guitar chords, and brass instruments

Examples

Ska music
The energetic rhythms of ska filled the dance floor with excitement.
He learned to play the trumpet by joining a local ska band.
Many modern bands are influenced by the upbeat style of ska.
