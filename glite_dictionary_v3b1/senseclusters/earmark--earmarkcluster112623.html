earmark

noun
distinctive identifying feature
Pronunciation: / ˈɪrˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈɪəˌmɑːk / (BrE)
a distinctive mark or feature that identifies a person or thing

Examples

The earmark of quality
Patience is often considered the earmark of a true leader.
The earmark of her writing is its sharp wit and clarity.
These paintings bear every earmark of the artist's early period.
