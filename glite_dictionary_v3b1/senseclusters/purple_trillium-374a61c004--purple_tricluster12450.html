purple trillium

noun
North American plant with purple flowers
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːpəl ˈtrɪliəm / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˈtrɪliəm / (BrE)
a perennial plant of eastern North America with malodorous pink to purple flowers and an astringent root traditionally used in folk medicine to ease childbirth

Examples

Native Americans used the root of purple trillium for medicinal purposes.
