peacock

verb
show off ostentatiously
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈpiː.kɑːk/ (AmE), /ˈpiː.kɒk/ (BrE)
To behave or dress in a very showy or ostentatious way in order to attract attention or admiration.

Examples

To peacock at parties
At the party, he always peacocks, hoping to be noticed by the most important guests.
The actor was peacocking on the red carpet in a bright, sequined jacket and large hat.
She peacocked around the office after the promotion, clearly enjoying the extra attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: show off - to try to impress others by displaying one's abilities, possessions, or appearance
  • synonym: strut - to walk or behave in a proud, confident way intended to attract notice
