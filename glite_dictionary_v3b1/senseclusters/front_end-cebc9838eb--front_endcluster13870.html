front end

noun
the forward-facing or prominent side
Pronunciation: / frʌnt ɛnd / (AmE), / frʌnt end / (BrE)
the side of something that faces forward or is most prominent, especially the part that leads or is encountered first

Examples

The front end of the car
He approached the front end of the queue, ready to check out.
Please load the packages at the front end of the truck.
The front end of the car was badly damaged in the accident.
