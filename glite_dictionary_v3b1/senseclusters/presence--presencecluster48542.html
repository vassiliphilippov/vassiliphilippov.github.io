presence

noun
an invisible spiritual being felt nearby
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛzəns / (AmE), / ˈprɛz(ə)ns / (BrE)
An invisible spiritual or supernatural being that is felt to be nearby.

Examples

A spiritual presence
Many visitors report experiencing a comforting presence during their stay at the retreat.
She sensed an unsettling presence in the empty room, though she saw nothing there.
The monks claimed to feel a mysterious presence in the ancient chapel at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ghost - the soul of a dead person, appearing to the living as a visible form
  • synonym: spirit - a supernatural being or essence
