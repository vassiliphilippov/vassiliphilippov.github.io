pointe

noun
ballet on tiptoes
8.5
Pronunciation: /pɔɪnt/ /; / /pwɑːnt/ (AmE), /pɔɪnt/ /; / /pwɑːnt/ (BrE)
a ballet position or technique in which a dancer supports their body on the tips of fully extended toes, usually wearing special shoes
