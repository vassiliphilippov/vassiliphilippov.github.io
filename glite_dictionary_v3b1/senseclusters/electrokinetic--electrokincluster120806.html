electrokinetic

adjective
movement by electric field
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊkɪˈnɛtɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊkɪˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to the movement of particles or fluids caused by an electric field

Examples

Electrokinetic particle movement
Scientists studied the electrokinetic behavior of ions in the new electrolyte solution.
The electrokinetic process is important in water purification technologies.
Researchers measured the electrokinetic properties of clay particles in the experiment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electrophoretic - relating to the movement of particles in a fluid under an electric field
