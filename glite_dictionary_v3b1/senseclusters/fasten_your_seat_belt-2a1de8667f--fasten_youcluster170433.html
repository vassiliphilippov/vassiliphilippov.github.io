fasten your seat belt

verb
secure your seat belt for safety
5.0 1.5
Pronunciation: /ˈfæs.ən jɔːr siːt bɛlt/ /; / /ˈfæs.ən jʊr siːt bɛlt/ /; / /ˈfæs.ən jər siːt bɛlt/ (AmE), /ˈfɑː.sən jɔː siːt bɛlt/ /; / /ˈfɑː.sən jə siːt bɛlt/ /; / /ˈfɑː.sən jʊə siːt bɛlt/ (BrE)
to secure the belt on your seat, especially in a vehicle or aircraft, to help ensure safety during travel

Examples

To fasten your seat belt before driving
Always fasten your seat belt before starting the car, no matter how short the journey.
The flight attendant reminded everyone to fasten your seat belt when the seat belt sign was illuminated.
The pilot announced that all passengers should fasten your seat belt as turbulence was expected.
