subaverage

noun
below average person
8.3
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈævərɪdʒ / (AmE), / sʌbˈævərɪdʒ / (BrE)
a person or thing considered to be below the average level of ability, quality, or performance, often used disparagingly

Examples

A subaverage in class
He was unfairly labeled a subaverage despite his dedication to the project.
Some teachers avoid calling students subaverage to prevent discouragement.
The team was dismissed as subaverage after a series of poor performances.

Related Terms

  • antonym: average - a person or thing at the typical or expected level
  • synonym: underachiever - a person who does not perform as well as expected
