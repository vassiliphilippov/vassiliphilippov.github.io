skinny

adjective
low-calorie (informal, food)
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈskɪ.niː/ (AmE), /ˈskɪ.niː/ (BrE)
(informal, food/beverage) low in calories or prepared in a diet-friendly way; reduced-fat or reduced-sugar compared with the regular version.

Examples

Skinny latte
For a lighter option, she ordered a skinny margarita with fresh lime and sugar-free syrup.
Many cafes now offer a skinny latte made with almond milk and no added sugar.
This salad is surprisingly skinny, containing fewer calories than its creamy counterpart.

Related Terms

  • antonym: full-fat - containing the normal or original amount of fat; not reduced-fat
  • synonym: low-calorie - containing relatively few calories
