Thysanura

noun
order of primitive, wingless insects
9.9
Pronunciation: / θaɪsəˈnjʊrə / (AmE), / θɪsəˈnjʊərə / (BrE)
an order of small, primitive, wingless insects including silverfish and firebrats; now largely obsolete as a taxonomic term
