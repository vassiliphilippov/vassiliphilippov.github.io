in stock

adjective
available for sale or use
Pronunciation: / ɪn stɑk / (AmE), / ɪn stɒk / (BrE)
available for use or sale; present in the inventory of a shop or supplier

Examples

In stock items
The item you ordered is currently in stock and ready to ship.

We only display products on our website that are in stock.
