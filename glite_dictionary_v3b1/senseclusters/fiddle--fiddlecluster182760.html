fiddle

verb
alter or falsify dishonestly
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪd.əl /; / ˈfɪdəl / (AmE), / ˈfɪd.əl /; / ˈfɪdəl / (BrE)
to cheat, alter, or falsify something in a dishonest way, especially to steal money or deceive
