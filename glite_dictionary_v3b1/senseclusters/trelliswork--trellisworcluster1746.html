trelliswork

noun
lattice plant support
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛlɪsˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈtrɛlɪsˌwɜːk / (BrE)
a structure made of interwoven strips of wood, metal, or other material, used to support climbing plants or as decoration

Examples

Garden trelliswork
The old garden featured beautiful trelliswork covered in blooming wisteria.
They installed new trelliswork to help the ivy grow along the fence.
Sunlight filtered through the intricate trelliswork onto the patio below.
The ornate trelliswork on the garden gate added elegance to the entrance.
Sunlight filtered through the wooden trelliswork in the conservatory, casting patterned shadows on the floor.
The architect designed the balcony with metal trelliswork for both privacy and decoration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: framework - a basic supporting structure
  • synonym: lattice - a structure of crossed strips forming a pattern of open spaces
  • synonym: screen - a partition or divider used to separate or decorate spaces
