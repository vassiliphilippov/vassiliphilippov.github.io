broomstick

noun
handle of a broom
Pronunciation: / ˈbrumˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈbruːm.stɪk / (BrE)
the handle of a broom, typically a long, slender stick to which the brush of the broom is attached

Examples

Wooden broomstick
He crafted a rustic flagpole out of an unused broomstick.
She propped the old broom against the wall, its broomstick worn smooth from years of use.
The children pretended to ride a broomstick like witches on Halloween night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broom - a cleaning implement consisting of bristles attached to a long handle
