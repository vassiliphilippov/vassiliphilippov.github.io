preprimer

noun
beginner reading book
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈpraɪmər / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈprɪmə / (BrE)
a book or text for children who are just starting to learn how to read, usually before a primer

Examples

A preprimer book
Each child in the class received a preprimer to help them learn their first words.
The school library has a special section for preprimers aimed at early readers.
Before moving on to a primer, students practice with a preprimer to build confidence.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: primer - a basic textbook for teaching children to read
  • synonym: reader - a book for teaching reading skills
