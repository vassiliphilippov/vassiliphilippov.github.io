Cynanchum

noun
genus of tropical African climbing plants
Pronunciation: / sɪˈnæn.kəm / (AmE), / sɪˈnan.kəm / (BrE)
A genus of perennial tropical African lianas (woody climbing plants) of the family Apocynaceae.

Examples

Cynanchum climbing plants
Several medicines have been derived from plants of the Cynanchum genus.
The Cynanchum vines climbed rapidly, covering the entire fence in a season.
The rare species of Cynanchum found in the region are studied by botanists.
