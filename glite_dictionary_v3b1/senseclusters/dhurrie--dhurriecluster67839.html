dhurrie

noun
flat-woven rug
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌri /; / ˈdʊri / (AmE), / ˈdʌri / (BrE)
a flat-woven rug or mat from India, usually made of cotton or wool, used as a floor covering

Examples

A dhurrie rug
The living room was decorated with a colorful dhurrie that brightened the entire space.
She bought a handmade dhurrie from the market during her trip to India.
A large dhurrie covered the floor, providing comfort and style to the bedroom.
They placed a colorful dhurrie in the living room to brighten the space.
The artisan explained how each dhurrie is woven by hand using traditional techniques.
She bought a blue dhurrie at the market to use as a picnic blanket.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carpet - a thick woven floor covering
  • synonym: kilim - a flat-woven rug or carpet, especially from the Middle East
  • synonym: mat - a piece of material placed on a floor for protection or decoration
