Boletus subvelutipes

noun
a species of bolete fungus with a velvety stalk
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈboʊ.lɪ.təs sʌb.və.ˈluː.tɪ.piːz/ (AmE), /ˈbɒl.ɪ.təs sʌb.və.ˈluː.tɪ.piːz/ (BrE)
A species of bolete fungus distinguished by a velvety stalk, usually a dingy brown cap, and rapidly blue-staining flesh when injured.
