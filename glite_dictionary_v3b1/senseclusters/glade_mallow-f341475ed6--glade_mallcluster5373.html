glade mallow

noun
a tall American herb with white flowers
9.1
Pronunciation: / ɡleɪd ˈmæloʊ / (AmE), / ɡleɪd ˈmaləʊ / (BrE)
A tall, coarse American herb with palmate leaves and clusters of small white unisexual flowers, commonly found in alluvial soils of eastern and central United States.

Examples

A glade mallow plant
Botanists discovered a rare glade mallow specimen in the forest reserve.
The glade mallow thrives in moist areas along riverbanks and streams.
The distinctive palmate leaves help you identify the glade mallow among other wildflowers.
