unmarketable

adjective
not able to be sold
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmɑrkətəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɑːkɪtəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be sold or not suitable for sale

Examples

An unmarketable product
Because of new regulations, some products became unmarketable overnight.
If the artwork is too niche, it may be considered unmarketable by most galleries.
The damaged goods were deemed unmarketable by the store manager.
Because the manuscript was poorly written, the publisher considered it unmarketable.
Many old homes are unmarketable unless they are extensively renovated.
The damaged fruit was declared unmarketable by the grocery store manager.

  • antonym: marketable - able or fit to be sold
