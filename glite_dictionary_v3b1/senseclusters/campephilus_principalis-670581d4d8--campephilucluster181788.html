Campephilus principalis

noun
large, nearly extinct woodpecker species
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkæmpɪˈfaɪləs prɪnˈsɪpəlɪs / (AmE), / ˌkam.pɛˈfiː.ləs prɪnˈsɪ.pə.lɪs / (BrE)
a large black-and-white woodpecker native to the southern United States and Cuba, known for its ivory-colored bill and thought to be nearly extinct
