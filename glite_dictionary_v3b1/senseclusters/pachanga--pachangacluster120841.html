pachanga

noun
Cuban dance music
8.8
Pronunciation: / pəˈtʃɑːŋɡə / (AmE), / pəˈtʃæŋɡə / (BrE)
a lively style of Latin American dance music that originated in Cuba, often played at parties

Examples

Pachanga music
The crowd cheered as the band started playing a fast pachanga.
She learned to dance the pachanga at a Latin music festival.
Many parties in Cuba feature energetic pachanga music to get people moving.
