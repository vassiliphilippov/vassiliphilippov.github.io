alloimmune

adjective
immune response to others
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæloʊɪˈmjun / (AmE), / ˌaləʊɪˈmjuːn / (BrE)
relating to an immune response against antigens from another individual of the same species

Examples

An alloimmune reaction
An alloimmune reaction can occur after an organ transplant.
Doctors monitor patients for alloimmune responses following blood transfusions.
The study focused on alloimmune diseases that affect newborns.

Related Terms

  • antonym: autoimmune - relating to an immune response against one's own tissues
