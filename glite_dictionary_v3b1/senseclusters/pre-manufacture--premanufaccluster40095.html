pre-manufacture

verb
to make in advance
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈmæn.jəˌfæktʃɚ / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈmæn.jʊˌfæk.tʃə / (BrE)
to manufacture or produce something in advance, usually in a factory, for later use or assembly

Examples

To pre-manufacture parts
The company decided to pre-manufacture the components to speed up the construction process.
They pre-manufactured the window frames in the workshop before sending them to the site.
By pre-manufacturing the panels, the builders reduced the time needed on site.

Related Terms

  • antonym: assemble - to put together parts to make a finished product
  • synonym: prefabricate - to manufacture parts in advance for later assembly
