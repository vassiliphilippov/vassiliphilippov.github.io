flagger

noun
traffic signal worker
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈflæɡər / (AmE), / ˈflæɡə / (BrE)
a person who uses flags to direct traffic or people, especially at roadworks or construction sites

Examples

Road flagger
A flagger stood at the intersection, waving cars through one lane at a time.
During the road repairs, the flagger made sure drivers slowed down for workers.
The construction company hired an experienced flagger to manage traffic flow safely.
A flagger stood at the intersection, waving cars through the construction zone.
The flagger signaled the drivers to slow down as they approached the work area.
During the parade, a flagger helped guide pedestrians safely across the street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: construction worker - a person who works at building sites
  • synonym: crossing guard - a person who helps people cross the street safely
  • synonym: traffic controller - a person who manages the flow of vehicles or pedestrians
