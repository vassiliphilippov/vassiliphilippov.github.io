papular

adjective
with small skin bumps
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpæpjələr / (AmE), / ˈpapjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by papules, which are small, raised bumps on the skin

Examples

Papular rash
The doctor noted a papular rash on the patient's arms and legs.
Allergic reactions can sometimes cause a papular eruption on the skin.
A papular condition may require different treatment than a flat rash.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: macular - relating to or characterized by macules, which are flat spots on the skin
  • degree higher: nodular - relating to or characterized by nodules, which are larger lumps under the skin
