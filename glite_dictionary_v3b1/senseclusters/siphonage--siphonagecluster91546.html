siphonage

noun
illegal diversion
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪfənɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪfənɪdʒ / (BrE)
the secret or illegal transfer of money, goods, or resources, often for personal benefit

Examples

Siphonage of funds
The investigation revealed siphonage of public funds by several officials.
Strict controls were put in place to prevent siphonage of company resources.
He was arrested for his role in the siphonage of valuable equipment from the warehouse.

Related Terms

  • antonym: donation - the act of giving money or goods freely
  • synonym: embezzlement - the theft or misappropriation of funds placed in one's trust
  • synonym: theft - the act of stealing something
