curmudgeonly

adjective
surly or bad-tempered, especially in an old person
9.9
Pronunciation: / kərˈmʌdʒənli / (AmE), / kəˈmʌdʒ(ə)nli / (BrE)
Having a surly, cranky, or bad-tempered manner, especially in an old person.

Examples

A curmudgeonly old man
Despite his curmudgeonly exterior, Grandpa has a kind heart.
Her boss’s curmudgeonly response discouraged any further discussion.
The curmudgeonly neighbor yelled at the kids for playing too close to his garden.

Related Terms

  • antonym: amiable - having a friendly and pleasant manner
  • synonym: gruff - rough or surly in manner or speech
