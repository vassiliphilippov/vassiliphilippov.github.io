unnarrated

adjective
without narration
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈnɛrˌeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈnæˌreɪtɪd /; / ʌnˈnærətɪd / (BrE)
not accompanied by narration or spoken commentary; not told or described by a narrator
