bastard

noun
offensive term for a stupid or irritating person
Pronunciation: / ˈbæstərd / (AmE), / ˈbɑːstəd / (BrE)
an offensive or harsh insult for a person, used to show strong dislike or contempt

Examples

You bastard
Don't be a bastard—just tell me the truth.
He's such a bastard for tricking everyone at work.
Some bastard scratched my new car while I was inside.
After losing the contract to a rival, she muttered that he was a real bastard.
He shouted at the bastard who had keyed his car in the parking lot.
I can't believe that bastard lied to me about everything.
