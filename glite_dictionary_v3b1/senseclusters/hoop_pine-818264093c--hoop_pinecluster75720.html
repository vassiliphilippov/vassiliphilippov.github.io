hoop pine

noun
Australian/New Guinea pine with valuable wood
Pronunciation: / huːp paɪn / (AmE), / huːp paɪn / (BrE)
a pine tree of Australia and New Guinea (Araucaria cunninghamii) valued for its light, even-textured wood

Examples

Hoop pine wood
Furniture makers often prefer hoop pine because of its smooth grain.
The hoop pine is widely grown for its high-quality timber.
We saw a plantation of hoop pine trees during our visit to Queensland.
