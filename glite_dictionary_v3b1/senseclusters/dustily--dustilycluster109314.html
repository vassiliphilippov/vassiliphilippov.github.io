dustily

adverb
in a dusty way
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌstɪli / (AmE), / ˈdʌstɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is covered with or resembles dust

Examples

Light shines dustily
The sunlight streamed dustily through the old curtains, revealing particles in the air.
He coughed as he walked dustily down the abandoned, unpaved road.
The attic smelled dustily after years of being closed off from the rest of the house.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cleanly - in a clean or tidy manner
  • synonym: dirtily - in a dirty or unclean manner
