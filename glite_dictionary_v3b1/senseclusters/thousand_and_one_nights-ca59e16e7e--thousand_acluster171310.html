Thousand and One Nights

proper noun
famous collection of Arabic folktales
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌθaʊzənd ənd ˈwʌn ˈnaɪts / (AmE), / ˌθaʊzənd ənd ˈwʌn ˈnaɪts / (BrE)
A celebrated collection of Middle Eastern and South Asian folktales, originally in Arabic, dating from the 10th century.
