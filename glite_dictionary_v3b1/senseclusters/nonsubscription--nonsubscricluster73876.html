nonsubscription

noun
lack of subscription
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.səbˈskrɪp.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.səbˈskrɪp.ʃən / (BrE)
the state or condition of not subscribing to a publication, service, membership, or financial offering

Examples

Nonsubscription to a magazine
Due to nonsubscription, the magazine was unable to reach its target audience.
The service offers both subscription and nonsubscription options for users.
Her nonsubscription to the gym meant she could not access the members-only classes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: subscription - the act of signing up for a publication, service, or membership
  • synonym: unsubscribed - not enrolled or signed up for a service or publication
