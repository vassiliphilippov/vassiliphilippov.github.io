unconditionally

adverb
without any conditions or limitations
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.kənˈdɪʃ.ə.nəl.i / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kənˈdɪʃ.ən.əl.i / (BrE)
in a way that is not limited by any conditions; absolutely, without reservations

Examples

To unconditionally surrender
He unconditionally surrendered to the police after days on the run.
She promised to support him unconditionally through every challenge.
The children were loved unconditionally by their parents.
He agreed unconditionally to the terms of the contract.
She loves her children unconditionally, no matter what they do.
The charity accepts all donations unconditionally, regardless of amount.
