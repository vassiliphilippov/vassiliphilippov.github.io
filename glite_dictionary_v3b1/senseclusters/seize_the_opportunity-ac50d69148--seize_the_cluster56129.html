seize the opportunity

verb
take advantage of a chance
3.4
Pronunciation: / siz ði ˌɑːpərˈtunəti / (AmE), / siːz ði ˌɒpəˈtjuːnəti / (BrE)
to take quick action to make use of or benefit from a chance when it arises

Examples

To seize the opportunity quickly
It's important to seize the opportunity to learn new skills while you're still in school.
When tickets became available, she immediately seized the opportunity to see her favorite band live.
You have to seize the opportunity when it comes your way, or you might regret it later.
