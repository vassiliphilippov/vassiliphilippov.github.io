mountain pine

noun
a tall pine tree of western North America
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntən paɪn / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn paɪn / (BrE)
a tall pine tree native to western North America, with stout blue-green needles and mature grey-brown bark in rectangular plates

Examples

North American mountain pine
A mountain pine can be recognized by its blue-green needles and distinctive bark.
The forest was dominated by mountain pine, stretching high into the sky.
Wildfires can threaten vast stands of mountain pine in this region.
