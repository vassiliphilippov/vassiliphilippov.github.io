Bedford cord

noun
heavy ribbed fabric used for clothing
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdfərd kɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbɛdfəd kɔːd / (BrE)
a strong, heavy fabric with raised cords or ribs, similar to corduroy, used chiefly for clothing and upholstery

Examples

A Bedford cord fabric
His jacket was made from a sturdy Bedford cord, perfect for winter weather.
The tailor recommended Bedford cord for a durable pair of trousers.
Vintage Bedford cord fabrics are prized by collectors for their unique texture.
