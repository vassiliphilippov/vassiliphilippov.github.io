breakfastless

adjective
without breakfast
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛkfəstləs / (AmE), / ˈbrɛkfəstləs / (BrE)
having not eaten breakfast or without having had breakfast

Examples

Breakfastless morning
She rushed out the door breakfastless because she was late for her meeting.
After a breakfastless morning, he found it hard to concentrate in class.
Many students arrive breakfastless when they have early exams.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hungry - feeling the need for food
  • antonym: well-fed - having eaten enough food
