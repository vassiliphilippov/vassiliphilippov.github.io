traumatize

verb
cause severe emotional or psychological distress
1.0 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈtrɔː.mə.taɪz/ /; / /ˈtrɑː.mə.taɪz/ (AmE), /ˈtrɔː.mə.taɪz/ (BrE)
