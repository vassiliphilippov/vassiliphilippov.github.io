directness

noun
quality of being straightforward or on course
Pronunciation: / dəˈrɛktnəs / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛktnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of moving straight toward a goal or target without deviation

Examples

Directness of movement
A hawk's directness in flight makes it a formidable hunter.
The directness of his approach impressed the interviewers.
We admired her directness in pursuing her ambitions without hesitation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: indirectness - the quality of not being straightforward or deviating from a course
  • synonym: straightforwardness - the quality of being straightforward, frank, or direct in action or speech
