cislunar

adjective
between Earth and the Moon
Pronunciation: / sɪsˈluːnɚ / (AmE), / sɪsˈluːnə / (BrE)
Situated or occurring in the region of space between the Earth and the Moon.

Examples

A cislunar orbit
A cislunar orbit allows satellites to operate between Earth and the Moon.
NASA is planning new missions to explore the cislunar region.
Scientists track debris in the cislunar space to prevent collisions.
