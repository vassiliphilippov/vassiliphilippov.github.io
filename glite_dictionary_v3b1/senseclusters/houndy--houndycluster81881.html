houndy

adjective
like a hound
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊndi / (AmE), / ˈhaʊndi / (BrE)
resembling or having the characteristics of a hound, especially in appearance or behavior

Examples

Houndy puppy

With its long snout and droopy ears, the sculpture looked very houndy.
Her new dog has a houndy personality, always sniffing and tracking scents in the yard.
