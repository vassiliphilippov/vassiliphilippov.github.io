crystallized fruit

noun
fruit preserved with sugar syrup and crystals
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪstəˌlaɪzd frut / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstəˌlaɪzd fruːt / (BrE)
Fruit preserved by being cooked in sugar syrup and coated with sugar crystals.

Examples

Crystallized fruit in sugar
Recipes often call for crystallized fruit in traditional holiday puddings.
She decorated the cake with crystallized fruit for a festive touch.
The crystallized fruit glistened on the bakery display, tempting customers to try a piece.
