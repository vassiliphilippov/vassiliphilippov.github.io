preponderantly

adverb
mainly; to a much greater extent
8.2
Pronunciation: / prɪˈpɑndɚəntli / (AmE), / prɪˈpɒndərəntli / (BrE)
Mostly or mainly; to a much greater extent in number, influence, or importance.

Examples

Used preponderantly in numbers
Support for the project came preponderantly from younger voters.
The committee was preponderantly in favor of the new policy.
The population in this area is preponderantly rural.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mainly - chiefly; for the most part
  • synonym: predominantly - mainly; for the most part
