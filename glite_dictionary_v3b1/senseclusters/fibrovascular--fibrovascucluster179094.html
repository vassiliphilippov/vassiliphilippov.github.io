fibrovascular

adjective
fibrous and vascular
8.7
Pronunciation: /faɪ.broʊ.ˈvæs.kjə.lər/ /; / /faɪ.broʊ.ˈvæs.kjʊ.lər/ (AmE), /faɪ.brəʊ.ˈvæs.kjʊ.lə/ /; / /faɪ.brəʊ.ˈvæs.kjə.lə/ (BrE)

Examples

In botany, fibrovascular bundles are essential for the transport of water and food in plants.
