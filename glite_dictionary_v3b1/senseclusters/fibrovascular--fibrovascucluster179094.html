fibrovascular

adjective
fibrous and vascular
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪbroʊˈvæskjələr / (AmE), / ˌfɪbrəʊˈvæskjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or consisting of both fibrous and vascular tissue, especially in plants

Examples

Fibrovascular tissue
The fibrovascular tissue in the leaf helps support its structure and transport nutrients.
Doctors examined the fibrovascular membrane that had formed behind the patient's retina.
In botany, fibrovascular bundles are essential for the transport of water and food in plants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fibrous - consisting of or resembling fibers
  • synonym: vascular - relating to vessels or ducts that carry fluids
