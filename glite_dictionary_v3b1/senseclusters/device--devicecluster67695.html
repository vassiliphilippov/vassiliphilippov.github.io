device

noun
mechanical or electronic invention
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
a thing made or adapted for a particular purpose, especially a mechanical or electronic invention

Examples

Electronic device
Many household devices now connect to the Internet for greater convenience.
She attached a small tracking device to her keychain in case she lost it.
The new medical device allows doctors to monitor heart rates remotely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gadget - a small mechanical or electronic tool or device
  • synonym: machine - an apparatus using or applying mechanical power
