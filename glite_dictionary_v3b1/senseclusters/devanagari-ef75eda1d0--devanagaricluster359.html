Devanagari

noun
writing system for Sanskrit and Hindi
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdeɪvəˈnɑɡəri / (AmE), / ˌdeɪvəˈnɑːɡəri / (BrE)
a syllabic writing system used for Sanskrit, Hindi, and several other South Asian languages

Examples

Devanagari script
Many classical texts of Hindu philosophy were originally written in Devanagari script.
Modern Hindi newspapers are printed in the Devanagari alphabet.
Students learning Sanskrit often start by mastering the Devanagari script.
