provident

adjective
careful in planning for the future
1.0 6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈprɑː.vɪ.dənt/ (AmE), /ˈprɒ.vɪ.dənt/ (BrE)
careful in planning and preparing for the future, especially by saving money or making provisions

Examples

A provident person
A provident person considers how today's actions affect tomorrow's well-being.
Her provident planning ensured the family never lacked for anything during hard times.
It is wise to be provident and save some money for emergencies.
Her provident habits ensured she always had enough savings for emergencies.
It is wise to be provident and put aside money for retirement early in your career.
The provident squirrel collects extra food to prepare for the winter.

  • synonym: prudent - acting with care and thought for the future
