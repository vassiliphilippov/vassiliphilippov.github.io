Vive la différence

interjection
expression celebrating diversity or individuality
Pronunciation: / viv lɑ diːˈfɛrəns / (AmE), / viv lɑ diːˈfɛrəns / (BrE)
An expression used to celebrate or appreciate diversity, individuality, or differences between people or things.

Examples

Vive la différence phrase
At the multicultural event, speakers encouraged attendees by saying, 'Vive la différence.'
She smiled at their distinct tastes and raised her glass, saying, 'Vive la différence!'
Whenever a debate sparked over their varying opinions, someone would exclaim, 'Vive la différence!'
