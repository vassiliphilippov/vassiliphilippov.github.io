day

noun
specific time or period
Pronunciation: / deɪ / (AmE), / deɪ / (BrE)
a particular period or era in history or in someone's life, often when someone or something was most prominent or successful

Examples

In my day
People say that opportunities are different these days than they were before.
She will always remember the day she graduated from college.
Those were the days when the city was full of excitement and possibilities.
After years of hard work, he finally got his day in the spotlight.
Every artist dreams of having a day when their work is truly appreciated.
She hopes her day will come when her talent is recognized.
In her day, she was regarded as one of the most talented actors in the country.
Many think classical music reached its peak in the day of Beethoven and Mozart.
The swing era was truly the day of big bands and jazz.
