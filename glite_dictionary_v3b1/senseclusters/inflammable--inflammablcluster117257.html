inflammable

adjective
easily set on fire
5.4
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈflæməbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈflaməbəl / (BrE)
easily set on fire; readily ignited and capable of burning rapidly.

Examples

Highly inflammable materials
Because the painting supplies are highly inflammable, they must be kept in a secure cabinet.
Store all inflammable materials away from sources of heat or sparks.
The factory contained several inflammable chemicals that required special handling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flammable - easily set on fire; synonym of 'inflammable'
  • antonym: nonflammable - not easily set on fire
