  • ice-cold
    1.0 2/m
    Presents temperature as sharply and immediately very cold
    vs icy: extremely cold; as cold as ice
  • freezing
    1.0 0.00/m
    Specifies temperature at or below the point when water becomes solid
    vs icy: extremely cold, at or below the temperature at which water turns to ice
  • glacial
    6.1 0.08/m
    Evokes glacier-like cold, often formal or metaphorical
    vs icy: Extremely cold; resembling ice or glaciers in temperature.
  • frozen
    1.0 0.06/m

    vs icy: Turned into ice or affected by freezing or severe cold.
  • frosty
