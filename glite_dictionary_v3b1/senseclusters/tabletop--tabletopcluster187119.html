tabletop

noun
flat surface of a table
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪbəlˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈteɪbəltɒp / (BrE)
the flat upper surface of a table on which objects can be placed or work can be done

Examples

A tabletop surface
A glass tabletop is easy to clean and looks modern.
Please clear everything off the tabletop before setting the dishes.
The carpenter sanded the wooden tabletop until it was perfectly smooth.
