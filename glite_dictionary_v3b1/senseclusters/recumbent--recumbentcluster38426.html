recumbent

adjective
lying down; reclining
8.5
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkʌmbənt / (AmE), / rɪˈkʌmbənt / (BrE)
lying down or leaning back in a position of comfort or rest

Examples

Recumbent position
After the long hike, she lay recumbent on the grass, staring up at the clouds.
He spent the afternoon reading in a recumbent position on the couch.
The statue depicted a recumbent figure resting peacefully.

Related Terms

  • antonym: upright - vertical or standing position
