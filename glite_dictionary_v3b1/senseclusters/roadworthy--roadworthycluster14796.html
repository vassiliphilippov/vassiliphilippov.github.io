roadworthy

adjective
safe for roads
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊdˌwɜːrði / (AmE), / ˈrəʊdˌwɜːði / (BrE)

Examples

Roadworthy vehicle

Before selling a used car, you must ensure it is roadworthy according to local regulations.
The inspector declared the vehicle roadworthy after a thorough safety check.
