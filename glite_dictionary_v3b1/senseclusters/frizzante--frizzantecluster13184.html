frizzante

adjective
slightly sparkling
8.6
Pronunciation: / frɪˈzɑnteɪ / (AmE), / frɪˈzanteɪ / (BrE)
slightly sparkling, especially used to describe wines with a gentle effervescence

Examples

Frizzante wine
We ordered a bottle of frizzante wine to enjoy with our seafood dinner.
The frizzante style is perfect for those who prefer a gentle sparkle in their drink.
She prefers frizzante wines over fully sparkling ones because they are lighter and less fizzy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sparkling - containing bubbles of gas; fizzy
  • antonym: still - not sparkling; without bubbles
