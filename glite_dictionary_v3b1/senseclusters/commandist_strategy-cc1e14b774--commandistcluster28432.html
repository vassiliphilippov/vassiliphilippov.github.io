commandist strategy

noun
authoritarian leadership approach
Pronunciation: / kəˈmændɪst ˈstrætədʒi / (AmE), / kəˈmændɪst ˈstrætədʒi / (BrE)
a leadership approach that emphasizes the exercise of authority and directive control over subordinates

Examples

Commandist strategy style
Her commandist strategy left little room for team input or collaboration.
Many employees felt frustrated by his commandist strategy in decision-making.
The company's commandist strategy led to fast implementation but low morale.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authoritarian leadership - leadership style characterized by strong central control and little input from others
