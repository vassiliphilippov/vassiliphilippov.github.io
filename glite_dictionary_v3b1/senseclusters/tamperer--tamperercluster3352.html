tamperer

noun
person who compacts material
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈtæm.pər.ər/ (AmE), /ˈtæm.pə.rə/ (BrE)
a person who compacts or packs down material using a tamper tool, such as soil, gravel, or coffee grounds

Examples

A tamperer packs soil
The construction site hired a skilled tamperer to ensure the soil was properly compacted.
A tamperer is needed to press down the coffee grounds before brewing espresso.
Railway maintenance crews often include a tamperer to pack the ballast under the tracks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: packer - a person or machine that packs or compresses material
