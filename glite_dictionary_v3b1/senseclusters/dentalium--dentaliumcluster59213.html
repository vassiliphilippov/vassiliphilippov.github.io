dentalium

noun
tusk shell bead
Pronunciation: / dɛnˈteɪliəm / (AmE), / dɛnˈteɪliəm / (BrE)
the shell of a marine mollusk of the genus Dentalium, often used as a bead, ornament, or traditional currency by Indigenous peoples.

Examples

Dentalium beads
The artist decorated the regalia with rows of polished dentalium for the ceremony.
Historically, dentalium was used as a form of currency among some Native American tribes.
She admired the intricate patterns made from dentalium on the traditional dress.
