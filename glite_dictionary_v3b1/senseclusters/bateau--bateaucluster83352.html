bateau

noun
flat-bottomed boat
9.4
Pronunciation: /bə.ˈtoʊ/ /; / /bæ.ˈtoʊ/ (AmE), /bə.ˈtəʊ/ /; / /bæ.ˈtəʊ/ (BrE)
