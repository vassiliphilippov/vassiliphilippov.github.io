bateau

noun
flat-bottomed boat
9.4
Pronunciation: / bəˈtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbætəʊ /; / bəˈtəʊ /; / baˈtəʊ / (BrE)
a small, flat-bottomed boat used on rivers or lakes, often for transport, pleasure, or fishing

Examples
