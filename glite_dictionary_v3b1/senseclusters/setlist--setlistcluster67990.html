setlist

verb
to arrange concert songs
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛtˌlɪst / (AmE), / ˈsɛtˌlɪst / (BrE)
to choose, arrange, or include songs or pieces to be performed in a concert or show

Examples

To setlist a show
The band setlisted their most popular songs for the festival performance.
She was asked to setlist the opening act for the charity concert.
Before the show, the manager setlists the songs to ensure a smooth flow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: improvise - to perform without prior arrangement
  • synonym: program - to plan or arrange the order of performances
