electee

noun
person chosen by vote
6.0
Pronunciation: /ɪ.lek.ˈtiː/ /; / /ə.lek.ˈtiː/ (AmE), /ɪ.lek.ˈtiː/ /; / /ə.lek.ˈtiː/ (BrE)
