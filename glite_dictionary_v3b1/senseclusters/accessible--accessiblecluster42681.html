accessible

adjective
able to be reached or entered
3.6
Pronunciation: / əkˈsɛsəbl̩ /; / əkˈsɛs.ə.bəl / (AmE), / əkˈsɛsɪbəl /; / əkˈsɛs.ɪ.bəl / (BrE)
able to be reached, entered, obtained, or used easily

Examples

Accessible by bus
Most public libraries are accessible via public transportation.
The exhibition is accessible to visitors with disabilities.
The remote village is not easily accessible by road, especially during winter.
Many of the documents are now accessible online.
The funds are accessible to all members of the organization.
With modern banking apps, your money is always accessible when you need it.

  • antonym: inaccessible - not able to be reached or approached
