pina

noun
agave core for spirits
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːnjə / (AmE), / ˈpiːnjə / (BrE)

Examples

Agave pina roasting

Each pina can weigh up to 100 kilograms before it is cooked for tequila production.
After roasting, the sweet pina is crushed to extract its juice for fermentation.
