strategical

adjective
relating to strategy
5.6
Pronunciation: / strəˈtɪdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / strəˈtiːdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or concerned with strategy; involving the identification and achievement of long-term aims or interests.

Examples

A strategical plan
Her role in the project was mainly strategical, overseeing the entire campaign.
The general laid out a strategical plan to secure the border regions.
Their decision to relocate was based on strategical considerations, not personal preference.
