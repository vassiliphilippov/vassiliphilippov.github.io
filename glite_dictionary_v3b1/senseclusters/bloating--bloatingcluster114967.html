bloating

noun
gas buildup in animals
5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈbloʊ.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈbləʊ.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
a condition in ruminant animals where the stomach or rumen fills with gas and swells dangerously

Examples

Cow bloating
The veterinarian treated the cow for bloating after it showed signs of discomfort and swelling.
Farmers must watch for bloating in cattle, especially after they graze on fresh, wet grass.
Severe bloating can be life-threatening if not addressed quickly in ruminant animals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: colic - severe abdominal pain caused by intestinal gas or obstruction
  • synonym: tympany - swelling of the abdomen due to gas, especially in animals
