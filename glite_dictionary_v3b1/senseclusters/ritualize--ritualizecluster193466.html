ritualize

verb
make into a ritual
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪtʃuəlˌaɪz /; / ˈrɪtʃuəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃuːəlaɪz /; / ˈrɪtʃuəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make something into a ritual or give it formalized, ceremonial features

Examples

To ritualize a tradition
If we ritualize this tradition, it may become a cherished part of our family history.
Many societies ritualize important life events such as marriage and coming of age.
Some companies ritualize meetings with specific routines to foster unity.
Many societies ritualise the coming-of-age process with elaborate ceremonies.
She tends to ritualise her morning routine, turning it into a calming practice.
The annual dinner has been ritualised over the years, complete with speeches and toasts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: formalize - to make something formal, often by giving it official structure
  • synonym: ritualize - make or evolve into a ritual (American spelling)
