wryness

noun
ironic facial expression
7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈraɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈraɪ.nəs/ (BrE)
a twisted or lopsided facial expression, often showing irony, amusement, or mild annoyance

Examples

A wryness in his smile
He answered the difficult question with a hint of wryness in his voice.
Her wryness was clear when she joked about the long wait at the airport.
A subtle wryness appeared on his lips as he listened to the story.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grimace - a twisted facial expression showing pain or disgust
  • synonym: irony - the use of words to express something different from their literal meaning
  • antonym: smile - a pleased, kind, or amused facial expression
