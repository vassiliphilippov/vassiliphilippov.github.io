price

verb
set the price for something
Pronunciation: / praɪs / (AmE), / praɪs / (BrE)
to decide the amount of money that should be charged for something; to set a price for.

Examples

To price a product
Make sure you price your services competitively to attract more customers.
Some luxury brands deliberately price their products higher to create an image of exclusivity.
The shop manager carefully priced the new items before putting them on display.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appraise - to assess the value or quality of something
  • synonym: value - to determine how much something is worth
