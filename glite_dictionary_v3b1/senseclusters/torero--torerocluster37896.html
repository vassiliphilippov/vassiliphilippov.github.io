torero

noun
bullfighter in Spanish bullfighting
Pronunciation: / toʊˈreɪroʊ / (AmE), / təˈrɛərəʊ / (BrE)
A bullfighter, especially the matador or a member of the supporting team, in a traditional Spanish bullfight.

Examples

The torero fights bulls
After the performance, the torero was honored for his bravery.
Each torero wears a distinctive and ornate costume during the bullfight.
The torero entered the ring to great applause from the crowd.

Related Terms

  • synonym: matador - the main bullfighter who kills the bull
