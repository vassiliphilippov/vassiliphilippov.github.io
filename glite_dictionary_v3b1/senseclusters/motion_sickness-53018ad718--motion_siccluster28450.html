motion sickness

noun
nausea from movement while traveling
4.5 2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmoʊ.ʃən ˈsɪk.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈməʊ.ʃən ˈsɪk.nəs/ (BrE)
A condition in which a person feels dizzy or nauseous as a result of movement, especially while traveling in a vehicle, boat, or plane.

Examples

Motion sickness on boats
He always takes medicine before flights because he suffers from motion sickness.
Many passengers experienced motion sickness during the rough ferry ride across the channel.
Virtual reality headsets sometimes cause motion sickness in users due to conflicting sensory input.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sea sickness - nausea caused by movement of a boat or ship
  • synonym: travel sickness - nausea experienced when traveling by vehicle, especially in UK usage
