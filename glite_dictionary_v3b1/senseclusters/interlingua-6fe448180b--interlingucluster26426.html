Interlingua

noun
constructed international auxiliary language
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈlɪŋɡwə / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈlɪŋɡwə / (BrE)
an artificial language designed as an international auxiliary language, based on vocabulary and grammar common to English and the Romance languages

Examples

Learning Interlingua language
Learning Interlingua can be easy for speakers of Romance languages and English.
Many scientific articles were once translated into Interlingua to promote international communication.
The conference included sessions conducted in Esperanto, Interlingua, and other auxiliary languages.
