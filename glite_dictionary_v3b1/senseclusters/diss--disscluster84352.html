diss

noun
insult; insulting song (slang)
8.6
Pronunciation: / dɪs / (AmE), / dɪs / (BrE)
an insulting remark or action; (informal, especially in music) an insulting song made to call out or attack another person or artist (a 'diss track').

Examples

A diss track
The rapper dropped a brutal diss aimed at his rival during the award-show performance.
When he ignored her comment, she called it a public diss and walked away angrily.
Fans argued over whether the new song was a clever diss or just a publicity stunt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diss track - a song created to insult or criticize another artist, often in rap or hip-hop
  • synonym: insult - a remark or action intended to offend or show disrespect
  • antonym: praise - to express approval or admiration; opposite of an insult
