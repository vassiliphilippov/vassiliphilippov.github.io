noir

adjective
dark, moody style
Pronunciation: / nwɑr / (AmE), / nwɑː / (BrE)
relating to a style of film or fiction that is dark, moody, and morally ambiguous in tone

Examples

Noir style movie
The movie's noir atmosphere was created with shadowy lighting and complex characters.
She enjoys reading noir detective novels set in rain-soaked cities.
The series has a distinctly noir feel, with its cynical heroes and bleak storylines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dark - having a gloomy or mysterious atmosphere
  • synonym: gritty - showing tough or harsh realism
  • antonym: lighthearted - cheerful and not serious in tone
