paperless

adjective
without official documents
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪpərləs / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpələs / (BrE)
lacking official papers or documentation, especially identification or immigration documents

Examples

Paperless immigrants
Many paperless immigrants face difficulties finding legal employment.
The organization helps paperless individuals access basic healthcare services.
Because he was paperless, he could not apply for a driver's license.

Related Terms

  • antonym: documented - having the required official papers or identification
  • synonym: undocumented - not having the official documents needed
