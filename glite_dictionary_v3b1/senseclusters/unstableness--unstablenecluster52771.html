unstableness

noun
the state of being unstable
5.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsteɪb(ə)lnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈsteɪb(ə)lnɪs / (BrE)

Examples

Emotional unstableness
Her emotional unstableness worried her friends and family.
Political unstableness often leads to economic challenges in the country.
The unstableness of the platform made it dangerous to stand on.
