recirculation

noun
the act of circulating again
6.1 4.2
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌsɝːkjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌsɜːkjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of moving something, especially a fluid such as air or water, through a system or area again

Examples

Air recirculation system
Many modern vehicles feature recirculation of cabin air to improve heating efficiency.
The recirculation of water in the fish tank helps maintain oxygen levels for the fish.
The recirculation system ensures waste gases are reduced before being released into the environment.
