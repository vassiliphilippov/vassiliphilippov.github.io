overactive

adjective
excessively or abnormally active
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈæktɪv / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈræktɪv / (BrE)
having or showing more activity than is normal or desirable; excessively active.

Examples

An overactive child
After drinking too much soda, the child became overactive and couldn't sit still.
Doctors diagnosed her with an overactive thyroid, which explained her constant restlessness.
The school counselor recommended strategies to help manage his overactive imagination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hyperactive - abnormally or extremely active
  • antonym: underactive - less active than normal
