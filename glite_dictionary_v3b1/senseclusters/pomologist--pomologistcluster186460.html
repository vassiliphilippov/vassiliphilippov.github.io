pomologist

noun
expert in fruit tree cultivation
9.9
Pronunciation: / pəˈmɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / pəˈmɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who is knowledgeable about or specializes in the study and cultivation of fruit trees.

Examples

A pomologist studies fruit
As a pomologist, she developed new hybrids of pears and plums.
The pomologist identified the apple variety growing in the old orchard.
The agricultural conference featured a renowned pomologist as its keynote speaker.

Related Terms

  • synonym: horticulturist - a person who grows fruits, vegetables, flowers, or ornamental plants
