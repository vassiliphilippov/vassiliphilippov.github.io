American spicebush

noun
aromatic North American shrub with yellow flowers and berries
8.7
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈmer.ɪ.kən spaɪs bʊʃ/ (AmE), /ə.ˈmer.ɪ.kən spaɪs bʊʃ/ (BrE)
