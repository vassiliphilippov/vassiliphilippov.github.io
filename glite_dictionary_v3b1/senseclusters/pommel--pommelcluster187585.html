pommel

noun
handgrip on a gymnastics pommel horse
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌməl / (AmE), / ˈpʌməl / (BrE)
one of the handgrips on a pommel horse, used by gymnasts during exercise routines

Examples

Gymnastics pommel
Each pommel on the horse must be securely fastened for safety.
Newcomers often struggle to keep both hands on the pommel while swinging their legs.
The gymnast gripped the pommel tightly as he prepared for his routine.
