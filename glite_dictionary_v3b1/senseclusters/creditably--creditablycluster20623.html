Synonyms

    commendably is clearly worthy of praise and often used for strong actions, while creditably suggests doing well but not brilliantly.
  • admirably
    3.3 2/m

  • laudably
    7.2 0.04/m

  • meritoriously
    7.9 0.01/m

  • praiseworthily
    8.8 0.00/m
    praiseworthily is a rare, bookish way to show praise, while creditably is common and fits everyday speech.
  • worthily
    6.9 0.09/m
    worthily means with true merit or dignity, while creditably often means performing well enough without deep praise.
  • respectably
    3.3 0.3/m
    respectably means behaving or doing something decently and acceptably, while creditably highlights being worthy of praise or respect.
  • deservedly
