caseworker

noun
someone providing social services and support
3.9 1.55/m
Pronunciation: / Ààke…™sÀåw…úrk…ôr / (AmE), / Ààke…™sÀåw…úÀêk…ô / (BrE)
a person whose job is to provide social services and support to individuals or families in need, often by managing their cases

Examples

A caseworker helps families
As a caseworker, she helps children find safe foster homes.
The caseworker listened carefully to his client's concerns before making recommendations.
The caseworker visited the family weekly to check on their progress.

Synonyms

  • welfare worker
    3.5 0.12/m
    a welfare worker focuses on giving benefits and practical help in government or local services, while a caseworker manages individual clients' long-term cases.
  • social worker
    3.1 49/m
    a social worker offers advice and emotional support in many settings, while a caseworker mainly handles and coordinates clients' formal cases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: social worker - a professional who works to improve the well-being of individuals and communities
