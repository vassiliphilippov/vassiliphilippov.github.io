chocolate sauce

noun
sweet sauce made from chocolate or cocoa
1.0 0.34/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt É…ëkl…ôt s…îs / (AmE), / Ààt É…íkl…ôt s…îÀês / (BrE)

Examples
