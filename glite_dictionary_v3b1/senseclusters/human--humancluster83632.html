human

adjective
characteristic of people
Pronunciation: / ˈhjuːmən / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmən / (BrE)
relating to people or human beings (not animals, objects, gods, or machines)

Examples

Human behavior

The novel explores both the dark and the noble aspects of human nature.
What is considered human behavior can vary greatly across cultures.
Despite her flaws, her human kindness was evident to everyone she met.
The novel explores the human experience in extraordinary circumstances.
The statue was given a distinctly human face and posture.
Medical researchers must consider both animal and human subjects in their studies.
She has a deep interest in human behavior and psychology.
The human brain is extremely complex compared to that of other mammals.
