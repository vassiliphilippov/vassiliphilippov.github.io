pre-independence

noun
time before independence
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpriː ɪnˈdɛpəndəns / (AmE), / ˌpriː ɪnˈdɛpəndəns / (BrE)
the period or condition before a country, region, or organization becomes independent

Examples

The pre-independence era
Many traditions from the pre-independence era still influence the country's culture today.
The novel is set in a pre-independence society struggling with colonial rule.
Historians often compare pre-independence and post-independence economic policies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: colonial period - the time when a country or region was under colonial rule
  • antonym: post-independence - the period after a country or region becomes independent
