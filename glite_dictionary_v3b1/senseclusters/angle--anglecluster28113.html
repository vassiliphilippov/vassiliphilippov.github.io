angle

verb
try to obtain something indirectly
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈaŋɡl / (BrE)
to try to obtain something indirectly, often by hinting or by using devious methods

Examples

To angle for praise
He's always angling for praise from his boss.
Rather than asking directly, she angled for help with her project.
She was clearly angling for an invitation to the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fish - try to obtain something by indirect means (as in 'fish for compliments')
