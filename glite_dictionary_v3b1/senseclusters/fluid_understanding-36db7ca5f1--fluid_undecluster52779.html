fluid understanding

noun
adaptable or changing perspective
Pronunciation: / ˈfluː.ɪd ˌʌn.dərˈstænd.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfluː.ɪd ˌʌn.dəˈstænd.ɪŋ / (BrE)
a way of thinking that is adaptable and open to change rather than fixed or rigid

Examples

A fluid understanding of ideas
Educators encourage a fluid understanding of cultural norms to support diverse classrooms.
In today's world, a fluid understanding of identity is becoming more common.
The team approached the complex problem with a fluid understanding of possible solutions.
