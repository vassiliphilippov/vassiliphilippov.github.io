Synonyms

  • unweeded
    4.8 0.01/m
    unweeded means a place is not cleared of weeds and looks neglected, while overgrown means plants spread uncontrollably.
  • rampant
    6.4 0.35/m
    rampant describes plants that grow fast and spread widely, while overgrown describes land already covered by too many plants.
  • rank
    7.1 0.03/m
    rank describes vegetation that grows thick and lush in a wild way, while overgrown describes places covered by excessive plants.
  • thicketed
    5.5 0.00/m
    thicketed means an area is full of dense bushes or small trees, while overgrown can mean any unwanted plants cover land.
