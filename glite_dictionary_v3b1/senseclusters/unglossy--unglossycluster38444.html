unglossy

adjective
plain; not polished
5.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡlɑːsi / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡlɒsi / (BrE)
plain and lacking a polished, stylish, or glamorous appearance; not glossy or superficially attractive

Examples

Unglossy magazine style
The magazine's unglossy layout gave it a more authentic and approachable feel.
She preferred unglossy photographs that captured real moments rather than staged perfection.
The film's unglossy style made the story feel raw and honest.

Related Terms

  • antonym: glossy - having a shiny, polished, or glamorous appearance
  • synonym: plain - simple and without decoration or glamour
  • synonym: unpolished - not refined or finished; lacking smoothness or sophistication
