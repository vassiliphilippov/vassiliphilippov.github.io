Chilean peso

noun
the currency of Chile
3.2
Pronunciation: / tʃɪˈliən ˈpeɪsoʊ / (AmE), / tʃɪˈliːən ˈpeɪsəʊ / (BrE)
the official currency of Chile, equal to 100 centesimos

Examples

The Chilean peso
He exchanged his dollars for Chilean pesos at the airport before leaving for Santiago.
The Chilean peso has experienced significant fluctuations against the US dollar.
The price of bread has increased to 1,000 Chilean pesos this year.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: Chilean centesimo - a former subunit of the Chilean peso, equal to one hundredth of a peso
  • synonym: peso - basic monetary unit in several Latin American countries
