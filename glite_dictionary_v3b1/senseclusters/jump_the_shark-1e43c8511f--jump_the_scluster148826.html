jump the shark

verb
reach a point of decline in quality
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒʌmp ðə ʃɑrk / (AmE), / dʒʌmp ðə ʃɑːk / (BrE)
(of a television show, film series, or similar) to reach a point at which it begins to decline in quality, often through a misguided or over-the-top event.

Examples

To jump the shark moment
Critics claim the series jumped the shark with its latest, unrealistic plot twist.
Many believed the show jumped the shark after introducing new characters in its final season.
The band jumped the shark when they changed their style completely and lost their original fan base.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outstay one's welcome - continue beyond the point of being wanted or useful
  • degree lower: peak - reach the highest point before declining
