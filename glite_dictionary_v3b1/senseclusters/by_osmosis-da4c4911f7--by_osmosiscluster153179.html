by osmosis

adverb
unconsciously, by gradual absorption
9.2
Pronunciation: / baɪ ɑzˈmoʊsɪs / (AmE), / baɪ ɒzˈməʊsɪs / (BrE)
in an informal or unconscious way, especially by gradually absorbing information or habits from those around you

Examples

Learn by osmosis
He became familiar with jazz by osmosis while growing up in New Orleans.
Many employees learn the unwritten rules of the office by osmosis rather than through manuals.
She picked up the new language by osmosis just by being around her friends.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deliberately - in a purposeful or intentional way
  • synonym: intuitively - by instinct or without conscious reasoning
