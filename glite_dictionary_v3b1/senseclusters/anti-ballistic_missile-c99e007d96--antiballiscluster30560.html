anti-ballistic missile

noun
missile to shoot down ballistic missiles
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.ti bəˈlɪs.tɪk ˈmɪs.əl / (AmE), / ˌæn.ti bəˈlɪs.tɪk ˈmɪs.aɪl / (BrE)
a missile designed to intercept and destroy intercontinental ballistic missiles in flight

Examples

An anti-ballistic missile system
An anti-ballistic missile system was deployed to protect the city from potential attacks.
Development of anti-ballistic missile technology has been a major focus for military engineers.
The country invested heavily in anti-ballistic missiles as part of its defense strategy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ballistic missile - a missile that is guided in the early part of its flight but falls freely as it approaches target
