Litchi chinensis

noun
tropical tree cultivated for lychee fruit
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈliː.tʃi tʃɪˈnɛnsɪs / (AmE), / ˈlɪtʃ.i tʃɪˈnɛnsɪs / (BrE)
A tropical evergreen tree native to southeastern China, widely cultivated for its edible, oval-shaped fruit known as the lychee.
