joining

noun
act of bringing things together
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of bringing two or more things into contact or combination, especially to enable communication or unity.

Examples

The joining of companies
At the ceremony, the joining of the two companies was announced to the public.
The joining of cables must be secure to ensure a stable signal.
Their joining of hands symbolized unity and support.

Related Terms

  • synonym: connection - a link or relationship between two or more things
  • antonym: separation - the process of keeping things apart
