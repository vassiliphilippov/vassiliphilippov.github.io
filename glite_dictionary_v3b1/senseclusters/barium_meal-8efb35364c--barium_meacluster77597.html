barium meal

noun
diagnostic X-ray test using barium
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛriəm miːl / (AmE), / ˈbeəriəm miːl / (BrE)
a medical test in which a patient swallows a barium solution to allow imaging of the digestive tract on X-rays

Examples

A barium meal test
A barium meal test can help detect problems in the esophagus or stomach.
After undergoing a barium meal, he was advised not to eat for a few hours.
The doctor ordered a barium meal to get a clearer image of her stomach.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barium swallow - an X-ray test where barium is swallowed to examine the upper digestive tract
