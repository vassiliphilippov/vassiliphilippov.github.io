Easter cactus

noun
a spring-flowering South American cactus
Pronunciation: / ˈiːstɚ ˈkæktəs / (AmE), / ˈiːstə ˈkæktəs / (BrE)
a spring-flowering South American cactus with oblong joints and coral-red flowers, often cultivated as a houseplant; sometimes classified in the genus Schlumbergera

Examples

The Easter cactus flowers
My Easter cactus produces brilliant pink flowers every spring.
She gave her grandmother an Easter cactus as a birthday gift because of its beautiful blooms.
The Easter cactus prefers well-drained soil and indirect sunlight for healthy growth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Christmas cactus - a related cactus species that blooms in winter
