sympetalous

adjective
having fused petals (botanical term)
7.7 9.2
Pronunciation: /sɪm.ˈpet.ə.ləs/ /; / /səm.ˈpet.ə.ləs/ (AmE), /sɪm.ˈpet.ə.ləs/ /; / /səm.ˈpet.ə.ləs/ (BrE)
(of a flower) having petals that are joined together, forming a tubular or funnel-shaped corolla.

Examples

A sympetalous corolla
A sympetalous corolla is characteristic of numerous plant species in the bellflower family.
Many members of the morning glory family have sympetalous flowers.
The botanist noted that the bloom was sympetalous, with petals fused into a tube.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gamopetalous - having the petals united; alternative term for sympetalous
  • antonym: polypetalous - having separate, unfused petals
