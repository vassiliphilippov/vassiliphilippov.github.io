jerkingly

adverb
with sudden movements
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɜrkɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːkɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner characterized by sudden, sharp, or abrupt movements

Examples

Moves jerkingly forward
The old car moved jerkingly down the bumpy road, making everyone inside bounce.
He woke up jerkingly from his nap when the alarm suddenly rang.
The puppet danced jerkingly across the stage, amusing the children in the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abruptly - in a sudden or unexpected way
  • antonym: smoothly - in a steady and even way, without sudden movements
