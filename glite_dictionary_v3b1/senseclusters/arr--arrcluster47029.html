arr

noun
music arranger credit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɑr / (AmE), / ɑː / (BrE)
an abbreviation meaning 'arranged by', used before a person's name to credit the arranger of a musical piece

Examples

Arr Smith on music
The concert program listed 'Nocturne, arr Johnson' to show who arranged the piece.
On the album cover, you can see 'Folk Song, arr Lee' beneath the title.
The sheet music read 'Traditional, arr Martinez' to credit the arrangement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arranged by - indicates the person who adapted or arranged a musical work
