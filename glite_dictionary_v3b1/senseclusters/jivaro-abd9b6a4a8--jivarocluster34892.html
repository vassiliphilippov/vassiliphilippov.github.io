Jivaro

noun
Amazonian language
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːvəˌroʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːvərəʊ / (BrE)
a Jivaroan language spoken by indigenous peoples in parts of the Amazon region of South America

Examples

Jivaro language
She studied the Jivaro language to document its unique grammar and vocabulary.
Linguists are working to preserve Jivaro as fewer young people speak it today.
The dictionary includes many words from Jivaro that have no direct English translation.
