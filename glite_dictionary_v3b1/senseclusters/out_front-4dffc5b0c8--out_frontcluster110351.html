out front

adverb
at the front
Pronunciation: / aʊt frʌnt / (AmE), / aʊt frʌnt / (BrE)
in a position at the front of a building or outside the main entrance

Examples

Waiting out front
The delivery driver left the package out front for us to pick up.
A group of students gathered out front before the school opened.
Please wait out front and I will come get you when I am ready.

Related Terms

  • antonym: indoors - inside a building
  • synonym: outside - in the open air or exterior of a place
