beauty salon

noun
shop for hair and beauty treatments
1.7 0.46/m
Pronunciation: / Ààbjuti s…ôÀål…ën / (AmE), / ÀàbjuÀêti s…ôÀål…ín / (BrE)
a shop where people go to have beauty treatments, such as having their hair cut or styled, facials, or manicures

Examples

A beauty salon appointment
Many brides book appointments at a beauty salon before their wedding day.
She goes to the beauty salon every few weeks for a facial and a haircut.
The new beauty salon on Main Street offers excellent manicure services.

Synonyms

  • salon
    1.5 5.89/m
    salon is a short common word for a place offering hair and beauty services, while beauty salon names that kind of shop.
  • beauty shop
    1.3 0.39/m
    beauty shop often sounds more informal and local in small towns, while beauty salon suggests a wider range of professional treatments.
  • beauty parlor
    2.3 0.61/m
    beauty parlor feels somewhat old-fashioned and informal in tone, while beauty salon is the modern, neutral name for such shops.
