beauty salon

noun
shop for hair and beauty treatments
Pronunciation: / ˈbjuti səˌlɑn / (AmE), / ˈbjuːti səˌlɒn / (BrE)
a shop where people go to have beauty treatments, such as having their hair cut or styled, facials, or manicures

Examples

A beauty salon appointment
Many brides book appointments at a beauty salon before their wedding day.
She goes to the beauty salon every few weeks for a facial and a haircut.
The new beauty salon on Main Street offers excellent manicure services.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barbershop - a shop where men's hair is cut and styled
  • synonym: hair salon - a shop where hairdressers cut and style hair
