boresight

noun
sight alignment tool
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔːrˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɔːsaɪt / (BrE)
a tool or adjustment used to align the sights or aiming device of a firearm or instrument with its barrel or reference axis

Examples

Boresight tool
The technician used a boresight to ensure the rifle's scope was properly aligned before testing.
Accurate boresight adjustment is essential for hitting distant targets in competitive shooting.
She checked the boresight of the instrument to guarantee precise measurements during the experiment.
