boxful

noun
amount that fills a box
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑksˌfʊl / (AmE), / ˈbɒksˌfʊl / (BrE)
The quantity or amount that a box can contain.

Examples

A boxful of photos
He received a boxful of old photographs from his grandmother.
She carried a boxful of books up the stairs.
We ordered a boxful of oranges from the farm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bagful - amount contained in a bag
  • synonym: boxload - amount that fills a box (alternative term)
  • synonym: handful - amount that fills a hand
