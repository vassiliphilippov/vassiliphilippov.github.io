deconstructionist

noun
follower of deconstruction
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌdiːkənˈstrʌkʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌdiːkənˈstrʌkʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who analyzes texts or ideas using the theory and methods of deconstruction

Examples

A deconstructionist critic
The deconstructionist challenged traditional interpretations of the poem in her lecture.
Many deconstructionists question the idea of fixed meanings in literature.
As a deconstructionist, he often analyzes the assumptions behind everyday language.

Related Terms

  • synonym: poststructuralist - a person who follows poststructuralist theories in philosophy or literary criticism
  • antonym: structuralist - a person who analyzes cultural phenomena using structuralism
