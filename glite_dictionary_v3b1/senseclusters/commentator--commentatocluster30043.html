commentator

noun
writer or speaker on current events
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːm.ənˌteɪ.t̬ɚ /; / ˈkɑː.mənˌteɪ.t̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.ənˌteɪ.tə / (BrE)
a person who explains, analyzes, or reports on events, especially in the news or on radio or television

Examples

A commentator on TV
As a renowned commentator, she explains global events in her weekly column.
The magazine hired a new commentator to cover economic news and trends.
The political commentator wrote a detailed analysis of the upcoming election.
Political commentators discussed potential outcomes of the election live on TV.
She became a well-known commentator for her in-depth coverage of international affairs.
The sports commentator gave insight into each team's strategy during the match.
