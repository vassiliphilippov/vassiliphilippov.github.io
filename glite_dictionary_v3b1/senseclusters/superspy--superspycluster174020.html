superspy

noun
exceptional secret agent
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərspaɪ / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəspaɪ / (BrE)
a secret agent who possesses extraordinary skills, intelligence, and abilities, often featured in fiction or movies

Examples

The superspy escapes
The famous superspy used her gadgets to escape from the enemy's headquarters.
In the movie, the superspy saved the world from a dangerous villain.
Everyone admired the superspy for his quick thinking and bravery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agent - a person who acts on behalf of another, often in secret
  • degree lower: spy - a person who secretly collects information about others
