Examples

Synonyms

  • set down
    3.6 0.58/m
    set down means to place a vehicle or person gently onto a surface, while land is the general act of coming down.
  • touch down
    3.6 2.35/m
    touch down describes an aircraft or spacecraft making contact with the ground after flight, while land covers coming down or bringing something down.
