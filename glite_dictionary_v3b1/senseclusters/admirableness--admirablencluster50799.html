admirableness

noun
quality of being admirable
Pronunciation: / ˈæd.mɚ.ə.bəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈæd.mɪ.rə.b(ə)l.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of deserving respect and approval; admirable excellence

Examples

The admirableness of character
Her admirableness is evident in the way she helps everyone without expecting anything in return.
Many admired the admirableness that defined her character and inspired others.
The admirableness of his actions was acknowledged by the entire community.
