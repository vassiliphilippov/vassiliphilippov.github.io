quick trot

noun
fast-paced steady walk or run
5.9 3.4
Pronunciation: / kwɪk trɑt / (AmE), / kwɪk trɒt / (BrE)
A movement that is faster than a walk but slower than a run, usually steady and brisk.

Examples

With a quick trot, she reached the classroom just before the bell rang.
