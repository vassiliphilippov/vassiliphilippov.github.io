boofheaded remark

noun
foolish or stupid comment
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊfˌhɛdəd rɪˈmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈbuːfhɛdɪd rɪˈmɑːk / (BrE)
an informal term for a foolish or stupid comment, often used to criticize someone's lack of thought or intelligence in what they have said

Examples

A boofheaded remark made
Every time he opens his mouth, a boofheaded remark seems to come out.
She regretted her boofheaded remark as soon as the words left her lips.
The meeting was sidetracked by a boofheaded remark from one of the new interns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boneheaded remark - a foolish or stupid comment
  • antonym: witty remark - a clever or amusing comment
