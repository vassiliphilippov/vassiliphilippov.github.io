astringently

adverb
sharply or harshly
8.1
Pronunciation: / əˈstrɪndʒəntli / (AmE), / əˈstrɪndʒəntli / (BrE)
in a way that is sharp, severe, or harsh in tone or effect

Examples

Speak astringently
The teacher spoke astringently to the students about their poor behavior.
Her review of the restaurant was written astringently, highlighting every flaw.
He criticized the proposal astringently, leaving no room for misunderstanding.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gently - in a mild or kind manner
  • synonym: severely - in a very harsh or strict manner
