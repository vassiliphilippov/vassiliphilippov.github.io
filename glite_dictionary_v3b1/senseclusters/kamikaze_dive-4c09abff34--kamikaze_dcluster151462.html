kamikaze dive

noun
recklessly daring plunge
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːməˈkɑːzi daɪv / (AmE), / ˌkamɪˈkɑːzi daɪv / (BrE)
a reckless or dangerously daring plunge, often with little regard for personal safety

Examples

A kamikaze dive attack
Her kamikaze dive towards the finish line surprised everyone at the track meet.
In his desperation, he took a kamikaze dive into the freezing river to save the child.
The stuntman performed a kamikaze dive off the highest platform during the show.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cautious approach - an action done with care and deliberate thought
  • synonym: reckless plunge - an unsafe or careless leap or movement
