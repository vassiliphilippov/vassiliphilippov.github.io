bear-baiting

noun
cruel animal spectacle
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛr ˌbeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbeə ˌbeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
an old form of entertainment in which a bear was chained and attacked by dogs for public amusement

Examples

Medieval bear-baiting show
Bear-baiting was a popular attraction in England until it was outlawed in the 19th century.
Crowds would gather to watch bear-baiting, despite the suffering it caused to the animals.
The cruelty of bear-baiting led to its eventual ban and the rise of animal welfare laws.

Related Terms

  • synonym: animal cruelty - the act of causing suffering to animals
  • synonym: bull-baiting - a similar blood sport involving dogs attacking a bull
