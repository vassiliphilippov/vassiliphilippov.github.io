almirah

noun
free-standing cupboard
Pronunciation: / ælˈmɪrə / (AmE), / ælˈmɪərə / (BrE)
a free-standing cupboard or wardrobe, especially common in South Asia, used for storing clothes or valuables

Examples

A almirah for clothes
She kept her clothes neatly folded in the almirah.
The old wooden almirah in the corner belonged to my grandmother.
He locked his important documents inside the almirah for safekeeping.

Related Terms

  • synonym: closet - a small room or space for storing clothes
  • synonym: wardrobe - a large piece of furniture for storing clothes
