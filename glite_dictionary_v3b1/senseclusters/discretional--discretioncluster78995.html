discretional

adjective
subject to one's discretion or judgment
5.2
Pronunciation: / dɪˈskrɛʃənəl / (AmE), / dɪˈskrɛʃənəl / (BrE)
left to individual choice or judgment; subject to one's own discretion.

Examples

A discretional choice
Certain allowances in the budget are discretional rather than mandatory.
The funds available for travel are discretional, depending on the manager's judgment.
The judge's ruling on the matter was discretional, allowing for a range of outcomes.
