Semitist

noun
Semitic languages scholar
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛmɪtɪst / (AmE), / ˈsɛmɪtɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies or is an expert in Semitic languages or cultures

As a leading Semitist, she has published many articles on ancient Semitic scripts.
