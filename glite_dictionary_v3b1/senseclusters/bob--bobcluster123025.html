bob

verb
move up and down quickly and repeatedly
2.7 2.37/m
Pronunciation: / bÉ‘b / (AmE), / bÉ’b / (BrE)
to move up and down repeatedly in a quick, short movement

Examples

To bob up and down
A red balloon bobbed above the crowd at the festival.
She watched the fishing float bob on the surface of the lake.
The small boat bobbed up and down on the gentle waves.

Synonyms

  • bobble
    3.7 0.10/m
    bobble bounces up and down lightly and often, while bob is a quick, short up-and-down movement.
  • jiggle
    3.2 2.55/m
    jiggle moves lightly up and down or from side to side in short rapid motions, while bob is only a quick up-and-down movement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bounce - to move quickly up, back, or away from a surface after hitting it
