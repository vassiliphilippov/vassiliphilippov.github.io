marginally

adverb
to a small or slight extent
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrdʒɪnəli / (AmE), / ˈmɑːdʒɪnəli / (BrE)
to only a slight or small extent; barely or slightly

Examples

Only marginally better
Her proposal was marginally more appealing to the committee than his.
The movie was marginally interesting, but I wouldn't watch it again.
The new model is marginally faster than the previous version.

Related Terms

  • antonym: considerably - to a large or noticeable degree
  • synonym: slightly - to a small degree; a little
