uninviting

adjective
not attractive or appealing
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.ɪnˈvaɪ.tɪŋ /; / ˌʌnɪnˈvaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪnˈvaɪ.tɪŋ /; / ˌʌnɪnˈvaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
not attractive, appealing, or welcoming; not likely to draw people in

Examples

An uninviting place
She hesitated at the door, put off by the room’s uninviting atmosphere.
The restaurant looked uninviting from the outside, so we decided to eat elsewhere.
The weather was cold and uninviting, discouraging anyone from going out.
Her tone was so uninviting that I hesitated to ask further questions.
The abandoned house had an uninviting appearance, with broken windows and peeling paint.
The water looked cold and uninviting despite the warm weather.
