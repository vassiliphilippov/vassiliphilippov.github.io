lung buster

noun
very strenuous physical activity
2.0
Pronunciation: / lʌŋ ˈbʌstɚ / (AmE), / lʌŋ ˈbʌstə / (BrE)
A very strenuous or intense physical activity, especially one that requires or taxes breathing.

Examples

A lung buster workout
Cycling up that steep road was a real lung buster for the whole team.
She loves including at least one lung buster in her training sessions to push her limits.
The coach made us do a lung buster at the end of practice to build stamina.
