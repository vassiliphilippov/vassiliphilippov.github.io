desquamation

noun
shedding or peeling of outer skin
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛskwəˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛskwəˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of shedding or peeling off the outer layer of skin in the form of flakes or scales

Examples

Desquamation of skin
After the allergic reaction, there was noticeable desquamation on her hands and feet.
In certain skin diseases, desquamation can be an important diagnostic sign.
The patient experienced significant desquamation following a severe sunburn.
