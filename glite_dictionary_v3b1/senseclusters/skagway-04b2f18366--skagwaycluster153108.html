Skagway

proper noun
town in southeastern Alaska; gold rush gateway
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈskæɡ.weɪ / (AmE), / ˈskæɡ.weɪ / (BrE)
A town in southeastern Alaska, historically known as a gateway to the Klondike during the Alaska Gold Rush.

Examples

Skagway town
