lil

noun
affectionate nickname
9.8
Pronunciation: / lɪl / (AmE), / lɪl / (BrE)
an informal nickname used to refer to a small or younger person, often as a term of affection or familiarity

Examples

His friends affectionately referred to him as lil because he was the youngest in the group.
At the family reunion, the older cousins teased their lil about his height.
