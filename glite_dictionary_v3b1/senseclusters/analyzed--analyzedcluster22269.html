analyzed

adjective
examined in detail; systematically considered
Pronunciation: / ˈænəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈan.ə.laɪzd / (BrE)
examined in detail and broken down to understand constituent elements, especially in a systematic or scientific way

Examples

A analyzed report
Her thoroughly analyzed report impressed the entire committee.
Once analyzed, the document showed several inconsistencies.
The analyzed data revealed important insights into customer behavior.
