uneducated person

noun
a person lacking formal education
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈɛdʒʊˌkeɪtɪd ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈɛdjʊˌkeɪtɪd ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who has not received much or any formal education; an ignorant person

Examples

An uneducated person
As an uneducated person, he struggled to understand the official documents.
It's unfair to judge an uneducated person solely based on their lack of schooling.
The job requires skills that an uneducated person might not possess.

Related Terms

  • antonym: educated person - a person with formal education or learning
  • synonym: ignoramus - an ignorant person; someone lacking knowledge or education
