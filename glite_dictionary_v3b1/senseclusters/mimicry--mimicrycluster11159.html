mimicry

noun
resemblance for protection in animals

Examples

Animal mimicry
Many harmless butterflies use mimicry to look like poisonous species and avoid being eaten.
Scientists study mimicry to understand how animals adapt to their environment for survival.
The phenomenon of mimicry can also be seen in plants that resemble stones to escape herbivores.
