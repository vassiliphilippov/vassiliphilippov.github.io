insomniac

noun
person unable to sleep
2.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɑːmniˌæk / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɒmniˌæk / (BrE)
a person who finds it difficult or impossible to sleep, especially as a result of insomnia

Examples

A lifelong insomniac
As an insomniac, he often reads late into the night.
Many insomniacs struggle to function during the day due to lack of sleep.
She joined a support group for insomniac adults looking for advice and community.

  • synonym: insomnia - inability to sleep
