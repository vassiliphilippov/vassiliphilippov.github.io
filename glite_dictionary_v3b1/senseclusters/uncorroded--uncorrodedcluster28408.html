uncorroded

adjective
not weakened
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəˈroʊdɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəˈrəʊdɪd / (BrE)
not weakened, damaged, or destroyed; remaining strong, intact, or unaffected, often used figuratively.

Examples

Uncorroded spirit
Despite years of hardship, his uncorroded optimism inspired everyone around him.
The ancient artifact was found uncorroded after centuries buried underground.
Her trust in her friends remained uncorroded even after many disappointments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: corroded - gradually destroyed or weakened, especially by chemical action
  • synonym: intact - not damaged or impaired; complete
