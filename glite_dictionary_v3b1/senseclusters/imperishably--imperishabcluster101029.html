imperishably

adverb
in an enduring way
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɛrɪʃəbli / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɛrɪʃəbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be destroyed, forgotten, or lost

Examples

Remembered imperishably
Her generosity was remembered imperishably by all who knew her.
The artist's work lives on imperishably in the hearts of admirers.
Their friendship was imperishably marked by trust and loyalty.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eternally - in a way that lasts forever
  • synonym: permanently - in a way that lasts for a long time or forever
  • antonym: temporarily - for a limited period of time
