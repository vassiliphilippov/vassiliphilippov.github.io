knifepoint

adjective
threatened with knife
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪfpɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈnaɪfpɔɪnt / (BrE)
involving the threat or use of a knife to intimidate or force someone to do something

Examples

Knifepoint robbery
The store was robbed at knifepoint late last night.
She handed over her wallet when threatened at knifepoint in the alley.
The victim described the terrifying experience of being held at knifepoint.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gunpoint - involving the threat or use of a gun to intimidate or force someone
