stymy

verb
hinder or obstruct progress
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪmi / (AmE), / ˈstaɪmi / (BrE)

Examples

New regulations may stymy the development of innovative technology in the region.
We can't allow fear of failure to stymy our ambition.
