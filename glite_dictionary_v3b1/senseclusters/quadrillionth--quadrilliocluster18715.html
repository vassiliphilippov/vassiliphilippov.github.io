quadrillionth

noun
one part in a quadrillion
Pronunciation: / kwɑːˈdrɪljənθ / (AmE), / kwɒˈdrɪljənθ / (BrE)
Each of one quadrillion equal parts into which something is divided; the ordinal number corresponding to one divided by a quadrillion.

Examples

A quadrillionth of a second
A change as small as a quadrillionth of a second can be significant in quantum experiments.
She owned less than a quadrillionth of the company's total stock.
The measurement was accurate to a quadrillionth of a meter.
