Meuse-Argonne Offensive

noun
major World War I American military campaign
9.9
Pronunciation: /mjuːz ˈɑːr.gɑːn əf.ˈen.sɪv/ /; / /mjuːz ˈɑːr.goʊn əf.ˈen.sɪv/ (AmE), /mjuːz ˈɑː.gɑːn əf.ˈen.sɪv/ /; / /mjuːz ˈɑː.gəʊn əf.ˈen.sɪv/ (BrE)
