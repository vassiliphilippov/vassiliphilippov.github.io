bowlike

adjective
resembling a ship's prow
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈbaʊˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling the front part of a ship, especially in being pointed or projecting forward

Examples

Bowlike front
The architect designed a bowlike entrance that jutted out from the building.
Her hat had a bowlike shape that reminded everyone of a ship's prow.
The sculpture's bowlike form gave it a sense of movement and direction.

Related Terms
