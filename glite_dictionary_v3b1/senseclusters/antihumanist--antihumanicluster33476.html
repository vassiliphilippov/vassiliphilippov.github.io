antihumanist

noun
opponent of humanism
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈhjuːmənɪst / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈhjuːmənɪst / (BrE)
a person who rejects or criticizes humanist values or principles

Examples

An antihumanist thinker
The philosopher was labeled an antihumanist because of his rejection of traditional human values.
Some modern thinkers are considered antihumanists due to their criticism of human-centered ideologies.
Her essay argued from an antihumanist perspective, challenging the importance of human experience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: critic - a person who expresses disapproval or finds fault
  • antonym: humanist - a person who supports humanist values or principles
