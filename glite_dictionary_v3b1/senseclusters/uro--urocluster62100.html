uro

noun
tail prefix
Pronunciation: / ˈjʊəroʊ / (AmE), / ˈjʊərəʊ / (BrE)
a combining form from Greek meaning 'tail', used in zoological and anatomical terms to refer to the tail or tail-like structures

Examples

Uropod structure
The term uropod refers to the tail appendage of certain crustaceans.
In zoology, uro is used as a prefix for words related to tails.
The scientist explained that uro comes from the Greek word for tail.
