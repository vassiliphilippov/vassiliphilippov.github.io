desideratum

noun
something needed or desired
6.8
Pronunciation: / dɪˌsɪdəˈreɪtəm / (AmE), / dɪˌsɪdərəˈtɑːtəm / (BrE)
something that is needed or strongly desired

Examples

A desideratum for success
A reliable internet connection is now a desideratum for most remote workers.
Adequate healthcare remains a desideratum in many developing countries.
For the committee, transparency was their primary desideratum when choosing a new leader.

Related Terms

  • antonym: luxury - something that is desired but not necessary
  • synonym: requirement - something that is needed or demanded
