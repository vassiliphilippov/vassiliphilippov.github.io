rollaway

adjective
movable on wheels
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊləˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊləˌweɪ / (BrE)
designed to be easily moved by rolling on wheels or casters

Examples

Rollaway bed
The hotel provided a rollaway bed for the extra guest in our room.
We stored the rollaway table in the closet when it was not needed.
A rollaway cot is convenient for small apartments with limited space.
