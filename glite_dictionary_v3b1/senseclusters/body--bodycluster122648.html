body

noun
physical structure of an organism
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːdi / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi / (BrE)
the physical structure of a person, animal, or plant, including all its parts (sometimes excluding head and limbs)

Examples

Human body
He exercised every day to keep his body healthy and strong.
She felt a sudden chill run through her entire body.
The doctor examined the patient's body for signs of illness.
After the accident, she had injuries on her body but her head was unharmed.
During the check-up, the doctor examined his body for any bruises.
They stretched their arms above their body to relieve muscle tension.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: corpse - a dead body, especially of a human
  • degree higher: organism - an individual animal, plant, or single-celled life form
  • synonym: torso - the trunk of the human body, excluding the head and limbs
