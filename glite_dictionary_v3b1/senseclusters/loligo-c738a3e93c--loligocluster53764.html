Loligo

noun
a genus of squids
9.2
Pronunciation: / loʊˈliːɡoʊ / (AmE), / ləʊˈliːɡəʊ / (BrE)
A genus of squids commonly found in temperate and tropical seas, known for their elongated bodies and fins running along the sides.
