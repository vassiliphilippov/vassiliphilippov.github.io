fresh look

noun
a new or updated appearance
1.0
Pronunciation: / frɛʃ lʊk / (AmE), / frɛʃ lʊk / (BrE)
a new or updated appearance, especially one that is clean, modern, or revitalized

Examples

A fresh look for spring
He wanted a fresh look for the spring season, so he bought new clothes.
She gave her living room a fresh look with some new furniture and bright paint.
The website was redesigned to present a fresh look to its customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: makeover - an overall change in appearance to make something look new or improved
  • antonym: old look - an appearance that is not new or modern
  • synonym: overhaul - a thorough repair or renovation, often giving a new appearance
