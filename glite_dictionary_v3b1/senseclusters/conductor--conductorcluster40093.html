conductor

noun
person collecting fares on public transport
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈdʌk.tər/ (AmE), /kən.ˈdʌk.tə/ (BrE)
a person whose job is to collect fares and look after passengers on a public vehicle, such as a bus or train

Bus conductor

On many city buses, the conductor helps elderly passengers find a seat.
The conductor walked down the aisle collecting tickets from the passengers.
