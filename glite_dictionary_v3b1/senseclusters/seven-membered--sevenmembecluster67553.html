seven-membered

adjective
having a ring of seven atoms (chemistry)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛvən ˈmɛmbɚd / (AmE), / ˈsɛvən ˈmɛmbəd / (BrE)
(of a chemical compound) having a ring structure consisting of seven atoms or members.

Examples

A seven-membered ring
Cycloheptane is a well-known seven-membered ring compound in organic chemistry.
Researchers synthesized a seven-membered heterocycle containing both nitrogen and oxygen atoms.
The seven-membered rings have unique properties compared to those with five or six members.
