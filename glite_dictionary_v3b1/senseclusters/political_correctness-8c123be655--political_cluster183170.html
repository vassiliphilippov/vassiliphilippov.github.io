political correctness

noun
avoidance of language or actions that may offend minority groups
Pronunciation: / pəˌlɪtɪkəl kəˈrɛktnəs / (AmE), / pəˌlɪtɪkəl kəˈrɛktnəs / (BrE)
the avoidance of language or behavior that could offend or marginalize groups of people who are socially disadvantaged or discriminated against

Examples

Political correctness in speech
He was careful to use political correctness in his speech to avoid offending anyone.
Some critics argue that political correctness limits freedom of expression.
The debate about political correctness in universities continues to generate strong opinions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cultural sensitivity - awareness of and respect for cultural differences
  • antonym: offensive language - language likely to cause offense
