foliage

noun
leaves of a plant or tree
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊliɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈfəʊlɪɪdʒ / (BrE)
The leaves of a plant or tree collectively.

Examples

Green foliage

Dense foliage provided the hikers with shade from the midday sun.
The orange and red foliage made the autumn landscape particularly beautiful.
