Examples

Chemical spin-off
During the chemical process, several useful spin-offs were created alongside the main compound.
One important spin-off of the project was a more efficient method of recycling materials.
The new cleaning solvent was a spin-off from the company's research into plastics.

Synonyms

  • byproduct
    3.6 1.59/m
    a byproduct is a second material formed during production, while a spin-off names that extra product but also appears in other contexts.
  • coproduct
    7.9 0.02/m
    a coproduct is made at the same time as another product and may be equally useful, while a spin-off is a secondary product.
