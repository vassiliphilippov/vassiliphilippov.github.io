medical social worker

noun
healthcare professional assisting patients' social needs
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈsoʊʃəl ˈwɝːkər / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈsəʊʃl ˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person who provides support to patients in hospitals or healthcare settings by addressing their social, emotional, and material needs

Examples

Medical social worker helps patients
As a medical social worker, he ensures patients have access to community resources when they leave the hospital.
The medical social worker organized counseling sessions for the patient's family after her surgery.
The doctor referred the elderly patient to the medical social worker for assistance with home care arrangements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: social worker - a person who works to help people with their social problems
