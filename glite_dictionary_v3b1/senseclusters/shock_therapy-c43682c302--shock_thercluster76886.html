shock therapy

noun
treatment for mental disorders using induced shocks
1.0 4.2
Pronunciation: /ʃɑːk ˈθer.ə.piː/ /; / /ʃɔːk ˈθer.ə.piː/ /; / /ʃɑːk ˈθɜːr.ə.piː/ (AmE), /ʃɒk ˈθe.rə.pɪ/ /; / /ʃɒk ˈθɜː.rə.pɪ/ (BrE)
