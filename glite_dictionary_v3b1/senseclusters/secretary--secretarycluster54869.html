secretary

noun
writing desk with drawers and a hinged surface
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkrətɛri / (AmE), / sɪˈkrɛtəri / (BrE)
a furniture piece with a hinged writing surface and drawers, used as a writing desk, often with shelves above

Examples

Antique secretary
