ephemerality

noun
quality of being short-lived
Pronunciation: / ɪˌfɛmɚˈræləti / (AmE), / ɪˌfɛmɪˈrælɪti / (BrE)
The quality or state of lasting for a very short time.

Examples

The ephemerality of life
Many poets are fascinated by the ephemerality of youth and beauty.
Social media trends are noted for their ephemerality and rapid turnover.
The ephemerality of a rainbow makes it all the more beautiful and precious.

Related Terms

  • antonym: permanence - the state or quality of lasting or remaining unchanged
  • synonym: transience - the state or fact of lasting only for a short period
