contango

noun
upward futures curve
9.9
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈtæŋ.ɡoʊ/ (AmE), /kən.ˈtæŋ.ɡəʊ/ /; / /kɒn.ˈtæŋ.ɡəʊ/ (BrE)
a situation in financial markets where the price for a future delivery is higher than the current price or a nearer delivery price

Examples
