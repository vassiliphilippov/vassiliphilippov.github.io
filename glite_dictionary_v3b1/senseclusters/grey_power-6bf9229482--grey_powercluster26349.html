grey power

noun
political influence of older adults
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪ ˈpaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ɡreɪ ˈpaʊ.ə / (BrE)
the political and social influence exerted by older adults, particularly as a voting bloc or advocacy group

Examples

Grey power has become increasingly important as the population ages.
