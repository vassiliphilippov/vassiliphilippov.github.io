truck

noun
handcart for moving heavy items
5.7 2.5
Pronunciation: / trʌk / (AmE), / trʌk / (BrE)
a handcart with two low wheels, a bottom ledge, and handles at the top, used for moving heavy objects such as crates

Examples

A truck carries boxes
He struggled to balance the stack of chairs on the small truck.
Please load the crates onto a truck so we can take them to the back room.
The warehouse worker used a truck to move the heavy boxes across the floor.
