service entrance

noun
an entrance for staff or deliveries
3.4 0.23/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs…ùÀê.v…™s Àå…õn.tr…ôns / (AmE), / Ààs…úÀê.v…™s Àå…õn.tr…ôns / (BrE)
an entrance in a building intended for use by staff, or for deliveries and removal of refuse, rather than for the main occupants or guests

Examples

The service entrance door
Deliveries should be made through the service entrance at the back of the building.
Rubbish is always taken out via the service entrance by the maintenance team.
The hotel staff entered quietly through the service entrance to avoid disturbing guests.

Synonyms

  • service door
    3.5 0.05/m
    service door is a single door for staff, deliveries, or rubbish, while service entrance means any entrance used for those functions.
  • servant's entrance
    6.7 0.05/m
    servant's entrance is an older term for a rear door used by domestic staff, while service entrance is the modern, neutral name.
