endoscopically

adverb
using an endoscope
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɛndoʊˈskɑpɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɛndəˈskɒpɪkli / (BrE)
using an endoscope or similar device to inspect hidden or internal parts, especially in non-medical contexts.

Examples

Endoscopically inspect a pipe
The engineer inspected the inside of the pipe endoscopically to locate the source of the leak.
Difficult-to-reach engine parts were examined endoscopically without dismantling the machinery.
The technician checked the ventilation system endoscopically to ensure there were no blockages.

Related Terms

  • antonym: manually - by hand, without using a device
  • synonym: visually - by means of sight or using a camera
