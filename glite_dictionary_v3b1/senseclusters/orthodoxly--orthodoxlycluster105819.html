orthodoxly

adverb
in a traditional way
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrθəˌdɑksli / (AmE), / ˈɔːθədɒksli / (BrE)
in a manner that strictly follows traditional or established rules, beliefs, or practices

Examples

To orthodoxly follow rules
She solved the problem orthodoxly, using the standard method taught in class.
The ceremony was conducted orthodoxly, following every traditional step.
He prefers to dress orthodoxly for formal occasions, never straying from the expected attire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: traditionally - in a way that follows long-established customs or beliefs
  • antonym: unconventionally - in a way that does not follow traditional or established rules
