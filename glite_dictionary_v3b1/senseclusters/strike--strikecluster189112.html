strike

verb
smooth with a strickle (measure grain, etc.)
Pronunciation: / straɪk / (AmE), / straɪk / (BrE)
To smooth or level (grain, powder, etc.) with a strickle in order to obtain an even measure.

Examples

To strike the grain

To measure flour precisely, you should strike the top with a flat edge or strickle.
