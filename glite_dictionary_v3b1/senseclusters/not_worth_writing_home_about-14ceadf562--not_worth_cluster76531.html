not worth writing home about

adjective
not impressive or noteworthy
Pronunciation: / nɑt wɜrθ ˈraɪtɪŋ hoʊm əˌbaʊt / (AmE), / nɒt wɜːθ ˈraɪtɪŋ həʊm əˌbaʊt / (BrE)
not particularly impressive, remarkable, or noteworthy; mediocre or ordinary

Examples

Not worth writing home about performance

The concert was not worth writing home about; the band seemed tired and the songs dragged on.
The hotel had clean rooms, but the service was not worth writing home about.
