behind

adverb
(of clock) showing earlier time than correct
2.6
Pronunciation: / bɪˈhaɪnd / (AmE), / bɪˈhaɪnd / (BrE)
(of a clock or watch) showing a time earlier than the correct time.

Examples

Behind on the clock
If the kitchen clock is behind, we might miss the train.
My watch is behind by ten minutes, so I was late.
The wall clock was behind, so we reset it to the right time.
