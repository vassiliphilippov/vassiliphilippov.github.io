Chihuahua

noun
very small dog breed from Mexico
Pronunciation: / tʃəˈwɑːwə / (AmE), / tʃɪˈwɑːwə / (BrE)
A very small dog breed with a rounded skull, large eyes, and usually smooth, short hair, originating from Mexico.

Examples

A Chihuahua dog
Many people admire the loyalty and bravery of their Chihuahua.
She adopted a Chihuahua from the animal shelter last year.
The Chihuahua shivered in the cold despite wearing a tiny sweater.

Related Terms

  • synonym: toy dog - a very small dog of a breed developed to be a pet
