randomizer

noun
device for random results
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrændəˌmaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈrændəˌmaɪzə / (BrE)
a device or program that generates random results or selections

Examples

Online randomizer tool
The online raffle uses a randomizer to pick the winning ticket each week.
Before starting the quiz, the teacher used a randomizer to assign students to teams.
Many video games include a randomizer to change the order of levels or items.

Related Terms

  • synonym: random number generator - a tool that produces numbers in a random sequence
  • synonym: selector - a device or program that chooses items from a set
