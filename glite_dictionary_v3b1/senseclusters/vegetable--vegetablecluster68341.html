vegetable

noun
edible plant or plant part used as food
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛdʒ.tə.bəl /; / ˈvɛdʒtəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒ.tɪ.bəl /; / ˈvɛdʒtəb(ə)l / (BrE)
a plant or part of a plant (such as a root, stem, leaf, or fruit) that is used as food, usually not sweet and often eaten in savory dishes

Examples

Fresh vegetable salad
Broccoli is a vegetable that many children dislike, but it is very nutritious.
Eating a variety of vegetables is important for a balanced diet.
They grow fresh vegetables in their backyard garden every summer.
Carrots are a root vegetable rich in vitamins.
Eating a variety of vegetable daily is important for good health.
She made a salad with fresh vegetable from the garden.
