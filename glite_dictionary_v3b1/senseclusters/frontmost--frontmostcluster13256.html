frontmost

adjective
farthest to the front
Pronunciation: / ˈfrʌntˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈfrʌntməʊst / (BrE)
Situated at the very front; preceding all others in position.

Examples

The frontmost row
He quickly moved to the frontmost row to get a better view of the stage.
The frontmost compartment contains emergency supplies.
The frontmost seat was reserved for the guest of honor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foremost - most prominent, important, or situated at the very front
