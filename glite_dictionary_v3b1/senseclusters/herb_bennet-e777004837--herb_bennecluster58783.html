herb bennet

noun
Eurasian yellow-flowered perennial plant
Pronunciation: / hɜrb ˈbɛnɪt / (AmE), / hɜːb ˈbɛnɪt / (BrE)
a hairy Eurasian perennial plant with small yellow flowers and an astringent root formerly used in medicine

Examples

Herb bennet plant
Botanists often refer to herb bennet by its scientific name, Geum urbanum.
The roots of herb bennet were once prized for their medicinal qualities.
You can find herb bennet growing wild in woodlands and hedgerows.
