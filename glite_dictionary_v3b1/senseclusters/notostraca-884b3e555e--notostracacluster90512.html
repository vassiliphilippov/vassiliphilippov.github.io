Notostraca

noun
order of small freshwater crustaceans
Pronunciation: / ˌnoʊtəˈstreɪkə /; / ˌnoʊtəˈstrækə / (AmE), / ˌnɒtəˈstrɑːkə /; / ˌnɒtəˈstrækə / (BrE)
an order of small freshwater crustaceans with a broad, shield-shaped shell, commonly known as tadpole shrimp
