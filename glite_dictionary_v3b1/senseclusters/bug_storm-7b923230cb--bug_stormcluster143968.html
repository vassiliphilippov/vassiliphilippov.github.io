bug storm

noun
sudden surge of software errors
Pronunciation: / bʌɡ stɔrm / (AmE), / bʌɡ stɔːm / (BrE)
a sudden and overwhelming influx of software errors or issues, typically causing system instability or failures

Examples

A bug storm hits
After the update, a bug storm overwhelmed the development team with error reports.
The company has protocols in place for handling a bug storm during critical software releases.
We had to postpone the launch because a bug storm disrupted the system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: software bug - an error or flaw in computer software
  • synonym: system crash - a complete failure of a computer system
