telecomputing

noun
remote computer use
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛlɪkəmˈpjuːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪkəmˈpjuːtɪŋ / (BrE)
the use of computers and networks to access, process, or exchange data remotely

Examples

Telecomputing allows access
With the rise of telecomputing, employees can now access company files from anywhere in the world.
Many universities use telecomputing to allow students to participate in online classes and discussions.
Advances in telecomputing have made remote work much more practical.

Related Terms

  • synonym: networking - connecting computers to share data and resources
  • synonym: telecommuting - working from a remote location using telecommunications
