Synonyms

  • herbal medicine
    3.3 0.4/m
    herbal medicine uses plants or plant extracts to prevent or treat illness in a medical system, while herbalism studies and practices those uses.
  • herbal therapy
    4.2 0.01/m
    herbal therapy uses plant extracts as treatments in care or healing, while herbalism is the study and practice of those uses.
  • botanical medicine
    4.7 0.02/m
    botanical medicine treats illness with plant remedies often in formal health care, while herbalism focuses on the study and practice.
  • phytotherapy
    9.6 0.03/m
    phytotherapy uses specific plant extracts as medical treatments in clinical settings, while herbalism covers both study and practical folk use.
