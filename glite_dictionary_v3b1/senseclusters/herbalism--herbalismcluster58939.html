herbalism

noun
plant-based medicine
Pronunciation: / ˈhɜrbəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈhɜːbəlɪzəm / (BrE)
the study or practice of using plants and plant extracts for medicinal or therapeutic purposes

Examples

Herbalism practice
Many cultures have practiced herbalism for centuries to treat common illnesses.
She decided to study herbalism to learn more about natural healing methods.
Modern medicine sometimes incorporates remedies from herbalism into its treatments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: homeopathy - a system of alternative medicine using highly diluted substances
  • synonym: phytotherapy - the use of plant-derived medications in the treatment and prevention of disease
