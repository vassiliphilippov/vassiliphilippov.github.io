push people away

verb
cause others to distance themselves
Pronunciation: / pʊʃ ˈpipəl əˈweɪ / (AmE), / pʊʃ ˈpiːpl əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to behave in a way that causes others to avoid or distance themselves from you, often unintentionally

Examples

To push people away emotionally
After his mother died, he started to push people away without even realizing it.
Her constant criticism only served to push people away rather than bring them closer.
If you don't let others in, you may find yourself pushing people away.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alienate - to cause someone to feel isolated or estranged
