at stake

adverb
at risk; in question
Pronunciation: / ət steɪk / (AmE), / ət steɪk / (BrE)
at risk of being lost, affected, or won; in a situation where something important may be gained or lost

Examples

At stake in game
He realized his reputation was at stake after the mistake was made public.
The outcome of the trial will determine what is at stake for the company.
With so much money at stake, the negotiations became very intense.
Lives could be at stake if the rescue is delayed.
The championship title is at stake in tomorrow's match.
With so much money at stake, the negotiations became increasingly intense.

Related Terms

  • synonym: in jeopardy - at risk of being lost or harmed
  • antonym: safe - not at risk or danger
  • antonym: secure - not at risk; safe
