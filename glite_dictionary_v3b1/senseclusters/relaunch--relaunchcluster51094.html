  • reinitiate
    5.9 0.08/m
    reinitiate means to begin a process again, often in technical settings, while relaunch focuses on starting or reintroducing something publicly after change or failure.
  • reintroduce
    3.9 3/m
    reintroduce means to bring something back into use or attention, while relaunch often adds a public or changed restart after a stop or failure.
  • reactivate
    4.3 1/m

  • start over
