daunting

adjective
intimidating or discouraging task
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɔːntɪŋ / (BrE)
Seeming difficult to deal with or intimidating; discouraging through fear.

Examples

A daunting challenge
Climbing Mount Everest is a daunting challenge even for experienced hikers.
Starting a new job in a big city can feel daunting at first.
The daunting prospect of public speaking makes many people nervous.

Related Terms

  • antonym: encouraging - giving confidence or hope
  • synonym: intimidating - making someone feel timid or frightened
