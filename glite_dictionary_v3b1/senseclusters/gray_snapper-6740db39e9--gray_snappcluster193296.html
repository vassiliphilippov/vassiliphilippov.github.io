gray snapper

noun
a marine food fish found off Florida
9.7
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪ ˈsnæpər / (AmE), / ɡreɪ ˈsnæpə / (BrE)
A predatory marine fish of the western Atlantic (Lutjanus griseus), found in shallow waters off the coast of Florida and valued as a game and food fish.
