death tax

noun
tax on the estate of a deceased person
6.3
Pronunciation: / dɛθ tæks /; / ˈdɛθ ˌtæks / (AmE), / dɛθ tæks /; / ˈdɛθ ˌtæks / (BrE)
a tax imposed on the transfer of property or estate to heirs after someone's death (inheritance or estate tax)

  • synonym: inheritance tax - a tax paid by a person who inherits money or property from someone who has died
