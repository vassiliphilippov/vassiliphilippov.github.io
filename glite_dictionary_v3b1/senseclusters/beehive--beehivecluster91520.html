beehive

noun
busy workplace
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːˌhaɪv / (AmE), / ˈbiːˌhaɪv / (BrE)
a place, especially a workplace, where people are very busy and active

Examples

A busy beehive
The hospital is always a beehive, with staff hurrying from room to room.
The kitchen became a beehive during the holidays, with everyone preparing food.
When I visited the newsroom, it was a beehive of activity as deadlines approached.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hive - a place full of activity or busy people
