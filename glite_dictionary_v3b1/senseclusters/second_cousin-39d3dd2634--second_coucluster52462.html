second cousin

noun
child of first cousin of one's parent
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkənd ˈkʌzən / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd ˈkʌz(ə)n / (BrE)
a child of a first cousin of one's parent; a person with whom one shares great-grandparents but not grandparents

Examples

My second cousins
At the family reunion, I met several second cousins for the first time.
I discovered that John is actually my second cousin through our shared great-grandparents.
They invited their second cousin to the wedding even though they hadn't seen her in years.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: first cousin - child of an uncle or aunt; cousin whose parent is sibling of one's parent
  • degree higher: third cousin - person whose great-great-grandparent is one's ancestor in common
