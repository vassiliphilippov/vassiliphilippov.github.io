transdermal

adjective
administered through the skin
3.2
Pronunciation: / trænzˈdɜrməl / (AmE), / tranzˈdɜːməl / (BrE)
(of medication or treatment) administered or absorbed through the unbroken skin, especially in the form of creams, ointments, or patches.

Examples

Transdermal cream
A transdermal cream can be an effective way to treat localized pain.
Researchers are developing new transdermal systems for hormone delivery.
The doctor prescribed a transdermal patch to deliver the medication steadily over 24 hours.

Related Terms

  • antonym: oral - (medicine) taken by mouth (as a route of medication administration)
  • synonym: topical - (medicine) applied to a particular place on or in the body, usually the skin
