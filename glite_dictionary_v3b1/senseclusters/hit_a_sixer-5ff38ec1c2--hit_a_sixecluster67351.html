hit a sixer

verb
score six runs in cricket in one shot
4.6
Pronunciation: / hɪt ə ˈsɪksər / (AmE), / hɪt ə ˈsɪksə / (BrE)
(in cricket) to score six runs with a single shot by hitting the ball over the boundary without it touching the ground.

Examples

To hit a sixer in cricket
Every young cricketer dreams of hitting a sixer in a big match.
She managed to hit a sixer in the last over, sealing the team's victory.
The crowd erupted when he hit a sixer over the stadium wall.
