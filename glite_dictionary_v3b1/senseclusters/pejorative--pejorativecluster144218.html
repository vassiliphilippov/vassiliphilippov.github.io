pejorative

noun
negative term
Pronunciation: / pɪˈdʒɔrətɪv / (AmE), / pɪˈdʒɒrətɪv / (BrE)
a word or expression that expresses criticism, disapproval, or a negative judgment

Examples

A pejorative word
The word 'geek' was once considered a pejorative, but its meaning has changed over time.
Avoid using any pejorative when describing people from different backgrounds.
Some terms that were once neutral have become pejorative in modern language.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compliment - a polite expression of praise or admiration
  • synonym: insult - a remark or action that is rude or offensive
  • synonym: slur - an insulting or damaging remark
