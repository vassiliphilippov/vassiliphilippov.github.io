accessional

adjective
relating to accession
Pronunciation: / əkˈsɛʃənəl / (AmE), / əkˈsɛʃənəl / (BrE)
Relating to or constituting an accession; connected with the act of gaining a new position, status, or the addition of something.

Examples

An accessional ceremony
Historians debated the significance of the accessional ceremonies held for the new monarch.
The accessional protocols specified the steps required for new members to join the organization.
The treaty included several accessional clauses to cover emerging issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accessory - something that adds to or supplements something else
