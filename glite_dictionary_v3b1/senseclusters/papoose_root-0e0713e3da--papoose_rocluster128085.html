papoose root

noun
medicinal herb with berrylike fruit
8.3
Pronunciation: / pəˈpus ruːt / (AmE), / pəˈpuːs ruːt / (BrE)
A tall perennial herb native to eastern North America and Asia, having blue berrylike fruit and a thick, knotty rootstock formerly used medicinally.

Examples

Papoose root plant
Herbalists sometimes refer to papoose root as blue cohosh.
Native Americans traditionally used papoose root as a remedy for various ailments.
The papoose root blooms in early spring and produces showy blue fruits.

  • synonym: blue cohosh - another common name for the same plant
