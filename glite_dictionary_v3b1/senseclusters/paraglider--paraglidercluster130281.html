paraglider

noun
paragliding pilot
Pronunciation: / ˈpærəˌɡlaɪdər / (AmE), / ˈpærəˌɡlaɪdə / (BrE)
a person who flies a lightweight, free-flying glider with no rigid structure, used in the sport of paragliding

Examples

A skilled paraglider
The experienced paraglider launched from the cliff and glided smoothly toward the valley.
Each paraglider must check their equipment carefully before taking off.
As a skilled paraglider, she enjoys exploring new mountain sites around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pilot - a person who operates an aircraft
  • synonym: skydiver - a person who jumps from an aircraft and descends by parachute
