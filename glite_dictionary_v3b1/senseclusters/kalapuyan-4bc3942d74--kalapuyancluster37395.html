Kalapuyan

noun
a native language of the Kalapuya people
Pronunciation: / ˌkæləˈpuːjiən / (AmE), / ˌkaləˈpuːjɪən / (BrE)
the extinct Penutian language formerly spoken by the Kalapuya people of Oregon.

Examples

Kalapuyan language
Linguists have studied the Kalapuyan language to understand its place among Penutian languages.
The Kalapuyan language was spoken in the Willamette Valley before English became dominant.
Very few written records of Kalapuyan remain, making it difficult to reconstruct its vocabulary.
