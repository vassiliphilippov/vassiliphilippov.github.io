Whistlerian

adjective
in Whistler's style
Pronunciation: / wɪsˈtɪəriən / (AmE), / wɪsˈtɪəriən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the style of artist James McNeill Whistler, especially his use of subtle tones and composition

Examples

Whistlerian painting style
The artist's use of soft colors and delicate lines gave the portrait a Whistlerian elegance.
Critics praised the Whistlerian atmosphere of the landscape, noting its subtle harmony and mood.
Her latest exhibition features several Whistlerian works inspired by nocturnal city scenes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impressionistic - relating to Impressionism or its style
