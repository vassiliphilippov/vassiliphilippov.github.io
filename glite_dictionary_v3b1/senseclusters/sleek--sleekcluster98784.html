sleek

adjective
smooth, glossy, and shiny
Pronunciation: / sliːk / (AmE), / sliːk / (BrE)
having a smooth, shiny, and often lustrous surface or texture; resembling silk in softness and appearance

Examples

The cat's sleek black fur shone in the sunlight.
