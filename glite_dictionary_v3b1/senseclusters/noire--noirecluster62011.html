noire

adjective
dark or mysterious style
Pronunciation: / nwɑːr / (AmE), / nwaː / (BrE)
French for 'black', used in English to describe something with a dark, mysterious, or pessimistic style, especially in art or film.

Examples

Noire style film
The director is known for his noire approach to storytelling, filled with shadows and suspense.
Her paintings have a distinctly noire atmosphere, evoking feelings of mystery and intrigue.
Critics praised the noire elements in the novel, noting its dark and moody tone.
