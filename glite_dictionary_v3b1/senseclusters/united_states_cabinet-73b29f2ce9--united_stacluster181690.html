United States Cabinet

noun
advisory body of senior U.S. government officials
3.7
Pronunciation: / junəˌteɪtɪd steɪts ˈkæbɪnət / (AmE), / juːˌnaɪtɪd steɪts ˈkæbɪnət / (BrE)
The group of senior officials, consisting of the heads of the executive departments, who advise the President of the United States.

Examples

The United States Cabinet
As a member of the United States Cabinet, she advised the President on educational policy.
The United States Cabinet meets regularly to discuss important national issues with the President.
The structure of the United States Cabinet has evolved over the course of American history.
