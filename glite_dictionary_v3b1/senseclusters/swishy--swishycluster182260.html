swishy

adjective
making a soft, swishing sound
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪʃi / (AmE), / ˈswɪʃi / (BrE)
making or resembling a soft, swishing or whispering sound, or moving in a smooth, flowing manner.

Examples

A swishy fabric
He chose a swishy fabric for his new curtains to create a light, airy feel in the room.
The dancer wore a swishy skirt that twirled beautifully as she spun.
With the rain falling steadily, the swishy sound of the windshield wipers was oddly soothing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: quiet - making little or no noise; antonym to noisy
  • synonym: rustling - making a soft, gentle sound like leaves or fabric being moved
