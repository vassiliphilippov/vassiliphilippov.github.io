booklist

noun
list of books
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊkˌlɪst / (AmE), / ˈbʊkˌlɪst / (BrE)
a list of books, often recommended or required for a particular purpose such as study or reading

Examples

A booklist for reading
The teacher handed out a booklist for summer reading.
I checked the booklist to make sure I had all the required novels for the course.
The library published a booklist of recommended titles for young readers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reading list - a list of books to be read, often for a course or project
  • synonym: syllabus - an outline of topics and readings for a course
