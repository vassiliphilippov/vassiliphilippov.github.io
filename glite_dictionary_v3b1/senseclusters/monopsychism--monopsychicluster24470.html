monopsychism

noun
shared universal intellect
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɒnoʊˈsaɪkɪzəm /; / ˌmɑːnoʊˈsaɪkɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəˈsaɪkɪzəm / (BrE)
the philosophical doctrine that all humans share a single, universal mind, intellect, or soul
