dooring

verb
to open car door into traffic
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɔːrɪŋ / (BrE)
to cause a collision or near-collision by opening a vehicle door into the path of a cyclist or other road user

Examples

To door a cyclist
The cyclist was injured after being doored by a passenger who did not look before opening the car.
Many cities have started campaigns to prevent dooring accidents involving cyclists and parked cars.
He apologized for almost dooring the delivery rider when he got out of his vehicle.
