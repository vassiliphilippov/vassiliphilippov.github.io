frontwoman

noun
female lead singer
Pronunciation: / ˈfrʌntˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈfrʌntˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who is the lead singer or main public representative of a group, especially a band or musical group

Examples

The frontwoman sings
The talented frontwoman brought energy and charisma to every performance.
Fans cheered as the frontwoman introduced the next song with a smile.
As the frontwoman of the band, she often spoke to the media on their behalf.
The band’s talented frontwoman captivated the audience with her powerful voice.
As the frontwoman of the campaign, she appeared on television to explain their goals.
The company chose a well-known actress as its new frontwoman to improve its public image.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frontman - a man who is the lead singer or main representative of a band
  • synonym: lead singer - the main singer in a band or musical group
  • antonym: member - a person who belongs to a group or organization
  • synonym: spokesperson - someone who speaks officially for a group or organization
