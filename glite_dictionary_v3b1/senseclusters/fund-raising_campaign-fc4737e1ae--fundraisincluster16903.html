fund-raising campaign

noun
campaign to raise money for a cause
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌnˌdreɪzɪŋ kæmˈpeɪn / (AmE), / ˈfʌndˌreɪzɪŋ kamˈpeɪn / (BrE)
an organized effort to collect money for a particular cause or project

Examples

A fund-raising campaign event
After the disaster, a fund-raising campaign was set up to help those affected.
Her involvement in the fund-raising campaign inspired many others to contribute.
The school launched a fund-raising campaign to build a new library for the students.
