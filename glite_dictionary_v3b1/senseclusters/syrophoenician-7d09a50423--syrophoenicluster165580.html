Syrophoenician

adjective
of Syrophoenicia
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪroʊfəˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsaɪərəʊfəˈniːʃən / (BrE)
relating to the ancient region of Syrophoenicia or its people, as mentioned in the Bible

Examples

Syrophoenician woman
The Syrophoenician woman is an important figure in the New Testament.
Archaeologists discovered artifacts from a Syrophoenician settlement near the coast.
The Syrophoenician culture blended elements of both Syrian and Phoenician traditions.
