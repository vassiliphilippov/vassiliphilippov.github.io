thar

noun
Himalayan wild goat
Pronunciation: / θɑr / (AmE), / tɑː / (BrE)
a wild goat native to the Himalayan mountains, known for its thick coat and ability to climb steep slopes

Examples

The thar goat
The thar is well adapted to steep, rocky slopes in the Himalayas.
We spotted a herd of thar grazing on the mountain ledge during our trek.
Conservationists are working to protect the habitat of the endangered thar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goat - a domesticated or wild animal with horns and a beard
  • synonym: ibex - a wild mountain goat with large curved horns
