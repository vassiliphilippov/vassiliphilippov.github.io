communicant

noun
person entitled to receive Communion
9.9 0.29/m
Pronunciation: / k…ôÀàmjun…™k…ônt / (AmE), / k…ôÀàmjuÀên…™k…ônt / (BrE)
a person who is entitled to receive Holy Communion in a Christian church, especially one who does so regularly.

Examples

A communicant receives communion
Each communicant approached the altar rail in silent reverence during the Easter service.
Only baptized members of the church can become a communicant and receive the sacrament.
She became a regular communicant after joining the congregation last year.

Related Terms
