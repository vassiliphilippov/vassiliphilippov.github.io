elevated

adjective
having administrator privileges
7.2 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / \u02c8\u025bl\u0259\u02ccve\u026at\u026ad / (AmE), / \u02c8\u025bl\u026ave\u026at\u026ad / (BrE)
(computing) having higher-than-normal access rights or privileges (for example, administrator or root permissions) that allow making system-level changes.

Examples

Elevated permissions
The installer requires elevated privileges to modify system files safely.
After authentication, the technician's account was temporarily elevated to perform the system update.
Only processes started from the administrator prompt run with elevated rights on this server.

Related Terms

  • synonym: privileged - having special access rights or permissions that normal users do not have.
  • antonym: unprivileged - lacking elevated access rights; restricted from performing system-level actions.
