hospital attendant

noun
hospital worker for non-medical duties
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈhɑː.spɪ.təl ə.ˈten.dənt/ (AmE), /ˈhɒs.pɪ.təl ə.ˈten.dənt/ (BrE)
a person employed in a hospital whose duties involve the care of patients and general maintenance, but do not include providing medical treatment

Examples

The hospital attendant helps patients
She thanked the hospital attendant for helping her family bring in the luggage.
The hospital attendant guided visitors to their rooms and answered questions in the hallway.
The hospital attendant's responsibilities included changing linens and assisting with patient transportation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nurse - a person trained to care for the sick, contrasting with a non-medical attendant
  • synonym: orderly - a hospital worker whose job includes cleaning and moving patients, similar to a hospital attendant
