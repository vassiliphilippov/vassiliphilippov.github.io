undeservingness

noun
lack of worthiness
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈzɜrvɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈzɜːvɪŋnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of not being worthy of something, such as praise, reward, or kindness

Examples

Feelings of undeservingness
She struggled with feelings of undeservingness after being offered the scholarship.
His sense of undeservingness made it hard for him to accept compliments from others.
Therapy helped him overcome the undeservingness he felt about his success.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unworthiness - the state of not being worthy or deserving
  • antonym: worthiness - the quality of being deserving or having value
