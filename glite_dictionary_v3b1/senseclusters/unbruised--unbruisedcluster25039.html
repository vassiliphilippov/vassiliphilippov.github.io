unbruised

adjective
not harmed or damaged
4.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbruːzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbruːzd / (BrE)
not harmed, damaged, or affected, especially emotionally or in reputation, by criticism or negative events

Examples

Unbruised confidence
Despite the harsh criticism, his confidence remained unbruised throughout the ordeal.
She walked away from the heated debate with her reputation unbruised.
After the difficult negotiations, the company's image was surprisingly unbruised.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unharmed - not injured or damaged
  • synonym: unscathed - not hurt or damaged
