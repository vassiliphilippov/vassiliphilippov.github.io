crankily

adverb
in an odd way
Pronunciation: / ˈkræŋkɪli / (AmE), / ˈkræŋkɪli / (BrE)
in a strange, eccentric, or unpredictable manner; behaving or acting oddly.

Examples

Crankily decorates a room
He decorated his office crankily, with mismatched chairs and bright, clashing colors everywhere.
The old inventor crankily explained his theories, jumping from one idea to another without warning.
She always organizes her books crankily, so no one else can find anything.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eccentrically - in a manner that is unusual or unconventional
  • antonym: normally - in a usual or expected way
