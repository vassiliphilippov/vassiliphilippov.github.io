taproom

noun
bar or room serving alcoholic drinks
Pronunciation: / ˈtæpˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈtæp.ruːm / (BrE)
a room or establishment where alcoholic drinks are served, typically over a counter

Examples

A taproom bar
After work, we met at the local taproom for a couple of drinks and some conversation.
The brewery recently opened a new taproom in the city center, offering seasonal beers.
This historic building once housed a popular taproom where townspeople would gather each evening.
