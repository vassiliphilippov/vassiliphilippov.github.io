charra

noun
traditional garment from Latin American cultures
Pronunciation: / tʃɑːˈrɑː / (AmE), / tʃɑːˈrɑː / (BrE)
a traditional garment, typically elaborately decorated, worn in some Latin American cultures, especially in Mexico

Examples

A charra dress
He wore a charra to celebrate his heritage at the festival.
Many dancers wore a colorful charra costume during the performance.
The charra is often adorned with intricate embroidery and silver details.
