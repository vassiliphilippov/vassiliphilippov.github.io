eternize

verb
make eternal or famous forever
4.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈtɜrnaɪz /; / ɪˈtɝː.naɪz / (AmE), / ɪˈtɜːnʌɪz /; / ɪˈtɜː.nʌɪz /; / ɪˈtɜːnaɪz / (BrE)
to make something last forever or immortalize, especially in memory, art, or fame

Examples

The grand monument was built to eternise the founder's name.
The poet hoped his verses would eternise the hero's deeds.
