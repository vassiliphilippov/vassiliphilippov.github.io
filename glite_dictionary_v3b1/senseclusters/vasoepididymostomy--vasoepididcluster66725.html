vasoepididymostomy

noun
male fertility surgery
9.9
Pronunciation: /veɪ.soʊ.ep.ɪ.dɪ.ˈdɪm.oʊ.stə.miː/ (AmE), /veɪ.səʊ.ep.ɪ.dɪ.ˈdɪm.ə.stəʊ.miː/ (BrE)
a surgical procedure in which the vas deferens is connected to the epididymis to restore fertility in men

Examples

A vasoepididymostomy surgery
After a failed vasectomy reversal, the surgeon performed a vasoepididymostomy to help the patient regain fertility.
A vasoepididymostomy is often recommended when there is a blockage in the epididymis.
The couple was hopeful after learning that a vasoepididymostomy could increase their chances of having children.
