gonad

noun
sex cell-producing gland (ovary or testis)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊ.næd / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊ.næd / (BrE)
A gland in animals that produces gametes (sex cells), such as an ovary or testis.

Examples

The gonads produce cells
A doctor may examine the gonads to assess reproductive health.
In mammals, the gonad develops early in embryonic life.
Ovaries and testes are examples of gonad organs in different sexes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ovary - the female gonad producing eggs
  • synonym: testis - the male gonad producing sperm
