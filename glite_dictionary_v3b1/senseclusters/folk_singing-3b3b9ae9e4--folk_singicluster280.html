folk singing

noun
singing folk songs
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊk ˌsɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfəʊk ˌsɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or style of singing traditional folk songs, often reflecting cultural or regional heritage

Examples

Folk singing style

The festival featured workshops on folk singing from different cultures around the world.
Many people enjoy folk singing as a way to connect with their heritage.
