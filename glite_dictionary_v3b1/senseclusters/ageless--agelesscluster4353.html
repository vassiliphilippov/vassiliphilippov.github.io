ageless

adjective
not aging
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪdʒləs / (AmE), / ˈeɪdʒləs / (BrE)
not appearing to grow old or not affected by the passage of time

Examples

Ageless beauty
The actress is known for her ageless appearance, looking the same for decades.
Some stories have an ageless quality that appeals to every generation.
He admired the ageless beauty of the ancient temple ruins.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ancient - very old; from a long time ago
  • synonym: eternal - lasting forever; without end
  • synonym: timeless - not affected by the passage of time; always fashionable or relevant
  • degree lower: youthful - having the qualities of youth; young in appearance or spirit
