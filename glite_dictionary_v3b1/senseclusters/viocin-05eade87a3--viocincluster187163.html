Viocin

noun
antibiotic used to treat tuberculosis
9.9
Pronunciation: / vaɪˈoʊsɪn / (AmE), / vaɪˈəʊsɪn / (BrE)
A basic polypeptide antibiotic (trade name) administered, often with other drugs, in the treatment of tuberculosis.

Examples
