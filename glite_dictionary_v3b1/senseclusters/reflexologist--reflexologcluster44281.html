reflexologist

noun
foot or hand therapist
Pronunciation: / ˌriːflɛkˈsɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌriːflɛkˈsɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person trained to apply pressure to specific areas of the feet or hands as a form of therapy

Examples

The reflexologist applies pressure
The reflexologist explained how pressure points in the feet could affect overall health.
After a long week, he booked an appointment with a reflexologist for relaxation.
Many people visit a reflexologist to help manage stress and improve well-being.

Related Terms

  • antonym: client - a person who receives professional services, such as therapy
  • synonym: massage therapist - a person trained to manipulate muscles and soft tissues for therapeutic purposes
