fenugreek seed

noun
aromatic spice seed used in cooking
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛnjəˌɡrik siːd / (AmE), / ˈfɛnjʊˌɡriːk siːd / (BrE)
the aromatic seeds of the fenugreek plant, used as a spice and flavoring, especially in curries and other Asian dishes

Examples

Ground fenugreek seed
She ground the fenugreek seed before adding it to the curry sauce.
The recipe calls for a teaspoon of fenugreek seed to enhance the flavor.
Traditional remedies sometimes include fenugreek seed to aid digestion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cumin seed - aromatic seeds used as spice, commonly in curries
  • synonym: mustard seed - pungent seeds used as a spice or condiment
