stalker

noun
person who hunts game by stealth
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔkər / (AmE), / ˈstɔːkə / (BrE)
a person who hunts animals by following them stealthily; someone who stalks game

Examples

A stalker hunts animals
As a skilled stalker, he knew how to blend in with his surroundings to avoid being noticed by animals.
The experienced stalker moved silently through the forest, searching for deer.
The royal estate hired a stalker to manage the deer population on the grounds.
