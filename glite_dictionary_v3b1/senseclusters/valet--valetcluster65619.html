valet

verb
to park a car for someone
5.5
Pronunciation: / væˈleɪ / (AmE), / ˈvælɪt / (BrE)
to park or take care of a car for someone as a service, especially at a hotel or restaurant

Examples

To valet a car

She always prefers to valet her car at upscale restaurants for convenience.
