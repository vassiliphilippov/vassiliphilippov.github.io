basinal

adjective
relating to a basin
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪsɪnəl / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsɪnəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a basin, especially in a geological context.

Examples

Basinal sediments
Geologists studied the basinal sediments to learn more about ancient environments.
Oil can accumulate in basinal areas where organic material settles over time.
The region's basinal structure influences both its water flow and its soil types.

Related Terms

  • synonym: basin - a large, natural or artificial depression in the surface of the land or seabed; also, a container for holding liquid
