vocal

adjective
relating to singing or the voice
Pronunciation: / ˈvoʊkəl / (AmE), / ˈvəʊkəl / (BrE)
relating to the singing voice, or designed for singing; involving or produced by the voice in singing

Examples

Vocal performance
He joined a vocal group specializing in jazz harmonies.
She has impressive vocal skills that captivate the audience.
The choir performed a challenging vocal arrangement at the concert.

Related Terms

  • antonym: instrumental - relating to music performed on musical instruments rather than sung
