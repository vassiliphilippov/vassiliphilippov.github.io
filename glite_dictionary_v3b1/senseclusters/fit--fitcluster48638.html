fit

noun
the manner something fits, especially clothing
3.5
Pronunciation: / fɪt / (AmE), / fɪt / (BrE)
the way in which something, especially a garment, fits a person or object; the degree of correct sizing or shaping

Examples

A good fit
She was pleased with the fit of the new dress she bought online.
The fit of these shoes is too tight for my feet.
The tailor ensured the fit of the suit was perfect for the client.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cut - the style or shape in which a garment is made
  • degree lower: size - the dimensions or magnitude of something, especially clothing
