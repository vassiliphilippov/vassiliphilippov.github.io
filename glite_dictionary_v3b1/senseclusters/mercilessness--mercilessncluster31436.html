mercilessness

noun
lack of mercy or compassion
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmɝː.sɪl.ɪs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmɜː.sɪl.ɪs.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of having no mercy or compassion; complete lack of pity or kindness

Examples

Complete mercilessness
Despite his opponent's pleas, the boxer fought with complete mercilessness.
Many were shocked by the mercilessness of the punishment imposed on the prisoners.
The dictator's mercilessness was evident in how he treated dissenters.
She was appalled by his utter mercilessness toward those in need.
The mercilessness of the regime shocked the world.
The film portrays the mercilessness of war in haunting detail.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mercy - compassion or forgiveness shown toward someone
  • synonym: pitilessness - the quality of having no pity; synonym for mercilessness
  • synonym: ruthlessness - the quality of being without pity or compassion
