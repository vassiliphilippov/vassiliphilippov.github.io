yeo

noun
small river (dialect)
9.9
Pronunciation: / joʊ / (AmE), / jəʊ / (BrE)
an old or dialect word for a small river or stream, especially in parts of England

Examples

A small yeo
The old map showed a yeo running along the edge of the village.
Children played by the yeo on warm summer afternoons in the countryside.
A narrow wooden bridge crossed the gentle yeo near the farm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: creek - a narrow, small river
  • degree higher: river - a large natural stream of water
