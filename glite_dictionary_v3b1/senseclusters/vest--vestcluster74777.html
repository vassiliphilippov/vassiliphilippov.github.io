vest

noun
sleeveless garment worn under a coat
1.9 1.3
Pronunciation: /vest/ (AmE), /vest/ (BrE)
A sleeveless garment worn by men under a coat as part of a suit, typically buttoned down the front.

Examples

A suit vest
After removing his coat, he adjusted his vest before sitting down.
He wore a crisp white vest under his tailored suit.
The groom's vest matched the color of his tie.

Related Terms

  • synonym: waistcoat - a sleeveless, buttoned garment worn over a shirt and under a jacket (British English)
