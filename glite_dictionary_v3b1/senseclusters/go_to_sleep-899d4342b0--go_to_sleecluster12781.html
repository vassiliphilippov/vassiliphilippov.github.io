go to sleep

verb
begin or prepare to sleep
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ tə sliːp / (AmE), / ɡəʊ tə sliːp / (BrE)
to begin sleeping or prepare to sleep, usually by lying down and closing your eyes

Examples

To go to sleep at night
After a long day, he couldn't wait to go to sleep.
She always reads a book before she goes to sleep.
The children were told to go to sleep early because of the trip tomorrow.
After a long day of hiking, I couldn't wait to go to sleep early.
He tends to go to sleep on the couch while watching TV.
If you go to sleep too late, you'll feel tired in the morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fall asleep - to enter a state of sleep
  • antonym: wake up - to cease sleeping; to become awake
