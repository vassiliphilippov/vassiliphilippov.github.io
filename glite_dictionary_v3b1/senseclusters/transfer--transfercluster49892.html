transfer

verb
change ownership
4.4
Pronunciation: / trænˈsfɚ / (AmE), / trænsˈfɜː / (BrE)
to officially change the ownership of something from one person or entity to another

Examples

To transfer property
The company decided to transfer all its assets to a new subsidiary.
Upon his retirement, he transferred the deed of the house to his daughter.
You must complete the paperwork to transfer the car ownership to your name.
