encounter

noun
unexpected or casual meeting
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkaʊntər /; / ɪnˈkaʊntɚ / (AmE), / ɪnˈkaʊntə / (BrE)
a meeting with someone or something, especially one that happens unexpectedly or by chance

Examples

An encounter with a stranger
During his hike, he had an unexpected encounter with a wild deer.
Our first encounter was completely by accident at the grocery store.
She described her encounter with the famous singer as brief but memorable.
A brief encounter in the hallway left a lasting impression on him.
She had an awkward encounter with her old boss at the supermarket.
Their first encounter was entirely by chance at a coffee shop.
