version

noun
an account from a specific viewpoint
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɝːʒən / (AmE), / ˈvɜːʃən / (BrE)
a particular form or account of something, as described or understood from a specific viewpoint

Examples

His version of events
Each witness gave a different version of the incident to the police.
His version of the story changed every time he told it.
I’d like to hear your version of what happened.

Related Terms

  • synonym: account - a report or description of an event or experience
  • antonym: fact - a thing that is known or proved to be true (often contrasted with version/interpretation)
  • synonym: narrative - a spoken or written account of connected events; a story
