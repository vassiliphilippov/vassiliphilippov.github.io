odious

adjective
extremely unpleasant; hateful
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊdiəs / (AmE), / ˈəʊdiəs / (BrE)
Extremely unpleasant and causing or deserving hatred or strong dislike.

Examples

An odious smell
She found his remarks so odious that she walked out of the meeting.
The company faced criticism for its odious labor practices overseas.
The dictator's odious policies caused widespread suffering.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abhorrent - inspiring disgust and loathing; repugnant
  • synonym: detestable - deserving intense dislike or hatred
  • antonym: pleasant - giving a sense of happy satisfaction or enjoyment
