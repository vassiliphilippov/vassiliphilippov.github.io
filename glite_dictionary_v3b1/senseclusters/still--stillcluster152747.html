still

adjective
not sparkling (of a drink)
3.2 0.33/m
Pronunciation: / stÉªl / (AmE), / stÉªl / (BrE)
(of a drink) not sparkling; lacking bubbles or effervescence, especially in reference to wine or water.

Examples

Still water
I prefer still water to sparkling when I eat out.
Many people enjoy a glass of still wine with their meal.
Please specify if you would like your mineral water still or sparkling.

Synonyms

  • uncarbonated
    3.3 0.01/m
    uncarbonated means a drink has no dissolved gas and appears flat, while still refers to non-sparkling water or wine.
  • noneffervescent
    9.8 0.00/m
    noneffervescent sounds formal and rare, while still is the common everyday word for non-sparkling drinks.
