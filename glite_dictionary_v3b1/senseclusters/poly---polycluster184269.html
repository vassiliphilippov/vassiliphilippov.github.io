poly-

x
prefix meaning many
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑli / (AmE), / ˈpɒli / (BrE)
a prefix meaning 'many' or 'more than one', used at the start of words

Examples

Poly- prefix meaning many
The word poly- is used in 'polygon' to mean a shape with many sides.
Scientists use poly- in terms like 'polyatomic' to describe molecules with many atoms.
Adding the prefix poly- to 'glot' forms 'polyglot', meaning a person who knows many languages.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mono- - prefix meaning one or single
  • synonym: multi- - prefix meaning many or much
