off

adjective
spoiled, unpalatable (of food/drink)
4.9
Pronunciation: / ɔf / (AmE), / ɒf / (BrE)
(of food or drink) no longer fresh and unpleasant to taste or smell; spoiled.

Examples

Off milk
Don't eat those eggs—they look a bit off.
I realized the meat was off when I tasted it.
The milk has gone off and smells sour.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fresh - (of food) recently made or obtained; not spoiled or stale
