peacock-blue

adjective
bright greenish-blue color
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːkɑk bluː / (AmE), / ˈpiːkɒk bluː / (BrE)
A bright greenish-blue color, resembling that of a peacock's neck and tail feathers.

Examples

A peacock-blue tie
He selected a peacock-blue tie to add a pop of color to his suit.
She wore a stunning dress in peacock-blue to the gala event.
The peacock-blue walls gave the living room a vibrant and lively atmosphere.
