vog

noun
volcanic air pollution
Pronunciation: / vɑɡ / (AmE), / vɒɡ / (BrE)
air pollution formed when volcanic gases, especially sulfur dioxide, mix with moisture and dust in the air

Examples

Thick vog pollution
After the volcano erupted, thick vog covered the nearby towns for days.
People with asthma are advised to stay indoors when vog levels are high.
The weather report warned of increased vog due to volcanic activity on the island.

Related Terms

  • synonym: haze - reduced visibility caused by fine particles in the air
  • synonym: smog - fog or haze combined with smoke and other pollutants
