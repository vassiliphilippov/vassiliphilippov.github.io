uppityness

noun
arrogant or presumptuous behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpətinəs / (AmE), / ˈʌpɪtinəs / (BrE)
the quality of behaving in a way that is considered arrogant or presumptuous, especially by acting above one's perceived social position

Examples

The uppityness attitude
Despite his background, he was often chastised for his uppityness and refusal to conform to traditional expectations.
Her uppityness made her unpopular among her colleagues, who saw her as pretentious.
Some critics accused the new manager of uppityness after she made sweeping changes to the company procedures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrogance - an attitude of superiority and self-importance
  • antonym: humility - modesty and lack of pride
