subtheme

noun
secondary theme
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌθim / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌθiːm / (BrE)
a secondary or supporting theme or idea that is part of a larger main theme or topic

Examples

Subtheme of friendship
The film's subtheme of friendship adds depth to the main story.
In her essay, she analyzed a subtheme that many readers often overlook.
The teacher asked the class to identify a subtheme in the poem besides its central message.
The essay discusses several subthemes that support the main argument.
In the film, friendship and loyalty are important subthemes beneath the central plot.
Teachers often ask students to identify a subtheme in a story to deepen their understanding.

Related Terms

  • synonym: motif - a recurring element or idea in a work
  • degree higher: theme - the main subject or idea in a work or discussion
