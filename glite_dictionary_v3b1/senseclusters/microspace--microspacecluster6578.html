microspace

noun
small digital environment
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌspeɪs / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌspeɪs / (BrE)
a small virtual or digital environment within software, a game, or an online platform where users can interact or perform activities

Examples

Online microspace
Each player can customize their own microspace in the online game.
The app allows users to create a private microspace for group discussions.
Teachers use a microspace to organize virtual classrooms and activities.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: metaverse - a collective virtual shared space
  • synonym: virtual space - a computer-generated environment where users can interact
