delectably

adverb
pleasantly or deliciously
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlɛktəbli / (AmE), / dɪˈlɛktəbli / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely pleasant, delicious, or delightful

Examples

A delectably spiced dish
The chef prepared a delectably spiced curry that everyone enjoyed.
She smiled delectably as she tasted the first bite of chocolate cake.
The air was delectably scented with fresh bread from the bakery.
