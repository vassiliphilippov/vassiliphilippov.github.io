plate

noun
the amount held by a plate
Pronunciation: / pleɪt / (AmE), / pleɪt / (BrE)
an amount of food or other substance served on a plate; the quantity that a plate can hold

Examples

A plate of food
After exercising, I finished an entire plate of fruit in minutes.
He ate a huge plate of spaghetti for dinner.
She brought out a plate of freshly baked cookies for everyone to share.

Related Terms

  • synonym: serving - an amount of food that is given to one person at one time
