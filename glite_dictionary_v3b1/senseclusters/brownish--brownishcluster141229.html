brownish

adjective
somewhat brown in color
Pronunciation: / ˈbraʊnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbraʊnɪʃ / (BrE)
having a color that is somewhat or slightly brown

Examples

A brownish color
She wore a brownish scarf that matched her boots perfectly.
The leaves turned a brownish hue as autumn approached.
The water looked brownish after the heavy rain.
