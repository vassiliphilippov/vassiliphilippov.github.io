Emperor Meiji

proper noun
emperor of Japan (1867–1912) who led modernization
Pronunciation: / ɛmˈpɪrər meɪˈdʒi / (AmE), / ɛmˈpɪərə ˈmeɪdʒi / (BrE)
the emperor of Japan from 1867 to 1912, known for leading the country's modernization and westernization during the Meiji period
