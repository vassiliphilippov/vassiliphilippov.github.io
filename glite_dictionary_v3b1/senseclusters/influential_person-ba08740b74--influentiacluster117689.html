influential person

noun
person with significant influence
1.0 2.7
Pronunciation: /ɪn.fluː.ˈen.ʃəl ˈpɜːr.sən/ (AmE), /ɪn.fluː.ˈen.ʃəl ˈpɜː.sən/ (BrE)

Examples
