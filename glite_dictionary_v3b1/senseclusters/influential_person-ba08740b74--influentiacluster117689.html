influential person

noun
person with significant influence
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnfluˈɛn(t)ʃ(ə)l ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ˌɪnfluˈɛn(t)ʃ(ə)l ˈpɜːs(ə)n / (BrE)
a person whose actions and opinions have a strong effect on the behavior or decisions of others or on events

Examples

An influential person in community
As an influential person in the community, her support was crucial to the project's success.
He aspired to become an influential person in the world of finance.
The magazine published a list of the most influential persons of the year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: follower - a person who accepts the leadership or opinions of another
  • synonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group
