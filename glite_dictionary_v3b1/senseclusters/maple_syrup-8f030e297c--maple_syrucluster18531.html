maple syrup

noun
sweet syrup from sugar maple sap
3.3 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈmeɪ.pəl ˈsɪ.rəp/ /; / /ˈmeɪ.pəl ˈsɜː.rəp/ (AmE), /ˈmeɪ.pəl ˈsɪ.rəp/ /; / /ˈmeɪ.pəl ˈsɜː.rəp/ (BrE)
