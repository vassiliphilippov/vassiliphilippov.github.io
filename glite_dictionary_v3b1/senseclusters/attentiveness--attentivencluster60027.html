attentiveness

noun
AI focus on relevant input
Pronunciation: / \u0259\u02c8t\u025bn.t\u026av.n\u0259s / (AmE), / \u0259\u02c8t\u025bn.t\u026av.n\u026as / (BrE)
In technology (especially machine learning), the degree to which an artificial intelligence system focuses its processing on the most relevant parts of input data, often implemented using attention mechanisms.

Examples

AI attentiveness
The chatbot's attentiveness to user intent improved after adding attention layers.
Engineers measured the model's attentiveness by checking which words received higher attention weights.
High attentiveness in the vision system helped it ignore background clutter and focus on faces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attention - the act or process of concentrating on particular information; in ML, a mechanism that weights inputs by importance
