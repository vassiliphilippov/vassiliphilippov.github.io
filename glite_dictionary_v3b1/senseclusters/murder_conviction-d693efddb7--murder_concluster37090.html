murder conviction

noun
being found guilty of murder in court
Pronunciation: / ˈmɜrdər kənˈvɪkʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɜːdə kənˈvɪkʃən / (BrE)
a formal declaration by a court that someone is guilty of murder

Examples

A murder conviction case
He is serving a life sentence after his murder conviction in 2019.
Journalists covered every detail of the high-profile murder conviction.
The appeal court overturned her murder conviction due to new evidence.

Related Terms

  • antonym: acquittal - a judgment that a person is not guilty of the crime with which they have been charged
  • degree lower: manslaughter conviction - conviction for the crime of manslaughter
