parfum

noun
fragrance ingredient
5.9
Pronunciation: /pɑːr.ˈfjuːm/ /; / /pɑːr.ˈfuːm/ (AmE), /pɑː.ˈfjuːm/ /; / /pɑː.ˈfuːm/ (BrE)
a mixture of fragrances used as an ingredient in cosmetics or household products, often listed on product labels

Examples

Parfum ingredient
Many shampoos list parfum as an ingredient to give them a pleasant scent.
If you have sensitive skin, check for parfum on the product label before using it.
Some people prefer products without parfum to avoid artificial fragrances.
