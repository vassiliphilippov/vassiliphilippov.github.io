meatiness

noun
full, rich quality
5.8 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàmiÀêtin…ôs / (AmE), / ÀàmiÀêtin…ôs / (BrE)
a full, rich, or heavy quality, especially in sound or tone, often used to describe music or voices

Examples

Meatiness of the voice
The meatiness of the singer's voice gave the performance a powerful presence.
Audio engineers often seek more meatiness in the lower frequencies of a recording.
The new speakers added a surprising meatiness to the sound of the orchestra.
