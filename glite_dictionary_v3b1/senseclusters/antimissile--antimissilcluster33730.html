antimissile

adjective
against missiles
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈmɪsəl / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈmɪsaɪl / (BrE)
designed to counteract, intercept, or destroy incoming missiles

Examples

Antimissile shield
The government invested in an antimissile shield to protect the capital city.
Engineers tested the new antimissile technology under extreme conditions.
An antimissile system can detect and destroy threats before they reach their target.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defensive - intended to protect against attack
  • antonym: offensive - actively attacking rather than defending
