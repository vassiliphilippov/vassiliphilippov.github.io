undeterred

adjective
not discouraged; not prevented from acting
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.dɪˈtɝd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈtɜːd / (BrE)
not discouraged or prevented from continuing, despite difficulties or setbacks

Examples

Undeterred by problems
Despite repeated failures, she remained undeterred in pursuit of her goal.
He continued to develop his ideas, undeterred by criticism from colleagues.
The hikers, undeterred by the bad weather, pressed on to the summit.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deterred - discouraged from acting or prevented; the opposite state of mind
