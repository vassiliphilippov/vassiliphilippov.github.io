vacation home

noun
house used during holidays or vacations
Pronunciation: / vəˈkeɪʃən hoʊm / (AmE), / vəˈkeɪʃən həʊm / (BrE)
a house or dwelling used for holidays or vacations, not as your main home

Examples

A vacation home in mountains
After retiring, they bought a vacation home in the mountains.
Their vacation home is only a short drive from the city.
We spent the summer relaxing at our vacation home by the lake.
Many families rent a vacation home for their summer holidays.
The Johnsons are considering buying a vacation home in Florida.
They spent two weeks at their vacation home by the lake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: second home - an additional dwelling owned or used by someone, often for holidays
