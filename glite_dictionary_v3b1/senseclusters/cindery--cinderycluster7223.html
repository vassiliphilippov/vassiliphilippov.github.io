cindery

adjective
like cinders
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪndəri / (AmE), / ˈsɪndəri / (BrE)
made of, covered with, or resembling cinders, ashes, or burnt material

Examples

A cindery landscape
The volcano left a cindery landscape that stretched for miles around the crater.
They built the path using cindery material to improve drainage after heavy rains.
The soil was so cindery that only certain hardy plants could grow there.
The cindery remains of the campfire still smoked in the morning.
After the volcano erupted, a cindery layer covered the nearby fields.
The old factory's cindery walls showed years of exposure to burning coal.
