schistosome dermatitis

noun
skin rash from schistosome larvae exposure

A skin rash caused by a sensitivity reaction to repeated invasion of the skin by schistosome larvae (cercariae), often from contact with contaminated water.

Examples

Schistosome dermatitis rash
After swimming in the lake, she developed schistosome dermatitis with itchy red bumps on her legs.
Doctors confirmed that the itchy rash was due to schistosome dermatitis, also known as swimmer's itch.
Frequent exposure to infested water increases the risk of schistosome dermatitis in rural communities.
