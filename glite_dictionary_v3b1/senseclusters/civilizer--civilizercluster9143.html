civilizer

noun
one who brings civilization
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪvəˌlaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈsɪvəlaɪzə / (BrE)
a person or thing that brings civilization or helps improve a society by making it more advanced or organized

Examples

A civilizer of society
The explorers saw themselves as civilizers bringing new ideas to distant lands.
Some people argue that not every civilizer had a positive impact on local cultures.
The novel tells the story of a civilizer who tries to change an isolated community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: barbarian - a person considered uncivilized or primitive
  • synonym: reformer - a person who works to improve society or institutions
