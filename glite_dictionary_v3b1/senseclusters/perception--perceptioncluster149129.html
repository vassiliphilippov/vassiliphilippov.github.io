perception

noun
awareness or understanding gained by perceiving
Pronunciation: / pərˈsɛpʃən / (AmE), / pəˈsɛpʃən / (BrE)
the ability to notice or understand something easily, or knowledge gained through the senses or the mind

Examples

Keen perception
Artists are often praised for their keen perception of the world around them.
Her perception of the situation helped her make the right decision.
His perception of the problem was shaped by his previous experiences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awareness - knowledge or perception of a situation or fact
  • antonym: ignorance - lack of knowledge or awareness
  • synonym: insight - deep understanding of a person or thing
