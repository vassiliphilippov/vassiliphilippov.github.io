just a pawn

noun
person being used by others
5.5
Pronunciation: / dʒʌst ə pɔn / (AmE), / dʒʌst ə pɔːn / (BrE)
A person who is used by others to achieve their own goals, without real power or control over the situation.

Examples

Just a pawn in game
He realized he was just a pawn in their elaborate scheme and had no real power.
Many soldiers are just a pawn in the political games of leaders.
She felt like just a pawn in the company's pursuit of higher profits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: puppet - a person who is controlled by others without real power
  • synonym: tool - a person used by others to achieve their aims
