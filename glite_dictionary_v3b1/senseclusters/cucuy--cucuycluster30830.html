cucuy

noun
bogeyman figure
9.9
Pronunciation: / kuˈkui / (AmE), / kuˈkui / (BrE)
a mythical monster or bogeyman, especially in Latin American folklore, used to scare children into behaving

The legend of the cucuy is told in many Latin American families to encourage good behavior.
