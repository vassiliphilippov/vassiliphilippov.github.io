stitchwork

noun
surgical sutures
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪtʃˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈstɪtʃˌwɜːk / (BrE)
the sutures or the act of suturing used to close a wound or surgical incision

Examples

Surgical stitchwork
The doctor checked the stitchwork to make sure the wound was healing properly.
Careful stitchwork is essential to prevent infection after surgery.
The nurse removed the stitchwork from the patient's arm once the cut had closed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stitch - a loop of thread or similar material used to close a wound
  • synonym: suture - a stitch or row of stitches holding together the edges of a wound
