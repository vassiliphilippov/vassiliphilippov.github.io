unreflective

adjective
lacking careful or serious thought
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈflɛk.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈflɛk.tɪv / (BrE)
Not showing careful thought or consideration; lacking reflection or serious thought.

Examples

An unreflective attitude
An unreflective attitude can lead to repeated mistakes and misunderstanding.
He acted in an unreflective manner, reacting instantly rather than thinking things through.
Her unreflective response revealed she hadn't truly considered the issue.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reflective - thoughtful; marked by careful consideration
  • synonym: thoughtless - lacking consideration or careful thought
