osteogenesis

noun
formation of bone
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɑstioʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (AmE), / ˌɒstiəʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (BrE)
the process by which new bone is formed in the body

Examples

Osteogenesis process
Osteogenesis is essential for healing broken bones.
Disorders affecting osteogenesis can lead to brittle or malformed bones.
Proper nutrition supports healthy osteogenesis during childhood and adolescence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ossification - the natural process of bone formation
