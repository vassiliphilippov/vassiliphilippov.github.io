circuit

noun
sequence of exercises (workout)
3.7 1.88/m
Pronunciation: / c8s5dd0.k6at / (AmE), / c8s5cd0.k6at / (BrE)
a sequence of different exercises or stations performed one after another, usually with little rest, as a form of physical training (circuit training).

Examples

Gym circuit
She completes a circuit at the gym, rotating through several exercise stations.
The coach designed three fast circuits to improve the team's endurance and strength.
During rehab he followed a chair-based circuit to rebuild mobility without heavy weights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: circuit training - a form of body conditioning using a sequence of exercise stations performed in rotation
  • synonym: workout - a period of physical exercise to improve fitness or health
