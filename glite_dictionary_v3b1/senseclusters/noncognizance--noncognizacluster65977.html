noncognizance

noun
intentional ignoring
9.9
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈkɑɡnɪzəns / (AmE), / nɒnˈkɒɡnɪzəns / (BrE)
the deliberate act of not noticing or acknowledging something; intentional ignoring or disregard.

Examples

Noncognizance of problems
The manager's noncognizance of repeated warnings led to further problems in the department.
His deliberate noncognizance of the issue frustrated those seeking a solution.
By maintaining noncognizance, the committee avoided addressing the controversial topic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disregard - the act of ignoring or paying no attention to something
  • degree lower: ignorance - lack of knowledge or awareness
  • antonym: recognition - the act of acknowledging or noticing something
