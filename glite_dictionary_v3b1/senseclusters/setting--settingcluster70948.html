setting

noun
mount in jewelry for holding a gem
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛt̬ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɛtɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

Ring setting
A secure setting is crucial to keep the stone from falling out.
