spike

verb
add alcohol to a drink
Pronunciation: / spaɪk / (AmE), / spaɪk / (BrE)
to add alcohol to (a drink, punch, etc.), often secretly or unexpectedly

Examples

To spike a drink

She caught him spiking her coffee with whiskey as a joke.
Someone must have spiked the lemonade; it tastes a bit odd.
