user-friendly

adjective
easy to use or understand
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːzər ˌfrɛndli /; / ˈjuːzɚ ˌfrɛndli / (AmE), / ˈjuːzə ˌfrɛndli / (BrE)
easy to use or understand, especially because the design is simple and effective

Examples

A user-friendly design
Our team redesigned the website to make it more user-friendly for first-time visitors.
Parents appreciate user-friendly apps that their children can use without any help.
The new phone boasts a user-friendly interface that even beginners can navigate easily.
The company redesigned the packaging to make it more user-friendly for older customers.
The instructions for assembling the furniture are very user-friendly and easy to follow.
We chose this app because it has a user-friendly interface suitable for beginners.

Related Terms

  • antonym: complicated - difficult to use or understand
  • synonym: intuitive - easy to understand without instruction
