Harakat al-Jihad al-Islami al-Filastini

proper noun
Palestinian militant Islamic group (founded 1979)
Pronunciation: / haɾaˈkat æl dʒɪˈhɑd æl ɪsˈlɑmi æl fɪlæsˈtiːni / (AmE), / haɾaˈkat al dʒɪˈhɑːd al ɪsˈlɑːmiː al fɪlæsˈtiːniː / (BrE)
A Palestinian militant organization, established in 1979, dedicated to the creation of an Islamic state in Palestine and the destruction of Israel. The group is smaller and more militant than Hamas.

Examples

Harakat al-Jihad al-Islami al-Filastini group
Tensions escalated after an attack claimed by Harakat al-Jihad al-Islami al-Filastini in Gaza.
The US government has designated Harakat al-Jihad al-Islami al-Filastini as a terrorist organization.
Unlike Hamas, Harakat al-Jihad al-Islami al-Filastini maintains a more exclusive focus on armed resistance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Fatah - major Palestinian political and military organization, secular
  • synonym: Hamas - a Palestinian Sunni-Islamic fundamentalist militant organization
