Chamaemelum nobilis

noun
Roman chamomile plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkeɪ.miˈmiː.ləm ˈnoʊ.bɪ.lɪs / (AmE), / ˌkeɪ.məˈmiː.ləm ˈnɒb.ɪ.lɪs / (BrE)
