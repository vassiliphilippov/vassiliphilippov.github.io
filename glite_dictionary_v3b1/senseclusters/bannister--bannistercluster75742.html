bannister

noun
staircase handrail
Pronunciation: / ˈbænɪstər / (AmE), / ˈbænɪstə / (BrE)
A handrail at the side of a staircase or balcony, designed to prevent people from falling.

Examples

Wooden bannister rail
Children often slide their hands along the bannister while running up the steps.
She grabbed the bannister to steady herself as she walked down the stairs.
The wooden bannister was polished until it gleamed in the morning light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: balustrade - a row of small columns topped by a rail, as on a staircase
