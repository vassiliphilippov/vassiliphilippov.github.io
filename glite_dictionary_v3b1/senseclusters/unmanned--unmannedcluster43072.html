unmanned

adjective
without a person or crew on board
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmænd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmænd / (BrE)
(of a vehicle, aircraft, or device) operated without a person or crew on board.

Examples

An unmanned space probe
NASA successfully launched an unmanned probe to study the outer planets.
The facility relies on an unmanned vehicle to transport materials across the factory floor.
The military tested an unmanned drone during the training exercise.
