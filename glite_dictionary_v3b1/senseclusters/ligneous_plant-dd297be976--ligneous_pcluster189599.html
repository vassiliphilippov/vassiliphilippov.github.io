ligneous plant

noun
plant with woody stems
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪɡniəs plænt / (AmE), / ˈlɪɡnɪəs plɑːnt / (BrE)
a plant that has hard, woody parts or tissues, especially in its stems

Examples

A ligneous plant stem
A pine tree is a classic example of a ligneous plant due to its woody trunk.
Because it has tough, woody stems, the oak is classified as a ligneous plant.
Most shrubs and trees are considered ligneous plants rather than herbaceous ones.

Related Terms

  • antonym: herbaceous plant - a plant lacking a woody stem; soft, green plants
  • synonym: woody plant - plant with hard, lignified tissues; synonym for ligneous plant
