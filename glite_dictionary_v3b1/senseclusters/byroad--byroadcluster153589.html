byroad

noun
minor or little-used country road
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪˌroʊd / (AmE), / ˈbaɪ.rəʊd / (BrE)
a small, less traveled side road, especially in the countryside

Examples

A quiet byroad
Exploring the countryside, she preferred the quiet charm of a byroad to the main highway.
The old byroad was nearly hidden beneath overhanging trees and tall grass.
We took a byroad that wound through peaceful farmland and avoided heavy traffic.

Related Terms

  • antonym: main road - a principal or major road with heavy traffic
  • synonym: side road - a minor road branching off from a main road
