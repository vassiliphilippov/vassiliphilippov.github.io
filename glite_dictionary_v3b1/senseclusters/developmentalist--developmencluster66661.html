developmentalist

noun
development advocate
Pronunciation: / dɪˌvɛləpˈmɛntəlɪst / (AmE), / dɪˌvɛləpˈmɛntəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies, supports, or promotes social or economic development, especially in policy or planning

Examples

Developmentalist policy expert
The developmentalist argued for new infrastructure projects to boost the local economy.
Many developmentalists focus on reducing poverty through education and job creation.
As a developmentalist, she worked closely with government agencies to design effective policies.
