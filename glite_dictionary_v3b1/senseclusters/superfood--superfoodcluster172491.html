superfood

noun
food believed to offer health benefits
3.9 0.27/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàsuÀêp…ôrÀåfuÀêd / (AmE), / ÀàsjuÀêp…ôÀåfuÀêd / (BrE)
A food that is considered especially nutritious or beneficial to health, often because it is particularly rich in vitamins, minerals, or antioxidants.

Examples

A superfood berry
Blueberries are commonly listed as a superfood due to their antioxidant properties.
Kale is often referred to as a superfood because of its high nutrient content.
Many health experts argue that the label superfood is more about marketing than science.

Synonyms

  • health food
    3.4 1.88/m
    health food is often natural, unprocessed, and eaten to support general wellbeing, while superfood claims unusually high levels of vitamins or antioxidants.
