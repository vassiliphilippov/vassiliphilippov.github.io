reconfirmation

noun
confirmation again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌkɑːnfərˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌkɒnfəˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of confirming something again, especially to verify an arrangement, agreement, or information

Examples

Reconfirmation of booking
The airline requested a reconfirmation of the flight reservation 48 hours before departure.
Please send a reconfirmation of your attendance to the meeting by tomorrow.
After the initial agreement, a formal reconfirmation was sent to all parties involved.
The hotel required a reconfirmation of our booking two days before arrival.
Please send a reconfirmation email to ensure your appointment is still scheduled.
After the schedule changed, the team asked for reconfirmation from all participants.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cancellation - the act of deciding that an arranged event will not happen
  • synonym: confirmation - a statement that something is true or has been arranged
