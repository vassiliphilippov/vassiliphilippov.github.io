pop

verb
take a pill or drug by mouth
6.8
Pronunciation: / pɑp / (AmE), / pɒp / (BrE)
to take a drug or pill, especially by mouth, usually quickly or casually

Examples

To pop a pill
Before the meeting, she popped her allergy pill.
He popped a couple of aspirin and went back to work.
Some people pop sleeping pills to help them rest at night.
