chromosomal

adjective
relating to chromosomes
Pronunciation: / ˌkroʊməˈsoʊməl / (AmE), / ˌkrəʊməˈsəʊməl / (BrE)
relating to or involving chromosomes, which are thread-like structures containing genetic information in cells.

Examples

Chromosomal change
Down syndrome is the result of a specific chromosomal change.
She underwent tests to detect any chromosomal problems before starting treatment.
The scientist explained that a chromosomal abnormality caused the disorder.

Related Terms

  • synonym: genetic - relating to genes or heredity
