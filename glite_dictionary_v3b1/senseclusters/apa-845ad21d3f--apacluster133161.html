APA

noun
American pale ale
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ piː ˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌeɪ piː ˈeɪ / (BrE)
a style of pale ale beer from the United States, known for its hoppy and often citrusy flavor

Examples

A cold APA beer
He ordered an APA at the bar because he preferred a citrusy, hoppy beer.
The brewery is famous for its award-winning APA with a refreshing grapefruit aroma.
If you like bold flavors, try their new APA on tap this weekend.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: IPA - India pale ale, a stronger and hoppier style of pale ale
  • antonym: lager - a type of beer that is lighter and less hoppy than pale ales
  • synonym: pale ale - a type of beer made with pale malt and moderate hops
