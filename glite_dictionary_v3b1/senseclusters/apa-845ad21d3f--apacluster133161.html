APA

noun
American pale ale
5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈeɪ.piː.eɪ/ (AmE), /ˈeɪ.piː.eɪ/ (BrE)
a style of pale ale beer from the United States, known for its hoppy and often citrusy flavor

Examples

A cold APA beer

The brewery is famous for its award-winning APA with a refreshing grapefruit aroma.
If you like bold flavors, try their new APA on tap this weekend.
