close the door on

verb
end or reject a possibility
1.0
Pronunciation: / kloʊz ðə dɔr ɑn / (AmE), / kləʊz ðə dɔːr ɒn / (BrE)
to reject or end the possibility of something happening

Examples

To close the door on chances
After being turned down twice, she decided to close the door on her dream of becoming an actor.
His resignation letter effectively closed the door on any chance of his return to the company.
Once we've made this decision, we are closing the door on other alternatives.
