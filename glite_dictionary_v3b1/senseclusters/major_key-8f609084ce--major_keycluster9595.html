major key

noun
musical key based on major scale
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪdʒɚ ki / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdʒə kiː / (BrE)
a musical key whose harmony is based on the major scale, typically producing a bright or happy sound

Examples

The symphony was composed in a major key, giving it an uplifting feel.
