Examples

The rigidness of the metal beam allows it to support heavy loads.
We need to test the rigidness of this plastic before using it in construction.

Synonyms

  • rigidity
    6.0 0.90/m
    rigidity is the usual word for being stiff and resisting bending or deformation, while rigidness names the same physical quality.
  • stiffness
    3.9 4.17/m
    stiffness feels like tightness in muscles or hard parts and suggests discomfort, while rigidness simply names resistance to bending.
  • inflexibility
    5.3 0.02/m
    inflexibility often shows an inability to bend in the body or object, while rigidness highlights being stiff and hard to bend.
  • unyieldingness
    8.1 0.00/m
    unyieldingness means firm and unwilling to give way in people or choices, while rigidness refers to physical stiffness and lack of bend.
