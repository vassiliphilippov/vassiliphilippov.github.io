winnable

adjective
able to be won
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪnəbəl / (AmE), / ˈwɪnəbəl / (BrE)
able to be won or achieved, especially with effort or under certain conditions

Examples

A winnable game
The coach told the team that the next game was completely winnable if they worked together.
Many experts believe the election is still winnable for either candidate.
With the right strategy, even difficult challenges can become winnable.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unwinnable - impossible to win or succeed in
