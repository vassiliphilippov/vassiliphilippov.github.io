opportune

adjective
at a suitable or advantageous time
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːpərˌtun / (AmE), / ˈɒpətjuːn / (BrE)
happening or done at a particularly suitable or advantageous time; well-timed

Examples

An opportune moment
Her suggestion came at an opportune moment and helped us avoid a major problem.
Their arrival proved opportune, as the meeting was about to begin.
We made an opportune stop at the store just before it closed for the night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inopportune - occurring at an inconvenient or inappropriate time
  • synonym: timely - happening at the right or appropriate time
