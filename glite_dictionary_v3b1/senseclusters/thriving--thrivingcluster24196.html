thriving

adjective
flourishing; growing or successful
Pronunciation: / ˈθraɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈθraɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
growing, developing, or succeeding vigorously; very successful or flourishing.

Examples

A thriving business
After years of hard work, the café has become a thriving local business.
Online shopping is a thriving sector of the global economy.
The city is known for its thriving arts scene, attracting creative people from around the world.

Related Terms

  • antonym: declining - becoming weaker or worse; deteriorating
  • synonym: flourishing - growing or developing successfully
  • synonym: prosperous - successful, especially in financial terms
