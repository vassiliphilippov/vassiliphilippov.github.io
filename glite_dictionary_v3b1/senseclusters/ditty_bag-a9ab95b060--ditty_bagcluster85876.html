ditty bag

noun
small bag for sailors' or soldiers' personal items
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪti bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈdɪti baɡ / (BrE)
a small bag used by sailors or soldiers to hold personal articles such as sewing kits and toiletries

Examples

A ditty bag for toiletries
