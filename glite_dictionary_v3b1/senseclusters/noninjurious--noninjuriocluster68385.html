noninjurious

adjective
not harmful
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnɪnˈdʒʊriəs / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪnˈdʒʊəriəs / (BrE)
not causing injury, harm, or damage

Examples

A noninjurious substance
The new cleaning solution is completely noninjurious to pets and children.
Doctors confirmed that the procedure was noninjurious and safe for all patients.
The insect's bite is noninjurious and does not cause any allergic reaction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harmless - not able or likely to cause harm
  • antonym: injurious - causing injury or harm
  • synonym: innocuous - not harmful or offensive
