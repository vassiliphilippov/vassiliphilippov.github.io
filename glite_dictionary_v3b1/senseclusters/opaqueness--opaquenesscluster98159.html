opaqueness

noun
obscurity or incomprehensibility of meaning
7.0
Pronunciation: / oʊˈpeɪknəs / (AmE), / əʊˈpeɪknɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being difficult to understand due to obscurity of meaning

Examples

Opaqueness of writing
Critics complained about the opaqueness of the poet's language, making the poems hard to interpret.
He was frustrated by the opaqueness of her explanation, which only added to his confusion.
The opaqueness of the legal document left many readers confused about its true implications.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clarity - the quality of being easy to understand
  • synonym: obscurity - the state of being difficult to understand
