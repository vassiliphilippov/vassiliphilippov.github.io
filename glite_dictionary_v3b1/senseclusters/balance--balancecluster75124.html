balance

noun
remaining amount in an account
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbæləns / (AmE), / ˈbæləns / (BrE)
the amount of money remaining in an account after all credits and debits have been accounted for

Examples

Bank balance
Please ensure your balance is sufficient before making a withdrawal.
She checked her account and was surprised by the low balance.
The monthly statement showed a balance of $250 remaining.
After all expenses were paid, the balance showed a small surplus.
Please check your account balance before making any withdrawals.
The accountant reviewed the balance to ensure the books were accurate.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deficit - the amount by which money spent is more than money received
  • antonym: surplus - an amount of something left over; more than needed
