service elevator

noun
elevator for freight or goods
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝː.vɪs ˈɛ.ləˌveɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.vɪs ˈɛ.lɪˌveɪ.tə / (BrE)
An elevator designed primarily for transporting freight or goods rather than passengers.

Examples

Service elevator for goods
A technician repaired the service elevator after it broke down yesterday.
Staff used the service elevator to move large boxes between floors.
The hotel guests are not allowed in the service elevator.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freight elevator - an elevator for carrying heavy goods or freight
  • antonym: passenger elevator - an elevator designed to carry people between floors
