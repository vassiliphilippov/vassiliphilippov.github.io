nonbelieving

adjective
not holding a belief
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.bɪˈlivɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.bɪˈliːvɪŋ / (BrE)
not accepting or holding a particular belief, especially in matters of religion

Examples

Nonbelieving parents
She was raised in a nonbelieving family that did not attend religious services.
His nonbelieving attitude sometimes led to debates with his religious friends.
The organization welcomes both nonbelieving and religious members to join their discussions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: faithful - having or showing strong belief, especially in religion
  • synonym: skeptical - having doubts or not easily convinced
