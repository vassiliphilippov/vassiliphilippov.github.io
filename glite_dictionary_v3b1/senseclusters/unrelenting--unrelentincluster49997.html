unrelenting

adjective
not stopping or becoming less intense
5.6 5.6
Pronunciation: /ʌn.rɪ.ˈlen.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ʌn.rɪ.ˈlen.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
continuing without stopping or becoming less severe or intense; not giving up or weakening

Examples
