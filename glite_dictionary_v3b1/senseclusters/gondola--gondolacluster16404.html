gondola

noun
open-topped railroad freight car
9.5 6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑndələ / (AmE), / ˈɡɒndələ / (BrE)
a low, flat-bottomed railroad freight car with fixed sides and no roof, used for transporting bulk materials

Examples

Railroad gondola car
After unloading coal from the gondola, the crew prepared it for its next shipment.
The train included a gondola filled with scrap metal headed for recycling.
Workers loaded gravel into the open-topped gondola at the quarry.

Related Terms
