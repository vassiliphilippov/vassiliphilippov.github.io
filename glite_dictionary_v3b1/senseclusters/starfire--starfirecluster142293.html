starfire

noun
sparkling firework
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrˌfaɪər / (AmE), / ˈstɑːˌfaɪə / (BrE)
a type of firework that creates many bright, star-like sparks when it explodes

Examples

Starfire fireworks
The children watched in awe as a starfire lit up the night sky during the celebration.
Each starfire burst into hundreds of sparkling lights, delighting the crowd at the park.
For the finale, the organizers launched a massive starfire that shimmered above the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: firework - an explosive device used for entertainment that produces light and sound
  • synonym: sparkler - a handheld firework that emits sparks
