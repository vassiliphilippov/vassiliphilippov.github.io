poha

noun
flattened rice flakes
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊhɑː / (AmE), / ˈpəʊhɑː / (BrE)
thin, dry flakes of rice used as an ingredient in cooking, especially in South Asian cuisine

Examples

Poha flakes
For breakfast, she made a spicy dish using poha mixed with vegetables and peanuts.
You should rinse the poha gently so it does not become mushy before cooking.
Many Indian snacks use poha as a main ingredient because it cooks quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beaten rice - rice that has been flattened into thin flakes
  • synonym: rice - a cereal grain used as a staple food
