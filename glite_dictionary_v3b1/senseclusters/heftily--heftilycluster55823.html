heftily

adverb
with force or weight
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛftɪli / (AmE), / ˈhɛftɪli / (BrE)
in a heavy, forceful, or vigorous manner

Examples

Push heftily
She pushed the door heftily when it got stuck in the frame.
The workers heftily loaded the crates onto the truck before noon.
He heftily tossed his backpack onto the couch after a long day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heavily - with a lot of weight or force
  • antonym: lightly - with little weight or force
