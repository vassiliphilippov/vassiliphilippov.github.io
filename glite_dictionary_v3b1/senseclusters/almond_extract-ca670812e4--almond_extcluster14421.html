almond extract

noun
flavoring made from almonds and alcohol
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːlmənd ɪkˌstrækt / (AmE), / ˈɑːmənd ɪkˌstrakt / (BrE)
a concentrated liquid flavoring made by macerating almonds in alcohol, used especially in baking

Examples

A few drops of almond extract
Add a few drops of almond extract to give the cake a rich, nutty flavor.
She prefers to use natural almond extract instead of artificial flavorings in her cookies.
The recipe called for both vanilla and almond extract to enhance the dessert's aroma.

Related Terms

  • synonym: almond essence - alternative term for almond extract
  • synonym: vanilla extract - flavoring made from vanilla beans macerated in alcohol
