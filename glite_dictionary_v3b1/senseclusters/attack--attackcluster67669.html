attack

noun
military offensive action
3.5
Pronunciation: / əˈtæk / (AmE), / əˈtak /; / əˈtæk / (BrE)
an act of using force or violence to harm someone or something; a military or violent action

Examples

A violent attack
A second attack was launched the following night under cover of darkness.
The attack caught the enemy by surprise and forced them to retreat.
The commander waited for the right moment to order the attack.
After the attack, emergency services rushed to help the wounded.
The general planned a surprise attack at dawn.
There was an attack on the embassy early this morning.
