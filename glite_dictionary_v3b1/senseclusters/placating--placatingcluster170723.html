placating

adjective
intended to calm or pacify
4.1 5.5
Pronunciation: /plə.ˈkeɪ.tɪŋ/ /; / /ˈpleɪ.kə.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /plə.ˈkeɪ.tɪŋ/ /; / /ˈpleɪ.kə.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
intended to pacify or calm someone, especially by yielding to their demands or making concessions

Examples

A placating smile

Her words took on a placating tone as she tried to resolve the argument.
The prime minister made several placating gestures to calm the protesters.
