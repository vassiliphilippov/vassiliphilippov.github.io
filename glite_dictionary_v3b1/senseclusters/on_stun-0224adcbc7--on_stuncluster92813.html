on stun

x
device setting that incapacitates without injury
5.9
Pronunciation: / ɑn stʌn / (AmE), / ɒn stʌn / (BrE)
(of a device or weapon) set to a mode that incapacitates a target without causing serious injury or death.

Examples

Set on stun mode
Before firing the ray gun, she made sure it was set on stun to avoid harming anyone.
Security forces were ordered to keep their weapons on stun during the operation.
The panel lit up when the device switched on stun, signaling it was ready for non-lethal use.

Related Terms

  • antonym: on kill - set to a mode that can cause serious harm or death
