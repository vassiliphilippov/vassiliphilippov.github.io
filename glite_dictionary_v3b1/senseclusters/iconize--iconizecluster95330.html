iconize

verb
to make iconic
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪkəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈaɪkənaɪz / (BrE)
to make something or someone into an icon or symbolic representation, or treat with great admiration

Examples

To iconize a person
The artist managed to iconize the city skyline in her latest painting.
Many brands try to iconize their logos to stand out in the market.
The film helped to iconize the actor as a cultural legend.
The media began to iconize the artist after her groundbreaking exhibition.
Some people iconize historical figures without considering their flaws.
Social movements often iconize certain leaders to inspire their followers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: criticize - to express disapproval or find fault with someone or something
  • antonym: deiconize - to remove iconic or symbolic status
  • synonym: deify - to treat someone or something as a god
  • synonym: idolize - to admire or worship someone greatly
  • synonym: symbolize - to represent something by a symbol
