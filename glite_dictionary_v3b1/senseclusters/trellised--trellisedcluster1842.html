trellised

adjective
lattice-patterned
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛlɪst / (AmE), / ˈtrɛlɪst / (BrE)
having or covered with a structure of latticework (a trellis), often for decoration or to support plants

Examples

Trellised garden archway
The garden featured a trellised archway covered in blooming roses.
She admired the trellised windows that added charm to the old cottage.
The restaurant's patio was shaded by a trellised roof supporting climbing vines.
The trellised vines created a beautiful green wall along the patio.
They enjoyed lunch under a trellised pergola covered in blooming wisteria.
A row of trellised tomatoes lined the edge of the vegetable garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: latticed - having a structure or pattern like a lattice
  • antonym: plain - without any pattern or decoration
