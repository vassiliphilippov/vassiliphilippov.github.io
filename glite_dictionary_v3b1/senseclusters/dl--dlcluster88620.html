dl

verb
informal verb: download
9.9
Pronunciation: / diː ˈɛl / (AmE), / diː ˈɛl / (BrE)
(informal) to download a file or data from the internet or another computer, often used in online chat or technical messages.

Examples

To dl a file
I'll dl the latest patch from the developer's site tonight.
She asked him to dl the photo album before the meeting started.
After noticing the bug, they dl the update immediately to fix the issue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: download - to copy or transfer data from a remote system to a local device
  • antonym: upload - to send data from a local device to a remote system; opposite of download
