allelopathy

noun
chemical influence between organisms
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˌliːləˈpæθi /; / əˌliːˈlɑːpəθi /; / əˌlɛləˈpæθi / (AmE), / əˌliːləˈpæθɪ /; / əˌliːˈlɒpəθi /; / əˌliːləˈpæθi / (BrE)
the process or effect of an organism (especially a plant) releasing chemicals that influence the growth, survival, or reproduction of other organisms nearby
