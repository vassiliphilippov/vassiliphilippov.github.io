magician

noun
person who performs magic or sorcery
3.5 3.0
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈdʒɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /mə.ˈdʒɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a person who performs magic tricks for entertainment or is believed to have magical powers

Examples

A magician performs tricks
As a child, he dreamed of becoming a magician and performing at grand theaters.
Some stories feature a powerful magician able to control the forces of nature.
The famous magician amazed the audience with his disappearing act.
At the birthday party, a magician entertained guests with incredible tricks.
Children watched in awe as the magician made the card disappear.
The magician pulled a rabbit out of his hat, amazing the entire audience.
