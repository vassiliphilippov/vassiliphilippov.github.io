ball-buster

noun
demanding or domineering woman (slang)
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔlˌbʌstɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlˌbʌstə / (BrE)
(slang) A woman who is regarded as very demanding or domineering, especially one who is seen as undermining men's confidence or control.

Examples

A strict ball-buster boss
He's afraid of dating a ball-buster after his last difficult relationship.
In the film, she plays a classic ball-buster who won't let any man get in her way.
Some coworkers said their manager was a real ball-buster who never showed any mercy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harpy - a shrewish, unpleasant woman
  • synonym: nag - a person, especially a woman, who constantly criticizes or complains
