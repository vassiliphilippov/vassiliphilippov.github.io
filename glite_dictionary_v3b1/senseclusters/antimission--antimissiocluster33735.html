antimission

noun
opposition to missions
Pronunciation: / ˌæntɪˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌæntɪˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
the policy, movement, or attitude of opposing religious missions or missionary activity

Examples

Antimission movement
The government launched an antimission campaign to restrict foreign religious groups.
Local leaders expressed their antimission stance during the community meeting.
The rise of antimission sentiment affected the work of several international organizations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antimissionary - opposed to missionary activity
  • antonym: pro-mission - supporting missionary activity
