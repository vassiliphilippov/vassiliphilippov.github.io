intercolonial

adjective
between colonies
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərkəˈloʊniəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəkəˈləʊniəl / (BrE)
relating to or occurring between two or more colonies, especially in historical or political contexts

Examples

The intercolonial railway
The intercolonial railway was built to connect the distant settlements.
Delegates attended an intercolonial meeting to discuss trade policies.
Historical records show frequent intercolonial disputes over territory.

Related Terms

  • antonym: domestic - relating to a single country or colony
  • synonym: international - existing or occurring between nations
