replow

verb

5.0

to plow a road, path, or land again, especially to clear it or improve its condition

Examples

To replow the road

After the storm, the farmer had to replow the field to break up the hardened soil.
They decided to replow the land to prepare it better for the next crop season.
Sometimes it is necessary to replow if weeds start growing back quickly.
