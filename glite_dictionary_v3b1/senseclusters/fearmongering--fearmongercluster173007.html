fearmongering

noun
spreading fear
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪrˌmʌŋɡərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪəˌmʌŋɡərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of deliberately spreading fear among people to influence their opinions or actions

Examples

Media fearmongering
The news report was criticized for its fearmongering about the new disease.
Some leaders use fearmongering to gain support for their policies.
Social media can sometimes become a platform for fearmongering during crises.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reassurance - the act of making someone feel less worried or afraid
  • synonym: scaremongering - the act of spreading alarming or exaggerated rumors
