bindery

noun
workshop where books are bound
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪndəri / (AmE), / ˈbaɪndəri / (BrE)
a workshop or place where books are bound together, especially by hand or using machines

Examples

A bindery workshop
He worked in a bindery as an apprentice before opening his own shop.
The old library still has a bindery where rare books are restored.
Tours of the bindery show how books are stitched and glued together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bookbinding - the process or art of physically assembling a book
