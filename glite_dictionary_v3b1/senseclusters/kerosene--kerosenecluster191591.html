kerosene

noun
flammable oil fuel for lamps/heaters
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛrəˌsiːn /; / ˈkɛrəsiːn / (AmE), / ˈkɛrəsiːn / (BrE)
a clear, flammable oil obtained from petroleum, used as a fuel in lamps, heaters, and engines

Examples
