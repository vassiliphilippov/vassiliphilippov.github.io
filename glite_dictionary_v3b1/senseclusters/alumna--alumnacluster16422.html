alumna

noun
female graduate of a school or college
Pronunciation: / əˈlʌm.nə / (AmE), / əˈlʌm.nə / (BrE)
A woman who has graduated from a particular school, college, or university.

Examples

An alumna of college
As an alumna, she donates regularly to support scholarships for future students.
She is an alumna of Stanford University, where she earned her degree in biology.
The university honored its oldest alumna during the graduation ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alumnae - female graduates of a school or college (plural)
  • synonym: alumni - male or mixed-gender graduates of a school or college (plural)
  • synonym: alumnus - a male graduate of a school or college
