Seahawk

proper noun
US Navy helicopter
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːˌhɑk / (AmE), / ˈsiːˌhɔːk / (BrE)
the Sikorsky SH-60 Seahawk, a US Navy multi-role helicopter used for anti-submarine warfare, search, and rescue missions

Examples

Navy Seahawk helicopter
The Seahawk landed smoothly on the deck after completing its patrol mission.
During the exercise, a Seahawk was deployed to search for submarines in the area.
The crew prepared the Seahawk for a late-night rescue operation over the ocean.
