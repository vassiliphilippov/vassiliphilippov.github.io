cystoid

noun
extinct marine echinoderm
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪstɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɪstɔɪd / (BrE)
a fossil marine echinoderm of the extinct class Cystoidea, characterized by a plated, globular or conical body
