spotted skunk

noun
small striped skunk species
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑt̬ɪd ˌskʌŋk / (AmE), / ˈspɒtɪd ˌskʌŋk / (BrE)
a small skunk native to the United States and Mexico, characterized by a marbled black and white coat

Examples

A spotted skunk animal
A spotted skunk can be recognized by the unique patterns on its fur.
Farmers sometimes see a spotted skunk searching for insects at night.
The spotted skunk is more agile and climbs trees more often than other skunks.
