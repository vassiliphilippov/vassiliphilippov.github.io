Ciudad Trujillo

proper noun
former name for Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic
9.5
Pronunciation: /si.uː.ˈdæd truː.ˈhiː.joʊ/ /; / /si.uː.ˈdæd truː.ˈdʒiː.joʊ/ (AmE), /si.uː.ˈdæd truː.ˈhiː.jəʊ/ /; / /si.uː.ˈdæd truː.ˈdʒiː.jəʊ/ (BrE)
the former name (1936–1961) of Santo Domingo, the capital city of the Dominican Republic, used during the regime of Rafael Trujillo

Examples

Ciudad Trujillo name
During Trujillo's dictatorship, Santo Domingo was renamed Ciudad Trujillo.
Many older maps of the Caribbean still mark the city as Ciudad Trujillo.
The city's name reverted from Ciudad Trujillo back to Santo Domingo after 1961.
