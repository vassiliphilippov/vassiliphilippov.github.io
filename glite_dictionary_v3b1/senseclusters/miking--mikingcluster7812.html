miking

noun
microphone placement
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of placing or using a microphone to record or amplify sound

Examples

Good miking technique
Good miking can make a big difference in the quality of a live performance recording.
The engineer spent extra time on miking the drum set to capture every detail.
Different miking techniques are used depending on the type of instrument and venue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amplification - the process of making sounds louder
  • synonym: recording - the process of capturing sound or music
