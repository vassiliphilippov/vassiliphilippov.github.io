vocality

noun
readiness to speak out
Pronunciation: / voʊˈkæləti / (AmE), / vəʊˈkælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being willing to express opinions or speak out openly

Examples

Vocality in meetings
Her vocality during meetings encouraged others to share their ideas as well.
The activist's vocality brought attention to important community issues.
Despite his usual vocality, he remained silent during the heated debate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outspokenness - the quality of expressing opinions openly and honestly
  • antonym: reticence - the quality of being reserved or unwilling to speak
