regard

verb
to consider or think of as
Pronunciation: / rɪˈɡɑrd / (AmE), / rɪˈɡɑːd / (BrE)
to consider or think of someone or something in a particular way

Examples

To regard as important
He regards honesty as the most important value in a friendship.
Many people regard her as one of the best writers of her generation.
The company is regarded as a pioneer in renewable energy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consider - to think carefully about or view in a particular way
  • antonym: disregard - to pay no attention to; to ignore
