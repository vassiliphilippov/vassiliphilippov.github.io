breezeway

noun
covered outdoor passage
5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈbriːz.weɪ/ (AmE), /ˈbriːz.weɪ/ (BrE)
a roofed outdoor passage that connects two buildings or parts of a building, usually open at the sides

Examples

The breezeway between buildings
The breezeway between the house and the garage kept us dry during the rain.
They decorated the breezeway with plants to make it more inviting.
A cool wind blew through the breezeway on summer evenings.
The house has a breezeway that links the kitchen to the garage.
We enjoyed sitting in the breezeway on warm summer evenings.
A covered breezeway connects the main building to the guest cottage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corridor - an indoor passage connecting rooms or buildings
  • synonym: walkway - a path or passage for walking
