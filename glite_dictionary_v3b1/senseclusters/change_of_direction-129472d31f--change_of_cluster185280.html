change of direction

noun
the act of changing direction
Pronunciation: / tʃeɪndʒ əv dəˈrɛkʃən / (AmE), / tʃeɪndʒ əv dɪˈrɛkʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of changing the direction in which something moves, faces, or is oriented

Examples

A quick change of direction
Athletes must be able to perform a quick change of direction during competition.
The sudden change of direction surprised the driver behind her.
There was a significant change of direction in the company's strategy last year.
