alchemically

adverb
in an alchemical way
9.9
Pronunciation: / ælˈkɛmɪkli / (AmE), / ælˈkɛmɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to alchemy or the process of transformation, especially in a mysterious or magical manner

Examples

To alchemically transform
The artist alchemically blended colors to create a unique effect on the canvas.
Some believed the potion was alchemically prepared to cure any illness.
The story describes how lead was alchemically turned into gold in ancient times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: magically - in a magical or supernatural way
  • antonym: scientifically - in a way based on science or scientific methods
