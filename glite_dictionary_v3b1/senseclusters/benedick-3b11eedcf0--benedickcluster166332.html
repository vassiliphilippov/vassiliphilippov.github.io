Benedick

proper noun
recently married man
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛnɪˌdɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɛnɪˌdɪk / (BrE)
a man who has recently married, especially one who was previously known for being a confirmed bachelor

Examples

A Benedick man
After years of resisting marriage, he surprised everyone by becoming a Benedick at forty.
The club joked that their last single member was now a Benedick after his wedding last weekend.
He was once proud of his bachelor status, but now he is happily a Benedick.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bachelor - an unmarried man
  • synonym: newlywed - a person who has recently married
