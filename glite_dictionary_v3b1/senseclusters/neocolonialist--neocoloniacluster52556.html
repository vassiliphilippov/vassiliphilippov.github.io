neocolonialist

adjective
indirect foreign control
9.6 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / Àånio äk…ôÀàlo äni…ôl…™st / (AmE), / ÀåniÀê…ô äk…ôÀàl…ô äni…ôl…™st / (BrE)
relating to the practice of indirectly controlling or influencing other countries, especially for economic or political advantage

Examples

Neocolonialist policies
Many activists criticized the agreement as a neocolonialist attempt to control local resources.
The company's neocolonialist policies led to protests in several developing countries.
Some scholars argue that foreign investment can have neocolonialist effects on national economies.

Synonyms

  • neocolonial
    9.9 0.01/m
    neocolonial is an adjective for indirect foreign control or influence, while neocolonialist refers to a person or policy that practices it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anti-colonial - opposed to colonialism or foreign domination
  • synonym: imperialist - relating to extending a country's power through colonization or force
