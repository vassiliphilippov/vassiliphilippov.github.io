unworked

adjective
not used or active
4.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɝːkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɜːkt / (BrE)
not used, employed, or put to work; idle or unused, especially referring to time, equipment, or capacity.

Examples

Unworked factory hours
After the layoffs, many unworked hours remained in the factory schedule.
The land remained unworked for years before the new owners started farming it.
Several unworked machines stood in the corner, gathering dust.

Related Terms

  • antonym: active - currently in use or operation
  • synonym: idle - not active or in use
  • synonym: unused - not being used
