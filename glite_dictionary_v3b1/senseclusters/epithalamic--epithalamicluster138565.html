epithalamic

adjective
wedding poem-related
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪθəˈlæmɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪθəˈlæmɪk / (BrE)
relating to an epithalamium, a poem or song written to celebrate a wedding or newlyweds.

Examples

Epithalamic poem
The poet wrote an epithalamic verse to honor the newly married couple.
Her speech had an epithalamic tone, celebrating the joy of the wedding day.
They included an epithalamic song in the ceremony to bless the marriage.
