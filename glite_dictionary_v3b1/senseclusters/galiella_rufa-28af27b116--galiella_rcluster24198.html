Galiella rufa

noun
a cup-shaped fungus of the Galiella genus
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈlaɪələ ˈruːfə / (AmE), / ɡæliˈɛlə ˈruːfə / (BrE)
A species of fungus with cup-shaped fruiting bodies that have a jellylike interior and a short stalk, found on decaying wood.

Galiella rufa fungus
