refill

noun
an additional supply of a prescription drug
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈriˌfɪl / (AmE), / ˈriːfɪl / (BrE)
an amount of a prescription drug that is provided again after the initial supply has been used up

Examples

A refill prescription
My pharmacy called to say my refill was ready for pickup.
The label indicates how many refills remain on this medication.
Your doctor can authorize another refill if needed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prescription - an instruction written by a medical practitioner for the dispensing of a medicine
