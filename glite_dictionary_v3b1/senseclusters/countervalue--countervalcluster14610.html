countervalue

noun
equivalent in exchange
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntərˌvæljuː / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntəˌvæljuː / (BrE)
something given or the value assigned as an equivalent in exchange, payment, or comparison (such as in another currency or in economic/military contexts)
