pillowy

adjective
soft and rounded
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪloʊi / (AmE), / ˈpɪləʊi / (BrE)
soft, plump, and fluffy, like a pillow

Examples

Pillowy soft texture
The bread had a pillowy texture that melted in my mouth.
She rested her head on the pillowy cushions of the sofa after a long day.
The clouds looked especially pillowy against the bright blue sky.
The bread had a pillowy texture that melted in your mouth.
She rested her head on the pillowy clouds in her dream.
The cat curled up on the pillowy comforter and purred contentedly.

  • synonym: fluffy - light and soft in texture
