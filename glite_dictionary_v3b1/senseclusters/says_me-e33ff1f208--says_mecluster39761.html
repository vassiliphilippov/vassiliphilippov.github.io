says me

x
colloquial way to state one's own opinion
Pronunciation: / sɛz mi / (AmE), / sɛz miː / (BrE)
Used informally to assert that a statement is one's own opinion or belief, often in a slightly humorous or defiant way.

Examples

says me jokingly
No one gets to decide what's best for me except says me.
That's not how the rules work, at least says me.
You think you're right, but says me, you're completely wrong.

Related Terms

  • synonym: in my opinion - used to introduce one's viewpoint or belief
  • antonym: says who - colloquial way to challenge another's authority or statement
