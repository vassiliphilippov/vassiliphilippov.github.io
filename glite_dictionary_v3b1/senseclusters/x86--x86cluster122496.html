x86

noun
Intel-based computer architecture family
5.5
Pronunciation: /eks ˈeɪ.ti sɪks/ /; / /æks ˈeɪ.ti sɪks/ (AmE), /eks ˈeɪ.ti sɪks/ /; / /æks ˈeɪ.ti sɪks/ (BrE)
