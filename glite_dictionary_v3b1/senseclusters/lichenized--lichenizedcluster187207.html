lichenized

adjective
covered with lichens
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪkəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈlɪkənaɪzd / (BrE)
covered with or having lichens growing on the surface.

Examples

Lichenized tree bark
The ancient tree trunk was lichenized, giving it a greenish, textured appearance.
Many rocks in the forest became lichenized after years of exposure to moisture and shade.
The lichenized statues in the garden blended naturally with the surrounding landscape.
