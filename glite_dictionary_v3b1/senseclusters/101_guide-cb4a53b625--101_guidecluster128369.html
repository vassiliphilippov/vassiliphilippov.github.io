101 guide

noun
introductory guide to a subject
Pronunciation: / wʌn oʊ wʌn ɡaɪd / (AmE), / wʌn əʊ wʌn ɡaɪd / (BrE)
A beginner's guide or introductory manual to a subject, often designed for those with no prior knowledge.

Examples

A 101 guide book
Before moving on to advanced classes, it's helpful to read a 101 guide on the subject.
She wrote a 101 guide to help newcomers understand basic programming concepts.
This book is the perfect 101 guide for anyone interested in digital marketing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beginner's guide - an introductory guide for people new to a subject
  • synonym: handbook - a book giving information such as facts on a particular subject
