entrant

noun
person entering a competition
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɛntrənt / (AmE), / ˈɛntrənt / (BrE)
a person or group that enters or takes part in a competition, activity, organization, or place

Examples

Race entrant

Only one entrant from each school is allowed to participate in this category.

As a recent entrant to the field, she brought fresh ideas to the team.
Each year, a new entrant is crowned champion of the marathon.
The contest is open to any entrant who registers before the deadline.
All entrants must fill out an application form before the deadline.
New entrants to the profession are required to undergo training.
The competition attracted hundreds of young entrants from around the world.
