watermelon-shaped

adjective
elongated like a prolate spheroid
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔːtərˌmɛlən ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəmɛlən ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having a form in which the polar diameter is greater than the equatorial diameter; resembling a prolate spheroid.

Examples

Watermelon-shaped asteroid
Some asteroids are nearly watermelon-shaped rather than perfectly spherical.
The balloon expanded into a watermelon-shaped form as more air was added.
The scientist used a model of a watermelon-shaped object to demonstrate the planet's rotation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prolate - elongated along the polar axis (as opposed to equatorial)
