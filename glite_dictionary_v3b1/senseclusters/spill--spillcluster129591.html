spill

noun
liquid accidentally poured or dropped
3.4
Pronunciation: / spɪl / (AmE), / spɪl / (BrE)
an amount or instance of liquid or substance accidentally flowing out of a container

Examples

Oil spill
The janitor quickly responded to the oil spill in the hallway.
The waiter wiped up the spill before it could stain the carpet.
There was a spill of coffee on the desk after she knocked over her mug.
He cleaned up the coffee spill on the kitchen floor immediately.
The spill from the oil tanker caused severe environmental damage.
Try to avoid a spill by holding the cup carefully when you walk.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: flood - an overflowing of a large amount of water beyond normal limits
  • synonym: leak - an unintended escape of liquid or gas
