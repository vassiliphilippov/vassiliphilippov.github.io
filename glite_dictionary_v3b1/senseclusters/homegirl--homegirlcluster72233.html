homegirl

noun
female companion from one's neighborhood or group
2.6 7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈhoʊm.gɜːrl/ (AmE), /ˈhəʊm.gɜːl/ (BrE)
a female friend from one's neighborhood, especially a fellow member of a group or community; often used in youth or urban slang to refer to a female companion from the same area, background, or gang.
