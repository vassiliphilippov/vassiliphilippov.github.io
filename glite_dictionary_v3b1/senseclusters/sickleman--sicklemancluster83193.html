sickleman

noun
manual crop harvester
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪkəlˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈsɪkəlˌmæn / (BrE)
a man who uses a sickle, especially for harvesting crops by hand

Examples

The sickleman harvesting wheat
The sickleman moved steadily through the golden wheat fields at sunrise.
Villagers watched as the experienced sickleman harvested the crops with swift, practiced motions.
During the festival, a sickleman demonstrated traditional harvesting techniques to the children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: farmer - a person who works on or manages a farm
  • synonym: reaper - a person who harvests crops
