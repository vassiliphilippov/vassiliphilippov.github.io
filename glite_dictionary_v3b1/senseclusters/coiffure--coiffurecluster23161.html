coiffure

verb
to style or arrange hair
Pronunciation: / kwɑˈfjʊr / (AmE), / kwɑːˈfjʊə / (BrE)
to arrange or style (hair), especially in an elaborate or careful way

Examples

To coiffure her hair
A specialist was hired to coiffure the actress's hair before the big event.
He spent an hour coiffuring his hair into perfect waves.
She skillfully coiffured her daughter's curls for the performance.
