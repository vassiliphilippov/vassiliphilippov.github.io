movement

noun
change in opinion or tendency
3.4 15/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàmuÀêv.m…ônt / (AmE), / ÀàmuÀêv.m…ônt / (BrE)
A general tendency or shift in public opinion or in a particular field or area.

Examples

Movement for change
A strong movement for education reform has emerged in recent years.
The movement against single-use plastics is gaining momentum worldwide.
There is a movement towards renewable energy within the industry.

Synonyms

  • trend
    2.3 73/m
    trend shows a general direction or popular idea over time, while movement implies a broader tendency or organized shift in opinion.
  • drift
    4.0 2.30/m
    drift is a slow, often unplanned change over time, while movement covers any general tendency or organized shift in opinion.
  • shift
    3.5 43/m
    shift is a specific change or move from one state to another, while movement is a more general tendency or direction in opinion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shift - a slight change in position, direction, or tendency
  • synonym: trend - a general direction in which something is developing or changing
