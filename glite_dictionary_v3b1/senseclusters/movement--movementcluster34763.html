noun
change in opinion or tendency

A general tendency or shift in public opinion or in a particular field or area.

Examples

Movement for change
A strong movement for education reform has emerged in recent years.
The movement against single-use plastics is gaining momentum worldwide.
There is a movement towards renewable energy within the industry.
