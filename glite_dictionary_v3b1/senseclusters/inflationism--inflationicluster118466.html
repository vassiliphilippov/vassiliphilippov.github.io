inflationism

noun
exaggeration tendency
8.2
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈfleɪ.ʃən.ɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈfleɪ.ʃən.ɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
the practice or tendency of exaggerating the importance, size, or value of something

Examples

Inflationism of achievements
The manager's inflationism made every minor success sound like a major breakthrough.
Critics accused the report of inflationism in its description of the project's impact.
Her habit of inflationism eventually led people to doubt her claims.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overstatement - the act of making something seem more important or larger than it is
  • antonym: understatement - the act of making something seem less important or smaller than it is
