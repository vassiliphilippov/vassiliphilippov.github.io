Damocles

proper noun
legendary Greek courtier of Dionysius the Elder
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdæməˌkliz / (AmE), / ˈdaməˌkliːz / (BrE)
A legendary Greek courtier of Dionysius the Elder, who, according to legend, was forced to sit under a sword suspended by a single hair to show the dangers of power and privilege.

Examples

Damocles sword story
He described his position as similar to that of Damocles, never sure when things might suddenly go wrong.
Many leaders find themselves in a Damocles situation, constantly aware of the risks tied to their authority.
The story of Damocles highlights the perils that can accompany great power.
