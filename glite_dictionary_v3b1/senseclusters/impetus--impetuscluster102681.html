Examples

A sudden impetus
A quick impetus sent the boat gliding away from the shore.
The runner gained momentum with an impressive impetus at the starting gun.
The sudden impetus of the wind knocked over the garden chairs.

Synonyms

  • impulsion
    7.9 0.00/m
    impulsion is the sudden act of giving a push or drive, while impetus names the force or energy moving a body.
  • impulse
    4.2 1.19/m
    impulse is a sudden physical push that changes momentum, while impetus often means the energy or ongoing force behind movement.
  • driving force
    4.1 3.27/m
    driving force is the cause or agency that pushes something forward, while impetus usually refers to the initial physical energy or push.
