westward

adjective
moving toward the west
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛstwərd / (AmE), / ˈwɛstwəd / (BrE)

Examples

Westward journey
Explorers set out on a westward expedition in search of new lands.
Strong winds pushed the smoke westward into neighboring towns.
They continued their westward journey across the plains.
