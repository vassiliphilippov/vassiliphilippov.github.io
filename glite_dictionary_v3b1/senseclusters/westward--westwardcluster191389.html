Synonyms

  • westbound
    2.8 0.4/m
    westbound is used for things moving or heading toward the west, while westward is the general directional word for that movement.
  • west
    1.0 20/m
    west often means toward or located in the west direction, while westward stresses the action of moving toward the west.
  • westerly
    3.7 0.2/m
    westerly is often used for winds or weather coming from or toward the west, while westward simply means moving toward the west.
  • westerly
    3.1 0.03/m
