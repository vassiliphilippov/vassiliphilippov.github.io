chylaceous

adjective
relating to or resembling chyle
Pronunciation: / kaɪˈleɪʃəs / (AmE), / kaɪˈleɪʃəs / (BrE)
Consisting of chyle or having the properties of chyle.

Examples

A chylaceous effusion may indicate damage to the lymphatic system.
