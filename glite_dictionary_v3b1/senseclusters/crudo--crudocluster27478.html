crudo

noun
raw fish or meat dish
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkruːdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkruːdəʊ / (BrE)
a dish of raw fish or meat, typically served thinly sliced with seasoning or dressing

Examples

A tuna crudo
The chef recommended the salmon crudo as a light and refreshing appetizer.
We shared a plate of tuna crudo topped with citrus and herbs at the new restaurant.
Her favorite starter is always a well-prepared crudo with olive oil and sea salt.
