peaberry

noun
single round coffee bean
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈpiːˌbɛri / (BrE)
a coffee bean that develops as a single, small, round seed inside the coffee cherry instead of the usual two flat-sided beans

Examples

A peaberry bean
Some coffee enthusiasts believe that a peaberry produces a sweeter and more flavorful cup.
The roaster separated the peaberries from the regular beans for a special batch.
You can sometimes find bags of peaberry coffee at specialty shops.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coffee bean - the seed of the coffee plant used to make coffee
