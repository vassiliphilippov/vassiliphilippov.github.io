tree cat

noun
arboreal wild cat
Pronunciation: / ˈtriː kæt / (AmE), / ˈtriː kæt / (BrE)
a small wild cat or similar animal that lives mainly in trees

A tree cat animal
The margay is a type of tree cat found in Central and South America.
Researchers observed a tree cat leaping gracefully from branch to branch in the rainforest.
Some species of tree cat are rarely seen on the ground, preferring to hunt in the canopy.
