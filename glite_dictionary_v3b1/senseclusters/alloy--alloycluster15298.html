alloy

verb
combine metals to make an alloy
6.3
Pronunciation: / əˈlɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈælɔɪ / (BrE)
to combine (a metal) with one or more other elements, usually metals, to form an alloy.

Examples

To alloy gold
Gold is often alloyed with other metals to create jewelry of various strengths.
Many manufacturers alloy copper with tin to produce bronze.
The company has developed a new method for alloying lightweight metals.
