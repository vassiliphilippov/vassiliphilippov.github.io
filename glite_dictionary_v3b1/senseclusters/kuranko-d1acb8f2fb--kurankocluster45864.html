Kuranko

noun
West African ethnic group
9.9
Pronunciation: / kuːˈræŋkoʊ / (AmE), / kuːˈræŋkəʊ / (BrE)
an ethnic group of West Africa, mainly living in Sierra Leone and Guinea

Examples

The Kuranko people
The Kuranko have a rich tradition of storytelling and music.
Many Kuranko communities are found in the northern regions of Sierra Leone.
Researchers studied the social structure of the Kuranko for their project on West African cultures.
