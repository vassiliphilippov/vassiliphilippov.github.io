kiva

noun
Pueblo ceremonial room
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːvə / (AmE), / ˈkiːvə / (BrE)
a round, often underground ceremonial room used by Pueblo peoples for meetings and religious rituals

Examples

A kiva room
The community gathered in the kiva to celebrate the annual festival.
Visitors were invited to observe a traditional dance inside the ancient kiva.
The walls of the kiva were decorated with symbolic paintings and carvings.
The ancient village featured a large kiva at its center for community gatherings.
Archaeologists carefully excavated the kiva to learn more about Pueblo religious practices.
Visitors can tour a reconstructed kiva to understand its cultural significance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: longhouse - a traditional communal dwelling used by some Indigenous peoples
  • synonym: pit house - a house partly dug into the ground, used by ancient peoples
