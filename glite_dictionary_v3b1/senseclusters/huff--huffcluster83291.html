huff

noun
a state of irritation or annoyance
5.6 7.0
Pronunciation: / hʌf / (AmE), / hʌf / (BrE)
a fit of anger or annoyance, often expressed by sulking or abrupt speech or action

Examples

She left the meeting in a huff when her proposal was rejected.
There's no need to get into a huff over such a small mistake.
