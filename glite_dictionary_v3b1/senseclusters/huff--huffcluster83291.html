a state of irritation or annoyance

a fit of anger or annoyance, often expressed by sulking or abrupt speech or action

Examples

A huff of anger

She left the meeting in a huff when her proposal was rejected.
There's no need to get into a huff over such a small mistake.

Synonyms

  • fit of frustration
    5.8 0.06/m

  • sulk
    4.3 0.28/m
    sulk is a quiet, withdrawn mood that lasts longer, while a huff is a short fit of annoyance often shown by sulking or sharp words.
  • miff
    8.1 0.01/m
    miff is a small, mild irritation people feel over a slight insult, while a huff is a stronger, outward fit of annoyance.
