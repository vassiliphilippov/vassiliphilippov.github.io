fisheye

adjective
relating to a fisheye lens
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃˌaɪ / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃˌaɪ / (BrE)
relating to or denoting a wide-angle lens that produces a strong visual distortion intended to create a wide panoramic or hemispherical image

Examples

Fisheye camera lens
The fisheye perspective distorted the straight lines in the building's architecture.
The photographer used a fisheye lens to capture the entire room in one shot.
You can achieve a dramatic effect by applying a fisheye filter to your images.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wide-angle - denoting a camera lens with a wider field of view than a standard lens
