future date

noun
a specified day or time in the future
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːtʃər deɪt / (AmE), / ˈfjuː.tʃə deɪt / (BrE)
a particular day or time that is set to occur after the present moment, often scheduled or planned for a specific event or activity

Examples

A future date
Please mark the future date of the meeting on your calendar.
The event has been postponed to a future date due to unforeseen circumstances.
We can discuss that topic at a future date when more information is available.

Related Terms

  • synonym: due date - the expected or scheduled date when something is supposed to happen or be completed
