plane section

noun
area made by a plane cutting a solid
Pronunciation: / pleɪn ˈsɛkʃən / (AmE), / pleɪn ˈsɛkʃən / (BrE)
(Geometry) the area formed when a plane cuts through a solid figure.

Examples

A plane section of cone
In conic sections, each type is defined as the plane section of a cone at a particular angle.
The architect used a plane section to illustrate how sunlight would enter the building.
The intersection of a cube with a diagonal plane forms a hexagonal plane section.
