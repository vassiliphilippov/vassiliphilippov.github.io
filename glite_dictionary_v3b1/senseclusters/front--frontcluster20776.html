front

noun
military line where armies face each other
Pronunciation: / frʌnt / (AmE), / frʌnt / (BrE)
the line or area where opposing armies face each other in a battle or during a war

Examples

At the war front
He spent several months at the front during the war.
News from the front indicated that the battle was intensifying.
The troops were sent to the front to reinforce the main line of defense.

Related Terms

  • synonym: battlefield - the area where a battle is fought
  • antonym: rear - the back line away from the main area of fighting
