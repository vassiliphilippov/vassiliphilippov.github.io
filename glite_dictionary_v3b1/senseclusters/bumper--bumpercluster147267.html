bumper

noun
bar on vehicle to absorb impact
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌmpɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʌmpə / (BrE)
a horizontal bar fixed at either end of a motor vehicle to absorb impact in a collision and protect the vehicle's bodywork

Examples

Car bumper
He replaced his damaged bumper after the minor collision.
Modern vehicles have bumpers designed to absorb more impact energy.
The car's front bumper was slightly scratched in the accident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fender - a part of a car that covers or protects the wheel area, chiefly in American English
