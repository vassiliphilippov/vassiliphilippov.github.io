microcosmically

adverb
in a miniature way
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkrəˈkɑːz.mɪkli / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəˈkɒz.mɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that represents a small system reflecting the characteristics of something much larger

Examples

Society microcosmically shown
The classroom operated microcosmically, mirroring the dynamics of society at large.
Her novel explores family life microcosmically to comment on broader cultural issues.
The experiment was designed microcosmically to simulate the effects on a national scale.
