fatherliness

noun
kind and protective paternal quality
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːðər.lɪ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈfɑːðə.lɪ.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being kind, caring, and protective in a manner characteristic of a father

Examples

His fatherliness showed
His fatherliness was evident in the gentle advice he gave his children.
She admired the fatherliness of her mentor, who always looked out for her well-being.
The fatherliness shown by the coach made all the players feel supported.

Related Terms

  • antonym: motherliness - the quality of being caring and protective like a mother
