unmount

verb
to disconnect device
5.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmaʊnt / (AmE), / ʌnˈmaʊnt / (BrE)
to disconnect or safely remove a device, disk, or file system from a computer or system

Examples

To unmount a drive
You should always unmount external hard drives before unplugging them from your computer.
The system will automatically unmount the disk if it is not in use.
He forgot to unmount the USB stick, which could lead to data loss.
Always unmount your USB drive before removing it from the computer to prevent data loss.
The technician instructed me to unmount the external hard disk before shutting down the system.
If you forget to unmount the device, you might corrupt your files.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eject - to safely remove a device from a computer
  • antonym: mount - to make a device or file system available for use
