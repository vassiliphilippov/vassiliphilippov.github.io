neo-

adjective
combining form meaning 'new' or 'recent'

Pronunciation: /ˈniː.oʊ/ /; / /ˈniː.əʊ/ (AmE), /ˈniː.əʊ/ /; / /ˈniː.oʊ/ (BrE)
A combining form meaning 'new', 'recent', or 'revived', used in the formation of compound words.

Examples

Neo- meaning new
In the word 'neonatal', the prefix neo- refers to newborn infants.
Many economic theories use the prefix neo- to describe updated versions of older models.
The term neo- is often attached to words to indicate something modern or revived, like 'neo-classical'.
