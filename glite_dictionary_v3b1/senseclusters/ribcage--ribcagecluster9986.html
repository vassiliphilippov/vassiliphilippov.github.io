ribcage

noun
framework of ribs
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪbˌkeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈrɪbˌkeɪdʒ / (BrE)
a framework of ribs or rib-like supports that forms the internal structure of something, often giving shape or protection

Examples

Ship's ribcage
The shipwright measured the wooden ribcage to ensure the hull retained its designed curvature.
The architect designed a building with a steel ribcage to support the glass walls.
The artist created a sculpture with a visible ribcage structure to highlight its internal form.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shell - a hard outer covering that protects or supports
  • synonym: skeleton - the internal supporting structure of an organism or object
