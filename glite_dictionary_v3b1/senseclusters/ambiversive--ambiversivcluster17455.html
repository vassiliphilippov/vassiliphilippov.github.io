ambiversive

adjective
exhibiting both introvert and extrovert traits
6.0 7.7
Pronunciation: /æm.bɪ.ˈvɜːr.sɪv/ (AmE), /æm.bɪ.ˈvɜː.sɪv/ (BrE)
(of a person or personality) intermediate between introversive and extroversive; showing characteristics of both introversion and extroversion.

Examples

Ambiversive personality
Because Sam is ambiversive, he enjoys both socializing with friends and spending time alone.
His ambiversive nature made it easy for him to adapt to different social environments.
The psychologist described her patient as ambiversive, possessing qualities of both introversion and extroversion.
