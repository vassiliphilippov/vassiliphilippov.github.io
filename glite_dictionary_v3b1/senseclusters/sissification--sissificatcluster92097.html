sissification

noun
making more feminine
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɪsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making someone appear or behave in a stereotypically feminine or effeminate way, often in a derogatory sense
