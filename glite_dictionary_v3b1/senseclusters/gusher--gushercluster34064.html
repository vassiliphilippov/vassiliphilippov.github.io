gusher

noun
oil well with strong natural flow
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌʃər / (AmE), / ˈɡʌʃə / (BrE)
an oil well that produces oil with such force that pumping is not required

Examples

An oil gusher
Early oil prospectors dreamed of finding a gusher that would make them rich.
The discovery of a gusher transformed the small town into a booming oil city.
The oil company struck a gusher on their first drilling attempt.
