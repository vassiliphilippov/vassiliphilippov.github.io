indisposition

noun
slight illness; mild unwell feeling
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪspəˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪspəˈzɪʃən / (BrE)
a slight illness or feeling of being unwell that prevents someone from doing something

Examples

A mild indisposition
Despite her indisposition, she managed to attend the event.
He stayed home from work due to a minor indisposition.
Her indisposition forced her to cancel the meeting at the last minute.

Related Terms

  • antonym: health - state of being free from illness or injury
  • synonym: illness - state of being ill or sick
