guard

noun
period of sentry duty
1.0 6.24/m
Pronunciation: / …°…ërd / (AmE), / …°…ëÀêd / (BrE)
the duty or period of watching over a place or people to protect them, especially by standing as a sentry

Examples

Night guard duty
During her guard, nothing unusual happened at the facility.
He was assigned the guard for the midnight shift.
Soldiers take turns standing guard outside the entrance each night.

Synonyms

  • guard duty
    3.3 0.45/m
    guard duty is the specific assignment to watch and protect a place, while guard refers to the general duty or person who watches.
  • sentry duty
    5.8 0.07/m
    sentry duty is the standing post for a soldier on watch, while guard is a more general duty of protecting people or places.
  • watch
    4.2 17/m
    watch is a period or team that stays alert to observe, while guard is the active duty of protecting by staying at a post.
  • sentry go
    9.7 0.00/m
    sentry go is a military phrase for a sentry's turn on post, while guard means the duty of watching or the person who watches.
