slender lady palm

noun
species of Chinese lady palm with slender stems
Pronunciation: / ˈslɛn.dɚ ˈleɪ.di pɑlm / (AmE), / ˈslɛn.də ˈleɪ.di pɑːm / (BrE)
A species of Chinese lady palm distinguished by its slender stems and finer sheath fibers than Rhapis excelsa.

Examples

A slender lady palm plant
She chose a slender lady palm to add elegance to the hotel lobby.
The slender lady palm is prized for its graceful appearance in indoor gardens.
Unlike the more robust Rhapis excelsa, the slender lady palm has finer stems and leaves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lady palm - a palm of the genus Rhapis grown as an ornamental plant
