hesychasm

noun
contemplative prayer tradition
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛsɪˌkæzəm / (AmE), / ˈhɛsɪˌkazəm / (BrE)
a mystical tradition of contemplative prayer and inner stillness in Eastern Orthodox Christianity

Examples

Hesychasm prayer practice
Monks in the Eastern Orthodox Church often practice hesychasm to seek spiritual peace.
His interest in hesychasm led him to visit monasteries on Mount Athos.
The teachings of hesychasm emphasize silence and repetitive prayer.
