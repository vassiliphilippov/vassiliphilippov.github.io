freedom

noun
the state of being free
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfri.dəm / (AmE), / ˈfriː.dəm / (BrE)
The power or right to act, speak, or think as one wants without hindrance or restraint.

Examples

Political freedom
Many nations have fought hard to win their freedom from colonial powers.
She values her freedom above anything else in life.
The constitution guarantees freedom of speech for all citizens.

Related Terms

  • synonym: liberty - the state of being free within society from oppressive restrictions
  • antonym: oppression - prolonged cruel or unjust treatment or control
