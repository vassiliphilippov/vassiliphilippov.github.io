bullhide

verb
to whip severely
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbʊl.haɪd/ (AmE), /ˈbʊl.haɪd/ (BrE)
to beat or whip someone or an animal severely, especially using a bullhide whip.

Examples

To bullhide a horse

He was known to bullhide his cattle to keep them under control during roundups.
