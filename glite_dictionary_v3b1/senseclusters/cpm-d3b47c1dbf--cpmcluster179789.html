CPM

noun
counts per minute
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈsiː.piː.em/ (AmE), /ˈsiː.piː.em/ (BrE)
a measurement of the number of events or detections recorded in one minute, especially in radiation or sensor readings

Examples

Radiation CPM reading
The Geiger counter displayed a CPM value that was higher than normal.
Scientists recorded the CPM to monitor changes in background radiation levels.
A sudden spike in CPM readings prompted an immediate safety inspection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Bq - a unit of radioactivity equal to one disintegration per second (becquerel)
  • synonym: cps - counts per second, a similar measurement to CPM but per second
