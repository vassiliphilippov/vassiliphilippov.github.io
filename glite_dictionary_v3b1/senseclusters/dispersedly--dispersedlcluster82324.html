dispersedly

adverb
in a scattered way
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspɜrsɪdli / (AmE), / dɪˈspɜːsɪdli / (BrE)
in a scattered or spread-out manner, not concentrated in one place

Examples

Dispersedly spread out
The wildflowers grew dispersedly throughout the open field.
Villages were arranged dispersedly along the winding river.
Books and papers lay dispersedly across his desk after a long night of studying.

Related Terms

  • antonym: densely - in a closely packed or crowded way
  • synonym: scatteredly - in a scattered or irregular way
