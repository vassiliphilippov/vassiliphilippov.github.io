uncollectable

adjective
cannot be collected
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəˈlɛktəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəˈlɛktəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be collected, especially referring to money or debts that cannot be recovered

Examples

Uncollectable debt
The bank considered the debt uncollectable after several failed attempts to contact the borrower.
Many businesses must write off uncollectable accounts at the end of the year.
If a bill remains unpaid for too long, it may become uncollectable.
