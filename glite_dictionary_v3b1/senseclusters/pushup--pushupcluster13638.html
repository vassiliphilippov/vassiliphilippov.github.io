pushup

noun
exercise raising body using arms
1.4 2.0
Pronunciation: /ˈpʊʃ.ʌp/ (AmE), /ˈpʊʃ.ʌp/ (BrE)
An exercise in which a person lies facing the floor and raises and lowers their body by straightening and bending their arms.

Examples

Pushups exercise
He does fifty pushups every morning as part of his workout.
I struggled to complete ten pushups without stopping.
Military training often includes sets of pushups to build upper body strength.
