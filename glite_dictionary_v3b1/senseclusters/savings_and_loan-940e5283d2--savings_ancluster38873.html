savings and loan

noun
type of financial institution specializing in home mortgages
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪvɪŋz ənd loʊn / (AmE), / ˈseɪvɪŋz ənd ləʊn / (BrE)
a financial institution that accepts savings deposits and is required by law to make a certain proportion of its loans as mortgages for residential property

Examples

A savings and loan bank
My parents took out a mortgage from the local savings and loan to buy their first house.
She works as a loan officer at a savings and loan downtown.
The collapse of several savings and loan associations in the 1980s caused a major financial crisis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thrift - a savings bank or savings and loan association; a financial institution focused on savings and mortgages
