nobility

noun
elevation of character or ideals
1.0 4.1
Pronunciation: /noʊ.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ /; / /nə.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ (AmE), /nəʊ.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ /; / /nə.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ (BrE)
