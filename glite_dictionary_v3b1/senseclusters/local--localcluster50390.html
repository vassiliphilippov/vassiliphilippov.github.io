local

noun
train or bus that stops at all stations
3.3 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈloʊ.kəl/ (AmE), /ˈləʊ.kəl/ (BrE)
a train or bus that stops at all stations or stops along its route, rather than only at major ones

Examples

The local train
He always rides the local to avoid missing any stops near his office.
The local is slower, but it’s the only option late at night.
We took the local because the express train wasn't running that day.
