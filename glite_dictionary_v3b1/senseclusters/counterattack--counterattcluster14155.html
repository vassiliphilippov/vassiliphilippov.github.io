counterattack

verb
to make a retaliatory attack
Pronunciation: / ˌkaʊntərəˈtæk / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntərəˈtæk / (BrE)
to attack in response to an enemy's attack; to make a retaliatory attack against an opponent

Examples

To counterattack quickly
After regaining their strength, the soldiers decided to counterattack at dawn.
The team quickly counterattacked, surprising their opponents and scoring a goal.
When the company was criticized in the media, it chose to counterattack with a strong public statement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attack - to take aggressive action against
  • antonym: defend - to protect against attack
