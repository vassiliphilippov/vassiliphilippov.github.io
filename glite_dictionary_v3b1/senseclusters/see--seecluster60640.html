see

noun
bishop's official seat or diocese center
9.9 3/m
Pronunciation: / siÀê / (AmE), / siÀê / (BrE)
the official seat or center of authority of a bishop, usually the location of the bishop's cathedral within a diocese

Examples

The Holy See
Rome is often called the Holy See, referring to the Pope's authority as bishop of Rome.
The Archbishop's see is located in Canterbury, making it a major center of the Anglican Church.
The bishop traveled to his see to preside over a special ceremony.
