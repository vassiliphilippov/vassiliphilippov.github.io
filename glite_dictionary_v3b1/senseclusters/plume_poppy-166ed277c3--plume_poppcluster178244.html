plume poppy

noun
Asian perennial with feathery white flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / plum ˈpɑːpi / (AmE), / pluːm ˈpɒpi / (BrE)
A tall perennial plant native to China and Japan, widely grown for its large, feathery clusters of creamy white flowers.
