balata tree

noun
tropical hardwood tree yielding balata gum
Pronunciation: / bəˈlɑːtə ˌtriː / (AmE), / bəˈlɑːtə ˌtriː / (BrE)
a tropical hardwood tree that produces balata gum and heavy red timber, native to Central and South America
