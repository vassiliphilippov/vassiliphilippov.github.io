commuter traffic

noun
traffic during commuting hours
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjuːtər ˈtræfɪk / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːtə ˈtræfɪk / (BrE)
the volume of vehicles or people traveling to or from work, especially during peak periods

Examples

Commuter traffic congestion
Road improvements are being made to ease commuter traffic congestion in the suburbs.
The city sees heavy commuter traffic each morning as workers head downtown.
We try to avoid commuter traffic by leaving before the rush hour begins.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rush hour - the busy part of the day when roads and public transport are crowded as people travel to or from work
