recordable

adjective
able to be recorded
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɔrdəbəl / (AmE), / rɪˈkɔːdəbəl / (BrE)
able to have information, sound, or images written or stored on a physical medium such as a CD, DVD, or tape.

Examples

Recordable CD
This CD is recordable, so you can save your favorite songs onto it.
The camera uses recordable DVDs to store video footage for later viewing.
Make sure you buy a recordable tape if you want to capture the entire concert.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nonrecordable - not able to be recorded on
  • synonym: rewritable - able to be recorded and erased multiple times
