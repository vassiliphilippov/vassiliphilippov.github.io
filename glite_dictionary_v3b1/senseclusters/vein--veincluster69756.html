Synonyms

  • nervure
    9.9 0.00/m
    nervure is the technical botanical name used in plant descriptions, while vein is the common everyday word for the same leaf rib.
  • nervus
    9.9 0.00/m
    nervus names a vein or rib in leaves or insect wings in scientific texts, while vein is the general name for leaf vascular ribs.
  • vascular bundle
    5.5 0.01/m
    vascular bundle is a strand of xylem and phloem inside stems and leaves, while vein refers to the visible leaf ribs or bundles.
  • vascular strand
    7.4 0.00/m
    vascular strand is a narrow tube of xylem and phloem within plant organs, while vein names the branching leaf rib visible on a leaf.
  • fibrovascular bundle
    8.7 0.00/m
    fibrovascular bundle is a supportive strand with fibers and vascular tissue in stems and leaves, while vein is the leaf's branching rib.
