lens of the eye

noun
transparent eye structure focusing light
2.4 3.6
Pronunciation: /lenz əv ðiː aɪ/ /; / /lenz ðə aɪ/ /; / /lenz ʌv ðiː aɪ/ (AmE), /lenz əv ðiː aɪ/ /; / /lenz ðə aɪ/ (BrE)
the transparent, biconvex structure behind the iris of the eye that focuses light onto the retina

Examples
