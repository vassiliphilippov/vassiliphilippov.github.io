jury

verb
to judge entries
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɜːri / (AmE), / ˈdʒʊəri / (BrE)
to judge or evaluate entries in a competition or contest as a member of a panel.

Examples

To jury a contest
She juried the regional art competition, selecting the most innovative pieces for awards.
Several experts juried the film festival to choose the best documentary.
He was invited to jury the national photography contest this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evaluate - to assess or determine the value or quality of something
  • synonym: judge - to form an opinion or decision about something
