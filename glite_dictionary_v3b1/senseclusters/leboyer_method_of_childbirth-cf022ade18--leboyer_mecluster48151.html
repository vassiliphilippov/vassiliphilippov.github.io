Leboyer method of childbirth

noun
childbirth method focused on gentle birth
Pronunciation: / ləˈbwaɪ.ər ˈmɛθ.əd əv ˈtʃaɪldˌbɜrθ / (AmE), / ləˈbɔɪ.ə ˈmɛθ.əd əv ˈtʃaɪldˌbɜːθ / (BrE)
A method of childbirth that seeks to minimize trauma for the newborn, involving delivery in a quiet, dimly lit room, gentle handling, and immediate mother-child bonding.

Examples

The Leboyer method of childbirth
Many couples choose the Leboyer method of childbirth to provide their newborn with a gentle introduction to the world.
She decided to follow the Leboyer method of childbirth after reading about its benefits for both mother and baby.
The Leboyer method of childbirth emphasizes minimal medical intervention and a calm environment during delivery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: caesarean section - surgical delivery of a baby
  • synonym: natural childbirth - method of giving birth without medical intervention
