Leboyer method of childbirth

noun
childbirth method focused on gentle birth
7.2
Pronunciation: /lə.ˈbɔɪ.jər ˈmeθ.əd əv ˈtʃaɪld.bɜːrθ/ /; / /lə.ˈbɔɪ.ər ˈmeθ.əd əv ˈtʃaɪld.bɜːrθ/ (AmE), /lə.ˈbɔɪ.jə ˈmeθ.əd əv ˈtʃaɪld.bɜːθ/ /; / /lə.ˈbɔɪ.ə ˈmeθ.əd əv ˈtʃaɪld.bɜːθ/ (BrE)
A method of childbirth that seeks to minimize trauma for the newborn, involving delivery in a quiet, dimly lit room, gentle handling, and immediate mother-child bonding.

The Leboyer method of childbirth emphasizes minimal medical intervention and a calm environment during delivery.
