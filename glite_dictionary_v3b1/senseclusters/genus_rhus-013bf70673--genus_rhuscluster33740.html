genus Rhus

noun
genus of shrubs and trees (sumacs)
9.0
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒiː.nəs ruːs/ (AmE), /ˈdʒiː.nəs ruːs/ /; / /ˈdʒɛn.əs ruːs/ (BrE)
A genus of deciduous or evergreen shrubs and small trees, commonly known as sumacs, found in temperate and subtropical regions; distinguished from related poisonous species in genus Toxicodendron.

Examples

Genus Rhus plants
Botanists have classified smooth sumac as part of the genus Rhus.
Most members of the genus Rhus are not poisonous, unlike those in the genus Toxicodendron.
The genus Rhus includes many species commonly known as sumacs.
