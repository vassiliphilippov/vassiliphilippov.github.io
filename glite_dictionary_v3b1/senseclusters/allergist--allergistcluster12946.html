allergist

noun
doctor specializing in allergies
Pronunciation: / ˈælərdʒɪst / (AmE), / ˈalədʒɪst / (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of allergies

Examples

Allergist doctor
After years of sneezing, I finally visited an allergist for a proper diagnosis.
Many children see an allergist if they have frequent reactions to certain foods.
The allergist recommended a series of tests to determine the cause of my symptoms.
