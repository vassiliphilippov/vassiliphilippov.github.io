costar

verb
to share lead role
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊˌstɑr / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌstɑː / (BrE)
to act as a main performer together with another person in a film, play, or show

Examples

To costar in a film
She will costar with a famous actor in the new drama series.
The two comedians costarred in a popular television show last year.
He is excited to costar in his first major film role.

Related Terms

  • synonym: star - to be the main performer in a film or show
