Podaxaceae

noun
family of gastromycete fungi
Pronunciation: / poʊˌdæksəˈsiː.i / (AmE), / pəʊˌdaksəˈsiː.i / (BrE)
A family of fungi belonging to the gastromycetes, characterized by their spore-bearing fruiting bodies.

Examples

Podaxaceae fungi family
Researchers have discovered several new species within the family Podaxaceae.
The Podaxaceae are often found in arid regions, where their fruiting bodies emerge after rainfall.
Unlike most mushrooms, members of the Podaxaceae produce spores inside enclosed structures.
