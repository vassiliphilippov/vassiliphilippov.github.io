navy bean

noun
a small white dried bean
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪvi ˌbin / (AmE), / ˈneɪvi ˌbiːn / (BrE)
A small white bean, commonly used in baked beans and soups, usually sold dried.

Examples

A navy bean soup
Navy beans are the traditional choice for making Boston baked beans.
My grandmother always kept a bag of dried navy beans in her pantry.
She added a cup of navy beans to the soup for extra protein.
