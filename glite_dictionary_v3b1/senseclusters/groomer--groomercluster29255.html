groomer

noun
child abuser
9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈgruː.mər/ (AmE), /ˈgruː.mə/ (BrE)

Examples

Online groomer

Parents are advised to talk to their children about the dangers of interacting with a groomer on social media.
The documentary exposed how a groomer gained the trust of vulnerable teenagers.
