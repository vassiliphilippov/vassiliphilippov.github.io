Marianist

adjective
of the Marianist order
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛəriənɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɑːrɪənɪst / (BrE)
relating to the Marianists, a Roman Catholic religious order, or their institutions, teachings, or traditions

Examples

Marianist school values
The school follows a Marianist philosophy that emphasizes service and community.
She attended a Marianist university known for its commitment to social justice.
Many Marianist traditions focus on education and helping the poor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Franciscan - relating to the Franciscan order, a Catholic religious group
  • synonym: Jesuit - relating to the Society of Jesus, a Catholic religious order
