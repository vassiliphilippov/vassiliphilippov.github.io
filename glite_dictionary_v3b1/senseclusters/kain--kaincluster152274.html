kain

noun
piece of cloth
Pronunciation: / kaɪn / (AmE), / kaɪn / (BrE)
a piece or length of cloth or fabric used for covering, wrapping, or household purposes, not specifically as clothing

Examples

A kain for covering
She used an old kain to cover the tray while it cooled on the counter.
The basket was wrapped in a colorful kain to keep the contents clean.
He folded the kain neatly and placed it on the shelf for later use.
