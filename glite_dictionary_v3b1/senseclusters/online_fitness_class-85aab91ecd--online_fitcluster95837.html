online fitness class

noun
exercise session conducted via internet
Pronunciation: / ˈɑnˌlaɪn ˈfɪtnɪs klæs / (AmE), / ˈɒnˌlaɪn ˈfɪtnɪs klɑːs / (BrE)
an organized exercise session conducted over the internet and accessible remotely

Examples

An online fitness class session
His mornings usually start with an online fitness class streamed to his living room.
Many gyms now offer online fitness classes for people who prefer to exercise remotely.
She joined an online fitness class to stay active while working from home.
