machinable

adjective
suitable for machines
5.1
Pronunciation: / məˈʃiːnəbəl / (AmE), / məˈʃiːnəbl / (BrE)
able to be processed, read, or handled by a machine or automated system.

Examples

Machinable data format
The data must be in a machinable format for the program to analyze it efficiently.
They designed the forms to be easily machinable by the scanning system.
A machinable document can be quickly processed without human intervention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automatable - able to be performed by automation or machines
  • antonym: manual - requiring human effort rather than machines
