metalsmith

noun
metalworker
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtəlˌsmɪθ / (AmE), / ˈmɛtəlˌsmɪθ / (BrE)
a person who makes or repairs objects out of metal by shaping and joining pieces

Examples

A metalsmith shapes metal
The metalsmith carefully shaped the copper into a decorative bowl.
Ancient tools were often made by a skilled metalsmith in the village.
She apprenticed with a metalsmith to learn how to create custom jewelry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blacksmith - a person who makes or repairs things made of iron
  • synonym: goldsmith - a person who makes or repairs objects made of gold
