cross-modal

adjective
involving different sensory modalities
2.9 6.5
Pronunciation: /krɔːs ˈmoʊ.dəl/ /; / /krɑːs ˈmoʊ.dəl/ (AmE), /krɒs ˈməʊ.dəl/ (BrE)
