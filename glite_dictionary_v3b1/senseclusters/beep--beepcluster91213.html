beep

verb
make a short, high-pitched sound
1.1 1.2
Pronunciation: /biːp/ (AmE), /biːp/ (BrE)
to make a short, loud, high-pitched sound, like those produced by horns or electronic devices

Examples

To beep the horn

She accidentally pressed a button, and the microwave suddenly beeped loudly.
The alarm clock beeped every morning at six o'clock, waking him up on time.
