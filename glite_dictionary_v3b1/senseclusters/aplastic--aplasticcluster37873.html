aplastic

adjective
unable to form cells
9.3
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈplæs.tɪk/ (AmE), /ə.ˈplæs.tɪk/ (BrE)
not able to form or develop new cells, especially in tissue or bone marrow

Examples

Aplastic anemia
The doctor explained that aplastic conditions prevent the bone marrow from producing enough blood cells.
After several tests, the child was found to have an aplastic disorder affecting her immune system.
Treatment for aplastic anemia often involves blood transfusions and medication to stimulate cell growth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hypoplastic - having underdeveloped or incomplete tissue or organ formation
