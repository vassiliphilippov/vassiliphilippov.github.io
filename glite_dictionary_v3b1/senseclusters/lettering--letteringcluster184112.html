lettering

noun
inscribed or carved letters/words
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛt̬ərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɛtərɪŋ / (BrE)
letters or words that are inscribed, carved, or painted on a surface, especially as decorative or identifying text

Examples

Engraved lettering

The artist chose a classic script for the shop's gold lettering.
