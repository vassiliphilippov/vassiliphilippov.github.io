preconceptional

adjective
before conception
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːkənˈsɛpʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌpriːkənˈsɛpʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to the period or conditions before conception or pregnancy

Examples

Preconceptional care
Doctors recommend preconceptional counseling for women planning to have a baby.
A healthy lifestyle during the preconceptional period can improve pregnancy outcomes.
Many clinics offer preconceptional checkups to identify potential health risks before pregnancy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postconceptional - relating to the period after conception
  • synonym: prenatal - relating to the period before birth
