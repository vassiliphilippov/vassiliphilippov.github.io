illuminatingly

adverb
in a brightening way
Pronunciation: / ɪˈluːməˌneɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪˈluːmɪneɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that produces or gives light, making something brighter or more visible

Examples

Candles burn illuminatingly
The candles flickered illuminatingly in the dark room, casting soft shadows on the walls.
The fireworks burst illuminatingly across the night sky, delighting the crowd below.
The artist arranged the lamps illuminatingly to highlight the details of her sculpture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brightly - in a way that gives off a lot of light
  • antonym: dimly - in a way that gives off little light
