rocking chair

noun
chair that rocks back and forth
2.6 1.4
Pronunciation: /ˈrɑː.kɪŋ tʃer/ (AmE), /ˈrɒk.ɪŋ tʃeə/ (BrE)
A chair mounted on curved pieces of wood or metal, allowing the sitter to rock back and forth.

Examples

A wooden rocking chair
He bought a wooden rocking chair for the nursery to soothe his baby to sleep.
Her grandmother sat quietly in the rocking chair by the window, knitting a scarf.
The porch looked inviting with two rocking chairs and a little table between them.
