rain shower

noun
a brief period of rain
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪn ˌʃaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈreɪn ˌʃaʊ.ə / (BrE)
a brief period of rain, usually sudden and not lasting long

Examples

A rain shower suddenly
After a short rain shower, the sun came out again.
The picnic was delayed when a rain shower passed over the park.
We took shelter under a tree during a sudden rain shower.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: downpour - a heavy and sudden fall of rain
  • degree lower: drizzle - light rain falling in very fine drops
