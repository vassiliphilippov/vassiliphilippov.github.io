ribbon

noun
strip of fabric awarded as a prize
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪb.ən / (AmE), / ˈrɪb.ən / (BrE)
A strip of fabric given as an award for winning a competition or to commemorate a special event.

Examples

A ribbon prize
Each winner received a blue ribbon for their achievements.
She proudly displayed the ribbon she won at the county fair.
The team celebrated after earning a championship ribbon for their hard work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: medal - a metal disc awarded as a prize in a competition or for bravery
  • synonym: trophy - a cup or other object awarded as a prize for a victory or achievement
