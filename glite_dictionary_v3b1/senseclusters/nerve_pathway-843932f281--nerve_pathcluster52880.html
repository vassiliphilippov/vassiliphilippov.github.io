nerve pathway

noun
route of nerve fibers in the brain
Pronunciation: / nɜrv ˈpæθˌweɪ / (AmE), / nɜːv ˈpɑːθweɪ / (BrE)
a bundle of myelinated nerve fibers that follows a specific route through the brain or nervous system, transmitting signals between different regions

Examples

A nerve pathway bundle
Damage to this nerve pathway can result in the loss of motor function.
Researchers mapped a new nerve pathway involved in decision making.
The doctor explained how the nerve pathway transmits information from the senses to the brain.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: nerve - a bundle of fibers transmitting impulses throughout the body
  • synonym: neural tract - a bundle of nerve fibers carrying similar signals in the nervous system
