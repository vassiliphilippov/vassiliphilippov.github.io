Synonyms

  • nerve tract
    6.2 0.01/m
    nerve tract is an anatomical term for a grouped bundle of nerve fibers with a common destination, while nerve pathway is a broader, descriptive phrase.
  • pathway
    6.3 4.10/m
    pathway is a general term for a route that transmits signals in the brain, while nerve pathway refers to a specific myelinated signal route.
  • tract
    7.9 0.55/m
    tract is a formal anatomical word for a bundle of nerve fibers in brain or spinal cord, while nerve pathway emphasizes myelinated signal transmission.
