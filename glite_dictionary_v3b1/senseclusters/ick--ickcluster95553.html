ick

interjection
expression of disgust
Pronunciation: / ɪk / (AmE), / ɪk / (BrE)

Ick! Gross
When she saw the moldy bread, she exclaimed, "ick!" and threw it away.
He wrinkled his nose and said, "ick, what is that smell?"
The slimy texture of the food made everyone say, "ick!" at the dinner table.
