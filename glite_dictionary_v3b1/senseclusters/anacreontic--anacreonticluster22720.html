anacreontic

adjective
light, lyrical poetry
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌænəˈkriːɑːntɪk / (AmE), / ˌænəˈkriːɒntɪk / (BrE)
relating to light, lyrical poetry, especially about love or wine, in the style of Anacreon

Examples

Anacreontic poetry
The festival featured several anacreontic songs celebrating the pleasures of life.
Her poetry, often anacreontic in tone, delighted audiences with its playful themes.
He composed an anacreontic verse about friendship and good wine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lyrical - expressing emotions in an imaginative and beautiful way
  • antonym: serious - solemn or thoughtful in character
