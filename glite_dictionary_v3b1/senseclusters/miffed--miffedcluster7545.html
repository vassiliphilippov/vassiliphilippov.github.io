miffed

adjective
slightly annoyed or irritated
Pronunciation: / mɪft / (AmE), / mɪft / (BrE)
Annoyed or slightly angry, especially because of something that seems unfair or rude.

Examples

Feeling miffed after blame
After being blamed for the error, she seemed visibly miffed during the meeting.
He sounded miffed that no one had consulted him about the plans.
She was miffed when her friends left without her.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annoyed - feeling slightly angry or irritated
  • antonym: pleased - feeling satisfied or happy
