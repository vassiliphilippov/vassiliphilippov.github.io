Himantopus mexicanus

noun
a stilt bird of the Americas with black neck and back
Pronunciation: / hɪˌmæntəpəs ˌmɛksɪˈkænəs / (AmE), / hɪˌmantəpəs ˌmɛksɪˈkanəs / (BrE)
A species of stilt found in the southwestern United States to northern South America, characterized by black plumage extending from the head down the back of the neck.
