box office

noun
total ticket sales revenue
5.4
Pronunciation: / bɑks ˈɑfɪs / (AmE), / bɒks ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
the amount of money earned from ticket sales for a film, performance, or event

Examples

Box office success
Despite positive reviews, the play was a box office disappointment.
The movie broke records at the box office during its opening weekend.
They track box office numbers to measure a film's commercial success.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gross - total earnings before deductions
  • antonym: net - earnings after all deductions
