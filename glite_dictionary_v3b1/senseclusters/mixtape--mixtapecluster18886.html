mixtape

noun
unofficial music album
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪksˌteɪp / (AmE), / ˈmɪksˌteɪp / (BrE)
an unofficial or promotional music album, often released independently by an artist and containing original tracks, remixes, or demos

Examples

An artist's mixtape
The artist released a new mixtape online to promote his upcoming tour.
Many fans discovered her music through a free mixtape she shared on social media.
He spent weeks creating a mixtape filled with exclusive tracks and remixes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: album - a collection of recorded music tracks released together
  • antonym: single - a song released individually rather than as part of an album
