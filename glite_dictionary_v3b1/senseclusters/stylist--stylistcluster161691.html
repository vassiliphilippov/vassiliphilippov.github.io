stylist

noun
artist or writer skilled in a particular style
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪlɪst / (AmE), / ˈstaɪlɪst / (BrE)
an artist or writer who is distinguished for their mastery of a particular style

Examples

Famous literary stylist
As a literary stylist, he transformed simple stories into elegant prose.

The novelist is known as a stylist whose language is both rich and precise.
