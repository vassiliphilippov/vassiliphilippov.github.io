corked

adjective
sealed with cork
Pronunciation: / kɔrkt / (AmE), / kɔːkt / (BrE)
sealed or closed with a cork, especially referring to bottles

Examples

Corked bottle
He opened a corked bottle of wine for dinner.
The old cellar was filled with corked bottles covered in dust.
Make sure the jar is properly corked to keep the contents fresh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sealed - closed tightly to prevent anything from entering or escaping
  • antonym: uncorked - having had the cork removed; not sealed with a cork
