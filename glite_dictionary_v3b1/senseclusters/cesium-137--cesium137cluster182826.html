cesium-137

noun
radioactive isotope of cesium
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːziəm wʌn ˈθɜːrti ˈsɛvən / (AmE), / ˈsiːziəm wʌn ˈθɜːti ˈsɛvən / (BrE)
a radioactive isotope of cesium, used in radiation therapy and as a tracer in nuclear studies

Examples

Cesium-137 isotope
After the nuclear accident, traces of cesium-137 were found in the surrounding environment.
Scientists use cesium-137 as a tracer to study soil erosion and sedimentation patterns.
The medical team used cesium-137 in targeted radiation therapy for cancer treatment.
