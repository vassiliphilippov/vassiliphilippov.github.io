postexperimental

adjective
after an experiment
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstɪkˌspɛrɪˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstɪkˌspɛrɪˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
relating to the period or activities that occur after an experiment has been completed

Examples

Postexperimental analysis
The postexperimental phase included detailed interviews with all participants.
Researchers conducted a postexperimental review to assess the reliability of their findings.
All data were carefully analyzed during the postexperimental period to ensure accuracy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: posttest - relating to a test given after an experiment or intervention
  • antonym: preexperimental - relating to the period or activities before an experiment
