tectonically

adverb
relating to Earth's crust
6.8
Pronunciation: / tɛkˈtɑnɪkli / (AmE), / tɛkˈtɒnɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to the movement or structure of the Earth's crust

Examples

Tectonically active region
The region is tectonically unstable, making it prone to frequent earthquakes.
Volcanoes often form in areas that are tectonically active.
Scientists study how continents move tectonically over millions of years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geologically - in a way that relates to the Earth's physical structure and substance
