volador

noun
Mexican pole dancer
Pronunciation: / voʊlɑˈðɔr / (AmE), / vəʊlɑˈdɔː / (BrE)
a participant in a traditional Mexican ceremony who performs an aerial dance by descending from a tall pole

Examples

The volador ceremony
The volador climbed the pole with skill before beginning his descent.
During the festival, each volador wore colorful traditional clothing.
Spectators watched in awe as the volador spun gracefully through the air.

  • synonym: performer - a person who entertains an audience
