lota

noun
South Asian water vessel
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊ.tə / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.tə / (BrE)
a small, round water vessel traditionally used in South Asia for storing or pouring water, typically made of brass, copper, or plastic

Examples

A brass lota
A brass lota is often used in Hindu religious rituals and daily hygiene.
During the trek, he carried a small lota for drinking and washing.
She filled the lota with fresh water from the well every morning.
