Defense Department

noun
US government department managing national defense
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɛns dɪˌpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛns dɪˌpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the federal department of the United States government responsible for safeguarding national security and overseeing the armed forces

Examples

The Defense Department headquarters

Several top generals briefed the Defense Department on the situation overseas.
The Defense Department announced a new security initiative in response to emerging threats.
