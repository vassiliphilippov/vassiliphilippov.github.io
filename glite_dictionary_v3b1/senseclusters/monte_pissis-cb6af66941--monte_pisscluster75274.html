Monte Pissis

proper noun
a high mountain in Argentina
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌmoʊnteɪ ˈpisis / (AmE), / ˌmɒnteɪ ˈpisis / (BrE)
a mountain in the Andes of Argentina, rising to 22,241 feet (6,793 meters); one of the highest peaks in South America
