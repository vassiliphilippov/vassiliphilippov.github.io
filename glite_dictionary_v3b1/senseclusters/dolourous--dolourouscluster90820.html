dolourous

adjective
showing sorrow; sorrowful
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊlərəs / (AmE), / ˈdəʊlərəs / (BrE)
Full of, expressing, or causing sorrow or grief.

Examples

A dolourous tone
She spoke in a dolourous tone, reflecting the sadness in her heart.
The knight's dolourous tale moved the entire court to tears.
The winds howled with a dolourous lament through the empty halls.
