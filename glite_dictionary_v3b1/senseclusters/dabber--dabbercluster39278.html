dabber

noun
marking tool
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdæbər / (AmE), / ˈdæbə / (BrE)
a small tool used to apply or mark with a small amount of a substance by dabbing (such as in games, crafts, or art)

Examples

A dabber tool
Each bingo player picked up a dabber to mark their numbers on the card.
She used a small dabber to apply paint to the edges of the canvas.
The craft kit included a dabber for spreading glue precisely.
She used a dabber to apply glue to the corners of the paper.
The artist dipped the dabber into the ink before pressing it onto the canvas.
At bingo night, each player marked their cards with a colorful dabber.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brush - a tool with bristles used for painting or cleaning
  • synonym: marker - a pen or tool used to make marks on a surface
