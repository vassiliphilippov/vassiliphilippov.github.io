disenfranchisement

noun
act of withdrawing rights or certification
9.5 7.4
Pronunciation: /dɪ.sɪn.ˈfræn.tʃaɪz.mənt/ /; / /dɪ.sɪn.ˈfræn.tʃɪz.mənt/ (AmE), /dɪ.sɪn.ˈfræn.tʃaɪz.mənt/ /; / /dɪ.sɪn.ˈfræn.tʃɪz.mənt/ (BrE)
the act of withdrawing the right to vote or terminating someone's rights, privileges, or certification, especially in a legal or organizational context
