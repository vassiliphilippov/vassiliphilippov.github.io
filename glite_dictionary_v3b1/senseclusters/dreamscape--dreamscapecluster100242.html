dreamscape

noun
surreal scene
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdriːmskeɪp / (AmE), / ˈdriːmskeɪp / (BrE)
a scene, image, or environment that is surreal, imaginative, or dreamlike, like something from a dream

Examples

A dreamscape scene
The artist created a dreamscape filled with floating islands and impossible colors.
Walking through the foggy forest felt like entering a mysterious dreamscape.
The movie's special effects transformed the city into a dazzling dreamscape.
The artist's painting depicted a colorful dreamscape filled with floating islands and impossible creatures.
As the music played, he felt transported into a peaceful dreamscape far from his daily worries.
The novel describes a surreal dreamscape where time and space seem to bend and shift.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fantasy - an imagined scene or situation that is not real
  • antonym: nightmare - a frightening or unpleasant dream
  • antonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist
