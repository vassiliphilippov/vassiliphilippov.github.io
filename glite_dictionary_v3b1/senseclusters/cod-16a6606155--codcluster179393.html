COD

adjective, adverb
payment upon delivery of goods
Pronunciation: / si oʊ ˈdi / (AmE), / siː əʊ ˈdiː / (BrE)
to be paid for when delivered, not in advance (collect on delivery)

Examples

COD payment
All items sent COD must be paid for upon delivery.
The company will ship your order COD if you don’t have a credit card.
You can request to pay COD when buying from some online stores.
The package will be sent COD and must be paid for upon delivery.
We ordered the furniture COD because we didn't want to pay until it arrived.
You can request COD service if you prefer not to pay online.
