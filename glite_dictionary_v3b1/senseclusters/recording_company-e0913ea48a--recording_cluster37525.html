recording company

noun
company producing and selling music recordings
3.3
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɔrdɪŋ ˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / rɪˈkɔːdɪŋ ˈkʌmpəni / (BrE)

Examples

She signed a contract with a major recording company to release her debut album.
The recording company was responsible for promoting the band's new single worldwide.
