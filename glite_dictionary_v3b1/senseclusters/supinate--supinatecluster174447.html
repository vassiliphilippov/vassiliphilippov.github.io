supinate

verb
turn palm upward; rotate limb outward
9.9 0.03/m

to turn the hand or forearm so that the palm faces upward or forward; or to rotate the leg or foot outward.

Examples

To supinate the wrist
Physical therapists may instruct patients to supinate their wrists as part of rehabilitation exercises.
To catch the ball properly, you need to supinate your forearm so your palm faces up.
When running, some people supinate their feet, putting stress on the outer edges.
