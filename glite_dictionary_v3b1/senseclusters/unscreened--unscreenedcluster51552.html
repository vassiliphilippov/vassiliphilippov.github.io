unscreened

adjective
not covered by screen
4.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈskriːnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈskriːnd / (BrE)
not protected or covered by a screen, mesh, or barrier; open to insects, view, or exposure

Examples

Unscreened window
We left the unscreened window open and mosquitoes came inside.
The porch was unscreened, so we had to deal with bugs in the evening.
He enjoyed the view from the unscreened balcony, despite the occasional bird flying in.

Related Terms

  • synonym: open - not closed or covered; exposed
  • antonym: screened - protected or covered by a screen or mesh
