bowling green

noun
field for playing bowls
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊlɪŋ ɡriːn / (AmE), / ˈbəʊlɪŋ ɡriːn / (BrE)
a level area of closely mowed grass used for playing the game of bowls

Examples

The bowling green area
After heavy rain, the bowling green needed extra care before matches could resume.
The tournament was held on the beautifully maintained bowling green at the local club.
Visitors admired the way the bowling green was trimmed and perfectly level.
