out of hand

adverb
not under control
Pronunciation: / aʊt əv hænd / (AmE), / aʊt əv hand / (BrE)
(of a situation) not under control; difficult or impossible to manage.

Examples

Get out of hand
His spending habits have spiraled out of hand this month.
If the situation gets out of hand, call the authorities immediately.
The party quickly got out of hand when uninvited guests arrived.

Related Terms

  • antonym: in hand - under control; manageable
  • synonym: out of control - not under control; unmanageable
