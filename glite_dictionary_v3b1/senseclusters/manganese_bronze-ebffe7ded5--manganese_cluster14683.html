manganese bronze

noun
hard brass alloy with manganese
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈmæŋ.ɡə.niːz brɑːnz/ /; / /ˈmæŋ.ɡə.nɪz brɑːnz/ (AmE), /ˈmæŋ.ɡə.niːz brɒnz/ (BrE)

Examples
