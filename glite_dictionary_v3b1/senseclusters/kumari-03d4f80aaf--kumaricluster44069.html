Kumari

proper noun
living goddess in Nepal
Pronunciation: / kuːˈmɑːri / (AmE), / kuːˈmɑːri / (BrE)
a prepubescent girl chosen and worshipped as a living goddess in Nepalese tradition

Examples

The Kumari goddess
Many people travel to Kathmandu to receive blessings from the Kumari during important festivals.
The Kumari is selected through a rigorous process based on ancient traditions.
Photographs of the Kumari are often seen in temples and cultural exhibitions in Nepal.

  • synonym: deity - a god or goddess
  • synonym: goddess - a female deity
