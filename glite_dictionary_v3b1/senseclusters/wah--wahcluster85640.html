wah

noun
musical sound effect
8.8
Pronunciation: / wɑ / (AmE), / wɑː / (BrE)
a distinctive sound effect in music, especially guitar, produced by a wah-wah pedal or similar device.

Examples

Guitar wah effect
The guitarist used a wah to make the solo stand out during the concert.
You can hear the classic wah effect in many funk and rock songs.
He pressed the pedal to add a dramatic wah to the riff.
