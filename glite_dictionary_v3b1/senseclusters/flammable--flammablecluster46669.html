Synonyms

  • combustible
    5.1 0.8/m
    combustible describes materials that burn easily and appears in formal safety texts, while flammable is the common everyday warning.
  • inflammable
    5.2 0.1/m
    inflammable is an older form that also means easily set on fire but can confuse learners, while flammable is clearer in use.
  • ignitable
    5.6 0.00/m
    ignitable sounds technical and points to catching fire from a spark or flame, while flammable is the general, everyday danger term.
