flammable

adjective
easily set on fire
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈflæm.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈflæm.ə.bəl / (BrE)
Easily set on fire; able to catch fire and burn quickly.

Examples

Flammable liquids
Store all flammable liquids in a safe, designated area away from heat sources.
The warning label indicates that the contents are flammable and should not be exposed to flames.
This material is highly flammable, so handle it with extreme care.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inflammable - easily set on fire; alternative word for flammable
  • antonym: nonflammable - not easily set on fire
