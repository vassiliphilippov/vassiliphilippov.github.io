hotheadedness

noun
impulsive anger
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑtˌhɛdɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈhɒtˌhɛdɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being quick to anger or act impulsively without thinking

Examples

His hotheadedness problem
His hotheadedness often got him into trouble at work.
She regretted her hotheadedness after shouting at her friend during the argument.
Because of his hotheadedness, he made decisions he later wished he could take back.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmness - the quality of being calm and not easily angered
  • synonym: impulsiveness - the tendency to act without thinking
  • synonym: rashness - the quality of acting without careful thought
