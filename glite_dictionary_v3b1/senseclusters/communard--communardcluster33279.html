communard

noun
commune member
9.1 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / Àåk…ëÀêmj…ôÀàn…ërd / (AmE), / Àåk…ímj äÀàn…ëÀêd / (BrE)
a person who lives in or supports a commune where property and responsibilities are shared among members

Examples

Modern communard
As a dedicated communard, she believed in sharing all resources equally among the group.
The communards held weekly meetings to discuss communal responsibilities and future plans.
He became a communard after seeking a lifestyle based on cooperation and mutual support.
