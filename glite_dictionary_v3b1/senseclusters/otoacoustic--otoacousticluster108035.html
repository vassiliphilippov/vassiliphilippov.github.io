otoacoustic

adjective
inner ear sounds
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊtoʊəˈkuːstɪk / (AmE), / ˌəʊtəʊəˈkuːstɪk / (BrE)
relating to sounds that are produced by the inner ear, often used in hearing tests

Examples

Otoacoustic emissions
Doctors use otoacoustic emissions to check the hearing ability of infants.
An otoacoustic test can help detect early signs of hearing loss.
The otoacoustic response is an important indicator of inner ear health.
