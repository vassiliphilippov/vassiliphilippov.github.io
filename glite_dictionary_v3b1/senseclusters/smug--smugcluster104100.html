smug

adjective
excessively self-satisfied or complacent
2.1
Pronunciation: / smʌɡ / (AmE), / smʌɡ / (BrE)
having or showing an excessive pride in oneself or one's achievements, often in a way that irritates others

Examples

A smug smile
Don't look so smug; the work isn't finished yet.
His smug attitude annoyed everyone in the room.
She wore a smug expression after winning the competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: complacent - showing uncritical satisfaction with oneself
  • antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low view of one's importance
