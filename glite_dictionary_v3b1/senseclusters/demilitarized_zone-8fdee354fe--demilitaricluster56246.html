Examples

A demilitarized zone
After years of negotiation, the nations agreed to establish a demilitarized zone as a buffer area.
The peace treaty specified that both armies must withdraw from the demilitarized zone by the end of the month.
Tourists are not allowed to enter the demilitarized zone along the border between the two countries.
