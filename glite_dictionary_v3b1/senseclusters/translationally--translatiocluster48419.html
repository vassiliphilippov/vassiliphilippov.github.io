translationally

adverb
in relation to translation
8.1
Pronunciation: / trænzˈleɪʃənəli / (AmE), / trænzˈleɪʃənəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to translation, especially in scientific or technical contexts such as biology or physics

Examples

Translationally study a gene
The gene was studied translationally to understand how it affects protein synthesis.
Researchers approached the problem translationally, applying findings from basic science to clinical practice.
The molecule behaves differently when analyzed translationally rather than rotationally.

Related Terms

  • synonym: translation - the process of moving something from one place or form to another, or converting genetic information into proteins
