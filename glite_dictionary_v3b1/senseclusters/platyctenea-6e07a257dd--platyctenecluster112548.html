Platyctenea

noun
order of marine ctenophores
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌplætɪkˈtiːniə / (AmE), / ˌplætɪkˈtiːniə / (BrE)
an order of marine invertebrates within the phylum Ctenophora, characterized by flattened, often creeping bodies and absence of tentacles in adults
