Synonyms

  • collusive
    8.6 0.26/m
    collusive describes secret, often illegal agreement between parties, while cozy suggests a friendly but secretly joint and self-serving relationship.
  • in cahoots
    7.6 0.51/m
    in cahoots means secretly working together for dishonest ends, while cozy suggests a closer, comfortable partnership with hidden benefit.
  • hand in glove
    7.0 0.13/m
    hand in glove describes very close, often suspicious cooperation, while cozy implies a warm, snug relationship that may hide collusion.

  • synonym: complicit - involved with others in an illegal or morally wrong activity
