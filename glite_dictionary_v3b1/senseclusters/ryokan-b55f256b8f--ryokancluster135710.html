Ryokan

proper noun
Japanese Zen poet
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɾjoʊˈkɑːn / (AmE), / ɾjəʊˈkæn / (BrE)
a Japanese Zen Buddhist monk and poet (1758–1831) known for his simple, nature-inspired poetry and calligraphy
