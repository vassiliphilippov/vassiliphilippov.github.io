unnaturally

adverb
in an unnatural way
1.0 3.8
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈnætʃ.ɜːr.ə.liː/ /; / /ʌn.ˈnætʃ.ɜːr.ə.liː/ (AmE), /ə.ˈnætʃ.ə.rə.liː/ /; / /ʌn.ˈnætʃ.ə.rə.liː/ (BrE)
