vainly

adverb
without success; to no avail
5.5 0.95/m

without success or result; in a way that produces no desired effect

Examples

To search vainly
He searched vainly for his missing keys until late at night.
She vainly hoped that things would improve, but nothing changed.
The team vainly attempted to score a goal in the final minutes of the match.
