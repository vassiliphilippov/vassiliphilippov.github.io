millrun

noun
water channel for a millwheel
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlˌrʌn / (AmE), / ˈmɪlˌrʌn / (BrE)
a channel that conducts the flow of water which turns a millwheel

Examples

The millrun channel
After heavy rain, debris clogged the millrun, causing the mill to stop.
Engineers designed the millrun to maximize water flow to the wheel.
The old mill still used the original millrun to channel water from the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: millrace - channel carrying water to or from a watermill wheel
