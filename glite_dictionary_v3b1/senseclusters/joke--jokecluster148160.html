joke

noun
something said or done to make people laugh
Pronunciation: / doʊk /; / dʒoʊk / (AmE), / dʒəʊk / (BrE)
something that is said or done to make people laugh; a funny story, remark, or action

Examples

A funny joke
He can't tell when someone is making a joke or being serious.
She told a hilarious joke at the party that made everyone laugh.
The comedian's jokes were the highlight of the evening.
He played a bizarre joke by arriving at work dressed as a clown.
Her idea of a joke was to swap all the office chairs around overnight.
The outrageous joke left everyone in the room shocked but laughing.
The whole meeting felt like a joke because everyone kept making puns.
Their friendly rivalry was all in good fun and considered a harmless joke.
There was so much laughter at the party because every activity turned into a joke.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gag - a joke or an amusing story; something said to provoke laughter
  • synonym: jest - a thing said or done for amusement; a joke
  • synonym: prank - a mischievous act or trick played for fun
  • antonym: seriousness - the opposite of being humorous or making jokes
