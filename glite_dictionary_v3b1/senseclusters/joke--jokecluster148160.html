Synonyms

  • gag
    3.7 2.49/m
    gag is a short, often staged comic trick or one-line remark to make people laugh, while joke is any funny story or action.
  • jest
    4.2 1.43/m
    jest is an old-fashioned or playful remark used in light talk or writing, while joke is a common, everyday funny remark or action.
  • funny story
    1.9 1.61/m
    funny story is a told account with a clear punchline and longer shape, while joke covers short remarks, acts, or stories that make people laugh.
