unlogged

adjective
not recorded
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɔgd / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɒgd / (BrE)
not recorded in a log or not tracked in a system or record

Examples

Unlogged entries
The technician discovered several unlogged entries in the database after the audit.
Because the transaction was unlogged, it was difficult to trace the source of the error.
Sensitive changes should never remain unlogged in a secure system.

Related Terms

  • antonym: logged - recorded in a log or system
  • synonym: unrecorded - not written down or officially documented
