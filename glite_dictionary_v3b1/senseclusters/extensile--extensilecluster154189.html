extensile

adjective
capable of being extended or stretched
4.1
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɛn.saɪl / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛn.saɪl / (BrE)
capable of being extended, stretched, or opened out; able to be protruded beyond the usual limits.

Examples

Extensile parts stretch
Many plants have extensile parts that allow them to adapt to their environment.
The frog's extensile tongue helps it catch insects at a remarkable distance.
The robotic arm features an extensile segment for reaching tight spaces.
