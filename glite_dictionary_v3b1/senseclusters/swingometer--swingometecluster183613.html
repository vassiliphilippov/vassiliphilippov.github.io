swingometer

noun
trend indicator graphic
Pronunciation: / swɪŋˈɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / swɪŋˈɒmɪtə / (BrE)
a chart, graphic, or device used to show changes in voting patterns, election results, or public opinion, especially during elections or on television
