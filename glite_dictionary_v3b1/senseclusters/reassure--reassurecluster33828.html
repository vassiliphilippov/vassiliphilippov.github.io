reassure

verb
give comfort or confidence; remove doubts
1.0 2.9
Pronunciation: /riː.ə.ˈʃʊr/ /; / /riː.ə.ˈʃɜːr/ (AmE), /riː.ə.ˈʃʊə/ /; / /rɪ.ə.ˈʃʊə/ (BrE)

Examples

To reassure a friend
Can you reassure me that everything will be fine?
His calm voice helped to reassure the nervous passengers.
The mother tried to reassure her child during the thunderstorm.
