woodie

noun
vintage car with wood
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊdi / (AmE), / ˈwʊdi / (BrE)
a vintage car, especially from the 1930s to 1950s, with wooden panels on its bodywork

Examples

A classic woodie car
He proudly drove his restored woodie along the coast during the summer festival.
The museum displayed a rare 1947 woodie as part of its classic car collection.
Many surfers in the 1950s used a woodie to transport their boards to the beach.

Related Terms

  • synonym: classic car - an old, often collectible, automobile
  • synonym: station wagon - a car with a large rear cargo area and a rear door
