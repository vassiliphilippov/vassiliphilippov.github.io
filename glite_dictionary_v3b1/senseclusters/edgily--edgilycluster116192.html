edgily

adverb
provocatively
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɛdʒɪli / (AmE), / ˈɛdʒɪli / (BrE)
in a provocative, daring, or unconventional manner that may shock or unsettle others

Examples

Paint edgily
The artist painted edgily, using unexpected colors and bold strokes to challenge the audience.
She spoke edgily about topics that most people avoided in polite conversation.
The comedian performed edgily, pushing boundaries with jokes that made some people uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boldly - in a confident and daring way
  • antonym: conventionally - in a traditional or usual way
  • synonym: provocatively - in a way that is likely to provoke or shock
