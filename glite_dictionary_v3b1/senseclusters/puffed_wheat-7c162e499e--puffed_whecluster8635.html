puffed wheat

noun
light, expanded wheat grains (cereal)
1.7 1.7
Pronunciation: /pʌft wiːt/ /; / /pʌft hwiːt/ (AmE), /pʌft wiːt/ /; / /pʌft hwiːt/ (BrE)
