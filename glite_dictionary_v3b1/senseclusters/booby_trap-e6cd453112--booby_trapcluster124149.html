booby trap

noun
unexpected difficulty or hidden danger
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːbi ˌtræp / (AmE), / ˈbuːbi ˌtræp / (BrE)
a hidden or unexpected problem or difficulty that is intended to catch someone by surprise

Examples

A booby trap in plans
Learning a new language can be straightforward, but there are always some booby traps along the way.
Negotiating the new regulations presented several booby traps for unwary businesses.
The contract was full of booby traps that caught many inexperienced parties off guard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pitfall - a hidden or unsuspected danger or difficulty
  • synonym: snag - an unexpected or hidden obstacle or drawback
