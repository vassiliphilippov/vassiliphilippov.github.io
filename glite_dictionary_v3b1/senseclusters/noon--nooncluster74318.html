noon

noun
midday; 12 o'clock in the daytime
Pronunciation: / nuːn / (AmE), / nuːn / (BrE)
The time of day when the sun is at its highest point, typically 12 o'clock in the daytime; midday.

Examples

At noon time
By noon, the sun was directly overhead, making it hard to find shade.
The meeting is scheduled to start promptly at noon tomorrow.
We always take our lunch break at noon when the office gets quiet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: midday - the middle part of the day; noon
