religionless

adjective
without religion
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlɪdʒənləs / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒənləs / (BrE)
not having, showing, or connected with any religion

Examples

Religionless person
He described himself as religionless and uninterested in spiritual matters.
The novel explores a religionless society where faith plays no role in daily life.
Some people prefer a religionless approach to ethics and morality.

Related Terms

  • antonym: religious - having or showing belief in religion
  • synonym: secular - not connected with religious matters
