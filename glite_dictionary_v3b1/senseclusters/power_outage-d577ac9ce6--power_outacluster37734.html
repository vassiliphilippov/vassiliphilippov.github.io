A period of time when the supply of electricity is interrupted, usually due to equipment failure or external events.

Examples

Frequent power outages
Frequent power outages have affected businesses in the area.
The recent storm caused a power outage that lasted several hours in our neighborhood.
We had to use candles during the power outage last night.

Synonyms

  • power failure
    3.4 0.63/m
    a power failure is an interruption of electricity often caused by equipment faults, while a power outage is the general event.
  • blackout
    3.9 2.46/m
    a blackout is a complete loss of power that often causes darkness, while a power outage is any interruption of electricity supply.
  • outage
    4.0 4.90/m
    an outage is a temporary stop to a service like electricity or internet, while a power outage refers specifically to electricity loss.
