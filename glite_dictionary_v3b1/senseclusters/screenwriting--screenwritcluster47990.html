screenwriting

noun
writing film scripts
Pronunciation: / ˈskriːnˌraɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskriːnˌraɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity, skill, or profession of writing scripts for movies or television shows

Examples

Good screenwriting can turn an ordinary story into an unforgettable film.
