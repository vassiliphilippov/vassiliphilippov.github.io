email

verb
send an electronic message
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈiːˌmeɪl / (AmE), / ˈiːˌmeɪl / (BrE)

Examples

To email a friend

I'll email you the details as soon as I get them.
She emailed her boss to request a day off next week.
