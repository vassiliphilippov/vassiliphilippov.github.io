deodorizer

noun
odor-removing product
3.5
Pronunciation: / diˈoʊdəˌraɪzər / (AmE), / diˈəʊdəraɪzə /; / diˈəʊdəˌraɪzə / (BrE)
a substance or device used to remove or mask unpleasant odors

Examples

A deodorizer spray
She placed a deodorizer in the closet to keep it smelling fresh.
The car's interior smelled better after using a new deodorizer under the seats.
Many people use a deodorizer in the kitchen to eliminate cooking smells.
After cleaning the kitchen, she used a deodorizer to eliminate the lingering smell of fish.
Many people keep a deodorizer in their cars to keep the air fresh.
The hotel room had a pleasant scent thanks to a discreet deodorizer placed near the vent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: air freshener - a product that adds a pleasant scent to the air
  • antonym: odor - an unpleasant smell
  • degree lower: perfume - a liquid with a pleasant smell, used on the body or in a room
