protozoologist

noun
scientist specializing in protozoa
5.5 8.7
Pronunciation: /proʊ.tə.zuː.ˈɑː.lə.dʒɪst/ /; / /proʊ.toʊ.zuː.ˈɑː.lə.dʒɪst/ (AmE), /prəʊ.tə.zuː.ˈɒl.ə.dʒɪst/ (BrE)
