mane

noun
long hair on an animal's neck
5.3 1.0
Pronunciation: / meɪn / (AmE), / meɪn / (BrE)
the long, thick hair that grows from the top or sides of the neck of some animals, such as horses or lions

Examples

A horse's mane
She brushed the horse's mane until it was smooth and glossy.
The lion's mane glistened in the sunlight as it roared.
The stallion tossed its thick mane back as it galloped across the field.
