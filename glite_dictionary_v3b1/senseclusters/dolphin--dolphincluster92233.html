dolphin

noun
a small toothed whale with a beaklike snout
2.9 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːlfɪn / (AmE), / ˈdɒlfɪn / (BrE)
a small, intelligent marine mammal with a streamlined body and a beaklike snout, belonging to the toothed whale family and larger than a porpoise

Examples

A playful dolphin
A group of dolphins swam alongside our boat as we crossed the bay.
Scientists have found that the dolphin is one of the most intelligent marine animals.
The aquarium has a new exhibit featuring a trained dolphin performing tricks for the audience.
