uncaught

adjective
not captured
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkɔt / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɔːt / (BrE)
not captured, detected, or apprehended, especially after an attempt to do so

Examples

An uncaught thief
The rare bird remained uncaught despite many attempts by wildlife photographers.
Several errors in the report went uncaught until the final review.
The hacker stayed uncaught for months before finally being identified.

Related Terms

  • antonym: captured - caught or taken into custody
  • synonym: unnoticed - not seen or observed
