vascular plant

noun
plant with vascular system (xylem and phloem)
3.9 0.16/m

A plant that has a specialized conducting system for transporting water, minerals, and nutrients, including ferns, gymnosperms, and angiosperms.

Examples

Vascular plant system
Ferns are a type of vascular plant that reproduce via spores rather than seeds.
The majority of land flora are vascular plants, which include trees, shrubs, and flowering plants.
Unlike mosses, a vascular plant has specialized tissues for transporting water and nutrients.

Synonyms
