sonde

noun
scientific probe
Pronunciation: / sɑnd / (AmE), / sɒnd / (BrE)
a scientific instrument or probe used to collect data from the atmosphere, ocean, or other environments

Examples

Weather sonde launch
The research team launched a weather balloon equipped with a sonde to gather temperature and humidity data.
A special sonde was lowered into the ocean to measure salinity at different depths.
Technicians checked the sonde before sending it into the atmosphere for data collection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: probe - an instrument used to explore or measure something
  • synonym: sensor - a device that detects or measures physical properties
