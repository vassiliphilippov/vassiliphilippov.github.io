acceptor

noun
chemical compound that receives electrons
Pronunciation: / əkˈsɛptɚ /; / əkˈsɛptər / (AmE), / əkˈsɛptə / (BrE)
a person, thing, or substance that receives or accepts something, especially in science or technology (such as an atom that receives electrons)
