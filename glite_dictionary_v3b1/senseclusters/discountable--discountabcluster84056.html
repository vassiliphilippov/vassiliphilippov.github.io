discountable

adjective
eligible for discount
5.7
Pronunciation: /dɪs.ˈkaʊn.tə.bəl/ (AmE), /dɪs.ˈkaʊn.tə.bəl/ (BrE)
able to be reduced in value by applying a discount rate, or eligible to be sold or discounted before maturity, especially in finance.

Examples

Discountable cash flows
Only cash flows that are discountable were included in the project's net present value estimate.
The bank accepts discountable bills that can be sold before their maturity date.
Not all securities are discountable under the current financial regulations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nonnegotiable - not able to be transferred or discounted
  • synonym: redeemable - able to be exchanged for money or goods
