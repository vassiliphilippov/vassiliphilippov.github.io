mesial

adjective
toward the midline of the body or dental arch
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːziəl / (AmE), / ˈmiːziəl / (BrE)
situated in or directed toward the midline or mesial plane of the body or dental arch

Examples

Mesial tooth direction
Orthodontists often refer to mesial and distal directions when discussing tooth movement.
Pain was noted on the mesial surface of the molar during examination.
The surgeon made an incision on the mesial side of the tooth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: distal - farther from the midline or center of the body or dental arch
