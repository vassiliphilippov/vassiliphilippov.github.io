overprescribe

verb
to prescribe excessively
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərprɪˈskraɪb / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəprɪˈskraɪb / (BrE)
to prescribe a drug or treatment more often or in greater amounts than necessary

Examples

To overprescribe antibiotics
Doctors should avoid the temptation to overprescribe antibiotics for minor illnesses.
Some experts warn that painkillers are often overprescribed in modern healthcare systems.
The government is introducing new guidelines to prevent physicians from overprescribing certain medications.

Related Terms

  • antonym: underprescribe - to prescribe less medication than is necessary
