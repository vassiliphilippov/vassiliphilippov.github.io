parkour

noun
athletic discipline involving efficient movement through obstacles
6.5
Pronunciation: / pɑrˈkʊr / (AmE), / pɑːˈkʊə / (BrE)
An athletic discipline involving efficient movement through obstacles in the environment, typically by running, jumping, climbing, and vaulting.

Examples

Parkour training
He has been practicing parkour for years and can jump across rooftops with ease.
The city park became a popular place for young people to try parkour on the weekends.
Videos of impressive parkour stunts have gone viral on social media.
