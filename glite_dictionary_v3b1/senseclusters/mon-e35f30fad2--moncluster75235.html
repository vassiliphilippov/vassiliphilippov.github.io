Mon

noun
abbreviation for Monday
Pronunciation: / mʌn / (AmE), / mʌn / (BrE)
An abbreviation for Monday, the second day of the week and the first day of the standard working week in many countries.

Examples

Mon morning
I usually feel tired on Mon mornings after the weekend.
She has a dentist appointment scheduled for Mon at 10 a.m.
The office will be closed on Mon due to the holiday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Monday - the second day of the week; the day following Sunday
