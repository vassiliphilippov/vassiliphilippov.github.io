Faux News

noun
false or misleading news
2.9
Pronunciation: / foʊˈnuːz / (AmE), / fəʊˈnjuːz / (BrE)
False or misleading news, especially when perceived as intentionally biased or deceptive.

Examples

Faux News websites
Critics argue that certain websites are major sources of Faux News.

Some social media posts have been flagged for spreading Faux News during the election.
