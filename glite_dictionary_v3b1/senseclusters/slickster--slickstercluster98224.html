slickster

noun
cunning or deceitful person
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪkstɚ / (AmE), / ˈslɪkstə / (BrE)
a person who is clever in a cunning or deceitful way

Examples

A slickster person
He may seem friendly, but he's a slickster who always has a hidden agenda.
The salesman turned out to be a real slickster, tricking people into bad deals.
Watch out for that slickster at the poker table; he's not to be trusted.

Related Terms

  • synonym: con artist - a person who deceives others by trickery
  • antonym: naive - showing lack of experience, wisdom, or judgement (opposite in social cunning)
  • synonym: swindler - a person who cheats others out of money or assets
