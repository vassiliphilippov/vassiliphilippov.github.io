flow off

(liquid) move away gradually

(of a liquid) to move away from a place gradually, typically in a steady stream.

Examples

Water flows off roof

Synonyms

  • flow away
    3.2 0.09/m
    flow away moves gradually with a current over distance, while flow off often means liquid runs away from a surface edge.
  • flow out
    2.9 1.00/m
    flow out pours or spills outward from inside something in a clear stream, while flow off implies moving away along a surface.
  • run off
    2.9 0.48/m
    run off drains away quickly, often as waste or rainwater down surfaces, while flow off suggests a slower steady movement.
  • drain
    1.0 16/m
    drain means liquid comes out or is removed to empty something, while flow off focuses on liquid moving away slowly in a stream.
