repose on

verb
to be based on (formal/theories)
3.7 6.5
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈpoʊz ɑːn/ /; / /rə.ˈpoʊz ɑːn/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈpəʊz ɒn/ /; / /rə.ˈpəʊz ɒn/ (BrE)
(formal) to be based on, or to depend on something as a foundation, especially for ideas, arguments, or theories.

Examples

Reposes on firm principles
His argument reposes on well-established scientific principles.
The credibility of the report reposed on thorough research and impartial analysis.
These laws repose on centuries of tradition and legal precedent.
