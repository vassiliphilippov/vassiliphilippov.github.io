mid-ocean

noun
central part of ocean
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪdˈoʊʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪdˈəʊʃən / (BrE)
the central part of an ocean, far from any land or coast

Examples

Lost in the mid-ocean
The sailors spent weeks in the mid-ocean without seeing any land.
A powerful storm developed while the ship was in the mid-ocean.
Scientists study marine life found only in the mid-ocean regions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coast - the land next to the sea
  • synonym: open sea - the part of the sea that is far from land
