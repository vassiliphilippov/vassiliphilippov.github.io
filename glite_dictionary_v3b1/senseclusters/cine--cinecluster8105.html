cine

noun
medical video sequence
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪni / (AmE), / ˈsɪni / (BrE)
a short sequence of moving images recorded by medical or scientific imaging equipment for review or analysis

Examples

A medical cine
The radiologist reviewed the cine to assess the patient's heart function.
A cine was created during the MRI to show the blood flow in real time.
Doctors often use a cine to analyze how well the valves in the heart are working.

Related Terms

  • antonym: still image - a single static picture, not moving
  • synonym: video - a recording of moving visual images
