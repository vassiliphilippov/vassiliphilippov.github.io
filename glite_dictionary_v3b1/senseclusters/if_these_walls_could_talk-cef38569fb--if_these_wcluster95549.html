if these walls could talk

x
expression about hidden stories in a place
Pronunciation: / ɪf ðiz wɔlz kəd tɑk / (AmE), / ɪf ðiːz wɔːlz kəd tɔːk / (BrE)
used to suggest that a place could reveal secrets or stories from the past if it were able to speak

Examples

If these walls could talk stories
After years of living in the apartment, she often joked, 'If these walls could talk, they'd have plenty to say about us.'
He glanced around the abandoned factory and whispered, 'If these walls could talk, what stories would they tell?'
Whenever I walk into the old library, I think to myself, if these walls could talk.
