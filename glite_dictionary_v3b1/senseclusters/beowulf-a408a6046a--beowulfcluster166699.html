Beowulf

proper noun
legendary Old English epic hero
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪ.oʊˌwʊlf / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.əʊ.wʊlf / (BrE)
the legendary hero of the Old English epic poem 'Beowulf,' who slays the monster Grendel, becomes king, and later dies fighting a dragon

Examples

Beowulf the hero
In the poem, Beowulf becomes a wise and respected king before his tragic death.
Many scholars study Beowulf to learn more about Old English literature.
The story of Beowulf tells of his battles with both Grendel and a fearsome dragon.
