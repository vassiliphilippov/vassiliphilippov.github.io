per diem

adverb
for each day; daily
Pronunciation: / pɚ ˈdiːəm / (AmE), / pə ˈdiːəm / (BrE)
for each day; daily; for every day

Examples

Paid per diem
Each research assistant receives a small stipend per diem while in the field.
Hotel guests are charged for parking per diem in addition to their room rates.
The company allows a travel expense reimbursement of fifty dollars per diem.
