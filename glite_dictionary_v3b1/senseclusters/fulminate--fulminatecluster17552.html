fulminate

verb
criticize vehemently or protest
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊlmɪˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈfʌlmɪneɪt / (BrE)
to express vehement protest or criticism

Examples

To fulminate against rules
Editorials across the country fulminated against the new tax proposal.
She often fulminates whenever the topic of environmental policy arises.
The senator fulminated against corruption in the government during his speech.

Related Terms
