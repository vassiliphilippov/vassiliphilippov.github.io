boot

noun
sturdy footwear covering foot and lower leg
Pronunciation: / but / (AmE), / buːt / (BrE)
A type of sturdy footwear that covers the whole foot and usually part or all of the lower leg, typically used for protection or fashion.

Examples

A pair of boots
He put on his boots before going out into the snow.
Her hiking boots are sturdy and waterproof for the mountain trails.
The children left muddy boots by the door after playing outside.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: sandal - open footwear consisting of a sole and straps, worn in warm weather
  • synonym: shoe - a covering for the foot, not extending above the ankle
