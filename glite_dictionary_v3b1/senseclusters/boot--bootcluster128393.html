boot

noun
sturdy footwear covering foot and lower leg
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /buːt/ (AmE), /buːt/ (BrE)
A type of sturdy footwear that covers the whole foot and usually part or all of the lower leg, typically used for protection or fashion.
