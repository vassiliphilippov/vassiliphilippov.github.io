chypre

noun
perfume style
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃiːprə / (AmE), / ˈʃiːprə / (BrE)
a type of perfume with citrus top notes and a mossy, woody base, often including oakmoss and patchouli

Examples

A chypre perfume
She always wore a chypre fragrance because she loved its earthy and sophisticated scent.
The new perfume is inspired by the classic chypre style, blending citrus and mossy notes.
Many vintage perfumes belong to the chypre family, known for their unique combination of ingredients.
