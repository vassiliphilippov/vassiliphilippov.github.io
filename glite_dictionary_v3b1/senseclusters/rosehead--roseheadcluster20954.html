rosehead

noun
decorative stud
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊzˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzˌhɛd / (BrE)
a nail, tack, stud, or button with a rose-shaped head, used for decoration or fastening (especially in furniture, clothing, or construction)
