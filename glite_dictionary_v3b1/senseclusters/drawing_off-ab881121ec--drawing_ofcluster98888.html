drawing off

noun
removal or draining from a source
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɔɪɪŋ ˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈdrɔːɪŋ ˌɒf / (BrE)
the act of removing or draining a liquid or other substance from a source

Examples

The drawing off of oil
After the drawing off of oil, the mixture was ready for testing.
Routine drawing off of fluids is necessary during maintenance of the machine.
The drawing off of excess water prevented flooding in the field.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: collection - the process of gathering a substance from a source
  • synonym: drainage - the process of draining liquid away from a place
