selling point

noun
feature making something attractive to buyers
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛlɪŋ pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈsɛlɪŋ pɔɪnt / (BrE)
an aspect or feature of a product or service that makes it attractive to buyers or customers

Examples

Important selling point
Energy efficiency is an important selling point for modern appliances.
One key selling point of the restaurant is its organic menu.
The spacious backyard is a major selling point for this house.
Location is often a key selling point when buying a house.
One unique selling point of their program is the lifetime support they offer.
The new smartphone's camera quality was its biggest selling point.
