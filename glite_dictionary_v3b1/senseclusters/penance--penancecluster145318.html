penance

noun
regret for past actions; act of self-punishment
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnəns / (AmE), / ˈpɛnəns / (BrE)
a feeling of regret for wrongdoing, or an act of self-punishment or self-discipline to show repentance or atone for a mistake

Examples

Do penance for mistakes
As penance, he apologized and promised to change his behavior.
He did extra volunteer work at the shelter as a form of penance for his mistakes.
She felt a deep penance after realizing how her actions had hurt her friend.
As penance for his mistake, he volunteered at the shelter every weekend for a month.
He confessed his lies and performed penance as instructed by the priest.
She walked barefoot to the shrine as an act of penance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atonement - the act of making amends for wrongdoing
  • synonym: remorse - deep regret or guilt for a wrong committed
  • synonym: repentance - sincere regret or remorse for wrongdoing
