annalist

noun
historian who writes annals
5.5 7.8
Pronunciation: /ˈæn.ə.lɪst/ (AmE), /ˈæn.ə.lɪst/ (BrE)
a historian who records events in chronological order, especially in the form of annals.

Examples

An annalist records events

The medieval annalist carefully recorded each year's notable events in the monastery's manuscript.
The work of an annalist provides invaluable insights into past societies.
