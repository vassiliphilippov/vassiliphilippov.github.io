meat puppet

noun
mindlessly controlled person
Pronunciation: / mit ˈpʌpɪt / (AmE), / miːt ˈpʌpɪt / (BrE)
a person who behaves or acts mindlessly, typically at the direction or control of others

Examples

Meat puppet controlled
After the intense meeting, she felt like a meat puppet, simply doing whatever her boss demanded.
He realized he had been a meat puppet for the company, never voicing his own ideas.
Online trolls create fake accounts as meat puppets to sway opinions in discussions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: puppet - a person or figure whose actions are controlled by someone else
