smudginess

noun
blurred quality
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈsmʌdʒ.ɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈsmʌdʒ.ɪ.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality or state of being smudged, blurred, or marked by stains or smears
