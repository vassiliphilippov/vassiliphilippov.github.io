proximo

adjective
of the next month
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑksɪˌmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈprɒksɪməʊ / (BrE)
(used especially in formal writing) of or relating to the next month after the current one.

Examples

Proximo month
He referenced the letter dated the 10th proximo in his reply.
The meeting is set for the 3rd proximo, so please mark your calendar.
Your payment is due on the 15th proximo according to the contract terms.

  • antonym: ultimo - of or relating to the previous month
