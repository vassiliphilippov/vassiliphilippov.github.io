apt

adjective
suitable or appropriate; fitting
8.5
Pronunciation: / æpt / (AmE), / æpt / (BrE)
suitable or appropriate in the circumstances; strikingly fitting or pertinent.

Examples

Apt example
He made an apt comparison that clarified the complex topic for everyone.
Her remarks were especially apt given the seriousness of the situation.
The quote she chose was apt for the introduction of her essay.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appropriate - suitable or proper in the circumstances
  • antonym: inappropriate - not suitable or proper in the circumstances
  • antonym: inapt - not appropriate or suitable
