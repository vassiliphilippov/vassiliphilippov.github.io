enigmatically

adverb
in a mysterious or puzzling manner
Pronunciation: / ɪˌnɪɡˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / ɪˌnɪɡˈmatɪkli / (BrE)
In a mysterious or puzzling manner; in a way that is difficult to interpret or understand.

Examples

To speak enigmatically
He spoke enigmatically about his past, leaving us all curious.
She smiled enigmatically and refused to explain her answer.
The artist left the message enigmatically hidden within the painting's details.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cryptically - in a mysterious, hidden, or ambiguous manner
  • antonym: plainly - in a clear and straightforward manner
