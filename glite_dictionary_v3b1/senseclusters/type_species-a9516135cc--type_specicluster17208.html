type species

noun
representative species of a genus (biology)
8.5
Pronunciation: / taɪp ˈspiːʃiz / (AmE), / taɪp ˈspiːʃiːz / (BrE)
(biology) The species that is considered the representative example of the defining features of its genus.

Examples

The type species example
The giant panda serves as the type species for the genus Ailuropoda.
The selection of a type species helps stabilize the nomenclature of a genus.
Zea mays is the type species of the genus Zea.
