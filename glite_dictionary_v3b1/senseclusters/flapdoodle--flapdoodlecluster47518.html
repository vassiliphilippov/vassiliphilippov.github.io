flapdoodle

noun
nonsense; silly ideas
Pronunciation: / ˈflæpˌduːdəl / (AmE), / ˈflæpˌduːdəl / (BrE)
nonsense or silly ideas that are not true or sensible

Examples

That is flapdoodle
He dismissed the rumor as complete flapdoodle.
Don't believe that story—it's just a bunch of flapdoodle.
Whenever he talks about his adventures, it's usually pure flapdoodle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonsense - ideas or speech that are silly or have no meaning
  • synonym: rubbish - something that is not true or is foolish
  • antonym: sense - reasonable or logical ideas
