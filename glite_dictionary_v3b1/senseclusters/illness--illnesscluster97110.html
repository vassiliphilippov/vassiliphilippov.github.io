Synonyms

  • disease
    2.3 194/m
    disease is a specific medical problem that affects parts of the body, while illness is the general experience of being unwell.
  • sickness
    1.0 5.38/m
    sickness is the everyday state of being unwell or nauseous, while illness is a broader medical condition with symptoms.
  • disorder
    4.3 46/m
    disorder is a clinical term for disturbed body or mind function, while illness focuses on the person's experience of symptoms.
  • condition
    3.7 65/m
    condition is a neutral medical label for a long-term health problem, while illness highlights how the body or mind feels.
  • malady
    7.9 1.33/m
