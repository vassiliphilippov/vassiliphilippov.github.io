shark-liver oil

noun
oil from shark livers, source of vitamin A
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑrk ˌlɪvɚ ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːk ˌlɪvə ɔɪl / (BrE)
a fatty yellow to brown oil obtained from the livers of sharks, used in leather dressing and as a source of vitamin A
