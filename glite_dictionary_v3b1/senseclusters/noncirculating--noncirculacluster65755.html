noncirculating

adjective
not moving or flowing
6.9
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈsɜːr.kjə.leɪ.tɪŋ/ /; / /nən.ˈsɜːr.kjə.leɪ.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈsɜː.kjʊ.leɪ.tɪŋ/ /; / /nən.ˈsɜː.kjʊ.leɪ.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
not moving or flowing within a normal circulation system, such as blood, air, or other fluids

Examples

Noncirculating blood
The doctor found a noncirculating area of blood that needed to be drained.
In some lakes, noncirculating water can lead to low oxygen levels for fish.
A noncirculating air pocket formed behind the closed window, making the room stuffy.

  • synonym: stagnant - not flowing or moving, often becoming stale
