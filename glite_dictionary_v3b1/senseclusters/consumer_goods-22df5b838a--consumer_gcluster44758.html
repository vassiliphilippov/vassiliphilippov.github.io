consumer goods

noun
goods for personal use or consumption
Pronunciation: / kənˈsuːmɚ ɡʊdz / (AmE), / kənˈsjuːmə ɡʊdz / (BrE)
goods produced for direct use by individuals, rather than for further production or resale

Examples

Consumer goods like food
Food, clothing, and appliances are all examples of consumer goods.
Retailers are stocking more environmentally friendly consumer goods each year.
The demand for consumer goods has increased rapidly in developing countries.

Related Terms

  • antonym: capital goods - goods used to produce other goods and services
