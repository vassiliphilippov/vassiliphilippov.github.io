scentless camomile

noun
European annual weed with white flowers, no scent
8.3 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs…õntl…ôs k…ôÀàmo ämiÀêl / (AmE), / Ààs…õntl…ôs k…ôÀàm…ô ämiÀêl / (BrE)
A European annual plant with white flowers and finely divided leaves, often found as a weed and sometimes cultivated, especially in North America. Unlike true camomile, it lacks a significant scent.

Examples

Scentless camomile plant
Farmers often regard scentless camomile as a troublesome weed in their crops.
She planted scentless camomile in her garden for its attractive flowers.
The field was dotted with scentless camomile among the other wildflowers.
