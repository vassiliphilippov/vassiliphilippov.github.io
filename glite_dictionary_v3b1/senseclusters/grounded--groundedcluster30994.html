grounded

adjective
calm and sensible
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraʊndɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊndɪd / (BrE)
calm, sensible, and having a realistic understanding of life

Examples

A grounded person
Despite his fame, he remains grounded and treats everyone with respect.
Her parents taught her to stay grounded no matter how successful she becomes.
Yoga helps me feel more grounded when life gets overwhelming.

Related Terms

  • synonym: level-headed - calm and able to think clearly in difficult situations
  • synonym: practical - concerned with real situations rather than ideas or theories
  • antonym: unrealistic - not sensible or practical; not based on reality
