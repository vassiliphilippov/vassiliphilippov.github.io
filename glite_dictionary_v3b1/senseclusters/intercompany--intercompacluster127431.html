intercompany

adjective
between companies
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˌɪntəkˈʌmpəni / (BrE)
occurring or existing between two or more companies

Examples

Intercompany agreement
The two firms signed an intercompany contract to share their distribution networks.
All intercompany transactions must be carefully documented for auditing purposes.
They created an intercompany task force to coordinate the merger process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corporate - relating to a corporation or company
  • antonym: intra-company - within a single company
