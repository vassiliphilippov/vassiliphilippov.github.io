valetudinarian

noun
person overly concerned with health
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌvælətjudɪˈneriən / (AmE), / ˌvalɪtjuːdɪˈneəriən / (BrE)
a person who is weak, sickly, or excessively concerned about their own health

Examples

A valetudinarian person
Despite feeling well, Tom became a valetudinarian, constantly worrying about every minor ache or pain.
Some believe that too much time spent reading about illnesses can turn someone into a valetudinarian.
The elderly woman was dismissed as a valetudinarian by her less sympathetic relatives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hypochondriac - person with an abnormal worry about their health
