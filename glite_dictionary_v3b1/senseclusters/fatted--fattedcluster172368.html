fatted

verb
to make fat
4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈfæt.ɪd/ /; / /ˈfæt.əd/ (AmE), /ˈfæt.ɪd/ /; / /ˈfæt.əd/ (BrE)
past tense and past participle of 'fat', meaning to feed or nourish an animal or person so that they become fat; to fatten.

Examples

They fatted the animals
They fatted the pigs for several weeks before sending them to market.
The farmer fatted his cattle before the winter to ensure they survived the cold months.
In preparation for the festival, the villagers fatted a calf for the feast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fattened - made or become fat, especially by feeding
  • antonym: starved - deprived of food; made thin or weak from lack of food
