Vichy regime

noun
France's WWII collaborationist government
9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈviː.ʃiː reɪ.ˈʒiːm/ /; / /ˈviː.ʃiː rə.ˈʒiːm/ (AmE), /ˈviː.ʃiː reɪ.ˈʒiːm/ /; / /ˈviː.ʃiː rə.ˈʒiːm/ (BrE)
the French government that ruled from 1940 to 1944 during World War II after France's defeat by Germany, collaborating with Nazi authorities
