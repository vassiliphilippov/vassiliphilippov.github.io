remorselessly

adverb
without pity or mercy
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmɔrsləsli / (AmE), / rɪˈmɔːsləsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows no pity or compassion; in a merciless manner.

Examples

Remorselessly criticized others
She remorselessly criticized his every mistake without offering any constructive advice.
The company remorselessly pursued profit at the expense of its workers' well-being.
Waves remorselessly battered the shoreline throughout the night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compassionately - in a way that shows compassion or sympathy
  • synonym: mercilessly - in a way that shows no mercy; pitilessly
  • synonym: pitilessly - without pity or compassion
