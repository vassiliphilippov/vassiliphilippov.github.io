titler

noun
title creator
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪtlər / (AmE), / ˈtaɪtlə / (BrE)
a person or device that creates or adds titles, especially to films, videos, or documents

Examples

Video titler software
The video titler made sure each scene had a clear and readable heading.
She used a titler to add opening credits to her short film.
Modern editing software often includes a built-in titler for creating stylish text overlays.
