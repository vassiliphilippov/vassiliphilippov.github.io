Australian hare's foot

noun
Australian fern with hairy rhizomes
Pronunciation: / əˈstreɪliən hɛrz fʊt / (AmE), / ɒˈstreɪliən heəz fʊt / (BrE)
A species of hare's-foot fern native to Australia, belonging to the genus Davallia, known for its hairy rhizomes resembling a hare's foot.

The Australian hare's foot thrives in well-drained soil and indirect sunlight in home gardens.
