linker

noun
thing that connects
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪŋkər / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋkə / (BrE)
a person or thing that connects or joins different parts together, especially in computing or language

Examples

Software linker
In programming, a linker combines different pieces of code into a single program.
The teacher explained how a linker can join two ideas in a sentence.
A linker is essential for connecting the modules in this system.

  • antonym: separator - a thing that keeps things apart
