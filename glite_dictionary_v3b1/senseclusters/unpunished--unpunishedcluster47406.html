unpunished

adjective
not punished for a wrongdoing
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpʌnɪʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈpʌnɪʃt / (BrE)
not having been punished for wrongdoing or a crime

Examples

Unpunished crime
He feared his mistake would remain unpunished and set a bad example for others.
No severe act should be left unpunished in a fair society.
The crime did not go unpunished, as the authorities quickly caught the culprit.
