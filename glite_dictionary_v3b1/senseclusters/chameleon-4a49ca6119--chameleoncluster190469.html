Chameleon

noun
constellation in the southern sky
Pronunciation: / kəˈmiːliən / (AmE), / kəˈmiːliən / (BrE)
a faint constellation in the southern sky, located near the constellations Apus and Mensa

Examples

The Chameleon constellation
Astronomers often struggle to locate Chameleon due to its faint stars.
She included Chameleon in her star chart of southern constellations.
The Chameleon constellation is best seen from the Southern Hemisphere.
