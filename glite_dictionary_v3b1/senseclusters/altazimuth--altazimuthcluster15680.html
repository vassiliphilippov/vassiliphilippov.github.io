altazimuth

noun
instrument measuring celestial altitude and azimuth
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈæl.təˌzɪ.məθ / (AmE), / ˈæl.tə.zɪ.məθ / (BrE)
an instrument for measuring the altitude and azimuth of celestial objects, often used in navigation and astronomy

Examples

Modern altazimuth mount
Modern altazimuth mounts allow telescopes to move smoothly in both axes.
Surveyors used an altazimuth to record the position of the star.
The observatory is equipped with a new altazimuth telescope for tracking satellites.
