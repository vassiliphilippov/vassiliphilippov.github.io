storyteller

noun
person who tells lies
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔːriˌtɛlər / (AmE), / ˈstɔːriˌtɛlə / (BrE)
A person who tells lies; someone who invents untrue stories to deceive others.

Examples

A storyteller of lies
Don’t believe her excuses; she’s a skilled storyteller when she’s in trouble.
He's such a storyteller—you can never trust a word he says.
I could tell by his wild explanation that he was just being a storyteller again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: liar - a person who tells lies
  • antonym: truth-teller - a person who tells the truth
