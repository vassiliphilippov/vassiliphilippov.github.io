neuropathic

adjective
nerve-related pain
Pronunciation: / ˌnuːrəˈpæθɪk / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəˈpæθɪk / (BrE)
relating to nerve pain or disorders affecting the nervous system

Examples

Neuropathic pain
The doctor prescribed medication to help manage her neuropathic pain.
Patients with diabetes are at risk of developing neuropathic complications in their feet.
Researchers are studying new treatments for neuropathic disorders affecting the nervous system.

Related Terms

  • antonym: musculoskeletal - relating to muscles and the skeleton
  • synonym: neurological - relating to the nervous system
