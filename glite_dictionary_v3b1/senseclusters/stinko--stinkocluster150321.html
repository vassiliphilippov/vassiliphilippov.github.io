stinko

noun
very bad thing
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪŋkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋkəʊ / (BrE)
an object, performance, or event that is very bad or of poor quality

Examples

A stinko show

The new video game turned out to be a real stinko despite all the hype.
After tasting the soup, he declared it a stinko and ordered something else.
