shikker

noun
habitual drunkard
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪkər / (AmE), / ˈʃɪkə / (BrE)
a person who is often drunk or habitually drinks too much alcohol

Examples

An old shikker
The old shikker could often be found sleeping on a park bench after a long night.
Nobody wanted to lend money to the shikker because they knew he would spend it on alcohol.
At family gatherings, Uncle Joe was known as the shikker who always told wild stories.

Related Terms
