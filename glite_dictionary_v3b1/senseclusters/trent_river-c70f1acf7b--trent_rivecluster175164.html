Trent River

proper noun
river in central England
Pronunciation: / trɛnt ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / trɛnt ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in central England that flows generally northeastward to join the Ouse River and form the Humber Estuary

Examples

The Trent River flood
Flooding of the Trent River has shaped the landscape over centuries.
Many historic towns are located along the Trent River.
The Trent River plays a crucial role in draining the Midlands of England.
