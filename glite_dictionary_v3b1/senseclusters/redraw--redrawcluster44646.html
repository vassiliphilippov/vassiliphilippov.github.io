redraw

verb
to update display
3.7 0.12/m
Pronunciation: / ËŒriËËˆdrÉ” / (AmE), / ËŒriËËˆdrÉ”Ë / (BrE)
to render a computer screen, window, or graphic again so that its contents update or display correctly

Examples

To redraw the screen
If the application window is covered and then uncovered, it may need to redraw its contents.
The graphics program will automatically redraw the image after you make changes.
Sometimes, resizing a window causes the screen to redraw incorrectly until you refresh it.

Synonyms

  • repaint
    5.4 0.03/m
    repaint updates a specific screen area by repainting pixels after a change, while redraw means to render the whole display again.
  • redisplay
    4.1 0.01/m
    redisplay shows content again on the screen after it was hidden, while redraw means to render graphics again to update their appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: refresh - to reload or update the contents of a display or page
  • synonym: render - to generate and display an image or graphic on a screen
