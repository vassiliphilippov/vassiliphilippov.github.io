Cosimo de' Medici

proper noun

Pronunciation: /ˈkoʊ.ziː.moʊ də ˈmed.ɪ.tʃi/ /; / /ˈkoʊ.ziː.moʊ deɪ ˈmed.ɪ.tʃi/ (AmE), /ˈkəʊ.ziː.məʊ də ˈmed.ɪ.tʃi/ /; / /ˈkəʊ.ziː.məʊ deɪ ˈmed.ɪ.tʃi/ (BrE)
(1389–1464) Italian financier and statesman; the first of the Medici political dynasty, influential in Florence and a patron of the arts.
