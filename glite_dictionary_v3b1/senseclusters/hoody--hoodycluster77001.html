hoody

noun
young thug (UK)
9.9 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆhÊŠdi / (AmE), / ËˆhÊŠdi / (BrE)
an informal term, chiefly British, for a young person who behaves in a threatening or criminal way; a thug or hoodlum

Examples
