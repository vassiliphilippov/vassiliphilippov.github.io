effeminization

noun
becoming more feminine
9.3
Pronunciation: / ɪˌfɛmɪnəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌfɛmɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making someone or something more feminine in appearance, behavior, or characteristics

Examples

Effeminization of style
The effeminization of fashion trends has led to more unisex clothing styles.
Some critics argue that media portrayals contribute to the effeminization of certain male characters.
Hormone therapy can result in the effeminization of physical features over time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feminization - the process of becoming more feminine
  • antonym: masculinization - the process of becoming more masculine
