to approve or provide something formally by means of a vote, especially within a legislative or official body

Examples

To vote funds
After a lengthy debate, the council voted the necessary resources for the new library.
Congress voted additional funds to support the disaster relief effort.
The board voted a substantial bonus to the retiring CEO.
