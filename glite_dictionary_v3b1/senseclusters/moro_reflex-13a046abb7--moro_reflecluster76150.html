Moro reflex

noun
infant startle reflex
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmɔː.roʊ ˈref.leks/ (AmE), /ˈmɔː.rəʊ ˈref.leks/ /; / /ˈmɒ.rəʊ ˈref.leks/ (BrE)
a normal reflex in young infants in which a sudden loud noise or movement causes the child to stretch out the arms and flex the legs
