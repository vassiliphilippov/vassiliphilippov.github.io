antihelix

noun
ear cartilage ridge
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈhiːlɪks / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈhiːlɪks / (BrE)
the curved ridge of cartilage on the outer ear, running parallel and just inside to the helix
