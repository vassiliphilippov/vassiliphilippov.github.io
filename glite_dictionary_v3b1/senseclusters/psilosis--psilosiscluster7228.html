psilosis

noun
loss or shedding of hair (medical)
Pronunciation: / saɪˈloʊsɪs / (AmE), / saɪˈləʊsɪs / (BrE)
A medical term for the loss or shedding of hair, especially as a symptom of disease.

Examples

Chronic stress is sometimes associated with psilosis in both men and women.
