tile

noun
graphical element in digital interfaces
3.7
Pronunciation: /taɪl/ /; / /ˈtaɪ.əl/ (AmE), /taɪl/ /; / /ˈtaɪ.əl/ (BrE)
a small, usually rectangular graphical element on a screen that represents an app, file, function, or a portion of a digital map; used in tile-based user interfaces and mapping systems.

Examples

App tile
She rearranged her phone's home screen tiles to group social and work apps by theme.
The map service downloaded each tile at different zoom levels to display the city quickly.
A live weather tile on my desktop shows current temperature and the hourly forecast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thumbnail - a small image used to represent a larger image, file, or page
