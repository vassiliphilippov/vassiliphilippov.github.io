unscaled

adjective
not climbed
6.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈskeɪld / (AmE), / ʌnˈskeɪld / (BrE)
not climbed, mounted, or overcome, especially referring to a wall, cliff, or obstacle.

Examples

Unscaled mountain face
The north face of the mountain remained unscaled for decades due to its dangerous conditions.
Despite many attempts, the ancient fortress walls were still unscaled by invaders.
The expedition aimed to conquer the last unscaled peak in the region.

Related Terms

  • antonym: scaled - climbed or surmounted
  • synonym: unclimbed - not yet climbed or ascended
