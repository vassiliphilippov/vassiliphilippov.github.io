flicker

noun
a large North American woodpecker
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈflɪkɚ / (AmE), / ˈflɪkə / (BrE)
A large North American woodpecker with a brownish plumage and spotted underparts, often feeding on the ground.

Examples

A northern flicker
A northern flicker drilled into the tree trunk searching for insects.
Several flickers were feeding among the fallen leaves.
We spotted a flicker in the park, recognizable by its spotted breast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: woodpecker - a bird with a strong bill for pecking at wood
