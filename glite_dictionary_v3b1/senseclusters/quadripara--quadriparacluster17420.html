quadripara

noun
woman who has given birth four times
9.9
Pronunciation: /kwɑː.drɪ.ˈpæ.rə/ /; / /kwɑː.drɪ.ˈpeɪ.rə/ (AmE), /kwɒd.rɪ.ˈpeə.rə/ /; / /kwɒd.rɪ.ˈpæ.rə/ (BrE)
