quadripara

noun
woman who has given birth four times
9.9
Pronunciation: / kwɑːˈdrɪpərə / (AmE), / kwɒˈdrɪpərə / (BrE)
(in obstetrics) a woman who has given birth to a viable infant in each of four pregnancies.

Examples

A quadripara woman
As a quadripara, her childbirth history was considered straightforward by the medical team.
Records showed that she was a quadripara with no complications in any of her pregnancies.
The patient was identified as a quadripara during her fourth delivery.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: multipara - woman who has given birth two or more times
  • degree lower: primipara - woman who has given birth once
