stock room

noun
room for storing goods and supplies
Pronunciation: / stɑk rum / (AmE), / stɒk ruːm / (BrE)
a room in a business or store where goods and supplies are stored before being put on sale or used

Examples

The stock room
All the extra merchandise is kept in the stock room until it is needed on the shop floor.
Our stock room is fully stocked ahead of the holiday season rush.
The manager asked her to organize the stock room after the morning deliveries.
