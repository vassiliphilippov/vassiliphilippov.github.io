pissant

noun
small, smelly ant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪsˌænt / (AmE), / ˈpɪsˌænt / (BrE)
a small ant, especially one that produces a strong, unpleasant smell when crushed

Examples

A pissant ant
