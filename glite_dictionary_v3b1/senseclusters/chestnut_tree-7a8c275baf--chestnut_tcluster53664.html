chestnut tree

noun
deciduous tree with edible nuts
6.2 2.0
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃes.nʌt triː/ /; / /ˈtʃest.nʌt triː/ (AmE), /ˈtʃes.nʌt triː/ /; / /ˈtʃest.nʌt triː/ /; / /ˈtʃes.nʌt tʃriː/ (BrE)
