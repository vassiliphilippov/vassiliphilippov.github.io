censorial

adjective
relating to a censor's duties or powers
Pronunciation: / sɛnˈsɔriəl / (AmE), / sɛnˈsɔːriəl / (BrE)
relating to a censor, especially to the duties or powers of a censor

Examples

Censorial powers
His censorial powers allowed him to suppress any publication deemed inappropriate.
Many criticized the government's censorial approach to artistic expression.
The committee acted in a distinctly censorial manner when reviewing the activities of the press.

Related Terms

  • synonym: censorious - critical or tending to find fault
