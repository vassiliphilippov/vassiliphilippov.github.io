bird watcher

noun
person who observes wild birds
6.1
Pronunciation: / bɝd ˈwɑtʃɚ / (AmE), / bɜːd ˈwɒtʃə / (BrE)
a person who observes and identifies wild birds, usually as a hobby or for study

Examples

A bird watcher notebook
As a lifelong bird watcher, she keeps detailed notes of every species she sees.
The bird watcher patiently waited for hours to spot a rare woodpecker.
The nature reserve is a popular destination for every serious bird watcher.
During spring migration, many bird watchers flock to the wetlands to spot rare species.
She became a dedicated bird watcher after spotting an owl in her backyard.
The bird watcher recorded every sighting in his well-worn notebook.
