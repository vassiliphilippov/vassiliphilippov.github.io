trick

noun
clever method or device

Examples

Synonyms

    7.7 2.33/m

  • artifice
    8.1 1.70/m
    artifice is a clever, formal means of deceit used to hide intent, while trick is a general clever method for many situations.
  • stratagem
    8.5 0.64/m
    stratagem is a planned scheme to outwit an opponent in conflict, while trick often refers to a single clever action.
  • ploy
    4.9 4.20/m

  • device
    4.9 7.67/m

  • hack
    4.0 3.01/m
    hack is a quick, informal clever way to solve problems or save time, while trick can also mean deception.
