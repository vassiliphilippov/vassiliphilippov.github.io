indirection

noun
use of indirect methods or actions
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.dəˈrɛk.ʃən /; / ˌɪndəˈrɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.dɪˈrɛk.ʃən /; / ˌɪndɪˈrɛkʃən / (BrE)
the use of indirect or evasive methods instead of being straightforward or direct
