Haplopappus acaulis

noun
herbaceous plant native to northwestern North America
9.9

A dark green, erect herb native to the northwestern United States and southwestern Canada, with stiff leaves in dense tufts and yellow flower heads; sometimes placed in the genus Haplopappus.
