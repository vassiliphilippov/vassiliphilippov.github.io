argentic

adjective
of silver with valence two
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈdʒɛntɪk / (AmE), / ɑːˈdʒɛntɪk / (BrE)
relating to compounds in which silver has a valence of two (+2 oxidation state)

Examples

Argentic compounds
Several argentic compounds are unstable and decompose easily when exposed to light.
The researchers synthesized a new argentic fluoride as part of their study.
Unlike argentous chloride, argentic chloride is rarely encountered in laboratory work.
