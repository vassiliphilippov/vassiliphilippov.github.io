wipe off

verb
to remove by wiping
1.0
Pronunciation: / waɪp ɑf /; / waɪp ɔf / (AmE), / waɪp ɒf / (BrE)
to remove something from a surface by rubbing, wiping, or erasing

Examples

He wiped off the spilled juice from the kitchen counter.
Please wipe off the fingerprints from the screen before returning the device.
