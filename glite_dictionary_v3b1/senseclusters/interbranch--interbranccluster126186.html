interbranch

adjective
between branches
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈbræntʃ / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈbrɑːntʃ / (BrE)
relating to or occurring between different branches of an organization or system, especially government

Examples

Interbranch communication
The new policy encourages interbranch communication to improve efficiency across the company.
There was an interbranch meeting to resolve the dispute between the judicial and legislative branches.
Effective interbranch collaboration is essential for a balanced government.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interdepartmental - between different departments
  • antonym: intraorganizational - within the same organization
