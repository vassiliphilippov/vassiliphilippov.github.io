Nintendo

noun
video game company
3.6
Pronunciation: / nɪnˈtɛndoʊ / (AmE), / nɪnˈtɛndəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for video games made or published by the company Nintendo, or for the company itself

Examples

Classic Nintendo games
We spent the evening playing old Nintendo games together in the living room.
He collects rare Nintendo cartridges from the 1980s and 1990s.
Many people grew up with Nintendo as their first introduction to video games.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Sega - a company known for making video games and consoles
