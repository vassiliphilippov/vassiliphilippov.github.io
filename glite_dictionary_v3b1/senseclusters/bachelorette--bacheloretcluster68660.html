bachelorette

noun
unmarried woman
Pronunciation: / ˌbætʃələˈrɛt / (AmE), / ˌbætʃələˈrɛt / (BrE)
an unmarried woman, especially one who is about to be married or is being celebrated before her wedding

Examples

Bachelorette party
The bachelorette enjoyed a fun night out with her friends before the wedding.
They decorated the room with balloons and banners for the bachelorette party.
As a bachelorette, she traveled the world before settling down.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bachelor - an unmarried man
  • synonym: spinster - an older unmarried woman
