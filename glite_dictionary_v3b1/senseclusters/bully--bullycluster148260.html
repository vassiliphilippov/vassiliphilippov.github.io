bully

noun
hired thug or ruffian (archaic)

Examples

Synonyms

  • intimidator
    7.5 0.13/m
    an intimidator frightens or threatens people to force obedience, while a bully targets weaker people and may also harm or control them.
  • harasser
    6.3 0.63/m
    a harasser repeatedly annoys, torments, or pressures someone, while a bully uses strength or threats to hurt weaker people.
  • victimizer
    8.3 0.26/m
    a victimizer harms or exploits people, often to gain from them, while a bully focuses on threatening weaker people.
  • threatener
    5.8 0.36/m
    a threatener makes threats or warns of harm, whereas a bully uses threats plus physical or emotional harm against weaker people.
