moon-splashed

adjective
covered patchily with moonlight
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːnˌsplæʃt / (AmE), / ˈmuːnˌsplæʃt / (BrE)
Covered or marked in patches of moonlight; illuminated here and there by moonlight.

Examples

Moon-splashed forest
A deer wandered through the moon-splashed forest, blending in with shadows and silver light.
She gazed at the moon-splashed water, mesmerized by the shimmering reflections.
The old terrace looked magical in the moon-splashed night.
