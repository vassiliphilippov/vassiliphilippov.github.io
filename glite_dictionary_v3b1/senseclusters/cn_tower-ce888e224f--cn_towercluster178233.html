CN Tower

proper noun
landmark communications and observation tower in Toronto
2.9
Pronunciation: / siː ɛn ˈtaʊɚ / (AmE), / siː ɛn ˈtaʊə / (BrE)
A communications and observation tower in Toronto, Canada, standing 553 meters (1,815 feet) tall, and recognized as one of the city's most iconic landmarks.

Examples

The CN Tower in Toronto
During their trip to Canada, they visited the CN Tower and dined at its revolving restaurant.
The CN Tower offers breathtaking views over the city of Toronto and Lake Ontario.
The CN Tower was once the tallest free-standing structure in the world.
