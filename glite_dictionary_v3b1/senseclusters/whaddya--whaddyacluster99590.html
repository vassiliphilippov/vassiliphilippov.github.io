whaddya

x
informal contraction
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑdiə / (AmE), / ˈwɒdiə / (BrE)
an informal spoken contraction of 'what do you', 'what are you', or 'what did you', used in casual conversation to start a question

Examples

Whaddya think
Whaddya think about going to the movies tonight?
Hey, whaddya need from the store before I leave?
Whaddya say we grab some coffee after class?
He turned to me and said, 'Whaddya think about the new movie?'
'Whaddya do after school yesterday?' his friend asked curiously.
If someone asks, 'Whaddya mean by that?', they want you to explain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gonna - informal contraction of 'going to'
  • synonym: wanna - informal contraction of 'want to'
  • synonym: what did you - standard form of the contraction 'whaddya'
