fairing

noun
aerodynamic vehicle cover
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfeərɪŋ / (BrE)
a smooth, shaped covering on a vehicle, especially an aircraft or motorcycle, designed to reduce air resistance or improve appearance

Examples

Motorcycle fairing cover
The airplane's fairing was carefully designed to improve its speed and fuel efficiency.
After installing a new fairing on his bicycle, he noticed less wind resistance during races.
The boat's fairing helps it glide smoothly through the water.
The airplane's fairing was designed to minimize drag and improve fuel efficiency.
Many motorcycles have a fairing to protect the rider from wind and debris.
After the accident, the damaged fairing had to be replaced before the car could race again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cowling - a removable cover placed over an engine for protection or to improve airflow
  • antonym: drag - the resistance of air or water to forward movement
  • synonym: spoiler - a device on a vehicle to reduce lift or increase stability
