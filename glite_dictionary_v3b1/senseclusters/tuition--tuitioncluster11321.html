tuition

noun
fee for instruction, esp. college or university
Pronunciation: / tuˈɪʃən / (AmE), / tjuˈɪʃən / (BrE)
the money that you pay for being taught, especially at a college or university

Examples

College tuition fees
He worked part-time to cover his college tuition.
Many students rely on scholarships to help pay their tuition.
The tuition at private universities can be very expensive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fee - an amount of money paid for a service
