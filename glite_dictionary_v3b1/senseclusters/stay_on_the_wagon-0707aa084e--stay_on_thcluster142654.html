stay on the wagon

verb
abstain from alcohol
7.6
Pronunciation: / steɪ ɑn ðə ˈwæɡən / (AmE), / steɪ ɒn ðə ˈwæɡən / (BrE)
to abstain from drinking alcoholic beverages, especially after a period of drinking

Examples

To stay on the wagon after drinking
After his health scare, he promised to stay on the wagon for good.
It can be hard to stay on the wagon during family celebrations.
Support from friends helps her stay on the wagon even when tempted.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fall off the wagon - to resume drinking alcohol after a period of abstinence
