solar dish

noun
concave reflector concentrating sunlight
1.0 5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊlɚ dɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlə dɪʃ / (BrE)
a large, concave mirror or reflector designed to concentrate sunlight onto a focal point, producing high temperatures for purposes such as energy generation or scientific research

Examples

A solar dish reflector
Heat from the solar dish was sufficient to melt salt for energy storage.
Researchers used a large solar dish to focus sunlight during the experiment.
The engineers installed a solar dish to generate electricity for the remote outpost.

Related Terms

  • synonym: solar collector - a device that collects and concentrates solar energy
