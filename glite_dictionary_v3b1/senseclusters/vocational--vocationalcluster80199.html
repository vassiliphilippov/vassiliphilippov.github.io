vocational

adjective
relating to job skills or training
7.0
Pronunciation: / voʊˈkeɪʃənl / (AmE), / vəʊˈkeɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to education, training, or skills that prepare people for specific trades, professions, or careers.

Examples

Vocational school
He attended a vocational school to become an electrician.
Many students choose vocational programs instead of traditional academic courses.
The government is increasing funding for vocational training to address the skills gap.

Related Terms

  • antonym: academic - relating to education, especially in universities, rather than technical or vocational subjects
