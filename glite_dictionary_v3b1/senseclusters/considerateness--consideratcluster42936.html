considerateness

noun
thoughtful regard for others
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɪdərətnəs / (AmE), / kənˈsɪdərətnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being thoughtful and attentive to the needs and feelings of others

Examples

Show considerateness to others
Acts of considerateness can brighten someone's entire day.
He thanked her for her considerateness during his difficult time.
Her considerateness towards her colleagues made her well liked in the office.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consideration - careful thought, especially for the feelings or needs of others
  • antonym: insensitivity - lack of concern for others' feelings
  • synonym: thoughtfulness - the quality of showing careful consideration or attention
