Gradgrind

proper noun
fact-focused person
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈgræd.ɡraɪnd/ (AmE), /ˈgræd.ɡraɪnd/ (BrE)
a person who is excessively concerned with facts and practicality, ignoring imagination or emotion (like the character Thomas Gradgrind in Dickens's 'Hard Times')
