natural riser

noun
someone who wakes up easily and early
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃɚəl ˈraɪzɚ / (AmE), / ˈnatʃrəl ˈraɪzə / (BrE)
a person who wakes up easily and early, usually without the need for an alarm

Examples

A natural riser person
Even on weekends, she's a natural riser and is up before the sun.
It's easier to work morning shifts if you're a natural riser.
My father is a natural riser and always enjoys the quiet of early mornings.

Related Terms
