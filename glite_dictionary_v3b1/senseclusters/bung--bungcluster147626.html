bung

verb
give a tip or gratuity
7.3
Pronunciation: / bʌŋ / (AmE), / bʌŋ / (BrE)
to give a tip or small payment to someone for a service, in addition to the agreed pay, especially informally or secretly

Examples

To bung a tip
Don't forget to bung the porter a little extra for carrying your bags.
He bunged the bartender a fiver for his quick service.
She always bungs the delivery driver a tip during the holidays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gratuity - a sum of money given to someone for a service performed
  • synonym: tip - to give a small amount of extra money for a service
