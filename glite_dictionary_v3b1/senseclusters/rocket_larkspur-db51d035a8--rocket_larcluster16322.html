rocket larkspur

noun
an ornamental plant with tall spiky flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑkɪt ˈlɑrkˌspɝ / (AmE), / ˈrɒkɪt ˈlɑːkspɜː / (BrE)
a plant of southern Europe with tall, unbranched spikes of blue, purple, or pink flowers, often cultivated as an ornamental; sometimes placed in the genus Delphinium.

Examples

Rocket larkspur flowers
Nurseries often sell rocket larkspur seeds in a variety of shades.
She planted rocket larkspur along the fence to add a splash of color in spring.
The garden was filled with the vibrant blue blossoms of the rocket larkspur.

Related Terms

  • synonym: larkspur - any plant of the genus Delphinium or Consolida, with tall spikes of flowers
