lazarette

noun
boat storage compartment
Pronunciation: / ˌlæzəˈrɛt / (AmE), / ˌlæzəˈrɛt / (BrE)
a small storage compartment, usually located at the stern of a boat or ship, used for equipment or supplies

Examples

Boat lazarette storage
The crew kept emergency supplies in the lazarette for easy access during a voyage.
Before setting sail, she checked the lazarette to make sure all the tools were stored properly.
Fishing gear is often stored in the lazarette at the back of the boat.

  • synonym: locker - a small storage space on a boat or ship
