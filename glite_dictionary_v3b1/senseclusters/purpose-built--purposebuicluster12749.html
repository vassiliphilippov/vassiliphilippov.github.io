purpose-built

adjective
specially designed for a specific use
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːpəs bɪlt / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpəs bɪlt / (BrE)
specially designed and constructed for a particular use or function.

Examples

A purpose-built factory
The company moved into a purpose-built factory on the edge of town.
These offices are purpose-built to maximize energy efficiency and employee comfort.
They live in a purpose-built apartment complex for senior citizens.

Related Terms

  • synonym: custom-built - built to individual specifications or requirements
