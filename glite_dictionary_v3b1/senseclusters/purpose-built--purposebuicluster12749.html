Examples

A purpose-built factory

These offices are purpose-built to maximize energy efficiency and employee comfort.
They live in a purpose-built apartment complex for senior citizens.

Synonyms

  • purpose-made
    4.8 0.04/m
    purpose-made is produced to serve a particular use and often sold ready for that role, while purpose-built is constructed for that exact function.
  • custom-built
    4.1 0.63/m
    custom-built is made to a user's specific requirements and often unique, while purpose-built is designed for a particular function rather than one person.
  • tailor-made
    4.0 0.61/m
    tailor-made is made to fit an individual's exact needs or body, while purpose-built is constructed for a specific functional use.
