sympathy

noun
shared feeling of sorrow or compassion
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪm.pə.θi / (AmE), / ˈsɪm.pə.θi / (BrE)
the feeling of understanding and sharing another person's feelings, especially their sorrow or sadness

Examples

Show sympathy for pain
His words were full of sympathy, but they could not ease her pain.
Many people sent cards and flowers as a gesture of sympathy.
She expressed her sympathy for the family after hearing about their loss.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apathy - lack of feeling or emotion
  • synonym: compassion - deep awareness of another's suffering and desire to alleviate it
  • synonym: empathy - ability to understand and share the feelings of another
