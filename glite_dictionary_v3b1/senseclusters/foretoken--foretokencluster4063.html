foretoken

noun
sign predicting something to come
Pronunciation: / fɔrˈtoʊkən / (AmE), / fɔːˈtəʊkən / (BrE)
a sign or event seen as indicating or predicting something to come; an omen or portent

Examples

A foretoken of change
Many saw the eclipse as a foretoken of significant political change.
Some considered the sudden storm to be a foretoken of disaster.
The old man believed the bird at his window was a foretoken of good luck.

Related Terms

  • synonym: augury - a sign of what will happen in the future; an omen
  • synonym: omen - a sign or event regarded as a portent of good or evil
  • synonym: portent - a sign or warning that a momentous or calamitous event is likely to happen
