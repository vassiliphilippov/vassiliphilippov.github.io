undereducated

adjective
insufficiently educated
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈɛdʒəˌkeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈɛdjʊkeɪtɪd / (BrE)
having received insufficient or poor education; lacking adequate formal education.

Examples

An undereducated person
Many children in remote areas remain undereducated due to a lack of schools and resources.
She felt undereducated compared to her colleagues who had attended prestigious universities.
The company offers job training programs for undereducated adults looking to improve their skills.

Related Terms

  • antonym: educated - having received education, especially a good one
  • synonym: uneducated - not having received any or much formal education
