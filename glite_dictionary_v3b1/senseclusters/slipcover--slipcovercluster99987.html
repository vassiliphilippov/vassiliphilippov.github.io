slipcover

noun
removable cloth cover for furniture
4.7 4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪpˌkʌvɚ / (AmE), / ˈslɪpˌkʌvə / (BrE)
a removable fitted cover made of cloth, designed to protect or decorate upholstered furniture

Examples

A slipcover for a sofa
Changing the slipcover is an easy way to update the room's look.
She bought a floral slipcover to protect her favorite armchair from stains.
The old sofa looked new again after we put a slipcover over it.
