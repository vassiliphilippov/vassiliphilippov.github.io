adjective

Examples

A premeditated crime
A premeditated action often carries a heavier legal penalty than a spontaneous one.
It was clear from the evidence that the theft was premeditated rather than a crime of opportunity.
The judge found the attack to be premeditated and sentenced the defendant accordingly.
