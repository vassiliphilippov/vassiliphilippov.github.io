juice

verb
to extract liquid
Pronunciation: / dʒus / (AmE), / dʒuːs / (BrE)
to extract liquid from fruits or vegetables by pressing or squeezing them

Examples

To juice an orange
Every morning, she likes to juice oranges for a fresh drink.
He bought a new machine to juice vegetables more efficiently.
Some people prefer to juice apples and carrots together for a sweet flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blend - to mix or combine substances, often by crushing
  • synonym: squeeze - to press something firmly to extract liquid
