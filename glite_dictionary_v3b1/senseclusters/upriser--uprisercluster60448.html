upriser

noun
early riser
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌraɪzər / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌraɪzə / (BrE)
a person who rises, especially one who gets up early or stands up from a sitting or lying position

Examples

An early upriser
The upriser enjoyed the quiet hours before the rest of the family woke up.
As a natural upriser, she always finished her work before noon.
He was known as an upriser who never missed a sunrise jog.

Related Terms

  • synonym: early bird - a person who gets up early in the morning
  • antonym: night owl - a person who stays up late and prefers to be active at night
