chemical compound

noun
substance made of chemically combined elements
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛmɪkəl ˈkɑmpaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪkəl ˈkɒmpaʊnd / (BrE)
A substance formed from two or more elements or ingredients that are chemically united in fixed proportions.
