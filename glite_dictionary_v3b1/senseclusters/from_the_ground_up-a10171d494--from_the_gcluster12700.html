from the ground up

adverb
from the very beginning
4.9
Pronunciation: / frəm ðə ɡraʊnd ʌp / (AmE), / frəm ðə ɡraʊnd ʌp / (BrE)
starting from the very beginning and completely building or developing something

Examples

Build from the ground up

The software was developed from the ground up to ensure maximum security.
They constructed the new school from the ground up in less than a year.
