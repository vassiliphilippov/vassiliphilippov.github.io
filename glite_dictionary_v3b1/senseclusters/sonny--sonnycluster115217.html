sonny

noun
informal term of address for a boy
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌni / (AmE), / ˈsʌni / (BrE)
used as a familiar or affectionate form of address to a boy or young man

Examples

Hello, sonny
All right, sonny, it's time to go home now.
Come here, sonny, and let me tie your shoes.
Listen, sonny, you shouldn't play near the road.

Related Terms

  • synonym: son - male child in relation to his parents
