steer

noun
castrated male cattle
6.1
Pronunciation: / stɪr / (AmE), / stɪə / (BrE)
a young male bovine animal that has been castrated and is raised for beef

Examples

A beef steer
Each steer was weighed before being sent to market.
The rancher separated the steer from the rest of the herd.
They raised several steers each year for beef production.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bull - an uncastrated male bovine animal
  • antonym: cow - a fully grown female bovine animal
  • synonym: ox - a domesticated bovine animal trained as a draft animal, typically castrated
