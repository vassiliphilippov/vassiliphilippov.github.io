masticate

verb
chew food with the teeth
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmæstəˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈmæstɪkeɪt / (BrE)
to chew (food) by biting and grinding it with the teeth, especially in a deliberate or thorough manner

Examples

To masticate food
Cows spend a large part of the day masticating their food in the pasture.
He nervously masticated his sandwich while waiting for the interview.
Your dentist recommends that you thoroughly masticate each mouthful before swallowing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chew - to crush food with the teeth before swallowing
  • antonym: swallow - to cause or allow something to pass down the throat
