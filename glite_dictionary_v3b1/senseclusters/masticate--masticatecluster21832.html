masticate

verb
chew food with the teeth
5.3 6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈmæ.stɪ.keɪt/ /; / /ˈmæs.tɪ.keɪt/ (AmE), /ˈmæ.stɪ.keɪt/ /; / /ˈmæs.tɪ.keɪt/ (BrE)
to chew (food) by biting and grinding it with the teeth, especially in a deliberate or thorough manner

Examples

Your dentist recommends that you thoroughly masticate each mouthful before swallowing.
