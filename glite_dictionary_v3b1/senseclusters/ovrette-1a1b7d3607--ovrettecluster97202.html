Ovrette

noun
a brand of oral contraceptive pill
Pronunciation: / oʊˈvrɛt / (AmE), / əʊˈvrɛt / (BrE)
A trademarked oral contraceptive containing norgestrel, used to prevent pregnancy.

Examples

Ovrette birth control
My doctor recommended Ovrette as a suitable birth control option for me.
Some women may experience irregular bleeding while taking Ovrette.
Unlike combination pills, Ovrette contains only a progestin hormone.
