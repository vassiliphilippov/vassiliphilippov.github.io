hand

noun
rotating pointer on a clock or watch
5.5
Pronunciation: / hænd / (AmE), / hænd / (BrE)
a rotating pointer on the face of a clock or watch that indicates the time by pointing to numbers or marks

Examples

Clock hand
She glanced at the hand to see if she was late.
The clock's hour hand pointed directly at twelve.
The minute hand moved slowly around the clock face.
