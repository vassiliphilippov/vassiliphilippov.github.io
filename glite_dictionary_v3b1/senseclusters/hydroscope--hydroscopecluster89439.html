hydroscope

noun
water-measuring instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪdrəˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdrəskəʊp / (BrE)
an instrument used to observe or measure the properties of water

Examples

Hydroscope for water testing
The researcher used a hydroscope to study the water quality in the river.
A hydroscope can help detect impurities in drinking water.
Engineers developed a new hydroscope for monitoring underwater environments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hydrometer - an instrument for measuring the density of liquids
  • antonym: telescope - an instrument for viewing distant objects
