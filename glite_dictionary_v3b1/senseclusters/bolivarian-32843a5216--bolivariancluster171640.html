Bolivarian

noun
supporter of Bolívar's ideas
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌboʊlɪˈvɛəriən / (AmE), / ˌbɒlɪˈvɛəriən / (BrE)
a person who supports or is a member of a political movement inspired by the ideas of Simón Bolívar

Examples

A Bolivarian leader
The Bolivarian addressed the crowd, calling for unity and social reform.
Many young activists identify as Bolivarians in support of regional independence.
During the rally, a passionate Bolivarian spoke about the legacy of Simón Bolívar.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conservative - a person who resists change and supports traditional values
  • synonym: revolutionary - a person who supports or participates in a revolution
