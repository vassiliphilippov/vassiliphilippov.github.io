qualifiedly

adverb
with reservations
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑːlɪˌfaɪɪdli / (AmE), / ˈkwɒlɪˌfaɪɪdli / (BrE)
in a limited or restricted way; not completely or without conditions

Examples

To qualifiedly agree
He qualifiedly supported the plan, expressing some doubts about its effectiveness.
The committee qualifiedly approved the new policy, pending further review.
She qualifiedly praised the movie, mentioning a few aspects she did not like.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conditionally - in a way that depends on certain conditions
  • antonym: unconditionally - without any conditions or limitations
