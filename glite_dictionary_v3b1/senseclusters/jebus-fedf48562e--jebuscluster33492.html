Jebus

proper noun
humorous 'Jesus'
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːbəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːbəs / (BrE)
a humorous or ironic misspelling of 'Jesus', often used online to express surprise or emphasis

Examples

Jebus! So tough
When he saw the final exam, he muttered 'Jebus, this is tough!' under his breath.
She shouted 'Jebus!' after narrowly avoiding the falling book.
On the internet, people sometimes write 'Jebus' instead of 'Jesus' for comic effect.
