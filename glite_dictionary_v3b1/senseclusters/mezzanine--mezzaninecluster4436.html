mezzanine

noun
first or lowest balcony in a theatre
7.4 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…õz.…ôÀånin / (AmE), / Ààm…õz.…ôniÀên / (BrE)
the first or lowest balcony in a theatre or auditorium, situated above the main floor and below higher balconies

Examples

Mezzanine seats
From the mezzanine, you can see both the performers and the orchestra pit clearly.
Tickets for the mezzanine sold out before any other section of the theatre.
We found our seats quickly on the mezzanine, which gave us a great view of the stage.

Synonyms

  • first balcony
    3.9 0.01/m
    first balcony is a plain descriptive term for the lower seating tier, while mezzanine is the single-word theatrical name for that same level.

Related Terms

  • synonym: balcony - platform projecting from the wall of a building, typically above the ground floor
  • synonym: gallery - upper floor or balcony in a theatre
