melaleuca

noun
evergreen myrtle genus
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛləˈluːkə / (AmE), / ˌmɛləˈluːkə / (BrE)
a tree or shrub (genus Melaleuca) native to Australia, often with papery bark and white or cream flowers
