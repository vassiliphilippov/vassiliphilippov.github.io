map

verb
determine genetic location on a chromosome
1.0
Pronunciation: / mæp / (AmE), / mæp / (BrE)
(Genetics) To establish the position of a gene, DNA sequence, or marker within a specific region of a chromosome.

Examples

To map a gene
Researchers were able to map the mutation responsible for the disease to chromosome 7.
Scientists mapped the gene's precise location using advanced sequencing techniques.
The laboratory is mapping the entire genetic structure of the organism.
