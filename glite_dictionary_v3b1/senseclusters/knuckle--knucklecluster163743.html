knuckle

verb
press or rub with the knuckles
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌkəl / (AmE), / ˈnʌkəl / (BrE)
to press, rub, or bend something using the knuckles or by curling the fingers at the joints

Examples

To knuckle your eyes
He knuckled his tired eyes after hours of reading.
She knuckled the dough vigorously before letting it rise.
The artist knuckled the charcoal across the canvas to create texture.
He knuckled his fingers nervously while waiting for the interview to begin.
She knuckled her hand to relieve the tension in her joints.
The child knuckled his fist before knocking on the door.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clench - to close your hand tightly into a fist
  • antonym: straighten - to make something not bent or curved
