top fermentation

noun
beer fermentation method with surface yeast
7.9 4.7
Pronunciation: /tɑːp fɜːr.mən.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /tɔːp fɜːr.mən.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /tɒp fɜː.mən.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /tɒp fə.mən.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a method of fermenting beer in which yeast rises to the surface of the liquid, occurring at higher temperatures and often used in ale production

Examples

Top fermentation method
The brewery specializes in top fermentation to create rich and complex ales.
Traditional ales are brewed using top fermentation, which imparts unique flavors to the beer.
Unlike lagers, top fermentation requires warmer temperatures to flourish.
