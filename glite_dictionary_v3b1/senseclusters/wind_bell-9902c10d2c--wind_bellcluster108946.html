wind bell

noun
decorative object that tinkles in the wind
4.7 2.9
Pronunciation: /wɪnd bel/ /; / /waɪnd bel/ (AmE), /wɪnd bel/ /; / /waɪnd bel/ (BrE)
A decorative object made of pieces of metal, glass, or pottery that hang loosely and make a tinkling sound when moved by the wind.

Examples

A wind bell hangs
Each time a breeze blows, the wind bell outside my window creates a soothing melody.
He bought a ceramic wind bell at the craft fair as a unique souvenir.
The gentle sound from the wind bell hung on the porch calmed her every evening.
