kora

noun
Buddhist pilgrimage walk
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkɔːr.ə/ (AmE), /ˈkɔː.rə/ (BrE)
a ritual walk or pilgrimage around a sacred site, especially in Tibetan Buddhism

Examples

Sacred kora walk
Many devotees travel to Tibet to complete the sacred kora around Mount Kailash.
The group began their kora at dawn, circling the temple in silent meditation.
Completing a kora is believed to bring spiritual merit in Tibetan Buddhism.
