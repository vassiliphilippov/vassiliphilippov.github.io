goldilocks

adjective
neither too much nor too little; just right
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈgoʊl.dɪ.lɑːks/ /; / /ˈgoʊl.də.lɑːks/ /; / /ˈgoʊl.dɪ.lɔːks/ (AmE), /ˈgəʊl.dɪ.lɒks/ /; / /ˈgəʊl.də.lɒks/ (BrE)
Informal/metaphorical: neither too much nor too little; suitably balanced or 'just right' for a particular situation.

Examples

Goldilocks zone
The team proposed a goldilocks solution that balanced risk and reward for investors.
Astronomers search for planets in the goldilocks zone where liquid water can exist.
They aimed for a goldilocks policy—neither too strict nor too lenient for families.
