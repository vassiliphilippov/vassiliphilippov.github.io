youth hostel

noun
inexpensive lodging for young travelers
4.5
Pronunciation: / juθ ˈhɑstl̩ / (AmE), / juːθ ˈhɒstl̩ / (BrE)
a place that provides inexpensive, supervised accommodation, especially for young people traveling, often with shared facilities and communal spaces

Examples

A youth hostel stay
Many backpackers choose to stay in a youth hostel to save money during their travels.
The youth hostel offers shared kitchen facilities and organized group activities for guests.
We met travelers from all over the world while staying at a youth hostel in Berlin.
