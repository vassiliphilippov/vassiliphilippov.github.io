youth hostel

noun
inexpensive lodging for young travelers
4.5 2.6
Pronunciation: /juːθ ˈhɑː.stəl/ /; / /juːθ ˈhoʊ.stəl/ (AmE), /juːθ ˈhɒ.stəl/ (BrE)
