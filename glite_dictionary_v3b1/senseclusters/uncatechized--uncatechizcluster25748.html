uncatechized

noun
person not religiously taught
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəˈtɛkaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəˈtɛkaɪzd / (BrE)
a person who has not received formal instruction in the basic principles of a religion, especially Christianity

Examples

An uncatechized believer
The missionary worked to teach the uncatechized about the core beliefs of the faith.
Many of the uncatechized in the region had never attended religious classes.
Efforts were made to reach the uncatechized before the major religious festival.

Related Terms

  • antonym: catechumen - a person receiving instruction in the basic principles of Christianity
  • synonym: uninitiated - a person who has not been introduced to a particular subject or activity
