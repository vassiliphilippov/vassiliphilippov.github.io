witch hazel

noun
shrub/tree used for astringent lotion
Pronunciation: / wɪtʃ ˈheɪzəl /; / ˈwɪtʃ ˌheɪzəl / (AmE), / wɪtʃ ˈheɪzəl /; / ˈwɪtʃ ˌheɪzəl / (BrE)
a shrub or small tree of the genus Hamamelis, whose bark and leaves are used to make an astringent lotion
