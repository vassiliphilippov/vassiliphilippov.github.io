cosset

verb
overindulge or pamper excessively
7.2 7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑː.sət/ (AmE), /ˈkɒ.sət/ /; / /ˈkɒ.sɪt/ (BrE)

Examples

To cosset children

The hotel cossets guests with personal attention and luxurious amenities.
