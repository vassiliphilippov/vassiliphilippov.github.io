cosset

verb
overindulge or pamper excessively
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːs.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒs.ɪt / (BrE)
to care for and protect someone too much, so that they do not experience anything unpleasant

Examples

To cosset children
Children who are cosseted may struggle to cope with challenges later in life.
She tends to cosset her youngest child, giving him whatever he wants.
The hotel cossets guests with personal attention and luxurious amenities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mollycoddle - to treat someone very indulgently or protectively
  • antonym: neglect - to fail to care for properly
  • synonym: pamper - to treat with excessive care and attention
