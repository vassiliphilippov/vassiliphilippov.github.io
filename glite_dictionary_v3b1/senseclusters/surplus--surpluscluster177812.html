surplus

noun
amount greater than needed
4.1 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈsɜːr.pləs/ /; / /ˈsɜːr.plʌs/ (AmE), /ˈsɜː.pləs/ /; / /ˈsɜː.plʌs/ (BrE)
an amount of something that is more than what is needed or used

Examples

A surplus of food
After the harvest, there was a surplus of wheat that could be sold to neighboring towns.
If you have a surplus of food, consider donating it to those in need.
The company reported a huge surplus in their annual earnings this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excess - an amount that is more than is necessary, permitted, or desirable
