Anthyllis barba-jovis

noun
a Mediterranean evergreen shrub with pale yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ænˈθɪlɪs ˌbɑrbəˈdʒoʊvɪs / (AmE), / ænˈθɪlɪs ˌbɑːbəˈdʒəʊvɪs / (BrE)
A silvery, hairy European shrub with evergreen foliage and pale yellow flowers, native to Mediterranean coastal regions.

Examples

The Anthyllis barba-jovis shrub
Botanists admire the silver foliage of Anthyllis barba-jovis when studying native European flora.

The Anthyllis barba-jovis grows well in dry, coastal climates along the Mediterranean.
