performable

adjective
able to be done
Pronunciation: / pərˈfɔrməbəl / (AmE), / pəˈfɔːməbəl / (BrE)
able to be carried out, acted, or accomplished, especially on stage or in practice

Examples

A performable task
The experiment is only performable with the right equipment and training.
Some plays are not easily performable by amateur actors due to their complexity.
The task seemed difficult but was actually quite performable with teamwork.

  • antonym: impossible - not able to be done or achieved
