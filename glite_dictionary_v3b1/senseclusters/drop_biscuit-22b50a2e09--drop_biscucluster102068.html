drop biscuit

noun
soft biscuit dropped onto a baking sheet
5.3 3.0
Pronunciation: /drɑːp ˈbɪ.skət/ /; / /drɔːp ˈbɪ.skɪt/ (AmE), /drɒp ˈbɪ.skɪt/ /; / /drɒp ˈbɪ.skət/ (BrE)
A biscuit made from dough with enough milk or liquid to allow it to be dropped from a spoon onto a baking sheet, rather than rolled and cut.

Examples

A drop biscuit dough
She spooned the drop biscuit dough onto the tray and baked them for fifteen minutes.
The recipe called for drop biscuits, which are much quicker to make than rolled ones.
With stew, a warm drop biscuit makes a comforting meal.
