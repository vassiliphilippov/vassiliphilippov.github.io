house

noun
children's game imitating family life
2.0
Pronunciation: / haʊs / (AmE), / haʊs / (BrE)
a children's game in which participants take on the roles of family members and imitate household life

Examples

Children play house
In preschool, children often enjoy taking turns being the parents when they play house.
The kids spent hours in the backyard playing house with their dolls and stuffed animals.
Whenever it rained, my sister and I would set up blankets indoors and pretend to play house.
