Aframomum melegueta

noun
West African spice plant; grains of paradise
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæfɹəˈmoʊməm məlɪˈɡeɪtə / (AmE), / ˌafɹəˈməʊməm mɛlɪˈɡiːtə / (BrE)
A West African plant of the ginger family, whose pungent, peppery seeds are used as a spice, also known as grains of paradise.
