stay-at-home

noun
official order to remain at home
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈsteɪ.ət.oʊm/ /; / /ˈsteɪ.æt.oʊm/ (AmE), /ˈsteɪ.ət.əʊm/ /; / /ˈsteɪ.æt.əʊm/ (BrE)
(in public health/government) an official order or directive requiring people to remain at home and avoid non-essential travel or gatherings, usually to slow the spread of disease.

Examples

Stay-at-home order
The government announced a nationwide stay-at-home order to curb the rapidly rising infection rate.
During the lockdown, many businesses closed after the city issued multiple stay-at-home directives.
The mayor extended the stay-at-home period by two weeks to reduce hospital admissions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lockdown - a set of strict restrictions on movement and activity, often imposed during a public-health emergency
  • synonym: quarantine - a restriction on movement for individuals who may have been exposed to an infectious disease
