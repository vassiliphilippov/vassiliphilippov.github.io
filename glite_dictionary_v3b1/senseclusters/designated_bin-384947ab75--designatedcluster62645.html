designated bin

noun
bin set aside for specific waste
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛzɪɡˌneɪtɪd bɪn / (AmE), / ˈdɛzɪɡneɪtɪd bɪn / (BrE)
a bin that is set apart for a specific type of waste or for waste disposal in a particular area

Examples

The designated bin for food
Only food waste should be disposed of in the designated bin marked green.
Please remember to place recyclables in the designated bin by the entrance.
The park installed a designated bin for used batteries near the exit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: waste bin - a container for holding waste
