omnibus

noun
public transport vehicle (historical)
5.1 8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈɑːm.nə.bəs/ /; / /ˈɑːm.nɪ.bəs/ (AmE), /ˈɒm.nɪ.bəs/ /; / /ˈɒm.nə.bəs/ (BrE)
a large vehicle designed to carry many passengers, especially one used for public transport; now mainly historical or literary usage for a bus

Examples
