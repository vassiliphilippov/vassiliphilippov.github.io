spoiling

noun
the act of damaging or ruining
Pronunciation: / ˈspɔɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspɔɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of damaging or ruining something or making it less enjoyable or valuable

Examples

Spoiling a surprise
Her deliberate spoiling of my painting was upsetting.
Repeated spoiling of the surprise ruined the party for everyone.
The spoiling of the food made it unsafe to eat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: damage - physical harm that impairs the value or function of something
  • antonym: preservation - the act of keeping something safe from harm or damage
