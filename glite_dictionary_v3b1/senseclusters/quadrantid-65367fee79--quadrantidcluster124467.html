Quadrantid

proper noun
annual meteor shower
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑːdrəntɪd / (AmE), / ˈkwɒdrəntɪd / (BrE)
the Quadrantid meteor shower, an annual event in early January when many meteors can be seen in the night sky

Examples

Quadrantid meteor shower
Every January, stargazers gather to watch the spectacular Quadrantid meteor shower.
The Quadrantid is known for its bright and fast meteors, making it a favorite among astronomers.
Cloudy weather made it difficult to see the Quadrantid this year, but some meteors were still visible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Perseid - a major meteor shower occurring in August
