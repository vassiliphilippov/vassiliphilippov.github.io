heroics

noun
brave actions
Pronunciation: / hɪˈroʊɪks / (AmE), / hɪˈrəʊɪks / (BrE)
brave or daring actions, especially those performed in difficult or dangerous situations

Examples

War heroics
The soldier's heroics during the rescue mission were praised by everyone.
Despite his heroics on the field, the team still lost the match.
She was embarrassed by the attention her heroics received after the accident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bravery - courageous behavior or actions
  • antonym: cowardice - lack of courage to face danger or difficulty
