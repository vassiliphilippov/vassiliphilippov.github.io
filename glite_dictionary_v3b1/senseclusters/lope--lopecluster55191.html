lope

noun
smooth three-beat horse gait
6.4
Pronunciation: / loʊp / (AmE), / ləʊp / (BrE)
a relaxed, easy, and steady way of moving, especially a slow run or gait

She settled into a comfortable lope that she could maintain for miles.
