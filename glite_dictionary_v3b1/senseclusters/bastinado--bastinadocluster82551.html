bastinado

noun
stick for beating feet as punishment
Pronunciation: / ˌbæstəˈnidoʊ / (AmE), / ˌbæstɪˈnɑːdəʊ / (BrE)
A stick or cudgel used for beating the soles of the feet as a form of punishment.

Examples

A bastinado stick
In some cultures, the bastinado was a feared instrument of corporal punishment.
Museum exhibits sometimes display a bastinado among other historical devices of discipline.
The guard threatened him with a bastinado if he did not obey.
