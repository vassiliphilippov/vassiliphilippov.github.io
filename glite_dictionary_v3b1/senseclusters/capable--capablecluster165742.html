Synonyms

  • able
    1.0 428/m
    able is the common word for having skill or power now, while capable suggests broader qualities or potential in many situations.
  • competent
    3.6 9.81/m
    competent is trained and shows enough skill to do a job well, while capable only says someone can do it.
  • up to
    2.8 8.94/m
    up to is an informal phrase saying someone meets a task or standard now, while capable describes general ability or qualities.
  • equal to
    2.8 9.08/m
    equal to compares someone with another or a task, while capable simply says a person has the needed ability or skill.
