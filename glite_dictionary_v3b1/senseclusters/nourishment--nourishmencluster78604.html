nourishment

noun
the provision of essential substances to support life
3.2 1.8
Pronunciation: /ˈnʌr.ɪʃ.mənt/ /; / /ˈnɜːr.ɪʃ.mənt/ /; / /ˈnʊr.ɪʃ.mənt/ (AmE), /ˈnʌr.ɪʃ.mənt/ /; / /ˈnʊr.ɪʃ.mənt/ (BrE)
food or other substances, or the act of providing them, that are needed for growth, health, and good condition

Examples

Healthy nourishment
During the illness, she received most of her nourishment through a feeding tube.
Fresh fruits and vegetables are excellent sources of nourishment for the body.
The baby needs regular nourishment to grow and develop properly.
Proper nourishment can speed up recovery after an illness.
She dedicated her life to the nourishment of the homeless in her community.
The nourishment of young children is vital for their development.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nutrition - the process of providing or obtaining the food necessary for health and growth
  • antonym: starvation - suffering or death caused by lack of food
