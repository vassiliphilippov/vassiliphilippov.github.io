Synonyms

    5.7 1/m
    sustenance is food or drink that keeps people alive and healthy, while nourishment includes both those substances and the act of providing them.
  • food
    1.0 300/m
    food is the everyday word for what people eat to live and grow, while nourishment also covers the act of providing these substances.
  • nutriment
    8.3 0.02/m
