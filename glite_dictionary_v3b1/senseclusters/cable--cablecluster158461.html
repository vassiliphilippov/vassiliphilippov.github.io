cable

noun
telegram sent abroad
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪ.bəl / (AmE), / ˈkeɪ.bəl / (BrE)
a message sent by telegraph, especially one sent overseas; a telegram.

Examples

Send a cable
He sent a cable to London to inform them of the situation.
In the early 20th century, international news was often transmitted by cable.
She received a cable from her family while traveling in Europe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: telegram - a message sent by telegraph
