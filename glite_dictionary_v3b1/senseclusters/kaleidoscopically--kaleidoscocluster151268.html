kaleidoscopically

adverb
in a shifting, colorful way
8.0
Pronunciation: /kə.laɪ.də.ˈskɑː.pɪ.kəl.i/ (AmE), /kə.laɪ.də.ˈskɒp.ɪ.kəl.i/ (BrE)
in a way that changes or shifts rapidly and with many colors or patterns, like a kaleidoscope

Examples

Lights change kaleidoscopically
The city lights flickered kaleidoscopically as the festival began.
Her thoughts shifted kaleidoscopically from one idea to another during the creative workshop.
The dancers moved kaleidoscopically across the stage, creating a mesmerizing display.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vividly - in a way that is very bright or intense in color
