iconically

adverb
in a symbolic way
Pronunciation: / aɪˈkɑnɪkli / (AmE), / aɪˈkɒnɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is highly representative, symbolic, or characteristic of something famous or important

Examples

Iconically famous places
The Eiffel Tower is iconically associated with Paris around the world.
She dressed iconically in a style that defined an entire generation.
The song is iconically linked to the summer of 1969.

Related Terms

  • synonym: symbolically - in a way that uses symbols to represent ideas or qualities
  • synonym: typically - in a way that shows the usual qualities of something
