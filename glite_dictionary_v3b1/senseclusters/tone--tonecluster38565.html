tone

verb
make muscles firmer through exercise
Pronunciation: / toʊn / (AmE), / təʊn / (BrE)
to make (muscles or part of the body) firmer and more defined through exercise or treatment

Examples

To tone your body
Regular workouts can help tone your arms and legs.
The new fitness routine claims to tone your entire body in just 30 days.
Yoga is a great way to tone muscles while improving flexibility.
