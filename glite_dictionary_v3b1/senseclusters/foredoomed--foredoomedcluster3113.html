foredoomed

adjective
certain to fail
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌfɔrˈduːmd / (AmE), / ˌfɔːˈduːmd / (BrE)
certain to fail or end badly from the very beginning

Examples

A foredoomed plan
The expedition was foredoomed by poor planning and bad weather from the start.
Many believed the project was foredoomed because of its unrealistic goals.
His attempt to change the system seemed foredoomed given the strong opposition.
