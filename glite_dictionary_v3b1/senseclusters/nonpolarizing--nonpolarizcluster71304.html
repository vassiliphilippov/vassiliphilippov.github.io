nonpolarizing

adjective
not affecting polarization
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈpoʊləˌraɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈpəʊləˌraɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
not affecting, changing, or sensitive to the polarization of light or electromagnetic waves

Examples

Nonpolarizing filter
The scientist chose a nonpolarizing filter to avoid altering the light's properties during the experiment.
A nonpolarizing lens is essential in optical systems that require the original polarization to be maintained.
Engineers installed nonpolarizing windows in the laboratory to ensure accurate laser measurements.
