Dropbox

proper noun
cloud file service
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɑpˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈdrɒpˌbɒks / (BrE)
a brand name for an online service that allows users to store and share files in the cloud

Examples

Save files to Dropbox
She saved all her vacation photos to Dropbox so she could access them from any device.
Our team uses Dropbox to share important documents and collaborate on projects.
After the meeting, he sent the presentation link via Dropbox to everyone in the group.
