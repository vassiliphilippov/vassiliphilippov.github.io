rage

noun
intense desire or passion
Pronunciation: / reɪdʒ / (AmE), / reɪdʒ / (BrE)
an intense and often uncontrollable desire for something

Examples

Rage for adventure
Despite his rage for adventure, he knew the risks of the journey ahead.
His rage for power led him to take drastic actions.
The artist's rage for perfection pushed her to work late into the night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apathy - lack of interest or concern
  • synonym: craving - a powerful desire for something
  • synonym: passion - strong and barely controllable emotion or desire
