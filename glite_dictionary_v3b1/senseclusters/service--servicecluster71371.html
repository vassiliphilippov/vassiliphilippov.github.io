service

noun
opening hit or stroke in sports
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːvɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
(in sports) the act of putting the ball into play by hitting or throwing it, especially at the start of a point in games such as tennis, volleyball, or badminton.

Examples

Tennis service
A fault on the first service put him under pressure.
He practiced his service for hours to improve his accuracy.
Her powerful service helped her dominate the first set.
