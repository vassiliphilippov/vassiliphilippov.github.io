hyperkinetic

adjective
excessive movement
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərkɪˈnɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəkɪˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or showing excessive or abnormal movement, especially as seen in certain medical conditions

Examples

Hyperkinetic disorder
The patient was diagnosed with a hyperkinetic disorder that caused rapid, involuntary movements.
Doctors observed hyperkinetic symptoms, including frequent jerking and twitching of the limbs.
A hyperkinetic child may have difficulty sitting still during class activities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hyperactive - showing unusually high levels of activity
  • antonym: hypokinetic - showing decreased or reduced movement
