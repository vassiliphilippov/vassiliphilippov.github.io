highest point

noun
the zenith or peak position
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪəst pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈhaɪɪst pɔɪnt / (BrE)
the zenith, peak, or uppermost position that something reaches; the greatest or most extreme point of something

Examples

The highest point reached
At noon, the sun is at its highest point in the sky.
She reached the highest point of her career with that achievement.
The trail leads to the highest point on the island, offering breathtaking views.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lowest point - the bottommost or least extreme point
  • synonym: peak - the pointed top of a mountain or a maximum level
  • synonym: zenith - the time or point of something's greatest success or power
