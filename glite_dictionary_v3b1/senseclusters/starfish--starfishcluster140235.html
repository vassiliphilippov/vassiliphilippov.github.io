starfish

noun
marine animal with a star-shaped body
2.6 1.8
Pronunciation: /ˈstɑːr.fɪʃ/ (AmE), /ˈstɑː.fɪʃ/ (BrE)
A marine animal with a star-shaped body, typically having five arms radiating from a central disk; a member of the class Asteroidea.

Examples

A starfish animal
A starfish moves slowly along the ocean floor using hundreds of tiny tube feet.
Children were fascinated by the bright orange starfish they found on the rocky shore.
The starfish can regenerate its arms if they are damaged by a predator.
