choroidal

adjective
of the choroid
9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɔɪdəl / (AmE), / kəˈrɔɪdəl / (BrE)
relating to the choroid, the layer of blood vessels between the retina and the sclera in the eye

Examples

Choroidal tumor
The specialist detected a choroidal tumor during the eye examination.
Inflammation of the choroidal layer can lead to vision problems.
Researchers are studying choroidal blood flow in patients with eye diseases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ocular - relating to the eye
  • synonym: retinal - relating to the retina of the eye
