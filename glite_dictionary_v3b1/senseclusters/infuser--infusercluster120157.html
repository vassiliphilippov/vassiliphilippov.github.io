infuser

noun
one who instills qualities
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfjuzər / (AmE), / ɪnˈfjuːzə / (BrE)
a person or thing that introduces or instills a quality, idea, or feeling into others or into a situation

Examples

Infuser of energy
As a coach, she acted as an infuser of confidence and focus in the young team.
The teacher was known as an infuser of curiosity and a love for learning.
He became an infuser of hope during difficult times in the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inspirer - a person who fills others with enthusiasm or motivation
  • synonym: instiller - a person who gradually imparts an idea or feeling
