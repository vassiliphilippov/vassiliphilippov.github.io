channeler

noun
magical energy user
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃænələr / (AmE), / ˈtʃænələ / (BrE)
a person who directs, controls, or focuses supernatural or magical energy, especially in fiction or games

Examples

Magic channeler
The ancient order trained each channeler to harness the power of the elements.
In the fantasy novel, a young channeler discovers her ability to control magical forces.
The game allows you to play as a channeler who can manipulate supernatural energy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sorcerer - a person who practices magic or sorcery
  • synonym: wizard - a person with magical powers, especially in stories
