darts

noun
game of throwing missiles at a dartboard
Pronunciation: / dɑrts / (AmE), / dɑːts / (BrE)
A game in which players throw small pointed missiles at a circular target (dartboard) to score points.

Examples

Competitive darts game
Competitive darts requires good aim and concentration.
She joined a local darts league to improve her skills.
They went to the pub to play a few games of darts before dinner.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: arrow - projectile similar to a dart used in archery
  • synonym: dartboard - the circular board used for playing darts
