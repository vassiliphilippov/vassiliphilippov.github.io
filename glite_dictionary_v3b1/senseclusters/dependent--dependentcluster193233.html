dependent

adjective
subject to authority or control of another
2.2
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɛndənt / (AmE), / dɪˈpɛndənt / (BrE)
(of a country, territory, or person) subject to the authority, control, or sovereignty of another.

Examples

Dependent territory
Hong Kong was once a dependent territory of the United Kingdom.
Several dependent states sought to gain full independence after the war.
The island is a dependent province governed by a distant country.

Related Terms

  • antonym: independent - free from outside control; not subject to the authority of another
  • synonym: subject - under the rule or control of another power; subordinate
