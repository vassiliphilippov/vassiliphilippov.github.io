broadband

adjective
operating over a wide frequency range
4.8 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈbrɔːd.bænd/ /; / /ˈbrɑːd.bænd/ (AmE), /ˈbrɔːd.bænd/ (BrE)
using or relating to a system or technology that operates over a wide range of frequencies, allowing high-speed data transmission (especially in telecommunications)

Examples

Broadband technology
A broadband amplifier can process signals from many different channels simultaneously.
Modern communication systems often depend on broadband technology for faster data transmission.
The engineer installed a broadband antenna to improve the range of frequencies received.
High-definition video streaming is possible thanks to broadband technology.
Many households rely on broadband connections for fast internet access.
The government is investing in rural broadband infrastructure to improve connectivity.
