Nabataean

noun
ancient Aramaic dialect
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnæbəˈtiːən / (AmE), / ˌnæbəˈtiːən / (BrE)
the form of Aramaic language used by the Nabataeans in their inscriptions and documents

Examples
