undashed

adjective
not broken
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdæʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈdæʃt / (BrE)
not broken, smashed, or destroyed; remaining whole or intact

Examples

Undashed glass
The delicate glass remained undashed after the earthquake shook the house.
She was relieved to find her phone undashed at the bottom of her bag.
Despite the chaos, the family photo frame was still undashed on the shelf.

Related Terms

  • antonym: broken - damaged and no longer in one piece
  • synonym: intact - not damaged or impaired; complete
