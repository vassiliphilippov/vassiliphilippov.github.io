colorblind

adjective
unable to distinguish colors
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlərˌblaɪnd / (AmE), / ˈkʌləˌblaɪnd / (BrE)
unable to see certain colors or to distinguish between some colors, usually due to a visual impairment

Examples

Colorblind vision
Because he is colorblind, he cannot tell red from green.
The test revealed that she was colorblind and had difficulty seeing certain shades.
Many colorblind people use special apps to help identify colors in daily life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: color vision deficiency - a reduced ability to see or distinguish colors
  • antonym: normal vision - the ability to see all colors normally
