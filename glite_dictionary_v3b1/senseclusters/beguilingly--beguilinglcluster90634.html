beguilingly

adverb
charmingly, deceptively
Pronunciation: / bɪˈɡaɪlɪŋli / (AmE), / bɪˈɡaɪlɪŋli / (BrE)
in a charming or attractive way that may be deceptive

Examples

To speak beguilingly
He spoke beguilingly, making everyone believe his story without question.
The advertisement beguilingly promised results that seemed too good to be true.
She looked at him beguilingly, hoping to persuade him to change his mind.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bluntly - in a direct and uncharming way
  • synonym: charmingly - in a pleasant or attractive manner
  • synonym: deceptively - in a way that gives a false impression
