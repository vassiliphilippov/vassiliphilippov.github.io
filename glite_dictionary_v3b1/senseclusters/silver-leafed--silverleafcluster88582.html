silver-leafed

adjective
having silvery leaves
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvɚ liːft / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə liːft / (BrE)
(of a plant) having leaves that are silvery in color or appearance.

Examples

Silver-leafed plant
A silver-leafed variety of sage lined the pathway with its shimmering foliage.
Gardeners often choose silver-leafed plants to create contrast in their flower beds.
The silver-leafed eucalyptus stood out against the green forest backdrop.
