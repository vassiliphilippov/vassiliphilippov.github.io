chanting

noun
intoning words in a rhythmic, repetitive way
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæn.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːn.tɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of singing or speaking words in a repeated, rhythmic, and often monotonous tone

Examples

The chanting crowd
A low chanting could be heard coming from the crowd gathered in the square.
Her chanting had a calming effect on everyone in the room.
The monks began their chanting before sunrise as part of the daily prayers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intoning - reciting or chanting in a particular tone
  • synonym: singing - the act of producing musical tones with the voice
