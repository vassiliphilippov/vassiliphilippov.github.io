parachute

noun
device for slowing a fall from an aircraft
2.1 1.7
Pronunciation: /ˈpær.ə.ʃuːt/ /; / /ˈpær.ə.tʃuːt/ (AmE), /ˈpær.ə.ʃuːt/ /; / /ˈpær.ə.tʃuːt/ (BrE)
A device made of fabric that fills with air and slows the fall of a person or object dropped from an aircraft.

Examples

A parachute jump
During training, each soldier must learn how to pack and deploy a parachute safely.
The pilot was able to escape the burning plane by using his parachute.
The skydiver floated gracefully to the ground under her colorful parachute.
