Aldous Huxley

proper noun
English writer and author of 'Brave New World'
Pronunciation: / ˈældəs ˈhʌksli / (AmE), / ˈældəs ˈhʌksli / (BrE)
(1894–1963) English writer best known for his novel 'Brave New World' and for his exploration of philosophical and social issues.
