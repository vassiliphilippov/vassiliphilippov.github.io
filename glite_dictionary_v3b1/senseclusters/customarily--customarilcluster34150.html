customarily

adverb
according to custom or tradition
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌstəmˌɛrɪli / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəm(ə)rəli / (BrE)
in a way that is usual or expected based on custom or tradition; according to accepted practices

Examples

Are customarily expected
Children are customarily expected to start school at the age of five or six.
In some cultures, guests are customarily offered tea upon arrival.
Meals are customarily served at specific times during the day.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rarely - not often; seldom
  • synonym: traditionally - in a way that follows tradition; according to tradition
  • synonym: usually - in most cases; generally
