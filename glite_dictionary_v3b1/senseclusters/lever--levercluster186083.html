lever

verb
move or force using leverage
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈliːvər / (AmE), / ˈliːvə / (BrE)
to move or force something, especially to open it, using a lever or similar tool

Examples

To lever a lid
If you can't lift the lid, try levering it up from the side.
She managed to lever the stuck window open with a screwdriver.
The mechanic levered the old tire off the rim with great effort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jimmy - to force open using a lever or similar tool
  • synonym: pry - to use force to try to open or move something
