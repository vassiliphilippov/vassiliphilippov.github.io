antimilitarist

noun
opponent of militarism
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈmɪlɪtərɪst / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈmɪlɪtərɪst / (BrE)
a person who opposes military influence, policies, or the use of armed forces

Examples

An antimilitarist leader
The antimilitarist argued that increased military spending would not lead to lasting peace.
As an outspoken antimilitarist, she attended rallies calling for disarmament.
Many antimilitarists believe that conflicts should be resolved through diplomacy rather than force.

Related Terms

  • antonym: militarist - a person who supports military power or the use of armed forces
  • synonym: pacifist - a person who believes in peace and opposes war or violence
