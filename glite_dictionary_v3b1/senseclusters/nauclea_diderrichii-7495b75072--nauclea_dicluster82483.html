Nauclea diderrichii

noun
large African tree yielding durable timber
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnaʊˈkliːə dɪˌdɛrɪˈkaɪ / (AmE), / ˌnaʊˈkliːə dɪˌdɛrɪˈkaɪ / (BrE)
