white bread

noun
bread made from refined wheat flour
Pronunciation: / waɪt brɛd / (AmE), / waɪt brɛd / (BrE)
bread made from refined and usually bleached wheat flour, with a light color and soft texture

Examples

Toast made with white bread is often served with jam and butter.
