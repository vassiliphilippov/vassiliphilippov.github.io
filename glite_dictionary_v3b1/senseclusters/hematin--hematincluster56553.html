hematin

noun
iron-containing red pigment from hemoglobin
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːmətɪn / (AmE), / ˈhiːmətɪn / (BrE)
a complex red pigment containing iron, formed from hemoglobin and involved in oxygen transport.
