print

noun
mark or impression left on a surface
2.0 9.07/m
Pronunciation: / prÉªnt / (AmE), / prÉªnt / (BrE)
a mark or impression left on a surface by an object, such as a hand, foot, or animal paw

Examples

A hand print
The child pressed her hand onto the clay, leaving a clear print.
The detective carefully examined the print left on the dusty windowsill.
There was a muddy print on the kitchen floor after the dog ran inside.

Synonyms

  • imprint
    4.2 1.38/m
    imprint is a clear mark made by pressure or a mold, while print is any mark left by a hand, foot, or paw.
  • impression
    4.1 5.83/m
    impression is a shallow mark or indentation left by pressure, while print is a physical mark left by a hand, foot, or paw.

Related Terms

  • synonym: footprint - a mark left by a foot on a surface
  • synonym: impression - a mark produced by pressure
  • synonym: stain - a mark left on a surface; sometimes as a result of something being pressed or dropped
