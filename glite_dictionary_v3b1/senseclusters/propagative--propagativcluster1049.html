propagative

adjective
relating to propagation or spread
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑː.pəˌɡeɪ.tɪv / (AmE), / ˈprɒ.pə.ɡə.tɪv / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by propagation, especially in terms of reproduction, spread, or transmission.

Examples

Propagative success
Certain agricultural methods focus on optimizing the propagative success of crops.
Scientists studied the propagative mechanisms of viruses in host cells.
The propagative ability of certain plants allows them to spread rapidly across new environments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: propagation - the act or process of reproducing, spreading, or transmitting
