organizer

noun
person who recruits union members
1.0 2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrɡəˌnaɪzər /; / ˈɔrɡəˌnaɪzər /; / ˈɔːr.ɡə.naɪ.zər / (AmE), / ˈɔːɡənaɪzə /; / ˈɔː.ɡə.naɪ.zə / (BrE)
a person who arranges, coordinates, or recruits people for a group, event, or activity

Examples

The organiser of the festival handled every detail with precision.
