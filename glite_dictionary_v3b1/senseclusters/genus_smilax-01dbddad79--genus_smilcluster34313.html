genus Smilax

noun
genus of climbing plants (Smilax)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈsmaɪ.læks / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈsmaɪ.laks / (BrE)
A genus of climbing or trailing plants in the family Smilacaceae or sometimes placed in Liliaceae, known for species such as greenbriers and catbriers.

Examples

Genus Smilax plants
Botanists often debate whether the genus Smilax belongs in Smilacaceae or Liliaceae.
Members of the genus Smilax can be found in temperate and tropical regions around the world.
The genus Smilax includes many species commonly known as greenbriers or catbriers.
