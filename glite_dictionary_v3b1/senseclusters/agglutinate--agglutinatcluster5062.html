agglutinate

adjective
joined or stuck together
Pronunciation: / əˈɡluːtɪˌneɪt / (AmE), / əˈɡluːtɪˌneɪt / (BrE)
united or joined together as if by glue; stuck together

Examples

Agglutinate cells
Certain bacteria produce substances that allow them to become agglutinate in clusters.
The agglutinate cells formed visible clumps under the microscope.
The agglutinate particles were difficult to separate due to their sticky surfaces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cohesive - characterized by or causing cohesion; tending to stick together
  • antonym: dispersed - separated or spread out; not joined together
