stoop

verb
bend forward, often habitually
Pronunciation: / stuːp / (AmE), / stuːp / (BrE)
to bend your body forward and downward, often at the waist

Examples

To stoop down
After years of working in the fields, he began to stoop as he walked.

She stooped down to pick up a coin from the ground.
The old woman stooped to tie her shoes.
