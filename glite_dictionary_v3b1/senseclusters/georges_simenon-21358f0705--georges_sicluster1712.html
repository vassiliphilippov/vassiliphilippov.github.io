Georges Simenon

proper noun
Belgian-born French detective novelist
Pronunciation: / ʒɔrʒ siˈmeɪnɒn /; / ʒɔrʒ siːməˈnɒn / (AmE), / ʒɔːʒ siˈmeɪnɒ̃ /; / ʒɔːʒ siməˈnɒ̃ / (BrE)
