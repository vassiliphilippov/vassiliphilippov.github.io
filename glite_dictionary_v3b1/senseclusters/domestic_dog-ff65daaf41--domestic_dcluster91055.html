domestic dog

noun
a domesticated mammal, companion animal
4.0
Pronunciation: / dəˈmestɪk dɔɡ / (AmE), / dəˈmɛstɪk dɒɡ / (BrE)
a domesticated mammal (Canis familiaris), descended from the wolf, kept by humans as a pet or working animal; occurs in many breeds
