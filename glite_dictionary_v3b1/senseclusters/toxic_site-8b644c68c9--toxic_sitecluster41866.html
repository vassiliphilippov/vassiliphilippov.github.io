toxic site

noun
location contaminated by hazardous waste
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑksɪk saɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɒksɪk saɪt / (BrE)
A location where hazardous waste has been dumped and identified by authorities as needing cleanup to remove contaminants.

Examples

A toxic site cleanup
Residents expressed concerns about living close to a toxic site due to health risks.
The government allocated funds to clean up the toxic site near the river.
The old factory was declared a toxic site after decades of chemical dumping.
