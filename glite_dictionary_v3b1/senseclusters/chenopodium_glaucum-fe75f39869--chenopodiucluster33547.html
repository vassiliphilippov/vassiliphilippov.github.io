An annual European plant of the goosefoot family, with spikes of greenish flowers and leaves that are white and hairy beneath, commonly found as a weed in North America.

Examples

Chenopodium glaucum plant
Chenopodium glaucum is often found growing along roadsides and riverbanks in North America.
Farmers sometimes have to remove Chenopodium glaucum from their fields to protect their crops.
The leaves of Chenopodium glaucum are distinctive, with their white, hairy undersides.
