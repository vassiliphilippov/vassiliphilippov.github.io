rising

adjective
sloping upward
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈraɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
sloping upward or showing an upward incline.

Examples

Rising road
A rising road wound through the mountains, offering breathtaking views.
From her window, she could see the rising land stretching toward the horizon.
The hikers stopped to rest on the rising path that led up the hill.
