military leader

noun
commander of military forces
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪləˌtɛri ˈliːdər / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri ˈliːdə / (BrE)
a person who commands or leads military forces

Examples

A military leader commands troops
She hoped to become a military leader and serve her country with honor.
The general was a highly respected military leader during the conflict.
Throughout history, many military leaders have shaped the fate of nations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: civilian leader - a person who leads without military experience or authority
  • synonym: commander - a person in authority, especially over a military unit
