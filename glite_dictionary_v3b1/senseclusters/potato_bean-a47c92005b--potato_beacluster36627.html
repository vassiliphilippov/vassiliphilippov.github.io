potato bean

noun
North American vine with edible tubers
Pronunciation: / pəˈteɪtoʊ biːn /; / pəˈteɪɾoʊ biːn / (AmE), / pəˈteɪtəʊ biːn / (BrE)
a North American climbing plant with edible tubers, formerly an important food source for Native Americans

Examples

North American potato bean
