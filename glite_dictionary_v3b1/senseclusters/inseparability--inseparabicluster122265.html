inseparability

noun
impossible to separate
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌsɛpərəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ɪnˌsɛpərəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being impossible to separate or divide from something or someone else

Examples

The inseparability of language
The inseparability of language and culture makes translation a complex task.
Their friendship was marked by a sense of inseparability that lasted for decades.
Many philosophers discuss the inseparability of mind and body in their theories.

Related Terms

  • antonym: separability - the quality of being able to be separated
  • synonym: unity - the state of being joined together as a whole
