waterside

noun
land beside a body of water
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔːtərˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəsaɪd / (BrE)
the land along the edge of a river, lake, or other body of water

Examples

The waterside land
A group of ducks gathered along the waterside to feed and bask in the sun.
They built their house on the waterside so they could enjoy the beautiful view every morning.
Walking by the waterside at dusk is a peaceful way to end the day.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inland - away from the water or coast
  • synonym: riverside - the land at the side of a river
  • synonym: seaside - the land along or near the sea
