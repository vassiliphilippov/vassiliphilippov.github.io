aster

noun
a plant with star-shaped flowers, genus Aster
Pronunciation: / ˈæstər / (AmE), / ˈɑːstə / (BrE)
a plant of the genus Aster or related genera, with star-shaped, often brightly colored flowers, typically flowering in the fall

The purple aster is a favorite among pollinators.
