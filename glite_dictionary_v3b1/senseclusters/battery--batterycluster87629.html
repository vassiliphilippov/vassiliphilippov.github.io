battery

noun
group of military guns or launchers
Pronunciation: / ˈbætəri / (AmE), / ˈbætəri / (BrE)
a group of guns or missile launchers positioned together and operated as a unit in the field, especially in the military

Examples

Artillery battery
During the exercise, the army set up a temporary battery of missile launchers.
The coastal defense battery was equipped with four cannons facing the sea.
The enemy targeted the battery, forcing the operators to seek cover.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artillery - large-caliber guns used in warfare on land
