freakishly

adverb
in an extremely unusual or abnormal way
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːkɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈfriːkɪʃli / (BrE)
In a way that is extremely unusual, abnormal, or unexpected.

Examples

To be freakishly strong
He was freakishly strong for someone of his size.
The old building stood freakishly tall against the low city skyline.
The weather has been freakishly unpredictable this month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abnormally - in a way that is not typical or usual
  • antonym: normally - in the usual or expected way
