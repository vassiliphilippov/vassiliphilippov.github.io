renewably

adverb
in a renewable way
4.2
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈnuː.ə.bliː/ /; / /rɪ.ˈnjuː.ə.bliː/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈnjuː.ə.bliː/ /; / /rɪ.ˈnuː.ə.bliː/ (BrE)
in a manner that allows something, such as a contract or license, to be renewed or extended repeatedly.
