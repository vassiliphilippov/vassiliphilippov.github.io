IPR

proper noun
Pacific affairs institute
Pronunciation: / aɪ piː ɑːr / (AmE), / aɪ piː ɑː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for the Institute of Pacific Relations, a historical international organization focused on Pacific-area affairs from 1925 to the 1960s

Examples

IPR meeting
The IPR played a significant role in discussions about international relations in the Pacific region.
Historians often reference the IPR when studying diplomatic history between Asia and the West.
Many important conferences on Pacific affairs were organized by the IPR during the mid-twentieth century.
