snooper

noun
person who pries into others' affairs
1.3 5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsnuː.pər/ (AmE), /ˈsnuː.pə/ (BrE)
a person who tries to find out about other people's private affairs, especially by secret or intrusive means

Examples

The snooper was caught trying to read confidential documents in the office.
You shouldn't act like a snooper and go through someone else's mail.
