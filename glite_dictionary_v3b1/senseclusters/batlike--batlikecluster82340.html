batlike

adjective
resembling a bat
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbætˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈbætˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or similar to a bat in appearance, movement, or manner

Examples

Batlike wings
The mysterious animal had batlike wings that stretched wide in the moonlight.
Her costume included a batlike mask and a flowing black cape.
The cave was filled with strange, batlike creatures hanging from the ceiling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: batty - crazy or eccentric (informal); also, relating to bats
  • antonym: birdlike - resembling a bird in appearance or manner
