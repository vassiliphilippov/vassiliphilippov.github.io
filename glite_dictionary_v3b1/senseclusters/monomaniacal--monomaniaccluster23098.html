monomaniacal

adjective
obsessed with one idea or subject
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːnoʊməˈnaɪəkəl / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəʊməˈnaɪəkəl / (BrE)
Obsessed with a single subject or idea to an excessive or abnormal degree.

Examples

A monomaniacal focus
His monomaniacal pursuit of perfection often alienated his colleagues.
She became monomaniacal about the project, thinking about it day and night.
The author's monomaniacal focus on one theme is evident throughout the novel.
