dolphin hugger

noun
advocate of dolphin/marine life protection
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːlfɪn ˈhʌɡər / (AmE), / ˈdɒlfɪn ˈhʌɡə / (BrE)
An informal term for a person who strongly supports the protection of marine life, especially dolphins.

Examples

A dolphin hugger activist
Critics sometimes dismiss activists as mere dolphin huggers, but their work has real environmental impact.
Many environmental groups are led by dedicated dolphin huggers fighting for stricter marine protections.
She was called a dolphin hugger because of her passionate efforts to save endangered species of dolphins.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tree hugger - a person who is very concerned about protecting the environment
