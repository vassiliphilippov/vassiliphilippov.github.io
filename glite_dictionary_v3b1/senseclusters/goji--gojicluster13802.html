goji

noun
Asian red berry
Pronunciation: / ˈgoʊdʒi / (AmE), / ˈgəʊdʒi / (BrE)
a small red berry from Asia, often eaten for its health benefits and used in traditional medicine

Examples

Dried goji berries
Many people add dried goji to their breakfast cereal for extra nutrition.
The health store sells organic goji in both berry and juice form.
Researchers are studying the possible benefits of goji for boosting the immune system.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: blueberry - a small blue edible berry
  • synonym: superfood - a food considered especially nutritious or beneficial to health
  • synonym: wolfberry - another name for the goji berry
