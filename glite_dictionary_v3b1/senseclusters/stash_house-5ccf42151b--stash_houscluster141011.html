stash house

noun
house used to hide illegal supplies
3.0
Pronunciation: / stæʃ haʊs / (AmE), / stæʃ haʊs / (BrE)
a house used for hiding weapons, drugs, or other illegal supplies

Examples

A stash house for drugs
Criminal organizations often use a stash house to store drugs and avoid detection.
She suspected that the abandoned building next door was being used as a stash house.
The police raided the stash house and found a large cache of illegal firearms.
