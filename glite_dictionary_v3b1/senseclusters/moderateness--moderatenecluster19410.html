moderateness

noun
the state of being moderate, especially in price
6.8 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…ëÀêd…ôr…ôtn…ôs /; / Ààm…ëÀê.d…ö.…ôt.n…ôs / (AmE), / Ààm…íd…ôr…ôtn…ôs /; / Ààm…íd.…ôr.…ôt.n…ôs / (BrE)
the quality of being moderate and not extreme in amount, behavior, opinion, or other aspects

Examples

The moderateness of prices
She appreciated the moderateness of the restaurant's prices compared to others in the area.
The moderateness of their rent allowed them to save money for other necessities.
The proposal was praised for its moderateness in both cost and scope.
He approached both pleasure and work with moderateness, never allowing either to take over his life.
Her moderateness in debates made her well-respected among colleagues.
Political moderateness is often valued in societies looking to avoid polarization.
