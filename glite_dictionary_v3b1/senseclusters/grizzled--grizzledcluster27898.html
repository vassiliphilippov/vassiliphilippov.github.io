grizzled

adjective
streaked with grey or white hair
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɪzəld / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪzəld / (BrE)
having hair, fur, or a beard that is streaked or mixed with grey or white

Examples

Grizzled grey hair
He ran his fingers through his grizzled hair, remembering his youth.
Her dog's grizzled coat made it look older than it really was.
The old man's grizzled beard gave him a distinguished appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gray - color between black and white, also hair color due to age
