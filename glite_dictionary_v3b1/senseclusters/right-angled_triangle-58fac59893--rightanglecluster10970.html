right-angled triangle

noun
triangle with a right angle
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪt ˌæŋgəld ˈtraɪˌæŋgəl / (AmE), / ˈraɪt ˌæŋɡld ˈtraɪˌæŋɡl / (BrE)

Examples
