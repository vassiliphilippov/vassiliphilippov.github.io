cliquish

adjective
prone to social exclusiveness; exclusive
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkliːkɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈklɪkɪʃ / (BrE)
tending to form exclusive groups that avoid including others who are considered outsiders or inferior

Examples

A cliquish group
His experiences at the cliquish school led him to value inclusiveness.
Some people found her friends to be too cliquish and hard to approach.
The club became so cliquish that new members felt unwelcome.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exclusive - limiting membership to a select group
  • antonym: open - willing to include others; not exclusive
