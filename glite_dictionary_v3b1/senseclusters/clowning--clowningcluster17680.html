clowning

noun
comic acts or performance, clown-like behavior
1.6 3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈklaʊnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklaʊnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of behaving in a funny or silly way to make people laugh, like a clown
