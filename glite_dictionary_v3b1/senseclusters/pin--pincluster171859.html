pin

noun
pinned social-media post
3.0
Pronunciation: /pɪn/ (AmE), /pɪn/ (BrE)
A message, post, or announcement fixed to the top of a social media profile, forum, or page so it remains prominently visible.

Examples

A top pin (online)
The company used a pin at the top of its page to highlight the job opening.
Before the event she saved a screenshot of the pin so volunteers knew the meeting details.
Moderators turned the announcement into a pin to prevent it from being lost among new posts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pinned post - a post intentionally fixed to remain at the top of a profile or discussion
  • synonym: sticky - a forum post kept at the top of a thread so it is always visible
