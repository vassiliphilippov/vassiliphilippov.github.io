courteously

adverb
in a polite and respectful way
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrt̬iəsli / (AmE), / ˈkɔːtiəsli / (BrE)
In a polite and respectful manner.

Examples

To courteously yield
Drivers are expected to courteously yield to pedestrians at crosswalks.
She greeted all the visitors courteously and offered them tea.
The manager courteously listened to each of our concerns before replying.

Related Terms

  • synonym: politely - in a polite manner
  • antonym: rudely - in an impolite or disrespectful manner
