nephroblastoma

A malignant tumor of the kidney occurring in young children, often associated with high blood pressure and blood in the urine.

Examples

Child nephroblastoma tumor
Early detection of nephroblastoma can significantly improve outcomes for young patients.
The child was diagnosed with nephroblastoma after presenting with abdominal pain and blood in the urine.
Treatment of nephroblastoma often involves surgery, chemotherapy, and sometimes radiation therapy.
