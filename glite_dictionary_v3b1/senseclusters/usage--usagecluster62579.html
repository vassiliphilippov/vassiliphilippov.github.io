usage

noun
accepted or habitual practice
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːsɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈjuːsɪdʒ / (BrE)
the way something is used or the usual practice of using it

Examples

Daily usage of water
It has become common usage to greet people with a handshake.
Old English usage differs greatly from modern conventions.
The usage of cell phones in class is strictly prohibited.
Limiting water usage is essential during drought conditions to conserve resources.
Recent data show that smartphone usage among teenagers has increased significantly in the past decade.
The software’s usage varies depending on the user’s needs and level of expertise.

  • synonym: practice - the actual application or use of an idea, belief, or method
  • synonym: use - the act of using something
  • synonym: utilization - the action of making practical use of something
