pass out

verb
lose consciousness suddenly
1.0
Pronunciation: / pæs aʊt / (AmE), / pɑːs aʊt / (BrE)
to become unconscious or faint suddenly

Examples

To pass out suddenly
He was so exhausted during the hike that he suddenly passed out on the trail.
If you don't eat something soon, you might pass out from hunger.
People sometimes pass out at the sight of blood or after receiving shocking news.
He felt dizzy and suddenly passed out during the ceremony.
If you hold your breath for too long, you might pass out.
She passed out from exhaustion after running the marathon.
