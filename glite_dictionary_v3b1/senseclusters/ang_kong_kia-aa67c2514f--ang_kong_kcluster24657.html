ang kong kia

noun
selfish or spoiled person
Pronunciation: / ɑŋ kɔŋ kiə / (AmE), / ɑŋ kɒŋ kiə / (BrE)
(informal, regional) A person who is selfish, overly attached to their belongings, or is spoiled and sensitive; often used to criticize someone's unwillingness to share or their pampered behavior.

Examples

The ang kong kia child
Her little brother is known as the family's ang kong kia because he never shares his snacks.
No one likes to play with an ang kong kia who whines when things don't go his way.
Stop being such an ang kong kia and share your toys with your friends!
