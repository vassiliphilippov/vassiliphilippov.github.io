shimmery

adjective
glistening or sparkling faintly
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪməri / (AmE), / ˈʃɪməri / (BrE)
shining with a soft, tremulous, or wavering light; glistening or sparkling faintly.

Examples

A shimmery light
Raindrops left a shimmery trail on the window.
She wore a shimmery top that caught the light as she moved.
The moonlight cast a shimmery glow over the quiet garden.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dull - lacking brightness or sheen; not shiny
  • synonym: shiny - having a smooth, polished, or glossy surface
