Hani

noun
Loloish language of southern Yunnan, China
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːni / (AmE), / ˈhɑːni / (BrE)
a Loloish language spoken by the Hani people in southern Yunnan Province, China, especially in Jingdong and Jinggu counties and along the Yuanjiang and Lancang rivers.
