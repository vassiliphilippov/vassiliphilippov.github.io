underfinanced

adjective
lacking funds
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈfaɪnænst / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈfaɪnænstd / (BrE)
lacking sufficient money or financial support to operate effectively

Examples

An underfinanced hospital
The underfinanced hospital could not afford to update its medical equipment.
Many underfinanced startups struggle to survive in a competitive market.
The project remained underfinanced despite several attempts to secure more investment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: underfunded - not having enough money to function properly
  • antonym: well-funded - having plenty of money or financial support
