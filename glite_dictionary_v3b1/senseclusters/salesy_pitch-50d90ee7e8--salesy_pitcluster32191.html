salesy pitch

noun
insincere or pushy sales message
9.9 6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪlzi pɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈseɪlzi pɪtʃ / (BrE)
a message or speech that is overtly focused on making a sale, often seen as insincere or pushy

Examples

A salesy pitch message
During the presentation, he avoided a salesy pitch and focused on solving our problems instead.
Many customers are turned off by salesy pitches and prefer honest advice.
The email was full of salesy pitches that made me lose interest in the product.
