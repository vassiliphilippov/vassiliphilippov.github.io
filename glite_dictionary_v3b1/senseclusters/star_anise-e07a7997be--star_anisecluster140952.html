star anise

noun
small tree; source of star-shaped spice
Pronunciation: / stɑr əˈnis / (AmE), / stɑː əˈniːs / (BrE)
A small evergreen tree native to Japan and Taiwan, whose star-shaped fruit is used as a spice; the flowers are not fragrant.

Examples

Star anise tree
A star anise tree can be identified by its glossy leaves and star-shaped fruit.
Despite its name, star anise blossoms have little to no fragrance.
The dried fruit of the star anise is often used in Asian cooking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Illicium verum - the spice-producing Chinese star anise plant
