fibro

noun
fibromyalgia (informal)
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪbroʊ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪbrəʊ / (BrE)
an informal name for fibromyalgia, a medical condition causing widespread muscle pain and fatigue

Examples

Fibro pain
After months of unexplained pain, she was diagnosed with fibro and began a rehabilitation programme.
Many people with fibro struggle to find effective treatments for their symptoms.
Support groups for fibro can help patients feel less isolated and more understood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fibromyalgia - a chronic disorder causing muscle pain and fatigue
