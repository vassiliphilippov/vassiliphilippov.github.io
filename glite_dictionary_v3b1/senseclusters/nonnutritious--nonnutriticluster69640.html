nonnutritious

adjective
lacking nutrients
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnnuˈtrɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˌnɒnnuːˈtrɪʃəs / (BrE)
not providing nourishment or useful nutrients for health or growth

Examples

A nonnutritious snack
Many popular snacks are nonnutritious and high in sugar or fat.
Children often prefer nonnutritious foods like chips and candy over fruits and vegetables.
Eating too many nonnutritious meals can lead to health problems over time.
