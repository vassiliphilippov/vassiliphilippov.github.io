Hall of Fame

noun
building honoring distinguished people
Pronunciation: / hɔl əv feɪm / (AmE), / hɔːl əv feɪm / (BrE)
a building or group of people that honors individuals who have achieved distinction in a particular field, often featuring trophies, plaques, or exhibits celebrating their accomplishments

Examples

The Hall of Fame museum
Baseball fans can visit the National Baseball Hall of Fame in Cooperstown.
She was inducted into the Hall of Fame after a remarkable career in sports.
The museum's new wing is dedicated as a Hall of Fame for local artists.

Related Terms

  • synonym: honor roll - a list of people recognized for outstanding achievement
