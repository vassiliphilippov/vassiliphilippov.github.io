ticketless

adjective
without a ticket
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪkɪtləs / (AmE), / ˈtɪkɪtləs / (BrE)
not having a valid ticket, especially when attending, traveling, or entering somewhere without paying or possessing a ticket

Examples

Ticketless travel
The inspector fined two ticketless commuters after finding they had boarded without paying.
Many fans tried to enter the concert ticketless, but security stopped them at the gate.
Traveling ticketless on public transport can result in a heavy fine if you are caught.

Related Terms

  • antonym: admitted - allowed to enter, especially with a ticket or permission
  • synonym: unauthorized - not having official permission or approval
