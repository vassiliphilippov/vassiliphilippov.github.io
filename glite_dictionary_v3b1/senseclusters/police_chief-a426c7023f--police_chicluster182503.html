police chief

noun
head officer of a police department
5.5 2.7
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈliːs tʃiːf/ /; / /poʊ.ˈliːs tʃiːf/ (AmE), /pə.ˈliːs tʃiːf/ /; / /pə.ˈlɪs tʃiːf/ (BrE)
The officer in charge of a police department or precinct, responsible for overseeing law enforcement operations.

Examples

Police chief of city
After years of dedicated service, she was promoted to police chief of the city.
The police chief addressed the community about recent crime rates at the town meeting.
The mayor consulted with the police chief following the incident downtown.
