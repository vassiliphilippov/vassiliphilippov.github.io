solid propellant rocket

noun
rocket using solid chemical fuel
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːlɪd prəˈpɛl·ənt ˈrɑːk·ɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɒlɪd prəˈpɛl·ənt ˈrɒk·ɪt / (BrE)
A rocket that uses a chemical propellant in solid form to generate thrust.

Examples
