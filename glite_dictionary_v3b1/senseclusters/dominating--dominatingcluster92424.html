dominating

adjective
rising high and overlooking surroundings
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːməˌneɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɒmɪˌneɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a height or viewpoint) overlooking and commanding the area around it; rising high above its surroundings.

Examples

Dominating hilltop view
From the dominating cliffs, you can watch the waves crash far below.
The castle was built on a dominating hill that allowed defenders to see for miles.
The city's skyline is defined by a dominating skyscraper that towers above all other buildings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commanding - having a position of dominance or control, especially a view
  • synonym: overlooking - having a view that looks down on an area
