worth a shot

x
worth trying; worth an attempt
Pronunciation: / wɝːθ ə ʃɑt / (AmE), / wɜːθ ə ʃɒt / (BrE)
used to say that something is worth trying, even if the outcome is uncertain

Examples

Worth a shot trying
I know the odds are low, but applying for the job is worth a shot.
It might not work, but it's worth a shot.
The recipe looks tricky, but I think it's worth a shot for dinner tonight.
