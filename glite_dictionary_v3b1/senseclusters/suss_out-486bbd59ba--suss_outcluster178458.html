suss out

verb
investigate or figure out something
9.9
Pronunciation: / sʌs aʊt / (AmE), / sʌs aʊt / (BrE)
to investigate or figure something out in order to understand it

Examples

To suss out the problem

Before signing the contract, make sure you suss out all the terms and conditions.
Can you suss out whether this restaurant has any vegetarian options?
It took him weeks to suss out what was really going on at the office.
