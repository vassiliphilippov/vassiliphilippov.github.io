hunter

noun
person who hunts animals
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈhʌn.tə / (BrE)
a person who hunts wild animals for food, sport, or profit

Examples

A skilled hunter
As a skilled hunter, he knew how to move without making a sound.
During the winter, local hunters search for game in the mountains.
The hunter tracked the deer silently through the dense forest.

Related Terms

  • antonym: poacher - person who hunts animals illegally
  • antonym: prey - an animal that is hunted and killed for food
  • synonym: trapper - person who catches wild animals, typically for their fur
