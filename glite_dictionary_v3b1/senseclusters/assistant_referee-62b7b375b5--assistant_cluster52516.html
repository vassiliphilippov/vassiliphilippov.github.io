assistant referee

noun
sports official assisting the referee
2.0 2.8
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈsɪs.tənt ref.ə.ˈriː/ (AmE), /ə.ˈsɪs.tənt ref.ə.ˈriː/ (BrE)
