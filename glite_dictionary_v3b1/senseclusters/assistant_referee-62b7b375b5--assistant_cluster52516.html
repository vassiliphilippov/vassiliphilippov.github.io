assistant referee

noun
sports official assisting the referee
Pronunciation: / əˈsɪstənt ˌrɛfəˈri / (AmE), / əˈsɪstənt ˌrɛfəˈriː / (BrE)
an official who assists the main referee, especially by judging out-of-bounds, offsides, or other specific aspects of a game

Examples

The assistant referee signals
Both teams respected the decisions made by the assistant referee.
During the match, the assistant referee kept a close watch on the sidelines.
The assistant referee raised the flag to signal an offside offense.

Related Terms

  • synonym: referee - the main official overseeing a game or match
