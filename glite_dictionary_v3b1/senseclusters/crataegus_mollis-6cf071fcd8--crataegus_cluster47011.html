Crataegus mollis

noun
a North American red-fruited hawthorn
9.9
Pronunciation: / krəˈtiːɡəs ˈmɑlɪs / (AmE), / krəˈtiːɡəs ˈmɒlɪs / (BrE)
A species of American hawthorn with red fruit, having stems and leaves densely covered with short woolly hairs.
