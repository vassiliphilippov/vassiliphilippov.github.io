Vidua

noun
genus of African whydahs (birds)
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪdjuːə /; / ˈvaɪdjuə / (AmE), / ˈvɪdjʊə / (BrE)
a genus of small African songbirds called whydahs, with males that have long tail feathers during the breeding season
