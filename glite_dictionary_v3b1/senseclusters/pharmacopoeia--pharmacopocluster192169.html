pharmacopoeia

noun
official list or collection of medicinal drugs
9.9 0.16/m
Pronunciation: / Àåf…ëÀêrm…ôk…ôÀàpiÀê…ô /; / Àåf…ërm…ôk…ôÀàpi…ô / (AmE), / Àåf…ëÀêm…ôk…ôÀàpiÀê…ô / (BrE)
A collection or official list of drugs and medicinal preparations, often with information on their uses, preparation, and standards.

Examples

The pharmacopoeia list
She studied the traditional pharmacopoeia of herbal remedies used in ancient medicine.
The hospital maintains an extensive pharmacopoeia to ensure they can treat a variety of conditions.
The national pharmacopoeia sets the standards for drug quality and safety in the country.
Medical professionals often refer to the pharmacopeia for information on dosage and drug standards.
The hospital's pharmacy must comply with all regulations stated in the national pharmacopeia.
The new edition of the pharmacopeia includes updated guidelines for drug preparation and testing.
