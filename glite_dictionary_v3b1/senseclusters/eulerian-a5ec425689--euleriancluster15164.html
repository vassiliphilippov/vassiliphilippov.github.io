Eulerian

adjective
of Euler or his work
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɔɪˈlɛriən / (AmE), / ɔɪˈlɪəriən / (BrE)
relating to the mathematician Euler or to mathematical concepts named after him, especially in mathematics and physics

Examples

Eulerian formula
The Eulerian path problem is a classic topic in graph theory.
In fluid dynamics, the Eulerian approach focuses on specific locations in the space through which fluids flow.
Many mathematical terms, such as Eulerian circuit, honor the contributions of Leonhard Euler.
