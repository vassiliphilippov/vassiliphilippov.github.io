bourse

noun
European stock exchange or financial market
9.6 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / b ärs / (AmE), / b…îÀês / (BrE)
A stock exchange, especially one in a European country; a financial market where securities are bought and sold.

Examples

European bourse
After the announcement, stock prices soared at the bourse.
Many investors follow trends on the bourse to guide their decisions.
The Paris bourse is one of the major financial markets in Europe.

Synonyms

  • stock market
