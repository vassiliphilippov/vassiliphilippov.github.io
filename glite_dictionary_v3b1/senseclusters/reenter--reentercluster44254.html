reenter

verb
to enter again
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈɛntər / (AmE), / ˌriːˈɛntə / (BrE)
to enter information or data again, especially into a computer system, form, or database

Examples

To reenter your password
If you make a mistake, you will need to reenter your password to continue.
The system asked her to reenter her email address after the session expired.
Please reenter the data carefully to avoid any errors in the report.

Related Terms

  • antonym: delete - to remove data or information from a system
  • synonym: input - to enter data or information into a system
