Brotulidae

noun
family of deep-sea fishes
Pronunciation: / broʊˈtjuːlɪˌdiː /; / broʊˈtuːlɪˌdiː / (AmE), / brəʊˈtjuːlɪˌdiː / (BrE)
a family of deep-sea fishes related to cusk-eels (Ophidiidae), with elongated bodies, found in marine environments

New species within the Brotulidae family are still being discovered by marine biologists.

The Brotulidae are found primarily in deep-sea habitats worldwide.
