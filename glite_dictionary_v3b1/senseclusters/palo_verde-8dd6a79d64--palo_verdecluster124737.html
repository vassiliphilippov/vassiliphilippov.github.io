palo verde

noun
spiny tree with green bark and yellow flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːloʊ ˈvɜːrdi / (AmE), / ˌpæləʊ ˈvɜːdi / (BrE)
A spiny tree native to the southwestern United States and Mexico, with blue-green bark and yellow flowers, often grown as an ornamental plant.

Examples

The palo verde tree
Every spring, the palo verde bursts into a brilliant display of yellow flowers.
The palo verde provides vital shade in desert gardens and parks.
We stopped under a palo verde to escape the midday sun.
