microvasculature

noun
tiny vessel network
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˈvæskjələˌtʃʊr / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˈvæskjʊlətʃə / (BrE)
the network of very small blood vessels or conducting vessels, especially in plant leaves and stems, that transport water and nutrients

Examples

Plant microvasculature
Scientists examined the microvasculature of the plant to understand how it copes with water stress.
Damage to the microvasculature can reduce the efficiency of nutrient transport in leaves.
Advanced imaging techniques reveal the complex patterns of microvasculature in different plant species.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capillaries - the smallest blood vessels or plant vessels
  • degree lower: vasculature - the arrangement or system of blood vessels or plant vessels
