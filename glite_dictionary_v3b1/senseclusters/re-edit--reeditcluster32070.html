re-edit

noun
revised version
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈɛdɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈɛdɪt / (BrE)
a new version of a text, film, or recording that has been edited again after its original release or creation

Examples

A movie re-edit
The studio released a special re-edit of the movie for its anniversary edition.
Fans were excited to see the re-edit of the novel with additional chapters.
After receiving feedback, the author published a re-edit to address the criticisms.

  • synonym: revision - a changed or updated version of something
