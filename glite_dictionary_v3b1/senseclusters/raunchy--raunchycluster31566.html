raunchy

adjective
sexually explicit; indecent; coarse
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑntʃi / (AmE), / ˈrɔːntʃi / (BrE)
sexually explicit, vulgar, or indecent in a way that is considered rude or shocking

Examples

A raunchy novel
He enjoys reading raunchy novels that push the boundaries of decency.
She criticized the film for its raunchy scenes and explicit dialogue.
The comedian's raunchy jokes shocked some members of the audience.
She wrote a raunchy romance novel that became a bestseller.
Some comedians are famous for their raunchy sense of humor.
The movie was criticized for being too raunchy for a family audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clean - not obscene, vulgar, or offensive
  • synonym: lewd - sexually unchaste or obscene
  • synonym: obscene - offensive or disgusting by accepted standards of morality and decency
  • synonym: salacious - conveying sexual matters in an indecent way
