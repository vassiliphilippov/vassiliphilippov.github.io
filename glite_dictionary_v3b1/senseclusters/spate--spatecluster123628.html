spate

noun
sudden and forceful outpouring
6.0 6.3
Pronunciation: / speɪt / (AmE), / speɪt / (BrE)
a sudden, strong outpouring or flood of water, especially after heavy rain

Examples

A spate of rain
After weeks of dry weather, a spate of heavy rain caused the river to overflow.

The company experienced a spate of new orders following its advertising campaign.
After the storm, the river was in spate and overflowed its banks.
Heavy rains sent a spate of water rushing through the valley.
Spring often brings a spate caused by melting mountain snow.
