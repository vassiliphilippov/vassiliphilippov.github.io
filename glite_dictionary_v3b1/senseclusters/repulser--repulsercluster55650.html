repulser

noun
one who drives back
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpʌlsər / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌlsə / (BrE)
a person, group, or thing that drives back or defeats an attack or attacker

Examples

Enemy repulser
The city became known as a strong repulser of enemy invasions throughout history.
As the main repulser of the attack, the general was awarded a medal for bravery.
The fortress acted as a powerful repulser against repeated assaults.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defender - a person who protects against attack
