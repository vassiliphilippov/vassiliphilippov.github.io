filaggrin

noun
skin protein in keratohyalin granules
9.9
Pronunciation: / fɪˈlæɡrɪn / (AmE), / fɪˈlæɡrɪn / (BrE)
a protein found in the keratohyalin granules of the skin, important for the structure and function of the epidermis

Filaggrin protein
