resemble

verb
be similar to; look like
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɛmbəl / (AmE), / rɪˈzɛmbəl / (BrE)
to look like or be similar to someone or something

Examples

To resemble a parent
Her painting style resembles that of the Impressionist masters.
Some mushrooms resemble edible varieties but are actually poisonous.
The twins resemble each other so closely that people often confuse them.

Related Terms

  • antonym: differ - to be unlike or dissimilar
  • synonym: imitate - to copy the manner or appearance of
