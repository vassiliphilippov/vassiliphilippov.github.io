white tea

noun
a delicate tea made from lightly oxidized leaves
3.1 3.1
Pronunciation: /waɪt tiː/ /; / /hwaɪt tiː/ (AmE), /waɪt tiː/ /; / /hwaɪt tiː/ (BrE)
