spina bifida

noun
congenital defect of the spinal column
5.8 7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌspaɪnə ˈbɪfɪdə / (AmE), / ˌspaɪnə ˈbɪfɪdə / (BrE)
a congenital defect in which the spinal column is incomplete, causing the spinal cord or its coverings to protrude outside the body
