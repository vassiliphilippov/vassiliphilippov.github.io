meddler

noun
person who interferes annoyingly
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛd.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈmed.lə / (BrE)
a person who interferes in matters that do not concern them, often in an annoying or officious way

Examples

A meddler in affairs
He resented the meddler who was always telling him how to run his business.
No one appreciates a meddler interfering in their personal affairs.
The neighbors warned me that she was a notorious meddler who gossiped about everyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: busybody - a person who is too interested in other people's private affairs
