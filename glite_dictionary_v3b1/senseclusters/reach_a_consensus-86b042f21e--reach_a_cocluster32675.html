reach a consensus

verb
agree among networked nodes (computing)
5.4
Pronunciation: / riːtʃ ə kənˈsɛnsəs / (AmE), / riːtʃ ə kənˈsɛnsəs / (BrE)
(in distributed computing) to have the nodes in a network agree on a single data value or system state, typically by running a consensus algorithm to ensure consistency and fault tolerance.

Examples

To reach a consensus in a network
Before committing the block, the network must reach a consensus on transaction order.
Engineers used Paxos to help the nodes reach a consensus despite several machine failures.
They finally reached a consensus after the leader election completed and logs matched.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agreement - harmony in opinion or state; general agreement.
  • synonym: consensus algorithm - a protocol used by distributed systems (for example Paxos or Raft) to have nodes agree on a single value or state.
