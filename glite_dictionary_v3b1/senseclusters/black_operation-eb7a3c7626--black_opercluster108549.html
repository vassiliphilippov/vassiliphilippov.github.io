black operation

noun
secretive untraceable covert operation
5.3 6.7
Pronunciation: /blæk ɑː.pə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /blæk ɒp.ə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
A secret operation conducted in such a way that the organization responsible cannot be identified, often for political or military purposes.

Examples

Secret black operations
He specialized in planning black operations that would be impossible to link to his government.
Journalists uncovered evidence of a black operation targeting dissidents abroad.
The intelligence agency carried out a black operation to destabilize the regime without leaving any trace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: black ops - abbreviation for black operations, informal/slang usage
  • synonym: covert operation - a military or intelligence operation that is secret or concealed
  • antonym: overt operation - an operation conducted openly, without secrecy
