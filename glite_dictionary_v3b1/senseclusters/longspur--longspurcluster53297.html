longspur

noun
grassland songbird
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔŋspɝ /; / ˈlɔŋspɜr / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋspɜː / (BrE)
a small songbird with a long claw on its hind toe, found in grasslands of North America and Eurasia
