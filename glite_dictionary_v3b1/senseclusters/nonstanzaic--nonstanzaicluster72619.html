nonstanzaic

adjective
without stanzas
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.stænˈzeɪ.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.stænˈzeɪ.ɪk / (BrE)
not arranged in stanzas or not following a stanzaic structure, especially in poetry

Examples

Nonstanzaic poem structure
The poet chose a nonstanzaic structure to give the work a more fluid feeling.
Unlike traditional forms, this nonstanzaic poem flows without clear breaks.
Her latest collection features several nonstanzaic pieces that challenge conventional poetry formats.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prose - written or spoken language in its ordinary form, without metrical structure
  • antonym: stanzaic - arranged in stanzas or following a stanzaic structure
