prepossessed

adjective
mentally absorbed
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːpəˈzɛst / (AmE), / ˌpriːpəˈzɛst / (BrE)
mentally absorbed or preoccupied with a particular thought, feeling, or idea

Examples

Prepossessed with worries
She was so prepossessed by her worries that she barely heard what anyone said.
The scientist remained prepossessed with his research, often forgetting to eat lunch.
He became prepossessed with the idea of moving abroad and talked about it constantly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absorbed - deeply interested or involved in something
  • synonym: distracted - unable to concentrate because of other thoughts
  • synonym: preoccupied - thinking about something so much that you ignore other things
