shadowland

noun
uncertain or mysterious place
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃædoʊˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈʃædəʊˌlænd / (BrE)
a place or state that is mysterious, unclear, or exists between reality and fantasy

Examples

A shadowland of dreams
The novel is set in a shadowland where nothing is quite as it seems.
After the accident, he felt trapped in a shadowland between memory and reality.
Artists often draw inspiration from the shadowland of dreams and imagination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: limbo - an uncertain or intermediate state or place
  • antonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist
