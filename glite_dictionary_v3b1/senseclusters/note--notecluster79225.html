Examples

To note a fact

The chairman noted that attendance had improved significantly this year.

Synonyms

  • remark
    2.1 15/m
    remark is a spoken or written comment that adds opinion, while note is a simple mention or brief observation.
  • point out
    1.8 94/m
    point out draws attention to something by showing or saying it clearly, while note usually means to mention or notice briefly.
  • mention
    1.0 180/m
    mention briefly refers to or names something without detail, while note can mean to point out or record something more clearly.
  • observe
    4.1 19/m
    observe expresses a thoughtful, often formal comment on something, while note is more general and can be casual or brief.
