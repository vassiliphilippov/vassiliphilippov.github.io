aftermarket

noun
replacement parts market
Pronunciation: / ˈæftərˌmɑrkɪt / (AmE), / ˈɑːftəmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
the market for replacement parts, accessories, or services for products after their original sale

Examples

The aftermarket for vehicles
Many car owners buy parts from the aftermarket to customize their vehicles.
The aftermarket for smartphone accessories has grown rapidly in recent years.
Some mechanics recommend using aftermarket parts because they can be less expensive than original ones.

Related Terms

  • antonym: original equipment - parts or accessories supplied by the original manufacturer
  • synonym: secondary market - a market for reselling goods or securities after their initial sale
