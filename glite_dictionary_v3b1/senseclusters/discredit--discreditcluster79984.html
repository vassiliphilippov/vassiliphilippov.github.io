discredit

noun
loss of respect or honor
3.7
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈkrɛdɪt / (AmE), / dɪsˈkrɛdɪt / (BrE)
Loss or lack of respect, honor, or reputation; the state of being regarded with disapproval or suspicion.

Examples

Discredit to the team
His dishonest actions brought discredit to the entire organization.
She worked hard to restore her family's reputation after years of discredit.
The team's repeated losses have put them in a state of discredit within the league.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disrepute - the state of being held in low esteem by the public
  • antonym: honor - high respect or esteem
