ridgetop

adjective
on ridge crest
5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈrɪdʒ.tɑːp/ (AmE), /ˈrɪdʒ.tɒp/ (BrE)
located on or relating to the highest part or crest of a ridge, especially in a landscape or mountain.

Examples

Ridgetop cabin
The cabin offered stunning views from its ridgetop location overlooking the valley below.
Hikers enjoyed a cool breeze as they walked along the ridgetop trail at sunrise.
Wildflowers bloomed in abundance across the ridgetop meadows each spring.

Related Terms

  • antonym: valley - a low area between hills or mountains
