Cheremiss

noun
member of a Finnish ethnic group in eastern Russia
Pronunciation: / ˌtʃɛrəˈmɪs / (AmE), / ˌtʃɛrəˈmɪs / (BrE)
a member of a rural Finnish people living in eastern Russia, also known as Mari
