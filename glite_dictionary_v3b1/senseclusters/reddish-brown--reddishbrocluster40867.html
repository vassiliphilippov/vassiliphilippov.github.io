reddish-brown

adjective
brown tinged with red
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdɪʃ ˈbraʊn / (AmE), / ˈrɛdɪʃ ˈbraʊn / (BrE)
Of a brown color with a tinge or shade of red.

Examples

Reddish-brown hair
Her hair had a beautiful reddish-brown hue that shimmered in the sunlight.
In autumn, the leaves turn a vibrant reddish-brown before falling to the ground.
The old bookshelf was made of reddish-brown mahogany wood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: auburn - a reddish-brown color, especially of hair
  • synonym: chestnut - a dark reddish-brown color
