wild senna

noun
North American medicinal herb
7.4
Pronunciation: / waɪld ˈsɛnə / (AmE), / waɪld ˈsɛnə / (BrE)
A North American perennial herb with medicinal leaves, sometimes classified under the genus Cassia.

Examples

Wild senna plant
Wild senna grows best in sunny areas with well-drained soil.
Many gardeners cultivate wild senna for its bright yellow flowers and medicinal properties.
The dried leaves of wild senna have long been used in traditional medicine.

Related Terms
