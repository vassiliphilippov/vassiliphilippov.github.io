holiday ham

noun
ham served on festive occasions
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːlɪdeɪ hæm / (AmE), / ˈhɒlɪdeɪ ham / (BrE)
a ham prepared and served as part of a meal for a festive occasion, such as Christmas or Easter

Examples

A holiday ham meal
Many families have their own secret recipe for preparing a holiday ham.
The centerpiece of their Easter feast was a glazed holiday ham.
We always order a holiday ham for our Christmas dinner.
