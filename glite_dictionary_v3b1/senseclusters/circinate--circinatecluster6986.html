circinate

adjective
shaped like a ring or spiral
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜːrsɪˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.sɪ.neɪt / (BrE)
Having the shape of a ring or being coiled in a ring or spiral, especially referring to plant organs.

Examples

A circinate leaf
Some tendrils show a circinate pattern as they curl around supports.
The circinate arrangement of the leaves helps protect the delicate bud.
The young fern fronds are circinate, slowly unrolling as they mature.
