possie

noun
group of friends
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑsi / (AmE), / ˈpɒsi / (BrE)
an informal group of friends or associates who regularly spend time together

Examples

A possie of friends
After school, the usual possie gathered at the cafe to plan the weekend trip.
He always hangs out with his possie at the basketball court after work.
Whenever there's a party, her possie is the first to arrive and the last to leave.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gang - a group of people who associate closely, sometimes with negative connotations
  • synonym: posse - a group of friends or associates
