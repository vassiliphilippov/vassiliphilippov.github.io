match

verb
provide funds equal to others
Pronunciation: / mætʃ / (AmE), / matʃ / (BrE)
to provide funds or resources equal to or complementary to those offered by another person or organization

Examples

To match a donation
For every euro you raise, sponsors will match your amount to support the project.
Our employer will match up to 5% of our retirement contributions.
The university agreed to match every dollar donated by alumni.

Related Terms

  • synonym: equal - to be the same in quantity, size, degree, or value
