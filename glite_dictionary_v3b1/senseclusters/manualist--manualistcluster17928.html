manualist

noun
sign language advocate
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmænjuəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈmænjuəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who uses or advocates the use of sign language or hand-based communication, especially in deaf education

Examples

Manualist uses sign language
The manualist presented research on the benefits of sign language in early childhood education.
As a proud manualist, she encouraged her students to communicate using their hands.
Debates between the manualist and oralist approaches have shaped deaf education for decades.
