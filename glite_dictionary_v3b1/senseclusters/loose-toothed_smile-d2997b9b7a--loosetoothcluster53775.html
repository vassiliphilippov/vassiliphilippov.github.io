loose-toothed smile

noun
a smile showing loose teeth
Pronunciation: / luːs tuːθt smaɪl / (AmE), / luːs tuːθt smaɪl / (BrE)
a smile that shows teeth which are not firmly in place, often due to them being loose or about to fall out

Examples

A loose-toothed smile
Children often have a loose-toothed smile around the age of six or seven.
Her loose-toothed smile charmed everyone at the birthday party.
The photograph captured his proud, loose-toothed smile after losing his first tooth.
