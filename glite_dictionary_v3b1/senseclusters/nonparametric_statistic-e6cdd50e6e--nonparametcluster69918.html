nonparametric statistic

noun
statistic not based on distribution parameters
8.6 6.5
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.pær.ə.ˈme.trɪk stə.ˈtɪs.tɪk/ (AmE), /nɒn.pær.ə.ˈme.trɪk stə.ˈtɪs.tɪk/ (BrE)
a statistic calculated without assuming any specific form or parameters for the underlying distribution

Examples

A nonparametric statistic
A nonparametric statistic is often used when the sample size is small and distributional assumptions cannot be made.
Many rank-based tests rely on a nonparametric statistic for analysis.
The researcher chose a nonparametric statistic due to the unknown distribution of the data.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonparametric - not relying on parameter assumptions
  • antonym: parametric statistic - a statistic computed with knowledge of distribution parameters
