pulp

verb
remove the pulp from (a fruit or vegetable)
4.0 4.9
Pronunciation: / pʌlp / (AmE), / pʌlp / (BrE)
to remove the soft, moist part inside a fruit or vegetable, leaving the skin or other parts

Examples

To pulp tomatoes
After boiling the tomatoes, you should pulp them to make a smooth sauce.
It can be tricky to pulp a mango without wasting much of the fruit.
The machine is used to pulp oranges for juice production.

Related Terms

  • synonym: core - remove the central part of a fruit, especially the seeds
