broadly

Examples

Synonyms

    4.2 0.01/m

  • broadly speaking
    3.7 0.16/m
    broadly speaking is a phrase used to state a general truth without details, while broadly means in a wide, extensive manner.
  • widely
    1.0 45/m
    widely describes something spread over many places or by many people, while broadly means with wide scope or generality.
  • extensively
    4.1 9.68/m
    extensively means covering a large area or done thoroughly, while broadly means in a wide or general way across topics.
