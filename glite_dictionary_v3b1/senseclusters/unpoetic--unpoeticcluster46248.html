unpoetic

adjective
not imaginative or beautiful
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnpoʊˈɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnpəʊˈɛtɪk / (BrE)
lacking beauty, imagination, or the qualities typically associated with poetry

Examples

An unpoetic explanation
His unpoetic explanation of the event left the audience unmoved.
The landscape was flat and unpoetic, with no striking features to inspire artists.
She found the technical manual too unpoetic for her creative taste.

Related Terms

  • antonym: poetic - having qualities of poetry; beautiful or imaginative
  • synonym: prosaic - lacking poetic beauty; ordinary or dull
