uncemented

adjective
not joined with cement
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnsɪˈmɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnsɪˈmɛntɪd / (BrE)
not fixed or joined using cement, especially referring to medical implants or construction materials.

Examples

Uncemented hip implant
The surgeon recommended an uncemented knee replacement for the younger patient.
Modern construction sometimes uses uncemented bricks to allow for easier repairs.
The uncemented implant allows bone to grow naturally around the device.
