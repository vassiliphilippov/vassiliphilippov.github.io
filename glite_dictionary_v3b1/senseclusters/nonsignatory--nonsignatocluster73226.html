nonsignatory

adjective
not having signed
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈsɪɡnəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈsɪɡnətəri / (BrE)
not having signed a particular agreement, treaty, or document; not a signatory to something

Examples

Nonsignatory countries
The agreement was discussed by both nonsignatory and signatory nations at the conference.
As a nonsignatory state, it was not bound by the treaty's requirements.
Several nonsignatory organizations expressed interest in joining the pact in the future.

Related Terms

  • antonym: signatory - having signed an agreement or treaty
