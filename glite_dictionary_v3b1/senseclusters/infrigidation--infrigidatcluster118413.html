infrigidation

noun
process of cooling or freezing for preservation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnfrɪdʒɪˈdeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪnfrɪdʒɪˈdeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of cooling or freezing something, especially food, in order to preserve it
