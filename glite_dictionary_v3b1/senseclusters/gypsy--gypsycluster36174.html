gypsy

noun
person with a nomadic, unconventional lifestyle
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪp.si / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪp.si / (BrE)
(sometimes offensive) A person who leads an unconventional, nomadic way of life, reminiscent of the traditional lifestyle of the Romani people.

Examples

A gypsy lifestyle
He admires the gypsy lifestyle, with its freedom and adventure.
She's always moving from city to city and living out of a suitcase, a true gypsy at heart.
Some artists embrace a gypsy existence, never staying in one place for long.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nomad - a member of a community that moves from place to place
  • antonym: settler - a person who settles in a new place, usually with a stable lifestyle
