DPhil

noun
British doctorate degree (esp. Oxford)
7.3 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / diÀê f…™l / (AmE), / diÀê f…™l / (BrE)
a doctorate degree, especially as awarded by some British universities such as Oxford, equivalent to a PhD

Examples

A DPhil degree
His DPhil thesis focused on global economic policies.
Many international scholars pursue a DPhil at British universities.
She was awarded a DPhil in history from Oxford University.

Synonyms

  • Doctor of Philosophy
    5.4 0.15/m
