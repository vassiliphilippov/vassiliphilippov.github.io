box

noun
rectangular outline in diagrams or forms
1.0 51/m
Pronunciation: / bÉ‘ks / (AmE), / bÉ’ks / (BrE)
a rectangular outline or drawing, typically used to enclose or highlight information in diagrams, forms, or on pages

Examples

A box on a form
Each decision point in the flowchart is shown as a box connected by arrows.
Please write your name inside the box at the top of the form.
She drew a box around the most important part of the diagram.
