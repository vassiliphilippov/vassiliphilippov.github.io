splicer

noun
genetically altered person (fiction)
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈsplaɪ.sər/ (AmE), /ˈsplaɪ.sə/ (BrE)
(in fiction and gaming) a person or creature who has been genetically altered or mutated by splicing, often portrayed as hostile or degraded.
