intrarenal

adjective
within the kidney
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈriːnəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈriːnəl / (BrE)
located or occurring within the kidney

Examples

Intrarenal mass
The ultrasound revealed an intrarenal mass that required further investigation.
Doctors identified an intrarenal blockage as the cause of the patient's symptoms.
Treatment focused on addressing the intrarenal infection to restore kidney function.

Related Terms
