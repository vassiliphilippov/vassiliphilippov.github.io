mannerable

adjective
easily controlled
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmænərəbəl / (AmE), / ˈmænərəbəl / (BrE)
easily taught, led, or controlled; docile and tractable

Examples

Mannerable puppy
The puppy was so mannerable that it quickly learned to sit and stay.
Teachers appreciate students who are mannerable and follow instructions without complaint.
Despite his young age, the colt was remarkably mannerable during training sessions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: docile - willing to be taught or led; easily managed
  • antonym: stubborn - unwilling to change or be controlled
  • synonym: tractable - easy to control or influence
