vig

noun
lively energy
Pronunciation: / vɪɡ / (AmE), / vɪɡ / (BrE)
informal term for lively energy or enthusiasm

Examples

Full of vig
She tackled the project with a lot of vig.
His vig was obvious as he led the team through the busy day.
Even after a long week, he still had enough vig to go hiking on Saturday.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apathy - lack of interest or energy
  • synonym: enthusiasm - strong excitement or interest
  • synonym: vigor - physical strength and good health; energy
