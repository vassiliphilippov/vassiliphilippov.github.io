formaldehyde

noun
a toxic colorless gas used in industry
8.1
Pronunciation: / fɔrˈmældəˌhaɪd / (AmE), / fɔːˈmældɪhaɪd / (BrE)
a colorless, poisonous gas with a strong smell, used in chemical manufacturing and as a preservative

Examples

Toxic formaldehyde gas
Exposure to formaldehyde may cause irritation of the eyes, nose, and throat.
Many household products contain small amounts of formaldehyde.
The laboratory uses formaldehyde to preserve biological specimens.
