thermal resistor

noun
electronic component with temperature-dependent resistance
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈθɜr.məl rɪˈzɪs.tər / (AmE), / ˈθɜː.məl rɪˈzɪs.tə / (BrE)
an electronic component whose resistance changes with temperature, used to compensate for temperature variations in circuits

Examples

A thermal resistor device
Engineers selected a thermal resistor to protect the circuit from overheating.
The thermal resistor in the sensor compensates for temperature changes that might affect the readings.
When the device heated up, its thermal resistor altered the current flow as expected.

Related Terms

  • synonym: resistor - an electrical component that resists the flow of current
  • synonym: thermistor - a resistor whose resistance varies significantly with temperature
