proportionate

adjective
in proper or appropriate proportion

Synonyms

  • commensurate
    7.5 2.12/m
    commensurate means matching in size or degree and is used in formal contexts, while proportionate implies a suitable, correct relation.
  • proportional
    4.7 5.76/m
    proportional describes a steady numerical or ratio link between amounts, while proportionate focuses on being suitably sized or appropriate.
