parthenogeny

noun
reproduction without fertilization
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːrθəˈnɑːdʒəni /; / ˌpɑrθəˈnɑdʒəni / (AmE), / ˌpɑːθəˈnɒdʒəni / (BrE)
the process of reproduction in which an egg develops into a new individual without being fertilized, especially in some plants and animals

Examples
