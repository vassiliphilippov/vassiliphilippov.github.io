copout

noun
avoidance of facing a difficulty
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑpˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈkɒpˌaʊt / (BrE)
an excuse or avoidance used to evade facing a difficulty or responsibility directly

Examples

A copout excuse
Calling in sick on the day of the exam was just a copout.
Choosing not to vote is often seen as a copout in a democracy.
His explanation sounded like a copout to avoid taking responsibility.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accountability - the state of being responsible or answerable; opposite of evasion
  • synonym: excuse - a reason or explanation to justify a failure or avoid responsibility
  • synonym: pretext - a false reason given to hide the real purpose
