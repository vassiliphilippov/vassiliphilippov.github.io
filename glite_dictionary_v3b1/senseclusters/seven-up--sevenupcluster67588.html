seven-up

noun
card game, variation of all fours
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛvənˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈsɛvənˌʌp / (BrE)
A card game, a variation of all fours, in which the game is won by reaching a total of seven points.

Examples

Play seven-up game
Grandpa taught us how to play seven-up when we were kids.
The rules of seven-up are similar to all fours but the winner is the first to reach seven points.
We played seven-up with our friends until late in the evening.
