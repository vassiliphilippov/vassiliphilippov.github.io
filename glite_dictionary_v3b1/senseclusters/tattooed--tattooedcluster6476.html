tattooed

adjective
with inked designs
Pronunciation: / təˈtuːd / (AmE), / təˈtuːd / (BrE)

Examples

Tattooed arms
She admired the tattooed patterns that covered his back and shoulders.
Many athletes are tattooed with symbols that have personal meaning.
The tattooed artist explained the story behind each of his designs.
