mantled ground squirrel

noun
a North American ground squirrel species
Pronunciation: / ˈmæntəld ɡraʊnd ˈskwɝl / (AmE), / ˈmæntld ɡraʊnd ˈskwɪrəl / (BrE)
a common black-striped, reddish-brown ground squirrel of western North America that resembles a large chipmunk

Examples

We spotted a mantled ground squirrel darting across the hiking trail in the Rockies.
