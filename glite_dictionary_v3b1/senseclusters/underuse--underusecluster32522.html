underuse

noun
insufficient use
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈjuːs / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈjuːs / (BrE)
the act of using something less than it could or should be used

Examples

Library underuse
The underuse of the library means many resources go unnoticed.
Doctors are concerned about the underuse of important vaccines in some regions.
Due to underuse, the community center may lose its funding next year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neglect - the act of not giving enough attention or care
  • antonym: overuse - the act of using something too much
