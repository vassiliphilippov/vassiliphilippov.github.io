backlash

noun
strong adverse reaction to events
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌlæʃ / (AmE), / ˈbæk.læʃ /; / ˈbak.laʃ / (BrE)
a strong and widespread negative reaction, especially to a social or political event or change

Examples

Public backlash
She faced an online backlash after making the controversial statement.
The government's new policy sparked a significant backlash among voters across the country.
There was a public backlash against the company's decision to close its local branches.
Her comment sparked immediate backlash on social media.
The company faced intense backlash after announcing changes to its privacy policy.
There was a public backlash against the new tax increases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reaction - a response to an event or situation
  • antonym: support - approval or backing for an idea or person
