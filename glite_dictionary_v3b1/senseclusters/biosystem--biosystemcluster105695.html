biosystem

noun
system of living things
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪoʊˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈbaɪəʊˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
a system formed by living organisms and their interactions with each other and the environment

Examples

Rainforest biosystem
Researchers analyzed the biosystem of the rainforest to understand its complex food web.
A healthy biosystem depends on the balance between plants, animals, and microorganisms.
Changes in climate can disrupt the biosystem of a lake, affecting all its inhabitants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ecosystem - a community of living organisms and their physical environment
  • degree lower: organism - an individual living thing
