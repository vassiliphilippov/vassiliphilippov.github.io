bunji-bunji

noun
Australian tree yielding poisonous bark
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌbʌn.dʒiˈbʌn.dʒi / (AmE), / ˌbʌn.dʒiˈbʌn.dʒi / (BrE)
An Australian timber tree, especially Alphitonia excelsa, whose bark is used to produce a poison.
