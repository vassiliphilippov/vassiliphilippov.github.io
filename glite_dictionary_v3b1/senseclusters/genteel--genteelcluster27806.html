genteel

adjective
refined and polite in manners or taste
7.7 1.74/m
Pronunciation: / d í…õnÀàtil / (AmE), / d í…õnÀàtiÀêl / (BrE)
polite, refined, or respectable, often in an affected or ostentatious way

Examples

A genteel manner
He spoke with a genteel accent that impressed his colleagues.
Her genteel appearance made her stand out at the informal gathering.
The house was decorated in a genteel style, with elegant furnishings and delicate china.
