universal joint

noun
mechanical coupling for moving shafts
Pronunciation: / juˈnɪvɚsəl dʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / juːˈnɪvəsəl dʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
a mechanical coupling that connects two rotating shafts, allowing movement in all directions and enabling transmission of power when the shafts are not in line

Examples

A universal joint coupling
A universal joint allows the steering shaft to rotate even when not perfectly aligned.
Engineers selected a universal joint to accommodate the changing angles between the connected shafts.
The universal joint in my car needed replacement after years of wear and tear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coupling - a device for connecting two shafts together at their ends
