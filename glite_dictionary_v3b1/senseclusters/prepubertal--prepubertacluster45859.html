prepubertal

adjective
at the stage just before puberty
2.8 6.3
Pronunciation: / priˈpjuːbərtəl / (AmE), / priːˈpjuːbətəl / (BrE)
(especially of humans) at or relating to the stage immediately before puberty, typically marked by rapid physical growth.

Examples

Prepubertal children
Doctors monitor the health of prepubertal children for signs of early development.
Some medications are not recommended for prepubertal patients due to their age and stage of growth.
The prepubertal phase is often associated with a notable increase in height.
