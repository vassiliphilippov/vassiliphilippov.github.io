mañana

noun
the morning
Pronunciation: / maˈnjɑːnə / (AmE), / maˈnjɑːnə / (BrE)
the morning; the period of the day from dawn until noon, used as a Spanish loanword in English

Examples

Early mañana
She woke early this mañana to catch the first commuter train into the city.
The café opens every mañana and serves fresh pastries until noon.
We planned to meet in the mañana before the city became too busy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: evening - the later part of the day, from late afternoon until night
  • synonym: morning - the early part of the day, from sunrise until noon
