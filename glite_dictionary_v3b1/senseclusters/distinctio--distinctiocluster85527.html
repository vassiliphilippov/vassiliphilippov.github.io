distinctio

noun
clarifying word meanings
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstɪŋkʃioʊ / (AmE), / dɪˈstɪŋkʃiəʊ / (BrE)
a rhetorical device in which a speaker or writer explains the different meanings of a word or phrase to avoid misunderstanding

Examples

Rhetorical distinctio
The professor used a distinctio to clarify the different senses of the word 'justice' in her lecture.
Writers often employ distinctio to ensure their audience understands exactly what they mean.
To prevent confusion, the lawyer made a distinctio between legal and moral responsibility.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ambiguity - uncertainty or inexactness of meaning
  • synonym: definition - an explanation of the meaning of a word or phrase
