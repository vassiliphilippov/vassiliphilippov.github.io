banca

noun
long wooden bench
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑŋkə / (AmE), / ˈbaŋkə / (BrE)
a long bench or seat, especially one made of wood, used for sitting outdoors or in public places

Examples

A wooden banca
She sat on the wooden banca beneath the trees while waiting for the market to open.
The old plaza was lined with stone banca for people to rest and enjoy the view.
Children gathered on the banca after school to share stories and snacks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bench - a long seat for several people
