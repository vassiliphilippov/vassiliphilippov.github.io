elocutionist

noun
trained public speaker
Pronunciation: / ˌɛləˈkjuːʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌɛləˈkjuːʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who is trained in the art of public speaking, especially in voice production and delivery

Examples

An elocutionist judges speeches
As an elocutionist, he was often invited to judge local speaking competitions.
She became an accomplished elocutionist, teaching young students how to project their voices.
The famous elocutionist captivated the audience with his powerful speech and expressive gestures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: speaker - a person who speaks, especially publicly
