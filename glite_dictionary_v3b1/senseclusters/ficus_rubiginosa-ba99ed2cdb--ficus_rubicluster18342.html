Ficus rubiginosa

noun
Australian ornamental fig tree
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪkəs ˌruːbɪdʒəˈnoʊsə / (AmE), / ˈfaɪkəs ˌruːbɪdʒɪˈnəʊsə / (BrE)
An Australian fig tree often cultivated as an ornamental plant, recognized for its resemblance to the banyan and sometimes used for brushwood.

Examples

The Ficus rubiginosa tree
Botanists compared the Ficus rubiginosa to the closely related banyan tree.
In its native Australia, the Ficus rubiginosa can grow quite large and provide excellent shade.
The gardeners planted a Ficus rubiginosa near the entrance as a striking centerpiece.

Related Terms

  • synonym: banyan - A tree with aerial prop roots, similar in appearance to Ficus rubiginosa
