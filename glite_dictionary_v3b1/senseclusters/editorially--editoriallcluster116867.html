editorially

adverb
in relation to an editorial article
5.0 5.5
Pronunciation: /e.dɪ.ˈtɔːr.ɪ.ə.liː/ /; / /ə.dɪ.ˈtɔːr.ɪ.ə.liː/ (AmE), /e.dɪ.ˈtɔːr.ɪ.ə.liː/ /; / /ə.dɪ.ˈtɔːr.ɪ.ə.liː/ (BrE)
