editorially

adverb
in relation to an editorial article
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɛdəˈtɔːrəli / (AmE), / ˌɛdɪˈtɔːrəli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to or by means of an editorial (an article expressing opinions or viewpoints, especially in a newspaper or magazine)

Examples

Editorially support a policy
She spoke editorially, presenting the magazine's official viewpoint.
The issue was addressed editorially in several leading journals.
The newspaper commented editorially on the government's new policy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: editorial - an article in a newspaper or magazine expressing the opinion of the editor or publisher
