waverer

noun
someone who hesitates or is indecisive
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪvərər / (AmE), / ˈweɪvərə / (BrE)
a person who is indecisive or hesitant, especially due to fear or lack of confidence

Examples

A waverer hesitates
As a waverer, he found it difficult to choose a career path.
Her friends described her as a waverer when it came to making big decisions.
There was no room for a waverer in times of crisis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decider - someone who decides confidently
  • synonym: doubter - someone who doubts or is uncertain
