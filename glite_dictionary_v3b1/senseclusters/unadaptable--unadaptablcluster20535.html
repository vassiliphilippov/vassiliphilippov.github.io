unadaptable

adjective
not able to adapt
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.əˈdæp.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈdæp.tə.bəl / (BrE)
not able or willing to adjust to new conditions or changes; not adaptable.

Examples

An unadaptable person
He struggled in his new job because he was too unadaptable to accept different ways of working.
Her unadaptable approach to teaching made it hard to connect with diverse students.
Some animals are unadaptable and cannot survive drastic environmental changes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adaptable - able to adjust to new conditions
