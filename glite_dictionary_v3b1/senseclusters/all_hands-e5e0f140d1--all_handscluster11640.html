all hands

noun
everyone available; all personnel
3.5
Pronunciation: / ɔl hændz / (AmE), / ɔːl hændz / (BrE)
everyone available to help, especially in a work or emergency situation

Examples

All hands on deck
During the storm, the manager called for all hands to help secure the warehouse.
The fire chief requested all hands respond to the emergency downtown.
With the deadline approaching, it was all hands in the office to finish the project.
