uncopyrighted

adjective
not under copyright
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈkɑːpiraɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈkɒpiraɪtɪd / (BrE)
not protected by copyright law and free from copyright restrictions

Examples

Uncopyrighted material
The article is uncopyrighted, so you can use it in your project without permission.
Many old books are now uncopyrighted and available for free online.
She prefers to share her photos as uncopyrighted works for anyone to enjoy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: copyrighted - protected by copyright law
  • synonym: public domain - not owned or controlled by anyone and free for public use
