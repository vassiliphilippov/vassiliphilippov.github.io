clairaudient

noun
person hearing spirits
Pronunciation: / ˌklɛrˈɔdiənt / (AmE), / ˌkleəˈɔːdiənt / (BrE)
a person who claims to be able to hear sounds or voices beyond normal human perception, especially from spirits or supernatural sources

Examples

A clairaudient person
The clairaudient claimed to hear messages from the spirit world during the séance.
As a practicing clairaudient, she often reported hearing voices no one else could detect.
Many people visited the clairaudient hoping to receive guidance from departed loved ones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clairvoyant - a person who claims to see things beyond normal perception
  • synonym: medium - a person who claims to communicate with spirits
  • antonym: skeptic - a person who doubts or questions supernatural claims
