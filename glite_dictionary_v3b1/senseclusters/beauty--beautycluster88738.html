beauty

noun
pleasing qualities to the senses
Pronunciation: / ˈbjuːti / (AmE), / ˈbjuːti / (BrE)
A combination of qualities, such as shape, color, or form, that pleases the aesthetic senses, especially the sight.

Examples

Natural beauty
Despite its simplicity, there is a certain beauty in this design.
She admired the beauty of the sculpture in the museum.
The beauty of the sunset took everyone’s breath away.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ugliness - the quality of being unpleasant or offensive to look at
