unevangelized

adjective
not converted to religion
Pronunciation: / ˌʌniˈvæn.dʒəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌniˈvæn.dʒəˌlaɪzd / (BrE)
not having been introduced to or converted by a particular religion, especially Christianity

Examples

Unevangelized communities
The organization focused its efforts on unevangelized communities in remote areas.
Many unevangelized tribes had never encountered missionaries before the 20th century.
He wrote a book about the challenges of reaching unevangelized populations around the world.

Related Terms

  • antonym: evangelized - having been introduced to or converted by a religion, especially Christianity
  • synonym: unconverted - not having adopted a particular religion or belief
