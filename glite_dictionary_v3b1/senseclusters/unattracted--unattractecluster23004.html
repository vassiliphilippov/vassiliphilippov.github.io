unattracted

adjective
not drawn by force
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.əˈtræktɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈtræktɪd / (BrE)
not pulled toward or affected by a force of attraction, such as magnetism or gravity

Examples

Unattracted by magnets
The wooden block remained unattracted even when placed next to the strong magnet.
Because the material is unattracted by static electricity, it is safe to use in electronics.
Unlike the metal pins, the plastic buttons were completely unattracted to the magnetic field.

Related Terms

  • antonym: attracted - pulled toward by a force
  • antonym: repelled - pushed away by a force
