albarello

noun
apothecary jar
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌælbəˈrɛloʊ / (AmE), / ˌalbaˈrɛləʊ / (BrE)
a tall, usually ceramic jar with a waisted shape, traditionally used by apothecaries to store ointments or dry drugs

Examples

Collectors often seek out a well-preserved albarello for its historical and artistic value.
