report

noun
written account of findings or results
2.2
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpɔrt /; / ɹɪˈpɔɹt / (AmE), / rɪˈpɔːt / (BrE)
a written or spoken account that gives information about something, such as a document, essay, or news story

The evening news included a detailed report on the election results.
