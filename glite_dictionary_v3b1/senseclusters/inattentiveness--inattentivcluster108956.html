inattentiveness

noun
lack of consideration or attention to others
5.4 7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.əˈtɛn.tɪv.nəs / (AmE), / ˌɪn.əˈtɛn.tɪv.nɪs /; / ˌɪn.əˈtɛn.tɪv.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of not paying attention or being careless, especially when it causes problems or shows lack of consideration for others

Examples

His inattentiveness during meetings
His inattentiveness during meetings often led to missed opportunities and misunderstandings.
She apologized for her inattentiveness to her colleague's concerns.
The child's inattentiveness caused him to overlook his friend's feelings.
Driving requires focus; even a moment of inattentiveness can be dangerous.
Her inattentiveness during class led to several missed assignments.
The nurse was dismissed due to repeated inattentiveness toward patient needs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: attentiveness - the quality of being attentive or paying attention
  • synonym: carelessness - lack of care or attention to detail
  • synonym: consideration - careful thought, typically over the needs or feelings of others
  • antonym: thoughtfulness - the quality of being considerate and attentive to others
