buhl

noun
inlaid furniture decoration of tortoiseshell and metal
Pronunciation: / bul / (AmE), / bjuːl / (BrE)
A form of inlaid decoration on furniture, using tortoiseshell and yellow or white metals arranged in scroll designs.

Examples

Buhl furniture decoration
Many collectors prize cabinets decorated with fine examples of buhl work.
Restorers carefully matched the original buhl motifs during the painstaking furniture repair.
