cheekiness

noun
rude or impertinent behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːkinəs / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːkinɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being rude or disrespectful in a playful or impertinent way

Examples

His cheekiness at school
His cheekiness often got him into trouble at school.
She admired her friend's cheekiness when speaking to authority figures.
There was a certain cheekiness in his grin that made everyone laugh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impudence - rude or disrespectful behavior
  • antonym: respectfulness - showing respect
