adjective

Examples

Saintly kindness

The nurse cared for her patients with a saintly kindness.

Synonyms

    7.1 0.02/m
    saintlike shows patient, gentle behavior in daily life, while saintly often has a stronger religious or moral sense.
  • virtuous
    4.3 4.01/m
    virtuous means having high moral standards and good character, while saintly suggests saint-like kindness and religious goodness.
  • sainted
    7.5 0.12/m
    sainted describes someone officially honored as a saint after death, while saintly describes a person's saint-like goodness in life.
