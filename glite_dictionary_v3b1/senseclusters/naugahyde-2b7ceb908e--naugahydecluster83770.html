Naugahyde

adjective
synthetic leather material
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔːɡəˌhaɪd / (AmE), / ˈnɔːɡəˌhaɪd / (BrE)
made of or covered with Naugahyde, a type of synthetic leather that resembles real leather

Examples

Naugahyde chair cover
The old sofa was covered in Naugahyde, which made it easy to clean.
He bought a jacket with a Naugahyde finish instead of real leather.
Many diners in the 1950s featured Naugahyde booths for a modern look.

Related Terms

  • antonym: leather - material made from animal skin, used for clothing and furniture
  • synonym: vinyl - a synthetic plastic material used as a leather substitute
