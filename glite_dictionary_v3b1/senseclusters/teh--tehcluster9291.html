teh

noun
Malay tea drink
Pronunciation: / teɪ / (AmE), / teɪ / (BrE)
a Malay and Indonesian word for tea, used in English especially in the names of Southeast Asian tea drinks

Examples

A cup of teh
She ordered a glass of teh tarik at the local café after lunch.
In Malaysia, teh ais is a popular way to enjoy tea with ice and milk.
The street vendor served hot teh with sweet condensed milk to his customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tea - a hot drink made by infusing dried leaves in water
