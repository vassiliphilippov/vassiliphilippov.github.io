relations

noun
dealings or connections between people or groups
Pronunciation: / rɪˈleɪʃənz / (AmE), / rɪˈleɪʃənz / (BrE)
the way in which two or more people, groups, or countries behave toward and deal with each other

Examples

Business relations
Business relations with our partners in Asia are currently under review.
Diplomatic relations between the two countries have improved over the last decade.
She always tried to maintain good relations with her neighbors for a peaceful life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: relationship - the way in which two people, groups, or countries are connected
