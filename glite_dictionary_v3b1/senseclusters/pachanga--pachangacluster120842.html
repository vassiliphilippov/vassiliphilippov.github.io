pachanga

noun
lively party
9.9
Pronunciation: / pəˈtʃɑŋɡə / (AmE), / pəˈtʃæŋɡə / (BrE)
an informal, lively party or celebration, especially one with dancing and music

Examples

A pachanga party
We had a great time at the pachanga last night, dancing until the early morning.
Every summer, the neighborhood hosts a big pachanga with music and delicious food.
They organized a surprise pachanga for her birthday, inviting all her friends and family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fiesta - a festive celebration or party, especially in Spanish-speaking cultures
  • synonym: gathering - a meeting or assembly of people for a social purpose
