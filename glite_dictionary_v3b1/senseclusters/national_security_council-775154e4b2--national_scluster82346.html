National Security Council

proper noun
governmental advisory committee on security and foreign affairs
4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈnæʃ.ə.nəl sɪ.ˈkjʊr.ɪ.tiː ˈkaʊn.səl/ /; / /ˈnæʃ.nəl sə.ˈkjʊr.ɪ.tiː ˈkaʊn.səl/ (AmE), /ˈnæʃ.ə.nəl sɪ.ˈkjʊə.rɪ.tiː ˈkaʊn.səl/ /; / /ˈnæʃ.nəl sɪ.ˈkjʊə.rɪ.tiː ˈkaʊn.səl/ (BrE)
