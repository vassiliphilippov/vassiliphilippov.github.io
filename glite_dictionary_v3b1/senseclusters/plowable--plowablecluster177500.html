plowable

adjective
suitable for plowing
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈplaʊəbəl / (AmE), / ˈplaʊəbəl / (BrE)
able to be plowed or suitable for plowing, especially referring to land or soil

Examples

Plowable land
After the snow melted, the farmer found the field plowable and ready for planting.
Heavy rains made the soil too wet to be plowable for several days.
Only the southern section of the land was plowable due to its softer texture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arable - suitable for growing crops
  • antonym: unplowable - not able to be plowed
