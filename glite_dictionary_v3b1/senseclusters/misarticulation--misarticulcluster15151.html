misarticulation

noun
abnormal joint alignment
9.3
Pronunciation: /mɪs.ɑːr.tɪ.ˈkjuː.leɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /mɪs.ɑː.tɪ.ˈkjuː.leɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
an abnormal connection, alignment, or contact between bones at a joint, such as a dislocation

Examples

Joint misarticulation
The X-ray revealed a small misarticulation in the wrist, explaining her ongoing pain.
After the accident, the doctor diagnosed a misarticulation of the shoulder joint.
Physical therapy was recommended to correct the misarticulation and restore normal movement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alignment - the correct positioning of bones or joints
  • synonym: dislocation - an injury where a bone is forced out of its normal position at a joint
