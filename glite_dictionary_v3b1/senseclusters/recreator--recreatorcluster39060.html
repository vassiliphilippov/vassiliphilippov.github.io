recreator

noun
person who recreates
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌriːkriˈeɪtər / (AmE), / ˌriːkriˈeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who creates or produces something again, especially by imitating or restoring the original

Examples

Recreator of old art
The recreator of the ancient mural spent years studying historical techniques.
As a skilled recreator, she rebuilt the model ship exactly as it appeared centuries ago.
The film's recreator paid close attention to every detail from the original version.

Related Terms

  • synonym: creator - a person who brings something into existence
  • antonym: destroyer - a person who damages or ruins something
  • synonym: restorer - a person who repairs or brings something back to its original condition
