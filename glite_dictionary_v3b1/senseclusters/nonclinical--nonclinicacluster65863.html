nonclinical

adjective
not for patient care
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈklɪnɪkəl /; / nɑnˈklɪnɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈklɪnɪkəl /; / nɒnˈklɪnɪkəl / (BrE)
not involving direct medical treatment or patient care; used for research, administration, or demonstration purposes
