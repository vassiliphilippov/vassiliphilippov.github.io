bhava

noun
mental state in spirituality
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːvə / (AmE), / ˈbɑːvə / (BrE)
a state of mind, feeling, or emotion, especially in Indian philosophy or spirituality

Examples

Express bhava in dance
The dancer expressed deep bhava through her graceful movements.
Meditation helps cultivate a positive bhava in daily life.
In classical Indian music, performers aim to convey the right bhava to the audience.
