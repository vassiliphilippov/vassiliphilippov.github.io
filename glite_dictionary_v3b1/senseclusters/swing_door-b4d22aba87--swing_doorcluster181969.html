swing door

noun
door opening both ways on hinges
Pronunciation: / swɪŋ dɔr / (AmE), / swɪŋ dɔː / (BrE)
A door that is mounted on a double hinge, allowing it to open in either direction.

Examples

A swing door
Hospitals often use swing doors because they allow easier movement in busy corridors.
Push gently on the swing door to enter the café—no need to pull.
The kitchen was separated from the dining room by a swing door that opened in both directions.
