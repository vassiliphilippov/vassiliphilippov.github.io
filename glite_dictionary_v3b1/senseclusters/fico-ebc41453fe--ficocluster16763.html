FICO

noun
credit score brand
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪkəʊ / (BrE)
a brand of credit score used by lenders to assess a person's creditworthiness

Examples

FICO score
A high FICO score can help you qualify for better loan rates.
Lenders often check your FICO before approving a credit card application.
Improving your FICO may take time, but it can save you money on interest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: VantageScore - an alternative brand of credit score
  • synonym: credit score - a number representing a person's creditworthiness
