shuttle bus

noun
bus providing regular transport between two points
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌt̬l bʌs / (AmE), / ˈʃʌtl bʌs / (BrE)
A bus that travels regularly between two predetermined points, especially for transporting passengers to and from airports, hotels, or other locations.

Examples

A shuttle bus service
After the conference, a shuttle bus will transport delegates back to their hotels.
The hotel offers a free shuttle bus to and from the airport every hour.
We took the shuttle bus from the parking lot to the main entrance of the stadium.

  • synonym: shuttle - vehicle making regular trips between two points
