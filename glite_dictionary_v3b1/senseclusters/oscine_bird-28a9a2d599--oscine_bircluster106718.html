oscine bird

noun
a passerine songbird with a special vocal organ
Pronunciation: / ˈɑsɪn bɜrd / (AmE), / ˈɒsɪn bɜːd / (BrE)
A passerine bird with a highly developed vocal organ, allowing complex songs; a member of the suborder Oscines, known as songbirds.

Examples

A oscine bird sings
An oscine bird can produce a wider range of sounds than most other avian species.
Scientists study the complex vocalizations of oscine birds to understand animal communication.
The melodious tones of the oscine bird filled the forest at dawn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: songbird - a bird known for singing, especially those in the suborder Oscines
