chittimwood

noun
tree yielding cascara sagrada
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪtəmˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪtɪmwʊd / (BrE)
A shrubby tree native to the Pacific coast of the United States, known for yielding cascara sagrada, a medicinal bark.

Examples

Pacific chittimwood tree
Several chittimwood trees stood along the edge of the Pacific coastal forest.
The bark of the chittimwood is harvested to produce cascara sagrada, a laxative.
Traditional healers have long valued the chittimwood for its medicinal properties.
