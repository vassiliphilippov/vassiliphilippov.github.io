nonaquatic

adjective
not related to water
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnəˈkwɑtɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnəˈkwɒtɪk / (BrE)
not living in, on, or related to water; terrestrial or land-based

Examples

A nonaquatic animal
Most mammals are nonaquatic and spend their entire lives on land.
The nonaquatic plants in the garden require regular watering during dry seasons.
Unlike fish, nonaquatic animals cannot survive for long periods in water.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aquatic - living in or related to water
  • synonym: terrestrial - living or growing on land
