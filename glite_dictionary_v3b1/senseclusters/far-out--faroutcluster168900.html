far-out

adjective
strikingly unconventional; avant-garde
8.8 0.74/m
Pronunciation: / f…ërÀàa ät / (AmE), / f…ëÀêÀàa ät / (BrE)
Strikingly or unusually unconventional or avant-garde, especially in style, ideas, or appearance.

Examples

A far-out idea
Her far-out ideas about art made her stand out from other students.
The movie's far-out plot twists left audiences amazed and confused.
Their fashion was so far-out that it became the trend of the season.
