mardy

noun
sulky person
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːdi / (BrE)
a person who is sulky, sullen, or bad-tempered, especially in a childish or petulant way

Examples

He is a mardy
He acted like a real mardy when he did not get his way.
No one wanted to sit next to the mardy at lunch because of his constant complaining.
She was being a mardy after losing the game, refusing to talk to anyone.
