airmobile

noun
helicopter-deployed unit
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrmoʊˌbiːl / (AmE), / ˈeəˌməʊbaɪl / (BrE)
a military unit or soldier trained to be transported and deployed by aircraft, especially helicopters

Examples

An airmobile unit
The airmobile was quickly deployed to the remote area by helicopter.
After landing, the airmobile secured the perimeter before the main force arrived.
Training for the airmobile includes rapid deployment and helicopter insertion techniques.

Related Terms

  • antonym: infantry - soldiers who fight on foot
  • synonym: paratrooper - a soldier trained to parachute from aircraft into combat
