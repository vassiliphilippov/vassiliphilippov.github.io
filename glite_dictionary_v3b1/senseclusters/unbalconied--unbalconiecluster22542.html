unbalconied

adjective
lacking balconies (building)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbæl.kə.nid / (AmE), / ʌnˈbælk.ə.nid / (BrE)
(of a building) not having balconies.

Examples

An unbalconied building
An unbalconied hotel room may not suit guests who enjoy sitting outside.
The old, unbalconied apartment block faced the busy street.
They preferred newer units because the unbalconied houses felt too enclosed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: balconied - having balconies
