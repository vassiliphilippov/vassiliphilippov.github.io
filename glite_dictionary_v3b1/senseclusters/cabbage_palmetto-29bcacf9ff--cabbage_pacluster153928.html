cabbage palmetto

noun
a fan-leaved palm of the southern U.S.
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkæb.ɪdʒ pælˈmɛt.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈkab.ɪdʒ pælˈmɛt.əʊ / (BrE)
A low-growing palm tree of the coastal southern United States, with fan-shaped leaves and edible leaf buds.

Examples

The cabbage palmetto tree
A row of cabbage palmettos lines the streets in parts of South Carolina.
Early settlers sometimes ate the heart of the cabbage palmetto as a delicacy.
The coastline is dotted with cabbage palmetto trees, giving the landscape a tropical feel.
