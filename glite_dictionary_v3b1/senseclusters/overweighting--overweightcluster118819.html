overweighting

noun
giving extra importance
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈweɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈweɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of giving something more importance, value, or weight than others in a calculation or decision

Examples

Overweighting tech stocks
The analyst warned against overweighting technology stocks in the portfolio.
By overweighting certain factors, the results of the study became less reliable.
Investors should avoid overweighting one sector to reduce risk in their portfolios.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emphasis - special importance or attention given to something
  • antonym: underweighting - the act of giving something less importance or value than others
