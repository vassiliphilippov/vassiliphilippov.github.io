deity

noun
a god or goddess; divine being
7.4 8.41/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàdiÀê.…™.ti / (AmE), / Ààde…™.…™.ti / (BrE)
a god or goddess; a supernatural being worshipped as having power over some part of the world or as the personification of a force

Examples

A deity of nature
In their mythology, every aspect of nature had its own deity watching over it.
Many ancient cultures worshipped a primary deity believed to rule the sky.
She considered the river to be a living deity who protected the valley.

Synonyms

    7.9 2.04/m

  • god
    3.7 44/m
    god is the common everyday word for a worshipped supernatural being, while deity sounds more formal and bookish.
