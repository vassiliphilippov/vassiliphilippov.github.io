pecan

noun
wood from a pecan tree
8.5
Pronunciation: / pɪˈkɑːn, ˈpiˌkæn / (AmE), / pɪˈkɑːn, ˈpiːkæn / (BrE)
The wood of the pecan tree, valued for its hardness and used in furniture and flooring.

Examples

Pecan wood furniture
The craftsman preferred to work with pecan due to its strength and finish.
The flooring was made of polished pecan, giving the room a warm, rich color.
They chose pecan for the cabinets because of its durability and attractive grain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hickory - wood of the hickory tree, closely related to pecan
