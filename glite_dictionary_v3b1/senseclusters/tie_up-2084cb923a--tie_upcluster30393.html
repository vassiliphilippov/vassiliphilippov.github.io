tie up

verb
invest money so it's unavailable
3.7 5.5
Pronunciation: / taɪ ʌp / (AmE), / taɪ ʌp / (BrE)
to invest money so that it is not readily available for other uses

Examples

Tie up money

Most of our capital is tied up in property, so we can't access it easily.
She regrets tying up all her money in a single investment fund.
