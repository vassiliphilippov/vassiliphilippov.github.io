Domenikos Theotocopoulos

proper noun
original name of El Greco, Spanish-Greek painter
Pronunciation: / doʊˈmiːnɪkoʊs θiˌoʊtəˈkɒpələs / (AmE), / dəˌmiːnɪkɒs θiːˌɒtəˈkɒpɒləs / (BrE)
the original name of El Greco (1541–1614), a Spanish painter of Greek origin, renowned for his religious works featuring elongated forms and dramatic color.

Examples

Domenikos Theotocopoulos Spanish painter
Domenikos Theotocopoulos spent much of his career in Toledo, producing paintings that are still admired worldwide.
Art historians often refer to Domenikos Theotocopoulos when discussing the influence of Greek artists in the Spanish Renaissance.
The works of Domenikos Theotocopoulos are famous for their emotional intensity and distinctive style.
