take down

verb
demolish or dismantle a structure
1.0
Pronunciation: / teɪk daʊn / (AmE), / teɪk daʊn / (BrE)
to demolish or dismantle a structure so that it is level with the ground

Examples

To take down a building
The city plans to take down several unsafe buildings over the summer.
They finally decided to take down the old stadium after years of debate.
Workers had to take down the damaged warehouse after the storm.
