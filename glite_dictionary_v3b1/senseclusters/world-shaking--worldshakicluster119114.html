world-shaking

adjective
of worldwide impact or importance
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːld ˌʃeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːld ˌʃeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
Having such great significance or impact that it affects or could affect the whole world.

Examples

A world-shaking event
Despite the announcement, the news was hardly world-shaking in the grand scheme of things.
It takes a world-shaking event to bring nations together in solidarity.
The scientist's world-shaking discovery changed the course of modern medicine forever.

Related Terms

  • synonym: earthshaking - having a tremendous or overwhelming effect
  • antonym: insignificant - having little or no importance or effect
  • synonym: world-shattering - having such importance as to have a major effect on the world
