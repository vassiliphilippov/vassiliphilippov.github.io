passage

noun
motion of one object relative to another
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsɪdʒ /; / ˈpasɪdʒ / (BrE)
the act or process of moving from one place, state, or condition to another
