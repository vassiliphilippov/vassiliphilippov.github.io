seismographic

adjective
relating to seismographs
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪzməˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌsaɪzməˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to or produced by a seismograph, especially in recording earthquakes or ground movements

Examples

Seismographic data
The seismographic readings indicated a minor earthquake in the region last night.
Scientists analyzed the seismographic records to determine the earthquake's epicenter.
Modern seismographic equipment can detect even the smallest ground vibrations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seismic - relating to earthquakes or vibrations of the earth
