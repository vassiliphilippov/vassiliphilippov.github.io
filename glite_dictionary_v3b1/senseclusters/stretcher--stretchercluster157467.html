stretcher

noun
frame for stretching canvas in painting
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɛtʃər / (AmE), / ˈstrɛtʃə / (BrE)
a wooden frame over which canvas is stretched and fixed, used as a support for oil painting

Examples

Canvas stretcher
A high-quality stretcher ensures the canvas remains taut over time.
After removing the finished work, he replaced the torn stretcher with a sturdier one.
The artist prepared her canvas by mounting it onto a stretcher before beginning the painting.
