extraconstitutional

adjective
not allowed by constitution
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstrəkɒnˌstɪˈtuːʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəkɒnˌstɪˈtjuːʃənəl / (BrE)
not authorized, provided for, or permitted by a constitution

Examples

Extraconstitutional action
The president's decision to dissolve parliament was considered extraconstitutional by many legal experts.
Any extraconstitutional actions taken by the government must be carefully reviewed by the courts.
The new law was challenged as extraconstitutional because it bypassed established legal procedures.

Related Terms

  • antonym: constitutional - allowed or provided for by a constitution
  • synonym: unconstitutional - not allowed by a constitution
