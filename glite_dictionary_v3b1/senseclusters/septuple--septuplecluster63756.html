septuple

adjective
having seven parts or units
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛp.tə.pəl / (AmE), / ˈsɛp.tjʊ.pəl / (BrE)
consisting of seven parts, units, or components; being seven times as much or as many.

Examples

A septuple set
The box contained a septuple set of keys, each one labeled for a different purpose.
The committee approved a septuple division of the funds to distribute them equally among seven projects.
The researchers studied the septuple star system discovered in the distant galaxy.
