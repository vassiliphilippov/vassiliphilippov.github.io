common sense

noun
sound practical judgment
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən sɛns / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən sɛns / (BrE)
the ability to think and behave in a reasonable way and to make good decisions based on practical judgment

Examples

Common sense thinking
It takes a little common sense to know not to touch a hot stove.
She used her common sense to solve the problem quickly.
When in doubt, rely on common sense rather than complicated rules.

Related Terms

  • antonym: foolishness - lack of good sense or judgment
  • synonym: good sense - good judgment or practical decision-making
