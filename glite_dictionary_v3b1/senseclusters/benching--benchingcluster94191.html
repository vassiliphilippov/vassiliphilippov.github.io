benching

noun
slang for romantic stalling
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛntʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɛntʃɪŋ / (BrE)
a slang term for keeping someone as a romantic option without committing to a relationship

Examples

Benching a date
After months of benching, she realized he was never going to commit to a real relationship.
Many people find benching hurtful because it leaves them waiting without clear answers.
He admitted to benching several people while deciding who he wanted to date seriously.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breadcrumbing - sending occasional messages to keep someone interested without commitment
  • synonym: ghosting - suddenly ending all communication with someone
