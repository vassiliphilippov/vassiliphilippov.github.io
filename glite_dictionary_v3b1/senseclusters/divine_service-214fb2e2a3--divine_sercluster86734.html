divine service

noun
religious ceremony or public worship
8.5
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvaɪn ˈsɝː.vɪs / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪn ˈsɜː.vɪs / (BrE)
a ceremony of public worship conducted according to prescribed religious rules

Examples

Attend a divine service
He was asked to lead the prayers at this evening's divine service.
Many people attend divine service each week at the local cathedral.
The choir sang beautifully during the divine service on Sunday morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: liturgy - a prescribed form or set of forms for public religious worship
  • synonym: worship - the act of showing reverence and adoration, especially in a religious context
