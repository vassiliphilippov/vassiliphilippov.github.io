law enforcement

noun
act of upholding laws
2.8
Pronunciation: / lɔː ɪnˈfɔːrsmənt / (AmE), / lɔː ɪnˈfɔːsmənt / (BrE)
The activity of ensuring that people obey the law, especially by preventing and investigating crime and enforcing legal measures.

Examples

Law enforcement agencies
Effective law enforcement requires cooperation between the police and the community.
He spent his career working in law enforcement before retiring as a detective.
Many people pursue a career in law enforcement to help protect their neighborhoods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: policing - the activity of maintaining order and enforcing the law
