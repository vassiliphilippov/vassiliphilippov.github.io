airtight argument

noun
an argument impossible to refute
4.9 3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈer.taɪt ˈɑːr.gjə.mənt/ (AmE), /ˈeə.taɪt ˈɑː.gjʊ.mənt/ (BrE)
an argument that is so well reasoned and complete that it cannot be disputed or disproven

Examples

An airtight argument
Before presenting, she made sure her airtight argument addressed every possible objection.
Her airtight argument left her opponents with nothing to say in response.
The lawyer built an airtight argument that persuaded the jury to acquit his client.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flawed argument - an argument containing mistakes or weaknesses; opposite of 'airtight argument'
  • synonym: unassailable - impossible to dispute or attack; similar to 'airtight' in reasoning
