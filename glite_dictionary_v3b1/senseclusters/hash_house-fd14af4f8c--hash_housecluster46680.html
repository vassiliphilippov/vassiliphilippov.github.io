hash house

noun
inexpensive restaurant, usually serving simple food
Pronunciation: / ˈhæʃ haʊs / (AmE), / ˈhæʃ haʊs / (BrE)
An inexpensive restaurant, especially one serving simple or mediocre food; often used informally or humorously.

Examples

A hash house restaurant
Back in his college days, he used to work weekends at a noisy hash house.
The hash house served huge portions but the food was nothing special.
We stopped at a little hash house on the highway for a quick lunch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diner - a small, inexpensive restaurant, often open late
