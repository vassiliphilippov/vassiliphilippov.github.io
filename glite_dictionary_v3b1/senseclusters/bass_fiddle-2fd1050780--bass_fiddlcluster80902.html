bass fiddle

noun
large low-pitched string instrument
5.5
Pronunciation: / beɪs ˈfɪd.əl / (AmE), / beɪs ˈfɪd.əl / (BrE)
the largest and lowest-pitched instrument of the violin family, also known as the double bass or contrabass

Examples

She learned to play the bass fiddle to join her school's jazz band.
The orchestra featured a talented musician playing the bass fiddle in the back row.
