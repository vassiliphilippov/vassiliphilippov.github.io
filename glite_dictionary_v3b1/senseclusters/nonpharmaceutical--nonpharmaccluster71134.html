nonpharmaceutical

adjective
not drug-related
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌfɑrməˌsuːtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌfɑːməˈsjuːtɪkəl / (BrE)
not related to the pharmaceutical industry or the manufacture and sale of drugs

Examples

Nonpharmaceutical companies
The government is encouraging investment in nonpharmaceutical sectors to diversify the economy.
Many nonpharmaceutical companies have started to develop health-related products.
She decided to pursue a career in a nonpharmaceutical field after graduating from university.
