hydrogeological

adjective
groundwater study
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪdroʊˌdʒiəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌhaɪdrəʊˌdʒiːəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study of groundwater and how it moves through soil and rocks

Examples

A hydrogeological report
The hydrogeological report identified several underground water sources in the region.
Engineers conducted a hydrogeological assessment before building the new dam.
Understanding the hydrogeological conditions is essential for managing water resources.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geological - relating to the study of the earth's physical structure
  • synonym: hydrological - relating to the study of water and its movement on and below the earth's surface
