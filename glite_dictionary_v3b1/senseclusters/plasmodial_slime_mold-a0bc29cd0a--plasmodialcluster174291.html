plasmodial slime mold

noun
type of slime mold (Myxomycetes)
Pronunciation: / plæzˈmoʊdiəl slaɪm moʊld / (AmE), / plazˈməʊdiəl slaɪm məʊld / (BrE)
a type of slime mold belonging to the class Myxomycetes, characterized by a multinucleate, amoeboid plasmodium stage in its life cycle
