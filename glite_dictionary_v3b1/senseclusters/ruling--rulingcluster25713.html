noun
legal explanation for a court's judgment

Examples

Court ruling

Synonyms

  • legal opinion
    6.1 0.33/m
    a legal opinion is a formal written explanation judges publish in court records, while a ruling is the court's official statement of reasons.
  • opinion
    7.0 20/m
    an opinion is a judge's formal written explanation of reasons in a case, while a ruling is the court's official statement of those reasons.
  • judicial decision
    5.7 0.40/m
    a judicial decision is the court's formal determination of a case outcome, while a ruling gives the court's stated reasons apart from the verdict.
