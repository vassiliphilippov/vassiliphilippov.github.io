reillumination

noun
restoring light
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪˌluːməˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːɪˌluːmɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of lighting something again or restoring its illumination

Examples

Reillumination of the building
The reillumination of the old theater brought new life to the city center.
After the blackout, the reillumination of the streets made everyone feel safer.
The reillumination of the artwork revealed details that had been hidden in shadow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: darkening - the act of making something less bright or unlit
  • synonym: illumination - the act of lighting or making something bright
  • synonym: relighting - the act of lighting something again
