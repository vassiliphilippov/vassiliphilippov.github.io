bpm

noun
Business Process Management (abbreviation)
Pronunciation: / bi pi ɛm / (AmE), / biː piː ɛm / (BrE)
(in business and IT) an abbreviation for Business Process Management: the practice and set of methods, tools, and technologies used to model, analyse, automate, and improve an organisation's workflows and processes.

Examples

Bpm software
Our company adopted bpm to streamline cross-department workflows and reduce manual handoffs.
She studied bpm methods to learn how to model processes and measure operational performance.
The consultancy recommended bpm tools to automate invoice approvals and speed up finance tasks.
