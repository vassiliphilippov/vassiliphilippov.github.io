federative

adjective
of a federation
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛdərətɪv / (AmE), / ˈfɛdərətɪv / (BrE)
relating to or having the characteristics of a federation or federal system of government

Examples

A federative system
The new constitution established a federative system to balance power between the central and regional governments.
Several countries have adopted federative principles to allow local regions more autonomy.
A federative structure can help unite diverse regions under one national government.

Related Terms

  • synonym: federal - relating to a system of government with central and regional authorities
  • antonym: unitary - relating to a system of government with a single central authority
