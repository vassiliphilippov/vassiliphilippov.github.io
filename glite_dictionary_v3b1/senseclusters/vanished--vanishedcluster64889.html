vanished

adjective
no longer existing; disappeared
Pronunciation: / ˈvænɪʃt / (AmE), / ˈvænɪʃt / (BrE)
having stopped existing; no longer present or in existence

Examples

Has vanished completely
All hopes of recovery had vanished by the end of the week.
He studied the ruins of a vanished civilization in South America.
Many species have vanished from the area due to habitat loss.

Related Terms

  • antonym: existing - having being or existence, especially currently
  • synonym: extinct - no longer in existence (especially of species)
  • synonym: gone - no longer present; departed
