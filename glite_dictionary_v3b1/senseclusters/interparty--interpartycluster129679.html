interparty

adjective
between parties
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈpɑːrti / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈpɑːti / (BrE)
occurring or existing between two or more parties, especially in legal or political contexts

Examples

Interparty talks
The leaders held interparty talks to resolve the ongoing dispute.
An interparty agreement was necessary to pass the new legislation.
The conference focused on interparty cooperation to address national issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bipartisan - involving two political parties
  • antonym: intraparty - occurring within a single party
