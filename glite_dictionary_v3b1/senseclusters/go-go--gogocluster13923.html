go-go

noun
energetic nightclub
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊ ˌɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊ ˌɡəʊ / (BrE)
a nightclub or dance club known for energetic dancing and lively music, often with a vibrant atmosphere.

Examples

Go-go club
The city is famous for its vibrant go-go scene, attracting young people every weekend.
They spent the evening dancing at a popular go-go downtown.
Loud music and flashing lights filled the go-go as the crowd danced energetically.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discotheque - a nightclub where people dance to recorded music
  • synonym: nightclub - a place open at night for dancing and entertainment
