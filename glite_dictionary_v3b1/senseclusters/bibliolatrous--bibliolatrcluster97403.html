bibliolatrous

adjective
excessively revering the Bible
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌbɪbliˈɑlətrəs / (AmE), / ˌbɪblɪˈɒlətrəs / (BrE)
Showing excessive reverence or worship for the Bible.

Examples

Bibliolatrous worship
A bibliolatrous approach can sometimes hinder broader religious understanding.

Some critics view the group's bibliolatrous beliefs as a barrier to open discussion and change.
