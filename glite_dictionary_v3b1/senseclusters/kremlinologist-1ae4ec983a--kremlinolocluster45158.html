Kremlinologist

noun
analyst of Russian politics
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɛmləˈnɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌkrɛmlɪˈnɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies and interprets the politics and policies of Russia or the former Soviet Union, especially by analyzing official statements and actions

Examples

Russian Kremlinologist
The Kremlinologist gave a detailed analysis of the new Russian foreign policy.
Many journalists consult a Kremlinologist to understand the government's secretive decisions.
As a leading Kremlinologist, she often appears on television to discuss Russian politics.
The Kremlinologist offered insights into the latest developments in Russian foreign policy.
As a leading Kremlinologist, she was often consulted by news organizations for her expertise.
Many Kremlinologists closely examine official speeches for hidden meanings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Russia expert - a person with specialized knowledge of Russia
  • synonym: Sovietologist - a person who studies the Soviet Union and its policies
  • synonym: political analyst - a person who studies and explains political events and trends
