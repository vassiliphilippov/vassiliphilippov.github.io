headily

adverb
impulsively or rashly
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdɪli / (AmE), / ˈhɛdɪli / (BrE)
in a headstrong, rash, or impulsive manner, without careful thought

Examples

Headily accept an offer
She accepted the offer headily, not considering the possible consequences.
They headily agreed to the plan, excited by the challenge.
He headily jumped into the debate without preparing his arguments.
