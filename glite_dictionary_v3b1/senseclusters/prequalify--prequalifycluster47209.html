prequalify

verb
to check eligibility early
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈkwɑːlɪfaɪ / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈkwɒlɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to determine or show in advance that someone or something meets the necessary requirements, especially before applying or participating
