bearing or producing
9.9
Pronunciation: / fɚ / (AmE), / fəː / (BrE)
a combining form meaning 'bearing', 'producing', or 'carrying', used in nouns and adjectives

Examples

Fer-bearing trees
The word 'conifer' uses the combining form fer to mean 'cone-bearing'.
In 'aquifer', the suffix fer indicates something that carries or contains water.
The term 'lucifer' includes fer, meaning 'light-bringer'.
