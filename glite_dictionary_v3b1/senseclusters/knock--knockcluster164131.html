knock

noun
an act of striking or hitting
1.3 5.76/m
Pronunciation: / nÉ‘k / (AmE), / nÉ’k / (BrE)
a sharp sound made by hitting a surface, especially a door, or by a mechanical fault

Examples

A knock at the door
He answered her question with a playful knock on the table.
The sudden knock on the window startled everyone in the room.
There was a loud knock at the door just after midnight.
She heard a soft knock at the door and went to see who it was.
The engine developed a strange knock after the oil ran low.
There was a sudden knock from the other side of the wall, startling everyone.
