shitheap

noun
something dirty or worthless (vulgar)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪtˌhiːp / (AmE), / ˈʃɪt.hiːp / (BrE)
a pile or large amount of something regarded as very dirty, worthless, or unpleasant

Examples

A shitheap of trash
After years of neglect, the house had become a shitheap full of trash and broken furniture.
He was angry to find his car reduced to a shitheap by vandals.
The report called the company’s finances a complete shitheap that needed immediate attention.

  • antonym: treasure - something valuable, prized, or esteemed
