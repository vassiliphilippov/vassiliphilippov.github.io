staring

adjective
wide open, fixed (of eyes)
4.8 1.6
Pronunciation: /ˈste.rɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈsteə.rɪŋ/ (BrE)
(of eyes) wide open and fixed in one direction, often as a result of fear, surprise, or intense concentration.

Examples

A staring gaze
Her staring gaze made everyone in the room uncomfortable.
I couldn't look away from his staring eyes as he stood frozen by the news.
The child's staring expression revealed a mix of awe and fear.
