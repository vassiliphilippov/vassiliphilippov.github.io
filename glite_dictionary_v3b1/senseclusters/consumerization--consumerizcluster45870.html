consumerization

noun
focus on consumption
8.0
Pronunciation: / kənˌsuːmərɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / kənˌsjuːmərɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process by which social life, institutions, or identities become focused on consumption and consumer preferences

Examples

Consumerization of society
The consumerization of technology has made advanced devices available to the general public.
Many experts debate the impact of consumerization on traditional educational values.
Some worry that the consumerization of healthcare could undermine patient care.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commercialization - the process of managing or running something mainly for financial gain
  • synonym: consumerism - the belief that buying and using goods and services is important for society
