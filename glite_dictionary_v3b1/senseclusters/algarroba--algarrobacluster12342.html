algarroba

noun
mesquite pod used for tanning and dyeing
Pronunciation: / ˌælɡəˈroʊbə / (AmE), / ˌalɡəˈrəʊbə / (BrE)
a mesquite tree native to the Americas, or its long pod, used in tanning, dyeing, or as animal feed

Examples
