firster

noun
person eager to be first
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfɜrstər / (AmE), / ˈfɜːstə / (BrE)
a person who tries to be the first to do or comment on something, especially online

Examples

Online firster
Every time a new video is uploaded, a firster rushes to comment before anyone else.
The forum moderators joked about awarding a prize to the fastest firster each day.
Some users find firsters annoying, while others see it as harmless fun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commenter - a person who posts comments online
