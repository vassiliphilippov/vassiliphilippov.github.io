Synonyms

  • present
    2.7 26/m
    present means to give something formally in a ceremony, while bestow feels more old-fashioned and is used for honors or gifts.
  • confer
    6.2 3.72/m
    confer means to officially give a degree, honor, or title, while bestow is used more broadly for gifts or awards.
  • grace someone with
    6.7 0.55/m
    grace someone with often means to give a pleasant favor or honor kindly, while bestow sounds more formal and general.
  • gift
    2.1 2.05/m
    gift means to give something as a present in everyday life, while bestow implies a more formal or honorable giving.
