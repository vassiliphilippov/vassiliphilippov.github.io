keep one's cool

verb
remain calm under pressure
3.3
Pronunciation: / kip wʌnz kul / (AmE), / kiːp wʌnz kuːl / (BrE)
to remain calm and in control of one's emotions, especially in a stressful or difficult situation

Examples

To keep her cool
Even during the heated debate, she managed to keep her cool and respond politely.
He struggled to keep his cool after receiving the unexpected news.
It's important to keep your cool when dealing with difficult customers.

Related Terms
