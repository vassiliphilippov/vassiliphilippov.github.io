angelus

noun
bell rung for Angelus prayer
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈændʒələs / (AmE), / ˈandʒələs / (BrE)
The sound of a bell rung in Roman Catholic churches to signal the time for reciting the Angelus prayer.

Examples

The angelus bell
As the angelus sounded, parishioners paused their work to recite the prayer.
The angelus rang at noon, echoing through the quiet village.
You could hear the church’s angelus from miles away every evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Angelus - A Roman Catholic prayer commemorating the Incarnation, recited morning, noon, and evening
