A species of iris with purple flowers and foul-smelling leaves, native to southern and western Europe and North Africa.

Examples

Iris foetidissima plant
Iris foetidissima is often grown in gardens for its attractive seed pods despite the unpleasant smell of its leaves.
Native to southern Europe, Iris foetidissima produces striking purple flowers in early summer.
The leaves of Iris foetidissima emit a foul odor when crushed, which helps distinguish it from other irises.
