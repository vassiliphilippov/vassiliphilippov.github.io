Mughal

adjective
of Mughal dynasty
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːɡəl / (AmE), / ˈmʊɡəl / (BrE)
relating to the Mughal dynasty, its culture, art, architecture, or administration in South Asia from the 16th to 19th centuries

Examples

The palace is a fine example of Mughal architecture, with its grand domes and intricate carvings.
She studied Mughal art to better understand the cultural history of India.
The exhibition featured rare Mughal manuscripts from the seventeenth century.
