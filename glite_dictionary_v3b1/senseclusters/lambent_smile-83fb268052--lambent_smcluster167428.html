lambent smile

noun
softly radiant smile
Pronunciation: / ˈlæmbənt smaɪl / (AmE), / ˈlæmbənt smaɪl / (BrE)
A smile that appears softly radiant, glowing, or gently luminous.

Examples

A lambent smile
Behind his reserved manner, a lambent smile would occasionally reveal his gentle spirit.
Her lambent smile lit up his dark day.
The actress greeted her fans with a lambent smile that seemed to warm the entire room.
