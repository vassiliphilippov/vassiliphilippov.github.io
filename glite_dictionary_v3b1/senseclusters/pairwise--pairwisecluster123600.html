pairwise

adjective
involving pairs
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛrˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈpeəˌwaɪz / (BrE)
relating to or involving pairs, especially when each pair is considered separately from others

Examples

Pairwise comparison
The students worked in pairwise teams to solve the math problems.
In the experiment, the scientists compared the results pairwise to identify differences.
The tournament used a pairwise elimination system to determine the winner.
