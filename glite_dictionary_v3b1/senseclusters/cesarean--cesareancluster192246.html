cesarean

noun
surgical birth by abdominal incision
3.9
Pronunciation: / sɪˈzɛriən /; / siˈzɛəriən /; / siˈzɛriən / (AmE), / sɪˈzɛːrɪən /; / siˈzɛəriən /; / siˈzɛːriən / (BrE)
the surgical delivery of a baby by cutting through the abdominal wall and uterus
