Synonyms

  • cesarean section
    3.7 0.04/m
    cesarean section is the standard medical phrase used in hospitals for this operation, while cesarean is the general surgical delivery term.
  • cesarean delivery
    3.6 0.07/m
    cesarean delivery describes the baby being born through a surgical cut in the belly, while cesarean names this surgical delivery.
  • C-section
    3.4 1.08/m
    C-section is the common informal name people use in speech, while cesarean is the formal medical name for the operation.
  • abdominal delivery
    3.6 0.00/m
    abdominal delivery is a descriptive, less common phrase used by some writers, while cesarean is the usual medical term for this surgery.
