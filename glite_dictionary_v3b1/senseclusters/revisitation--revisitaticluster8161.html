revisitation

noun
return or reconsideration
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌvɪzɪˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌvɪzɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of visiting, considering, or dealing with something again, especially for further thought or analysis

Examples

A revisitation of ideas
The director's latest movie is a revisitation of themes from his earlier work.
Her essay offers a thoughtful revisitation of the novel's main arguments.
The museum's new exhibit is a revisitation of ancient art with a modern perspective.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reexamination - the act of examining something again
  • synonym: review - the process of examining or assessing something again
