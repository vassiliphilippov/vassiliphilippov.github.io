turtlelike

adjective
resembling a turtle
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝːtəlˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈtɜːtəlˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having characteristics of a turtle, especially in appearance or movement

Examples

Turtlelike slow movement
The old car moved at a turtlelike pace down the country road.
Her turtlelike patience allowed her to finish the difficult puzzle without frustration.
The sculpture had a turtlelike shell that made it stand out in the garden.
