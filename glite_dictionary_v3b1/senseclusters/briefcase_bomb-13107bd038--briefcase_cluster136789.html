briefcase bomb

noun
bomb hidden in a briefcase
Pronunciation: / ˈbriːfˌkeɪs bɑm / (AmE), / ˈbriːfˌkeɪs bɒm / (BrE)
A bomb hidden inside a briefcase, typically designed to be concealed and detonated remotely or by a timer.

Examples

A hidden briefcase bomb
A briefcase bomb was used in the attempted assassination of the official.

The police discovered a briefcase bomb in the train station before it could detonate.
