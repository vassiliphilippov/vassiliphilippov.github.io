spurner

noun
person who scornfully rejects
Pronunciation: / ˈspɝːnər / (AmE), / ˈspɜːnə / (BrE)
a person who rejects someone or something with disdain or contempt

Examples

A spurner of offers
He took pride in being a spurner of mediocrity.
The spurner of all invitations, Mark rarely attended any social gatherings.
Throughout her career, she became known as a spurner of conventional wisdom.

Related Terms

  • antonym: embracer - a person who readily accepts or welcomes something or someone
  • synonym: rejecter - a person who refuses to accept or believes in something
