nana

noun
slang for young woman
Pronunciation: / ˈnænə / (BrE)
an informal or slang term for a young woman or girl, used mainly in British English

Examples

A young nana
He spent the evening talking to a friendly nana at the party.
Some people use the word nana to refer to a stylish young woman.
In certain British cities, you might hear someone call their friend a nana.

Related Terms

  • synonym: girl - a female child or young woman
  • synonym: woman - an adult female person
