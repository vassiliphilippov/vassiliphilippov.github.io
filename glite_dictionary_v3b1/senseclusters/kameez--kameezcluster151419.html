kameez

noun
long South Asian tunic
Pronunciation: / kəˈmiz / (AmE), / kəˈmiːz / (BrE)
A long tunic traditionally worn in South Asia, often with a salwar or churidar.

Examples

A kameez tunic
His mother helped him choose a new kameez for the festival.
She wore a beautiful blue kameez embroidered with silver thread to the wedding.
Traditional dress often includes both a kameez and matching salwar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kurta - another style of South Asian tunic
  • synonym: salwar - loose trousers often worn with a kameez
