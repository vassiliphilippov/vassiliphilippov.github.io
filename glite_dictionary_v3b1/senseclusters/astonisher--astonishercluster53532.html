astonisher

noun
source of amazement
Pronunciation: / əˈstɑːnɪʃər / (AmE), / əˈstɒnɪʃə / (BrE)
a person or thing that causes great surprise or amazement

Examples

A real astonisher
The new roller coaster is a real astonisher for thrill-seekers at the amusement park.
Her performance in the championship game was an astonisher that left the crowd cheering.
That painting is an astonisher and draws visitors from all over the world.
