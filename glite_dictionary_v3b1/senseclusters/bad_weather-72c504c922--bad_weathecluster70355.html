bad weather

noun
unpleasant or unsuitable weather
1.0
Pronunciation: / bæd ˈwɛðɚ / (AmE), / bæd ˈwɛðə / (BrE)
weather conditions that are unpleasant or unsuitable for outdoor activities, often involving rain, wind, or storms.

Examples

Severe bad weather
Farmers worry when bad weather threatens the harvest.
The flight was delayed due to bad weather conditions.
We had to cancel the picnic because of the bad weather.
