dermatophyte

noun
skin-infecting fungus
Pronunciation: / ˈdɜːrmətəˌfaɪt / (AmE), / ˈdɜːmətəˌfaɪt / (BrE)
a type of fungus that causes infections of the skin, hair, or nails

Examples

A dermatophyte fungus
A dermatophyte can cause athlete's foot and other skin problems.
Doctors often prescribe antifungal creams to treat dermatophyte infections.
Ringworm is a common condition caused by a dermatophyte affecting the skin.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bacteria - single-celled microorganisms, some of which cause infections
  • synonym: fungus - an organism such as a mold, yeast, or mushroom
