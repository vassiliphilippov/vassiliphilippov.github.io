unstimulated

adjective
not excited or interested
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈstɪmjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈstɪmjʊleɪtɪd / (BrE)
not feeling interested, excited, or encouraged to act or respond

Examples

Feeling unstimulated at work
She felt unstimulated by the lack of new challenges in her job.
The students remained unstimulated during the long and monotonous lecture.
Without creative projects, he quickly became unstimulated and restless.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bored - feeling tired and uninterested
  • antonym: stimulated - feeling excited or encouraged to act
