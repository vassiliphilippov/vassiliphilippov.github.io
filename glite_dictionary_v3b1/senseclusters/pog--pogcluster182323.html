pog

noun
non-combat military member
9.9
Pronunciation: / pɑɡ / (AmE), / pɒɡ / (BrE)
an informal term for a military service member who works in non-combat roles, not assigned to infantry or combat arms

Examples

Pog in the army
Some soldiers jokingly call anyone not on the front lines a pog.
He was proud of his work, even if others saw him as a pog.
Logistics and support staff are often referred to as pogs in military slang.

Related Terms

  • antonym: grunt - an infantry soldier, especially one in combat roles
