announcement

noun
formal public statement or notice
Pronunciation: / əˈnaʊnsmənt / (AmE), / əˈnaʊnsmənt / (BrE)
a public or formal statement that gives information about something

Examples

A public announcement
The company issued an announcement regarding the new policy changes.
The principal's announcement about the upcoming holiday was met with cheers from the students.
We awaited the official announcement of the election results all evening.
Please listen carefully to the announcement before boarding the train.
The company made an announcement about the launch of its new product.
The wedding announcement was posted in the local newspaper.

Related Terms

  • antonym: denial - a statement that something is not true or did not happen
  • synonym: statement - a definite or clear expression in speech or writing
