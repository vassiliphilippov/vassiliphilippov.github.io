correspondent

noun
someone who writes letters to another
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌkɔrəˈspɑndənt / (AmE), / ˌkɒrəˈspɒndənt / (BrE)
a person who communicates with another by exchanging letters, especially regularly

Examples

A regular correspondent
As a young man, he was an avid correspondent, exchanging letters with friends across the world.
Each correspondent in the pen pal program writes at least one letter every month.
She became a lifelong correspondent of her favorite author after writing him a fan letter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pen pal - a person with whom one exchanges letters regularly, especially as a hobby
