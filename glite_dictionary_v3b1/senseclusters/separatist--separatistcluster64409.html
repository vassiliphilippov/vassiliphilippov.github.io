separatist

noun
person who advocates separation from a larger group
6.3 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈse.pə.rə.tɪst/ (AmE), /ˈse.pə.rə.tɪst/ (BrE)
a person who supports the separation of a group of people from a larger body on the basis of ethnicity, religion, or nationality

Examples

A separatist leader
He was labeled a separatist after openly supporting the province's secession.
Some separatist groups demand complete independence from the central government.
The separatist movement in the region gained significant international attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: secessionist - person who supports withdrawal, especially from a political entity
  • antonym: unionist - person who supports unity, especially political union
