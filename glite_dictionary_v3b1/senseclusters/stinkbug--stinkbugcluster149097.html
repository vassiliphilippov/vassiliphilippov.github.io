stinkbug

noun
mild teasing insult
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪŋkˌbʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋkˌbʌɡ / (BrE)
an informal, mildly teasing term for a person who is annoying or unpleasant

Examples

Calling someone a stinkbug
Don't be such a stinkbug—just let your sister have a turn.
He called his friend a stinkbug after she played a practical joke on him.
Whenever you complain about chores, you sound like a real stinkbug.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nuisance - someone who causes annoyance
  • synonym: pest - an annoying person
