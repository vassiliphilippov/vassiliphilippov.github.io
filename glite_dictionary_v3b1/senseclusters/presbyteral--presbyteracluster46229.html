presbyteral

adjective
of church elders
9.9
Pronunciation: / prɛzˈbɪtərəl / (AmE), / prɛzˈbɪtərəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a presbyter or elders in the Christian church

Examples

Presbyteral council
The presbyteral council met to discuss important decisions for the parish.
He was appointed to a presbyteral role after many years of service in the church.
The ceremony followed ancient presbyteral traditions passed down through generations.
