stroboscopically

adverb
with strobe effect
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌstroʊbəˈskɑpɪkli / (AmE), / ˌstrɒbəˈskɒpɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or uses a stroboscope or stroboscopic effect, often involving rapid flashes of light.

Examples

Move stroboscopically under lights
The dancers moved stroboscopically under the flashing club lights.
During the experiment, the machine operated stroboscopically to capture high-speed motion.
The lightning illuminated the sky stroboscopically during the intense storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intermittently - in a way that occurs at irregular intervals
  • antonym: steadily - in a continuous and even manner
