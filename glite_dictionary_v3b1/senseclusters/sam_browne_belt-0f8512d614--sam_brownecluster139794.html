Sam Browne belt

noun
leather belt with shoulder strap (uniform)
8.1
Pronunciation: / sæm braʊn bɛlt / (AmE), / sam braʊn belt / (BrE)
A leather belt supported by a strap passing over the right shoulder, used chiefly as part of a military or police uniform to support a sword or other equipment.

Examples

A Sam Browne belt strap
Many military dress uniforms include a Sam Browne belt as standard issue.
The Sam Browne belt was designed to help carry the weight of a sword or pistol.
The officer fastened his Sam Browne belt before heading out on parade.
