trigeminal

adjective
of facial nerve
Pronunciation: / traɪˈdʒɛmɪnəl / (AmE), / traɪˈdʒɛmɪnəl / (BrE)
relating to the trigeminal nerve, the main nerve responsible for sensation in the face and motor functions such as biting and chewing

Examples

Trigeminal response
The doctor examined the patient's trigeminal response to check for nerve damage.
Pain in the trigeminal area can indicate a neurological disorder.
The trigeminal nerve is essential for facial sensation and movement.
