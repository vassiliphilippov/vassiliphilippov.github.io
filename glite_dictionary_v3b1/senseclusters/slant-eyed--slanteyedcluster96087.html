slant-eyed

adjective
having eyes angled or slanted (offensive when used for people)
9.9
Pronunciation: / slænt aɪd / (AmE), / slɑːnt aɪd / (BrE)
having eyes that are angled or slanted, especially used (often offensively) to describe people perceived as having narrow, upward-slanting eyes

Examples

Slant-eyed look
He made an offensive comment about her being slant-eyed, which upset many people.
Some creatures in folklore are depicted as slant-eyed beings.
The villain in the novel was described as slant-eyed and menacing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: narrow-eyed - having eyes that appear narrow or small
