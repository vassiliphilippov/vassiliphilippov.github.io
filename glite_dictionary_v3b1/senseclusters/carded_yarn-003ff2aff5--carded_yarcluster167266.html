carded yarn

noun
yarn processed by carding only
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrdɪd jɑrn / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdɪd jɑːn / (BrE)
Yarn that has been processed through carding but not combed, resulting in a less smooth texture.

Examples

Carded yarn texture
Sweaters made from carded yarn often have a softer, bulkier feel.
The factory produces both carded yarn and combed yarn for different textile needs.
They ordered several bales of carded yarn for weaving traditional carpets.

Related Terms

  • antonym: combed yarn - yarn that has been both carded and combed for extra smoothness
