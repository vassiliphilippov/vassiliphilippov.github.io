scarfpin

noun
pin for fastening a scarf or tie
Pronunciation: / ˈskɑrfˌpɪn / (AmE), / ˈskɑːfˌpɪn / (BrE)
A decorative pin used to fasten a scarf or tie in place, especially at the neck.

Examples

A scarfpin for scarves
He wore a gold scarfpin to secure his silk tie.
His elegant suit was accented by a sparkling scarfpin at his collar.
The antique scarfpin was passed down through generations in her family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tiepin - a pin used to hold a necktie in place
