olestra

noun
fat substitute
8.1
Pronunciation: / oʊˈlɛstrə / (AmE), / əʊˈlɛstrə / (BrE)
a synthetic fat substitute used in some snack foods to reduce calories without adding fat

Examples

Olestra fat substitute
Some potato chips are made with olestra to lower their fat content.
The use of olestra in snacks allows people to enjoy the taste without consuming extra fat.
Many consumers check labels to see if olestra is included in their favorite foods.
