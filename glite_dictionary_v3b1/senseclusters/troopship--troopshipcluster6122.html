troopship

noun
military ship for transporting troops
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtruːpˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈtruːpʃɪp / (BrE)
A ship designed or used for transporting soldiers, especially during wartime.

Examples

A troopship carries soldiers
After decommissioning, the old troopship was converted into a passenger ferry.
During World War II, my grandfather sailed across the Atlantic on a troopship.
The troopship arrived at dawn, carrying thousands of soldiers to the front lines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: transport ship - a ship used to move people or goods, especially military personnel
