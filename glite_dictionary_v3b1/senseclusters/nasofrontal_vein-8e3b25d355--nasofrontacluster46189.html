nasofrontal vein

noun
vein in the front middle of the eye socket
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌneɪzoʊˈfrʌntəl veɪn / (AmE), / ˌneɪzəʊˈfrʌntəl veɪn / (BrE)
a vein located in the front middle part of the eye socket, connecting the superior ophthalmic vein with the angular vein

Examples

The nasofrontal vein near eye
During the surgery, the doctor carefully avoided injuring the nasofrontal vein.
Swelling in the area can sometimes affect the function of the nasofrontal vein.
The nasofrontal vein is important in draining blood from the nose and forehead regions.
