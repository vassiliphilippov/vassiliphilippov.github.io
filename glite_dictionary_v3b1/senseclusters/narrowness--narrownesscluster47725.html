narrowness

noun
the quality of being narrow
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈnæroʊnəs / (AmE), / ˈnærəʊnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being narrow; having little width

Examples

Narrowness of the road
He was concerned about the narrowness of the doorway when moving the furniture in.
The narrowness of her perspective limited her understanding of the issue.
The narrowness of the path made it difficult for two people to walk side by side.

Related Terms

  • antonym: broadness - the quality or state of being broad or wide
  • synonym: narrow - having a small distance from side to side
  • antonym: width - the measurement or extent of something from side to side
