April

proper noun
the fourth month of the year
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.prəl / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.prɪl / (BrE)
The fourth month of the year, coming after March and before May.

Examples

April month
Her birthday is in April, so she always celebrates with spring flowers.
The weather usually starts to warm up in April after a cold winter.
We are planning a family vacation next April to take advantage of the holidays.
