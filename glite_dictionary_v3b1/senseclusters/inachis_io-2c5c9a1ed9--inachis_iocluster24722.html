Inachis io

noun
European butterfly with eyespot markings
Pronunciation: / ɪˈneɪkɪs ˈiː.oʊ / (AmE), / ɪˈneɪkɪs ˈiː.əʊ / (BrE)
A European butterfly, commonly known as the peacock butterfly, with reddish-brown wings each marked with a purple eyespot.
