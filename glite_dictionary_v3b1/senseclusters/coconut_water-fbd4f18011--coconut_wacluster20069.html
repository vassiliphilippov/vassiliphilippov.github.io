coconut water

noun
clear liquid inside a coconut
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊkəˌnʌt ˈwɔtər / (AmE), / ˈkəʊkənʌt ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
the clear or whitish liquid found inside a fresh coconut, often consumed as a beverage

Examples

coconut water drink
Many athletes prefer coconut water as a natural source of hydration.
She drank a glass of coconut water to refresh herself after her workout.
The street vendor sells chilled coconut water straight from freshly cut coconuts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coconut milk - white, milky liquid made by grating coconut flesh and mixing it with water
