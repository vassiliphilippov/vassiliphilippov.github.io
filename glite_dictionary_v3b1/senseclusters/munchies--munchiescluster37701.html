munchies

noun
craving for snacks
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌntʃiz / (AmE), / ˈmʌntʃiz / (BrE)
a strong craving or desire to eat snacks or food, especially suddenly or between meals (sometimes caused by using marijuana)

Examples

Have the munchies
After the party, everyone had the munchies and ordered a large pizza.
He always gets the munchies late at night and raids the fridge for snacks.
Some people experience the munchies after using cannabis and crave salty or sweet foods.
After the long hike, everyone had the munchies and wanted something to eat.
Watching late-night TV often gives me the munchies.
We brought plenty of munchies to share at the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appetite - a natural desire to eat
  • synonym: craving - a strong desire for something, especially food
  • synonym: snack - a small amount of food eaten between meals
