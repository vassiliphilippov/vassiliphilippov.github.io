culler

noun
person who removes unwanted items
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlər / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
a person or thing that selects or removes items, especially those that are unwanted or inferior

Examples

Fruit culler
The culler sorted out the damaged fruit from the batch.
A skilled culler ensures only the best products reach the market.
In wildlife management, a culler may be responsible for reducing animal populations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: remover - a person or thing that takes something away
  • synonym: selector - a person or thing that chooses or selects
