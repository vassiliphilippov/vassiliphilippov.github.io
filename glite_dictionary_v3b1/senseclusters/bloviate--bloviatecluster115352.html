bloviate

verb
speak at length in a pompous way
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbloʊ.vi.eɪt / (AmE), / ˈbləʊ.vi.eɪt / (BrE)
to speak or write at length in a pompous or boastful manner, often with little real content

Examples

To bloviate loudly
Instead of bloviating, try giving a concise and clear explanation.
She was annoyed by his habit to bloviate rather than answer questions directly.
The politician tends to bloviate during every speech, rarely getting to the main point.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orate - to speak formally, often at length
  • synonym: ramble - to talk or write in a lengthy, confused, or inconsequential way
