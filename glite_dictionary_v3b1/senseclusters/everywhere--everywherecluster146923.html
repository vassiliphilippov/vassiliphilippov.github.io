everywhere

adverb
in all places
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɛvriˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈɛvriwɛə / (BrE)
In or to all places; in every place.

Examples

People everywhere
After the announcement, people everywhere started talking about the news.
Fast food restaurants can be found everywhere in the city.
She looked everywhere for her lost keys, but couldn't find them.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nowhere - not in or to any place
  • degree lower: somewhere - in or to some place
