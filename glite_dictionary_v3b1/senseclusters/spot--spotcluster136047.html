spot

noun
lamp projecting a focused beam
7.9
Pronunciation: / spɑt / (AmE), / spɒt / (BrE)
a lamp that projects a strong, focused beam of light onto a specific area, often used to highlight a performer on stage

Examples

Stage spot
She waited nervously in the spot, knowing all eyes were on her.
The stage manager adjusted the spot so that it illuminated only the lead singer.
Two powerful spots lit up the center of the stage for the final act.
