bluish

adjective
somewhat blue in color
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbluːɪʃ / (BrE)

Examples

A bluish color
He noticed a bluish mark on his arm after bumping into the table.
The early morning sky had a bluish hue before the sun rose.
The old photograph had a bluish tint that made it look antique.
