lipid granulomatosis

noun
disorder with widespread xanthomas due to lipid metabolism issues
9.4
Pronunciation: /ˈlaɪ.pɪd ɡræ.njʊ.lə.ˈmæt.ə.sɪs/ /; / /ˈlɪp.ɪd ɡræ.njʊ.lə.ˈmæt.ə.sɪs/ (AmE), /ˈlɪp.ɪd ɡræ.njʊ.lə.ˈmæt.ə.sɪs/ /; / /ˈlɪp.ɪd ɡræ.njuː.lə.ˈmæt.ə.sɪs/ (BrE)
A medical condition characterized by widespread xanthomas, especially on the elbows and knees, often associated with a disorder of lipid metabolism.

Examples

Lipid granulomatosis disease
Doctors diagnosed her with lipid granulomatosis after noticing characteristic xanthomas on her knees and elbows.
The presence of lipid granulomatosis often indicates an underlying lipid metabolism disorder.
Treatment for lipid granulomatosis focuses on managing cholesterol levels and preventing further xanthoma development.
