pro-capitalist

adjective
supporting capitalism
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊˈkæpɪtəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌprəʊˈkæpɪtəlɪst / (BrE)
supporting or favoring the economic system of capitalism

Examples

Pro-capitalist policies
The new government introduced several pro-capitalist reforms to encourage private investment.
Her pro-capitalist views were evident in her support for free market policies.
Critics argue that pro-capitalist measures can increase inequality in society.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anti-capitalist - opposed to the economic system of capitalism
  • synonym: capitalist - supporting private ownership and free markets
