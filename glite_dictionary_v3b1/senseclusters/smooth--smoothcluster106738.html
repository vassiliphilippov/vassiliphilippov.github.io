smooth

noun
an act of smoothing
Pronunciation: / smuð / (AmE), / smuːð / (BrE)
an act of making a surface even or free from roughness by smoothing it

Examples

A gentle smooth
A gentle smooth of the fabric removed the wrinkles.
He finished the painting with a final smooth over the canvas.
With a quick smooth, she tamed the flyaway hairs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stroke - a gentle movement of the hand over a surface
