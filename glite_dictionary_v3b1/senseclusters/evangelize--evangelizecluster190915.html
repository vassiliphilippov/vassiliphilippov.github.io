evangelize

verb
preach or spread the Christian faith
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvæn.dʒəˌlaɪz /; / ɪˈvæn.dʒə.laɪz / (AmE), / ɪˈvan.dʒɪ.laɪz /; / ɪˈvan.dʒə.laɪz / (BrE)
to try to convert people to Christianity by preaching or spreading its teachings

Examples

To evangelize about Jesus
He felt called to evangelize after his spiritual awakening.
The missionaries traveled across Africa to evangelize remote communities.
They used radio broadcasts to evangelize to people in distant regions.
She felt called to evangelize among her local community through service and prayer.
Some television programs are designed to evangelize a wider audience about Christianity.
The missionaries traveled overseas to evangelize remote villages.
Many television programs aim to evangelise to a broader audience.
She devoted her life to evangelising among the local population.
The church sent volunteers to evangelise in remote villages.
