hyssop

noun
aromatic plant used in cooking and medicine
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪsəp / (AmE), / ˈhɪsəp / (BrE)
an aromatic plant with pungent leaves, used in cooking, herbal medicine, and sometimes in perfumery

Examples

Dried hyssop leaves
Dried hyssop is sometimes steeped in hot water to make a herbal tisane.
The chef added a few leaves of hyssop to enhance the salad's flavor.
They grew hyssop alongside other herbs in the kitchen garden.
Hyssop oil is sometimes added to perfumes for its fresh scent.
She grows hyssop in her garden for both cooking and medicinal use.
The herbal tea was flavored with hyssop and lemon.

  • synonym: oregano - aromatic herb used in cooking
