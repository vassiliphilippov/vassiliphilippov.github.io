Synonyms

  • roentgenography
    9.9 0.00/m
    roentgenography uses X-rays to make internal images, while radiography also includes images made with other radiation types.
  • X-ray photography
    3.8 0.00/m
    X-ray photography produces images with X-rays like photographic film, while radiography is the broader medical or scientific imaging process.
  • X-raying
    5.5 0.02/m
    X-raying gets images of body parts by using X-rays, while radiography is the formal name for this imaging process.
  • skiagraphy
    9.9 0.00/m
    skiagraphy produces images using radiation beyond visible light, while radiography is the common medical term for such imaging.
