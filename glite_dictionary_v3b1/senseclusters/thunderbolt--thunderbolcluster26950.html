thunderbolt

noun
shocking surprise; sudden shock
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈθʌndərˌboʊlt / (AmE), / ˈθʌndəbəʊlt / (BrE)
a sudden and unexpected event that causes great surprise or shock

Examples

A thunderbolt of news
Her sudden decision to move abroad came as a thunderbolt to her friends.
It was a thunderbolt when she revealed the family secret during dinner.
The announcement of his resignation was a real thunderbolt to the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bombshell - something that causes great surprise or shock
  • synonym: shock - a sudden upsetting or surprising event
