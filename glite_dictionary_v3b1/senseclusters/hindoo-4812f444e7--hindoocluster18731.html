Hindoo

noun
archaic term for an Indian or Hindu
Pronunciation: / hɪnˈdu /; / ˈhɪn.duː / (AmE), / hɪnˈduː /; / ˈhɪn.duː / (BrE)
(archaic) a person from India or a follower of Hinduism; an old spelling of 'Hindu'
