a knack for

noun
natural talent for something
Pronunciation: / ə næk fɔr / (AmE), / ə næk fə / (BrE)
a special natural ability or talent for doing something well

Examples

A knack for cooking
After years of practice, he developed a knack for cooking delicious meals.
James really has a knack for picking thoughtful gifts for his friends.
She has a knack for solving complex problems quickly and efficiently.
