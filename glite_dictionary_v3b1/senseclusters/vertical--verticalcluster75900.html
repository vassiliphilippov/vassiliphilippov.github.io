vertical

adjective
upright; at right angles to the horizon
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈvɝː.tɪ.kəl /; / ˈvɝː.t̬ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˈvɜː.tɪ.kəl / (BrE)
