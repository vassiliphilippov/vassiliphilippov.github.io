detrital

adjective
of dead organic matter
8.3
Pronunciation: / dɪˈtraɪtəl / (AmE), / dɪˈtraɪtəl / (BrE)
relating to or consisting of detritus, especially dead organic matter or decomposed plant and animal material.

Examples

Detrital organic matter
The riverbed was covered with a thick detrital layer made up of decaying leaves and twigs.
Scientists studied the detrital material to understand the ecosystem's nutrient cycle.
Many soil organisms depend on detrital particles for food and shelter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inorganic - not consisting of or deriving from living matter
  • synonym: organic - relating to or derived from living matter
