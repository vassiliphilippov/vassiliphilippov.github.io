first symptom

noun
earliest sign of illness
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝst ˈsɪmptəm / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ˈsɪmptəm / (BrE)
the earliest observable sign or indication of a medical condition

Examples

The first symptom of illness
A persistent cough may be the first symptom of a respiratory infection.
Fatigue is often the first symptom people notice when they are coming down with the flu.
For many patients, pain is the first symptom that prompts them to see a doctor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: early sign - an initial indication or evidence of an issue or change
  • degree lower: symptom - a sign or indication of a disease or condition
