ski rack

noun
holder for skis on a vehicle
Pronunciation: / ski ˌræk / (AmE), / ski ˌræk / (BrE)
a device attached to a vehicle for securely holding skis during transport

Examples

A ski rack on car
After buying new skis, she invested in a sturdy ski rack for her SUV.
I mounted my ski rack before heading to the mountains for the weekend.
The car's ski rack can hold several pairs of skis securely.
