enfeoffment

noun
granting of land under feudal law
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈfef.mənt/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈfef.mənt/ (BrE)
under the feudal system, the granting of land to a person in exchange for a pledge of service, usually formalized by a legal deed
