fallenness

noun
state of collapse
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔlənnəs / (AmE), / ˈfɔːlənnəs / (BrE)
the state of having fallen down, collapsed, or being in a ruined condition

Examples

Statue's fallenness
The fallenness of the old statue was a sad reminder of the earthquake.
After the storm, the fallenness of the trees blocked the main road for days.
Historians studied the fallenness of the ancient city to understand its decline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collapse - a sudden failure or falling down
  • synonym: ruin - the state of being destroyed or severely damaged
  • antonym: uprightness - the state of being upright or standing
