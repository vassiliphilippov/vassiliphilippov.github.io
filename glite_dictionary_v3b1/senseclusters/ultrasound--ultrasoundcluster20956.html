Synonyms

  • ultrasonography
    5.1 0.10/m
    ultrasonography refers to the technical imaging procedure using sound in clinics, while ultrasound is the familiar, shorter name people use.
  • sonography
    5.6 0.09/m
    sonography is a medical practice using high-frequency sound to image organs and fetuses, while ultrasound is the common everyday term.
  • echography
    7.4 0.00/m
    echography names the image made by reflected sound in some texts, while ultrasound is the more common term used by doctors and patients.
  • US
    3.7 0.00/m
    US is an abbreviation doctors write in medical notes for the imaging procedure, while ultrasound is the full spoken and written word.
