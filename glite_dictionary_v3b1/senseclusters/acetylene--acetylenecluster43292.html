acetylene

noun
a colorless flammable gas used in welding
7.1
Pronunciation: / əˈsɛt͡lˌin / (AmE), / əˈsiːtɪliːn / (BrE)
a colorless flammable gas (C2H2), used chiefly in welding and in organic synthesis

Examples

Acetylene gas cylinder
Always check for gas leaks when working with acetylene cylinders.
Highly pressurized acetylene is commonly used in oxyacetylene welding for cutting metals.
The laboratory prepared acetylene by reacting calcium carbide with water.
