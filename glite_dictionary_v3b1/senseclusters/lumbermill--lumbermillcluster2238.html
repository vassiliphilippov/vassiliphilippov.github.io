lumbermill

noun
place where logs are made into lumber
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌmbɚˌmɪl / (AmE), / ˈlʌmbəˌmɪl / (BrE)
a building or facility where logs are processed into lumber

Examples

The lumbermill processes logs
After the storm, the fallen trees were taken to the lumbermill to be cut and prepared.
The lumbermill on the outskirts of town has been operating for over fifty years.
They shipped the timber to the lumbermill for processing into planks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sawmill - a mill where logs are sawed into boards
