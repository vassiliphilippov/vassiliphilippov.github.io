daggered

adjective
marked with † symbol
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdæɡərd / (AmE), / ˈdæɡəd / (BrE)
marked with a dagger symbol (†), often to indicate a footnote, cross-reference, or special status in a text or list

Examples

A daggered name
The names of deceased authors were daggered in the bibliography for clarity.
In the list, all outdated products are daggered to show they are discontinued.
Each daggered entry in the document refers to a special note at the bottom of the page.
