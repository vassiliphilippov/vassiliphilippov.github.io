monolingually

adverb
in a monolingual manner
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːnəˈlɪŋɡwəli / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəˈlɪŋɡwəli / (BrE)
In a manner involving or using only one language.

Examples

To speak monolingually
Instructions were given monolingually, so non-native speakers struggled to understand.
She prefers reading monolingually even when translations are available.
The children were raised monolingually until they began school.
