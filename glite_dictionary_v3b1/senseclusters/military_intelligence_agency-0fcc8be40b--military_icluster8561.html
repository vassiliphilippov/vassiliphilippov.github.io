military intelligence agency

noun
organization gathering and analyzing military information
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri ɪnˈtɛlədʒəns ˈeɪdʒənsi / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns ˈeɪdʒənsi / (BrE)
An organization within a country's armed forces responsible for gathering, analyzing, and using information of military significance.

Examples

After years of covert training, she joined a military intelligence agency as an analyst.
Reforms aimed to improve oversight and transparency within the nation's military intelligence agency.
