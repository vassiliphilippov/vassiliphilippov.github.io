multilayer

adjective
having multiple layers
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌleɪər / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌleɪə / (BrE)
having more than one layer placed on top of each other

Examples

Multilayer cake
The multilayer cake had chocolate, vanilla, and strawberry sections stacked together.
Scientists developed a multilayer material to improve insulation in buildings.
He bought a multilayer jacket to stay warm during the winter hike.
