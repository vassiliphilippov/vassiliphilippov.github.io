marmite

noun
large cooking pot, often with legs
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrˌmaɪt /; / ˈmɑːrˌmaɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɑː.maɪt /; / ˈmɑːmaɪt / (BrE)
a large, heavy pot (usually metal) used for cooking soup or stew, or the soup or stew cooked in it
