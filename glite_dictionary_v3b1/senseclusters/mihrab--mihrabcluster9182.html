mihrab

noun
prayer niche in a mosque or on a prayer rug
Pronunciation: / miˈrɑb /; / ˈmiː.rɑːb / (AmE), / ˈmiː.hræb /; / ˈmiː.rɑːb / (BrE)
a niche or decorative design in a mosque wall or on a Muslim prayer rug that shows the direction of Mecca for prayer
