carbonless

adjective
without carbon
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrbənləs / (AmE), / ˈkɑːbənləs / (BrE)
not containing carbon atoms or carbon-based compounds; made without carbon.

Examples

Carbonless material
The laboratory required carbonless containers to avoid contamination during the experiment.
They developed a carbonless process to produce cleaner chemicals for industrial use.
Some specialized papers are designed to be carbonless for sensitive scientific applications.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carbon-based - containing or made from carbon
  • synonym: carbon-free - not containing any carbon
