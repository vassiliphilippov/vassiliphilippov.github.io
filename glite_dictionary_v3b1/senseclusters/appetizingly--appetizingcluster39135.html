appetizingly

adverb
in an appealing manner
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈæpəˌtaɪzɪŋli /; / ˈæpɪˌtaɪzɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈæpɪˌtaɪzɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that makes food look or smell appealing or tempting

Examples

The advertisement appetizingly described the new smartphone's features and design.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deliciously - in a way that tastes or smells very pleasant
  • antonym: unappealingly - in a way that is not attractive or tempting
  • antonym: unappetizingly - in a way that does not look or smell appealing to eat
