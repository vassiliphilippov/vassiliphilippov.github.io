warm

adjective
describing colors like red, orange, or yellow
Pronunciation: / wɔrm / (AmE), / wɔːm / (BrE)
(of a color) having a tone that suggests heat, such as red, orange, or yellow.

Examples

Warm colors
She prefers decorating her living room with warm shades like red and orange.
Sunset skies are often filled with warm hues that evoke comfort and energy.
The artist chose a palette of warm colors to create a lively atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cool - (of a color) having a tone that suggests calm or coldness, such as blue or green
