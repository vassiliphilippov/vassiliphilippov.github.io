military formation

noun
arrangement of troops
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri fɔrˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri fɔːˈmeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
an arrangement of soldiers or military units in a particular pattern or structure for a specific purpose

Examples

A military formation parade
During the parade, tanks rolled through the city in a precise military formation.
The general ordered the troops to break their military formation and take cover.
The soldiers advanced in a tight military formation across the field.
