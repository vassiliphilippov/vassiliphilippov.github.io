overmodest

adjective
excessively or affectedly modest
9.5 6.3
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ˈmɑː.dɪst/ /; / /oʊ.vər.ˈmɑː.dəst/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.ˈmɒd.ɪst/ /; / /əʊ.və.ˈmɒd.əst/ (BrE)
Displaying excessive modesty, often to the point of being affected or insincere, especially in a playful or provocative manner.

Examples

An overmodest person
His overmodest behavior sometimes made people suspect he was not genuine.
She gave an overmodest smile, clearly fishing for compliments.
The actor's overmodest acceptance speech was charmingly self-effacing.
