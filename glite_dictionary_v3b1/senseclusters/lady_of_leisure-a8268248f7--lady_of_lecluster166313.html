lady of leisure

noun
woman with much free time; not working
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪdi əv ˈliʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈleɪdi əv ˈlɛʒə / (BrE)
a woman who does not have a paid job and has ample free time to enjoy leisure activities

Examples

A lady of leisure
After winning the lottery, she became a true lady of leisure and traveled the world.
Although she calls herself a lady of leisure, she manages several charity organizations.
Many novels from the 19th century describe the lifestyle of a lady of leisure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: man of leisure - a man who does not have to work and enjoys a leisurely lifestyle
