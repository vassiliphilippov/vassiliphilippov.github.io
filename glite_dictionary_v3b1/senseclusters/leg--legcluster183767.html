leg

noun
part of trousers covering the leg
2.4
Pronunciation: / lɛɡ / (AmE), / lɛɡ / (BrE)
the part of a pair of trousers that covers one of the wearer's legs

Examples

The leg of jeans
A hole appeared in the leg of his jeans after he tripped.
She rolled up the leg of her trousers to wade through the stream.
The tailor shortened the leg to give the pants a better fit.
