windowsill

noun
horizontal base of a window frame
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪn.doʊ.sɪl / (AmE), / ˈwɪn.dəʊ.sɪl / (BrE)
A narrow shelf or horizontal structure at the base of a window, forming part of the window frame.

Examples

A windowsill shelf
A cat was sitting on the windowsill watching the birds outside.
Condensation had gathered along the windowsill after the chilly night.
She placed a pot of bright tulips on the windowsill.
