pent-up

adjective
suppressed; not expressed (esp. emotions)
9.5 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈpent.ʌp/ (AmE), /ˈpent.ʌp/ (BrE)
(of emotions, energy, etc.) having been held back or suppressed and not expressed or released.

Examples

Pent-up emotions
After months of isolation, she finally released her pent-up frustrations in a long conversation with her friend.
The child's pent-up excitement was obvious when he saw the presents.
Years of pent-up anger eventually led to an emotional outburst.
