spice cake

noun
cake flavored with spices
3.2
Pronunciation: / spaɪs keɪk / (AmE), / spaɪs keɪk / (BrE)
A cake flavored with a mixture of spices, such as cinnamon, nutmeg, or cloves.

Examples

A spice cake slice

The aroma of spice cake filled the kitchen as it baked in the oven.
Would you like a slice of spice cake with your tea?
