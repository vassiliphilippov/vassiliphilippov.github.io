reexpression

noun
restating in new form
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪkˈsprɛʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːɪkˈsprɛʃən / (BrE)
the act of expressing something again, often in a different way or form

Examples

Clear reexpression of ideas
The reexpression of the theory helped students understand the concept more easily.
After some confusion, the manager's reexpression of the instructions clarified the task.
Her reexpression of the poem gave it a fresh and modern feel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paraphrase - a restatement of something using different words
  • synonym: restate - to say something again in a different way
