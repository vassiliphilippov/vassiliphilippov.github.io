self-supporting

adjective
financially independent
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf səˈpɔrtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌself səˈpɔːtɪŋ / (BrE)
Able to financially support oneself without the help of others.

Examples

Self-supporting person
After college, he became self-supporting and moved into his own apartment.
Most scholarships aim to help students become self-supporting adults.
She takes pride in being self-supporting and never asks her parents for money.
