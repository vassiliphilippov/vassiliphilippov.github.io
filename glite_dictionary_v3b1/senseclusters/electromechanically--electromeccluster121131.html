electromechanically

adverb
by electrical and mechanical means
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊməˈkænɪkli / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊməˈkænɪkli / (BrE)

The timing system operates electromechanically to ensure precise coordination.
