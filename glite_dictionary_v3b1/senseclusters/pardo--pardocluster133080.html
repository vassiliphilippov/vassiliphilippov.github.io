pardo

noun
brownish-gray color
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːdəʊ / (BrE)
a brownish-gray or tawny color, often used to describe a dull or muted shade between brown and gray

Examples

The old photograph had faded to a pardo tone over the years.
