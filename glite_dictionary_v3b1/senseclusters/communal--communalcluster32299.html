communal

adjective
relating to a local district or community
4.3
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjunl̩ / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːn(ə)l / (BrE)
relating to a local administrative district or community, especially with reference to local government, services, or elections

Examples

Communal council meeting
Every town has its own communal council responsible for local affairs.
He attended a meeting of the communal authorities to discuss public projects.
The government held communal elections in all provinces last year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: municipal - relating to a town or local government area
