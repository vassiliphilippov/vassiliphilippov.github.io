slop

noun
wet animal feed made from kitchen scraps
3.0
Pronunciation: / slɑp / (AmE), / slɒp / (BrE)
wet, unappetizing food, especially for animals, or weak, watery food or drink

Examples

Pig slop
Regulations now restrict what kind of slop can be given to livestock.
She collected kitchen slop to feed the animals after dinner.
The farmer poured a bucket of slop into the pigsty each morning.
After tasting the slop in the cafeteria, he lost his appetite completely.
The kitchen staff dumped the leftover slops into buckets for the pigs.
The refugees were given nothing but slop to eat during the crisis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: swill - kitchen refuse given to pigs as food
