vocal organ

noun
organ involved in speech production
3.8 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / Ààvo äk…ôl Àà…îr…°…ôn / (AmE), / Ààv…ô äk…ôl Àà…îÀê…°…ôn / (BrE)
any of the organs, such as the larynx, tongue, lips, or palate, involved in producing speech sounds.

Examples

The vocal organ system
Damage to any vocal organ can affect a person's ability to speak clearly.
Speech therapists study the function of each vocal organ to help patients recover their voices.
The larynx is a major vocal organ responsible for producing sound.

Synonyms

  • organ of speech
    4.4 0.01/m
    organ of speech is a formal anatomical term used in textbooks and papers, while vocal organ is a general name for parts that make speech.
  • speech organ
    4.1 0.00/m
    speech organ means body parts that shape speech sounds, while vocal organ is a more formal, medical term for those parts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: larynx - voice box in the throat involved in speech
