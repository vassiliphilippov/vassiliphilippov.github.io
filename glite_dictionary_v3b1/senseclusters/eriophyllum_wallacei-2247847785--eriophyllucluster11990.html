Eriophyllum wallacei

noun
species of small woolly-flowered desert plant
Pronunciation: / ˌɛri.oʊˈfɪliəm ˈwɒləsi / (AmE), / ˌɛri.əʊˈfɪliəm ˈwɒləsi / (BrE)
