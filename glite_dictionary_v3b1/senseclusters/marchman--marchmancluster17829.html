marchman

noun
protest march participant
6.5 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…ërt Ém…ôn / (AmE), / Ààm…ëÀêt Ém…ôn / (BrE)
a person who takes part in a public march or demonstration, especially as a protester

Examples

Protest marchman
The police kept a close watch on each marchman as the protest moved through the city.
Every marchman in the demonstration carried a placard calling for equal rights.
As a dedicated marchman, she attended rallies to support environmental causes.

Synonyms

  • demonstrator
    3.4 7.33/m
    demonstrator joins public protests or marches to show strong views, while marchman is a person who takes part in such events.
  • marcher
    3.4 1.13/m
    marcher walks in a procession, parade, or protest, while marchman specifically refers to someone taking part in a public march or demonstration.
  • protester
    2.9 15/m
    protester shows public objection, often at rallies, while marchman is a participant in a march or demonstration, not only objecting.
