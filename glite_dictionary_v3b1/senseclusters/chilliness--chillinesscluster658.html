chilliness

noun
lack of warmth or enthusiasm
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪliːnəs / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪlɪnɪs / (BrE)
a lack of warmth or enthusiasm in manner or feeling; coldness or reserve in attitude or response

Examples

Social chilliness
Despite his usual chilliness, he tried to be polite to the guests.
Her unexpected chilliness during the meeting surprised everyone.
There was a noticeable chilliness in her voice when she replied.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coldness - lack of warmth or enthusiasm; aloofness
  • antonym: warmth - friendliness or enthusiasm in manner or feeling
