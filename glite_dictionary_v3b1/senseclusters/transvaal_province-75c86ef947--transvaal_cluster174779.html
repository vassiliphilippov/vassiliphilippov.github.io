Transvaal Province

proper noun
former South African province
Pronunciation: / trænzˈvɑl ˈprɑvɪns / (AmE), / tranzˈvɑːl ˈprɒvɪns / (BrE)
a former province in northeastern South Africa, originally inhabited by Bantu-speaking Africans and later colonized by the Boers

Transvaal Province history
