curse the weather

verb
express anger at the weather
6.2
Pronunciation: / kɝs ðə ˈwɛðɚ / (AmE), / kɜːs ðə ˈwɛðə / (BrE)
to express frustration or anger at the weather, especially when it causes inconvenience or problems

Examples

To curse the weather often
Every time it rains on her picnic day, she can't help but curse the weather.
I try not to curse the weather, but the endless rain is getting to me.
The hikers cursed the weather as the storm ruined their plans.
