noun
digital buy-to-own (media)
7.7 0.08/m
Pronunciation: / i \u025bs ti / (AmE), / i\u02d0 \u025bs ti\u02d0 / (BrE)
Abbreviation of Electronic Sell-Through: a digital purchase model in which consumers buy and permanently own a copy of digital media (for example, a film or album).

Examples

EST movie purchase
The studio reported strong EST sales after releasing the movie on digital storefronts.
Consumers prefer EST when they want to permanently own a downloaded film or TV show.
Many platforms offer movies both for rent and as EST purchases to suit different customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digital download - a downloaded copy of digital media that a consumer can purchase and keep
  • synonym: electronic sell-through - the full term for the digital buy-to-own model, where purchased media is owned permanently
