suavely

adverb
in a suave or charming manner
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑːvli / (AmE), / ˈswɑːvli / (BrE)
In a smooth, confident, and charming manner; with suavity.

Examples

To speak suavely
He suavely navigated the awkward conversation with effortless charm.
She suavely introduced herself to the guests, immediately winning them over.
The spokesman answered every difficult question suavely, never losing his composure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: awkwardly - in a manner lacking grace or smoothness
  • synonym: suave - (of a person or manner) charming, confident, and elegant
