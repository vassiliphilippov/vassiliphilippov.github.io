icemaker

noun
ice supplier
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈaɪsˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person or company that produces, harvests, or supplies ice, especially for sale or delivery

Examples

Icemaker delivers ice
The icemaker delivered several large bags of ice to the café before the morning rush.
During the summer festival, the local icemaker was in high demand.
We called the icemaker to supply ice for the outdoor wedding reception.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ice manufacturer - a company that produces ice on a large scale
  • synonym: ice supplier - a business or person that provides ice to customers
