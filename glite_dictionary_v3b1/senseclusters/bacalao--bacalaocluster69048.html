bacalao

noun
cod used as food
Pronunciation: / ˌbɑːkəˈlaʊ / (AmE), / ˌbakəˈlaʊ / (BrE)
cod fish, especially as used for food in Spanish and Latin American cuisine, not necessarily salted or dried

Examples

Fresh bacalao fillet
The chef prepared a delicious stew using fresh bacalao from the market.
In many Spanish recipes, bacalao is the main ingredient, often cooked with tomatoes and olives.
They bought salted bacalao to make a traditional dish for the holiday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cod - a type of large marine fish used for food
