goat willow

noun
a broad-leaved European willow tree
6.2 7.2
Pronunciation: /goʊt ˈwɪl.oʊ/ /; / /gəʊt ˈwɪl.oʊ/ (AmE), /gəʊt ˈwɪ.ləʊ/ /; / /goʊt ˈwɪl.oʊ/ (BrE)
a much-branched Old World willow tree with large catkins and relatively broad leaves, native to Europe and Asia (Salix caprea).

Examples
