tea

noun
gossip; juicy information
8.9 2.65/m
Pronunciation: / tiÀê / (AmE), / tiÀê / (BrE)
Informal slang for gossip, personal or sensational information about someone or something, often private or scandalous.

Examples

Spill the tea
When she heard about the breakup, she couldn't wait to share the tea with her friends.
On the forum, users post the latest tea about celebrities and political scandals every hour.
She whispered the tea into my ear, and I immediately sent a private message to confirm.
