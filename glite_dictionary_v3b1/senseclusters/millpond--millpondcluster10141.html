millpond

noun
pond supplying water to a mill
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪl.pɑnd / (AmE), / ˈmɪl.pɒnd / (BrE)
a pond created by damming a stream to provide water power for operating a mill

Examples

A millpond pond
In winter, children would skate on the frozen millpond near the village.
Over time, the millpond silted up and became a rich habitat for wildlife.
The old watermill still stands beside the millpond, surrounded by reeds and willows.
