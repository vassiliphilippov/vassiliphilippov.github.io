bloodflower

noun
tropical ornamental plant
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌdˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈblʌdˌflaʊə / (BrE)
a tropical plant with bright red or orange flowers, often grown as an ornamental in gardens

Examples

A bloodflower plant
The gardener planted a bloodflower to add vibrant color to the backyard.
Butterflies are often attracted to the bright blossoms of the bloodflower.
She admired the striking red petals of the bloodflower during her walk through the botanical garden.
