stretcher

noun
device for stretching items
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɛtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈstrɛtʃə / (BrE)
a device used to make something larger or looser by stretching it, such as shoes or gloves

Examples

Shoe stretcher
A glove stretcher was needed to fit her hands comfortably.
He used a stretcher to loosen his new leather shoes.
Some shoe stores offer a stretcher service for tight footwear.
