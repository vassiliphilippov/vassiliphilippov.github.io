glandular disorder

noun
disorder affecting glands
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæn.dʒʊ.lɚ dɪsˈɔr.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡlæn.djʊ.lə dɪsˈɔː.də / (BrE)
A medical condition involving abnormal function or disease of one or more glands in the body.

Examples

A glandular disorder diagnosis
A family history of glandular disorder may increase your risk of developing similar conditions.
Certain glandular disorders can cause symptoms like fatigue and weight changes.
The doctor diagnosed her with a glandular disorder that required ongoing treatment.
