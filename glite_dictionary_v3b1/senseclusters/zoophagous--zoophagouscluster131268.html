zoophagous

adjective
feeding on animals; carnivorous
5.9
Pronunciation: / zoʊˈɑːfəɡəs / (AmE), / zəʊˈɒfəɡəs / (BrE)
(of an animal) feeding on other animals; carnivorous.

Examples

Zoophagous animals
Many spiders are zoophagous, preying on insects and other small arthropods.
Some amphibians become zoophagous as they mature, shifting from herbivorous larvae to carnivorous adults.
The zoophagous habits of lions are well documented by biologists.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carnivorous - feeding on animals
  • antonym: herbivorous - feeding on plants
  • antonym: omnivorous - feeding on both plants and animals
