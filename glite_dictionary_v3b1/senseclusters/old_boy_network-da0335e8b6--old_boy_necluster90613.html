old boy network

noun
informal professional support network based on social ties
5.2 6.3
Pronunciation: /oʊld bɔɪ ˈne.twɜːrk/ /; / /əʊld bɔɪ ˈne.twɜːrk/ (AmE), /əʊld bɔɪ ˈne.twɜːk/ (BrE)
an informal system where men with similar backgrounds, especially from the same schools or social groups, use their connections to help each other professionally, often excluding outsiders
