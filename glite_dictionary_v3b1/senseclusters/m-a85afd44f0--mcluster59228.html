M

noun
a megabyte (1,000,000 bytes)
5.8
Pronunciation: / ɛm / (AmE), / ɛm / (BrE)
a unit of digital information, such as a megabyte (1,000,000 bytes) or mebibyte (1,048,576 bytes)

Examples

2 M file size
Each photo occupies about 2 M of storage space.
The file size is over 10 M, so it may take a while to download.
Your email attachment must not exceed 5 M.
Each file on this server is less than 1 M in size.
The database has grown to over 800 M since last year.
This video will take up about 450 M on your hard drive.

  • synonym: mebibyte - A unit of digital information equal to 2^20 (1,048,576) bytes
