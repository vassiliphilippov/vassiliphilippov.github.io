Synonyms

  • megabyte
    3.0 0.5/m
    megabyte is a decimal unit of about one million bytes used on disks, while M is a general unit label that can mean different sizes.
  • mebibyte
    8.7 0.00/m
    mebibyte is a binary unit of 1,048,576 bytes used by programs and tools, while M is a general unit label that can mean either type.
