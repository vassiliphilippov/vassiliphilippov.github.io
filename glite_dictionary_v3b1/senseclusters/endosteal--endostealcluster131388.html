endosteal

adjective
inside bone
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈdɑstiəl / (AmE), / ɛnˈdɒstiəl / (BrE)
located within or relating to the inner surface of a bone

Examples

Endosteal surface of bone
The surgeon placed an endosteal implant directly into the patient's jawbone.
An endosteal layer lines the inner surface of long bones in the body.
Dentists often recommend endosteal implants for patients who have lost teeth.

Related Terms
