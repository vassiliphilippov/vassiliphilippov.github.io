splashboard

noun
plank on boat to stop splashing
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsplæʃˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈsplæʃˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a protective plank or barrier along the side of a boat, designed to prevent water from splashing over the gunwale.

Examples

Boat splashboard
The fisherman repaired the damaged splashboard before setting out to sea again.
The old sailboat’s splashboard was worn, letting spray onto the deck in rough weather.
Waves hit the side of the boat, but the splashboard kept most of the water out.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gunwale - the upper edge of the side of a boat
