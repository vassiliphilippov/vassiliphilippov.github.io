Nancere language

noun
a Chadic language of Chad
Pronunciation: / ˈnænˌsɛr ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈnanˌsɛə ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A Chadic language spoken in Chad, particularly in the Tandjile Region, Tandjile Ouest department, and the Béré, Delban, and Kélo subprefectures.

Examples

Nancere language spoken
He is fluent in French as well as the Nancere language.
Researchers are working to document the grammar of the Nancere language.
The Nancere language is spoken by several communities in southwestern Chad.
