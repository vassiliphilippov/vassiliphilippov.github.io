intersessional

adjective
between sessions
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈsɛʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈsɛʃənəl / (BrE)
happening or existing between sessions of a meeting, course, or official body

Examples

Intersessional meeting
The committee scheduled an intersessional meeting to address urgent issues before the next official session.
During the intersessional period, students were encouraged to review the material independently.
Several important decisions were made at the intersessional conference held between annual meetings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interim - temporary or occurring between events
  • degree lower: sessional - relating to a session or sessions
