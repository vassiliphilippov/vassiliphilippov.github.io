renvoi

noun
textual cross-reference
Pronunciation: / rɑ̃ˈvwɑ / (AmE), / rɒ̃ˈvwɑː / (BrE)
a cross-reference in a text, index, or bibliography that directs the reader to another place for information

Examples

Renvoi in a glossary
The glossary included a helpful renvoi to related terms for further reading.
A renvoi in the bibliography guided readers to the main article on the subject.
Without a clear renvoi, it was difficult to find the detailed explanation in the document.
