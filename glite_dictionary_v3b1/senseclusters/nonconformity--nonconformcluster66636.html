nonconformity

noun
lack of harmony or correspondence
4.9 6.0
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.kən.ˈfɔːr.mə.tiː/ (AmE), /nɒn.kən.ˈfɔː.mə.tiː/ (BrE)
The state of not being in agreement or harmony with something, especially a lack of correspondence between things.

Examples

Nonconformity with standards
His proposal was rejected due to its nonconformity with established procedures.
The inspector noted several nonconformities in the building's structure.
There was a clear nonconformity between the design and the client’s instructions.

  • synonym: incongruity - the state of being out of place or lacking harmony
