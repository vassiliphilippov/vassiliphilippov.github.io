solo homer

noun
home run with no runners on base
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊloʊ ˈhoʊmər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊləʊ ˈhəʊmə / (BrE)
A home run hit when there are no runners on base, resulting in only one run scored.

Examples

A solo homer hit
Despite his impressive solo homer, the team still lost the game.
He hit a solo homer in the seventh inning to break the tie.
The team's only score came from a solo homer by their star player.
