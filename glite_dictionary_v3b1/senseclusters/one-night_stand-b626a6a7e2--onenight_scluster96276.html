one-night stand

noun
single-night performance or show
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌn naɪt ˌstænd / (AmE), / ˈwʌn naɪt ˌstænd / (BrE)
a performance, show, or entertainment held at a particular place on only one night

Examples

A one-night stand concert
After the one-night stand, the musicians packed up and left for the next city.
The touring theatre company scheduled a one-night stand in our town.
Their jazz band was booked for a one-night stand at the historic club.
