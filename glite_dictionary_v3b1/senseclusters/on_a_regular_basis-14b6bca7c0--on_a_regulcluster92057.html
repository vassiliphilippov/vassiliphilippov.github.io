on a regular basis

adverb
repeatedly and consistently
Pronunciation: / ɑn ə ˈrɛɡjələr ˈbeɪsɪs / (AmE), / ɒn ə ˈrɛɡjʊlə ˈbeɪsɪs / (BrE)
happening repeatedly with a consistent pattern or frequency.

Examples

To do on a regular basis
It's important to back up your files on a regular basis to prevent data loss.
She goes for a walk on a regular basis to stay healthy.
Team meetings are held on a regular basis every Monday morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: regularly - at regular intervals; frequently and consistently
