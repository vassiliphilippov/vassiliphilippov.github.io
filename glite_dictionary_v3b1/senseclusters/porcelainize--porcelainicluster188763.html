porcelainize

verb
to coat with porcelain or porcelain-like layer
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔrs(ə)lənˌaɪz / (AmE), / ˈpɔːsəlɪˌnaɪz / (BrE)
to coat something with a layer of porcelain or with a surface resembling porcelain

Examples

To porcelainize a surface
Many antique bathtubs were porcelainized to give them a smooth, glossy finish.
Technicians carefully porcelainize the surface to improve its appearance and durability.
The laboratory will porcelainize the metal crown to match the surrounding teeth.
