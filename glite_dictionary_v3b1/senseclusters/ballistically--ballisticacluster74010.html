ballistically

adverb
extremely angrily
7.9
Pronunciation: / bəˈlɪstɪkli / (AmE), / bəˈlɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in an extremely angry, agitated, or explosively emotional manner

Examples

React ballistically
He reacted ballistically when he found out his car had been towed without warning.
The coach went ballistically after the referee made a controversial call during the game.
She responded ballistically to the unexpected criticism from her manager.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmly - in a peaceful or relaxed manner
  • synonym: furiously - in a very angry or intense way
