blanky

noun
child's blanket
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈblæŋki / (AmE), / ˈblæŋki / (BrE)
an informal or affectionate term for a child's blanket, often used for comfort

Examples

A child's blanky
The little girl refused to go anywhere without her blanky in her arms.
He cried until his mother found his favorite blanky under the bed.
At nap time, every child grabbed their blanky and settled down quietly.
