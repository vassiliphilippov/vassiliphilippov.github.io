sodomitical

adjective
morally corrupt
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɑːdəˈmɪtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌsɒdəˈmɪtɪkəl / (BrE)
morally depraved, corrupt, or wicked; used as a strong condemnation of vice or immorality.

Examples

Sodomitical society
The novel depicted a sodomitical society where corruption was widespread.
He was accused of leading a sodomitical lifestyle by his harshest critics.
The preacher warned against the sodomitical influences he believed were tearing families apart.
