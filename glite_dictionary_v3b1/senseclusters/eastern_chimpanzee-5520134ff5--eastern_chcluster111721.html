eastern chimpanzee

noun
subspecies of chimpanzee from east-central Africa
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈiːstərn tʃɪmˈpænzi / (AmE), / ˈiːstən tʃɪmˈpænzi / (BrE)
a subspecies of chimpanzee native to east-central Africa, characterized by long hair and closely related to central chimpanzees

Examples

The eastern chimpanzee population
Because of habitat loss, the population of the eastern chimpanzee has declined in recent decades.
Researchers studied a group of eastern chimpanzees in their natural habitat.
The eastern chimpanzee is found mainly in Uganda and western Tanzania.

Related Terms
