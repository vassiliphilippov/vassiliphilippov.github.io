sprawl out

verb
lie or sit spread out in a relaxed way
Pronunciation: / sprɔl aʊt / (AmE), / sprɔːl aʊt / (BrE)
to lie or sit with arms and legs spread out in a relaxed or careless way

Examples

To sprawl out on couch
After a long day, he liked to sprawl out on the couch and watch TV.
She found her cat sprawled out across the bed, taking up all the space.
The children sprawled out on the grass, enjoying the summer sun.

Related Terms

  • antonym: curl up - to lie or sit with the body bent into a rounded shape
  • synonym: stretch out - to extend one's body or limbs to full length
