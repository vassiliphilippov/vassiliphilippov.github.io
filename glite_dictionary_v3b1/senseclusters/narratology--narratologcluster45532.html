narratology

noun
study of narratives
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnærəˈtɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌnærəˈtɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the academic study of narrative structure and the ways stories are told

Examples

Study of narratology
She decided to specialize in narratology for her graduate studies in literature.
The professor introduced key concepts of narratology to help students analyze different novels.
Modern narratology explores how stories are structured across various media.

Related Terms

  • synonym: literary theory - the study of the methods and principles used in interpreting literature
  • synonym: narrative - a spoken or written account of events; a story
