shitpost

noun
low-quality or provocative online post
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪtˌpoʊst / (AmE), / ˈʃɪt.pəʊst / (BrE)
An online post intended to be of low quality, humorous, or provocative, often for the sake of trolling or disrupting discussion.

Examples

A funny shitpost
He admitted that his comment was just a shitpost meant to get a reaction.
Some communities embrace shitposts as part of their online culture.
The forum was flooded with shitposts after the controversial announcement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meme - A humorous image, video, or piece of text spread online
  • degree lower: spam - Unsolicited or irrelevant online messages
  • synonym: troll - A person who deliberately posts inflammatory or off-topic messages online
