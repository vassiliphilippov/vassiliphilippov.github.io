can

verb
preserve food in a can or tin
8.5
Pronunciation: / kæn / (AmE), / kæn / (BrE)
to preserve food by sealing it in an airtight can or tin, usually after heating to destroy bacteria

Examples

To can tomatoes
Learning how to can tomatoes properly requires paying attention to food safety guidelines.
My grandmother likes to can peaches every summer with fresh fruit from her orchard.
Vegetables that are canned at home can last for months without refrigeration.
