railwayman

noun
male railway employee
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪlˌweɪmæn / (AmE), / ˈreɪlweɪmən / (BrE)
a man who works for a railway, especially as an employee involved in its operation or maintenance.

Examples

A railwayman worker
During the strike, every railwayman in the region walked off the job.
His father was a proud railwayman who worked on the tracks for over thirty years.
The railwayman greeted passengers with a friendly smile every morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: railroad worker - a person who works for a railway or railroad
  • antonym: railwaywoman - a woman who works for a railway
