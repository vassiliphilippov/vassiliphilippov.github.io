mistakable

adjective
easily confused with something else
4.0
Pronunciation: / mɪˈsteɪkəbəl / (AmE), / mɪˈsteɪkəbl̩ / (BrE)
easily confused with or mistaken for something else

Examples

Highly mistakable product
Because the packaging was so similar, the generic product was highly mistakable for the brand-name version.
Certain poisonous mushrooms are mistakable for edible varieties, so foragers must be careful.
The twins' voices are so alike that they are easily mistakable on the phone.
