juvenile-onset diabetes

noun

A form of diabetes mellitus occurring in children or young people, characterized by early onset, the need for insulin treatment, and symptoms such as excessive thirst and urination.

Examples

Juvenile-onset diabetes in children
Doctors diagnosed her with juvenile-onset diabetes when she was only eight years old.
Many families learn to recognize the early symptoms of juvenile-onset diabetes in children.
Regular insulin injections are necessary to manage juvenile-onset diabetes effectively.
