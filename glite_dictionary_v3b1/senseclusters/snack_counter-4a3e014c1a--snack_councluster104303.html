snack counter

noun
place serving snacks and drinks
Pronunciation: / snæk ˈkaʊntɚ / (AmE), / snæk ˈkaʊntə / (BrE)
a small, inexpensive bar or counter where light meals and drinks are served

Examples

The snack counter area
During the break, students lined up at the snack counter for chips and juice.
The museum has a snack counter where visitors can buy coffee and pastries.
We grabbed a sandwich and a soda at the snack counter before the movie started.
