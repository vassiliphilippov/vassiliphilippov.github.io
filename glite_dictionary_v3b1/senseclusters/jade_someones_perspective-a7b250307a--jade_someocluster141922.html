jade someone's perspective

verb
make someone cynical or weary
4.7
Pronunciation: / dʒeɪd ˈsʌmwʌnz pərˈspɛktɪv / (AmE), / dʒeɪd ˈsʌmwənz pəˈspɛktɪv / (BrE)
to make someone cynical, weary, or less open-minded due to experience or overexposure

Examples

To jade someone's perspective

It's important not to let negative experiences jade your perspective on future opportunities.
Years of bitter negotiations eventually jaded his perspective on international cooperation.
