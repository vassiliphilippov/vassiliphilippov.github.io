amitotic

adjective
of cell division without mitosis
8.0 8.7
Pronunciation: /ə.mɪ.ˈtɑː.tɪk/ /; / /eɪ.mɪ.ˈtɑː.tɪk/ (AmE), /ə.mɪ.ˈtɒ.tɪk/ /; / /eɪ.mɪ.ˈtɒ.tɪk/ (BrE)
Relating to cell division by simple splitting of the nucleus without the formation of chromosomes or a mitotic spindle.

Examples

Amitotic cell division
Most muscle cells reproduce through an amitotic process rather than mitotic division.
The presence of amitotic nuclei is a distinguishing feature in certain tissues.
Unlike mitotic division, amitotic cell division lacks chromosome visibility.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mitotic - relating to or characterized by mitosis (typical cell division)
