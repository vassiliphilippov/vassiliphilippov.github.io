outflux

noun
outward flow
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌflʌks / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌflʌks / (BrE)
a flow or movement of something outward from a place

Examples

Outflux of people
The outflux of people from the city increased after the new policy was announced.
Scientists measured the outflux of gases from the volcano during the eruption.
A sudden outflux of capital can destabilize a country's economy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: influx - a flow of something into a place
  • synonym: outflow - a movement of something out of a place
