Heimdall

proper noun
Marvel Comics / MCU guardian of the Bifrost
Pronunciation: / \u02c8ha\u026am\u02ccd\u0251l / (AmE), / \u02c8ha\u026amd\u0251\u02d0l / (BrE)
(in Marvel Comics and the Marvel Cinematic Universe) a fictional Asgardian character who guards the Bifrost and serves as the watchman of Asgard.
