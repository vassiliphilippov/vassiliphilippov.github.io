parve

adjective
food with no meat or dairy (Judaism)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːrvə / (AmE), / ˈpɑːvə / (BrE)
(of food) containing neither meat nor milk, and therefore permitted to be eaten with either according to Jewish dietary laws.

Examples

Parve food
A parve soup can be enjoyed with either a dairy or a meat dish.
Many desserts in the bakery are labeled as parve so they can be served after a meat meal.
You must check the packaging to make sure the margarine is parve before using it in a kosher kitchen.

  • antonym: meaty - containing meat (Judaism)
