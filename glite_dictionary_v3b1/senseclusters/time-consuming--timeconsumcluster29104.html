(of a task or process) taking a lot of time and often requiring patience.

Examples

Time-consuming task
Cooking a traditional meal from scratch can be time-consuming.
Filling out the application form proved more time-consuming than expected.
Sorting all these files is a very time-consuming task.

Synonyms

  • lengthy
    3.5 11/m
    lengthy means lasting for a long time and often feels tedious, while time-consuming emphasizes needing much time and effort.
  • drawn-out
    4.6 0.80/m
    drawn-out describes events that are stretched and tedious, while time-consuming stresses that a task needs a lot of time to finish.
  • protracted
    6.6 2.92/m
    protracted is a more formal word for something made to last longer, while time-consuming focuses on the time and patience needed.
