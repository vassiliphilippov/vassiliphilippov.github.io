hootenanny

noun
unnamed object
Pronunciation: / ˈhuːtəˌnæni / (AmE), / ˈhuːtənæni / (BrE)
an informal term for an object or device whose name is unknown or forgotten

Examples

A handy hootenanny
Can you hand me that hootenanny on the shelf next to the toolbox?
I used a small hootenanny to fix the loose screw in the chair.
Whenever I forget the name of a tool, I just call it a hootenanny.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whatchamacallit - an informal term for something whose name is not known
