Mecoptera

noun
order of carnivorous insects with beaklike heads
Pronunciation: / məˈkɑːptərə / (AmE), / məˈkɒptərə / (BrE)
an order of carnivorous insects typically having long membranous wings and elongated, beaklike heads with chewing mouthparts at the tip

Examples

The Mecoptera insect
Scientists discovered several new species of Mecoptera in the remote forest region.
