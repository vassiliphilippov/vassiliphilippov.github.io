business agent

noun
agent managing business affairs, esp. for workers
4.1 4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪznəs ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person who manages business affairs for another, especially one who represents employees in dealings with employers

Examples

Business agent manages contracts
As a business agent, she oversees contracts and resolves disputes between employees and management.
The business agent met regularly with employers to discuss working conditions and salaries.
The union appointed a business agent to negotiate with the company on behalf of its members.
