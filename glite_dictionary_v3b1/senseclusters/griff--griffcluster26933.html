griff

noun
mythical lion-eagle
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡrɪf / (AmE), / ɡrɪf / (BrE)
a mythical creature with the body of a lion and the head and wings of an eagle

Examples

Artists often painted the griff as a symbol of strength and protection.
In the legend, a single griff watched over the mountain pass for centuries.
