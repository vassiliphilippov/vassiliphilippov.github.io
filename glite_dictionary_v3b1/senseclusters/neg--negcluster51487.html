neg

noun
photographic negative
9.3
Pronunciation: / nɛɡ / (AmE), / nɛɡ / (BrE)
a photographic negative; a piece of film showing an image with light and dark areas reversed.

Examples

Old neg film
He carefully placed each developed neg into sleeves for long-term storage.
The photographer scanned the old negs to create digital copies of her work.
You need the original neg to make a high-quality print of the photo.

Related Terms

  • synonym: negative - a photographic image with reversed light and dark areas
  • antonym: print - a positive photographic image made from a negative
