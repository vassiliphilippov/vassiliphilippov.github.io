monkey

noun
a long-tailed primate
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki / (BrE)
A small to medium-sized primate with a long tail, belonging to a group distinct from prosimians and apes.

Examples

A monkey climbs trees
A curious monkey watched us from the branches above.
Scientists often study the behavior of monkeys to understand primate intelligence.
The zoo has several species of monkey in their rainforest exhibit.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ape - a primate without a tail or with a very short tail
