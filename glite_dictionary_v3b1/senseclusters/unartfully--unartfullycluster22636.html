unartfully

adverb
without skill or grace
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɑrtfəli / (AmE), / ʌnˈɑːtfəli / (BrE)
in a way that lacks skill, grace, or artistry

Examples

Unartfully explains a plan
He unartfully tried to explain his mistake, making the situation even more confusing.
The painting was unartfully restored, leaving visible marks and uneven colors.
She unartfully dodged the question, making her intentions obvious to everyone.
