Santa Ana

noun
hot, dry wind in southern California
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌsæntə ˈænə / (AmE), / ˌsæntə ˈænə / (BrE)
a strong, hot, dry wind that blows from the deserts of southern California toward the Pacific coast, typically in winter and autumn

Examples

Santa Ana winds blow
Every autumn, the Santa Ana sweeps through Los Angeles, bringing unseasonably warm temperatures.
Residents prepared for the arrival of the Santa Ana by clearing dry brush from their yards.
The Santa Ana winds are expected to increase the risk of wildfires this week.
