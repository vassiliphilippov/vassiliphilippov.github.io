Germania

proper noun
ancient name for Germany
Pronunciation: / dʒərˈmeɪniə / (AmE), / dʒɜːˈmeɪniə / (BrE)
the ancient Roman name for the region that is now Germany and surrounding areas

Examples

Ancient Germania region
The Roman Empire expanded its borders to include parts of Germania in the first century AD.
Historians study ancient maps to understand how Germania was perceived by the Romans.
Tacitus wrote a famous book describing the customs of the tribes living in Germania.
