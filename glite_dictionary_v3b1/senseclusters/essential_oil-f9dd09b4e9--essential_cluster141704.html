essential oil

noun
aromatic oil from plants for fragrance or flavor
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈsen.ʃəl ɔɪl/ /; / /ə.ˈsen.ʃəl ɔɪl/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈsen.ʃəl ɔɪl/ /; / /ɪ.ˈsen.ʃəl ɔɪ.əl/ (BrE)
An aromatic oil obtained from a plant, containing its characteristic fragrance or flavor, used in perfumes, flavorings, and aromatherapy.

Examples
