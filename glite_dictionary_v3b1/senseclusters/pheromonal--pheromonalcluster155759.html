pheromonal

adjective
relating to pheromones
Pronunciation: / ˌfɛrəˈmoʊnəl / (AmE), / ˌfɛrəˈməʊnəl / (BrE)
relating to or caused by pheromones, the chemicals animals use to communicate with each other

Examples

pheromonal trail
The pheromonal trail left by the ants guided the rest of the colony to the food source.
Scientists studied the pheromonal responses of moths to better understand their mating behavior.
Some perfumes claim to have pheromonal effects that attract others.
