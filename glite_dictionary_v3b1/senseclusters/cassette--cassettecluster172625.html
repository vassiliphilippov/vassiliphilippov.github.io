cassette

noun
magnetic tape in a plastic case
3.8
Pronunciation: / kəˈsɛt / (AmE), / kəˈsɛt / (BrE)
A small, flat case containing a length of magnetic tape, used for recording and playing back sound or video.

Examples

A cassette tape
He used to record songs from the radio onto a cassette.
She found an old cassette of her favorite band in the attic.
The film is available on DVD and cassette.
