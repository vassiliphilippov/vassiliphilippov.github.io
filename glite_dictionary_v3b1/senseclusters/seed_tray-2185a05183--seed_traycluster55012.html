seed tray

noun
container for sowing seeds and seedlings
2.8 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / sid tre…™ / (AmE), / siÀêd tre…™ / (BrE)
a shallow container used for sowing seeds and raising young plants or cuttings, especially in horticulture

Examples

A seed tray with compost
After filling the seed tray with compost, she carefully spaced out the tomato seeds.
Each seed tray should be labeled to keep track of the different plant varieties.
We placed the cuttings in a seed tray to encourage root growth before transplanting them.

Synonyms

  • flat
    7.2 0.70/m
    flat is a shallow box used in greenhouses to start many seedlings, while seed tray is a general container for sowing and raising young plants.
  • seedbox
    4.0 0.00/m
    seedbox is a container used for holding or storing seeds and sometimes sowing, while seed tray is mainly for sowing and growing young plants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: propagator - covered container used for raising seedlings in a protected environment
