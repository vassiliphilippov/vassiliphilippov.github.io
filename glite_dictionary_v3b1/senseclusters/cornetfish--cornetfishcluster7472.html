cornetfish

noun
slender tropical fish with tubular snout
7.3 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnɪtˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnɪtˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
A slender tropical marine fish with a long tubular snout and bony plates in place of scales, belonging to the family Fistulariidae.
