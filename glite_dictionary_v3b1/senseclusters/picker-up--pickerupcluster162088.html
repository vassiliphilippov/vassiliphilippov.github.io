picker-up

noun
person who collects items, esp. litter
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪkərˈʌp / (AmE), / ˌpɪkəˈʌp / (BrE)
a person who collects or gathers items, especially rubbish or litter, from a particular place

Examples

A picker-up of rubbish
She became known as the local picker-up after organizing weekly beach sweeps.
The picker-up of the litter was praised by the community for their efforts.
Volunteers acted as picker-ups during the city's annual park cleanup.
