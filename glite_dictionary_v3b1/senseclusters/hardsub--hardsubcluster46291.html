hardsub

verb
embed permanent subtitles
8.4 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / c8h51rdccs8cb / (AmE), / c8h51d0dccs8cb / (BrE)
(in video production) to add subtitles permanently into a video's image so they cannot be turned off or removed.

Examples

To hardsub a movie
The studio chose to hardsub the international release to avoid subtitle compatibility issues.
Before uploading, he hardsubbed the interviews so viewers would always see the translated text.
They are hardsubbing older footage that lacks a separate subtitle track for streaming.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burn in - to permanently add text or graphics into video frames (often used for subtitles).
  • antonym: softsub - to add subtitles as a separate file or stream that can be turned on or off (opposite of hardsub).
