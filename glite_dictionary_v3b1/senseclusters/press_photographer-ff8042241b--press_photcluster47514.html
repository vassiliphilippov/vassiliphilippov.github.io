press photographer

noun
news media photographer
3.2 2.8
Pronunciation: /pres fə.ˈtɑː.grə.fər/ (AmE), /pres fə.ˈtɒ.grə.fə/ (BrE)
a photographer who takes photographs for newspapers or other news media, usually to accompany news stories

Examples

Press photographers take pictures

She worked as a press photographer for a leading national newspaper.
The press photographer arrived early to capture the politicians entering City Hall.
