wrenchingly

adverb
with violent force
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛntʃɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈrɛntʃɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that involves a sudden, forceful twist or pull

Examples

Wrenchingly open the door
He pulled the door open wrenchingly when it got stuck in the frame.
The mechanic wrenchingly loosened the rusted bolt after several attempts.
She twisted the lid wrenchingly until it finally came off the jar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forcibly - using physical force or strength
  • antonym: gently - in a soft or mild manner
