ruckle

verb
make wrinkles or folds
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌkəl / (AmE), / ˈrʌkəl / (BrE)
to make wrinkles or folds in something, especially by pressing, creasing, or gathering it

Examples

To ruckle a shirt
Be careful not to ruckle your shirt while ironing it.
She tried not to ruckle the tablecloth as she smoothed it over the table.
The movement of the bed sheets would often ruckle the blanket at the foot of the bed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crease - to make a line or mark by folding or wrinkling
  • synonym: wrinkle - to form small lines or folds on a surface
