rift

verb
to split apart
4.1
Pronunciation: / rɪft / (AmE), / rɪft / (BrE)
to split or break apart, especially in a dramatic or forceful way

Examples

To rift the earth
The powerful earthquake rifted the valley, creating a deep chasm between the two sides.
Over time, tectonic forces rift the land, forming new geological features.
A sudden explosion rifted the rock, scattering debris across the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crack - to break without dividing into separate parts
  • antonym: join - to bring or come together
  • synonym: split - to break or divide into parts
