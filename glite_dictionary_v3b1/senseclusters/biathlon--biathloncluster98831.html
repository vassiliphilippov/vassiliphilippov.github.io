biathlon

noun
two-discipline contest
3.8
Pronunciation: / baɪˈæθlɑn / (AmE), / baɪˈæθlən / (BrE)
a sporting event or contest consisting of two different disciplines or tests, often held together as a single competition

Examples

A biathlon event
The winter biathlon combines cross-country skiing and rifle shooting in a single race.
She trained for months to compete in the summer biathlon at the local sports festival.
The festival included a creative biathlon where painters had to sketch and then write short stories.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duathlon - a sporting event with two disciplines, usually running and cycling
  • degree higher: triathlon - a sporting event with three different disciplines
