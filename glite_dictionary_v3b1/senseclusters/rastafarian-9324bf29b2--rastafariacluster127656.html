Rastafarian

adjective
relating to Rastafarianism or Rastafarians
Pronunciation: / ˌrɑːstəˈfɛriən / (AmE), / ˌrastəˈfɑːrɪən / (BrE)
of or relating to Rastafarianism or its followers.

Examples

Rastafarian culture
Many Rastafarian beliefs emphasize peace, equality, and a connection to Africa.
She wore her hair in Rastafarian dreadlocks to express her cultural identity.
The Rastafarian community has a rich tradition of music and spiritual practice.
