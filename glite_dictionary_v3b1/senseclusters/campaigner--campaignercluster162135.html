campaigner

noun
person seeking election to office
6.6 3.1
Pronunciation: /kæm.ˈpeɪn.ər/ (AmE), /kæm.ˈpeɪn.ə/ (BrE)
a person who actively works in a campaign, especially for political or social change, including someone seeking election

Examples

A campaigner for change
As a dedicated campaigner, she traveled extensively to meet with voters across the state.
The campaigner gave a passionate speech at the town hall meeting.
The campaigner promised to address healthcare reform if elected.
The campaigner collected signatures to support the new environmental law.
As a dedicated campaigner, she organized rallies to raise awareness about human rights.
Many young campaigners use social media to spread their message quickly.
