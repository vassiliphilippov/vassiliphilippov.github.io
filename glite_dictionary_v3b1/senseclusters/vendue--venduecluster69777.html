vendue

noun
public sale to highest bidder; auction
6.4
Pronunciation: / vɛnˈdu / (AmE), / vɛnˈdjuː / (BrE)
a public sale in which goods or property are sold to the highest bidder; an auction

Examples

A vendue sale
During the colonial period, public vendues were common methods of selling property.
He attended the vendue hoping to acquire rare antiques at a good price.
The contents of the estate will be sold at a vendue next week.
