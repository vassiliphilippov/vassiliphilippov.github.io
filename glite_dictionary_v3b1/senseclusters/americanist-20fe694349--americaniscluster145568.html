Americanist

noun
scholar of the Americas
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛrɪkənɪst / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkənɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies the cultures, languages, or history of the Americas

Examples

An Americanist scholar
The Americanist delivered a lecture on pre-Columbian civilizations in South America.
As an Americanist, she focuses on the preservation of indigenous languages.
Many Americanists attend international conferences to share their research findings.
The conference brought together many leading Americanists from universities around the world.
As an Americanist, she specializes in the indigenous languages of South America.
Several Americanists contributed articles to the new encyclopedia on Latin American history.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anthropologist - a person who studies human societies and cultures
  • antonym: europeanist - a person who studies the cultures or history of Europe
  • synonym: historian - a person who studies or writes about history
