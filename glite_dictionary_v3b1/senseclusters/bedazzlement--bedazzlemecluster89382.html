bedazzlement

noun
state of amazement
Pronunciation: / bɪˈdæzəlmənt / (AmE), / bɪˈdæzlmənt / (BrE)
a state of being greatly impressed, amazed, or dazzled by something very bright or impressive

Examples

Total bedazzlement
The magician's performance left the audience in complete bedazzlement.
She stared at the sparkling jewels with a sense of bedazzlement.
The city's lights at night filled him with bedazzlement and awe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astonishment - a feeling of great surprise or wonder
  • antonym: indifference - lack of interest or concern
  • synonym: wonder - a feeling of amazement and admiration
