shunter

noun
a small locomotive for moving railway cars
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌntər / (AmE), / ˈʃʌntə / (BrE)
A small railway locomotive used for moving train cars short distances, especially within a rail yard, but not for long trips.

Examples

A shunter locomotive
A diesel shunter is stationed near the depot for moving passenger coaches.
During the night shift, the shunter operates to assemble trains for the morning schedule.
The shunter quickly rearranged the freight cars in the busy rail yard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: switcher - a small locomotive used for switching train cars, chiefly American term
