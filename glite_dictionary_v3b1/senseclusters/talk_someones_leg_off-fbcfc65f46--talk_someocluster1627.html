talk someone's leg off

verb
talk to someone at great length
Pronunciation: / tɔk ˈsʌmwʌnz lɛg ɔf / (AmE), / tɔːk ˈsʌmwʌnz lɛg ɒf / (BrE)
to talk to someone for a very long time, often excessively or to the point of tiring them

Examples

To talk your leg off
Be careful mentioning movies around Tim—he'll talk your leg off about his favorites.
If you get him started on sports, he'll talk your leg off for hours.
She managed to talk my leg off during the entire train ride.
