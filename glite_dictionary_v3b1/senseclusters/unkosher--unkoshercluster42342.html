unkosher

adjective
not kosher
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkoʊʃər / (AmE), / ʌnˈkəʊʃə / (BrE)
not in accordance with Jewish dietary law; not fit or permitted to be eaten or used under kosher rules

Examples

Unkosher food
The chef explained that the dish was unkosher because it contained both meat and dairy.
Some products in the store are clearly labeled as unkosher for those who follow dietary laws.
He accidentally bought unkosher snacks, so he could not serve them at the event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forbidden - not allowed by law or rules
  • antonym: kosher - in accordance with Jewish dietary law
