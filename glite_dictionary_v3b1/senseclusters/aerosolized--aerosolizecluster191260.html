aerosolized

adjective
converted to or existing as an aerosol
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr.əˌsɑː.laɪzd /; / ˈɛr.oʊ.səˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈɛə.rəˌsɒ.laɪzd /; / ˈɛː.rə.səˌlaɪzd / (BrE)
in the form of ultramicroscopic solid or liquid particles dispersed or suspended in air or gas

Examples

Aerosolized particles

Many medications can be aerosolised to allow patients to inhale them directly into their lungs.
