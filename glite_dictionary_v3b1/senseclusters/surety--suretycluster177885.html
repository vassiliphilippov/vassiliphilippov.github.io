surety

noun
certainty; something established
6.6 0.25/m
Pronunciation: / Àà É är…ôti / (AmE), / Àà É…îÀêr…ôti / (BrE)
the state of being certain or assured; something that is clearly established or beyond doubt

Examples

A sense of surety
His calm expression gave me a feeling of surety in our decision.
We lacked the surety that our plan would succeed, so we revised it further.
You can move forward with the project now that there is surety about the funding.
