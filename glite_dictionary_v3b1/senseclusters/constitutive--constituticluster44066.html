constitutive

adjective
forming an essential part or element

forming an essential part or element of something; having the power to establish or give existence to something.

Examples

A constitutive element
A constitutive element of the theory is its emphasis on cooperation.
Genetic factors are constitutive in determining eye color.
Water is constitutive of all living organisms.

Synonyms

  • constituent
    6.5 2.87/m
    constituent is an essential part that physically makes up something, while constitutive helps create a thing's basic form or existence.
  • constitutional
    5.9 0.27/m
    constitutional relates to the basic nature or rules of something, while constitutive means forming or bringing that thing into being.
  • organic
    3.9 3.66/m
    organic is an inherent part that grows naturally within a system, while constitutive means it forms or establishes the system's existence.
