creaseproof

adjective
resistant to creasing (of fabrics)
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːsˌpruːf / (AmE), / ˈkriːsˌpruːf / (BrE)
(of fabric) resistant to wrinkling; not likely to form creases easily.

Examples

Creaseproof fabric
She prefers buying creaseproof clothing to minimize ironing.
The creaseproof pants stayed smooth even after a long day of wear.
This shirt is made from creaseproof fabric, making it perfect for travel.
