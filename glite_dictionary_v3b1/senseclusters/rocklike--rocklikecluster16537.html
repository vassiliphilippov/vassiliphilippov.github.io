rocklike

adjective
resembling rock; especially hard or solid
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑkˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈrɒkˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling rock, especially in being hard, solid, or durable

Examples

Rocklike hardness
She set her jaw in a rocklike determination that nothing could shake.
The ancient statue felt rocklike to the touch, cold and unyielding.
The mountain's surface was rocklike, making it difficult for plants to take root.
