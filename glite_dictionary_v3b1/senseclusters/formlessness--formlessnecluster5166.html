formlessness

noun
lack of shape
7.2 0.12/m
Pronunciation: / Ààf…îÀêrml…ôsn…ôs / (AmE), / Ààf…îÀêml…ôsn…ôs / (BrE)
the quality of lacking a clear or definite shape, structure, or form

Examples

The formlessness of clouds
The formlessness of the clouds made them look like ever-changing shapes in the sky.
Artists sometimes use formlessness to create a sense of mystery in their work.
The formlessness of the liquid allowed it to fill any container it was poured into.

Synonyms

  • shapelessness
    6.1 0.03/m
    shapelessness is the state of having no clear or attractive shape, while formlessness is the broader quality of lacking definite structure or form.
  • amorphousness
    7.8 0.03/m
    amorphousness describes lacking a definite shape or character in a scientific or formal way, while formlessness sounds more general and plain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amorphousness - the quality of being without a definite shape or form
  • synonym: shapelessness - the state of having no distinct shape
  • antonym: structure - the arrangement or organization of parts to form a whole
