yellow-crowned night heron

noun
North American heron with a yellow crown
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌjɛloʊ ˈkraʊnd ˈnaɪt ˌhɛrən / (AmE), / ˌjɛləʊ ˈkraʊnd ˈnaɪt ˌhɛrən / (BrE)
a North American heron (Nyctanassa violacea) with a distinctive yellow crown, active mostly at night

Examples

The yellow-crowned night heron
Photographers traveled to the wetland to capture images of the rare yellow-crowned night heron in its natural habitat.
The yellow-crowned night heron is often seen hunting for crabs along the Gulf Coast in the evening.
Unlike other herons, the yellow-crowned night heron is most active after sunset.
