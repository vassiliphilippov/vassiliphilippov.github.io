Sandinista

noun
Nicaraguan revolutionary
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌsændəˈnistə / (AmE), / ˌsændɪˈniːstə / (BrE)
a member of the Nicaraguan revolutionary group or political movement known as the Sandinistas
