mummichog

noun
small Atlantic coast killifish
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌmɪˌtʃɑɡ / (AmE), / ˈmʌmɪtʃɒɡ / (BrE)
A small, silver-and-black killifish found in saltwater marshes along the Atlantic coast of the United States.

Examples

Small mummichog fish
Fishermen sometimes use mummichog as bait for larger fish in saltwater marshes.
She spotted a school of mummichogs darting through the tidal creek.
The mummichog is often studied by scientists because of its adaptability to harsh environments.
