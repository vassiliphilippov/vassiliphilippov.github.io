aspect

verb
to look at carefully
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈæspɛkt / (AmE), / ˈæspɛkt / (BrE)
to look at or regard something, especially in a careful or formal way (archaic or formal)

Examples

She aspected the painting
She aspected the painting carefully before deciding what to say.
The judge aspected the evidence with a critical eye.
He aspected the landscape, searching for any sign of movement.
