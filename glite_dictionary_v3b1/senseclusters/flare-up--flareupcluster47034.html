flare-up

noun
sudden intense occurrence
3.7 4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfleər.ʌp/ (AmE), /ˈfleər.ʌp/ (BrE)
a sudden and intense occurrence, especially of something undesirable such as violence, emotion, or activity

Examples

A flare-up of violence
After weeks of calm, a flare-up of hostilities threatened the peace talks.
My allergies had a serious flare-up after I visited the park.
There was a sudden flare-up of violence in the city last night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: subsidence - a gradual sinking or lessening of intensity
