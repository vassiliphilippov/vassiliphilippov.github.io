uncrumple

verb
to become smooth again
4.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkrʌmpəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈkrʌmpəl / (BrE)
to make something smooth again, or to become smooth again, after it has been crumpled or wrinkled

Examples

To uncrumple a shirt

The artist uncrumpled the canvas and started painting again.

After being packed in a suitcase, he tried to uncrumple his shirt for the meeting.
The artist uncrumpled the paper to reuse it for a new sketch.
