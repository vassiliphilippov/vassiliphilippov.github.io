demanding

adjective
requiring much effort or skill
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmændɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈmɑːndɪŋ / (BrE)
Requiring a lot of effort, time, attention, or skill.

Examples

A demanding job
Her new boss is extremely demanding and expects perfection from the whole team.
Raising twins can be demanding both physically and mentally.
The training program was so demanding that only a few participants completed it.
