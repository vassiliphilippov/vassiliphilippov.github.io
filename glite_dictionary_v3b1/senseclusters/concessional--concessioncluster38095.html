concessional

adjective
on easier terms
6.8
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɛʃənəl / (AmE), / kənˈsɛʃənəl / (BrE)
offered on more favorable or easier terms than usual, often to help certain groups or individuals

Examples

Concessional loan rates
The bank offers concessional interest rates to students applying for education loans.
Many developing countries receive concessional aid from international organizations.
Farmers benefited from concessional prices on seeds and fertilizers this season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: preferential - giving an advantage or more favorable treatment
  • antonym: standard - usual or regular, not offering special advantages
