An intersection where a railway line and a road cross at the same level, often controlled by barriers or signals when a train is approaching.

Examples

A grade crossing gate

Several accidents have occurred at the old grade crossing due to poor visibility.
The town installed new signals at the grade crossing to improve safety for both cars and trains.
