cappuccino coffee

noun
espresso-based coffee with steamed milk
4.7 1.7
Pronunciation: / kæpəˈtʃinoʊ ˈkɑfi / (AmE), / kæpəˈtʃiːnəʊ ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
A drink made from equal parts espresso and steamed milk, often topped with foamed milk and sometimes cinnamon or nutmeg.

Examples

A cappuccino coffee drink
After lunch, he enjoyed a rich cappuccino coffee with friends in the plaza.
She ordered a cappuccino coffee with extra cinnamon at the café.
The barista expertly prepared a foamy cappuccino coffee for the customer.
