unconventional

adjective
not conforming to accepted customs or rules
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.kənˈvɛn.ʃə.nəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈvɛnʃənl /; / ˌʌn.kənˈvɛn.ʃə.nəl / (BrE)
not following what is usual, accepted, or traditional
