propose

verb
nominate for office or position
5.2
Pronunciation: / prəˈpoʊz / (AmE), / prəˈpəʊz / (BrE)
to suggest someone for appointment to an office, job, or honor; to nominate

Examples

To propose a candidate
At the meeting, someone proposed him for the position of club president.
The committee decided to propose her as a candidate for the board of directors.
They plan to propose Dr. Wilson for a national science award next year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nominate - to formally suggest someone for a job or position
