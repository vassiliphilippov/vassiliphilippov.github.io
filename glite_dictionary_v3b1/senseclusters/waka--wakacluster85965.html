waka

noun
Māori canoe
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːkə / (AmE), / ˈwɒkə / (BrE)
a Māori canoe, especially a large carved canoe used for transport, fishing, ceremonies, or warfare

Examples

A carved waka canoe
The tribe paddled the carved waka across the harbor during the ceremony.
A traditional waka was displayed at the entrance of the museum.
They built a new waka for the annual river festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boat - a small vessel for traveling on water
  • synonym: canoe - a narrow boat pointed at both ends and moved with a paddle
