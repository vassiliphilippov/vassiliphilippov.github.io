saliferous

adjective
containing or yielding salt
Pronunciation: / səˈlɪfərəs / (AmE), / səˈlɪfərəs / (BrE)
(of rock, soil, etc.) containing or producing salt, especially in significant or exploitable quantities.

Examples

Saliferous rock layer
A saliferous layer was found beneath the surface during geological surveys.
Extensive saliferous deposits have been discovered in the region.
The soil is highly saliferous, making it unsuitable for most crops.

Related Terms

  • synonym: saline - containing or impregnated with salt
