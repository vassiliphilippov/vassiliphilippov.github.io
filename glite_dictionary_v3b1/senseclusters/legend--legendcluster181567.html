legend

noun
famous person known for great achievements
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛdʒənd / (AmE), / ˈlɛdʒənd / (BrE)
a person who is famous and admired for their extraordinary achievements or abilities

Examples

A sports legend
After decades of innovation, Marie Curie is considered a legend in science.
He became a legend for his breathtaking performances on the football field.
Many young musicians aspire to one day be called a legend by their fans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hero - a person admired for their courage, outstanding achievements, or noble qualities
  • synonym: icon - a person or thing widely admired especially for having great influence
  • antonym: nobody - a person of no importance or influence
