soccer ball

noun
ball used in soccer
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑkər bɔl / (AmE), / ˈsɒkə bɔːl / (BrE)
An inflated spherical ball used in playing the game of soccer.

Examples

A soccer ball
After practice, she cleaned mud off her soccer ball before going home.
He kicked the soccer ball across the field and scored a goal.
The children took turns passing the soccer ball during recess.
