dyeing

noun
coloring with dye
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of applying dye to change the color of a material, especially fabric or hair, permanently

Examples

To dye fabric
She spent the afternoon dyeing her old T-shirts bright red and blue.
The dyeing of wool requires careful attention to achieve an even color.
Traditional dyeing techniques are still practiced in many rural communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coloring - the act of adding color to something
