coffee-table book

noun
large, decorative book for display
5.9 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑː.fiː ˈteɪ.bəl bʊk/ /; / /ˈkɔː.fiː ˈteɪ.bəl bʊk/ (AmE), /ˈkɒf.ɪ ˈteɪ.bəl bʊk/ /; / /ˈkɔːf.ɪ ˈteɪ.bəl bʊk/ (BrE)
A large, usually expensive and visually attractive book, often featuring photographs or illustrations, intended to be displayed and browsed casually, especially on a coffee table.

Examples

A coffee-table book
Guests often browse through the coffee-table book while waiting in the living room.
She bought a coffee-table book on architecture as a gift for her friend.
The new art coffee-table book features stunning photographs from around the world.
