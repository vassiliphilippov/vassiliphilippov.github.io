flippy

adjective
lively or bouncy
Pronunciation: / ˈflɪpi / (AmE), / ˈflɪpi / (BrE)
having a lively, bouncy, or flipping quality, often used to describe clothing or movement that moves or swings easily

Examples

Flippy skirt twirling
She wore a flippy skirt that twirled as she danced.
His new haircut has a flippy style that moves when he walks.
The curtains have a flippy edge that waves in the breeze.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bouncy - lively and springy in movement
  • synonym: flowy - hanging loosely and moving easily
