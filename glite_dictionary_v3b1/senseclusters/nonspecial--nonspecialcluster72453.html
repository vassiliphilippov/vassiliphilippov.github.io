nonspecial

adjective
ordinary; not unique
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈspɛʃəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈspɛʃəl / (BrE)
not special; lacking unique, distinctive, or specific qualities

Examples

A nonspecial design
The nonspecial design of the chair makes it suitable for any room.
He preferred a nonspecial meal that everyone could enjoy.
Most of the options on the menu are nonspecial and appeal to a wide range of tastes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ordinary - not unusual or special; normal
  • antonym: special - better, greater, or different from what is usual
