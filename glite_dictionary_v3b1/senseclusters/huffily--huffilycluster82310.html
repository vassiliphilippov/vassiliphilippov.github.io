huffily

adverb
in an annoyed or offended manner
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌfɪli / (AmE), / ˈhʌfɪli / (BrE)

Examples

She replied huffily when asked why she was late to the meeting.
The child huffily refused to eat his vegetables.
