worthiness

noun
the quality of being deserving or valuable
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːði.nəs / (AmE), / ˈwɜː.ði.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of having value, merit, or being deserving of something

Examples

Sense of worthiness
Many struggle with feelings of worthiness in challenging times.
She questioned her own worthiness for the award.
The committee evaluated each applicant's worthiness before making a decision.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unworthiness - the quality of lacking value or merit
