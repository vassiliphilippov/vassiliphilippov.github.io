bilimbi

noun
sour fruit-bearing tree of East India
Pronunciation: / bɪˈlɪmbi / (AmE), / bɪˈlɪmbi / (BrE)
an East Indian evergreen tree (Averrhoa bilimbi) widely cultivated for its very sour, green fruit, used especially in cooking and preserves

Examples

A bilimbi tree
A mature bilimbi tree can produce hundreds of fruits every year.
She used bilimbi to add a tangy note to the fish curry.
The bilimbi is prized in Southeast Asian cuisine for its sharp, acidic flavor.
