trituration

noun
powder from grinding
9.8
Pronunciation: /trɪt.ʃə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ /; / /trɪ.tə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /trɪt.ʃə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ /; / /trɪ.tə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a powder or fine substance produced by grinding or crushing a material into small particles

Examples

A trituration of powder
The pharmacist prepared a trituration of the active ingredient before mixing it with other compounds.
After several hours of grinding, the mineral was reduced to a fine trituration suitable for analysis.
Proper trituration ensures that the medication is evenly distributed in each tablet.
