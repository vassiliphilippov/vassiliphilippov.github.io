pharmaceutic

adjective
relating to pharmacy or pharmacists
Pronunciation: / ˌfɑːrməˈsuːtɪk / (AmE), / ˌfɑːməˈsuːtɪk / (BrE)
relating to pharmacy, pharmacists, or the preparation and dispensing of medicines.
