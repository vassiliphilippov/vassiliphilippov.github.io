laceleaf

noun
aquatic aquarium plant
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪsˌliːf / (AmE), / ˈleɪsˌliːf / (BrE)
an aquatic plant from Madagascar with delicate, net-like leaves, often used in aquariums

Examples

Aquarium laceleaf
The laceleaf added a unique texture to the aquarium with its delicate, see-through leaves.
Many aquarists choose the laceleaf for its striking appearance and easy care.
A healthy laceleaf can become the centerpiece of a freshwater tank.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aponogeton - a genus of aquatic plants including the laceleaf
  • synonym: water lily - an aquatic plant with floating leaves and showy flowers
