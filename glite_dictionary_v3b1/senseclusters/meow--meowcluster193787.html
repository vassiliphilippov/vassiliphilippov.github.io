meow

verb
make a sound like a cat
Pronunciation: / miˈaʊ / (AmE), / miˈaʊ / (BrE)
(of a cat) to make a characteristic crying sound; to utter a meow.

Examples

To meow loudly
My neighbor’s cat meows every morning to be let inside.
She could hear her cat meowing outside the door.
The kitten meowed loudly when it was hungry.
All night long, stray cats would miaou under my window.
She could hear her pet miaou for attention from the hallway.
The little kitten began to miaou softly at the door.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meow - make a cat-like sound
  • synonym: mew - to make a soft crying sound, especially as a cat or gull
