The distance measured from the tip of one wing to the tip of the other wing of an aircraft, bird, or other flying object when fully extended.

Examples

A wide wingspan
A wider wingspan helps gliders stay aloft for longer periods.
The airplane's wingspan was too wide for the small hangar.
The eagle has a wingspan of over two meters, making it one of the largest birds of prey.
