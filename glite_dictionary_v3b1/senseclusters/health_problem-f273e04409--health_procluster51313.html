Synonyms

  • condition
    3.7 65/m
    a condition is often a long-term medical state that needs ongoing care, while a health problem is any issue that stops normal functioning.
  • illness
    1.1 58/m
    an illness is a bodily or mental state with clear symptoms, while a health problem can be any condition that limits daily activity.
  • disease
    2.3 194/m
    a disease is a clear medical disorder that affects a body part with known signs, while a health problem can be broader and less specific.
  • ill health
    3.0 1.00/m
