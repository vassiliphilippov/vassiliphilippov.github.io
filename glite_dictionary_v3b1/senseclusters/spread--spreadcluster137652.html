spread

verb
to cover a surface with a layer
Pronunciation: / sprɛd /; / spɹɛd / (AmE), / sprɛd /; / spɹɛd / (BrE)
to cover, extend, or distribute something over an area or surface; to open or stretch out

Examples

To spread butter
He spread the paint evenly across the wall.
Please spread the icing over the top of the cake.
She used a knife to spread butter on her toast.
He spreads mayonnaise evenly on both slices of bread.
She likes to spread butter on her toast every morning.
The gardener spread compost over the flowerbeds.
An oil slick began to spread across the lake after the accident.
The smell of fresh bread spread through the entire house.
Wildflowers spread over the hillside each spring.
