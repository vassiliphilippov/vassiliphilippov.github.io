dinky

adjective
small and attractive (British informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪŋki / (AmE), / ˈdɪŋki / (BrE)
(British, informal) small and attractive in a neat, pretty way.

Examples

A dinky bracelet
She wore a dinky little bracelet that sparkled in the sunlight.
The cottage looked dinky but was surprisingly comfortable inside.
They served the dessert in dinky glass bowls with matching spoons.
