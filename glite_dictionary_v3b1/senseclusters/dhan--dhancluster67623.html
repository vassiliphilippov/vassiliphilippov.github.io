dhan

noun
unhusked rice plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɑːn / (AmE), / dɑːn / (BrE)
a term for rice plants or unhusked rice, especially used in South Asia

Examples

Harvested dhan sacks
The farmers stored the harvested dhan in large sacks before processing.
Heavy rains threatened to damage the dhan still standing in the fields.
After drying the dhan, the villagers prepared it for milling into rice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paddy - unhusked rice or the rice plant
  • degree higher: rice - the grain of the rice plant, especially after removing the husk
