doxie

noun
dachshund dog
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑksi / (AmE), / ˈdɒksi / (BrE)
an informal term for a small, short-legged dog of the dachshund breed

Examples

A cute doxie
Their playful doxie loves to chase tennis balls around the backyard.
We adopted a friendly doxie from the animal shelter last year.
The doxie barked excitedly whenever someone rang the doorbell.
