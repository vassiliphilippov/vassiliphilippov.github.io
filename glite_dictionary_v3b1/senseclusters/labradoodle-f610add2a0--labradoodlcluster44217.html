Labradoodle

noun
Labrador-Poodle cross
Pronunciation: / ˈlæbrəˌduːdəl / (AmE), / ˈlæbrəˌduːdəl / (BrE)
a dog breed that is a cross between a Labrador Retriever and a Poodle, often known for being friendly and having a curly coat

Examples

A Labradoodle dog
Their family adopted a playful Labradoodle from the local animal shelter last year.
The Labradoodle is popular among people with allergies because of its low-shedding coat.
At the park, a friendly Labradoodle greeted everyone with a wagging tail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Labrador Retriever - a popular breed of retriever dog known for its friendly nature
  • synonym: Poodle - a breed of dog known for its intelligence and curly coat
  • synonym: goldendoodle - a crossbreed dog between a Golden Retriever and a Poodle
