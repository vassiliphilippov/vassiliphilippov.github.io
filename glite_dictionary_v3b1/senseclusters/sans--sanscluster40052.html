sans

particle
without (combining form)
Pronunciation: / sænz / (AmE), / sænz / (BrE)
used as a combining form before words to mean 'without', especially in compounds like 'sans-serif'.

Examples

Sans-serif style
The salad was served sans dressing to accommodate her dietary restrictions.
He arrived at the meeting sans his usual briefcase, looking more relaxed than usual.
They decorated the room sans any balloons or streamers for a minimalist look.

Related Terms

  • antonym: with - having or including something
  • synonym: without - not having or lacking something
