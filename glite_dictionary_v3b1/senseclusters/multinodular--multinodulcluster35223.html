multinodular

adjective
having many lumps
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈnoʊdʒələr / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈnəʊdjʊlə / (BrE)
having or involving many small lumps or nodes, especially in a medical context

Examples

A multinodular mass
The ultrasound revealed a multinodular mass in the patient's thyroid gland.
Doctors often monitor multinodular goiters for changes in size or symptoms.
A multinodular liver can indicate certain chronic diseases.
