luxury

noun
expensive comfort or indulgence
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌkʃəri /; / ˈlʌɡʒəri, ˈlʌkʃəri / (AmE), / ˈlʌkʃəri /; / ˈlʌkʃəri, ˈlʌɡʒəri / (BrE)
a state of great comfort, elegance, or opulence, often involving expensive or unnecessary things
