peaceably

adverb
in a calm, non-violent manner
3.6 0.7/m
Pronunciation: / Ààpis…ôbli / (AmE), / ÀàpiÀês…ôbli / (BrE)
in a way that is calm and does not involve argument, violence, or disturbance; in a peaceable manner

Examples

Settle peaceably
