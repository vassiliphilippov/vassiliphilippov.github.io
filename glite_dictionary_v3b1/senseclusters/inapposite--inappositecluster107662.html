inapposite

adjective
not suitable or relevant
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈæpəzɪt / (AmE), / ɪnˈapəzɪt / (BrE)
not suitable or appropriate in a particular situation; out of place or irrelevant.

Examples

An inapposite remark
He apologized for his inapposite remark during the meeting.
Her comments were completely inapposite to the subject under discussion.
The analogy was interesting, but ultimately inapposite to the problem at hand.

Related Terms

  • antonym: appropriate - suitable or proper in the circumstances
  • synonym: irrelevant - not connected with or relevant to something
