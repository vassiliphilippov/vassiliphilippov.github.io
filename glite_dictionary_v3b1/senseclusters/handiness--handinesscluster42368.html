handiness

noun
manual skill or dexterity
Pronunciation: / ˈhændinɪs / (AmE), / ˈhændinɪs / (BrE)
skill and ease in using the hands; manual dexterity

Examples

Handiness with tools
Developing handiness is essential for artists and craftsmen.
Her handiness with tools impressed everyone in the workshop.
You need a certain level of handiness to fix small appliances at home.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clumsiness - lack of skill or grace, especially with the hands
  • synonym: dexterity - skill in performing tasks, especially with the hands
