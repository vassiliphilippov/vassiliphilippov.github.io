thyroid

noun
gland in the neck secreting hormones
Pronunciation: / ˈθaɪ.rɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈθaɪ.rɔɪd / (BrE)
A large ductless gland in the neck that secretes hormones regulating growth and metabolism.

Examples

Thyroid gland
An underactive thyroid can cause fatigue and weight gain.
He was diagnosed with a thyroid disorder after his blood tests.
The doctor examined her thyroid to check for any swelling.
