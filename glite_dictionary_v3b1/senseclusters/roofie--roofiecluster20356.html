roofie

noun
slang sedative drug
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈruːfi / (AmE), / ˈruːfi / (BrE)
a slang term for a powerful sedative drug (such as Rohypnol) used to secretly make someone unconscious or unable to resist, often for criminal purposes
