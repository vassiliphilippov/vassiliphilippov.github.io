piciform bird

noun
nonpasserine climbing bird group (woodpeckers, etc.)
8.9 8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈpɪk.ɪ.fɔːrm bɜːrd/ /; / /ˈpɪk.ə.fɔːrm bɜːrd/ (AmE), /ˈpɪk.ɪ.fɔːm bɜːd/ /; / /ˈpɪk.ə.fɔːm bɜːd/ (BrE)
Any of a group of nonpasserine insectivorous birds, usually with strong bills for boring wood, including woodpeckers and their relatives.
