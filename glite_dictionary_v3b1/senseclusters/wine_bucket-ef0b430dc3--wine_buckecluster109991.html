wine bucket

noun
bucket for chilling wine bottles
Pronunciation: / waɪn ˈbʌk.ɪt / (AmE), / waɪn ˈbʌk.ɪt / (BrE)
a bucket filled with ice, used to chill and serve a bottle of wine

Examples

A wine bucket with ice
A silver wine bucket stood next to the table during the celebration.
She placed the bottle in a wine bucket filled with ice before serving dinner.
The waiter brought a wine bucket to our table so our white wine would stay cold.
