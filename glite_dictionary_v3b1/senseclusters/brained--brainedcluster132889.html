brained

adjective
hit on the head
9.1
Pronunciation: / breɪnd / (AmE), / breɪnd / (BrE)
having been struck on the head and injured, stunned, or killed by a blow.

Examples

Brained by a ball
The boxer was completely brained by the powerful punch in the final round.
After the accident, he was found brained and barely conscious on the ground.
The victim lay brained at the scene, showing no signs of movement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concussed - having suffered a concussion or head injury
  • antonym: unharmed - not injured or hurt
