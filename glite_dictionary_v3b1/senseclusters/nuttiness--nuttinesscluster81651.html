nuttiness

noun
silliness or craziness
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌtinəs / (AmE), / ˈnʌtinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being silly, strange, or amusingly crazy

Examples

Her nuttiness shines
Her nuttiness always made family gatherings more entertaining.
The comedian's nuttiness was the highlight of the show.
A little nuttiness can make life much more enjoyable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eccentricity - unusual or odd behavior
  • synonym: madness - the state of being mentally ill or extremely foolish
  • antonym: seriousness - the quality of being solemn or thoughtful
