compañero

noun
fellow student
Pronunciation: / kəmˌpɑːnˈjeɪroʊ / (AmE), / kəmˌpænˈjeərəʊ / (BrE)
a fellow student or classmate; someone who attends classes with you

Examples

Class compañero
After moving to a new school, she quickly made friends with her compañero in math class.
Each compañero helped organize the group project for their science course.
He asked his compañero to explain the homework assignment after class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: classmate - a person who is in the same class as you at school or college
  • synonym: friend - a person you know well and like
