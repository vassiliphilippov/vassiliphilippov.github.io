rose hip

noun
fruit of a rose plant
9.6 3.1
Pronunciation: /roʊz hɪp/ (AmE), /rəʊz hɪp/ (BrE)
the small, round fruit of a rose plant, typically red or orange, used in jams, jellies, teas, and for medicinal purposes

Examples

Bright rose hips
After flowering, the bush produced dozens of bright red rose hips.
Many herbal supplements include extracts from rose hips for their purported health benefits.
You can make tea from dried rose hips because they are high in vitamin C.
