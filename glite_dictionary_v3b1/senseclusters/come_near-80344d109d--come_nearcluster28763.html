come near

verb
approach in time
4.8
Pronunciation: / kʌm nɪr / (AmE), / kʌm nɪə / (BrE)
To approach or draw close in time; to be about to happen.

Examples

The time comes near
As the deadline comes near, everyone in the office works overtime to finish the project.
Her graduation day is coming near, and she's getting excited for the ceremony.
Winter came near, bringing shorter days and colder nights.
