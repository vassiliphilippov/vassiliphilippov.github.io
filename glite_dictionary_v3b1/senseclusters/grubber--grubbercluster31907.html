grubber

noun
person or tool that digs
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrʌbər / (AmE), / ˈɡrʌbə / (BrE)
a person or tool that digs or searches for something, especially in the ground

Examples

A grubber tool
The old grubber spent hours looking for coins in the field.
He used a grubber to loosen the soil before planting the seeds.
As a child, she was a keen grubber of interesting rocks and fossils.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digger - a person or machine that digs
  • synonym: searcher - a person who looks for something
