remind

verb
cause someone to remember
Pronunciation: / ɹɪˈmaɪnd / (AmE), / ɹɪˈmaɪnd / (BrE)
to cause someone to remember something; to make someone think of something they must do or have experienced

Examples

To remind about milk
Could you remind me to buy milk on the way home?
She set an alarm to remind herself of the meeting.
This photo reminds me of our last holiday together.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forget - to fail to remember
  • synonym: recall - to bring back a memory into someone's mind
