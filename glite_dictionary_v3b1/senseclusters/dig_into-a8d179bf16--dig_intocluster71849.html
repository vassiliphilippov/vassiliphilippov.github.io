dig into

verb
examine or investigate thoroughly
Pronunciation: / dɪɡ ˈɪntu / (AmE), / dɪɡ ˈɪntu / (BrE)
to examine, explore, or investigate something physically or thoroughly, often by searching beneath the surface

Examples

Curious children sometimes dig into the dirt to look for worms and insects.
The scientists decided to dig into the ancient ruins, hoping to uncover hidden artifacts.
