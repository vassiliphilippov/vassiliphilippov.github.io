superheavy

adjective
extremely heavy
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈhɛvi / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈhɛvi / (BrE)
extremely heavy or having much more weight than usual

Examples

Superheavy machinery
The superheavy machinery required a special crane to lift it into place.
Only a few athletes can compete in the superheavy weight class at the Olympics.
Transporting a superheavy load across the bridge required careful planning and permits.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lightweight - weighing very little
  • synonym: massive - very large and heavy
