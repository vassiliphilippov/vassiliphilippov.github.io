snobbish

adjective
having the attitude of a snob
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɑbɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsnɒbɪʃ / (BrE)
Displaying the attitude or characteristics of a snob; inclined to social exclusiveness and looking down on those considered inferior.

Examples

Snobbish attitude
He dismissed the restaurant as too casual, sounding almost snobbish in his criticism.
Her snobbish attitude made it hard for her to make friends outside her social circle.
People found her snobbish for refusing to talk to anyone not wearing designer clothes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrogant - having an exaggerated sense of one's own importance or abilities
  • antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low view of one's importance
