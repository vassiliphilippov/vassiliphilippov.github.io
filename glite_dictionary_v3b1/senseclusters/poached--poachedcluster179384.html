poached

adjective
cooked by gentle simmering in water
6.6 3.0
Pronunciation: /poʊtʃt/ (AmE), /pəʊtʃt/ (BrE)
(of food) cooked by gently simmering in water or another liquid just below boiling point.

Examples

Poached eggs
I usually prefer my eggs poached with a side of toast.
The chef served salmon poached in white wine and herbs.
Vegetables poached in broth retain more of their flavor and nutrients.
