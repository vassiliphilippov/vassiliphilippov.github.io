plutocrat

noun
wealthy person with power
Pronunciation: / ˈpluːtəˌkræt / (AmE), / ˈpluːtəkræt / (BrE)
A person who has power and influence because of their great wealth.

Examples

A plutocrat with power
As a self-made plutocrat, he donated millions to political campaigns.
Critics argue that plutocrats have too much influence over government policy.
The country was dominated by a handful of plutocrats who controlled major industries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oligarch - A member of a small ruling group, especially for corrupt or selfish purposes
  • antonym: pauper - A very poor person
