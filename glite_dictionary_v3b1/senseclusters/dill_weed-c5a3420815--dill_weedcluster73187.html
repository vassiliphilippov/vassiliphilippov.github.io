dill weed

noun
aromatic leaves of the dill plant used in cooking
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪl wiːd / (AmE), / ˈdɪl wiːd / (BrE)
The feathery leaves of the dill plant, used as a seasoning in cooking.

Examples

Fresh dill weed
I always keep a jar of dill weed in my spice rack for making salads and soups.
She sprinkled fresh dill weed over the potatoes to give them extra flavor.
The recipe calls for a teaspoon of dried dill weed, not dill seed.
