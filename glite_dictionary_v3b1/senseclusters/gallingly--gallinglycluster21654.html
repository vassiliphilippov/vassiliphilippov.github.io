gallingly

adverb
causing irritation
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɔlɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːlɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that causes physical soreness, irritation, or chafing, especially by rubbing against the skin

Examples

Gallingly rough fabric
The rough fabric pressed gallingly against his skin all day.
Her shoes fit so poorly that they rubbed gallingly on her heels.
The collar of his shirt chafed gallingly during the long walk.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comfortably - in a way that causes no discomfort or irritation
  • synonym: irritatingly - in a way that causes annoyance or discomfort
