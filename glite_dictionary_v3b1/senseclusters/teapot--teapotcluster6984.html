teapot

noun
pot for brewing tea
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːˌpɑt / (AmE), / ˈtiːˌpɒt / (BrE)
A container with a spout, handle, and lid, used for brewing and serving tea.

Examples

A teapot with tea
She poured the hot water into the teapot to steep the tea leaves.
The antique teapot had a beautiful hand-painted design.
Would you like another cup from the teapot?
