gardenia

noun
fragrant flowering shrub or tree
3.0
Pronunciation: / ɡɑrˈdiːniə / (AmE), / ɡɑːˈdiːniə / (BrE)
A shrub or small tree of the genus Gardenia, grown for its large, fragrant white or yellow flowers.

Examples

A gardenia flower
He gave her a bouquet with a single gardenia to symbolize his admiration.
Many gardeners prize the gardenia for its striking appearance and sweet scent.
The gardenia in her garden bloomed with fragrant white flowers every spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: camellia - a shrub with large, showy flowers, often grown for ornament
