fer-de-lance

noun
a large, venomous Central/South American pit viper
Pronunciation: / ˌfɛər də ˈlɑːns / (AmE), / ˌfeə də ˈlɑ̃ːs / (BrE)
a large and highly venomous pit viper found in Central and South America, known for its aggressive nature

Examples

A fer-de-lance can deliver a potentially deadly bite if provoked.
Researchers wore protective boots while studying the habitat of the fer-de-lance.
The fer-de-lance is responsible for more human fatalities in its range than any other snake.
