shawl

verb
to wrap with a shawl
6.3 0.04/m
Pronunciation: /  É…îl / (AmE), /  É…îÀêl / (BrE)
to cover or wrap someone or something with a shawl, or to envelop as if with a shawl, often figuratively.

Examples
