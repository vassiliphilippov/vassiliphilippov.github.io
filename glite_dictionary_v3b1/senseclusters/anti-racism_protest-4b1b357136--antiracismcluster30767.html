anti-racism protest

noun
public demonstration against racial discrimination
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈreɪsɪzəm ˈproʊtɛst / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈreɪsɪzəm ˈprəʊtɛst / (BrE)
A public demonstration or gathering against racial discrimination or in support of racial equality.

Examples

An anti-racism protest march
After the incident, several groups joined together for an anti-racism protest.
The university students planned an anti-racism protest to call for social justice.
Thousands attended the anti-racism protest held in the city center.

  • synonym: protest - a public demonstration expressing strong objection
