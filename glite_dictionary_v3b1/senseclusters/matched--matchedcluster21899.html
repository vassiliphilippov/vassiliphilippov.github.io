matched

adjective
having harmonizing qualities
Pronunciation: / mætʃt / (AmE), / mætʃt / (BrE)
(of things or people) going well together or having harmonizing qualities.

Examples

Matched colors
The matched curtains and cushions gave the living room a cohesive look.
The jeweler displayed a set of matched pearls in the window.
They are a perfectly matched couple, sharing many interests and values.
