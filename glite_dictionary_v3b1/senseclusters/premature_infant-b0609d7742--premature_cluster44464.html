premature infant

noun
baby born before full gestation
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːməˈtʃʊr ˈɪnfənt / (AmE), / ˌpriːməˈtʃʊə ˈɪnfənt / (BrE)
a baby born before 37 weeks of pregnancy have been completed

Examples

A premature infant
Doctors are working to improve survival rates for premature infants worldwide.
Most premature infants need extra support to help them breathe and feed.
The hospital has a special unit to care for premature infants.
