non-overlapping

adjective
not touching or sharing
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌoʊvərˈlæpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌəʊvəˈlæpɪŋ / (BrE)
not covering, touching, or sharing any part with something else

Examples

Non-overlapping areas
The two teams work in non-overlapping areas to avoid confusion.
We scheduled the meetings at non-overlapping times so everyone could attend both.
The circles on the diagram are non-overlapping to show separate groups.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disjoint - having no elements or parts in common
  • antonym: overlapping - covering or sharing a part with something else
