haberdasher

noun
seller of men's clothing
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhæbərˌdæʃər / (AmE), / ˈhæbədæʃə / (BrE)
a person who sells men's clothing, accessories, or small sewing items like buttons and thread

Examples

A haberdasher shop
He apprenticed as a haberdasher before starting his own business.
Many gentlemen relied on their local haberdasher for hats, ties, and shirts.
The haberdasher on the corner carries the finest suits in town.
The haberdasher helped me choose the right buttons for my shirt project.
In the old town, a haberdasher sold both sewing supplies and men's hats.
She visited the haberdasher to buy thread and needles for her new dress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: draper - a person who sells cloth and sewing materials
  • synonym: outfitter - a shop or person that sells men's clothes, especially outdoor or specialized clothing
  • synonym: tailor - a person who makes or alters clothing
