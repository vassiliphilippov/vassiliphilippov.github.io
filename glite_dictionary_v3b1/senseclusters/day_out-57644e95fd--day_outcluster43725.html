day out

noun
leisure trip away from home for a day
Pronunciation: / deɪ aʊt / (AmE), / deɪ aʊt / (BrE)
A trip or excursion taken for leisure, typically away from home and lasting one day.

Examples

A day out with friends
A day out with friends can be a wonderful way to relax and recharge.
Their favorite day out was a picnic by the lake every summer.
We decided to have a day out at the zoo to enjoy the sunshine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excursion - a short journey or trip, especially for leisure
