Examples

Synonyms

    7.4 2.81/m

  • prototypical
    6.8 1.12/m
    prototypical is the original model that later examples copy, while archetypal suggests a classic type with strong, known traits.
  • paradigmatic
    8.3 1.07/m

  • classic
    3.4 36/m
