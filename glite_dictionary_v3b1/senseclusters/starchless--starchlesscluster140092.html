starchless

adjective
without starch
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrtʃləs / (AmE), / ˈstɑːtʃləs / (BrE)
Containing no starch; lacking starch.

Examples

A starchless seed
Certain mutant seeds remain starchless even after prolonged exposure to light.
She follows a starchless diet to help manage her blood sugar levels.
The researchers observed that the starchless plants displayed unusual growth patterns.

Related Terms

  • antonym: starchy - containing or resembling starch
