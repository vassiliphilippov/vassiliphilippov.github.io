powerwash

verb
clean with high-pressure water spray
2.6 3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊərˌwɑʃ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊəˌwɒʃ / (BrE)
to clean a surface using a high-pressure water spray, especially to remove dirt, old paint, or mildew before painting

Examples

To powerwash a driveway
It's best to powerwash sidewalks every spring to keep them looking clean.
The contractor powerwashed the house to remove all traces of mildew and peeling paint.
We need to powerwash the deck before staining it this weekend.
