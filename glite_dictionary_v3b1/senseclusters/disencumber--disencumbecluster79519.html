disencumber

verb
free from burden or difficulty
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsɪnˈkʌmbər / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɪnˈkʌmbə / (BrE)
to free or release from a burden, difficulty, or obstacle

Examples

To disencumber oneself
He finally managed to disencumber himself from a long-standing financial obligation.
She worked hard to disencumber her schedule so she could focus on her family.
The reforms were designed to disencumber the company of excessive regulation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extricate - to free from constraint or difficulty
