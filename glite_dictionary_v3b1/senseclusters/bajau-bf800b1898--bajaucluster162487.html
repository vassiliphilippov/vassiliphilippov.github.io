Bajau

noun
Austronesian language
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːdʒaʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːdʒaʊ / (BrE)
any of several related Austronesian languages spoken by the Bajau (Sama-Bajau) peoples of Southeast Asia

Examples

Bajau language
She learned to speak Bajau from her grandparents in Sabah.
The Bajau language has many dialects across different islands.
Researchers are documenting the unique features of Bajau for linguistic studies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Malay - an Austronesian language spoken in Malaysia, Indonesia, and Brunei
