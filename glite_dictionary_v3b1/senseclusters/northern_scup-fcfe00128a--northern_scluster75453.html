northern scup

noun
Atlantic coastal fish; valued as panfish
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrðərn skʌp / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən skʌp / (BrE)
A fish valued as a panfish, found in the Atlantic coastal waters of North America from South Carolina to Maine.

Examples

The northern scup fish
Fishermen often catch northern scup in the coastal waters off New England.
Many anglers consider northern scup a delicious option for pan-frying.
The restaurant featured grilled northern scup as one of its daily seafood specials.
