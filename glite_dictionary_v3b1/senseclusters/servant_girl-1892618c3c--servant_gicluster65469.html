servant girl

noun
female household servant
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜrvənt ɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvənt ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a girl or young woman who works as a servant, typically in a household

Examples

The servant girl works
In Victorian novels, the servant girl is often portrayed as hardworking yet invisible.
She began her career as a servant girl before eventually running her own business.
The wealthy family employed a servant girl to help with chores around the house.
