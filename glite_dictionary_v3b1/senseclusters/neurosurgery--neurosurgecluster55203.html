neurosurgery

noun
surgery on the nervous system
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊˈsɝːdʒəri / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˈsɜːdʒəri / (BrE)
The branch of surgery concerned with the treatment of disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves.

Examples

Brain neurosurgery
Advancements in neurosurgery have greatly improved outcomes for those with brain tumors.
She is specializing in neurosurgery because she is fascinated by the complexities of the brain and nervous system.
The patient required neurosurgery after sustaining a severe head injury.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neurosurgeon - a surgeon specializing in operations on the nervous system
