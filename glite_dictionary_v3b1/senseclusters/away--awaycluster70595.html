away

adjective
baseball: on the far side from the batter
4.2
Pronunciation: / əˈweɪ / (AmE), / əˈweɪ / (BrE)
(of a baseball pitch) on the side of home plate farthest from the batter; outside.

Examples

Away pitch outside
Coaches often tell left-handed pitchers to work the ball away against right-handed hitters.
That was a tough call; I thought the ball was a bit away from the plate.
The pitcher kept throwing away pitches to avoid giving the batter anything to hit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outside - (of a pitch) not close to the batter; away from the plate
