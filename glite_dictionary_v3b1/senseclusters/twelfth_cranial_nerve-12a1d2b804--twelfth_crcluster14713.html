twelfth cranial nerve

noun
cranial nerve controlling tongue muscles
7.2
Pronunciation: /twelfθ ˈkreɪ.niː.əl nɜːrv/ /; / /twelf ˈkreɪ.niː.əl nɜːrv/ (AmE), /twelfθ ˈkreɪ.niː.əl nɜːv/ /; / /twelf ˈkreɪ.niː.əl nɜːv/ (BrE)
