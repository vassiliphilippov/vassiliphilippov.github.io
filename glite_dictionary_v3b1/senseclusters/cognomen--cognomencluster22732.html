cognomen

noun
familiar or informal name; nickname
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɑɡˈnoʊ.mən / (AmE), / kɒɡˈnəʊ.mən / (BrE)
a familiar or informal name for a person, often a shortened or affectionate form of their given name

Examples

His cognomen Lex
Although his birth name was Alexander, he was known everywhere by his cognomen, Lex.
Her friends always called her by her cognomen instead of her full name.
The famous general's cognomen reflected his fearless reputation on the battlefield.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nickname - an informal, often affectionate, name for a person or thing
