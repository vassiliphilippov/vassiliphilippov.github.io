millstone

noun
large grinding stone for grain
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈmɪlstəʊn / (BrE)
one of a pair of large, heavy, flat stones used for grinding grain in a mill by rotating against one another

Examples

A millstone for grinding
Over centuries, the millstone has symbolized hard labor and sustenance.
The millstone ground the wheat into fine flour for baking bread.
The old flour mill still has its original millstone on display for visitors.
