sore throat

noun
painful and inflamed throat
Pronunciation: / sɔr θroʊt / (AmE), / sɔː θrəʊt / (BrE)
A painful or irritated condition of the throat, commonly caused by infection or inflammation.

Examples

A sore throat pain
Drinking hot tea can help soothe a sore throat during a cold.
His sore throat made it difficult to swallow.
She stayed home from work because she had a sore throat and a fever.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pharyngitis - inflammation of the pharynx
