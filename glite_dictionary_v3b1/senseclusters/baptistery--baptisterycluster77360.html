baptistery

noun
place for baptisms
Pronunciation: / ˈbæptɪˌstɛri / (AmE), / ˈbæptɪstri / (BrE)
a part of a church or a separate building where baptisms are performed

Examples

Ancient baptistery building
The ancient baptistery stood beside the main cathedral.
Tourists admired the beautiful mosaics inside the baptistery during their visit.
The ceremony was held in the baptistery attached to the old church.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cathedral - the principal church of a diocese, containing the bishop's seat
  • synonym: font - a basin or receptacle for baptismal water in a church
