comminuted

adjective
broken into pieces
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːməˌnuːtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɒmɪˌnjuːtɪd / (BrE)
broken into several small pieces, especially describing a bone that is fractured in multiple parts

Examples

Comminuted bone fracture
The X-ray revealed a comminuted fracture in the patient's arm after the accident.
A comminuted bone injury often requires surgery to repair the multiple fragments.
Doctors explained that a comminuted fracture is more complex than a simple break.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fractured - broken or cracked, especially referring to bone
  • antonym: intact - not broken or damaged
