buster

noun
person or thing that breaks up or overcomes something
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌstər / (AmE), / ˈbʌstə / (BrE)
a person or thing that breaks up, destroys, or overcomes something, often used in compounds to denote someone who combats or eliminates a specified thing

Examples

Myth buster
As a renowned myth buster, she enjoyed disproving popular misconceptions.
The city hired a new crime buster to lead its police department.
The government appointed a sanctions buster to investigate companies breaking trade laws.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breaker - a person or thing that breaks something
