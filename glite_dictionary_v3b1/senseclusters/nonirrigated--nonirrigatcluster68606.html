nonirrigated

adjective
without artificial watering
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈɪrɪˌɡeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈɪrɪˌɡeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not supplied with water by artificial means, relying only on natural rainfall.

Examples

A nonirrigated field
The farmer planted wheat in the nonirrigated section of his land, hoping for enough rain.
Yields from nonirrigated fields are often lower during dry seasons.
Many traditional crops are well suited to nonirrigated agriculture in this region.

Related Terms

  • antonym: irrigated - supplied with water by artificial means
  • synonym: rainfed - dependent on rainfall for water
