reimagine

verb
to create anew
4.7
Pronunciation: /riː.ɪ.ˈmædʒ.ɪn/ /; / /rɪ.ˈmædʒ.ɪn/ (AmE), /riː.ɪ.ˈmædʒ.ɪn/ /; / /rɪ.ˈmædʒ.ɪn/ (BrE)
