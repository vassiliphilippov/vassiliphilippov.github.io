bullet

noun
projectile fired from a gun
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlət / (AmE), / ˈbʊlɪt / (BrE)
A small metal projectile that is fired from a gun.

Examples

A bullet from a gun
Soldiers are trained to avoid incoming bullets during combat.
The detective examined the bullet that was lodged in the wall.
The police recovered the bullet as evidence from the crime scene.
