riding master

noun
person who teaches horse riding
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪdɪŋ ˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈraɪdɪŋ ˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to teach people how to ride horses and about horsemanship

Examples

A riding master teaches
She studied under a renowned riding master before opening her own stable.
The riding master carefully explained the basics of posting trot to the new riders.
The students gathered in the arena as the riding master demonstrated proper rein technique.
