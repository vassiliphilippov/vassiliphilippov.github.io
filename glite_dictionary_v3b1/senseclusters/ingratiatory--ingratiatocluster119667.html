ingratiatory

adjective
intended to gain favor or approval
7.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡreɪʃiəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡreɪʃiətəri / (BrE)
intended to gain approval or favor, often by trying to please someone (sometimes in a way that is not sincere)
