valorous

adjective
showing great courage
Pronunciation: / ˈvælɚəs / (AmE), / ˈvæl(ə)rəs / (BrE)
showing great courage, especially in the face of danger

Examples

A valorous act
He was recognized for his valorous service during the war.
Stories of valorous knights are common in medieval literature.
The valorous actions of the firefighters saved many lives that night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brave - showing readiness to face and endure danger or pain
  • antonym: cowardly - lacking courage; excessively afraid
  • synonym: valiant - showing courage or determination
