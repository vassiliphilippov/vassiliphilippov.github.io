integrity

noun
state of being whole or unbroken
7.0 3.6
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈte.grɪ.tiː/ /; / /ɪn.ˈte.grə.tiː/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈte.grɪ.tiː/ /; / /ɪn.ˈte.grə.tiː/ (BrE)
