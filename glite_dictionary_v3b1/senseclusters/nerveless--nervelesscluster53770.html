nerveless

adjective
calm and self-controlled under pressure
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈnɜrv.ləs / (AmE), / ˈnɜːv.ləs / (BrE)

The pilot remained nerveless during the emergency landing and inspired confidence in everyone on board.
