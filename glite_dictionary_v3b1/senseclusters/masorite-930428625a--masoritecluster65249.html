Masorite

noun
scholar of the Masorah in Jewish tradition
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈmæ.sə.raɪt/ /; / /ˈmæ.sə.rɪt/ (AmE), /ˈmæ.sə.raɪt/ /; / /ˈmæ.sə.rɪt/ (BrE)
