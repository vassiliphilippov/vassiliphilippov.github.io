Punjab

proper noun
historical region in South Asia
Pronunciation: / pʊnˈdʒɑb / (AmE), / pʊnˈdʒɑːb / (BrE)
a large historical region in northwestern India and northern Pakistan, known for its fertile land and distinct cultural heritage

Examples
