all-weather jacket

noun
jacket suitable for all weather conditions
Pronunciation: / ˌɔlˈwɛðɚ ˈdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ˌɔːlˈwɛðə ˈdʒækɪt / (BrE)
a jacket designed to be suitable for wearing in any weather condition, typically resistant to wind and rain

Examples

An all-weather jacket
An all-weather jacket is a smart choice for outdoor enthusiasts.
He relied on his all-weather jacket when traveling through different climates.
She wore her all-weather jacket during the unpredictable spring hike.

Related Terms

  • synonym: raincoat - a waterproof coat worn to protect against rain
