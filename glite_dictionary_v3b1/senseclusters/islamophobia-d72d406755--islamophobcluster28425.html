Islamophobia

noun
prejudice against Islam or Muslims
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɪs.ləˈmoʊ.fi.ə / (AmE), / ˌɪs.ləˈməʊ.fi.ə / (BrE)
Dislike of or prejudice against Islam or Muslims, especially as a political force.

Examples

Islamophobia in society
Debates about Islamophobia often arise in discussions of immigration policy.
She spoke out against Islamophobia in her community.
The rise of Islamophobia has been documented by several international organizations.
