hydrocodone

noun
opioid pain medication
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪdrəˈkoʊdoʊn / (AmE), / ˌhaɪdrəˈkəʊdəʊn / (BrE)
a prescription opioid drug used to relieve moderate to severe pain, and sometimes to suppress coughing

Examples

Prescription hydrocodone medicine
The doctor prescribed hydrocodone to help manage her severe back pain after surgery.
Some cough syrups contain hydrocodone to reduce persistent coughing.
Patients should take hydrocodone only as directed to avoid the risk of addiction.
