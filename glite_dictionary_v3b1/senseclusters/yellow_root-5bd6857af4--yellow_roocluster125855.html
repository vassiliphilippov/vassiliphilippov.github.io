yellow root

noun
a perennial herb with a yellow rootstock
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊ ˌruːt / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˌruːt / (BrE)
A perennial herb native to northeastern United States, recognized by its thick, knotted yellow rootstock and large rounded leaves.

Examples

The yellow root plant
The botanist carefully dug up the yellow root to study its structure.
Traditional medicine often uses yellow root for its supposed healing properties.
You can identify yellow root by its bright yellow underground stem and large leaves.
