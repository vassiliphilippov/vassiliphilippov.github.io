yellow root

noun
a perennial herb with a yellow rootstock
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊ ˌruːt / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˌruːt / (BrE)
A perennial herb native to northeastern United States, recognized by its thick, knotted yellow rootstock and large rounded leaves.
