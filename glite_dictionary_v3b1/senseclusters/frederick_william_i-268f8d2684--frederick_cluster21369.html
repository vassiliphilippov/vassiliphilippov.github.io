Frederick William I

proper noun
King of Prussia, 1713–1740
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛdərɪk ˈwɪljəm ðə ˈfɜrst / (AmE), / ˈfrɛdrɪk ˈwɪljəm ði ˈfɜːst / (BrE)
(1688–1740) King of Prussia from 1713 to 1740, noted for reforming and strengthening the Prussian army.

Examples
