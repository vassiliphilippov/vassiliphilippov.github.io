quillback

noun
freshwater fish species
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪlˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈkwɪlˌbæk / (BrE)
a North American freshwater fish with a long, quill-like dorsal fin, found in rivers and lakes
