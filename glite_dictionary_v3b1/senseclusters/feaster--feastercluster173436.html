feaster

noun
person who eats a lot
4.2 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàfiÀêst…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàfiÀêst…ô / (BrE)
a person who eats at a feast or large meal, especially at a celebration

Examples
