MSL

noun
pharmaceutical liaison specialist
8.8
Pronunciation: / ɛm ɛs ɛl / (AmE), / ɛm ɛs ɛl / (BrE)
a specialist who communicates scientific and clinical information between pharmaceutical companies and healthcare professionals

Examples

MSL position
After completing her PhD, she accepted a job as an MSL at a large pharmaceutical company.
The MSL provided doctors with the latest clinical trial data on the new medication.
Many scientists transition into an MSL role to bridge research and clinical practice.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sales representative - a person who sells products or services for a company
