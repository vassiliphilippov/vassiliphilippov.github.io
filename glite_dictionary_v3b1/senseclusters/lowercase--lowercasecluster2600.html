lowercase

adjective
written in small, not capital, letters
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊərˌkeɪs / (AmE), / ˈləʊəkeɪs / (BrE)
(of a letter) written in small form, not in capital or upper-case form.

Examples

Lowercase letter
Her handwriting mixes lowercase and uppercase letters for emphasis.
Please fill out the form using only lowercase letters.
The password is case-sensitive, so be sure to use lowercase characters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: uppercase - written in capital letters
