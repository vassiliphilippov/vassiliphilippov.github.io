starburst

noun
radiating light effect
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːrbɜrst / (AmE), / ˈstɑːbɜːst / (BrE)
a visual effect where a bright point of light appears with radiating rays, often seen in photography or optics

Examples

Starburst effect photo
The photographer captured a beautiful starburst effect around the sun by using a small aperture.
At night, the streetlights created a starburst pattern in the rainy window.
You can achieve a starburst in your photos by adjusting your camera settings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flare - a sudden burst of bright light, often with radiating lines
  • synonym: glare - a strong, harsh light that can obscure vision
