tweaky

adjective
nervous and fidgety
9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈtwiː.ki/ /; / /ˈtwiː.kɪ/ (AmE), /ˈtwiː.ki/ /; / /ˈtwiː.kɪ/ (BrE)
nervous, restless, or overly focused on small details, often in a fidgety or agitated way (sometimes due to stimulants or lack of sleep)

Examples

Tweaky after coffee
After three cups of coffee, he started to feel tweaky and couldn't sit still.
She gets a bit tweaky when everything isn't perfectly organized on her desk.
The loud noises made the dog tweaky and anxious all afternoon.
After drinking too much coffee, he felt tweaky and couldn't sit still during the meeting.
She looked tweaky after staying up all night to finish her project.
His tweaky behavior made his friends worry that he hadn't slept in days.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - not showing nervousness or anxiety
  • synonym: jittery - feeling nervous or unable to relax
