mericarp

noun

(Botany) One of the segments into which a split fruit (schizocarp) separates at maturity, each typically containing a single seed.

Examples

A mericarp segment
Each mericarp contains a single seed and separates from its partner at maturity.
In fennel, the fruit splits into two mericarps when ripe.
