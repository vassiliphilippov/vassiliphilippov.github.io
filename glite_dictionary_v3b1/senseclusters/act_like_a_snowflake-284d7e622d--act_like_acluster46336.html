act like a snowflake

verb
respond overly sensitively or defensively
Pronunciation: / ækt laɪk ə ˈsnoʊˌfleɪk / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə ˈsnəʊˌfleɪk / (BrE)
to respond with excessive sensitivity or defensiveness, especially in reaction to criticism

Examples

To act like a snowflake
At the meeting, she acted like a snowflake when given constructive feedback.
He tends to act like a snowflake whenever someone points out his mistakes.
Whenever someone questions his ideas, he acts like a snowflake and gets offended.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overreact - to respond more emotionally or forcibly than is justified
  • antonym: shrug off - to ignore criticism or problems without getting upset
  • synonym: take offense - to become upset or annoyed, often unnecessarily
