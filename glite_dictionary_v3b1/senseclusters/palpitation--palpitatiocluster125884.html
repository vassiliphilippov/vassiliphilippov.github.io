palpitation

noun
a shaky or trembling motion
Pronunciation: / ˌpælpəˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpælpɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a rapid or trembling motion, especially a rhythmic trembling or quivering

Examples

Curtain palpitation
A slight palpitation of the curtain revealed someone hiding behind it.
He struck a match with a palpitation that betrayed his nervousness.
The palpitation in her hands was noticeable when she tried to hold the cup steady.

Related Terms

  • synonym: quiver - a slight, rapid shaking or trembling movement
  • synonym: tremor - a slight shaking movement or sound, especially in the body
