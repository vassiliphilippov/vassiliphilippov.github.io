patrimonial

adjective
inherited by descent (esp. legal)
Pronunciation: / ˌpætrɪˈmoʊniəl / (AmE), / ˌpætrɪˈməʊniəl / (BrE)
inherited or inheritable by established rules, especially through family descent or legal inheritance

Examples

A patrimonial system
Many monarchies maintained power through a patrimonial system of inheritance.
Old laws governed the transfer of patrimonial property in the region.
The family's patrimonial estate had been passed down for generations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hereditary - transmitted from parent to offspring
