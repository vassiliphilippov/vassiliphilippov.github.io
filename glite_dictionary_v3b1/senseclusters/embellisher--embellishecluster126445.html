embellisher

noun
one who decorates
6.0
Pronunciation: / ɛmˈbɛlɪʃər / (AmE), / ɪmˈbɛlɪʃə / (BrE)
a person or thing that decorates or adds extra details to something to make it more attractive

Examples

A dress embellisher
The embellisher carefully placed sparkling jewels on the wedding dress.
As an embellisher of stories, he often added imaginative details to make them more interesting.
The artist acted as an embellisher, transforming plain objects into beautiful works of art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adorner - someone who adds beauty or ornament to something
  • synonym: decorator - a person who decorates rooms or objects
