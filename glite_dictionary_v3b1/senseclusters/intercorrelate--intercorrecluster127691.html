intercorrelate

verb
to relate mutually
8.5
Pronunciation: /ɪn.trə.ˈkɔːr.ə.leɪt/ (AmE), /ɪn.trə.ˈkɒr.ə.leɪt/ (BrE)
to have a mutual or reciprocal relationship or connection with each other, where two or more things affect each other
