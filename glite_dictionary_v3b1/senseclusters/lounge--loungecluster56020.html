lounge

noun
long upholstered seat for several people
Pronunciation: / laʊndʒ / (AmE), / laʊndʒ / (BrE)
A long upholstered seat designed to accommodate more than one person, often used for relaxing in living rooms or lounges.

Examples

A lounge chair
She relaxed on the big lounge while reading her favorite book.
The hotel suite featured a spacious lounge upholstered in soft leather.
We bought a new lounge for the living room to fit all our guests comfortably.

Related Terms

  • synonym: couch - a long, comfortable seat for two or more people
  • synonym: sofa - an upholstered seat with arms and a back, for two or more people
