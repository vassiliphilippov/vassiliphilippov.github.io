metallographic

adjective
metal structure analysis
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtələˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɛtələˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to the microscopic study of the structure and properties of metals

Examples

A metallographic examination
The metallographic examination showed tiny flaws in the alloy's grain structure.
A metallographic report is essential for understanding the failure of metal components.
Engineers used metallographic techniques to compare different steel samples.

  • synonym: metallurgical - relating to the science of metals
