maintainer

noun
person who upholds or maintains something
Pronunciation: / meɪnˈteɪnɚ / (AmE), / meɪnˈteɪnə / (BrE)
a person who upholds, supports, or keeps something in good condition or operation

Examples

A maintainer of software
Open-source projects often rely on a dedicated maintainer to oversee updates and changes.
She has become a key maintainer of traditional customs in her community.
The maintainer of the building ensures everything stays in working order.

Related Terms

  • synonym: supporter - a person who supports a cause, idea, or person
  • synonym: sustainer - a person who supports or maintains something
