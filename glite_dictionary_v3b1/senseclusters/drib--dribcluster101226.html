drib

verb
to drool liquid
9.9
Pronunciation: / drɪb / (AmE), / drɪb / (BrE)
to let saliva or another liquid fall slowly from the mouth, especially unintentionally; to drool.

Examples

Babies drib milk
The baby would often drib milk onto his bib during feeding.
After the dental procedure, she started to drib water from the side of her mouth.
He tried not to drib soup while eating with a shaky hand.

Related Terms
