bunker mentality

noun
defensive or isolationist mindset
9.9 6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbʌŋ.kər men.ˈtæl.ə.tiː/ /; / /ˈbʌŋ.kər mən.ˈtæl.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /ˈbʌŋ.kə men.ˈtæl.ə.tiː/ /; / /ˈbʌŋ.kə mən.ˈtæl.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
an attitude of extreme defensiveness or isolation, often resulting from repeated criticism, attack, or pressure

Examples

Bunker mentality in teams
Sports teams on losing streaks sometimes fall into a bunker mentality, mistrusting outsiders and ignoring advice.
The company's bunker mentality made it resistant to necessary changes.
Years of criticism from the media led the administration to develop a bunker mentality.

  • synonym: siege mentality - the feeling of being constantly attacked or oppressed, leading to defensiveness or isolation
