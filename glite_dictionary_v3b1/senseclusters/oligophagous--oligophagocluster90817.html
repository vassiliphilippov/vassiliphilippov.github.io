oligophagous

adjective
eating few food types
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːlɪˈɡɑːfəɡəs / (AmE), / ˌɒlɪˈɡɒfəɡəs / (BrE)
feeding on only a few specific types of food, especially certain plants

Examples

Oligophagous insect feeding
The insect is oligophagous, feeding only on a few species of plants in the region.
Many caterpillars are oligophagous and will not eat leaves from unrelated plants.
Scientists studied the oligophagous habits of the beetle to understand its impact on local crops.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: monophagous - feeding on only one type of food
  • degree higher: polyphagous - feeding on many different types of food
