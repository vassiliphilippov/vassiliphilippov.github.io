tuffy

noun
informal tough person
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌfi / (AmE), / ˈtʌfi / (BrE)
an informal nickname for a person who is tough, resilient, or able to handle difficult situations

Examples

A real tuffy kid
Everyone called him tuffy because he never gave up.
After recovering from her injury, she earned the nickname tuffy among her teammates.
Despite the challenges, little tuffy always found a way to succeed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: softie - a person who is gentle or easily affected emotionally
  • synonym: tough guy - a person who is strong or resilient
