pout

noun
grimace with protruding lips
4.8 3.5
Pronunciation: /paʊt/ (AmE), /paʊt/ (BrE)
A facial expression in which the lips are pushed forward, especially in annoyance, sulkiness, or to look attractive.

Examples

A sad pout
He tried to hide his disappointment, but his pout gave him away.
When she didn't get her way, a clear pout appeared on her face.
With a dramatic pout, the child refused to eat his vegetables.
