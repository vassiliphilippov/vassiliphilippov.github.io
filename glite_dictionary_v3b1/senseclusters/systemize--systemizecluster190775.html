systemize

verb
arrange systematically
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪstəməˌaɪz /; / ˈsɪs.tə.maɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɪstəməˌaɪz /; / ˈsɪs.tɪ.maɪz / (BrE)
to arrange or organize according to a system; to make systematic

Examples

To systemize work processes
In order to solve the issue, we need to systemize our approach and set clear protocols.
Researchers are working to systemize the vast amount of data collected from the experiment.
The new manager plans to systemize the company's workflow to improve efficiency.
Efforts to systemise data collection have improved the accuracy of the research.
It is important to systemise laboratory procedures to ensure consistent results.
The organization began to systemise its training programs for all employees.
