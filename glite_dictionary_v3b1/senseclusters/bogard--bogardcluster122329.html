bogard

noun
selfish taker
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊɡɑrd / (AmE), / ˈbəʊɡɑːd / (BrE)
a person who selfishly takes or keeps something that should be shared with others

Examples

A bogard at parties
Don't be a bogard and let others have some of the snacks too.
He acted like a real bogard during the game, never passing the ball.
At the party, she was called a bogard for taking all the best seats.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hog - a person who takes more than their share
  • antonym: share - to give a portion to others
