Lake Volta

proper noun
large artificial lake in Ghana

Pronunciation: /leɪk ˈvoʊl.tə/ /; / /leɪk ˈvəʊl.tə/ (AmE), /leɪk ˈvəʊl.tə/ /; / /leɪk ˈvɒl.tə/ (BrE)
