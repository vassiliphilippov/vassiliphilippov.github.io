Lake Volta

proper noun
large artificial lake in Ghana
5.4
Pronunciation: / leɪk ˈvoʊltə / (AmE), / leɪk ˈvɒltə / (BrE)
A large artificial lake in southeastern Ghana, created by damming the Volta River; one of the world's largest man-made lakes.

Examples

Lake Volta shores
Fishing is an important industry for communities located along the shores of Lake Volta.
Many tourists visit Lake Volta to enjoy boat rides and explore the scenic beauty of the region.
The construction of the Akosombo Dam led to the creation of Lake Volta in the 1960s.
