hyperintellectual

adjective
excessively intellectual
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəl / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpərˌɪntəˈlɛktjʊəl / (BrE)
extremely or excessively intellectual in character, style, or manner

Examples

Hyperintellectual writing style
The professor's hyperintellectual lectures were difficult for most students to follow.
Some viewers found the film's hyperintellectual dialogue off-putting and hard to relate to.
Her hyperintellectual approach to art criticism impressed some but alienated others.
