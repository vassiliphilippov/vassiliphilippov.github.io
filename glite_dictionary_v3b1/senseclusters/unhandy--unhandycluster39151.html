unhandy

adjective
awkward to use
5.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhændi / (AmE), / ʌnˈhændi / (BrE)
difficult or awkward to use, handle, or access; not convenient

Examples

An unhandy tool
The old vacuum cleaner was too unhandy for cleaning the stairs.
She found the new software unhandy compared to the previous version.
Because of its size, the toolbox was rather unhandy to carry around the house.
The kitchen drawers are unhandy because they are placed too high to reach easily.
Carrying groceries up the narrow, unhandy staircase was always a challenge.
He found the remote control unhandy since it was missing several important buttons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awkward - difficult to use or handle
  • synonym: clumsy - not easy to use or handle; lacking grace
  • antonym: handy - easy to use or convenient
  • synonym: inconvenient - causing trouble or difficulty; not convenient
