ring-binder

noun
folder with rings for loose papers
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪŋ ˌbaɪndər / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋ ˌbaɪndə / (BrE)
a folder with rings that can be opened to hold and organize loose sheets of paper or magazines

Examples

A ring-binder folder
Each student brought a ring-binder to store their assignments throughout the year.
She filed all her class notes in a ring-binder to keep them organized.
The office supplies included a blue ring-binder for storing important documents.
