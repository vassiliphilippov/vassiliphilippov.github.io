non-yielding

adjective
not flexible or giving
6.1
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈjiːldɪŋ / (AmE), / nɒnˈjiːldɪŋ / (BrE)
not giving way under pressure or not easily bent, influenced, or changed

Examples

Non-yielding surface
The non-yielding material made the helmet extremely safe for cyclists.
Her non-yielding attitude made negotiations very difficult.
A non-yielding surface is required for the foundation of the building.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flexible - able to bend or adapt easily
  • synonym: inflexible - not willing to change or adapt
  • synonym: rigid - not able to be bent or changed easily
