Synonyms

  • come near
    1.8 1.72/m
    come near means moving closer in space or time in everyday speech, while approach is a neutral verb for moving nearer.
  • close
    1.5 30/m
    close often means moving toward and reducing distance in general use, while approach is a formal neutral verb for coming nearer.
  • draw near
    2.6 1.40/m
    draw near sounds a little formal or literary and shows gradual movement, while approach is more everyday and neutral.
  • draw close
    2.9 1.38/m
    draw close feels literary and suggests gently coming together, while approach is a plain verb for moving nearer in distance or time.
  • near
    1.0 9.37/m
    near as a verb is shorter and slightly more formal in writing, while approach is a common neutral verb for moving closer.
  • come on
    1.4 1.45/m
    come on often means moving slowly toward a place but also has other meanings, while approach is a clear neutral verb.
