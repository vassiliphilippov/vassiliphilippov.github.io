unlooped

adjective
without loops
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈluːpt / (AmE), / ʌnˈluːpt / (BrE)
not formed into a loop or not having any loops

Examples

The unlooped rope end
The unlooped end of the rope was left hanging.
She preferred to keep her necklace unlooped for a simpler look.
Make sure the wire remains unlooped to avoid any tangling.
