orchardist

noun
orchard manager
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrtʃərdɪst / (AmE), / ˈɔːtʃədɪst / (BrE)
a person who owns, manages, or works in an orchard, especially one who grows fruit trees professionally

Examples

The orchardist grows fruit
The experienced orchardist carefully selected the best varieties of apples for the region.
Many orchardists attend annual conferences to learn about new fruit-growing techniques.
As an orchardist, she spends most of her days tending to the trees and monitoring their health.

Related Terms

  • synonym: farmer - a person who owns or manages a farm
  • synonym: gardener - a person who tends and cultivates a garden
