navigability

noun
ease of moving through a website or app
6.8
Pronunciation: /næv.ɪ.gə.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ /; / /næv.ə.gə.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ (AmE), /næv.ɪ.gə.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ /; / /næv.ə.gə.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ (BrE)
(in computing and user-interface design) the ease with which users can move through, find information in, and understand a website, app, or other digital system.

Examples

Website navigability
The redesign significantly improved the site's navigability, so customers located products much more quickly.
During testing, users praised the app's clean layout and easy navigability across menus and settings.
Improving a website's navigability often reduces support requests and increases time spent on key pages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: navigable - able to be navigated or moved through (in digital contexts, an interface that users can move through easily)
  • synonym: usability - the ease with which people can use an interface to achieve goals effectively and satisfactorily
