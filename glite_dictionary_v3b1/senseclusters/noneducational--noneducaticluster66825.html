noneducational

adjective
not about education
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌɛdʒəˈkeɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃənəl / (BrE)
not related to education, teaching, or learning

Examples

Noneducational programs
The organization offers both educational and noneducational programs for the community.
He spent his free time on noneducational activities like playing video games and watching movies.
Some websites are blocked at school because they are considered noneducational.
