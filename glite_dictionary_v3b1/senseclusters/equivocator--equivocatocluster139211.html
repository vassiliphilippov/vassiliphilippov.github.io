equivocator

noun
one who avoids clear answers
8.6
Pronunciation: / ɪˈkwɪvəˌkeɪtər / (AmE), / ɪˈkwɪvəkeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who avoids giving a clear or direct answer, often by being deliberately ambiguous or evasive

Examples

An equivocator avoids answers
As an experienced equivocator, the politician managed to avoid answering the controversial question directly.
In the heated debate, she refused to be an equivocator and spoke her mind clearly.
The detective grew frustrated with the equivocator who continually dodged straightforward questions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prevaricator - a person who speaks or acts in an evasive way
  • antonym: straight-shooter - someone who gives clear, direct answers and opinions
