inconsistently

adverb
in an inconsistent manner
2.2
Pronunciation: / ɪn.kənˈsɪs.tənt.li / (AmE), / ɪn.kənˈsɪs.tənt.li / (BrE)
in a way that does not stay the same, follow the same pattern, or show consistency

Examples

Apply inconsistently rules
He applied the rules inconsistently, causing frustration among his team.
She has been performing inconsistently throughout this season, sometimes surprising everyone with her skill and other times making mistakes.
The data was recorded inconsistently, which made the results difficult to analyze.

Related Terms

  • antonym: consistently - in a manner showing consistency
  • synonym: regularly - done at regular intervals; in a regular manner
