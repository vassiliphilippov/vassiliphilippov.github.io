ticket booth

noun
place where tickets are sold
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪkɪt buːθ / (AmE), / ˈtɪkɪt buːθ / (BrE)
A small office or structure where tickets for admission to events, transportation, or venues are sold.

Examples

The ticket booth window
She bought her train pass at the ticket booth in the station.
The ticket booth closes thirty minutes after the movie begins.
There was a long line at the ticket booth before the show started.

Related Terms

  • synonym: box office - the office where tickets of admission are sold, often for a theater or cinema
