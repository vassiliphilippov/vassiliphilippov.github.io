Friedrich Gottlieb Klopstock

proper noun
German poet (1724–1803)
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːdrɪk ˈɡɒtliːb ˈklɔpˌstɒk / (AmE), / ˈfriːdrɪç ˈɡɔtliːp ˈklɔpstɔk / (BrE)
(1724–1803) A German poet celebrated for his epic and lyrical poetry, especially 'The Messiah', which greatly influenced German literature.
