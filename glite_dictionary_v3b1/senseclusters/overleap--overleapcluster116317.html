overleap

verb
jump or leap across
8.0 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Àåo äv…ôrÀàliÀêp / (AmE), / Àå…ô äv…ôÀàliÀêp / (BrE)
to jump across or leap over something, especially an obstacle

Examples

To overleap a barrier
He narrowly failed to overleap the barrier during the race.
In her excitement, the dog tried to overleap the garden fence.
The athlete managed to overleap the wide stream with ease.

Synonyms

  • leap
    1.0 21/m
    leap is a quick, general jump from one place to another, while overleap emphasizes jumping across a clear obstacle.
  • vault
    4.4 2.62/m
    vault is a jump usually using hands or a pole to get over something, while overleap is any jump across an obstacle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vault - to leap or spring over something, especially using the hands or a pole
