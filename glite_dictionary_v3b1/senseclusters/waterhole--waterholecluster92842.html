waterhole

noun
natural animal drinking spot
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌhəʊl / (BrE)
a natural pool or pond where animals gather to drink water, especially in dry regions

Examples

Elephants at the waterhole
During the dry season, herds of elephants gather at the waterhole to drink and bathe.
The photographer waited patiently by the waterhole to capture images of rare wildlife.
Many animals travel long distances to reach the only waterhole in the area.

Related Terms

  • antonym: desert - a dry, barren area of land with little water
  • synonym: oasis - a fertile area in a desert with water and vegetation
  • synonym: watering hole - a place where animals go to drink water
