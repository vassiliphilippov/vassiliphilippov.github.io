dangly

adjective
hanging loosely
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdæŋli / (AmE), / ˈdæŋli / (BrE)
hanging loosely and able to swing or move freely

Examples

A pair of dangly earrings
She wore a pair of dangly earrings that sparkled in the light.
The cat played with the dangly string hanging from the window.
He decorated the tree with dangly ornaments of all shapes and colors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fixed - firmly attached and not able to move freely
  • synonym: swinging - moving back and forth or from side to side while hanging
