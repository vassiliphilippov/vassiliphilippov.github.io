Zhou dynasty

noun
Chinese dynasty, 1122–221 BC
7.8
Pronunciation: /dʒoʊ ˈdaɪ.nə.stiː/ (AmE), /dʒəʊ ˈdɪn.ə.stiː/ (BrE)
the imperial dynasty that ruled China from around 1122 to 221 BC, notable for its cultural and philosophical developments, including the emergence of Confucianism and Taoism

Examples
