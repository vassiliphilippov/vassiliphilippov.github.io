arteria pudenda

noun
artery supplying the external genital organs
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˈtɪəriə pjuːˈdɛndə / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriə pjuːˈdɛndə / (BrE)
Either of a group of arteries that supply blood to the external genital organs in humans.

Examples

arteria pudenda artery
Damage to the arteria pudenda can lead to complications in genital blood flow.
During surgery, care must be taken not to injure the arteria pudenda vessels.
The arteria pudenda plays a crucial role in supplying blood to the perineal region.
