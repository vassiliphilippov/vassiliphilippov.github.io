snorkeler

noun
person using snorkel
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɔrkələr / (AmE), / ˈsnɔːkələ / (BrE)
a person who swims near the surface of water using a snorkel to breathe and observe underwater life

Examples

A snorkeler swims
The snorkeler floated quietly above the coral reef, watching the colorful fish swim by.
Each snorkeler must wear a mask and fins for a better underwater experience.
As a beginner snorkeler, she stayed close to the shore and enjoyed the clear water.
The experienced snorkeler pointed out a sea turtle gliding through the clear water.
Each snorkeler wore a brightly colored vest for safety during the tour.
As a beginner snorkeler, she was amazed by the variety of fish near the reef.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scuba diver - a person who swims underwater using a self-contained breathing apparatus
  • synonym: swimmer - a person who swims for sport or recreation
