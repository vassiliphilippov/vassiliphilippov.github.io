Examples

To bear a child

Synonyms

  • give birth
    1.4 8.93/m
    give birth is the common everyday phrase for having a baby, while bear is more formal and suits animals or plants.
  • birth
    2.3 1.26/m
    birth as a verb appears in writing to mean producing offspring, while bear is a general verb for animals or fruit.
  • have
    1.7 93/m
    have (of a woman or animal) is the common informal way to say she gives birth, while bear sounds more formal.
