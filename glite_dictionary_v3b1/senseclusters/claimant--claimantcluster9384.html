claimant

noun
person making a claim
Pronunciation: / ˈkleɪ.mənt / (AmE), / ˈkleɪ.mənt / (BrE)
A person who makes a claim, especially for a benefit, right, or title.

Examples

A claimant for benefits
Every claimant must submit the necessary documents before the deadline.
Multiple claimants emerged, each insisting they had sole rights to the invention.
The claimant provided evidence to support her eligibility for the compensation fund.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plaintiff - person who brings a case against another in a court of law
