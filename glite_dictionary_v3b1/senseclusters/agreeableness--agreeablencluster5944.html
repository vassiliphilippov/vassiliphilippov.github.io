agreeableness

noun
quality of being pleasant and friendly
Pronunciation: / əˈɡriəbəlnəs / (AmE), / əˈɡriːəblnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being pleasant, friendly, and easy to get along with; a personality trait characterized by kindness, cooperativeness, and warmth

Examples

High agreeableness in people
Employers often value agreeableness when hiring for customer service positions.
Her agreeableness made her a popular member of the team.
While his agreeableness helped him make friends, he sometimes found it hard to say no.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disagreeableness - the quality of being unpleasant or difficult to get along with
  • synonym: friendliness - the quality of being friendly and kind
