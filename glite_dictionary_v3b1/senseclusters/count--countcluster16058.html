count

noun
nobleman, rank equal to a British earl
4.1
Pronunciation: / kaʊnt / (AmE), / kaʊnt / (BrE)
A nobleman in certain European countries with a rank equivalent to an earl in Britain.

Examples

A French count
In some countries, a count is addressed as 'Your Excellency.'
Several guests at the ball included a count and his family from Italy.
The French count presided over his vast estate with elegance and power.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: baron - a nobleman of lowest hereditary rank, below a count or earl
  • degree higher: duke - a nobleman of the highest hereditary rank, above a count or earl
  • synonym: earl - a British nobleman ranking above a viscount and below a marquess
