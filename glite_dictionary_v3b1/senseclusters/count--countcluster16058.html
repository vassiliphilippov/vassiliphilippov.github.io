count

noun
nobleman, rank equal to a British earl
4.1 6.6
Pronunciation: /kaʊnt/ (AmE), /kaʊnt/ (BrE)
A nobleman in certain European countries with a rank equivalent to an earl in Britain.

Examples

A French count

Several guests at the ball included a count and his family from Italy.
The French count presided over his vast estate with elegance and power.
