theocentric

adjective
God-centered
Pronunciation: / ˌθiːoʊˈsɛntrɪk / (AmE), / ˌθiːəʊˈsɛntrɪk / (BrE)
having God as the central focus or most important element in thought or practice

Examples

Theocentric worldview
The medieval worldview was largely theocentric, placing God at the center of all understanding.
Her philosophy is deeply theocentric, guiding her decisions through faith.
In a theocentric society, religious beliefs influence laws and cultural values.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anthropocentric - focused on humans as the central element
  • synonym: deocentric - centered on God or a deity
