straw man

noun
figure made of straw to scare birds
6.5 4.5
Pronunciation: / strɔː mæn / (AmE), / strɔː mæn / (BrE)
an object in the shape of a human figure, typically made of straw and old clothes, placed in fields to scare away birds from crops

Examples

A straw man in the field
As a child, she was afraid of the old straw man near the vegetable patch.
Several straw men stood in the field, swaying gently in the wind.
The farmer built a straw man to protect his cornfield from crows.
