take up

verb
pick up or lift, often by scooping
Pronunciation: / teɪk ʌp / (AmE), / teɪk ʌp / (BrE)
to pick up or lift something, especially using a scooping motion

Examples

Take up the soup
He used a large spoon to take up the soup from the pot.
The gardener bent down to take up some soil with her trowel.
With a quick motion, she managed to take up all the fallen marbles.
