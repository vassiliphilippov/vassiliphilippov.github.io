conducting

noun
directing an orchestra or choir
Pronunciation: / kənˈdʌktɪŋ / (AmE), / kənˈdʌktɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of directing an orchestra or choir during a musical performance

Examples

Orchestra conducting
He gave a masterclass on conducting techniques to aspiring young musicians.
Her passion for conducting led her to study at a prestigious music academy.
The art of conducting requires both musical knowledge and leadership skills.
