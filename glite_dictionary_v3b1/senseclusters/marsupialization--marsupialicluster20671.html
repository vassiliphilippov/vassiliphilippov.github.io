marsupialization

noun
surgical pouch creation
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɑrˌsuːpiəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / mɑːˌsuːpiəlaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / mɑːˌsjuːpiəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
