side

noun
spin given to a ball (sports)
Pronunciation: / saɪd / (AmE), / saɪd / (BrE)
(in sports) spin given to a ball by striking it on one side or releasing it with a twist, affecting its path or bounce.

Examples

Side spin on the ball
He missed the shot because too much side altered the ball's intended path.
In table tennis, adding side to the serve can confuse your opponent.
The player applied side to the cue ball to make it curve around the obstacle.
