resign

verb
formally give up a right or claim
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / rɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
to formally give up a right, possession, or claim; to relinquish or surrender something

Examples

To resign a claim
After years of dispute, he finally decided to resign his claim to the property.
She chose to resign her rights to the inheritance in favor of her brother.
The prince was forced to resign his title after the scandal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abdicate - to formally renounce a throne, right, or responsibility
  • synonym: relinquish - to voluntarily cease to keep or claim; give up
  • antonym: retain - to keep possession of something
