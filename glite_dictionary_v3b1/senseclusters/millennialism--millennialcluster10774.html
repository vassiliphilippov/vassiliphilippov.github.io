millennialism

noun
belief in thousand-year peace
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɪˈlɛniəlɪzəm / (AmE), / mɪˈlɛniəlɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief in a future thousand-year period of peace and happiness, especially as foretold in Christian theology

Examples

Christian millennialism
Many Christian denominations have different interpretations of millennialism and its significance.
The preacher's sermon focused on the hope offered by millennialism in troubled times.
Debates about millennialism have shaped religious movements throughout history.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apocalypticism - belief in an impending end of the world or a major transformation
  • synonym: millenarianism - belief in a coming transformation of society, often involving a thousand-year reign
