linear

adjective
output proportional to input (in electronics)
8.1 4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈlɪn.iː.ər/ (AmE), /ˈlɪn.iː.ə/ (BrE)
(of a circuit or device) having an output directly proportional to the input; operating without distortion or nonlinearity.

Examples

Linear amplifier
A linear amplifier ensures the output signal accurately follows the input.
Engineers prefer linear devices in applications where signal distortion must be minimized.
The linear response of the circuit makes it ideal for audio processing.
