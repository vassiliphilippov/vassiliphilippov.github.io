trouser clip

noun
clip worn by cyclists to restrain trousers
Pronunciation: / ˈtraʊzər klɪp / (AmE), / ˈtraʊzə klɪp / (BrE)
A metal or plastic clip worn around a cyclist's ankle to prevent trousers from getting caught in the bicycle chain.

Examples

Trouser clips for cyclists
Cyclists often use trouser clips to keep their pants from getting soiled by the bike chain.
I forgot my trouser clip this morning, and my trousers got caught in the gears.
The store sells trouser clips in a variety of colors and designs.
