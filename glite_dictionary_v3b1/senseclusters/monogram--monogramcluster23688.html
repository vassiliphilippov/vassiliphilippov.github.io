monogram

verb
to add initials
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑnəˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈmɒnəˌɡræm / (BrE)
to embroider, print, or add initials or a joined-letter design to an object as decoration or a mark of ownership

Examples

To monogram a towel
She had the handkerchief monogrammed with her initials before the wedding.
Many luxury towels are monogrammed to show the owner's initials.
He monogrammed his suitcase so it would be easy to identify at the airport.
