motorhome

noun
vehicle for living
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtərˌhoʊm / (AmE), / ˈməʊtəhəʊm / (BrE)
a large vehicle designed for travel and living, equipped with beds, kitchen, and other home facilities

Examples

A motorhome trip
They spent their summer driving across Europe in a motorhome.
The motorhome has a small kitchen, a bathroom, and two beds.
Many retirees choose to live in a motorhome and travel full-time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: campervan - a smaller vehicle equipped for camping and sleeping
  • synonym: caravan - a trailer equipped for living, towed by a car
