centavo

noun
fractional monetary unit in several countries
6.3
Pronunciation: / sɛnˈtɑːvoʊ / (AmE), / sɛnˈtɑːvəʊ / (BrE)
A monetary subunit equal to one hundredth of the basic unit in various countries, including many in Latin America and the Philippines.

Examples

One centavo coin
In the Philippines, one peso is equal to one hundred centavos.
She paid five centavos for a piece of candy at the market.
The old coin collection included several rare centavos from the 19th century.
