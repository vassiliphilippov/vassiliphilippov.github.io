abstractionist

adjective
relating to abstract (non-representational) art
Pronunciation: / əbˈstrækʃənɪst / (AmE), / əbˈstrækʃənɪst / (BrE)
(of art) not representing or imitating external reality or the objects of nature; relating to abstract art.

Examples

Abstractionist painting style
Her abstractionist style set her apart from other painters in the exhibit.
Many critics praised the abstractionist approach for its emotional intensity.
The gallery featured a collection of abstractionist works that challenged traditional perspectives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abstract - (of art) not representing realistic forms; existing in thought or as an idea
