partner

verb
to provide with a partner; to associate together
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrt.nɚ /; / ˈpɑːrtnər / (AmE), / ˈpɑːt.nə /; / ˈpɑːtnə / (BrE)
to join or associate with someone as a partner in an activity, business, or relationship

Examples

To partner with someone
Each actor was partnered with a dancer for the competition.
She partnered with a local charity to organize the event.
The teacher partnered the students for the science project.
Fred wanted to partner with a local company to expand his business.
In the dance competition, each contestant must partner a member of the opposite team.
She partnered her brother in the mixed doubles tennis tournament.

  • synonym: pair - to arrange in pairs
