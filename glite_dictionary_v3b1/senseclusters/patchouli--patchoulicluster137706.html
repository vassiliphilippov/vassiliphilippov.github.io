patchouli

noun
perfume from the patchouli plant
Pronunciation: / pəˈtʃuːli / (AmE), / pəˈtʃuːli / (BrE)
a strong, musky perfume made from the oil of the patchouli plant, often used in incense and fragrances

Examples

Patchouli perfume scent
Many people associate the smell of patchouli with the 1960s counterculture.
She dabbed a little patchouli on her wrists before leaving the house.
The room was filled with the heady scent of patchouli.
