road agent

noun
highway robber (esp. Old West)
Pronunciation: / roʊd ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / rəʊd ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person, especially in the American West during the 19th century, who stops and robs travelers on the road, often targeting stagecoaches or vehicles
