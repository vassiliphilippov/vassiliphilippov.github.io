noncarnivorous

adjective
not eating meat
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkɑrˈnɪvərəs / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkɑːˈnɪvərəs / (BrE)
not feeding on or consuming animal flesh

Examples

Noncarnivorous animals
Koalas are noncarnivorous mammals that feed almost exclusively on eucalyptus leaves.
The noncarnivorous diet of these birds consists mainly of seeds and fruit.
Many people choose a noncarnivorous lifestyle for ethical or health reasons.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carnivorous - feeding on animal flesh
  • synonym: herbivorous - feeding mainly on plants
