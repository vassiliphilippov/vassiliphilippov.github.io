Ursus

noun
genus including the brown bear and related species
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɝː.səs / (AmE), / ˈɜː.səs / (BrE)
The genus of mammals that includes the brown bear and, in some classifications, all true bears.

Examples

Ursus genus bears
Researchers debated whether the spectacled bear should also be placed in Ursus.
The genus Ursus includes some of the largest terrestrial carnivores.
The polar bear's scientific name is Ursus maritimus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bear - large heavy mammal with thick fur and a very short tail
