public violence

noun
violent act by a mob in public
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪk ˈvaɪələns / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk ˈvaɪələns / (BrE)
Violent actions carried out by a group or mob in a public place, often resulting in disorder or disturbance.

Examples

Public violence in streets
Reports of public violence have increased in several urban areas over the past year.
The city imposed a curfew to prevent further public violence after the protests turned chaotic.
The government condemned the outbreak of public violence following the controversial court ruling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: riot - a violent public disturbance by a crowd
