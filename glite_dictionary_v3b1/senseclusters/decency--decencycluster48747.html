decency

noun
quality of being polite and respectable
3.0 6.77/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàdiÀês…ônsi / (AmE), / ÀàdiÀês…ônsi / (BrE)
the quality or behavior of being polite, respectful, and following accepted moral or social standards

Examples

Basic human decency
Basic human decency requires us to treat others with respect.
She showed great decency by helping the stranger in need.
There is a certain level of decency expected in the workplace.
He acted with proper decency even when others did not.
It is a matter of decency to treat everyone with respect.
Many people believe that decency in public life is declining.
