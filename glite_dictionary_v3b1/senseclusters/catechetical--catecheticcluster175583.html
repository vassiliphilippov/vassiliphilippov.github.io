catechetical

adjective
relating to religious instruction or catechesis
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌkætəˈkɛtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌkatɪˈkɛtɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving catechesis, especially religious instruction by means of questions and answers.

Examples

Catechetical teaching method
She has a catechetical style of teaching that encourages questions from her students.
The bishop emphasized the importance of catechetical instruction in building a strong foundation of faith.
The church holds catechetical classes every Sunday for children preparing for confirmation.
