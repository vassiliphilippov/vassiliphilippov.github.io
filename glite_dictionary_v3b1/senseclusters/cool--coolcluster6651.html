cool

adjective
unfriendly, unenthusiastic, or unresponsive
3.5
Pronunciation: / kuːl / (AmE), / kuːl / (BrE)
not friendly, enthusiastic, or responsive; showing a lack of warmth or enthusiasm.

Examples

Cool response
Despite his efforts, the group remained cool towards his proposal.
Her cool response suggested she was not interested in the invitation.
There was a rather cool atmosphere in the meeting after the disagreement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aloof - not friendly or forthcoming; cool and distant
  • antonym: warm - friendly or enthusiastic in manner
