back and forth

adverb
moving in opposite directions repeatedly
1.0
Pronunciation: / bæk ənd fɔrθ / (AmE), / bæk ənd fɔːθ / (BrE)
in one direction and then in the opposite direction, often repeatedly

Examples

Move back and forth
She paced back and forth while waiting for the important phone call.
The children ran back and forth across the playground all afternoon.
The pendulum swung back and forth in a steady rhythm.
