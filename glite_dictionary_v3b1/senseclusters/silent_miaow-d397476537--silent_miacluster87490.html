silent miaow

noun
weak complaint
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪlənt miˈaʊ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪlənt mɪˈaʊ / (BrE)
a quiet or weak complaint, protest, or plea that is easily ignored or goes unnoticed

Examples

A silent miaow for help
Her request for help was just a silent miaow that no one in the busy office noticed.
He made a silent miaow about the unfair rules, but the teacher ignored him.
Sometimes a silent miaow is all someone can manage when they feel powerless.

Related Terms

  • antonym: outcry - a strong and public protest or complaint
  • synonym: whimper - a low, weak sound expressing pain or complaint
