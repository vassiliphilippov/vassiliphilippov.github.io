boi

interjection
informal exclamation
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɔɪ / (AmE), / bɔɪ / (BrE)
an informal exclamation used to express surprise, disbelief, amusement, or exasperation, especially online or in speech

Examples

Boi please
boi, you really thought that was a good idea?
When he saw the price, he just said, 'boi!'
After hearing the story, all I could say was 'boi, that's wild.'
