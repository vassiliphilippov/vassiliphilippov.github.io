somnambulatory behavior

noun
actions performed while sleepwalking
9.3 8.0
Pronunciation: /sɑːm.ˈnæm.bjʊ.lə.ri bɪ.ˈheɪ.vjər/ /; / /sɑːm.ˈnæm.bə.lə.ri bɪ.ˈheɪ.vjər/ (AmE), /sɒm.ˈnæm.bjʊ.lə.ri bɪ.ˈheɪ.vjə/ /; / /sɒm.ˈnæm.bə.lə.ri bɪ.ˈheɪ.vjə/ (BrE)

Examples

The safety of children with somnambulatory behavior requires extra precautions around stairs and doors.
