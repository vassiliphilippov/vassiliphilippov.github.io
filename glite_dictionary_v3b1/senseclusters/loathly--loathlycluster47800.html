loathly

adjective
highly offensive; disgusting
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊðli / (AmE), / ˈləʊðli / (BrE)
Highly offensive or disgusting; causing aversion or strong dislike.

Examples

A loathly stench
A loathly stench filled the abandoned cottage, making it impossible to enter.
His loathly deeds were condemned by all who heard of them.
The witch appeared in a loathly form to frighten the knights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: repellent - causing disgust or aversion
