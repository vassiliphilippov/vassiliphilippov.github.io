coffee break

noun
short rest period at work for coffee
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑfi ˌbreɪk /; / ˈkɔfi breɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi ˌbreɪk /; / ˈkɒfi breɪk / (BrE)
a short break from work to have coffee or other refreshments
