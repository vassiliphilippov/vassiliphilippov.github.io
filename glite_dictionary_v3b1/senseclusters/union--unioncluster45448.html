union

noun
state of being joined or combined
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːniən /; / ˈjunjən /; / ˈjun.jən / (AmE), / ˈjuːnɪən /; / ˈjuːniən /; / ˈjuː.njən / (BrE)
the act or state of being joined or combined to form one thing (including marriage, communities, materials, healing, or mating)
