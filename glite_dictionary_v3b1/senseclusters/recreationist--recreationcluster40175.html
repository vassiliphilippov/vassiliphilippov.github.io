recreationist

noun
recreation program manager
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛkriˈeɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌrɛkriˈeɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who plans, organizes, or manages recreational activities, programs, or facilities

Examples

A recreationist plans events
The city hired a recreationist to plan summer programs for local youth and families.
As a recreationist, she organizes sports leagues and community events throughout the year.
Many parks employ a recreationist to ensure activities are safe and enjoyable for everyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: event coordinator - a person who organizes and manages events
  • synonym: recreation director - a person in charge of recreational programs or facilities
