light-headed

adjective
silly; frivolous; not serious
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtˌhɛdəd / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
silly or lacking seriousness; inclined to frivolous or giddy behavior.

Examples

Light-headed jokes
During the meeting, her light-headed remarks lightened the mood but distracted from serious discussion.
His light-headed approach to responsibility worried his teachers and parents.
The comedian's light-headed attitude made the audience laugh throughout the evening.

Related Terms

  • antonym: serious - thoughtful, earnest, or not joking
  • synonym: silly - showing a lack of seriousness or common sense
