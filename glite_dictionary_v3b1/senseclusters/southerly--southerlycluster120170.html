southerly

adjective
situated in or facing south
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌðərli / (AmE), / ˈsʌðəli / (BrE)
situated in, facing, moving toward, or coming from the south (especially of direction, place, or wind)

Examples

Southerly direction
The birds migrated along a southerly route for the winter.
The storm is expected to move in a southerly direction tomorrow.
We enjoyed the warm sun from our southerly balcony.
A strong southerly breeze made the afternoon feel much warmer than usual.
Cold fronts are expected to move in after the southerly storm has passed.
The sailors adjusted their course to take advantage of the southerly winds.

Related Terms

  • antonym: northerly - situated in or facing north
  • synonym: southern - situated in or directed toward the south
