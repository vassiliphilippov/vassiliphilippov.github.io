lactational

adjective
milk production
6.3
Pronunciation: / lækˈteɪʃənəl / (AmE), / lækˈteɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to the production or secretion of milk by mammals

Examples

The lactational period
The lactational period is crucial for the nutrition of newborn animals.
Doctors studied the lactational changes in hormone levels after childbirth.
Proper diet during the lactational phase supports both mother and infant health.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lactating - producing milk
  • antonym: nonlactating - not producing milk
