beg on one's knees

verb
plead earnestly and humbly
1.0 5.6
Pronunciation: /beɡ ɑːn wʌnz niːz/ /; / /beɡ ən wʌnz niːz/ /; / /beɡ ən wʌns niːz/ (AmE), /beɡ ɒn wʌnz niːz/ /; / /beɡ ən wʌnz niːz/ /; / /beɡ ɒn wʌns niːz/ (BrE)
to plead earnestly and humbly, often expressing deep desperation or supplication

Examples

To beg on one's knees earnestly
After making a terrible mistake, he had to beg on his knees for a second chance.
No matter how hard he begged on his knees, she wouldn't change her mind.
She refused to beg on her knees, insisting on maintaining her dignity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: implore - to beg someone earnestly or desperately
  • synonym: plead - to make an emotional or earnest appeal
