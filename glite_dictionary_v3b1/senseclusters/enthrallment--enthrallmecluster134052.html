enthrallment

noun
state of being fascinated or captivated
7.7
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈθrɔlmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈθrɔːlmənt / (BrE)
a state of being captivated or deeply fascinated by something wonderful or unusual

Examples

Deep enthrallment with music
His enthrallment with classical music began when he was a young child.
She listened in enthrallment as the storyteller described distant lands and magical creatures.
The audience watched the performance in complete enthrallment, unable to look away.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boredom - the state of being bored, the opposite of fascination
  • synonym: captivation - the state of being intensely interested or charmed
