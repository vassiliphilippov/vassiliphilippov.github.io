easterly

adjective
situated in or toward the east
Pronunciation: / ˈiːstɚli / (AmE), / ˈiːstəli / (BrE)
Situated in or facing toward the east.

Examples

Easterly side
The city expanded into its easterly districts as the population grew.
The house has an easterly view, allowing us to see the sunrise every day.
We hiked along the easterly edge of the forest, enjoying the morning sunlight.
