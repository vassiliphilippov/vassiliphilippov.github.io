lumpsucker

noun
a small marine fish with a sucking disc
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌmpˌsʌkər / (AmE), / ˈlʌmpˌsʌkə / (BrE)
A small marine fish, typically with a rounded body and modified pelvic fins forming a sucking disc, belonging to the family Cyclopteridae.

Examples

A lumpsucker fish
Divers observed a brightly colored lumpsucker hiding among the seaweed.
Juvenile lumpsuckers are often seen near rocky shorelines in spring.
The lumpsucker attached itself to the glass wall of the aquarium with its suction disc.
