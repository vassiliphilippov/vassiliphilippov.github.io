intersexuality

noun
intersex identity or movement
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˌsɛkʃuˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌɪntəsɛkʃuˈælɪti / (BrE)
the condition or social identity of having biological traits that do not fit typical definitions of male or female, and the related community or movement advocating for rights and recognition

Examples

Social intersexuality
The organization works to promote awareness and acceptance of intersexuality in society.
Many people are learning more about intersexuality and the challenges faced by intersex individuals.
Laws protecting the rights of those with intersexuality are being discussed in several countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gender identity - a person's internal sense of their own gender
  • synonym: intersex - a person born with reproductive or sexual anatomy that does not fit typical definitions of male or female
