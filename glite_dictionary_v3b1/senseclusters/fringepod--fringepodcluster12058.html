fringepod

noun
annual herb with winged one-seeded fruits
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪndʒˌpɑd / (AmE), / ˈfrɪndʒˌpɒd / (BrE)
An annual herb with deeply divided basal leaves and slender clusters of small white flowers, followed by one-seeded winged fruits.

Examples

The fringepod plant
After blooming, the fringepod produces winged seeds that are easily carried by the wind.
The delicate white flowers of the fringepod attract many pollinators in the meadow.
We found a patch of fringepod growing near the riverbank in early spring.
