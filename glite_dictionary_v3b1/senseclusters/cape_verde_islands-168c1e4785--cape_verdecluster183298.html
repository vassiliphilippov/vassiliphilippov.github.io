Cape Verde Islands

proper noun
archipelago off West Africa (Cabo Verde)
Pronunciation: / keɪp vɜrd ˈaɪləndz / (AmE), / keɪp vɜːd ˈaɪləndz / (BrE)
a group of islands in the Atlantic Ocean located off the western coast of Africa, near Senegal; now known as Cabo Verde
