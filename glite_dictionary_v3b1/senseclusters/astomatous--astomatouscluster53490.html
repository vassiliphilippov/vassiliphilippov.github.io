astomatous

adjective
lacking a mouth or mouthlike opening
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈstoʊmətəs / (AmE), / əˈstəʊmətəs / (BrE)
Having no mouth or mouthlike opening, especially used in zoology and biology.

Examples

Astomatous organism
An astomatous organism does not require a mouth for its mode of feeding.
Some parasitic worms are astomatous and absorb nutrients through their skin.
The astomatous nature of certain larvae makes them unique among related species.
