tend

verb
care for; look after
Pronunciation: / tɛnd / (AmE), / tɛnd / (BrE)
to care for, look after, or manage someone or something

Examples

To tend a garden
Every morning, she tends the garden carefully before breakfast.
He tends to his elderly father's needs with great dedication.
The nurse tended the patient during his recovery in the hospital.
During the summer, college students often tend local cafes for extra income.
It's his duty to tend the information desk during the afternoon shift.
She has tended the bookstore on the corner for over twenty years.
