aerodynamicist

noun
airflow scientist
9.2
Pronunciation: /er.oʊ.daɪ.ˈnæm.ɪst/ (AmE), /eə.rəʊ.daɪ.ˈnæm.ɪst/ (BrE)
a scientist or engineer who studies the movement of air around objects and designs shapes to reduce air resistance

Examples

The aerodynamicist studies air
The aerodynamicist worked with the design team to make the airplane more fuel-efficient.
As an aerodynamicist, she analyzes how wind affects high-speed trains.
Every modern race car relies on the expertise of an aerodynamicist to maximize speed.
