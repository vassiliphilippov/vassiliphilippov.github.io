pledge

verb
make a solemn formal promise
5.2 3.3
Pronunciation: / plɛdʒ / (AmE), / plɛdʒ / (BrE)
to make a solemn or formal promise or commitment, or to give something as a guarantee

Examples

To pledge loyalty
Every new citizen must pledge allegiance to the country during the ceremony.
She pledged to support her friend no matter what happened.
The mayor pledged that taxes would not be increased this year.
After the ceremony, she pledged never to reveal the secret.
He pledged his support to the new environmental project.
The company pledges to reduce its carbon emissions by 2030.
He pledged his word that he would keep the information confidential.
She pledged her support to the charity during the fundraiser.
The company pledged its assets as collateral for the loan.
