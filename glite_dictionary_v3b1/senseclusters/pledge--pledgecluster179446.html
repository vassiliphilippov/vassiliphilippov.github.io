Synonyms

  • vow
    3.5 10/m
    vow is a very solemn personal promise often made at events like weddings or ceremonies, while pledge is broader and can be a formal guarantee.
  • swear
    4.6 36/m
    swear is to make an oath or solemn statement, often in court or religion, while pledge often means promising or giving a guarantee.
  • promise
    1.0 98/m
    promise is a general assurance people use in daily speech, while pledge is more formal and can also mean giving something as guarantee.
  • covenant
    8.1 0.15/m
    covenant is a formal legal or religious agreement written between parties, while pledge is often a single solemn promise or guarantee.
