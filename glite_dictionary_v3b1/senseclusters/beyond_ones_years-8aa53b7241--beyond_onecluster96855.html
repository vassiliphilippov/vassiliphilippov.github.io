beyond one's years

adjective
more mature than one's age suggests
1.0
Pronunciation: / biˈjɑnd wʌnz jɪrz / (AmE), / bɪˈjɒnd wʌnz jɪəz / (BrE)
More mature, experienced, or wise than would be expected for someone of their age.

Examples

Beyond her years maturity
Her understanding of complex issues is beyond her years.
People often say she's wise beyond her years when they hear her speak.
The boy's patience and kindness were remarkable and clearly beyond his years.
