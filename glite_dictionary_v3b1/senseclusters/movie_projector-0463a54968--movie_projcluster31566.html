movie projector

noun
device for projecting films
2.7 0.05/m

a device that projects a series of images from a reel of film onto a screen to create the appearance of moving pictures

Examples

Synonyms

  • film projector
    3.4 0.06/m
    film projector is the usual, everyday name for a device that shows moving images from film, while movie projector is a broader, informal term.
  • cine projector
    4.6 0.01/m
    cine projector is a technical or older term used by cinemas and technicians to show film frames, while movie projector is the common modern name.
