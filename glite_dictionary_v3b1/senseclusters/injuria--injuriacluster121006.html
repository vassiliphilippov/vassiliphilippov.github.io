injuria

noun
act of injustice
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʒʊəriə / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒʊəriə / (BrE)
an act of injustice or unfair treatment, especially in archaic or literary contexts

Examples

An injuria suffered
The villagers suffered many injuria at the hands of the corrupt officials.
In ancient texts, injuria is often used to describe acts of oppression.
The poet lamented the injuria inflicted upon the innocent by the powerful.

Related Terms

  • synonym: injustice - unfair treatment or a violation of rights
  • antonym: justice - fairness or the quality of being just
  • synonym: wrong - an act that is morally or legally wrong
