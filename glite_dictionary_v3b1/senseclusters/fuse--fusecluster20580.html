fuse

noun
cord or tube for igniting explosives
6.1 2.51/m
Pronunciation: / fjuz / (AmE), / fjuÀêz / (BrE)
a device consisting of a cord or tube filled with combustible material, used to ignite explosives or propellants

Examples

A bomb fuse
A slow-burning fuse was used to control the timing of the explosion.
Make sure the fuse is securely attached to the firework before lighting it.
The technician lit the fuse and quickly moved to a safe distance.

Synonyms

  • safety fuse
    4.2 0.02/m
    safety fuse is a slow-burning cord used to light detonators from a safe distance, while fuse is a simple combustible tube for igniting explosives.
  • fuze
    9.9 0.02/m
    fuze is an igniter in munitions that often uses mechanical or electrical triggers, while fuse is a simple burning cord used to light explosives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detonator - a device used to set off an explosive
