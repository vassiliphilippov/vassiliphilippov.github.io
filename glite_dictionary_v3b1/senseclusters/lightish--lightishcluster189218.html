lightish

adjective
somewhat light
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtɪʃ / (BrE)
somewhat light in color, weight, or intensity; fairly light but not completely

Examples

A lightish green dress
She chose a lightish green dress for the summer party.
The suitcase felt lightish compared to the others in the group.
We painted the kitchen a lightish yellow to make it feel brighter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dark - having little or no light; not light in color
  • degree higher: light - having a pale color or not heavy
  • synonym: pale - having a very light color
