blowingly

adverb
by blowing
Pronunciation: / ˈbloʊɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈbləʊɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner produced by blowing, wind, or breath

Examples

Blowingly in the wind
The curtains moved blowingly in the breeze coming through the open window.
During the parade, the flags waved blowingly above the marching band.
Leaves scattered blowingly across the yard as the autumn wind picked up.
