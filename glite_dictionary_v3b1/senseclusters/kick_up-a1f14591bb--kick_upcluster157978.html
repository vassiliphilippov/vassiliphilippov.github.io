kick up

noun
first movement in a handstand (gymnastics)
Pronunciation: / kɪk ʌp / (AmE), / kɪk ʌp / (BrE)
A movement in which one raises the feet backward with the hands on the ground, typically as the first step in performing a handstand.

Examples

A strong kick up
Make sure your kick up is strong enough to get both legs over your head.
Practicing the kick up daily can improve your balance and strength.
She struggled at first with the kick up but soon managed to hold a handstand.
