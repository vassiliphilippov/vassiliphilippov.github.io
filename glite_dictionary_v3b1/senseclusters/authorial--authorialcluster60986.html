authorial

adjective
relating to an author
Pronunciation: / əˈθɔriəl / (AmE), / ɔːˈθɔːrɪəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of an author; made or done by an author.

Examples

The authorial voice
Critics praised the authorial voice evident throughout her essays.
In his biography, there are few authorial intrusions, letting the facts speak for themselves.
The novel contains several authorial comments that reveal the writer’s personal opinions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: writerly - relating to or characteristic of a writer
