corona

noun
circle of light around a luminous object
6.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈroʊnə / (AmE), / kəˈrəʊnə / (BrE)
A circle or set of concentric circles of light seen around a luminous object, such as the sun or moon, caused by diffraction or other optical phenomena.

Examples

Moon corona
A brilliant corona surrounded the full moon, lighting up the night sky.
During the solar eclipse, the sun’s corona became visible to the naked eye.
There was a colorful corona around the streetlamp due to the mist in the air.

Related Terms

  • synonym: halo - a circle of light appearing to surround the sun or moon
