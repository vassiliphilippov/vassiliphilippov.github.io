unacademic

adjective
not scholarly
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnækəˈdɛmɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnækəˈdɛmɪk / (BrE)
not related to, typical of, or suitable for academic study or institutions

Examples

Unacademic job skills
She took an unacademic route to success, learning most of her skills on the job.
His interests are mostly unacademic, such as sports and music.
The workshop offered a relaxed, unacademic environment for creative thinking.

Related Terms

  • antonym: academic - relating to education, schools, or scholarly study
  • synonym: nonacademic - not connected with academic study or institutions
