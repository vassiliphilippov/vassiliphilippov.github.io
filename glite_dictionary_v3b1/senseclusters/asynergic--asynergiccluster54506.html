asynergic

adjective
relating to lack of synergy (medical)
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪsɪˈnɜrdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌeɪsɪˈnɜːdʒɪk / (BrE)
(of muscles or organs) lacking the normal coordination of movement; relating to asynergy.

Examples

Asynergic muscle contractions
After the stroke, he had asynergic contractions in his upper limb.
Patients with asynergic muscle movement may experience difficulties with coordination.
The echocardiogram showed an asynergic segment of the left ventricle.
