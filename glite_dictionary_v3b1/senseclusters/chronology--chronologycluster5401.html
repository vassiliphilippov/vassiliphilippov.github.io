chronology

noun
record of events by order of occurrence
Pronunciation: / krəˈnɑlədʒi /; / krəˈnɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / krəˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the order or arrangement of events in the order they happened, or a record of this order
