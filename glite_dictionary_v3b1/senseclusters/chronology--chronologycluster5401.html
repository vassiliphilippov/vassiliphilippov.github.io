chronology

noun
record of events by order of occurrence
Pronunciation: / krəˈnɑlədʒi /; / krəˈnɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / krəˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the order or arrangement of events in the order they happened, or a record of this order

Examples

A chronology of events

The chronology of the king's reign was carefully documented by historians.
The police created a detailed chronology of events leading up to the accident.

The chronology of events leading up to the war was carefully documented.
