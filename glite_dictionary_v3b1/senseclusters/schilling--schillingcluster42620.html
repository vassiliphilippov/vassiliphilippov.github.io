schilling

noun
former Austrian currency unit
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Austria before the adoption of the euro, used until 2002

Examples

Old schillings coins
He kept a few old schillings as souvenirs from his travels in Austria.
Shops in Vienna no longer accept the schilling as legal tender.
The Austrian schilling was replaced by the euro in 2002.
