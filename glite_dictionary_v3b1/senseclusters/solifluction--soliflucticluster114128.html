solifluction

noun
slow soil movement
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊlɪˈflʌkʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɒlɪˈflʌkʃən / (BrE)
the slow downhill movement of water-saturated soil, especially in cold regions where the ground thaws seasonally
