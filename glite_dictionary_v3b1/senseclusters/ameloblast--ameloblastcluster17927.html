ameloblast

noun
cell that forms tooth enamel
Pronunciation: / əˈmiːləˌblæst / (AmE), / əˈmiːləbˌlɑːst / (BrE)
a specialized cell that forms the enamel of a developing tooth

Examples

A ameloblast cell
Damage to the ameloblasts can result in enamel defects in the mature tooth.
During tooth development, ameloblasts are responsible for the secretion of enamel proteins.
Researchers study the function of the ameloblast to better understand enamel formation.
