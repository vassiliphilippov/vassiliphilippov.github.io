isolette

noun
infant incubator
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪsəˈlɛt / (AmE), / ˌaɪsəˈlɛt / (BrE)
a small, enclosed crib with controlled temperature and humidity used to care for premature or ill newborns

Examples

A isolette crib
The nurse carefully placed the newborn into the isolette to monitor his vital signs.
Parents could visit their baby through the clear walls of the isolette in the neonatal unit.
The hospital purchased a new isolette to improve care for premature infants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incubator - a device used to keep premature or ill babies warm and protected
