ressentiment

noun
collective bitterness
Pronunciation: / rɛˌsɑ̃tiˈmɑ̃ / (AmE), / rɛˌsɒ̃tiˈmɒ̃ / (BrE)
a deep and lasting feeling of bitterness and hostility, especially because of feeling wronged, powerless, or treated unfairly
