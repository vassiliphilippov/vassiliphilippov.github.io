Rhodesia

proper noun
former name of Zimbabwe, African country
8.6
Pronunciation: / roʊˈdiːʒə / (AmE), / rəʊˈdiːzɪə / (BrE)
the former name of a country in south-central Africa, which became Zimbabwe after gaining independence from the United Kingdom in 1980

Examples

Rhodesia before independence
Her family lived in Rhodesia before moving to South Africa in the 1970s.
In the 1960s, Rhodesia declared independence from Britain, leading to international controversy.
Many historical maps still include Rhodesia as the name for present-day Zimbabwe.
