viscid mushroom

noun
cultivated edible mushroom, enoki
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪsɪd ˈmʌʃˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈvɪsɪd ˈmʌʃˌrʊm / (BrE)
a cultivated edible mushroom with a slender stem and small cap, popular in Japanese cuisine; also known as enoki

Examples

The viscid mushroom enoki
Enoki, or viscid mushroom, is valued for its delicate texture and mild flavor.
Fresh viscid mushrooms are widely used in soups and salads in Japan.
The market sells a variety of viscid mushrooms alongside other exotic fungi.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enoki mushroom - a cultivated mushroom with small cap and long stem used in East Asian cuisine
