cortisone

noun
a steroid hormone used to treat inflammation
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrtɪˌzoʊn / (AmE), / ˈkɔːtɪzəʊn / (BrE)
a steroid hormone produced by the adrenal cortex, used medically to treat inflammation and other conditions

Examples

Cortisone treatment
Cortisone is sometimes used to treat severe allergic reactions.
Long-term use of cortisone can lead to side effects such as weight gain.
The doctor prescribed cortisone injections to reduce the swelling in her knee.
