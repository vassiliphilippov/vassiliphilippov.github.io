shearing

noun
the act of cutting off wool or hair
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of cutting off wool, hair, or similar material from an animal or object, usually with shears or clippers

Examples

The shearing of sheep
After the shearing, the wool is sorted and cleaned for processing.
He learned the art of shearing from his grandfather, who was a skilled shepherd.
The shearing of the sheep takes place every spring at the farm.
