bashing

noun
severe criticism
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbæʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbæʃɪŋ / (BrE)
harsh or severe criticism or verbal attack, often repeated or excessive

Examples

Media bashing
The celebrity faced constant bashing from the press after the incident.
Online bashing can be very hurtful and damaging to a person's reputation.
He was tired of the endless bashing he received for his unpopular opinions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: criticism - the act of expressing disapproval or pointing out faults
  • antonym: praise - the act of expressing approval or admiration
