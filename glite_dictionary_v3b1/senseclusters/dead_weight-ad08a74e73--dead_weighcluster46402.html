dead weight

noun
a heavy or oppressive burden
2.1 3.6
Pronunciation: /ded weɪt/ (AmE), /ded weɪt/ (BrE)
a heavy object, person, or responsibility that is difficult to carry or that makes progress harder (can be literal or figurative)
