tig

noun
arc welding method
Pronunciation: / tɪɡ / (AmE), / tɪɡ / (BrE)
a method of arc welding that uses a non-consumable tungsten electrode and an inert gas to produce precise welds

Examples

Tig welding process
The welder used tig to join the thin aluminum sheets with great precision.
Learning tig requires steady hands and careful control of the welding torch.
For delicate metalwork, many professionals choose tig over other welding techniques.

Related Terms

  • antonym: MIG welding - metal inert gas welding, a different arc welding process
  • synonym: TIG welding - tungsten inert gas welding, a precise arc welding process
