unturned

adjective
not examined
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtɜrnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɜːnd / (BrE)
not examined, investigated, or explored; left as it is or not dealt with.

Examples

No clue unturned
The detective promised to leave no clue unturned in the search for the missing child.
Many stones were left unturned during the rushed investigation.
She was determined that no possibility would remain unturned in her quest for answers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: explored - examined or investigated thoroughly
  • synonym: untouched - not affected, changed, or dealt with
