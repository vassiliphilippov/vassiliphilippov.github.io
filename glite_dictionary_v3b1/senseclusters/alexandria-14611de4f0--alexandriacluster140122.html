Alexandria

proper noun
port city in northern Egypt
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˌælɪɡˈzændriə / (AmE), / ˌælɪɡˈzændriə / (BrE)
A major port city in northern Egypt on the Mediterranean Sea, founded by Alexander the Great and historically the capital of ancient Egypt.
