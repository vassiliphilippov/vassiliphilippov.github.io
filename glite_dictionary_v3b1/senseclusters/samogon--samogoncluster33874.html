samogon

noun
homemade alcohol
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːməˌɡɒn / (AmE), / ˈsæməˌɡɒn / (BrE)
a strong homemade alcoholic drink, especially common in Russia and Eastern Europe

Examples

A glass of samogon
He poured a glass of samogon for each guest at the table.
Many villagers make their own samogon using traditional family recipes.
The taste of homemade samogon can be much stronger than store-bought spirits.

  • degree lower: vodka - a clear distilled alcoholic beverage from Eastern Europe
