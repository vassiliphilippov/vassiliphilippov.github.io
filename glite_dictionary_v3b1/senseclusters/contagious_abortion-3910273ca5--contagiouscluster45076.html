contagious abortion

noun
infectious disease causing animal abortion
Pronunciation: / kənˈteɪdʒəs əˈbɔrʃən / (AmE), / kənˈteɪdʒəs əˈbɔːʃən / (BrE)
an infectious disease of domestic animals, such as cattle and sheep, that often causes spontaneous abortion and can be transmitted to humans.

Examples

Contagious abortion disease
The outbreak of contagious abortion threatened to wipe out the farmer's entire herd.
Veterinarians implemented strict measures to control contagious abortion in the region.
Zookeepers are trained to prevent the spread of contagious abortion among susceptible livestock.
