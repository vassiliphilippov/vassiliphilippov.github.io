gumshield

noun
mouthguard for sports
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌmˌʃiːld / (AmE), / ˈɡʌmʃiːld / (BrE)
A protective device worn over the teeth and gums to reduce injury during contact sports, especially boxing.

Examples

A gumshield for boxing
Boxers are required to wear a gumshield during matches to protect against injuries.
He forgot his gumshield and had to borrow one for rugby practice.
The dentist recommended a custom-fitted gumshield for better comfort and protection.
