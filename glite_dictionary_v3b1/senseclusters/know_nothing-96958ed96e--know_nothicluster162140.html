know nothing

verb
be completely uninformed
Pronunciation: / noʊ ˈnʌθɪŋ / (AmE), / nəʊ ˈnʌθɪŋ / (BrE)
to have no knowledge or information about something; to be completely uninformed

Examples

To know nothing about science
Despite hours of study, I still know nothing about quantum physics.
She realized she knew nothing about gardening until she tried to grow tomatoes.
When pressed for details, he claimed to know nothing about the incident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: be ignorant - to lack knowledge or awareness
  • antonym: be informed - to possess knowledge or information
