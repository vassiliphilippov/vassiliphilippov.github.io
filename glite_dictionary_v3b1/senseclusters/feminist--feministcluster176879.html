feminist

noun
supporter of feminism
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛmənɪst / (AmE), / ˈfɛmɪnɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports feminism and equal rights for women

Related Terms

  • synonym: activist - a person who campaigns for social or political change
  • synonym: feminism - the advocacy of women's rights on the ground of the equality of the sexes
  • antonym: misogynist - a person who dislikes, despises, or is strongly prejudiced against women
  • antonym: sexist - a person who discriminates based on gender
