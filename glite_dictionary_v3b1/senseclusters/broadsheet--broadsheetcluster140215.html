broadsheet

noun
large advertising sheet distributed widely
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɔːdˌʃiːt / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːdʃiːt / (BrE)
a large sheet of paper, often used as an advertisement or notice, intended for wide distribution

Examples

A festival broadsheet
Each household received a colorful broadsheet in the post advertising the festival.
Several local businesses distributed a broadsheet announcing their spring discounts to residents.
The charity designed a broadsheet to raise awareness about their latest campaign.

Related Terms

  • synonym: circular - an advertisement or notice distributed to many people
  • synonym: flyer - a small handbill advertising an event or product
