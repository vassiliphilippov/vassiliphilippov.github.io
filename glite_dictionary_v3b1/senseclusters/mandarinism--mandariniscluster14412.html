mandarinism

noun
bureaucratic style
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmændəˌrɪnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈmændərɪnɪzəm / (BrE)
the style, practices, or influence of mandarins, especially in government bureaucracy or formal language

Examples

The mandarinism style
The report criticized the department's mandarinism for making simple procedures unnecessarily complex.
Some writers avoid mandarinism in their work to make their ideas clearer to readers.
He believed that mandarinism in the organization slowed down important decisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bureaucracy - a system of government or management with many rules and officials
  • antonym: simplicity - the quality of being easy to understand or not complicated
