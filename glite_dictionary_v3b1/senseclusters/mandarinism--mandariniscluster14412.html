mandarinism

noun
bureaucratic style
Pronunciation: / ˈmændəˌrɪnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈmændərɪnɪzəm / (BrE)
the style, practices, or influence of mandarins, especially in government bureaucracy or formal language

Examples

He believed that mandarinism in the organization slowed down important decisions.
