wilderness

noun
wild, uninhabited natural area
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪldɚnəs / (AmE), / ˈwɪldənɪs / (BrE)
an area of land that has not been significantly modified by human activity, remaining in a natural and uninhabited state

Examples

A wilderness area
After the fire, the once-cultivated land quickly reverted to wilderness.
The national park aims to protect the remaining wilderness for future generations.
We spent two weeks hiking through the vast wilderness of Alaska.

Related Terms

  • antonym: civilization - an area with settled communities and human development
  • synonym: desert - a dry, barren area of land
  • synonym: forest - a large area covered chiefly with trees and undergrowth
