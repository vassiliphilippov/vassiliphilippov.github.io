unpatted

adjective
not gently touched
7.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpætɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpætɪd / (BrE)

Examples

Unpatted dog in corner
The shy dog remained unpatted in the corner during the party.
After the ceremony, the trophy sat unpatted on the table, waiting for its winner.
The kitten looked lonely and unpatted while the others played nearby.
