antiseptically

adverb
in a germ-free way
Pronunciation: / ˌæntɪˈsɛptɪkli / (AmE), / ˌæntɪˈsɛptɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that is very clean or free from germs, often using antiseptics

Examples

Clean antiseptically
The surgical instruments were handled antiseptically to prevent infection.
She cleaned the kitchen counters antiseptically after preparing raw chicken.
The nurse dressed the wound antiseptically to ensure it healed properly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cleanly - in a clean or neat way
  • antonym: contaminated - having been made impure by exposure to germs or harmful substances
  • synonym: sterilely - in a completely clean or germ-free way
