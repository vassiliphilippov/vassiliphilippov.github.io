flyback

noun
chronograph reset feature
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈflaɪˌbæk / (BrE)
a chronograph feature that allows the timing hand to be reset to zero and immediately restarted with a single action

Examples

Flyback chronograph function
The pilot used the flyback function to quickly time each leg of the flight.
A watch with a flyback is especially useful for timing rapid, consecutive events.
He demonstrated how the flyback mechanism allows instant resetting and restarting of the stopwatch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chronograph - a watch with a built-in stopwatch function
  • synonym: stopwatch - a device used for measuring time intervals
