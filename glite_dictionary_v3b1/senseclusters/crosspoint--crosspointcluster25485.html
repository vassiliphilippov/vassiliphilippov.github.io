crosspoint

noun
intersection point
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔsˌpɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌpɔɪnt / (BrE)
a place where two lines, paths, or circuits intersect, especially in electronics or network systems

Examples

A crosspoint in circuits
The technician identified a faulty crosspoint in the network switch.
Each crosspoint on the matrix allows a unique connection between input and output lines.
In the diagram, the crosspoint marks where the two circuits intersect.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intersection - a point where two or more things cross
  • synonym: junction - a place where things join or meet
