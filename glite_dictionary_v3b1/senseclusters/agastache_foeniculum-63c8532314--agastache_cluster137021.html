Agastache foeniculum

noun
anise-scented North American herb
Pronunciation: / əˈɡæstəki foʊˈnɪkjələm / (AmE), / əˈɡæstəkɪ fəʊˈnɪkjʊləm / (BrE)
A much-branched North American herb with a fragrance similar to fennel, commonly known as anise hyssop, used in gardens and herbal teas.
