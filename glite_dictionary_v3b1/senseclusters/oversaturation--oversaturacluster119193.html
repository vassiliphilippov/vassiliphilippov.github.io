a situation where the supply of a product or service greatly exceeds demand, leading to reduced sales and profits

Examples

Oversaturation of the market
The oversaturation of coffee shops in the city made it hard for new businesses to survive.
Analysts warned that oversaturation in the streaming market could lead to company closures.
Due to oversaturation, many products remained unsold on store shelves for months.

Synonyms

  • oversupply
    4.5 0.48/m
    oversupply is an excess of goods that lowers prices in the market, while oversaturation describes supply far above demand that cuts sales.
  • glut
    6.8 1.33/m
    glut is a large excess of a product that lowers its value, while oversaturation focuses on too much supply that reduces sales.
  • overabundance
    5.7 0.45/m
    overabundance means simply having more than needed, while oversaturation implies market supply far above demand that harms profits.
