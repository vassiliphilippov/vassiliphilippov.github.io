direction

noun
focus of attention or purpose
Pronunciation: / dəˈrɛkʃən, daɪˈrɛkʃən / (AmE), / dəˈrɛkʃən, dɪˈrɛkʃən / (BrE)
the degree to which a person's attention, efforts, or aspirations are focused toward a particular aim or purpose

Examples

Direction in life
After graduating, she struggled to find direction in her career.
His life lacked direction until he discovered his passion for teaching.
The new project gave the team a sense of direction and motivation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aimlessness - lack of direction or purpose
  • synonym: focus - concentration of attention or effort
