materialman

noun
supplier of building materials
Pronunciation: / məˈtɪriəlˌmæn / (AmE), / məˈtɪəriəlˌmæn / (BrE)
a person or company that supplies building materials to a construction project, especially in a legal context

Examples

The materialman delivers lumber
The materialman delivered the lumber to the construction site early in the morning.
If the builder does not pay, the materialman can file a lien against the property.
Several materialmen attended the meeting to discuss supply contracts for the new project.
