impactive

adjective
relating to impact
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpæktɪv / (AmE), / ɪmˈpæktɪv / (BrE)
causing or relating to a physical impact, collision, or forceful strike.

Examples

Impactive force
The impactive force of the hammer broke the stone in two.
Engineers studied the impactive effects of debris hitting the spacecraft.
The impactive sound echoed through the hall after the heavy door slammed shut.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impactful - having a strong effect or impact
