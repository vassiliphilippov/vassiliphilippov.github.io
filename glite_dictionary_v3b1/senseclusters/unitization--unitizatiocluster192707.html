unitization

noun
conversion of an investment trust into a unit investment trust
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌjunɪtəˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌjuːnɪtəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪtəˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌjuːnɪtaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of converting an investment trust into a unit investment trust, where individual units can be bought and sold by investors
