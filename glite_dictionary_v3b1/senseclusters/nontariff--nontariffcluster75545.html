nontariff

noun
trade restriction measure
7.7
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈtær.ɪf/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈtær.ɪf/ (BrE)
a regulatory or administrative measure that restricts or controls trade without using tariffs

Examples

A nontariff rule
A single nontariff can make it difficult for small exporters to enter that market.
The government introduced a new nontariff to control the import of certain goods.
Many countries use at least one nontariff to protect their domestic industries.

  • antonym: tariff - a tax or duty on imported or exported goods
  • synonym: trade barrier - a government policy or regulation that restricts international trade
