order Ganoidei

noun
group of extinct primitive fishes with bony scales
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər ɡæniˈoʊdiː / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ˌɡænɔɪˈdiː / (BrE)
a group of mostly extinct primitive bony fishes characterized by armor-like bony scales, formerly recognized in zoological classification

Examples

Order Ganoidei fishes
Armor-like scales are a distinctive feature of fish in the order Ganoidei.

The fossils of the order Ganoidei provide valuable insight into the evolution of fish.
