alimentary tract

noun
the digestive passage from mouth to anus
Pronunciation: / ˌæləmənˈtɛri trækt / (AmE), / ˌælɪmənˈtɜːri trækt / (BrE)
the tubular passage in the body, extending from the mouth to the anus, through which food passes and is digested and eliminated

Examples

The alimentary tract passage
Disorders of the alimentary tract can affect nutrition and overall health.
Food travels through the alimentary tract where it is digested and absorbed.
The doctor explained how the alimentary tract breaks down and expels waste from the body.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digestive tract - the passage through which food passes in the body
  • synonym: gastrointestinal tract - the main part of the digestive system from stomach to anus
