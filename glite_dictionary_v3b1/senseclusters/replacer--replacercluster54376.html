replacer

noun
text substitution tool
4.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpleɪsər / (AmE), / rɪˈpleɪsə / (BrE)
a program, tool, or function that finds and substitutes specified text or data with other text or data

Examples

Text replacer tool
The replacer quickly updated all outdated terms in the document.
She used a replacer to change every instance of 'colour' to 'color' in the file.
A good replacer can save hours of manual editing for large projects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: editor - a program used to modify text or data
  • synonym: find and replace - a function that searches for and replaces text
