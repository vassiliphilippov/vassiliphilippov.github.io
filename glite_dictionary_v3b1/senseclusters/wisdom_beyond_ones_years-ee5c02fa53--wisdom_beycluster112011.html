wisdom beyond one's years

noun
maturity or judgment unusual for age
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪzdəm bɪˈjɑnd wʌnz jɪrz / (AmE), / ˈwɪzdəm bɪˈjɒnd wʌnz jɪəz / (BrE)
greater understanding, judgment, or maturity than is typical for someone of a particular age
