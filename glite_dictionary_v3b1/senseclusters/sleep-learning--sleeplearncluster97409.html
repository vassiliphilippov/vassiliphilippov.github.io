sleep-learning

noun
learning while asleep using recordings
Pronunciation: / sliːp ˈlɜrnɪŋ / (AmE), / sliːp ˈlɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of acquiring knowledge or skills by receiving information while asleep, often through recordings
