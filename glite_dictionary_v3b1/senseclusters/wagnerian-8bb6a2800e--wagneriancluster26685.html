Wagnerian

adjective
relating to Richard Wagner or his music
Pronunciation: / wɑːɡˈnɪriən / (AmE), / wæɡˈnɪəriən / (BrE)
Relating to the German composer Richard Wagner or his music, especially as characterized by dramatic intensity, complexity, or grand scale.

Examples

Wagnerian opera
Her performance had a distinctly Wagnerian quality, with powerful vocals and dramatic flair.
Some critics admire the Wagnerian complexity of his later symphonies.
The opera was staged with a Wagnerian grandeur that captivated the audience.
