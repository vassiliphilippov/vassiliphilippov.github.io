haycart

noun
cart for hay
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪkɑrt / (AmE), / ˈheɪkɑːt / (BrE)
a cart designed for transporting hay, typically pulled by horses or oxen on a farm

Examples

A haycart on farm
The old haycart stood in the barn, ready for the next harvest season.
Workers filled the haycart with bales before heading back to the farmhouse.
A pair of oxen pulled the loaded haycart across the field at sunset.
