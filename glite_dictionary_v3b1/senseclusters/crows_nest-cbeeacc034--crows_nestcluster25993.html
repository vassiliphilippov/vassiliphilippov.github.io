crow's nest

noun
lookout platform high on a ship's mast
6.9
Pronunciation: / kɹoʊz nɛst / (AmE), / krəʊz nɛst / (BrE)
A small platform near the top of a ship's mast, used as a lookout point.

Examples

The crow's nest platform
From the crow's nest, the lookout could spot distant ships and obstacles.
The sailor climbed to the crow's nest to search for land on the horizon.
They replaced the old crow's nest during the ship’s restoration.
