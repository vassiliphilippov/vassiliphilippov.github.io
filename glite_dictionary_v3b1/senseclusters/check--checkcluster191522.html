check

noun
mark indicating completion or verification
2.2
Pronunciation: / tʃɛk / (AmE), / tʃɛk / (BrE)
a mark (✔) placed beside an item to show it has been noted, verified, or completed

Examples

A check mark
He drew a check next to his name to indicate attendance.
Place a check in the box next to each task you've finished.
The teacher put a check by every correct answer.

Related Terms
