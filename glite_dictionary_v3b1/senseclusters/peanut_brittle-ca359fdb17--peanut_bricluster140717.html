peanut brittle

noun
hard candy with peanuts
9.7 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈpiː.nʌt ˈbrɪt.əl/ /; / /ˈpiː.nət ˈbrɪt.əl/ (AmE), /ˈpiː.nʌt ˈbrɪt.əl/ /; / /ˈpiː.nət ˈbrɪt.əl/ (BrE)
