rauli

noun
South American timber tree
Pronunciation: / raʊˈliː / (AmE), / raʊˈliː / (BrE)
a large South American tree valued for its strong, durable timber

Examples

Rauli wood furniture

Conservation efforts aim to protect the native rauli forests from overharvesting.
