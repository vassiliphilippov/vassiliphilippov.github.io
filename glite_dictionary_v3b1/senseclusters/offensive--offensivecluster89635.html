offensive

noun
military attack or campaign
3.1
Pronunciation: / əˈfɛnsɪv / (AmE), / əˈfɛnsɪv / (BrE)
a large-scale military attack or series of attacks; an aggressive action against an enemy

Examples

A military offensive
That spring, the general planned a surprise offensive against enemy lines.
The Allies launched a massive offensive to regain control of the region.
The latest offensive pushed the front deeper into occupied territory.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defensive - military action or attitude focused on resisting attack
