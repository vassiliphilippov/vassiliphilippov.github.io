pretend

noun
imaginative make-believe play
2.9
Pronunciation: / prɪˈtɛnd / (AmE), / prɪˈtɛnd / (BrE)
imaginative play or activity that is not real; make-believe.

Examples

Children's pretend play
Sometimes adults join in the fun of pretend with their kids.
The children spent the afternoon playing pretend in their backyard.
Their game was just pretend, but it felt real for a while.

  • antonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist
