heated

adjective
made warm or hot
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːtɪd / (AmE), / ˈhiːtɪd / (BrE)
made warm or hot, especially by means of external energy such as fire, electricity, or sunlight.

Examples

Heated room
After working out in the gym, his face was heated and flushed.
She placed her coffee mug on a heated coaster to keep it warm.
The water in the heated pool stayed comfortable all evening.
