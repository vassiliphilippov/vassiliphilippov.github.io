unpromotable

adjective
not marketable
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnprəˈmoʊtəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌʌnprəˈməʊtəbl̩ / (BrE)
not suitable or able to be advertised, publicized, or marketed to the public

Examples

Unpromotable movie
The director worried that the film was unpromotable due to its controversial subject matter.
Some products are considered unpromotable because they do not appeal to a wide audience.
The company decided not to invest in the unpromotable project after reviewing its marketing potential.

Related Terms

  • antonym: promotable - able to be advertised or marketed
  • synonym: unmarketable - not able to be sold or marketed
