hurrah

verb
shout ‘hurrah!’ in celebration
6.9
Pronunciation: / həˈrɑ /; / həˈreɪ / (AmE), / həˈrɑː /; / hʊˈreɪ / (BrE)
to shout 'hurrah!' as an expression of joy, approval, or encouragement

Examples

To hurrah loudly
Everyone hurrahed when the results were announced.
The crowd began to hurrah as the winning team walked onto the field.
They hurrahed the speaker at the end of his inspiring address.
The crowd began to hurray as the team scored the winning goal.
Children hurray when the magician finishes his trick at the party.
Fans hurray for their favorite singer during the concert.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boo - to shout to show disapproval
  • synonym: cheer - to shout for joy or encouragement
