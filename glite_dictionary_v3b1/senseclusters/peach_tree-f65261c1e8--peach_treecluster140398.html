peach tree

noun
tree that produces peaches
1.0 0.28/m
Pronunciation: / pit É tri / (AmE), / piÀêt É triÀê / (BrE)
A deciduous tree cultivated for its juicy, edible fruit, the peach, commonly grown in temperate regions.

Examples

A peach tree branch
Farmers often graft new varieties onto established peach trees to improve fruit quality.
She picked a ripe peach from the old peach tree by the fence.
The peach tree in our backyard blossoms beautifully every spring.

Synonyms

  • Prunus persica
    7.9 0.00/m
