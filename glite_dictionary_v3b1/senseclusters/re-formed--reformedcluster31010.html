re-formed

adjective
formed again or newly created
Pronunciation: / riˈfɔrmd / (AmE), / riˈfɔːmd / (BrE)
formed again or anew; having been created again after ceasing to exist or being dissolved

Examples

The re-formed band
After years apart, the re-formed band released a new album.
Many members rejoined the re-formed club after its relaunch.
The re-formed committee quickly set new goals for the organization.

Related Terms

  • antonym: original - existing or occurring at the beginning; first
  • synonym: reconstituted - having been formed again from original elements
