flowable

adjective
able to flow easily
Pronunciation: / ˈfloʊəbəl / (AmE), / ˈfləʊəbəl / (BrE)
able to flow easily, especially referring to a liquid or powder that can move smoothly.

Examples

Flowable liquid mixture
The mixture must remain flowable so it can be poured into the mold without difficulty.
Engineers designed a flowable concrete that spreads evenly across the surface.
For this recipe, the batter should be flowable but not too runny.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fluid - able to flow and change shape easily
  • antonym: viscous - having a thick, sticky consistency and not flowing easily
