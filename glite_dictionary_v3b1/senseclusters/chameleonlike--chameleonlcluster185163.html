chameleonlike

adjective
easily adaptable
7.5
Pronunciation: / kəˈmiːliənˌlaɪk / (AmE), / kəˈmiːliənˌlaɪk /; / kəˈmiːliːənˌlaɪk / (BrE)
changing appearance, behavior, or opinions easily to fit different situations or environments (sometimes in an insincere way)

Examples

Chameleonlike behavior
Her chameleonlike personality made her popular in many different social circles.
The actor's chameleonlike talent allowed him to play a wide range of roles convincingly.
He adopted a chameleonlike approach to fit in with each new team he joined.
Her chameleonlike attitude made it hard to know what she truly believed.
Politicians are sometimes criticized for their chameleonlike positions on important issues.
He adopted a chameleonlike approach, blending in with every new group he met.
