pleiad

noun
Pleiades star
Pronunciation: / ˈpliːæd / (AmE), / ˈpliːad / (BrE)
a star that is a member of the Pleiades star cluster in the constellation Taurus

Examples

A pleiad star
Through the telescope, we could see a single pleiad shining brightly among the others.
Astronomers have studied each pleiad to learn more about the cluster's formation.
The guide pointed out a pleiad that was barely visible to the naked eye.
