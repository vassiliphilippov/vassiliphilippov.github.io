hash slinger

noun
diner cook or server (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈhæʃ ˌslɪŋɚ / (AmE), / ˈhæʃ ˌslɪŋə / (BrE)
an informal term for a cook or server, especially in a diner or cheap restaurant

Examples

A hash slinger cook
After years as a hash slinger, she opened her own diner across town.
Every Sunday morning, the local hash slinger would greet customers with a smile and steaming cups of coffee.
The old movie featured a wisecracking hash slinger flipping pancakes behind the counter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: short-order cook - cook who prepares simple, quickly made meals
