bafflingly

adverb
in a confusing way
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbæf.lɪŋ.liː/ (AmE), /ˈbæf.lɪŋ.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that is very confusing or difficult to understand

Examples

A bafflingly complex problem
The solution to the puzzle was bafflingly complex for most participants.
He bafflingly ignored all the advice given to him by his friends.
The movie's ending was bafflingly abrupt, leaving the audience confused.
The instructions were bafflingly complicated for such a simple device.
He bafflingly refused to answer any of the reporter's questions.
The results of the experiment were bafflingly different from what anyone expected.
