ambulance

noun
emergency medical vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈæm.bjə.ləns / (AmE), / ˈæm.bjʊ.ləns / (BrE)
a vehicle specially equipped to carry sick or injured people to and from hospital, especially in emergencies

Examples

An ambulance vehicle
All the ambulances in the city were sent to help at the scene of the fire.
She called for an ambulance after witnessing the accident.
The ambulance arrived within minutes to take the injured man to the hospital.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emergency vehicle - vehicle used by emergency services such as police, fire, and medical
  • antonym: hearse - vehicle for transporting the dead to a funeral or grave
