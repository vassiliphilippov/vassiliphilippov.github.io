headhunting

noun
recruiting top talent
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌhʌntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌhʌntɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of searching for and recruiting highly qualified people for important jobs, especially executives

Examples

Headhunting top talent
The company hired a firm specializing in headhunting to find a new CEO.
Many professionals are approached through headhunting rather than traditional job applications.
He started his career in headhunting before moving into human resources management.

Related Terms

  • synonym: job placement - the act of finding a suitable job for someone
  • synonym: recruitment - the process of finding and hiring new employees
