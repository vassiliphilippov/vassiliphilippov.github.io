supranuclear

adjective
above a nucleus
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːprəˈnuːkliɚ / (AmE), / ˌsuːprəˈnjuːkliə / (BrE)
located or occurring above a nucleus, especially in a cell or in the brain

Examples

Supranuclear brain area
The neurologist identified a supranuclear abnormality affecting the patient's eye movements.
Researchers studied the supranuclear pathways involved in controlling facial muscles.
Damage to supranuclear regions can result in specific movement disorders.
