diclofenac potassium

noun
NSAID for pain and inflammation
4.0
Pronunciation: / daɪˌkloʊfəˌnæk pəˈtæsiəm / (AmE), / dɪˌkləʊfənæk pəˈtæsɪəm / (BrE)
A nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used to treat pain and inflammation, commonly known by the trade name Cataflam.
