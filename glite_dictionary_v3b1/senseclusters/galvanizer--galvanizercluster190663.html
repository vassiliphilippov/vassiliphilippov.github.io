galvanizer

noun
worker who zinc-coats metal
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡælvəˌnaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈɡælvəˌnaɪzə /; / ˈɡalvənʌɪzə / (BrE)
a skilled worker who coats iron or steel with zinc to protect it from rust

Examples

Factory galvanizer
After years of work, he became the lead galvanizer at the factory.
The galvanizer ensured that every steel beam was thoroughly coated for maximum protection.
Without the expertise of a galvanizer, the metal parts would quickly rust and deteriorate.
As a certified galvaniser, she ensures that the metal components resist corrosion for many years.
Modern manufacturing relies on the skills of a galvaniser to produce durable building materials.
The galvaniser applied a protective layer of zinc to the steel beams in the factory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: galvanizer - variant American spelling for the same occupation
  • synonym: plater - a person who coats metal plates with another metal
