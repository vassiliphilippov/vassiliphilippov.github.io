vexilla

noun
ceremonial banners
Pronunciation: / vɛkˈsɪlə / (AmE), / vɛkˈsɪlə / (BrE)
banners or flags, especially those used in Roman military or church ceremonies; plural of vexillum

Examples

Roman vexilla banners
The ancient procession carried several vexilla through the streets.
During the ceremony, colorful vexilla were displayed at the front of the church.
Roman soldiers marched beneath their legion's vexilla as a sign of unity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: banners - long pieces of cloth with symbols or messages, used as flags
  • synonym: flags - pieces of cloth with distinctive designs used as symbols
