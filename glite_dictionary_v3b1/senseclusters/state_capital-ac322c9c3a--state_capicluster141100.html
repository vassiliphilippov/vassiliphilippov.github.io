state capital

noun
capital city of a political subdivision
6.9
Pronunciation: / steɪt ˈkæpɪtəl / (AmE), / steɪt ˈkapɪtəl / (BrE)
The city in a state or similar political division that serves as the seat of government.

Examples

The state capital city
Albany is the state capital of New York.
Many annual events are held in the state capital building.
Tour groups often visit the state capital to see the legislature in action.
