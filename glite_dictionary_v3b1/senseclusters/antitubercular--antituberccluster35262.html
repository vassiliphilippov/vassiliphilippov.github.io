antitubercular

adjective
against tuberculosis
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæntitaɪˈbɜːrkjələr / (AmE), / ˌæntitjuːˈbɜːkjʊlə / (BrE)
designed to prevent or treat tuberculosis, a serious infectious disease

Examples

Antitubercular medicine
The doctor prescribed an antitubercular medication to help cure the infection.
Many antitubercular drugs must be taken for several months to be effective.
Researchers are developing new antitubercular treatments to combat drug-resistant strains.

Related Terms
