bless

verb
make holy by religious rite
Pronunciation: / blɛs / (AmE), / blɛs / (BrE)
to perform a religious or formal act (such as a prayer or making the sign of the cross) to make something holy, protected, or blessed

Examples

The monk blessed the holy water before sprinkling it on the congregation.
The priest will bless the new church during the ceremony next week.

He blessed his newborn daughter as part of the ceremony.
The priest blessed the congregation at the end of the service.
She asked the bishop to bless her newborn baby.
The pope will bless the palms before the procession begins.
