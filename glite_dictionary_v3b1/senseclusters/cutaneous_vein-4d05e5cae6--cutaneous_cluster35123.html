cutaneous vein

noun
a superficial vein under the skin
8.2 7.2
Pronunciation: / kjuˈteɪniəs veɪn / (AmE), / kjuːˈteɪnɪəs veɪn / (BrE)
a vein located in the subcutaneous tissue that drains blood into deeper veins

Examples

Cutaneous vein in skin
Some medications are administered through a cutaneous vein to provide rapid absorption.
The doctor located the cutaneous vein before inserting the needle for the blood test.
Varicose veins are a common problem affecting the cutaneous veins in the legs.
