schmucky attitude

noun
foolish or obnoxious attitude
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃmʌki ˈætɪˌtud / (AmE), / ˈʃmʌki ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
An attitude that is foolish, obnoxious, or contemptible in a way that is likely to irritate others.

Examples

Schmucky attitude at meeting

Her schmucky attitude made everyone feel uncomfortable.
If you keep that schmucky attitude, no one will want to work with you.
