jambon

noun
ham (esp. French-style cured pork)
6.2
Pronunciation: / ʒæmˈbɒn / (AmE), / ʒæ̃ˈbɒ̃ / (BrE)
a cut of meat from the thigh of a pig, especially when cured or smoked; ham, often used in reference to French-style preparations
