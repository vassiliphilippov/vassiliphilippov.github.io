successful

adjective
having achieved success or a favorable result
Pronunciation: / səkˈsɛsfəl / (AmE), / səkˈsɛsfʊl / (BrE)
achieving the intended goal or having a favorable result; having achieved success

Examples

A successful person
His first novel was successful both critically and commercially.
She became a successful lawyer after years of dedication and hard work.
The company launched a successful marketing campaign that doubled its sales.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accomplished - highly skilled due to having achieved much
  • antonym: unsuccessful - not achieving the intended result; failing to succeed
