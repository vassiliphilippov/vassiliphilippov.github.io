sudoku

noun
number-based logic puzzle
3.7
Pronunciation: / suˈdoʊ.ku / (AmE), / suːˈdəʊ.kuː / (BrE)
a logic puzzle in which numbers from 1 to 9 are placed in a 9×9 grid so that each row, column, and 3×3 box contains each number exactly once

Examples

A sudoku puzzle
Every morning she completes a sudoku puzzle with her coffee.
He bought a book filled with challenging sudokus to solve during his commute.
Online sudoku games have become very popular for both adults and children.
