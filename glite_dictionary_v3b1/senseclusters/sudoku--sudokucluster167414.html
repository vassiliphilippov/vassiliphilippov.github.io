sudoku

noun
number-based logic puzzle
3.7 3.8
Pronunciation: /suː.ˈdoʊ.kuː/ /; / /sʊ.ˈdoʊ.kuː/ (AmE), /suː.ˈdəʊ.kəʊ/ /; / /sʊ.ˈdəʊ.kəʊ/ (BrE)
