GAO

noun
U.S. government accountability agency
7.2 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / d íiÀê e…™ o ä / (AmE), / d íiÀê e…™ …ô ä / (BrE)
an independent U.S. federal agency that serves as the investigative arm of Congress, ensuring the accountability of the executive branch and the government to Congress and the public

Examples

The GAO report
According to the GAO, the new policy failed to save significant taxpayer money.
Congress often requests audits and investigations from the GAO.
The GAO released a report highlighting inefficiencies in federal spending.

Synonyms

  • United States Government Accountability Office
    5.9 0.00/m
    United States Government Accountability Office is the agency's full formal name used in official reports and writing, while GAO is the common abbreviation people use.
  • Government Accounting Office
    4.8 0.00/m
    Government Accounting Office uses 'Accounting' in its name and sounds money-focused, while GAO implies a broader Government Accountability role.
