hawk moth

noun
large moth known for hovering flight
5.5 5.2
Pronunciation: /hɔːk mɔːθ/ /; / /hɑːk mɔːθ/ /; / /hɔːk moʊθ/ (AmE), /hɔːk mɒθ/ /; / /hɔːk məʊt/ /; / /hɔːk mɔːθ/ (BrE)
a moth with long, narrow forewings capable of powerful flight and hovering over flowers to feed, belonging to the family Sphingidae

Examples

Unlike most moths, the hawk moth is known for its ability to hover while feeding.
