Synonyms

  • enlargement
    3.3 1.64/m
    enlargement is a physical increase in size or amount, while extension is often an added part that makes something larger.
  • expansion
    3.3 28/m
    expansion is a spreading increase in size or scope, while extension is often a specific added part or lengthening.
  • augmentation
    6.1 0.99/m
    augmentation is a formal word for making something greater in degree or amount, while extension often means a physical part added.
  • lengthening
    3.7 0.33/m
