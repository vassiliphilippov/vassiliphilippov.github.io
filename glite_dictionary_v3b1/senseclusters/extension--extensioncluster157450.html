extension

noun
amount or range something extends
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɛnʃən / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛn.ʃən /; / ɪkˈstɛnʃən /; / ɪkˈsten.ʃən / (BrE)
something added to make something longer, larger, or to increase its size, such as a new room, a part added to furniture, or the act of straightening a limb
