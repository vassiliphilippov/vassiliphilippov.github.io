precisionism

noun
American art style
Pronunciation: / prɪˈsɪʒənˌɪzəm / (AmE), / prɪˈsɪʒənɪzəm / (BrE)
an American art style of the early 20th century marked by sharp lines, clear forms, and a focus on industrial subjects
