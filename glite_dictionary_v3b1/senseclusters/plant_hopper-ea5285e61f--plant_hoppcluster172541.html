plant hopper

noun
sap-feeding insect related to leafhoppers
5.9 5.4
Pronunciation: /plænt ˈhɑːp.ər/ (AmE), /plɑːnt ˈhɒp.ə/ /; / /plænt ˈhɒp.ə/ (BrE)
A small insect related to leafhoppers and spittlebugs, usually found on plants but rarely causing damage to cultivated crops.

Examples

Small plant hoppers
Several plant hoppers were discovered on the tomato plants in the greenhouse.
The plant hopper is easily distinguished by its wedge-shaped body and jumping ability.
Unlike aphids, plant hoppers seldom cause major damage to crops.
