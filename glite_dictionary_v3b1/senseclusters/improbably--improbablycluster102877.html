improbably

adverb
in an unlikely or hard-to-believe manner
7.2
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈprɑːbəbli / (AmE), / ɪmˈprɒbəbli / (BrE)
In a way that is unlikely to happen or difficult to believe.

Examples

An improbably lucky event
Improbably, she found her lost ring in a field after several months.
Improbably, the tiny startup managed to outperform major tech giants.
He survived the avalanche, which seemed improbably against all odds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incredibly - in a way that is hard to believe; extremely
  • antonym: probably - likely to happen or be true
