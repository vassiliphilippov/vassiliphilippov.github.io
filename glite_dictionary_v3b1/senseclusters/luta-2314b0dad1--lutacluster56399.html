Luta

proper noun
industrial region in northeastern China
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈluːˌtɑː / (AmE), / ˈluːˌtɑː / (BrE)
an industrial region in northeastern China at the southern tip of the Liaodong Peninsula, formerly combining the cities of Dalian and Lushun.

Examples

Luta region
Historically, Luta comprised the cities now known as Dalian and Lushun.
Many foreign businesses established operations in Luta during its industrial expansion.
The rapid development of Luta contributed significantly to China’s industrial growth in the twentieth century.
