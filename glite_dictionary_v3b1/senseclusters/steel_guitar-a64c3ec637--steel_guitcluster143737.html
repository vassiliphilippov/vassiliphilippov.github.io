steel guitar

noun
guitar played with a steel bar for glissando
7.4
Pronunciation: / stiːl ɡɪˈtɑr / (AmE), / stiːl ɡɪˈtɑː / (BrE)
a guitar with steel strings played by sliding a steel bar over the strings to create a glissando effect, commonly used in country and Hawaiian music
