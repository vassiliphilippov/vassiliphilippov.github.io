appetizer

noun
small dish served before a meal
Pronunciation: / ˈæpɪˌtaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈæpɪˌtaɪzə /; / ˈæpɪtaɪzə / (BrE)
a small dish of food or a drink served before the main part of a meal to stimulate the appetite

Examples

A small appetizer
Her favorite appetizer is bruschetta topped with tomatoes and basil.
The party began with several light appetizers and sparkling wine.
We ordered a delicious shrimp appetizer before the main course arrived.
Each guest was served a light appetiser to start the dinner.
Stuffed mushrooms are my favourite appetiser at this restaurant.
We ordered a delicious appetiser before the main course arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appetizer - small dish served before a meal (American spelling)
  • antonym: dessert - sweet course served at the end of a meal
  • antonym: main course - the principal dish of a meal
  • synonym: starter - a small dish served before the main course of a meal (especially British English)
