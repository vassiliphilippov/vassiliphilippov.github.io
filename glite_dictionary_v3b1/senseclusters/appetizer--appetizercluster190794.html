appetizer

noun
small dish served before a meal
2.5 2.2
Pronunciation: /ˈæp.ə.taɪ.zər/ /; / /ˈæp.ɪ.taɪ.zər/ (AmE), /ˈæp.ɪ.taɪ.zə/ /; / /ˈæp.ə.taɪ.zə/ (BrE)
