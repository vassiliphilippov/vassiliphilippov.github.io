sleeving

noun
material for sleeves
Pronunciation: / ˈsliːvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsliːvɪŋ / (BrE)
fabric or material used to make, line, or reinforce the sleeves of a garment

Examples

Jacket sleeving
The tailor ordered extra matching sleeving to reinforce the old jacket's worn arms.
She chose a soft cotton sleeving to make the shirt more comfortable.
Special waterproof sleeving was used to protect the coat's sleeves from rain.
