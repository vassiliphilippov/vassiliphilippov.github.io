beer

noun
alcoholic drink from cereals and hops
1.5 1.0
Pronunciation: /bɪr/ /; / /biːr/ (AmE), /bɪə/ (BrE)
An alcoholic drink made from the fermentation of cereals, especially barley, and flavored with hops.

Examples

A glass of beer

He sampled local beer at breweries across Europe.
Would you like a cold beer with your dinner?

Related Terms

  • synonym: lager - a type of beer brewed and stored at low temperatures
  • antonym: wine - an alcoholic drink made from fermented grapes or other fruits
