northernness

noun
the quality of being northern
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrðərnnəs / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðənnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being located in, characteristic of, or associated with the north

Examples

The northernness of climate
She felt a distinct northernness in the air as they traveled farther from the equator.
The artist was inspired by the unique northernness of the remote Highlands.
The book explores the northernness of Scandinavian culture and landscapes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: southernness - the quality of being southern or located in the south
