creationist

adjective
supporting creationism
6.8
Pronunciation: / kriˈeɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / kriˈeɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
relating to or supporting the belief that the universe and life were created by a divine being.

Examples

Creationist view
The school board debated whether to include creationist perspectives in the science curriculum.
A creationist viewpoint often contrasts with the theory of evolution in biology classes.
Some museums feature creationist exhibits that present alternative explanations for the origin of life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: creationism - the belief that the universe and life were created by a divine being
  • antonym: evolutionary - relating to the theory that species develop over time through natural selection
