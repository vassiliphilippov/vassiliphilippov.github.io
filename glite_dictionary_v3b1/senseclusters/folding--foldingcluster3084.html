folding

adjective
able to be folded for storage or transport
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfəʊldɪŋ / (BrE)
(of furniture or equipment) designed to be folded for easy storage or transport.

Examples

Folding chair
A folding bike is convenient for commuting and storage in small apartments.
He sat on a folding chair while waiting for the train.
We brought a folding table to the picnic for extra space.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collapsible - able to be folded or compressed into a smaller space
