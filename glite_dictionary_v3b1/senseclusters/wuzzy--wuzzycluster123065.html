wuzzy

noun
soft comfort toy
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌzi / (AmE), / ˈwʌzi / (BrE)
a small soft toy or stuffed animal, especially one kept for comfort by a child

Examples

A wuzzy toy
As a child, she never went to bed without her favorite wuzzy by her side.
The little boy brought his wuzzy to school for show-and-tell.
Even as a teenager, he kept his old wuzzy on the shelf in his room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comfort object - an item, often a toy or blanket, kept for emotional comfort
  • synonym: plushie - a soft toy made of plush fabric, often in the shape of an animal
  • synonym: stuffed animal - a soft toy in the shape of an animal, filled with soft material
