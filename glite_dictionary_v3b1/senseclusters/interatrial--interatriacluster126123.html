interatrial

adjective
between heart atria
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈeɪtriəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈreɪtrɪəl / (BrE)
located or occurring between the two atria of the heart

Examples

The interatrial septum
The interatrial septum separates the left and right atria in the human heart.
Doctors examined the interatrial region for possible defects after the surgery.
An interatrial shunt can affect the flow of blood between the heart's upper chambers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intraatrial - located within an atrium of the heart
