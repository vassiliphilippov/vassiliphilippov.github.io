Synonyms

  • blockage
    4.1 2/m
    blockage is a common word for something that stops flow in pipes or vessels, while occlusion is used more in medical or technical contexts.
  • obstruction
    4.4 2/m
    obstruction is a physical item that blocks a way or tube, while occlusion can mean the same and is often medical.
  • closure
    4.4 3/m
    closure is the act of closing something or ending movement, while occlusion focuses on blocking flow inside a passage or vessel.
  • stoppage
    5.0 0.04/m
    stoppage is a sudden halt in flow or work, often used for machines or strikes, while occlusion names a physical block.
