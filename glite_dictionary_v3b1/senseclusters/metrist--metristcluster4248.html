metrist

noun
person who measures
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtrɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɛtrɪst / (BrE)
a person who measures physical quantities or calibrates measuring instruments

Examples

Laboratory metrist
The metrist checked the laboratory scales and recorded precise measurements for the experiment log.
A skilled metrist ensures that all equipment is properly calibrated before use.
Every year, the company hires a metrist to verify the accuracy of its measuring devices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calibrator - a person who adjusts or sets measuring instruments
  • synonym: technician - a person skilled in the technical details of a subject or occupation
