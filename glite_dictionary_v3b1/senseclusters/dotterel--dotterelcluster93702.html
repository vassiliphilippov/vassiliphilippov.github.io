dotterel

noun
a rare upland Eurasian plover
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːtərəl / (AmE), / ˈdɒtərəl / (BrE)
a small, rare plover (Charadrius morinellus) found in upland areas of Eurasia, noted for its tame behavior

Examples

A dotterel bird
Birdwatchers hiked to the high moors hoping to catch a glimpse of a dotterel.
Conservationists are working to protect the habitats of the endangered dotterel.
The dotterel is known for allowing humans to approach quite closely.

Related Terms
