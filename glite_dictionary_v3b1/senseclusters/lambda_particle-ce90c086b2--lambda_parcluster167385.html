lambda particle

noun

a neutral baryon with isotopic spin 1, important in particle physics, also known as the lambda baryon

Examples

The lambda particle
A lambda particle is neutral, distinguishing it from other baryons like the proton.
Physicists first observed the lambda particle in cosmic rays during the 1950s.
The decay of a lambda particle results in the production of a proton and a pion.
