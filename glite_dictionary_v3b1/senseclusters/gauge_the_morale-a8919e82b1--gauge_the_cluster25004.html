gauge the morale

verb
assess a group's emotional state
4.5 5.1
Pronunciation: /geɪdʒ ðə mə.ˈræl/ /; / /geɪdʒ ðiː mə.ˈræl/ (AmE), /geɪdʒ ðə mə.ˈrɑːl/ /; / /geɪdʒ ðiː mə.ˈrɑːl/ (BrE)

Examples

It's important to regularly gauge the morale in any large organization.
