paleographic

adjective
ancient writing study
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪliəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌpeɪliəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to the study or style of ancient writing, scripts, or manuscripts

Examples

Paleographic study
