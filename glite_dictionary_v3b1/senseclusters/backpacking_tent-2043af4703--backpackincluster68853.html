backpacking tent

noun
lightweight tent for backpackers
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌpækɪŋ tɛnt / (AmE), / ˈbækˌpækɪŋ tɛnt / (BrE)
a lightweight tent designed to be easily carried in a backpack, typically used by hikers or campers on multi-day trips

Examples

A backpacking tent for hiking
Her backpacking tent was surprisingly spacious despite being so light and compact.
We bought a new backpacking tent for our trip along the Appalachian Trail.
You need a sturdy backpacking tent when camping in unpredictable weather.
