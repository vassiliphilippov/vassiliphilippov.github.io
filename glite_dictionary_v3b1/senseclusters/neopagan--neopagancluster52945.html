neopagan

noun
modern pagan follower
Pronunciation: / ˌnioʊˈpeɪɡən / (AmE), / ˌniːəʊˈpeɪɡən / (BrE)
a person who follows a modern revival of ancient pagan religious practices or beliefs

Examples

A neopagan believer
The local community center hosted a gathering for neopagan rituals and celebrations.
As a neopagan, she observes the solstices and other ancient festivals.
Many neopagans draw inspiration from Celtic and Norse traditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heathen - a person who does not belong to a widely held religion
  • synonym: pagan - a person holding religious beliefs other than those of the main world religions
