failure

noun
a person who habitually fails
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪljər / (AmE), / ˈfeɪljə / (BrE)
a person who does not succeed, especially someone who habitually fails or loses

Examples

He is a failure
After losing another job, he felt like a total failure.
Despite many attempts, she refused to see herself as a failure.
The team labeled him a failure after his repeated mistakes.
