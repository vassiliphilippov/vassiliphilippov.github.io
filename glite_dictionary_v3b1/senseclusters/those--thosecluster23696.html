those

pronoun
distant people or things
Pronunciation: / ðoʊz / (AmE), / ðəʊz / (BrE)
a demonstrative pronoun used to refer to people or things that have already been mentioned or are farther away in distance or time

Examples

Those are fresher
I prefer those over here because they are much fresher.
Can you hand me those on the top shelf, please?
The paintings you saw yesterday are not as colorful as those in this gallery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: that - demonstrative pronoun for a single thing farther away
  • antonym: these - demonstrative pronoun for people or things that are close
