recliner

noun
a chair with adjustable reclining back and footrest
Pronunciation: / rɪˈklaɪnɚ / (AmE), / rɪˈklaɪnə / (BrE)
a comfortable chair with a back that can be tilted backward and a footrest that raises so the user can recline

Examples

A recliner chair
After a long day at work, she loves putting her feet up in the recliner.
He relaxed in his recliner and watched television all evening.
The living room features a leather recliner next to the fireplace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: armchair - a comfortable chair with side supports for a person's arms
