water-insoluble

adjective
not soluble in water
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔt̬ər ɪnˈsɑljubəl / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ɪnˈsɒljʊbəl / (BrE)
not able to dissolve in water; incapable of forming a solution with water.

Examples

Water-insoluble substance
Chalk is considered water-insoluble and will not dissolve even after hours in water.
Plastic bags made of water-insoluble polymers persist in the environment.
The pill is coated with a water-insoluble material to protect it from dissolving in the stomach.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insoluble - impossible to dissolve, especially in water
  • antonym: water-soluble - able to dissolve in water
