nauseatingly

adverb
in a disgusting way
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔziˌeɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈnɔːziˌeɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely unpleasant or causes strong disgust, sometimes making you feel sick

Examples

Nauseatingly strong smell
The room was nauseatingly hot and filled with a strong chemical smell.
He smiled nauseatingly as he accepted the award, making some people uncomfortable.
The dessert was nauseatingly sweet, and I could not finish it.
The dessert was nauseatingly sweet, making it hard for anyone to finish.
He was nauseatingly polite, which made his compliments seem insincere.
The room was nauseatingly messy, with clothes and trash everywhere.
