den mother

noun
female supervisor or caretaker of a group
Pronunciation: / dɛn ˈmʌðɚ / (AmE), / dɛn ˈmʌðə / (BrE)
a woman who supervises or looks after a group, especially of children or young people, often acting as a caretaker or mentor

Examples

Office den mother
In the start-up office, Jane took on the role of den mother, making sure everyone felt welcome and supported.
Sarah was the den mother for the local Cub Scout troop, organizing activities and caring for the children.
Since their parents worked late, Mrs. Lee became the unofficial den mother for the neighborhood kids after school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chaperone - a person who supervises young people
  • synonym: mentor - an experienced and trusted adviser
