panful

noun
quantity filling a pan
Pronunciation: / ˈpænˌfʊl / (AmE), / ˈpænˌfʊl / (BrE)
the amount that fills a pan

Examples

A panful of eggs
She made a panful of scrambled eggs for breakfast.
A single panful of stew was enough to feed the whole family.
He poured a panful of water into the sink to wash the dishes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: potful - the amount that fills a pot
  • synonym: spoonful - the amount that fills a spoon
