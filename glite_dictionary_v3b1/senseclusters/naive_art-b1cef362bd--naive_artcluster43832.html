naive art

noun
art by untrained artists, childlike style
6.3 5.1
Pronunciation: /naɪ.ˈiːv ɑːrt/ (AmE), /naɪ.ˈiːv ɑːt/ /; / /nɑː.ˈiːv ɑːt/ (BrE)
A genre of visual art created by artists without formal training, often characterized by a childlike simplicity in subject matter and technique.
