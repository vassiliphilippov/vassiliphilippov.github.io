merveilleux

noun
French meringue dessert
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɛr.vɛlˈjɜː / (AmE), / mɛʁ.vɛ.jø / (BrE)
a French dessert consisting of layers of meringue and whipped cream, often coated with chocolate shavings

Examples

A chocolate merveilleux
At the bakery, she chose a chocolate merveilleux for dessert.
The chef prepared a delicate merveilleux topped with whipped cream and chocolate flakes.
He had never tasted a merveilleux before visiting Paris.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meringue - a dessert made from whipped egg whites and sugar
  • synonym: pavlova - a dessert made of meringue, cream, and fruit
