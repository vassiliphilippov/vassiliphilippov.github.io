god of war

noun
deity associated with war
3.3
Pronunciation: / ɡɑd əv wɔr / (AmE), / ɡɒd əv wɔː / (BrE)
a deity worshipped as having power over war and granting victory in battle

Examples

The god of war Ares
In ancient Greece, Ares was revered as the god of war and invoked before battles.
Many cultures have their own version of a god of war who is believed to grant victory to warriors.
The temple was dedicated to the god of war, and soldiers offered sacrifices there before combat.
