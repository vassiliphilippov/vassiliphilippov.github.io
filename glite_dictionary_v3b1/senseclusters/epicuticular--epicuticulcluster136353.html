epicuticular

adjective
on outer cuticle
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪkjuːˈtɪkjələr / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪkjuːˈtɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or located on the outermost layer of a cuticle, especially in plants or insects

Examples

Epicuticular wax layer
The epicuticular wax on the leaf surface reduces water loss in dry climates.
Scientists studied the epicuticular structures of insect wings under a microscope.
Damage to the epicuticular layer can make plants more vulnerable to disease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cuticular - relating to the cuticle or outer layer
