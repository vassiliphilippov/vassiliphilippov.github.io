signature

noun
cryptographic proof of origin and integrity
6.8 0.91/m
Pronunciation: / c8s6a61.n59.t835a / (AmE), / c8s6a61.n59.t8359 / (BrE)
a cryptographic value produced from a message and a private key that verifies the message's origin and ensures it has not been altered.

Examples

Digital signature
The email included a signature that validated its origin and integrity.
Before installing the update, the system checked the package's signature to ensure it hadn't been tampered with.
Open-source projects require contributors to sign commits; the signature proves the author's identity and message integrity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cryptographic signature - a value computed using cryptographic algorithms to authenticate a message
  • synonym: digital signature - a cryptographic signature applied to digital data to prove origin and integrity
