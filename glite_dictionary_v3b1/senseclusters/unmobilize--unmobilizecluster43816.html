unmobilize

verb
to demobilize
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈmoʊbəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈməʊbɪlaɪz / (BrE)
to cause people, troops, or resources to stop being mobilized and return to normal status; to demobilize.

Examples

To unmobilize troops
After the treaty, the government unmobilized the reserve units and sent the soldiers home.
The country quickly unmobilized its emergency teams once the crisis was over.
Once peace was declared, they began to unmobilize military resources across the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demobilize - to release troops or resources from active service
  • antonym: mobilize - to prepare and organize people or resources for action
