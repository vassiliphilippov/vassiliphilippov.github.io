constructionist

noun
builder or worker
5.2
Pronunciation: / kənˈstrʌkʃənɪst / (AmE), / kənˈstrʌkʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who builds or erects structures, such as a builder or construction worker

Examples

A skilled constructionist
The experienced constructionist supervised the team as they assembled the steel framework.
Every constructionist on the site wore a hard hat and safety vest for protection.
As a skilled constructionist, she was responsible for ensuring the building met all safety codes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: builder - a person who constructs buildings
  • synonym: laborer - a person doing physical work, especially on a building site
