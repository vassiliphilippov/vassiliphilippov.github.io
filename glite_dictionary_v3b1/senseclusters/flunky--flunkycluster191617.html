flunky

noun
servile or obsequious subordinate
7.6 6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈflʌŋ.kiː/ /; / /ˈflʌŋ.kɪ/ (AmE), /ˈflʌŋ.kiː/ /; / /ˈflʌŋ.kɪ/ (BrE)
