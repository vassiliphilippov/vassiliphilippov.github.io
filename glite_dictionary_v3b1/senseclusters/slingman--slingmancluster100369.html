slingman

noun
worker securing slings
9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈslɪŋ.mən/ /; / /ˈslɪŋ.mæn/ (AmE), /ˈslɪŋ.mən/ /; / /ˈslɪŋ.mæn/ (BrE)
a worker who attaches or secures slings to heavy loads so they can be lifted or moved by a crane or hoist

Examples

Crane slingman
The experienced slingman made sure the load was secure before signaling the crane operator.
A skilled slingman is essential for safe lifting operations on construction sites.
Before the heavy beam was hoisted, the slingman checked all the connections carefully.

Related Terms

  • antonym: operator - a person who controls machinery, such as a crane
  • synonym: rigger - a worker who sets up and secures equipment for lifting heavy objects
