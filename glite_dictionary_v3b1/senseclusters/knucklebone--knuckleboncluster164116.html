knucklebone

noun
children's tossing game
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌkəlˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈnʌkəlbəʊn / (BrE)
a traditional children's game played by tossing and catching small bones or similar objects, similar to jacks

Examples

A knucklebone game
Children gathered in a circle to play knucklebone using small stones they found nearby.
She learned the rules of knucklebone from her grandmother, who played it as a child.
At the festival, the older girls taught us how to play knucklebone with smooth pebbles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jacks - a game where small objects are tossed and caught, often with a ball
