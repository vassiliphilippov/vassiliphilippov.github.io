boaty atmosphere

noun
ambiance evoking being on a boat
4.5 6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊti ˈætməsˌfɪr / (AmE), / ˈbəʊti ˈatməsˌfɪə / (BrE)
An ambiance or feeling that is reminiscent of being on a boat, often evoking nautical themes, decor, or sensations.

Examples

A boaty atmosphere feeling
Decorating with navy stripes and anchors can easily create a boaty atmosphere at your summer party.

The lakeside café has a boaty atmosphere, with ropes and wooden beams everywhere.
