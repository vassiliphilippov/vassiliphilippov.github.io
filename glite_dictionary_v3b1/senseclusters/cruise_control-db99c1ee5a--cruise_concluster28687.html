cruise control

noun
system maintaining set vehicle speed
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkruz kənˌtroʊl /; / ˈkruːz kənˌtroʊl / (AmE), / ˈkruːz kənˌtrəʊl / (BrE)
an automated system in a vehicle that keeps a steady speed without the driver having to press the accelerator
