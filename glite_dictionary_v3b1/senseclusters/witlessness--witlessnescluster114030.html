witlessness

noun
lack of wit
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪtləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈwɪtləsnəs / (BrE)
the state of lacking wit, cleverness, or intelligence

Examples

  • antonym: intelligence - the ability to learn, understand, and think
  • synonym: stupidity - lack of intelligence or common sense
  • antonym: wit - the ability to say or write things that are clever and usually funny
