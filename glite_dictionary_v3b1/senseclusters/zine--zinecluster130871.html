zine

noun
self-published magazine
8.2
Pronunciation: / ziːn / (AmE), / ziːn / (BrE)
a small, self-published magazine, often focused on niche, personal, or unconventional topics

Examples

A zine magazine

Many zines are distributed at music festivals and art fairs.
He collects zines about underground comics and independent music scenes.
