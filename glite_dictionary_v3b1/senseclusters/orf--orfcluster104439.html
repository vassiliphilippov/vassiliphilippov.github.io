orf

noun
viral disease in livestock
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɔrf / (AmE), / ɔːf / (BrE)
a contagious viral skin disease affecting sheep and goats, sometimes transmissible to humans

Examples

Orf infection in sheep
The veterinarian diagnosed the lamb with orf after noticing sores around its mouth.
Farmers must take precautions to prevent the spread of orf among their flocks.
Humans can contract orf through direct contact with infected animals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: foot-and-mouth disease - a different contagious viral disease affecting livestock
