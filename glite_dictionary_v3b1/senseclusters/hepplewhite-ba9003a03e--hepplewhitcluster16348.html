Hepplewhite

noun
elegant furniture style
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhep.əl.waɪt/ (AmE), /ˈhep.əl.waɪt/ (BrE)
a piece of furniture made in or characteristic of the elegant, neoclassical style designed by George Hepplewhite in the late 18th century

Examples

Hepplewhite chair
The antique shop displayed a beautiful Hepplewhite sideboard with delicate inlay work.
Collectors often seek out original Hepplewhite chairs for their graceful, shield-shaped backs.
They furnished the dining room with a set of Hepplewhite pieces to create a classic look.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Queen Anne - a style of furniture popular in early 18th-century England, noted for its graceful lines
