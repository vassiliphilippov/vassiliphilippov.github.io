mucky trail

noun
dirty or muddy path
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌki treɪl / (AmE), / ˈmʌki treɪl / (BrE)
A path that is dirty, muddy, or covered in filth.

Examples

A mucky trail after rain
After the storm, the hikers struggled along the mucky trail to reach the campsite.
The mucky trail left their boots caked in dirt and grass.
They decided to take a detour to avoid the mucky trail after a night of heavy rain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clean trail - a path free of mud or dirt
  • synonym: muddy trail - a path covered in mud
