chitty

noun
informal written note
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪti / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪti / (BrE)
a short informal written note, especially one recording a debt or a promise to pay

Examples

A payment chitty
He handed the cashier a chitty to collect his payment later.
The manager wrote a chitty authorizing the delivery of supplies.
She kept a chitty as proof that she owed her friend some money.
