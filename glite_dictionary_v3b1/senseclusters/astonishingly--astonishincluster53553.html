astonishingly

adverb
in a way that amazes or surprises
Pronunciation: / əˈstɑːnɪʃɪŋli / (AmE), / əˈstɒnɪʃɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that causes great surprise or amazement.

Examples

Astonishingly good results
Astonishingly, the small startup managed to outperform all of its larger competitors.
She completed the marathon astonishingly fast, beating her personal record by twenty minutes.
The painting was astonishingly detailed, capturing even the tiniest features.
