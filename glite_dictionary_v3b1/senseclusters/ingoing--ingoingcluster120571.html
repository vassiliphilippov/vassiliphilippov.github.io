ingoing

adjective
initial; carried forward
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌɡoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌɡəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
present at the beginning of a period or carried forward into a new accounting period; initial or existing at the start

Examples

Each department must report its ingoing assets at the start of the quarter.
