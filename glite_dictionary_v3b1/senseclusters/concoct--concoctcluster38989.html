concoct

verb
devise or invent (a plan, idea, etc.)
Pronunciation: / kənˈkɑkt / (AmE), / kənˈkɒkt / (BrE)
to invent or devise a story, plan, or excuse, often using creativity or deception

Examples

To concoct a story
He loves to concoct elaborate stories to entertain his friends.
She managed to concoct an excuse to avoid the meeting.
The team worked late into the night to concoct a strategy for success.
He is always concocting new ways to get out of trouble.
She managed to concoct an elaborate excuse for being late.
The reporter concocted a sensational story to attract readers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: devise - to plan or invent by careful thought
  • synonym: fabricate - to invent false information in order to deceive someone
  • synonym: invent - to create or design something that has not existed before
