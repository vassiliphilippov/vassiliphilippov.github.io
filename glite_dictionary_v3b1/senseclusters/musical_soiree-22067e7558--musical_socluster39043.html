musical soiree

noun
evening gathering for music listening
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːzɪkəl swɑːˈreɪ / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪkəl ˈswɑːreɪ / (BrE)
A social gathering held in the evening for the purpose of listening to music, often featuring live performances in a private or intimate setting.

Examples

A musical soiree event
Invitations to the exclusive musical soiree were highly sought after among music lovers.
The annual musical soiree at the manor has become a cherished community tradition.
They hosted a musical soiree in their living room featuring a local string quartet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recital - a musical performance, usually by a single performer or small group
