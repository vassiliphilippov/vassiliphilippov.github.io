agenesis

noun
failure of body part to develop
5.1 9.3
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈdʒe.nə.sɪs/ /; / /eɪ.ˈdʒe.nə.sɪs/ (AmE), /ə.ˈdʒe.nə.sɪs/ /; / /eɪ.ˈdʒe.nə.sɪs/ (BrE)
