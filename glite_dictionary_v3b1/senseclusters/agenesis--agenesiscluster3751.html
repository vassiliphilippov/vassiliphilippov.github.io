agenesis

noun
failure of body part to develop
5.1
Pronunciation: / əˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (AmE), / əˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (BrE)
the absence or failure of development of a body part or organ, usually due to a congenital condition

Examples

Cerebral agenesis
Cerebral agenesis can lead to significant neurological complications.
Doctors discovered partial agenesis of the corpus callosum during the scan.
The infant was diagnosed with agenesis of the left kidney.
