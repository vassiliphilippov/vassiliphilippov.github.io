adjacently

adverb
next to something
Pronunciation: / əˈdʒeɪsəntli / (AmE), / əˈdʒeɪsəntli / (BrE)
in a way that is next to or adjoining something else

Examples

Located adjacently to rooms
The two classrooms are located adjacently on the first floor of the building.
Several shops were built adjacently to create a small shopping center.
The parking spaces are arranged adjacently for easy access to the entrance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apart - separated by a distance; not next to
  • synonym: beside - at the side of; next to
