coproprietor

noun
joint owner
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊprəˈpraɪətər / (AmE), / ˌkəʊprəˈpraɪətə / (BrE)
a person who owns something together with one or more other people

Examples

Each coproprietor share
Each coproprietor is responsible for a share of the building's maintenance costs.
The two sisters became coproprietors of the family restaurant after their parents retired.
As a coproprietor, he must agree with the other owners before making major decisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: co-owner - a person who owns something jointly with others
  • degree lower: proprietor - an owner of a business or property
  • antonym: sole owner - a person who owns something alone
