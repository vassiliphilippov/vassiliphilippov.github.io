quarter-hour

noun
fifteen-minute period
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔɚtɚ-ˌaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtə-ˌaʊə / (BrE)
A period of fifteen minutes; one quarter of an hour.

Examples

A quarter-hour period
I'll call you back in a quarter-hour when I'm free.
The ferry runs every quarter-hour during peak times.
We have only a quarter-hour left before the meeting starts.
