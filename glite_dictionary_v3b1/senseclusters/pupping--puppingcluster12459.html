pupping

noun
birth of young animals
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpʌpɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of giving birth to puppies or young animals, especially in certain mammals like dogs or seals

Examples

Seal pupping season
The researchers observed the pupping of seals along the remote coastline.
Spring is the main pupping season for many wild dog species.
During pupping, the mother requires a quiet and safe environment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calving - the act of giving birth to a calf
  • synonym: whelping - the act of a dog or other animal giving birth
