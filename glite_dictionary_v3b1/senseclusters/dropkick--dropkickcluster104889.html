dropkick

noun
football/rugby kick dropped and kicked as it touches ground
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɑpˌkɪk / (AmE), / ˈdrɒpˌkɪk / (BrE)
A kick, especially in football or rugby, made by dropping the ball and kicking it as it rebounds from the ground.

Examples

A dropkick in rugby
His successful dropkick tied the score before halftime.
Mastering the dropkick is crucial for rugby players aiming for accuracy and distance.
The player attempted a dropkick for a field goal in the final seconds of the game.

Related Terms

  • antonym: punt - A kick in which the ball is dropped and kicked before it touches the ground
