Sir Gawain

proper noun
Arthurian knight; Arthur's nephew
9.2
Pronunciation: /sɜːr ˈɡɑː.wən/ /; / /sɜːr ˈɡə.weɪn/ /; / /sɜːr ˈɡɑː.weɪn/ (AmE), /sɜː ˈɡɑː.weɪn/ /; / /sɜː ˈɡə.weɪn/ (BrE)
A nephew of King Arthur and one of the knights of the Round Table in Arthurian legend, renowned for his bravery and courtesy.

Examples
