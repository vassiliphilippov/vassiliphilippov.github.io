chyloderma

noun
swelling of the scrotum from lymphatic obstruction
7.0 9.9
Pronunciation: /kaɪ.lə.ˈdɜːr.mə/ (AmE), /kaɪ.lə.ˈdɜː.mə/ (BrE)
swelling of the scrotum caused by chronic obstruction of lymphatic vessels, often due to parasitic infection

Examples

Chyloderma swelling
Chyloderma is most commonly associated with infections such as filariasis in tropical regions.
The patient was diagnosed with chyloderma following persistent swelling in the scrotal area.
Treatment of chyloderma may require both surgery and medication to address the underlying lymphatic blockage.
