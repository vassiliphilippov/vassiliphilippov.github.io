snottily

adverb
rudely or arrogantly
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɑːtɪli / (AmE), / ˈsnɒtɪli / (BrE)
in a rude, arrogant, or disdainful manner

Examples

Speak snottily to others
He answered snottily when the teacher asked him a simple question.
The customer snottily dismissed the waiter's suggestion for dessert.
She snottily refused to join the group, thinking she was better than everyone else.
