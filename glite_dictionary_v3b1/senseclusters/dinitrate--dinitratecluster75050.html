dinitrate

noun
compound with two nitrates
7.9
Pronunciation: / daɪˈnaɪtreɪt / (AmE), / daɪˈnaɪtreɪt / (BrE)
a chemical compound that contains two nitrate groups in its molecular structure

Examples

A dinitrate medicine
Nitroglycerin is a well-known dinitrate used in medicine to treat heart conditions.
The laboratory synthesized a new dinitrate for use in chemical research.
Some explosives are based on the properties of dinitrate compounds.
