market analyst

noun
person analyzing financial or economic markets
1.0 3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrkət ˈænəˌlɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪt ˈænəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies and evaluates conditions affecting a market, especially financial markets, to provide advice or forecasts

Examples

Market analyst studies trends
As a market analyst, she reviews global economic trends to advise investors.
Many companies rely on a market analyst to interpret complex financial data.
The market analyst predicted that stock prices would rebound after the earnings reports.
