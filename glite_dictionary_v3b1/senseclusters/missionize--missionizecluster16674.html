missionize

verb
to promote beliefs
8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪʃ.ə.naɪz/ (AmE), /ˈmɪʃ.ə.naɪz/ (BrE)
to try to persuade people to accept or support a particular belief, cause, or ideology, often in a secular or political context

Examples

To missionize supporters
The organization aimed to missionize communities by sharing its environmental message.
Activists tried to missionize the public through social media campaigns and public events.
He spent years traveling to missionize for his political cause in different regions.
