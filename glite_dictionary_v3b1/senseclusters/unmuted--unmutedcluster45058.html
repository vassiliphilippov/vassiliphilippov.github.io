unmuted

adjective
sound turned on
3.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmjuːtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmjuːtɪd / (BrE)
having the sound turned back on or no longer silenced, especially on a device or application

Examples

Unmuted microphone
She realized her microphone was unmuted during the entire meeting.
After the break, the teacher asked everyone to stay unmuted for the discussion.
The video started playing automatically with the sound unmuted.
