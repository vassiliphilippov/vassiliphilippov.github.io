Tetrapanax

noun
plant pith for paper
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛtrəˈpænæks / (AmE), / ˌtɛtrəˈpænæks / (BrE)
the soft, spongy central tissue of the Tetrapanax papyrifer plant, traditionally used to make thin paper and crafts

Examples

Tetrapanax pith paper
The artist used Tetrapanax to create delicate sheets of traditional rice paper.
Crafters value Tetrapanax for its soft texture and versatility in making decorations.
In some regions, Tetrapanax is harvested specifically for its pith to produce fine paper.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rice paper - thin paper made from plant pith, often used for art or crafts
