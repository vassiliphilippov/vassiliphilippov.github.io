hand

noun
member of a ship's crew
1.0
Pronunciation: / hænd / (AmE), / hænd / (BrE)
a person who does manual or physical work, such as a crew member on a ship or a worker on a farm or ranch

Examples

Farm hand
Only a few experienced hands remained on board during the harsh winter voyage.
The captain called for every hand to prepare the ship for the incoming storm.
They needed an extra hand to help with the rigging.
During the busy season, extra hands are needed to manage the livestock.
The ranch owner welcomed the new hand who started work last week.
They hired an experienced hand to help with the autumn harvest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laborer - a person doing unskilled manual work
