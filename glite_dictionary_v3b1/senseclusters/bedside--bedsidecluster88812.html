bedside

noun
space beside a bed
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛd.saɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɛd.saɪd / (BrE)
the area or space next to a bed, especially when referring to someone attending to a sick or dying person

Examples

At the bedside nurse
Family and friends gathered at his bedside during his final hours.
She placed a glass of water on the table by his bedside before leaving the room.
The nurse remained at the bedside throughout the night to monitor the patient's condition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bed - a piece of furniture for sleeping
