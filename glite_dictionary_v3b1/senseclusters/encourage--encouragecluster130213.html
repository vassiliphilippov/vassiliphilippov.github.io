encourage

verb
give support or confidence; inspire hope
1.2 2.0
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈkɜːr.ɪdʒ/ /; / /ɪn.ˈkʌr.ɪdʒ/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈkʌr.ɪdʒ/ (BrE)
to give someone support, confidence, or hope so they will do or continue to do something

Examples
