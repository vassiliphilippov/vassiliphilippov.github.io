insult

verb
to offend or disrespect someone
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsʌlt / (AmE), / ɪnˈsʌlt / (BrE)
to speak to or treat someone with disrespect or scornful abuse

Examples

To insult a friend
He didn't mean to insult you, but his jokes were inappropriate.
She felt deeply hurt when they insulted her ideas during the meeting.
Stop insulting your brother and try to be more supportive.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compliment - to politely congratulate or praise
  • synonym: offend - to cause someone to feel upset or annoyed
