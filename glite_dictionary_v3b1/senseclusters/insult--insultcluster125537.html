Examples

  • revile
    8.2 1.86/m
    revile is to speak about someone angrily and with strong insults, while insult is any disrespectful speech toward a person.
  • disparage
    7.7 2.20/m

  • demean

  • slam
    6.3 1.11/m
    a slam is a sharp, often public critical remark, while insult is a broader act of disrespect or scorn in speech.
