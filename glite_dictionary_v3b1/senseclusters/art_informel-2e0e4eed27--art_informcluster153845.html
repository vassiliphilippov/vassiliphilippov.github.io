Art Informel

noun
1950s French abstract painting style
9.4
Pronunciation: / ɑrt ɛn.fɔrˈmɛl / (AmE), / ɑːt ɛ̃.fɔːˈmɛl / (BrE)
A style of painting originating in France in the 1950s, characterized by irregular, spontaneous applications of color, and comparable to abstract expressionism.
