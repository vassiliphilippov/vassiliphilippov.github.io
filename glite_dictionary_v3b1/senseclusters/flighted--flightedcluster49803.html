flighted

adjective
having feathers; able to fly
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈflaɪtɪd / (BrE)
having feathers or equipped with structures that enable flight

Examples

Bright flighted arrows
Arrows flighted with bright feathers are easier to spot in the grass.
The flighted bird soared high above the forest.
Unlike its flighted relatives, the kiwi cannot leave the ground.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flightless - unable to fly; lacking the ability to fly
