Saint Christopher-Nevis

proper noun
Caribbean country (Saint Kitts and Nevis)
5.9
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈkrɪstəfər ˈniːvɪs / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈkrɪstəfə ˈniːvɪs / (BrE)
