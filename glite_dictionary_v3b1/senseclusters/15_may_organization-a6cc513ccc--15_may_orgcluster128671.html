15 May Organization

proper noun
Palestinian militant group (1979–1980s)
9.9
Pronunciation: / fɪfˈtiːn meɪ ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / fɪfˈtiːn meɪ ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
