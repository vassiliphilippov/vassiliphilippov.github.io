bovinely

adverb
in a cow-like way
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊvɪnli / (AmE), / ˈbəʊvɪnli / (BrE)
in a way that is slow, dull, or resembling a cow

Examples

To answer bovinely
He answered the question bovinely, barely showing any interest in the topic.
She moved bovinely through the crowded hallway, not rushing despite the noise.
The students listened bovinely to the long lecture, their faces blank and unresponsive.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lively - in an energetic or active manner
  • synonym: sluggishly - in a slow or inactive manner
