belongingness

noun
feeling of acceptance
5.6
Pronunciation: / bɪˈlɔŋɪŋnəs / (AmE), / bɪˈlɒŋɪŋnəs / (BrE)
the feeling of being accepted and included as part of a group or community

Examples

Social belongingness
A strong sense of belongingness can improve a person's mental health and happiness.
Schools try to foster belongingness among students from different backgrounds.
The club gave her a feeling of belongingness she had never experienced before.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alienation - the feeling of being isolated or not belonging
  • synonym: inclusion - the state of being included within a group or structure
