partialness

noun
incompleteness; not being whole
7.7 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ëÀêr É…ôln…ôs / (AmE), / Ààp…ëÀê É…ôln…™s / (BrE)
The state or quality of being only a part or incomplete; not total or whole.

Examples

The partialness of knowledge
Recognizing the partialness of our knowledge is important in scientific inquiry.
She was frustrated by the partialness of the research, which failed to address several key points.
The partialness of his explanation left the audience wanting more details.
