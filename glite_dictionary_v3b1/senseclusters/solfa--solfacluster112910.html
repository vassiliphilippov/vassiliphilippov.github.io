solfa

noun
music pitch system using do, re, mi, etc.
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊlˌfɑː / (AmE), / ˈsɒlfɑː / (BrE)
a method of teaching singing and pitch using the syllables do, re, mi, fa, sol, la, and ti

Examples

Learning solfa method
Learning solfa can help beginners quickly read sheet music and identify pitches.
The choir practiced each part using the solfa system before singing the actual lyrics.
The teacher introduced the children to solfa with a fun song featuring do, re, and mi.

Related Terms

  • synonym: solfège - another term for the system of syllables for musical pitches
