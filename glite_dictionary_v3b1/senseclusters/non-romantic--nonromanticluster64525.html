non-romantic

adjective
practical, unsentimental
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn roʊˈmæntɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒn rəʊˈmæntɪk / (BrE)

Her non-romantic view of the project helped the team stay grounded and realistic.
