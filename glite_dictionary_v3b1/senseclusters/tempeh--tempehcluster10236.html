tempeh

noun
fermented soybean food
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛmpeɪ / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpeɪ / (BrE)
a firm, protein-rich food made from fermented soybeans, commonly used as a meat substitute

Examples

A tempeh dish
Many vegetarians use tempeh as a source of protein in their meals.
The chef marinated tempeh before grilling it for a smoky flavor.
You can crumble tempeh into salads or stir-fries for added texture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: meat - the flesh of animals used as food
  • synonym: tofu - a soft food made from soybeans, often used as a meat substitute
