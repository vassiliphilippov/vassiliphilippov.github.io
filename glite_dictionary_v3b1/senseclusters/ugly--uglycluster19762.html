ugly

adjective
morally bad or offensive
Pronunciation: / ˈʌɡli / (AmE), / ˈʌɡli / (BrE)
morally bad or offensive; wicked or reprehensible, especially in actions or events.

Examples

Ugly behavior
She was disgusted by the ugly corruption at the heart of the government.
The city was shaken by an ugly crime that stunned its citizens.
The scandal revealed some ugly truths about the organization's leadership.

Related Terms

  • antonym: admirable - deserving respect or approval
  • synonym: despicable - deserving hatred and contempt; morally reprehensible
  • synonym: vile - morally despicable or abhorrent
