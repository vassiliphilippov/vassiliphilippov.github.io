score

noun
amount owed; bill at a bar or restaurant
8.3
Pronunciation: / skɔr / (AmE), / skɔː / (BrE)
an amount owed, especially at a bar or restaurant; a bill or tab

Examples

Bar score
He asked the bartender to add the wine to his score for the night.
In old taverns, regulars often kept a running score behind the bar.
When I left the table, my score included two dinners and a bottle of wine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bill - a statement of money owed for goods or services supplied
  • synonym: tab - an informal term for a bill or amount owed at a bar or restaurant
