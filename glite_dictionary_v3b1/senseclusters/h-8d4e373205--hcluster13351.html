H

noun
chemical symbol for hydrogen element
Pronunciation: / eɪtʃ / (AmE), / eɪtʃ / (BrE)
the symbol for hydrogen, a colorless, odorless, highly flammable diatomic gas; the lightest and most abundant chemical element in the universe

Examples

H atom
Rocket fuel often uses liquid H for its high energy output.
The periodic table lists H as the first chemical element.
Water is composed of two atoms of H and one atom of oxygen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hydrogen - a colorless, odorless, highly flammable gas; the simplest element
