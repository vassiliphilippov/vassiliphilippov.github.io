folky

adjective
traditional in style
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊki / (AmE), / ˈfəʊki / (BrE)
having a simple, traditional, or folk-like style, especially in music or art

Examples

Folky music
The band played a folky tune that reminded everyone of old country songs.
She decorated her apartment in a folky style with handmade quilts and wooden furniture.
His paintings have a folky charm that appeals to people who love traditional art.

  • synonym: rustic - simple and typical of the countryside
