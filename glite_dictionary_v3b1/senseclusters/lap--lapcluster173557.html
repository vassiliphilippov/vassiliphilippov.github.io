lap

noun
circuit around a track or course
Pronunciation: / læp / (AmE), / læp / (BrE)
a single circuit of a track, course, or similar path, especially in a race or competition

Examples

Run a lap
Each runner must do four laps to finish the mile.
She completed the final lap with impressive speed to win the race.
The crowd cheered as the cars began their last lap around the circuit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: circuit - a complete journey around a course or track
  • degree higher: race - a competition of speed, often consisting of several laps
  • degree lower: segment - a part of a journey or process (less specific to racing)
