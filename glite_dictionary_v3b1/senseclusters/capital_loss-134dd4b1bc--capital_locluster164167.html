capital loss

noun
loss on sale of an asset
2.4 4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkæp.ɪ.təl lɔːs/ /; / /ˈkæp.ə.təl lɔːs/ (AmE), /ˈkæp.ɪ.təl lɒs/ /; / /ˈkæp.ə.təl lɒs/ (BrE)
A loss incurred when a capital asset is sold for less than its purchase price.

Examples

A capital loss on stocks
After selling the stock at a lower price, he had to report a capital loss on his taxes.
Investors try to avoid a capital loss by analyzing market trends before selling their assets.
The company experienced a capital loss when it sold its property for less than its original purchase price.

Related Terms

  • antonym: capital gain - the profit from selling an asset for more than its purchase price
  • synonym: loss - the fact or process of losing something or someone
