Adalat

noun
South Asian court
Pronunciation: / əˈdɑːlət / (AmE), / əˈdɑːlət / (BrE)
a court of law or a formal legal hearing, especially in South Asian countries

Examples

Village Adalat
The villagers brought their dispute before the Adalat to seek a fair resolution.
A special Adalat was set up to handle cases related to land rights in the region.
She was summoned to the Adalat to testify about the incident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: court - a place where legal cases are heard and decided
  • synonym: tribunal - a type of court with authority to judge claims or disputes
