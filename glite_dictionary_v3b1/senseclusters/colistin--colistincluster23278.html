colistin

noun
antibiotic for resistant infections
7.9
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈlɪs.tɪn/ /; / /koʊ.ˈlɪs.tɪn/ (AmE), /kə.ˈlɪs.tɪn/ /; / /kə.ˈlɪs.tən/ (BrE)
