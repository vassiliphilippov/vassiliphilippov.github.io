wild apple

noun
wild-growing apple tree with small fruit
1.9
Pronunciation: / waɪld ˈæpəl / (AmE), / waɪld ˈapəl / (BrE)
Any of various species of apple tree that grow naturally without cultivation, usually bearing small, acidic fruit.

Examples

Wild apple tree
Some foragers gather wild apples to make cider and preserves.
The wild apple tree blossoms earlier than most cultivated varieties.
The hedgerow was dotted with wild apples, their tart fruit attracting birds and deer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crab apple - small, sour apple or the tree it grows on
  • antonym: cultivated apple - domesticated apple tree grown for edible fruit
