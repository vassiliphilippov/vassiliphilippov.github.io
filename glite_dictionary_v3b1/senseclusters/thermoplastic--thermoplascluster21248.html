a plastic material that becomes soft when heated and hardens when cooled, a process that can be repeated without significant chemical change

Examples

A thermoplastic material
Polyethylene is a common thermoplastic used in packaging and containers.
Unlike thermoset plastics, a thermoplastic can be remelted and reshaped multiple times.
When heated, the thermoplastic can be molded into various shapes for manufacturing.
