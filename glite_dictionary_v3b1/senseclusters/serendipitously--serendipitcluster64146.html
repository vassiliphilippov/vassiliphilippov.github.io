serendipitously

adverb
by happy chance
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛrənˈdɪpɪtəsli / (AmE), / ˌsɛrənˈdɪpɪtəsli / (BrE)
in a way that happens by chance and results in a happy or beneficial outcome

Examples

To serendipitously find luck
He serendipitously met an old friend while traveling abroad for work.
The scientist serendipitously found a solution to the problem during a routine experiment.
They serendipitously stumbled upon a beautiful park hidden behind the city center.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accidentally - by chance, without intention
  • antonym: intentionally - on purpose, with intention
