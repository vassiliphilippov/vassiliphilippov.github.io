Caucasian cast

noun
group of white actors in a cast
7.9
Pronunciation: / kɔˈkeɪʒən kæst / (AmE), / kɔˈkeɪzɪən kɑːst / (BrE)
a group of actors in a film, play, or show who are all or mostly Caucasian (white) in ethnicity

Examples

A Caucasian cast
Producers are aiming for more diversity after years of favoring a Caucasian cast in their major projects.
The director faced criticism for choosing a Caucasian cast in a story set in Asia.
The play's Caucasian cast brought a particular interpretation to the historical drama.
