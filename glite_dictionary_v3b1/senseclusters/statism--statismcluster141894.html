statism

noun
state control system
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪtɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈsteɪtɪzəm / (BrE)
a political system or belief in which the state has substantial centralized control over social and economic affairs

Examples

Centralized statism control
Some economists argue that statism can hinder innovation and economic growth.
The country's shift toward statism led to increased government regulation of industries.
Debates about statism often focus on the balance between state power and individual freedom.

Related Terms
