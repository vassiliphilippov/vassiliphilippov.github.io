Vitruvian

adjective
of Vitruvius or his ideas
Pronunciation: / vɪˈtruːviən / (AmE), / vɪˈtruːvɪən / (BrE)
relating to the Roman architect Vitruvius or his principles, especially in architecture and design

Examples

Vitruvian principles in design
The architect designed the facade with Vitruvian symmetry and balance in mind.
Many Renaissance artists studied Vitruvian theories to perfect their understanding of proportion.
The museum features a Vitruvian courtyard inspired by ancient Roman designs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: classical - relating to ancient Greek or Roman principles in art or architecture
