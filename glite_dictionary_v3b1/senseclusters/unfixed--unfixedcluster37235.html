unfixed

adjective
not settled or definite
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪkst / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪkst / (BrE)
not defined, settled, or made definite; lacking clear or fixed content or form

The plan was still unfixed, with many details yet to be decided.
