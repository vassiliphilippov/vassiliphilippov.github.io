arty-crafty

adjective
pretentiously artistic; overly charming
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrtiˈkræfti / (AmE), / ˌɑːtiˈkrɑːfti / (BrE)
Pretentiously or affectedly artistic; overly charming in a cloying way.

Examples

An arty-crafty style
His studio had an arty-crafty look that tried too hard to impress visitors.
She finds the décor at that café a bit too arty-crafty for her taste.
The film was criticized as being arty-crafty rather than genuinely creative.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artsy-craftsy - showing an affected or contrived artistic quality
  • synonym: pretentious - attempting to impress by affecting greater importance or merit than is actual
