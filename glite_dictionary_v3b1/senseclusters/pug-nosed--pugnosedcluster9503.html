pug-nosed

adjective
having a short, flat nose
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌɡˌnoʊzd /; / pʌɡ ˈnoʊzd / (AmE), / ˈpʌɡ.nəʊzd /; / pʌɡ ˈnəʊzd / (BrE)
having a short, flat, and rounded or upturned nose, like that of a pug dog
