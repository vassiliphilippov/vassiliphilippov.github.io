subversion

noun
undermining or overthrowing authority
Pronunciation: / səbˈvɝːʒən / (AmE), / səbˈvɜːʃən / (BrE)
the act of undermining or overthrowing an established authority, especially a government

Examples

Subversion of government
Fears of subversion led to stricter security measures in the country.
The accused was charged with subversion for attempting to overthrow the government.
Through propaganda and secret meetings, they planned acts of subversion against the regime.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insurrection - an act of rebellion or uprising against authority
  • antonym: loyalty - faithful allegiance to a government or leader
  • synonym: sedition - conduct or speech inciting rebellion against authority
