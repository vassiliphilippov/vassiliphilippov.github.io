unlooked-for

adjective
not expected; unforeseen
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlʊkt fɔr / (AmE), / ʌnˈlʊkt fɔː / (BrE)
not expected or anticipated; unforeseen.

Examples

An unlooked-for opportunity
An unlooked-for opportunity arose when he received a call from an old friend.
Her success was an unlooked-for joy for the entire family.
They reacted quickly to the unlooked-for changes in the market.

Related Terms

  • antonym: foreseen - predicted or expected in advance
  • synonym: unexpected - not anticipated or predicted
