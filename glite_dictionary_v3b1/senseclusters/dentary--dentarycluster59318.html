dentary

adjective
of the lower jaw
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛntəri / (AmE), / ˈdɛntəri / (BrE)
relating to the dentary bone or the lower jaw, especially in vertebrates.

Examples

Dentary bone structure
The paleontologist identified the dentary structure in the ancient reptile's skull.
A dentary bone supports the lower teeth in most mammals.
Researchers compared the dentary features of different fossil species to understand their evolution.
