unwilled

adjective
not deliberate or intended
5.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɪld / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɪld / (BrE)
not done by deliberate intention, desire, or volition; not willed or intended

Examples

An unwilled reaction
His unwilled reaction revealed more than he intended.
Sometimes change comes about through unwilled circumstances beyond anyone's control.
The poem spoke of an unwilled longing that surprised the narrator.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intentional - done on purpose; deliberately
  • synonym: spontaneous - performed or occurring as a result of sudden impulse
  • antonym: voluntary - done, given, or acting of one's own free will
