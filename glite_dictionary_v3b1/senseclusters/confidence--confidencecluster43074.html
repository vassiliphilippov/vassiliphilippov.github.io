confidence

noun
a secret entrusted to someone
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːn.fɪ.dəns / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.fɪ.dəns / (BrE)
a secret or private matter that is entrusted to someone and is not to be revealed to others

Examples

A confidence shared
As a doctor, she was used to hearing her patients' confidences.
He shared the confidence only with his closest friend.
She told him her deepest confidence, trusting he would never betray her trust.
