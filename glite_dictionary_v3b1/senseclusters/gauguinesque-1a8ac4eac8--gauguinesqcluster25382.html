Gauguinesque

adjective
in the style of Paul Gauguin
9.8
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊˌɡæ̃ˈnɛsk / (AmE), / ɡəʊˌɡænˈɛsk / (BrE)
Resembling or characteristic of the style or works of Paul Gauguin, especially in bold colors and simplified forms.

Examples

Gauguinesque bold colors
She wore a dress with Gauguinesque patterns, bold and evocative.
The artist's latest series features Gauguinesque colors and tropical scenery.
The critic described the painting’s lush, Gauguinesque landscapes as both vibrant and dreamlike.
