recherche

adjective
lavishly elegant or refined
Pronunciation: / rəˌʃɛrˈʃeɪ / (AmE), / rəˈʃɛə / (BrE)
Lavishly elegant, refined, or rare, especially in a way that is exquisitely chosen or sought after.

Examples

A recherche style
His taste in music is rather recherche, favoring obscure Baroque compositions.
She wore a recherche gown that stunned everyone at the gala.
The restaurant's décor was strikingly recherche, with every detail carefully considered for maximum effect.

Related Terms

  • antonym: crude - in a natural or raw state; not yet processed or refined
  • synonym: elegant - pleasingly graceful and stylish in appearance or manner
  • synonym: refined - with elegant and cultured manners and taste
