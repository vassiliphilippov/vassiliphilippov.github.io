algometry

noun
measurement of pain sensitivity
Pronunciation: / ælˈɡɑːmətri / (AmE), / ælˈɡɒmətri / (BrE)
the measurement of sensitivity to pain, especially as a response to pressure

Examples

Algometry of pain
Advances in algometry have improved how we evaluate chronic pain conditions.
Researchers rely on algometry to standardize pain sensitivity measurements in clinical trials.
The doctor used algometry to assess the patient’s pain threshold.
