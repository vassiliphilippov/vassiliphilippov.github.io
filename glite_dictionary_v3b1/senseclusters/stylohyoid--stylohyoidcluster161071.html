stylohyoid

adjective
of styloid and hyoid
9.9
Pronunciation: /staɪ.loʊ.ˈhaɪ.ɔɪd/ (AmE), /staɪ.ləʊ.ˈhaɪ.ɔɪd/ (BrE)
relating to the muscle, ligament, or structures connecting the styloid process and the hyoid bone

Examples

Stylohyoid ligament

Damage to the stylohyoid ligament can affect neck movement and speech.
Surgeons must be careful not to injure the stylohyoid structures during throat operations.
