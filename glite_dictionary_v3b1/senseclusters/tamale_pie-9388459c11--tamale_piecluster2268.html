tamale pie

noun
casserole with meat and cornbread topping
9.4
Pronunciation: / təˈmɑːli paɪ / (AmE), / təˈmɑːli paɪ / (BrE)
A savory casserole of seasoned meat and vegetables, typically topped with cornbread, inspired by Mexican tamales.

Examples

A tamale pie casserole
For dinner, we enjoyed a delicious tamale pie topped with melted cheese and jalapeños.
My grandmother makes tamale pie every Christmas as part of our family tradition.
The recipe combines ground beef, tomatoes, and cornmeal to create a hearty tamale pie.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tamale - traditional Mexican dish of seasoned meat wrapped in dough and steamed
