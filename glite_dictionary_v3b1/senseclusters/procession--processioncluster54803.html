Examples

Funeral procession
A long procession of cars followed the hearse through the streets.
The annual religious festival featured a colorful procession through the city center.

Synonyms

  • cavalcade
    7.9 0.54/m
    a cavalcade is a formal parade of people, animals, or vehicles used in ceremonies and celebrations, while a procession is any orderly ceremonial line.
  • train
    1.3 4.85/m
    a train is a long line or series of connected people, animals, or vehicles, while a procession is mainly an orderly ceremonial movement.
  • march
    2.6 7.18/m
    a march is a public walk by people often for protest or support, while a procession is usually an orderly ceremonial movement.

  • antonym: retreat - the act of withdrawing, especially from danger or a difficult situation
