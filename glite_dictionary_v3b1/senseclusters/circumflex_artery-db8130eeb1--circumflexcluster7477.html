circumflex artery

noun
an artery that curves around a structure
5.3 6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːkəmˌflɛks ˈɑːrˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkəmˌflɛks ˈɑːtəri / (BrE)
any of several arteries that follow a curved path around a structure, especially one of the arteries supplying the heart or shoulder region

Examples

The circumflex artery path
Anatomy students must memorize the path of the circumflex artery around the heart.
Blockage of the left circumflex artery can result in a heart attack.
During the operation, the surgeon carefully avoided damaging the circumflex artery.
