Bondo

noun
auto body filler
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑndoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɒndəʊ / (BrE)
a brand of putty used to fill and repair dents or damage in car bodies, especially before painting

Examples

Bondo car repair
He applied Bondo to the dented fender before sanding it smooth.
After the accident, she used Bondo to repair the scratches on her car door.
The mechanic recommended using Bondo to fill the holes before repainting the vehicle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: filler - a substance used to fill holes or cracks in surfaces
  • synonym: putty - a soft substance used to fill small holes or cracks
