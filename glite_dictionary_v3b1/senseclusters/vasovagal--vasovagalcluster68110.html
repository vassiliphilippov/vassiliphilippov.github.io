vasovagal

adjective
vagus nerve effect
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌveɪzoʊˈveɪɡəl / (AmE), / ˌveɪsəʊˈveɪɡəl / (BrE)
relating to the action of the vagus nerve on blood vessels, often resulting in fainting or a sudden drop in blood pressure

Examples

Vasovagal response
A vasovagal reaction can occur when someone is frightened by the sight of blood.
Doctors explained that her fainting was due to a vasovagal episode during the procedure.
Standing up too quickly sometimes triggers a vasovagal response in sensitive individuals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orthostatic - relating to an upright posture, often used for blood pressure changes
  • synonym: syncopal - relating to or causing fainting
