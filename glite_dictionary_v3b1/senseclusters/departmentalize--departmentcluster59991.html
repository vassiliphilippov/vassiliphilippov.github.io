departmentalize

verb
to divide into departments
6.4
Pronunciation: / dɪˌpɑːrtˈmɛntəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / dɪˌpɑːtˈmɛntəlaɪz / (BrE)
to divide an organization or system into separate departments, each with its own responsibilities

Examples

To departmentalize a company
Many large organizations departmentalize their staff to improve efficiency and management.
The school decided to departmentalize its curriculum to better serve students' interests.
After the merger, the company departmentalized its operations to handle the increased workload.
The new manager decided to departmentalize the company to improve workflow and accountability.
Many large organizations departmentalize their operations to handle complex tasks more efficiently.
After rapid growth, the startup began to departmentalize its teams for better management.

Related Terms

  • antonym: centralize - to bring activities together under a single authority
  • synonym: organize - to arrange or structure systematically
