starship

noun
spacecraft for interstellar travel (sci-fi)
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈstɑːʃɪp / (BrE)
A spacecraft, especially in science fiction, designed to carry a crew into interstellar space.

Examples

A starship in space
Many classic science fiction novels feature a starship as the main setting for the story.
The starship engaged its warp drive and vanished into the depths of space.
The crew trained for months before embarking on their journey aboard the new starship.
