inhumanly

adverb
without compassion
7.4
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈhjuː.mən.liː/ /; / /ɪn.ˈhuː.mən.liː/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈhjuː.mən.liː/ /; / /ɪn.ˈhuː.mən.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that lacks human qualities such as kindness, compassion, or mercy

Examples

Inhumanly cruel treatment
The animals were inhumanly neglected by their owners for weeks.
During the war, some prisoners were treated inhumanly by the guards.
The dictator ruled inhumanly, showing no mercy to his opponents.

Related Terms
