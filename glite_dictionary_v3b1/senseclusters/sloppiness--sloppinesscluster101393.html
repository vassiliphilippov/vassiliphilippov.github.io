sloppiness

noun
lack of order or tidiness
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈslɑːpinəs /; / ˈslɑːpi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈslɒpinɪs /; / ˈslɒp.i.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being careless, untidy, or lacking attention to detail

Examples

Sloppiness in work
His sloppiness at work eventually led to several mistakes in the reports.
Teachers often complain about the sloppiness of students' handwriting.
The sloppiness of the room made it hard to find anything.
She was criticized for the sloppiness in her research methods.
The sloppiness of his handwriting made it hard to read the exam answers.
There is no room for sloppiness when it comes to surgery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carelessness - lack of attention or thought to avoiding harm or errors
  • antonym: neatness - the quality of being tidy and orderly
