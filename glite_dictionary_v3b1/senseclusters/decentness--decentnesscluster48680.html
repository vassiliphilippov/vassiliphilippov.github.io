decentness

noun
acceptable quality
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːsəntnəs / (AmE), / ˈdiːsntnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being acceptable, adequate, or meeting a reasonable standard

Examples

The decentness of a place
The decentness of the hotel surprised us, given its low price.
She appreciated the decentness of his behavior during the meeting.
We were satisfied with the decentness of the meal, even if it was not exceptional.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adequacy - the state of being sufficient or acceptable
  • antonym: insufficiency - the state of not being enough or adequate
