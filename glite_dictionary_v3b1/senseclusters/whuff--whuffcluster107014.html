whuff

noun
dull thudding sound
9.9
Pronunciation: / wʌf / (AmE), / wʌf / (BrE)
a dull, heavy sound made by a blow or something falling softly but forcefully

Examples

A whuff on the floor
The book hit the carpet with a soft whuff that barely made a sound.
He dropped his backpack onto the couch, and it landed with a gentle whuff.
A sudden whuff echoed through the room as the cushion fell to the floor.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: bang - a sudden loud noise, often from a blow or impact
  • degree lower: tap - a light touch or gentle sound
  • synonym: thud - a dull sound made by a heavy object hitting something
