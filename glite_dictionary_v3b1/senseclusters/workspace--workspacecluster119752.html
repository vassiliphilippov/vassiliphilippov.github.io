workspace

noun
area allocated for work
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜːrk.speɪs / (AmE), / ˈwɜːk.speɪs / (BrE)
an area or room where you do your work, especially in an office or at a computer

Examples

A tidy workspace
My new office has a much larger workspace with plenty of room for my computer and files.
Please tidy your workspace before leaving at the end of the day.
The company redesigned the workspace to encourage more collaboration between employees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: workstation - a desk or area with equipment where one works, especially with a computer
