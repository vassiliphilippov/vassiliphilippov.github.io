eyeblink

verb
to close eyelids quickly
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪˌblɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈaɪˌblɪŋk / (BrE)
to close and open one or both eyelids very quickly, usually as a reflex or reaction.

Examples

To eyeblink quickly
When the bright light flashed, she could not help but eyeblink in surprise.
Some animals eyeblink more frequently to keep their eyes moist.
He tried not to eyeblink during the staring contest with his friend.
