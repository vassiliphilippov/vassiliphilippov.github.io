snatch block

noun
pulley block with side opening
Pronunciation: / snætʃ blɑk / (AmE), / snatʃ blɒk / (BrE)
a pulley block designed with an opening feature so it can receive the standing part or bight of a rope without threading the end through

Examples

A snatch block pulley
Before lifting the load, ensure the snatch block is correctly closed and locked.
The mechanic attached a snatch block to the tree to redirect the winch line.
Whenever a change of direction was needed, they used a snatch block for the heavy lifting.
