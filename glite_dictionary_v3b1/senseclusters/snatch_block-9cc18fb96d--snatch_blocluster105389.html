snatch block

noun
pulley block with side opening
8.3 7.4
Pronunciation: / snætʃ blɑk / (AmE), / snatʃ blɒk / (BrE)
a pulley block designed with an opening feature so it can receive the standing part or bight of a rope without threading the end through
