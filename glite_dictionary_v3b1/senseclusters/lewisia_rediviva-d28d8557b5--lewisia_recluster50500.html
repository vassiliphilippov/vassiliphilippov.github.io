Lewisia rediviva

noun
a succulent plant, Montana state flower
9.9
Pronunciation: / luˈiːziə ˌrɛdɪˈvaɪvə / (AmE), / luˈiːziə ˌrɛdɪˈvaɪvə / (BrE)
a low-growing succulent plant native to the Rocky Mountains, noted for its deep to pale pink flowers and fleshy, starchy roots; also known as the Montana state flower
