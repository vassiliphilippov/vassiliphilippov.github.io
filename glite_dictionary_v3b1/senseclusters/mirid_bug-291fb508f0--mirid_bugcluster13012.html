mirid bug

noun
plant-feeding insect of the Miridae family
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪrɪd bʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈmɪrɪd bʌɡ / (BrE)
A small plant-feeding insect of the family Miridae, commonly known as a leaf bug, found on a variety of plants.
