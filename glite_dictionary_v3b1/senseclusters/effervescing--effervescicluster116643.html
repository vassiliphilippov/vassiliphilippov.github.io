Examples

Effervescing mineral water
A glass of effervescing mineral water can be refreshing on a hot day.

The winemaker observed the effervescing must as fermentation began.

Synonyms

  • effervesce
    8.2 0.03/m
    effervesce means a liquid produces small gas bubbles and becomes fizzy, while effervescing describes that bubbling action as it happens.
  • bubble
    1.0 2.90/m
    bubble means to form or release visible round bubbles in a liquid, while effervescing focuses on fine fizz from dissolved gas.
  • froth
    3.0 0.65/m
    froth forms a layer of foam or many small bubbles on a liquid's surface, while effervescing usually describes continuous fizzing throughout.
  • sparkle
    3.0 0.34/m
    sparkle shows bright tiny bubbles or light on a drink's surface, while effervescing refers to active gas bubbling through the whole liquid.

  • antonym: flat - not bubbling; lacking effervescence (of a drink)
