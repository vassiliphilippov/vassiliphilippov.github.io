Mexican husk tomato

noun
plant with edible tomato-like fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛksɪkən hʌsk təˈmeɪtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɛksɪkən hʌsk təˈmɑːtəʊ / (BrE)
An annual plant native to Mexico and the southern United States, with edible purplish sticky fruits resembling small tomatoes, often used in cooking.

Examples
