lunisolar calendar

noun
calendar based on lunar and solar cycles
7.0 7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌluː.niˈsoʊ.lɚ ˈkæl.ən.dɚ / (AmE), / ˌluː.nɪˈsəʊ.lə ˈkæl.ɪn.də / (BrE)
a calendar that is based on both the lunar months and the solar year, combining features of lunar and solar calendars

Examples

A lunisolar calendar
A lunisolar calendar uses months based on the moon and years adjusted to match the sun.
Many ancient civilizations relied on a lunisolar calendar to organize their agricultural and religious activities.
The Hebrew calendar is a well-known example of a lunisolar calendar.

Related Terms

  • antonym: solar calendar - a calendar based solely on the solar year
