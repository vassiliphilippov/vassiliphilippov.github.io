sidewinder

noun
an infrared-guided air-to-air missile
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdˌwaɪndər / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdˌwaɪndə / (BrE)
a type of air-to-air missile that uses an infrared homing device to track and destroy enemy aircraft

Examples

Sidewinder missile
Modern air forces still rely on the sidewinder for close-range aerial combat.
Old footage showed a sidewinder missile locking onto its heat-emitting target.
The fighter jet launched a sidewinder to intercept the enemy aircraft.
