venae pudendum

noun
vein in the external genital area
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈviːnaɪ pjuːˈdɛndəm / (AmE), / ˈviːniː pjuːˈdɛndəm / (BrE)
a vein or group of veins located in the external genital area (pudendum) of the body, especially in women.
