homeostatic

adjective
relating to homeostasis or internal stability
Pronunciation: / ˌhoʊ.mi.oʊˈstæt.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌhəʊ.mi.əʊˈstæt.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by homeostasis; tending to maintain internal stability in an organism or system.

Examples

Homeostatic balance
A disruption in homeostatic balance can lead to various health issues.
Scientists study how homeostatic feedback loops regulate temperature and other vital functions.
The body's homeostatic mechanisms work continuously to maintain a stable internal environment.
