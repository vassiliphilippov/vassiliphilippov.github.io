flaker

noun
unreliable person
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪkər / (AmE), / ˈfleɪkə / (BrE)
a person who often cancels plans or fails to show up as promised

Examples

A social flaker
I stopped making plans with him because he is such a flaker.
You can’t count on a flaker to show up when you need them.
Her friends were frustrated by how much of a flaker she had become.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flake - a person who is unreliable or often cancels plans
  • antonym: reliable person - someone who can be depended on to keep promises
