tender

adjective
showing gentleness or sympathy
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛndɚ /; / ˈtɛn.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɛndə /; / ˈten.də / (BrE)
showing gentleness, kindness, or emotional sensitivity; affectionate

Examples

Tender hug

He spoke in a tender voice that soothed the frightened child.
She gave him a tender hug before saying goodbye.
The film captures the tender moments between a mother and her baby.
