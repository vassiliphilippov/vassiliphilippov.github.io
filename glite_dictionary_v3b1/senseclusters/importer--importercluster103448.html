importer

noun
person or company that imports goods
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɔrtər / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɔːtə / (BrE)
a person or company that brings goods or services into a country from abroad for sale

Examples

A major importer of cars
As an importer, she attends international trade fairs to find new products.
Many importers must comply with strict government regulations.
The company recently became a leading importer of electronic goods in the region.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exporter - a person or company that sells goods to another country
