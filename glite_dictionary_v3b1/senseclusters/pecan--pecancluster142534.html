Examples

Synonyms

  • pecan tree
    2.7 0.25/m
    pecan tree is the common phrase for the large nut-producing tree, while pecan is the shorter name that can mean the nut or tree.
  • Carya illinoensis
    9.9 0.00/m
    Carya illinoensis is the scientific Latin name used by botanists, while pecan is the everyday word for the same tree.
  • Carya illinoinsis
    9.9 0.00/m
    Carya illinoinsis is a misspelled scientific form seen in texts, while pecan is the common everyday name for the tree and its nuts.
