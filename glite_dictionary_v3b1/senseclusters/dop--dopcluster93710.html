dop

noun
film camera chief
Pronunciation: / diː oʊ piː / (AmE), / diː əʊ piː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for director of photography, the person responsible for camera work and lighting in a film or television production

Examples

Film DOP
The dop worked closely with the director to achieve the film's unique visual style.
After reviewing the footage, the dop suggested adjusting the lighting for the next scene.
Many aspiring filmmakers dream of becoming a dop in the movie industry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cinematographer - the person responsible for the camera and lighting in film production
