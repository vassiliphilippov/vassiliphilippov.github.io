An imaginary evil being or spirit, typically used to frighten children; a bogle or goblin. Sometimes, when used as a proper name, refers to the Devil.

Examples

Scary bogy under bed
As a child, she feared the bogy lurking under her bed.
In some folklore, the bogy is considered another name for the Devil.
The old house was said to be haunted by a terrifying bogy.
