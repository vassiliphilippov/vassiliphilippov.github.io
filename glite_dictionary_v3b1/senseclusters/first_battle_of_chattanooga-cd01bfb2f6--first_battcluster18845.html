First Battle of Chattanooga

proper noun
1863 Civil War battle near Chattanooga
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝst ˈbætəl əv ˌtʃætəˈnuːɡə / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ˈbætəl ɒv ˌtʃætəˈnuːɡə / (BrE)
a major 1863 battle of the American Civil War where Union forces led by Ulysses S. Grant decisively defeated Confederate troops near Chattanooga, Tennessee
