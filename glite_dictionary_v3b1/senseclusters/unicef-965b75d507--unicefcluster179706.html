UNICEF

proper noun
United Nations agency for children's aid
Pronunciation: / ˈjuː.nəˌsɛf / (AmE), / ˈjuː.nɪˌsɛf / (BrE)
an international agency of the United Nations that provides aid to improve the education, health, and welfare of children and mothers, especially in developing countries

Examples

UNICEF aid programs
UNICEF has launched several initiatives to improve access to clean drinking water in rural areas.
Many celebrities support UNICEF as part of their humanitarian efforts.
The school organized a fundraiser to donate money to UNICEF's vaccination campaign.

Related Terms

  • synonym: United Nations - an international organization founded to promote peace, security, and cooperation
