nonfaculty

noun
non-teaching staff
6.3
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈfækəlti / (AmE), / nɒnˈfækəlti / (BrE)
a person employed by a school, college, or university who is not a member of the teaching faculty

Examples

A nonfaculty employee
The university relies on nonfaculty employees to manage its administrative operations.
Many nonfaculty members attended the staff appreciation event last Friday.
Budget cuts affected both faculty and nonfaculty positions at the college.

Related Terms

  • antonym: faculty - the teaching staff at a school, college, or university
  • synonym: staff - employees of an organization, especially non-teaching personnel
