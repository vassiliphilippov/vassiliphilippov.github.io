bleh

noun
mild apathy
Pronunciation: / blɛ / (AmE), / blɛ / (BrE)
a feeling of listlessness, mild unhappiness, or lack of interest or enthusiasm

Examples

A feeling of bleh
After a week of rain, I felt a sense of bleh and didn't want to do anything.
He described his mood as pure bleh after staying up all night studying.
Sometimes, a little exercise can help you shake off that bleh feeling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apathy - lack of interest or enthusiasm
  • antonym: enthusiasm - strong excitement or interest
