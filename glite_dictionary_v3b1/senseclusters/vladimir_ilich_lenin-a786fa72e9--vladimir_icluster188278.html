Vladimir Ilich Lenin

proper noun
Russian revolutionary leader and first Soviet head
Pronunciation: / vlɑːˈdɪmɪr iˈlitʃ ˈlɛnɪn / (AmE), / vləˈdɪmɪə iˈlɪtʃ ˈlɛnɪn / (BrE)
(1870–1924) Russian revolutionary leader and founder of the Bolsheviks; the first head of the Soviet Union.
