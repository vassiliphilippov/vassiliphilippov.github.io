fusionism

noun
combining styles or genres
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfjuː.ʒə.nɪ.zəm/ /; / /ˈfjuː.ʒən.ɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈfjuː.ʒə.nɪ.zəm/ /; / /ˈfjuː.ʒən.ɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
