fusionism

noun
combining styles or genres
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːʒənɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈfjuːʒənɪzəm / (BrE)
the practice or tendency of combining different styles, genres, techniques, or cultural elements, especially in art, music, or cuisine

Examples

Artistic fusionism
The artist's fusionism brought together classical painting techniques and modern digital effects.
Many contemporary chefs embrace fusionism to create unique dishes that blend global flavors.
The music festival celebrated fusionism by featuring bands that mix jazz, rock, and electronic styles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hybridization - the process of mixing different elements to create something new
  • antonym: purism - strict adherence to traditional forms or styles
