fig

noun
sweet pear-shaped fruit
3.2
Pronunciation: / fɪɡ / (AmE), / fɪɡ / (BrE)
A soft, sweet fruit with a thin skin that is usually yellowish or purple, eaten fresh, dried, or preserved.

Examples

A fresh fig
A fresh fig tastes delicious with a drizzle of honey.
She added dried fig to the granola for extra sweetness.
They preserved the fig harvest by making jam.
