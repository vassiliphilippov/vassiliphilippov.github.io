boat

noun
small vessel for travel on water
Pronunciation: / boʊt / (AmE), / bəʊt / (BrE)
a small vessel designed for traveling on water, especially for personal or recreational use

Examples

A boat trip
A small boat can be quickly launched from the shore in case of emergency.
She learned to steer a boat when she was just ten years old.
We rented a boat and spent the afternoon rowing on the lake.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: raft - a flat structure for floating on water, usually made of planks or logs fastened together
  • degree higher: ship - a large vessel for traveling on water, typically for commercial or military use
