currant

noun
shrub bearing edible berries (genus Ribes)
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝːənt / (AmE), / ˈkʌr.ənt / (BrE)
any of various small deciduous shrubs of the genus Ribes, bearing clusters of edible red, black, or white berries.
