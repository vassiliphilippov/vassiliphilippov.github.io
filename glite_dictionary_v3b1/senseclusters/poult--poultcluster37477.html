poult

noun
young bird
Pronunciation: / poʊlt / (AmE), / pəʊlt / (BrE)
a young domestic bird, especially a young turkey or chicken, that has not yet reached maturity

Examples

A young poult

Each poult needs warmth and special care during its first weeks.
