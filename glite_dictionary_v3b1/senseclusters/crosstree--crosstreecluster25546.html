crosstree

noun
mast support beam
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkrɑːs.triː/ /; / /ˈkrɔːs.triː/ (AmE), /ˈkrɒs.triː/ (BrE)
a horizontal beam fixed across a ship's mast to support the rigging and provide a platform for sailors

Examples

The crosstree beam
The sailor climbed up to the crosstree to adjust the sails.
From the crosstree, she had a clear view of the horizon and approaching ships.
The old ship's crosstrees were made of sturdy oak to withstand strong winds.

Related Terms
