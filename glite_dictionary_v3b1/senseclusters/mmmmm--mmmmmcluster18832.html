mmmmm

interjection
hesitation sound
Pronunciation: / mːːːːː / (AmE), / mːːːːː / (BrE)
used to indicate that the speaker is thinking, hesitating, or considering something before responding

Examples

Mmmmm thinking
She looked at the menu and said, 'mmmmm, I can't decide what to order.'
After hearing the question, he replied with a thoughtful 'mmmmm' before giving his answer.
'Mmmmm,' she murmured, considering whether to accept the invitation or not.

Related Terms

  • synonym: er - a sound used when pausing to think or hesitate
  • synonym: um - a sound used to show hesitation or uncertainty
