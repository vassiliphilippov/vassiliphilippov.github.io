pin a rose on your nose

x
sarcastic way to acknowledge praise or arrogance
Pronunciation: / pɪn ə roʊz ɑn jʊr noʊz / (AmE), / pɪn ə rəʊz ɒn jɔː nəʊz / (BrE)
A sarcastic or humorous expression used to congratulate someone for their achievement or to suggest that they are being arrogant or self-congratulatory.

Examples

Pin a rose on your nose
If you finished first again, pin a rose on your nose.
Whenever he boasts about his grades, his sister tells him to pin a rose on your nose.
“Oh, so you cooked dinner by yourself? Pin a rose on your nose,” she joked.
