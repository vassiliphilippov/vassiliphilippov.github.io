fembot

noun
artificial-seeming woman
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛmˌbɑt / (AmE), / ˈfɛmˌbɒt / (BrE)
a slang and often offensive term for a woman who appears mechanical, unemotional, or excessively artificial in behavior.

Examples

She acts like a fembot
Some critics accused the celebrity of acting like a fembot during interviews, never showing real emotion.
He joked that the customer service agent sounded like a fembot because of her scripted responses.
Calling someone a fembot can be considered rude and disrespectful.
