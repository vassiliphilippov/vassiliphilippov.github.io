multigravida

noun
woman pregnant multiple times
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈɡrævɪdə / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈɡrævɪdə / (BrE)
a woman who has been pregnant more than once

Examples

A multigravida woman
The study compared the experiences of multigravida women with those pregnant for the first time.
As a multigravida, she was familiar with the symptoms of early pregnancy.
Doctors may monitor a multigravida differently than a woman expecting her first child.

Related Terms

  • antonym: primigravida - a woman who is pregnant for the first time
