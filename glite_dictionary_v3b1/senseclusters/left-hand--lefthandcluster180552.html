left-hand

adjective
designed for the left hand
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛft ˌhænd / (AmE), / ˈlɛft ˌhænd / (BrE)
(of an object) designed or intended to be used with the left hand.

Examples

Left-hand scissors
He preferred the left-hand door handle because it felt more natural to him.
She couldn't find her left-hand glove anywhere.
The store sells left-hand scissors for people who find regular ones difficult to use.

  • antonym: right-hand - designed for the right hand
