Uighur

noun
historical Central Asian writing system
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːgər / (AmE), / ˈwiːɡə / (BrE)
an alphabetic script derived from Aramaic, historically used to write the Uighur language and other Central Asian languages

Examples

Uighur script
Linguists study the evolution of the Uighur alphabet and its influence on neighboring scripts.
Many manuscripts from Central Asia were written in the Uighur script.
The Uighur script was adapted for use by the Mongols in their own writing system.
