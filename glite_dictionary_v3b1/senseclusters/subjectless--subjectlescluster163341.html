subjectless

adjective
without a subject
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbdʒɪktləs / (AmE), / ˈsʌbdʒɪktləs / (BrE)
having no clear subject, topic, or identifiable person or thing that something is about

Examples

Subjectless poetry
The poet's subjectless verses left readers searching for meaning in the imagery.
Some modern art is intentionally subjectless, focusing on color and form instead of recognizable figures.
The discussion became subjectless as participants drifted from topic to topic without direction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abstract - not representing recognizable objects or people
  • antonym: thematic - having or relating to a clear subject or theme
