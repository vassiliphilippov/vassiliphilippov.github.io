interphone

noun
internal telephone system for communication within a building
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntɚˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈɪntəˌfəʊn / (BrE)
a telephone-based communication system connecting different rooms within a building, ship, or similar structure

Examples

The interphone system
Please call me on the interphone if you need anything during the meeting.
The captain used the interphone to give instructions to the engine room.
The hospital's interphone system allows staff to communicate quickly between different departments.
