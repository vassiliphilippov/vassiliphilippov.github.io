magical

adjective
relating to magic or supernatural powers
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmædʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈmadʒɪkəl / (BrE)
possessing or using supernatural powers, or having qualities that seem to be produced by magic

Examples

Magical powers
He found a magical amulet that was said to bring good luck.
Her performance on stage was magical, completely enchanting the audience.
The castle was hidden by a magical barrier that only wizards could pass through.

Related Terms

  • synonym: magic - having or using supernatural powers
  • antonym: ordinary - not magical; having nothing extraordinary or supernatural
