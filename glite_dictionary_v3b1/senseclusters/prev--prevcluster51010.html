prev

verb
to go to previous item
8.8
Pronunciation: / prɛv / (AmE), / prɛv / (BrE)
to move back to or display the previous item, especially in a sequence or list, often used in digital controls.

Examples

To prev a photo
If you want to see the earlier photo, just tap prev on your screen.
She clicked prev to review the previous slide in her presentation.
Use the prev button to go back to the last song in the playlist.

Related Terms

  • synonym: back - to return to a previous position or item
  • antonym: next - to move forward to the following item
