cerebrospinal fluid

noun
clear fluid around the brain and spinal cord
5.3 4.3
Pronunciation: /sə.rə.broʊ.ˈspaɪ.nəl ˈfluː.ɪd/ /; / /sə.rə.brə.ˈspaɪ.nəl ˈfluː.ɪd/ /; / /sə.rə.broʊ.ˈspaɪ.nəl ˈfluː.əd/ (AmE), /se.rə.brə.ˈspaɪ.nəl ˈfluː.ɪd/ /; / /sə.rə.brə.ˈspaɪ.nəl ˈfluː.əd/ (BrE)
the clear liquid produced in the ventricles of the brain that surrounds and protects the brain and spinal cord
