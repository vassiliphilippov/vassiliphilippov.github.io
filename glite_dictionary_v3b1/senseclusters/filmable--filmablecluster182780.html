filmable

adjective
capable of being made into a film
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪlməbəl / (AmE), / ˈfɪlməbl / (BrE)
(of a story or literary work) suitable to be adapted into a film or motion picture.

Examples

A filmable story
Some works of fiction are simply not filmable despite their literary merit.
The director looks for filmable stories that can captivate audiences on the big screen.
The novel was considered highly filmable due to its vivid imagery and compelling plot.
