hive

noun
man-made container for bees
Pronunciation: / haɪv / (AmE), / haɪv / (BrE)
a structure, either natural or made by people, that is a home for bees

Examples

A hive of bees
Several hives stood in the sunny corner of the garden.
The beekeeper carefully opened the hive to check on the bees.
They harvested honey from the hive in late summer.
Smoke drifted from the hive as the beekeeper tried to calm the bees.
The wild bees built their hive in the hollow trunk of an old oak tree.
We found a natural hive while hiking through the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beehive - structure in which bees are kept, especially a man-made one
