unmannerly

adjective
rude; lacking proper manners
2.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmænɚli / (AmE), / ʌnˈmænəli / (BrE)
behaving in a rude or socially unacceptable way

Examples

An unmannerly person
His unmannerly comments offended many people at the dinner party.
It was unmannerly of him to interrupt the speaker so abruptly.
She was criticized for her unmannerly conduct during the formal meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: polite - showing good manners and respect for others
  • synonym: rude - impolite; not showing respect for others
