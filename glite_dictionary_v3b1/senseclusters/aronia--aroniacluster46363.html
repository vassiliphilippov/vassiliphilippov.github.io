aronia

noun
shrub with dark berries
Pronunciation: / əˈroʊniə / (AmE), / əˈrəʊniə / (BrE)
a type of shrub native to North America that produces small, dark berries, also known as chokeberry

Examples

Aronia shrub in garden
The aronia bushes in the garden are full of ripe berries.
Many people add aronia berries to smoothies for their health benefits.
Farmers have started growing aronia because the berries are rich in antioxidants.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: blueberry - a small, round, blue fruit from a different shrub
  • synonym: chokeberry - a berry from the aronia shrub
