basket case

noun
person overwhelmed or situation in ruins
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbæskɪt ˌkeɪs / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪt ˌkeɪs / (BrE)
a person who is unable to cope with life or a situation that is in a state of complete failure or disarray

Examples

A basket case person
After the tough week, she felt like a basket case.
He laughed at himself for being a total basket case.
The project was a basket case by the time we finished.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mess - a situation that is confused or full of problems
  • synonym: wreck - person or thing in a ruined state; often used similarly
