culturalist

adjective
focused on culture
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌl.tʃər.əl.ɪst/ /; / /ˈkʌl.tʃə.rəl.ɪst/ (AmE), /ˈkʌl.tʃə.rəl.ɪst/ /; / /ˈkʌl.tʃər.əl.ɪst/ (BrE)
relating to the belief that culture is the main factor in explaining behavior, events, or institutions.

Examples

Culturalist approach
The culturalist perspective highlights the importance of traditions in shaping social norms.
Her culturalist analysis of the novel focused on the influence of local customs.
Many educators adopt a culturalist approach to make lessons more relevant to students' backgrounds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cultural - relating to the ideas, customs, and social behavior of a society
  • antonym: structuralist - relating to the theory that elements of human culture must be understood in terms of their relationship to a larger structure
