spiderlike

adjective
resembling a spider
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪ.dɚˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈspaɪ.dəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a spider, especially in appearance, movement, or structure.

Examples

Spiderlike legs
She wore a costume with long, spiderlike appendages that creeped everyone out.
The insect's spiderlike legs helped it scuttle quickly across the floor.
The robot moved with a spiderlike grace as it navigated the narrow tunnels.
