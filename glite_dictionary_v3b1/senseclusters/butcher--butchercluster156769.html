butcher

noun
incompetent person who makes mistakes
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʊtʃə / (BrE)
a person who makes mistakes due to incompetence, especially in carrying out a task or job

Examples

An butcher at work
After seeing how the task was handled, I knew the project had been given to a butcher.
Everyone thought he was the right man for the job, but he turned out to be a real butcher.
The repairs were completely botched by some butcher the company hired.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bungler - person who habitually makes mistakes or messes things up
  • antonym: expert - person skilled or highly competent in a particular area
