earnings report

noun
company financial results statement
Pronunciation: / ˈɝː.nɪŋz rɪˈpɔrt / (AmE), / ˈɜː.nɪŋz rɪˈpɔːt / (BrE)
a financial statement issued by a company that shows its operating results over a specific period, typically quarterly or annually

Examples

Quarterly earnings report
After the disappointing earnings report, the stock price fell sharply.
Investors eagerly awaited the quarterly earnings report to assess the company's performance.
The company's earnings report showed a significant increase in profit compared to last year.
