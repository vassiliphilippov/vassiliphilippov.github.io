crampon

noun
device for lifting heavy objects
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkræm.pɑn / (AmE), / ˈkræm.pɒn / (BrE)
a hinged metal device with curved bars used for raising or lifting heavy objects

Examples

Metal crampon lifter
A sturdy pair of crampons is essential for moving such heavy machinery.
He attached the crampon carefully before attempting to hoist the crate.
The workers secured the beam using a crampon to lift it into position.
