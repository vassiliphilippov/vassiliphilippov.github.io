Synonyms

  • parallel
    4.4 3/m
    a parallel is a direct comparison that points out matching features, while analogy is a broader comparison used to explain or reason.
  • correspondence
    4.2 1/m
    correspondence is a close connection or similarity between items in structure or meaning, while analogy is a comparison used to explain by showing similarities.
  • similitude
    9.8 0.03/m
