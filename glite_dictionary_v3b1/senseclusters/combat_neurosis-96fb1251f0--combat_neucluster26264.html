Examples

Combat neurosis symptoms
During the war, little was understood about combat neurosis and its long-term psychological effects.
Many soldiers returning from battle exhibited signs of combat neurosis, struggling to readjust to civilian life.
The doctor diagnosed him with combat neurosis after witnessing his anxiety attacks and recurring nightmares.
