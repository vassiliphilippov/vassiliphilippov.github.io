decliner

noun
falling stock
7.5
Pronunciation: / dɪˈklaɪnər / (AmE), / dɪˈklaɪnə / (BrE)
a stock or other traded security that has decreased in price during a trading session

Examples

Stock decliner
The financial report listed each decliner and the percentage it dropped during the day.
Investors were concerned as the number of decliners outpaced gainers on the stock exchange.
