blackthorn liqueur

noun
alcoholic drink made from blackthorn fruit
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈblækˌθɔrn lɪˈkjʊr / (AmE), / ˈblækˌθɔːn lɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
a strong alcoholic drink made from blackthorn fruit (sloes), typically sweet and flavored
