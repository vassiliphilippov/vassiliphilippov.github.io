Para River

proper noun
estuary in northern Brazil
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːrə ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˌpɑːrə ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a large estuary in northern Brazil into which the Tocantins River flows, forming part of the Amazon River system

Examples

The Para River estuary
Many species of fish can be found in the waters of the Para River.
The Para River is a vital waterway for transportation in northern Brazil.
The city of Belém is located near the mouth of the Para River.
