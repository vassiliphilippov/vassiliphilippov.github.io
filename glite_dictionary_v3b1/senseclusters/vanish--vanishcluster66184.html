Synonyms

  • disappear into thin air
    4.4 0.16/m

  • vanish off the face of the earth
    8.1 0.02/m
    vanish off the face of the earth suggests someone disappears with no trace anywhere, while vanish means to disappear suddenly and often mysteriously.
  • disappear
    1.0 92/m
    disappear simply means to stop being seen or to cease to exist, while vanish implies a sudden, complete, often mysterious disappearance.
