elephant ear

noun
tropical plant with large heart-shaped leaves
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɛlɪfənt ɪr / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪfənt ɪə / (BrE)
a tropical plant of the genus Alocasia, known for its large, showy, heart-shaped leaves and boat-shaped spathe with reddish berries

Examples

Large elephant ear plant
After planting the elephant ear bulbs, she watered them thoroughly.
