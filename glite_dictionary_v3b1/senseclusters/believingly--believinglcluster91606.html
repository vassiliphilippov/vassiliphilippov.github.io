believingly

adverb
in a credulous or trusting way
Pronunciation: / bɪˈlivɪŋli / (AmE), / bɪˈliːvɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows trust or conviction; in a credulous manner.

Examples

Look believingly at
He gazed believingly at the sky, hoping his wish would come true.
She believingly accepted her friend's explanation, despite previous doubts.
The audience listened believingly to the storyteller's fantastic tales.

Related Terms

  • synonym: credulously - in a gullible or trusting way
