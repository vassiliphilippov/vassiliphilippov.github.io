butterfly flower

noun
tropical shrub with pink-purple flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌt̬ɚˌflaɪ ˈflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəˌflaɪ ˈflaʊə / (BrE)
A shrub or small tree native to Dutch Guiana, known for clusters of pink flowers streaked with purple.
