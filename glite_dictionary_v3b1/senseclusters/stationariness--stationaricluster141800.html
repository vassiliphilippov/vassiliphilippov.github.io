stationariness

noun
the state of being stationary
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪʃəˌneri.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsteɪʃənəri.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being stationary; remaining in one place without movement

Examples

The stationariness of objects
He admired the stationariness of the bird as it perched motionless on the branch.
Periods of stationariness in economic growth often lead to concerns among policymakers.
The stationariness of the statue made it a reliable landmark for travelers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immobility - the state of being immobile; lack of movement
  • antonym: motion - the act or process of moving; movement
