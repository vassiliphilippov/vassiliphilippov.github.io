weepy

adjective
inclined to cry or weep easily
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːpi / (AmE), / ˈwiːpi / (BrE)
tending to cry easily; feeling inclined to weep.

Examples

A weepy mood
After hearing the news, he felt surprisingly weepy all afternoon.
She always gets weepy during sad movies.
The end of the novel made even the toughest readers a little weepy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerful - noticeably happy and optimistic
  • synonym: tearful - full of tears; about to cry
