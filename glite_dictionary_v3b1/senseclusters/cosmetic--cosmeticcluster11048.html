cosmetic

adjective
improving appearance only; not functional
1.0
Pronunciation: / kɑzˈmɛtɪk / (AmE), / kɒzˈmɛtɪk / (BrE)
intended to improve appearance only, not function; for looks rather than practical effect

Examples

Cosmetic changes
He argued that the new policy was just a cosmetic measure and wouldn't solve the real problem.
The changes to the software were mostly cosmetic and didn't affect how it worked.
The company spent a fortune on cosmetic improvements to the building's facade.
He decided on a cosmetic procedure to reduce the appearance of scars.
Many people use cosmetic products to enhance their appearance.
The changes to the software were mainly cosmetic and did not impact its performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decorative - serving to make something look more attractive rather than being functional
  • antonym: functional - designed to be practical or useful rather than attractive
