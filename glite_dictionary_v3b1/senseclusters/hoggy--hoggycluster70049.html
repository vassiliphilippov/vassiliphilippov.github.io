hoggy

adjective
greedy or selfish
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑɡi / (AmE), / ˈhɒɡi / (BrE)
greedy or behaving in a way that resembles a hog, especially by taking more than one's share

Examples

Hoggy behavior at dinner
Don't be so hoggy with the pizza—let others have some too.
His hoggy behavior at the buffet annoyed the other guests.
She can get a bit hoggy when it comes to sharing her favorite snacks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generous - willing to give and share with others
  • synonym: greedy - wanting more than is needed or fair
  • synonym: selfish - caring only about oneself and not others
