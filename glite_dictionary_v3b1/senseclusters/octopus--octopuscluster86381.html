octopus

noun
octopus tentacles as food
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɑktəˌpʊs / (AmE), / ˈɒktəpəs / (BrE)
the tentacles of an octopus prepared and served as food, especially in Mediterranean or Asian cuisine

Examples
