powerless

adjective
without ability or influence; lacking power
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊɚləs / (AmE), / ˈpaʊələs / (BrE)
without the ability, influence, or power to do something

Examples

Feel powerless to act
After the storm, the residents felt powerless to restore electricity without help.
As a child, she often felt powerless to change her circumstances.
He stood by, powerless to prevent the argument from escalating.

Related Terms

  • synonym: helpless - unable to defend oneself or act without help
  • antonym: powerful - having great power or influence
