carpet shark

noun
shark with flattened, mottled body
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːr.pət ˌʃɑrk /; / ˈkɑːrpɪt ˌʃɑrk / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.pɪt ˌʃɑːk /; / ˈkɑːpɪt ˌʃɑːk / (BrE)
a type of bottom-dwelling, camouflaged shark found near the ocean floor, belonging to the order Orectolobiformes
