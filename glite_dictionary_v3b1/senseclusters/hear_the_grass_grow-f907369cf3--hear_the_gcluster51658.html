hear the grass grow

verb
be extremely quiet or still
2.4 8.7
Pronunciation: /hiːr ðə græs groʊ/ /; / /hɪər ðə græs groʊ/ (AmE), /hɪə ðə grɑːs grəʊ/ /; / /hɪə ðə græs grəʊ/ (BrE)
to be in an environment or situation so quiet and still that even the faintest sounds seem noticeable; to experience extreme silence or stillness.

Examples
