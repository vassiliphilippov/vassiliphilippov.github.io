cryonic

adjective
relating to cryonics or deep-freeze preservation
8.5
Pronunciation: / kraɪˈɑnɪk / (AmE), / kraɪˈɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to the preservation of bodies at very low temperatures, especially for possible future revival, as in cryonics.

Examples

Cryonic preservation
Advances in cryonic technology have sparked debates on the ethics of reanimation.
Some science fiction stories explore cryonic suspension as a way to travel into the distant future.
The patient was placed in a cryonic chamber immediately after legal death.
