primeval

adjective
from the earliest ages; ancient
6.5 8.8
Pronunciation: / praɪˈmiːvəl / (AmE), / praɪˈmiːvəl /; / prɪˈmiːvəl / (BrE)
(of something) belonging to the earliest ages; ancient or original.

Examples

The primeval forest

Scientists studied the primaeval oceans to learn about the origins of life.
The explorers trekked through a primaeval forest untouched by modern civilization.
