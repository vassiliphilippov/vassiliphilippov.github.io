Docklands

proper noun
redeveloped dock area
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑkˌlændz / (AmE), / ˈdɒkˌlændz / (BrE)
the name for a redeveloped urban area in some cities, originally used for docks and shipping, now often featuring offices, homes, and leisure facilities

Examples

The Docklands area
Many new businesses have opened in the Docklands since the area was redeveloped.
The Docklands offer a mix of modern apartments and historic warehouses along the river.
Tourists enjoy walking along the waterfront in the Docklands district.

Related Terms

  • synonym: waterfront - an area of a city next to a body of water, often redeveloped for public use
