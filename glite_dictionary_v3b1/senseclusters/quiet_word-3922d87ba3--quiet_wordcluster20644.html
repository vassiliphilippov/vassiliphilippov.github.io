quiet word

noun
private or discreet conversation
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkwaɪət wɝːd / (AmE), / ˈkwaɪət wɜːd / (BrE)
a brief, private, and discreet conversation, often about a sensitive matter or to give advice

Examples

Have a quiet word

Could I have a quiet word before the others arrive?
He pulled me aside for a quiet word about my recent performance at work.
