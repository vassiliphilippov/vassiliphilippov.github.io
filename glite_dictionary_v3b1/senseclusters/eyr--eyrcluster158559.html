eyr

noun
bird of prey nest
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪər / (AmE), / ɪə / (BrE)
an old or rare word for a nest, especially one belonging to a bird of prey

Examples

Eagle's eyr
The falcon guarded its eyr perched high above the valley.
Legends spoke of a golden egg hidden in the ancient eyr on the mountain.
From the eyr, the young eaglets could see the entire forest below.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aerie - a large nest of a bird of prey, usually built high in a tree or on a cliff
  • synonym: nest - a structure built by birds to lay eggs and raise their young
