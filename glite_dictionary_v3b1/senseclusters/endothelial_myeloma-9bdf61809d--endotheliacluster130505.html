endothelial myeloma

noun
type of bone marrow cancer
Pronunciation: / ˌɛn.doʊˈθi.li.əl maɪˈɛl.oʊ.mə / (AmE), / ˌɛn.dəʊˈθiː.li.əl maɪˈɛl.ə.mə / (BrE)
A malignant tumor of the bone marrow, usually found in the pelvis or long bones.

Examples
