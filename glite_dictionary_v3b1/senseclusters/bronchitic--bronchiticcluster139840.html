bronchitic

adjective
suffering from bronchitis
Pronunciation: / brɑnˈkɪtɪk / (AmE), / brɒŋˈkɪtɪk / (BrE)
Suffering from or prone to bronchitis.

Examples

Bronchitic cough
Children living in polluted cities are more likely to become bronchitic than those in rural areas.
During the winter, his bronchitic cough always seemed to get worse.
The doctor noted that she had a bronchitic condition that required attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bronchitis - inflammation of the bronchi in the lungs
