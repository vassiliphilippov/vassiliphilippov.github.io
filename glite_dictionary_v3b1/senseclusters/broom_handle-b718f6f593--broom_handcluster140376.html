broom handle

noun
the handle of a broom
Pronunciation: / bruːm ˈhændl̩ / (AmE), / bruːm ˈhændl / (BrE)
the long, usually cylindrical part used to hold and operate a broom.

Examples

A broom handle grip
He used a broom handle to reach something that had fallen behind the cabinet.
Please make sure the broom handle is screwed in tightly before using it.
She snapped the broom handle while trying to sweep up heavy debris.
