silky cornel

noun
North American shrub with purplish stems and blue fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlki ˈkɔr.nəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪlki ˈkɔː.nəl / (BrE)
a shrub native to eastern North America, characterized by purplish stems and blue fruit

Examples

The silky cornel shrub
Gardeners appreciate the silky cornel for its attractive blue fruit and purplish stems.
In autumn, the leaves of the silky cornel turn a striking shade of red.
The silky cornel is often found near wetlands in eastern North America.
