accusatory

adjective
expressing accusation or blame
4.3
Pronunciation: / əˈkjuː.səˌtɔr.i / (AmE), / əˈkjuː.səˌtɔː.ri / (BrE)
Expressing or implying accusation or blame.

Examples

Accusatory glance
She shot him an accusatory glance when she saw the mess in the kitchen.
The lawyer's accusatory tone made the witness uncomfortable.
There was an accusatory note in her voice as she questioned his whereabouts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accusing - suggesting blame; expressing accusation
  • antonym: defensive - intended to defend or protect; not accusing
