co-design

verb
to design jointly
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊdɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / ˌkəʊdɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
to design something together with one or more other people, sharing responsibility and ideas

Examples

To co-design a project
The students will co-design the new playground with their teachers and parents.
Engineers and artists co-designed the innovative exhibition space for the museum.
We are looking for volunteers to help co-design the community garden project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collaborate - to work together with others on a project
  • degree lower: design - to create or plan something
