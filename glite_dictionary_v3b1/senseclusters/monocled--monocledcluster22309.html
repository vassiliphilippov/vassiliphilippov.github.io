monocled

adjective
wearing a monocle
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑː.nə.kəld / (AmE), / ˈmɒn.ə.kəld / (BrE)
wearing a monocle (a single round eyeglass for one eye)

Examples

A monocled gentleman
A monocled gentleman greeted us at the entrance, exuding old-fashioned charm.
She dressed as a monocled detective for the costume party, complete with a top hat and cane.
The villain in the film was portrayed as a sinister, monocled aristocrat.
