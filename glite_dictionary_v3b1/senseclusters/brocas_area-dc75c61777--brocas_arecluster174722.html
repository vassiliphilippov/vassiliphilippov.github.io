Broca's area

noun
region of brain involved in speech production
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbroʊkəz ˌɛriə / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊkəz ˌeəriə / (BrE)
a region of the brain in the left frontal lobe responsible for the motor aspects of speech production

Examples

Broca's area in brain
Broca's area is typically found in the left hemisphere of the brain.
Damage to Broca's area can result in speech difficulties known as Broca's aphasia.
Scientists studied the activity of Broca's area during different language tasks.
