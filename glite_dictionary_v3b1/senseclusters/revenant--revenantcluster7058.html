revenant

noun
someone who returns from the dead
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛvənənt /; / ˈrɛvəˌnænt / (AmE), / ˈrɛvənənt / (BrE)
a person or spirit who returns from the dead or after a long absence, especially in stories or folklore

Examples

A revenant ghost
In the old tale, a revenant haunted the village, seeking justice for past wrongs.
Many cultures have legends of a revenant returning to visit the living.
The film features a chilling scene where the revenant appears at the stroke of midnight.
In folklore, a revenant is often depicted as a restless spirit seeking closure.
She was welcomed like a revenant returning from a distant exile.
The revenant surprised everyone when he appeared at his childhood home after many years away.
