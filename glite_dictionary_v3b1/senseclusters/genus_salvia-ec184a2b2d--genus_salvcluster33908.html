genus Salvia

noun
plant genus; includes sage
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈsæl.vi.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈsæl.vi.ə / (BrE)
A large genus of shrubs and subshrubs in the mint family, including sage, widely grown for their aromatic leaves and flowers.
