TX

noun
cryptocurrency transaction
8.1
Pronunciation: /tiː eks/ /; / /træn.ˈzæk.ʃən/ (AmE), /tiː eks/ /; / /træn.ˈzæk.ʃən/ (BrE)
(in cryptocurrency and blockchain contexts) an abbreviation for a transaction — an on‑chain transfer of cryptocurrency or tokens recorded on a blockchain.

Examples

Crypto TX
I received a TX from the exchange and it shows as completed on the network.
The block explorer shows the TX confirming and adding coins to my wallet balance.
Developers warned that unconfirmed TXs can cause delays for users sending small amounts.
