tall buttercup

noun
a tall yellow-flowered buttercup plant
Pronunciation: / tɔl ˈbʌt̬ɚˌkʌp / (AmE), / tɔːl ˈbʌtəkʌp / (BrE)
a perennial European buttercup (Ranunculus acris) with yellow flowers, widely naturalized in North America

Examples

Wild tall buttercup flowers
As a child, she liked to pick wild tall buttercup flowers from the meadow.
Gardeners often remove tall buttercup because it can spread rapidly and outcompete native species.
The field was filled with blooming tall buttercup plants in early spring.
