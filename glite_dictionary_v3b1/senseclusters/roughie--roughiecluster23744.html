roughie

noun
violent person
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌfi / (AmE), / ˈrʌfi / (BrE)
a violent or aggressive person, especially one who intimidates or bullies others

Examples

A violent roughie
The shopkeeper was afraid of the local roughie who often threatened customers.
After dark, a group of roughies gathered on the corner, making people nervous.
No one wanted to confront the roughie who caused trouble at every event.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gentleman - a polite and well-mannered man
  • synonym: ruffian - a violent, lawless person
  • synonym: thug - a violent criminal or bully
