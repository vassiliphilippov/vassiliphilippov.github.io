bob

noun
long racing sled with steering
Pronunciation: / bɑb / (AmE), / bɒb / (BrE)
a long racing sled for two or more people, equipped with a steering mechanism, used in the sport of bobsledding.

Examples

Racing bob
After hours of practice, they learned how to steer the bob down sharp curves.
