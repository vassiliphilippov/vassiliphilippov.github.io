epistolary

adjective
written as a series of letters
Pronunciation: / ɪˈpɪstəˌlɛri / (AmE), / ɪˈpɪstələrɪ / (BrE)
(of a literary work) written in the form of letters or correspondence.

Examples

An epistolary novel
An epistolary friendship developed between the two scholars after years of correspondence.
Her collection of epistolary essays offers a unique perspective on historical events.
The novel is written in an epistolary style, with each chapter as a letter.
