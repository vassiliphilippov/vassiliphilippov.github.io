sporadically

adverb
irregularly; occasionally
4.8
Pronunciation: / spəˈrædɪkli / (AmE), / spəˈrædɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that happens at irregular intervals or only occasionally; not regularly or continuously.

Examples

Checks sporadically
He checks his email sporadically, so responses may be delayed.
Rain has fallen sporadically over the past few weeks, disrupting the drought.
The meetings are held sporadically throughout the year, depending on demand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: occasionally - at infrequent or irregular intervals
  • antonym: regularly - at uniform or predictable intervals, the opposite of sporadically
