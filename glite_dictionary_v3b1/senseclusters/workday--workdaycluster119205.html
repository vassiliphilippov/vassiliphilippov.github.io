workday

noun
a day when work is done

A day on which work is usually done, especially a day that is not a weekend or holiday.

Examples

A busy workday
He prefers to leave early to avoid traffic on his workday.
Many people find it hard to exercise after a long workday.
My typical workday starts at 8 a.m. and ends at 5 p.m.

Synonyms

  • working day
    1.2 0.7/m
    working day is a formal term for a day when people normally work, while workday names the individual day someone spends working.
  • weekday
    1.0 6/m
    weekday is a calendar day, usually Monday to Friday, while workday is a day when people actually go to work.

Related Terms
