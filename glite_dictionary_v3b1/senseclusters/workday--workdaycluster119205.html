workday

noun
a day when work is done
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːkˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːk.deɪ / (BrE)
A day on which work is usually done, especially a day that is not a weekend or holiday.

Examples

A busy workday
He prefers to leave early to avoid traffic on his workday.
Many people find it hard to exercise after a long workday.
My typical workday starts at 8 a.m. and ends at 5 p.m.

Related Terms

  • synonym: weekday - a day of the week except Saturday or Sunday
  • antonym: weekend - Saturday and Sunday, when most people do not work
