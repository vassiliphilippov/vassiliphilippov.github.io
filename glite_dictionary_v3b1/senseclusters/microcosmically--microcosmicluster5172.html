microcosmically

adverb
on a tiny scale
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkrəˈkɑːzəmɪkli / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəˈkɒzəmɪkli / (BrE)
in a very small or minute way; on a tiny scale, without reference to a larger whole

Examples

To study microcosmically
The scientist analyzed the sample microcosmically to understand its smallest features.
When viewed microcosmically, the differences between the two materials became clear.
The artist painted the scene microcosmically, focusing on the tiniest details of each object.

Related Terms

  • antonym: macroscopically - on a large or broad scale
  • synonym: minutely - in a very detailed or small-scale way
