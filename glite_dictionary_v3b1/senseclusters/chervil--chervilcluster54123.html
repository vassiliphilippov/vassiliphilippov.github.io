chervil

noun
a culinary herb with finely divided leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɜr.vəl /; / ˈtʃɝːvəl / (AmE), / ˈtʃɜː.vɪl /; / ˈtʃɜːvɪl / (BrE)
an aromatic herb with finely divided leaves, used in cooking to add a delicate flavor to dishes such as salads, soups, and eggs
