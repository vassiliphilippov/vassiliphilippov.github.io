misarticulate

verb
to pronounce incorrectly
9.3
Pronunciation: /mɪs.ɑːr.ˈtɪk.jə.lət/ (AmE), /mɪs.ɑː.ˈtɪk.jə.lət/ (BrE)
to pronounce a word or sound incorrectly or unclearly

Examples

To misarticulate a word
Young children often misarticulate certain sounds when they are first learning to speak.
The speech therapist helped the student who tended to misarticulate the letter R.
If you misarticulate words during a presentation, your message may be misunderstood.
Young children often misarticulate certain consonants when they are first learning to speak.
The speech therapist helped the student who would sometimes misarticulate difficult words.
If you misarticulate a word, people might not understand what you mean.
