A wide road specially built for fast traffic, with two or more lanes in each direction and limited access points.

Examples

Synonyms

  • freeway
    2.2 12/m
    freeway is built without intersections or traffic lights and carries continuous fast traffic, while motorway has limited access and multiple lanes.
  • expressway
    2.8 2/m
    expressway is designed for fast travel with limited crossings and higher speeds, while motorway has strict access control and multiple lanes.
  • superhighway
    3.5 0.4/m
    superhighway is an especially wide, well-built road for very fast travel and many lanes, while motorway usually refers to a major limited access road.
  • thruway
    4.1 0.3/m
    thruway is often used as a road name for high-speed limited access routes, while motorway is the general term for that highway type.
  • throughway
    4.1 0.07/m
    throughway is a less common name for a broad highway that allows uninterrupted travel, while motorway is the usual term for such limited access roads.

Related Terms

  • synonym: highway - a main road, especially in the US, for travel by the public
