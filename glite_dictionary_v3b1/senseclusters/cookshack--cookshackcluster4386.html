cookshack

noun
outdoor cooking shed
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊkˌʃæk / (AmE), / ˈkʊkˌʃæk / (BrE)
a small building or shed used for cooking, especially outdoors or at a work site

Examples

Ranch cookshack
The ranch hands gathered in the cookshack every morning for a hearty breakfast.
During the summer camp, meals were prepared in a rustic cookshack near the lake.
Smoke drifted from the old cookshack as lunch was being made for the workers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cabin - a small, simple house or shelter
  • synonym: kitchen - a room or area where food is prepared and cooked
