nationally

adverb
in a nationwide manner
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnæʃənəli / (AmE), / ˈnæʃnəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or affects the whole country (not just a local area)

Examples

Expand nationally
The company plans to expand nationally over the next five years.
The festival is celebrated nationally and attracts visitors from all over the country.
The policy was implemented nationally to ensure consistency across all regions.
That company is nationally known for its customer service.
The event was nationally televised, attracting viewers from every state.
This disease has become a nationally recognized health concern.

Related Terms

  • antonym: locally - in a way that relates to a particular area or neighborhood
  • antonym: regionally - on a regional, not national, scale
