semitropic

adjective
relating to subtropical climate or regions
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈtrɑpɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈtrɒpɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of regions just outside the tropics, especially resembling subtropical climate or conditions.

Examples

Semitropic climate regions
Many plants found in this area thrive in a semitropic environment.
The region experiences semitropic weather, with mild winters and hot, humid summers.
They moved to a semitropic climate to escape harsh northern winters.
