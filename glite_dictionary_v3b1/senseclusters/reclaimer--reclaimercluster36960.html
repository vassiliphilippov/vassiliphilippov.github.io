reclaimer

noun
person or machine recovering
5.3
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkleɪmər / (AmE), / rɪˈkleɪmə / (BrE)
a person or machine that recovers or retrieves materials or resources for reuse

Examples

Industrial reclaimer
The reclaimer at the recycling plant sorts metals from other materials automatically.
As a skilled reclaimer, she finds new uses for discarded furniture.
Modern factories use a reclaimer to recover valuable resources from industrial waste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recycler - a person or device that processes materials to be reused
  • synonym: scavenger - a person who searches for and collects usable items from waste
  • antonym: waster - a person or thing that uses resources carelessly or inefficiently
