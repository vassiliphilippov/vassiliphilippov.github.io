unworn

adjective
not used or damaged
3.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɔrn / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɔːn / (BrE)
not used, worn, or showing any signs of use; still in original condition

Examples

Completely unworn shoes
The shoes looked completely unworn, as if they had just come out of the box.
Despite being decades old, the book's cover remained unworn and free of creases.
The path through the forest was almost unworn, with grass growing tall across it.
The shoes were completely unworn and still in their original box.
He donated several unworn shirts that he never had a chance to use.
The unworn jacket was given as a gift but did not fit her.

Related Terms

  • synonym: new - not previously used or owned
  • synonym: pristine - in perfect, original condition; unspoiled
  • antonym: used - having been owned or worn before
  • antonym: worn - damaged or changed by continuous use
