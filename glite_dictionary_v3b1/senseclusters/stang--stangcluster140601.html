stang

verb
to secure with a bar
9.9
to fasten or secure a door, gate, or shutter with a bar or pole.

Examples

To stang a door
They stang the barn doors with a heavy pole to keep out the wind.
Before leaving, she stanged the gate to make sure no animals could get in.
During storms, villagers would stang their shutters for extra protection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bar - to fasten with a bar or rod
  • antonym: unfasten - to open or remove a fastening
