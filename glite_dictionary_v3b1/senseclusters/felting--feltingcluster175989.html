felting

noun
matting fibers process
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛltɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɛltɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of matting and compressing fibers, especially wool, to create a dense fabric

Examples

Wool felting process
Traditional felting techniques are used to make warm hats and slippers from wool.
She learned felting in an art class and now creates colorful wall hangings.
The process of felting can turn loose fibers into a strong, durable fabric.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fulling - a process of cleaning and thickening woolen cloth
  • antonym: weaving - the process of making fabric by interlacing threads
