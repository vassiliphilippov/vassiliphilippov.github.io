enjoyably

adverb
in an enjoyable manner
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʒɔɪəbli / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒɔɪəbli / (BrE)
In a way that gives pleasure or enjoyment.

Examples

To enjoyably spend time
She spent her weekend enjoyably reading novels and listening to music.
The children played enjoyably in the garden all afternoon.
We completed the project enjoyably as a team, making the work feel less like a chore.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pleasantly - in a pleasant or pleasing manner
  • antonym: unenjoyably - in a way that is not enjoyable
