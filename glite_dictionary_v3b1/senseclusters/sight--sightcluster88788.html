sight

verb
aim using the sights of a gun
Pronunciation: / saɪt / (AmE), / saɪt / (BrE)
to take aim by looking through the sights of a gun or other device

Examples

To sight a target
He sighted the target carefully before pulling the trigger.
She sights the deer through her rifle's scope.
The sniper sighted down the barrel, waiting for the perfect moment.
