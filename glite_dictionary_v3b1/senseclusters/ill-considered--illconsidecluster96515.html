ill-considered

adjective
lacking careful consideration
4.1
Pronunciation: / ɪl kənˈsɪdɚd / (AmE), / ɪl kənˈsɪdəd / (BrE)
Not carefully thought out or planned; lacking due consideration or reflection.

Examples

Ill-considered remarks
His ill-considered remarks offended many people at the meeting.
Rushing into marriage proved to be an ill-considered decision for them both.
The company made an ill-considered investment that resulted in significant losses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ill-judged - not showing good judgment; lacking thought or care
  • synonym: rash - acting or done without careful consideration of the possible consequences
  • antonym: well-considered - carefully thought out; showing careful consideration
