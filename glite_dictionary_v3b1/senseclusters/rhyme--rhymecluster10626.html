rhyme

noun
a short poem or verse with rhyming lines
Pronunciation: / raɪm / (AmE), / raɪm / (BrE)
A short poem or verse, especially one in which the sounds at the ends of lines correspond with each other.

Examples

A nursery rhyme
Children often learn simple rhymes to help with language development.
She wrote a clever rhyme for her friend's birthday card.
The poet composed a rhyme about the changing seasons.

  • synonym: verse - a line of poetry, or poetry as a genre
