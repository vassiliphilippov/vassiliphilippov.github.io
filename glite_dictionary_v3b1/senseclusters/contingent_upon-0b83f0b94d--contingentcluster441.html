contingent upon

adjective
dependent on conditions
Pronunciation: / kənˈtɪndʒənt əˈpɒn / (AmE), / kənˈtɪndʒənt əˈpɒn / (BrE)
dependent on certain conditions or circumstances; determined by what may happen.

Examples

Be contingent upon conditions
Access to funding is contingent upon meeting certain performance targets.
Her acceptance was contingent upon the approval of the committee.
The job offer is contingent upon the successful completion of a background check.
