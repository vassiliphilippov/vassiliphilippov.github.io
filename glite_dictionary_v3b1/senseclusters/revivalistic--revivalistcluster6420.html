revivalistic

adjective
relating to revivalism
Pronunciation: / rɪˌvaɪ.vəˈlɪs.tɪk / (AmE), / rɪˌvaɪ.vəˈlɪs.tɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of revivalism, especially in religion or cultural movements.

Examples

Revivalistic religious movement
Her revivalistic preaching style attracted large crowds seeking spiritual renewal.
Some art movements take a revivalistic approach by bringing back historical styles.
The music had a revivalistic energy, reminiscent of earlier gospel traditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: revivalist - a person who promotes or is associated with revivalism
