phonetic transcription

noun
systematic representation of speech sounds
5.0 5.7
Pronunciation: /fə.ˈne.tɪk træn.ˈskrɪp.ʃən/ (AmE), /fə.ˈne.tɪk træn.ˈskrɪp.ʃən/ (BrE)
a written representation of speech sounds, using symbols to show each distinct sound in a word or phrase

Examples

A detailed phonetic transcription
A detailed phonetic transcription can reveal subtle differences between speakers’ accents.
Linguists use phonetic transcription to accurately record spoken languages.
Most dictionaries include a phonetic transcription of each word to help with pronunciation.
