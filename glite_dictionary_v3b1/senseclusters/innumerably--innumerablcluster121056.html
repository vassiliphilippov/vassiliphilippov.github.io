innumerably

adverb
countless times
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnuːmərəbli / (AmE), / ɪˈnjuːmərəbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be counted; in countless numbers or amounts

Examples

Stars innumerably many
The night sky was innumerably filled with stars, stretching as far as the eye could see.
She had innumerably more questions than answers after reading the complex article.
The city lights sparkled innumerably along the river, creating a breathtaking view.

Related Terms

  • synonym: countlessly - in a way that cannot be counted
  • synonym: endlessly - in a way that seems to have no end
  • antonym: finitely - in a way that can be counted or limited
