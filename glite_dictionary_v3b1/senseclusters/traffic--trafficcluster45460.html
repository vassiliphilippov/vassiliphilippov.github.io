traffic

verb
trade, often illegal
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtræfɪk / (AmE), / ˈtræfɪk / (BrE)
to buy, sell, or deal in goods illegally or in something forbidden

Examples

To traffic drugs
He was arrested for attempting to traffic narcotics into the country.
Some criminal organizations traffic in endangered animal species.
The group used small boats to traffic illegal goods across the border.
He was arrested for conspiring to traffic large quantities of narcotics.
International laws were established to combat those who traffic in human beings.
The organization was caught attempting to traffic endangered animal parts across borders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: smuggle - to move goods illegally into or out of a country
  • synonym: trade - to buy and sell goods and services
