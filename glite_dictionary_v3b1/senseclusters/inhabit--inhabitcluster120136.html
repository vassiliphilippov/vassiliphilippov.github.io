Examples

To inhabit a city

Polar bears inhabit the icy regions of the Arctic.

Synonyms

  • reside
    3.0 11/m
    reside is a formal word for living in a home long-term, while inhabit is more neutral and used for people or animals.
  • live
    1.0 331/m
    live is the common everyday word for having a home in a place, while inhabit sounds slightly more formal and written.
  • dwell
    3.2 2.78/m
    dwell sounds old-fashioned or literary for living somewhere, while inhabit is more neutral and used in facts or reports.
  • occupy
    3.0 8.26/m
    occupy can mean taking up space or using a building, while inhabit specifically means living there as a resident.
  • settle
    2.5 29/m
    settle often means move to a place and make a home, while inhabit focuses on living there rather than the move.

Related Terms

  • antonym: desert - to leave a place so that it is no longer inhabited
  • synonym: occupy - to reside in or take possession of a place
