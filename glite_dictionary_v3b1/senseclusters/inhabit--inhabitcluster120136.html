inhabit

verb
to live in or occupy a place
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈhæbɪt / (AmE), / ɪnˈhabɪt / (BrE)
to live in or occupy a place or environment, especially as a permanent resident

Examples

To inhabit a city
In the past, nomadic tribes inhabited these deserts.
Many rare species inhabit the rainforests of South America.
Polar bears inhabit the icy regions of the Arctic.

Related Terms

  • antonym: desert - to leave a place so that it is no longer inhabited
  • synonym: occupy - to reside in or take possession of a place
