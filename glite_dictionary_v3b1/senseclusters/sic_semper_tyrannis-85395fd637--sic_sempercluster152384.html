Sic semper tyrannis

x
against tyranny
Pronunciation: / sɪk ˈsɛmpər tɪˈrænɪs / (AmE), / sɪk ˈsɛmpə tɪˈranɪs / (BrE)
a Latin phrase meaning 'thus always to tyrants', used to express opposition to tyranny or oppressive rulers

Examples

He whispered Sic semper tyrannis as a symbol of resistance against the dictator.
The phrase Sic semper tyrannis is often used to show defiance toward oppressive leaders.
