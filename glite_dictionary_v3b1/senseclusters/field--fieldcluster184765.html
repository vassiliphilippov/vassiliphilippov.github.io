field

noun
location for practical work or research
Pronunciation: / fiːld / (AmE), / fiːld / (BrE)
an area outside a studio, office, or laboratory where practical work is carried out or data is collected, especially by researchers or professionals

Examples

Work in the field

Journalists often travel into the field to gather firsthand information for their stories.
The biologist spends months each year working in the field observing animal behavior.
