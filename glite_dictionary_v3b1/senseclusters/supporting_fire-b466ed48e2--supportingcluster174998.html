supporting fire

noun
gunfire provided by supporting units in combat
Pronunciation: / səˈpɔrtɪŋ faɪɚ / (AmE), / səˈpɔːtɪŋ faɪə / (BrE)
gunfire provided by supporting units to protect or assist another military unit during combat operations.

Examples

Supporting fire from tanks
The infantry advanced while the tanks provided supporting fire from the ridge.
They requested supporting fire to suppress the enemy positions ahead.
Without adequate supporting fire, the assault team was forced to retreat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: covering fire - gunfire intended to protect troops by keeping enemy forces under fire
