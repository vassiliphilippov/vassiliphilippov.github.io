refereeing

noun
officiating as a referee
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛfəˈriɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌrɛfəˈriːɪŋ / (BrE)
The act or job of acting as a referee, especially in a sports game or competition.

Examples

Refereeing a match
He has years of experience in refereeing high school soccer matches.
Her interest in refereeing grew after taking a course on sports officiating.
The standard of refereeing was questioned after several controversial calls.
