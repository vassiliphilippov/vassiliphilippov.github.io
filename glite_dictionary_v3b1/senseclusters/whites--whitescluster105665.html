whites

noun
egg white portion
1.0
Pronunciation: /waɪts/ /; / /hwaɪts/ (AmE), /waɪts/ /; / /hwaɪts/ (BrE)
the clear, protein-rich part of eggs, especially when separated from the yolks for cooking or baking

Examples

Egg whites
For the recipe, you need to beat the whites until they are light and fluffy.
She carefully separated the yolks from the whites to make a meringue.
Many people use only the whites of eggs to reduce fat in their diet.
