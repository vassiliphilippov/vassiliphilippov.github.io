roseate spoonbill

noun
a pink wading bird with a spoon-shaped bill
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊziɪt ˈspunˌbɪl / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzɪət ˈspuːnbɪl / (BrE)
a large North and South American wading bird with a pink body and a distinctive spoon-shaped bill

Examples

The roseate spoonbill bird
The vivid pink feathers of the roseate spoonbill make it easy to identify among other wading birds.
We spotted a roseate spoonbill standing in the shallow water, searching for food.
Wildlife photographers travel to Florida to capture images of the rare roseate spoonbill.
