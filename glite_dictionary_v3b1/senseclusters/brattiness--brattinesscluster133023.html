brattiness

noun
unruly, impolite behavior (like a brat)
4.3 7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbræti.nəs / (AmE), / ˈbræti.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of behaving in an unruly, impolite, or spoiled manner, like a brat.
