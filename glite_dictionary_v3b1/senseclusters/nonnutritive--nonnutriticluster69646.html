nonnutritive

adjective
lacking nutrition
7.5
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈnuːtrɪtɪv / (AmE), / nɒnˈnjuːtrɪtɪv / (BrE)
not providing nutrition or nourishment

Examples

Nonnutritive sweeteners
Many artificial sweeteners are considered nonnutritive because they do not provide calories.
The doctor recommended a nonnutritive supplement for patients who need to avoid extra calories.
Some chewing gums are labeled as nonnutritive since they do not add nutritional value.
