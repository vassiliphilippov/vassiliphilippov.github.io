fiction

noun
an invented or false statement
1.9 1.7
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪk.ʃən/ (AmE), /ˈfɪk.ʃən/ (BrE)
something that is invented or imagined and not true; a false or made-up story or statement.

Examples

A fiction story
She accused her brother of spreading fiction about her to their parents.
The alibi he gave turned out to be complete fiction.
The politician’s explanation was dismissed as pure fiction by the press.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lie - a false statement made with deliberate intent to deceive
  • antonym: truth - a true or actual account, as opposed to something invented
