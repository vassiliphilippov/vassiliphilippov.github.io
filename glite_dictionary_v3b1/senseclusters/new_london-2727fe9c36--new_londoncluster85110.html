New London

proper noun
town in southeastern Connecticut, USA
Pronunciation: / nuː ˈlʌn.dən / (AmE), / njuː ˈlʌn.dən / (BrE)
A town in southeastern Connecticut, USA, near Long Island Sound, historically known as a major whaling center in the 19th century.
