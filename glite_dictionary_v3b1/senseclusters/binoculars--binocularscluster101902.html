binoculars

noun
optical device for seeing distant objects
2.9 2.4
Pronunciation: / bɪˈnɑːkjələrz / (AmE), / bɪˈnɒkjʊləz / (BrE)
An optical instrument with two lenses, designed to be used with both eyes to view distant objects clearly.

Examples

A pair of binoculars
From the balcony, we used binoculars to watch the ships far out at sea.
She raised her binoculars to get a better view of the mountains.
The birdwatcher always carries binoculars during his walks in the forest.
