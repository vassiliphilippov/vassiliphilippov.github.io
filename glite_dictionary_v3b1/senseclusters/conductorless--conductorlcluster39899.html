conductorless

adjective
without a conductor
Pronunciation: / kənˈdʌktərləs / (AmE), / kənˈdʌktələs / (BrE)
without a conductor or leader, especially in reference to an orchestra or vehicle

Examples

Conductorless orchestra
The orchestra gave a remarkable conductorless performance, relying on close communication among the musicians.
Some modern trains are now conductorless, using automated systems instead of human operators.
A conductorless choir requires every member to pay extra attention to timing and cues.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conducted - directed or led by a conductor
  • synonym: unled - not guided or directed by a leader
