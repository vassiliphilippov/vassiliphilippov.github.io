gem

noun
precious or semiprecious stone in jewelry
3.0
Pronunciation: / dʒɛm / (AmE), / dʒɛm / (BrE)
a precious or semi-precious stone that is cut and polished for use in jewelry or decoration

Examples

A sparkling gem
Rubies and sapphires are popular gems used in fine jewelry.
She wore a ring set with a sparkling gem on her finger.
The museum has a collection of ancient gems cut into intricate shapes.
After finding a colorful gem on the beach, they had it polished and set in a pendant.
She wore a necklace set with a brilliant gem that sparkled in the sunlight.
The museum had an impressive display of rare gems from around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jewel - a precious stone, typically one that has been cut and polished
  • synonym: precious stone - a valuable stone used in jewelry, such as a diamond, ruby, or emerald
