Synonyms

  • gemstone
    3.3 0.87/m
    a gemstone is a formal name for a cut, polished precious or semi-precious stone, while a gem is the common everyday word.
  • jewel
    1.8 6.95/m
    a jewel is often seen as a finished ornament or a precious stone, while a gem usually means the stone itself.
  • precious stone
    3.4 0.52/m
    a precious stone refers to a rare, very valuable gemstone used in fine jewelry, while a gem can mean any precious or semi-precious stone.
  • stone
