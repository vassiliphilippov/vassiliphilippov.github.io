blotting paper

noun
paper used to absorb ink
Pronunciation: / ˈblɑt̬ɪŋ ˌpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈblɒtɪŋ ˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
Absorbent paper used to soak up excess ink from writing or printing.

Examples

Blotting paper sheet
He dabbed the wet signature with blotting paper to prevent it from smudging.
Keep a sheet of blotting paper handy when writing with a fountain pen.
The artist used blotting paper to create a unique texture on the painting.
