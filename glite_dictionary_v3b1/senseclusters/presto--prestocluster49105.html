presto

adverb
suddenly; by magic
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛs.toʊ / (AmE), / ˈprɛs.təʊ / (BrE)
used to indicate that something happens suddenly or instantly, especially in a magical or dramatic way.

Examples

And presto change
She pressed the button and, presto, the lights came back on.
The magician waved his wand and, presto, the rabbit appeared out of the hat.
You add the water, stir for ten seconds, and presto—your meal is ready!

Related Terms

  • antonym: gradually - slowly or in small steps over time
  • synonym: suddenly - quickly and unexpectedly
