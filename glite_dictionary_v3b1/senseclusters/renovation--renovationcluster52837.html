renovation

noun
restoration to former good condition
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛnəˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛnəˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of repairing, updating, or restoring a building or object to make it better or like new

Examples

Home renovation
After a major renovation, the library now looks as good as new.
Many businesses remain open during renovation to minimize losses.
The old mansion was closed for renovation after many years of neglect.
After months of renovation, the historic theater finally reopened to the public.
The museum closed for extensive renovation to modernize its galleries.
They decided a complete renovation of the kitchen was necessary before moving in.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decay - the process of declining or falling into disrepair
  • synonym: repair - to restore something broken or damaged to good condition
  • synonym: restoration - the act of bringing something back to its original condition
