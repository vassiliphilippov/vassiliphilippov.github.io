replicant

noun
exact copy
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛplɪkənt / (AmE), / ˈrɛplɪkənt / (BrE)
an exact copy or duplicate of something, especially a reproduced version of an original object or being

Examples

A perfect replicant
The scientist created a replicant of the rare flower for further study.
In the science fiction film, a replicant was almost indistinguishable from a real human.
The artist was impressed by the detailed replicant of his famous sculpture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: original - the first or earliest form of something, not a copy
  • synonym: replica - an exact copy or model of something, especially on a smaller scale
