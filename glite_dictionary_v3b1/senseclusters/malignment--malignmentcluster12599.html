malignment

noun
slanderous defamation
5.5
Pronunciation: / məˈlaɪnmənt / (AmE), / məˈlaɪnmənt / (BrE)
The act of making false or defamatory statements about someone; slanderous defamation.

Examples

Continuous malignment
Continuous malignment of public figures can have lasting consequences for their personal lives.
She considered legal action to stop the malignment of her character in the tabloid press.
The author's reputation suffered greatly due to the malignment spread by his critics.
