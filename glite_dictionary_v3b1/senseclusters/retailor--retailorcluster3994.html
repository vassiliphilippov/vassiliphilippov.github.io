retailor

noun
seller to consumers
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈriːteɪlər / (AmE), / ˈriːteɪlə / (BrE)
a person or business that sells goods directly to consumers; a retailer

Examples

A retailor shop
The retailor offered discounts to attract more customers during the holiday season.
As a small retailor, she focused on providing excellent customer service.
Many shoppers prefer to buy directly from a local retailor rather than online.

Related Terms

  • antonym: wholesaler - a person or business selling goods in large quantities to retailers
