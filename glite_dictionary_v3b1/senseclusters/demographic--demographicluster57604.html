demographic

noun
a population group defined by shared traits
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛməˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌdɛməˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
a particular group of people within a population, defined by specific characteristics such as age, gender, or income

Examples

Young demographic
Marketers often analyze the demographic most likely to buy luxury goods.
Our city’s aging demographic is leading to increased demand for healthcare services.
The company is targeting the 18-to-34-year-old demographic with its new product line.

Related Terms

  • synonym: population - all the inhabitants of a particular area or group
