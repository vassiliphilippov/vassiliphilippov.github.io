suppressible

adjective
able to be controlled
Pronunciation: / səˈprɛsəbəl / (AmE), / səˈprɛsɪbəl / (BrE)
able to be stopped, controlled, or prevented from continuing or developing

Examples

Suppressible symptoms
The allergic reaction is suppressible with the right medication.
Some negative thoughts are suppressible through mindfulness techniques.
The spread of the virus proved to be suppressible with strict public health measures.

  • antonym: unstoppable - impossible to prevent or control
