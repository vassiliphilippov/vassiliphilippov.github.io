choo

interjection
train sound exclamation
Pronunciation: / tʃuː / (AmE), / tʃuː / (BrE)
an exclamation imitating the sound of a train, often used by children when playing or calling attention to a train.

Examples

Choo like a train
The children ran around the playground shouting choo as they pretended to be trains.
Whenever the train passed by, he would wave and yell choo excitedly.
She made a loud choo noise to get her little brother's attention during their game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: choo-choo - a child's word for a train, imitating its sound
