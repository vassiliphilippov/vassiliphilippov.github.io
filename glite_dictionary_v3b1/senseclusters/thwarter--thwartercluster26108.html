thwarter

noun
person who obstructs others' efforts
Pronunciation: / ˈθwɔrtər / (AmE), / ˈθwɔːtə / (BrE)
a person who systematically obstructs or prevents the efforts or actions of others

Examples

A thwarter of plans
Despite the team's determination, a single thwarter managed to delay the project for weeks.
He gained a reputation at work as the perennial thwarter of new initiatives.
Whenever progress was made, a thwarter appeared to put up new obstacles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obstructor - a person who obstructs or hinders something
  • antonym: supporter - a person who helps or encourages something
