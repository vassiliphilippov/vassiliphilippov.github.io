mansion house

noun
large, impressive residence
Pronunciation: / ˈmænʃən ˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈmænʃən ˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A large, impressive, and often stately house, typically serving as the residence of a wealthy person or family.
