descendent

noun
a person descended from an ancestor
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɛndənt / (AmE), / dɪˈsɛndənt / (BrE)
a person who is descended from a particular ancestor

Examples

A descendent of kings
Many Native Americans are proud descendents of ancient tribes.
She is a direct descendent of the first settlers in the region.
The antique was passed down from one descendent to another.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ancestor - a person from whom one is descended
  • synonym: descendant - a person descended from a particular ancestor (variant spelling)
