plenum

noun
meeting with all members present
Pronunciation: / ˈplɛnəm / (AmE), / ˈpliːnəm / (BrE)
A meeting of a legislative or other deliberative body in which all members are present.

Examples

Party plenum
All party members are expected to attend the plenum scheduled for Thursday afternoon.
Decisions made at the annual plenum are binding for the entire committee.
The bill was discussed in several subcommittees before reaching the plenum for a final vote.
