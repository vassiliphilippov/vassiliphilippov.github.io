bonito

noun
flesh of a Pacific fish related to tuna
Pronunciation: / boʊˈniːtoʊ /; / bəˈniːtoʊ / (AmE), / bəˈniːtəʊ / (BrE)
a medium-sized marine fish of the mackerel family, related to tuna, often used as food
