bonito

noun
flesh of a Pacific fish related to tuna
8.4
Pronunciation: / boʊˈniːtoʊ /; / bəˈniːtoʊ / (AmE), / bəˈniːtəʊ / (BrE)
a medium-sized marine fish of the mackerel family, related to tuna, often used as food

Related Terms

  • degree lower: mackerel - a smaller, fast-swimming fish of the same family as bonito
  • synonym: skipjack tuna - a species of tuna closely related to bonito, often used to make dried flakes in Japanese cuisine
  • degree higher: tuna - the flesh of the large food fish related to bonito
