unappetizing

adjective
not appealing in taste, smell, or look
6.0 3.9
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈæp.ɪ.taɪ.zɪŋ/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈæp.ɪ.taɪ.zɪŋ/ (BrE)
