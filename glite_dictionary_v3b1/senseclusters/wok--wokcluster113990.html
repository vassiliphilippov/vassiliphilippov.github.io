wok

noun
round-bottomed frying pan, esp. in Chinese cooking
5.8
Pronunciation: / wɑk / (AmE), / wɒk / (BrE)
a large, round-bottomed pan, typically made of metal, used for frying and stir-frying food, especially in Chinese and East Asian cooking

Examples

A wok pan
A traditional wok distributes heat evenly for perfect stir-fries.
