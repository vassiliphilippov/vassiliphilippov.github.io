ergonomic

adjective
relating to efficiency and comfort in design
2.6 4.2
Pronunciation: /ɜːr.ɡə.ˈnɑː.mɪk/ (AmE), /ɜː.ɡə.ˈnɒ.mɪk/ (BrE)
Designed or arranged for efficiency and comfort in working environments; relating to ergonomics.

Examples

Ergonomic tools
Doctors recommend using ergonomic tools to prevent workplace injuries.
The company invested in ergonomic keyboards to reduce wrist strain among employees.
This office chair has an ergonomic design to support your back during long hours of work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ergonomics - the study of people's efficiency in their working environment
