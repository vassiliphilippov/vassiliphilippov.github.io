throwdown

noun
informal contest
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈθroʊˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊˌdaʊn / (BrE)
an informal contest, competition, or challenge to prove skill or ability

Examples

A cooking throwdown
The two teams organized a friendly throwdown to see who could build the tallest tower.
At the end of the festival, there was a barbecue throwdown between the best local chefs.
She challenged her friends to a dance throwdown at the party last night.

  • synonym: showdown - a decisive confrontation or contest
