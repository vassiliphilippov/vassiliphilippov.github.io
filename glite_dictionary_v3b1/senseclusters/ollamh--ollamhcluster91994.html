ollamh

noun
master poet or scholar
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɒləm / (AmE), / ˈɒləm / (BrE)

Examples

The king consulted the ollamh before making important decisions for the kingdom.
Becoming an ollamh required years of study and mastery of traditional knowledge.
