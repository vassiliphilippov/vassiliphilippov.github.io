turfing daisy

noun
a perennial ground-cover herb with small white flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜr.fɪŋ ˈdeɪ.zi /; / ˈtɝːfɪŋ ˈdeɪzi / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.fɪŋ ˈdeɪ.zi /; / ˈtɜːfɪŋ ˈdeɪzi / (BrE)
a low, mat-forming perennial herb with small white flowers, native to Turkey/Asia Minor, sometimes classified in the genus Matricaria and used as ground cover
