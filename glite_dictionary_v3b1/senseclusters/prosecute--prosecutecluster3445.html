prosecute

verb
charge and try someone in court for a crime
1.0 5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈprɑː.sə.kjuːt/ /; / /ˈprɑː.sɪ.kjuːt/ (AmE), /ˈprɒ.sɪ.kjuːt/ /; / /ˈprɒ.sə.kjuːt/ (BrE)
