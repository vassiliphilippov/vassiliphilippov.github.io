topographer

noun
surface-mapping device
8.6
Pronunciation: /tə.ˈpɑː.ɡrə.fər/ (AmE), /tə.ˈpɒ.ɡrə.fə/ (BrE)
a machine or instrument that measures and creates a detailed map of a surface, especially the cornea of the eye

Examples

An eye topographer
The doctor used a topographer to examine the shape of the patient's cornea before surgery.
Modern eye clinics often rely on a topographer for accurate surface mapping.
A topographer can detect subtle changes in the cornea that may affect vision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: map - a visual representation of an area or surface
  • synonym: tomographer - a device that creates cross-sectional images of the body
