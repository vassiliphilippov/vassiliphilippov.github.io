Mauretanian

noun
ancient North African person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɔːrɪˈteɪniən / (AmE), / ˌmɔːrɪˈteɪniən / (BrE)
a person from the ancient North African kingdom or Roman province of Mauretania

Examples

A Mauretanian leader
A Mauretanian ambassador arrived at the Roman court to negotiate a trade agreement.
Artifacts from a Mauretanian tomb were displayed in the museum's ancient history section.
The historian wrote about the influence of Mauretanians on Mediterranean trade routes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Roman - a person from ancient Rome or its empire
