calpulli

noun
Aztec community group
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɑlˈpuːli / (AmE), / kælˈpʊli / (BrE)
a traditional clan or community group that formed the basic social unit in Aztec society

Examples

Aztec calpulli group
In Aztec cities, each calpulli managed its own land and resources.
Young members of the calpulli learned traditional crafts from their elders.
The calpulli played a key role in organizing religious ceremonies and festivals.
