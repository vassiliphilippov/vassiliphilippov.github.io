unfancied

adjective
plain or simple
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfænsiːd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfænsiːd / (BrE)
plain or simple in style; not elaborate, ornate, or expensive.

Examples

She preferred an unfancied dress for the ceremony, valuing comfort over style.
