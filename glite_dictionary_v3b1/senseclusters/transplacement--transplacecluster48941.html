transplacement

noun
act of moving
Pronunciation: / trænsˈpleɪsmənt / (AmE), / trænsˈpleɪsmənt / (BrE)
the act or process of moving something from one place to another

Examples

Transplacement of goods
The transplacement of ancient artifacts requires careful planning and expertise.
In reconstructive surgery, the transplacement of tissues can help restore function.
The transplacement of the company headquarters improved access for employees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: displacement - the moving of something from its place or position
  • synonym: relocation - the act of moving to a new place
