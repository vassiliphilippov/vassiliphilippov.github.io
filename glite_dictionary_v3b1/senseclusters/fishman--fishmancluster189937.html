fishman

noun
person who sells fish
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃmən / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃmən / (BrE)
a person who catches, sells, or deals in fish, especially in dialectal or informal contexts

Examples

The fishman sells fish
The local fishman arrives at the market before sunrise every day.
Children watched as the fishman cleaned his catch by the riverside.
We bought fresh salmon from the friendly fishman at the harbor.

Related Terms
