come to Jesus moment

noun
moment of major realization or reckoning
9.7 8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌm tə ˈdʒiːzəs ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈkʌm tə ˈdʒiːzəs ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
an occasion when someone realizes the truth about a situation or confronts an important issue, often resulting in a significant change in attitude or behavior

Examples

A come to Jesus moment

Her come to Jesus moment happened when she realized she needed a better work-life balance.
The coach called a meeting that quickly became a come to Jesus moment for the entire team.
