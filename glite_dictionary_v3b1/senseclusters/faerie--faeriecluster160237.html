faerie

noun
the enchanted world of fairies
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪ.əri / (AmE), / ˈfeəri / (BrE)
the magical realm inhabited by fairies; an enchanted supernatural world, often found in folklore and literature

Examples

The realm of faerie
Legends say that mortals rarely return unchanged from the realm of faerie.
Many ancient tales are set in the mysterious land of faerie.
The poet described his journey through faerie as a dreamlike adventure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fairy - a small imaginary being of human form with magical powers
  • synonym: fairyland - the mythical land or realm where fairies live
