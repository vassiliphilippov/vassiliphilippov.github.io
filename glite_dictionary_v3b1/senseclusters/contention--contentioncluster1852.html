contention

noun
the act of competing; rivalry
Pronunciation: / kənˈtɛnʃən / (AmE), / kənˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
the act of competing with others for something such as a prize or position

Examples

In contention for the prize
After months of fierce contention, the two companies finally settled their dispute.
Several candidates dropped out, leaving only two in serious contention for the leadership.
She remains in strong contention for the gold medal despite the setback.

Related Terms

  • synonym: competition - the act of competing against others
  • synonym: conflict - a serious disagreement or argument, often a synonym in some contexts
