Arizona ash

noun
species of ash tree native to the US Southwest
Pronunciation: / əˈrɪzoʊnə æʃ / (AmE), / əˈrɪzənə æʃ / (BrE)
a small, shrubby ash tree (Fraxinus velutina) native to the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico

Examples

A Arizona ash tree
A mature Arizona ash can provide ample shade in desert gardens.
