cor

noun
literary word for heart
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɔr / (AmE), / kɔː / (BrE)
an old or literary word for the heart, especially as the center of emotion or feeling

Examples

A brave cor
The poet wrote of a brave cor beating in the hero's chest.
Legends speak of a king whose cor was pure and unyielding.
In ancient verses, the cor often symbolized courage and love.
