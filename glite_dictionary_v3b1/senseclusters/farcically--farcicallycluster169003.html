farcically

adverb
in a ridiculous or absurd way
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑrsɪkli / (AmE), / ˈfɑːsɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that is ridiculous or absurd, like a farce.

Examples

To farcically misunderstand
He managed to farcically misunderstand even the simplest instructions.
The plan failed farcically, leaving everyone in disbelief at the confusion.
Their attempts at diplomacy devolved farcically into a shouting match.
