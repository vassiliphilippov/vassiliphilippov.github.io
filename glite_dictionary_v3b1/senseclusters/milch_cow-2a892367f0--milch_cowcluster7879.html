milch cow

noun
cow kept for producing milk
Pronunciation: / mɪltʃ kaʊ / (AmE), / mɪltʃ kaʊ / (BrE)
A cow kept or raised specifically for producing milk.

Examples

A milch cow farm
In rural areas, owning a milch cow is a sign of prosperity.
The milch cow grazed peacefully in the field after being milked.
The farmer bought a milch cow to supply fresh milk for his family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dairy cow - a cow bred and kept specifically for milk production
