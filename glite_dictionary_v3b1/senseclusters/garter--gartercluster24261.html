garter

verb
to fasten with garter
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrtər / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːtə / (BrE)
to fasten or secure with a garter, especially a stocking or similar garment.

Examples

To garter a stocking
She carefully gartered her stocking before the performance on stage.
In the old days, men often gartered their socks to keep them from slipping down.
The dancer gartered her leg with a bright ribbon for the show.
