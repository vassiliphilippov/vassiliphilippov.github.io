Plasmodiophora

noun
genus of plant-parasitic slime mold-like organisms
Pronunciation: / plæzˌmoʊdiəˈfɔrə / (AmE), / plazˌməʊdiˈɒfərə / (BrE)
A genus of minute plant-parasitic organisms resembling slime molds, including species that cause disease in plants such as clubroot in cabbages.

Examples

Plasmodiophora plant parasite
Control of Plasmodiophora is important for maintaining healthy cabbage fields.
Researchers examined the life cycle of Plasmodiophora under laboratory conditions.
The pathogen Plasmodiophora is responsible for causing clubroot disease in cruciferous crops.
