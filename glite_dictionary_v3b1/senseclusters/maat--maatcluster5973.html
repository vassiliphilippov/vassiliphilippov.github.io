maat

noun
naval rank
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɑt / (AmE), / mɑːt / (BrE)
a naval rank in some European navies, especially Dutch and German, equivalent to a petty officer or able seaman

Examples

Navy maat
The maat was responsible for supervising the deck crew during the voyage.
After several years of service, he was promoted to maat in the German navy.
The captain relied on the maat to train new recruits and maintain discipline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: able seaman - an experienced sailor in the navy
  • synonym: petty officer - a non-commissioned officer in a navy
