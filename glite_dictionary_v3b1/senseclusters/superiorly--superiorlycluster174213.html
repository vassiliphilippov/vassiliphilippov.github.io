superiorly

adverb
in a higher position
Pronunciation: / suːˈpɪriɚli / (AmE), / suːˈpɪəriəli / (BrE)
in a position above or higher than another part, especially in anatomical contexts

Examples

Superiorly placed organ
In the diagram, the lungs are shown superiorly to the liver.
The brain is located superiorly within the cranial cavity compared to the spinal cord.
The ribs are positioned superiorly to the pelvis in the human skeleton.

Related Terms

  • synonym: above - at a higher level or position
  • antonym: inferiorly - in a position below or lower than another part
