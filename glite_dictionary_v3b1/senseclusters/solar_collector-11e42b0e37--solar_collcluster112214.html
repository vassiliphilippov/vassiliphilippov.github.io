solar collector

noun
device that concentrates sun's rays for heat
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊlər kəˈlɛktər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlə kəˈlɛktə / (BrE)
a device, often shaped as a concave mirror, that concentrates the rays of the sun to produce high temperatures

Examples

A large solar collector
By using a large solar collector, the laboratory was able to reach temperatures suitable for scientific experiments.
Engineers designed a new type of solar collector that is more efficient at concentrating sunlight.
The solar collector was installed on the roof to provide hot water for the building.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parabolic mirror - a reflective surface used to focus light, often concave
  • synonym: solar panel - a flat device that converts sunlight into electricity
