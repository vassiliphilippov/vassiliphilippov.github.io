wheatflake

noun
crisp flake cereal made from wheat
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːtˌfleɪk / (AmE), / ˈwiːtˌfleɪk / (BrE)
a small, crisp, flattened piece of wheat, typically eaten as a breakfast cereal

Examples
