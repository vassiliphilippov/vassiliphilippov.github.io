cheesily

adverb
in a corny way
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːzɪli / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːzɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is corny, overly sentimental, or lacking in style or sophistication

Examples

Cheesily smiling actor
He grinned cheesily at the audience after telling his joke.
The movie ended cheesily with a dramatic love confession in the rain.
She waved cheesily at the camera, making everyone laugh at her exaggerated gesture.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: awkwardly - in a way that is lacking grace or smoothness
  • synonym: cornily - in a way that is overly sentimental or lacking originality
  • antonym: stylishly - in a way that is fashionable and sophisticated
