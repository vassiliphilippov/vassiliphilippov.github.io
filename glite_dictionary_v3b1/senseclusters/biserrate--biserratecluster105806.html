biserrate

adjective
double-serrated; doubly notched edge
9.4 9.1
Pronunciation: / baɪˈsɛreɪt / (AmE), / baɪˈsɛreɪt / (BrE)
(of a leaf or margin) having saw-like notches, each of which is itself notched in a similar way.

Examples

Biserrate leaves
A botanist identified the specimen by its biserrate leaves, which set it apart from similar species.
The leaf margins of some holly species are distinctly biserrate, giving them a unique texture.
Under the microscope, the biserrate pattern of the fern's edge became clearly visible.
