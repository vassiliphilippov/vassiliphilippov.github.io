capitulation

noun
the act of surrendering under terms
5.8
Pronunciation: / kəˌpɪtʃ.əˈleɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / kəˌpɪt.jʊˈleɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of surrendering or giving up, especially under agreed conditions

Examples

Military capitulation
Negotiators struggled to agree on the terms of capitulation before ending the conflict.
The general announced the capitulation of his troops after a long siege.
The sudden capitulation of the city came as a shock to its citizens.

Related Terms

  • antonym: resistance - the act of opposing or withstanding
  • synonym: surrender - the act of giving up or yielding to another
