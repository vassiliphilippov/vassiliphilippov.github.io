rewind time

verb
to imagine going back in time
1.0
Pronunciation: / riˈwaɪnd taɪm / (AmE), / ˌriːˈwaɪnd taɪm / (BrE)
to imagine or wish that one could go back to an earlier moment in the past; to reflect on or revisit past events, often with regret or longing.

Examples

To rewind time with regret
If I could rewind time, I would have spent more time with my family.
No matter how much we want to rewind time, we have to accept the past.
Sometimes people wish they could rewind time to fix their mistakes.
