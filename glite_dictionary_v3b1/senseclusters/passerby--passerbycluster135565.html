passerby

noun
a person passing by
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˌpæsərˈbaɪ / (AmE), / ˌpɑːsəˈbaɪ / (BrE)
a person who happens to be going past a place, especially by chance

Examples

A passerby on the street
A passerby helped the woman carry her groceries across the street.
She thanked a passerby for giving her directions to the museum.
The police asked a passerby if he had seen anything unusual near the building.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bystander - a person who is present at an event or incident but does not take part
