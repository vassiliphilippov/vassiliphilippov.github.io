wise man

noun
a man known for wisdom and guidance
4.1 3.4
Pronunciation: /waɪz mæn/ (AmE), /waɪz mæn/ (BrE)
a man who is respected for his wisdom and trusted as a guide or advisor

Examples

The village often turned to the wise man for advice in difficult times.
