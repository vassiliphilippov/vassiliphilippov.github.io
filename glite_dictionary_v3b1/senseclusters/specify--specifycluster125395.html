specify

verb
determine or describe essential qualities
1.0 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈspe.sə.faɪ/ /; / /ˈspe.sɪ.faɪ/ (AmE), /ˈspe.sɪ.faɪ/ /; / /ˈspe.sə.faɪ/ (BrE)
to state or describe something clearly and exactly, often by listing details, requirements, or conditions

Examples

To specify the details
Scientists attempt to specify the key characteristics that define a new species.
The committee must specify what makes a successful project before awarding the grant.

Please specify the exact time you will arrive.
The contract must specify the terms of payment in detail.
