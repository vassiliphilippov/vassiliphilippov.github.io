unconcerned

adjective
not involved or worried
1.0 3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.kənˈsɝːnd /; / ˌʌnkənˈsɝnd /; / ˌʌn.kənˈsɝnd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kənˈsɜːnd /; / ˌʌnkənˈsɜːnd / (BrE)
not worried, anxious, or interested; showing a lack of concern or interest

Examples
