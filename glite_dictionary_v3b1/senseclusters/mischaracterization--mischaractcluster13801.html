mischaracterization

noun
incorrect description
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪskærɪktəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪskærɪktəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
an incorrect or unfair description of someone or something

Examples

A mischaracterization of facts
The article was criticized for its mischaracterization of the scientist's research.
He felt that the movie's portrayal was a complete mischaracterization of the real events.
Her lawyer argued that the accusations were based on a mischaracterization of the facts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distortion - a false or misleading account of something
  • antonym: representation - an accurate or fair description of something
