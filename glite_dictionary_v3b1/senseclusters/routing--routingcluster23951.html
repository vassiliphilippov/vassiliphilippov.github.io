routing

noun
crushing defeat
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈraʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
a decisive or overwhelming defeat in a contest, game, or battle

Examples

A routing defeat
The home team suffered a routing that left their fans in shock.

The unexpected routing of the defending champions made headlines across the country.
