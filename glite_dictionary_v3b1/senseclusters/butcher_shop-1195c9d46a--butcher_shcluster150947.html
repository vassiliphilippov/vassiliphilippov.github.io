butcher shop

noun
shop selling meat and poultry

Examples

The butcher shop sells meat

Synonyms

  • meat market
    1.1 0.33/m

  • butchery
    4.7 0.06/m
    butchery is the place or trade that slaughters and prepares meat, while butcher shop is a retail shop that mainly sells fresh meat.
