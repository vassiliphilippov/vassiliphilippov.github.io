tremulous

adjective
quivering voice from fear or weakness
6.3 6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈtre.mjə.ləs/ /; / /ˈtre.mjʊ.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈtre.mjʊ.ləs/ /; / /ˈtre.mjə.ləs/ (BrE)
(of a person's voice) shaking or quivering slightly, especially from fear or weakness.

Examples

Tremulous voice
He answered the question in a tremulous whisper, afraid of making a mistake.
Her tremulous voice betrayed her nervousness during the speech.
The child's tremulous tone was barely audible as she spoke to the principal.
