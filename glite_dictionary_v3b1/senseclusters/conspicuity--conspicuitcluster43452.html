conspicuity

noun
visibility; noticeability
Pronunciation: / kənˌspɪkjuˈɪti / (AmE), / kənˌspɪkjuˈɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being easily seen or noticed

Examples

High conspicuity sign
The conspicuity of the warning sign helped prevent accidents on the road.
Bright colors increase the conspicuity of safety equipment in low-light conditions.
Designers focused on the conspicuity of the new logo to make it stand out.

Related Terms

  • antonym: obscurity - the state of being difficult to see or notice
  • synonym: visibility - the state of being able to be seen
