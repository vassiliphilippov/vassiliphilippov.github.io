Swedish turnip

noun
edible yellow-rooted vegetable plant
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈswidɪʃ ˈtɚnɪp /; / ˈswidɪʃ ˈtɜrnɪp / (AmE), / ˈswiːdɪʃ ˈtɜːnɪp / (BrE)
a large, round, yellow root vegetable (also called rutabaga or swede), or the plant it comes from
