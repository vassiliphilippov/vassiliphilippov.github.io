Aerobacter aerogenes

noun
species of Gram-negative bacteria
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛrəˈbæktər ˌɛrəˈdʒiːniːz / (AmE), / ˌɛərəʊˈbæktə ˌɛərəʊˈdʒiːniːz / (BrE)
A species of Gram-negative, aerobic bacteria that produces gas and acid from sugars and is sometimes involved in the souring of milk.
