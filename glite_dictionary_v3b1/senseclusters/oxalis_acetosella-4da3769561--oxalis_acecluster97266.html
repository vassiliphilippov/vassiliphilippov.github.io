Oxalis acetosella

noun
Eurasian plant with trifoliate leaves and white, purple-veined flowers
Pronunciation: / ɑkˈsælɪs ˌæsɪˈtosələ / (AmE), / ɒkˈsælɪs ˌæsɪˈtəʊsɪlə / (BrE)
A small Eurasian woodland plant with heart-shaped, three-part leaves and white flowers marked with purple veins; commonly known as wood sorrel.
