tronc

noun
donation collection box
Pronunciation: / trɒŋk / (AmE), / trɒŋk / (BrE)
a box or container for collecting money, especially alms or donations, often found in churches or public places

Examples

Church tronc donations
The old church still has a wooden tronc near the entrance for visitors' donations.
Coins clinked as people dropped them into the tronc to help the needy.
A small sign above the tronc explained that all contributions would go to local charities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alms box - a box for collecting money for the poor
  • synonym: donation box - a container for collecting voluntary contributions
