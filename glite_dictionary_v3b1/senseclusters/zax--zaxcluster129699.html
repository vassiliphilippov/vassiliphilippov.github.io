zax

verb
to shape slate
9.9
Pronunciation: / zæks / (AmE), / zæks / (BrE)
to cut, trim, or shape slate using a zax, a tool used by slaters

Examples

To zax a slate
The roofer expertly zaxed each slate to fit the unique angles of the roof.
Before installing the tiles, she zaxed the edges for a perfect fit.
He learned how to zax slate pieces during his apprenticeship as a slater.
