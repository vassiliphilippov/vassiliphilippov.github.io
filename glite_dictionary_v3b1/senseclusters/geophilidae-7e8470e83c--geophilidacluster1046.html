Geophilidae

noun
family of soil-dwelling centipedes
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒiːˈɑːfɪlɪˌdiː /; / dʒiˌɑːfɪˈlɪdi / (AmE), / dʒiːˈɒfɪlɪˌdiː /; / dʒiːˌɒfɪˈlɪdiː / (BrE)
