unsub

verb
to cancel subscription
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈʌnsʌb / (AmE), / ˈʌnsʌb / (BrE)
to cancel a subscription or stop receiving messages, updates, or content from a service or channel

Examples

To unsub from emails
If you no longer want updates, you can easily unsub from the newsletter.
Many users choose to unsub from channels that post too frequently.
I had to unsub after the service started sending too many promotional emails.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unsubscribe - to remove oneself from a subscription or mailing list
