Ayn Rand

proper noun
Russian-American novelist and philosopher
6.5
Pronunciation: / aɪn rænd / (AmE), / aɪn rænd / (BrE)
(1905–1982) Russian-born American writer and philosopher, known for her novels and for developing the philosophical system of Objectivism.
