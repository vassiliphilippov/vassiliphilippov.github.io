weenie

noun
small sausage; hot dog style sausage
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːni / (AmE), / ˈwiːni / (BrE)
A smooth-textured sausage made from minced beef or pork, usually smoked, often served in a bread roll.

Examples

A weenie hot dog
At the ballpark, I bought a weenie wrapped in a soft bun.
He put mustard and ketchup on his weenie before eating it at the picnic.
The kids asked if they could roast weenies over the campfire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hot dog - a cooked sausage in a sliced bun
  • synonym: wiener - another term for the same type of sausage
