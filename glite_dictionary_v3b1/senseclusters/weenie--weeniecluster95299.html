weenie

noun
small sausage; hot dog style sausage
6.8 0.73/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàwiÀêni / (AmE), / ÀàwiÀêni / (BrE)
A smooth-textured sausage made from minced beef or pork, usually smoked, often served in a bread roll.

Examples

A weenie hot dog

Synonyms

    wiener is the common standard word for this smoked sausage in English, while weenie is a more informal, childish variant.
  • frankfurter
    2.0 0.19/m
    frankfurter is the full, slightly formal name often seen on menus, while weenie is a shorter, more casual term.
  • hot dog
    1.0 7.86/m
    hot dog is a cooked sausage served in a sliced bun with condiments, while weenie refers to the sausage itself.
