top hat

noun
tall formal men's hat
1.4
Pronunciation: / tɑp hæt / (AmE), / tɒp hæt / (BrE)
a tall, cylindrical man's hat, usually made of silk or beaver fur, traditionally worn on formal occasions

Examples

A top hat for men
He wore a top hat and a black tuxedo to the gala.
In old photographs, gentlemen often appear in suits and top hats.
The magician pulled a rabbit out of his top hat.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bowler hat - a hard felt hat with a rounded crown
