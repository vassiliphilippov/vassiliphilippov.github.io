exchange contracts

verb
formally swap legal documents in a transaction
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstʃeɪndʒ ˈkɑnˌtrækt͡s / (AmE), / ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ ˈkɒntrækts / (BrE)
to formally swap signed legal documents, especially in property transactions, making an agreement legally binding

Examples
