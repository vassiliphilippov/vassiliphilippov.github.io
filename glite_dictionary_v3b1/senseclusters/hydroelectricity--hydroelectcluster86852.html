Examples

Hydroelectricity power plant
Engineers are designing new dams to maximize hydroelectricity production while protecting local ecosystems.
Much of Norway's energy comes from hydroelectricity generated by its rivers and waterfalls.
The shift to hydroelectricity has significantly reduced the country's carbon emissions.

Synonyms

  • hydropower
    3.6 0.77/m
    hydropower refers to energy made from moving water using turbines and dams, while hydroelectricity names the electricity produced from that energy.
  • hydro
    3.7 1.66/m
    hydro often means the local power station or electricity from water in casual speech, while hydroelectricity names the electricity produced.
  • waterpower
    3.9 0.03/m
    waterpower is the general force of falling or moving water used for machines, while hydroelectricity is the electricity made from that force.
