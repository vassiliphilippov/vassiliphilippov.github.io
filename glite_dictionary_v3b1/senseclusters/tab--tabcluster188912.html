tab

verb
to select for a role
7.1
Pronunciation: / tæb / (AmE), / tæb / (BrE)
to choose or officially select someone for a particular job, role, or task.

Examples

To tab a leader
She was tabbed to lead the new marketing project after her impressive presentation.
The coach tabbed him as the team's next captain for his leadership skills.
After months of speculation, the board finally tabbed a new CEO.
