fireback

noun
fireplace metal plate
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌbæk / (BrE)
a metal plate placed at the back of a fireplace to protect the wall and reflect heat into the room

Examples

Cast iron fireback
The antique fireback in the living room was decorated with a family crest.
Installing a fireback can help increase the efficiency of your fireplace.
The old cottage still had its original cast iron fireback behind the logs.
