workbox

noun
container for sewing or work materials
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːk.bɑks / (AmE), / ˈwɜːk.bɒks / (BrE)
a box or container used to hold tools, materials, and equipment for work, especially for sewing

Examples

A workbox for sewing
She kept all her needles and threads neatly arranged in her workbox.
The old wooden workbox had compartments for buttons and spools of thread.
Whenever she sat down to mend clothes, she brought out her trusty workbox.

  • synonym: toolbox - container for holding tools
