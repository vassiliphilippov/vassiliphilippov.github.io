lehr

noun
glass cooling oven
Pronunciation: / lɛr / (AmE), / lɜː / (BrE)
a long oven used to slowly cool glassware after shaping to prevent cracking

Examples

A lehr oven
After shaping, the glass vases were placed in the lehr to cool slowly and evenly.
The factory installed a new lehr to improve the quality of their glass products.
If glassware is not cooled in a lehr, it may crack due to rapid temperature changes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: furnace - an enclosed structure for heating materials to high temperatures
  • synonym: kiln - a furnace or oven for firing, drying, or baking materials
