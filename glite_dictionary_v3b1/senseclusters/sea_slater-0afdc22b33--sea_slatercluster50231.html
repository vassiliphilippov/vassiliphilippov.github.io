sea slater

noun
marine isopod crustacean
6.5
Pronunciation: / siː ˈsleɪtər / (AmE), / siː ˈsleɪtə / (BrE)
a large, flattened marine isopod crustacean, typically found in rocky coastal areas, resembling a woodlouse

Examples

A sea slater creature
Children were fascinated by the sea slater they found during their nature walk.
The sea slater is sometimes called the sea louse because of its appearance.
We spotted a sea slater crawling among the rocks at low tide.
