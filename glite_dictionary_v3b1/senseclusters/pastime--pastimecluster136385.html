pastime

noun
activity done for enjoyment in free time
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpæstaɪm / (AmE), / ˈpɑːstaɪm / (BrE)
An activity that is done regularly for enjoyment, especially during one's free time.

Examples

A pastime activity
Chess has been his favorite pastime since childhood.
Gardening is a popular pastime for people of all ages.
Many people take up painting as a relaxing pastime after retirement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hobby - an activity done regularly in one's leisure time for pleasure
  • synonym: occupation - an activity that occupies one's time; pastime
