hockey stick

noun
graph shape with sharp rise
4.6
Pronunciation: / \u02c8h\u0251ki st\u026ak / (AmE), / \u02c8h\u0252ki st\u026ak / (BrE)
A shape of a graph or chart that is flat or slowly rising for a long period and then turns into a rapid, sharp upward rise, resembling a hockey stick.

Examples

Hockey stick graph
Recent climate graphs show a hockey stick pattern, highlighting rapid warming in recent decades.
Startups often hope for a hockey stick in revenue after their product gains market traction.
Economists warned the report displayed a clear hockey stick, with growth spiking after policy changes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hockey-stick graph - a graph showing a long period of little change followed by a sudden large increase
