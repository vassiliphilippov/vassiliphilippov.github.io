antifa

noun
anti-fascist activist
Pronunciation: / ænˈtiːfə / (AmE), / ænˈtiːfə / (BrE)
a person who supports or is a member of a movement opposing fascism, often participating in protests or direct action

Examples

An antifa protester
Several antifa gathered downtown to protest against the far-right rally.
The mayor addressed concerns about violence between antifa and opposing groups.
Some members of antifa wore masks during the demonstration to protect their identities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anti-fascist - a person who opposes fascism
  • antonym: fascist - a person who supports fascism
