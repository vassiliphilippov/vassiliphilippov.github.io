stereotyper

noun
printing plate maker
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛriəˌtaɪpər / (AmE), / ˈstɪəriəˌtaɪpə / (BrE)
a person or machine that makes printing plates by creating molds from set type

Examples

Printing stereotyper
The stereotyper worked late to prepare the plates for the morning edition.
Advances in technology have made the job of a stereotyper largely obsolete.
A skilled stereotyper was essential for efficient newspaper production in the past.

Related Terms

  • synonym: printer - a person or machine that produces printed material
  • synonym: typesetter - a person who arranges type for printing
