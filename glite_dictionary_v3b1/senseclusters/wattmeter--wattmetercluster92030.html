wattmeter

noun
device for measuring electrical power in watts
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑtˌmiːtər / (AmE), / ˈwɒtˌmiːtə / (BrE)
an instrument for measuring the flow of electric power in watts within an electrical circuit

Examples

Electric wattmeter
High-quality wattmeters are essential in testing electrical appliances for energy efficiency.
The engineer used a wattmeter to monitor the power output of the generator.
To ensure accuracy, the wattmeter must be calibrated before each experiment.
