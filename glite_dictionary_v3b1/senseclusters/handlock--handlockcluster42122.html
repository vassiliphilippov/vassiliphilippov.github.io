handlock

noun
metal wrist shackle for restraint
8.3 8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈhænd.lɑːk/ (AmE), /ˈhænd.lɒk/ (BrE)
A shackle consisting of a metal loop that can be locked around the wrist, usually used in pairs to restrain a person's hands.

Examples

A handlock shackle
He struggled to free himself from the handlock, but the metal held firm around his wrist.
Police found an old set of handlocks in the evidence room, rusted from years of disuse.
The guard secured the suspect's wrists with a pair of handlocks before escorting him to the station.
