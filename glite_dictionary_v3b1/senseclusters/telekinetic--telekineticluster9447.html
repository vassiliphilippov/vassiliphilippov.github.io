telekinetic

noun
person with mind powers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛlɪkɪˈnɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪkɪˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
a person who is able to move or influence objects using only mental power

Examples

A telekinetic character
The movie features a young telekinetic who can bend metal with her mind.
Rumors spread quickly about the mysterious telekinetic living in the small town.
The scientists were amazed by the telekinetic who could move objects without touching them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: psychic - a person who claims to have supernatural mental abilities
  • synonym: telepath - a person who can communicate thoughts directly from mind to mind
