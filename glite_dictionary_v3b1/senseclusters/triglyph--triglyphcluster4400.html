triglyph

noun
decorative grooved block
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪɡlɪf / (AmE), / ˈtrɪɡlɪf /; / ˈtraɪɡlɪf / (BrE)
a rectangular decorative block with three vertical grooves, used in the frieze of classical (especially Doric) architecture
