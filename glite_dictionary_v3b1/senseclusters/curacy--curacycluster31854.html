curacy

noun
position of a curate
Pronunciation: / ˈkjʊrəsi / (AmE), / ˈkjʊərəsi / (BrE)
the position or office of a curate, or the period during which one serves as a curate

Examples

A curacy position
After ordination, he was given a curacy in a small rural parish.
During his curacy, he gained valuable pastoral experience.
She completed her curacy before moving on to lead her own congregation.

Related Terms
