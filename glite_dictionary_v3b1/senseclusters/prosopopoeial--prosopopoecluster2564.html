prosopopoeial

adjective
of personification speech
Pronunciation: / ˌprɑːsəpəˈpiːəl / (AmE), / ˌprɒsəpəˈpiːəl / (BrE)
relating to the rhetorical device of speaking as if another person or object is speaking

Examples

A prosopopoeial speech
The novel's prosopopoeial passages allowed animals to express their thoughts directly.
Her prosopopoeial approach made the statue seem to speak to the audience.
A prosopopoeial technique can make inanimate objects come alive in literature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: personificatory - relating to personification
