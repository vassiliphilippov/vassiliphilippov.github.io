tuffet

noun
low seat or footstool
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌf.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈtʌf.ɪt / (BrE)
A low seat or footstool, often padded, used to support the feet of a seated person.

Examples

A padded tuffet
He used the small tuffet to rest his tired feet after a long day.
She sat on a tuffet by the fireplace, reading her book.
The nursery rhyme describes Little Miss Muffet sitting on a tuffet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ottoman - a padded cushion used as a seat or footstool
  • synonym: stool - a simple seat without back or arms
