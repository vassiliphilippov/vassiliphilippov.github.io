anti-inflammatory drug

noun
medicine reducing inflammation
1.4 2.9
Pronunciation: /æn.ti.ɪn.ˈflæm.ə.tɔːr.iː drʌg/ /; / /æn.taɪ.ɪn.ˈflæm.ə.tɔːr.iː drʌg/ (AmE), /æn.ti.ɪn.ˈflæm.ə.tə.riː drʌg/ /; / /æn.tɪ.ɪn.ˈflæm.ə.tə.riː drʌg/ (BrE)
