anti-inflammatory drug

noun
medicine reducing inflammation
Pronunciation: / ˌænti ɪnˈflæməˌtɔri drʌɡ / (AmE), / ˌænti ɪnˈflamətəri drʌɡ / (BrE)
a medicine that is used to reduce inflammation or swelling in the body

Examples

An anti-inflammatory drug
After her injury, she took an anti-inflammatory drug to manage the inflammation and discomfort.
Some people experience side effects when taking a strong anti-inflammatory drug daily.
The doctor prescribed an anti-inflammatory drug to help reduce the patient's joint pain and swelling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: NSAID - non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug; a major class of anti-inflammatory drugs
