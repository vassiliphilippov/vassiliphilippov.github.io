genetically

adverb
by means of genes or heredity
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈnɛtɪkli / (AmE), / dʒəˈnɛtɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to genes or heredity; by genetic mechanisms.

Examples

To be genetically predisposed
John is genetically predisposed to have curly hair.
Some diseases are genetically determined and passed from parents to children.
These crops have been genetically modified to resist certain pests.
