bluebottle

noun
a metallic blue blowfly
Pronunciation: / ˈbluˌbɑt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌbɒt.əl / (BrE)
a large fly with a metallic blue body, often found around decaying matter and known for its loud buzzing sound

Examples

A buzzing bluebottle
A bluebottle flew noisily around the kitchen, attracted by the garbage.
During the summer, bluebottles often gather near compost bins and waste areas.
He recognized the loud buzzing as coming from a bluebottle trapped against the window.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blowfly - a type of fly often found around decaying matter
