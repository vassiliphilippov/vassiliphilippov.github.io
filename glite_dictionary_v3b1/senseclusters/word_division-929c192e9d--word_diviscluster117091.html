word division

noun
breaking a word at line end
4.1
Pronunciation: / wɝːd dɪˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / wɜːd dɪˈvɪʒən / (BrE)
the separation of a word into parts, especially when breaking a word at the end of a line in writing or printing
