Delphinus

noun
genus of dolphins
9.3
Pronunciation: / dɛlˈfaɪnəs / (AmE), / dɛlˈfɪnəs / (BrE)
A genus of marine mammals in the family Delphinidae, commonly known as the common dolphin.
