squeeze

verb
hold someone closely in your arms
1.0 5/m
Pronunciation: / skwiÀêz / (AmE), / skwiÀêz / (BrE)
to hold someone tightly in your arms, usually to show affection

Examples

To squeeze a friend
He squeezed his friend goodbye at the airport.
She gave her son a big squeeze before he went to school.
Whenever they meet, they squeeze each other tightly in a warm hug.
