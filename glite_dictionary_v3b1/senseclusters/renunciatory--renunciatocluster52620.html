renunciatory

adjective
giving up or rejecting
Pronunciation: / rɪˈnʌnsiəˌtɔri / (AmE), / rɪˈnʌnsiətəri / (BrE)
expressing or relating to the act of giving up, rejecting, or renouncing something

Examples

A renunciatory attitude
Her renunciatory attitude toward material possessions inspired many of her friends.
The leader's renunciatory statement marked a turning point in the negotiations.
He wrote a renunciatory letter, formally giving up his claim to the inheritance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abdicative - relating to giving up power or responsibility
  • antonym: affirmative - expressing agreement or acceptance
