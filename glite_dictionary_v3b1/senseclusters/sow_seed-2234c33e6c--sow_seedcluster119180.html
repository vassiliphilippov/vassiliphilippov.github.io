sow seed

verb
to plant seeds for growth
2.2 0.24/m
Pronunciation: / so ä sid / (AmE), / s…ô ä siÀêd / (BrE)
to plant seeds in the ground in order for them to grow

Examples

To sow seed in fields
Farmers sow seed in the fields after the last frost has passed.
He learned how to properly sow seed to ensure a healthy crop.
In March, it's time to sow seed for your vegetable garden.

Synonyms

  • sow
    1.8 3.49/m
    sow is a simple verb for putting seeds into soil on farms, while sow seed names the specific action of planting seeds.
  • seed
    1.3 0.63/m
    seed is a verb meaning to plant seeds or to make plants produce seeds, while sow seed focuses on planting seeds in the ground.
  • plant
    1.0 27/m
    plant means to put seedlings, seeds, or young plants into the soil to grow, while sow seed refers only to planting seeds.

Related Terms
