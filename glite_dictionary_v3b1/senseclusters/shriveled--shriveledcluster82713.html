shriveled

adjective
diminished in strength or intensity
Pronunciation: / ˈʃrɪvəld / (AmE), / ˈʃrɪvəld / (BrE)
reduced in vitality, strength, or effectiveness; diminished in intensity or importance.

Examples

Shriveled hopes
After months of neglect, their shriveled hopes for success finally disappeared.
Once a booming business, it now survives on shriveled profits.
The shriveled attendance numbers concerned the event organizer.

  • synonym: withered - dried up or diminished in strength or vitality
