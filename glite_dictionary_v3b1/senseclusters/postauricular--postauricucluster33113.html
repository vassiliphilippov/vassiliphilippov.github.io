postauricular

adjective
behind the ear
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstɔːˈrɪkjələr / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstɔːˈrɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
located or occurring behind the external part of the ear

Examples

The postauricular area
The doctor examined the postauricular area for signs of infection.
A small lump was found in the postauricular region during the checkup.
Swelling in the postauricular space can indicate an underlying ear problem.

Related Terms

  • antonym: preauricular - situated in front of the ear
  • synonym: retroauricular - situated behind the ear
