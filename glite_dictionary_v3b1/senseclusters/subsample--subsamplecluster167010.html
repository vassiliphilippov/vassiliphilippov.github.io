subsample

verb
to reduce data points
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌsæmpəl / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌsɑːmpəl / (BrE)
to select or create a smaller group or set from a larger sample, dataset, or signal for analysis or processing

Examples

Engineers often subsample high-resolution images to speed up processing times.
