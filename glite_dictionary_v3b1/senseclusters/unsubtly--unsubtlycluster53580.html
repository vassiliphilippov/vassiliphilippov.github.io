unsubtly

adverb
obviously, not delicately
5.9
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈsʌt.liː/ /; / /ən.ˈsʌt.liː/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈsʌt.liː/ /; / /ən.ˈsʌt.liː/ (BrE)
