fro

adverb
away or back
Pronunciation: / froʊ / (AmE), / frəʊ / (BrE)
away from a place or back, used mainly in the phrase 'to and fro' to indicate movement in one direction and then the opposite.

Examples

To and fro
The boat rocked fro and to as the waves grew stronger.
He paced fro and to in the hallway, waiting for news.
The lantern swung fro and to in the night breeze.

Related Terms

  • synonym: to and fro - back and forth repeatedly
