sensorimotor area

noun
region of the brain integrating sensory and motor functions
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛnsəriˈmoʊtər ˈɛriə / (AmE), / ˌsɛnsəriˈməʊtə ˈɛəriə / (BrE)
a region of the cerebral cortex that includes both the precentral and postcentral gyri and is involved in integrating sensory and motor functions

Examples

The sensorimotor area of brain
Damage to the sensorimotor area can result in both motor and sensory deficits.
Researchers used brain imaging to identify activity in the sensorimotor area during the task.
The sensorimotor area plays a crucial role in coordinating movement and sensory input.

Related Terms

  • synonym: motor cortex - region of the brain involved in planning and executing movement
  • synonym: sensory cortex - part of the cerebral cortex that receives and processes sensory information
