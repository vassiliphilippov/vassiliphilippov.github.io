as-needed approach

noun
flexible method used when required
Pronunciation: / æz ˈnidɪd əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / æz ˈniːdɪd əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a method or strategy in which actions or resources are used only when required by circumstances, allowing flexibility

Examples

An as-needed approach
An as-needed approach can help organizations respond quickly to changes in demand.
The company adopted an as-needed approach to hiring during the unpredictable economic climate.
Their as-needed approach means resources are only committed when absolutely necessary.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flexible approach - a strategy that allows for changing actions based on circumstances
