potsie

noun
children's tossing game
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑtsi / (AmE), / ˈpɒtsi / (BrE)
a children's game in which players toss small objects, such as stones, into marked areas or targets, often played on sidewalks

Examples

Play potsie outside
After school, the children drew squares with chalk and played potsie on the sidewalk.
She taught her younger brother how to play potsie using small stones as markers.
Many adults remember playing potsie with friends during their childhood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hopscotch - a children's game involving hopping and tossing a marker onto numbered spaces
