submissive

adjective
inclined to obey or yield to others
Pronunciation: / səbˈmɪsɪv / (AmE), / səbˈmɪsɪv / (BrE)
willing to obey or yield to others, often without resistance or in a humble way

Examples

Submissive nod
He gave a submissive nod in response to his manager’s instructions.
Her submissive attitude made it easy for others to take advantage of her.
The dog became submissive after being trained by its new owner.
His submissive attitude made him vulnerable to exploitation at work.
She remained submissive in the face of harsh criticism from her superiors.
The cult demanded submissive loyalty from all of its members.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compliant - willing to agree with others; obedient
  • antonym: domineering - asserting one's will over others in an arrogant way
  • synonym: obedient - willing to obey orders or rules
