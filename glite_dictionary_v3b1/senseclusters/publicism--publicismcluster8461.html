publicism

noun
public relations work
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪˌsɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪˌsɪzəm / (BrE)
the practice of creating and managing publicity or a public image for people, organizations, or products

Examples

Publicism campaign
The company invested heavily in publicism to boost its brand after the product launch.
Effective publicism can help organizations recover from negative publicity.
She built her career in publicism by managing high-profile clients' public images.

Related Terms

  • synonym: promotion - activities to advertise or publicize something
  • synonym: public relations - the professional maintenance of a favorable public image
