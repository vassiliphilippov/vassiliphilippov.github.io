impinger

noun
air particle collector
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɪndʒər / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɪndʒə / (BrE)
a device used to collect particles from air or gas, often for scientific analysis

Examples

Air impinger device

An impinger is often used in environmental studies to monitor air quality.

The technician placed the impinger in the airflow to collect dust particles for analysis.
Researchers used an impinger to monitor bacteria levels in the hospital's ventilation system.
After sampling, the impinger was carefully cleaned to avoid contamination between tests.
