persnickety

adjective
overly fussy; paying too much attention to detail
9.3 7.8
Pronunciation: /pər.ˈsnɪ.kə.tiː/ /; / /pər.ˈsnɪ.kɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /pə.ˈsnɪ.kə.tiː/ /; / /pə.ˈsnɪ.kɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
being overly concerned with small or trivial details; fussy or particular (often used informally)

Examples

A persnickety person
His persnickety nature made group work challenging, as he corrected even the smallest errors.
She has a persnickety attitude when organizing documents, insisting everything be in order.
The chef was so persnickety about every garnish that he delayed the meal.
Don't be so persnickety—no one cares if the towels aren't folded exactly.
His persnickety attitude made it hard to enjoy the simple things.
She is so persnickety about her desk that everything must be perfectly aligned.
