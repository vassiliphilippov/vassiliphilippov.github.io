doxycycline

noun
an antibiotic drug
Pronunciation: / ˌdɑksiˈsaɪklin / (AmE), / ˌdɒksɪˈsaɪkliːn / (BrE)
an antibiotic belonging to the tetracycline group, used to treat a wide range of bacterial infections

Examples

doxycycline antibiotic
Allergic reactions to doxycycline are rare but should be reported immediately.
My doctor prescribed doxycycline to treat my sinus infection.
Some cases of Lyme disease are effectively managed with doxycycline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tetracycline - an antibiotic from which doxycycline is derived; also a class of antibiotics
