swimming hole

noun
natural pool suitable for swimming
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪmɪŋ hoʊl / (AmE), / ˈswɪmɪŋ həʊl / (BrE)
A small natural pool or deep section of a stream, river, or creek that is suitable for swimming.

Examples

The swimming hole by river
On hot summer days, locals like to cool off in the swimming hole by the old bridge.
The children spent the afternoon splashing and playing in their favorite swimming hole.
They discovered a hidden swimming hole while hiking along the creek.
