Corypha umbraculifera

noun
a tall Asian palm tree with large leaves
Pronunciation: / ˌkɔːrɪfə ˌʌmbrəˌkjuːlɪˈfɪərə / (AmE), / ˌkɒrɪfə ˌʌmbrəˌkjuːlɪˈfɪərə / (BrE)
