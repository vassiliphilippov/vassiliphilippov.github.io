nonreligious

noun
person with no religion
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnrɪˈlɪdʒəs / (AmE), / ˌnɒnrɪˈlɪdʒəs / (BrE)
a person who does not follow, practice, or belong to any religion

Examples

A nonreligious person
The number of nonreligious in the country has increased over the past decade.
Many nonreligious choose to celebrate holidays in a secular way.
Some nonreligious feel strongly about keeping religion separate from government.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agnostic - a person who believes that nothing is known or can be known about the existence of God
  • synonym: atheist - a person who does not believe in the existence of any god
  • antonym: believer - a person who has religious faith
