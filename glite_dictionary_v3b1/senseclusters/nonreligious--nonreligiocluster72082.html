nonreligious

noun
person with no religion
4.6
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.rɪ.ˈlɪ.dʒəs/ /; / /nən.rɪ.ˈlɪ.dʒəs/ (AmE), /nɒn.rɪ.ˈlɪ.dʒəs/ /; / /nən.rɪ.ˈlɪ.dʒəs/ (BrE)

Examples

A nonreligious person

Many nonreligious choose to celebrate holidays in a secular way.
Some nonreligious feel strongly about keeping religion separate from government.
