unpatted

adjective
not smoothed by pats
7.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpætɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpætɪd / (BrE)
not having been smoothed, pressed, or shaped by gentle pats, as with dough, hair, or fabric

Examples

Unpatted dough surface
The surface of the dough was unpatted, giving the bread a rustic appearance.
Her hair looked messy and unpatted after she took off her hat.
The fabric remained unpatted, so it showed many small wrinkles.

Related Terms

  • antonym: patted - smoothed or shaped by gentle pats
