Barolo

proper noun
Italian wine region
Pronunciation: / bɑːˈroʊloʊ / (AmE), / bəˈrəʊləʊ / (BrE)
a famous wine-producing region in Piedmont, Italy, known for its high-quality red wines made from Nebbiolo grapes

Examples

Barolo wine region
