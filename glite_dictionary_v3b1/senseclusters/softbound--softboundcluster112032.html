softbound

noun
paperback book
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔftˌbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈsɒftˌbaʊnd / (BrE)
a book that has a flexible paper cover instead of a hard cover; a paperback edition

Examples

A softbound book
She prefers to buy softbound books because they are easier to carry.
The new novel was released in both hardcover and softbound editions.
He found a rare softbound copy of the classic in a used bookstore.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hardcover - a book with a rigid protective cover
  • synonym: paperback - a book with a soft paper cover
