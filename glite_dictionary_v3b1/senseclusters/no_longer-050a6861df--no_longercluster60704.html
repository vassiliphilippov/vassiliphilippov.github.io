no longer

adverb
not now; formerly but not anymore
Pronunciation: / noʊ ˈlɔŋɡər / (AmE), / nəʊ ˈlɒŋɡə / (BrE)
Not now; used to indicate that something happened in the past but does not happen or is not the case now.

Examples

He no longer works
After the incident, he no longer trusted his colleagues.
She no longer lives in the city.
We no longer use typewriters in the office.

Related Terms

  • synonym: no more - not any longer; not now
