playing card

noun
card used in card games
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪɪŋ ˌkɑrd / (AmE), / ˈpleɪɪŋ ˌkɑːd / (BrE)
One of a set of rectangular cards marked with numbers, symbols, or pictures, used for playing various card games.

Examples

A playing card deck
A joker is a special playing card found in many modern decks.
Children were building a house of playing cards on the living room floor.
He shuffled the playing cards before dealing them to the players.

Related Terms

  • synonym: card - a piece of stiff paper or thin cardboard, in this sense used in games
