stereographer

noun
3D image specialist
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌstɛriˈɑːɡrəfər / (AmE), / ˌstɪəriˈɒɡrəfə / (BrE)
a person who creates or manages stereoscopic images or 3D visual effects, especially in film or photography

Examples

The stereographer creates 3D images
The stereographer worked closely with the director to achieve stunning 3D effects in the movie.
A skilled stereographer can make virtual reality experiences feel incredibly lifelike.
During production, the stereographer adjusted the cameras to enhance the depth of each scene.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cinematographer - a person responsible for the camera work and lighting in film
  • synonym: photographer - a person who takes photographs
