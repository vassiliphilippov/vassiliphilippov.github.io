lever

verb
to use borrowed funds
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈliːvər / (AmE), / ˈliːvə / (BrE)
to use borrowed money or resources to increase the potential return of an investment or business activity

Examples

To lever money
The company decided to lever its assets to expand operations.
Startups often lever outside capital to accelerate their growth.
He tried to lever his small investment into a much larger return.

Related Terms

  • synonym: invest - to put money into something to try to earn more money
  • synonym: leverage - to use something to maximum advantage, especially borrowed money in finance
