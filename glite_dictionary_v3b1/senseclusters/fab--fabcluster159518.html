fab

noun
semiconductor fabrication plant
8.1 0.63/m
Pronunciation: / fÃ¦b / (AmE), / fÃ¦b / (BrE)
(industry) A fabrication facility where semiconductor devices (integrated circuits) are manufactured, often used specifically for plants that process silicon wafers.

Examples

A chip fab
The company is expanding its fab to increase chip production.
Small startup designers outsource wafer runs to large fabs that offer advanced process nodes.
The government visited the new microchip fab to discuss subsidies and security measures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fabrication plant - a factory where products are manufactured; in context, a plant that fabricates semiconductor devices
  • synonym: foundry - a company or plant that manufactures chips for other companies; often used interchangeably with 'fab' in semiconductor contexts
