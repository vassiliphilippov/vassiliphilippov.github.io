vivat

x
Latin exclamation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈviːvæt / (AmE), / ˈviːvæt / (BrE)
an exclamation meaning 'may he/she/it live', used to express a wish for someone's long life or success, especially in Latin texts or mottos

Examples

Vivat in a motto
The crowd shouted vivat as the new leader entered the hall.
In old university ceremonies, students would often chant vivat to honor their professors.
The inscription on the monument reads vivat to celebrate the city's founder.
