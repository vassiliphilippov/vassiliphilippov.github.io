jam box

noun
portable stereo device (slang)
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒæm ˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈdʒam ˌbɒks / (BrE)
an informal term for a portable stereo, especially one that plays music loudly and is battery-powered

Examples

A jam box speaker
He carried his jam box to the park and played music for everyone.
In the 1980s, a jam box was the must-have accessory for music lovers.
She set her jam box on the windowsill and turned up the volume.
