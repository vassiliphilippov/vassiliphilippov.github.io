Prosecco

proper noun
Italian sparkling wine
Pronunciation: / proʊˈsɛkoʊ / (AmE), / prəˈsɛkəʊ / (BrE)
a sparkling white wine from Italy, typically served chilled and enjoyed as an aperitif
