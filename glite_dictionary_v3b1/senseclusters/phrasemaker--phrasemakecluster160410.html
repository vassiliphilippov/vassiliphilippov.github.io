phrasemaker

noun
phrase-generating tool
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪzˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈfreɪzˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a machine, computer program, or tool that automatically generates or suggests phrases, slogans, or captions

Examples

An online phrasemaker

She used an online phrasemaker to help her come up with creative captions for her photos.
Many writers find a phrasemaker useful when they need inspiration for headlines or slogans.
