enraptured

adjective
filled with intense delight or joy
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈræptʃɚd / (AmE), / ɪnˈræptʃəd / (BrE)
filled with intense delight or joy; carried away by great pleasure

Examples

To be enraptured by music
He listened enraptured to the storyteller's magical tales.
She looked enraptured by the beauty of the sunset.
The audience sat enraptured as the orchestra performed the symphony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delighted - feeling or showing great pleasure
  • antonym: miserable - feeling extremely unhappy or uncomfortable
