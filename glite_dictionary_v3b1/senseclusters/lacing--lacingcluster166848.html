lacing

noun
covertly adding drugs to a drink
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈleɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of covertly adding a drug or other harmful substance to a drink or food, typically to incapacitate, poison, or otherwise harm someone.

Examples

Lacing a drink
There have been reports of lacing drinks with fentanyl at several nightclubs last year.
She warned her friends about possible lacing of beverages at the crowded festival tents.
Investigators found evidence of lacing in multiple samples, suggesting deliberate attempts to incapacitate victims.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spike - to add a drug or alcohol to someone's drink secretly; often used for malicious intent.
