Synonyms

  • dissemination
    6.6 3/m
    dissemination spreads information and ideas widely through people and media, while diffusion describes spreading ideas or substances across a wide area.
  • spreading
    2.3 0.03/m
    spreading means extending something over a larger area or among more people, while diffusion often implies a gradual or natural mixing process.
  • dispersion
    6.5 1/m
    dispersion is the scattering of parts over space, often by force or movement, while diffusion usually means gradual mixing or spread.
  • dispersal
    5.2 2/m
    dispersal is the sending away or spreading of people, seeds, or groups, while diffusion covers broader passive spreading of things.
  • scattering
    4.2 0.2/m
    scattering shows items thrown or spread in many directions quickly, while diffusion often means slow, even movement across an area.
  • circulation
    4.0 3/m
    circulation means movement around a system or repeated flow of news or money, while diffusion means simple spreading outward over area.
