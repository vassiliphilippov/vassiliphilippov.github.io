gluteal vein

noun
vein draining the gluteal muscles
5.3
Pronunciation: / ɡluːtiəl veɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡluːtɪəl veɪn / (BrE)
a vein that drains blood from the gluteal muscles on each side of the body

Examples

Gluteal vein blood flow
A blockage in the gluteal vein can lead to swelling and discomfort in the buttock area.
Imaging revealed an abnormal clot within the patient's gluteal vein.
The surgeon carefully avoided the gluteal vein during the hip procedure.
