landowning

adjective
owning land
Pronunciation: / ˈlændˌoʊnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlændˌəʊnɪŋ / (BrE)
owning land or property, especially as a notable or defining characteristic

Examples

Landowning families
The landowning families controlled much of the countryside for generations.
He came from a landowning background, which influenced his political views.
In the past, only the landowning citizens were allowed to vote in local elections.

Related Terms

  • antonym: landless - not owning any land
  • synonym: propertied - owning property, especially land
