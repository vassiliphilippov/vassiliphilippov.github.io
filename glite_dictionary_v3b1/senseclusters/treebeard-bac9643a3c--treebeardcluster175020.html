Treebeard

proper noun
fictional Ent leader
Pronunciation: / ˈtriːˌbɪrd / (AmE), / ˈtriːˌbɪəd / (BrE)
a fictional tree-like character, leader of the Ents, in J.R.R. Tolkien's 'The Lord of the Rings'

Examples

Treebeard character
In the forest of Fangorn, the hobbits first meet Treebeard and learn about the Ents.
Treebeard leads the Ents in their march against Saruman's fortress at Isengard.
Many fans consider Treebeard one of the most memorable characters in Tolkien's stories.
