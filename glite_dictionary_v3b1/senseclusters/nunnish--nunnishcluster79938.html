nunnish

adjective
like a nun
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈnʌnɪʃ / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a nun, especially in appearance, dress, or behavior

Examples

nunnish dress style

He commented on her nunnish clothing, which was plain and covered most of her body.
The room had a nunnish simplicity, with only the bare essentials for living.
