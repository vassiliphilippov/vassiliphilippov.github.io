yad

noun
Torah reading pointer
Pronunciation: / jɑd / (AmE), / jɑːd / (BrE)
a pointer, often made of silver, used to follow the text when reading from a Torah scroll

Examples

Silver yad pointer
The rabbi used a silver yad to read from the Torah.
During the ceremony, the child carefully moved the yad along the sacred text.
A beautifully decorated yad was presented to the synagogue as a gift.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pointer - an object used to indicate or follow text
