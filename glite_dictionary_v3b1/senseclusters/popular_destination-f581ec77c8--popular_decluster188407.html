popular destination

noun
a place frequently visited by tourists
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːpjələr ˌdɛstəˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈpɒpjʊlə ˌdɛstɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
a well-known place that attracts many visitors, especially tourists

Examples

A popular destination
During the summer, the national park is a popular destination for families.
Paris is a popular destination for honeymooners from around the world.
The island has become a popular destination for adventure seekers and backpackers.
