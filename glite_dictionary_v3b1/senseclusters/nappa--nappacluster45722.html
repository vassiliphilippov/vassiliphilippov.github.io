nappa

noun
soft full-grain leather
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈnæpə / (AmE), / ˈnæpə / (BrE)
a soft, full-grain leather made from unsplit animal hide, commonly used in clothing or upholstery

Examples

Nappa leather jacket
The designer jacket was made from high-quality nappa for a luxurious feel.
She preferred nappa seats in her car because they were soft and comfortable.
Many luxury handbags use nappa to achieve a smooth, elegant finish.
