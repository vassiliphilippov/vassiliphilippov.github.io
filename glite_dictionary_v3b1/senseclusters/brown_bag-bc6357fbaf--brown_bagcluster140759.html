brown bag

noun
simple paper bag for carrying items
Pronunciation: / braʊn bæɡ / (AmE), / braʊn bæɡ / (BrE)
a simple paper bag, typically of brown color, used for carrying items such as packed lunches.

Examples

A brown bag lunch
He always brought his sandwich to work in a brown bag.
Schools often encourage students to use a brown bag for their lunch.
She handed me a brown bag filled with snacks for the trip.
