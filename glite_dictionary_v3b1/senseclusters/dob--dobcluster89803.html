dob

noun
small amount of substance
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɑb / (AmE), / dɒb / (BrE)
a small amount or dab of a substance, such as cream or paint, applied in one spot

Examples

A dob of cream

The artist added a dob of blue paint to the corner of the canvas.
He used just a dob of glue to fix the broken piece.
