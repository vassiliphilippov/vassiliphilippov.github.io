strong gale

noun
very strong wind (Beaufort force 9)
4.9
Pronunciation: / strɔŋ ɡeɪl / (AmE), / strɒŋ ɡeɪl / (BrE)
A wind with a speed of 47 to 54 knots, corresponding to force 9 on the Beaufort scale.

Examples

A strong gale
A strong gale lashed the coastline, forcing fishermen to stay ashore.
During the night, a strong gale caused several trees to topple over.
The weather forecast warned of a strong gale approaching from the north.
