untrainable

adjective
cannot be taught
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtreɪnəbl̩ /; / ʌnˈtreɪnəbl / (AmE), / ʌnˈtreɪnəbl̩ /; / ʌnˈtreɪnəbl / (BrE)
impossible or extremely difficult to teach or train; not able to be trained or used for training

Some animals are considered untrainable due to their unpredictable behavior.
If the data is too noisy, the model may become untrainable and fail to learn any patterns.
