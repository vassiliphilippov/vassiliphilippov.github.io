henpeck

noun
dominated spouse
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhen.pek/ (AmE), /ˈhen.pek/ (BrE)
a person, especially a man, who is constantly criticized or controlled by their partner, usually their wife

Examples

A henpeck husband
He was known as a henpeck because his wife made all the decisions at home.
Some people unfairly label quiet husbands as a henpeck in their social circles.
The movie portrayed the main character as a classic henpeck who never stood up for himself.

Related Terms

  • antonym: domineering - asserting one's will over another in an arrogant way
  • synonym: pushover - a person who is easily influenced or controlled by others
