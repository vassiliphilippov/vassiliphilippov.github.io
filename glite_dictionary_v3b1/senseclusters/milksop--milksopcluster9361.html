milksop

noun
timid or weak man or boy
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlkˌsɑp / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkˌsɒp / (BrE)
a man or boy who is considered weak, timid, or lacking in courage or assertiveness

Examples

A milksop man
Although he was once seen as a milksop, he later proved his bravery.
Everyone called him a milksop because he never stood up for himself.
The villain mocked his opponent as a milksop who was afraid of a real fight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coward - someone who lacks courage
  • antonym: hero - a person noted for courageous acts
