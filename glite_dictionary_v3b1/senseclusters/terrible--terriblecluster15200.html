Synonyms

  • horrible

  • horrid
    4.1 3/m
    horrid feels old-fashioned and strongly unpleasant, while terrible is more common and used in many situations.
  • dreadful

  • horrendous
    7.0 4/m

  • atrocious
    7.0 1/m

  • abysmal

  • wretched
    5.7 0.6/m

  • woeful

  • deplorable
