quantum mechanics

noun
branch of physics studying atomic and subatomic systems
4.4 4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkwɑːn.təm mə.ˈkæn.ɪks/ (AmE), /ˈkwɒn.təm mə.ˈkæn.ɪks/ (BrE)
