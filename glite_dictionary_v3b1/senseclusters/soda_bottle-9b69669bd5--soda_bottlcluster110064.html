soda bottle

noun
bottle for soft drinks
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊdə ˌbɑt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊdə ˌbɒtl̩ / (BrE)
A bottle designed for containing soft drinks such as soda.

Examples

A soda bottle
