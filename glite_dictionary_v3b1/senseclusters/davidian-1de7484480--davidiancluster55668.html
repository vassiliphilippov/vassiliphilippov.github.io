Davidian

adjective
of King David
Pronunciation: / deɪˈvɪdiən / (AmE), / deɪˈvɪdiən / (BrE)
relating to the biblical King David, his dynasty, or qualities associated with him.

Examples

Davidian kingship
The Davidian tradition played a significant role in shaping ancient Israelite culture.
Many psalms are considered to have Davidian origins due to their themes and style.
The historian wrote about the Davidian line of kings in his latest book.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Davidic - relating to King David or his lineage
