fixer-upper

noun
house needing repair, low price
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪksər ˈʌpər / (AmE), / ˌfɪksə ˈʌpə / (BrE)
A house or other dwelling in need of repair, typically sold at a low price.

Examples

A fixer-upper house
After searching for weeks, she finally found a charming fixer-upper within her budget.
They bought a fixer-upper and spent months renovating it themselves.
This neighborhood is full of fixer-uppers with lots of potential for investors.
