turn on

verb
to excite or stimulate someone
1.0
Pronunciation: / tɝn ɑn / (AmE), / tɜːn ɒn / (BrE)
to make someone feel excited, stimulated, or aroused (emotionally, mentally, or sexually)

He said that confidence in a partner can turn on both men and women.
That scene in the movie really turns on some viewers.
