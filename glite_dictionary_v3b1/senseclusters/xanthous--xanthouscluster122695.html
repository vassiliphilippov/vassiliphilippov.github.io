xanthous

adjective
yellowish or egg-yolk colored
Pronunciation: / ˈzænθəs / (AmE), / ˈzanθəs / (BrE)
yellow or yellowish in color; resembling the color of an egg yolk or the hue between green and orange on the spectrum

The painter chose a xanthous hue to brighten the living room walls.
