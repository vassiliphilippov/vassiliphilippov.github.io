crossover

noun
car-based SUV-like vehicle
Pronunciation: / c8kr54scco8av5a / (AmE), / c8kr52scc598av59 / (BrE)
a passenger vehicle built on a car platform that combines features of a car and a sport-utility vehicle, typically offering higher seating position, flexible cargo space, and car-like handling.

Examples

Crossover vehicle
The family chose a crossover for its efficiency and ample cargo space.
Manufacturers now offer several compact crossovers with hybrid engines and advanced safety features.
She parked the sporty crossover under the tree before meeting her friends downtown.

Related Terms

  • synonym: SUV - a sport-utility vehicle combining passenger car and off-road features; often used interchangeably with 'crossover' in everyday speech.
