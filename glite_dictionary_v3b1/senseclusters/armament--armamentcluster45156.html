armament

noun
military weapons and equipment
7.0 1.66/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…ërm…ôm…ônt / (AmE), / Àà…ëÀêm…ôm…ônt / (BrE)
military weapons and equipment used by a country or armed force

Examples

Modern armament
Many countries prioritize the modernization of their military armament to maintain security.
Naval armament was a key factor in the outcome of the battle.
The country increased its armament in response to growing regional tensions.

Synonyms

  • arms
    2.8 0.06/m
    arms are weapons and military kit regarded together, while armament names the military weapons and equipment used by a country.
  • weaponry
    4.2 4.23/m
    weaponry refers to all the weapons a group uses, while armament means the military weapons and equipment of a country or force.
  • munition
    7.6 2.35/m
    munition covers weapons, ammunition, and supplies used in fighting, while armament focuses on a country's full military weapons and equipment.
  • arsenal
    5.2 4.47/m
    arsenal names a country's stockpile or place for weapons and ammunition, while armament means the wider military weapons and equipment.
  • implements of war
    7.0 0.04/m
    implements of war are objects or tools used in fighting, while armament refers to a state's weapons and military equipment.
