laborist

noun
supporter of labor movement
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈleɪ.bə.rɪst/ (AmE), /ˈleɪ.bə.rɪst/ (BrE)
a person who supports or is associated with labor movements or labor political parties

Examples

Laborist party member
The laborist spoke passionately about improving working conditions for all employees.
As a dedicated laborist, she attended every union rally in her city.
Many laborists believe in the importance of collective bargaining for fair wages.
