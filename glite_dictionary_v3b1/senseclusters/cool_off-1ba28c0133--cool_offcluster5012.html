Synonyms

  • cool down
    2.5 0.48/m
    cool down means becoming less hot or less emotionally intense, while cool off often implies a casual or gradual calming.
  • cool
    4.1 4.65/m
    cool means to lose heat or feeling slowly in many contexts, while cool off focuses on calming or reducing strong heat.
  • calm down
    1.4 14/m
    calm down means to become less angry or excited, while cool off can also mean becoming less hot physically.
  • simmer down
    4.1 0.36/m
    simmer down tells someone to become less angry slowly, while cool off can be used for both heat and emotion.
  • cool it
    6.2 1.09/m
    cool it is an informal command to stop being noisy or angry, while cool off describes becoming calmer by oneself.
  • chill out
    4.9 1.94/m
    chill out means relax and stop worrying in an informal way, while cool off also refers to losing heat as well.
  • settle down
    2.5 2.64/m
    settle down means become calm or peaceful for a longer time, while cool off usually means a quicker drop in heat or anger.
