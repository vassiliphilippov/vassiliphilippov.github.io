vocationalization

noun
making education job-focused
8.2
Pronunciation: /voʊ.keɪ.ʃə.nəl.aɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /vəʊ.keɪ.ʃə.nəl.aɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of making education or training more focused on specific jobs or practical skills

Examples

The vocationalization of education
Many countries are increasing the vocationalization of their high school curricula to meet labor market needs.
The vocationalization of education can help students gain practical skills for immediate employment.
Debates continue about the benefits and drawbacks of the vocationalization of university programs.
