zipless

adjective
without a zipper
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈzɪpləs / (AmE), / ˈzɪpləs / (BrE)
not having a zipper or zip fastener, especially on clothing or bags

Examples

Zipless jacket
He preferred zipless jackets because they were easier to put on quickly.
The designer released a new line of zipless handbags for the summer season.
Many children find zipless clothing more comfortable and simple to wear.

Related Terms

  • antonym: zippered - having a zipper or zip fastener
