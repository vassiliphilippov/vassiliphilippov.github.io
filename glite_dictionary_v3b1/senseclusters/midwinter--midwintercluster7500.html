midwinter

noun
the middle of winter
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪdˈwɪntər / (AmE), / ˌmɪdˈwɪntə / (BrE)
The middle part of the winter season, often considered the coldest period.

Examples

Cold midwinter days
During midwinter, daylight hours are at their shortest.
Snow fell heavily throughout much of midwinter that year.
We celebrated the festival in the depths of midwinter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: midsummer - the middle of summer
