cyclostyle

noun
duplicating device for cutting stencils
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪkləˌstaɪl / (AmE), / ˈsɪkləʊstaɪl / (BrE)
a device consisting of a writing implement with a small toothed wheel, used to cut holes in a stencil for duplicating documents

Examples

A cyclostyle machine
Before the advent of modern photocopiers, offices often relied on the cyclostyle for document duplication.
He demonstrated how the cyclostyle made rapid copies by cutting fine holes in wax paper.
The secretary used a cyclostyle to prepare stencils for the newsletter.
