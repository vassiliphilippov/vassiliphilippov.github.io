nonprovisional

adjective
not temporary
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnprəˈvɪʒənəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnprəˈvɪʒənəl / (BrE)
not temporary or preliminary; fully established, official, or final in status

Examples

A nonprovisional patent
The company filed a nonprovisional patent application for its invention.
A nonprovisional agreement was signed, making the terms legally binding.
Once the nonprovisional status is granted, the process cannot be reversed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: final - coming at the end; last or ultimate
  • antonym: provisional - temporary or serving for the time being
