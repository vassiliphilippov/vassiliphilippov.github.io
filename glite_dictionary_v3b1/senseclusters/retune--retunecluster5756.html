retune

verb
to adjust frequency
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈtuːn / (AmE), / ˌriːˈtjuːn / (BrE)
to adjust the settings or frequency of a device, especially a radio or television, to improve performance or reception

Examples

To retune the TV
If your television loses channels, you may need to retune it to restore them.
The mechanic had to retune the engine for better performance after the repairs.
Please retune your radio to the new frequency to continue listening to the broadcast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adjust - to change something slightly to make it better or more suitable
  • synonym: tune - to adjust a device to receive a particular signal or frequency
