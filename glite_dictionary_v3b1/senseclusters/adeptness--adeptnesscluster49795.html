adeptness

noun
skill or proficiency
Pronunciation: / əˈdɛptnəs / (AmE), / əˈdɛptnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being highly skilled or proficient at something

Examples

His adeptness with computers
His adeptness with computers made him an invaluable member of the team.
She demonstrated remarkable adeptness at solving complex mathematical problems.
The chef’s adeptness in the kitchen impressed all the guests.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ineptitude - lack of skill or ability
  • synonym: proficiency - a high degree of competence or skill; expertise
