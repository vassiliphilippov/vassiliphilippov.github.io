nodulation

noun
formation of nodules
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑdʒəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒdjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of forming or introducing rounded nodules, especially in metals such as cast iron during manufacturing

Examples

Nodulation in cast iron
Engine blocks made from ductile iron owe their strength to controlled nodulation during casting.
The metallurgist explained how nodulation improves the durability of certain alloys.
Proper nodulation is essential for achieving the desired properties in cast iron products.
