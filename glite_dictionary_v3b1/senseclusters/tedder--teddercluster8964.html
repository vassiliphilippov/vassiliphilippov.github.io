tedder

noun
hay-spreading machine
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛdər / (AmE), / ˈtɛdə / (BrE)
a machine used to spread and turn hay in a field to help it dry evenly

Examples

Hay tedder machine
The farmer used a tedder to help the hay dry evenly.
After cutting the grass, they ran the tedder across the field to speed up drying.
Modern tedders can cover large areas quickly, making hay production more efficient.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baler - a machine that compresses hay into bales
  • synonym: rake - a tool or machine for gathering or smoothing hay or grass
