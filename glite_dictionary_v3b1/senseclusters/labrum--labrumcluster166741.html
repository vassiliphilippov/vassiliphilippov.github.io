labrum

noun
outer shell rim
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪbrəm / (AmE), / ˈleɪbrəm / (BrE)
the outer lip or rim of the opening of a gastropod or other mollusc shell

Examples

Snail shell labrum
The labrum of the snail shell was smooth and slightly curved.
Collectors often examine the labrum to identify different species of molluscs.
Damage to the labrum can affect the overall appearance of the shell.
