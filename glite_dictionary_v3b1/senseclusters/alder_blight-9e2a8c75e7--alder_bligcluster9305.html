alder blight

noun
plant disease of alders caused by aphids
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔldɚ blaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɔːldə blaɪt / (BrE)
A disease of alder trees caused by the woolly alder aphid, resulting in damage to the plant.

Examples

Alder blight disease
After a summer of heavy infestation, several trees died due to persistent alder blight.
Gardeners are encouraged to inspect their alders regularly to prevent alder blight from spreading.
The forester noticed a severe case of alder blight affecting the young trees along the riverbank.
