rez

verb
to revive in game
Pronunciation: / rɛz / (AmE), / rɛz / (BrE)
to restore a character or player to life in a video game after they have died

Examples

To rez a player
Can you rez me before the next wave of enemies arrives?
The healer quickly rezzed the fallen teammate during the boss fight.
If you die, wait for someone to rez you instead of respawning.
