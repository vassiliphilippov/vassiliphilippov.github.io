exceptionless

adjective
without exceptions
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsɛpʃənləs / (AmE), / ɪkˈsɛpʃənləs / (BrE)
having no exceptions; applying in every case without deviation

Examples

Exceptionless rule
The new policy is exceptionless and must be followed by everyone in the company.
Grammar rules in this chapter are exceptionless, making them easier to learn.
He admired the exceptionless fairness of her decisions in every situation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absolute - not limited or restricted; total
  • antonym: conditional - subject to one or more conditions or exceptions
  • synonym: universal - applying to all cases or situations
