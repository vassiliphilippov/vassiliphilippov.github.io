lag

noun
slat of wood forming side of barrel/bucket
Pronunciation: / læɡ / (AmE), / læɡ / (BrE)
a thin, narrow slat of wood used to form the side of a barrel or bucket

Examples

Barrel lag
Each lag was carefully shaped to fit snugly against its neighbors in the barrel.
Replacing a damaged lag restored the barrel's ability to hold water.
The cooper inspected the lags for cracks before assembling the bucket.
