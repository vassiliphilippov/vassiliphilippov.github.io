loyally

adverb
in a loyal manner
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔɪ.ə.li / (AmE), / ˈlɔɪ.ə.li / (BrE)
In a manner that shows loyalty or faithfulness.

Examples

To stand loyally by
Despite the setbacks, he remained loyally by his friend's side.
She served her country loyally throughout her entire career.
The employees supported the company loyally during difficult times.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disloyally - in a manner that lacks loyalty
  • synonym: faithfully - in a manner that shows loyalty or devotion
