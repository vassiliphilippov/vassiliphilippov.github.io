boi

noun
informal for boy
9.7
Pronunciation: / bɔɪ / (AmE), / bɔɪ / (BrE)
an informal or playful way to refer to a boy, young man, or friend, often used online or in text messages

Examples

Hey boi!
Hey boi, are you coming to the party tonight?
The meme featured a dog with the caption 'here come dat boi!'
She greeted her friend with a cheerful 'what's up, boi?' as he walked in.

  • antonym: girl - a female child or young woman
