sodalite

noun
a blue mineral found in igneous rocks
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊdəˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈsəʊdəˌlaɪt / (BrE)
a glassy blue or blue-violet mineral consisting of sodium aluminum silicate and sodium chloride, found in igneous rocks

Examples

Blue sodalite mineral
Sodalite is popular with collectors due to its striking blue color.
Some igneous rocks contain veins or crystals of sodalite embedded within them.
