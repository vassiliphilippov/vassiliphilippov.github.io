bistered

adjective
colored with bister pigment
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪstərd / (AmE), / ˈbɪstəd / (BrE)
colored with, or as if with, bister; having a brownish-yellow or grayish-brown tint.

Examples

Bistered brown color
A bistered wash gave the landscape painting a warm, antique feeling.
The ancient parchment appeared bistered after centuries of exposure to smoke and dust.
The once-white ceiling became bistered from years of candle smoke.

Related Terms
