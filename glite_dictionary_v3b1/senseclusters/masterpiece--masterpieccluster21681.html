Synonyms

  • masterwork
    5.4 0.73/m
    masterwork is a general English term for an outstanding creation, while masterpiece names someone's best or most famous work.
  • magnum opus
    8.3 0.36/m
    magnum opus refers to the single greatest work by someone, while masterpiece can mean any outstanding or celebrated creation.
  • chef-d'oeuvre
    9.9 0.02/m
    chef-d'oeuvre is a borrowed art term used in museums, while masterpiece is the common everyday word for an outstanding work.
