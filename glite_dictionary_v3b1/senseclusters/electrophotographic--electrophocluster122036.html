electrophotographic

adjective
printing with electrical charges
8.3
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˌfoʊtəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˌfəʊtəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to a printing process that uses electrical charges to create images on a surface

Examples

Electrophotographic printing process
The company invested in new electrophotographic printers to improve print quality and speed.
An electrophotographic process is commonly used in modern photocopiers and laser printers.
She studied the electrophotographic method to understand how images are formed using static electricity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: digital - relating to technology that uses digital signals or data
  • synonym: xerographic - relating to a dry photocopying process using electrostatic charges
