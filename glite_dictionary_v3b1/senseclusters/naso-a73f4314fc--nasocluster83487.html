Naso

proper noun
Italian surname
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːsoʊ / (AmE), / ˈnɑːsəʊ / (BrE)
an Italian family name, historically associated with figures such as the Roman poet Publius Ovidius Naso

Examples

Naso family name
Her grandmother proudly spoke about the Naso surname and their ancestors from Sicily.
The famous Roman poet Ovid was born Publius Ovidius Naso.
Several families in southern Italy still carry the name Naso today.
