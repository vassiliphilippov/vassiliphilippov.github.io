put down

verb
to place something down; to set down
1.0
Pronunciation: / pʊt daʊn / (AmE), / pʊt daʊn / (BrE)
to place something in a particular position by lowering it from your hands

Examples

To put down the bag
Could you put down the box on that table, please?
He put down his cup of coffee and looked out the window.
She put down her bag on the floor and sighed with relief.
He carefully put down his glass on the table before speaking.
Please put down the boxes next to the door.
She put down the sleeping baby on the bed without waking her.
