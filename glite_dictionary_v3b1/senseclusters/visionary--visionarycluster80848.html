visionary

noun
person with great foresight or imagination
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪʒəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒənəri / (BrE)
a person who has the ability to imagine or plan the future with great foresight or creativity

Examples

A visionary leader
As a visionary, she introduced ideas that were decades ahead of her time.
Many successful tech entrepreneurs are considered modern-day visionaries.
The artist was labeled a visionary for his innovative approach to sculpture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dreamer - a person who dreams or is lost in thought; sometimes used for someone with impractical ideas
  • antonym: realist - a person who accepts a situation as it is and deals with it accordingly
