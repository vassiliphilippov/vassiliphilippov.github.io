denigrator

noun
unfair critic
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛnɪˌɡreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈdɛnɪˌɡreɪtə / (BrE)
a person who unfairly criticizes or belittles someone or something

Examples

A denigrator of ideas
Despite the harsh words of the denigrator, the team remained confident in their project.
She refused to let any denigrator undermine her achievements.
Online forums sometimes attract a denigrator who tries to discourage others.

Related Terms

  • synonym: critic - a person who expresses disapproval or finds fault
  • antonym: supporter - a person who approves of or defends someone or something
