Inclined to dispute or argue; prone to disagreement, often in a contentious manner.

Examples

A disputative person
Despite his disputative reputation, he managed to settle the issue peacefully.
Her disputative manner often led to heated debates at work.
The meeting dragged on due to several disputative participants unwilling to compromise.

  • argumentative
    5.3 1.04/m
    argumentative often speaks up to challenge others and starts arguments, while disputative more generally means prone to disagree or argue.
  • contentious
    7.4 5.03/m

  • quarrelsome

  • dissentious
