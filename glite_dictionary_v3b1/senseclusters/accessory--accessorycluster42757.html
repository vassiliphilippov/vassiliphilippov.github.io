accessory

adjective
supplementary or additional support
Pronunciation: / əkˈsɛsəri / (AmE), / əkˈsɛsəri / (BrE)
providing additional support or help, but not essential; supplementary.

Examples

Accessory wires
In this design, the accessory wires are hidden for a cleaner appearance.
Most modern cars have accessory systems to support comfort and safety features.
The hospital bought an accessory generator to ensure backup power during emergencies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ancillary - providing necessary support to the primary activities
  • synonym: auxiliary - providing supplementary or additional help and support
