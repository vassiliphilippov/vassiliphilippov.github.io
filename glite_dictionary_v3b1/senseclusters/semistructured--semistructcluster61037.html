semistructured

adjective
partly organized
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈstrʌktʃɚd / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈstrʌktʃəd / (BrE)
partly organized or arranged, but not completely fixed or strictly ordered in structure

Examples

Semistructured interviews
The survey used semistructured interviews to allow for both set questions and open discussion.
Many modern databases can handle semistructured data like emails or web pages.
A semistructured approach gives employees some guidelines but also flexibility in their work.
