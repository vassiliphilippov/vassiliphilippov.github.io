dexamethasone

noun
synthetic corticosteroid drug
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛksəˈmɛθəˌsoʊn / (AmE), / ˌdɛksəˈmiːθəsəʊn / (BrE)
a synthetic corticosteroid drug used to treat inflammatory and autoimmune conditions, allergies, and certain cancers

Examples

Dexamethasone treatment
Dexamethasone is often administered before certain chemotherapy treatments to prevent side effects.
Researchers noted that high doses of dexamethasone significantly decreased inflammation in trial participants.
The doctor prescribed dexamethasone to reduce the patient's severe allergic reaction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hydrocortisone - another corticosteroid hormone, also used as a drug
  • synonym: prednisone - another synthetic corticosteroid drug
