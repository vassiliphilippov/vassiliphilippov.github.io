golf widower

noun
man left alone as wife plays golf often
8.6
Pronunciation: / ɡɑlf ˈwɪd.oʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ɡɒlf ˈwɪd.ə.ə / (BrE)
A man whose wife frequently plays golf, leaving him alone for extended periods.

Examples

A golf widower man
As a golf widower, Mark has learned to enjoy solo hobbies when his wife is away playing.
Many golf widowers find friendship with others in the same boat during golf season.
Since retirement, Tom has become something of a golf widower as his wife spends every weekend at the course.
