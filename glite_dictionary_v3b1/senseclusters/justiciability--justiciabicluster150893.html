justiciability

noun
suitability for court
Pronunciation: / dʒəˌstɪʃiəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / dʒʌˌstɪsɪəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality or condition of a matter being appropriate for a court to hear and decide as a legal issue
