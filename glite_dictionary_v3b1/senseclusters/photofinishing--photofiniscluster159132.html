photofinishing

noun
developing photographs
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊˈfɪnɪʃɪŋ /; / ˈfoʊtoʊˌfɪnɪʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊˈfɪnɪʃɪŋ /; / ˈfəʊtəʊˌfɪnɪʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or business of developing and producing finished photographs from film or digital files

Examples

Photofinishing shop
He took his old film rolls to a photofinishing shop to have them developed.
Many people now use digital services instead of traditional photofinishing labs.
The photofinishing process includes both developing the film and making prints.
We used a local photofinishing service to develop our vacation pictures from film.
Many people now do their own photofinishing at home using digital printers.
The shop specializes in high-quality photofinishing for both amateur and professional photographers.
