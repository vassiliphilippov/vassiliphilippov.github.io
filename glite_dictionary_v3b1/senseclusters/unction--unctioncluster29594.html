unction

noun
medicated ointment or salve
Pronunciation: / ˈʌŋkʃən / (AmE), / ˈʌŋkʃən / (BrE)
a semisolid preparation, often containing a medicine, applied externally to soothe irritation or as a remedy

Examples

Medicated unction for skin
A special medicated unction was prescribed to treat her skin irritation.
She rubbed the herbal unction onto his sore muscles for relief.
The doctor recommended applying a thick unction to soothe the rash.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ointment - a smooth substance applied to the skin for medical purposes
  • synonym: salve - an oily substance used to soothe or heal the skin
