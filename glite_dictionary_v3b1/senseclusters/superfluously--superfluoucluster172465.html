superfluously

adverb
in an excessive or unnecessary way
3.0 8.1
Pronunciation: / suˈpɝː.flu.əs.li / (AmE), / suːˈpɜː.flʊ.əs.li / (BrE)
In a way that is more than necessary; excessively or unnecessarily.

Examples

To superfluously explain
He superfluously explained a simple concept that everyone already understood.
She apologized superfluously after already having made her point clear.
The room was superfluously decorated, with far more flowers than needed for the occasion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: essentially - in an essential or necessary manner
  • synonym: unnecessarily - without necessity; not needed
