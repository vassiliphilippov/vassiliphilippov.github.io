war bride

noun
woman marrying serviceman during wartime
3.4 4.7
Pronunciation: / wɔr braɪd / (AmE), / wɔː braɪd / (BrE)
a woman who marries a soldier or serviceman during a war, often from another country and sometimes moving to the soldier's home country
