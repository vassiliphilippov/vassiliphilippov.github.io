squirrel cage

noun
frantic busyness with no progress
7.5 7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈskwɜːr.əl keɪdʒ/ (AmE), /ˈskwɪr.əl keɪdʒ/ (BrE)
an environment or situation filled with constant, frantic activity but offering little or no real progress

Examples

Life in a squirrel cage
After months in the squirrel cage of city life, she needed a break in the countryside.
He often compared his hectic schedule to running in a squirrel cage: always moving, but getting nowhere.
The team found themselves trapped in a squirrel cage of endless meetings and paperwork.
