sullen

adjective
bad-tempered or gloomy and silent
6.2 3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈsʌl.ən/ (AmE), /ˈsʌl.ən/ (BrE)
Bad-tempered or gloomy and silent, showing irritation or ill humor by a gloomy silence.

Examples

The sullen teenagers refused to participate in the family activities all weekend.
