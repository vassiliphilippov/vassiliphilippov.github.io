sullen

adjective
bad-tempered or gloomy and silent
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌlən / (AmE), / ˈsʌl.ən / (BrE)
Bad-tempered or gloomy and silent, showing irritation or ill humor by a gloomy silence.

Examples

Sullen teenager
After the argument, he sat in the corner with a sullen expression on his face.
Her sullen silence made it clear that she was still upset about the decision.
The sullen teenagers refused to participate in the family activities all weekend.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerful - noticeably happy and optimistic
  • synonym: morose - having a sullen and gloomy disposition
