garpike

noun
long-snouted North American fish
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrˌpaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːˌpaɪk / (BrE)
A large, primitive predatory fish native to North America, with a long snout and hard, diamond-shaped scales.
