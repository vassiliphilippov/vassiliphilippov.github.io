noninflationary

adjective
without causing inflation
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnɪnˈfleɪʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪnˈfleɪʃənəri / (BrE)
not causing or associated with an increase in prices or inflation

Examples

Noninflationary policies
The government introduced noninflationary policies to keep the cost of living stable.
Experts praised the noninflationary growth achieved by the country's economy last year.
A noninflationary environment is ideal for long-term investment and planning.
