patchboard

noun
board for connecting circuits with patchcords
Pronunciation: / ˈpætʃˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈpætʃbɔːd / (BrE)
a board used in telecommunications or electronics, equipped with multiple sockets, where circuits can be connected and arranged using patchcords

Examples

An old patchboard
Old telephone exchanges often used a patchboard to manually route calls.
She labeled each connection on the patchboard for easy troubleshooting.
The technician connected the cables to the patchboard to complete the telephone circuit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: switchboard - a board with switches and connections for routing telephone calls or electrical circuits
