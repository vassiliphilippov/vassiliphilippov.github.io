asymmetry

noun
lack of symmetry
4.6 5.2
Pronunciation: /eɪ.ˈsɪm.ə.triː/ /; / /eɪ.ˈsɪm.ɪ.triː/ (AmE), /eɪ.ˈsɪm.ə.trɪ/ /; / /eɪ.ˈsɪm.ɪ.trɪ/ (BrE)
The absence of symmetry; the quality of having parts or aspects that are not the same in shape, size, or arrangement.

Examples

Body asymmetry
Biologists study asymmetry in animal body structures to understand evolutionary patterns.
The artist used asymmetry in her sculpture to create a sense of movement.
There is a clear asymmetry between the two sides of the design.
