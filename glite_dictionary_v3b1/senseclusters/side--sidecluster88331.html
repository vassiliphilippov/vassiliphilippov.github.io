side

noun
left or right half of the body
Pronunciation: / saɪd / (AmE), / saɪd / (BrE)
either the left or right half of the human or animal body, especially between the ribs and the hip

Examples

Pain in the side
After running for an hour, she felt a sharp pain in her side.
He carried the heavy bag pressed against his right side.
The doctor asked him to lie on his left side during the examination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flank - the side of a person's or animal's body between the ribs and the hip
