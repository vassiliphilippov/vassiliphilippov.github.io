antimiscegenation

adjective
opposed to interracial marriage
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪmɪˌsɛdʒəˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌæntɪmɪˌsɛdʒəˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
opposed to marriage or sexual relations between people of different races

Examples

Antimiscegenation laws
Many states once had antimiscegenation laws that prohibited interracial marriage.
The activist spoke out against antimiscegenation policies in the early twentieth century.
Public opinion on antimiscegenation has changed significantly over the past fifty years.

Related Terms

  • antonym: miscegenation - the mixing of people of different races through marriage or relationships
  • synonym: segregationist - supporting the separation of people by race
