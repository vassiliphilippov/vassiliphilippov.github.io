clangor

noun
loud resonant noise
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈklæŋər / (AmE), / ˈklæŋə / (BrE)
a loud, continuous, and resonant noise, often a repeated or metallic clang

Examples

The clangor of bells

She woke suddenly to the clangour of pots and pans in the kitchen below.

  • synonym: din - a loud, unpleasant, and prolonged noise
  • antonym: silence - absence of any sound or noise
