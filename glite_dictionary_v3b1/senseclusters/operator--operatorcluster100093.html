operator

noun
shrewd or unscrupulous manipulator
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɑː.pəˌreɪ.t̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈɒp.ər.eɪ.tə / (BrE)
a person, often shrewd or unscrupulous, who skilfully manages to get what they want, especially by circumventing difficulties or rules

Examples

A clever operator
He's a real operator, always finding a way around the system.
In politics, you have to be an operator to survive.
The company hired an operator to handle the tough negotiations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: innocent - a person lacking experience or guile; opposite of a shrewd operator
  • synonym: manipulator - a person who controls or influences others cleverly or unscrupulously
  • synonym: schemer - a person who forms secret or underhand plans
