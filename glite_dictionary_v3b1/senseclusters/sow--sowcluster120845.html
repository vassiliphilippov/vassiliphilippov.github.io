sow

noun
adult female pig
4.9
Pronunciation: / saʊ / (AmE), / saʊ / (BrE)
an adult female pig, especially one that is kept for breeding

Examples

A mother sow
Farmers often separate the sow from other pigs before she farrows.
The sow gave birth to a large litter of piglets on the farm.
The veterinarian examined the sow to ensure she was healthy during her pregnancy.
