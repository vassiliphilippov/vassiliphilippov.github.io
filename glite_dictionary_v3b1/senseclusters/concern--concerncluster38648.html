Synonyms

  • have to do with
    2.7 30/m
    have to do with is a plain phrase meaning something is connected or related, while concern is the formal verb for being relevant.
  • relate
    2.2 15/m
    relate means to have a connection or link in general, while concern means to be relevant or to have to do with.
  • pertain
    4.6 5/m
    pertain is a more formal verb used to show something is relevant to a subject, while concern is common in everyday speech.
  • refer
    3.0 5/m
    refer usually means to be about or to point to something directly, while concern means simply to be related or relevant.
  • bear on
    5.1 1/m
    bear on is a formal phrase that often suggests having an effect or influence, while concern means to be relevant or related.
