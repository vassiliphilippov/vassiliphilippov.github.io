evaporitic

adjective
formed by evaporation
Pronunciation: / ɪˌvæpəˈrɪtɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌvæpəˈrɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or formed by the evaporation of water, especially resulting in the formation of minerals or rocks

Examples

Evaporitic mineral deposits
The region is known for its evaporitic deposits, which include large amounts of salt and gypsum.
Scientists study evaporitic rocks to understand ancient climates and water bodies.
Many evaporitic minerals are found in arid environments where lakes have dried up.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sedimentary - relating to rocks formed by the accumulation of sediments
