unsuspended

adjective
not in suspension
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnsəˈspɛndɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnsəˈspɛndɪd / (BrE)
not held in suspension in a liquid or gas; settled or separated from the surrounding medium

Examples

Unsuspended particles
The solid particles were unsuspended after the solution was left to settle overnight.
Clear water indicates that the impurities are now unsuspended and have settled at the bottom.
In the experiment, the dye remained unsuspended and quickly separated from the liquid.

Related Terms

  • antonym: suspended - kept floating or dispersed in a liquid or gas
