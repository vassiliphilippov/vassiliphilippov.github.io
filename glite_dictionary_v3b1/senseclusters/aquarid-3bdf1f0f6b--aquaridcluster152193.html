Aquarid

adjective
of Aquarids meteor showers
Pronunciation: / ˈækwərɪd / (AmE), / ˈakwərɪd / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the Aquarids meteor showers, such as the Eta Aquarids or Delta Aquarids.

Examples

Aquarid meteor shower
The Aquarid display was especially bright this year, attracting many stargazers to the countryside.
Scientists observed an Aquarid meteor streaking across the early morning sky.
Many photographers captured stunning images of the Aquarid shower during its peak.
