Gerardus Mercator

proper noun
Flemish geographer and creator of Mercator projection
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈrɑːrdəs mərˈkeɪtər / (AmE), / dʒəˈrɑːrdəs məˈkɑːtə / (BrE)
(1512–1594) A Flemish geographer and cartographer, best known for inventing the Mercator projection map.

Examples

Gerardus Mercator projection map
Gerardus Mercator revolutionized cartography by developing a new way to project the globe onto a flat map.

The work of Gerardus Mercator still influences modern navigation and mapmaking techniques.
