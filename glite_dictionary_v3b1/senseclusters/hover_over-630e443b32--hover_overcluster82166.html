hover over

verb
hold cursor over a UI element
3.5 0.32/m
Pronunciation: / \u02c8h\u028cv\u025a \u02c8o\u028av\u025a / (AmE), / \u02c8h\u028cv\u0259 \u02c8\u0259\u028av\u0259 / (BrE)
to move and hold the computer pointer or cursor over a screen element (such as an icon, link, or button) so that additional information appears or an effect is triggered.

Examples

Hover over an icon
If you hover over the help icon, a tooltip appears with extra guidance.
On the website, she accidentally hovered over the menu and a submenu opened immediately.
Try to hover over the highlighted area to see the image caption without clicking.

Synonyms

  • hover
    4.1 0.90/m
    hover means placing the cursor over an element without clicking to show information, while hover over is the instruction phrase.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mouse over - to move the pointer over a UI element to trigger an event or show information; often written 'mouseover'.
