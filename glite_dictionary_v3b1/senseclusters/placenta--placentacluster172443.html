placenta

noun
placental extract used in cosmetics
7.3
Pronunciation: /plə.ˈsen.tə/ (AmE), /plə.ˈsen.tə/ (BrE)
A cosmetic ingredient consisting of extract derived from the placenta of mammals, used in skincare products for its purported regenerative or anti-ageing effects.

Examples

Placenta extract cream
Some luxury serums contain placenta extract that manufacturers claim helps reduce fine lines.
She refused to buy the cream after learning it listed placenta among its active ingredients.
Regulations in some countries restrict using animal-derived placenta in cosmetics without clear safety data.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extract - a substance obtained by removing or concentrating specific components from raw material
  • synonym: placental extract - an extract prepared from animal placenta, often used as an active ingredient in cosmetics
