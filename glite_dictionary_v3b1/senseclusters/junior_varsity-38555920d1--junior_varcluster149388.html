junior varsity

noun
school or college team below varsity level
4.5 0.16/m
Pronunciation: / Àåd íuÀêni…ôr Ààv…ëÀêrs…ôti / (AmE), / Àåd íuÀêni…ô Ààv…ëÀês…ôti / (BrE)
(in North American schools and colleges) a sports team representing a school or college that competes at a level below the main varsity team.

Examples

The junior varsity team
He made the junior varsity basketball team as a freshman and hopes to move up next year.
Many students participate in junior varsity sports before reaching the varsity level.
The junior varsity players practiced right after the varsity squad was finished.
