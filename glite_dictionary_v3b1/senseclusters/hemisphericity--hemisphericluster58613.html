hemisphericity

noun
hemispherical shape
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛmɪsfəˈrɪsəti / (AmE), / ˌhɛmɪsfəˈrɪsəti / (BrE)
the state or quality of being hemispherical in shape or having the form of half a sphere

Examples

Hemisphericity of a dome
The hemisphericity of the ancient dome made it a marvel of engineering.
Scientists measured the hemisphericity of the planet's polar ice cap.
The artist was inspired by the hemisphericity of soap bubbles in her work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flatness - the quality of being flat or level
  • synonym: sphericity - the quality of being spherical or like a sphere
