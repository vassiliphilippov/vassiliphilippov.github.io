vasa

noun
anatomical tubes
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪsə / (AmE), / ˈvɑːsə / (BrE)
the plural form of 'vas', referring to tubes or ducts in the body, especially in anatomy

Examples

The vasa in body

Damage to the vasa can affect the transport of important bodily fluids.
In anatomy class, we studied the structure and function of various vasa in the human body.
