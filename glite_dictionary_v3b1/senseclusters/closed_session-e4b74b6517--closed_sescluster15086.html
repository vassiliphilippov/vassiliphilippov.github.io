closed session

noun
private meeting not open to the public
2.8 4.5
Pronunciation: /kloʊzd ˈseʃ.ən/ (AmE), /kləʊzd ˈseʃ.ən/ (BrE)
A meeting or part of a meeting, especially of a legislative body, that is not open to the public.

Examples

A closed session
After the public hearing, the board went into a closed session to deliberate.
Journalists were not admitted to the closed session of the city council.
The committee met in a closed session to discuss matters of national security.
