housebound

adjective
unable to leave home, usually due to illness
2.5 4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhaʊs.baʊnd/ (AmE), /ˈhaʊs.baʊnd/ (BrE)
Unable to leave one's home, especially because of illness or old age.

Examples

Housebound after surgery
After her surgery, she was housebound for several weeks while she recovered.
Many housebound individuals appreciate visits from friends and family.
The organization provides meals for the elderly who are housebound.
