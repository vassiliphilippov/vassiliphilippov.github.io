propyl radical

noun
monovalent group from propane (C3H7–)
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊ.pɪl ˈræd.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ.pɪl ˈræd.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
a monovalent organic group (C3H7–) derived from propane, occurring as a substituent in many organic compounds

Examples

The propyl radical is often encountered as a substituent in organic synthesis reactions.
