Synonyms

  • give back
    1.0 14/m
    give back is used when someone returns an item by hand to its owner, while pass-back focuses on handing it back.
  • return
    1.0 47/m
    return is a general word for taking or sending something back, while pass-back stresses physically handing it back.
  • send back
    1.5 3.25/m
    send back means to send an item to where it came from, while pass-back usually means handing it personally.
  • bring back
    1.7 11/m
    bring back means to carry something back to its starting place, while pass-back means handing it to the previous holder.
  • put back
    1.0 15/m
    put back means placing something in its original position, while pass-back means giving it back to the person.
