associator

noun
group member
Pronunciation: / əˈsoʊsiˌeɪtər / (AmE), / əˈsəʊsiˌeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who is a member, supporter, or affiliate of an association or organized group

Examples

Associator in a group
After years of volunteering, he became an associator with the local historical society.
Each associator is expected to attend monthly meetings and participate in events.
The club welcomed new associators at the annual gathering last weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: affiliate - a person or organization officially attached to a larger body
  • synonym: member - a person who belongs to a group or organization
  • antonym: outsider - a person who does not belong to a particular group
