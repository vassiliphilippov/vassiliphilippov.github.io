topaz

noun
precious gemstone mineral
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊˌpæz / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.pæz / (BrE)
a hard, transparent mineral, usually yellow, brown, or clear, used as a gemstone (sometimes also refers to a yellow variety of quartz used as a gemstone)

Examples

A topaz ring
A rare blue topaz was on display in the museum's mineral collection.
She wore a necklace set with a sparkling topaz pendant.
The jeweler admired the brilliant cut of the yellow topaz.
Mineralogists classify citrine as a type of yellow quartz, but true topaz is more rare.
She wore a beautiful ring set with a yellow topaz.
The necklace was adorned with large topaz stones that sparkled in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gemstone - a precious or semi-precious stone, especially when cut and polished or engraved
  • synonym: yellow quartz - a variety of quartz with a yellow coloration
