rheumy

adjective
damp or moist, often unpleasantly so
5.3 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈruːmi / (AmE), / ˈruːmi / (BrE)
(of air, eyes, etc.) damp, moist, or wet, often in an unpleasant or unhealthy way.

Examples

Rheumy air
After a day in the rheumy fog, his clothes felt cold and damp.
Her eyes, rheumy with age, filled with tears as she spoke.
The rheumy air clung to everything in the old basement, making it smell musty.
