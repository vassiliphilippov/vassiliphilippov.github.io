netball

noun
team sport with ball and hoop
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛtˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈnɛtbɔːl / (BrE)
A team sport played by two teams of seven players, in which the objective is to score points by throwing a ball through an opponent's raised hoop.

Examples

A netball game
Many students join the netball team to improve their fitness and teamwork skills.
She played netball throughout high school and competed at regional tournaments.
The school is building new courts for basketball and netball practice.
