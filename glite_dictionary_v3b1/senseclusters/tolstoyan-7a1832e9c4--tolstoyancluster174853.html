Tolstoyan

noun
follower of Tolstoy
Pronunciation: / toʊlˈstɔɪən / (AmE), / tɒlˈstɔɪən / (BrE)
a person who follows or supports the religious, social, or philosophical beliefs of Leo Tolstoy

Examples

A Tolstoyan thinker
As a Tolstoyan, he believed in nonviolence and lived a simple life.
The Tolstoyans gathered to discuss their ideas about peace and social reform.
She became a Tolstoyan after reading Tolstoy's writings on morality and religion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anarchist - a person who believes in or tries to bring about a society without government or laws
  • synonym: pacifist - a person who believes in or promotes peace and opposes war or violence
