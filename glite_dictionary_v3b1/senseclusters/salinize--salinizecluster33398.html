salinize

verb
to add salt
Pronunciation: / ˈsæləˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsælənaɪz /; / ˈsælɪnaɪz / (BrE)
to make something salty or increase its salt content, especially soil or water

Examples

To salinize the soil
Farmers worry that improper irrigation could salinize their fields over time.
The process to salinize water is sometimes used to create salt for industrial purposes.
If you salinize the soil too much, it can harm plant growth.
Farmers must be careful not to salinize their fields through improper irrigation practices.
Over time, the use of salty water can salinize the soil and harm crops.
Some industrial processes can unintentionally salinize nearby rivers and lakes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: desalinate - to remove salt from something
  • synonym: salt - to add salt to something
