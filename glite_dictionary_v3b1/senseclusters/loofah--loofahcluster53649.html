loofah

noun
tropical climbing plant with sponge-like fruit
6.4 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàluÀê.f…ô / (AmE), / ÀàluÀê.f…ô / (BrE)
A tropical climbing plant of the cucumber family with cylindrical fruit, the fibrous interior of which is used as a sponge when mature.

Examples

Loofah plant
A mature loofah can be peeled and dried to create natural cleaning sponges.
Gardeners often harvest loofah for its unique sponge-like interior.
The loofah grows vigorously in warm climates, producing long, green fruits.
