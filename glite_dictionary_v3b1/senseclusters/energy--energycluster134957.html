energy

noun
source of usable power
Pronunciation: / ˈɛnɚdʒi / (AmE), / ˈɛnədʒi / (BrE)
a source of power or the capacity to do work, such as electricity, fuel, heat, or in physics

Examples

Solar energy
Many scientists are searching for cleaner forms of energy to combat climate change.
The country is investing in renewable energy sources like solar and wind.
Wind turbines are used to generate energy for homes and businesses.
During photosynthesis, plants convert light energy into chemical energy.
The battery stores chemical energy that can power a flashlight.
Thermal energy is transferred from the hot coffee to the surrounding air.

Related Terms

  • synonym: power - ability to do work or produce energy
  • synonym: work - the energy transferred by a force (physics)
