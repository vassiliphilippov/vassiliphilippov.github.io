nonindigenous

adjective
not native
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnɪnˈdɪdʒənəs / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪnˈdɪdʒənəs / (BrE)
not originally from a particular place; introduced from another region or country

Examples

Nonindigenous species
The government is concerned about the spread of nonindigenous species in local rivers.
Many nonindigenous plants can disrupt the natural balance of an ecosystem.
Efforts are being made to control nonindigenous animals that threaten native wildlife.

Related Terms

  • antonym: indigenous - originating or occurring naturally in a particular place
  • synonym: non-native - not originally from a particular place
