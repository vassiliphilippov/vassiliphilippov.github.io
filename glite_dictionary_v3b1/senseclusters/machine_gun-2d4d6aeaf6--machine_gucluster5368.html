machine gun

noun
rapidly firing automatic gun
1.0
Pronunciation: / məˈʃin ɡʌn / (AmE), / məˈʃiːn ɡʌn / (BrE)
A fully automatic firearm capable of rapid continuous fire, often mounted or operated by a crew.

Examples

A machine gun burst
He heard the rapid bursts of a machine gun echoing across the battlefield.
The old army jeep still had a mounted machine gun attached to its roof.
The soldiers positioned a machine gun at the entrance to defend the base.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: assault rifle - A selective-fire rifle that uses intermediate cartridges
  • degree lower: submachine gun - A portable automatic firearm designed to be held and fired with one hand
