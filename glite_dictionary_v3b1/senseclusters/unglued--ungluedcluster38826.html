unglued

adjective
not glued together
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡluːd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡluːd / (BrE)
not fastened or held together with glue; physically separated from something to which it was attached

Examples

Sticker gets unglued
The label became unglued after being exposed to water for several hours.
She noticed that the wallpaper was unglued at the corners and needed repair.
The child's craft project fell apart when the pieces got unglued.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detached - separated or disconnected
  • antonym: glued - fastened or held together with glue
