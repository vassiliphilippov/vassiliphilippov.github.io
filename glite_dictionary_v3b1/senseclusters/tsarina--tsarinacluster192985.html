tsarina

noun
wife or widow of a czar; Russian empress
Pronunciation: / zɑːˈriːnə /; / tsɑːˈriːnə / (AmE), / tsɑːˈriːnə /; / zɑːˈriːnə / (BrE)
the wife or widow of a czar, or a female czar; a Russian empress (especially in Russia before 1917).
