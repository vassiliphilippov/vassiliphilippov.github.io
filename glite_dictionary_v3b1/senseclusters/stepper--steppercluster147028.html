stepper

noun
electric motor that moves in small steps
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛpər / (AmE), / ˈstɛpə / (BrE)
a type of electric motor that moves or rotates in small, precise steps, allowing for controlled movement

Examples

Stepper motor
A stepper is ideal for applications that require precise positioning, such as 3D printers.
The robot's arm movements are powered by small steppers for accuracy.
You need a special controller to drive a stepper efficiently.
