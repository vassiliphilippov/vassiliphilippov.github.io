firing chamber

noun
part of a gun holding the cartridge before firing
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɹɪŋ ˌtʃeɪmbɚ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪərɪŋ ˌtʃeɪmbə / (BrE)
the part of a gun or firearm where the cartridge or charge is placed before firing

Examples

The firing chamber of a gun
He inspected the firing chamber to ensure there were no obstructions.
Proper maintenance of the firing chamber is essential for gun safety.
The bullet is loaded into the firing chamber before the trigger is pulled.
