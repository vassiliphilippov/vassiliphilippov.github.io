obi

noun
Japanese sash
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊbi / (AmE), / ˈəʊbi / (BrE)
a wide sash or belt worn with traditional Japanese clothing such as a kimono

Examples

A kimono obi
She chose a colorful obi to match her kimono for the festival.
The obi is often tied in elaborate knots for special occasions.
He admired the intricate patterns on her silk obi during the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kimono - a traditional Japanese garment
  • synonym: sash - a long strip of cloth worn around the waist or over the shoulder
