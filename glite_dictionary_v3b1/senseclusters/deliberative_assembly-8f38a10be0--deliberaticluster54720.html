deliberative assembly

noun
formal body for discussion and decision-making
Pronunciation: / dəˈlɪbərətɪv əˈsɛmbli / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪbərətɪv əˈsɛmbli / (BrE)
a formal gathering in which members meet to discuss and decide on matters through orderly, reasoned debate

Examples

A deliberative assembly meets
A deliberative assembly ensures that important decisions are debated before a final resolution is made.
Each club member has the right to speak and vote in the deliberative assembly.
The United Nations General Assembly is a deliberative assembly that addresses global issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assembly - a group of people gathered together for a common purpose
