seborrheic keratosis

noun
benign wart-like skin growth
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛbəˈriːɪk ˌkɛrəˈtoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌsiːbəˈriːɪk ˌkɛrəˈtəʊsɪs / (BrE)
