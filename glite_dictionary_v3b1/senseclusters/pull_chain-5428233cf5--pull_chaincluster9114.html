pull chain

noun
chain used to operate a device by pulling
6.9
Pronunciation: / pʊl tʃeɪn / (AmE), / pʊl tʃeɪn / (BrE)
a chain with a handle at the end, used to operate a mechanism when pulled, such as flushing a toilet or turning on a light
