African lily

noun
evergreen African plant with violet-blue flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈæf.rɪ.kən ˈlɪl.i / (AmE), / ˈæf.rɪ.kən ˈlɪl.i / (BrE)
A plant native to Africa, with bright green evergreen leaves and large clusters of usually violet-blue flowers.

Examples

The African lily flowers
Several African lily plants lined the pathway of the botanical garden.
She bought an African lily to add vibrant color to her yard.
The African lily is prized for its striking and colorful blooms in summer gardens.
