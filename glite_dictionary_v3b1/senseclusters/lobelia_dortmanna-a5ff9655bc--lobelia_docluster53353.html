Lobelia dortmanna

noun
a perennial aquatic herb with blue flowers
Pronunciation: / loʊˈbiːliə dɔrtˈmænə / (AmE), / ləʊˈbiːlɪə dɔːtˈmanə / (BrE)
An aquatic perennial herb native to Europe and North America, with spongy submerged leaves and racemes of blue flowers held above the water.
