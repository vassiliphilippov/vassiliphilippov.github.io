nutpick

noun
tool for extracting nuts
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌtpɪk / (AmE), / ˈnʌtpɪk / (BrE)
a small pointed tool used to extract the edible part from the shell of a nut

Examples

A metal nutpick
She carefully used a nutpick to remove the almond from its shell.
The set included a silver nutpick and a matching nutcracker for the holiday table.
After cracking the pecan, he reached for the nutpick to get the pieces out.
