intrude on

verb
enter or interfere without permission
3.3 3.7
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈtruːd ɑːn/ /; / /ɪn.ˈtruːd ən/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈtruːd ɒn/ /; / /ɪn.ˈtruːd ən/ (BrE)
