blow

verb
form by blowing air (often glass)
Pronunciation: / bloʊ / (AmE), / bləʊ / (BrE)
to form or shape something, especially glass, by forcing air into it through a tube

Examples

To blow glass
It takes years of practice to blow delicate vases with perfect symmetry.
Museum visitors watched as the craftsman expertly blew a bowl from molten glass.
The artisan demonstrated how to blow an intricate glass ornament in the workshop.
