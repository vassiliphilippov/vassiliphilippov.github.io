suggestion

noun
inducing hypnosis
1.9 7.0
Pronunciation: / səɡˈdʒɛstʃən / (AmE), / səɡˈdʒɛstʃən / (BrE)
the process by which a person is guided into a state of hypnosis through verbal or other cues

Examples

Hypnotic suggestion
Hypnotherapists often rely on verbal suggestion to achieve the desired mental state.
The therapist used gentle suggestion to help the patient enter a hypnotic trance.
Through repeated suggestion, the hypnotist was able to induce deep relaxation.
