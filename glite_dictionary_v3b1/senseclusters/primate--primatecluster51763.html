primate

noun
chief bishop or archbishop in some churches
8.7 7.8
Pronunciation: /ˈpraɪ.meɪt/ /; / /ˈpraɪ.mət/ (AmE), /ˈpraɪ.meɪt/ /; / /ˈpraɪ.mət/ (BrE)
