unselfish

adjective
not selfish or greedy
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɛl.fɪʃ / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛl.fɪʃ / (BrE)
willing to put the needs or interests of others before your own; not selfish

Examples

An unselfish person
He showed his unselfish nature by always helping friends in need.
Her unselfish act inspired everyone to volunteer for the charity project.
The team's success was due to their unselfish cooperation and support for one another.
His unselfish support allowed his friend to succeed.
It was an unselfish act to donate anonymously.
She made an unselfish decision to give her time to the community project.

  • antonym: selfish - lacking consideration for others; concerned chiefly with one's own personal profit or pleasure
