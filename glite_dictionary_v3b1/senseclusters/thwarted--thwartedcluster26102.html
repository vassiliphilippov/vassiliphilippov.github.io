thwarted

adjective
prevented or made unsuccessful
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈθwɔrtɪd / (AmE), / ˈθwɔːtɪd / (BrE)
Unsuccessful despite efforts; prevented from achieving something.

Examples

A thwarted plan
He felt thwarted in his ambition to become a professional musician.
Her thwarted hopes led her to try a different approach to the problem.
The plan was thwarted by unexpected bad weather.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foiled - prevented from succeeding
  • synonym: frustrated - feeling annoyed because of being prevented from achieving something
