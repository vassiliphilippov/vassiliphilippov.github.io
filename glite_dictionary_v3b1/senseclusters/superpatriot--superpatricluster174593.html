superpatriot

adjective
extremely patriotic
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈpeɪtriət / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈpeɪtriət / (BrE)
showing or expressing extreme or excessive patriotism, often to an unreasonable degree

Examples

Superpatriot speech
The candidate's superpatriot rhetoric sometimes alienated those who valued international cooperation.
Some critics accused the group of being superpatriot in their refusal to accept any criticism of the country.
His superpatriot attitude led him to dismiss any foreign influence as negative.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nationalistic - having strong identification with one's own nation
  • degree lower: patriotic - having or showing love for one's country
  • antonym: unpatriotic - not having or showing love for one's country
