fire chief

noun
head of a fire department
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚ tʃiːf / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə tʃiːf / (BrE)
the person in charge of a fire department, responsible for overseeing firefighting operations and administration

Examples

The fire chief arrives
After many years of service, she became the city's first female fire chief.
The fire chief coordinated the response to the apartment building blaze.
The fire chief held a press conference to update the public about the incident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: firefighter - a person whose job is to put out fires
  • synonym: police chief - head of a police department
