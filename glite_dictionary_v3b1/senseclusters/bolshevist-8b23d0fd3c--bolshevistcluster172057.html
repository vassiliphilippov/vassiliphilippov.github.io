Bolshevist

noun
member of the Bolshevik party (Lenin's followers)
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊlʃəˌvɪst / (AmE), / ˈbɒlʃəˌvɪst / (BrE)
A member of the Bolshevik party, the left-wing majority group that followed Lenin and became the Russian Communist Party.

Examples

A Bolshevist leader
After the revolution, the Bolshevist agenda quickly transformed Russian society.
He studied the theories and tactics of the Bolshevists in political science class.
The rise of the Bolshevist movement dramatically influenced global politics in the twentieth century.
