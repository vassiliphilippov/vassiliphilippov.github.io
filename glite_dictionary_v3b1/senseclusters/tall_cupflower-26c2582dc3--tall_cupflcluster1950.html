tall cupflower

noun
ornamental Chilean flowering herb
6.2
Pronunciation: / tɔl ˈkʌpˌflaʊɚ / (AmE), / tɔːl ˈkʌpˌflaʊə / (BrE)
A shrubby Chilean herb with bluish-white tubular flowers, cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Examples

The tall cupflower plant
Gardeners often plant tall cupflower to add subtle color to their borders.
The tall cupflower thrives in well-drained soils and is prized for its ornamental value.
The bluish-white blossoms of the tall cupflower attract pollinators throughout the summer.
