undisclosable

adjective
not able to be revealed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪsˈkloʊzəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪsˈkləʊzəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be revealed or made known to others

Examples

An undisclosable secret
The terms of the agreement are undisclosable to anyone outside the company.
For legal reasons, some information remains undisclosable to the public.
He explained that the client’s identity was undisclosable due to privacy laws.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confidential - meant to be kept secret or private
  • antonym: disclosable - able to be revealed or made known
  • synonym: secret - kept hidden from others
