stupid

adjective
mentally numb or dazed (temporary)
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstuː.pɪd, ˈstjuː.pɪd / (AmE), / ˈstjuː.pɪd / (BrE)
(of a person) temporarily unable to think clearly or react normally, especially as a result of shock, fatigue, or a blow; mentally numb.

Examples

Stupid with shock
After hitting his head, he sat on the ground, stupid and motionless for several minutes.
He was so stupid with exhaustion that he could hardly answer their questions.
The news left her stupid with shock, unable to process what had happened.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alert - able to think or react quickly and clearly
  • synonym: dazed - unable to think or react properly; confused
