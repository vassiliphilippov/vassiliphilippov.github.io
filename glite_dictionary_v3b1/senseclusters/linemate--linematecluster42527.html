linemate

noun
teammate on same line
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪnˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪnˌmeɪt / (BrE)
a teammate who plays on the same line as you in team sports such as hockey or soccer

Examples

Each linemate contributed to the team's impressive victory.
She celebrated the goal with her linemate on the ice.
The coach praised his linemate for setting up the perfect scoring opportunity.
During the game, she communicated constantly with her linemates to coordinate their attacks.
He became close friends with his linemate after playing together for several seasons.
