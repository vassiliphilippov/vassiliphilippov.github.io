coquettish

adjective
behaving in a flirtatious manner
Pronunciation: / koʊˈkɛtɪʃ / (AmE), / kəˈkɛtɪʃ / (BrE)
Behaving in a playful or flirtatious way to attract attention or admiration, especially in a manner characteristic of a coquette.

Examples

Coquettish glances
He found her coquettish glances both charming and mysterious.
Her coquettish smile caught everyone's attention at the party.
Wearing a coquettish dress, she danced playfully across the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flirtatious - behaving as if attracted to someone, playfully showing romantic or sexual interest
