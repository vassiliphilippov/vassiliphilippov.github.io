Hollerith card

noun
punched card for data recording
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːlərɪθ kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈhɒlərɪθ kɑːd / (BrE)
a card used to record data in the form of punched holes, once widely used in early computing and data processing
