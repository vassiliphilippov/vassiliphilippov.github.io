intranasal

adjective
through the nose
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈneɪzəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈneɪzəl / (BrE)
administered, occurring, or placed within or through the nose

Examples

Intranasal medication
The doctor prescribed an intranasal medication to help relieve her congestion.
Some vaccines are now available in intranasal form for easier administration.
He used an intranasal spray every morning during allergy season.
