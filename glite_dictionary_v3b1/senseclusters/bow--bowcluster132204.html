bow

noun
a gesture of respect or greeting by bending forward
1.0 3.17/m

an act of bending the head or upper body forward as a sign of respect, greeting, or acknowledgment

Examples

A deep bow
He gave a deep bow before the queen.
She greeted her elders with a traditional bow.

She received a standing ovation and made a graceful bow.
The actors took a final bow before leaving the stage.
