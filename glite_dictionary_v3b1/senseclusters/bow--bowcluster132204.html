bow

noun
a gesture of respect or greeting by bending forward
1.1 1.0
Pronunciation: /baʊ/ (AmE), /baʊ/ (BrE)
an act of bending the head or upper body forward as a sign of respect, greeting, or acknowledgment

Examples

A deep bow
He gave a deep bow before the queen.
She greeted her elders with a traditional bow.
With a polite bow, the conductor acknowledged the applause.
After the orchestra finished playing, the conductor gave a deep bow to the cheering audience.
She received a standing ovation and made a graceful bow.
The actors took a final bow before leaving the stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curtain call - an appearance by performers at the end of a performance to receive applause
  • synonym: curtsy - a formal gesture of respect or greeting made by women, involving bending the knees
  • degree lower: nod - a small movement of the head downward and upward, often as a greeting or sign of agreement
