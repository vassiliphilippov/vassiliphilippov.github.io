blind date

noun
arranged meeting with an unknown person
Pronunciation: / blaɪnd deɪt / (AmE), / blaɪnd deɪt / (BrE)
an arranged meeting or social engagement between two people who have not previously met, typically with the aim of starting a romantic relationship

Examples

To go on a blind date
After a disappointing blind date, he decided to try online dating instead.
She was very nervous before her first blind date last weekend.
They met on a blind date set up by mutual friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: date - a social or romantic appointment
