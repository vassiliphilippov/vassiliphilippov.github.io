Aufklärung movement

noun
18th-century rationalist intellectual movement
9.9
Pronunciation: / aʊfˈklɛrʊŋ ˈmuːvmənt / (AmE), / aʊfˈklɛərʊŋ ˈmuːvmənt / (BrE)
An intellectual movement in 18th-century Europe, particularly in Germany, emphasizing reason, individual rights, and critical thinking.
