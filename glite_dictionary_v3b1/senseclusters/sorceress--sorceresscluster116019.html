sorceress

noun
female practitioner of magic
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔrsərɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsərɪs / (BrE)
a woman who is believed to have magical powers and practices sorcery; a female sorcerer

Examples

A sorceress with powers
In the play, the main antagonist was a powerful sorceress who controlled the fate of the kingdom.
Legends often tell of a wise sorceress living deep in the forest.
The sorceress cast a mysterious spell over the village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sorcerer - a person, especially a man, who practices magic or sorcery
  • synonym: warlock - a man who practices witchcraft or magic
  • synonym: witch - a woman thought to have magical powers, especially evil ones
  • synonym: wizard - a man who is believed to have magical powers
