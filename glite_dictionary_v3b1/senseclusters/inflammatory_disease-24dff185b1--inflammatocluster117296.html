inflammatory disease

noun
disease marked by inflammation
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈflæməˌtɔri dɪˈziz / (AmE), / ɪnˈflamətəri dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
A disease characterized by inflammation of tissues or organs.

Examples

Chronic inflammatory disease
Doctors are studying the causes of chronic inflammatory diseases like Crohn's disease.
