uninflected

adjective
not inflected; monotone or unchanged in form
5.6 6.4
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ɪn.ˈflek.tɪd/ (AmE), /ʌn.ɪn.ˈflek.tɪd/ (BrE)
(of speech, words, or forms) not varied by inflection; monotonous or not altered in pitch, tone, or grammatical form.

Examples

Uninflected speech
Her uninflected speech made the lecture difficult to follow.
In some languages, many words remain uninflected regardless of their grammatical role.
The actor was criticized for delivering lines in an uninflected tone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: monotone - (of speech) having a single unvaried tone or pitch
