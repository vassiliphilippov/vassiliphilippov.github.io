countrified

adjective
having rural or rustic qualities
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌn.trəˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˈkʌn.trɪ.faɪd / (BrE)
having the characteristics or style of the countryside; rustic, rural, or unsophisticated.

Examples

A countrified style
His decorating style is charmingly countrified, with lots of wooden furniture and floral prints.
She wore a countrified dress and lived on a quiet farm lane.
They transformed the city house with countrified touches, making it cozy and relaxed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rustic - having a simple or rural quality
  • antonym: urban - characteristic of a city or town, not rural
