musicologically

adverb
in a way relating to musicology
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmjuː.zɪ.kəˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˌmjuː.zɪ.kəˈlɒ.dʒɪ.kli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to the study or science of music (musicology).

Examples

To study musicologically
His analysis approached the symphony musicologically rather than just aesthetically.
The composer’s works are musicologically significant for their use of folk melodies.
This interpretation is musicologically sound according to recent scholarship.
