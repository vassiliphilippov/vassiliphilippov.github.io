unbuilding

noun
demolition process
7.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of taking apart, dismantling, or demolishing a building or structure

Examples

Urban unbuilding project
The city encouraged unbuilding as part of its urban renewal plan.
Careful unbuilding allows materials to be reused instead of wasted.
The unbuilding of the old factory made way for a new park.

Related Terms

  • antonym: construction - the process of building something
  • synonym: demolition - the act of destroying or tearing down a building
