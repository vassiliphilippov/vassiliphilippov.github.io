cork

noun
stopper for a wine bottle
2.3
Pronunciation: / kɔrk / (AmE), / kɔːk / (BrE)
a stopper, typically made from the bark of the cork oak, used to seal the opening of a bottle, especially a wine bottle

Examples

Wine bottle cork
Don't forget to put the cork back in the bottle to keep the wine fresh.
He pulled the cork out of the wine bottle with a loud pop.
The cork broke off inside the bottle, making it difficult to open.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cap - a protective cover or lid used to seal a bottle
  • synonym: stopper - object used to close the mouth of a container
