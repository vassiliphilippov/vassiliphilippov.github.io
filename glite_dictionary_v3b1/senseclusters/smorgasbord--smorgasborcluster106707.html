smorgasbord

noun
varied buffet meal, esp. Scandinavian
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɔrɡəsˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈsmɔːɡəsˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a meal served buffet-style with a variety of hot and cold dishes, originally of Scandinavian origin

Examples

A smorgasbord buffet
Every Sunday, the hotel offers a traditional Scandinavian smorgasbord for brunch.
Guests helped themselves to the smorgasbord set up along the side of the hall.
The wedding reception featured an elaborate smorgasbord with delicacies from many countries.
