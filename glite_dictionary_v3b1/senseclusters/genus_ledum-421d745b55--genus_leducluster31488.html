genus Ledum

noun
genus of evergreen shrubs
9.9

A genus of evergreen shrubs native to north temperate regions, formerly classified in the heath family.

Examples

Characteristics of the genus Ledum are leathery leaves and white flowers.
The genus Ledum includes several species commonly known as Labrador tea.
