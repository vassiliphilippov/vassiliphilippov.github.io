decelerate

verb
reduce speed; slow down
7.2 4.4
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈsel.ə.reɪt/ /; / /diː.ˈsel.ə.reɪt/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈsel.ə.reɪt/ /; / /diː.ˈsel.ə.reɪt/ (BrE)
