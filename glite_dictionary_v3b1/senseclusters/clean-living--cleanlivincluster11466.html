clean-living

adjective
living in a morally pure and healthy way
2.9 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkliːn.lɪv.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈkliːn.lɪv.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
leading a life that is morally pure and often also abstaining from unhealthy habits such as drinking, smoking, or drug use

Examples

Clean-living person
His clean-living reputation made him a role model for young people.
Many athletes credit their success to a clean-living lifestyle.
She has always been known for her clean-living and kind nature.
