fin de siècle

noun
end of a century, esp. the 19th century
9.9 7.8
Pronunciation: /fɪn də ˈsiː.kəl/ /; / /fæn də ˈsiː.kəl/ (AmE), /fɪn də ˈsiː.kəl/ /; / /fæn də ˈsiː.kəl/ (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of the end of a century, especially the 19th century, often suggesting fashionable decadence or world-weariness.

Examples

The fin de siècle mood
Discussions about culture during the fin de siècle often focused on changing values and artistic innovation.
Many artists of the fin de siècle period were obsessed with themes of decadence and decline.
The novel captures the fin de siècle mood of Paris, filled with both anxiety and excitement.
