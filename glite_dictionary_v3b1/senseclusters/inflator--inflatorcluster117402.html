inflator

noun
device for pumping air into objects
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfleɪtər / (AmE), / ɪnˈfleɪtə / (BrE)
a device, typically operated by hand or foot, used to pump air into something such as a tire or inflatable object

Examples

A inflator pump
A portable inflator can be a lifesaver if you get a flat tire on the road.
He used an inflator to pump air into his bicycle tire before heading out.
The new air mattress comes with its own electric inflator for easy setup.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pump - a device for moving fluids, including air
