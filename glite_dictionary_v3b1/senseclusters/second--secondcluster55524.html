second

noun
SI unit of time; 1/60 of a minute
1.7 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈse.kənd/ (AmE), /ˈse.kənd/ (BrE)
A unit of time equal to one sixtieth of a minute, used as the standard basic unit of time in the International System of Units (SI).

Examples

Sixty seconds in a minute
It takes about sixty seconds for the kettle to boil.
The athlete completed the race in just under ten seconds.
Time was measured to the nearest second during the experiment.
