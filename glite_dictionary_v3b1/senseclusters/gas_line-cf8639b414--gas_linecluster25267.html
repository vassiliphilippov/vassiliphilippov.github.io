gas line

noun
pipeline for transporting natural gas
1.3
Pronunciation: / ɡæs laɪn / (AmE), / ɡæs laɪn / (BrE)
A pipe or system of pipes used to transport natural gas from one place to another.

Examples

Broken gas line
A leak in the gas line forced the evacuation of the building.
Before digging, you should always check for a gas line in the area.
The plumber repaired the cracked gas line behind the stove.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pipeline - a long pipe, typically underground, for conveying gas, oil, etc. over long distances
