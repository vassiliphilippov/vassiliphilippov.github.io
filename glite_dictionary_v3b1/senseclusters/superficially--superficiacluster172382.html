superficially

adverb
in a shallow or surface-level way
5.3 1.78/m
Pronunciation: / suÀê.p…öÀàf…™ É.…ôl.i / (AmE), / ÀåsuÀê.p…ôÀàf…™ É.…ô.li / (BrE)
In a way that is only concerned with the surface or appearance; not thoroughly or deeply.

Examples

To be superficially friendly
He was superficially charming but lacked genuine warmth.
She understood the topic only superficially and struggled with the test questions.
The building was superficially cleaned before the inspection, but the dirt remained underneath.
