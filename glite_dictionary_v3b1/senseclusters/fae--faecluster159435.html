fae

noun
mythical fairy being
9.9
Pronunciation: / feɪ / (AmE), / feɪ / (BrE)
a mythical being or spirit, similar to a fairy, found in folklore and legends

Examples

The fae folk
In ancient tales, the fae are said to live in hidden forests and meadows.
Some people leave gifts outside at night, hoping to please the fae.
The story describes a magical world ruled by the mysterious fae.
