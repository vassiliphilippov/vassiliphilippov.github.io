pasty

adjective
pale, unhealthy-looking (of skin)
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪsti / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsti / (BrE)
(of a person's face or skin) pale and unhealthy in appearance; resembling paste in color.

Examples

Pasty face
After the illness, his pasty complexion was a cause for concern among his friends.
She looked pasty and tired after staying up all night to study.
The dim light made his face appear even more pasty than usual.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pallid - pale, typically because of poor health
  • antonym: ruddy - having a healthy, reddish color
