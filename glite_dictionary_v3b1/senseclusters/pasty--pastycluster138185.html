pasty

adjective
pale, unhealthy-looking (of skin)
6.7 0.55/m
Pronunciation: / Ààpe…™sti / (AmE), / Ààp…ëÀêsti / (BrE)
(of a person's face or skin) pale and unhealthy in appearance; resembling paste in color.

Examples

Pasty face
