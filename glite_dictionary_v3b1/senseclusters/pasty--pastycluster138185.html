pasty

adjective
pale, unhealthy-looking (of skin)
7.1 5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈpeɪ.sti/ /; / /ˈpæ.sti/ (AmE), /ˈpeɪ.sti/ /; / /ˈpæ.sti/ (BrE)
