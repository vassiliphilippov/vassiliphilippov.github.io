toffee

noun
hard chewy caramelized sweet
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːfi / (AmE), / ˈtɒfi / (BrE)
A hard, chewy sweet made by boiling sugar and butter, often with flavorings, until caramelized and then cooling the mixture.
