exegetic

adjective
pertaining to interpretation of texts
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɛksəˈdʒɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛksɪˈdʒiːtɪk / (BrE)
relating to the explanation or interpretation of texts, especially scripture.

Examples

An exegetic analysis
Her exegetic analysis of the poem revealed hidden layers of meaning.
Many scholarly articles adopt an exegetic perspective when discussing religious scriptures.
The professor's exegetic approach helped students understand the ancient text.
