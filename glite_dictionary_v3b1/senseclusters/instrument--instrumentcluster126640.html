instrument

noun
semantic role for means used in an action
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnstrəmənt / (AmE), / ˈɪnstrʊmənt / (BrE)
(Linguistics) The semantic role of an entity, typically inanimate, used by an agent to perform an action or initiate a process.

Examples

Instrument in a sentence
In the sentence 'She cut the bread with a knife', the knife serves as the instrument.
Linguists classify 'with a pen' as an instrument phrase in 'He wrote the letter with a pen'.
The word 'hammer' functions as an instrument in the statement 'He hit the nail with a hammer'.
