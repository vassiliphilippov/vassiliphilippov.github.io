case of the Mondays

noun
feeling lethargic on Monday
6.8 6.8
Pronunciation: /keɪs əv ðə ˈmʌn.deɪz/ (AmE), /keɪs əv ðə ˈmʌn.deɪz/ (BrE)
A feeling of unhappiness, lethargy, or lack of motivation typically experienced at the beginning of the workweek, especially on Monday.

Examples

A case of the Mondays
By lunchtime, his case of the Mondays started to fade as he got busier.
Every time I struggle to get out of bed on Monday, I blame it on a case of the Mondays.
The whole office seemed to have a case of the Mondays after the long weekend.
