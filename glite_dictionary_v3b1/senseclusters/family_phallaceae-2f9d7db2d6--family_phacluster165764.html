family Phallaceae

noun
family of fungi (true stinkhorns)
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli fæˈleɪəsiː / (AmE), / ˈfamɪli faˈleɪəsɪː / (BrE)
A family of fungi belonging to the order Phallales, comprising the true stinkhorns known for their unpleasant odor.

Examples

The family Phallaceae
Researchers discovered a new species in the family Phallaceae in the tropical rainforest.
The family Phallaceae includes several species noted for their foul odor and unusual shapes.
The distinctive fruiting bodies of the family Phallaceae make them easy to identify among fungi.
