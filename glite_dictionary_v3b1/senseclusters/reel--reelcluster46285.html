reel

noun
a roll of film for projection or filming
4.4 1.79/m
Pronunciation: / ril / (AmE), / riÀêl / (BrE)
a roll of photographic film wound around a cylinder for use in a movie projector or camera

Examples

A film reel
Each reel contains about twenty minutes of footage for the documentary.
She bought an old reel of black-and-white film at the auction.
The projectionist loaded the reel of film onto the movie projector.

Synonyms

  • spool
    3.2 2.13/m
    a spool is a simple cylinder for winding thread, tape, or film for storage, while a reel is a film roll made for projection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spool - a cylinder on which wire, thread, or film is wound
