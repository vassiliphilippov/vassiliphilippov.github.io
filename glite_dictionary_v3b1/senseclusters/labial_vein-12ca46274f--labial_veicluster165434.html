labial vein

noun
vein draining the lips of the vulva
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪ.bi.əl veɪn / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.bi.əl veɪn / (BrE)
a vein that drains blood from the lips of the vulva

Examples

Labial vein of vulva
