doormat

noun
mat for wiping shoes at a door
2.1 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈdɔːr.mæt/ (AmE), /ˈdɔː.mæt/ (BrE)
A mat placed outside or just inside an exterior door for people to wipe their shoes on before entering.

Examples

A doormat at the door
I bought a new doormat with a cheerful welcome message on it.
Please wipe your feet on the doormat before coming inside.
The doormat was muddy after the rainstorm yesterday.
