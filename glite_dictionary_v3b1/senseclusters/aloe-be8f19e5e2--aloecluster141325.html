Aloe

noun
genus of succulent plants
1.9 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈæ.loʊ/ /; / /ˈæ.ləʊ/ (AmE), /ˈæ.ləʊ/ (BrE)

Examples

Aloe plants require little water and plenty of sunlight to thrive indoors.
More than 500 species belong to the Aloe genus, including Aloe vera.
The medicinal gel from the leaves of the Aloe is used to soothe minor burns.
