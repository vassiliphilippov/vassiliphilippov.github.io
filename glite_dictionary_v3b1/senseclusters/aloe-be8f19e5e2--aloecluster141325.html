Aloe

noun
genus of succulent plants
1.9
Pronunciation: / əˈloʊ / (AmE), / əˈləʊ / (BrE)
a genus of succulent plants, native mainly to Africa, with fleshy leaves and often used for ornamental or medicinal purposes

Examples

Aloe plant leaves
Aloe plants require little water and plenty of sunlight to thrive indoors.
More than 500 species belong to the Aloe genus, including Aloe vera.
The medicinal gel from the leaves of the Aloe is used to soothe minor burns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Aloe vera - a species of Aloe widely used for its medicinal gel
