stickwork

noun
skill with a stick
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪkˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈstɪkˌwɜːk / (BrE)
the skillful use or manipulation of a stick, especially in sports or decorative woodwork

Examples

Hockey stickwork
Her excellent stickwork helped the team win the hockey match.
The ornate stickwork on the old house's porch was carefully restored.
He practiced his stickwork every day to improve his performance on the field.

  • synonym: stickhandling - the control of a stick, especially in hockey
