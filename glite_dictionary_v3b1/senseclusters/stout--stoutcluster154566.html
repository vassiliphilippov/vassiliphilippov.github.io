stout

noun
a strong dark beer
3.2
Pronunciation: / staʊt / (AmE), / staʊt / (BrE)
A strong, very dark, heavy-bodied ale made from pale malt, roasted unmalted barley, and often caramel malt with hops.

Examples

A pint of stout
Dark stouts often have a creamy texture and robust flavor.
He ordered a pint of stout at the pub after work.
The brewery is famous for its rich chocolate stout with hints of coffee.

Related Terms

  • synonym: porter - a dark style of beer developed in London, related to stout
