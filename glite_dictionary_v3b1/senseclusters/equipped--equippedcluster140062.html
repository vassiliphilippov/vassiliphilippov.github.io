equipped

adjective
carrying weapons; armed
Pronunciation: / ɪˈkwɪpt / (AmE), / ɪˈkwɪpt / (BrE)
armed or provided with weapons; carrying weapons, especially for combat or security purposes.

Examples

Equipped with weapons
Every soldier must be properly equipped before entering the combat zone.
The guards were equipped with rifles and body armor.
The special police unit is fully equipped for riot control operations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: armed - carrying weapons, especially for combat
  • antonym: unarmed - not carrying weapons
