bum

noun
a homeless person; vagrant (informal)
7.1 2/m
Pronunciation: / bÊŒm / (AmE), / bÊŒm / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who has no permanent home or job and often lives by begging or doing odd jobs; a vagrant.

Examples

A homeless bum
He offered his sandwich to a bum sitting on the park bench.
Some people wrongly assume every bum chooses to live on the streets.
The old bum by the station told fascinating stories of his travels.

Synonyms

  • tramp
    6.1 0.7/m
    tramp is a person who wanders widely and often begs or does casual work, while bum is an informal name for someone similar.
  • vagrant
    7.7 0.9/m
    vagrant is a formal or legal term for someone without a home who may beg or move around, while bum is the informal everyday word.
  • hobo
    8.4 1/m
    hobo is a traveling worker who seeks temporary jobs and often moves by train, while bum is an informal word for someone who usually begs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drifter - a person who moves from place to place without a permanent home or job
