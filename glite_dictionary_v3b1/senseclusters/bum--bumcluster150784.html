bum

noun
a homeless person; vagrant (informal)
Pronunciation: / bʌm / (AmE), / bʌm / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who has no permanent home or job and often lives by begging or doing odd jobs; a vagrant.

Examples

A homeless bum
He offered his sandwich to a bum sitting on the park bench.
Some people wrongly assume every bum chooses to live on the streets.
The old bum by the station told fascinating stories of his travels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drifter - a person who moves from place to place without a permanent home or job
  • synonym: tramp - a person who travels from place to place on foot, usually doing odd jobs or begging
