compatible

adjective
able to exist together harmoniously
1.0 4.1
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpætəbl̩ /; / kəmˈpæt̬.ə.bəl / (AmE), / kəmˈpætəbl̩ /; / kəmˈpæt.ɪ.bəl / (BrE)
able to exist, work, or be used together without conflict or special changes

Examples
