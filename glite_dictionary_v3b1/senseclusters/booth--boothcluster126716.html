booth

noun
partially enclosed restaurant/bar seating
Pronunciation: / buːθ / (AmE), / buːθ / (BrE)
a table in a restaurant or bar surrounded by two high-backed benches, providing a semi-private seating area

Examples

A restaurant booth
The cozy booth made our meal feel more private and comfortable.
They reserved a booth for six people at their favorite diner.
We asked the waiter if we could sit in a booth by the window.
