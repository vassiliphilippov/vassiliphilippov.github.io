synoecious

adjective
bearing mixed male and female organs
Pronunciation: / sɪˈniːʃəs / (AmE), / sɪˈniːsɪəs / (BrE)
(of a plant or gametoecium) having both male and female reproductive organs mixed within the same structure.

Examples

Synoecious plants
Researchers observed that synoecious mosses had a higher reproductive success than dioecious ones.
Some liverwort species are synoecious, with both sexes present in each gametoecium.
The presence of synoecious organs ensures that self-fertilization can occur within a single plant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: monoecious - having male and female reproductive organs on the same plant but in separate flowers
