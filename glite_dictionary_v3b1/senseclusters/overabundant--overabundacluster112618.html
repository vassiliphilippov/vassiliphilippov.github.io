overabundant

adjective
excessively abundant
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərəˈbʌndənt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərəˈbʌndənt / (BrE)
existing or produced in excessively large amounts; more than is needed or wanted.

Examples

An overabundant supply
Critics argued that the movie was overabundant in special effects, which distracted from the story.
In recent years, rainfall has been so overabundant that the fields have flooded.
The menu was overabundant with choices, making it difficult to decide what to order.
