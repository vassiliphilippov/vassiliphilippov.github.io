congruence

noun
state of agreement
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑŋɡruəns / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡruəns / (BrE)
the state of being in agreement or having the same shape, size, or form, especially in mathematics

Examples

Mathematical congruence
The congruence of their goals made working together much easier.
In geometry, congruence means two figures have exactly the same size and shape.
There was a clear congruence between his words and his actions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conformity - the state of being in agreement or harmony
  • antonym: discrepancy - a lack of agreement or similarity
