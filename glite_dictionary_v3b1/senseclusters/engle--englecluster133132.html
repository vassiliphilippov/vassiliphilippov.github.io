engle

noun
fireplace or hearth
Pronunciation: / ˈɛŋɡl / (BrE)
a fireplace or hearth, especially in Scottish or northern English dialects, where a fire burns in a home

Examples

Warm engle fire
The family gathered around the engle to warm themselves after a long day in the fields.
Old tales were often shared beside the glowing engle on cold Scottish nights.
She placed another log on the engle to keep the cottage warm through the evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fireplace - a structure for holding an open fire in a home
  • synonym: hearth - the floor of a fireplace or the area in front of it
