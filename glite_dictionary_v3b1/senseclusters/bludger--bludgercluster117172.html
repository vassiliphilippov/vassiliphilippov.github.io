bludger

noun
slang lazy person
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌdʒə / (BrE)
a slang term for a person who avoids work or is habitually lazy

Examples

A lazy bludger
Everyone called him a bludger because he never did his share of the work.
If you keep acting like a bludger, you will get in trouble at your job.
She was tired of being labeled a bludger by her classmates.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hard worker - a person who works diligently and puts in effort
  • synonym: slacker - a person who avoids work or effort
