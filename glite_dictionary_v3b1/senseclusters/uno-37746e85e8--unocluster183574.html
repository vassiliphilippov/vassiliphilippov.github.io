Uno

proper noun
Fiat car model
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈuːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈuːnəʊ / (BrE)
a small economy car model produced by the Italian manufacturer Fiat, popular in the 1980s and 1990s

Examples

Fiat Uno car
He learned to drive in his family's old Uno before buying a newer car.
The Uno was known for its fuel efficiency and compact size.
Many students in the city still use a Uno for daily commuting.
