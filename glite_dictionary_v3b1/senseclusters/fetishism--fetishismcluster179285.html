fetishism

noun
sexual attraction to objects or nonsexual body parts
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛtɪˌʃɪzəm /; / ˈfɛt̬.ɪˌʃɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈfɛtɪˌʃɪzəm /; / ˈfet.ɪ.ʃɪ.zəm / (BrE)
