catty-cornered

adjective
diagonally across from something
Pronunciation: / ˈkæti ˌkɔrnərd / (AmE), / ˈkæti ˌkɔːnəd / (BrE)
Situated or positioned diagonally across from something, especially across the corner of a square or rectangular space.

Examples

Catty-cornered position
She ran catty-cornered across the playground to meet her friend waiting on the opposite corner.
The bakery is catty-cornered from the post office on Main Street.
Their house sits catty-cornered from ours, so we can see their porch from our window.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diagonal - connecting two nonadjacent corners of a polygon or rectangle
  • antonym: opposite - situated across from a particular position, but not necessarily diagonally
