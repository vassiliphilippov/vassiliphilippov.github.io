wink-wink

noun
implied shared secret or understanding
9.3 7.2
Pronunciation: /wɪŋk wɪŋk/ (AmE), /wɪŋk wɪŋk/ (BrE)
An indirect gesture or remark that implies a shared secret, joke, or unspoken understanding between people.

Examples

A wink-wink gesture

She gave a wink-wink when she mentioned her 'meeting', making everyone laugh.
The advert used a wink-wink reference to the product's unexpected uses.
