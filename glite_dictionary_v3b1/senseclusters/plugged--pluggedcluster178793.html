plugged

adjective
blocked or obstructed
4.6
Pronunciation: / plʌɡd / (AmE), / plʌɡd / (BrE)
completely blocked or obstructed so that nothing can pass through.

Examples

Plugged pipe
After the pipe burst, the main sewer became plugged with debris.
My nose is so plugged that I can hardly breathe.
The kitchen sink was plugged and wouldn’t drain properly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blocked - completely obstructed or blocked
  • antonym: unplugged - not blocked or not obstructed
