nondefective

adjective
without faults
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑndɪˈfɛktɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒndɪˈfɛktɪv / (BrE)
not having any faults, flaws, or defects; in good working condition

Examples

A nondefective product
The technician confirmed that the nondefective units could be shipped to customers immediately.
Only nondefective items passed the final quality inspection at the factory.
Customers expect every nondefective product to function as advertised.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defective - having a fault or flaw; not working properly
  • synonym: flawless - without any imperfections or faults
