joint

verb
cut meat apart at the joint
Pronunciation: / dʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / dʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
to separate a piece of meat at the joint, especially by cutting

Examples

To joint a chicken
It takes practice to properly joint a large piece of meat without wasting any parts.
She watched a video to learn how to joint a duck for her family dinner.
The butcher expertly jointed the chicken before preparing it for roasting.
