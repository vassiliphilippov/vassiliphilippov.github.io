blinky

noun
flashing indicator light
Pronunciation: / ˈblɪŋki / (AmE), / ˈblɪŋki / (BrE)
a small light or indicator that flashes to show status or an alert, especially on electronic devices

Examples

A red blinky
The blinky on my laptop turns red when the battery is low.
If the blinky starts flashing, you should check the device immediately.
He noticed a green blinky on the router indicating a stable connection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indicator - a device that shows the state or level of something
  • synonym: warning light - a light that signals a problem or alert
