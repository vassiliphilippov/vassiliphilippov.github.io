investment company

noun
financial institution investing pooled funds
1.2 2.6
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈvest.mənt ˈkʌm.pə.niː/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈvest.mənt ˈkʌm.pə.niː/ (BrE)
a financial institution that pools money from individual investors and invests it in securities issued by other companies

Examples

An investment company manages assets
An investment company manages assets on behalf of individual shareholders.
Regulations require an investment company to disclose its holdings to investors regularly.
