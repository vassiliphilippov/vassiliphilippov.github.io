covalently

adverb
by sharing electrons
Pronunciation: / koʊˈveɪləntli / (AmE), / kəʊˈveɪləntli / (BrE)
in a way that involves the sharing of electron pairs between atoms

Examples

To covalently bond atoms
The two elements are covalently bonded to form a stable molecule.
Water molecules are held together covalently through shared electron pairs.
In organic chemistry, carbon atoms often connect covalently to other elements.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ionically - in a way that involves the transfer of electrons between atoms
