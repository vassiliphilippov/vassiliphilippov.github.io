off-the-shelf

adjective
ready-made and available for purchase
6.2 3.3
Pronunciation: /ɔːf.ðə.ˈʃelf/ /; / /ɑːf.ðə.ˈʃelf/ (AmE), /ɒf.ðə.ˈʃelf/ /; / /ɔːf.ðə.ˈʃelf/ (BrE)
(especially of clothing or products) made in standard sizes or forms and available for immediate purchase from stock.

Examples

An off-the-shelf product
I bought an off-the-shelf dress for the party instead of getting one tailored.
Many businesses use off-the-shelf software because it's quicker and cheaper than custom development.
The company offers both custom solutions and off-the-shelf products to meet customer needs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: custom-made - made to individual order or specification
  • synonym: ready-made - manufactured in advance, not custom-made
