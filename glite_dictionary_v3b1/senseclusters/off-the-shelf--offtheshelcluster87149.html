Examples

An off-the-shelf product

Synonyms

  • off-the-rack
    4.4 0.36/m
    off-the-rack is clothing made in standard sizes and sold from store stock, while off-the-shelf covers similar ready-made goods.
  • off-the-peg
    6.0 0.02/m
    off-the-peg is a British phrase for ready-made clothes available to buy immediately, while off-the-shelf applies to ready-made products generally.
  • ready-to-wear
    3.9 0.02/m
    ready-to-wear is the fashion industry term for ready-made clothing sold in shops, while off-the-shelf refers to any ready-made product.
