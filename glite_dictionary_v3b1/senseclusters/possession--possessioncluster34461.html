possession

noun
something that is owned
Pronunciation: / pəˈzɛʃ.ən / (AmE), / pəˈzɛʃ.ən / (BrE)
an object or item that belongs to someone; something owned or held by a person

Examples

Personal possessions
After the fire, she was grateful for every possession she had left.
Pack only your essential possessions before moving to a new city.
The thief made off with his most valuable possession, a family heirloom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: belonging - an item that someone owns
  • antonym: dispossession - the loss of possession of something
  • synonym: property - something owned or possessed
