imprint

noun
publisher's details in a book
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmˌprɪnt / (AmE), / ˈɪm.prɪnt / (BrE)
the name, address, and edition details of a publisher printed in a book, usually at the bottom of the title page

Examples

Book imprint details
Scholars often check the imprint of a book to verify its authenticity and edition.
The imprint at the base of the title page showed a London address and a 1923 publication date.
The first edition of the novel was released under a new imprint.
