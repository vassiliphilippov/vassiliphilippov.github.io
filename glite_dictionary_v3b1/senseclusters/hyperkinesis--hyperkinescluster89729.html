hyperkinesis

noun
excessive movement disorder
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərkɪˈniːsɪs / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəkɪˈniːsɪs / (BrE)
a medical condition characterized by excessive movement or abnormal muscular activity

Examples

Hyperkinesis disorder
The doctor diagnosed the child with hyperkinesis after observing constant fidgeting and restlessness.
Treatments for hyperkinesis often include behavioral therapy and medication.
Children with hyperkinesis may have difficulty concentrating in school due to their excessive movement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hypokinesis - reduced movement or muscular activity
