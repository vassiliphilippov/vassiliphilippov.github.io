lighting-up

noun
turning lights on; legal period for vehicles
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtɪŋ ʌp / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtɪŋ ʌp / (BrE)
The act or time of turning lights on, especially when referring to the legal requirement to use vehicle lights after dark.

Examples

The lighting-up ceremony
The town square looked magical at lighting-up when all the decorations were switched on.
They always go for a walk at lighting-up to admire the city lights.
You must turn on your headlights at lighting-up to comply with road safety laws.
