defense force

noun
military group defending a country or area
3.7 3.2
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈfens fɔːrs/ /; / /diː.ˈfens fɔːrs/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈfens fɔːs/ /; / /diː.ˈfens fɔːs/ (BrE)
an organized group or body of people trained and equipped and responsible for defending a country or area against attack, typically serving as its military or armed forces.
