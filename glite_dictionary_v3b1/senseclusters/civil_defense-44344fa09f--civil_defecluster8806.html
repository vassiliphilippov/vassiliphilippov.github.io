civil defense

noun
organized civilian protection in emergencies
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪvəl dɪˈfɛns / (AmE), / ˈsɪvəl dɪˈfɛns / (BrE)
the organization and training of civilians to protect themselves and their communities during times of war, disaster, or emergencies

Examples

Civil defense volunteers
During the war, many countries relied heavily on civil defense volunteers to protect local communities.
He taught civil defense classes about emergency evacuations and first aid.
The city launched a civil defense campaign to prepare residents for natural disasters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emergency services - organizations providing urgent assistance in crises
  • antonym: military defense - protection by a nation's armed forces
