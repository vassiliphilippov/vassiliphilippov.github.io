reverse

noun
gear for moving backward
2.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɜrs / (AmE), / rɪˈvɜːs / (BrE)
the gear in a machine or vehicle that causes it to move backward

Examples

Reverse in a car
Make sure the machine is not in reverse before starting it.
She accidentally put the car in reverse and backed into the fence.
The bus rolled backward after the driver shifted into reverse.
