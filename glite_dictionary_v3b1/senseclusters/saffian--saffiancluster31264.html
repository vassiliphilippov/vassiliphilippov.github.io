saffian

adjective
made of fine goatskin
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsæfiən / (AmE), / ˈsæfiən / (BrE)
made of or covered with saffian leather, a fine goatskin leather used for luxury goods.

Examples

Saffian purse design
She carried a small saffian purse that complemented her tailored black coat.
The notebook was bound in saffian leather, giving it a luxurious feel.
Collectors prize saffian book covers for their durability and elegance.
