quarantine

noun
isolation to stop disease spread
6.3 4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈkwɔː.rən.tiːn/ /; / /ˈkwɔː.rən.tɪn/ (AmE), /ˈkwɒ.rən.tiːn/ /; / /ˈkwɒ.rən.tɪn/ /; / /ˈkwɒ.rən.taɪn/ (BrE)

Examples

A two-week quarantine
