dogskin

noun
skin of a dog
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔgˌskɪn / (AmE), / ˈdɒgˌskɪn / (BrE)
the skin or hide of a dog, sometimes used as material for making objects or clothing

Examples

Dogskin material
The ancient artifact was wrapped in dogskin to preserve it.
Some traditional drums are made using dogskin for their covers.
He examined the old coat, noticing it was crafted from dogskin.
