miss a call

verb
fail to answer a phone call
2.3
Pronunciation: / mɪs ə kɑl / (AmE), / mɪs ə kɔːl / (BrE)
to fail to answer a telephone call before it stops ringing

Examples

To miss a call from boss

If you miss a call, check your voicemail for any important messages.
