impetuously

adverb
in a sudden or impulsive manner
5.0 6.2
Pronunciation: /ɪm.ˈpetʃ.uː.əs.liː/ /; / /ɪm.ˈpet.juː.əs.liː/ (AmE), /ɪm.ˈpet.jʊ.əs.liː/ /; / /ɪm.ˈpetʃ.ʊə.liː/ (BrE)
In a sudden, forceful, or impulsive manner, without careful thought or caution.

Examples

To act impetuously
He impetuously quit his job after a minor disagreement with his boss.
She spoke impetuously, without considering the consequences of her words.
They invested their savings impetuously, later realizing they should have waited for better advice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impulsively - in an impulsive way, acting without forethought
