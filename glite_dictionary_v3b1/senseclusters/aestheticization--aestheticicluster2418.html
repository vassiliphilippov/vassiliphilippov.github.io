aestheticization

noun
making beautiful
8.6
Pronunciation: / iˌsθɛtɪsəˈzeɪʃən /; / ɛsˌθɛtɪsəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / iˌsθɛtɪsaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / iːsˌθɛtɪsaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making something appear beautiful or focusing on its artistic qualities, sometimes ignoring practical or moral aspects

Examples

The aestheticization of art
The aestheticization of industrial spaces can transform them into vibrant community centers.
Some critics argue that the aestheticization of violence in films can be problematic.
Urban planners discussed the aestheticization of public transportation to make it more appealing.
The aestheticization of urban spaces can make cities more visually appealing but may overlook residents' needs.
Critics argued that the aestheticization of poverty in the photographs distracted from the real social issues.
Some artists use aestheticization to transform everyday objects into works of art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beautification - the act of making something more beautiful
  • antonym: functionalization - the process of making something practical or useful
  • antonym: uglification - the process of making something less attractive
