visor

noun
armor plate on helmet for face protection
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪzɚ / (AmE), / ˈvʌɪzə / (BrE)
a movable part of a medieval helmet, fitted with eye slits, that protects the face

Examples

Helmet visor
A broken visor left the soldier's face unprotected in battle.
Her museum exhibit includes a medieval helmet with a hinged visor.
The knight lowered his visor before entering the tournament arena.
