formidably

adverb
in a formidable manner
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrmɪdəbli / (AmE), / ˈfɔːmɪdəbli / (BrE)
In a way that causes fear or respect by being large, powerful, or intense.

Examples

To be formidably skilled
The boxer was formidably skilled, making his opponents hesitant to challenge him.
The company is formidably successful, dominating the market year after year.
The mountain rose formidably above the valley, its cliffs casting long shadows.

Related Terms

  • synonym: formidable - inspiring fear or respect through being impressively large, powerful, or intense
