unirradiated

adjective
not exposed to radiation
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪˈreɪdieɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈreɪdieɪtɪd / (BrE)
not exposed to radiation or not having undergone irradiation

Examples

The unirradiated vegetables
The unirradiated vegetables were kept separate from those treated with radiation.
Researchers analyzed both unirradiated and irradiated samples to compare the effects.
It is important to label food as unirradiated if it has not been exposed to radiation.
