anticyclonic

adjective
relating to high-pressure atmospheric systems
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.ti.saɪˈklɑ.nɪk / (AmE), / ˌæn.ti.saɪˈklɒ.nɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the atmosphere around a high-pressure center, where winds typically move outward and clockwise in the Northern Hemisphere.

Examples

Anticyclonic weather pattern
Pilots receive warnings during strong anticyclonic episodes due to the stable, clear air and poor visibility at low levels.
The anticyclonic system persisted for several days, resulting in unusually high temperatures.
Weather forecasters predicted anticyclonic conditions bringing dry skies and calm winds over much of the region.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cyclonic - relating to or characteristic of cyclones (low pressure systems)
