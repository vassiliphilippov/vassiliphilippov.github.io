skullduggery

noun
dishonest or deceitful behaviour
Pronunciation: / skʌlˈdʌɡəri / (AmE), / skʌlˈdʌɡəri / (BrE)
Deceitful or dishonest behaviour; trickery or underhanded activity, especially for personal gain.

Examples

Political skullduggery
Business rivals accused each other of skullduggery to win the lucrative contract.
The investigation uncovered a web of skullduggery at the highest levels of government.
Years later, his skullduggery during the campaign was finally exposed in the press.

Related Terms

  • antonym: honesty - the quality of being honest or truthful
  • synonym: trickery - the practice of deception or fraud
