all-embracing

adjective
comprehensive; including everything
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɔl ɛmˈbreɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌɔːl ɪmˈbreɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
Including everything or everyone; comprehensive in scope or content.

Examples

An all-embracing view
Her philosophy was all-embracing, respecting ideas from many different cultures.
The report offered an all-embracing analysis of the city's economic challenges.
They adopted an all-embracing approach to education, including arts, sciences, and sports.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comprehensive - covering or including everything
  • antonym: limited - restricted in extent, amount, or scope
