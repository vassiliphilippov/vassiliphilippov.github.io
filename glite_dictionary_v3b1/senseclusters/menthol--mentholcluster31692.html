menthol

noun
minty compound used in flavours and medicines
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛnˌθɔl /; / ˈmɛnˌθɑl / (AmE), / ˈmɛnθɒl / (BrE)
a crystalline substance with a minty smell and taste, used in medicines, lotions, and flavorings to give a cooling sensation

Examples

Menthol cream
Applying a menthol lotion can help soothe sore muscles after exercise.
Many cough drops contain menthol to relieve throat irritation.
The menthol in the cream left a refreshing, cool feeling on her skin.
A cooling sensation is felt when menthol is applied to the skin.
Some cough drops contain menthol to help soothe a sore throat.
The toothpaste has a refreshing menthol flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mint - an aromatic herb with a fresh, cool flavor
  • synonym: peppermint - an aromatic plant whose leaves and oil are the source of menthol
