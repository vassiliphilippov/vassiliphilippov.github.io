annon

noun
tropical fruit with scaly rind and black seeds
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.ɒn / (AmE), / ˈæn.ɒn / (BrE)
A sweet tropical fruit with a thick, scaly rind and shiny black seeds, commonly known as the sugar apple or custard apple.

Examples

A annon fruit
Annon is prized in many tropical countries for its sweet, custard-like taste.
She scooped out the creamy flesh of the annon for dessert.
The market had fresh annon on display alongside mangos and papayas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: custard apple - edible fruit of various Annona species with sweet flesh
  • synonym: sugar apple - sweet tropical fruit with creamy pulp and bumpy skin (Annona squamosa)
