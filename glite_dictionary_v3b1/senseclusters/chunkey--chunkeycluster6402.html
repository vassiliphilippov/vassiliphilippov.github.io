chunkey

noun
game target stone
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌŋki / (BrE)
a disc-shaped stone or object used as the target in the traditional Native American game of chunkey

Examples

Chunkey stone target
Archaeologists discovered a chunkey stone at the site of an ancient settlement.
Players would roll the chunkey across the field while others tried to land their spears near it.
The museum displayed a well-preserved chunkey used in Native American games centuries ago.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hoop and stick - a traditional game involving rolling a hoop and chasing it with a stick
