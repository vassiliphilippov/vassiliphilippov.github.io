crassly

adverb
insensitively
Pronunciation: / ˈkræsli / (AmE), / ˈkrɑːsli / (BrE)
in a way that shows a lack of sensitivity, refinement, or consideration for others

Examples

To crassly ignore feelings
He crassly dismissed her concerns without listening to what she had to say.
The advertisement crassly exploited people's fears to sell products.
She crassly joked about a serious issue, upsetting many in the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insensitively - in a way that lacks concern for others' feelings
  • synonym: rudely - in an impolite or disrespectful manner
  • antonym: tactfully - in a way that shows sensitivity and skill in dealing with others
