jiff

noun
brief time unit
9.9
Pronunciation: /dʒɪf/ (AmE), /dʒɪf/ (BrE)
an informal, very short unit of time used in computing, electronics, or physics, with duration varying by context

Examples

A jiff in computing
The computer completes the calculation in just a jiff.
Wait a jiff while the device resets itself.
In programming, a jiff can represent different time intervals depending on the system.
