pretty

noun
small decorative object
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪti / (AmE), / ˈprɪti / (BrE)
a small object or decoration that is attractive or charming, often used for adornment.

Examples

A box of pretties
She collected pretties from every country she visited as souvenirs.
The children admired the colorful pretties displayed on the festival table.
He bought a few pretties to decorate his new apartment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bauble - a small, showy ornament or decoration
  • synonym: trinket - a small decorative object of little value
