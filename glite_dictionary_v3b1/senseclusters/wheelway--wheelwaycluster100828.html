wheelway

noun
groove for wheel
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːlˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈwiːlˌweɪ / (BrE)
a groove, channel, or track in a machine or structure that guides or supports a wheel or axle

Examples

Machine wheelway
The roller must stay in its wheelway to ensure the machine operates smoothly.
Dirt and debris can accumulate in the wheelway, causing the wheels to jam.
Engineers designed a special wheelway to support the heavy axles of the conveyor system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: groove - a long, narrow cut or depression in a surface
  • synonym: track - a groove or path that guides movement
