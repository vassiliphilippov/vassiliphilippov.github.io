hebephrenic

adjective
affected by disorganized schizophrenia
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːbəˈfrɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌhiːbɪˈfriːnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by a form of schizophrenia marked by disorganized behavior, inappropriate emotions, and foolish or senseless laughter.

Examples

Hebephrenic schizophrenia
Certain support groups specialize in helping families of hebephrenic individuals.
His diagnosis was hebephrenic schizophrenia, which explained many of his regressive actions.
The patient displayed hebephrenic symptoms, including inappropriate laughter and erratic behavior.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disorganized schizophrenia - a former subtype of schizophrenia with similar symptoms
