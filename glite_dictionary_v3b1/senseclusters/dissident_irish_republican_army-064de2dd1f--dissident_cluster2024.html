Dissident Irish Republican Army

proper noun
Irish republican breakaway paramilitary group
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪsɪdənt ˈaɪrɪʃ rɪˈpʌblɪkən ˈɑːr ˈɛm / (AmE), / ˈdɪsɪdənt ˈaɪərɪʃ rɪˈpʌblɪkən ˈɑː ˈɛm / (BrE)
