clusterfuck

noun
chaotic, badly managed situation (vulgar slang)
Pronunciation: / ˈklʌstɚˌfʌk / (AmE), / ˈklʌstəˌfʌk / (BrE)
A situation that is extremely chaotic, disorganized, or badly managed, often resulting in everything going wrong.

Examples

Total clusterfuck
After the new policy was implemented, the whole system became a clusterfuck.
That meeting was a complete clusterfuck, with arguments and confusion everywhere.
The project quickly turned into a clusterfuck after everyone missed the deadline.
