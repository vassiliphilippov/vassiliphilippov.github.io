musk clover

noun
a musky-scented annual European herb
2.7
Pronunciation: / mʌsk ˈkloʊvɚ / (AmE), / mʌsk ˈkləʊvə / (BrE)
a low-growing annual European herb of the genus Medicago, naturalized in America, with a musky scent, resembling alfilaria

Examples

Musk clover plant
Musk clover has been naturalized across parts of North America.
Gardeners appreciate the fragrant aroma of musk clover in their herb beds.
The field was dotted with musk clover and other wildflowers in early summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alfilaria - a plant similar in appearance and growth to musk clover
