perseverant

adjective
persistent; determined
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌpɜrsəˈvɪrənt / (AmE), / ˌpɜːsɪˈvɪərənt / (BrE)
continuing to try to achieve something despite difficulties or obstacles

Examples

Perseverant attitude
Despite many setbacks, his perseverant nature helped him reach his goals.
The perseverant student studied every night until she mastered the subject.
A perseverant team can overcome even the toughest challenges together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: determined - having a strong will to achieve something
  • synonym: persistent - continuing firmly despite difficulty
  • antonym: quitting - giving up easily
  • synonym: resilient - able to recover quickly from difficulties
