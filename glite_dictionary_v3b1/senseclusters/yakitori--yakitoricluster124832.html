yakitori

noun
Japanese chicken eatery
Pronunciation: / jɑːˌkiːˈtɔːri / (AmE), / jæˌkɪˈtɔːri / (BrE)
a small Japanese restaurant or bar that specializes in serving grilled skewered chicken, known as yakitori

Examples

Yakitori restaurant
They found a cozy yakitori near the station and enjoyed freshly grilled chicken skewers.
The chef at the yakitori prepared each skewer right in front of the customers.
After work, friends often gather at a yakitori to relax and share food.

Related Terms

  • synonym: izakaya - a type of informal Japanese bar serving drinks and snacks
  • synonym: restaurant - a place where people pay to sit and eat meals
