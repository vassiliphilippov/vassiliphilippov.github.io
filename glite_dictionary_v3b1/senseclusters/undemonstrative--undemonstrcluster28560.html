undemonstrative

adjective
not openly displaying emotion
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.dɪˈmɑn.strə.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈmɒn.strə.tɪv / (BrE)
(of a person or behavior) not inclined to openly display emotion or feelings.

Examples

An undemonstrative person
He was undemonstrative, preferring to keep his feelings to himself.
His undemonstrative manner made it difficult for others to read his emotions.
Though undemonstrative by nature, she cared deeply for her friends.

Related Terms

  • antonym: demonstrative - openly expressive of emotions
