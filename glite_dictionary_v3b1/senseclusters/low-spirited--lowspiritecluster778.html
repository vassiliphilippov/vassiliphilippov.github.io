low-spirited

adjective
feeling sad or dejected
Pronunciation: / ˌloʊˈspɪrɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌləʊˈspɪrɪtɪd / (BrE)
Feeling sad, lacking in energy or enthusiasm; gloomy or dejected.

Examples

Low-spirited person
Despite the celebration, Mary remained low-spirited and withdrawn.
His low-spirited demeanor made the whole room seem somber.
She felt low-spirited for days after hearing the disappointing news.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depressed - in a state of unhappiness or despondency
  • synonym: downcast - feeling despondent or dejected
  • antonym: high-spirited - full of energy, enthusiasm, or courage
