purlieus

noun
surrounding areas
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrliˌuːz / (AmE), / ˈpɜːlɪjuːz / (BrE)
the areas near or surrounding a particular place, especially those frequently visited by someone

Examples

City purlieus
He wandered through the purlieus of the old city in search of inspiration.
The children explored the purlieus of the park every afternoon after school.
Writers often find ideas in the purlieus of their favorite neighborhoods.
