scone

noun
small baked cake, often served with tea
Pronunciation: / skoʊn, skɑn / (AmE), / skɒn, skəʊn / (BrE)
a small, round or triangular cake made from flour, fat, and milk, often with eggs and sometimes sugar, baked on a griddle or in an oven, typically eaten with butter or jam.

Examples

Fresh scones with jam
She served fresh scones with clotted cream and strawberry jam for afternoon tea.
The café's signature scone is packed with blueberries and finished with a sugar glaze.
Warm from the oven, the scones were the highlight of our brunch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biscuit - a small baked good that is crisp and crumbly
