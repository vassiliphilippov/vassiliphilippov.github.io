stocked with

adjective
supplied with a lot of something
Pronunciation: / stɑkt wɪð / (AmE), / stɒkt wɪð / (BrE)
containing or supplied with a large quantity of something specified; provided with more than enough of what is needed.

Examples

Stocked with many books
Her library was stocked with mystery novels from all over the world.
The pantry is stocked with enough food to last a month.
They opened a new shop stocked with a wide range of organic produce.
