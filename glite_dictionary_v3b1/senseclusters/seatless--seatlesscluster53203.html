seatless

adjective
without representation
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːtləs / (AmE), / ˈsiːtləs / (BrE)
not having a seat or representation in a legislative, parliamentary, or governing body

Examples

Seatless party
After the election, the party was completely seatless in the national parliament.
Several small groups remained seatless despite gaining a significant number of votes.
The reform left many former politicians seatless and out of the decision-making process.

Related Terms

  • antonym: represented - having representation in a governing body
  • synonym: unrepresented - not having representatives in a governing body
