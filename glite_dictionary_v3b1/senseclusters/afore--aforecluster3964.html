afore

preposition
archaic before/in front of
Pronunciation: / əˈfɔr / (AmE), / əˈfɔː / (BrE)
in front of or before, used to indicate position or time; now archaic or dialectal

Examples

Afore the king
The knight knelt afore the king to pledge his loyalty.
In the old tale, the hero stood afore the gates at dawn.
She arrived afore sunrise, waiting quietly for the others.

Related Terms

  • antonym: after - later than in time or position
  • synonym: before - earlier than in time or position
