author's name

noun
name identifying the author of a work
Pronunciation: / ˈɔθɚz neɪm / (AmE), / ˈɔːθəz neɪm / (BrE)
the name that is displayed on a work to identify its author, typically appearing in the by-line

Examples

Author's name printed
Her author's name was printed in bold letters above the story.
Please make sure the author's name is spelled correctly on the title page.
The publisher asked if he wanted to use a pseudonym instead of his real author's name.
