record

to register, show, or notice information or measurements, especially by a device or instrument

Examples

To record a result
Her heart barely recorded a reaction when she heard the news.
No change was recorded by the sensors during the experiment.
The device did not record any movement last night.
Her heart monitor records every heartbeat during the procedure.
The meter recorded higher energy usage than expected this month.
The pressure gauge recorded a sudden drop in temperature last night.

Synonyms

  • register
    4.6 5.56/m
    register usually shows a measurement on an instrument display, while record means to store or note that information for later use.
  • read
    3.9 18/m
    read is when a device shows or accesses data, while record means saving those measurements so they can be reviewed later.
  • log
    3.0 12/m
    log means making a written or systematic note, while record can mean the device's act of registering or storing measurements.
