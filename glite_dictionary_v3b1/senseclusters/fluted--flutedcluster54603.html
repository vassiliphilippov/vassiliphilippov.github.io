fluted

adjective
melodious, flute-like
Pronunciation: / ˈfluːtɪd / (AmE), / ˈfluːtɪd / (BrE)
having a clear, melodious, and flute-like tone, especially of a sound or voice

Examples

Fluted voice
The bird's fluted call echoed through the early morning forest.
Her fluted voice charmed everyone at the concert.
A fluted melody drifted from the open window, filling the street with music.
