liter

noun
metric unit of volume/capacity
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈliːtər / (AmE), / ˈliːtə / (BrE)
a metric unit of capacity equal to 1,000 cubic centimeters or roughly 1.75 pints

Examples

One liter bottle
Milk is often sold in one liter cartons in many countries.
She drank a full liter of water after her workout.
The fuel tank can hold up to fifty liters.
He bought a litre of milk from the shop.
The car’s fuel tank has a capacity of fifty litres.
This bottle can hold up to two litres of water.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: cubic meter - the base SI unit of volume equal to 1,000 liters
  • synonym: liter - American English spelling of 'litre'
  • degree lower: milliliter - one thousandth of a liter
