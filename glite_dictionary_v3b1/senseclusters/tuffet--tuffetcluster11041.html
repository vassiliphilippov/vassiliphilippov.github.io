tuffet

noun
small stool or grass clump
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌfɪt / (AmE), / ˈtʌfɪt / (BrE)
a small clump of grass or a low, small seat or stool, often used for sitting

Examples

A grassy tuffet
The child perched on a tuffet while watching the butterflies in the garden.
She placed her feet on a soft tuffet beside the fireplace.
A rabbit hid behind a tuffet of grass near the pond.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clump - a small group of things, such as grass or trees, growing closely together
  • synonym: stool - a simple seat without a back or arms
