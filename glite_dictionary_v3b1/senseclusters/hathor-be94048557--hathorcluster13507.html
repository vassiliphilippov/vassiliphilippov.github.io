Hathor

proper noun
Egyptian goddess
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhæθɔr / (AmE), / ˈhæθɔː / (BrE)
an ancient Egyptian goddess associated with love, music, beauty, and motherhood

Examples

Goddess Hathor
The ancient Egyptians worshipped Hathor as the goddess of love and joy.
Many temples were built in honor of Hathor throughout Egypt.
Artists often depicted Hathor with cow horns and a sun disk on her head.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Aphrodite - the Greek goddess of love and beauty
  • synonym: Isis - an Egyptian goddess of magic and motherhood
