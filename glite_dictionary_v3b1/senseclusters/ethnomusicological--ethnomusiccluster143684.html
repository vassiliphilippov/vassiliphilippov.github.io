ethnomusicological

adjective
about music and culture
Pronunciation: / ˌɛθnoʊˌmjuːzɪkəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɛθnəʊˌmjuːzɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study of music within its cultural and social contexts

Examples

An ethnomusicological study
The professor gave an ethnomusicological lecture on the role of music in indigenous ceremonies.
Her ethnomusicological research focused on the instruments used in South American folk traditions.
They attended an ethnomusicological conference to discuss music and cultural identity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: musicological - relating to the scholarly study of music
