disgustingly

adverb
in a disgusting way or to a disgusting degree
3.1
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈɡʌstɪŋli / (AmE), / dɪsˈɡʌstɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that causes disgust; to an extent or in a way that is repulsive or offensive.

Examples

Behaves disgustingly
He behaves disgustingly when he doesn't get his way.
The food was disgustingly greasy and left a bad taste in my mouth.
The kitchen was disgustingly dirty after the party.
