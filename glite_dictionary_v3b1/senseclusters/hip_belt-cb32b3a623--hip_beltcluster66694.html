hip belt

noun
strap around hips
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪp bɛlt / (AmE), / ˈhɪp bɛlt / (BrE)
a strap worn around the hips, especially on backpacks, to help support weight or carry items

Examples

Backpack hip belt
She tightened the hip belt on her backpack before starting the hike.
A well-padded hip belt can make carrying heavy loads much more comfortable.
The hip belt distributes the weight of the pack to your hips instead of your shoulders.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shoulder strap - a strap worn over the shoulder to help carry a bag
  • synonym: waist belt - a belt worn around the waist for support or carrying items
