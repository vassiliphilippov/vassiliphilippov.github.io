obsessor

noun
source of obsession
Pronunciation: / əbˈsɛsər / (AmE), / əbˈsɛsə / (BrE)
a person or thing that causes someone to become obsessed or that persistently occupies the mind

Examples

An obsessor thought
The idea of failure became an obsessor that haunted him day and night.
For years, the mysterious painting acted as an obsessor in her life.
He tried to ignore the obsessor that kept him awake with endless thoughts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distraction - something that diverts attention
  • synonym: preoccupation - something that dominates one's thoughts
