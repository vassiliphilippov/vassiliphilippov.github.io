boing

noun
bouncing sound
8.2
Pronunciation: /bɔɪŋ/ (AmE), /bɔɪŋ/ (BrE)
a sound that imitates the noise made when something bounces or springs back quickly

Examples

A loud boing
The cartoon character jumped on a spring with a loud boing.
When the rubber ball hit the floor, it made a funny boing sound.
Everyone laughed as the toy made a boing each time it bounced.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bounce - the sound or action of rebounding off a surface
  • antonym: thud - a dull sound made by a heavy object hitting something
