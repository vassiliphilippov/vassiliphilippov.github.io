superfat

noun
extra oil in soap
9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈsuː.pər.fæt/ /; / /ˈsjuː.pər.fæt/ (AmE), /ˈsjuː.pə.fæt/ /; / /ˈsuː.pə.fæt/ (BrE)
the extra amount or percentage of oil left unreacted in soap making to increase moisturizing properties

Examples

Soap superfat level
The soap maker adjusted the superfat level to create a gentler bar for sensitive skin.
A higher superfat percentage can make handmade soap more moisturizing but also softer.
Careful calculation of superfat is important to avoid leaving too much oil in the finished soap.
