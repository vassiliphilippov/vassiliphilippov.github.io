rattleweed

noun
plant with rattling pods
Pronunciation: / ˈrætəlˌwiːd / (AmE), / ˈrætəlˌwiːd / (BrE)
a plant whose seed pods produce a rattling sound when shaken or blown by the wind

Examples

Wild rattleweed plant
The children shook the rattleweed pods and laughed at the noise they made.
In late summer, the dry rattleweed plants could be heard rustling in the breeze.
Farmers often recognize rattleweed by the distinctive sound of its seed pods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: locoweed - a plant, often toxic, sometimes called rattleweed in some regions
  • synonym: rattlebox - a plant with seed pods that rattle when shaken
