bractlet

noun
small leaf-like structure in plants
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbræktlɪt / (AmE), / ˈbræktlɪt / (BrE)
a small bract, particularly one subtending a flower or a smaller part within a cluster

Examples

Small bractlet under flower

The bractlet is often mistaken for a petal due to its color and position.
The tiny bractlets at the base of each flower are barely visible to the naked eye.
