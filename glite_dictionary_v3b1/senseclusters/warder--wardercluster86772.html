warder

noun
prison guard (especially British use)
7.4 0.48/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…îrd…ôr / (AmE), / Ààw…îÀêd…ô / (BrE)
a person employed to guard prisoners in a prison; a prison officer, especially in British English

Examples
