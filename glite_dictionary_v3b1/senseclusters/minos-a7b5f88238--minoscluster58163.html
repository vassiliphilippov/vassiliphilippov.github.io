MINOS

proper noun
Fermilab neutrino-oscillation experiment (acronym)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɛm aɪ ɛn oʊ ɛs / (AmE), / ɛm aɪ ɛn əʊ ɛs / (BrE)
A particle-physics experiment (Main Injector Neutrino Oscillation Search) at Fermilab that studied neutrino oscillations using a long-baseline neutrino beam.

Examples

MINOS experiment
Researchers presented new oscillation data collected by MINOS at the international physics conference.
The team compared measurements from the near and far detectors used in MINOS to study neutrino disappearance.
Funding decisions for follow-up projects were influenced by results first reported by MINOS.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Fermilab - a U.S. national laboratory that hosts particle-physics experiments
  • synonym: neutrino oscillation experiment - an experiment designed to observe or measure the phenomenon of neutrinos changing type during flight
