roof of the mouth

noun
upper surface inside the mouth
Pronunciation: / ruːf əv ðə maʊθ / (AmE), / ruːf əv ðə maʊθ / (BrE)
the upper surface inside the mouth that separates the oral and nasal cavities; also known as the palate

Examples

The high roof of the mouth
A high, arched roof of the mouth can sometimes affect speech.
The doctor examined the child's roof of the mouth for any abnormalities.
When you drink hot coffee too quickly, you might burn the roof of the mouth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: palate - the anatomical term for the roof of the mouth
