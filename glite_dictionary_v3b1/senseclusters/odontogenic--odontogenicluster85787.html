odontogenic

adjective
tooth origin
9.9
Pronunciation: / oʊˌdɑntəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / əʊˌdɒntəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
originating from or related to the formation or development of teeth

Examples

Odontogenic cyst
The surgeon identified the cyst as odontogenic after reviewing the X-rays.
Certain jaw tumors are classified as odontogenic because they arise from tooth-forming tissues.
An odontogenic infection can spread from a decayed tooth to the surrounding bone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dental - relating to the teeth
  • antonym: nonodontogenic - not originating from the teeth
