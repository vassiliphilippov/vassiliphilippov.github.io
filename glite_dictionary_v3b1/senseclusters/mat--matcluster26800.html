mat

verb
become tangled or felted
8.5 4.0
Pronunciation: / mæt / (AmE), / mæt / (BrE)
to become or make tangled into a thick mass (especially hair or fur)

Examples

To mat hair
Her hair began to mat after days without brushing.
If wool is left in the rain, it will often mat into clumps.
The fibers matted together during the washing process.
After playing outside, the dog's fur started to mat.
If you don't brush wool regularly, it will mat into tight clumps.
The wind caused her hair to mat together.
