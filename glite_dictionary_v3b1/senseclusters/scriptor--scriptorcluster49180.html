scriptor

noun
historical scribe
Pronunciation: / skrɪpˈtɔr / (AmE), / skrɪpˈtɔː / (BrE)
a person who writes, especially a scribe in historical or medieval contexts

Examples

Medieval scriptor of texts
The ancient scriptor spent years copying religious texts by hand.
In medieval monasteries, a scriptor was responsible for producing illuminated manuscripts.
The library displayed a quill once used by a famous scriptor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: author - a person who writes books or other texts
  • synonym: scribe - a person who copies out documents, especially in the past
