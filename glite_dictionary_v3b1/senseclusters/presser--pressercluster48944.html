presser

noun
clothes pressing worker
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛsər / (AmE), / ˈprɛsə / (BrE)
a person or machine that presses clothes or other items to remove wrinkles or shape them

Examples

A shirt presser
The presser at the dry cleaner made sure every shirt was perfectly smooth.
Modern factories often use an automated presser to handle large amounts of laundry.
She worked as a presser for several years before becoming a supervisor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ironer - a person or device that irons clothes
  • antonym: washer - a person or machine that washes clothes
