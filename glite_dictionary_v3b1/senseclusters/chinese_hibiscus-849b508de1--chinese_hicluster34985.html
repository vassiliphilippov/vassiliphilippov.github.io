Chinese hibiscus

noun
showy Asiatic shrub with large red flowers
4.7
Pronunciation: / tʃaɪˈniːz hɪˈbɪskəs / (AmE), / tʃaɪˈniːz hɪˈbɪskəs / (BrE)
A large shrub or small tree native to Asia, known for its large, showy red or deep-red flowers, commonly grown as an ornamental plant.

Examples

The Chinese hibiscus flower
Gardeners prize the Chinese hibiscus for its striking blooms and glossy green leaves.
She planted a Chinese hibiscus near the porch to add color to her backyard.
The bright red flowers of the Chinese hibiscus brighten up gardens throughout tropical regions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hibiscus - any plant of the genus Hibiscus with large colorful flowers
