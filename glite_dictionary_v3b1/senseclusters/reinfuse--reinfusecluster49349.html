reinfuse

verb
to infuse again
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˈfjuːz / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈfjuːz / (BrE)
to infuse something again, especially by steeping tea, herbs, or similar material a second time to extract more flavor or strength

Examples

To reinfuse tea leaves
After finishing her first cup, she decided to reinfuse the tea leaves for a stronger flavor.
Some herbalists recommend that you reinfuse the herbs to make the most of their benefits.
If you want a richer taste, you can reinfuse the coffee grounds with hot water.
