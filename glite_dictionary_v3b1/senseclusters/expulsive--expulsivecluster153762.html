expulsive

adjective
driving out force
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspʌlsɪv / (AmE), / ɪkˈspʌlsɪv / (BrE)
having the power or function of driving out or expelling something

Examples

Expulsive muscle contractions
The doctor described the muscle contractions as expulsive during childbirth.
Coughing is an expulsive action that helps clear the airways.
The volcano erupted with expulsive force, sending ash high into the sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expelling - forcing something out
  • antonym: retentive - having the ability to keep or hold something in
