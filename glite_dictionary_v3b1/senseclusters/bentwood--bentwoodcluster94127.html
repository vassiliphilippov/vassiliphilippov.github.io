bentwood

noun
wood steamed and bent for furniture
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛntˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈbɛntˌwʊd / (BrE)
Wood that has been made flexible by steaming and is then bent into curved shapes, especially for making furniture.

Examples

Bentwood furniture
Many antique rocking chairs are made using bentwood techniques.
She admired the graceful curves of the bentwood frame on the café chair.
The designer crafted a sleek set of bentwood armchairs for the living room.
