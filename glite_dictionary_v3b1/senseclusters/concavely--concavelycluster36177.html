concavely

adverb
in a concave manner
3.2 6.7
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈkeɪv.lɪ/ (AmE), /kən.ˈkeɪv.lɪ/ (BrE)
In a concave manner; in a way that curves inward like the inside of a bowl or sphere.

Examples

Curved concavely
The dish is concavely curved, allowing liquids to pool in the center.
The snow had drifted concavely against the side of the barn after the storm.
The umbrella was concavely shaped to better collect rainwater.

Related Terms

  • antonym: convexly - in a convex way (curving outward)
