shredded

adjective
cut or torn into thin strips (esp. food)
Pronunciation: / ˈʃrɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈʃrɛdɪd / (BrE)
(of food) prepared by cutting or tearing into thin strips or pieces, especially with a grater.

Examples

Shredded cheese
A bowl of shredded lettuce was served alongside the tacos.
He sprinkled shredded cheese on top of the pasta before baking it.
The recipe calls for two cups of shredded cabbage and one carrot.
