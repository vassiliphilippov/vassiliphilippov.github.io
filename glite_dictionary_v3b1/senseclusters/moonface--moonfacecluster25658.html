moonface

noun
rounded, puffy face
8.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmuːn.feɪs/ (AmE), /ˈmuːn.feɪs/ (BrE)
a rounded, swollen appearance of the face, often caused by certain medical conditions or medications

Examples

Steroid moonface
After several months of steroid therapy, she noticed a moonface developing.
The doctor explained that moonface is a common side effect of the medication.
His moonface gradually disappeared once the treatment ended.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cushingoid - having physical features similar to Cushing's syndrome, such as a rounded face
  • synonym: edema - swelling caused by excess fluid in body tissues
