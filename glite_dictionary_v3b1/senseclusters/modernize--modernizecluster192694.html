modernize

verb
make or become more modern
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːdərˌnaɪz /; / ˈmɑdərˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɒdənaɪz / (BrE)
to make something more modern or up to date, especially by improving it or using new technology or ideas
