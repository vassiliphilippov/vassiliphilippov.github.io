nopal

noun
a cactus with edible fruits and pads
6.5
Pronunciation: / noʊˈpɑl /; / ˈnoʊpæl / (AmE), / ˈnəʊpal / (BrE)
a type of cactus with edible pads and fruits, commonly used in Mexican food and agriculture
