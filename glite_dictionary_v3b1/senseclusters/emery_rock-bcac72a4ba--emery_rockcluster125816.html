emery rock

noun
manufactured abrasive material used in grindstones
8.2 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈem.ər.iː rɑːk/ /; / /ˈem.ər.ɪ rɑːk/ (AmE), /ˈem.ə.riː rɒk/ /; / /ˈem.ə.rɪ rɒk/ (BrE)
