emery rock

noun
manufactured abrasive material used in grindstones
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɛməri rɑk / (AmE), / ˈɛməri rɒk / (BrE)
A hard material made from a mixture of emery dust and a binder, molded into shapes such as grindstones for abrasive purposes.

Examples

An emery rock wheel
He replaced the worn wheel with a new emery rock disc on the grinding machine.
Manufacturers prefer emery rock because it is durable and cost-effective for abrasive applications.
The old workshop still used emery rock grindstones to sharpen its tools.
