necrotic

adjective
affected by tissue death (necrosis)
Pronunciation: / nɪˈkrɑtɪk / (AmE), / nɪˈkrɒtɪk / (BrE)
affected by or relating to the death of body tissue (necrosis).

Examples

Necrotic skin tissue
Samples of necrotic skin were analyzed in the laboratory.
The patient's foot had become necrotic due to poor blood circulation.
The surgeon removed the necrotic tissue to prevent the infection from spreading.

Related Terms

  • synonym: necrosis - the death of most or all of the cells in an organ or tissue
