nonsustainable

adjective
not defensible
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnsəˈsteɪnəbəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnsəˈsteɪnəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be defended, justified, or maintained by evidence or reason, especially regarding an argument or position

Examples

Nonsustainable argument
The theory was quickly dismissed as nonsustainable once new evidence was presented.
Critics argued that the company's claims were nonsustainable and lacked proper data.
His position became nonsustainable after the facts contradicted his statements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indefensible - impossible to defend or justify
  • antonym: sustainable - able to be maintained or defended
  • synonym: untenable - not able to be defended or justified
