blindness to

noun
lack of awareness or understanding
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈblaɪndnəs tu / (AmE), / ˈblaɪndnəs tə / (BrE)
a lack of awareness or understanding about something; failure to recognize or acknowledge a fact, problem, or reality

Examples

Blindness to risks
Her blindness to the risks led her to take unnecessary chances.
His blindness to the needs of others made him seem insensitive.
The company’s blindness to environmental issues harmed its reputation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: awareness of - conscious knowledge or understanding of a fact or situation
  • synonym: ignorance of - lack of knowledge, understanding, or awareness about something
