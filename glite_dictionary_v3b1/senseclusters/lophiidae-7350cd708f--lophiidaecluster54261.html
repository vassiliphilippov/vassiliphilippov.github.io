Lophiidae

noun
family of anglerfish
Pronunciation: / loʊˈfaɪɪˌdi / (AmE), / lɒˈfɪɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of large-headed marine fishes commonly known as anglers or anglerfish

Examples

Lophiidae angler fish
Members of Lophiidae are found in deep-sea environments worldwide.
Some species in the Lophiidae family possess a lure to attract prey.
The bizarre appearance of the Lophiidae makes them fascinating subjects for marine biologists.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anglerfish - common name for members of Lophiidae with bioluminescent lures
