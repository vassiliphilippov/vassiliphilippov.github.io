baitcasting

noun
fishing with overhead reel
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪtˌkæstɪŋ /; / ˈbeɪtkæstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪtˌkɑːstɪŋ /; / ˈbeɪtkɑːstɪŋ / (BrE)
a method of fishing that uses a reel mounted above the rod to cast and control bait or lures
