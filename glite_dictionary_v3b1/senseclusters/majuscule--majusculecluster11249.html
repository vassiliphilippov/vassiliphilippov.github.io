majuscule

noun
large or capital letter (uppercase)
9.6 6.7
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈdʒʌs.kjuːl/ (AmE), /mə.ˈdʒʌs.kjuːl/ (BrE)
a large letter, such as a capital or uppercase letter, used at the beginning of sentences, proper names, or for emphasis

Examples

A majuscule at start
In ancient manuscripts, the text was written entirely in majuscule script.
The teacher asked the students to write their names in majuscule letters.
The word 'London' begins with a majuscule as it is a proper noun.
