curved

adjective
having a smoothly bending shape
1.0 0.65/m
Pronunciation: / k…ùvd / (AmE), / k…úÀêvd / (BrE)
having a smooth, continuously bending shape or surface, without sharp angles

Examples

Smooth curved lines
Her signature was distinguished by its elegant, curved lines.
The curved handle made the mug easier to grip.
We admired the curved staircase in the old mansion.

Synonyms

  • curvilinear
    7.9 0.39/m
    curvilinear describes shapes made of smooth, flowing lines in technical contexts, while curved is the common everyday term.
  • arched
    3.2 0.70/m
    arched refers to a shape like an arch, often structural, while curved describes any smooth bending form without sharp angles.
  • rounded
    2.4 0.00/m
    rounded means having edges or ends smoothed into a circular curve, while curved means a continuous bend without sharp angles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curvy - having many curves, especially used for body shapes
  • antonym: straight - extending in one direction without curves or bends
