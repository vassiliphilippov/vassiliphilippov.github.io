netless

adjective
without a net
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛtləs / (AmE), / ˈnɛtləs / (BrE)
lacking a net or without any net, especially in technical or sporting contexts

Examples

Netless pond
The fish escaped from the netless pond easily.
Playing on a netless court made the game much more challenging.
The netless goalposts were difficult for the players to use during practice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bare - not covered or protected by anything
  • antonym: netted - covered or enclosed with a net
