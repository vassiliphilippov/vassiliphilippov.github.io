Quichua

proper noun
Andean people and language
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkitʃwɑ / (AmE), / ˈkiːtʃwə / (BrE)
an indigenous people and language group of the Andes region in South America, especially in Ecuador and Peru

Examples

Quichua people
Many members of the Quichua community live in the highlands of Ecuador.
The Quichua language is taught in some rural schools to preserve cultural heritage.
Researchers have documented traditional music among the Quichua people of the Andes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Quechua - the broader indigenous people and language family of the Andes
