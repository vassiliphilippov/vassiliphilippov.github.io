criminal prosecution

noun
legal proceedings against a defendant for a crime
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪmɪnəl ˌprɑːsɪˈkjuːʃən / (AmE), / ˈkrɪmɪnəl ˌprɒsɪˈkjuːʃən / (BrE)
the process of instituting and conducting legal proceedings against someone accused of a crime

Examples

Criminal prosecution process
Failure to cooperate could result in criminal prosecution and severe penalties.
She faced a criminal prosecution for her alleged involvement in the fraud scheme.
The criminal prosecution of the suspect began after months of careful investigation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: acquittal - a judgment that a person is not guilty of a crime
  • synonym: prosecution - the continuation of legal proceedings against someone
