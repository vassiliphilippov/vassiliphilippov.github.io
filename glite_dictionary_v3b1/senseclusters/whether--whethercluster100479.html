whether

conjunction
introducing alternatives
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛðər / (AmE), / ˈwɛðə / (BrE)
used to introduce two or more alternatives or possibilities in a sentence

Examples

Decide whether to go
She could not decide whether to stay at home or go out with her friends.
Let me know whether you are coming to the meeting tomorrow.
He asked whether the restaurant was open on Sundays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: if - used to introduce a condition or possibility
