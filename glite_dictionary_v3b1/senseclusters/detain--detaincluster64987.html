detain

verb
to delay or hold back
Pronunciation: / dɪˈteɪn / (AmE), / dɪˈteɪn / (BrE)
to delay or hold back someone or something, often making them late or preventing them from proceeding

Examples

To detain a guest
I'm sorry I'm late; I was detained by a last-minute phone call.
She was detained at work and missed the beginning of the concert.
The storm detained several flights at the airport.
