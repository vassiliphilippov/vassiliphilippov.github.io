somatosense

noun
the body's sensory systems for touch, pain, and position
9.9 8.8
Pronunciation: / soʊˈmæt̬.oʊˌsɛns / (AmE), / səˈmat.əʊ.sɛns / (BrE)
any of the bodily senses that mediate touch, pressure, temperature, pain, and body position or movement.

Examples
