wear

verb
to display an expression
1.7
Pronunciation: / wɛr / (AmE), / wɛə / (BrE)
to have or display an expression, style, or appearance (especially on your face or in your manner)

Examples

To wear a smile
Despite her nerves, she wore a confident smile throughout the interview.
He wore a frown when he heard the disappointing news.
She always wears a look of determination before a big meeting.
He wore a sad expression on his face all day.
She always wears her hair in a ponytail at work.
The judge wears a stern look whenever she enters the courtroom.
