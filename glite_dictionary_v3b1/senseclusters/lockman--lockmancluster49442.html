lockman

noun
man in charge of canal lock
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑkˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈlɒkˌmæn / (BrE)
a man responsible for operating and maintaining a lock on a canal

Examples

The lockman on duty
As the only lockman on duty, he managed the entire section himself.
Local tourists watched as the experienced lockman deftly operated the old machinery.
The lockman opened the gates to let the barge through the canal.
