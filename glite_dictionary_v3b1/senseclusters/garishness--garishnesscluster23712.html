Synonyms

  • gaudiness
    7.2 0.04/m
    gaudiness is overly showy and often seen as tasteless decoration, while garishness focuses on harsh brightness and color that shocks.
  • flashiness
    5.6 0.05/m
    flashiness draws quick attention with bright, flashy display, while garishness stresses excessive brightness and poor taste in color and decoration.
  • showiness
    6.5 0.01/m
    showiness aims to attract notice by elaborate display, while garishness often seems tasteless because of harsh color or decoration.
  • splashiness
    7.2 0.00/m
    splashiness uses bold, flashy effects to stand out, while garishness feels excessive and lacks subtlety in color or taste.
  • tawdriness
    8.7 0.05/m
    tawdriness looks cheap and gaudy, often with low quality materials, while garishness focuses on excessive brightness or showiness.
  • meretriciousness
    9.9 0.00/m
    meretriciousness is superficially attractive and seems false or empty, while garishness is mainly about loud, harsh color or decoration.
