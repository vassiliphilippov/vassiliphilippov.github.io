unsweetened

adjective
not containing added sugar
4.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈswiːtənd / (AmE), / ʌnˈswiːtənd / (BrE)
(of food or drink) not containing added sugar or anything to make it sweet.

Examples

Unsweetened coffee
I prefer my coffee unsweetened to enjoy its natural flavor.
She bought unsweetened applesauce as a healthier alternative.
The recipe calls for unsweetened cocoa powder, not the sweetened kind.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sweetened - containing added sugar or another sweetener
