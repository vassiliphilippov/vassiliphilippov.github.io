recyclability

noun
ability to be recycled
4.8
Pronunciation: / riˌsaɪkləˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / riˌsaɪkləˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being able to be recycled or processed for reuse

Examples

Product recyclability
Manufacturers are working to improve the recyclability of their products to reduce environmental impact.
The recyclability of glass makes it a popular choice for sustainable packaging.
Before buying, check the recyclability of the material to ensure it can be processed after use.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disposability - the quality of being intended for single use and then thrown away
  • synonym: reusability - the quality of being able to be used again
