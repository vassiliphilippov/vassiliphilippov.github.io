scourer

noun
person who cleans by scrubbing
Pronunciation: / ˈskaʊɚɚ / (AmE), / ˈskaʊərə / (BrE)
a person who cleans a surface thoroughly by scrubbing or scouring

Examples

A kitchen scourer
As a professional scourer, she made sure every corner of the kitchen shone.
In the past, a scourer was often employed to clean public buildings.
The company hired a scourer to remove years of grime from the old tiles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cleaner - a person or thing that cleans
  • synonym: scour - to clean or brighten a surface by rubbing it hard
