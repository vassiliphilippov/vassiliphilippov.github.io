poison camas

noun
a toxic North American perennial plant
7.2 8.5
Pronunciation: /ˈpɔɪ.zən ˈkæ.məs/ /; / /ˈpɔɪ.zən ˈkɑː.məz/ (AmE), /ˈpɔɪ.zən ˈkɑː.məz/ /; / /ˈpɔɪ.zən ˈkæ.məs/ (BrE)
a perennial plant native to central and southern United States, known for its toxic bulbs and similarity in appearance to edible species; also called death camas.
