Examples

Contract signee
Each signee is responsible for understanding the terms of the contract before signing.
The document requires the signature of every signee to become valid.
As the signee, you are legally bound by the agreement you have signed.

Synonyms

  • signer
    2.7 0.91/m

  • signatory
    6.1 2.10/m
    signatory refers to a person or organization that signs and is legally bound, while signee means the individual who signs the document.
  • undersigned
    5.7 0.02/m
