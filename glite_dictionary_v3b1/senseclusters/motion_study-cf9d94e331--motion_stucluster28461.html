motion study

noun
analysis of task efficiency
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊʃən ˌstʌdi / (AmE), / ˈməʊʃən ˌstʌdi / (BrE)
an analysis of a task or job to determine the most efficient way to complete it by minimizing time and effort

Examples

A motion study analysis
A thorough motion study revealed unnecessary steps in the process, saving both time and resources.
Before changing the workflow, the manager recommended performing a motion study to evaluate its effectiveness.
The engineers conducted a motion study to improve efficiency on the assembly line.

Related Terms

  • synonym: time study - a similar analysis focusing on time taken to complete tasks
