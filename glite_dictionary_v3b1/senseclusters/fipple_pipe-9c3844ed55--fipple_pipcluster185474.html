fipple pipe

noun
tubular wind instrument with fipple mouthpiece
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪpəl paɪp / (AmE), / ˈfɪpəl paɪp / (BrE)
a tubular wind instrument, typically with eight finger holes and a fipple mouthpiece, such as a recorder or tin whistle
