tummy tuck

noun
cosmetic surgery of the abdomen
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌmi tʌk / (AmE), / ˈtʌmi tʌk / (BrE)
A cosmetic surgical procedure to remove excess skin and fat from the abdomen and tighten the abdominal muscles.

Examples

A tummy tuck surgery
After her pregnancy, she decided to have a tummy tuck to tighten her stomach area.
Despite the cost, many people opt for a tummy tuck to improve their appearance.
The plastic surgeon explained the risks associated with a tummy tuck procedure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abdominoplasty - plastic surgery of the abdomen to improve its appearance
