ride

noun
journey in a vehicle
2.9
Pronunciation: / raɪd / (AmE), / raɪd / (BrE)
a journey in a vehicle, especially in a car, bus, or train

Examples

A car ride
After dinner, they went for a ride around the city to see the lights.
It was a long ride from her house to the airport.
The children enjoyed their ride to school in the yellow bus.

Related Terms
