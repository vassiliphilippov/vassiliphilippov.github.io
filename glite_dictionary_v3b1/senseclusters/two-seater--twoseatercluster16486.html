two-seater

noun
automobile with two seats
3.3 2.9
Pronunciation: /tuː ˈsiː.tər/ (AmE), /tuː ˈsiː.tə/ (BrE)
An automobile with seats for two people, especially an open car with a front seat and a rumble seat.

Examples

A two-seater car
He bought a vintage two-seater for weekend drives in the countryside.
The sporty two-seater was perfect for city commuting, but not for family trips.
They restored an old two-seater with a rumble seat from the 1930s.
