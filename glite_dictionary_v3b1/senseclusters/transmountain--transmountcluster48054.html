transmountain

noun
mountain-crossing route
7.2
Pronunciation: / trænzˈmaʊn.tən / (AmE), / trænzˈmaʊn.tɪn / (BrE)
a road, tunnel, or passage that crosses over or through a mountain or mountain range

Examples

A transmountain road
The new transmountain allows travelers to reach the valley much faster than before.
Heavy snow closed the transmountain for several days during the winter.
Engineers designed the transmountain to withstand frequent landslides and harsh weather.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mountain pass - a route through a mountain range
  • synonym: tunnel - an underground passage, often through a mountain
