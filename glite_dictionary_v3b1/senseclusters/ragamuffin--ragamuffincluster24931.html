ragamuffin

noun
poorly dressed child or person
9.3 6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈræɡəˌmʌfɪn / (AmE), / ˈraɡəmʌfɪn / (BrE)
a child who is poorly dressed and often dirty; a person, especially a child, in ragged, shabby clothes.

Examples

A ragamuffin child
A young ragamuffin begged for coins outside the station.
He used to be a ragamuffin, running barefoot through the alleys.
The children looked like ragamuffins as they played in the muddy street.
