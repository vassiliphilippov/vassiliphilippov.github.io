desk-bound

adjective
restricted to office or desk work
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛskˌbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈdɛskˌbaʊnd / (BrE)
restricted to working at a desk or in an office, rather than doing physically active work

Examples

Desk-bound work
A desk-bound lifestyle can lead to various health problems if you don't exercise regularly.
After years of being desk-bound, he longed for a more active profession.
The desk-bound manager envied his colleagues who spent their days out in the field.
