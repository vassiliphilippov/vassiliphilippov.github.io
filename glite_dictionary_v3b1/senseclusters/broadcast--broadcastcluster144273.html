broadcast

verb
transmit by radio or television
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɔdˌkæst / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːdkɑːst / (BrE)
to send out a program by radio or television for public reception

Examples

To broadcast a match
The game will be broadcast live on national television tonight.
The station broadcasts news updates every hour throughout the day.
They broadcast the emergency warning to all local radio channels.
