splint

noun
a thin strip of wood
Pronunciation: / splɪnt / (AmE), / splɪnt / (BrE)
a small, thin strip of wood, often used for lighting fires or in crafts.

Examples

Wooden splint
Carefully breaking off a splint, she lit the campfire with it.
He used a splint to transfer the flame from the candle to the fireplace.
The basket was woven from thin splints of willow.
