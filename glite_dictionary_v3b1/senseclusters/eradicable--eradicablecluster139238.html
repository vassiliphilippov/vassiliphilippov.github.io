eradicable

adjective
able to be eliminated or removed
4.1
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrædɪkəbl̩ / (AmE), / ɪˈrædɪkəbl̩ / (BrE)
Able to be eradicated, eliminated, or completely removed.

Examples

Eradicable disease
Corruption in the agency proved less eradicable than officials had hoped.
Some diseases are considered eradicable through widespread vaccination efforts.
The weed was tough but still eradicable with persistent treatment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ineradicable - unable to be eradicated or removed
  • synonym: removable - able to be removed
