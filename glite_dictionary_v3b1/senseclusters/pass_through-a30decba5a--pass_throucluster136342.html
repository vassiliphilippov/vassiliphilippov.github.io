pass through

verb
cause to move or filter through
Pronunciation: / pæs ˈθru / (AmE), / pɑːs ˈθruː / (BrE)
to cause something to move or be filtered through something else

Examples

Water passes through a filter
In this process, gases are passed through the membrane to remove toxins.
The laboratory technician passed through the liquid to obtain a filtered sample.
To separate the impurities, they pass through the solution using a fine mesh.
