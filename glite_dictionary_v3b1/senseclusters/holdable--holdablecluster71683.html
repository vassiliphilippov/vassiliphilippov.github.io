holdable

adjective
able to be held
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊldəbəl / (AmE), / ˈhəʊldəbəl / (BrE)
able to be held or grasped in the hands

Examples

A holdable device
The new phone is lightweight and easily holdable with one hand.
For safety, make sure all tools are holdable by children before use.
The sculpture was small enough to be holdable during the demonstration.

Related Terms
