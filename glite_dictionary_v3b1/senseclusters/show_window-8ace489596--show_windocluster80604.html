show window

noun
shop window for displaying goods
Pronunciation: / ʃoʊ ˈwɪndoʊ / (AmE), / ʃəʊ ˈwɪndəʊ / (BrE)
a window in the front of a shop or store, used for displaying goods to attract customers

Examples

A show window display
She paused in front of the show window, admiring the sparkling jewelry on display.
The bakery's show window was filled with an assortment of freshly baked breads and pastries.
The latest fashions were arranged beautifully in the show window to attract passersby.

Related Terms

  • synonym: display window - a window in a shop for displaying merchandise
  • synonym: shop window - British term for a show window or display window
