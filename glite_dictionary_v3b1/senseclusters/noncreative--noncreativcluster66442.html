noncreative

adjective
lacking originality
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.kriˈeɪ.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.kriˈeɪ.tɪv / (BrE)
not showing imagination or originality; lacking creativity

Examples

Noncreative solution
His approach to solving problems was very noncreative and followed the same routine every time.
The advertising campaign was criticized for being noncreative and failing to attract attention.
Teachers encourage students to avoid noncreative answers and think outside the box.

Related Terms

  • antonym: creative - having the ability to create or invent new things
  • synonym: unimaginative - not showing creativity or original thought
