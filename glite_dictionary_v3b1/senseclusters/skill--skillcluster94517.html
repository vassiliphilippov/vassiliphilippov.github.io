skill

noun
digital-assistant software module
4.8
Pronunciation: /skɪl/ (AmE), /skɪl/ (BrE)
(in computing) a software module or capability for a virtual assistant or smart speaker that performs a specific task when invoked by the user.

Examples

A skill for Alexa
Developers released a new skill for the virtual assistant that books rides and tracks fares.
You can enable the weather skill to hear daily forecasts on your smart speaker every morning.
Companies often develop custom skills to let customers order products through their voice assistants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: app - a software application providing a specific service or function
  • synonym: capability - a feature or function that a system can perform
