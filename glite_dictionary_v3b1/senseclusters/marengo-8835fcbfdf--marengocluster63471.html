Marengo

noun
site of Napoleon's 1800 victory over Austria
9.8
Pronunciation: / məˈrɛŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / məˈrɛŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
the site of a battle in 1800 where Napoleon Bonaparte's French forces defeated the Austrians, marking a significant victory during the Napoleonic Wars

Examples

Battle of Marengo
After Marengo, Napoleon's reputation as a military leader was firmly established.
Historians still study the tactics used at Marengo to understand Napoleonic warfare.
The Battle of Marengo in 1800 proved to be a turning point in Napoleon's Italian campaign.
