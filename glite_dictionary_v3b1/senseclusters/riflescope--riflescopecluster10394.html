riflescope

noun
telescopic rifle sight
4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈraɪ.fəl.skoʊp/ (AmE), /ˈraɪ.fəl.skəʊp/ (BrE)
an optical device mounted on a rifle that magnifies distant targets to help with aiming

Examples

A riflescope lens
He carefully cleaned the riflescope to ensure a clear view before the competition.
The soldier adjusted the riflescope to focus on a target over 300 meters away.
A high-quality riflescope can greatly improve accuracy when hunting or target shooting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: iron sights - non-optical aiming devices on firearms
  • synonym: scope - an optical instrument for viewing distant objects
