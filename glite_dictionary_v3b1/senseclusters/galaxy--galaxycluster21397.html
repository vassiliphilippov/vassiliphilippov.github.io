galaxy

noun
a perennial evergreen herb with white flowers and glossy leaves
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæləksi / (AmE), / ˈɡælək(siː) / (BrE)
A perennial evergreen herb (Galax urceolata) native to the southeastern United States, with spikes of tiny white flowers and round to heart-shaped glossy leaves that turn reddish or purplish in autumn.
