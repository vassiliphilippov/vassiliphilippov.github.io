impassively

adverb
without showing emotion
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpæsɪvli / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɑːsɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that shows no emotion or reaction; without expressing feelings

Examples

To watch impassively
He watched the argument unfold impassively, refusing to take sides.
She listened impassively as the manager explained the company's decision.
The defendant sat impassively throughout the entire trial, betraying no sign of remorse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emotionlessly - without emotion or feeling
  • antonym: passionately - showing or caused by strong feelings or emotions
