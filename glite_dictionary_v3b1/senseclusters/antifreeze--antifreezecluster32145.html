antifreeze

noun
liquid that prevents freezing in engines
3.1 2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.t̬iˌfriːz / (AmE), / ˈæn.tiˌfriːz / (BrE)
a liquid added to the water in an engine's cooling system to prevent it from freezing in cold temperatures

Examples

Antifreeze liquid
If you forget to use antifreeze, your engine could be damaged by freezing.
It's important to check the antifreeze level in your car before winter arrives.
The mechanic added more antifreeze to the radiator to protect it from the cold.
