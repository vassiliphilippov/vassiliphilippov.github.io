tawa

noun
New Zealand evergreen tree
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːwə / (AmE), / ˈtɑːwə / (BrE)
an evergreen tree (Beilschmiedia tawa) native to New Zealand, known for its smooth bark and dense canopy

Examples

New Zealand tawa
The tawa is a common sight in New Zealand's native forests.
Birds often nest in the thick branches of the tawa.
The smooth bark of the tawa makes it easy to identify among other trees.
