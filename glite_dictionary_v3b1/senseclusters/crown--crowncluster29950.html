crown

verb
form/place at the topmost part
Pronunciation: / kraʊn / (AmE), / kraʊn / (BrE)
to be or put at the top of something, or to act as the final or most important part

Examples

To crown a tree
A golden ornament crowned the top of the Christmas tree.
A large glass dome crowns the center of the hotel lobby.
A magnificent statue crowns the hill, visible from miles away.
Her successful album release crowned years of hard work and dedication.
The grand fireworks display crowned the evening's festivities.
Winning the championship crowned his outstanding sports career.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cap - to cover or put something on top of
  • synonym: end - to bring to a conclusion; finish
