crown

verb
form/place at the topmost part
4.5 3.6
Pronunciation: /kraʊn/ /; / /kroʊn/ (AmE), /kraʊn/ (BrE)
to be or put at the top of something, or to act as the final or most important part

Examples

A magnificent statue crowns the hill, visible from miles away.

The grand fireworks display crowned the evening's festivities.
Winning the championship crowned his outstanding sports career.
