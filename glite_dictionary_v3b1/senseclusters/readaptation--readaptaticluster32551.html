readaptation

noun
adapting again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌædæpˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌædæpˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of adapting again to a changed environment, situation, or condition

Examples

Readaptation after travel
After a long stay abroad, his readaptation to his home country took several months.
Physical therapy is essential for the readaptation of patients recovering from major injuries.
The company supported employees in their readaptation to office work after remote working.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adaptation - the process of changing to suit new conditions
  • synonym: adjustment - the process of becoming used to a new situation
