life

noun
period of existence or functioning
1.0
Pronunciation: / laɪf / (AmE), / laɪf / (BrE)
the period during which something exists or functions, especially between birth and death

Examples

Bridge life
During its life, the bridge was repaired several times.
She dedicated her life to helping others.
The life of a light bulb depends on how often it is used.

Related Terms

  • antonym: death - the end of the period of life; the cessation of existence
  • synonym: lifetime - the duration of a person's or thing's existence
