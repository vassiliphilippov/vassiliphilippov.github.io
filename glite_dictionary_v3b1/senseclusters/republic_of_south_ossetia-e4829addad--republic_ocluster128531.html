Republic of South Ossetia

proper noun
disputed region in the South Caucasus
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv saʊθ ɒˈsɛtiə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv saʊθ ɒˈsiːtɪə / (BrE)
A region in the South Caucasus that declared independence from Georgia and is recognized by a few countries as an independent state, but is considered by most of the international community to be part of Georgia.
