running away

noun
the act of leaving without permission
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnɪŋ əˈweɪ / (AmE), / ˈrʌnɪŋ əˈweɪ / (BrE)
the act of leaving a place, especially without permission, where you are expected or supposed to be

Examples

His running away from home
After his running away from home, they searched the whole neighborhood for him.
Authorities recorded several incidents of running away from the school last year.
The child's running away caused great worry for his parents.
