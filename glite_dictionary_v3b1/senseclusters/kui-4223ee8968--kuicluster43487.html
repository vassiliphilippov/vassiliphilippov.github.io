Kui

noun
a Dravidian ethnic group in India
9.9
Pronunciation: / kuːˈi / (AmE), / kuːˈi / (BrE)
A member of the Dravidian people living mainly in southeastern India, particularly in Odisha and Andhra Pradesh.

Examples

Kui community in India
Anthropologists have studied the traditional villages of the Kui in eastern India.
Many Kui people still speak their indigenous language at home.
