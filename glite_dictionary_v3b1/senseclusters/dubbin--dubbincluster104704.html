dubbin

noun
leather-softening and waterproofing substance
9.9 8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈdʌb.ɪn/ /; / /ˈdʌb.ən/ (AmE), /ˈdʌb.ɪn/ /; / /ˈdʌb.ən/ (BrE)
