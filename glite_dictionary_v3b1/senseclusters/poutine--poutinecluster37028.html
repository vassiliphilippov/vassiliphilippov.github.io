poutine

noun
Canadian dish of fries with cheese curds and gravy
6.3
Pronunciation: / puˈtin / (AmE), / puːˈtiːn / (BrE)
A Canadian dish consisting of french fries topped with cheese curds and covered with brown gravy.

Examples

Canadian poutine dish
