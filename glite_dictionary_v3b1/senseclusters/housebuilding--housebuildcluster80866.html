housebuilding

noun
building houses
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊsˌbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or business of constructing houses

Examples

Housebuilding industry
The government invested heavily in housebuilding to address the housing shortage.
Rapid housebuilding has changed the landscape of the small town.
After the recession, housebuilding slowed down significantly across the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: construction - the process of building something, especially large structures
  • antonym: demolition - the act of destroying or tearing down buildings
