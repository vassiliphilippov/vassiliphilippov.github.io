solar facula

noun
bright spot on the Sun's photosphere
9.2 8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊlər ˈfækjələ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlə ˈfækjʊlə / (BrE)

Examples

Bright solar faculae
Scientists study solar faculae to better understand energy output variations from the Sun.
Solar observatories regularly monitor the development of solar faculae near active sunspot regions.
The brightness of a solar facula can make it stand out even more than surrounding areas on the Sun.
