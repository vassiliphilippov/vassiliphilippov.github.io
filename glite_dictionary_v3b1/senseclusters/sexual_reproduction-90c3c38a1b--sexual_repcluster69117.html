sexual reproduction

noun
biological reproduction by gamete fusion
3.4 0.45/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs…õk Éu…ôl ÀåriÀêpr…ôÀàd åk É…ôn / (AmE), / Ààs…õk Éu…ôl ÀåriÀêpr…ôÀàd åk É…ôn / (BrE)
the process of reproduction involving the union or fusion of male and female gametes, resulting in offspring with genetic material from both parents

Examples

Sexual reproduction process
Most animals rely on sexual reproduction to create genetic diversity in their populations.
Plants such as flowering species often use sexual reproduction to produce seeds.
Unlike bacteria, which typically reproduce asexually, humans require sexual reproduction to have offspring.

Synonyms

  • amphimixis
    9.9 0.00/m
    amphimixis is the technical biological term for reproduction by gamete fusion, while sexual reproduction is the general process producing offspring from two parents.
  • fecundation
    9.9 0.01/m
    fecundation is the act when male and female gametes join to fertilize an egg, while sexual reproduction covers the whole process to make offspring.
  • syngamy
    9.9 0.01/m
    syngamy is the cell or nuclear fusion of two gametes inside reproduction, while sexual reproduction is the broader process that produces new individuals.
