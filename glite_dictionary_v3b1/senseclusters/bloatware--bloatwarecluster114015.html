bloatware

noun
overloaded software
Pronunciation: / ˈbloʊtˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈbləʊtˌweə / (BrE)
unwanted or unnecessary software that takes up space and slows down a computer or device

Examples

Preinstalled bloatware
Many users uninstall bloatware from their new computers to improve performance.
The latest update turned the once simple app into bloatware that takes up too much space.
Companies often include bloatware on devices, even though most people never use it.
My new phone was full of bloatware that I never use.
Many users complain that bloatware slows down their computers.
He spent an hour removing bloatware from his laptop after buying it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adware - software that automatically displays or downloads advertising
  • antonym: lightweight - software that uses few resources and is efficient
  • antonym: malware - software designed to harm or exploit a computer
  • synonym: software - programs and applications used by a computer
