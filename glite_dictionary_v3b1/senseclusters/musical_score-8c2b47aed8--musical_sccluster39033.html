musical score

noun
written music; full arrangement for a composition
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːzɪkəl skɔr / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪkəl skɔː / (BrE)
A written or printed representation of a musical composition, showing all the vocal and instrumental parts arranged on separate staves.

Examples

A musical score sheet
Composers often annotate the musical score with expressive directions for performers.
She bought a musical score of her favorite symphony to study the arrangement.
The conductor spread the musical score across the stand before rehearsal began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sheet music - written representation of music, usually a single part or song
