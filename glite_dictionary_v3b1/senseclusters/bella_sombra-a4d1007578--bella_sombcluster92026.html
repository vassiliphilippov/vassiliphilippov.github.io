bella sombra

noun
South American evergreen tree with broad trunk
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌbɛl.ə ˈsɒm.brə / (AmE), / ˌbɛl.ə ˈsɒm.brə / (BrE)
A fast-growing evergreen tree native to South America, noted for its broad trunk with high water content and dark green oval leaves.
