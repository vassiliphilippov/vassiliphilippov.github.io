ill-starred

adjective
marked by misfortune or bad luck
Pronunciation: / ɪlˈstɑrd / (AmE), / ɪlˈstɑːd / (BrE)
Having or marked by bad luck or destined for misfortune.

Examples

An ill-starred event
She felt that her ill-starred decision had set her on a path of regret.
The ill-starred expedition ended in tragedy before reaching its goal.
Their ill-starred marriage was plagued by constant misfortune from the very beginning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ill-fated - doomed to failure or disaster
  • synonym: unlucky - having or bringing misfortune
