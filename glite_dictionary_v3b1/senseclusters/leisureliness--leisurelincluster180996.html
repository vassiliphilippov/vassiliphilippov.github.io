leisureliness

noun
the state of being leisurely or unhurried
Pronunciation: / ˈliʒərli.nəs / (AmE), / ˈlɛʒəli.nɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being relaxed and unhurried; slowness by virtue of being leisurely.

Examples

With leisureliness and calm
He completed his chores with a leisureliness that irritated his hurried colleagues.
The leisureliness of the afternoon stroll was just what she needed to unwind.
There is a certain leisureliness to life in small towns that many city dwellers find appealing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: haste - speed; urgency
  • synonym: leisure - free time when not working; use of free time
