intuitional

adjective
based on intuition
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.tuˈɪʃ.ən.əl / (AmE), / ˌɪn.tjuːˈɪʃ.ən.əl / (BrE)
relating to or based on intuition rather than logical reasoning

Examples

An intuitional feeling
Her intuitional sense told her that something was wrong, even without evidence.
The artist relied on an intuitional process to create his unique paintings.
Some leaders make decisions in an intuitional way rather than through careful analysis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intuitive - based on what one feels to be true without reasoning
  • antonym: logical - based on clear reasoning and facts
