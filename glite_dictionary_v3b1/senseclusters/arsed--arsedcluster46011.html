arsed

adjective
willing to make effort
Pronunciation: / ɑːst / (BrE)
feeling bothered or willing to make the effort to do something, usually used in negative statements

Examples

Not arsed to try
He wasn't arsed about finishing the project on time.
If you can't be arsed to help, just say so.
She rarely feels arsed to cook after a long day at work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bothered - feeling concerned enough to take action
  • synonym: motivated - having the desire or willingness to do something
  • antonym: uninterested - not willing or not caring to do something
