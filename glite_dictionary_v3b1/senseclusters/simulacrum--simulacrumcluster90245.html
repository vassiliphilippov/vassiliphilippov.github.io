simulacrum

noun
representation or image, often in art
3.0 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪmjəˈleɪkrəm / (AmE), / ˌsɪmjʊˈleɪkrəm / (BrE)
An image or representation of someone or something, especially a likeness of a person, often in sculpture or art.

Examples

A simulacrum statue
An artist created a lifelike simulacrum of the writer for the anniversary exhibition.
The bronze simulacrum stood in the public square, honoring the city's founder.
The museum displayed a simulacrum of the ancient queen carved out of marble.

Related Terms

  • synonym: image - a representation of the external form of a person or thing
