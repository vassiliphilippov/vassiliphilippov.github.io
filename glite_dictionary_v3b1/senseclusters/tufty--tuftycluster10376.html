tufty

adjective
with small tufts
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌfti / (AmE), / ˈtʌfti / (BrE)
having or covered with small clumps or tufts, especially of hair, grass, or fur

Examples

Tufty grass
The rabbit's tufty tail twitched as it hopped through the garden.
He wore a hat to cover his tufty hair on windy days.
Patches of tufty grass grew between the cracks in the pavement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shaggy - having long, thick, or messy hair or fur
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface; not rough or bumpy
