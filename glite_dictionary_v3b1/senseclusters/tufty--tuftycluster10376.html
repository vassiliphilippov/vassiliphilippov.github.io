tufty

adjective
with small tufts
5.5 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆtÊŒfti / (AmE), / ËˆtÊŒfti / (BrE)
having or covered with small clumps or tufts, especially of hair, grass, or fur

Examples

Tufty grass
The rabbit's tufty tail twitched as it hopped through the garden.
He wore a hat to cover his tufty hair on windy days.
Patches of tufty grass grew between the cracks in the pavement.

Synonyms

  • tufted
    5.5 0.07/m
    tufted has neat or decorative bunches of soft threads, hair, or feathers, while tufty means being covered in small natural tufts.
  • tussocky
    8.1 0.01/m
    tussocky describes ground with thick clumps of grass or plants, while tufty describes being covered with many small tufts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shaggy - having long, thick, or messy hair or fur
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface; not rough or bumpy
