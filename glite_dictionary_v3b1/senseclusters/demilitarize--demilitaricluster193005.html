demilitarize

verb
remove military capability
1.0
Pronunciation: / diˈmɪlɪtəˌraɪz /; / diːˈmɪlɪtəˌraɪz / (AmE), / diˈmɪlɪtəraɪz /; / diˈmɪlɪtəˌraɪz /; / diːˈmɪlɪtəraɪz / (BrE)
