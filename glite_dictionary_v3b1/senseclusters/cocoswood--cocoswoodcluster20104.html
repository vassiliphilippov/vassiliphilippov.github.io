cocoswood

noun
wood from the granadilla tree for instruments
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊkəsˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈkəʊkəsˌwʊd / (BrE)
the wood of the granadilla tree, used especially for making musical instruments such as clarinets

Examples

Cocoswood for instruments
Instrument makers prize cocoswood for its beauty and durability.
Some musicians believe that cocoswood gives wind instruments a uniquely rich sound.
The clarinet is often crafted from cocoswood due to its excellent tonal qualities.
