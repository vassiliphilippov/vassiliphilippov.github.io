channel

noun
means of selling products
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃænəl / (AmE), / ˈtʃanəl / (BrE)
a means by which a company's products are sold to customers, such as through retailers, wholesalers, or direct sales

Examples

Sales channel
Many startups choose direct sales as their primary channel to reach customers.
Selecting the right channel can greatly influence a product's success in the market.
The company expanded its channels by partnering with several major online retailers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distribution channel - route or pathway for getting products from producer to consumer
