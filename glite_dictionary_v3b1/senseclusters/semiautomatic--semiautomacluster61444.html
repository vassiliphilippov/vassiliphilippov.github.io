semiautomatic

adjective
partly automatic; partially self-operating
1.0 4.5
Pronunciation: /se.mi.ɔː.tə.ˈmæt.ɪk/ /; / /siː.mi.ɔː.tə.ˈmæt.ɪk/ /; / /sɪ.mi.ɔː.tə.ˈmæt.ɪk/ (AmE), /se.mi.ɔː.tə.ˈmæt.ɪk/ /; / /siː.mi.ɔː.tə.ˈmæt.ɪk/ (BrE)
partly automatic; (of a machine, device, or firearm) performing some tasks automatically but requiring manual input for others

Examples

The new rifle is a semiautomatic weapon designed for rapid but controlled firing.
