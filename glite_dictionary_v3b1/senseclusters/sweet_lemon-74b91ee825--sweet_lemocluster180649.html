sweet lemon

noun
a lemon variety with mildly sweet fruit
3.1 2.8
Pronunciation: /swiːt ˈlem.ən/ (AmE), /swiːt ˈlem.ən/ (BrE)
A variety of lemon tree that produces fruit with a mildly sweet, rather than tart, flavor; also, the fruit of this tree.

Examples

A sweet lemon fruit
She prefers sweet lemon in her desserts because of its gentle flavor.
The orchard specializes in growing sweet lemon alongside other citrus fruits.
Unlike regular lemons, a sweet lemon can be eaten fresh without added sugar.
