Cross of Lorraine

noun
a cross with two unequal bars, symbolic of Lorraine
Pronunciation: / krɔs əv ləˈreɪn / (AmE), / krɒs əv ləˈreɪn / (BrE)
a cross consisting of a vertical bar with two horizontal crossbars, the lower one longer than the upper, used as a symbol of Lorraine and in various historical contexts
