toadshade

noun
a woodland trillium with reddish flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊdˌʃeɪd / (AmE), / ˈtəʊdʃeɪd / (BrE)
A woodland plant of the northeastern United States, a type of trillium with sessile leaves and red or purple, pungent-smelling flowers.
