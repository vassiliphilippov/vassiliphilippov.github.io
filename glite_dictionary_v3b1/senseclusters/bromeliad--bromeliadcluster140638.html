bromeliad

noun
tropical plant family
Pronunciation: / broʊˈmiːliˌæd / (AmE), / brəˈmiːliˌad /; / brəˈmiːliˌæd / (BrE)
a tropical plant of the pineapple family, often with colorful leaves or flowers, commonly grown as an ornamental

Examples

A bromeliad plant
The gardener placed a bright red bromeliad in the center of the display.
Many bromeliads can grow without soil, attaching themselves to trees in the rainforest.
She admired the unique shape of the bromeliad leaves in the botanical garden.
The gardener placed a beautiful bromeliad in the center of the tropical display.
Many bromeliads can grow without soil, attaching themselves to trees in the rainforest.
She admired the bright pink leaves of the bromeliad on her windowsill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fern - a non-flowering plant with feathery leaves, often found in rainforests
  • synonym: orchid - a tropical plant with showy flowers, often grown as an ornamental
  • synonym: pineapple - a tropical fruit and plant in the bromeliad family
