ringed snake

noun
European snake with yellow collar
Pronunciation: / rɪŋd sneɪk / (AmE), / rɪŋd sneɪk / (BrE)
a harmless European snake, typically grey with a bright yellow or white collar around its neck; common in England

Examples

A ringed snake with collar
A ringed snake quickly slithered into the grass when it heard footsteps approaching.
Many people mistake the harmless ringed snake for a more dangerous species due to its size.
The ringed snake is often found near ponds and streams in the English countryside.
