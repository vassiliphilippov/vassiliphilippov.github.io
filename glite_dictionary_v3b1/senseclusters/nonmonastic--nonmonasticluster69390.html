nonmonastic

adjective
not monastic
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnməˈnæstɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnməˈnæstɪk / (BrE)
not relating to or characteristic of a monastery or monastic life

Examples

A nonmonastic life
She preferred a nonmonastic environment, enjoying the freedom of city life.
The retreat offered both monastic and nonmonastic accommodations for visitors.
His nonmonastic approach to spirituality focused on community involvement rather than seclusion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monastic - relating to monks or monasteries
