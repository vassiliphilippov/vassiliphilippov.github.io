stumpage

noun
value of standing timber
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌmpɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈstʌmpɪdʒ / (BrE)
the value or amount of standing timber on a piece of land, especially as timber to be cut and sold

Examples

Stumpage value
The company paid a high price for the stumpage on that forested land.
Accurate stumpage estimates are important for both landowners and logging companies.
The value of stumpage can fluctuate depending on market demand for timber.

Related Terms

  • synonym: timber - wood prepared for use in building and carpentry
