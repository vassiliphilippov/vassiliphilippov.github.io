captionless

adjective
without a caption
Pronunciation: / ˈkæpʃənləs / (AmE), / ˈkæpʃənləs / (BrE)
without a caption or descriptive text accompanying an image, illustration, or video

Examples

Captionless photos
The art gallery displayed a series of captionless photographs to encourage personal interpretation.
He posted a captionless image on social media, leaving his followers to guess its meaning.
The book included several captionless illustrations scattered throughout its pages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: uncaptioned - not provided with a caption
