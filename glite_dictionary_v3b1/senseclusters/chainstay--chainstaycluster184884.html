chainstay

noun
bicycle frame tube
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪnˌsteɪ / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪnˌsteɪ / (BrE)
the part of a bicycle frame that connects the bottom bracket to the rear wheel hub

Examples

The chainstay part
The chainstay must be strong enough to handle the force from the pedals.
He cleaned the chainstay to remove mud after the mountain bike race.
A cracked chainstay can make a bicycle unsafe to ride.
The mechanic inspected the chainstay for cracks after the accident.
A longer chainstay can improve the stability of a touring bicycle.
Dirt and mud often collect on the chainstay during off-road rides.

Related Terms

  • synonym: down tube - the tube on a bicycle frame running from the head tube to the bottom bracket
  • synonym: seatstay - the tube on a bicycle frame connecting the seat tube to the rear wheel hub
