campfire

noun
small outdoor fire for camping
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkæmpˌfaɪər / (AmE), / ˈkæmpˌfaɪə / (BrE)
a small outdoor fire used for warmth or cooking, especially when camping

Examples

A campfire at night
After a long hike, they built a campfire to stay warm.
The children roasted marshmallows over the campfire.
We sat around the campfire telling stories late into the night.
