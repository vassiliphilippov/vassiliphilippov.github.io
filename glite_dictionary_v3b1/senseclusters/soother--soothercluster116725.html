soother

noun
calming object
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːðər / (AmE), / ˈsuːðə / (BrE)
an object, especially a pacifier, used to calm or comfort a baby

Examples

Baby soother
The nurse handed the crying baby a soother to help him settle down.
Many parents keep a spare soother in their bag when traveling with infants.
A warm cup of tea can be a gentle soother after a stressful day.

Many parents keep a spare soother in the diaper bag just in case.
After dropping his soother, the baby began to cry until it was returned.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pacifier - a rubber or plastic object for babies to suck on for comfort
