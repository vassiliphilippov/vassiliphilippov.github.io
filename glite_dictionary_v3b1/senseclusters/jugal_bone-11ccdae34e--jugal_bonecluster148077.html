jugal bone

noun
bone forming cheek prominence
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuɡəl boʊn / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːɡəl bəʊn / (BrE)
the bone forming the arch beneath the eye and contributing to the prominence of the cheek, especially in vertebrate skulls

Examples

The jugal bone arch
Damage to the jugal bone can affect the shape of the face.
Paleontologists identified the dinosaur from the distinctive jugal bone.
The jugal bone is prominent in many reptile skulls.

Related Terms

  • synonym: zygomatic bone - the bone that forms the prominence of the cheek in humans
