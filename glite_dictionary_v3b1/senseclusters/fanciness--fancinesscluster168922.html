fanciness

noun
imaginative quality
5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈfæn.sɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈfæn.iː.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈfæn.sɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈfæn.iː.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being imaginative, whimsical, or full of creative ideas rather than simply decorative or elaborate

Examples

Some people prefer simplicity over the fanciness of elaborate designs.
