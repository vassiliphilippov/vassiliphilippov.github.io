parcel post

noun
postal service for sending packages
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːr.səl poʊst / (AmE), / ˈpɑː.səl pəʊst / (BrE)
a system within the postal service for sending packages rather than regular letters

Examples

Parcel post service
If you want to ship large items, parcel post is usually more economical than courier services.
She received the books through parcel post a few days after ordering them online.
The package was sent via parcel post to ensure safe delivery.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: express mail - faster postal service for urgent delivery
  • synonym: mail - letters and packages sent by post
  • synonym: registered mail - mail service providing proof of posting and delivery
