double

noun
a twofold raise in a card game
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌbəl / (AmE), / ˈdʌb(ə)l / (BrE)
an act of raising the stakes in a card game by a factor of two

Examples

A double in bridge
He made a bold double during the bidding round in bridge.
Her double forced the opponents to reconsider their strategy.
If you think they're bluffing, you can call or make a double.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: redouble - to raise the stakes again to an even higher level, especially after a double
