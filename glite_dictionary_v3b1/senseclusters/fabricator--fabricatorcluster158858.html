fabricator

noun
manufacturer or maker of parts
4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈfæ.brɪ.keɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈfæ.brɪ.keɪ.tə/ (BrE)
A person, company, or factory that makes or assembles products or parts, especially metal components or semiconductor wafers.

Examples

A metal fabricator
The fabricator announced a breakthrough in 3nm chip fabrication at the conference.
We hired a local fabricator to produce stainless steel brackets for the new handrails.
Many automotive fabricators have switched to automated welding for faster production.
