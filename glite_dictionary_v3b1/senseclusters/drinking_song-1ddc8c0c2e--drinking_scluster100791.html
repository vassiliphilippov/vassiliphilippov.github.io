drinking song

noun
song celebrating drinking, sung at parties
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ sɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ sɒŋ / (BrE)
a song that celebrates drinking and is traditionally sung at social gatherings where alcohol is consumed

Examples

A drinking song at parties
After a few beers, Thomas enthusiastically led everyone in a classic drinking song.
Many traditional drinking songs tell stories about friendship and celebration.
The group raised their glasses and began to sing a rowdy drinking song.
