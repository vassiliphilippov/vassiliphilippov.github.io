alstroemeria

noun
a plant with showy South American flowers
Pronunciation: / ˌælstroʊˈmɪəriə / (AmE), / ˌalstrɪˈmɪərɪə / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Alstroemeria, native to South America, known for its attractive, colorful flowers often used in bouquets.

Examples

Bright alstroemeria flowers
Gardeners often plant alstroemeria to add color to their summer flowerbeds.
She arranged a bouquet featuring pink alstroemerias and white roses.
The alstroemeria is prized for its vibrant petals and long-lasting blooms.

Related Terms
