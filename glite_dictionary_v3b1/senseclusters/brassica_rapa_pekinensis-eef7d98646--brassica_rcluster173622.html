Brassica rapa pekinensis

noun
variety of Chinese cabbage
Pronunciation: / brəˈsɪkə ˈreɪpə ˌpɛkɪˈnɛnsɪs / (AmE), / brəˈsɪkə ˈreɪpə ˌpɛkɪˈnɛnsɪs / (BrE)
A cultivated variety of Chinese cabbage with an elongated head and broad-stalked leaves, commonly used as a vegetable in East Asian cuisine.
