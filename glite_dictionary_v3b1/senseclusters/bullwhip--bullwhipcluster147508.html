bullwhip

verb
to strike with a whip
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbʊl.wɪp/ (AmE), /ˈbʊl.wɪp/ (BrE)
to strike or beat with a bullwhip (a long, heavy whip), especially to move or control cattle

He was accused of bullwhipping the horses, which led to an investigation.
