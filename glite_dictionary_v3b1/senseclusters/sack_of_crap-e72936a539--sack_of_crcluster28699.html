sack of crap

noun
thing of very poor quality or value
9.9
Pronunciation: / sæk əv kræp / (AmE), / sæk əv kræp / (BrE)
something regarded as worthless, of very poor quality, or contemptible

Examples

A sack of crap product
He spent $50 on what turned out to be a sack of crap that broke after one day.
Many people thought the new computer was a sack of crap and returned it immediately.
The review called the novel a sack of crap, disappointing fans of the author.
