suborder Tarsioidea

noun
suborder of primates with tarsiers
Pronunciation: / sʌbˌɔrdɚ tɑrˈsiɔɪdiə / (AmE), / sʌbˌɔːdə tɑːˈsɪɔɪdɪə / (BrE)
A taxonomic suborder of primates that includes the tarsiers, sometimes classified within the suborder Prosimii.

Examples

Suborder Tarsioidea primates
Members of the suborder Tarsioidea are characterized by their long hind limbs and large eyes.
Recent studies suggest that the suborder Tarsioidea should be separated from the lemurs and lorises.
Scientists have debated the classification of the suborder Tarsioidea within the primate order.
