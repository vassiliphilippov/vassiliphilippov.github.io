mirliton

noun
chayote vegetable; simple instrument
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪrlɪˈtɒn / (AmE), / ˌmɪrlɪˈtɒn / (BrE)
a pear-shaped green vegetable also known as chayote, or a simple musical instrument made from a tube and membrane

Examples

Sliced mirliton in stew
She added sliced mirliton to the stew for extra flavor.
The children laughed as they played a tune on a homemade mirliton.
In some regions, mirliton is a popular ingredient in traditional dishes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chayote - a green, pear-shaped vegetable from the gourd family
  • synonym: kazoo - a simple musical instrument that modifies the player's voice
