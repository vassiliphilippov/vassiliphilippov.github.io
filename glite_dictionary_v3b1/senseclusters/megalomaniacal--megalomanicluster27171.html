megalomaniacal

adjective
obsessed with power or self-importance
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛɡəˌloʊməˈnaɪəkəl / (AmE), / ˌmɛɡələʊməˈnʌɪəkəl / (BrE)
Having or showing an obsession with power or grandiose ideas of one's own importance.

Examples

Megalomaniacal ambitions
Her megalomaniacal ambitions ultimately led to her downfall.
Some critics described the dictator's plans as megalomaniacal and unrealistic.
The company's megalomaniacal CEO demanded total loyalty from everyone in the organization.
