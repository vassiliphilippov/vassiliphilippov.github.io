Mount McKinley

proper noun
highest mountain in North America (Alaska)
4.9
Pronunciation: /maʊnt mək.ˈɪn.liː/ (AmE), /maʊnt mək.ˈɪn.liː/ (BrE)
a mountain in south-central Alaska, formerly the official name for Denali, the highest peak in North America at 20,310 feet (6,190 meters)

Examples

From the park entrance, the snow-capped peak of Mount McKinley can sometimes be seen towering above the clouds.
Many climbers dream of reaching the summit of Mount McKinley despite its challenging weather conditions.
