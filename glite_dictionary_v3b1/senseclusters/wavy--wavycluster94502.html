Synonyms

  • ripply
    4.4 0.04/m
    ripply shows small moving waves like on water, while wavy describes larger, smoother curves on hair or cloth.
  • wavelike
    5.0 0.08/m
    wavelike looks or moves like a wave in shape or motion, while wavy often means fixed smooth curves on surfaces.
  • undulatory
    8.7 0.01/m
    undulatory has a formal tone and describes wave motion or form, while wavy is common and describes simple curved shapes.
  • wavery
    6.4 0.00/m
    wavery means having an uneven, wavering outline and sounds rare or old-fashioned, while wavy is common and clear for curves.
