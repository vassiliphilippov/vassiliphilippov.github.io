arteria alveolaris superior

noun
artery supplying upper teeth
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɑːr.ˈtɪr.iː.ə æl.viː.ə.ˈlær.ɪs suːp.ˈɪr.iː.ər/ (AmE), /ɑː.ˈtɪə.riə æl.viː.ə.ˈlær.ɪs suː.ˈpɪə.riə/ (BrE)
the artery that supplies blood to the upper teeth and adjacent gums

Examples

Arteria alveolaris superior artery
During dental surgery on the upper jaw, special care must be taken not to damage the arteria alveolaris superior.
Inflammation near the arteria alveolaris superior can lead to complications in dental procedures.
The arteria alveolaris superior supplies blood to all the upper teeth and some alveolar processes.
