homeworld

noun
place of origin
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmwɜːrld / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmwɜːld / (BrE)
the place or environment where a person or group feels most comfortable or at home, often their original or native world.

Examples

Her true homeworld
After years in advertising, she returned to the theatre, her real homeworld where everything made sense.
For many immigrants, their homeworld remains a place of cherished memories and traditions.
The science fiction novel describes an alien species searching for their lost homeworld.

Related Terms

  • synonym: homeland - the country or place where someone was born or feels they belong
  • synonym: origin - the point or place where something begins or is created
