Chelonidae

noun
family of sea turtles
9.5
Pronunciation: / kəˈloʊnɪdiː / (AmE), / kɪˈlɒnɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of large marine turtles that includes green turtles and hawksbill turtles.

Examples

The Chelonidae family
Research on Chelonidae has contributed greatly to our understanding of marine turtle conservation.
Species in the Chelonidae family, such as the green turtle, are found in tropical and subtropical waters worldwide.
The Chelonidae family is distinguished by their paddle-like limbs, perfectly adapted for life in the ocean.
