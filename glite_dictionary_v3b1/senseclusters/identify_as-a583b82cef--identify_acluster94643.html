identify as

verb
to consider oneself as having a particular identity
3.2
Pronunciation: / aɪˈdɛntɪˌfaɪ æz / (AmE), / aɪˈdɛntɪfaɪ æz / (BrE)
to recognize or describe oneself as belonging to a particular group, category, or identity; to consider oneself as having a specified identity

Examples

To identify as a local
After moving to Spain, he now identifies as a local.
Many students identify as environmentalists and are active in campus groups.
She identifies as non-binary and uses they/them pronouns.
