penetrate

verb
to insert the penis during sex
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnəˌtreɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɛnɪˌtreɪt / (BrE)
(of a man) to insert the penis into the vagina or anus of someone.

Examples

To penetrate the vagina
For pregnancy to occur, the sperm must be released inside after the penis penetrates the vagina.
He was charged with assault because he penetrated his victim without consent.
In some states, the law requires that the accused must have penetrated the victim for the act to be considered rape.
