jager

noun
hunter of wild animals
Pronunciation: / ˈjeɪɡər / (AmE), / ˈjeɪɡə / (BrE)
a person who hunts wild animals, especially for food or sport

Examples

A jager hunts deer
The experienced jager tracked the wounded stag through the snow until dusk.
In the old forests, a skilled jager was highly respected by the villagers.
The jager returned to camp with several rabbits and a wild turkey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hunter - a person who hunts animals
  • antonym: poacher - a person who hunts animals illegally
