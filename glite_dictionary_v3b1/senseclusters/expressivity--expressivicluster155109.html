expressivity

noun
ability to express
6.9
Pronunciation: / ɪkˌsprɛˈsɪvəti / (AmE), / ɪkˌsprɛˈsɪvəti / (BrE)
the quality of expressing thoughts or feelings clearly and effectively

Examples

Vocal expressivity
The expressivity of her voice made the song especially moving for the audience.
Artists often strive for greater expressivity in their work to connect with viewers.
His expressivity on stage set him apart from other performers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expressiveness - the ability to convey emotions or ideas clearly
  • antonym: inexpressiveness - the lack of ability to show thoughts or feelings
