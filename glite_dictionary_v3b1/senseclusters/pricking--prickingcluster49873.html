pricking

noun
act of piercing with a point
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈprɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of puncturing or piercing something with a small, sharp point

Examples

A pricking pain
A pricking sensation ran through her arm after the injection.
He felt a sharp pricking when he accidentally touched the thorn.
The tailor marked the fabric with a light pricking for guidance before sewing.
