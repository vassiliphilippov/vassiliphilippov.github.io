climate crisis

noun
urgent environmental emergency caused by climate change
2.3 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈklaɪ.mət ˈkraɪ.sɪs/ /; / /ˈklaɪ.mɪt ˈkraɪ.səs/ (AmE), /ˈklaɪ.mət ˈkraɪ.sɪs/ /; / /ˈklaɪ.mɪt ˈkraɪ.sɪs/ (BrE)
