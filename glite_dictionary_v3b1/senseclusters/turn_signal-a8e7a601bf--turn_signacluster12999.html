turn signal

noun
blinking vehicle light for indicating turns
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝːn ˌsɪɡ.nəl / (AmE), / ˈtɜːn ˌsɪɡ.nəl / (BrE)
a blinking light on a vehicle that indicates which direction the driver intends to turn or change lanes

Examples

Use turn signal before turning
Always use your turn signal before changing lanes to alert other drivers.
He forgot to activate his turn signal, causing confusion for the drivers behind him.
The turn signal on her car blinked as she prepared to make a left turn.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hazard lights - flashing vehicle lights used to indicate a problem or emergency
  • synonym: indicator - a device that shows the direction a vehicle will turn
