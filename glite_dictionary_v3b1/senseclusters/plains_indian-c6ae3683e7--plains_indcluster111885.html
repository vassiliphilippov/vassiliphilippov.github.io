Plains Indian

noun
member of a Great Plains Native American tribe
6.5
Pronunciation: /pleɪnz ˈɪn.diː.ən/ /; / /pleɪnz ˈɪn.dɪ.ən/ (AmE), /pleɪnz ˈɪn.dɪ.ən/ /; / /pleɪnz ˈɪn.diː.ən/ (BrE)

Examples

A Plains Indian tepee
