Fluttershy

proper noun
My Little Pony character
Pronunciation: / ˈflʌtərʃaɪ / (AmE), / ˈflʌtəʃaɪ / (BrE)
a gentle and shy character from the animated television series 'My Little Pony', known for her kindness and love of animals
