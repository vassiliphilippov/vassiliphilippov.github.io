bethink

verb
consider or remember carefully
Pronunciation: / bɪˈθɪŋk / (AmE), / bɪˈθɪŋk / (BrE)
to think about or remember something carefully; to remind yourself of something

Examples

To bethink a memory
As he wandered the halls, he bethinking memories of his childhood.
Before answering, he bethought himself of his earlier promise.
She bethought herself of a solution to the problem.
Before replying, she bethought herself and chose her words carefully.
He suddenly bethought himself that he had left the door unlocked.
She bethought herself of the promise she had made long ago.

  • antonym: forget - to fail to remember
  • synonym: remember - to bring to mind or think of again
