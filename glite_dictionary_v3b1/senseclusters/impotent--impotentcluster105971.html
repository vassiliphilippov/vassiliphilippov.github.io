impotent

adjective
powerless; lacking ability or strength
Pronunciation: / ˈɪm.pə.tənt / (AmE), / ˈɪm.pətənt / (BrE)
lacking power or ability to act effectively; powerless or helpless

Examples

Impotent against injustice
He felt impotent to stop the injustice he witnessed.
Many people experience impotent anger when they cannot change a difficult situation.
The government appeared impotent in the face of the growing crisis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: powerful - having great power or strength
  • synonym: powerless - lacking power or ability
