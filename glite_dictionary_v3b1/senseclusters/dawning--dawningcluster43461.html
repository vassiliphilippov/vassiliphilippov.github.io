dawning

noun
first light of day; early morning
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɔːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the time when the first light appears in the morning; the beginning of daylight

Examples

The dawning of morning
He promised to return by the dawning of the next morning.
The forest was eerily quiet at dawning, with only birds breaking the silence.
We set out on our hike at the dawning of a new day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dawn - the time of day when light first appears in the sky
  • antonym: night - the period of darkness between sunset and sunrise
  • degree higher: sunrise - the time when the sun rises above the horizon
