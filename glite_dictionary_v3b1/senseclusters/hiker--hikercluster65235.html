hiker

noun
person who walks long distances for pleasure
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪkɚ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪkə / (BrE)
a person who goes for long walks in the countryside or wilderness for pleasure or exercise

Examples

A hiker on trail
A group of hikers followed the winding trail through the forest.
Every experienced hiker knows the importance of packing enough water for a long trek.
The hiker stopped to rest and enjoy the view from the mountain ridge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trekker - someone who goes on long, often challenging journeys on foot
  • synonym: walker - a person who walks, especially for exercise or pleasure
