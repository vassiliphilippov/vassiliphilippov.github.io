passional

noun
devotional book on Christ
Pronunciation: / pæˈʃənəl / (AmE), / pəˈʃɪənəl / (BrE)
an archaic devotional book or written account describing the Passion or sufferings of Christ

Examples

A passional book
The monk spent hours reading the ancient passional in the candlelit chapel.
A rare passional was discovered among the monastery's oldest manuscripts.
Scholars studied the illuminated passional to learn about medieval religious practices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: passion play - a dramatic presentation depicting the Passion of Christ
