scalado

noun
attack with ladders
9.9
Pronunciation: / skəˈlɑːdoʊ / (AmE), / skəˈlɑːdəʊ / (BrE)
an attack on a fortified place in which ladders are used to climb and scale the walls

Examples

A scalado attack
The general ordered a daring scalado to break through the enemy's defenses.
During the night, the troops prepared their ladders for the planned scalado at sunrise.
Historical records describe a successful scalado that led to the capture of the fortress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assault - a violent attack, especially on a fortified place
  • synonym: siege - a military operation in which a place is surrounded and cut off
