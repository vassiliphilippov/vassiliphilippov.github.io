nonnumeral

adjective
not numerical
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈnuːmərəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈnjuːmərəl / (BrE)
not relating to, representing, or expressed as a number or numeral

Examples

Nonnumeral words
In this sentence, 'several' is a nonnumeral word indicating quantity.
The teacher asked for a nonnumeral answer, such as 'many' or 'few', instead of a specific number.
Some quantifiers, like 'some' or 'all', are considered nonnumeral in grammar.

Related Terms

  • antonym: numeral - relating to or representing a number
  • antonym: quantitative - relating to the amount or number of something
