rearview mirror

noun
camera-based rearview display
3.4 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / r6ar.vju ccm6ar.5a / (AmE), / r6a59.vju ccm6ar.59r / (BrE)
A vehicle mirror system that displays a live video image from a rear-facing camera on a screen, instead of using a reflective glass surface.

Examples

Digital rearview mirror
The new model comes standard with a rearview mirror that shows a bright, wide-angle camera feed at night.
She tilted the visor and the rearview mirror automatically switched to the camera view for better visibility.
Many owners replaced their old glass mirrors with a rearview mirror that streams a wider field of view.
