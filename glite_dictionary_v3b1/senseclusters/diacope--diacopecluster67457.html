diacope

noun
repetition with interruption
9.9
Pronunciation: /daɪ.ˈæk.ə.piː/ /; / /dɪ.ˈæk.ə.piː/ (AmE), /daɪ.ˈæk.ə.piː/ /; / /dɪ.ˈæk.ə.piː/ (BrE)
