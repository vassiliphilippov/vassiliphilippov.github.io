orange

noun
a citrus tree bearing oranges
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrɪndʒ, ˈɑːrɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ / (BrE)
A citrus tree of the genus Citrus, especially Citrus × sinensis, that bears sweet, round, orange-colored fruits.

Examples

Orange tree
Farmers in Florida grow thousands of orange trees for their fruit.
She planted an orange in her garden, hoping it would thrive.
The orange in our backyard produces fruit every winter.
