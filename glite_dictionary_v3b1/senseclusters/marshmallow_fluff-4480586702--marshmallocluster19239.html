marshmallow fluff

noun
sweet white marshmallow-like spread
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrʃˌmæloʊ flʌf / (AmE), / ˈmɑːʃˌmæl.əʊ flʌf / (BrE)
a very sweet, soft white spread made to resemble the texture and flavor of marshmallow candy, often used as a dessert topping or filling

Examples

Marshmallow fluff spread
She spread marshmallow fluff on her toast for a sugary treat.
The sandwich was filled with peanut butter and marshmallow fluff.
You can use marshmallow fluff to make quick and easy fudge.
