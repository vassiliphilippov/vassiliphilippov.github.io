micropenis

noun
abnormally small penis (medical)
8.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Àåma…™.kro äÀàpiÀê.n…™s / (AmE), / Àåm å…™.kr…ô äÀàpiÀê.n…™s / (BrE)
A medical condition in which the penis is abnormally small, typically defined as a stretched penile length significantly below the average for age and development.

Examples

A micropenis condition
A diagnosis of micropenis is made when penile length falls significantly below the average for the child's age.
Psychological support is important for individuals diagnosed with micropenis and their families.
Treatment options for micropenis often include hormone therapy in early childhood.
