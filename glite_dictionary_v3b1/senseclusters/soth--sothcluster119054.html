soth

interjection
archaic affirmation
9.9
Pronunciation: / soʊθ / (AmE), / səʊθ / (BrE)
an archaic interjection used to emphasize the truth of a statement, meaning 'truly' or 'indeed'

Examples

Soth indeed
Soth, I have never heard such a remarkable tale in all my years.
He declared, 'Soth! The rumors are true, and the king has returned.'
The old knight exclaimed 'Soth' to show his agreement with the council's decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indeed - used to emphasize a statement or response
  • synonym: verily - truly; certainly; used to affirm a statement
