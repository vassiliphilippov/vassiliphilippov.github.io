trading operations

noun
activities managing financial trades at a brokerage
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪdɪŋ ˌɑpəˈreɪʃənz / (AmE), / ˈtreɪdɪŋ ˌɒpəˈreɪʃənz / (BrE)
The activities related to conducting and managing financial transactions, especially at a brokerage, including executing trades and maintaining customer records.

Examples

Efficient trading operations
Efficient trading operations are essential for reducing errors in large brokerages.
She was hired to oversee the trading operations department and ensure regulatory compliance.
The bank enhanced its trading operations to handle more complex financial instruments.
