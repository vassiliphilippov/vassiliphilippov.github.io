literal interpretation

noun
understanding words by their exact meaning
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪtərəl ˌɪntərprəˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈlɪtərəl ˌɪntəprɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
an interpretation of words or text that follows their explicit, exact meaning, without considering metaphor or exaggeration

Examples

Literal interpretation of text
His literal interpretation of her words caused some confusion in the conversation.
Some readers prefer a literal interpretation of the Bible, while others seek deeper, symbolic meanings.
The instructions require a literal interpretation to avoid costly mistakes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: figurative interpretation - an interpretation based on metaphorical or symbolic meaning
