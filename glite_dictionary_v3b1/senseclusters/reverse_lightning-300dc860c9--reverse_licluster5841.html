reverse lightning

noun
upward blue lightning from storm tops
2.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɜrs ˈlaɪtnɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈvɜːs ˈlaɪtnɪŋ / (BrE)
a rare atmospheric phenomenon involving discharges lasting about 10 milliseconds, bursting from the tops of storm clouds in blue cones that widen as they flash upward
