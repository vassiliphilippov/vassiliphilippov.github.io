apricot

noun
tree bearing peach-like edible fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈæp.rɪ.kɑt, ˈeɪ.prɪ.kɑt /; / ˈæp.rɪˌkɑt, ˈeɪ.prɪˌkɑt / (AmE), / ˈæp.rɪ.kɒt, ˈeɪ.prɪ.kɒt /; / ˈeɪ.prɪ.kɒt / (BrE)
a small, round fruit with soft, yellow to reddish skin and a sweet, slightly tart flavor; also, the tree that produces this fruit
