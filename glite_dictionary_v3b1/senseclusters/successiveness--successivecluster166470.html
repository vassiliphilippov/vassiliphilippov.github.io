successiveness

noun
the state of following in sequence
Pronunciation: / səkˈsɛs.ɪv.nəs / (AmE), / səkˈsɛs.ɪv.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of things occurring one after another in time; sequential order.

Examples

The successiveness of events
Critics admired the successiveness in how the artist arranged the paintings.
The successiveness of the events made the investigation much easier.
The successiveness of the tasks allowed for a smooth workflow in the office.
