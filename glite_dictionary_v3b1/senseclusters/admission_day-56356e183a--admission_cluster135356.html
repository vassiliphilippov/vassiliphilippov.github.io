Admission Day

noun
U.S. state admission holiday
Pronunciation: / ədˈmɪʃ.ən deɪ / (AmE), / ədˈmɪʃ.ən deɪ / (BrE)
In some U.S. states, a legal holiday commemorating the date the state was admitted to the Union.

Examples

Admission Day holiday
California celebrates Admission Day on September 9 each year.
Schools and offices may close in observance of Admission Day.
The governor issued a proclamation in honor of Admission Day.
