torpedo

noun
electric ray of the Torpediniformes order
9.9
Pronunciation: / tɔrˈpiːdoʊ / (AmE), / tɔːˈpiːdəʊ / (BrE)
a slow-moving, bottom-dwelling ray of the order Torpediniformes, with a rounded body and electric organs on each side of the head that can emit strong electric discharges

Examples

An electric torpedo
A diver spotted a large torpedo near the rocky seabed during the expedition.
Marine biologists studied the torpedo to understand how its electric organs functioned.
The torpedo hides under the sand, waiting for prey to come close before using its electric shock.

  • synonym: electric ray - a ray fish capable of producing electric discharges
