torpedo

noun
electric ray of the Torpediniformes order
9.9 7.7
Pronunciation: /tɔːr.ˈpiː.doʊ/ /; / /tər.ˈpiː.doʊ/ (AmE), /tɔː.ˈpiː.dəʊ/ /; / /tə.ˈpiː.dəʊ/ (BrE)
