enamel

noun
hard outer layer of a tooth
5.9 4.2
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈnæ.məl/ /; / /ə.ˈnæ.məl/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈnæ.məl/ /; / /ə.ˈnæ.məl/ (BrE)
the hard, white, outer layer that covers and protects the crown of a tooth

Examples

Tooth enamel
Brushing your teeth helps to keep the enamel strong and free from decay.
Certain acidic foods and drinks can erode tooth enamel over time.
Dentists recommend fluoride toothpaste to protect enamel and prevent cavities.
