unabsorbable

adjective
cannot be taken in
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəbˈzɔrbəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnəbˈzɔːbəbəl / (BrE)

The loss was so great that it became unabsorbable within the current financial year.
