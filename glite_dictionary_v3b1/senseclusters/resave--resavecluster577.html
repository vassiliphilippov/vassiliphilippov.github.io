resave

noun
saving again
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈseɪv / (AmE), / ˌriːˈseɪv / (BrE)
an act of saving something again, especially a file or document, to update or preserve changes

Examples

A quick resave
After making edits, a quick resave ensured her changes were not lost.
The software prompted a resave when it detected an updated version of the file.
A resave is often necessary after recovering a document from a backup.
