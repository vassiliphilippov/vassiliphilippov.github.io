meadowlark

noun
North American songbird with a yellow breast
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdoʊˌlɑrk / (AmE), / ˈmɛdəʊˌlɑːk / (BrE)
A North American songbird with a yellow breast, known for its melodious song.

Examples

Meadowlarks sing sweetly
Meadowlarks are often found in open grasslands and prairies.
The bright yellow breast of the meadowlark makes it easy to identify.
We spotted a meadowlark singing on a fence post during our walk.
