dearest

noun
beloved person; term of endearment
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪrɪst / (AmE), / ˈdɪərɪst / (BrE)
a person who is loved very much; used as a term of endearment for someone one cares about deeply

Examples

My dearest friend
He whispered goodnight to his dearest and turned off the light.
My dearest, thank you for standing by me through everything.
She wrote a letter to her dearest while he was away at war.

Related Terms

  • synonym: darling - a person who is very dear; term of affection
