take it

verb
assume or infer (something)
Pronunciation: / teɪk ɪt / (AmE), / teɪk ɪt / (BrE)
used to indicate that one is assuming or inferring something from what has been said or seen

Examples

I take it you disagree
I take it you disagree with the decision.
I take it you’ve finished your work already?
So, you take it that they’ve left the building?

Related Terms

  • synonym: assume - to suppose something without proof
  • synonym: presume - to suppose something is true based on probability
