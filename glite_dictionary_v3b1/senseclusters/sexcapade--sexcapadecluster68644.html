sexcapade

noun
sexual adventure or illicit affair
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkskəˌpeɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɛkskəˌpeɪd / (BrE)
an episode involving sexual adventure, especially one considered illicit or unconventional

Examples

Celebrity sexcapades
Hollywood films often exaggerate the sexcapades of celebrities for dramatic effect.
She finally confided to her friends about the wild sexcapade from her younger days.
The tabloid published details of the senator's sexcapade that shocked the public.
