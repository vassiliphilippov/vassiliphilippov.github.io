skyjacker

noun
aircraft hijacker
9.3
Pronunciation: /skaɪ.ˈdʒæk.ər/ (AmE), /skaɪ.ˈdʒæk.ə/ (BrE)
a person who illegally takes control of an aircraft by force, usually to demand something or change its destination

Examples

The skyjacker threatened crew
The skyjacker threatened the crew and demanded to be flown to another country.
Authorities quickly responded to the report of a skyjacker on board the international flight.
After the incident, security measures were increased to prevent future skyjackers from boarding planes.
