valgus

noun
limb deformity; part turned outward
Pronunciation: / ˈvælɡəs / (AmE), / ˈval.ɡəs / (BrE)
A deformity in which a part of a limb is abnormally displaced away from the midline of the body.

Examples

A valgus deformity
After the injury, she developed a mild valgus at her ankle joint.
Correction of a valgus alignment often requires surgical intervention.
The surgeon explained that the patient's knee showed a pronounced valgus deformity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: varus - a deformity in which a part of a limb is abnormally displaced toward the midline of the body
