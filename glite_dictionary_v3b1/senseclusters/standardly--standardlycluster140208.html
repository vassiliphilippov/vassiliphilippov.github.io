standardly

adverb
in standard form
Pronunciation: / ˈstændərdli / (AmE), / ˈstændədli / (BrE)
in the usual, standard, or accepted way; according to what is generally done or expected

Examples

Used standardly in writing
In academic writing, it is important to use words standardly to avoid confusion.
The teacher standardly corrects students' pronunciation to match the accepted form.
This word is standardly pronounced with the stress on the first syllable.
The medication is standardly prescribed for patients with high blood pressure.
This test is standardly administered at the beginning of each school year.
The equipment was standardly inspected before use to ensure safety.
The software is standardly configured to save files in the Documents folder.
Students are standardly assigned to classes based on their grade level.
This feature is standardly included in all new models of the device.
