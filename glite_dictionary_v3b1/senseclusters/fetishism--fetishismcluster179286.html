fetishism

noun
belief in magical power of objects
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛt̬.ɪˌʃɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈfɛt.ɪˌʃɪz.əm / (BrE)
the belief that certain objects possess magical powers and are worthy of worship

Examples

Religious fetishism
Anthropologists studied the tribe's fetishism and their reverence for sacred stones.
He wrote an essay exploring the role of fetishism in early religious practices.
In some cultures, fetishism centers around objects believed to grant good fortune.
