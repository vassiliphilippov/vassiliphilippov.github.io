scout car

noun
fast, armored military reconnaissance vehicle
Pronunciation: / skaʊt kɑr / (AmE), / skaʊt kɑː / (BrE)
a fast, lightly armored military vehicle with four-wheel drive and an open top, used for reconnaissance

Examples

A scout car mission
During the reconnaissance mission, their scout car was lightly damaged by shrapnel.
He drove the scout car swiftly over the rough terrain, scanning the horizon for movement.
The platoon advanced ahead in a scout car to survey enemy positions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: armored car - a military vehicle covered with protective armor
