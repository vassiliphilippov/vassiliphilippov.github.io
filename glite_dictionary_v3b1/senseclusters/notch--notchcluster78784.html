notch

noun
a small cut or indentation
Pronunciation: / nɑtʃ / (AmE), / nɒtʃ / (BrE)
a small cut, indentation, or V-shaped mark on an edge or surface, often made for record, measurement, or decoration

Examples

A notch on wood
He carved a notch on the stick for each day he was there.
She cut a notch in her belt after losing weight.
There’s a small notch in the handle to mark the center.
He carved a notch in the tree to mark the trail.
The carpenter made a small notch to fit the two pieces together.
The coin had a notch on its edge, making it easy to identify.
He carved a notch into the wooden stick to mark his progress.
She counted the notches on her belt to remember her trips.
The knife left a deep notch in the blade after striking the metal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indentation - a deep recess or notch on the edge or surface of something
  • synonym: nick - a small cut, generally shallow or minor
  • antonym: protrusion - something that sticks out from a surface; opposite of an indentation
