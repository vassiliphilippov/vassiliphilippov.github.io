notch

noun
a small cut or indentation
5.3 3.2
Pronunciation: /nɑːtʃ/ (AmE), /nɒtʃ/ (BrE)
a small cut, indentation, or V-shaped mark on an edge or surface, often made for record, measurement, or decoration

Examples
