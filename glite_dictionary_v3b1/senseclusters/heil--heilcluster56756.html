heil

noun
authoritarian salute
9.9
Pronunciation: / haɪl / (AmE), / haɪl / (BrE)
a salute, shout, or greeting expressing praise or allegiance, especially associated with authoritarian or extremist movements

Examples

A loud heil
The crowd responded with a loud heil as the leader entered the hall.
He raised his arm in a traditional heil during the ceremony.
The use of the heil is now widely condemned due to its historical associations.

  • synonym: salute - a gesture or expression of respect or greeting
