punt

noun
a type of football kick
Pronunciation: / pʌnt / (AmE), / pʌnt / (BrE)
(in football) a kick in which the ball is dropped from the hands and kicked before it touches the ground.

Examples

A long punt
A short punt gave the opposition good field position.
His 60-yard punt drew applause from the crowd.
The team executed a perfect punt to pin their opponents deep in their own territory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drop kick - a kick in which the ball is dropped and kicked as it touches the ground
  • synonym: kickoff - a kick that starts play in football
