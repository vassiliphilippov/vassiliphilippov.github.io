summarizer

noun
one who summarizes
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌməˌraɪzər / (AmE), / ˈsʌməˌraɪzə / (BrE)
a person or tool that creates a brief summary of information or content

Examples

The summarizer tool
The summarizer condensed the lengthy report into a single page of key points.
Using an online summarizer can help students quickly review important information before exams.
As a skilled summarizer, she could explain complex topics in just a few sentences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abridger - a person who shortens a text while keeping its main ideas
  • antonym: expander - a person or tool that makes something longer or more detailed
  • synonym: summarize - to give a brief statement of the main points
