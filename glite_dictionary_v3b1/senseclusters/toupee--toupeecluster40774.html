toupee

noun
small hairpiece for baldness
8.3
Pronunciation: / tuːˈpeɪ / (AmE), / tuːˈpeɪ / (BrE)
a small hairpiece worn to cover partial baldness, typically by men

Examples

A toupee hairpiece
He wore a toupee to hide his thinning hair.
Many people could not tell that he was wearing a toupee.
The wind nearly blew his toupee off during the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wig - an artificial covering of hair for the head, covering all or most of it
