mediate

verb
be intermediate or a link between two things
6.3 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈmiː.di.eɪt/ /; / /ˈmiː.deɪt/ (AmE), /ˈmiː.di.eɪt/ /; / /ˈmiː.deɪt/ (BrE)
to occupy an intermediate or middle position or to serve as a connecting link or stage between two things

Examples

Air mediates contact
A layer of air mediates between the ocean and the atmosphere.
The book mediates the gap between scientific research and public understanding.
This cultural movement mediates between traditional customs and modern practices.
