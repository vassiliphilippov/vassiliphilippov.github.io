kiss my ass

x
express rude defiance or dismissal
Pronunciation: / kɪs maɪ æs / (AmE), / kɪs maɪ ɑːs / (BrE)
(vulgar, offensive) Used to rudely tell someone that you do not care about their opinion or demand, or to show strong defiance or dismissal.

Examples

To kiss my ass
If anyone tries to stop me, they can just kiss my ass.
If you don't like my decision, you can kiss my ass and walk away.
She was so frustrated she told her boss to kiss my ass and quit on the spot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: go to hell - used to angrily tell someone to leave or express scorn
  • synonym: screw you - rude phrase used to express dismissal or contempt
