crank call

noun
malicious or prank telephone call
Pronunciation: / kræŋk kɔl / (AmE), / kræŋk kɔːl / (BrE)
a telephone call made maliciously or as a prank, often to annoy or harass the recipient

Examples

A crank call prank
After receiving yet another crank call, she decided to report the harassment to the police.
Teenagers sometimes make crank calls for amusement, not realizing how disruptive they can be.
The radio host recounted a bizarre crank call he once received during a live show.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prank call - a phone call made as a prank to trick, annoy, or joke with someone
