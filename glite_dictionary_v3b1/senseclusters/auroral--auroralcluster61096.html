auroral

adjective
characteristic of the dawn
7.0
Pronunciation: / ɔːˈrɔrəl / (AmE), / ɔːˈrɔːrəl / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling the dawn; characteristic of early morning light.

Examples

Auroral light at dawn
A faint auroral glow filtered through the trees at daybreak.
The poet described the auroral colors of the early morning sky.
The sky was painted with an auroral light as the sun began to rise.
