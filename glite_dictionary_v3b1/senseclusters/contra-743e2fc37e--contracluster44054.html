Contra

noun
Nicaraguan anti-Sandinista guerrilla member
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːntrə / (AmE), / ˈkɒntrə / (BrE)
a member of a guerrilla group that fought against the left-wing Sandinista government in Nicaragua during the 1980s

Examples

A Contra fighter
As a Contra, he participated in cross-border raids against the Sandinista army.
Reports detailed the activities of several Contras operating near the Honduran border.
The United States secretly funded the Contras despite controversy over their human rights record.
