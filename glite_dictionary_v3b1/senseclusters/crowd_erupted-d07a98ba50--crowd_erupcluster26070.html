crowd erupted

verb
when an audience reacts loudly and suddenly
5.1 3.3
Pronunciation: /kraʊd ɪ.ˈrʌp.tɪd/ /; / /kraʊd ə.ˈrʌp.tɪd/ (AmE), /kraʊd ɪ.ˈrʌp.tɪd/ /; / /kraʊd ə.ˈrʌp.tɪd/ (BrE)
