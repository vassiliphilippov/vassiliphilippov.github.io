punditry

noun
expert commentary
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌndɪtri / (AmE), / ˈpʌndɪtri / (BrE)
the activity of giving expert opinions or commentary, especially in the media

Examples

Political punditry
The political talk show was filled with heated punditry after the debate.
Many viewers are skeptical of the constant punditry on cable news channels.
Social media has given rise to a new wave of online punditry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: analysis - detailed examination and explanation of something
  • synonym: commentary - spoken or written discussion giving opinions or explanations
  • antonym: reporting - the act of presenting news or information without personal opinion
