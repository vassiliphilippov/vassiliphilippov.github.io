bop

verb
to hit or strike hard
2.6
Pronunciation: / bɑp / (AmE), / bɒp / (BrE)
to hit or strike someone or something hard, usually in a quick or playful way

Examples

To bop his head
He jokingly bopped his friend on the head with a rolled-up newspaper.
If you don't pay attention, the ball might bop you right in the nose.
She bopped the punching bag with surprising strength during her workout.

Related Terms

  • antonym: caress - to touch or stroke gently
  • synonym: punch - to hit with a closed fist
  • synonym: smack - to hit with a sharp slap or blow
