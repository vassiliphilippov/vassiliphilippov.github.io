quarter

noun
US or Canadian 25-cent coin
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtə / (BrE)
A United States or Canadian coin worth one fourth of a dollar (25 cents).

Examples

A quarter coin
Do you have a quarter I can borrow for the parking meter?
She dropped a quarter into the vending machine and selected a soda.
The arcade game costs one quarter per play.
