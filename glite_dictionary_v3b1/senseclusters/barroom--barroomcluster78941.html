barroom

noun
room for serving alcoholic drinks
4.0 1.02/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…ërÀårum / (AmE), / Ààb…ëÀêÀåruÀêm / (BrE)
A room or establishment where alcoholic drinks are served at a counter.

Examples

A barroom counter
After work, he liked to relax in his favorite barroom.
The old barroom was filled with laughter and music every night.
They met in a barroom on the edge of town.

  • public house

  • tavern
    3.5 6.60/m
