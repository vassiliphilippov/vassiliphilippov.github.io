deprecatingly

adverb
modestly or self-belittling
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛprəˌkeɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈdɛprɪˌkeɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that belittles or downplays oneself, often to show modesty or humor

Examples

Speak deprecatingly about yourself
He spoke deprecatingly about his achievements, insisting they were nothing special.
She smiled deprecatingly when complimented on her artwork, saying anyone could have done it.
The author deprecatingly described his own writing as just average in the interview.

Related Terms

  • antonym: proudly - in a way that shows pride or self-respect
  • synonym: self-deprecatingly - in a way that makes fun of or belittles oneself
