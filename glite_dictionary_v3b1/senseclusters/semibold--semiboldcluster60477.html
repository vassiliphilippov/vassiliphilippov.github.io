semibold

noun
medium font weight
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛmiˌboʊld / (AmE), / ˈsɛmiˌbəʊld / (BrE)
a typeface or font weight that is slightly heavier than regular but lighter than bold

Examples

A semibold font
The designer used a semibold font for the section titles to make them stand out.
Please select semibold for all subheadings in the document to improve readability.
This typeface offers regular, semibold, and bold weights for different design needs.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: bold - a typeface or font weight that is heavy and thick
  • degree lower: regular - a standard or normal typeface or font weight
