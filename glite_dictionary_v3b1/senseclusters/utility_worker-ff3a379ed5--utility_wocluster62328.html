utility worker

noun
worker who performs various tasks as needed
Pronunciation: / juˈtɪlɪti ˈwɜrkər / (AmE), / juːˈtɪlɪti ˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
a worker who is employed to perform a variety of tasks as needed, often in maintenance or public service sectors

Examples

The utility worker repairs
After the storm, the utility worker was called in to restore power to several neighborhoods.
As a utility worker, she learned to handle electrical repairs, water line issues, and sanitation tasks.
The company hired several utility workers during the busy season to help with maintenance and emergency repairs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: handyman - a person skilled at a wide range of repairs, typically around the home
