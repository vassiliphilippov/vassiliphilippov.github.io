nonsensory

adjective
not involving senses
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈsɛnsəri / (AmE), / nɒnˈsɛnsəri / (BrE)
not related to or involving the senses or sensory perception

Examples

Nonsensory influences
The researchers examined nonsensory influences on decision-making in the experiment.
Some learning difficulties are caused by nonsensory factors rather than problems with hearing or sight.
The therapist considered both sensory and nonsensory aspects of the patient's experience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sensory - relating to the senses or sensation
