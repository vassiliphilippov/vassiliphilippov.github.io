gas-washing bottle

noun
laboratory bottle for passing gas through liquid
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæs ˌwɑʃɪŋ ˈbɑtəl / (AmE), / ˈɡas ˌwɒʃɪŋ ˈbɒtl̩ / (BrE)
A laboratory bottle designed for passing gas through a liquid to saturate the liquid or remove impurities from the gas.

Examples

A gas-washing bottle in lab
During the demonstration, the instructor showed how a gas-washing bottle can remove contaminants from a gas stream.
The chemist used a gas-washing bottle to purify the air before introducing it into the reaction chamber.
To saturate the solution with chlorine, the technician connected a gas-washing bottle to the supply line.
