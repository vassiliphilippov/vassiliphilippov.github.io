emulsification

noun
mixing of liquids
6.3
Pronunciation: /ɪ.mʌl.sɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ə.mʌl.sɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɪ.mʌl.sɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ə.mʌl.sɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
