emulsification

noun
mixing of liquids
6.3
Pronunciation: / ɪˌmʌlsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌmʌlsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of mixing two liquids that do not normally combine (like oil and water) so that one is dispersed in the other as small droplets
