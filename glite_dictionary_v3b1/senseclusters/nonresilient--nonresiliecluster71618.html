nonresilient

adjective
not able to recover quickly or easily
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.rɪˈzɪl.jənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.rɪˈzɪl.i.ənt / (BrE)
Not able to recover quickly or easily from difficulties; lacking resilience.

Examples

A nonresilient economy
A nonresilient economy struggles to recover from sudden downturns or crises.
After the fire, the old, nonresilient materials in the building crumbled and could not be restored.
His nonresilient attitude made it hard for him to cope with failure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: resilient - able to recover quickly from difficulties; flexible
