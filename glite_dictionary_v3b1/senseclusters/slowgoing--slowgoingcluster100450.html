slowgoing

adjective
not energetic or enterprising; slow-paced
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsloʊˌɡoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsləʊˌɡəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
not inclined to be energetic or enterprising; proceeding at a slow pace.

Examples

A slowgoing person
Her slowgoing attitude made it difficult to meet deadlines in the fast-paced environment.
Progress was slowgoing, but eventually the team finished the project.
The committee's slowgoing approach frustrated some of the more ambitious members.

Related Terms

  • antonym: energetic - showing or involving great activity or vitality
  • synonym: lethargic - feeling sluggish and lacking energy
