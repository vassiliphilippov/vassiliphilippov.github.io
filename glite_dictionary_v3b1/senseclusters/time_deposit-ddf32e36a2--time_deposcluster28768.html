time deposit

noun
bank deposit held for a fixed period
5.2 4.5
Pronunciation: /taɪm dɪ.ˈpɑː.zɪt/ /; / /taɪm də.ˈpɑː.zɪt/ (AmE), /taɪm dɪ.ˈpɒ.zɪt/ /; / /taɪm də.ˈpɒ.zɪt/ (BrE)
a bank deposit that cannot be withdrawn before a specified date or period of notice, typically evidenced by a certificate

Examples

A time deposit certificate
He invested his savings in a time deposit to earn higher interest.
Many banks offer special rates for new time deposit accounts.
You cannot withdraw funds from a time deposit before the maturity date without penalty.

Related Terms

  • synonym: certificate of deposit - a certificate issued by a bank to a person depositing money for a specified length of time
  • antonym: demand deposit - a bank deposit that can be withdrawn on demand without advance notice
