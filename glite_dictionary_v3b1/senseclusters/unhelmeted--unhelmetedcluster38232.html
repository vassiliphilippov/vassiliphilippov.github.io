unhelmeted

adjective
without a helmet
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɛlmɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛlmɪtɪd / (BrE)
not wearing or not provided with a helmet

Examples

Unhelmeted motorcyclist
The motorcyclist was riding unhelmeted despite the safety regulations.
Children should never be allowed to cycle unhelmeted on public roads.
The report highlighted the risks faced by unhelmeted construction workers.
