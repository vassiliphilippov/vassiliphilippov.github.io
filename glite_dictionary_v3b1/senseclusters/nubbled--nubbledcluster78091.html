nubbled

adjective
bumpy or knotted surface
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌbəld / (AmE), / ˈnʌbəld / (BrE)
having a surface covered with small lumps, knots, or raised bumps, especially in fabric or material

Examples

A nubbled fabric
The nubbled fabric gave the chair a unique, tactile feel.
She preferred the nubbled texture of the sweater over smoother materials.
A nubbled surface can add visual interest to home decor items.

Related Terms

  • antonym: smooth - having an even, flat surface without lumps or bumps
  • synonym: textured - having a noticeable surface feel or pattern
