loan shark

noun
person charging extortionate loan interest
Pronunciation: / loʊn ʃɑrk / (AmE), / ləʊn ʃɑːk / (BrE)
a person who lends money at extremely high and often illegal interest rates, typically to those unable to borrow from legitimate sources

Examples

A loan shark lender
Authorities warned citizens about the dangers of dealing with a loan shark.
Desperate for cash, he borrowed money from a loan shark despite the risks.
The loan shark threatened his clients if they couldn't pay back their debts on time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: usurer - a person who lends money at unreasonably high rates of interest
