weary

adjective
physically and mentally fatigued
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪri / (AmE), / ˈwɪəri / (BrE)
feeling or showing tiredness, especially after exertion or sustained effort; physically and mentally fatigued

Examples

Weary traveler
After a long day of work, she felt weary and ready for sleep.
His weary eyes told the story of endless nights without proper rest.
We grew weary of waiting for the delayed train.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tired - lacking strength or energy because of the need for rest
