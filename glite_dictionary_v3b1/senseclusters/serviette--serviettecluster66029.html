serviette

noun
table napkin (mainly British)
Pronunciation: / ˌsɜːr.viˈɛt / (AmE), / ˌsɜː.viˈet / (BrE)
A small piece of cloth or paper used at meals to wipe the mouth and protect clothing.

Examples

A serviette at meals
Could you pass me a serviette, please?
She placed a serviette neatly on her lap before starting her meal.
The restaurant folded each serviette into an elegant shape.
