halfheartedly

adverb
without enthusiasm
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌhæfˈhɑːrtɪdli / (AmE), / ˌhɑːfˈhɑːtɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that shows little enthusiasm, energy, or commitment

Examples

To halfheartedly agree
She halfheartedly agreed to join the team, not really wanting to participate.
The students halfheartedly clapped at the end of the long presentation.
He halfheartedly cleaned his room, leaving most of the mess untouched.

Related Terms

  • antonym: enthusiastically - in a way that shows great excitement or interest
  • synonym: reluctantly - in an unwilling or hesitant way
