girth

verb
fasten a saddle by tying a band
6.0
Pronunciation: / ɡɜrθ / (AmE), / ɡɜːθ / (BrE)
to fasten a saddle on a horse by tying a band (a girth) around its belly

Examples

To girth a horse
After girthing the mare, she led it out to the paddock.
Make sure to girth the saddle tightly so it doesn't slip.
The stable hand carefully girthed the horse before the ride.

Related Terms
