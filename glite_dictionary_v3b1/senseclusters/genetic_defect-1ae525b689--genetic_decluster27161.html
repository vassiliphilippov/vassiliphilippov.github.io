Synonyms

  • genetic disease
    3.5 0.58/m
    genetic disease is the usual medical name for illnesses from genes in people, while genetic defect sounds more like a gene fault.
  • genetic disorder
    4.2 0.64/m
    genetic disorder is the clinical term for conditions caused by gene or chromosome errors, while genetic defect refers to a faulty gene.
  • genetic abnormality
    4.6 0.13/m
    genetic abnormality describes unusual gene or chromosome features seen in tests, while genetic defect means a harmful fault causing disease.
  • hereditary disease
    3.9 0.13/m
    hereditary disease refers to illnesses passed down through families, while genetic defect highlights a faulty gene rather than family pattern.
  • inherited disease
    3.0 0.16/m
    inherited disease stresses that parents often pass an illness to children, while genetic defect stresses the faulty gene itself.
  • inherited disorder
    4.2 0.07/m
    inherited disorder names conditions passed from parents by genes, while genetic defect focuses on the specific faulty gene causing problems.
  • hereditary condition
    4.0 0.05/m
    hereditary condition is a broad, often gentle label for traits passed in families, while genetic defect sounds like a clear gene fault.
