homosocial

adjective
same-sex social
Pronunciation: / ˌhoʊməˈsoʊʃəl / (AmE), / ˌhəʊməˈsəʊʃəl / (BrE)
describing social relationships or environments involving only people of the same gender, not romantic or sexual

Examples

Homosocial relationships
The club had a homosocial atmosphere, with most members being men who socialized mainly with each other.
Many traditional workplaces were once highly homosocial, limiting opportunities for women.
Researchers studied the effects of homosocial bonding in all-male sports teams.
The fraternity maintained a homosocial environment where only men participated in activities.
Many sports teams develop strong homosocial bonds among their members.
The novel explores homosocial friendships in an all-girls school setting.
The club provided a homosocial environment where men could discuss their interests freely.
Many sports teams foster strong homosocial bonds among their members.
The novel explores homosocial friendships between women in a historical setting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heterosocial - involving social relationships between people of different sexes
  • antonym: homosexual - attracted to people of the same gender
