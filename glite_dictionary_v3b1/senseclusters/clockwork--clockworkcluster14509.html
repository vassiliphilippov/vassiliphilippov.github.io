clockwork

noun
geared mechanism driven by a spring
4.5 2.06/m
Pronunciation: / Ààkl…ëÀêk.w…ùÀêk / (AmE), / Ààkl…ík.w…úÀêk / (BrE)
a mechanism consisting of a series of geared wheels driven by a coiled spring, like those found inside mechanical clocks or watches

Examples

He marveled at the intricate clockwork inside the old grandfather clock.

The antique toy was powered by a clockwork mechanism that needed winding every hour.
