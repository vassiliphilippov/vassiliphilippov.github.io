halophytic

adjective
salt-tolerant (plants)
9.9
Pronunciation: /hæ.lə.ˈfɪt.ɪk/ /; / /hə.lə.ˈfɪt.ɪk/ (AmE), /hæ.lə.ˈfɪt.ɪk/ /; / /hə.lə.ˈfɪt.ɪk/ (BrE)
able to grow or thrive in salty soil or water, especially referring to plants.

Examples

Halophytic plants
Many halophytic species are found in coastal salt marshes around the world.
The halophytic nature of these plants allows them to survive where others cannot.
Researchers study halophytic plants to develop crops that can grow in saline soils.
