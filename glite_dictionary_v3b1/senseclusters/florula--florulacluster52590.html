florula

noun
local plant list
Pronunciation: / flɔːˈruːlə / (AmE), / flɒˈruːlə / (BrE)
a list or record of the plant species found in a small or specific area

Examples

A valley florula
The researcher created a florula to document all the plant species in the valley.
A detailed florula can help conservationists understand the biodiversity of a small island.
Students were assigned to compile a florula for the school garden as part of their project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: checklist - a list of items to be checked or considered
  • degree higher: flora - the plants of a particular region or period
