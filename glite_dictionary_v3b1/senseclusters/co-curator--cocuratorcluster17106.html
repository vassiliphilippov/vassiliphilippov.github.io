co-curator

noun
joint exhibition organizer
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊ kjʊˈreɪtər / (AmE), / ˌkəʊ kjʊˈreɪtə / (BrE)
a person who shares the responsibility of organizing and managing an exhibition or collection with another curator

Examples

The co-curator role
She worked as a co-curator for the international photography exhibition.
The co-curator collaborated closely with artists to design the gallery layout.
As co-curator, he helped select the pieces displayed in the museum's new collection.
