night vision

noun
device mode for low-light imaging
3.8
Pronunciation: /naɪt ˈvɪʒ.ən/ (AmE), /naɪt ˈvɪʒ.ən/ (BrE)
a feature or mode in cameras, phones, vehicles, or specialized equipment that produces images or improves visibility in low-light or dark conditions, often using infrared sensors or computational enhancement.

Examples

Night vision camera
The latest smartphones advertise an advanced night vision mode, producing clear photos in near darkness.
Police cars now use thermal cameras and built-in night vision to spot pedestrians on dark roads.
Many action cameras offer electronic night vision that increases brightness without using visible light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: low-light photography - techniques and settings used to take photographs when there is little available light
  • synonym: night mode - a camera or phone setting designed to improve photos taken in low light
