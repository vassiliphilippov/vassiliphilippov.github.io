radioprotection

noun
protection from harmful radiation
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌreɪdioʊprəˈtɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌreɪdiəʊprəˈtɛkʃən / (BrE)
the protection of people or objects from the harmful effects of radiation, especially ionizing radiation

Examples

Radioprotection measures
Research into radioprotection has become increasingly important with the use of medical imaging.
The lead suit provides essential radioprotection for workers in nuclear plants.
These guidelines establish best practices for radioprotection during X-ray procedures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: radiation shielding - barrier or material used to block or reduce radiation
