cinnamon toast

noun
toast with cinnamon and sugar
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪnəmən toʊst / (AmE), / ˈsɪnəmən təʊst / (BrE)
Bread that is toasted and spread with butter, then sprinkled with a mixture of cinnamon and sugar, sometimes with other spices.

Examples

Cinnamon toast with butter
A sprinkle of nutmeg can add extra flavor to your cinnamon toast.
For breakfast, she made cinnamon toast and served it with hot tea.
He remembers eating cinnamon toast as a child on cold winter mornings.
