whipped cream

noun
light, aerated beaten cream
2.9
Pronunciation: / wɪpt kriːm / (AmE), / wɪpt kriːm / (BrE)
Cream that has been beaten until it becomes light and fluffy, often used as a dessert topping or ingredient.

Examples

Whipped cream topping
He ordered a slice of pie with whipped cream on the side.
She topped her hot chocolate with a generous dollop of whipped cream.
The cake was decorated with fresh strawberries and whipped cream.
