manqué

adjective
person who failed to achieve an ambition
Pronunciation: / mɑnˈkeɪ / (AmE), / mɒŋˈkeɪ / (BrE)
(of a person) having failed to become what they might have been; unfulfilled in realizing an ambition.

Examples

A manqué artist
Despite his writing talents, he considered himself a novelist manqué after abandoning his first book.
Many viewed him as a politician manqué due to his unfulfilled ambitions in public office.
She is a ballerina manqué, having left dance school to pursue a business career.
