tyke

noun
uncouth or ill-bred person
Pronunciation: / taɪk / (AmE), / taɪk / (BrE)
an uncouth or ill-bred person, lacking culture or refinement

Examples

Rude tyke
Despite his wealth, many saw the arrogant businessman as a mere tyke.
She refused to associate with such a tyke, preferring the company of refined friends.
The villagers considered him a tyke for his rude behavior and lack of manners.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gentleman - a man of good education and refined manners
  • synonym: lout - an uncouth or aggressive person
  • synonym: ruffian - a violent or lawless person
