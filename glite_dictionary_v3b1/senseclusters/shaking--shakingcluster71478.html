Synonyms

  • shake
    1.0 50/m
    shake is the general verb for moving or causing movement quickly, while shaking describes that ongoing movement or trembling.
  • vibrate
    2.8 6.01/m
    vibrate means moving very quickly back and forth in small steps, while shaking is a more general quick back-and-forth movement.
  • quiver
    4.5 5.34/m
    quiver is to tremble with small, rapid motions often from strong feeling, while shaking covers any quick back-and-forth movement.
  • shiver
    1.2 13/m
    shiver is a slight uncontrollable trembling usually from cold or fear, while shaking can be any quick vibrating motion.
  • wobble
    3.8 3.50/m
    wobble is to move unsteadily from side to side in an irregular way, while shaking is more even rapid vibration.
  • jitter
    4.6 0.32/m
    jitter is to shake in quick, nervous jerks often from anxiety, while shaking is a broader physical back-and-forth motion.
  • wiggle
    1.0 4.86/m
    wiggle is to make small playful or careful side-to-side movements, while shaking is a strong or vibrating back-and-forth motion.
  • quake
    4.2 0.39/m
    quake is to shake violently, often as in earthquakes, while shaking can be gentler or faster small vibrations.
