premillennialism

noun
belief in Christ's return before millennium
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːmɪˈlɛniəlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌpriːmɪˈlɛniəlɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief that Christ will return to earth before a thousand-year period of peace and righteousness

Examples

Premillennialism belief
Many evangelical groups adhere to premillennialism as a central part of their faith.
The debate between premillennialism and postmillennialism has shaped Christian theology for centuries.
His interpretation of biblical prophecy is rooted in premillennialism.

Related Terms

  • synonym: millenarianism - the belief in a coming major transformation of society, often involving a thousand-year reign
  • antonym: postmillennialism - the belief that Christ will return after a thousand-year period of peace
