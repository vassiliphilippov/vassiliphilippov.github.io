male

adjective
of the sex that produces sperm or pollen
Pronunciation: / meɪl / (AmE), / meɪl / (BrE)
of or denoting the sex that produces gametes (such as sperm or pollen) which can fertilize the female's egg or ovule.

Examples

Male flowers
In some bird species, only the male has brightly colored feathers.
Many plants have both male and female flowers on the same stem.
The male lion is distinguished by its impressive mane.
