pull for the underdog

verb
support the less favored competitor
8.0
Pronunciation: / pʊl fər ði ˈʌndɚˌdɑɡ / (AmE), / pʊl fə ði ˈʌndədɒɡ / (BrE)
to support or root for the competitor who is expected to lose or is less favored to win

It is inspiring to see so many fans pull for the underdog in the tournament.
