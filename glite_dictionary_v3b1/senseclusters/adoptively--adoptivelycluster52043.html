adoptively

adverb
in an adoptive manner
5.6
Pronunciation: / əˈdɑptɪvli / (AmE), / əˈdɒptɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to adoption or adoptive relationships

Examples

Adoptively placed child
The child was adoptively placed with a loving family at a young age.
He was adoptively connected to his siblings through his parents' decision.
She identifies adoptively with her new family traditions and culture.
