advocaat

noun
Dutch egg liqueur
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈædvəˌkɑt / (AmE), / ˈædvəkɑːt / (BrE)
a sweet, creamy liqueur made from eggs, sugar, and brandy, originally from the Netherlands

Examples

A glass of advocaat
She served a glass of advocaat with dessert at the holiday party.
Traditional Dutch recipes often include advocaat as an ingredient in festive desserts.
He enjoyed sipping advocaat by the fireplace during the winter months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brandy - a strong alcoholic spirit distilled from wine or fermented fruit juice
  • synonym: eggnog - a sweet, creamy drink made with eggs, milk, sugar, and alcohol
