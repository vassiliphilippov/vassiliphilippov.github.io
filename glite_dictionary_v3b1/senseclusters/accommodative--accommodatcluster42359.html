accommodative

adjective
willing to adapt or help
3.6 5.5
Pronunciation: / əˈkɑməˌdeɪtɪv /; / əˈkɑːməˌdeɪtɪv / (AmE), / əˈkɒməˌdeɪtɪv / (BrE)
