tel

noun
ancient settlement mound
9.9
Pronunciation: /tel/ (AmE), /tel/ (BrE)
a mound or hill formed by the accumulated remains of ancient settlements, often an archaeological site in the Middle East

Examples

Ancient tel site
The archaeologists began digging at the ancient tel to uncover evidence of early human life.
Many important artifacts have been found in the layers of a tel in Israel.
Standing atop the tel, you can see the outline of the old city walls.
