round scad

noun
small fusiform Atlantic fish
4.5
Pronunciation: / raʊnd skæd / (AmE), / raʊnd skad / (BrE)
A small, spindle-shaped fish of the western Atlantic, belonging to the family Carangidae, commonly found in coastal waters.
