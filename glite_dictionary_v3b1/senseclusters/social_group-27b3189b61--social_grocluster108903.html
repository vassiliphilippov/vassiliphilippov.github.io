social group

noun
group sharing social relationship
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəl ɡruːp / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃl ɡruːp / (BrE)
a group of people who interact and share a common social relationship or identity

Examples

A social group of friends
A family can be considered a social group with strong emotional bonds.
Every social group develops its own norms and traditions.
Researchers studied the dynamics within the social group over several months.
