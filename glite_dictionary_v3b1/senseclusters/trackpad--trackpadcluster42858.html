trackpad

noun
touch-sensitive input device
Pronunciation: / ˈtrækˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈtrækˌpæd / (BrE)
a flat, touch-sensitive surface on a computer or device used to control the cursor or pointer

Examples

Laptop trackpad
Many laptops come with a built-in trackpad instead of a separate mouse.
He adjusted the sensitivity of his trackpad to make it easier to use.
If your trackpad stops working, you can connect an external mouse.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mouse - a handheld device used to control a computer cursor
  • synonym: touchpad - a touch-sensitive input device for controlling a cursor
