Magnolia fraseri

noun
deciduous magnolia tree native to southeastern US
8.8
Pronunciation: / mæɡˈnoʊliə ˈfreɪzəri / (AmE), / mæɡˈnəʊliə ˈfreɪzəri / (BrE)
A small deciduous tree of the magnolia family, native to the southeastern United States, with large leaves arranged in coiled clusters at the branch tips.
