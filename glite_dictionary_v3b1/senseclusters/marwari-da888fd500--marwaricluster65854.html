Marwari

noun
Indian horse breed
Pronunciation: / mɑːrˈwɑːri / (AmE), / mɑːˈwɑːri / (BrE)
a horse breed from the Marwar region of India, known for its inward-turning ear tips and endurance

Examples

Marwari horse breed
The Marwari is easily recognized by its unique inward-curving ears.
Many riders admire the Marwari for its endurance and loyalty on long journeys.
A beautiful Marwari led the parade during the festival in Rajasthan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Arabian - a horse breed known for its stamina and distinctive head shape
  • synonym: Thoroughbred - a horse breed famous for racing and speed
