broad in the beam

adjective
having a wide width (esp. of a boat or body)
9.9
Pronunciation: / brɔd ɪn ðə bim / (AmE), / brɔːd ɪn ðə biːm / (BrE)
having a wide width, especially used of a boat for stability, or sometimes referring humorously to a person's hips or body

Some swimmers are naturally broad in the beam, which can help with buoyancy.
