deworming

adjective
removing parasitic worms
Pronunciation: / diˈwɜrmɪŋ / (AmE), / diːˈwɜːmɪŋ / (BrE)
describing medicine or treatment intended to remove parasitic worms from the body of humans or animals

Examples

Deworming medicine
The veterinarian recommended a deworming treatment for the new puppies.
Farmers schedule regular deworming procedures to keep their livestock healthy.
A deworming medicine is often given to children in areas where parasites are common.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anthelmintic - a substance that destroys or expels parasitic worms
