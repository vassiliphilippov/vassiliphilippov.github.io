Outer Mongolia

proper noun
historical term for present-day Mongolia
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtər mɒnˈɡoʊliə /; / ˈaʊtər mɒŋˈɡoʊliə / (AmE), / ˈaʊtə mɒŋˈɡəʊliə / (BrE)
the region that is now the country of Mongolia, distinguished from Inner Mongolia (which is part of China); used especially in historical contexts
