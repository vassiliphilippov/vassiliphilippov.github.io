belt

verb
sing loudly and forcefully
Pronunciation: / bɛlt / (AmE), / bɛlt / (BrE)
to sing (a song or note) loudly and with great force

Examples

To belt a song
Even though he had a cold, he belted through the performance with surprising energy.
She took the microphone and belted out her favorite song at the karaoke bar.
The singer belted the chorus so powerfully that the entire audience cheered.

Related Terms

  • antonym: croon - to sing softly or gently
  • synonym: shout - to speak or call out loudly
