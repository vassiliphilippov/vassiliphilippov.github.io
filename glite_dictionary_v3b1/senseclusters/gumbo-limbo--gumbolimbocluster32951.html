gumbo-limbo

noun
tropical American tree with reddish bark
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌmboʊ ˈlɪmboʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌmbəʊ ˈlɪmbəʊ / (BrE)
a tropical American tree (Bursera simaruba) with smooth reddish bark, yielding a resin used in cements and varnishes

Examples

The gumbo-limbo tree
Locals harvest the resin from the gumbo-limbo tree to use in natural varnishes.
The gumbo-limbo is known for its distinctive red bark and resilience in tropical climates.
We saw a towering gumbo-limbo during our hike in the Caribbean forest.
