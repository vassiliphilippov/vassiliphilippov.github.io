sound perception

noun
recognition and interpretation of sounds
1.9
Pronunciation: / saʊnd pərˈsɛpʃən / (AmE), / saʊnd pəˈsɛpʃən / (BrE)
the process of recognizing and interpreting sounds as meaningful phenomena

Examples

Sound perception process
Advances in technology have improved sound perception for people with hearing aids.
Children with normal hearing typically develop sound perception very early in life.
Musicians often train their sound perception to distinguish subtle differences in pitch and tone.
