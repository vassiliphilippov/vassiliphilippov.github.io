drill

noun
systematic training by repetition
4.2 2.8
Pronunciation: /drɪl/ (AmE), /drɪl/ (BrE)
a training exercise or repeated practice to improve a skill or memory (including military or educational contexts)

Examples

Fire drill practice
Daily language drill improved their pronunciation and confidence.
Military drill often involves marching in formation again and again until perfected.
The teacher used a drill to help students memorize their multiplication tables.
Each morning, the recruits participated in a drill to perfect their marching skills.
Spectators watched the impressive drill during the military parade.
The sergeant led the drill with sharp commands and precise movements.

  • synonym: maneuver - movement or series of moves requiring skill, especially in military context
  • synonym: practice - the repeated exercise of an activity to improve skill
  • synonym: rehearsal - practice or trial performance for later full performance
