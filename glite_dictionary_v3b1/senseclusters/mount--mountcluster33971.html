mount

noun
an act of climbing up
4.8 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / maÊŠnt / (AmE), / maÊŠnt / (BrE)
an act of climbing up something, especially a hill, mountain, or other elevation

Examples

A steep mount
Her daring mount up the steep rocks impressed the entire group.
The team's final mount to the summit was slowed by harsh weather.
We rested before attempting the exhausting mount to the castle.
