God knows how

adverb
used to express ignorance of how something was done
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡɑd noʊz haʊ / (AmE), / ɡɒd nəʊz haʊ / (BrE)
used to say that the way something happened is unknown or hard to explain

Examples

God knows how he did it
God knows how he managed to finish the project on time.
She made it through the exam, God knows how, given how little she studied.
The old car started this morning—God knows how after all those problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: somehow - by some means; in some unspecified way
