Revolutionary United Front

proper noun
Sierra Leone rebel group (1980s–2000s)
9.9
Pronunciation: / rəˌvoʊluˈʃɜnɛri ˈjuːnaɪtɪd ˈfʌnt / (AmE), / rɪˌvɒljʊˈʃənəri ˈjuːnaɪtɪd ˈfʌnt / (BrE)
a rebel group active in Sierra Leone from the 1980s to early 2000s, known for fighting to overthrow the government, seizing control of diamond-producing areas, and committing widespread atrocities including the use of child soldiers

Examples

Revolutionary United Front rebel group
Many civilians suffered at the hands of the Revolutionary United Front during the conflict.
The Revolutionary United Front was responsible for some of the worst atrocities during the Sierra Leone Civil War.
The United Nations imposed sanctions due to the actions of the Revolutionary United Front.
