flyspeck

noun
tiny spot, esp. from fly excrement
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪˌspɛk / (AmE), / ˈflaɪˌspɛk / (BrE)
a tiny dark spot or stain, especially one made by the excrement of a fly

Examples

A flyspeck stain
A flyspeck on the white tablecloth caught his attention during dinner.
She wiped the flyspeck off the window with a cloth.
The old painting was covered in flyspecks after years of neglect.
