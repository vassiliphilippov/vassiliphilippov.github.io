wild oat grass

noun
a type of grass similar to oats
2.4
Pronunciation: / waɪld oʊt ɡræs / (AmE), / waɪld əʊt ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a species of grass commonly found in meadows and pastures, known for its resemblance to cultivated oats

Examples

The wild oat grass plant
After the rain, patches of wild oat grass sprang up throughout the meadow.
She identified several types of wild oat grass growing at the edge of the field.
The cows grazed lazily among the wild oat grass of the summer pasture.
