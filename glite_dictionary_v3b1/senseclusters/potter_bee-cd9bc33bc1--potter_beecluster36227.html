potter bee

noun
solitary bee building mud nests
9.5 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ët…ôr bi / (AmE), / Ààp…ít…ô biÀê / (BrE)
A solitary bee that builds its nest from mud or pebbles, cemented together and often attached to a plant.

Examples

A potter bee nest
I spotted a potter bee carefully constructing its nest on the stem of a rose bush.
The garden wall had several potter bee nests attached to its sunlit surface.
Unlike honeybees, the potter bee works alone and prefers building nests with mud.
