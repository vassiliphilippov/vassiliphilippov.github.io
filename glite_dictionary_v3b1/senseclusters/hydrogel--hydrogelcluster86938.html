hydrogel

noun
a water-based gel
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪdroʊˌdʒɛl / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdrəʊˌdʒɛl / (BrE)
A gel in which water is the main component of the dispersion medium, commonly used in medical and scientific applications.

Examples

A hydrogel dressing
A hydrogel dressing helps keep wounds moist, promoting faster healing.
Scientists developed a hydrogel to slowly release medication over time.
The contact lenses are made from a soft hydrogel material that allows oxygen to reach the eyes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gel - a semi-solid substance that can flow like a thick liquid
