meditation

noun
deep or prolonged contemplation
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛdəˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of engaging in deep and continuous thought, usually on a serious or philosophical subject

Examples

Meditation on life
His meditation on the poem revealed layers of hidden meaning.
Regular meditation helps her maintain clarity and peace of mind.
She fell into a state of meditation while pondering the meaning of life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contemplation - long and thoughtful observation or consideration
  • synonym: reflection - serious thought or consideration
