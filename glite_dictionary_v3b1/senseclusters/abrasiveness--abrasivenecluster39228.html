abrasiveness

noun
quality of being rough or causing abrasion
6.0 5.5
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈbreɪ.sɪv.nəs/ (AmE), /ə.ˈbreɪ.sɪv.nəs/ (BrE)
The quality of being rough or harsh enough to cause wear, scratching, or scraping to other materials.

Examples
