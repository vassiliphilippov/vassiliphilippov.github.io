acclaim

verb
praise or applaud enthusiastically
Pronunciation: / əˈkleɪm / (AmE), / əˈkleɪm / (BrE)
to praise or welcome someone or something enthusiastically and publicly

  • antonym: boo - to show disapproval by shouting or making noise
  • antonym: criticize - to express disapproval of someone or something
  • synonym: praise - to express warm approval or admiration of someone or something
