sausage meat

noun
minced, spiced meat for sausages
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔsɪdʒ ˌmit / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsɪdʒ ˌmiːt / (BrE)
meat that is minced, mixed with spices, and used to make sausages or cooked as patties

Examples

Minced sausage meat

You can use sausage meat as a filling for pies or Scotch eggs.
