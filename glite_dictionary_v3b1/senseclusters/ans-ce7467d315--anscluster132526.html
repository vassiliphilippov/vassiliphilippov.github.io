Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ ˌɛn ˈɛs / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˌɛn ˈɛs / (BrE)
abbreviation for 'autonomic nervous system', the part of the nervous system that controls involuntary body functions

Examples

ANS abbreviation
The ANS regulates heart rate, digestion, and breathing automatically.
Doctors study the ANS to understand disorders affecting involuntary body functions.
Stress can cause the ANS to trigger a fight-or-flight response.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autonomic nervous system - the part of the nervous system controlling involuntary actions
  • antonym: central nervous system - the part of the nervous system consisting of the brain and spinal cord
