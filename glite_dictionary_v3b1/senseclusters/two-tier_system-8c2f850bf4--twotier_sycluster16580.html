two-tier system

noun
a system with two levels or layers
Pronunciation: / tu ˈtɪr ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / tuː ˈtɪə ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
a system or structure with two distinct levels or layers, often used for pricing, service, or access

Examples

A two-tier system of wages
A two-tier system of wages led to discontent among the employees.
Many schools criticize the two-tier system as unfair to less privileged students.
The company operates a two-tier system for health insurance, offering basic and premium plans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tiered system - a system arranged in multiple grades or layers
