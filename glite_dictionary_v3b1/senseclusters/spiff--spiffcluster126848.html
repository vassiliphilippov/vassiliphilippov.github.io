spiff

noun
sales bonus
8.8
Pronunciation: / spɪf / (AmE), / spɪf / (BrE)
a small bonus or reward given, especially to sales staff, for selling a particular product or achieving a target

Examples

A spiff bonus
The company offered a spiff to employees who sold the most products each month.
Each time she sold an extended warranty, she earned a small spiff as a reward.
Managers use a spiff to motivate the sales team during busy seasons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bonus - an extra amount of money given as a reward
  • synonym: commission - a payment to someone for selling something, usually a percentage of the sale
