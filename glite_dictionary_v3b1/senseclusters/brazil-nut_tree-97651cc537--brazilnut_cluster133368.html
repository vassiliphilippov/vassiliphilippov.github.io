brazil-nut tree

noun
South American tree producing Brazil nuts
5.9
Pronunciation: / brəˈzɪl nʌt tɹi / (AmE), / brəˈzɪl nʌt tɹiː / (BrE)
A tall South American tree (Bertholletia excelsa) that produces Brazil nuts, known for its large woody fruits and economic importance.

Examples

The brazil-nut tree fruit
Harvesting nuts from the brazil-nut tree provides income for local communities.
Many animals rely on the brazil-nut tree for food and shelter.
The brazil-nut tree is one of the tallest and oldest trees in the Amazon rainforest.
