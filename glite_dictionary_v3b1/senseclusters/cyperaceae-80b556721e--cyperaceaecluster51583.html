Cyperaceae

noun
family of grass-like sedge plants
9.9
Pronunciation: / saɪˈpɛr.əˌsiː /; / saɪpəˈreɪsiˌi / (AmE), / sɪˌpɛə.rəˈsiː /; / sɪˌpɛrəˈsiːiː / (BrE)
a large family of grass-like flowering plants, known as sedges, which includes bulrushes, papyrus, cotton grass, chufa, and similar plants
