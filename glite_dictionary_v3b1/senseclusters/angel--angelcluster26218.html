angel

noun
financial backer for a venture
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪndʒəl / (AmE), / ˈeɪndʒəl / (BrE)
a person who provides financial backing for a business venture, especially a start-up

Examples

Angel investor
After months of searching, they finally found an angel willing to fund their project.
Many entrepreneurs rely on an angel for the initial capital needed to grow.
The start-up was able to launch thanks to an angel who believed in its mission.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angel investor - a person who invests personal funds in a start-up business
  • synonym: backer - a person who gives financial support to a project or person
