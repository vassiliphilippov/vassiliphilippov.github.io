degenerate

adjective
morally corrupt; unrestrained by convention
4.3
Pronunciation: / dɪˈdʒɛnərət / (AmE), / dɪˈdʒɛnərət / (BrE)
lacking moral standards or self-control; unrestrained by convention or morality, often regarded as morally corrupt or depraved

Examples

Degenerate behavior
She refused to associate with degenerate individuals who ignored all social norms.
Some considered the nightclub a place for degenerate behavior and excess.
The degenerate lifestyle of the wealthy elite shocked many in the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depraved - morally corrupt; wicked
  • antonym: virtuous - having high moral standards
