dripper

noun
liquid-releasing device
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɪpər / (AmE), / ˈdrɪpə / (BrE)
a device that releases liquid in small, controlled drops, especially for watering plants or making coffee

Examples

Coffee dripper
The gardener installed a dripper to water each plant efficiently.
A dripper is essential for making pour-over coffee at home.
Modern irrigation systems often use a dripper for precise water delivery.
The gardener installed a dripper at the base of each plant to save water.
A single dripper can provide enough moisture for a small potted tree.
Modern irrigation systems often use a dripper to deliver water directly to the roots.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dropper - a small tube used to release liquid in drops
  • synonym: emitter - a device that releases water in irrigation systems
  • antonym: sprinkler - a device that sprays water over an area
