storm center

noun
central region of lowest pressure in a storm
9.9 3.6
Pronunciation: / stɔrm ˈsɛntɚ /; / stɔrm ˈsɛntər / (AmE), / stɔːm ˈsɛntə / (BrE)
the central area of a storm where the barometric pressure is lowest and the weather is most severe, or a place used to coordinate storm response
