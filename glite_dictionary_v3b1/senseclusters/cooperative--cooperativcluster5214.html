cooperative

adjective
working together with others
1.5 3.0
Pronunciation: /koʊ.ˈɑː.pə.reɪ.tɪv/ /; / /kə.ˈɑː.pə.reɪ.tɪv/ (AmE), /kəʊ.ˈɒp.ə.rə.tɪv/ /; / /kə.ˈɒp.ə.rə.tɪv/ (BrE)
