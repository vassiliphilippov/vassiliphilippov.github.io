trash

noun
nonsense; worthless talk
Pronunciation: / træʃ / (AmE), / træʃ / (BrE)
nonsensical or worthless talk or writing.

Examples

Talk trash
Don't listen to his trash; none of it makes the slightest sense.
The article was nothing but trash from start to finish.
Tired of all this trash, she walked out of the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drivel - silly or senseless talk
  • synonym: nonsense - spoken or written words that have no meaning or make no sense
  • antonym: sense - reasonable or meaningful talk or writing
