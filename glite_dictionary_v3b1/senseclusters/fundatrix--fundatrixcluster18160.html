fundatrix

noun
aphid colony founder
Pronunciation: / fʌnˈdeɪtrɪks / (AmE), / fʌnˈdeɪtrɪks / (BrE)
a female aphid or insect that produces the first generation of offspring and starts a new colony

Examples

Each fundatrix can give rise to hundreds of aphids in just a few weeks.
