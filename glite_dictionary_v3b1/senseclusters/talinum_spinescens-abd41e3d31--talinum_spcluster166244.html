Talinum spinescens

noun
a southwestern US plant with spiny leaf midribs

Pronunciation: /ˈtæl.ɪ.nəm spaɪ.ˈnes.əns/ /; / /ˈtæl.ɪ.nəm spɪ.ˈnes.əns/ (AmE), /ˈtæl.ɪ.nəm spaɪ.ˈnes.əns/ /; / /ˈtæl.ɪ.nəm spɪ.ˈnes.əns/ (BrE)
A low cushion-forming plant native to the southwestern United States, with rose to crimson-magenta flowers and leaf midribs that become spines when the leaves die.
