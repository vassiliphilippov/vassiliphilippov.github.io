prescription drug

noun
drug requiring a doctor's prescription
Pronunciation: / prɪˈskrɪpʃən drʌɡ / (AmE), / prɪˈskrɪpʃən drʌɡ / (BrE)
A drug that can be obtained only with a written order from a doctor or dentist.

Examples

A prescription drug
He was arrested for possessing a prescription drug without a valid prescription.
Some prescription drugs may cause side effects if not taken properly.
You need a doctor's note to buy a prescription drug at the pharmacy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: over-the-counter drug - a drug available without a prescription
