Synonyms

  • prescription medicine
    3.3 0.39/m
    prescription medicine is the everyday phrase for a drug needing a doctor's written order, while prescription drug is the formal legal category.
  • ethical drug
    4.1 0.00/m
    ethical drug is a technical, older term used by pharmacists and writers, while prescription drug is the common modern phrase.
  • prescribed medication
    3.2 0.32/m

  • prescription
    3.0 14/m
