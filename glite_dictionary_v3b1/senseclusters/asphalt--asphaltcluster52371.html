asphalt

noun
mixture for paving roads, roofing, etc.
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈæsfɔlt /; / ˈæsfəlt / (AmE), / ˈasfɑːlt / (BrE)
a dark, sticky material (a mixture of bitumen and stone, gravel, or sand) used for making roads and for waterproofing
