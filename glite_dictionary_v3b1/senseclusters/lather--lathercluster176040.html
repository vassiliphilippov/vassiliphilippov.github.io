lather

verb
to form foam or bubbles
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlæðər /; / ˈlæðɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɑːðə / (BrE)
to form or apply a mass of small bubbles or foam, especially by rubbing soap or a similar substance with water

Examples

To lather your hands
She rubbed her hands until the soap began to lather.
The shaving cream lathered quickly once mixed with water.
This shampoo doesn't lather as much as others, but it cleans well.
Before shaving, you should lather your face with cream.
He lathered the dog with shampoo to make sure it was clean.
She lathered her hands with soap before rinsing them under the tap.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foam - a mass of small bubbles formed on or in liquid
  • synonym: soap - to apply soap to something
