arterially

adverb
via main routes
8.4
Pronunciation: /ɑːr.ˈtɪr.i.əl/ /; / /ɑːr.ˈtɪr.iː.əl/ (AmE), /ɑː.ˈtɪə.ri.əl/ /; / /ɑː.ˈtɪə.riː.əl/ (BrE)
by means of main or primary routes or channels, especially major roads or transport arteries

Examples

Arterially linked roads
The city is arterially connected to neighboring towns by several major highways.
During rush hour, traffic flows arterially from the suburbs into the downtown area.
Goods are distributed arterially to ensure fast delivery along the main transport routes.
