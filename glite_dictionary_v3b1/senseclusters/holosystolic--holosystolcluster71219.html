holosystolic

adjective
throughout systole
Pronunciation: / ˌhoʊloʊsɪˈstɑlɪk / (AmE), / ˌhɒləʊsɪˈstɒlɪk / (BrE)
occurring throughout the entire period of systole in the heart

Examples

Holosystolic heart murmur
The cardiologist noted a holosystolic murmur during the patient's heart examination.
A holosystolic sound can indicate certain types of heart valve problems.
Doctors listen for holosystolic murmurs to help diagnose heart conditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: diastolic - relating to the period when the heart relaxes between beats
  • synonym: pansystolic - lasting through the entire systole of the heart
