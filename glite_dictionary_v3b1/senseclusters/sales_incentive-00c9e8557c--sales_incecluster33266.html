sales incentive

noun
customer promotional offer
3.8 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / se6alz 6anc8s5bnt6av / (AmE), / se6alz 6anc8s5bnt6av / (BrE)
a customer-facing promotional offer (such as a discount, free gift, or buy-one-get-one deal) intended to encourage immediate purchases.

Examples

Special sales incentive
The retailer offered a weekend sales incentive of 20% off to clear out old inventory.
As a holiday sales incentive, the company included a free gift with every purchase over fifty dollars.
To boost subscriptions, they introduced a limited-time sales incentive giving three months free for new customers.

Synonyms

  • sales promotion
    2.5 0.07/m
    sales promotion covers planned activities like discounts or contests to boost sales, while sales incentive is a direct customer offer to encourage immediate purchases.
  • promo
    4.1 1.92/m
    promo often means a short ad or general special offer for a product, while sales incentive is a direct customer facing deal to prompt immediate buying.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discount - a reduction in the usual price offered to customers, often used as an incentive to buy
  • synonym: sales promotion - a short-term marketing activity designed to stimulate customer purchases or increase product interest
