walky-talky

noun
handheld two-way radio
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˌwɑːkiˈtɑːki / (AmE), / ˌwɔːkiˈtɔːki / (BrE)
a small, portable radio transceiver used for two-way voice communication

Examples

A walky-talky device
Before cell phones, a walky-talky was essential for security teams at large events.
He clipped the walky-talky to his belt before heading out on the hike.
The children used a walky-talky to talk with each other from different rooms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: two-way radio - radio that can both transmit and receive signals
