brushup

noun
practice to refresh or improve skills
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbrʌʃˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈbrʌʃˌʌp / (BrE)
A period of practice or study aimed at improving skills or refreshing one's memory of something.

Examples

A brushup course
A brushup in first aid can be helpful for summer camp counselors.
I need a brushup on my French before the trip to Paris.
The team scheduled a quick brushup before the final game of the season.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: cram - study intensively over a short period
  • synonym: refresher - a short activity or course as a reminder or update on a subject
  • synonym: review - an act of going over something again for improvement or memory
