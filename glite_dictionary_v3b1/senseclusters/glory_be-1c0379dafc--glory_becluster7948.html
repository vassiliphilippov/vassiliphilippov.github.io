glory be

interjection
expression of relief or joy
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɔri ˈbi / (AmE), / ˈɡlɔːri ˈbiː / (BrE)
used as an exclamation to express relief, joy, or surprise

Examples

Glory be!
Glory be! I thought I'd lost my wallet, but here it is.
Glory be, the sun is finally shining after all that rain!
She exclaimed, "Glory be, what a wonderful surprise!"
