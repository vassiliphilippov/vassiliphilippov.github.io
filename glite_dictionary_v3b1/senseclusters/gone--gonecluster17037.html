dead, destroyed, or doomed; having no chance of survival or escape (including as a euphemism for death)

Examples

He is gone forever

Synonyms

  • deceased
    3.5 0.03/m
    deceased is a formal label used in official records and notices, while gone is often a softer, everyday way to say someone died.
  • departed
    4.2 0.01/m
    departed is a gentle, often poetic word for someone who died and is remembered, while gone is more general and can mean dead or lost.
  • at rest
    6.1 0.21/m
    at rest is a respectful phrase used on tombstones or obituaries, while gone can be casual and also mean destroyed or doomed.
  • done for
    7.7 1.23/m
    done for is a casual phrase meaning ruined or past saving, while gone often refers to death or being completely destroyed.
