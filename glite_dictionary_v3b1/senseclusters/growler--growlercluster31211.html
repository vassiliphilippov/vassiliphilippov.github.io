growler

noun
beer-carrying jug
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraʊlər / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊlə / (BrE)
a container, typically a jug or bottle, used to carry draft beer from a bar or brewery

Examples

A beer growler
He filled a growler with fresh craft beer at the local brewery.
They brought two growlers to share at the barbecue in the park.
You can return your empty growler for a refill at most taprooms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bottle - a container with a narrow neck used for storing drinks
  • synonym: jug - a large container for liquids, usually with a handle and spout
