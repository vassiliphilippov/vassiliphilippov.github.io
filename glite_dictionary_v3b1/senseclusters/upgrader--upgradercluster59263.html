upgrader

noun
person or device updating
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌɡreɪdər / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌɡreɪdə / (BrE)
a person or device that improves or updates something to a newer or better version

Examples

Software upgrader
The automatic upgrader ensures your software is always up to date.
As an experienced upgrader, she helps companies modernize their computer systems.
The device acts as an upgrader for older televisions, allowing them to access new features.

Related Terms

  • antonym: downgrader - a person or device that reverts something to an older or less advanced version
  • synonym: installer - a person or program that puts software or equipment in place
