slug it out

verb
fight or compete intensely until the end
Pronunciation: / slʌɡ ɪt aʊt / (AmE), / slʌɡ ɪt aʊt / (BrE)
to fight or compete with great determination until a winner is decided

Examples

To slug it out fiercely

Rivals slugged it out on the court until the final whistle.
The two candidates will slug it out during the televised debate next week.
