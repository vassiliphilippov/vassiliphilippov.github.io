notification

noun
formal act of informing; message of notice
Pronunciation: / ˌnoʊ.tə.fəˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌnəʊ.tɪ.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of formally informing someone or giving notice of something; a message that communicates information or a warning

Examples

Meeting notification
She posted a notification on the bulletin board to alert everyone about the meeting.
The app sends a push notification every time you get a new message.
You will receive a notification by email once your order has been shipped.

Related Terms

  • synonym: announcement - a formal public statement about a fact, occurrence, or intention
  • degree lower: warning - a statement or event that indicates a possible or impending danger, problem, or other unpleasant situation
