reutilize

verb
to use again
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈjuːtəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˌriːˈjuːtɪlaɪz /; / ˌriːˈjuːtəˌlaɪz / (BrE)
to use something again, especially after it has been processed, repaired, or for a new purpose
