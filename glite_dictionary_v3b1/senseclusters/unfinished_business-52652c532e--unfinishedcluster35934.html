unfinished business

noun
incomplete work or unresolved matters
1.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪnɪʃt ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪnɪʃt ˈbɪznɪs / (BrE)
work or tasks that have not been completed or resolved.

Examples

Deal with unfinished business
Despite the late hour, he stayed at the office to deal with his unfinished business.
She left the department with a lot of unfinished business to handle.
The meeting ended abruptly, leaving several issues as unfinished business for next time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incomplete - not finished or lacking completion
