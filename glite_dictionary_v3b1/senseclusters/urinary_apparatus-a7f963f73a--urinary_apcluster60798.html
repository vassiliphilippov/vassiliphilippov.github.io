Synonyms

  • urinary system
    3.8 0.03/m
    urinary system is the common name for the body organs that make and store urine, while urinary apparatus sounds slightly more technical.
  • urinary tract
    3.4 1.08/m
    urinary tract refers to the tubes and organs that carry and store urine, while urinary apparatus covers the whole urine-making system.
  • genitourinary system
    5.2 0.01/m
    genitourinary system includes reproductive organs along with urine organs, while urinary apparatus usually refers more narrowly to urine-related organs.
  • genitourinary apparatus
    7.4 0.00/m
    genitourinary apparatus names the combined reproductive and urinary parts, while urinary apparatus can mean only the urine-making organs.
  • urogenital system
    5.5 0.01/m
    urogenital system combines the reproductive and urinary organs in one term, while urinary apparatus focuses on urine production and removal.
  • urogenital apparatus
    9.1 0.00/m
    urogenital apparatus names both reproductive and urinary parts in some contexts, while urinary apparatus often means only urine organs.
