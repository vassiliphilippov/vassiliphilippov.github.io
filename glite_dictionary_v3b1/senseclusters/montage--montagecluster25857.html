montage

noun
art composed from assembled images or paper pieces
4.0 6.2
Pronunciation: / mɑnˈtɑʒ / (AmE), / mɒnˈtɑːʒ / (BrE)
an artistic composition or sequence made by combining several images, scenes, or pieces into a single unified whole (as in art or film)

Examples

Photo montage
She created a colorful montage to capture her memories from the trip.
The art exhibit featured a striking montage of vintage photographs and magazine clippings.
The film began with a rapid montage of city scenes and bustling crowds.
The film opened with a powerful montage showing the city changing over several decades.
In her art class, she created a montage of family photographs to capture her childhood memories.
The commercial used a quick montage of happy customers to highlight the product's popularity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collage - an artistic composition made by gluing items onto a surface
  • synonym: sequence - a series of related scenes or images shown in order
