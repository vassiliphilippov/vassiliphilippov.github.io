learn

verb
discover or become aware of
1.0
Pronunciation: / lɝn / (AmE), / lɜːn / (BrE)
to discover or find out about something, often by chance rather than deliberate study

Examples

To learn the news
He learns of his promotion through a company-wide email.
I learned that the meeting was rescheduled only after I arrived at the office.
She learned about the surprise party from a mutual friend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discover - to become aware of something not previously known
  • synonym: find out - to discover information, especially by making an effort
