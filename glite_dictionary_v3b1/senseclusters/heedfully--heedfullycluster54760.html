heedfully

adverb
with care and attention
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhid.fəli / (AmE), / ˈhiːd.fʊli / (BrE)
In a careful and attentive manner; with close attention or caution.

Examples

To heedfully check
He heedfully checked the documents for any errors before submitting them.
She listened heedfully to every word of the instructions.
The committee heedfully considered all aspects of the proposal before making a decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carefully - in a way that involves great attention
  • antonym: heedlessly - in a careless or inattentive way
