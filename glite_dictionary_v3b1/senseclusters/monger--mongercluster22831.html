monger

noun
person who promotes
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌŋ.gər/ /; / /ˈmɑːŋ.gər/ (AmE), /ˈmʌŋ.gə/ (BrE)
a person who promotes, spreads, or deals in something, often used in compounds to indicate what is being promoted or sold

Examples

A fear monger
The newspaper accused him of being a monger of rumors and false information.
In medieval times, a fish monger would sell fresh catch at the market.
Some people call those who spread fear about the future a fear monger.
