Synonyms

  • displeasure
    3.9 2.42/m
    displeasure is a quiet feeling of annoyance or dissatisfaction, while disapproval is a judgment or official negative opinion.
  • distaste
    4.1 3.49/m
    distaste is a mild aversion often about taste or style, while disapproval is a broader negative opinion about behavior.
  • dislike
    1.0 4.12/m
    dislike is a simple feeling of not liking someone or something, while disapproval includes opinion and judgment.
  • objection
    3.6 20/m
    objection is a spoken or formal statement of opposition, while disapproval can be an inner feeling or a public opinion.
