secretory

adjective
relating to secretion or producing secretion
2.7 6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsiː.krəˌtɔːr.i / (AmE), / sɪˈkriː.təri / (BrE)
relating to or producing a secretion, especially in the context of cells or glands in the body

Examples

Secretory cells
Abnormal secretory function in the glands can cause various disorders.
The secretory activity of sweat glands increases in hot weather.
The pancreas contains secretory cells that release digestive enzymes.
