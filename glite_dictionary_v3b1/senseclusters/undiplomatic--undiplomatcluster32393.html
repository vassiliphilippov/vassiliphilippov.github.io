undiplomatic

adjective
lacking tact or diplomacy
2.5 5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌdɪpləˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪpləˈmætɪk / (BrE)
not having or showing skill in dealing with people or sensitive situations; lacking tact or diplomacy.

Examples

It would be undiplomatic to raise such a delicate issue at the meeting.
She admitted that her undiplomatic approach had made negotiations more difficult.
