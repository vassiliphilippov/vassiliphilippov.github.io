arm in arm

adverb
with arms linked while walking
3.4
Pronunciation: / ɑrm ɪn ɑrm / (AmE), / ɑːm ɪn ɑːm / (BrE)
with arms linked together, especially while walking closely side by side

Examples

Arm in arm walking
After years apart, the sisters reunited and strolled arm in arm around the park.
The two friends walked arm in arm through the city streets.
Tourists moved arm in arm, taking in the sights together.
