Count Dracula

proper noun
the famous fictional vampire from Bram Stoker's novel
Pronunciation: / kaʊnt ˈdrækjələ / (AmE), / kaʊnt ˈdrækjʊlə / (BrE)
the fictional vampire count and antagonist in Bram Stoker's 1897 gothic novel 'Dracula', often representing the archetypal vampire in literature and popular culture

Examples

Count Dracula vampire
In Bram Stoker's novel, Count Dracula travels from Transylvania to England to spread his curse.
