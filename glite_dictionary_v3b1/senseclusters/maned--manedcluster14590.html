maned

adjective
having a mane
Pronunciation: / meɪnd / (AmE), / meɪnd / (BrE)
having a mane, especially referring to animals like lions or horses

Examples

A maned wolf
The maned wolf is known for its long legs and reddish fur.
A maned horse galloped across the open field, its hair flowing in the wind.
Visitors admired the maned lion resting in the shade at the zoo.

Related Terms

  • antonym: maneless - lacking a mane
  • synonym: manes - long hair on the neck of certain animals
