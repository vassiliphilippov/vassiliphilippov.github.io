urbanite

noun
city dweller
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrbəˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɜːbənaɪt / (BrE)
a person who lives in a city or urban area, especially someone used to city life

Examples

An urbanite in city
As an urbanite, she enjoyed the fast pace of city life.
Many urbanites rely on public transportation to get around.
The festival attracted urbanites from all over the metropolitan area.
Many urbanites enjoy the convenience of having shops and restaurants nearby.
As an urbanite, she was used to the fast pace of city life.
Some urbanites find it difficult to adjust to rural living.

Related Terms

  • synonym: city dweller - a person who lives in a city
  • antonym: rural dweller - a person who lives in the countryside
