dose

noun
amount of medicine or substance taken at once
1.0
Pronunciation: / doʊs / (AmE), / dəʊs / (BrE)
an amount of a medicine, drug, or other substance taken at one time

Examples

A dose of medicine
Be sure not to exceed the recommended dose on the label.
He received a high dose of radiation during the treatment.
The doctor prescribed a dose of antibiotics to be taken twice a day.
After his allergy attack, he needed a strong dose of medication to recover.
She accidentally missed her morning dose and felt unwell all day.
You should take one dose of this antibiotic every six hours.
