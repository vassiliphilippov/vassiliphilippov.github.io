flippancy

noun
lack of seriousness; inappropriate levity
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈflɪpənsi / (AmE), / ˈflɪpənsi / (BrE)
behavior or remarks lacking proper respect or seriousness; inappropriate levity.

Examples

Flippancy in speech
Her flippancy during the meeting irritated the manager.
Parents were shocked by the flippancy in his response to serious questions.
When discussing important topics, flippancy is generally considered disrespectful.

Related Terms

  • synonym: levity - lack of seriousness or lightness in manner
  • antonym: seriousness - the quality of being solemn or earnest
