aeronautical engineer

noun
engineer specializing in aircraft design
Pronunciation: / ˌɛroʊˈnɔtɪkəl ɛnˈdʒɪnɪr / (AmE), / ˌeərəˈnɔːtɪkəl enˈdʒɪnɪə / (BrE)
a person trained in the design, construction, and operation of aircraft

Examples

Aeronautical engineer designs planes
After graduating, she became an aeronautical engineer and helped design environmentally friendly passenger planes.
As an aeronautical engineer, he is responsible for ensuring the safety and efficiency of new jet engines.
The company is seeking an experienced aeronautical engineer to join its research and development team.
