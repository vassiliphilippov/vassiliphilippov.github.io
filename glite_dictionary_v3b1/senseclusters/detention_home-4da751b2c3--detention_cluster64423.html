detention home

noun
institution for temporary juvenile detention
Pronunciation: / dɪˈtɛnʃən hoʊm / (AmE), / dɪˈtɛnʃən həʊm / (BrE)
an institution where juvenile offenders are kept temporarily, usually under the supervision of a juvenile court

Examples

A detention home
After several months in a detention home, he was released to his family.
She volunteers at the local detention home, helping juveniles with their education.
The judge ordered the youth to be placed in a detention home while awaiting trial.
