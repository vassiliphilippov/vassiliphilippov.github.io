freddo

adjective
cold (Italian menu)
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈfreddoʊ / (BrE)
served cold or chilled, especially referring to food or drink in Italian contexts.

Examples

Freddo pasta salad
On the menu, a freddo salad means it will be served chilled rather than warm.
He ordered a freddo coffee to cool down on the hot afternoon.
The chef recommended the freddo pasta for a refreshing summer meal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cold - having a low temperature, especially when compared to the temperature of the human body
  • antonym: hot - having a high temperature
