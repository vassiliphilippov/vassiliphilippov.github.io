know-all

noun
a person who thinks they know everything
7.7 0.20/m
Pronunciation: / Ààno äÀå…ël / (AmE), / Ààn…ô äÀå…îÀêl / (BrE)
a person who behaves as if they know everything and refuses to accept advice or information from others

Examples

A know-all person
Don't be such a know-all; sometimes it's okay to admit you don't have all the answers.
Nobody likes a know-all who never listens to anyone else's ideas.
She comes across as a bit of a know-all at meetings, which annoys some coworkers.
