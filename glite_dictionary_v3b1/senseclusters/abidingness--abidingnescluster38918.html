abidingness

noun
consistent compliance
Pronunciation: / əˈbaɪdɪŋnəs / (AmE), / əˈbaɪdɪŋnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of consistently accepting, following, or complying with rules, decisions, or authority

Examples

Abidingness in rules
Her abidingness in complying with regulations impressed the auditors during the audit.
The team's abidingness to the new safety protocols ensured a smooth transition.
He was praised for his abidingness in respecting the community's rules and traditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defiance - open resistance to authority or rules
  • synonym: obedience - the act of following orders or rules
