interest

noun
a reason or motive; benefit
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.tɚ.ɪst / (AmE), / ˈɪn.trəst / (BrE)
a reason or motive for wanting something to be done or achieved, often for someone's benefit or advantage

Examples

In the interest of safety
In the interest of fairness, both sides were given equal time to speak.
Parents often act in the best interest of their children.
The company made changes in the interest of improving worker safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advantage - a condition or circumstance that puts one in a favorable or superior position
  • synonym: benefit - an advantage or profit gained from something
  • antonym: detriment - the state of being harmed or damaged; the opposite of benefit or interest
