trifecta

noun
horse racing bet
8.9
Pronunciation: /traɪ.ˈfek.tə/ (AmE), /traɪ.ˈfek.tə/ (BrE)
a bet in horse racing where the bettor must predict the first three finishers in the exact order, or the winning combination itself

Examples

Win a trifecta bet
He placed a trifecta and correctly picked the top three horses in the race.
Winning the trifecta at the derby brought her a large payout.
Many bettors dream of hitting a trifecta but few succeed.
