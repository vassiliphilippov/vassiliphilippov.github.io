phony

noun
deceptive or insincere person
4.5 5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈfoʊ.niː/ (AmE), /ˈfəʊ.niː/ (BrE)
a person who pretends to have beliefs, feelings, or qualities that they do not really have in order to deceive others
