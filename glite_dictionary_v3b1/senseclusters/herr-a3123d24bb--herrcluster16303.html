Herr

noun
German title for a man
Pronunciation: / hɛr / (AmE), / hɛː / (BrE)
a German title of respect for a man, used like 'Mr.' before a man's name

Examples

Herr Schmidt
During our trip to Berlin, we often heard people refer to men as Herr.
The letter was addressed to Herr Müller, the director.
We were introduced to Herr Schmidt at the conference.
Herr Schmidt will arrive at the conference at noon.
The invitation was addressed to Herr and Frau Meier.
We spoke to Herr Müller about the arrangements for the event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Mr - title used before a man's surname or full name
