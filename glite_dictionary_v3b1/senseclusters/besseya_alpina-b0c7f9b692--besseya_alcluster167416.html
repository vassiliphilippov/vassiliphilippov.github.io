Besseya alpina

noun

Pronunciation: / ˈbɛsiə ælˈpaɪnə / (AmE), / ˈbɛsiə ælˈpiːnə / (BrE)
A small perennial plant with dense spikes of pale bluish-violet flowers, native to high, cold meadows from Wyoming and Utah to New Mexico.
