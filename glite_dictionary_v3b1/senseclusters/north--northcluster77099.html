Synonyms

  • due north
    2.9 0.33/m
    due north points exactly to the geographic pole, while north means the general compass direction shown at the top of maps.
  • N
    1.0 2.98/m
    N is the compass label on maps and instruments, while north is the full word used for that direction.
  • northward
    1.0 0.14/m
    northward means moving toward the top of most maps or toward higher latitudes, while north names the fixed compass point at zero degrees.
