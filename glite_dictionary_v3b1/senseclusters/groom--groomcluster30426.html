groom

verb
to care for one's appearance
1.0 2.0
Pronunciation: /gruːm/ (AmE), /gruːm/ (BrE)
to clean, brush, or care for the appearance of a person or animal

Examples

To groom a dog
Every morning, she grooms herself carefully before going to work.
He always grooms meticulously before important meetings.
The cat sat on the windowsill grooming its fur in the sunlight.
Children should be taught how to groom themselves and maintain good hygiene.
She spends time every morning grooming her horse to keep its coat shiny.
The dog owner carefully groomed the poodle before the competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dress - to put on clothes; to prepare oneself for appearance
  • synonym: preen - to tidy and arrange one's appearance
  • synonym: tidy - to make something neat and orderly
