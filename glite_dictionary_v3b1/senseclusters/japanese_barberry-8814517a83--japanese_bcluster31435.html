Japanese barberry

noun
a shrub with red berries used in hedges
7.0
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈpæniz bɑːrˈbɛri / (AmE), / dʒəˈpæn.iːz ˈbɑː.bər.i / (BrE)
a compact deciduous shrub (Berberis thunbergii) with small leaves and persistent red berries, widely grown for hedges and landscaping

Examples
