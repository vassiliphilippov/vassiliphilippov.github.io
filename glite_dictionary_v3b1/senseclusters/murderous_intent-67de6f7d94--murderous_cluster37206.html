murderous intent

noun
intention to kill or seriously harm
3.6 6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmɜːr.dər.əs ɪn.ˈtent/ /; / /ˈmɜːr.drəs ɪn.ˈtent/ (AmE), /ˈmɜː.dər.əs ɪn.ˈtent/ /; / /ˈmɜː.drəs ɪn.ˈtent/ (BrE)
the intention to kill someone or cause them serious harm, often with malice or premeditation
