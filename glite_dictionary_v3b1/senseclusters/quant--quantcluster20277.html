quant

noun
financial mathematician
9.3
Pronunciation: / kwɑnt / (AmE), / kwɒnt / (BrE)
a person who uses advanced mathematics and statistical methods to analyze financial markets and develop trading strategies

Examples

A quant at a bank
The hedge fund relies on a team of quants to design its complex trading algorithms.
After earning his PhD in mathematics, he became a successful quant on Wall Street.
Many quants use computer models to predict market trends and manage risk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: analyst - a person who examines and interprets data or information
  • synonym: trader - a person who buys and sells financial assets
