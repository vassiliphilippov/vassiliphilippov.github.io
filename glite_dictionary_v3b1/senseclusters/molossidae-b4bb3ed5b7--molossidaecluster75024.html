Molossidae

noun
family of bats (mastiff/freetail bats)
Pronunciation: / məˈlɒsɪˌdiː /; / moʊˈlɒsɪdiː / (AmE), / məˈlɒsɪˌdiː /; / məˈlɒsɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of bats known as mastiff bats or free-tailed bats, characterized by robust bodies and long tails that extend beyond the tail membrane
