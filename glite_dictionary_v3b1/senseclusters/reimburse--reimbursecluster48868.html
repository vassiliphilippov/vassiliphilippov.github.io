reimburse

verb
to pay back money spent or lost

to pay back money to someone for costs or losses they have had

Examples

To reimburse expenses
I was reimbursed after submitting the medical bills to my insurance.
Please remember to reimburse any costs you pay on behalf of the organization.
The company will reimburse you for your travel expenses.
Employees are required to submit receipts to reimburse costs incurred during business trips.

Synonyms

  • compensate
    4.2 7.55/m
    compensate means giving payment or help for loss or injury, while reimburse means paying back money someone already spent.
  • refund
    1.1 5.23/m
    refund is returning the price of a purchase to a buyer, while reimburse is paying someone back for work or expenses.
  • repay
    1.7 3.63/m
    repay means returning borrowed money to a lender, while reimburse means paying someone back for costs they already had.
  • pay back
    1.6 8.01/m
    pay back means returning borrowed money to someone, while reimburse means giving money to cover expenses they already paid.
