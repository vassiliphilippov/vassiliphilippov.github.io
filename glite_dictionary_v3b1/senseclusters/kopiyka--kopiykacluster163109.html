kopiyka

noun
monetary unit of Ukraine
Pronunciation: / koʊˈpiːkə / (AmE), / kəˈpiːkə / (BrE)
A monetary unit of Ukraine, equal to one one-hundredth of a hryvnia.

Examples

A kopiyka coin
A single kopiyka coin is now rarely used in everyday transactions in Ukraine.
She collected old kopiyka coins as a hobby, fascinated by their changing designs.
The market vendor gave me change in kopiykas after I paid with a 5-hryvnia note.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: hryvnia - basic monetary unit of Ukraine
  • synonym: kopek - monetary unit of Russia and other former Soviet states, equal to one-hundredth of a ruble
