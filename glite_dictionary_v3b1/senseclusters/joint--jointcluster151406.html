joint

noun
marijuana cigarette
4.6
Pronunciation: / dʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / dʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
a cigarette made by rolling marijuana leaves in paper for smoking

Examples

Smoke a joint
He passed the joint around the circle during the party.
She rolled a joint before they went to the concert.
The smell of a freshly lit joint filled the air.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blunt - a cigar hollowed out and filled with marijuana
  • synonym: spliff - a cigarette containing marijuana, sometimes mixed with tobacco
