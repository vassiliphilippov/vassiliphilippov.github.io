paleoclimatologist

noun
ancient climate scientist
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪlioʊklaɪməˈtɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌpeɪliəʊklaɪməˈtɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies ancient climates by analyzing geological and biological evidence

Examples

The paleoclimatologist studies climate
The paleoclimatologist examined sediment layers to reconstruct the Earth's climate history.
As a paleoclimatologist, she often travels to remote locations to collect ancient ice samples.
A team led by a paleoclimatologist discovered evidence of dramatic climate shifts millions of years ago.

Related Terms

  • synonym: climatologist - a scientist who studies climate and weather patterns
  • synonym: geologist - a scientist who studies the Earth's physical structure and substances
