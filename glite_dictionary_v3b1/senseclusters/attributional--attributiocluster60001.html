attributional

adjective
relating to assigning causes
Pronunciation: / ˌætrɪˈbjuːʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌætrɪˈbjuːʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to the process of assigning a cause or reason to something

Differences in attributional thinking can affect how individuals respond to challenges.
