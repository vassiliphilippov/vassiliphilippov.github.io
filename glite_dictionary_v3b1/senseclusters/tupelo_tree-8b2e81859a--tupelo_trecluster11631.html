tupelo tree

noun
North American swamp tree (gum tree)
7.0 6.7
Pronunciation: /tə.ˈpiː.loʊ triː/ /; / /tjuː.ˈpiː.loʊ triː/ (AmE), /tə.ˈpiː.ləʊ triː/ /; / /tjuː.ˈpiː.ləʊ triː/ (BrE)
