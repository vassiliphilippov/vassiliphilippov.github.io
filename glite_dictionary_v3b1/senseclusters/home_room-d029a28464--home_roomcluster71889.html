home room

noun
classroom for administrative and attendance purposes in schools
8.6 1.9
Pronunciation: /hoʊm ruːm/ /; / /hoʊm rʊm/ (AmE), /həʊm ruːm/ /; / /həʊm rʊm/ (BrE)
