love feast

noun
religious communal meal (Christianity)
Pronunciation: / lʌv fiːst /; / ˈlʌv ˌfist /; / ˈlʌv ˌfiːst / (AmE), / lʌv fiːst /; / ˈlʌv ˌfiːst / (BrE)
