prepatellar

adjective
in front of kneecap
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːpəˈtɛlər / (AmE), / ˌpriːpəˈtɛlə / (BrE)
located in front of the kneecap (patella)

Examples

Prepatellar bursa
The doctor diagnosed him with prepatellar bursitis after he complained of pain in front of his knee.
Wearing knee pads can help prevent prepatellar injuries in people who kneel frequently for work.
Swelling in the prepatellar area may indicate inflammation of the bursa.

Related Terms

  • antonym: retropatellar - located behind the kneecap
