loansharking

noun
illegal money lending
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊnˌʃɑrkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈləʊnˌʃɑːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the illegal practice of lending money at extremely high interest rates, often using threats or violence to collect debts
