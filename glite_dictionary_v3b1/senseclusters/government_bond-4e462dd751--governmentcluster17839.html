Synonyms

  • government security
    4.0 0.24/m
    government security is a broader term for bonds and agency debt, while government bond refers to a specific government-issued loan.
  • Treasury obligations
    6.4 0.01/m
    Treasury obligations are formal legal US debt securities sold by the government, while government bond can mean similar debt in any country.
  • Treasury
    6.9 2.73/m
    Treasury often means US government debt securities in finance talk, while government bond refers more broadly to any country's government loan.
