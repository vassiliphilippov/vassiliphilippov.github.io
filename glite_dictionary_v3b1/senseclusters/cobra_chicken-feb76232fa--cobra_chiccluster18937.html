cobra chicken

noun
slang for Canada goose
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊbɹə ˈtʃɪkən / (AmE), / ˈkəʊbɹə ˈtʃɪkɪn / (BrE)
a humorous slang term for a Canada goose, referring to its aggressive behavior and long neck

Examples

The cobra chicken attacks
I've never seen a cobra chicken chase someone so quickly before!
Many parks are home to flocks of cobra chickens during the spring.
The cobra chicken hissed at everyone who came too close to the pond.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Canada goose - a large North American goose species, Branta canadensis
