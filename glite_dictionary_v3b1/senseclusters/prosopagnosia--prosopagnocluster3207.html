prosopagnosia

noun
face blindness
9.9
Pronunciation: /prə.sə.pæg.ˈnoʊ.ziː.ə/ /; / /prə.sə.pæg.ˈnoʊ.ʒə/ (AmE), /prɒ.sə.pæg.ˈnəʊ.zɪ.ə/ /; / /prɒ.sə.pæg.ˈnəʊ.ʒə/ (BrE)
a neurological condition where a person cannot recognize faces, often due to brain injury or present from birth
