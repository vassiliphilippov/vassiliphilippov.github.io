prosopagnosia

noun
face blindness
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊsəpæɡˈnoʊziə /; / ˌproʊsoʊpæɡˈnoʊziə / (AmE), / ˌprɒsəpæɡˈnəʊziə /; / ˌprɒsəʊpæɡˈnəʊziə / (BrE)
a neurological condition where a person cannot recognize faces, often due to brain injury or present from birth
