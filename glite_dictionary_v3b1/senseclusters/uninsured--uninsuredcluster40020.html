uninsured

adjective
not covered by insurance
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈʃʊrd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈʃʊəd / (BrE)
not covered by insurance; lacking insurance protection.

Examples

Uninsured driver
After losing his job, Tom found himself uninsured for the first time in years.
Many families remain uninsured because they cannot afford health coverage.
The driver was fined for being uninsured during the accident.
