at one time

adverb
formerly; in the past
Pronunciation: / ət wʌn taɪm / (AmE), / ət wʌn tʌɪm / (BrE)
in the past; formerly; at a previous point in time

Examples

At one time he was rich
At one time, he was considered the best player in the league.
At one time, this building was the city’s main post office.
Her family at one time owned most of the land in the area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: formerly - previously; in the past
  • antonym: nowadays - at the present time; in contrast to the past
