odd fellow

noun
eccentric or unusual person
Pronunciation: / ɑd ˈfɛloʊ / (AmE), / ɒd ˈfɛləʊ / (BrE)
a person who is considered eccentric or unusual in comparison to others in a group

Examples

An odd fellow at school
At school, people sometimes called him an odd fellow because of his unusual clothes and ideas.
Despite being an odd fellow, he was well-liked for his generosity and unique sense of humor.
The new neighbor turned out to be quite an odd fellow, collecting teapots from all over the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eccentric - a person with unconventional and slightly strange views or behavior
  • antonym: normal - conforming to a standard; usual, typical, or expected
  • synonym: weirdo - an informal term for a very strange person
