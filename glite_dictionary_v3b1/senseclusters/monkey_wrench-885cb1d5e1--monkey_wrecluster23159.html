monkey wrench

noun
an unexpected obstacle or problem
9.9 5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋki ˌrɛntʃ / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki ˌrɛntʃ / (BrE)
An unexpected problem or obstacle that interferes with a plan or process.

Examples

Monkey wrench in plans
His unexpected visit was a monkey wrench in her carefully arranged afternoon.
Just when we thought everything was set, a power outage put a monkey wrench in our schedule.
The resignation of our team leader threw a real monkey wrench into our project plans.
