spherical triangle

noun
triangle on a sphere's surface
Pronunciation: / ˈsfɪr.ɪ.kəl ˈtraɪ.æŋ.ɡəl / (AmE), / ˈsfɪə.rɪ.kəl ˈtraɪ.æŋ.ɡl̩ / (BrE)
A figure on the surface of a sphere, formed by three intersecting great circle arcs, each representing a side of the triangle.

Examples

A spherical triangle figure
A spherical triangle is used in navigation to solve problems involving coordinates on the globe.
Astronomers often calculate distances on the celestial sphere using spherical triangles.
The area of a spherical triangle depends on its angle excess.

Related Terms

  • synonym: triangle - a polygon with three sides and three angles
