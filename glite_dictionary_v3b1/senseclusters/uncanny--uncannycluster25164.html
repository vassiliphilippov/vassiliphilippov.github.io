uncanny

adjective
strange or mysterious; beyond normal
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkæni / (AmE), / ʌnˈkæni /; / ʌnˈkani / (BrE)
strange or mysterious, especially in a way that is unsettling or difficult to explain

Examples

An uncanny ability
He has an uncanny ability to predict what will happen next.
The forest was so quiet that it felt uncanny to walk through it alone.
There was an uncanny resemblance between the two strangers.
Her uncanny resemblance to the old family portrait shocked us all.
The magician performed an uncanny trick that seemed to defy explanation.
There was an uncanny silence in the abandoned house that made everyone uneasy.
