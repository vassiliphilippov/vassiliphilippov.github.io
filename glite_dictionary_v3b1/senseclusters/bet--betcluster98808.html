Synonyms

  • wager
    4.4 0.58/m
    wager is a formal way to risk money in contests or writing, while bet is the common everyday word.
  • stake
    5.3 2.03/m
    stake means to risk money, reputation, or other value on an outcome, while bet usually means placing a simple money wager.
  • punt
    7.6 0.20/m
    punt means to place a bet, especially on sports and in informal British talk, while bet is the general term.
  • play
    4.2 18/m
    play means to risk money by gambling in games or casinos in general, while bet means to stake money on a specific outcome.
