bet

verb
state with confidence; wager
3.2 1.4
Pronunciation: /bet/ (AmE), /bet/ (BrE)
to risk money or something valuable on the result of an event or competition; to wager

Examples

To bet money
He bet his friend that it would rain tomorrow.
I bet she will win the competition this year.
You can bet that prices will rise again before summer.
He likes to bet a small amount on every football game he watches.
Many people bet on horse races during the annual festival.
She decided to bet all her savings on her favorite team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gamble - to take risky action in the hope of a desired result, especially involving money
  • degree higher: guarantee - promise with certainty
  • degree lower: predict - to say that something will happen in the future
  • synonym: wager - to risk money or something of value on the outcome of an event
