snowshoeing

noun
walking on snow
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊˌʃuːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌʃuːɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of walking over snow using special wide shoes called snowshoes

Examples

To snowshoe in winter
We spent the afternoon snowshoeing through the quiet forest.
Many tourists try snowshoeing for the first time during their winter holidays.
He enjoys snowshoeing because it allows him to explore snowy landscapes at a slow pace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hiking - the activity of walking long distances in the countryside
  • synonym: skiing - the sport of moving over snow on skis
