crosspiece

noun
transverse support or brace
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɑsˌpis /; / ˈkrɔsˌpis / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌpiːs / (BrE)
a horizontal piece of material (such as wood or metal) fixed across something else to provide support or connect parts
