dragon's mouth

noun
bog orchid with showy magenta-pink flower
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdræɡənz maʊθ / (AmE), / ˈdræɡənz maʊθ / (BrE)
a bog orchid (Arethusa bulbosa) native to North America, usually with a single fragrant magenta-pink flower and a wide gaping corolla
