act

noun
a deed; something done
1.0
Pronunciation: / ækt / (AmE), / ækt / (BrE)
a thing that someone does; a deed or action

Examples

An act of kindness
An act of kindness can make someone's day much better.
His brave act saved the child from the burning building.
The government passed an act to improve healthcare standards.

Related Terms

  • synonym: action - the process of doing something to achieve an aim
  • synonym: deed - an action that is performed intentionally or consciously
  • antonym: omission - the act of leaving out or neglecting to do something
