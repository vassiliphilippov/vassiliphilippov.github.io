stereospecific

adjective
dependent on molecular shape
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌstɛrioʊspəˈsɪfɪk / (AmE), / ˌstɪəriəʊspəˈsɪfɪk / (BrE)
describing a chemical reaction where the result depends on the spatial arrangement of atoms in the reactant
