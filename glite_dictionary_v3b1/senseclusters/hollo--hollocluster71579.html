hollo

verb
utter a sudden loud cry
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑloʊ /; / ˈhɑː.loʊ / (AmE), / ˈhɒləʊ /; / ˈhɒl.əʊ / (BrE)
to shout or call out loudly, especially to attract attention or as a greeting

Examples

To hollo for help
He holloed so loudly that everyone turned to look at him.
She holloed for help when she saw smoke rising from the barn.
The shepherd holloed across the field to call his dog back.
He holloed to his friend across the field, waving his arms enthusiastically.
Suddenly, a voice holloed from the woods, startling the group of hikers.
They could hear the children holloing as they ran down the hill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: halloo - to shout or cry out to attract attention
  • synonym: shout - utter a loud call or cry
  • antonym: whisper - speak very softly using breath instead of vocal cords
  • synonym: yell - shout loudly, for pain, excitement, or attention
