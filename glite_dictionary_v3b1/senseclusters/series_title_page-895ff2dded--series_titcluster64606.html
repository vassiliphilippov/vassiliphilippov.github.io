series title page

noun
added title page showing series title
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪriz ˈtaɪtəl peɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriːz ˈtaɪtəl peɪdʒ / (BrE)
An additional title page in a book that displays the title of a series, often including other information about the series.

Examples

The series title page
Some reference books include publication details on the series title page for library cataloging purposes.
The series title page appears before the main title page in many academic volumes.
The librarian checked the series title page to confirm the books belonged to the same series.

Related Terms

  • synonym: title page - the page at the front of a book displaying the title, author, and publisher
