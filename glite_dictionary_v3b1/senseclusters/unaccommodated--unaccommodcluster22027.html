unaccommodated

adjective
not adapted or adjusted
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈkɑːməˌdeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈkɒməˌdeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not adapted, adjusted, or made suitable for a particular purpose, situation, or user

Examples

Many traditional teaching methods are unaccommodated to the needs of modern students.
The software was unaccommodated for international users, offering no language options.
