nonenzymic

adjective
not involving enzymes
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnɛnˈzaɪmɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɛnˈzaɪmɪk / (BrE)
not involving, produced by, or related to enzymes

Examples

A nonenzymic reaction
The chemical change was found to be nonenzymic and did not require any biological catalysts.
Researchers studied the nonenzymic browning of food during storage.
Some reactions in the body can occur through nonenzymic processes as well as enzymic ones.

Related Terms

  • antonym: enzymic - involving or produced by enzymes
