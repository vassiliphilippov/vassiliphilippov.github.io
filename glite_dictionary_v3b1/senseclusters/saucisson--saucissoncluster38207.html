saucisson

noun
French cured sausage
Pronunciation: / soʊˈsiːsɒn / (AmE), / soʊˈsiːsɒn / (BrE)
a type of dry, cured French sausage, usually made from pork and served sliced as a snack or appetizer

Examples

A saucisson slice
At the picnic, they enjoyed slices of saucisson with fresh bread and cheese.
The French market offered a wide variety of saucisson for tasting and purchase.
He packed some saucisson in his lunch for a flavorful snack during the hike.
