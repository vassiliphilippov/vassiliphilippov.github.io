fire tower

noun
tower for spotting fires
5.1 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈfaɪ.ər ˈtaʊ.ər/ /; / /faɪr ˈtaʊ.ər/ /; / /faɪr taʊr/ (AmE), /ˈfaɪ.ə ˈtaʊ.ə/ (BrE)
