tetanic

adjective
relating to or causing tetany
9.9
Pronunciation: / təˈtænɪk / (AmE), / tɛˈtænɪk / (BrE)
relating to or causing tetany, a condition marked by muscular spasms

Examples

Tetanic contraction
A drop in blood calcium levels may lead to tetanic muscle contractions.
Some toxins have a tetanic effect on the muscular system.
The patient displayed a tetanic response after the nerve was stimulated.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tetanus - a bacterial disease causing muscle stiffness and spasms
  • synonym: tetany - a medical condition with muscle spasms due to low calcium
