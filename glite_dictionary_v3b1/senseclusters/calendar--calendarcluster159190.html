calendar

noun
list or schedule of events
Pronunciation: / ˈkæl.ən.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈkæl.ən.də / (BrE)
a list, schedule, or register of planned events, such as appointments, meetings, or court cases

Examples

Work calendar events
