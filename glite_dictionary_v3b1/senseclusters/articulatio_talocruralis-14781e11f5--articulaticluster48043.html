articulatio talocruralis

noun
ankle joint (anatomical term)
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˌtɪkjəˌleɪʃioʊ ˌtæloʊˈkruːrəlɪs / (AmE), / ɑːˌtɪkjʊˌleɪʃɪəʊ ˌtæləʊˈkruːrəlɪs / (BrE)
the anatomical name for the ankle joint, where the tibia and fibula meet the talus bone
