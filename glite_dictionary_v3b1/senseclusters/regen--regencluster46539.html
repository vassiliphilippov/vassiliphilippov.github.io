regen

noun
health recovery (games)
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈriːdʒɛn / (AmE), / ˈriːdʒɛn / (BrE)
an informal term for the process of regaining health, energy, or resources, especially in video games

Examples

Health regen in games
After taking damage, the character needs time for regen before fighting again.
The game offers fast regen if you stand still for a few seconds.
Players often wait for their regen to finish before entering the next battle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: healing - the process of becoming healthy again
  • synonym: regeneration - the process of regrowing or restoring lost or damaged parts
