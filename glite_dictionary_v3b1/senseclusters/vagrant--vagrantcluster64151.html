vagrant

adjective
continually changing or wandering
7.3 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈveɪ.grənt/ /; / /ˈvæ.grənt/ (AmE), /ˈveɪ.grənt/ /; / /ˈvæ.grənt/ (BrE)
continually changing, especially by moving from place to place or from one occupation to another; wandering or unsettled

Examples

Vagrant lifestyle
During the 1960s, many vagrant youths traveled across the country in search of new experiences.
Her thoughts took a vagrant course, drifting from one subject to another.
The vagrant nature of his career meant he rarely stayed in one job for long.
