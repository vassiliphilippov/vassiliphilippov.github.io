lookalike

noun
person or thing resembling another
Pronunciation: / ˈlʊkəˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈlʊkəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
a person or thing that closely resembles another in appearance

Examples

A lookalike celebrity
The celebrity's lookalike was often mistaken for her at public events.
At the party, there was a contest to find the best Elvis lookalike.
Some animals have a natural lookalike to help them avoid predators.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doppelganger - a person who looks exactly like someone else
  • synonym: double - a person who looks very similar to another, especially a celebrity
  • antonym: original - the person or thing that is being imitated or resembled
