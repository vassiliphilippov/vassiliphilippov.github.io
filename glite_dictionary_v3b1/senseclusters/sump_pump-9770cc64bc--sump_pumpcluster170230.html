sump pump

noun
pump for removing liquid from a sump
7.0
Pronunciation: / sʌmp pʌmp / (AmE), / sʌmp pʌmp / (BrE)
A pump designed to remove water or other liquid from a sump, typically used to prevent flooding in basements or low-lying areas.

Examples

A sump pump system
After the flood, the sump pump had to be replaced due to damage.
Many homeowners install a sump pump to prevent water accumulation in basements.
The sump pump in the basement kept running during the heavy rainstorm.
