Thai

noun
language spoken in central Thailand
Pronunciation: / taɪ / (AmE), / taɪ / (BrE)
the language of the Thai people, spoken mainly in central Thailand and Bangkok, which forms a branch of the Tai language family

Examples

Thai language

She is fluent in both English and Thai.
