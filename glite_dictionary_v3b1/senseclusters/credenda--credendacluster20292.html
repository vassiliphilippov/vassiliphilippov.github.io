credenda

noun
articles of belief
Pronunciation: / krəˈdɛndə / (AmE), / krɪˈdɛndə / (BrE)
things that are to be believed, especially as religious doctrine or articles of faith

Examples

The credenda of faith
The council discussed the essential credenda of the faith.
Scholars debated which credenda should be included in the new statement of beliefs.
Throughout history, different churches have defined their credenda in various ways.

Related Terms

  • synonym: creed - a formal statement of religious beliefs
  • synonym: dogma - a principle or set of principles laid down by an authority as incontrovertibly true
