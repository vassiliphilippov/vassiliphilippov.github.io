gouty

adjective
afflicted with gout
4.6 5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡaʊti / (AmE), / ˈɡaʊti / (BrE)
Suffering from or affected by gout, a medical condition causing painful swelling in the joints.

Examples

Gouty arthritis pain
He walked with a limp because of his gouty ankle, which had flared up again.
In old age, many nobles were described as gouty due to their rich diets.
The doctor examined the patient's gouty toes, noting the characteristic swelling and redness.
