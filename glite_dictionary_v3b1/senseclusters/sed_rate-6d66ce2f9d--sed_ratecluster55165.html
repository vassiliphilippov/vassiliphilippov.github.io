sed rate

noun
rate at which blood cells settle; test for inflammation
9.9 4.6
Pronunciation: /sed reɪt/ (AmE), /sed reɪt/ (BrE)
a blood test that measures how quickly red blood cells settle in a sample, used to detect inflammation in the body
