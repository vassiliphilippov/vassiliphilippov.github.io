swimmer's itch

noun
itchy rash from waterborne parasites
3.1 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈswɪm.ərz ɪtʃ/ (AmE), /ˈswɪm.əz ɪtʃ/ (BrE)
an itchy rash caused by an allergic reaction to certain microscopic parasites that burrow into the skin, typically after swimming in infested water
