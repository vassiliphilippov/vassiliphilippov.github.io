unpayable

adjective
impossible to pay
5.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpeɪəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈpeɪəbl / (BrE)
impossible to pay, especially because the amount is too large or unaffordable

Examples

An unpayable debt
After the new taxes were introduced, the bills became unpayable for many families.
The company faced unpayable debts after several years of financial losses.
Some loans have such high interest rates that they are almost unpayable.

Related Terms

  • antonym: affordable - able to be paid for or afforded
  • synonym: insurmountable - impossible to overcome or deal with
