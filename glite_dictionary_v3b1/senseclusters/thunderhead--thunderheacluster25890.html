Examples

A large thunderhead

Farmers watched the thunderhead forming, knowing that rain was likely to follow.
The setting sun cast a golden glow along the rim of the massive thunderhead.

Synonyms

  • cumulonimbus
    9.7 0.06/m

  • thundercloud
    2.5 0.21/m
    thundercloud is a large dark cloud full of electrical charge and heavy rain, while thunderhead is the bright rounded cumulus mass before a storm.
