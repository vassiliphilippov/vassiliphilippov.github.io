unsupported

adjective
not maintained by vendor; no official updates
4.5
Pronunciation: /ən.sə.ˈpɔːr.tɪd/ /; / /ʌn.sə.ˈpɔːr.tɪd/ (AmE), /ən.sə.ˈpɔː.tɪd/ /; / /ʌn.sə.ˈpɔː.tɪd/ (BrE)
Not (any longer) officially maintained, updated, or assisted by the product's manufacturer or provider; lacking vendor support or updates.

Examples

Unsupported software
Many older printers are now unsupported, so manufacturers no longer provide drivers or security updates.
The software was declared unsupported after the vendor stopped issuing patches for vulnerabilities.
Using an unsupported operating system can expose businesses to security risks and compliance problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deprecated - (of software or features) officially discouraged from use and typically not updated; often replaced by newer alternatives.
  • antonym: supported - officially maintained or assisted by a manufacturer or provider; receiving updates and help.
