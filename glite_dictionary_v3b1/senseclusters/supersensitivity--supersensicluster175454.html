supersensitivity

noun
extreme sensitivity
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˌsɛnsəˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˌsɛnsɪˈtɪvɪti / (BrE)
the state of being extremely sensitive to physical, emotional, or environmental stimuli

Examples

Emotional supersensitivity
Her supersensitivity to criticism made group work challenging.
Some people have supersensitivity to certain foods, causing allergic reactions.
The medication reduced his supersensitivity to loud noises after the accident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hypersensitivity - an excessive or abnormal sensitivity to a stimulus
  • antonym: insensitivity - lack of sensitivity to stimuli or emotions
