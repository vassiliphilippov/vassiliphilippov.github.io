shortie

noun
slang for partner
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɔrti / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːti / (BrE)
an informal term for a person's romantic partner, especially a girlfriend or someone they are attracted to

Examples

His shortie at the party
He introduced his shortie to all his friends at the barbecue.
Everyone at the party wanted to talk to his shortie because she was so charming.
She posted a photo with her shortie on social media to celebrate their anniversary.
