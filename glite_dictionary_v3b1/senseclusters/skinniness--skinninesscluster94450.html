skinniness

noun
quality of being very thin
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪni.nəs / (AmE), / ˈskɪni.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality or condition of being very thin, especially in a way that suggests a lack of flesh

Examples

Extreme skinniness
He overcame his childhood skinniness through a regular exercise and diet regimen.
She was worried that her skinniness made her look unhealthy.
The model's skinniness was a topic of discussion at the fashion show.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leanness - the quality of having little fat
  • antonym: plumpness - the quality of being plump or slightly fat
  • synonym: thinness - the quality of being thin
