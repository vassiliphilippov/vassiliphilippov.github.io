coffee stall

noun
stand selling hot coffee and snacks
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːfi stɔl / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi stɔːl / (BrE)
a small stand, often movable, that sells hot coffee and sometimes food, usually operating at night or in public places

Examples

A coffee stall at night
After the movie, they stopped at a coffee stall for a snack and a drink.
He grabbed a cup from the coffee stall before catching the early train.
The busy street is lined with coffee stalls serving late-night customers.
