barographic

adjective
relating to a barograph
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌbæɹəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌbærəˈɡrafɪk / (BrE)
relating to or recorded by a barograph (an instrument that measures and records atmospheric pressure)

Examples

Barographic chart
A detailed barographic chart is essential for tracking atmospheric changes over time.
Meteorologists studied the barographic data to predict future weather patterns.
The barographic records showed a significant drop in pressure during the storm.
