tax cut

noun
reduction in government taxes
1.3
Pronunciation: / tæks kʌt / (AmE), / tæks kʌt / (BrE)
a reduction in the amount of taxes imposed by a government

Examples

A tax cut plan
A sudden tax cut can have a major impact on national spending and investment.
Many voters supported the candidate because of her promise of a tax cut.
The government announced a substantial tax cut to stimulate the economy.
