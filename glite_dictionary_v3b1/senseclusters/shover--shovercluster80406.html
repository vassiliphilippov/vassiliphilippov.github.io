shover

noun
person who pushes
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌvər / (AmE), / ˈʃʌvə / (BrE)
a person who pushes someone or something, especially forcefully or rudely

Examples

A shover in wrestling
Despite her small size, she quickly earned a reputation as a shover during the wrestling match.
If you act like a shover on the subway, people will get annoyed with you.
The little boy was known as a shover on the playground, always pushing others out of his way.
