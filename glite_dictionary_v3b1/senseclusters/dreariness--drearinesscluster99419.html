dreariness

noun
quality of being dull or gloomy
9.9 6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɪriənɪs / (AmE), / ˈdrɪəriːnɪs / (BrE)
A quality or state of being dull, gloomy, or lacking in interest or excitement.

Examples

The dreariness of life
She tried to escape the dreariness of her daily routine by picking up a new hobby.
The dreariness of the long winter affected everyone's mood.
Walking through the deserted streets, he felt overwhelmed by their dreariness.
