postpaid

adjective
payment after use
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstˈpeɪd / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈpeɪd / (BrE)
describing a service or account for which payment is made after use rather than in advance

Examples

Postpaid phone plan
She prefers a postpaid phone plan so she can pay monthly.
Many utility bills are postpaid, meaning you pay after using the service.
With a postpaid account, you receive a bill at the end of each month.

Related Terms

  • antonym: prepaid - paid for in advance before use
