weediness

noun
full of weeds; thinness
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːdinəs / (AmE), / ˈwiːdinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being full of weeds or appearing thin and weak

Examples

Weediness of a field
The weediness of the field made it difficult to grow healthy crops.
His weediness made him look much younger and weaker than his classmates.
After months of neglect, the garden's weediness was obvious to everyone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lushness - the quality of being rich and abundant in growth
  • synonym: thinness - the quality of being thin or lacking strength
