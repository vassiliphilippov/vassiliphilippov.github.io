clacker

noun
person making clacking sounds
Pronunciation: / ˈklækər / (AmE), / ˈklækə / (BrE)
a person who makes a repeated sharp clacking sound, such as by typing loudly or walking in hard-soled shoes

Examples

Loud clacker at work
The constant noise from the clacker at the next desk made it hard to concentrate.
Everyone could hear the clacker coming down the hallway in her hard shoes.
He became known as the office clacker because of his loud typing.
