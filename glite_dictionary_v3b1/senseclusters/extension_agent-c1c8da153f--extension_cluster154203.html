extension agent

noun
agricultural advisor for rural communities
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɛnʃən ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛnʃən ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
an advisor employed by a government agency or educational institution to help people in rural areas with farming methods and home economics

Examples

Local extension agent
Many rural families rely on advice from their local extension agent for crop management.
The extension agent visited the farm to demonstrate new irrigation techniques.
The extension agent's workshop on home canning was well attended by the community.
