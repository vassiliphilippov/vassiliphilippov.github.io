irresolute

adjective
uncertain; lacking determination
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrɛzəˌlut / (AmE), / ɪˈrɛzəluːt / (BrE)
uncertain or unable to make decisions quickly; lacking firmness or determination

Examples

An irresolute answer
Facing the opposing arguments, he gave an irresolute response that satisfied no one.
Her irresolute manner made it hard for the team to trust her leadership.
The manager remained irresolute in the face of tough decisions, delaying action for several days.

Related Terms

  • antonym: resolute - having or showing determination
