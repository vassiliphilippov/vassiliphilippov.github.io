Synonyms

  • aerobiotic
    8.4 0.00/m
    aerobiotic describes organisms that need oxygen to live and is often used in biology, while aerobic names oxygen-using organisms or processes.
  • aerophilic
    9.5 0.00/m
    aerophilic means needing free air or oxygen, often used for organisms, while aerobic is the widely used general term for oxygen-dependent life or processes.
  • aerophilous
    9.9 0.00/m
    aerophilous describes organisms that depend on free air or oxygen and appears as a variant form, while aerobic is the standard modern term.
