aerobic

adjective
requiring oxygen; related to oxygen use
2.2 3.9
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈroʊ.bɪk/ /; / /eə.ˈroʊ.bɪk/ (AmE), /eə.ˈrəʊ.bɪk/ /; / /ə.ˈrəʊ.bɪk/ (BrE)
needing or using free oxygen for life or activity, especially referring to organisms or processes

Examples

Aerobic bacteria

Regular aerobic exercise improves heart and lung function.
