aerobic

adjective
requiring oxygen; related to oxygen use
Pronunciation: / ɛˈroʊbɪk / (AmE), / ɛəˈrəʊbɪk / (BrE)
needing or using free oxygen for life or activity, especially referring to organisms or processes

Examples

Aerobic bacteria
Aerobic bacteria need oxygen to survive and multiply.
Regular aerobic exercise improves heart and lung function.
The industry uses aerobic fermentation to produce certain foods and medicines.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anaerobic - not needing or relating to the absence of oxygen
