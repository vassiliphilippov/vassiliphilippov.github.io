velvety-haired

adjective
having hair like velvet
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛl.və.ti hɛrd / (AmE), / ˈvɛl.vɪ.ti heəd / (BrE)
Having hair that is soft and smooth like velvet to the touch.

Examples

Velvety-haired spaniel
He admired the velvety-haired spaniel sitting gracefully at her feet.
Her velvety-haired cat purred contentedly as she brushed it.
The princess was known for her velvety-haired beauty and gentle manners.
