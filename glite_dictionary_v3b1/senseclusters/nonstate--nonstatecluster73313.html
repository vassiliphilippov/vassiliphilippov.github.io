nonstate

adjective
not government-controlled
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈsteɪt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈsteɪt / (BrE)
not controlled by, belonging to, or associated with a government or state

Examples

Nonstate actors
Several nonstate organizations provided aid after the disaster.
The conference focused on the role of nonstate actors in international relations.
Funding came from nonstate sources rather than government agencies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: private - not owned or controlled by the government
  • antonym: state - controlled or owned by the government
