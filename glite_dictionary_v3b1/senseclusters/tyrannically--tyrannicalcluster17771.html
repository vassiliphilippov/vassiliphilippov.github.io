tyrannically

adverb
in an oppressive way
7.9
Pronunciation: / tɪˈrænɪkli / (AmE), / tɪˈrænɪkli / (BrE)
in a cruel, harsh, or oppressive way, like a tyrant

Examples

To rule tyrannically
The dictator ruled tyrannically, silencing anyone who disagreed with him.
Workers complained that their manager acted tyrannically by refusing any requests for time off.
Throughout history, some leaders have governed tyrannically to maintain absolute control.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cruelly - in a way that causes pain or suffering
  • antonym: kindly - in a gentle or caring way
  • synonym: oppressively - in a harsh or unjust manner
