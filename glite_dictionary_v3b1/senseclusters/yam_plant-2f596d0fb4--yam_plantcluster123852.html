yam plant

noun
tropical vine with edible tuberous roots
4.3 2.8
Pronunciation: / jæm plænt / (AmE), / jæm plɑːnt / (BrE)
Any of several tropical vines of the genus Dioscorea, many of which have edible tuberous roots commonly known as yams.

The yam plant thrives in tropical climates and is a major food source in West Africa.
