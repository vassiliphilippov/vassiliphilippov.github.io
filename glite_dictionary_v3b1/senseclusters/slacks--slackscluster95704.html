slacks

noun
casual or semi-formal pants
8.3
Pronunciation: / slæks / (AmE), / slæks / (BrE)
a type of pants for casual or semi-formal wear, typically looser than formal trousers; usually used in the plural

Examples

A pair of slacks
He wore a crisp shirt and a pair of slacks to the meeting.
She prefers slacks over skirts for everyday wear.
These slacks are comfortable enough for travel yet smart enough for the office.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: jeans - casual pants made of denim
  • synonym: trousers - outer garment covering each leg, generally more formal
