recumbency

noun
lying down state
8.7
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkʌmbənsi / (AmE), / rɪˈkʌmbənsi / (BrE)
the state of lying down or being in a resting position

Examples

Period of recumbency
After the surgery, the doctor recommended several hours of recumbency to aid recovery.
The animal's prolonged recumbency was a sign of illness that concerned the veterinarian.
During the examination, the patient maintained recumbency to ensure accurate results.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reclining - the act of leaning or lying back
  • antonym: standing - the state of being upright on the feet
  • synonym: supine - lying flat on one's back
