foundational

adjective
from a foundation
Pronunciation: / faʊnˈdeɪʃənəl / (AmE), / faʊnˈdeɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or provided by a charitable or grant-making foundation, especially in the context of support or funding.

Examples

Foundational grant
The museum received foundational funding to expand its educational programs.
Many community projects rely on foundational grants to get started.
The scholarship was made possible through foundational support from a local charity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: governmental - provided by a government or public authority
  • synonym: philanthropic - relating to charitable giving or organizations
