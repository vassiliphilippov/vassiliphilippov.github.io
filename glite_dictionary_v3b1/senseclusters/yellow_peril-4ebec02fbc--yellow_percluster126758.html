yellow peril

noun
racist term for threat from Asians
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊ ˈpɛrəl / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈpɛrəl / (BrE)
an outdated and offensive idea or stereotype that East Asian peoples or nations are a threat to Western countries

Examples
