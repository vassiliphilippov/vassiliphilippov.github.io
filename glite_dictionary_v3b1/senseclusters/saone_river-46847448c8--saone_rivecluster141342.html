Saone River

proper noun
river in eastern France, tributary of the Rhone
5.6
Pronunciation: / soʊn ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / səʊn ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in eastern France that flows south to join the Rhone, making it the main tributary of the Rhone River
