unswallowable

adjective
impossible to swallow
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈswɑːloʊəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈswɒləwəbl̩ / (BrE)
impossible or extremely difficult to swallow

Examples

An unswallowable pill
The tablet was so large it was almost unswallowable for most patients.
Some fish bones are sharp and completely unswallowable without risk.
He found the chunk of meat unswallowable and had to spit it out.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inedible - not fit or safe to eat
  • antonym: swallowable - able to be swallowed
