FBI agent

noun
law-enforcement officer of the FBI
Pronunciation: / ˌɛf.biˈaɪ ˈeɪ.dʒənt / (AmE), / ˌɛf.biˈaɪ ˈeɪ.dʒənt / (BrE)
a special law-enforcement officer employed by the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) in the United States

Examples

FBI agent investigates crime
After years of training, she finally became an FBI agent specializing in cybercrime.
He was hired as an FBI agent shortly after graduating from law school.
The FBI agent questioned the suspect about his whereabouts on the night of the robbery.
