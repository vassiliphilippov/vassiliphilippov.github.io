infectious laughter

noun
laughter that spreads to others
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɛkʃəs ˈlæftɚ / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɛkʃəs ˈlɑːftə / (BrE)
laughter that is so full of joy it easily makes others start laughing as well

Examples

Infectious laughter spreads
Her infectious laughter quickly lifted the mood in the entire room.
His infectious laughter could be heard all the way down the hallway.
The comedian's infectious laughter made everyone in the audience burst into giggles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contagious laughter - laughter that spreads from person to person
