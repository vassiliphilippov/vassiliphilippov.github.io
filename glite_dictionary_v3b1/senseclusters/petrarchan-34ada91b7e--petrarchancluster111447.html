Petrarchan

noun
admirer of Petrarch
9.9
Pronunciation: / pəˈtrɑrkən / (AmE), / pɛˈtrɑːkən / (BrE)
a person who admires, imitates, or writes in the style of Petrarch, especially in composing Petrarchan sonnets

Examples

A Petrarchan poet
As a devoted Petrarchan, she spent years perfecting her own sonnet sequence.
The literary club included several Petrarchans who admired Italian Renaissance poetry.
He was called a Petrarchan because of his fascination with idealized love in his poems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sonneteer - a person who writes sonnets
