defrost

verb
remove frost or ice
1.7 0.89/m
Pronunciation: / diÀàfr…îst / (AmE), / diÀêÀàfr…íst / (BrE)
to remove frost or ice from (something), or to become free of frost or ice

Examples

To defrost the freezer
I need to defrost the windshield before I can drive to work.
Please defrost the chicken before cooking it for dinner.
The freezer automatically defrosts every few hours to prevent ice buildup.

Synonyms

  • thaw
    1.1 5.58/m
    thaw is to become soft or liquid after freezing, while defrost means to remove frost or ice from something.
  • unfreeze
    3.5 0.10/m
    unfreeze means to make or become unfrozen or soft, while defrost focuses on removing frost or ice from items.
  • deice
    6.7 0.02/m
    deice means removing ice from surfaces like planes or cars, while defrost can mean clearing ice or letting food thaw.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thaw - to become unfrozen or to cause something frozen to become unfrozen
