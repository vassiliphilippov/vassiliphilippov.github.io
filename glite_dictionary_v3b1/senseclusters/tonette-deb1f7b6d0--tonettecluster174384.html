Tonette

noun
plastic wind instrument
Pronunciation: / toʊˈnɛt / (AmE), / təʊˈnɛt / (BrE)
a small, simple plastic wind instrument, often used to teach music to children in schools

Examples

School Tonette lessons
Many children learn to play music on a Tonette in school.
The music teacher handed each student a Tonette for the lesson.
She practiced simple melodies on her Tonette every afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ocarina - a small wind instrument with finger holes and a mouthpiece
  • synonym: recorder - a woodwind instrument often used in music education
