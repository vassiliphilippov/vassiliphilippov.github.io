fiber

noun
optical fiber for data transmission

a thin strand of glass or plastic used to transmit data as pulses of light in telecommunications and networking; (also) a cable or system using such strands.

Examples

Fiber optic cable
The company installed new fiber to bring high-speed internet to rural neighborhoods.
Many homes switched to fiber connections for more reliable video calls and faster downloads.
Engineers ran a loop of fiber through the building to support future network upgrades.

Synonyms

  • fiber optic cable
    3.9 0.7/m
    fiber optic cable is a complete cable made of many optical fibers for high-speed links, while fiber can mean a single strand or the system.
  • glass fiber
    4.1 0.2/m
    glass fiber is a very thin glass strand used in optics or materials, while fiber can mean glass or plastic strands or the whole cable.
