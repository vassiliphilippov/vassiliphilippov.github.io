stick it to the man

verb
resist or challenge authority
9.9
Pronunciation: / stɪk ɪt tu ðə mæn / (AmE), / stɪk ɪt tʊ ðə mæn / (BrE)
to openly resist, defy, or challenge those in power or authority, especially in a rebellious or subversive way

Examples

To stick it to the man openly
By exposing the corporation's wrongdoing, the whistleblower managed to stick it to the man.
He tried to stick it to the man by voting against the proposed regulation.
Teenagers often feel the urge to stick it to the man through small acts of rebellion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defy - to openly resist or refuse to obey
  • antonym: submit - to yield to the authority or will of another
