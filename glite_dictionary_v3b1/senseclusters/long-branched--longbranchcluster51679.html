long-branched

adjective
having long branches
Pronunciation: / lɔŋ bræntʃt / (AmE), / lɒŋ brɑːntʃt / (BrE)
having long branches; characterized by or possessing branches that are long in comparison to others of its kind.

Examples

Long-branched shrubs
Several long-branched shrubs were pruned to allow more sunlight through.
The long-branched coral provides shelter for many marine species.
They admired the long-branched fig tree that shaded the garden.
