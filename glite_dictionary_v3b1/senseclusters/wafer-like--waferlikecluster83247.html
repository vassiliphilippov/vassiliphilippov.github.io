wafer-like

adjective
resembling a thin, crisp wafer
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪfər laɪk / (AmE), / ˈweɪfə laɪk / (BrE)
having the appearance or texture of a thin, crisp wafer; thin and flat.

Examples

A wafer-like texture
He built a wafer-like structure with delicate, thin sheets of metal.
The chocolate was topped with a wafer-like layer of caramel that melted in your mouth.
The leaves of the plant are wafer-like, almost translucent in the sunlight.
