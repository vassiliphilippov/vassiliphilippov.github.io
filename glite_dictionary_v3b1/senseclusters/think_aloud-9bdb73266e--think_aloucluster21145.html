think aloud

verb
to verbalize thoughts as they occur
2.6
Pronunciation: / θɪŋk əˈlaʊd / (AmE), / θɪŋk əˈlaʊd / (BrE)
to say your thoughts as they come, without intending to address anyone in particular

Examples

To think aloud clearly
During meetings, she often thinks aloud to clarify her ideas for the team.
He was thinking aloud when he proposed the plan, not making a formal suggestion.
Sometimes it helps to think aloud to sort through complex problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: talk to oneself - to speak to oneself, not to others
