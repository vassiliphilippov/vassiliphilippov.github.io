unalluring

adjective
not attractive
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈlʊrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈljʊərɪŋ / (BrE)
not attractive or appealing; lacking charm or interest

Examples

An unalluring place
The building's unalluring exterior did not reflect its beautiful interior design.
Despite his talent, his unalluring personality made it hard for him to make friends.
The advertisement was so unalluring that few people paid attention to it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plain - simple or ordinary in appearance
  • synonym: unattractive - not pleasing to look at or appealing
