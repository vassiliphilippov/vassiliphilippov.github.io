hearing dog

noun
dog trained to assist the deaf
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪrɪŋ dɔg / (AmE), / ˈhɪərɪŋ dɒg / (BrE)
a dog trained to assist people who are deaf or hard of hearing by signaling the occurrence of specific sounds

Examples

Hearing dog trained
Her hearing dog accompanies her everywhere, ready to help when needed.
The hearing dog alerted its owner to the ringing doorbell.
Training a hearing dog takes several months of specialized education.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guide dog - a dog trained to lead visually impaired people
