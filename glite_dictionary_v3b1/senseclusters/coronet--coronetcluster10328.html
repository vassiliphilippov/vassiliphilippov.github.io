coronet

noun
small crown for nobles
Pronunciation: / ˌkɔːrəˈnɛt / (AmE), / ˌkɒr.əˈnɛt / (BrE)
a small crown worn by nobles or members of the aristocracy, signifying rank below that of a sovereign

Examples

A golden coronet
Each coronet was uniquely decorated with family emblems and jewels.
The duchess's coronet sparkled in the candlelight as she entered the ballroom.
The young baron wore a coronet at the royal ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crown - an ornamental headdress worn by a monarch
