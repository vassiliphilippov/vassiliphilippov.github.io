intracardially

adverb
within the heart
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɪn.trə.ˈkɑːr.dɪ.ə.lɪ/ (AmE), /ɪn.trə.ˈkɑː.dɪ.ə.lɪ/ (BrE)
by way of or within the heart, especially referring to the administration of substances directly into the heart

Examples

Injected intracardially
The doctor injected the medication intracardially to ensure it reached the heart quickly.
During the emergency, the drug was administered intracardially to revive the patient.
Some procedures require substances to be delivered intracardially for immediate cardiac effect.
