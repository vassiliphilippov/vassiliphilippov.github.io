inheriting

adjective
having legal right to inherit
2.1 3.2
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈhe.rɪ.tɪŋ/ /; / /ɪn.ˈhe.rə.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈhe.rɪ.tɪŋ/ /; / /ɪn.ˈhe.rə.tɪŋ/ (BrE)

Examples
