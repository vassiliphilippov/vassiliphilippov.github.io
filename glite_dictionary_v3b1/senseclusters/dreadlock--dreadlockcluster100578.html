dreadlock

noun
a rope-like braid or mat of hair
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɛd.lɑk / (AmE), / ˈdrɛd.lɒk / (BrE)
a rope-like strand of hair formed by matting or braiding; often worn as part of a hairstyle popularized by the Rastafarian movement or various other cultures

Examples

Long dreadlocks
Many people choose to grow dreadlocks as a form of cultural expression.
She wore her dreadlocks piled high on her head for the interview.
The musician's long dreadlocks swung as he danced on stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braid - a length of hair or other material made up of three or more interlaced strands
  • synonym: plait - British term for a braid of hair
