brief

adjective
very short (clothing)
Pronunciation: / briːf / (AmE), / briːf / (BrE)
(of clothing) very short in length; covering little of the body.

Examples

Brief shorts
His swimsuit was so brief that it barely met the competition rules.
She wore a brief skirt during the summer festival.
The new fashion trend features brief shorts for both men and women.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abbreviated - (of clothing) made shorter than usual
  • antonym: long - (of clothing) covering most of the body
