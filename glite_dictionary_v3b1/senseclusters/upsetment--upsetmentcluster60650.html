upsetment

noun
physical overturning
7.6
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈsɛtmənt / (AmE), / ʌpˈsɛtmənt / (BrE)
the act or result of overturning or disturbing the position of something, especially physically

Examples

Upsetment of the cart
The upsetment of the cart spilled all the apples onto the road.
Heavy winds led to the upsetment of several outdoor market stalls.
After the upsetment of the canoe, everyone had to swim to shore.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disturbance - an interruption of a settled condition
  • synonym: overturn - to turn something over or upside down
  • antonym: stability - the state of being steady and not changing
