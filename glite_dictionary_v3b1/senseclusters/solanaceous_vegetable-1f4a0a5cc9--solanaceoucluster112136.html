solanaceous vegetable

noun
vegetable from the nightshade family
8.4
Pronunciation: / soʊləˈneɪʃəs ˈvɛdʒtəbl̩ / (AmE), / səʊləˈneɪʃəs ˈvɛdʒtəbl̩ / (BrE)
A vegetable that is a fruit of a plant from the Solanaceae family, especially genera such as Solanum, Capsicum, and Lycopersicon.

Examples

Solanaceous vegetables like tomatoes
Gardeners often rotate their crops to reduce diseases that affect solanaceous vegetables like peppers and tomatoes.
Potatoes, tomatoes, and eggplants are all considered solanaceous vegetables.
Some people avoid solanaceous vegetables due to concerns about alkaloids found in these plants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nightshade - plant of the Solanaceae (nightshade) family
