boarding

noun
structure or barrier made of boards
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔrdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdɪŋ / (BrE)
A structure or barrier made from boards, especially used for enclosing, covering, or protecting an area.

Examples

Protective boarding
Temporary boarding was set up to protect the storefront windows during renovations.
The construction site was surrounded by boarding to keep out unauthorized visitors.
They removed the old boarding from the shed before repainting it.
