mind-bending

adjective
producing strong mental effects
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪndˌbɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪndˌbɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
Extremely affecting the mind, especially in a way that alters perception or causes hallucinations.

Examples

A mind-bending experience
He described the art installation as a truly mind-bending experience.
Some drugs have mind-bending effects that alter the user's perception of reality.
The movie's mind-bending plot kept the audience guessing until the very end.

  • synonym: psychedelic - producing profound changes in perception or consciousness
