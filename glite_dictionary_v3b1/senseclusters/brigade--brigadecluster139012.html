brigade

noun
coordinated online group
5.8
Pronunciation: / brɪˈɡeɪd / (AmE), / brɪˈɡeɪd / (BrE)
a coordinated group of people on the internet who act together to support or attack a person, idea, or post, typically by mass-posting, commenting, voting, or sharing.

Examples

Online brigade
An online brigade of fans flooded the hashtag with supportive messages.
The political brigade coordinated thousands of replies to influence the trending topic.
Moderators warned that a brigade appearing suddenly could distort the discussion unfairly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: campaign - an organised set of actions intended to influence opinion or achieve a goal
  • synonym: mob - a large group acting together, often disorderly; sometimes used of hostile online groups
