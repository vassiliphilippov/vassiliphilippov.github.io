four-ply

adjective
having four layers or strands
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌplaɪ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌplaɪ / (BrE)
made with four layers or strands, especially of material such as yarn, wood, or tissue

Examples

Four-ply tissue paper
She bought a pack of four-ply tissues for the winter season.
The cable uses four-ply wiring for added strength and durability.
This blanket is made from four-ply yarn, making it extra warm.
