pixyish

adjective
playfully mischievous
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪksiɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɪksiɪʃ / (BrE)
having a playful, mischievous, or elfin quality, like a pixie

Examples

A pixyish smile
Her pixyish smile made everyone in the room feel at ease.
He had a pixyish sense of humor that kept his friends laughing.
The actress brought a pixyish charm to her role in the comedy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elfin - small and charming in a mischievous way
  • synonym: impish - playfully mischievous or naughty
  • antonym: serious - solemn or not playful
