telephotograph

noun
photograph taken with a telephoto lens
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛləˈfoʊtəˌɡræf / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˈfəʊtəˌɡrɑːf / (BrE)
A photograph taken using a telephoto lens, allowing distant objects to be captured as though they are closer.

Examples

Telephotograph of wildlife
Many news agencies depend on the telephotograph to document events happening far from the action.
She hung a telephotograph of the city skyline on her living room wall.
The wildlife photographer captured a stunning telephotograph of the rare bird from a safe distance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: photograph - a picture made using a camera
