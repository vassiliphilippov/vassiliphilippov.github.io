Britannia

proper noun
British royal ship name
7.0
Pronunciation: / brɪˈtæniə / (AmE), / brɪˈtænɪə / (BrE)
the name given to several British ships and royal yachts, especially those associated with the British monarchy

Examples

Royal Britannia yacht
The Britannia sailed on many important royal voyages during the twentieth century.
Tourists can now visit the retired Britannia in Edinburgh to learn about its history.
Several ships named Britannia have played a role in British maritime tradition.
