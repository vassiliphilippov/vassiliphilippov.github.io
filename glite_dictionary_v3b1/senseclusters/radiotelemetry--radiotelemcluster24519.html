radiotelemetry

noun
remote data transmission
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌreɪdioʊtəˈlɛmɪtri / (AmE), / ˌreɪdiəʊtɪˈlɛmɪtri / (BrE)
the use of radio waves to transmit data from remote instruments or devices to a receiver

Examples

Radiotelemetry data transmission
Researchers used radiotelemetry to monitor the heart rates of wild animals in their natural habitat.
The team relied on radiotelemetry to collect weather data from remote mountain stations.
Advances in radiotelemetry have improved the tracking of migratory birds over long distances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: telemetry - the process of recording and transmitting data from remote sources
