three-ring circus

noun
circus with three simultaneous performance rings
5.7 3.3
Pronunciation: /θriː rɪŋ ˈsɜːr.kəs/ (AmE), /θriː rɪŋ ˈsɜː.kəs/ /; / /friː rɪŋ ˈsɜː.kəs/ (BrE)
