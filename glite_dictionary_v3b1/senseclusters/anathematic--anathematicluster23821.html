anathematic

adjective
formally cursed
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌænəθəˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌænəθəˈmætɪk / (BrE)
formally cursed, excommunicated, or relating to something condemned by religious authority

Examples

Anathematic writings
The writings were considered anathematic by the church and were banned from circulation.
His anathematic views led to his exclusion from the religious community.
Declaring the ritual anathematic, the council forbade its practice among the faithful.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accursed - under a curse; doomed
  • antonym: blessed - consecrated or favored by God
