pantothenic acid

noun
a B vitamin involved in metabolism
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpæn.təˈθɛn.ɪk ˈæs.ɪd / (AmE), / ˌpæn.təˈθen.ɪk ˈæs.ɪd / (BrE)
a vitamin of the B complex group that helps the body metabolize fats, carbohydrates, and certain amino acids, and is present in many foods

Examples

Pantothenic acid vitamin
A deficiency in pantothenic acid is rare since it is found in a wide variety of foods.
Many energy drinks are fortified with pantothenic acid to support metabolism.
Studies show that pantothenic acid plays a vital role in synthesizing coenzyme A.
