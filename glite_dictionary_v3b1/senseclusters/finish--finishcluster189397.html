finish

noun
lingering taste of wine after swallowing
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪʃ / (BrE)
(of wine) the taste left on the palate after the wine is swallowed, especially as part of wine tasting evaluation.

Examples

A bitter finish
A bitter finish can indicate a wine that needs more aging.
The sommelier praised the wine’s clean finish as its best attribute.
This red has a long, smooth finish with hints of blackberry and spice.
