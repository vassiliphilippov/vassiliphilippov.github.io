untuned

adjective
not in tune
5.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtuːnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtjuːnd / (BrE)
not properly adjusted to the correct pitch or standard, especially in music

Examples

Untuned violin
The orchestra struggled to play together because the untuned violin sounded off.
He realized the guitar was untuned before starting the performance.
An untuned radio can produce a lot of static and unclear sounds.

Related Terms

  • antonym: in tune - properly adjusted to the correct pitch
  • synonym: out of tune - not matching the correct pitch
