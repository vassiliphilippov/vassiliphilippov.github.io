rebaptism

noun
symbolic renaming
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈbæptɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌriːˈbæptɪzəm / (BrE)
a figurative act of giving a new name or identity to a person, organization, or thing, symbolizing a fresh start or rebranding

Examples

Rebaptism of a company
The rebaptism of the old theater marked a new era for the arts community.
After the merger, the company underwent a rebaptism to reflect its new mission.
The city celebrated its rebaptism with a festival to honor its renewed identity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rebranding - the process of changing the image or identity of a company or product
  • synonym: renaming - the act of giving a new name to something
