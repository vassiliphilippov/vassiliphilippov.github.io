reproductivity

noun
ability to reproduce
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌriːprəˌdʌkˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˌriːprəˌdʌkˈtɪvəti / (BrE)
the ability of an organism or species to produce offspring or reproduce

Examples

Reproductivity of animals
The reproductivity of certain plants depends on environmental conditions such as temperature and rainfall.
Scientists are concerned about the declining reproductivity of fish in polluted rivers.
Improved nutrition can have a positive effect on the reproductivity of livestock.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fertility - the natural ability to produce offspring
  • antonym: infertility - the inability to produce offspring
