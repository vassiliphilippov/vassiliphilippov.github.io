baste

verb
strike violently and repeatedly
6.8
Pronunciation: / beɪst / (AmE), / beɪst / (BrE)
to strike someone violently and repeatedly, especially with great force

Examples

To baste him hard
During the heated argument, one man tried to baste the other with a rolled-up newspaper.
If you keep insulting him, he might baste you right in the face.
She threatened to baste her noisy neighbor if he didn't quiet down.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beat - to strike repeatedly; to hit over and over
  • synonym: clobber - to hit someone hard and repeatedly
