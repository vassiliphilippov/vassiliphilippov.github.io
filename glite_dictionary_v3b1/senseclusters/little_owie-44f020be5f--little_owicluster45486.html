little owie

noun
minor injury (child's term)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬l ˈaʊ.i / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl ˈaʊ.i / (BrE)
A small injury or slight hurt, often used by or with children in informal contexts.

Examples

A little owie
Even a little owie can feel like a big deal to a child.
Let me kiss your little owie and make it feel better.
My son fell and got a little owie on his knee, but he was brave about it.
