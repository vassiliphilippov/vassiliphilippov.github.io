fiery

adjective
suggestive of fire or flames
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪəri / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəri / (BrE)
having qualities of fire; very hot, burning, or suggestive of flames

Examples

Fiery explosion
A fiery meteor streaked across the night with a brilliant, burning trail.
A fiery sunset lit up the entire sky with shades of orange and red.
The fiery furnace radiated heat throughout the cold stone chamber.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blazing - burning brightly or intensely
  • antonym: cool - having a low temperature or lacking warmth
