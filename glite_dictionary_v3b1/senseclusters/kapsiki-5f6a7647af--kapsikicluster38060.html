Kapsiki

noun
a Chadic language of Nigeria and Cameroon
9.9
Pronunciation: / kæpˈsiːki / (AmE), / kapˈsiːki / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken mainly south of Lake Chad, particularly in Adamawa State, Nigeria, and the Mandara mountains region.
