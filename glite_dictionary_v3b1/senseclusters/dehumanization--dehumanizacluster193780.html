Synonyms

  • depersonalization
    9.9 0.09/m
    depersonalization treats a person as an object and removes individual traits, while dehumanization more broadly strips dignity and human qualities.
  • animalization
    9.9 0.00/m
    animalization compares people to animals to show loss of human traits, while dehumanization is a wider removal of dignity and personhood.

Related Terms
