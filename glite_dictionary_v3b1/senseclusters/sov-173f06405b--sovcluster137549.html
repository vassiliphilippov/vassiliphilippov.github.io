SOV

noun
asset preserving value
9.3
Pronunciation: / ɛs oʊ vi / (AmE), / ɛs əʊ viː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'store of value', meaning an asset that is expected to maintain or increase its value over time

Examples

Gold as SOV
Gold has long been considered a reliable SOV by investors around the world.
Some people argue that real estate is a better SOV than cash during times of inflation.
Central banks often hold foreign currency reserves as an SOV to protect national wealth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: investment - an asset purchased with the expectation it will generate income or appreciate
  • antonym: liability - something that represents a financial obligation or debt
  • synonym: store of value - an asset that maintains its value over time
