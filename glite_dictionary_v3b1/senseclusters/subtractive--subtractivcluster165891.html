subtractive

adjective
relating to subtraction
Pronunciation: / səbˈtræktɪv / (AmE), / səbˈtraktɪv / (BrE)
relating to or involving subtraction; reducing or taking away.

Examples

Subtractive method
Our approach to budgeting was subtractive, identifying unnecessary expenses to cut.
The lens corrected the vision problem through a subtractive process, removing excess material.
The printer uses the subtractive color model to blend inks and create various hues.
