talkfest

noun
discussion event
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔkˌfɛst / (AmE), / ˈtɔːkˌfɛst / (BrE)
an event, meeting, or program where there is a lot of discussion or conversation, often with many participants and sometimes little action

Examples

A talkfest event
The annual talkfest attracted experts from around the world to discuss climate change.
She appeared as a guest on a popular talkfest to share her views on education reform.
After hours of lively conversation, the talkfest finally came to an end.
The annual summit became a talkfest with little progress on the main issues.
Instead of making decisions, the board meeting turned into a long talkfest.
Many attendees left the talkfest feeling that nothing had really been accomplished.

Related Terms

  • antonym: action - the process of doing something to achieve a result
  • synonym: conference - a large meeting for discussion
  • synonym: debate - a formal discussion on a particular topic with opposing arguments
  • synonym: panel discussion - a conversation among several people on a specific topic, often broadcast
