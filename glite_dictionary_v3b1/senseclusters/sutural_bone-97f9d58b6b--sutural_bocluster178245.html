sutural bone

noun
tiny bone in skull sutures
9.9 8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːtʃərəl boʊn / (AmE), / ˈsjuːtʃərəl bəʊn / (BrE)
a small, irregular bone found within the sutures between the major bones of the skull

Examples

A sutural bone in skull
A sutural bone is often found near the lambdoid suture.
Some people have more than one sutural bone in their skull.
The x-ray clearly showed a sutural bone within the cranial suture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wormian bone - a small irregular bone in the sutures of the skull, especially in the lambdoidal suture
