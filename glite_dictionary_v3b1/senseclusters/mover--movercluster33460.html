mover

noun
company that moves belongings
1.8 1.24/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàmuÀêv…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàmuÀêv…ô / (BrE)
a person or company whose job is to move people's belongings from one place to another

Examples

Professional mover
If you don't have a truck, it's best to call a professional mover.
The mover arrived early to start packing up the office furniture.
We hired a mover to help us relocate to our new home.
One of the movers accidentally dropped a box, but nothing was broken.
The movers arrived early in the morning to start loading the truck.
We hired professional movers to handle our fragile items during the move.

Synonyms

  • moving company
    2.8 0.29/m
    moving company is a business that transports people's belongings between homes, while mover can mean a person or company doing the move.
  • removal company
    3.0 0.03/m
    removal company is the service that moves furniture and boxes during relocation, while mover can be either the worker or the company.
  • remover
    3.4 0.12/m
    remover is the worker who carries furniture and boxes into trucks, while mover can mean either that worker or a whole moving firm.
  • relocator
    4.4 0.01/m
    relocator is a person or company that moves people or staff between cities, while mover usually refers to someone who moves possessions.

Related Terms
