cripplingly

adverb
severely or extremely
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪp.lɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈkrɪp.lɪŋ.li / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely severe or causes great difficulty or limitation

Examples

Cripplingly high bills
The family struggled under cripplingly high medical bills for years.
Some regions are cripplingly dependent on a single industry for employment.
The drought was cripplingly severe, affecting crops across the entire country.
He was cripplingly nervous before his first public speech and could barely stand.
The cost of living in the city is cripplingly high for many young people.
She became cripplingly self-conscious whenever she had to speak in class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extremely - to a very great degree
  • antonym: mildly - in a gentle or not severe way
  • synonym: severely - to a very great degree; seriously
