sphenoethmoidal

adjective
of sphenoid and ethmoid
Pronunciation: / ˌsfiːnoʊˌɛθˈmɔɪdəl / (AmE), / ˌsfiːnəʊˌɛθˈmɔɪdəl / (BrE)
relating to both the sphenoid and ethmoid bones in the skull

Examples

Sphenoethmoidal recess
The sphenoethmoidal recess is an important anatomical feature in the nasal cavity.
Surgeons must be careful when operating near the sphenoethmoidal region to avoid damaging vital structures.
The infection had spread to the sphenoethmoidal area, causing severe sinus pain.
