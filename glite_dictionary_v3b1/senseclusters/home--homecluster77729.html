home

noun
baseball: base runners must reach to score
Pronunciation: / hoʊm / (AmE), / həʊm / (BrE)
(in baseball) the base, marked by a flat rubber plate, where the batter stands and which a runner must touch to score a run; home plate.

Examples

Home plate
He hit a long fly ball and safely returned to home to score the winning run.
The crowd cheered as the runner slid into home just ahead of the tag.
The umpire signaled that the player missed home on his way to scoring.

Related Terms
