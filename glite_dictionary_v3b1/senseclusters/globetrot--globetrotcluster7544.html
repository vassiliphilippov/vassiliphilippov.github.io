globetrot

verb
to travel widely
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡloʊbˌtrɑt / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊbˌtrɒt / (BrE)
to travel to many different countries, especially for pleasure or adventure

Examples

To globetrot the world
After college, she decided to globetrot for a year before starting her career.
Many people dream of being able to globetrot and experience new cultures.
He spent his twenties globetrotting and collecting stories from every continent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: explore - to travel through an unfamiliar area to learn about it
  • antonym: stay - to remain in one place
  • synonym: travel - to go from one place to another, especially over a distance
