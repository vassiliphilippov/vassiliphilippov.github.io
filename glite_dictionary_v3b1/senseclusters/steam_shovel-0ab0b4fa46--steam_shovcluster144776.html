steam shovel

noun
excavating machine powered by steam
5.9 4.6
Pronunciation: /stiːm ˈʃʌv.əl/ (AmE), /stiːm ˈʃʌv.əl/ (BrE)
a large machine powered by steam, used for digging and moving earth or rock, especially in construction or mining
