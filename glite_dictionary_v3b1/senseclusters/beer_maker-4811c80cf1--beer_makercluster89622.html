beer maker

noun
person who brews beer
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪr ˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈbɪə ˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who brews beer or ale from malt, hops, and water

Examples

A beer maker brews beer
After years as a home beer maker, she decided to open her own microbrewery.
Many craft beer makers experiment with unique flavors and brewing methods.
The local beer maker uses traditional recipes to create his signature ales.
