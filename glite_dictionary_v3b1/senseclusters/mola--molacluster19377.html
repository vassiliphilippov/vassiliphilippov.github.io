A very large, ocean-dwelling fish with an oval, laterally compressed body, high dorsal and anal fins, and a rudder-like tail; found in warm seas worldwide, also known as the ocean sunfish.

Examples

The mola can weigh over a thousand kilograms and is often seen basking near the surface of the ocean.
