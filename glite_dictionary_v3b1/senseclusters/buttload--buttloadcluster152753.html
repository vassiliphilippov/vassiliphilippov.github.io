buttload

noun
large barrel or quantity
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtˌloʊd / (AmE), / ˈbʌtˌləʊd / (BrE)
a large cask or barrel, or the amount it holds, historically used as a unit of liquid volume for wine or ale (about 108–126 gallons)

Examples

A buttload of wine
The cellar contained a buttload of ale ready for the festival.
Historically, a buttload was a standard measure for shipping wine across Europe.
They discovered an old buttload in the ruins of the ancient winery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barrel - a large cylindrical container or the amount it holds
  • synonym: hogshead - a large cask or unit of liquid measure, especially for alcohol
