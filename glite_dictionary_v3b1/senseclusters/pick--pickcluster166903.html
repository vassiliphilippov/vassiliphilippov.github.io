pick

noun
tool for breaking rock or earth
Pronunciation: / pɪk / (AmE), / pɪk / (BrE)
a heavy iron tool with a wooden handle and a curved head that is pointed at both ends, used for breaking up hard surfaces such as rock or earth

Examples

A mining pick
He grabbed his trusty pick before heading out to clear the trail.
The miners dug into the hillside with a pick to reach the mineral vein.
With every swing of the pick, pieces of stone broke free from the wall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pickaxe - a large tool with a heavy metal head used for breaking hard surfaces, similar to a pick
