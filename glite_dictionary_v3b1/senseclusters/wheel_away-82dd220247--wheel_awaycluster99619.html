wheel away

verb
remove by rolling on wheels
Pronunciation: / wiːl əˈweɪ / (AmE), / wiːl əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to move or remove something by rolling it on wheels

Examples

To wheel away a bike
After fixing the flat tire, they wheeled away the bike from the garage.
Paramedics quickly wheeled away the patient on a stretcher.
The janitor quietly wheeled away the cleaning cart after finishing his work.
