cabbage butterfly

noun
white butterfly, larvae feed on cabbage
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkæbɪdʒ ˌbʌtərˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈkabɪdʒ ˌbʌtəflaɪ / (BrE)
A common white butterfly, especially of the genus Pieris, whose larvae feed on cabbage and related plants.
