Fuscoboletinus paluster

noun
edible fungus with pinkish-purple cap and yellow pores
Pronunciation: / ˌfʌskoʊboʊˈlɛtɪnəs pəˈlʌstər / (AmE), / ˌfʌskəʊbəʊˈliːtɪnəs pəˈlʌstə / (BrE)
