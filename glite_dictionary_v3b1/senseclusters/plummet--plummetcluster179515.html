plummet

noun
metal bob on a plumb line
4.4 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈplʌ.mət/ (AmE), /ˈplʌ.mɪt/ /; / /ˈplʌ.mət/ (BrE)
A small, heavy metal weight, especially one used at the end of a plumb line to determine vertical alignment.

Examples

A plummet weight
