scare away

verb
make flee due to fear
1.0
Pronunciation: / skɛr əˈweɪ / (AmE), / skeə əˈweɪ /; / skeə(r) əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to make someone or something leave or stay away by making them afraid or intimidated

Rising crime rates may scare away potential investors from the area.
The loud noise was enough to scare away the birds from the garden.
