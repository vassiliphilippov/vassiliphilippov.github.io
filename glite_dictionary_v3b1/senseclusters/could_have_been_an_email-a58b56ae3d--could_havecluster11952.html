could have been an email

x
suggests a meeting was unnecessary; email would suffice
Pronunciation: / kʊd həv bɪn ən iːˌmɛl / (AmE), / kəd həv bɪn ən iːˌmeɪl / (BrE)
used to suggest that a meeting or event was unnecessary and the information could have been communicated via email instead

Examples

It could have been an email
After sitting through the entire presentation, I realized it could have been an email.
Honestly, most of these weekly check-ins could have been an email instead of taking up our afternoon.
That hour-long conference could have been an email and saved everyone a lot of time.
