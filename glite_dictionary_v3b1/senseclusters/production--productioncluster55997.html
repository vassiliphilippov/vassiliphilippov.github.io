production

noun
presentation of evidence in court (legal)
9.6
Pronunciation: / prəˈdəkʃən / (AmE), / prəˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
(Law) The act of presenting or exhibiting something, such as documents or evidence, in a court of law.

Examples

Production of evidence
Failure to comply with the production of records can result in legal penalties.
The defense counsel requested the production of all relevant medical reports during the trial.
The judge ordered the production of the signed contract before the next hearing.
