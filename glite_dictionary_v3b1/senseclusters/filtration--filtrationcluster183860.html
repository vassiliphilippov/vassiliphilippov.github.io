filtration

noun
process of filtering liquids or gases
2.3
Pronunciation: / fɪlˈtreɪʃən / (AmE), / fɪlˈtreɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of passing a liquid or gas through a filter to remove solids or unwanted particles

Examples

Air filtration
Air filtration is essential in operating rooms to maintain a sterile environment.
The laboratory conducted tests on the effectiveness of different filtration methods.
The water purification system uses filtration to remove impurities and contaminants.
During filtration, the residue remains on the filter paper while the liquid passes through.
Our laboratory uses a special system for rapid filtration of samples.
The filtration of drinking water removes harmful contaminants.
