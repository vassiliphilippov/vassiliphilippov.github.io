veal parmesan

noun
breaded veal cutlet with tomato sauce and cheese
Pronunciation: / vil pɑːr.məˈzæn / (AmE), / viːl ˌpɑː.məˈzæn / (BrE)
A dish consisting of breaded and sautéed veal cutlet, typically topped with tomato sauce and cheese.

Examples

Veal parmesan dish
At the Italian dinner, we enjoyed homemade veal parmesan with a fresh green salad.
She ordered veal parmesan because she loves the combination of tender veal, tomato sauce, and melted cheese.
The restaurant's veal parmesan was served piping hot with a generous helping of spaghetti.
