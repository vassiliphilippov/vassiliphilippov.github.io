Nemertina

noun
a phylum of slender marine worms
9.9
Pronunciation: / nɛˈmɜːrtɪnə / (AmE), / nɪˈmɜːtɪnə / (BrE)
a phylum of slender, mostly marine, unsegmented worms with a long proboscis used for hunting, also known as ribbon worms.

Examples

The Nemertina worm
Marine biologists study the unique hunting methods of Nemertina.
Scientists found several species belonging to the Nemertina phylum in the tidal pools.
The Nemertina are also called ribbon worms because of their long, slender bodies.
