Samarra

proper noun
ancient Mesopotamian culture
Pronunciation: / səˈmɑːrə / (AmE), / səˈmɑːrə / (BrE)
an early prehistoric archaeological culture of northern Mesopotamia, noted for its unique pottery and early irrigation systems

Examples

Samarra archaeological culture
The Samarra culture is famous for its finely decorated pottery and early use of irrigation.
Archaeologists have uncovered ancient settlements belonging to the Samarra culture in northern Mesopotamia.
Distinctive spiral patterns on pottery are a hallmark of the Samarra archaeological culture.
