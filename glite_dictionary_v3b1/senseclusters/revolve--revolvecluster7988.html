revolve

verb
move in a circle around a center or axis
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɑlv / (AmE), / rɪˈvɒlv / (BrE)
to move in a circle or orbit around a central point or axis

Examples

Satellites revolve around the Earth to provide global communication coverage.
The planets revolve around the sun in precise orbits.
