stepson

noun
husband's or wife's son by a former marriage
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛpˌsʌn / (AmE), / ˈstɛp.sʌn / (BrE)
A son of one's spouse by a previous marriage or relationship.

Examples

My stepson will be joining us for dinner this evening.
