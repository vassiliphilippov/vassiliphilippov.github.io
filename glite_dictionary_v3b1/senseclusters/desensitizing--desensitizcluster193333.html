desensitizing

adjective
making less sensitive or responsive
8.2 5.5
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈsen.sə.taɪ.zɪŋ/ /; / /dɪ.ˈsen.sɪ.taɪ.zɪŋ/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈsen.sɪ.taɪ.zɪŋ/ /; / /dɪ.ˈsen.sə.taɪ.zɪŋ/ (BrE)
making someone or something less sensitive or responsive to physical or emotional stimuli

Examples

Repeated exposure to violent media has a desensitizing effect on viewers.
