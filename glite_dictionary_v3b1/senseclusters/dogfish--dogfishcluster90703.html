dogfish

noun
a small shark species
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔɡˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
Any of several small sharks found in coastal waters, typically with a slender body and sharp, spiny fins.

Examples

Small shark dogfish
Fishermen often catch dogfish while looking for cod in shallow waters.
Marine biologists study dogfish to learn more about shark behavior and evolution.
The dogfish is sometimes used in fish and chips in some parts of the UK.
