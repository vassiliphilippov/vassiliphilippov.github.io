antituberculosis

adjective
against tuberculosis
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪtuːˌbɜːrkjəˈloʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌæntaɪtjuːˌbɜːkjʊˈləʊsɪs / (BrE)
designed to prevent or treat tuberculosis

Examples

Antituberculosis medicine
The patient was given an antituberculosis drug to help fight the infection.
Doctors recommend antituberculosis therapy for those diagnosed with active tuberculosis.
Research into new antituberculosis medications is ongoing due to drug resistance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antimicrobial - designed to kill or inhibit microorganisms
  • antonym: tubercular - relating to or affected by tuberculosis
