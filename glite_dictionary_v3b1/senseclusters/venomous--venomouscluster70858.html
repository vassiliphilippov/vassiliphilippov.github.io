venomous

adjective
producing and capable of injecting venom
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛnəməs / (AmE), / ˈvɛnəməs / (BrE)
(of an animal or its bite or sting) capable of injecting venom by means of a bite or sting, and thus capable of causing harm or death.

Examples

Venomous snake bite
She recognized the scorpion as a venomous species native to the desert.
Some spiders are harmless, while others are dangerously venomous.
The bite of a venomous snake requires immediate medical attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: poisonous - containing or producing poison; harmful if ingested or absorbed
