riffler

noun
sample splitting device
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈrɪ.flər/ (AmE), /ˈrɪ.flə/ (BrE)
a device used to divide a bulk sample of grain, ore, or powder into smaller, representative portions for analysis

Examples

Sample riffler
The technician used a riffler to ensure each test received an equal portion of the mineral sample.
A riffler is essential in laboratories that analyze bulk powders for quality control.
Before analysis, the bulk grain was divided using a riffler to obtain representative samples.

Related Terms

  • synonym: divider - a tool used to separate or split materials into parts
  • synonym: sampler - a device or tool used to take samples for analysis
