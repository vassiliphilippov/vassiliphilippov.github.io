neurographic

adjective
nerve imaging
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to the recording or imaging of nerves or the nervous system

Examples

Neurographic imaging test
A neurographic test can reveal abnormalities in nerve function.
Doctors used neurographic imaging to assess the patient's nerve damage.
The clinic specializes in neurographic procedures for diagnosing neurological disorders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electrographic - relating to electrical recording, especially of biological activity
  • synonym: neurological - relating to the nervous system
