hydrangea

noun
a shrub with large, showy flowers
7.1
Pronunciation: / haɪˈdreɪndʒə / (AmE), / hɪˈdrændʒɪə / (BrE)
a deciduous or evergreen shrub of the genus Hydrangea, often grown for its large clusters of brightly colored flowers

Examples

A hydrangea bush
After pruning, the old hydrangea produced even more flowers the following season.
She planted a hydrangea bush near the front porch for a burst of summer color.
The garden was filled with blooming hydrangeas in shades of blue and pink.
