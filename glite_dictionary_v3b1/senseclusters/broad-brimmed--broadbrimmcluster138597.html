broad-brimmed

adjective
having a wide brim (esp. of hats)
8.3
Pronunciation: / brɔd ˈbrɪmd / (AmE), / brɔːd ˈbrɪmd / (BrE)
(of hats) having a wide brim extending outward from the crown.

Examples

Broad-brimmed hat
During the parade, many people chose broad-brimmed hats for extra shade.
She wore a broad-brimmed hat to protect herself from the sun.
The actor was easily recognized by his distinctive broad-brimmed fedora.
