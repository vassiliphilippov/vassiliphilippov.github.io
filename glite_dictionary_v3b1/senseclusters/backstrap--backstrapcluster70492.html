backstrap

noun
strap along the back
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌstræp / (AmE), / ˈbækˌstræp / (BrE)
a strap or part that runs along the back of an object, garment, or animal, often providing support or attachment

Examples

A backstrap on a backpack
The backpack's backstrap made it more comfortable to carry heavy loads.
She adjusted the backstrap on her sandal for a better fit.
The saddle's backstrap helped keep it securely in place on the horse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: belt - a band worn around the waist or used for support
  • synonym: shoulder strap - a strap that goes over the shoulder to support or carry something
