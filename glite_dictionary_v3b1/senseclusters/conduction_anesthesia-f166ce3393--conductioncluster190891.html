conduction anesthesia

noun
anesthesia by blocking nerve conduction
6.2 4.5
Pronunciation: / kənˈdʌk.ʃən ænˈiːs.θi.ə /; / kənˈdʌkʃən ˌænəsˈθiːʒə / (AmE), / kənˈdʌk.ʃən ænˈiːs.θi.ə /; / kənˈdʌkʃən ˌanɪsˈθiːziə / (BrE)
a form of anesthesia in which sensation in an area is blocked by applying an anesthetic agent to the nerve supplying that area
