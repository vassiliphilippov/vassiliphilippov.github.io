thorny skate

noun
species of spiny cold-water fish
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈθɔrni skeɪt / (AmE), / ˈθɔːni skeɪt / (BrE)
a species of cold-water bottom-dwelling fish with spines on its back, growing up to 40 inches long

Examples

The thorny skate fish
Fishermen often recognize the thorny skate by the distinctive spines on its back.
Researchers are studying the decline in thorny skate populations in recent years.
The thorny skate inhabits the cold, deep waters of the North Atlantic Ocean.
