Synonyms

  • atonement
    7.5 2.17/m
    atonement is the act of making amends for a sin or wrong, while expiation names the formal removal of guilt.
  • penance
    7.4 1.29/m
    penance is a self-chosen act of regret or punishment to show repentance, while expiation focuses on removing guilt.
  • reparation
    6.8 1.08/m
    reparation is compensation or action to repair harm, while expiation focuses on clearing moral guilt rather than simply paying.
  • amends
    3.5 0.00/m
    amends are actions or money given to repair a wrong, while expiation often denotes a deeper moral or religious clearing of guilt.
  • satisfaction
    3.9 1.40/m
    satisfaction is redress or compensation after a complaint or injury, while expiation is about making guilt vanish.
  • propitiation
    9.9 0.23/m
    propitiation is pleasing or appeasing someone, often a god, while expiation is removing guilt or blame.
