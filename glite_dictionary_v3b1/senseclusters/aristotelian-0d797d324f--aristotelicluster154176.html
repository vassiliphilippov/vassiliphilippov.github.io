Aristotelian

adjective
related to Aristotle or his philosophy
Pronunciation: / ˌærɪstəˈtiliən / (AmE), / ˌærɪstəˈtiːliən / (BrE)
relating to Aristotle or his philosophy, methods, or ideas

Examples

Aristotelian philosophy
Her research is based on Aristotelian logic and methods.
Many medieval philosophers were influenced by Aristotelian thought.
The Aristotelian approach contrasts with modern scientific methodologies.
