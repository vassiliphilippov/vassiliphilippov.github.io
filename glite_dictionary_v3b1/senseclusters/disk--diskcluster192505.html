disk

verb
break up soil with a disk harrow
Pronunciation: / dɪsk / (AmE), / dɪsk / (BrE)
to break up and smooth soil by drawing a disk harrow over it.

Examples

To disk the field

The farmer decided to disk the field before planting the new crop.
Workers disked the plot to prepare it for spring seeding.
