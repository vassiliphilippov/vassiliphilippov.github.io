beastie

noun
mild insult
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːsti / (AmE), / ˈbiːsti / (BrE)
an informal, usually mild term for a person or animal regarded as naughty, unpleasant, or mischievous

Examples

A naughty beastie
She called her brother a beastie after he hid her favorite book as a prank.
The teacher laughed and said, 'You little beastie!' when the child played a harmless trick.
Sometimes, their cat acts like a real beastie and knocks things off the table.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brute - a rough or cruel person
  • synonym: rascal - a mischievous or cheeky person, especially a child
