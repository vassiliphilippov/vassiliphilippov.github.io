restless

adjective
unable to relax or be still
2.7 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈres.ləs/ /; / /ˈres.lɪs/ (AmE), /ˈres.ləs/ /; / /ˈres.lɪs/ (BrE)
unable to relax or stay still; constantly moving or active, often because of anxiety, boredom, or uneasiness

Examples
