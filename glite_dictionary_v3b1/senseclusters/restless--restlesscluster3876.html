Synonyms

  • fidgety
    4.8 0.64/m
    fidgety is full of small nervous movements and sounds, while restless means a broader need to move or cannot relax.
  • restive
    8.3 0.68/m
    restive is tense and often quietly resisting control, while restless is a more general inability to stay still or relax.
  • jittery
    4.5 1.36/m
    jittery feels shaky and shows quick jerky movements, while restless covers any constant moving from worry, boredom, or unease.
  • twitchy
    4.6 0.40/m
    twitchy is jumpy and has sudden small twitches, while restless describes longer, general inability to sit still or relax.
  • agitated
    4.1 2.20/m
    agitated looks upset, noisy, or emotionally disturbed, while restless usually means quietly unable to relax or stay still.
  • fretful
    6.6 0.44/m
    fretful shows quiet worry or irritation and complaining, while restless focuses on moving about and not being able to relax.
