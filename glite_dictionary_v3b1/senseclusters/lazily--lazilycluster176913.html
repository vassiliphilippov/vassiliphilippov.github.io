lazily

adverb
by delaying loading until needed (computing)
5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈleɪ.zɪ.lɪ/ /; / /ˈleɪ.zə.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈleɪ.zɪ.lɪ/ /; / /ˈleɪ.zə.lɪ/ (BrE)
(in computing) in a way that delays loading or computing something until it is actually needed, to save time or resources.

Examples

Images load lazily
The page loads images lazily to speed up initial rendering.
The app lazily loads profile pictures as users scroll through the feed.
By default, the module lazily evaluates expressions only when their result is required.

Related Terms

  • antonym: eagerly - immediately; the opposite of delaying evaluation or loading
  • synonym: lazy loading - a technique that delays loading resources until they are needed (computing)
