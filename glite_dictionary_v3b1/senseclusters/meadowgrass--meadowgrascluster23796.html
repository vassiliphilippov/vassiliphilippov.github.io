meadowgrass

noun
grass thriving in moist meadows
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdoʊˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈmɛdəʊˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
Any of several types of grass that grow well in moist areas, especially in meadows.

Examples

Green meadowgrass
After the spring rains, the meadowgrass grew tall and vibrant across the field.
Seeds of meadowgrass are often mixed into lawn blends for their resilience in damp soils.
The cows grazed peacefully among the lush meadowgrass near the stream.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bluegrass - a type of meadow grass, especially Poa pratensis
