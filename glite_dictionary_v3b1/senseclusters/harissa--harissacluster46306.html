harissa

proper noun
Lebanese pilgrimage site
Pronunciation: / hɑːˈrɪsə / (AmE), / həˈrɪsə / (BrE)
a town and hilltop pilgrimage site in Lebanon, above Jounieh, famous for the shrine of Our Lady of Lebanon

Examples

Harissa pilgrimage site
Many tourists visit Harissa each year to admire the panoramic views of the coast.
The cable car ride up to Harissa offers breathtaking scenery over the city of Jounieh.
Pilgrims gather at Harissa to celebrate religious festivals at the famous shrine.
