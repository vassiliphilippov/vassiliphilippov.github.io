deesis

noun
Christian art motif
Pronunciation: / diˈiːsɪs / (AmE), / diˈiːsɪs / (BrE)
a traditional depiction in Christian art showing Christ between the Virgin Mary and John the Baptist, often in a scene of intercession

Examples

Deesis icon
The cathedral's main altar displays a beautiful deesis above the sanctuary.
Art historians studied the deesis to understand its symbolic meaning in Byzantine mosaics.
A famous deesis can be seen in the Hagia Sophia, featuring Christ, Mary, and John the Baptist.

Related Terms

  • synonym: icon - a religious image used in Christian worship
  • synonym: triptych - a work of art divided into three sections
