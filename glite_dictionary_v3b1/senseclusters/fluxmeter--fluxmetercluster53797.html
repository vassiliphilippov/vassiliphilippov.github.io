fluxmeter

noun
instrument for measuring magnetic flux
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈflʌksˌmiːtər / (AmE), / ˈflʌksˌmiːtə / (BrE)
An instrument used to measure magnetic flux, by detecting the current induced in a coil.

Examples

A fluxmeter instrument
A fluxmeter is essential for accurately determining the total magnetic flux through a coil.
The physicist calibrated the fluxmeter before starting his magnetic field experiments.
When the coil was exposed to a changing magnetic field, the fluxmeter displayed fluctuating readings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gaussmeter - an instrument measuring the strength of a magnetic field
