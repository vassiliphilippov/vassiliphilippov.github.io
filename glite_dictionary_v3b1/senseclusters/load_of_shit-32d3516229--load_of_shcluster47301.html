load of shit

noun
nonsense; complete falsehood (vulgar slang)
5.2
Pronunciation: / loʊd əv ʃɪt / (AmE), / ləʊd əv ʃɪt / (BrE)
something that is not true or is complete nonsense; a completely false or ridiculous statement

Examples

A load of shit
Don’t listen to her story; it’s just a load of shit.
Everything he said at the meeting was a total load of shit.
He tried to justify his actions, but it sounded like a load of shit to everyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bullshit - something that is nonsense or false, usually intentionally
  • synonym: load of rubbish - a statement or idea that is silly or not true (less vulgar)
  • antonym: truth - statement or idea that is true
