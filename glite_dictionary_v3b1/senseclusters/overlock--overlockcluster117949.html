overlock

verb
to sew fabric edges
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌlɑk / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəlɒk / (BrE)
to sew over the edge of fabric with a special stitch to prevent it from fraying

Examples

To overlock fabric edges
It is important to overlock the seams to make the garment more durable.
She overlocked the fabric edges before starting the main stitching.
Many sewing machines can overlock automatically for a professional finish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hem - to fold and sew the edge of fabric to prevent fraying
  • synonym: serge - to finish the edge of fabric with an overcasting stitch
