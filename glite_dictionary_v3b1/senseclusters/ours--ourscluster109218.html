ours

pronoun
possessive pronoun
Pronunciation: / aʊɚz / (AmE), / aʊəz / (BrE)
used to refer to something that belongs to or is connected with the speaker and one or more other people

Examples

Ours is the red one
This umbrella is ours, so please do not take it by mistake.
The victory was especially sweet because ours was the underdog team.
She brought her own lunch, but ours was delivered from the new cafe.
The blue house at the end of the street is ours.
After the meeting, the decision was clearly ours to make.
These seats are ours, so please do not take them.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mine - possessive pronoun for something belonging to me
  • synonym: ourselves - reflexive form referring to us
  • antonym: theirs - possessive pronoun for something belonging to them
  • antonym: yours - possessive pronoun for something belonging to you
