kept up

adjective
maintained in good condition
Pronunciation: / kɛpt ʌp / (AmE), / kɛpt ʌp / (BrE)
Maintained in good condition; properly cared for and preserved over time.

Examples

A kept up car
A kept up car will last much longer than one that is ignored.
The garden was beautifully kept up throughout the summer months.
We rented a house that was well kept up by its owners.
