patinize

verb
to coat with a patina
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpæt̬.əˌnaɪz /; / ˈpætɪˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈpæt.ɪ.naɪz /; / ˈpætɪˌnaɪz / (BrE)
to coat or treat a surface (especially metal) so that it develops a patina, typically to achieve a desired color or texture
