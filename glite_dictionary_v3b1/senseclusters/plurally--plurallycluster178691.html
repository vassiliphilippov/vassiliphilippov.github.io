plurally

adverb
in plural form
Pronunciation: / ˈplʊrəli / (AmE), / ˈplʊərəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or uses the plural form of a word

Examples

Used plurally in English
In English, some words are used plurally to refer to more than one object.
The term 'scissors' is always used plurally in sentences.
Certain nouns can be used both singularly and plurally depending on the context.

Related Terms

  • antonym: singularly - in a way that relates to or uses the singular form
