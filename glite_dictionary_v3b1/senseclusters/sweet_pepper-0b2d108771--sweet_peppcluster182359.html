sweet pepper

noun
mild, bell-shaped salad pepper
1.1 1.4
Pronunciation: /swiːt ˈpep.ər/ (AmE), /swiːt ˈpep.ə/ (BrE)
a large, mild, thick-walled pepper (usually bell-shaped) eaten as a vegetable, or the plant that produces it
