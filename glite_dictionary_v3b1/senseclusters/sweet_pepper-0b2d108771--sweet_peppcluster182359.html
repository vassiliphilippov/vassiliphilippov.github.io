sweet pepper

noun
mild, bell-shaped salad pepper
1.1
Pronunciation: / swit ˈpɛpɚ / (AmE), / swiːt ˈpɛpə / (BrE)
a large, mild, thick-walled pepper (usually bell-shaped) eaten as a vegetable, or the plant that produces it

Examples

A sweet pepper plant
A sweet pepper plant can produce dozens of bell-shaped fruits in a season.
She added chopped sweet pepper to the salad for extra color and crunch.
The recipe calls for both red and green sweet peppers.
I added diced sweet pepper to the salad for extra color and crunch.
Roasted sweet pepper makes a delicious addition to sandwiches and pasta dishes.
The recipe calls for one green sweet pepper, finely chopped.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bell pepper - a large, mild, bell-shaped variety of sweet pepper
  • antonym: chili pepper - a hot, pungent variety of capsicum
