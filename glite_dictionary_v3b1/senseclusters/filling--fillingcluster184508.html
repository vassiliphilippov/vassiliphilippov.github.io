filling

noun
material used to fill a tooth cavity
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
a material used by a dentist to fill a cavity in a tooth after decay has been removed

Examples

Dental filling
After my dental appointment, I discovered I needed another filling in one of my molars.
She chose a white composite filling so it would blend in with her other teeth.
The dentist said the old filling had started to crack and needed to be replaced.
