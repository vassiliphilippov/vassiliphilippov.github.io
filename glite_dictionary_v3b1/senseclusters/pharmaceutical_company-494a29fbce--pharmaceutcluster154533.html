pharmaceutical company

noun
company producing medicinal drugs
3.4 2.67/m
Pronunciation: / Àåf…ërm…ôÀàsuÀêt…™k…ôl Ààk åmp…ôni / (AmE), / Àåf…ëÀêm…ôtÀàsuÀêt…™k…ôl Ààk åmp…ôni / (BrE)

Examples
