abuse

verb
use something wrongly or excessively
Pronunciation: / əˈbjus / (AmE), / əˈbjuːz / (BrE)
to use something in a wrong, improper, or excessive way

Examples

To abuse power
Doctors warn that young people should never abuse prescription medicines.
He began to abuse alcohol after losing his job.
Some athletes abuse steroids to enhance performance, despite the risks.
If you abuse your privileges, they may be revoked.
Some employees abuse their access to confidential data for personal gain.
The software was never meant to be abused for illegal activities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: misuse - use something in the wrong way or for the wrong purpose
