laggy

adjective
slow or delayed
Pronunciation: / ˈlæɡi / (AmE), / ˈlæɡi / (BrE)
describing a device, connection, or software that is slow to respond or has noticeable delays

Examples

Laggy phone
After the update, my phone became so laggy that it was hard to use.
Gamers often complain when online matches feel laggy due to a slow internet connection.
The video call was laggy, making it difficult to follow the conversation.
My computer felt laggy after I installed the new software update.
The video call was so laggy that we could barely understand each other.
Her phone became laggy when too many apps were running at once.

Related Terms

  • antonym: responsive - reacting quickly and positively
  • synonym: sluggish - moving or responding slowly
