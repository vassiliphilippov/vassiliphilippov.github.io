clam

noun
burrowing marine mollusk with a shell
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /klæm/ (AmE), /klæm/ (BrE)
a marine mollusk with a shell that closes tightly, living buried in sand or mud, especially along the seashore

Examples

A clam shell

The fishermen caught several clams to sell at the market.
We dug in the sand and found a large clam near the shoreline.
