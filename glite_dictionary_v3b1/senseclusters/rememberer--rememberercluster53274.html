rememberer

noun
memory aid
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmɛmbərər / (AmE), / rɪˈmɛmbərə / (BrE)
an object, note, or aid used to help someone remember something

Examples

A rememberer note
He kept a small stone in his pocket as a rememberer to call his mother.
The sticky note on her computer was a helpful rememberer for her daily tasks.
She wore a bracelet as a rememberer of her promise to stay positive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mnemonic - a device or trick to help remember information
  • synonym: reminder - something that helps you remember something else
