overmix

verb
to mix too much
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈmɪks / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈmɪks / (BrE)
to mix ingredients or a substance too much, especially in cooking, so that it negatively affects the result

Examples

The recipe warns not to overmix the ingredients after adding the flour.
