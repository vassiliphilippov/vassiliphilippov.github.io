bob

verb
to make a quick curtsy (respectful gesture)
Pronunciation: / bɑb / (AmE), / bɒb / (BrE)
(of a girl or woman) to make a quick curtsy as a sign of respect, usually by bending the knees.

Examples

To bob a curtsy
At the rehearsal, all the dancers bobbing in unison was a lovely sight.
The young girl bobbed politely before the duchess entered the room.
When she approached the stage, she bobbed gracefully to greet the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curtsy - to lower the body by bending the knees as a polite gesture, especially by women
