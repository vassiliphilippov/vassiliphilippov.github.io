chinoiserie

noun
Chinese-influenced European art style or decoration
6.2 8.7
Pronunciation: / ʃɪˈnwɑːzəri / (AmE), / ʃɪˈnwɑːzəri / (BrE)
an artistic style or decorative object that reflects Chinese motifs and influences, especially as popular in European art of the 18th century

Examples

Chinoiserie porcelain
She collects antique chinoiserie porcelain from eighteenth-century Europe.
The influence of chinoiserie can be seen in the ornate patterns of the furniture.
The living room was decorated with chinoiserie wallpaper featuring intricate landscapes and birds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orientalism - the imitation or depiction of aspects of Eastern cultures in the West, often with exoticism
