staycation

noun
vacation at home
Pronunciation: / steɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / steɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
a vacation spent at home or nearby rather than traveling to another place

Examples

A relaxing staycation
After a busy year, they chose a staycation to unwind without the stress of travel.
Many people enjoyed a staycation during the summer instead of going abroad.
A staycation can be a great way to explore your own city and save money.

Related Terms

  • synonym: holiday - a period of time when you do not have to go to work or school
  • synonym: vacation - a period of time spent away from home or work for rest or travel
