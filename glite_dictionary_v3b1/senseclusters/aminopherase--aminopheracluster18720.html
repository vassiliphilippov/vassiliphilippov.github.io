aminopherase

noun
enzyme catalyzing transamination
Pronunciation: / əˌminoʊfəˈreɪs / (AmE), / əˌmiːnəʊfəˈreɪs / (BrE)
an enzyme in the class of transferases that catalyzes the transfer of an amino group from an amino acid to another molecule (transamination)
