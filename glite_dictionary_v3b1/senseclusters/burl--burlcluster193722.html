burl

noun
lump or bump in yarn or fabric
8.9
Pronunciation: / bɝːl / (AmE), / bɜːl / (BrE)
a small, rounded lump or irregularity in yarn or fabric, either as an imperfection or created intentionally for effect

Examples

A fabric burl
Inspectors rejected the scarf because a large burl marred its surface.
Some fabrics are designed with a burl to add texture and visual interest.
The weaver noticed a burl in the otherwise smooth woolen thread.
