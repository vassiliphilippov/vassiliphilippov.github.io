grippingly

adverb
tightly or firmly
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɪpɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪpɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that involves holding something tightly or firmly with the hands.

Examples

Grippingly hold the rope
She held the umbrella grippingly as the wind threatened to blow it away.
The child clung grippingly to his mother's hand while crossing the busy street.
He grippingly grasped the rope to prevent himself from falling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: firmly - in a strong or secure way
  • antonym: loosely - in a way that is not tight or firm
