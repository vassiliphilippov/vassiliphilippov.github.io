contribution

noun
a submitted article or story for publication
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑntrəˈbjuʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒntrɪˈbjuːʃən / (BrE)
an article, story, or other piece of writing submitted for publication, especially as part of a collection

Examples

Contribution to a magazine
Each contribution in the collection offered a unique perspective on urban life.
Her contribution to the anthology was a moving short story about family.
We accepted several excellent contributions for inclusion in the magazine's spring issue.
