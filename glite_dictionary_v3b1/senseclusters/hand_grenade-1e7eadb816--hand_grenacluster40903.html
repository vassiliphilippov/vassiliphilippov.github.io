hand grenade

noun
small bomb thrown by hand
1.0
Pronunciation: / hænd ɡrəˈneɪd / (AmE), / hænd ɡrəˈneɪd / (BrE)
A small bomb designed to be thrown by hand, consisting of an explosive charge in a metal casing with a triggering mechanism.

Examples

A hand grenade explosion
After the explosion, fragments from the hand grenade scattered across the field.
He was trained to pull the pin and toss a hand grenade safely during his military service.
The soldier threw a hand grenade into the enemy bunker to clear the way.
