reader

noun
person reading lessons in church service
7.4 1.0/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàriÀêd…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàriÀêd…ô / (BrE)
a person who reads aloud during a church service, especially in certain Christian denominations, or someone holding a minor clerical office who has this duty.

Examples

Church reader
He was appointed reader after years of dedicated service to the parish.
In some churches, the reader is responsible for leading the scripture readings.
The reader stood at the lectern and read the gospel to the congregation.
