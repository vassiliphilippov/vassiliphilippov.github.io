loop

verb
move in a circular path, esp. in flight
4.3
Pronunciation: / luːp / (AmE), / luːp / (BrE)
to move in or cause to move in a circular or curved course, especially to perform a complete vertical circle in the air, as an airplane.

Examples

To loop in the sky
As the kite rose higher, it looped gracefully in the afternoon sky.
The pilot skillfully looped the plane in a perfect arc above the crowd.
The roller coaster loops three times before coming to a stop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: circle - to move in a circle around something
