powder store

noun
storehouse for weapons and ammunition
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊdər stɔr / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdə stɔː / (BrE)
A storage place, especially on a warship, where weapons and ammunition are kept.

Examples

The powder store room
A fire near the powder store could have led to a terrible explosion.
Inspectors checked the powder store before the ship set sail.
The sailors were ordered to keep the powder store locked at all times.

  • synonym: magazine - a place for storing ammunition and explosives
