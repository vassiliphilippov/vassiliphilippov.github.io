auditory perception

noun
process of recognizing and interpreting sounds
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːdɪˌtɔri pərˈsɛpʃən / (AmE), / ˈɔːdɪtəri pəˈsɛpʃən / (BrE)
the process of recognizing and interpreting sounds as meaningful information through hearing

Examples

Auditory perception skills
Children with strong auditory perception skills often excel in learning languages.
Damage to certain brain areas can affect a person's auditory perception.
Musicians regularly engage their auditory perception to distinguish notes and rhythms.
