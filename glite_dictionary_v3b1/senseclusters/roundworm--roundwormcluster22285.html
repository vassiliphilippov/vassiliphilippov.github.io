roundworm

noun
type of worm, some are parasites
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊndˌwɜrm / (AmE), / ˈraʊndˌwɜːm / (BrE)
an unsegmented worm with a long, cylindrical body that is pointed at both ends, some of which are parasitic

Examples

Because roundworms can be transmitted through soil, it's important to practice good hygiene when gardening.
Most species of roundworm are harmless and live freely in the environment.
