tank shell

noun
explosive projectile from a tank cannon
Pronunciation: / tæŋk ʃɛl / (AmE), / tæŋk ʃɛl / (BrE)
an explosive projectile fired from the main gun of a tank

Examples

A tank shell explosion
A single tank shell can penetrate several centimeters of armor.
Soldiers took cover as tank shells exploded around their position.
The destroyed vehicle was hit by a tank shell during the battle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artillery shell - explosive projectile fired by larger artillery pieces
  • antonym: bullet - projectile fired from small arms
