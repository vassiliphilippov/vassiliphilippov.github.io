selection

noun
choosing features (machine learning)
4.4
Pronunciation: / səˈlɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / sɪˈlɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
(in machine learning) the process of choosing a subset of input variables (features) from a larger set to improve model performance, reduce overfitting, or simplify the model.

Examples

Feature selection
In our experiments, careful selection of features improved accuracy and reduced overfitting significantly.
Feature-engineering tools automate the selection of relevant variables for faster model training.
A common approach to selection is using regularization or tree-based importance scores to choose features.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feature selection - the process of selecting a subset of relevant features for building a machine-learning model
