cratered

adjective
full of holes
Pronunciation: / ˈkreɪtərd / (AmE), / ˈkreɪtəd / (BrE)
covered with many holes or depressions resembling craters.

Examples

Cratered surface
The abandoned parking lot was cratered after years of neglect and weather damage.
After the meteor shower, the landscape appeared cratered and uneven.
The old battlefield was cratered from countless explosions during the war.
