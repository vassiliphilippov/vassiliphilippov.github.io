Angora goat

noun
breed of goat producing mohair
4.0
Pronunciation: / ænˈɡɔr.ə ɡoʊt / (AmE), / ænˈɡɔː.rə ɡəʊt / (BrE)
A domestic breed of goat known for its long, silky hair, which is used to produce true mohair.

Examples

The Angora goat hair
An Angora goat requires regular shearing to maintain the quality of its hair.
Farmers in South Africa raise Angora goats as a key part of the textile industry.
The Angora goat is highly prized for its soft, luxurious mohair fleece.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mohair - the fiber obtained from Angora goats
