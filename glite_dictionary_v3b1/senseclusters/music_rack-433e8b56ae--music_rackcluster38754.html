music rack

noun
stand for holding sheet music
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuː.zɪk ræk / (AmE), / ˈmjuː.zɪk ræk / (BrE)
a stand or support designed to hold sheets of printed music for musicians while playing an instrument
