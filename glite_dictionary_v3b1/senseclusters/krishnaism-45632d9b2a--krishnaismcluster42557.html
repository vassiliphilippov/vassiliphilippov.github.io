Krishnaism

noun
worship of Krishna as Supreme God in Hinduism
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪʃnəˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈkrɪʃnəˌɪzəm / (BrE)
a religious tradition within Hinduism characterized by the worship of Krishna as the Supreme God, regarded as the 8th avatar of Vishnu

Examples

Krishnaism worship
Krishnaism emphasizes devotion to Krishna as the highest form of worship in Hinduism.
Many followers of Krishnaism participate in festivals such as Janmashtami to celebrate Krishna's birth.
Scholars study Krishnaism to understand the development of devotional movements in India.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Vaishnavism - worship of Vishnu and his avatars in Hinduism
