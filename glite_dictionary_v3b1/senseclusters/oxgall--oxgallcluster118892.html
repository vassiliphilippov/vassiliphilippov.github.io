oxgall

noun
liquid from ox gallbladder
Pronunciation: / ˈɑksˌɡɔl / (AmE), / ˈɒksˌɡɔːl / (BrE)
a liquid obtained from the gallbladder of an ox, used in painting and cleaning to improve the flow of watercolors or remove grease
