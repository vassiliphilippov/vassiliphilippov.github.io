laryngeal

adjective
relating to the larynx
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛrɪnˈdʒiəl / (AmE), / ˌlarɪnˈdʒiːəl / (BrE)
relating to or situated in the larynx (the voice box in the throat)

Examples

Laryngeal tumor
A laryngeal tumor can cause difficulty in breathing and swallowing.
He developed a laryngeal infection that affected his ability to speak.
The doctor explained the importance of laryngeal muscles in producing sound.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pharyngeal - relating to the pharynx
