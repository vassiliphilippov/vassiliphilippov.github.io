curly-coated retriever

noun
English breed of retriever with curly coat
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˌkɝːli ˌkoʊtɪd rɪˈtrivər / (AmE), / ˌkɜːli ˌkəʊtɪd rɪˈtriːvə / (BrE)
a breed of large, strong dog from England with a tightly curled black or liver-colored coat, known for retrieving game from land or water
