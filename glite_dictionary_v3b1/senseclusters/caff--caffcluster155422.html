caff

noun
informal British term for a cafe
Pronunciation: / kæf / (AmE), / kæf / (BrE)
An informal British term for a small, inexpensive restaurant or cafe, often serving simple food and drinks.

Examples

A small caff
Let's grab a bite at the caff down the road for lunch.
Most locals prefer the neighborhood caff to fancy restaurants.
She worked mornings in a busy caff near the train station.

  • synonym: cafe - a small restaurant serving light meals and drinks
