corrugator

noun
muscle that wrinkles skin
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkɔːr.ə.ɡeɪ.tər/ /; / /ˈkɑːr.ə.ɡeɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈkɒr.ə.ɡeɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a muscle that contracts to produce wrinkles, especially in the forehead by drawing the eyebrows together

Examples

Corrugator muscle frown
The corrugator muscle is responsible for creating vertical lines between your eyebrows when you frown.
Botox injections can relax the corrugator to reduce forehead wrinkles.
When you concentrate deeply, your corrugator contracts and pulls your eyebrows together.
