reflection

noun
indication or evidence of something
Pronunciation: / rɪˈflɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / rɪˈflɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
something that shows, expresses, or is evidence of a particular situation, quality, or attitude

Examples

Reflection of kindness
Her generosity is a reflection of her kind nature.
Poor attendance may be a reflection of student dissatisfaction with the course.
The country's low crime rate is a reflection of its strong community values.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expression - something that shows what someone feels or thinks
  • synonym: manifestation - a clear sign or indication of something
