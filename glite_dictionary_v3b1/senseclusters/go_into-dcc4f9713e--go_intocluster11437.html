Synonyms

  • enter
    1.0 70/m
    enter is a more formal verb for coming into places or states, while go into is common in everyday speech.
  • go in
    1.0 100/m
    go in is a casual way to move into a room or area, while go into can also mean entering a state or topic.
  • get into
    2.0 15/m
    get into means moving or joining something actively, while go into is more neutral for simply entering a place or state.
  • get in
    1.0 60/m
    get in is used for entering vehicles or small spaces quickly, while go into covers places, areas, or states more broadly.
  • set foot
    3.2 5/m
