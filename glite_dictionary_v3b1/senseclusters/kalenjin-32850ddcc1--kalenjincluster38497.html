Kalenjin

proper noun
Kenyan ethnic group
9.9
Pronunciation: / kæˈlɛndʒɪn / (AmE), / kæˈlɛndʒɪn / (BrE)
a member of a group of closely related ethnic communities living mainly in Kenya

Examples

A Kalenjin runner
The Kalenjin have a rich tradition of music and storytelling.
Many world-class runners come from the Kalenjin community in Kenya.
She learned about the customs of the Kalenjin during her visit to the Rift Valley.

Related Terms
