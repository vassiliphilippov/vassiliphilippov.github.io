manbot

noun
emotionless man
Pronunciation: / ˈmænˌbɑt / (AmE), / ˈmænˌbɒt / (BrE)
an informal, derogatory term for a man who behaves in a mechanical, emotionless, or unthinking way

Examples

Emotionless manbot
He followed every instruction like a manbot, never questioning or showing any feelings.
Some people called him a manbot because he rarely expressed his own opinions.
At meetings, his manbot behavior made it hard for colleagues to connect with him.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automaton - a person who acts in a mechanical or unemotional way
  • antonym: individualist - a person who acts independently and with emotion
  • synonym: robot - a machine that performs tasks automatically
