universitas

noun

the whole or totality of something; the state of being universal or all-encompassing, especially in a philosophical context

Examples

Universitas of all things
The philosopher discussed the universitas of existence, emphasizing the interconnectedness of all things.
In medieval texts, the term universitas often referred to the collective body or whole community.
He believed in the universitas of moral values, arguing they apply to everyone equally.
