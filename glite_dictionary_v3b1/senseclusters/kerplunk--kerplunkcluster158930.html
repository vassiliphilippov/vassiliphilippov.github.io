kerplunk

noun
heavy splash sound
Pronunciation: / kərˈplʌŋk / (AmE), / kəˈplʌŋk / (BrE)
a sudden, heavy splash or thudding sound made when something falls or hits a surface

Examples

A loud kerplunk
The stone landed in the pond with a loud kerplunk that startled the ducks.
We dropped the watermelon and it hit the ground with a huge kerplunk.
As soon as he let go, the book fell off the shelf with a kerplunk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: splash - the sound of something hitting or falling into liquid
  • synonym: thud - a dull, heavy sound made by an object falling or hitting something
