bondmaid

noun
female slave or servant without wages
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑndˌmeɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɒndˌmeɪd / (BrE)
a female slave or woman who is forced to serve another person without pay

Examples

A bondmaid servant
In ancient times, a bondmaid had no freedom or rights of her own.
She was born a bondmaid but dreamed of a different life.
The lord's household was staffed by several bondmaids and servants.
Bound by law and duty, the bondmaid toiled every day in her master's household.
In the ancient tale, a humble bondmaid rises to become queen.
The lord regarded the bondmaid as little more than property.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bondman - a male slave or serf
  • synonym: bondwoman - a female slave or serf
  • degree lower: servant - a person who performs duties for others, especially in a household
  • synonym: slave - a person who is the legal property of another and is forced to obey them
