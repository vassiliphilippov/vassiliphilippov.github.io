spewer

noun
someone who emits abusive remarks
Pronunciation: / \u02c8spju\u02d0\u0259r / (AmE), / \u02c8spju\u02d0\u0259 / (BrE)
Informal. a person who repeatedly emits angry, abusive, or provocative words or comments, especially online.

Examples

Online spewer of hate
In the comments thread, a persistent spewer posted angry messages every hour.
She ignored the obvious spewer who tried to provoke readers with personal attacks.
The moderator banned the worst spewer after dozens of hateful comments appeared.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insulter - someone who says rude or offensive things to hurt or offend others
  • synonym: troll - a person who posts inflammatory messages online to provoke others
