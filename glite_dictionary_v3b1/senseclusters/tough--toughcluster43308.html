tough

adjective
not gentle or sentimental (person)
2.8
Pronunciation: / tʌf / (AmE), / tʌf / (BrE)
(of a person) not easily moved by emotion or sentimentality; strong and resilient in character, often harsh or unsympathetic.

Examples

Tough character
Despite her tough exterior, she cares deeply about her friends.
The coach has a reputation for being tough but fair with his team.
You have to be pretty tough to survive in that business.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gentle - kind and mild in temperament
  • synonym: hard - showing no sympathy or affection; emotionally strong
