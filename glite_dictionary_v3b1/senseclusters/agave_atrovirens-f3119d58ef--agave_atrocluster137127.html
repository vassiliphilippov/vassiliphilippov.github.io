Agave atrovirens

noun
Mexican agave plant used for pulque and mezcal
Pronunciation: / əˈɡɑː.vi ˌætrəʊˈvaɪrənz / (AmE), / əˈɡɑː.vi ætrəʊˈvɪərɛnz / (BrE)
A species of agave native to Mexico, used especially to make pulque and as a source of the liquor mezcal.

Examples

Agave atrovirens plant
Mezcal made from Agave atrovirens is prized for its distinctive flavor.
The large leaves of Agave atrovirens are often harvested for their valuable sap.
Traditional Mexican farmers cultivated Agave atrovirens to produce pulque for ceremonial occasions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mezcal - a distilled alcoholic beverage made from agave
  • synonym: pulque - a traditional Mexican alcoholic beverage made from fermented agave sap
