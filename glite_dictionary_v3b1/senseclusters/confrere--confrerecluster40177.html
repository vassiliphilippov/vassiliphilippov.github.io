confrere

noun
colleague or fellow member
Pronunciation: / kɑnˈfrɛr / (AmE), / kɒnˈfrɛə / (BrE)
a person who is a fellow member of a profession, organization, or class; a colleague or peer

Examples

Confreres in art
Her confreres in the art world praised her innovative techniques.
She met with her confreres at the annual academic conference.
The lawyer enjoyed discussing recent legal cases with his confrere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: colleague - a person with whom one works, especially in a profession or business
  • synonym: peer - a person who is equal to another in abilities, qualifications, age, background, and social status
