calculation

noun
careful or strategic planning
Pronunciation: / ˌkælkjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkælkjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
careful planning or consideration of actions, especially with a specific or strategic intent

Examples

Careful calculation of plans
Her every move was made with cold calculation to achieve her goals.
The success of the campaign was due more to careful calculation than to luck.
There was a sense of calculation behind his generous offer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deliberation - the process of carefully considering or discussing something
  • antonym: impulsiveness - acting without forethought or careful planning
