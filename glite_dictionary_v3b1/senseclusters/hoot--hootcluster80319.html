hoot

verb
make the call of an owl
4.2
Pronunciation: / huːt / (AmE), / huːt / (BrE)
(of an owl) to make its characteristic low, resonant call.

Examples

Owls hoot at night
Every night, the barn owl hoots from its perch in the old tree.
The forest was silent except for the occasional hooting of an owl.
We could hear an owl hoot in the distance as night fell.
