Examples

Synonyms

  • eyehole
    7.4 0.04/m
    eyehole is a simple round opening in cloth or leather for a cord or hook, while eyelet is often reinforced.
  • grommet
    8.1 0.45/m
    grommet is a separate metal or plastic ring placed in a hole to protect it, while eyelet can be stitched or ringed.
  • grummet
    9.9 0.00/m
    grummet is an older word for a small metal ring used to run ropes, while eyelet names a reinforced hole in fabric.
  • cringle
    9.9 0.00/m
