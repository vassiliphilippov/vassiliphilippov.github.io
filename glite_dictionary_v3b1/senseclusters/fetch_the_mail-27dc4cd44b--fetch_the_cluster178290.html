fetch the mail

verb
go and collect letters and packages
2.3
Pronunciation: / fɛtʃ ðə meɪl / (AmE), / fɛtʃ ðə meɪl / (BrE)
to go and collect letters and packages that have been delivered, especially from a mailbox or post office

Examples

To fetch the mail from the box

I always fetch the mail as soon as the mail carrier leaves.
She forgot to fetch the mail yesterday, so the box was full this morning.
