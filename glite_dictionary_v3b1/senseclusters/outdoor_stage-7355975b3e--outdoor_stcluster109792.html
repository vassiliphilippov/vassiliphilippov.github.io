outdoor stage

noun
platform for performances outdoors
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌdɔɚ steɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌdɔː steɪdʒ / (BrE)
a raised platform built outside for performances, speeches, or other public events

Examples

An outdoor stage performance
After the rain stopped, the actors performed on the outdoor stage to a large audience.
Temporary lights were set up to illuminate the outdoor stage during the festival.
The concert will be held on an outdoor stage in the park this Saturday evening.
