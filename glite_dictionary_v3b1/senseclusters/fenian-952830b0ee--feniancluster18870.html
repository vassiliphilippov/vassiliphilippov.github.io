Fenian

adjective
Irish republican movement
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːniən / (AmE), / ˈfiːniən / (BrE)
relating to the Fenian movement, its members, or their ideas and activities in 19th–early 20th century Irish republicanism

Examples

Fenian movement
The Fenian uprising of 1867 was a significant event in Irish history.
Many songs from the era celebrate Fenian heroes and their struggle for independence.
The government closely monitored Fenian activities throughout the late nineteenth century.

Related Terms

  • synonym: republican - supporting a republic or republicanism
  • antonym: unionist - supporting the political union between Ireland and Great Britain
