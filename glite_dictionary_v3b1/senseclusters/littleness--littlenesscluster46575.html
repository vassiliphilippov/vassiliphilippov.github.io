littleness

noun
pettiness; lack of generosity
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪtnəs / (AmE), / ˈlɪt.lɪnəs / (BrE)
pettiness or lack of generosity, especially in trivial matters

Examples

Petty littleness
His littleness became apparent when he quibbled over a few cents.
She showed remarkable patience in the face of such littleness from her colleagues.
Their constant littleness about minor details made negotiations unpleasant.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generosity - the quality of being kind and generous
  • synonym: pettiness - concern with trivial or unimportant matters, especially in a mean-spirited way
