noncompetitively

adverb
in a noncompetitive manner
Pronunciation: / nɑn.kəmˈpɛtə.tɪv.li / (AmE), / nɒn.kəmˈpɛt.ɪ.tɪv.li / (BrE)
In a way that does not involve competition or rivalry; without competing against others.

Examples

To noncompetitively join sports
She prefers to participate noncompetitively in sports, focusing on enjoyment rather than winning.
The grant was distributed noncompetitively, so no one had to apply against others.
The scholarships will be awarded noncompetitively to all qualifying applicants.

Related Terms

  • antonym: competitively - in a competitive manner; with competition
  • synonym: noncompetitive - not competitive; lacking competition or rivalry
