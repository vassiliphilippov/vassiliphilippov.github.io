leveling

noun
making things equal or uniform
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛvəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɛv(ə)lɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making things equal or uniform in standard, amount, or level

Examples

Leveling incomes
Leveling efforts aim to reduce income inequality in the country.
Some critics argue that excessive leveling can discourage individual achievement.
The new policy is designed for the leveling of opportunities among all students.

Related Terms

  • synonym: equalization - the process of making things equal
  • synonym: standardization - the process of making things conform to a standard
