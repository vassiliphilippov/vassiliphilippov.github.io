Synonyms

  • imperil
    7.8 1.01/m
    imperil is a formal verb meaning to put someone into serious danger, while endanger is more general and common.
  • jeopardize
    6.3 8.37/m
    jeopardize means to put plans or a person's position at risk, while endanger focuses on physical harm or safety.
  • menace
    4.6 1.68/m
    menace means to threaten or pose a clear, active danger, while endanger means to expose someone to risk of harm.
