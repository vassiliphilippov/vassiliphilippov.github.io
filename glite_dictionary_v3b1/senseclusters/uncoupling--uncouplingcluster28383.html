uncoupling

noun
act of separating
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkʌplɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈkʌplɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of separating things that were previously joined or connected

Examples

Uncoupling train cars
The uncoupling of the train cars was done quickly to avoid delays.
Scientists studied the uncoupling of the two chemical processes in the experiment.
After years of partnership, the uncoupling of the companies surprised many in the industry.

Related Terms

  • antonym: connection - the act of joining or linking things together
  • synonym: separation - the act of moving things apart or dividing them
