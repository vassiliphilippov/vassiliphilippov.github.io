backplate

noun
armor plate protecting the back
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌpleɪt / (AmE), / ˈbækˌpleɪt / (BrE)
A piece of plate armor designed to protect the back, typically part of a cuirass.

Examples

Armor backplate
He carefully attached the backplate to complete his suit of armor.
Replicas of medieval backplates are on display in the museum.
The knight's backplate was dented after the fierce battle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breastplate - piece of plate armor for the front of the torso
