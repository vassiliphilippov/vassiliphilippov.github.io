Datura

noun
genus of poisonous, trumpet-flowered plants
9.6
Pronunciation: / dəˈtʊrə /; / dəˈtʊr.ə / (AmE), / dəˈtjʊərə /; / dəˈtjʊə.rə / (BrE)
a genus of plants with large, trumpet-shaped flowers, known as thorn apples; many species are poisonous and some are used as hallucinogens

Examples

Datura plant

The Datura plant's trumpet-shaped flowers bloom at night and can be quite striking.
