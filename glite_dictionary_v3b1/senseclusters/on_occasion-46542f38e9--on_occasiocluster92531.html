on occasion

adverb
sometimes; from time to time
4.9
Pronunciation: / ɑn əˈkeɪʒən / (AmE), / ɒn əˈkeɪʒən / (BrE)
sometimes; from time to time, but not often or regularly.

Examples

Go on occasion
I go to the theater on occasion, when there's a show that interests me.
She likes to travel on occasion, but prefers staying at home most of the time.
We meet on occasion to catch up and share stories about our lives.
