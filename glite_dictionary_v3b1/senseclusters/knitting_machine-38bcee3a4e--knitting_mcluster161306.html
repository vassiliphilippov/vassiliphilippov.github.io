knitting machine

noun
machine for making knitted fabrics
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪtɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈnɪtɪŋ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine used to produce knitted fabrics automatically by interlocking loops of yarn

Examples

Modern knitting machines
Modern knitting machines can produce intricate designs in a matter of minutes.
She learned how to operate the knitting machine during her training.
The factory invested in a new knitting machine to increase sweater production.
