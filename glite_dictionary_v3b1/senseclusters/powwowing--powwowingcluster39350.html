powwowing

noun
folk healing practice
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊˌwaʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊˌwaʊɪŋ / (BrE)
a traditional folk healing practice involving rituals and remedies, especially among Pennsylvania Dutch communities

Examples

Traditional powwowing rituals
Many families in rural Pennsylvania still practice powwowing for minor illnesses and protection.
She learned the art of powwowing from her grandmother, who was well known in the community.
Books about powwowing describe rituals, prayers, and herbal remedies passed down through generations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: folk medicine - traditional healing practices based on cultural beliefs
  • synonym: shamanism - spiritual healing practices involving rituals
