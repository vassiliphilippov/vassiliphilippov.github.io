mucker

noun
laborer or close friend
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌkər / (AmE), / ˈmʌkə / (BrE)
a person who does dirty or menial work, or informally, a close friend or companion

Examples

Old mucker friend
He worked as a mucker in the mines for many years.
After school, I always hang out with my best mucker at the park.
The muckers cleaned up the stables before the horses arrived.
