liquidator

noun
person who kills, often systematically
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪkwɪˈdeɪtər / (AmE), / ˌlɪkwɪˈdeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who kills another, especially in a premeditated or systematic manner, often for political or criminal reasons

Examples

Political liquidator
He was known as the chief liquidator of the criminal syndicate's enemies.
The investigation uncovered evidence linking the liquidator to several high-profile assassinations.
The regime sent a notorious liquidator to eliminate political opponents abroad.
