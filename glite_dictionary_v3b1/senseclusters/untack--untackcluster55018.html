untack

verb
to remove horse gear
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtæk / (AmE), / ʌnˈtæk / (BrE)
to remove the saddle, bridle, and other riding equipment from a horse after riding

Examples

To untack a horse
After the ride, she carefully untacked her horse and brushed its coat.
It is important to untack the horse before letting it rest in the stable.
He learned how to untack quickly after his first riding lesson.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dismount - to get off a horse or bicycle
  • antonym: tack - to put riding equipment on a horse
