polony

noun
a type of seasoned sausage
Pronunciation: / pəˈloʊni / (AmE), / pəˈləʊni / (BrE)
A type of seasoned sausage made from finely ground meat, similar to or another name for Bologna sausage.

Examples

A slice of polony
At the picnic, there were platters of polony and cold cuts on the table.
Children often enjoy polony in their school lunchbox sandwiches.
He made a sandwich with slices of polony and cheese.
