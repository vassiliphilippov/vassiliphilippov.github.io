tender groin

noun
painful or sensitive groin area
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛndɚ ˈɡrɔɪn / (AmE), / ˈtɛndə ˈɡrɔɪn / (BrE)
a painful or sensitive area in the groin, often indicating injury or inflammation

Examples

A tender groin area
A tender groin can be a sign of a muscle strain or hernia.

The player missed several games because of a tender groin after training.
