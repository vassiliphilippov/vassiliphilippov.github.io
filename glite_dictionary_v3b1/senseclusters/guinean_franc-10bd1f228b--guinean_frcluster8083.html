Guinean franc

noun
currency unit of Guinea
Pronunciation: / ˌɡɪniən ˈfræŋk / (AmE), / ˌɡɪniːən ˈfræŋk / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Guinea, equal to 100 centimes

Examples

A Guinean franc note
He withdrew cash from the ATM in the form of Guinean francs.
The price was listed as 500,000 Guinean francs for the vehicle.
Travelers should exchange dollars for Guinean francs upon arrival in Guinea.
