microwave radar

noun
radar using microwave pulses for detection
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌweɪv ˈreɪˌdɑr / (AmE), / ˈmaɪ.krəʊˌweɪv ˈreɪ.dɑː / (BrE)
a radar system that uses pulses of microwave radiation to detect and determine the distance and location of objects

Examples

Microwave radar system
Air traffic controllers use microwave radar to track planes even in poor visibility.
Modern weather stations often rely on microwave radar to monitor approaching storms.
The ship was equipped with advanced microwave radar for navigation in foggy conditions.

  • synonym: radar - system for detecting objects by using radio waves
