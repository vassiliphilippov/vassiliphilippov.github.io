untreatable

adjective
cannot be cured
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtriːtəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈtriːtəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to cure or heal with available treatments

Examples

Untreatable disease
The doctors explained that the disease was untreatable with current medical technology.
Some rare conditions remain untreatable despite years of research.
He was devastated to learn his illness was considered untreatable by specialists.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incurable - impossible to cure
  • antonym: treatable - able to be cured or managed with treatment
