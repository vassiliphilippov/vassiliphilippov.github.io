subpolar

noun
region near polar areas
7.4
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈpoʊlər / (AmE), / sʌbˈpəʊlə / (BrE)
a region just outside the polar areas, typically marked by cold climates and transitional ecosystems between polar and temperate zones

Examples

The subpolar region
Many unique animal species have adapted to life in the subpolar.
The subpolar is known for its long, harsh winters and short summers.
Scientists study climate change effects in the subpolar to understand global patterns.
