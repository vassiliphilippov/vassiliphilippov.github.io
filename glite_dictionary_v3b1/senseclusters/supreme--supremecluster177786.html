supreme

adjective
final or ultimate stage
1.0
Pronunciation: / səˈprim / (AmE), / suːˈpriːm / (BrE)
being the last or final, especially in someone's life or progress; ultimate or final in a series or process.

Examples

Supreme sacrifice
At the end of life, some people face the supreme challenge of saying goodbye.
He made the supreme sacrifice for his country during the war.
Her decision was the supreme act of courage in a long struggle for justice.
