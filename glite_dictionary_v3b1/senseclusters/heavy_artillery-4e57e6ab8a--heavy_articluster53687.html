heavy artillery

noun
large-caliber military guns
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛvi ɑɹˈtɪlɚi / (AmE), / ˈhɛvi ɑːˈtɪləri / (BrE)
large guns with a high caliber, used in warfare for bombardment and destruction of targets at long range

Examples

Heavy artillery guns
Enemy tanks were destroyed by a barrage of heavy artillery during the night attack.
The general called for heavy artillery to support the infantry’s advance across the battlefield.
The sound of heavy artillery echoed through the mountains, shaking the nearby village.
