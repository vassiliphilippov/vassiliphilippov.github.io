anisometropic

adjective
having different refractive power in each eye
5.7 9.6
Pronunciation: /æn.ɪ.sə.mə.ˈtrɑː.pɪk/ (AmE), /æn.ɪ.sə.mə.ˈtrɒp.ɪk/ (BrE)
relating to or affected by a difference in the refractive power of the two eyes

Examples

Children who are anisometropic may require glasses to prevent amblyopia from developing.

The patient was diagnosed as anisometropic after the eye examination revealed significant differences between her eyes.
