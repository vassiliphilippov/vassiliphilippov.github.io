filariform

adjective
thread-like shape
Pronunciation: / fɪˈlɛrɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / fɪˈlærɪfɔːm / (BrE)
shaped like a thread or filament, especially in biological contexts

Examples

Filariform shape
The larvae develop a filariform appearance as they mature in the host.
Under the microscope, the fungus displayed long, filariform hyphae.
The doctor explained that the worm enters the body in its filariform stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: filamentous - composed of or resembling filaments
  • synonym: threadlike - resembling a thread in form
