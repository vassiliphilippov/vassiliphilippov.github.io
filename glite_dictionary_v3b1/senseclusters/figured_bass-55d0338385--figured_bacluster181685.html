Synonyms

  • thorough bass
    9.9 0.00/m
    thorough bass is the older, traditional name for the same written bass with figures in Baroque music, while figured bass is the common modern term.
  • continuo
    9.9 0.04/m
    continuo is the played bass part realized by keyboard and cello using figures to guide harmony, while figured bass names the written notation with numbers.
  • basso continuo
    9.9 0.02/m
    basso continuo is the ensemble or practice that realizes the bass and harmonies in performance, while figured bass is the written numbered notation.
