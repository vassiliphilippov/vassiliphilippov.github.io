pang of nostalgia

noun
sudden longing for the past
Pronunciation: / pæŋ əv nəˈstældʒə / (AmE), / pæŋ əv nɒˈstaldʒə / (BrE)
a sudden sharp feeling of longing for the past

Examples

A pang of nostalgia
As she walked through her old neighborhood, a sudden pang of nostalgia brought back childhood memories.
Every time she hears that song, she feels a pang of nostalgia for the summers spent at the lake.
Watching the vintage film filled him with a pang of nostalgia for his university days.

  • synonym: pain - a sudden sharp discomfort or distress
