brent

noun
species of small migratory goose
8.4
Pronunciation: / brɛnt / (AmE), / brɛnt / (BrE)
a small dark goose that breeds in the Arctic and migrates south in winter, also known as the brent goose.

Examples

A brent goose
Every autumn, thousands of brent arrive from the Arctic to winter in southern estuaries.
The distinctive call of the brent can often be heard at dawn in coastal marshes.
We watched a flock of brent feeding along the shoreline during their migration.
