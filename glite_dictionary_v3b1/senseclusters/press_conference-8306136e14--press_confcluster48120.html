press conference

noun
meeting for journalists to ask questions
3.1 15/m
Pronunciation: / pr…õs Ààk…ëÀên.f…ö.…ôns / (AmE), / pr…õs Ààk…ín.f…ôr.…ôns / (BrE)
an official meeting where journalists are invited to ask questions and receive information from someone, such as a politician or celebrity

Examples

A press conference meeting
After winning the championship, the coach answered questions at a press conference.
Reporters packed the room for the celebrity's first press conference in years.
The president held a press conference to announce the changes in policy.
After the match, the coach answered questions at the press conference.
The CEO announced the new policy during a press conference broadcast live on television.
The government will hold a press conference to address the latest economic figures.

Synonyms

  • news conference
    3.2 8.50/m
    news conference often shows an organization giving official statements, while press conference highlights journalists asking questions and receiving information.

Related Terms

  • synonym: briefing - a meeting providing information or instructions, especially to reporters
  • synonym: interview - a conversation where questions are asked to obtain information
  • synonym: news conference - a meeting to give information to reporters
