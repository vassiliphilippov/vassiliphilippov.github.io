ill-timed

adjective
done at a bad or unsuitable time
Pronunciation: / ɪlˈtaɪmd / (AmE), / ɪlˈtaɪmd / (BrE)
Happening or done at an unsuitable or inconvenient time.

Examples

An ill-timed joke
He made an ill-timed joke just as the boss entered the room.
Her ill-timed comment interrupted the important discussion.
The party was canceled due to ill-timed rainstorms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: untimely - done at an inappropriate or premature time
  • antonym: well-timed - done at a suitable or appropriate time
