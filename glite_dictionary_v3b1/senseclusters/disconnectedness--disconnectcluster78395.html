disconnectedness

noun
state of being disconnected
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪskəˈnɛktɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌdɪskəˈnɛktɪdnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being disconnected or lacking connection

Examples

Feeling disconnectedness from others
After moving to a new city, she felt a strong sense of disconnectedness from her old friends.
Some people believe that technology increases our disconnectedness from the natural world.
The disconnectedness between management and staff led to frequent misunderstandings in the company.
