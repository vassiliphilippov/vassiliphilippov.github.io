Stymphalian

adjective
of Stymphalus or its birds
Pronunciation: / stɪmˈfeɪliən / (AmE), / stɪmˈfeɪliən / (BrE)
relating to Stymphalus, a region in ancient Greece, or to the legendary Stymphalian birds of Greek mythology

Examples

Stymphalian birds legend
The legend tells of the Stymphalian birds with deadly bronze beaks and feathers.
He studied the ancient myths about the Stymphalian lake and its monstrous creatures.
The Stymphalian challenge was one of the famous labors of Hercules.
