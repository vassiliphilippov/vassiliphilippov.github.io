cross the picket line

verb
work during a strike; defy a strike
8.1 5.8
Pronunciation: /krɔːs ðə ˈpɪk.ɪt laɪn/ (AmE), /krɒs ðə ˈpɪk.ɪt laɪn/ (BrE)
to go to work or enter a workplace during a strike, especially in defiance of a group of striking workers

Examples

Management offered bonuses to those willing to cross the picket line during the strike.
Some workers refused to cross the picket line out of solidarity with the strikers.
