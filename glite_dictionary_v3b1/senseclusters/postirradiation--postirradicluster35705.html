postirradiation

adjective
after radiation exposure
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstɪˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstɪˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
occurring or existing after exposure to radiation, especially as a result of medical treatment

Examples

Postirradiation effects
The doctor monitored the patient's postirradiation recovery closely for any complications.
Some postirradiation effects may not appear until months after the initial treatment.
Researchers are studying ways to reduce postirradiation tissue damage in cancer patients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: postoperative - occurring after a surgical operation
  • antonym: preradiation - occurring before exposure to radiation
