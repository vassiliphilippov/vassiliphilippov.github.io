unconstrain

verb
to remove restrictions
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈstreɪn / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈstreɪn / (BrE)
to remove constraints or restrictions from something; to free from limits.

Examples

To unconstrain limits
The new software update is unconstraining users by allowing more customization options.
By unconstraining the rules, the manager encouraged creativity among the team members.
The government hopes to unconstrain the market to boost economic growth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: liberate - to set free from restrictions or control
  • antonym: restrict - to limit the actions or movement of someone or something
