white potato vine

noun
plant with edible tubers and poisonous vines

Pronunciation: /waɪt pə.ˈteɪ.toʊ vaɪn/ /; / /hwaɪt pə.ˈteɪ.toʊ vaɪn/ (AmE), /waɪt pə.ˈteɪ.təʊ vaɪn/ /; / /hwaɪt pə.ˈteɪ.təʊ vaɪn/ (BrE)
