Pretorian

adjective
corrupt or politically venal, like the Praetorian Guard
5.8
Pronunciation: / priˈtɔːriən / (AmE), / prɪˈtɔːrɪən / (BrE)
Characteristic of or resembling the corrupt or politically venal nature of the Praetorian Guard; marked by corruption or political manipulation.

Examples

A Pretorian culture
A Pretorian culture had taken root in the organization, rewarding flattery and punishing dissent.
Critics accused the cabinet of adopting Pretorian practices to maintain their grip on power.
The agency developed a Pretorian attitude, putting ambition and politics above justice.
