vodun

noun
Vodun spirit
9.9
Pronunciation: / voʊˈduːn / (AmE), / vəˈduːn / (BrE)
a spirit or supernatural being worshipped or honored in the Vodun religion, especially in West Africa and the Caribbean

Examples

A powerful vodun

During the ceremony, offerings were made to the vodun believed to control the harvest.
Stories about the vodun are passed down through generations in West African communities.
