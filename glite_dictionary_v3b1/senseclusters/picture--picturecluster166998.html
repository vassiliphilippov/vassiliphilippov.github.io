picture

noun
a mental image or impression
1.5 2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪktʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃə / (BrE)
a clear or vivid mental image or a graphic verbal description created in the mind

Examples

A mental picture
Her description gave me a vivid picture of life in the 19th century.
She had a clear picture in her mind of the house where she grew up.
Try to form a mental picture of your ideal holiday destination.
He gave us a bleak picture of the country's future.
Her words created a fascinating picture in the minds of the audience.
The author paints a picture of rural life that is both realistic and moving.
