pay-station

noun
self-service payment kiosk
3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpeɪ.steɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ˈpeɪ.steɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
A self-service machine or kiosk that accepts payments (coins, cards, or contactless) for parking, transit fares, tickets, or similar services.

Examples

Parking pay-station
The driver paid at the pay-station in the lot to park for two hours.
City workers installed a new pay-station near the bus stop for contactless fare payments.
She forgot coins, so she used the smartphone app instead of the pay-station at the gate.
