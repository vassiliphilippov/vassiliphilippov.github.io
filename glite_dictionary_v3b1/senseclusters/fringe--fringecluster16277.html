fringe

verb
decorate the edge with a fringe
9.0
Pronunciation: / frɪndʒ / (AmE), / frɪndʒ / (BrE)
to decorate the edge of something with a fringe (an ornamental border of threads or material)

Examples

To fringe a curtain
She decided to fringe the edge of the curtain for a decorative touch.
The craftsperson carefully fringed the tablecloth with bright threads.
They are learning how to fringe scarves as part of the textile workshop.
A golden border fringed her ceremonial robe, giving it a regal appearance.
Bright flowers fringed the walkway leading up to the house.
Delicate lace fringed the edge of the handkerchief.
