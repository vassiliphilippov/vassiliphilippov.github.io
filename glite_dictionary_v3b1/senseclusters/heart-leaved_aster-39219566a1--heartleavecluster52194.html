heart-leaved aster

noun
perennial woodland aster of eastern North America
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrt ˌlivd ˈæstər / (AmE), / ˈhɑːt ˌliːvd ˈastə / (BrE)
A perennial woodland aster of eastern North America, recognized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of pale flowers.
