violation

noun
entry onto property without permission
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌvaɪ.əˈleɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌvaɪ.əˈleɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
The act of entering another person's property without right or permission; an instance of trespassing.

Examples

Violation of property
Entering the premises without the owner's consent constitutes a violation of property rights.
Police were called to investigate a suspected violation of the restricted area.
The security cameras caught footage of the violation last night when someone entered the yard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trespass - entering someone's land or property without permission
