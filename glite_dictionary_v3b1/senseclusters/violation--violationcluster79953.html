The act of entering another person's property without right or permission; an instance of trespassing.

Examples

Violation of property
Entering the premises without the owner's consent constitutes a violation of property rights.
Police were called to investigate a suspected violation of the restricted area.
The security cameras caught footage of the violation last night when someone entered the yard.

Synonyms

  • trespass
    4.7 1.19/m
    trespass is the legal act of entering someone's property without permission, while violation names that unlawful entry.
  • encroachment
    6.9 1.07/m
    encroachment is gradually taking someone else's land or rights over time, while violation describes a single act of trespassing.
  • intrusion
    6.6 6.48/m
    intrusion is entering a place or someone's privacy in an unwelcome way, while violation specifically means entering property without permission.
