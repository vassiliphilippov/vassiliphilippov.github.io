keep abreast

verb
remain up to date; stay informed
5.0
Pronunciation: / kip əˈbrɛst / (AmE), / kiːp əˈbrɛst / (BrE)
to remain up to date with the latest news, information, or developments in a particular area.

Examples

To keep abreast of news
It is important to keep abreast of changes in technology to remain competitive.
She subscribes to several journals to keep abreast of developments in her field.
They kept abreast of the news throughout the crisis.
