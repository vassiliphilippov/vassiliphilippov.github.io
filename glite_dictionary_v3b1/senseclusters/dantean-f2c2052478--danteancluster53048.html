Dantean

adjective
relating to Dante Alighieri or his works
9.4
Pronunciation: / dɑnˈteɪ.ən / (AmE), / danˈtiː.ən / (BrE)
relating to Dante Alighieri or his writings, especially The Divine Comedy

Examples

Dantean style
Her paintings explore themes of suffering and redemption in a Dantean manner.
Scholars often debate the Dantean influences present in modern literature.
The poem had a distinctly Dantean vision of the afterlife, full of vivid and elaborate imagery.
