troop transport

noun
vehicle for carrying soldiers
5.1
Pronunciation: / truːp ˈtrænspɔrt / (AmE), / truːp ˈtrænspɔːt / (BrE)
a vehicle, by land, sea, or air, designed to carry soldiers from one place to another, especially in military operations

Examples

A troop transport vehicle
During the war, the navy escorted a troop transport across dangerous waters.
The army loaded their equipment onto the troop transport before leaving for the base.
They watched as a troop transport helicopter landed in the clearing.

  • synonym: transport - a vehicle used for carrying people or goods
