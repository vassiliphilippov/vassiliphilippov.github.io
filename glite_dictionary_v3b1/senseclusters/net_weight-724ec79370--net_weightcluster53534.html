net weight

noun
product's weight excluding packaging
1.0
Pronunciation: / nɛt weɪt / (AmE), / nɛt weɪt / (BrE)
the weight of a product without including the weight of its packaging

Examples

The net weight label
Food labels often display the net weight in both ounces and grams.
Please make sure to subtract the packaging to calculate the net weight.
The net weight of the cereal is listed as 500 grams on the box.
