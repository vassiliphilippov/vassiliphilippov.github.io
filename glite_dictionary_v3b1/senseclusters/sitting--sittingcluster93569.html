sitting

adjective
in an upright position on a seat
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person) positioned with the torso upright and the buttocks resting on a surface, typically with the legs bent at the knees.

Examples

Sitting position
After hours of sitting at his desk, he needed to stretch his legs.
She found her grandmother sitting on the porch, watching the sunset.
The children remained sitting quietly while the teacher spoke.
