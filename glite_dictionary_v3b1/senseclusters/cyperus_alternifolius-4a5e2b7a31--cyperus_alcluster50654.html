Cyperus alternifolius

noun
African ornamental water plant with umbrellalike leaves
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪˈpɪrəs ˌɔːlˌtɜrnəˈfoʊli.əs / (AmE), / ˌsɪpərəs ˌɔːltəˈnɪfəʊliˌʌs / (BrE)
an African sedge widely grown as an ornamental water plant, notable for its umbrellalike cluster of slender, grasslike leaves at the stem tips
