turnoff

noun
road leaving a main road
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝːnˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnˌɒf / (BrE)
a road or path that leaves the main road to go in another direction

Examples

Highway turnoff
Take the next turnoff to get to the lake.
There's a small turnoff just past the farmhouse where you can park.
We almost missed the turnoff in the thick fog last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exit - a road leading off a main road, especially from a highway
