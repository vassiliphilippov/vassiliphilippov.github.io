unusable

adjective
not fit or able to be used
2.3 1.12/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàjuÀêz…ôb…ôl / (AmE), /  ånÀàjuÀêz…ôbl / (BrE)
not fit or suitable for use; incapable of being used.

Examples

An unusable file
Because the file was corrupted, it became unusable for our project.
Much of the storm-damaged furniture was deemed unusable by the movers.
The old printer was completely unusable after the ink spilled inside.

Synonyms

  • unserviceable
    7.4 0.04/m
    unserviceable describes equipment or items that are worn or broken and withdrawn from use, while unusable simply means cannot be used.
  • inoperable
    7.1 0.24/m
    inoperable usually means a machine or system cannot function as intended, while unusable is a broader term for something not fit to use.
  • nonfunctional
    4.4 0.18/m
    nonfunctional describes something that does not work or perform its job, while unusable means it cannot be used at all.
