Chalcididae

noun
a family of tiny parasitic wasps
9.9
Pronunciation: / kælˈsɪdɪˌdi / (AmE), / kalˈsɪdɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of tiny parasitic wasps, commonly known as chalcid flies, many of which are important in biological control

Examples

Chalcididae family wasps
Many species in the Chalcididae family are used as natural enemies of agricultural pests.
Researchers discovered a new member of the Chalcididae in the tropical rainforest.
The Chalcididae are known for their metallic coloration and small size.
