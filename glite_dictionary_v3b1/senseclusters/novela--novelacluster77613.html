novela

noun
Latin American TV drama
5.0
Pronunciation: / noʊˈvɛlə / (AmE), / nəʊˈvɛlə / (BrE)
a long, dramatic fictional story, especially a serialized television drama popular in Latin America

Examples

A novela episode
Every evening, her family gathered to watch their favorite novela together.
The new novela quickly became a sensation across Latin America.
He learned Spanish by following the dramatic plotlines of a popular novela.

Related Terms

  • antonym: miniseries - a short television series with a set number of episodes
  • synonym: soap opera - a serialized television drama focusing on personal relationships
