Muhammadanism

noun
outdated term for Islam
Pronunciation: / məˌhæməˈdænɪzəm / (AmE), / mʊˌhæməˈdænɪzəm / (BrE)
an outdated and often offensive term for the religion of Islam, referring to the faith founded upon the teachings of Muhammad as described in the Koran

Examples

Muhammadanism religion
In 19th-century literature, the term Muhammadanism was commonly used to refer to the religion of Islam.
Modern scholars recommend using 'Islam' instead of Muhammadanism when describing the faith.
The use of the word Muhammadanism is now widely regarded as inaccurate and offensive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Islam - the religion based on the teachings of the prophet Muhammad
