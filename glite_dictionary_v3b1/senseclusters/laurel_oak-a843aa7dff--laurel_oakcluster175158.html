laurel oak

noun
a semi-evergreen oak tree of the southeastern US
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔrəl oʊk / (AmE), / ˈlɔːrəl əʊk /; / ˈlɒrəl əʊk / (BrE)
a type of oak tree native to the United States, with shiny leaves like those of the laurel
