inhaler

noun
device for inhaling medicated vapor
1.0 2.7
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈheɪ.lər/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈheɪ.lə/ (BrE)
a device that releases a medicated vapor for inhalation, often used to relieve nasal congestion or treat respiratory conditions
