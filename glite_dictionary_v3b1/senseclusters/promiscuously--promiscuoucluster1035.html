promiscuously

adverb
in a promiscuous or indiscriminate manner
Pronunciation: / prəˈmɪskjuəsli / (AmE), / prəˈmɪskjuːəsli /; / prəˈmɪskjʊəsli / (BrE)
in a way that is unselective, disordered, or involves having casual sexual relationships with many different partners

Examples

Behave promiscuously at parties
He was rumored to behave promiscuously at every social gathering.
It was frowned upon in her community to date promiscuously without serious intentions.
The novel describes a group of people living promiscuously in a chaotic flat.
He made friends promiscuously, never caring about their backgrounds or interests.
She sampled cuisines promiscuously, eager to try a bit of everything at the festival.
The books were stacked promiscuously on the shelves, with no apparent order.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indiscriminately - in a random or haphazard way, without careful choice
  • synonym: promiscuous - having or involving many sexual partners or relationships
