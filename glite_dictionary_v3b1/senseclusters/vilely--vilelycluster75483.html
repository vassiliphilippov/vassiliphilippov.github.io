vilely

adverb
in a despicable or unpleasant way
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪlli / (AmE), / ˈvaɪlli / (BrE)
In a way that is morally despicable or extremely unpleasant.

Examples

To vilely insult
She was vilely insulted by the rumors that spread about her.
The soldiers were treated vilely by their captors during the war.
The villain vilely betrayed his closest friends for personal gain.
