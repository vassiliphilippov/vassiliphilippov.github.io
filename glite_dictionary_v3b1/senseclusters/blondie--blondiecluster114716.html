blondie

noun
person with blond hair
Pronunciation: / ˈblɑndi / (AmE), / ˈblɒndi / (BrE)
a person, especially a woman or girl, who has light yellowish hair

Examples

A smiling blondie
The new student was a cheerful blondie who quickly made friends with everyone.
People often called her blondie because of her striking hair color.
At the party, a little blondie danced happily in the center of the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blonde - a woman or girl with light yellowish hair
  • antonym: brunette - a person, especially a woman or girl, with dark brown or black hair
