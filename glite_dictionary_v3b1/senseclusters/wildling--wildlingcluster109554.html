wildling

noun
feral animal
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪldlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪldlɪŋ / (BrE)
an animal that lives in the wild or has returned to a wild, untamed state after being domesticated

Examples

A stray wildling
The abandoned cat soon became a wildling, hunting for food in the forest.
After escaping from the farm, the pig lived as a wildling in the hills.
Conservationists tracked a group of wildlings that had once been pets.

Related Terms

  • antonym: domesticated - an animal tamed and kept by humans
  • synonym: feral - an animal in a wild state, especially after escape from captivity
