unequal

adjective
not up to a task or challenge
7.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈniː.kwəl/ /; / /ʌ.ˈniː.kwəl/ (AmE), /ʌ.ˈniː.kwəl/ /; / /ə.ˈniː.kwəl/ (BrE)
not having the necessary qualities, ability, or resources to do something successfully; not up to a task or challenge

Examples

Unequal to the task
After hours of hiking, he realized he was unequal to the challenge of climbing the mountain.
She felt unequal to the responsibility of leading the team alone.
The player seemed unequal to the demands of professional competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inadequate - lacking the quality or quantity required; insufficient
  • antonym: sufficient - having enough resources or qualities for a task; adequate
