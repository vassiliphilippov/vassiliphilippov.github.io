cute

adjective
attractive in a pretty or endearing way
Pronunciation: / kjuːt / (AmE), / kjuːt / (BrE)
attractive in a charming, pretty, or endearing way, especially because of smallness or youthfulness

Examples

A cute puppy
Everyone thought the puppy was cute with its big eyes and floppy ears.
She wore a cute dress to the party last night.
Their new apartment is small but really cute and cozy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adorable - inspiring great affection; delightful; charming
  • synonym: pretty - pleasing or attractive to the eye in a delicate way
  • antonym: ugly - unpleasant or repulsive in appearance
