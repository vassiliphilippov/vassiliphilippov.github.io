small cap

noun
capital letter same height as lowercase
9.1
Pronunciation: / smɔl kæp / (AmE), / smɔːl kap / (BrE)
a capital letter designed to be the same height as a lowercase letter, used especially in typography for stylistic effect or emphasis

Examples

Small cap letters
