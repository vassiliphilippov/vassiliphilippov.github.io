chugalug

noun
quick drink
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌɡəˌlʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌɡəˌlʌɡ / (BrE)
an act of drinking a beverage, especially alcohol, very quickly and often in one continuous motion

Examples

A chugalug of beer

At the party, someone challenged her to a chugalug of beer.
The crowd cheered as he performed a daring chugalug on stage.
