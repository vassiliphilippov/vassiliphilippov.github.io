incommensurably

adverb
beyond comparison
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnkəˈmɛnʃərəbli / (AmE), / ˌɪnkəˈmɛnʃərəbli / (BrE)
to a degree that cannot be measured or compared; vastly or extremely

Examples

To incommensurably differ
The two cultures developed incommensurably different traditions over the centuries.
Her achievements are incommensurably greater than those of her peers.
The value of the artwork is incommensurably higher than its material cost.
The new technology is incommensurably more efficient than the previous system.
Her kindness was incommensurably greater than anyone else I have met.
The two artists' styles are incommensurably different, making comparison difficult.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comparably - in a way that can be compared
  • synonym: immeasurably - in a way that cannot be measured
  • antonym: slightly - to a small degree or extent
  • synonym: vastly - to a very great extent or degree
