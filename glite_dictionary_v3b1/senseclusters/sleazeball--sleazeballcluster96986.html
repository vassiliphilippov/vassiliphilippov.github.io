sleazeball

noun
dishonest person
Pronunciation: / ˈsliːzbɔl / (AmE), / ˈsliːzbɔːl / (BrE)
a person who is dishonest, immoral, or generally unpleasant

Examples

A sleazeball person
Nobody wanted to do business with that sleazeball after hearing about his past scams.
She called her ex-boyfriend a sleazeball because of the way he treated people.
The politician was exposed as a sleazeball in a major news investigation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gentleman - a man who is polite and honorable
  • synonym: scoundrel - a person who behaves dishonestly or unscrupulously
  • synonym: villain - a wicked or evil person
