perch

noun
elevated place for sitting or resting
6.6 3.3
Pronunciation: /pɜːrtʃ/ (AmE), /pɜːtʃ/ (BrE)
a high, narrow, or elevated place or object where a person, bird, or animal can sit or rest

Examples

A bird's perch
From his perch in the tree, the boy could see everything happening below.
She found a comfortable perch on the stone wall and watched the parade.
The photographer balanced on a narrow perch to get a better shot of the city.
A wooden perch was installed in the aviary for the birds to rest on.
Owls often sit quietly on a perch at dusk, watching for prey.
The canary fluffed its feathers and hopped onto its perch in the cage.
