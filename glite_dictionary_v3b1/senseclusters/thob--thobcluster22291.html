thob

noun
Middle Eastern robe
9.9
Pronunciation: / θoʊb / (AmE), / θəʊb / (BrE)
a long, loose robe traditionally worn by men in the Middle East

Examples

A white thob
He wore a crisp white thob to the family celebration.
Many men in Saudi Arabia choose a thob for formal occasions.
The tailor measured him carefully for a new thob before the festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kaftan - a long, loose garment with sleeves
