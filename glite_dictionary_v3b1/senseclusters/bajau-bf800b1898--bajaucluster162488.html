Bajau

proper noun
Southeast Asian ethnic group
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːdʒaʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːdʒaʊ / (BrE)
a member of a seafaring ethnic group native to Southeast Asia, especially Malaysia and the Philippines

Examples

A Bajau person
The Bajau have lived on boats for generations, traveling between islands in Southeast Asia.
Many Bajau communities rely on fishing and diving for their livelihood.
Researchers have studied the Bajau for their remarkable ability to dive deep underwater.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Sama-Bajau - the collective term for related seafaring ethnic groups in Southeast Asia
