schlep

noun
awkward or foolish person (informal)
Pronunciation: / ʃlɛp / (AmE), / ʃlɛp / (BrE)
An awkward, clumsy, or inept person, often regarded as foolish or lacking in intelligence (informal).

Examples

A clumsy schlep
Don't be such a schlep—pay attention to where you're going!
He always acts like a total schlep at parties, spilling drinks everywhere.
I can't believe I let that schlep fix my sink; now it's leaking worse than before.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doofus - a foolish or stupid person
  • synonym: klutz - a clumsy person
