frame

verb
construct by assembling parts
2.0
Pronunciation: / freɪm / (AmE), / freɪm / (BrE)
to construct something by fitting or joining parts together, typically to form a supporting structure

Examples

To frame a house
He framed a wooden shed at the back of his garden last summer.
The company will frame the new houses before winter arrives.
They spent all day framing the stage for the school play.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assemble - to fit together the parts of something
  • synonym: build - to construct by putting parts or materials together
