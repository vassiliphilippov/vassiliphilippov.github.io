plain

adjective
simple, direct, and unembellished
7.8 1.8
Pronunciation: /pleɪn/ (AmE), /pleɪn/ (BrE)
expressed in a simple, direct way, without trying to hide the truth or avoid unpleasantness; unembellished or blunt

Examples

Sometimes the plain truth is exactly what people need to hear, even if it stings.
