exitus

noun
medical term for death
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛksɪtəs / (AmE), / ˈɛksɪtəs / (BrE)
a medical term for death or the end of a disease process

Examples

Patient exitus
The doctor noted exitus in the patient's medical record after all resuscitation efforts failed.
In some reports, exitus is used to indicate the official time of death.
The nurse informed the family about the patient's exitus early that morning.

Related Terms
