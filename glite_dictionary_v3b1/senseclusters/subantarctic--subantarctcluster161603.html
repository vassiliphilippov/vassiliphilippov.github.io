subantarctic

noun
region near Antarctic
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbænˈtɑːrktɪk / (AmE), / ˌsʌbænˈtɑːktɪk / (BrE)
the region just north of the Antarctic, including its islands, seas, and unique ecosystems

Examples

The subantarctic islands
Many rare bird species breed only in the subantarctic during the summer months.
The harsh climate of the subantarctic makes scientific research challenging but rewarding.
Tourists visit the subantarctic islands to see unique wildlife and dramatic landscapes.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: Antarctic - the region around the South Pole, including Antarctica and its surrounding waters
  • synonym: subarctic - the region just south of the Arctic, with cold but not polar climates
