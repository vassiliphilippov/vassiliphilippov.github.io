scented candle

noun
candle infused with fragrance
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntɪd ˈkændl̩ / (AmE), / ˈsɛntɪd ˈkændl / (BrE)
A candle that has been infused with a fragrance and releases it when burned.

Examples

A scented candle
I lit a scented candle to make the room smell fresh after cleaning.
She likes to relax in the bath with a scented candle flickering nearby.
The store sells a wide variety of scented candles, from lavender to vanilla.

Related Terms

  • synonym: candle - a block of wax with a central wick that is burned to produce light
