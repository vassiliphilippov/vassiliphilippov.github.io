mattress

noun
large pad for sleeping on
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈmæ.trəs/ /; / /ˈmæ.trɪs/ (AmE), /ˈmæ.trəs/ /; / /ˈmæ.trɪs/ (BrE)
