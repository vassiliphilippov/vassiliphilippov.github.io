nonoperative

adjective
not functioning or surgical
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈɑpəˌreɪtɪv / (AmE), / nɒnˈɒpərətɪv / (BrE)
not functioning, not in use, or not involving surgical operation

Examples

Nonoperative treatment
The doctor recommended a nonoperative approach to treat the injury.
After the update, the software became nonoperative and required a reinstall.
Some patients prefer nonoperative treatments to avoid surgery when possible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inoperative - not working or not effective
  • antonym: operative - functioning or involving surgery
