basal

adjective
basic, fundamental
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪsəl / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsəl / (BrE)
forming or relating to the essential or basic part of something; fundamental.

Examples

Basal need
Good nutrition is a basal requirement for healthy growth in all mammals.
Teachers often use basal readers to help children develop essential reading skills.
The researcher explained that breathing is a basal human function necessary for survival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: basic - forming an essential foundation; fundamental
  • synonym: fundamental - forming a necessary base or core; of central importance
