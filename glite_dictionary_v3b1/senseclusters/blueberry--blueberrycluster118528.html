blueberry

noun
a shrub producing edible blue berries
Pronunciation: / ˈbluˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌbɛri / (BrE)
Any of various shrubs of the genus Vaccinium that produce small, typically blue edible berries.

Examples

Blueberry bush
She planted three blueberry shrubs along the garden path.
The blueberry bush in our backyard produces fruit every summer.
Wild blueberry plants grow abundantly in northern forests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cranberry - a shrub of the genus Vaccinium with edible red berries
  • synonym: huckleberry - a shrub similar to blueberry producing edible berries
