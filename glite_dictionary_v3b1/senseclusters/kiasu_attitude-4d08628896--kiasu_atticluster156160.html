kiasu attitude

noun
fearful, competitive mindset
9.2 8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈkjɑː.suː ˈæt.ə.tuː/ /; / /kjɑː.ˈsuː ˈæt.ə.tuː/ (AmE), /ˈkjɑː.suː ˈæt.ɪ.tjuːd/ /; / /kjɑː.ˈsuː ˈæt.ɪ.tjuːd/ (BrE)
A mindset characterized by an intense fear of missing out or losing to others, often resulting in competitive or selfish behavior.

Examples

kiasu attitude in life
Her kiasu attitude pushed her to arrive at every sale before anyone else.
In group projects, his kiasu attitude led him to volunteer for every task.
Many parents develop a kiasu attitude about their children's education, enrolling them in numerous classes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fear of missing out - anxiety that others are having experiences one is not; commonly abbreviated as FOMO
  • synonym: kiasu - fear of losing out; a person or mindset that is overly competitive or afraid to lose
