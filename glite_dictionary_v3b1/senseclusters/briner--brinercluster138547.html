briner

noun
person who pickles food
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbraɪnər / (AmE), / ˈbraɪnə / (BrE)
a person who makes or uses brine to preserve food, especially by pickling or salting

Examples

A pickle briner
The experienced briner shared her secret recipe for crunchy pickles with the class.
As a skilled briner, he prepares jars of olives and cucumbers every summer.
The family relied on their grandfather, the village briner, to preserve vegetables for winter.
