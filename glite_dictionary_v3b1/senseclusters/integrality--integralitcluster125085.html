integrality

noun
quality of being whole or complete
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.təˈɡrælə.ti / (AmE), / ˌɪn.tɪˈɡræl.ə.ti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being whole, total, or complete; entirety.

Examples

The integrality of research
She was praised for her commitment to the integrality of her research methods.
The integrality of the plan gave everyone confidence in its success.
We must maintain the integrality of the data throughout the process.
