fish eagle

noun
large fish-eating hawk
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃ ˌiːɡəl / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃ ˌiːɡl̩ / (BrE)
A large, mainly fish-eating hawk found worldwide, known for building bulky nests often occupied for years.

Examples

The fish eagle swoops
The fish eagle swooped down and snatched a trout from the lake.
Throughout the years, the same pair of fish eagles returned to the massive nest.
We spotted a fish eagle building its nest high above the riverbank.

Related Terms

  • synonym: osprey - a large raptor that feeds mainly on fish
