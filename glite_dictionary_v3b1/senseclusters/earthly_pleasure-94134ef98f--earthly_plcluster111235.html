earthly pleasure

noun
material or physical enjoyment
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrθli ˈplɛʒər / (AmE), / ˈɜːθli ˈplɛʒə / (BrE)
enjoyment or satisfaction derived from material, physical, or worldly experiences, as opposed to spiritual or intellectual ones

Examples

Earthly pleasures of life
After years of self-denial, he finally allowed himself some earthly pleasures.
Many people seek earthly pleasures to find happiness and fulfillment.
Monks often renounce earthly pleasures in pursuit of a spiritual life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: spiritual pleasure - enjoyment derived from spiritual or intellectual experiences
