Sergei Rachmaninoff

proper noun
Russian composer and pianist (1873–1943)
Pronunciation: / sɚˈgeɪ rækməˌnɔf / (AmE), / səˈgeɪ rækˈmænɪˌnɒf / (BrE)
(1873–1943) a Russian composer, pianist, and conductor known for his virtuosic piano works and Romantic style.
