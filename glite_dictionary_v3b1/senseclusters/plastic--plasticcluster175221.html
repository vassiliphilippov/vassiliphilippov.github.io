plastic

noun
synthetic moldable material
Pronunciation: / ˈplæstɪk / (AmE), / ˈplæstɪk / (BrE)
a synthetic material made from a wide range of organic polymers that can be molded into shape while soft and then set into a rigid or slightly elastic form

Examples

Plastic bottle
Plastic is widely used for packaging because it is lightweight and durable.
She collected pieces of plastic from the beach during the cleanup event.
The company recycles plastic bottles to reduce environmental waste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: polymer - a substance made of large molecules composed of many repeated subunits
