superiorly

adverb
arrogantly, condescendingly
7.7
Pronunciation: / suːˈpɪriɚli / (AmE), / suːˈpɪərɪəli / (BrE)
in a way that shows an attitude of being better than others; arrogantly or condescendingly

Examples

Spoke superiorly
She answered the question superiorly, making her classmates feel uncomfortable.
The manager spoke superiorly to his team, which hurt their motivation.
He looked at his opponent superiorly, convinced he would win easily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrogantly - in a proud and self-important way
  • synonym: condescendingly - in a way that shows you think you are better than others
  • antonym: humbly - in a modest or respectful way
