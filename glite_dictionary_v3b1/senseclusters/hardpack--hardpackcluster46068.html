hardpack

adjective
firmly compacted
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrdˌpæk / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdˌpæk / (BrE)
firmly packed and compact, especially referring to snow, soil, or a surface that is hard and solid

Examples

Hardpack snow surface
The trail was covered in hardpack snow, making it easy to walk without sinking.
Cyclists prefer riding on hardpack dirt because it offers a smoother and faster surface.
After several days of sun, the muddy field turned into hardpack ground.
