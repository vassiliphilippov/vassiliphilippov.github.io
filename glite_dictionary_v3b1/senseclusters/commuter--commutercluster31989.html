commuter

noun
person who regularly travels to work
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjutɚ / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːtə / (BrE)
a person who travels regularly some distance between home and work, especially between a suburb and a city

Examples

A commuter train
As a daily commuter, she spends nearly two hours traveling to her office.
The city's train system is designed to accommodate thousands of commuters every morning.
The government plans to offer discounts to commuter rail passengers.
