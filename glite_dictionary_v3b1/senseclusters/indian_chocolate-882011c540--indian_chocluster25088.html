Indian chocolate

noun
perennial plant with nodding brown-purple flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən ˈtʃɑklət / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈtʃɒklət / (BrE)
A perennial plant of northern temperate regions, having pinnate leaves and a few nodding flowers with a brown-purple calyx and orange and pink petals.
