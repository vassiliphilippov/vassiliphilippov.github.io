swanlike

adjective
graceful or elegant
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑːnˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈswɒnˌlaɪk / (BrE)
graceful or elegant in appearance or movement, resembling a swan

Examples

Swanlike grace
The dancer moved across the stage with a swanlike elegance that captivated the audience.
He admired her swanlike posture as she stood by the window, lost in thought.
The model's swanlike neck made her stand out in every photograph.

Related Terms

  • antonym: awkward - lacking grace or smoothness
  • synonym: graceful - showing elegance and smoothness in movement or appearance
