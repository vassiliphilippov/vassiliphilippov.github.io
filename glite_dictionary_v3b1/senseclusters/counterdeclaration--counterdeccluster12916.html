counterdeclaration

noun
opposing statement
Pronunciation: / ˌkaʊntərˌdɛkləˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntəˌdɛkləˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
a formal statement made to oppose or respond to another declaration

Examples

A counterdeclaration statement
The organization released a counterdeclaration to address the accusations made by its competitor.
After the initial announcement, a counterdeclaration was quickly drafted by the opposing party.
The court considered both the original declaration and the counterdeclaration before making a decision.

  • synonym: rebuttal - a statement that contradicts or refutes another statement
