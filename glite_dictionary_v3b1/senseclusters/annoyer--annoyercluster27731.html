annoyer

noun
person who teases or irritates
4.2
Pronunciation: / əˈnɔɪ.ər / (AmE), / əˈnɔɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person who irritates or teases others, often by mocking or provoking curiosity

Examples

The annoyer teases friends
At the party, the annoyer made everyone laugh and groan with his playful jibes.
She found her little brother to be a constant annoyer with his endless pranks.
The persistent annoyer wouldn't stop poking fun at his classmates during the lesson.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comforter - someone who brings comfort rather than annoyance
  • synonym: tease - a person who makes fun of or provokes others
  • synonym: tormentor - someone who deliberately causes annoyance or pain
