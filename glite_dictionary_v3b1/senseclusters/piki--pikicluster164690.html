piki

noun
thin blue corn bread
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːki / (AmE), / ˈpiːki / (BrE)
a thin, crisp bread made from blue cornmeal, traditionally prepared by some Native American peoples

Examples

Traditional piki bread
She learned how to make piki from her grandmother.
At the festival, visitors sampled traditional piki prepared on a hot stone.
The blue corn used for piki gives it a unique flavor and color.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bread - a staple food made from flour and water, usually baked
  • synonym: tortilla - a thin, flat bread made from corn or wheat
