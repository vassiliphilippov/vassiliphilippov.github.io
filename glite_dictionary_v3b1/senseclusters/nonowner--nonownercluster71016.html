nonowner

adjective
not owning
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈoʊnər / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈəʊnə / (BrE)
not owning the thing or property mentioned, especially used before a noun to describe such a person.

Examples

Nonowner resident
The building has special parking spaces reserved for nonowner residents.
A nonowner driver may need different insurance coverage than someone who owns a car.
Workshops were organized to help nonowner tenants understand their rights.

Related Terms

  • antonym: owner - a person who owns something
  • synonym: tenant - a person who rents or occupies property owned by someone else
