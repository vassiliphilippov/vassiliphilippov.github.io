elevenses

noun
mid-morning snack
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛvənzɪz / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛvənzɪz / (BrE)
a light snack or drink taken around eleven o'clock in the morning, especially in Britain

Examples

Light elevenses snack
We stopped for elevenses during our long walk in the countryside.
At the office, everyone gathers in the kitchen for elevenses around eleven o'clock.
She brought homemade biscuits to share at elevenses with her friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tea break - a short break from work to have tea or coffee
