noncombative

adjective
not aggressive
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkəmˈbætɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkəmˈbætɪv / (BrE)
not aggressive or violent; not wanting to fight or argue; peaceful

Examples

A noncombative attitude
Despite the heated discussion, her response remained noncombative and calm.
The teacher appreciated his noncombative attitude during the group project.
Even when criticized, he stayed noncombative and tried to resolve the issue peacefully.
The discussion remained noncombative, with both sides listening respectfully to each other.
During the protest, organizers encouraged a noncombative approach to avoid any violence.
The diplomat was praised for his noncombative attitude during tense negotiations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aggressive - ready or likely to attack or argue
  • antonym: combative - eager to fight or argue
  • synonym: nonviolent - not using or involving physical force
  • synonym: peaceful - not wanting to fight or argue
