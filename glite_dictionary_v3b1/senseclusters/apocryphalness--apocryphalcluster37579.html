apocryphalness

noun
quality of being dubious or untrue
Pronunciation: / əˈpɑː.krə.fəl.nəs /; / əˈpɑːkrəfəl nɪs / (AmE), / əˈpɒ.krɪ.fəl.nəs /; / əˈpɒkrɪf(ə)lnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being of doubtful authenticity or not considered genuine or true
