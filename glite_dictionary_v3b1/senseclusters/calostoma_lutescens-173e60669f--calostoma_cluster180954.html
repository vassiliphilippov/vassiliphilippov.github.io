Calostoma lutescens

noun
species of fungus with yellow spore case
Pronunciation: / kæ.ləˈstoʊ.mə luːˈtɛs.ənz / (AmE), / ka.lɒˈstəʊ.mə luːˈtes.enz / (BrE)
A species of fungus with a leathery stalk and a globular fruiting body with a pale yellow spore case, found among gasteromycetes.
