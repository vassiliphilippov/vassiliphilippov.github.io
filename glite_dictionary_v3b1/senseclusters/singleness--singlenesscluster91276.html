singleness

noun
sincerity; lack of hypocrisy
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋɡl̩nəs / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡl̩nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being sincere and undivided, without hypocrisy or pretense

Examples

Singleness of purpose
He faced the task with singleness of purpose, never wavering from his goal.
Her singleness of heart was evident in every action she took.
The singleness of her intentions made her trustworthy among her peers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hypocrisy - the practice of claiming beliefs or feelings one does not possess
  • synonym: sincerity - the absence of pretense or hypocrisy
