Joycean

noun
James Joyce admirer
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔɪsiən / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪsiən / (BrE)
a person who admires, studies, or is strongly influenced by the writer James Joyce or his works

Examples

A Joycean scholar
As a dedicated Joycean, she traveled to Dublin for Bloomsday every year.
The literary club attracted many Joyceans who enjoyed discussing modernist novels.
He considers himself a Joycean after reading Ulysses several times.
