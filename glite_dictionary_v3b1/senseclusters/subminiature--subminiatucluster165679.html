subminiature

noun
very small component
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbˈmɪniətʃɚ / (AmE), / ˌsʌbˈmɪnɪətʃə / (BrE)
a very small electronic component, especially a tiny vacuum tube used in early radios and instruments
