paddleball

noun
ball-and-paddle toy
Pronunciation: / ˈpædəlˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈpædəlˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a game or toy in which a small ball attached to a paddle by an elastic string is hit repeatedly with the paddle

Examples

A paddleball toy
He entertained himself for hours with a paddleball while waiting for his friends.
The child tried to break her record for most consecutive hits with the paddleball.
At the fair, she won a bright red paddleball as a prize.

Related Terms

  • synonym: table tennis - a game played with small paddles and a lightweight ball on a table
  • synonym: yo-yo - a toy consisting of a spool that is spun up and down on a string
