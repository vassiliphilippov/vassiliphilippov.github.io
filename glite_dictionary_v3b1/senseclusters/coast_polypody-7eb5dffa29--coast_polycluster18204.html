coast polypody

noun
fern of western North America
Pronunciation: / koʊst ˈpɑlɪˌpoʊdi / (AmE), / kəʊst ˈpɒlɪˌpəʊdi / (BrE)
a stiff, leathery-leaved fern native to western North America, with ovate fronds divided to the midrib

The coast polypody fern
Botanists study the unique adaptation of the coast polypody to its rocky, coastal habitat.

We spotted a rare coast polypody while hiking in northern California.
