sulfuric

adjective
relating to sulfur or sulfur compounds
2.8
Pronunciation: / sʌlˈfjʊrɪk /; / səlˈfjʊrɪk / (AmE), / sʌlˈfjʊərɪk / (BrE)
of, relating to, or containing sulfur, especially in compounds in which sulfur has a high oxidation state.

Examples

Sulfuric acid
Sulfuric smoke poured from the chemical plant after the accident.
The factory handled sulfuric compounds with strict safety precautions.
The researcher studied the properties of sulfuric solutions in the laboratory.
Factories must prevent the release of sulphuric fumes into the environment.
The laboratory technician handled the sulphuric acid with extreme care.
The reaction produced a strong sulphuric smell in the room.
