bookbag

noun
school bag
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊkˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈbʊkˌbæɡ / (BrE)
a bag used for carrying books, notebooks, and other school supplies, especially by students

Examples

A bookbag for school
She packed her bookbag with textbooks and notebooks before leaving for school.
His bookbag was so heavy that he struggled to carry it up the stairs.
The teacher asked everyone to place their bookbags at the back of the classroom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: backpack - a bag carried on the back with shoulder straps
  • synonym: satchel - a bag with a strap, used for carrying books
