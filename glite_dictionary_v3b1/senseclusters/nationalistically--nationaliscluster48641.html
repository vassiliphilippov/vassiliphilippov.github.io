nationalistically

adverb
with strong national pride
Pronunciation: / ˌnæʃənəˈlɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌnæʃənəˈlɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that strongly supports or promotes one's own nation, often putting it above others

Examples

Support nationalistically
The team was supported nationalistically by fans waving flags and singing anthems.
He spoke nationalistically about the country's achievements at the conference.
Some newspapers reported the event nationalistically, focusing only on local successes.
The leader spoke nationalistically about protecting local industries from foreign competition.
Some newspapers report nationalistically, focusing only on stories that favor their own country.
During the debate, she argued nationalistically for stricter immigration policies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: internationally - in a way that involves or relates to more than one nation
  • synonym: patriotically - in a way that shows love and devotion to one's country
