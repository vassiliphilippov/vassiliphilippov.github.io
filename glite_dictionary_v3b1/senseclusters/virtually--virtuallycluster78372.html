Synonyms

  • almost
    1.0 400/m
    almost means very nearly complete or true, while virtually stresses something is almost so in reality or effect.
  • nearly
    1.0 110/m
    nearly means being very close to something but not quite there, while virtually often reads as almost the same result.
  • practically
    2.5 30/m
    practically is used when something is almost true in practice, while virtually is similar but can sound slightly more formal.
  • just about
    2.7 40/m
    just about is a casual way to say nearly or almost, while virtually is more formal and used in wider contexts.
  • approximately
    3.2 20/m
    approximately is used for numbers and measures to show an estimate, while virtually is broader and not only for amounts.
  • roughly
    1.6 17/m
    roughly gives a coarse or quick estimate, while virtually implies a closer, nearly exact state or condition.
