order Stegocephalia

noun
extinct group of primitive amphibians
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrdər ˌstiːɡoʊsəˈfeɪliə / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ˌstiːɡəʊsəˈfiːlɪə / (BrE)
in former classifications, a division of class Amphibia comprising all pre-Jurassic and some later extinct large salamandriform amphibians.
