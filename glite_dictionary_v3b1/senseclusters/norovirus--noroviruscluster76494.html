norovirus

noun
stomach virus
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrəˌvaɪrəs / (AmE), / ˈnɔːrəˌvaɪrəs / (BrE)
a virus that causes stomach and intestinal illness, with symptoms like vomiting and diarrhea

Examples

Norovirus infection
The cruise ship was quarantined after an outbreak of norovirus affected dozens of passengers.
Doctors warned that norovirus spreads quickly in schools and care homes.
After eating at the restaurant, several people became ill with norovirus.
The school closed for cleaning after a norovirus outbreak affected dozens of students.
Doctors warned that norovirus spreads easily through contaminated food and surfaces.
After eating at the restaurant, several people became ill with norovirus symptoms.
The outbreak of norovirus on the cruise ship made many passengers sick.
Schools sometimes close temporarily due to a norovirus outbreak among students.
Proper handwashing can help prevent the spread of norovirus in communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gastroenteritis - inflammation of the stomach and intestines causing vomiting and diarrhea
  • antonym: influenza - a virus that causes respiratory illness, not stomach illness
  • synonym: rotavirus - a virus that causes gastroenteritis, especially in children
