crudity

noun
the state of being raw or unrefined
8.2 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàkruÀêd…ôti / (AmE), / ÀàkruÀêd…ôti / (BrE)
the state or quality of being raw, unrefined, or lacking sophistication

Examples

Artistic crudity
Despite its crudity, the film had an undeniable, wild energy that captivated audiences.
Some critics complained about the crudity of the early sketches in the exhibit.
The crudity of the landscape impressed upon them the power of untouched nature.

  • unsophistication

  • primitiveness
    8.0 0.04/m

  • primitivism
