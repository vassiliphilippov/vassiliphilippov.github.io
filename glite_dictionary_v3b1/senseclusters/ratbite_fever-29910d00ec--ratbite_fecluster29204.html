ratbite fever

noun
disease from a rat or mouse bite
8.4 7.4
Pronunciation: /ræt baɪt ˈfiː.vər/ (AmE), /ræt baɪt ˈfiː.və/ (BrE)
either of two infectious diseases transmitted to humans by the bite of a rat or mouse, characterized by fever, headache, nausea, and skin eruptions.
