donor card

noun
card authorizing organ donation after death
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊnər kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈdəʊnə kɑːd / (BrE)
an official card carried by a person indicating consent to organ donation for transplantation after their death

Examples

Carry a donor card
She carried her donor card in her wallet at all times.
The hospital staff checked his donor card to confirm his wishes.
When he renewed his driver's license, he also registered for a donor card.

Related Terms

  • synonym: organ donor - a person who donates an organ or tissue
