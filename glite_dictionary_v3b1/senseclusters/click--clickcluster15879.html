click

noun
a short, sharp metallic sound
Pronunciation: / klɪk / (AmE), / klɪk / (BrE)
a short, sharp sound like that made by a switch or latch snapping into place, often metallic in quality

Examples

A door click
She heard a faint click as the door unlocked.
The machine made a loud click before powering down.
With a click of the pen, he began to write.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clack - a sharp, abrupt sound, such as two hard objects striking each other
  • degree higher: clatter - a continuous rattling sound as of hard objects striking each other
  • synonym: snap - a sudden, sharp cracking sound
