pov

noun
abbreviation for 'point of view'
8.4 8.44/m
Pronunciation: / pi o ä vi / (AmE), / piÀê …ô ä viÀê / (BrE)
Abbreviation for 'point of view'; used to express a person's perspective or opinion, especially in informal contexts or social media.

Examples

pov in story
From her pov, the decision made perfect sense.

Synonyms

  • viewpoint
    4.1 12/m

  • standpoint
    3.6 14/m
    standpoint is a formal or evaluative position used in arguments, while pov is the casual social media abbreviation for that perspective.
