Pomolobus pseudoharengus

noun
species of shad-like fish (alewife)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊmoʊˈloʊbəs ˌsuːdoʊhəˈrɛŋɡəs / (AmE), / ˌpɒmələʊˈbʌs ˌsjuːdəʊhəˈrɛŋɡəs / (BrE)
A species of shad-like fish, commonly known as the alewife, that migrates up rivers to spawn, and is often salted or smoked for food.
