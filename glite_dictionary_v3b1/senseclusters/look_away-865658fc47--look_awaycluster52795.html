look away

verb
avert one's gaze
1.0
Pronunciation: / lʊk əˈweɪ / (AmE), / lʊk əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to turn one's eyes or face away from someone or something; to avert one's gaze

Examples

To look away quickly
If something makes you uncomfortable, it's okay to look away.
She tried to look away but couldn't avoid seeing the accident happen.
When the embarrassing scene appeared on screen, he immediately looked away.
