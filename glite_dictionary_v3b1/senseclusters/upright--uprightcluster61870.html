upright

verb
to set vertically
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈʌp.raɪt / (BrE)
to set something in a vertical or standing position after it has fallen or been tilted

Examples

To upright a chair
After the wind knocked over the chair, he quickly uprighted it.
The workers upright the statue that had fallen during the earthquake.
It took three people to upright the heavy bookshelf after it tipped over.

Related Terms

  • synonym: raise - to lift something to a higher position
  • antonym: tip over - to cause something to fall from a vertical to a horizontal position
