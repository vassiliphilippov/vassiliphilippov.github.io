decking

verb
to install deck surface
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɛkɪŋ / (BrE)
covering or fitting an area with decking material, especially by installing a deck surface.

Examples

To deck a patio
They spent the weekend decking the patio to create a level space for outdoor furniture.
The workers are decking the new balcony with weather-resistant boards.
After planning the layout, she started decking the backyard to make it more inviting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: installing - putting something in place for use
  • antonym: removing - taking something away or off
