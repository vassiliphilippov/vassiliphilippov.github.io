runner

noun
person who delivers messages or documents
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnər / (AmE), / ˈrʌnə / (BrE)
a person employed to deliver messages, documents, or packages, especially within an office or between organizations

Examples

Office runner
A runner quickly transported the signed documents between the departments.
He sent a runner to deliver the urgent contract to their office.
The law firm hired a runner to carry messages between the courtroom and their office.
