overreading

noun
excessive reading
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈriːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈriːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of reading more material than is necessary or advisable, often to excess or for too long

Examples

Exam overreading
His habit of overreading before exams often left him feeling exhausted.
Parents worried that their daughter's overreading was affecting her social life.
Too much overreading can sometimes make it harder to remember important details.

Related Terms

  • synonym: binge-reading - reading large amounts in a short period
  • antonym: skimming - reading quickly to get the main ideas
