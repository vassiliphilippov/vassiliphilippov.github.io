spyglass

noun
small handheld telescope
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪˌɡlæs / (AmE), / ˈspaɪˌɡlɑːs / (BrE)
A small, handheld refracting telescope used for viewing distant objects, especially at sea or by explorers.

Examples

A spyglass telescope
He bought an antique spyglass as a gift for his grandfather.
The captain raised his spyglass to scan the horizon for enemy ships.
Through the spyglass, she could see the tiny island in the distance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: telescope - an optical instrument for making distant objects appear nearer
