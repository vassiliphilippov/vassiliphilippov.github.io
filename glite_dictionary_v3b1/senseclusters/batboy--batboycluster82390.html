batboy

noun
tabloid creature
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbætˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈbætˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
a fictional half-bat, half-human character popularized by American tabloid newspapers, especially the Weekly World News

Examples

Tabloid batboy
The Weekly World News featured batboy on its cover, capturing readers' imaginations in the 1990s.
Many people remember the bizarre stories about batboy from old supermarket tabloids.
A movie was inspired by the adventures of batboy, the half-bat, half-human character.
