raspberry

noun
a sound of derision made with the lips
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈræzˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈrɑːzbəri / (BrE)
a rude sound made by blowing air through the lips, used to express displeasure or contempt

Examples

Give a raspberry sound
A mischievous child gave a big raspberry to her teacher.
He blew a raspberry to show what he thought of the idea.
The crowd greeted the unpopular speaker with a loud raspberry.
