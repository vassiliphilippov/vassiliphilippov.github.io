various

adjective
considered individually; respective
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛriəs / (AmE), / ˈveəriəs / (BrE)
considered individually and separately; respective or individual (used before a plural noun to refer to each item separately)

Examples

Various owners
Students left the meeting for their various classes.
The committee members voiced their various opinions on the matter.
The judges returned to their various positions after the break.

Related Terms

  • synonym: respective - relating separately to each of two or more things
