status

noun
social or professional standing
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪtəs, ˈstætəs / (AmE), / ˈsteɪtəs, ˈstætəs / (BrE)
a person's or thing's social or professional position compared to others; the level of importance or respect that someone or something has in a society, organization, or group

Examples

High status person
His new job gave him a higher status within the company.
Owning a home is often seen as a mark of social status.
The club only admits members of a certain status.
