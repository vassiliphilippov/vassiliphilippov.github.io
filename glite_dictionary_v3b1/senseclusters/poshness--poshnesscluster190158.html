poshness

noun
elegance or upper-class style
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈpɒʃnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being elegant, stylish, or typical of the upper class

Examples

The poshness of restaurant
The poshness of the restaurant made it a popular spot for celebrations.
He admired the poshness of her accent and the way she dressed.
Despite its poshness, the hotel staff were friendly and welcoming.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elegance - the quality of being graceful and stylish
  • synonym: luxury - a state of great comfort or elegance
  • antonym: plainness - the quality of being simple or lacking decoration
