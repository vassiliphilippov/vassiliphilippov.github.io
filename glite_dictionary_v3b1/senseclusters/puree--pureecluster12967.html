puree

verb
blend or crush food to a smooth paste
2.7 4.0
Pronunciation: / pjʊˈreɪ / (AmE), / pjʊəˈreɪ / (BrE)
to make food smooth and creamy by crushing or blending it thoroughly, especially by rubbing it through a sieve or using a blender.

Examples

To puree vegetables
After steaming the carrots, he pureed them for his infant daughter.
She decided to puree the soup to make it smoother for the guests.
Some recipes call for you to puree the sauce before serving.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blend - to mix or combine two or more substances so that they become a single smooth substance
  • synonym: mash - to crush food into a soft, pulpy mass
