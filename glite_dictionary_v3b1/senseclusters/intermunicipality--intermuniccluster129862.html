intermunicipality

adjective
between municipalities
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərmjuːˌnɪsɪˈpæləti / (AmE), / ˌɪntəmjuːˌnɪsɪˈpælɪti / (BrE)
existing or occurring between two or more municipalities, or shared by multiple municipal governments

Examples

Intermunicipality agreement
The intermunicipality committee coordinated efforts to manage waste across several towns.
An intermunicipality agreement was signed to share emergency services between neighboring cities.
They discussed intermunicipality cooperation to improve regional transportation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intercity - existing or occurring between cities
  • antonym: municipal - relating to a single city or town and its government
