fluty

adjective
like a flute
Pronunciation: / ˈfluːti / (AmE), / ˈfluːti / (BrE)
having a clear, high, and musical sound similar to that of a flute

Examples

Fluty voice
The singer's fluty voice captivated everyone in the audience.
A fluty melody drifted through the open window on the summer breeze.
Children laughed as a fluty whistle echoed across the playground.

Related Terms

  • antonym: harsh - having an unpleasant, rough, or grating sound
  • synonym: melodious - having a pleasant and musical sound
