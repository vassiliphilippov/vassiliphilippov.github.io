mentalize

verb
to interpret mental states
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛntəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɛntəlaɪz / (BrE)
to understand or interpret the thoughts, feelings, or intentions of oneself or others

Examples

To mentalize feelings
Therapists help patients mentalize their feelings and thoughts.
Children gradually learn to mentalize as they develop social skills.
It can be difficult to mentalize the intentions behind someone's actions in a stressful situation.
