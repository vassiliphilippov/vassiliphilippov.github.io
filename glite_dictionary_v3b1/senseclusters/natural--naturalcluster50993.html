natural

adjective
normal or expected; without abnormalities
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃɚəl / (AmE), / ˈnatʃ(ə)rəl / (BrE)
functioning or happening in the usual, expected, or instinctive way; normal or healthy

Examples

Natural process
Doctors were pleased with the patient's natural healing process.
Her recovery was swift because her body had a natural resistance to the infection.
It is natural for parents to worry about their children.
For many people, helping others is a natural impulse.
Her natural reaction was to smile warmly at the compliment.
It felt natural for him to take charge in an emergency.

Related Terms

  • antonym: abnormal - deviating from the normal; unusual or unexpected
  • synonym: automatic - done as a reflex or without conscious thought
  • antonym: deliberate - done consciously and intentionally
  • synonym: instinctive - done without conscious thought, prompted by instinct
  • synonym: normal - conforming to the usual, typical, or expected standard
