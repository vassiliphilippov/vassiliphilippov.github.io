natural

adjective
normal or expected; without abnormalities
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈnæ.tʃə.rəl/ (AmE), /ˈnæ.tʃə.rəl/ (BrE)
functioning or happening in the usual, expected, or instinctive way; normal or healthy

Examples

Natural process
Doctors were pleased with the patient's natural healing process.
