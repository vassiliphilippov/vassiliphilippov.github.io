Tajik

noun
a person from Tajikistan or nearby regions
6.5
Pronunciation: / tɑːˈdʒiːk /; / tɑˈdʒik / (AmE), / tɑːˈdʒiːk /; / ˈtɑːdʒiːk / (BrE)
a person who is a native, inhabitant, or member of the Tajik ethnic group, mainly found in Tajikistan and nearby regions
