administrative division

noun
region designated for administrative purposes
1.6
Pronunciation: / ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪtɪv dɪˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪtɪv dɪˈvɪʒən / (BrE)
A region or district established by a government or authority for administrative purposes.

Examples

A administrative division region
Boundaries of each administrative division are clearly marked on the national map.
Each state in the country is an administrative division with its own local government.
The city council is responsible for managing the administrative division it governs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: province - an administrative division of certain countries, such as Canada or China
