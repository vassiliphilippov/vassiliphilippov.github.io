perboric acid

noun
a white crystalline acid used in bleaching
Pronunciation: / pərˈbɔrɪk ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / pəˈbɒrɪk ˈæsɪd / (BrE)
a white, crystalline acid derived from boric acid, used mainly in bleaching and as an oxidizing agent

Examples
