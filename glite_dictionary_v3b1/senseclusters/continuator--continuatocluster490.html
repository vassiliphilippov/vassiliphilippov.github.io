continuator

noun
one who carries on
Pronunciation: / kənˈtɪnjuˌeɪtər / (AmE), / kənˈtɪnjueɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that continues or carries on something begun by others

Examples

The continuator of work
As the continuator of the project, he made sure the original vision was respected.
The new director became the continuator of her predecessor's educational reforms.
Historians often act as the continuator of stories from the past.

Related Terms

  • antonym: originator - a person who creates or starts something
  • synonym: successor - a person who follows and takes over a position or role
