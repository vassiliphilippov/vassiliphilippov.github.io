shield wall

noun
formation of soldiers with shields for defense
6.2
Pronunciation: / ʃild wɑl / (AmE), / ʃiːld wɔːl / (BrE)
a formation of soldiers standing close together in a line, each holding a shield to create a continuous defensive barrier

Examples

A shield wall formation
Historical reenactors demonstrated how a shield wall was used in ancient battles.
The shield wall broke only after hours of relentless assault from the enemy.
The Vikings quickly formed a shield wall to defend their position against the invaders.
