digitately

adverb
in a finger-like (digitate) manner
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪdʒɪtətli / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒɪtətli / (BrE)
In a manner that is arranged like the fingers of a hand, typically spreading from a common point.

Examples

Arms spread digitately
Some sea stars have arms that spread digitately from a central disc.
The leaves are arranged digitately at the top of the stem.
The veins in the palm leaf branch digitately, resembling outstretched fingers.
