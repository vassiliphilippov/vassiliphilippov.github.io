unprivileged

adjective
lacking advantages
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈprɪvəlɪdʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈprɪvɪlɪdʒd / (BrE)
not having special rights, advantages, or immunities that others may have

Examples

Unprivileged children
The charity provides scholarships for unprivileged students who cannot afford tuition.
Many unprivileged communities lack access to clean water and healthcare.
He dedicated his career to supporting unprivileged youth in urban areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disadvantaged - lacking basic resources or opportunities
  • antonym: privileged - having special rights or advantages
