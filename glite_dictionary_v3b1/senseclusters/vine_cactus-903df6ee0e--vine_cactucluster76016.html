vine cactus

noun
desert shrub with spiny branches and red flowers
8.6
Pronunciation: / vaɪn ˈkæktəs / (AmE), / vaɪn ˈkæktəs / (BrE)
A desert shrub of the southwestern United States and Mexico, with slender, spiny branches that produce foliage and clusters of red flowers after the rainy season.

Examples

The vine cactus blooms
After the rains, the vine cactus blooms with clusters of vibrant red flowers.
Many hikers are fascinated by the unique appearance of the vine cactus in the Arizona desert.
The spiny branches of the vine cactus make it well adapted to arid environments.
