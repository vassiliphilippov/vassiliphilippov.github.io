detectably

adverb
in a noticeable way
Pronunciation: / dɪˈtɛktəbli / (AmE), / dɪˈtɛktəbli / (BrE)
in a way that can be noticed, discovered, or identified

Examples

Not detectably present
The medication was not detectably present in his blood after 24 hours.
No detectably harmful substances were found in the air samples.
The difference in taste was detectably subtle to most people.

Related Terms

  • antonym: invisibly - in a way that cannot be seen or detected
  • synonym: noticeably - in a way that is easy to see or recognize
