horseshoeing

noun
fitting horseshoes
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrsˌʃuɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːʃuːɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or skill of fitting horses with horseshoes to protect their hooves

Examples

Horseshoeing a horse
He took a course in horseshoeing before working at the stable.
Proper horseshoeing is essential for a horse's health and performance.
The art of horseshoeing has been practiced for centuries by skilled farriers.
