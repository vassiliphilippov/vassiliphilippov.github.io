left-handed

adjective
using or for the left hand
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛftˈhændɪd / (AmE), / ˌlɛftˈhændɪd / (BrE)
using the left hand by preference, or designed to be used with the left hand

Examples

Left-handed scissors
My brother is left-handed and always uses special golf clubs.
She bought a pair of left-handed scissors for her art class.
These left-handed desks are designed for students who write with their left hand.

Related Terms

  • antonym: right-handed - using or intended for the right hand
