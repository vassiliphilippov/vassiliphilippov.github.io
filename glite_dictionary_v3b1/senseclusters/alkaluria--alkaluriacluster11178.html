alkaluria

noun
alkaline urine condition
Pronunciation: / ˌælkəˈlʊriə / (AmE), / ˌalkəˈljʊəriə / (BrE)
A medical condition in which urine, normally slightly acidic, becomes alkaline.

Examples

Alkaluria condition
Dietary changes can sometimes lead to temporary alkaluria in healthy individuals.
Long-term alkaluria may indicate an underlying metabolic disorder.
The doctor diagnosed alkaluria after analyzing the patient's urine sample.
