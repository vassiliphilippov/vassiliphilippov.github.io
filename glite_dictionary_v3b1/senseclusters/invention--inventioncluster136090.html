invention

noun
the act of creating something new
1.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɛnʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɛnʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of creating something new, especially through original thought or imagination

Examples

The company's latest invention has revolutionized the tech industry.
Thomas Edison is famous for his invention of the electric light bulb.
