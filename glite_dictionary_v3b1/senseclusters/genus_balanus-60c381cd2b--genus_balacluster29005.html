genus Balanus

noun
barnacle genus; type of Balanidae
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs bəˈleɪ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs bəˈleɪ.nəs / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Balanidae, comprising certain barnacle species found on rocks and ship hulls.
