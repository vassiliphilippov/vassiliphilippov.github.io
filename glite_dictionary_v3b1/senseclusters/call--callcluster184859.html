call

noun
divine or inner urge to pursue a vocation
6.1 7.1
Pronunciation: / kɔl / (AmE), / kɔːl / (BrE)
a strong inner urge or sense of duty, often believed to come from a divine source, to follow a particular path or career

Examples

A call to serve
He waited patiently, hoping one day to receive the call to priesthood.
Many people feel a call to serve their communities through religious work.
She experienced a call to become a teacher, believing it was her true purpose in life.
