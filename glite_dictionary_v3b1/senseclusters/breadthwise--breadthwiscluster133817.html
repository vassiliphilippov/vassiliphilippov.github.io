Synonyms

  • broadwise
    8.6 0.00/m
    broadwise means across the width of something in ordinary speech, while breadthwise names the same direction more directly.
  • breadthways
    8.1 0.00/m
    breadthways describes movement across the shorter side of an object, while breadthwise names that movement across the width.
  • crosswise
    4.1 0.00/m
    crosswise means in a line that cuts across, often oddly or unexpectedly, while breadthwise means simply across the width.
  • transversely
    6.7 0.1/m
    transversely means at right angles to the main line and is more formal, while breadthwise simply means across the width.
