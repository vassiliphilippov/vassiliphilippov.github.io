reader

noun
a book for students learning to read
Pronunciation: / ˈriːdər / (AmE), / ˈriːdə / (BrE)
a book, especially one in a graded series, used by students learning to read or improve reading skills.

Examples

Beginner reader
Children begin with a simple reader before progressing to more complex books.
The school provides a new reader for each level of its literacy program.
The teacher assigned chapters from the second-grade reader for homework.

Related Terms

  • synonym: primer - an elementary textbook for teaching children to read
