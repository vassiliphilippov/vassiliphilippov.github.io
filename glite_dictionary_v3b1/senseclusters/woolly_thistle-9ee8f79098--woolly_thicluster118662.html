woolly thistle

noun
a white hairy thistle with pale purple flowers
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈwʊ.lɪ ˈθɪs.əl/ /; / /ˈwʊ.liː ˈθɪs.əl/ (AmE), /ˈwʊ.lɪ ˈθɪs.əl/ /; / /ˈwʊ.liː ˈθɪs.əl/ (BrE)
