unmarked

adjective
not noticed or recognized
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmɑrkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɑːkt / (BrE)
not noticed, observed, celebrated, or recognized; not marked or acknowledged in any way.

Examples

Unmarked success
His achievements did not go unmarked by the community.
The anniversary passed unmarked, with no celebration or mention.
The kindness he showed never went unmarked by those around him.

Related Terms

  • antonym: marked - noticed or given attention; recognized or acknowledged
