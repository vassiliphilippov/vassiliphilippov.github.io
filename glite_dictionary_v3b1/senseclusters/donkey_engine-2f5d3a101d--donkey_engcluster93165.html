donkey engine

noun
small auxiliary engine, especially on ships
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑŋki ˌɛndʒɪn / (AmE), / ˈdɒŋki ˌɛndʒɪn / (BrE)
A small auxiliary engine, especially one used on ships to operate machinery such as a windlass.

Examples

Ship donkey engine
The crew relied on the donkey engine for operating various mechanical gear.
The old steamship still uses a donkey engine to raise the anchor.
They repaired the donkey engine before lifting the cargo onto the deck.

Related Terms

  • synonym: auxiliary engine - an engine used to support the main engine, especially on ships
