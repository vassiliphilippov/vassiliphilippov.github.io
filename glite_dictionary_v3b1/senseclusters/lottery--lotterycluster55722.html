lottery

noun
game of chance with random prizes
2.3 7.64/m
Pronunciation: / Ààl…ëÀê.t…ô.ri / (AmE), / Ààl…ít.…ôr.i / (BrE)
A game or method of raising money in which people buy numbered tickets and prizes are given to the holders of numbers drawn at random.

Examples

Lottery ticket win
He bought a lottery ticket hoping to win the million-dollar jackpot.
Many people view life as a kind of lottery, full of unpredictable events.
The school raised money for charity by organizing a lottery.

Synonyms

  • raffle
    3.3 0.20/m
    raffle sells numbered tickets to raise funds at local events, while lottery often runs larger public draws for money prizes.
  • lotto
    4.4 1.07/m
    lotto is a number game where players mark patterns on cards, while lottery is a wider ticket draw used to give prizes or raise money.

Related Terms

  • synonym: raffle - a lottery in which the prizes are goods rather than money
  • synonym: sweepstake - a type of lottery where each ticket holder wins the whole prize
