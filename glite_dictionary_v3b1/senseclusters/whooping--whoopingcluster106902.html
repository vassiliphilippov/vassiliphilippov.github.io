whooping

noun
loud excited shout
Pronunciation: / ˈwuːpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwuːpɪŋ / (BrE)
a loud, enthusiastic shout or cry, often expressing excitement or joy

Examples

Loud whooping
The stadium erupted in whooping as the home team scored the winning goal.
We could hear the whooping of children playing in the park nearby.
After the announcement, there was loud whooping from the excited audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: applause - clapping to show approval or enjoyment
  • synonym: cheering - shouting in support or celebration
  • antonym: silence - complete absence of sound
