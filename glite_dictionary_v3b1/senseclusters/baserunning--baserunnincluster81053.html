baserunning

adjective
baseball base running
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪsˌrʌnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsˌrʌnɪŋ / (BrE)
relating to the act of running between bases in baseball or softball.

Examples

Baserunning skills
The coach praised her baserunning skills after she scored from second base.
Good baserunning can make a big difference in close baseball games.
He attended a clinic focused on improving baserunning techniques for young players.

Related Terms

  • synonym: base-stealing - the act of advancing to the next base while the pitcher is delivering the ball
