unbearded

adjective
without beard or tuft
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɪrdɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɪədɪd / (BrE)
not having a beard or beard-like feature (such as facial hair on a person, or a tuft on a plant or animal)

Examples

The unbearded iris
The unbearded iris is popular among gardeners for its smooth petals.
Some species of wheat are classified as unbearded due to the absence of bristles on their ears.
The young man remained unbearded well into his twenties.
The unbearded youth looked younger than his classmates.
In the portrait, the king appears unbearded, which was unusual for that era.
He remained unbearded throughout his life, preferring a clean-shaven look.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bearded - having a beard or beard-like tuft of hairs
  • synonym: clean-shaven - having no facial hair because it has been shaved off
