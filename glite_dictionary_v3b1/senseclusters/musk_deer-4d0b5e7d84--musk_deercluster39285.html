musk deer

noun
small Asiatic deer valued for musk
5.1
Pronunciation: / mʌsk dɪr / (AmE), / mʌsk dɪə / (BrE)
a small, heavy-limbed deer found in upland regions of central Asia, with males secreting a musk valued for use in perfumes

Examples

The musk deer male
Illegal hunting for their scent glands has endangered the musk deer population in some regions of Asia.
In the wild, musk deer are shy animals that prefer mountainous forests.
The musk deer is known for the musky scent produced by the male during mating season.
