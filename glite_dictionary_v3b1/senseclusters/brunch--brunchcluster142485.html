brunch

noun
meal combining breakfast and lunch
Pronunciation: / brʌntʃ / (AmE), / brʌntʃ / (BrE)
a meal that combines breakfast and lunch, usually eaten in the late morning or early afternoon

Examples

A big brunch
On Sundays, we like to have brunch at our favorite café.
She invited her friends over for brunch to celebrate her birthday.
The hotel served a delicious brunch for the guests after the wedding ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breakfast - the first meal of the day, typically eaten in the morning
  • synonym: lunch - a meal eaten in the middle of the day
