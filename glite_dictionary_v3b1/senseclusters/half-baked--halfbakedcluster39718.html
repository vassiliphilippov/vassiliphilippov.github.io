half-baked

adjective
not fully cooked
Pronunciation: / hæfˈbeɪkt / (AmE), / hɑːfˈbeɪkt / (BrE)
(of food) not cooked thoroughly or completely; partially baked.

Examples

Half-baked bread
She complained that her pizza arrived half-baked and cold.
The bread was still half-baked and doughy in the middle.
You should never eat half-baked chicken because it could be unsafe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: undercooked - not cooked thoroughly
  • antonym: well-cooked - cooked thoroughly
