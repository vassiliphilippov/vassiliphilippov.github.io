congested

adjective
overcrowded or blocked
Pronunciation: / kənˈdʒɛstɪd / (AmE), / kənˈdʒɛstɪd / (BrE)
too full or crowded, making movement or flow difficult

Examples

Congested streets
The city center was congested with traffic after the concert ended.
Her nose felt congested because of her cold.
During the holidays, airports often become extremely congested with travelers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blocked - unable to move or flow because of an obstruction
  • antonym: clear - free from obstruction or crowding
  • synonym: crowded - full of people or things, leaving little space
