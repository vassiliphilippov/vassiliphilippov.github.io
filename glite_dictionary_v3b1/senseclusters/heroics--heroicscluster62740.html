heroics

noun
gaming: heroic-difficulty content
8.3
Pronunciation: /hɪ.ˈroʊ.ɪks/ (AmE), /hɪ.ˈrəʊ.ɪks/ (BrE)
(informal, gaming) high-difficulty game content (such as dungeons, missions, or encounters) labeled 'heroic', often played for better rewards or challenge.

Examples

Online heroics
The guild ran several heroics to gear up for the raid.
She prefers doing heroics because they drop better equipment than normal dungeons.
Players formed a party to tackle the weekly heroics challenge and earn rare rewards.
