cliffhanging

adjective
at cliff's edge
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪfˌhæŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklɪfˌhæŋɪŋ / (BrE)
positioned at the edge of a cliff or projecting over a steep drop, often appearing dramatic or precarious

Examples

Cliffhanging view
The ancient castle had a cliffhanging view that attracted many tourists.
They built their house in a cliffhanging spot overlooking the valley below.
Photographers love the cliffhanging location for its breathtaking scenery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overhanging - extending or hanging over something below
  • synonym: perched - situated in a high or precarious position
