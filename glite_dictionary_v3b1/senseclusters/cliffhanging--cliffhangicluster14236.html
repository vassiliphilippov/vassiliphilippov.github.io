cliffhanging

adjective
at cliff's edge
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪfˌhæŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklɪfˌhæŋɪŋ / (BrE)
positioned at the edge of a cliff or projecting over a steep drop, often appearing dramatic or precarious
