prehearing

adjective
before a hearing
6.6
Pronunciation: /priː.ˈhɪr.ɪŋ/ /; / /prə.ˈhɪr.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /priː.ˈhɪə.rɪŋ/ /; / /prə.ˈhɪə.rɪŋ/ (BrE)

Examples
