subeditor

noun
assistant editor in publishing
5.5 5.2
Pronunciation: /səb.ˈe.dɪ.tər/ /; / /ˈsʌb.ə.dɪ.tər/ (AmE), /səb.ˈe.dɪ.tə/ /; / /ˈsʌb.ə.dɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a person whose job is to check and prepare written material for publication as an assistant to the main editor, especially by correcting errors and ensuring accuracy and consistency

Examples

The subeditor checks articles
After the subeditor reviewed the article, she returned it to the main editor for approval.
He started his career as a subeditor at a local newspaper.
The subeditor corrected several factual errors before the magazine went to print.
