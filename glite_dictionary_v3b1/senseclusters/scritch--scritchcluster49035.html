scritch

verb
to gently scratch
8.8
Pronunciation: /skrɪtʃ/ (AmE), /skrɪtʃ/ (BrE)

Examples

To scritch a cat

She loves to scritch her cat under the chin every morning.
He gently scritched the rabbit's back until it relaxed.
