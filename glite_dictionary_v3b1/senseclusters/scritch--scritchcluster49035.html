scritch

verb
to gently scratch
8.8
Pronunciation: / skrɪtʃ / (AmE), / skrɪtʃ / (BrE)
to gently scratch or rub, especially an animal's fur or skin, often to give comfort or pleasure

Examples

To scritch a cat
The dog wagged its tail happily when I scritched behind its ears.
She loves to scritch her cat under the chin every morning.
He gently scritched the rabbit's back until it relaxed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pet - to stroke or touch an animal gently
  • synonym: scratch - to rub or scrape lightly with the nails or a sharp object
