pugging

noun
sound-deadening layer
9.3
Pronunciation: /ˈpʌg.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈpʌg.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
a layer or mass of clay or similar material used to fill gaps, reduce sound, or for making pottery or repairs

Examples

Clay pugging layer
The old house had pugging made of clay between the wooden floorboards.
To improve soundproofing, the contractor installed pugging in the ceiling.
Removing the damaged pugging revealed the original beams underneath.
The potter used pugging to create a smooth base for the new vase.
After mixing the old shards, the studio produced fresh pugging for repairs.
High-quality pugging is essential for making durable ceramic pieces.
