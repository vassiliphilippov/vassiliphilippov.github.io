unlocker

noun
one who enables potential
5.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɑːkər / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɒkə / (BrE)
a person or thing that helps someone discover, develop, or make use of their abilities, ideas, or potential

Examples

Unlocker of talent
As a teacher, she saw herself as an unlocker of her students' creativity and confidence.
The new software acts as an unlocker for hidden features in the device.
He became an unlocker of talent, helping young athletes reach their full potential.

Related Terms

  • synonym: facilitator - a person who makes an action or process easier
  • synonym: mentor - an experienced person who guides and advises someone
