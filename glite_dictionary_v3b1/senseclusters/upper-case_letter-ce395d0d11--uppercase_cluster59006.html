Synonyms

  • capital letter
    1.0 0.29/m
    a capital letter is the common name for the large first letter of names and sentences, while an upper-case letter sounds slightly more technical.
  • uppercase
    2.2 0.34/m
    uppercase refers to large letters used for names or emphasis, while an upper-case letter is the written hyphenated form learners often see.
  • capital
    1.2 3.26/m
    capital can mean a large first letter or a city, while an upper-case letter only means the large letter used in names and sentences.
  • majuscule
    8.4 0.05/m
    majuscule is a technical term for large letters in typography, while an upper-case letter is the common name friendly to learners.
