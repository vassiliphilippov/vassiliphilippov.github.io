budget for

verb
allocate funds in a budget
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌdʒət fɔr / (AmE), / ˈbʌdʒɪt fə / (BrE)
to plan the amount of money that will be needed for a particular purpose and include it in a budget

Examples

To budget for expenses
It’s important to budget for unexpected expenses when planning your holiday.
They had not budgeted for such rapid growth in demand.
We always budget for training and development in our department.
