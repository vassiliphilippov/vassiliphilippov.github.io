remains

noun
excavated archaeological relics
1.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmeɪnz / (AmE), / rɪˈmeɪnz / (BrE)
objects or materials from the past, especially those that have been discovered through excavation and are of archaeological or historical interest.

Examples

Ancient remains
Archaeologists carefully catalogued the remains found at the historic dig site.
Some of the earliest human remains in the region date back over 5,000 years.
The ancient remains were uncovered during the construction of the new railway line.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artifact - an object made by a human being, typically an item of cultural or historical interest
  • synonym: relic - an object surviving from an earlier time, usually of historical or sentimental interest
