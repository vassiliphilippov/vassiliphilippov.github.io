stewing

noun
cooking food slowly in liquid

the act or process of cooking food slowly in liquid over low heat after first bringing it to a boil

Examples

Stewing meat slowly
Stewing is an ideal method for preparing tougher cuts of meat.
The recipe calls for several hours of stewing to develop the flavors.
Vegetarians often use stewing to make hearty dishes with beans and vegetables.

Synonyms

  • stew
    1.3 0.90/m
    stew means to cook food slowly in liquid for a long time, while stewing names the process that follows initial boiling and slow simmering.
  • simmering
    3.2 0.20/m
    simmering keeps liquid just below boiling and cooks gently, while stewing normally starts with a boil then cooks slowly at low heat.

  • synonym: braising - cooking food by browning it first, then simmering it in liquid in a covered pot
