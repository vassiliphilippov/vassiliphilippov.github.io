azido radical

noun
chemical group N₃⁻ from hydrazoic acid
Pronunciation: / əˈziːdoʊ ˈræd.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / əˈziːdəʊ ˈræd.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
a univalent chemical group (N₃⁻) derived from hydrazoic acid, consisting of three nitrogen atoms
