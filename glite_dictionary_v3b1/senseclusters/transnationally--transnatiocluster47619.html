transnationally

adverb
across countries
7.2
Pronunciation: / trænzˈnæʃənəli / (AmE), / trænzˈnæʃənəli / (BrE)
in a manner that involves or extends across multiple countries

Examples

Work transnationally
The organization works transnationally to address global environmental issues.
Many companies now operate transnationally to reach a wider market.
Research on climate change is often conducted transnationally by teams from different countries.

Related Terms

  • antonym: domestically - within a single country
  • synonym: internationally - involving or occurring between two or more countries
