huggy

adjective
tight-fitting clothing
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌɡi / (AmE), / ˈhʌɡi / (BrE)
fitting closely to the body in a way that is tight or form-fitting

Examples

Huggy sweater
She wore a huggy sweater that showed off her figure.
The huggy jeans were comfortable but very tight around the legs.
He prefers huggy shirts when exercising because they do not get in the way.

Related Terms

  • antonym: loose - not fitting tightly or closely
  • synonym: snug - fitting closely and comfortably
