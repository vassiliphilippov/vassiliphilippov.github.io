fringed gecko

noun
a gliding gecko with body fringe
Pronunciation: / frɪndʒd ˈɡɛkoʊ / (AmE), / frɪndʒd ˈɡɛkəʊ / (BrE)
a gecko with membranous expansions along the sides of its body, limbs, and tail that enable it to glide short distances

Examples

A fringed gecko gliding
Scientists observed a fringed gecko making controlled glides to escape predators in the rainforest.
The fringed gecko uses the flaps on its skin to glide between tree branches.
Unlike most lizards, the fringed gecko is adapted for moving through the air as well as on trees.
