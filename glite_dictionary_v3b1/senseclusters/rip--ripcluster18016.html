rip

verb
tear or be torn violently
Pronunciation: / rɪp / (AmE), / rɪp / (BrE)
to tear or be torn quickly and forcefully; to pull apart or split open violently

Examples

To rip a shirt
He ripped the envelope open to read the letter inside.
She accidentally ripped her shirt on a nail while climbing the fence.
The strong winds ripped the flag from its pole during the storm.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mend - to repair something that is torn or broken
  • synonym: tear - to pull apart or damage by pulling; to rip
