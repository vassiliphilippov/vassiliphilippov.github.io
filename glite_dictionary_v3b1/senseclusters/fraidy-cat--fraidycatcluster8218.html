fraidy-cat

noun
easily frightened person
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪdiˌkæt / (AmE), / ˈfreɪdiˌkæt / (BrE)
a person, especially a child, who is easily frightened or scared

Examples

A fraidy-cat child
Don't be such a fraidy-cat; it's just a harmless spider.
As a child, I was a real fraidy-cat when it came to thunderstorms.
Everyone called him a fraidy-cat for not watching the scary movie.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brave - showing courage and not easily frightened
  • synonym: coward - a person who lacks courage
  • synonym: scaredy-cat - a person who is easily scared
