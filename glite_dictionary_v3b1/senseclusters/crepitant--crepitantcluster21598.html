crepitant

adjective
crackling sound
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛpɪtənt / (AmE), / ˈkrɛpɪtənt / (BrE)
making a crackling or rattling sound, especially as heard in the lungs or joints in medical examination

Examples

Crepitant lung sound
The doctor detected a crepitant noise when listening to the patient's chest with a stethoscope.
A crepitant sensation was felt in the joint after the injury.
The nurse reported a crepitant sound during the respiratory examination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crackling - making a series of short, sharp noises
  • antonym: silent - making no sound
