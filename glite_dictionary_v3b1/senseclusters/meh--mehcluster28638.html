meh

interjection
indifference expression
Pronunciation: / mɛ / (AmE), / mɛ / (BrE)
used to express indifference, lack of enthusiasm, or that something is unremarkable

Examples

A simple meh
When I asked how the movie was, he just shrugged and said meh.
The food at the new restaurant was meh, nothing special at all.
She felt meh about going to the party and decided to stay home instead.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whatever - used to show lack of interest or concern
  • antonym: wow - used to express surprise or admiration
