upswelling

noun
raised surface area
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈʌp.swel.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈʌp.swel.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
a raised area or lump on a surface, often caused by pressure or natural forces such as frost.

Examples

Frost upswelling
The heavy frost caused an upswelling in the pavement, making the road uneven.
After the tree roots grew under the sidewalk, an upswelling appeared and created a tripping hazard.
Engineers inspected the bridge for any sign of upswelling that might indicate structural problems.
