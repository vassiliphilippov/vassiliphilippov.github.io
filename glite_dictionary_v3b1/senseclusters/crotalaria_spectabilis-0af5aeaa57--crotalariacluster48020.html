Crotalaria spectabilis

noun
species of erect subshrub with rattle pods
9.9
Pronunciation: / kroʊˌtæləriə spɛkˈteɪbɪlɪs / (AmE), / krəʊˌtæləriə spɛkˈteɪbɪlɪs / (BrE)
A species of erect subshrub native to India, having purple-tinted flowers and inflated pods with seeds that rattle when ripe.

Examples

Crotalaria spectabilis plant
