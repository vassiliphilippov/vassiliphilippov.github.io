epilating wax

noun
wax for hair removal from skin
9.1 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈep.ɪ.leɪ.tɪŋ wæks/ (AmE), /ˈep.ɪ.leɪ.tɪŋ wæks/ (BrE)
a cosmetic product made of resins and waxes, applied hot to the skin and pulled away after cooling to remove unwanted hair

Examples

Natural epilating wax
Before your appointment, ask if they use a natural epilating wax to avoid irritation.
It's important to test the temperature of epilating wax before applying it to sensitive skin.
Many beauty salons use epilating wax for effective hair removal on legs and arms.
