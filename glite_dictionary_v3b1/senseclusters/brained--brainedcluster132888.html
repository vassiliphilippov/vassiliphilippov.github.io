brained

adjective
having a certain brain
Pronunciation: / breɪnd / (AmE), / breɪnd / (BrE)
having a brain of a particular type or quality, often used in compounds to describe intelligence or thinking style

Examples

Sharp-brained student
He is often called quick-brained because he solves problems so fast.
The movie features a group of sharp-brained detectives working together.
Some people think of themselves as right-brained or left-brained depending on their skills.
