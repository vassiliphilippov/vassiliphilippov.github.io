prom

noun
formal school dance/gathering
2.5 8.07/m
Pronunciation: / prÉ‘m / (AmE), / prÉ’m / (BrE)
a formal dance or gathering held by a high school or college class at the end of the academic year, especially for older students

Examples

High school prom
He invited his best friend to be his date for the prom.
Many students look forward to prom as the highlight of their senior year.
She wore a beautiful blue dress to the prom last night.

Synonyms

    6.5 0.05/m
