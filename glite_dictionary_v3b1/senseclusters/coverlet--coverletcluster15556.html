coverlet

noun
decorative quilted bedspread
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌvərlət / (AmE), / ˈkʌvəlɪt / (BrE)
a decorative bedspread, typically quilted, used as a covering for a bed

Examples

A coverlet on bed
A light blue coverlet added a touch of color to the guest room.
She spread the new coverlet over the bed to complete the room's look.
The old-fashioned coverlet was hand-stitched by her grandmother.

Related Terms
