Indian salad

noun
herb with edible leaves in the US
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən ˈsæləd / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈsaləd / (BrE)
a perennial herb with white flowers, sometimes used for its edible leaves as greens in the southeastern United States
