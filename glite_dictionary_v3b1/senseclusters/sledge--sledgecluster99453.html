sledge

noun
vehicle for travel over snow
2.7 0.20/m

Examples

A sledge ride
In remote villages, people often travel by sledge when the roads are covered in deep snow.
She watched as the sledge glided smoothly over the frozen lake, pulled by a pair of strong huskies.
The team loaded the supplies onto the sledge before setting off across the snow.

Synonyms

    a sled is often small and used for sliding or being pulled, while a sledge usually refers to a larger travel vehicle pulled by animals.
