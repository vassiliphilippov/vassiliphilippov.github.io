zoophile

noun
animal lover
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈzoʊəˌfaɪl / (AmE), / ˈzəʊəˌfaɪl / (BrE)
a person who has a strong affection for and cares deeply about animals

Examples

Animal-loving zoophile
As a dedicated zoophile, he spent his weekends volunteering at the animal shelter.
The club welcomes any zoophile who wants to help protect wildlife.
Her reputation as a zoophile grew after she rescued several stray cats in her neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • antonym: animal hater - a person who dislikes or avoids animals
  • synonym: animal lover - a person who loves and cares for animals
