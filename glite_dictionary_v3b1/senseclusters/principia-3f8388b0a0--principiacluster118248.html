Principia

proper noun
Newton's scientific work
9.6
Pronunciation: / prɪnˈsɪpiə / (AmE), / prɪnˈsɪpiə / (BrE)
the commonly used short title for Isaac Newton's foundational scientific work, Philosophiae Naturalis Principia Mathematica, on motion and gravity
