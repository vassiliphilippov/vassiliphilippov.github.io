rundle

noun
step or spoke of a ladder or wheel
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌndəl / (AmE), / ˈrʌndəl / (BrE)
one of the crosspieces forming a step of a ladder or the spokes of a wheel

Examples

A rundle step
Each rundle must be securely fixed to ensure the ladder is safe to use.

The old wooden ladder was missing a rundle near the top.
