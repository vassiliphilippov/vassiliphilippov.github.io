streeter

noun
person on the street
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈstriːtər / (AmE), / ˈstriːtə / (BrE)
a person who is walking or present on the street, especially as a pedestrian

Examples

A streeter crossing
The streeter waited patiently at the crosswalk for the light to change.
A local news crew interviewed a streeter about the new traffic regulations downtown.
During the parade, every streeter stopped to watch the colorful floats go by.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bystander - a person who is present at an event but not involved
  • synonym: pedestrian - a person walking along a road or in a city
