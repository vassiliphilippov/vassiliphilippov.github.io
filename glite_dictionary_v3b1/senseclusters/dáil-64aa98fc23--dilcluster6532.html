Dáil

noun
Irish parliamentary session
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɔɪl / (AmE), / dɔɪl / (BrE)
a meeting or session of Dáil Éireann, the lower house of the Irish parliament

Examples

Dáil session
The new law was debated in the Dáil before being passed.
She watched the live broadcast of the Dáil discussing education reform.
After the election, the Dáil convened to select a new prime minister.

Related Terms

  • synonym: congress - a formal meeting or assembly for discussion
  • synonym: parliament - the national legislative body of a country
