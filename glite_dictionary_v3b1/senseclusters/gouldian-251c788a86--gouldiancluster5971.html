Gouldian

noun
colorful Australian finch
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡuːldiən / (AmE), / ˈɡuːldiən / (BrE)
a small, brightly colored Australian finch (Erythrura gouldiae) known for its vivid plumage

Examples

A Gouldian finch
The pet store had a beautiful Gouldian with a bright purple chest and yellow belly.
Many bird enthusiasts admire the vivid colors of the Gouldian in their aviaries.
A Gouldian can be recognized by its striking red, black, or yellow head.

Related Terms

  • synonym: canary - a small, yellow songbird often kept as a pet
  • synonym: finch - a small songbird with a short, thick beak
