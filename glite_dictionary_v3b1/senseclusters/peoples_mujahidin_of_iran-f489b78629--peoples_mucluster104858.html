People's Mujahidin of Iran

proper noun
Iranian political organization with Marxist-Islamist ideology
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːpəlz muːˈdʒɑːhedin əv aɪˈrɑːn / (AmE), / ˈpiːplz mjuːˈhædɪn əv ɪˈrɑːn / (BrE)
a political organization founded in the 1960s in Iran, originally opposing the Shah and now opposed to the Islamic fundamentalist regime, noted for its Marxist-Islamist ideology and armed actions
