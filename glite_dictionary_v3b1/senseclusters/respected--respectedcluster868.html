respected

adjective
admired or esteemed
1.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈspɛktɪd / (AmE), / rɪˈspɛktɪd / (BrE)
admired or held in high esteem by others; regarded with respect.

Examples

A respected person
He became a respected member of the community through years of dedicated service.
She is a respected leader in her field, known for her integrity and expertise.
The school is highly respected for its rigorous academic programs.
