hackle

verb
to make hairs stand up
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhækəl / (AmE), / ˈhækəl / (BrE)
to cause the hairs or feathers on an animal or person to stand up, usually due to fear, anger, or alarm

Examples

To hackle a cat's fur
The cat began hackling when it saw the dog approach.
A sudden noise in the dark made his skin hackle with fear.
When threatened, some birds hackle their feathers to appear larger.
