donut patrol

noun
humorous term for trivial supervision or police on duty
8.5 7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊnət pəˌtroʊl / (AmE), / ˈdəʊnət pəˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
A humorous or informal term for a group tasked with overseeing something trivial, especially used to refer jokingly to police officers on duty.

Examples

The donut patrol joke
After midnight, the security guards referred to themselves as the donut patrol to lighten the mood.
He laughed when his friends called the neighborhood watch the donut patrol.
The officers didn't mind being nicknamed the donut patrol during their overnight shift.
