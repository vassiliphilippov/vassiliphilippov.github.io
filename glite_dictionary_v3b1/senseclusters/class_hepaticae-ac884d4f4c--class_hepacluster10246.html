class Hepaticae

noun
taxonomic class of liverworts
9.9
Pronunciation: / klæs hɛˈpætɪsiː / (AmE), / klɑːs hɛˈpætɪsiː / (BrE)
A major class of non-vascular plants known as liverworts, which includes the orders Anthocerotales, Jungermanniales, Marchantiales, and Sphaerocarpales.
