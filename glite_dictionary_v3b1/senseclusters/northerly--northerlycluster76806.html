northerly

noun
wind blowing from the north
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrðɚli / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðəli / (BrE)
A wind that blows from the north.

Examples

A strong northerly
A strong northerly brought cooler temperatures to the coast overnight.
The sailors waited for a northerly before setting out to sea.
Yesterday, a brisk northerly swept across the fields, clearing the morning mist.

Related Terms

  • antonym: southerly - a wind that blows from the south
