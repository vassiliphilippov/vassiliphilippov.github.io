rah

interjection
shout of encouragement
8.5
Pronunciation: / rɑː / (AmE), / rɑː / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express excitement, encouragement, or approval, especially at sports events

Examples

Crowd shouts rah
The crowd erupted with a loud rah as the home team scored the winning point.
She shouted rah to cheer on her friends during the race.
With every goal, the fans responded with an enthusiastic rah from the stands.

  • synonym: hooray - an exclamation of joy or approval
