clumsy

adjective
awkward to handle due to shape
2.2 1.19/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆklÊŒmzi / (AmE), / ËˆklÊŒmzi / (BrE)
difficult to handle or manage, especially because of awkward shape or size.

Examples

Clumsy box
A clumsy mug with an uneven handle kept slipping out of his grasp.
Carrying the clumsy bundle up the stairs was more difficult than she had expected.
The large, clumsy box wouldn't fit through the doorway without turning it sideways.
