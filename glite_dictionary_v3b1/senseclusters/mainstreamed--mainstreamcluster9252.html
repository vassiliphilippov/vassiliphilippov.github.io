mainstreamed

adjective
placed in regular school classes
4.6 7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪnˌstrimd / (AmE), / ˈmeɪnstriːmd / (BrE)
(of a person, especially one with a disability) placed in regular school classes rather than in special education settings.

Examples

Being mainstreamed in school
Advocates argue that being mainstreamed helps foster inclusion and social growth.
Parents were pleased to see their daughter mainstreamed into the regular classroom environment.
The school has recently mainstreamed several students who previously attended special classes.
