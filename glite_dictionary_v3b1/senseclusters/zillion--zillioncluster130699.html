zillion

noun
a very large indefinite number (hyperbolic)
Pronunciation: / ˈzɪljən / (AmE), / ˈzɪlɪən /; / ˈzɪljən / (BrE)
an extremely large but indefinite number, used for emphasis

Examples

A zillion times
I must have told you a zillion times not to leave your keys there.
She has a zillion things to do before the wedding.
There are a zillion stars visible in the night sky from here.
It feels like I've answered this question a zillion times already.
She gave me a zillion excuses for being late to the meeting.
There are a zillion reasons to visit Paris in the spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bazillion - an extremely large, indefinite number (informal synonym)
  • degree lower: billion - A thousand million (in the US and modern British usage)
  • synonym: gazillion - an extremely large, indefinite number (informal synonym)
  • degree lower: million - A very large number; one thousand thousand
