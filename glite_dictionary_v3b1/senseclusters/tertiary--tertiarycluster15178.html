tertiary

noun
third level or stage
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜːrʃieri / (AmE), / ˈtɜːʃəri / (BrE)
a third level, stage, or item in a sequence or system, especially in education or classification.

Examples

Tertiary education
In the new curriculum, students can choose from several tertiaries after completing secondary school.
The report analyzed data from all three tertiaries in the healthcare system.
Many countries invest heavily in their tertiaries to improve higher education outcomes.
