photochromic

adjective
changes color with light
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtəˈkroʊmɪk / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəˈkrəʊmɪk / (BrE)
describing a material or substance that changes color when exposed to light

Examples

Photochromic lenses darken
These photochromic lenses automatically darken when you step outside into the sunlight.
The scientist tested a photochromic dye that changed from clear to blue under UV light.
Many modern sunglasses use photochromic technology to adapt to different lighting conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: photosensitive - reacting to light, especially by changing in some way
