bifunctional

adjective
having two functions
7.5
Pronunciation: /baɪ.ˈfʌŋk.ʃən.əl/ /; / /bɪ.ˈfʌŋk.ʃən.əl/ (AmE), /baɪ.ˈfʌŋk.ʃən.əl/ /; / /bɪ.ˈfʌŋk.ʃən.əl/ (BrE)
having two functional groups or reactive sites within a single molecule, especially in chemistry

Examples

Bifunctional enzyme
A bifunctional enzyme can catalyze two different types of chemical reactions.
Scientists designed a bifunctional drug that targets two pathways in the body.
The bifunctional molecule played a key role in the new polymer's structure.
