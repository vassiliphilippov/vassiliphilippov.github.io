herd

verb
move or keep animals as a group
Pronunciation: / hɝd / (AmE), / hɜːd / (BrE)
to keep, move, or drive animals, especially as a group

Examples

To herd sheep
Ranchers often herd cattle across great distances during the season.
She learned to herd goats as a child on her family's farm.
The farmer will herd the sheep into the barn before sunset.
