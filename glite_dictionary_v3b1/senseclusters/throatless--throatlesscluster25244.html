throatless

adjective
without a throat
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈθroʊtləs / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊtləs / (BrE)
lacking a throat or having no visible throat

Examples

Throatless statue
The ancient statue appeared throatless, with its head merging directly into the torso.
Some birds are nearly throatless, making their songs sound very different.
The artist sculpted a throatless figure to create a sense of mystery.
