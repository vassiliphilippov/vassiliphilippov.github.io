conspiratorial

adjective
suggestive of conspiracy
4.3
Pronunciation: / kənˌspɪr.iˈtɔr.i.əl / (AmE), / kənˌspɪr.iˈtɔː.ri.əl / (BrE)
Suggesting or characteristic of a secret plan by a group to do something unlawful or harmful.

Examples

Conspiratorial tone
His conspiratorial tone made everyone at the table uneasy.
She lowered her voice to a conspiratorial whisper so no one else would hear.
They exchanged conspiratorial glances before slipping out of the room.
