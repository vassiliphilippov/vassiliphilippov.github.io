overdoer

noun
person who exaggerates
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈduːər / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈduːə / (BrE)
a person who does something too much or to an excessive degree

Examples

An overdoer on stage
The director warned the actor not to become an overdoer on stage.
As an overdoer, she often added unnecessary details to her presentations.
Critics said the comedian was an overdoer who relied too much on exaggerated gestures.
As an overdoer, she often stayed late at the office to finish extra tasks.
The doctor warned the overdoer to slow down and take more breaks.
Many overdoers struggle to find a healthy balance between work and rest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exaggerator - a person who makes things seem more extreme than they are
  • antonym: moderate - a person who avoids extremes in behavior or style
  • synonym: show-off - a person who tries to attract attention by behaving in an exaggerated way
  • antonym: slacker - a person who avoids work or effort
  • synonym: workaholic - a person who works excessively and compulsively
