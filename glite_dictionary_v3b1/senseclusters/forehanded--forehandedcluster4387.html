forehanded

adjective
racket stroke with palm facing forward
7.2 5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌfɔrˈhændɪd / (AmE), / ˌfɔːˈhændɪd / (BrE)
(of a racket stroke) made with the palm of the hand facing in the direction of the stroke, as in tennis or other racket sports.

Examples

A strong forehanded shot can put opponents on the defensive.

She practiced her forehanded swings for hours before the tournament.
