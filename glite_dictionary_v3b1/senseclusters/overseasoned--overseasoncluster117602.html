overseasoned

adjective
too much seasoning
4.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Àåo äv…ôrÀàsiÀêz…ônd / (AmE), / Àå…ô äv…ôÀàsiÀêz…ônd / (BrE)

Examples
