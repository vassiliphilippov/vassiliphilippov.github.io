halfer

noun
half-dollar coin
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhælfər / (AmE), / ˈhɑːfə / (BrE)
an informal name for a coin worth half a dollar, especially the U.S. fifty-cent piece

Examples

A silver halfer
He showed me a rare halfer from the 1960s that he found in his grandfather's collection.
You don't see a halfer in circulation very often these days.
She paid for the snack with a shiny halfer she had saved.
