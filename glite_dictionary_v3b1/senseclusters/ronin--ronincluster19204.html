ronin

noun
student retaking exams
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊniːn / (AmE), / ˈrəʊniːn / (BrE)
in Japan, a student who has failed a school or university entrance exam and spends time studying to try again

Examples

A student ronin
After failing the university entrance exam, he became a ronin and studied for another year.
Many ronin attend special schools to prepare for their next attempt at the exams.
Being a ronin can be stressful, but some students use the time to improve their skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: repeat student - a student who repeats a year or exam
