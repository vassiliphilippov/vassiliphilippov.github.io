indorse

verb
guarantee as meeting a standard
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɔrs / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɔːs / (BrE)
to guarantee that something meets a particular standard or quality; to certify officially

Examples

To indorse a product
He refused to indorse any claims about the safety of the equipment.
Her signature served to indorse the certificate as genuine.
The association will only indorse products that meet strict quality guidelines.
