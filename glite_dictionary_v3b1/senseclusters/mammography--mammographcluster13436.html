mammography

noun
breast X-ray screening procedure
4.7 3.5
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈmɑːr.ɡrə.fiː/ (AmE), /mə.ˈmɑː.ɡrə.fiː/ (BrE)
a medical procedure that uses X-rays to detect tumors or abnormalities in breast tissue

Examples

Annual mammography screening
Annual mammography is recommended for women over the age of 40.
Her doctor suggested a mammography after she noticed a lump in her breast.
The hospital has introduced new digital mammography equipment for early detection of breast cancer.
