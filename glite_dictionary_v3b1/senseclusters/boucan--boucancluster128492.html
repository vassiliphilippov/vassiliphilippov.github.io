boucan

noun
meat-smoking frame
Pronunciation: / buːˈkæn / (AmE), / buːˈkæn / (BrE)
a wooden frame used for smoking meat or the smokehouse where meat is smoked, especially in the Caribbean

Examples

A boucan for smoking
The pirates prepared their meat on a boucan before setting sail.
Early settlers built a boucan to preserve game for the long journey.
The aroma from the boucan filled the air as the meat slowly smoked.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barbecue - a structure or device for cooking food over an open fire
  • synonym: smokehouse - a building where meat or fish is cured with smoke
