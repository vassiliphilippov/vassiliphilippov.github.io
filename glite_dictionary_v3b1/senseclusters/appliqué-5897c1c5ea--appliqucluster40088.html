appliqué

verb
to sew fabric designs
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌæplɪˈkeɪ / (AmE), / ˌæplɪˈkeɪ / (BrE)
to sew a piece of fabric onto another fabric to create a decorative design or pattern

Examples

To appliqué a patch
She decided to appliqué a butterfly onto her daughter's dress for a personal touch.
The artist appliquéd colorful shapes onto the wall hanging to create a vibrant scene.
You can appliqué different fabrics to make your quilt more interesting and unique.
She spent the afternoon appliquéing bright flowers onto her daughter's dress.
Many traditional quilts are made by carefully appliquéing shapes onto a plain background.
He learned to appliqué fabric animals onto pillow covers in his sewing class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embroider - to decorate fabric with needlework designs
  • antonym: plain - not decorated or patterned
  • antonym: remove - to take something away from fabric
