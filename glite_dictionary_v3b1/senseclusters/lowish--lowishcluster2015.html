lowish

adjective
slightly sad
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈləʊɪʃ / (BrE)
feeling somewhat sad or in slightly low spirits

Examples

Feeling lowish
She was feeling a bit lowish after hearing the disappointing news.
On rainy days, I sometimes get lowish and prefer to stay indoors.
He seemed lowish this morning, so I asked if everything was okay.
