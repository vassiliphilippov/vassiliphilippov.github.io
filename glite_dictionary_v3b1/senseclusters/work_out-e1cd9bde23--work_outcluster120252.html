work out

verb
devise or figure out (a plan, idea, solution)

Synonyms

  • figure out

  • puzzle out
    4.4 0.32/m
    puzzle out is working hard to make sense of something confusing, while work out is more general and can include planning.
  • reason out
    4.8 0.25/m
    reason out means solve or decide by logical thinking step by step, while work out can be less strictly logical.
  • solve
    1.3 80/m
    solve means find an answer to a problem or question, often formally, while work out can also mean plan or understand.
  • work
    1.0 13/m
    work means finding a solution or understanding something in general, while work out often adds planning or careful calculation.
