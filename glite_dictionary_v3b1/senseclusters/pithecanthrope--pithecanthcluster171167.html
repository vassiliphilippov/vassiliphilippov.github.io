pithecanthrope

noun
ape-like ancestor
Pronunciation: / pɪˈθikənˌθroʊp / (AmE), / pɪˈθiːkənˌθrəʊp / (BrE)
an extinct primate or early human ancestor with ape-like features

Examples

An extinct pithecanthrope
The discovery of a pithecanthrope skull provided new insights into human evolution.
Scientists debated whether the fossil truly belonged to a pithecanthrope or another early species.
In the museum, a reconstructed pithecanthrope stands next to modern human skeletons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ape-man - an early human ancestor with both ape and human characteristics
  • synonym: hominid - a member of the biological family that includes humans and their ancestors
