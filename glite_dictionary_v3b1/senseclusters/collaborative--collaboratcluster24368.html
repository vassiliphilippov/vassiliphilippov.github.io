collaborative

adjective
involving cooperation or teamwork
Pronunciation: / kəˈlæbɚˌeɪtɪv / (AmE), / kəˈlæb(ə)rətɪv / (BrE)
involving or produced by two or more people or groups working together

Examples

Collaborative project
Advances in medicine often result from collaborative efforts across different countries.
She prefers a collaborative approach to problem-solving, involving everyone's perspective.
The two departments undertook a collaborative project to streamline the production process.
