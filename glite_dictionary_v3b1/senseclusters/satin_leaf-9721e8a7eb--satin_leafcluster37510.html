satin leaf

noun
tropical American tree with glossy leaves
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsætən liːf / (AmE), / ˈsætɪn liːf / (BrE)
A tropical American tree with dark, hard, heavy wood and small plum-like purple fruit, known for its glossy foliage.
