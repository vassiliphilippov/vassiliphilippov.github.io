ogival

adjective
cumulative frequency curve
9.9
Pronunciation: / oʊˈdʒaɪvəl / (AmE), / əʊˈdʒaɪvəl / (BrE)
relating to an ogive, especially describing a cumulative frequency curve or graph in statistics that shows totals up to successive values.

Examples

Ogival curve graph
The students plotted an ogival curve to represent the cumulative distribution of test scores.
An ogival graph is useful for showing how totals build up over time in a dataset.
Researchers used an ogival line to display the cumulative rainfall throughout the year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cumulative - increasing by successive additions
