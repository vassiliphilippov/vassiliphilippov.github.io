(figurative, business/tech) the maximum rate of growth or expansion that a company, product, or system can sustain over time before growth levels off or stabilizes.

Examples

The platform's user base reached terminal velocity, with signups and engagement plateauing despite heavy marketing.
Investors cheered when revenue climbed quickly, then worried as the startup approached terminal velocity.
