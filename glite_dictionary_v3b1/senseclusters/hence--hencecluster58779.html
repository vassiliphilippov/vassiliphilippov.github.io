From this time; after the present time.

Examples

A year hence
A month hence, the new law will be enforced.
The effects of this decision will be felt generations hence.
They plan to expand the business five years hence.

Synonyms

  • henceforth
    6.7 1.31/m
    henceforth is formal and marks actions continuing from now on, while hence is shorter and fits general written or spoken time reference.
  • henceforward
    7.5 0.08/m
    henceforward is used to say starting now and continuing into the future, while hence is shorter and more flexible in use.
  • hereafter
    5.3 1.46/m
    hereafter is common in documents to mean from this time onward, while hence is more general and appears in everyday speech.
