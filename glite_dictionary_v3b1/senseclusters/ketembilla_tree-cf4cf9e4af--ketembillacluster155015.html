ketembilla tree

noun
tropical shrub with edible maroon-purple fruit
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɪˈtɛmbɪlə tri / (AmE), / kɪˈtɛmbɪlə triː / (BrE)
a small, spiny shrub native to Sri Lanka and India, cultivated for its maroon-purple fruit with sweet, gooseberry-like pulp

Examples
