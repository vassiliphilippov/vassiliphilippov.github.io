fingerling

noun
a young or small fish
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡərlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡəlɪŋ / (BrE)
a young or small fish, especially one that is newly hatched and about the size of a human finger

Examples

Small fingerling fish
After a few months, the fingerlings will be large enough to move into the main pond.
The hatchery released thousands of fingerlings into the river to boost the fish population.
The lake teems with fingerling trout in the early spring.
