sangreal

noun
Holy Grail (archaic)
Pronunciation: / sænˈɡriːəl / (AmE), / sænˈɡriːəl / (BrE)
an old term for the Holy Grail, the legendary cup sought in medieval legend, especially in Arthurian tales

Examples

The sangreal quest
In ancient stories, knights embarked on quests to find the sangreal.
Legends say the sangreal holds miraculous powers for those who discover it.
The search for the sangreal is a central theme in many Arthurian romances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Holy Grail - the legendary cup said to have been used by Jesus at the Last Supper
