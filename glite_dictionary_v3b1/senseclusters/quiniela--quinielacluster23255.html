quiniela

noun
numbers lottery game
9.9
Pronunciation: / kiːniˈɛlə / (AmE), / kiːniˈɛlə / (BrE)
a lottery game, especially popular in Spanish-speaking countries, where players pick numbers and win if those numbers are drawn

Examples

A quiniela ticket
Every Friday, she buys a ticket for the quiniela hoping to win some extra money.
The quiniela is a popular way for people to try their luck in Argentina.
He checked the results of the quiniela as part of his morning routine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lottery - a game of chance in which people buy tickets to win prizes
  • synonym: raffle - a game in which people buy numbered tickets and prizes are given to those whose numbers are drawn
