lyric poem

noun
short, songlike poem expressing emotion
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪrɪk ˈpoʊəm / (AmE), / ˈlɪrɪk ˈpəʊəm / (BrE)
a short poem expressing personal feelings or thoughts, often with a songlike quality

Examples

A lyric poem
In class, we learned to distinguish between a lyric poem and a narrative poem.
She recited a beautiful lyric poem about the arrival of spring.
The lyric poem conveyed the poet’s deep longing and sorrow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: epic poem - a lengthy narrative poem, often about heroic deeds
  • synonym: ode - a type of lyric poem, usually serious and formal in tone
  • synonym: sonnet - a 14-line poem, often a type of lyric poem
