impotently

adverb
in a powerless or helpless way
Pronunciation: / ˈɪm.pə.tənt.li / (AmE), / ˈɪm.pət.ənt.li / (BrE)
In a way that lacks power or ability to act; helplessly or powerlessly.

Examples

Yell impotently
He yelled impotently at the retreating thief, frustrated by his own inaction.
She waited impotently as events unfolded beyond her control.
The guards looked impotently at the advancing fire, unable to stop it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: helplessly - in a helpless manner
