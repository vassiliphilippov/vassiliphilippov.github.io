Brumaire

noun
month in the French Revolutionary calendar
Pronunciation: / bruːˈmɛr / (AmE), / bruːˈmɛə / (BrE)
the second month of the French Revolutionary calendar, corresponding approximately to October and November, known as the month of mist

Examples

The month Brumaire
During Brumaire, the fields were often shrouded in thick mist each morning.
Historians often refer to the events of Brumaire when discussing the end of the French Revolution.
The coup of 18 Brumaire marked the rise of Napoleon to power.
