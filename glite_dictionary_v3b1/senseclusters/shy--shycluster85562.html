shy

adjective
just short of a number or amount
6.8
Pronunciation: / ʃaɪ / (AmE), / ʃaɪ / (BrE)
(of a number or amount) less than or just short of a particular number, often by a small amount.

Examples

Shy of a number
Her savings are a few dollars shy of what she needs for the trip.
The committee lacked one vote shy of a majority.
We finished the race just one shy of the top ten.

Related Terms

  • synonym: short - being less than the required or expected amount
  • synonym: under - below a certain amount or number
