illumination

noun
decorative lighting
4.2
Pronunciation: / ɪˌluːməˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌljuːmɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
bright lighting or decorative lights used to create a festive or special atmosphere

Examples

Festival illumination
The festival featured beautiful illumination along the riverbanks, attracting many visitors each night.
During the celebration, the main square was filled with colorful illumination and lively music.
They admired the illumination of the old castle, which glowed brightly against the night sky.

Related Terms

  • antonym: darkness - the absence of light
  • synonym: lighting - the arrangement or use of lights for effect
