rudapithecus

noun
extinct Miocene genus of hominoids
Pronunciation: / ˌruːdəˈpɪθɪkəs / (AmE), / ˌruːdəˈpɪθɪkəs / (BrE)
an extinct genus of fossil hominoids from northern central Hungary, dating to the late Miocene epoch

Examples

Fossil rudapithecus
Fossils of rudapithecus have provided insights into the evolution of European apes.
Researchers studied the jaw structure of rudapithecus to understand its diet and lifestyle.
The discovery of rudapithecus in Hungary dates back to the late Miocene period.
