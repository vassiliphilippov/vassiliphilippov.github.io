nonqualifying

adjective
not meeting requirements
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈkwɑlɪˌfaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈkwɒlɪˌfaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
not meeting the requirements to qualify or not counting toward qualification or selection

Examples

A nonqualifying round
Because of his low score, he was placed in a nonqualifying group for the next round.
The athlete's time was considered nonqualifying due to a technical violation.
Only the top ten finishers advance, so everyone else is in a nonqualifying position.
The race was a nonqualifying event, so the results did not affect the championship standings.
He participated in a nonqualifying round to gain more experience before the main competition.
Because it was a nonqualifying match, the players were more relaxed and experimental.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ineligible - not allowed or qualified to do or receive something
  • antonym: qualifying - meeting the necessary requirements or standards
