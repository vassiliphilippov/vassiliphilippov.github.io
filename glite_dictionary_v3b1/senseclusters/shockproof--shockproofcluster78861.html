shockproof

adjective
prevents electric shocks
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑkˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈʃɒkˌpruːf / (BrE)
designed to prevent a person from receiving an electric shock when using it

Examples

Shockproof blender
The new blender is fitted with a shockproof casing to protect users from electric shocks.
Always check that your power tools are shockproof before using them in wet conditions.
The laboratory uses shockproof equipment to ensure the safety of all staff members.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dangerous - likely to cause harm or injury
  • synonym: insulated - protected to prevent the passage of electricity
