psych up

verb
mentally prepare oneself
5.4
Pronunciation: / saɪk ʌp / (AmE), / saɪk ʌp / (BrE)
to prepare oneself mentally for something, often by becoming excited or enthusiastic

Examples

To psych up before a game
I need to psych up before giving my big presentation.
She psyched up herself by listening to her favorite songs.
The team tried to psych up in the locker room before the game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: motivate - to stimulate someone's interest or enthusiasm
