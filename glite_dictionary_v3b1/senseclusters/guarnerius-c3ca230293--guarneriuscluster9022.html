Guarnerius

noun
violin made by Guarneri family
Pronunciation: / ɡwɑːrˈnɪəriəs / (AmE), / ɡwɑːˈnɪəriəs / (BrE)
A violin made by a member of the Guarneri family, renowned for their craftsmanship in Cremona, Italy.

Examples

A Guarnerius violin
Experts authenticated the violin as a genuine Guarnerius crafted in Cremona.
She performed the concerto on a rare Guarnerius from the eighteenth century.
The priceless Guarnerius was displayed in a secure glass case at the museum.
