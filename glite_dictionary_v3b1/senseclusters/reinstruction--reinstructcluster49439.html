reinstruction

noun
repeated training
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˈstrʌkʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈstrʌkʃən / (BrE)
the act of teaching or training someone again to refresh, update, or clarify their knowledge or skills

Examples

Staff reinstruction session

The training program includes reinstruction sessions for employees who need extra support.
Sometimes reinstruction is necessary to ensure everyone understands the safety procedures.
