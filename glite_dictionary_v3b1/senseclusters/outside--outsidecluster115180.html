outside

noun
outer part or surface
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtsaɪd /; / ˈaʊt.saɪd / (AmE), / ˈaʊtsaɪd /; / ˈaʊt.saɪd / (BrE)
the external side or surface of something; the outer part away from the inside

Examples

The outside of the box
From the outside, the building looks quite ordinary.
Please leave your shoes on the outside before entering.
She painted the outside of the house a bright yellow.
Frost had formed on the outside of the car windows overnight.
He painted only the outside of the house, leaving the interior untouched.
The outside of the box was decorated with bright wrapping paper.
