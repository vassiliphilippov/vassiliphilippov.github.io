ooooh

noun
sound of admiration
Pronunciation: / uː / (AmE), / uː / (BrE)
a vocal sound or exclamation made to express surprise, admiration, or pleasure

Examples

A loud ooooh
A loud ooooh filled the room when the magician revealed his final trick.
There was a collective ooooh as the fireworks lit up the night sky.
The children let out an excited ooooh when they saw the birthday cake.

  • antonym: boo - a vocal sound expressing disapproval or surprise
