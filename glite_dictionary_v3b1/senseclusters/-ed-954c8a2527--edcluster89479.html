-ed

x
past tense suffix
1.0
Pronunciation: / d / (AmE), / d / (BrE)
a suffix added to regular verbs to form the past tense and past participle

Examples

-ed for past tense
She walked to school every day last year.
The cake was baked by my grandmother for my birthday.
He finished his homework before dinner.

Related Terms

  • antonym: -ing - suffix forming the present participle of verbs
