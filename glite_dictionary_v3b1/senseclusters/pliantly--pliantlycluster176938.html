pliantly

adverb
with flexibility
Pronunciation: / ˈplaɪəntli / (AmE), / ˈplaɪəntli / (BrE)
in a way that is easily bent, shaped, or influenced; with flexibility or willingness to yield

Examples

Bend pliantly in wind
The young tree bent pliantly in the strong wind without breaking.
He pliantly followed the coach's instructions, eager to improve his skills.
The fabric draped pliantly over the edge of the table, creating soft folds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flexibly - in a way that can be easily bent or adapted
  • antonym: stubbornly - in a way that refuses to change or yield
