untradeable

adjective
cannot be traded
6.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtreɪdəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈtreɪdəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be bought, sold, or exchanged with others

Examples

An untradeable item
The rare sword is untradeable, so players cannot exchange it with each other.
Some digital assets are untradeable due to copyright restrictions.
Because the ticket is untradeable, you cannot sell it to someone else.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nontransferable - not able to be transferred to another person
  • antonym: tradeable - able to be bought, sold, or exchanged
