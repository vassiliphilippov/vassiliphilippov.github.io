terminus

noun
end point of a transport route
4.5 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈtɜːr.mɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈtɜː.mɪ.nəs/ (BrE)
the final station or end point of a railway, bus, or other transport route

Examples

At the terminus, all passengers must leave the bus.
