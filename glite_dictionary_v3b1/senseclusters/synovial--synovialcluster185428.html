synovial

adjective
relating to synovia (joint fluid)
Pronunciation: / sɪˈnoʊviəl / (AmE), / sɪˈnəʊviəl / (BrE)
relating to or secreting synovia, the lubricating fluid found in certain joints and tendons of the body

Examples

Synovial fluid
A sample of synovial fluid was analyzed to diagnose the joint disorder.
Arthritis can lead to inflammation of the synovial membrane in the knee joint.
The doctor explained that synovial fluid helps reduce friction between bones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: synovia - the clear, lubricating fluid in joints
