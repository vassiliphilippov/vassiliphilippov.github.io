flat coat

noun
preliminary coat of paint or primer
7.6 4.3
Pronunciation: /flæt koʊt/ (AmE), /flæt kəʊt/ (BrE)
the first or preliminary coat of paint or primer applied to a surface before the final coats

Examples

A flat coat of paint

Before adding color, it's important to use a flat coat as a base layer.
The painter explained that the flat coat helps cover up any stains or marks on the surface.
