tackily

adverb
in a sticky manner
6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈtæk.ə.liː/ /; / /ˈtæk.ɪ.liː/ (AmE), /ˈtæk.ɪ.liː/ /; / /ˈtæk.ə.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that is slightly sticky or not fully dry to the touch.

Examples

Paint dries tackily
The paint dried tackily, leaving fingerprints whenever someone touched the wall.
After spilling juice, the floor remained tackily damp for hours.
The glue set tackily at first, so she waited before pressing the pieces together.
