extension service

noun
university outreach education program
9.2
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɛn.ʃən ˌsɜr.vɪs / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛn.ʃən ˌsɜː.vɪs / (BrE)
a program offered by colleges or universities to provide educational opportunities to people who are not enrolled as regular students

Examples

Agricultural extension service
Many rural farmers improved their techniques through advice provided by the agricultural extension service.
She attended a creative writing workshop run by the college's extension service.
The university's extension service offers evening classes in computer science for working professionals.
