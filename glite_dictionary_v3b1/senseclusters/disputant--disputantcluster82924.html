disputant

noun
person taking part in a dispute
3.6
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspjuːtənt / (AmE), / dɪˈspjuːtənt / (BrE)
a person who takes part in a dispute or argument, especially one who is skilled at or enjoys controversy

Examples

A skilled disputant
As a skilled disputant, she often challenged the views of her colleagues in meetings.
Long after the debate ended, the disputants continued their heated discussion outside.
The two disputants presented their cases clearly before the mediator.

Related Terms

  • synonym: debater - a person who argues in formal debates
  • synonym: opponent - a person who opposes or competes with another in a dispute or contest
