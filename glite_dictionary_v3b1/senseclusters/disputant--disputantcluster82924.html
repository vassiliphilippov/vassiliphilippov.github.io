disputant

noun
person taking part in a dispute
3.6 6.7
Pronunciation: /dɪs.ˈpjuː.tənt/ (AmE), /dɪs.ˈpjuː.tənt/ (BrE)

Examples

Long after the debate ended, the disputants continued their heated discussion outside.
The two disputants presented their cases clearly before the mediator.
