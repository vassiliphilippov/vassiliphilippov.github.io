ringmaster

noun
circus show director
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪŋˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
the person who introduces performers and directs the acts in a circus

Examples

As a child, she dreamed of becoming a ringmaster and leading the circus parade.
The ringmaster introduced the acrobats with a flourish.
The ringmaster wore a bright red tailcoat and top hat.
