storer

noun
one who stores
4.2 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààst…îÀêr…ôr / (AmE), / Ààst…îÀêr…ô / (BrE)
a person or thing that stores or keeps items for future use

Examples

A storer of goods
The warehouse acts as a storer of goods before they are shipped to stores.
A refrigerator is an essential storer of perishable food in every home.
The squirrel is a natural storer of nuts for winter.

Synonyms

  • stockpiler
    5.3 0.01/m
    stockpiler gathers and keeps very large supplies for future use, while storer is any person or thing that stores items.
  • depositary
    8.1 0.20/m
    depositary is a person or place trusted to hold things safely, while storer simply keeps items for later use.
  • depository
    5.9 1.38/m
    depository holds items in a place or facility for safekeeping, while storer can be a person or thing that stores goods.
