storer

noun
one who stores
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔːrər / (AmE), / ˈstɔːrə / (BrE)
a person or thing that stores or keeps items for future use

Examples

A storer of goods
The warehouse acts as a storer of goods before they are shipped to stores.
A refrigerator is an essential storer of perishable food in every home.
The squirrel is a natural storer of nuts for winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hoarder - a person who collects and keeps a large amount of something
  • synonym: keeper - a person who looks after or is responsible for something
