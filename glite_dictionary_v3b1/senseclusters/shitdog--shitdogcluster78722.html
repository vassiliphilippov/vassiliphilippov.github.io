shitdog

noun
slang for troublesome dog
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪtdɔg / (AmE), / ˈʃɪtdɒg / (BrE)
a slang and vulgar term for a dog that is messy, troublesome, or badly behaved

Examples

Noisy shitdog
My neighbor's shitdog always digs up the garden and barks all night.
He called the stray a shitdog after it knocked over the trash cans again.
Some people think their shitdog is cute, but others find it annoying.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mutt - a dog of mixed breed, sometimes used informally for a troublesome dog
  • antonym: well-behaved dog - a dog that behaves properly and does not cause trouble
