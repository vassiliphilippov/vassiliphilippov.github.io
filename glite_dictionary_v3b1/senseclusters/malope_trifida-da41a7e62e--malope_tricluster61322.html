Malope trifida

noun
Mediterranean flowering plant with purple-red flowers
Pronunciation: / məˈloʊpi traɪˈfaɪdə / (AmE), / məˈləʊpi trɪˈfɪdə / (BrE)
A flowering plant native to the western Mediterranean, known for its deep purple-red flowers surrounded by three large heart-shaped bracts.
