hairdressing

noun
the cutting and styling of hair
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrˌdrɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈheəˌdrɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or profession of cutting, styling, and caring for people's hair, including washing, coloring, and arranging it

Examples

Hairdressing salon
Advances in hairdressing have introduced new techniques for styling and coloring hair.
Many people visit salons for professional hairdressing before special occasions.
She decided to study hairdressing at the local vocational school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barbering - the cutting and grooming of hair, especially men's hair
  • synonym: hairstyling - the practice or process of arranging hair
