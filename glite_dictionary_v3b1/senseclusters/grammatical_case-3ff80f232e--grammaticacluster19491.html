Examples

Grammatical case system
English retains some traces of grammatical case in its pronoun system, such as 'he' and 'him'.
Many languages, such as Russian and Latin, use grammatical case to show the role of nouns in sentences.
The concept of grammatical case can be challenging for learners whose native language does not use cases.
