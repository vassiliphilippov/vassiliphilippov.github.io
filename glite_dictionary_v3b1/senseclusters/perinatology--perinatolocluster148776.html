perinatology

noun
medical study of pregnancy and newborn care
8.2 8.0
Pronunciation: /pər.ɪ.nə.ˈtɑː.lə.dʒiː/ /; / /pɪr.ɪ.nə.ˈtɑː.lə.dʒiː/ (AmE), /pə.rɪ.nə.ˈtɒl.ə.dʒiː/ /; / /pɪ.rɪ.nə.ˈtɒl.ə.dʒiː/ (BrE)
the branch of medicine that deals with the care of the mother and fetus during the period shortly before and after birth, especially focusing on high-risk pregnancies and newborns

Examples

Perinatology care
Advances in perinatology have greatly improved outcomes for both mothers and newborns.
She decided to specialize in perinatology after witnessing the complexities of high-risk pregnancies.
The hospital's perinatology department collaborates closely with neonatologists and obstetricians.
