respirometry

noun
measurement of respiration
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛspɪˈrɑːmətri / (AmE), / ˌrɛspɪˈrɒmətri / (BrE)
the scientific measurement of respiration rates in living organisms

Examples

Advanced respirometry devices
The laboratory is equipped with advanced respirometry devices for studying animal metabolism.
Researchers used respirometry to monitor the oxygen consumption of the plants under different conditions.
Accurate respirometry is essential for understanding how diseases affect breathing in patients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calorimetry - the measurement of heat produced by chemical reactions or physical changes
  • synonym: spirometry - the measurement of the air capacity of the lungs
