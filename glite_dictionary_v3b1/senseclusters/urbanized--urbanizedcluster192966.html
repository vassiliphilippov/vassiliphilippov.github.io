urbanized

adjective
having urban characteristics
Pronunciation: / ˈɝːbəˌnaɪzd /; / ˈɜːrbəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈɜːbənaɪzd / (BrE)
having been made more like a city in character or appearance, especially through development or growth.

Examples

An urbanized area
Much of the country has become increasingly urbanized over the past century.
She grew up in a highly urbanized area with little access to nature.
Wildlife habitats are shrinking as urbanized regions expand outward.
Conservation efforts are challenging in highly urbanised areas.
Much of the region has become increasingly urbanised over the past few decades.
The urbanised zone stretches far beyond the city’s official boundaries.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rural - relating to the countryside rather than towns or cities
  • synonym: urban - relating to a city or town
  • synonym: urbanized - having adopted or been given the characteristics of a city or town (US spelling)
