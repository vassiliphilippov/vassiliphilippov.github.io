whipping

noun
punishment by beating with a whip or strap
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of beating a person or animal with a whip, strap, or similar object as a form of punishment

Examples

Public whipping

Historical accounts describe slaves being subjected to cruel whipping as discipline.
The prisoner received a severe whipping for trying to escape.
