main beam headlamp

A car headlamp that provides a bright, centre-weighted distribution of light primarily for use when driving without oncoming traffic, as it is liable to dazzle other drivers.

Examples

When driving late at night on empty country roads, I always switch on the main beam headlamp for better visibility.
