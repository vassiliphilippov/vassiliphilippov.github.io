resecure

verb
to fasten again
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌriːsɪˈkjʊr / (AmE), / ˌriːsɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
to make something safe or fastened again after it has become loose, insecure, opened, or loosened

Examples

The technician needed to resecure the cables to prevent any accidents.
After checking the package, please resecure the tape to keep it closed.
The technician had to resecure the wires that had come loose during transport.
Remember to resecure the gate after you leave the yard.
