calesa

noun
Filipino horse carriage
Pronunciation: / kəˈleɪsə / (AmE), / kəˈleɪsə / (BrE)
a light, two-wheeled horse-drawn carriage, especially used for transportation in the Philippines

Examples

A calesa ride
The couple took a romantic ride in a calesa around the historic district.
In Manila, you can still see a calesa carrying passengers through busy streets.
The guide explained the history of the calesa to the interested tourists.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carriage - a vehicle with wheels that is pulled by a horse
  • synonym: rickshaw - a light two-wheeled vehicle pulled by a person or bicycle
