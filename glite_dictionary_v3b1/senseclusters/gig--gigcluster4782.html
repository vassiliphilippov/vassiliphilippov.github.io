gig

noun
musicians' performance booking
5.0
Pronunciation: / ɡɪɡ / (AmE), / ɡɪɡ / (BrE)

Examples

A music gig
After months of practice, their first gig was a huge success.
She travels from city to city, playing gigs at local venues.
The band landed a gig at the famous jazz club downtown.
