franchiser

noun
business rights grantor
5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈfræn.tʃaɪ.zər/ (AmE), /ˈfræn.tʃaɪ.zə/ (BrE)
a person or company that grants another party the right to operate a business using its brand and business model

Examples

The franchiser grants rights
The franchiser provided detailed training to all new franchisees before they opened their stores.
Every franchiser must ensure that their brand standards are maintained across all locations.
The franchiser collects a percentage of sales from each franchisee as part of the agreement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: franchisee - a person or company that is granted the right to operate a business under a franchiser's brand
  • synonym: licensor - a person or company that gives permission to use something under a license
