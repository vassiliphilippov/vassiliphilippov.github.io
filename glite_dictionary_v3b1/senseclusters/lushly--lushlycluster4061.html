lushly

adverb
in a rich manner
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌʃli / (AmE), / ˈlʌʃli / (BrE)
in a rich, healthy, and attractive way, especially referring to growth or appearance

Examples

Lushly covered hills
The hills were lushly covered with wildflowers after the spring rains.
The film was lushly photographed, with vibrant colors and beautiful scenery.
Her garden is always lushly planted, overflowing with greenery and blooms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: luxuriantly - in a way that is rich and abundant
  • antonym: sparsely - in a way that is thin or not dense
