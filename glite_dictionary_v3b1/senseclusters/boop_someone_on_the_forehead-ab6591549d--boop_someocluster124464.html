boop someone on the forehead

verb
tap someone lightly on the forehead
8.6
Pronunciation: / buːp ˈsʌmwʌn ɑn ðə ˈfɔːrhɛd / (AmE), / buːp ˈsʌmwən ɒn ðə ˈfɔːhɛd / (BrE)
to tap someone lightly and playfully on the forehead, often to be affectionate or silly
