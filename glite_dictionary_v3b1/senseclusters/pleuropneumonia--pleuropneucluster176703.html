pleuropneumonia

noun
inflammation of lungs and pleura
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌplʊrəˌnuːˈmoʊniə / (AmE), / ˌplʊərəʊnjuːˈməʊniə / (BrE)
A medical condition in which both the pleura (lining of the lungs) and the lungs themselves are inflamed, combining aspects of pleurisy and pneumonia.

Examples

Severe pleuropneumonia infection
Effective treatment of pleuropneumonia requires addressing both the lung infection and the inflammation of the pleural lining.
Patients with pleuropneumonia often experience severe chest pain and difficulty breathing.
The veterinarian diagnosed the cow with pleuropneumonia after observing its symptoms and reviewing the test results.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pleurisy - inflammation of the pleura
  • synonym: pneumonia - inflammation of the lungs
