solfeggio

noun
singing exercise using musical syllables
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: / sɒlˈfɛdʒ.i.oʊ /; / sɔlˈfɛdʒioʊ / (AmE), / sɒlˈfɛdʒ.i.əʊ /; / sɒlˈfɛdʒiəʊ / (BrE)
a method or exercise in music where you sing scales or melodies using syllables like do, re, mi, fa, sol, la, and ti to practice pitch and sight-singing
