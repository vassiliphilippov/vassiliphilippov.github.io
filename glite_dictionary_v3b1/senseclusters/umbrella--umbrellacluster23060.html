umbrella

noun
formation of planes providing aerial protection
7.3
Pronunciation: / əmˈbrɛlə / (AmE), / ʌmˈbrɛlə / (BrE)
a protective formation of military aircraft maintained over ground operations or targets to defend against attack

Examples

Air umbrella defense
Allied forces maintained an umbrella of fighter planes over the advancing troops.
Commanders requested a stronger air umbrella before commencing the ground assault.
The city was protected by an umbrella of bombers and interceptors throughout the campaign.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shield - anything that protects or defends
