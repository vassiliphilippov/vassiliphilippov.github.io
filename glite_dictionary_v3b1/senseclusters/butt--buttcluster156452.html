butt

verb
strike or push with the head or horns
Pronunciation: / bʌt / (AmE), / bʌt / (BrE)
to strike, push, or thrust against something or someone with the head or horns

Examples

To butt heads
She accidentally butted her friend while turning around quickly.
The angry ram butted the fence until a piece broke off.
The goat kept butting the tree with its horns in search of food.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ram - to strike or drive against something forcefully
