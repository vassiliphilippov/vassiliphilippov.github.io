all day long

adverb
for the whole day
Pronunciation: / ɔl deɪ lɔŋ / (AmE), / ɔːl deɪ lɒŋ / (BrE)
throughout the entire day; during every part of the day.

Examples

All day long listening
He could listen to his favorite songs all day long without getting bored.
She waited for the package all day long, but it never arrived.
The children played outside all day long until the sun set.

Related Terms

  • synonym: all night long - throughout the entire night
