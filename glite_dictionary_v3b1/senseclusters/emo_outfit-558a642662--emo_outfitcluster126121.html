emo outfit

noun
style of clothing linked to emo subculture
Pronunciation: / ˈiːmoʊ ˈaʊtfɪt / (AmE), / ˈiːməʊ ˈaʊtfɪt / (BrE)
A style of clothing associated with the emo subculture, typically featuring dark colors, band T-shirts, skinny jeans, and expressive accessories.

Examples

A emo outfit style
He spent hours putting together the perfect emo outfit for the music festival.
Her emo outfit featured black skinny jeans and a band hoodie.
Many teens express their individuality through an emo outfit.
