culturist

noun
culture promoter
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌltʃərɪst / (AmE), / ˈkʌltʃərɪst / (BrE)
a person who promotes, supports, or is interested in culture or physical culture

Examples

Art culturist
The culturist organized art workshops for the community.
As a dedicated culturist, she encouraged young people to appreciate music and literature.
The local culturist promoted healthy living and regular exercise in the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bodybuilder - a person who develops their muscles through exercise
  • synonym: culturalist - a person who studies or values culture
