forge

verb
devise or invent with effort
4.7 4.95/m
Pronunciation: / f…îrd í / (AmE), / f…îÀêd í / (BrE)
to create or devise (an idea, plan, explanation, theory, or principle) after considerable thought or effort

Examples

To forge a plan
After much debate and discussion, the committee managed to forge a new strategy for the project.
Great leaders are often those who forge visionary plans in times of crisis.
The company needed to forge a compelling explanation for their sudden growth.

Synonyms

    4.8 14/m

  • formulate
    4.5 11/m

  • contrive
    7.3 1.68/m

  • invent
    3.2 9.64/m
