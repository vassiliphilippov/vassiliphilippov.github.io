family Rhizobiaceae

noun
family of rod-shaped bacteria
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli raɪˌzoʊbiˈeɪsiˌiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli raɪˌzəʊbiːˈeɪsiːiː / (BrE)
A small family of rod-shaped bacteria, some of which are important in nitrogen fixation in plants.
