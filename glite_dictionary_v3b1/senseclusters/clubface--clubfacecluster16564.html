clubface

noun
golf club striking surface
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈklʌbˌfeɪs / (AmE), / ˈklʌbˌfeɪs / (BrE)
the front surface of a golf club that makes contact with the ball

Examples

Golf clubface angle
The angle of the clubface can greatly affect the direction of your shot.
He checked the clubface for dirt before hitting the ball.
A square clubface at impact helps the ball travel straight.

  • synonym: clubhead - the entire head of a golf club
