nonfraternal

adjective
not related to fraternities
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnfrəˈtɜrnəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnfrəˈtɜːnəl / (BrE)
not connected with or belonging to a fraternity or fraternal organization

Examples

Nonfraternal club
The club was proud of its nonfraternal status, welcoming anyone interested in volunteering.
Unlike many organizations on campus, this group is completely nonfraternal and does not require membership fees.
Their nonfraternal approach made the event accessible to all students, regardless of background.
