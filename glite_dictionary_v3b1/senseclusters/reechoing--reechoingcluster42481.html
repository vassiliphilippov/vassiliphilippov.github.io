reechoing

adjective
repeatedly echoing (of sound)
9.9
Pronunciation: / riˈɛkoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / riˈɛkəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
(of sounds) repeating or being repeated by reflection, like an echo that occurs again or repeatedly.

Examples

Reechoing laughter
Her laughter was reechoing through the old stone hall.
The reechoing footsteps filled the empty corridor, lingering after each step.
The reechoing sounds of the choir created a haunting atmosphere in the cathedral.

Related Terms

  • synonym: echoing - producing an echo or repeated sound
