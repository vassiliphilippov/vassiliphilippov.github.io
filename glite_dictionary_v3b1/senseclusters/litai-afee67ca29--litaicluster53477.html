Litai

proper noun
Greek goddesses of prayer
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtaɪ / (AmE), / ˈliːtaɪ / (BrE)
in Greek mythology, the goddesses who personify prayer and repentance

Examples

Pray to the Litai
In ancient rituals, people would call upon the Litai to seek forgiveness for their actions.
The poet described the Litai as gentle goddesses who follow in the footsteps of punishment.
Prayers to the Litai were believed to bring mercy from the gods in Greek mythology.
