watch in awe

verb
observe with admiration or amazement
4.2
Pronunciation: / wɑtʃ ɪn ɔ / (AmE), / wɒtʃ ɪn ɔː / (BrE)
to observe something with a strong feeling of admiration or amazement

Examples

To watch in awe at events
Spectators watch in awe every year as athletes break new records at the Olympic Games.
The children watched in awe as the fireworks lit up the night sky.
We watched in awe while the artist transformed a blank canvas into a beautiful painting.
