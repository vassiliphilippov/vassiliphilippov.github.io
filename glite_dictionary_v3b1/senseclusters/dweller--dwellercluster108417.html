dweller

noun
person or animal living in a place
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdwɛlɚ / (AmE), / ˈdwɛlə / (BrE)
a person or animal that lives in or at a particular place

Examples

dweller in a cave
A cave dweller left these ancient paintings on the rock walls.
Many forest dwellers rely on the river for their water supply.
The city dweller longed for a simpler life in the countryside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inhabitant - a person who lives in a particular place
  • synonym: resident - a person who lives somewhere permanently or on a long-term basis
  • antonym: visitor - a person visiting a place but not living there
