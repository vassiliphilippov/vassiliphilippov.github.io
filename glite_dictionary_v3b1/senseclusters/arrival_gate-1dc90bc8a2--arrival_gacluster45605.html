arrival gate

noun
airport gate for arriving passengers
Pronunciation: / əˈraɪvəl ɡeɪt / (AmE), / əˈraɪvəl ɡeɪt / (BrE)
A gate at an airport where passengers disembark from incoming flights.

Examples

The arrival gate area
Due to security regulations, only ticketed passengers can go beyond the arrival gate.
Please proceed to arrival gate B5 to meet the incoming flight from Paris.
The family waited anxiously at the arrival gate, eager to see their relatives.

Related Terms

  • antonym: departure gate - gate at an airport where passengers embark on flights
