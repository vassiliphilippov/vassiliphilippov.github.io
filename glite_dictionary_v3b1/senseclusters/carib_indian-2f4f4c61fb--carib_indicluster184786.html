Carib Indian

noun
a member of the Carib people
Pronunciation: / ˈkærɪb ˈɪndiən / (AmE), / ˈkærɪb ˈɪndiən / (BrE)
a member of an indigenous people of northeastern South America and the Lesser Antilles.

Examples

The Carib Indians were known for their maritime skills and knowledge of the islands.
