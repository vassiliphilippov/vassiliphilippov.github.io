beddy-bye

noun
child's term for bedtime
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdi ˌbaɪ / (AmE), / ˈbɛdi ˌbaɪ / (BrE)
A childish or affectionate term for bedtime or going to sleep.

Examples

Time for beddy-bye
After a long day at the zoo, Jimmy was fast asleep at beddy-bye.
She read her daughter a story before beddy-bye.
The twins brushed their teeth and got ready for beddy-bye.

Related Terms
