kph

noun
kilometres per hour (unit of speed)
2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈkeɪ.piː.eɪtʃ/ (AmE), /ˈkeɪ.piː.eɪtʃ/ (BrE)
An abbreviation for 'kilometres per hour'; a unit for measuring speed equal to the number of kilometres traveled in one hour.

Examples

kph speed limit
Speed limits are posted in kph in most countries outside the United States.
The car was traveling at 90 kph along the highway.
The train can reach speeds of up to 320 kph.
