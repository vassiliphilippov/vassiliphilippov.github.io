information science

noun
study of managing and using information
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.fərˈmeɪ.ʃən ˈsaɪ.əns / (AmE), / ˌɪn.fəˈmeɪ.ʃən ˈsaɪ.əns / (BrE)
