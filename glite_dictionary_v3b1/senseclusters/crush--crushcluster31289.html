crush

noun
act of pressing or squeezing with force
3.1 2.4
Pronunciation: /krʌʃ/ (AmE), /krʌʃ/ (BrE)
an act of pressing or squeezing something with force so that it breaks, loses its shape, or is destroyed

Examples

The grape crush
The grape harvest is followed by the traditional crush to make the wine.
The machines are designed for the crush of recycled materials.
There was a loud crush as the car hit the wall.
