polycephalous

adjective
having multiple leaders
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːliˈsɛfələs / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˈsɛfələs / (BrE)
having more than one head or leader, especially referring to an organization with several centers of authority.

Examples

Polycephalous organization
The company became polycephalous after appointing several directors with equal authority.
A polycephalous government can make decision-making more complex and slower.
The project failed because its polycephalous leadership led to constant disagreements.

Related Terms

  • antonym: autocratic - ruled by one person with absolute power
  • synonym: decentralized - not controlled by a single central authority
