moon carrot

noun
plant of the genus Seseli
3.7
Pronunciation: / mun ˈkærət / (AmE), / muːn ˈkærət / (BrE)
any plant of the genus Seseli, characterized by dense clusters (umbels) of small white or pink flowers and finely divided leaves

Examples

The moon carrot plant

Wild moon carrot can often be found growing in rocky, sunlit fields.
