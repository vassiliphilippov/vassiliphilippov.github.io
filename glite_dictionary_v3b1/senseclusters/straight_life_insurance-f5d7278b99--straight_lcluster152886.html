straight life insurance

noun
life insurance with fixed yearly premiums
5.6 4.9
Pronunciation: /streɪt laɪf ɪn.ˈʃʊr.əns/ (AmE), /streɪt laɪf ɪn.ˈʃʊə.rəns/ (BrE)
A type of life insurance for a fixed amount where the premium is paid annually in the same amount throughout the policyholder’s lifetime.

Examples

Buy straight life insurance
He decided to purchase straight life insurance to guarantee support for his family.
The premiums for straight life insurance remain constant year after year.
Unlike term policies, straight life insurance offers coverage for the insured's entire lifetime.

Related Terms
