twine

verb

Examples

To twine threads together
He twined together the strips of leather to make a simple rope.

The vines quickly twine around the metal fence as they grow.

Synonyms

  • entwine
    5.4 1.80/m
    entwine often describes threads or feelings wrapped closely together, while twine means twisting strands or wrapping something around objects.
  • intertwine
    5.4 4.92/m
    intertwine means two or more things twist together and form a pattern, while twine refers to twisting strands or wrapping one thing around another.
