Sturm und Drang

noun
German movement: emotional individualism
Pronunciation: / ʃtʊrm ʊnt drɑŋ / (AmE), / ʃtɜːm ʊnt draŋ / (BrE)
a German literary movement of the late 18th century emphasizing intense emotion and individualism, often resulting in turbulent or dramatic expression

Examples

Sturm und Drang movement
Critics have noted the influence of Sturm und Drang on his dramatic style.
Her first novel is filled with the Sturm und Drang characteristic of adolescent emotion.
The film captures the Sturm und Drang of the revolutionary era.
