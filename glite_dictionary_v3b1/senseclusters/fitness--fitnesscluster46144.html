fitness

noun
good physical condition; being in shape
2.4 1.5
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪt.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈfɪt.nəs/ (BrE)
The condition of being physically healthy and strong, often as a result of exercise and proper nutrition.

Examples

Physical fitness level
Athletes often have to maintain a high level of physical fitness to perform at their best.
Her daily exercise routine has greatly improved her fitness over the past year.
Many people join gyms to maintain their fitness and stay healthy.
