worst

adverb
to the highest degree of badness
1.0
Pronunciation: / wɝst / (AmE), / wɜːst / (BrE)
To the greatest degree in a negative or undesirable way; to the highest degree of badness or inferiority.

Examples

Of all the competitors, she performed the worst in the final round.
The eastern regions were the worst affected by the storm.
