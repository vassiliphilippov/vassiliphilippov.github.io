captain

noun
person commanding a merchant ship
1.9 1.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkæp.tən/ (AmE), /ˈkæp.tɪn/ (BrE)
the person in command of a ship or aircraft

Examples

Ship captain
After years of service, she finally became captain of a major cargo vessel.

After years of experience, she became the captain of one of the largest passenger airships.
Every captain must be trained to handle emergency situations at high altitudes.
The captain successfully navigated through turbulent weather to land the airship safely.
