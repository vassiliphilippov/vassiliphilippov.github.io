sausage-like

adjective
resembling a sausage
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔsɪdʒ laɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsɪdʒ laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or shaped like a sausage, especially in being cylindrical and rounded

Examples

Sausage-like shape
The caterpillar has a sausage-like body that helps it move easily along branches.
She described the strange rock as sausage-like because of its long, rounded shape.
The dough was rolled into a sausage-like form before being sliced and baked.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cylindrical - having the shape of a cylinder
  • synonym: round - having a curved, circular, or spherical shape
