Mohammedan

adjective
relating to Muhammad or Islam (archaic)
Pronunciation: / moʊˈhæmədən / (AmE), / məˈhæmɪdən / (BrE)
of or relating to Muhammad, the founder of Islam, or to the religion of Islam (now considered archaic and often inappropriate; 'Muslim' is preferred)

Examples

Mohammedan law
In the 19th century, Western writers often referred to Islamic architecture as Mohammedan architecture.
References to Mohammedan customs are found in many older academic texts.
The scholar wrote a detailed history of Mohammedan law in South Asia.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Muslim - a follower of the religion of Islam; relating to Islam or its followers
