laughing owl

noun
almost extinct New Zealand owl
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlæfɪŋ aʊl / (AmE), / ˈlɑːfɪŋ aʊl / (BrE)
a nearly extinct owl native to New Zealand, known for its distinctive laughing call; also called the 'whēkau'

Examples

The laughing owl
Early settlers described the laughing owl's call as eerie and haunting.
Several museums still display preserved laughing owls collected in the 19th century.
The laughing owl was once common throughout New Zealand but is now believed extinct.
