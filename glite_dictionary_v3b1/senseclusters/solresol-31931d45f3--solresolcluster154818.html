Solresol

noun
a constructed language based on musical notes
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑl.rɛ.soʊl / (AmE), / ˈsɒl.rɛ.sɒl / (BrE)
an artificial language developed in the 19th century, based on the seven notes of the musical scale, with vocabulary formed from combinations of these syllables and able to be communicated via sound, color, gesture, and other modes
