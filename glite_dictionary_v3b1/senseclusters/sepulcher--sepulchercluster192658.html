sepulcher

verb
to bury in a tomb
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛpəlkər / (AmE), / ˈsɛpəlkə / (BrE)
to place a dead body in a tomb or grave, especially in a formal or ceremonial way

Examples

To sepulcher a king
The pharaoh was sepulchered with treasures for the afterlife.
Legends say the hero was sepulchered beneath the ancient oak tree.
After the ceremony, the family sepulchered their loved one in the family crypt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bury - to put a dead body in the ground
  • synonym: entomb - to place a dead body in a tomb
  • antonym: exhume - to dig out a buried body
