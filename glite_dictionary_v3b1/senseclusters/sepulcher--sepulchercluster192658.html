sepulcher

verb
to bury in a tomb
9.9
Pronunciation: /sə.ˈpʌl.tʃər/ /; / /sə.ˈpʌl.kər/ (AmE), /sə.ˈpʌl.kə/ /; / /sə.ˈpʌl.tʃə/ (BrE)
