Karakum Desert

proper noun
a desert in Turkmenistan
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːrəˈkuːm ˈdɛzərt / (AmE), / ˌkærəˈkuːm ˈdɛzət / (BrE)
A large desert region in Turkmenistan, situated south of the Aral Sea, known for its arid climate and sand dunes.

Examples

The Karakum Desert region
Despite its harsh conditions, the Karakum Desert is home to several unique plant and animal species.
The Karakum Desert covers more than 70 percent of Turkmenistan's territory.
Travelers often marvel at the vast stretches of sand in the Karakum Desert.
