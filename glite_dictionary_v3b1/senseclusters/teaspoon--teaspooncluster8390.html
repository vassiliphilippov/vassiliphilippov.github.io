teaspoon

noun
small spoon for tea or measuring ingredients
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈtiː.spuːn/ /; / /ˈtiː.spən/ (AmE), /ˈtiː.spuːn/ /; / /ˈtiː.spʊn/ (BrE)
A small spoon used for stirring tea or coffee and for measuring small quantities of ingredients, typically holding about one fluid dram (approximately 5 milliliters).

Examples

A teaspoon for coffee
He added two teaspoons of sugar to his coffee.
Please hand me a teaspoon for my yogurt.
The recipe calls for a teaspoon of vanilla extract.
