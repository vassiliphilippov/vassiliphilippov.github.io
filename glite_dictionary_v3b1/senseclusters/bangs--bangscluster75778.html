bangs

noun
loud explosive noises
Pronunciation: / bæŋz / (AmE), / bæŋz / (BrE)
sudden loud noises or explosions, often repeated in quick succession

Examples

Loud bangs outside
We woke up to several loud bangs coming from the construction site next door.
The fireworks display ended with a series of bright flashes and echoing bangs.
She jumped in surprise when she heard bangs outside her window late at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: booms - deep, loud, resonant sounds
  • synonym: explosions - violent bursts with loud noise
  • antonym: silence - complete absence of sound
