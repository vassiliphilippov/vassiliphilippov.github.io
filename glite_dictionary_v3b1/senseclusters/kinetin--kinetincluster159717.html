kinetin

noun
plant growth hormone
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪnəˌtɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɪnɪtɪn / (BrE)
a plant hormone (natural or synthetic) that stimulates cell division and growth in plants, and is also used in research and skincare for its anti-aging effects
