trifold

noun
three-panel brochure
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈtraɪfəʊld / (BrE)
a brochure, pamphlet, or piece of paper folded into three sections to create six panels

Examples

A trifold pamphlet
The travel agency handed me a trifold with details about their vacation packages.
Each student designed a colorful trifold for the science fair presentation.
Please include a trifold in the welcome packet for new employees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: booklet - a small book with a few pages
  • synonym: brochure - a small booklet or pamphlet containing information
  • synonym: leaflet - a printed sheet of paper for distribution, usually folded
