attending

noun
supervising doctor
6.0
Pronunciation: / əˈtɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈtɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
a doctor who has completed all training and is responsible for supervising medical students and residents in a hospital

Examples

Attending doctor
The attending led the team during morning rounds at the hospital.
After years of training, she finally became an attending in the cardiology department.
Medical students often ask the attending questions about patient care during their rotations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consultant - a senior doctor with full responsibility for patients, especially in British English
  • antonym: resident - a doctor in training after medical school but before becoming fully qualified
