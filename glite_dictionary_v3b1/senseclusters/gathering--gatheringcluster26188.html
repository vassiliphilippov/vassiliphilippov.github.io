gathering

noun
small folds in fabric made by sewing
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæðɚɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡæðərɪŋ / (BrE)
A sewing technique in which small folds or puckers are made by pulling a thread tight along a line of stitching.

Examples

Sewing gathering
Learning how to sew a gathering is essential for making ruffles and decorative effects.
She added a gathering to the waist of the dress to give it more shape.
The sleeves were finished with a neat gathering at the cuffs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pleat - a fold in cloth made by doubling material over
