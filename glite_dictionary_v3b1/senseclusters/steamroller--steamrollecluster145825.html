steamroller

noun
overwhelming, crushing force
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈstiːmˌroʊlər / (AmE), / ˈstiːmˌrəʊlə / (BrE)
an overwhelming or unstoppable force that crushes opposition or anything in its path

Examples

A political steamroller
Her determination was a steamroller, pushing all doubts and obstacles aside.
Public outrage became a steamroller that could not be stopped.
The company moved through the market like a steamroller, crushing smaller competitors along the way.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bulldozer - a person or force that uses strong, often ruthless means to achieve an objective
  • synonym: juggernaut - an overwhelmingly powerful force or institution
