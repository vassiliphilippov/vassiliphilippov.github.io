cybercrime

noun
crime involving computers or the internet
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪbərˌkraɪm / (AmE), / ˈsʌɪbəˌkrʌɪm / (BrE)
Criminal activities carried out by means of computers or the internet, such as identity theft, fraud, or unauthorized data access.

Examples

Cybercrime activities
Authorities are developing new strategies to combat cybercrime on a global scale.
Many companies have increased their security budgets to prevent cybercrime.
Phishing is a common type of cybercrime that targets unsuspecting individuals online.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hacking - the act of gaining unauthorized access to computer systems
