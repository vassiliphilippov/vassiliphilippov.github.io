sit down

verb
assign or show someone to a seat
Pronunciation: / sɪt daʊn / (AmE), / sɪt daʊn / (BrE)
to show someone to a seat or assign a seat to someone

Examples

To sit down a guest
He politely sat down his elderly aunt next to the window.
She sat down the children at the dining table before dinner.
The usher sat down the guests in the front row.
