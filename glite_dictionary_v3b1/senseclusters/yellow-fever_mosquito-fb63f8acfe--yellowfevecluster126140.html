yellow-fever mosquito

noun
mosquito that spreads yellow fever and dengue
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌjɛloʊˈfivɚ məˈskitoʊ / (AmE), / ˌjɛləʊˈfiːvə məˈskiːtəʊ / (BrE)
a mosquito species (Aedes aegypti) that transmits yellow fever, dengue, and other viral diseases

Examples

Health officials are working to control the spread of the yellow-fever mosquito in urban areas.
Preventing bites from the yellow-fever mosquito is crucial to stopping outbreaks of yellow fever and dengue.
The yellow-fever mosquito is a primary vector of several serious viral infections in tropical regions.
