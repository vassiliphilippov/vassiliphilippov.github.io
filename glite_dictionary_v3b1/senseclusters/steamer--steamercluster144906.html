steamer

noun
cooking vessel for steaming food
4.1 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈstiː.mər/ (AmE), /ˈstiː.mə/ (BrE)
a cooking vessel used for preparing food by exposing it to steam

Examples

A vegetable steamer
I placed the vegetables in the steamer to keep their nutrients intact.
Our new bamboo steamer is perfect for making dumplings.
She cooked the fish in a steamer instead of frying it.
