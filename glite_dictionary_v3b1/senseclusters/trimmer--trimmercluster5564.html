trimmer

noun
machine for trimming timber or wood
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪmɚ /; / ˈtrɪmər / (AmE), / ˈtrɪmə / (BrE)
a device or machine used for cutting or trimming things evenly and neatly, such as hair, plants, or wood

Examples

Hair trimmer
After sawing the plank, he ran it through the trimmer to achieve a perfect length.
Modern lumber mills rely on automatic trimmers for efficient processing of timber.
The carpenter used a trimmer to smooth the edges of the wooden boards.
He bought a new trimmer to keep his beard neat and tidy.
The gardener used a trimmer to shape the bushes along the walkway.
Before the party, she quickly ran the trimmer over her hair for a clean look.
