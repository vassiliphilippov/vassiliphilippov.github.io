playthrough

noun
full game session
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪˌθruː / (AmE), / ˈpleɪˌθruː / (BrE)
a complete session or recording of someone playing a video game from beginning to end

Examples

Game playthrough video
I watched a playthrough of the new adventure game before deciding to buy it.
Her first playthrough of the puzzle game took over six hours to complete.
Many gamers upload a playthrough to help others see the entire story and gameplay.
