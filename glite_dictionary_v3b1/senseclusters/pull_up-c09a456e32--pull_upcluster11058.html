pull up

verb
stop a vehicle after driving
Pronunciation: / pʊl ʌp / (AmE), / pʊl ʌp / (BrE)
to stop or slow down a vehicle or your movement after moving

She pulled up her car when she saw the accident ahead.
The taxi pulled up right outside the entrance and waited for us.
