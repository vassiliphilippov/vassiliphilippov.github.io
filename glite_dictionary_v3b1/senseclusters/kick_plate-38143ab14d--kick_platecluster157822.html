kick plate

noun
protective door plate
Pronunciation: / kɪk pleɪt /; / ˈkɪk pleɪt / (AmE), / kɪk pleɪt /; / ˈkɪk pleɪt / (BrE)
a metal or plastic plate fixed to the lower part of a door or cabinet to protect it from damage by feet or equipment
