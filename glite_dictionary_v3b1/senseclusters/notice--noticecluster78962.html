notice

noun
official statement to end an agreement
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊ.tɪs /; / ˈnoʊtɪs / (AmE), / ˈnəʊ.tɪs /; / ˈnəʊtɪs / (BrE)
an official or formal written statement, such as ending an arrangement or demanding payment

Examples

To give notice
She handed in her notice yesterday and will finish work in two weeks.
Tenants are required to give one month's notice before leaving the apartment.
The company sent a formal notice to terminate the lease.
If you ignore the notice, additional penalties may apply.
She received a notice from the utility company for the outstanding balance.
The landlord sent a notice demanding payment of the overdue rent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demand - an official request for payment of money owed
  • synonym: notification - an act of informing someone
  • synonym: reminder - a written message reminding someone of an upcoming or overdue payment
