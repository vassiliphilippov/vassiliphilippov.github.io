ceaselessly

adverb
without stopping; continuously
5.3 0.74/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàsiÀêsl…ôsli / (AmE), / ÀàsiÀêsl…ôsli / (BrE)
In a way that does not stop; without interruption or end.

Examples

To ceaselessly work
He ceaselessly searched for new solutions to old problems.
She worked ceaselessly to improve conditions in the community.
The rain fell ceaselessly all night, drumming on the roof.

  • unremittingly

  • unhaltingly
