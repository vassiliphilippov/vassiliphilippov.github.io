intergroup

adjective
between groups
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈɡruːp / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈɡruːp / (BrE)
happening or existing between two or more different groups

Examples

Intergroup cooperation
The organization focuses on intergroup cooperation to resolve conflicts.
Researchers studied intergroup relations among students from different backgrounds.
An intergroup dialogue was organized to encourage open communication.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intragroup - happening within a single group
