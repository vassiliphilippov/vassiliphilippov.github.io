colloidal

adjective
relating to or consisting of a colloid
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɔɪ.dəl / (AmE), / kəˈlɔɪ.dəl / (BrE)
relating to, consisting of, or having the properties of a colloid, especially one in which substances are microscopically dispersed throughout another substance

Examples

Colloidal solution
Many medicines are delivered in colloidal form to improve absorption in the body.
Milk is a natural example of a colloidal substance, with fat droplets dispersed in water.
The scientist prepared a colloidal solution of silver nanoparticles for the experiment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: colloid - a mixture in which one substance of microscopically dispersed insoluble particles is suspended throughout another substance
