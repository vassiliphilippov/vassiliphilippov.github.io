uplighting

noun
upward decorative lighting
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌlaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌlaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
lighting that is directed upwards to illuminate a wall, ceiling, or architectural feature for decorative effect

Examples

Uplighting on walls
The wedding planner recommended uplighting to enhance the ambiance of the reception hall.
Modern hotels often use colored uplighting to create a stylish atmosphere in their lobbies.
They installed uplighting at the base of the columns to highlight the building's architecture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accent lighting - lighting used to highlight specific features or areas
  • antonym: downlighting - lighting directed downwards, usually from the ceiling
