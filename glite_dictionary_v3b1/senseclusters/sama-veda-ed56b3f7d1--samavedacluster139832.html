Sama-Veda

noun
collection of Vedic chants and melodies
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsɑː.mə ˈveɪ.də/ (AmE), /ˈsɑː.mə ˈveɪ.də/ (BrE)
one of the four Vedas, consisting of a collection of mantras and melodies intended for ritual chanting, used alongside the Rig-Veda in Hindu tradition
