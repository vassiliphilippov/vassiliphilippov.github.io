ecologically

adverb
in an ecological manner
Pronunciation: / ˌiː.kəˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˌiː.kəˈlɒdʒ.ɪ.kli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to ecology or the environment.

Examples

To ecologically protect nature
Many conservationists are trying to find ecologically sustainable solutions to current environmental problems.
The new policy is ecologically sound but may require significant investment.
These farming practices are not only ecologically responsible but also economically viable.
