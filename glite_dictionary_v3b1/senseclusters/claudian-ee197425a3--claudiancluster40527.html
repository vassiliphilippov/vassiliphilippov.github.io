Claudian

adjective
of Claudius family or Claudian
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈklɔːdiən / (AmE), / ˈklɔːdɪən / (BrE)
relating to the Roman Claudius family or to the poet Claudian

Examples

Claudian dynasty
The Claudian dynasty played a significant role in the history of ancient Rome.
Many scholars have studied the Claudian period for its political and cultural developments.
The poet's style is often described as distinctly Claudian in its elegance and complexity.
