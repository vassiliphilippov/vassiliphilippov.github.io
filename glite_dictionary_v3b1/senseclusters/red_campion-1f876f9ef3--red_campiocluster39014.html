red campion

noun
a European wildflower with red or pink flowers
Pronunciation: / rɛd ˈkæm.pi.ən / (AmE), / rɛd ˈkæm.pɪ.ən / (BrE)
a European wildflower with bright pink or red blossoms, sometimes classified in the genus Lychnis

Examples

Bright red campion flowers
Children love to pick the red campion flowers along the country lanes.
She planted several red campion among the bluebells to add a splash of color to her garden.
The red campion is especially beautiful in woodland clearings during late spring.
