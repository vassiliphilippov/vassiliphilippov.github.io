gummy

adjective
coated with sticky gum
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌmi / (AmE), / ˈɡʌmi / (BrE)
sticky or glue-like; covered with or resembling gum or something adhesive

Examples

The gummy residue on the label made it hard to peel off completely.
The tape left a gummy mark on the glass that took time to clean.
