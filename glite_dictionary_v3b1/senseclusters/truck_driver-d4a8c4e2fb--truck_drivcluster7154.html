truck driver

noun
person who drives a truck for work
1.0
Pronunciation: / trʌk ˈdraɪvɚ / (AmE), / trʌk ˈdraɪvə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to drive a truck, especially to transport goods over long distances

Examples

A truck driver transports goods
After becoming a truck driver, she enjoyed the freedom of the open road.
Many truck drivers spend long hours behind the wheel each week.
The truck driver delivered goods to several cities across the country.
