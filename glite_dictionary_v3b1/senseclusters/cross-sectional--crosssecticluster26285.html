cross-sectional

adjective
relating to a cross section
Pronunciation: / krɑs ˈsɛkʃənəl /; / ˌkrɔsˈsɛkʃənl / (AmE), / krɒs ˈsɛkʃənəl /; / ˌkrɒsˈsɛkʃən(ə)l / (BrE)
relating to or showing a cross section (a cut across something to reveal its internal structure or features)
