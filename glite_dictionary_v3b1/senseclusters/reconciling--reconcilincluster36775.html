reconciling

adjective
bringing into harmony
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛkənˌsaɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɛk.ən.saɪ.lɪŋ / (BrE)
Tending to bring people, ideas, or things into agreement or harmony.

Examples

A reconciling gesture
A reconciling gesture can heal even the deepest wounds in a relationship.
Her reconciling attitude helped resolve the years-long dispute between the two departments.
The leader's reconciling words brought calm to the divided community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: irreconcilable - impossible to bring into agreement or harmony
  • synonym: reconcilable - able to be brought into agreement or harmony
