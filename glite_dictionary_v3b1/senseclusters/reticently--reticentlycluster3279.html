reticently

adverb
in a reserved or quiet manner
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛt.ə.sənt.li / (AmE), / ˈrɛt.ɪ.sənt.li / (BrE)
In a manner showing reluctance to speak or share information; in a reserved or silent way.

Examples

To reticently speak
He reticently offered a brief explanation for his absence.
She reticently declined to give her opinion during the meeting.
When questioned about his personal life, he responded reticently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reticent - not revealing one's thoughts or feelings readily
