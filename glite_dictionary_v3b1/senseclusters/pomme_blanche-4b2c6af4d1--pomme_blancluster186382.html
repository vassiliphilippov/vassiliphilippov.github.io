pomme blanche

noun
North American plant with edible roots
Pronunciation: / pɑm blɑnʃ / (AmE), / pɒm blɒ̃ʃ / (BrE)
A perennial plant of central North America with densely hairy foliage and edible tuberous roots.

Examples

The pomme blanche plant
She harvested the pomme blanche tubers in late summer for their nutty flavor.
The roots of the pomme blanche were once an important food source for Native Americans.
We found several pomme blanche plants growing along the riverbank.
