nib

noun
writing point of a pen
2.8 5.7
Pronunciation: /nɪb/ (AmE), /nɪb/ (BrE)
the pointed part at the end of a pen, or a small pointed or projecting piece (such as a piece of chocolate or cocoa)

Examples

Pen nib
Calligraphers often use pens with different nibs to achieve varied line thickness.

Make sure the nib is clean before you begin writing.

She sprinkled cocoa nibs on top of the cake for extra flavor.
