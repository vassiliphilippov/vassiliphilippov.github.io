banally

adverb
in an unoriginal way
7.3
Pronunciation: / bəˈnæli / (AmE), / bəˈnɑːli / (BrE)
in a way that is unoriginal, boring, or lacking in freshness or interest

Examples

To banally say
The story was banally written, offering nothing new to the genre.
He banally repeated the same jokes everyone had heard before.
Her speech was banally predictable, lacking any original ideas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dully - in a boring or uninteresting way
  • antonym: interestingly - in a way that is engaging or holds attention
