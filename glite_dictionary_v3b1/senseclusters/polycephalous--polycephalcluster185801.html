polycephalous

adjective
having many heads
9.9
Pronunciation: /pɑːl.ɪ.ˈse.fə.ləs/ (AmE), /pɒl.ɪ.ˈse.fə.ləs/ (BrE)
having multiple head-like structures, such as flower heads or fruiting bodies, especially in plants, fungi, or colonial animals

Examples

Polycephalous fungus
The scientist studied a polycephalous fungus that produced several fruiting bodies from one base.
Some thistle species are polycephalous, displaying clusters of flower heads on each plant.
The coral colony was described as polycephalous due to its many head-like structures.
