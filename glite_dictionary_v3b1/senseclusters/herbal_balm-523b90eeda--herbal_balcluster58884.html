herbal balm

noun
soothing ointment made from herbs
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhɝː.bəl ˈbɑm / (AmE), / ˈhɜː.bəl ˈbɑːm / (BrE)
A soothing ointment or salve made from herbs, typically used to relieve pain or promote healing of the skin.

Examples

A herbal balm ointment
A gentle herbal balm can help soothe sunburned areas after a long day outdoors.
She applied the herbal balm to her dry skin every night.
The massage therapist recommended a lavender herbal balm for relaxation and relief from sore muscles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ointment - a smooth substance applied to the skin for medicinal purposes
  • synonym: salve - an ointment used to promote healing or protect the skin
