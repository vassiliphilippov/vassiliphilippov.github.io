whammy

noun
a devastating setback
Pronunciation: / ˈwæmi / (AmE), / ˈwæmi / (BrE)
a serious or devastating setback or misfortune

Examples

A big whammy
After a series of whammies, the project was finally abandoned.
Losing his job was a real whammy for Tom and his family.
The company took a whammy when its main client went bankrupt.

Related Terms
