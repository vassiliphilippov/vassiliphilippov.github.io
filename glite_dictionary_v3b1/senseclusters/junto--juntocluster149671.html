junto

noun
secretive political clique
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌn.toʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌn.təʊ / (BrE)
A small, often secret group or clique, especially one that seeks power or influence by intrigue.

Examples

A secret junto
A powerful junto in the city council influenced major policy decisions for years.
Rumors spread about a secret junto manipulating the election results.
The ruling junto met behind closed doors to plan their next move.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cabal - a secret group of plotters
  • synonym: clique - a small, exclusive group of people
