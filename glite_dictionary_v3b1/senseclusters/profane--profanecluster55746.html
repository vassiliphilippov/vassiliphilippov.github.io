profane

verb
treat sacred things with disrespect
6.9 5.8
Pronunciation: / proʊˈfeɪn / (AmE), / prəˈfeɪn / (BrE)
to treat something sacred with irreverence or disrespect; to violate the sanctity of a place, object, or language

Examples

To profane a temple
It was considered a grave offense to profane the name of their deity.
Some people believe that careless language can profane cultural traditions.
The vandals attempted to profane the ancient temple with graffiti.
