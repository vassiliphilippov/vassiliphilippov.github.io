precociousness

noun
early intellectual or developmental maturity
Pronunciation: / prɪˈkoʊʃəsnəs / (AmE), / prɪˈkəʊʃəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of having developed certain abilities or intelligence at an earlier age than usual

Examples

Child precociousness
Her precociousness astonished her teachers, who were not accustomed to such advanced thinking from a child.
Some parents worry that precociousness in one area might lead to social challenges.
The precociousness of his musical talent became apparent at a very young age.
