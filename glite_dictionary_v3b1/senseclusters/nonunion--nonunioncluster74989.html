nonunion

adjective
not affiliated with a trade union
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈjunjən / (AmE), / nɒnˈjuːnjən / (BrE)
not belonging to, or not recognizing or permitting affiliation with, a trade union

Examples

Nonunion workplace
After a dispute, employees voted to switch from a nonunion to a unionized workplace.
Many construction sites today are staffed by nonunion laborers.
The company only hires nonunion workers to keep costs down.

Related Terms

  • antonym: union - belonging to or recognizing a trade union
