bird's-nest fungus

noun
cup-shaped fungus with egg-like structures
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝdz nɛst ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdz nɛst ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
Any of a group of fungi of the family Nidulariaceae, characterized by a cup-shaped fruiting body containing several small, egg-like structures that enclose spores.

Naturalists are fascinated by how the spores are released from the bird's-nest fungus.
