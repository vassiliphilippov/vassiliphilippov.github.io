knocking

noun
a sharp, repetitive sound or noise
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnɒkɪŋ / (BrE)
a sharp, repetitive sound, especially one made by striking a surface such as a door or occurring in an engine or machinery

Examples

Engine knocking sound
Lately I've noticed a strange knocking from the engine when I drive.
The knocking at the door startled everyone in the room.
There was a persistent knocking coming from upstairs late at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rap - a quick, sharp knock or sound
  • synonym: thump - a heavy dull sound, like a fall or heavy knock
