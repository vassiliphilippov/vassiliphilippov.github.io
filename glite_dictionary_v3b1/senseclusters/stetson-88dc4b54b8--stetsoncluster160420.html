Stetson

noun
wide-brimmed felt cowboy hat
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛtsən / (AmE), / ˈstɛtsən / (BrE)
a type of wide-brimmed felt hat with a high, creased crown, traditionally associated with cowboys in the western United States

Examples

A Stetson hat
A battered old Stetson hung on the coat rack by the door.
He tipped his Stetson as a polite greeting to the townsfolk.
Many country musicians wear a Stetson when they perform.
