vetturino

noun
carriage driver in Italy
Pronunciation: / ˌvɛtʊˈriːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˌvɛtʊˈriːnəʊ / (BrE)
a person who drives or operates a horse-drawn carriage for hire, especially in Italy

Examples

The vetturino drives horses
The vetturino guided his horses carefully through the narrow streets of Florence.
Tourists enjoyed a ride with a friendly vetturino during their visit to Rome.
The old photograph showed a vetturino waiting beside his elegant carriage.
The vetturino waited patiently outside the inn for passengers needing a ride to the next town.
Travelers often relied on a local vetturino to transport their luggage and goods between villages.
She hired a vetturino to take her through the countryside in a horse-drawn carriage.
