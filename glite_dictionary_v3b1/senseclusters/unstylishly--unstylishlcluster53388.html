unstylishly

adverb
without style
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstaɪlɪʃli / (AmE), / ʌnˈstaɪlɪʃli / (BrE)
in a way that lacks style or fashion sense

Examples

Dressed unstylishly
He arrived at the party unstylishly, wearing mismatched clothes and old sneakers.
Despite her talent, she was often criticized for dressing unstylishly at public events.
The actor appeared unstylishly in a wrinkled shirt and faded jeans during the interview.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plainly - in a simple or unadorned way
  • antonym: stylishly - in a fashionable or elegant way
