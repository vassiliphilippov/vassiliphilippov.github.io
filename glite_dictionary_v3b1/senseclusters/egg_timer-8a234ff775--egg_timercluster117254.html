egg timer

noun
timer for boiling eggs
4.8 1.5
Pronunciation: /eg ˈtaɪ.mər/ (AmE), /eg ˈtaɪ.mə/ (BrE)
a device, often a small hourglass or timer, used to measure the time required to boil eggs, typically lasting around three minutes

Examples

An egg timer device
Our old egg timer is shaped like an hourglass and lasts exactly three minutes.
Remember to use the egg timer so you don't overcook the eggs.
She set the egg timer before placing the eggs in boiling water.
