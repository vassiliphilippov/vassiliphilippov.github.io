shirting

noun
fabric for making shirts
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɜrtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɜːtɪŋ / (BrE)
fabric, typically cotton or a cotton blend, used for making shirts, especially men's dress shirts.

Examples

Cotton shirting fabric
Choosing the right shirting is essential for making comfortable work shirts.
Several bolts of striped shirting arrived at the fabric shop this morning.
The tailor recommended a high-quality shirting for my new dress shirt.

  • synonym: poplin - a strong cotton fabric used for shirts and dresses
