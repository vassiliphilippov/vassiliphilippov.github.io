F

interjection
internet interjection to pay respects
Pronunciation: / əf / (AmE), / əf / (BrE)
Used in online chat and social media to express respect, sympathy, or ironic condolence (originating from the meme “Press F to pay respects”).

Examples

Type F in chat
When the streamer announced the outage, viewers posted F in the chat to pay respects.
After the character died, thousands of players typed F to show sympathy and disbelief.
Fans dropped a single F in the thread after the server crashed during the tournament.

Related Terms

  • synonym: RIP - used online to express sympathy or acknowledge someone's death or misfortune
  • synonym: press F to pay respects - the original meme phrase prompting users to press the F key to show respect
