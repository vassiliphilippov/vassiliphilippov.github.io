springboard for

noun
starting point for progress or advantage
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɪŋbɔrd fɔr / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋbɔːd fə / (BrE)
A thing that serves as a starting point for further development, progress, or success.

Examples

A springboard for success
The internship acted as a springboard for his entry into the tech industry.
Their collaboration was a springboard for new ideas and creative solutions.
Winning the local championship became a springboard for her successful international career.
