cloze test

noun
passage with blanks to test reading skill
5.6 6.1
Pronunciation: / kloʊz tɛst / (AmE), / kləʊz tɛst / (BrE)
a test used to assess reading ability in which words are removed from a passage and the reader is required to fill in the blanks

Examples

English cloze test
Creating a cloze test is a popular technique in English language teaching.
Students found the cloze test challenging because some of the missing words were difficult to guess.
The teacher used a cloze test to evaluate the students' comprehension skills.

Related Terms
