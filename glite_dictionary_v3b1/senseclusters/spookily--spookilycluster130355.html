spookily

adverb
in an eerily frightening way
Pronunciation: / ˈspuːkɪli / (AmE), / ˈspuːkɪli / (BrE)
In a way that is eerily or unnaturally frightening or strange.

Examples

To smile spookily
He smiled spookily from behind his Halloween mask.
The old abandoned house was spookily silent at midnight.
The sky looked spookily dark before the storm began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eerily - In a strange and frightening way
