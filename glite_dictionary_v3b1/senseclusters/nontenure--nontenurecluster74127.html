nontenure

adjective
without permanent position
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈtɛnjər / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈtɛnjə / (BrE)
not having or relating to a permanent or tenured position, especially in an academic context

Examples

Nontenure professor
She accepted a nontenure teaching job at the university.
Many nontenure faculty members face job insecurity each year.
The college hired several nontenure instructors for the new semester.

Related Terms

  • synonym: temporary - lasting for only a limited period of time
  • antonym: tenured - having a permanent position, especially in academia
