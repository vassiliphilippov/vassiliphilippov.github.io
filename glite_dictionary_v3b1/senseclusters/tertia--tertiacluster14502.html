tertia

noun
innermost flight feather
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtɜːr.ʃə/ (AmE), /ˈtɜː.ʃə/ (BrE)
a type of flight feather found on the innermost part of a bird's wing, closest to the body

Examples

Bird's tertia feather
The ornithologist carefully examined the tertia to assess the bird's health.
Damage to the tertia can affect a bird's ability to fly long distances.
During molting season, the tertia are often replaced along with other wing feathers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: primary - the outermost flight feather on a bird's wing
  • antonym: secondary - a flight feather located between the primaries and tertia on a bird's wing
