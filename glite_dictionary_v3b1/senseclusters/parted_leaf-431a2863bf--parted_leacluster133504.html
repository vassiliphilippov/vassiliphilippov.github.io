parted leaf

noun
leaf deeply divided into lobes
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrt̬ɪd liːf / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtɪd liːf / (BrE)
A leaf whose margins are incised almost to the base, forming distinct divisions or lobes.

Examples

A parted leaf shape
Botanists often classify plants based on features like a parted leaf or entire margins.
The maple tree in the garden has a distinctive parted leaf structure that makes it easy to identify.
You can recognize parsley by its characteristic parted leaf compared to other herbs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lobed leaf - a leaf with rounded or pointed projections (lobes) along its margin
