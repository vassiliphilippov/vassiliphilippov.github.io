ACH

noun
electronic payment system
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ siː ˈeɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˌeɪ siː ˈeɪtʃ / (BrE)
an electronic payment system (Automated Clearing House) used to transfer money between bank accounts in the United States

Examples

ACH payment system
The company paid employees by ACH, so salaries arrived in their accounts within two business days.
Many utility bills can be paid automatically each month using ACH transfers.
She set up an ACH payment to make her mortgage easier to manage.
