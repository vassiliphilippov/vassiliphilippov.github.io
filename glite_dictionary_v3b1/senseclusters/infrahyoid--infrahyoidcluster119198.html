infrahyoid

noun
neck muscle below hyoid
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɪn.frə.ˈhaɪ.ɔɪd/ (AmE), /ɪn.frə.ˈhaɪ.ɔɪd/ (BrE)
a muscle located below the hyoid bone in the neck, involved in depressing the hyoid during swallowing or speech

Examples

An infrahyoid muscle
The surgeon carefully separated the infrahyoid to access the deeper structures of the neck.
Damage to an infrahyoid can affect swallowing and speech functions.
During anatomy class, we learned how each infrahyoid muscle contributes to lowering the hyoid bone.
