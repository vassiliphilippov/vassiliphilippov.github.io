Tarzan

noun
a very strong and agile man (figurative)
4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈtɑːr.zən/ /; / /ˈtɑːr.zæn/ (AmE), /ˈtɑː.zən/ /; / /ˈtɑː.zæn/ (BrE)
