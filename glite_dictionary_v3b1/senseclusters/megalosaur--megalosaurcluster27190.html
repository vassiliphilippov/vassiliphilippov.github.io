megalosaur

noun
large carnivorous Jurassic dinosaur
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛɡələˌsɔr / (AmE), / ˈmɛɡələˌsɔː / (BrE)
a large, carnivorous, bipedal dinosaur of the Jurassic or early Cretaceous periods, known from fossils found in Europe

Examples

A megalosaur fossil
A fossilized skeleton of a megalosaur was discovered in England in the early 19th century.
Scientists believe the megalosaur hunted smaller dinosaurs and large mammals.
The megalosaur was one of the first dinosaurs to be described scientifically.
