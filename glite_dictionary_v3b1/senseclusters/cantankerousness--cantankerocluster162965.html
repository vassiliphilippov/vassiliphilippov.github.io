cantankerousness

noun
bad-tempered quality
Pronunciation: / kænˈtæŋkərəsnəs / (AmE), / kænˈtæŋkərəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being bad-tempered, argumentative, or difficult to deal with

Examples

His cantankerousness behavior
His cantankerousness often led to arguments with his coworkers.
The old man's cantankerousness made it hard for anyone to help him.
Despite her cantankerousness, her friends knew she had a kind heart.

Related Terms

  • antonym: agreeableness - the quality of being pleasant and easy to get along with
  • synonym: irascibility - the tendency to get angry easily
