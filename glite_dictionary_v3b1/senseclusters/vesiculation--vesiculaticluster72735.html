vesiculation

noun
formation of small fluid-filled sacs in the skin
Pronunciation: / vɛˌsɪkjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / vɛˌsɪkjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of forming vesicles or small fluid-filled sacs, especially beneath the skin

Examples

Vesiculation under skin
Doctors noted vesiculation around the injection site during examination.
Early vesiculation may indicate a bullous skin disorder.
The patient exhibited significant vesiculation after exposure to the allergen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blistering - the formation of blisters on the skin
  • antonym: ulceration - the formation of ulcers in tissue
