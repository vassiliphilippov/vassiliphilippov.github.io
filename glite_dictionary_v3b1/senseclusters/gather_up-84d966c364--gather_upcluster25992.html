Synonyms

  • pick up
    2.5 60/m
    pick up means lifting or collecting items by hand or giving someone a ride, while gather up means bringing things together by hand.
  • scoop up
    2.8 3.46/m
    scoop up is quickly lifting things with cupped hands or a container, while gather up means collecting and bringing items together more generally.
  • collect
    1.1 40/m
    collect is bringing items together over time or in an organized way, while gather up often means quickly picking them up by hand.
  • round up
    3.2 5.07/m
    round up is gathering people or animals into one place, often by herding, while gather up focuses on picking objects together by hand.
  • swoop up
    4.2 0.02/m

  • take up
