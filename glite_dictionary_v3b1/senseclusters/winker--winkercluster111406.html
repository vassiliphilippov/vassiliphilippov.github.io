winker

noun
a person who winks
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪŋkər / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋkə / (BrE)
a person who winks, especially habitually or in a noticeable way.

Examples

A friendly winker
He was known as the office winker, always signaling jokes with a quick wink.
It's hard to take a winker seriously during serious conversations.
The winker at the party amused everyone with her dramatic facial expressions.
