Guinean

noun
native or inhabitant of Guinea
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪniən / (AmE), / ˈɡɪniːən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Guinea (a country in West Africa)

Examples

A Guinean person
Many Guineans live in the capital city of Conakry.
She learned about Guinean culture through her host family during her travels.
The new ambassador is a proud Guinean who has represented his country for years.
