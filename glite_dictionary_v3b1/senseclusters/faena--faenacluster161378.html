faena

noun
animal butchering process
9.9
Pronunciation: / faɪˈeɪnə / (AmE), / faɪˈeɪnə / (BrE)
the process of killing and preparing animals for meat, especially in a slaughterhouse or abattoir

Examples

Slaughterhouse faena
The workers completed the nightly faena before the trucks arrived to collect the meat.
Strict hygiene standards are enforced during every faena at the modern abattoir.
After the faena, the meat is inspected and prepared for distribution to local markets.

  • synonym: slaughter - the killing of animals for food
