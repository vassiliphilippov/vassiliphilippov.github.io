unshuttered

adjective
with no shutters or open shutters
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃʌtɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃʌtəd / (BrE)
(of a window or building) not fitted with shutters, or having the shutters open.

Examples

Unshuttered windows
At night, light spilled from the unshuttered windows onto the empty street.
The old house had tall, unshuttered windows that let in the morning sun.
Through the unshuttered doorway, a warm breeze drifted into the quiet room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shuttered - fitted with or having closed shutters
