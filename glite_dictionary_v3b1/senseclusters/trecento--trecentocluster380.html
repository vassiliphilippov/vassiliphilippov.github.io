trecento

noun
Italian 14th century
Pronunciation: / treɪˈtʃɛntoʊ / (AmE), / treɪˈtʃɛntəʊ / (BrE)
the 14th century, especially as it relates to Italian art, literature, and culture

Examples

The trecento period
The trecento was a period of great innovation in Italian painting and sculpture.
Many famous poets and artists emerged during the trecento in Florence.
Historians often study the trecento to understand the roots of the Renaissance.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: quattrocento - the 15th century in Italian art and culture
