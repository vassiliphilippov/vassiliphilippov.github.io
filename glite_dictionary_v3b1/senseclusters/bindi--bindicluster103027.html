bindi

noun
forehead decoration
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪndi / (AmE), / ˈbɪndi / (BrE)
a decorative dot or mark worn on the forehead, especially by women in South Asia as a cultural or religious symbol

Examples

A red bindi
She chose a red bindi to match her traditional sari for the wedding.
Many women wear a bindi as a symbol of cultural identity and beauty.
At the festival, children decorated their foreheads with a colorful bindi.
