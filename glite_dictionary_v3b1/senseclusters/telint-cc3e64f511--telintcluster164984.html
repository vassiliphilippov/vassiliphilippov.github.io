TELINT

noun
intelligence from foreign telemetry
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛlɪnt / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪnt / (BrE)
intelligence obtained by intercepting, processing, and analyzing foreign telemetry data, especially for military purposes

Examples

Foreign TELINT data
Accurate TELINT is crucial for assessing foreign weapons capabilities.
Advancements in TELINT have improved the detection of unauthorized satellite communications.
The agency relied on TELINT to monitor missile tests conducted abroad.
