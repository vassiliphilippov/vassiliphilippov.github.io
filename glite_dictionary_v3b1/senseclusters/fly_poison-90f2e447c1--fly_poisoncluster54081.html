fly poison

noun
a toxic plant used as fly poison
Pronunciation: / flaɪ ˈpɔɪzən / (AmE), / flaɪ ˈpɔɪzn̩ / (BrE)
a poisonous North American plant (Amianthium muscitoxicum) with toxic bulbs that have been used as a fly poison

Examples

The fly poison plant
Be careful when hiking, as all parts of fly poison are highly toxic.
Farmers once used the bulb of fly poison to kill insects in their barns.
The white, star-shaped flowers of fly poison appear in early summer.
