Vietcong

noun
supporter of Vietcong
Pronunciation: / ˌviːɛtˈkɒŋ / (AmE), / ˌviːɛtˈkɒŋ / (BrE)
a person who supports or sympathizes with the Vietcong and their cause, not necessarily an active member

Examples

Vietcong supporter
During the war, anyone suspected of being a Vietcong faced serious consequences.
He was accused of acting as a Vietcong sympathizer by the authorities.
Some villagers secretly supported the Vietcong during the conflict.
