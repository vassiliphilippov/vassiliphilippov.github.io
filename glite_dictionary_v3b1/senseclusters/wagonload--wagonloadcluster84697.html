wagonload

noun
large quantity
4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈwæɡ.ən loʊd/ (AmE), /ˈwæɡ.ən ləʊd/ (BrE)
a very large amount or number of something, as much as would fill a wagon

Examples

A wagonload of messages
After the show, she received a wagonload of messages praising her performance.
We brought a wagonload of apples back from the orchard.
He has a wagonload of experience in managing large projects.
