armament

noun
arming for war; military equipment supply
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrməmənt / (AmE), / ˈɑːməmənt / (BrE)
the process of supplying or equipping with military weapons and equipment, especially in preparation for war

Examples

Armament buildup
Efforts towards universal armament led to an arms race before the conflict.
International treaties often regulate the armament of nations to prevent escalation.
The country's increased armament raised concerns among its neighbors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disarmament - the process of reducing or abolishing military forces and weapons
  • synonym: rearmament - the process of building up again a supply of military weapons and equipment
