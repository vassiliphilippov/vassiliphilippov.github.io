pagan

noun
follower of a polytheistic or ancient religion
4.7 5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈpeɪ.gən/ (AmE), /ˈpeɪ.gən/ /; / /pə.ˈgɑːn/ (BrE)
a person who follows a polytheistic, ancient, or non-mainstream religion, especially not Christianity, Judaism, or Islam
