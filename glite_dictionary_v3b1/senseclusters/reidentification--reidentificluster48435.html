reidentification

noun
matching data to identity
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌriːaɪˌdɛntɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːaɪˌdɛntɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of matching anonymous or de-identified data with the identity of the person it belongs to

Examples

Data reidentification
Strict safeguards are needed to prevent reidentification of personal information.
The risk of reidentification increases when multiple datasets are combined.
Researchers must consider the possibility of reidentification when sharing anonymized health records.
