stimulant drug

noun
a drug that stimulates body or mind
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪmjələnt drʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈstɪmjʊlənt drʌɡ / (BrE)
a drug that temporarily increases physiological or mental activity

Examples

Stimulant drug use
Athletes are sometimes tested for illegal stimulant drugs during competitions.
Caffeine is a common stimulant drug found in coffee and energy drinks.
The doctor warned her to avoid any stimulant drug before bedtime.

Related Terms

  • antonym: depressant drug - a drug that reduces physiological or nervous activity
  • synonym: stimulant - a substance that raises levels of physiological or nervous activity
