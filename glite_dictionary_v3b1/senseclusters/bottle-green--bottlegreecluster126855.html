bottle-green

adjective
dark greyish-green color
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːtl ɡriːn / (AmE), / ˈbɒtl ɡriːn / (BrE)
A dark to moderate greyish-green color, like that of a green glass bottle.

Examples

Bottle-green color
He painted the kitchen cabinets bottle-green for a classic, vintage look.
She wore a stunning bottle-green dress to the reception last night.
The curtains in the study were a deep shade of bottle-green.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forest green - a dark green color resembling the color of forest foliage
