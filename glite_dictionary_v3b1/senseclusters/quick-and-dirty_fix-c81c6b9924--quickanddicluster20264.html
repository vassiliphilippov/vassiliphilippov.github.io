quick-and-dirty fix

noun
hastily made or makeshift solution
Pronunciation: / kwɪk ənd ˈdɝːti fɪks / (AmE), / kwɪk ənd ˈdɜːti fɪks / (BrE)
a solution that is implemented quickly and without attention to detail, often as a temporary measure

Examples

A quick-and-dirty fix solution
He admitted the quick-and-dirty fix wouldn't last, but it would do for now.
Sometimes, a quick-and-dirty fix is all you need to solve a pressing problem.
We implemented a quick-and-dirty fix to get the website running before the big event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: workaround - a method for overcoming a problem or limitation in a system or program
