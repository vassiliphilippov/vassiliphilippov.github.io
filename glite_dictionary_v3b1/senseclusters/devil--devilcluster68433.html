devil

verb
annoy or disturb persistently
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈdɛvəl / (BrE)
to annoy or disturb someone persistently, especially with minor irritations

Examples

To devil someone
Flies deviled us during the entire picnic, making it hard to enjoy our food.
She was deviled by doubts about her decision, unable to rest easy.
The constant noise from the construction site has started to devil the neighbors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annoy - to make someone slightly angry; to bother
  • synonym: irritate - to make someone a little angry or impatient
