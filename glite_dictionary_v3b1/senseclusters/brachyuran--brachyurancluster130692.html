brachyuran

noun
a true crab (infraorder Brachyura)
Pronunciation: / brəˈkjuːrən / (AmE), / brəˈkjʊərən / (BrE)
a crab of the infraorder Brachyura, characterized by a short, broad body and a reduced abdomen; a true crab

Examples

A brachyuran crab
Most commercially harvested crabs are members of the brachyuran group.
Scientists discovered a new species of brachyuran in the coral reef.
The brachyuran exhibits a distinct body shape compared to other crustaceans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crab - a marine crustacean with a broad flattened body, short tail, and pincers
