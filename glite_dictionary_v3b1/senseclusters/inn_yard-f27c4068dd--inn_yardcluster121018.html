inn yard

noun
courtyard of an inn
5.5
Pronunciation: / ɪn jɑrd / (AmE), / ɪn jɑːd / (BrE)
a courtyard or open area at an inn, often used for gatherings or for keeping horses

Examples

The inn yard
The actors performed their play in the inn yard before a lively crowd.
Horses were tied up in the inn yard while their owners rested inside.
Merchants met in the inn yard to discuss business before continuing their journey.
The actors performed their play in the bustling inn yard before a lively crowd.
Travelers gathered in the inn yard to share stories and rest their horses.
The old inn yard was paved with cobblestones and surrounded by wooden balconies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: courtyard - an open space surrounded by buildings or walls
  • synonym: inn - a place where travelers can stay and eat
