potentate

noun
an all-powerful ruler or monarch
6.3 7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈpoʊ.tən.teɪt/ (AmE), /ˈpəʊ.tən.teɪt/ (BrE)
a ruler who has great power, especially one who is not limited by laws or constitution

Examples

A potentate ruler
Many tales tell of a potentate whose word was absolute and whose power was immense.
The ancient kingdom was ruled by a potentate who faced no opposition from his people.
Throughout history, advisors often tried to restrain the excesses of an ambitious potentate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: despot - a ruler with absolute power, typically one who exercises it cruelly
  • synonym: monarch - a king, queen, or emperor
  • antonym: subject - a person under the rule of a sovereign
