yappy

adjective
noisy and talkative
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈjæpi / (AmE), / ˈjæpi / (BrE)
talking or barking a lot in a high-pitched, often annoying way

Examples

A yappy puppy
The yappy puppy barked at every sound it heard outside the window.
Her yappy neighbor never stopped talking during the entire meeting.
He found the constant yappy chatter in the café distracting while he tried to work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chatty - talking a lot in a friendly way
  • synonym: noisy - making a lot of loud or unpleasant sounds
  • antonym: quiet - making little or no noise
