pyromancer

noun
person who practices pyromancy or fire magic
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪrəˌmænsər / (AmE), / ˈpaɪ.rəˌmɑːn.sə / (BrE)
a person who practices pyromancy, the supposed art of divining by fire or flames, or one who has magical powers over fire

Examples

The pyromancer controls fire
In the fantasy novel, the pyromancer summoned a wall of fire to protect her companions.
Legends speak of a mysterious pyromancer who could predict the future through fire.
The young wizard aspired to become a powerful pyromancer with control over flame.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pyromancy - divination by means of fire or flames
