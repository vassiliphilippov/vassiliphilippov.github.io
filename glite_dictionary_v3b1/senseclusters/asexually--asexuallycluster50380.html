asexually

adverb
without sexual reproduction
2.5
Pronunciation: / əˈsɛkʃuəli / (AmE), / əˈsɛksjʊəli / (BrE)
In a way that does not involve sexual reproduction; without the involvement of sex cells.

Examples

To reproduce asexually
Bacteria often multiply asexually through a process called binary fission.
Some species of starfish can regenerate lost limbs and reproduce asexually.
The plant cuttings rooted and grew asexually in the greenhouse.
