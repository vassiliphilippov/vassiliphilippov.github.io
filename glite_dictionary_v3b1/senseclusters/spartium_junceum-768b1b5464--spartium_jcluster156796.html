Spartium junceum

noun
a Mediterranean shrub used in basketry
9.9
Pronunciation: /spɑːr.ˈtɪ.əm dʒʌn.ˈsiː.əm/ /; / /spɑːr.ˈtiː.əm dʒuːn.ˈsiː.əm/ (AmE), /spɑː.ˈtɪ.əm dʒʌn.ˈsiː.əm/ /; / /spɑː.ˈtiː.əm dʒuːn.ˈsiː.əm/ (BrE)
A tall, thornless shrub with pale yellow flowers and flexible, rush-like twigs used in basketry, native to southwestern Europe and the Mediterranean region, and naturalized in California.

Examples

Tall Spartium junceum shrub
Spartium junceum has become naturalized in parts of California due to its hardiness.
Artisans often use the flexible twigs of Spartium junceum for traditional basket weaving.
The hillsides were covered with blooming Spartium junceum in early summer.
