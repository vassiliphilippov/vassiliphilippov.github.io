wiggle worm

noun
restless or fidgety person (esp. child)
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪɡəl wɜrm / (AmE), / ˈwɪɡl̩ wɜːm / (BrE)
an informal term for a person, especially a child, who is very restless or fidgety and has difficulty staying still

Examples

The wiggle worm child
During class, the teacher gently asked the wiggle worm in the front row to try and focus on the lesson.
It's not easy to buckle a seatbelt on a little wiggle worm who's excited for the trip.
My nephew is such a wiggle worm that he can't sit through dinner without moving.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fidget - a person who fidgets or cannot sit still
