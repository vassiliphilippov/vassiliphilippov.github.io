voyeuristically

adverb
in a voyeur's manner; with secretive observation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌvɔɪ.jɚˈɪs.tɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˌvɔɪ.əˈrɪs.tɪ.kli / (BrE)
In a manner characteristic of a voyeur; in a way that involves secretly watching others, often for sexual pleasure or curiosity.

Examples

To watch voyeuristically
He watched the argument voyeuristically from behind the curtain without being seen.
Some reality TV shows are designed to appeal to viewers who enjoy observing others voyeuristically.
The paparazzi followed the celebrity voyeuristically, eager to capture her private moments.
