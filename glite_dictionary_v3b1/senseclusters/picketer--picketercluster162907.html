picketer

noun
protest participant
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkətər / (AmE), / ˈpɪkɪtə / (BrE)
a person who stands or marches outside a place, especially a workplace, to protest or support a cause

Examples

A picketer with sign
The picketer held a sign demanding fair wages outside the company headquarters.
Police monitored the group as each picketer walked peacefully along the sidewalk.
During the strike, dozens of picketers gathered at the main entrance every morning.
The picketer stood outside the factory gates holding a sign for fair wages.
Police monitored the group as each picketer peacefully marched in front of the building.
During the strike, every picketer wore a badge showing their union membership.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demonstrator - a person taking part in a public demonstration
  • synonym: protester - a person who publicly expresses strong objection or disapproval
  • synonym: striker - a worker who refuses to work as a form of protest
