deselection

noun
removal of selection
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌdiːsəˈlɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌdiːsɪˈlɛkʃən / (BrE)
the action or process of unselecting an item or option, especially in a user interface, or the state of being unselected

Examples

File deselection
Accidental deselection of important files can lead to data loss during backup.
The software update introduced a new shortcut for quick deselection of all items.
After making a deselection, the user had to reselect the correct options before proceeding.

Related Terms

  • antonym: selection - the action of choosing or marking an item as chosen
  • synonym: uncheck - to remove a checkmark or selection from an option
