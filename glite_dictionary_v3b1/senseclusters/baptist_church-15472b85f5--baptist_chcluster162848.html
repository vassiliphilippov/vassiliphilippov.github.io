Baptist Church

noun
Protestant church practicing believers' baptism
Pronunciation: / ˈbæptɪst tʃɜrtʃ / (AmE), / ˈbæptɪst tʃɜːtʃ / (BrE)
a Protestant Christian church that practices baptism only of voluntary believers, typically by full immersion

Examples

Baptist Church congregation
Many Baptist Church congregations are involved in missionary work around the world.
She was baptized at the local Baptist Church last summer.
The Baptist Church in town hosts community events every weekend.
