pallial

adjective
of the mantle
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpæliəl / (AmE), / ˈpæliəl / (BrE)
relating to the pallium or mantle, especially the soft outer body wall of molluscs and some other invertebrates

Examples

Pallial cavity
The pallial cavity of the oyster contains its gills and other vital organs.
Researchers observed changes in the pallial region of the snail after exposure to pollutants.
The pallial groove is an important anatomical feature in many bivalve species.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mantle - the soft outer body wall of molluscs and some invertebrates
