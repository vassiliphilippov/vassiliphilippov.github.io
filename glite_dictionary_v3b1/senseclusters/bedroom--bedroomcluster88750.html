bedroom

noun
room used for sleeping
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdˌɹum / (AmE), / ˈbɛd.ruːm / (BrE)
A room in a house or other dwelling used primarily for sleeping.

Examples

A bedroom in a house
Guests will be staying in the spare bedroom during the holidays.
She painted her bedroom walls a calming shade of blue.
The house has a large bedroom with a beautiful view of the garden.

Related Terms

  • antonym: living room - a room for general everyday use in the house
  • synonym: sleeping room - another term for a room used mainly for sleeping
