rubberiness

noun
flexible texture
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌbəriˌnɛs / (AmE), / ˈrʌbəriˌnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being like rubber, especially in flexibility, stretchiness, or texture

Examples

The rubberiness of material
The rubberiness of the material made the shoes comfortable for walking long distances.
She noticed the rubberiness of the cooked pasta and decided it needed more time.
The toy's rubberiness allowed it to bend without breaking, making it safe for children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elasticity - the ability to return to original shape after stretching
  • antonym: rigidity - the quality of being stiff and not flexible
