boilersuit

noun
protective coverall for dirty work
2.9 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈbɔɪ.lər.suːt/ /; / /ˈbɔɪ.lər.sjuːt/ (AmE), /ˈbɔɪ.lə.sjuːt/ /; / /ˈbɔɪ.lə.suːt/ (BrE)
A loose-fitting protective garment covering the whole body except for the head, hands, and feet, worn over ordinary clothes for dirty or heavy work.

Examples

A boilersuit for work
Before painting, she put on a boilersuit to keep her clothes free of splashes.
The mechanic wore a boilersuit to protect his clothes while working on the engine.
Workers are required to wear a boilersuit in the factory for safety reasons.
