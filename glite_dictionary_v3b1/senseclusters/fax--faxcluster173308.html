fax

verb
send by facsimile machine
2.8 4.03/m
Pronunciation: / fÃ¦ks / (AmE), / fÃ¦ks / (BrE)
to send (a document or image) by a facsimile machine over a telephone line

Examples

To fax a letter

Synonyms

    7.0 0.00/m

  • telefax
    7.7 0.00/m
    telefax is an older or less common term for sending documents by fax, while fax is the modern everyday verb.
