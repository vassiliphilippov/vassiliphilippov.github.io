bustard quail

noun
small, quail-like ground bird
8.8 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌstərd kweɪl / (AmE), / ˈbʌstəd kweɪl / (BrE)
a small, quail-like ground-dwelling bird of southern Eurasia and northern Africa, lacking a hind toe and inhabiting grassy plains
