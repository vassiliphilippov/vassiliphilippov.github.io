old timer's disease

noun
humorous term for age-related forgetfulness
6.9
Pronunciation: / oʊld ˈtaɪmɚz dɪˌziz / (AmE), / əʊld ˈtaɪməz dɪˌziːz / (BrE)
an informal and humorous term for forgetfulness or memory loss, especially as a result of aging

Examples

Old timer's disease forgetfulness
My dad always blames old timer's disease when he forgets where he put his keys.
She joked that she must have old timer's disease after forgetting her own phone number.
Whenever he mixes up our names, he blames it on old timer's disease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dementia - a group of symptoms affecting memory, thinking, and social abilities
