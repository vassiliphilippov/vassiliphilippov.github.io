clog dancer

noun
person who performs clog dancing
1.9
Pronunciation: / klɑɡ ˈdænsər / (AmE), / klɒɡ ˈdɑːnsə / (BrE)
a person who performs clog dancing, a traditional dance using wooden-soled shoes

Examples

Clog dancers perform steps
Many clog dancers preserve the traditions of their ancestors through their performances.
She trained for years to become a skilled clog dancer.
The best clog dancer in the region performed at the county fair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clog dancing - a type of traditional dance performed wearing clogs
