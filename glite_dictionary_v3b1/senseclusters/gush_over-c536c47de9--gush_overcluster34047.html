gush over

verb
express enthusiastic admiration
Pronunciation: / ɡʌʃ ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / ɡʌʃ ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to express enthusiastic or excessive admiration or affection for someone or something, often in an unrestrained way

Examples

To gush over a celebrity
Fans were gushing over the latest smartphone as soon as it was released.
She would often gush over her new puppy, telling everyone how cute he was.
The reporters couldn't help but gush over the actor's stunning performance on stage.
