Synonyms

    5.1 5/m
    spew is used for throwing out large amounts quickly in talking and writing, while spew out adds more force.
  • spout
    3.7 0.5/m
    spout sends a steady jet or stream of liquid or gas, while spew out often means a sudden uncontrolled burst.
  • eject
    4.1 0.9/m
    eject forces something out, often by a machine or impact, while spew out suggests a large, sudden, hard-to-control release.
  • expel
    5.0 2/m
