winter crookneck

noun
a type of hard-rinded winter squash with a curved neck
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪn.tɚ ˈkrʊk.nɛk / (AmE), / ˈwɪn.tə ˈkrʊk.nɛk / (BrE)
a type of squash with a hard rind and an elongated recurved neck, grown on various plants and harvested in winter

Examples

Fresh winter crookneck
She prepared a hearty soup using freshly harvested winter crookneck from the farmers' market.
The elongated neck of the winter crookneck makes it easy to slice for baking.
The garden produced several large winter crookneck squash this season.
