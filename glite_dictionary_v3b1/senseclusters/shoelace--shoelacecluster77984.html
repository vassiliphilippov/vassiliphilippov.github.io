shoelace

noun
lace for fastening a shoe
Pronunciation: / ˈʃuˌleɪs / (AmE), / ˈʃuːleɪs / (BrE)
a string or cord used for fastening a shoe by tying it

Examples

Shoelaces come undone
His shoelaces kept coming undone during the run.
She bent down to tie her shoelace before crossing the street.
The boy tripped because his shoelace was untied.
He bent down to tie his shoelace quickly before crossing the street.
Her new sneakers have bright red shoelaces that match her jacket.
One of my shoelaces came undone during my run this morning.

  • synonym: shoestring - a string or cord used for lacing a shoe
