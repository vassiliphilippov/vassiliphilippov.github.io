seismic

adjective
relating to earthquakes or earth vibrations
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪzmɪk / (AmE), / ˈsaɪzmɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or caused by an earthquake or earth vibrations.

Examples

A seismic survey
A seismic survey was conducted to study the underground rock formations.
Her discovery had a seismic impact on the field of geology.
The region is known for its seismic activity, including frequent minor earthquakes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tectonic - relating to the structure of the earth's crust and the processes that take place within it
