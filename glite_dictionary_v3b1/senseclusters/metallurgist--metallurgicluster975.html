metallurgist

noun
expert in the science of metals
3.5
Pronunciation: / məˈtælɚdʒɪst / (AmE), / məˈtælədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who is skilled in or studies the science and technology of metals, including their extraction, refining, alloying, and fabrication

Examples

A metallurgist studies metals
After years of study, she became a leading metallurgist at the steel plant.
Many industries rely on the expertise of a metallurgist to ensure the quality of their products.
The metallurgist developed a new technique for extracting rare metals from ore.
