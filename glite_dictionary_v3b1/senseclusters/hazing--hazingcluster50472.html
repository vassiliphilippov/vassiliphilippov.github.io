hazing

noun
initiation ritual
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈheɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
a ritual or activity involving harassment, humiliation, or challenges to initiate someone into a group

Examples

College hazing ritual
Many colleges have strict rules against hazing to protect new students.
The fraternity was suspended after reports of hazing during their initiation week.
Some organizations believe hazing builds group loyalty, but it often causes harm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bullying - the act of intimidating or mistreating someone repeatedly
  • synonym: initiation - the process of being formally accepted into a group
