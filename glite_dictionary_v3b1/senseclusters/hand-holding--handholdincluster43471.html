hand-holding

noun
holding hands
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌhoʊldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhændˌhəʊldɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of holding another person's hand, especially to show affection or provide support

Examples

Romantic hand-holding
The couple walked down the street, their hand-holding making them look very much in love.
Parents often use hand-holding to keep their children safe in crowded places.
During the scary part of the movie, there was a lot of hand-holding in the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embrace - the act of holding someone closely in one's arms
  • synonym: hug - an act of holding someone tightly in one's arms
