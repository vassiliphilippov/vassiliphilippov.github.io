Platycephalidae

noun
family of flathead fishes
Pronunciation: / ˌplætɪˌsəˈfælɪdiː /; / plætɪˌsɛfəˈlɪdiː / (AmE), / ˌplætɪˌsɛˈfælɪdiː /; / plætɪˌsiːfəˈlɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of marine fishes, known as flatheads, with flattened heads and found mainly in the Indo-Pacific region
