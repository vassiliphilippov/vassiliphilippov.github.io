pre-enlistment

noun
before joining military
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːɪnˈlɪstmənt / (AmE), / ˌpriːɪnˈlɪstmənt / (BrE)
the period or process before someone officially joins the military or an organization

Examples

Pre-enlistment period
All candidates must pass a pre-enlistment medical examination before they can join the army.
The pre-enlistment phase includes interviews and background checks for all applicants.
During pre-enlistment, recruits receive information about what to expect in basic training.

Related Terms

  • antonym: enlistment - the act of joining the military or an organization
