stunning

adjective
extremely surprising or shocking
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstʌnɪŋ / (BrE)
extremely surprising, shocking, or having a powerful effect

Examples

Stunning news
Her team suffered a stunning defeat in the championship final.
It was a stunning announcement that left the audience in complete silence.
The news of his resignation was a stunning surprise to everyone in the office.
News of the accident had a stunning impact on the entire community.
She received a stunning blow to the head during the match, knocking her out instantly.
The explosion had a stunning effect, leaving everyone confused and disoriented.

  • antonym: mild - not strong or extreme in effect
  • synonym: shocking - causing a feeling of surprise and sometimes disapproval
