stash

verb
store or hide for later use
3.1 4.2
Pronunciation: /stæʃ/ (AmE), /stæʃ/ (BrE)
to store or hide something, especially money or valuables, for future use, often secretly

Examples

To stash money

She likes to stash chocolate bars in her desk drawer in case she gets hungry.
They have been stashing supplies in the basement all winter.
