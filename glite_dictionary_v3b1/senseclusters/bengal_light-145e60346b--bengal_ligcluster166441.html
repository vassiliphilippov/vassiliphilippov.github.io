Bengal light

noun
type of firework emitting steady colored light
Pronunciation: / bɛnˈɡɑl laɪt / (AmE), / bɛnˈɡæl laɪt / (BrE)
a type of firework that produces a steady, bright colored light, originally blue, used formerly as a signal and now mainly for display

Examples

A Bengal light firework
During the festival, children enjoyed watching the blue glow of a Bengal light in the night sky.
For the grand finale, the organizers lit a series of Bengal lights along the riverbank.
The sailors waved a Bengal light to attract attention during the rescue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flare - a sudden brief burst of bright flame or light, often used as a signal
  • synonym: sparkler - a handheld firework that emits sparks when lit
