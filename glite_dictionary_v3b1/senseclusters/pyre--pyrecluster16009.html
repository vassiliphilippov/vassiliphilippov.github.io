pyre

noun
a pile for burning a body at a funeral
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈpaɪə / (BrE)
A pile of wood or other combustible material used for burning a dead body as part of a funeral rite.

Examples

A funeral pyre
A funeral pyre was built by the river, waiting to be lit at sunset.
The smoke from the burning pyre rose slowly into the evening sky.
Villagers gathered around the pyre to pay their last respects to the deceased.

Related Terms

  • synonym: funeral - a ceremony marking someone's death
