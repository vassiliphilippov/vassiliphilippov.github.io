interchain

adjective
between chains
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪn / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈtʃeɪn / (BrE)
relating to or occurring between two or more chains, especially in the context of blockchain technology

Examples

Interchain communication protocol
The platform supports interchain transactions, making it easier to transfer assets between blockchains.
Developers are working on interchain protocols to improve communication across different networks.
Security is a major concern in interchain operations involving multiple blockchain systems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cross-chain - involving or connecting different blockchains
  • antonym: intrachain - occurring within a single chain
