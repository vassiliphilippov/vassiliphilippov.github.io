Howard Carter

proper noun
English archaeologist; discoverer of Tutankhamun's tomb
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊərd ˈkɑrtər / (AmE), / ˈhaʊəd ˈkɑːtə / (BrE)
(1873–1939) English archaeologist and Egyptologist best known for discovering and excavating the tomb of Tutankhamun in 1922.

Examples

Howard Carter discovery
Howard Carter made one of the most remarkable archaeological discoveries of the twentieth century.
As a young archaeologist, Howard Carter worked throughout Egypt before achieving worldwide fame.
