retry

noun
second attempt
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈriːˌtraɪ / (AmE), / ˈriːˌtraɪ / (BrE)
an attempt to do something again, especially after failing the first time

Examples

A retry attempt
After the first failed login, the system gave him one more retry before locking the account.
You only have a single retry to complete the level in this game.
The teacher allowed a retry on the test for students who did not pass.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attempt - an act of trying to do something
  • antonym: success - the achievement of a goal or purpose
  • synonym: try - an effort to do or accomplish something
