transaction

noun
blockchain record of value transfer
Pronunciation: / trænˈzækʃən / (AmE), / trænˈzækʃən /; / trænˈzækʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
a recorded operation in computing or digital systems, especially in blockchain or cryptocurrencies, that documents the transfer of value or information

Examples

Blockchain transaction
The miner confirmed the transaction on the blockchain network this morning.
Users waited as the pending transaction moved into a block and became secure on the ledger.
Each transaction includes sender, recipient, and amount fields that nodes validate before adding it.
Every new transaction on the blockchain is timestamped and visible to all network participants.
The wallet showed three pending transactions that required confirmation before the funds would move.
During the audit, they traced each suspicious transaction back to its origin address on the ledger.

Related Terms

  • synonym: payment - the act of giving money in exchange for goods, services, or debt settlement; often implemented as a transaction in crypto contexts
  • synonym: record - an entry that preserves information about an event or action
  • synonym: transfer - the act of moving something (such as funds or data) from one place or person to another
