compact car

noun
small, economical automobile
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmˌpækt kɑr / (AmE), / ˈkɒmpækt kɑː / (BrE)
a small, fuel-efficient automobile, typically designed for use in cities and offering seating for up to five passengers

Examples

The rental agency offered a variety of compact cars for customers traveling alone or in small groups.
