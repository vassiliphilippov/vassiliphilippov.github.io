punditocracy

noun
rule by commentators
9.5
Pronunciation: /pʌn.dɪ.ˈtɑː.krə.siː/ (AmE), /pʌn.dɪ.ˈtɒ.krə.siː/ (BrE)
a group of influential commentators or experts, especially in politics or the media, who shape public opinion

Examples
