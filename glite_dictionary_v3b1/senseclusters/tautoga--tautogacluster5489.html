tautoga

noun
a species of edible marine fish (tautog)
Pronunciation: / tɔːˈtoʊɡə / (AmE), / tɔːˈtəʊɡə / (BrE)
A large, edible marine fish (Tautoga onitis) found along the Atlantic coast of North America, also known as the blackfish or tautog.
