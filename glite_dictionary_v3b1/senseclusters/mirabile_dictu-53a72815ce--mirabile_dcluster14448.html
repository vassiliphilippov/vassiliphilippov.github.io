mirabile dictu

interjection
expression of amazement
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɪˌræbɪleɪ ˈdɪktuː / (AmE), / mɪˌræbɪleɪ ˈdɪktuː / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express surprise or wonder at something remarkable or unexpected

Examples

Mirabile dictu!
The underdog team won the championship, and the crowd exclaimed, mirabile dictu.
She finished the marathon in record time—mirabile dictu—despite her injury.
The ancient manuscript was, mirabile dictu, found intact after centuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incredible - difficult to believe; extraordinary
  • synonym: unbelievable - so remarkable as to be hard to accept as true
