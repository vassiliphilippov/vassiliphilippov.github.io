quilted bedspread

noun
bed cover made like a thin quilt
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪltɪd ˈbɛdˌsprɛd / (AmE), / ˈkwɪltɪd ˈbɛdˌsprɛd / (BrE)
A bed covering made like a thin quilt, typically with a stitched or padded design.

Examples

A quilted bedspread design
After washing, the quilted bedspread dried quickly in the sun.
She chose a quilted bedspread with a floral pattern for her guest room.
The hotel rooms each feature a crisp white quilted bedspread for added comfort.

Related Terms
