safe harbor

noun
place or situation offering protection or refuge
Pronunciation: / seɪf ˈhɑrbɚ / (AmE), / seɪf ˈhɑːbə / (BrE)
a place or situation offering protection or refuge from danger or difficulty

Examples

A safe harbor from danger
Many people look for a safe harbor in times of uncertainty.
The port served as a safe harbor during the storm.
The witness was offered a safe harbor after testifying in court.
