stereo system

noun
audio equipment for 3D sound playback
3.0 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈste.ri.oʊ ˈsɪs.təm/ /; / /ˈstɪ.ri.oʊ ˈsɪs.təm/ (AmE), /ˈste.ri.əʊ ˈsɪs.təm/ /; / /ˈstɪ.ri.əʊ ˈsɪs.təm/ (BrE)
A set of electronic equipment for playing recorded music or sound, usually featuring two or more loudspeakers to create a three-dimensional audio effect.

Examples

A stereo system setup
He set up his new stereo system in the living room for optimal sound quality.
Many cars now come equipped with a built-in stereo system for entertainment.
The music sounded amazing when played through a high-quality stereo system.
