atonalistic

adjective
relating to atonalism in music
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪˌtoʊnəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌeɪˌtəʊnəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of atonalism, especially in music; lacking a tonal center or traditional harmony.

Examples

Atonalistic music style
Her dissertation focuses on the atonalistic tendencies in twentieth-century symphonies.
Some critics argue that atonalistic techniques can create emotional distance in music.
The composer's new work is highly atonalistic, challenging the listener's sense of harmony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atonal - not written in any key or mode; lacking a tonal center
