brimmingly

adverb
overflowing manner
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪmɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈbrɪmɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is full to the top or overflowing, often with emotion or enthusiasm

Examples

Smiling brimmingly with pride
She smiled brimmingly with pride at her accomplishment.
The children laughed brimmingly as they played in the park.
He accepted the award brimmingly with gratitude and excitement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overflowingly - in a way that overflows or is more than full
