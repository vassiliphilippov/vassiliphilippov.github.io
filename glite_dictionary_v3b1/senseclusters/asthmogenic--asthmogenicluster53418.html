asthmogenic

adjective
causing asthma
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌæsməˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌazməˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
able to cause or trigger asthma symptoms or attacks

Examples

Asthmogenic dust
Exposure to asthmogenic dust can worsen breathing problems.
Some cleaning products are highly asthmogenic and should be avoided by sensitive individuals.
Doctors warn that asthmogenic environments can be dangerous for children with respiratory issues.
