accel

adverb
music: gradually faster
Pronunciation: / əkˈsɛl / (AmE), / əkˈsɛl / (BrE)
in music, an instruction to play gradually faster, used as an abbreviation of accelerando

Examples

Accel marking in music
The conductor signaled for the orchestra to follow the accel marking in the score.
When you see accel written above the staff, start playing a little faster each measure.
The pianist noticed the accel and increased the tempo as the piece progressed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accelerando - an instruction to gradually increase the speed of music
  • antonym: ritardando - an instruction to gradually slow down the speed of music
