period piece

noun
work evoking a historical era
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪriəd piːs / (AmE), / ˈpɪəriəd piːs / (BrE)
a work of art, literature, or film that derives much of its value from its detailed depiction of a particular historical period

Examples

A period piece painting
Collectors value this vintage chair as a true period piece from the 18th century.
The film is a stunning period piece that captures the elegance of Victorian England.
The playwright is known for creating period pieces that transport audiences to different centuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: costume drama - a play or film set in a particular historical period, with elaborate costumes
