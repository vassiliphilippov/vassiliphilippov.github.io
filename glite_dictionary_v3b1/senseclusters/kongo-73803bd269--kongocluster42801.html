Kongo

proper noun
African river and kingdom
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
a major river in central Africa and the name of a historical kingdom in the same region

Examples

Kongo River
The Kongo River flows through several countries in central Africa.
Historians study the ancient Kongo kingdom to learn about African civilizations.
Many communities along the Kongo depend on the river for transportation and fishing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Congo - alternative spelling for the river and region in central Africa
