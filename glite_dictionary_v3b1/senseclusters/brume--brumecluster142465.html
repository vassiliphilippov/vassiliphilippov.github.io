brume

noun
poetic mist or fog
9.9
Pronunciation: / bruːm / (AmE), / bruːm / (BrE)
a poetic or literary word for mist or fog, especially as it covers a landscape

Examples

Morning brume
A pale brume drifted over the quiet valley at dawn.
The mountain peaks rose above the morning brume, glowing in the sunlight.
Through the thick brume, the distant lighthouse was barely visible.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clarity - the quality of being clear
  • synonym: fog - thick cloud of tiny water droplets near the ground
  • synonym: mist - a mass of fine droplets of water in the air near the ground
