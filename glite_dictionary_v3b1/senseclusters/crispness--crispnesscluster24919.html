crispness

noun
pleasing firmness and freshness
5.6 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkrɪsp.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈkrɪsp.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being firm, fresh, and easily broken or snapped, especially in food, materials, or fabrics
