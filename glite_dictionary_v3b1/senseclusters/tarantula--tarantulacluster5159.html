tarantula

noun
large hairy spider, esp. of southern Europe
7.3
Pronunciation: / təˈræntʃələ / (AmE), / təˈræntjʊlə / (BrE)
a large, hairy spider, especially one found in warm regions, known for its size and appearance but not usually dangerous to humans
