Islamophobic

adjective
prejudiced against Muslims
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪslɑːməˈfoʊbɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪslɑːməˈfəʊbɪk / (BrE)
showing or having prejudice, fear, or hostility toward Muslims or Islam

Examples

Islamophobic comments
The politician was criticized for making Islamophobic remarks during the interview.
Many organizations work to combat Islamophobic attitudes in society.
She spoke out against the Islamophobic behavior she witnessed on the subway.
