Indian bean

noun
climbing vine with edible beans, grown in South Asia
3.0 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈɪn.diː.ən biːn/ /; / /ˈɪn.dɪ.ən biːn/ (AmE), /ˈɪn.dɪ.ən biːn/ /; / /ˈɪn.diː.ən biːn/ (BrE)
