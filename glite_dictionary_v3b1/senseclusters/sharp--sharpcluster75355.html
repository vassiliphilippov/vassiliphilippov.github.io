sharp

noun
musical symbol for a raised note
1.9
Pronunciation: / ʃɑːrp / (AmE), / ʃɑːp / (BrE)
a musical symbol (♯) indicating that the note should be played one semitone higher in pitch.

Examples

A sharp symbol
Many students struggle to remember which sharps are in a given scale.
The pianist accidentally played a sharp instead of a natural.
There is a sharp in the key signature of G major.
