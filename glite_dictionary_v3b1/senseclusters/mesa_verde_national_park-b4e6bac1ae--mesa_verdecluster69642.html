Mesa Verde National Park

proper noun
national park in Colorado with ancient cliff dwellings
Pronunciation: / ˌmeɪsə ˈvɜrdi ˌnæʃənəl ˈpɑrk / (AmE), / ˌmeɪsə ˈvɜːdi ˌnæʃənəl ˈpɑːk / (BrE)
a national park in Colorado, United States, known for its well-preserved prehistoric cliff dwellings built by the Ancestral Puebloans
