Rajpoot

noun
member of Hindu warrior caste in northern India
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːdʒˌpuːt / (AmE), / ˈrɑːdʒˌpuːt / (BrE)
a member of the dominant Hindu warrior caste in northern India, traditionally recognized for their military and aristocratic status

Examples

A Rajpoot warrior
As a Rajpoot, he followed a strict code of honor and bravery.
Many Rajpoot families still maintain their ancestral traditions today.
The Rajpoot clans played a significant role in the history of northern India.
