butteriness

noun
buttery quality
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtəri nəs / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəri nəs / (BrE)
the quality of tasting, smelling, or feeling like butter, especially in food or texture

Examples

Butteriness of the croissant
The butteriness of the croissant made it especially delicious.
She loved the butteriness of the mashed potatoes at the restaurant.
The sauce added a pleasant butteriness to the steamed vegetables.
