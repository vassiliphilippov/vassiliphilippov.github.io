king snake

noun
nonvenomous North American snake
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪŋ ˌsneɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋ ˌsneɪk / (BrE)
A nonvenomous North American snake of the genus Lampropeltis, known for feeding on other snakes and small mammals.

Examples

A king snake
A black and white king snake slithered across the hiking trail ahead of us.
Many people keep a king snake as a pet because it is nonvenomous and easy to care for.
The king snake is known for preying on venomous snakes such as rattlesnakes.
