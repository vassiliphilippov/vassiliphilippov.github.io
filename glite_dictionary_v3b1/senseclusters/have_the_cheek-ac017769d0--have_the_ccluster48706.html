have the cheek

verb
to act with audacity or impudence
9.6 6.5
Pronunciation: /hæv ðə tʃiːk/ /; / /həv ðə tʃiːk/ (AmE), /hæv ðə tʃiːk/ /; / /həv ðə tʃiːk/ (BrE)
