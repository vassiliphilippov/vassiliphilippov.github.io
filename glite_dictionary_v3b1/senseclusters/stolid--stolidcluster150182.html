stolid

adjective
showing little emotion or excitement
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːlɪd / (AmE), / ˈstɒlɪd / (BrE)
showing little or no emotion or sensibility; not easily excited or moved

Examples

Even during the chaos of the emergency, the doctor remained stolid and focused on his work.
Her father was a stolid man who rarely laughed or cried, no matter the occasion.
