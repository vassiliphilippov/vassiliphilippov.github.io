teasel

noun
prickly plant of the genus Dipsacus
8.4 7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtiː.zəl/ (AmE), /ˈtiː.zəl/ (BrE)
a tall prickly plant of the genus Dipsacus, native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa, with flower heads surrounded by spiny bracts; formerly used in textile processing.

Examples

The spiny bracts of the teasel make it easy to recognize among other wildflowers.
