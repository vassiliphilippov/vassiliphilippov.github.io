sternum

noun
bone in the center of the chest
4.1 0.95/m
Pronunciation: / Ààst…úrn…ôm / (AmE), / Ààst…úÀên…ôm / (BrE)
the long, flat bone located in the center of the chest, connecting the clavicles and the first seven pairs of ribs

Examples

The sternum bone
Blunt trauma to the sternum can cause serious injury to the chest area.
During the surgery, the surgeon made an incision along the sternum.
The doctor placed his stethoscope just above my sternum to listen to my heartbeat.

Synonyms

  • breastbone
    3.4 0.72/m
