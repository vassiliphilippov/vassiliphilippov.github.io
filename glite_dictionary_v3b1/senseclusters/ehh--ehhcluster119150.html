ehh

interjection
uncertainty or hesitation
Pronunciation: / ɛ / (AmE), / ɛ / (BrE)
used to express uncertainty, hesitation, or lack of enthusiasm in response to a question or suggestion

Examples

Ehh, maybe
When asked if he wanted pizza or burgers, he just said ehh and shrugged.
"Are you excited for the movie tonight?" "Ehh, I could take it or leave it."
She gave an ehh when her friends suggested going to the same restaurant again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meh - used to show indifference or lack of interest
  • antonym: yeah - used to express agreement or affirmation
