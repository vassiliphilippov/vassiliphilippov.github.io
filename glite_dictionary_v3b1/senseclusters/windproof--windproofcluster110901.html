windproof

verb
to block wind
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈwɪndˌpruːf / (BrE)
to make something resistant to wind or prevent wind from entering or affecting it

Examples

To windproof a house
They decided to windproof the old barn before the storm season began.
You should windproof your tent to stay warm during the camping trip.
The engineer helped windproof the windows to prevent drafts in the office.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insulate - to protect something by surrounding it with material that reduces the passage of heat, sound, or electricity
  • synonym: seal - to close tightly to prevent the passage of air, liquid, or gas
