cold

adjective
known thoroughly; without error
Pronunciation: / koʊld / (AmE), / kəʊld / (BrE)
(informal) Learned or known so thoroughly as to be recalled or performed perfectly, without hesitation.

Examples

Know it cold
He recited the entire speech cold, without missing a word.
If you know these rules cold, you'll never get confused during the exam.
She had the multiplication tables cold before the big test.
