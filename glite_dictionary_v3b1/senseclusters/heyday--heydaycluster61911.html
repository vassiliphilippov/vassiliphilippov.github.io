heyday

noun
period of greatest success or popularity
7.1 4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈheɪ.deɪ/ (AmE), /ˈheɪ.deɪ/ (BrE)
The period of a person's or thing's greatest success, popularity, or vigor.

Examples

In heyday of fame
In his heyday, the actor was recognized all over the world.
Many believe that classical music had its heyday in the nineteenth century.
The company's heyday ended when new technology changed the industry.
