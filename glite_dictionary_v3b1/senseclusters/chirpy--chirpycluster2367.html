chirpy

adjective
cheerful and lively
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɜːrpi / (AmE), / ˈtʃɜːpi / (BrE)
cheerful and lively; noticeably happy and energetic.

Examples

Chirpy mood
After a good night's sleep, he felt much more chirpy and optimistic about the day.
She sounded chirpy on the phone this morning, full of excitement for her new job.
The children were chirpy as they got ready for the school field trip.
