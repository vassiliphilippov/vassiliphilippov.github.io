stress

verb
give emphasis or importance to
4.8 2.6
Pronunciation: /stres/ (AmE), /stres/ (BrE)
to give particular emphasis or importance to something when speaking or writing

Examples

To stress a point
I want to stress how important it is to submit your application on time.
She always stresses the value of hard work to her students.
The teacher stressed that questions should be asked if anything was unclear.
