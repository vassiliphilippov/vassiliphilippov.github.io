upaya

noun
Buddhist skillful means
9.9
Pronunciation: / uːˈpaɪə / (AmE), / uːˈpaɪə / (BrE)
a Buddhist concept referring to skillful or appropriate means used to help others achieve enlightenment

Examples

Buddhist upaya teaching
The monk explained how upaya allows teachers to adapt their methods to each student.
Through upaya, the master found creative ways to guide his followers toward enlightenment.
Understanding upaya is essential for anyone studying Mahayana Buddhism.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expedient - a means of attaining an end, especially one that is convenient but possibly improper
  • synonym: wisdom - the quality of having experience, knowledge, and good judgment
