trigone

noun
triangular bladder area
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪɡoʊn / (AmE), / ˈtrɪɡəʊn / (BrE)
a triangular area, especially the region at the base of the urinary bladder between the openings of the ureters and urethra

The trigone area
