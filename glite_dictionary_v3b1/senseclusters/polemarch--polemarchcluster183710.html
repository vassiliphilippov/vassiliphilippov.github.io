polemarch

noun
ancient Greek magistrate
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpoʊ.lə.mɑːrk/ /; / /ˈpɑː.lə.mɑːrk/ (AmE), /ˈpəʊ.lə.mɑːk/ (BrE)
