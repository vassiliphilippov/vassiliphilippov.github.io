Saratoga chip

noun
thin, crisp fried potato slice
Pronunciation: / ˌsærəˈtoʊɡə tʃɪp / (AmE), / ˌsærəˈtəʊɡə tʃɪp / (BrE)
a thin, crisp slice of potato fried in deep fat, an early name for what is now called a potato chip
