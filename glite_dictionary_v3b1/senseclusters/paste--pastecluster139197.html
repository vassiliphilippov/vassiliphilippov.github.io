paste

verb
attach or insert with glue or by copying
Pronunciation: / peɪst / (AmE), / peɪst / (BrE)
to attach, join, or cover something using paste or glue, or to insert copied material into a new location (such as in a document)

Examples

To paste text
After copying the paragraph, just paste it into your document wherever you want.
She carefully pasted the magazine clippings into her scrapbook.
You can paste the photo onto the poster with glue.
Before painting, they pasted the ceiling with a moisture-resistant layer.
He carefully pastes wallpaper in the living room, making sure there are no bubbles.
The workers pasted the posters onto the walls overnight.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cut - to remove a section of text or material in order to move or delete it
  • synonym: glue - to stick or fasten with an adhesive substance
