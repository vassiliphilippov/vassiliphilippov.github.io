archenemy

noun
main enemy
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrtʃˈɛnəmi /; / ˌɑrtʃˈɛnəmi / (AmE), / ˌɑːtʃˈɛnəmi / (BrE)
a person's or character's main and most important enemy, often in a long-standing rivalry

Sherlock Holmes often outsmarts his archenemy, Professor Moriarty.
