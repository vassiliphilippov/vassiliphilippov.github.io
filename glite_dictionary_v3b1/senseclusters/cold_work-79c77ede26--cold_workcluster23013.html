cold work

verb
shape metal without heat
7.8 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / ko äld w…úrk / (AmE), / k…ô äld w…úÀêk / (BrE)
to shape (metal) by mechanical means at room temperature, without the application of heat.

Examples

To cold work metal
If you cold work steel, you can achieve precise dimensions without altering its structure by heat.
Sheet metal is often cold worked to improve its strength and hardness.
Some alloys are difficult to cold work because they become too brittle at room temperature.
