stay home

verb
remain in one's residence
Pronunciation: / steɪ hoʊm / (AmE), / steɪ həʊm / (BrE)
to remain in one's residence instead of going out

Examples

To stay home all day
During the storm, we decided to stay home for safety.
If you are feeling unwell, please stay home from work.
She prefers to stay home on weekends and read books.
