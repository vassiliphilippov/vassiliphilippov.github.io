crystal clear

adjective
perfectly clear; easily understood
1.0 2.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkrɪs.təl klɪr/ (AmE), /ˈkrɪs.təl klɪə/ (BrE)
extremely easy to see, hear, or understand; obvious or perfectly clear in meaning

Examples

Crystal clear explanation
He spoke in a crystal clear voice that everyone could hear.
Her explanation of the process was crystal clear and easy to follow.
The manager's instructions were crystal clear, leaving no room for misunderstanding.
His motives were crystal clear to everyone present in the room.
It needs to be crystal clear how the new policy will affect us.
She made her expectations crystal clear before the meeting began.
