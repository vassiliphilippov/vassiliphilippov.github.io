chargeless

adjective
electrically neutral
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑrdʒləs / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːdʒləs / (BrE)
having no electric charge; electrically neutral, neither positive nor negative

Examples

Chargeless particle
A neutron is a chargeless particle found in the nucleus of an atom.
Because it is chargeless, the particle was unaffected by the electric field.
Scientists studied the behavior of chargeless molecules in different environments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neutral - having no net electric charge
