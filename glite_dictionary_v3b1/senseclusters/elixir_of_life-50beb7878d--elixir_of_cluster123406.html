elixir of life

noun
mythical substance granting immortality
5.8
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɪksər əv laɪf / (AmE), / ɪˈlɪksə əv laɪf / (BrE)
a mythical or hypothetical substance thought to grant immortality or maintain youth and life indefinitely, especially in alchemical tradition
