chartplotter

noun
marine navigation device
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑrtˌplɑtər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːtˌplɒtə / (BrE)
an electronic device that displays nautical charts and assists with marine navigation

Examples

Marine chartplotter

A modern chartplotter can display both maps and real-time GPS data for the crew.
