non-totalitarian

adjective
not totalitarian
Pronunciation: / nɑn toʊˌtæləˈtɛriən / (AmE), / nɒn təʊˌtælɪˈteəriən / (BrE)
not relating to or characteristic of a totalitarian system or government

Examples

Non-totalitarian government
After the revolution, the nation adopted a non-totalitarian constitution that protected individual rights.
Many citizens hoped for a non-totalitarian government that would allow freedom of speech and assembly.
The new leader promised to establish a non-totalitarian political system with fair elections.

Related Terms

  • synonym: democratic - relating to a system of government by the whole population, typically through elected representatives
  • antonym: totalitarian - relating to a system of government that is centralized and dictatorial
