afternoon breeze

noun
light wind in the afternoon
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌæftɚˈnun briːz / (AmE), / ˌɑːftəˈnuːn briːz / (BrE)
a light wind that occurs during the afternoon, often providing relief from the heat

Examples

A gentle afternoon breeze
A gentle afternoon breeze rustled the pages of her book as she read outside.
The afternoon breeze carried the scent of flowers across the garden.
We sat on the porch and enjoyed the afternoon breeze cooling the air.
