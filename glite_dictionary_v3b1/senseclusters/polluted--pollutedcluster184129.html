polluted

adjective
contaminated by harmful substances
Pronunciation: / pəˈluːtɪd / (AmE), / pəˈluːtɪd / (BrE)
contaminated or made harmful by the presence of pollutants or harmful substances

Examples

Polluted water
Children are taught not to swim in polluted water for their safety.
Many cities struggle to reduce their polluted air during the summer months.
The river was so polluted that fish could no longer survive in it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clean - free from dirt, marks, or pollutants
  • synonym: contaminated - made impure or unsafe by the introduction of pollutants or toxins
