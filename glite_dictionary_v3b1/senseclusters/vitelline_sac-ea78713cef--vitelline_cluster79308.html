vitelline sac

noun
membrane supporting circulation in embryos
Pronunciation: / vɪˈtɛlɪn sæk / (AmE), / vɪˈtɛlɪn sæk / (BrE)
a membranous structure in mammalian embryos that serves as a circulatory system until the heart becomes functional

Examples

The vitelline sac membrane
Defects in the vitelline sac can lead to abnormal embryonic development.
Researchers studied the blood flow within the vitelline sac during early pregnancy.
The vitelline sac provides nutrients to the developing embryo before the placenta is formed.
