glove-like

adjective
resembling a glove
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlʌvˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡlʌvˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having separate sections or projections for each finger, resembling the shape or structure of a glove

Examples

Glove-like fingers
The costume included glove-like sleeves that fit each finger individually.
Some diving suits have glove-like attachments to allow for better grip underwater.
The robot's glove-like hands could pick up delicate objects with precision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fingered - having parts or sections for the fingers
  • antonym: mitt-like - resembling a mitten, without separate sections for each finger
