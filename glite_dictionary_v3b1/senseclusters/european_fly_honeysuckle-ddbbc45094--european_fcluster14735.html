European fly honeysuckle

noun
Eurasian shrub with yellowish-white flowers and red fruit
9.0
Pronunciation: / jʊˌroʊpiən flaɪ ˈhʌniˌsʌkəl / (AmE), / jʊəˌrəʊpiən flaɪ ˈhʌniˌsʌkəl / (BrE)
a Eurasian shrub (Lonicera xylosteum) with pairs of yellowish-white flowers and bright scarlet fruit, often cultivated as an ornamental plant
