pheasant

noun
large long-tailed game bird
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛzənt / (AmE), / ˈfɛznt / (BrE)
a large bird with a long tail, native to Asia and other parts of the Old World, often introduced elsewhere and hunted for sport or food

Examples

Wild pheasant bird
A pheasant flew out of the bushes as we walked through the field.
He roasted a wild pheasant for the family dinner.
We spotted several brightly colored pheasants near the edge of the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: partridge - a short-tailed game bird related to the pheasant
  • degree lower: quail - a small, short-tailed game bird
