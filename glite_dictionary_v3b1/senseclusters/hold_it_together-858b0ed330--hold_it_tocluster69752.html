hold it together

verb
maintain composure or control
Pronunciation: / hoʊld ɪt təˈɡɛðər / (AmE), / həʊld ɪt təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to remain calm and maintain self-control, especially in a difficult situation

Examples

To hold it together in crisis
After hearing the shocking news, she struggled to hold it together in front of her friends.
He managed to hold it together during the crisis and supported his family.
Sometimes, it's hard to hold it together when everything seems to go wrong.

Related Terms

  • synonym: keep one's cool - to stay calm in a stressful situation
  • antonym: lose it - to lose control of one's emotions
