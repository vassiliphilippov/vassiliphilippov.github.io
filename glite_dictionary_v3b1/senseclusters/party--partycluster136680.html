party

verb
take part in a social gathering
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrti / (AmE), / ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
to have or take part in social gatherings with music, dancing, and celebration, often involving drinking and enjoyment

Examples

To party all night
She loves to party with her friends every weekend.
They partied so hard that some neighbors complained about the noise.
We decided to party until sunrise after finishing our exams.

Related Terms

  • synonym: celebrate - to observe a special day or event with festivities
