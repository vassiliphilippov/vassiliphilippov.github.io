assassinate

verb
to murder an important person
Pronunciation: / əˈsæs.ɪ.neɪt / (AmE), / əˈsæs.ɪ.neɪt / (BrE)
to murder an important or prominent person, especially for political or ideological reasons

Examples

To assassinate a leader
Historical figures have been assassinated for their political beliefs.
Terrorists attempted to assassinate the ambassador during his speech.
The president was assassinated while traveling abroad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kill - to cause the death of someone or something
  • synonym: murder - the unlawful killing of another person
