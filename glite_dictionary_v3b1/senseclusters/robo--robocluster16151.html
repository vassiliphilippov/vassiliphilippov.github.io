robo

noun
informal robot
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊboʊ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊbəʊ / (BrE)

Examples

A toy robo
The kids built a small robo for their science project at school.
She bought a new cleaning robo that vacuums the entire house automatically.
At the tech fair, every booth had a different kind of robo on display.
