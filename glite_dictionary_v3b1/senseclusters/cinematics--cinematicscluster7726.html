cinematics

noun
video game cutscenes
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪnəˈmætɪks / (AmE), / ˌsɪnəˈmætɪks / (BrE)
short, usually non-interactive video or animated sequences in a video game that present story or events

Examples

Game cinematics sequence
The cinematics in the new game are so impressive that they feel like scenes from a movie.
Players can choose to skip the cinematics if they want to get straight to the action.
After each level, a short cinematics sequence reveals more about the characters' journey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cutscene - a non-interactive scene in a video game that advances the story
  • antonym: gameplay - the interactive part of a video game
