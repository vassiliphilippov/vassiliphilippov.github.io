incontestably

adverb
undeniably
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnkənˈtɛstəbli / (AmE), / ˌɪnkənˈtɛstəbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be disputed or doubted

Examples

The results were incontestably clear
The results were incontestably in favor of the new policy.
She was incontestably the most qualified candidate for the position.
The painting is incontestably a masterpiece of modern art.

Related Terms

  • antonym: arguably - in a way that can be supported by argument but not certain
  • synonym: indisputably - in a way that cannot be challenged or questioned
  • synonym: undeniably - in a way that cannot be denied
