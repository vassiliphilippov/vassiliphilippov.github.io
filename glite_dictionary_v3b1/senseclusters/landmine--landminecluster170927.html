landmine

noun
buried explosive device
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlændˌmaɪn / (AmE), / ˈlændˌmaɪn / (BrE)
an explosive device placed on or under the ground, designed to detonate when triggered by pressure or contact

Examples

Hidden landmine explosion
The soldiers carefully marked the area where a landmine was discovered.
Many civilians are injured each year by hidden landmines left after conflicts.
Special teams work to remove every landmine from former battlefields to make them safe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: booby trap - a hidden device intended to cause harm when triggered
  • synonym: mine - an explosive device placed in the ground or water
