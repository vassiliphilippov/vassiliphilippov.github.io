unilingual

noun
person using one language
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːnɪˈlɪŋɡwəl / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪˈlɪŋɡwəl / (BrE)
a person who speaks or uses only one language

Examples

Unilingual speaker
In some remote areas, most residents are unilingual and speak only their native language.
The school offers special programs for unilingual students to help them learn a second language.
Being unilingual can make it harder to travel or work in multilingual countries.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bilingual - a person who speaks two languages fluently
  • synonym: monolingual - a person who speaks only one language
