valved

adjective
having valves (esp. in brass instruments)
Pronunciation: / vælvd / (AmE), / vælvd / (BrE)
(of a brass instrument) having valves for altering pitch.

Examples

Valved trumpet
A valved trumpet allows for more complex melodies than a natural trumpet.
Modern brass instruments are typically valved, making them more versatile.
The orchestra featured both valved and valveless horns.

Related Terms

  • antonym: valveless - (of a brass instrument) lacking valves
