EOM

noun
end of month
Pronunciation: / i oʊ ɛm / (AmE), / iː əʊ ɛm / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'end of month', used to refer to the last day of a month in business or accounting contexts

Examples

Report by EOM
Please submit all expense reports by EOM to ensure timely processing.
The project deadline has been extended to EOM for all teams.
Bonuses will be calculated based on sales figures as of EOM.

Related Terms

  • synonym: month-end - the end of a calendar month
