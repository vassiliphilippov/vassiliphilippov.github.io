valedictory oration

noun
farewell speech at graduation
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌvæləˈdɪktəri ɔːˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌvalɪˈdɪktəri ɔːˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
A formal farewell speech, especially one delivered by an outstanding member of a graduating class at a graduation ceremony.

Examples

A valedictory oration speech
He spent weeks crafting his valedictory oration to express gratitude to his teachers and classmates.
She was honored to deliver the valedictory oration at her university's commencement ceremony.
The audience listened attentively as the top student gave a heartfelt valedictory oration.
