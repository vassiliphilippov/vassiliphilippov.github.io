indicia

noun
identifying marks
9.6
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈdɪ.ʃə/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈdɪ.ʃə/ (BrE)
signs, marks, or features that indicate or identify something, especially as evidence or proof

Examples

Indicia of a crime
The detective looked for indicia of forced entry at the crime scene.
Official indicia on the document proved its authenticity.
The package bore several indicia showing it had passed through customs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: concealment - the act of hiding something
  • synonym: evidence - facts or signs that show something exists or is true
  • synonym: sign - an object, quality, or event whose presence indicates something
