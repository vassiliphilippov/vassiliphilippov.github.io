worthy

noun
an important or respected person
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrði / (AmE), / ˈwɜːði / (BrE)
a person who is important, respected, or honorable, often used with humorous or ironic intent

Examples

A local worthy
At the banquet, all the town’s worthies gathered to celebrate the new hospital.
Some of the region’s most celebrated worthies attended the launch of the new library.
The mayor invited several local worthies to speak at the anniversary event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: notable - a famous or important person
