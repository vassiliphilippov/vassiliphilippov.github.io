clip-clop

noun
sound of horse's hooves on a hard surface
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪpˌklɑp / (AmE), / ˈklɪpˌklɒp / (BrE)
the sound made by the hooves of a horse or similar animal as they strike a hard surface, typically represented as a rhythmic, repeating noise

Examples

The clip-clop sound
He listened to the steady clip-clop as the carriage approached.
The clip-clop of horses echoed down the narrow cobblestone street.
The familiar clip-clop reassured her that the old cart was on its way.
