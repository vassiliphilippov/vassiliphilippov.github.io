Crescentia

noun
genus of tropical American trees
9.9
Pronunciation: / krəˈsɛn(t)ʃiə /; / krɛˈsɛnʃiə / (AmE), / krɛˈsɛn.tsi.ə /; / krɛˈsɛnʃiə / (BrE)
