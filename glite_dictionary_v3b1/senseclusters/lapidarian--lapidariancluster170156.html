lapidarian

adjective
inscribed on stone
Pronunciation: / ˌlæpɪˈdɛriən / (AmE), / ˌlæpɪˈdɛːrɪən / (BrE)
(of writing, records, etc.) inscribed on stone; relating to inscriptions made on stone monuments.

Examples

Lapidarian inscription
Only a few surviving documents from that era exist, most of them lapidarian in nature.
Scholars studied the lapidarian records to reconstruct the city's lost history.
The ancient temple's walls bore a lapidarian inscription honoring the gods.
