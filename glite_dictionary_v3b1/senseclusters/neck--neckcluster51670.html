neck

noun
part connecting head to body
Pronunciation: / nɛk / (AmE), / nɛk / (BrE)
the part of the body connecting the head to the rest of the body in humans and animals

Examples

Stiff neck
He turned his neck to see what was behind him.
She wore a delicate chain around her neck.
The giraffe has an unusually long neck compared to other animals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: throat - the front part of the neck, containing the pharynx and larynx
