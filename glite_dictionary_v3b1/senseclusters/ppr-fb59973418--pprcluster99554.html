PPR

noun
plastic for pipes
6.7
Pronunciation: / piː piː ɑːr / (AmE), / piː piː ɑː / (BrE)
a type of durable plastic, polypropylene random copolymer, commonly used for water pipes and plumbing fittings

Examples

PPR water pipes
The plumber recommended using PPR pipes because they resist corrosion and withstand high temperatures.
Many modern buildings use PPR fittings for their water supply systems.
Due to its durability, PPR is often chosen for both hot and cold water installations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: PVC - a common plastic used for pipes and fittings
  • synonym: polypropylene - a type of plastic used in various products
