azotobacter biofertilizer

noun
biofertilizer with Azotobacter bacteria
Pronunciation: / ˌæzəˌtoʊbəˈtɛkər ˌbaɪoʊˌfɝt̬ɪˈlaɪzər / (AmE), / ˌazətəʊbəˈtɛkə ˌbʌɪəʊˌfəːtɪˈlaɪzə / (BrE)
A biofertilizer containing bacteria of the genus Azotobacter, used to enhance soil fertility by fixing atmospheric nitrogen.

Examples

Azotobacter biofertilizer use
Many farmers use azotobacter biofertilizer to improve the nitrogen content of their soil naturally.
Researchers are developing new types of azotobacter biofertilizer to enhance sustainable agriculture.
The application of azotobacter biofertilizer has been shown to increase crop yields in organic farming.

  • synonym: biofertilizer - a substance containing living microorganisms, applied to soil to increase its fertility
