acrimony

noun
bitterness or harshness in manner
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈækrəˌmoʊni / (AmE), / ˈækrɪməni / (BrE)
a bitter or harsh manner, tone, or feeling; sharpness in speech or disposition

Examples

Sharp acrimony
He spoke with unusual acrimony, surprising even his close friends.
Their divorce was marked by acrimony and unresolved disputes.
There was a great deal of acrimony in the debate over the new policy.
