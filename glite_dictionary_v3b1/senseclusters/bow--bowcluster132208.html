bow

noun
something curved in shape
Pronunciation: / boʊ / (AmE), / bəʊ / (BrE)
something that is curved in shape, often resembling an arch or a bend

Examples

Bow of a bridge
She admired the graceful bow of the old stone bridge.
The archer strung his bow with great care.
The rainbow formed a perfect bow across the sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arc - a curved shape forming part of a circle
  • synonym: curve - a smoothly flowing, continuous line or surface that differs from a straight line
