flighty

adjective
unpredictable or frivolous (person)
4.7 5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪti / (AmE), / ˈflaɪti / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) unpredictable, excitable, or easily distracted; not steady or reliable

Examples

A flighty person
He worried about trusting such a flighty business partner.
Her flighty nature made it difficult for her to stick to any one plan.
The flighty actress was known for changing her mind at the last minute.
Despite his flighty temperament, the dog quickly became attached to the family.
Her flighty nature made it difficult for her to focus on any task for long.
The young horse was too flighty for inexperienced riders to handle safely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capricious - given to sudden and unaccountable changes of mood or behavior
  • synonym: fickle - changing frequently, especially as regards one's loyalties or affections
  • antonym: steady - firmly fixed, constant, dependable
