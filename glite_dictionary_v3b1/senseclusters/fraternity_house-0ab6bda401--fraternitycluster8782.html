fraternity house

noun
house for members of a college fraternity
3.3 0.23/m
Pronunciation: / fr…ôÀàt…úrn…ôti ha äs / (AmE), / fr…ôÀàt…úÀên…ôti ha äs / (BrE)
A house used as a residence by members of a college fraternity, typically serving as their official gathering place and living quarters.

Examples

A fraternity house
After joining the fraternity, he moved into the fraternity house with his new brothers.
The college campus was dotted with fraternity houses, each with its own traditions and parties.
The university set strict rules on noise levels in every fraternity house near the dormitories.
