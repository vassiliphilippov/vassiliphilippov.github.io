firster

noun
first-time participant
Pronunciation: / ˈfɜrstər / (AmE), / ˈfɜːstə / (BrE)
a person who is doing something for the first time

Examples

A firster in class
As a firster in the competition, she was nervous but excited.
The club welcomes every firster and helps them feel comfortable.
Being a firster at the conference, he asked many questions to learn more.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beginner - a person who is new to an activity or skill
  • synonym: novice - someone with little or no experience in a field
  • antonym: veteran - a person with long experience in a particular field
