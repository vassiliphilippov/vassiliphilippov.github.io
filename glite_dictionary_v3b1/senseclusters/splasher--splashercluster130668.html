splasher

noun
protective cover above a wheel
Pronunciation: / ˈsplæʃər / (AmE), / ˈsplæʃə / (BrE)
a protective covering or panel, especially on a vehicle, that prevents mud or water from splashing onto people or the vehicle

Examples

A splasher panel
A missing splasher can lead to mud splattering the body of the vehicle.
Many bicycles are equipped with a splasher to keep mud off the rider’s clothes.
The car’s splasher was damaged after driving through deep puddles.
After driving through the puddle, the car's splasher prevented water from hitting the windows.
She attached a new splasher to her raincoat for added protection.
The bicycle's splasher kept most of the mud from reaching his clothes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fender - a part of a vehicle that frames a wheel well to prevent road debris splashing up
  • synonym: mudguard - a curved cover over a vehicle wheel to prevent mud from being thrown up
