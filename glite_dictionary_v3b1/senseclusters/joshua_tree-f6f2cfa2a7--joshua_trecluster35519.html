Joshua tree

noun
desert tree-like yucca of the southwestern US
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑʃuə tri / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒʃuə tri / (BrE)
A large, branching yucca plant native to the southwestern United States, with spiky leaves and clusters of greenish white flowers.
