insecticidal

adjective
able to kill insects; relating to insecticide
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnˌsɛktɪˈsaɪdəl / (AmE), / ˌɪn.sɛk.tɪˈsaɪ.dəl / (BrE)
relating to or capable of killing insects, especially by means of an insecticide.

Examples

Insecticidal activity
Researchers tested the insecticidal activity of the plant extract in the laboratory.
Some household cleaners have insecticidal properties effective against ants and cockroaches.
The farmer sprayed an insecticidal solution to protect his crops from pests.
