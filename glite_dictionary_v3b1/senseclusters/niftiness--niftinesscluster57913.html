niftiness

noun
cleverness or style
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪftinəs / (AmE), / ˈnɪftinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being clever, attractive, or well-designed

Examples

The niftiness of design
The niftiness of the new app impressed everyone at the launch event.
He admired the niftiness of her solution to the tricky problem.
The kitchen's niftiness made cooking there a real pleasure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cleverness - the quality of being smart or inventive
  • antonym: dullness - the quality of being uninteresting or lacking originality
  • synonym: style - a distinctive and attractive appearance or design
