riverside

noun
the bank or edge of a river
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪvərˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈrɪvəsaɪd / (BrE)

Examples

The riverside park

There is a beautiful path that follows the riverside for several miles.
