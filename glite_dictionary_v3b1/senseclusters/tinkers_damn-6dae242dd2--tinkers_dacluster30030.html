tinker's damn

noun
thing of little or no value
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪŋkərz dæm / (AmE), / ˈtɪŋkəz dam / (BrE)
A thing or amount regarded as having little or no value or importance.

Examples

Not worth a tinker's damn
He doesn't give a tinker's damn about office politics.
She tried her best, but it didn’t make a tinker's damn of difference.
The old jacket isn’t worth a tinker's damn anymore.

Related Terms

  • synonym: damn - something of little value (in the idiomatic phrase 'not worth a damn')
  • synonym: red cent - something of very little value (as in the phrase 'not worth a red cent')
