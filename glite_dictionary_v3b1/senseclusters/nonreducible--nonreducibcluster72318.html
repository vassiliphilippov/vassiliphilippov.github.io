nonreducible

adjective
cannot be simplified
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnrɪˈduːsəbəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnrɪˈdjuːsəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be made simpler or broken down into smaller parts or forms

Examples

Nonreducible fraction
The mathematician explained that the fraction was nonreducible and could not be simplified further.
Some chemical compounds are nonreducible under normal laboratory conditions.
The problem is nonreducible, so we must approach it differently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irreducible - not able to be reduced or simplified
  • antonym: reducible - able to be made simpler or broken down
