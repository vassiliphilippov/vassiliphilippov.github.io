bosque

noun
riverbank forest
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːskeɪ / (AmE), / ˈbɒskeɪ / (BrE)
a forested area, especially one along a river in the southwestern United States

Examples

A bosque along river
We walked for hours through the cool shade of the bosque along the Rio Grande.
Many animals make their homes in the bosque near the riverbanks.
The cottonwood trees in the bosque turn golden in the fall.
We hiked through the cottonwood bosque near the river.
Birdwatchers often visit the bosque to observe rare species along the water.
The city built a trail that winds through the lush bosque beside the Rio Grande.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forest - a large area covered chiefly with trees and undergrowth
  • synonym: woodland - land covered with trees
