thermohydrometric

adjective
concerning measurement of temperature and water properties
Pronunciation: / ˌθɜːrmoʊˌhaɪdroʊˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌθɜːməʊˌhaɪdrəʊˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
Relating to the measurement of temperature and properties of fluids, especially water.

Examples

Thermohydrometric analysis
A thermohydrometric analysis was conducted to assess changes in water temperature and flow during the experiment.
Accurate thermohydrometric readings are crucial in climate and environmental studies.
The researchers developed a new thermohydrometric device for monitoring river systems.
