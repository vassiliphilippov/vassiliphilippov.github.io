probationer

noun
nurse in training on trial period
Pronunciation: / proʊˈbeɪʃənər / (AmE), / prəˈbeɪʃənə / (BrE)
a nurse in training who is undergoing a trial or evaluation period before being fully qualified

Examples

Nurse probationer
After six months as a probationer, she was accepted into the full nursing staff.
As a probationer, he worked long hours and learned from experienced nurses.
Each probationer is assigned a mentor to guide them during their initial placement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trainee nurse - a person training to be a nurse
