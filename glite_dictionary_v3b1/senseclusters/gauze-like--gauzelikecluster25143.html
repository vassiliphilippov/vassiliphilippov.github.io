gauze-like

adjective
very thin and light, like gauze
Pronunciation: / ɡɔz laɪk / (AmE), / ɡɔːz laɪk / (BrE)
Very thin and light, resembling gauze; so fine or delicate as to be semi-transparent.

Examples

A gauze-like fabric
She wore a gauze-like scarf that fluttered in the slightest breeze.
The curtains were made from a gauze-like fabric that let soft sunlight filter into the room.
Through the gauze-like mist, the silhouette of the mountain appeared ghostly and faint.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diaphanous - light, delicate, and translucent
  • antonym: opaque - not able to be seen through; not transparent
