angularly

adverb
in a stiff manner
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡjələrli / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡjʊləli / (BrE)
in a stiff, awkward, or jerky manner, lacking smoothness or fluidity of movement

Examples

Moves angularly on stage
He walked angularly across the stage, his movements sharp and abrupt.
The robot moved angularly, making sudden turns at each corner.
She gestured angularly, her arms moving in stiff, jerky motions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awkwardly - in a clumsy or uncomfortable way
  • antonym: smoothly - in a fluid or graceful manner
