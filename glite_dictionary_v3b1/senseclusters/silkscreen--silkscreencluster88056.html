silkscreen

noun
print made with a mesh stencil process
5.8 0.11/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs…™lkÀåskrin / (AmE), / Ààs…™lkÀåskriÀên / (BrE)
a print made by forcing ink through a fine mesh screen that has areas blocked off by a stencil, creating a design on the surface below

Examples

A silkscreen print
A limited-edition silkscreen of the band's logo was sold to fans at the concert.
She learned how to create a silkscreen in her printmaking class last semester.
The artist showcased a vibrant silkscreen of a city skyline at the gallery.

Synonyms

  • silk screen print
    4.2 0.01/m
    a silk screen print is made by pressing ink through a mesh blocked by a stencil, while silkscreen refers to that stencil screen printing method.
  • serigraph
    9.0 0.06/m
    a serigraph is an art print often sold and signed by its maker, while silkscreen is the general stencil screen printing method.
