sense organ

noun
artificial sensor for machines
4.8
Pronunciation: /sens ˈɔːr.gən/ (AmE), /sens ˈɔː.gən/ (BrE)
(in robotics and computing) an artificial sensor or set of sensors in a machine that performs the function of a biological sense organ, allowing the machine to detect light, sound, distance, temperature, or other environmental signals.

Examples

Robot sense organ
The autonomous drone relies on cameras and LiDAR as its sense organ.
Engineers fitted the inspection robot with a thermal sense organ to find heat loss in pipes.
In the lecture, she compared the rover's radar to a biological sense organ.

  • synonym: sensor - a device that detects or measures a physical property and records or signals its presence
