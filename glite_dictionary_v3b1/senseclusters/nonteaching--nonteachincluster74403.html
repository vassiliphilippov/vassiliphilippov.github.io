nonteaching

noun
duties not involving instruction
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈtiːtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈtiːtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
work, duties, or activities in an educational setting that do not involve teaching or direct instruction

Examples

Nonteaching duties
She was assigned several nonteaching tasks, including organizing school events and managing records.
Many staff members handle nonteaching responsibilities such as supervising the library or coordinating activities.
His nonteaching duties take up almost as much time as his classroom work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: administrative - relating to the organization and management of an institution
  • antonym: teaching - the act or profession of instructing students
