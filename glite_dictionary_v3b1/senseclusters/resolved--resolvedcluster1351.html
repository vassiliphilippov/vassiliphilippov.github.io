resolved

adjective
determined; firmly decided
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɑlvd / (AmE), / rɪˈzɒlvd / (BrE)
Determined to do something and not willing to change your mind.

Examples

Resolved to act
After months of uncertainty, she felt resolved to take on the new challenge.
Despite many setbacks, the team remained resolved to finish the project on time.
He sounded resolved when he announced his plan to move abroad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: determined - showing firmness and purpose; not easily persuaded to give up
  • antonym: undecided - not having made a decision or resolution
