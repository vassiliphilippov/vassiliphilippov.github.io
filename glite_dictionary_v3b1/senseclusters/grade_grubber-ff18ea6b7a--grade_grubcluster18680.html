grade grubber

noun
student overly concerned with grades
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪd ˌɡrʌb.ər / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪd ˌɡrʌb.ə / (BrE)
a student who is excessively concerned with getting high grades, often seeking extra points or favors from teachers

Examples

A grade grubber student
Most students found his grade grubber tendencies a bit annoying.
She's such a grade grubber that she emails the professor about every missed point.
The teacher called him a grade grubber for his constant requests.
