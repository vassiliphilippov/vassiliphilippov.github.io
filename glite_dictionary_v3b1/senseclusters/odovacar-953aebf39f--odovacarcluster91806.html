Odovacar

proper noun
Germanic leader, first barbarian king of Italy
9.9

A Germanic leader who overthrew the last Western Roman Emperor in 476 AD and became the first barbarian king of Italy.

Examples

Odovacar the king
Odovacar deposed Romulus Augustulus and assumed control of Italy in 476 AD.
After defeating Orestes, Odovacar established his rule over the Italian peninsula.
Historians often credit Odovacar with ending the Western Roman Empire.
