provisioner

noun
supplier of food or supplies, esp. to army
5.4
Pronunciation: / prəˈvɪʒənər / (AmE), / prəˈvɪʒənə / (BrE)
a person or organization responsible for supplying food and other provisions, especially to an army or large group

Examples

Army provisioner
As a provisioner, she managed contracts for food and equipment for the troops.
During wartime, the role of the provisioner became even more critical for survival.
The army relied on their trusted provisioner to deliver rations to the front lines.
