fansite

noun
fan-made website
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfænˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈfanˌsaɪt / (BrE)
a website created and maintained by fans that is dedicated to a particular person, group, or topic

Examples

A fansite about actors
She updates her fansite every week with news about the actor.
The fansite has a large community of people who share photos and stories.
Many people visit the fansite to find rare interviews and videos.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fan page - an online page dedicated to a celebrity, group, or topic, run by fans
  • antonym: official website - the main website run by the person or group themselves
