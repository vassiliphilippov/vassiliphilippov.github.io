bow-wow

noun
the bark of a dog
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊ ˌwaʊ / (AmE), / ˈbaʊ ˌwaʊ / (BrE)
the bark or sound made by a dog (the word 'bow-wow' imitates this sound)

Examples

A loud bow-wow
Children laughed every time they heard the dog's happy bow-wow near the playground.
The little puppy's playful bow-wow echoed through the garden as it chased its tail.
We were awakened by the loud bow-wow of dogs barking in the street.
From outside, a loud bow-wow echoed through the quiet house.
She imitated a dog's bow-wow during the performance.
The puppy's sudden bow-wow made the children laugh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bark - the sharp explosive cry of a dog
  • synonym: woof - the bark or vocal sound of a dog
