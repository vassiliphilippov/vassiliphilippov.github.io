outrage

noun
a shocking or disgraceful event
3.2 3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌreɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈaʊt.reɪdʒ / (BrE)
a shocking, cruel, or disgraceful event or action that causes strong anger or indignation
