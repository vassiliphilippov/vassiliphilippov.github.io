lather

noun
foam from sweating, esp. on a horse
9.9 5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈlæð.ər/ (AmE), /ˈlɑː.ðə/ /; / /ˈlæð.ə/ (BrE)
Foam or froth produced by excessive sweating, especially on the skin of a horse or other animal.

Examples

Horse in lather
After a long ride, the horse was covered in lather and breathing heavily.
The jockey wiped away the lather from the horse's neck before leading it to water.
Working in the heat, the oxen were soon in a lather.
