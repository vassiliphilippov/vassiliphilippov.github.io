metrician

noun
expert in measurement
Pronunciation: / məˈtrɪʃən / (AmE), / mɪˈtrɪʃən / (BrE)

A metrician checks data

As a skilled metrician, she developed new methods for measuring customer satisfaction.
Every research team needs a metrician to ensure reliable data collection and analysis.
