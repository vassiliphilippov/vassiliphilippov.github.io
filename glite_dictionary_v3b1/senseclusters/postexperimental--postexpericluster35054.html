postexperimental

adjective
after experimental phase
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstˌɛkˌspɛrɪˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˌɛkˌspɛrɪˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
relating to a stage, movement, or approach that follows and often responds to experimental methods, especially in art, music, or research.

Examples

Postexperimental art style
The artist's postexperimental period focused on traditional techniques after years of abstract work.
Many critics see the new movement as distinctly postexperimental, blending structure with innovation.
Her latest book explores postexperimental approaches to narrative, moving beyond earlier avant-garde styles.

Related Terms

  • antonym: experimental - involving new ideas or methods, especially in art or science
  • synonym: postmodern - relating to a movement that comes after and reacts to modernism
