interconvert

verb
to change between forms
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərkənˈvɜrt / (AmE), / ˌɪntəkənˈvɜːt / (BrE)
to change from one form, substance, or state into another and back again, especially in scientific contexts

Examples

To interconvert forms
Under certain conditions, the two isomers can interconvert rapidly in solution.
Scientists discovered that the compounds interconvert when exposed to light and heat.
The process allows energy and matter to interconvert in nuclear reactions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: convert - to change something into a different form or use
  • synonym: transform - to change completely in form or appearance
