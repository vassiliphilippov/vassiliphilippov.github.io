requiescat

noun
prayer for the dead
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛkwiˈɛskæt / (AmE), / ˌrɛkwɪˈɛskæt / (BrE)
A prayer or wish for the rest and peace of the soul of a deceased person.

Examples

A requiescat prayer
At the funeral, the priest offered a solemn requiescat for the departed.
Many poets have written a requiescat as a tribute to loved ones lost.
The inscription on the tombstone simply read 'R.I.P.' for 'requiescat in pace'.

Related Terms

  • synonym: requiem - a Mass for the repose of the souls of the dead
