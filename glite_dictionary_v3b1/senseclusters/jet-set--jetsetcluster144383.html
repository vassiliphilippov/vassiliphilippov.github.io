jet-set

verb
travel frequently in style
7.4
Pronunciation: / dʒɛt sɛt / (AmE), / dʒɛt sɛt / (BrE)
to travel frequently and in style, especially by private jet, as part of a wealthy or fashionable lifestyle.

Examples

To jet-set around
Celebrities began to jet-set around the world after the film's release.
They jet-set between Cannes and New York every summer for fashion events.
After their success, she spent a year jet-setting to exclusive parties and galas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: globetrot - to travel widely and frequently
  • synonym: travel - to make journeys from one place to another, often frequently
