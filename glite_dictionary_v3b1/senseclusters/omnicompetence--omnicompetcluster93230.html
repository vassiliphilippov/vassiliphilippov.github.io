omnicompetence

noun
authority in all matters
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɑːm.nɪ.ˈkɑːm.pə.təns/ (AmE), /ɒm.nɪ.ˈkɒm.pɪ.təns/ (BrE)
the legal or official authority to decide on or act in all matters within a particular jurisdiction or organization

Examples

Legal omnicompetence
The new constitution granted omnicompetence to the national assembly over all legislative matters.
With omnicompetence, the board can make decisions affecting every department in the company.
Some argue that no single body should have omnicompetence due to the risk of unchecked power.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authority - the power or right to give orders and make decisions
  • synonym: jurisdiction - the official power to make legal decisions and judgments
  • antonym: limitation - a restriction or boundary on power or authority
