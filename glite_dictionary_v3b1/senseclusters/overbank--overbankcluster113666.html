overbank

adjective
beyond river banks
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌbæŋk / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌbæŋk / (BrE)
relating to the area beyond a river's bank that is covered or affected by floodwaters, especially during flooding

Examples

Overbank area flooding
The overbank areas were covered with silt after the river flooded last spring.
Scientists studied the overbank deposits to understand the history of flooding in the region.
Farmers worry about crops planted in overbank zones because of frequent water damage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: floodplain - flat land near a river that is often flooded
