bourne

noun
intermittent stream
Pronunciation: / bɔrn / (AmE), / bɔːn / (BrE)
a small stream or brook, especially one that flows only at certain times of the year

Examples

A bourne flows
After heavy rain, the bourne behind the village began to flow again.
Wildflowers grew along the banks of the bourne in spring.
The hikers stopped to rest beside a quiet bourne in the woods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brook - a small stream
  • synonym: creek - a narrow stream, especially in North America
  • degree higher: river - a large natural stream of water
