unearned revenue

noun
income received without direct work
4.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɝnd ˈrɛvəˌnu / (AmE), / ʌnˈɜːnd ˈrɛvənjuː / (BrE)
personal income received without direct work, such as dividends, interest, or rent income

Examples

Unearned revenue from dividends
Dividends from stocks are considered unearned revenue for tax purposes.
Interest on savings accounts is a common form of unearned revenue for individuals.
The accountant carefully recorded the company's unearned revenue in the financial statement.

Related Terms
