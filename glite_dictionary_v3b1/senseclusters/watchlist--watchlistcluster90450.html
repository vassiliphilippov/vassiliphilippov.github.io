watchlist

noun
list for monitoring
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑtʃˌlɪst / (AmE), / ˈwɒtʃˌlɪst / (BrE)
a list of people, organizations, or items that are being closely monitored for a particular reason

Examples

Terrorist watchlist
The government placed several individuals on its watchlist due to security concerns.
She added the new movie to her watchlist so she would remember to see it later.
Banks often keep a watchlist of accounts with suspicious activity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blacklist - a list of people or things regarded as unacceptable or to be avoided
  • synonym: surveillance - close observation of people or groups
