off

adjective
below usual standard
6.2
Pronunciation: / ɑf / (AmE), / ɒf / (BrE)
(of a performance, day, etc.) below the usual or satisfactory standard; not as good as normal.

Examples

Off day
Her singing was noticeably off during the performance.
I'm sorry, I'm a bit off today and can't concentrate.
It was an off night for the team, and they lost easily.
