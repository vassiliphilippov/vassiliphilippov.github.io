predramatic

adjective
before drama
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːdrəˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌpriːdrəˈmætɪk / (BrE)
relating to the period or features existing before the development of drama or theatrical forms

Examples

Predramatic rituals
The predramatic rituals of ancient societies often involved music and dance.
Researchers study predramatic traditions to understand the origins of theater.
Some predramatic performances laid the groundwork for later dramatic storytelling.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dramatic - relating to drama or the performance of plays
