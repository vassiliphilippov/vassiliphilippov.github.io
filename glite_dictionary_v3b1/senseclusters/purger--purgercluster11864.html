purger

noun
remover of impurities
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrdʒər / (AmE), / ˈpɜːdʒə / (BrE)
a person or thing that removes unwanted elements or cleanses something by getting rid of impurities

Examples

A purger of policies
The new manager acted as a purger of outdated policies in the company.
A water filter serves as a purger by removing harmful substances from drinking water.
During the renovation, the team hired a purger to clear out all the old furniture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accumulator - a person or thing that gathers or collects things
  • synonym: cleanser - a person or thing that cleans or purifies
  • synonym: eliminator - someone or something that removes or gets rid of something
