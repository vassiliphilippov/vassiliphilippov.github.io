resend

verb
to send again
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈsɛnd / (AmE), / ˌriːˈsɛnd / (BrE)
to send something again, especially a message, email, or document that was not received or needs to be repeated

Examples

To resend an email
If you did not receive the file, I will resend it to you right away.
She is resending the invitation to everyone who missed it.
Please resend your application if you have not heard back from us.
If you did not receive my email, I can resend it right away.
Please resend the invitation to everyone who missed it the first time.
She had to resend the package because it was returned to her address.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forward - to send a message or document to another person
  • antonym: recall - to take back or withdraw a sent message
  • synonym: send - to cause something to go or be taken to a place
