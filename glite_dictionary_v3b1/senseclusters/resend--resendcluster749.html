resend

verb
to send again
2.0
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈsend/ /; / /riː.ˈsend/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈsend/ /; / /riː.ˈsend/ (BrE)
to send something again, especially a message, email, or document that was not received or needs to be repeated
