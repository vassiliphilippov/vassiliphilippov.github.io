nonsignatory

noun
not a treaty signer
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈsɪɡnəˌtɔri / (AmE), / nɒnˈsɪɡnətəri / (BrE)
a country, organization, or person that has not signed a particular agreement or treaty

Examples

A nonsignatory nation
The nonsignatory nations were not required to follow the new environmental regulations.
As a nonsignatory, the company was not legally bound by the agreement's terms.
Several nonsignatory states expressed interest in joining the treaty in the future.

Related Terms

  • synonym: participant - a person or group involved in an activity or agreement
  • antonym: signatory - a party that has signed an agreement or treaty
