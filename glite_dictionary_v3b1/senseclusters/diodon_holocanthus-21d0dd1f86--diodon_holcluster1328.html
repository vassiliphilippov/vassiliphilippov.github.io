Diodon holocanthus

noun
species of spiny pufferfish
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdaɪ.ə.dɑːn hoʊ.lə.ˈkæn.θəs/ (AmE), /ˈdaɪ.ə.dɒn həʊ.lə.ˈkæn.θəs/ (BrE)
a species of spiny pufferfish, also known as the long-spined porcupinefish, found in tropical and subtropical marine waters, distinguished by its elongated spines and ability to inflate when threatened
