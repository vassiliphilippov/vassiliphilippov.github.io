markswoman

noun
woman skilled at shooting
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrksˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈmɑːksˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who is skilled at shooting, especially with a gun

Examples

A skilled markswoman
As a skilled markswoman, she teaches others how to handle firearms safely.
She trained for years to become a top markswoman in the national team.
The competition was won by an accomplished markswoman from Sweden.
