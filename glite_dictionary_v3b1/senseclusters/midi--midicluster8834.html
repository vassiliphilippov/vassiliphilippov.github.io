midi

adjective
mid-calf length (of clothing)
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdi /; / ˈmiːdi / (AmE), / ˈmɪdi /; / ˈmiːdi / (BrE)
(of a skirt or dress) having a hemline at mid-calf length

Examples

Midi skirt style
She wore a midi skirt to the office, which looked both elegant and professional.
The midi length is popular because it works well for both casual and formal occasions.
This season's collection features several midi dresses in floral patterns.
She chose a flowing midi dress for the afternoon wedding to balance comfort and style.
The boutique displayed several pleated midi skirts perfect for office wear in cooler months.
If you're unsure, try a high-waisted midi to see where it falls on your calves.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: maxi - (of clothing) having a hemline at the ankle or floor-length
  • degree lower: mini - (of clothing) having a very short hemline, above the knee
  • synonym: tea-length - a dress or skirt length that falls below the knee and above the ankle, similar to a midi
