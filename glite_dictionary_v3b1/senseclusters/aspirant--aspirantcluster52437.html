aspirant

noun
ambitious person with goals
Pronunciation: / ˈæspərənt, əˈspaɪrənt / (AmE), / ˈæspɪrənt, əˈspaɪərənt / (BrE)
a person who has ambitions to achieve a particular goal or position, especially someone young or just starting out

Examples

A young aspirant
Among the group of aspirants, only two were selected for the scholarship.
She was an aspirant to the national chess championship, practicing every day after school.
The company is seeking an aspirant who is eager to take on new challenges.

Related Terms

  • synonym: candidate - a person who applies for a job or is nominated for election
  • synonym: contender - a person competing for a chance to win something
  • degree higher: winner - a person who wins something
