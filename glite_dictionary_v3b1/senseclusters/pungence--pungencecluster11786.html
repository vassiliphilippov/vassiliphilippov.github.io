pungence

noun
sharp taste or smell
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌndʒəns / (AmE), / ˈpʌndʒəns / (BrE)
the quality of having a strong, sharp, or biting taste or smell

Examples

Pungence of garlic
The pungence of the garlic made the dish unforgettable.
He wrinkled his nose at the pungence coming from the old refrigerator.
Some people enjoy the pungence of strong blue cheese, while others find it overwhelming.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acidity - the quality of being sour or sharp in taste
  • antonym: blandness - the quality of lacking strong taste or smell
