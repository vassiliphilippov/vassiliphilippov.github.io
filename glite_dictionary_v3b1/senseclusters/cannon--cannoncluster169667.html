cannon

noun
billiards stroke striking two balls
5.7 6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkæn.ən/ (AmE), /ˈkæn.ən/ (BrE)
A shot in billiards or snooker in which the cue ball strikes two other balls successively.

Examples

Cannon shot in snooker
He potted the red with a clever cannon off the black.
It's difficult to judge the angle for a successful cannon in snooker.
The player set up a cannon to keep the break going.
