hotdogging

verb
to show off with stunts
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑtˌdɔɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɒtˌdɒɡɪŋ / (BrE)
to perform daring or showy stunts, especially in sports, to attract attention or impress others

Examples

To hotdog for cheers
The snowboarder was hotdogging down the slope, drawing cheers from the crowd.
Some players get injured when they start hotdogging instead of focusing on the basics.
He was hotdogging on his bike, trying to impress his friends with risky tricks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grandstand - to perform or act in a way meant to impress onlookers
  • synonym: show off - to behave in a way intended to attract attention or admiration
  • antonym: understate - to present something as less important or impressive than it is
