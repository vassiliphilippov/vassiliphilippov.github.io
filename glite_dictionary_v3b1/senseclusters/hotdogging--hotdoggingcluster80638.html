hotdogging

verb
to show off with stunts
9.3
Pronunciation: /ˈhɑːt.dɑː.gɪŋ/ /; / /ˈhɑːt.dɔː.gɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈhɒt.dɒ.gɪŋ/ (BrE)
