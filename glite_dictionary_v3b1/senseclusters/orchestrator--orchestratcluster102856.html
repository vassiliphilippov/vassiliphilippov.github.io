orchestrator

noun
person who arranges music for orchestras
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrkɪˌstreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈɔːkɪstreɪtə / (BrE)
a person who arranges music, especially for orchestras

Examples

Orchestrator of a symphony

The film's music was brought to life by a talented orchestrator who understood every instrument's role.
