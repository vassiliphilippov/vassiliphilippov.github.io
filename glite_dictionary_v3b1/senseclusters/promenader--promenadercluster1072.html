promenader

noun
participant in promenade
Pronunciation: / ˌprɑːməˈneɪdər / (AmE), / ˌprɒməˈneɪdə / (BrE)
a person who takes part in a promenade, especially as part of a formal procession or dance figure

Examples

Folk dance promenader
Each promenader wore a colorful costume during the traditional folk dance.
The promenaders moved gracefully around the ballroom in a grand procession.
As a skilled promenader, she led her group through the intricate dance steps.
