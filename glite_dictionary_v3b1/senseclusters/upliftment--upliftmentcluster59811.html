upliftment

noun
improvement or encouragement
5.9
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈlɪftmənt / (AmE), / ʌpˈlɪftmənt / (BrE)
the process of improving someone's situation, status, or emotional state

Examples

Social upliftment
The organization is dedicated to the upliftment of underprivileged children through education.
Her words of encouragement led to the upliftment of the entire team.
Many community projects focus on the upliftment of local residents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advancement - the process of moving forward or making progress
  • antonym: depression - a state of feeling sad or hopeless
  • synonym: improvement - the act of making something better
