omphacite

noun
green metamorphic mineral
Pronunciation: / ˈɑmfəˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɒmfəˌsaɪt / (BrE)
a green mineral found in some metamorphic rocks, especially eclogite, composed mainly of pyroxene group silicates

Examples

Green omphacite mineral
The geologist identified omphacite in the rock sample collected from the mountain.
Under the microscope, the green crystals were confirmed to be omphacite by their structure.
Eclogite is often recognized by the presence of omphacite and garnet minerals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eclogite - a dense metamorphic rock containing omphacite and garnet
  • synonym: jadeite - a green pyroxene mineral often found in metamorphic rocks
