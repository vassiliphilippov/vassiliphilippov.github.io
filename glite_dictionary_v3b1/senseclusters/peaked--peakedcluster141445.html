peaked

adjective
having a pointed or peaked shape
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːkt / (AmE), / ˈpiːkt / (BrE)
having a shape that rises to a peak or pointed tip

Examples

Peaked roof
A row of peaked tents lined the festival grounds, each with a bright flag on top.
The architect designed a house with a peaked roof to shed snow easily.
The mountain's peaked summit was visible from miles away.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flat - having a level or even surface, without peaks or points
  • synonym: pointed - having a sharp or tapering end or tip
