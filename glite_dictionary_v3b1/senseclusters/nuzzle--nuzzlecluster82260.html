nuzzle

verb
settle closely in a cozy position
3.8 0.12/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆnÊŒzÉ™l / (AmE), / ËˆnÊŒzÉ™l / (BrE)
to settle or arrange oneself in a comfortable and cozy position, often by pressing or nestling closely against something or someone

Examples

To nuzzle under blankets
On cold mornings, the kitten would nuzzle under the covers to stay warm.
She nuzzled against his shoulder as they watched the movie.
The dog likes to nuzzle into the blankets before falling asleep.
