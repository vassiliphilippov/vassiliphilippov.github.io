hotpot

noun
stew of meat and potatoes
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑt.pɑt / (AmE), / ˈhɒt.pɒt / (BrE)
A stew of meat and potatoes cooked slowly in a tightly covered pot.

Examples

A hotpot stew
She prepared a hearty hotpot for the family dinner.
The restaurant is famous for its traditional lamb hotpot.
We enjoyed a delicious hotpot on a cold winter night.
