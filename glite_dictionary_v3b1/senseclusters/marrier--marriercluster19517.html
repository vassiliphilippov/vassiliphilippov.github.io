marrier

noun
wedding officiant
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmæriər / (AmE), / ˈmæriə / (BrE)
a person who officiates at a wedding and is legally or religiously authorized to perform a marriage ceremony

Examples

Wedding marrier
The couple chose their close friend to be the marrier at their outdoor ceremony.
As a licensed marrier, she has officiated dozens of weddings in the past year.
They were grateful to have a compassionate marrier guide them through their vows.

Related Terms

  • synonym: celebrant - a person who performs a formal ceremony, especially a wedding
  • synonym: officiant - a person who leads a religious or civil ceremony, especially a wedding
