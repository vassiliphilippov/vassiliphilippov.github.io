Archer

noun
person born under Sagittarius (astrology)
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrtʃər / (AmE), / ˈɑːtʃə / (BrE)
(Astrology) a person born when the sun is in Sagittarius, traditionally from November 22 to December 21.
