skeletonize

verb
to remove flesh
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈskɛlətəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈskɛlɪtənaɪz / (BrE)
to remove flesh or tissue from something so that only the skeleton remains

Examples

To skeletonize bones
The scientists needed to skeletonize the animal remains for their research project.
After years in the desert, the sun and wind had skeletonized the carcass.
The museum staff carefully skeletonizes specimens before displaying them to the public.
