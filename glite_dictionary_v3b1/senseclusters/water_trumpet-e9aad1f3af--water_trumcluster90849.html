water trumpet

noun
aquatic plant of the genus Cryptocoryne
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtər ˈtrʌmpət / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˈtrʌmpɪt / (BrE)
Any of several evergreen perennial plants of the genus Cryptocoryne, native to tropical Asia, that grow in fresh or brackish water.

Examples

A water trumpet plant
After planting a water trumpet in the pond, she noticed small white flowers blooming underwater.
Many species of water trumpet thrive in the soft, slightly acidic waters of Southeast Asia.
The water trumpet is a popular choice for aquariums because of its attractive foliage.
