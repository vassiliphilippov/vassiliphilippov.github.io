nonmoral

adjective
not involving morality
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈmɔrəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈmɒrəl / (BrE)
not related to, concerned with, or involving questions of morality or ethical judgment

Examples

Nonmoral question
The scientist insisted that their research was nonmoral and did not address ethical questions.
Some laws are purely nonmoral, focusing only on administrative procedures.
Deciding what color to paint a room is a nonmoral choice with no ethical implications.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amoral - not concerned with or lacking a sense of right and wrong
  • antonym: moral - concerned with principles of right and wrong
