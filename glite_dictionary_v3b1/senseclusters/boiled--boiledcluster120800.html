boiled

adjective
cooked in hot water
Pronunciation: / bɔɪld / (AmE), / bɔɪld / (BrE)
(of food) cooked in hot water or another liquid that is brought to a boil.

Examples

Boiled vegetables
After being boiled, the vegetables became soft and easy to eat.
She ordered boiled eggs for breakfast every morning.
The chef served boiled potatoes as a side dish with the steak.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: fried - (of food) cooked in hot fat or oil
  • degree lower: steamed - (of food) cooked by steam
