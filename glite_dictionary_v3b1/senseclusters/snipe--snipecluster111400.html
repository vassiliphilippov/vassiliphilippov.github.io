snipe

noun
mocking remark
Pronunciation: / snaɪp / (AmE), / snaɪp / (BrE)
a clever or mocking remark intended to hurt or annoy someone

Examples

A snipe comment
She ignored the snipe from her coworker and continued with her presentation.
His constant snipes during the meeting made everyone uncomfortable.
After hearing another snipe about his work, he decided to confront his colleague.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compliment - a polite expression of praise or admiration
  • synonym: insult - a disrespectful or scornful remark
  • synonym: taunt - a remark made to provoke or mock someone
