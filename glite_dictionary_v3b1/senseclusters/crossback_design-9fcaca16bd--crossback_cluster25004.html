crossback design

noun
a design with straps crossing at the back
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔsˌbæk dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsbæk dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
a style in which straps or bands cross each other at the back, commonly found in clothing such as dresses, tops, or swimsuits
