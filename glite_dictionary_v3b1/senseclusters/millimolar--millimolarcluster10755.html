millimolar

adjective
one-thousandth molar
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪlɪˈmoʊlər / (AmE), / ˌmɪlɪˈməʊlə / (BrE)
describing a solution with a concentration of one thousandth of a mole per liter

Examples

Millimolar solution
The chemist measured the millimolar concentration of the salt in the solution.
A millimolar solution is often used in laboratory experiments for precise reactions.
They diluted the acid to a millimolar level before starting the test.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: micromolar - describing a solution with one millionth of a mole per liter
  • degree higher: molar - describing a solution with one mole of solute per liter
