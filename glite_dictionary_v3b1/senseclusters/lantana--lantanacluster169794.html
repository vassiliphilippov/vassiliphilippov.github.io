lantana

noun
genus of flowering shrubs with colorful clusters
Pronunciation: / lænˈtænə / (AmE), / lænˈtɑːnə / (BrE)
A genus of about 150 species of flowering shrubs, commonly called shrub verbenas, known for their colorful flower clusters.

Examples

A lantana shrub
She planted a row of lantana along the fence to add color throughout the summer.
The vibrant blooms of the lantana attract butterflies and bees to the garden.
While lantana is popular for landscaping, in some areas it can become invasive.
