ethicist

noun
a specialist in ethics
Pronunciation: / ˈɛθɪsɪst / (AmE), / ˈɛθɪsɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies or is an expert in ethics, especially a philosopher specializing in moral principles and values

Examples

An ethicist studies morals
As an ethicist, she contributed to the debate about medical decision-making.
Not every philosopher is an ethicist, but all ethicists engage with questions of right and wrong.
The company consulted an ethicist before developing its new privacy policy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moralist - person concerned with morality or moral conduct
  • synonym: philosopher - a person engaged or learned in philosophy
