Stegosaurus

noun

A genus of large, slow-moving, herbivorous dinosaurs of the late Jurassic period, characterized by distinctive upright plates along the back and spikes on the tail.

Examples

A Stegosaurus dinosaur
A Stegosaurus could grow up to nine meters in length and had distinctive plates along its back.
Scientists believe the tail spikes of the Stegosaurus were used for defense against predators.
The fossilized remains of a Stegosaurus were discovered in Colorado.
