clairvoyant

adjective
able to perceive beyond normal senses
7.0
Pronunciation: / klɛrˈvɔɪənt / (AmE), / klɛəˈvɔɪənt /; / kleəˈvɔɪənt / (BrE)
having or claiming to have the ability to perceive events or information beyond normal senses, especially about the future

Examples

Clairvoyant powers
Despite his clairvoyant instincts, no one believed his warnings until it was too late.
She claimed to be clairvoyant and could sense things before they happened.
Some believe that animals are clairvoyant and can detect natural disasters in advance.
He dismissed the idea that anyone could truly be clairvoyant about the future.
Her clairvoyant abilities astonished all who visited her for readings.
The fortune-teller claimed to be clairvoyant and predicted great changes in my life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: psychic - having abilities to perceive information hidden from the normal senses
  • antonym: rational - based on or in accordance with reason or logic; not supernatural
