reclaimer

noun
land restoration agent
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkleɪmər / (AmE), / rɪˈkleɪmə / (BrE)
a person, company, or organization that makes land or water usable again by draining, filling, or other engineering methods

Land reclaimer
