launder money

verb
conceal origins of illegal money
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔndɚ ˈmʌni / (AmE), / ˈlɔːndə ˈmʌni / (BrE)
to process illegally obtained money so that it appears to come from legitimate sources

Examples

To launder money illegally
He was convicted of attempting to launder money from the drug trade.
Officials discovered a complex scheme used to launder money across international borders.
The criminal organization would launder money through a series of shell companies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: money laundering - the criminal process of making illegally gained money appear legitimate
