inextensible

adjective
not able to be stretched
Pronunciation: / ɪnɪkˈstɛnsɪbəl / (AmE), / ɪnɛkˈstɛnsɪbəl / (BrE)
Not able to be stretched or extended; incapable of being lengthened.

Examples

An inextensible cable
Because the cable is inextensible, it maintains a fixed length under tension.
Engineers require inextensible fibers for certain types of structural reinforcements.
The rope is made from an inextensible material, making it suitable for climbing.
