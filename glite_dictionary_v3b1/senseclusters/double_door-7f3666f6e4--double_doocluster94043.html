double door

noun
pair of side-by-side doors
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌbəl dɔr / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəl dɔː / (BrE)
A pair of doors that are hinged side by side and meet in the middle when closed, usually forming a wider entrance.

Examples

A pair of double doors
Please make sure both double doors are locked before leaving the building tonight.
Sunlight streamed through the glass double doors leading onto the patio.
The grand entrance to the hall was marked by a set of double doors made from solid oak.
