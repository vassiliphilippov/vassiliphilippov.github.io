time exposure

noun
long exposure in photography
Pronunciation: / taɪm ɪkˈspoʊʒɚ /; / taɪm ɪkˈspoʊʒər / (AmE), / taɪm ɪkˈspəʊʒə / (BrE)
a photograph taken with a long exposure time, or the act of exposing film or a sensor for a long period to capture more light or motion

Examples

A time exposure photo
A time exposure can help reveal star trails in the night sky.

A time exposure can reveal the movement of cars as glowing lines on the road.
He used a time exposure to capture the star trails across the night sky.
The photographer created a beautiful time exposure of the waterfall, making the water look silky and smooth.
