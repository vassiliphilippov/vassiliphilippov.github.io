void

adjective
completely empty, containing nothing
5.3
Pronunciation: / vɔɪd / (AmE), / vɔɪd / (BrE)
completely empty; containing nothing; not filled with anything.

Examples

Void room
After the fire, the house stood void and silent, stripped of all life and possessions.
His words echoed through the void hall, emphasizing the emptiness within.
The laboratory was left void of any equipment or furniture after the move.
