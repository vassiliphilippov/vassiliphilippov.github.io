telepathist

noun
person claiming telepathic ability
Pronunciation: / təˈlɛpəθɪst / (AmE), / tɛˈlɛpəθɪst / (BrE)
a person who claims to have the ability to communicate thoughts directly from one mind to another without using the usual senses

Examples

Famous telepathist
During the experiment, the self-proclaimed telepathist attempted to transmit his thoughts to the volunteer.
Some scientists doubt the existence of true telepathists and demand more evidence.
The main character in the novel is a gifted telepathist who can sense danger before anyone else.
