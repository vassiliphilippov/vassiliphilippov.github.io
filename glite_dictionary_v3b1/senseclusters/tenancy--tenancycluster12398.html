tenancy

noun
the state of being a tenant
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛnənsi / (AmE), / ˈtɛnənsi / (BrE)
The possession or occupancy of land, property, or accommodation by a tenant for a specified period under a lease or rental agreement.

Examples

A tenancy agreement
During their tenancy, the tenants are responsible for keeping the property in good condition.
He signed a one-year tenancy agreement for the apartment.
The tenancy will expire at the end of next month unless renewed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lease - a contract granting use or occupation of property during a specified period in exchange for rent
  • synonym: occupancy - the act of living in or using a building or land as a tenant
  • antonym: ownership - the act, state, or right of possessing property
