contently

adverb
with quiet happiness
Pronunciation: / kənˈtɛntli / (AmE), / kənˈtɛntli / (BrE)
in a way that shows quiet happiness or satisfaction

Examples

To feel contently happy
The dog lay contently at his owner's feet after a long walk.
She smiled contently as she watched the sunset from her balcony.
After finishing his meal, he sighed contently and leaned back in his chair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: happily - in a happy or joyful way
  • synonym: peacefully - in a calm and quiet way
  • antonym: restlessly - in a way that shows inability to relax or be still
