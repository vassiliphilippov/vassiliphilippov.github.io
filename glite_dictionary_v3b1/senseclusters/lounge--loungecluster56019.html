lounge

noun
waiting or relaxation room in public places
2.3 12/m
Pronunciation: / laÊŠndÊ’ / (AmE), / laÊŠndÊ’ / (BrE)
a room in a public place, such as a hotel or airport, with comfortable seating where people can wait or relax

Examples

Airport lounge
He relaxed in the comfortable lounge while waiting for his meeting.
The hotel lounge offered free drinks and snacks for guests.
We waited in the airport lounge until our flight was called.

Synonyms

    1.8 1.04/m
    a waiting area is a marked space with seats often open to many people, while a lounge feels more private and comfortable.
