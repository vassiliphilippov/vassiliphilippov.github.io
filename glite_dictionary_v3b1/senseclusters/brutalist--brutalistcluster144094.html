brutalist

noun
supporter of Brutalism
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːtəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈbruːtəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who designs, builds, or supports buildings in the Brutalist architectural style, characterized by raw concrete and bold forms

Examples

A famous brutalist
The brutalist presented a design featuring exposed concrete and geometric shapes.
As a proud brutalist, she defended the value of functional, unadorned architecture.
Many critics disagreed with the brutalist approach to the new city hall project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: modernist - a person who supports modern architecture or design
  • antonym: traditionalist - a person who prefers traditional styles or methods
