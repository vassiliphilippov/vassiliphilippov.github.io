understandingly

adverb
in a sympathetic or perceptive way
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈstændɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈstændɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows sympathetic or perceptive comprehension; with understanding.

Examples

To smile understandingly
He smiled understandingly when she explained her mistake.
She listened understandingly as her friend shared her troubles.
The teacher nodded understandingly, realizing the student was nervous.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sympathetically - in a way that shows sympathy or understanding
