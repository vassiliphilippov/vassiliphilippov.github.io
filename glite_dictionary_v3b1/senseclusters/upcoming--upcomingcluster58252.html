upcoming

adjective
happening soon; forthcoming
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌkʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌkʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
Happening or appearing soon; about to take place in the near future.

Examples

The upcoming event
She is preparing for the upcoming conference next week.
The school announced its upcoming events for the semester.
We need to plan for the upcoming changes in the company.

Related Terms

  • synonym: approaching - coming closer in time or space
  • synonym: forthcoming - about to happen or appear
  • antonym: past - having already happened or occurred
