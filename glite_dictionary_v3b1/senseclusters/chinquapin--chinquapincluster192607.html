chinquapin

noun
small edible nut from southern U.S. chestnut trees
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪŋkəˌpɪn / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪŋkəˌpɪn /; / ˈtʃɪŋkəpɪn / (BrE)
