flat

noun
British term for apartment
4.4
Pronunciation: / flæt / (AmE), / flæt / (BrE)
A set of rooms forming an individual residence, typically on one floor and within a larger building; an apartment.

Examples

A city flat
She moved into a new flat near the city center last week.
The flat was spacious and had a beautiful balcony.
They rented a flat overlooking the river in London.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apartment - a suite of rooms forming a separate residence within a building
