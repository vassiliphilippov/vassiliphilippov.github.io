trephine

noun
surgical instrument for removing bone
9.3
Pronunciation: / trəˈfaɪn / (AmE), / trɪˈfiːn / (BrE)
a surgical instrument with a cylindrical blade used to remove circular sections of bone, especially from the skull.

Examples

Surgical trephine tool
A trephine is often employed in cranial surgeries to create access for further treatment.
After sterilizing the trephine, the neurosurgeon began the delicate procedure.
The surgeon used a trephine to cut a precise opening in the patient's skull.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trepan - a similar surgical instrument for cutting out circular pieces of bone
