congratulatory letter

noun
official letter expressing congratulations
1.0
Pronunciation: / kənˌɡrætʃəˌleɪtɔri ˈlɛtər / (AmE), / kənˌɡratjʊˈleɪtəri ˈlɛtə / (BrE)
an official letter written to acknowledge and celebrate someone's achievements or success

Examples

After winning the award, he was honored with a congratulatory letter from the mayor.

The committee sent a congratulatory letter to the team for their outstanding achievement.
