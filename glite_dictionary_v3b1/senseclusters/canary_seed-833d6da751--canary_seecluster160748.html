canary seed

noun
mixture of seeds for caged birds
Pronunciation: / kəˈnɛri sid / (AmE), / kəˈneəri siːd / (BrE)
a mixture of seeds, traditionally including canary grass seed, used as food for caged birds such as canaries and finches

Examples

Fresh canary seed
A proper diet for caged canaries should always include fresh canary seed.
Some birds prefer sunflower seeds over canary seed in their feeders.
We bought a bag of canary seed at the pet shop for our finches.
