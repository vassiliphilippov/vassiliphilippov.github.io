harvester

noun
software that gathers online data
6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈhɑːr.və.stər/ /; / /ˈhɑːr.vɪ.stər/ (AmE), /ˈhɑː.və.stə/ /; / /ˈhɑː.vɪs.tə/ (BrE)
(computing) a software program or automated agent that automatically collects large amounts of data from online sources for analysis or storage.

Examples

A data harvester
The marketing team used a harvester to collect public social media posts for sentiment analysis.
After the breach, investigators found a hidden harvester that had scraped customer emails overnight.
Researchers trained a harvester to pull price data from thousands of online stores daily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scraper - software that extracts specific data from websites
  • synonym: web crawler - a program that automatically browses the web to index or collect pages
