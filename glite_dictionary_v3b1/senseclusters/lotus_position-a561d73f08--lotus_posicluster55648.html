lotus position

noun
yoga sitting posture with crossed legs
2.1 5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊ.təs pəˈzɪʃ.ən /; / ˈloʊ.t̬əs pəˈzɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.təs pəˈzɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
a sitting posture with legs crossed and each foot placed on the opposite thigh, commonly used in yoga and meditation
