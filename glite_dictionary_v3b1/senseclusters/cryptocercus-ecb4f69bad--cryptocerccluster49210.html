Cryptocercus

noun
genus of wood-dwelling cockroaches
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɪptoʊˈsɜrkəs / (AmE), / ˌkrɪptəʊˈsɜːkəs / (BrE)
a genus of cockroaches that live in and eat wood in temperate forests, closely related to termites
