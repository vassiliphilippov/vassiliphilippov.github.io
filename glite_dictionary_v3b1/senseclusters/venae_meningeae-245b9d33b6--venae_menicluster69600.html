venae meningeae

noun
meningeal veins of the brain and spinal cord
Pronunciation: / ˈviː.ni mɛˌnɪn.dʒiˈi / (AmE), / ˈviː.ni mɛˌnɪn.dʒɪˈiː / (BrE)
the veins that accompany the meningeal arteries in the membranes covering the brain and spinal cord
