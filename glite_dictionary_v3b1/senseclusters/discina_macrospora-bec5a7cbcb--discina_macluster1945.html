Discina macrospora

noun
A species of disk-shaped brown fungus
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsaɪnə ˌmækroʊˈspɔrə / (AmE), / dɪˈsiːnə ˌmakrəʊˈspɔːrə / (BrE)
A species of fungus with a flat or saucer-shaped fertile body, brown on the upper surface, and a short stalk; not recommended for eating.
