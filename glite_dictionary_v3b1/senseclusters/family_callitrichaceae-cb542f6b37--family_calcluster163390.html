family Callitrichaceae

noun
family of aquatic dicot herbs (water-starworts)
Pronunciation: / ˌfæməli kəˌlɪtrɪˈkeɪsiˌiː / (AmE), / ˌfæmɪli kəˌlɪtrɪˈkeɪsiːˌiː / (BrE)
