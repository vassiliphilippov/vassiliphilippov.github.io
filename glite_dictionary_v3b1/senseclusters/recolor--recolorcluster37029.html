Synonyms

    dye is to change color using a staining liquid on fabric or hair, while recolor can mean any method including paint or editing.
  • repaint
    1.3 1.40/m
    repaint means to paint something again to refresh or change its look, while recolor covers any way of changing color, even digital edits.
  • overdye
    7.4 0.00/m
