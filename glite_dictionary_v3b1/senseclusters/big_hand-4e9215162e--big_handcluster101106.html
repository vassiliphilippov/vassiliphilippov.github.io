big hand

noun
minute hand on a clock
Pronunciation: / bɪɡ hænd / (AmE), / bɪɡ hand / (BrE)
the longer hand on a clock or watch that points to the minutes

Examples

Clock big hand
Children often learn to tell time by first recognizing the big hand and the small hand.
Please move the big hand to the twelve when resetting the clock.
The big hand is pointing at the six, so it's half past the hour.

Related Terms

  • synonym: minute hand - the hand on a clock that indicates the minutes
  • antonym: small hand - the shorter hand on a clock that shows the hours
