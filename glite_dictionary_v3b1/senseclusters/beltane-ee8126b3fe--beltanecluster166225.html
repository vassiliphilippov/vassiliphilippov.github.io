Beltane

noun
Celtic spring festival
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbel.teɪn/ /; / /ˈbel.tən/ (AmE), /ˈbel.teɪn/ /; / /ˈbel.tən/ (BrE)
an ancient Celtic festival celebrated on May 1st to mark the beginning of summer, often with bonfires and rituals

Examples

The Beltane festival
Every year, the village celebrates Beltane with music, dancing, and a large bonfire.
Historians study the rituals performed during Beltane to understand ancient Celtic beliefs.
On the first of May, families gather to mark Beltane and welcome the coming of summer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Samhain - a Celtic festival marking the end of the harvest season
