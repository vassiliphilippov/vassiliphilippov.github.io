scribble

noun
messy or illegible handwriting
Pronunciation: / ˈskrɪb.əl / (AmE), / ˈskrɪb.əl / (BrE)
a piece of writing that is messy or difficult to read, especially due to poor handwriting

Examples

Messy scribble
Her hurried scribble made the shopping list hard to decipher.
I couldn't read his scribble on the note he left me.
The teacher struggled to make sense of the scribble on the homework assignment.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: handwriting - writing by hand, style of writing
  • synonym: scrawl - carelessly written or drawn writing
