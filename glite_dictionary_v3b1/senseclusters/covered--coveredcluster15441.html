covered

adjective
enclosed or topped with something
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌvɚd / (AmE), / ˈkʌvəd / (BrE)
having a layer or object over or around something; enclosed, topped, or protected by something

Examples

Covered in dust
During the dust storm, the cars were quickly covered in sand.
She prefers buying covered parking spaces in the city.
The picnic was held under a covered pavilion to protect against rain.
