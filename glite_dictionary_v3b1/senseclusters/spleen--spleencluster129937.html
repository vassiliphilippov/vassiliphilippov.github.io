spleen

noun
resentful anger; spite
8.1 6.9
Pronunciation: /spliːn/ (AmE), /spliːn/ (BrE)
a feeling of bitterness or resentful anger, especially long-lasting or ill-tempered; spite

Examples

Bitter spleen
He was full of spleen after hearing the unfair criticism.
She vented her spleen on her unsuspecting colleagues during the meeting.
The writer's spleen is evident in his harsh review of the play.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anger - a strong feeling of displeasure or hostility
  • synonym: malice - the intention or desire to do evil; ill will
