poison milkweed

noun
poisonous milkweed plant
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔɪzən ˈmɪlkwid / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪzən ˈmɪlkwiːd / (BrE)
a type of milkweed native to the southwestern United States and Mexico that is poisonous to livestock

Examples

Poison milkweed plant
Cattle should be kept away from poison milkweed because it can be fatal if ingested.
The rancher identified several patches of poison milkweed growing near the grazing fields.
Veterinarians warn that poison milkweed poses a serious risk to livestock in the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: milkweed - any of the several plants of the genus Asclepias
  • degree lower: poisonous plant - a plant that contains substances harmful or lethal to animals or humans
