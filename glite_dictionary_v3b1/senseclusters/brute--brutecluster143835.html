brute

adjective
lacking human sensibility; beast-like
3.6
Pronunciation: / bruːt / (AmE), / bruːt / (BrE)
Resembling a beast; lacking human sensibility, compassion, or reasoning.

Examples

Brute force
His brute behavior shocked everyone at the dinner party.
Survival in the wild often comes down to brute strength rather than intelligence.
The soldiers used brute force to break down the door.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beastly - like or characteristic of a beast
  • synonym: bestial - like a beast; lacking reason or intelligence
  • antonym: humane - showing compassion or benevolence
