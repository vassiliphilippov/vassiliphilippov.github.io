mountainy

adjective
full of mountains
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntəni / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntəni / (BrE)
having many mountains or resembling a mountainous region

Examples

A mountainy landscape
The mountainy landscape was covered in mist early in the morning.
We drove through a mountainy region with winding roads and steep cliffs.
Her hometown is known for its mountainy scenery and fresh air.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flat - having little or no elevation or hills
  • synonym: mountainous - having many mountains
