warlock

noun
male witch or sorcerer
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔr.lɑk / (AmE), / ˈwɔː.lɒk / (BrE)
a man who practices witchcraft or magic, especially one who is believed to have made a pact with evil spirits or the devil

Examples

A warlock practices magic
In the story, a powerful warlock is defeated by a young apprentice.
Legends say the ancient warlock could summon storms with a wave of his hand.
The villagers feared the mysterious warlock who lived at the edge of the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: witch - a person, especially a woman, who is believed to practice magic
