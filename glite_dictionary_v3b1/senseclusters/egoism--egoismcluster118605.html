egoism

noun
self-centered concern for one's own interests
7.2 0.35/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàiÀê…°o äÀå…™z…ôm / (AmE), / ÀàiÀê…°…ô äÀå…™z…ôm / (BrE)
the tendency to be primarily concerned with one's own interests and welfare, sometimes to the exclusion of others

Examples

Personal egoism
Her egoism made it difficult for her to form genuine friendships.
Philosophers have long debated whether human behavior is driven by egoism or altruism.
The manager's egoism was evident in his inability to consider his team's needs.

Synonyms

  • selfishness
    3.0 3.85/m
    selfishness is acting to gain personal advantage with little concern for others, while egoism is a general concern for one's own interests.
  • self-centeredness
    5.8 0.25/m
    self-centeredness focuses on one's own needs and feelings in daily life, while egoism focuses on pursuing one's interests.
  • selfism
    9.5 0.00/m
    selfism describes a belief in putting oneself above others, while egoism describes a broader habit of favoring one's own interests.
  • self-interest
    4.0 5.54/m
    self-interest means choosing what benefits you most, often without caring for others, while egoism is a general self-focused tendency.
  • egocentrism
    8.8 0.26/m
    egocentrism sees the world only from oneself and ignores others' viewpoints, while egoism focuses on protecting one's interests.
  • egocentricity
    8.2 0.14/m
    egocentricity shows a strong inward focus on personal views and needs, while egoism centers on self-interest and personal gain.
  • self-absorption
    8.1 0.54/m
    self-absorption is deep preoccupation with one's thoughts and feelings, while egoism is an outward focus on self-benefit and interests.
