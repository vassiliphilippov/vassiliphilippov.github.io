mallet

noun
a stick used to play percussion instruments
4.6 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈmæl.ɪt/ /; / /ˈmæ.leɪ/ (AmE), /ˈmæl.ɪt/ /; / /ˈmæ.leɪ/ (BrE)
a light stick with a rounded head, used to strike percussion instruments such as chimes, marimbas, and glockenspiels
