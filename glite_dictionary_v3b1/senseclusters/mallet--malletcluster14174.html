mallet

noun
a stick used to play percussion instruments
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmælɪt / (AmE), / ˈmælɪt / (BrE)
a light stick with a rounded head, used to strike percussion instruments such as chimes, marimbas, and glockenspiels

Examples

Percussion mallet
He lost one mallet during the performance and had to finish with just the other.
The percussionist carefully selected a mallet to play the delicate notes on the glockenspiel.
You must use a soft mallet for the marimba to produce a warm tone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drumstick - a stick used to strike drums and other percussion instruments
