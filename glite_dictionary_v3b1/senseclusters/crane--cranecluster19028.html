crane

verb
to lift with a crane
3.7
Pronunciation: /kreɪn/ (AmE), /kreɪn/ (BrE)
to move or lift something using a crane, a large machine with a long arm for lifting heavy objects

Examples

To crane heavy boxes
The workers spent the morning craning steel beams onto the top floor of the building.
Last night, engineers were craning the new bridge sections into place over the river.
They carefully craned the heavy machinery onto the ship for transport.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hoist - to lift or raise something, especially using ropes or machinery
  • antonym: lower - to move something to a lower position
