unfashionable

adjective
not fashionable or trendy
1.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfæʃənəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈfæʃ(ə)nəbl /; / ʌnˈfæʃənəbl / (BrE)
not in style or not following current fashion trends

Examples

Unfashionable clothes
He felt self-conscious wearing his unfashionable jacket to the party.
It suddenly became unfashionable to use that kind of language in public.
Vintage shops often carry unfashionable garments from previous decades.
His clothes looked unfashionable compared to his classmates' trendy outfits.
She refuses to wear anything unfashionable, always keeping up with current trends.
The restaurant's décor is now considered unfashionable by modern standards.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fashionable - conforming to the current style or trends
  • antonym: stylish - fashionable and elegant
