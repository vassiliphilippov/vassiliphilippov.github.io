demurrer

noun
legal objection to opponent's pleadings
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmɜrər /; / dɪˈmɜːrər / (AmE), / dɪˈmɜːrə /; / dɪˈmʌrə / (BrE)
a formal objection in a court case, arguing that the opponent's legal claim is not sufficient, without disputing the facts
