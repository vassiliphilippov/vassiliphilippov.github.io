whichever

determiner
any item chosen
Pronunciation: / wɪˈtʃɛvər / (AmE), / wɪˈtʃɛvə / (BrE)
used to refer to any one or more items from a group, without specifying which.

Examples

Whichever book you want
You may take whichever book you like from the shelf.
Choose whichever color suits you best for the project.
The winner can select whichever prize they prefer from the table.

Related Terms

  • synonym: any - one or some of a thing or number of things, no matter how much or how many
  • synonym: whatever - any thing or things, no matter what
