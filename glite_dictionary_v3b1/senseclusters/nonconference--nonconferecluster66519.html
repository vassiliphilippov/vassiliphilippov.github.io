nonconference

noun
game between conferences
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈkɑnfərəns / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈkɒnfərəns / (BrE)
a sporting event or game played between teams that belong to different conferences, especially in college sports

Examples

Nonconference game
The basketball team scheduled a tough nonconference to prepare for the regular season.
Fans were excited to see their school face a rival in a rare nonconference matchup.
Winning a nonconference can boost a team's national ranking early in the season.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conference game - a game played between teams in the same conference
