qualmish

adjective
easily disgusted
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑːmɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkwɒmɪʃ / (BrE)
feeling sick, uneasy, or easily disgusted, especially in the stomach or by unpleasant sights
