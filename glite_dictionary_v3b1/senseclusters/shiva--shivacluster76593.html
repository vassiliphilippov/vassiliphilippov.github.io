shiva

noun
Jewish seven-day mourning period
Pronunciation: / ˈʃiːvə / (AmE), / ˈʃɪvə / (BrE)
In Judaism, a period of seven days of mourning observed after the death of a close relative.

Examples

To sit shiva
After her grandmother passed away, the family gathered to sit shiva together.
Several friends visited the house during shiva to offer condolences and support.
They lit a memorial candle on the first day of shiva as part of the tradition.
