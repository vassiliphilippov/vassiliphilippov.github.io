avoidant

noun
person who avoids intimacy
Pronunciation: / əˈvɔɪdənt / (AmE), / əˈvɔɪdənt / (BrE)
a person who avoids close emotional relationships or social contact, often due to fear of rejection or discomfort with intimacy

Examples

An avoidant in relationships
As an avoidant, he found it difficult to open up to new friends.
Therapists often help an avoidant address their fear of emotional closeness.
The avoidant in the group preferred to keep conversations light and impersonal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: introvert - a person who prefers solitary activities and finds social interaction draining
  • antonym: sociable - a person who enjoys being with others and seeks social interaction
