Crown

noun
the monarch or monarchy as a symbol of authority
6.5 6.98/m
Pronunciation: / kraÊŠn / (AmE), / kraÊŠn / (BrE)
(in a monarchy) the reigning monarch or the state as represented by the monarch, especially as a symbol of authority and power.

Examples

The Crown authority
Legal proceedings were initiated by the Crown against the company.
The land technically belongs to the Crown and is leased to farmers.
The rebels declared independence from the Crown in the eighteenth century.

Synonyms
