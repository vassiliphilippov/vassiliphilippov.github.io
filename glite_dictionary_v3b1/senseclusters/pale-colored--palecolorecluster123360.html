pale-colored

adjective
having a pale color
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪl ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈpeɪl ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having a pale or light color; not strongly colored or vivid

Examples

Pale-colored feathers
Many birds have pale-colored feathers that help them blend into dry grasslands.
She chose a pale-colored dress for the summer party.
The walls in the living room are painted a pale-colored shade to make the space feel bigger.
