affectionate term

noun
word showing fondness or love
Pronunciation: / əˈfɛk.ʃə.nət tɜrm / (AmE), / əˈfɛk.ʃ(ə)nət tɜːm / (BrE)
a word or expression used to show fondness, love, or tenderness toward someone

Examples

An affectionate term for friends
'Buddy' is an affectionate term some people use for close friends.
He always calls his daughter by an affectionate term like 'sweetheart' or 'darling'.
In some cultures, using an affectionate term for elders is a sign of respect.
