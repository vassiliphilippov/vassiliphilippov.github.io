quicksand

noun
a mass of loose, wet sand that sucks objects down
5.0 4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪkˌsænd / (AmE), / ˈkwɪk.sænd / (BrE)
a deep mass of loose, wet sand into which heavy objects can sink and become trapped

Examples

Many explorers feared getting trapped in quicksand during their jungle adventures.
