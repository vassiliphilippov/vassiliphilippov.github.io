suppressor

noun
device that reduces unwanted electrical currents
5.1 5.7
Pronunciation: / səˈprɛsɚ / (AmE), / səˈprɛsə / (BrE)
an electrical device used to prevent or reduce unwanted electrical currents, especially noise or interference

Examples

Power suppressor device
A power surge damaged the suppressor in the amplifier circuit.
The technician installed a suppressor to prevent interference from other electronic devices.
Without a suppressor, you might hear a buzzing noise from the speakers.
