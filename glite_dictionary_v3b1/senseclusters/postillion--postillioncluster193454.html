postillion

noun
person riding lead horse to guide carriage
5.4 9.9
Pronunciation: /pɑː.ˈstɪl.jən/ /; / /pə.ˈstɪl.jən/ (AmE), /pɒ.ˈstɪl.jən/ /; / /pə.ˈstɪl.jən/ (BrE)
A person who rides the leading left-hand horse of a pair or team drawing a carriage, especially when there is no coachman.

Examples

Without a coachman, the postilion expertly guided the horses through the busy streets.
