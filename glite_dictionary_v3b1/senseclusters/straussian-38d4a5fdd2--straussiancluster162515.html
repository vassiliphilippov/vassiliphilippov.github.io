Straussian

noun
follower of Leo Strauss
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstraʊsiən / (AmE), / ˈstraʊsiən / (BrE)
a person who follows or is influenced by the ideas or philosophy of Leo Strauss

Examples

Many Straussians debate the interpretation of ancient texts in modern politics.
She became a Straussian after studying the works of Leo Strauss in graduate school.
