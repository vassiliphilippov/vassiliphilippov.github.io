supply officer

noun
military officer handling logistics and supplies
3.5
Pronunciation: / səˈplaɪ ˌɑfəsər / (AmE), / səˈplaɪ ˌɒfɪsə / (BrE)
a commissioned officer in the military responsible for matters relating to logistics, such as the supply of equipment, provisions, and materials

Examples

The supply officer manages equipment
As the supply officer, she was in charge of distributing food rations to the battalion.
During the operation, the supply officer coordinated shipments of ammunition and fuel.
The supply officer ensured that all troops received their equipment before deploying.

Related Terms

  • synonym: logistics officer - a military officer responsible for planning and coordinating logistics
