finishing coat

noun
final layer applied to a surface
5.5 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪn.ɪ.ʃɪŋ koʊt/ (AmE), /ˈfɪn.ɪ.ʃɪŋ kəʊt/ (BrE)
