pill

noun
object shaped like a tablet
2.2
Pronunciation: / pɪl / (AmE), / pɪl / (BrE)
something that is shaped like or similar in size to a small tablet of medicine

Examples

Pill-shaped object
A row of tiny robots lined up, each shaped like a pill.
He carved a wooden pill as a miniature art project for school.
The candies were so round and small, each looked exactly like a pill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capsule - a small soluble container with medicine
  • synonym: tablet - a small, solid dose of medicine for swallowing
