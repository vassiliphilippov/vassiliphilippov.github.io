liturgically

adverb
in a worship-related way
7.9
Pronunciation: / lɪˈtɝːdʒɪkli / (AmE), / lɪˈtɜːdʒɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to public worship or religious ceremonies

Examples

Prayers said liturgically
The prayers were recited liturgically according to ancient tradition.
The festival was celebrated liturgically in churches across the country.
The congregation responded liturgically during the special Easter service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ceremonially - in a way that relates to formal ceremonies
  • antonym: secularly - in a way that is not connected to religion
