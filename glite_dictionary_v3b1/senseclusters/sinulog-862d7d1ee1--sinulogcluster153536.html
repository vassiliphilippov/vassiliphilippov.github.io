Sinulog

proper noun
Philippine festival
Pronunciation: / siˈnuːlɔg / (AmE), / siˈnuːlɒg / (BrE)
a major annual cultural and religious festival held in Cebu City, Philippines, featuring street dancing and religious processions

Examples

Sinulog festival
Every January, thousands of people gather in Cebu City to celebrate the Sinulog festival.
Colorful costumes and lively dances are highlights of the Sinulog parade.
Many tourists plan their trips to the Philippines to experience the vibrant Sinulog celebration.
