perspectivist

adjective
based on perspective
9.9
Pronunciation: /pər.ˈspek.tɪ.vɪst/ (AmE), /pə.ˈspek.tɪ.vɪst/ (BrE)
relating to the view that knowledge or perception is shaped by individual perspective

Examples

Perspectivist theory
Her perspectivist approach to art history highlights how interpretation changes with the viewer.
The debate took a perspectivist turn, focusing on how each person's background shapes their beliefs.
A perspectivist reading of the novel reveals multiple possible meanings depending on the reader.

Related Terms

  • synonym: subjective - influenced by personal feelings or opinions
