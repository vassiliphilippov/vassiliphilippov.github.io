pubococcygeus exercises

noun
pelvic muscle strengthening exercises
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpjuːboʊˌkɑksɪˈdʒiːəs ˈɛksɚˌsaɪzɪz / (AmE), / ˌpjʊbəʊˌkɒksɪˈdʒiːəs ˈɛksəsaɪzɪz / (BrE)
Exercises aimed at strengthening the pubococcygeus muscles, usually to improve bladder control and pelvic floor health.

Examples

Pubococcygeus exercises for women
Doctors often recommend pubococcygeus exercises to help women recover bladder control after childbirth.
Regular practice of pubococcygeus exercises can strengthen the pelvic floor muscles and prevent urinary leakage.
Some physical therapists teach pubococcygeus exercises to patients as part of a program to improve pelvic health.
