lagniappe

noun
small bonus gift with a purchase
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlæniˌæp / (AmE), / ˈlænɪap / (BrE)
a small gift, especially one given by a merchant to a customer with a purchase

Examples

A lagniappe gift
Every customer receives a small lagniappe with their meal at the restaurant.
It's a tradition in New Orleans for merchants to offer a lagniappe to loyal customers.
The shopkeeper included a handful of candies as a lagniappe with my purchase.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bonus - something extra given on top of what is due
  • synonym: gratuity - a gift or tip given to someone for a service or favor
