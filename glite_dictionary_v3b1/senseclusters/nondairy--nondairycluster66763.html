nondairy

adjective
without milk
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈdɛri / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈdeəri / (BrE)
not containing milk or any ingredients made from milk

Examples

Nondairy milk
She prefers nondairy ice cream because she is lactose intolerant.
There are many nondairy alternatives to regular milk in the supermarket.
The recipe calls for nondairy butter to make it suitable for vegans.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dairy - containing or made from milk
  • synonym: dairy-free - not containing any milk or milk products
