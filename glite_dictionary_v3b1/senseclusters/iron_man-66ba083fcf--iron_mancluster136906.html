iron man

noun
man of exceptional physical endurance
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪərn mæn / (AmE), / ˈaɪən mæn / (BrE)
a man who possesses extraordinary physical strength or endurance.

Examples

The iron man athlete
Despite his age, the veteran firefighter was known as the iron man of the department.
He proved himself an iron man by completing the grueling 100-mile race.
It takes an iron man to compete in such extreme conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: strongman - a man of great physical strength, especially one who performs feats of strength as entertainment
