biflagellate

adjective
having two flagella
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪfləˈdʒɛlət / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪfləˈdʒɛlət / (BrE)
(of a cell or organism) having two flagella.

Examples

Biflagellate cells

The algae produced a biflagellate zoospore during reproduction.
