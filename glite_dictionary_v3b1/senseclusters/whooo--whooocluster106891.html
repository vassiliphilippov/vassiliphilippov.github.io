whooo

interjection
owl hoot sound
9.9
Pronunciation: / wuː / (AmE), / wuː / (BrE)
an imitative sound used to represent the hoot or call of an owl

Examples

Whooo of an owl
In the quiet forest, we suddenly heard a distant whooo echoing through the trees.
The children imitated the owl's whooo during their nighttime adventure.
A soft whooo drifted in from the garden as the moon rose.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hoot - the characteristic call of an owl
