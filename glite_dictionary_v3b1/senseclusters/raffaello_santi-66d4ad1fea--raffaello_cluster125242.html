Raffaello Santi

proper noun
Italian High Renaissance painter and architect
Pronunciation: / rəˌfɑːˌɛloʊ ˈsanti / (AmE), / raˌfaːˌɛloʊ ˈsanti / (BrE)
(1483–1520) An Italian painter and architect of the High Renaissance, known for his perfectly balanced compositions and influential frescoes.
