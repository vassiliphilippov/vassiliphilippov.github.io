blennioid fish

noun
type of elongated marine fish
Pronunciation: / blɛˈniːɔɪd fɪʃ / (AmE), / blɪˈniːɔɪd fɪʃ / (BrE)
any of a group of elongated, mostly scaleless marine fishes with large pectoral fins and reduced pelvic fins, typically found in shallow coastal waters.
