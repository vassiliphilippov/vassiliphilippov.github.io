balloon vine

noun
climbing plant with balloon-like seed pods
7.1
Pronunciation: / bəˈlun vaɪn / (AmE), / bəˈluːn vaɪn / (BrE)
a woody climbing plant with inflated, balloon-like seed pods, native to tropical regions of India, Africa, and America

Examples

The balloon vine plant
Botanists study the balloon vine for its medicinal properties and ornamental value.
Children were fascinated by the way the balloon vine seemed to float across the old fence.
The balloon vine in her garden produced curious, puffed seed pods every summer.
