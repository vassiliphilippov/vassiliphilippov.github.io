surmount

verb
overcome, deal with successfully
Pronunciation: / sərˈmaʊnt / (AmE), / səˈmaʊnt / (BrE)
to deal successfully with a difficulty or obstacle; to overcome

Examples

To surmount a challenge
After months of hard work, she finally surmounted the language barrier.
The company surmounted several financial obstacles to reach profitability.
With determination, he surmounted the challenges of his new job.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conquer - successfully overcome a problem or weakness
  • synonym: overcome - succeed in dealing with a problem or difficulty
  • antonym: succumb - fail to resist pressure or temptation
