third

noun
one of three equal parts
Pronunciation: / θɝd / (AmE), / θɜːd / (BrE)
Each of three equal parts into which something is divided.

Examples

One third of the cake
One third of the students in the class passed the exam with honors.
She divided the cake into thirds so everyone could have an equal piece.
The land was split into thirds among the three siblings.
