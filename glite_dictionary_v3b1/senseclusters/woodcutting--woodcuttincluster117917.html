woodcutting

noun
cutting down trees
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊdˌkʌtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwʊdˌkʌtɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or skill of cutting down trees or chopping wood, especially for fuel or construction

Examples

Woodcutting for firewood
He spent the afternoon woodcutting to gather enough firewood for the winter.
Many rural families rely on woodcutting as a source of income and fuel.
The art of woodcutting requires strength, skill, and knowledge of different types of trees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forestry - the science or practice of managing forests
  • synonym: logging - the process of cutting down trees for timber
