espresso machine

noun
machine for brewing espresso coffee
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈsprɛsoʊ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ɛˈsprɛsəʊ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine used to brew espresso coffee by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans under high pressure

Examples

Baristas often spend years mastering how to use an espresso machine properly.

The café upgraded to a commercial espresso machine to serve more customers quickly.
