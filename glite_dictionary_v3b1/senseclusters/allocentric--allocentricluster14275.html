allocentric

adjective
world-centered perspective
Pronunciation: / ˌæləˈsɛntrɪk / (AmE), / ˌaləˈsɛntrɪk / (BrE)
relating to a spatial perspective or representation that is centered on the environment or objects rather than the observer

Examples

Allocentric map
The navigation system uses an allocentric map to help users find their way in unfamiliar cities.
Researchers studied how children develop allocentric spatial awareness as they grow older.
Unlike egocentric strategies, allocentric approaches rely on external landmarks for orientation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: egocentric - centered on oneself or the observer's own position
