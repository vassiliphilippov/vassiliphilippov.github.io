LGBTQIA+

noun
community of non-normative gender/sexual identities
Pronunciation: / ˌɛlˌdʒiːˌbiːˌtiːˌkjuːˈaɪˈeɪ ˈplʌs / (AmE), / ˌɛl.dʒiːˌbiːˌtiːˌkjuːˈaɪˈeɪ ˈplʌs / (BrE)
the community of people who identify as lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer or questioning, intersex, asexual, or other non-normative gender or sexual identities
