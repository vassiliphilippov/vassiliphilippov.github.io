motion

noun
gesture used to communicate a signal
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.ʃən / (BrE)
a movement, especially of the hands or body, used to communicate a signal or message

Examples

Hand motion
He understood the coach's motion and moved to the left side of the field.
She gave a quick motion with her hand to signal him to stop.
The waiter responded to the customer's subtle motion for the bill.
