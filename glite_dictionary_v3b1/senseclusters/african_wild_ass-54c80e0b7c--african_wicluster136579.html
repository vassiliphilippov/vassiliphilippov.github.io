African wild ass

noun
wild ass native to northeast Africa
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌæfrɪkən waɪld ˈæs / (AmE), / ˌaf.rɪ.kən waɪld ˈas / (BrE)
A wild species of ass native to the arid regions of northeastern Africa, ancestor of the domestic donkey.

Examples

A African wild ass herd
Researchers observed a small herd of African wild asses near the Ethiopian border.
The African wild ass is critically endangered due to habitat loss and hunting.
Unlike domestic donkeys, the African wild ass has distinctive leg striping.

Related Terms

  • synonym: donkey - domesticated ass derived from the African wild ass
