adsorbent

noun
material that adsorbs another substance
Pronunciation: / ædˈsɔrbənt / (AmE), / adˈsɔːbənt / (BrE)
a material that has the ability to adsorb another substance onto its surface

Examples

An adsorbent material
Activated carbon is a common adsorbent used in water purification systems.

Zeolites act as an adsorbent in the process of gas separation.
