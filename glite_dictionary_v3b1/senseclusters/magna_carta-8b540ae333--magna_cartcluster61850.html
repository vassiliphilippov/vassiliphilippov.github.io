Magna Carta

proper noun
historic English legal document (1215)
Pronunciation: / ˌmæɡnə ˈkɑrtə / (AmE), / ˌmæɡnə ˈkɑːtə / (BrE)
an important historical document signed in 1215 that limited the powers of the English monarch and established certain legal rights
