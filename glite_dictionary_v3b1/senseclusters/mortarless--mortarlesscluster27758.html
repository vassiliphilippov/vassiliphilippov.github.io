mortarless

adjective
without mortars
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrtərləs / (AmE), / ˈmɔːtələs / (BrE)
not involving, containing, or affected by mortar weapons; free from the use or presence of military mortars

Examples

Inspectors confirmed that the base was now completely mortarless and free of heavy artillery.
