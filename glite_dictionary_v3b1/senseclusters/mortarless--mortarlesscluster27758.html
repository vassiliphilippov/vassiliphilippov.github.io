mortarless

adjective
without mortars
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrtərləs / (AmE), / ˈmɔːtələs / (BrE)
not involving, containing, or affected by mortar weapons; free from the use or presence of military mortars

Examples

Mortarless border zone
The peace agreement required all sides to keep the border mortarless for the duration of the ceasefire.
After the conflict, the city center was declared a mortarless zone to ensure civilian safety.
Inspectors confirmed that the base was now completely mortarless and free of heavy artillery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: armed - equipped with weapons
  • synonym: demilitarized - free from military forces or weapons
