manitou

noun
Algonquian spirit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmænɪtuː / (AmE), / ˈmænɪtuː / (BrE)
a spirit or supernatural force in the traditional beliefs of some Algonquian-speaking Indigenous peoples of North America
