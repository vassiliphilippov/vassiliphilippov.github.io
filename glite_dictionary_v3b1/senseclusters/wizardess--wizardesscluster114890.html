wizardess

noun
skilled woman
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪzərdɛs / (AmE), / ˈwɪzədɛs / (BrE)
an informal or humorous term for a woman who is extremely skilled or clever at a particular activity

Examples

Tech wizardess
Everyone in the team admired her as the wizardess who could fix any technical issue.
The young chess wizardess defeated all her opponents with ease.
Her reputation as a wizardess in the kitchen was well deserved after the delicious meal she prepared.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wizard - a person who is very skilled or clever at something
