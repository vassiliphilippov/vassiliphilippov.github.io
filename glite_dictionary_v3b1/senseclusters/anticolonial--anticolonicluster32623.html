anticolonial

noun
opponent of colonialism
Pronunciation: / ˌænti.kəˈloʊniəl / (AmE), / ˌænti.kəˈləʊniəl / (BrE)
a person who opposes colonial rule or supports independence from colonial powers

Examples

An anticolonial leader
The anticolonial spoke passionately about the need for self-determination and freedom.
Many anticolonials played key roles in the country's struggle for independence.
As an anticolonial, she organized protests against foreign control of local resources.

  • synonym: nationalist - a person who advocates for national independence or self-government
