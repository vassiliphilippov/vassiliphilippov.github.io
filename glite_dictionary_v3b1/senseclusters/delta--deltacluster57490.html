delta

noun
the fourth Greek letter (Δ, δ)
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛltə / (AmE), / ˈdɛltə / (BrE)
the fourth letter of the Greek alphabet (Δ, δ), often used in mathematics and science to represent a difference or change in a quantity

A small delta in speed can make a big difference in a race.
