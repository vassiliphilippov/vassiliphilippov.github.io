fiche

noun
microfilm card
9.8
Pronunciation: / fiːʃ / (AmE), / fiːʃ / (BrE)
a small sheet or card, especially of microfilm, used to store and display information or records

Examples

Microfilm fiche
The librarian retrieved the article from a fiche stored in the archives.
Researchers often use fiches to access old census records quickly.
He learned to operate the machine that displays information from a fiche.

Related Terms

  • synonym: card - a small, flat piece of stiff paper or plastic used for information
  • synonym: microfiche - a flat piece of film containing microphotographs of documents
