sugary

adjective
containing sugar; sweet
Pronunciation: / ˈsuɡəri / (AmE), / ˈʃʊɡəri / (BrE)
containing sugar or tasting sweet like sugar

Examples

Sugary food
Many breakfast cereals are surprisingly sugary despite being marketed as healthy.
She avoids sugary drinks to maintain a healthy diet.
The cake was too sugary for my taste, so I didn't finish it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sweet - having the taste or characteristic of sugar
  • antonym: unsweetened - not made sweet or containing sugar
