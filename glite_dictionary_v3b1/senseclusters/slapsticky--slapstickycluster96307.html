slapsticky

adjective
silly physical humor
Pronunciation: / ˈslæpˌstɪki / (AmE), / ˈslæpˌstɪki / (BrE)
funny in a silly, exaggerated, or clumsy physical way, like slapstick comedy

Examples

A slapsticky comedy
The comedian's slapsticky performance had the whole crowd laughing at his wild antics.
She prefers more slapsticky movies with lots of pratfalls and silly chases.
Their slapsticky sense of humor made even the most serious meetings entertaining.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slapstick - comedy involving exaggerated physical actions
  • antonym: subtle - not obvious or showy; understated
