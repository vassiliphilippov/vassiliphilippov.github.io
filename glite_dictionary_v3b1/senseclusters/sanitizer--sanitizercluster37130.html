sanitizer

noun
data cleaning tool
Pronunciation: / ˈsænɪˌtaɪzər /; / ˈsænɪtaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈsænɪˌtaɪzə /; / ˈsænɪtaɪzə / (BrE)
a software tool that removes sensitive information or finds programming errors to make files or programs safer
