maximalism

noun
demanding the most
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈmæk.sɪ.mə.lɪ.zəm/ /; / /ˈmæk.sə.mə.lɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈmæk.sɪ.mə.lɪ.zəm/ /; / /ˈmæk.sə.mə.lɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
a policy or approach that seeks the greatest possible demands or outcomes, often rejecting compromise

Examples

Political maximalism
The party's maximalism made it difficult to reach an agreement with their opponents.
Critics argue that maximalism in negotiations can lead to unnecessary conflict and delays.
Her approach was defined by maximalism, always pushing for the most ambitious goals possible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extremism - the holding of extreme political or religious views
  • antonym: minimalism - a policy or approach that seeks the smallest or simplest demands or outcomes
