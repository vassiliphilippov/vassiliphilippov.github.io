tympanoplasty

noun
surgical repair of the eardrum or middle ear bones
7.1 9.0
Pronunciation: /tɪm.pə.nə.ˈplæs.tiː/ (AmE), /tɪm.pə.nə.ˈplæs.tiː/ (BrE)
A surgical procedure to repair defects or injuries in the eardrum or the small bones of the middle ear.
