gossypol

noun
toxic cotton compound
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːsɪˌpɑl / (AmE), / ˈɡɒsɪpɒl / (BrE)
a toxic yellow compound found in cotton plants, sometimes used in medicine or scientific research

Examples

Toxic gossypol compound
Scientists are investigating whether gossypol can be used to treat certain types of cancer.
High levels of gossypol in cottonseed can be harmful to animals if not removed.
The yellow pigment in the cotton plant is called gossypol and is known for its toxicity.
