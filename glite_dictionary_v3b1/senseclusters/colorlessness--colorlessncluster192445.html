colorlessness

noun
lack of color
9.9 5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌl.ər.ləs.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈkʌ.lə.ləs.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality or state of being without color or hue; lacking chromatic color
