multiport

adjective
with multiple ports
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltipɔrt / (AmE), / ˈmʌltipɔːt / (BrE)
having or involving more than one port or connection point, especially for devices or equipment

Examples

Multiport device
The technician installed a multiport switch to connect all the computers in the office.
A multiport charger can power several devices at the same time.
They upgraded to a multiport router for better network flexibility.

Related Terms

  • antonym: single-port - having only one port or connection point
