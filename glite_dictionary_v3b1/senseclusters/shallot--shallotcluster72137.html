shallot

noun
a mild-flavored onion used in cooking
7.2
Pronunciation: / ʃəˈlɑt / (AmE), / ʃəˈlɒt / (BrE)
a small, mild-flavored bulb in the onion family, used in cooking to add flavor; also, the plant that produces such bulbs
