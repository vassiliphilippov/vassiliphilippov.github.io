Synonyms

    4.1 2/m
    a blockage fills a pipe or tube and stops flow, while an obstruction means any physical thing that blocks movement.
  • barrier
    2.8 8/m
    a barrier is a built structure that stops access or keeps things apart, while an obstruction is any physical blockage in a passage.
  • roadblock
    3.9 2/m
    a roadblock is an obstacle placed to stop or slow progress on a road or goal, while an obstruction is any physical blocker.
  • bottleneck
    4.6 1.0/m

  • obstacle
