unpitying

adjective
showing no pity or mercy
5.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpɪtiɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɪtiɪŋ / (BrE)
showing no pity, compassion, or mercy; harsh and unfeeling

Examples

An unpitying judge
Despite her pleas, the judge remained unpitying and delivered a harsh sentence.
His unpitying attitude alienated many of his colleagues.
The novel describes the passage of time as an unpitying force.
