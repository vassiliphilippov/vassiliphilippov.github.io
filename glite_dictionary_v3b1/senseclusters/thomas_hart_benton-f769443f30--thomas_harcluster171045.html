Thomas Hart Benton

proper noun
American painter; Regionalist artist
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑməs hɑrt ˈbɛntən / (AmE), / ˈtɒməs hɑːt ˈbɛntən / (BrE)
(1889–1975) United States artist noted for his paintings depicting life in the Midwest and South, central to the Regionalist art movement.
