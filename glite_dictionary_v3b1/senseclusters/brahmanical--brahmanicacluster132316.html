brahmanical

adjective
supporting Brahmin dominance
9.9
Pronunciation: / brɑːˈmænɪkəl / (AmE), / brɑːˈmænɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or supporting the social or political dominance of Brahmins, often reinforcing caste hierarchies in society

Examples

Brahmanical dominance
The new law was seen as a challenge to the brahmanical traditions that shaped the region's social order.
Activists spoke out against brahmanical attitudes that perpetuate inequality in education.
Her research explores how brahmanical values have influenced cultural practices over centuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: casteist - supporting or promoting caste-based discrimination
  • antonym: egalitarian - believing in or promoting equal rights for all people
