hysterical

adjective
affected by hysteria; uncontrollably emotional
4.2 3.2
Pronunciation: /hɪ.ˈste.rɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /hɪ.ˈste.rɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
extremely or uncontrollably emotional, often in a way that seems unreasonable or out of control

Examples

Hysterical behavior
During the crisis, some people experienced hysterical behavior that seemed uncontrollable.
She burst into hysterical laughter when she heard the news.
The patient's hysterical reactions made diagnosis difficult for the doctors.
During the emergency, a hysterical crowd rushed to the exits.
She became hysterical when she heard the unexpected news.
The comedian's performance was so hysterical that the audience couldn't stop laughing.
