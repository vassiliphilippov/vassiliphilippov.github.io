Synonyms

  • hysteretic
    9.9 0.00/m
    hysteretic sounds technical and describes uncontrolled emotional excitement in medical settings, while hysterical is common and everyday.
  • hysteric
    8.6 0.04/m
    hysteric suggests a clinical link to mental disorder and loss of control, while hysterical can be used more broadly in speech.
  • hyperemotional
    7.9 0.01/m
    hyperemotional means showing very intense feelings that often change quickly, while hysterical implies loss of control or panic.
  • overemotional
    5.0 0.14/m
    overemotional describes being too emotional in a judged way, while hysterical suggests extreme, often uncontrollable emotional outbursts.
