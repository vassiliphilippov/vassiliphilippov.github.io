squirrel-cage motor

noun
type of electric motor with cage-like rotor
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɝːrəl keɪdʒ ˈmoʊtər / (AmE), / ˈskwɪrəl keɪdʒ ˈməʊtə / (BrE)
An electric motor that uses a rotor shaped like a squirrel cage, commonly found in induction motors.
