Synonyms

  • reserve
    3.1 7/m
    reserve means keeping something back for future use or someone, while earmark means assigning it to a specific purpose or recipient.
  • appropriate
    6.9 1/m
    appropriate means officially allocating funds or resources in a budget or law, while earmark means setting them aside for a named purpose.
