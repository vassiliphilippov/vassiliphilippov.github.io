invoice

verb
send a bill to someone
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌvoɪs / (AmE), / ˈɪnvɔɪs / (BrE)
to send someone a bill for goods or services provided

Examples

To invoice a client

She invoiced the client as soon as the project was finished.
The firm will invoice you for any additional work required.
