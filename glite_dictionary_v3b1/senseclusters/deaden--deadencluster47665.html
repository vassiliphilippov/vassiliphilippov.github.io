deaden

verb
convert mercury to powdered form
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛdən / (AmE), / ˈdɛd.ən / (BrE)
To convert metallic mercury into a grey powder composed of minute globules, typically by shaking it with chalk or fatty oil.

Examples

To deaden mercury
Chemists sometimes deaden mercury by shaking it vigorously with chalk.
In the laboratory, he carefully deadened the mercury before proceeding with the next step.
To improve safety, the procedure includes instructions on how to deaden metallic mercury.
