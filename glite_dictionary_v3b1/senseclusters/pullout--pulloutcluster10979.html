pullout

noun
removable section or roadside stop
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊlˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈpʊlˌaʊt / (BrE)
a section that can be removed from a publication, or a place at the side of a road where vehicles can stop temporarily

Examples

Pullout map
The magazine included a pullout map of the city for tourists.
We stopped at a pullout to take photos of the beautiful mountain view.
The newspaper's weekend edition had a special pullout section on local events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insert - a separate page or section included in a publication
  • synonym: lay-by - a place at the side of a road for vehicles to stop
