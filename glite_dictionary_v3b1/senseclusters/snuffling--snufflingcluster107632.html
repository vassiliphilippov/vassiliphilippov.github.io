snuffling

adjective
making sniffing sounds; prone to sniffle
3.1 5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsnʌf.əlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsnʌf.əlɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) making repeated sniffing sounds, especially because of a cold or blocked nose; prone to sniffle.

Examples

A snuffling sound
A snuffling sound came from under the puppy's blanket as it searched for food.
Even after her cold improved, she was still slightly snuffling in the mornings.
The snuffling child kept reaching for tissues during the movie.
