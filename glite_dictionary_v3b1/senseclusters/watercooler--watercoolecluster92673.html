watercooler

noun
workplace gossip
3.1 0.11/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…ît…öÀåkuÀêl…ö / (AmE), / Ààw…îÀêt…ôkÀåkuÀêl…ô / (BrE)
informal conversation or gossip among employees at work, especially that which happens around a drinking-water dispenser

Examples

Watercooler chat
The latest company news quickly became watercooler talk among the staff.
After the meeting, everyone gathered for some watercooler conversation about the new manager.
Rumors about the merger spread at the watercooler before any official announcement was made.
