Leonese

noun
language of León
9.9
Pronunciation: / liˈoʊniz / (AmE), / liˈəʊniːz / (BrE)
a Romance language historically spoken in the province of León and nearby areas in northwestern Spain

Examples

Leonese language

Efforts are being made to preserve Leonese as an important part of regional culture.
