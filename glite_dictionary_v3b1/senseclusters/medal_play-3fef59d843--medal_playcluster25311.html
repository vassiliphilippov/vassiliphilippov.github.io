medal play

noun
golf scoring by total strokes
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛd.əl pleɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɛd.əl pleɪ / (BrE)
(in golf) a scoring system where the winner is determined by the total number of strokes taken, also known as stroke play.

Examples

Medal play golf
In medal play, every stroke counts toward your final score.
She prefers medal play because it rewards consistency over the entire round.
The championship was decided using medal play instead of match play.
