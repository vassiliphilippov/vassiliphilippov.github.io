warm-up routine

noun
preparatory exercises before activity
3.2 0.04/m

A series of light exercises or activities performed before a more intense physical activity to prepare the body and reduce the risk of injury.

Examples

A warm-up routine before exercise
Before the soccer match, the team went through a thorough warm-up routine on the field.
Her daily warm-up routine includes stretching, light jogging, and jumping jacks to get her muscles ready.
It's important to complete a warm-up routine to prevent injuries before starting any strenuous exercise.
