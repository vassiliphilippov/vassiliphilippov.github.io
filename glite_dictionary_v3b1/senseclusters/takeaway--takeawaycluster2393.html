takeaway

noun
a concession by employees in labor talks
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪkəˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈteɪkəˌweɪ / (BrE)
a concession or a benefit given up by employees in labor negotiations, typically as part of an agreement to help reduce a company's costs

Examples

Contract takeaway
Although some pay raises were granted, the healthcare changes were seen as a takeaway by many workers.
Management insisted on a takeaway regarding overtime pay to control rising costs.
The union's agreement to fewer vacation days was a significant takeaway in the contract negotiations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concession - something given up or conceded, especially in negotiations
