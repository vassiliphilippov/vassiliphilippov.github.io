tonally

adverb
in relation to tone or tonal qualities
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊnəli / (AmE), / ˈtəʊnəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to tone, especially in sound, color, or style; with respect to tonal qualities

Examples

Speak tonally flat
His speech was tonally flat, lacking any enthusiasm or emotion.
The film is tonally inconsistent, shifting from comedy to drama without warning.
The painting is tonally harmonious, with gentle gradations of color throughout.
