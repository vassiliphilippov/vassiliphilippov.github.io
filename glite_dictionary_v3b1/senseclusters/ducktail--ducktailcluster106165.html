ducktail

noun
upward car spoiler
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌkˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈdʌkˌteɪl / (BrE)
a small rear spoiler or car body shape that curves upward at the back, resembling a duck's tail, used to improve stability or aerodynamics

Examples

Car ducktail spoiler
The classic sports car featured a distinctive ducktail that set it apart from other models.
Adding a ducktail can help improve a vehicle's stability at high speeds.
Many car enthusiasts admire the unique look of a ducktail on vintage models.
