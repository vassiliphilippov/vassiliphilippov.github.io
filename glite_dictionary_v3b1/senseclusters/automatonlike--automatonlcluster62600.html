automatonlike

adjective
resembling a mechanical or unthinking machine
6.8
Pronunciation: / ɔˌtɑməˈtɑnˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ɔːˌtæməˈtɒnˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling the mechanical or unthinking functioning of a machine; lacking spontaneity or emotion

Examples

Automatonlike behaviour
During the meeting, her replies became increasingly automatonlike, as if she was on autopilot.
His automatonlike performance made it clear he was merely going through the motions.
She responded to every question in an automatonlike manner, without showing any emotion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: human - demonstrating human feelings or spontaneity
  • synonym: mechanical - acting like a machine; lacking emotion or spontaneity
