lifting device

noun
machine for lifting heavy loads
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪftɪŋ dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / ˈlɪftɪŋ dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
A machine or piece of equipment designed to lift or move heavy loads.

Examples

A lifting device machine
A forklift is a common type of lifting device used in warehouses.
Always inspect the lifting device before operating it for safety reasons.
The workers used a lifting device to move the steel beams into position.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crane - a large machine for lifting and moving heavy objects
  • synonym: hoist - a mechanical device for lifting or lowering a load
  • synonym: jack - a device for raising heavy objects off the ground
