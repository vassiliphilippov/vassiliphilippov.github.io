Pronunciation: / ˈɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a suffix used to form nouns meaning a person who studies or is an expert in a particular subject

Examples

Ologist suffix in words
A geologist studies the structure and history of the Earth.
If you are interested in animals, you might become a zoologist.
The hospital hired a new cardiologist to treat heart patients.
