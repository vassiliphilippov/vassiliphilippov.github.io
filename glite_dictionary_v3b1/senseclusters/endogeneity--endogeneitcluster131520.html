endogeneity

noun
mutual cause and effect
9.9
Pronunciation: /en.də.dʒə.ˈne.tɪ/ /; / /en.də.dʒə.ˈnɪ.tɪ/ (AmE), /en.də.dʒə.ˈne.tɪ/ /; / /en.də.dʒə.ˈnɪ.tɪ/ (BrE)
the state or quality of originating from within a system, process, or organism, including situations where cause and effect influence each other
