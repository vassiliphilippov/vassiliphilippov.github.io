fragrantly

adverb
blatantly, obviously
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪɡrəntli / (AmE), / ˈfreɪɡrəntli / (BrE)
in a conspicuously obvious or blatant manner, especially in reference to wrongdoing or offense

Examples

Fragrantly break the law
The company fragrantly ignored safety rules, putting workers at risk while cutting costs.
He fragrantly broke the law, showing no concern for the consequences.
The politician fragrantly disregarded the regulations during the campaign.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blatantly - in an open and unashamed manner
  • synonym: flagrantly - in a shockingly noticeable or obvious way
  • antonym: secretly - in a hidden or concealed manner
