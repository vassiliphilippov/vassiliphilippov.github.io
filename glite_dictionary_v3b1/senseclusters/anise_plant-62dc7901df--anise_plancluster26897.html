anise plant

noun
aromatic Mediterranean plant used as flavoring
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈænɪs plænt / (AmE), / ˈænɪs plɑːnt / (BrE)
A Mediterranean plant (Pimpinella anisum) cultivated for its aromatic seeds, used as flavoring and in medicine.

Examples

The anise plant seeds
The anise plant thrives in warm climates and produces flavorful seeds.
The tea was flavored with the seeds of the anise plant.
You can grow your own anise plant in a sunny spot in the garden.
