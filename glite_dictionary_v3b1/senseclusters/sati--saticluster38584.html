sati

noun
Buddhist mindfulness
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːti / (AmE), / ˈsʌti / (BrE)
in Buddhism, the mental quality of attentive awareness, recollection, or mindfulness of the present moment

Examples

Buddhist sati practice
Practicing sati can help reduce stress and improve emotional well-being.
The monk explained that sati is essential for developing wisdom in Buddhist practice.
Many people attend meditation retreats to deepen their sati and concentration.
