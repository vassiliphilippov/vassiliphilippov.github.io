little rugrat

noun
mischievous child (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪtəl ˈrʌɡˌræt / (AmE), / ˈlɪt.əl ˈrʌɡ.ræt / (BrE)
an informal and often affectionate term for a mischievous child

Examples

A little rugrat child
Her little rugrat drew all over the walls with crayons again.
I can't believe how fast those little rugrats can make a mess.
Our neighbors have two little rugrats who play in the yard every afternoon.

Related Terms

  • antonym: angel - a very well-behaved child
  • synonym: brat - a badly behaved child
