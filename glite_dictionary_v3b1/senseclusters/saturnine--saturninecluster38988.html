saturnine

adjective
gloomy or sullen in mood or appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈsæt̬.ɚ.naɪn /; / ˈsæt.ərˌnaɪn / (AmE), / ˈsat.ə.nʌɪn /; / ˈsat.jʊə.naɪn / (BrE)
(of a person or their manner) gloomy, moody, or sullen; having a dark or bitter temperament
