siren

noun
device that emits a loud warning sound
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪrən /; / ˈsaɪɚn / (AmE), / ˈsaɪərən / (BrE)
a device that makes a loud, wailing sound to warn or signal people of danger

Examples

Police siren
A loud siren warned the neighborhood of the approaching storm.
As soon as the tornado siren sounded, everyone rushed to the shelter.
The fire truck's siren could be heard from several blocks away.
Please pull over if you hear a police siren behind you.
The fire truck raced down the street with its siren blaring.
The tornado siren sounded, warning everyone to take cover.
