pipi

noun
childish word for urine
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːpiː / (AmE), / ˈpiːpiː / (BrE)
a childish or informal word for urine or the act of urinating

Examples

To do a pipi
The little boy whispered that he needed to pipi before the movie started.
She asked her daughter if she wanted to pipi before going to sleep.
At preschool, children often say pipi when they need to use the bathroom.
