spaceward

adverb
towards outer space
Pronunciation: / ˈspeɪs.wɚd / (AmE), / ˈspeɪs.wəd / (BrE)
In the direction of outer space.

Examples

Soar spaceward quickly
As the rocket lifted off, it soared spaceward at tremendous speed.
She gazed spaceward, dreaming of becoming an astronaut one day.
The telescope is pointed spaceward to capture images of distant galaxies.
