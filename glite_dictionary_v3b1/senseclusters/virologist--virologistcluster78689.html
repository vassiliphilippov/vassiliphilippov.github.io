virologist

noun
specialist in computer viruses
6.5
Pronunciation: /vaɪ.ˈrɑː.lə.dʒɪst/ (AmE), /vaɪ.ˈrɒ.lə.dʒɪst/ (BrE)
(in cybersecurity) a specialist who studies computer viruses, malware, and their behaviour in order to detect, analyze, and defend against digital infections.

Examples

Cybersecurity virologist
The cybersecurity firm hired a virologist to analyze how the worm moved across corporate networks.
As a virologist, she studies malware behaviour to design better antivirus and containment tools.
At the conference, the virologist presented a paper on cross‑platform virus propagation and defence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: computer virology - the study of computer viruses, worms, and related malicious code
  • synonym: malware analyst - a professional who studies and analyzes malicious software (malware) to understand behaviour and develop defenses
