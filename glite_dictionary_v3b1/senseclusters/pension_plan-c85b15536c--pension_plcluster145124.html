pension plan

noun
retirement savings scheme
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnʃən plæn / (AmE), / ˈpɛnʃən plan / (BrE)
a scheme in which money is regularly put aside by an employer, employee, or both, to provide income after retirement

Examples

A pension plan contribution
He contributed a fixed amount each month to his pension plan.
Many companies offer a pension plan to help employees save for retirement.
The government is considering reforms to the national pension plan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: retirement plan - a plan to provide income after a person stops working
