jar

noun
a cylindrical container for storage
Pronunciation: / dʒɑr / (AmE), / dʒɑː / (BrE)
a wide-mouthed cylindrical container, typically made of glass or pottery, used for storing food or other substances

Examples

A glass jar
He found an old jar buried in the garden.
Please put the cookies back in the jar after you're done.
She kept homemade jam in a large jar on the kitchen shelf.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bottle - a narrow-necked container for liquids
  • synonym: tin - a metal container for storage, especially food
