extradiegetic

adjective
outside story world
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstrədaɪəˈdʒɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrədaɪəˈdʒɛtɪk / (BrE)
existing or occurring outside the story world of a film, book, or narrative

Examples

Extradiegetic music
The film's extradiegetic music set the mood but was not heard by the characters.
An extradiegetic narrator explained the background before the story began.
Critics discussed the use of extradiegetic elements to influence the audience's emotions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: diegetic - existing within the story world of a narrative
