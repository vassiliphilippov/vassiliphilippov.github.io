sport shirt

noun
casual short-sleeved shirt
Pronunciation: / spɔrt ʃɜrt / (AmE), / spɔːt ʃɜːt / (BrE)
A casual shirt with short sleeves, designed for comfort rather than formal wear, often worn for leisure or informal occasions.

Examples

A sport shirt with short sleeves
He wore a crisp sport shirt and khaki pants to the barbecue.
Her favorite summer outfit is a light sport shirt and white trousers.
You can dress down on Fridays and wear a sport shirt to the office.

Related Terms

  • synonym: polo shirt - a casual short-sleeved shirt with a collar and two or three buttons at the neck
