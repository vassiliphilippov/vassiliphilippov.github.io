have a fit

verb
suddenly become very angry
5.8
Pronunciation: / hæv ə fɪt / (AmE), / hæv ə fɪt / (BrE)
to suddenly become very angry or upset and show it strongly

Examples

She nearly had a fit when she saw the price of the dress.
Try not to have a fit over such a small mistake.
