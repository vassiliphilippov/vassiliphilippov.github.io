deuteranopic

adjective
unable to see green; type of color blind
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌduː.tə.rəˈnɑː.pɪk / (AmE), / ˌdjuː.tər.əˈnɒ.pɪk / (BrE)
Unable to perceive the color green or to distinguish green from purplish-red, as in a type of color blindness.

Examples

Deuteranopic color blindness
Because he was deuteranopic, he had difficulty choosing ripe tomatoes among green ones.
Special filters can help deuteranopic individuals better distinguish between red and green colors.
The deuteranopic condition affects about 1% of males in some populations.
