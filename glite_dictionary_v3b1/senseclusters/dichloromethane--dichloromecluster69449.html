dichloromethane

noun
volatile liquid solvent
8.7
Pronunciation: / daɪˌklɔɹoʊˈmɛθeɪn / (AmE), / daɪˌklɔːrəˈmiːθeɪn / (BrE)
a volatile, nonflammable liquid used as a solvent, paint remover, and refrigerant

Examples

dichloromethane solvent
Always wear gloves when handling dichloromethane due to its potential health risks.
Because dichloromethane evaporates quickly, it is commonly used in paint removers.
The laboratory used dichloromethane as a solvent to extract the compound.
