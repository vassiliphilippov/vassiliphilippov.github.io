embarcadero

noun
wharf or landing place
Pronunciation: / ɛmˌbɑːrkəˈdɛroʊ / (AmE), / ɛmˌbɑːkəˈdɛərəʊ / (BrE)
a wharf or landing place where ships dock and goods or passengers are loaded or unloaded

Examples

Busy embarcadero dock
The ferry arrived at the embarcadero just as the sun was setting over the bay.
Vendors set up their stalls along the embarcadero to sell fresh seafood to tourists.
We enjoyed a long walk down the embarcadero, watching ships come and go.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dock - a structure where ships are loaded, unloaded, or repaired
  • synonym: pier - a platform extending from shore into water for use as a landing place
