sensible

adjective
aware or conscious of something
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnsəbəl / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsɪbəl / (BrE)
aware of or conscious of something, especially through intuition or intellect

Examples

Sensible of danger
He was sensible that his remarks had made an impact on the team.
I am sensible of the difficulties faced by the organization.
She became sensible of someone watching her from across the street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conscious - aware of and responding to one's surroundings
  • antonym: unaware - not aware or conscious of something
