Synonyms

  • renunciation
    8.2 0.37/m
    renunciation is a formal public giving up of a right or claim, while relinquishment is a general act of giving up rights or duties.
  • renouncement
    8.9 0.02/m
    renouncement is a formal statement or act of giving up a claim or possession, while relinquishment is the general surrender of rights or responsibilities.
  • abandonment
    5.9 0.68/m
    abandonment means leaving or giving up property without intent to reclaim it, while relinquishment covers giving up claims, positions, or duties more broadly.
