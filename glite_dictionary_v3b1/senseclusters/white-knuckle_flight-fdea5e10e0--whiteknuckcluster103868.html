white-knuckle flight

noun
extremely frightening flight
Pronunciation: / waɪt ˈnʌkəl flaɪt / (AmE), / waɪt ˈnʌkl flaɪt / (BrE)
a flight that is extremely frightening, causing intense nervousness or fear

Examples

A white-knuckle flight
After a white-knuckle flight through severe turbulence, the passengers applauded when the plane finally landed safely.
My first business trip included a white-knuckle flight over the Rocky Mountains during a snowstorm.
She vowed never to fly again after experiencing a white-knuckle flight full of terrifying bumps and sudden drops.
