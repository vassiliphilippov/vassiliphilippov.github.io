crosshair

noun
targeting mark
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔsˌhɛr / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌheə / (BrE)
a mark or set of intersecting lines (usually in a plus shape) in an optical device or on a screen, used to help aim or indicate a precise point
