observe

verb
make a remark or comment
1.0
Pronunciation: / əbˈzɝv / (AmE), / əbˈzɜːv / (BrE)
to say or mention something as a comment, especially formally or thoughtfully

Examples

She observed a fact
During the meeting, she observed that the budget needed closer review.
He observed in his speech that teamwork was essential to success.
The professor observed that several students were absent today.

Related Terms

  • synonym: note - to mention something in passing; to observe or point out
  • synonym: remark - to say or write something as a comment
