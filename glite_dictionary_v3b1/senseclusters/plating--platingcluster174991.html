plating

noun
application of a thin metal layer
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpleɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of covering a surface with a thin layer of metal, or the thin layer of metal itself

Examples

Gold plating
Gold plating is often used for electrical connectors to prevent corrosion.
Industrial plating processes can enhance both the appearance and durability of metal products.
The silver plating on the antique tray has started to wear off.
Old coins often have a gold plating that gives them their shiny appearance.
The company specializes in chrome plating for automotive parts.
The silver plating on the cutlery began to wear off after years of use.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bare - without a covering or coating
  • synonym: coating - a layer of any substance covering a surface
  • synonym: electroplating - the process of depositing a layer of metal by electrolysis
