sodium cyanide

noun
poisonous chemical compound (NaCN)
1.7 5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊdiəm saɪˈænaɪd / (AmE), / ˈsəʊdiəm saɪˈənaɪd / (BrE)

Examples
