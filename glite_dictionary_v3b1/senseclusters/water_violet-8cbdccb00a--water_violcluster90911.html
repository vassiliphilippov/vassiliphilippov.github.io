water violet

noun
aquatic plant with violet flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtər ˈvaɪələt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˈvaɪələt / (BrE)
a European and western Asian aquatic plant with submerged and floating leaves and violet flowers, also known as featherfoil (Hottonia palustris)

Examples

Water violet plant
Water violet typically grows in shallow ponds and slow-moving streams across Europe.
During spring, the water violet produces delicate violet flowers above the water's surface.
The aquarium featured several water violets among its selection of aquatic plants.
