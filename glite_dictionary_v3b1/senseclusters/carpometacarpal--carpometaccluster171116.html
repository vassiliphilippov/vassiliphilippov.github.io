carpometacarpal

adjective
of wrist and hand bones
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːrpoʊˌmɛtəˈkɑːrpəl / (AmE), / ˌkɑːpəʊˌmɛtəˈkɑːpəl / (BrE)
relating to the joint or area between the carpal (wrist) and metacarpal (hand) bones in the hand

Examples

Carpometacarpal joint pain
The doctor explained that the carpometacarpal joint is often affected by arthritis in older adults.
Pain in the carpometacarpal region can make gripping objects difficult.
Surgery may be required if the carpometacarpal joint becomes severely damaged.
