slaty

adjective
grayish-blue or gray, like slate
Pronunciation: / ˈsleɪti / (AmE), / ˈsleɪti / (BrE)
Having the color or appearance of slate; grayish-blue or gray like slate or granite.

Examples

Slaty gray color
Her eyes were a deep, slaty gray that seemed to change with the light.
Large rocks with a slaty sheen lined the riverbank.
The slaty sky of dawn promised rain.
