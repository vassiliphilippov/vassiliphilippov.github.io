pot

noun
slang for a rounded belly
8.4 1.17/m
Pronunciation: / pÉ‘t / (AmE), / pÉ’t / (BrE)
(slang) A potbelly; a large, rounded stomach, especially as a result of weight gain.

Examples

A big pot belly
