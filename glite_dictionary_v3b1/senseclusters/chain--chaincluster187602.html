chain

noun
series of hills or mountains
Pronunciation: / tʃeɪn / (AmE), / tʃeɪn / (BrE)
a series of hills or mountains connected together to form a line or range

Examples

A mountain chain
A snow-capped chain rose in the distance, marking the national border.
The Andes form the longest chain of mountains in the world.
They hiked across a rugged chain separating the two valleys.
