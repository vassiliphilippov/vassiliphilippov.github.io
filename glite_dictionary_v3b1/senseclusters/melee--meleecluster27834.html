melee

noun
confused, noisy fight
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛleɪ, meɪˈleɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɛleɪ, ˈmeɪleɪ / (BrE)
A confused, noisy fight involving many people.

Examples

A melee fight
Objects flew through the air in the chaotic melee at the concert.
Several fans were injured in the melee that broke out after the game.
The police struggled to restore order during the melee in the streets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brawl - a rough or noisy fight, usually in a public place
  • degree higher: riot - a violent public disorder or disturbance
  • degree lower: skirmish - a minor or brief fight or battle
