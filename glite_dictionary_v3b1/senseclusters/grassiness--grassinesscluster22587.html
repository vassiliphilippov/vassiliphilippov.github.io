grassiness

noun
quality of being grassy
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræsɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈɡrɑːsɪnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of resembling, tasting, or smelling like grass

Examples

Grassiness in flavor
The tea had a pleasant grassiness that reminded him of spring meadows.
Some people enjoy the grassiness in certain white wines, while others find it too strong.
The chef described the olive oil's subtle grassiness as a sign of its freshness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: earthiness - the quality of smelling or tasting like earth or soil
  • synonym: herbaceousness - the quality of having characteristics of herbs or plants
