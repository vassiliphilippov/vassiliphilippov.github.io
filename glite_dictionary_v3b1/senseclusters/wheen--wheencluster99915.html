wheen

noun
small quantity
Pronunciation: / wiːn / (BrE)
a small number or quantity of people or things, especially in Scottish English

Examples

A wheen of people
There were only a wheen of people at the gathering.
He brought a wheen of apples from the market for us to share.
Just a wheen of changes were made to the original plan.

Related Terms

  • antonym: many - a large number of people or things
