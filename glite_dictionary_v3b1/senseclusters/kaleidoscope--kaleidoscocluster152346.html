kaleidoscope

noun
optical toy with changing patterns
7.2 4.9
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈlaɪ.də.skoʊp/ (AmE), /kə.ˈlaɪ.də.skəʊp/ (BrE)
a toy consisting of a tube containing mirrors and pieces of colored glass, whose reflections produce changing symmetrical patterns when the tube is rotated

Examples

A kaleidoscope toy
As she turned the kaleidoscope, vibrant patterns shifted and changed before her eyes.
Children are often fascinated by the colorful images a kaleidoscope creates.
He remembered his childhood days spent gazing into a kaleidoscope for hours.
