satisfying kerplunk

noun
a pleasing impact sound
Pronunciation: / ˈsætɪsˌfaɪɪŋ kərˈplʌŋk / (AmE), / ˈsætɪsˌfaɪɪŋ kəˈplʌŋk / (BrE)
a pleasing, resonant sound of impact, often described as enjoyable or gratifying to hear

Examples

A satisfying kerplunk sound
He dropped the stone into the well, and it made a satisfying kerplunk at the bottom.
The ball landed in the bucket with a satisfying kerplunk, making everyone smile.
There’s something about the satisfying kerplunk of a letter falling through the mail slot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kerplunk - imitative word for a heavy, sudden sound of something falling or landing
