piano lesson

noun
instruction in playing the piano
Pronunciation: / piˈænoʊ ˈlɛsən / (AmE), / piˈænəʊ ˈlɛsən / (BrE)
an occasion or period of instruction in playing the piano, typically given by a teacher to a student

Examples

A piano lesson with teacher
After just one piano lesson, he was already able to play a simple song.
His parents encouraged him to take piano lessons to develop his musical skills.
She has a piano lesson every Saturday morning with her music teacher.
