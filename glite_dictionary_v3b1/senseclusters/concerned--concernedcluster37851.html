concerned

adjective
involved, especially in wrongdoing
3.5 44/m
Pronunciation: / k…ônÀàs…ùnd /; / k…ônÀàs…ùÀênd / (AmE), / k…ônÀàs…úÀênd / (BrE)

Examples
