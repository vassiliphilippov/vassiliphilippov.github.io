PMT

noun
loan payment function
Pronunciation: / piː ɛm tiː / (AmE), / piː ɛm tiː / (BrE)
a spreadsheet function that calculates the regular payment amount for a loan based on interest rate, number of periods, and principal

Examples

Excel PMT function
You can use the PMT function in Excel to find out your monthly mortgage payment.
The accountant explained how the PMT formula helps calculate loan repayments quickly.
By entering the interest rate and loan amount, the PMT function gives the payment per period.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loan - money that is borrowed and expected to be paid back
  • synonym: payment - an amount of money paid regularly
