screamy

adjective
intended to frighten
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈskriːmi / (AmE), / ˈskriːmi / (BrE)
designed to frighten or shock, often causing people to scream, especially in films or scenes

Examples

Screamy horror movie
The haunted house was filled with screamy special effects that startled everyone.
She prefers suspenseful movies over screamy horror films with lots of jump scares.
The director added several screamy moments to make the audience jump in their seats.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scary - causing fear or alarm
  • degree higher: terrifying - causing extreme fear
