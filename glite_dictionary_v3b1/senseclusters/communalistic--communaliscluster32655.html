communalistic

adjective
group-based loyalty

showing strong loyalty to one's own community or group, often leading to conflict with other groups.

Examples

Communalistic loyalty
The leader's communalistic views made it difficult to unite the different groups in the city.
Some media outlets were criticized for their communalistic coverage of the conflict.
A communalistic attitude can sometimes increase divisions between neighboring communities.
