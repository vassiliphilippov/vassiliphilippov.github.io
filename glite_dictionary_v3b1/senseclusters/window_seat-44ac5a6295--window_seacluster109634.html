window seat

noun
bench built into a window recess
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndoʊ ˌsiːt / (AmE), / ˈwɪndəʊ ˌsiːt / (BrE)
a long seat or bench built into the recess of a window, often providing storage or a comfortable place to sit and enjoy the view

Examples

A window seat bench
Cushions and a throw blanket made the window seat a cozy spot for morning coffee.
She spent the afternoon curled up on the window seat reading her favorite novel.
The new house features a window seat overlooking the garden, perfect for relaxing.
