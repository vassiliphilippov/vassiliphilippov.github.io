flicky

adjective
unpredictable or erratic
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈflɪki / (AmE), / ˈflɪki / (BrE)
unreliable or unpredictable in behavior or performance, often changing suddenly or without clear reason

Examples

Flicky phone behavior
Since the update, her phone's performance has been flicky, crashing or restarting without warning.
The internet connection was so flicky that video calls kept dropping unexpectedly.
His mood can be quite flicky, changing from cheerful to annoyed in minutes.
