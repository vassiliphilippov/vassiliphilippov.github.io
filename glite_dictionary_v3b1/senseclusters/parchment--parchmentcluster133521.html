parchment

noun
prepared animal skin for writing
3.2 6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːrtʃmənt / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtʃmənt / (BrE)
a material made from the skin of a sheep or goat, specially prepared for writing or printing on, used especially in the past

Examples

Ancient parchment scroll

Medieval scribes carefully inscribed texts onto sheets of parchment.
The ancient manuscript was preserved on parchment for centuries in the monastery library.
