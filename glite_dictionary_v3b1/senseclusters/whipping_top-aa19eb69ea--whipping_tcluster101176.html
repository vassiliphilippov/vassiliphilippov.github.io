whipping top

noun
toy top spun by whipping
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪpɪŋ tɑp / (AmE), / ˈwɪpɪŋ tɒp / (BrE)
A child's toy, shaped like a top, that is spun by striking it with a whip or cord.
