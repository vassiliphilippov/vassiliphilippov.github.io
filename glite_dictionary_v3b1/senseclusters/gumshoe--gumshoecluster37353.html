gumshoe

noun
informal term for detective
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌmˌʃu / (AmE), / ˈɡʌmˌʃuː / (BrE)
an informal term for a detective, especially one who works quietly and discreetly

Examples

A clever gumshoe
Every good mystery novel needs a sharp gumshoe to solve the case.
The gumshoe followed the suspect through the crowded market, careful not to be spotted.
With years of experience, the gumshoe knew how to blend into the shadows.
