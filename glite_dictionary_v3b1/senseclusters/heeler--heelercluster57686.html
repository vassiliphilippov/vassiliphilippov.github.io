heeler

noun
herding dog
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːlər / (AmE), / ˈhiːlə / (BrE)
a type of energetic dog, bred or trained to herd cattle by nipping at their heels

Our heeler loves to run and is always eager to work.
