Castilian

noun
native or inhabitant of Castile
Pronunciation: / kæˈstɪliən / (AmE), / kæˈstɪliən / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of Castile, a region in central Spain.

Examples

A proud Castilian

Many Castilians are proud of their regional traditions and cuisine.
The Castilian led the group with stories from his homeland.
