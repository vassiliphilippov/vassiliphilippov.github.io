inordinate

adjective
excessive; beyond what is reasonable
6.1 6.1
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈɔːr.də.nɪt/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈɔː.dɪ.nət/ (BrE)
Much greater or more than is reasonable, normal, or expected.

Examples

An inordinate amount
He took inordinate pride in his vast collection of stamps.
Her inordinate appetite for sweets worried her parents.
The inordinate amount of paperwork delayed the project.
