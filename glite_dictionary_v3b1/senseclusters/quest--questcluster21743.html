quest

noun
long or difficult search for something
3.2
Pronunciation: / kwɛst / (AmE), / kwɛst / (BrE)
a long or determined search for something important, often involving adventure or difficulty

Examples

A quest for treasure
Her lifelong quest for knowledge led her to travel the world.
The knight embarked on a quest to find the lost treasure.
They joined a quest to discover new sources of clean energy.
She is on a quest to discover the truth about her family's past.
The knight embarked on a dangerous quest to retrieve the legendary sword.
Their quest for the lost city took them deep into the jungle.
