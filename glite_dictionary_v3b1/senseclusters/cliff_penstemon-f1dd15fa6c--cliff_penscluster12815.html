cliff penstemon

noun
wildflower with large pink flowers on cliffs
Pronunciation: / klɪf ˈpɛnstəmən / (AmE), / klɪf ˈpɛnstɪmən / (BrE)
a perennial wildflower native to western North America, known for its large pink or rose flowers growing on rocky ledges and cliffs

Botanists trekked through the canyon to study the unique habitat of the cliff penstemon.
