unvaryingly

adverb
in a constant manner
5.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈvɛriɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈveəriɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that does not change or remains constant over time

Examples

Arrives unvaryingly on time
The train arrived unvaryingly at 8:00 every morning, no matter the weather.
He answered unvaryingly with the same polite phrase each time he was asked.
The teacher's tone remained unvaryingly calm throughout the difficult discussion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consistently - in the same way over time
  • antonym: irregularly - in a way that does not happen at regular times or intervals
  • synonym: steadily - in a regular and even way
