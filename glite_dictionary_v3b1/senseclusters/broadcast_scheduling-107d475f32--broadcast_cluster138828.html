broadcast scheduling

noun
planning broadcast program schedules
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɔdˌkæst ˈskɛdʒuˌlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːdkɑːst ˈʃedjuːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of planning and arranging the sequence and timing of programs on broadcast media such as television, radio, or online channels
