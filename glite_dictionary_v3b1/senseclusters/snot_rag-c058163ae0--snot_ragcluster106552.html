snot rag

noun
slang for tissue or handkerchief for the nose
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɑt ˌræɡ / (AmE), / ˈsnɒt ˌræɡ / (BrE)
an informal and often derogatory term for a cloth or tissue used to wipe nasal mucus

Examples

She refused to touch the dirty snot rag on the table.
