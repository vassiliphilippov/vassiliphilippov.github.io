staller

noun
person who delays
7.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààst…îl…ôr / (AmE), / Ààst…îÀêl…ô / (BrE)
a person who deliberately delays or avoids taking action or making a decision

Examples

A staller in meetings
The project was delayed because the staller refused to approve the final plans.
Everyone grew frustrated with the staller who kept postponing the decision.
As a known staller, he often avoided making choices until the last minute.
