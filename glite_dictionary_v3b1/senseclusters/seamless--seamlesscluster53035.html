seamless

adjective
perfectly consistent and coherent
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːmləs / (AmE), / ˈsiːmləs / (BrE)
having no apparent gaps, inconsistencies, or interruptions; perfectly consistent and coherent.

Examples

Seamless transition
Her presentation was so seamless that the audience barely noticed the transitions between topics.
The design of the app offers a seamless user experience from start to finish.
The two companies merged in a seamless manner, with no disruption to customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coherent - logical and consistent; forming a unified whole
  • antonym: fragmented - broken into separate or disconnected parts; lacking unity
