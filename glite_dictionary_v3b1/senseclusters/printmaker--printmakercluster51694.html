printmaker

noun
artist who creates prints
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪntˌmeɪkɚ / (AmE), / ˈprɪntˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
an artist who designs and creates prints by means of printmaking techniques such as etching, engraving, or lithography

Examples

A printmaker artist
As a printmaker, he experimented with different types of ink and paper.
She studied under a master printmaker to perfect her engraving skills.
The museum is holding a retrospective of Japan's most famous printmaker.

  • synonym: printmaking - the art or technique of making prints
