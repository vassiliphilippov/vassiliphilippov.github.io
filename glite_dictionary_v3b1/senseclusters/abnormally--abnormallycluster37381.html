abnormally

adverb
in an unusual or atypical way

In a way that is not normal; unusually or atypically.

Examples

An abnormally high level
His reaction to the medication was abnormally severe, which concerned his doctors.
Temperatures rose abnormally high for this time of year, surprising everyone.
The patient's heart was beating abnormally fast during the examination.
