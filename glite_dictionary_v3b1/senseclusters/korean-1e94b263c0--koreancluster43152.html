Korean

noun
a person from Korea
Pronunciation: / kəˈriən / (AmE), / kəˈriːən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Korea, especially one who speaks the Korean language

Many Koreans celebrate the Lunar New Year with special foods and traditions.
