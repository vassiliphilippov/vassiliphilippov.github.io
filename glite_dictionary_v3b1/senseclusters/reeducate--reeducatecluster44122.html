reeducate

verb
to teach again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈɛdʒəˌkeɪt /; / ˌriːˈɛdʒuˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈɛdjuːkeɪt / (BrE)
to teach or train someone again, especially to change their beliefs, behavior, or understanding
