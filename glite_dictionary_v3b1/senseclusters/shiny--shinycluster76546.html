Synonyms

  • glossy
    2.6 4.57/m
    glossy has a smooth, mirror-like surface often used for paper or hair, while shiny is more general.
  • lustrous
    5.3 0.85/m
    lustrous gives a soft, glowing shine that looks gentle and rich, while shiny just means it reflects light.
  • gleaming
    3.2 0.06/m
    gleaming shows a bright clear reflection like clean metal or windows, while shiny is a general reflective quality.
  • glistening
    3.4 0.00/m
    glistening shines with many small sparkles, often from wetness or oil on a surface, while shiny simply reflects light.
  • shining
    1.0 0.42/m
    shining looks bright and clear because something is clean or polished, while shiny focuses on reflecting light smoothly.
  • polished
    2.1 3.19/m
    polished is made smooth and shiny by rubbing or treating the surface, while shiny only describes the result.
  • burnished
    6.5 0.07/m
    burnished has a bright, metal-like shine from careful polishing, while shiny is the simple word for reflecting light.
  • glazed
    3.5 0.01/m
    glazed has a smooth, glass-like coating often on pottery or food after firing or baking, while shiny is a general surface quality.
  • bright
    1.0 45/m
    bright gives a strong light or vivid color and can mean clever, while shiny only describes a surface that reflects light.
