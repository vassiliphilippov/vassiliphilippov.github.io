how

adverb
degree or amount
Pronunciation: / haʊ / (AmE), / haʊ / (BrE)
used to ask or talk about the degree, amount, or extent of something.

Examples

How much money
How much does this laptop cost in your store?
Can you tell me how quickly the train will arrive?
She wondered how difficult the exam would be this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: what - used to ask for information about something
  • antonym: why - used to ask for a reason or explanation
