sociometry

noun
quantitative study of social relationships
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊsiˈɑːmətri / (AmE), / ˌsəʊsiˈɒmətri / (BrE)
the quantitative study of social relationships, especially within groups, to measure patterns of interaction and preferences among members

Examples

Advances in sociometry have improved how organizations map informal networks among employees.
Dr. Moreno pioneered the use of sociometry in group therapy sessions.
