clearly

adverb
in an intelligible or obvious manner
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪrli /; / ˈklɪr.li / (AmE), / ˈklɪəli /; / ˈklɪə.li / (BrE)
in a way that is easy to see, hear, or understand; in an intelligible or perceptible manner

Examples

Speak clearly
He spoke clearly, making it easy for the audience to follow his presentation.
She explained the process clearly so that everyone could understand.
The sign was clearly visible from the road.
Please speak clearly so everyone can understand you.
The instructions were clearly written on the label.
Through the window, she could clearly see the mountains in the distance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intelligibly - in a way that can be easily understood
  • synonym: obviously - in a way that is easily perceived or understood
  • antonym: unclearly - in a way that is not easy to understand or is ambiguous
  • antonym: vaguely - in a way that is not clear or definite
