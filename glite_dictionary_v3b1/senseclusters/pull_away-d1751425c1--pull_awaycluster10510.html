Synonyms

  • withdraw
    2.8 20/m
    withdraw means to leave or stop taking part in a group or activity, while pull away focuses on creating distance from others.
  • distance oneself from
    7.0 4/m
    distance oneself from means to deliberately separate emotionally or socially, while pull away can mean starting to move away physically or socially.
  • walk away
    3.5 30/m
    walk away means to leave a place or situation deliberately and calmly, while pull away can mean simply to start moving away.
  • peel off
    4.1 0.1/m
    peel off means to leave a group or formation and move in a different direction, while pull away can be any movement away.
  • draw back
    4.1 0.2/m

  • move back
    1.7 13/m
    move back means to return or retreat to a previous or more distant spot, while pull away means to begin creating distance from others.
