pig face

noun
silly or ridiculous facial expression
Pronunciation: / pɪɡ feɪs / (AmE), / pɪɡ feɪs / (BrE)
an exaggeratedly silly or ridiculous facial expression, often resembling the features of a pig

Examples

Make a pig face
At the party, everyone tried their best pig face during the photo contest.
She caught her little brother making a pig face in the bathroom mirror.
Whenever the teacher turned her back, Tom pulled a pig face to make the class laugh.
