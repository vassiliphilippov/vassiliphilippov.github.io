pleasure ground

noun
amusement park or recreational area
Pronunciation: / ˈplɛʒɚ ɡraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈplɛʒə ɡraʊnd / (BrE)
a park or area equipped with amusements and facilities for public entertainment; an amusement park

Examples
