anklet

noun
jewellery worn around the ankle
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋklət / (AmE), / ˈæŋklɪt / (BrE)
a piece of jewellery worn around the ankle

Examples

A gold anklet
Many cultures have traditional meanings for the anklet as a piece of jewelry.
She wore a delicate silver anklet that jingled with every step.
The artisan designed a beaded anklet to match the necklace.
