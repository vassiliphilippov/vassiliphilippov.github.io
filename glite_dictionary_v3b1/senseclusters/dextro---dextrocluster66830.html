dextro-

x
prefix: right side
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛkstɹoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdɛkstrəʊ / (BrE)
a prefix meaning 'right' or 'on the right side', used in scientific and medical terms

Examples

Dextro-cardia condition
The doctor explained that dextro-cardia means the heart is located on the right side of the chest.
In chemistry, dextro-rotation refers to the clockwise rotation of polarized light.
The prefix dextro- is often used in scientific terms to indicate the right side.

Related Terms

  • antonym: levo- - a prefix meaning 'left' or 'on the left side'
