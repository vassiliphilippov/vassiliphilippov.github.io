tsk

verb
make a 'tsk' sound to show disapproval
Pronunciation: / tsk / (AmE), / t͜sk / (BrE)
to make a 'tsk' sound, expressing disapproval or annoyance

Examples

To tsk in disapproval
He quietly tsked as the children ignored his warnings.
She tsked in disappointment when she saw the mess in the kitchen.
The teacher tsking softly made the whole class fall silent.
