glorification

noun
the portrayal of something as ideal
7.2 5.5
Pronunciation: /glɔː.rɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /glɔː.rə.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /glɔː.rɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /glɔː.rə.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
