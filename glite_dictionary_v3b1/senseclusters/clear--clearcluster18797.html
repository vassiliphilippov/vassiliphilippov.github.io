clear

verb
to remove throat obstruction by making a sound
1.0
Pronunciation: / klɪr / (AmE), / klɪə / (BrE)
to free the throat of an obstruction or mucus by making a rasping sound

Examples

To clear your throat
He stopped mid-sentence to clear his throat before continuing.
She tried to clear her throat quietly during the meeting.
The teacher cleared her throat to get the students' attention.
