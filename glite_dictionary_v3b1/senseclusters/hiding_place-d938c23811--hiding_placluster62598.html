hiding place

noun
place suitable for concealment
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪdɪŋ ˌpleɪs / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdɪŋ ˌpleɪs / (BrE)
a place where someone or something can be concealed safely from discovery

Examples

A secret hiding place
He searched for a hiding place when he heard footsteps approaching.
Police discovered the stolen jewels in a secret hiding place under the floorboards.
The children found a perfect hiding place behind the old oak tree.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hideout - a place where someone hides to avoid danger or capture
  • synonym: refuge - a place providing safety or shelter
