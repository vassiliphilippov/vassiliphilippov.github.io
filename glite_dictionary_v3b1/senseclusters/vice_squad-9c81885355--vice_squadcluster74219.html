vice squad

noun
police unit targeting vice crimes
Pronunciation: / vaɪs skwɑd / (AmE), / vaɪs skwɒd / (BrE)
a police division assigned to investigate and suppress crimes such as gambling and prostitution

Examples

The vice squad arrests
After a tip-off, the vice squad arrested three people involved in running a gambling den.
Members of the vice squad conducted several raids on suspected brothels last night.
The city established a vice squad to tackle illegal gambling and prostitution rings.
