wye

noun
Y-shaped junction
9.9 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / waÉª / (AmE), / waÉª / (BrE)

Examples

Plumbing wye fitting
The plumber installed a wye to connect the new drain line to the main pipe.
At the wye, the railway tracks split, allowing trains to change direction.
A wye fitting is often used in plumbing to join three pipes together.
