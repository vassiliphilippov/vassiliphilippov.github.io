crisper

noun
refrigerator compartment
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkrɪ.spər/ (AmE), /ˈkrɪ.spə/ (BrE)
a compartment in a refrigerator designed to keep fruits and vegetables fresh by maintaining humidity

Examples

Fridge crisper drawer
She put the carrots in the crisper to keep them fresh for the week.
The lettuce wilted because it was left out instead of being placed in the crisper.
Most modern refrigerators have a separate crisper for fruits and vegetables.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vegetable drawer - a refrigerator drawer for storing vegetables
