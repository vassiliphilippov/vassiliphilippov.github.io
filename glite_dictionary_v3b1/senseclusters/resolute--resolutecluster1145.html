resolute

noun
determined person
4.8 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / Ààr…õz…ôÀåluÀêt / (AmE), / Ààr…õz…ôluÀêt / (BrE)
a person who is determined and unwavering in their actions or beliefs

Examples

A resolute stands firm
The resolutes refused to back down despite the challenges they faced.
Among the crowd, only a few resolutes continued to protest until the end.
History often remembers the resolutes who stood up for their beliefs.
