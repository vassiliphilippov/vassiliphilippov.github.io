dried apricot

noun
apricot preserved by drying
4.5
Pronunciation: / draɪd ˈæprɪˌkɑt / (AmE), / draɪd ˈæprɪkɒt / (BrE)
an apricot that has been preserved by drying, often used as a sweet snack or in cooking

Examples

The recipe calls for soaking the dried apricot before mixing it into the cake batter.
