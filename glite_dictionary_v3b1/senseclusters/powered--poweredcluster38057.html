powered

adjective
operated by a specific type of power
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊ.ɚd / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.əd / (BrE)
(often in combination) operated or propelled by a particular type of power or energy.

Examples

Petrol-powered vehicles
New regulations affect all petrol-powered vehicles sold after 2025.
The island is now powered entirely by solar energy.
They built a powered wheelchair for her mobility needs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unpowered - not operated by an external power source
