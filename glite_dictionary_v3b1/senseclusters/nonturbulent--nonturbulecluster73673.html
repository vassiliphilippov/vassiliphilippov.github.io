nonturbulent

adjective
not turbulent; having smooth flow
6.7 6.3
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈtɜːr.bjə.lənt/ /; / /nɑːn.ˈtɜːr.bjʊ.lənt/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈtɜː.bjʊ.lənt/ /; / /nɒn.ˈtɜː.bjə.lənt/ (BrE)
