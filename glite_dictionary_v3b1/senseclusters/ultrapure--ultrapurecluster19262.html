ultrapure

adjective
extremely pure
Pronunciation: / ˌʌltrəˈpjʊr / (AmE), / ˌʌltrəˈpjʊə / (BrE)
extremely pure, containing almost no impurities or contaminants

Examples

Ultrapure water
The scientists required ultrapure chemicals to ensure accurate results in their experiments.
Manufacturing computer chips demands ultrapure silicon to prevent defects.
They filtered the water several times to achieve an ultrapure state for the test.
