woodworking vise

noun
carpenter's clamp with padded jaws
7.1 4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈwʊd.wɜːr.kɪŋ vaɪs/ (AmE), /ˈwʊd.wɜː.kɪŋ vaɪs/ (BrE)
