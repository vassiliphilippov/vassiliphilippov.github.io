multifocal

adjective
with multiple focal points
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈfoʊkəl / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈfəʊkəl / (BrE)
having or involving more than one focal point, especially in lenses or vision correction

Examples

Multifocal lenses
The optometrist suggested multifocal glasses to help her see both near and distant objects clearly.
Advances in multifocal lens technology have improved vision correction for many patients.
He found that multifocal contact lenses made reading and driving much easier.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: bifocal - having two focal points, especially in lenses
  • degree lower: single-vision - having only one focal point, especially in lenses
