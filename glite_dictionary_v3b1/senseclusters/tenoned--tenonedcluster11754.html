tenoned

adjective
joined with a tenon
8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈten.ənd/ (AmE), /ˈten.ənd/ (BrE)
joined or fitted using a projecting piece of wood called a tenon, especially in carpentry or woodworking

Examples

Tenoned wood joint
The craftsman used a tenoned joint to connect the legs to the table frame.
Many traditional doors are built with tenoned and mortised corners for extra strength.
The bookshelf was carefully tenoned to ensure it would last for years.
