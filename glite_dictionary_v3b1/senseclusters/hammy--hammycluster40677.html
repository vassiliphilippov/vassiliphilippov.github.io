hammy

adjective
exaggerated or overacted (esp. in acting)
Pronunciation: / ˈhæmi / (AmE), / ˈhæmi / (BrE)
(of acting or behavior) exaggerated or overdone in an affectedly dramatic way; overacted.

Examples

A hammy performance
His performance in the play was criticized as hammy and unrealistic.
Some viewers found her delivery hammy, but others enjoyed the showy style.
The actor tends to take a hammy approach, exaggerating every emotion for comic effect.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overacted - performed with excessive exaggeration or emotion
  • antonym: subtle - delicate and not obvious; opposite in style to exaggerated acting
