misborn child

noun
child born under dire circumstances
Pronunciation: / mɪsˈbɔrn tʃaɪld / (AmE), / mɪsˈbɔːn tʃʌɪld / (BrE)
A child born under unfortunate or ominous circumstances.

Examples

A misborn child legend
Legends often tell of a misborn child who rises to fulfill a prophecy.
She feared her son would be labeled a misborn child due to the circumstances of his birth.
The villagers whispered about the fate of the misborn child.
