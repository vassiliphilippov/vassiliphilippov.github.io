peacetime

noun
a period without war
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːstaɪm / (AmE), / ˈpiːstaɪm / (BrE)
A period during which a country or region is not involved in war.

Examples

During peacetime prosperity
After decades of conflict, the country finally experienced peacetime prosperity.
Many technological advances were developed during peacetime rather than war.
The army was much smaller in peacetime than during wartime.

Related Terms

  • antonym: wartime - a period during which a country is at war
