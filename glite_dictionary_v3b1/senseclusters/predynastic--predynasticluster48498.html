predynastic

noun
era before dynasties
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːdaɪˈnæstɪk / (AmE), / ˌpriːdaɪˈnæstɪk / (BrE)
the period in a region's history before the establishment of organized dynastic rule

Examples

The predynastic period
Archaeologists study pottery from the predynastic to learn about early communities.
The predynastic of Egypt reveals much about the origins of its civilization.
Artifacts from the predynastic help historians understand how societies developed before kings ruled.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dynastic - relating to a period ruled by a dynasty
  • synonym: prehistoric - relating to the time before written history
