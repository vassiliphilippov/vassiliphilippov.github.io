Kenyan shilling

noun
currency of Kenya
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛniən ˈʃɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɛnjən ˈʃɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Kenya, equal to 100 cents.
