unviable

adjective
not able to survive
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈvaɪəbl̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈvaɪəbl̩ / (BrE)
not capable of living, developing, or surviving under normal conditions

Examples

Unviable seeds
The scientists determined that the plant seeds were unviable after several failed attempts to grow them.
Doctors explained that the fetus was unviable and would not survive outside the womb.
Due to harsh environmental conditions, many animal embryos become unviable before birth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonviable - not able to survive or develop
  • antonym: viable - able to live, develop, or survive
