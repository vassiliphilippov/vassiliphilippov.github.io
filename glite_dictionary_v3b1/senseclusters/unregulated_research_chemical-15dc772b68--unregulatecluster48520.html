unregulated research chemical

noun
research chemical lacking regulatory oversight
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɛɡjəˌleɪtɪd rɪˈsɜrtʃ ˈkɛmɪkəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɛɡjʊleɪtɪd rɪˈsɜːtʃ ˈkɛmɪkəl / (BrE)
a chemical substance used for scientific research that is not controlled or monitored by regulatory authorities

Examples

Unregulated research chemical use
He conducted experiments with unregulated research chemicals before stricter regulations were introduced.
Many online vendors offer unregulated research chemicals, which may pose health risks to users.
The importation of unregulated research chemicals has raised safety concerns among health officials.
