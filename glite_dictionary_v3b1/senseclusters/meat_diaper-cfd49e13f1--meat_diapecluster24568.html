meat diaper

noun
slang for absorbent pad in packaged meat
6.4
Pronunciation: / mit ˈdaɪpɚ / (AmE), / miːt ˈdaɪpə / (BrE)
a humorous or slang term for the absorbent pad placed under packaged raw meat to soak up excess liquid

Examples

The meat diaper pad
Before cooking the steak, she discarded the meat diaper from the bottom of the tray.
He laughed when he first heard someone call it a meat diaper at the supermarket.
The meat diaper does a good job of keeping the packaging from leaking.
