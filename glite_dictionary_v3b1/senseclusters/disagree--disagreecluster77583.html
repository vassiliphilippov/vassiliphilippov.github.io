disagree

verb
to have a different opinion
Pronunciation: / dɪsəˈɡri / (AmE), / dɪsəˈɡriː / (BrE)
to have or express a different opinion; to not agree

Examples

To disagree on ideas
Her parents often disagree about how to discipline their children.
If you disagree with this proposal, please explain your reasons.
It's natural for people to disagree on complex issues like politics or religion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: agree - to have the same opinion; to consent
