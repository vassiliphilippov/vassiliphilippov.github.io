febrile

adjective
relating to fever
8.5 0.51/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàfiÀêbra…™l / (AmE), / ÀàfiÀêbr å…™l / (BrE)
Relating to or characterized by fever; having or showing symptoms of a fever.

Examples

A febrile illness
A febrile illness can sometimes cause additional complications in elderly patients.
Children often become febrile during viral infections in winter.
The patient demonstrated a febrile response after the surgery.

Synonyms

  • feverish
    3.5 0.99/m
    feverish is commonly used to mean feeling hotter than normal and unwell, while febrile sounds more formal and medical.
  • fevered
    4.4 0.09/m
    fevered is used for intense or excited states and often in vivid phrases, while febrile means showing true fever symptoms.

Related Terms

  • antonym: afebrile - not having or showing symptoms of a fever
