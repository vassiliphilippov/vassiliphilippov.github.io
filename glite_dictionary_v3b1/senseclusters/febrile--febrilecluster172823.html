febrile

adjective
relating to fever
7.8 8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːbraɪl / (AmE), / ˈfiːbrʌɪl / (BrE)
Relating to or characterized by fever; having or showing symptoms of a fever.

Examples

A febrile illness
A febrile illness can sometimes cause additional complications in elderly patients.
Children often become febrile during viral infections in winter.
The patient demonstrated a febrile response after the surgery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: afebrile - not having or showing symptoms of a fever
