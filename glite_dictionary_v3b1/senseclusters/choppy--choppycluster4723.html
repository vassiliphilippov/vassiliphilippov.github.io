choppy

adjective
rough with small waves (sea/water)
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑpi / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒpi / (BrE)
rough, uneven, or interrupted, especially describing water with many small waves

Examples

Choppy water
Strong winds made the usually calm lake quite choppy today.
The boat rocked in the choppy water, making some passengers uneasy.
We decided to postpone our swim because the ocean looked too choppy.
The lake became choppy as the wind picked up in the afternoon.
Her voice sounded choppy on the phone due to a poor connection.
Surfers prefer choppy waves for practicing their skills.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - (of water or the sea) free from waves or disturbance
  • synonym: rough - having an uneven or irregular surface or movement
