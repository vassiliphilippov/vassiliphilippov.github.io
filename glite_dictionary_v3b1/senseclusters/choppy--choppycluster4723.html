choppy

adjective
rough with small waves (sea/water)
9.6 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃɑː.piː/ /; / /ˈtʃɑː.pɪ/ (AmE), /ˈtʃɒ.piː/ /; / /ˈtʃɒ.pɪ/ (BrE)
rough, uneven, or interrupted, especially describing water with many small waves

Examples
