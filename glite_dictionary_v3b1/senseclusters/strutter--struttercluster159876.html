strutter

noun
supporting structural member
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈstrʌtər / (AmE), / ˈstrʌtə / (BrE)
a structural component that acts as a strut, providing support or reinforcement in construction.

Examples

Steel strutter
The engineer inspected the strutter to ensure it could handle the weight of the new roof.
A damaged strutter can compromise the stability of the entire structure.
They replaced the old wooden strutter with a steel one for added strength.

Related Terms

  • synonym: strut - a structural support designed to resist compression
