kay

interjection
informal agreement
8.4
Pronunciation: / keɪ / (AmE), / keɪ / (BrE)
used informally to express agreement, acceptance, or acknowledgment, similar to 'okay'.

Examples

Kay, no problem
"Can you send me the report by noon?" "Kay, I'll get it done."
If you want to meet at 5 instead of 4, that's kay with me.
She nodded and said "kay" before leaving the room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: no - used to express refusal or disagreement
  • synonym: okay - used to express agreement or acceptance
  • synonym: sure - used to show agreement or willingness
