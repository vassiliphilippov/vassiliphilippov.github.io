atrium

noun
central open space in a building
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.tri.əm / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.tri.əm / (BrE)
A central open area within a building, often with a glass roof and typically open to several floors and sometimes to the sky.

Examples

Glass-roofed atrium
A spacious atrium greeted visitors with natural light pouring in from above.
Many modern office buildings have an atrium at their center to provide a communal gathering space.
The hotel features an impressive glass-roofed atrium filled with tropical plants.
