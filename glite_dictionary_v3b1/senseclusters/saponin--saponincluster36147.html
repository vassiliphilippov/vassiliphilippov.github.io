saponin

noun
soapy plant compound used in detergents
9.9 7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈsæ.pə.nɪn/ (AmE), /ˈsæ.pə.nɪn/ (BrE)
any of a group of plant-derived compounds that produce a soap-like foam in water and are used in detergents, foaming agents, and emulsifiers

Examples

The detergent uses natural saponin as a mild, eco-friendly ingredient.
