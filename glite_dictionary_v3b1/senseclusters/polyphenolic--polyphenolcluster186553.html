polyphenolic

noun
compound with phenol groups
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑlifəˈnɑlɪk / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪfiːˈnɒlɪk / (BrE)
a chemical compound that contains multiple phenol groups, often found in plants and known for antioxidant properties

Examples

Plant polyphenolics
Scientists are studying how polyphenolics in green tea may help protect against certain diseases.
The extract contains several polyphenolics that act as natural antioxidants.
Many fruits and vegetables are rich in polyphenolics, which can benefit human health.

Related Terms

  • synonym: phenolic - relating to or containing phenol
  • synonym: polyphenol - a compound with multiple phenol units
