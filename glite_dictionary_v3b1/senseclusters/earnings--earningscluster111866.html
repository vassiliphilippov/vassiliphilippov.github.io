earnings

noun
profit after expenses
Pronunciation: / ˈɝː.nɪŋz /; / ˈɝːnɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈɜː.nɪŋz /; / ˈɜːnɪŋz / (BrE)
money or profit received from work, business, or investment

Examples

Net earnings
After calculating depreciation, their net earnings were reported to shareholders.
Strong sales contributed to higher earnings this year compared to last year.
The company's quarterly earnings exceeded analysts' expectations.
Despite working extra hours, his weekly earnings remained the same.
Her earnings last year allowed her to buy a new house.
Most of his earnings are saved for his children's education.

  • antonym: loss - the amount by which expenses exceed revenues
  • synonym: pay - money received for work or services
  • synonym: profit - financial gain after all expenses
  • synonym: wages - regular payment for work, typically paid weekly or daily
