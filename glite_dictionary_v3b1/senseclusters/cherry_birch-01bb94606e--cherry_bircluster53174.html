cherry birch

noun
birch tree with spicy bark, used for furniture
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛr.i bɝːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛr.i bɜːtʃ / (BrE)
a birch tree native to eastern North America with spicy brown bark, yielding a volatile oil and hard, dark wood used in furniture making

Examples

A cherry birch tree
Botanists found a stand of mature cherry birch in the eastern Appalachian forest.
The spicy aroma of cherry birch bark can be noticed after a rainfall.
The wood from the cherry birch is prized by furniture makers for its strength and beauty.
