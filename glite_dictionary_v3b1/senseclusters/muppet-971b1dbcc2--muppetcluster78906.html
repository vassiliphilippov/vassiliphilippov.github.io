Muppet

noun
foolish person
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌpɪt / (AmE), / ˈmʌpɪt / (BrE)
an informal term, especially in British English, for a foolish or incompetent person who makes silly mistakes

Examples

You silly Muppet
He forgot his keys again, and his friends called him a Muppet for the third time this week.
Don't be such a Muppet—read the instructions before you start assembling the furniture.
She laughed and said, 'You absolute Muppet!' when he spilled coffee on his shirt.

Related Terms

  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence or skill
  • synonym: idiot - a person who acts in a very stupid or silly way
