engage

verb
take part in war or battle
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
to take part in or begin a conflict, war, battle, or campaign against an enemy

Examples

To engage the enemy
Allied forces engaged in a lengthy campaign to liberate the region.
The navy engaged enemy ships just off the coast.
The two armies engaged in fierce combat at dawn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: combat - to fight or engage actively in battle
  • antonym: surrender - to give up or yield in a conflict or war
  • synonym: wage - to carry on (a war or campaign)
