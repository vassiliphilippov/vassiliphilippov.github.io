screech owl

noun
small North American owl with a distinctive call
Pronunciation: / skritʃ aʊl / (AmE), / skriːtʃ aʊl / (BrE)
a small owl found in North and Central America, known for its loud screeching or whistling call

Examples

Biologists studied the nesting habits of the local screech owls for several months.
