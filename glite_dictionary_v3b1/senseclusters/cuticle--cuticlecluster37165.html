cuticle

noun
dead skin at base of nail
5.0 5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːtɪkəl /; / ˈkjuː.tɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˈkjuːtɪkəl /; / ˈkjuː.tɪ.kəl / (BrE)
the layer of skin (dead or living) at the base of a fingernail or toenail

Examples

Cuticle care
A manicure usually involves caring for the nail and the cuticle.
Always use a cuticle oil to keep your cuticle healthy and moisturized.
Gently push back the cuticle rather than cutting it to avoid infections.
During a manicure, the technician gently pushed back the cuticle to tidy up the nails.
Sometimes, damage to the cuticle can lead to painful infections near the nails.
Too much cleaning with harsh soap can dry out the cuticle around your fingernails.
