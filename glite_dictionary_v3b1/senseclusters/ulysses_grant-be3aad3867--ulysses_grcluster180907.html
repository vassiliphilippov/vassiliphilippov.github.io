Ulysses Grant

proper noun
18th U.S. President and Civil War general
Pronunciation: / juːˈlɪsiz ɡrænt / (AmE), / juːˈlɪsiːz ɡrɑːnt / (BrE)
(1822–1885) The 18th President of the United States and commander of the Union armies during the American Civil War.
