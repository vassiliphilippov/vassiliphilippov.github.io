Quinquagesima

noun
Sunday before Ash Wednesday
9.9
Pronunciation: / kwɪŋkwəˈdʒɛsɪmə / (AmE), / kwɪŋkwəˈdʒɛsɪmə / (BrE)
the Sunday immediately before Ash Wednesday, marking the last Sunday before the beginning of Lent in the Christian calendar

Examples

Quinquagesima Sunday
Many churches hold special services on Quinquagesima to prepare for the start of Lent.
She recalled attending Quinquagesima services as a child in her parish.
The theme of forgiveness is often emphasized during Quinquagesima celebrations.
