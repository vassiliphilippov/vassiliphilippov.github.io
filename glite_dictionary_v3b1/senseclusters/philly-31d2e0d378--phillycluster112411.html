Philly

noun
steak and cheese sandwich
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪli / (AmE), / ˈfɪli / (BrE)
a sandwich made with thinly sliced steak and melted cheese, originally from Philadelphia, also called a Philly cheesesteak
