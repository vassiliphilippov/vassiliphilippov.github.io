aplite

noun
fine-grained granitic rock
Pronunciation: / ˈæp.laɪt / (AmE), / ˈæp.laɪt / (BrE)
A light-colored, fine-grained igneous rock composed mainly of quartz and feldspars, typically found in granite formations.
