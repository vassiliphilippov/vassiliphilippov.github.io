Synonyms

  • corrigendum
    9.9 0.03/m
    a corrigendum is a formal published notice that corrects errors, while an erratum names the original error in the text.
  • typographical error
    3.0 0.1/m
    a typographical error is a mistake made while typing or setting text, while an erratum is the note that records and corrects it.
  • misprint
    2.2 0.3/m
    a misprint is a small mechanical mistake in printed text, while an erratum is the formal notice that points out and corrects such errors.
