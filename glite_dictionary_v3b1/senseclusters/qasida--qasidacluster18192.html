qasida

noun
Arabic praise poem
Pronunciation: / kɑːˈsiːdə / (AmE), / kɑːˈsiːdə / (BrE)
a long, formal poem in Arabic, Persian, or Urdu literature, often written to praise someone or something, but also used for elegy, satire, or criticism
