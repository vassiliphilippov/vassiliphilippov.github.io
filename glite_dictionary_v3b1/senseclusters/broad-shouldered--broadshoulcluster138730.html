broad-shouldered

adjective
having wide shoulders
1.3
Pronunciation: / brɔd ˈʃoʊldɚd / (AmE), / brɔːd ˈʃəʊldəd / (BrE)
having shoulders that are wide and broad in proportion to the body

Examples

A broad-shouldered athlete
A broad-shouldered athlete walked confidently onto the field.
She admired his broad-shouldered frame as he carried the boxes.
The lifeguard was a broad-shouldered man with a deep tan.

Related Terms

  • antonym: narrow-shouldered - having shoulders that are narrow
