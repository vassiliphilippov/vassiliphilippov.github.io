Geordie

noun
dialect spoken in Newcastle-upon-Tyne area
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔr.di / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔː.di / (BrE)
the nonstandard dialect spoken by natives of Newcastle-upon-Tyne and surrounding areas in northeast England

Examples

Geordie accent
It can be difficult for outsiders to understand strong Geordie dialect.
Many people find the Geordie accent charming and distinctive.
She speaks fluent Geordie after living in Newcastle for years.
