memorial park

noun
cemetery with landscaped grounds
1.0
Pronunciation: / məˈmɔːriəl ˈpɑrk / (AmE), / mɪˈmɔːriəl ˈpɑːk / (BrE)
a cemetery with landscaped grounds and minimal use of traditional gravestones, designed to serve as a restful park for remembering the deceased

Examples

A memorial park cemetery
Families gathered at the memorial park to remember their loved ones on Memorial Day.
The memorial park features gardens and peaceful walking paths among the graves.
The city dedicated a new memorial park on the edge of town.
