photobomb

verb
to appear in someone else's photo as a prank
6.8 5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈfoʊ.toʊ.bɑːm/ /; / /ˈfoʊ.tə.bɑːm/ (AmE), /ˈfəʊ.təʊ.bɒm/ /; / /ˈfəʊ.tə.bɒm/ (BrE)
to unexpectedly or intentionally appear in someone else's photograph, often as a joke or prank, usually to spoil or add humor to the picture

Examples

To photobomb a selfie
It's always funny when someone photobombs a serious family photo.
My little brother tried to photobomb every picture we took at the party.
We were posing in front of the museum when a stranger photobombed our selfie.
