embroidery frame

noun
device for holding fabric in embroidery
4.5
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbroʊdəri freɪm / (AmE), / ɪmˈbrəʊdəri freɪm / (BrE)
a frame, often made of two hoops, used for holding fabric taut while embroidering

Examples

An embroidery frame holder
After finishing the border, she removed the cloth from the embroidery frame.
It is easier to keep your stitches neat if you use an embroidery frame.
She secured the fabric in the embroidery frame before starting her new design.
