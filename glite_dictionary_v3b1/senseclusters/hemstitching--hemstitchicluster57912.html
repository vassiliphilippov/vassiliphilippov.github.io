hemstitching

noun
decorative embroidery stitch
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛmˌstɪtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɛmˌstɪtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
a decorative embroidery stitch in which parallel threads of fabric are drawn out and the remaining threads are grouped together with stitches

Examples

Hemstitching on fabric
Antique handkerchiefs often feature beautifully detailed hemstitching as a sign of skilled craftsmanship.
She spent hours mastering different techniques, including hemstitching, to decorate her linens.
The fine hemstitching along the edge of the napkins gave them an elegant finish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embroidery - the craft or process of decorating fabric with needle and thread
