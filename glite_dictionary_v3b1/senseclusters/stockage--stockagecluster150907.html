stockage

noun
enclosure of stakes
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑkɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈstɒkɪdʒ / (BrE)
an enclosure or defensive barrier made of stakes or posts, used for protection

Examples

Wooden stockage fence
The pioneers constructed a stockage to keep out wild animals at night.
Soldiers took shelter behind the stockage during the attack.
A wooden stockage surrounded the small settlement for added security.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fort - a fortified building or military stronghold
  • synonym: stockade - a strong enclosure or barrier made of posts or stakes
