Anglican

adjective
of the Anglican Church
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡlɪkən / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡlɪkən / (BrE)
Relating to the Anglican Church, its members, or its traditions.

Examples

An Anglican church
She attended an Anglican service every Sunday with her family.
The Anglican Church is known for its distinctive liturgy and history.
The Anglican tradition traces its roots back to the Reformation in England.
