prefiguration

noun
foreshadowing event or example
8.8 8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˌfɪɡjəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːˌfɪɡjʊˈreɪʃən /; / ˌpriːfɪɡjʊˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
