nonprehensile

adjective
unable to grasp or hold
9.9
Pronunciation: / nɑn.prɪˈhɛn.səl / (AmE), / nɒn.prɪˈhɛn.saɪl / (BrE)
(of a body part or appendage) not able to grasp or hold objects.

Examples

Nonprehensile feet
Many birds have nonprehensile feet and cannot grasp branches tightly.
The robot was designed with nonprehensile arms suited only for pushing or sliding items.
Unlike monkeys, cows have nonprehensile tails that cannot be used to grip objects.

Related Terms

  • antonym: prehensile - able to grasp or hold objects
