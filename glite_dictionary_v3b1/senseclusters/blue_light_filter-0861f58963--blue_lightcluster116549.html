blue light filter

noun
filter reducing blue light from screens
2.2 2.9
Pronunciation: /bluː laɪt ˈfɪl.tər/ (AmE), /bluː laɪt ˈfɪl.tə/ (BrE)
a filter or screen feature that reduces the amount of blue light emitted from digital devices to minimize eye strain or improve sleep

Examples

A blue light filter screen
Many laptops now come with a built-in blue light filter option for users.
She activated the blue light filter on her smartphone to reduce eye strain at night.
Some people buy special glasses that act as a blue light filter while working on their computer.
