ebony wood

noun
dark, dense hardwood from ebony trees
Pronunciation: / ˈɛbəni wʊd / (AmE), / ˈɛbəni wʊd / (BrE)
a dense, dark hardwood from ebony trees, highly valued for its rich color and used in fine furniture and musical instruments

Examples

Dark ebony wood
Collectors prize antique chess sets made from ebony wood and ivory.
The violin's fingerboard is crafted from ebony wood for durability and elegance.
To achieve a luxurious finish, the cabinetmaker selected ebony wood for the inlay.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mahogany - a reddish-brown hardwood used chiefly for furniture
  • synonym: rosewood - a dark, dense tropical hardwood used for fine furniture and instruments
