staggered

adjective
extremely surprised
Pronunciation: / ˈstæɡɚd / (AmE), / ˈstæɡəd / (BrE)
extremely surprised or shocked by something unexpected

Examples

Staggered by news
I was staggered by the amount of support we received from the community.
He looked staggered when he heard the unexpected news about his promotion.
They were staggered to see how much the city had changed since their last visit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amazed - filled with great surprise or wonder
  • synonym: astonished - very surprised or amazed
  • antonym: unimpressed - not feeling admiration or interest
