Synonyms

  • detect
    3.2 43/m
    detect is to discover a hidden or hard-to-find thing, often with tools or tests, while sense covers feeling or noticing.
  • discern
    5.8 9.48/m
    discern means spotting subtle or unclear differences after careful look or thought, while sense is general awareness of things.
  • notice
    1.0 164/m
    notice means see or pay attention to something right away, often by choice, while sense can be more physical or intuitive.
  • feel
    1.0 550/m
    feel describes being aware of touch, pain, or emotion from inside, while sense has a wider meaning including tools and intuition.
