sextette

noun
group of six things
7.1
Pronunciation: / sɛkˈstɛt / (AmE), / sɛkˈstɛt / (BrE)
A group or set of six similar things considered as a unit.

Examples

A sextette of chairs
A sextette of brightly colored chairs surrounded the round table.
In the final scene, a sextette of dancers took the stage together.
The jazz band performed as a sextette for the first time last night.
