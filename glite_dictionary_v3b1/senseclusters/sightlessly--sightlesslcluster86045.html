sightlessly

adverb
without seeing
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪtlɪsli / (AmE), / ˈsaɪtlɪsli / (BrE)
in a way that does not use sight or as if unable to see

Examples

To move sightlessly
The owl glided sightlessly through the darkness, searching for prey.
He wandered sightlessly in the thick fog, unable to find his way.
The statue stared sightlessly ahead, its eyes blank and unseeing.
He walked sightlessly through the dark hallway, feeling his way along the wall.
The cat stared sightlessly out the window, lost in thought.
She reached sightlessly for her glasses on the nightstand in the early morning light.
The old dog wandered sightlessly around the familiar yard.
She reached out sightlessly in the darkness, searching for the light switch.
The statue stared sightlessly into the distance, its eyes unseeing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blindly - without seeing or without careful thought
  • antonym: visibly - in a way that can be seen
  • antonym: visually - in a way that involves seeing or sight
