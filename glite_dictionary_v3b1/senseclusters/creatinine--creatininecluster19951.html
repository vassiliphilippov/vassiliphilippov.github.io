creatinine

noun
muscle waste product
Pronunciation: / kriˈætəˌnin / (AmE), / kriˈætɪniːn / (BrE)
a chemical waste product produced by muscles and filtered out of the blood by the kidneys

Examples

Creatinine blood level
Doctors check creatinine levels to assess kidney function.
A sudden rise in creatinine can indicate problems with the kidneys.
The laboratory reported that her creatinine was within the normal range.

Related Terms

  • synonym: creatine - a compound in muscles that helps produce energy
  • synonym: urea - a waste product formed in the liver and excreted in urine
