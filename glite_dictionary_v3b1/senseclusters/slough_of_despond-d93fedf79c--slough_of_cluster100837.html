slough of despond

noun
state of deep depression
Pronunciation: / slaʊ əv dɪ'spɑnd /; / slaʊ əv dɪˈspɑnd / (AmE), / slaʊ əv dɪ'spɒnd /; / slaʊ əv dɪˈspɒnd / (BrE)
a state of deep despair or extreme sadness that is difficult to escape
