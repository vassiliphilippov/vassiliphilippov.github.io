scammonyroot

noun
tropical American herb with a large starchy root
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈskæməniˌruːt / (AmE), / ˈskaməniˌruːt / (BrE)
A tropical American perennial herb with prostrate or climbing stems and a very large starchy root; sometimes considered a source of the sweet potato.

Examples

The scammonyroot plant
Botanists are still debating whether the scammonyroot is related to the cultivated sweet potato.
The enormous scammonyroot can weigh several kilograms and store large amounts of starch.
Traditional healers sometimes used the scammonyroot for its purported medicinal properties.
