clearheaded

adjective
mentally focused and not confused
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪrˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈklɪəˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
not mentally confused; able to think clearly and make sensible decisions

Examples

A clearheaded leader
A clearheaded leader is crucial in times of crisis.
Despite the chaos around him, Max remained clearheaded and made rational decisions.
The anesthetic wore off, and she finally felt clearheaded again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clear-minded - having a clear and focused mind
  • antonym: confused - unable to think clearly or act sensibly
  • synonym: lucid - expressed clearly; easy to understand; able to think clearly
