wind up

verb
prepare to throw by swinging the arm
4.3 4.1
Pronunciation: /waɪnd ʌp/ /; / /wɪnd ʌp/ (AmE), /waɪnd ʌp/ /; / /wɪnd ʌp/ (BrE)
(in baseball, cricket, etc.) to move the throwing arm in a circular motion before pitching or throwing the ball.

Examples

You must wind up properly to get enough power behind the throw.
