enchant

verb
attract, captivate someone
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtʃænt / (AmE), / ɪnˈtʃɑːnt / (BrE)
to attract, delight, or fascinate someone, often in a way that feels magical

The fairy tale continues to enchant children around the world.
