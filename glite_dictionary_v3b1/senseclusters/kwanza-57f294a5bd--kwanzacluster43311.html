Kwanza

noun
African-American cultural festival
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑːn.zə / (AmE), / ˈkwæn.zə / (BrE)
A week-long festival celebrating African-American culture and heritage, observed from December 26 to January 1.

Examples

Celebrate Kwanza festival
Every year, our family gathers to celebrate Kwanza with music and traditional foods.
Many schools teach students about the history and meaning of Kwanza during the holidays.
The principles of Kwanza emphasize unity and community among African-Americans.
