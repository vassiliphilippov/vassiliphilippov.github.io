normocytic

adjective
normal-sized red blood cells
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɔrmoʊˈsɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɔːməˈsɪtɪk / (BrE)
having red blood cells that are normal in size.

Examples

Normocytic anemia
The doctor explained that a normocytic anemia means the red blood cells are of normal size.
Her blood test results were described as normocytic, ruling out certain types of anemia.
A normocytic blood sample suggests the problem is not related to cell size.

Related Terms

  • antonym: macrocytic - having red blood cells that are larger than normal
  • antonym: microcytic - having red blood cells that are smaller than normal
