in forma pauperis

adjective
without court fees
Pronunciation: / ɪn ˈfɔːrmə ˈpɔːpərɪs / (AmE), / ɪn ˈfɔːmə ˈpɔːpərɪs / (BrE)
allowed to proceed in court without payment of fees due to inability to pay

Examples

In forma pauperis status granted
The court granted her application to proceed in forma pauperis because she could not afford the filing fees.
He filed his lawsuit in forma pauperis after demonstrating his financial hardship.
Many prisoners submit appeals in forma pauperis when they lack the means to pay legal costs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indigent - lacking money or resources
