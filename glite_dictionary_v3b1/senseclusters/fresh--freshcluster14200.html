fresh

adjective
not canned or preserved (of food)
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /freʃ/ (AmE), /freʃ/ (BrE)
(of food or drink) recently made, gathered, or produced; not preserved, stale, or spoiled

Examples

Related Terms

  • synonym: new - recently created or obtained; not old or existing before
  • antonym: preserved - kept from spoiling by canning, freezing, or drying
  • antonym: stale - (of food) no longer fresh; musty, old, or spoiled
