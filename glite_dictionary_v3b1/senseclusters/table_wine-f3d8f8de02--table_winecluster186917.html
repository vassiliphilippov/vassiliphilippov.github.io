table wine

noun
wine served with meals, not over 14% alcohol
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪ.bəl waɪn / (AmE), / ˈteɪ.bəl waɪn / (BrE)
Wine with an alcohol content not exceeding 14 percent, typically served with meals.

Examples

A glass of table wine
He prefers a simple table wine rather than something expensive.
The restaurant offers a French table wine with every dinner menu.
They ordered a bottle of table wine to accompany their meal.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dessert wine - wine with a higher alcohol content, usually served with dessert
  • antonym: fortified wine - wine with added alcohol, higher than typical table wine
