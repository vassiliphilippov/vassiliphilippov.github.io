rehydrate

verb
to add water again
3.9
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈhaɪ.dreɪt/ /; / /rɪ.ˈhaɪ.dreɪt/ (AmE), /riː.ˈhaɪ.dreɪt/ /; / /rɪ.ˈhaɪ.dreɪt/ (BrE)
to add water or moisture to something that is dry, especially to restore it to its original state (including the body, food, or objects)
