Punjabi language

noun
language spoken in Punjab region
Pronunciation: / pʌnˈdʒɑːbi ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / pʌnˈdʒɑːbi ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the language of the Punjab region, spoken mainly in Pakistan and India

Examples

Punjabi language spoken widely
Many people in Canada speak Punjabi language as their first language.
She is fluent in both English and Punjabi language.
The movie was dubbed into the Punjabi language for local audiences.
