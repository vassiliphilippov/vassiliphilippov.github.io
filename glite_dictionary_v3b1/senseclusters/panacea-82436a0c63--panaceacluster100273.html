Panacea

proper noun
goddess of healing in Greek mythology
9.9
Pronunciation: / pəˈnæsiə / (AmE), / pəˈnæsɪə / (BrE)
(in Greek mythology) the goddess of healing, daughter of Aesculapius and sister of Hygeia.

Examples

Panacea goddess healing
In ancient tales, Panacea was believed to have the power to cure any illness.
Mythology describes Panacea as the compassionate daughter of Aesculapius, the god of medicine.
Statues of Panacea were often placed in healing temples across Greece.
