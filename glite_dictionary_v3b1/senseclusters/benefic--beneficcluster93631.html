benefic

adjective
having a beneficial influence
Pronunciation: / bəˈnɛfɪk / (AmE), / bɪˈnɛfɪk / (BrE)
Exerting a favorable or beneficial influence; having a positive effect.

Examples

Benefic influence
Ancient texts often refer to benefic influences guiding human destiny.
Her benefic presence brought calm and reassurance to the troubled community.
In astrology, Jupiter is usually considered a benefic planet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beneficial - producing good or helpful results
  • antonym: malefic - exerting a harmful influence (especially in astrology)
