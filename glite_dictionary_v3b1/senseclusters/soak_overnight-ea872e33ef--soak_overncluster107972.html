soak overnight

verb
immerse in liquid for a night
Pronunciation: / soʊk ˌoʊvərˈnaɪt / (AmE), / səʊk ˌəʊvəˈnaɪt / (BrE)
to immerse something in water or other liquid and leave it for an entire night, typically to soften or prepare it for use

Examples

To soak overnight dried beans

The instructions say to soak overnight for best results before cooking the rice.
