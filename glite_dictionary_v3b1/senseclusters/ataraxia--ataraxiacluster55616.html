ataraxia

noun
serene calmness; peace of mind
Pronunciation: / ˌæt̬əˈræksiə / (AmE), / ˌatəˈraksɪə / (BrE)
A state of serene calmness or absence of emotional disturbance; peace of mind, especially as considered by ancient philosophers.

Examples

Achieve ataraxia state
After years of soul-searching, she finally achieved a sense of ataraxia that nothing could disturb.
Meditation helped him cultivate ataraxia even in stressful situations.
Stoic philosophers regarded ataraxia as the ultimate goal of a wise person.

Related Terms

  • antonym: agitation - a state of anxiety or nervous excitement
  • synonym: tranquility - a state of being calm and peaceful
