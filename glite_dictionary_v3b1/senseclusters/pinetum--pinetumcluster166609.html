pinetum

noun
plantation of pine or conifer trees
6.4
Pronunciation: / paɪˈniːtəm / (AmE), / paɪˈniːtəm / (BrE)
an area, often a part of a botanical garden or arboretum, where pine trees and other conifers are grown for scientific or ornamental purposes
