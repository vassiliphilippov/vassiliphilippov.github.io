plum tree

noun
tree that bears plums
Pronunciation: / plʌm tri / (AmE), / plʌm triː / (BrE)
a tree that produces plums, an edible oval fruit with smooth skin and a single hard stone

Examples

A plum tree in orchard
Birds love to nest in the old plum tree near the orchard.
She planted a plum tree to enjoy its blossoms in spring and fruit in autumn.
The plum tree in our backyard is covered with ripe fruit in summer.

Related Terms
