iron eagle

noun
an eagle figure made of iron symbolizing power
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪɚn ˈiːɡəl / (AmE), / ˈaɪən ˈiːɡl̩ / (BrE)
a sculpture or artistic representation of an eagle made of iron, often used as a symbol of strength, power, and liberty
