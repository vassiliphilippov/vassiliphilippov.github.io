undernourish

verb
provide with too little nourishment
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈnɜrɪʃ / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈnʌrɪʃ / (BrE)
to provide with insufficient nourishment for proper growth, health, or development

Examples

To undernourish children
If you undernourish your body, it may not function properly over time.
Many parents do not realize that feeding only snacks can undernourish a growing child.
Zoo animals may become ill if keepers accidentally undernourish them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: malnourish - to supply with insufficient or unbalanced nourishment
