glitteringly

adverb
in a glowing manner
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɪtərɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈɡlɪtərɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is highly flattering, glowing, or shining with brilliance, especially in praise or description

Examples

Glitteringly praised
The movie was glitteringly reviewed by critics for its stunning visuals and performances.
She spoke glitteringly of her team's achievements at the awards ceremony.
The author's new book was glitteringly described in several major newspapers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brilliantly - in a very impressive or skillful way
  • antonym: dully - in a way that lacks brightness or excitement
