biogeographer

noun
scientist of species distribution
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪoʊdʒiˈɑːɡrəfər / (AmE), / ˌbaɪəʊdʒɪˈɒɡrəfə /; / ˌbaɪəʊdʒiˈɒɡrəfə / (BrE)
a scientist who studies the distribution of living organisms across different geographic regions
