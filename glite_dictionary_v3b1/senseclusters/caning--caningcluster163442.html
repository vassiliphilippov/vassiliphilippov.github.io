caning

noun
punishment with a cane
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkeɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of punishing someone by hitting them with a cane or stick, especially as a form of discipline

Examples

School caning
The school banned caning as a form of discipline decades ago.
Many countries have made caning illegal in educational institutions.
The principal faced criticism for allowing caning to continue at the school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beating - the act of hitting someone repeatedly as punishment
  • synonym: corporal punishment - physical punishment involving bodily pain
  • antonym: reward - something given in recognition of service or achievement
