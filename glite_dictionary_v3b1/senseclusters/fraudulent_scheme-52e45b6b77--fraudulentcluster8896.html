fraudulent scheme

noun
illegal or dishonest plan for profit
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɔdʒələnt skiːm / (AmE), / ˈfrɔːdjʊlənt skiːm / (BrE)
an illegal or dishonest plan intended to deceive people and gain money or other benefits unlawfully

Examples

A fraudulent scheme
He was arrested for his role in a fraudulent scheme involving counterfeit checks.
The financial advisor was found guilty of running a fraudulent scheme that cost clients millions.
The police uncovered a fraudulent scheme that tricked hundreds of people into investing in a fake company.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fraud - wrongful or criminal deception for personal or financial gain
  • antonym: legitimate enterprise - a lawful and honest business activity
  • synonym: scam - a dishonest scheme or fraud
