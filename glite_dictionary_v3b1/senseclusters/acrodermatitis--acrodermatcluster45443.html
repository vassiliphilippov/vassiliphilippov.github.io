acrodermatitis

noun
skin inflammation on extremities
9.9
Pronunciation: /æk.roʊ.dɜːr.mə.ˈtaɪ.tɪs/ /; / /ə.kroʊ.dɜːr.mə.ˈtaɪ.tɪs/ (AmE), /æk.rəʊ.dɜː.mə.ˈtaɪ.tɪs/ /; / /ə.krəʊ.dɜː.mə.ˈtaɪ.tɪs/ (BrE)
a medical condition characterized by inflammation of the skin on the hands, feet, or other extremities

Examples

Acrodermatitis on hands
The child developed acrodermatitis with red patches on his hands and feet.
Doctors often check for acrodermatitis when a patient presents with skin inflammation on the extremities.
Treatment for acrodermatitis usually involves topical creams and managing underlying causes.
