temporal vein

noun
vein draining the temple area
4.1 5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈtem.pə.rəl veɪn/ (AmE), /ˈtem.pə.rəl veɪn/ (BrE)
any of several veins that drain blood from the temple (temporal) region of the head.

Examples

Temporal vein in head
A blockage in the temporal vein can cause swelling in the temple area.
The surgeon carefully avoided injuring the temporal vein during the operation.
Visible pulsation of a temporal vein may indicate increased blood flow to the head.
