nonimitative

adjective
not imitative; original
9.0
Pronunciation: / nɑːnˈɪmɪteɪtɪv / (AmE), / nɒnˈɪmɪtətɪv / (BrE)
not marked by or given to imitation; original or not imitative.

Examples

A nonimitative style

Her nonimitative approach to painting set her work apart from her contemporaries.
The composer is known for his highly nonimitative musical style.
