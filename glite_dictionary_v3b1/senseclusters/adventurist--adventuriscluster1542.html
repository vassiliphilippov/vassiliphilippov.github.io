adventurist

noun
risk-taking opportunist
Pronunciation: / ədˈvɛntʃərɪst / (AmE), / ədˈvɛntʃərɪst / (BrE)
a person who takes risky or unprincipled actions, especially in politics, business, or military affairs, to gain personal advantage

Examples

Adventurist leader
The company suffered losses after an adventurist leader made several reckless investments.
Some politicians are criticized as adventurists for pursuing risky strategies to gain power.
The board replaced the adventurist with someone known for a more careful approach.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cautious person - someone who avoids risks and acts carefully
  • synonym: opportunist - a person who takes advantage of situations for personal gain
  • synonym: risk-taker - someone who is willing to take risks
