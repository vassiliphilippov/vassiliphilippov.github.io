prescription

noun
a doctor-ordered drug or medicine
2.2
Pronunciation: / prɪˈskrɪpʃən / (AmE), / prɪˈskrɪpʃən / (BrE)
a drug or medicine that can only be obtained with written authorization from a doctor or dentist

Examples

A prescription drug
She forgot to refill her prescription before her trip abroad.
Some painkillers are only available by prescription from a licensed physician.
The pharmacy will not release the prescription without your doctor's signed note.

Related Terms

  • antonym: over-the-counter - a drug available without a prescription
