vanilla pudding

noun
sweet vanilla-flavored dessert
Pronunciation: / vəˈnɪl.ə ˈpʊd.ɪŋ / (AmE), / vəˈnɪl.ə ˈpʊd.ɪŋ / (BrE)
A sweet, smooth dessert flavored with vanilla and usually thickened with flour rather than eggs.

Examples

A bowl of vanilla pudding
A bowl of homemade vanilla pudding is a comforting treat on a cold evening.
She made vanilla pudding for dessert and served it with fresh berries.
You can use vanilla pudding as a filling for cakes or pastries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chocolate pudding - sweet chocolate-flavored custard-like pudding
  • synonym: custard - a dessert made with milk and eggs thickened by heat
