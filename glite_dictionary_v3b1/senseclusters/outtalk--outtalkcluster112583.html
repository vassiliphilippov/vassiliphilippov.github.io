outtalk

verb
to talk over
Pronunciation: / ˌaʊtˈtɔk / (AmE), / ˌaʊtˈtɔːk / (BrE)
to talk more, longer, or more persistently than someone else, especially to win an argument or prevent them from being heard

No matter how hard I tried, I could not outtalk my older sister.
