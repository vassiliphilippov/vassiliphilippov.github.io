outtalk

verb
to talk over
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈaʊt.tɑːk/ /; / /ˈaʊt.tɔːk/ (AmE), /ˈaʊt.tɔːk/ (BrE)
to talk more, longer, or more persistently than someone else, especially to win an argument or prevent them from being heard

  • synonym: outargue - to defeat someone in an argument
