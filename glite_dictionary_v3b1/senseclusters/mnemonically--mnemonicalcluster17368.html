mnemonically

adverb
relating to memory
7.9
Pronunciation: / nɪˈmɑːnɪkli / (AmE), / nɪˈmɒnɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or helps with remembering things

Examples

Organize mnemonically
The teacher organized the vocabulary words mnemonically to make them easier for students to recall.
He created a story mnemonically to remember the order of the planets.
The instructions were arranged mnemonically so that everyone could remember the steps.

Related Terms

  • synonym: memorably - in a way that is easy to remember
