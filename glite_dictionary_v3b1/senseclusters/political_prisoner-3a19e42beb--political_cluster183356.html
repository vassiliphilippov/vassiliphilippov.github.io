political prisoner

noun
person jailed for their political beliefs
1.0 3.4
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪdɪkəl ˈprɪzənər / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈprɪzənə / (BrE)
a person who is imprisoned because of their political beliefs or activities, rather than for committing a common crime

Examples
