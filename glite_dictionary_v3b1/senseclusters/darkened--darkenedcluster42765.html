darkened

adjective
made dark or less lit
3.4 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd…ërk…ônd / (AmE), / Ààd…ëÀêk…ônd / (BrE)
(of a place or object) made dark or having less light than usual; not illuminated.

Examples

Darkened room
She sat alone in the darkened room, waiting for the storm to pass.
The darkened hallway made it difficult to see where we were going.
The movie began in a darkened theater as the audience grew quiet.
