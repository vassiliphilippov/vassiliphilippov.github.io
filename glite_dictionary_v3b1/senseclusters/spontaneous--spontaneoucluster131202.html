spontaneous

adjective
happening without external cause or planning
2.9 3.5
Pronunciation: /spɑːn.ˈteɪ.niː.əs/ /; / /spən.ˈteɪ.niː.əs/ (AmE), /spɒn.ˈteɪ.nɪ.əs/ /; / /spən.ˈteɪ.nɪ.əs/ (BrE)
happening or done naturally, without planning or being caused by something outside

Examples

A spontaneous decision
Her spontaneous decision to travel surprised everyone.
The crowd erupted in spontaneous applause after the performance.
The scientist observed a spontaneous chemical reaction in the lab.
He gave a spontaneous speech that moved the entire audience.
Sometimes the best adventures happen when you make a spontaneous decision.
Their laughter was so spontaneous that everyone in the room joined in.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deliberate - done consciously and intentionally
  • synonym: impromptu - done without being planned or rehearsed
  • antonym: planned - deliberately arranged in advance
