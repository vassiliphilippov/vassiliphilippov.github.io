corvina

noun
fish or wine grape
8.6
Pronunciation: / kɔːrˈviːnə / (AmE), / kɔːˈviːnə / (BrE)
a type of fish in the drum family or a grape variety used in Italian wines

Examples

Fresh corvina fillet
The chef prepared grilled corvina with lemon and herbs for the special dinner.
Valpolicella wines are often made from the corvina grape, which gives them a rich flavor.
Fishermen in South America often catch corvina along the coast during the summer months.
