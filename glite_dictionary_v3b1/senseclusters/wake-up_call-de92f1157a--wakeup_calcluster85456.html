wake-up call

noun
telephone call to wake someone up
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪk ˌʌp kɔl / (AmE), / ˈweɪk ˌʌp kɔːl / (BrE)
a telephone call arranged to be received at a specified time in order to wake someone up, typically in a hotel

Examples

Hotel wake-up call
If you need a wake-up call, just let the front desk know.
She forgot to request a wake-up call and almost missed her flight.
The hotel receptionist scheduled a wake-up call for me at 6:30 a.m.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alarm call - a telephone call to wake someone up, especially in hotels
  • synonym: alarm clock - a clock that can be set to sound an alarm at a desired time
