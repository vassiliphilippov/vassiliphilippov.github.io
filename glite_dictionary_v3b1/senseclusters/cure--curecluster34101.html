cure

noun
medicine or treatment that cures
Pronunciation: / kjʊr / (AmE), / kjʊə / (BrE)
A medicine, treatment, or therapy that makes an illness go away or relieves pain.

Examples

A cure for cancer
Many believe that laughter is the best cure for stress.
Scientists are searching for a cure for cancer.
The new cure was approved after rigorous testing.
