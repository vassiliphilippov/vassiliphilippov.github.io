coastside

noun
area near coast
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊstˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈkəʊstˌsaɪd / (BrE)
the land or area located along or near a coast

Examples

The coastside area
They dreamed of living on the coastside to be close to the ocean breeze.
Many tourists visit the coastside during summer for its beautiful beaches.
Wildlife is abundant along the coastside, making it a popular spot for nature lovers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inland - the area away from the coast
  • synonym: seaside - the area by the sea, especially for recreation
