dedicatory

noun
inscription of dedication
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈde.dɪ.kə.tɔːr.iː/ /; / /ˈde.də.kə.tɔːr.iː/ (AmE), /ˈde.dɪ.kə.triː/ /; / /ˈde.dɪ.keɪ.triː/ (BrE)
a short inscription, note, or poem in a book or on an object stating to whom or for what purpose it is dedicated

Examples

A dedicatory in a book
The author wrote a heartfelt dedicatory to his parents at the start of the novel.
She smiled as she read the dedicatory inscribed on the inside cover of the old book.
A brief dedicatory on the statue honored the town's founder.
