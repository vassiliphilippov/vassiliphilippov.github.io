Chinese primrose

noun
an ornamental primrose plant native to China
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʧaɪ.niz ˈprɪmˌroʊz / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪ.niːz ˈprɪm.rəʊz / (BrE)
