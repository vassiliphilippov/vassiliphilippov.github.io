altar girl

noun
girl serving as an acolyte
Pronunciation: / ˈɔltɚ ɡɝl / (AmE), / ˈɔːltə ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a girl who serves as an acolyte assisting the celebrant in a church service

Examples

Altar girl serves Mass
As an altar girl, she helped prepare the sacred vessels before Mass.
During the ceremony, the altar girl carried the processional cross with confidence.
The church recently began allowing both boys and altar girls to serve at the altar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acolyte - a person assisting in religious services, especially in Christian worship
  • synonym: altar boy - a boy who serves as an acolyte in church services
