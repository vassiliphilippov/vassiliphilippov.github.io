fictionist

noun
writer of fiction
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪkʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˈfɪkʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who writes works of fiction, such as novels or short stories

Examples

A fictionist writes novels

Aspiring to become a successful fictionist, he attended several creative writing workshops.
Many readers eagerly await the next novel from their favorite fictionist.
