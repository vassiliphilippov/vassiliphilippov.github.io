cheerful tone

noun
happy, positive way of speaking
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪr.fəl toʊn / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪə.fəl təʊn / (BrE)
a manner of speaking that is happy, positive, and expresses cheerfulness

Examples

A cheerful tone
Even when delivering bad news, she managed to maintain a cheerful tone.
His cheerful tone on the phone immediately made me feel at ease.
The teacher greeted the class in a cheerful tone every morning, setting a positive mood for the day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pleasant tone - a tone of voice that is agreeable or friendly
  • antonym: serious tone - a manner of speaking that is earnest or solemn
