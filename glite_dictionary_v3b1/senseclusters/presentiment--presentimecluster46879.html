presentiment

noun
a foreboding or premonition
Pronunciation: / prɪˈzɛntəmənt / (AmE), / prɪˈzɛntɪmənt / (BrE)
a feeling or intuition that something, especially something unpleasant, is about to happen

Examples

A presentiment of danger
A vague presentiment of danger lingered in his mind as he walked home.
Just before the announcement, a presentiment of trouble swept over the crowd.
She couldn't shake the presentiment that something terrible was about to occur.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foreboding - a fearful apprehension; a feeling that something bad will happen
  • synonym: premonition - a strong feeling that something is about to happen, especially something unpleasant
