mature

verb
cause a sore/wound to ripen and discharge pus
4.7
Pronunciation: / məˈtʃʊr / (AmE), / məˈtjʊə / (BrE)

Examples

To mature a boil

The doctor used a warm compress to help the wound mature more quickly.
