sail

verb
move smoothly and effortlessly
5.4
Pronunciation: / seɪl / (AmE), / seɪl / (BrE)
to move smoothly and effortlessly, as if gliding through air or over a surface

Examples

To sail through the air
A paper airplane can sail smoothly through the classroom if thrown just right.
The dancer seemed to sail across the stage, her movements effortless and graceful.
The eagle opened its wings and began to sail high above the mountain peaks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: float - to move or hover gently and lightly, as if suspended
  • synonym: glide - to move smoothly and quietly, as if with little effort
