smackdown

noun
decisive confrontation
Pronunciation: / ˈsmækˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈsmækˌdaʊn / (BrE)
a decisive defeat or confrontation, especially one that is public or dramatic

Examples

A smackdown victory
The underdog team delivered a surprising smackdown to the reigning champions.
After hours of heated discussion, the meeting ended in a political smackdown.
Fans cheered as the wrestler prepared for the final smackdown of the night.
