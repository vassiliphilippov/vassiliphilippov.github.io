hyperplane

noun
mathematical subspace
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪpərˌpleɪn / (AmE), / ˈhaɪpəˌpleɪn / (BrE)
a flat subspace in mathematics that has one dimension less than the space it is in

Examples

A hyperplane in space
A hyperplane in three-dimensional space is a regular plane.
In machine learning, a hyperplane can separate different classes of data.
The equation defines a hyperplane that divides the space into two halves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plane - a flat, two-dimensional surface in mathematics
  • synonym: subspace - a space that is contained within another space
