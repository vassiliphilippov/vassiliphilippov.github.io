hyperplane

noun
mathematical subspace
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhaɪ.pər.pleɪn/ (AmE), /ˈhaɪ.pə.pleɪn/ (BrE)

Examples

A hyperplane in space
A hyperplane in three-dimensional space is a regular plane.
In machine learning, a hyperplane can separate different classes of data.
The equation defines a hyperplane that divides the space into two halves.
