heartedness

noun
kind or sincere quality
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrtɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being kind, warm, or sincere in attitude or behavior

Examples

Warm heartedness
Her heartedness made everyone feel welcome at the party.
The heartedness of his apology convinced her to forgive him.
People admired the heartedness he showed toward strangers in need.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coldness - lack of warmth or friendliness
  • synonym: sincerity - the quality of being honest and genuine
  • synonym: warmth - friendliness and kindness in manner
