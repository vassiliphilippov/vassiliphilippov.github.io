impinge

verb
go beyond the usual limit
6.9 6.8
Pronunciation: /ɪm.ˈpɪndʒ/ (AmE), /ɪm.ˈpɪndʒ/ (BrE)

Examples

Rising costs could eventually impinge on our plans for expansion.
The new building's design may impinge on the neighboring park's open space.
