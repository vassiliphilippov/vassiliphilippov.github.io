privateersman

noun
crewmember of a privateer
Pronunciation: / ˌpraɪvəˈtɪrzmən / (AmE), / ˌpraɪvəˈtɪəzmən / (BrE)
an officer or crew member of a privateer, especially one who engages in authorized maritime warfare for profit.

Examples

A privateersman at sea
As a young privateersman, he sailed the Atlantic in search of enemy merchant ships.
Many privateersmen operated under letters of marque from their governments.
The career of a privateersman was both lucrative and dangerous during wartime.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corsair - a pirate, especially one operating in the Mediterranean
  • synonym: pirate - a person who attacks and robs ships at sea
