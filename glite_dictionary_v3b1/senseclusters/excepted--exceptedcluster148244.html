excepted

verb
to leave out
4.5
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsɛptɪd / (AmE), / ɪkˈsɛptɪd / (BrE)
not included or left out from a group, rule, or statement

Examples

John excepted students

The museum is open every day, holidays excepted.
Everyone was invited to the dinner, family members excepted.
