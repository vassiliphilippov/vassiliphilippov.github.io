ECB

noun
cricket governing body
Pronunciation: / iː siː biː / (AmE), / iː siː biː / (BrE)
the England and Wales Cricket Board, the organization responsible for governing and promoting cricket in England and Wales

Examples

ECB cricket board
The ECB announced new safety measures for players ahead of the upcoming season.
Many young cricketers dream of being selected by the ECB for the national team.
The ECB is responsible for organizing major cricket tournaments in England and Wales.
