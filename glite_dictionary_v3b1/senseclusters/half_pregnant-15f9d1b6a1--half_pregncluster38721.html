half pregnant

adjective
impossible partial commitment situation
Pronunciation: / hæf ˈprɛɡnənt / (AmE), / hɑːf ˈprɛɡnənt / (BrE)
in a situation where only a full commitment is possible, with no room for partial involvement or indecision

Examples

Be half pregnant
Her boss said the company can't be half pregnant with the merger—they need to make a clear decision.
When it comes to telling the truth, you can't be half pregnant; honesty requires total transparency.
You either support the new policy or you don't; you can't be half pregnant about it.
