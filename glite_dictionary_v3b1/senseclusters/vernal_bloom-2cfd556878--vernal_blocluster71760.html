vernal bloom

noun
flowers appearing in spring
Pronunciation: / ˈvɝː.nəl bluːm / (AmE), / ˈvɜː.nəl bluːm / (BrE)
A mass of flowers that appear in the spring.

Examples

A vernal bloom
Every year, the park is covered in a spectacular vernal bloom.
Photographers flock to the countryside to capture the short-lived vernal bloom.
The vernal bloom brings vivid color to the orchard after a long winter.
