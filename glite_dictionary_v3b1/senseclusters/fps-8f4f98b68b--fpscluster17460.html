FPS

noun
feet per second
Pronunciation: / ɛf pi ɛs / (AmE), / ɛf pi ɛs / (BrE)
a unit of speed equal to one foot traveled in one second, commonly used to measure velocity, especially of projectiles

Examples

Bullet FPS
The bullet's speed was measured at 2700 FPS during the test.
Many air rifles advertise their muzzle velocity in FPS to attract buyers.
To convert FPS to meters per second, multiply the value by 0.3048.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meters per second - a metric unit of speed equal to one meter traveled in one second
  • synonym: miles per hour - a unit of speed measuring miles traveled in one hour
