piriform lobe

noun
pear-shaped brain region in olfactory system
9.8
Pronunciation: /ˈpɪr.ə.fɔːrm loʊb/ /; / /ˈpɪr.ɪ.fɔːrm loʊb/ (AmE), /ˈpɪr.ɪ.fɔːm ləʊb/ /; / /ˈpɪr.ə.fɔːm ləʊb/ (BrE)
