lamboid suture

noun
skull joint between occipital and parietal bones
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlæm.bɔɪd ˈsu.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈlam.bɔɪd ˈsjuː.tʃə / (BrE)
the serrated joint between the occipital and parietal bones of the skull

Examples

The lamboid suture joint

The lamboid suture is easily visible in cranial X-rays of children.
