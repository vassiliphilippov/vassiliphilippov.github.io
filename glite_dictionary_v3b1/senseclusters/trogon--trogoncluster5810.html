trogon

noun
a brightly colored New World forest bird
Pronunciation: / ˈtroʊɡɒn / (AmE), / ˈtrəʊɡɒn / (BrE)
A brightly colored forest bird of tropical regions of the Americas, known for its lustrous plumage and long tail.

A trogon bird
Birdwatchers visit the rainforest hoping to catch a glimpse of a rare trogon.
