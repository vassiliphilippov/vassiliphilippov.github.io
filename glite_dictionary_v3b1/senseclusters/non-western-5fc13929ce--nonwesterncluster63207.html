non-Western

noun
person from outside the West
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈwɛstərn / (AmE), / nɒnˈwɛstən / (BrE)
a person, group, or society from cultures or countries outside the Western world, especially in contrast to Western societies

Examples

Non-Western people
The conference welcomed many non-Western participants to share their unique perspectives.
Some non-Western societies have different approaches to education and family life.
Researchers studied the influence of globalization on non-Western cultures.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Westerner - a person from the Western world
