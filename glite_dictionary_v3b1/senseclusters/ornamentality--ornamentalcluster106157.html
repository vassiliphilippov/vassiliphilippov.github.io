ornamentality

noun
decorative quality
Pronunciation: / ˌɔrnəˌmɛnˈtæləti / (AmE), / ˌɔːnəˌmɛnˈtælɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being decorative or serving as an ornament

Examples

Architectural ornamentality
The ornamentality of the ancient palace was evident in its detailed carvings and gold accents.
Some people prefer minimalism, while others enjoy the ornamentality of richly decorated spaces.
The designer added elements of ornamentality to make the product more visually appealing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decorativeness - the quality of being decorative
  • antonym: plainness - the quality of being simple or not decorated
