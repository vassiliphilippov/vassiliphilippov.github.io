arcade

verb
to add arches
8.6
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈkeɪd / (AmE), / ɑːˈkeɪd / (BrE)
to provide a building or space with an arcade or a series of arches.

Examples

To arcade an entrance
The architects arcaded the entrance to give the building a classical look.
They arcaded the courtyard, creating a shaded walkway for visitors.
After the renovation, the once plain hall was beautifully arcaded with stone arches.
