justice

noun
fairness; quality of being just
1.0 2.2
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒʌ.stɪs/ /; / /ˈdʒʌ.stəs/ (AmE), /ˈdʒʌ.stɪs/ /; / /ˈdʒʌ.stəs/ (BrE)
the quality or principle of being fair and morally right, or the system that decides what is fair (especially in law)
