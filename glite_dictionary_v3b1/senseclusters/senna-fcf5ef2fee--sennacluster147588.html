Senna

noun
genus of shrubs, trees, and herbs (Fabaceae)
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈse.nə/ (AmE), /ˈse.nə/ (BrE)
A genus of shrubs, trees, and herbs in the family Fabaceae, many species of which have previously been classified in the genus Cassia.

Examples

Senna plants
Botanists debated whether certain shrubs belonged to Senna or the genus Cassia.
Many species in the genus Senna are used as medicinal plants in traditional remedies.
The leaves of several Senna plants act as natural laxatives due to their chemical properties.
