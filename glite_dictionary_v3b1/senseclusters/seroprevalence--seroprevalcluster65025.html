seroprevalence

noun
rate of antibodies
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪəroʊˈprɛvələns / (AmE), / ˌsɪərəʊˈprɛvələns / (BrE)
the proportion of individuals in a population who have detectable antibodies to a specific disease in their blood

Examples

The seroprevalence rate
The seroprevalence of the disease increased after the outbreak in the city.
Health officials conducted a study to determine the seroprevalence among healthcare workers.
A high seroprevalence suggests that many people have already been exposed to the virus.

Related Terms

  • antonym: incidence - the rate of new cases of a disease in a population
  • synonym: seropositivity - the presence of specific antibodies in the blood
