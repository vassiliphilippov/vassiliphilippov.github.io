flappable

adjective
easily upset
Pronunciation: / ˈflæpəbəl / (AmE), / ˈflæpəbəl / (BrE)
easily upset, disturbed, or made nervous, especially in stressful situations

Examples

Easily flappable person
He became noticeably flappable when the meeting grew tense and chaotic.
Despite her experience, she was still flappable in high-pressure situations.
The coach prefers players who are calm rather than flappable during important games.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nervous - easily agitated or alarmed
  • antonym: unflappable - not easily upset or disturbed; calm under pressure
