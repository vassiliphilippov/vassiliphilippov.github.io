tulsi

noun
holy basil plant
Pronunciation: / ˈtʊlsi / (AmE), / ˈtʊlsi / (BrE)
a tropical plant, also known as holy basil, whose aromatic leaves are used in cooking, traditional medicine, and Hindu religious rituals
