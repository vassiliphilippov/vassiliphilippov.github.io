general

noun
a general principle or idea
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛnɚəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnərəl / (BrE)
an overall principle, fact, or idea that applies to a whole class or group, rather than to specific details or cases

Examples

A basic general

The doctor explained the general but did not address my individual case.
