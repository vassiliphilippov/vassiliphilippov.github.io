iconicity

noun
quality of resemblance
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪkəˈnɪsəti / (AmE), / ˌaɪkəˈnɪsəti / (BrE)
the quality of resembling or directly representing the thing it stands for, especially in language or symbols

Examples

Symbolic iconicity
The iconicity of the heart symbol makes its meaning easy to understand.
In sign language, high iconicity helps learners remember new signs more easily.
Artists often use iconicity to create images that closely resemble real objects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: representation - the act of depicting or standing for something
  • antonym: symbolism - the use of symbols to represent ideas or qualities
