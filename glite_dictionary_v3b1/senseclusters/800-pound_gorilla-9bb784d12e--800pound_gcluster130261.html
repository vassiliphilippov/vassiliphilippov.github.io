800-pound gorilla

noun
a dominant or powerful entity
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪt ˌhʌn.drəd paʊnd ɡəˈrɪl.ə / (AmE), / ˈeɪt ˌhʌn.drəd paʊnd ɡəˈrɪl.ə / (BrE)
an entity that is so powerful or dominant that it operates with little regard for opposition or constraints, especially in a market or industry

The presence of an 800-pound gorilla can intimidate even established companies in the sector.
