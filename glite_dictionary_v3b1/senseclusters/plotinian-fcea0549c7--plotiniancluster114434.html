Plotinian

adjective
of Plotinus's philosophy
Pronunciation: / ploʊˈtɪniən / (AmE), / plɒˈtɪniən / (BrE)
relating to the ideas or philosophy of Plotinus, a Greek philosopher and founder of Neoplatonism

Examples

Plotinian philosophy
The professor gave a lecture on Plotinian concepts of the soul and the One.
Her research focuses on Plotinian influences in early Christian thought.
Many scholars debate the true meaning of Plotinian mysticism in ancient philosophy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Neoplatonic - relating to Neoplatonism, a philosophical system based on Plato's ideas
