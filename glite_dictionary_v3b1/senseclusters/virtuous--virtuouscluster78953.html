virtuous

adjective
remaining sexually pure (archaic, of a woman)
9.6 0.35/m
Pronunciation: / Ààv…ùÀêt Éu…ôs / (AmE), / Ààv…úÀêt Éu…ôs / (BrE)
(of a woman, sometimes archaic) remaining in a state of sexual virginity; chaste.

Examples

Virtuous maiden
In Victorian novels, a truly virtuous heroine was often expected to remain pure until marriage.
She was praised as a virtuous woman who guarded her chastity above all else.
The story emphasized that only the virtuous maidens were chosen for the sacred ceremony.

Synonyms

  • vestal

  • pure
