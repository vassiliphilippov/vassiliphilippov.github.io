rhyolitic

adjective
containing rhyolite
Pronunciation: / ˌraɪəˈlɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌraɪəˈlɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or containing rhyolite, a light-colored volcanic rock rich in silica

Examples

Rhyolitic lava domes
The region is known for its rhyolitic lava domes and dramatic landscapes.
Scientists collected samples of rhyolitic ash from the ancient volcanic eruption.
The pale color of the rhyolitic rocks made them easy to spot among the darker stones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: andesitic - relating to or containing andesite, a type of volcanic rock
  • antonym: basaltic - relating to or containing basalt, a dark volcanic rock
