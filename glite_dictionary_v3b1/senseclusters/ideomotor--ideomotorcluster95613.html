ideomotor

adjective
involuntary movement
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪdiəˈmoʊtər / (AmE), / ˌaɪdiəˈməʊtə / (BrE)
relating to involuntary physical movements that occur as a result of ideas or thoughts rather than conscious intention

Examples

Ideomotor response
The psychologist studied the ideomotor responses of participants during the experiment.
Some magic tricks rely on the ideomotor effect to make objects appear to move on their own.
The patient's ideomotor actions were triggered by simple suggestions from the therapist.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reflex - an automatic physical response to a stimulus
