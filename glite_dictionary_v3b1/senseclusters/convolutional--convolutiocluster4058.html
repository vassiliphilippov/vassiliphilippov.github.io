convolutional

adjective
having folds or ridges
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑnvəˈluːʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌkɒnvəˈluːʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or having convolutions, especially referring to the folds or ridges of an organ such as the brain.
