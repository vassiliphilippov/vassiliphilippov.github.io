telescreen

noun
surveillance display device
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛləˌskriːn / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌskriːn / (BrE)
a screen that both displays images and monitors people, especially for surveillance purposes

Examples

Surveillance telescreen
The telescreen in the office could not be turned off, ensuring constant surveillance.
Citizens were always aware that the telescreen might be watching and listening to them.
He glanced nervously at the telescreen before speaking, afraid of being overheard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: monitor - a device used to observe or check activities
  • synonym: surveillance camera - a camera used to observe people for security
