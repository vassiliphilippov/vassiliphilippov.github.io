superbomb

noun
very powerful bomb
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌbɑm / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəbɒm / (BrE)
an extremely powerful bomb, especially one using nuclear fusion such as a hydrogen bomb

Examples

A superbomb explosion
The invention of the superbomb marked a turning point in the arms race.
Scientists debated the ethical implications of building a superbomb during the Cold War.
A single superbomb has the potential to destroy an entire city.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: atomic bomb - a bomb that uses nuclear fission to release energy
  • synonym: hydrogen bomb - a nuclear bomb using fusion to release energy
