splashy

adjective
having bright, irregularly colored patches
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsplæʃi / (AmE), / ˈsplæʃi / (BrE)
(of a surface or object) covered with patches of bright color, often in a striking or irregular manner.

Examples

Splashy colors
She chose a splashy dress decorated in bright, contrasting patches.
The artist created a splashy mural with bold, irregular swaths of color.
Their new living room features splashy cushions that brighten the entire space.

Related Terms
