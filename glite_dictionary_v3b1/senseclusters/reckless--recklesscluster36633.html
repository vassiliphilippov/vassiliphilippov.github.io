Examples

A reckless decision
He made a reckless decision to invest all his money without proper research.
Her reckless behavior worried everyone who cared about her safety.
The driver was fined for his reckless disregard for the speed limits.

Synonyms

  • rash
    4.3 0.09/m
    rash describes acting without careful thought and is often impulsive or quick, while reckless ignores danger and possible consequences.
  • foolhardy
    7.4 1.04/m
    foolhardy is boldly taking big risks to show bravery, while reckless is acting without concern for danger or consequences.
  • impetuous
    8.0 0.68/m
    impetuous acts suddenly from strong feeling or impulse, while reckless ignores danger and likely harm when acting.
  • incautious
    6.7 0.25/m
    incautious is not careful about risks or danger in choice or action, while reckless shows a total lack of concern for consequences.
  • heedless
    7.3 0.71/m
    heedless pays no attention to warnings or signs, while reckless goes further and ignores danger and possible bad results.
  • irresponsible
    3.2 9.39/m
    irresponsible fails to think about duties or the effects on others, while reckless ignores danger and likely harmful outcomes.
