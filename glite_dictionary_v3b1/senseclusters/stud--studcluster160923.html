stud

noun
a poker game variant with face-up cards
Pronunciation: / stʌd / (AmE), / stʌd / (BrE)
A type of poker in which each player receives some cards face down and the remaining cards are dealt face up, with betting after each card is dealt.

Examples

Stud poker game
He prefers stud because of its unique betting structure and open cards.
Many casinos offer stud as one of their primary poker games.
We played a few rounds of stud poker at the club last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: draw poker - poker game where players may replace cards in their hand
  • synonym: hold'em - poker variant where players use community cards with two private cards
