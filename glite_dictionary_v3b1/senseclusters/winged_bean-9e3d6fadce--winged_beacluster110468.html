winged bean

noun
a tropical twining plant with winged pods
8.9 5.5
Pronunciation: /wɪŋd biːn/ /; / /ˈwɪŋ.gɪd biːn/ /; / /wɪndʒd biːn/ (AmE), /wɪŋd biːn/ /; / /ˈwɪŋ.gɪd biːn/ /; / /wɪndʒd biːn/ (BrE)
a tropical climbing plant producing edible pods with four wing-like ridges and tuberous roots, cultivated in the Old World tropics
