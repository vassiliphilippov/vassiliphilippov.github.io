in writing

adverb
in written or printed form
5.6
Pronunciation: / ɪn ˈraɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪn ˈraɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
expressed or recorded in written or printed form, rather than spoken

Examples

Agreed in writing
He promised verbally, but nothing has been confirmed in writing yet.
Her resignation was not official until it was received in writing.
Please ensure that any complaints are submitted in writing.
