obloquy

noun
strong public criticism or verbal abuse
6.7 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑː.blə.kwi /; / ˈɑːbləˌkwi / (AmE), / ˈɒb.lə.kwi /; / ˈɒbləkwɪ / (BrE)
strong public criticism or verbal abuse that causes disgrace or shame

Examples

The scientist faced obloquy when her controversial findings were released to the public.
