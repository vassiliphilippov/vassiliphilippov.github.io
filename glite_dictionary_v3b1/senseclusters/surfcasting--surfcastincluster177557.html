surfcasting

noun
shore fishing method
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜrfˌkæstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɜːfˌkɑːstɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of fishing by casting a line from the shore into the sea, usually using specialized equipment

Examples

Morning surfcasting trip
Many enthusiasts enjoy surfcasting at sunrise when the fish are most active near the shore.
He bought a new rod specifically designed for surfcasting on the Atlantic coast.
During their vacation, the family tried surfcasting and caught several large fish from the beach.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angling - the sport or pastime of fishing with a rod and line
  • antonym: fly fishing - a method of fishing using artificial flies as bait
