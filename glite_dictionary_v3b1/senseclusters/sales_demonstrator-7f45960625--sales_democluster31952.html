sales demonstrator

noun
person who demonstrates products for sales
1.4 2.9
Pronunciation: /seɪlz ˈde.mən.streɪ.tər/ (AmE), /seɪlz ˈde.mən.streɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a person whose job is to show and explain the features of a product to potential customers, usually to encourage them to buy it

Examples

Sales demonstrator shows products
As a sales demonstrator, she travels to different stores every week to present kitchen appliances.
The sales demonstrator skillfully showed how the new vacuum cleaner worked to the curious crowd.
The company hired a sales demonstrator to boost their presence at the trade fair.
