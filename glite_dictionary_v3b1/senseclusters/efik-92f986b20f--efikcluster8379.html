Efik

proper noun
Nigerian ethnic group
Pronunciation: / ˈɛfɪk / (AmE), / ˈɛfɪk / (BrE)
a member of an ethnic group from southeastern Nigeria or the language spoken by this group

Examples

An Efik person
The Efik are known for their elaborate traditional ceremonies and cuisine.

Many Efik people live in the Cross River State of Nigeria.
