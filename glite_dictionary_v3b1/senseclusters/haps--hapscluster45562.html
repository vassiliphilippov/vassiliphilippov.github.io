haps

noun
chance events
Pronunciation: / hæps / (AmE), / hæps / (BrE)
events or occurrences that happen by chance or luck, often regarded as fate (archaic or literary)

Examples

Strange haps of fate
They accepted the sudden change as mere haps of fate rather than deliberate design.
In old tales, heroes often trust in haps to guide their journeys.
The poet wrote about the haps and misfortunes that shaped his life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: design - a plan or intention behind events
  • synonym: fate - the development of events beyond a person's control
  • synonym: fortune - chance or luck, especially as affecting someone's life
