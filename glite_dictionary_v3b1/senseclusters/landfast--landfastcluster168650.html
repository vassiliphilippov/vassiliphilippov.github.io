landfast

adjective
attached to land
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlændˌfæst / (AmE), / ˈlændˌfɑːst / (BrE)
attached or connected to the land, especially describing sea ice that is held in place along a coastline

Examples

Landfast ice
The landfast ice stretched for miles along the northern shore, forming a solid barrier.
Researchers set up their equipment on the stable landfast ice near the coast.
Unlike drifting ice, landfast ice remains fixed in place throughout the winter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: drift ice - sea ice that floats freely and is not attached to land
