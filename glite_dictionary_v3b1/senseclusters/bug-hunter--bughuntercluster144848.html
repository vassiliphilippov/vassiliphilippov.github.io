bug-hunter

noun
software vulnerability finder
Pronunciation: / \u02c8b\u028c\u0261 \u02cch\u028cnt\u0259r / (AmE), / \u02c8b\u028c\u0261 \u02cch\u028cnt\u0259 / (BrE)
(in computing and cybersecurity) a person who searches for and reports software bugs or security vulnerabilities, often participating in bug-bounty programs.

Examples

Bug-hunter reports bugs
A renowned bug-hunter helped the company fix critical vulnerabilities during the external security audit.
Several bug-hunters reported the memory leak on the forum, earning praise from the developer team.
She earned a reputation as a meticulous bug-hunter after reporting dozens of serious vulnerabilities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bug bounty hunter - a person who finds and reports vulnerabilities to companies in return for rewards
  • synonym: security researcher - a specialist who investigates and analyses security vulnerabilities in software and systems
