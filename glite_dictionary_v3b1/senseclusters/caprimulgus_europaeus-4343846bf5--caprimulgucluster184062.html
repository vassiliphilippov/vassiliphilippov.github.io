Caprimulgus europaeus

noun
species of nocturnal bird (European nightjar)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkæprɪˈmʌlɡəs juːˌroʊˈpiːəs / (AmE), / ˌkæprɪˈmʌlɡəs juːˌrəʊˈpiːəs / (BrE)
The scientific name for the European nightjar, a nocturnal bird found across Europe and western Asia, also known as the Old World goatsucker.
