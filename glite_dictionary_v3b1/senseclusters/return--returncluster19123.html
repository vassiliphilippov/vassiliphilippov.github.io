Synonyms

  • go back
    1.0 120/m
    go back is the everyday phrase for moving or changing to an earlier place or state, while return is neutral and slightly more formal.
  • come back
    1.3 102/m
    come back often shows movement toward the speaker or a restored condition, while return is more formal and neutral.
  • revert
    4.2 5.85/m
    revert describes going back to an earlier state or method, often in formal or technical contexts, while return is more general.
  • resume
    3.1 17/m
    resume means start an activity again after a pause, while return covers coming back to places, states, or activities.
  • reenter
    3.2 1.23/m
    reenter means go into a place or situation again from outside, while return can mean coming back more generally.
  • turn back
    2.1 12/m
    turn back means change direction and go the other way, often suddenly, while return is simply coming back.

Related Terms

  • antonym: vanish - to disappear quickly and completely
