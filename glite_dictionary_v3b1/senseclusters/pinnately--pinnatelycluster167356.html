pinnately

adverb
in a feather-like arrangement
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪn.ət.li / (AmE), / ˈpɪn.ət.li / (BrE)
In a manner resembling a feather, especially with parts arranged on either side of a common axis, as in certain leaves.

Examples

Leaves pinnately arranged
Acacia trees often have pinnately compound leaves.
Many ferns grow with their fronds divided pinnately from a central stalk.
The rose's leaves are arranged pinnately along the stem.
