convo

noun
informal conversation
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑnvoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɒnvəʊ / (BrE)
an informal or casual conversation

Examples

A quick convo
We had a quick convo before the meeting started.
She called her friend for a late-night convo about their weekend plans.
After the game, the players enjoyed a relaxed convo in the locker room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: argument - a heated or angry exchange of views
  • synonym: chat - an informal talk or conversation
  • synonym: discussion - the act of talking about something with others
