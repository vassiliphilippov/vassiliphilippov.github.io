boll weevil

noun
pest beetle damaging cotton crops

Pronunciation: /boʊl ˈwiː.vəl/ /; / /boʊl ˈwiː.vɪl/ (AmE), /bɒl ˈwiː.vəl/ /; / /bəʊl ˈwiː.vəl/ (BrE)
