presswire

noun
newswire dispatch
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛsˌwaɪɚ /; / ˈprɛsˌwaɪər / (AmE), / ˈprɛsˌwaɪə / (BrE)
a service that distributes news reports or announcements to media outlets, or a news report sent by such a service

Examples

A presswire service
The breaking news was first shared through a presswire before appearing on television.
Journalists rely on the presswire for up-to-date information on global events.
A presswire provided details about the government’s new policy early this morning.
The company sent a presswire to announce its new product launch to journalists.
Many organizations use a presswire to quickly distribute important news to the media.
After the event, a detailed presswire was shared with all major news outlets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: newswire - a service that supplies news reports to media organizations
  • synonym: press release - an official statement issued to media giving information on a particular matter
