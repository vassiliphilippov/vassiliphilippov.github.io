chappal

noun
South Asian sandal
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌpəl / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌpəl / (BrE)
a simple, flat sandal, usually made of leather, commonly worn in South Asia

Examples

A leather chappal
He left his chappal at the door before entering the house.
During the hot summer, she prefers wearing chappals instead of closed shoes.
The market was full of colorful chappals made by local artisans.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shoe - a covering for the foot, typically with a sturdy sole
