throwout

noun
baseball out by throw
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈθroʊˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊˌaʊt / (BrE)
a play in baseball or softball where a fielder throws the ball to get a runner out.

Examples

A throwout at second
The catcher's quick throwout at second base stopped the runner from stealing.
A perfect throwout by the right fielder ended the inning.
Fans cheered after the shortstop made a difficult throwout to first base.

Related Terms

  • synonym: putout - an action that results in a player being out in baseball
  • antonym: strikeout - an out in baseball caused by three strikes
