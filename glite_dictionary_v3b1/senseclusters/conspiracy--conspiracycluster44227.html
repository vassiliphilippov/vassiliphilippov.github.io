Synonyms

  • plot
    4.3 9.63/m
    plot is a secret plan by a group to do harm or illegal acts, while conspiracy is the same idea for organised wrongdoing.
  • machination
    9.3 1.49/m
    machination is a secret, often clever plan aimed at harm or gain, while conspiracy is a wider organised plot by several people.
  • collusion
    8.4 7.67/m
    collusion is secret cooperation to cheat or deceive others for benefit, while conspiracy covers any secret plan to commit harm.
  • confederacy
    8.9 0.12/m
    confederacy is a secret agreement or group formed to act against others, while conspiracy names the secret plan itself.
  • contrivance
    8.1 0.15/m
    contrivance is a clever or artificial plan often used to solve problems or deceive, while conspiracy implies illegal secret plotting.
