licenced

adjective
officially authorized; having a licence
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪsənst / (AmE), / ˈlaɪsənst / (BrE)
Having official permission or authorization to do something, especially by a recognized authority.

Examples

A licenced electrician
Only a licenced electrician is allowed to carry out this repair work.
They hired a licenced surveyor to examine the property boundaries.
You should always consult a licenced nutritionist before starting a new diet regimen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authorized - officially approved or permitted
  • synonym: certified - formally recognized as meeting certain standards
  • antonym: unlicensed - not officially authorized or permitted
