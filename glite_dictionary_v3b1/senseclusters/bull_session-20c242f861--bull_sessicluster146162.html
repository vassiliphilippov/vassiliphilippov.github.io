bull session

noun
informal group discussion
5.9
Pronunciation: / bʊl ˈsɛʃən / (AmE), / bʊl ˈsɛʃən /; / bʊl ˈsɛʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
an informal group conversation or discussion, often without a set topic or agenda

Examples

A bull session conversation
Every Friday, the friends would gather for a bull session at the local diner.
The team's late-night bull session turned into a serious planning meeting.
We spent hours in a bull session talking about life after graduation.
After dinner, we sat around the table for a lively bull session about our weekend plans.
Late at night, college students often have a spontaneous bull session in the dorm lounge.
The manager called a quick bull session to gather everyone's input before making a decision.
