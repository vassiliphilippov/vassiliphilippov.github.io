smallmouthed black bass

noun
freshwater fish of the bass family with a small mouth
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɔlˌmaʊθt blæk bæs / (AmE), / ˈsmɔːlˌmaʊθt blæk bæs / (BrE)
A North American freshwater fish (Micropterus dolomieu) of the sunfish family, distinguished by its relatively small mouth and jaw that falls below the eye.

Examples

The smallmouthed black bass is popular among anglers for its fighting spirit and agility.
Unlike the largemouth, the smallmouthed black bass prefers cooler, clearer waters with plenty of cover.
We caught a large smallmouthed black bass near the rocky shoreline yesterday morning.
