hemispherically

adverb
relating to a hemisphere
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛmɪˈsfɛrɪkli / (AmE), / ˌhɛmɪˈsfɛrɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or affects one hemisphere, especially of the brain.

Examples

Hemispherically affected brain
The brain injury affected her language skills hemispherically, with changes mostly on the left side.
Researchers observed that memory functions are often organized hemispherically in the brain.
After the operation, movement returned hemispherically, first on one side and then the other.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laterally - in a way that relates to the side or sides
