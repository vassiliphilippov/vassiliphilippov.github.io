palace car

noun
luxurious railway passenger car
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpælɪs kɑr / (AmE), / ˈpælɪs kɑː / (BrE)
a luxurious passenger railway car with individual seats, used for daytime travel and requiring an extra fare

Examples

A palace car ticket
During the 19th century, a palace car was a symbol of luxury on long train rides.
He paid extra for an individual chair in the palace car, enjoying the elegant surroundings.
The wealthy travelers reserved a seat in the palace car for added comfort on their journey.
