epiphysial

adjective
relating to the epiphysis of a bone
Pronunciation: / ɪˌpɪˈfɪziəl / (AmE), / ɪˌpɪˈfɪziəl / (BrE)
relating to the epiphysis, the end part of a long bone that develops separately from the shaft

Examples

Epiphysial plate
A fracture involving the epiphysial plate can affect bone growth in children.
Researchers studied the effect of hormones on epiphysial cartilage development.
The epiphysial line is visible on X-rays until full skeletal maturity is reached.
