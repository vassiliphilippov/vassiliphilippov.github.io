recording

noun
device with stored sounds or images
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɔrdɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈkɔːdɪŋ / (BrE)
something (such as a file, tape, or signal) on which sounds or images have been captured and can be played back

Examples

Audio recording
Police reviewed the video recording for evidence.
She listened to an old jazz recording from the 1950s.
The recording of their wedding ceremony was stored on a DVD.
I have a recording of the concert we attended last year.
She deleted the recording from her phone to free up space.
The police listened carefully to the recording of the suspect’s phone call.

Related Terms

  • synonym: album - collection of recorded music tracks released together
  • synonym: record - to capture and store (sounds, images, data, etc.)
  • synonym: tape - magnetic recording medium for audio or video
