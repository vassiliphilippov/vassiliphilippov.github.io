exotic

adjective
from a foreign country; strikingly unusual
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɑt̬ɪk /; / ɪɡˈzɑː.tɪk / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɒtɪk /; / ɪɡˈzɒ.tɪk / (BrE)
unusual, interesting, or attractive because it comes from a foreign country or is very different from what is familiar

Examples

An exotic animal
His apartment is decorated with many exotic artifacts collected during his travels.
She prepared an exotic dish using rare spices.
The garden was filled with exotic flowers from South America.
He loves traveling to exotic locations that are off the beaten path.
Her collection includes several exotic pieces of jewelry from Asia and Africa.
The garden was filled with exotic plants brought from distant countries.
