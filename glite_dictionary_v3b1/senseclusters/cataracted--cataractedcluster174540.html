cataracted

adjective
clouded by cataract
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkætəˌræktɪd / (AmE), / ˈkætəræktɪd / (BrE)
having a cloudy or opaque lens in the eye that impairs vision, as caused by a cataract

Examples

A cataracted eye
The surgeon examined the patient's cataracted eye before recommending treatment.
Many elderly people develop cataracted lenses that affect their ability to see clearly.
After years of vision problems, her cataracted eyes were finally restored through surgery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - easy to see through; not cloudy
  • synonym: cloudy - not clear or transparent
