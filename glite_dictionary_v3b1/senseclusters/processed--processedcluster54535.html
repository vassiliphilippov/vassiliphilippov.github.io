processed

adjective
prepared or altered from natural state
1.0 2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑː.sɛst / (AmE), / ˈprɒ.sɛst /; / ˈprəʊ.sɛst / (BrE)
changed from its natural state, usually by chemicals or machines, to preserve it or make it ready for use
