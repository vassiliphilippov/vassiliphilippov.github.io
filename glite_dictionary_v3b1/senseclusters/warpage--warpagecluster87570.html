warpage

noun
bending or distortion
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔːrpɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːpɪdʒ / (BrE)
the process or result of something becoming bent, twisted, or distorted out of its original shape

Examples

Plastic warpage
Excessive heat can cause warpage of plastic parts.
The wooden door suffered warpage after years of exposure to moisture.
Engineers tested the metal for warpage before using it in construction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deformation - the act of changing shape, especially by bending or twisting
  • synonym: distortion - the state of being twisted or altered from the original form
  • antonym: straightening - the act of making something straight again
