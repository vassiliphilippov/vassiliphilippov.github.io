warpage

noun
bending or distortion

the process or result of something becoming bent, twisted, or distorted out of its original shape

Examples

Plastic warpage
Excessive heat can cause warpage of plastic parts.
The wooden door suffered warpage after years of exposure to moisture.
Engineers tested the metal for warpage before using it in construction.

Synonyms

  • warp
    6.3 1.36/m
    warp is a sudden twist or bend in an object's shape, while warpage is the full process or result of that bending.
  • deformation
    5.9 1.85/m
    deformation is a change in shape that often damages material, while warpage refers specifically to bending or twisting out of original shape.
  • distortion
    5.0 2.40/m
    distortion is a change that alters appearance or accuracy, often in images or sound, while warpage is physical bending of objects.
