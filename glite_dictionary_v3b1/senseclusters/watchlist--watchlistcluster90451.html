watchlist

noun
list of media to watch
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑtʃˌlɪst / (AmE), / ˈwɒtʃˌlɪst / (BrE)
a personal list of films, TV shows, or other media that someone plans to watch in the future

Examples

Her watchlist is full of movies she wants to see over the weekend.
Before traveling, he updated his watchlist with several popular TV series.
