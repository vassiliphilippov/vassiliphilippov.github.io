splashback

noun
liquid rebounding
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsplæʃˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈsplæʃˌbæk / (BrE)
a quantity of liquid that is thrown or rebounds back onto a surface or person after hitting something

Examples

Water splashback
The chef installed a panel to prevent splashback from the sink while washing dishes.
She got splashback on her clothes after pouring water into the hot pan too quickly.
A sudden splashback from the puddle soaked his shoes as the car drove by.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spatter - small drops of liquid scattered or splashed
  • synonym: spray - liquid sent out in tiny drops
