fatten

verb
make or become fatter or plumper
Pronunciation: / ˈfætən / (AmE), / ˈfætən / (BrE)
to make or become fatter or plumper, especially by feeding well

Examples

To fatten cattle
Farmers often fatten their cattle before selling them at the market.
He tried to fatten the puppy with hearty meals after it was rescued.
The chef uses butter to fatten up the sauce for added richness.
