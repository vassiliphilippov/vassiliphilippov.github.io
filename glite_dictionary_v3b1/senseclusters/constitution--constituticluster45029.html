constitution

noun
composition or structure
3.9 2.88/m
Pronunciation: / Àåk…ën.st…™Ààtu. É…ôn / (AmE), / Àåk…ín.st…™ÀàtjuÀê. É…ôn / (BrE)
The way in which something is made up or arranged; the composition or structure of something.

Examples

Strong constitution
Her tough physical constitution enabled her to complete the marathon.
The chemical constitution of the alloy makes it unusually strong.
The soil's constitution affects which plants can thrive in the area.

Synonyms

  • composition
    4.1 22/m
    composition describes how parts are arranged or combined to make a whole, while constitution refers to the thing's built-in structure or order.
  • makeup
    2.8 7.00/m
    makeup names the parts or qualities that form a person or thing in everyday speech, while constitution means the formal overall structure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: composition - the makeup or constitution of something
  • synonym: structure - the arrangement or organization of parts to form an entity
