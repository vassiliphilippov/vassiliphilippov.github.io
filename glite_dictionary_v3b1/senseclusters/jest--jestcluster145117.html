jest

noun
playful act or joke
7.9
Pronunciation: / dʒɛst / (AmE), / dʒɛst / (BrE)
a joke, humorous remark, or playful act

Examples

A funny jest
His comment was made in jest, not to be taken seriously.
She played her prank in harmless jest, hoping to make everyone laugh.
The friends exchanged stories and jest throughout the evening.
Although it was meant as a jest, his comment offended some of the guests.
He made a lighthearted jest during the meeting to ease the tension.
Her playful jest brought smiles to everyone around the dinner table.

Related Terms

  • antonym: earnest - seriousness or sincerity of manner or attitude
  • synonym: gag - a joke or an amusing story, especially one told by a comedian
  • synonym: joke - something said or done to provoke laughter
  • synonym: prank - a playful trick or practical joke
  • synonym: quip - a witty or clever remark
  • antonym: seriousness - the quality or state of being serious, not joking or jesting
