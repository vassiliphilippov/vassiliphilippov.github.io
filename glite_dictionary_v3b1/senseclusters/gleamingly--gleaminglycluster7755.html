gleamingly

adverb
in a splendid manner
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡliːmɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈɡliːmɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shines brightly or impresses with brightness (either literally or figuratively)

Examples

Shines gleamingly bright
The new building stood gleamingly in the sunlight, attracting everyone's attention.
She gleamingly accepted the award, her smile lighting up the stage.
The team performed gleamingly in the finals, impressing both fans and judges.
The silverware was arranged gleamingly on the table for the special dinner.
Sunlight poured through the window, making the tiles shine gleamingly in the kitchen.
Her eyes looked gleamingly excited as she opened the gift.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brilliantly - in a very impressive or skillful way
  • antonym: dully - in a way that lacks brightness or liveliness
