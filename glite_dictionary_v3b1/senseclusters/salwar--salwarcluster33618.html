salwar

noun
loose-fitting trousers worn in South Asia
8.5 7.1
Pronunciation: / sɑːlˈwɑːr / (AmE), / sælˈwɑː / (BrE)
a pair of light, loose-fitting trousers that are tight at the ankles, traditionally worn by women in South Asia, typically with a kameez.
