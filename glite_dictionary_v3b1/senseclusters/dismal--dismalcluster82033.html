dismal

adjective
depressing; causing dejection
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪzməl / (AmE), / ˈdɪzməl / (BrE)
causing a feeling of sadness or hopelessness; depressing or gloomy

Examples

Dismal weather
Her grades were so dismal that she considered changing her major.
The dismal weather ruined our plans for a picnic.
The abandoned house had a dismal atmosphere that made everyone uneasy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerful - noticeably happy and optimistic
  • synonym: gloomy - dark or poorly lit and likely to cause a feeling of sadness
