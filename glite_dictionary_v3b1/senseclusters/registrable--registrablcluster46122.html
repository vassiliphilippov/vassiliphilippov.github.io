registrable

adjective
able to be registered
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdʒɪstrəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈrɛdʒɪstrəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be officially recorded or entered in a register, especially according to legal requirements

Examples

A registrable trademark
Only registrable trademarks can be protected under the law.
The property is not registrable because it does not meet the legal requirements.
Applicants must ensure their invention is registrable before submitting it to the patent office.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recordable - able to be officially recorded
  • antonym: unregistrable - not able to be officially registered
