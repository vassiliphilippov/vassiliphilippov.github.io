amrita

noun
Ayurvedic medicinal plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɑmˈriːtə / (AmE), / ʌmˈriːtə / (BrE)
a medicinal climbing shrub (Tinospora cordifolia) used in traditional Ayurvedic medicine for its health benefits
