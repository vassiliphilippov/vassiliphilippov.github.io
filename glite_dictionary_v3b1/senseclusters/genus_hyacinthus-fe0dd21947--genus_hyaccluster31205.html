genus Hyacinthus

noun
genus of flowering plants (hyacinths)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs haɪˈæsɪnθəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs hʌɪˈasɪnθəs / (BrE)
A genus of bulbous flowering plants, often placed in the family Hyacinthaceae, known for the hyacinth flowers.
