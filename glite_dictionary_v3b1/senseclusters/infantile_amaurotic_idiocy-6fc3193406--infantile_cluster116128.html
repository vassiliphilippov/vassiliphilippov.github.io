infantile amaurotic idiocy

noun
hereditary metabolic disorder (Tay-Sachs disease)
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfæntaɪl əˌmjʊrˈɑtɪk ˌɪdiˈɑsi / (AmE), / ɪnˈfæntaɪl əˌmjʊəˈrɒtɪk ˌɪdiˈɒsi / (BrE)
a hereditary disorder of lipid metabolism, most common among Jews of eastern European descent, where lipid accumulation in the nervous tissue leads to early childhood death

Examples

Infantile amaurotic idiocy disease
Infantile amaurotic idiocy is a devastating disease that leads to progressive loss of mental and physical abilities in children.
Researchers are studying the genetic mutations responsible for infantile amaurotic idiocy to better understand its progression.
The family received a heartbreaking diagnosis of infantile amaurotic idiocy in their youngest child.
