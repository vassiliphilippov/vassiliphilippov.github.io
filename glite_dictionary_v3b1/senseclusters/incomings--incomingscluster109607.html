incomings

noun
received money or items
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnkʌmɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈɪnkʌmɪŋz / (BrE)
money or items that are received, especially as income or arrivals

Examples

Monthly incomings
The accountant recorded all incomings and outgoings for the month.
Their incomings increased after they launched the new product line.
You should keep track of your incomings to manage your budget effectively.

Related Terms

  • synonym: income - money received, especially on a regular basis
  • antonym: outgoings - money that is spent or paid out
  • synonym: revenue - total money received by a business or organization
