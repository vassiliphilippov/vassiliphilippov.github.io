vestibulospinal

adjective
nerve pathway for balance
9.9
Pronunciation: / vɛˌstɪbjəloʊˈspaɪnəl / (AmE), / vɛˌstɪbjʊləʊˈspaɪnəl / (BrE)
relating to nerve pathways that connect the vestibular system to the spinal cord, especially in controlling balance and posture

Examples

Vestibulospinal pathways
The vestibulospinal tract plays a crucial role in helping the body maintain balance.
Damage to vestibulospinal pathways can result in problems with posture and coordination.
Researchers studied the effects of exercise on vestibulospinal function in older adults.
