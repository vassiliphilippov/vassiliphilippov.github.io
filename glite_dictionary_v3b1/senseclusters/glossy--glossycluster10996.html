adjective

Examples

Glossy paper finish
She prefers photos developed on a glossy finish for their vibrant look.
The glossy leather handbag caught the sunlight as she walked.
The magazine was printed on expensive glossy paper that made the photos stand out.
After washing, her hair looked especially glossy in the sunlight.
The magazine cover had a glossy finish that made the colors stand out.
The showroom floor was so glossy that you could almost see your reflection in it.

Synonyms

  • lustrous
    5.3 0.85/m

  • glassy
    3.9 1.24/m

  • polished
    2.1 3.19/m

  • shining
    1.0 0.42/m

  • burnished
