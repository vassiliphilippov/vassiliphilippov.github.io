murderous look

noun
fierce or violent expression
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɝːdərəs lʊk / (AmE), / ˈmɜːdərəs lʊk / (BrE)
a very fierce or violent expression, as if capable of murder

Examples

A murderous look of anger
He gave me a murderous look when I touched his phone.
She fixed him with a murderous look that made him stop talking immediately.
The coach shot a murderous look at the referee after the controversial call.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: stare - a fixed look with eyes open wide
