Negara Brunei Darussalam

proper noun

Pronunciation: / nəˈɡɑːrə bruˈnaɪ dəˌruːsɑːˈlɑːm / (AmE), / nəˈɡɑːrə bruˈnaɪ dəˌrʊːsɑːˈlɑːm / (BrE)
the official name for Brunei, a sultanate country located on the northwestern coast of Borneo, which gained independence from Great Britain in 1984
