clothes pin

noun
fastener for attaching clothes to a clothesline
1.5 1.3
Pronunciation: /kloʊðz pɪn/ /; / /kloʊz pɪn/ (AmE), /kləʊðz pɪn/ /; / /kləʊz pɪn/ (BrE)
a small clip, typically made of wood or plastic, used to attach clothes to a clothesline while drying

Examples
