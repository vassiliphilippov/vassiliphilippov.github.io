clootie

noun
Scottish boiled pudding
Pronunciation: / ˈkluːti / (AmE), / ˈkluːti / (BrE)
a traditional Scottish pudding made with dried fruit and spices, boiled in a cloth, or the cloth itself

Examples

A clootie pudding
For Hogmanay, her grandmother always made a delicious clootie filled with raisins and spices.
The recipe calls for the pudding mixture to be wrapped in a clootie before boiling.
At the Scottish festival, visitors could sample a traditional clootie alongside other regional dishes.

  • synonym: pudding - a sweet or savory dish, often steamed or boiled
