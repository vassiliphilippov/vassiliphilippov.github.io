hot seat

noun
position of intense scrutiny or pressure
7.3 3.7
Pronunciation: / hɑt sit /; / ˈhɑt siːt / (AmE), / hɒt siːt /; / ˈhɒt siːt / (BrE)
a position where someone is under intense pressure, scrutiny, or criticism, especially because of responsibility

Examples
