capping machine

noun
device for capping bottles
Pronunciation: / ˈkæpɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈkæpɪŋ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
A manual or automatic device used to seal bottles with caps.

Examples

The capping machine seals bottles
After filling, each bottle passes under the capping machine to be securely closed.
The capping machine at the factory can seal thousands of bottles per hour.
They had to repair the capping machine when it started applying the caps unevenly.
