manned

adjective
operated by people; crewed
8.9 3.2
Pronunciation: /mænd/ (AmE), /mænd/ (BrE)
(of a vehicle, craft, or mission) having a crew of people to operate or be present in it.

Examples

A manned space mission
A manned mission to Mars would require careful planning and significant resources.
Before sending manned flights, engineers tested many unmanned rockets.
The International Space Station is a manned spacecraft with astronauts living and working onboard.

  • antonym: unmanned - not having a crew; operated without people
