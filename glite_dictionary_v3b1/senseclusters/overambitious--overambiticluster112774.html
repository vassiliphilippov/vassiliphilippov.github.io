overambitious

adjective
excessively ambitious
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvəræmˈbɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəræmˈbɪʃəs / (BrE)
Having or showing ambitions or goals that are excessively high or unrealistic.

Examples

Overambitious plans
His overambitious approach often leads to disappointment when expectations are not met.
She is sometimes criticized for her overambitious plans that go beyond practical limits.
The project failed because the initial goals were overambitious and unattainable.
