In a way that shows full knowledge, awareness, or intent; deliberately.

Examples

To knowingly hide facts
He knowingly withheld information from the committee.
She knowingly broke the rule, accepting the consequences that might follow.
The company knowingly sold products that did not meet safety standards.

Synonyms

  • wittingly

    wittingly is done with clear awareness and intent, while knowingly focuses on the person's full knowledge of the action.
  • intentionally
    3.1 11/m
    intentionally means acting on purpose by choice, while knowingly highlights that a person had full awareness of what they did.
  • deliberately
    3.7 22/m
    deliberately suggests careful thought and planning, while knowingly means the person had awareness even without long planning.
  • purposely
    3.5 4.57/m
    purposely is used for acts done with a clear aim or plan, while knowingly stresses the actor's awareness.
  • on purpose
    1.6 6.03/m

  • willfully
    6.0 2.65/m
    willfully implies stubborn or defiant intent to break rules, while knowingly simply means the person knew or understood their action.
