aculeus

noun
sharp-pointed plant projection
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈkjuːliəs / (AmE), / əˈkjuːlɪəs / (BrE)
a stiff, sharp-pointed projection or process, especially one occurring on a plant

Examples

Plant aculeus
A plant's aculeus can help deter insects and animals from feeding on it.
Scientists examined the sharp aculeus on the stem of the wild rose.
The cactus is covered in stiff aculei that protect it from herbivores.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spine - a stiff, sharp-pointed structure on a plant or animal
  • synonym: thorn - a stiff, sharp, woody projection from a plant
