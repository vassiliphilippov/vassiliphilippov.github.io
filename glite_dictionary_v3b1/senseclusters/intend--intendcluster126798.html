intend

verb
mean; aim to express
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɛnd / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛnd / (BrE)
to mean or aim to convey or express a particular idea or message

Examples

Intend a meaning
His words intend only kindness, not criticism.
I never intended to imply anything negative with my comments.
What did you intend by that remark?
