whispering dome

noun
dome with special acoustic effects
6.1 8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪ.spə.rɪŋ doʊm/ /; / /ˈhwɪ.spə.rɪŋ doʊm/ (AmE), /ˈwɪ.spə.rɪŋ dəʊm/ /; / /ˈhwɪ.spə.rɪŋ dəʊm/ (BrE)
an architectural space beneath a dome or arch where sounds made at particular points can be heard clearly at other distant points, due to unique acoustic properties

Examples

The whispering dome
Engineers studied the whispering dome to understand its unique sound-carrying properties.
The acoustics of the whispering dome allow two people to hear each other from opposite sides.
