paunchiness

noun
having a protruding belly
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔntʃi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈpɔːntʃɪnɪs / (BrE)
the state or quality of having a noticeably protruding belly

Examples

Noticeable paunchiness
Despite his paunchiness, he moved with surprising agility on the tennis court.
His paunchiness became more pronounced after years of enjoying rich food and little exercise.
The character's paunchiness was exaggerated for comic effect in the film.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obesity - the condition of being very overweight
  • antonym: slenderness - the quality of being thin and graceful
