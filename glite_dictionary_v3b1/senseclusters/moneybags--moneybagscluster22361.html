moneybags

noun
wealthy person
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌniˌbæɡz / (AmE), / ˈmʌniˌbæɡz / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who has a lot of money or is very wealthy

Examples

Rich moneybags at work
After his business took off, everyone started calling him moneybags at the office.
She may seem like a moneybags, but she still lives a simple life.
The new student was quickly nicknamed moneybags because of his expensive clothes and gadgets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: millionaire - a person whose assets are worth at least a million units of currency
  • antonym: pauper - a very poor person
  • synonym: tycoon - a wealthy and powerful businessperson
