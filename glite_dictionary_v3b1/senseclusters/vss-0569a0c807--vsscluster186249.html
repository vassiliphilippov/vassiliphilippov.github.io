VSS

noun
video surveillance system
Pronunciation: / viː ɛs ɛs / (AmE), / viː ɛs ɛs / (BrE)
an arrangement of cameras and monitors used for security and monitoring, known as a video surveillance system

Examples

Building VSS upgrade
The company upgraded its VSS to improve security throughout the building.
A modern VSS can be monitored remotely from a smartphone or computer.
After the incident, they reviewed the footage from the VSS to identify the intruder.
