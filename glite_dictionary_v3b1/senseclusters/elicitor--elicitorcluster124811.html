elicitor

noun
response-triggering agent
8.8
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈlɪ.sɪ.tər/ /; / /ə.ˈlɪ.sɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈlɪ.sɪ.tə/ /; / /ə.ˈlɪ.sɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a substance or agent that causes or triggers a response, especially a defensive reaction in plants or organisms
