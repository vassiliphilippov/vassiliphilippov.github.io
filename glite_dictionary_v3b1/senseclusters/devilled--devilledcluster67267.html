devilled

verb
tormented or pestered
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛvəld / (AmE), / ˈdɛvəld / (BrE)
past tense and past participle of 'devil': to torment, pester, or cause persistent worry or distress to someone

Examples

Devilled by worries
He felt devilled by doubts about his decision throughout the night.
The team was devilled by injuries all season, making it hard to win games.
She had been devilled by worries about her exam results for weeks.
