disorder

noun
disturbance of normal physical or mental function

A physical or medical condition in which normal functioning of the body or mind is disturbed.

Examples

A mental disorder
He was diagnosed with a rare eating disorder that required special treatment.
Many sleep disorders can be managed with lifestyle changes.
The new drug is used to treat anxiety disorder in adults.

Synonyms

  • disease
    2.3 194/m
    disease is a medical condition with a clear cause and set of symptoms, while disorder is a broader problem of bodily or mental function.
  • illness
    1.1 58/m
    illness is the personal experience of feeling unwell with clear symptoms, while disorder is a clinical label for disturbed bodily or mental functioning.
  • dysfunction
    6.1 6.90/m
    dysfunction is a technical term for impaired working of an organ or system, while disorder describes a medical condition that disrupts normal functioning.

Related Terms
