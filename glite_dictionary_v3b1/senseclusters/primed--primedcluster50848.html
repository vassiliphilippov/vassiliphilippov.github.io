primed

adjective
ready and prepared for action
4.6 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / praÉªmd / (AmE), / praÉªmd / (BrE)
ready and prepared to take a particular action or experience; strongly disposed or on the verge of doing something (usually followed by 'to' or 'for').

Examples

Markets primed for action
Markets were primed for a sharp reaction following the announcement.
She is primed to deliver her speech at a moment's notice.
The team was primed for victory after months of hard training.
