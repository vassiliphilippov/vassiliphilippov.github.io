kW

noun
unit equal to 1,000 watts (power)
3.4
Pronunciation: / kiː ˈdʌbljuː / (AmE), / kiː ˈdʌbljuː / (BrE)
a unit of power equal to 1,000 watts, commonly used to measure electrical output or consumption.

Examples

kW of power
Household solar panels are often rated at several kW of capacity.
The car's electric motor has a maximum output of 150 kW.
The generator produces enough power to supply 50 kW continuously.
