bathroom

noun
room with bath/shower and toilet
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbæθˌruːm /; / ˈbæθ.ruːm / (AmE), / ˈbɑːθruːm /; / ˈbɑːθ.ruːm / (BrE)
a room in a house or building used for personal hygiene, usually containing a toilet, sink, and a bath or shower

Examples

The public bathroom on the first floor is being renovated.
