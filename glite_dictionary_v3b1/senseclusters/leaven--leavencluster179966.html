leaven

noun
substance that makes dough rise
8.0 4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈle.vən/ (AmE), /ˈle.vən/ (BrE)
a substance, such as yeast or baking powder, that causes dough or liquid to ferment and rise

Examples

Bread leaven
She added the leaven and waited patiently for the dough to rise.
Too much leaven can make the dough rise too quickly and collapse.
Yeast is a common leaven used in bread making.
