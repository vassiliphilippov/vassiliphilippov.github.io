middlebrow

noun
moderately intellectual art or literature
1.5 6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪd.əl.braʊ/ (AmE), /ˈmɪd.əl.braʊ/ (BrE)
art, literature, or other cultural works regarded as having moderate intellectual or artistic value, appealing to average tastes

Examples

Middlebrow novels
He preferred avant-garde films to middlebrow entertainment commonly shown at mainstream theaters.
Many popular book clubs choose middlebrow novels that are easy to read but still thought-provoking.
The festival was criticized for featuring mostly middlebrow performances instead of challenging works.
