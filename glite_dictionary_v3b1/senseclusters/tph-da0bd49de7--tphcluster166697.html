TPH

sym
trains per hour
Pronunciation: / ti pi eɪtʃ / (AmE), / tiː piː eɪtʃ / (BrE)
an abbreviation for '[something] per hour', used to indicate the number or amount of something happening or processed in one hour (such as trains, tons, or transactions)

Examples

A higher TPH rating means the equipment can process more material each hour.

We need to increase our TPH to handle more customer orders efficiently.
