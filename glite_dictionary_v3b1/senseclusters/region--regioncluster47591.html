region

noun
area of activity, thought, or knowledge
2.3 12/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàriÀêd í…ôn / (AmE), / ÀàriÀêd í…ôn / (BrE)
a particular area of activity, thought, or knowledge, especially one that is discussed or considered separately

Examples

Region of science
In the region of mathematics, abstract concepts are explored in great depth.
The book discusses the region of science fiction that deals with artificial intelligence.
This debate moves us into the region of moral philosophy rather than practical politics.

Synonyms

  • area
    2.0 87/m
    area is a neutral word for a subject or field used in study or work, while region highlights a part considered separately.
  • realm
    5.1 21/m
    realm is a slightly formal word for a field or world of interest, while region is a general area discussed on its own.
  • territory
    4.8 12/m
    territory often means an area someone controls or claims, while region simply means a part of activity or knowledge.
  • province
    4.8 2.97/m
    province implies what belongs to a person or group to manage, while region names any separate area of activity or thought.

Related Terms

  • synonym: domain - an area of interest or activity; a field
  • synonym: realm - a field or domain of activity or interest
