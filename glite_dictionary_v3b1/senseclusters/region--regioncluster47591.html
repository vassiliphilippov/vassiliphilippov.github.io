region

noun
area of activity, thought, or knowledge
Pronunciation: / ˈriːdʒən / (AmE), / ˈriːdʒən / (BrE)
a particular area of activity, thought, or knowledge, especially one that is discussed or considered separately

Examples

Region of science
In the region of mathematics, abstract concepts are explored in great depth.
The book discusses the region of science fiction that deals with artificial intelligence.
This debate moves us into the region of moral philosophy rather than practical politics.

  • synonym: realm - a field or domain of activity or interest
