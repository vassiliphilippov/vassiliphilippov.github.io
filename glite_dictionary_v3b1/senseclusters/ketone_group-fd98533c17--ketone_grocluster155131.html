ketone group

noun
chemical functional group of ketones
1.9 4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkiː.toʊn ɡruːp/ /; / /ˈkiː.təʊn ɡruːp/ (AmE), /ˈkiː.təʊn ɡruːp/ (BrE)
a functional group in organic compounds, consisting of a carbon atom double-bonded to an oxygen atom and bonded to two other carbon atoms, characteristic of ketones
