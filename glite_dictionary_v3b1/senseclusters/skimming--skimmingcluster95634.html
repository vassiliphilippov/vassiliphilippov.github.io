skimming

noun
illegal copying of card data
8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈskɪm.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈskɪm.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the illegal act of copying information from a credit or debit card using a hidden electronic device (a skimmer), typically at an ATM or point-of-sale terminal.

Examples

Card skimming
ATMs across the city were compromised by skimming devices that copied customers' card data.
She reported skimming at a petrol station after noticing several unauthorized charges on her account.
Banks warn customers to inspect card readers carefully to reduce the risk of skimming and fraud.

Related Terms

  • synonym: card cloning - creating a copy of a payment card's data to make unauthorized transactions
  • synonym: card fraud - the illegal use, copying or stealing of payment-card information for unauthorized transactions
