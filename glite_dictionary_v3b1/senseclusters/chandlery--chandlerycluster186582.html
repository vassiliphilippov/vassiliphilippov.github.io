chandlery

noun
ship supply store
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃændləri / (AmE), / ˈtʃændləri / (BrE)
a store that sells supplies and equipment, especially for ships or boats

Examples

Boat chandlery
Before setting sail, they visited the chandlery to purchase extra life jackets and navigation charts.
The harbor's old chandlery is famous for its wide selection of marine ropes and anchors.
He found a replacement lantern at the local chandlery after his broke during the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hardware store - a shop selling tools and equipment for home and building
  • synonym: outfitter - a store that sells equipment and supplies for outdoor activities
