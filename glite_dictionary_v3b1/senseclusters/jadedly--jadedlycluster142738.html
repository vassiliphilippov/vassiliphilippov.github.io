jadedly

adverb
in a weary way
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒeɪ.dɪd.liː/ /; / /ˈdʒeɪ.dəd.liː/ (AmE), /ˈdʒeɪ.dɪd.liː/ /; / /ˈdʒeɪ.dəd.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that shows tiredness, weariness, or lack of enthusiasm because of having experienced too much of something
