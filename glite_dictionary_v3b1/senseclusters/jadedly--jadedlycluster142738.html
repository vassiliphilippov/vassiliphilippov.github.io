jadedly

adverb
in a weary way
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒeɪdɪdli / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪdɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that shows tiredness, weariness, or lack of enthusiasm because of having experienced too much of something

Examples

To answer jadedly
After hours of answering the same questions, she responded jadedly to the next customer.
He jadedly scrolled through the endless emails, barely paying attention to the content.
The actor jadedly delivered his lines, showing little excitement for the role.
He jadedly scrolled through the endless emails, barely paying attention to any of them.
After years in the industry, she responded jadedly to the latest announcement.
The students listened jadedly to the repeated instructions at the start of every class.

Related Terms

  • antonym: energetically - in a lively or vigorous manner
  • antonym: enthusiastically - in a way that shows excitement or eagerness
  • synonym: wearily - in a tired or exhausted manner
