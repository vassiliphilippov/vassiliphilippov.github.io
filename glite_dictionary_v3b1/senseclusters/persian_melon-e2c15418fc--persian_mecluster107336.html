Persian melon

noun
type of muskmelon with pale, non-musky flesh
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜːrʒən ˈmɛlən /; / ˈpɜrʒən ˈmɛlən / (AmE), / ˈpɜːʃ(ə)n ˈmɛlən /; / ˈpɜːʃən ˈmɛlən / (BrE)
a type of sweet melon, usually large and oblong, with a smooth rind and colorful flesh, known as the Persian melon
