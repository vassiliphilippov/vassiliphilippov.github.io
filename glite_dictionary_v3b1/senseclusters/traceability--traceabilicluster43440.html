traceability

noun
ability to track origins
6.1
Pronunciation: /treɪ.sə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /treɪ.sə.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ (AmE), /treɪ.sə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /treɪ.sə.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ (BrE)
the ability to track or verify the history, location, or use of something, especially in a process or supply chain
