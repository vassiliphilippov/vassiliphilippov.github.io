silica gel

noun
porous, moisture-absorbing silicon dioxide
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlɪkə dʒɛl / (AmE), / ˈsɪlɪkə dʒɛl / (BrE)
A granular, highly absorbent form of silicon dioxide used chiefly as a drying and moisture-absorbing agent.

Examples

Packet of silica gel
Packets of silica gel are often found in new shoes to keep them dry.
The silica gel crystals change color when they have absorbed moisture.
You should not eat silica gel, even though it is non-toxic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: desiccant - a substance used to absorb moisture; drying agent
