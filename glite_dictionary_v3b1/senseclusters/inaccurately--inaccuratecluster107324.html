Examples

  • imprecisely
    5.4 0.11/m
    imprecisely means done without exact detail or clear limits, while inaccurately focuses on being factually wrong or not precise.
  • inexactly
    5.7 0.02/m
    inexactly means not exact or detailed, while inaccurately stresses wrongness or lack of precision in everyday practice.
  • mistakenly
    3.1 4.16/m

  • wrongly

  • faultily
