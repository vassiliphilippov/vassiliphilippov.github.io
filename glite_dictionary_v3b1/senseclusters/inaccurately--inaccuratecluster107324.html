inaccurately

adverb
in an incorrect or imprecise way
1.7
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈækjərətli / (AmE), / ɪnˈækjʊərətli / (BrE)
In a way that is not accurate; done without correctness or precision.

Examples

To speak inaccurately
He completed the report inaccurately, missing several important figures.
If you fill out the form inaccurately, your application may be delayed.
The measurements were recorded inaccurately, leading to invalid results.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accurately - in a way that is correct or precise
