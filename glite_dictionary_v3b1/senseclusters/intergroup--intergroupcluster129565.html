intergroup

noun
event between groups
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈɡruːp / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈɡruːp / (BrE)
a meeting, match, or event that takes place between two or more different groups or teams

Examples

An intergroup match
The annual intergroup brings together students from different schools for friendly competition.
Each intergroup is organized to encourage cooperation and understanding between the teams.
Our club is preparing for the upcoming intergroup against the neighboring community center.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intragroup - occurring within a single group
  • synonym: tournament - a series of contests between teams or players
