basisphenoid

noun
skull base bone
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌbeɪsɪˈsfiːnɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌbeɪsɪˈsfiːnɔɪd / (BrE)
a bone located at the base of the skull in some vertebrates, forming part of the cranial floor

Examples

The basisphenoid bone
The basisphenoid is an important structure in the skulls of many reptiles and birds.
In some mammals, the basisphenoid fuses with other bones during development.
Damage to the basisphenoid can affect the stability of the cranial base.
