birdcall

noun
device for imitating bird sounds
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːdˌkɑl / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdˌkɔːl / (BrE)
A device designed to imitate the sound of a bird, often used by hunters or bird watchers.

Examples

A birdcall device
She collects different types of birdcalls for her birdwatching hobby.
The hunter used a birdcall to attract wild turkeys in the forest.
With a simple squeeze, the birdcall mimicked a robin's song perfectly.
