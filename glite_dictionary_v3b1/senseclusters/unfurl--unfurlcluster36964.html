unfurl

verb
spread out from a folded or rolled state
8.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɜrl / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɜːl / (BrE)
to unroll, unfold, or spread out something that has been rolled or folded up, or to become unrolled or spread out

Examples

To unfurl a flag
As spring arrived, the young leaves slowly started to unfurl from the tree branches.
She watched the sails unfurl in the morning breeze as the boat prepared to set off.
The crowd cheered as they began to unfurl the team’s massive flag.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fold - to bend something over upon itself
  • antonym: roll up - to fold or curl something into a cylindrical shape
