Equus caballus przewalskii

noun
endangered wild horse of central Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈekwəs kəˈbæləs prɛˈzælskɪ.i / (AmE), / ˈe.kwəs kəˈbæl.əs prɛˈzæl.skiː / (BrE)
A rare and endangered subspecies of wild horse native to central Asia, closely resembling an ass in appearance.

Examples

Wild Equus caballus przewalskii
Scientists monitor the health of Equus caballus przewalskii to ensure the species does not become extinct.
The Equus caballus przewalskii population has been reintroduced to several reserves in Mongolia.
Unlike domesticated horses, Equus caballus przewalskii has a sturdy build and stands out for its dun coloring.
