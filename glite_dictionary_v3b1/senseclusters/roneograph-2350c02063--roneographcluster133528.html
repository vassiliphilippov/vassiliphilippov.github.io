Roneograph

noun
rotary duplicator using a stencil (trade mark)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊniəˌɡræf / (AmE), / ˈrəʊniəˌɡrɑːf / (BrE)
A trademarked rotary duplicator that uses a stencil to reproduce documents by pressing ink through the stencil.

Examples

The Roneograph machine
After the Roneograph broke, they had to find another way to duplicate exam papers.
In the 1950s, many schools relied on a Roneograph for handouts.
The secretary made multiple copies of the report using the Roneograph.
