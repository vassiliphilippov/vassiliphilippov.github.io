botnet

noun
network of bots
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑtnɛt / (AmE), / ˈbɒtnɛt / (BrE)
a network of computers or automated programs (bots) that are controlled by someone, usually to perform harmful online actions like sending spam or attacking websites
