Rickettsiaceae

noun
family of bacteria-like microorganisms
Pronunciation: / rɪˌkɛtsiˈeɪsiː / (AmE), / rɪˌkɛtsiˈeɪsiː / (BrE)
A family of microorganisms resembling bacteria, found in arthropods and capable of causing disease in vertebrates.
