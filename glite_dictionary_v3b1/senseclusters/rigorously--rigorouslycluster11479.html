rigorously

adverb
in a strict or thorough manner
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪɡərəsli / (AmE), / ˈrɪɡərəsli / (BrE)
In a strict, thorough, and careful manner; with great attention to rules, procedures, or standards.

Examples

To rigorously check work
She rigorously reviews her notes after every lecture.
The laboratory protocols were followed rigorously to ensure accurate results.
The new safety measures are being rigorously enforced by management.
