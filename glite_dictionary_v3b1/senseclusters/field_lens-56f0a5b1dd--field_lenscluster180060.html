field lens

noun
lens farthest from the eye in optical device
7.3 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / fiÀêld l…õnz / (AmE), / fiÀêld l…õnz / (BrE)
the lens in a compound optical device that is farthest from the eye, used to increase the field of view

Examples

A field lens in binoculars
Most binoculars have a field lens to provide a wider field of view.
The field lens helps to direct light rays within the microscope for a clearer image.
When cleaning a telescope, be careful not to scratch the field lens.
