CRS

noun
travel booking system
6.8
Pronunciation: / si ɑr ɛs / (AmE), / si ɑː ɛs / (BrE)
a computerized system used by travel agencies and airlines to manage bookings and reservations for flights, hotels, and other services

Examples

CRS booking system
Most travel agents rely on a CRS to book flights and hotels for their clients.
The airline upgraded its CRS to improve efficiency and reduce booking errors.
Learning to use a CRS is essential for anyone working in the travel industry.

Related Terms

  • antonym: manual booking - making reservations without a computerized system
