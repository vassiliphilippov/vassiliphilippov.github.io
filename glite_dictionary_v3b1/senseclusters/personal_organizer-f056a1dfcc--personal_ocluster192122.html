personal organizer

noun
electronic device for managing personal information
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːsənl ˈɔrɡəˌnaɪzɚ /; / ˈpɜrsənl ˈɔːrɡəˌnaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl ˈɔːɡənaɪzə / (BrE)
a small electronic device used to manage personal information such as schedules, contacts, and notes; often functions as a diary, address book, and alarm clock

Examples

A personal organizer device
He used his personal organizer to keep track of all his appointments and phone numbers.
In the early 2000s, having a personal organizer was considered essential for busy professionals.

I rely on my personal organiser to keep track of meetings and deadlines.
