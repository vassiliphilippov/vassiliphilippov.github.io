rennin

noun
enzyme that coagulates milk
9.3 0.01/m

an enzyme found in the stomachs of young mammals that causes milk to coagulate, used in cheese-making

Examples

Rennin enzyme
Cheese makers use rennin extracted from calves to curdle milk.
Infant mammals produce rennin to digest their mother's milk effectively.
Vegetarian cheese alternatives often use microbial enzymes instead of rennin.
