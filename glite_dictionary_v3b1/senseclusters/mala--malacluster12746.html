mala

noun
prayer beads
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːlə / (AmE), / ˈmɑːlə / (BrE)
a string of beads used for counting prayers or mantras, especially in Hinduism and Buddhism

Examples

Prayer mala beads
During meditation, she quietly moved each bead of her mala with her fingers.
He bought a new mala at the temple to help him focus during his prayers.
The mala is often made from seeds, wood, or gemstones, depending on tradition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rosary - a string of beads used in Christian prayer
