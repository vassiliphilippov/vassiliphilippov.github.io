otology

noun
study of the ear (medicine)
Pronunciation: / oʊˈtɑlədʒi / (AmE), / əʊˈtɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of medicine that deals with the structure, function, and diseases of the ear

Examples

Otology medicine
Recent advances in otology have led to better treatments for hearing loss.

The conference brought together leading experts in otology from around the world.
