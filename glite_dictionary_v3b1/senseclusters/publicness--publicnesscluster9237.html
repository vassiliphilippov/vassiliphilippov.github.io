publicness

noun
state of being public
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪknəs / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪknəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being open, accessible, or visible to everyone, or being owned or managed by the government or community rather than privately
