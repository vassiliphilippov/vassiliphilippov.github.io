trimolecular

adjective
involving three molecules
Pronunciation: / ˌtraɪmoʊˈlɛkjələr / (AmE), / ˌtraɪməˈlɛkjʊlə / (BrE)
involving or consisting of three molecules, especially in a chemical reaction

Examples

A trimolecular reaction
A trimolecular reaction is much less common than a bimolecular one in chemistry.
Scientists studied the trimolecular process to understand how three molecules interact simultaneously.
The rate of a trimolecular reaction depends on the concentration of all three reactants.

  • antonym: unimolecular - involving a single molecule
