pamphlet

noun
small informative booklet
Pronunciation: / ˈpæm.flət / (AmE), / ˈpæm.flɪt / (BrE)
a small booklet or leaflet containing information or arguments about a single subject

Examples

A pamphlet about politics
He read a political pamphlet arguing for environmental reform.
She picked up a pamphlet at the museum entrance detailing current exhibits and events.
The organization distributed a pamphlet on recycling to raise public awareness.
A pamphlet on local history was available at the library desk.
She handed me a pamphlet about recycling at the community fair.
The clinic provides a pamphlet explaining common side effects of the medication.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: book - a written or printed work consisting of pages bound together
  • synonym: brochure - a small book or magazine with information about a product or service
