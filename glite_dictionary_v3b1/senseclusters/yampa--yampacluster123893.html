yampa

noun
edible North American plant
Pronunciation: / ˈjæmpə / (AmE), / ˈjæmpə / (BrE)
a North American plant with edible roots, traditionally used as food by Indigenous peoples

Examples

The yampa root
The yampa was a staple food for several Indigenous communities in the western United States.
Early explorers learned to dig and cook yampa roots from local tribes.
Botanists study the yampa for its nutritional value and historical significance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: root vegetable - an edible plant root used as food
