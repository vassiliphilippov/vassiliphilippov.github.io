noncharitable

adjective
not generous or kind
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈtʃærətəbəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈtʃærətəbəl / (BrE)
not showing kindness, generosity, or goodwill toward others

Examples

Noncharitable behavior
His noncharitable attitude made it difficult for the team to work together.
The review was surprisingly noncharitable given the effort put into the project.
She regretted her noncharitable remarks after seeing how hurt her friend was.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unkind - not friendly or considerate toward others
