nonrenewal

noun
ending without extension
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.rɪˈnuː.əl / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.rɪˈnjuː.əl / (BrE)
the act of not extending or continuing something, such as a contract, agreement, or policy

Examples

The nonrenewal of contract
The nonrenewal of her contract left her searching for a new job.
Due to repeated violations, the company faced nonrenewal of its business license.
He was surprised by the sudden nonrenewal of his gym membership.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expiration - the coming to an end of a period or term
  • antonym: renewal - the act of extending or continuing something
