alleviator

noun
remedy that relieves pain or discomfort
6.7 5.9
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈliː.vi.eɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ə.ˈliː.vi.eɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a substance or treatment that reduces pain or discomfort without curing the underlying problem

Examples

A pain alleviator
Although an alleviator can provide relief, it does not address the underlying cause.
He searched for a natural alleviator to help with his headaches.
The medication acted as an alleviator of her chronic back pain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cure - a remedy that eliminates a disease or condition
  • synonym: palliative - a remedy that relieves symptoms without curing the disease
