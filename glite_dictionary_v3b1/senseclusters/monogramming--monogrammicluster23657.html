monogramming

noun
decorating with initials
4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑː.nə.græm.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈmɒ.nə.græm.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act or process of decorating an object with a monogram (a person's initials), usually by embroidery or printing

Examples

To monogram a handkerchief
She started monogramming handkerchiefs for her friends as a hobby.
The shop offers monogramming services for towels, bags, and shirts.
Many people enjoy monogramming gifts to make them more personal and special.
The company offers monogramming on towels for a personalized touch.
She learned monogramming to add her initials to her handkerchiefs.
Many luxury bags feature monogramming as a sign of exclusivity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: engraving - the process of cutting a design into a hard surface
  • synonym: personalization - the act of making something unique to a person
