Louis Henri Sullivan

proper noun
U.S. architect, coined 'form follows function'
8.2
Pronunciation: / luːˈiː ˈhɛnri səˈlɪvən / (AmE), / luːˈiː ˈhɛnri sʌˈlɪvən / (BrE)
