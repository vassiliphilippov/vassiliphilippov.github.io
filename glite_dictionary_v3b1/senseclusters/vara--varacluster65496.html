vara

noun
unit of length (Spanish, ≈ yard)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑːrə / (AmE), / ˈvɑːrə / (BrE)
a Spanish unit of length, approximately equal to a yard, with varying values in different localities

Examples

A vara of length
A distance of fifty varas could vary greatly depending on the region.
Builders in colonial Texas often used the vara as the standard unit of measurement.
The old land grant documents measured property lines in varas instead of feet.
