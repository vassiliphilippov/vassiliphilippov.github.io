overbuild

verb
to build excessively
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈbɪld / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈbɪld / (BrE)
to construct more buildings or structures than are needed or allowed in a particular area

Examples

To overbuild a city
The city warned that developers should not overbuild in the downtown area.
If you overbuild on this land, you may violate local zoning laws.
Some neighborhoods have been overbuilt, leading to traffic and overcrowding problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overdevelop - to develop land with too many buildings or structures
  • antonym: underbuild - to build less than is needed or allowed
