Vasa

proper noun
Swedish warship
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑːsə / (AmE), / ˈvɑːsə / (BrE)
a 17th-century Swedish warship that sank on its maiden voyage in 1628 and is now preserved and displayed in Stockholm

Examples

Vasa warship museum
The Vasa is one of the best-preserved 17th-century ships in the world.
Visitors to Stockholm often tour the Vasa Museum to see the historic vessel.
After centuries underwater, the Vasa was raised and carefully restored for public display.
