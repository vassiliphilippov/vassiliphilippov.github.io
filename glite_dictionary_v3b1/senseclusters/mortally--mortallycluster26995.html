mortally

adverb
in a manner causing death or showing extreme intensity
7.1 1.14/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…îrt…ôli / (AmE), / Ààm…îÀêt…ôli / (BrE)
in a way that causes death or in a way that is extreme and intense (such as hatred or fear)

Examples

To be mortally afraid
He was mortally afraid of snakes and never went near the forest.
She looked at him with a mortally intense hatred that could not be disguised.
The soldier was mortally wounded in battle, but managed to deliver one final message.
