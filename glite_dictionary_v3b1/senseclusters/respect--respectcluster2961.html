respect

noun
particular aspect or detail
Pronunciation: / rɪˈspɛkt / (AmE), / rɪˈspɛkt / (BrE)
a particular aspect, point, or detail under consideration; a specific regard in which something is viewed or judged

Examples

In some respects
In some respects, her performance exceeded our expectations.
The policy is similar to ours in every respect except one.
This solution is preferable in that respect because it saves time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aspect - a particular feature or part of something
  • synonym: regard - a particular consideration or respect for something
