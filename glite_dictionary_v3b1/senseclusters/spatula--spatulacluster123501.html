spatula

noun
tool with thin, flexible blade for mixing or spreading
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈspætʃələ / (AmE), / ˈspatjʊlə /; / ˈspætjʊlə / (BrE)
a hand tool with a flat, flexible blade used for mixing, spreading, lifting, or turning food or other soft substances

She used a spatula to flip the pancakes without breaking them.
