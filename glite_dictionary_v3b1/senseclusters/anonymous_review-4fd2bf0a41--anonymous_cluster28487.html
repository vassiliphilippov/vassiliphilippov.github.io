anonymous review

noun
a review with undisclosed author
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈnɑː.nə.məs rɪˈvjuː / (AmE), / əˈnɒn.ɪ.məs rɪˈvjuː / (BrE)
a review in which the identity of the author is not revealed

Examples

An anonymous review online
She preferred to write an anonymous review so her coworkers wouldn't know her opinion.
Some websites allow users to submit an anonymous review to encourage honest feedback.
The magazine published an anonymous review of the new novel, sparking speculation about its author.
