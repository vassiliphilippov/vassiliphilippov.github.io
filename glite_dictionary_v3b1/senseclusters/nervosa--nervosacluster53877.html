nervosa

adjective
veined; with veins
Pronunciation: / nərˈvoʊsə / (AmE), / nəˈvəʊsə / (BrE)
used in scientific names to mean 'veined' or 'having prominent veins', especially in plants or animals

The species name nervosa refers to the plant's leaves, which have clearly visible veins.
In zoology, animals with the term nervosa in their name often show prominent vein patterns.
