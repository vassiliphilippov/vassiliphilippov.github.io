harmoniously

adverb
in a balanced way
Pronunciation: / hɑrˈmoʊniəsli / (AmE), / hɑːˈməʊniəsli / (BrE)
in a way that is well balanced, peaceful, or pleasingly combined together

Examples

Play harmoniously in concert
The musicians played harmoniously, creating a beautiful sound that filled the hall.
Different cultures can live harmoniously in the same community when there is mutual respect.
The colors in the painting blend harmoniously to create a pleasing effect.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discordantly - in a way that lacks harmony or agreement
  • synonym: peacefully - in a calm and quiet way, without conflict
