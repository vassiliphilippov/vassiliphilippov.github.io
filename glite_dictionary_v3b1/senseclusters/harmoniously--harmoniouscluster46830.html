harmoniously

adverb
in a balanced way

Pronunciation: / h…ërÀàmo äni…ôsli / (AmE), / h…ëÀêÀàm…ô äni…ôsli / (BrE)

Examples

The colors in the painting blend harmoniously to create a pleasing effect.
