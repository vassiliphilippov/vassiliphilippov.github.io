Synonyms

  • biopsychology
    7.1 0.01/m
    biopsychology studies how brain processes affect the mind and behavior, while physiological psychology links bodily functions with behavior and mental activity.
  • psychobiology
    7.4 0.05/m
    psychobiology investigates how biology such as genes, hormones, or brain systems influence behavior and mind, while physiological psychology focuses on bodily processes linked to behavior.
  • psychophysiology
    9.9 0.04/m
    psychophysiology measures body responses like heart rate to study psychological states, while physiological psychology examines broader bodily processes related to behavior and mind.
