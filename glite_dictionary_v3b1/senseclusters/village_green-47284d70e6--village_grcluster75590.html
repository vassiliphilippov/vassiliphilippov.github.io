village green

noun
grassy communal area in a village
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪlɪdʒ ˌɡrin / (AmE), / ˈvɪlɪdʒ ˌɡriːn / (BrE)
a common area of grassy land in a village, often used for recreation or community events

Examples

The village green
Children played cricket on the village green during the summer holidays.
Her cottage overlooks the village green, where neighbors often gather.
The annual fair is held every August on the village green.
