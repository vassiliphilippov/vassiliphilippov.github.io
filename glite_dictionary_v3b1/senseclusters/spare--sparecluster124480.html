spare

adjective
free or available time
1.0
Pronunciation: / spɛr / (AmE), / spɛə / (BrE)
extra or available for use; not needed at the moment

Examples

Spare time
Do you have any spare minutes to talk later this afternoon?
In his spare moments, he volunteers at the animal shelter.
She likes to read in her spare time after work.
Do you have a spare battery for my camera?
I always keep a spare key hidden outside in case I lose my main one.
The mechanic replaced the flat tire with the spare.
He stored his old clothes in the spare room.
There were two spare chairs left over after everyone was seated.
We keep a spare set of keys in case we lose the originals.

  • synonym: reserve - something kept back or saved for future use
