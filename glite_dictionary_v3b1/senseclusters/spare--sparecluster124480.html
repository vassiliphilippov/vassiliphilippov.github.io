Synonyms

  • extra
    1.0 90/m
    extra means additional or more than usual, while spare implies something kept unused and available for use later.
  • free
    1.0 29/m
    free describes something not in use or occupied now, while spare often means an extra item kept and not needed at the moment.
  • surplus
    3.4 0.13/m
    surplus describes an amount left over after need, while spare suggests a kept extra item that you can use if needed.
  • excess
    4.1 9.96/m
    excess means more than is permitted or desirable and often has a negative sense, while spare is simply extra or available.
