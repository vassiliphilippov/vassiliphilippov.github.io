lacing

noun
cord for fastening shoes or garments
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈleɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
a cord threaded through eyelets or hooks to fasten and tighten the edges of a shoe, garment, or similar item together

Examples

Shoe lacing
He tightened the lacing on his boots before going outside.
She replaced the worn lacing on her sneakers with a brighter color.
The corset's lacing was decorated with delicate ribbon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shoelace - a string or cord used to fasten shoes
