puppetlike

adjective
resembling a puppet
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌpɪtˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈpʌpɪtˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or behaving like a puppet, especially in being controlled or moved by others

Examples

Puppetlike movement
Her puppetlike walk in the play made the audience laugh.
He followed the instructions in a puppetlike manner, without question.
The robot's puppetlike movements fascinated the children at the science fair.

Related Terms

  • antonym: independent - acting on one's own, not controlled by others
  • synonym: mechanical - acting or moving like a machine, without emotion
