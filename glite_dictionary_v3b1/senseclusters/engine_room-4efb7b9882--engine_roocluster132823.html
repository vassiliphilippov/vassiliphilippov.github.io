engine room

noun
room for housing engines (esp. on a ship)
Pronunciation: / ˈɛn.dʒɪn ˌrum /; / ˈɛn.dʒɪn ˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈɛn.dʒɪn ˌruːm / (BrE)
the room or compartment in a ship, train, or building where the engine and machinery are located and operated

Examples
