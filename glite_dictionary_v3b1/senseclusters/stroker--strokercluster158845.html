stroker

noun
engine with longer stroke
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstroʊkər / (AmE), / ˈstrəʊkə / (BrE)
an engine or engine part designed to increase the length of piston travel, raising displacement and power

Examples

Stroker engine upgrade
He installed a stroker in his car to boost its horsepower for racing.
A stroker allows the engine to produce more torque by increasing the piston travel.
Many performance enthusiasts choose a stroker to get more power from their engines.
