hope

noun
reason for optimism about the future
Pronunciation: / hoʊp / (AmE), / həʊp / (BrE)
a reason, person, or thing that gives you a feeling that something good may happen

Examples

A hope for others
Her recovery offered a hope that others in similar situations could improve too.
The recent scientific breakthrough has given new hope to patients with the disease.
There was little hope of finding survivors after the disaster.
After the injury to their star player, the rookie became the team's only hope for winning.
For many voters, the candidate represented their last hope for real change.
She is considered the best hope for a breakthrough in cancer research.

Related Terms

  • antonym: despair - complete loss of hope
  • synonym: expectation - the belief that something will happen
  • synonym: promise - grounds for expectation of success or improvement
