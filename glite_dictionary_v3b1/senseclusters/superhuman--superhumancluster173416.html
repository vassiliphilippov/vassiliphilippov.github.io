superhuman

noun
person with extraordinary powers
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈhjuːmən / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈhjuːmən / (BrE)
a person who has abilities or powers far greater than those of an ordinary human

Examples

A superhuman hero
The story follows a superhuman who can fly and lift enormous weights with ease.
Scientists debate whether technology could ever create a real superhuman in the future.
Legends often describe superhuman warriors who perform impossible feats in battle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hero - a person admired for courage or outstanding achievements
  • antonym: mortal - an ordinary human being without special powers
