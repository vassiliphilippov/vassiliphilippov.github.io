tissue paper

noun
thin, soft paper for wrapping or hygiene
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪʃu ˌpeɪpər / (AmE), / ˈtɪʃuː ˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
a very thin, soft paper, usually translucent, used especially for wrapping delicate items or for hygiene purposes

Examples

A piece of tissue paper
A piece of tissue paper floated gently to the ground as she opened the gift.
She wrapped the fragile vase in tissue paper before placing it in the box.
The artist used tissue paper to create colorful collages for her project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tissue - soft thin paper used especially as a disposable handkerchief or for wrapping
