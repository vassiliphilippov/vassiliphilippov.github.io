size of it

noun
that's the true situation
Pronunciation: / saɪz əv ɪt / (AmE), / saɪz əv ɪt / (BrE)
used to indicate that a statement summarizes the true situation or facts; that's how things stand

Examples

That's the size of it
He asked if I failed the exam, and I had to admit that's the size of it.
If you're saying the company is closing down, then that's the size of it.
“She won't be coming to the party?” “No, that's about the size of it.”
