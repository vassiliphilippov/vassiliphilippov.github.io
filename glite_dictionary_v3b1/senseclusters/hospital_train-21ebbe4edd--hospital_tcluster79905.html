hospital train

noun
train for transporting the sick or wounded
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːspɪtl treɪn /; / c8h51d0sp6atl tre6an / (AmE), / ˈhɒspɪtl treɪn /; / c8h52sp6atl tre6an / (BrE)
a train specially equipped to transport and/or treat sick or wounded people, often used in emergencies or war
