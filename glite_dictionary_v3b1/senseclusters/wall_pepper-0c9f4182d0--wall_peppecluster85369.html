wall pepper

noun
a creeping sedum plant with yellow flowers
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: / wɔl ˈpɛpɚ / (AmE), / wɔːl ˈpɛpə / (BrE)
A mossy European sedum plant (Sedum acre) with creeping stems and yellow flowers, often used as a ground cover.

Examples

Wall pepper plant
Wall pepper thrives in dry, rocky areas where other ground covers may not survive.
She planted wall pepper along the pathway to create a soft, colorful border.
The old garden walls were covered in wall pepper, brightening the stones with yellow blooms.
