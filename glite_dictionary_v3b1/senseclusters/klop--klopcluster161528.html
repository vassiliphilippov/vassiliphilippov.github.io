klop

noun
public bench
9.9
Pronunciation: / klɒp / (AmE), / klɒp / (BrE)
a long bench or seat for several people, often found in parks or public places, especially in Slavic regions

Examples

Wooden klop
They rested on the old wooden klop while watching children play in the square.
The park was quiet except for a couple sitting together on a klop under the trees.
After a long walk, we found a klop near the fountain to sit and relax.
