trial

noun
difficult or upsetting experience
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪ.əl / (AmE), / ˈtraɪ.əl / (BrE)
a difficult, annoying, or upsetting experience; something that tests one's patience or endurance

Examples

A hard trial
Caring for three young children by herself was a real trial for her.
His chronic illness was a constant trial to endure.
They faced many trials during their first year in the new country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ordeal - a severe or trying experience
  • antonym: pleasure - a feeling of happy satisfaction and enjoyment
  • synonym: tribulation - a cause of great trouble or suffering
