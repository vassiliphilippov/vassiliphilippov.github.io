finery

noun
elaborate or decorative clothing and accessories
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪnəri / (AmE), / ˈfaɪnəri / (BrE)
elaborate or decorative clothing and accessories, especially worn for special occasions or to attract attention

Examples

Colorful finery
Despite the rain, the parade participants marched proudly in their colorful finery.
She dressed herself in her finest finery for the gala evening.
The royal wedding was a spectacle of jewels and extravagant finery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plainness - the quality of being simple, unadorned, or lacking decoration
  • synonym: regalia - the distinctive clothing or insignia worn by members of a particular group, often for ceremonial purposes
