marketing cost

noun
expenses for promoting and selling items
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrkətɪŋ kɒst / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪtɪŋ kɒst / (BrE)
the total expenses incurred in promoting and selling a product or service, including advertising, distribution, and sales efforts.

Examples

Rising marketing costs

The company reduced its marketing costs by switching to digital advertising.
We need to analyze whether our marketing costs are delivering a good return on investment.
