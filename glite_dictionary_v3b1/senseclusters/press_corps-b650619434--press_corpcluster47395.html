press corps

noun
group of journalists covering the same area
Pronunciation: / prɛs kɔːr(z) / (AmE), / prɛs kɔːz / (BrE)
A group of journalists from different media organizations who report on the same subject, event, or institution.

Examples

The press corps travels
Many members of the press corps traveled with the president during his overseas trip.
She joined the local press corps to report on city council meetings.
The White House press corps gathered for the daily briefing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: journalist - a person who writes or reports the news
  • synonym: media - organizations that provide news and information
