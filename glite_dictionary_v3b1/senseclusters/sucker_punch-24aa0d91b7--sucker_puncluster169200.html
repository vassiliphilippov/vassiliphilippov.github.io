sucker punch

noun
an unexpected punch
8.3 5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌkər pʌnt͡ʃ /; / ˈsʌkər pʌntʃ / (AmE), / ˈsʌkə pʌntʃ / (BrE)
an unexpected and unfair attack, especially a punch delivered without warning; can also refer to a sudden, hurtful remark or action

Examples

A sucker punch attack
