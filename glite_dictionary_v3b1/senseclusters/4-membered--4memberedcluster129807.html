4-membered

adjective
having a four-membered ring (chemistry)
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌmɛmbɚd / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌmɛmbəd / (BrE)
(of a chemical compound) containing a ring structure consisting of four atoms or groups.
