sociodemographics

noun
social and demographic data
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊsioʊˌdɛməˈɡræfɪks /; / ˌsoʊsiˌoʊˌdɛməˈɡræfɪks / (AmE), / ˌsəʊsiəʊˌdɛməˈɡræfɪks / (BrE)
the social and demographic characteristics of a population or group, such as age, gender, income, and education, or the data about these characteristics
