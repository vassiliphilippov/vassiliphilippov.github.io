excommunicatory

adjective
relating to exclusion
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkskəˈmjunɪkəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˌɛkskəˈmjuːnɪkətəri / (BrE)
relating to or involving the act of officially excluding someone from membership in a group, especially a church

Examples

An excommunicatory action
The church leaders took an excommunicatory stance against those who broke the rules.
An excommunicatory letter was sent to the member after repeated warnings.
The organization adopted excommunicatory measures to enforce its strict code of conduct.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excommunicant - a person who has been officially excluded from a church
  • antonym: inclusive - allowing or including everyone
