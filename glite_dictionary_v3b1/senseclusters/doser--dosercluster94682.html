doser

noun
person in makeshift shelter
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊzər / (AmE), / ˈdəʊzə / (BrE)
a person who sleeps in temporary or makeshift accommodation, often without a permanent home

Examples

A homeless doser
The city provided warm meals for every doser seeking shelter during the cold winter nights.
After losing his job, he became a doser and slept wherever he could find a safe spot.
Volunteers handed out blankets to each doser they met under the bridge.
