Hamelia erecta

noun
a tropical shrub with bright red-orange flowers
9.8
Pronunciation: / hæˈmiːliə ɪˈrɛktə / (AmE), / hæˈmiːliə ɪˈrɛktə / (BrE)
A shrub native to Florida, the West Indies, Mexico, and Brazil, with attractive orange to crimson flowers; commonly grown as an ornamental plant.

Examples

Hamelia erecta shrub
In tropical landscapes, Hamelia erecta is prized for its vibrant color and easy maintenance.
