tetrahedron

noun
solid with four triangular faces
6.4 6.1
Pronunciation: /te.trə.ˈhiː.drən/ (AmE), /te.trə.ˈhiː.drən/ (BrE)
A solid shape in geometry that has four plane triangular faces, six straight edges, and four vertices.

Examples

A tetrahedron shape
A regular tetrahedron has all four faces as equilateral triangles.
He built a model of a tetrahedron for his geometry project.
The molecular shape of methane is that of a tetrahedron.
