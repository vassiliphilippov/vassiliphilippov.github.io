golden pheasant

noun
brightly coloured Asian pheasant
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldən ˈfɛznt / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈfiːznt / (BrE)
a brightly coloured crested pheasant native to the mountains of western and central Asia, known for the striking appearance of the male

Examples

The golden pheasant bird
Many zoos feature the golden pheasant in their aviaries because of its vivid colors.
Photographers travelled to China to capture images of the rare golden pheasant in its natural habitat.
The male golden pheasant is famous for its brilliant red and gold plumage.
