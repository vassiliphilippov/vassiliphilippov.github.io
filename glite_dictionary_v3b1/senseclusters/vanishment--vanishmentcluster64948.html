vanishment

noun
complete disappearance
Pronunciation: / ˈvænɪʃmənt / (AmE), / ˈvænɪʃmənt / (BrE)
the act or process of disappearing completely or ceasing to be visible

Examples

The vanishment of city
The sudden vanishment of the ancient city remains a mystery to historians.
After the storm, the vanishment of the boat was reported to the coast guard.
The story describes the magical vanishment of the castle at midnight.

Related Terms

  • antonym: appearance - the act of becoming visible or present
  • synonym: disappearance - the act of someone or something ceasing to be visible or present
