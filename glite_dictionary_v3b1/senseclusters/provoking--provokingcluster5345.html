provoking

adjective
causing annoyance or anger
Pronunciation: / prəˈvoʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / prəˈvəʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
Causing annoyance, anger, or resentment.

Examples

Provoking comments
His provoking behavior eventually led to an argument.
She found the long, unexplained wait at the doctor’s office particularly provoking.
The manager's provoking comments upset the entire team during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irritating - causing annoyance or slight anger
  • antonym: soothing - having a calming or relieving effect
