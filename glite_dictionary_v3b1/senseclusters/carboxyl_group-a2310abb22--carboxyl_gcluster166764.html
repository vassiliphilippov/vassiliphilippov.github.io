carboxyl group

A functional group consisting of a carbonyl and a hydroxyl group (-COOH), found in and characteristic of organic acids.

Examples

A carboxyl group in acids
A molecule may contain more than one carboxyl group, influencing its acidity.
The carboxyl group is a defining feature of carboxylic acids like acetic acid.
The loss of a carboxyl group during metabolism is called decarboxylation.
