adjudicate

verb
to settle a dispute officially
7.8 1.67/m
Pronunciation: / …ôÀàd íud…™Àåke…™t /; / …ôÀàd íuÀêd…™Àåke…™t / (AmE), / …ôÀàd íuÀêd…™ke…™t / (BrE)
to make an official decision or judgment about a dispute, especially in a court of law or competition

Examples

To adjudicate a case
A third party was brought in to adjudicate the business disagreement.
It took the referee several minutes to adjudicate on the contested goal.
The committee will adjudicate the dispute between the two parties.
An independent body was appointed to adjudicate disputes arising from the contract.
It took several months for the court to adjudicate the complex lawsuit.
The panel will adjudicate the competition and announce the winners next week.
