weigh-in

noun
official pre-competition weight measurement
4.5 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈweɪ.ɪn/ /; / /ˈweɪ.ən/ (AmE), /ˈweɪ.ɪn/ /; / /ˈweɪ.ən/ (BrE)
an official event, especially in sports, where competitors' body weight is measured before a competition to check it meets the required limits
