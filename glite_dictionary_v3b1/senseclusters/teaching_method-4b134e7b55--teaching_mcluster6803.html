teaching method

noun
approach or technique used in teaching
Pronunciation: / ˈtitʃɪŋ ˈmɛθəd / (AmE), / ˈtiːtʃɪŋ ˈmɛθəd / (BrE)
A particular approach or technique used for instructing students or conveying knowledge.

Examples

A teaching method approach
Every school should consider the most suitable teaching method for its pupils.
Group work is an effective teaching method for encouraging student collaboration.
The teacher experimented with a new teaching method to engage her students more effectively.
