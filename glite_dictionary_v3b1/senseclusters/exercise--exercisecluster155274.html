Synonyms

  • practice
    1.0 41/m
    practice is doing something repeatedly to improve a skill, while exercise often refers to a planned routine or task for training.
  • drill
    2.9 2.97/m
    drill uses strict repetition and correction in training settings like the military or class, while exercise is general repeated practice to learn.
  • train
    1.0 21/m
    train means preparing physically or mentally over time, often under a coach or program, while exercise is the repeated activity itself.
