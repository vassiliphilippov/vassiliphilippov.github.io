exercise

verb
practice or train repeatedly
Pronunciation: / ˈɛksərˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɛksəsaɪz / (BrE)
to perform an activity repeatedly or regularly in order to learn or improve a skill

Examples

To exercise skills
The pianist exercised her fingers with scales before the recital.
The students exercised the new vocabulary every day to master the language.
You should exercise your math skills regularly to stay sharp.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drill - repeat exercises to learn a skill thoroughly
  • synonym: practice - perform an activity repeatedly to acquire or improve a skill
