taxi fare

noun
charge for a taxi ride
Pronunciation: / ˈtæksi fɛr / (AmE), / ˈtæksi feə / (BrE)
the amount of money charged for a ride in a taxicab

Examples

The taxi fare cost
He asked the driver how much the taxi fare would be before getting in.
The taxi fare from the airport to the hotel was more expensive than I expected.
They split the taxi fare equally among all the passengers.
