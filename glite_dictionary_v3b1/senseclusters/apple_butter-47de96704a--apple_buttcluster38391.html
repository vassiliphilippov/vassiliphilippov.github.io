apple butter

noun
sweet spread made from cooked apples
Pronunciation: / ˈæpəl ˌbʌtər / (AmE), / ˈæpəl ˌbʌtə / (BrE)
A thick, dark, sweet spread made by slow-cooking apples with sugar and spices until smooth and concentrated.

Examples

Sweet apple butter
I prefer apple butter over regular jam because it's less sweet and more flavorful.
She spread apple butter on a warm piece of toast for breakfast.
The homemade apple butter filled the kitchen with a delicious aroma of spices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jam - fruit preserve made by boiling fruit with sugar
