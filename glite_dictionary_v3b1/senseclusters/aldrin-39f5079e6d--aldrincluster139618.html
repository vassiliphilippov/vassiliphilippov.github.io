Aldrin

proper noun
surname of astronaut
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːldrɪn / (AmE), / ˈɔːldrɪn / (BrE)
a surname, most famously borne by Buzz Aldrin, the American astronaut who was the second person to walk on the Moon

Examples

Buzz Aldrin
Buzz Aldrin became world-famous after walking on the Moon in 1969.
The science teacher showed a photo of Aldrin standing on the lunar surface.
Many people admire Aldrin for his contributions to space exploration.
