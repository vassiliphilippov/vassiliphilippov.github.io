multivibrator

noun
signal-generating circuit
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltɪˈvaɪbreɪtər / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈvaɪbreɪtə / (BrE)

A simple multivibrator can be built using just a few transistors and resistors.
The waveform produced by the multivibrator was displayed on the oscilloscope.
