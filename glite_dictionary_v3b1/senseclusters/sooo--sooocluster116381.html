sooo

adverb
informal hesitation marker
9.3
Pronunciation: / soʊː / (AmE), / səʊː / (BrE)
used informally to introduce a statement, prompt more information, or express hesitation or doubt in conversation

Examples

Sooo, what now
Sooo, what do you want to do this weekend?
He paused and said, 'sooo, are you coming to the party or not?'
She looked uncertain and started with 'sooo...' before giving her answer.
