lay-by

noun
roadside stopping area for vehicles
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪˌbaɪ / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.baɪ / (BrE)
a designated paved area beside a main road where vehicles can stop temporarily, often for rest or emergencies

Examples

A lay-by beside road
The police asked the driver to stop in the nearest lay-by.
There was a food truck parked in the lay-by serving hot drinks.
We pulled into a lay-by to check the map and stretch our legs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rest area - an area at the side of a road where motorists can rest and use facilities
