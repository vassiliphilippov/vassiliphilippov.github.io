glorify

verb
give glory or great praise to
2.8 5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɔːrɪˌfaɪ /; / ˈɡlɔːrɪfaɪ /; / ˈɡlɔːr.əˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡlɒrɪˌfaɪ /; / ˈɡlɔːrɪfaɪ /; / ˈɡlɔː.rɪ.faɪ / (BrE)
to praise, honor, or make something or someone seem more admirable or important than they really are
