lanolin

noun
waxy substance from wool used in cosmetics
Pronunciation: / ˈlænəlɪn / (AmE), / ˈlanəlɪn / (BrE)
a yellow, waxy or fatty substance from sheep's wool, used in ointments, lotions, and skin creams to soften and protect the skin
