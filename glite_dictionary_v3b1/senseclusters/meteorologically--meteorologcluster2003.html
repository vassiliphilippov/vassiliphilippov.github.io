meteorologically

adverb
in relation to the weather
Pronunciation: / ˌmitiərəˈlɑdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˌmiːtiərəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to the weather or atmospheric conditions.

Examples

Used meteorologically
Meteorologically, last year was one of the driest on record.
Meteorologically, the area is prone to sudden storms throughout the summer months.
The decision was made because it was meteorologically unsafe for the flight to take off.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meteorological - relating to the weather or atmosphere
