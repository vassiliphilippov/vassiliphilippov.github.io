cremaster

noun
muscle in male groin
9.9
Pronunciation: / krɪˈmæstər / (AmE), / krɪˈmɑːstə / (BrE)
a thin muscle that raises and lowers the testicle in males to regulate temperature

Examples

Cremaster muscle movement
The cremaster muscle contracts to pull the testicle closer to the body in cold weather.
Doctors examined the function of the cremaster during the physical assessment.
In response to touch, the cremaster may cause the testicle to rise slightly.
