daydreamy

adjective
lost in thought
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪˌdriːmi / (AmE), / ˈdeɪˌdriːmi / (BrE)
having a dreamy, absent-minded, or imaginative quality, as if lost in thought or daydreams
