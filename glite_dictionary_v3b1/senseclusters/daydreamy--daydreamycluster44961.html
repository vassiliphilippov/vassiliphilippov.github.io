daydreamy

adjective
lost in thought
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪˌdriːmi / (AmE), / ˈdeɪˌdriːmi / (BrE)
having a dreamy, absent-minded, or imaginative quality, as if lost in thought or daydreams

Examples

Daydreamy expression
He had a daydreamy look on his face as he watched the clouds drift by.
Her daydreamy attitude sometimes made it hard for her to pay attention in class.
The artist's daydreamy style was reflected in the soft colors of her paintings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absent-minded - tending to be forgetful or distracted
  • synonym: dreamy - having a gentle, pleasant, or absent-minded quality
  • antonym: focused - concentrating on a particular task or thought
