displacer

noun
device or person that moves
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈpleɪsər / (AmE), / dɪsˈpleɪsə / (BrE)
a person or device that moves something from its usual or original place

Examples

Air displacer
The displacer in the engine helps regulate the flow of air.
A skilled displacer can move heavy furniture with ease.
The technician replaced the faulty displacer to restore the machine's function.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mover - a person or thing that moves something
  • synonym: remover - a person or thing that takes something away
