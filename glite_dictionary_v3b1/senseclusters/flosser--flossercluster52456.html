flosser

noun
show-off person
Pronunciation: / ˈflɑsər / (AmE), / ˈflɒsə / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who shows off their wealth, possessions, or style to impress others

Examples

A flashy flosser
He acted like a flosser at the party, showing off his designer clothes and expensive watch.
Everyone knew she was a flosser because she always talked about her new car and luxury vacations.
Instead of being humble, he preferred to be a flosser and brag about his wealth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braggart - a person who boasts about achievements or possessions
  • antonym: modest person - someone who does not show off or boast
  • synonym: show-off - a person who tries to attract attention by displaying their abilities or possessions
