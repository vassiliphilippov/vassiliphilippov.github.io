vibriosis

noun
bacterial infection
9.1
Pronunciation: /vɪb.rɪ.ˈoʊ.sɪs/ (AmE), /vɪb.rɪ.ˈəʊ.sɪs/ (BrE)
an infection caused by bacteria of the genus Vibrio, often contracted from contaminated water or seafood
