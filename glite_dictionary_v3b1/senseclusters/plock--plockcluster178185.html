plock

noun
small plot of land
an old or dialect word for a small plot of land or a tuft of grass

Examples

A grassy plock
The sheep grazed on a plock near the stream.
He inherited a tiny plock at the edge of the village.
Wildflowers grew in every plock along the hillside.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: field - an area of open land, especially for farming
  • synonym: patch - a small area of land or surface
