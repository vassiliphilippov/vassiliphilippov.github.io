Haile Selassie

proper noun
emperor of Ethiopia (1892–1975) and Rastafarian figure
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪli səˈlɑːsi / (AmE), / ˈhaɪli sɛˈlæsi / (BrE)
(1892–1975) Emperor of Ethiopia and prominent figure worshipped by Rastafarians, known for his leadership and contributions to Ethiopian history.

Examples

Haile Selassie Emperor Ethiopia
Haile Selassie addressed the League of Nations to condemn the Italian invasion of Ethiopia.
Many Rastafarians regard Haile Selassie as the returned messiah of the Bible.
The reign of Haile Selassie marked significant modernization reforms in Ethiopia.
