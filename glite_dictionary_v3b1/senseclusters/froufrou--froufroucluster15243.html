froufrou

noun
decorative frills
Pronunciation: / ˈfruˌfru / (AmE), / ˈfruːfruː / (BrE)
decorative frills or elaborate ornamentation, especially on clothing or accessories

Examples

Lace and froufrou
Her wedding gown was adorned with layers of froufrou and sparkling beads.
He preferred simple styles without any froufrou or extra decoration.
The boutique specialized in dresses full of ribbons, lace, and froufrou.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lace - a delicate decorative fabric
  • antonym: plainness - lack of decoration or ornamentation
  • synonym: ruffle - a strip of fabric gathered for decoration
