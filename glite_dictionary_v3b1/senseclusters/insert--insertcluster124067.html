Examples

Magazine insert
An insert showing the letter's contents helped the audience follow the plot.
During editing, several inserts were placed to clarify details for viewers.
The director decided to add an insert of the ticking clock for suspense.
Every issue contains a special insert with exclusive discounts for subscribers.
Please remove the promotional insert before recycling the newspaper.
The magazine arrived with a colorful insert advertising summer fashion.
Every hour, a commercial insert targets regional advertisers within the national broadcast.
The network left room for each affiliate to air their own insert.
The station played a short insert during the break for local news updates.
