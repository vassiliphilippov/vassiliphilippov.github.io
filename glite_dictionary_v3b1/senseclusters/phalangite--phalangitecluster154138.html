phalangite

noun
ancient Macedonian soldier
9.9
Pronunciation: / fəˈlæŋɡaɪt / (AmE), / fəˈlæŋɡaɪt / (BrE)

Examples

Alexander the Great relied on the discipline of each phalangite during his campaigns.
A single phalangite could hold his ground against several opponents thanks to his training.
