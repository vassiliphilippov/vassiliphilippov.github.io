kibble

noun
crafted taming food in games
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkɪb.əl/ (AmE), /ˈkɪb.əl/ (BrE)
(in video games, especially creature-taming survival games) a specially prepared food item made from particular ingredients and used to tame, attract, or improve the taming success of creatures; typically distinct from ordinary pet food and treated as a crafted in-game item.

Examples

Game kibble
In Ark: Survival Evolved, kibble is a crafted food used to tame high-level dinosaurs.
Players carefully mix rare ingredients to create kibble that gives the best chance to tame creatures.
The wiki listed recipes for each type of kibble, showing which eggs and meat produce optimal results.
