showerproof

adjective
resistant to light rain
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaʊərˌpruːf / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊəˌpruːf / (BrE)
able to resist light rain but not completely waterproof

Examples

Showerproof coat
He bought a showerproof coat for his trip to the rainy city.
The showerproof material kept her dry during the brief drizzle.
You should wear showerproof clothing if the forecast predicts only light rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: water-resistant - able to resist water to some extent but not entirely
  • degree higher: waterproof - completely impervious to water
