nonnomadic

adjective
not nomadic; settled
6.0 5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.noʊˈmædɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.nəʊˈmædɪk / (BrE)
(of a person, group, or lifestyle) not nomadic; settled or not given to wandering.

Examples

A nonnomadic lifestyle
Many ancient civilizations were nonnomadic and built permanent settlements.
The census distinguishes between nonnomadic residents and those with migratory lifestyles.
Unlike their ancestors, today's community members are largely nonnomadic and remain in one region.
