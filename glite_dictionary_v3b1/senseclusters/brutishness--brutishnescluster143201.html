brutishness

noun
crudeness or stupidity
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːtɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈbruːtɪʃnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being crude, coarse, or lacking intelligence and refinement

Examples

Social brutishness
The brutishness of his behavior shocked everyone at the formal dinner.
Some critics argued that the film glorified the brutishness of its main character.
She was disappointed by the brutishness displayed during the heated debate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coarseness - the quality of being rough or lacking refinement
  • antonym: refinement - the quality of being cultured or polished
  • synonym: stupidity - the quality of being unintelligent or lacking sense
