unsentimentally

adverb
without emotion or sentiment
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌsɛn.tɪˈmɛn.t̬əli / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌsɛn.tɪˈmen.təli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows little or no emotion or sentiment; without romanticizing or emotional involvement.

Examples

To unsentimentally decide
He unsentimentally decided to sell the family home after his parents passed away.
She unsentimentally packed away the old photographs and donated them to charity.
The detective unsentimentally described the facts of the case in court.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sentimentally - in a sentimental manner; with emotion or sentiment
