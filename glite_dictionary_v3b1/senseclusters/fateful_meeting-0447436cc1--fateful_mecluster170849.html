fateful meeting

noun
significant or life-changing encounter
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪtfəl ˈmiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfeɪtfəl ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
an encounter that has a significant or decisive impact on future events, often changing the course of one's life

Examples
