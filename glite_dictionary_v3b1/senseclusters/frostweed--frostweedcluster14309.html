frostweed

noun
a North American plant that forms ice crystals at first frost
4.9 8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈfrɔːst.wiːd/ /; / /ˈfrɑːst.wiːd/ (AmE), /ˈfrɒst.wiːd/ (BrE)
a perennial plant native to eastern North America, known for its early yellow flowers and the formation of ice crystals on its stems during the first frosts

Examples

Frostweed ice ribbons
Botanists are fascinated by how frostweed produces delicate ribbons of ice during the season's first frost.
