stiff-backed

adjective
having a rigid or inflexible back
1.8 6.9
Pronunciation: / stɪfˈbækt / (AmE), / stɪfˈbækt / (BrE)
having a straight, rigid, or inflexible back or posture; often indicating formality or severity

Examples

A stiff-backed person

The stiff-backed chair was uncomfortable after sitting for an hour.
The soldiers remained stiff-backed throughout the entire ceremony.
