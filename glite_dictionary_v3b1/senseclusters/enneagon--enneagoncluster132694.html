enneagon

noun
nine-sided polygon
Pronunciation: / ˈɛniəˌɡɑn / (AmE), / ˈɛniəˌɡɒn / (BrE)
a flat geometric shape with nine straight sides and nine angles

Examples

A enneagon shape
The math teacher asked the students to draw an enneagon on their graph paper.
Each side of the enneagon was exactly five centimeters long.
Architects sometimes use the shape of an enneagon in modern building designs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonagon - a polygon with nine sides and nine angles
