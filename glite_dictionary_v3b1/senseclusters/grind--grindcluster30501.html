grind

noun
the act of grinding into powder
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡraɪnd / (AmE), / ɡraɪnd / (BrE)
the act or process of reducing something to powder or small fragments by crushing or grinding

Examples

A grind of spices
After the grind of the coffee beans, the kitchen was filled with a rich scent.
The final grind of the spices produced a fine, aromatic powder.
The process includes a grind before mixing the ingredients together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pulverization - the act of crushing or grinding something into very small particles
