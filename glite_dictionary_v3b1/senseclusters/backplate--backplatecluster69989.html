backplate

noun
rigid plate on an electronic device
6.4
Pronunciation: / c8bkccple6at / (AmE), / c8bkccple6at / (BrE)
A rigid plate attached to the rear of an electronic device or component (for example a graphics card, smartphone, or case panel) that protects, supports, or helps dissipate heat.

Examples

Device backplate
The new graphics card features a brushed aluminum backplate for protection and improved cooling.
She removed the backplate from her phone to replace the battery and clean the contacts.
Many custom PC builders add decorative RGB lighting to a metal backplate to personalize their rigs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heat sink - a device or component that conducts and dissipates heat away from electronic parts
  • synonym: plate - a flat or curved piece of metal or other material used as a covering or support
