elemi

noun
fragrant resin from certain tropical trees
Pronunciation: / ˈɛləˌmi / (AmE), / ˈɛləmi / (BrE)
a fragrant resin obtained from certain tropical trees, especially of the genus Canarium, used in incense, varnishes, and perfumes

Examples

Elemi resin
Harvesters collect elemi resin from the tree trunks during the dry season.
The artist mixed elemi into his varnish to give it a glossy finish.
You can smell the subtle aroma of elemi in this traditional incense blend.
