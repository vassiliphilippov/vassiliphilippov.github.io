acquaintanceship

noun
less intimate relationship than friendship
Pronunciation: / əˈkweɪntənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / əˈkweɪntənʃɪp / (BrE)
a relationship or association with someone that is less close or intimate than a friendship

Examples

A acquaintanceship with neighbors
He valued their acquaintanceship, even though they were not close friends.
Our acquaintanceship began at a conference several years ago.
Their acquaintanceship grew over time as they met at more social events.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: friendship - the state of being friends; a close relationship of mutual affection
