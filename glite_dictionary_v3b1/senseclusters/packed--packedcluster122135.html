packed

adjective
extremely crowded or full
Pronunciation: / pækt / (AmE), / pækt / (BrE)
extremely crowded or filled to capacity; having no room for more people or things

Examples

Packed with people
The concert hall was packed with enthusiastic fans last night.
The subway trains are always packed during rush hour.
We tried to get a seat at the restaurant, but it was absolutely packed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: empty - containing nothing; not filled or occupied
  • synonym: jam-packed - extremely full or crowded
