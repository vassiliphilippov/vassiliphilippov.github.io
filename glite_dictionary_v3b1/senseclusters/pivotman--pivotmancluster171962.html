pivotman

noun
central player
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪvətˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈpɪvətˌman / (BrE)
a player or person who serves as the central point around which others move or operate, especially in sports or teamwork

Examples

Team pivotman
The pivotman passed the ball quickly to his teammate during the fast break.
In the relay team, the pivotman coordinated the handoffs to ensure smooth transitions.
As the pivotman of the group, she kept everyone organized and focused on the task.

Related Terms

  • synonym: center - the central player or position in a team or group
  • synonym: leader - a person who leads or directs a group
