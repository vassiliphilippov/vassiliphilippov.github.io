twistiness

noun
curviness or winding
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪstinəs / (AmE), / ˈtwɪstinəs / (BrE)
the quality of having many twists, turns, or curves

Examples

Related Terms

  • antonym: rigidity - the quality of being stiff and not bending or twisting easily
  • antonym: straightness - the quality of being straight, without bends or curves
