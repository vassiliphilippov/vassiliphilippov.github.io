cryptomnesia

noun
mistaken original memory
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɪptoʊmˈniːʒə / (AmE), / ˌkrɪptəmˈniːziə / (BrE)
the phenomenon where you believe an idea is original, but it is actually a forgotten memory, leading you to unintentionally copy someone else's idea
