reticuloendothelial

adjective
immune cell system
Pronunciation: / rɪˌtɪkjuloʊˌɛndoʊˈθiːliəl / (AmE), / rɪˌtɪkjʊləʊˌɛndəʊˈθiːliəl / (BrE)
relating to a network of cells and tissues involved in immune defense and phagocytosis in the body

Examples

The reticuloendothelial system
The reticuloendothelial system plays a key role in removing bacteria from the bloodstream.
Doctors studied the reticuloendothelial response to the new vaccine in laboratory animals.
Certain diseases can weaken the reticuloendothelial function, making infections more likely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immune - relating to the body's defense against disease
  • synonym: phagocytic - relating to cells that engulf and digest particles
