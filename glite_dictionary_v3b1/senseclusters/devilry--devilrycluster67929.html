devilry

noun
wicked or cruel behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛvəlri / (AmE), / ˈdɛvəlri /; / ˈdɛv(ə)lri / (BrE)
wicked, cruel, or mischievous behavior; actions showing malice, evil intent, or playful mischief
