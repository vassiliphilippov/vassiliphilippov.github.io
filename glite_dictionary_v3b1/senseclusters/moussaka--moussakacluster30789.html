moussaka

noun
Greek/Middle Eastern baked dish with eggplant and meat
9.2 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmuː.sə.kə/ /; / /ˈmuː.sɑː.kə/ (AmE), /ˈmuː.sə.kə/ /; / /ˈmuː.sɑː.kə/ (BrE)
A baked dish of eggplant and ground meat, usually lamb, layered with tomatoes, onions, and spices, and topped with a creamy white sauce and eggs.

Examples

Traditional moussaka dish
