Synonyms

  • dispersal
    5.2 2.04/m
    dispersal is the act of spreading things widely over an area, while dispersion names that same general process of scattering.
  • scattering
    4.2 0.27/m
    scattering is the act of sending things outward in many directions, while dispersion is the general process of spreading over an area.
  • scatter
    4.0 0.97/m
    scatter often means a random or disorderly spreading of items in space, while dispersion is a neutral term for spreading.
  • diffusion
    5.8 2.30/m
    diffusion describes slow mixing or movement of particles or ideas through a medium, while dispersion covers general spreading.
  • distribution
    3.0 18/m
    distribution focuses on how things are shared or arranged among places or people, while dispersion means things are spread out.
  • spreading
    2.3 0.02/m
    spreading refers to the action of extending or covering a larger area over time, while dispersion emphasizes scattering widely.
  • spread
    2.5 9.56/m
    spread is the result or area covered when something increases or extends, while dispersion highlights the act of scattering widely.
  • dissemination
    6.6 3.51/m
    dissemination means spreading information or ideas widely to people, while dispersion refers to physical or general scattering across an area.
