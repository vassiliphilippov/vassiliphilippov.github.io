career path

noun
sequence of jobs in one's career
1.0
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɪr pæθ / (AmE), / kəˈrɪə pɑːθ / (BrE)
the sequence of jobs and positions that a person holds during their working life, showing progression and development in their profession

Examples

A career path plan
Choosing a career path early can help you set clear goals for the future.
Many people change their career paths several times as their interests evolve.
She wanted to follow a career path in medicine, starting as a nurse and eventually becoming a doctor.
