lucency

noun
light area on X-ray
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈluːsənsi / (AmE), / ˈluːsənsi / (BrE)
an area on an X-ray or radiographic image that appears lighter because it is less dense than surrounding tissue

Examples

X-ray lucency
The radiologist identified a lucency in the patient's left lung on the X-ray.
A small lucency was visible near the bone fracture, suggesting possible infection.
Doctors monitor any changes in lucency to assess the progress of healing.
