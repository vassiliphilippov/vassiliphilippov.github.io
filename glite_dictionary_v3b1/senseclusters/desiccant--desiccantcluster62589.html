desiccant

noun
substance used to absorb moisture
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛsɪkənt / (AmE), / ˈdɛsɪkənt / (BrE)
A substance that absorbs moisture and promotes drying, commonly used to keep environments or products dry.

Examples

A desiccant packet
Replace the desiccant regularly to maintain optimal humidity levels.
Silica gel is often used as a desiccant in packaging to prevent moisture damage.
The canisters contain a desiccant to keep the contents dry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drying agent - substance used to remove water or moisture
