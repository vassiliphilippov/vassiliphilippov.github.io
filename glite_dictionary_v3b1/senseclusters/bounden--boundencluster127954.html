bounden

adjective
morally obligatory; required by duty
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊndən / (AmE), / ˈbaʊndən / (BrE)
(of a duty, responsibility, etc.) required by moral or legal obligation; obligatory.

Examples

Bounden duty
As a citizen, it is your bounden responsibility to obey the law.
He felt it was his bounden duty to care for his elderly parents.
She regarded honesty as her bounden obligation, no matter the circumstances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obligatory - required by a legal, moral, or other rule; compulsory
