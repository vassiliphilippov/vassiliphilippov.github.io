man hour

noun
unit of work (one person for one hour)
9.9
Pronunciation: / mæn ˌaʊɚ / (AmE), / mæn ˌaʊə / (BrE)
a unit of measurement equal to the amount of work done by one person in one hour, used especially for planning and estimating labor in projects or industry

Examples

Save man hours
Reducing errors could save significant man hours every month.
The project is expected to take over 500 man hours to complete.
The repair required about 20 man hours from the maintenance team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: work hour - an hour spent working; general time unit for labor
