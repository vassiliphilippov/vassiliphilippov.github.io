carpel

noun
female reproductive organ of a flower
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːr.pəl / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.pəl / (BrE)
the female reproductive organ of a flower, consisting of an ovary, a stigma, and typically a style, and forming part of or a simple pistil

Examples

A carpel in flower
Each sunflower contains hundreds of carpels arranged in a spiraled pattern.
Some flowers have only one carpel, while others have several fused together.
The scientist examined the structure of the carpel under a microscope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pistil - the female reproductive part of a flower, consisting of one or more carpels
