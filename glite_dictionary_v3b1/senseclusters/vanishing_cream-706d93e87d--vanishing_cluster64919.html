vanishing cream

noun
cosmetic cream for softening and cleaning skin
4.0 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈvæn.ɪ.ʃɪŋ kriːm/ (AmE), /ˈvæn.ɪ.ʃɪŋ kriːm/ (BrE)
A lightly textured cosmetic cream that is applied to the skin to soften and clean it, typically leaving no visible residue.

Examples

Vanishing cream for skin
After washing her face, she used vanishing cream as part of her nightly skincare routine.
Many early advertisements promoted vanishing cream as an essential beauty product for women.
She applied a layer of vanishing cream to her face every morning to keep her skin soft.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cold cream - a thick, oily cream used to cleanse or moisturize the skin
