chattering

noun
high-pitched animal noise
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæt̬.ɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃæt.ər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
a high-pitched, continuous noise, especially that made by birds or monkeys

Examples

Bird chattering
The constant chattering of the monkeys echoed through the rainforest canopy.
The zoo was filled with the excited chattering of parrots and other tropical birds.
We woke up to the chattering of sparrows outside our window at dawn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chirping - short, sharp, high-pitched sounds made by small birds or insects
  • synonym: twittering - continuous light, high-pitched sounds made by birds
