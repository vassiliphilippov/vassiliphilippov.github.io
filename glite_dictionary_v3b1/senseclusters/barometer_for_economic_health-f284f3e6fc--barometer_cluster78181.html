barometer for economic health

noun
indicator of economic conditions
Pronunciation: / bəˈrɑːmɪtər fər iːkəˈnɑːmɪk hɛlθ / (AmE), / bəˈrɒmɪtə fə ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪk hɛlθ / (BrE)
An indicator or measure of a country's economic conditions or financial wellbeing.

Examples

A barometer for economic health
Analysts view consumer spending as a key barometer for economic health in the country.

Unemployment rates are often used as a barometer for economic health.
