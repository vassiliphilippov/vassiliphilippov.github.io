clairaudient

adjective
perceiving supernatural sounds
Pronunciation: / ˌklɛrˈɔdiənt / (AmE), / ˌkleəˈɔːdiənt / (BrE)
able to hear sounds or voices that are believed to come from beyond normal human perception, especially from spirits or supernatural sources

Examples

Clairaudient medium
The medium claimed she was clairaudient and could hear messages from the spirit world.
Some people believe that being clairaudient allows them to receive guidance from unseen sources.
He described his clairaudient experiences as hearing voices no one else could hear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clairaudience - the supposed ability to hear supernatural sounds or voices
  • synonym: clairvoyant - able to perceive things beyond normal sensory contact
