plainie

noun
simple and unpretentious style or approach
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪni / (AmE), / ˈpleɪni / (BrE)
A simple, unpretentious way of living, dressing, or presenting oneself.

Examples

Their home has a plainie feel with minimal decoration and simple furnishings.
