scrape

verb
automatically extract web data
5.3
Pronunciation: /skreɪp/ (AmE), /skreɪp/ (BrE)
(in computing) to extract data from websites or other online sources, typically using automated scripts or tools to collect structured information from web pages.

Examples

To scrape website data
Researchers scrape data from public websites to analyze consumer trends and real-time behavior.
The startup scrapes thousands of product pages daily to build its searchable database.
Before scraping any site, she checked the robots.txt file and the site's terms of service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extract - to obtain or remove data from a larger set or source
  • synonym: harvest - to collect large amounts of data automatically from web sources
