tight

adverb
firmly or closely (adverb)
1.3
Pronunciation: / taɪt / (AmE), / taɪt / (BrE)
in a way that is firmly or closely fixed in place; not loosely

Examples

Hold tight
He gripped her hand tight as they crossed the street.
She held the lid on tight so nothing could spill out.
The door was shut tight against the wind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: firmly - in a strong and secure way
  • antonym: loosely - in a way that is not tight or firmly fixed
