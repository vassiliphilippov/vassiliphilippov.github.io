zombi

noun
supernaturally reanimated corpse
Pronunciation: / ˈzɑːm.bi / (AmE), / ˈzɒm.bi / (BrE)
a dead body brought back to life by supernatural means, or a spirit or supernatural force believed to reanimate the dead (especially in folklore or voodoo)

Examples

A zombi rises
In the story, a zombi stalks the villagers at night after rising from its grave.
Legend has it that a witch doctor can turn a corpse into a zombi with dark magic.
Many horror films feature a zombi as a terrifying central figure.
According to the myth, a zombi was responsible for bringing the villager back to life.
Some practitioners in Haiti believe in the power of the zombi to control the living.
Stories of the zombi have influenced literature and film around the world.
