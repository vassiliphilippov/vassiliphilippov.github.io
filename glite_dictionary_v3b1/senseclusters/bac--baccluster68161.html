bac

noun
French school exam
Pronunciation: / bæk / (AmE), / bæk / (BrE)
the French national examination taken at the end of high school for graduation and university admission

Examples

Pass the bac
After passing the bac, he was able to apply to several French universities.
Many students feel nervous before taking the bac at the end of high school.
She celebrated with her friends after receiving her bac results.
