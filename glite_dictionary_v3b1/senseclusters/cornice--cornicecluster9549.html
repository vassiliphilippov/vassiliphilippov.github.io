cornice

noun
ornamental frame concealing curtain fixtures
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnɪs / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnɪs / (BrE)
a decorative molding or horizontal framework at the top of a wall, window, or building, often used to hide curtain fixtures or as an architectural feature

Examples

The elaborate cornice in the dining room added a touch of elegance to the space.
The historic building features a beautifully detailed cornice running around the upper edge of every room.
