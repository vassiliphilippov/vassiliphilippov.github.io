besan

noun
chickpea flour
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪsɑn / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsən / (BrE)
a flour made from ground chickpeas, commonly used in South Asian cooking

Examples

Ground besan flour
She made fritters using besan and spices for a traditional snack.
Many Indian sweets are prepared with besan as a main ingredient.
You can use besan to make gluten-free pancakes and savory dishes.
