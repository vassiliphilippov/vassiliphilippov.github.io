overbearingness

noun
domineering or arrogant quality
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈberɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈbeərɪŋnəs / (BrE)

Examples

The overbearingness of the teacher stifled the students' creativity.
