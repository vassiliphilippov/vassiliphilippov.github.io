Albuca

noun
genus of South African bulbous plants
Pronunciation: / ælˈbjuːkə / (AmE), / ælˈbjuːkə / (BrE)
a genus of bulbous flowering plants native to South Africa, sometimes placed in the subfamily Hyacinthaceae

Examples

Albuca plant
Gardeners often choose Albuca for its attractive spiral leaves and easy cultivation.
Several varieties of Albuca can bloom indoors when given plenty of sunlight.
The Albuca genus includes several species prized for their delicate, nodding flowers.
