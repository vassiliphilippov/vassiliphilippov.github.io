dummy

noun
an ignorant or foolish person
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌmi / (AmE), / ˈdʌmi / (BrE)
a person who is regarded as stupid or lacking intelligence

Examples

He is a dummy
I felt like such a dummy when I answered the question incorrectly.
Only a real dummy would forget their keys three times in a row.
Why did you do that, you dummy?

Related Terms

  • synonym: fool - a person lacking good sense or judgment
  • antonym: genius - a very intelligent or gifted person
  • synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
