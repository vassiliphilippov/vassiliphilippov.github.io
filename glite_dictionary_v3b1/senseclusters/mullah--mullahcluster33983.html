mullah

noun
Muslim religious leader or scholar
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmʊl.ə / (AmE), / ˈmʌl.ə / (BrE)
a Muslim religious leader or scholar, especially one trained in Islamic law and doctrine; often the head of a mosque

Examples

A mullah in mosque
After years of study, he became a respected mullah in his community.
Many villagers rely on the mullah for guidance on religious and legal matters.
The mullah delivered a powerful sermon to the congregation during Friday prayers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imam - a Muslim leader of prayer in a mosque
