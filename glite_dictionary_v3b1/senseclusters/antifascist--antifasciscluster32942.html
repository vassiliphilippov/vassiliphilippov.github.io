antifascist

noun
opponent of fascism
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈfæʃɪst / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈfæʃɪst / (BrE)
a person who actively opposes fascism or fascist ideologies

Examples

Antifascist protester
The antifascist spoke passionately at the rally against authoritarianism.
Many antifascists joined together to organize community events promoting democracy.
As an outspoken antifascist, she often participated in peaceful protests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anti-Nazi - a person who opposes Nazi ideology
  • antonym: fascist - a person who supports fascism
