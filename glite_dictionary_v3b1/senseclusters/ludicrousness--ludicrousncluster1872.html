ludicrousness

noun
absurd quality
Pronunciation: / ˈluːdɪkrəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈluːdɪkrəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being so absurd or ridiculous that it causes laughter or disbelief

Examples

The ludicrousness of excuse
The ludicrousness of his excuse made it impossible for anyone to believe him.
She laughed at the sheer ludicrousness of the plan they had come up with.
Critics pointed out the ludicrousness of the movie's plot twists.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absurdity - the quality of being absurd or nonsensical
  • synonym: ridiculousness - the state of being ridiculous or laughable
  • antonym: seriousness - the quality of being serious or solemn
