overreaching

adjective
excessively ambitious; overconfident
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈritʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈriːtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
having excessive ambition or self-confidence; attempting to achieve more than is reasonable or possible

Examples

Overreaching ambition
His overreaching ambitions ultimately led to his downfall.
She was warned that her overreaching goals might be impossible to accomplish in such a short time.
The company’s overreaching expansion plans stretched its resources too thin.
