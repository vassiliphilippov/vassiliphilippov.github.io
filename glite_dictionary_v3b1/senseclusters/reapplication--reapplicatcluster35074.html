reapplication

noun
applying again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of applying something again, especially after its effect has worn off or been removed

Examples

Reapplication of sunscreen
After swimming, the reapplication of sunscreen is necessary to protect your skin.
The instructions recommend the reapplication of the adhesive every few hours for best results.
If the stain persists, a reapplication of the cleaning solution may be needed.
