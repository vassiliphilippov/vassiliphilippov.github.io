chalk up

verb
record a score or achievement
8.5 3.8
Pronunciation: /tʃɑːk ʌp/ /; / /tʃɔːk ʌp/ (AmE), /tʃɔːk ʌp/ (BrE)
to achieve, gain, or record a point, score, victory, or amount (especially in a game, competition, or as an accomplishment)
