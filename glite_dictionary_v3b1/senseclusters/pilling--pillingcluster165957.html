pilling

noun
making pills
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of forming a substance into small, solid pills, especially in pharmaceutical manufacturing

Examples

Medicine pilling process
Advances in pilling have made it easier to produce consistent doses of medication.
The pharmaceutical company invested in new pilling equipment to improve efficiency.
Quality control is essential during the pilling stage to ensure each pill meets standards.

  • synonym: tableting - the process of forming substances into tablets
