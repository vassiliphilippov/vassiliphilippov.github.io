whizbang

noun
high-velocity artillery shell
9.9 8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪz.bæŋ/ (AmE), /ˈwɪz.bæŋ/ (BrE)
an explosive device (such as an artillery shell or firecracker) that makes a whizzing sound followed by a bang
