colossus

noun
giant statue or figure
6.7
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɑːsəs / (AmE), / kəˈlɒsəs / (BrE)
a very large statue or something or someone of enormous size, power, or importance

Examples

The ancient colossus
The ancient colossus stood at the entrance to the harbor, welcoming sailors from afar.
He became a colossus in the world of technology, admired by millions.
The new bridge is a modern colossus stretching across the wide river.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dwarf - a person or thing of unusually small size
  • synonym: giant - a being or thing of great size or power
