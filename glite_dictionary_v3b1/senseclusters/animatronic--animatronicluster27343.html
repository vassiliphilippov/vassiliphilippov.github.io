animatronic

noun
robotic figure
7.2
Pronunciation: /æn.ə.mə.ˈtrɑː.nɪk/ /; / /æn.ɪ.mə.ˈtrɑː.nɪk/ (AmE), /æn.ə.mə.ˈtrɒ.nɪk/ /; / /æn.ɪ.mə.ˈtrɒ.nɪk/ (BrE)
a mechanically or electronically controlled model or figure designed to move in a lifelike way, often used for entertainment or display

Examples

A animatronic dinosaur
The museum featured a life-sized animatronic dinosaur that roared and moved its tail.
Children were fascinated by the animatronic animals in the theme park's jungle ride.
The movie used a realistic animatronic to create the illusion of a living alien creature.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: puppet - a movable model of a person or animal controlled by strings or hands
  • synonym: robot - a machine capable of carrying out complex actions automatically
