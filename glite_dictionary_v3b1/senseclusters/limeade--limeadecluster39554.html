limeade

noun
sweetened lime juice beverage
Pronunciation: / laɪˈmeɪd / (AmE), / laɪˈmeɪd / (BrE)
A sweetened drink made with lime juice and water, often served chilled.

Examples

A glass of limeade
On a hot summer day, nothing is more refreshing than a glass of limeade.
She added fresh mint leaves to the pitcher of limeade for extra flavor.
The restaurant serves homemade limeade alongside their signature tacos.
