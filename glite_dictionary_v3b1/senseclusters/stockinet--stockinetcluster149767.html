stockinet

noun
soft knitted fabric for clothes
Pronunciation: / ˌstɑk.əˈnɛt / (AmE), / ˌstɒk.ɪˈnɛt / (BrE)
A soft, loosely knitted fabric, especially used for infants' clothing and undergarments.

Examples

Soft stockinet fabric
Her undergarments were made of breathable stockinet for extra comfort.
The newborn was wrapped in a soft stockinet blanket.
Traditional stockinet is often used in hospitals to cover dressings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jersey - a plain knitted fabric, often used for clothing
