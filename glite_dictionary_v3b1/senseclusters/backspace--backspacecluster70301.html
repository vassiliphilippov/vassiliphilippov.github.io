backspace

noun
a keyboard key that moves the cursor back
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌspeɪs / (AmE), / ˈbæk.speɪs /; / ˈbækˌspeɪs / (BrE)
the key on a keyboard or typewriter that moves the cursor backward or deletes the previous character
