sunfall

noun
mythic sun disaster
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌfɔl / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌfɔːl / (BrE)
a fictional or mythic event in which the sun falls from the sky, is destroyed, or brings about catastrophe

Examples

The great sunfall
The ancient legend warned of a sunfall that would plunge the world into eternal darkness.
In the story, civilization collapses after a sudden sunfall devastates the planet.
Artists have depicted the mythical sunfall as a moment of both terror and beauty.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apocalypse - a catastrophic event marking the end of the world
  • synonym: doomsday - the last day of the world's existence
