musical

noun
play or film with singing and dancing
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuː.zɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˈmjuː.zɪ.kəl / (BrE)
A play or film in which singing and dancing are prominent features alongside dialogue and action.

Examples

A Broadway musical
Her favorite musical is 'The Phantom of the Opera', which she has seen three times.
The school's annual musical featured talented students singing and dancing on stage.
We bought tickets to see the new musical on Broadway next month.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: opera - a dramatic work combining text and musical score, usually entirely sung
