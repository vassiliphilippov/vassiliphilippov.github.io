Thanksgiving

proper noun
annual holiday in the US and Canada with family feasts
Pronunciation: / θæŋksˈɡɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / θæŋksˈɡɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
An annual national holiday celebrated in the United States on the fourth Thursday of November and in Canada on the second Monday of October, originally commemorating a harvest feast held in 1621 by the Pilgrims and the Wampanoag, now observed with family gatherings and meals.

Examples

Thanksgiving Day
Many people travel long distances to visit relatives for Thanksgiving.
The Thanksgiving parade is a beloved holiday tradition in many cities.
We always celebrate Thanksgiving with a big family dinner.
