prover

noun
testing device
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpruː.vər/ (AmE), /ˈpruː.və/ (BrE)
a device used to test or calibrate the accuracy of measuring instruments, especially gas or water meters

Examples

Meter prover
The technician used a prover to ensure the water meter was reading correctly.
Before installation, each gas meter was checked with a prover for accuracy.
A portable prover is essential for field calibration of industrial meters.
