hopped-up

adjective
vehicle with engine modified for more power
Pronunciation: / hɑpt ˈʌp / (AmE), / hɒpt ˈʌp / (BrE)
(of a vehicle) having its engine modified to increase power and performance.

Examples

Hopped-up car engine
He drove a hopped-up Mustang that left everyone behind at the lights.
The teenagers spent the weekend working on their hopped-up hot rod.
With its hopped-up engine, the car was built for speed.
