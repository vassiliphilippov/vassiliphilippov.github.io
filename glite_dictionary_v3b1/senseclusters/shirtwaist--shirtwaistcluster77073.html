shirtwaist

noun
woman's buttoned blouse
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɜrtˌweɪst / (AmE), / ˈʃɜːtˌweɪst / (BrE)
a woman's blouse or dress with a fitted waist, styled like a shirt, especially popular in the early 20th century
