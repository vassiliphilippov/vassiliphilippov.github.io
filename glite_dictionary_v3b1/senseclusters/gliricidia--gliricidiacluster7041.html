gliricidia

noun
genus of small poisonous flowering trees
3.5
Pronunciation: / ɡlɪˌrɪsɪˈdiːə / (AmE), / ɡlɪˌrɪsɪˈdiːə / (BrE)
A genus of small deciduous trees native to Central America, valued for their durable dark wood and dense clusters of pink, nectar-rich flowers; all plant parts are poisonous.
