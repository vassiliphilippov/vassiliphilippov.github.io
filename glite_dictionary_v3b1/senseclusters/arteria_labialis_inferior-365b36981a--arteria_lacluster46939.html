arteria labialis inferior

noun
artery supplying the lower lip
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˈtɪriə læˈbeɪli.əs ɪnˈfɪri.ər / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪərɪə læˈbeɪlɪ.əs ɪnˈfɪəri.ə / (BrE)
A branch of the facial artery that supplies blood to the lower lip.

Examples

Arteria labialis inferior branch
An injury to the arteria labialis inferior can cause significant bleeding in the lower lip.
During surgery on the mouth, the arteria labialis inferior must be carefully preserved.
The arteria labialis inferior branches from the facial artery to supply the lower lip.
