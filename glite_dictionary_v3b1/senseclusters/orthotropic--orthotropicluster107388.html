orthotropic

adjective
different properties by direction
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɔːr.θoʊ.ˈtrɑː.pɪk/ (AmE), /ɔː.θəʊ.ˈtrɒp.ɪk/ (BrE)
having different physical or mechanical properties in three mutually perpendicular directions

Examples

Orthotropic material properties
The engineer explained that orthotropic materials are essential for certain bridge designs.

The new composite panel is highly orthotropic, making it suitable for aerospace applications.
