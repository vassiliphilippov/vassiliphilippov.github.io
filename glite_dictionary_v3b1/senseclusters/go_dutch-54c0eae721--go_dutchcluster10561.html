go Dutch

verb
share expenses equally
8.5
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ ˈdʌtʃ /; / ɡoʊ dʌtʃ / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ˈdʌtʃ /; / ɡəʊ dʌtʃ / (BrE)
to share the cost of something, especially a meal, so that each person pays for themselves
