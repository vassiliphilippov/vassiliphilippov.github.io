uncommissioned

adjective
not formally requested
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəˈmɪʃənd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəˈmɪʃənd / (BrE)
not created for or requested by a client or authority; produced without a formal commission

Examples

Uncommissioned artwork
The artist displayed several uncommissioned sculptures at the local gallery.
He wrote an uncommissioned article about the city's history for his own enjoyment.
Many street murals are uncommissioned works created without official approval.

Related Terms

  • antonym: commissioned - formally requested or authorized, especially for a work of art or project
  • synonym: unsolicited - not asked for or requested
