bodice ripper

noun
romantic novel with passionate or forceful scenes
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑdɪs ˌrɪpər / (AmE), / ˈbɒdɪs ˌrɪpə / (BrE)
A romantic novel, typically set in the past, that includes scenes of passion and physical intimacy, often with elements of sexual force or violation.

Examples

A bodice ripper novel
Critics have debated whether the modern bodice ripper empowers or objectifies its heroines.
She enjoys reading a bodice ripper now and then, especially on rainy afternoons.
The new historical bodice ripper shot to the top of the bestseller lists within weeks of its release.

Related Terms

  • synonym: romance novel - a novel primarily focused on romantic relationships
