uncleanly

adjective
habitually unclean or dirty
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈklinli / (AmE), / ʌnˈkliːnli / (BrE)
habitually unclean or dirty, especially in habits or ways of living.

Examples

His uncleanly habits
She found his uncleanly ways difficult to tolerate in a shared apartment.
Some animals are considered uncleanly because of their living conditions.
The uncleanly habits of the tenants caused issues throughout the building.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unclean - not clean; dirty
