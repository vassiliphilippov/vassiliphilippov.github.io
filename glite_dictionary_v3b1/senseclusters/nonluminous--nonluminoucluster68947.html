nonluminous

adjective
not emitting light
6.8
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈluːmɪnəs / (AmE), / nɒnˈluːmɪnəs / (BrE)
not emitting light; not shining or glowing by its own source

Examples

A nonluminous object
The moon is a nonluminous object and shines only by reflecting sunlight.
Most planets in our solar system are nonluminous and can be seen only when illuminated by the sun.
Unlike stars, nonluminous bodies do not produce their own light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dark - having little or no light
  • antonym: luminous - emitting or giving off light
