waterworn

adjective
smoothed or shaped by water
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtərˌwɔrn / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌwɔːn / (BrE)
(especially of rocks) worn smooth or shaped by the action of water over time.

Examples

Waterworn rocks
Ancient tools made from waterworn pebbles were found at the archaeological site.
She collected waterworn shells and rocks during her walk on the beach.
The waterworn stones glistened in the bright sunlight along the riverbank.
