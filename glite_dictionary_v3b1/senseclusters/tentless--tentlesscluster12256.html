tentless

adjective
without a tent
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛntləs / (AmE), / ˈtɛntləs / (BrE)
without a tent; lacking the shelter or protection of a tent

Examples

tentless campers
After the storm, many campers were left tentless and had to sleep under the stars.
The hikers became tentless when their equipment was lost during the river crossing.
A sudden gust of wind left the festival-goers tentless for the night.

  • synonym: shelterless - without any shelter or protection
