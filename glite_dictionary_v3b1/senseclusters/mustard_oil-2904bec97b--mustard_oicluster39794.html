mustard oil

noun
oil extracted from mustard seeds
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌstərd ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈmʌstəd ɔɪl / (BrE)
a yellowish oil obtained from the seeds of the mustard plant, used in cooking, massage, and in making soap

Examples

Mustard oil for cooking
Soap manufacturers sometimes replace animal fats with mustard oil as a plant-based alternative.
The chef prefers to cook spicy dishes using mustard oil because of its pungent flavor.
Traditional Indian medicine often uses mustard oil for therapeutic massage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: olive oil - oil extracted from olives
  • degree higher: vegetable oil - oil extracted from plants, used in cooking and industry
