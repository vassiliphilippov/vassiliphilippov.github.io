forthrightly

adverb
in a direct and straightforward way
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrθˌraɪtli / (AmE), / ˈfɔːθˌraɪtli / (BrE)
In a direct, straightforward, and honest manner, without evasion or ambiguity.

Examples

Speak forthrightly
If you address the issue forthrightly, people are more likely to respect your opinion.
She answered all of the interview questions forthrightly, impressing the panel with her honesty.
The manager spoke forthrightly about the company's challenges during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: directly - in a straight or honest way, without evasion
  • antonym: evasively - in an indirect or ambiguous manner, avoiding the point
