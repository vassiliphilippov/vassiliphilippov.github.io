forthrightly

adverb
in a direct and straightforward way
9.9 5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrθˌraɪtli / (AmE), / ˈfɔːθˌraɪtli / (BrE)

Examples

Speak forthrightly

She answered all of the interview questions forthrightly, impressing the panel with her honesty.
The manager spoke forthrightly about the company's challenges during the meeting.
