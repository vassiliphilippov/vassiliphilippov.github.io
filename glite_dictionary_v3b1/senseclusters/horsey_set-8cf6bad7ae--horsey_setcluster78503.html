horsey set

noun
group keen on horses and riding
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrsi sɛt / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsi sɛt / (BrE)
A group of people who are enthusiastic about horses, particularly those involved in horseback riding and horse racing.

Examples

The horsey set community
His latest girlfriend is said to be part of the local horsey set.
Members of the horsey set gathered for the annual equestrian ball.
She grew up among the horsey set in the English countryside.
