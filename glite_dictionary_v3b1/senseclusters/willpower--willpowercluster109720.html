(in video games and role-playing games) a measurable resource or meter representing a character's mental stamina, resistance, or ability to perform special actions; often depleted by actions and replenished by rest or items.

Examples

Willpower meter
In the RPG, the hero's willpower meter drops each time he casts a powerful mind spell.
Players must manage their willpower carefully to resist fear effects and perform special actions.
After resting at the inn, her willpower fully regenerated, allowing more spells during battles.
