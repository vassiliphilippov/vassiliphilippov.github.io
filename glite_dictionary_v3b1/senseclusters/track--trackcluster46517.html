track

noun
circular path on a magnetic disk (computing)
7.1
Pronunciation: / træk / (AmE), / træk / (BrE)
(Computing) One of the circular paths on a magnetic disk that serve as a guide for reading and writing data.

Examples

Disk track
Each track on the hard drive is divided into sectors for data storage.
Formatting a disk erases the contents of every track and sector.
Technicians examined the damaged track to recover information from the disk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sector - A subdivision of a track on a magnetic disk for storing data
