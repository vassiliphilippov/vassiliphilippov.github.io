crabbed

adjective
hard to read
9.3
Pronunciation: /kræbd/ (AmE), /kræbd/ (BrE)
difficult to read or understand, especially because of cramped, awkward, or irregular style or handwriting

Examples

The professor's notes were so crabbed that students struggled to make sense of them.
She tried to decipher the crabbed handwriting in the old letter.
The legal document was written in a crabbed style that confused many readers.
