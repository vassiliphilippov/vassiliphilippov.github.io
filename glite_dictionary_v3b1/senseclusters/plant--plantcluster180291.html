plant

noun
person secretly placed in a group to act
5.9
Pronunciation: / plænt / (AmE), / plɑːnt / (BrE)
a person or thing secretly placed in a group to deceive or gather information

Examples

An undercover plant
The magician revealed that his volunteer was actually a plant in the audience.
The prank relied on a plant pretending to be an unsuspecting passerby.
They suspected the enthusiastic fan was a plant to generate excitement for the show.
He suspected that the new employee was actually a plant sent by the competition.
She accused her colleague of being a plant in the organization.
The drugs found in his locker were later revealed to be a plant by the authorities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decoy - a person or thing used to lure someone into a trap
  • synonym: mole - a person who secretly provides information to an enemy or competitor
  • synonym: stooge - a person who is secretly working with performers to mislead an audience
