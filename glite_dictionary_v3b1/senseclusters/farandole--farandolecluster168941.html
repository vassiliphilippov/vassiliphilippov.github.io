farandole

noun
lively Provençal chain dance
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfærənˈdoʊl / (AmE), / ˌfar.ənˈdɒl / (BrE)
a lively traditional dance from Provence in which dancers join hands and perform various figures

Examples

A farandole dance
