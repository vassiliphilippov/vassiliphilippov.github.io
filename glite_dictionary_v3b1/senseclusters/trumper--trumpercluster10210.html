trumper

noun
trumpet player
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌmpər / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpə / (BrE)
a person who plays or sounds a trumpet, especially in an archaic or dialectal context

Examples

Castle trumper
The old castle was awakened by the sound of a trumper at sunrise.
In the village festival, a trumper led the parade with lively music.
Legends tell of a brave trumper who signaled the start of battle.
