pharisee

noun
self-righteous or sanctimonious person
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛrəˌsi / (AmE), / ˈfærəˌsiː / (BrE)
a person who is self-righteous or hypocritically pious

Examples

A pharisee attitude
His pharisee attitude made it hard for others to trust his sincerity.
Many people saw him as a pharisee, always preaching virtue but rarely practicing it himself.
She warned the congregation about becoming pharisees who judge others without self-reflection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hypocrite - a person who pretends to have moral standards they do not possess
