grad student

noun
student in postgraduate studies
8.8
Pronunciation: / ɡræd ˈstudənt / (AmE), / ɡræd ˈstjuːdənt / (BrE)
a student who is engaged in studies beyond the undergraduate level, typically working toward a master's or doctoral degree

Examples

A grad student studies
After college, he became a grad student at a prestigious university.
Many grad students balance teaching with their own research projects.
My sister is a grad student working on her PhD in physics.

Related Terms

  • antonym: undergraduate - a student studying for their first degree
