fink

verb
confess or inform on someone (informal)
Pronunciation: / fɪŋk / (AmE), / fɪŋk / (BrE)
(informal) To confess to a punishable or disapproved act, especially by informing on others, usually under pressure.

Examples

To fink to police
He refused to fink on his friends, despite the police questioning him for hours.
She vowed never to fink, no matter what threats they made.
Some worry that a coworker might fink if the boss applies enough pressure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inform - to disclose information about someone, especially to authorities
