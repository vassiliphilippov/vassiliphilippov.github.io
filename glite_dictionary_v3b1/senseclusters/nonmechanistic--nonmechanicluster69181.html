nonmechanistic

adjective
not mechanistic; not based on mechanism
6.0
Pronunciation: / nɑnˌmɛkəˈnɪstɪk / (AmE), / nɒnˌmɛkəˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
Not relating to or based on the principles of mechanism; not explained or governed by purely mechanical or deterministic processes.

Examples

Nonmechanistic explanation
Her theory integrates nonmechanistic explanations with traditional scientific models.
Some critics argue that art appreciation cannot be reduced to a nonmechanistic set of rules.
The researcher proposed a nonmechanistic approach to understanding human consciousness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mechanistic - relating to or resembling the philosophy of mechanism
