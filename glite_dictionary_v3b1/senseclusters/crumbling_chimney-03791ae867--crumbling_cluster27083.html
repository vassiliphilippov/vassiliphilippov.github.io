crumbling chimney

noun
deteriorating or broken-down chimney
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌmblɪŋ tʃɪmni / (AmE), / ˈkrʌmblɪŋ ˈtʃɪmni / (BrE)
a chimney that is deteriorating or falling apart, often with pieces breaking off or eroding

Examples

A crumbling chimney
After years of neglect, the cottage was left with a dangerously crumbling chimney.
The old house had a crumbling chimney that needed repair.
Workers were called in to rebuild the crumbling chimney before it collapsed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chimney - a vertical channel for conveying smoke from a fire through a structure
