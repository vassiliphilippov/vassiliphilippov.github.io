siloed

adjective
isolated in groups
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪloʊd / (AmE), / ˈsaɪləʊd / (BrE)
separated or isolated from others, especially within an organization, often leading to a lack of communication or cooperation

Examples

Siloed teams
The marketing and sales teams remained siloed, making it difficult to share important information.
When departments are siloed, innovation and collaboration often suffer.
Efforts to break down siloed structures can improve communication across the company.

Related Terms

  • antonym: connected - joined or linked with others
  • synonym: isolated - separated from others
