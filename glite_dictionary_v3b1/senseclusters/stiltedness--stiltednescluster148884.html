stiltedness

noun
being on stilts
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪltɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈstɪltɪdnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being raised or supported above the ground by stilts, especially in buildings or structures

Examples

Stiltedness of houses
The stiltedness of the beach cottages helps protect them from flooding during storms.
Engineers considered the stiltedness of the structure essential for building on marshy land.
The unique stiltedness of the restaurant gives diners a beautiful view over the water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elevation - the height above a given level, especially the ground
