monkey metal

noun
cheap or low-quality metal alloy
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋki ˈmɛt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki ˈmɛtl̩ / (BrE)
An informal term for cheap or low-quality alloy, often used in inexpensive manufactured goods or decorative objects.

Examples

Cheap monkey metal
He dismissed the cheap jewelry as nothing more than monkey metal with a shiny coating.
Many collectible items from the 1950s used monkey metal to cut costs.
The old toy car's wheels are made of monkey metal and break easily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pot metal - cheap metal alloy with low melting point, often used in mass production
