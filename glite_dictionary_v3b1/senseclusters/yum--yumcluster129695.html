yum

interjection
expressing tasty delight
7.5
Pronunciation: / jʌm / (AmE), / jʌm / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express pleasure at the taste or smell of something delicious

Examples

Yum! So tasty
When she tasted the fresh strawberries, she exclaimed, "Yum! These are so sweet!"
The smell of freshly baked bread made everyone say, "Yum!" as they entered the kitchen.
He took a bite of the pizza and said, "Yum, this is exactly what I needed after a long day."

Related Terms

  • antonym: ew - an exclamation expressing disgust
  • synonym: yummy - tasting very good or delicious
