Xeranthemum annuum

noun
a species of everlasting flower
Pronunciation: / zɛrˈænθəməm æˈnuəm / (AmE), / zɪəˈrænθɛməm æˈnjuːəm / (BrE)
A species of everlasting flower usually bearing purple blooms, native to southern Europe to Iran and widely cultivated.
