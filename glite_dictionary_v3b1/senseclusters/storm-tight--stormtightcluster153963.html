storm-tight

adjective
resistant to storms
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔːrmtʌɪt / (AmE), / ˈstɔːmtaɪt / (BrE)
designed or constructed to prevent wind and rain from entering during a storm

Examples

Storm-tight windows
The new windows are storm-tight and can withstand strong winds and heavy rain.
They installed storm-tight doors to protect the basement from flooding during hurricanes.
A storm-tight roof is essential in areas prone to severe weather.
