thrower

noun
person who shapes pottery on a wheel
Pronunciation: / ˈθroʊ.ər / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊ.ə / (BrE)
a craftsman who shapes pottery on a potter's wheel and bakes it in a kiln

Examples

Skilled pottery thrower
Every thrower develops a unique style that is reflected in their pottery.
She apprenticed as a thrower before opening her own studio.
The experienced thrower demonstrated how to center the clay on the wheel.
