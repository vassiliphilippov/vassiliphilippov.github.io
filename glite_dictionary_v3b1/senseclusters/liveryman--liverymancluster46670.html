liveryman

noun
man working in a livery stable
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪvəriˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈlɪvəriˌman / (BrE)
a man employed to care for horses and do related work in a livery stable

Examples

A liveryman cares for horses
As a young liveryman, he learned how to brush horses and clean tack.
The liveryman led the horses to their stalls after a long day's ride.
The old liveryman shared stories of the many travelers who passed through the stable.

Related Terms
