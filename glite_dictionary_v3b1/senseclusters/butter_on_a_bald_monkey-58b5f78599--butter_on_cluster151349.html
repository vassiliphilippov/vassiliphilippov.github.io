butter on a bald monkey

noun
an absurd or ineffective attempt
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtər ɑn ə bɔld ˈmʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈbʌtə ɒn ə bɔːld ˈmʌŋki / (BrE)
An absurd or futile attempt; a foolish action that is unlikely to achieve the desired result.

Examples

Like butter on a bald monkey
Fixing the leak with duct tape proved to be butter on a bald monkey—completely useless.
His efforts to explain quantum physics to preschoolers were like butter on a bald monkey.
The manager's proposal to boost morale with unpaid overtime was just butter on a bald monkey.
