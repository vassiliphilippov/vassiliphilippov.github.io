lobeless

adjective
without lobes
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊbləs / (AmE), / ˈləʊbləs / (BrE)
having no lobes or lacking any lobed parts, especially in leaves or organs

Examples

lobeless leaves
The botanist noted that the species had lobeless leaves, making it easy to identify.
Unlike its relatives, this flower produces lobeless petals that are smooth and continuous.
The diagram shows a lobeless liver, which is rare in mammals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: entire - having a smooth, unbroken edge without lobes or divisions
  • antonym: lobed - having one or more rounded or pointed projections (lobes)
