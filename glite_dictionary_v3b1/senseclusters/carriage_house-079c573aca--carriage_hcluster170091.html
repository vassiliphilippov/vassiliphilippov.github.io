carriage house

noun
building for storing carriages or vehicles
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkærɪdʒ haʊs / (AmE), / ˈkærɪdʒ haʊs / (BrE)
A small building originally used to store carriages and other vehicles, often found on the grounds of older houses.

Examples

Old carriage house
A vintage horse-drawn carriage was discovered in the abandoned carriage house.
The old mansion still has its original carriage house standing beside the driveway.
They renovated the carriage house into a cozy guest cottage.
