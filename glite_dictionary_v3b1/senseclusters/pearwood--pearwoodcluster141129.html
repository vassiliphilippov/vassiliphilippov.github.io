pearwood

noun
wood from pear tree
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛrˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈpeəwʊd / (BrE)
the hard, fine-grained wood obtained from the pear tree, often used in woodworking and instrument making

Examples

pearwood for instruments
The craftsman selected pearwood for its smooth texture and elegant finish.
Many high-quality recorders are made from pearwood because of its excellent acoustic properties.
She admired the antique box carved from solid pearwood in the museum display.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boxwood - hard, fine-grained wood from the box tree
  • synonym: maple - wood from the maple tree, used in furniture and instruments
