putt

noun
a gentle golf stroke on the green
7.8
Pronunciation: / pʌt / (AmE), / pʌt / (BrE)
a stroke in golf made by gently hitting the ball along the green towards the hole, usually using a putter

Examples

A short putt
He missed the short putt by just an inch.
She made a perfect putt to win the tournament.
The golfer studied the green carefully before attempting his next putt.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chip - a short shot in golf lofted onto the green from close by
  • antonym: drive - a long, powerful shot in golf, usually from the tee
