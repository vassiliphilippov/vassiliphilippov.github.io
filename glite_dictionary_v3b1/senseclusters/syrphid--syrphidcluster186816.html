syrphid

noun
hoverfly species
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜːrfɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɜːfɪd / (BrE)
a fly of the family Syrphidae, commonly known as a hoverfly or flower fly, often seen hovering near flowers

Examples

A syrphid on flowers
A syrphid hovered over the blossoms in the garden, searching for nectar.
The bright colors of the syrphid made it look similar to a bee.
Gardeners appreciate the presence of syrphids because they help pollinate flowers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bee - a flying insect known for pollination and making honey
  • synonym: hoverfly - a fly that hovers near flowers, belonging to the family Syrphidae
