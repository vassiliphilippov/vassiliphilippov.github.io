transportability

noun
ease of being moved
5.4
Pronunciation: / trænˌspɔrtəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / trænˌspɔːtəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality of being able to be easily moved or transported from one place to another

Examples

Transportability of equipment
The transportability of the new medical device allows doctors to use it in remote areas.
When choosing camping gear, transportability is an important factor for hikers.
The transportability of the sculpture made it easy to display in different galleries.

Related Terms

  • antonym: immobility - the state of being unable to move or be moved
  • synonym: portability - the quality of being easily carried or moved
