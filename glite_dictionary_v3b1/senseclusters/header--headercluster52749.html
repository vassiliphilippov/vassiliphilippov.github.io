header

noun
structural beam supporting ends of joists, etc.
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdɚ /; / ˈhɛdər / (AmE), / ˈhɛdə / (BrE)
a horizontal support or element at the top of an opening or wall, such as a beam or a brick laid sideways
