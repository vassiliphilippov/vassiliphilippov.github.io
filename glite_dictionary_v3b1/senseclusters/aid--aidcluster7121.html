Synonyms

  • help
    1.0 151/m
    help is the everyday action of making something easier for someone, while aid is general support often used in emergencies.
  • assistance
    1.0 65/m
    assistance sounds more formal and often appears in writing or official contexts, while aid is common in urgent or practical help.
  • relief
    1.3 17/m
    relief is help sent to reduce suffering after a disaster, while aid covers many kinds of support in need.
  • support
    1.0 64/m
    support is ongoing money or material help to keep something working, while aid often means immediate help in times of need.
