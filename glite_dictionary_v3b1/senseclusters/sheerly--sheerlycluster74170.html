sheerly

adverb
completely; purely
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪrli / (AmE), / ˈʃɪəli / (BrE)
completely or purely, with nothing else involved or influencing the situation

Examples

Sheerly by effort
He succeeded sheerly through determination and hard work, without any outside help.
The mountain rose sheerly from the valley floor, creating a dramatic landscape.
Her decision was made sheerly out of necessity, not desire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: entirely - completely; in every way
  • antonym: partially - in part; not completely
  • synonym: purely - entirely or only; with nothing else involved
