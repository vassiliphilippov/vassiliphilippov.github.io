purge

noun
act of clearing from blame or stigma
7.9 0.20/m
Pronunciation: / p…ùÀêd í / (AmE), / p…úÀêd í / (BrE)
the act of clearing oneself or someone else from blame, suspicion, or stigma

Examples

A legal purge
He sought a legal purge to restore his reputation.
Her testimony acted as a formal purge of the longstanding allegations.
The ritual was seen as a purge of past transgressions for the entire community.
