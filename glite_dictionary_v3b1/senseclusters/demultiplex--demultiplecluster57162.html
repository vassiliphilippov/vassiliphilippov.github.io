demultiplex

verb
to separate signals
Pronunciation: / ˌdiːˌmʌltɪˈplɛks / (AmE), / ˌdiːˌmʌltɪˈplɛks / (BrE)
to separate multiple combined signals or data streams into their original individual channels.

Examples

To demultiplex data streams
The software can demultiplex the video feed into several distinct channels for analysis.
Engineers must demultiplex the combined signals before processing the data further.
After transmission, the device will demultiplex the audio and video streams for playback.

Related Terms

  • antonym: multiplex - to combine multiple signals into one channel
  • synonym: separate - to divide or split into different parts
