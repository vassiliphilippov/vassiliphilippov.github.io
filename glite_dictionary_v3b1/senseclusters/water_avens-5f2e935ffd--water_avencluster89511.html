water avens

noun
perennial plant with nodding flowers
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚ ˌævənz / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌævənz / (BrE)
a perennial plant (Geum rivale) of the north temperate zone, with pinnate leaves and nodding flowers that have a brown-purple calyx and orange-pink petals, typically found in moist habitats
