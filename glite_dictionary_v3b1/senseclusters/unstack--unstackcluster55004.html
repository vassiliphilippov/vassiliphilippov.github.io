unstack

verb
to remove from stack
4.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstæk / (AmE), / ʌnˈstak /; / ʌnˈstæk / (BrE)
to remove items from a stack or pile so they are no longer arranged on top of each other

Examples

After the event, volunteers helped to unstack the chairs and tables in the hall.
