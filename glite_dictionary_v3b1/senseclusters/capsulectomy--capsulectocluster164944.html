capsulectomy

noun
removal of tissue capsule
Pronunciation: / ˌkæpsjəˈlɛktəmi / (AmE), / ˌkæpsjʊˈlɛktəmi / (BrE)
a surgical procedure to remove a capsule of tissue, especially one that forms around an implant

Examples

A capsulectomy surgery
The surgeon recommended a capsulectomy to remove the scar tissue around the breast implant.
After experiencing pain and tightness, she underwent a capsulectomy to improve her symptoms.
A capsulectomy may be necessary if the capsule around an implant becomes too thick or hard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capsulotomy - a surgical incision into a capsule of tissue
  • synonym: excision - the act of cutting something out, especially surgically
