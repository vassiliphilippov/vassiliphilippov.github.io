bossa nova

noun
Brazilian music style
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˌbɑsə ˈnoʊvə / (AmE), / ˌbɒsə ˈnəʊvə / (BrE)
a style of Brazilian music that blends samba rhythms with jazz influences, known for its smooth and relaxed sound

Examples

A bossa nova song
The band played a classic bossa nova that filled the café with a relaxed atmosphere.
She learned to dance bossa nova while visiting Rio de Janeiro last summer.
Many famous jazz musicians have experimented with bossa nova in their recordings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jazz - a genre of music characterized by improvisation and swing
  • synonym: samba - a lively Brazilian dance and music style
