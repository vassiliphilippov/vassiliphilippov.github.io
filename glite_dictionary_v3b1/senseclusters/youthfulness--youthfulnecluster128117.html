youthfulness

noun
the quality of being young and energetic
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈjuθf(ə)lnəs / (AmE), / ˈjuːθf(ə)lnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being young, fresh, and energetic

Examples

Full youthfulness
