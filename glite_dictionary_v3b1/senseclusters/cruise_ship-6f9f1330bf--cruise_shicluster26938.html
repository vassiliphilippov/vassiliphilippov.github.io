cruise ship

noun
large passenger ship for pleasure cruises
5.8
Pronunciation: / kruːz ʃɪp / (AmE), / kruːz ʃɪp / (BrE)
A large passenger ship used for commercial pleasure voyages, where the journey and amenities are part of the experience.

Examples

A large cruise ship
A cruise ship docked at the port early this morning, bringing thousands of tourists.
Many people enjoy the wide range of entertainment offered aboard a cruise ship.
We spent a week traveling the Caribbean on a luxurious cruise ship.
