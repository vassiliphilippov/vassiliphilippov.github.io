red-berried elder

noun
Eurasian shrub with red berries
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛd ˈbɛrid ˈɛldɚ / (AmE), / ˌrɛd ˈbɛrid ˈɛldə / (BrE)
a shrub or small tree (Sambucus racemosa) with clusters of red berries, found in Eurasia and North America
