shipboard duty

noun
naval service while aboard a ship
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪpbɔrd ˈduːti / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpbɔːd ˈdjuːti / (BrE)
Military or naval service performed while stationed aboard a ship at sea.

Examples

Shipboard duty at sea
After years of shipboard duty, she transferred to a desk job on shore.
He was assigned to shipboard duty for six months during his naval service.
Many sailors prefer shipboard duty because of the opportunity to travel.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shore duty - military service performed at a land-based station as opposed to at sea
