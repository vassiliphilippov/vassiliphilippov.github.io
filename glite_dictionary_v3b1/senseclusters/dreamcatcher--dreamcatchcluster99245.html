dreamcatcher

noun
object to catch bad dreams
Pronunciation: / ˈdriːmˌkætʃər / (AmE), / ˈdriːmˌkætʃə / (BrE)
a handmade object, often a hoop with a woven net and feathers, hung above beds to protect sleepers from bad dreams

Examples

A dreamcatcher with feathers
A colorful dreamcatcher hung above the child's bed to keep nightmares away.
She bought a handmade dreamcatcher at the local craft fair for her friend.
Many people believe that a dreamcatcher brings peaceful sleep and positive dreams.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amulet - an object believed to protect against evil or bad luck
  • synonym: talisman - an object thought to bring good luck or protection
