dreamcatcher

noun
object to catch bad dreams
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdriːmˌkætʃər / (AmE), / ˈdriːmˌkætʃə / (BrE)
a handmade object, often a hoop with a woven net and feathers, hung above beds to protect sleepers from bad dreams

Many people believe that a dreamcatcher brings peaceful sleep and positive dreams.
