-ite

x
noun-forming suffix
8.1
Pronunciation: / aɪt / (AmE), / aɪt / (BrE)
a suffix used to form nouns for people who are followers, supporters, members, or inhabitants of a place, group, or ideology

Examples

A New Yorkite
A person from Brooklyn is often called a Brooklynite.
The term Israelite refers to a member of the ancient Hebrew nation.
Supporters of a particular leader might be labeled as Smithites.

Related Terms

  • synonym: -ian - a suffix forming nouns for people belonging to a place or group
  • synonym: -ist - a suffix forming nouns for people who practice or are concerned with something
