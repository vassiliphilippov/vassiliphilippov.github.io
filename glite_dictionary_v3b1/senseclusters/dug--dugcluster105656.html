dug

noun
udder or teat, esp. in animals
Pronunciation: / dʌɡ / (AmE), / dʌɡ / (BrE)
an udder, breast, or teat of a female mammal, especially in reference to livestock or, historically, to human anatomy

Examples

A dug of cow
In old literature, 'suckling at the dug' often referred to breastfeeding.
The cow's dug was full and ready for milking by morning.
The shepherd checked each ewe's dug for signs of infection.
