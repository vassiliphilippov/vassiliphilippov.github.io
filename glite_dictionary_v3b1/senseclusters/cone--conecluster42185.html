cone

noun
a shape with a circular base and tapering sides
Pronunciation: / koʊn / (AmE), / kəʊn / (BrE)
a solid or hollow shape with a circular base that narrows smoothly to a point, or an object with this shape (such as a traffic cone)

Examples

A traffic cone
A traffic cone blocked the entrance to the parking lot.
In math class, we learned how to calculate the volume of a cone.
The ice cream was served in a cone rather than a cup.
After the parade, orange cones blocked off several streets for cleanup.
She placed a small cone of incense on the altar before the ceremony.
The children played with a brightly colored cone at the playground.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cylinder - a solid with straight sides and a circular base and top
  • antonym: sphere - a perfectly round geometrical object in three-dimensional space
