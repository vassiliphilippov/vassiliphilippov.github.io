mariner

noun
sailor; person serving on a ship
4.1 6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmærənɚ / (AmE), / ˈmærɪnə / (BrE)
a person who works as a sailor, especially one who navigates or serves on a ship at sea

Examples

Experienced mariner
As a young mariner, he traveled to distant ports across the world.
The experienced mariner guided the crew safely through the stormy seas.
The story tells of a lone mariner lost at sea for weeks.
