wallaby

noun
small or medium-sized Australian marsupial
9.9 4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈwɑː.lə.bɪ/ /; / /ˈwɔː.lə.bɪ/ (AmE), /ˈwɒ.lə.bɪ/ (BrE)
