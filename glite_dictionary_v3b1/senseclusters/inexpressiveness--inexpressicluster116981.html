inexpressiveness

noun
difficulty to express
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnɪkˈsprɛsɪvnəs / (AmE), / ˌɪnɪkˈsprɛsɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being impossible or extremely difficult to express or describe in words

Examples

Inexpressiveness of feelings
The inexpressiveness of his emotions made it hard for others to understand how he felt.
She was struck by the inexpressiveness of the vast, silent desert around her.
Sometimes, the beauty of a moment is defined by its inexpressiveness and the silence it brings.

Related Terms

  • antonym: expressiveness - the quality of effectively conveying thought or feeling
  • synonym: indescribability - the state of being impossible to describe
