delint

verb
to remove lint
Pronunciation: / diˈlɪnt / (AmE), / diˈlɪnt / (BrE)
to remove lint or fine fibers from something, especially from cotton seeds or fabric

Examples

To delint cotton seeds
Workers delint cotton seeds before planting them to improve germination.
She used a special brush to delint her sweater after washing it.
Machines are designed to delint large quantities of fabric quickly and efficiently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clean - to make something free from dirt or unwanted substances
  • degree lower: lint - small fibers or fluff that come off fabric or cotton
