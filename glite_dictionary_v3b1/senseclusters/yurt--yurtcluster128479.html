yurt

noun
portable circular tent dwelling
8.1 0.65/m
Pronunciation: / j…ùt / (AmE), / j…úÀêt / (BrE)
A circular, portable tent with a domed roof, traditionally used by nomadic peoples of Central Asia.

Examples

A yurt tent

The nomads set up their yurt on the grassy steppe for the summer months.
