balance

noun
what is left over; remainder
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbæl.əns / (AmE), / ˈbæl.əns / (BrE)
the part remaining after the rest has been taken, used, or dealt with

Examples

The balance left
After dividing the cake among the children, she put the balance in the fridge.
He paid most of the bill and left the balance for his friend to cover later.
Out of 100 tickets, the balance was returned to the box office.
