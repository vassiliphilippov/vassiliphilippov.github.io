total nutsucker

noun
vulgar insult for something disappointing
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊt̬əl ˈnʌtˌsʌkər / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtəl ˈnʌtˌsʌkə / (BrE)
A vulgar and offensive term used to express strong disappointment with something or someone; a derogatory insult for a person or thing considered deeply unsatisfactory.

Examples

A total nutsucker insult
