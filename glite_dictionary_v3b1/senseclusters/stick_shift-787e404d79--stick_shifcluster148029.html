stick shift

noun
manual vehicle transmission with gear lever
9.9 4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪk ˌʃɪft /; / stɪk ʃɪft / (AmE), / ˈstɪk ˌʃɪft /; / stɪk ʃɪft / (BrE)
a type of car transmission that you operate manually using a gear stick and clutch pedal, instead of automatically

Examples

A stick shift car
He prefers driving cars with a stick shift rather than an automatic transmission.
Learning to use a stick shift can be challenging for some new drivers.
The rental agency only had vehicles with a stick shift available.
His first car was an old stick shift that he bought from his uncle.
Many young drivers today have never learned how to operate a stick shift.
She prefers driving a stick shift because it gives her more control over the car.
