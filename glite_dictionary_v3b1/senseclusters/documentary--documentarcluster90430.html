documentary

adjective
factual about real-life events (film, TV, etc.)
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌdɑːk.jəˈmen.t̬ɛr.i / (AmE), / ˌdɒk.jʊˈmen.təri / (BrE)
(of a film, television program, radio broadcast, or photograph) presenting factual information about real-life events, typically using actual images or interviews with people involved.

Examples

Documentary film style
She won an award for her documentary photography capturing life in rural villages.
The documentary approach of the film brought attention to social issues.
The series is known for its documentary style, featuring real interviews with witnesses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: factual - concerned with what is actually the case rather than interpretations
  • antonym: fictional - not based on real events; invented or imaginary
