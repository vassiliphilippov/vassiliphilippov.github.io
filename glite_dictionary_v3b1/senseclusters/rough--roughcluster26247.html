rough

noun
preliminary sketch
4.7
Pronunciation: / rʌf / (AmE), / rʌf / (BrE)
a preliminary drawing or sketch that shows the basic idea or layout of a design or artwork

Examples

A rough sketch
The designer created several roughs to explore different ideas for the new logo.
Before painting the mural, she showed the client a few roughs for approval.
The team reviewed the roughs and selected the best concept to develop further.

Related Terms

  • synonym: draft - a first version of a drawing or document
  • antonym: final - the completed and finished version of a work
  • synonym: sketch - a simple, quickly made drawing showing the main features
