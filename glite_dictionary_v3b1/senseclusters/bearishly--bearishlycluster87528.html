bearishly

adverb
expecting price decline
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛrɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈbɛərɪʃli / (BrE)
in a manner that expects prices, especially in financial markets, to fall or decline

Examples

Act bearishly in markets
Traders responded bearishly to the disappointing economic data released this morning.
The market moved bearishly after the central bank announced higher interest rates.
Analysts spoke bearishly about the company's future due to declining sales figures.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bullishly - in a manner that expects prices to rise
  • synonym: pessimistically - in a way that expects negative outcomes
