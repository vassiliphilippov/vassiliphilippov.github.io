preadmission

noun
before official admission
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːədˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːədˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or period before someone is officially accepted or admitted, especially to a hospital or institution

Examples

Preadmission process
The hospital requires a preadmission interview for all patients.
During the preadmission process, patients must complete several forms and health screenings.
Colleges often have a preadmission period where applicants submit documents before being accepted.

Related Terms

  • antonym: admission - the act of being accepted or allowed to enter
