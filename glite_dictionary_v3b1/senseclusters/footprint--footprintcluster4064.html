Synonyms

  • footmark
    2.4 0.03/m
    footmark is a mark left by a foot or shoe on a surface, while footprint is the usual name for that same impression.
  • print
    2.0 9.07/m
    print is a mark left by an object like a hand, paw, or foot, while footprint refers specifically to a foot or shoe impression.
  • imprint
    4.2 1.38/m
    imprint is a mark made by strong pressure or by a device, while footprint is the mark made by a foot or shoe.
  • impression
    4.1 5.83/m
    impression is an indentation or a copy left by pressure in many contexts, while footprint is specifically a foot or shoe mark.
