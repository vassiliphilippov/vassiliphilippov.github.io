multitrack

noun
audio recording device
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌtræk / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌtræk / (BrE)
a machine or system that records or plays back multiple separate audio tracks at the same time

Examples

A multitrack machine
The band used a multitrack to record each instrument separately for better sound quality.
Modern studios rely on a digital multitrack for complex music production.
After upgrading their equipment, they could edit vocals and drums on the multitrack independently.

  • synonym: recorder - a device for recording sound or images
