dodge

verb
move suddenly to avoid
3.0
Pronunciation: / dɑdʒ / (AmE), / dɒdʒ / (BrE)
to move quickly and suddenly to avoid someone or something

Examples

To dodge a ball

He managed to dodge past the security guard without being noticed.
She was quick enough to dodge out of the way of the falling branch.
The squirrel dodged across the road, narrowly avoiding the car.
