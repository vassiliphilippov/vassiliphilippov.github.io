collage

verb
to assemble artwork
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɑːʒ / (AmE), / kəˈlɑːʒ / (BrE)
to create an artwork by assembling various pieces of paper, images, or materials into a single composition

Examples

To collage paper pieces
The children collaged colorful paper scraps to make a vibrant mural for the classroom.
She collages old photographs and fabric pieces to create unique art for her gallery shows.
For the project, we collaged magazine images onto a large poster board.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assemble - to put together different parts to form a whole
  • synonym: combine - to join or mix two or more things together
