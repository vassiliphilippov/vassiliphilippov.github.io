muley

adjective
stubborn or obstinate
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːli / (AmE), / ˈmjuːli / (BrE)
stubborn or obstinate in behavior, like a mule

Examples

Muley attitude
Despite everyone's advice, he remained muley and refused to compromise.
Her muley attitude made it difficult to reach an agreement with the team.
The child grew muley when asked to clean his room, insisting he would not do it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flexible - willing to change or adapt
  • synonym: obstinate - refusing to change behavior or ideas
  • synonym: stubborn - unwilling to change one's mind or attitude
