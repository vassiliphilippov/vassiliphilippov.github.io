The standard time zone that is four hours behind Greenwich Mean Time, used in parts of eastern Canada, Bermuda, Puerto Rico, and the Virgin Islands.

Examples

Atlantic Time zone
Flights between Boston and Bermuda must account for the difference in Atlantic Time.
The clocks in Nova Scotia are set to Atlantic Time, which is one hour ahead of Eastern Time.
When it is noon in Atlantic Time, it is 11:00 a.m. in the Eastern Time zone.

Synonyms

  • Atlantic Standard Time
    3.9 0.00/m
    Atlantic Standard Time is the formal standard tied to Coordinated Universal Time in some provinces, while Atlantic Time names the same zone tied to Greenwich Mean Time.
