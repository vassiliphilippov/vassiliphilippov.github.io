dragginess

noun
quality of dragging
Pronunciation: / ˈdræɡinəs / (AmE), / ˈdræɡinəs / (BrE)
the quality of hanging down so that something rubs or drags on the ground or another surface

Examples

Dragginess of curtains
The dragginess of the curtain made it easy to trip over.
She disliked the dragginess of her long skirt on muddy paths.
The dragginess of the tablecloth caused it to catch on the chair legs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drag - to pull along the ground or another surface
  • synonym: snag - to catch or tear on something while moving
