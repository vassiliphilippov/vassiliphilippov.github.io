general

adjective
affecting the whole body
1.0 3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛnɚəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnərəl / (BrE)
(of a medical condition or treatment) affecting the whole or entire body rather than a particular part.

Examples

General anesthesia
After the surgery, she remained unconscious due to the general anesthetic.
The doctor noted that the patient exhibited general symptoms such as fever and fatigue.
Unlike local anesthesia, a general anesthetic induces sleep throughout the entire body.
