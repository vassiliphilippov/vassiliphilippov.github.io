nep

noun
fiber knot or lump
Pronunciation: / nɛp / (AmE), / nɛp / (BrE)
a small knot or lump of fiber found in wool or fabric, often considered a flaw

Examples

A nep in fabric
The quality inspector found several neps in the finished cloth.
Removing each nep by hand can be a time-consuming process in textile production.
A high number of neps can reduce the value of wool fabric.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flaw - an imperfection or defect in a material
  • synonym: knot - a hard mass or lump in a material such as wood or fabric
