pinstriped

adjective
having very thin parallel stripes
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnˌstraɪpt / (AmE), / ˈpɪnˌstraɪpt / (BrE)
(of fabric or clothing) having very thin, usually parallel, stripes as a pattern.

Examples

Classic pinstriped trousers
Classic pinstriped trousers never go out of style.
He wore a pinstriped suit to the business meeting.
The tailor recommended a pinstriped fabric for a more formal look.
