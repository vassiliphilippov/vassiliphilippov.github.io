overmatch

noun
superior strength
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌmætʃ / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌmætʃ / (BrE)
a state of having superior strength, force, or capability that allows one side to defeat or dominate another

Examples

A clear overmatch
The new fighter jets provided a clear overmatch against enemy aircraft in the region.
Technological advances can create an overmatch that changes the balance of power in a conflict.
The team’s overmatch in experience was obvious during the championship game.
