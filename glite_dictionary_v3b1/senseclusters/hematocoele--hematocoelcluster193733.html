hematocoele

noun
swelling from blood in a body cavity
9.9
Pronunciation: / hiːˈmætəˌsil /; / hiːˈmætəˌkoʊl / (AmE), / hɪˈmatəʊˌsiːl /; / hɪˈmætəkəʊl / (BrE)
a swelling caused by an accumulation of blood in a body cavity, especially in the membrane covering the testis

Examples

A hematocoele swelling
A testicular injury may result in a hematocoele, requiring prompt medical attention.
The patient presented with scrotal swelling, which was diagnosed as a hematocoele.
The surgeon identified a hematocoele during the procedure and drained the accumulated blood.
A haematocoele can occur following trauma to the testicular area.
Immediate treatment was necessary to prevent complications from the haematocoele.
The surgeon diagnosed a haematocoele after noticing the swelling in the patient's scrotum.
