hematocoele

noun
swelling from blood in a body cavity
Pronunciation: / hiːˈmætəˌsil /; / hiːˈmætəˌkoʊl / (AmE), / hɪˈmatəʊˌsiːl /; / hɪˈmætəkəʊl / (BrE)
a swelling caused by an accumulation of blood in a body cavity, especially in the membrane covering the testis
