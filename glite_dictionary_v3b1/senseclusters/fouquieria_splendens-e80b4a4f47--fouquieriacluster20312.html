Fouquieria splendens

noun
desert shrub of SW US and Mexico (ocotillo)
Pronunciation: / fuˈkwiːriə splɛnˈdɛnz / (AmE), / fuːˈkwɪərɪə splɛnˈdɛnz / (BrE)
A desert shrub native to the southwestern United States and Mexico, with slender spiny stems that bear leaves and red flowers after rain; commonly called ocotillo.
