Synonyms

  • insurrection
    9.1 2/m
    insurrection is an organized violent attempt to topple rulers, while revolution is a large change that replaces a whole system.
  • rebellion
    4.1 8/m
    rebellion is an armed movement that fights authority over time, while revolution is a sudden, large change that transforms society.
  • uprising
    5.1 13/m
    uprising is a short, often local action by people against authority, while revolution is a broad, deep change of society or government.
