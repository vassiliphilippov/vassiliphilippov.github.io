flake off

verb

(of a surface or material) to break away or come off in thin, small pieces.

Examples

Paint flakes off
Be careful when sanding; some paint may flake off easily.
The old wallpaper started to flake off after years of neglect.
Tiny bits of dry skin would flake off in the cold winter air.
