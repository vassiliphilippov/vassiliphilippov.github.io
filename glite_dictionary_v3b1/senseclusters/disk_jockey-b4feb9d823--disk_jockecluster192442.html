disk jockey

noun
person who announces and plays recorded music
4.9
Pronunciation: / dɪsk ˈdʒɑki / (AmE), / dɪsk ˈdʒɒki / (BrE)
a person whose job is to announce and play popular recorded music, especially on the radio or at events

Examples

Famous disk jockey
She dreamed of becoming a famous disk jockey for a major radio station.
The disk jockey kept the crowd dancing late into the night at the wedding reception.
The club hired a talented disk jockey to mix music every Saturday.
Many couples hire a professional disc jockey for their wedding receptions.
She works as a disc jockey at a popular local radio station.
The disc jockey played my favorite song during the party last night.
