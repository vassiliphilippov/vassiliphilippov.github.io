banner

noun
long decorative or advertising sign
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbænɚ /; / ˈbænər / (AmE), / ˈbænə / (BrE)
a long strip of cloth or paper with a sign, slogan, or design, used for decoration, advertising, or as a flag

Examples

A banner over the street
Colorful banners decorated the streets for the festival.
The shop displayed a bright red banner to announce its grand opening.
Volunteers held a large banner above the crowd during the demonstration.
A colorful banner hung over the main street during the festival.
He proudly waved his team’s banner after they won the championship.
The soldiers marched beneath the banner of their regiment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flag - piece of cloth with a symbol or design, used as a symbol of a country or institution
  • synonym: pennant - a long, narrow flag, often used for signaling or identification
