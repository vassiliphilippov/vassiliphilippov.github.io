ground ivy

noun
trailing aromatic plant of the mint family
6.8 6.1
Pronunciation: /graʊnd ˈaɪ.viː/ (AmE), /graʊnd ˈaɪ.viː/ (BrE)
a trailing European plant of the mint family with rounded leaves and small purplish flowers, often grown in hanging baskets and naturalized in North America

Examples

Ground ivy plant

Many gardeners use ground ivy as a decorative plant in hanging baskets.
