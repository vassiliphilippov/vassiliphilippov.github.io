Fatima

proper noun
daughter of Muhammad, revered in Islam
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑː.tɪ.mə / (AmE), / ˈfæ.tɪ.mə / (BrE)
the youngest daughter of the Prophet Muhammad and wife of the fourth caliph, Ali; especially revered by Shiite Muslims

Examples

Fatima daughter of Muhammad
Many Shiite Muslims hold Fatima in the highest regard for her piety and devotion.
Stories of Fatima emphasize her role as a model of compassion and strength in Islamic tradition.
The city of Fes is believed to have been founded by descendants of Fatima.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Ali - fourth caliph of Islam, husband of Fatima
  • synonym: Muhammad - Prophet of Islam, father of Fatima
