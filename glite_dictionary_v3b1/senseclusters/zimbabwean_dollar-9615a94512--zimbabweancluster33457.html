Zimbabwean dollar

noun
unit of currency in Zimbabwe
Pronunciation: / zɪmˈbɑːwiən ˈdɑːlər / (AmE), / zɪmˈbɑːwiən ˈdɒlə / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Zimbabwe

Examples

The Zimbabwean dollar
He exchanged his U.S. dollars for Zimbabwean dollars at the airport.
The government introduced a new Zimbabwean dollar denomination to combat hyperinflation.
The price of bread soared to a million Zimbabwean dollars during the peak of inflation.
