asylee

noun
person seeking asylum
Pronunciation: / əˈsaɪli / (AmE), / əˈsaɪli / (BrE)
a person who has been granted asylum or is seeking asylum in a foreign country for protection from danger or persecution

Examples

An asylee seeking protection
The government provided housing and support for each asylee during the application process.
After months of waiting, the asylee finally received a decision on her case.
Lawyers volunteered their time to help every asylee understand their rights.
The country welcomed hundreds of asylees fleeing political violence.
Each asylee must go through a legal process before receiving protection.
The organization helps asylees adjust to life in their new communities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deportee - a person who is expelled from a country
  • synonym: refugee - a person who flees their country due to war or persecution
