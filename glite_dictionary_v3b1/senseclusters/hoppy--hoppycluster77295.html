hoppy

adjective
strong hop flavor
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑpi / (AmE), / ˈhɒpi / (BrE)
having a strong flavor or aroma of hops, especially in beer

Examples

Hoppy beer taste
This craft IPA is especially hoppy, with a bold and refreshing bitterness.
If you enjoy a hoppy taste, try the new pale ale from the local brewery.
Some people find hoppy beers too bitter, while others love their strong flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bitter - having a sharp, strong taste, often from hops in beer
  • antonym: malty - having a flavor or aroma of malt, often sweet or rich
