demolishment

noun
destruction of buildings
4.9
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmɑlɪʃmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈmɒlɪʃmənt / (BrE)
the act of destroying or tearing down a building or structure

Examples

Demolishment of a building
The demolishment of the old factory cleared space for a new community center.
After the demolishment was complete, the area was ready for redevelopment.
Residents watched the demolishment of the historic building with mixed emotions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demolition - the act of tearing down or destroying a structure
