bilge well

noun
compartment for collecting seepage on a ship
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪldʒ wɛl / (AmE), / ˈbɪldʒ wɛl / (BrE)
(nautical) A compartment or recess in the bottom of a ship where seepage collects to be pumped out.

Examples

The bilge well compartment
Routine maintenance includes cleaning the bilge well to prevent blockages in the drainage system.
The engineer checked the bilge well for any signs of excess water before the ship set sail.
Water that leaks into the hull is collected in the bilge well and then pumped overboard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bilge - the lowest part of a ship's hull, where water collects
