relieved

adjective
free from worry
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlivd / (AmE), / rɪˈliːvd / (BrE)
feeling happy and relaxed because something unpleasant or worrying has ended or been avoided

Examples

Relieved after worry
She felt relieved when the exam results showed she had passed.
After hours of searching, he was relieved to find his lost wallet.
We were all relieved that the storm had finally ended.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anxious - feeling worried or uneasy
  • synonym: comforted - feeling reassured or less worried
