wakey wakey, eggs and bakey

x
playful breakfast wake-up phrase
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪki ˈweɪki ɛɡz ənd ˈbeɪki / (AmE), / ˈweɪki ˈweɪki ɛɡz ənd ˈbeɪki / (BrE)
A playful or humorous phrase used to wake someone up, especially at breakfast time, often suggesting that eggs and bacon are ready.

Examples

wakey wakey, eggs and bakey in the morning
His mom always says wakey wakey, eggs and bakey to get him out of bed on weekends.
She woke her brother with a cheerful shout of wakey wakey, eggs and bakey before breakfast.
When the smell of cooking drifted upstairs, Dad called out, 'wakey wakey, eggs and bakey!'
