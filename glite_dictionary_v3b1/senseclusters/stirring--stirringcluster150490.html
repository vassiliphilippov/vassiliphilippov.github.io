Examples

Constant stirring with a spoon

Synonyms

  • mixing
    1.8 5.11/m
    mixing is the general process of joining substances into one, while stirring is the manual action of moving a tool through a liquid.
  • blending
    2.7 0.07/m
    blending mixes substances thoroughly, often with strong motion or a machine, while stirring is usually manual and gentler with a spoon.
  • agitation
    4.8 0.30/m
    agitation means vigorous shaking or motion to mix quickly, while stirring is a gentler, tool-driven action like using a spoon.
