stirring

noun
mixing a liquid or substance
Pronunciation: / ˈstɜrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɜːrɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of mixing a liquid or substance with a tool such as a spoon or stick

Examples

Constant stirring with a spoon
After several minutes of stirring, the sugar dissolved completely.
She used a wooden spoon for stirring the soup on the stove.
The recipe requires constant stirring to prevent lumps from forming.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mixing - the act of combining or blending substances
