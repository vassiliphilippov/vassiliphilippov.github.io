guarana

noun
Brazilian soft drink
Pronunciation: / ˌɡwɑːrəˈnɑː / (AmE), / ˌɡwɑːrəˈnɑː / (BrE)
a sweet, carbonated soft drink flavored with extracts of the guarana fruit, especially popular in Brazil

Examples

Brazilian guarana soda
In Brazil, many people enjoy drinking guarana with their meals.

The store sells several brands of guarana imported from South America.
