tracheotomize

verb
to create airway surgically
9.9
Pronunciation: /trə.ˈkiː.ə.tə.maɪz/ /; / /ˈtreɪ.kiː.ə.tə.maɪz/ (AmE), /trə.ˈkiː.ə.tə.maɪz/ /; / /ˈtreɪ.kiː.ə.tə.maɪz/ (BrE)
to perform a surgical procedure to create an opening in the trachea to allow breathing

Examples

To tracheotomize a patient
In a life-threatening situation, the doctor had to tracheotomize the patient to restore breathing.
Only trained surgeons are qualified to tracheotomize someone in a hospital setting.
The emergency team quickly tracheotomized the accident victim to prevent suffocation.
