triple

noun
baseball hit for three bases
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪpəl / (AmE), / ˈtrɪpəl / (BrE)
(in baseball) a hit that allows the batter to reach third base safely on a single play.

Examples

A triple in baseball
Despite the pitcher’s best efforts, the batter managed a triple to center field.
She scored a triple that helped her team take the lead.
The crowd cheered as he hit a triple in the bottom of the ninth.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: double - (baseball) a hit that allows the batter to reach second base
  • degree higher: home run - (baseball) a hit that allows the batter to circle all bases and score
