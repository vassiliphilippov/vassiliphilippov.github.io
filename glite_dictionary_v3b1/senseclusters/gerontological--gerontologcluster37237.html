gerontological

adjective
relating to the study of aging
9.5
Pronunciation: / dʒɛrəntəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / dʒɛrəntəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study of aging and the problems of elderly people or to the practice of gerontology.

Examples

Gerontological medicine
Advances in gerontological medicine are helping people lead healthier lives as they age.
She is conducting a gerontological study focusing on social support in late adulthood.
The conference featured several experts in gerontological care and research.

Related Terms
