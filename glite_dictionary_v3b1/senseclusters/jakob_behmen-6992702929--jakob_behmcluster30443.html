Jakob Behmen

proper noun
German mystic and theosophist (1575–1624)
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒæk.əb ˈbeɪ.mən/ (AmE), /ˈdʒæk.əb ˈbeɪ.mən/ (BrE)
