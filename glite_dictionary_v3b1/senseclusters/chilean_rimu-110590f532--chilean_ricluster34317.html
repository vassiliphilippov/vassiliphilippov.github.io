Chilean rimu

noun
a hardy shrub from southern Chile
9.9
Pronunciation: / tʃɪˈliən ˈraɪmu / (AmE), / tʃɪˈliːən ˈriːmuː / (BrE)
a hardy, prostrate, spreading shrub of the Podocarpaceae family, resembling the mountain rimu and native to the mountains of southern Chile

Unlike other members of its family, the Chilean rimu spreads prostrate along the rocky ground.
