Bible thumper

noun
zealously outspoken religious person
4.2 9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪbəl ˌθʌmpər / (AmE), / ˈbaɪbəl ˌθʌmpə / (BrE)
A person who is aggressively or ostentatiously fervent in their religious beliefs, especially Christianity, and often vocal about promoting them.

Examples

His grandmother was a devoted Bible thumper who never missed a chance to talk about faith.
The politician was accused of being a Bible thumper during his campaign speeches.
