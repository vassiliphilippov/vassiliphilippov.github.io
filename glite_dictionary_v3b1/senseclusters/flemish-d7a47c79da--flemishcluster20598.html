Flemish

noun
member of the Flemish people of Belgium
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈflɛmɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈflɛmɪʃ / (BrE)
a member of an ethnic group speaking Flemish and living in northern and western Belgium

Examples

The Flemish are known for their rich tradition in art and literature.
