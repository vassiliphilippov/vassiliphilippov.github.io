Diegueño

noun
Native American people of southern California
Pronunciation: / diˈeɪɡwəˌnjoʊ / (AmE), / diˈeɪɡwɛn.jəʊ / (BrE)
A member of a Native American people from southern California and northern Baja California.

Examples

Diegueño people
Efforts to preserve Diegueño language and culture continue today.
Many Diegueño traditions are passed down through oral storytelling.
The Diegueño people have lived in the region for centuries.
