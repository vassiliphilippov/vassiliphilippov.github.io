dexterity

noun
skill in using the hands
Pronunciation: / dɛkˈstɛr.ɪ.ti / (AmE), / dɛkˈstɛr.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
Skill and ease in using the hands or body; adroitness or agility.

Examples

Dexterity with hands
Her dexterity with the sewing needle was impressive.
The magician demonstrated incredible dexterity during his card tricks.
Video games can help improve hand-eye coordination and dexterity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adroitness - skill and cleverness, especially in using the hands or mind
  • antonym: clumsiness - lack of skill or grace in movement, especially of the hands
