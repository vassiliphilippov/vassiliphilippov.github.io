Skill and ease in using the hands or body; adroitness or agility.

Examples

Dexterity with hands
Her dexterity with the sewing needle was impressive.
The magician demonstrated incredible dexterity during his card tricks.
Video games can help improve hand-eye coordination and dexterity.

Synonyms

  • manual dexterity
    4.9 0.17/m
    manual dexterity is skill and ease using the hands for precise tasks, while dexterity can mean hand or whole-body agility.
  • deftness
    5.1 0.16/m
    deftness is quick, skillful movement especially with the hands, while dexterity refers to skill and ease in movement or tasks.
  • adroitness
    7.5 0.08/m
    adroitness shows clever skill in action or thought, while dexterity focuses more on physical hand or body skill and ease.
  • handiness
    4.3 0.00/m
    handiness means being good with simple tools and small repairs, while dexterity covers physical skill and quickness more broadly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clumsiness - lack of skill or grace in movement, especially of the hands
