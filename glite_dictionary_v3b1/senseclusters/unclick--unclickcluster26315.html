unclick

verb
to release a latch
6.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈklɪk / (AmE), / ʌnˈklɪk / (BrE)
to release or disengage a catch, latch, or fastening that was previously secured with a click

Examples

To unclick a latch
She had to unclick the safety latch before folding the stroller into the car.
He quickly unclicked his seatbelt as soon as the car stopped.
To remove the cover, simply unclick the tabs on both sides.

Related Terms

  • antonym: click - to fasten or engage with a clicking sound
  • synonym: unlock - to open or release something that is locked
