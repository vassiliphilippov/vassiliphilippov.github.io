order Foraminifera

noun
an order of shell-bearing protozoans
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdɚ fəˌræməˈnɪfərə / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə fəˌræmɪˈnɪfərə / (BrE)
A large order of chiefly marine protozoans with perforated shells, known as foraminifers.
