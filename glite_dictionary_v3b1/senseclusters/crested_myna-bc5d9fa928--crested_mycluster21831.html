crested myna

noun
species of crested bird native to southeastern Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛstɪd ˈmaɪnə / (AmE), / ˈkrɛstɪd ˈmaɪnə / (BrE)
a dark brown bird of southeastern Asia, recognized by its prominent crest and often found in urban areas

Examples
