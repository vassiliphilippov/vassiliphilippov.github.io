midyear

noun
middle of the year
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌjɪr / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌjɪə / (BrE)
the middle part of the year, typically around June or July

Examples

The company holds its financial review at midyear to assess progress.
Many students take a break from classes around midyear.
