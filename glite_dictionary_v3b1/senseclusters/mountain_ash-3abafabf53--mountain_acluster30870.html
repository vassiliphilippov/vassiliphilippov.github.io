mountain ash

noun
a tree of the genus Sorbus
9.1 5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntən æʃ / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn æʃ / (BrE)
a tree of the genus Sorbus, especially the rowan, with pinnate leaves and clusters of white flowers and red or orange berries
