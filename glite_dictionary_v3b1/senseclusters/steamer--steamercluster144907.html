steamer

noun
ship powered by steam engines
3.9 1.70/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàstiÀêm…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàstiÀêm…ô / (BrE)
A ship powered by one or more steam engines, often used for transporting people or goods.

Examples

A river steamer
He collects models of historic steamers from the nineteenth century.
The company operated a fleet of steamers on the Great Lakes for many decades.
The old steamer sailed up the river carrying passengers to distant towns.

Synonyms
