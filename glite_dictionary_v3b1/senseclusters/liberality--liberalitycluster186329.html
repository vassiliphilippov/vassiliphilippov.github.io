liberality

noun
generosity; open-handedness
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪbəˈræləti / (AmE), / ˌlɪbəˈrælɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being generous or open-handed; willingness to give freely or act with generosity

Examples

His liberality with money
He was respected for his liberality in both thought and action.
Her liberality towards the less fortunate earned her admiration throughout the community.
The foundation supports educational projects thanks to the liberality of its donors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generosity - willingness to give more than is necessary or expected
  • antonym: stinginess - lack of generosity; unwillingness to give or spend
