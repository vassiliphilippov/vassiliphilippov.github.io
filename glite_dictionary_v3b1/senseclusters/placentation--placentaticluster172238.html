placentation

noun
arrangement of ovules/placentas in a plant ovary
Pronunciation: / ˌplæsənˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌplæsɛnˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the arrangement of ovules within the placenta of a plant ovary, or of the placentas within the ovary

Examples

Plant placentation
Marginal placentation is commonly found in pea and bean plants.
The study focused on how different forms of placentation influence seed development.
The type of placentation can help botanists identify different species of flowering plants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ovule - a structure in seed plants that develops into a seed after fertilization
  • synonym: placenta - the organ in the uterus of pregnant mammals or the tissue by which nutrients pass to the seed in plants
