intersystem

adjective
between systems
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
relating to or occurring between two or more systems

Examples

Intersystem communication
The intersystem interface allows different networks to exchange information efficiently.
Researchers studied intersystem interactions to understand how the two ecosystems affect each other.
An intersystem agreement was signed to coordinate efforts between the two organizations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intrasystem - within a single system
