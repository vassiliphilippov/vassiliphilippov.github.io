Mithraic

adjective
relating to Mithraism or its god Mithras
Pronunciation: / mɪˈθreɪɪk / (AmE), / mɪˈθreɪɪk / (BrE)
of or relating to Mithraism, an ancient Persian religion, or its god Mithras

Examples

Mithraic religion
Scholars have debated the influence of Mithraic beliefs on early Christian traditions.
Several ancient carvings depict Mithraic rituals performed in underground temples.
The Mithraic mysteries were popular among Roman soldiers in the 2nd and 3rd centuries CE.
