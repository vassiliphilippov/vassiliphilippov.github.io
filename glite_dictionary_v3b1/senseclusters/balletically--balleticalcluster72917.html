balletically

adverb
gracefully, like ballet
7.4
Pronunciation: / bəˈlɛtɪkli / (AmE), / bəˈlɛtɪkli / (BrE)
in a graceful and elegant manner, like a ballet dancer

Examples

Moves balletically gracefully
The cat leaped balletically onto the windowsill, barely making a sound.
He spun balletically across the polished floor during the dance competition.
The leaves fell balletically from the trees, swirling gently in the autumn breeze.

Related Terms

  • antonym: awkwardly - in a clumsy or ungraceful way
  • synonym: gracefully - in a smooth and elegant way
