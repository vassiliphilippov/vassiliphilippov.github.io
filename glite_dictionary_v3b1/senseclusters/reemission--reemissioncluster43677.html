reemission

noun
issuing again
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːɪˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of issuing or sending something again, such as a document, certificate, or official communication

Examples

Bank reemission of cards
After the error was found, the bank processed the reemission of the updated statements.
The reemission of the license was necessary due to a change in the applicant's address.
Government regulations require the reemission of certificates if any information is incorrect.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cancellation - the act of making something no longer valid
  • synonym: reissue - the act of issuing something again
