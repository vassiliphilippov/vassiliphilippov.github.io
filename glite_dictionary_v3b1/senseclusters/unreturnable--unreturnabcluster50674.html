unreturnable

adjective
cannot be repaid
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈtɜrnəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈtɜːnəbl / (BrE)
impossible or very difficult to repay, return, or reciprocate in kind.

Examples

Unreturnable kindness
The kindness she showed was so great that it felt unreturnable.
He gave an unreturnable gift that changed her life forever.
Some favors are so generous they seem almost unreturnable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irreversible - impossible to reverse or undo
  • antonym: reciprocable - able to be returned or repaid in kind
