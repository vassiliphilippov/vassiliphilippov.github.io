amicability

noun
friendliness; warmth of manner
Pronunciation: / ˌæmɪkəˈbɪləti /; / ˌæmɪkəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌamɪkəˈbɪlɪti /; / ˌæmɪkəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being friendly and pleasant; a tendency to avoid conflict

Her amicability made her a pleasure to work with, even in stressful situations.
