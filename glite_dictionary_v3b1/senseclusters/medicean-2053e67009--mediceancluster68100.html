Medicean

adjective
of the Medici family
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛdɪˈsiːən / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɪˈsiːən / (BrE)
relating to the Medici family, especially their influence in Renaissance Italy

Examples

Medicean art patronage
The Medicean patronage of the arts led to the creation of many masterpieces in Florence.
Historians often discuss the Medicean era as a turning point in European culture.
The Medicean influence can still be seen in the architecture of the city.
