dole out

verb
distribute something in small amounts
7.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /doʊl aʊt/ (AmE), /dəʊl aʊt/ /; / /dɒl aʊt/ (BrE)
to give or distribute something to several people, usually in small amounts or over a period of time
