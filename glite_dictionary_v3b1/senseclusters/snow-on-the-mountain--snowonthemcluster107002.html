snow-on-the-mountain

noun
annual plant with white-bracted flowers
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌsnoʊ ɑn ðə ˈmaʊntən / (AmE), / ˌsnəʊ ɒn ðə ˈmaʊntɪn / (BrE)
A plant of the western United States (Euphorbia marginata) with showy white-bracted flower clusters and very poisonous milky sap.

Examples

Snow-on-the-mountain plant
Be careful handling snow-on-the-mountain because its milky sap is highly poisonous.
She planted snow-on-the-mountain for its decorative white-bracted flowers.
The garden featured a beautiful patch of snow-on-the-mountain along the walkway.
