chicle

noun
natural gum from the sapodilla tree
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkl / (BrE)
a natural gum obtained from the sapodilla tree, formerly used as the base for chewing gum

Examples

Natural chicle gum
Synthetic gums have largely replaced chicle in modern gum production.
The workers collected chicle by tapping the bark of sapodilla trees.
Traditional chewing gum was made from chicle harvested in Central America.
