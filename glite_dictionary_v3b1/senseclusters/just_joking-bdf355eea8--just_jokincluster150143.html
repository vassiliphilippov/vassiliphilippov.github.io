just joking

x
not meant seriously; as a joke
Pronunciation: / dʒəst ˈdʒoʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / dʒʌst ˈdʒəʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
used to say that what was said was meant humorously and not seriously

Examples

Just joking with you
Don't worry, he's just joking with you—he didn't mean it.
I'm just joking; I didn't mean to offend you.
She said she had eaten all the cake, but she was just joking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: just kidding - used to show something was not meant seriously
  • antonym: serious - not meant as a joke; sincere
