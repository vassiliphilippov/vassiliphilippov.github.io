recommission

verb
to assign again
6.6
Pronunciation: /riː.kə.ˈmɪʃ.ən/ /; / /rɪ.kə.ˈmɪʃ.ən/ /; / /rə.kə.ˈmɪʃ.ən/ (AmE), /riː.kə.ˈmɪʃ.ən/ /; / /rɪ.kə.ˈmɪʃ.ən/ /; / /rə.kə.ˈmɪʃ.ən/ (BrE)
to put something (such as a ship or facility) back into service or use after it has been inactive

Examples

The factory was recommissioned to produce medical supplies during the emergency.
