arancini

noun
Italian rice balls
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrənˈtʃiːni / (AmE), / ˌærənˈtʃiːni / (BrE)
Italian rice balls filled with ingredients such as cheese or meat, coated in breadcrumbs, and deep-fried.

Examples

Fried arancini balls
We ordered a plate of arancini as an appetizer at the Italian restaurant.
The chef prepared homemade arancini filled with mozzarella and peas for the party.
I love the crispy texture of arancini served with a side of marinara sauce.
