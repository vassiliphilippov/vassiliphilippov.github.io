confer

verb
to grant (a degree, honor, etc.) officially
8.3 5.0
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈfɜːr/ (AmE), /kən.ˈfɜː/ (BrE)
to officially give or grant (a degree, honor, right, or title) to someone

Examples

To confer a degree
After much deliberation, the board conferred full membership on the new partners.
Each year, the college confers honorary degrees to distinguished guests.
The committee decided to confer the award upon Dr. Lee for her outstanding research.
