mention

verb
tag a user to notify them online
3.0
Pronunciation: / c8m5bn.t8359n / (AmE), / c8m5bn.t8359n / (BrE)
(on social media or collaborative platforms) to include or tag another user (often with @username) in a post or comment so that they are directly notified or linked.

Examples

To mention a user
You can mention a colleague in the comment to make sure they see the update.
She mentioned the designer by writing @alex so he would see the message.
If you mention someone in a post, the platform usually sends them a notification.

Related Terms

  • synonym: notify - to send an alert or message to inform someone about something
  • synonym: tag - to label or identify someone in a social-media post, often creating a link to their profile
