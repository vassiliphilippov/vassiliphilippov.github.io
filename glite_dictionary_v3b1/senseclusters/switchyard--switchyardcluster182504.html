switchyard

noun
train or circuit sorting area
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪtʃˌjɑrd / (AmE), / ˈswɪtʃˌjɑːd / (BrE)
an area where trains or electrical circuits are switched, sorted, or organized

Examples

The switchyard area
The engineer directed the train into the switchyard for sorting before its next journey.
Technicians worked in the switchyard to reroute electrical circuits during the maintenance.
Freight cars waited in the switchyard until they were assigned to their respective trains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depot - a place where vehicles or goods are stored and maintained
  • synonym: rail yard - an area where trains are stored, sorted, or loaded
