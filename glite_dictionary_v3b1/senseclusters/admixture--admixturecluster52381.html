admixture

noun
added ingredient; mixture component
Pronunciation: / ædˈmɪkstʃɚ /; / ædˈmɪkstʃər / (AmE), / ədˈmɪkstʃə /; / ædˈmɪkstjə / (BrE)
something that is added and mixed with another thing, or the result of mixing different things together

Examples

Chemical admixture
Ancient concrete often contained an admixture of volcanic ash to improve durability.
The recipe calls for an admixture of herbs to enhance the flavor.
There was a noticeable admixture of optimism in his otherwise cautious remarks.
A small admixture of foreign words enriches the English language.
Cultural admixture can create unique traditions within a community.
The admixture of water and cement forms the basis of concrete.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mixture - a combination of two or more substances
