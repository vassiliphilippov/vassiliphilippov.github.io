morphologist

noun
form and structure expert
8.8
Pronunciation: / mɔrˈfɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / mɔːˈfɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies the form and structure of things, especially in biology or language

Examples

A morphologist studies form
The morphologist identified subtle differences in the shapes of the bird species.
As a morphologist, she analyzes the structure of words in different languages.
A skilled morphologist can reveal how plants adapt their forms to new environments.
The morphologist examined the differences in leaf shapes among the plant samples.
As a morphologist, she specializes in analyzing the structure of animal cells.
A morphologist can also study how words are formed in different languages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biologist - a scientist who studies living organisms
  • synonym: linguist - a person who studies language
