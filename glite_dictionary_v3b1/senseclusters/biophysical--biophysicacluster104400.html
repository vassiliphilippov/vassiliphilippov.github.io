biophysical

adjective
physical aspects of life
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊˈfɪz.ɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.əʊˈfɪz.ɪkəl / (BrE)

Examples

Biophysical processes
Researchers study the biophysical mechanisms that control how cells communicate with each other.
Understanding the biophysical environment of a habitat helps scientists predict how species will respond to change.
The biophysical properties of proteins are essential for their biological functions.
