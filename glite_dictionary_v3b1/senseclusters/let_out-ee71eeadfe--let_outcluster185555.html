let out

verb
allow to leave or be released
1.0
Pronunciation: / lɛt aʊt / (AmE), / lɛt aʊt / (BrE)

Examples

To let out a dog

She promised to let out the dog before leaving for work.
The teacher let out the students early due to the assembly.
