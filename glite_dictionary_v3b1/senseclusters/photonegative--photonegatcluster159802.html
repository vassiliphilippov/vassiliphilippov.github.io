photonegative

adjective
from photographic negative
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊˈnɛɡətɪv / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊˈnɛɡətɪv / (BrE)
relating to or produced from a photographic negative, especially describing film, plates, or images made using negatives.

Examples

Photonegative film image
The photonegative image revealed details that were not visible in the original photograph.
She carefully handled the photonegative film to avoid damaging the images.
Old photonegative plates are often stored in archives for historical preservation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: photographic - relating to photographs or photography
  • antonym: positive - an image with normal tones, opposite of a negative
