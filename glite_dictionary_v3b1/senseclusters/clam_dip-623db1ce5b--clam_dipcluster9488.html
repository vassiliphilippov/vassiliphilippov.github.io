clam dip

noun
a creamy dip made with clams and cheese
Pronunciation: / klæm dɪp / (AmE), / klæm dɪp / (BrE)
A cold, creamy dip made from chopped clams and soft cream cheese, often served as an appetizer with crackers or chips.

Examples

A bowl of clam dip
She brought a bowl of clam dip to the party, and it was gone in minutes.
This recipe for clam dip calls for fresh chives and a hint of lemon juice.
We served crackers and clam dip as appetizers before dinner.
