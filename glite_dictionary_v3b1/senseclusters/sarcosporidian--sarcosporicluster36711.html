sarcosporidian

noun
parasite of vertebrate muscles
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɑr.kə.spəˈrɪd.i.ən / (AmE), / ˌsɑː.kə.spəˈrɪd.i.ən / (BrE)
a protozoan parasite that infects the muscles of vertebrates, often forming visible cysts within muscle tissue
