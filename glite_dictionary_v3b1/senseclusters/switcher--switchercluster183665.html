switcher

noun
device or person changing settings
4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈswɪ.tʃ.ər/ (AmE), /ˈswɪ.tʃ.ə/ (BrE)
a device or person that changes connections, channels, or settings from one to another

Examples

Video switcher
The video technician used a switcher to alternate between camera feeds during the live broadcast.
A skilled switcher can quickly change the lighting scenes in a theater production.
He bought a new HDMI switcher to connect multiple devices to his television.
