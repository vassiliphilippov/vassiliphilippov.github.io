procuratorate

noun
office of a procurator
Pronunciation: / proʊˈkjʊrətərət / (AmE), / prəˈkjʊərətərət / (BrE)
the office, duties, or area of authority of a procurator, especially as an administrative or financial authority in ancient Rome

Examples

Roman procuratorate
The provincial procuratorate managed tax collection and imperial estates during the Roman administration.
Historians study the role of the procuratorate in maintaining order in Roman provinces.
The procuratorate was responsible for overseeing financial matters in several regions of the empire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: governor - an official in charge of a region or province
  • synonym: procurator - an official who manages affairs for another, especially in ancient Rome
