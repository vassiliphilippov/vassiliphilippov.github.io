restate

verb
express again in different words
3.4 2.04/m
Pronunciation: / riÀàste…™t / (AmE), / riÀêÀàste…™t / (BrE)
to say or express something again, often in a different way or for emphasis

Examples

To restate the question
Let me restate the question to make sure everyone understands.
She had to restate her opinion because it was misunderstood the first time.
The manager asked him to restate the proposal using simpler language.
