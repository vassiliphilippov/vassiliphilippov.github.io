paddle-like

adjective
resembling a paddle
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpædəlˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈpædəlˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having a shape or appearance similar to a paddle, especially broad and flat

Examples

Paddle-like feet
The platypus has paddle-like feet that help it swim efficiently in the water.
Her hands were broad and paddle-like, perfect for rowing the boat.
The leaves of the plant are paddle-like, wide and flat to catch more sunlight.

Related Terms

  • antonym: narrow - having a small width, not broad or wide
  • synonym: spatulate - broad and flat, shaped like a spatula
