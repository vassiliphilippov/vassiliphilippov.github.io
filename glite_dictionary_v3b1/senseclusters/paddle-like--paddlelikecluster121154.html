paddle-like

adjective
resembling a paddle
Pronunciation: / ˈpædəlˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈpædəlˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having a shape or appearance similar to a paddle, especially broad and flat

Examples

Her hands were broad and paddle-like, perfect for rowing the boat.
The leaves of the plant are paddle-like, wide and flat to catch more sunlight.
