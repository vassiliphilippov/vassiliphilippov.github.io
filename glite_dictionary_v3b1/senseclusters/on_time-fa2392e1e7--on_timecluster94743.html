on time

adjective
paid for in regular installments
4.8
Pronunciation: / ɑn taɪm / (AmE), / ɒn taɪm / (BrE)
(of a purchase) paid for in regular installments over a period rather than in a single payment.

Examples

Buy a car on time
Many people purchase cars on time to make the payments more manageable.
The store offered special deals for customers purchasing appliances on time.
They bought their new furniture on time because they couldn't afford to pay all at once.

Related Terms

  • synonym: on credit - bought by promising to pay later
