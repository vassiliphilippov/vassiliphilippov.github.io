unaerated

adjective
not supplied with oxygen (e.g., blood)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɛrˌeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈeərˌeɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a liquid, especially blood) not supplied or mixed with oxygen; lacking aeration.

Examples
