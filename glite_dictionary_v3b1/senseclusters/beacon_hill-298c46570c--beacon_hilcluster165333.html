Beacon Hill

proper noun
historic Boston district; Massachusetts State House location
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːkən hɪl / (AmE), / ˈbiːkən hɪl / (BrE)
a historic and fashionable district of Boston, Massachusetts, known for its Federal-style rowhouses and the Massachusetts State House

Examples
