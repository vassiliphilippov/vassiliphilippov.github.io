Synonyms

  • obligated
    4.2 0.9/m
    obligated is required by law or personal conscience to act, while duty-bound expresses a strong moral responsibility to act.
  • bound
    6.0 1/m
    bound is often used for legal or contractual duty, while duty-bound stresses a personal, moral sense of responsibility.
  • obliged
    4.4 0.01/m
    obliged feels like a social or moral push to act politely, while duty-bound shows a firm inner responsibility to act.
