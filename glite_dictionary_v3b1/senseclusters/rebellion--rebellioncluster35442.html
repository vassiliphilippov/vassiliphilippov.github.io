Synonyms

  • insurrection
    9.1 1/m
    insurrection is a sudden, violent attempt to overthrow a government, while rebellion is organized armed resistance against authority.
  • revolt
    4.6 3/m
    revolt is a direct act by people to remove a ruler, while rebellion is broader organized armed opposition against authority.
  • uprising
    5.1 7/m
    uprising is a local or popular movement using force, while rebellion is an organized armed effort to challenge authority.
  • rising
    6.8 0.3/m
    rising is an often older word for a people-led armed revolt, while rebellion is a general organized resistance against authority.
  • insurgency
    8.4 3/m
    insurgency is a long armed campaign by rebels using hit-and-run tactics, while rebellion is the act of organized resistance against authority.
  • insurgence
    8.7 0.06/m
    insurgence is a rare term for an armed revolt or uprising, while rebellion is the common word for organized challenge to authority.
