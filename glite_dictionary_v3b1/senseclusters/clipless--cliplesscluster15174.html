clipless

adjective
bicycle pedal type
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪpləs / (AmE), / ˈklɪpləs / (BrE)
describing a type of bicycle pedal that attaches to special shoes for a secure connection, without traditional toe clips

Examples

Clipless bike pedals
He switched to clipless pedals to improve his cycling efficiency.
Many professional riders use clipless systems for better control and power transfer.
Learning to use clipless pedals can take some practice for new cyclists.

Related Terms

  • antonym: platform - a flat bicycle pedal without clips or attachments
