nonacceptance

noun
refusal to accept something
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.əkˈsɛp.təns / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.əkˈsɛp.təns / (BrE)
the act of rejecting or not accepting something, especially an offer or proposal

Examples

His nonacceptance of invitation
His nonacceptance of the invitation was conveyed in a short, polite letter.
She dealt with the nonacceptance of her idea by proposing an alternative solution.
The company's nonacceptance of the proposal surprised all the board members.

Related Terms

  • antonym: acceptance - the act of consenting to receive or undertake something
  • synonym: rejection - the act of refusing something
