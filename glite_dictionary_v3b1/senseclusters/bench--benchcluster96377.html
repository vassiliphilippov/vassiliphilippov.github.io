bench

noun
reserve players on a sports team
Pronunciation: / bɛntʃ / (AmE), / bɛntʃ / (BrE)
the reserve players on a sports team who are not playing in the game but may be substituted in

Examples

Bench players
Our bench is deep, so we always have strong substitutes waiting.
She came off the bench and immediately made an impact.
The coach looked to the bench for fresh players late in the game.

Related Terms

  • antonym: starting lineup - the group of players who begin a game on the field or court
