unpeg

verb
to remove a peg
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpɛɡ / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɛɡ / (BrE)
to remove a peg from something or release something that is fastened with a peg

Examples

To unpeg the clothes
She quickly unpegged the laundry from the clothesline as the rain started.
Before folding the tent, you need to unpeg it from the ground.
He carefully unpegged each photo from the display board.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detach - to separate something from what it is attached to
  • antonym: peg - to fasten or secure with a peg
