hair grease

noun
a thick hair styling product
Pronunciation: / hɛr ɡris / (AmE), / heə ɡriːs / (BrE)
a thick, oily substance applied to the hair to add shine and moisture, or to style it

The barber recommended a light hair grease to style my curly hair.
