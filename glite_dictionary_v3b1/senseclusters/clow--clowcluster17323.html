clow

noun
animal claw (dialect)
9.9
a claw or talon of an animal, used in some regional dialects

Examples

Animal clow
The fox left deep clow marks on the soft soil near the barn.
She found a broken clow beside the nest in the old tree.
Farmers sometimes feared the sharp clow of a hawk swooping down on their chickens.

Related Terms

  • synonym: claw - a sharp, curved nail on the foot of an animal
  • synonym: talon - a large, hooked claw, especially of a bird of prey
