immunofluorescent

adjective
using fluorescent antibodies
8.2
Pronunciation: / ɪˌmjunəʊflʊˈrɛsənt / (AmE), / ɪˌmjuːnəʊflʊˈrɛsənt / (BrE)
relating to or showing the use of fluorescent-labeled antibodies to detect substances in laboratory samples

Examples
