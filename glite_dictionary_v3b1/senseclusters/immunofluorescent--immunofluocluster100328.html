immunofluorescent

adjective
using fluorescent antibodies
Pronunciation: / ɪˌmjunəʊflʊˈrɛsənt / (AmE), / ɪˌmjuːnəʊflʊˈrɛsənt / (BrE)
relating to or showing the use of fluorescent-labeled antibodies to detect substances in laboratory samples

Examples

Immunofluorescent antibody test
The laboratory used an immunofluorescent method to identify the presence of the virus in the sample.
Researchers observed immunofluorescent staining to locate specific proteins within the tissue sections.
An immunofluorescent assay can help diagnose certain infections quickly and accurately.
The cells appeared immunofluorescent under the microscope after the staining procedure.
Researchers used an immunofluorescent marker to detect the presence of the virus in tissue samples.
An immunofluorescent signal confirmed the antibody had successfully bound to its target.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fluorescent - emitting light as a result of absorbing radiation
  • synonym: immunohistochemical - relating to a technique using antibodies to detect substances in tissues
