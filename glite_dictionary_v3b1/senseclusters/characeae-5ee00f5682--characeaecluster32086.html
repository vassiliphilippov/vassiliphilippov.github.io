Characeae

noun
family of green algae (stoneworts)
Pronunciation: / kəˈreɪɪˌsiː / (AmE), / kəˈreɪsiːiː / (BrE)
a family of green algae known as stoneworts, typically found in fresh water and superficially resembling horsetail ferns
