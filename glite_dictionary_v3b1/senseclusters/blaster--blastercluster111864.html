blaster

noun
fictional energy weapon
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈblæstər / (AmE), / ˈblɑːstə / (BrE)
a fictional or toy weapon that shoots energy beams or projectiles, often seen in science fiction

Examples

In the game, you can upgrade your blaster to shoot more powerful energy bolts.

The hero drew his blaster and prepared to face the alien invaders.
Children ran around the yard, each holding a brightly colored blaster in their hands.
In the movie, the villain's blaster could destroy an entire spaceship with one shot.

  • synonym: ray gun - a fictional gun that emits energy rays
