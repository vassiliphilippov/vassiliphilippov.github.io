Kleistian

adjective
of Kleist's style
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈklaɪstiən / (AmE), / ˈklaɪstiən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the works or style of Heinrich von Kleist, a German writer

Examples

Kleistian drama style
The director's adaptation had a distinctly Kleistian sense of psychological tension.
Her writing style is often described as Kleistian due to its dramatic intensity.
The novel's plot twists are reminiscent of Kleistian themes of fate and conflict.
