chemically

adverb
in a way relating to chemistry
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛmɪkli / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪkli /; / ˈkɛmɪkəli / (BrE)
in a way that involves chemistry or chemicals

Examples

React chemically
The two solutions react chemically to produce a new compound.
The two substances may look alike, but they are chemically very different.
This vegetable has been chemically treated to increase its shelf life.
Food can be chemically altered during processing.
The soil was chemically treated to kill weeds.
This material breaks down chemically over time when exposed to light.
