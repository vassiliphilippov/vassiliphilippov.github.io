electroacoustic

adjective
electronic and acoustic
7.2
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊəˈkuːstɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊəˈkuːstɪk / (BrE)
relating to the combination or interaction of electronic and acoustic sound or technology

Examples

Electroacoustic music
The band performed an electroacoustic set, blending synthesizers with traditional guitars.
She is interested in electroacoustic engineering, which involves both sound and electronic systems.
Many modern composers experiment with electroacoustic techniques in their music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electronic - relating to devices or music using electronic technology
