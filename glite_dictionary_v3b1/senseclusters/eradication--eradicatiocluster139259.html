eradication

noun
complete destruction or removal of something
Pronunciation: / ɪˌrædɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌrædɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the complete destruction or removal of something, especially something unwanted

Examples

Complete eradication
Complete eradication of poverty remains a complex global challenge.
Government agencies are working towards the eradication of invasive species in the wetlands.
The eradication of smallpox is considered one of medicine's greatest achievements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elimination - the act of removing or getting rid of something
  • synonym: extermination - the act of destroying completely, especially living things
  • antonym: preservation - the act of keeping something from being destroyed
