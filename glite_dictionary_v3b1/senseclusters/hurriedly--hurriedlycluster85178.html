hurriedly

adverb
in a hurried or hasty manner
Pronunciation: / ˈhɝːɪdli / (AmE), / ˈhʌrɪdli / (BrE)
In a hurried or hasty manner; quickly and with little care or thought.

Examples

To hurriedly leave
She hurriedly gathered her belongings and left the room.
The letter was hurriedly written just before the mailman arrived.
They hurriedly finished their dinner when they realized they were late.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carefully - with attention and caution
  • synonym: hastily - done with excessive speed or urgency
