Germanism

noun
German-derived word or custom
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɜːrmənɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːmənɪzəm / (BrE)
a word, phrase, or custom borrowed from German or characteristic of German language or culture

Examples

A Germanism in language
The word 'doppelganger' is a well-known Germanism used in English.
Many culinary terms in English, such as 'sauerkraut', are actually Germanisms.
The tradition of the Christmas tree is a cultural Germanism adopted in many countries.

Related Terms
