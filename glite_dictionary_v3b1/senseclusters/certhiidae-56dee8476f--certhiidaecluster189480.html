Certhiidae

noun
family of creeper birds
9.9
Pronunciation: / sərˈθɪɪˌdiː / (AmE), / səˈθɪɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of small, slender, tree-climbing birds commonly known as creepers, found in Eurasia and North America.
