nonuse

noun
failure to use
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈjus / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈjuːs / (BrE)
the state or condition of not using something

Examples

The nonuse period
The nonuse of the building for several years led to its deterioration.
Trademark nonuse can result in the loss of legal protection for the brand.
After years of nonuse, the property was declared abandoned by the city.
The nonuse of the gym membership meant he wasted his money.
Prolonged nonuse of the machinery caused it to rust and break down.
Doctors warned that nonuse of the injured arm could lead to muscle weakness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abandonment - the act of giving up or leaving something unused
  • synonym: disuse - the state of no longer being used
  • antonym: use - the act of employing or making use of something
