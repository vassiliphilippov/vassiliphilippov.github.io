unforgeable

adjective
cannot be faked
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɔrdʒəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɔːdʒəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to fake, copy, or imitate fraudulently

Examples

An unforgeable passport
The new passport features a security chip that makes it unforgeable.
Digital signatures are designed to be unforgeable for secure online transactions.
The artist's unique technique rendered his work virtually unforgeable by imitators.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authentic - genuine and not a copy or fake
  • antonym: forged - copied or imitated fraudulently
  • synonym: genuine - real and exactly what it appears to be
