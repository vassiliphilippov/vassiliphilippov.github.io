psychic

adjective
relating to supernatural phenomena
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪkɪk / (AmE), / ˈsaɪkɪk / (BrE)
Relating to phenomena or abilities that cannot be explained by physical science, often regarded as supernatural.

Examples

Psychic powers
Claims of psychic abilities often attract both skeptics and believers.
Many people believe in psychic phenomena such as telepathy and precognition.
The show explored various psychic experiences that defy scientific explanation.
