monotheist

noun
believer in one god
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːnəˌθiɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɒnəʊˌθiːɪst / (BrE)
A person who believes that there is only one God.

Examples

A monotheist believer
As a monotheist, he worships only one deity.
Christianity, Islam, and Judaism each require their followers to be a monotheist.
The monotheist rejected the existence of multiple gods.

Related Terms

  • antonym: atheist - a person who does not believe in any god
  • antonym: polytheist - a person who believes in more than one god
