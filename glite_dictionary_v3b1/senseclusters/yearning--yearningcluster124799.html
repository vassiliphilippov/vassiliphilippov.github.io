yearning

noun
strong persistent longing or desire
Pronunciation: / ˈjɝːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈjɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
a strong and persistent longing or desire, especially for something that is difficult or impossible to obtain

Examples

A deep yearning
Despite his success, he felt an unfulfilled yearning for something more meaningful.
His yearning for adventure led him to travel the world alone.
There was a deep yearning in her heart for the family she lost.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contentment - a state of happiness and satisfaction, opposite of yearning
  • synonym: craving - an intense desire for something
  • synonym: longing - a strong wish for something, especially something unattainable
