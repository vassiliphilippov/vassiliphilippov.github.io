spirograph

noun
device recording breathing patterns
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪrəˌɡræf / (AmE), / ˈspaɪərəˌɡrɑːf / (BrE)
a medical instrument used to record the depth and rate of breathing movements

Examples

A spirograph machine
A technician carefully calibrated the spirograph before starting the pulmonary function test.
Researchers analyzed the spirograph readings to assess the effects of exercise on breathing patterns.
The doctor used a spirograph to monitor the patient's lung function during the examination.
