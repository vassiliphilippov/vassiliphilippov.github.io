sonless

adjective
without a son
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnləs / (AmE), / ˈsʌnləs / (BrE)
not having a son or sons

Examples

Sonless family
The royal family was sonless, which caused concern about the future of the throne.
She often wondered how her life would be different if she were not sonless.
In some cultures, being sonless can affect a family's social status.
