prix fixe

adjective
fixed-price complete meal
9.9
Pronunciation: / priː ˈfɪks / (AmE), / priː ˈfiːks / (BrE)
(of a meal, especially in a restaurant) offered as a complete meal with limited choices at a fixed price.

Examples

Prix fixe dinner
Many upscale restaurants offer a prix fixe menu during special events.
She opted for the prix fixe option, knowing it was a better deal than ordering separately.
The prix fixe dinner included an appetizer, main course, and dessert.

Related Terms

  • antonym: à la carte - meals priced and ordered separately, not as a set menu
