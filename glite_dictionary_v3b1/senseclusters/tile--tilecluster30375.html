tile

noun
rectangular slab for covering surfaces
1.0
Pronunciation: / taɪl / (AmE), / taɪl / (BrE)
a flat, thin piece of hard material, such as ceramic or stone, used for covering roofs, floors, or walls

Examples

A ceramic tile
She chose a blue mosaic tile for the kitchen backsplash.
The bathroom floor was covered with ceramic tile.
Workers replaced the old roof tiles after the storm damaged them.
Each tile was carefully placed to create a patterned effect on the floor.
The house had traditional clay tiles covering its sloping roof.
The roof was repaired with new tiles after the storm damaged the old ones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shingle - a thin, flat piece of material used for roofing, similar but typically narrower than a tile
