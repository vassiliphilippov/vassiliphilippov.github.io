sunbathe

verb
sit or lie in the sun for tanning
1.0 2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈsʌn.beɪð/ (AmE), /ˈsʌn.beɪð/ (BrE)
to sit or lie in the sun, especially to make your skin brown by exposure to sunlight

Examples

To sunbathe on beach
During their vacation in Greece, they enjoyed the chance to sunbathe on the beach every morning.
Many tourists sunbathe by the pool despite warnings about the dangers of too much sun.
She likes to sunbathe in her backyard whenever the weather is warm and sunny.
