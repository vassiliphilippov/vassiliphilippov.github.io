aptness

noun
quality of being suitable or appropriate
Pronunciation: / ˈæpt.nəs / (AmE), / ˈæpt.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being especially fitting or appropriate for a particular situation

Examples

The aptness of a remark
Critics praised the aptness of the film's title.
She admired the aptness with which he chose his words.
The aptness of his response impressed everyone at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appropriateness - the quality of being suitable or proper in the circumstances
  • antonym: inappropriateness - the state of being unsuitable or not fitting
