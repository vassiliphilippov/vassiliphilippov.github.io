paraffin

noun
waxy solid from petroleum used for candles, coating
6.4 3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛrəˌfɪn /; / ˈpæɹ.ə.fɪn / (AmE), / ˈpærəfɪn /; / ˈpær.ə.fɪn / (BrE)
a white or colorless, waxy solid made from petroleum, used in candles, waterproofing, and as a preservative

Examples
