retirement fund

noun
sum set aside for post-retirement income
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtaɪɚmənt fʌnd / (AmE), / rɪˈtaɪəmənt fʌnd / (BrE)
A sum of money or savings set aside to provide income to an individual after they retire from work, often paid out as regular payments.

Examples

A retirement fund plan
Many employers offer a retirement fund as part of their benefits package.
Sarah contributed to her retirement fund every month to ensure a comfortable future.
Without a retirement fund, it's hard to maintain your lifestyle after leaving the workforce.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annuity - an income paid at regular intervals for a period of time, often after retirement
  • synonym: pension - a regular payment made to a retired person
