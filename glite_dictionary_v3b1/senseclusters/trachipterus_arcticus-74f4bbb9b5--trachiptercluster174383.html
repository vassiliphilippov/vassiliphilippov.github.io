Trachipterus arcticus

noun
a species of deep-sea ribbonfish
Pronunciation: / trəˌkɪptərəs ɑrˈktɪkəs / (AmE), / trəˌkɪptərəs ɑːkˈtɪkəs / (BrE)
A species of deep-sea ribbonfish found in northern Atlantic waters, known for its long, slender body and silvery appearance.
