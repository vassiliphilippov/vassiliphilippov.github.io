musette

noun
small bag or French music
8.2
Pronunciation: / mjuˈzɛt / (AmE), / mjuːˈzɛt / (BrE)
a small shoulder bag, especially one used by cyclists or soldiers, or a style of lively French dance music

Examples

French musette dance
The cyclist grabbed a musette filled with snacks during the race.
She danced to the lively musette at the festival.
He bought a vintage musette to use as a stylish shoulder bag.
