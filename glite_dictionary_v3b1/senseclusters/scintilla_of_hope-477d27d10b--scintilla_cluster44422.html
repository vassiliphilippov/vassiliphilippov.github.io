scintilla of hope

noun
a tiny amount of hope
Pronunciation: / sɪnˈtɪlə əv hoʊp / (AmE), / sɪnˈtɪlə əv həʊp / (BrE)
a very small amount of hope

Examples

A scintilla of hope
Despite the grim news, she still held on to a scintilla of hope for recovery.
The verdict left him with only a scintilla of hope for appeal.
There wasn’t even a scintilla of hope left in their eyes after the announcement.

  • synonym: ray of hope - a small sign of hope in a difficult situation
