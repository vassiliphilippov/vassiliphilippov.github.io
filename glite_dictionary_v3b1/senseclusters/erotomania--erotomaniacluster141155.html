erotomania

noun
obsessive sexual desire
Pronunciation: / ɪˌroʊtəˈmeɪniə / (AmE), / ɪˌrɒtəˈmeɪniə / (BrE)
an excessive or obsessive sexual desire, especially as a psychological condition; historically, an abnormal craving for sexual activity

Examples

Obsessive erotomania
In the nineteenth century, doctors sometimes diagnosed patients with erotomania for behaviors considered inappropriate.
The term erotomania is now rarely used in modern medical practice.
Victorian literature occasionally references erotomania as a supposed cause of scandalous conduct.

Related Terms

  • antonym: frigidity - lack of sexual desire or responsiveness
  • synonym: hypersexuality - abnormally increased sexual activity or desire
  • synonym: nymphomania - excessive sexual desire in women
