involve

verb
to make complicated or intricate
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɑlv / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɒlv / (BrE)
to make something complex or intricate; to make a situation or process complicated or difficult to understand

Examples

Involve many steps

The legal proceedings involved a series of complicated steps that confused many participants.
The project quickly involved more people and processes than originally anticipated.
