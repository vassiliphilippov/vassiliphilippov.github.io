ville

noun
small town or city
Pronunciation: / vɪl / (AmE), / vɪl / (BrE)
a small town or city, especially in French-speaking areas or as part of a place name

Examples

A small ville
They spent the summer in a quiet ville in the countryside.
The festival attracts visitors from every nearby ville each year.
Many French place names end with ville, meaning town or city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: city - a large and significant urban area
  • synonym: town - a human settlement larger than a village but smaller than a city
  • degree lower: village - a small settlement, usually in a rural area
