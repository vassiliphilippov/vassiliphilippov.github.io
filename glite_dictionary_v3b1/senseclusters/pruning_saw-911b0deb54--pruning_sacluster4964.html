pruning saw

noun
hand saw for pruning trees
Pronunciation: / ˈpruːnɪŋ sɔ / (AmE), / ˈpruːnɪŋ sɔː / (BrE)
a handsaw with a narrow blade, designed specifically for pruning trees and shrubs

Examples

A pruning saw blade
A good pruning saw is essential for maintaining healthy trees in your garden.
After sharpening the pruning saw, she trimmed the bushes more easily.
He used a pruning saw to remove the dead branches from the apple tree.

Related Terms

  • synonym: handsaw - a saw operated by hand, as opposed to a power saw
