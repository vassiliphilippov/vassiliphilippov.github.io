geocentrism

noun
Earth-centered worldview
9.6
Pronunciation: /dʒiː.oʊ.ˈsen.trɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /dʒiː.əʊ.ˈsen.trɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
the belief or worldview that the Earth or human life is the central or most important element of existence.

Examples

Cultural geocentrism
Some ancient cultures were based on geocentrism, believing the universe revolved around the Earth.
Modern science has replaced geocentrism with a more accurate understanding of our place in the cosmos.
Environmental debates sometimes reveal a subtle geocentrism that prioritizes human needs over those of other species.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anthropocentrism - the belief that humans are the most important entity in the universe
  • antonym: heliocentrism - the belief that the sun is at the center of the universe or solar system
