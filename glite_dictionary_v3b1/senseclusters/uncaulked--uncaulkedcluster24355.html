uncaulked

adjective
not caulked or sealed
6.0 7.3
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈkɑːkt/ /; / /ʌn.ˈkɔːkt/ /; / /ən.ˈkɑːkt/ /; / /ən.ˈkɔːkt/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈkɔːkt/ /; / /ən.ˈkɔːkt/ (BrE)
Not caulked or sealed; having gaps unfilled with caulk, so that air, water, or other substances can pass through.

Examples

Uncaulked gaps
Be sure to check for any uncaulked gaps around the bathtub before starting your renovation.
Rainwater leaked through the uncaulked window frame, damaging the wall beneath it.
The boat began to take in water due to several uncaulked seams in its hull.
