conversationally

adverb
in an informal, conversational manner
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːnvərˈseɪʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃənəli / (BrE)
in a manner that is typical of informal speech or conversation, often using colloquial expressions

Examples

Speak conversationally clearly
Even in interviews, he prefers to speak conversationally rather than using technical jargon.
She explained the topic conversationally, making everyone feel comfortable.
The author addresses the reader conversationally, as if they were old friends.
