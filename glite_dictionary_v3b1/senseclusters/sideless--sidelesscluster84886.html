sideless

adjective
neutral; impartial
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdləs / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdləs / (BrE)
not supporting or favoring any side in a dispute or argument; neutral or impartial

Examples

Sideless judge
The judge remained sideless throughout the trial, ensuring a fair process for everyone.
As a moderator, she tried to be completely sideless during the heated discussion.
His sideless attitude made him a trusted mediator in community disputes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: biased - showing an unfair preference for one side
  • synonym: impartial - treating all sides equally; unbiased
  • synonym: neutral - not supporting any side in a conflict or argument
