wildcat union

noun
independent, unofficial labor union
8.0 6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈwaɪld.kæt ˈjuː.njən/ /; / /ˈwaɪld.kæt ˈjuː.ni.ən/ (AmE), /ˈwaɪld.kæt ˈjuː.njən/ /; / /ˈwaɪld.kæt ˈjuː.ni.ən/ (BrE)
an independent labor union formed by workers without official recognition or affiliation with an established union

Examples

A wildcat union forms
After months of frustration, a small group created a wildcat union to demand improved safety measures.
Management refused to negotiate with the newly formed wildcat union.
The workers established a wildcat union when their concerns were not addressed by the official organization.
