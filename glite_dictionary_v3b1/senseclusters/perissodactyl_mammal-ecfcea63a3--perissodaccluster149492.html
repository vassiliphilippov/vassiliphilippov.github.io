perissodactyl mammal

noun
hoofed mammal with odd number of toes
9.9 8.4
Pronunciation: /pə.rɪs.ə.ˈdæk.təl ˈmæm.əl/ (AmE), /pə.rɪs.ə.ˈdæk.təl ˈmæm.əl/ (BrE)
A placental mammal with hooves and an odd number of toes on each foot, including horses, rhinoceroses, and tapirs.
