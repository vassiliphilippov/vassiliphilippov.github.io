renewal

noun
conversion of wasteland into usable land
1.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˈnuːəl / (AmE), / rɪˈnjuːəl / (BrE)
the process of converting wasteland into land suitable for habitation or cultivation

Examples

Urban renewal project
Environmentalists supported the renewal of industrial wastelands to create green spaces for the community.
Many cities around the world are embarking on land renewal projects to revitalize dilapidated neighborhoods.
The government invested heavily in urban renewal to transform abandoned lots into parks and housing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reclamation - the process of making land suitable for cultivation
