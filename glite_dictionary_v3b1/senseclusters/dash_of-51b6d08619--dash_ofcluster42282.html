dash of

noun
a small amount (esp. in food/drink)
Pronunciation: / dæʃ ʌv / (AmE), / dæʃ əv / (BrE)
a small amount of something, especially when added to food or drink to enhance flavor

Examples

A dash of salt
Add a dash of salt to the soup before serving.
She wore a red scarf for a dash of color.
The cocktail needs just a dash of bitters to finish it off.
