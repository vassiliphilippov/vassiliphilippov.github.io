art

noun
visual images in a publication
Pronunciation: / ɑrt / (AmE), / ɑːt / (BrE)
photographs, drawings, or other visual elements included in a publication such as a book, magazine, or newspaper.

Examples

Art for a magazine
She worked on designing the art for the new cookbook.
The art in this magazine was created by several talented photographers.
The publisher requested higher-resolution art for the cover.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illustration - a picture or diagram that helps make something clear or attractive, especially in a book or magazine
