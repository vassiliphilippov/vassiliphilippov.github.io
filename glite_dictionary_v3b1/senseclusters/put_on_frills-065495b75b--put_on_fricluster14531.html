put on frills

verb
add unnecessary embellishments
7.6
Pronunciation: / pʊt ɑn frɪlz / (AmE), / pʊt ɒn frɪlz / (BrE)
to add unnecessary decorations or embellishments, often to make something appear more elaborate or impressive than needed
