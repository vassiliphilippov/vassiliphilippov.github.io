oddness

noun
strangeness or eccentricity
Pronunciation: / ˈɑdnəs / (AmE), / ˈɒdnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being strange or unconventional, especially in a way that is difficult to explain

Examples

His oddness stood out
As a child, he was teased for his oddness but grew up to be admired for his unique perspective.
Her oddness made her stand out in a crowd of conformists.
Many appreciated the oddness of his artistic vision, finding it refreshingly original.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eccentricity - unusual or unconventional behavior
  • antonym: normality - the state of being normal; absence of strangeness
