bioactivity

noun
effect on organisms
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊ.ækˈtɪv.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.əʊ.ækˈtɪv.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the effect that a substance has on living organisms or cells

Examples

Bioactivity of plants
Scientists are studying the bioactivity of plant extracts to find new medicines.
The bioactivity of the compound was tested on several types of bacteria.
Understanding the bioactivity of chemicals helps researchers develop safer products.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biological activity - the effect a substance has on living tissue
  • antonym: toxicity - the degree to which a substance can harm living organisms
