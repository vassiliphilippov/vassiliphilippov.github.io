carbon dichloride

noun
chemical; antiparasitic agent
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːr.bən daɪ.ˈklɔː.raɪd/ (AmE), /ˈkɑː.bən daɪ.ˈklɔː.raɪd/ (BrE)
A chemical compound (CCl₂), used as an anthelmintic agent to treat infections caused by hookworms and other nematodes.
