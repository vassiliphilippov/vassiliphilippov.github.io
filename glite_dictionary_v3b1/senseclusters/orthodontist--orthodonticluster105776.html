orthodontist

noun
specialist dentist for straightening teeth
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːrθəˈdɑːntɪst / (AmE), / ˌɔːθəˈdɒntɪst / (BrE)
a dentist who specializes in the diagnosis, prevention, and correction of irregularities of the teeth and jaws

Examples

The orthodontist visit
After visiting the orthodontist, I learned I needed a retainer.
My orthodontist recommended braces to straighten my teeth.
She has regular checkups with her orthodontist every six months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dentist - a person qualified to treat the diseases and conditions that affect the teeth and gums
