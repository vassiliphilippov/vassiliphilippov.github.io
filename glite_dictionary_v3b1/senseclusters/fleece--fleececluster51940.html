fleece

noun
soft, bulky fabric for clothing
4.0
Pronunciation: / fliːs / (AmE), / fliːs / (BrE)
a soft, warm fabric with a deep pile, used mainly for clothing such as jackets and blankets

Examples

Fleece jacket
Many hikers prefer fleece clothing because it is lightweight and warm.
She wore a fleece jacket on the chilly morning.
This fleece blanket is incredibly soft and cozy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wool - fibers from sheep or other animals, often used in textiles
