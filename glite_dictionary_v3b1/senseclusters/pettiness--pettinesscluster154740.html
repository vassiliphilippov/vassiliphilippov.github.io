pettiness

noun
concern with trivial matters; narrowness of mind
4.9 5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛt̬i.nəs /; / ˈpɛt̬.i.nəs / (AmE), / ˈpet.i.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being overly concerned with small or unimportant matters, often showing a lack of generosity or narrow-mindedness
