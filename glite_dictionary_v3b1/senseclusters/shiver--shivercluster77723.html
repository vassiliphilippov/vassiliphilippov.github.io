Synonyms

  • tremble
    2.7 2.65/m
    tremble is a sudden shaking caused by cold, fear, or excitement, while shiver is a quick tingling body movement.
  • shudder
    4.1 2.41/m
    shudder is a sudden strong shake from fear, cold, or disgust, while shiver is usually a smaller tingling tremor.
  • quiver
    3.9 0.70/m
    quiver is a slight, often emotional trembling seen in voices or hands, while shiver is a physical tingling from cold or fear.
  • tremor
    5.3 2.42/m
    tremor is an involuntary shaking often linked to illness or nerves, while shiver is a short cold or fear-induced tremble.
