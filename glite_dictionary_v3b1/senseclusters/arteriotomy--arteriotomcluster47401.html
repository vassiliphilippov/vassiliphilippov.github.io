arteriotomy

noun
surgical artery incision
9.8
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˌtɪriˈɑːtəmi / (AmE), / ɑːˌtɪəriˈɒtəmi / (BrE)
a surgical incision made into an artery

Examples

An arteriotomy incision
The surgeon performed an arteriotomy to access the blocked vessel.
An arteriotomy is often necessary during certain vascular surgeries.
Careful closure of the arteriotomy site is important to prevent bleeding.
