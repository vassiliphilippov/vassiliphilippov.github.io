region

noun
an approximate amount or number
8.8 1.6
Pronunciation: /ˈriː.dʒən/ (AmE), /ˈriː.dʒən/ (BrE)
an approximate amount or number; used especially in the phrase 'in the region of' to mean 'about' or 'approximately'

Examples

Region of $1000
He earns something in the region of $60,000 a year.
Repairs could take in the region of six weeks to complete.
The total cost will be in the region of fifteen thousand dollars.
