blink

noun
rapid closing and opening of the eyelids
1.0
Pronunciation: / blɪŋk / (AmE), / blɪŋk / (BrE)
a quick closing and opening of the eyelids, usually as a reflex

Examples

A quick blink
A sudden blink can protect your eyes from small debris or dust.
After staring at the computer for hours, he felt his blink rate slow down.
She gave a startled blink when the bright light flashed in her eyes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wink - a closing and opening of one eye, often as a signal
