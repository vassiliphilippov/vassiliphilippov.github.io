Examples

Rumbling of thunder
He woke up to the rumbling of his empty stomach.
Suddenly, a deep rumbling came from beneath the ground, shaking the windows.
The rumbling of the distant train could be heard throughout the night.

Synonyms

  • grumble
    2.7 0.17/m
    grumble is a low, continuous, dull sound from distant thunder or movement, while rumbling is a louder, deeper noise that lasts longer.
  • roll
    2.7 5.50/m
    roll is a deep, rolling sound from thunder or drums that comes in waves, while rumbling is steadier and more dull.
  • hum
    1.0 6.25/m
    hum is a low, steady, often soft sound from machines or bees, while rumbling is louder, deeper, and more forceful.
