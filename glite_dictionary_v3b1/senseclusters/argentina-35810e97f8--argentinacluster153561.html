Argentina

proper noun
country in southern South America
Pronunciation: / ˌɑr.dʒənˈtiː.nə / (AmE), / ˌɑː.dʒənˈtiː.nə / (BrE)
a republic in southern South America, the second largest country on the continent, known for its varied landscape and cultural heritage.

Examples

Argentina beef exports
Beef is a significant export for Argentina, and its cuisine features world-famous grilled meats.
Maradona is considered a national hero in Argentina for his achievements in football.
The tango originated in Argentina, where it remains an important part of the country's culture.
