hackery

noun
clever problem-solving
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhæk.ə.rɪ/ (AmE), /ˈhæk.ə.rɪ/ (BrE)
the use of clever, quick, or unorthodox methods to solve problems, especially in computing or programming

Examples

Programming hackery
The software was built with a lot of hackery to make it work on time.
Some programmers admire hackery for its creativity, while others prefer more standard solutions.
The team's hackery allowed them to fix the bug just before the presentation.

  • synonym: innovation - the introduction of new ideas or methods
