legal assistant

noun
AI/software assistant for legal tasks
3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈliː.gəl ə.ˈsɪs.tənt/ /; / /ˈlɪ.gəl ə.ˈsɪs.tənt/ (AmE), /ˈliː.gəl ə.ˈsɪs.tənt/ /; / /ˈlɪ.gəl ə.ˈsɪs.tənt/ (BrE)
(in computing and legal technology) a software or AI system that assists lawyers by performing tasks such as drafting documents, legal research, review, or other routine legal work.

Examples

AI legal assistant software
The firm deployed an AI-powered legal assistant to draft contracts overnight and support research.
During discovery, the team used a cloud-based legal assistant to review thousands of documents quickly.
After the pilot, partners agreed the legal assistant reduced simple tasks, freeing staff for client meetings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: AI assistant - a software program that uses artificial intelligence to help users perform tasks.
  • synonym: paralegal - a human professional trained to assist lawyers with legal work; often performs tasks similar to those automated by legal-assistant software.
