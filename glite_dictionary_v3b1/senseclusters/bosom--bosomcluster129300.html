bosom

noun
part of clothing covering the chest
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊzəm / (AmE), / ˈbʊz.əm /; / ˈbʊzəm / (BrE)
the chest or breast, especially a woman's breast, or the part of clothing that covers this area

Examples

A bosom of her dress
He tucked the letter into the bosom of his shirt for safekeeping.
She pinned a delicate brooch to her bosom before leaving the house.
The lace on the bosom of her dress was beautifully embroidered.
A sharp pain in her bosom made her pause and sit down immediately.
She held the infant close to her bosom, soothing him gently.
The actress wore a gown that accentuated her bosom at the gala.
He held secrets close to his bosom, trusting no one.
She pressed the letter to her bosom and smiled softly.
The baby slept peacefully against his mother's bosom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chest - the front surface of a person's or animal's body between the neck and the abdomen
