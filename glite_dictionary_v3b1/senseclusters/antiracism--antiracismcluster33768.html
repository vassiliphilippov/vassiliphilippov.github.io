antiracism

noun
opposition to racism
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈreɪsɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈreɪsɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief in or practice of actively opposing racism and promoting racial equality

Examples

An antiracism campaign
The school launched an antiracism campaign to educate students about diversity and inclusion.
Many companies are adopting antiracism policies to create a fairer workplace.
She attended workshops on antiracism to better understand how to support equality.

Related Terms

  • antonym: racism - prejudice or discrimination based on race
  • synonym: tolerance - willingness to accept people of different backgrounds
