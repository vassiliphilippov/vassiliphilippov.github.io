king cobra

noun
large venomous Asian snake
Pronunciation: / kɪŋ ˈkoʊbrə / (AmE), / kɪŋ ˈkəʊbrə / (BrE)
a very large venomous snake (Ophiophagus hannah) of southeastern Asia, the largest of all cobras, known for its potent venom and distinctive hood.

Examples

The king cobra snake
A single bite from a king cobra contains enough neurotoxin to kill an adult elephant.
Herpetologists traveled to India to observe the king cobra in its natural habitat.
The king cobra can reach a length of over five meters, making it the largest venomous snake in the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cobra - a highly venomous snake of the family Elapidae, native to Africa and Asia, that spreads the skin of its neck into a hood when threatened
