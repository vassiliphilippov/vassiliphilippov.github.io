screamingly

adverb
to an extreme degree; very
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈskriːmɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈskriːmɪŋli / (BrE)
to an extreme or intense degree, especially used to describe something extremely funny or noticeable

Examples

A screamingly funny joke
She found his attempt at dancing screamingly awkward yet oddly charming.
That was a screamingly obvious mistake that no one should have missed.
The movie was screamingly funny, making everyone in the audience burst out laughing.
