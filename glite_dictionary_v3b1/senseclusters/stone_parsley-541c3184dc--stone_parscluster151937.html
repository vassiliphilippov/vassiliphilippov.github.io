stone parsley

noun
roadside herb with parsley-like leaves and aromatic seeds
Pronunciation: / stoʊn ˈpɑːrsli / (AmE), / stəʊn ˈpɑːsli / (BrE)
a plant with parsley-like leaves and white or pink flowers, usually referring to species in the genus Seseli
