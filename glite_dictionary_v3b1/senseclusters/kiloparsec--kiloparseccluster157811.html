kiloparsec

noun
astronomical distance unit
Pronunciation: / ˌkɪloʊˈpɑːrsɛk / (AmE), / ˌkɪləʊˈpɑːsɛk / (BrE)
a unit of distance in astronomy equal to one thousand parsecs

Examples

Five kiloparsecs
The star cluster is located nearly five kiloparsecs from Earth.
Astronomers measured the distance between the two galaxies as about ten kiloparsecs.
Distances within the Milky Way are often expressed in kiloparsecs for convenience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: light-year - the distance light travels in one year
  • degree higher: megaparsec - an astronomical unit of distance equal to one million parsecs
  • degree lower: parsec - an astronomical unit of distance equal to about 3.26 light-years
