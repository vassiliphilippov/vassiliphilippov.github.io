misdefine

verb
to define incorrectly
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsdɪˈfaɪn / (AmE), / ˌmɪsdɪˈfaɪn / (BrE)
to give an incorrect or inaccurate definition of something

Examples

To misdefine a word
If you misdefine a concept in your essay, your argument may become unclear.
Teachers should avoid misdefining important terms when explaining new topics.
He realized he had misdefined the word during the class discussion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: define - to state the meaning of a word or phrase
  • synonym: misinterpret - to understand or explain something incorrectly
