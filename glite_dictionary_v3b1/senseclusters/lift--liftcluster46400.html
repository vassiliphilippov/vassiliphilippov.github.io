lift

noun
device added in shoe to increase height
5.6
Pronunciation: / lɪft / (AmE), / lɪft / (BrE)
a device or material inserted into a shoe or boot to make the wearer appear taller or to compensate for a shortened leg

Examples

A shoe lift
After his surgery, he wore a lift in his left shoe to adjust his gait.
Some people use a lift to appear slightly taller in formal shoes.
The podiatrist recommended a custom-made lift for his orthopedic needs.
Heavier lifts in the boots can add extra height for the wearer.
If the lift comes loose, the heel may feel unstable when walking.
The cobbler replaced the worn lift on my shoe to make it comfortable again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heel - the back part of a shoe supporting the heel of the foot
