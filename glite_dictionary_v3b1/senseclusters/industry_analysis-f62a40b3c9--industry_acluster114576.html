industry analysis

noun
study of an industry's structure and trends
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndəstri əˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / ˈɪndəstri əˈnæləsɪs / (BrE)
A detailed examination of the factors, trends, and competitive dynamics within a particular industry to assess its attractiveness or potential for a business.

Examples

Detailed industry analysis
A solid industry analysis helps investors identify potential risks and opportunities.
Her business plan impressed the panel with its in-depth industry analysis of the renewable energy sector.
The company's success was largely due to a thorough industry analysis conducted before entering the market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: competitor analysis - assessment of strengths and weaknesses of current and potential competitors
  • synonym: market analysis - an examination of a specific market's conditions and potential
