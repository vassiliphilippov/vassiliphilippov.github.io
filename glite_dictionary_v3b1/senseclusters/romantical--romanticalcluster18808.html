romantical

adjective
idealistic or fanciful
Pronunciation: / roʊˈmæntɪkəl / (AmE), / rəʊˈmæntɪkəl / (BrE)
characterized by idealism, strong emotion, or a fanciful and picturesque view rather than practical reality.

Examples

Romantical dreamer
He had a romantical vision of life in the countryside, full of beauty and adventure.
Her romantical notions often clashed with the practical concerns of daily living.
The novel is written in a romantical style, emphasizing emotion over realism.

Related Terms

  • antonym: realistic - accepting things as they are; practical
  • synonym: romantic - showing strong feelings of love or idealism
