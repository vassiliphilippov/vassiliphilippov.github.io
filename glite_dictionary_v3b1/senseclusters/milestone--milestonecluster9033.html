milestone

noun
stone distance marker by a road
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪlstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈmaɪlstəʊn / (BrE)
a stone marker placed alongside a road indicating the distance to a particular place

Examples

A road milestone
A weathered milestone stood hidden among the overgrown grass beside the highway.
Many milestones from the Roman period can still be found along ancient roads.
The old milestone at the crossroads marks five miles to the next town.
