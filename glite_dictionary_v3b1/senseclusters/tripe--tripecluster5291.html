tripe

noun
edible animal stomach lining
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪp / (AmE), / ˈtraɪp / (BrE)
the edible lining of the stomach of a ruminant animal, such as a cow, often used in cooking

Examples

A tripe stew
He bought fresh tripe from the butcher for his grandmother's recipe.
Some people enjoy eating tripe in spicy soups or broths.
The chef prepared a traditional stew using tripe as the main ingredient.
