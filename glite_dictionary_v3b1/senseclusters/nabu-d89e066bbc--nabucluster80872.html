Nabu

proper noun
Babylonian god of wisdom and writing
9.9 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / Ààn…ëÀêbuÀê / (AmE), / Ààn…ëÀêbuÀê / (BrE)
In Babylonian mythology, the god of wisdom, writing, and agriculture, and patron of scribes and scholars.

Examples

Nabu god of wisdom
As the god of wisdom, Nabu was invoked by scholars seeking guidance.
Many ancient texts were inscribed in honor of Nabu, the patron of scribes.
The temple dedicated to Nabu was a center of learning in ancient Babylon.

Synonyms
