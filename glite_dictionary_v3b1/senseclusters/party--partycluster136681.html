party

noun
group assembled for an activity
1.4 28/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ërti / (AmE), / Ààp…ëÀêti / (BrE)
a group of people who are temporarily formed for a particular activity or purpose

Examples

A party of tourists
A party of scientists arrived to survey the area.
The hikers joined a party headed up the mountain.
The rescue party set out just before dawn.

Synonyms

  • company
    1.7 0.85/m
    company is a temporary group formed for a shared activity or visit, while party is a group formed for a specific purpose.
  • gathering
    1.0 14/m
    gathering is a meeting for a social occasion or purpose, while party is a more lively, often celebratory group gathered temporarily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expedition - a group organized for a particular purpose, especially exploration
  • synonym: group - a number of people or things that are located or gathered together
