eulogistic

adjective
expressing praise formally
7.1 8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːləˈdʒɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌjuːləˈdʒɪstɪk / (BrE)
expressing or containing praise, especially in a formal or elaborate way

Examples

Eulogistic speech
Her speech was eulogistic, filled with heartfelt compliments for the late professor.
Reviews for the play were overwhelmingly eulogistic, commending both the cast and the direction.
The editor wrote a eulogistic article in praise of the retiring founder.
