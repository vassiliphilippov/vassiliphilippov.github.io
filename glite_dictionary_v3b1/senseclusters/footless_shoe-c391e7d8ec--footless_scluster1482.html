footless shoe

noun
shoe exposing parts of the foot
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊtləs ʃuː / (AmE), / ˈfʊtləs ʃuː / (BrE)
A shoe that does not cover the foot completely, typically leaving parts of the foot exposed.

Examples

A footless shoe
After trying footless shoes, he found them perfect for casual walks on the beach.
She wore a pair of footless shoes to the summer party, enjoying the airy feel.
The new fashion line includes several styles of footless shoes for warmer weather.
