wasteyard

noun
place where waste materials are dumped
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪstˌjɑrd / (AmE), / ˈweɪstˌjɑːd / (BrE)
a piece of land used for dumping waste materials

Examples

An abandoned wasteyard
Plans were announced to clean up the abandoned wasteyard and turn it into a park.
The old factory site is now used as a wasteyard for local construction debris.
Toxic chemicals were found leaking from the wasteyard into the nearby river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dump - a place where waste or rubbish is taken and left
  • synonym: landfill - a site for the disposal of waste materials by burial
