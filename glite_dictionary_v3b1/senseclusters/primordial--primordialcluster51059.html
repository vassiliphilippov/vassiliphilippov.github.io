primordial

adjective
existing from the beginning; original
Pronunciation: / praɪˈmɔrdiəl / (AmE), / praɪˈmɔːdiəl / (BrE)
existing from the very beginning; in an earliest or original stage or state.

Examples

A primordial state
She felt a primordial fear as the storm raged outside, connecting her to ancient ancestors.
Some scientists study the primordial soup from which life first emerged on Earth.
The primordial universe was a hot, dense place before stars or planets formed.
