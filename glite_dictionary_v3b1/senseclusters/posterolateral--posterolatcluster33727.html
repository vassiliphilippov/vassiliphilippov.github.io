posterolateral

adjective
at back and side
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstɚoʊˈlætərəl / (AmE), / ˌpɒstərəʊˈlætərəl / (BrE)
located at or relating to the back and side of a body part

Examples

The posterolateral side
The doctor noted swelling on the posterolateral side of the patient's ankle.
An MRI revealed a tear in the posterolateral ligament of the knee.
Physical therapy focused on strengthening the posterolateral muscles of the hip.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anterolateral - located at the front and side of a body part
  • synonym: posterior - located at the back of a body part
