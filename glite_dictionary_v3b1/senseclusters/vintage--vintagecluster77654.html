vintage

adjective
classic and old
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪntɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈvɪntɪdʒ / (BrE)
describing something old, classic, and valued because it comes from a previous time

Examples

Vintage clothing style
She wore a beautiful vintage dress to the party.
They decorated their living room with vintage furniture from the 1960s.
He collects vintage cars and enjoys restoring them to their original condition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antique - an old and valuable object, often collectible
  • antonym: modern - relating to the present or recent times
