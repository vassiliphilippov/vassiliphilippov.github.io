form

noun
document with spaces for information
Pronunciation: / fɔrm / (AmE), / fɔːm / (BrE)
a printed or digital document with designated spaces for information to be filled in

Examples

Fill in a form
Each applicant must fill in a separate form for registration.
He downloaded the medical form from the hospital's website.
Please complete the form and return it to the office by Friday.
