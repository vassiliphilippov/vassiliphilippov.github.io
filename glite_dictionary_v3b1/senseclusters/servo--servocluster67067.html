servo

adjective
relating to servomechanisms
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜrˌvoʊ / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvəʊ / (BrE)
Relating to or involving a servomechanism or servomechanisms; used to describe devices or systems that include automatic control mechanisms.

Examples

Servo system
A servo mechanism automatically corrected the steering trajectory.
Modern robotics often use servo motors for accurate movement.
The engineer installed a servo control system to enhance the machine's precision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: servomechanism - an automatic device that uses error-sensing feedback to correct the performance of a mechanism
