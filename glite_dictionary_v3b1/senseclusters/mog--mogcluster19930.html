mog

noun
informal cat
8.9
Pronunciation: / mɒɡ / (BrE)
an informal term for a cat, especially a domestic one, used mainly in British English

Examples

A sleepy mog
The old mog curled up by the fireplace every evening.
She adopted a stray mog from the animal shelter last week.
You can often find a mog sunbathing on the garden wall in the afternoon.

  • antonym: dog - a domesticated canine animal
