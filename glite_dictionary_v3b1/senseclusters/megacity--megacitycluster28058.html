megacity

noun
very large influential city
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛɡəˌsɪti / (AmE), / ˈmɛɡəsɪti /; / ˈmɛɡəˌsɪti / (BrE)
a very large city, usually with a population of over ten million people, often with significant economic, cultural, or political influence
