hematopoiesis

noun
formation of blood cells in the body
Pronunciation: / ˌhiː.mə.toʊ.pɔɪˈiː.sɪs /; / hiˌmætəpoʊˈiːsɪs /; / hiːmoʊpɔɪˈiːsɪs /; / ˌhiːmoʊpɔɪˈiːsɪs / (AmE), / ˌhiː.mə.təʊ.pɔɪˈiː.sɪs /; / hiːˌmatəʊpɔɪˈiːsɪs /; / hiːməʊpɔɪˈiːsɪs /; / ˌhiːməʊpɔɪˈiːsɪs / (BrE)
the process of formation and development of blood cells in the body, especially in the bone marrow
