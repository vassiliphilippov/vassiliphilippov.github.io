Synonyms

  • hematogenesis
    9.9 0.00/m
    hematogenesis denotes the formation of blood cells, while hematopoiesis stresses both formation and later development or maturation in the marrow.
  • hemogenesis
    9.9 0.00/m
    hemogenesis means forming blood cells inside the body, while hematopoiesis also includes their later development and stages in the marrow.
  • sanguification
    9.9 0.00/m
    sanguification names blood cell formation using a Latin-root word, while hematopoiesis is the modern medical term for formation and development.
