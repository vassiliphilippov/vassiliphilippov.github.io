hematopoiesis

noun
formation of blood cells in the body
9.9 8.7
Pronunciation: /hiː.mə.tə.pɔɪ.ˈiː.sɪs/ /; / /hɪ.mə.tə.pɔɪ.ˈiː.sɪs/ (AmE), /hiː.mə.tə.pɔɪ.ˈiː.sɪs/ /; / /hɪ.mə.tə.pɔɪ.ˈiː.sɪs/ (BrE)
the process of formation and development of blood cells in the body, especially in the bone marrow
