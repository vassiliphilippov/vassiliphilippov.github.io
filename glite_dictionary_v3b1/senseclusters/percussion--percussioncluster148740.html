percussion

noun
playing percussion instruments
7.5 3.8
Pronunciation: / pərˈkʌʃən / (AmE), / pəˈkʌʃən /; / pəˈkʌʃ.ən / (BrE)
musical instruments that make sound when struck, shaken, or scraped, or the act or group of playing them in music
