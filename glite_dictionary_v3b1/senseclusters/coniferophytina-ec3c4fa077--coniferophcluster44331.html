Coniferophytina

noun
cone-bearing gymnosperm plant group
Pronunciation: / kəˌnɪfərəˈfaɪtɪnə / (AmE), / kəˌnɪfərəˈfɪtɪnə /; / kəˌnɪfərəˈfʌɪtɪnə / (BrE)
a botanical subdivision of cone-bearing gymnosperms (conifers and their relatives), including both living and extinct groups, dating back to the Carboniferous period
