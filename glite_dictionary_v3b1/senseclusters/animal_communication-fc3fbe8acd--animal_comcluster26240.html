animal communication

noun
communication among animals of the same species
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈænɪməl kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈænɪməl kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the transmission of information or signals between animals of the same species, typically through sounds, gestures, scents, or other means

Examples

Animal communication signals
Birdsong is a common form of animal communication used to attract mates and mark territory.
Researchers observed complex animal communication patterns among honeybees during their waggle dance.
Scientists study animal communication to understand how dolphins convey information to one another.
