speech

noun
formal reprimand; lengthy rebuke
Pronunciation: / spiːtʃ / (AmE), / spiːtʃ / (BrE)
a lengthy or formal reprimand or lecturing, typically delivered as a rebuke

Examples

A long speech
After coming home late, his mother gave him a long speech about responsibility.
He dreaded hearing another speech from his boss about punctuality.
The coach delivered a harsh speech to the team following their poor performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lecture - a long talk of admonition or warning; reprimand
  • synonym: talking-to - an act of admonishing or rebuking someone
