Gita

noun
sacred Hindu scripture; part of the Mahabharata
9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈgiː.tə/ /; / /ˈdʒiː.tə/ (AmE), /ˈgiː.tə/ /; / /ˈdʒiː.tə/ (BrE)
