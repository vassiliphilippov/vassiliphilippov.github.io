moonbeam

noun
a ray of moonlight
Pronunciation: / ˈmunˌbiːm / (AmE), / ˈmuːnˌbiːm / (BrE)
A ray of light coming from the moon.

Examples

A moonbeam shines
A single moonbeam shone through the window and lit up the room.
He watched the moonbeams dance on the surface of the lake.
She reached out as if to catch a moonbeam in her hands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sunbeam - a ray of sunlight
