Synonyms

  • sacred

    sacred is treated with deep reverence in religion and ritual, while hallowed is often used for places or traditions honoured over time.
  • holy
    2.7 51/m

  • consecrated
    7.6 0.00/m
    consecrated is formally made sacred by a religious rite, while hallowed usually means widely honoured or respected over time.
  • sanctified
    7.4 0.00/m

  • blessed
    4.8 0.47/m
    blessed is given approval or a prayer and can mean fortunate, while hallowed means officially or widely regarded as holy and respected.
