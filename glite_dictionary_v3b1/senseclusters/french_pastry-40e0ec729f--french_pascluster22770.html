French pastry

noun
sweet pastry typical of French baking
Pronunciation: / frɛntʃ ˈpeɪstri /; / ˌfrɛntʃ ˈpeɪstri / (AmE), / frɛntʃ ˈpeɪstri /; / ˌfrɛntʃ ˈpeɪstri / (BrE)
a sweet, often filled or elaborately prepared baked good or dessert from France, known for its delicate texture and rich flavor
