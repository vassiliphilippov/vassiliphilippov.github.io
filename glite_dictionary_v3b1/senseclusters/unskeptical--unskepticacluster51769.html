unskeptical

adjective
easily believing
7.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈskɛptɪkəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈskɛptɪkəl / (BrE)
willing to believe things easily and not inclined to doubt or question

Examples

An unskeptical child
The unskeptical child believed every story her older brother told her.
He remained unskeptical despite hearing several conflicting reports about the event.
An unskeptical attitude can sometimes lead people to accept false information.

Related Terms

  • synonym: credulous - too ready to believe things
  • synonym: gullible - easily fooled or tricked
  • antonym: skeptical - doubting or questioning the truth of something
