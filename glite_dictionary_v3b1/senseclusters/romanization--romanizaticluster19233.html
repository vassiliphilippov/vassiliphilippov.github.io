romanization

noun
writing in Latin alphabet
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌroʊmənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrəʊmənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of writing a language or converting text using the Roman (Latin) alphabet instead of its original script

Examples

The guide included both the original script and its romanization for each phrase.
