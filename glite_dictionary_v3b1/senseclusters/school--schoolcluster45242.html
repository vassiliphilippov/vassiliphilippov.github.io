school

noun
group of fish swimming together
9.5
Pronunciation: / skuːl / (AmE), / skuːl / (BrE)
a large group of fish or aquatic animals swimming together

Examples

A school of fish
A school of dolphins appeared near the boat, moving gracefully through the waves.
The divers were mesmerized by a school of colorful fish darting through the coral reef.
We spotted a huge school of sardines during our snorkeling trip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shoal - a large group of fish; often used interchangeably with 'school'
