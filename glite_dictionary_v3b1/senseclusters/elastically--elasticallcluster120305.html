elastically

adverb
in a flexible way
4.8
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlæstɪkli / (AmE), / ɪˈlæstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that allows for quick and flexible expansion or contraction to adapt to changing conditions or demands

Examples

Adapts elastically
The company can adjust its workforce elastically to meet seasonal demand.
Modern cloud platforms allocate resources elastically as user needs change.
The system responded elastically to the unexpected increase in traffic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flexibly - in a way that can easily change or adapt
  • antonym: rigidly - in a way that does not change or adapt
