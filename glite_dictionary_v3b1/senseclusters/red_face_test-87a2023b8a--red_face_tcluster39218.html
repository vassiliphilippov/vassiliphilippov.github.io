red face test

noun
test based on showing embarrassment or guilt
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛd ˌfeɪs tɛst / (AmE), / ˈrɛd ˌfeɪs tɛst / (BrE)
an informal standard or observation based on whether someone appears embarrassed or guilty, typically indicated by blushing

Examples

The red face test shows
Her awkward silence during the interview was a classic red face test for discomfort.
If your explanation doesn't pass the red face test, people might not believe you're telling the truth.
The policy failed the red face test when questioned by the press during the conference.
