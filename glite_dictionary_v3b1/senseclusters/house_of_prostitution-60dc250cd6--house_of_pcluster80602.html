house of prostitution

noun
brothel; building for prostitution
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊs əv ˌprɑːstəˈtuːʃən / (AmE), / ˈhaʊs əv ˌprɒstɪˈtjuːʃən / (BrE)
A building where prostitutes are available to provide sexual services to clients.

Examples

A house of prostitution building
He was arrested for operating a house of prostitution illegally within the city limits.
Strict regulations were enforced to monitor every house of prostitution in the district.
The police raided the house of prostitution in response to numerous complaints from neighbors.
