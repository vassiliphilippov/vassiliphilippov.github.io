do a job on

verb
damage or spoil badly
5.0
Pronunciation: / du ə dʒɑb ɑn / (AmE), / duː ə dʒɒb ɒn / (BrE)

Examples

Vandals did a job on the abandoned house, smashing windows and spray-painting walls.
Whoever parked here did a job on your car's bumper.
