rudderless ship

noun
person or group lacking direction
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌdɚləs ʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈrʌdələs ʃɪp / (BrE)
A person or group lacking direction, leadership, or purpose.

Examples

A rudderless ship in life
After losing her job, she wandered through life like a rudderless ship.
The company became a rudderless ship after the CEO resigned suddenly.
Without a clear vision, the team felt like a rudderless ship drifting aimlessly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leaderless - without a leader
  • synonym: rudderless - lacking a rudder; metaphorically, lacking direction or leadership
