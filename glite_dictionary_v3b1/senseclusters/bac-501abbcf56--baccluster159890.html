BAC

noun
blood alcohol level
4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbiː.eɪ.siː/ (AmE), /ˈbiː.eɪ.siː/ (BrE)
the amount of alcohol present in a person's blood, usually expressed as a percentage to measure intoxication

Examples

BAC level
The driver was arrested because his BAC was over the legal limit.
You should never drive if your BAC is too high.
The test showed her BAC was 0.08 percent after the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blood alcohol level - the amount of alcohol in the bloodstream
  • antonym: sobriety - the state of not being affected by alcohol
