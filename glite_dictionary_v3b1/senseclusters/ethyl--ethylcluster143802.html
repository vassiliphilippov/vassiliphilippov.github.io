ethyl

noun
chemical radical C2H5
Pronunciation: / ˈiːθɪl / (AmE), / ˈiːθɪl / (BrE)
a chemical group or radical with the formula C2H5, derived from ethane by removing one hydrogen atom

Examples

An ethyl group
Adding an ethyl substituent can significantly change a compound's physical properties.
