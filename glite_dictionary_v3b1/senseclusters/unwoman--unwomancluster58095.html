unwoman

noun
ostracized woman
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʌnˌwʌmən / (AmE), / ˈʌnˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who has been deprived of her status, rights, or recognition as a woman, often through expulsion or ostracism in a dystopian context

Examples

An unwoman in exile
In the novel, anyone who defied the rules risked being declared an unwoman and sent to the colonies.
She feared becoming an unwoman if she continued to speak out against the authorities.
The regime used the threat of being labeled an unwoman to control the female population.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outcast - a person who is rejected or excluded from society
  • synonym: pariah - a person who is despised or avoided by others
