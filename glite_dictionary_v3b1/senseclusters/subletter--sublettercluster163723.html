subletter

noun
person who subleases
5.6
Pronunciation: /sə.ˈblet.ər/ /; / /ˈsʌb.lə.tər/ (AmE), /sə.ˈblet.ə/ /; / /ˈsʌb.lə.tə/ (BrE)
a person who rents out all or part of a property they are leasing to another person through a sublease

Examples

Subletter rents out room
The subletter agreed to rent the apartment for six months while the original tenant was abroad.
Before becoming a subletter, she checked the lease to make sure subleasing was allowed.
A subletter must often get permission from the landlord before renting out the space.

Related Terms

  • antonym: landlord - a person who owns and rents out property
  • antonym: sublessee - a person who rents property from a subletter
  • synonym: tenant - a person who rents property from a landlord
