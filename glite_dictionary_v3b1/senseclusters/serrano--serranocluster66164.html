serrano

noun
Spanish cured ham
Pronunciation: / səˈrɑːnoʊ / (AmE), / səˈrɑːnəʊ / (BrE)
a type of Spanish cured ham, usually thinly sliced and served as a cold meat

Examples

Sliced serrano ham
We ordered a plate of serrano to share as an appetizer before dinner.
The chef recommended pairing the serrano with fresh bread and tomatoes.
At the market, you can find high-quality serrano imported from Spain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prosciutto - an Italian dry-cured ham served thinly sliced
