strategian

noun
expert in strategy, especially military
Pronunciation: / strəˈtɪdʒiən / (AmE), / strəˈtɪdʒiən / (BrE)
a person who is skilled in devising plans or strategies, especially for warfare or competition

Examples

A strategian plans
As a renowned strategian, she devised tactics that outmaneuvered her rivals.
Many consider Napoleon to be one of history's greatest strategians.
The military relied on the advice of a seasoned strategian during the campaign.
