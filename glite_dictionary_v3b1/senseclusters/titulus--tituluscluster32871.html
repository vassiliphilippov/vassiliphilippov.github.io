titulus

noun
title or heading
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪtʃʊləs / (AmE), / ˈtɪtjʊləs / (BrE)
a title, heading, or short caption, especially in a written work or manuscript

Examples

Book titulus
The ancient manuscript began with a titulus written in ornate Latin script.
Each chapter in the book was introduced by a short titulus summarizing its content.
She carefully restored the faded titulus at the top of the medieval painting.
