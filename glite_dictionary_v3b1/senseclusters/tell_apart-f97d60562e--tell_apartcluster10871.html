tell apart

verb
distinguish between; differentiate
5.3 2.5
Pronunciation: /tel ə.ˈpɑːrt/ (AmE), /tel ə.ˈpɑːt/ (BrE)
to recognize or show the difference between two or more people or things; to distinguish one from another

Examples
