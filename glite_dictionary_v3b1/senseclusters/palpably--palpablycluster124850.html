palpably

adverb
clearly; noticeably
Pronunciation: / ˈpæl.pəb.li / (AmE), / ˈpal.pəb.li / (BrE)
in a way that is obvious or easily noticed; noticeably or clearly.

Examples

Is palpably clear
His relief was palpably visible after the doctor gave him good news.
She was palpably disappointed when she didn't hear her name announced.
The tension in the room was palpably high as everyone waited for the decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obviously - in a way that is easily perceived or understood
