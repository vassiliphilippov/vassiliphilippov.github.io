social organization

noun
structure of social relationships
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊʃəl ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃən /; / ˈsoʊʃəl ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsəʊʃl ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃn /; / ˈsəʊʃəl ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the structure and system of relationships and roles that organize people within a society or group
