hellbender

noun
large aquatic North American salamander
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlˌbɛndər / (AmE), / ˈhɛlˌbɛndə / (BrE)
a large aquatic salamander (Cryptobranchus alleganiensis) native to rivers and streams in eastern North America, noted for its flattened body and wrinkled skin.

Examples

A large hellbender
Biologists captured a hellbender in the river to study its habitat.
Efforts are underway to protect the endangered hellbender from pollution and habitat loss.
The hellbender is the largest salamander species found in North America.
