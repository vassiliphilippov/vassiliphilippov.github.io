estradiol

noun
potent female estrogen hormone
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛstrəˈdaɪˌɒl /; / ˌɛstrəˈdaɪɒl / (AmE), / ˌɛstrəˈdaɪ.ɒl /; / ˌiːstrəˈdaɪɒl / (BrE)
the principal and most potent naturally occurring estrogen hormone in women, used medically to treat hormone deficiencies and certain cancers
