accelerator

noun
pedal controlling vehicle speed
1.0 2.5
Pronunciation: /ək.sə.ˈleɪ.tər/ /; / /æk.sə.ˈleɪ.tər/ /; / /ɪk.sə.ˈleɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ək.sə.ˈleɪ.tə/ /; / /æk.sə.ˈleɪ.tə/ /; / /ɪk.sə.ˈleɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a pedal or device in a vehicle that controls the engine and increases speed

Examples

Car accelerator pedal
A sudden push on the accelerator made the car lurch forward.
