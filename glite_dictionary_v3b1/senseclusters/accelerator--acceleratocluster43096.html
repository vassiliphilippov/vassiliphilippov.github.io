Synonyms

  • accelerator pedal
    3.0 0.07/m
    accelerator pedal is the metal foot pedal drivers press to make a car go faster, while accelerator is the common name for that control.
  • gas pedal
    2.5 0.80/m
    gas pedal is the foot pedal people press to increase a car's speed, while accelerator is the technical or general term.
  • gas
    2.5 4.72/m
    gas is a casual name drivers use for the pedal that speeds a car up, while accelerator is the neutral label.
  • throttle
    6.1 4.49/m
