bro

noun
macho young man
8.6
Pronunciation: /broʊ/ (AmE), /brəʊ/ (BrE)
a young man who acts in a stereotypically macho, sporty, or fraternity-like way, often associated with 'bro' culture.

Examples

A party bro
He acted like a total bro at the party, talking loudly and high-fiving everyone.
Some people find the bro attitude annoying, while others think it's just harmless fun.
The group of bros gathered to watch the big football game together.
