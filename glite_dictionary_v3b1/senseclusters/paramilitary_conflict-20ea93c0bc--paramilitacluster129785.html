paramilitary conflict

noun
conflict between non-military armed groups
5.2 6.6
Pronunciation: /pær.ə.ˈmɪl.ɪ.tɜːr.iː ˈkɑːn.flɪkt/ (AmE), /pær.ə.ˈmɪl.ɪ.triː ˈkɒn.flɪkt/ (BrE)
A violent struggle involving armed groups that are not part of an official military force.

Examples

Paramilitary conflict violence
International organizations struggle to intervene in a paramilitary conflict due to the lack of clear authority.
The peace treaty ended decades of paramilitary conflict in the border areas.
Villagers were often caught in the crossfire during the paramilitary conflicts of the 1990s.

  • synonym: insurgency - an armed rebellion against authority
