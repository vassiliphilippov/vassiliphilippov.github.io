spitting cobra

noun
African cobra that spits venom
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪtɪŋ ˈkoʊbrə / (AmE), / ˈspɪtɪŋ ˈkəʊbrə / (BrE)
A venomous African cobra known for defending itself by spitting venom, which can cause blindness if it contacts the eyes.

Examples

A spitting cobra venom
A spitting cobra is more likely to spit than bite when provoked.
The spitting cobra can shoot venom at threats several feet away.
The ranger warned tourists to wear eye protection near spitting cobras.
