jiggle

noun
slight irregular shaking motion
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪɡəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪɡəl / (BrE)
a slight, irregular shaking or wobbling movement

Examples

A jiggle of jelly
She noticed a jiggle in the pudding as she set it on the table.
The jiggle of the camera made the image blurr slightly.
There was a slight jiggle in his walk after the long car ride.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shake - to move back and forth or up and down with quick movements
  • synonym: wobble - an unsteady or rocking motion
