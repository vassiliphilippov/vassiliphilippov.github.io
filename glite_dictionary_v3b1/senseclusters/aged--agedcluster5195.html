aged

adjective
kept to improve quality or preservation
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪdʒd /; / eɪdʒd / (AmE), / ˈeɪdʒd /; / eɪdʒd / (BrE)
kept or matured for a period of time to improve flavor or quality (used for food, drink, tobacco, etc.)
