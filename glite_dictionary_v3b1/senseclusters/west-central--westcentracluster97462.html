west-central

adjective
in the western central part of a region
1.6
Pronunciation: / wɛst ˈsɛntrəl / (AmE), / wɛst ˈsɛntrəl / (BrE)
Situated in or relating to the western central part of a region, especially of a country such as the United States.

Examples

West-central region
Several rare species are unique to the west-central forests of Oregon.
She grew up in a small town in west-central Texas.
The west-central region of the United States is known for its mountainous landscape.
