mist coat

noun
diluted paint primer
Pronunciation: / mɪst koʊt / (AmE), / mɪst kəʊt / (BrE)
a thin, diluted layer of paint applied to a surface, usually as a primer before the final coat

Examples

A mist coat on walls
Before painting the walls, apply a mist coat to help the final color stick better.
The decorator recommended a mist coat on the new plaster to prevent peeling.
A proper mist coat ensures that the top layers of paint adhere smoothly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: primer - a preparatory coating put on materials before painting
  • antonym: topcoat - the final layer of paint applied to a surface
