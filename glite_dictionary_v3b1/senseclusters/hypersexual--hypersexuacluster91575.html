hypersexual

adjective
excessive sexual desire
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˈsɛkʃuəl / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəˈsɛkʃuəl / (BrE)
having or showing an unusually high or excessive level of sexual desire or activity

Examples

Hypersexual behavior
The character in the novel was described as hypersexual, constantly seeking new romantic encounters.
Some medical conditions can cause a person to become hypersexual as a symptom.
The therapist noted that his hypersexual behavior was affecting his relationships.

Related Terms

  • synonym: libidinous - having or showing strong sexual desire
