United States Senate

noun
upper house of U.S. Congress
Pronunciation: / ˌjuː.nəˈtaɪtɪd steɪts ˈsen.ɪt / (AmE), / ˌjuː.nɪtɪd steɪts ˈsen.ɪt / (BrE)
