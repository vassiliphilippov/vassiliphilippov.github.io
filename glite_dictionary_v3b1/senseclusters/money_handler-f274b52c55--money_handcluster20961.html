money handler

noun
person dealing with money transactions
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌni ˌhændlər / (AmE), / ˈmʌni ˌhændlə / (BrE)
a person whose job involves receiving, investing, or paying out money

Examples

A money handler employee
As a money handler, she oversees all deposits and withdrawals for the company.
Every department has a designated money handler responsible for budgeting and payments.
The casino employs a trusted money handler to manage large sums during business hours.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cashier - person who handles cash transactions in a business
  • synonym: treasurer - person responsible for managing money in an organization
