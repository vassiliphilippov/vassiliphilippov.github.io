ultimate

noun
flying disc sport
Pronunciation: / ˈʌltəmət / (AmE), / ˈʌltɪmət / (BrE)
a team sport played with a flying disc, where teams score points by catching the disc in the opposing end zone

Examples

Ultimate frisbee game
He started playing ultimate in high school and quickly became passionate about the sport.
The university's ultimate team practices three times a week on the main field.
Many people enjoy the fast pace and teamwork involved in ultimate.
