lecanomancy

noun
divination with water
Pronunciation: / lɛˈkænəˌmænsi / (AmE), / lɛˈkænəmænsi / (BrE)
a form of divination that interprets patterns in water contained in a basin or bowl

Examples

Lecanomancy with water
In ancient cultures, priests performed lecanomancy to gain insight into future events.
The ritual of lecanomancy involved carefully observing the ripples in a bowl of water.
Some historians study lecanomancy as part of the history of mystical practices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: divination - the practice of seeking knowledge of the future by supernatural means
  • synonym: hydromancy - divination by water or liquids
