Synonyms

  • suffocate
    6.1 2.83/m
    suffocate means to be unable to breathe or die from lack of air, while stifle means to prevent normal breathing.
  • asphyxiate
    9.3 0.49/m
    asphyxiate is a formal medical word for stopping oxygen and causing suffocation, while stifle is more general for stopping breathing.
  • smother
    6.4 0.61/m
    smother means to cover someone's face or block air so they cannot breathe, while stifle means to stop normal breathing.
  • choke
    3.7 9.13/m
    choke means to have the airway blocked by food or an object, while stifle means to prevent someone from breathing generally.
  • strangle
    6.2 5.30/m
    strangle means to squeeze the throat to stop breathing and often kill, while stifle is a broader term for stopping normal breathing.
  • strangulate
    9.6 0.00/m
    strangulate means to compress the throat and cut off air in a violent act, while stifle simply means to prevent normal breathing.
