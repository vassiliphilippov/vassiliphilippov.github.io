carry on

verb
continue speaking after a pause
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkæri ɑn / (AmE), / ˈkæri ɒn / (BrE)
to continue doing something, especially after an interruption or despite difficulties

Examples

To carry on working

The festival has carried on for over a hundred years without interruption.
They wanted to carry on the family tradition of baking bread each Sunday.
