menses

noun
monthly menstrual discharge in women
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛnˌsiz / (AmE), / ˈmɛnsiːz / (BrE)
the monthly discharge of blood from the uterus in women of reproductive age, occurring from puberty to menopause

Examples

Monthly menses flow
Heavy or irregular menses can sometimes indicate underlying health issues.
She often feels fatigued during her menses and needs extra rest.
The doctor asked about the regularity of her menses during the check-up.

Related Terms

  • synonym: menstruation - the process of discharging blood and other material from the uterus monthly
