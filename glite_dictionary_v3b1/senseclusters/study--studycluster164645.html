study

verb
think deeply or meditate
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌdi / (AmE), / ˈstʌdi / (BrE)
to think deeply and at length, often for spiritual or philosophical purposes; to meditate

Examples

To study in silence
Each morning, she would study in silence, reflecting on her life and spiritual path.
He sat by the window to study the mysteries of existence in solitude.
In the old monastery, monks study for hours, seeking enlightenment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meditate - to focus one's mind for a period of time for spiritual purposes or relaxation
  • synonym: ponder - to think about something carefully, especially before making a decision
