flackery

noun
excessive promotion
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈflæk.ər.iː/ (AmE), /ˈflæk.ə.riː/ (BrE)
the work or behavior of a publicist, especially when involving excessive or aggressive promotion

Examples

Publicist flackery

Tired of all the flackery, she decided to focus on the facts instead of the promotion.
