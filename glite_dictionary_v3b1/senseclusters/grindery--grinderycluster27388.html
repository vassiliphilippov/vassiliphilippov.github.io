grindery

noun
workshop for sharpening
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraɪndəri / (AmE), / ˈɡraɪndəri / (BrE)
an old term for a workshop where tools or blades are sharpened or ground

Examples

The grindery workshop
The blacksmith sent the dull blades to the grindery for sharpening.
In the past, every village had a grindery to keep tools in good condition.
The old grindery was filled with the sound of spinning wheels and flying sparks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grindhouse - a place where grinding or sharpening is done
  • synonym: workshop - a room or building where things are made or repaired
