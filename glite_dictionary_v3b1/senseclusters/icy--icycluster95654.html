icy

adjective
shiny and slick like ice
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsi / (AmE), / ˈaɪsi / (BrE)
shiny and slick, as if covered with a thin layer of ice

Examples

Icy sidewalk
After freezing rain, the icy sidewalks were dangerously slick for pedestrians.
Morning sun reflected off the icy branches, making them shine like glass.
The car spun out of control on the icy road surface.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slick - smooth and slippery
  • synonym: slippery - difficult to hold or walk on due to smoothness or moisture
