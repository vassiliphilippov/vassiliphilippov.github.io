crystalline

adjective
having the structure of crystals
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪstəˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstəlɪn / (BrE)
consisting of or containing crystals; having the structure or appearance of crystals

Examples

Crystalline structure

Salt can form beautiful crystalline structures as it dries.
The mineral sample was clearly crystalline under the microscope.
