Saint Patrick

proper noun
patron saint of Ireland
3.9
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈpætrɪk / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈpætrɪk / (BrE)
A Christian missionary and the patron saint of Ireland, credited with bringing Christianity to Ireland in the 5th century.

Examples

Saint Patrick Day

Legends say that Saint Patrick used the shamrock to explain the Holy Trinity.
