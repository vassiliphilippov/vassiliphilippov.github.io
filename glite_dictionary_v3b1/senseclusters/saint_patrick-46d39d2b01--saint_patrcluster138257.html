Saint Patrick

proper noun
patron saint of Ireland
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈpætrɪk / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈpætrɪk / (BrE)
A Christian missionary and the patron saint of Ireland, credited with bringing Christianity to Ireland in the 5th century.

Examples

Saint Patrick Day
Saint Patrick is remembered for converting much of Ireland to Christianity.
Legends say that Saint Patrick used the shamrock to explain the Holy Trinity.
Many people around the world celebrate Saint Patrick's life on March 17th.

Related Terms

  • synonym: St. Patrick's Day - an annual holiday on March 17th honoring Saint Patrick
