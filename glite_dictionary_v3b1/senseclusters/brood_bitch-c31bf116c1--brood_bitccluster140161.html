brood bitch

noun
female dog used for breeding
Pronunciation: / bruːd bɪtʃ / (AmE), / bruːd bɪtʃ / (BrE)
A female dog kept specifically for breeding purposes.

Examples

A brood bitch dog
A well-cared-for brood bitch is essential for a successful breeding program.
She retired her oldest brood bitch after several years of producing champion offspring.
The kennel owner selected the healthiest brood bitch to produce the next litter of puppies.
