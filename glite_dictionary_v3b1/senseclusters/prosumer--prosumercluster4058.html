prosumer

noun
advanced consumer
Pronunciation: / proʊˈsuːmər / (AmE), / prəˈsjuːmə / (BrE)
a consumer who purchases or uses professional-grade products, often with advanced knowledge or semi-professional skills

Examples

A prosumer buyer
Many camera companies design products specifically for the prosumer market.
As a prosumer, he invests in high-end audio equipment for his home studio.
The new software appeals to both professionals and prosumers who want advanced features.
The camera is aimed at the prosumer market, offering both manual controls and automatic modes.
Many prosumers want equipment that bridges the gap between basic and professional models.
This software is perfect for a prosumer who needs more features than a beginner but less complexity than a professional.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: amateur - a person who engages in an activity for pleasure rather than as a profession
  • synonym: enthusiast - a person who is very interested in a particular activity or subject
  • degree higher: professional - a person who does an activity as a paid occupation
