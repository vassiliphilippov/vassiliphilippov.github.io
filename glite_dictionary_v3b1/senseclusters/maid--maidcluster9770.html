maid

noun
unmarried girl or virgin (archaic)
Pronunciation: / meɪd / (AmE), / meɪd / (BrE)
(archaic) An unmarried girl or young woman, especially one who is a virgin.

Examples

A young maid
In old ballads, a noble maid is often pursued by many suitors.
Legends speak of a maid whose virtue was never questioned.
The poet wrote of a fair maid lost in the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maiden - a young unmarried woman, especially a virgin
