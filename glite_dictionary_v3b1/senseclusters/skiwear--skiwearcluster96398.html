skiwear

noun
clothing for skiing
Pronunciation: / ˈskiˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈskiːˌwɛə / (BrE)
clothing worn for skiing or at ski resorts, often providing warmth, protection, or style

Examples

Warm skiwear
Before heading to the slopes, he made sure his skiwear was warm enough for the cold weather.
Modern skiwear combines style with advanced materials for better performance.
She bought new skiwear for her first skiing trip in the Alps.
She packed her most fashionable skiwear for the winter holiday in the Alps.
The resort's lounge was filled with people showing off their designer skiwear.
Even if you do not ski, wearing stylish skiwear is part of the experience at many mountain resorts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: après-ski - social activities and fashion after skiing
  • synonym: outerwear - clothing worn over other clothes for warmth or protection outdoors
  • synonym: sportswear - clothing designed for sports or physical exercise
