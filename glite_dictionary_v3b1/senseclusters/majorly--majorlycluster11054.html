majorly

adverb
mainly; mostly
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪdʒɚli / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdʒəli / (BrE)
mainly or for the most part; predominantly, especially in informal speech

Examples

Majorly about students
The new policy will majorly affect students who live off campus.
She was majorly impressed by the performance and couldn't stop talking about it.
The discussion was majorly focused on improving customer service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mostly - mainly; for the most part
  • antonym: partly - to some extent; not completely
  • synonym: primarily - mainly; chiefly
