craniopharyngioma

noun
benign brain tumor
9.9
Pronunciation: /kreɪ.niː.oʊ.fə.ˈrɪn.dʒiː.oʊ.mə/ (AmE), /kreɪ.niː.əʊ.fə.ˈrɪn.dʒiː.əʊ.mə/ (BrE)
a rare, noncancerous tumor that develops near the pituitary gland at the base of the brain

Examples

Children diagnosed with craniopharyngioma may need long-term hormone therapy after treatment.
