wold

noun
open rolling upland area
9.9
Pronunciation: / woʊld / (AmE), / wəʊld / (BrE)
an area of open rolling upland, often treeless, especially in Britain

Examples

The wold hills
A gentle breeze swept across the sunlit wold, rippling through the tall grass.
Ancient settlements are often found on the open wold above the river valley.
The Yorkshire wolds are famous for their picturesque scenery and vibrant wildlife.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moor - an open, uncultivated upland area, often with heather
