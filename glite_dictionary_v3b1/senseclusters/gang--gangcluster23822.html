gang

noun
group of workers
9.7
Pronunciation: / ɡæŋ / (AmE), / ɡæŋ / (BrE)
a group of people who work, spend time, or associate closely together

Examples

A gang of friends
A gang of builders was busy laying bricks at the new site.
He led a gang of electricians during the factory renovation project.
The road repair gang started work at dawn to avoid the traffic.
After moving to the new city, she quickly found a gang of friends to hang out with.
Every Saturday night, he goes out with his gang to the movies.
The whole gang met up at Lisa's house for pizza and games.
The gang met every Friday night to plan their activities.
A gang of teenagers was seen helping clean up the park after the festival.
The police are investigating a gang suspected of several robberies in the area.
