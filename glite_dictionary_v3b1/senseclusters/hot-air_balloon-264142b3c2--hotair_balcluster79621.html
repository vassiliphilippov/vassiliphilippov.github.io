hot-air balloon

noun
balloon aircraft using heated air for flight
4.0
Pronunciation: / hɑt ɛr bəˈlun / (AmE), / hɒt eə bəˈluːn / (BrE)
a large balloon that is filled with heated air and carries people in a suspended basket for flight

Examples

A hot-air balloon ride
He proposed to her during a romantic hot-air balloon ride.
The festival featured dozens of hot-air balloons taking off at sunrise.
We soared over the countryside in a brightly colored hot-air balloon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: balloon - a large bag filled with gas or air, often for flight or decoration
