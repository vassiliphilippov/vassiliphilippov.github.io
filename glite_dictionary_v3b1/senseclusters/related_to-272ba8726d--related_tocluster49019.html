Synonyms

  • have to do with
    2.7 29/m
    have to do with is an informal phrase for connection, while related to is a neutral general expression used in many contexts.
  • pertain
    4.6 10/m
    pertain means to directly apply to or concern a subject, while related to indicates any kind of connection or link.
  • relate
    2.2 33/m
    relate means to show a clear connection or link, while related to describes something already linked or associated.
  • tie in
    4.1 0.69/m
    tie in means to connect logically or fit with another idea, while related to simply says two things are connected.
  • connected
    2.5 0.00/m
    connected describes an existing link or relationship, while related to is broader and can mean many kinds of association.
  • connect
    2.3 25/m
    connect means to join or make a link between things, while related to describes an existing association rather than creating it.
  • relevant
    3.3 67/m
    relevant means closely connected and appropriate for a topic, while related to simply marks any connection without judging fit.
  • concern
    2.1 45/m
    concern means to affect or be about something, while related to simply states that two things have a connection.
  • refer
    3.0 14/m
    refer means to point to or be about something, while related to describes a general connection or association between things.
