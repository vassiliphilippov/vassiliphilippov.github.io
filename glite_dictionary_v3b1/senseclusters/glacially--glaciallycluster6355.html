glacially

adverb
by glacial action
6.2 6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡleɪʃəli / (AmE), / ˈɡleɪsɪəli / (BrE)
By glacial action; relating to or as a result of the movement or action of a glacier.

Examples

Glacially shaped valleys
Many features of the landscape were formed glacially over thousands of years.
Scientists studied glacially deposited sediments beneath the lake.
The valley was glacially sculpted into a distinctive U-shape.
