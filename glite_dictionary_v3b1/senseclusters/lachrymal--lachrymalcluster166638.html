lachrymal

adjective
relating to tear-producing organs
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlæk.rə.məl /; / ˈlækrɪməl / (AmE), / ˈlæk.rɪ.məl /; / ˈlakrɪməl / (BrE)
relating to tears or the organs that produce tears (such as glands or ducts)

Examples

The doctor explained that the lachrymal glands produce tears to keep our eyes moist.
