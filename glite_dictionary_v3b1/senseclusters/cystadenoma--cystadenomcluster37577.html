cystadenoma

noun
benign cystic tumor
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪstəˌdəˈnoʊmə / (AmE), / ˌsɪstəˌdəˈnəʊmə / (BrE)
a benign tumor that develops from glandular tissue and contains cysts

Examples

An ovarian cystadenoma
The surgeon removed a cystadenoma from the patient's ovary during the operation.
A cystadenoma is usually non-cancerous but may require treatment if it grows large.
After the scan, the doctor explained that the mass was a cystadenoma and not a malignant tumor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adenoma - a benign tumor of glandular origin
  • antonym: carcinoma - a malignant tumor arising from epithelial tissue
