cyberbullying

adjective
online harassment
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪbərˌbʊliɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪbəbʊliɪŋ / (BrE)
relating to messages, actions, or behavior that harass or intimidate someone through digital technology

Examples

Cyberbullying messages
The school has a strict policy against cyberbullying behavior among students.
She received several cyberbullying comments after posting her opinion online.
Many organizations offer resources to help victims of cyberbullying threats.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bullying - the act of intimidating or mistreating someone repeatedly
  • synonym: online harassment - harassment that takes place over the internet
  • antonym: supportive - providing encouragement or help instead of harm
