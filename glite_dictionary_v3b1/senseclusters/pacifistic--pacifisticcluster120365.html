pacifistic

adjective
opposed to war or violence
Pronunciation: / ˌpæsəˈfɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌpæsɪˈfɪstɪk / (BrE)
strongly opposed to war and violence; favoring peace

Examples

A pacifistic attitude
He was criticized for his pacifistic stance during times of political unrest.
Her pacifistic beliefs prevented her from supporting any military action.
The movement was guided by a pacifistic philosophy that valued negotiation over conflict.

Related Terms

  • antonym: militaristic - in favor of war or military solutions
  • synonym: peaceful - not involving war or violence
