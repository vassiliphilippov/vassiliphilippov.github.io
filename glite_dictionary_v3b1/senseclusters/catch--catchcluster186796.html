catch

noun
act of apprehending a criminal
1.0
Pronunciation: / kætʃ / (AmE), / katʃ /; / kætʃ / (BrE)
the act or result of catching something, or the amount or thing that is caught (especially fish or animals)

Examples

  • synonym: grab - a sudden attempt to seize something
  • synonym: haul - the amount caught or taken at one time, especially by fishing
  • synonym: snatch - a quick, sudden taking
