burst

noun
sudden intense occurrence
1.0 3.0
Pronunciation: /bɜːrst/ (AmE), /bɜːst/ (BrE)
a sudden, brief period of intense activity or emotion

Examples

A burst of applause
A sudden burst of applause erupted in the theater at the end of the performance.
She spoke calmly at first, then in a burst of anger, shouted at the crowd.
We ran for shelter when a burst of rain started pouring down.
A sudden burst of laughter erupted from the audience.
After a burst of creativity, he painted three pictures in one day.
She felt a burst of energy and finished the project in an hour.
