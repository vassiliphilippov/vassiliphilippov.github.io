autographic

adjective
written in the author's own handwriting
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːtəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈɡrafɪk / (BrE)
(of a document or signature) written in the author's own handwriting.

Examples

An autographic signature
Her autographic signature increased the value of the book.
Scholars were excited to discover an autographic manuscript among the archives.
The museum displayed an autographic letter written by the novelist.

Related Terms

  • synonym: holograph - a document written entirely in the handwriting of the person whose signature it bears
