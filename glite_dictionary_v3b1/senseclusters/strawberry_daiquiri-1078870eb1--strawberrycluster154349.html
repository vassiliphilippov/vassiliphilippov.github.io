strawberry daiquiri

noun
cocktail with rum and crushed strawberries
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɔːˌbɛri daɪˈkiri / (AmE), / ˈstrɒbəri daɪˈkɪəri / (BrE)
A cocktail made with rum, lime juice, sugar, and crushed strawberries, typically served over ice or blended.

Examples

A strawberry daiquiri cocktail
For dessert, they sipped on homemade strawberry daiquiris and shared a slice of pie.
She ordered a frozen strawberry daiquiri to enjoy by the pool.
The bartender blended fresh strawberries into her strawberry daiquiri.
