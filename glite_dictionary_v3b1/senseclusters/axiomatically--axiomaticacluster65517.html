axiomatically

adverb
in a way based on axioms
9.7 7.5
Pronunciation: /æk.sɪ.ə.ˈmæt.ɪ.kəl.iː/ (AmE), /æk.sɪ.ə.ˈmæt.ɪ.kəl.iː/ (BrE)
In a manner based on or relating to axioms; as something taken to be self-evident or universally accepted.

Examples
