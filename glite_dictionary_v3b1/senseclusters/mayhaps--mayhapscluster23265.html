mayhaps

adverb
perhaps; maybe
9.9
Pronunciation: / meɪˈhæps / (AmE), / meɪˈhæps / (BrE)
used to express uncertainty or possibility; perhaps or maybe (old-fashioned or literary)

Examples

Mayhaps we meet
Mayhaps we shall meet again someday, when the time is right.
He spoke in riddles, saying, 'mayhaps the answer lies within your heart.'
The old book whispered secrets, and mayhaps some truths were hidden in its pages.

Related Terms

  • antonym: certainly - without any doubt; definitely
  • synonym: maybe - possibly; perhaps
  • synonym: perhaps - possibly but not certainly; maybe
