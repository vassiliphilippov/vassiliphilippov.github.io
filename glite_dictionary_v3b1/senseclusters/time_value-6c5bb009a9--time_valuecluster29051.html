time value

noun
duration of a musical note
Pronunciation: / taɪm ˈvæl.ju / (AmE), / taɪm ˈvæl.juː / (BrE)
(music) the relative duration of a musical note, measured in relation to other notes within a piece or measure.

Examples

Time value of notes
Teachers often ask students to identify the time value of different musical notes.
The time value of a quarter note is half that of a half note.
Understanding time value is essential when learning to read sheet music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: note value - the duration of a musical note, represented by symbols
