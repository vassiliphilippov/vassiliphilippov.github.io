the Great Calamity

proper noun
the Irish Potato Famine (1846–1851)
9.1
Pronunciation: /ðə greɪt kə.ˈlæm.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /ðə greɪt kə.ˈlæm.ə.tiː/ (AmE), /ðə greɪt kə.ˈlæm.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /ðiː greɪt kə.ˈlæm.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
