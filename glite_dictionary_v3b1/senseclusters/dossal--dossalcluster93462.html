dossal

noun
ornamental hanging behind a church altar
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːsəl / (AmE), / ˈdɒsəl / (BrE)
an ornamental hanging of rich fabric, usually placed behind the altar in a church or at the sides of a chancel

Examples

A dossal hanging
During the restoration, the faded dossal was carefully cleaned and repaired.
The cathedral's new dossal depicted scenes from the lives of local saints.
The richly embroidered dossal added grandeur to the church's sanctuary.
