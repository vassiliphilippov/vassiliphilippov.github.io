leanly

adverb
in a thin manner
4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈliːn.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈliːn.lɪ/ (BrE)
in a way that is thin, spare, or without excess

Examples

To be leanly built
The runner was leanly built, with little body fat and strong muscles.
The room was leanly furnished, containing only a bed and a chair.
He managed the project leanly, avoiding any unnecessary expenses.

Related Terms

  • antonym: richly - in a way that is full, abundant, or luxurious
  • synonym: slenderly - in a slender or slim manner
  • synonym: sparely - in a way that is without excess or unnecessary detail
