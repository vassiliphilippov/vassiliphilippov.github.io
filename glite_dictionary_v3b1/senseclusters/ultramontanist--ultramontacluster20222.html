ultramontanist

adjective
favoring papal supremacy
9.9
Pronunciation: /ʌl.trə.ˈmɑːn.tə.nɪst/ (AmE), /ʌl.trə.ˈmɒn.tə.nɪst/ (BrE)
supporting or relating to the belief in strong papal authority over local church leaders, especially in the Roman Catholic Church

Examples

Ultramontanist views
The bishop's ultramontanist stance shaped his approach to church governance.
Debates between ultramontanist and localist clergy were common in the nineteenth century.
Her ultramontanist beliefs led her to support decisions made directly by the Pope.
