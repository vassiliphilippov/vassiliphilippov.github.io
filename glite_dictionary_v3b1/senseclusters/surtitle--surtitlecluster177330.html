surtitle

noun
projected translation above stage
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜrˌtaɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈsɜːˌtaɪtəl / (BrE)
a translation of the words of a foreign opera or musical performance, projected on a screen above the stage for the audience

Examples

Foreign surtitles
Many theaters now provide surtitles for performances in foreign languages.
Reading the surtitles helped me understand the meaning of each aria.
The opera featured surtitles so the audience could follow the Italian lyrics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: subtitle - translation of dialogue in films or television, displayed at the bottom of the screen
