prodroma

noun
early symptom of disease onset
8.2
Pronunciation: / proʊˈdroʊmə / (AmE), / prəʊˈdrəʊmə / (BrE)
An early symptom indicating the onset of a disease or the approach of an attack.

Examples

Early prodroma symptom
Recognizing the prodroma may help prevent full onset of the illness.
Some patients experience a headache as the prodroma of a migraine.
The presence of a prodroma can assist doctors in making an early diagnosis.
