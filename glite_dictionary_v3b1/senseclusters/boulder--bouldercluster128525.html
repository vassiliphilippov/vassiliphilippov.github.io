boulder

verb
to climb rocks without ropes
6.6
Pronunciation: /ˈboʊl.dər/ (AmE), /ˈbəʊl.də/ (BrE)
to climb large rocks or short rock faces without using ropes or harnesses, usually at low heights.

Examples

She started bouldering because it requires less equipment than traditional climbing.
They spent the afternoon bouldering on the large rocks by the river.
