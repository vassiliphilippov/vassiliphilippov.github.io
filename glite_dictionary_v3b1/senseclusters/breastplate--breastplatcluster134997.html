breastplate

noun
chest armor; front of a cuirass
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛstˌpleɪt / (AmE), / ˈbrɛs(t)ˌpleɪt / (BrE)
A piece of armor covering the chest, forming the front part of a cuirass.

Examples

A breastplate of armor
Archaeologists discovered a bronze breastplate from the Roman era at the excavation site.
He admired the detailed engravings on the ancient breastplate displayed in the museum.
The knight's breastplate gleamed in the sunlight as he entered the arena.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cuirass - a piece of armor consisting of breastplate and backplate fastened together
