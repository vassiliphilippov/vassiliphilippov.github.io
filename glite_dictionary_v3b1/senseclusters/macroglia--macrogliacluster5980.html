macroglia

noun
large neuroglial cells in nervous tissue
9.9
Pronunciation: /mæk.rə.ˈgliː.ə/ (AmE), /mæk.rə.ˈgliː.ə/ (BrE)
Large neuroglial cells in nervous tissue, including astrocytes and oligodendrocytes, as distinct from microglia.
