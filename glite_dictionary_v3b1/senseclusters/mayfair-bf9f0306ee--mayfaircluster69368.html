Mayfair

noun
spring festival in May
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪˌfɛr / (AmE), / ˈmeɪˌfeə / (BrE)
a public fair or festival traditionally held in the month of May, often to celebrate spring (historical)

Examples

A lively Mayfair
Every year, the town square was decorated for the lively Mayfair.
Children wore flower crowns and danced at the Mayfair to welcome the warmer weather.
The tradition of Mayfair dates back centuries in many English villages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: May Day - a traditional festival marking the beginning of May
  • synonym: carnival - a public festival with music, dancing, and entertainment
