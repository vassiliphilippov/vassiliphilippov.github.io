separably

adverb
in a manner allowing separation
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛpɚəbli / (AmE), / ˈsɛpərəbli / (BrE)
In a manner that allows separation or individuation; in such a way that things can be considered or treated apart from each other.

Examples

To think separably
Although the twins look alike, their personalities should be judged separably.
Each element of the system can function separably from the rest.
The advantages and disadvantages of the policy must be analyzed separably.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inseparably - in a way that cannot be separated
  • synonym: separately - as a separate entity; not together
