thoron

noun
radioactive radon isotope
Pronunciation: / ˈθɔːrɒn / (AmE), / ˈθɔːrɒn / (BrE)
a radioactive isotope of radon, also known as radon-220, found in certain radioactive decay processes

Examples

Thoron radioactive gas
Scientists measured the presence of thoron in the laboratory air.
Exposure to thoron can be a concern in areas with high levels of radioactive minerals.
The decay of thoron produces alpha particles that are detected by specialized instruments.
