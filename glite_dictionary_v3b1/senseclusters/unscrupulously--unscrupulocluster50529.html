unscrupulously

adverb
in a morally unprincipled way
7.8 6.4
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈskruː.pjə.lə.sliː/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈskruː.pjə.lə.sliː/ (BrE)
In a way that shows no moral principles or scruples; without regard for what is right or wrong.

Examples

To unscrupulously manipulate data
He unscrupulously manipulated the data to support his false claims.
She unscrupulously took credit for her colleague's achievements.
The company unscrupulously exploited its workers without concern for their well-being.
