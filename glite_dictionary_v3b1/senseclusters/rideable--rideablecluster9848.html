rideable

adjective
suitable for riding
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪdəbəl / (AmE), / ˈraɪdəbəl / (BrE)
suitable or safe to be ridden, or able to be ridden

Examples

Rideable mountain trail
The mountain trail was rough, but still rideable for experienced cyclists.
After the snow was cleared, the road became rideable again.
He checked if the old horse was still rideable before taking it out.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unrideable - not suitable or safe to be ridden
  • synonym: usable - able to be used
