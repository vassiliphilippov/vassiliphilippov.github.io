unprotestingly

adverb
without protest
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnprəˈtɛstɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌʌnprəˈtɛstɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that does not show protest or objection

Examples

To follow unprotestingly
He followed the instructions unprotestingly, not raising any concerns.
The children sat unprotestingly as the teacher explained the new rules.
She handed over the documents unprotestingly, accepting the outcome.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obediently - in a way that shows willingness to comply
  • antonym: reluctantly - in a way that shows unwillingness or hesitation
