maternal

adjective
characteristic of a mother
3.8 7.61/m
Pronunciation: / m…ôÀàt…úrn…ôl / (AmE), / m…ôÀàt…úÀên…ôl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a mother, especially in showing care, kindness, or affection.

Examples

Maternal love
His aunt's maternal instincts made her the family's go-to for comfort.
She showed a maternal concern for the well-being of her students.
The nurse offered maternal care and support to the frightened children.

Synonyms

  • motherly
    2.3 1.02/m
    motherly is warm and openly caring in personal situations, while maternal sounds more formal and also names biological relations.
  • motherlike
    3.8 0.00/m
    motherlike describes resemblance to a mother's behavior or style, while maternal refers to mother-related care or biological relation.
