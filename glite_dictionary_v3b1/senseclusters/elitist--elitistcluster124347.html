elitist

adjective
believing in superiority
Pronunciation: / ɪˈliːtɪst / (AmE), / ɪˈliːtɪst / (BrE)
believing that certain people or groups are superior and deserve special treatment

Examples

Elitist views
Many criticized the politician for his elitist attitude toward ordinary citizens.
The club was seen as elitist because it only accepted wealthy members.
Her elitist opinions made it difficult for her to relate to her coworkers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: egalitarian - believing that all people are equal and deserve equal rights
  • synonym: snobbish - having or showing the attitude of people who think they are better than others
