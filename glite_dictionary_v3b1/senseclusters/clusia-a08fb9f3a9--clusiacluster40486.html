Clusia

noun
genus of tropical American aromatic trees or shrubs
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkluːziə / (AmE), / ˈkluːziə / (BrE)
A genus of tropical American aromatic trees or shrubs, often epiphytic and including some species that are stranglers.
