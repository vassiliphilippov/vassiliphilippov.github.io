comedy

noun
humorous drama with a happy ending
2.9 1.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑː.mə.diː/ /; / /ˈkɑː.mə.dɪ/ (AmE), /ˈkɒm.ə.dɪ/ /; / /ˈkɒm.ə.diː/ (BrE)
A type of drama or literature that is amusing and light, and usually ends happily.

Examples

The new play is a delightful comedy that had the audience laughing all night.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: satire - comedy that ridicules vice or folly, often in a witty or ironic way
  • antonym: tragedy - serious drama with a sorrowful or disastrous conclusion
