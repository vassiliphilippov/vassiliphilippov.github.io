go-as-you-please

adjective
free from rules or conventions
Pronunciation: / ˌgoʊ æz jə ˈpliz / (AmE), / ˌgəʊ æz jə ˈpliːz / (BrE)
Not restricted by rules, laws, or conventions; free and unconstrained.

Examples

A go-as-you-please style
He enjoyed the go-as-you-please attitude of the festival, where everyone did as they liked.
Many artists prefer a go-as-you-please approach to creativity, ignoring traditional techniques.
The club's events follow a go-as-you-please schedule, with no set order or requirement to participate.

Related Terms

  • antonym: strict - following rules or conventions closely; the opposite quality
  • synonym: unconstrained - not subjected to constraints or restrictions
  • synonym: unrestricted - not limited or controlled in any way
