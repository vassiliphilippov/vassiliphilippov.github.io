together

adverb
with cooperation or in unison
1.8
Pronunciation: / təˈɡɛðər / (AmE), / təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
in cooperation or with a shared purpose; acting jointly or in unison

Examples

Work together
If we work together, we can achieve much more than alone.
They laughed together at the funny story he told.
We solved the problem together and finished ahead of schedule.
If we act together, we can accomplish more than alone.
The entire team worked together to develop the new product.
They organized the event together, ensuring every detail was perfect.

Related Terms

  • antonym: separately - without others; not in cooperation or in company
