chinning bar

noun
bar for chin-ups or pull-ups
9.2 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃɪn.ɪŋ bɑːr/ (AmE), /ˈtʃɪn.ɪŋ bɑː/ (BrE)
a horizontal bar fixed at a suitable height, used for performing chin-ups or pull-ups by gripping it with the hands and lifting the chin above the bar

Examples

A chinning bar exercise
He installed a chinning bar in his garage to work out at home.
She struggled at first to do a single pull-up on the chinning bar.
The gym's chinning bar is perfect for building upper body strength.
