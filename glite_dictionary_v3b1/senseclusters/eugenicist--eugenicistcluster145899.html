eugenicist

noun
supporter of eugenics
9.9
Pronunciation: / juːˈdʒɛnɪsɪst /; / juˈdʒɛnɪsɪst / (AmE), / juːˈdʒɛnɪsɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or studies the improvement of the human population through selective breeding

Examples

Many people today criticize the ideas promoted by a eugenicist as unethical and discriminatory.
A eugenicist may advocate for policies aimed at improving genetic qualities in future generations.
