multiconductor

adjective
with multiple conductors
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltikoʊnˈdʌktər / (AmE), / ˌmʌltikənˈdʌktə / (BrE)
having or involving more than one electrical conductor within a single cable or unit

Examples

Multiconductor cable
The technician installed a multiconductor cable to connect the control panel to the machinery.
For safety and efficiency, the design called for multiconductor wiring throughout the building.
A multiconductor unit simplifies the process of organizing complex electrical systems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: multiwire - consisting of or using several wires
  • antonym: single-conductor - having only one electrical conductor
