clemency

noun
mercy or leniency, especially in justice
7.7 8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈklɛmənsi / (AmE), / ˈklɛmənsi / (BrE)
the act of showing mercy or leniency, especially by a person or authority charged with administering justice

Examples

Show clemency to prisoners
Amnesty International urged the government to show clemency to the political detainees.
Her appeal for clemency was considered by the court before passing judgment.
The governor granted clemency to several prisoners facing lengthy sentences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leniency - the quality of being more merciful or tolerant than expected
  • synonym: mercy - compassion or forgiveness shown to someone
  • antonym: severity - harshness or strictness, especially in administering justice
