swing voter

noun
voter with unpredictable party allegiance
8.0 3.5
Pronunciation: /swɪŋ ˈvoʊ.tər/ (AmE), /swɪŋ ˈvəʊ.tə/ (BrE)
a person who does not consistently support any single political party and whose vote may determine the outcome of an election

Examples

Swing voter decides election
As a swing voter, she listened carefully to both parties before making her decision.
Pollsters are eager to predict how swing voters will influence this year's election outcome.
The candidates focused their campaigns on states with the highest percentage of swing voters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: partisan - a committed member of a political party
  • synonym: undecided voter - a voter who has not made up their mind about which party or candidate to support
