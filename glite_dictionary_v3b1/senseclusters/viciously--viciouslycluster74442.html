viciously

adverb
in a cruel or violent way
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪʃ.əs.li / (AmE), / ˈvɪʃ.əs.li / (BrE)
In a cruel, violent, or savage manner.

Examples

To be viciously attacked
She was viciously criticized by her opponents in the debate.
The dog viciously defended its territory from intruders.
The protesters were viciously dispersed by riot police during the demonstration.
