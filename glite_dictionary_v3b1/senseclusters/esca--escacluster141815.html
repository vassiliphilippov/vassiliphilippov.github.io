esca

noun
anglerfish lure
Pronunciation: / ˈɛskə / (AmE), / ˈɛskə / (BrE)
the fleshy, often luminous growth on an anglerfish that is used to attract prey

Examples

Anglerfish esca
The anglerfish waved its esca to entice smaller fish in the deep sea.
Scientists studied how the esca emits light to attract prey in darkness.
Without its esca, the anglerfish would struggle to catch food in the ocean depths.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lure - something used to attract or tempt prey or victims
