nearsightedly

adverb
with poor distance vision
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪrˌsaɪtɪdli / (AmE), / ˈnɪəˌsaɪtɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that shows inability to see distant objects clearly or with a lack of foresight

Examples

Look nearsightedly at signs
She looked nearsightedly at the street signs, struggling to read them from a distance.
He nearsightedly ignored the long-term consequences of his decision.
The student nearsightedly squinted at the board, unable to make out the writing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: farsightedly - in a way that shows ability to see distant objects clearly or with foresight
  • synonym: myopically - in a way that shows nearsightedness or lack of foresight
