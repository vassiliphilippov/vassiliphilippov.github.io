banns

noun
public announcement of intended marriage
7.0 8.8
Pronunciation: /bænz/ (AmE), /bænz/ (BrE)
a public announcement in a church of an intended marriage, made so that anyone who knows of a legal reason why the marriage should not take place can declare it

Examples

Publish the banns
It is still customary in some countries to publish the banns before a marriage can take place.
The banns were read aloud in church three Sundays before the wedding.
They decided to forgo the traditional reading of the banns and have a civil ceremony instead.
