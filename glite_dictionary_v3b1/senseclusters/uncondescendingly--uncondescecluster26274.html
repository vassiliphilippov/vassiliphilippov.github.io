uncondescendingly

adverb
without superiority
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkɒndɪˈsɛndɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌʌnkɒndɪˈsɛndɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that does not show a sense of superiority or patronizing attitude toward others

Examples

Speak uncondescendingly to others
He offered advice uncondescendingly, making everyone feel comfortable and valued.
The teacher spoke uncondescendingly to her students, treating them as equals.
She answered all the questions uncondescendingly, never making anyone feel foolish.

Related Terms

  • antonym: condescendingly - in a way that shows a feeling of superiority
  • synonym: respectfully - in a way that shows respect
