Theodosian

adjective
of Theodosius's era
Pronunciation: / ˌθiːəˈdoʊziən / (AmE), / ˌθiːəˈdəʊziən / (BrE)
relating to the Roman emperor Theodosius or the period of his reign

Examples

Theodosian Code
The Theodosian Code was a major legal document in the late Roman Empire.
Historians often refer to the Theodosian period when discussing changes in Roman law.
Artifacts from the Theodosian era reveal much about the culture of that time.
