Psilotum

noun
genus of primitive fern-like plants
Pronunciation: / saɪˈloʊtəm / (AmE), / saɪˈləʊtəm / (BrE)
a genus of primitive, fern-like plants in the family Psilotaceae, commonly known as whisk ferns
