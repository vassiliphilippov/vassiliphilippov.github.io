cartographic

adjective
relating to maps or map-making
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːrtəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌkɑːtəˈɡrafɪk / (BrE)
relating to the making or study of maps and charts.

Examples

Cartographic technology
