feast

verb
to delight oneself; to enjoy intensely
9.6 3.0
Pronunciation: /fiːst/ (AmE), /fiːst/ (BrE)
to delight or gratify oneself, especially by giving one's senses or mind abundant pleasure from something

Examples

To feast on beauty
He would often feast on the beauty of the changing autumn leaves.
On their hike, the children feasted their senses on the sights and sounds of the forest.
She stopped to feast her eyes on the brilliant sunset.
