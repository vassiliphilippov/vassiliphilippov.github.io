abeyance

noun
temporary suspension or inactivity
9.0
Pronunciation: / əˈbeɪ.əns / (AmE), / əˈbeɪ.əns / (BrE)
A temporary state in which something is not being used or is suspended; a period of inactivity.

Examples

Held in abeyance
Our plan to expand the business is being held in abeyance until further notice.
Talks between the two parties remain in abeyance due to ongoing disagreements.
The inheritance was kept in abeyance until the rightful heir could be determined.

Related Terms

  • antonym: continuation - the action of carrying on without interruption
  • synonym: suspension - the state of being temporarily stopped or inactive
