unshrinkable

adjective
not able to be shrunk
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃrɪŋkəbl̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃrɪŋkəbl / (BrE)
Incapable of being shrunk, diminished, or reduced in size or amount.

Examples

An unshrinkable material
Even under intense heat, the material proved to be completely unshrinkable.
The company developed an unshrinkable plastic for use in harsh environments.
This fabric is advertised as unshrinkable, even after many washes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shrinkable - able to be shrunk or reduced in size
