depictive

adjective
representing in a recognizable manner
8.3
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɪktɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈpɪktɪv / (BrE)
(of art or language) representing objects, scenes, or people in a way that is recognizable; serving to depict.

Examples

A depictive style
Her writing style is highly depictive, painting vivid pictures with words.
The artist's most depictive paintings closely resemble real-life landscapes.
Unlike abstract pieces, depictive art aims to portray subjects that the viewer can identify.

Related Terms

  • antonym: abstract - (of art) not representing recognizable reality
  • synonym: representational - (of art) showing recognizable subjects or objects
