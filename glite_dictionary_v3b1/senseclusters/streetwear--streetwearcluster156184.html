streetwear

adjective
urban fashion style
Pronunciation: / ˈstriːtˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈstriːtˌwɛə / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of casual, fashionable clothing inspired by urban youth culture and street style

Examples

Streetwear style
The designer's new collection features streetwear pieces inspired by graffiti and skate culture.
Many young people prefer a streetwear look for its comfort and trendiness.
She launched a streetwear brand that quickly became popular among urban youth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: formal - suitable for official or important occasions; opposite of casual
  • synonym: urban - relating to a city or town, especially in style or culture
