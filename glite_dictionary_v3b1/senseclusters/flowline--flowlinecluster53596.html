flowline

noun
pipe for oil or gas
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfloʊ.laɪn/ (AmE), /ˈfləʊ.laɪn/ (BrE)
a pipe or conduit that transports oil, gas, or other fluids from a well or production facility to another location for processing or storage

Examples

Oil flowline
The engineers repaired the damaged flowline to restore the transfer of oil from the well.
A new flowline was installed to connect the subsea installation to the processing facility.
Regular inspections of the flowline help prevent leaks and ensure safe operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conduit - a channel or pipe for conveying fluids
  • synonym: pipeline - a long pipe used to transport oil, gas, or other fluids over distances
