phone

noun
electro-acoustic transducer for sound (ear device)
Pronunciation: / foʊn / (AmE), / fəʊn / (BrE)
A device that converts electrical signals into sound, typically worn over or inserted into the ear for listening.

Examples

Headset phones
Please use your phones to monitor the audio levels during the recording.
She replaced her old phones with a new set that offered better sound quality.
The technician handed me a pair of phones to check the clarity of the mix.

Related Terms

  • synonym: earphone - small audio device for the ear
  • synonym: headphone - pair of small speakers for ears, worn over the head
