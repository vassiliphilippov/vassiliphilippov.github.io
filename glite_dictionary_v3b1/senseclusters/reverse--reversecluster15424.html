reverse

adjective
opposite or contrary in direction or effect
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɝːs / (AmE), / rɪˈvɜːs / (BrE)
contrary or opposite to what is usual or expected; turned or directed backward (used as an adjective)

Examples

Reverse order
In a reverse situation, the roles of the two teams were switched.
The reverse side of the coin has a different design.
They took the reverse route to avoid traffic on the main road.
Please put the car in reverse before backing out of the driveway.
She glanced in the mirror to check for cars before switching into reverse.
The train moved in reverse to couple with the back carriage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: normal - conforming to a standard; usual
  • synonym: opposite - completely different; contrary
  • synonym: rearward - directed toward the back
