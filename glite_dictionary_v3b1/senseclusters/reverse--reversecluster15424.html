Examples

Synonyms

  • contrary
    3.5 15/m
    contrary means totally different from what is expected, while reverse means something turned backward or opposite to the usual.
  • inverse
    6.1 2.58/m
    inverse shows an opposite order or effect often in relationships, while reverse means generally opposite or turned backward.
  • rearward
    7.0 0.20/m
    rearward points toward the back or rear, while reverse also means opposite but more often describes being turned backward.
