capitalize

verb
convert reserve funds into capital
1.0 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkæp.ɪ.tə.laɪz/ /; / /ˈkæp.ə.tə.laɪz/ (AmE), /ˈkæp.ɪ.tə.laɪz/ /; / /ˈkæp.ə.tə.laɪz/ (BrE)
to supply a business with capital, such as by converting funds or attracting investments and loans

Examples

To capitalize a company
After years of saving profits, the firm plans to capitalize the accumulated funds.
Several companies capitalized their retained earnings by issuing new shares to shareholders.
The board decided to capitalize the company's reserves to fund expansion.
Startups often seek to capitalize their businesses by attracting venture capital and loans.
The company was capitalized through a mix of private investment and bank loans.
The partners decided to capitalize the new firm with both equity and debt.
