splinterless

adjective
not likely to splinter or shatter
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsplɪntɚlɪs / (AmE), / ˈsplɪntəlɪs / (BrE)
(of a material or object) resistant to shattering or splintering; not likely to break into sharp fragments.

Examples

Splinterless wood
For playground equipment, splinterless wood is preferred to protect children from injury.
Her new cutting board is made of a splinterless composite material.
The company installed splinterless glass panels for improved safety in the lobby.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shatterproof - resistant to shattering; does not break easily
