Alstroemeria

noun
genus of South American flowering plants
Pronunciation: / ˌælstroʊˈmɪəriə / (AmE), / ˌalstrəʊˈmɪəriə / (BrE)
A genus of showy South American flowering plants, commonly called Peruvian lilies or lily of the Incas, with leafy stems and colorful, funnel-shaped flowers.

Examples

The Alstroemeria flower
Botanists have classified several species within the Alstroemeria genus native to South America.
Gardeners often choose Alstroemeria for borders and flower arrangements due to its striking appearance.
The Alstroemeria is popular for its long-lasting cut flowers and vibrant colors.
