thermidor

noun
seafood dish; French calendar month
Pronunciation: / ˈθɜːrmɪˌdɔr / (AmE), / ˈθɜːmɪˌdɔː / (BrE)
a creamy seafood dish, especially made with lobster, or the eleventh month in the French Revolutionary calendar

Examples

Lobster thermidor
He ordered the lobster thermidor at the restaurant for a special treat.
The chef prepared a delicious thermidor using fresh seafood and a creamy sauce.
In the French Revolutionary calendar, Thermidor was the eleventh month, falling in mid-summer.
