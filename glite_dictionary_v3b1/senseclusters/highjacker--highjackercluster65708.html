highjacker

noun
someone who illegally takes control of a vehicle
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪˌdʒækər / (AmE), / ˈhaɪˌdʒækə / (BrE)
a person who illegally seizes or takes control of a vehicle, especially by force (often used for aircraft)

Examples

The highjacker seizes vehicle
Police arrested the highjacker shortly after he seized the delivery truck.
The highjacker demanded money before allowing the passengers to leave the bus.
The train was stopped by a masked highjacker late last night.
Police quickly responded when they learned there was a highjacker on board.
Several highjackers took control of the airplane during the night.
The highjacker threatened the pilot and demanded to be flown to another country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hijacker - a person who unlawfully takes control of a vehicle, especially an aircraft
