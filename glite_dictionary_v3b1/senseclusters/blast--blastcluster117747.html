blast

verb
send a mass message
3.8
Pronunciation: /blæst/ (AmE), /blɑːst/ (BrE)
to send a message, announcement, or email to a large group of people at the same time, especially for promotion or information.

Examples

To blast an email
The marketing team blasted a product announcement to all subscribers this morning.
Before the sale, they were blasting promotional messages to thousands of customers.
Can you blast the newsletter to our mailing list after you finish?

Related Terms
