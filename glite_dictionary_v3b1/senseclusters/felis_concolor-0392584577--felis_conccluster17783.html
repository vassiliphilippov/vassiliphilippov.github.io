Felis concolor

noun
scientific name for the cougar (a large American wild cat)
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːlɪs ˈkɑn.kə.lɔr / (AmE), / ˈfiːlɪs ˈkɒn.kə.lɔː / (BrE)
