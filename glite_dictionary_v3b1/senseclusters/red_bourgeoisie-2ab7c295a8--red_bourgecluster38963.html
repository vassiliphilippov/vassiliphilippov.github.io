red bourgeoisie

noun
wealthy elite in a socialist context
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / r…õd b ärÀà íw…ëzi / (AmE), / red b ä…ôÀà íw…ëÀêzi / (BrE)
Members of the middle or upper class who hold wealth and power in a socialist or Communist state, often criticized for living luxuriously while professing socialist ideals.

Examples

The red bourgeoisie class
His lifestyle was more typical of the red bourgeoisie than of a true socialist.
She accused the red bourgeoisie of ignoring the needs of ordinary workers.
The new policy was designed to curb the influence of the red bourgeoisie in the government.

  • synonym: bourgeoisie - the middle class, especially in Marxist contexts
