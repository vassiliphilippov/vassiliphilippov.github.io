must

adjective
highly recommended; essential
1.6
Pronunciation: / mʌst / (AmE), / mʌst / (BrE)
Highly recommended or essential, especially referring to something one should not miss or fail to experience.

Examples

A must read
Her new novel is a must for anyone interested in contemporary fiction.
That museum is a must if you are visiting Paris.
The guide lists all the must restaurants in the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: essential - extremely important or necessary
  • antonym: optional - not compulsory or necessary
