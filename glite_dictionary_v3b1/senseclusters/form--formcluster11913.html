form

noun
the shape or configuration of something
1.3 90/m
Pronunciation: / f…îrm / (AmE), / f…îÀêm / (BrE)
the visible shape, appearance, or structure of something (physical or abstract)

Examples

The form of a statue

The building's unique form makes it a landmark in the city skyline.
The sculptor studied the form of the marble before beginning his work.

Synonyms

  • shape
    1.0 42/m
    shape is the visible outline or appearance of something, while form covers both visible shape and abstract structure.
  • conformation
    7.0 0.72/m
    conformation is the shape formed by how parts fit together, while form means general visible shape or overall structure.
  • configuration
    4.8 13/m
    configuration is the arrangement or setup of parts for a purpose, while form is the visible shape or structure.
