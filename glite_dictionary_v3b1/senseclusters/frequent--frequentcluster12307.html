frequent

adjective
happening often; recurring
1.0 28/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàfriÀêkw…ônt / (AmE), / ÀàfriÀêkw…ônt / (BrE)
happening or appearing often; occurring repeatedly or at short intervals

Examples

Frequent visits
He made frequent visits to his grandmother's house.
Power outages have become more frequent during the summer months.
This is a frequent problem in large cities.
Complaints about the noise have become increasingly frequent over the past month.
She is a frequent visitor to the local art gallery.
The trains to the city are frequent during rush hour.
