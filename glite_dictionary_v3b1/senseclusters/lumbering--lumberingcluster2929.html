lumbering

adjective
moving heavily and clumsily
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌmbərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlʌmbərɪŋ / (BrE)
moving in a slow, heavy, and awkward way, usually because of large size or weight.

Examples

Lumbering elephant
His lumbering footsteps echoed in the empty hallway.
She watched the lumbering truck struggle up the steep hill.
The lumbering elephant made its way slowly through the jungle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ponderous - slow and clumsy because of great weight
