paleographical

adjective
about ancient writing
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪliəˈɡræfɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌpæliəˈɡræfɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study or analysis of ancient writing systems and manuscripts

Examples

Paleographical study of manuscripts
The museum hosted a paleographical exhibition featuring rare ancient manuscripts.
Her paleographical expertise helped identify the origins of the old scroll.
A paleographical approach can reveal how writing styles changed over centuries.
