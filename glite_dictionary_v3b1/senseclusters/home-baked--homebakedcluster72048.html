home-baked

adjective
baked at home
Pronunciation: / hoʊm ˈbeɪkt / (AmE), / həʊm ˈbeɪkt / (BrE)
(of food) baked at home rather than bought from a shop or bakery.

Examples

Home-baked bread
His grandmother is famous for her home-baked pies during the holidays.
I always prefer home-baked bread over store-bought brands.
She brought a plate of home-baked cookies to the party.
