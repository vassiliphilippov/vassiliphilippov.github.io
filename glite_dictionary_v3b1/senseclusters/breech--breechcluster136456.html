breech

noun
feet-first birth position
8.4 0.25/m
Pronunciation: / briÀêt É / (AmE), / briÀêt É / (BrE)
a position in childbirth where the baby is delivered feet or buttocks first instead of head first

Examples

Breech birth
The ultrasound revealed that the baby was in a breech position before delivery.
Doctors often discuss options with parents when a breech birth is likely.
A breech presentation can sometimes require a cesarean section for a safe delivery.

Synonyms

  • breech presentation
    6.3 0.03/m
    breech presentation is the formal medical name for a baby positioned feet or buttocks first, while breech is the shorter everyday word.

Related Terms
