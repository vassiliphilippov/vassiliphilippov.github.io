likker

noun
informal drunkard
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪkər / (AmE), / ˈlɪkə / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who habitually drinks alcoholic beverages; a drunkard

Examples

Old likker at the bar
Everyone in the neighborhood called him a likker because he drank at the tavern nightly.
The old likker stumbled out of the bar, singing loudly to himself.
She warned her son not to become a likker like his uncle.
