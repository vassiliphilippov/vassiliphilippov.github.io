become

verb
to enhance the appearance of; to suit
Pronunciation: / bɪˈkʌm / (AmE), / bɪˈkʌm / (BrE)

Examples

The elegant gown became her, highlighting her graceful posture.
This old-fashioned style does not become him at all.
