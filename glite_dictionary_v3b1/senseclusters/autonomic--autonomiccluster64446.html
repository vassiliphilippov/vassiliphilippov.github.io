autonomic

adjective
self-managing computing systems
Pronunciation: / cc51d0.t59c8n51d0.m6ak / (AmE), / cc54d0.t59c8n52m.6ak / (BrE)
(in computing) describing software, systems, or networks that manage, configure, and heal themselves automatically with little or no human intervention.

Examples

Autonomic computing system
Many enterprises are adopting autonomic computing to reduce manual configuration and maintenance.
The new server cluster uses autonomic policies to detect failures and reconfigure itself automatically.
Research teams developed an autonomic network that optimises traffic and heals performance problems without intervention.

Related Terms

  • related: autonomic nervous system - the biological system that inspired the computing metaphor of automatic internal regulation
  • synonym: self-healing - able to detect and correct faults automatically
  • synonym: self-managing - able to operate and maintain itself without external control
