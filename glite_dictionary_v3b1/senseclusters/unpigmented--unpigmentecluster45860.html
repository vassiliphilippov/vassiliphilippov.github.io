unpigmented

adjective
lacking pigment or color
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.pɪɡˈmɛn.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.pɪɡˈmɛn.tɪd / (BrE)
having no pigment; lacking coloration caused by natural pigments

Examples

An unpigmented animal
Albino animals are completely unpigmented and have white fur or skin.
She noticed a small, unpigmented patch on her hand after her injury.
The unpigmented tissue appears nearly transparent under the microscope.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pigmented - having pigment; colored by natural pigments
