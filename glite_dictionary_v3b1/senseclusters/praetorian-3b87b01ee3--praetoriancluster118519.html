Praetorian

noun
member of Roman imperial bodyguard
Pronunciation: / priˈtɔriən / (AmE), / priːˈtɔːriən / (BrE)
a member of the Praetorian Guard, the elite bodyguard of Roman emperors in ancient Rome.

Examples

A Praetorian Guard
Historically, the Praetorian Guard held significant political power in ancient Rome.
Several Praetorians were implicated in the plot against the ruler.
The emperor trusted his Praetorian with guarding the entrance to his private chambers.
