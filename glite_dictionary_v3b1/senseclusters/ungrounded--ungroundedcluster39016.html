ungrounded

adjective
not electrically grounded
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡraʊndɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡraʊndɪd / (BrE)
not connected to the earth or to an electrical ground, lacking a protective grounding connection

Examples

Ungrounded wire
The technician discovered that the wire was ungrounded, posing a risk of electric shock.
It is dangerous to use ungrounded outlets in your home, especially in wet areas.
Before installing the new appliance, make sure it is not connected to an ungrounded socket.

Related Terms

  • antonym: grounded - connected to the earth or an electrical ground for safety
