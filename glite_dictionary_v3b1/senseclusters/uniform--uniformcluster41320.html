uniform

noun
distinctive clothing worn by a group
2.2 1.5
Pronunciation: /ˈjuː.nə.fɔːrm/ /; / /ˈjuː.nɪ.fɔːrm/ (AmE), /ˈjuː.nɪ.fɔːm/ (BrE)
A set of clothes of a distinctive design worn by members of the same organization or group, especially for identification.

Examples

A school uniform
All the students are required to wear a uniform to school.
She recognized the soldier by his military uniform.
The security guards marched by in their crisp, matching uniforms.
