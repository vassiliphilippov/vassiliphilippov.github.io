quirkily

adverb
in an odd way
6.8 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / Ààkw…úrk…™li / (AmE), / Ààkw…úÀêk…™li / (BrE)
in a manner that is unusual, odd, or full of peculiar characteristics

Examples

Dresses quirkily

She answered the interview questions quirkily, making everyone in the room smile.
