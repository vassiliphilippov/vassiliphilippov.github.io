knee pad

noun
protective pad for the knee
2.1
Pronunciation: / niː pæd / (AmE), / niː pæd / (BrE)
a protective pad worn over the knee to prevent injury, especially during sports or physical activity

Examples

Knee pads for work
All construction workers must wear knee pads when working on their knees for long periods.
My son forgot his knee pad before hockey practice and had to borrow one from a teammate.
The volleyball player wore knee pads to protect herself from hard falls on the court.
