hymenally challenged

adjective
euphemism for lacking an intact hymen
9.9
Pronunciation: / haɪˈmɛnəli ˈtʃælɪndʒd / (AmE), / haɪˈmɛnəli ˈtʃælɪndʒd / (BrE)
A humorous or euphemistic term used to describe someone who does not have an intact hymen, especially implying sexual experience or absence of virginity.
