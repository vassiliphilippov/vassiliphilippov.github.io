wormhole

noun
space-time tunnel
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrmˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈwɜːmˌhəʊl / (BrE)
a hypothetical tunnel in space or space-time that connects distant points, allowing travel between them (in science or science fiction)
