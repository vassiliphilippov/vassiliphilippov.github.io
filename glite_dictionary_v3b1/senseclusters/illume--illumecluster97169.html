illume

verb
to light up or illuminate
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / …™ÀàluÀêm / (AmE), / …™ÀàluÀêm / (BrE)
to light up; to illuminate; to introduce light into something

Examples

To illume a room
Candles were lit to illume the hall during the power outage.

The rising sun began to illume the misty landscape outside her window.
