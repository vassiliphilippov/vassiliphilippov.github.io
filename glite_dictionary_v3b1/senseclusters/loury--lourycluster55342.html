loury

adjective
gloomy or overcast
Pronunciation: / ˈlaʊəri / (AmE), / ˈlaʊəri / (BrE)
gloomy, dark, or overcast, especially used to describe weather or a somber mood

Examples

A loury sky
The loury sky made the whole afternoon feel somber and cold.
She looked out the window at the loury clouds gathering above the city.
His mood was as loury as the weather outside, silent and heavy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerful - noticeably happy and optimistic
  • synonym: gloomy - dark or poorly lit, especially in a way that creates a depressing atmosphere
