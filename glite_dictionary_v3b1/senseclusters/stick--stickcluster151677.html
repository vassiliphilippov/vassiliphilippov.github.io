stick

noun
marijuana cigarette (slang)
Pronunciation: / stɪk / (AmE), / stɪk / (BrE)
A slang term for a marijuana cigarette, made by rolling marijuana leaves in paper for smoking.

Examples

A stick of marijuana
He offered me a stick at the party, but I declined.
It was his first time trying a stick with his friends.
They rolled a stick and shared it during the concert.
