hair stroke

noun
cosmetic eyebrow tattoo technique
6.3
Pronunciation: /heər stroʊk/ (AmE), /heə strəʊk/ (BrE)
a cosmetic tattooing technique that creates very fine, hair-like marks to imitate natural eyebrow hairs, used to shape or fill brows.

Examples

Eyebrow hair stroke
The esthetician used hair stroke techniques today to sculpt natural-looking brows.
After microblading, she noticed several fine hair strokes blending perfectly with her real eyebrows.
Many salons advertise the hair stroke eyebrow service for clients wanting a subtle, long-lasting result.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eyebrow tattooing - the practice of tattooing pigment into the eyebrow area to enhance shape or fullness
  • synonym: microblading - a semi-permanent cosmetic technique using fine blades to make hair-like incisions and deposit pigment in the skin
