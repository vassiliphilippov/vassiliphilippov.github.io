blister pack

noun
packaging with plastic cover and cardboard backing
8.5 3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈblɪstɚ pæk / (AmE), / ˈblɪstə pæk / (BrE)
A type of packaging in which a product is sealed between a clear plastic cover and a cardboard backing.

Examples

A blister pack of tablets
