Rumex obtusifolius

noun
a species of broad-leaved dock plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈruː.mɛks əbˌtuː.səˈfoʊ.li.əs / (AmE), / ˈruː.mɛks ɒbˌtjuː.sɪˈfəʊ.li.əs / (BrE)
a European dock with broad, blunt-tipped leaves and bitter rootstock, common as a weed in North America, also known as broad-leaved dock
