Sarracenia flava

noun
yellow pitcher plant species of southeastern U.S.
Pronunciation: / sæɹ.əˈsiː.ni.ə ˈflæ.və / (AmE), / sar.əˈsiː.ni.ə ˈflɑː.və / (BrE)
A species of pitcher plant native to the southeastern United States, with erect yellow, trumpet-shaped pitchers featuring wide mouths and upright lids.
