vibrant cultural scene

noun
lively and dynamic artistic environment
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪbrənt ˈkʌltʃərəl siːn / (AmE), / ˈvaɪbrənt ˈkʌltʃərəl siːn / (BrE)
a lively and dynamic environment characterized by active artistic and cultural activities, such as music, art, theater, and festivals

Examples

A vibrant cultural scene
A vibrant cultural scene has developed in the old industrial district, with new galleries and music venues opening each month.
The city attracts visitors from around the world because of its vibrant cultural scene.
The mayor pledged to support artists and musicians in order to foster a truly vibrant cultural scene.

Related Terms

  • synonym: art scene - community and activities related to arts in a place
  • synonym: creative community - group of people engaged in creative and artistic work
