fringe

noun
decorative hanging border of threads or tassels
7.4 3.8
Pronunciation: /frɪndʒ/ (AmE), /frɪndʒ/ (BrE)
an ornamental border of threads, tassels, or other material, hanging loosely from the edge of something such as clothing, curtains, or rugs

Examples

A fringe on a scarf
His scarf was decorated with a woolen fringe at both ends.
She sewed a delicate fringe along the edge of the tablecloth to give it an elegant touch.
The old lampshade had a golden fringe that shimmered in the light.

  • synonym: trim - decorative material used on the edge of something, especially clothing
