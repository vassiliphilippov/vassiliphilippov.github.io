holed

adjective
containing holes
Pronunciation: / hoʊld / (AmE), / həʊld / (BrE)
having one or more holes in it, often as a result of damage or wear

Examples

Holed socks

The old fence was holed in several places, letting small animals through.
After years of use, the tent became holed and leaked during rainstorms.
