deathless

adjective
never dying; immortal
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛθləs / (AmE), / ˈdɛθləs / (BrE)
Never dying; lasting forever; immortal.

Examples

Deathless fame
His memory achieved a deathless fame in the hearts of his people.
Legends tell of a deathless spirit haunting the mountain.
The poet’s deathless words inspired generations.
