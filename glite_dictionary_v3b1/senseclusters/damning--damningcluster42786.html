damning

adjective
showing strong guilt
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdæmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdæmɪŋ / (BrE)
showing strong evidence that someone or something is very bad or wrong

Examples

Damning evidence
The report contained damning details about the company's illegal activities.
Her damning testimony convinced the jury of the defendant's guilt.
The documentary presented damning evidence that changed public opinion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exonerating - showing that someone is not guilty or is free from blame
  • synonym: incriminating - showing evidence of guilt or wrongdoing
