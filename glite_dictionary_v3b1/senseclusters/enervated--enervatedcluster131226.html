enervated

adjective
lacking energy or vitality
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɛnərˌveɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈɛnəˌveɪtɪd / (BrE)
lacking energy, strength, or vitality; weakened or drained of force.

Examples

Feeling enervated after exercise
After running the marathon, she felt completely enervated and could barely walk.
The hot, humid weather left us enervated and unwilling to move.
Weeks of sleepless nights had made him enervated and unfocused at work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exhausted - very tired; completely used up
  • antonym: invigorated - filled with energy or life; given vitality
