paleological

adjective
ancient times study
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪliəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌpeɪliəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study of ancient times or prehistoric periods

Examples

Paleological study
The museum's paleological collection includes fossils from the Jurassic period.
She published a paleological study on the origins of early human tools.
Experts used paleological methods to date the ancient cave paintings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: archaeological - relating to the study of human history through excavation
  • antonym: modern - relating to the present or recent times
