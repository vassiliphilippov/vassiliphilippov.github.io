accompanist

noun
software or device that provides musical backing

(in music technology) a program, device, or service that provides musical accompaniment for a performer, often using synthesized instruments or AI to follow tempo, harmony, and expression.

Examples

AI accompanist
The rehearsal app served as an AI accompanist, providing automatic accompaniment for the singer.
During practice he used a virtual accompanist that adjusted tempo to match his phrasing.
Several conservatory students relied on smartphone accompanists to rehearse unfamiliar songs between lessons.
