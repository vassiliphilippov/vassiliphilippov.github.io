potato worm

noun
hawkmoth larva feeding on potato/tomato plants
5.7
Pronunciation: / pəˈteɪɾoʊ wɜrm / (AmE), / pəˈtəʊtəʊ wɜːm / (BrE)
the large, green, white-striped larva of certain hawkmoths, especially one that feeds on potato and tomato plants

Examples

The potato worm larva
A single potato worm can destroy multiple leaves before being noticed.
Gardeners often struggle to control the damage done by potato worms each summer.
The farmer found a potato worm eating leaves in his tomato patch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tobacco hornworm - large green caterpillar that feeds on tobacco plants
