getter

noun
vacuum gas absorber
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɛtər / (AmE), / ˈɡɛtə / (BrE)
a substance or device placed inside a vacuum tube or system to absorb unwanted gases and maintain the vacuum

Examples

Vacuum tube getter
The getter inside the vacuum tube needed to be replaced after years of use.
A fresh getter helps ensure the vacuum system remains free of residual gases.
Technicians checked the getter to confirm it was still effectively absorbing unwanted gases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absorber - a material or device that takes in or soaks up another substance
