thanksgiving

noun
prayer of thanks before a meal
Pronunciation: / θæŋksˈɡɪvɪŋ /; / ˌθæŋksˈɡɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / θæŋksˈɡɪvɪŋ /; / ˌθæŋksˈɡɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
an act, prayer, or expression of thanks, especially to God or a higher power

Examples

A thanksgiving prayer
Before dinner, we all sat quietly while Sarah offered a thanksgiving for the food.
His grandfather led the thanksgiving, expressing gratitude for their gathering and the meal.
It is customary in their family to say a brief thanksgiving before every meal.
The family gathered for a special thanksgiving after the storm had passed.
She offered a quiet thanksgiving for her good health.
Many people attend church to give thanksgiving for their blessings.

  • synonym: gratitude - the feeling of being thankful
  • synonym: praise - an expression of approval or admiration
