seeder

noun
tool for removing seeds from fruit
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːdər / (AmE), / ˈsiːdə / (BrE)
A kitchen utensil used to remove seeds from fruit.

Examples

Fruit seeder
After slicing the apples, she reached for the seeder.
I used a seeder to prepare the cherries for the pie.
This handy seeder makes removing pomegranate seeds much easier.

Related Terms
