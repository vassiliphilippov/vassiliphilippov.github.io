paramedian

adjective
near the midline
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈmiːdiən / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈmiːdiən / (BrE)
situated near or on either side of the midline of the body

Examples

A paramedian incision
The surgeon made a paramedian incision to access the abdominal cavity safely.
A paramedian approach is often chosen to avoid major blood vessels near the midline.
The doctor noted swelling in the paramedian region of the patient's lower back.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: lateral - situated away from the midline of the body
  • degree higher: median - situated at the midline of the body
