convoy

noun
group escorting for protection
2.7 3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːn.vɔɪ/ (AmE), /ˈkɒn.vɔɪ/ (BrE)
the act or process of accompanying and protecting vehicles, ships, or people while they are in transit, especially to guard them from danger or attack

Examples

Military convoy escort
A military convoy guarded the trucks as they traveled through the conflict zone.
During wartime, merchant vessels often moved in convoy to avoid submarine attacks.
The ships sailed together in a convoy to ensure their safety.
