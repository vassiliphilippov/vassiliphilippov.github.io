follicular

adjective
relating to a follicle
8.2
Pronunciation: / fəˈlɪkjələr / (AmE), / fəˈlɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or consisting of a follicle, especially in biological or medical contexts.

Examples

Follicular cancer cells
Certain thyroid cancers are classified as follicular or papillary, depending on their appearance under a microscope.
The follicular phase is an important stage in the menstrual cycle.
The doctor noted several follicular structures on the patient's scalp.
