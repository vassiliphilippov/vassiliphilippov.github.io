trippingly

adverb
in a quick, light manner
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪpɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈtrɪpɪŋli / (BrE)
in a quick, light, and lively manner; with nimble or graceful movement.

Examples

To trip lightly
Birds moved trippingly along the garden path, searching for food.
He spoke trippingly, his words flowing smoothly and rapidly.
The actress danced trippingly across the stage, delighting the audience.
