red willow

noun
Eurasian willow with red or purple twigs
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛd ˈwɪloʊ / (AmE), / ˈrɛd ˈwɪləʊ / (BrE)
a type of Eurasian willow with reddish or purple twigs and bark rich in tannin

Examples
