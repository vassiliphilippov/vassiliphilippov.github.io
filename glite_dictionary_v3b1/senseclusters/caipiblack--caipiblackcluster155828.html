caipiblack

noun
cocktail with black vodka and lime
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkaɪpiˈblæk / (AmE), / ˌkaɪpɪˈblæk / (BrE)
A cocktail made as a variation of the Caipirinha, using black vodka instead of cachaça, typically mixed with lime and sugar.

Examples

A caipiblack cocktail
At the cocktail masterclass, we learned how to make a caipiblack with fresh limes and sugar.
He ordered a caipiblack at the trendy downtown bar.
The caipiblack is a creative twist on the traditional Caipirinha, swapping in black vodka.
