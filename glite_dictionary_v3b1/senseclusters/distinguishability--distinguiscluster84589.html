distinguishability

noun
ability to be distinguished
4.0 8.1
Pronunciation: / dɪˌstɪŋɡwɪʃəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / dɪˌstɪŋɡwɪʃəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
