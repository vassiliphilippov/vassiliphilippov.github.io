bierstube

noun
German beer tavern
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːrˌstuːbə / (AmE), / ˈbɪəˌʃtuːbə / (BrE)
a German-style bar or tavern where beer is served, often in a casual and social setting

Examples

A bierstube bar
We enjoyed traditional German food and drinks at the local bierstube.
The atmosphere in the bierstube was lively, with people singing and laughing together.
During Oktoberfest, every bierstube in the city is filled with visitors from around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beer hall - a large room or building where beer is served, often with communal tables
  • synonym: pub - a bar or tavern, especially in Britain, serving drinks and food
