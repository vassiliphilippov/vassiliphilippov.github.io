fieldsman

noun
cricket player in the fielding side
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːldzmən / (AmE), / ˈfiːldzmən / (BrE)
a member of a cricket team who is fielding rather than batting, especially a male player

Examples

A fieldsman in cricket
As a fieldsman, he was known for his agility and sharp reflexes.
The fieldsman at deep square leg caught the ball brilliantly.
The captain changed the position of the fieldsman to anticipate the batsman's shot.
