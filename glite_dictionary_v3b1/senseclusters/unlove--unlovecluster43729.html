unlove

noun
absence of love
9.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlʌv / (AmE), / ʌnˈlʌv / (BrE)
the absence or loss of love or affection, especially in a relationship; lack of love.

Examples

A feeling of unlove
A sense of unlove grew between the friends after their long disagreement.
The poem spoke of unlove as a cold shadow in the heart.
He could not explain the unlove that had replaced their once strong bond.

Related Terms

  • antonym: affection - a feeling of liking or love
  • synonym: indifference - lack of interest or concern
