chameleon

noun
changeable or inconstant person
Pronunciation: / kəˈmiliən / (AmE), / kəˈmiːliən / (BrE)
a person who changes their opinions, behavior, or appearance to suit different situations or people

Examples

A social chameleon
He's a real chameleon, always adapting to whoever he's talking to.
Her chameleon personality makes it hard to know where she truly stands.
In the corporate world, being a chameleon can help you fit in with different teams.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shape-shifter - a person or thing that frequently changes their form or identity
  • antonym: steady - a person who is firm and unchanging in character or behavior
