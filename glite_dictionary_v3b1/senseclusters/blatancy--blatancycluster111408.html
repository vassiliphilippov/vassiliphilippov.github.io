blatancy

noun
quality of being glaringly obvious and offensive
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbleɪ.tən.si / (AmE), / ˈbleɪ.tən.si / (BrE)
the quality of being conspicuously and offensively obvious

Examples

The blatancy of lies
Many critics were disturbed by the blatancy of product placement in the movie.
She was frustrated by the blatancy with which the rules were ignored.
The blatancy of his political ambitions surprised even his closest allies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blatant - very obvious and often offensive
  • antonym: subtlety - the quality of being delicate or not obvious
