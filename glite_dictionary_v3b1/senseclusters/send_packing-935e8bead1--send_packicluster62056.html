send packing

verb
dismiss or force to leave
Pronunciation: / sɛnd ˈpækɪŋ / (AmE), / sɛnd ˈpækɪŋ / (BrE)
to make someone leave suddenly and clearly show they are not wanted

If you disrespect the coach, he won’t hesitate to send you packing.
