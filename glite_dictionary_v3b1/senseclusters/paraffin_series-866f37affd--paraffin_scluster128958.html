paraffin series

noun
series of saturated hydrocarbons (alkanes)
6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈpær.ə.fɪn ˈsɪr.iːz/ (AmE), /ˈpær.ə.fɪn ˈsɪə.riːz/ (BrE)
