Pyrrhonian

adjective
extremely skeptical
Pronunciation: / pɪˈroʊniən / (AmE), / pɪˈrəʊniən / (BrE)
relating to the philosophy of Pyrrho, especially showing or advocating extreme skepticism about the possibility of certain knowledge

Examples

Pyrrhonian skepticism
The philosopher adopted a Pyrrhonian approach, questioning every claim presented to him.
Her Pyrrhonian attitude made her reluctant to accept any theory without strong evidence.
In debates, a Pyrrhonian thinker often challenges the very basis of knowledge itself.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dogmatic - strongly expressing opinions as if they were facts
  • synonym: skeptical - having doubts or questioning accepted beliefs
