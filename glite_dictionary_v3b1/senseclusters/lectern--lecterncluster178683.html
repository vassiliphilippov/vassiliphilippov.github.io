lectern

noun
sloped stand for holding texts during speeches

A tall stand with a sloping top on which a book, speech, or notes can be placed when reading aloud, lecturing, or giving a presentation.

Examples

A lectern stand
During the church service, the Bible rested on a wooden lectern.
He adjusted the height of the lectern so everyone could see his presentation.
The speaker placed her notes on the lectern before beginning her address.
