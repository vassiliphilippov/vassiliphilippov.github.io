keep the wolf from the door

verb
avoid extreme poverty or hunger
Pronunciation: / kip ðə wʊlf frəm ðə dɔɹ / (AmE), / kiːp ðə wʊlf frəm ðə dɔː / (BrE)
to manage to avoid hunger or extreme poverty, often by making just enough money or resources to survive

Examples

To keep the wolf from the door
