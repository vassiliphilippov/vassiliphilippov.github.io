steepled

adjective
hands shaped like steeple
7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈstiː.pəld/ (AmE), /ˈstiː.pəld/ (BrE)
with fingers or hands pressed together so that they form a shape like a church steeple, usually with fingertips touching and palms apart

Examples

Steepled fingers gesture
He sat quietly with his steepled fingers as he listened to the discussion.
Her steepled hands suggested she was deep in thought during the meeting.
The teacher looked at the class over her steepled fingertips, waiting for silence.
