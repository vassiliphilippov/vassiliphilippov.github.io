french-fry

verb
cook food by deep-frying in fat
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛntʃ ˌfraɪ / (AmE), / ˈfrɛntʃ ˌfraɪ / (BrE)
to cook food by immersing it in hot fat or oil, typically until it is crisp and golden brown

Examples

To french-fry potatoes
He accidentally burned his hand while french-frying onion rings.
Restaurants often french-fry large batches of potatoes every day.
She decided to french-fry the chicken pieces for a crispy texture.
