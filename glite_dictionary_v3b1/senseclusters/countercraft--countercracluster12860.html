countercraft

noun
opposing tactics
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntərˌkræft / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntəˌkrɑːft / (BrE)
the use of techniques or strategies to counteract an opponent's tactics or technology, especially in intelligence or security contexts

Examples

New countercraft techniques
The intelligence team developed new countercraft to stay ahead of enemy surveillance.
Advances in technology have made countercraft an essential part of modern security operations.
Training in countercraft helped the agents avoid detection during their mission.

Related Terms

  • synonym: countermeasure - an action or device designed to prevent or reduce the effect of an attack
  • synonym: defense - the act of protecting against attack or harm
  • antonym: offense - the act of attacking or taking the initiative against an opponent
