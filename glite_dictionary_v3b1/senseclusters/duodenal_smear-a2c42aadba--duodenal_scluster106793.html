duodenal smear

noun
sample taken from duodenum for analysis
7.4
Pronunciation: / duˈoʊdənəl smɪr / (AmE), / djuːˈiːdənəl smɪə / (BrE)
a laboratory sample of material taken from the duodenum, often used for microscopic examination or analysis

Examples

A duodenal smear sample
A duodenal smear can help diagnose infections in the upper part of the small intestine.
Microscopic analysis of a duodenal smear revealed the presence of Giardia.
The doctor ordered a duodenal smear to look for parasites.
