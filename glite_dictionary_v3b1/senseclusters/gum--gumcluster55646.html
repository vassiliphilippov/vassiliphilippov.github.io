gum

noun
tissue in the mouth around the teeth
3.7 1.0
Pronunciation: /gʌm/ (AmE), /gʌm/ (BrE)
the firm pink tissue in the mouth that surrounds the bases of the teeth and covers the jawbone

Examples

Bleeding gums
Healthy gums are usually pink and do not swell or bleed easily.
Her gums bled slightly during the dental check-up.
You should brush your teeth gently to avoid damaging your gums.
