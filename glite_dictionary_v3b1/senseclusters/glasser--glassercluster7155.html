glasser

noun
glass object maker
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæsər / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːsə / (BrE)
a person who makes glass objects by blowing, shaping, or casting molten glass

Examples

Skilled glasser crafts vases
The experienced glasser created beautiful vases and bowls in the workshop.
Visitors watched as the glasser skillfully shaped the glowing glass into a delicate sculpture.
Becoming a master glasser requires years of practice and patience.
