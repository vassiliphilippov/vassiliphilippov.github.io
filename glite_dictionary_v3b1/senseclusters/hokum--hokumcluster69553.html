hokum

noun
nonsense; meaningless talk or ideas
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊkəm / (AmE), / ˈhəʊkəm / (BrE)
nonsense or meaningless speech, writing, or ideas; something considered silly or without logical sense.

Examples

Pure hokum story
His story about being abducted by aliens is pure hokum.
Most experts agree that the conspiracy theory is nothing but hokum.
She dismissed the rumor as complete hokum.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drivel - silly or meaningless talk
  • synonym: nonsense - speech or writing that makes no sense or has no meaning
  • antonym: sense - something meaningful or coherent
