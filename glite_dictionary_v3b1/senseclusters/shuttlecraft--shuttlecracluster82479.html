shuttlecraft

noun
small space vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌt̬lˌkræft / (AmE), / ˈʃʌtlˌkrɑːft / (BrE)
a small vehicle used for short trips, especially to transport people or goods between spacecraft or stations

Examples

The shuttlecraft vehicle
The shuttlecraft carried the astronauts from the space station to the surface of the moon.
After the mission, the damaged shuttlecraft was carefully inspected by engineers.
Passengers waited patiently as the shuttlecraft prepared for departure to the main ship.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shuttle - a vehicle that travels regularly between two places
  • synonym: spacecraft - a vehicle designed for travel in outer space
