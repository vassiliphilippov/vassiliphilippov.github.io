troutlike

adjective
resembling a trout
Pronunciation: / ˈtraʊtˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈtraʊtˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having characteristics of a trout, especially in appearance or behavior

Examples

Troutlike fish
The scientist described the newly discovered species as troutlike due to its slender body and spotted skin.
Her painting captured the troutlike shimmer of the fish swimming in the clear stream.
Some salmon have a distinctly troutlike appearance, making them hard to tell apart.
