pad

noun
flat piece of soft material
3.8
Pronunciation: / pæd / (AmE), / pad / (BrE)
a flat piece of soft or absorbent material used for protection, comfort, or to hold ink

Examples

A pad for wounds
He placed a pad over the wound to stop the bleeding.
Knee pads are important when playing volleyball to prevent injury.
You should use a pad under the hot dish to protect the table.
I need a new pad because the old one has dried out.
She pressed the rubber stamp onto the pad before marking the envelope.
The ink from the pad transferred perfectly onto the paper.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cushion - a soft pillow or pad for sitting, kneeling, or resting
  • synonym: inkpad - an alternative term for a pad used with stamps
  • synonym: padding - soft material used to protect or stuff something
