rump

noun
fleshy hindquarters of an animal
3.5 0.59/m

Examples

Horse's rump

Synonyms

  • croup
    9.9 0.03/m
    croup is the top rear area of a horse above the hind legs, while rump names the same area on many animals.
  • hindquarters
    6.6 0.00/m
    hindquarters means the broad rear part including hips and thighs, while rump refers to the smaller upper rear area above the hind legs.
