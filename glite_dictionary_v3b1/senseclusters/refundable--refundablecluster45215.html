refundable

adjective
can be returned
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfʌndəbl / (AmE), / rɪˈfʌndəbl / (BrE)
able to be given back or returned, especially referring to money that can be repaid to the payer

Examples

Refundable ticket
The airline ticket is refundable if you cancel at least 24 hours before departure.
Please check if the application fee is refundable before you submit your payment.
Some products are not refundable once they have been opened or used.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nonrefundable - not able to be returned or repaid
  • synonym: returnable - able to be returned to a place or person
