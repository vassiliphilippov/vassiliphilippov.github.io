Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈfʌn.də.bəl/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈfʌn.də.bəl/ (BrE)

Examples

Please check if the application fee is refundable before you submit your payment.
Some products are not refundable once they have been opened or used.
