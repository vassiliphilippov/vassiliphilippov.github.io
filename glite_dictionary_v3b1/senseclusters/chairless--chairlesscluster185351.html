chairless

adjective
without chairs
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛərləs / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛːləs / (BrE)
without a chair or lacking chairs in a place or situation

Examples

Chairless room
The outdoor event was completely chairless, so everyone brought their own blankets.
After moving to the new apartment, the living room remained chairless for several weeks.
We had a chairless meeting and sat on the floor.
