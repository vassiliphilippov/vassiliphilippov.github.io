meticulously

adverb
with great attention to detail
1.4
Pronunciation: / məˈtɪkjələsli / (AmE), / məˈtɪkjʊləsli / (BrE)
In a careful and precise manner, paying great attention to detail.

Examples

To meticulously organize notes
He meticulously organized his notes before the meeting.
The artist meticulously painted every detail of the landscape.
The report was meticulously checked for errors before submission.
