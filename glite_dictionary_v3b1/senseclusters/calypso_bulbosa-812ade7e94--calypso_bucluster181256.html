Calypso bulbosa

noun
rare bog orchid with solitary pinkish flower
9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɪpsoʊ bʌlˈboʊsə / (AmE), / kəˈlɪpsəʊ bʌlˈbəʊsə / (BrE)
A rare orchid of north temperate bogs, with a solitary white to pink flower marked with purple at the tip, growing above a single basal leaf.
