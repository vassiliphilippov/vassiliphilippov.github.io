feel the burn

verb
experience muscle burning during exercise
Pronunciation: / fil ðə bɝn / (AmE), / fiːl ðə bɜːn / (BrE)
to experience a strong burning sensation in the muscles during or after intense physical exercise

Examples

To feel the burn in muscles
After climbing five flights of stairs, she could really feel the burn in her legs.
If you feel the burn during your workout, it means your muscles are working hard.
Trainers often encourage clients to push themselves until they feel the burn.
