Examples

Override a rule
Company policy will override any individual manager's decision.
Her desire to help others overrides her own personal interests.
Safety warnings should always override convenience in this laboratory.

Synonyms

    overrule is when a person or body in authority rejects a decision, while override means to cancel or be more important than a rule.
  • overturn
    5.6 8/m
    overturn is when a higher court or body officially cancels a law or judgment, while override means to overrule or take priority over a decision.
  • reverse
    4.9 8/m
    reverse is to officially change or cancel a previous decision, often in law, while override is to set aside or have higher authority over a rule.
  • quash
    8.1 0.7/m
    quash is to declare a legal decision or charge invalid, usually by a court, while override means to overrule or cancel a rule or order.
