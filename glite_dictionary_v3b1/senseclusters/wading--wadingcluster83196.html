wading

noun
walking in shallow water
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈweɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of walking with your feet in shallow water

Examples

Wading in water
After a long hike, we enjoyed some wading in the lake.
The children spent the afternoon wading near the shore, laughing and splashing.
They went wading in the stream to cool off their feet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wade - to walk through water or another substance that impedes movement
