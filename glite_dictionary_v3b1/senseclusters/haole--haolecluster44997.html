haole

adjective
non-Hawaiian origin
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊli / (AmE), / ˈhaʊli / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of people or culture not native to Hawaii, especially those of white or mainland origin

Examples

Haole customs
Her haole mannerisms stood out at the traditional Hawaiian celebration.
Locals sometimes refer to tourists as haole when they visit the islands.
He felt self-conscious about his haole accent while speaking with native Hawaiians.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foreign - from or characteristic of another country or place
  • antonym: native - originating from a particular place, especially Hawaii
