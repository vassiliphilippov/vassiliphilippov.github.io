fly in the face of

verb
openly contradict or oppose
7.9 6.1
Pronunciation: /flaɪ ɪn ðə ˈfeɪs əv/ /; / /flaɪ ɪn ðiː ˈfeɪs əv/ (AmE), /flaɪ ɪn ðə ˈfeɪs əv/ /; / /flaɪ ɪn ðə ˈfeɪs ɒv/ (BrE)
