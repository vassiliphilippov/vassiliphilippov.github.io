nonpathogenic

adjective
not disease-causing
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌpæθəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌpæθəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
not causing disease or not capable of causing disease

Examples

Nonpathogenic bacteria
Most bacteria found in fermented foods are nonpathogenic and safe for human consumption.
Scientists study nonpathogenic strains to understand how harmful bacteria evolve.
The laboratory confirmed that the sample contained only nonpathogenic microorganisms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harmless - not able or likely to cause harm
  • antonym: pathogenic - able to cause disease
