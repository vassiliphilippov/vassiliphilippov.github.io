religionism

noun
excessive religious zeal or piety
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlɪdʒənˌɪzəm / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒənɪzəm /; / rɪˈlɪdʒənɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
exaggerated or excessive religious zeal, piety, or devotion, often involving intolerance or extreme practices
