license number

noun
software activation or product identifier
2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈlaɪ.səns ˈnʌm.bər/ (AmE), /ˈlaɪ.səns ˈnʌm.bə/ /; / /ˈlaɪ.səns ˈnʌm.ə/ (BrE)
a unique alphanumeric code provided to a user or purchaser that authorizes or activates licensed software, a digital service, or a subscription.

Examples

Software license number
Please enter your license number from the confirmation email to activate the software on this computer.
The company stores customers' license number and purchase date to verify subscriptions during support calls.
After purchase, she received a license number and instructions for entering it into the app to unlock premium features.

Related Terms

  • synonym: license key - a code used to activate or unlock software; often synonymous with a software license number
  • synonym: product key - a sequence of characters provided with software used to register or activate the product
  • synonym: serial number - a unique code assigned to an item or product for identification and tracking
