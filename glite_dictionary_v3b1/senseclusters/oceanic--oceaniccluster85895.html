oceanic

adjective
of or pertaining to the open ocean
2.4 3.3
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊʃiˈænɪk /; / oʊˈʃæniɪk / (AmE), / ˌəʊsiˈænɪk / (BrE)
relating to, occurring in, or living in the open ocean or sea, especially far from land

Examples
