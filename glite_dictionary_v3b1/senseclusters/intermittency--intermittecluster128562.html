intermittency

noun
quality of being intermittent
6.2 7.4
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.ˈmɪt.ən.siː/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.ˈmɪt.ən.siː/ (BrE)
the quality or state of being intermittent; occurring at irregular intervals or with breaks in continuity

Examples

The intermittency of signals
Her migraines were characterized by their intermittency, coming and going without warning.
Researchers are investigating ways to deal with the intermittency of renewable energy sources like wind and solar.
The intermittency of the power supply caused frequent disruptions throughout the day.
