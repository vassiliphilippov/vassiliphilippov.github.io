Hamlet

proper noun
main character in Shakespeare's tragedy
Pronunciation: / ˈhæmlət / (AmE), / ˈhæmlɪt / (BrE)
the prince of Denmark and main character in Shakespeare's tragedy, noted for his indecision and desire to avenge his father's murder
