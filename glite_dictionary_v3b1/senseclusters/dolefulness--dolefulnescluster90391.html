dolefulness

noun
deep sadness or sorrow
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊl.fəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈdəʊl.fəl.nəs / (BrE)
A state of deep sadness or sorrow, especially caused by grief or affliction.

Examples

Deep dolefulness
Her face was marked by a dolefulness that lingered long after the funeral.
The novel captures the dolefulness of life in a war-torn country.
There was a certain dolefulness in his voice whenever he spoke of his childhood.

Related Terms

  • antonym: joy - a feeling of great happiness or pleasure
  • synonym: sadness - the state of being sad; emotional pain
  • synonym: sorrow - deep sadness, especially caused by loss or disappointment
