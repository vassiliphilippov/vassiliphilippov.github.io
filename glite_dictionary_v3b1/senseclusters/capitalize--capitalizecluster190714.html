capitalize

verb
treat expenditure as a capital asset (accounting)
2.2 5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkæp.ɪ.tə.laɪz/ /; / /ˈkæp.ə.tə.laɪz/ (AmE), /ˈkæp.ɪ.tə.laɪz/ /; / /ˈkæp.ə.tə.laɪz/ (BrE)
in business and accounting, to treat money spent as a capital asset rather than as an expense to be deducted immediately

Examples

To capitalize costs
Accountants must decide whether to capitalize or expense these expenditures based on established rules.
Certain development costs can be capitalized and depreciated over time according to accounting standards.
The company decided to capitalize the cost of new equipment instead of recording it as an expense.

Related Terms
