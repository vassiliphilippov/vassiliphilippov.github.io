laxly

adverb
in a lenient or careless way
Pronunciation: / ˈlæksli / (AmE), / ˈlæksli / (BrE)
In a permissively lenient or careless manner.

Examples

Follow laxly rules
If safety protocols are followed laxly, accidents are more likely to happen.
She supervised her team laxly, rarely checking on their progress.
The rules were enforced laxly, leading to widespread confusion among staff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leniently - in a permissively mild or merciful manner
  • antonym: strictly - in a precise, rigorously controlled manner
