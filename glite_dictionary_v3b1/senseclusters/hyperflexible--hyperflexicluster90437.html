hyperflexible

adjective
excessively bendy
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˈflɛksəbəl / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəˈflɛksəbəl / (BrE)
able to bend or move joints beyond the normal or typical range of motion

Examples

Hyperflexible joints
Some gymnasts are naturally hyperflexible and can perform impressive splits and backbends.
Her hyperflexible joints made her more prone to certain injuries during sports.
Doctors advised the hyperflexible child to be careful with physical activities.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: flexible - able to bend easily without breaking
  • antonym: stiff - difficult to bend or move
