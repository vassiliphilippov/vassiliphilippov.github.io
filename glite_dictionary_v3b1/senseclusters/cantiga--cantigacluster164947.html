cantiga

noun
medieval lyric poem
Pronunciation: / kænˈtiːɡə / (AmE), / kænˈtiːɡə / (BrE)
a traditional or folk song or poem, especially from Spanish or Portuguese culture; can also refer to a medieval Galician–Portuguese lyric poem

  • synonym: ode - a lyric poem expressing praise or emotion
