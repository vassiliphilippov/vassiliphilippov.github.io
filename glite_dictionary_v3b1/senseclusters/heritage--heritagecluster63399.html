Synonyms

  • inheritance
    3.2 4.46/m
    inheritance is the property or money someone receives after a person's death, while heritage names an estate or title passed by law.
  • patrimony
    8.2 0.75/m
    patrimony means family property or endowment passed through generations, often formal, while heritage is a legal estate or title passed to an heir.
  • legacy
    6.1 1.04/m
    legacy is a gift of personal property left in a will, while heritage usually refers to land, title, or estate passed to an heir.
