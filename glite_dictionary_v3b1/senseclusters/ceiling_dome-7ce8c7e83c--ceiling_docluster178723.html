ceiling dome

noun
ceiling-mounted domed light fixture
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːlɪŋ doʊm / (AmE), / ˈsiːlɪŋ dəʊm / (BrE)
A ceiling-mounted light fixture in which the bulb is concealed behind a translucent dome, usually made of glass, that diffuses the light.

Examples

A ceiling dome light
A ceiling dome is ideal for rooms with low ceilings where pendant lights are impractical.
The frosted glass ceiling dome gives off a soft, even glow throughout the hallway.
We replaced the old chandelier with a modern ceiling dome in the living room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flush mount light - a ceiling light fixture mounted directly to the ceiling without gaps
