hex

An evil spell or magic curse, usually believed to bring bad luck or misfortune.

Examples

A witch's hex
She believed her streak of bad luck was because of a hex cast by a jealous rival.
The old tale tells of a wicked hex that haunted the family for generations.
The villagers feared that someone had placed a hex on their crops after a poor harvest.
