poyou

noun
a South American armadillo species
Pronunciation: / poʊˈjuː / (AmE), / pəʊˈjuː / (BrE)
An Argentine armadillo with six movable bands and hairy underparts, known for its distinctive appearance.

Examples

The poyou armadillo
Scientists observed a poyou foraging for insects in its natural habitat.
The poyou is mainly found in the grasslands of northern Argentina.
The poyous are distinguished by their six movable bands and hairy bodies.
