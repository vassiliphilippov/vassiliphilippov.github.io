bitterweed

noun
European weed with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪtərˌwiːd / (AmE), / ˈbɪtəˌwiːd / (BrE)
a European weed with spiny tongue-shaped leaves and yellow flowers, now also found in the United States

Examples

Spiny bitterweed plant
Gardeners often struggle to remove bitterweed because of its spiny leaves.
Some livestock avoid grazing where bitterweed is abundant.
The field was covered in bitterweed after a wet spring.
