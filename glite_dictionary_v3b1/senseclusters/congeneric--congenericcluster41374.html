congeneric

noun
organism of the same genus
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑn.dʒəˈnɛr.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.dʒəˈnɛr.ɪk / (BrE)
An animal or plant belonging to the same genus as another organism.

Examples

A congeneric animal
Many congenerics share similar physical traits due to their common ancestry.
Researchers compared the behaviors of congenerics to understand evolutionary differences.
The two species are considered congenerics as they both belong to the same genus.
