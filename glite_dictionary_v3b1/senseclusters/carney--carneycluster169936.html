carney

noun
traveling carnival
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrni / (AmE), / ˈkɑːni / (BrE)
a traveling fair or carnival with rides, games, and attractions, often set up temporarily in a town

Examples

The carney comes to town
Every summer, the carney sets up in the park and draws huge crowds.
We spent the evening at the carney, riding the Ferris wheel and eating cotton candy.
The children were excited when the carney arrived with its colorful tents and flashing lights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fair - an event with amusements, rides, and games, often held annually
