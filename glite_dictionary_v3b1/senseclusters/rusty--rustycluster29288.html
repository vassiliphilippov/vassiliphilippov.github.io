rusty

adjective
brownish-red, like rust
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌsti / (AmE), / ˈrʌsti / (BrE)
having the color of rust; brownish-red like iron that has rusted

Examples

Rusty brown color
A rusty stain marked the edge of the metal fence.
She wore a rusty sweater that matched the autumn leaves perfectly.
The old bicycle was covered in a rusty hue from years in the rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reddish-brown - tinged with red and brown, similar to rust color
  • synonym: rust - reddish-brown substance formed on iron when it corrodes
