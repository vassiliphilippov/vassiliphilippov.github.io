unowned

adjective
having no owner
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈoʊnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈəʊnd / (BrE)
not owned or possessed by anyone; having no owner.

Examples

An unowned land
Much of the land in this area is unowned and open to the public.
The dog appeared to be unowned and wandered the streets searching for food.
The lost wallet remained unowned until someone returned it to the police.
