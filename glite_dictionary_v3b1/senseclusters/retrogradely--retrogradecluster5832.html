retrogradely

adverb
in a backward manner
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛtroʊˌɡreɪdli / (AmE), / ˈrɛtrəʊˌɡreɪdli / (BrE)
in a backward direction or manner, especially referring to movement opposite to the usual flow in medical or scientific contexts

Examples

Retrogradely move fluid
The contrast agent moved retrogradely through the patient's artery during the procedure.
In some cases, nerve signals can travel retrogradely instead of following the normal path.
The researchers observed the substance spreading retrogradely along the neural pathway.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anterogradely - in a forward direction, especially in medical contexts
  • synonym: backward - in the direction behind or opposite to the usual
