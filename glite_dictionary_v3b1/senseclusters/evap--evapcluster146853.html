evap

verb
to turn into vapor
9.2
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈvæp/ /; / /ˈɪ.væp/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈvæp/ /; / /ˈɪ.væp/ (BrE)
to change from a liquid into a vapor, or to disappear by vaporization; used informally as a short form of 'evaporate'.

Examples

To evap quickly
If you leave the solvent uncovered, most of it will evap within an hour.
The puddle began to evap quickly under the hot afternoon sun.
Let the water evap before adding the next ingredient to the mixture.
