resuscitation

noun
the act of reviving someone
5.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˌsʌsɪˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / rɪˌsʌsɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of reviving someone from unconsciousness or apparent death, often by medical means

Examples

Cardiac resuscitation
Advanced techniques in resuscitation have saved countless lives over the years.
Immediate resuscitation increased the likelihood of survival for the drowning victim.
The doctors performed resuscitation after the patient collapsed in the emergency room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: death - the end of life or consciousness
  • synonym: revival - the act of bringing something back to life or consciousness
