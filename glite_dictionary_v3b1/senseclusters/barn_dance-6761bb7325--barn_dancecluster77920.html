barn dance

noun
country dance party, usually in a barn
8.3
Pronunciation: / bɑrn dæns / (AmE), / bɑːn dɑːns / (BrE)
a social event, often in a barn or similar rural venue, featuring traditional country dancing to live or recorded music

Examples

A barn dance event
The barn dance was filled with laughter, music, and lively foot-stomping dances.
They hired a band to play at their wedding barn dance in the countryside.
We're going to a barn dance this Saturday night at the local community center.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hoedown - an informal country dance with folk music
