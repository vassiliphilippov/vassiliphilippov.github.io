poor little rich girl

noun
a wealthy but emotionally deprived young woman
Pronunciation: / pʊr ˈlɪt̬əl rɪtʃ ɡɝl / (AmE), / pɔː ˈlɪtəl rɪtʃ ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a wealthy young woman, often portrayed or perceived as emotionally neglected or unhappy despite her financial privilege

Examples

A poor little rich girl
At the party, she complained so much that she came across as a poor little rich girl.
The movie tells the story of a poor little rich girl searching for real friendship.
The tabloids painted her as a poor little rich girl whose money couldn't buy happiness.
