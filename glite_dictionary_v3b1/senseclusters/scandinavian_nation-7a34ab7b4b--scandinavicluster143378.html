Scandinavian nation

noun
a country in Scandinavia
Pronunciation: / ˌskændəˈneɪviən ˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌskandɪˈneɪvɪən ˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
a country that is part of Scandinavia, typically Denmark, Norway, or Sweden

The three main Scandinavian nations are Norway, Sweden, and Denmark.
