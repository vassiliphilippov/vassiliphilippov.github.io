Hawaiian thank you
Pronunciation: / məˈhɑːloʊ / (AmE), / məˈhɑːləʊ / (BrE)
a Hawaiian word used to express thanks or gratitude, sometimes used in English, especially in Hawaii

Examples

Say mahalo in Hawaii
After the meal, he turned to the chef and said mahalo with a warm smile.
Visitors to Hawaii often hear locals say mahalo when expressing appreciation.
She wrote mahalo at the end of her thank-you note to her host.
