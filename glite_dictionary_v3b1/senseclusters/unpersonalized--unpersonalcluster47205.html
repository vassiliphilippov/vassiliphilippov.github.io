unpersonalized

adjective
generic or anonymous
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnpɜːrsəˌnaɪzd /; / ˌʌnpɚˈsɒnəlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnpɜːsənəlaɪzd /; / ˌʌnpɜːˈsəʊnəlaɪzd / (BrE)
not made or adapted for a particular person; generic or not specific to an individual

Examples

Unpersonalized message
The company sent an unpersonalized email to all its customers about the policy update.
Researchers used unpersonalized data to ensure participants' privacy was protected.
Many advertisements are now personalized, but some websites still show only unpersonalized content.
The advertisement was unpersonalized and did not address any of my interests.
Many customers ignore unpersonalized emails because they feel less relevant.
She received an unpersonalized letter that could have been sent to anyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anonymized - with identifying information removed
  • synonym: generic - not specific to any individual; general
  • antonym: personalized - tailored to a specific individual
