write for the drawer

verb
to write without intending to publish
Pronunciation: / raɪt fər ðə drɔr / (AmE), / raɪt fə ðə drɔː / (BrE)
to write with no intention of publishing the work; to compose privately for oneself

Examples

To write for the drawer

He spent years writing for the drawer before sharing his poems with anyone.
Many authors write for the drawer when they want their creative freedom without outside pressure.
