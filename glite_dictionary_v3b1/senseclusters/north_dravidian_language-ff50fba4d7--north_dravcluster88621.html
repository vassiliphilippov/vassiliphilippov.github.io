North Dravidian language

noun
subgroup of Dravidian languages in eastern India
9.9
Pronunciation: / nɔrθ drəˈvɪdiən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / nɔːθ drəˈvɪdɪən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A subgroup of Dravidian languages spoken primarily in eastern India, including languages such as Brahui, Kurukh, and Malto.
