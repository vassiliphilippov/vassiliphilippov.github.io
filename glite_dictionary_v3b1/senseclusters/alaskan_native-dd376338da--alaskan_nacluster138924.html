Alaskan Native

noun
indigenous person from Alaska
Pronunciation: / əˈlæskən ˈneɪtɪv / (AmE), / əˈlɑːskən ˈneɪtɪv / (BrE)
a member or descendant of any of the indigenous peoples of Alaska

Examples

Alaskan Native people
As an Alaskan Native, she participates in traditional festivals held each year.
Many Alaskan Natives maintain a close connection to their ancestral lands.
The museum featured artwork created by Alaskan Natives to celebrate their rich cultural heritage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Native American - a member of any of the indigenous peoples of North, Central, or South America
