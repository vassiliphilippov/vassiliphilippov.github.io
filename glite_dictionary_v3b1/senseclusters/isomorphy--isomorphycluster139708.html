Examples

Biological isomorphy
Many marine animals display isomorphy in their skeletal structures across different species.
Some plant groups are notable for their isomorphy throughout the different generations in their life cycles.
The isomorphy between the adult and larval forms allows scientists to trace evolutionary relationships.
