roly-poly

adjective
short and plump; round in shape
Pronunciation: / ˌroʊli ˈpoʊli /; / ˈroʊli ˌpoʊli / (AmE), / ˌrəʊli ˈpəʊli /; / ˈrəʊli ˌpəʊli / (BrE)
short, round, and plump in shape (often used in a playful or affectionate way)

Examples

Roly-poly child
He was a cheerful, roly-poly man with a booming laugh.
She gave her roly-poly cat an affectionate squeeze before feeding it dinner.
The toddler looked especially roly-poly in his thick winter coat.
The roly-poly kitten chased its tail around the living room.
Children laughed at the roly-poly clown tumbling across the stage.
He gave the roly-poly puppy a gentle pat on its round belly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chubby - having a plump and rounded shape
  • antonym: slender - thin in an attractive or graceful way
  • antonym: thin - having little fat on the body
