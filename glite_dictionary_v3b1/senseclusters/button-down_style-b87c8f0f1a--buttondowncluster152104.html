button-down style

noun
shirt with a buttoned collar
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtən daʊn staɪl / (AmE), / ˈbʌt(ə)n daʊn staɪl / (BrE)
A style of shirt featuring a collar with buttons that fasten it to the shirt.

Examples

A button-down style shirt
For a business meeting, he wore a classic button-down style with a navy suit.
She prefers the button-down style because it keeps the collar neat throughout the day.
The button-down style has become a staple in many professional wardrobes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: button-down shirt - A shirt with a collar fastened by buttons
  • degree lower: polo shirt - A casual shirt with a collar and a few buttons, usually without collar buttons
