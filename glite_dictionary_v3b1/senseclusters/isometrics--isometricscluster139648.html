isometrics

noun
muscle-building exercises without movement
3.1 6.0
Pronunciation: /aɪ.sə.ˈmet.rɪks/ /; / /aɪ.soʊ.ˈmet.rɪks/ (AmE), /aɪ.sə.ˈmet.rɪks/ (BrE)
a system of exercises in which muscles are contracted against resistance without changing length, to build strength without movement

Examples

Isometrics exercises
He incorporated isometrics into his daily workout routine to increase his strength.
Physical therapists often recommend isometrics for patients recovering from injuries.
Unlike weight lifting, isometrics require the muscles to contract without any visible movement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dynamic exercise - exercise involving movement and muscle contraction
  • synonym: isometric exercise - exercise involving muscle contraction without joint movement
