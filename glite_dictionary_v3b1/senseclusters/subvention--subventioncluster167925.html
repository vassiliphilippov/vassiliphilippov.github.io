subvention

noun
grant of financial aid to an institution
9.3 9.3
Pronunciation: / səbˈvɛnʃən / (AmE), / səbˈvɛnʃən / (BrE)
an amount of money given by a government or other organization to help fund an activity or institution, especially in education or the arts

Examples

A government subvention
Thanks to the government subvention, the new art center was able to open its doors to the public.
The university received a substantial subvention from the state to support its research program.
Without a regular subvention, many educational institutions would struggle to maintain their operations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grant - a sum of money given by a government or organization for a particular purpose
  • synonym: subsidy - money given by a government to support an organization or business
