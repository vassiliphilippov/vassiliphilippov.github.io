sunporch

noun
enclosed glass room for sunlight

A room, usually attached to a house, that is enclosed mainly with glass to let in sunlight and provide an airy, bright space.

Examples

A sunporch room

Their sunporch is filled with lush plants and wicker chairs.
We drank tea every afternoon on the sunporch, enjoying the warmth even in winter.
