Norway maple

noun
large Eurasian maple tree with yellow autumn leaves
6.8 5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔr.weɪ ˈmeɪ.pəl / (AmE), / ˈnɔː.weɪ ˈmeɪ.pəl / (BrE)
a large deciduous tree native to Eurasia, naturalized in North America, with five-lobed leaves that turn yellow in autumn, widely cultivated for ornament

Examples

The Norway maple tree

Gardeners often choose the Norway maple for its hardiness and attractive foliage.
The Norway maple has become a common sight along city streets in North America.
