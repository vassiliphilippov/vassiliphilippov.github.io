drone

noun
remotely controlled pilotless aircraft
Pronunciation: / droʊn / (AmE), / drəʊn / (BrE)
an aircraft without a pilot that is operated by remote control, typically used for surveillance, delivery, or recreational purposes

Examples

A flying drone
Regulations now require drone operators to register their devices with the authorities.
She filmed breathtaking landscape videos with a camera-equipped drone.
The military deployed a drone to monitor the border area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unmanned aerial vehicle - another term for a remotely controlled pilotless aircraft
