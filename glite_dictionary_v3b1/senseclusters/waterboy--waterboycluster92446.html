waterboy

noun
person who waters animals
3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈwɔː.tər.bɔɪ/ /; / /ˈwɑː.tər.bɔɪ/ (AmE), /ˈwɔː.tə.bɔɪ/ (BrE)
a person, usually a boy, whose job is to provide water to animals or plants, especially on a farm or ranch

Examples

Farm waterboy
The young waterboy made sure every horse had enough to drink before breakfast.
As a waterboy, he spent his mornings carrying buckets to the garden and the stables.
During the hot summer, the waterboy was always busy keeping the animals hydrated.
