willpower

noun
self-control or determination
1.7 3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪl.paʊ.ər/ /; / /ˈwɪl.paʊr/ (AmE), /ˈwɪl.paʊ.ə/ (BrE)
the ability to control your actions, feelings, or desires, especially in difficult situations

Examples

Despite the obstacles, she used her willpower to finish the marathon.
It takes a lot of willpower to resist eating sweets every day.
