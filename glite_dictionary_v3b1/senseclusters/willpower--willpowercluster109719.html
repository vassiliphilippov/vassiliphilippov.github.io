Examples

Strong willpower
It took a lot of willpower to resist the temptation of eating the cake.
She relied on her willpower to stick to her new exercise routine.
Sometimes, willpower alone is not enough to change a bad habit.
Building strong willpower can help you achieve your long-term goals.

Synonyms

  • self-control
    3.3 4.82/m
    self-control is the steady ability to manage emotions and actions in the moment, while willpower is the inner push to resist temptation.
  • self-discipline
    4.4 1.53/m
    self-discipline means keeping to regular habits and rules to reach goals, while willpower is the moment's effort to resist urges.
  • self-command
    7.0 0.02/m
    self-command is calm, firm control of feelings and actions in public, while willpower is inner strength in private struggle.
  • restraint
    4.8 7.09/m
    restraint is holding back impulses or behavior in a situation, while willpower is the personal force to keep resisting over time.
