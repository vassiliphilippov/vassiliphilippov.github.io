leeringly

adverb
in a suggestive way
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪrɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈlɪə.rɪŋ.li / (BrE)
in a sly, unpleasant, or suggestive way, often making others feel uncomfortable

Examples

To look leeringly at someone
He glanced leeringly at the actress as she walked past the table.
The man at the bar watched her leeringly, making her feel uncomfortable.
She noticed her coworker looking leeringly at her during the meeting.
He smiled leeringly at his rival, making everyone uncomfortable in the room.
The group of teenagers glanced leeringly at the teacher as she walked by.
She was stared at leeringly by someone lurking in the shadows.

Related Terms

  • antonym: innocently - in a way that shows no improper or sexual intent
  • antonym: kindly - in a friendly or gentle manner
  • synonym: mockingly - in a way that makes fun of someone or something
  • synonym: sneeringly - in a way that shows contempt or scorn
  • synonym: suggestively - in a way that implies something improper or sexual
