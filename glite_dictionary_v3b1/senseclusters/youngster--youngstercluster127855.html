youngster

noun
child or young person
Pronunciation: / ˈjʌŋstər / (AmE), / ˈjʌŋstə / (BrE)
a child or young person, especially one who is not yet an adult

Examples

A youngster playing
A group of youngsters were playing soccer in the park all afternoon.
It's important to encourage youngsters to read from an early age.
The youngster quickly learned how to ride a bicycle without training wheels.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adult - a fully grown person
  • synonym: child - a young human being below the age of puberty
