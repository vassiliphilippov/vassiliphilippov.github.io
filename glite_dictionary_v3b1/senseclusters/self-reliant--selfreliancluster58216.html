self-reliant

adjective
independent; reliant on oneself
3.3 3.7
Pronunciation: /self.rɪ.ˈlaɪ.ənt/ /; / /self.riː.ˈlaɪ.ənt/ (AmE), /self.rɪ.ˈlaɪ.ənt/ /; / /self.riː.ˈlaɪ.ənt/ (BrE)
Able to rely on one's own abilities or resources rather than those of others; independent in action and judgment.

Examples

A self-reliant person
A self-reliant person doesn't wait for others to solve their problems.
Rural living can foster a self-reliant attitude in children.
She became increasingly self-reliant after moving to a new city alone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dependent - needing help or support from others; not self-sufficient
  • synonym: independent - not subject to control by others; self-governing
