concern

verb
to cause worry or occupy the mind
1.0
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɜrn / (AmE), / kənˈsɜːn / (BrE)
to worry or occupy the thoughts of; to cause someone to feel anxious or interested about something

Examples

The news concerns parents
Environmental issues concern many young people today.
It doesn't concern me if he arrives late for the meeting.
The growing deficit began to concern the finance committee members.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trouble - to cause distress or concern
  • synonym: worry - to feel anxious or troubled about actual or potential problems
