needly

adjective
needle-like quality
Pronunciation: / ˈniːdli / (AmE), / ˈniːdli / (BrE)
resembling or having the qualities of a needle, especially being thin, pointed, or needle-like

Examples

Needly leaves
The pine tree's needly leaves covered the forest floor.
She wore a scarf made from a soft, needly yarn that felt gentle on her skin.
The cactus had needly spines that made it difficult to touch.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blunt - not sharp or pointed
  • synonym: needle-shaped - having the shape of a needle; long and thin
  • synonym: spiky - having sharp points or spikes
