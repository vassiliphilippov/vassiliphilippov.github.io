venepuncture

noun
puncturing a vein
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈviː.nɪ.pʌŋk.tʃər/ /; / /ˈviː.nə.pʌŋk.tʃər/ (AmE), /ˈviː.nɪ.pʌŋk.tʃə/ /; / /ˈviː.nə.pʌŋk.tʃə/ (BrE)
the act of puncturing a vein with a needle, usually to draw blood or give an injection

Examples

A venepuncture procedure
