pachytene

noun
third stage of prophase in meiosis
Pronunciation: / ˈpækiˌtin / (AmE), / ˈpækɪtiːn / (BrE)
the third stage of the prophase of meiosis, during which homologous chromosomes are fully paired and crossing-over may occur

Examples

The pachytene stage
Crossing-over between chromatids takes place during the pachytene stage of meiosis.
Errors during pachytene can lead to genetic abnormalities in gametes.
Scientists observed that homologous chromosomes are most closely aligned in pachytene.
