calmly

adverb
in a calm manner
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmli / (AmE), / ˈkɑːmli / (BrE)
in a calm, peaceful, or untroubled way; without showing anger, nervousness, or agitation

Examples

Speak calmly
Despite the emergency, she answered the questions calmly and clearly.
He spoke calmly to the rioting students, hoping to restore order.
She calmly explained the situation, even though her heart was racing.
He handled the stressful situation calmly and professionally.
She spoke calmly despite the chaos around her.
The teacher asked the children to wait calmly in line.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nervously - in an anxious or agitated manner
  • synonym: peacefully - in a peaceful and quiet manner
