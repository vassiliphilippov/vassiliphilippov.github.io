holding

noun
an owned asset or property
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊldɪŋ / (BrE)
something, such as property, shares, or other assets, that is owned by an individual, company, or organization

Examples

Company holdings
Investors are advised to diversify their holdings to reduce risk.
She sold several of her holdings in the company to raise money for her startup.
The museum's art holdings include several rare paintings from the 18th century.

  • synonym: property - something owned by someone; a possession
