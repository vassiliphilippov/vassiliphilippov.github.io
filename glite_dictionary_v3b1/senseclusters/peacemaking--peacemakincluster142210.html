peacemaking

adjective
promoting peace
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːsˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpiːsˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
serving to promote, achieve, or maintain peace, especially by reducing or resolving conflict

Examples

Peacemaking efforts
The organization played a peacemaking role during the international negotiations.
Her peacemaking attitude helped resolve the argument between her friends.
A peacemaking approach is essential in situations of ongoing conflict.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conciliatory - intended to make peace or reduce hostility
  • antonym: hostile - unfriendly or antagonistic
