luminism

noun
American art movement emphasizing light in landscapes
6.7 9.4
Pronunciation: /ˈluː.mɪ.nɪ.zəm/ /; / /ˈluː.mə.nɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈluː.mɪ.nɪ.zəm/ /; / /ˈluː.mə.nɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
an American artistic movement of the mid-19th century, characterized by realistic landscape paintings that emphasize atmospheric light and aerial perspective
