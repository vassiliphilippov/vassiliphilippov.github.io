temporale

noun
movable church feasts
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛmpəˈreɪli / (AmE), / ˌtɛmpəˈrɑːleɪ / (BrE)
the part of the Christian liturgical calendar that includes movable feasts such as Easter and related celebrations

Examples

Temporale feasts
The choir prepared special hymns for the temporale celebrations during Easter.
Understanding the temporale helps clergy plan the church's annual schedule of services.
The temporale includes important feasts that change dates each year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sanctorale - the part of the church calendar for fixed saints' days
