ratifier

noun
one who approves formally
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈræt.ɪ.faɪ.ər/ /; / /ˈræt.ə.faɪ.ər/ (AmE), /ˈræt.ɪ.faɪ.ə/ /; / /ˈræt.ə.faɪ.ə/ (BrE)
a person, group, or thing that formally approves or confirms an agreement, law, or decision
