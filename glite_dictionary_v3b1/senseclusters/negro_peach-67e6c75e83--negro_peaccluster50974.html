negro peach

noun
African shrub with reddish warty fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈniːɡroʊ piːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈniːɡrəʊ piːtʃ / (BrE)
a stout, spreading or semi-climbing tropical African shrub with round, brownish-red, warty fruit

Examples
