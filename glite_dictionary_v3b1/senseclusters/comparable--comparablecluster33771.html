comparable

noun
similar item for comparison
5.1 0.14/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ëÀêmp…ôr…ôb…ôl / (AmE), / Ààk…ímp…ôr…ôb…ôl / (BrE)
a property, asset, or item that is similar to another and used as a basis for comparison, especially in real estate or finance

Examples

A real estate comparable
The real estate agent used several comparables to determine the value of the house.
Banks often require a list of comparables before approving a mortgage loan.
Appraisers look for comparables in the same neighborhood to estimate property prices.

Synonyms

  • comp
    7.8 0.39/m
    comp is an informal short word used for a similar property in pricing, while comparable is the standard term in real estate.
  • analog
    6.0 6.01/m
    analog is a general word for something similar across fields, while comparable refers to an item used as a direct basis for comparison.
  • parallel
    4.4 6.15/m
    parallel refers to a likeness or close match of form or idea, while comparable is an item used specifically for financial or real estate comparison.
