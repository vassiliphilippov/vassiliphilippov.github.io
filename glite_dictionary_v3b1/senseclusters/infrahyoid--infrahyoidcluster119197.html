infrahyoid

adjective
below the hyoid bone
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnfrəˈhaɪɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌɪnfrəˈhaɪɔɪd / (BrE)
situated below the hyoid bone, especially referring to certain muscles in the neck

Examples

Infrahyoid muscles
The infrahyoid muscles play a key role in swallowing and speech.
During the operation, the doctor identified the infrahyoid region to avoid damaging important structures.
An injury to the infrahyoid area can affect neck movement and function.
