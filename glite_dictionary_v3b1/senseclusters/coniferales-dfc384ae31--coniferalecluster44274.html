Coniferales

noun
order of evergreen trees and shrubs (pines, firs, etc.)
9.4
Pronunciation: / kəˌnɪfəˈreɪliːz / (AmE), / kəˌnɪfəˈreɪliːz /; / kɒˌnɪfəˈreɪliːz / (BrE)
