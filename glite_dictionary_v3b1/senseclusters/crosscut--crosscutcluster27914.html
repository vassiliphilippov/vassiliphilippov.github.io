crosscut

verb
to alternate scenes
6.8 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆkrÉ”sËŒkÊŒt / (AmE), / ËˆkrÉ’sËŒkÊŒt / (BrE)
to edit or arrange film, television, or narrative by alternating between two or more scenes to show simultaneous events or create contrast

Examples

To crosscut scenes
The director used crosscutting to build suspense by showing the hero and villain moving closer to each other.
In the final scene, the film editor was crosscutting between the concert and the storm outside.
By crosscutting the two storylines, the movie made the connection between the characters clear.

Synonyms

  • intercut
    7.7 1.13/m
    intercut inserts or alternates shots inside a sequence to mix elements, while crosscut alternates between separate scenes to show simultaneous action.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intercut - to insert scenes between other scenes in a film or narrative
  • synonym: splice - to join two pieces of film or tape together
