pulverized

adjective
in fine powder or tiny particles
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌlvəˌraɪzd / (AmE), / ˈpʌlvəraɪzd / (BrE)
consisting of or reduced to fine powder or tiny particles by crushing, grinding, or other means.

Examples

Pulverized rock
After the rock was pulverized, it was used to make cement.
The pulverized sugar dissolved quickly in the tea.
The recipe calls for pulverized almonds to add texture and flavor.
After hours of grinding, the ore became a pulverised powder.
Pulverised sugar dissolves faster than regular granulated sugar in beverages.
The chef added some pulverised garlic to the sauce for extra flavor.

Related Terms

  • antonym: granulated - formed into grains or granules, not finely powdered
  • synonym: powdered - reduced to powder or consisting of powder
