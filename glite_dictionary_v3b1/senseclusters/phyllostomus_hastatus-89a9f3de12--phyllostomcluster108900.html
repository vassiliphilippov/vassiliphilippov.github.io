Phyllostomus hastatus

noun
species of leaf-nosed bat
Pronunciation: / fɪˌlɒstəʊməs hæˈsteɪtəs / (AmE), / fɪˌlɒstəʊməs hæˈstatəs / (BrE)
A species of large leaf-nosed bat native to Central and South America, known for its complex social behavior.

Examples

Phyllostomus hastatus bat
Conservationists are concerned about habitat loss affecting populations of Phyllostomus hastatus in the Amazon rainforest.
Researchers observed a group of Phyllostomus hastatus roosting in a hollow tree in Panama.
The Phyllostomus hastatus is noted for its unusually sophisticated social structure among bats.
