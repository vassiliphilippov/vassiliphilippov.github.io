cassoulet

noun
French bean stew
Pronunciation: / ˌkæs.uˈleɪ / (AmE), / ˌkæs.uˈleɪ / (BrE)
a rich, slow-cooked French stew made with beans and various meats such as pork, sausage, and duck

Examples

French cassoulet stew
The chef prepared a traditional cassoulet using white beans and duck confit.
On our trip to France, we enjoyed a hearty cassoulet in a small village restaurant.
She spent hours simmering the cassoulet to bring out all the flavors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: casserole - a dish cooked slowly in an oven, often with meat and vegetables
  • synonym: stew - a dish of meat and vegetables cooked slowly in liquid
