forefather

noun
male ancestor or family founder
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌfɑðɚ /; / ˈfɔrˌfɑðər / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌfɑːðə / (BrE)
an ancestor or founder from an earlier generation, especially someone important in starting a family, nation, or tradition
