watershed moment

noun
critical turning point; major change
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔːtərˌʃɛd ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəʃɛd ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
An event or period that marks a significant turning point or important change.

Examples

A watershed moment
For many voters, the debate represented a watershed moment in the election campaign.
The discovery of penicillin was a watershed moment in the history of medicine.
Winning the championship was a watershed moment in her athletic career.

Related Terms

  • synonym: milestone - an important event that marks a significant stage
  • synonym: turning point - a time at which a decisive change occurs
