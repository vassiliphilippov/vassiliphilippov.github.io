get a new job

verb
obtain new employment
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt ə nu dʒɑb / (AmE), / ɡɛt ə njuː dʒɒb / (BrE)
to secure a position of employment different from one currently held

Examples

To get a new job quickly
After being laid off, he worked hard to get a new job in marketing.
Many graduates hope to get a new job soon after finishing university.
She wants to get a new job by the end of the month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: find a job - obtain employment, especially for the first time or after unemployment
