referentiality

noun
quality of referring
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛfərɛnʃiˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌrɛfərɛnʃɪˈælɪti / (BrE)
the quality of a word or expression that refers to or points to something else, such as an object, idea, or person

Examples

Referentiality of words
The referentiality of certain words helps readers understand what is being discussed.
In linguistics, referentiality is important for analyzing how language connects to the real world.
Pronouns rely on referentiality to make their meaning clear in a sentence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: denotation - the direct meaning or reference of a word
  • synonym: reference - the act of mentioning or alluding to something
