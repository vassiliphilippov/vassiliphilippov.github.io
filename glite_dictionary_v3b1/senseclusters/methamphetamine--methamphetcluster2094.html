methamphetamine

noun
potent stimulant drug; often abused
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛθ.æmˈfɛtəˌmin / (AmE), / ˌmɛθ.æmˈfɛtəˌmiːn / (BrE)
A powerful, highly addictive stimulant drug used medically to treat ADHD and obesity, but often abused illegally; commonly known as 'crystal meth' when abused.

Examples

Methamphetamine drug use
Doctors may prescribe methamphetamine for certain cases of ADHD, but its use is carefully controlled.
He became addicted to methamphetamine after being introduced to it by friends.
The police seized a large quantity of methamphetamine during the raid.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amphetamine - a stimulant drug related to methamphetamine, but less potent
  • synonym: crystal meth - street name for the illegally abused form of methamphetamine
