muddiness

noun
cloudy or unclear quality
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌd.i.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmʌd.i.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being cloudy, turbid, or not clear, particularly in liquids or sound.

Examples

Muddiness in water
The muddiness of his explanation left everyone more confused than before.
The muddiness of the river increased after the storm washed soil into the water.
There was a muddiness in the sound, making the speaker difficult to understand.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clarity - the quality of being clear
  • synonym: turbidity - the quality of being cloudy or opaque in a liquid
