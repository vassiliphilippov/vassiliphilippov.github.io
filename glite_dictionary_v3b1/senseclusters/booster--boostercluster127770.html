booster

noun
game power-up that temporarily boosts abilities
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːstər / (AmE), / ˈbuːstə / (BrE)
(in video games) an item or power-up that temporarily increases a player's abilities, score, experience gain, or other performance measures.

Examples

Game booster
During the final level she picked up a booster that doubled her damage for two minutes.
Players can buy temporary boosters from the in-game shop to increase experience gain.
After the patch, most boosters last only thirty seconds to prevent unfair advantages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buff - a temporary increase to a character's abilities or stats in a game
  • synonym: power-up - an item in a game that gives temporary abilities or bonuses to the player
