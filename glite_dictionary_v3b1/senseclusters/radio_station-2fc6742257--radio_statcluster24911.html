radio station

noun
station producing radio broadcasts
5.6 1.2
Pronunciation: /ˈreɪ.diː.oʊ ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ˈreɪ.dɪ.oʊ ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ˈreɪ.diː.əʊ ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ˈreɪ.dɪ.əʊ ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
an organization or place that produces and broadcasts audio programs, either over the radio (AM/FM) or online

Examples

Local radio station
Many communities rely on their local radio station for news and emergency updates.
She tunes in to her favorite radio station every morning during her commute.
The radio station hosted a live concert for local bands on Friday night.
The small online radio station gained listeners worldwide within weeks of launching.
She programs music playlists for a community radio station that streams live every Sunday afternoon.
Universities often run a student-run radio station streaming talks and shows to alumni online.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broadcasting station - facility transmitting radio or television programs
  • synonym: internet radio - audio content streamed over the internet rather than via traditional radio frequencies
  • antonym: television station - station for producing and transmitting television broadcasts
