engagement

noun
military conflict or battle
3.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
a fight or battle between opposing military forces during a war

Examples

A military engagement
After a brief engagement, both sides withdrew to their respective positions.
The general planned the engagement carefully to minimize casualties among his soldiers.
The troops prepared for an engagement that would determine control of the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: battle - a sustained fight between large organized armed forces
  • degree lower: skirmish - a minor or preliminary fight between small groups of troops
