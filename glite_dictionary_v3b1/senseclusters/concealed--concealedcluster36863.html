Synonyms

  • hidden
    1.1 7/m
    hidden is the everyday word for something kept out of sight, while concealed sounds more formal and careful.
  • out of sight
    3.4 0.8/m
    out of sight describes something not visible from view, while concealed suggests it is purposely kept hidden or covered.
  • covert
    6.8 7/m
    covert describes secret actions or plans kept from view, while concealed mainly means something physically hidden from sight.
  • secret
    2.0 40/m
    secret refers to information or actions kept from others, while concealed focuses on being physically kept out of sight.
