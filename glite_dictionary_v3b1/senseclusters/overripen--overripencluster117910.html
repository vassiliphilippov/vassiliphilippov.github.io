overripen

verb
to become too ripe
5.9
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ˈraɪ.pən/ /; / /ˈoʊ.vər.raɪ.pən/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.ˈraɪ.pən/ /; / /ˈəʊ.və.raɪ.pən/ (BrE)
to cause something, especially fruit or crops, to become too ripe or spoiled by ripening for too long

Examples

To overripen bananas
If you leave bananas in the sun, they will overripen and become mushy.
The peaches overripened on the tree because no one picked them in time.
Farmers must harvest crops before they overripen and lose their quality.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: rot - to decay or decompose due to natural processes
  • synonym: spoil - to become unfit for use or consumption
