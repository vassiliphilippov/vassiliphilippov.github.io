Synonyms

  • warrant
    4.6 18/m
    a warrant is a formal legal paper that lets officials act in court or police matters, while authorization is a general official permission or instruction.
  • permit
    2.1 17/m
    a permit is a specific paper that gives someone legal leave to do an activity, while authorization is a broader official permission or instruction.
  • clearance
    4.4 7.09/m
    a clearance is permission to enter or operate in restricted places after checks, while authorization is any official permission or instruction.
  • mandate
    5.3 10/m
    a mandate is an official order that tells people to act, while authorization is a document that gives permission or sometimes instruction.
  • official document
    3.3 0.57/m
    an official document states rights or agreements in formal writing, while authorization is a specific document that grants permission or gives orders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authorization - official permission or instruction; American spelling
  • synonym: licence - an official document giving you permission to do something
  • synonym: permit - an official document giving someone authorization to do something
  • antonym: prohibition - the act of forbidding something, especially by law
