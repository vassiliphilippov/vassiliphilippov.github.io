authorization

noun
official document giving permission
2.6 3.4
Pronunciation: /ɔː.θə.rə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɔː.θə.rə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
an official document that grants permission or gives an instruction or command

Examples

Travel authorization document
Please present your travel authorization at the checkpoint.
The committee issued a written authorization allowing the project to proceed.

Entry is forbidden unless you show a valid authorisation at the gate.

You will need an authorisation from your manager to access this area.
