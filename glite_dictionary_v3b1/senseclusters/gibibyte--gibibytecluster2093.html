gibibyte

noun
unit of digital information (1,073,741,824 bytes)
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪbiˌbaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡɪbɪbʌɪt / (BrE)
A unit of digital information equal to 1,073,741,824 bytes (2^30 bytes), or 1,024 mebibytes.

Examples

One gibibyte size
Most operating systems now report memory sizes in gibibytes instead of gigabytes.
One gibibyte is exactly 1,073,741,824 bytes.
The new hard drive has a capacity of two gibibytes.
