vicarage

noun
residence of a vicar or parson
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪkəɹɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈvɪkərɪdʒ / (BrE)
an official residence provided by a church for its vicar, parson, or rector

Examples

The vicarage garden
After the Sunday service, tea was often served in the vicarage garden.
The historic vicarage has been preserved as a local heritage site.
The new vicar moved into the old vicarage at the edge of the village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parsonage - official residence of a parson
  • synonym: rectory - official residence of a rector
