squint-eye

noun
person with strabismus
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɪnt aɪ / (AmE), / ˈskwɪnt aɪ / (BrE)
a person who has strabismus (a condition in which the eyes do not properly align with each other)

Examples

A squint-eye person
He was self-conscious about being called a squint-eye by other children at school.
Historically, people with strabismus were sometimes referred to as squint-eyes.
The doctor diagnosed the child as a squint-eye and recommended corrective surgery.

Related Terms
