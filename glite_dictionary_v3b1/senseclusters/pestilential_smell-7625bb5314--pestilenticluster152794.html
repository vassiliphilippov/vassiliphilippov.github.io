pestilential smell

noun
extremely foul, disease-like odor
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛstəˈlɛnʃəl smɛl / (AmE), / ˌpɛstɪˈlɛnʃl̩ smɛl / (BrE)
an extremely unpleasant and foul odor, especially one reminiscent of disease or decay

Examples

A pestilential smell lingers
After the flood, a pestilential smell lingered in the basement for weeks.
No one could stand the pestilential smell coming from the abandoned house.
The pestilential smell from the garbage filled the entire alleyway.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aroma - a pleasant smell
  • synonym: stench - a very unpleasant smell
