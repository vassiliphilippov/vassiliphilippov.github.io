crushable

adjective
easily compressed
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌʃəbəl / (AmE), / ˈkrʌʃəbəl / (BrE)
able to be crushed or easily compressed out of shape

Examples

Crushable suitcase
The suitcase is made of crushable fabric, making it easy to store in small spaces.
He prefers crushable hats because they can fit into his backpack without losing shape.
The packaging uses crushable materials to reduce shipping costs and save space.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compressible - able to be pressed or squeezed into a smaller space
  • antonym: rigid - not able to be bent or changed in shape easily
