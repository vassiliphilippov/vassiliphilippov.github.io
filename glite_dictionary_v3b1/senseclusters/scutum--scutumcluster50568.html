scutum

noun
bony plate near ear
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈskjuːtəm / (AmE), / ˈskjuːtəm / (BrE)
a small bony plate or ridge in the temporal bone next to the middle ear

Examples

Ear scutum
The surgeon identified the scutum while performing the delicate ear procedure.
Damage to the scutum can affect the structures of the middle ear.
A clear view of the scutum is important during certain types of ear surgery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: temporal bone - the bone forming part of the side of the skull and enclosing the middle and inner ear
