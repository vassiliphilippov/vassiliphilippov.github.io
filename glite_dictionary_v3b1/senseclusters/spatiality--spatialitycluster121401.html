spatiality

noun
the property of relating to space
Pronunciation: / ˌspeɪʃiˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌspeɪʃɪˈælɪti / (BrE)
the quality, condition, or property of being related to or occupying space

Examples

The spatiality of space
Artists frequently explore the concept of spatiality in their installations, manipulating the viewer's perception of space.
The spatiality of modern architecture often blurs the boundary between interior and exterior environments.
The spatiality of urban landscapes can influence how people navigate and experience cities.

Related Terms
