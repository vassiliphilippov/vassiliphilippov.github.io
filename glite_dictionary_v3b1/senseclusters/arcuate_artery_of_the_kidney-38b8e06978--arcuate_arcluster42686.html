arcuate artery of the kidney

noun
curved artery in the kidney
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrkjuˌeɪt ˈɑrtəri əv ðə ˈkɪdni / (AmE), / ˈɑːkjuˌeɪt ˈɑːtəri əv ðə ˈkɪdni / (BrE)
