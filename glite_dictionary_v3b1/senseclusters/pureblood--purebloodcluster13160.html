pureblood

noun
animal of unmixed lineage (esp. horses)
Pronunciation: / ˈpjʊrˌblʌd / (AmE), / ˈpjʊəˌblʌd / (BrE)
An animal, especially a horse, of unmixed or pure lineage; a pedigreed animal whose ancestry is of the same breed.

Examples

A champion pureblood
After years of careful selection, they produced a prize-winning pureblood that was highly sought after.
Many breeders keep detailed records to ensure the lineage of each pureblood animal.
The ranch specializes in breeding pureblood Arabian horses for international competitions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thoroughbred - a horse of pure breed, especially one of a breed developed for racing
