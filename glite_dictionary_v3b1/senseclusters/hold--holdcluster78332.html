hold

noun
power or influence over others
3.0
Pronunciation: / hoʊld / (AmE), / həʊld / (BrE)

Examples

A hold on someone

She has a strong hold over her team, motivating them to achieve results.
The old traditions still maintain a powerful hold on rural communities.
