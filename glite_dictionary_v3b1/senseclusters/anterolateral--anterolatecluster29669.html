anterolateral

adjective
front and side position
Pronunciation: / ˌæntəroʊˈlætərəl / (AmE), / ˌæntərəʊˈlætərəl / (BrE)
located toward the front and side of something, especially a part of the body

Examples

The anterolateral muscles
The physical therapist examined the anterolateral muscles of the patient's leg for signs of weakness.
Pain in the anterolateral region of the shoulder can indicate a rotator cuff injury.
The anterolateral approach is often used in hip replacement surgeries to minimize muscle damage.
