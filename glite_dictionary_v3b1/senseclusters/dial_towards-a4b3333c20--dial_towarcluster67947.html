dial towards

verb
shift focus toward a goal or approach
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪəl təˈwɔrdz / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəl təˈwɔːdz / (BrE)
to gradually shift focus or direction so as to aim at a particular goal, approach, or outcome

Examples

To dial towards success
As the project faces delays, they decided to dial towards efficiency improvements.
In light of new evidence, the committee will dial towards a revised set of recommendations.
Our marketing strategy needs to dial towards younger consumers to stay relevant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gravitate toward - move gradually towards a particular direction or idea
