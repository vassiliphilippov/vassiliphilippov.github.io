sequin

noun
small shiny decoration for clothes
3.2 3.10/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàsiÀê.kw…™n / (AmE), / ÀàsiÀê.kw…™n / (BrE)
A small, shiny, disc-shaped piece of material that is sewn onto clothing or accessories as decoration.

Examples

A sequin on dress
He sewed a single sequin onto each corner of the scarf for decoration.
Her dress was covered in golden sequins that glittered in the light.
The dazzling sequins made her outfit stand out at the party.

Synonyms

  • spangle
    7.2 0.19/m
    a spangle is a small flat shiny piece often sewn on costumes for sparkle, while a sequin is usually a round disc sewn onto clothing.
  • diamante
    8.0 0.01/m
    a diamante is a small crystal-like gem used for bright sparkle on clothing, while a sequin is a flat disc sewn as decoration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spangle - small sparkling object used to decorate clothing
