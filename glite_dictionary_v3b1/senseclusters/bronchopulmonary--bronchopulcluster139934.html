bronchopulmonary

adjective
of bronchi and lungs
Pronunciation: / ˌbrɑŋkoʊˈpʌlməˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˌbrɒŋkəʊˈpʌlmənəri / (BrE)
relating to both the bronchi and the lungs

Examples

bronchopulmonary infections

Doctors are researching new treatments for bronchopulmonary diseases in children.
A bronchopulmonary specialist examined the results of the lung function test.
