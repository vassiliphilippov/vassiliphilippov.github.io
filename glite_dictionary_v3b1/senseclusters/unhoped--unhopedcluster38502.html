unhoped

adjective
unexpected to the point of not being hoped for
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhoʊpt / (AmE), / ʌnˈhəʊpt / (BrE)
So unexpected that it was not even hoped for; beyond what could be imagined or anticipated.

Examples

An unhoped invitation
She received an unhoped invitation to address the conference.
The explorers regarded the oasis as an unhoped blessing in the desert.
Their discovery was so unhoped that it took everyone by surprise.
