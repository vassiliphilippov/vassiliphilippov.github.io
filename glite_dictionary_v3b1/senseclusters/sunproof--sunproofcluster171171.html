sunproof

adjective
resistant to sunlight
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌpruːf / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌpruːf / (BrE)
not affected, damaged, or changed by exposure to sunlight

Examples

Sunproof cover
She bought a sunproof cover for her patio furniture to protect it during the summer.
The new curtains are made from sunproof fabric that will not fade over time.
He wore a sunproof hat to shield his head from the strong midday sun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sun-resistant - able to withstand exposure to sunlight without damage
  • antonym: sun-sensitive - easily affected or damaged by sunlight
