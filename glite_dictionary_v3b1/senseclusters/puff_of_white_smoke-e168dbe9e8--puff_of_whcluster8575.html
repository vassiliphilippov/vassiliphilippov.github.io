puff of white smoke

noun
a brief burst of white smoke
5.5 3.2
Pronunciation: / pʌf ʌv waɪt smoʊk / (AmE), / pʌf ɒv waɪt sməʊk / (BrE)
a small, brief burst of white smoke, typically seen coming from a chimney, engine, or similar source
