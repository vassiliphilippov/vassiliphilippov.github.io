tease

noun
person who mocks or provokes playfully
2.1 3.0
Pronunciation: /tiːz/ (AmE), /tiːz/ (BrE)
a person who habitually mocks or annoys others, or who provokes curiosity or desire, often playfully

Examples

He was such a tease, dropping hints about the surprise but never actually revealing it.
