thumb a ride

verb
to solicit a ride by hitchhiking
Pronunciation: / θʌm ə ɹaɪd / (AmE), / θʌm ə ɹaɪd / (BrE)
to solicit a free ride in a passing vehicle by signaling with one's thumb, usually while standing by the roadside

Examples

To thumb a ride roadside
After his car broke down, he decided to thumb a ride to the nearest town.
Tourists often thumb a ride when exploring the countryside with little money.
We watched as she tried to thumb a ride along the highway during the rainstorm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hitchhike - to travel by getting free rides in passing vehicles
