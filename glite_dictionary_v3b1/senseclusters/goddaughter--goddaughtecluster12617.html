goddaughter

noun
female godchild
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːdˌdɔtər / (AmE), / ˈɡɒdˌdɔːtə / (BrE)
A female child for whom a person has undertaken responsibility at her baptism, usually in a Christian tradition.

Examples

My goddaughter sent
My goddaughter sent me a beautiful card for my birthday.
She became the proud goddaughter of the church's minister last Sunday.
Whenever my goddaughter visits, we bake cookies together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: godchild - a child for whom one becomes sponsor at baptism
  • antonym: godson - a male child for whom a person has undertaken responsibility at baptism
