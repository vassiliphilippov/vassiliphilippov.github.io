unrepented

adjective
without regret
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈpɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈpɛntɪd / (BrE)
not feeling or showing regret or remorse for wrongdoing or mistakes

Examples

An unrepented criminal
Despite the evidence against him, he remained unrepented throughout the trial.
Her unrepented attitude shocked everyone at the meeting.
He left the room unrepented, refusing to admit any fault.

Related Terms

  • synonym: remorseless - not feeling guilt or regret for wrongdoing
  • antonym: repentant - feeling or showing regret for wrongdoing
  • synonym: unapologetic - not showing regret or remorse for actions
