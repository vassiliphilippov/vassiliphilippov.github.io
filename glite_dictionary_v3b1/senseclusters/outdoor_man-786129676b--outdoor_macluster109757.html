outdoor man

noun
man who enjoys outdoor activities
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌdɔr mæn / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌdɔː mæn / (BrE)
a man who enjoys or prefers outdoor activities such as hiking, fishing, or camping

Examples

An outdoor man hiking

You can always spot an outdoor man by his rugged boots and practical clothing.
