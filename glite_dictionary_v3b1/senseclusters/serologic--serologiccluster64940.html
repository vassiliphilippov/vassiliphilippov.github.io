serologic

adjective
relating to serology or blood serum tests
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛrəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɪərəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
Relating to serology, the scientific study or diagnostic examination of blood serum.

Examples

A serologic test
A serologic test can detect the presence of antibodies in a patient's blood.
Doctors ordered serologic studies to confirm the diagnosis of the infection.
The laboratory specializes in serologic analysis for autoimmune diseases.
