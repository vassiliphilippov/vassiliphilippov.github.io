magnifique

interjection
exclamation of admiration
Pronunciation: / mæɡnɪˈfik / (AmE), / maɡˈniːfiːk / (BrE)
used as an exclamation to express strong approval, admiration, or delight, similar to 'magnificent!'

Examples

Magnifique! after a show
After tasting the chef's special dessert, she exclaimed, 'magnifique!' with a big smile.
The final scene of the play was so moving that the audience shouted 'magnifique!' in unison.
He looked at the completed painting and said, 'magnifique!' to express his admiration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bravo - an exclamation used to show approval or praise
  • synonym: wonderful - an exclamation expressing delight or admiration
