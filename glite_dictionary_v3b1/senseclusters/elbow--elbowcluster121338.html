elbow

noun
a bent pipe fitting
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɛlˌboʊ / (AmE), / ˈɛl.bəʊ / (BrE)
a sharp bend or angle, especially in a pipe, road, or river; or a pipe fitting with a sharp bend

Examples

After the elbow in the road, you'll see the cottage on your left.
Engineers planned a elbow in the pipeline to avoid the rocky area.
The river makes a sharp elbow just past the old bridge.
