apocalypticism

noun
belief in world’s end
Pronunciation: / əˌpɑːkəˈlɪptɪˌsɪzəm /; / əˌpɑːkəˈlɪptɪsɪzəm / (AmE), / əˌpɒkəˈlɪptɪˌsɪzəm /; / əˌpɒkəˈlɪptɪsɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief or expectation that the end of the world or a major catastrophe is coming soon
