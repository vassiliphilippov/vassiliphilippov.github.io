dowitcher

noun
a shorebird resembling a snipe
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊˌɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈdaʊɪtʃə / (BrE)
a shorebird of the sandpiper family, resembling a snipe, found in North America and Asia

Examples

A dowitcher bird
A dowitcher uses its long bill to probe mudflats for food.
Birdwatchers were excited to spot a rare dowitcher along the marshy shoreline.
Several dowitchers migrated through the reserve during the spring.
