Examples

  • unwellness
    8.1 0.01/m
    unwellness is a simple term for feeling unwell, while unhealthiness implies a more serious or lasting impairment.
  • sickliness
    7.4 0.01/m
    sickliness means a tendency to be often ill over time, while unhealthiness refers to a current state of poor health.
  • unhealthfulness
    7.7 0.00/m
    unhealthfulness describes something that causes poor health, while unhealthiness names the person's own unhealthy state.
  • insalubrity
    9.9 0.01/m
    insalubrity is a formal word for unsanitary conditions that harm health, while unhealthiness is a neutral state of poor health.
  • insalubriousness
    9.9 0.00/m
    insalubriousness refers to places or conditions that harm health, while unhealthiness refers to a person's poor physical health.
  • unwholesomeness
    8.1 0.02/m
