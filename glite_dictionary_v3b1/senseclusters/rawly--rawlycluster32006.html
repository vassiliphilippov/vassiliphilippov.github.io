rawly

adverb
painfully or sorely
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔli / (AmE), / ˈrɔːli / (BrE)
in a way that is physically sore, irritated, or exposed, often causing pain or discomfort

Examples

Skin rawly exposed
After hiking all day, his feet were rawly blistered from the tight boots.
The cold wind blew rawly against her exposed skin as she walked home.
He spoke rawly about his recent struggles, not hiding his pain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gently - in a mild or soft way
  • synonym: painfully - in a way that causes pain
