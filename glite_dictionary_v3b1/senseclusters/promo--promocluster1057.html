promo

noun
discount code or voucher
5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈproʊ.moʊ/ (AmE), /ˈprəʊ.məʊ/ (BrE)
Informal. A promotional code, voucher, or offer that gives a discount or other benefit when used at checkout.

Examples

Promo code
She entered the promo at checkout and the total dropped by twenty percent instantly.
Many websites display promos on the homepage that you can copy and paste into the cart.
If you sign up for the newsletter, they will send a promo for free shipping on your first order.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coupon - a voucher or code that gives the holder a discount or other promotional benefit
  • synonym: discount code - a sequence of letters or numbers entered at checkout to receive a price reduction
  • synonym: voucher - a document, code, or electronic token that can be exchanged for a discount or free item
