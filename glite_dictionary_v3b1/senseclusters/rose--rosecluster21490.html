rose

adjective
pale purplish-pink color
Pronunciation: / roʊz / (AmE), / rəʊz / (BrE)
having a pale purplish-pink color resembling that of a rose flower

Examples

Rose-colored shirt
He bought a rose-colored shirt for the summer party.
The curtains in her bedroom were a soft rose that brightened the space.
The sky turned a gentle rose at sunset after a long, hot day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pink - a pale red color
  • degree higher: red - the color at the end of the visible spectrum, next to orange
