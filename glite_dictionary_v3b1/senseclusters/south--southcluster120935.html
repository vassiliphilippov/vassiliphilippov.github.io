south

noun
southern area of a place
Pronunciation: / saʊθ / (AmE), / saʊθ / (BrE)
the southern part of a country, region, or city

Examples

The south of France
Many farms are located in the south of France, where the climate is warmer.
She moved to the south of the city to be closer to her family.
There are distinct cultural traditions in the south compared to the north.
