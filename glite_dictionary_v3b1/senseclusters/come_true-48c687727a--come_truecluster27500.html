come true

verb
to become reality; be fulfilled
1.0 10/m
Pronunciation: / k åm tru / (AmE), / k åm truÀê / (BrE)
to happen in reality or become actual, especially what has been wished for or imagined

Examples

Dreams come true
Her childhood dream to be a doctor finally came true after years of study.
Many people work hard to make their wishes come true.
Sometimes, things you never expect can suddenly come true.

Synonyms

  • materialize
    5.8 6.75/m
    materialize often means to appear or become visible in a real form, while come true refers to wishes or hopes becoming real.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fail - to not happen or be realized (used as an antonym in this context)
  • synonym: materialize - to become real or happen as planned or hoped for
