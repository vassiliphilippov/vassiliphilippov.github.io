prevailing

adjective
most frequent or widespread
6.4
Pronunciation: / prɪˈveɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / prɪˈveɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
Most frequent or widespread at a particular time or in a particular place.

Examples

The prevailing winds
In this region, the prevailing winds come from the west.
She adapted to the prevailing customs of her new country.
The prevailing opinion among experts is that climate change is accelerating.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dominant - most important, powerful, or influential
