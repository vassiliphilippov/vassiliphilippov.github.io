Eucalyptus rostrata

noun
Australian red gum tree with durable wood
Pronunciation: / juːˈkʌlɪptəs rɒˈstreɪtə / (AmE), / juːˈkælɪptəs rɒˈstrɑːtə / (BrE)
a species of somewhat crooked red gum tree, native to Australia, chiefly growing along rivers and valued for its durable reddish wood used in heavy construction
