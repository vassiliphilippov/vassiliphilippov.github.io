druther

noun
personal preference
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdrʌðər / (AmE), / ˈdrʌðə / (BrE)
a preference or choice, usually used in the plural to mean what someone would prefer to do

Examples

My druthers in life
If I had my druthers, I would travel the world every year.
Given his druthers, he would always choose pizza for dinner.
She said, 'My druthers are to work from home whenever possible.'

Related Terms

  • synonym: choice - the act of selecting among options
  • synonym: preference - a greater liking for one alternative over another
