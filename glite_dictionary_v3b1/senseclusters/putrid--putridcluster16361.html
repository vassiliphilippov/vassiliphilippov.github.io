putrid

adjective
decaying with a foul odor
5.9 5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈpjuː.trɪd/ /; / /ˈpjuː.trəd/ (AmE), /ˈpjuː.trɪd/ /; / /ˈpjuː.trəd/ (BrE)

Examples

Putrid smell
