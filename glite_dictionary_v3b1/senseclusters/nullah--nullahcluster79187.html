nullah

noun
ravine or gully in southern Asia
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌlə / (AmE), / ˈnʌlə / (BrE)
a steep-sided ravine or gully, especially in southern Asia, usually dry except during the rainy season

Examples

A nullah in monsoon
During the monsoon, the usually dry nullah flooded and cut off the village from the road.
Leopards are sometimes spotted hiding in the deep nullahs near the forest's edge.
The hikers followed the narrow nullah to find a cooler spot during the heat of the day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gully - a narrow channel formed by the action of water
  • synonym: ravine - a deep, narrow gorge with steep sides
