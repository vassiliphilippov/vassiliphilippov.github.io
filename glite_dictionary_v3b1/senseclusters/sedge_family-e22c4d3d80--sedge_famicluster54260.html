sedge family

noun
the plant family Cyperaceae
5.2
Pronunciation: / sɛdʒ ˈfæməli / (AmE), / sɛdʒ ˈfamɪli / (BrE)
a large family of grass-like flowering plants, Cyperaceae, including bulrushes, chufas, cotton grasses, papyrus, and umbrella plants
