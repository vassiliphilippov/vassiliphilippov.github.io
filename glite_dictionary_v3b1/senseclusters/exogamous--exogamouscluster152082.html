exogamous

adjective
pertaining to union with outsiders
Pronunciation: / ɛkˈsɑɡəməs /; / ɛkˈsɑː.ɡə.məs / (AmE), / ɛkˈsɒɡəməs /; / ɛkˈsɒ.ɡə.məs / (BrE)
relating to marriage, union, or fertilization with someone or something from outside one's own group (such as a family, clan, tribe, or species)
