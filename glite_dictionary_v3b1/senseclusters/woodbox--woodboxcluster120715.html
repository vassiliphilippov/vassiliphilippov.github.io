woodbox

noun
wooden storage box
3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈwʊd.bɑːks/ (AmE), /ˈwʊd.bɒks/ (BrE)
a box made of wood, especially one used for storing or carrying things such as firewood

Examples

A woodbox for firewood
She packed fragile ornaments into the old woodbox before moving to the new apartment.
The tools were neatly arranged in a sturdy woodbox in the garage.
He found an antique woodbox at the flea market and used it as a coffee table.
He stacked logs neatly in the woodbox beside the fireplace.
The old cabin had a large woodbox to keep firewood dry during winter.
Before starting the fire, she checked if the woodbox was full.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cardboard box - a box made of cardboard used for storage or shipping
  • synonym: crate - a large container, often made of wood, used for transporting or storing things
  • synonym: firewood - wood used as fuel for a fire
  • synonym: log basket - a basket for holding logs or firewood
