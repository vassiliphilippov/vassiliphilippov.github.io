reactivity

noun
responsiveness to stimuli or change
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌriːækˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˌriːækˈtɪvɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being responsive to stimulation or change, especially to a stimulus or external influence

Examples

High reactivity to change
Chemical reactivity can vary widely between different substances.
His high reactivity to loud noises made busy environments difficult for him.
The doctor assessed the patient's reactivity by testing her reflexes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: insensitivity - lack of responsiveness to stimuli
  • synonym: responsiveness - the quality of reacting quickly and positively
