forward

moving toward the front or ahead

Synonyms

  • ahead
    1.0 91/m
    ahead is commonly used to mean being in front or moving forward in space or time, while forward names general direction toward the front.
  • frontward
    4.7 0.00/m
    frontward sounds formal and is rare in normal speech, while forward is the common word for moving or facing toward the front.
