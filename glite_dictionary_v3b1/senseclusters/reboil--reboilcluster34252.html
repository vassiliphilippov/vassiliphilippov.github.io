reboil

verb
to boil again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈbɔɪl / (AmE), / ˌriːˈbɔɪl / (BrE)
to boil something again after it has already been boiled

Examples

To reboil water
If the tea has gone cold, you can reboil the water before making a new cup.
She decided to reboil the pasta to make sure it was hot enough for dinner.
You should not reboil eggs, as it can affect their texture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boil - to heat a liquid until it bubbles and turns to vapor
