speculatively

adverb
in a speculative manner
Pronunciation: / spɛk.jə.lə.tɪv.li / (AmE), / spɛk.jʊ.lə.tɪv.li / (BrE)
In a way that involves guessing, considering possibilities, or forming theories without firm evidence.

Examples

To think speculatively
He stroked his chin speculatively before responding to the proposal.
She glanced at the old map speculatively, wondering if any treasures might still be hidden there.
The investors watched the stock’s sudden jump speculatively, considering whether to buy in or wait.

Related Terms

  • antonym: definitively - in a way that is clear and final
  • synonym: tentatively - in a hesitant or uncertain manner
