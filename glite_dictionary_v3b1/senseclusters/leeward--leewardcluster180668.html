leeward

adjective, adverb
away from the wind
7.9 5.2
Pronunciation: /ˈliː.wərd/ /; / /ˈluː.ərd/ (AmE), /ˈliː.wəd/ /; / /ˈljuː.əd/ (BrE)
on or toward the side or direction away from the wind; sheltered from the wind

Examples
