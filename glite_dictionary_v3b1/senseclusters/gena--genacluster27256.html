gena

noun
insect head part
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnə / (BrE)
the side part of an insect's head located below the eyes

Examples

Insect gena hairs
The gena of the beetle is covered in fine hairs.
Scientists examined the gena to identify differences between insect species.
Damage to the gena can affect how an insect moves its head.
