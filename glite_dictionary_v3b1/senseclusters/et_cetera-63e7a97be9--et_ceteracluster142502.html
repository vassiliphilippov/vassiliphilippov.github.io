et cetera

x
and so on
3.3
Pronunciation: / ɛt ˈsɛtərə / (AmE), / ɛt ˈsɛtərə / (BrE)
used to indicate that there are more items in a list that are similar to those already mentioned

Examples

Et cetera in lists
The recipe calls for flour, sugar, eggs, et cetera.
He packed his bag with clothes, toiletries, et cetera for the trip.
We discussed the budget, the schedule, et cetera at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: and so forth - used to indicate more similar items follow
  • synonym: and so on - used to show that a list continues in a similar way
