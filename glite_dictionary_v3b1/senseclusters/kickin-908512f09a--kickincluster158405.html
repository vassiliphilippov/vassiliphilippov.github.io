kickin'

verb
to strike with foot
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪkɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɪkɪn / (BrE)
informal spelling of 'kicking', meaning to strike or propel something with the foot.

Examples

Kickin' the ball
He was kickin' the ball towards the goal with surprising force.
They spent the afternoon kickin' around in the park with friends.
She kept kickin' the door until someone finally answered.
