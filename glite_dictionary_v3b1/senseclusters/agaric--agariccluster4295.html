agaric

noun
type of fungus used for tinder or punk
7.0 9.6
Pronunciation: / əˈɡærɪk / (AmE), / əˈɡarɪk / (BrE)
a type of mushroom or fungus with a cap and gills on the underside (order Agaricales), sometimes used in traditional fire-starting

Examples

A agaric mushroom
Collectors sometimes dried agaric for use in traditional fire-starting methods.
In the past, agaric was commonly used to prepare the punk for slow-burning fuses.
The spongy texture of agaric makes it suitable for use as tinder.
She photographed a rare agaric she found growing beneath an oak tree.
Some species of agaric are edible, while others can be highly poisonous.
The forest floor was dotted with bright red agaric mushrooms.
