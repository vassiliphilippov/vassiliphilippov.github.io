earbud

noun
small in-ear headphone
Pronunciation: / ˈɪrˌbʌd / (AmE), / ˈɪəˌbʌd / (BrE)
a small headphone or device that fits inside the ear and is used to listen to audio privately

Related Terms

  • synonym: earphone - a small device placed in or over the ear to listen to sound
  • synonym: headphone - a device worn over or in the ears to listen to audio
  • antonym: speaker - a device that plays audio out loud
