efflorescence

noun
peak period of flourishing or productivity
Pronunciation: / ˌɛf.ləˈrɛs.əns / (AmE), / ˌɛf.ləˈrɛs.əns / (BrE)
the period during which something flourishes or reaches its highest point of development

Examples

Artistic efflorescence
Her career reached its efflorescence in the late 1990s, when she won several major awards.
Many consider the 19th century to be the efflorescence of Russian literature.
The city experienced its cultural efflorescence during the Renaissance.
