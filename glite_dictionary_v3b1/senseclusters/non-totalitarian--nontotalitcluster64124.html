non-totalitarian

noun
opponent of totalitarianism
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn toʊˌtæləˈtɛriən / (AmE), / ˌnɒn təʊˌtælɪˈteəriən / (BrE)
a person, group, or government that does not support or practice totalitarian control or policies

Examples

Non-totalitarian leaders
The new constitution was drafted by a group of non-totalitarians seeking to protect civil liberties.
Many non-totalitarian leaders advocated for free elections and open debate.
After years of strict rule, the country finally elected a non-totalitarian government.

Related Terms

  • synonym: democrat - a person who supports democracy
  • antonym: totalitarian - a person who supports totalitarian control
