urticate

verb
cause a stinging sensation, like nettles
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrtɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈɜːtɪˌkeɪt /; / ˈɜːtɪkeɪt / (BrE)
to cause a stinging or itching sensation, especially by contact with nettles

Examples

To urticate the skin
Brushing against the plant will urticate the skin, leaving a painful rash.
Some caterpillars urticate predators with their tiny venomous hairs.
The jellyfish's tentacles can urticate swimmers, causing an intense burning sensation.
During the ritual, participants would urticate themselves with fresh nettles.
Some insects protect themselves by secreting chemicals that urticate potential predators.
The leaves of the plant can urticate the skin if handled without gloves.
