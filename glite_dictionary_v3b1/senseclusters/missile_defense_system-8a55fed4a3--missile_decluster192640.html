missile defense system

noun
system for defending against missiles
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪsəl dɪˈfɛns ˌsɪstəm /; / ˈmɪsəl dɪˈfɛns ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈmɪsaɪl dɪˈfɛns ˌsɪstəm /; / ˈmɪsaɪl dɪˈfɛns ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
A system of weapons and technology designed to detect, track, intercept, and destroy incoming missiles to protect against attacks, often as part of naval or military defense.

Examples

NATO members are investing heavily in joint missile defence systems to bolster security.
The failure of the missile defence system raised concerns about the fleet's vulnerability.
The ship was equipped with a state-of-the-art missile defence system to protect against enemy attacks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anti-missile system - a system specifically designed to intercept and destroy missiles
  • synonym: missile defense - the action or systems used to intercept attacking missiles
