missile defense system

noun
system for defending against missiles
2.4 3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪs.əl dɪ.ˈfens ˈsɪs.təm/ /; / /mɪ.ˈsaɪl dɪ.ˈfens ˈsɪs.təm/ (AmE), /ˈmɪs.əl dɪ.ˈfens ˈsɪs.təm/ /; / /mɪ.ˈsaɪl dɪ.ˈfens ˈsɪs.təm/ (BrE)
A system of weapons and technology designed to detect, track, intercept, and destroy incoming missiles to protect against attacks, often as part of naval or military defense.
