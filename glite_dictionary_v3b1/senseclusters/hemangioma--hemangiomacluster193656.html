hemangioma

noun
benign tumor of blood vessels
7.8 8.2
Pronunciation: / hɪˌmændʒiˈoʊmə /; / hiːˌmændʒiˈoʊmə / (AmE), / hɪˌmandʒɪˈəʊmə /; / hiːˌmandʒiˈəʊmə / (BrE)
A benign tumor consisting of a mass of blood vessels, often appearing as a birthmark.

Examples

A hemangioma birthmark
