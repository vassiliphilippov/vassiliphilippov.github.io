editorialization

noun
editing process
Pronunciation: / ˌɛdɪˌtɔːriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɛdɪˌtɔːriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making editorial decisions about selecting, organizing, and presenting content for publication

Examples

Editorialization of content
The editorialization of the magazine ensured that only the most relevant stories were published.
Her role involved the careful editorialization of news articles before they went to print.
Effective editorialization can greatly influence how readers perceive the content of a publication.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curation - the selection and organization of content for presentation
  • synonym: editing - the process of preparing written material for publication
