striga

noun
parasitic crop weed
Pronunciation: / ˈstraɪɡə / (AmE), / ˈstrɪɡə / (BrE)
a genus of parasitic plants that infest and damage crops, especially in Africa

Examples

Striga weed problem
Many farmers in sub-Saharan Africa lose crops each year due to striga infestations.
Researchers are developing new methods to control the spread of striga in maize fields.
The presence of striga can significantly reduce the yield of important food crops.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parasite - an organism that lives on or in another and harms it
  • synonym: witchweed - a common name for plants of the genus Striga
