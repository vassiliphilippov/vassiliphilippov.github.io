counterterm

noun
opposite term
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntərˌtɜrm / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntəˌtɜːm / (BrE)
a word or expression that expresses the opposite or negation of another term

Examples

Logical counterterm
In logic, 'false' is the counterterm to 'true'.
The teacher asked for a counterterm to balance the argument.
Every positive statement in mathematics often has a counterterm representing its negation.
