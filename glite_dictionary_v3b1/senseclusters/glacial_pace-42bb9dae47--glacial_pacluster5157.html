glacial pace

noun
extremely slow speed
Pronunciation: / ˈɡleɪʃəl peɪs / (AmE), / ˈɡleɪsɪəl peɪs / (BrE)
an extremely slow rate of progress or movement

Examples

A glacial pace
Despite our team's efforts, the project moved at a glacial pace and missed its deadlines.
Economic reforms are happening at such a glacial pace that few notice any difference.
The negotiations proceeded at a glacial pace, testing everyone's patience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: breakneck speed - dangerously or extremely fast rate
  • synonym: snail's pace - very slow rate of movement or progress
