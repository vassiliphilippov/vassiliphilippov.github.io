clustered bellflower

noun
a flowering plant with dense clusters of bell-shaped flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈklʌstɚd ˈbɛlˌflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈklʌstəd ˈbɛlˌflaʊə / (BrE)
a perennial plant native to Europe and temperate Asia, having dense clusters of violet-blue to white bell-shaped flowers

Examples

The clustered bellflower blooms
The clustered bellflower blooms in early summer, bringing vibrant color to the garden.
The dense violet-blue clusters of the clustered bellflower make it popular with landscapers.
We planted a row of clustered bellflower along the fence to attract bees and butterflies.
