literary movement

noun
group of writers with shared literary goals
5.5 6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬əˌrɛri ˈmuːvmənt / (AmE), / ˈlɪtərəri ˈmuːvmənt / (BrE)
A group or trend in literature, typically involving several authors over a similar period, sharing common styles, themes, or aims.
