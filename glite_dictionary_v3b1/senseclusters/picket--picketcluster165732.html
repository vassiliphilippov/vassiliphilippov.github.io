picket

verb
secure an animal to a stake or peg
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɪkɪt / (BrE)
to fasten an animal, especially a horse or goat, to a stake or peg driven into the ground by means of a rope or chain

Examples

To picket a horse
Remember to picket your animals before setting up camp.
The farmer decided to picket the horse to keep it from wandering off.
We picketed the goat near some shade trees for the afternoon.
