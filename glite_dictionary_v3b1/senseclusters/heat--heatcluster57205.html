heat

noun
system or supply of warmth for a building
1.0 24/m
Pronunciation: / hit / (AmE), / hiÀêt / (BrE)
the system or supply of warmth provided to a building, especially as a utility

Examples

Turn up the heat

We pay extra for heat during the winter months in our apartment building.
