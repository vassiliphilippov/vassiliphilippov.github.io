umbrellalike

adjective
resembling an umbrella
4.3
Pronunciation: / ʌmˈbɹɛl.əˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ʌmˈbrel.əˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the appearance of an umbrella, especially in shape or structure

Examples

Umbrellalike shape

The mushroom had an umbrellalike cap, perfect for sheltering tiny insects during a rainstorm.
