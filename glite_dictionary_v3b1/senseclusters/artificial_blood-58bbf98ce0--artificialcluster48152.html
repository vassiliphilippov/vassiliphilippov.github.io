artificial blood

noun
synthetic liquid used as a blood substitute
4.0
Pronunciation: / ɑːr.tɪˈfɪʃ.əl blʌd / (AmE), / ɑː.tɪˈfɪʃ.əl blʌd / (BrE)
a liquid designed to transport oxygen in the body, used as a temporary substitute for human blood
