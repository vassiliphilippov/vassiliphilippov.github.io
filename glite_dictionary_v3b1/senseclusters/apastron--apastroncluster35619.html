apastron

noun
farthest orbital point
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈpæstrən / (AmE), / əˈpæstrən / (BrE)
the point in the orbit of a star or planet where it is farthest from the object it orbits

Examples

The apastron point
The planet moves slowest at apastron in its elliptical orbit.
Astronomers calculated the distance between the two stars at apastron to study their interaction.
At apastron, the gravitational pull between the objects is at its weakest.

Related Terms

  • antonym: periastron - the point in the orbit where a star or planet is closest to the object it orbits
