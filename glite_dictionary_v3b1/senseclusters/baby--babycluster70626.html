baby

noun
immature or childish person
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪ.bi / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi / (BrE)
A person who is immature or behaves in a childish way.

Examples

Stop being a baby
Don't be such a baby—it's just a little rain!
She called him a baby for whining about doing his chores.
Whenever he loses a game, he acts like a baby.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adult - a person who is fully grown or developed
  • synonym: child - a young person, especially below the age of puberty
