nepotist

noun
person who favors relatives or friends
5.3 7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛpətɪst / (AmE), / ˈnɛpətɪst / (BrE)
a person who uses their power or influence to show favoritism to relatives or close friends, especially by giving them jobs

Examples

A nepotist promotes relatives
After years of being labeled a nepotist, the official finally promoted someone outside his family circle.
Many believe a nepotist undermines trust and fairness in the workplace.
The corporation's president was criticized as a nepotist for hiring his siblings and cousins for top positions.
