cocker spaniel

noun
a breed of small, silky-haired dog
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑkɚ ˈspæniəl / (AmE), / ˈkɒkə ˈspæniəl / (BrE)
A small dog breed with wavy, silky hair, originally developed in England, known for its affectionate nature.

Examples

A cocker spaniel dog
She takes her cocker spaniel for a long walk every morning.
The cocker spaniel wagged its tail excitedly when it saw its owner.
Their new pet is a playful cocker spaniel with a shiny golden coat.
