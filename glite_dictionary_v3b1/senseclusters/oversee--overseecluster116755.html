oversee

verb
supervise, direct work
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɚˈsi / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈsiː / (BrE)

Examples

To oversee the work

It's important to oversee the project closely so deadlines are met.
She was hired to oversee the company's financial operations.
