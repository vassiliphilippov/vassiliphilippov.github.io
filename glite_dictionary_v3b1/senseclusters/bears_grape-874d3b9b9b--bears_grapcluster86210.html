bear's grape

noun
evergreen shrub with red berries
8.7 9.0
Pronunciation: /berz greɪp/ (AmE), /beəz greɪp/ (BrE)
An evergreen mat-forming shrub of North America and northern Eurasia, with small white flowers and red berries; the leaves turn red in autumn.
