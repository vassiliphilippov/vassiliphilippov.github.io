Hernan Cortez

proper noun
Spanish conquistador who conquered the Aztecs
6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈhɜːr.nən kɔːr.ˈtez/ (AmE), /ˈhɜː.nən kɔː.ˈtez/ (BrE)
