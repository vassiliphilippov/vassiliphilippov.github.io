Jagganath

proper noun
avatar of Vishnu in Hinduism
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌɡəˌnɑθ / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌɡənɑːθ / (BrE)
A form of the Hindu god Vishnu, particularly worshipped in the city of Puri, India, and regarded as a major deity in Hinduism.

Examples

Jagganath temple Puri
Legends say that Jagganath is an incarnation of Lord Vishnu.
The temple of Jagganath is an important pilgrimage site for Hindus.
Thousands of devotees gather to see the idol of Jagganath during the annual festival in Puri.
