smush

verb
to squeeze together
7.8
Pronunciation: / smʊʃ / (AmE), / smʊʃ / (BrE)
to press, squeeze, or crowd people or things together into a small space

Examples

To smush together
We all had to smush into the tiny booth at the cafe to fit.
The kids tried to smush as many pillows as possible into the suitcase.
If you smush the bread in your bag, it will get flat and messy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crush - to press something so hard that it loses its shape or is damaged
  • antonym: separate - to move things or people apart
  • synonym: squash - to press or squeeze something so that it becomes flat or crushed
