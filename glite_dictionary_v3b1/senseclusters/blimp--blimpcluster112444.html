blimp

noun
small nonrigid airship
Pronunciation: / blɪmp / (AmE), / blɪmp / (BrE)
a small nonrigid airship, typically used for observation, advertising, or as a barrage balloon

Examples

A blimp over stadium
A blimp carrying a camera hovered over the football stadium during the game.
During the parade, a giant advertising blimp floated above the city streets.
The military once used the blimp for surveillance during wartime operations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: zeppelin - a large rigid airship
