bistate

adjective
two distinct states
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪˌsteɪt / (AmE), / ˈbaɪˌsteɪt / (BrE)
having or involving two distinct states or conditions

Examples

Bistate mode
The device is designed to function in a bistate mode, either on or off.
A bistate switch can only be in one of two positions at any time.
The experiment measured the response of the bistate system under different conditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: multistate - having more than two states or conditions
