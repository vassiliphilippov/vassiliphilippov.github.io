gastrotomy

noun
surgical stomach incision
8.9
Pronunciation: / ɡæˈstrɑːtəmi / (AmE), / ɡæˈstrɒtəmi / (BrE)
a surgical operation in which an incision is made into the stomach

Examples

A gastrotomy operation
The doctor decided a gastrotomy was necessary to remove the swallowed coin.
After the gastrotomy, the patient was monitored closely for signs of infection.
A gastrotomy allows surgeons direct access to the inside of the stomach.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gastrectomy - surgical removal of part or all of the stomach
  • synonym: laparotomy - a surgical incision into the abdominal cavity
