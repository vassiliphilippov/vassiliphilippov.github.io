masked

adjective
obscured for privacy (computing)
Pronunciation: / mæskt / (AmE), / mɑːskt / (BrE)
(in computing and data handling) having sensitive or identifying parts hidden, replaced, or obscured so the data cannot be used to identify individuals.

Examples

Masked data
In the anonymized dataset, user IDs are masked to protect personal information.
The security team verified that the credit card numbers were correctly masked before sharing.
When exporting logs, ensure sensitive fields remain masked so external partners cannot access them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anonymized - processed so that individuals cannot be identified from the data
  • synonym: redacted - edited or obscured to remove sensitive information before release
  • antonym: unmasked - having identifying or sensitive information revealed
