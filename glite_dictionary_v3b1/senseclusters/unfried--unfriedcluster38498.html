unfried

adjective
not fried
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfraɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfraɪd / (BrE)
not cooked by frying in oil or fat

Examples

Unfried chicken
The restaurant serves delicious unfried chicken for those looking for a healthier meal.
She prefers unfried vegetables to keep her meals light and nutritious.
You can choose between unfried or fried fish with your order.
