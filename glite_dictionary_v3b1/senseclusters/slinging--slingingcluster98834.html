slinging

noun
throwing with a wide, sweeping motion
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈslɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of throwing something with a wide, sweeping motion, as if using a sling

Examples

Powerful slinging of discus
Her powerful slinging of the discus earned her a gold medal.
The slinging of stones was once a common method of hunting.
With a practiced slinging of his arm, he sent the rope over the branch.
