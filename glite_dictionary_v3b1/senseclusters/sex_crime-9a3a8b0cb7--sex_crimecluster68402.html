sex crime

noun
a crime involving a sexual act without consent
Pronunciation: / sɛks kraɪm / (AmE), / seks kraɪm / (BrE)
A crime involving a sexual act that is committed without consent or by force or threat, often defined by statute.

Examples

Sex crime case
Reporting a sex crime can be extremely difficult for victims.
Support groups help survivors recover after experiencing a sex crime.
The new law increases penalties for anyone convicted of a sex crime.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rape - unlawful sexual intercourse carried out forcibly
  • synonym: sexual assault - unwanted sexual contact or activity, often by force
