burin

noun
engraving tool with a sharp point
Pronunciation: / ˈbjʊr.ɪn / (AmE), / ˈbjʊər.ɪn / (BrE)
a steel engraving tool with a sharp, pointed end used for incising designs onto metal, stone, or wood

Examples

A burin tool
A skilled engraver can create remarkably detailed images with a simple burin.
His workshop contained several burins of different sizes and shapes for various engraving techniques.
The artist used a burin to carefully carve intricate patterns on the metal plate.
