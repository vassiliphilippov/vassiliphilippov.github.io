peteca

noun
Brazilian shuttlecock
Pronunciation: / pəˈtɛkə / (AmE), / pəˈtɛkə / (BrE)
a small feathered or padded object used as a shuttlecock in the Brazilian game peteca, hit by hand between players

Examples

Hit the peteca
He tossed the peteca high into the air and slapped it across the court.
Children gathered in the park to play with a colorful peteca after school.
The feathers on her peteca made it easy to spot during the fast-paced game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shuttlecock - a feathered object hit back and forth in badminton
