polar

adjective
relating to Earth's poles
1.8 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈpoʊ.lər/ (AmE), /ˈpəʊ.lə/ (BrE)
relating to the North or South Pole of the Earth, or to very cold regions or climates near the poles

Examples

Polar region
A polar air mass brought unusually cold weather to the city last winter.
Antarctica is a polar region, with temperatures that remain below freezing for most of the year.
The expedition collected samples from several polar ice caps to study climate change.
She shivered in the polar temperatures inside the old, unheated house.
The hikers braved polar winds during their expedition across the tundra.
Winter in this region can bring polar conditions lasting for weeks.
A polar expedition requires careful planning and specialized equipment.
Many species have adapted to the extreme cold of polar regions.
The polar ice caps are melting due to rising global temperatures.

  • antonym: equatorial - relating to or near the equator
  • synonym: frigid - Very cold in temperature; freezing
  • antonym: tropical - Very hot, relating to the tropics
