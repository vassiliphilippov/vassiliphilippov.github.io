anchorman

noun
male television news host or coordinator
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋkərˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈæŋkəmæn / (BrE)
A male television news reporter who hosts and coordinates a broadcast, often linking reports from correspondents.

Examples

The anchorman reports news
He became the first Black anchorman on the national evening news.
The anchorman introduced the special report before handing over to the foreign correspondent.
The veteran anchorman calmly delivered breaking news as reporters updated him from the field.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anchorwoman - female television news host or coordinator
  • synonym: newscaster - person who presents the news on TV or radio
  • synonym: newsreader - person who reads news on television or radio
