chauvinism

noun
fanatical patriotism
5.0 7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈʃoʊ.vɪ.nɪ.zəm/ /; / /ˈʃəʊ.vɪ.nɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈʃəʊ.vɪ.nɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
Fanatical or aggressive patriotism, especially when it is prejudiced and unreasonable.

Examples

Extreme chauvinism for country
Her chauvinism became apparent whenever she spoke about her homeland.
Many critics argued that the film promoted a dangerous form of chauvinism.
The politician's chauvinism alienated those who favored peaceful international relations.
