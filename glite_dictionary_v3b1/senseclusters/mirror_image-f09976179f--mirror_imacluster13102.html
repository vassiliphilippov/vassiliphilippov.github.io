mirror image

noun
image with left and right reversed
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪrər ˌɪmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmɪr.ər ˌɪm.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
an image or likeness of something in which the left and right sides are reversed, as seen in a mirror

Examples

A perfect mirror image
His friend's personality was almost a mirror image of his own, but with subtle differences.
The letter 'b' is a mirror image of the letter 'd'.
When she looked into the water, she saw her mirror image staring back at her.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reflection - an image seen in a mirror or shiny surface
