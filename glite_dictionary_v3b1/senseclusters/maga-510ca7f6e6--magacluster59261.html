MAGA

noun
supporter of MAGA movement
Pronunciation: / ˈmægə / (AmE), / ˈmægə / (BrE)
a person who supports the Make America Great Again movement or its political goals in the United States

Examples

A MAGA supporter
A group of MAGA gathered outside the rally to show their support for the candidate.
He wore a red hat with the MAGA slogan during the parade.
Some MAGA have strong opinions about immigration policies in the United States.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Democrat - a member or supporter of the Democratic Party in the US
  • synonym: Trump supporter - a person who supports Donald Trump
