pseudomilitary

noun
imitation armed group
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːdoʊˈmɪlɪˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˌsjuːdəʊˈmɪlɪtəri / (BrE)
a group or organization that is not an official military but imitates military structure, appearance, or tactics

Examples

A pseudomilitary group
The town was controlled by a pseudomilitary that set up roadblocks and enforced its own rules.
A pseudomilitary group marched through the streets in uniforms, trying to look official.
The government warned citizens about the activities of a dangerous pseudomilitary operating in the region.

Related Terms

  • antonym: military - the official armed forces of a country
  • synonym: paramilitary - a group organized like a military force but not part of the official military
