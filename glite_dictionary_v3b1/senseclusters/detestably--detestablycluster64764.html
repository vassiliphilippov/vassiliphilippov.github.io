detestably

adverb
in a hateful or offensive manner
4.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈtɛstəbli / (AmE), / dɪˈtɛstəbli / (BrE)
in a way that arouses intense dislike or hatred; in a hateful, offensive manner

Examples

Behave detestably
He lied detestably to avoid responsibility, which only made things worse.
She treated her colleagues detestably and was soon reprimanded for her behavior.
The villain in the novel acted detestably, making readers despise him.
