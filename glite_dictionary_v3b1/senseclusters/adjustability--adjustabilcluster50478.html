adjustability

noun
ability to adapt
3.8
Pronunciation: / əˌdʒʌstəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / əˌdʒʌstəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality of being able to be changed or adapted easily to different conditions or needs

Examples

The adjustability of chairs
The adjustability of the office chair allows users of different heights to sit comfortably.
Engineers praised the adjustability of the new device for its ease of customization.
Parents appreciate the adjustability of the stroller as their child grows.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flexibility - the ability to change or be changed easily
  • antonym: rigidity - the quality of being stiff and not able to change
