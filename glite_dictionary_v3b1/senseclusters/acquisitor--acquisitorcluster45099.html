acquisitor

noun
person who acquires
8.2
Pronunciation: / əˈkwɪzɪtər / (AmE), / əˈkwɪzɪtə / (BrE)
a person or organization that acquires or obtains something, especially in business contexts

Examples

The acquisitor company
The acquisitor expanded its portfolio by purchasing several smaller firms.
As an experienced acquisitor, she was skilled at identifying valuable assets.
The board approved the acquisitor's proposal to merge with a competitor.
