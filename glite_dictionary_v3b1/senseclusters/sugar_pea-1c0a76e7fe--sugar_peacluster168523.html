sugar pea

noun
variety of edible pea with flat pods
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʊɡɚ pi / (AmE), / ˈʃʊɡə pi /; / ˈʃʊɡə piː / (BrE)
a type of pea plant with flat, edible pods that are eaten whole, usually while still unripe

Examples
