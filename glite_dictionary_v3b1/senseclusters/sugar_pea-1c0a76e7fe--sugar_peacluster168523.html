Synonyms

  • snow pea
    2.3 0.40/m
    snow pea is a common name for the flat, edible pods eaten whole when unripe, while sugar pea is an alternative name.
  • edible-podded pea
    4.4 0.00/m
    edible-podded pea describes peas with thicker, softer pods you can eat too, while sugar pea has very flat pods eaten whole.
