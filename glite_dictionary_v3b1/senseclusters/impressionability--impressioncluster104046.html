Synonyms

  • susceptibility
    7.1 3.04/m
    susceptibility means being easily harmed or affected by things, while impressionability means being easily influenced by people or experiences.
  • recipiency
    9.9 0.00/m
    recipiency is open and able to receive ideas or impressions, while impressionability means being easily influenced by outside factors.
  • suggestibility
    8.2 0.14/m
    suggestibility is easily swayed by others' suggestions or cues, while impressionability is a general tendency to be influenced.
  • persuadability
    7.6 0.03/m
