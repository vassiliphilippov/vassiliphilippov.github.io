expression of annoyance at repetition
Pronunciation: / hɪr wi ɡoʊ əˈɡɛn / (AmE), / hɪə wi ɡəʊ əˈɡen / (BrE)
used to express annoyance or resignation when something is happening again, especially something tiresome or frustrating

Examples

Here we go again now
Here we go again with another power outage during a storm.
Here we go again, the project deadline has been pushed back for the third time.
My sister is complaining about her job—here we go again.
