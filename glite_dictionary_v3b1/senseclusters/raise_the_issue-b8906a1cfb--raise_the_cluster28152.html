raise the issue

verb
bring up a topic for discussion
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /reɪz ðiː ˈɪʃ.uː/ /; / /reɪz ðə ˈɪʃ.uː/ /; / /reɪz ðiː ˈɪʃ.juː/ (AmE), /reɪz ðiː ˈɪʃ.uː/ /; / /reɪz ðə ˈɪʃ.uː/ /; / /reɪz ðiː ˈɪʃ.juː/ (BrE)
to bring up a topic, question, or problem for discussion or consideration, including creating or logging a problem or task in a system (such as in software or project management)

Examples
