running

noun
carrying the ball in American football
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrʌnɪŋ / (BrE)
(American football) a play in which a player carries the ball through or past the opposing team, rather than passing it.

Examples

Running rush
A strong defense can make running much more difficult for the offense.
During practice, the coach focused on improving the players' running techniques.
The team relied heavily on their running to control the clock and gain yardage.
