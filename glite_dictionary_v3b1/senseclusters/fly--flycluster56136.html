fly

noun
fishing lure designed to look like an insect
Pronunciation: / flaɪ / (AmE), / flaɪ / (BrE)
an artificial fishing lure made to resemble an insect, used in fly fishing to attract fish

Examples

A fishing fly
Different species of fish respond to different types of flies used by anglers.
He tied a bright new fly before casting his line into the river.
The fisherman crafted the perfect fly to mimic a mayfly hatching on the water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lure - something used to attract fish, especially in angling
