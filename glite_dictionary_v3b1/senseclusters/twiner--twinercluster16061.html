twiner

noun
climbing plant
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtwaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈtwaɪnə / (BrE)
a plant that climbs by winding its stem around a support

Examples

Climbing twiner
Morning glory is a common twiner found in many gardens.
The twiner wrapped itself tightly around the wooden pole for support.
Some twiners can quickly cover fences and trellises during the summer months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: creeper - a plant that grows along the ground or up surfaces
  • synonym: vine - a plant with a long, thin stem that climbs or trails
