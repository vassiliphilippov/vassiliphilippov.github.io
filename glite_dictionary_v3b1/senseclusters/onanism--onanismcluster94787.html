onanism

noun
birth control by withdrawal method
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊnəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈɒnənɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
A method of birth control in which sexual intercourse is started but the penis is withdrawn before ejaculation to prevent pregnancy.

Examples

Onanism as birth control
Medical professionals generally do not recommend onanism as a reliable form of birth control.
Some religious groups strictly prohibit onanism as a method of contraception.
The couple practiced onanism in an attempt to avoid having children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coitus interruptus - intentional withdrawal of the penis before ejaculation during intercourse
