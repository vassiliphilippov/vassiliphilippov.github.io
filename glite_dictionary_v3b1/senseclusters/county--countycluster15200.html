county

noun
US: main local administrative division of a state
1.9 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkaʊn.tiː/ /; / /ˈkaʊn.iː/ (AmE), /ˈkaʊn.tiː/ /; / /ˈkaʊn.iː/ (BrE)

Examples
