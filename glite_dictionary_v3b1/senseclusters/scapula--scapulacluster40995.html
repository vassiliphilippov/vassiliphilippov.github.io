scapula

noun
shoulder blade bone
Pronunciation: / ˈskæp.jə.lə / (AmE), / ˈskæp.jʊ.lə / (BrE)
either of the two flat, triangular bones located on each side of the upper back, forming part of the shoulder

Examples

The scapula bone
Her doctor pointed out a shadow near the right scapula on the X-ray.
Pain between the scapulae can indicate muscle strain or nerve issues.
The athlete fractured his scapula during the intense game.
