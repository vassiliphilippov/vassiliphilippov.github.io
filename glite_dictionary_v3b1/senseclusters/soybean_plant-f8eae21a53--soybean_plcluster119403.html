soybean plant

noun
plant producing soybeans; cultivated crop
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔɪˌbin plænt / (AmE), / ˈsɔɪˌbiːn plɑːnt / (BrE)
an erect, bushy annual plant with trifoliate leaves and purple to pink flowers, grown extensively for its edible, oil-rich seeds

Examples

A soybean plant grows
Each soybean plant can yield hundreds of pods filled with nutritious beans.
Farmers rotate corn with soybean plants to improve soil fertility.
The soybean plant is widely cultivated in Asia for its protein-rich seeds.
