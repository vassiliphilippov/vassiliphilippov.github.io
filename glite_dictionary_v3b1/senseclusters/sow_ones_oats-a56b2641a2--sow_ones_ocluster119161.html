sow one's oats

verb
behave promiscuously (esp. when young)
Pronunciation: / soʊ wʌnz oʊts / (AmE), / səʊ wʌnz əʊts / (BrE)
to behave in a promiscuous or self-indulgent way, especially when young, often involving casual sexual relationships

Examples

To sow one's oats in youth
He regrets nothing about sowing his oats in college, seeing it as a formative time in his life.
In his twenties, he spent several years sowing his oats before settling down and starting a family.
They say it is natural for young people to sow their oats before committing to a serious relationship.
