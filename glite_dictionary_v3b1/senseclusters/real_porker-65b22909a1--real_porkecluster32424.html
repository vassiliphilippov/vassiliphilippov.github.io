real porker

noun
someone who eats excessively (informal)
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈriːəl ˈpɔrkər / (AmE), / ˈrɪəl ˈpɔːkə / (BrE)
an informal term for someone who eats excessively or greedily

Examples

A real porker eats
After the third helping of dessert, everyone called him a real porker at the table.
Don't be such a real porker—leave some food for the rest of us!
When it comes to pizza, she turns into a real porker and can't stop eating.

  • antonym: light eater - someone who eats only small amounts of food
