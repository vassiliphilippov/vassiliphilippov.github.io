bicentenary

noun
200th anniversary or its celebration
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.sɛnˈtiː.nɛr.i / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪ.sɛnˈtiː.nəri / (BrE)
The 200th anniversary of a significant event or the celebration marking this occasion.

Examples

The bicentenary of independence
Next year marks the bicentenary of the university, and a series of events are planned.
The museum held a special exhibition to commemorate the bicentenary of Charles Darwin's birth.
The town celebrated its bicentenary with a parade and fireworks.
