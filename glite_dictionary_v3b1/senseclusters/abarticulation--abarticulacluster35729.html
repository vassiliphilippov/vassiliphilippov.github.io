abarticulation

noun
medical: dislocation of a joint
Pronunciation: / ˌæbˌɑrtɪkjuˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌæbˌɑːtɪkjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
dislocation or separation of a joint; the condition where bones in a joint become displaced from their normal alignment

Examples

Abarticulation of joint
Prompt treatment of abarticulation is essential to prevent long-term joint damage.
The X-ray confirmed an abarticulation of the shoulder joint following the accident.
The surgeon discussed the risks associated with any surgical correction of abarticulation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dislocation - the displacement of a bone from its joint
