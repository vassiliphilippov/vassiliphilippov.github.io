particle

noun
element of a particle system
5.3 3.71/m
Pronunciation: / c8p51d0rt6ak59l / (AmE), / c8p51d0t6akl / (BrE)
(in computer graphics) a tiny simulated element used in a particle system to represent visual effects such as smoke, fire, sparks, or dust.

Examples

Particle system effects
The film's particle system uses many tiny virtual particles to simulate smoke.
To create realistic embers, the artist tuned the particle size, lifetime, and color variation.
During the demo, thousands of particles burst from the explosion, creating a convincing fireball effect.

Related Terms

  • synonym: particle system - a simulation technique that uses many small elements to create fuzzy or complex visual effects
  • synonym: sprite - a small 2D image or animation often used to represent a particle or object in graphics
