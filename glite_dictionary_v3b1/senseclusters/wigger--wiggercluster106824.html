wigger

noun
offensive slang insult
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪɡər / (AmE), / ˈwɪɡə / (BrE)
an offensive slang term for a white person who adopts mannerisms, language, or style associated with Black culture

Examples

A wigger person
The use of the word wigger is widely considered offensive and inappropriate.
He was unfairly labeled a wigger because of his taste in music and fashion.
Many people find the term wigger disrespectful and hurtful.
