wicking

adjective
moisture-drawing
4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪ.kɪŋ/ /; / /ˈwɪ.kəŋ/ (AmE), /ˈwɪ.kɪŋ/ /; / /ˈwɪ.kəŋ/ (BrE)

Examples

This wicking material helps you stay dry even on hot summer days.
The socks are made from a wicking blend that prevents blisters by moving moisture away.
