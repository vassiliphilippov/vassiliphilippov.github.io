well-lighted

adjective
brightly lit by artificial light
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛlˌlaɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈwɛlˌlaɪtɪd / (BrE)
Provided with plenty of artificial light; brightly lit.

Examples

A well-lighted room
A well-lighted entrance made the building feel safe even at night.
It's important to work in a well-lighted area to avoid straining your eyes.
The hallway was well-lighted, making it easy to see every corner.
