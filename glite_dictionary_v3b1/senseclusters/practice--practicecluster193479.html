practice

noun
repeated exercise to improve skill
Pronunciation: / ˈpræktɪs / (AmE), / ˈpræktɪs / (BrE)
the repeated exercise of an activity or skill to improve proficiency

Examples

Piano practice
Daily practice is essential if you want to master the piano.
The team's success is a result of months of diligent practice.
With enough practice, she became an excellent public speaker.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neglect - the state or fact of not practicing or giving attention to a skill
  • synonym: rehearsal - the act of practicing something, such as a play or music, before performing it in public
  • synonym: training - the process of learning the skills that you need for a particular job or activity
