uncontacted

adjective
not communicated with
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈkɑːntæktɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈkɒntæktɪd / (BrE)
not having had communication or interaction with the outside world or with other groups of people

Examples

Uncontacted tribe
Researchers discovered an uncontacted tribe deep in the rainforest.
The government has strict policies to protect uncontacted peoples from outside influence.
Many uncontacted communities remain hidden in remote regions of the world.

Related Terms

  • antonym: connected - having communication or contact with others
  • synonym: isolated - separated from others; alone or apart
