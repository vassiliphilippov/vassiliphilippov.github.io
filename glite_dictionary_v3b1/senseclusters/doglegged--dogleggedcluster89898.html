doglegged

adjective
bent at an angle
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔɡˌlɛɡd / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡˌlɛɡd / (BrE)
having a sharp bend or angle, resembling the shape of a dog's leg

Examples

A doglegged road
The old road was doglegged, making it difficult for large trucks to pass.
Golfers must be careful when approaching a doglegged fairway on the course.
The river takes a doglegged turn just before reaching the village.

  • synonym: zigzag - having a series of sharp turns or angles
