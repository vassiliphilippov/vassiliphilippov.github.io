amazon

noun
a type of tropical green parrot
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈæm.əˌzɑn / (AmE), / ˈæm.ə.zɒn / (BrE)
A chiefly green, tropical American parrot of the genus Amazona, known for its ability to mimic sounds and its popularity as a pet.

Examples

A pet amazon parrot
He has kept a pet amazon for over ten years and taught it many words.
The amazon squawked loudly from its perch beside the window.
Wild amazons are often seen in the forests of Central and South America.
