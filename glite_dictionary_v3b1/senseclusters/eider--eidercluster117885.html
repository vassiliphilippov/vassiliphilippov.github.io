eider

noun
a northern sea duck known for its soft down
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪdər / (AmE), / ˈaɪdə / (BrE)
A large sea duck of the Northern Hemisphere, valued for the fine soft down of the female's breast, used for stuffing bedding and clothing.

Examples

The eider duck
Eiderdown quilts are made using feathers plucked from eider ducks.
The female eider lines her nest with warm down from her own breast.
We saw a flock of eider swimming near the rocky shore.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duck - a waterbird with a broad blunt bill, short legs, webbed feet, and a waddling gait
