spirit world

noun
the realm of spiritual beings
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪrɪt ˌwɝːld / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪt ˌwɜːld / (BrE)
a realm, often imagined or believed in various religious and cultural traditions, as the place where spiritual beings such as angels, demons, or ghosts exist

Examples

The spirit world realm
Many cultures tell stories about the spirit world and its mysterious inhabitants.
Shamans claim to communicate with the spirit world during their rituals.
She believes her ancestor guides her from the spirit world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: afterlife - life after physical death, often considered as a supernatural realm
  • degree higher: heaven - a place believed to be the home of God, angels, and the good after death
  • degree lower: hell - a place believed to be the abode of evil spirits or the damned after death
