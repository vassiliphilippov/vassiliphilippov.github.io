householding

noun
home self-sufficiency
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊshoʊldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaʊshəʊldɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of producing goods or services at home for a household's own use, often to achieve self-sufficiency or save money

Examples

Self-sufficient householding
During difficult economic times, many families turned to householding to meet their basic needs.
The couple practiced householding by baking their own bread and making clothes for their children.
Interest in householding has grown as people seek more sustainable lifestyles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: homesteading - living self-sufficiently by producing most necessities at home
  • synonym: subsistence farming - growing food mainly for one's own family's use
