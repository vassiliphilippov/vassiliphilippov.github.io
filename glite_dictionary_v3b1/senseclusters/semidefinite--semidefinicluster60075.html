semidefinite

adjective
not strictly definite
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈdɛfɪnət / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈdɛfɪnət / (BrE)
describing a matrix or form that satisfies certain non-negativity conditions but is not strictly definite
