invective

noun
abusive or insulting language
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɛktɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɛktɪv / (BrE)
insulting, abusive, or highly critical language

Examples

Bitter invective
Her blog post was filled with bitter invective directed at the company.
In the heat of the argument, he unleashed a torrent of invective that shocked everyone present.
The politician responded to criticism with a stream of invective against his opponents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abuse - cruel and violent or insulting treatment
  • antonym: praise - expression of approval or admiration
