whereby

conjunction
by which
3.9
Pronunciation: / wɛrˈbaɪ / (AmE), / weəˈbaɪ /; / wɛəˈbaɪ / (BrE)
by which or through which something happens or is accomplished or achieved

Examples

An agreement whereby both win
They signed an agreement whereby both companies would share the technology.
The school introduced a system whereby students can submit assignments online.
He created a plan whereby everyone benefits from the new policy.
They signed an agreement whereby both companies would share the profits equally.
