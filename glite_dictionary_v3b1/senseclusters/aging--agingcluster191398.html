aging

noun
process of growing older
6.4 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈeɪ.dʒɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈeɪ.dʒɪŋ/ (BrE)
The process of growing older and showing the effects of advancing age in living organisms.

Examples

Human aging
Aging is a natural part of life that affects every living organism.
Many people try to maintain an active lifestyle to delay the signs of aging.
Research seeks ways to slow the effects of aging on the human body.
Many societies face challenges related to their ageing population.
Researchers are studying ways to slow down ageing and increase health in later life.
The ageing process can lead to changes in the skin, hair, and vision.

  • synonym: senescence - the biological process of aging and gradual deterioration
  • antonym: youth - the state or period of being young
