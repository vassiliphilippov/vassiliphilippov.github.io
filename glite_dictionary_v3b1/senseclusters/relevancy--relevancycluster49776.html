relevancy

noun
the state of being relevant
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛl.ə.vən.si / (AmE), / ˈrɛl.ɪ.vən.si / (BrE)
the quality or state of being closely connected or appropriate to what is being considered

Examples

The relevancy of facts
Her research was praised for its direct relevancy to current social issues.
In court, the lawyer challenged the relevancy of the evidence presented.
The relevancy of his comments was questioned during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: relevance - the quality of being relevant; significance to the matter at hand
