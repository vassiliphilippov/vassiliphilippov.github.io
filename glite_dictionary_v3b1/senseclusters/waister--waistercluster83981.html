waister

noun
sailor on main deck
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪstər / (AmE), / ˈweɪstə / (BrE)
a sailor who works on the main deck of a ship, especially in the waist area

Examples

The waister on deck
The waister scrubbed the deck as the ship sailed on.
During the storm, the waister helped secure loose equipment on the main deck.
As a young waister, he learned the basics of seamanship from older crew members.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deckhand - a sailor who performs manual work on a ship's deck
  • antonym: officer - a person in a position of authority on a ship
