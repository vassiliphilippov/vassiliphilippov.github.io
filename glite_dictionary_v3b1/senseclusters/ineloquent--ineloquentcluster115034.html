ineloquent

adjective
lacking eloquence
7.5
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈel.ə.kwənt/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈel.ə.kwənt/ (BrE)
not able to express oneself clearly, effectively, or persuasively in speech or writing

Examples

An ineloquent speech
Despite his ineloquent manner, his message was sincere and heartfelt.
The candidate's ineloquent answers made it difficult for voters to understand his policies.
Her ineloquent explanation left the audience confused about the main point.

Related Terms

  • antonym: eloquent - able to express oneself clearly and effectively
  • synonym: inarticulate - unable to express ideas or feelings clearly
