dangerous situation

noun
hazardous or threatening circumstance
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪndʒərəs sɪtʃuˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈdeɪndʒərəs sɪtjʊˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
A circumstance in which there is serious risk of harm, injury, or death.

Examples

A dangerous situation
He was praised for his bravery after helping others escape a dangerous situation.
Police officers are often trained to stay calm during a dangerous situation.
The hikers found themselves in a dangerous situation when the storm intensified unexpectedly.

  • antonym: safe situation - a situation free from danger or risk
