Maryland yellowthroat

noun
North American warbler species
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛrɪlənd ˈjɛloʊˌθroʊt / (AmE), / ˈmɛrɪlənd ˈjɛləʊˌθrəʊt / (BrE)
A small North American warbler (Geothlypis trichas), notable for the male's black facial mask and yellow throat.
