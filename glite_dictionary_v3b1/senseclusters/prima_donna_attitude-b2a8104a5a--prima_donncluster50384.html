prima donna attitude

noun
arrogant or high-maintenance behavior
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːmə ˈdɑnə ˈætɪˌtjud / (AmE), / ˌpriːmə ˈdɒnə ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
a tendency to act in an arrogant, self-important, or overly demanding manner, like a temperamental star

Examples

Prima donna attitude problem
Coaches don’t tolerate a prima donna attitude from any player on the team.
Despite her talent, her prima donna attitude made her difficult to work with.
His prima donna attitude drove several of his colleagues to quit the project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrogance - a sense of superiority and self-importance
  • antonym: humility - a modest or low view of one's own importance
