Privatdozent

noun
German university lecturer
Pronunciation: / priˈvɑt doʊˌtsɛnt / (AmE), / priˈvɑːt dəʊˌtsɛnt / (BrE)
a person who is qualified to teach at a German university but does not hold a professorship

Examples

A Privatdozent at university
After completing his habilitation, he was appointed as a Privatdozent at the university.
The Privatdozent offered advanced seminars in philosophy for graduate students.
As a Privatdozent, she could supervise doctoral candidates but did not have a permanent professorship.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lecturer - a person who teaches at a university or college
  • antonym: professor - a senior academic with a professorship at a university
