mainstreaming

noun
becoming widely accepted
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈmeɪn.striː.mɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈmeɪn.striː.mɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process by which something becomes widely accepted and integrated into mainstream society or culture

Examples

Mainstreaming of new ideas
The mainstreaming of remote work has changed how companies operate around the world.
Social media played a key role in the mainstreaming of new fashion trends.
The mainstreaming of environmental awareness has led to more sustainable business practices.

Related Terms

  • antonym: marginalization - the process of making something less important or excluded from the mainstream
  • synonym: popularization - the act of making something popular or accessible to the general public
