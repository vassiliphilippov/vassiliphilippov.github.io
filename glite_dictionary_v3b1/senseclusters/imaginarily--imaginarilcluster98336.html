imaginarily

adverb
involving imaginary numbers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmædʒɪˌnɛrəli / (AmE), / ɪˈmædʒɪˌnɛərɪli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to imaginary numbers or the imaginary part of a complex number, especially involving the imaginary unit i.

Examples

Imaginarily solve equations

When multiplied imaginarily, the value acquires a factor of i and rotates in the complex plane.
Some solutions exist only imaginarily, with no real number representation.
