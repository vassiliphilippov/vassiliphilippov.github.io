mosque

noun
Muslim place of worship
Pronunciation: / mɑsk / (AmE), / mɒsk / (BrE)
A building where Muslims gather to worship, usually featuring a minaret.

Examples

The mosque building
The call to prayer echoed from the minaret of the mosque every afternoon.
The city built a new mosque to accommodate the growing Muslim community.
Tourists are welcome to visit the historic mosque during certain hours.
