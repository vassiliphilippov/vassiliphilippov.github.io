befog

verb
make less visible or clear
5.4 7.0
Pronunciation: / bɪˈfɑɡ / (AmE), / bɪˈfɒɡ / (BrE)
to make something less visible or clear; to obscure or cloud

Examples

To befog the view
Confusing explanations can befog the main points for the audience.
His memory was befogged by years of neglect and time.
The mist began to befog the city skyline as night fell.
