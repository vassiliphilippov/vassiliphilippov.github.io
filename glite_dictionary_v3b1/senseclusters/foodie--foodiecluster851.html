foodie

noun
person passionate about good food and drink
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfuːdi / (AmE), / ˈfuːdi / (BrE)
a person who has a particular interest in and enjoys good food and drink, often seeking new dining experiences

Examples

A foodie loves food
As a passionate foodie, he travels the world in search of unique culinary experiences.
Her Instagram feed is a dream come true for any foodie who loves dessert.
The new restaurant downtown is attracting every foodie in the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gourmet - a person who has a discerning palate and enjoys fine food and drink
