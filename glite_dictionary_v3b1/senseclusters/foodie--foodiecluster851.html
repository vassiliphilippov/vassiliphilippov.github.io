foodie

noun
person passionate about good food and drink
5.9 0.81/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàfuÀêdi / (AmE), / ÀàfuÀêdi / (BrE)
a person who has a particular interest in and enjoys good food and drink, often seeking new dining experiences

Examples

A foodie loves food
As a passionate foodie, he travels the world in search of unique culinary experiences.
Her Instagram feed is a dream come true for any foodie who loves dessert.
The new restaurant downtown is attracting every foodie in the city.

Synonyms

  • gourmet
    6.5 5.50/m
    a gourmet prefers refined, high-quality food and careful dining, while a foodie is an informal enthusiast who seeks new eating experiences.
  • epicure
    9.3 0.26/m
    an epicure enjoys very refined, discriminating flavors and quiet dining, while a foodie is a casual fan who seeks new restaurants and trends.
  • epicurean
    9.1 0.03/m
    an epicurean seeks rich pleasure in fine food and drink, while a foodie is an informal explorer of new dining experiences.
  • gastronome
    8.6 0.09/m
    a gastronome studies and celebrates fine cooking in a formal way, while a foodie is a relaxed eater who loves trying new places.
