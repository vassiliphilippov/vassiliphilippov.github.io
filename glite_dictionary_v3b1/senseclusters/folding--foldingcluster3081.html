folding

noun
the act of folding
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfəʊldɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of bending or laying one part over another; the action of folding something

Examples

Folding a paper
After folding the paper, she created an intricate origami figure.
Proper folding of the laundry helps keep clothes wrinkle-free.
The chef demonstrated the folding of dough to his students.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unfolding - the act or process of opening or spreading out something that was folded
