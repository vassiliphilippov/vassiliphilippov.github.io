puling

noun
weak or plaintive cry
Pronunciation: / ˈpjuːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpjuːlɪŋ / (BrE)
a weak, thin, or plaintive cry or complaint, especially one that sounds feeble or whiny

Examples

The puling of a puppy
The puling of the lost puppy could be heard from the alley late at night.
She was annoyed by the constant puling from the children when they did not get their way.
A faint puling came from the box, revealing a litter of newborn kittens inside.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheer - a shout of encouragement or joy
  • degree higher: wail - a long, loud, high-pitched cry of pain or grief
  • synonym: whimper - a low, feeble sound expressing pain or distress
