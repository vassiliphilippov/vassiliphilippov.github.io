pill pusher

noun
person who unethically sells drugs
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪl ˌpʊʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɪl ˌpʊʃə / (BrE)
a person who promotes or sells drugs, especially unethically or illegally

Examples

Illegal pill pusher
Authorities arrested the suspected pill pusher for distributing prescription medications without a license.
She was accused of being a pill pusher after several patients became addicted to painkillers.
The doctor warned his patients to stay away from any pill pusher promising miracle cures.

Related Terms

  • antonym: doctor - a person licensed to practice medicine
  • synonym: drug dealer - a person who sells illegal drugs
