equinovarus

adjective
foot twisted inward
Pronunciation: / ˌiːkwɪnoʊˈvɛərəs / (AmE), / ˌiːkwɪnəʊˈvɑːrəs / (BrE)
having or characterized by a deformity of the foot in which it is twisted downward and inward

Examples

Equinovarus foot
The doctor diagnosed the newborn with an equinovarus deformity that would require corrective treatment.
Children with equinovarus feet often need special shoes or braces to help them walk.
Surgery may be necessary if equinovarus is severe and does not improve with physical therapy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clubfoot - a congenital deformity in which the foot is twisted out of shape or position
