naptime

noun
daytime sleep period
Pronunciation: / ˈnæpˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈnæpˌtaɪm / (BrE)
a short period during the day set aside for sleeping, especially for young children

Examples

Afternoon naptime
Every afternoon at preschool, the children look forward to naptime after lunch.
If I skip naptime, I feel tired for the rest of the day.
The teacher dimmed the lights to signal that naptime was about to begin.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bedtime - the usual time for going to bed at night
  • synonym: siesta - a short nap taken in the early afternoon
