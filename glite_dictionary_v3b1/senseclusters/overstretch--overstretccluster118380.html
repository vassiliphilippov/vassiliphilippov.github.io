to strain or extend (a part of the body, especially a muscle or tendon) beyond its normal limit, especially resulting in damage or injury

Examples

To overstretch a muscle
Athletes are taught to warm up carefully to avoid overstretching their muscles.
If you overstretch your hamstring, you might not be able to walk properly for days.
She overstretched her back while lifting heavy boxes at work.
