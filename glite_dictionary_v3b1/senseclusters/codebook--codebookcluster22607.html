codebook

noun
book of codes
4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkoʊd.bʊk/ (AmE), /ˈkəʊd.bʊk/ (BrE)
a book or document that lists codes and explains their meanings or how to use them

Examples

The analyst consulted the codebook to understand the abbreviations used in the report.
Each research project required a detailed codebook to ensure consistent data entry.
