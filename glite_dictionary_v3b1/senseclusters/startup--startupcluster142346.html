startup

noun
newly formed company, often tech
3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈstɑːr.tʌp/ (AmE), /ˈstɑː.təp/ /; / /ˈstɑː.tʌp/ (BrE)
a newly created company, especially in technology or high-growth industries

Examples

A tech startup
The local startup attracted investors with its innovative app and scalable revenue model.
