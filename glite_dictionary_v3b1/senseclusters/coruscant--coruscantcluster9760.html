coruscant

adjective
flashing or sparkling with light
Pronunciation: / kəˈrʌsənt / (AmE), / kəˈrʌskənt / (BrE)
Emitting brief flashes of bright light; sparkling or glittering.

Examples

A coruscant trail
A coruscant trail of sparks followed the firework into the night sky.
Her jewelry was coruscant in the ballroom's brilliant lighting.
The coruscant surface of the lake caught the last rays of sunset.
