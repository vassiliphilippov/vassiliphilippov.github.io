Afrocentrist

adjective
focused on African perspective
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌæfroʊˈsɛntrɪst / (AmE), / ˌæfrəʊˈsɛntrɪst / (BrE)
emphasizing African peoples, histories, or cultures as central to the interpretation of events or ideas

Examples

Afrocentrist perspective
The museum's Afrocentrist approach highlights the achievements of African civilizations.
Her Afrocentrist perspective challenges traditional narratives in world history.
Many educators are adopting Afrocentrist methods to provide a more inclusive curriculum.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Afrocentric - centered on or relating to African culture and history
  • antonym: Eurocentrist - emphasizing European culture or history as central
