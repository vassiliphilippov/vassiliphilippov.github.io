venery

noun
sexual indulgence
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛnəri / (AmE), / ˈviːnəri / (BrE)
sexual indulgence or uncontrolled sexual desire, especially in an archaic or literary context

Examples

Poems of venery
In ancient poetry, venery was often depicted as both a blessing and a curse.
The character's downfall was attributed to his unchecked venery and reckless behavior.
She warned her son about the dangers of venery and the importance of self-control.
