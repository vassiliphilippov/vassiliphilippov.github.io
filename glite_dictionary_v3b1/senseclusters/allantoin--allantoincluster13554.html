allantoin

noun
skin healing compound
Pronunciation: / əˈlæntɔɪn / (AmE), / əˈlæntɔɪn / (BrE)
a chemical compound used in skin care products to promote healing and soothe irritated skin

Examples

Allantoin cream
Many lotions contain allantoin to help moisturize and protect the skin.
The cream's main ingredient, allantoin, is known for its soothing properties.
Dermatologists often recommend products with allantoin for sensitive or irritated skin.
