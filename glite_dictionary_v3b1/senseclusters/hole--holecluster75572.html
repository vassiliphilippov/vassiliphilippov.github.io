Synonyms

  • opening
    1.0 19/m
    opening is a space that allows access or passage through something, while hole is any kind of gap or hollow in an object.
  • aperture
    7.0 0.90/m
    aperture is a small or controlled opening in a surface, often technical, while hole is more general and common.
  • cavity
    3.9 2.69/m
    cavity is an inner hollow often natural or in a body, while hole can be a simple open gap through something.
  • hollow
    3.8 1.86/m
    hollow is a sunken or empty space usually shaped or depressed, while hole is any opening or gap in an object.
  • gap
    1.0 14/m
    gap is an open space between objects or areas, often horizontal or distant, while hole is a single opening in something.
