malposed

adjective
abnormally positioned (esp. tooth)
Pronunciation: / ˈmælˌpoʊzd / (AmE), / ˈmalpəʊzd / (BrE)
(of a part, especially a tooth) abnormally positioned; not in the correct anatomical place.

Examples

Crowded malposed teeth
Crowded and malposed teeth can be more difficult to clean properly.
Radiographs revealed a malposed wisdom tooth impacting the adjacent molar.
The dentist recommended braces to correct her malposed upper incisors.
