Platte

proper noun
river in Nebraska, U.S.
Pronunciation: / plæt / (AmE), / plæt / (BrE)
A river in Nebraska, United States, that flows eastward and becomes a tributary of the Missouri River.

Examples

Platte River
Flooding along the Platte can sometimes impact local farms and communities.
The Platte is known for its wide, shallow nature and sandy islands.
