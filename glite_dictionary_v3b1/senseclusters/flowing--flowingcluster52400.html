flowing

noun
smooth movement of fluids
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfloʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfləʊɪŋ / (BrE)
the movement characteristic of a fluid, such as a liquid or gas, as it moves smoothly and continuously

Examples

Smooth flowing water
Scientists studied the flowing of air around the wing to improve the plane's design.
The flowing of water through the riverbed has carved out deep channels over time.
The smooth flowing of the lava fascinated the observers during the volcanic eruption.
