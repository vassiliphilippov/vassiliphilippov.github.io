glim

noun
faint light or lamp
Pronunciation: / ɡlɪm / (AmE), / ɡlɪm / (BrE)
an old-fashioned word for a lamp or a faint light, especially in the dark

Examples

A glim lamp
He lit a glim to find his way through the dark corridor.
A faint glim shone from the window, guiding them home.
Without a glim, the old cellar was pitch black.

Related Terms

  • antonym: darkness - the absence of light
  • synonym: glow - a soft, steady light
  • synonym: lamp - a device that produces light
