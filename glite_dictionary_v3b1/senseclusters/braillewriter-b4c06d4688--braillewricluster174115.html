Braillewriter

noun
Braille transcriber
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪlˌraɪtər / (AmE), / ˈbreɪlˌraɪtə / (BrE)
a person who writes or transcribes text into Braille for visually impaired readers

Examples

A Braillewriter transcribes
The school hired a Braillewriter to make textbooks accessible for blind students.
As a volunteer Braillewriter, she helped create materials for the local library.
Each Braillewriter plays a vital role in supporting visually impaired readers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scribe - a person who copies out documents
  • synonym: transcriber - a person who converts spoken or written material into another form
