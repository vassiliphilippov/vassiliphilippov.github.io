nonresponsive

adjective
not adapting to devices
6.6
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.rɪ.ˈspɑːn.sɪv/ /; / /nən.rɪ.ˈspɑːn.sɪv/ (AmE), /nɒn.rɪ.ˈspɒn.sɪv/ /; / /nən.rɪ.ˈspɒn.sɪv/ (BrE)
not automatically adjusting its layout or behavior for different screen sizes or devices

Examples

Nonresponsive website
The old website is nonresponsive, making it difficult to use on smartphones and tablets.
Users complained that the app was nonresponsive when viewed on smaller screens.
A nonresponsive design can frustrate visitors who access the site from different devices.
