blatter

verb
to chatter noisily
9.9 0.00/m
to talk loudly or chatter noisily, especially in a foolish or meaningless way

Examples

To blatter nonsense
The children would blatter excitedly during recess, filling the playground with noise.
He tends to blatter on about topics no one else finds interesting.
At the party, people blattered away without noticing how late it was getting.
