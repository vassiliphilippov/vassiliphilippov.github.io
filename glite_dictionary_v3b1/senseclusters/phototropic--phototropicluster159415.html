phototropic

adjective
growing toward light
7.5
Pronunciation: /foʊ.tə.ˈtroʊ.pɪk/ (AmE), /fəʊ.tə.ˈtrɒp.ɪk/ (BrE)
describing an organism that grows or moves toward a source of light

Examples

Phototropic plants grow
Many plants are phototropic, bending their stems toward the nearest light source.
The phototropic behavior of algae helps them maximize photosynthesis in aquatic environments.
Researchers observed that the seedlings displayed a strong phototropic response in the laboratory.
