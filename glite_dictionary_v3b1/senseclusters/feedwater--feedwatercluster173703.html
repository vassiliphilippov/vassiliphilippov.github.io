feedwater

noun
water for boilers
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːdˌwɔtər / (AmE), / ˈfiːdˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
water that is supplied to a boiler or steam generator to produce steam

Examples

Feedwater quality
The technician monitored the feedwater quality to prevent damage to the boiler.
Low feedwater levels can cause serious problems in steam generation systems.
Proper treatment of feedwater is essential for efficient power plant operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boiler water - water used inside a boiler for steam production
  • degree higher: steam - the vapor produced when water is heated in a boiler
