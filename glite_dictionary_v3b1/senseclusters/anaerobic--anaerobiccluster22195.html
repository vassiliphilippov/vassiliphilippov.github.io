anaerobic

adjective
not requiring oxygen
1.5 5.2
Pronunciation: /æn.ə.ˈroʊ.bɪk/ (AmE), /æn.ə.ˈrəʊ.bɪk/ (BrE)
(of a process or organism) not needing oxygen or happening in the absence of oxygen

Examples

Anaerobic metabolism
During intense exercise, the body may rely on anaerobic metabolism to produce energy.
Most bacteria in the soil are anaerobic and thrive where oxygen is absent.
Some medical conditions encourage the growth of anaerobic bacteria in wounds.
Some bacteria are anaerobic and thrive in environments without oxygen.
The treatment plant uses anaerobic digestion to break down waste.
Yeast can reproduce under anaerobic conditions, producing alcohol as a by-product.
