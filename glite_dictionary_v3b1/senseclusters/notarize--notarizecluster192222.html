notarize

verb
certify by a notary public
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊtəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈnəʊtəraɪz / (BrE)
to have a document officially certified by a notary public as authentic or legitimate

Examples

To notarize a document
She quickly notarized the letter while we waited in her office.
The lawyer will notarize your contract for a small fee.
We need to notarize these documents before submitting them to the court.
She arranged for her landlord to notarise the lease agreement.
The lawyer was asked to notarise the power of attorney before it could be submitted.
You must notarise these documents for them to be accepted by the embassy.
