refried beans

noun
beans cooked and fried, Mexican dish
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈfraɪd ˈbiːnz / (AmE), / ˌriːˈfraɪd ˈbiːnz / (BrE)
Dried beans that have been cooked, mashed, and then fried with lard or oil and seasonings, commonly used in Mexican cuisine.

Examples

Refried beans with cheese
Refried beans are a staple in many Mexican dishes.
He likes to spread refried beans on his toast for breakfast.
I ordered a side of refried beans with my burrito.
