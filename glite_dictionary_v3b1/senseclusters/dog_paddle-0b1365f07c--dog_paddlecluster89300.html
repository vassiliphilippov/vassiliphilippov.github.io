dog paddle

noun
basic swimming stroke imitating a dog
4.3 2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔɡ ˌpæd.əl / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡ ˌpæd.əl / (BrE)
an elementary swimming stroke in which the arms and legs move alternately, resembling the way a dog swims

Examples

A dog paddle stroke
He crossed the pool using a slow and clumsy dog paddle.
Many children learn to swim using the dog paddle before progressing to more advanced strokes.
The puppy instinctively began a dog paddle as soon as it hit the water.
