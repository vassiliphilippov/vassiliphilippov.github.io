ungentle

adjective
harsh or rough
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdʒɛntəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈdʒɛntəl / (BrE)
not gentle; harsh, rough, or lacking kindness in manner or action

Examples

Ungentle words
His ungentle criticism made the team feel discouraged.
The ungentle handling of the puppy upset the children.
She was surprised by his ungentle response to her question.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gentle - kind, mild, or soft in manner or action
  • synonym: harsh - unpleasantly rough or severe
  • synonym: rough - not gentle or smooth in manner or action
