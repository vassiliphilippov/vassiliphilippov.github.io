hinge on

verb
depend on; be contingent upon
5.1
Pronunciation: / hɪndʒ ɑn / (AmE), / hɪndʒ ɒn / (BrE)
(of a plan, situation, or outcome) to depend entirely on or be determined by something else.

Examples

To hinge on a decision
Her decision hinges on whether she gets the job offer.
Our future plans hinged on the outcome of the negotiations.
The success of the project will hinge on securing enough funding.

Related Terms

  • antonym: be independent of - not to depend on something
  • synonym: depend on - to rely on or be determined by something
  • synonym: rest on - (of a result or situation) to rely on something as a base
