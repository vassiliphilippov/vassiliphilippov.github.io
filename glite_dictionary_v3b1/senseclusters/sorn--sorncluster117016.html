sorn

verb
to beg for hospitality
Pronunciation: / sɔrn / (AmE), / sɔːn / (BrE)
to obtain food or lodging by imposing on others, especially without paying for it

Examples

To sorn a meal

Travelers in the past sometimes sorned shelter from generous villagers.
It is considered rude to sorn at every opportunity instead of contributing.
