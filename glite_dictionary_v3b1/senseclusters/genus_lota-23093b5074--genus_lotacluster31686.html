genus Lota

noun
genus of freshwater fish (burbot)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈloʊ.tə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈləʊ.tə / (BrE)
A genus of freshwater fish in the family Gadidae, containing only the burbot (Lota lota).
