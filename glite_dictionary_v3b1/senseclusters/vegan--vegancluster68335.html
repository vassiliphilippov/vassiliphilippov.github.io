vegan

adjective
not containing animal products (adjective)
Pronunciation: / ˈviːɡən / (AmE), / ˈviːɡən / (BrE)
(of food, products, or a diet) not containing any animal products.

Examples

Vegan food choices
Many restaurants now offer vegan options for customers with dietary restrictions.
This chocolate cake is entirely vegan, so it contains no eggs or dairy.
You can find vegan shampoos that are free from animal-derived ingredients.
