fatal

adjective
causing death
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈfeɪtəl / (BrE)
causing death; resulting in someone’s death.

Examples

Fatal accident
Failure to wear a seat belt can have fatal consequences.
The disease is fatal if left untreated.
The victim suffered a fatal wound during the accident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deadly - causing death or capable of causing death
  • antonym: nonfatal - not causing death
