mil

noun
former Cypriot monetary unit
Pronunciation: / mɪl / (AmE), / mɪl / (BrE)
a former Cypriot monetary unit equal to one thousandth of a pound, used before the adoption of the euro

Examples

A Cypriot mil
Few people remember using the mil since Cyprus switched to cents and then to the euro.
Many coins from Cyprus before 1983 had their value expressed in mils.
The old Cypriot one-pound note was subdivided into 1,000 mils.
