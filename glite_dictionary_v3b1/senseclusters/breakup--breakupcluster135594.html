breakup

noun
ending of a relationship
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪkˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈbreɪkʌp / (BrE)
the act or process of ending a relationship or association, especially between romantic partners or groups

Examples

A breakup with someone
After their breakup, they found it difficult to stay friends.
She struggled to move on after her recent breakup.
The breakup of the alliance led to increased tensions in the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: divorce - the legal dissolution of a marriage
  • antonym: reconciliation - the restoration of friendly relations
  • synonym: separation - the act of moving or being moved apart
