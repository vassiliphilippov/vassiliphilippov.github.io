crescendo

adjective
gradually increasing in loudness (music)
9.5 5.3
Pronunciation: /krə.ˈʃen.doʊ/ /; / /krɪ.ˈʃen.doʊ/ (AmE), /krə.ˈʃen.dəʊ/ /; / /krɪ.ˈʃen.dəʊ/ (BrE)
(of music) gradually increasing in loudness or intensity.
