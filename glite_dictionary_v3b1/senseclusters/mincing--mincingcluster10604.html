mincing

adjective
affectedly delicate or refined
9.4 7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɪnsɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person’s manner, speech, or movement) affectedly delicate or refined, often in a way that seems artificial.

Examples

Mincing steps

She walked with a mincing step, pretending to be more refined than she was.
