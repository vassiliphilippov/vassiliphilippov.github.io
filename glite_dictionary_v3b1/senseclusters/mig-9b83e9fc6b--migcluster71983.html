MiG

proper noun
Russian jet aircraft
Pronunciation: / miː ˈɡiː / (AmE), / miː ˈɡiː / (BrE)
a Russian-made military jet aircraft, especially those produced by the Mikoyan-Gurevich design bureau

Examples

A MiG fighter jet
The air force displayed its latest MiG at the international aviation expo.
During the conflict, several MiGs were seen patrolling the border region.
A retired pilot shared stories about flying a MiG during his military career.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bomber - a military aircraft designed to drop bombs
  • synonym: fighter jet - a fast military aircraft designed for air-to-air combat
