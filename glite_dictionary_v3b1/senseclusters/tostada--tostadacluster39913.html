tostada

noun
Mexican flat crispy tortilla with toppings
Pronunciation: / toʊˈstɑːdə /; / toʊˈstɑdə / (AmE), / təʊˈstɑːdə /; / tɒˈstɑːdə / (BrE)
a Mexican dish made with a flat, crispy fried tortilla topped with beans, meat, cheese, vegetables, or other fillings
