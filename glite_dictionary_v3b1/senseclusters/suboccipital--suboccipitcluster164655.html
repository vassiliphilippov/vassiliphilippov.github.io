suboccipital

adjective
beneath occipital bone
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbɑkˈsɪpɪtəl / (AmE), / ˌsʌbɒkˈsɪpɪtəl / (BrE)
located beneath or near the occipital bone at the back of the skull

Examples

Suboccipital region pain
The physical therapist applied gentle pressure to the suboccipital area to relieve the patient's headache.
Tension in the suboccipital muscles can contribute to neck pain and stiffness.
An injury to the suboccipital region may affect head movement and posture.
