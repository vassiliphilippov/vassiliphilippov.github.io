spindle

noun
a sharp-pointed device for holding objects
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪndəl / (AmE), / ˈspɪndl̩ / (BrE)
a slender, pointed device used for holding objects, especially paper, by impaling them

Examples

Paper spindle holder
Be careful not to prick your finger on the spindle in the office.
She placed the receipts on the spindle to keep them organized.
The old-fashioned spindle next to the cashier was filled with order slips.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skewer - a long, sharp pin used for holding things together
  • synonym: spike - a long, pointed metal rod
