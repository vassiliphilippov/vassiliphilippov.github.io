capriole

noun
a horse's upward leap with a hind-leg kick (dressage)
4.1 9.2
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈpriː.oʊl/ /; / /ˈkæp.ri.əl/ (AmE), /kə.ˈpriː.əʊl/ /; / /ˈkæp.ri.əl/ (BrE)
