capriole

noun
a horse's upward leap with a hind-leg kick (dressage)
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkæpriˌoʊl / (AmE), / ˈkæpriəʊl / (BrE)
In dressage, a leap performed by a horse in which it jumps upward and flicks its hind legs outward at the height of the jump.

Examples

Dressage capriole
Spectators were amazed when the Andalusian executed a stunning capriole in the arena.
The disciplined Lusitano performed a capriole perfectly during the classical dressage demonstration.
To master the capriole, the rider and horse must train together for years.
