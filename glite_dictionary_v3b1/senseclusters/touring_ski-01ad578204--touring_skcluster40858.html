touring ski

noun
ski designed for ski touring with special bindings
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtʊrɪŋ ski / (AmE), / ˈtʊərɪŋ ski / (BrE)
A ski specially designed for ski touring, equipped with bindings that allow the heel of the boot to be free for climbing and fixed for descending.

Examples

Touring skis with bindings
He adjusted the bindings on his touring skis before starting the uphill climb.
Many backcountry adventurers prefer touring skis for their versatility on varied terrain.
Modern touring skis combine lightweight materials with advanced binding systems.
