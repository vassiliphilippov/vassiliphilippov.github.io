overhead

noun
transparency sheet for projector
3.0 4.3
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ˈhed/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.ˈhed/ (BrE)
a sheet of transparent material for use with an overhead projector, typically for displaying text or images to an audience

Examples

Overhead projector sheet
Before digital presentations, we often used an overhead to show notes during meetings.
She wrote the answers on an overhead so everyone in the room could see.
The teacher placed the overhead on the projector to display the diagram to the class.
