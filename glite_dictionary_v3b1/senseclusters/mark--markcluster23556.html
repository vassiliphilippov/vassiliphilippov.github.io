mark

noun
score or grade received in tests or exams
Pronunciation: / mɑrk / (AmE), / mɑːk / (BrE)
a number, letter, or symbol indicating the quality of a student's work or performance, especially in tests or examinations

Examples

Students often worry about the marks they get in important tests.
Your mark in mathematics has improved this semester.
