ukulele

noun
a small four-stringed guitar
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːkəˈleɪli / (AmE), / ˌjuːkəˈleɪli / (BrE)
A small four-stringed guitar of Hawaiian origin, typically used to play light, cheerful music.

Examples

A ukulele player
He strummed his ukulele while singing a cheerful island tune.
She learned to play the ukulele during summer camp and performed at the talent show.
The sweet sound of the ukulele filled the beach as the sun set.
