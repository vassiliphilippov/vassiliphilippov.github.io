shut-eye

noun
informal term for sleep
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌtˌaɪ / (AmE), / ˈʃʌtˌaɪ / (BrE)
informal term meaning sleep or rest, especially at night.

Examples

Get some shut-eye
After driving all night, I really need some shut-eye before we continue our trip.
He could barely keep his eyes open and knew he should get some shut-eye.
She always says a little shut-eye in the afternoon keeps her energized.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sleep - the natural state of rest for the mind and body
