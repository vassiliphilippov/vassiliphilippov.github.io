garbage

noun
discarded or waste food
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrbɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbɪdʒ / (BrE)
waste material, especially unwanted or spoiled food that is discarded, typically from a kitchen

Examples

Kitchen garbage
Please remember to put the garbage outside before the truck arrives.
She separates food garbage from recyclables for composting.
The kitchen garbage needs to be taken out because it smells bad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rubbish - waste material; things that are no longer wanted
  • synonym: trash - things that are considered worthless or thrown away, especially in American English
