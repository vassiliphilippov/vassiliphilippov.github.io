jerrican

noun
liquid storage container
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛrɪˌkæn / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛrɪˌkæn / (BrE)
a flat-sided, portable container used for storing or transporting liquids, especially fuel or water

After the storm, volunteers distributed clean water in large jerricans to the affected families.
