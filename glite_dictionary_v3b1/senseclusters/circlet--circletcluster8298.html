circlet

noun
decorative metal band for the head
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːklɪt /; / ˈsɜrklət / (AmE), / ˈsɜːklɪt / (BrE)
a small ornamental band or ring, often made of metal, worn around the head or used as a decoration

Examples

A golden circlet
He crafted a golden circlet that fit perfectly on the performer's brow.
Medieval queens often adorned their heads with a jeweled circlet during formal ceremonies.
The princess wore a delicate circlet of silver leaves on her wedding day.
Each guest at the banquet received a silver circlet as a token.
She wore a delicate circlet of flowers around her head at the festival.
The ancient coins had a small circlet engraved in the center.
