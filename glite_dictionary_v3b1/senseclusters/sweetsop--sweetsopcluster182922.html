sweetsop

noun
tropical tree with sweet, scaly fruit
9.9 8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈswitˌsɑp / (AmE), / ˈswiːtˌsɒp / (BrE)
A tropical American tree (Annona squamosa) bearing sweet, pulpy fruit with a thick, scaly rind and shiny black seeds.
