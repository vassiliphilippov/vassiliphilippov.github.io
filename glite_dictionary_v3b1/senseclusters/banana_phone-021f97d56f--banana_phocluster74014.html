banana phone

noun
novelty phone shaped like a banana
Pronunciation: / bəˈnænə foʊn / (AmE), / bəˈnænə fəʊn / (BrE)
a toy shaped like a banana that functions as a phone, or a banana used humorously as a pretend phone
