runner

noun
baseball player on base
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnər / (AmE), / ˈrʌnə / (BrE)
a baseball player on the batting team who is on a base or attempting to reach a base

Examples

Baseball runner
If a runner leaves the base too early, he can be called out.
The runner slid safely into third base, avoiding the tag.
Two runners were on base when the batter hit a home run.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baserunner - a player on base in baseball
