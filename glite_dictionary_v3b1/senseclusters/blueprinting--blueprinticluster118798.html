blueprinting

noun
copying technical drawings
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːˌprɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌprɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of making copies of technical drawings or plans using the blueprint method, which produces white lines on a blue background

Examples

Blueprinting technical drawings
The architect oversaw the blueprinting of the new building's floor plans.
Advances in technology have largely replaced traditional blueprinting with digital methods.
Before digital printing, blueprinting was the standard way to reproduce engineering drawings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blueprint - a copy of a technical drawing made using the blueprint process
  • synonym: drafting - the act of drawing technical plans or designs
