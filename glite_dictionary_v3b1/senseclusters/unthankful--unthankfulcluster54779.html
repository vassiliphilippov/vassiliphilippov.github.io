unthankful

adjective
not feeling or showing gratitude
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈθæŋkfəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈθaŋkfəl / (BrE)
not feeling or showing gratitude; lacking thanks or appreciation

Examples

Unthankful attitude
His unthankful attitude disappointed those who had helped him succeed.
It is sad to see children grow up to be so unthankful to their parents.
She considered it unthankful to ignore the sacrifices others had made for her.

Related Terms

  • antonym: thankful - feeling or showing gratitude
  • synonym: ungrateful - not showing gratitude or appreciation
