nestling

noun
young bird not yet fledged
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛstlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnɛstlɪŋ / (BrE)
a young bird that has not yet developed feathers for flight and remains in the nest

Examples

A nestling bird
A tiny nestling chirped softly as its parents brought food to the nest.
It is crucial to avoid disturbing the nestlings during breeding season.
The biologist carefully measured the weight of each nestling before releasing it.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: fledgling - a young bird that has just acquired feathers for flight and left the nest
