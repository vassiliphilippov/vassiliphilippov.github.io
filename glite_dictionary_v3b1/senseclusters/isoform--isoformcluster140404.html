isoform

noun
variant of gene product
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsəˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈaɪsəʊˌfɔːm / (BrE)
a variant form of a protein or molecule, usually produced from the same gene, that has a slightly different structure but similar function to others of its type
