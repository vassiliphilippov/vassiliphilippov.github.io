stone

noun
crystalline mineral used as a gemstone
1.0
Pronunciation: / stoʊn / (AmE), / stəʊn / (BrE)
a crystalline mineral that can be cut and polished for use as jewelry; a gemstone

Examples

A diamond stone
Each earring features a small ruby stone that catches the light.
She admired the deep blue stone in her engagement ring.
The necklace was adorned with a sparkling stone set in gold.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gem - a precious or semi-precious stone, especially when cut and polished
  • synonym: jewel - a precious stone, typically as part of jewelry
