roadhead

noun
end of a road
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊdhɛd / (AmE), / ˈrəʊdhɛd / (BrE)
the farthest point that a road extends, especially in remote or undeveloped areas

Examples

The roadhead point
We left our car at the roadhead and continued the journey on foot.
The village is located just beyond the roadhead, accessible only by a narrow path.
Supplies were delivered as far as the roadhead before being carried by mule into the mountains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: terminus - the final point or end of a transportation route
  • synonym: trailhead - the starting point of a trail
