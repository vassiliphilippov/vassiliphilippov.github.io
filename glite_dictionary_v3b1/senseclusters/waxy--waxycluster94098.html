waxy

adjective
having pale, wax-like appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈwæksi / (AmE), / ˈwæksi / (BrE)
having a pale appearance resembling that of wax, especially in reference to skin or complexion

Examples

Waxy skin
After the long illness, his skin appeared almost waxy under the hospital’s harsh lights.
She grew waxy as the news sank in, her face drained of color.
The child’s waxy complexion alarmed the doctor, who ordered immediate tests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: livid - discolored from a bruise; pale or ashen
  • synonym: pale - having little color or intensity, especially of the face or skin
