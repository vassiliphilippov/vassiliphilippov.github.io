monobloc

noun
single-piece object
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑnəˌblɑk / (AmE), / ˈmɒnəˌblɒk / (BrE)
an object or structure made from a single piece of material, often used in engineering or furniture

Examples

A monobloc engine
The new engine uses a monobloc design to improve strength and reduce weight.
Many outdoor chairs are made as a single monobloc of molded plastic.
The sculpture was carved from a monobloc of marble, giving it a seamless appearance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modular - made from separate parts that can be connected
  • synonym: one-piece - made from a single piece or part
