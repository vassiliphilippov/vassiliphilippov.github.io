mung

verb
clean or transform data (computing)
9.9
Pronunciation: / mʌŋ / (AmE), / mʌŋ / (BrE)
(in computing) to clean, transform, or otherwise manipulate raw data or files, often in an ad hoc or unstructured way, to make them suitable for analysis or processing.

Examples

To mung data files
Data scientists mung raw logs before analysis to clean and organize the dataset.
She spent the afternoon munging the customer CSV file to fix inconsistent dates and missing IDs.
Automated tools can mung sensor readings so machine-learning models receive consistent inputs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clean - to remove errors, inconsistencies, or irrelevant parts from data
  • synonym: data wrangling - the process of cleaning and transforming raw data into a format suitable for analysis
  • synonym: preprocess - to perform initial processing on data to prepare it for further analysis
