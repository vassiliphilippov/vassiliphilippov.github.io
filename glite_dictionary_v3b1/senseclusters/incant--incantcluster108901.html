incant

verb
to chant ritual words
9.4
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkænt / (AmE), / ɪnˈkænt / (BrE)
to chant or recite words, especially as part of a spell or ritual

Examples

To incant a spell
The shaman began to incant mysterious words as the ceremony started.
During the ritual, the group would incant the same phrase in unison.
She incanted the spell softly, hoping for a positive outcome.
The sorcerer began to incant ancient words as the ritual started.
She tried to incant a protective spell before entering the dark forest.
Legends say that only the high priest can incant the sacred verses correctly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enchant - to put someone under a spell or charm
  • synonym: recite - to say aloud from memory, especially formally
