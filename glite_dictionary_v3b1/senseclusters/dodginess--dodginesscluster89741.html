dodginess

noun
riskiness or suspicion
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːdʒinəs / (AmE), / ˈdɒdʒinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being suspicious, risky, dishonest, or not trustworthy

Examples

She avoided the business because of the dodginess surrounding its financial practices.
There was a sense of dodginess about the offer that made me hesitate.
