dodginess

noun
riskiness or suspicion
8.5 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd…ëÀêd íin…ôs / (AmE), / Ààd…íd íin…ôs / (BrE)
the quality of being suspicious, risky, dishonest, or not trustworthy

Examples

The dodginess of deal
The dodginess of the deal made her hesitate before signing any papers.
He was concerned about the dodginess of the neighborhood after dark.
They ignored the dodginess of the old wiring until the lights started to spark dangerously.

She avoided the business because of the dodginess surrounding its financial practices.
There was a sense of dodginess about the offer that made me hesitate.
