footless statue

noun
statue without feet
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊtləs ˈstætʃu / (AmE), / ˈfʊtlɪs ˈstatjuː / (BrE)
a statue without feet, typically as a result of damage or ancient sculptural style

Examples

An ancient footless statue
Experts restored the ancient footless statue to preserve its remaining features.
Many artifacts from the temple, including a footless statue, were displayed in the gallery.
The footless statue stood in the center of the exhibition hall, attracting curious visitors.
