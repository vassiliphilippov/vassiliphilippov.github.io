clear

adjective
free from guilt or worry
Pronunciation: / klɪr / (AmE), / klɪə / (BrE)
free from guilt, blame, suspicion, or worry

Examples

She met the interviewer’s gaze with clear and honest eyes.
After months of investigation, the judge finally declared her clear of all charges.
Once the evidence was revealed, his name was clear of any suspicion.
The committee found the manager clear of wrongdoing in the case.
