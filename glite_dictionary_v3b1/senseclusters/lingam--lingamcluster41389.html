lingam

noun
Hindu symbol of Shiva
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪŋəm / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋəm / (BrE)
a symbol in Hinduism representing Shiva, typically in the form of a phallus, signifying generative power and divine energy

Examples

A lingam symbol
An ancient stone lingam was discovered during the archaeological excavation.
Many devotees pay homage to the lingam as a representation of divine creation.
The temple featured a sacred lingam in its central shrine.
