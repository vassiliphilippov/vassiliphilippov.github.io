Brahui

noun
member of Dravidian ethnic group in Pakistan
9.9
Pronunciation: / brəˈhuːi / (AmE), / brəˈhuːi / (BrE)
A member of a Dravidian ethnic group living mainly in Pakistan, particularly in Baluchistan.

Examples

Brahui people
Anthropologists have studied the unique heritage of the Brahui people.
Many Brahui live in the province of Baluchistan in Pakistan.
The Brahui have preserved their language despite being surrounded by non-Dravidian speakers.
