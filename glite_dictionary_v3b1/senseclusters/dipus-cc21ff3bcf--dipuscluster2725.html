Dipus

noun
genus of three-toed jerboas
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪpəs /; / ˈdaɪ.pəs / (AmE), / ˈdɪpəs /; / ˈdɪp.əs / (BrE)
a genus of jerboas (small jumping rodents) in the family Dipodidae, known for having three toes on each hind foot; the type genus of Dipodidae
