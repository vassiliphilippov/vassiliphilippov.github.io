exeunt

verb
to leave the stage
Pronunciation: / ˈɛksiˌʌnt / (AmE), / ˈɛksiˌʌnt /; / ˈɛksiːʌnt / (BrE)
used as a stage direction to show that two or more characters leave the stage together

Examples
