landscape painting

noun
art depicting natural scenery
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlænd.skeɪp ˌpeɪn.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlan.dskeɪp ˌpeɪn.tɪŋ / (BrE)
A genre of art that focuses on the depiction of natural scenery such as mountains, valleys, trees, rivers, and forests.

Examples

Landscape painting of mountains
He began his career with portraits but soon became renowned for his landscape painting.
Her favorite landscape painting shows rolling hills under a bright sunset.
The museum has a special exhibition of nineteenth-century landscape paintings.

Related Terms

  • antonym: portrait painting - a painting representing a person
  • synonym: seascape - a painting depicting the sea or ocean
