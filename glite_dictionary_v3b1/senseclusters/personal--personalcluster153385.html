personal

adjective
relating to grammatical person
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːsənl / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl / (BrE)
(Grammar) Relating to grammatical person, as in verb forms or pronouns that indicate the speaker, listener, or others.

Examples

Personal pronoun
He taught his students how to identify personal pronouns in a sentence.
In Latin, many verb endings are personal, reflecting the subject of the action.
The verb has different personal forms depending on who is performing the action.
