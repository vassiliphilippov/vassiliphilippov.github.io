reoxidize

verb
to oxidize again
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈɑːksɪˌdaɪz / (AmE), / ˌriːˈɒksɪdaɪz / (BrE)
to oxidize again or to undergo oxidation for a second or subsequent time

Examples

To reoxidize metal surface
If the cleaned surface is left exposed, it may reoxidize quickly in the air.
Chemists observed that the compound would reoxidize after being reduced in the experiment.
To prevent the metal from starting to reoxidize, a protective coating was applied immediately.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oxidize - to combine with oxygen or cause to undergo oxidation
  • antonym: reduce - to remove oxygen from a substance or gain electrons
