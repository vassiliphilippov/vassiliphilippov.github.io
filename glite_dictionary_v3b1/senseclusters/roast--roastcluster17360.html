roast

adjective
meat cooked by dry heat
1.2
Pronunciation: / roʊst / (AmE), / rəʊst / (BrE)
(Of meat) cooked by dry heat in an oven or over an open fire.

Examples

Roast chicken dinner
He prefers roast beef to boiled meat at family gatherings.
She served slices of roast lamb with potatoes and vegetables.
The chef prepared a delicious roast chicken for Sunday dinner.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boiled - cooked in water or other liquid at boiling temperature
  • antonym: grilled - cooked on a grill over direct heat
