ambulance chaser

noun
an unethical lawyer seeking accident cases
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈæmbjələns ˌtʃeɪsər /; / ˈæm.bjə.ləns ˌʧeɪ.sɚ / (AmE), / ˈæmbjələns ˌtʃeɪsə /; / ˈæm.bjə.ləns ˌtʃeɪ.sə / (BrE)
an informal, often negative term for a lawyer who looks for accident victims to encourage them to file lawsuits, usually for the lawyer's own benefit

Examples

Ambulance chasers seek clients
She refused to work for a firm with a reputation for being full of ambulance chasers.
Some people think hiring an ambulance chaser only worsens the suffering of accident victims.
The newspaper ran an expose on the practices of an ambulance chaser who pressured clients to sue.
After the crash, the media accused the attorney of being an ambulance chaser hungry for business.
His reputation as an ambulance chaser made clients and colleagues distrust him.
She resented being called an ambulance chaser just because she handled personal injury cases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: personal injury lawyer - a lawyer specializing in representing accident victims, usually in civil lawsuits
  • synonym: shyster - a lawyer acting in a dishonest or unethical way
