southern

adjective
of the southern U.S. or its culture
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌðərn / (AmE), / ˈsʌðən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the southern region of the United States, especially in terms of culture, traditions, or geography.

Examples

Southern hospitality
He spoke with a southern accent that immediately revealed his roots.
She grew up in a southern town known for its rich history and warmth.
The restaurant specializes in southern cuisine, offering dishes like fried chicken and cornbread.

Related Terms

  • antonym: northern - relating to or characteristic of the northern region of the United States
