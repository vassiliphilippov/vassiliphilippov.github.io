later

adjective
happening toward the end
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪtər /; / ˈleɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈleɪtə / (BrE)
happening or existing at a time after something else; subsequent in time or stage

Examples

Later years
In his later years, he focused on writing poetry instead of novels.
The later chapters of the book explore advanced topics in detail.
The symptoms became more severe in the later stages of the illness.
At a later stage in life, he decided to write his memoirs.
Many of his later paintings show a distinct change in style.
The second, later edition includes updated research and references.

Related Terms

  • antonym: earlier - happening or existing before the usual time or before something else
  • antonym: early - occurring at or near the beginning of a period or stage
  • synonym: subsequent - coming after something in time; following
