submaximal

adjective
below maximum
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈmæksɪməl / (AmE), / sʌbˈmæksɪməl / (BrE)
less than the maximum possible level, amount, or intensity

Examples

Submaximal heart rate
The doctor recommended exercising at a submaximal heart rate to avoid overexertion.
During training, athletes often perform submaximal tests to measure their endurance.
A submaximal effort can still lead to significant improvements in fitness over time.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: maximal - at the greatest possible level or amount
  • degree lower: minimal - at the smallest possible level or amount
