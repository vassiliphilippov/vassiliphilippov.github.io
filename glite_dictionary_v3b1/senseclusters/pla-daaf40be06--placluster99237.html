PLA

noun
biodegradable plastic
Pronunciation: / pi ɛl eɪ / (AmE), / piː ɛl eɪ / (BrE)
a biodegradable plastic called polylactic acid, often used in 3D printing and packaging

Examples

PLA filament
Many 3D printers use PLA because it is easy to work with and environmentally friendly.
The packaging was made from PLA, so it will break down naturally over time.
She prefers PLA filament for her projects due to its low environmental impact.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: PET - a common plastic used for bottles and packaging
  • degree higher: plastic - a synthetic material made from polymers
  • synonym: polylactic acid - a biodegradable thermoplastic made from renewable resources
