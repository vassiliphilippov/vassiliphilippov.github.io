solo

adverb
done without anyone else
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊloʊ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊləʊ / (BrE)
In a manner done without anyone else; alone or unaccompanied.

Examples

Travel solo
After years of flying with a copilot, she finally flew solo across the Atlantic.
He performed the entire piano recital solo, impressing everyone with his confidence.
The mountaineer decided to climb the peak solo, without any guides or companions.
