narrow-body aircraft

noun
single-aisle commercial airliner
Pronunciation: / ˈnæroʊ ˈbɑdi ˈɛrˌkræft / (AmE), / ˈnærəʊ ˈbɒdi ˈeəˌkrɑːft / (BrE)
a type of commercial airliner that has a single aisle, typically used for short to medium-haul flights

Examples

A narrow-body aircraft type
Many airlines use narrow-body aircraft for domestic and regional routes.
Planes like the Airbus A320 are popular models of narrow-body aircraft.
The new narrow-body aircraft can accommodate up to 200 passengers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: wide-body aircraft - a commercial airliner with two or more aisles
