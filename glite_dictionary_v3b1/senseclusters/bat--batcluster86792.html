bat

noun
flying nocturnal mammal
3.3 1.0
Pronunciation: /bæt/ (AmE), /bæt/ (BrE)
a nocturnal mammal with leathery wings, capable of flight, and adapted for echolocation, resembling a small mouse in appearance

Examples

A flying bat

Many people are fascinated by the ability of a bat to navigate in complete darkness using echolocation.
Researchers tracked the migration patterns of fruit bats across tropical forests.
