moderne

noun
streamlined modern style
8.4
Pronunciation: /moʊ.ˈdɜːrn/ /; / /mə.ˈdɜːrn/ (AmE), /məʊ.ˈdɜːn/ /; / /mə.ˈdɜːn/ (BrE)
a building, piece of furniture, or object designed in the streamlined, modern style popular in the early 20th century

Examples

A moderne building
The museum's new wing is a classic moderne with smooth curves and chrome details.
She bought a vintage moderne chair that perfectly matches her living room decor.
Many buildings from the 1930s feature the distinctive look of moderne architecture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: art deco - a decorative style of the 1920s and 1930s featuring bold geometric shapes
  • synonym: modernist - relating to modernism in art, architecture, or design
  • antonym: traditional - following older styles or customs
