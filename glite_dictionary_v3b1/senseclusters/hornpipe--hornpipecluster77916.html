hornpipe

noun
music for the hornpipe dance
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrnˌpaɪp / (AmE), / ˈhɔːnˌpaɪp / (BrE)
a lively piece of music written for the hornpipe dance, characterized by its distinctive rhythm.

Examples

Hornpipe music piece
She practiced the hornpipe on her violin for the upcoming dance competition.
The band played a lively hornpipe that got everyone on their feet.
The concert featured several traditional folk tunes, including a spirited hornpipe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reel - a type of lively folk dance and its music
  • antonym: waltz - a slow ballroom dance and its music in triple time
