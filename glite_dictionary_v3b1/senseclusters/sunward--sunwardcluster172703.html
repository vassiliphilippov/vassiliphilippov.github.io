sunward

adjective
toward the sun
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnwərd / (AmE), / ˈsʌnwəd / (BrE)
facing or directed toward the sun or the side that receives sunlight

Examples

Sunward side of the house
The plants on the sunward side of the house grew faster than those in the shade.
We moved our chairs to the sunward balcony to enjoy the afternoon light.
The sunward windows made the living room bright and warm all day.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shady - in or giving shade; away from the sun
  • synonym: sun-facing - oriented toward the sun
