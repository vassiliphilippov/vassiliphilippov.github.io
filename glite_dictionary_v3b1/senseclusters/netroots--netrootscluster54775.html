netroots

noun
online political activists
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛtˌruːts / (AmE), / ˈnɛtˌruːts / (BrE)
a community of political activists and commentators who organize and influence politics mainly through the internet, especially using blogs and social media

Examples

The netroots community
The netroots mobilized thousands of supporters for the candidate through online petitions and social media campaigns.
Many political movements now rely on the netroots to spread their message quickly and widely.
Thanks to the netroots, fundraising efforts for the campaign exceeded all expectations.
The netroots played a major role in organizing the protest through social media platforms.
Many politicians now pay close attention to the opinions of the netroots before making announcements.
A heated debate erupted among the netroots after the new policy was introduced online.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blogosphere - the collective community of all blogs and bloggers on the internet
  • antonym: establishment - the traditional or dominant groups in society or politics
  • synonym: grassroots - ordinary people in a movement, especially at a local level
