suspenseful atmosphere

noun
an environment full of tension and excitement
Pronunciation: / səˈspɛnsfəl ˈætməsˌfɪr / (AmE), / səˈspɛnsfʊl ˈætməsˌfɪə / (BrE)
an environment or mood that creates excitement and tension, often leaving people uncertain about what will happen next

Examples

A suspenseful atmosphere in a story
A suspenseful atmosphere can keep readers turning pages late into the night.
The director successfully created a suspenseful atmosphere throughout the entire film.
Thunder rumbled in the distance, adding to the suspenseful atmosphere in the old mansion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: relaxed atmosphere - a mood or environment that is calm and easy-going
  • synonym: tense atmosphere - a mood or environment that is full of tension or anxiety
