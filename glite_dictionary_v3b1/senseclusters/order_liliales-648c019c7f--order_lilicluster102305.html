order Liliales

noun
order of monocotyledonous plants
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər lɪˈlaɪəliːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə lɪˈleɪliːz / (BrE)
a taxonomic order of monocotyledonous flowering plants that includes families such as Amaryllidaceae, Liliaceae, and Iridaceae

Examples

Order Liliales plants
Members of the order Liliales are characterized by their parallel leaf venation and flower structure.
Recent studies have revised the classification within the order Liliales based on genetic evidence.
The order Liliales includes many well-known ornamental flowers such as lilies and tulips.
