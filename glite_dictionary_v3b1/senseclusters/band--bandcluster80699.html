band

noun
range of frequencies between limits
5.2
Pronunciation: / bænd / (AmE), / bænd / (BrE)
a range of frequencies between two specified limits, especially as used in radio communications or other technical fields

Examples

Radio band
Each television channel is assigned a specific frequency band.
Signals outside the designated band may cause interference.
The FM radio band ranges from 88 to 108 megahertz.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bandwidth - the width of a band of frequencies
  • synonym: spectrum - the entire range of wavelengths or frequencies of electromagnetic radiation
