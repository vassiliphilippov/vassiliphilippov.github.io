microsatellite

noun
small satellite or DNA repeat
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌsætəˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌsætəlaɪt / (BrE)
a very small artificial satellite or a short, repeating DNA sequence used in genetic studies

Examples

A microsatellite in orbit
Researchers use microsatellites to track genetic variation in animal populations.
A microsatellite can be launched to observe weather patterns from space.
The study identified several microsatellite markers linked to inherited diseases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: minisatellite - a longer repeating DNA sequence used in genetic studies
  • synonym: satellite - an object that orbits a planet or another body in space
