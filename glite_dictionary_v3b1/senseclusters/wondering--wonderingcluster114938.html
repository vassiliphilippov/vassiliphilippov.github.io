wondering

adjective
showing curiosity or questioning
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌndərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwʌndərɪŋ / (BrE)
showing curiosity or a desire to know or learn something; feeling inquisitive or speculative.

Examples

A wondering look
She gave him a wondering look, curious about what he would say next.
The children stood in wondering silence, trying to understand the magician’s trick.
With a wondering smile, Mia peered at the ancient artifact on the table.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inquisitive - showing curiosity or asking questions
  • synonym: speculative - engaged in, expressing, or based on conjecture rather than knowledge
