consignee

noun
person receiving delivered goods
9.5
Pronunciation: / kənˈsaɪˌni / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.sɪˈniː / (BrE)
a person or company to whom goods are sent or delivered, especially in shipping or trade

Examples

The consignee receives goods
As the designated consignee, she was responsible for inspecting the delivered merchandise.
The package could not be delivered until the consignee provided identification.
The shipping company must notify the consignee upon arrival of the goods.

Related Terms

  • antonym: consignor - the person or company sending goods to another
