rowdy

noun
noisy troublemaker
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊdi / (AmE), / ˈraʊdi / (BrE)
a person who behaves in a noisy, rough, or disruptive way, especially in a group

Examples

A rowdy at a party
The rowdy was escorted out of the stadium after starting a fight in the stands.
A group of rowdies disrupted the quiet evening with loud music and shouting.
Teachers struggled to control the rowdy during the school assembly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hooligan - a violent or aggressive person, especially in a group
  • antonym: peacemaker - a person who tries to create harmony and stop conflict
  • synonym: troublemaker - a person who causes problems or disturbances
