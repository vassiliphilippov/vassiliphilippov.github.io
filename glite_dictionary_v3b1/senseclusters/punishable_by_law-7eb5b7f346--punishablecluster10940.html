punishable by law

adjective
subject to legal penalties
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌnɪʃəbəl baɪ lɔ / (AmE), / ˈpʌnɪʃəbl baɪ lɔː / (BrE)
describing an act or offense that is subject to legal penalties

Examples

Punishable by law offense
Acts of discrimination in the workplace may be punishable by law in some countries.
Dumping toxic waste into the river without a permit is punishable by law.
Fraud is considered a serious crime and is punishable by law.
