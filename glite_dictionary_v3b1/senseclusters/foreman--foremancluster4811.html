foreman

noun
supervisor of workers
3.1 6.76/m
Pronunciation: / Ààf…îr.m…ôn / (AmE), / Ààf…îÀê.m…ôn / (BrE)
A person who supervises and directs a group of workers, especially in a factory or on a building site.

Examples

Factory foreman
As a foreman, he ensures that all projects are completed on schedule.
She discussed her safety concerns with the foreman on the construction site.
The foreman assigned the tasks for the day before the shift started.

Synonyms

  • foreperson
    4.0 0.01/m
    foreperson is a gender-neutral leader used in modern workplaces, while foreman names a male supervisor on a work site.
  • forewoman
    6.2 0.03/m
    forewoman is a female leader who runs a team on a site, while foreman names the male version of that role.
  • supervisor
    2.8 23/m
    supervisor checks and guides workers to meet standards in many settings, while foreman often leads hands-on on a building or factory site.
  • gaffer
    8.1 0.04/m
    gaffer is an informal British word for a site boss or foreman, while foreman is widely used in industrial contexts.
  • overseer
    4.8 1.89/m
    overseer is a formal, broader manager who may direct whole departments, while foreman usually supervises a specific crew on site.
