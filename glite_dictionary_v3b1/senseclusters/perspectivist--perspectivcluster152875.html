perspectivist

adjective
using perspective in art
Pronunciation: / pərˈspɛktɪvɪst / (AmE), / pəˈspɛktɪvɪst / (BrE)
relating to or using the techniques of perspective in visual art to show depth and spatial relationships

Examples

Perspectivist painting style
The artist's perspectivist approach made the landscape appear almost three-dimensional.
Many Renaissance painters were known for their perspectivist techniques in depicting space.
Her perspectivist drawings give viewers a strong sense of depth and distance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perspectival - relating to perspective or point of view in art or philosophy
