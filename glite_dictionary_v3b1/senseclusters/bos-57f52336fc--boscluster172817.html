Bos

noun
genus including wild and domestic cattle
7.7
Pronunciation: /bɑːs/ (AmE), /bɒs/ (BrE)
a genus of large ruminant mammals that includes wild and domestic cattle, such as oxen, cows, and yaks

Examples

Bos animals
Biologists classify domestic cows and yaks in the genus Bos.
The extinct aurochs is a member of the genus Bos and the ancestor of modern cattle.
There are several species within the genus Bos, including the gaur and the banteng.
