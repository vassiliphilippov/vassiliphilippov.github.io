pill bottle

noun
container for holding pills
Pronunciation: / pɪl ˈbɑtɫ̩ / (AmE), / pɪl ˈbɒtəl / (BrE)
a small container, usually made of plastic or glass, used for holding and dispensing pills or tablets

Examples

A pill bottle
He accidentally dropped the pill bottle, and the tablets scattered across the floor.
She took her prescription out of the pill bottle and drank a glass of water.
The label on the pill bottle listed important dosage information and warnings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: medicine bottle - a bottle for storing liquid medicine or pills
