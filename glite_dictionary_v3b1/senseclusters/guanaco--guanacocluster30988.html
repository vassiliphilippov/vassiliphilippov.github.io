guanaco

noun
a wild South American mammal related to the llama
9.3 0.07/m
Pronunciation: / …°w…ôÀàn…ëÀêko ä / (AmE), / …°waÀàn…ëÀêk…ô ä / (BrE)
A wild South American mammal related to the llama, found in arid regions and grasslands, with a slender body and soft wool.

Examples

A herd of guanacos
A herd of guanacos grazed quietly in the Patagonian steppe.
The coat of the guanaco is prized for its softness and warmth.
Unlike llamas, guanacos are wild animals and are not domesticated.
