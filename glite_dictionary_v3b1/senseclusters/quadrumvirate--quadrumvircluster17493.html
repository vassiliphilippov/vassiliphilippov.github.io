quadrumvirate

noun
group of four men
Pronunciation: / kwɑːˈdrʌm.vəˌrət / (AmE), / kwɒˈdrʌm.vɪ.rət / (BrE)
A group or association of four men, especially in a political or administrative context.

Examples

A quadrumvirate of leaders
A quadrumvirate of generals seized control after the coup.

The board was governed by a quadrumvirate during the transitional period.
