one for the road

noun
final drink before leaving
5.0
Pronunciation: / wʌn fər ðə roʊd / (AmE), / wʌn fə ðə rəʊd / (BrE)
a final drink, usually alcoholic, taken shortly before departing

Examples

Have one for the road
He ordered one for the road even though he was running late.
It's getting late, but I think I'll have one for the road with you.
Shall we have one for the road before leaving the pub?

Related Terms

  • synonym: nightcap - an alcoholic drink taken late in the evening before going to bed
