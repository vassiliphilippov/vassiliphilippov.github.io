unguarded

adjective
not cautious or protected
1.0 4.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡɑrdɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡɑːdɪd / (BrE)
not showing caution or watchfulness; lacking protection or defense; open to risk or being careless.

Examples

Unguarded comment

She spoke in an unguarded moment, revealing more than she intended.
The door was left unguarded, allowing the thief easy access.
