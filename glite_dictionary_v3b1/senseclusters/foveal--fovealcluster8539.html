foveal

adjective
of a small anatomical pit
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊviəl / (AmE), / ˈfəʊviəl / (BrE)
relating to a fovea, especially the small central area of the retina responsible for sharp vision

Examples

Foveal vision area
The doctor noted a foveal indentation on the patient's retina during the examination.
Researchers studied the foveal region to better understand visual acuity in humans.
A foveal depression was visible on the bone after the injury.
Damage to the foveal region can cause significant loss of sharp vision.
Researchers studied foveal responses to different light conditions in the eye.
Reading small text relies heavily on foveal vision for clarity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foveate - having a small pit or depression
  • antonym: peripheral - relating to the outer area of the retina or vision
  • synonym: retinal - relating to the retina of the eye
