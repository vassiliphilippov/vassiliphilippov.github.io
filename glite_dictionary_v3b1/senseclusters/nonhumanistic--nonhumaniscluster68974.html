nonhumanistic

adjective
not human-centered
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnhjuːməˈnɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnhjuːməˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
not centered on human beings or human values; not relating to humanism

Examples

The scientist proposed a nonhumanistic approach to studying animal intelligence.
Some philosophies take a nonhumanistic view, emphasizing the importance of nature as a whole.
The documentary presented a nonhumanistic perspective on environmental change, focusing on ecosystems rather than people.
The organization adopted a nonhumanistic philosophy that prioritized technology over individual well-being.
Some critics argue that the policy is too nonhumanistic and ignores people's needs.
The film presents a nonhumanistic vision of society, focusing on machines rather than humans.
