lookahead

noun
future move analysis
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈlʊkəˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈlʊkəˌhɛd / (BrE)
the process of considering or simulating possible future moves or states to guide decisions, especially in algorithms or games

Examples

Chess lookahead
The chess program uses lookahead to evaluate the consequences of each possible move.
Effective lookahead can help a company anticipate market changes and stay competitive.
In computer science, lookahead is essential for optimizing decision-making algorithms.

  • synonym: planning - the act of making decisions about future actions
