uneconomically

adverb
wastefully; inefficiently
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌʌniːkəˈnɑːmɪkli / (AmE), / ˌʌniːkəˈnɒmɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that does not save money or resources; inefficiently or wastefully

Examples

To operate uneconomically
The company operated uneconomically for years before finally closing down.
If you use electricity uneconomically, your bills will be much higher.
The project was managed uneconomically, leading to unnecessary expenses.

Related Terms

  • antonym: economically - in a way that saves money or resources
  • synonym: inefficiently - in a way that wastes resources or effort
  • synonym: wastefully - in a way that uses more than necessary
