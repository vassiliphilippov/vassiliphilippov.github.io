trustworthiness

noun
quality of being trustworthy
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌstwɚðinɪs / (AmE), / ˈtrʌstwəːðɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being reliable and deserving of trust and confidence

Examples

High trustworthiness
His trustworthiness made him an ideal candidate for the job.
People often question the trustworthiness of news found on social media.
The company prided itself on its trustworthiness and integrity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reliability - the quality of being trustworthy or of consistently performing well
  • antonym: untrustworthiness - the lack of being trustworthy
