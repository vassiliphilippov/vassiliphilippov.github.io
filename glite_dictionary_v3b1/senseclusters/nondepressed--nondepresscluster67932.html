nondepressed

adjective
not affected by depression
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑndɪˈprɛst / (AmE), / ˌnɒndɪˈprɛst / (BrE)
not experiencing feelings of sadness or depression; not affected by depression

Examples

Nondepressed patients
The research focused on nondepressed patients to compare their responses with those who had depression.
After several months of therapy, she was considered nondepressed by her doctor.
The nondepressed group showed higher levels of motivation in the study.

Related Terms

  • antonym: depressed - feeling sad or affected by depression
  • synonym: healthy - in good physical or mental condition
