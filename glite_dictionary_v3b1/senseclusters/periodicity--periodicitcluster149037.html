periodicity

noun
the state of recurring at regular intervals
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪriəˈdɪsəti / (AmE), / ˌpɪəriəˈdɪsəti / (BrE)
the quality of recurring or happening at regular intervals

Examples

Natural periodicity
Circadian rhythms display a natural periodicity of about twenty-four hours.
The periodicity of the comet's appearance fascinated astronomers for generations.
We observed a clear periodicity in the data, with activity rising every four hours.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frequency - the rate at which something occurs or is repeated
