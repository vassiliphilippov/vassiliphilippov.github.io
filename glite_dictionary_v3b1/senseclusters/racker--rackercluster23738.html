racker

noun
person who arranges
Pronunciation: / ˈrækər / (AmE), / ˈrækə / (BrE)
a person or device that arranges, organizes, or places items on a rack or in order

Examples

Bottle racker
The racker carefully placed each wine bottle on the shelf in the correct order.
In the laboratory, a racker is responsible for organizing test tubes after experiments.
The company hired a racker to keep the warehouse shelves neat and accessible.

  • synonym: organizer - a person who arranges or puts things in order
