comatic

adjective
in a coma
Pronunciation: / koʊˈmætɪk / (AmE), / kəʊˈmætɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by a coma, a state of deep unconsciousness

Examples

Comatic patient
After the accident, the doctors described his condition as comatic and closely monitored his vital signs.
The patient remained comatic for several days before finally regaining consciousness.
Medical staff are trained to care for individuals in a comatic state.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comatose - in a state of deep unconsciousness
  • antonym: conscious - awake and aware of one's surroundings
