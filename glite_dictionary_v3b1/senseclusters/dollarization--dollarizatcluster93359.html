dollarization

noun
use of foreign currency
7.2
Pronunciation: /dɑː.lə.rɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /dɑː.lə.rə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /dɒl.ə.rɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /dɒl.ə.rə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the use of the US dollar or another foreign currency in place of the local currency, either officially or unofficially
