lean

noun
slant or tilt from vertical
Pronunciation: / lin / (AmE), / liːn / (BrE)
a deviation of a line or surface from the vertical; a slant or tilt

Examples

The lean of a tower
The architect measured the lean of the old building before starting the renovations.
The ship's sudden lean startled the passengers on board.
They were concerned about the lean of the tree after the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inclination - a slope or slant; deviation from vertical
  • synonym: tilt - a sloping position or movement; inclination
