vena cephalica

noun
large vein on the outer arm
8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈviː.nə sə.fə.ˈlɪk.ə/ (AmE), /ˈviː.nə sə.fə.ˈlɪk.ə/ (BrE)
a large vein along the outer side of the arm that drains into the axillary vein

Examples

The vena cephalica vein
Damage to the vena cephalica can complicate blood draws or intravenous injections.
During the procedure, the surgeon located the vena cephalica for venous access.
The vena cephalica is often visible under the skin on the outer side of the forearm.
