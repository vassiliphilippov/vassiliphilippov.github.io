expeditionary

adjective
intended for foreign military operations
3.7
Pronunciation: / ɪkˌspɛdɪˈʃɛneri / (AmE), / ɪkˌspɛdɪˈʃənəri / (BrE)
(of military forces) intended for or relating to operations in foreign territories.

Examples

Expeditionary military forces
Many soldiers volunteered for the expeditionary unit during the conflict.
The country sent an expeditionary force to support its allies overseas.
The government announced plans for a new expeditionary command specializing in rapid deployments.
