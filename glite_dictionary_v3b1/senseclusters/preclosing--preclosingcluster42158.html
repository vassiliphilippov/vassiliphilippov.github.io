preclosing

adjective
before transaction closing
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈkloʊzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈkləʊzɪŋ / (BrE)
happening or arranged before the official closing of a transaction, especially in real estate or finance

Examples

Preclosing checklist
The preclosing checklist must be completed before signing the final documents.
A preclosing inspection ensures that all repairs have been made before the sale is finalized.
Lawyers reviewed the preclosing documents to confirm all terms were correct.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postclosing - happening after the official closing of a transaction
  • synonym: preliminary - happening before the main event or activity
