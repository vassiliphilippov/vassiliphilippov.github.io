weightlessly

adverb
casually, without concern
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈweɪt.ləs.liː/ /; / /ˈweɪt.lɪs.liː/ (AmE), /ˈweɪt.ləs.liː/ /; / /ˈweɪt.lɪs.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that shows little emotional burden, seriousness, or concern; casually or without worry

Examples

To speak weightlessly
She spoke weightlessly about her mistakes, showing no sign of regret.
He laughed weightlessly, as if nothing could trouble him.
The news was delivered weightlessly, making it seem unimportant to everyone listening.

Related Terms
