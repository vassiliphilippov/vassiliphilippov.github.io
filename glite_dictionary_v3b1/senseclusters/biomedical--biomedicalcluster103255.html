biomedical

adjective
relating to science applied to medicine
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊˈmɛd.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.əʊˈmɛd.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to the application of the principles and methods of the natural sciences, especially biology, to medicine or healthcare.

Examples

Biomedical science
Many advances in biomedical science have improved patient care over the last decade.
She works in a biomedical research lab developing new treatments for infectious diseases.
The university offers a degree program in biomedical engineering.
