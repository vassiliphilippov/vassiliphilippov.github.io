delightfulness

noun
pleasant quality
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlaɪtfəlnəs / (AmE), / dɪˈlaɪtfəlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being very pleasant, charming, or giving great pleasure

Examples

The delightfulness of garden
The delightfulness of the garden made it a favorite spot for afternoon walks.
Everyone commented on the delightfulness of the meal at the new restaurant.
She spoke with such delightfulness that the whole room felt brighter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: charm - a quality that makes someone or something attractive or pleasant
  • synonym: enjoyment - the state or process of taking pleasure in something
  • antonym: unpleasantness - the quality of being disagreeable or not enjoyable
