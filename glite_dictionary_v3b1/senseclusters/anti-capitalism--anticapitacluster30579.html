anti-capitalism

noun
opposition to capitalism
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.tiˈkæp.ɪ.təˌlɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˌantiˈkap.ɪ.təl.ɪ.zəm / (BrE)
A political or social ideology or movement that opposes capitalism as an economic system.

Examples

Anti-capitalism movement
Debates about anti-capitalism often arise in discussions of global economic inequality.
Her writings explore anti-capitalism through both historical and contemporary perspectives.
The rally was organized to promote anti-capitalism and support for workers' rights.

Related Terms

  • antonym: capitalism - an economic system based on private ownership and free markets
  • degree higher: socialism - an economic system in which production is owned collectively or by the government
