blood sport

noun
sport involving animal killing or injury
Pronunciation: / blʌd spɔrt / (AmE), / blʌd spɔːt / (BrE)
a sport that involves the killing or injuring of animals, typically for entertainment, such as hunting or bullfighting

Examples

Blood sport hunting animals
He wrote an article condemning blood sport and advocating for the protection of wildlife.
Many animal rights groups have called for a ban on blood sport such as fox hunting.
The public's attitude toward blood sport has changed in recent decades, with growing concern for animal welfare.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hunting - the activity of chasing and killing wild animals
