St. Vitus

proper noun
Christian martyr and patron saint of epilepsy
9.7
Pronunciation: /seɪnt ˈvaɪ.təs/ /; / /seɪnt ˈvɪ.təs/ (AmE), /seɪnt ˈvaɪ.təs/ /; / /seɪnt ˈvɪ.təs/ (BrE)
A Christian martyr and saint who is venerated as the patron of those suffering from epilepsy and Sydenham's chorea (St. Vitus' dance).
