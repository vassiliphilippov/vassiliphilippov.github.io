flusher

noun
hunting dog for flushing
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈflʌʃər / (AmE), / ˈflʌʃə / (BrE)
a hunting dog trained to drive birds or small animals out of hiding so they can be shot or caught

Examples

Bird flusher
The flusher darted through the underbrush, sending a flock of quail into the air.
Hunters often rely on a skilled flusher to locate hidden pheasants in dense grass.
Their spaniel is an excellent flusher and always finds birds quickly during hunts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pointer - a hunting dog that indicates the presence of game by pointing
  • synonym: retriever - a hunting dog trained to find and bring back game
