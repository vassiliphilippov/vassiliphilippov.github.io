unblossomed

adjective
not yet opened
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈblɑːsəmd / (AmE), / ʌnˈblɒsəmd / (BrE)
not having opened into a flower or not yet fully developed

Examples

Unblossomed flower buds
The unblossomed roses lined the garden path, waiting for the warmth of spring.
She admired the unblossomed cherry trees, knowing they would soon be covered in pink flowers.
His talent was still unblossomed, but his teachers saw great potential in him.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blossomed - having opened into a flower or reached full development
  • synonym: budding - beginning to develop or show promise
