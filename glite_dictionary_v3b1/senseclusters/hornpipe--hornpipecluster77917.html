hornpipe

noun
obsolete ancient woodwind instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrnˌpaɪp / (AmE), / ˈhɔːnˌpʌɪp / (BrE)
an ancient single-reed woodwind instrument, usually made of bone or horn, now obsolete

Examples

Ancient hornpipe instrument
Archaeologists discovered a hornpipe at the ancient settlement site.
References to the hornpipe can be found in several medieval manuscripts.
The shepherd played a hornpipe as the sun set over the hills.
