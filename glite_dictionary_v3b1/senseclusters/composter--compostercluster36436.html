composter

noun
ticket stamping machine
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːm.poʊ.stər/ (AmE), /ˈkɒm.pəʊ.stə/ (BrE)
a machine used to mark or validate passenger tickets by stamping or punching them

Examples

Ticket composter
The conductor slid each paper ticket into the composter to stamp the travel date and time.
Before boarding the train, passengers must use the composter to validate their tickets.
A broken composter at the station caused delays as staff had to check tickets manually.

Related Terms

  • synonym: punch - a tool for making holes in tickets as a form of validation
  • synonym: validator - a device that confirms the validity of a ticket
