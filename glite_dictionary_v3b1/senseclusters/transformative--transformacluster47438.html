transformative

adjective
adding new meaning
7.2
Pronunciation: / trænsˈfɔːrmətɪv / (AmE), / trænsˈfɔːmətɪv / (BrE)
in copyright law, describing a use that adds new expression, meaning, or message to a copyrighted work, potentially qualifying as fair use

Examples

Transformative use
The judge ruled that the artist's remix was transformative and did not infringe on the original copyright.
A transformative use can help support a fair use defense in court.
Legal experts debated whether the documentary's use of film clips was truly transformative.

Related Terms

  • antonym: derivative - based on or developed from something else
  • antonym: original - completely new and not derived from something else
