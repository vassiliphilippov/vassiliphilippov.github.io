mocking thrush

noun
American songbird able to mimic other birds
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑkɪŋ ˌθrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈmɒkɪŋ ˌθrʌʃ / (BrE)
a thrush-like American songbird known for its ability to mimic the songs of other birds

Examples

A mocking thrush bird
A mocking thrush can imitate the calls of other birds remarkably well.
Researchers observed a mocking thrush mimicking the song of a finch.
The Galapagos Islands are home to several unique species of mocking thrush.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mockingbird - an American songbird also known for mimicking other birds
