urucum

noun
red dye plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌuːruːˈkuːm / (AmE), / ˌuːruːˈkuːm / (BrE)
a tropical plant whose seeds are used to produce a red dye or spice, also known as annatto

Examples

Urucum plant seeds
The chef added urucum to give the dish a rich color.
Farmers harvest urucum seeds to sell as a natural food coloring.
Traditional communities use urucum for both cooking and body paint.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annatto - a natural red-orange dye or spice from the seeds of the achiote tree
