oceanside

adjective
near the ocean
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊʃənˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈəʊʃənˌsaɪd / (BrE)
located next to or very close to the ocean

Examples

Oceanside hotel room
They enjoyed breakfast on the oceanside terrace, listening to the waves.
The hotel offers stunning oceanside views from every room.
We took a long walk along the oceanside path at sunset.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inland - located away from the coast or ocean
  • synonym: seaside - located by or near the sea
