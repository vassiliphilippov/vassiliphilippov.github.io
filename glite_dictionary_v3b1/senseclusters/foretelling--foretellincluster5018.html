foretelling

noun
prediction of the future, especially supernaturally
Pronunciation: / fɔrˈtɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / fɔːˈtɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of predicting what will happen in the future, or a statement about what will happen

Examples

A foretelling of events
Skeptics doubted the authenticity of his foretelling at the village fair.
Some people claim to possess the art of foretelling through reading tarot cards.
The ancient priestess was famous for her foretelling of disasters and victories.
Her foretelling of the coming storm turned out to be correct.
Many cultures value the art of foretelling to guide important decisions.
The fortune teller's foretelling was that he would travel to distant lands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prediction - a statement about what will happen in the future
  • synonym: prophecy - a prediction of what will happen in the future, especially one made under divine inspiration
