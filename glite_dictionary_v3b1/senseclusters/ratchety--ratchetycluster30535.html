ratchety

adjective
irritable or cranky
9.3
Pronunciation: /ˈrætʃ.ə.tiː/ /; / /ˈrætʃ.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /ˈrætʃ.ə.tiː/ /; / /ˈrætʃ.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
bad-tempered, irritable, and easily annoyed, especially in a way that shows impatience or crankiness

Examples

Ratchety mood
After a long day without rest, she became quite ratchety and snapped at her friends.
The children knew to avoid their ratchety neighbor when he was working in the yard.
Lack of coffee in the morning always makes me feel a bit ratchety at work.
