hexagonally

adverb
in a hexagonal way
Pronunciation: / hɛkˈsæɡənəli / (AmE), / hɛkˈsæɡənəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or has the shape of a hexagon

Examples

Arranged hexagonally
The snowflakes are arranged hexagonally under the microscope.
The tiles were laid out hexagonally to create a unique pattern on the floor.
Some minerals crystallize hexagonally, giving them their distinctive shapes.
