pre-holiday

noun
time before holiday
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈhɑːləˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈhɒləˌdeɪ / (BrE)
the period of time just before a holiday, often marked by preparations or anticipation

Examples

The pre-holiday period
The store was crowded during the pre-holiday rush as people shopped for gifts.
Many families start their pre-holiday traditions a week before Christmas.
She felt stressed by all the pre-holiday preparations at work and at home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: holiday season - the period around and including a holiday
  • antonym: post-holiday - the period immediately after a holiday
