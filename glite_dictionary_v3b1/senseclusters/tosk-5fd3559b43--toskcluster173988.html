Tosk

noun
Albanian dialect spoken in southern Albania
Pronunciation: / tɑsk / (AmE), / tɒsk / (BrE)
the dialect of Albanian spoken in southern Albania and in some areas of Greece and Italy

Examples

Tosk dialect
Linguists have studied the Tosk dialect to better understand the history of the Albanian language.
Many southern Albanians speak Tosk rather than Gheg in their daily conversations.
The influence of Tosk can be seen in the official standard of Albanian today.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Gheg - the Albanian dialect spoken in the north of Albania
