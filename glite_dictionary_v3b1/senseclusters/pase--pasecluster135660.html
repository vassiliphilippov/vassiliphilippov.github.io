pase

noun
entry permit
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːseɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːseɪ / (BrE)
an official or informal pass or permit that allows someone to enter a place or attend an event

Examples

A pase card
You will need a pase to enter the concert hall tonight.
The guard checked each visitor's pase before letting them through the gate.
Without a pase, you cannot attend the private event.
