aeroelastic

adjective
airflow-structure interaction
Pronunciation: / ˌɛroʊɪˈlæstɪk / (AmE), / ˌeərəʊɪˈlæstɪk / (BrE)
relating to the interaction between aerodynamic forces and the flexibility of structures, especially in aircraft

Examples

Aeroelastic forces
The engineers analyzed the aeroelastic behavior of the bridge under strong winds.
Modern aircraft design must consider aeroelastic effects to ensure safety and performance.
A failure to account for aeroelastic forces can lead to dangerous vibrations in airplane wings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aerodynamic - relating to the movement of air and its interaction with solid objects
  • synonym: structural - relating to the structure or framework of something
