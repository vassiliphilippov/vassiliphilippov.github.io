perforation

noun
line of small holes for easy tearing
5.4 3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpɝːfəˈreɪʃən /; / ˌpɝː.fəˈreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌpɜːfəˈreɪʃən /; / ˌpɜː.fəˈreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a row or series of small holes made in paper or another material, usually to make it easier to tear or separate

The doctor discovered a perforation in the patient's colon during surgery.
