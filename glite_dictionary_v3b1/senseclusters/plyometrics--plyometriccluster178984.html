plyometrics

noun
explosive muscle exercises
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌplaɪoʊˈmɛtrɪks / (AmE), / ˌplaɪəʊˈmɛtrɪks / (BrE)
a type of exercise that involves rapid and repeated stretching and contracting of muscles to increase power

Examples

Plyometrics training exercises
Many athletes include plyometrics in their training to improve jumping ability.
The coach demonstrated several plyometrics to help the team build explosive strength.
Incorporating plyometrics into your workout can boost your speed and agility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calisthenics - exercises using body weight for strength and flexibility
  • antonym: isometrics - exercises involving muscle contraction without movement
