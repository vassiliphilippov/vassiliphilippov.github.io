In a way that is fair and not influenced by personal feelings or interests; without bias.

Examples

To judge impartially
As a teacher, she tries to treat all of her students impartially.
Reporters are expected to cover the news impartially without showing personal bias.
The judge listened to both sides impartially before making a decision.
