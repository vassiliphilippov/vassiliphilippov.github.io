boosterism

noun
enthusiastic promotion
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːstərˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈbuːstəˌɪzəm / (BrE)
enthusiastic promotion or support of a person, place, or idea, often to encourage growth or success

Examples

City boosterism efforts
The mayor's boosterism helped attract new businesses to the city.
Local boosterism was evident in the campaign to host the international sports event.
Her boosterism for the new project inspired others to get involved.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advocacy - public support for or recommendation of a cause or policy
  • antonym: criticism - the expression of disapproval or finding faults
  • synonym: promotion - the act of supporting or encouraging something
