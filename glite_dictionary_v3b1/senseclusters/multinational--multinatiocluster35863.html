multinational

noun
company in many countries
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltɪˈnæʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈnæʃənəl / (BrE)
a company or organization that operates in several different countries

Examples

A multinational operates
Many multinationals have offices in major cities across the globe.
The government introduced new regulations for multinationals operating within its borders.
Working for a multinational can offer opportunities to travel and experience different cultures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corporation - a large company or group of companies acting as a single organization
  • antonym: domestic company - a company that operates only in its home country
