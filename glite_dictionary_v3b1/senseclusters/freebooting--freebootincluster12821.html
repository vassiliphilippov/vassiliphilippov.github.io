freebooting

adjective
piratical or plundering
Pronunciation: / ˈfriˌbuːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌbuːtɪŋ / (BrE)
engaging in or characteristic of piracy, plundering, or lawless opportunism

Examples

Freebooting pirates
The freebooting adventurers sailed the seas in search of treasure and excitement.
His freebooting attitude made him both admired and feared among his peers.
The company was accused of freebooting tactics in its aggressive market expansion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: honest - acting in a truthful and lawful way
  • synonym: lawless - not obeying or controlled by laws
  • synonym: piratical - relating to or characteristic of pirates
