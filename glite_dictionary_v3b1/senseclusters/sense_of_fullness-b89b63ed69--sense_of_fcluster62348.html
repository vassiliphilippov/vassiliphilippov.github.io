sense of fullness

noun
feeling of being full after eating
1.4 3.2
Pronunciation: /sens əv ˈfʊl.nəs/ /; / /sens ə ˈfʊl.nəs/ (AmE), /sens əv ˈfʊl.nəs/ /; / /sens ə ˈfʊl.nəs/ (BrE)
the feeling of being full or satisfied after eating, often resulting in a lack of desire to eat more
