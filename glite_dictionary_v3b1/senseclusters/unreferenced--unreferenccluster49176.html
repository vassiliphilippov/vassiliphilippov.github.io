unreferenced

adjective
not pointed to
7.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɛfərənst / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɛfərənst / (BrE)
not pointed to or accessed by any pointer or reference, making it inaccessible in a program.

Examples

Unreferenced variable
If a variable is unreferenced, it may be removed by the garbage collector.
The function left several objects unreferenced, causing a memory leak in the application.
After the process ended, the allocated memory remained unreferenced and was not reclaimed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: referenced - pointed to or accessed by a pointer or reference
