unseeingly

adverb
without noticing
8.2
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈsiː.ɪŋ.lɪ/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈsiː.ɪŋ.lɪ/ (BrE)
in a way that does not notice or register what is being seen, as if looking without really seeing

Examples

Look unseeingly ahead
He walked unseeingly down the street, lost in his own thoughts.
She gazed unseeingly at the painting, her mind elsewhere.
The student stared unseeingly at the board, not hearing the teacher's question.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absently - in a way that shows you are not paying attention
  • antonym: attentively - in a way that shows you are paying close attention
