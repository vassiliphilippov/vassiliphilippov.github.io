postmenstrual

adjective
after menstruation
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstˈmɛnstruəl / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈmɛnstruəl / (BrE)
occurring or relating to the period of time after menstruation has ended

Examples

Postmenstrual phase
Some women feel more energetic during the postmenstrual phase of their cycle.
The doctor explained that certain symptoms may appear in the postmenstrual period.
Tracking postmenstrual changes can help identify patterns in overall health.

Related Terms
