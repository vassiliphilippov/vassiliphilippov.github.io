discharged

adjective
having lost your job
2.9
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈtʃɑrdʒd / (AmE), / dɪsˈtʃɑːdʒd / (BrE)
having been dismissed from a job or position; no longer employed by an organization

Examples

Being discharged from work
After the company restructured, half the staff were discharged without warning.
He was discharged last month and has been looking for work ever since.
Several discharged workers filed complaints about the lack of severance pay.

Related Terms

  • antonym: employed - having a paid job; the opposite state of being fired or discharged
  • synonym: fired - dismissed from a job, typically for misconduct or poor performance
  • synonym: laid off - dismissed from a job, often due to business conditions rather than personal fault
