unthematic

adjective
not related to a theme
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnθəˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnθɪˈmætɪk / (BrE)
not related to or lacking a theme, especially a musical or literary subject.

Examples

An unthematic passage
His analysis found certain passages to be largely unthematic and lacking clear motifs.
The artist preferred an unthematic approach, avoiding any central idea or concept.
The new movement in the symphony is entirely unthematic, providing contrast to previous sections.

Related Terms

  • antonym: thematic - relating to or having a theme
