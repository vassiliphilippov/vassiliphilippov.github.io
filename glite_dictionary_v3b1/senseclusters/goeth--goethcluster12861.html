goeth

verb
archaic 'goes'
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊɪθ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊɪθ / (BrE)
archaic third person singular present tense of 'go', used in older English texts

Examples

He goeth daily
He goeth to the market every morning in the old tale.
In the ancient manuscript, it is written that she goeth forth at dawn.
The knight goeth bravely into battle, as the story recounts.
