postie

noun
postal delivery scooter
9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈpoʊ.stiː/ (AmE), /ˈpəʊ.stiː/ (BrE)
an informal term for a small motorcycle or scooter used by postal workers to deliver mail, especially in New Zealand and Australia

Examples

Postie bike
The postie zipped down the street, delivering letters to every house on her route.
He bought an old postie at auction and restored it for weekend rides.
In Australia, you often see a postie weaving through traffic with a bag full of mail.
