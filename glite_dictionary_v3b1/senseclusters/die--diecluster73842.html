die

verb
figuratively be overwhelmed by emotion
Pronunciation: / daɪ / (AmE), / daɪ / (BrE)
(informal) To be overwhelmed by an intense emotion such as embarrassment, laughter, or shame, as if unable to endure it.

Examples

To die of laughter
I was so embarrassed at the mistake that I thought I would die on the spot.
She almost died of shame when her secret was revealed.
We died laughing at the comedian's outrageous jokes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collapse - to fall down or give way suddenly, often due to lack of strength (figurative synonym for 'die' with laughter, etc.)
