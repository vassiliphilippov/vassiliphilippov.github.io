Plastic Paddy

noun
inauthentic adopter of Irish identity
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈplæstɪk ˈpædi / (AmE), / ˈplæstɪk ˈpædi / (BrE)
a person who adopts Irish customs or identity inauthentically, typically without Irish heritage

Examples

Plastic Paddy identity
Although she had never been to Ireland, Lisa was called a Plastic Paddy for her exaggerated Irish accent.
During the parade, some locals complained about the influx of Plastic Paddies celebrating St. Patrick's Day.
He was sensitive about being labeled a Plastic Paddy by his Irish friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Poseur - a person who pretends to be what they are not, especially to impress others
