cabinetmaking

noun
the craft of making cabinets and furniture
Pronunciation: / ˈkæbɪnətˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkabɪnɪtˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the craft or trade of making fine furniture, especially cabinets, by skilled woodworking and joinery

Examples

Cabinetmaking trade
After studying cabinetmaking, she started her own furniture business.
He has devoted his entire career to cabinetmaking and produces exquisite custom pieces.
Traditional cabinetmaking requires patience, precision, and a deep knowledge of wood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: joinery - the craft of a joiner, especially woodwork joints
