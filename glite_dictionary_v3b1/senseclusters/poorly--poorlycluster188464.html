Synonyms

  • badly
    1.0 13/m
    badly is a common informal adverb meaning not well or effective, while poorly is slightly more formal and measured.
  • unsatisfactorily
    5.6 0.01/m
    unsatisfactorily describes failing to meet expectations or standards, while poorly is more general and used in many contexts.
  • inadequately
    4.4 0.4/m
    inadequately means not enough or sufficient for a purpose, while poorly refers more broadly to being done badly or unsatisfactorily.
  • ill
    4.1 0.3/m
    ill can mean done badly or with bad intent and also sick, while poorly mainly means not well or unsatisfactory.
