Flint River

proper noun
river in western Georgia, USA
4.9
Pronunciation: / flɪnt ˈrɪvɚ / (AmE), / flɪnt ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in western Georgia, USA, that flows south to join the Chattahoochee River at the Florida border, forming the Apalachicola River
