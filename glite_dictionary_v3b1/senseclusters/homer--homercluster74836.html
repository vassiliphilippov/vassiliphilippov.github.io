homer

noun
baseball: hit scoring a run (home run)
6.9 4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈhoʊ.mər/ (AmE), /ˈhəʊ.mə/ (BrE)
(informal) A hit in baseball that allows the batter to round all the bases and score a run, also known as a home run.

Examples

He hit a homer
Fans cheered loudly as their star player smashed another homer out of the park.
He hit a towering homer in the ninth inning to win the game.
The rookie has already hit five homers this season.
