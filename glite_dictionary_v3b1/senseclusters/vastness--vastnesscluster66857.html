vastness

noun
immense size or extent
Pronunciation: / ˈvæst.nəs / (AmE), / ˈvɑːst.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being very great in size, extent, or number

Examples

The vastness of space
He stood in awe at the vastness of the ocean stretching before him.
Scientists continue to be amazed by the vastness of the universe.
The vastness of the desert made them feel truly insignificant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enormity - the great size, extent, or seriousness of something
  • synonym: immensity - the extremely large size, extent, or degree of something
  • antonym: smallness - the state of being small in size or amount
