skeletally

adverb
in a minimal way
7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈskel.ɪ.tə.liː/ /; / /ˈskel.ə.tə.liː/ (AmE), /ˈskel.ɪ.tə.liː/ /; / /ˈskel.ə.tə.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that is basic, minimal, or only includes the essential structure or outline

Examples

Skeletally outlined plan
The report was skeletally written, providing only the main points without explanation.
Their plan was skeletally outlined, missing important steps and details.
He described the process skeletally, leaving out most of the technical information.
