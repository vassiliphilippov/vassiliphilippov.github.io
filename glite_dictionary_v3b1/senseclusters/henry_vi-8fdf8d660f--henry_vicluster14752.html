Henry VI

proper noun
King of England (1422–1461, 1470–1471)
7.6
Pronunciation: /ˈhɛn.rɪ ðə sɪksθ/ /; / /ˈhɛn.rɪ ðə sɪkθ/ (AmE), /ˈhɛn.rɪ ðə sɪksθ/ /; / /ˈhɛn.rɪ ðə sɪkθ/ (BrE)
