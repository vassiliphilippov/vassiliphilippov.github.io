bower

noun
shaded shelter or plant framework
7.2 8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈbaʊ.ə / (BrE)

Examples

A leafy bower

The roses grew over the bower, creating a cool retreat from the afternoon sun.
We enjoyed tea beneath the leafy bower at the garden's edge.
