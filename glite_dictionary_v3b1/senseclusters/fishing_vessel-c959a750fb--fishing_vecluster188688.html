fishing vessel

noun
ship or boat for catching fish
3.0 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪʃ.ɪŋ ˈvɛs.əl/ (AmE), /ˈfɪʃ.ɪŋ ˈvɛs.əl/ (BrE)
