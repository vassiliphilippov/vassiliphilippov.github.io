in quarantine

5.8
Pronunciation: /ɪn ˈkwɔːr.ən.tiːn/ /; / /ɪn ˈkwɑːr.ən.tiːn/ /; / /ɪn ˈkwɔːr.ən.tɪn/ (AmE), /ɪn ˈkwɒr.ən.tiːn/ /; / /ɪn ˈkwɒr.ən.tɪn/ /; / /ɪn ˈkwɒr.ən.taɪn/ (BrE)
(in computing) in a state where a file, email, or program is isolated and blocked from running or interacting with the system to prevent the spread of malware.

Examples

File in quarantine
Suspicious email attachments are kept in quarantine until the security team completes a scan.
After detection, the antivirus placed the malicious executable in quarantine rather than deleting it.
The admin reviewed files currently in quarantine and decided which could be safely restored.

Related Terms

  • synonym: isolate - to separate a suspicious or infected file so it cannot affect the rest of the system
  • synonym: quarantine - a state of isolation to prevent spread of disease or malware
