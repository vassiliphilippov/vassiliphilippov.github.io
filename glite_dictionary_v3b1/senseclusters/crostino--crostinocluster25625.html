crostino

noun
toasted bread appetizer
Pronunciation: / kroʊˈstiːnoʊ / (AmE), / krɒˈstiːnəʊ / (BrE)
a small piece of toasted or grilled bread, often served with a savory topping as an appetizer

Examples

A crostino with tomato
The chef prepared a delicious crostino topped with tomato and basil for each guest.
At the party, we enjoyed a variety of crostini with different cheeses and spreads.
She ordered a mushroom crostino as a starter before the main course arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bruschetta - grilled bread rubbed with garlic and topped with olive oil and salt
  • synonym: canapé - a small piece of bread or pastry with a savory topping, served as an appetizer
