premier

adjective
earliest; first in time
1.0
Pronunciation: / prɪˈmɪr / (AmE), / ˈprɛmiə / (BrE)
Coming before all others in time; earliest or first in a sequence or occurrence.

Examples

Premier performance
Her premier performance on stage drew a standing ovation from the audience.
The theater hosted the premier showing of the film last night.
This is our company’s premier product launch in Asia.
