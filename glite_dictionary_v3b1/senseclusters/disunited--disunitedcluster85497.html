disunited

adjective
having lost unity, divided
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪs.juːˈnaɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌdɪs.juːˈnaɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
No longer united; having been divided or having lost unity.

Examples

A disunited group
A disunited organization cannot achieve success as efficiently as a unified one.
After years of conflict, the nation remained deeply disunited on major political issues.
The team was disunited and failed to cooperate during the crucial match.

Related Terms

  • synonym: divided - separated into parts or groups
  • synonym: fragmented - broken into pieces or parts
  • antonym: united - joined together as one
