business meeting

noun
formal work-related meeting
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪznəs ˈmiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

A business meeting room

Many important decisions are made during a business meeting.
