portentousness

noun
ominous quality
Pronunciation: / pɔrˈtɛntəsnəs / (AmE), / pɔːˈtɛntəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being ominous or giving the impression that something important or serious is about to happen

Examples

The portentousness of a storm
The portentousness of the dark clouds made everyone hurry indoors.
There was a certain portentousness in her voice as she announced the news.
The film's music added to the portentousness of the final scene.

Related Terms

  • antonym: casualness - the quality of being relaxed or unconcerned
  • synonym: foreboding - a feeling that something bad will happen
  • synonym: ominousness - the state of being threatening or suggesting something bad will happen
