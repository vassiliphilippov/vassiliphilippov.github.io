mitt

noun
baseball glove for fielders
4.8 2.6
Pronunciation: /mɪt/ (AmE), /mɪt/ (BrE)
A large glove with sections for the thumb and fingers, used by baseball fielders to catch the ball.

Examples

Baseball mitt
A worn mitt can make it harder to field ground balls.
He forgot his mitt at home and had to borrow one for the game.
The outfielder stretched out his mitt to make the catch.
