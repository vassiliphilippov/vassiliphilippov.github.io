spherically

adverb
in a spherical manner
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsfɪr.ɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˈsfɪə.rɪ.kli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or resembles a sphere; in a spherical manner.

Examples

To form spherically
Raindrops often form spherically due to surface tension.
The room was decorated spherically, with round lanterns hanging from the ceiling.
The virus was observed to assemble spherically within the host cell.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cubically - in the manner of a cube; in three dimensions involving cubes
