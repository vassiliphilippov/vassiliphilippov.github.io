sociosexuality

noun
acceptance of casual sex
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊsiˌoʊsɛkʃuˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌsəʊsiəʊsɛkʃuˈælɪti / (BrE)
the degree to which a society, culture, or group accepts or permits casual sexual relationships

Examples

Cultural sociosexuality norms
Researchers study sociosexuality to understand how different cultures view dating and relationships.
A society with high sociosexuality may be more accepting of casual encounters.
Changes in sociosexuality can reflect broader shifts in cultural values over time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sexual conservatism - preference for restricting sexual activity to committed relationships
  • synonym: sexual permissiveness - willingness to allow or accept sexual activity outside committed relationships
