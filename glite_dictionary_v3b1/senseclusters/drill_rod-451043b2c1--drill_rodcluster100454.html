drill rod

noun
carbon steel for drills and dowels
1.7
Pronunciation: / drɪl rɑd / (AmE), / drɪl rɒd / (BrE)
A type of high-quality carbon steel used for making rock drills, dowels, and machine parts requiring high strength and durability.
