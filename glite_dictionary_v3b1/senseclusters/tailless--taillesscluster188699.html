tailless

adjective
lacking a tail
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪl.ləs / (AmE), / ˈteɪl.ləs / (BrE)
(of an animal or object) not having a tail.

Examples

Tailless animal
Some lizards can become tailless after losing their tails to predators.
The tailless cat is a rare breed known as the Manx.
The artist designed a tailless kite that soared higher in strong winds.
