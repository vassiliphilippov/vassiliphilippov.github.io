conservationism

noun
environmental protection belief
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑnsərˈveɪʃənɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌkɒnsəˈveɪʃənɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief in or practice of protecting the environment and natural resources from harm or depletion

Examples

Environmental conservationism
Many people support conservationism to ensure that future generations can enjoy clean air and water.
The rise of conservationism has led to new laws protecting endangered species.
Her passion for conservationism inspired her to volunteer at the local wildlife reserve.

Related Terms

  • synonym: environmentalism - the advocacy of protecting the environment
  • antonym: exploitation - the act of using resources selfishly or wastefully
