weediness

noun
cannabis-like quality
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːdinəs / (AmE), / ˈwiːdinəs / (BrE)
the quality of smelling, tasting, or seeming like cannabis, especially in an informal or noticeable way

Examples

Weediness in a smell
The beer's unusual weediness reminded some people of a cannabis aroma.
Some customers complained about the weediness of the new herbal tea blend.
The chef tried to reduce the weediness in the sauce by using fewer strong herbs.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: herbal - having a taste or smell like herbs
  • synonym: skunky - having a strong, pungent smell similar to cannabis
