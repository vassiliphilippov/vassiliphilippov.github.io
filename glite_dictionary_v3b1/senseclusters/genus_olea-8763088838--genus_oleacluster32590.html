genus Olea

noun
genus of olive trees and related plants
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs oʊˈliː.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs əʊˈliː.ə / (BrE)
a genus of evergreen trees and shrubs, including the olive, with oily one-seeded fruits

Examples

Genus Olea trees
All trees in the genus Olea produce fruits with a single hard seed, commonly known as drupes.
Botanists often study the genus Olea because of its economic importance, especially the olive tree.
The genus Olea includes a number of species found throughout Africa, Asia, and Europe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: olive - the small oval fruit of Olea europaea, used as food or for oil
