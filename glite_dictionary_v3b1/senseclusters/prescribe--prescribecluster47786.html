prescribe

verb
recommend as beneficial
4.6
Pronunciation: / prɪˈskraɪb / (AmE), / prɪˈskraɪb / (BrE)
to recommend a course of action or remedy as being beneficial or advisable

Examples

To prescribe a healthy diet
Her nutritionist prescribes eating more vegetables and fruits every day.
Many doctors prescribe regular exercise to improve overall health.
Parents often prescribe rest and fluids for children with a cold.
