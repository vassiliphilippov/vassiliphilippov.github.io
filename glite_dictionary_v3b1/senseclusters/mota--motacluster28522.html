mota

noun
tiny particle
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtə / (AmE), / ˈməʊtə / (BrE)
a very small particle or speck, especially of dust; a tiny bit

Examples

A mota of dust
A single mota of dust floated in the beam of sunlight across the floor.
He brushed a mota of flour from his shirt after baking bread.
Not even a mota of evidence was found at the crime scene.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grain - a small, hard particle of a substance
  • synonym: speck - a very small spot or particle
