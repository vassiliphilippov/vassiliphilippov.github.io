Collinsonia canadensis

noun
an herbaceous plant native to eastern North America
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑlɪnˈsoʊniə kəˌnædɛnˈsɪs / (AmE), / ˌkɒlɪnˈsəʊnɪə kəˌnædɛnˈsɪs / (BrE)
