errorless

adjective
without error; flawless
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr.ɚ.ləs / (AmE), / ˈɛr.ə.ləs / (BrE)
Not containing any mistakes; completely free from error.

Examples

An errorless report

The team played an errorless game, impressing both fans and critics.
Their performance on the test was nearly errorless, with only one minor mistake.
