street sign

noun
sign on or by a street
Pronunciation: / ˈstrit saɪn / (AmE), / ˈstriːt saɪn / (BrE)
A sign placed near or along a road, usually displaying traffic regulations, street names, or directions.

Examples

A street sign showing speed
Please obey the street sign indicating the speed limit on this road.
The city plans to replace every damaged street sign by the end of the month.
We missed our turn because the street sign was covered by tree branches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: road sign - sign installed beside a road to provide information or instructions to drivers
