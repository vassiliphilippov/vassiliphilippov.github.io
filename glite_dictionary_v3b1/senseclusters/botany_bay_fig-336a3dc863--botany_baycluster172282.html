Botany Bay fig

noun
Australian tree resembling banyan
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑtəni beɪ fɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈbɒtəni beɪ fɪɡ / (BrE)
An Australian tree (Ficus macrophylla) resembling the banyan, often grown as an ornamental and introduced elsewhere as brushwood.

Examples

The Botany Bay fig tree
Gardeners in South Africa sometimes plant the Botany Bay fig for quick-growing brushwood.
The Botany Bay fig is a striking feature of many Australian parks and gardens.
With its massive canopy, the Botany Bay fig offers excellent shade in public spaces.
