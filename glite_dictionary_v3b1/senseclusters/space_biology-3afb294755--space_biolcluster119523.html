space biology

noun
biological study of life in space
1.8 4.4
Pronunciation: /speɪs baɪ.ˈɑːl.ə.dʒiː/ (AmE), /speɪs baɪ.ˈɒl.ə.dʒɪ/ (BrE)
the scientific study of how living organisms are affected by the conditions of outer space and the search for extraterrestrial life

Examples

Space biology research
Advances in space biology have helped us understand how microgravity affects muscle and bone density.
She is pursuing a doctorate in space biology to study how plants grow aboard spacecraft.
The conference focused on new research findings in space biology and their implications for future Mars missions.
