window trimmer

noun
person who decorates shop windows
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndoʊ ˌtrɪmər / (AmE), / ˈwɪndəʊ ˌtrɪmə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to design and arrange displays in shop windows to attract customers

Examples

Window trimmer designs displays
As a window trimmer, she spent hours designing elaborate displays for the boutique.
The window trimmer created a festive holiday scene to attract more customers to the store.
The department store hired a talented window trimmer to update their visual merchandising for the new season.
