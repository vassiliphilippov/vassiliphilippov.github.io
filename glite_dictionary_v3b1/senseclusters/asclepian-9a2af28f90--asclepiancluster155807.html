Asclepian

adjective
of Asclepius or healing
Pronunciation: / əˈskliːpiən / (AmE), / əˈskliːpiən / (BrE)
relating to Asclepius, the ancient Greek god of medicine, or to ancient Greek healing practices

Examples

Asclepian temples
The ancient city was known for its Asclepian temples dedicated to healing the sick.
Scholars study Asclepian traditions to understand early medical practices in Greece.
The physician followed Asclepian methods, combining ritual with herbal remedies.
