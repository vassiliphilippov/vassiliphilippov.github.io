burrito

noun
Mexican dish of stuffed tortilla
Pronunciation: / bəˈɹiːtoʊ / (AmE), / bəˈriːtəʊ / (BrE)
A Mexican dish consisting of a flour tortilla wrapped around a filling such as beans, rice, or meat.

Examples

A burrito with beans
I ordered a chicken burrito with extra cheese for lunch.
She loves making homemade burritos filled with beans and vegetables.
The restaurant is famous for its spicy beef burrito.
