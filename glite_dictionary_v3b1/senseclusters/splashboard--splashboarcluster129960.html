splashboard

noun
protective panel against splashes
Pronunciation: / ˈsplæʃˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈsplæʃˌbɔːd / (BrE)
A panel or cover designed to protect people or surfaces from splashing water, mud, or similar substances.

Examples

Kitchen splashboard
She installed a splashboard behind the sink to protect the wall from water.
The old carriage still had its original splashboard to keep mud off the passengers.
The truck's splashboard was dented after driving through muddy roads.

Related Terms

  • synonym: splashback - A panel to protect walls from water or food splashes, especially behind a sink or stove.
