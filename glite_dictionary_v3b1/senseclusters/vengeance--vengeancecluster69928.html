Synonyms

  • revenge
    3.0 22/m
    revenge is a personal act of harming someone to settle a score, while vengeance is punishing someone as payback or justice.
  • retribution
    7.5 4.23/m
    retribution is a formal, deserved punishment meant to restore moral balance, while vengeance is often personal and driven by anger.
  • retaliation
    5.2 7.00/m
    retaliation is doing harm in return, often as a quick practical response, while vengeance is a deliberate act of punishment.
  • reprisal
    8.3 1.94/m
    reprisal is a retaliatory action, often by states or armies after an attack, while vengeance is personal punishment or payback.
  • requital
    9.9 0.01/m
    requital is an old-fashioned word for returning a favor or punishment, while vengeance is harming someone as personal payback.
