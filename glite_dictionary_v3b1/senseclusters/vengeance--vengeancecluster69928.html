vengeance

noun
punishment or harm in retaliation
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛn.dʒəns / (AmE), / ˈvɛn.dʒəns / (BrE)
the act of punishing or harming someone in retaliation for a real or perceived wrong, especially as a form of justice or retribution

Examples

Swear vengeance
He swore vengeance upon those who ruined his family.
Instead of seeking vengeance, she chose to forgive her enemies.
Many tales are fueled by a hero's quest for vengeance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forgiveness - the act of stopping being angry or resentful towards someone for an offense or mistake
  • synonym: revenge - the act of inflicting harm or punishment in return for an injury or wrong
