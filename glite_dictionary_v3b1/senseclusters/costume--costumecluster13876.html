costume

noun
special attire for plays or parties
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːstuːm / (AmE), / ˈkɒstjuːm / (BrE)
a set of clothes worn to look like a particular character, theme, or for a special event (such as in a play, film, or at a party)

Examples

A pirate costume
He wore a pirate costume to the Halloween party last year.
Her Cinderella costume won the competition at the fancy dress ball.
She designed a remarkable costume for the school's Shakespeare production.
Children often dress up in fancy costumes for Halloween.
She wore a Victorian costume to the themed party.
The actor changed into his superhero costume backstage.

  • synonym: outfit - a set of clothes worn together, especially for a particular occasion
