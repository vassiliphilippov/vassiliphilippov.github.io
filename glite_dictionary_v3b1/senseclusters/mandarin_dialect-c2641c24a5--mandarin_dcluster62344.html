Mandarin dialect

noun
standard Chinese language variety
Pronunciation: / ˈmændəɹɪn ˈdaɪəlɛkt / (AmE), / ˈmændərɪn ˈdaɪəlɛkt / (BrE)
the standard variety of Chinese based on the Beijing dialect, used as the official language of China

Examples

The Mandarin dialect standard
Many Chinese communities overseas use the Mandarin dialect for communication.
She is fluent in both Cantonese and Mandarin dialect.
The Mandarin dialect is taught in schools throughout China.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Cantonese - a major Chinese dialect spoken in southern China
  • synonym: Mandarin - standard Chinese language based on the Beijing dialect
  • synonym: Putonghua - another term for standard Mandarin Chinese
