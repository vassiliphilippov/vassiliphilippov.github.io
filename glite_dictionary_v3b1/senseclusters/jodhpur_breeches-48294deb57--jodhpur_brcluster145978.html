jodhpur breeches

noun
close-fitting riding trousers
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑd.pɚ ˈbriː.tʃɪz / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒd.pə ˈbriː.tʃɪz / (BrE)
Riding trousers that are flared at the hips and thighs and fit snugly from the knee to the ankle, designed to be worn with riding boots.

Examples

Jodhpur breeches with boots
His jodhpur breeches fit snugly inside his polished riding boots.
She wore jodhpur breeches and a tailored jacket for her riding lesson.
The classic jodhpur breeches were made of sturdy fabric to withstand daily use.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: breeches - short trousers fastened just below the knee; traditional form
  • synonym: jodhpurs - riding trousers with a similar style, but often refers to the full garment
