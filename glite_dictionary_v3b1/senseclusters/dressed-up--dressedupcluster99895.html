dressed-up

adjective
wearing formal or fancy clothes
Pronunciation: / drɛst ʌp / (AmE), / drɛst ʌp / (BrE)
Wearing fancy or formal clothing, often for a special occasion.

Examples

Dressed-up for party
Even children love to get dressed-up for special occasions such as birthdays and holidays.
He felt out of place because he wasn't dressed-up like everyone else at the party.
She always gets dressed-up for weddings, wearing elegant dresses and jewelry.

Related Terms

  • antonym: casual - wearing informal or everyday clothing
  • synonym: formal - wearing clothing suitable for formal occasions
