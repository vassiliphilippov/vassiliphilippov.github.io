esteem

verb
to regard or consider
7.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈstim / (AmE), / ɪˈstiːm / (BrE)
to regard someone or something with respect or admiration

Examples

To esteem a friend
Despite his failures, he esteemed himself capable of great achievements.
Her teachers esteem her as one of the brightest students in the class.
The committee esteemed him a suitable candidate for the position.
His achievements are esteemed throughout the academic community.
She is highly esteemed by her colleagues for her integrity and dedication.
We always esteem honesty above all other qualities in our leaders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consider - to think about or view in a certain way
  • antonym: disdain - to regard with contempt or scorn
  • synonym: regard - to consider or look upon in a particular way
  • synonym: respect - to admire or have a high opinion of someone or something
