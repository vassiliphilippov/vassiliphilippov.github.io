Synonyms

  • look up to
    2.8 3.39/m
    look up to is to admire and see someone as a role model, while esteem means to regard them with deep respect.
  • honor
    2.3 31/m
    honor is to show public or formal respect, often by a ceremony, while esteem is a personal feeling of admiration.
  • prize
    3.8 5.02/m

  • value
    1.9 28/m
    value is to see someone or something as useful or important, while esteem means to admire and hold them in respect.
