non-negotiable

adjective
cannot be changed
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn nəˈɡoʊʃiəbl / (AmE), / ˌnɒn nɪˈɡəʊʃəbl / (BrE)
not open to discussion, change, or modification; fixed and cannot be changed

Examples

Non-negotiable rule
Attendance at the training session is non-negotiable for all employees.
The deadline for submitting your application is non-negotiable.
For safety reasons, the rules are non-negotiable.
The deadline for submitting your application is non-negotiable and must be met by everyone.
Her decision to move abroad was non-negotiable, no matter what her friends said.
Wearing a helmet on the construction site is a non-negotiable safety rule.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flexible - able to be changed or adapted
