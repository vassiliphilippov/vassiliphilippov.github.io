bubby

noun
grandmother (informal)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌbi / (AmE), / ˈbʌbi / (BrE)
an affectionate term for a grandmother, especially in Jewish families

Examples

Jewish bubby
Every Sunday she visited her bubby who baked challah and told family stories.
My bubby always made the best chicken soup for the holidays.
When I was a child, my bubby taught me how to knit scarves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grandma - an informal term for grandmother
  • synonym: grandmother - the mother of one's parent
  • antonym: grandpa - an informal term for grandfather
