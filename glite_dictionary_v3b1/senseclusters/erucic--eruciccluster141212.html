erucic

adjective
from erucic acid
Pronunciation: / ɪˈruːsɪk / (AmE), / ɪˈruːsɪk / (BrE)
relating to or derived from erucic acid, a fatty acid found in some plant oils

Examples

Erucic acid content
Scientists tested the erucic content in several types of vegetable oils.
The new variety of rapeseed is bred to be low in erucic acid.
Some health regulations limit the amount of erucic acid in edible oils.
