gawkiness

noun
awkwardness; lack of grace
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɔːki.nəs / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːki.nəs / (BrE)
awkwardness in movement or posture; lacking grace or elegance

Examples

His gawkiness in movement

Her gawkiness was evident when she tripped over her own feet during the dance.
The boy's gawkiness made him self-conscious during the school play.
