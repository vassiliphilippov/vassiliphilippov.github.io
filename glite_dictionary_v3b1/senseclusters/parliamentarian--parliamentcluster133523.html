parliamentarian

noun
expert in parliamentary procedure
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːrləmən'tɛriən / (AmE), / ˌpɑːləmən'teəriən / (BrE)
an expert in the rules and procedures of a parliament or other deliberative assembly

Examples

Expert parliamentarian
As a certified parliamentarian, she ensured that all debates followed the correct rules.
The parliamentarian advised the chair on the proper procedure for the vote.
When confusion arose, the parliamentarian clarified the assembly's bylaws.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chairperson - the presiding officer of a meeting or assembly
  • synonym: legislator - a person who makes laws
