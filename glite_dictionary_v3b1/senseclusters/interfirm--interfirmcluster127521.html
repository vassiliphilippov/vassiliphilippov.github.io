interfirm

adjective
between companies
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈfɜrm / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈfɜːm / (BrE)
occurring or existing between different companies or firms

Examples

Interfirm competition
Interfirm competition can drive down prices and benefit consumers.
The study analyzed interfirm relationships in the automotive industry.
Successful interfirm collaboration often leads to new business opportunities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intrafirm - occurring within a single company or firm
