brown thrush

noun
large songbird with reddish-brown plumage
8.0
Pronunciation: / braʊn θrʌʃ / (AmE), / braʊn θrʌʃ / (BrE)
a common large songbird of the eastern United States with reddish-brown plumage, also known as the brown thrasher

Examples

A brown thrush bird
A brown thrush built its nest in the dense bushes near the river.
The brown thrush is known for its impressive mimicking abilities.
