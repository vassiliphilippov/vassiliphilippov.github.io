cambion

noun
supernatural changeling
9.9

an archaic term for a changeling, a child believed to have been left or created by supernatural beings in place of a human child

Examples

A cambion baby

The villagers feared the baby was a cambion left by fairies during the night.
Stories of the cambion were told to explain mysterious illnesses in children.
