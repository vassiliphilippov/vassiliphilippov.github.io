desktop

noun
the top surface of a desk
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛskˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈdɛsktɒp / (BrE)
the flat upper surface of a desk, on which work may be done

Examples

Wooden desktop surface
A scratch ran across the entire length of the old wooden desktop.
She cleared everything off the desktop before starting her work.
The lamp took up most of his small desktop.

Related Terms
