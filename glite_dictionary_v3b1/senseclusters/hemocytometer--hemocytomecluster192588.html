hemocytometer

noun
blood cell counter
9.1
Pronunciation: /hiː.moʊ.saɪ.ˈtɑː.mɪ.tər/ (AmE), /hiː.məʊ.saɪ.ˈtɒ.mɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a special glass device with a grid, used under a microscope to count blood cells or other microscopic cells in a liquid sample
