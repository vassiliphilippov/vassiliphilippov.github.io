grate

verb
make a grinding sound
6.6
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪt / (AmE), / ɡreɪt / (BrE)
to make a harsh, scraping sound by rubbing things together, or to scratch a surface by rubbing

Examples

To grate teeth
She couldn't help but grate her teeth during the tense meeting.
The chair legs grated unpleasantly on the tiled floor as he pushed it back.
The loud train wheels grated against the metal tracks as it slowed down.
He heard a strange noise as the branches grated against the window all night.
Rough sandpaper will grate the paint off the old bench.
The cat began to grate the door with her claws until someone let her out.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grind - to make a grating noise by rubbing harshly
  • synonym: rub - to move something back and forth against a surface
  • synonym: scrape - to scratch or remove a surface by rubbing
