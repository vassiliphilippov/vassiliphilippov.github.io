wrinkle

noun
small fold or crease in a surface
6.3 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈrɪŋ.kəl/ /; / /rɪŋkl/ (AmE), /ˈrɪŋ.kəl/ /; / /rɪŋkl/ (BrE)
A small crease, fold, or ridge in a normally smooth surface, especially in skin, fabric, or other materials.

Examples

A wrinkle in fabric
He tried to smooth out the wrinkle in the tablecloth before the guests arrived.
She noticed a new wrinkle forming around her eyes.
The shirt was full of wrinkles after being left in the laundry basket.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crease - a line or ridge produced on paper or cloth by folding, pressing, or crushing
  • synonym: fold - a line or mark where cloth, paper, etc. has been bent
  • antonym: smoothness - the quality of having an even and regular surface
