diasporic

adjective
of dispersed communities
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪəˈspɔrɪk /; / daɪ.əˈspɔːrɪk / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəˈspɒrɪk /; / daɪ.əˈspɒrɪk / (BrE)
relating to people, communities, or cultures that have been dispersed from their original homeland and live in different places
