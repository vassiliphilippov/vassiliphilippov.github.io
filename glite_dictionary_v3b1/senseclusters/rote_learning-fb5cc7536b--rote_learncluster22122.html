rote learning

noun
memorization by repetition
Pronunciation: / roʊt ˈlɜrnɪŋ /; / roʊt ˈlɝːnɪŋ / (AmE), / rəʊt ˈlɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
learning or memorizing information by repeating it over and over, often without understanding its meaning
