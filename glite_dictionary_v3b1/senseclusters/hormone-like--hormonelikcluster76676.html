hormone-like

adjective
similar to a hormone
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔːrmoʊn laɪk / (AmE), / ˈhɔːməʊn laɪk / (BrE)
having properties or effects similar to those of a hormone

Examples

Hormone-like effects
The new medication has hormone-like effects that help regulate metabolism.
Scientists discovered a hormone-like substance produced by certain plants.
Some chemicals in the environment can act in a hormone-like manner in the body.
