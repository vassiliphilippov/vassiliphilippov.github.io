Chamaeleo

noun
genus of chameleons
9.9
Pronunciation: / kæˈmiːlioʊ / (AmE), / kəˈmiːlɪəʊ / (BrE)
a genus of lizards in the family Chamaeleontidae, comprising several species of chameleons found mainly in Africa and parts of Europe and Asia
