by right

adverb
justly or legally entitled
7.9
Pronunciation: / baɪ raɪt / (AmE), / baɪ raɪt / (BrE)
according to justice or legal entitlement; rightly or by law

Examples

To be by right entitled
As a citizen, you are by right entitled to a fair trial.
He should, by right, receive the promotion.
She is, by right, the owner of this property.

Related Terms

  • antonym: by force - by the use of strength or coercion rather than justice or law
  • synonym: by law - according to legal rules; legally
  • synonym: rightfully - in accordance with justice or law
