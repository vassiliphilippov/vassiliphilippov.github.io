instrumental

noun
music without vocals
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnstrəˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌɪnstrəˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
a piece of music performed using only instruments, without any singing or vocals

Examples

A jazz instrumental
The jazz band played a lively instrumental that got everyone dancing.
She prefers listening to instrumentals while studying because there are no distracting lyrics.
The movie's emotional scene was accompanied by a soft piano instrumental.

Related Terms

  • synonym: composition - a piece of music created by a composer
  • antonym: song - a piece of music with singing or vocals
