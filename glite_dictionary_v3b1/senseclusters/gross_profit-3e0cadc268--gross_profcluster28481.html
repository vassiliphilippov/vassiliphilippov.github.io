gross profit

noun
profit after deducting costs of goods sold
3.2 0.27/m
Pronunciation: / …°ro äs Ààpr…ëÀêf…™t / (AmE), / …°r…ô äs Ààpr…íf…™t / (BrE)
The difference between a company's net sales and the cost of goods sold, before deducting operating expenses or taxes.

Examples

High gross profit margin
High gross profit margins indicate efficient production and sales strategies.
The company's gross profit increased by 10% compared to last year.
To calculate gross profit, subtract the cost of goods sold from total revenue.
