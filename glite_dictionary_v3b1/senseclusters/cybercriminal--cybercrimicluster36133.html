cybercriminal

noun
online lawbreaker
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪbərˌkrɪmɪnəl / (AmE), / ˈsaɪbəˌkrɪmɪnəl / (BrE)
a person who uses computers or the internet to commit illegal acts

Examples

A cybercriminal hacker
The police tracked down the cybercriminal who had stolen thousands of credit card numbers.
A skilled cybercriminal can access private data without being detected for months.
Many companies invest in cybersecurity to protect themselves from cybercriminals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: criminal - a person who breaks the law
  • synonym: hacker - a person who gains unauthorized access to computer systems
  • antonym: law-abider - a person who follows the law
