uniserial

adjective
single row arrangement
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːnɪˈsɪriəl / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪˈsɪəriəl / (BrE)
arranged in a single row, line, or sequence; used in biology and mathematics to describe things ordered in a single series
