microstructural

adjective
small-scale structure
7.6
Pronunciation: /maɪ.krə.ˈstrʌk.tʃər.əl/ /; / /maɪ.kroʊ.ˈstrʌk.tʃər.əl/ (AmE), /maɪ.krə.ˈstrʌk.tʃə.rəl/ /; / /maɪ.kəʊ.ˈstrʌk.tʃə.rəl/ (BrE)
relating to the small-scale structure of a material, especially as observed under a microscope

Examples

Microstructural analysis
The microstructural analysis revealed tiny cracks within the metal sample.
Changes in temperature can alter the microstructural features of ceramics.
Researchers studied the microstructural differences between the two types of polymers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: macrostructural - relating to the large-scale structure of a material
  • synonym: microscopic - too small to be seen without a microscope
