coloratura

noun
elaborate vocal ornamentation in singing
Pronunciation: / ˌkʌl.ər.əˈtʊr.ə / (AmE), / ˌkɒl.ər.əˈtjʊər.ə / (BrE)
ornate vocal music or singing characterized by elaborate runs, trills, and rapid passages, especially in operatic works
