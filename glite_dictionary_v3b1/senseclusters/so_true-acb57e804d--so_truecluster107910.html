so true

x
expression of strong agreement
Pronunciation: / soʊ truː / (AmE), / səʊ truː / (BrE)
used to express strong agreement with a preceding statement or sentiment

Examples

She said the weather affects your mood—so true!
When my friend said laughter is the best medicine, I could only say, 'so true.'
