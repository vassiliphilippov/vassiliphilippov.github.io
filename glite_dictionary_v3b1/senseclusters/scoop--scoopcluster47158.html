scoop

noun
quantity held by a scoop
Pronunciation: / skuːp / (AmE), / skuːp / (BrE)
the amount that a scoop can hold, used as a measurement for powders, liquids, or other substances

Examples

A scoop of powder
Add one scoop of protein powder to the blender.
She measured a scoop of coffee into the filter.
The recipe calls for two scoops of ice cream in each bowl.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spoonful - the amount a spoon will hold
