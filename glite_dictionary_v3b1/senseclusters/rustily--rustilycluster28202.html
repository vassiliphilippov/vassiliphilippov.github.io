rustily

adverb
in a rusty manner
5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈrʌs.tɪ.liː/ /; / /ˈrʌs.tə.liː/ (AmE), /ˈrʌs.tɪ.liː/ /; / /ˈrʌs.tə.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that is affected by rust or resembles rust, often producing a rough or creaky sound

Examples

Hinges creak rustily
The hinges squeaked rustily every time someone opened the door.
After years of neglect, the bicycle chain moved rustily along the gears.
The old tractor started up rustily on the chilly morning.
