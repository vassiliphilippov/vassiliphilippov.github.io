exogenously

adverb
from outside source
8.5
Pronunciation: / ɛkˈsɑdʒənəsli / (AmE), / ɛkˈsɒdʒənəsli / (BrE)
in a manner that originates from outside a system or organism

Examples

Added exogenously
The researchers introduced the chemical exogenously to observe its impact on cell growth.
Certain nutrients must be supplied exogenously because the body cannot produce them.
The disease was triggered exogenously by environmental factors rather than genetic ones.

Related Terms

  • antonym: endogenously - in a manner that originates from within a system or organism
