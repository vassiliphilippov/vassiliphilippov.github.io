story

noun
temporary social-media post
Pronunciation: / c8st54ri / (AmE), / c8st54d0ri / (BrE)
A short photo, video, or multimedia post on social-media platforms that is visible for a limited time (often 24 hours) before disappearing.

Examples

Post a story online
She shared a story on Instagram that showed her morning coffee and commute.
Influencers often plan their stories to keep followers engaged throughout the day.
The company posted a quick story with a discount code that expired the next day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: post - a message, image, or other content that someone uploads to a social-media platform
  • synonym: status update - a short message or piece of content shared on social media to inform followers about something
