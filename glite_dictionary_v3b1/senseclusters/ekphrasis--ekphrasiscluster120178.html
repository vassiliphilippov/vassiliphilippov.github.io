ekphrasis

noun
artistic description of art
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛkfrəsɪs /; / ɛkˈfreɪsɪs / (AmE), / ˈɛkfrəsɪs /; / ɛkˈfreɪsɪs / (BrE)
a vivid literary or artistic description of a work of art, often making it feel alive for the reader or audience
