main street

noun
principal street in a town
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪn ˌstrit / (AmE), / ˈmeɪn ˌstriːt / (BrE)
A street that serves as the principal thoroughfare for traffic in a town or city.

Examples

The main street
After the renovation, main street became a popular place for evening strolls.
Most of the town's restaurants and shops are located along main street.
The parade marched down main street as crowds cheered on both sides.
