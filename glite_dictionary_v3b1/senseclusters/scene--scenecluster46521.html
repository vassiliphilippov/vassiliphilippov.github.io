scene

noun
sphere or environment of activity
1.5
Pronunciation: / siːn / (AmE), / siːn / (BrE)
A particular sphere of activity or context regarded as an observable environment, especially in relation to specific fields such as politics, religion, or the arts.

Examples

Music scene
He quickly became well-known on the startup scene in Silicon Valley.
Many artists got their start in the underground theater scene.
The local jazz scene has become much more vibrant in recent years.

  • synonym: arena - a field or sphere of activity, interest, or competition
  • synonym: sphere - an area or field of activity or expertise
