paseo

noun
public promenade
8.6
Pronunciation: / pɑˈseɪ.oʊ / (AmE), / pæˈseɪ.əʊ / (BrE)
a public walkway or promenade, especially in Spanish-speaking regions, used for strolling or socializing

Examples

City paseo
Every evening, families gather to walk along the paseo in the center of town.
The new paseo by the river has become a popular spot for joggers and cyclists.
Street performers entertained crowds on the busy paseo during the festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boulevard - a wide street in a city, often with trees or a walkway
  • synonym: promenade - a public place for walking, often along a waterfront
