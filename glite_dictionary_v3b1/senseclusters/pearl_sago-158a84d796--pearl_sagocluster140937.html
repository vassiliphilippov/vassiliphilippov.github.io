pearl sago

noun
sago in small, round grains
9.5
Pronunciation: / pɜrl ˈseɪɡoʊ / (AmE), / pɜːl ˈseɪɡəʊ / (BrE)
sago that has been processed into small, round, pearl-like grains, commonly used in cooking and desserts
