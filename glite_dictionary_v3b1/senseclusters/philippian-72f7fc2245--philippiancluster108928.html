Philippian

noun
person from Philippi (ancient Macedonia)
9.9
Pronunciation: / fɪˈlɪpiən / (AmE), / fɪˈlɪpiən / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of Philippi, an ancient city in Macedonia.

Examples

Ancient Philippian artifacts
Archaeologists discovered artifacts once used by ancient Philippians in their daily lives.
Historians study the culture and traditions of the Philippians to understand Macedonia's influence.
The apostle Paul wrote a letter addressed to the Philippians in the New Testament.
