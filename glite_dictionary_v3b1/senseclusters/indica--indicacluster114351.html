indica

noun
cannabis variety
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndɪkə / (AmE), / ˈɪndɪkə / (BrE)
a type of plant, especially a species or variety of cannabis known for its relaxing effects

Examples

Indica cannabis plant
Some people use indica strains to help them relax before sleep.
The effects of indica are often described as calming and soothing.
Growers often choose indica plants for their shorter flowering times.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sativa - a different species or variety of cannabis with more energizing effects
