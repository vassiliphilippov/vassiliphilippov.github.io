brevity

noun
conciseness; shortness of duration
8.5 4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbrev.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /ˈbrev.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
the quality of being brief or expressing something in few words (conciseness), especially in speech or writing

Examples

The brevity of speech
His speech was praised for its brevity and clarity.
She admired the brevity of the author's writing style.
The brevity of the meeting surprised everyone.
In journalism, brevity is often valued over long explanations.
She was known for her brevity when writing emails.
The brevity of his speech impressed the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conciseness - the quality of being concise, using few words
  • antonym: verbosity - the use of more words than needed; wordiness
