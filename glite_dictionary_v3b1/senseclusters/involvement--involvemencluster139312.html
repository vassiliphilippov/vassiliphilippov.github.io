involvement

noun
interest or engagement (noun)
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɑlvmənt /; / ɪnˈvɑːlvmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɒlvmənt / (BrE)
the act or state of taking part in, being engaged with, or included in an activity, group, or situation; interest or participation

Examples

Active involvement in projects
A deep involvement with music can enrich a person's life.
Her involvement in community projects shows her genuine concern for others.
The teacher praised his involvement in classroom discussions.
Her involvement in the local community theater helped her make many friends.
Parents' involvement in their children's education is considered highly beneficial.
The company's involvement in environmental projects has improved its public image.
His involvement in the project led to significant improvements.
The company denied any involvement with the fraudulent activities.
There was no evidence of his involvement in the controversial decision.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exclusion - the act of being left out or not allowed to take part
  • synonym: interest - the feeling of wanting to know or learn about something
  • synonym: participation - the act of taking part in something
