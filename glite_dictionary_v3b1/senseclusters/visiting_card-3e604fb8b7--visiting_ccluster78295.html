visiting card

noun
small card left to show a visit
1.5 5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪzɪtɪŋ kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈvɪzɪtɪŋ kɑːd / (BrE)
a small card bearing a person's name and sometimes address, left as a token of a visit or to announce one's presence

Examples

A visiting card given

She left her visiting card at the reception desk when no one was home.
