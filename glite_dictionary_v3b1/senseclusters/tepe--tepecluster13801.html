tepe

noun
hill or summit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛpeɪ /; / teˈpeɪ / (AmE), / ˈtɛpeɪ /; / teˈpeɪ / (BrE)
a hill or mound, especially in Turkish regions or archaeology

Examples

Ancient tepe
We climbed the grassy tepe to enjoy the view over the valley at sunset.
A small village sits at the base of the tepe, surrounded by fields.
From the top of the tepe, you can see for miles in every direction.
The archaeologists excavated the ancient tepe to uncover its secrets.
Many early villages in Anatolia were built on a tepe for protection and visibility.
Artifacts found within the tepe revealed details about the region's prehistoric culture.
