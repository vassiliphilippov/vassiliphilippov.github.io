administrative body

noun
organization with administrative responsibilities
5.4
Pronunciation: / ədˌmɪnəˈstreɪtɪv ˈbɑdi / (AmE), / ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪtɪv ˈbɒdi / (BrE)
an organization or group responsible for managing and overseeing certain administrative functions or duties

Examples

A administrative body manages services
A new administrative body was formed to manage the local parks and recreation services.
Every department reports its progress directly to the main administrative body.
The administrative body oversees all financial transactions within the organization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authority - an organization with official power to make decisions or enforce rules
  • synonym: committee - a group of people appointed for a specific administrative function
