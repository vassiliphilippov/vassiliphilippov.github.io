bell arch

noun
rounded arch on corbels
Pronunciation: / bɛl ɑrtʃ / (AmE), / bɛl ɑːtʃ / (BrE)
an architectural arch with a rounded shape resting on corbels, resembling the outline of a bell.

Examples

A bell arch shape
A bell arch can often be found in Romanesque and Gothic architecture.
Architects admired the elegant design of the bell arch in the ancient monastery.
The old church features a bell arch above the entrance, supported by carved corbels.
