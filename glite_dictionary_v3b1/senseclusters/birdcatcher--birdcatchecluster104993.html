birdcatcher

noun
person or device catching birds
5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbɜːrd.kætʃ.ər/ (AmE), /ˈbɜːd.kætʃ.ə/ (BrE)
a person or device that catches birds, often for sport, study, or pest control

Examples

The birdcatcher uses nets
The experienced birdcatcher used special nets to safely capture rare species for research.
A mechanical birdcatcher was installed in the orchard to protect the fruit from flocks.
In the past, a birdcatcher might supply songbirds to wealthy households as pets.
