Scotch tape

noun
transparent adhesive tape
Pronunciation: / skɑtʃ teɪp / (AmE), / skɒtʃ teɪp / (BrE)
A brand of transparent or semi-transparent adhesive tape used for sealing, attaching, or mending objects.

Examples

A roll of Scotch tape
A roll of Scotch tape was sitting on the teacher’s desk.
He repaired the torn page with a strip of Scotch tape.
She used Scotch tape to wrap the gift neatly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Sellotape - A popular brand of transparent adhesive tape, especially in the UK
