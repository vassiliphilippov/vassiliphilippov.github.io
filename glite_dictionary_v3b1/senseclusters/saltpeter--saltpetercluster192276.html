saltpeter

noun
potassium nitrate compound
Pronunciation: / sɔltˈpiːtər / (AmE), / sɒltˈpiːtə /; / sɔːltˈpiːtə / (BrE)
a white crystalline compound, potassium nitrate (KNO3), a naturally occurring mineral used especially in fertilizers, food preservation, and explosives such as gunpowder
