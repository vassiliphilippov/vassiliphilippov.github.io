shipment

noun
the act of sending goods or cargo
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪpmənt / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpmənt / (BrE)
the act of sending goods or cargo to a particular place, especially by ship, train, truck, or aircraft

Examples

Shipment of goods
A delay in the shipment caused problems for the company.
The shipment of medical supplies is scheduled to arrive tomorrow.
They tracked the shipment online to ensure it reached the customer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delivery - the action of delivering goods; the bringing of parcels or goods to a recipient
  • synonym: dispatch - the sending of goods to a destination
