noncontributing

adjective
not historically significant
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkənˈtrɪbjutɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkənˈtrɪbjuːtɪŋ / (BrE)
not adding to the historical, architectural, or cultural significance of a designated area, especially in preservation contexts

Examples

Noncontributing building status
The new apartment building is classified as noncontributing within the historic district.
Many noncontributing structures were built after the area received its heritage designation.
A noncontributing property does not affect the overall historical value of the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contributing - adding to the historical or cultural value of an area
