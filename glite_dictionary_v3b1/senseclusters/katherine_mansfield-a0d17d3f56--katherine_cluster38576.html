Katherine Mansfield

proper noun
New Zealand modernist short story writer
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkæθərɪn ˈmænsfiːld / (AmE), / ˈkæθərɪn ˈmɑːnzfiːld / (BrE)
(1888–1923) a New Zealand writer renowned for her modernist short stories, noted for their psychological insight and innovative style.
