gallery-goer

noun
person in upper seats
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæləri ˌɡoʊər / (AmE), / ˈɡæləri ˌɡəʊə / (BrE)
a person who regularly attends events or performances in the gallery section of a theater or similar venue

Examples

Theater gallery-goer
As a devoted gallery-goer, she never missed a single play at the old theater.
The gallery-goers cheered loudly from the upper balcony during the final act.
He preferred being a gallery-goer because the seats were affordable and the view was unique.

Related Terms

  • synonym: audience member - someone attending a performance or event
  • synonym: spectator - a person who watches an event or performance
