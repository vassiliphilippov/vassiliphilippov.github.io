hold up

verb
rob at gunpoint or by threat
2.8
Pronunciation: / hoʊld ʌp / (AmE), / həʊld ʌp / (BrE)

Examples

The masked man tried to hold up the bank before fleeing the scene.
Two men attempted to hold up a convenience store last night.
