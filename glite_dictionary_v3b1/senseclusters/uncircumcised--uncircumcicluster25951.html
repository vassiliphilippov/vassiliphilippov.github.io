uncircumcised

adjective
not circumcised
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈsɜːrkəmˌsaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈsɜːkəmsaɪzd / (BrE)
not having had the foreskin of the penis removed by circumcision

Examples

Uncircumcised boys
In some cultures, it is common for boys to remain uncircumcised throughout their lives.
The doctor explained the differences between being uncircumcised and circumcised to the parents.
He learned that being uncircumcised is normal in many parts of the world.

Related Terms
