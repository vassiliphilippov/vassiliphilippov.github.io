indecisiveness

noun
inability to make decisions
4.7 4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.dɪˈsaɪ.sɪv.nəs /; / ˌɪndɪˈsaɪsɪvnəs / (AmE), / ˌɪn.dɪˈsaɪ.sɪv.nəs /; / ˌɪndɪˈsʌɪsɪvnɪs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being unable to make decisions quickly or firmly; a lack of determination
