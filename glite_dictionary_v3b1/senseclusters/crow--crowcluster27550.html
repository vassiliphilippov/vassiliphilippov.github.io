crow

noun
the cry of a rooster
4.7
Pronunciation: / kroʊ / (AmE), / krəʊ / (BrE)
The loud, shrill cry of a cock (rooster), or an imitation of this sound.

Examples

A rooster's crow
He tried to imitate the crow loudly, much to everyone's amusement.
The crow of the rooster woke us up at dawn.
The farm was quiet except for the sudden crow coming from the henhouse.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: cackle - the harsh sound made by a hen or goose
  • synonym: crowing - the act of making a cock's characteristic sound
