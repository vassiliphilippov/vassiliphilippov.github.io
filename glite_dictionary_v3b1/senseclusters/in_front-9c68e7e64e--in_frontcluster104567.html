in front

adverb
ahead; at the front
Pronunciation: / ɪn frʌnt / (AmE), / ɪn frʌnt / (BrE)
in a position ahead or at the front, especially in relation to another person or object.

Examples

Stand in front
Please stand in front so you don't block anyone's view.
She always sits in front of the classroom to see the board clearly.
There was a long line of cars in front of the building.

  • antonym: behind - at or towards the back of
