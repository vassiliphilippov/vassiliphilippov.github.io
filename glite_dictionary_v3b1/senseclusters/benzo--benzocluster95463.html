benzo

noun
chemical prefix
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛnzoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɛnzəʊ / (BrE)
a prefix used in chemical names to indicate a compound derived from benzene or containing a benzene ring

Examples

Benzo group in chemistry
The chemist labeled the sample with the benzo prefix to show it contained a benzene ring.
Many aromatic compounds have names that begin with benzo to indicate their structure.
In organic chemistry, the term benzo is often used when discussing derivatives of benzene.

Related Terms

  • synonym: benzene - an aromatic hydrocarbon with the formula C6H6
