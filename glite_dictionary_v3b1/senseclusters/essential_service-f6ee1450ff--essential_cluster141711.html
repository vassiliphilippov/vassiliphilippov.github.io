essential service

noun
necessary service for health and safety
4.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈsɛnʃəl ˈsɝːvɪs / (AmE), / ɪˈsɛnʃl ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
a service considered necessary for the health, safety, or well-being of individuals, especially those requiring assistance

Examples
