Pamelor

noun
brand of tricyclic antidepressant
Pronunciation: / ˈpæmələr / (AmE), / ˈpæmɪlə / (BrE)
A brand name for nortriptyline, a tricyclic antidepressant drug used to treat depression, often in combination with psychotherapy.

Examples

Pamelor medicine
After months of unsuccessful treatments, her doctor prescribed Pamelor to help manage her depression.
My grandmother found that Pamelor significantly improved her mood when she was struggling with low-level depression.
You should not take Pamelor together with certain other medications due to possible dangerous interactions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Elavil - another brand of tricyclic antidepressant (amitriptyline)
  • synonym: nortriptyline - the generic drug of which Pamelor is a brand name
