branch

noun
tributary river or stream
Pronunciation: / bræntʃ / (AmE), / brɑːntʃ / (BrE)
a stream or river that flows into a larger one; a tributary

Examples

River branch
After heavy rain, the branch sometimes overflows its banks.
The small branch joined the main river just south of town.
We set up camp near a shallow branch that fed into the big stream.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tributary - a river or stream flowing into a larger river or lake
