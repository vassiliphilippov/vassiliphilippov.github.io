unhemmed

adjective
without a hem
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɛmd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛmd / (BrE)
not finished with a hem; having raw or unfinished edges, especially in fabric or clothing

Examples

Unhemmed edges
The tailor noticed the unhemmed sleeves and offered to fix them.
After washing, the unhemmed edges of the fabric began to fray.
She wore an unhemmed dress that gave her outfit a casual look.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hemmed - finished with a hem; edges folded and sewn
  • synonym: unfinished - not completed or lacking a final touch
