pink water

noun
wastewater from processing
Pronunciation: / pɪŋk ˈwɔtər / (AmE), / pɪŋk ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
wastewater from meat, poultry, or food-processing plants that is pink due to blood, fats, and tissue, and usually requires treatment

Examples

Strict regulations require regular testing of pink water from food-processing plants.
Technicians monitor the color and composition of pink water to ensure proper treatment.
