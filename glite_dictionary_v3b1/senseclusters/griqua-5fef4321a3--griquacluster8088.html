Griqua

noun
Khoekhoe language
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːkwə / (AmE), / ˈɡriːkwə / (BrE)
a Khoekhoe language historically spoken by the Griqua people in parts of southern Africa

Examples

Griqua language
