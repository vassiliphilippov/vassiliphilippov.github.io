well-qualified

adjective
having suitable qualifications or skills
Pronunciation: / ˌwɛl ˈkwɑləˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˌwɛl ˈkwɒlɪfaɪd / (BrE)
Having the necessary or excellent qualifications, skills, or experience for a particular job or activity.

Examples

A well-qualified engineer
Only well-qualified engineers should attempt to operate this machinery.
She is a well-qualified candidate for the management position.
The university seeks a well-qualified individual to lead the research team.
