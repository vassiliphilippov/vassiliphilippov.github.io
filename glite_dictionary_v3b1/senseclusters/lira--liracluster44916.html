lira

noun
Turkish currency unit
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪr.ə /; / ˈliːrə /; / ˈlɪrə / (AmE), / ˈlɪə.rə /; / ˈlɪərə / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Turkey (still in use), and formerly in Italy and Malta before the euro
