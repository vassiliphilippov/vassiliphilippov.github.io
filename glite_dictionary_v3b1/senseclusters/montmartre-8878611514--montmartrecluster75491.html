Montmartre

proper noun
hill in Paris famous for artists and bohemian history
7.5
Pronunciation: /mɑːn.ˈmær.trə/ /; / /mɑːn.ˈmɑːr.trə/ (AmE), /mɒn.ˈmɑː.trə/ /; / /mɒn.ˈmær.trə/ (BrE)
