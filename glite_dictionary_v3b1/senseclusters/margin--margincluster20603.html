margin

noun
amount allowed for flexibility or error
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrdʒɪn / (AmE), / ˈmɑːdʒɪn / (BrE)
an amount allowed for error, safety, or flexibility; the difference by which something is achieved or allowed

Examples
