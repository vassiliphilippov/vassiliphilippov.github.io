centralizer

noun
device for alignment
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntrəˌlaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈsɛntrəlaɪzə / (BrE)
a person, device, or thing that brings things to a central point or under central control

Examples

The centralizer device
The centralizer ensures the pipes stay aligned during installation.
A centralizer is used in oil wells to keep the casing centered.
The engineer checked the centralizer before starting the next phase of the project.
The new policy made the agency a key centralizer of decision-making power.
In mathematics, the centralizer of an element consists of all elements that commute with it.
As a centralizer, she worked to unify the company's various departments under one system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aligner - a device that aligns or arranges things in a straight line
  • synonym: controller - a person or device that directs or regulates something
  • antonym: decentralizer - a person or thing that distributes control away from a central authority
  • antonym: disperser - a device or person that spreads things out or separates them
