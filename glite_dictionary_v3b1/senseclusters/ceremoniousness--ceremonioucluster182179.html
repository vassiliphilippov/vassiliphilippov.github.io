ceremoniousness

noun
formality or ceremonial behavior
9.1
Pronunciation: / sɛrəˈmoʊniəsnəs / (AmE), / ˌsɛrɪˈməʊniəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being very formal or polite, especially in conduct or manner; behavior characterized by ceremony or ritual

Examples

The ceremoniousness of events
His ceremoniousness at the wedding impressed all the guests.
The meeting was conducted with a surprising degree of ceremoniousness.
They admired the ceremoniousness of the traditional tea ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: formality - rigid observance of rules or customs
  • antonym: informality - lack of formal or ceremonious behavior
