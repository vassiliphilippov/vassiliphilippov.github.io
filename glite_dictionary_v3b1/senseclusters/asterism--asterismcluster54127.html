asterism

noun
six-rayed star pattern in crystals
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈæs.təˌrɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈas.tə.rɪ.zəm / (BrE)
A star-shaped figure with six rays visible in certain crystals when viewed under reflected or transmitted light, especially in gemstones.

Examples

Gemstone asterism
Star sapphires are prized for the beautiful asterism seen when they are moved in light.
The asterism in the sapphire made it particularly valuable to collectors.
Under the jeweler's lamp, a clear asterism appeared on the surface of the gem.

Related Terms

  • synonym: star stone - A gemstone, such as a ruby or sapphire, exhibiting asterism
