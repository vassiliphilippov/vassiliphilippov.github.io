unhomeliness

noun
feeling of not belonging
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhoʊmli.nəs /; / ʌnˈhoʊmliˌnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈhəʊmli.nəs /; / ʌnˈhəʊmliˌnəs / (BrE)
a feeling of discomfort, strangeness, or not belonging in a place or situation
