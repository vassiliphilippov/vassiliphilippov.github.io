unramified

adjective
not branched
9.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈræməˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈræmɪfaɪd / (BrE)
not divided into branches or subdivisions; remaining as a single, undivided part.

Examples

Unramified channel
The river flowed in an unramified channel through the valley.
In mathematics, an unramified extension has no branching points.
The plant's main stem remained unramified throughout its growth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: branched - having branches or subdivisions
  • synonym: simple - not divided or complicated; single
