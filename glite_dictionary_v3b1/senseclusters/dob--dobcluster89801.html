dob

noun
informer, tattletale
Pronunciation: / dɑb / (AmE), / dɒb / (BrE)
a person who informs an authority about someone else's wrongdoing, especially in a school or group setting

Examples

A dob at school
No one wanted to be the dob in the group, so they kept quiet about the prank.
He was called a dob after telling the teacher who broke the window.
Being labeled a dob can make it hard to make friends at school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: snitch - someone who informs on others, often to authorities
  • synonym: tattletale - a person who tells on others, especially children
