stuff

verb
prepare and fill dead animal for mounting
8.2
Pronunciation: / stʌf / (AmE), / stʌf / (BrE)
to fill the skin of a dead animal with material to preserve its shape, especially for display; to prepare (an animal) for taxidermy

Examples

To stuff an animal
After hunting season, he learned how to stuff pheasants for decoration.
It takes great skill to properly stuff and mount an animal for display.
The museum decided to stuff the rare eagle for their wildlife exhibit.
