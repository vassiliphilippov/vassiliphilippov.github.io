landless

adjective
not owning land
Pronunciation: / ˈlændləs / (AmE), / ˈlændləs / (BrE)
Not owning or having any land, especially as property.

Examples

Landless people
After losing their farm, the family became landless and struggled to survive.
Many landless workers migrated to the cities in search of employment.
The government introduced reforms to assist landless peasants.

Related Terms

  • antonym: landed - owning land, especially in large amounts
