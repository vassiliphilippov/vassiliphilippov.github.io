Examples

Cushion of air
A thin cushion beneath the vehicle allows it to traverse both land and water.
Engineers improved the cushion to reduce friction and increase speed.

Synonyms

  • shock absorber
    3.9 0.46/m
    a shock absorber is fitted to vehicle suspension to damp sudden jolts, while a cushion spreads force and lessens impact on parts or under a vehicle.
  • buffer
    4.2 1.36/m
    a buffer is a general device or material that absorbs shock in many settings, while a cushion is a specific padding or air layer.
