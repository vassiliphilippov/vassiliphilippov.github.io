defiance

noun
open resistance; bold disobedience
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfaɪəns / (AmE), / dɪˈfaɪəns / (BrE)
open resistance or bold disobedience to authority or rules

Examples

Open defiance of rules
Her defiance of the rules inspired others to speak up for themselves.
In an act of defiance, the protesters refused to leave the premises.
The look of defiance on his face angered the teacher.
Her defiance in the face of the authorities inspired others to protest.
His refusal to apologize was an act of defiance against the rules.
The students wore black armbands in defiance of the school ban.
His defiance of the school rules landed him in detention.
She stared back at her accuser in open defiance.
The protestors acted in defiance of the new law.
