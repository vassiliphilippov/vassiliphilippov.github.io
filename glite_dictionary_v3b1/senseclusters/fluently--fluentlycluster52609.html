fluently

adverb
smoothly and easily (esp. in language)
Pronunciation: / ˈfluːəntli / (AmE), / ˈfluːəntli / (BrE)
In a way that is smooth, effortless, and accurate, especially when referring to speaking or writing a language.

Examples

To speak fluently
He can read and write in German fluently, impressing his colleagues.
She learned to speak Spanish fluently after living in Madrid for a year.
The child answered all the questions fluently during the oral exam.
