wild wheat

noun
wheat species growing in the wild
6.2 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / wa…™ld wiÀêt / (AmE), / wa…™ld wiÀêt / (BrE)
a type of wheat that grows naturally without cultivation, particularly those found in Palestine and considered the ancestors of domesticated wheat

Examples

Synonyms

    9.5 0.01/m

  • Triticum dicoccum dicoccoides
    9.9 0.00/m
    Triticum dicoccum dicoccoides is the scientific Latin name for a wild emmer variety used by botanists, while wild wheat is a broad common term.
