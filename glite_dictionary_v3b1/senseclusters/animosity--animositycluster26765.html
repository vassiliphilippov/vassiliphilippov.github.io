animosity

noun
strong hostility; active hatred
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.ɪˈmɑː.sə.t̬i / (AmE), / ˌæn.ɪˈmɒs.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
Strong hostility or active hatred toward someone or something.

Examples

Strong animosity
Political debates sometimes generate unexpected animosity among close friends.
She felt no animosity toward her former boss, despite their disagreements.
Years of competition had bred a deep animosity between the two families.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enmity - a state or feeling of active opposition or hostility
  • antonym: friendliness - the quality of being friendly; amicable behavior
  • synonym: hostility - hostile or unfriendly behavior; opposition or antagonism
