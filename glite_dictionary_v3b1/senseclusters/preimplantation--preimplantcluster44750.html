preimplantation

adjective
before embryo implantation
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːɪmˌplænˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːɪmˌplænˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
occurring or existing before the implantation of an embryo in the uterus

Examples

Preimplantation genetic testing

Researchers are studying the effects of preimplantation environments on embryo development.
