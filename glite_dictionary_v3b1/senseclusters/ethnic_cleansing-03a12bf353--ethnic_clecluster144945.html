ethnic cleansing

noun
mass expulsion or killing of an ethnic group
8.2 8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈeθ.nɪk ˈklen.zɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈeθ.nɪk ˈklen.zɪŋ/ (BrE)
the deliberate and systematic removal, expulsion, or killing of members of an ethnic or religious group from a particular area, often using violence or intimidation
