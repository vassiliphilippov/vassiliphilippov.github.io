aggregator

noun
person or organization that gathers things
Pronunciation: / ˈæɡ.rəˌɡeɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈæɡ.rɪ.ɡeɪ.tə / (BrE)
a person or organization that collects items or information from various sources and compiles them for others to use

Examples

An aggregator of data
As an aggregator, his job is to collect and organize market data for clients.
She became a well-known aggregator of vintage postcards.
The news aggregator curates headlines from hundreds of sources.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collector - a person who collects things as a hobby or job
