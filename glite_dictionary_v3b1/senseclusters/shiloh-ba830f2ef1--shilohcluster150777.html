Shiloh

proper noun
Civil War battle in Tennessee, 1862
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaɪloʊ / (AmE), / ˈʃaɪləʊ / (BrE)
a major battle fought in 1862 during the American Civil War in southwestern Tennessee, notable for its high casualties and strategic consequences
