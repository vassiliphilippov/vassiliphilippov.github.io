bluecap bird

noun
small bird with a blue cap
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːˌkæp bɝd / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌkæp bɜːd / (BrE)
A small bird characterized by a distinctive blue cap on its head; commonly refers to the blue tit or similar species.

Examples

A bluecap bird in garden
A bluecap bird visited the garden every morning to feed on insects.
Many birdwatchers are eager to see a bluecap bird during spring walks.
The bright feathers of the bluecap bird make it easy to spot among the foliage.
