tall meadow grass

noun
a coarse Eurasian forage grass
5.2 4.9
Pronunciation: /tɔːl ˈme.doʊ græs/ (AmE), /tɔːl ˈme.dəʊ grɑːs/ (BrE)
a coarse, perennial Eurasian grass resembling oat, found on roadside verges, rough grassland, and hay meadows, and introduced in North America as forage

Farmers often use tall meadow grass as a source of hay for their livestock.
