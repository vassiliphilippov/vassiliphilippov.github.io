tall meadow grass

noun
a coarse Eurasian forage grass
Pronunciation: / tɔl ˈmɛdoʊ ɡræs / (AmE), / tɔːl ˈmɛdəʊ ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a coarse, perennial Eurasian grass resembling oat, found on roadside verges, rough grassland, and hay meadows, and introduced in North America as forage

Examples

Tall meadow grass hay
Farmers often use tall meadow grass as a source of hay for their livestock.
On our walk, we noticed how the tall meadow grass towered above the wildflowers nearby.
The field was dotted with patches of tall meadow grass swaying in the breeze.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oat grass - any grass resembling oat, especially of the genus Arrhenatherum
