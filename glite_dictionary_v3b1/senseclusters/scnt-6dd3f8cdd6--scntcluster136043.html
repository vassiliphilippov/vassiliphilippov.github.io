SCNT

noun
abbreviation for somatic cell nuclear transfer (genetic process)
Pronunciation: / ɛs si ɛn ti / (AmE), / ɛs si ɛn ti / (BrE)
an abbreviation for somatic cell nuclear transfer, a process in which the nucleus of a somatic cell is transferred into an egg cell from which the nucleus has been removed

Examples

SCNT process
Advancements in SCNT have opened new possibilities for regenerative medicine.
Scientists debate the ethical implications of human cloning through SCNT.
The researchers used SCNT to clone the sheep, resulting in the famous animal named Dolly.
