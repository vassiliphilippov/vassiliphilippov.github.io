sweeten the pill

verb
make something unpleasant more acceptable
Pronunciation: / ˈswitn̩ ðə pɪl / (AmE), / ˈswiːtən ðə pɪl / (BrE)
to make something unpleasant seem more acceptable or easier to deal with, often by adding something pleasant

Examples

To sweeten the pill with cake
She brought cake to sweeten the pill of telling the team about the budget cuts.
The new rules were unpopular, so management tried to sweeten the pill by offering extra vacation days.
To sweeten the pill, they added a bonus to the salary package.

Related Terms

  • synonym: soften the blow - to make the effect of something less unpleasant
