midweekly

adjective
occurring in the middle of the week
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪdˈwiːkli / (AmE), / ˌmɪdˈwiːkli / (BrE)
Happening or taking place in the middle of the week, typically around Wednesday.

Examples

A midweekly service
A special midweekly service was announced for the congregation.
She received a midweekly update from her manager to track project progress.
The club holds its midweekly meetings every Wednesday evening.
