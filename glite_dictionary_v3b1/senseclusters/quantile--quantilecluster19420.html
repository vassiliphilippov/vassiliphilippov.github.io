quantile

noun
statistical cutoff value
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkwɑːn.taɪl/ /; / /ˈkwɔːn.taɪl/ (AmE), /ˈkwɒn.taɪl/ (BrE)
a value that divides a set of data or a probability distribution into equal-sized groups or intervals (such as quartiles or percentiles)
