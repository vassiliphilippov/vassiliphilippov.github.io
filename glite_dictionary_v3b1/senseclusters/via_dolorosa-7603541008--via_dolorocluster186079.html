Via Dolorosa

proper noun
route in Jerusalem of Jesus's crucifixion
Pronunciation: / ˌviː.ə ˌdoʊ.ləˈroʊ.sə / (AmE), / ˌviː.ə ˌdɒl.əˈrəʊ.sə / (BrE)
the path in Jerusalem believed to be the route Jesus walked on the way to his crucifixion, significant in Christian tradition and pilgrimage

Examples

The Via Dolorosa path
The Via Dolorosa winds through the narrow streets of Jerusalem’s Old City.
Thousands of pilgrims journey along the Via Dolorosa each year during Holy Week.
Walking the Via Dolorosa, he paused at each station to reflect on the passion of Christ.
