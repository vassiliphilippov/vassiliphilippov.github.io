Lake Victoria

proper noun
the largest lake in Africa
Pronunciation: / leɪk vɪkˈtɔriə / (AmE), / leɪk vɪkˈtɔːrɪə / (BrE)
the largest lake in Africa and the second largest freshwater lake in the world, located between Uganda, Kenya, and Tanzania; a primary source of the Nile River
