happen

verb
occur or take place
Pronunciation: / ˈhæpən / (AmE), / ˈhap(ə)n /; / ˈhæpən / (BrE)
to occur, take place, or come into existence, especially by chance or without planning

Sometimes, the things we imagine seem impossible until they happen in real life.
