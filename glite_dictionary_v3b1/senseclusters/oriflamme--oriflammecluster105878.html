oriflamme

noun
red royal banner of early French kings
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrəˌflæm / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪflam / (BrE)
A red or orange-red flag or standard, especially the banner carried as a battle standard by early French kings.

Examples

Royal oriflamme banner
Legends say that the oriflamme inspired great courage in the soldiers of France.
The knight marched beneath the fluttering oriflamme into battle.
The museum displayed a reconstruction of the medieval oriflamme.
