fontanelle

noun
soft gap in an infant's skull
9.6 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / Àåf…ëÀên.t…ôÀàn…õl / (AmE), / Àåf…ín.t…ôÀàn…õl / (BrE)
a soft membranous gap between the bones of a baby's skull where bone formation is not complete, allowing for growth of the brain and skull

Examples

A fontanelle in baby
A bulging fontanelle may be a sign of increased pressure in an infant's brain.
The anterior fontanelle usually closes by 18 months of age.
The doctor gently examined the baby's fontanelle during the checkup.

Synonyms

  • fontanel
    9.2 0.05/m
    fontanel is the common short medical form used by doctors and parents, while fontanelle is the more formal scientific term.
  • soft spot
    6.1 0.11/m
    soft spot is the everyday, nontechnical phrase parents use when touching a baby's skull, while fontanelle is the medical term.
