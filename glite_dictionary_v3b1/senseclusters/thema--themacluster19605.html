thema

noun
central idea or topic
Pronunciation: / ˈθiːmə / (AmE), / ˈθiːmə / (BrE)
a subject, topic, or central idea, especially in a discussion, text, or musical work

Examples

Main thema of the story
The composer introduced a new thema in the second movement of the symphony.
In her essay, she explored the main thema of identity and belonging.
The discussion kept returning to the central thema of environmental responsibility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: theme - the main subject or idea in a work of art or discussion
  • synonym: topic - the subject of a conversation, discussion, or text
