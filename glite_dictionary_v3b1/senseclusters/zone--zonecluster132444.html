zone

verb
regulate an area's permitted uses
3.4 3.4
Pronunciation: / zoʊn / (AmE), / zəʊn / (BrE)
to divide or designate an area into sections for a specific purpose

Examples

To zone land
Several neighborhoods have been zoned exclusively for residential use.
The city council voted to zone the area for commercial development only.
Urban planners worked late to decide how to zone the newly acquired land.
Designers often zone rooms using different colors or materials to distinguish areas.
The city council decided to zone the land for residential use only.
The large open-plan office was zoned to create quiet workspaces for employees.
