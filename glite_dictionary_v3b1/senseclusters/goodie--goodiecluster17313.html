goodie

noun
small treat or sweet
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʊdi / (AmE), / ˈɡʊdi / (BrE)

Examples

A goodie bag

The party bags were filled with all sorts of goodies for the guests.
She brought a homemade goodie to share with her coworkers.
