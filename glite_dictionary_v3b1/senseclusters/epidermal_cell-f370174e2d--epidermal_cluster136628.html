epidermal cell

noun
cell forming the outer skin layer
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈdɜrməl sɛl / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈdɜːməl sɛl / (BrE)
any of the cells that make up the outermost layer of skin or the epidermis of a plant

Examples

Plant epidermal cell
In plants, each epidermal cell helps regulate water loss and gas exchange.
Researchers isolated an epidermal cell from a leaf for microscopic analysis.
The epidermal cell acts as a barrier protecting internal tissues from pathogens and injury.
