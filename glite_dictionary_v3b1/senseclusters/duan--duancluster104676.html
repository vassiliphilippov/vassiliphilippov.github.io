duan

noun
martial arts rank
Pronunciation: / dwæn / (AmE), / dwæn / (BrE)
a rank or level awarded in Chinese martial arts or other graded systems to indicate skill or achievement

Examples

The fifth duan
After years of dedication, she was promoted to the fifth duan in kung fu.
Earning a higher duan requires passing both physical and theoretical tests.
He proudly displayed his certificate for achieving the third duan in tai chi.

Related Terms

  • synonym: belt - a colored band indicating rank in martial arts
  • synonym: dan - a rank or grade in Japanese martial arts
