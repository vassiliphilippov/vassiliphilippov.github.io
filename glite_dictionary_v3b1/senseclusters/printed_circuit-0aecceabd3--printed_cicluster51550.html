printed circuit

noun
electronic circuit on a board with printed connections
5.7 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈprɪn.tɪd ˈsɜːr.kɪt/ /; / /ˈprɪn.təd ˈsɜːr.kət/ (AmE), /ˈprɪn.tɪd ˈsɜː.kɪt/ /; / /ˈprɪn.təd ˈsɜː.kɪt/ (BrE)
