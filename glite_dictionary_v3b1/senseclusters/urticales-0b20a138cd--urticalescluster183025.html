Urticales

noun
order of dicotyledonous plants
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɝːˈtɪkəˌliːz / (AmE), / ɜːˈtɪkəˌliːz / (BrE)
an order of dicotyledonous plants that includes families such as Moraceae, Urticaceae, and Ulmaceae.

Examples

The Urticales order
Recent genetic studies have led to changes in the classification of the Urticales.
The Urticales include a wide variety of economically important plants.
The family Moraceae, which includes figs and mulberries, belongs to the Urticales.
