ice melter

noun
substance used for melting ice
6.3
Pronunciation: / aɪs ˈmɛltɚ / (AmE), / aɪs ˈmɛltə / (BrE)
A substance, in liquid or solid form, that is used to melt ice, especially on pavements, driveways, or roads.

Examples

An ice melter product
Most ice melter products work by lowering the freezing point of water.
She sprinkled ice melter on the sidewalk to make it safe for pedestrians.
The garage keeps a bag of ice melter handy for slippery steps during winter.

  • synonym: deicer - chemical used to remove or prevent ice
