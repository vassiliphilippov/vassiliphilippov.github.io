runback

noun
return of a kicked ball
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈrʌnˌbæk / (BrE)
a play in sports where a player returns a kicked or intercepted ball by running it toward the opponent's end

Examples

His 80-yard runback changed the momentum of the game.
