Eastern Time

noun
time zone for eastern U.S. (UTC−5)
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈiːstərn taɪm / (AmE), / ˈiːstən taɪm / (BrE)
the standard time of the time zone covering the eastern part of the United States, which is five hours behind Coordinated Universal Time (UTC−5) and observed during both standard and daylight saving periods

Examples

Eastern Time zone
Flight arrivals are usually given in local time, so make sure you convert to Eastern Time if needed.
Television programs often list their start times in both Eastern Time and Pacific Time.
The meeting is scheduled for 10:00 a.m. Eastern Time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Eastern Daylight Time - the daylight saving time in the eastern U.S. time zone (UTC−4)
  • synonym: Eastern Standard Time - the standard (winter) time in the eastern U.S. time zone (UTC−5)
