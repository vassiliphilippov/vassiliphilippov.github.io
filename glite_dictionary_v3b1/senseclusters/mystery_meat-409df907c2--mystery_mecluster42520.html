mystery meat

noun
unidentifiable or dubious meat, often low-quality
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪstəri miːt / (AmE), / ˈmɪstəri miːt / (BrE)
food, especially meat, whose origin, type, or contents are unknown or doubtful, often considered unappetizing or of low quality

Examples

A plate of mystery meat
She poked at the mystery meat on her plate, trying to guess what it was.
The cafeteria served its usual mystery meat for lunch again today.
We all laughed about the bizarre taste of last night's mystery meat at summer camp.
