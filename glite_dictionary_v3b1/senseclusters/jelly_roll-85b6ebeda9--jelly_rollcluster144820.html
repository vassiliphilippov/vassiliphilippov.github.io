jelly roll

noun
roll of pre-cut fabric strips for quilting

A commercially prepared roll of pre-cut fabric strips (typically about 2.5 inches wide) used by quilters for patchwork and quilting projects.

Examples

A fabric jelly roll

The pattern called for two jelly rolls and several matching solids for the borders.
