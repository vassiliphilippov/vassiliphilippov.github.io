jelly roll

noun
roll of pre-cut fabric strips for quilting
Pronunciation: / c8d925bli ro8al / (AmE), / c8d925bli r598al / (BrE)
A commercially prepared roll of pre-cut fabric strips (typically about 2.5 inches wide) used by quilters for patchwork and quilting projects.

Examples

A fabric jelly roll
She bought a colorful jelly roll of cotton strips to simplify her quilting project.
The pattern called for two jelly rolls and several matching solids for the borders.
She unrolled a jelly roll and cut the strips into patchwork pieces for the quilt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pre-cut fabric roll - a bundle of fabric strips cut to a standard width and sold together for quilting
