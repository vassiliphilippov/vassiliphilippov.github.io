critical

adjective
of critics or criticism
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈkrɪtɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to critics, criticism, or careful analysis, especially of art or literature

Examples

Critical review
Her critical essay on modern poetry was published in a respected literary journal.
Many authors hope for critical recognition, even if commercial success proves elusive.
The movie received critical acclaim for its compelling performances and direction.
A critical approach to literature helps students gain deeper insights into a text.
His critical review of the film considered both strengths and weaknesses with careful thought.
The professor's critical examination of the data provided new perspectives on the results.
