holiness

noun
quality of being holy
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊ.li.nəs / (AmE), / ˈhəʊ.li.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being holy; spiritual purity or sanctity

Examples

The holiness of relics
He spoke with awe about the holiness of the ancient relic found in the monastery.
Many people are inspired by the holiness of Mother Teresa's life and work.
The temple is a place regarded for its holiness and spiritual significance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: impiety - lack of respect for the sacred
  • synonym: sanctity - the state of being sacred or holy
