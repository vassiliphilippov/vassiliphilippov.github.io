branch off

verb
diverge from a main route or topic
7.6 2.8
Pronunciation: /bræntʃ ɔːf/ /; / /bræntʃ ɑːf/ (AmE), /brɑːntʃ ɒf/ /; / /bræntʃ ɒf/ (BrE)
(of a road, path, or subject) to separate from a main route, area, or topic and go in a different direction.
