bank

noun
a reserve supply or stock
Pronunciation: / bæŋk / (AmE), / baŋk / (BrE)
a supply or stock kept in reserve for future use, especially in case of emergencies

Examples

A bank of blood
Hospitals often use a bank of donor organs when matching patients.
Scientists accessed a bank of genetic data to identify the disease.
The laboratory maintains a bank of rare blood types for emergencies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reserve - something kept back for future use
  • synonym: stockpile - a large accumulated stock of goods or materials
