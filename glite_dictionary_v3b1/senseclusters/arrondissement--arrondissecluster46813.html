arrondissement

noun
administrative district
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˌrɑːndɪsˈmɑ̃ / (AmE), / əˌrɒndɪsˈmɒ̃ /; / aˌrɒndɪsˈmɒ̃ / (BrE)
an administrative district or division, especially in France or French-speaking cities such as Paris

Examples

Paris arrondissement
Each arrondissement in Paris has its own mayor and council.
The city was divided into several arrondissements for administrative efficiency.
He moved to a quieter arrondissement to escape the busy city center.
The city of Paris is divided into twenty arrondissements, each with its own town hall.
The mayor attended a meeting with officials from every arrondissement in the region.
Each arrondissement is responsible for managing local services and administration.
They stayed in the fifth arrondissement during their trip to Paris.
Each arrondissement in Paris has its own town hall and local government.
The museum is located in a quiet arrondissement away from the city center.

Related Terms

  • synonym: borough - a town or district with its own local government
  • degree lower: commune - the smallest administrative division in France, below an arrondissement
  • degree higher: department - a larger administrative division in France above an arrondissement
  • synonym: district - an area marked off for administrative or other purposes
