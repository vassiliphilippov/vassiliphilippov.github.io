reaction

noun
chemical process forming new substances
7.6
Pronunciation: / riˈækʃən / (AmE), / riˈækʃən / (BrE)
a process in which one or more substances undergo a chemical change to form different substances

Examples

Chemical reaction
A reaction occurs when hydrogen and oxygen combine to form water.
She explained how the rate of the reaction increased with temperature.
The color change showed that a chemical reaction had taken place.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chemical reaction - a reaction involving chemical change
  • degree lower: decomposition - a reaction where a compound breaks into simpler substances
  • degree higher: synthesis - a reaction forming a compound from simpler substances
