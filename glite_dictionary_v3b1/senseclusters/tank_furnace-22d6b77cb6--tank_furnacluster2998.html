tank furnace

noun
furnace for continuous glass melting
Pronunciation: / tæŋk ˈfɜrnəs / (AmE), / tæŋk ˈfɜːnɪs / (BrE)
A furnace in which measured raw materials are fed at one end and molten glass is drawn from the other, used in glass manufacturing.

Examples

A tank furnace for glass
Improvements in tank furnace technology have increased efficiency in the glass industry.
The tank furnace operates continuously to supply molten glass for production lines.
Workers monitor the temperature and flow of materials in the tank furnace every shift.
