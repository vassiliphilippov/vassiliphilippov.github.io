communalist

noun
supporter of communalism
8.9 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / k…ôÀàmjun…ôl…™st / (AmE), / k…ôÀàmjuÀên…ôl…™st / (BrE)
a person who supports communalism, especially one who promotes the interests of a particular religious or ethnic community, sometimes causing division or conflict

Examples

A communalist leader
The communalist argued that his community deserved special rights within the country.
Many leaders warned that communalist rhetoric could lead to increased tensions between groups.
She was labeled a communalist after repeatedly supporting only her own ethnic group in public debates.

Synonyms

  • tribalist
    8.7 0.13/m
    a tribalist fiercely defends their own local group and excludes outsiders, while a communalist promotes a religious or ethnic community's interests, often causing division.
  • sectarian
    8.2 0.11/m
    a sectarian shows strong loyalty to a religious or political sect and fights outsiders, while a communalist promotes a particular community's interests and may cause conflict.
  • ethnicist
    9.0 0.03/m
    an ethnicist favors one ethnic group and shows bias in jobs or politics, while a communalist supports an ethnic or religious community's interests.
  • factionalist
    9.1 0.00/m
    a factionalist backs a small faction inside an organization to gain power, while a communalist promotes a religious or ethnic community and may split people.

  • synonym: sectarian - a person strongly supporting the interests of a particular group, often causing division
