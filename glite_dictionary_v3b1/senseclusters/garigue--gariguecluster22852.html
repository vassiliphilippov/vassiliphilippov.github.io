garigue

noun
Mediterranean scrubland
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈriːɡ / (AmE), / ɡəˈriːɡ / (BrE)
a type of low, open scrubland with aromatic herbs and small shrubs, found on dry, rocky Mediterranean hills

Examples

Dry garigue hills
The hillsides were dotted with garigue, filling the air with the scent of wild thyme.
Many rare plants thrive in the unique environment of the garigue.
Walking through the garigue, we spotted several species of aromatic herbs and small shrubs.
