licking

noun
a beating; a thrashing
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
a decisive or severe defeat, especially in sports or competition, or a physical beating

Examples

A licking in sports
After losing the game, the team admitted they had taken a real licking on the field.
If you don't clean your room, you might get a licking from your mother.
The boxer suffered a brutal licking in the final round.
After a humiliating licking, the coach vowed to rebuild the squad.
Last night, our team took a real licking from their opponents.
The senator suffered a political licking in the primary election.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beating - an act of striking repeatedly; a physical punishment
  • synonym: thrashing - a heavy defeat or beating
  • antonym: victory - the act of defeating an opponent
