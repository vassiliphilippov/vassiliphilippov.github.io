snarl

verb
to say angrily or sharply
4.5 4.7
Pronunciation: /snɑːrl/ (AmE), /snɑːl/ (BrE)
to say something in an angry, sharp, or abrupt tone

Examples

To snarl a warning
"Don't touch that!" he snarled at his little brother.
She snarled a warning before slamming the door behind her.
The referee snarled at the protesting players to step back.

Related Terms
