wingnutty idea

noun
eccentric or unconventional idea
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪŋˌnʌti aɪˈdiə / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋˌnʌti aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
An idea that is eccentric, odd, or highly unconventional, often in a way that seems extreme or bizarre.

Examples

A wingnutty idea
His wingnutty idea for a floating bicycle made everyone laugh, but he was serious.
She always comes to meetings with at least one wingnutty idea for us to consider.
Whenever she shares a wingnutty idea, it sparks the team's creativity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conventional idea - an idea that conforms to accepted standards or norms
  • synonym: eccentric idea - an idea that is unconventional or odd
