sty

noun
eyelid infection; red swelling
Pronunciation: / staɪ / (AmE), / staɪ / (BrE)
an infection of the sebaceous gland at the edge of the eyelid, causing a red, painful swelling

Examples

Eye sty
A sty usually heals on its own within a week.
If you frequently get a sty, it's important to keep your eyes clean.
Sarah woke up with a painful sty on her upper eyelid.
