motor home

noun
vehicle with living quarters for travel
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtər hoʊm / (AmE), / ˈməʊtə həʊm / (BrE)
a large vehicle equipped with living quarters, used for traveling and camping

Examples

A motor home trip
Our family rented a motor home for our camping trip in the mountains.
The motor home was fully equipped with a kitchen, bathroom, and sleeping area.
They spent their summer driving across the country in a motor home.
