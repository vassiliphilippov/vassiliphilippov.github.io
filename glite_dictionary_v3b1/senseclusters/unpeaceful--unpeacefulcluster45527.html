unpeaceful

adjective
not peaceful, lacking calm or tranquility
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpisfəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈpiːsfl̩ / (BrE)
not peaceful; characterized by a lack of peace, calm, or tranquility

Examples

An unpeaceful event
Many feared the upcoming election would be an unpeaceful event.
The country experienced several unpeaceful years during the civil war.
Their divorce resulted in an unpeaceful household, upsetting the children.

Related Terms

  • antonym: peaceful - free from disturbance; tranquil
