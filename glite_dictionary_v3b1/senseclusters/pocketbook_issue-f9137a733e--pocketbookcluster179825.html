pocketbook issue

noun
issue affecting personal finances
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑkɪtˌbʊk ˈɪʃu / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪtbʊk ˈɪsjuː / (BrE)
an issue, especially in politics, that directly affects people's personal finances or economic well-being

Examples

Pocketbook issues like taxes
Candidates focused their campaigns on pocketbook issues like taxes and jobs.
The rising cost of healthcare has become a major pocketbook issue for many families.
Voters often decide based on pocketbook issues that directly impact their daily lives.
