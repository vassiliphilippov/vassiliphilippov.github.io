husbandless

adjective
without a husband
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌzbəndləs / (AmE), / ˈhʌzbəndləs / (BrE)
not having a husband, either because of being unmarried, widowed, or divorced

Examples

A husbandless woman
After her husband passed away, she lived a husbandless life in the countryside.
The novel tells the story of a husbandless woman raising her children alone.
In some cultures, being husbandless can bring social challenges for women.

Related Terms

  • antonym: married - having a husband or wife
  • synonym: single - not married or in a romantic relationship
  • synonym: widowed - having lost a spouse by death and not remarried
