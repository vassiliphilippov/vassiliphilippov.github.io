tangent curve

noun
graph of y = tan x
4.9 6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈtæn.dʒənt kɜːrv/ (AmE), /ˈtæn.dʒənt kɜːv/ (BrE)
the graph of the equation y = tan x, representing how the tangent function varies as the angle x changes

Examples

The tangent curve graph
The tangent curve has vertical asymptotes at every odd multiple of π/2.
The graphing calculator can plot a tangent curve to help visualize its periodic nature.
When studying trigonometry, students often examine the properties of the tangent curve.
