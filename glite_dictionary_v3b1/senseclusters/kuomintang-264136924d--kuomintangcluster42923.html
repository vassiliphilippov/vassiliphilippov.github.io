Kuomintang

proper noun
Chinese nationalist political party
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔːmɪnˌtɑŋ / (AmE), / ˌkwəʊmɪnˈtæŋ / (BrE)
A Chinese political party founded in 1911 by Sun Yat-sen, which governed China until 1949 and has since been the ruling or major party in Taiwan.

Examples

The Kuomintang party
After losing the civil war, the Kuomintang retreated to Taiwan and continued to govern there.
Many older Taiwanese still support the Kuomintang because of its historical legacy.
The Kuomintang played a key role in shaping modern Chinese history.
