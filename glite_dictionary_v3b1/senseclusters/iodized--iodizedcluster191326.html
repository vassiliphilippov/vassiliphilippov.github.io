iodized

adjective
treated with iodine
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ.əˌdaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.ə.daɪzd / (BrE)
(of a substance) treated or mixed with iodine, especially to prevent deficiency of the element in the diet.

Examples

Iodized salt
Bakeries often use iodized flour to ensure proper nutrition.
Many countries require iodized salt to help prevent iodine deficiency in the population.
She bought a bag of iodized salt for use in all her recipes.
Most table salt today is iodised to help prevent thyroid problems in the population.
She checked the label to confirm the product was iodised before purchasing it.
The government initiated a program to provide iodised salt in rural areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: iodized - American spelling: treated or mixed with iodine
  • antonym: non-iodized - not treated with iodine
  • antonym: plain - not containing added iodine
