Holy Communion

noun
the Christian rite of receiving the Eucharist
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌhoʊli kəˈmjunjən / (AmE), / ˌhəʊli kəˈmjuːnjən / (BrE)
the Christian sacrament commemorating the Last Supper by partaking of bread and wine; also, the act of receiving the Eucharist

Examples

Holy Communion ceremony
Children usually receive their first Holy Communion around the age of seven.
She attended Holy Communion every Sunday morning at her local church.
Visitors are invited to join the congregation in Holy Communion during the service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Eucharist - the Christian ceremony commemorating the Last Supper
  • synonym: Mass - the celebration of the Eucharist in the Roman Catholic Church
