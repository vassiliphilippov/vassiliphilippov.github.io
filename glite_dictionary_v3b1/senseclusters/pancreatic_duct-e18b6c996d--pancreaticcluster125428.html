pancreatic duct

noun
duct connecting pancreas and intestine
5.1 3.2
Pronunciation: /pæn.kriː.ˈæt.ɪk dʌkt/ /; / /pæŋ.krɪ.ˈæt.ɪk dʌkt/ (AmE), /pæŋ.krɪ.ˈæt.ɪk dʌkt/ /; / /pæn.krɪ.ˈæt.ɪk dʌkt/ (BrE)
the duct that connects the pancreas to the intestine, allowing pancreatic juices to flow into the digestive tract
