dragon's eye

noun
Asian fruit similar to lychee
Pronunciation: / ˈdræɡənz aɪ / (AmE), / ˈdraɡənz aɪ / (BrE)
An Asian tropical fruit similar to the lychee, with a translucent pulp and smooth, tan skin; also known as longan.

Examples

A dragon's eye fruit
In some Asian desserts, dragon's eye is used alongside lychee for its unique flavor.
She bought a bag of fresh dragon's eye from the market to make a fruit salad.
The translucent flesh of the dragon's eye is both sweet and refreshing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lychee - similar Asian fruit with rougher shell
