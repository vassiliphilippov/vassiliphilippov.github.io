hood

noun
engine cover on a vehicle
7.7
Pronunciation: / hʊd / (AmE), / hʊd / (BrE)
a protective or functional covering or part that covers or shields something (such as a car engine, plant, animal, camera lens, vent, or carriage)

Examples
