rate of attrition

noun
speed at which something decreases in size or number
Pronunciation: / reɪt əv əˈtrɪʃən / (AmE), / reɪt əv əˈtrɪʃən / (BrE)
the speed at which the size or number of something decreases, especially over a period of time

Examples

The rate of attrition of troops
During the war, the rate of attrition of troops was higher than expected.
Schools are trying to reduce the rate of attrition among first-year students.
The company's high rate of attrition raised concerns among its investors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attrition rate - the percentage rate at which something is reduced or lost
