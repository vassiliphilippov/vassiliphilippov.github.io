fellow

noun
member of a learned society or institution
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛloʊ / (AmE), / ˈfɛləʊ / (BrE)
a member of a learned society or academic institution, typically awarded as a mark of distinction or honor

Examples

A fellow of the college
After years of dedication, he finally became a fellow at the prestigious medical college.
As a fellow of the academy, she participates in annual conferences and events.
She was named a fellow of the Royal Society in recognition of her research.
