Synonyms

    4.8 1/m
    interchangeable fits items that work the same and can swap without loss of function, while exchangeable means they can be traded.
  • substitutable
    5.9 0.00/m
    substitutable describes something you can use in place of another without changing function, while exchangeable focuses on trading or swapping.
  • swappable
    3.9 0.02/m
    swappable sounds informal and describes items you can swap easily, while exchangeable covers trading or replacing similarly valued things.
  • replaceable
    3.3 0.7/m
    replaceable means something can be put back by another, while exchangeable stresses trading or swapping items of the same kind or value.
  • fungible
    9.2 0.3/m
    fungible is a technical term for goods that fully replace each other in trade or law, while exchangeable is broader everyday trade.
