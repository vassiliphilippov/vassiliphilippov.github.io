spoiler

noun
person who overindulges someone
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈspɔɪlɚ / (AmE), / ˈspɔɪlə / (BrE)
a person who overindulges someone, often by giving them too much of what they want, thereby potentially spoiling their character or behavior

Examples

Child spoiler
As a spoiler, he found it hard to say no to his children, often giving them anything they wanted.
My grandmother was a notorious spoiler, always buying me treats and gifts.
Some believe that being a spoiler harms children's ability to handle disappointment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indulger - a person who gives in to someone's wishes or desires
