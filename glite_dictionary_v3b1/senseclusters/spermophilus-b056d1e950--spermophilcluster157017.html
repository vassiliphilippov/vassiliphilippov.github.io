Spermophilus

noun
a genus of typical ground squirrels
9.9
Pronunciation: / spərˈmɑːfɪləs / (AmE), / spəːˈmɒfɪləs / (BrE)
a genus of ground squirrels commonly referred to as typical ground squirrels, found mostly in Eurasia and North America

Examples

The Spermophilus squirrel
Biologists observed a group of Spermophilus digging complex burrow systems in the field.
The Spermophilus genus includes many species native to the steppes of Eurasia.
Unlike tree squirrels, members of Spermophilus are adapted to life on the ground.
