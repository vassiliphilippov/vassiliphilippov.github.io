seme

noun
unit of meaning
9.9
Pronunciation: / siːm /; / ˈsiːm /; / ˈsiːmi / (AmE), / siːm /; / ˈsiːm /; / ˈsiːmi / (BrE)
the smallest unit of meaning in linguistics or semiotics; a basic element that helps define the meaning of a word or sign

Examples

A seme of meaning

The word 'dog' and 'wolf' share several semes but differ in others.
Understanding the concept of a seme can clarify how words are related in meaning.
In linguistic analysis, each seme helps define the meaning of a word.
The concept of a seme is important in understanding how words convey complex ideas.

In semiotics, a seme is the smallest unit of meaning in a sign system.
The red light serves as a seme for drivers to stop at the intersection.
Each gesture in the dance can be interpreted as a distinct seme with its own meaning.
