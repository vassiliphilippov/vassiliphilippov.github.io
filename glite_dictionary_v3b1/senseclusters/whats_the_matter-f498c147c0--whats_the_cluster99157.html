what's the matter

x
inquiry expressing concern about a problem
Pronunciation: / wʌts ðə ˈmætɚ / (AmE), / wɒts ðə ˈmætə / (BrE)
used to ask someone what is wrong or why they are upset, often expressing concern

Examples

What's the matter?
If you don't tell me what's the matter, I can't help you solve it.
She noticed he looked unhappy and gently asked, what's the matter?
Whenever my son is quiet, I ask him what's the matter to make sure he's okay.
