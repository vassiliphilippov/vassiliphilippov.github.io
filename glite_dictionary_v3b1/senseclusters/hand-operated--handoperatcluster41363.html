hand-operated

adjective
operated by hand
Pronunciation: / ˈhænd ˌɑːpəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈhænd ˌɒpəˌreɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a device or machine) operated or controlled by hand rather than automatically or by power.

Examples

Hand-operated coffee grinder
A hand-operated coffee grinder gives you more control over the result.
For safety reasons, the gate is hand-operated instead of electric.
The museum displayed a collection of hand-operated tools from the 19th century.

Related Terms

  • antonym: automatic - operated by a machine without direct human control
  • synonym: manual - operated by hand rather than automatically or by power
