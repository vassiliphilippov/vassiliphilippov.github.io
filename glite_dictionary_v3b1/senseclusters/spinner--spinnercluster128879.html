spinner

noun
spinning toy or device
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪnər / (AmE), / ˈspɪnə / (BrE)
a small toy or device that spins quickly when flicked, used for fun or stress relief

Examples

The colorful spinner spun quickly on the table, catching everyone's attention.
Many students brought a spinner to school as a fun way to relax.
