absolutistic

adjective
relating to absolutism or absolute authority
7.4 8.1
Pronunciation: /æb.sə.luː.ˈtɪs.tɪk/ (AmE), /æb.sə.luː.ˈtɪs.tɪk/ (BrE)
relating to or characterized by absolutism, especially supporting totalitarian principles or absolute authority

Examples

Absolutistic rule

Such absolutistic thinking leaves little room for compromise or dissent.
The new leader enacted absolutistic policies that limited all forms of opposition.
