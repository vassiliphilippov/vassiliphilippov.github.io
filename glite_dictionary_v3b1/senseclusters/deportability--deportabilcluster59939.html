deportability

noun
being subject to deportation
9.4
Pronunciation: / dɪˌpɔːrtəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / dɪˌpɔːtəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality or condition of being subject to removal from a country by legal process

Examples

Changes in immigration law can affect the deportability of many non-citizens.
Her lawyer argued that her deportability was not justified under the current regulations.
