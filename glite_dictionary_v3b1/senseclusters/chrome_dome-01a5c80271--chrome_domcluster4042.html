chrome dome

noun
slang for a bald head
Pronunciation: / kroʊm doʊm / (AmE), / krəʊm dəʊm / (BrE)
a humorous or slang term for a bald head, especially when it is smooth and shiny

Examples

A shiny chrome dome
As he walked into the office, the lights reflected off his chrome dome.
People often tease my uncle about his shiny chrome dome.
The football coach proudly displayed his chrome dome at the awards dinner.
