legless

adjective
not having legs
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛɡ.ləs / (AmE), / ˈlɛɡ.ləs / (BrE)

Examples

Legless person

Some species of amphibians are legless, resembling large worms or snakes.
The gecko is a legless lizard that moves by slithering along the ground.
