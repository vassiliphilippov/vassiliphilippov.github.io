Chen caerulescens

noun
a North American wild goose species (snow goose)
Pronunciation: / kɛn sɛəˌruːlˈɛskɛnz / (AmE), / ˈkɛn siːəˌruːlˈɛskɛnz / (BrE)
a species of North American wild goose, also known as the snow goose, with dark plumage in summer and white plumage in winter
