Fiji time

noun
relaxed attitude toward time
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːdʒi taɪm / (AmE), / ˈfiːdʒi taɪm / (BrE)
an informal and relaxed approach to time and punctuality, characteristic of Fijian culture

Examples

Fiji time culture
Meetings often start late when everyone follows Fiji time.

When you're on Fiji time, being late isn't a big deal.
