subliminal

adjective
below the threshold of conscious awareness
4.9
Pronunciation: / səˈblɪmənl / (AmE), / sʌbˈlɪmɪnəl / (BrE)
(of a stimulus or influence) below the threshold of conscious perception; not experienced consciously but potentially able to affect thoughts or behavior.

Examples

A subliminal message
He was unaware of the subliminal influence the background music had on his mood.
Some advertisements include subliminal messages intended to influence consumers without their awareness.
The study revealed that subliminal cues could still affect participants’ choices.

Related Terms

  • antonym: supraliminal - above the threshold of conscious perception
