re-introduction

noun
the act of bringing back something rejected before
Pronunciation: / ˌriː.ɪn.tɹəˈdʌk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌriː.ɪn.tɹəˈdʌk.ʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the act or process of proposing or bringing back something that was previously rejected or discontinued

Examples

The re-introduction of policy
Following criticism, the re-introduction of the policy was postponed.
The committee voted against the re-introduction of the previously rejected rule.
There was widespread debate about the re-introduction of wolves into the region.
