Englisher

noun
non-Amish person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃər / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃə / (BrE)
a person who is not a member of the Amish or Mennonite communities, especially someone from mainstream society
