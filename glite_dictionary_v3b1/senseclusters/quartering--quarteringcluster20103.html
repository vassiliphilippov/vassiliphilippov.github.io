quartering

noun
military accommodations or lodging
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔrdərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtərɪŋ / (BrE)
accommodations provided, especially to military personnel, for living or lodging

Examples

Military quartering
During the war, the town objected to the quartering of troops in private homes.
The general inspected the quartering arrangements to ensure they met proper standards.
The soldiers were grateful for the comfortable quartering provided at the barracks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: billet - a place where soldiers are lodged temporarily
