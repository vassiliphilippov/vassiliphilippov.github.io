comic book

noun
magazine of comic strips or stories
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑː.mɪk bʊk / (AmE), / ˈkɒ.mɪk bʊk / (BrE)
A magazine or bound collection featuring comic strips or stories presented in comic-strip format, often focusing on superheroes, humor, or adventure.

Examples

A comic book series
He used to spend every Saturday afternoon reading his favorite comic book series.
She collects rare comic books and keeps them in pristine condition.
The film was adapted from a popular comic book published in the 1980s.
