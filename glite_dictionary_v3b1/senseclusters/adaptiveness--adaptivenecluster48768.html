adaptiveness

noun
ability to adjust
Pronunciation: / əˈdæptɪvnəs / (AmE), / əˈdæptɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being able to adjust easily to new conditions or changes

Examples

High adaptiveness
Her adaptiveness helped her succeed in a rapidly changing work environment.
The adaptiveness of the species allowed it to survive in different climates.
Employers value adaptiveness when hiring for roles that require learning new skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flexibility - the ability to change or be changed easily
  • antonym: rigidity - the quality of being unable or unwilling to change
