nonconformism

noun

Synonyms

  • nonconformity
    7.7 0.23/m
    nonconformity names a refusal to follow accepted social or religious rules, while nonconformism stresses the active practice or principle of that stance.
  • unorthodoxy
    8.8 0.02/m
    unorthodoxy refers to holding beliefs that break from accepted customs, while nonconformism highlights the practice or principle of not following them.
  • unconformity
    9.3 0.00/m
    unconformity describes the state of not following accepted customs or standards, while nonconformism names the guiding practice or principle behind that choice.
  • nonconformance
    8.1 0.03/m
    nonconformance names failure to meet formal rules or requirements, while nonconformism speaks to a broader practice or belief of not following social customs.
