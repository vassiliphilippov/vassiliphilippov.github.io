Santa Sofia

proper noun
historic cathedral and mosque in Istanbul
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌsɑːn.tə soʊˈfiː.ə / (AmE), / ˌsæn.tə səʊˈfiː.ə / (BrE)
a monumental building in Istanbul, originally built as a Byzantine Christian cathedral in the 6th century, later converted into a mosque, and now a museum; renowned for its architectural and historical significance

Examples

Santa Sofia museum
Santa Sofia has served as both a church and a mosque over its long history.
During our stay in Istanbul, we made sure to visit Santa Sofia to admire its majestic domes and mosaics.
Many art historians consider Santa Sofia a masterpiece of Byzantine architecture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Hagia Sophia - the Turkish and Greek name for the same historic monument in Istanbul
