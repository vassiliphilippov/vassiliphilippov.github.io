Santa Sofia

proper noun
historic cathedral and mosque in Istanbul
Pronunciation: / ˌsɑːn.tə soʊˈfiː.ə / (AmE), / ˌsæn.tə səʊˈfiː.ə / (BrE)
a monumental building in Istanbul, originally built as a Byzantine Christian cathedral in the 6th century, later converted into a mosque, and now a museum; renowned for its architectural and historical significance
