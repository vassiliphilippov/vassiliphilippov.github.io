matrix

noun
tissue at base of nail
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪ.trɪks / (AmE), / ˈmeɪ.trɪks / (BrE)
the layer of tissue at the base of a fingernail or toenail from which the nail grows

Examples

Nail matrix
Damage to the matrix can cause permanent deformity of the nail.
Infections near the matrix may affect nail growth.
The matrix is responsible for producing new nail cells.
