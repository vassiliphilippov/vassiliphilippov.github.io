homeschooling

noun
education at home
3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈhoʊm.skuː.lɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈhəʊm.skuː.lɪŋ/ (BrE)
the practice of educating children at home instead of sending them to a traditional school

Examples

Homeschooling children
Many parents choose homeschooling to provide a personalized education for their children.
The homeschooling community often organizes group activities and field trips.
Some families switch to homeschooling because they want more flexibility in their schedules.
Some parents choose homeschooling to provide a personalized learning experience for their children.
The rise of online resources has made homeschooling more accessible to families around the world.
After moving to a rural area, they decided that homeschooling was the best option for their kids.

Related Terms

  • synonym: home education - the education of children at home by parents or tutors
  • antonym: schooling - education received at a school
  • synonym: unschooling - an informal learning approach without a fixed curriculum, often at home
