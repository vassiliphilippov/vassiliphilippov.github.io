sweet

adjective
not soured or preserved (esp. milk)
1.0 2.17/m
Pronunciation: / swit / (AmE), / swiÀêt / (BrE)
(of food, especially milk) not soured, fermented, or preserved; fresh.

Examples

Sweet milk delivery
Farmers delivered sweet milk to homes daily before refrigeration was common.
She prefers sweet milk in her tea, rather than the tangy flavor of soured milk.
The recipe specifically calls for sweet cream, not the cultured kind.

Synonyms

  • unfermented
    5.6 0.04/m
    unfermented means not changed by fermentation and is technical, while sweet describes milk that tastes not sour and fresh.
  • fresh
    1.0 51/m
    fresh means recently produced and ready to eat, while sweet describes milk that tastes not sour or spoiled.
