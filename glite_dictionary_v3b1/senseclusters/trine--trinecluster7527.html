trine

noun
astrological aspect
Pronunciation: / traɪn / (AmE), / traɪn / (BrE)
an aspect in astrology where two planets are 120 degrees apart, considered harmonious or favorable

Examples

A trine aspect
The astrologer explained that a trine between the Sun and Jupiter brings good fortune.
Many believe that a trine aspect in a birth chart indicates natural talent.
When two planets form a trine, their energies are said to flow easily together.
The astrologer explained that a trine between the Sun and Moon brings harmony.
Her birth chart features several trines, which are believed to indicate natural talents.
A trine aspect is often seen as a positive influence in astrology.

Related Terms

  • antonym: opposition - an astrological aspect of 180 degrees, often seen as difficult or tense
  • degree lower: sextile - an astrological aspect of 60 degrees, considered positive but less strong than a trine
  • antonym: square - an astrological aspect of 90 degrees, often seen as challenging
