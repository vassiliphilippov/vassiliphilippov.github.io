raki

noun
anise-flavored spirit
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːki / (AmE), / ˈræki / (BrE)
a strong, clear, anise-flavored alcoholic drink popular in Turkey and the Balkans

Examples

Turkish raki glass
At the end of the meal, they offered us a glass of raki as a traditional gesture.
Many people enjoy drinking raki with meze in Turkish restaurants.
He brought a bottle of raki back from his trip to Istanbul.
