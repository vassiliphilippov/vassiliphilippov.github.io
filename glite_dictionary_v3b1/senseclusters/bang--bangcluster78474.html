bang

noun
a sudden loud noise
Pronunciation: / bæŋ / (AmE), / bæŋ / (BrE)
a sudden loud noise or a forceful blow or impact

Examples

A loud bang
Suddenly, a bang from the engine signaled that something had gone wrong.
The door closed with a loud bang that startled everyone in the room.
We heard a bang upstairs and rushed to see what had happened.
After the accident, she remembered nothing except feeling a painful bang to her head.
He got a bang on the elbow during the match but kept playing.
The cabinet door shut with a loud bang, startling everyone in the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bash - a heavy blow
  • synonym: boom - a deep, prolonged, resonant sound
  • synonym: crash - a sudden loud noise, as of something breaking or falling
  • synonym: knock - a sharp blow or impact
  • degree lower: tap - a light touch or gentle blow
  • antonym: whisper - a soft, low sound or voice
