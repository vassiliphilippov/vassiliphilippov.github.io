antithetical

adjective
opposite or in conflict; contrasted
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.təˈθɛt.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌæn.tɪˈθet.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
directly opposed or contrasted; mutually incompatible or opposite in character or purpose.

Examples

Antithetical views
His views on the issue were antithetical to those of the majority.
The new policy is antithetical to everything the company once stood for.
Their beliefs are fundamentally antithetical and cannot be reconciled.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contrary - opposite in nature, direction, or meaning
  • synonym: opposite - completely different; being the other of two related things
