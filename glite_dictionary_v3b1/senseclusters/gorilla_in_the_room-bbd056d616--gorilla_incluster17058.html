gorilla in the room

noun
obvious but ignored problem
8.7
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈrɪl.ə ɪn ðə ruːm / (AmE), / ɡəˈrɪl.ə ɪn ðə ruːm / (BrE)
an obvious problem or issue that everyone is aware of but chooses to ignore or avoid discussing

Examples

The gorilla in the room
By not addressing the gorilla in the room, the team failed to solve its biggest challenge.
She finally brought up the gorilla in the room during the meeting, forcing everyone to confront the issue.
The company's declining sales are the gorilla in the room that no one wants to talk about.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elephant in the room - a major problem or controversial issue which is ignored or avoided
