mechanically

adverb
in a machinelike, emotionless way
6.7 3.6
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈkæn.ɪk.li/ /; / /mə.ˈkæn.ɪk.liː/ (AmE), /mə.ˈkæn.ɪk.li/ /; / /mə.ˈkæn.ɪk.liː/ (BrE)
In a way that shows no emotion or spontaneity, as if performed by a machine.

Examples

She smiled mechanically
After years of practice, she played the piano piece almost mechanically.
He responded mechanically to each question, lacking genuine enthusiasm.
She nodded mechanically, barely listening to the discussion.
