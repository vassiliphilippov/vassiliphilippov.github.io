underfed

adjective
not getting enough food
4.6 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈʌn.dər.fed/ (AmE), /ˈʌn.də.fed/ (BrE)

Examples

The veterinarian was concerned about the underfed puppies brought into the shelter.
