underfed

adjective
not getting enough food
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈfɛd / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈfɛd / (BrE)
not having enough food to eat; lacking adequate nourishment

Examples

An underfed child
During the drought, many families became weak and underfed due to food shortages.
The documentary showed underfed children in need of urgent medical care.
The veterinarian was concerned about the underfed puppies brought into the shelter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: malnourished - not getting enough nutrients or proper nutrition
  • antonym: well-fed - having plenty of good food to eat
