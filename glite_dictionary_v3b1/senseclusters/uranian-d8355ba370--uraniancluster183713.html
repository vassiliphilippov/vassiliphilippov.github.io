Uranian

noun
inhabitant of Uranus
9.9
Pronunciation: / jʊˈreɪniən / (AmE), / jʊˈreɪniən / (BrE)
a fictional inhabitant or native of the planet Uranus, often appearing in science fiction stories

Examples

A Uranian alien
The explorers were surprised to meet a curious Uranian on the icy surface of the planet.
In the novel, a Uranian helps the human crew repair their damaged spaceship.
Legends say that every Uranian can communicate through telepathy across great distances.
