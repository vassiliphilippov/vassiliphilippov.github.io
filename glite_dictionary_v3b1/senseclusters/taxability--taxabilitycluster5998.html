taxability

noun
liability to taxation
Pronunciation: / tæk.səˈbɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / tæk.səˈbɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being liable to taxation; the status of being subject to tax under law

Examples

The taxability of income
Questions about the taxability of overseas income are common among expatriates.
The taxability of certain benefits depends on current government regulations.
We reviewed the taxability of the settlement payments before filing the report.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nontaxable - not subject to tax under law
  • synonym: taxable - subject to tax by law
