knick

noun
small decorative object
Pronunciation: / nɪk / (AmE), / nɪk / (BrE)
an informal shortened form of 'knickknack', meaning a small decorative object or trinket

Examples

A knick on the shelf
She arranged each knick carefully on the shelf in her living room.
After cleaning, he noticed a missing knick from the collection on the mantelpiece.
The antique shop was filled with every kind of knick imaginable.
