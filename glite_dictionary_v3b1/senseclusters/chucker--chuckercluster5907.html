chucker

noun
illegal cricket bowler
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌkər / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌkə / (BrE)
a cricket player, especially a bowler, who is judged to have an illegal throwing action instead of a proper bowling motion

Examples

Cricket chucker
The umpire called the chucker for his illegal bowling action during the match.
Controversy arose when a famous player was labeled a chucker after several complaints.
Some believe the rules for identifying a chucker in cricket should be clearer.
