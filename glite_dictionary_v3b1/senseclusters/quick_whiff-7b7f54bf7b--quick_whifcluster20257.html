quick whiff

noun
brief, quick inhalation of smell
3.8
Pronunciation: / kwɪk wɪf / (AmE), / kwɪk wɪf / (BrE)
a brief inhalation to take in the scent of something quickly

Examples

A quick whiff of perfume
He opened the jar and took a quick whiff before passing it around.
The dog gave the stranger a quick whiff and wagged its tail.
With a quick whiff of the ocean breeze, she felt instantly refreshed.
