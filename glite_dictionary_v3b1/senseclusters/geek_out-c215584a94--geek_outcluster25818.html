geek out

verb
become excited about a specialized topic
7.5
Pronunciation: / ɡik aʊt / (AmE), / ɡiːk aʊt / (BrE)
to show great excitement or enthusiasm, often about a highly specialized or technical subject

Examples

To geek out excitedly
I totally geeked out at the comic convention last weekend.
She was geeking out about the rare collectibles in the shop.
Whenever a new superhero movie is released, he tends to geek out over every detail.
