skeletal system

noun
framework of bones and cartilages in the body
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈskɛlətl ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈskɛlətəl ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
the framework of bones and cartilages that supports and protects the body of an animal

Examples

The skeletal system structure
Calcium is important for keeping the skeletal system healthy and strong.
Injury or disease can weaken the skeletal system, affecting mobility and strength.
The skeletal system provides structure and support for the human body.
