airing

noun
public discussion of a subject
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈeə.rɪŋ / (BrE)
an occasion when a subject or issue is discussed openly and publicly, often to encourage debate or consideration of different views

Examples

Public airing of ideas
Let's give the proposal a fair airing before making any decisions.
The documentary ensured the subject got a much-needed public airing.
The issue received a thorough airing during the meeting, with everyone contributing their thoughts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: debate - a formal discussion on a particular topic
  • synonym: discussion - the act of talking about something to reach a decision or exchange ideas
