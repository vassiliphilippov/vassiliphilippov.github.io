bargeboard

noun
gable roof board
9.0
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑːrdʒ.bɔːrd/ /; / /ˈbɑːrdʒ.boʊrd/ (AmE), /ˈbɑːdʒ.bɔːd/ /; / /ˈbɑːdʒ.bəʊd/ (BrE)
