bargeboard

noun
gable roof board
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑrdʒˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbɑːdʒˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a board attached to the projecting edge of a gable roof, often for strength or decorative effect

Examples

Decorative bargeboard
The carpenter carefully installed the bargeboard to protect the roof's edge from the weather.
Victorian houses often feature a decorative bargeboard along the gable.
Replacing the damaged bargeboard improved both the appearance and durability of the roof.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fascia - a board running along the edge of a roof
