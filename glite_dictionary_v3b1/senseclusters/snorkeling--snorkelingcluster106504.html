snorkeling

noun
swimming on water's surface using a snorkel
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɔrkəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsnɔːkəlɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity of swimming on or through a body of water while equipped with a diving mask, a shaped tube called a snorkel, and usually fins, allowing the swimmer to observe underwater attractions for extended periods with relatively little effort.

Examples

Snorkeling with fins
Before snorkeling, always check that your mask and snorkel fit properly.
Her favorite vacation activity is snorkeling in clear tropical waters.
We spent the afternoon snorkeling along the coral reef, watching colorful fish swim by.

Related Terms

  • synonym: free diving - underwater diving without breathing apparatus; holding one's breath
  • synonym: scuba diving - swimming underwater using a self-contained underwater breathing apparatus
