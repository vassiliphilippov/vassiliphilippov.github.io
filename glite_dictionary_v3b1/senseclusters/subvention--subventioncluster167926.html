subvention

noun
financial support or assistance
Pronunciation: / səbˈvɛnʃən / (AmE), / sʌbˈvɛnʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of providing financial support or assistance, especially from a government or organization

Examples

Subvention from donors
Many universities rely on subvention from private donors to fund research initiatives.
The government announced a new subvention program to support renewable energy projects.
Without the annual subvention, the community arts center would have had to close its doors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: subsidy - a sum of money granted by the government or a public body to assist an industry or business
