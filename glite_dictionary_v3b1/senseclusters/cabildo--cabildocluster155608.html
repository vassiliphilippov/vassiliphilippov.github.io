cabildo

noun
colonial town council
Pronunciation: / kəˈbɪldoʊ / (AmE), / kəˈbɪldəʊ / (BrE)
a town council or municipal building in Spanish-speaking countries, especially during colonial times

Examples

Cabildo building
The cabildo played a central role in the administration of colonial cities.
Tourists often visit the old cabildo to learn about the city's history.
During festivals, the cabildo becomes the center of community celebrations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: city hall - the building where a city government is based
  • synonym: municipal council - a local governing body for a town or city
