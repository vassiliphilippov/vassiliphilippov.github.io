heating element

noun
component that generates heat in appliances
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhitɪŋ ˌɛləmənt / (AmE), / ˈhiːtɪŋ ˌɛlɪmənt / (BrE)
The part of a heater or electrical appliance that converts energy into heat.

Examples

Electric heating element
Electric kettles use a heating element to boil water quickly and efficiently.
She replaced the faulty heating element in her space heater herself.
The oven stopped working because the heating element burned out.
