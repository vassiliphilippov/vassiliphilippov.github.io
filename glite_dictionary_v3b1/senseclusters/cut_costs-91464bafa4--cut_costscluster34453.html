cut costs

verb
reduce expenses
Pronunciation: / kʌt kɔsts / (AmE), / kʌt kɒsts / (BrE)
to reduce the amount of money that is spent, especially in order to improve efficiency or profitability

Examples

To cut costs quickly
By switching to more efficient equipment, we can significantly cut costs each year.
Many families are looking for ways to cut costs during tough economic times.
