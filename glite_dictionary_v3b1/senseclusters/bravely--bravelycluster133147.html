bravely

adverb
in a courageous manner
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪvli / (AmE), / ˈbreɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that shows courage or bravery.

Examples

To bravely face fears
Even though he was frightened, he bravely faced his fears.
She bravely spoke out against injustice in her community.
The soldiers bravely defended their position despite overwhelming odds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: courageously - in a way that shows courage
  • antonym: fearfully - in a way that shows fear rather than courage
