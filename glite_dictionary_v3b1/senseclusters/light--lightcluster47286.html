light

adjective
using small or lightweight equipment (military or industry)
5.4
Pronunciation: / laɪt / (AmE), / laɪt / (BrE)
using small, lightweight, or less powerful equipment (military, industry, or elements)

Examples

Light industry
Many developing countries focus on light industry rather than heavy manufacturing.
The commander decided to deploy light infantry for the fast-moving operation.
The patrol carried only light weapons to remain agile.
In nuclear reactors, light elements are preferred as moderators due to their atomic characteristics.
Ordinary water is composed of light hydrogen and oxygen atoms.
Scientists compare the properties of light and heavy water in experiments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heavy - (of the military or industry) using large or powerful arms or equipment
