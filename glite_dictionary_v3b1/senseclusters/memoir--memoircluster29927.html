memoir

noun
scholarly or scientific essay
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛmwɑr / (AmE), / ˈmɛmwɑː / (BrE)
An essay or written account on a scientific or scholarly topic, often published as part of academic literature.

Examples

A scientific memoir
His memoir addressed advances in medical research over the last decade.
She published a memoir on the early theories of electricity.
The journal featured a memoir summarizing the latest archaeological findings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: monograph - a detailed scholarly paper on a single subject
  • synonym: treatise - a written work dealing formally with a subject
