berry

verb
to produce berries
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛri / (AmE), / ˈbɛri / (BrE)
to produce or become covered with berries, especially referring to a plant during its fruiting period

Examples

Bushes berry in autumn
The old hawthorn is berrying heavily this autumn, attracting flocks of thrushes.
Many shrubs in the garden will start berrying in late summer.
After a rainy spring, the bushes are berrying more than usual this year.
