pompadour

noun
hairstyle with hair brushed up from forehead
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːmpəˌdɔr / (AmE), / ˈpɒmpədɔː / (BrE)
a hairstyle in which the front hair is brushed or swept upward from the forehead and worn high over the forehead, sometimes over a roll

Examples

Classic pompadour hairstyle
After her haircut, she tried a modern twist on the traditional pompadour.
He styled his hair in a classic pompadour for the party.
The rockabilly singer was famous for his slick pompadour and flashy suits.
