Australian pea

noun
evergreen vine with edible pods
Pronunciation: / əˈstreɪliən piː / (AmE), / ɒˈstreɪlɪən piː / (BrE)
a South African evergreen vine with rosy purple flowers and edible pods, sometimes grown as green manure and occasionally classified in the genus Dolichos

Examples

Australian pea vine
Clusters of rosy purple flowers covered the Australian pea vine throughout spring.
The farmer planted Australian pea in the field to improve the soil's fertility.
You can harvest and cook the young pods of the Australian pea much like snap beans.
