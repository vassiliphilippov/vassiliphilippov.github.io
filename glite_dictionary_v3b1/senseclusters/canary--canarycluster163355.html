canary

noun
small songbird, often yellow
6.5
Pronunciation: / kəˈnɛri / (AmE), / kəˈneəri / (BrE)
a small songbird, especially a yellowish-green finch native to the Canary Islands, often kept as a pet for its singing

Examples

Pet canary singing
The canary filled the room with its cheerful song each morning.
They bought a bright yellow canary for their daughter’s birthday.
Wild canaries are native to the Canary Islands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: finch - a small to medium-sized passerine bird, often with a conical beak
