savoy cabbage

noun
variety of cabbage with crinkly leaves
Pronunciation: / səˈvɔɪ ˈkæbɪdʒ / (AmE), / səˈvɔɪ ˈkabɪdʒ / (BrE)
a type of cabbage with a round, compact head and crinkly, wrinkled leaves, used as a vegetable
