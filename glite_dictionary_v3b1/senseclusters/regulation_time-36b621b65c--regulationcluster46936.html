regulation time

noun
normal duration of a sports game
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛɡjəˈleɪʃən taɪm / (AmE), / ˌrɛɡjʊˈleɪʃən taɪm / (BrE)
(in sports) the normal period set by the rules for the duration of a game, not including any extra time or overtime.

Examples

End of regulation time
If the teams are tied at the end of regulation time, they will go into a penalty shootout.
She scored the winning goal just before regulation time expired.
The match ended in regulation time with a score of 2-2, so extra time was needed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: overtime - additional time played when a game is tied at the end of regulation time
