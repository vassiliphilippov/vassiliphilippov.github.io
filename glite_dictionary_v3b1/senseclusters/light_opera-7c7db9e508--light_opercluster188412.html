light opera

noun
comic or lighthearted opera
1.0
Pronunciation: / laɪt ˈɑː.pɚ.ə / (AmE), / laɪt ˈɒp.ər.ə / (BrE)
a type of opera with a light or comic theme, usually shorter and less serious than grand opera

Examples

A light opera show
Many people prefer light opera to more dramatic works because of its playful tone.
She performed in a light opera at the community theater last summer.
This season, the local company will stage a classic light opera for families.
