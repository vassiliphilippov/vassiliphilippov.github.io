toothily

adverb
in a biting manner
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːθɪli / (AmE), / ˈtuːθɪli / (BrE)
in a biting, harsh, or aggressive manner, especially in speech or behavior that is sharp or cutting

Examples

Speak toothily to a rival
She smiled toothily at her rival, making her discomfort clear.
The critic responded toothily to the author's latest book, sparing no harsh words.
He laughed toothily, his amusement edged with a hint of menace.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gently - in a mild or soft manner
  • synonym: sharply - in a harsh or cutting manner
