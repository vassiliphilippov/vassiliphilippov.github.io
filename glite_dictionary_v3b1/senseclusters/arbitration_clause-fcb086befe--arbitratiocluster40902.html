arbitration clause

noun
contract term requiring arbitration of disputes

Pronunciation: /ɑːr.bɪ.ˈtreɪ.ʃən klɔːz/ /; / /ɑːr.bə.ˈtreɪ.ʃən klɔːz/ (AmE), /ɑː.bɪ.ˈtreɪ.ʃən klɔːz/ /; / /ɑː.bə.ˈtreɪ.ʃən klɔːz/ (BrE)

Examples

Many businesses use an arbitration clause to avoid lengthy litigation.
