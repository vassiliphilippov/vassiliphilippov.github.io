dosemeter

noun
instrument that measures radiation dose
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊsɪˌmiːtər / (AmE), / ˈdəʊsɪˌmiːtə / (BrE)
an instrument used to measure an absorbed dose of ionizing radiation, such as X-rays or radioactivity
