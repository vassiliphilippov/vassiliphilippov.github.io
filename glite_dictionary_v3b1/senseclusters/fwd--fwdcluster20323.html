fwd

noun
front-wheel drive
8.0
Pronunciation: / ɛf ˈdʌbəlju ˈdi / (AmE), / ɛf ˈdʌbəljuː ˈdiː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for front-wheel drive, a vehicle layout where the engine powers the front wheels

Examples

The new model is available in both fwd and all-wheel drive versions.
Many compact cars use fwd to improve fuel efficiency and handling.
He preferred fwd vehicles for driving in snowy conditions.
