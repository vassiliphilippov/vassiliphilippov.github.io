postmarking

noun
applying a postmark
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstˌmɑrkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstˌmɑːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of applying a postmark to mail to indicate the date and place of mailing

Examples

Accurate postmarking
Accurate postmarking is important for tracking the delivery date of letters.
The postmarking of packages ensures proof of when they were sent.
Modern machines have automated the postmarking process in large postal facilities.

Related Terms
