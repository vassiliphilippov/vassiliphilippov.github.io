levy

verb
assemble or enlist troops by authority
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛvi / (AmE), / ˈlɛvi / (BrE)
to assemble or enlist (troops or an army), especially by official order or authority

Examples

To levy soldiers
In times of crisis, governments may levy troops from the population.
The feudal lord was authorized to levy soldiers for the upcoming campaign.
The king decided to levy an army to defend the kingdom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recruit - enlist new people into the armed forces
