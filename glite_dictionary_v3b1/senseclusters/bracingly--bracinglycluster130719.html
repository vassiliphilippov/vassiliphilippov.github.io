bracingly

adverb
refreshingly, invigoratingly
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪsɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈbreɪsɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is refreshing, invigorating, or stimulating, often making you feel more awake or alert

Examples

Bracingly cold air
The mountain air felt bracingly cold as we reached the summit.
She spoke bracingly about the challenges ahead, inspiring everyone in the room.
After a bracingly honest conversation, they finally understood each other better.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dully - in a way that lacks interest or excitement
  • synonym: invigoratingly - in a way that gives energy or strength
  • synonym: refreshingly - in a pleasantly new or different way
