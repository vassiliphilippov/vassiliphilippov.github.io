lacto-vegetarianism

noun
vegetarian diet including dairy products
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌlæktoʊ vɛdʒəˈtɛriːəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌlæktəʊ vɛdʒɪˈteəriːənɪzəm / (BrE)
a type of vegetarian diet that includes dairy products as well as vegetables, fruits, grains, and nuts, but excludes meat, fish, and eggs.

Examples

Lacto-vegetarianism diet
Lacto-vegetarianism allows for cheese and yogurt, unlike veganism.
Many people adopt lacto-vegetarianism for ethical or religious reasons.
The festival catered specifically to those following lacto-vegetarianism.
