Marfan syndrome

noun
genetic connective tissue disorder
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːrfæn ˈsɪnˌdroʊm / (AmE), / ˈmɑːfæn ˈsɪndrəʊm / (BrE)
a genetic disorder affecting connective tissue, often resulting in tall stature, long limbs, and heart or eye problems

Examples

Marfan syndrome symptoms
The doctor suspected Marfan syndrome because the patient was unusually tall with long fingers.
People with Marfan syndrome often need regular heart checkups to monitor their health.
Her diagnosis of Marfan syndrome explained her flexible joints and vision problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Ehlers-Danlos syndrome - a group of genetic disorders affecting connective tissue
  • synonym: homocystinuria - a genetic disorder with symptoms similar to Marfan syndrome
