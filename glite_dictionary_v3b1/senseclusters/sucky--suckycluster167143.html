sucky

adjective
very bad; unpleasant
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌki / (AmE), / ˈsʌki / (BrE)
very bad, disappointing, or unpleasant in quality or experience, especially in informal speech

Examples

A sucky day
It was a really sucky day because everything seemed to go wrong.
The movie got sucky reviews from almost every critic.
I had a sucky experience with their customer service last week.
