patchwork quilt

noun
quilt made from sewn fabric patches
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpætʃ.wɜrk kwɪlt /; / ˈpætʃˌwɜrk kwɪlt / (AmE), / ˈpætʃ.wɜːk kwɪlt /; / ˈpætʃwɜːk kwɪlt / (BrE)
