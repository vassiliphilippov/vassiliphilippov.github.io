sanctum

noun
sacred or private place; sanctuary
Pronunciation: / ˈsæŋktəm / (AmE), / ˈsaŋktəm / (BrE)
A sacred or private place, especially one regarded as holy or used for worship or pilgrimage; a sanctuary.

Examples

Temple sanctum
Many devotees traveled for miles to reach the holy sanctum of the shrine.
She entered the inner sanctum, a place rarely seen by outsiders.
The ancient temple stood as a sanctum for thousands of pilgrims each year.
