handwear

noun
items worn on the hands (e.g. gloves, mittens)
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈhændˌwɛə / (BrE)
items of clothing worn on the hands, such as gloves or mittens

Examples

Warm handwear
During the winter, handwear like gloves and mittens is essential for staying warm.
She forgot her handwear and her fingers were numb in the cold.
Store shelves are stocked with various types of handwear for different activities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glove - a piece of clothing covering the hand and wrist
  • synonym: mitten - a type of glove with two sections, one for the thumb and the other for the fingers
