virtue

noun
sexual morality, chastity
Pronunciation: / ˈvɝː.tʃu / (AmE), / ˈvɜː.tʃuː / (BrE)
Morality or chastity, especially in reference to sexual purity.

Examples

A woman's virtue
In some cultures, a woman's virtue was highly valued and strictly protected.
Many classic novels focus on the theme of a heroine's virtue being put to the test.
She was praised for preserving her virtue in difficult circumstances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chastity - the state of refraining from sexual intercourse or being sexually pure
