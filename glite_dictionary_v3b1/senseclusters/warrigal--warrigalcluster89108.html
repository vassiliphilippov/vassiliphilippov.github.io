warrigal

noun
Australian wild dog; dingo
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrɪɡəl / (AmE), / ˈwɒrɪɡəl / (BrE)
A wolflike yellowish-brown wild dog native to Australia; also known as a dingo.

Examples

Wild warrigal dog
Farmers often worry about warrigals preying on their livestock.
The warrigal is an important figure in many Aboriginal stories.
Unlike domestic dogs, the warrigal is well adapted to the harsh Australian outback.
