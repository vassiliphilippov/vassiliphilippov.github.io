submitter

noun
person who submits something for consideration
Pronunciation: / səbˈmɪtər / (AmE), / səbˈmɪtə / (BrE)
a person who formally presents something, such as an application, proposal, or document, for consideration or decision by others

Examples

The submitter of a form
As the original submitter, she was responsible for providing all the supporting documents.
Every submitter must include a cover letter with their manuscript.
The competition organizers thanked each submitter for participating in the event.

Related Terms

  • antonym: responder - someone who responds to a request or call for submissions
