Hypermastigina

noun
group of cellulose-producing flagellate protozoa
9.9
Pronunciation: / haɪpərˌmæstɪˈdʒaɪnə / (AmE), / hʌɪpəˌmastɪˈdʒiːnə / (BrE)
A taxonomic group of cellulose-producing flagellate protozoa, often found in the guts of wood-eating insects.
