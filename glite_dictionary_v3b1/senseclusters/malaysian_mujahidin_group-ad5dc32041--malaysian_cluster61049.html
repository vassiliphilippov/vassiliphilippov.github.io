Malaysian Mujahidin Group

proper noun
Southeast Asian militant Islamist group (est. 1993)
9.9
Pronunciation: / məˈleɪʒən muːˈdʒɑːhɪdiːn ɡruːp / (AmE), / məˈleɪziən mjuːˈdʒɑːhɪdiːn ɡruːp / (BrE)
a clandestine militant Islamist organization, formed in 1993, that has operated in Southeast Asia and is linked to al-Qaeda, advocating militant Islam and involved in terrorist activities in Indonesia, Malaysia, Singapore, and the Philippines.
