tele-

x
prefix for distance
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛli / (AmE), / ˈtɛli / (BrE)
a prefix meaning 'far' or 'distant', used at the start of words related to distance or communication over distance

Examples

tele- prefix
The prefix tele- is found in words like 'telephone' and 'television'.
When you see tele- at the start of a word, it often relates to distance.
The word 'telegraph' uses the tele- prefix to show communication over long distances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trans- - a prefix meaning 'across' or 'beyond'
