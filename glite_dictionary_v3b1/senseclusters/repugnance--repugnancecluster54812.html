repugnance

noun
strong feeling of dislike
9.2
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpʌɡnəns / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌɡnəns / (BrE)
a strong feeling of dislike or disgust; intense aversion.

Examples

Strong repugnance feeling
His repugnance at the sight of blood was evident.
She felt a deep repugnance towards cruelty of any kind.
There was clear repugnance in her voice when she spoke of the incident.

Related Terms

  • antonym: attraction - a feeling of being drawn to someone or something
  • synonym: aversion - a strong feeling of dislike or opposition
  • synonym: disgust - a strong sense of revulsion or profound disapproval aroused by something unpleasant or offensive
