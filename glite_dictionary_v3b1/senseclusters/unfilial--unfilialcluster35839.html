unfilial

adjective
lacking filial respect
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪliəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪliəl / (BrE)
not showing the respect, love, or duty expected from a child toward their parent or parents

Examples

Unfilial behaviour
His unfilial attitude deeply hurt his aging parents.
The community frowned upon her unfilial neglect of her family responsibilities.
It was considered unfilial to ignore the needs of one's parents in their old age.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disrespectful - showing a lack of respect
  • antonym: filial - showing the respect or duty of a child to a parent
