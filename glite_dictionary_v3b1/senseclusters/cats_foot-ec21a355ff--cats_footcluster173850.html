cat's foot

noun
a low-growing perennial herb with white flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈkæts ˌfʊt / (AmE), / ˈkæts ˌfʊt / (BrE)
a low-growing perennial herb with leaves covered in whitish down and clusters of small white flowers, commonly found in temperate regions.
