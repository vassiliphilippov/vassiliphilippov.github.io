bedroom suite

noun
set of matching bedroom furniture
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdˌruːm swiːt / (AmE), / ˈbɛdruːm swiːt / (BrE)
A matching set of furniture designed for use in a bedroom, typically including a bed, dresser, and one or more nightstands.
