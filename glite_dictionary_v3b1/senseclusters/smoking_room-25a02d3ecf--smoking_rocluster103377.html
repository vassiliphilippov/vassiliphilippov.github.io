smoking room

noun
room set apart for smokers
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsmoʊkɪŋ ruːm / (AmE), / ˈsməʊkɪŋ ruːm / (BrE)
A room in a hotel, club, or other public place set apart for people to smoke in.

Examples

A smoking room
He stepped into the smoking room to enjoy a cigar after dinner.
The hotel offers a smoking room for guests who wish to smoke indoors.
You can find the smoking room near the main lounge on the second floor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lounge - a public room (as in a hotel or airport) for waiting or relaxing
