crick

noun
painful muscle spasm in neck or back
6.6 6.3
Pronunciation: / krɪk / (AmE), / krɪk / (BrE)
a sudden, painful muscle spasm, especially in the neck or back, often limiting movement temporarily

Examples

A crick in the neck
A sudden crick in my back made it hard to stand up straight.
He woke up with a crick in his neck after sleeping awkwardly.
She couldn't turn her head because of a painful crick that developed during the drive.
