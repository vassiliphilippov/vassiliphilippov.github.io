crest

noun
tuft or ridge on an animal's head
3.7 3.5
Pronunciation: /krest/ (AmE), /krest/ (BrE)
a tuft or ridge of feathers, fur, or skin on the head of a bird or other animal, often used for display or identification

Examples

Bird crest
Certain lizards have a colorful crest running along their heads and necks.
The cockatoo raised its crest when it became excited.
The robin's small crest gave it a distinctive look among other birds.
