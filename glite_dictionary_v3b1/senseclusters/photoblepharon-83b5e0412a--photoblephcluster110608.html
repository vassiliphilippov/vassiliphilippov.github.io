Photoblepharon

noun
genus of flashlight fish
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊˈblɛfərɒn / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊˈblɛfərɒn / (BrE)
A genus of flashlight fishes in the family Anomalopidae, native to reefs in the Western Pacific Ocean, known for their bioluminescent organs.

Examples

Biologists study the Photoblepharon to better understand the evolution of bioluminescence in marine fish.
