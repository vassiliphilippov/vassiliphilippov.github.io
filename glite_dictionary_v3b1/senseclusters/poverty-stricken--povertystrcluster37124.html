poverty-stricken

adjective
extremely poor; lacking resources
5.1 0.80/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ëÀêv…ôrti Àåstr…™k…ôn / (AmE), / Ààp…ív…ôti Àåstr…™k…ôn / (BrE)
Extremely poor; having very little money or resources and needing help from others.

Examples

Poverty-stricken area
International aid was sent to the poverty-stricken region after the disaster.
Many poverty-stricken families struggle to provide enough food for their children.
She grew up in a poverty-stricken neighborhood where resources were scarce.
