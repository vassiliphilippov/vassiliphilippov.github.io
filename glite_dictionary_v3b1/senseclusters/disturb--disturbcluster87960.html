disturb

verb
interrupt or destroy peace/quiet
1.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstɝb / (AmE), / dɪˈstɜːb / (BrE)
to interrupt or destroy the peace or quiet of someone or something

Examples

To disturb the peace
If you need anything, just ask—otherwise, I won't disturb you.
Please do not disturb the animals while they are eating.
The loud music from next door disturbed his peaceful evening.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - state of peace or tranquility
  • synonym: interrupt - to stop the continuous progress of an activity
