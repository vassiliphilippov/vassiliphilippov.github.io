Atlantic spiny dogfish

noun
small Atlantic dogfish shark species
5.1
Pronunciation: / ətˈlæntɪk ˈspaɪni ˈdɔɡˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ətˈlæntɪk ˈspaɪni ˈdɒɡˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a small, slender shark (Squalus acanthias) found in Atlantic coastal waters of North America and Europe, noted for its sharp dorsal spines and common use in scientific dissection
