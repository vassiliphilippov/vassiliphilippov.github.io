lidless

adjective
always watchful or unblinking
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪdləs / (AmE), / ˈlɪdləs / (BrE)
(literary) always watchful or unblinking, as if unable to close the eyes; constantly observing.

Examples

Lidless eye
In the story, the mountain was said to have a lidless eye, always awake and watching.
Some described his lidless gaze as piercing and uncanny.
The fortress was guarded by a lidless camera that recorded every movement.
