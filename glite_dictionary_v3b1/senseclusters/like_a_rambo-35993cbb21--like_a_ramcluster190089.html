like a rambo

adverb
bravely or aggressively, like Rambo
Pronunciation: / laɪk ə ˈræmboʊ / (AmE), / laɪk ə ˈræmbəʊ / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely brave, aggressive, or fearless, like the character Rambo; boldly and with determination

Examples

Like a rambo attack
He stormed into the negotiations like a rambo, refusing to compromise.
They defended their position like a rambo, showing no signs of fear.
Whenever she faces a challenge, she handles it like a rambo and never backs down.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bravely - in a brave manner
  • degree higher: recklessly - acting without regard for danger or consequences
  • antonym: timidly - in a way that shows a lack of courage or confidence
