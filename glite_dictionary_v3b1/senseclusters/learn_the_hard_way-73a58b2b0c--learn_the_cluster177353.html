learn the hard way

verb
gain knowledge through difficulty or mistakes
1.0 4.9
Pronunciation: /lɜːrn ðə hɑːrd weɪ/ /; / /lɜːrn ðiː hɑːrd weɪ/ (AmE), /lɜːn ðə hɑːd weɪ/ /; / /lɜːn ðiː hɑːd weɪ/ (BrE)
