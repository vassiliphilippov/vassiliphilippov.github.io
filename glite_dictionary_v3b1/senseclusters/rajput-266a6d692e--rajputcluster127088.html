Rajput

proper noun
Indian warrior caste
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːdʒˌpʊt / (AmE), / ˈrɑːdʒˌpʊt / (BrE)
a member of a Hindu warrior caste from northern India, historically known for their military traditions and ruling dynasties

Examples

Rajput dynasty ruler
The Rajput clans played a significant role in shaping the history of Rajasthan.
Many Rajput rulers built impressive forts and palaces across northern India.
He proudly traced his ancestry to a noble Rajput family.
