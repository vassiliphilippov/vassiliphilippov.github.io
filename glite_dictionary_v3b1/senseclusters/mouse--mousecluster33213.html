mouse

noun
swollen bruise, especially near the eye
7.9
Pronunciation: / maʊs / (AmE), / maʊs / (BrE)
A swollen bruise, especially one around the eye, caused by a blow.

Examples

A black mouse
After slipping on the ice, he showed up to school with a nasty mouse under his left eye.
She wore sunglasses all week to cover the mouse she got during the soccer match.
The boxer left the ring sporting a large mouse as a memento of the fight.
