header

noun
hitting a soccer ball with the head
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdɚ / (AmE), / ˈhɛdə / (BrE)
(in soccer) the act of hitting the ball with your head.

Examples

Score with a header
He scored a goal with a powerful header in the second half.
Her perfectly timed header sent the crowd into a frenzy.
The striker missed an easy header from close range.
