unpublishable

adjective
not suitable for publication
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpʌblɪʃəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈpʌblɪʃəbl / (BrE)
not suitable or fit to be published, especially because of content, quality, or standards.

Examples

An unpublishable article
Much of his early writing was considered too controversial and unpublishable at the time.
Some passages in the memoir were so explicit that they rendered the book unpublishable.
The editor rejected the manuscript, stating that it was unpublishable in its current form.

Related Terms

  • antonym: publishable - suitable to be published
