unpublishable

adjective
not suitable for publication
3.5 6.0
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈpʌb.lɪ.ʃə.bəl/ /; / /ən.ˈpʌb.lɪ.ʃə.bəl/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈpʌb.lɪ.ʃə.bəl/ /; / /ən.ˈpʌb.lɪ.ʃə.bəl/ (BrE)
not suitable or fit to be published, especially because of content, quality, or standards.

Examples
