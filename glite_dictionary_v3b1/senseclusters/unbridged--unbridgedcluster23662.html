unbridged

adjective
without a bridge
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbrɪdʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbrɪdʒd / (BrE)
not having a bridge or not connected by a bridge

Examples

An unbridged river
The wide river remained unbridged, forcing villagers to use boats to cross.
For decades, the canyon was unbridged, isolating the two communities on either side.
The unbridged stream made it difficult for hikers to continue their journey after heavy rain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bridged - having a bridge or being connected by a bridge
