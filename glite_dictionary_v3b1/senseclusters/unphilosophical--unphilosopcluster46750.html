unphilosophical

adjective
not philosophical
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌfɪləˈsɑfɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌfɪləˈsɒfɪkəl / (BrE)

Examples

The discussion became unphilosophical when they ignored the underlying principles.
He gave an unphilosophical answer, avoiding any abstract or theoretical ideas.
