spelunking

noun
cave exploration
Pronunciation: / spəˈlʌŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / spəˈlʌŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of exploring caves for recreation or adventure

Examples

Go spelunking in caves
They spent the weekend spelunking in the vast network of underground caves.
Many adventure seekers enjoy spelunking as a way to discover hidden natural wonders.
Before spelunking, it is important to have the right equipment and training.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mountaineering - the sport of climbing mountains
