knocked-out

adjective
damaged; worn out
Pronunciation: / nɑktˈaʊt / (AmE), / nɒktˈaʊt / (BrE)
(informal) Damaged, worn out, or no longer functioning properly, typically as a result of heavy use or impact.

Examples

The knocked-out fence

Most of the machines in that workshop are pretty knocked-out from years of hard use.
The old truck is so knocked-out that it can barely run anymore.
