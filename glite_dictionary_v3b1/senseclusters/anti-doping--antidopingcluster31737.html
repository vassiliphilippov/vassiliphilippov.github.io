anti-doping

noun
drug control in sports
3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈæn.ti ˈdoʊ.pɪŋ/ /; / /ˈæn.tiː ˈdoʊ.pɪŋ/ /; / /ˈæn.taɪ ˈdoʊ.pɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈæn.tɪ ˈdəʊ.pɪŋ/ /; / /ˈæn.ti ˈdəʊ.pɪŋ/ (BrE)
the system or practice of preventing and detecting the use of banned performance-enhancing drugs in sports through testing and enforcement

Examples
