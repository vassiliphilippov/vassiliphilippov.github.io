praetorianism

noun
military rule in government
9.9
Pronunciation: /priː.ˈtɔːr.i.ə.nɪz.əm/ /; / /preɪ.ˈtɔːr.i.ə.nɪz.əm/ (AmE), /priː.ˈtɔː.ri.ə.nɪz.əm/ /; / /preɪ.ˈtɔː.ri.ə.nɪz.əm/ (BrE)
