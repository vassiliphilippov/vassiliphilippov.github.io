praetorianism

noun
military rule in government
9.9
Pronunciation: / priːˈtɔːriəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / priːˈtɔːriəˌnɪzəm /; / priːˈtɔːriənɪzəm / (BrE)
a system or practice in which the military controls or strongly influences the government, often through intervention or dominance
