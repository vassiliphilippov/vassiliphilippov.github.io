nonfamilial

adjective
not from same family
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnfəˈmɪliəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnfəˈmɪliəl / (BrE)
not related to or inherited from family; not running in families

Examples

Nonfamilial disease
The disease is considered nonfamilial because it does not run in families.
Researchers found that the two plant species are nonfamilial despite their similar appearance.
In this study, only nonfamilial cases of the condition were included for analysis.
Doctors determined that the cancer was nonfamilial and not linked to the patient's genetic background.
A nonfamilial case of the disorder suggests environmental factors may be involved.
Researchers are studying both familial and nonfamilial forms of the condition to understand its causes.
