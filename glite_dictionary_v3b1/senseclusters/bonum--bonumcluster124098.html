bonum

noun
property or goods
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊnəm / (AmE), / ˈbəʊnəm / (BrE)
property, goods, or possessions, especially as a legal or archaic term.

Examples

Bonum of the estate
The will specified how the bonum of the estate should be divided among the heirs.
In ancient legal documents, bonum often referred to all of a person's possessions.
The court ruled that the disputed land was part of the family's bonum.
