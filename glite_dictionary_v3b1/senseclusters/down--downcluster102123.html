down

noun
rolling treeless highland
Pronunciation: / daʊn / (AmE), / daʊn / (BrE)
(usually in plural) A rolling treeless highland with little soil, especially found in southern England.

Examples

The grassy down
A flock of sheep grazed peacefully on the open down above the village.
Many hikers enjoy long walks across the grassy downs in summer.
The South Downs are famous for their picturesque landscapes and chalk cliffs.
