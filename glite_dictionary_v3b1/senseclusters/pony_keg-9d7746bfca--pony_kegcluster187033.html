pony keg

noun
small beer barrel for small groups
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊni kɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊni kɛɡ / (BrE)
a small barrel for beer, typically containing a few gallons and used for serving a small group of people

Examples

A pony keg of beer
A pony keg is easier to handle than a full-sized keg and perfect for small events.
The bar offers an option to rent a pony keg for private parties.
We ordered a pony keg of craft beer for the weekend barbecue.
