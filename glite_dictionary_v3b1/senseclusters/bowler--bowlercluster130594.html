bowler

noun
hard felt hat with rounded crown
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊlər / (AmE), / ˈbəʊlə / (BrE)
a hard felt hat with a rounded crown and a narrow brim, traditionally worn by men

Examples

Classic bowler hat
He tipped his bowler politely as he entered the shop.
In London, the bowler became a symbol of British businessmen.
The photograph showed a gentleman in a black bowler and suit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: derby - an American term for a bowler hat
  • degree lower: fedora - a felt hat with a creased crown and wide brim
