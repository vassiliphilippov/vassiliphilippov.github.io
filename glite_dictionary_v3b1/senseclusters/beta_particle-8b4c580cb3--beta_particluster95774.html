beta particle

noun
electron/positron from radioactive decay
4.6 5.2
Pronunciation: /ˈbeɪ.tə ˈpɑːr.tɪ.kəl/ /; / /ˈbeɪ.tə ˈpɑːr.tə.kəl/ (AmE), /ˈbiː.tə ˈpɑː.tɪ.kəl/ /; / /ˈbeɪ.tə ˈpɑː.tɪ.kəl/ (BrE)

Examples

During nuclear decay, a beta particle can be emitted from the nucleus of an atom.
