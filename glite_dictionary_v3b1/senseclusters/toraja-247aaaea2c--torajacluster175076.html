Toraja

proper noun
Indonesian ethnic group
Pronunciation: / toʊˈrɑːdʒə / (AmE), / tɒˈrɑːdʒə / (BrE)
a member of an indigenous ethnic group from the highlands of South Sulawesi, Indonesia

Examples

Toraja people
The Toraja are famous for their unique traditional houses and elaborate funeral rituals.
Many tourists visit South Sulawesi to learn about the culture of the Toraja people.
A Toraja village often features beautifully carved wooden buildings and ancestral sites.
