digger wasp

noun
solitary wasp that digs nests in soil
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪɡər wɑsp / (AmE), / ˈdɪɡə wɒsp / (BrE)
a solitary wasp that digs nests in soil and supplies them with paralyzed insects for its larvae to feed on

Examples

The digger wasp digs nests
After paralyzing a caterpillar, the digger wasp drags it into her underground nest.
Many gardeners appreciate the role digger wasps play in controlling pest populations.
The digger wasp carefully excavates a tunnel in the sandy soil for her eggs.

Related Terms
