tanglefoot

noun
sticky insect trap; cheap alcohol
Pronunciation: / ˈtæŋɡəlˌfʊt / (AmE), / ˈtæŋɡəlˌfʊt / (BrE)
a sticky substance used to trap insects on plants, or slang for cheap, strong alcoholic drink

Examples

Tanglefoot sticky trap
The gardener applied tanglefoot around the base of the tree to stop ants from climbing.
He joked that the cheap whiskey tasted like tanglefoot after a long day at work.
Some people use tanglefoot to protect their rose bushes from crawling insects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flypaper - sticky paper used to catch flies
  • synonym: moonshine - illegally made or very strong alcohol
