laud

verb
to formally praise or glorify
Pronunciation: / lɑd / (AmE), / lɔːd / (BrE)
to praise or glorify someone or something, especially in a formal or public way

Examples

To laud great work
His efforts to improve education have been widely lauded by experts in the field.
Many critics laud the film for its powerful storytelling and stunning visuals.
The author was lauded for her outstanding contribution to literature.

Related Terms

  • antonym: criticize - indicate the faults of someone or something in a disapproving way
  • synonym: extol - praise enthusiastically
  • synonym: praise - express warm approval or admiration of someone or something
