outhouse

noun
a small separate building used as a toilet
5.8 1.61/m

A small structure separate from a main building, used as a toilet, typically with a bench having holes.

Examples

A outhouse toilet
Modern plumbing replaced the outhouse on most rural properties decades ago.
The old cabin still had an outhouse instead of an indoor toilet.
We had to walk through the snow to use the outhouse at night.
