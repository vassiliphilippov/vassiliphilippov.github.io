goodnight

noun
bedtime farewell
Pronunciation: / ɡʊdˈnaɪt / (AmE), / ɡʊdˈnaɪt / (BrE)
an expression or gesture used to say farewell before going to bed or ending the day

Examples

A gentle goodnight kiss
She gave her son a gentle kiss and whispered goodnight before turning off the light.
After the party, everyone said goodnight and went their separate ways.
He waved and called out goodnight as he left the building.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goodbye - a word used to express farewell
  • antonym: hello - a greeting used when meeting someone
