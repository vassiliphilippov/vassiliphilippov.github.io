accountability

noun
responsibility to justify actions
Pronunciation: / əˌkaʊntəˈbɪlɪti /; / 59ccka8ant59c8b6al6ati / (AmE), / əˌkaʊntəˈbɪlɪti /; / 59ccka8ant59c8b6al6ati / (BrE)
the state of being responsible for your actions and having to explain or justify them to others

Examples

Effective accountability
Effective accountability helps prevent corruption in government institutions.

Civil rights groups argue that algorithmic accountability requires clear explanations and easy access to remedies.
