unalone

adjective
not alone
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈloʊn / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈləʊn / (BrE)

Examples

The dog made him feel unalone during the long nights.
After joining the club, he was happy to be unalone at events.
