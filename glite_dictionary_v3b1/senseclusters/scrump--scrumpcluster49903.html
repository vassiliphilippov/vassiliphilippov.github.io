scrump

verb
to steal fruit
9.9
Pronunciation: / skrʌmp / (AmE), / skrʌmp / (BrE)
to steal fruit, especially apples, from an orchard or garden

Examples

To scrump apples
As children, they would often scrump apples from the farmer's orchard after school.
The boys planned to scrump some pears from the neighbor's garden at dusk.
He was caught trying to scrump fruit during the village fair.

Related Terms

  • antonym: return - to give something back to its owner
  • synonym: steal - to take something without permission
