peepul

noun
a sacred fig tree of India
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːpəl / (AmE), / ˈpiːpəl / (BrE)
A large fig tree (Ficus religiosa), native to India and regarded as sacred by Buddhists and Hindus.

Examples

A peepul tree
Buddha is believed to have attained enlightenment under a peepul tree.
The peepul tree is a common sight near temples across northern India.
Villagers often gather in the shade of the ancient peepul at the center of town.
