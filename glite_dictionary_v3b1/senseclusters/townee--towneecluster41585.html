townee

noun
city dweller unfamiliar with country life
9.8 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆtaÊŠni / (AmE), / ËˆtaÊŠni / (BrE)
A person who lives in a town, especially someone unfamiliar with or unsympathetic to country life; often used to describe a flashy or slick male city dweller.

Examples

A townee in town
He felt out of place, a true townee at the rural festival.
Some country folk distrust any townee who tries to tell them how to farm.
The locals recognized him as a townee by his flashy suit and city accent.

  • towner

  • townsman
    4.8 0.07/m
    a townsman is a formal or old-fashioned word for a man from a town, while a townee implies a flashy, unsympathetic town resident.

Related Terms
