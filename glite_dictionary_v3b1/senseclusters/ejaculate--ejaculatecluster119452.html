ejaculate

noun
fluid containing sperm released during ejaculation
Pronunciation: / ɪˈdʒækjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈdʒækjʊleɪt / (BrE)
the thick fluid containing sperm released from the male reproductive tract during ejaculation

Examples

A male ejaculate
Some infertility issues can be traced to abnormalities in the ejaculate.
The analysis revealed that his ejaculate contained a high concentration of sperm.
The doctor collected a sample of ejaculate for testing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: semen - the fluid containing sperm from the male reproductive tract
  • synonym: sperm - the male reproductive cell, or sometimes the fluid containing it
