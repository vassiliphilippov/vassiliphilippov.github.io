undeveloping

adjective
not progressing
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈvɛləpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈvɛləpɪŋ / (BrE)
not developing or no longer progressing in growth or advancement

Examples

An undeveloping area
The undeveloping area lacked the infrastructure needed for new businesses to thrive.
Many experts worry about the future of undeveloping economies in a rapidly changing world.
Policies were introduced to support growth in undeveloping regions facing economic decline.

Related Terms

  • antonym: developing - growing or becoming more advanced
  • synonym: stagnant - not growing or changing; inactive
