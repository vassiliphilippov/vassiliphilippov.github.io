Examples

Is she around now

Synonyms

  • about
    1.0 18/m
    about is used to say something is in or moving through an area, while around means in the same general place nearby.
  • nearby
    1.0 35/m
    nearby means not far from a place and often describes position, while around suggests being in the wider local area.
  • near
    1.0 12/m
    near means at a short distance from something and often points to close position, while around means in the general area nearby.
  • hereabouts
    5.4 0.04/m
    hereabouts refers to this general area close to the speaker, while around is more neutral and can mean nearby or in different places.
  • thereabouts
    4.9 0.06/m
    thereabouts means in or near that place and gives a rough location, while around means in the area generally and can be less exact.
