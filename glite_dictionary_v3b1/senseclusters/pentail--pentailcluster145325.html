pentail

noun
Malaysian tree shrew with fringed tail
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈpɛnˌteɪl / (BrE)
A small brown tree shrew native to Malaysia, distinguished by its naked tail bordered on both sides with long, stiff hairs near the tip.

Examples

A pentail shrew
Biologists observed a pentail foraging for insects in the forest canopy.
The pentail is known for its unique tail, which helps it balance in the trees of Malaysia.
Unlike other tree shrews, the pentail has a tail fringed with long hairs along its tip.
