floorman

noun
stock exchange worker
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔːrmæn / (AmE), / ˈflɔːmæn / (BrE)
a person who works on the trading floor of a stock exchange, executing orders or assisting brokers

Examples

Stock floorman
The experienced floorman managed to execute several large trades before the market closed.
As a floorman, she was responsible for relaying orders quickly to the brokers.
During the busy morning session, the floorman kept a close eye on the trading activity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broker - a person who arranges transactions between buyers and sellers
  • synonym: trader - a person who buys and sells financial assets
