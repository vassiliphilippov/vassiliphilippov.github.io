wool-dyed yarn

noun
yarn colored with wool dye
Pronunciation: / wʊl daɪd jɑrn / (AmE), / wʊl daɪd jɑːn / (BrE)
yarn that has been colored using dye applied to wool fibers

Examples

wool-dyed yarn colors
Carefully spun wool-dyed yarn brings depth and texture to handmade rugs.
She used wool-dyed yarn to knit a vibrant scarf for her friend.
The textile market featured stalls selling specialty wool-dyed yarn in unique shades.
