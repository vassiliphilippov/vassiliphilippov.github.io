Chimariko language

noun
extinct language of the Chimariko people
Pronunciation: / tʃɪˈmɑːrɪkoʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / tʃɪˈmɑːrɪkəʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
