bristly

adjective
covered with stiff hairs or spines
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪsli / (AmE), / ˈbrɪsli / (BrE)
having or covered with short, stiff hairs, spines, or similar projections

Examples

Bristly beard
After a week without shaving, his beard became thick and bristly.
The hedgehog's back felt bristly when she tried to pet it.
We walked through the bristly undergrowth, careful not to scratch our legs.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: hairy - covered with hair
  • synonym: spiky - having sharp points or spikes
  • synonym: spiny - covered with spines or thorns
