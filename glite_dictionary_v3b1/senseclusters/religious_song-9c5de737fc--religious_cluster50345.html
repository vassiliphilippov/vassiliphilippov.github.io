religious song

noun
song with religious content
2.8
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlɪdʒəs sɔŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəs sɒŋ / (BrE)
a song with religious content, composed or sung as part of worship or religious observance

Examples

A religious song of worship
Many traditional festivals begin with a religious song to bring the community together.
She learned a new religious song for Sunday school.
The choir performed a beautiful religious song at the Christmas service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anthem - a song of praise or devotion, often used in religious or ceremonial contexts
  • synonym: hymn - a song of praise, especially to God or a deity
  • antonym: secular song - a song not related to religion
