when in Rome

x
follow local customs when elsewhere
7.6
Pronunciation: / wɛn ɪn roʊm / (AmE), / wɛn ɪn rəʊm / (BrE)
used to say that you should follow the customs of the place you are visiting or living in

Examples

When in Rome, do as locals
As the saying goes, when in Rome, try to blend in with the locals.
He wore traditional dress to the ceremony because, you know, when in Rome.
My motto while traveling is when in Rome; I always sample new foods.
