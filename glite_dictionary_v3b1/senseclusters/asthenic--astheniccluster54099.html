asthenic

adjective
lacking strength or vitality
5.6
Pronunciation: / əˈsθɛnɪk / (AmE), / asˈθɛnɪk /; / æsˈθɛnɪk / (BrE)
physically weak or having a slender, fragile build

Examples

An asthenic body
A strict diet and insufficient rest left him feeling continuously asthenic.
After her long illness, she appeared asthenic and required help walking to the next room.
The physician noted the patient's asthenic condition in his medical report.
As a child, he was asthenic, often falling ill and lacking physical strength.
Her asthenic appearance suggested she needed to gain weight for better health.
The patient was described as asthenic with a slight build and little muscle mass.

Related Terms

  • antonym: robust - having a strong and healthy physique
  • antonym: vigorous - having or showing strength, energy, or force
  • synonym: weak - lacking strength or power
