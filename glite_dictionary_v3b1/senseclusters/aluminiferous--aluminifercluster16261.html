aluminiferous

adjective
containing alum or aluminum
Pronunciation: / əˌluːməˈnɪfərəs / (AmE), / əˌljuːmɪˈnɪfərəs / (BrE)
(of a mineral or rock) containing alum or aluminum.

Examples

Aluminiferous ore
Bauxite is an aluminiferous ore used in the production of aluminum.
Geologists sampled aluminiferous clay from the mining site.
The company investigates deposits of aluminiferous minerals for industrial extraction.
