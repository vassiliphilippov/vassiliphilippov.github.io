reflecting

adjective
designed to reflect, as in a device or surface
1.0 2.8
Pronunciation: / rɪˈflɛktɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈflɛktɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a surface or device) causing reflection or designed to reflect light, sound, or other forms of energy.

Examples

A reflecting surface
A reflecting microscope uses mirrors to direct light onto the specimen.
She admired the reflecting surface of the calm lake at sunrise.
The reflecting telescope allowed astronomers to observe distant galaxies clearly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mirror - an object that reflects light perfectly
  • antonym: refracting - describes optical systems that bend (refract) light instead of reflecting it
