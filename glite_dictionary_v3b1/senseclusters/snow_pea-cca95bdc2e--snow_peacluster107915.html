snow pea

noun
a pea with edible flat pods
7.6
Pronunciation: / snoʊ piː /; / snoʊ pi / (AmE), / snəʊ piː / (BrE)
a type of pea with flat, edible pods that are eaten whole while unripe

Examples

Fresh snow peas
At the market, I bought a bag of snow peas for tonight's dinner.
She added fresh snow peas to the stir-fry for a crisp, sweet flavor.
The salad was topped with sliced snow peas and a light sesame dressing.
Fresh snow peas make a great addition to salads or can be eaten raw as a snack.
I added some snow peas to the stir-fry for extra crunch and color.
We need to buy a bag of snow peas for tonight's Asian-inspired dinner.

Related Terms

  • antonym: garden pea - the common pea with rounded, non-edible pods
  • synonym: snap pea - a similar edible-pod pea, but with a plumper pod
  • synonym: sugar snap pea - a type of pea with thick edible pods and sweet flavor
