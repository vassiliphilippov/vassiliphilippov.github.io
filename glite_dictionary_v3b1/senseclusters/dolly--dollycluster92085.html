dolly

noun
movable camera support platform
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑli / (AmE), / ˈdɒli / (BrE)
a small wheeled platform used for moving a camera smoothly during filming

Examples

Camera dolly
The film crew placed the camera on a dolly to shoot smooth tracking shots.
They rented a dolly for the music video to achieve cinematic camera angles.
Using a dolly allowed the director to create fluid movement in the scene.

Related Terms

  • synonym: camera crane - a large device for moving a camera vertically and horizontally
