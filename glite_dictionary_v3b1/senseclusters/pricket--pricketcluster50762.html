pricket

noun
male deer in its second year
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪk.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈprɪk.ɪt / (BrE)
A male deer in its second year, typically with unbranched antlers.

Examples

A young pricket deer
In autumn, a pricket can be identified by its single-pointed antlers.
The hunter spotted a pricket grazing at the edge of the forest.
The park ranger explained that the young stag was a pricket before growing branching antlers.
