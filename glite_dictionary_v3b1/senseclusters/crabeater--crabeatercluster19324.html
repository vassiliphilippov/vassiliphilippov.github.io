crabeater

noun
Antarctic seal species
Pronunciation: / ˈkræbˌiːtər / (AmE), / ˈkræbˌiːtə / (BrE)
a type of seal found in Antarctic waters that mainly eats krill, not crabs

Examples

Antarctic crabeater seal
The crabeater is one of the most numerous seal species in the world.
Scientists observed a group of crabeaters resting on the Antarctic ice.
Despite its name, the crabeater mainly feeds on krill rather than crabs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leopard seal - a large predatory seal found in Antarctic waters
  • synonym: seal - a marine mammal with flippers, found in cold seas
