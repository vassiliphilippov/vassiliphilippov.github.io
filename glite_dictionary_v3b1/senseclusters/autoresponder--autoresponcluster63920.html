autoresponder

noun
automatic email reply system
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːtoʊrɪˈspɑːndər / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəʊrɪˈspɒndə / (BrE)
a computer program or service that automatically sends a reply to emails or messages

Examples

An autoresponder message
The company uses an autoresponder to confirm every order placed on their website.
After emailing customer support, I received an autoresponder message saying they would reply within 24 hours.
Setting up an autoresponder can help businesses stay in touch with new subscribers automatically.
The autoresponder sent a confirmation email as soon as I registered for the event.
If you email the support team after hours, their autoresponder will let you know when they will reply.
She set up an autoresponder to inform clients that she was on vacation.
The autoresponder sent a confirmation message to every new subscriber.
If you are on vacation, you can set up an autoresponder to inform people of your absence.
Our customer service uses an autoresponder to acknowledge all incoming requests instantly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: auto-reply - an automatic response sent to incoming emails
  • antonym: manual reply - a response to an email written and sent by a person
  • synonym: out-of-office reply - an automatic message sent when someone is unavailable
