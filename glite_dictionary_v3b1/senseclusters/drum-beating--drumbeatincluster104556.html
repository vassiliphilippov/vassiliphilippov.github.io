drum-beating

adjective
promoting or campaigning
Pronunciation: / ˈdrʌmˌbiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdrʌmˌbiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
actively promoting, publicizing, or campaigning to attract attention or gain support for something

Examples

Drum-beating campaign
The candidate's drum-beating speeches drew large crowds to every event.
Some critics disliked the drum-beating advertisements for the new product launch.
The drum-beating campaign overshadowed more thoughtful discussions about the issue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: promotional - intended to advertise or publicize something
  • antonym: subdued - not loud, intense, or forceful in promotion
