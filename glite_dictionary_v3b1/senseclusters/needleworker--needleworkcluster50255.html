needleworker

noun
person who works with a needle (sewing, embroidery)
Pronunciation: / ˈnidlˌwɜrkər / (AmE), / ˈniːdlˌwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person who sews, embroiders, or creates handcrafts using a needle

Examples

A needleworker sewing
As a passionate needleworker, she enjoyed both sewing and embroidery in her free time.
The exhibition showcased the works of a famous needleworker from the 19th century.
The skilled needleworker spent hours creating intricate designs on the fabric.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seamstress - a woman who sews, especially professionally
  • synonym: tailor - a person who makes, repairs, or alters clothing
