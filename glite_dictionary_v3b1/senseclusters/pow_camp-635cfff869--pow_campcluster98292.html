POW camp

noun
camp for prisoners of war
Pronunciation: / pi oʊ ˈdʌbəlju ˌkæmp / (AmE), / piː əʊ ˈdʌbəljuː ˌkamp / (BrE)
a camp where prisoners of war are detained, typically during a conflict or war

Examples

A POW camp site
