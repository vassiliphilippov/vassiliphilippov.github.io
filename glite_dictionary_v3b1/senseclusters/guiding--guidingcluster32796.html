guiding

adjective
directing or leading
Pronunciation: / ˈɡaɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
providing direction, leadership, or influence

Examples

A guiding principle
A guiding principle in her life is honesty and kindness.
He placed a guiding hand on her shoulder to help her through the crowd.
The lighthouse served as a guiding light for ships in the fog.
A guiding philosophy helped the company stay true to its mission.
He always relied on his guiding principles to make difficult decisions.
The organization outlined its guiding values to inspire employees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: directing - providing direction or control
  • synonym: directive - giving authoritative instructions or guidance
  • synonym: influential - having great influence on someone or something
  • antonym: misguided - having or showing faulty judgment or reasoning
  • antonym: misleading - giving the wrong direction or guidance
