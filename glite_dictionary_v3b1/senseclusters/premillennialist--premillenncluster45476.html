premillennialist

adjective
believing Christ returns before millennium
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːmɪˈlɛniəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌpriːmɪˈlɛniəlɪst / (BrE)
relating to the belief that Christ will return before a thousand-year period of peace on earth.

Examples

Premillennialist belief system
The church's premillennialist views influenced its interpretation of biblical prophecy.
A premillennialist perspective expects Christ's return before a thousand-year reign of peace.
Many religious scholars debate the implications of a premillennialist doctrine for modern believers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postmillennialist - believing Christ will return after a thousand-year period of peace
