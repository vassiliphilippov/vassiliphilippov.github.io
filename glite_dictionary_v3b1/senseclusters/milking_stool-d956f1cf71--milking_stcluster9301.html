milking stool

noun
three-legged stool for milking cows
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlkɪŋ stuːl / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkɪŋ stʊːl / (BrE)
a low, three-legged stool with a rounded seat, used for sitting while milking a cow

Examples

A milking stool with three legs

The old farmer sat on his milking stool every morning to milk the cows by hand.
