maybe

adverb
perhaps; possibly
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪ.bi / (AmE), / ˈmeɪ.biː / (BrE)
possibly; perhaps; used to express uncertainty or possibility about something.

Examples

Maybe later
Maybe we can go for a walk later if the weather improves.
I'm not sure if he'll come to the party, but maybe he'll surprise us.
She hasn't decided on a major yet; maybe she'll choose biology.

  • synonym: perhaps - used to express uncertainty or possibility
