fall into old habits

verb
return to former behavior patterns
1.8 3.0
Pronunciation: /fɔːl ˈɪn.tuː oʊld ˈhæb.ɪts/ /; / /fɔːl ˈɪn.tə oʊld ˈhæb.ɪts/ /; / /fɑːl ˈɪn.tuː oʊld ˈhæb.ɪts/ (AmE), /fɔːl ˈɪn.tuː əʊld ˈhæb.ɪts/ /; / /fɔːl ˈɪn.tə əʊld ˈhæb.ɪts/ (BrE)
to begin behaving in the same way as before, especially after trying to change or improve one's behavior

Examples

To fall into old habits

Whenever under pressure, he tends to fall into old habits and procrastinate.
