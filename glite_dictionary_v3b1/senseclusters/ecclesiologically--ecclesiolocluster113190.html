ecclesiologically

adverb
in a church-related way
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˌkliːziəˈlɑdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ɪˌkliːziəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the study or structure of the Christian church or its organization

Examples

An ecclesiologically important building
The cathedral was ecclesiologically important because of its influence on later church architecture.
The committee evaluated the proposal ecclesiologically, considering its impact on church governance.
He wrote ecclesiologically about the differences between various Christian denominations.
