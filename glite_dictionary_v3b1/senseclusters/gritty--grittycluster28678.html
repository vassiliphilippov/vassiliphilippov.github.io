gritty

adjective
showing courage and resolve
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɪti / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪti / (BrE)
showing courage and resolve in difficult or dangerous situations; brave and determined

Examples

Gritty hero
Despite the dangers, her gritty determination kept the team moving forward.
He gave a gritty performance under extreme pressure.
The gritty firefighter entered the burning building without hesitation.
