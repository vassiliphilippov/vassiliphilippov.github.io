soldier

verb
to persist bravely
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊldʒər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊldʒə / (BrE)
to continue doing something bravely and persistently despite difficulties or obstacles

Examples

To soldier on bravely
Despite feeling exhausted, she soldiered on and finished the marathon.
He soldiered through the tough times after losing his job.
They soldiered on with the project even when resources were limited.

Related Terms

  • antonym: give up - to stop trying or abandon an effort
  • synonym: persevere - to keep trying despite difficulties
