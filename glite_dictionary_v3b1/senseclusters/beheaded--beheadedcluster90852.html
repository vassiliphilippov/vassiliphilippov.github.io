beheaded

adjective
with the head cut off (usually killed)
Pronunciation: / bɪˈhɛdɪd / (AmE), / bɪˈhɛdɪd / (BrE)
Having had the head cut off, especially as a form of execution or killing.

Examples

Beheaded statues found
Several beheaded statues were discovered in the ruined temple.
The ancient legend tells of a ghost that appears where it was beheaded.
The traitor was beheaded after being found guilty of treason.
