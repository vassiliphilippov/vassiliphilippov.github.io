fine line

noun
subtle distinction or boundary
Pronunciation: / faɪn laɪn / (AmE), / faɪn laɪn / (BrE)
a very subtle distinction or boundary between two closely related things or ideas

Examples

A fine line between ideas
Artists sometimes walk a fine line between creativity and chaos.
She realized there’s a fine line between helping and interfering.
There is often a fine line between being persistent and being pushy in sales.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boundary - a dividing line or limit between things
  • synonym: distinction - a difference or contrast between similar things or people
