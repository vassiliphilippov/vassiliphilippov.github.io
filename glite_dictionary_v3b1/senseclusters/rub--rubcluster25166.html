rub

noun
act of rubbing or wiping
Pronunciation: / rʌb / (AmE), / rʌb / (BrE)
an act of moving one's hand or a cloth repeatedly over a surface, typically to clean, dry, or polish it

Examples

A quick rub with a towel
After a brisk rub with a towel, the dog was finally dry.
He gave the table a quick rub to remove the dust.
The silverware regained its shine after a good rub.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wipe - an act of wiping something
