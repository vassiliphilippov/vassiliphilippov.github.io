American lobster

noun
lobster species from Atlantic coast of North America
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈlɑːbstər / (AmE), / əˈmerɪkən ˈlɒbstə / (BrE)
a large lobster of the Atlantic coast of North America, valued as seafood

Examples

Live American lobster
Fishermen in Maine caught hundreds of American lobster last season.
The American lobster is prized for its sweet, tender meat in many coastal restaurants.
Unlike other species, the American lobster has large, prominent claws.
