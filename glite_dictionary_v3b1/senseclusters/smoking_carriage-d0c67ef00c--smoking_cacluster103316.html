smoking carriage

noun
railway car where smoking is allowed
Pronunciation: / ˈsmoʊkɪŋ ˈkærɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsməʊkɪŋ ˈkarɪdʒ / (BrE)
a railway passenger car or compartment where smoking is permitted

Examples

The smoking carriage
He chose to travel in the smoking carriage so he could enjoy a cigarette during the journey.
She remembered the thick haze that once filled every smoking carriage on the morning train.
When regulations changed, the old smoking carriage was converted into a standard compartment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: non-smoking carriage - a railway passenger car where smoking is not allowed
