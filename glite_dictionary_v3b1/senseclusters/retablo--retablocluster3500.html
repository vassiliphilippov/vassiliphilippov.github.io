retablo

noun
altar screen
9.9
Pronunciation: / rɛˈtɑːbloʊ / (AmE), / rɛˈtæbləʊ / (BrE)
an ornamental structure or screen behind and above an altar in a church, often decorated with carvings, paintings, or sculptures

Examples

Church retablo screen
The ornate retablo in the cathedral was covered in gold leaf and intricate carvings.
Visitors admired the colorful paintings on the old wooden retablo behind the altar.
Restorers carefully cleaned the centuries-old retablo to reveal its original details.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reredos - a decorative screen or wall behind an altar
