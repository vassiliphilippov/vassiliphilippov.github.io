centrifuge

noun
machine for separating particles by spinning
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntrəˌfjudʒ / (AmE), / ˈsentrɪfjuːdʒ / (BrE)
a machine that uses rapid spinning to separate substances of different densities, especially particles from a liquid

Examples

A centrifuge machine
After spinning in the centrifuge, the different components of the mixture were clearly visible.
Modern laboratories rely on a centrifuge for many of their tests.
The scientist placed the blood samples in the centrifuge to separate the plasma.
