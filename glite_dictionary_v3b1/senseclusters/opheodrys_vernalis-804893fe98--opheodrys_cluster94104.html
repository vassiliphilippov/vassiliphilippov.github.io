Opheodrys vernalis

noun
species of small green snake
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊfiˈɑːdrɪs vərˈnælɪs / (AmE), / ˌəʊfiˈəʊdrɪs vəˈnɑːlɪs / (BrE)
A small, slender, nonvenomous green snake found in western and central regions of the United States, also known as the smooth green snake.
