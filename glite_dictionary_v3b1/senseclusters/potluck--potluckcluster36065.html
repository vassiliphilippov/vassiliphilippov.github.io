potluck

noun
shared meal where guests bring food
9.3 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈpɑːt.lʌk/ (AmE), /ˈpɒt.lʌk/ (BrE)
a meal or gathering where each guest contributes a dish to be shared with others; also, whatever food or chance happens to be available, especially for unexpected guests

Examples

A potluck dinner
Arriving late, she was grateful there was still plenty to eat at the potluck.
We decided to have a potluck dinner, so everyone brought their favorite dish.
You never know what you'll get at a potluck, which makes it so much fun.
