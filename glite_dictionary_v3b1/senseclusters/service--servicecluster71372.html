service

noun
obligatory acts of a feudal tenant for their lord
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜːrvɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
(historical, law) The acts rendered by a feudal tenant to their lord as the condition for holding land.

Examples

Feudal service
Failure to perform the required service could result in loss of land.
The nature of the service owed was specified in the grant of property.
Under the feudal system, the tenant's service to the lord could include military aid.
