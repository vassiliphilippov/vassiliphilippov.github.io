acneiform

adjective
resembling acne
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈæk.ni.əˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈæk.ni.ɪ.fɔːm / (BrE)
(of a skin eruption or lesion) resembling acne in appearance.

Examples

Acneiform skin eruption
An acneiform eruption can sometimes be mistaken for true acne by non-specialists.
Some medications may cause acneiform eruptions as a side effect.
The doctor noted an acneiform rash on the patient's back.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acne - a skin condition marked by red pimples on the skin
