Entebbe

proper noun
town in Uganda, site of 1976 hostage rescue
7.9
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈtɛbi / (AmE), / ɛnˈtɛbi / (BrE)
A town in southern Uganda on the shores of Lake Victoria, known for its international airport and as the site of a 1976 hostage rescue by Israeli commandos.

Examples

Entebbe town
Many tourists visit the botanical gardens in Entebbe before exploring other parts of the country.
The 1976 rescue operation put Entebbe on the global map for its historical significance.
The international airport in Entebbe serves as the main gateway to Uganda.
