unlifted

adjective
not raised
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɪftɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɪftɪd / (BrE)
not having been lifted or raised from a place or position

Examples

Unlifted box
The heavy box remained unlifted in the corner despite several attempts.
After the storm, many fallen branches were still unlifted from the ground.
The curtain was unlifted, leaving the stage hidden from the audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lifted - having been raised or moved upward
  • synonym: unmoved - not having been moved from its place
