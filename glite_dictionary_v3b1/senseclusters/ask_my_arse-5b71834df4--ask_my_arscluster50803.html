dismissive retort of disbelief or refusal
Pronunciation: / æsk maɪ ɑrs / (AmE), / ɑːsk maɪ ɑːs / (BrE)
(British, informal, vulgar) A dismissive phrase indicating disbelief or refusal to help, implying the speaker thinks the request or statement is absurd.

Examples
