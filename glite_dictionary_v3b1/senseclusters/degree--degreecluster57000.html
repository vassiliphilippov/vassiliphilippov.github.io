degree

noun
unit for measuring angles
Pronunciation: / dɪˈɡri / (AmE), / dɪˈɡriː / (BrE)
a unit of measurement for angles, equal to one three-hundred-and-sixtieth of a complete circle

Examples

A 90 degree angle
A triangle's angles always add up to 180 degrees.
He turned the dial exactly ninety degrees to the right.
The protractor measures an angle of 45 degrees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: radian - SI unit for measuring angles, based on arc length
