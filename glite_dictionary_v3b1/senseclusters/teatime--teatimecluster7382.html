teatime

noun
afternoon meal with tea and light food
Pronunciation: / ˈtiˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈtiːtaɪm / (BrE)
the time in the afternoon when tea and a light meal of sandwiches or cakes is traditionally served, especially in Britain

Examples

Afternoon teatime
Many English traditions revolve around teatime, making it a special part of the day.
She invited her friends over for teatime in her lovely garden.
We always gather in the lounge at teatime for cakes and conversation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: afternoon tea - a light meal including tea, sandwiches, and cakes served in the afternoon
