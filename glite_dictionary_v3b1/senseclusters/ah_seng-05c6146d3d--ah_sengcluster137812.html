Ah Seng

noun
tech-savvy young man stereotype (Singapore/Malaysia)

Pronunciation: / ɑ sɛŋ / (AmE), / ɑ sɛŋ / (BrE)
(Singapore, Malaysia) A stereotype of a tech-savvy young man, often characterized by a keen interest in the latest gadgets and technology.

Examples

The Ah Seng in office
Everyone turns to the Ah Seng in the office whenever there is a tech problem.

You can always spot an Ah Seng by the number of gadgets he carries.
