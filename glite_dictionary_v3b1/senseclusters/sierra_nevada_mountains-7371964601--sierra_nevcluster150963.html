Sierra Nevada Mountains

proper noun
mountain range in California and Nevada
3.2
Pronunciation: / siˈɛɹə nəˈvædə ˈmaʊntɪnz / (AmE), / siˈeəɹə nɪˈvɑːdə ˈmaʊntɪnz / (BrE)
a mountain range in eastern California, extending into Nevada, known for its scenic beauty and containing Mount Whitney, the highest peak in the contiguous United States

Examples

The Sierra Nevada Mountains range
Many rivers originate in the Sierra Nevada Mountains before flowing down to California's Central Valley.
The Sierra Nevada Mountains are famous for their granite cliffs, alpine lakes, and national parks.
We spent our summer hiking in the Sierra Nevada Mountains, enjoying the breathtaking scenery.
