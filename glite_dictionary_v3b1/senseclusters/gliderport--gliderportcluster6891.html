gliderport

noun
airfield for gliders
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlaɪdərˌpɔrt / (AmE), / ˈɡlaɪdəˌpɔːt / (BrE)
an airfield or designated area where gliders take off, land, and are stored

Examples

Pilots must follow strict safety rules when operating at the gliderport.
