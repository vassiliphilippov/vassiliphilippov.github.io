worth

noun
a quantity equivalent to a stated value
Pronunciation: / wɜrθ / (AmE), / wɜːθ / (BrE)
an amount or quantity of something equivalent in value to a specified sum or measurement

Examples

Ten dollars' worth
He bought five dollars' worth of stamps.
She poured a cup's worth of milk into the batter.
The store had a year's worth of supplies in stock.
