Synonyms

  • inexpensive
    1.0 12/m
    inexpensive means it costs little in general, while affordable means it fits most people's budgets or financial means.
  • low-priced
    1.0 1.03/m
    low-priced describes a product with a low sticker price, while affordable says people can actually afford to buy it.
  • low-cost
    2.0 5.27/m
    low-cost often refers to cheap production or service expense, while affordable focuses on whether people can pay for it.
