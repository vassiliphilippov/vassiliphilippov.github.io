dope fiend

noun
person addicted to drugs
9.9 0.13/m
Pronunciation: / Ààdo äp fiÀênd / (AmE), / Ààd…ô äp fiÀênd / (BrE)
an informal and often derogatory term for a person addicted to drugs, especially narcotics

Examples
