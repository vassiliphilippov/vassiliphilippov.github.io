leadenly

adverb
slow or heavy manner
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛdənli / (AmE), / ˈlɛdənli / (BrE)
in a slow, heavy, or dull manner, as if weighed down

Examples

She moves leadenly
After hearing the disappointing news, she walked leadenly out of the office.
The old man climbed the stairs leadenly, each step seeming to take great effort.
He answered the question leadenly, his voice lacking any enthusiasm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heavily - in a way that is heavy or burdensome
  • antonym: lightly - in a way that is not heavy or burdensome
  • synonym: sluggishly - in a slow or inactive manner
