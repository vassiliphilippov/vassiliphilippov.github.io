droopingly

adverb
in a drooping manner
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdruːpɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈdruːpɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner characterized by drooping or hanging downward.

Examples

To droopingly hang
He sighed droopingly as he listened to the sad news.
The old flag hung droopingly from the worn-out pole.
The sunflower bowed its head droopingly after a week without rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drooping - hanging or bending downward; limp
