Pteropus hypomelanus

noun
species of fruit bat (small flying fox)
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛrəpəs ˌhaɪpoʊməˈleɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈtɛrəpəs ˌhɪpəʊməˈleɪnəs / (BrE)
A species of fruit bat, commonly known as the small flying fox or island flying fox, found in Southeast Asia and the western Pacific islands.

Examples

Pteropus hypomelanus fruit bat
Conservationists are studying the declining numbers of Pteropus hypomelanus in several island habitats.
Researchers observed a large colony of Pteropus hypomelanus roosting in the mangrove trees.
The Pteropus hypomelanus primarily feeds on fruits and blossoms found in tropical forests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fruit bat - any bat of the family Pteropodidae that eats fruit and nectar
