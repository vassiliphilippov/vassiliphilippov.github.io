aerialist

noun
a performer of acrobatics in the air
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɛriəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈeəriəlɪst / (BrE)
an acrobat who performs aerial feats, typically on a rope, trapeze, or similar apparatus above the ground

Examples

A aerialist on trapeze
As a child, she dreamed of becoming an aerialist in a famous circus.
The aerialist swung gracefully from the trapeze, captivating the entire audience.
The show featured an aerialist performing daring stunts high above the stage.
