lively

adjective
elastic or springy (of objects)
5.9 5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪvli / (AmE), / ˈlaɪvli / (BrE)
(of a physical object) elastic or springy; rebounding readily after pressure or impact.

Examples

Lively mattress
The new mattress felt especially lively under pressure, quickly returning to shape.
This tennis ball is much too lively for tournament play—it bounces too high.
Walking across the lively turf, her footsteps barely left a mark.

Related Terms

  • synonym: springy - bouncing back quickly when pressed or squeezed
  • antonym: stiff - not easily bent; rigid
