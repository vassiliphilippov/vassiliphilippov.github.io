unapproachability

noun
the state of being unapproachable
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.əˌproʊ.tʃəˈbɪl.ɪ.ti / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˌprəʊ.tʃəˈbɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
The quality of being difficult to approach or reach, especially because of appearing unfriendly or inaccessible.

Examples

Her unapproachability in behavior
Her unapproachability contrasted sharply with her sister's warm and open manner.
People were put off by the artist's air of unapproachability.
The manager's unapproachability made it difficult for employees to share their concerns.

Related Terms

  • antonym: approachability - the quality of being easy to approach or reach
