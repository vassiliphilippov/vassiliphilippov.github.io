hutzpah

noun
shameless audacity; boldness
9.9 7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈhʊt.spə/ /; / /ˈhʌt.spə/ (AmE), /ˈhʌt.spə/ /; / /ˈhʊt.spə/ (BrE)
