neither here nor there

x
irrelevant; of no importance
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈniðɚ hɪr nɔɹ ðɛɹ / (AmE), / ˈniːðə hɪə nɔː ðeə / (BrE)
used to say that something is irrelevant or has no importance to the subject being discussed

Examples

Neither here nor there meaning
His past experience is neither here nor there in this situation.
Some may criticize the decision, but that's neither here nor there.
Whether she arrives early is neither here nor there—the meeting will start on time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beside the point - not directly related to the topic
  • synonym: irrelevant - not connected with the subject
  • antonym: relevant - directly relating to the matter at hand
