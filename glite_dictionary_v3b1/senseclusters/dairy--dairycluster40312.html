dairy

noun
milk-based foods
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛri / (AmE), / ˈdeəri / (BrE)
food products made from milk, such as cheese, butter, and yogurt

Examples

Dairy products
She avoids dairy because she is lactose intolerant.
The supermarket has a large section for dairy products like cheese and yogurt.
Many recipes call for dairy ingredients such as milk or butter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: milk - the white liquid produced by cows, goats, or other animals
  • antonym: non-dairy - not containing milk or milk products
