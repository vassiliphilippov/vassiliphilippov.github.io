buffered

adjective
protected by a barrier
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌfərd / (AmE), / ˈbʌfəd / (BrE)
protected from sudden change, impact, or harm by a barrier or cushion

Examples

Buffered from noise
The laboratory samples are buffered from temperature changes by special containers.
Her savings account acted as a buffered reserve during difficult times.
The city park is buffered from traffic noise by a row of tall trees.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exposed - not protected from harm or danger
  • synonym: shielded - protected from danger or harm
