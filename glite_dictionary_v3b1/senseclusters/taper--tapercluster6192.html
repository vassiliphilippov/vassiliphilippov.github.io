taper

verb
become or make gradually narrower
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪpər / (AmE), / ˈteɪpə / (BrE)
to make or become gradually narrower at one end, often coming to a point

Examples

To taper the end
She tapered the candle so it would fit into the holder.
The craftsman used a knife to taper the end of the wooden pole.
These jeans taper towards the ankle for a slimmer fit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: narrow - become or make less wide
