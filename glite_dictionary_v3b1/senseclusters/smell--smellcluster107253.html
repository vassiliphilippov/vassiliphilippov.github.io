smell

noun
an odor noticed by the nose
Pronunciation: / smɛl / (AmE), / smɛl / (BrE)
an odor, scent, or the act of smelling something

Examples

A bad smell
She noticed an unusual smell coming from the garage.
The flowers release a sweet smell in the evenings.
The kitchen was filled with the smell of freshly baked bread.
He noticed a strange smell coming from the kitchen.
The smell of freshly cut grass reminds me of summer.
The fresh smell of coffee woke her up in the morning.
He took a deep breath, enjoying the smell of the pine trees around him.
One whiff was enough to remind her of the smell of fresh bread baking in the oven.
The smell of coffee in the morning always makes me feel energized.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aroma - a pleasant, often distinctive smell
  • degree higher: fragrance - a pleasant, sweet smell
  • synonym: odor - a distinctive smell, especially one that is unpleasant
  • synonym: scent - a distinctive smell, especially a pleasant one
  • synonym: sniff - to draw in air audibly through the nose to detect a smell
  • degree higher: stench - an extremely unpleasant smell
  • degree higher: stink - a strong unpleasant smell
