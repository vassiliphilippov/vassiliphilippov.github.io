clod

noun
lump or mass of earth or clay
4.6 0.82/m
Pronunciation: / klÉ‘d / (AmE), / klÉ’d / (BrE)
a lump or compact mass of earth, clay, or similar material

Examples

A clod of earth
A heavy clod stuck to the bottom of his boot after walking through the field.
She kicked a clod of dirt across the garden path.
The rain softened the ground, making every clod easy to break apart.

Synonyms
