outthink

verb
to think more cleverly
5.9
Pronunciation: /aʊt.ˈθɪŋk/ (AmE), /aʊt.ˈθɪŋk/ (BrE)
to think more cleverly or quickly than someone else, especially to gain an advantage

Examples
