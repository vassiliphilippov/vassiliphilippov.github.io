Synonyms

  • CE
    4.2 0.00/m
    CE is the common modern form used in dates to avoid religious reference, while Common Era is the full secular phrase.
  • C.E.
    3.9 0.00/m
    C.E. is the punctuated abbreviation seen in some texts and citations, while Common Era is the unabbreviated secular term.
  • Anno Domini
    8.2 0.04/m
    Anno Domini names years after Christ and keeps a Christian tone, while Common Era offers a secular alternative for dating.
  • Christian era
    3.0 0.2/m
    Christian era refers to history from Jesus's birth with a religious focus, while Common Era is the neutral calendar label.
