zowie

interjection
surprise exclamation
Pronunciation: / ˈzaʊi / (AmE), / ˈzaʊi / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express surprise or excitement, especially in an old-fashioned or playful way

Examples

Zowie! What a trick
When the magician pulled a rabbit from the hat, the children shouted zowie in amazement.
He opened the gift and exclaimed zowie when he saw the new bicycle.
The roller coaster was so fast that all I could say was zowie!

  • synonym: wow - an exclamation of surprise or admiration
