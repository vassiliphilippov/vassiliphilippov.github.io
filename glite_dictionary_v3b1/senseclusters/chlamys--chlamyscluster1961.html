chlamys

noun
outer floral parts (calyx and corolla)
Pronunciation: / ˈklæmɪs / (AmE), / ˈklamɪs / (BrE)
(Botany) The collective outer parts of a flower, consisting of the calyx and corolla, which enclose the stamens and pistils.
