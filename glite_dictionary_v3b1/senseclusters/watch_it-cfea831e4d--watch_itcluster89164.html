watch it

verb
be careful; a warning or caution
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑtʃ ɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɒtʃ ɪt / (BrE)
used as a warning to be careful or cautious, especially to avoid danger, trouble, or offending someone

Examples

To watch it carefully
Hey, watch it—the coffee is hot!
If you keep talking like that, you should really watch it.
You’d better watch it when crossing that busy street.
