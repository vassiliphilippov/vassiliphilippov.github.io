swarthy

adjective
having dark skin color
Pronunciation: / ˈswɔrði / (AmE), / ˈswɔːði / (BrE)
(of a person or complexion) having a naturally dark skin color.

Examples

A swarthy man
He was tall and swarthy, with deep brown eyes and black hair.
Her swarthy complexion contrasted sharply with her sister's pale skin.
The sailor's swarthy face was weathered by years at sea.
