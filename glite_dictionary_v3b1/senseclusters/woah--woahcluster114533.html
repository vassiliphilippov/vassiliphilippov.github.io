woah

interjection
expression of surprise
Pronunciation: / woʊ / (AmE), / wəʊ / (BrE)
used to express surprise, amazement, or disbelief

Examples

Woah, that's amazing
Woah, I did not expect the movie to end like that!
When he saw the price of the car, he just said, 'Woah.'
Woah, you finished the project in just one day?

Related Terms

  • synonym: whoa - used to express surprise or to tell someone to stop
  • synonym: wow - used to show amazement or admiration
