spheroid

noun
solid figure formed by rotating an ellipse
3.8 5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsfɪrˌɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈsfɪə.rɔɪd / (BrE)
A solid figure resembling a sphere, formed by rotating an ellipse about one of its axes.

Examples

A geometric spheroid
In geometry class, we learned how to calculate the volume of a spheroid.
Some seeds are shaped like a spheroid, making them easy to roll on a surface.
The planet is not a perfect sphere but rather a slightly flattened spheroid.
