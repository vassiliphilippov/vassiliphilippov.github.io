droppy

adjective
limp or sagging
5.3 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆdrÉ‘pi / (AmE), / ËˆdrÉ’pi / (BrE)
hanging down loosely or lacking firmness; limp or sagging

Examples

Droppy leaves
After the rain, the leaves on the tree looked droppy and heavy.
His shirt became droppy after being soaked in water.
The old curtains hung in a droppy way, making the room look untidy.

Synonyms

  • dangly
    3.9 0.13/m
    dangly hangs loosely and can swing or move, while droppy describes something limp and lacking firmness as it hangs.
