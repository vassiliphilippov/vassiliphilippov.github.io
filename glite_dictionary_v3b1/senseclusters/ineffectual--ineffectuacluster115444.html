Examples

An ineffectual effort

His ineffectual attempts to fix the car only made things worse.
The new policy was completely ineffectual in reducing crime rates.

Synonyms

  • ineffective
    3.4 9.06/m
    ineffective describes a method that does not work in practice, while ineffectual describes someone or thing lacking real power.
  • inefficacious
    9.4 0.03/m
    inefficacious sounds formal and is used in technical contexts, while ineffectual is more general and can describe weak people.
  • unavailing
    8.2 0.10/m
    unavailing describes attempts that fail to help, while ineffectual focuses on lacking power or ability to produce results.
  • futile
    6.0 6.54/m
    futile means pointless or hopeless effort that cannot succeed, while ineffectual often means simply weak or ineffective in practice.
