narrowcast

adjective
targeted to a group
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈnæroʊˌkæst / (AmE), / ˈnærəʊˌkɑːst / (BrE)
intended for or directed at a specific, limited audience rather than the general public

Examples

Narrowcast content for doctors
The company launched a narrowcast campaign aimed only at healthcare professionals.
This narrowcast approach ensures the message reaches only those who need the information.
Unlike traditional advertising, narrowcast content is designed for a select audience.
