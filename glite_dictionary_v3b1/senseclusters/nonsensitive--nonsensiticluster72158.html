nonsensitive

adjective
not requiring security classification
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈsɛnsɪtɪv / (AmE), / nɒnˈsɛnsɪtɪv / (BrE)
(of information or material) not classified as requiring security protection; not subject to security restrictions.

Examples

A nonsensitive document
All nonsensitive documents are available to the public without restriction.
The company ensures that nonsensitive data is stored on less secure servers.
You can share this report since it contains only nonsensitive information.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sensitive - requiring protection for reasons of security; classified
