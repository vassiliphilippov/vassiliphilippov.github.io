jaywalker

noun
person crossing street illegally
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒeɪˌwɑkər / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪˌwɔːkə / (BrE)
a person who crosses a street in a careless or illegal way, especially disregarding traffic rules or signals

Examples

A jaywalker crossing street
It is dangerous to be a jaywalker in a city with heavy traffic.
Signs warn jaywalkers that they may be fined for crossing the street illegally.
The police stopped the jaywalker when he crossed the busy intersection against the light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pedestrian - a person walking, especially in an area also used by vehicles
