focus

noun
maximum clarity or distinctness
1.9 2.1
Pronunciation: /ˈfoʊ.kəs/ /; / /ˈfoʊ.kɪs/ (AmE), /ˈfəʊ.kəs/ /; / /ˈfəʊ.kɪs/ (BrE)
the state of being clear and sharp, either in an image or in an idea or situation

Examples

Sharp focus
Her ideas came into sharp focus after the discussion with her mentor.
The controversy served to bring into focus the differences between the two parties.
The report helped bring the main issue into focus for the committee.
Her face was completely out of focus in the photograph.
Please adjust the lens to achieve the best focus for the microscope slide.
The camera was unable to bring the subject into focus in the dim light.

  • synonym: clarity - clearness; the quality of being clear
