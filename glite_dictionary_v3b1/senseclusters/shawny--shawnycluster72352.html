shawny

noun
showy perennial herb with edible leaves
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɔːni / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːni / (BrE)
A showy perennial herb with white flowers; its leaves are sometimes eaten as greens in the southeastern United States.

Examples

The shawny plant
In the spring, the shawny brightens up the fields with its white blossoms.
Locals in the southeastern U.S. often gather shawny for use in traditional dishes.
The leaves of the shawny can be cooked and eaten like spinach.
