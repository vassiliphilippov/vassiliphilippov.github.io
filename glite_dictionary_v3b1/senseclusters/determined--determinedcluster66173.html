determined

adjective
ascertained or established by investigation
Pronunciation: / dɪˈtɝːmɪnd /; / dɪˈtɝː.mɪnd / (AmE), / dɪˈtɜːmɪnd /; / dɪˈtɜː.mɪnd / (BrE)
decided, established, or fixed, especially by investigation, analysis, or authority

Examples

Determined by facts
Her age was determined by examining historical records and family documents.
The determined cause of the failure was a faulty component in the system.
The determined value of the chemical's concentration matched the predicted level.
Access to the facility is determined according to security regulations.
The official opening date has been determined by the organizers.
The winner will be determined by a panel of judges.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ascertained - discovered or made certain, often through investigation
  • synonym: set - fixed or arranged in advance
  • antonym: undetermined - not established or decided
