plantain

noun
starchy tropical fruit, cooked as staple
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈplænˌteɪn /; / ˈplænˌteɪn, plænˈteɪn / (AmE), / ˈplæn.tɪn /; / ˈplæntɪn, ˈplænˌteɪn / (BrE)
a tropical or subtropical plant related to the banana, with starchy fruit that is usually cooked and eaten as food
