jay

noun
a brightly colored, crested bird
5.8
Pronunciation: / dʒeɪ / (AmE), / dʒeɪ / (BrE)
A brightly colored bird of the crow family, typically having blue or other vivid plumage and a crest; found in both the Old and New Worlds.

Examples

A jay bird
A jay landed on the branch, its blue feathers shining in the sun.
The garden was visited by a beautiful jay with a bright crest.
We heard the loud call of a jay as we walked through the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crow - a large, black bird of the same family as jays
  • synonym: magpie - a long-tailed bird of the crow family, often noisy and black-and-white
