dystonic

adjective
abnormal muscle contractions
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈtɑnɪk / (AmE), / dɪsˈtɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by involuntary muscle contractions that cause abnormal movements or postures

Examples

Dystonic muscle movements
The neurologist observed dystonic postures in the patient's neck and shoulders.
Some medications can help reduce dystonic movements in people with certain neurological disorders.
Her dystonic symptoms made it difficult for her to write or hold objects steadily.

Related Terms

  • antonym: normal - not affected by abnormal muscle contractions
  • synonym: spastic - relating to or affected by muscle spasms
