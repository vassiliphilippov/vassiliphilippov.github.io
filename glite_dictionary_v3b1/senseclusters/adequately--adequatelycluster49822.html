adequately

adverb
in a sufficient or satisfactory way
3.1 3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈæd.ɪ.kwət.li / (AmE), / ˈad.ɪ.kwət.li / (BrE)
in a way that is sufficient for a particular purpose; to a satisfactory or acceptable degree

Examples

To adequately meet needs
Our needs were adequately met by the available supplies.
She explained the procedure adequately to reassure the nervous patient.
The report was adequately researched and addressed all the main issues.
