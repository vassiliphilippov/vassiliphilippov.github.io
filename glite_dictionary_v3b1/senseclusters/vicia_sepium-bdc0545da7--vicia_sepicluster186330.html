Vicia sepium

noun
a European purple-flowered vetch
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪʃiə ˈsiːpiəm / (AmE), / ˈvɪsɪə ˈsiːpiəm / (BrE)
a European species of vetch with slender stems and purple flowers, often found growing as a weed in hedges

Examples

Vicia sepium purple flowers
Vicia sepium can often be found growing wild along country hedgerows.
Gardeners sometimes struggle to control Vicia sepium because it spreads quickly.
The purple flowers of Vicia sepium add color to many European meadows in spring.
