17th

adjective
seventeenth in order
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛv(ə)nˌtiːnθ / (AmE), / ˈsɛv(ə)ntiːnθ / (BrE)
Coming next after the sixteenth in a sequence; being number seventeen in order.

Examples

The 17th place
She finished in 17th place in the marathon, just ahead of her teammate.
The event will be held on the 17th of July this year.
This is the 17th time he has visited the museum.
