Synthroid

proper noun
thyroid hormone medication
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪnθrɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɪnθrɔɪd / (BrE)
a brand name for a synthetic thyroid hormone medication used to treat hypothyroidism

Examples

Synthroid medication
After her diagnosis of hypothyroidism, her doctor prescribed Synthroid to help regulate her hormone levels.
Many patients take Synthroid daily to manage their thyroid conditions effectively.
It is important to take Synthroid at the same time each day for best results.
