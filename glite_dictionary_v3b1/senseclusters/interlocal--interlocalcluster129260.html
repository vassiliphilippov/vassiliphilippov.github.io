interlocal

noun
local government agreement
8.1
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.ˈloʊ.kəl/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.ˈləʊ.kəl/ (BrE)
a formal agreement between two or more local governments or authorities to cooperate or share services

Examples

An interlocal agreement
The city and county entered into an interlocal to manage emergency services together.
An interlocal can help small towns share the costs of public transportation.
The new interlocal agreement improved cooperation between neighboring municipalities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intergovernmental agreement - an agreement between different government entities to work together
