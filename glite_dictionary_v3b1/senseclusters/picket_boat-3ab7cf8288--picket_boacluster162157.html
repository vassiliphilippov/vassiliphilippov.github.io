picket boat

noun
small naval patrol or guard boat
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkət boʊt / (AmE), / ˈpɪkɪt bəʊt / (BrE)
a small boat used to patrol or guard an area, often serving as a lookout or sentry in naval operations

Examples

A picket boat patrol
A crew of five manned the picket boat, keeping watch for any potential threats.
During the exercise, a picket boat reported an unidentified vessel approaching the fleet.
The harbor was guarded by a picket boat that patrolled the entrance day and night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: patrol boat - small vessel used for patrolling waterways
