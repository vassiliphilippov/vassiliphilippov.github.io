comarca

noun
regional district
Pronunciation: / koʊˈmɑrkə / (AmE), / kəˈmɑːkə / (BrE)
a traditional administrative region or district, especially in Spain or Portugal

Examples

Comarca region
The comarca of La Garrotxa is known for its volcanic landscape.
Each comarca in Spain has its own unique traditions and festivals.
He grew up in a small village within the comarca of Alentejo.

Related Terms

  • synonym: municipality - a town or district with local government
  • synonym: province - a principal administrative division of certain countries
