the Virgin

proper noun
the Virgin Mary, mother of Jesus
Pronunciation: / ðə ˈvɝːdʒɪn / (AmE), / ði ˈvɜːdʒɪn / (BrE)
(in Christianity) the mother of Jesus Christ, especially venerated by Roman Catholics as the Virgin Mary.
