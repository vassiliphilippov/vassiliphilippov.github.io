overcutting

noun
excessive cutting
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈkʌtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈkʌtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of cutting more than is necessary or permitted, especially in forestry or mining

Examples

Illegal overcutting trees
Strict regulations are needed to prevent overcutting in protected forests.
The mining company was fined for repeated overcutting beyond their permitted area.
Unchecked overcutting can lead to serious environmental problems and loss of wildlife habitat.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conservation - the protection and careful management of natural resources
  • synonym: deforestation - the removal of a large area of trees from land
