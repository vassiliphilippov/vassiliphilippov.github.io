tombstone

noun
stone marking a grave
3.7 2/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàtuÀêmÀåsto än / (AmE), / ÀàtuÀêmst…ô än / (BrE)
a stone marker placed over or next to a grave, usually inscribed with the deceased person's name and dates

Examples

An old tombstone
She spent the afternoon cleaning her ancestors' tombstone in the old churchyard.
The family gathered at the cemetery to place flowers beside the tombstone.
The name on the tombstone had faded with time, but it was still legible.

Synonyms

  • gravestone
    2.6 2/m
    a gravestone is a stone marker at a grave, often with an inscription, while a tombstone is the general name for it.
  • headstone
    3.5 2/m
    a headstone is a stone set at the head of a grave with name and dates, while a tombstone is any grave marker.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gravestone - a stone that marks a grave
