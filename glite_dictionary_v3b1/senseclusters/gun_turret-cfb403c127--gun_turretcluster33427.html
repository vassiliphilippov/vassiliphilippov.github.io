gun turret

noun
rotating structure housing guns
6.7
Pronunciation: / ɡʌn ˈtɝː.ət / (AmE), / ɡʌn ˈtʌr.ɪt / (BrE)
a structure on a ship, aircraft, or vehicle that houses guns and can be rotated to aim them

Examples

A gun turret on tank
Technicians spent hours repairing the jammed gun turret on the tank.
The battleship's gun turret rotated smoothly to track the approaching aircraft.
The museum features a restored gun turret from a World War II fighter plane.
