monocular

adjective
using one eye
6.5
Pronunciation: / məˈnɑːkjələr / (AmE), / məˈnɒkjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to, using, or involving only one eye

Examples

Monocular vision
The scientist studied the monocular vision of certain fish species.
A monocular microscope allows observation with just one eye.
Some birds rely on monocular cues to judge distance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: binocular - relating to or using both eyes
