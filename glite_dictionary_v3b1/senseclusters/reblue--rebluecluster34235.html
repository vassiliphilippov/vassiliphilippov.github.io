reblue

verb
to make blue again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈbluː / (AmE), / ˌriːˈbluː / (BrE)
to make something blue again, especially by dyeing or treating it to restore its blue color

Examples

To reblue metal parts
The technician needed to reblue the metal parts to restore their original finish.
After years in the sun, the curtains had to be reblued to look vibrant again.
She decided to reblue her favorite jeans after the color faded from washing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dye - to color something with a dye
  • antonym: fade - to lose color or brightness
