ovariectomy

noun
surgical removal of an ovary
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvəriˈɛktəmi / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəriˈɛktəmi / (BrE)
a surgical procedure to remove one or both ovaries

Examples

Bilateral ovariectomy
A bilateral ovariectomy is sometimes performed to reduce the risk of ovarian cancer.
The patient underwent an ovariectomy to treat her ovarian cyst.
Veterinarians often perform an ovariectomy as part of spaying female animals.
