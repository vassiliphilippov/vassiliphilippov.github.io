dealer

noun
person who buys and sells goods
2.7 16/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàdiÀêl…ö / (AmE), / ÀàdiÀêl…ô / (BrE)
a person or business that buys and sells goods or assets, such as art, cars, or gold

Examples

Art dealer
As a dealer in fine art, she attends many international auctions.
Car dealers across town are offering special discounts this weekend.
The antique dealer had a shop full of rare and valuable items.
Many investors buy gold from a certified bullion dealer.
Our company is the largest automobile dealer in the state.
The art dealer specializes in rare paintings from the 19th century.

Synonyms

  • trader
    2.1 16/m
    a trader buys and sells goods or assets to make a profit, while a dealer often runs a shop for specific items.
  • reseller
    3.7 1.17/m
    a reseller buys products to sell on, often adding services, while a dealer is a seller who may trade in specific goods or assets.
  • merchant
    2.1 16/m
    a merchant runs larger or formal trading operations, often wholesale or retail, while a dealer usually works with specific items or customers.
  • tradesperson
    3.5 0.00/m
    a tradesperson usually runs a small shop or offers crafts, while a dealer buys and sells a range of goods or assets.
  • merchandiser
    4.5 0.45/m
    a merchandiser plans and promotes products in stores, while a dealer focuses on buying and selling goods or assets directly.
  • monger
    8.2 0.09/m
    a monger keeps and sells a particular commodity, often in one market, while a dealer trades in various goods or assets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: retailer - a person or business that sells goods directly to consumers
  • synonym: trader - a person or firm that buys and sells goods, currencies, or stocks
