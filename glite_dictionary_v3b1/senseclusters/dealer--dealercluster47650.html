dealer

noun
person who buys and sells goods
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːlɚ / (AmE), / ˈdiːlə / (BrE)
a person or business that buys and sells goods or assets, such as art, cars, or gold

Examples

Art dealer
As a dealer in fine art, she attends many international auctions.

The antique dealer had a shop full of rare and valuable items.
Many investors buy gold from a certified bullion dealer.
Our company is the largest automobile dealer in the state.
The art dealer specializes in rare paintings from the 19th century.

Related Terms

  • synonym: merchant - a person or company involved in wholesale trade
  • synonym: retailer - a person or business that sells goods directly to consumers
  • synonym: trader - a person or firm that buys and sells goods, currencies, or stocks
