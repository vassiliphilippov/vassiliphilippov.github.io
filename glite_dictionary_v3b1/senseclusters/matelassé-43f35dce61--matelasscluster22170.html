matelassé

noun
quilted-look fabric
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmætəˈlɑːseɪ / (AmE), / ˌmatləˈseɪ / (BrE)
a heavy fabric with a raised, quilted appearance, used for bedspreads, jackets, or upholstery, or an item made from this fabric
