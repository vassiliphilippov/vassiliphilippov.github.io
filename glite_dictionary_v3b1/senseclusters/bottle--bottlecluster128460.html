bottle

noun
container for liquids, usually with a narrow neck
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːtl̩ / (AmE), / ˈbɒt.l̩ / (BrE)
a glass or plastic container with a narrow neck, used for storing drinks or other liquids

Examples

A glass bottle
Please recycle your glass bottle after you finish drinking.
She poured the wine into a clean bottle and sealed it with a cork.
There was a half-empty bottle of water on the kitchen table.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flask - a container for liquids, often for carrying alcoholic drinks
