tobogganist

noun
a person who rides a toboggan
Pronunciation: / təˈbɑɡənɪst / (AmE), / təˈbɒɡənɪst / (BrE)
a person who rides or races on a toboggan, especially for sport or recreation

Examples

A tobogganist races
As a skilled tobogganist, she won several medals in winter competitions.
Many children dream of becoming a professional tobogganist after their first ride.
The tobogganist sped down the icy slope, leaning to steer the sled.
