Synonyms

  • punctual
    3.2 0.93/m
    punctual describes a person or service that arrives at the agreed time, while on time names the expected arrival.
  • punctually
    3.5 0.14/m
    punctually means doing something exactly at the appointed moment, while on time is the more common everyday phrase.
  • right on time
    1.2 0.87/m
    right on time means arriving at the very minute planned and often in speech, while on time is neutral and standard.
  • on the dot
    2.8 0.29/m
    on the dot means at the exact minute shown on a clock, while on time is used more broadly for schedules.
  • on schedule
    2.0 2.68/m
    on schedule means a plan is progressing as planned over time, while on time focuses on single punctual moments.
