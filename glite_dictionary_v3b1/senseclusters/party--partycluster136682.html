party

noun
political organization or group
3.2 169/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ëÀêrti / (AmE), / Ààp…ëÀêti / (BrE)
an organized group of people with shared political aims or opinions, seeking to influence or control government policy, typically by contesting elections

Examples

Political party
Forming a third party is often challenging in a system dominated by two major ones.
He joined a new party that promised to fight corruption in the government.
The two main parties debated heavily before the election.

Synonyms

  • political party
    2.7 16/m
    political party is the formal, clear term used in writing and news for an organized political group, while party is the shorter, everyday word.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coalition - a temporary alliance of political parties forming a government or of states
  • synonym: faction - a small organized dissenting group within a larger one, especially in politics
  • antonym: government - the group of people with the authority to govern a country or state
