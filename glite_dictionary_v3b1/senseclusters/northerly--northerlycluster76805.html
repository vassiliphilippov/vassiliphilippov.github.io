northerly

adjective
coming from the north (wind, weather)
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrðɚli / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðəli / (BrE)
(of wind, weather, etc.) coming from the north.

Examples

Northerly wind
A sudden northerly breeze cooled the afternoon heat.
During winter, northerly gales often bring snow to the region.
The sailors prepared for the northerly wind approaching the coast.

Related Terms

  • antonym: southerly - (of wind, weather, etc.) coming from the south
