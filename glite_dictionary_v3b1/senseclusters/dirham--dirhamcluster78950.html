dirham

noun
Moroccan unit of currency
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪr.hæm /; / ˈdɪr.hɑːm /; / ˈdɪrˌhæm /; / ˈdɪr.ham /; / ˈdɪrˌhɑm / (AmE), / ˈdɪə.hæm /; / ˈdɪər.hæm /; / ˈdɪːr.ham /; / ˈdɪə.ham /; / ˈdɪərˌhæm / (BrE)
