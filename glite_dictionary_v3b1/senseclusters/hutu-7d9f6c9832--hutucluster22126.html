Hutu

proper noun
Rwandan ethnic group
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhuːtuː / (AmE), / ˈhuːtuː / (BrE)
a member of a Central African ethnic group primarily living in Rwanda and Burundi

Examples

The Hutu people
The majority of the population in Rwanda identify as Hutu.
During the 20th century, tensions between the Hutu and Tutsi groups shaped the region's history.
Many Hutu communities rely on agriculture as their main source of income.
