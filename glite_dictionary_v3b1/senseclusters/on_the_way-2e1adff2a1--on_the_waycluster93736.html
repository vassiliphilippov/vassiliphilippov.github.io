on the way

adverb
en route; in the process of going somewhere
1.3
Pronunciation: / ɑn ðə weɪ / (AmE), / ɒn ðə weɪ / (BrE)
travelling towards or en route to a particular place; in the process of arriving

Examples

To be on the way
Let me know when you're on the way so I can meet you.
She called to say she was on the way to the office.
The firefighters are on the way and should arrive soon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: en route - travelling to a destination
