microtaggant

noun
microscopic identification particle
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˈtæɡənt / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˈtæɡənt / (BrE)
a microscopic identification particle used to trace explosives, hazardous materials, or prevent counterfeiting
