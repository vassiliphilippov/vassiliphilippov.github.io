European beggar-ticks

noun
Eurasian plant; type of bur marigold
Pronunciation: / jʊˈroʊpiən ˈbɛɡər tɪks / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən ˈbɛɡə tɪks / (BrE)
A species of bur marigold native to temperate Eurasia, characterized by its yellow flowers and barbed seeds that attach to animals and clothing.
