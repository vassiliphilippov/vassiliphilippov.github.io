bakery

noun
shop where baked goods are made or sold
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪkəɹi / (AmE), / ˈbeɪkəri / (BrE)
a place where bread, cakes, and other baked goods are made or sold

Examples

A bakery shop
He works at a bakery that makes award-winning cakes and cookies.
She bought fresh bread from the local bakery every morning.
The smell of pastries filled the air as I passed by the bakery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confectionery - a shop selling sweets and candies
  • synonym: patisserie - a shop where French pastries and cakes are sold
