papershell

noun
thin-shelled nut
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪpərˌʃɛl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpəˌʃɛl / (BrE)
a nut or seed that has a very thin, easily broken shell

Examples

A papershell pecan
The papershell pecan is easier to crack than other varieties.
Farmers prefer papershell almonds because they are simpler to process and eat.
She bought a bag of papershell walnuts for holiday baking.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hardshell - a nut or seed with a hard, tough shell
  • synonym: softshell - a nut or seed with a soft, easily broken shell
