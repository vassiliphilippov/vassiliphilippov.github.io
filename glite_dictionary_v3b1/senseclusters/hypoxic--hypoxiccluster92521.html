hypoxic

adjective
low oxygen level
8.1
Pronunciation: /haɪ.ˈpɑːk.sɪk/ (AmE), /haɪ.ˈpɒk.sɪk/ (BrE)
having a lower than normal level of oxygen in the body or a specific area

Examples

A hypoxic condition
The climber became hypoxic at high altitude and needed supplemental oxygen.
Doctors monitored the hypoxic newborn closely in the intensive care unit.
If a patient is hypoxic, immediate treatment is often necessary to restore oxygen levels.

Related Terms
