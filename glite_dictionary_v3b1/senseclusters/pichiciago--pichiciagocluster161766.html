pichiciago

noun
small Argentine armadillo species
9.9
Pronunciation: / pitʃiˈtʃiɑːɡoʊ / (AmE), / piːtʃɪˈtʃiːəɡəʊ / (BrE)
a very small armadillo native to Argentina, with pale silky hair and pink bony plates on its head and neck

Examples

The pichiciago armadillo
Researchers recently discovered a pichiciago in a protected reserve after months of searching.
The pichiciago spends most of its life burrowing underground in the grasslands of Argentina.
Unlike other armadillos, the pichiciago has delicate pink plates on its head and neck.
