transcontinental

adjective
spanning or crossing a continent
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˌtrænzˌkɑːn.tɪˈnen.tl̩ / (AmE), / ˌtrænzˌkɒn.tɪˈnen.tl̩ / (BrE)
spanning, crossing, or extending across a continent; situated on or relating to the farther side of a continent.

Examples

A transcontinental flight
A transcontinental flight from New York to Los Angeles takes about six hours.
The building of the transcontinental railway transformed trade and travel across the country.
The company launched a new transcontinental shipping service connecting Asia and Europe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intercontinental - existing or occurring between continents
