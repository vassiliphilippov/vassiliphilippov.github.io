humoral

adjective
relating to bodily fluids
Pronunciation: / ˈhjuːmərəl / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmərəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving bodily fluids, especially in regard to physiological or immune responses.

Examples

Humoral immunity
Diseases can be classified based on whether they affect humoral or cellular immunity.
Medical researchers studied the humoral components of the immune system.
The humoral immune response involves antibodies circulating in body fluids.
