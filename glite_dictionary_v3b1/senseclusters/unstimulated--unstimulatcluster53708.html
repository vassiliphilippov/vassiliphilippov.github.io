unstimulated

adjective
not externally triggered
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstɪmjəˌleɪtɪd /; / ˌʌnˈstɪmjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈstɪmjʊleɪtɪd /; / ˌʌnˈstɪmjʊleɪtɪd / (BrE)
not affected by external stimulation; in a natural, resting, or inactive state (especially in medical or physiological contexts)

Examples

Researchers compared the response of unstimulated and stimulated nerve cells in the experiment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spontaneous - happening naturally, without external cause
  • antonym: stimulated - caused to respond or react by external influence
