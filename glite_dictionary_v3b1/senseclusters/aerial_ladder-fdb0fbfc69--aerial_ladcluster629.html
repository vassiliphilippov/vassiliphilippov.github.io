aerial ladder

noun
mechanically extendible ladder on fire truck
3.3 4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɛriəl ˈlædər / (AmE), / ˈeəriəl ˈlædə / (BrE)
a long, mechanically extendible ladder mounted on a fire truck, used to reach high places during firefighting or rescue operations

Examples
