tenderer

noun
bidder for contract
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈten.dər.ər/ (AmE), /ˈten.də.rə/ (BrE)
a person or company that formally offers to do work, supply goods, buy something, or make a payment, especially in business or finance

Examples

The tenderer submits offer
The government selected the most qualified tenderer for the construction project.
Each tenderer must submit their proposal by the end of the month.
The lowest tenderer was awarded the contract after a thorough review.
The tenderer submitted all the required documents for the acquisition process.
Each tenderer must meet strict financial criteria before their offer is considered.
During the takeover, the tenderer proposed to buy sixty percent of the company's shares.
The tenderer submitted the full amount to clear the outstanding balance.
At the auction, the highest tenderer won the painting with a generous offer.
The cashier accepted the payment from the tenderer without any issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bidder - a person or company that makes an offer, especially at an auction or for a contract
  • synonym: contractor - a person or company that undertakes a contract to provide materials or labor
  • antonym: debtor - a person who owes money
  • synonym: payer - a person who pays money
  • antonym: seller - a person or company offering something for sale
