crepitation

noun
sharp crackling or snapping sound
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɛpɪˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkrɛpɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a sharp, crackling or snapping sound, such as that produced by friction or pressure; especially used in medical or natural contexts

Examples

Lung crepitation
Doctors listened for crepitation in the patient's lungs as a sign of infection.
The crepitation of twigs underfoot echoed through the quiet forest.
The sudden crepitation from the fireplace startled everyone in the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crackle - a series of short sharp cracking sounds
  • synonym: pop - a short sharp sound like a small explosion or bursting
