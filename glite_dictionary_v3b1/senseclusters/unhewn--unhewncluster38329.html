unhewn

adjective
not cut or shaped; rough
8.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhjun / (AmE), / ʌnˈhjuːn / (BrE)
(of stone, wood, etc.) not cut or shaped with a tool; left in a natural or rough state.

Examples

An unhewn log
A rough bench made of unhewn logs stood outside the cabin.
The ancient wall was built from unhewn rocks collected from the nearby hills.
The path was lined with unhewn stones, giving it a natural appearance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hewn - cut or shaped with a tool, especially stone or wood
