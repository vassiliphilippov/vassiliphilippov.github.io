fluorinate

verb
to add fluorine
Pronunciation: / ˈflʊrɪˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈflɔːrɪˌneɪt / (BrE)
to treat or combine a substance with fluorine, often to change its chemical properties

Examples

To fluorinate compounds
Chemists often fluorinate organic compounds to make them more resistant to heat.
The process to fluorinate water is carefully controlled in laboratories.
Some pharmaceuticals are fluorinated to enhance their effectiveness and stability.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chlorinate - to treat or combine a substance with chlorine
  • synonym: oxidize - to combine or treat with oxygen
