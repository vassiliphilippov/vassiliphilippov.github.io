downstate

adjective
southern part of state
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊnˌsteɪt / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnˌsteɪt / (BrE)
located in or relating to the southern or less urban part of a state, especially in the United States

Examples

Downstate towns
Many people from the city enjoy spending weekends in downstate areas for a change of pace.
The university is located in a downstate region known for its beautiful countryside.
She accepted a job offer in a downstate community to be closer to her family.
