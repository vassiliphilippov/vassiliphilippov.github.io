honk

verb
slang: to vomit
7.2
Pronunciation: / hɑŋk / (AmE), / hɒŋk / (BrE)
(slang) To vomit; to eject the contents of the stomach through the mouth.

Examples

To honk after drinking
At the party, someone drank too much and ended up honking in the bathroom.
He felt so sick after the roller coaster ride that he had to honk behind the bushes.
I nearly honked when I smelled that spoiled milk.
