maison

noun
fashion house
Pronunciation: / meɪˈzɔ̃ /; / meɪˈzɒn / (AmE), / meɪˈzɒ̃ /; / ˈmɛzɒ̃ / (BrE)
a fashion house or luxury brand, especially a well-known or long-established company (from the French word for 'house')

Examples

She dreamed of working for a prestigious maison known for its elegant designs.
The century-old maison released a new perfume that quickly became a bestseller worldwide.
