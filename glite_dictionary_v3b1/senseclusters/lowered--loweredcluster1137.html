lowered

adjective
situated or moved to a lower position
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊərd / (AmE), / ˈləʊəd / (BrE)
positioned or moved to be below the normal or expected level, or below the surrounding area.

Examples

Lowered head
He entered the room with a lowered head, feeling embarrassed.
She spoke softly, her lowered eyes avoiding his gaze.
The car has a lowered suspension for better handling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depressed - lowered in level or amount, especially in reference to physical position
  • antonym: raised - positioned or moved to a higher place or level
