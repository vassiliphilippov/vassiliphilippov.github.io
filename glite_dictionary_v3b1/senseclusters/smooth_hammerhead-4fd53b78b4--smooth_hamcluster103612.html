smooth hammerhead

noun
a species of hammerhead shark
6.4
Pronunciation: / smuːð ˈhæmərˌhɛd / (AmE), / smuːð ˈhæməhɛd / (BrE)
a species of hammerhead shark (Sphyrna zygaena) with a smooth, curved head, commonly fished for its hide and liver

Examples

The smooth hammerhead shark
Despite its size, the smooth hammerhead is considered less dangerous to humans than other hammerhead sharks.
Fishermen value the smooth hammerhead for its vitamin-rich liver and durable hide.
The smooth hammerhead is often found in temperate and tropical coastal waters worldwide.
