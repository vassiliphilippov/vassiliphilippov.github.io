entrepreneur

noun
person who starts a business
Pronunciation: / ɑntrəprəˈnɜr / (AmE), / ɒntrəprəˈnɜː / (BrE)
a person who starts a business, taking on financial risks in the hope of profit

Examples

A entrepreneur starts business
As an entrepreneur, she took the risk of launching her own tech startup.
He became a successful entrepreneur after years of hard work and perseverance.
The city offers several programs to support young entrepreneurs in their ventures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: businessperson - a person engaged in commercial or industrial business
