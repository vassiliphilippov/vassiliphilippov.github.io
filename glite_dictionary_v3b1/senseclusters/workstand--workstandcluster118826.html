workstand

noun
support frame for work
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːkstænd / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkstænd / (BrE)
a sturdy frame or stand used to support an object or equipment while working on it

Examples

A workstand for tools
He placed the bicycle on the workstand to repair the chain.
The mechanic used a workstand to hold the engine steady during maintenance.
A portable workstand makes it easier to assemble furniture at home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stand - a piece of furniture or equipment used to hold something
  • synonym: workbench - a strong table for working on projects or repairs
