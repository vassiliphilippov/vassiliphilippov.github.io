unsynchronized

adjective
not happening at the same time
1.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɪŋkrəˌnaɪzd /; / ʌnˈsɪŋkrənaɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɪŋkrənaɪzd / (BrE)
