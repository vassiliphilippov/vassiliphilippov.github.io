enfilade of rooms

noun
a suite of aligned, connected rooms
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɛn.fəˈleɪd əv ˈruːmz / (AmE), / ˌen.fɪˈlɑːd əv ˈruːmz / (BrE)
A series of rooms aligned with each other so that their doorways are in a straight line, allowing a clear vista through the entire suite.

Examples

An enfilade of rooms aligned
The architect designed an enfilade of rooms for optimal light and dramatic views.
The palace boasts an impressive enfilade of rooms that stretches the length of the building.
Visitors admired the seamless enfilade of rooms as they walked through the historic mansion.
