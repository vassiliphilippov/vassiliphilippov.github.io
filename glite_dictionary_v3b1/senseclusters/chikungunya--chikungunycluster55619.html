chikungunya

noun
mosquito-borne disease
9.2
Pronunciation: /tʃɪ.kən.ˈgʌn.jə/ /; / /tʃɪ.kʊŋ.ˈgʊn.jə/ (AmE), /tʃɪ.kən.ˈgʌn.jə/ /; / /tʃɪ.kʊŋ.ˈgʊn.jə/ (BrE)
a viral disease transmitted by mosquitoes that causes fever, joint pain, and other symptoms (also, the virus that causes this disease)

Examples

Chikungunya virus infection
The doctor diagnosed her with chikungunya after she returned from her trip to the tropics.
Health officials warned that chikungunya outbreaks often follow heavy rains.
People infected with chikungunya usually experience severe joint pain and high fever.
The doctor explained that chikungunya is spread by the bite of infected mosquitoes.
After traveling to the tropics, she developed symptoms of chikungunya and needed medical attention.
Researchers are working on a vaccine to prevent chikungunya outbreaks in affected regions.
