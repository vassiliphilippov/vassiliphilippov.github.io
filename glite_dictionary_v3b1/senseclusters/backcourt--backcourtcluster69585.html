backcourt

noun
basketball guards
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌkɔrt / (AmE), / ˈbækˌkɔːt / (BrE)
the area of a basketball court farthest from the basket, or the players (usually guards) who mainly play in that area

Examples

Strong backcourt duo
The team's backcourt dominated the game with their fast breaks and sharp shooting.
In the second half, the experienced backcourt helped control the pace of the match.
A strong backcourt is essential for both offense and defense in basketball.

After the rebound, the players rushed to defend their backcourt against a fast break.
The coach instructed the guards to press in the backcourt to force a turnover.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guard - a basketball player who plays in the backcourt and handles the ball
