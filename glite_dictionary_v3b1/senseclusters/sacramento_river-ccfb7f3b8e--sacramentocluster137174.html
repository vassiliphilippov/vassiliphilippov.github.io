Sacramento River

proper noun
major river in northern California
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˌsækrəˈmɛntoʊ ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˌsækrəˈmɛntəʊ ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a major river in northern California that rises near Mount Shasta and flows south to the San Francisco Bay

Examples

Sacramento River in California
Sacramento River is the longest river entirely within California, stretching nearly 400 miles.
