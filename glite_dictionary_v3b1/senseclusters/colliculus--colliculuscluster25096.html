colliculus

noun
brain structure
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɪkjələs / (AmE), / kɒˈlɪkjʊləs / (BrE)
a small rounded structure in the brain, especially found in the midbrain region

Examples

Superior colliculus
The superior colliculus helps coordinate eye movements and visual attention.
Damage to the inferior colliculus can affect hearing and auditory processing.
Researchers studied the function of the colliculus in response to sensory stimuli.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nucleus - a group of nerve cells in the brain or spinal cord
