wind deflection

noun
sideways deviation due to wind
1.0
Pronunciation: / wɪnd dɪˈflɛkʃən / (AmE), / wɪnd dɪˈflɛkʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the sideways deviation of a projectile in flight caused by the wind

Examples

Wind deflection of projectile
Pilots must account for wind deflection when planning their approach.
The accuracy of artillery is greatly affected by wind deflection over long distances.
The sniper calculated the wind deflection before taking his shot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wind drift - deviation of a flying object caused by wind
