trot

verb
make an animal move at a trot
2.1 0.49/m
Pronunciation: / trÉ‘t / (AmE), / trÉ’t / (BrE)
to make a horse or other animal move at a trot, or to ride a horse at a trot

Examples

To trot a horse
He trots his dog in the park every morning, teaching it to move with grace.
She trotted her pony over to the stable after the trail ride.
The rider skillfully trotted the horse around the arena for the judges.
She loves to trot her horse around the ring every morning.
The instructor told him to trot before moving on to a canter.
They trotted slowly along the winding country path.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: gallop - to make a horse move at a gallop
  • degree lower: walk - to make an animal move at a walking pace
