sleeping

adjective
in low-power or standby state
3.4
Pronunciation: / c8slid0p6a4b / (AmE), / c8slid0p6a4b / (BrE)
Of a device, system, or application: in a low-power or standby state, temporarily inactive but able to resume full operation quickly.

Examples

Sleeping computer mode
After a few minutes of inactivity, the office computer went into sleeping mode to save energy.
The camera's indicator showed it was sleeping, but pressing the shutter woke it instantly.
The smartwatch briefly shows sleeping on the screen while sensors calibrate after charging.

Related Terms

  • antonym: active - fully operational and not in a low-power or standby state
  • synonym: standby - a low-power state in which a device is ready to resume full operation quickly
