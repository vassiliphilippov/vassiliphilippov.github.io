undermaster

noun
assistant teacher
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
an assistant teacher or subordinate master, especially in a school setting

Examples

The undermaster supervises students
The undermaster supervised the students during recess while the headmaster was away.
As an undermaster, she assisted with organizing classroom activities and grading assignments.
The school hired a new undermaster to help manage the growing number of students.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assistant teacher - a person who helps a main teacher in a classroom
  • antonym: headmaster - the principal or main teacher in a school
