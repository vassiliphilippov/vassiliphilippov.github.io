fellow

noun
person in the same group or profession
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛloʊ / (AmE), / ˈfɛləʊ / (BrE)
a person who is a member of the same group, class, profession, or activity as another

Examples

Fellow students
He exchanged ideas with his fellows at the annual conference.
Several fellows in the writers' group offered helpful feedback on his story.
The new researcher quickly made friends with her fellows in the laboratory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: colleague - a person with whom one works in a profession or business
