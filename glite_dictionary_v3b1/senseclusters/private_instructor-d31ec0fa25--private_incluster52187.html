private instructor

noun
person who provides individual tuition
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪvət ɪnˈstrʌktər / (AmE), / ˈpraɪvət ɪnˈstrʌktə / (BrE)
a person who provides individual or small-group tuition outside of an institutional setting, such as in singing, acting, or academics

Examples

A private instructor teaches
Many parents choose a private instructor for subjects where their child struggles in school.
She hired a private instructor to improve her piano skills before the recital.
The private instructor met with students at their homes for one-on-one lessons.
