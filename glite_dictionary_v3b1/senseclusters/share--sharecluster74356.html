share

noun
online sharing or share count
3.1 9.16/m
Pronunciation: / \u0283\u025br / (AmE), / \u0283e\u0259 / (BrE)
(in social media and online analytics) an instance of sharing a piece of content or the recorded number of times content has been shared.

Examples

A post gets shares
The article's first paragraph received thousands of shares within hours of being posted online.
The marketing team tracked each share to measure how widely the campaign content spread.
After the celebrity retweeted it, the post's daily share count jumped dramatically.

Related Terms

  • synonym: engagement - user interactions with online content, such as likes, comments, and shares
  • synonym: repost - to post the same content again on social media, often by another user
