private

adjective
not open to the public; restricted access
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪ.vət / (AmE), / ˈpraɪ.vət / (BrE)
intended for or belonging to one person or group only; not open or shared with others

Examples

Private property
Much of the land in the area is private property, not open to the public.
Only club members are allowed into the private lounge.
They held a private meeting to discuss the matter confidentially.
Each guest has a private bathroom attached to their room.
He kept his private documents locked in a secure drawer.
We enjoyed a private dinner away from the crowded restaurant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: personal - belonging to or affecting a particular person
  • antonym: public - open to or concerning the people as a whole
