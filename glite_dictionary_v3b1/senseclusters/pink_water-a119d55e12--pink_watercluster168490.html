pink water

noun
water colored pink
6.1
Pronunciation: /pɪŋk ˈwɔː.tər/ /; / /pɪŋk ˈɑː.tər/ (AmE), /pɪŋk ˈwɔː.tə/ (BrE)
water in lakes, ponds, or other bodies that appears pink because of algae, bacteria, or minerals coloring it

Examples

The pond's pink water shimmered in the sunlight, creating a striking natural scene.
