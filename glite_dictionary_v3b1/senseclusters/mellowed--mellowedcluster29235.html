mellowed

adjective
pleasantly aged in flavor
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛloʊd / (AmE), / ˈmɛl.əʊd / (BrE)
(of food or drink) having developed a full, rich, and pleasant flavor as a result of proper aging.

Examples

Mellowed flavor
A glass of mellowed whiskey is the perfect way to end a meal.
The mellowed cheese had a deep, nuanced taste after two years of aging.
These mellowed plums have a sweetness that only comes with time.
