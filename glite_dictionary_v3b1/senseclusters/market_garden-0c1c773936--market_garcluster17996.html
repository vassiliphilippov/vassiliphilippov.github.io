market garden

noun
garden for commercial crops
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrkət ˌɡɑrdn̩ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪt ˌɡɑːdn̩ / (BrE)
A garden where fruit and vegetables are grown for sale, rather than for personal use.

Examples

A market garden farm
After retiring, Sarah started a market garden to sell her vegetables at the weekend market.
The town is known for its many market gardens lining the outskirts.
They run a small market garden that supplies local restaurants with fresh produce.
