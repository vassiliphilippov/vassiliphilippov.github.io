getcha

verb
to understand (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɛtʃə / (AmE), / ˈɡɛtʃə / (BrE)
an informal contraction of 'get you', meaning to understand or catch what someone is saying

Examples

I getcha now
"I getcha, no need to explain further," he replied with a smile.
Whenever you talk about science, it takes me a minute to getcha.
She nodded and said, "Yeah, I getcha now."

Related Terms

  • antonym: miss - to fail to understand or notice
  • synonym: understand - to grasp the meaning of something
