find one's way

verb
navigate or progress towards a goal
Pronunciation: / faɪnd wʌnz weɪ / (AmE), / faɪnd wʌnz weɪ / (BrE)
to reach a destination or achieve understanding or progress, often by overcoming difficulties or uncertainty.

Examples

To find her way home
After getting separated from the group, she managed to find her way back to camp before dark.
He asked for directions to help him find his way through the unfamiliar city.
It can take years for young people to find their way in life and discover their passions.

  • synonym: navigate - to plan and direct the course of a journey
