backbar

noun
bar storage area
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌbɑr / (AmE), / ˈbækˌbɑː / (BrE)
the shelves or counter behind a bar where bottles, glasses, and bar tools are stored and displayed

Examples

The backbar shelves
The bartender arranged the bottles neatly on the backbar before opening.
Colorful lights illuminated the backbar, making the bottles look even more appealing.
She reached for a glass from the backbar to prepare the next cocktail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bar counter - the main counter where drinks are served in a bar
