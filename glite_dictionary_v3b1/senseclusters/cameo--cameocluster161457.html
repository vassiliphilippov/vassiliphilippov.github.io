cameo

verb
to appear briefly
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkæmioʊ / (AmE), / ˈkæmiəʊ / (BrE)
to make a brief appearance in a film, show, or event, often as a special guest

Examples

To cameo in a movie
The director decided to cameo in the final scene of the movie.
Several celebrities cameoed during the season finale, surprising the audience.
She was thrilled to cameo in her favorite television series for one episode.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guest star - to appear as a special guest in a show or film
  • antonym: star - to play the main role in a film or show
