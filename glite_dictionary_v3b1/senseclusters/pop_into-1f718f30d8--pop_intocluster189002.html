pop into

verb
occur suddenly in the mind
3.9
Pronunciation: / pɑp ˈɪntu / (AmE), / pɒp ˈɪntu / (BrE)
(of a thought, idea, or memory) to occur to someone suddenly and briefly; to come into someone's mind without deliberate effort.

Examples

An idea pops into mind
Suddenly a useful recipe idea popped into my head while I was washing the dishes.
Every time I try to concentrate, random memories pop into my mind and distract me.
An image of their old house popped into her thoughts during the quiet meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: come to mind - to be remembered or thought of
  • synonym: occur to - to come into someone's mind or be thought of by someone
