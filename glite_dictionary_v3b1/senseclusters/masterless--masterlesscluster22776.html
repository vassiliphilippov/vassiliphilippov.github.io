masterless

adjective
without a central controller (computing)
7.4 0.00/m

(in computing) operating without a single central master server or controller; using a decentralized or peer-to-peer architecture so that no node has exclusive control.

Examples

Masterless system
The new database runs in masterless mode, allowing any node to accept write requests without a single controller.
They chose a masterless architecture to avoid a single point of failure in production.
During the workshop, we deployed a masterless cluster that used consensus to coordinate nodes.

Synonyms

  • hostless
    6.8 0.00/m
    hostless describes systems that run without a central server, while masterless emphasizes a peer-to-peer architecture with no single controlling node.
  • leaderless
    5.1 0.4/m
    leaderless refers to groups with no person in charge, while masterless refers specifically to computing systems without a central controller.

Related Terms

  • antonym: centralized - controlled or coordinated by a single central authority or server; opposite of masterless.
  • synonym: decentralized - not organized around a single central authority; control is distributed across components.
  • synonym: distributed - spread across multiple machines or locations rather than centralized in one place.
