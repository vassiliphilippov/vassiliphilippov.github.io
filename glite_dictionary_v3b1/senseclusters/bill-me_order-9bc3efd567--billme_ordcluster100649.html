bill-me order

noun
order billed after delivery
Pronunciation: / bɪl mi ˈɔrdər / (AmE), / bɪl mi ˈɔːdə / (BrE)
an order for goods or services that is received without immediate payment, with the customer being billed at a later date

Examples

A bill-me order processed
For large corporate clients, we frequently process bill-me orders instead of requiring upfront payment.
She placed a bill-me order for the equipment to simplify the purchasing process.
The company accepted a bill-me order from the university, allowing payment at a later date.

Related Terms

  • synonym: invoice order - an order where goods are supplied and payment is requested via invoice
