bum

verb
to travel as a vagrant
Pronunciation: / bʌm / (AmE), / bʌm / (BrE)
to travel or live by asking strangers for rides, money, or food, often moving from place to place without a permanent home.

Examples

To bum across country
After college, he bummed around the country, staying with friends and strangers.
They bummed rides from truck drivers to get to the next city.
For a year, she bummed her way through Europe, living on very little money.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beg - to ask people for money or food because you are poor
  • antonym: settle - to stay in one place and make it your home
  • synonym: wander - to move from place to place without a fixed plan
