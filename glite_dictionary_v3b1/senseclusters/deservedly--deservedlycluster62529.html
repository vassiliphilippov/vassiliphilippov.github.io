deservedly

adverb
in a way that is earned or justified
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzɝːvɪdli / (AmE), / dɪˈzɜːvɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that is right or appropriate because of what someone has done; as something rightly earned or merited

Examples

Lost privileges deservedly
He lost his privileges deservedly after repeatedly breaking the rules.
She was deservedly praised for her outstanding performance in the play.
The team deservedly won the championship after a season of hard work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: justly - in a fair or deserved manner
  • antonym: undeservedly - in a way that is not deserved
