bench jockey

noun
sideline supporter in sports
6.1
Pronunciation: / bɛntʃ ˈdʒɑki / (AmE), / bɛntʃ ˈdʒɒki / (BrE)
a person who, while not actively playing in a game, supports and encourages their team from the sidelines

Examples

A bench jockey cheers
After his injury, he took on the role of bench jockey to keep morale high.
Despite not starting the game, he was a vocal bench jockey throughout the match.
She became known as the team's best bench jockey, always offering encouragement to her teammates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheerleader - a person who leads cheers and encourages a team from the sidelines
