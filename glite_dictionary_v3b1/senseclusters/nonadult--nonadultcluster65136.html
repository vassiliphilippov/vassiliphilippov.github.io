nonadult

noun
person under legal age
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnəˈdʌlt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnəˈdʌlt / (BrE)
a person who has not reached the legal age of adulthood; a minor.

Examples

A nonadult cannot vote
The movie is not suitable for nonadults due to its mature content.
Only nonadult members under eighteen are eligible for reduced program fees this summer.
The law protects nonadults from working in hazardous environments.
