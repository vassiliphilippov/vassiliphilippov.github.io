hub

verb
to connect to hub
6.3
Pronunciation: /hʌb/ (AmE), /hʌb/ (BrE)
to connect a device, system, or network to a central hub, or to provide something with a hub.

Examples

To hub devices

Before the upgrade, they hubbed the printers to ensure everyone could access them.
We hubbed the devices so that data could flow through a single point.
